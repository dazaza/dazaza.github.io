Here are 5 blog services that allow you to bind a custom domain for free: 1. **WordPress.com** - Offers a free plan, and while custom domains usually require an upgrade, you can bind a custom domain if you opt for a paid plan. However, some features might be limited in the free version. 2. **Blogger (Blogspot)** - Allows you to connect a custom domain for free. You just need to verify ownership of the domain through your domain registrar. 3. **Wix** - While their free plan includes ads, Wix allows you to connect a custom domain without additional charges by upgrading to a paid plan for full customization. 4. **Weebly** - Supports custom domains, though to fully utilize this feature without a subdomain, you’ll typically need a paid plan. The basic plan is free but has limitations. 5. **Ghost (free tier)** - Ghost offers a free tier with limited functionality. For custom domains, you generally need to move to a higher plan, but technical users may find workarounds. Please note that while these platforms offer free options, advanced domain settings or removing platform branding often requires moving to a paid plan. Always check the latest terms and conditions as they may change over time.
