The correct method of floor waxing

Floor polishing and waxing can remove stains and add gloss, prevent insect damage, moisture, cracking, extend the life of the floor, and is a necessary measure for floor maintenance.

Generally, there are several steps to wax the floor:

Wax selection: Apply protective wax on the floor surface. After solidification, it will isolate air, water, dust, making it easier to clean and prevent wear, thus extending its service life while providing anti-slip and anti-static effects; Professional personnel ensure the wax liquid is evenly distributed with an appropriate thickness, creating a bright and beautiful appearance. The wax used is Superbao high-grade no-polish wax.

Floor dust removal: First, use steel wool pad on the wax brush and move back and forth along the grain of the floor, scrubbing hard to thoroughly clean up garbage, dirt, and grime. Then wipe the floor with a slightly damp mop before waxing.

Waxing cycle: Wax every 2 or 3 months. The amount can be gradually reduced, with a little more wax used for the first time. For every 30 square meters of floor, about 300 grams of floor wax can be applied. When waxing, apply it evenly on the floor surface and let it "soak in". After lightly wiping and finishing the wax, let the floor fully absorb it and dry completely. Then use a dry soft cloth to polish the floor back and forth until it is smooth and shiny.
