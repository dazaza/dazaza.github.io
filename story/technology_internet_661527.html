New Trend for Women's Beauty: Full-body Whitening

by wswen356 on 2012-02-09 10:03:04

Whitening is an evergreen topic for beauty-loving women. As the saying goes, "being white can cover up three ugliness". Influenced by this concept, women's pursuit and desire for whitening are just like humanity's pursuit of light - it has never stopped. Reporters have discovered that with the progress of the times and technology, people's requirements for whitening have also been upgraded. They don't just focus on facial whitening; they equally value body whitening care. "To be beautiful, one must be white all over" has become their firm belief. Mature women prefer to go to professional beauty institutions for full-body whitening care.

With the upcoming summer, full-body whitening is becoming increasingly popular among women. In the eyes of many women, daily facial whitening care is a necessary routine, but whitening other parts of the body should not be overlooked. The delicate hands, arms, necks, and other areas are all parts that women highly value for whitening. These areas most easily reveal a woman's age secrets. If these areas are not properly cared for, it will greatly reduce the overall whitening effect for women. Ms. Wang, who is considering body whitening care, told reporters that she wants to do body whitening care now because she is worried that when wearing spaghetti-strap dresses or backless outfits in the summer, her arms, neck, and other exposed areas may affect her overall beauty.

A beauty consultant from a slimming beauty institution told reporters that modern women no longer only focus on facial whitening; they pay more attention to their overall beauty effect. Everyone has a beauty-loving heart, and body whitening care projects are especially favored by mature women. It was introduced that these women have a certain level of consumption ability and mature consumption concepts. Regular visits to beauty institutions for care are very effective ways for whitening. Generally speaking, facial whitening care is done once a week, while body whitening care is done about every half a month. Whitening care at beauty institutions is more professional. Just taking deep cleaning care as an example, its effect cannot be reached by daily home care, and body whitening care needs to be done in professional beauty institutions.

Some dietary habits in daily life are also beneficial for whitening.

In addition, some dietary habits in daily life are also beneficial for whitening. Women can choose fruits rich in vitamin C; eating more of these fruits helps with whitening. It is understood that fresh fruits such as cucumbers, strawberries, tomatoes, and oranges are rich in vitamin C. A large amount of vitamin C can help melanin reduction and also prevent the formation of dark spots and freckles, making the skin exude health and vitality. You should eat less animal liver and beans because the copper or zinc contained in these foods can make the skin darker.

Mary Kay reminds women who want to whiten their skin to remember to drink plenty of water and not to replace water with beverages. Water is the best substance to promote metabolism, which can moderately replenish the moisture within cells. Not only does it help the normal operation of various organs, but it can also take away waste inside the body. You should develop the habit of drinking water irregularly and not wait until you're thirsty to drink water, because being thirsty indicates that the degree of dehydration in your body is already quite severe. Drink water on an empty stomach right after waking up in the morning; adding a slice of lemon to the water makes the beauty effect more noticeable. Please drink a small glass of water 30 minutes before going to bed at night so that the cells can fully absorb it, effectively preventing the generation of wrinkles. Additionally, try to keep your diet light and choose appropriate whitening skincare products.
