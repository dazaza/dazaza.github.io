Sputtr: A One-Stop Search Engine - Fresh and Sweet IT.Net

by xjyzhenai on 2009-05-06 09:32:01

When we need to look up information, we often rely on search engines. In fact, many people like to set their browser homepage to various search engines. The results from different major search engines are often quite different, so we are accustomed to switching between multiple search engine services.

image

Until the arrival of Sputtr. It is a brand-new, simple search method that helps you find the most relevant information. Its homepage includes all the search tools you need, saving your valuable time!

image

Just enter the keywords you want to look up in the search box, then click on the icon of the search engine you want to use, and Sputtr will call up that engine to search for the keywords you need.

In the era of network diversification, service providers complement each other's strengths, creating a vibrant and varied landscape with their own unique advantages. Therefore, it's impossible for us to use the services of only one provider. Sputtr is actually very simple; it just gathers commonly used search services together, making them conveniently available for people to use and saving the time spent on switching between them. At the same time, it also changes people's search habits.
