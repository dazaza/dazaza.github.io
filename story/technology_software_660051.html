The mourning ceremony of Chen Na, the Fudan anti-cancer female teacher who passed away, was held this morning. Netizens mourned her spontaneously.

by n7ssd2mlp on 2012-02-07 11:09:54

Ms. Li is a Weibo friend of Juans, although the insurance industry she works in does not have much intersection with education, when talking about Juan, she told the Oriental Net reporter that she has been following Juan's Weibo and was moved by her story. "She is still very young, it's unfortunate for such a disease to befall her, and while she was sick, she still published her inner journey online, which deeply moved me. Today, I specially came to see her off."

The bright smile conveys Juan's love for life. The photos are sourced from the internet.

After the eulogy by the Dean of the School of Social Development and Public Policy at Fudan University, the crowd quietly and orderly queued up to bid farewell to the deceased. On both sides, TV screens played a rotation of Juan's photos from her lifetime. Many people had red eyes as they saw her off. The Oriental Net reporter noticed that students, middle-aged people, and elderly individuals with white hair were all present among those who came to pay their respects. Many had never even met Juan, but they were all deeply moved by her story.

Reported by Oriental Net journalists Sun Xiaofei, Cao Lei, and Mao Lijun on April 23: The red outdoor jacket contrasts sharply with the mountains behind her, but what touches the heart most is her radiant smile. At 10 AM today, the memorial service for Juan, the female teacher at Fudan University who fought cancer, was held at the Yide Hall of the Baoshan District Funeral Home. The Oriental Net reporter observed on-site that before 10 AM, more than a thousand students and netizens had spontaneously come to pay their respects, filling the Yide Hall where the ceremony was held.

Mr. Meng had also never met Juan, but as an educator himself, he came to see her off with admiration. In his impression, Juan was exceptionally outstanding, and society greatly needs such role models. "Juan set a good example for the youth and educators, and is highly worthy of respect. Today, I came with a heavy heart to see her off."

Yide Hall is one of the larger halls in the Baoshan District Funeral Home. The eight large characters "Virtuous and Upright, Eternal Remembrance" make the entire hall appear solemn. Before 10 AM, the hall was already filled with people who had come to mourn. In the center of the hall, Juan, dressed in red, stands out with her photo taken before she fell ill, showing a radiant smile against the backdrop of the mountains, conveying the deceased's love for life and nature. The Oriental Net reporter observed at the memorial service site that apart from the teachers and students organized by Fudan University and Shanghai Jiao Tong University to come and pay their respects, there were also many students and netizens who had spontaneously come to see Juan off. Outside the hall, people continued to arrive in long queues.
