QQ security card

by yinyin on 2008-06-18 12:42:53

QQ Password Protection Card is a security service provided by Tencent for account protection. It is a card with numbers arranged in 10 rows and 8 columns. When performing sensitive operations (such as transferring equipment in Fantasy games, modifying QQ password), the system will prompt the user to input the numbers from three positions on the password protection card. Only when all inputs are correct will the system allow further operation.

Who should use the Password Protection Card?

The QQ Password Protection Card is designed for users who urgently need to enhance the security of their QQ account:

1) If your account is very important to you, such as having important contacts like clients;

2) If your internet environment is not secure, such as using public computers in internet cafes;

3) If your account has a large amount of Q Coins or advanced game equipment.

How to obtain a Password Protection Card?

1) You can purchase a QQ Card with an attached password protection function at Tencent QQ Card sales points (such as newspaper kiosks, software stores, computer markets, and internet cafes).

2) You can directly log in to the QQ Password Protection Card website, where you can freely collect a graphic-based Password Protection Card and save it to your computer, email, or phone.

To ensure the security of your account, please do not use second-hand Password Protection Cards whose serial number coating has already been scratched off.
