Six Promotion Techniques for Q&A Platforms

by aptech_919 on 2012-02-25 18:41:57

I would like to share with you six promotion tips for Q&A platforms from my love of online marketing studio. The main Q&A platforms include: Baidu Zhidao, Sou Sou Wenwen and Tianya Answers (these three have relatively high weight). Proper use of Q&A platforms can bring traffic to the website and increase external links, which is very beneficial for online marketing and SEO. Let's take a look at the promotion skills of Q&A platforms below, (*^__^*) hehe...

Tip One: Control Quantity

Q&A websites usually rely on their own search engines. If you do too much, it may result in the deletion of related Q&A pages, or even account/IP blocking, and in serious cases, the site may be penalized. Here, "penalizing" does not refer to search engine penalties but rather that once there is a link to this site in the submitted questions, it will not be approved. For Baidu Zhidao, out of three questions, two can have links and one should not; for Sou Sou Wenwen, all three can have links, but you should not set them as the best answer at the same time; Yahoo Knowledge Hall allows links.

Tip Two: Improve Quality

Both questions and answers need to be carefully set. The questions should naturally incorporate keywords, and the answers should ideally include several instances of the keyword, even allowing for some keyword stuffing. A higher keyword density can benefit rankings, but it must still appear natural and be readable.

Tip Three: Diversify Categories

When asking questions, don't do it under the same category. As long as it's relevant, you can ask across different categories to avoid overly single links. Many sites collect these Q&As, but some only collect from one category. By asking across multiple categories, different websites can collect your Q&A, making the link sources more diverse.

Tip Four: Join a Team

Joining a team is quite necessary. Questions answered by a team appear more professional and trustworthy. For Sou Sou Wenwen, sometimes submitted answers with links won't pass and require repeated modifications, but the chances of passing are higher when answering under the team's name. Another advantage is that it's less likely to expose your account.

Tip Five: Q&A Links

The Q&A doesn't always have to link back to the website. Linking similar Q&As to the same website might raise suspicion among visitors, making it obvious that it's specifically for promotion. Using multiple links pointing to the Q&A page increases its weight and improves ranking. For Baidu, since it's Baidu's own link, it avoids the trouble of external link verification failing.

Tip Six: Indirect Promotion

These Q&As themselves have high weight and good rankings, but if there are some external links pointing to them, the rankings will be even better. Choose an important Q&A page, create other similar Q&As, and have all the links point to this important page. Then, make a few links to this Q&A on Tieba or other forums. The weight increases quickly. Once the ranking goes up, it brings stable traffic. An increased weight adds a high-quality external link to the website, becoming indirect promotion.
