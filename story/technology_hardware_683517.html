She had flagged down many vehicles, but none of them stopped. Upon hearing the cries for help, Liu Congyu and his wife quickly got out of the car and discovered an elderly person lying beside a crashed vehicle. The elderly person, speaking a heavy, barely understandable dialect, cried while gesturing towards their arm: "My arm is broken, my arm is broken, save us."

Seeing the elderly person lying on the ground curled up in pain, pale-faced, and moaning incessantly, the couple Liu Congyu immediately helped the elder into their car. After getting into the car, a relative of the elder tearfully said, "You are truly good people; I have already flagged down many cars, but no one stopped..."

Liu Congyu, the rescuer: Being a good person isn't hard.

After arriving at the hospital, the three members of Liu Congyu's family sprang into action. One stayed with the child to take care of the two injured elders, while another busied themselves handling registration, X-rays, tests, hospital admission procedures, all the way until the injured elder was sent into the operating room. Watching doctors and nurses rushing in and out, the elder's condition was under control, and their son was also heading from afar to Zhenjiang. Only then did the Liu family quietly leave the hospital.

"Did you not worry about the cost when you paid upfront for the rescue, in case the other party couldn’t or wouldn’t pay back?" After paying upfront to save the person, many people asked Liu Congyu such questions. "I didn't think about it, if they don't repay, then so be it," Liu Congyu replied calmly. He said that in the eyes of many, his offer of help consumed time, money, and involved risks. But if he, like the several other drivers, hadn't stopped the car, there might have been yet another case of 'ignoring someone in distress' reported in the media, becoming a new example of society growing increasingly cold-hearted. But he didn't want things to be that way.

Afterward, the elder's son sent many text messages on behalf of the entire family to Liu Congyu: "On behalf of my whole family, I thank you and your family for your selfless help and great kindness" and "Because of your righteous actions, my father received timely treatment, and his condition has been well controlled. The doctors said that without your timely assistance, my father’s hand might have become disabled. We cannot express our gratitude in words..."

"Reality is a mirror reflecting human hearts. When you actively show kindness, reality will never return a grotesque image to you." Liu Congyu said that in his view, being a good person isn't difficult, nor does it carry so much risk. Those isolated cases of 'doing good and being deceived' should not become excuses for people to avoid doing good deeds. He hopes more people can express their kind side to the world.

The couple Liu Congyu comforted the elder not to worry while calling a friend in Zhenjiang to ask which hospital in Zhenjiang had the best orthopedic technology. The friend told them that the orthopedic department of the 359th Hospital of the PLA was the best. After obtaining the agreement of the two elders, the couple drove directly to the 359th Hospital in Zhenjiang.

On the afternoon of December 31, 2011, Liu Congyu, a cadre of the Discipline Inspection Committee of the Jiangsu Provincial Department of Justice, and his wife Jiang Jing, who worked at the Jinling Women Police Center, were driving home with their child for the holiday. When the car was traveling on the Shanghai-Nanjing Expressway near Jurong, they suddenly noticed traffic congestion on the highway and realized something must have happened. Indeed, there was a five-car pile-up, and the accident scene was extremely gruesome. Due to the complex road conditions, the traffic flow was slow.

Encountering a terrible accident, the family of three was unafraid of trouble.

"During New Year's Day, you encountered an accident on your way home and your family paid to rescue others. If you encounter another accident during the Spring Festival trip home, would you still rescue?" "Why wouldn't we?" Yesterday, during casual chats with colleagues, the story of Liu Congyu's family unexpectedly paying for the rescue on the Shanghai-Nanjing Expressway a week ago came to light. Facing many friends' well-intentioned warnings about "doing good and being deceived," Liu Congyu smiled faintly: "In my opinion, being a good person isn't difficult."
