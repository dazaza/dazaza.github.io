5 Secrets to Whiter Skin from Pores

by wswen356 on 2012-02-15 12:48:04

All fashionably dressed women want to carry out whitening maintenance, but before purchasing whitening products, please first confirm whether the reason for the skin not being white is caused by pores. Many people have the problem of enlarged pores, and obvious pores are also a factor that makes the skin tone look uneven and dull. By improving the pores, once the pores become whiter, the skin texture will look like tofu skin, which everyone admires! Exclusive secrets! Key points of pore whitening summarized: it's all about making the skin experience temperature changes.

The first two key steps are to successfully master temperature changes, choosing to increase or decrease skin temperature as appropriate. As long as this principle is properly applied, you can easily improve acne and pore problems at home.

Warming care: removing dirt and oil from pores.

Secret 1: Steam your face with hot vapor. This method is very simple. Just pour hot water into a heat-resistant basin and start immediately. Bring your face close to use the steam to help, for about 3-5 minutes. You can also buy a facial steamer on the market, just add water and plug it in to start maintenance.

*Close your eyes and let the warm vapor from the basin blow on your skin.

Secret 2: Hot towel compress. This beauty secret greatly helps relax and circulate the skin. Just prepare a hot towel to achieve the effect of making pimples rise to be cleared. After hot compressing, wash your face lightly with clear water, and the effect will be amazing!

*Prepare a clean towel, heat it up, then fold it into a strip about five centimeters wide.

*Start from the forehead, cover your cheeks with the warm towel, wrap your facial skin, then gently press the towel with your palms to make the heat effective.

Cooling care: shrinking and tightening large pores.

Secret 3: Use cold packs. Cold packs used to keep drinks cold are also great for those who want to tighten their pores! Besides using them in daily maintenance, when engaging in outdoor activities, you can bring a small refrigerator with cold packs and icy drinks. When the outside temperature rises, and you feel more sebum secretion and stickiness, take out a small towel wrapped around the cold pack and lightly apply it on your face. Not only will it cool down your skin, but it will also prevent pores from expanding due to high temperatures.

*You can buy such small cold packs in a daily goods store. With this convenient tool, it helps shrink pores more effectively.

*Wrap the cold pack in a small towel to avoid excessive stimulation from the cold pack directly on the skin. Wrap it into a small rectangle.

*Lightly apply the towel wrapped around the cold pack on your whole face, especially on the prominent pores of the cheeks and the oily T-zone, letting the pores instantly tighten.

Quick hydration: replenishing moisture in a short time.

Secret 4: Moisturizing essence wet compress. When you feel the skin on your cheeks becoming dry and tight, or frequently experiencing makeup floating and peeling issues, it's time to strengthen the hydration and supplement water. In emergency situations like dates, you can use this secret to rescue your skin and make it soft and elastic.

*Take out moisturizing toner or moisturizing essence, and soak an appropriate amount on a cotton pad.

*After ensuring the cotton pad is fully soaked with the beauty solution, tear it into two pieces and lightly apply it on your skin.

*Stick it on your cheeks and wait for about 5 minutes, allowing the ingredients to penetrate fully and achieve immediate hydration effects.

Protecting whitening: never neglect sunscreen.

Secret 5: Complete sunscreen application 20 minutes before going out. After seriously spending time on pore-tightening maintenance and buying expensive whitening essence to apply, isn't the goal to cultivate pore-free, fair skin? Therefore, at the last crucial step, sunscreen must not be overlooked. Once sunlight UV rays directly shine on the skin for a long time, they will cause spots and aging. Apply sunscreen 15-30 minutes before going out to completely protect against the sun. Combine it with timely reapplication, so that the sun has no chance to harm your skin.

*Apply an appropriate amount of sunscreen evenly on every inch of your skin, carefully applying and confirming that all small areas have been covered with sunscreen.
