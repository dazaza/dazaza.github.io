The principle of形象thinking character recognition for the whole brain

by youershizi on 2009-07-02 10:59:05

The whole brain education has created a brand-new character recognition method, whose core invention is to use the characteristics of Chinese characters as pictophonetic symbols. First, Chinese characters are transformed into pictures that are very close to the shape or meaning of the characters, so that children use their right brain instead of left brain to memorize. There are two kinds of fast character recognition tools: character recognition cards and VCD discs. When using the VCD disc for character recognition, what the children see first is not the character but a picture. Then, by means of animation, this picture is slowly turned into a character. After a while, the character is restored to the previous picture, and this process is repeated several times.........
