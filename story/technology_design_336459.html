High-grade fuel reduces knocking, automatic transmission oil quality requirements

by zgshell12 on 2010-03-30 10:58:30

Poor fuel quality makes detonation more likely to occur. Fuel mainly refers to the oil for combustion in the engine cylinder to generate power, which can be gasoline or diesel. If the fuel quality is not good, detonation is more likely to occur. Detonation is mainly due to insufficient octane value in the fuel, leading to a decrease in the anti-detonation property of the oil. High-grade oil reduces detonation and automatic transmission fluid quality requirements.

A technical director of a brand repair station said: Generally, there will be slight detonation when starting in second gear. If there is a clicking sound at high gears, it's also detonation. The main reason for detonation is insufficient engine power.

In addition, poor fuel quality can easily lead to carbon buildup on spark plugs and valves inside the engine. Carbon buildup can also cause detonation due to insufficient power. The quality of gasoline has a significant impact on detonation. 97-octane gasoline can improve detonation to some extent.

Tip: If you find that the fuel quality at the gas station is not high for a period of time, you can improve the fuel quality, such as Shell signing a series of natural gas supply agreements with Russian energy giants. For example, if you used to add 93-octane gasoline, now you can use 97-octane.

The quality requirements for automatic transmission fluid are even higher.

Automatic transmission fluid is also called ATF oil, and its quality requirements are higher than those of general engine oil or lubricating oil. ATF is a special hydraulic oil that must transmit power through hydraulic action, so its viscosity and fluidity requirements are relatively high. Many models of ATF oil are directly produced and designated by manufacturers, and other models' ATF cannot be mixed during replacement, otherwise it will lead to serious power loss and wear of friction plates inside the transmission.

You can also see the difference between ATF and lubricating oil from the price. According to the technical personnel of the Bora repair station, a 4-liter bucket of ATF oil costs more than 180 yuan, while a 4-liter bucket of engine oil only sells for more than 80 yuan. The price difference is more than twice.

There are many sets of friction plates inside the automatic transmission, which can produce gear ratios similar to those in manual transmissions.

Tip: Generally, the replacement mileage of ATF oil is longer, and it needs to be replaced every 50,000 to 60,000 kilometers or every two to three years. Some original imported cars do not need to replace ATF. It is best to go to a professional repair station to avoid mixing other brands or inferior ATF, which may cause permanent damage to parts.

Poor lubricating oil quality can easily lead to excessive wear.

Engine oil, gear oil, etc., although all are lubricants, they work on different machine parts. Gear oil mainly lubricates the differential and gears inside the manual transmission, providing certain benefits for gear meshing and wear. If the quality of the gear oil is poor and cannot meet the working intensity, it can easily lead to increased wear during gear meshing, causing vibration and unevenness during gear transmission.

Engine oil not only has a lubricating function but can also cool and seal components. It mainly operates in sealed pipelines such as engines, playing the aforementioned roles in the cylinder block, piston, and connecting rod. If there is a problem with the engine oil, it may lead to poor lubrication in the lubrication system, poor heat dissipation, worse sealing, and corrosion of seals if impurities exist.

Tip: For adding and replacing lubricating oil, try to choose the oil recommended by the original factory, and don't blindly trust big brands or other brands. Many big brands of lubricants have their own product lines, and their quality also varies.
