Introduction to the plot of "The Prestigious Bride":

The Cao family, though wealthy and prestigious, harbors many unknown contradictions and struggles within. Cao Tianji, the second son of the Cao family, is kind-hearted and elegant in demeanor. However, from childhood, he has been trapped in a deep sense of guilt that he cannot escape. She arranged for the smart, beautiful, and cunning Xiang Xuexin to marry her eldest son Cao Tianyu unconditionally, while letting her second son Cao Tianji marry Xiang Xueling, the younger sister of Xiang Xuexin.
