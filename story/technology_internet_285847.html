How the dry cleaning industry faces the financial crisis

by 8eocomcom on 2009-11-29 19:57:01

In the face of a financial storm, will you passively hide in a tree hole "waiting for summer" or actively take on the challenge and "tend towards the mountain of tigers"? They, who have already been greatly affected by the economic crisis, are also tired of leaving their funds at banks to "sleep soundly." Therefore, they choose to act at the right time and seize opportunities.

Many people might think that, in such a financial crisis, with so many unemployed individuals and graduates struggling to find jobs, and so few goods being sold, how could anyone dare to talk about big investments?

Actually, there is still a large amount of idle capital in China, suppressed by strong investment urges, waiting for good opportunities to emerge and ready to act. This is because various opportunities are hidden within the financial crisis. Since every country's development requires sufficient capital, the reduction in investment costs and thresholds has attracted some investors with sharp commercial insight, who become more courageous after each setback and continue to invest.

Defensive Investment: "Asset Protection"

For those who wish to minimize risks as much as possible, defensive investment should be the most acceptable investment method. Currently, the safest wealth management method is to directly deposit money in the bank, as this aligns with Chinese people's financial habits, believing that banks are safe. However, under the current financial crisis, is it really safe to keep money in the bank? Not necessarily. Currently, central banks around the world are continuously lowering interest rates, and some financial experts predict that many countries may implement zero interest rates. Considering the inflation caused by the financial crisis, the one-year deposit in the bank might lose quite a bit of value when withdrawn upon maturity.

Therefore, choosing a defensive investment method that you can accept is also a good strategy to preserve asset value. In a market filled with crises, retail investors must learn the "bear market survival techniques." Clever use of diversified investment tools such as funds, stocks, foreign exchange, bonds, and gold can help mitigate risks and protect assets while preparing for the next bull market.

Wang Meiyi, who works at a logistics company in Shanghai, mentioned her recent investment direction: "Nowadays, many people are afraid to buy houses, but we shouldn't panic over real estate. Instead, we can shift our focus from residential properties to commercial properties. Although the downward trend in housing prices has already formed, investing in commercial properties, especially in second-tier business districts in first-tier cities, could be an opportunity."

For this view, many people agree. Successful commercial zones are considered good investment products. Mature commercial real estate usually has a higher price, but offers stable returns with less risk. After the cooling down of the housing market, these properties have greater appreciation potential. During a housing market downturn, they offer certain resistance to depreciation and maintain value, making them ideal investment and wealth management products today.

However, in investments where defense is the primary goal, the basic principle is the effective combination of diversified investments. Financial advisors suggest that the personal investment ratio for late 2009 should be: 20%-30% in stocks and stock funds, 30%-50% in bonds and bond funds, and 20%-50% in bank deposits and short-term wealth management products. Do not put all your funds into one basket. By doing so, the risk will significantly decrease.

For example, recently, gold investment has been very popular, and many people intend to invest more in this area. "Personally, I believe that investing in gold carries relatively lower risks. In the current environment, it’s possible that the gold market will fluctuate, making it suitable for medium to long-term investments. The preservation function of gold can help you avoid many financial risks. If managed well, the returns are relatively stable and substantial," said freelance investor Mr. Zuo, indicating his intention to increase gold investments.

However, during the global financial turmoil this year, gold prices have often fluctuated dramatically, confusing many investors and experts. If gold is just part of your portfolio, you may find yourself caught between wanting to enter and exit the market, missing out on investment opportunities.

Risk-Taking Investment: Finding the Right "Gold-Mining" Channel

Any investment carries risks, including aggressive investments. But if you want high returns and rewards, you need to carefully choose risky investments.

Choosing financial products allows for both small and large investments, and some investors prefer the real industry sector, especially projects of a certain scale, which aren't something that can be done with just a few tens of thousands of yuan.

Currently, two types of investment projects are highly favored: daily consumer goods and education. Among various industries, it is important now to choose sectors with fast cash flow. During economic downturns, delayed payments are serious issues. If you choose industries with slower cash flow, even newly established large enterprises can easily fail due to insufficient cash reserves. Sufficient cash flow ensures survival at least.

If you're considering investments in daily consumer goods, focus on traditional mass consumption industries. During these times, the basic consumer market is the most reliable, whether in prosperous or depressed economic periods. For instance, dining, laundry, car washing, etc., are both fast-cash-flow industries and mass daily consumption industries. Consumption can be divided into mass consumption and luxury consumption. Starbucks, for example, represents leisure consumption, and joining its franchise should be approached cautiously. Similarly, investments in tourism and automobile industries should also be handled carefully.

The convenience store industry is consistently viewed favorably by insiders and the public, despite going through expansion phases and reshuffles. It is currently gradually entering a mature and profitable phase.

Mr. Wang Chao from the Huaxi District Development Department of Youshi Supermarket Chain Co., Ltd. in Hangzhou told reporters: "You Shi holds franchise explanation meetings every week. Recently, many people have come to inquire, including those seeking entrepreneurial opportunities after being laid off, as well as many young people, some of whom explicitly state that they choose to invest in convenience stores to secure additional income."

According to Mr. Wang, opening a simple daily necessities orange-sign You Shi convenience store requires 180,000 yuan, while a green-sign You Shi with fresh hot food equipment needs more than 300,000 yuan. Including three years of rent, employee expenses, water and electricity fees, product losses, consultation management fees, etc., a green-sign You Shi would need at least 600,000 to 700,000 yuan initially. For ordinary citizens, this is not a small investment project. Moreover, the location of the store is crucial. If chosen well and operated attentively, profits could be substantial. However, if chosen poorly, failure is also highly possible, making the risk significant.

Besides daily consumer goods, the education industry is also rapidly warming up. Under the backdrop of globalization, many industries have been impacted by the financial crisis. However, experts unanimously believe that the financial crisis's winter is precisely the "spring" for the education industry.

While many industries are constrained by funding issues, Rui Si successfully raised 30 million yuan in venture capital last year-end. Regarding this, the CEO of Nanjing Ruifadi International Education Development Co., Ltd., Dong Yufeng, stated in an interview that education globally has rigid demand. Specifically for China, there is a saying, "No matter how poor, education cannot be neglected; no matter how bitter, children cannot be neglected." The entire Asian region and the world place great importance on education, which will not decline due to the financial crisis but will likely increase due to its impact.

To illustrate with an example, imagine a middle-aged person affected by the financial tsunami losing their job. They would surely think, "It's impossible that everyone is laid off; those who remain employed must be more capable than me." Thus, they would find reasons - perhaps they didn't receive enough education in some areas in the past. Therefore, they wouldn't let their children follow the same path, as the arrival of a financial crisis is beyond individual control.

The earliest form of education available now is early childhood education centers targeting infants a few months old up to school-age children. While some doubt this type of educational program, many others strongly support it. As more parents embrace this concept, its operational status becomes increasingly prosperous.

Golden Baby Early Learning Center is acclaimed as the "global first brand in early childhood education," founded in the United States in 1976. Currently, there are more than 500 educational outlets in 27 advanced countries and regions worldwide, including the UK, USA, Canada, Switzerland, France, and Asia. Specifically in Zhejiang Province, there are multiple branches in cities like Hangzhou, Ningbo, Wenzhou, and Yiwu.

In the Prosperous Commercial Plaza on Nanyuan Road in Yiwu, there is currently the largest "Golden Baby" franchise in Jinhua Region. This early learning center, covering over 1,000 square meters, is operated by Jin Liliang, a young entrepreneur in his prime. "Initially, I took my own child to attend early learning classes at Golden Baby in Hangzhou and was immediately captivated by their educational philosophy. At that moment, I had the idea to invest and open my own branch," Jin Liliang shared with the reporter, explaining how he initially conceived the idea to join the early learning center franchise.

Shortly after, he invested over 2 million yuan, combining his savings and loans, to open this "Golden Baby." Since its opening in May last year, it has been operating for more than half a year. He conducted a detailed analysis of Yiwu residents' economic income and demographic structure beforehand. Reality proved that many parents in Yiwu gradually accepted this educational philosophy. Currently, 260 children have become members of his early learning center, with courses priced around 8,000 yuan being the most popular, and the business is thriving.

"Although the current economic environment isn't great, I believe that identifying market prospects accurately still makes it worthwhile to invest, just like early childhood education. I feel its development prospects are excellent, worthy of investment," Jin Liliang said.

Of course, similar to mass consumption and education industries, the investment amounts are not small, and the risks and returns are relatively high. With accurate and unique insights, one can become a successful investor in "turbulent times."

Rich people drive domestic demand through investment.

In fact, investment and stimulating domestic demand are interrelated, as evident from the series of plans launched by the State Council to stimulate domestic investment and demand.

Therefore, in an economic environment that isn't optimistic, investors at all levels are still carefully seeking investment directions. Because investment costs are relatively reduced at this time, and they can create jobs and stimulate domestic demand, which is beneficial to society.

Under infrastructure construction, foreign countries and the U.S. are similar in this regard. According to the current macroeconomic situation and railway development plans, China plans to complete infrastructure investment of 600 billion yuan in the latter half of 2009. Based on quota calculations, achieving this scale of investment requires 20 million tons of steel and 120 million tons of cement, creating 6 million job opportunities. Accelerating small-scale railway construction will significantly promote investment and external demand.

Given the current economic situation, the central government's decision to increase investment and expand domestic demand is uniquely Chinese. China has such a vast provincial market, and increasing investment and expanding domestic demand complement each other, serving as an excellent solution to the financial crisis.

But increasing investment doesn't mean saving all enterprises. Chengdu Branch President of the Construction Bank of Hubei Province, Gong Shuzi, believes that companies need to develop to a certain stage, undergo fluctuations to preserve good enterprises, and eliminate those that don't keep up with historical trends. Just like eliminating small cotton mills nationwide years ago because their production levels couldn't meet demands, eliminating small nitrogen fertilizer plants follows the same logic. Scientific development means eliminating high-energy-consuming ones that should be phased out, benefiting the country and the people.

The state increases investment and distributes cash to common people for consumption to stimulate domestic demand. Thus, for investors, putting all money into banks during a financial crisis is indeed a safer way to avoid risks, but to ensure personal asset preservation or appreciation, finding the right investment channel to increase wealth quality can also stimulate domestic demand and create jobs. Of course, this doesn't represent blind or impulsive investment.

In today's global economy, amidst frequent bad news from the U.S., helping foreign consumers and investors regain confidence and enhance their consumption capacity and investment willingness is critical. The key to stimulating domestic demand lies in boosting consumption and investment, with household consumption being the most efficient and cost-effective GDP engine.

Economist Fan Gang, a member of the Monetary Policy Committee of the Central Bank, stated that expanding domestic demand mainly involves investment and consumption. Initiating consumption is difficult to achieve quickly, and the main channel for promoting domestic demand is investment.

Facing the economic situation in 2009, rich people don't need to hold onto their wallets too tightly. Before investing, they should conduct a risk tolerance level test and fully understand themselves. Then, based on their risk tolerance, construct a financial portfolio for 2009. However, this has a prerequisite, which is confidence.

"The current notion of 'investors surviving the winter' is questionable. Whether this 'winter' truly exists is debatable," said Chi Qinhui, Director of the Institute of Finance at the Development Research Center of the State Council. Looking at the overall condition of domestic enterprises and listed companies, the disclosed semi-annual reports indicate that the performance of listed companies is still acceptable. The Chinese stock market will undoubtedly remain positive in the long term. The biggest support for financial markets is investor confidence, and the current major issue facing the stock market is a lack of confidence.

There is another view suggesting that the influence of the U.S. subprime mortgage crisis is gradually fading, and thus its economic situation may improve. If various factors gradually clarify, the U.S. subprime mortgage crisis peaks, and its economy turns upward, the dollar strengthens genuinely, and the surrounding market conditions improve, market confidence will be restored.

In the context of an economic crisis, blind pessimism is not advisable. A crisis consists of both "danger" and "opportunity," akin to the two sides of a coin. Investment opportunities are always present; the key lies in vision and courage. Relying on investment to boost domestic demand is not only a practical measure for the situation but also a patriotic action.
