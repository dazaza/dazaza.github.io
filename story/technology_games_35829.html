On November 20, 2007 at 2:00 PM, the new extreme racing online game "Roller Wind" (Xplay), exclusively operated by China Games Center www.chinagames.net, officially began its closed beta test. Simultaneously, the beta testing reward event was also launched.

Beta Testing Launch Time

November 20, 2007, 2:00 PM

Gold 200 Beta Test Codes - Be the first to experience! Each ID card is limited to applying for one beta test code. The account verification with Zhongyou will be required. There are only 199 competitors, and a laptop is within reach!

Content of the Beta Test Event

I. Jump Combo Evaluation

Inline skates, where gravity is your only enemy! Accelerate fully, jump freely!

Event Rules:

1. Players will upload screenshots of COMBOs from the game to the event page and compete with all players for the highest COMBO count.

2. The top 20 players in terms of COMBO count will receive rewards.

3. The images submitted by players must be original screenshots from the game. Any images with modified COMBO counts will result in disqualification from the event.

4. Players can review the works participating in the event. If cheating images are discovered, they can report them to us and receive our reporting reward.

Event Rewards:

A set of Level 10 equipment during the open beta test, plus 50 game coins.

II. Bug Submission

Collaboratively create a top-tier game; treasures are no longer miracles!

Every new game has some bugs, and timely discovery and submission to the official team are important measures for the healthy development of the game. Official receipt of feedback and prompt bug fixes are crucial demonstrations of responsibility to the players.

Event Rules:

1. During the beta test period, based on your experience with Xplay Roller Wind, propose opinions or suggestions about the game. You will have the chance to share in 3.6 million game treasures!

2. During the beta test period, players who discover inherent bugs in the game will be heavily rewarded by Zhongyou for their assistance to the game.

Prize Settings:

1. Outstanding Opinion and Suggestion Award: A full set of Level 10 equipment during the open beta test, plus 50 game coins.

2. Bug Discovery Award: One piece of rare attribute equipment during the open beta test, plus 100 game coins.

III. Leave Comments and Win Prizes

Gold 200 Beta Test Codes - Did you get yours? Don't feel discouraged if you didn't. Even without entering the game, there are surprises waiting for you! The official team will randomly select some lucky players from all those who upload comments. These players will have the opportunity to receive exquisite gifts from the official team. Still unclear about the event rules? Read on.

Event Rules:

1. The last digit of the player's nickname upload number must match the lucky number of the day.

2. The nickname uploaded by the player must include the keyword of the day, which will be released in riddle form.

If a player's comment meets both of these conditions simultaneously, you are the lucky player we're looking for.

To encourage players to use more creative nicknames and leave the most creative comments, the official team has also specially established the Most Creative Nickname Award and the Best Creativity Grand Prize. The Most Creative Nickname Award will evaluate the funniest and most meaningful nicknames based on the nicknames uploaded by players. The Best Creativity Award will evaluate the most constructive comments based on the players' comments.

Prize Settings:

Wind of Luck Award: One grand prize every day, with surprises every day.

Lucky Award: 100 winners. Each winner receives a set of non-sellable Roller Wind equipment.

Most Creative Nickname Award: 3 winners. Each winner receives a non-sellable ashtray from Roller Wind (Zhongyou).

Best Creative Comment Award: 3 winners. Each winner receives a pair of rare Roller Wind skates and a non-sellable Roller Wind T-shirt.

For more information, please follow the official website of Zhongyou's "Roller Wind".
