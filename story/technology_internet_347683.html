If there were no prior statements, readers would never believe that today's market crash is a fake one. Even though there was a warning yesterday to understand that the market often feints, it is still difficult to hold stocks with confidence. The real estate suppression policies have come with unusual speed, especially the sudden increase of the down payment ratio for second homes to 50%, and the loan interest rate to more than 1.1 times the preferential rate. For multiple homes, there needs to be a significant increase, which is a substantial strong medicine. The rebound of real estate stocks after probing the bottom is still just a counterattack in the short term. We need to wait for the policy effects. If there is no significant drop in volume and price in the property market, then the mid-term market trend can be expected. The financial and real estate sectors are closely related. In this case, the spring for heavy-weight stocks is far from coming. What reason is there to be bullish on the mid-term market trend?

At the beginning of the year, there was a prediction that this year would indeed be the most complicated one! First, let's talk about the technical aspects. The trading volume has suddenly dropped to the billion level, shrinking by nearly half compared to three days ago. Such a reduction in volume is enough to support the 20-day moving average of the Shanghai Stock Exchange and the ascending trend line of the Shenzhen Stock Exchange. All contracts of the CSI 300 Index Futures are rising across the board, with larger increases as we go further into the future. This shows the optimistic judgment of the market on the mid-term trend. Valuation levels and earnings growth are the most important basis. The SSE Composite Index at 3100 points and the CSI 300 Index at 3300 points are absolutely not high. The economy achieved an ideal start of high growth and low inflation in the first quarter. Many first and second-tier blue-chip stocks reported pre-increases in the first quarter. There's no reason to short sell. Just look at the U.S. stock market, where small positive lines keep setting new highs. Economically, it cannot be compared with China.

Taking advantage of the launch timing of the stock index futures, people don't know what's going on, so scaring them is possible. The means of scaring them is talking about policy suppression of the real estate sector. However, real estate developers have already locked in high growth for the next two years. The property market in the first quarter saw price increases and record-breaking sales. Even if the policies are fierce, they will at most affect 2012. With performance at eight thousand or ten thousand points and stock prices at two or three thousand points, how much room is there left for suppression!

In contrast, small-cap newly listed stocks are making waves again, getting a breather. Unfortunately, they have become yesterday's news. I advise you not to chase them! The purpose or function of the introduction of stock index futures is clear: to smooth out fluctuations, make the market more mature, accelerate the establishment of investment concepts, and promote the rational allocation of resources. How could it possibly further widen the polarization between the two extremes! With the basic judgment made, one can completely remain unaffected by any changes.
