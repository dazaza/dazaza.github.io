Stone crystal surface treatment can be simply explained as: the hardening and polishing treatment of stones, which is a chemical treatment that changes the molecular structure of the marble surface. The main principle of stone crystal surface treatment is to use crystal surface care machinery (weighted machine or crystallizer) together with fiber pads (white pads), steel wool, crystal surface care liquid (or crystal powder, crystal paste). When these are dry-ground against the stone surface, heat is generated. Under the action of heat and acidifying agents, calcium ions precipitated from the marble recombine with fluorosilicate to form new fluorosilicate (a concrete strengthening agent) on the surface. At the same time, the high-gloss resin in the crystal surface care agent combines with it to form a thin layer of extremely hard (scratch-resistant, wear-resistant) and highly glossy (with excellent观赏性) crystal layer.

The role of stone crystal surface treatment is to restore the brightness of the stone surface, increase its hardness, and the effect lasts for a long time.
