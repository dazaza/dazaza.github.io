The rapid development of modern science and technology has not only promoted economic growth in society but also brought a strong impact on people's thinking and social management systems, including police work. As the level of public security monitoring systems deepens gradually, prisons are becoming more open to the public, accepting social supervision. Monitoring has become a focal point in achieving scientific policing, information efficiency, and technological advancement, opening up new management models.

Monitoring technology plays a crucial role in preventing Guangdong Public Security issues, integrating a security system with daily management to form a comprehensive management system. To achieve prison administration modernization and ensure safety, after twenty years of construction, Guangdong's public security monitoring technology prevention system has evolved from a simple analog system into an intelligent, digital one. Currently, the prison guard system meets basic requirements set by public security departments based on continuous integration of information and network technologies, providing efficient, fast work environments within the prison. Advanced digital and networked devices are introduced, strengthening prison security to ensure safety while establishing a powerful platform for integrated management of various functional systems and intelligent control equipment. Efforts are being made to develop prison products that promote humane and standardized management, advancing the modernization and standardization of prison administration.

### Building Goals for Monitoring Technology Prevention System

The public security monitoring technology prevention system is a multi-disciplinary, multi-functional technical protection management system. Its construction should be seamlessly integrated with police supervision and information system development, closely linking intelligent network monitoring and developing a control system with new prevention technologies.

Guangzhou access service proposes: In general construction, the public security monitoring technology prevention system should establish a versatile and intelligent command center for rapid response, forming an effective and efficient monitoring network for both law enforcement and daily supervision, as well as a three-level networking technology prevention management system at provincial, city, and county levels to achieve modern and scientific public security supervision and management.

Prevention and management technical functions should include information collection, transmission, monitoring, recording, management, integration, and realization of various management functions:

- **Modern Management**: Utilizing advanced networking, digital technology, and equipment to implement comprehensive, all-weather monitoring and management, improving the level of modern management.

- **Intelligent Interaction and Management**: Controlling, quickly and efficiently monitoring and managing all activities in monitored areas, detecting and stopping all kinds of accidents, ensuring prison security and smooth law enforcement work.

- **Human Management**: Using effective technological management tools to achieve human management, protecting the legitimate rights and interests under surveillance, implementing and enforcing norms, and establishing an open, fair, and equitable law enforcement management image.

- **Network Management**: Using technical equipment and workflow management tools, setting duty positions responsibilities and work procedures, dynamically monitoring the disposal of alarm events, jobs, and conducting statistical analysis and online evaluation, online monitoring.

Through seamless links between information management systems and supervision through prevention information systems, the prison administration ensures information is fast and smooth, allowing prison leaders and higher authorities to monitor real-time work, achieving efficient and safe prison management and supervision of law enforcement.

### New Ideas for Building Prison Guard Systems

In recent years, with the rapid development of information technology and network technology, video image monitoring has been extended from mere surveillance to law enforcement management and security management, integrating into the daily work of prison management, evaluation, and technical prevention. Technical prevention has become an indispensable tool for prison administration. Guangdong Province's construction supervisor must adapt to the transformation of working mechanisms, standardization, and management needs, boldly innovating management methods while closely monitoring law enforcement and daily management to improve its level of law enforcement management. I propose the following eight aspects for the improvement and development of the prevention system and the establishment of a new type of prison guard system.

#### Modulus Combination of Digital Video and Audio Monitoring System

The video and audio surveillance system is the core of technical security and the focus of technical prevention system construction. Over the years, Guangdong Province's prison guard system has been using analog control systems, but their defects cannot meet today’s information era needs, necessitating digitization.

Currently, there is an increasing demand for high-definition monitors. Transitioning from analog to digital high-definition is the inevitable trend for video image monitoring, though technical and market challenges remain to achieve true high-definition digital monitoring. Due to financial constraints and digital technology and equipment limitations, it is suitable for public security monitoring systems to combine modulus ways of building new guard systems.

Guangdong Public Security regulators are trying to combine front-end analog cameras with video encoders to form means of video and audio information acquisition, back-end digital matrices, centralized storage, and intelligent integrated management platforms controlling alarms, access control, patrols, and perimeter control systems. Shifting from analog technology to module-based prevention systems is a significant direction for Guangdong Province's prevention system construction.

#### Integrated Service Management System

To adapt to gradually integrated services, accept social supervision and inspection needs, a comprehensive law enforcement supervision window can be established. This integrates existing family member (lawyer) visits, receiving visitors, and prisoner releases into an integrated service management system focusing on law enforcement openness, fairness, transparency, benefiting societal supervision, and establishing a good law enforcement image.

In the General Services Office, large-screen displays can be set up as external reception and publicity windows. Large screens display relevant laws, regulations, notices, meeting notices, family-related information, money transfers, material deliveries, and other relevant data. The player image data can be used for publicity and education.

Through the monitoring information system, family (or lawyer) meetings can be unified and managed, extending meeting rooms and providing assistance intercom buttons. Managers can set visiting hours and overtime prompts to manage calls effectively.

The monitoring center and the total multimedia control center can communicate with families, lawyers, and meeting rooms via calls linked with the video surveillance system.

#### Access Attendance System Inspection

Nowadays, electronic business and network management have been gradually promoted in prisons. By combining LAN monitoring with access control, inspection times, attendance, work shifts, duty tours, and shift changes can be uniformly managed. At each entrance, 1-2 cameras can be installed to monitor credit card door-opening situations, capturing video and images that can be compared with cardholder pictures before entry. If conditions are not met, an alarm signal will be triggered.

All access points, visit channels, attendance, online checking, and job evaluations can be conducted online. The time and attendance system, combined with personal identification, location identification, timeout alarms, automatic monitoring and recording, and auto sound and light alarms, offers robust functionality. The system software automatically generates various comprehensive management reports for each reader, door-opening, and alarm event, serving as inspection and attendance certificates. Queries can be made based on time, specific doors (card readers), or specific cardholders, with calls and statistical summaries.

#### Smart Perimeter Guard System

Using existing high-voltage grids, infrared detectors, and other monitoring equipment, a smart perimeter protection system can be formed by introducing intelligent video technology analysis and high-voltage power systems, linked to alarm systems to achieve restricted area perimeter intrusion alarms, statistics, and trace analysis for intelligent management features.

High-resolution cameras can be installed around the prison walls, setting virtual border fences in the intelligent video analysis system to provide real-time dynamic monitoring of warning areas. When illegal entries occur, the system triggers an alarm box indicating the alarm location and direction, prompting on-duty personnel to take measures to stop further intrusions.

#### Intelligent Coordinated Control System

This is a large-scale linkage intelligent control system across the entire prevention system, featuring strong linkage functions interfaced with electronic maps. Subsystems like reporting, access visits and attendance, emergency alarms, perimeter detection, prevention of prohibited items or alarms operate with corresponding video cameras, audio, lighting, etc., to implement linkage, completing actions such as displaying images, audible alarms, turning on/off lights, and AB door controls.

During operation, the system displays corresponding video surveillance footage, automatically captures, records, and highlights irregularities in the total control room with light signals.

In case of access control or emergency alarms, the system automatically follows, displays corresponding video surveillance footage, sets conditions for monitoring alarms, lights, radios, access control, and lighting subsystems. The AB door access control system enters shutdown mode until all alarms are lifted, then returns to normal operating mode.

#### Versatile Multimedia Control Center

Since most prisons are located in suburban areas far from bureaus, establishing a remote video conferencing system in prisons is essential. In the prison multimedia conference room, the technical protection system integrates video conferencing, video surveillance, prison situation analysis, multimedia presentations, forming a multifunctional multimedia control center, one of the basic subsystems of the guard system.

Multimedia conference rooms in prisons are equipped with large-screen displays, computers (including signal switching), network interfaces, and other audio equipment, offering multi-media conferences, technical protection system control center functions. Meanwhile, the total control center provides real-time monitoring, command scheduling, and remote management functions; it can access the public security information network, manage various types of dynamic information, police patrol attendance statistics, and daily management situations; and broadcast live meetings and video conferences.

#### Intelligent Device Management System

Efficient, fast management of technology equipment and information technology equipment, anti-comprehensive monitoring, and management are necessary requirements and also a direction of development for the intelligent guard system.

The intelligent device management system collects, transmits, displays, stores information from video and audio monitoring systems, manages various functional subsystems' equipment, and monitors the running status of computers, network equipment, and information management systems in real-time. When abnormal situations occur, such as video loss, disk failure, video stops, link failure, device offline, or human destruction, the system can implement monitoring, automatic recording, alarms, and achieve intelligent device management.

#### Intelligent Integrated Management Platform

The guard system is the core of the integrated management of prisons and prevention systems, serving as a centralized management and control platform for subsystems. It requires a powerful integrated platform. Currently, Guangdong Public Security monitoring technology prevention system's intelligent integrated management platform construction is still in its initial stage, lacking excellent implementation cases.

The intelligent integrated management platform should be based on electronic maps as the operating platform, linking subsystems such as video and audio monitoring, reporting intercoms, access control inspection attendance, perimeter guards, emergency alarms, integrated service management, and educational systems. It should centrally manage and monitor various types of alarm events, interact with each other, and track, statistically analyze, and network-supervise various equipment operations and alarm information, centralized information visit attendance management.

The intelligent integrated platform manages major power equipment, radio and television programs' automatic playback time and content, and according to police radio linkage conditions, automatically opens emergency broadcasting.

The intelligent integrated management platform can link and manage each subsystem of the prevention system, ensuring the security and stable operation of the guard system.

#### Accelerate Network Construction, Develop Remote Monitoring

With the combination of computer image processing technology and security technologies, a new generation of digital processing, network transfer as core technology characterized by network, high-definition, intelligent security is rapidly developing. Digital surveillance and remote monitoring have become important directions for security system development. The public security system monitoring technology prevention building should seize this direction, accelerating the pace of network construction and developing remote monitoring technology.

In 2008, Guangdong Provincial Public Security's monitoring system technology included audio-video surveillance systems in the provincial public security video surveillance system, accessing the provincial public security classification step-by-step video special network, establishing three video surveillance system control centers at provincial, municipal, and county (district) levels to achieve all-round monitoring, command, scheduling, and dynamic management.

Accelerating the establishment of the provincial public security department's video surveillance system monitoring control center is a priority. Currently, Guangdong Public Security monitoring sites have established local area networks and connected with local city and county public security bureaus through fiber-optic networks, achieving control and supervision issues in prison administration work, timely commanding and handling emergencies, maintaining social stability.

Guangdong Province's construction supervisor of the technical protection system is in a critical period of upgrading to digital and networked systems. The Pearl River Delta region has seized the opportunity, with some prisons exploring valuable innovations in intelligent building technology defense systems, such as using digital monitoring, centralized storage, and remote monitoring technology to improve image quality and achieve rapid queries. Combining electronic technology and information systems in prisons enhances daily management. Some judicial investigators have developed indoor self-screen monitoring products, with HD capture, access, and high-definition display technology continuing to mature, achieving cross-regional, inter-provincial city high-speed backbone networks and high-speed local area networks gradually becoming a reality. I believe that the full realization of network, high-definition, and intelligent technology prevention systems will soon come.
