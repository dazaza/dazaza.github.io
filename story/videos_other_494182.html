Hong Kong drama "Uninvited Appointment" Episode 4: The Devil Appears, Scaring Dongjian

One night during Dongjian's hospital stay, an incredible event occurred! The "devil" that he had encountered at the brink of life and death appeared again, leaving behind an apple...

Three months later, a male patient named Taifeng sought treatment, and Dongjian discovered that he had no heartbeat. This person's eyes and the apple in his hand reminded Dongjian of the scene when he had pleaded with the "devil" for revival. Taifeng set conditions for Dongjian to remain in the world, constantly monitoring his character and preparing to reclaim his life at any time.

Dongjian accompanied his mother Jin Huaxi shopping in the market, discussing the matter of Huimei referring patients. Dongjian expressed that he didn't care about it, only valuing his mother's feelings. His father Jiang Zhida came to have dinner with Huaxi, refusing to comply with her wishes, which made Dongjian lose his temper.

Broadcast date of Hong Kong drama "Uninvited Appointment": September 12, 2011 (Monday)

Hong Kong drama "Uninvited Appointment" Episode 2: Jing Tian Invites Dongjian Again

Dongjian complained about Zhida's past affairs, letting Chuqiao use a legal marriage certificate, causing Huaxi in the countryside to endure silently for many years. Zhida had no rebuttal, blaming Huaxi for failing to educate their son properly while inquiring about Dongjian's clinic business. Huaxi voluntarily handed over all her household expenses to satisfy Zhida. She defended Zhida, mentioning how he earned big money from the stock market and subsequently purchased two shops, hoping her son would let go.

Not wanting to be troubled by family matters, Dongjian decided to move back to the lab temporarily to develop new drugs. Pharmacologist Wen Jialing was glad to see Dongjian return and immediately shared the good news of receiving support from a pharmaceutical company. However, upon visiting the company, they found it had just been acquired by "Hongzhi Pharmaceutical." Just as Dongjian and Jialing were about to leave, they unexpectedly met Tang Jingtian, the head of "Hongzhi."

Hong Kong drama "Uninvited Appointment" Episode 1: Huimei Secretly Refers Patients

Doctor Jiang Dongjian carefully explained the drug effects to the patients. Nurse and half-sister Jiang Huimei reminded him that his consultation time was tight and secretly referred patients to the neighboring traditional Chinese medicine clinic. As Huimei and Dr. Song Chuqiao are biological mother and daughter, patients received priority treatment; Dongjian had already seen through Huimei's scheme and proactively went to the TCM clinic to hand over the patient's medical records for Chuqiao's reference, only to be teased by her.

Jingtian highly supported Dongjian and Jialing's professionalism and invited them to collaborate back at the pharmaceutical company. Unfortunately, Dongjian strongly disliked Jingtian's profit-driven nature and refused his invitation on the spot due to not wanting more patients to suffer. Conversely, he held great hope for the development of new drugs for a female patient named Fang Xiaofang three years ago.

Dongjian became distracted while driving, suspecting he had encountered Xiaofang again, leading to a serious traffic accident. Memories suddenly surfaced of his collaboration with Jingtian in developing drugs, which ended due to unforeseen variables, indirectly causing the death of a patient. He also remembered meeting Xiaofang three years ago...

When intern nurse Xiaofang announced Dongjian's death to Huaxi and others, she suddenly heard screams from the operating room. Everyone rushed back and were shocked to see Dongjian sitting up as if nothing happened, passionately confessing his love to Xiaofang.
