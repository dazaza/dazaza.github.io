by zxyasdn20 on 2011-07-27 16:01:37

Sophomore leads the creation of a guide to campus周边 dining for freshmen, evoking nostalgia in upperclassmen

Beijing Daily News (Intern reporter Xu Chao) - "At the beginning, human nature is good. Not eating noodles, what a pity." Don't nitpick this "Three-Character Classic," it's a "Delicious Food Three-Character Classic" created by students at Beijing University of Chemical Technology's Changping campus. It has not only been embraced by freshmen but also stirred up nostalgia among upperclassmen.

The creator of this "Delicious Food Three-Character Classic" is Miao Jiansong, a student from Beijing University of Chemical Technology's class of 2009.

After school started, Xiao Miao and a few roommates met up at their usual noodle shop outside the school, named "At the Beginning," for a reunion dinner.

"At the beginning, human nature is good..." One roommate stared at the shop sign and blurted out a line, which sparked a lively discussion. By the end of the meal, a "Delicious Food Three-Character Classic" was born.

That night, they posted their self-created "Three-Character Classic" online, attracting over 800 views and more than 50 shares.

"Too talented!" said a freshman, who mentioned that his class was just planning a group dinner, and the "Delicious Food Three-Character Classic" summarized by the seniors was like a guiding light for them.

"This Three-Character Classic really reminds me of those university days," said Zhao Zhihua, who just graduated this year. He added that such an innovative freshman guide was the first he had ever seen.

He still remembers that a large serving of fried pork loin mixed noodles at the At the Beginning Noodle Shop cost only 8 yuan and came in ample portions, rarely could anyone finish it, making it the most cost-effective option among these shops.

It was learned that around Beijing University of Chemical Technology, there are also the Dongguan Southli Community, Petroleum University, and Law and Political Science University (Changping Campus), housing thousands of people.

A staff member from the Western Pastry Shop mentioned in the "Delicious Food Three-Character Classic" as having poor cost-performance said that their main target customers are student couples with an average consumption of about 50 yuan per person, so students may find it unaffordable.

The self-created Delicious Food Three-Character Classic by Beijing University of Chemical Technology students:

At the Beginning, human nature is good; not eating noodles, what a pity. Pot noodles come with free eggs; small yellow fish for six and a half yuan. Go to Panji to eat baked eggs; Laoganma can offer extra rice. Qinxiang Garden, Western Pastry Shop; poor cost-performance. Want to lose weight? Go to the porridge shop; a bowl of porridge equals a bowl of rice. Want some meat? Go to the meat pancake shop; dumpling soup weighs two and a half jin. The dumpling restaurant's fillings are hard to spot; the taste is strange and the dishes come slowly. No fear of distance, go to the rice skin shop; Roujiamo tastes fresh and fragrant. For a small gathering, go to the hotpot fish shop; few dishes but unlimited fish. Xia Asian supermarket offers full bowl meals; Hao Shi holds up the sky. Water radish, big restaurant; delicious dishes at affordable prices. Qinglu Garden, grand hotel; one meal costs tens of thousands.
