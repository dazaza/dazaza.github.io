A fool once gave me the best love.

by lili1 on 2012-02-05 11:42:10

There was a fool who once gave me the best love.

The morning in October was already quite cool, and I would feel a cold breeze on my shins while jogging. I hid my shoulders under the quilt and refused to get up. Xu Xu forcibly pulled me out and accompanied her to jog slowly on the playground. I complained quietly, "I hate it. Why do I have to accompany you to lose weight? I'm not fat!"

Xu Xu agreed, saying, "Ruan Qiqi, if you keep eating and sleeping, you will become a big pig! I pull you out of bed because I don't want to see you abuse yourself!"

Thinking about becoming a pig is indeed a bit scary, I slowly walked to the podium and introduced myself, "Hello everyone, I am Ruan Qiqi, I weigh 80 kilograms!" Then my little belly was cruelly exposed outside the clothes, almost squeezed open. I thought for a moment, shook my head, and secretly made a determination to defend youth and maintain my figure by running all the way.

Suddenly, a very fat man came out from the bushes, holding a bunch of blue and white daisies. Those flowers I was familiar with were exactly the daisies blooming along the road outside the school, but I really couldn't remember where I had seen this person?

He chuckled, "My name is He Chuan, we met at the end of last month in the canteen."

Xu Xu remembered a second before me, "Oh, I remember now, you are Su Lian's high school classmate, what brings you here?"

As Xu Xu said this, a smile appeared on her face, and that look made me want to rush up and beat her up.

But I still couldn't remember, how could such an elegant beauty like Su Lian know such a low-level guy? But why did this fat guy come here? He should weigh over 200 pounds, I thought secretly.

Suddenly, Xu Xu grinned strangely and whispered in my ear, "Qiqi, he seems to be here for you?" Sure enough, as Xu Xu finished speaking, the fat guy had already brought the flowers to my face. He seemed not to know what to say, just smiled foolishly like a steamed bun, his eyes squinted into slits, and his ear roots were so red.

What nonsense! I was extremely annoyed. Which girl would want her admirer to be a steamed bun? In a prestigious university, any guy picked at random would be a handsome man over 70 points. Looking at He Chuan, my expression suddenly changed. People exercising on the playground had already looked over here, and some troublemakers whistled. I glared at He Chuan fiercely, considering his kind of admiration as an insult.

I didn't say a word and walked away from him in large strides. On the contrary, Xu Xu, with great interest, started chatting with him: "Hey, boy, your eyesight is pretty good. However, there are so many guys chasing after our Qiqi, and no one has succeeded yet. It should be quite challenging. You need to work hard..."

I really wanted to use super glue to seal Xu Xu's mouth tightly. This world is really disheartening, and situations I want to avoid happen anyway.

One day during lunch break, someone downstairs called my name. It was definitely He Chuan, the big steamed bun. He was really patient, coming to our class every day for a whole week. I guessed that the daisies by the roadside were almost all picked by him. Maybe after they were all gone, he would give up!

I jumped out of bed, went to the window, wanting to completely shut out his annoying voice. Xu Xu said, "I seem to remember Su Su saying that He Chuan has a very strong sense of self-respect, the kind that can't bear injury." In these few seconds, I changed the direction of the force applied by my hand on the windowsill. I opened the window, leaned out, and smiled at him, saying, "I like Xu Xu. You know, we are always together and sleep together!"

Xu Xu behind me heard this and pulled me, saying, "Dead Qiqi, I'm not a lesbian. Don't ruin my reputation!"

Before autumn was over, rumors about Ruan Qiqi being in love spread. I was truly wronged. I never said I was dating He Chuan. I just stopped looking down on or yelling at him, and I also stopped throwing the daisies on the ground in front of him.

Speaking of which, He Chuan wasn't that bad either. He was just a bit overweight and a bit quiet, but he was particularly good to me and my sisters. This fat guy delivered breakfast to our class every morning on time. He Chuan stammered, "Ruan Qiqi, you shouldn't lose weight. Your figure is good like this?" His nagging was just like my mother. Then he handed me hot soy milk and buns. I took the soy milk and told him, "You keep this for yourself!" and turned around to walk into the classroom. Since seeing him, I haven't eaten buns anymore. I wonder if it's due to some kind of fear.

During a professional class, the teacher was writing furiously on the podium. The damn Yang Yang supported his head and looked at me. I raised my hand and waved, indicating that I would hit him if he kept looking. Suddenly, the classroom became noisy. I looked up towards the window and saw He Chuan, the steamed bun, standing at the door of the classroom. Yang Yang led the laughter, laughing exaggeratedly while hitting the table and laughing, "Wife, you've come to send daisies?" Because of his mouth opening and closing, a piece of white chalk was shot into his mouth by the teacher. First, the whole class was shocked, then burst out laughing. I glared at him, and he awkwardly didn't know whether to laugh or sit down. At this moment, I smiled at him, "Idiot, you deserve it!"

Seeing me smile at him, he was greatly honored and swallowed his saliva. It seemed that he had swallowed the piece of chalk. No wonder the ancients said, "It's hardest to accept the kindness of a beautiful woman..." I shook my head.

He Chuan was still standing at the door. I raised my hand and signaled the teacher to go out for a moment. Then I ran outside, "Qiqi, you check for a long time every day in the discipline department. How about I ask my friend to help you get into the arts department? There's nothing much to do there?" That fat guy said to me.

"Did you come to find me just for this matter?" He nodded. "Do you know that you have wasted a lot of my time, and I am still in class? Moreover, I am doing well in the discipline department, I don't need to change. Thank you, please don't make such an entrance next time, my heart can't take it. Goodbye, no escort needed!" I walked back into the classroom, leaving him alone for 5 seconds, then he silently left.

"Hey, wife, what did he tell you?" Yang Yang, who seemed to swallow the piece of chalk wasn't enough, whispered to me.

"Yang Yang, stop making noise, we're having class. Let's talk after class." Xu Xu stepped forward to smooth things out for me, and he lay on the desk, annoyed.

On a weekend, He Chuan rushed excitedly to find us, "Ruan Qiqi, can you come down?" I had headphones in my ears, nodding my head and listening to music. "Qiqi, He Chuan is looking for you, go down and see him?" Xu Xu shook me and said.

"I'm not going, are you annoying? He's not idle, why does he keep coming to find me?" I turned around and ignored him.

"Come on, come on, otherwise, we'll disturb other dormitories, and they will complain. Come, I'll go with you." Pulled by Xu Xu, I unwillingly followed her downstairs.

"What's the matter? Don't you need to take a nap?" I took off one headphone and said to him. Suddenly, he pulled out a box from his bag. Xu Xu took it from me and opened it. It was a ring, a small platinum ring, with the label still not torn off, priced at 2460 RMB. I finally couldn't help asking, "He Chuan, are you annoying? I don't like your type of guys!" I felt it wasn't enough, so I grabbed the ring from Xu Xu's hand and threw it at his face. The ring rolled to the other side beside his feet. Suddenly, I felt a bit guilty, but I still didn't say anything, gritted my teeth, and turned back into the dormitory.

In fact, I have always wanted to explain clearly to He Chuan, telling him that I cannot be with him, but I just couldn't find a proper excuse. Perhaps I am kind-hearted, fearing to hurt his innocence and confidence.

In front of the dormitory window, I saw He Chuan standing there like a wooden chicken without moving. Xu Xu picked up the ring for him and placed it in his hand. I don't know if there were tears on his chubby face. After Xu Xu came back, she remained silent.

Xu Xu was the first friend I met after coming to Shanghai. She stuck to me, telling me everything, and she knew me the most. But at this moment, she coldly said word by word, "Ruan Qiqi, one day you will regret it." I shivered, but soon covered myself with the quilt and fell asleep deeply. In my dream, I saw myself flying to the suburbs... I was fearless, flying high, shining like stars in the night.

After celebrating with my roommates for the end of our single subject exam, on the way back after crazy playing, Yang Yang smiled at me and said, "Ruan Qiqi, I really can't underestimate you. I found myself increasingly admiring you. You need to take responsibility for me!"

I swung my hand and hit his head, "Looking for death, don't flatter me too much!"

"Oh, wife, you're a bad kid, violent tendency. If you cripple me, you have to support me for life!" Yang Yang laughed strangely and jumped to the side.

"Yes, yes, yes, if you cripple someone, people will care, but not me. Don't worry, I will show great mercy and send you to the psychiatric hospital to cultivate your nature!" We laughed all the way back to school.

"Look, that guy is like a brown bear." Following Yang Yang's finger, I saw the lonely figure of He Chuan in gray-white clothes disappear from our sight.

Love really makes people deaf and blind. Only when you walk far away do you realize that a fool's love was once precious. But when you look back, everything cannot return to the starting point.

I don't know how long it will take before the person I like realizes that there was also a fool who gave all his love to him...
