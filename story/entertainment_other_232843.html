QQ Zone emotional diary: Love is a burden when you give up.

by lwf225 on 2009-09-03 18:17:41

Tags: QQ Zone Diary, Classic QQ Zone Diary, Touching QQ Zone Diary. When you no longer love me, when loving me has become your burden, and when our love has turned into pain, then I choose to let go.

I give up on you because of my love for you:

Because I love you, I don't want to see you unhappy;

Because I love you, I don't want to see you endure inner struggles;

Because I love you, I don't want to see your forced smiles;

Because I love you, I'm willing to set you free.

When love has become a thing of the past, why bother chasing it? Forcing won't bring happiness, it can only widen the cracks between us and deepen our pain. If you really want to leave, I have nothing to say but let you go.

I once thought that you were a kite and I held the string in my hand, no matter where you fly, I would always be your final destination. Now I finally understand, loving you shouldn't mean binding you. The past is like the wind, let it go with the wind. When everything becomes empty, only memories remain with me. Time can dilute everything, including my heart that loves you.

I don't want you to see my tears because I fear that you might soften, but you wouldn't be happy. Because I love you, I won't use tears to force you to stay, so I let you go. Your heart has already gone far away, why should I keep your body here? Although I desire an eternal relationship, if it's just a luxury, I'd rather have had what we had. Having experienced your love is enough. Because there's a kind of love called letting go.

Giving up isn't selfless dedication. Letting go of you is not only out of love for you, but also a way of taking care of myself. It breaks my heart to let go of you, but I won't regret it. Making you disappear from my life is because "a long-term pain is worse than a short-term one". Even when I grow old and am near death, I will still have no regrets. Because I once loved you.

Because I love you, I hope you're happy. Someone said that there's no eternal love in this world. Between you and me, if even a brief love cannot exist, then it's better to let each other go. Loving you means letting you pursue your own happiness. As long as you're happy, I'll be happy too.

I care about everything about you. If you want to leave me, I won't blame you, I can only blame myself, blaming myself for loving you too much. Perhaps my excessive indulgence made you accustomed to calmness, perhaps my excessive leniency made you feel no responsibility, perhaps my excessive affection put heavy pressure on you, or perhaps the beautiful appearance of love made you lose direction. It's just that we have fate but no destiny.

When you want to leave, please don't concern yourself with me. Just tell me that you no longer love me, and you want to leave. I will definitely let you go without begging you to stay, even if I hear the sound of my heart breaking. When you leave, please don't look back. Looking back is a mistake, it's unfair to both of us. Once you've left, don't regret it anymore.

Because I love you, I should let you go. Because I know that there's a kind of love called giving up, and that's the deepest love for you.
