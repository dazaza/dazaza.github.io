The Nokia N8 is a multimedia smart fashion phone with a 12-megapixel camera that has been popular since its launch last year. Today, the Hong Kong version of this model is being offered by merchants at a new low price of 2699 yuan, making it a very reliable time to purchase.

The Nokia N8 is currently the smartphone with the highest pixel count in Nokia's lineup, featuring a 12-megapixel camera and a xenon flash typically found in professional digital cameras, providing strong photography performance. Moreover, the N8 uses the all-new Symbian^3 smart operating system, which is compatible with most Symbian S60 V5 platform software, offering powerful expansion capabilities.

The Nokia N8 features an aluminum unibody design, with extensive use of metal elements for an excellent grip feel. The front of the device sports a 3.5-inch AMOLED screen with a resolution of 360x640 pixels, delivering outstanding display effects in multimedia applications due to the OLED material. Equipped with a 680MHz ARM11 CPU, 512MB ROM, and 256MB RAM, the N8's specifications are average among high-end devices. The user interface of the N8 undoubtedly belongs to the Symbian^3 interface. The most noticeable improvement of Symbian^3 is the speed boost. One of the standout features of the Nokia N8 is its 12-megapixel Carl Zeiss certified lens, offering wide-angle vision, auto-focus, mechanical shutter functionality, and equipped with a xenon flash, providing extremely powerful photography capabilities. In terms of network support, the N8 not only supports WCDMA networks but also Wi-Fi functionality.
