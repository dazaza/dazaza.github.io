A touching story

The story begins like this:

Starting from the year 2063, one day, an old man went to the hospital and received a notice of liver cancer. The notice said that the old man had only one month left to live. He was very depressed, but after crying for a while, he let it go. After all, he was already 81 years old. Afterwards, the man tore up the notice, threw it away, leaned on his cane, and wobbled back to his home.

His wife was one year younger than him. A year ago, due to an accident, she became a vegetative state patient. Now, she could only rely on him feeding her water and food to sustain her life. Since their daughter was always busy, although the elderly couple's life was not short of money, it was quiet. Only the daily calls from her daughter became his mental support.

Thinking about his physical examination results, and looking at his bedridden partner, he choked up. After thinking for a long time, he finally made a decision: to end his own life.

He said: "Then what about you? My stinky? If I'm gone, who will be with you? Who will protect you?"

As he thought, his eyes reddened. "Dear, why don't we go together? Let's lean on each other as a couple."

Afterwards, he found a box of sleeping pills at home, poured all the tablets into a cup, waited until they dissolved into colorless liquid, then divided the water between himself and his partner...

The daughter called on time, but waiting for her was only the "beep... beep... beep..." sound of the phone ringing.

"Every time at this hour, dad would wait by the phone and immediately answer my call. What happened today?" She wondered, "Maybe dad went out to buy groceries and hasn't returned yet? No, although dad is old, he wouldn't forget the time to talk to me on the phone, would he...?" A sense of foreboding surged in her heart...

At the hospital, the doctors were doing everything they could to save these two elderly people. Because the old man kept holding his partner's hand tightly, in order to save time, the doctors had to use two stretchers side by side to push them.

In the emergency room, the doctors hooked up the respirator and carried out a series of rescue activities. In the emergency room, apart from the hurried footsteps of the medical staff, there was only the sound of the emergency equipment rubbing against each other. The daughter and family members cried nearby...

At this moment, the old man suddenly felt that his body was very light, and the liver which usually hurt him so much now felt unusually comfortable. He didn't know why he had such a relaxed experience, feeling as if he had returned to his youth...

The time was in 2001, it was a day in May. Back then, May had seven days off. One of his female friends invited him out to play. He asked who else would be there? His friend said: There's a girl, you also know her, she's my good friend. So he went, and met her.

They had known each other before, also meeting at school, but their acquaintance was limited to knowing each other's names.

Sometimes, women can change a lot. When she was in junior high school, she looked like a tomboy, but four years later, she had become elegant and curvaceous. And although he was still inexperienced, he was also dazzling.

It was through this coincidental meeting that they began a beautiful story... They strolled together in the snow, wandered together in the rain...

Just as the old man was about to touch his younger self, a bright flash of white light passed, and the scene changed to a room full of red. Looking up, it read "### and ### New Wedding Ceremony".

Accompanied by the dream wedding, he wore a neatly pressed suit, holding her in a pure white wedding dress, slowly walking down the red carpet. Under the witness of relatives and friends, they became the happiest couple. He heard himself say to her: "Stinky, from now on, no matter what happens, I will always be by your side to protect you." She replied to him: "Fatty, I am willing to accompany you until the end of our lives." After saying that, the happiest couple kissed each other.

The old man watched with joy, tears welling up in his eyes again. But who knew, that annoying white light flashed again. This time, when he opened his eyes, he saw himself arguing with her.

"I just like this piece of clothing, even though it's expensive, I just want to buy it," she said.

"How expensive! We've been married for four years. I've been saving every penny, wearing the same clothes for three years without changing them. You, on the other hand, want to change every few days. With this money, we could do something else, right? Besides, that piece of clothing isn't worth that much anyway," he said.

"I don't care. I just like it, and I just want to buy it," she pouted.

"Well, fine, you decide," he said angrily. Actually, he also felt bad, after all, they weren't short of money. They had a house, a car, and a substantial deposit. But maybe because he had developed the habit of frugal living since childhood, he said that she should decide, but in his heart, he was very unwilling.

The old man cursed inside: "You bastard, life can last for several decades, she married you, and didn't mind marrying you, and you treat her like this..." As he cursed, two streams of sorrowful tears flowed down his face...

When the old man wiped his eyes dry, he unknowingly came to the corridor outside the delivery room. His younger self was sitting on a bench outside the delivery room, waiting for news inside. At this moment, a clear baby cry sounded, and the man couldn't contain his excitement, jumping up and down. Not long after, the door to the delivery room opened, and a nurse came out holding a baby, telling him: "She gave birth, it's a girl." He glanced at the child, then turned around and asked: "How's the mother?" The nurse said: "Both mother and child are very healthy." Only then did he exhale deeply...

The old man looked at all this and wanted to reach out and touch the baby's forehead, because this was his own adorable daughter that he used to hold... "Doctor, please, can you use the best technology to save my parents? Please, I'll kneel down!" A hurried voice interrupted the old man, and upon closer inspection, it was his own daughter and son-in-law pleading desperately with the doctor.

The old man was confused, whose plea was his daughter making? Glancing over, he suddenly noticed himself lying in bed. How could he be lying in bed? Wasn't he standing here?

At this moment, with the sound of "drip", the electrocardiogram on the old man's body became a straight line. The doctors quickly performed cardiopulmonary resuscitation. Within a minute, a familiar yet long-lost voice beside the old man called out: "Fatty? I'm here."

This voice was so familiar, wasn't it his dearest wife "Stinky"? Where was she? The old man turned around and saw "Stinky" standing next to him. She had become beautiful again, and young again, as if she had returned to the scene of walking in the snow in 2001. He hugged her tightly, unable to believe it was true: "Stinky, you don't recognize me anymore..." Before he finished speaking, the electrocardiogram of his partner lying on the stretcher beside him also became a straight line.

"Dad, Mom..." The daughter's heart-wrenching shouts echoed throughout the hospital.

He and she wanted to tell their daughter not to worry about them, because they could be together again, but no matter how they shouted, the daughter and son-in-law continued to cry as if they didn't hear anything...

There was nothing they could do, so the old couple helplessly walked out of the hospital.

Looking up, the sky was red, like blood, incredibly beautiful. They had never seen such a beautiful sunset.

Hand in hand, the old couple walked affectionately towards the end of the world...
