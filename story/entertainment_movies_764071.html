Synopsis:

Laughing (played by Sam Hui Tin-Wah) is believed to be an undercover police officer, but due to a drug trafficking case, he is pursued by Superintendent Pan Sir (played by Bryan Leung Tat-Wah).

In fact, before joining the police force, Laughing was a gang member who had always followed his boss, Big Brother (played by Anthony Wong Chau-Sang), in the criminal world. Big Brother took good care of Laughing, and Laughing regarded him as a close brother. In order to protect his territory and illegal businesses, Big Brother required Laughing to join the police force so that he could gain intelligence on police operations.

Laughing performed exceptionally well at the police academy, but fatefully, he never got the chance to stay in the police force because Anti-Triad Superintendent Sin Sir (played by Yu Jim-Yuen) sensed Laughing's underworld connections and assigned him as an undercover agent. Thus, Laughing became a "double undercover," and Big Brother was pleased to have Laughing sent into the triad to deal with his rival, Seatop (played by Louis Koo). Amidst this already complicated situation, another event made Laughing's position even more complex—he fell in love with Seatop's sister Karen (played by Cecilia Cheung).

Facing choices between justice, loyalty, and love, how will Laughing handle the situation? No matter which side he chooses, it would be considered betrayal. Which side will Laughing ultimately choose?
