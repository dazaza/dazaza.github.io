What's the Difference With Custom Applications?

by jojozou on 2012-02-04 15:14:20

Customer-specific applications are application software specifically designed for customers based on special features or needs as required by the customer. On the other hand, packaged software is designed according to the common needs of users worldwide, whereas customer-specific applications can be designed according to the current needs of the client, with time and money being the only limiting factors. Quality and design are very important for a successful customer-specific application. In terms of uniqueness and value, it can play an important role in the customer's site. However, the customer must ensure whether the future returns are worth their investment. From the current situation, the Internet has developed into a global information network, its scale and number of users are second only to the public telephone network (PSTN) and cable television network. The traditional telecommunications network and the next-generation network will coexist for a considerable period of time. Therefore, whether in technology or business, the integration of traditional PSTN and IP data networks has become an inevitable trend in network development. VoIP technology is a custom application that integrates PSTN and IP networks.

A VoIP system should ideally provide a unified interface that can be conveniently used for secondary development (which can be carried out by third parties). In today's increasingly mature softswitch architecture, how to develop and apply VoIP systems based on the softswitch architecture is meaningful. With the rapid development of optical networks and the application of digital transmission technology, issues such as bandwidth and quality of service, which were previously considered bottlenecks in data communication networks, have been resolved one by one, promoting the rapid development of IP technology and driving various applications towards IP convergence. VOIP telephone/VOIP network telephone (also known as IPPHONE or VoIP) services are a typical application among them.

Building your own call center to conduct enterprise services involves significant upfront investment compared to outsourcing services. These two aspects require the accumulation of time and experience. A mature outsourcing service operator first possesses a custom application program tested through years of business operations, which can help client enterprises start services within a short period and ensure the stability of service levels. Secondly, the target customer database accumulated through operational practices can help customer companies accurately establish their business development direction; Thirdly, outsourcing service operators achieve maximum efficiency through the reasonable combination of multiple businesses, thereby reducing service costs and providing price advantages.
