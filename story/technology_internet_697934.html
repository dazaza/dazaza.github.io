This weekend, the regional selection competition of Dongguan's Third English Speaking Contest, known as the "Number One English Speaking Competition in Dongguan", will officially kick off at the Jinxiu Mountain River project site. More than 300 English elites from all walks of life will gather here for the first round of preliminary selection PK. The theme of this contest is set as "Happy Dongguan", aiming to enhance the English learning atmosphere in Dongguan, strengthen the communication between Dongguan and the world, thereby promoting the cultural construction of Dongguan and the development of society. This is also one of the series of community cultural activities at Jinxiu Mountain River.

This year's contest is jointly organized by the Foreign Propaganda Office of Dongguan Municipal Committee, Dongguan Municipal Foreign Affairs Bureau, the Communist Youth League Committee of Dongguan Municipal, and Dongguan Radio and Television Station, with the Education Bureau of Dongguan taking charge of the organization. It can be said to be highly authoritative. And this contest has been held up to the third edition so far. Up to now, the scale and influence of Dongguan's English Speaking Contest are considered the best among English competitions in Dongguan, and it is also one of the most important cultural events in Dongguan, enjoying the reputation of the "Number One English Speaking Competition in Dongguan".

In terms of cultural construction, Songshan Lake has always been at the forefront. Currently, Songshan Lake has introduced more than thirty cultural creative enterprises, covering industrial design, animation, film and television, internet, etc. According to Dongguan's 12th Five-Year Plan, Songshan Lake will become the leading base for Dongguan's cultural creative industry. As the first and largest residential project in Songshan Lake, Jinxiu Mountain River strives to strengthen community cultural construction and improve the cultural atmosphere of the community. Actively applying to host the oral speaking contest is a responsibility and obligation as the leading residential project in Songshan Lake.

It is reported that this contest will be divided into primary school group, junior high school group, senior high school group, and adult group, with three stages: selection, preliminary, and final. Ultimately, eight contestants from each group will enter the final. All English enthusiasts and foreign nationals working and living in Dongguan can sign up to participate.
