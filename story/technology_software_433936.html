Charming Cities in the Eyes of Foreign Talents

The other day, the results of the 2010 Charming China - The Most Attractive Chinese Cities in the Eyes of Foreign Talents were announced. Ten cities made it to the list including Beijing, Shanghai, Dalian, Hangzhou, Shenzhen, Tianjin, Qingdao, Xiamen, Yantai and Wuhu.

It is the first time in China that foreign talents and experts who have made outstanding contributions to China's economic and social development are organized to select the most attractive Chinese cities. This selection activity closely follows the theme of city opening-up and talent introduction, trying to display the style of Chinese cities in introducing talents and intelligence while exploring the value orientation of foreign talents working and starting businesses in China, guiding cities to pay attention to the construction of soft environment for talent introduction so as to attract more high-level overseas talents to start businesses and innovate in China.

★Prospects Comment: Since the reform and opening up, the number of foreign talents in our country has greatly increased. I believe their arrival will bring the catfish effect to the current talent market.

Yunnan Students Gradually Heat Up Studying Abroad in Thailand

Compared with Western countries, low cost is one of the characteristics of studying in Thailand. It is understood that currently, the annual tuition fee for undergraduate study in Thailand is about 10,000-20,000 RMB, and the annual tuition fee for master's degree is about 40,000 RMB. Including living expenses, the total annual expenditure is usually less than 60,000 RMB. This is very attractive to salaried families in China who hope to send their children to study abroad.

With the deepening cooperation between China and Thailand, the demand for Thai language talents is increasing. The head of the Student Affairs Department of Yunnan Provincial Department of Education believes that especially with Yunnan's geographical advantages and the construction of a bridgehead for China's opening up to the southwest, there is an urgent need for related professional talents. In the future, Yunnan will recruit more Thai international students and also need a large number of Thai language teachers, so the employment prospect of studying in Thailand is broad.

★Prospects Comment: Compared with the expensive fees for studying in Europe and America, Thailand's low tuition fees for studying abroad and the popularity of small languages have won the favor of students.

Shortage of High-end Talents in Private Banks

Once hit by the financial crisis, the private banking business of foreign banks has regained vitality and started to vigorously cultivate and recruit talents to support the development of their business. However, the shortage of relevant customer managers has become an obstacle to their development in the domestic high-end market. It is reported that the annual turnover rate in the private banking industry is about 20%.

★Prospects Comment: Controlling the loss rate while training talents is the key to business development.

More than 4,000 people find jobs, five types of talents are in great demand

The other day, the first job fair of Hainan's "Thousands of Enterprises, Tens of Thousands of Positions" Employment Promotion Program attracted more than 15,000 people to seek jobs on site, and more than 4,000 job seekers reached employment agreements with employers on site. In terms of talent demand, hotel, sales, real estate, human resources, and front-line production technology talents ranked in the top five.

It was learned that the "Thousands of Enterprises, Tens of Thousands of Positions" Large-scale Talent Recruitment Month would continue from May 6th to May 27th.

★Prospects Comment: Those who want to seek development opportunities in other provinces and cities might as well go and try.

Grassroots Hospitals Desperately Need Male Nurses

The job fair held in Hunan attracted 260 medical institutions and pharmaceutical enterprises across the country. On-site, several male students majoring in nursing were immediately surrounded by multiple medical institutions. "Our hospital is now desperately short of male nurses. They can start working directly once they come, and even get subsidies and bonuses during the internship period," said a recruitment officer of a hospital. Currently, this hospital only has three male nurses, and one left last year.

Nowadays, more and more male patients prefer to be cared for by male nurses during treatment. The director of the Employment Guidance Center analyzed that many hospitals have realized that in clinical emergency rescue, moving paralyzed patients and critically ill patients, and performing traction for fracture patients, male nurses have more obvious advantages.

★Prospects Comment: As male nurses become increasingly scarce, society's recognition of male nurses is getting higher and higher. In the future, there will be more and more males in the ranks of nurses in hospitals.
