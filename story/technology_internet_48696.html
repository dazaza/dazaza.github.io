Precision marketing becomes a trend, enterprise SMS will be widely applied

by now on 2008-02-21 17:40:13

A set of data released not long ago by the China’s Ministry of Information Industry showed that, as of December 2007, the number of mobile phone users in China reached 547.286 million accounts and the mobile phone popularization rate was 41.6%. The large number of mobile phone users in China has created a “Thumb Economy” environment. Mobile information products based on this kind of “Thumb Economy” environment have become a powerful tool for SMEs to conduct targeted marketing in our country. Corporate SMS is the mainstream application of mobile business model. Relevant marketing experts said that corporate SMS is now at the stage of preparing for large-scale application.

With the penetration rate of domestic mobile terminals increasing continuously, some advertisers have realized the effect and prospect of mobile SMS. The accuracy, interactivity, calculability and manageability of mobile SMS with high delivery rate have deeply impressed advertisers. The mobile information market is gradually evolving into a fresh market with infinite business opportunities. The widespread application of corporate SMS has made Chinese SMEs realize the concept of mobile information office which can save expenditure and improve efficiency. Now.Cn (www.now.cn), one of the providers of corporate SMS platforms, has launched an SMS experience storm - buy space and get SMS and the Times SMS platform. If you buy in large quantities, the price can be as low as 0.045 yuan per message. Its responsible person said, "For modern enterprises, corporate SMS is a new technology and means for enterprise promotion and management. It can reduce the cost and risk of management and promotion, and meet the real needs of fast-paced enterprise office applications. This business mode is not only an innovation in promotional means, but also an innovation in enterprise management mode. It is a major trend for SME promotion and office automation."

The author learned that SMS platforms can provide enterprises or government agencies with the function of communicating with internal employees, suppliers and customers who own mobile phones through computers simultaneously. It is widely used in daily work such as sending meeting notices, event notices, product promotion, announcements, customer contact, social group member management, etc. within enterprises. It has become the fastest and most economical communication tool for information exchange, greatly improving promotional efforts and work efficiency. Nowadays, shopping malls, restaurants, star hotels, decoration companies, beauty salons, etc. are all popular to release advertisements via mobile SMS. In addition to promotional information, merchants also like to send greetings via SMS to win customer affection, such as reminding to add clothes when the weather changes or sending blessings during festivals. Traditional media need production, layout and editing, and can only be published at fixed times, which is restricted by time and space. However, mobile SMS can be published at any time, with fast speed. Mobile users across the country can receive it. For short, flat and fast or urgent publicity activities, mobile SMS is very suitable. Most mobile users will check the received SMS, and only after checking the short message, it will be deleted. Therefore, the viewing rate can reach more than 95% or even 100%, which no other media can achieve. The low cost of SMS is also an important reason why corporate SMS is popular.

Marketing experts introduced that the application of corporate SMS should classify the customer groups and treat them differently according to their characteristics; besides, the "degree" should be well controlled. It should not be too frequent, making customers feel annoyed, nor should it be too "neglected", which cannot achieve the desired effect. It is best to reasonably mix and send knowledge-based public welfare SMS, promotional SMS, and blessing SMS, which will achieve twice the result with half the effort. At present, precise marketing is undoubtedly one of the most promising directions recognized by the advertising industry. The application of corporate SMS has eliminated the obstacles of information communication, achieved information anytime, anywhere, and anytime, leading the trend of enterprise promotion and office automation. Under the impetus of the popular experience activities of large service providers such as Times Interconnect, it will be popularized faster.
