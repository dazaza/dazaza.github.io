During the argument between Ma Yanming and Zhang Xian, Wang Hui struck Ma Yanming twice. Yao Qingwei told reporters that if Zhang Pingxuan's family is indeed in difficulty, both he and lawyer Lan He are willing to provide help within their capabilities and humanitarian support.
