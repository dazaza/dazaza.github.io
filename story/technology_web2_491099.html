Fault phenomenon: During the instant of channel switching, there is a normal image which then immediately turns into a blue background. This happens with the Beijing Sharp TV repair phone service, but when receiving signals from some individual TV stations, there is a fault phenomenon where the image alternates with the blue background.

Sharp TV, alternating images and blue background

Technical data for Beijing Sharp TV repair phone service.

Analysis and handling: Such a fault phenomenon may be caused by an abnormal station identification signal on pin 47 of the microprocessor DQA1 in the infrared remote control system. At this point, the microprocessor issues a blue screen operation command, resulting in a blue background raster. However, since sometimes there is an alternation between the image and the blue background, it indicates that the pin can receive insufficient amplitude station identification signals for some strong station signals. Therefore, after connecting the closed-circuit TV signal line, the voltage on pin 47 of DQAI was measured to be 0V upon turning on the machine; when there is an image during the instant of pressing the channel conversion key, the voltage on this pin rises to +4.6V, then drops to 0V, causing the image to disappear and the blue background to appear again. Thus, the issue lies in the related circuit components connected to this pin. Finally, upon inspection, it was found that the bias resistor RA71 (560kΩ) of VQA1 had an open circuit. Replacing it with a new component of the same model, RA71, and conducting a power-on test resolved the fault in the color TV.
