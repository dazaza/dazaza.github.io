I miss you when the wind blows.

by mimi456 on 2012-01-27 21:26:15

When the wind blows, I think of you.

You are my destiny. She once said that. Mo Yan, you shouldn't say it anymore.

So, when the wind blows, I think of you.

Chu Xi has returned. His return was completely unexpected.

In the past, Mo Yan wanted to wait for Chu Xi's return and she would definitely go to the station to pick him up. Chu Xi said: Mo Yan, at that time, you will hold my arm, and we will walk back together, okay?

On that day, Mo Yan was with her friends in the supermarket when she suddenly received Chu Xi's call: Mo Yan, do you know where I am now?

For some unknown reason, Mo Yan's heart suddenly beat strongly. Mo Yan turned around and asked: Where? Chu Xi said he had just arrived home. Mo Yan instantly felt her heartbeat slow down by half a beat. Is Chu Xi really back? Although it was different from what she had expected, which was her going to the station to pick him up and then they would slowly walk back together, Chu Xi had said that as long as he came back, he would look for Mo Yan.

Mo Yan's voice trembled slightly: Chu Xi, when will you come over?

Chu Xi paused for a few seconds on the other end of the phone: Mo Yan, don't rush, I'll come over in a few days. At that time, you have to accompany me to buy a computer.

A long time ago, Chu Xi had told Mo Yan that when he came back, he would ask Mo Yan to accompany him to buy a computer.

Chu Xi returned on the evening of the 16th. Today is the 23rd. Just now, Mo Yan saw two missed calls without notes. Mo Yan called back, it was Chu Xi's number. Chu Xi told Mo Yan that this was his new number, given when installing broadband.

Mo Yan only asked when it was installed, Chu Xi said yesterday. Mo Yan knew that Chu Xi had broken his promise again. Mo Yan didn't say much, she asked Chu Xi if he had any business calling her? Chu Xi said: Mo Yan, why don't you ever call me?

Mo Yan thought, except for last night when she went to bed early and didn't call, she had been calling Chu Xi all along. Every time she called Chu Xi, he was always watching TV, and each time he told Mo Yan: Mo Yan, I'm watching TV, hang your QQ, and I'll chat with you.

Mo Yan said: Chu Xi, my QQ is always online, it's just set to invisible. Actually, Mo Yan wanted to say that her QQ was always online just for Chu Xi, but disappointingly, Chu Xi never looked for Mo Yan. That day too, Mo Yan waited until 11 pm but still didn't see any message from Chu Xi. Mo Yan knew that Chu Xi must have forgotten again.

Tomorrow, the 24th

Christmas Eve. Mo Yan took three days off sick leave. She thought, if Chu Xi came tomorrow, she would definitely take a taxi and rush to the dormitory to wait for Chu Xi. But, Mo Yan knew that Chu Xi definitely wouldn't come out now. With a computer, Chu Xi would definitely be playing "Wai Wai" (a game). In the past, once, Mo Yan let Chu Xi sing to her, and Chu Xi said he would definitely sing to Mo Yan when he came back, saying it was embarrassing to sing over the phone.

Once, Chu Xi's junior held a wedding in "Wai Wai", Chu Xi asked Mo Yan if she would come to "Wai Wai", saying he would sing tonight. Mo Yan said she had no time, she still had to work. The next day, Chu Xi told Mo Yan that he sang "All My Life With You" to the bride last night in "Wai Wai". Mo Yan asked: Why did you sing to the bride instead of the groom singing?

Chu Xi smiled and said: What's the big deal, isn't it just the people in "Wai Wai" cheering?

Mo Yan was speechless: You're willing to sing in the internet cafe, but when I ask you to sing my favorite "No Breakup Love" over the phone, you feel embarrassed?

Chu Xi was upset: When I come back, I will definitely sing it for you!

When the wind blows, I think of you.

Chu Xi.

When every hope turns into disappointment, those disappointments become deeper each time, until they become numb and there's no feeling left. The sweet words spoken over the phone have become somewhat laughable lies.

Mo Yan is just a simple girl, hoping someone will care about her. When it's cold, someone can hold her hand and tell her: Mo Yan, you won't be cold. When she's unhappy, someone can make her laugh.

Mo Yan doesn't know how long she can keep holding on? How long?
