Once didn't care

by ecyxiaoyanj on 2011-09-11 17:24:21

The lack of cherishing in the past has led to today's regret. Unpleasant memories are unbearable to recall, and now I know it's all too late.

Sleepless tonight, insomnia every night, who can understand the taste of heartbreak? Getting drunk tonight, sorrowful alcohol intoxication, the more drunk, the more heartbroken. The cruel heartbreak makes me lose myself. I will not hold on to you anymore, really won't. Your betrayal is probably heaven's punishment for me because I once hurt the girl who loved me the most.

It takes hundreds of years to share a boat ride, thousands of years to share a pillow. Countless looks back in the past life exchange for one passing encounter in this life. I want to hide this relationship, hide it deep in my heart and never speak of it again. This emotion will be an indelible scar in my life, even erasing my four-character online name. I wish I could be your lover in this life, but it's impossible. If possible, I would reserve your next life, okay? In this life, I didn't take good care of you, went through so many things, and finally realized that the one who truly treats you well and doesn't make you suffer or sad is the one you didn't know to cherish. The person you didn't know to cherish is the one who loves you the most, silently giving without expecting anything in return, and quietly leaving when you no longer need her without a word. And thus, you lost her forever. Every time I think of her, I have an indescribable feeling. Whenever I am lonely, helpless, and alone, I miss her the most. I especially miss the days we spent washing our hair together, walking, eating, and watching her playful face. Those beautiful times will accompany my memories for a lifetime. Can I face you calmly? Can I face your betrayal? I wish this life would end sooner so that the next life would come quickly, allowing me to repay the emotional debt I owe you in this life and free myself from the sins of this life. I will guard you for eternity... Now, my ending has already paid the price for the sins I committed. I just feel I owe you too much. In the next life, I will protect you for a lifetime until the day we grow old, okay? Only you treat me the best. Now you have your own life, I cannot disrupt your life. I don't want you to see me sad because that would make you sad for me, and I don't deserve such sacrifice from you.
