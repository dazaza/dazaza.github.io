Expert Analysis: The Value of Industry-Specific Chinese Domain Names Continues to Rise

by now on 2007-09-12 10:41:30

A few days ago, there were news reports that the Chinese domain names such as "notebook.cn" and other 5 Chinese domain names were transferred for tens of thousands of US dollars. Recently, Beijing second-hand housing.network, Shanghai second-hand housing.company and other Chinese domain names have been hotly traded online again, drawing people's attention to the huge potential value of industry-related Chinese domain names. As early as the initial launch of Chinese domain names in 2000, Chinese domain names in popular industries such as home appliances, tobacco, automobiles, medicine, food, and automobiles were crazily registered. Industry insiders said that this heated discussion on industry-related Chinese domain names would surely drive up the value of industry-related domain names again.

Chinese domain names, with their mother tongue advantage and full support from mainstream browsers, have been greatly promoted and applied. For a company, adopting Chinese domain names has obvious advantages: it has clear meaning, is simple and easy to remember, making it unforgettable at a glance, and greatly enhancing brand promotion efficiency. In addition, if relevant industry-related Chinese domain names are registered, such as "tire.COM" and "tire.HK", customers only need to enter these domain names in the address bar to directly reach the company website, making it easier to promote the company and products, while also increasing more potential customers. Finally, Chinese is very easy to be indexed by search engines, greatly improving the chances of the website being searched when customers look for related information.

In recent years, the domestic demand for information on product and raw material supply and demand, and industrial development trends has led to the rapid development of industry websites, resulting in a series of well-known industry websites such as China Towel Network and China Jewelry Network. Industry websites inevitably hope to own industry-related mark domain names, which can only reflect their leading position in the industry, such as jewelry.CN and jewelry.COM.

On the other hand, domain names, like company names and trademarks, are unique. Industry-related domain names are even rarer. Once registered and used, peers or competitors will not have the opportunity to use these domain names again. This makes industry-related Chinese domain names more precious, and their value continues to increase. It is understood that a machinery company in Fujian protected its enterprise product brand by registering all its products' Chinese domain names, such as "piston.HK", "piston.TW", "piston ring.HK", "piston ring.TW", etc., taking the industry lead.

Experts predict that industry-related Chinese domain names have great value but limited quantity. With the further popularization of Chinese domain names, their value will continue to increase, gaining more recognition and affirmation from people. At the same time, their prices will also soar.
