Dazzling City Series: The Abandoned Wife's Great Comeback

Introduction to Dazzling City Series: The Abandoned Wife's Great Comeback. On the first day of her time-travel adventure, she encounters an embarrassing scene. This innocent young girl loses face by spurting nosebleeds. How can this be dealt with when a tyrant still wants to have something with her? Her resistance results in her legs being broken and having to serve everyone. Hmm, these men aren't bad either, at least better than that filthy tyrant. Should she play along for fun? Before she could finish fantasizing, the tyrant suddenly arrives, bundles her up, and carries her away. Four days and four nights of inhuman torture, followed by merciless ridicule and humiliation. It doesn't matter; she has no heart or lungs, nor any extra feelings for unworthy people. Picking up his letter of divorce, she covers her face and cries, remembering to act...

Not wanting to bear the identity of an abandoned wife, her market value remains incredibly good. Handsome men line up just for her dazzling smile. Princes, emperors, even the leader of a demonic sect... Which one is more enchanting, resembling her tyrannical ex-husband?

She is carefree, without too many extravagant desires, only seeking warmth and food. Unexpectedly, her stunning beauty attracts numerous handsome men bowing down. What overturns her fate? Is it love, power tactics, or some predetermined destiny? When the splendor of the dynasty begins, and the clouds disperse, she smiles gently, "You all, I can't afford!"
