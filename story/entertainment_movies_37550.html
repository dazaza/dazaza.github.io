The program anchor Yan Shouyi who struggled out from a small mountain village may not be handsome, but he is enough to become the "idol" of all Chinese TV audiences. The talk show he hosted 《有一说一》 always attracts audiences' attention no matter they are kids or old people. Once his program begins, they will sit in front of the television honestly and wait for his wonderful words. However, this host who always persuades the participants to tell the truth and to say what they want to say in the program has to make up one lie after another in his life. Although Yan Shouyi has married, he can't resist the temptation of young girls and maintains a lover relationship with an editor named May from a magazine. But you can't wrap fire in paper, and the traces are still left after a sexual affair: because he was afraid that his wife would know his whereabouts, Yan Shouyi turned off his mobile phone, and as a result, his wife called her husband's good friend Fei Mo. Although Fei Mo tried his best to cover up, he was still found by Yan Shouyi's wife Wenjuan Yu. Wenjuan Yu who had already suspected finally found the tooth mark of May on Yan Shouyi's body, and divorce is the only choice for them. It could be said that Yan Shouyi should be "liberated" after the divorce, but this divorce didn't bring him ease, instead, it brought him a lot of trouble. After the divorce, Yan Shouyi made friends with a female teacher from the drama academy named Xue Shen, who has a typical little woman character and is very suspicious and jealous. Once, in order to find out the truth, she followed Yan Shouyi and a group of men to the sauna room and insisted on participating in their activities. Editor May is also a headache for Yan Shouyi. Her youthfulness and self-will have brought impact to Yan Shouyi's spiritual world while bringing him endless troubles. In addition, Yan Shouyi's ex-wife also asked him to help her find a job. Yan Shouyi constantly wavered between these three people and constantly used his mobile phone to maintain the complicated relationship among these four people. Yan Shouyi's colleague Fei Mo, who has been playing the role of an intellectual, thought about Yan Shouyi's affairs: troublesome. But later, he himself also got into trouble, because his secret lover was revealed, and he fell into the same trouble as Yan Shouyi.
