A Hint of Shyness at Yunxuan Pavilion (Part II)

by xiaoshuo2n on 2012-03-07 19:50:32

"Alright, sure, how do we calculate the rent?" "180 a week for one person, you know, it's also written online." "Then what about a month?" Both of them grabbed their phones and started calculating, "180*52/12=780." "700 then." "Uh, no. No bargaining." "You said negotiable online." "Alright then, Yunxuan Pavilion, 750." "Oh, what's included?" "Nothing is included." "Are you robbing me? Then 740." "It's 750, no more bargaining." Xuanxuan was already losing patience; she hated haggling with men. "Don't be like that, 750 including something." "Then include the internet fee." "How much is the internet fee?" "8.5 a month." "Ah!! Joking right? Just include the internet fee." "Hmm, that's it, take it or leave it." Xuanxuan was about to go downstairs when the boy grabbed her shoulder, "Hey, don't go. Can you include more? How about shared meals?" "Hahaha, sure, stew twice a week, eat out for the rest, you can have the soup." After saying this, Xuanxuan looked up at the sky, fortunately her brother came. "Younger brother, this kid is bargaining with me!!!" Her brother glanced at Xuanxuan and walked away directly, "You talk since you're the landlord." The boy beside her was smirking, "Hehe, are you her younger brother?" Her brother smiled, "Your accent is the same as my friend, are you Yang Fan?" "Hehe yes." Xuanxuan, after hearing this, said, "Also, your accent does indeed sound similar, and judging by your clothes, you seem to be from there." "Is it that obvious?" "Alright, hurry up and decide, I'll show you around downstairs, check out the kitchen and such." Then Xuanxuan led the boy downstairs, walking to the stairs, Xuanxuan made a mysterious gesture towards the kitchen, "Hey, Kean, a fellow townsman wants to rent a room, do you two know each other?" Turning back to the boy who followed her down, "You two aren't pretending not to know each other, are you?" While talking, the boy peeked into the kitchen. "Hey, how did you end up here?" Hearing this, Xuanxuan's heart skipped a beat. Goodness, they really do know each other!!! Alright, then Kean walked out from the kitchen and asked in the local language, "You're coming to rent a room? Alone? Didn't bring your girlfriend?" "Yeah, alone." "Then why not rent in the neighboring district? It's only 160 a week and pretty decent too." Some of what Kean said in the local language I couldn't quite understand, just got the gist. Xuanxuan started cursing Kean in her mind, you want your friend not to rent here, alright, then today's deal is over. Kean took his friend outside, chatted for a while, then came back. "You should draft a contract for him, he wants to rent." Ah, now it was Xuanxuan's turn to be stunned, didn't say he wasn't going to rent, how come after stepping out for a smoke he wants to rent again. This side, Xuanxuan stared at the boy's happy face and wanted to punch him. "Want to rent huh, considering you're Kean's friend, alright, let's continue discussing in my room." "You still don't want to rent, do you?" "Hey, this room is so small." "Say that again, my room, you dare criticize my room!!! Brother, come kick him out, he dared say my room isn't good."
