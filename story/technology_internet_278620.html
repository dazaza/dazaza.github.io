Skills and medals, King of the Battlefield - The skill system introduced by AVA is indeed a revolutionary initiative in FPS online games. Enhancing firepower, reducing gun-switching speed, increasing movement speed, improving defense capabilities – these auxiliary attribute enhancements and improvements will indeed give players a higher survival rate on the battlefield filled with bullets and bombs. Furthermore, the over thirty different types of soldier skills born from the characteristics of reconnaissance soldiers, infantry, and snipers will provide the most advantageous and practical tactical support for these three soldier classes, allowing different characteristic soldiers to display even stronger combat power.

In addition to highly personalized features, the medal system has also brought a wave of honor into AVA. The medals launched mainly consist of three categories: skill medals, hero medals, and tactical medals. At the end of each game, meeting certain conditions will earn corresponding medals, along with corresponding rewards or game coins. Among them, "hero medals" can be obtained repeatedly, but "tactical medals" can only be obtained once.

Medals represent the achievements players have attained in the game, earning envious looks in the first moment. After an intense battle, you will gain respect through your own strength and win glory.
