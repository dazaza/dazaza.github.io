When purchasing, you should not only look at the appearance and style but also the material. Ask about after-sales service clearly before buying. Find out whether the product comes with a seal ring, triangle valve, if installation is included, if after-sales services are provided, and if there is a warranty.

Also, when buying faucets, don't forget to count the accessories, otherwise, it might be troublesome if they don't fit upon installation. Generally, the accessories for basin faucets include the drain, lift rod, faucet fixing bolts, copper fixing plates, washers; bathtub faucets also require showerheads, two inlet hoses, brackets, etc., as standard accessories. Properly produced mixing faucets come with installation dimension diagrams and user manuals when they leave the factory, so pay attention to this when selecting. Imported products need even closer examination.

Material: After choosing the style, understand the manufacturing material of the faucet. Currently, the internal valve cores of faucets on the market mostly use steel ball valves and ceramic valves. Steel ball valves, with their durable steel balls and strong pressure resistance, have become the leading new generation valve core. The downside is that the rubber rings used for sealing are prone to wear and tear and aging. Ceramic valves, on the other hand, inherently have good sealing performance, and faucets using ceramic valve cores provide a more comfortable and smooth feel. Copper-made faucets are considered premium because copper has antibacterial and disinfectant properties. However, it's difficult for consumers to identify the quality of copper, so the best method is to purchase well-known brand products.

Experts suggest that bathroom tiles should differ from those in living rooms and bedrooms. Bathrooms should use tiles with better anti-slip properties, such as anti-slip floor tiles. These tiles usually have pleated patterns or embossed points carved on their surface to increase friction between the tile surface and human feet or shoe soles, thereby greatly reducing the probability of slipping and bumping.

Style: Currently, faucets on the market are generally divided into two types: single-handle faucets and double-handle faucets. Single-handle faucets only require one hole, while double-handle ones can be either four-inch or eight-inch holes, depending on the style of the sink purchased.

Smell: Listen to the flushing sound. The effectiveness of the toilet flushing depends on the pipe design and is also related to the height of the water tank water level and the water parts. High-quality products usually have a smaller flushing sound.

Appearance: When selecting, take brightness, no bubbles, no defects, and no scratches as qualified standards. Generally, imported faucets have more than 10 microns of plating, aiming to prevent rust, enhance aesthetics, and ensure durability. Experts reveal that when selecting, press the faucet surface with your finger; if the fingerprint quickly disappears, the coating is good; if it gets worse, the quality is poorer. After selecting the appearance, try the faucet's feel; whether the switch is smooth, and if the up-down and left-right switch amplitude is large enough to stably adjust the water temperature. Usually, an up-down range of 30 degrees and a left-right range of 90 degrees is optimal. But remember, a light faucet does not necessarily mean good feel.

During the selection of floor tiles, consumers can use samples for simple self-tests to compare the slipperiness of the tile surface when wet. Additionally, pay attention to the maintenance of bathroom tiles. Since anti-slip tiles have uneven surfaces and are not wear-resistant, it's best to wear soft-soled slippers when entering the bathroom to reduce tile surface wear. This extends the tile's lifespan and ensures slip-resistance. Of course, to prevent slips and falls in the bathroom, besides purchasing good-quality anti-slip tiles, consumers must also be mindful of their habits, walking slowly in the bathroom to prevent accidents.

Basin faucets and kitchen faucets are generally installed on the countertop center decoration home network, thus their inlet pipes can be either hard pipes or flexible pipes, usually around 35 centimeters long. For easy connection, a valve must be installed at the junction between the household water pipe and the faucet inlet pipe. This valve is called a triangle valve, which should definitely be purchased together with the faucet to avoid unnecessary trouble. Triangle valves come in different sizes, normally 3/8 inch and 1/2 inch. Also, note that when installing the triangle valve, don't install it too low to avoid insufficient length of the faucet inlet pipe, causing unnecessary trouble. Generally, installing it about 50 to 60 centimeters above the ground is sufficient.

Feel: Read the product quality. Check if the component connections are intact and smooth. Try to select products with an enamel surface inside the toilet drainage pipe, which can be checked by touch. A smoother pipe facilitates better drainage, which counterfeit inferior products often fail to achieve.
