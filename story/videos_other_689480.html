Passengers in the rear-end EMU train said they were saved by a man in a black T-shirt and wanted to thank him in person

by xue94fwsh on 2012-03-04 09:50:22

The picture shows the rescue scene at the Yituo Hospital Attached to Wenzhou Medical College. Zheng Zhiyue took the photo. According to the report, after the accident happened last night, the health department of our city immediately activated the emergency plan and organized all the ambulances from the 120 Emergency Center to rush to the accident site as soon as possible. At the same time, the ambulances from major hospitals in the urban area, as well as those from Yueqing, Ruian, Yongjia, and Cangnan were also dispatched to rescue the injured.

By 1:00 AM today, a total of 53 ambulances with over 100 medical staff had been dispatched, transferring more than 100 injured people in over 100 trips. Ten hospitals including the First Affiliated Hospital of Wenzhou Medical College, the Second Affiliated Hospital of Wenzhou Medical College, the Second People's Hospital of Wenzhou, the Third People's Hospital of Wenzhou, the Integrated Traditional Chinese and Western Medicine Hospital, the No. 118 Hospital of the PLA, Wenzhou Dongou Hospital, Wenzhou Hand and Foot Surgery Hospital, Wenzhou Lucheng People's Hospital, and Wenzhou Kangning Hospital received the injured for treatment. Last night, the Health Department of Zhejiang Province organized three medical teams to go to the accident site to carry out medical rescue operations. The medical team from Hangzhou arrived in Wenzhou around 3:30 AM today.

The nearest cities, Taizhou and Lishui, each sent two medical teams totaling 35 people who arrived in our city around 1:00 AM today. They provided support at Wenzhou Kangning Hospital, Lucheng District People's Hospital, the Second People's Hospital of Wenzhou, and the Third People's Hospital of Wenzhou. Huang Xiaoling

Second Affiliated Hospital of Wenzhou Medical College

At about 10:10 PM last night, the first patient was brought to the Second Affiliated Hospital of Wenzhou Medical College by a passerby. The green channel was immediately opened, and the patient was quickly carried onto a stretcher. A nurse ran briskly towards the emergency room. The registration of names, numbering, assessment of injuries, and assignment of dedicated personnel for treatment began, thus starting a race against death. Subsequently, patients were continuously brought to the hospital by ambulance.

Outside the emergency room, apart from the medical staff, many enthusiastic citizens spontaneously came to help. Two local college students passing by the hospital stayed upon learning the situation, saying, "We can push the stretcher or carry patients, just let us know if we are needed."

Around 10:00 PM last night, Dean Qu Jia of Wenzhou Medical College and President Gu Dingying of the Second Affiliated Hospital of Wenzhou Medical College and other hospital leaders successively came to the emergency room to understand the situation. The directors of various departments and head nurses on leave also voluntarily requested to join and rushed to the hospital from their homes. You Doudou, Huang Peishi

Eighth Branch of the First Affiliated Hospital of Wenzhou Medical College

At 10:25 PM last night, an ambulance from the Eighth Branch of the First Affiliated Hospital of Wenzhou Medical College brought two injured people. As soon as the ambulance arrived, the doctors and nurses on standby immediately started the rescue. After a simple examination, the doctor stitched the wound of the more severely injured person. The other less severely injured person was arranged for an X-ray examination.

Relevant hospital officials introduced that besides arranging ambulances and dispatching doctors and nurses to participate in the rescue at the accident site, there were more than 40 people on standby in the emergency department of the branch, ready to admit new injured people.

As of 12:15 AM today, the branch admitted a total of three injured people, all of whom have been arranged for hospitalization. From the examination results, the three patients mainly suffered from external injuries; two had lumbar vertebra fractures, and one had a clavicle fracture. At the time of this report, the vital signs of the patients were relatively stable. Lü Jinkē, You Doudou, Huang Peishi

Second People's Hospital of Wenzhou

At about 10:00 PM last night, several ambulances were parked outside the emergency center of the Second People's Hospital of Wenzhou. In the emergency room, medical staff were busy rescuing the critically injured people who were constantly being brought in.

Relevant personnel of the Second People's Hospital introduced that due to the large number of injured people, all medical staff stationed in Wenzhou were present that night. The hospital has分流ed patients to various medical departments for preliminary examinations and is fully rescuing the injured. Zhu Bin

Integrated Traditional Chinese and Western Medicine Hospital

At 9:40 PM last night, in the emergency room of the Integrated Traditional Chinese and Western Medicine Hospital, the hospital's responsible person notified the hospital's medical staff one by one via mobile phone to return to the hospital on standby. At 10:00 PM that night, Dr. Liu Dongxu, director of the orthopedics department, among others, went to the scene by ambulance.

At the accident site, it was seen that in addition to the medical ambulances from major hospitals in the urban area, ambulances from counties and cities such as Yueqing and Cangnan had also arrived on standby. By 12:30 AM today, medical staff from various hospitals were still on standby at the scene. Pan Dayuan

Kangning Hospital

Wang Lianyue, executive vice president of Wenzhou Kangning Hospital, introduced that by 11:30 PM last night, 42 injured people had been sent to the hospital for treatment. To facilitate treatment, each injured person had a number attached to their arm. Temporary beds were set up in the outpatient hall, and mats were laid on the ground for the injured to lie down.

The hospital treated the injured according to the severity of their conditions, performing surgery immediately for critical cases and treating skin wounds for minor cases. Huang Xiaoling, Ao Xia, Wang Xiongtao

Injured People's Accounts

Because he was lying down, he survived unharmed

At 11:00 PM last night, at the Third People's Hospital of Wenzhou, Mr. Li from the D301 train said that he was traveling back to his hometown in Fujian. He was lying down in the 3rd carriage when it fell, and it was precisely because he was "lying down" that he survived unharmed. At that time, he was with two children and a woman, moving from the 3rd carriage to the 4th carriage. Because the window of the 4th carriage was broken by the rescue personnel into a large hole, they four were successfully rescued at 9:44 PM that night.

Doctors diagnosed that Mr. Li had bruises on his left scapula and posterior scapula, and a torn earlobe under his right ear. When interviewed, he was sitting in a corner of the emergency hall, saying that he would let the doctors treat the more seriously injured people first, and it didn't matter if he waited a little longer.

I met Yin Lei from the Diligent Road Brigade of the Lucheng Fire Department in the injection room of the Third People's Hospital of Wenzhou. He directly rushed to the accident site after completing a firefighting task. Due to the rescue, he hurt his foot, and a teammate lying beside him fainted from exhaustion. He said that the three of them were an action squad, rescuing the soft sleeper carriage, and they saved a total of four people. Zhou Linzi

Thanks to someone holding her tightly, she wasn't thrown out

Lu Xiaowen, 26 years old, was one of the less seriously injured. He told reporters that he was from Zhangzhou, Fujian, and traveled with six colleagues from Hangzhou, taking the D301 train. The 2nd carriage where he was located was sandwiched between two other carriages, and the six people's location happened to be at the connection point of the two carriages, which allowed them to survive unharmed.

Xiaoping (a pseudonym) and her mother are from Fujian. They traveled to Hangzhou during the summer vacation. At that time, they were in the 3rd carriage. "There was lightning and rain at the time, and suddenly the carriage swayed violently, followed by complete darkness," Xiaoping said. "I was instantly thrown to the lower bunk on the other side, thanks to an aunt who held me tightly, I wasn't thrown out." Zhu Bin

Shandong girl looking for her mother Song Hua

Nine-year-old Xiao Rui (a pseudonym) from Shandong sat barefoot on a wheelchair with a bruised and swollen left eye. Due to unclear internal injuries, with the nurse's consent, the reporter carried Xiao Rui to take a CT scan. Fortunately, the result showed only a slight blood swelling on Xiao Rui's head. While the reporter was asking the nurse about Xiao Rui's condition, Xiao Rui pulled the reporter's hand and asked to look for her mother and brother. It turned out that her mother Song Hua and her 12-year-old brother Xiao Xiang (a pseudonym) were also on the accident train. The reporter immediately dialed her mother Song Hua's mobile phone based on the number Xiao Rui provided, but as of 1:00 AM, they were still unable to get in touch with her, similar to symptoms of rhinitis. Zhu Bin

The car stopped for a while before the incident

Mr. Fu from Lucheng was injured in the accident with a fractured clavicle. He was seated in seat 60 of carriage 5 of the D301 train. He described the situation at the time:

It was still thundering and raining with lightning outside. I heard the broadcast saying that the next station was Wenzhou South Station. I prepared my luggage to get off the train. But shortly after the broadcast, the car stopped. After waiting for a while, the broadcast said it was a temporary stop.

After stopping for a while, the car started again. I thought we should arrive at the station soon. The car was running pretty fast, and I was a bit worried that the car might have gone past the station. But after a few minutes, the accident happened.

I felt a strong impact at that moment, and I was just at the junction of two carriages.

The car finally stopped. At that time, I felt that my mind was still clear, my feet could move, but my hands couldn't lift, and it hurt badly. Soon, the rescue personnel arrived.

At first, the door near me couldn't be opened, so the rescue personnel guided us to exit through the door of the rear carriage. I walked by myself at first, then the rescue personnel helped me down. None of the passengers in my carriage were injured. Lü Jinkē

Reporting safety to family in native dialect

Yang Jingjing, who was sent to the Second Affiliated Hospital of Wenzhou Medical College for treatment, is 30 years old and from Putian, Fujian. He returned home with his wife by high-speed train. With a face full of blood, he endured the pain and asked anxiously, "My wife is also on the train, is she okay?"

"I only remember hearing a few loud clangs, and the carriage suddenly flipped down, and it was completely dark," recalled Lian Dequn, a 48-year-old injured passenger from Fuzhou. Lian Dequn was sent to the hospital with his injured hands raised in front of his chest, unable to move. He ignored the treatment, asking the nurse to hold his mobile phone close to his ear, struggling to report safety to his family in his native dialect, unwilling to hang up.

Seven-year-old Cai Hengyi and his father were sent to the hospital together. The young child kept shouting in pain and thirst. When the nurse wiped the child's lips with a cotton swab, the caring father consoled, "Be good!" while gently kissing his son's forehead and smiling to encourage the child. You Doudou, Huang Peishi

The little boy crawled out of the carriage by himself

An eight-year-old boy named Zhao Zhu sat alone in the corridor receiving intravenous fluids without adult companionship. His whole body was blackened, and his big eyes sparkled with fear. The reporter handed him a bottle of water and comforted him, "You're safe now, don't be afraid."

Zhao Zhu said, "I'm not afraid, but my aunt lost her memory." Since he was sent to the hospital by ambulance, Zhao Zhu got separated from his family, and he didn't know where his mother was.

Zhao Zhu said that his family of seven traveled to Hangzhou and was returning to Fuzhou by high-speed train. At the time, he wasn't asleep and stood by the door, watching a foreign passenger waiting to get off. He wanted to see how the high-speed train door opened. "Suddenly, there was a violent shake, and a chair hit my head, then a lot of luggage pressed on me."

"When I woke up, a lot of things were pressing on me, and my feet couldn't be pulled out. I struggled to crawl out, stepping on some people. I don't know if I hurt them." After Zhao Zhu crawled out of the carriage, he found his aunt, who was so scared that she temporarily lost her memory and didn't know why she was on the train or why she was there. On the contrary, the child led the frightened aunt to the rescue personnel and took the ambulance to the hospital. Huang Xiaoling, Ao Xia, Wang Xiongtao

To the guy in the Nike T-shirt, thank you

At Wenzhou Kangning Hospital, Zhu Yalan kept mentioning, "A young man in a Nike T-shirt saved me, I don't know where he is now?"

Zhu Yalan traveled from Shanghai to Fuzhou and was in carriage 3 at the time. When she felt the violent collision, she instinctively grabbed the railing on the bunk bed, but the carriage swayed too much, and she couldn't reach the railing. She could only curl up and hug her head, feeling the darkness as the vehicle descended.

Zhu Yalan wanted to open the door of the carriage to escape, but the door was stuck and she couldn't open it. At this time, a panting person came over and said to her, "The doors of the previous carriages are all stuck. I pulled a lot of people out, walking all the way here, and I'm almost out of strength." This young man and Zhu Yalan pushed the carriage door together, climbed out of the carriage, and the young man returned to the train. Zhu Yalan got on the road and was sent to Kangning Hospital by nearby villagers who heard the news.

"In the darkness, I only remember he was wearing a black Nike T-shirt. I really want to find him and say thank you." Zhu Yalan said. Huang Xiaoling, Ao Xia, Wang Xiongtao
