Properly store documents and transfer ownership in time after the holiday. If I choose a house during the Spring Festival, but it happens that the housing management department and banks are not working, so I cannot transfer the ownership, what should I do? Answer: Generally, intermediary companies will require each employee to complete the purchase procedures for customers and owners before the Spring Festival. However, if buyers cannot handle the relevant transfer procedures in time during the Spring Festival, there is no need to worry. Just properly store the relevant documents and go to the relevant departments to handle the relevant procedures in time after the Spring Festival holiday, which will not have a significant impact. Reminder: During the Spring Festival, there may be illegal intermediaries taking advantage of the situation to create chaos. They often attract customers with discounts and other preferential activities. It is suggested that homebuyers must choose reputable brand intermediary companies. Paying a deposit only makes it easy to encounter owner breaches. I liked a second-hand house before the New Year and paid the deposit, but did not sign the contract. Is this risky? Answer: The phenomenon of paying deposits before the New Year and signing contracts after the New Year is not common, and this practice carries some risks for buyers. Without a written contract, the transaction does not have actual protection. Due to the influence of the real estate off-season before the Spring Festival, house prices are relatively low, while after the Spring Festival, with the arrival of a new round of sales peak season, some owners may breach the contract unilaterally. Prompt: In case of an owner breach, intermediary companies generally require staff to coordinate between the buyer and seller. If unable to resolve, reputable companies will promptly refund the deposit. Clear weather facilitates observation of lighting conditions. What details should be paid attention to when selecting a house on-site during the Spring Festival? Answer: Due to the short daylight hours in winter, it's best to choose a sunny day to observe the lighting and ventilation of the house. This is also the best season to inspect the house heating system, as cold weather can easily cause water pipes to burst. Attention should be paid to the age of the water pipes and whether there are any cracks on their surfaces. Prompt: Make contact with the intermediary in advance to verify the viewing time and location, because intermediary personnel move frequently during the Spring Festival, avoiding unnecessary trouble for homebuyers.
