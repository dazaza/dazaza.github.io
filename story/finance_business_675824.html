Studies have shown that random calorie intake increases oxidative damage to brain cells compared to restricted calorie intake, which damages learning and memory. Plus, schizophrenic patients tend not to exercise, making them susceptible to obesity, hypertension, and diabetes, even if they don't take antipsychotic drugs. Suitable for those who are emotionally indifferent, stare blankly, and laugh or talk to themselves.
