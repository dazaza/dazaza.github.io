Introduction to the plot of Embrace You in This Life:

Lin Xiaomin, who works as a white-collar employee at a large company after graduating from university, has been corresponding with Wu Dalong, a platoon leader of a certain Tibetan border defense unit, as pen pals for five years. They agreed that when the number of letters reaches 365, their relationship will automatically be upgraded, and they plan to meet.

Just as Lin Xiaomin is about to meet Wu Dalong in Tibet, Wu Dalong is severely injured by high-voltage electricity while rescuing the lives and property of Tibetan compatriots. The first time Lin Xiaomin meets Wu Dalong, he is suffering from severe burns and facing double-arm amputation. Lin Xiaomin is shocked and feels repelled, plus her colleague Qiu Shasha strongly advises her not to suffer unnecessarily and leave quickly, so she decides to leave Wu Dalong.

However, when she sees Wu Dalong, who has had his arms amputated, not eating, drinking, or speaking, and has already given up hope of living, Lin Xiaomin cannot bear to leave immediately. She wants to wait until Wu Dalong can eat and recover a little before leaving.

Wu Dalong doesn't want to burden Lin Xiaomin, so to make her leave, he deliberately starts eating, which moves Lin Xiaomin, and she stays again. Due to Lin Xiaomin's care and the arrival of Wu Dalong's father, Wu Dalong finally gets out of the valley and regains the courage to live again. Seeing Wu Dalong improve, Lin Xiaomin prepares to return to work. At this moment, bone lesions are detected in Wu Dalong's left leg, requiring another amputation, cutting off his left leg. Lin Xiaomin can't bear it; a person who has already lost both arms cannot lose another leg. Therefore, Lin Xiaomin, who has already reached the train station, stays again.
