Fuzhou Power Supply Company conducts three-level联动visits, achieving a win-win cooperation with Fuyao Glass

Fuzhou Power Supply Company's three-level联动visit: a win-win situation for both Fuyao Glass – Corporate Dynamics - Industry News - Building Materials Network [China Glass Network]

"Reliable power supply, worry-free production, this is a mutually beneficial situation for both our companies," said He Shimeng, Vice President of Fuyao Glass Industry Group, on May 13th to the relevant responsible persons of Fuzhou Power Supply Company who came for a visit.

To strengthen exchanges and communication with customers and provide more high-quality, convenient services, starting from May 10th, Fuzhou Power Supply Company carried out a "three-level联动visit" activity over the course of a month. The main leaders of the company visited relevant departments of the Municipal Party Committee and the Municipal Government as well as key large enterprise customers in industrial zones, the marketing department visited small and medium-sized enterprise customers, and grassroots power supply offices visited enterprises and rural customers within their jurisdictions. They sought to understand the electricity usage status of customers and the difficulties encountered during the production process, humbly listening to opinions and suggestions put forward by customers.

Fuyao Glass Industry Group is the world's fourth largest automotive glass manufacturer, accounting for about 10% of the global automotive glass market share and over 60% domestically. The annual electricity consumption at the Fuzhou location of the group exceeds 2 billion kilowatt-hours. During the visit, the person in charge of Fuzhou Power Supply Company carefully understood the production scale, process, and electricity usage situation of Fuyao Group, and solicited whether they were satisfied with the quality and reliability of the power supply, sincerely listening to and accepting the opinions and suggestions of customers.

He Shimeng said that in 1988, Fuyao was just a small factory, and at the time, there were at least hundreds of power outages per year. Since glass production has very high requirements for the reliability of power supply, electricity usage had always been a bottleneck. Nowadays, each production line has dual-circuit power supply, ensuring very stable power supply during both the financial crisis and peak summer periods. There’s no longer any need to worry about sudden power outages disrupting production, and they can often enjoy extended power supply services. He Shimeng expressed his amazement at the tremendous development changes in electricity supply, and greatly praised the transformation of service concepts by power supply enterprises, improving service levels, and changing from the past "electricity tyrant" to the "electricity nanny" image.

It is reported that conducting an annual "three-level联动customer visit" activity, going door-to-door to listen to customer voices, and humbly accepting customer suggestions has been a tradition upheld by Fuzhou Power Supply Company for many years. Chen Ronghua, Standing Committee Member of the Fujian Provincial Federation of Industry and Commerce, People's Representative of Fuzhou City, and General Manager of Fuzhou Huaxin Food Co., Ltd., gave high praise to this practice. He said that for many years, Fuzhou Power Supply Company has taken the initiative to visit customers, face-to-face communicating and exchanging with them. For the issues and suggestions raised by customers, they have always humbly accepted them. Problems that could be resolved on-site were handled immediately through on-site office work. For those that couldn’t be solved on-site, detailed responses and solutions were provided, allowing customers to truly experience high-quality, convenient services.
