The "Yilin Qingyin" exhibition is held at the Suifeng Art Gallery.

by xyxgyp1998 on 2012-02-10 19:11:07

On January 3, 2012, the "Yi Lin Qing Yin Shao Da Qiao, Shui Tian Zhong Painting Exhibition" was held at the Sui Feng Art Museum on the seventh floor of Jinbao Hui. The exhibition aimed to pursue elegant and pure art [calligraphy and painting wholesale] taste, advocating a kind of supremely good and true academic spirit. It was one of the higher academic levels and larger scale events in the Chinese art world. Vice Minister of the State Council Legislative Affairs Office Bu Fengtao, Deputy Chief of Staff of the PLA 305 Hospital Zhang Qinghua, Political Commissar of the Graduate School of the National Defense University Zhang Jichun, Vice Chairman of the National Development and Reform Commission Wang Pingsheng, Executive Vice President of the China Charity Federation Zhang Hanxing, Honorary Chairman of the China Artists Association Jin Shangyi, Chairman of the China Artists Association Liu Dawei, President of the Central Academy of Fine Arts Pan Gongkai, Director of the Beijing Painting Academy Wang Mingming, Honorary Director of the National Painting Academy Long Rui, Director of the National Painting Academy Yang Xiaoyang, Director of the National Art Museum of China Fan Di'an, Deputy Director of the National Museum Chen Lusheng, Deputy Director of the China Art Research Institute Tian Liming, Secretary of the Party Committee of the Central Academy of Fine Arts Yang Li, as well as nearly 200 other renowned figures from the art world attended the opening ceremony. Vice Chairman of the China Artists Association Wu Changjiang and Director of the Gansu Provincial Literature and History Research Institute Shui Tianchang also sent congratulatory telegrams.

Shao Dapian and Shui Tianzhong are famous theorists in the field of contemporary Chinese art theory. For decades, they have been active in the forefront of art theory research and art criticism. Their writings, which possess historical insight and profound thought, have had an important impact on contemporary Chinese art. However, as scholars, apart from engaging in art theory research and art criticism, they also ventured into practice, using painting to cultivate their minds and broaden their horizons, creating many outstanding works of art that transcend the mundane. This exhibition gathered 67 carefully created paintings by Shao Dapian and Shui Tianzhong over the past few years, including 38 ink paintings by Shao Dapian and 29 oil paintings by Shui Tianzhong. The works reflect the basic appearance of the two gentlemen's painting creation practices during their leisure time. In recent years, it has been a unique and refreshing exhibition in the Chinese art world, serving as a strong rebuttal to the current trend in the Chinese art circle of greed for fame and profit while neglecting sentiment, valuing technology while ignoring scholarship. This exhibition has important academic value and far-reaching historical significance for the construction of contemporary Chinese elite culture.

Honorary Chairman of the China Artists Association Jin Shangyi sincerely praised: The contemporary art world especially needs painters with deep learning and sharp vision. The exhibition of these two artists stands out uniquely, allowing us to see another style that goes beyond technique and focuses on spiritual expression, making it a very academically valuable exhibition. Chairman of the China Artists Association Liu Dawei represented the China Artists Association in offering warm and sincere congratulations to the exhibition, believing that the achievements of these two artists in art theory have made outstanding contributions to the brilliance of New China's art and the construction and development of Chinese art theory.
