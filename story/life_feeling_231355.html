QQ Zone emotional diary: ．．．The woman in between．．．

by lwf225 on 2009-08-31 18:20:13

{August. Pitiful upon meeting.}

I've never really liked the hot month of August. It's as if I have an innate fear of it. I stay at home for long periods of time, rarely going out. Life is ultimately monotonous and uneventful. So, I give myself more excitement. For example, cold and spicy food. The nerves on my taste buds no longer lie dormant; they begin to awaken.

I haven't seen many people again. Missing opportunities means just that—missing them.

B said, "You should see your old classmates more often. If everyone acted like you, how could any friendship survive?" I smiled faintly. All this emotion—it’s too much for me to bear. I can't handle it or repay it. My meager affection is only given to those who need me. And those fleeting passersby are nothing but beautiful illusions, destined to be transient visitors. They occasionally surface, leaving a fleeting impression.

Forgive me, I am but a small woman, guarding my humble love, giving what little warmth I can.

{Love. A facade.}

I repeatedly listened to "THE ROSE." A rose appeared before my eyes. A deep blue sky, emerald green earth, white fences—only it bloomed with such decadence, transforming into a light and graceful sprite.

Love, is the seed of our union alone, which in spring, becomes a rose.

All of his past has become traces fading in my mind. A period of guilt and self-reproach had plunged me into a great whirlpool. When all the ambiguities were completely exposed under the fierce sunlight, I realized that some nameless loves are nothing but facades. No blame, no resentment. In the world of love, there is no right or wrong. This is the natural truth.

As for now, I'm content with everything. He, even if he is also a facade, I would willingly indulge in it. Brilliant as fireworks, this is the essence of love.

{He/She. Intertwined to death.}

I just want to say a few words to a few people.

Pao, if we meet again, it will be after many years. I know, the one who protects me most is you, and the one who blesses me most is also you. Please remember, I will always remember you. Even if we get lost in the crowd, I will still recognize you at a glance.

Zihao, the man I feel sorry for. The world is fair. When you bravely choose a life entirely different from the past, God will surely open a window for you. Please don't bury yourself. You can do it. Stand up bravely and believe in yourself.

Jiao, Fangzi, Chao, Qiao, Liu, Ru, Y. Because of that day's arrival, we had the chance to meet. Some things, once carved, cannot be erased. Like our stories.

Xiaojie, I love you. I say I love you. When I see a good man return with exhaustion to the warm embrace, I start to let go of my long-standing and intense longing, heartache, and reluctance for you.

Qin, march forward proudly. Your world can be very wonderful. Darkness no longer exists. Those people, events, and objects from the past are just that—past. Don't let them entangle you anymore. You should travel lightly.

Regarding countless hims and hers, tangled in the rights and wrongs here. Only a few remain deeply in my heart.

{Departure. Without saying BYE.}

Whether in reality or fantasy, people leave one after another. That's how this world works. People gather and disperse, moons wax and wane, skies brighten and darken.

I always keep smiling.

People often say that my writings carry sadness. But who can be truly worry-free? I don't want to accumulate sorrow in my heart, so I release it through words. Life gives us the right to guess, and we must explore moving forward. Joy or sorrow, these are insights into life.

Please believe me, I am happy. Even with farewells, it's the same.

The road is long. We must learn not to say BYE when we leave. This is a promise to ourselves and a sincere response to the other party.

We will meet again.

{Coldness at high places, stop upon landing.}

I am always an observer. Not participating. Not discussing. But not unhappy. I watch them, whether happy or sad. Days pass thus, fresh and clear, as if I were among them. This is an environment full of seductive charm. We are constantly drawn. I am not afraid because I have yet to see.

There was a little girl. She called me Big Sister. Before that, we weren't familiar. Then, she told me her story. She trusted me. I knew my existence had gained some value. But, Little Girl, are you okay? Can your smile continue?

I met a woman. She lacked security. But I liked her. I liked her personality. She was bold, direct, and openly showed her sharpness. But, we were just passersby, unable to become friends. This was my choice, and hers too. We both understood clearly.

One day, I was questioned by someone, which chilled my heart. Understanding me isn't easy. Though we were friends, in the end, we couldn't continue. Well, then it doesn't matter anymore.

I am no longer affected by anyone. I write on my own, letting it settle. Upon reflection, there are always some words that need to be secretly conveyed, like a black box hiding secrets.

I changed the profile picture, the background, and the music and story accompanying the narration. I always strive for perfection when creating something myself. Or perhaps, only things made by oneself can satisfy one's thoughts. Standing out uniquely, favored above all others. Adding favorite phrases on top, making me believe that such stories will eventually happen.

{Settling Youth, Losing It Calmly.}

Some things must be detached. Some things must be taken seriously.

Regarding the matter of changing my name, I've been hesitating, reluctant to let go. However, one day I suddenly realized that many things have turned superficial, pursuing a simple kind of freedom. No restraints.

I deleted many articles, like deleting many past memories. But it's not completely erased; I always keep them in the recycle bin. I want to find references when reminiscing.

I said, "I'm really getting old, so I want something to remember." Yes, I'm old. I tell myself this.

Therefore, changing my name has become a necessity for me.

From this moment on, Nian is Qi Mi. Qi Mi is Nian.

Nian has shaped Qi Mi. Or, Qi Mi has completed Nian.

All the prosperity, adorned with splendor.

I've gotten used to putting this gratitude at the end, inconspicuous, yet never to be overlooked. It fills my heart with warmth, allowing me to type out a lot of beautiful or harsh words.

Love continues amidst spreading warfare. Thanks again to a certain man. You've always been there, steadfast and unwavering in your waiting and protection.

Moving forward hand in hand. Originally one entity. His and her times of prosperity will forever remain.
