National security is treated like Olympic champions, fans喊Li Zhangshu on the spot

by qinglai888 on 2009-12-15 12:13:14

The treatment of National Security being received is on par with Olympic champions as fans call out Lee Chang-soo's name on-site

According to reports, last night at the National Stadium, with the team song "National Security Always Strives for First" ringing out once again, a series of celebratory activities following Beijing Guoan Football Club's first league championship came to an end. This morning, the team will fly back to Kunming to prepare for the YOYO test on December 26th. Although the "National Security Forever Thanks You" thank-you evening lasted two hours, all the real highlights were created by the Guoan players, leaving other big-name stars immediately overshadowed in comparison.

The performance began at 7:30 PM. After a dance routine, the appearance of the Guoan players sparked the first climax. After all the players came out, team captain Xu Yunlong held the "Fire God Cup" and walked onto the stage shaped like the "Guoan emblem," standing on the golden star when Xu Yunlong's face broke into a smile. "That moment felt just like we had just won the championship, honestly, it was really exciting." Xu Yunlong then passed the trophy around to his teammates one by one, who all raised the golden cup high to salute the audience on-site. The fans from the Guoan Fans Association sat in the upper stands opposite the VIP box, their chants of "Guoan are champions" turning the National Stadium into the Workers' Stadium.

Compared to the appearance of the Guoan players, Wei Wei's subsequent performance not only failed to elicit applause from the audience but even caused some awkward silences. Even Wei Wei's extensive stage experience couldn't fully resolve the cold atmosphere. In the subsequent performances, the actors all pulled out their best tricks, yet the on-site fans still weren't fully engaged. It wasn't until Xu Yunlong, Du Wenhui, Wang Changqing, Yan Xiangchuan, and other players performed "True Hero" live that the second climax was reached. Before Yu Quan sang "The Most Beautiful," some fans went up on stage and presented the team with a trophy made of premium chocolate, also eliciting strong applause from the on-site audience.

At 9:40 PM, Song Zuying's "Good Days" brought the evening to its conclusion. In the final stages, the Guoan players once again walked onto the stage waving goodbye to the fans, bringing the performance to its final climax. "We've seen plenty of celebrity performances, but we prefer the Guoan players." As one fan said while walking away: "Actually, just listening to the players chat with us would be great. Such opportunities are truly rare."

The three national team players Yang Hao, Yang Zhi, and Huang Bowen, who did not attend the event on-site, sent faxes expressing their gratitude to the fans. After the event, Guoan's championship celebration activities officially came to an end. Meanwhile, Tao Wei, Yang Pu, and seven other players formally bid farewell to their identities as Guoan players and will face the Guoan team in their new roles next season.

■ Captain's Performance

Captain Xu Yunlong Becomes Top Star

Xu Yunlong Delivers Stand-up Comedy On-site

Guoan captain Xu Yunlong was undoubtedly the most dazzling figure at last night's gala. Not only did he appear last holding the "Fire God Cup" and lead the singing of "True Hero," but he also delivered a segment of "stand-up comedy" on-site.

The gala arranged for an interactive segment between a football-loving family and the Guoan team, with Xu Yunlong representing the team on stage. As soon as he stepped onto the stage, he was embraced by the third-generation female fan of the family. "No problem, I'm married," Xu Yunlong's comment elicited laughter from the on-site fans.

When the father of the fan family spoke on behalf of his family and mentioned hoping that "Xu Yunlong plays for another 20 years," Xu Yunlong, holding the microphone, said: "I'll do my best; let's see how long I can hold out." The players and Hong Yuanshu, sitting below the stage, burst out laughing after hearing this.

During the closing moments of the interactive segment, Xu Yunlong shared his feelings on-site, saying: "I was chatting with my roommate Yang Pu and asked him if there were times he wanted to cry. Yang Pu said he cried when he saw TV footage of the fans. Not just him, all of us teammates have similar feelings. We really thank all the fans." After these words, the venue once again erupted with chants of "Guoan are champions."

After stepping off the stage, Xu Yunlong immediately handed the chocolate trophy from the football-loving family to head coach Hong Yuanshu. "All those words were my true feelings at the time; it's really not easy for the fans," Xu Yunlong said.

■ Leadership Concerns

Beijing Municipal Government Receives the Team

Guoan Team's Treatment Comparable to Olympic Athletes

Before attending the "thank-you evening," Beijing Guoan players first visited the Beijing Municipal Government where they were received by the city's Party Secretary Liu Qi and other municipal leaders. The location was on the second floor of the Beijing Municipal Government office building, the same place where municipal leaders previously received Beijing athletes participating in the Olympics.

The reception began around 5:15 PM and lasted nearly 30 minutes. Led by Beijing's Party Secretary Liu Qi, Mayor Guo Jinlong, Vice Mayors Cai Fuchao, Ji Lin, Liu Jingmin, and Huang Wei comforted all members of the Beijing Guoan team. Also present at the event were Sun Kanglin, Director of the Beijing Sports Bureau, Li Shilin, Chairman of CITIC Guoan Group, General Manager Sun Yali, Deputy Chairman Luo Ning, as well as Beijing Guoan Football Club General Manager Li Xiaoming and Deputy Chairman Zhang Lu.

After speeches by Sun Kanglin and Li Shilin, Liu Qi gave the concluding speech on behalf of the Beijing Municipal Government. Besides encouraging Guoan to continue promoting the spirit of "always striving for first," he also expressed hopes for vigorously developing youth football and doing well in nurturing future talent.

■ Celebrity Support

Jiang Kun Jokes About Three Coaches Praising Fans

Lee Chang-soo Returns to Korea Today

In last night's performance, the actors not only liked football but were also mostly Guoan enthusiasts. Among them, Jiang Kun and Dai Zhicheng performed the crosstalk piece "Happy Outside," where Jiang Kun's question "Who has the greatest ability in the Guoan team?" resonated the most.

Dai Zhicheng successively threw out the names of three coaches: Jin Zhiyang, Lee Chang-soo, and Hong Yuanshu. But all were denied one by one by Jiang Kun, with reasons being: Jin Zhiyang didn't win the championship; Lee Chang-soo was dismissed midway; Hong Yuanshu became a coach too late. The final answer given was: the fans have the greatest ability, having supported Guoan through rain or shine for 16 years.

Not only was Lee Chang-soo mentioned in the crosstalk, but his image also appeared multiple times on the large screen on-site, with some fans shouting out "Lee Chang-soo's" name on-site. Lee Chang-soo will leave Beijing today and return to Korea. "Thank you all," Lee Chang-soo often says in recent interviews: "The most important thing is that the Guoan team has won the championship."
