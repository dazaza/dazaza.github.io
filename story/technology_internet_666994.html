Juventus and Milan are competing for the 19-year-old Nordic wonder striker who has scored 12 goals in 18 matches, breaking Finland's record.

by bpxwoxrt on 2012-02-16 12:45:27

On Wednesday afternoon this week, the Italian website "Football Market" revealed that Juventus and Milan have already been involved in the battle for Risqi. However, Risqi's home team, Turku from the Finnish Super League, has not yet decided to let him go. It is reported that the price of this talented player is around 1.5 million euros, making him a real potential stock. The winter transfer market, which will soon open, has drawn attention to the futures of big-name stars such as Benzema, Dzeko, and Suarez. Meanwhile, some young talents with exceptional skills are also sought after by various major clubs, even though the competition for these talents mostly remains at the level of covert warfare, unlike the heart-stirring drama surrounding established superstars. Recently, a new star in the Finnish football scene named Ruup-Risqi has caught the attention of Serie A giants Juventus and Milan. At this moment, the former Holy Alliance both have needs in their forward lines: Juventus wants to find a replacement for Amauri, and the aging of the team's forward line urgently requires fresh blood; AC Milan is searching for a new spearhead to fill the void left by Inzaghi. And now, the rookie king of Finnish football, Risqi, has entered the sights of both teams simultaneously. Since his first participation in the Finnish Super League on August 22, Risqi has started 12 times and contributed an astonishing 11 goals, a goal-scoring rate unmatched in the Finnish Super League. On September 22, 2010, Risqi scored a hat-trick again in the Finnish Super League match against AC Oulu. Thus, Risqi created a record unprecedented in Finnish football, becoming the first player to score a hat-trick in three competitions - the Finnish Super League, the Finnish First Division, and the Finnish League Cup - in a single season. Up to now in the 2010 Finnish Super League, the 19-year-old Risqi has played 18 games for Turku, scoring 12 goals, with a quite astonishing goal-scoring rate. Now, this newly emerged star has been labeled as the new Forssel by the Finnish media. As we all know, Forssel, who once played for Chelsea, is a famous striker in Finnish football. Interestingly, Risqi's older brother by two years, Riku Risqi, also plays for Turku. The midfield genius is equally talented and is often compared to Finnish legend Litmanen. It is reported that Serie A promoted team Cesena is interested in inviting the 21-year-old Riku Risqi to join. Who is Ruup-Risqi? Born in 1991, he just entered professional football in 2010 and quickly rose to fame like a rocket. In the league cup game against MyPa in 2010, Risqi performed brilliantly, scoring a hat-trick and beginning to shine. In May this year, he was loaned to Viikingit, a Finnish first division (the second-tier league in Finland, with the top league being the Finnish Super League) team, and Risqi shone again, scoring a hat-trick in the game against FC Popa. After witnessing Risqi's outstanding performance away from home, Turku quickly recalled Risqi and entrusted him with important responsibilities.
