Plot Summary: End of the Century Rebellion

In the process of human society gradually evolving towards a higher civilization, there are always many dangerous variables within us humans... At the end of the 21st century, some humans underwent genetic mutations due to radiation infections, transforming into super-speed, high-endurance powerful radiants. With the increase in infected mutants, the government became extremely panicked, fearing that these mutants would gather and form anti-government forces. Thus, they implemented isolation measures by force, conducting inhumane experiments on the mutants, followed by extermination policies, which also led to racial conflicts and imminent resistance.

A highly skilled mutant warrior, Violet (played by Milla Jovovich), steps forward to fight fire with fire, acting like a swift killing machine aimed at opposing the extermination policy strongly promoted by government official Daxus. The key to the entire extermination plan lies in a mysterious time bomb designed by the government. Finding and destroying this time bomb becomes Violet's core mission. With the help of mutant Garth, Violet is shocked to discover that a young boy, Six (played by Cameron Bright), who grew up in the laboratory, is actually this deadly time bomb. Out of maternal instinct, Violet decides to take the little boy along and go on the run for survival...
