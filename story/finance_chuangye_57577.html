Dear netizens, after a long time of planning and preparation, the Socks Affinity Forum is about to open to the public. We are now urgently recruiting talents with forum management experience. There are more than ten moderator positions waiting for you! Talented you are welcome to join our team! If you are interested in participating in the construction of our forum and serving as a moderator, please specify which section you would like to apply for. The Socks Affinity Forum will be more wonderful with your participation!

[About Moderators]

Dear netizens who aspire to be moderators: A moderator is the foundation of a section, and we have always believed this. Please carefully consider your dedication and ability. Dedication refers to the heart to do things, and ability refers to the capability to accomplish tasks. "The heart to do things" is the most important condition for being a moderator! If you just want to be a moderator for the sake of it without the intention to fulfill the responsibilities of a moderator, then we hope you don't apply and give the opportunity to those who are truly dedicated to doing the job! As for ability, it refers to "the capability to do things". In fact, being a moderator doesn't require any special skills. Of course, having a good understanding of the board is necessary. Besides that, what's needed is time! Being a moderator requires a significant amount of time spent on the site. If your time is insufficient, even if you have the heart, how can you do a good job as a moderator? Therefore, before you apply, please carefully consider this issue. If you have both the heart and the ability, we sincerely welcome you to join the ranks of the 'sock' Socks Affinity moderators!

[Application Qualifications]

Please go to the community - modify personal information first. If your personal information is incomplete or incorrect, we will not process your application. As long as you meet the qualifications for applying as a moderator, please fill out the "Moderator Application Form" in this section. After completing the relevant information, you can apply. The review time by the station is approximately 1-7 days.

-Moderator Application Qualifications-

1. Passed the general certification of this station.

2. Registered on the 'sock' forum for at least one month.

3. Published more than 3 good articles on the board (please provide article links).

4. Each person is limited to applying for two board moderators.

5. Community points exceed 100 points; Children under 12 years old are not recommended to apply for moderator positions for the sake of their physical and mental development and maturity. Teenagers aged 12-18 should inform their parents (guardians) to read and agree to the application content when applying for moderator positions. Upon submitting the application, the station assumes that the applicant has obtained the understanding and agreement of the parents (guardians) to serve as a moderator.

※Note: Those who have been banned from posting for more than a week (excluding a week), within three months from the date of lifting the ban, shall not apply for any moderator positions; those found to deliberately flood posts will be permanently disqualified from applying for moderator positions after an announcement; the initiator of a new board will serve as the first moderator; each board can have up to 4 moderators.

★Each person can only serve as a moderator for two boards. Review time: 1-7 days to complete, the length of time will vary depending on your application conditions, please wait patiently.

[Moderator Rights and Responsibilities]

Moderators need to pay attention to the rights, obligations, and other related matters concerning moderators, while also keeping an eye on the matters in the board management area and cooperating accordingly. If users post articles that violate the regulations of the 'sock' Socks Affinity, the moderator has the right and obligation to delete them. Whenever there is a verbal conflict among users on the board, the moderator has the responsibility to mediate disputes impartially. If the user still refuses to listen to advice, the administrator can be notified to assist in handling the situation. Moderators should promote discussions on the board and try to frequently post articles or answer questions related to the board content, establishing the style and theme of the discussion area. Moderators should mark excellent articles on the board and include the articles and answers to common questions in the highlights section for the convenience of board friends to query relevant information. If there is an argument within the board, the moderator should take on the task of coordinating. If the discussion area encounters users arguing irrationally, the moderator must actively intervene to maintain proper discourse in the discussion area. If there is deliberate flooding on the board, the moderator should actively dissuade and delete the flooding posts, otherwise it will be considered as failing to perform duties. If the moderator participates in flooding or uses moderator privileges to achieve their purposes, it will be regarded as serious dereliction of duty and subject to criticism until the removal of privileges.

[Moderator Duties]

Moderator work and permissions: Delete useless posts, set highlight posts, create a discussion atmosphere on the board, submit accounts that need to be banned to the administrator. The deletion criteria should be grasped by the moderators of the board. In principle, board management should be handled strictly by the moderators.

[After Moderator Appointment]

Moderators should publish posts to announce their governance plans and board development plans; they should be able to describe them specifically and in detail; the moderator's governance plan must be published within the corresponding board and remain pinned until the moderator resigns. Moderators must manage the board with sufficient time.

[Process for Applying as a Moderator] Confirm application qualifications - fill out the application form and publish it - current moderators and administrators review - probationary moderator - board affairs review whether qualified to become a full-time moderator - transfer to formal moderator. The difference between probationary and full-time moderator permissions: cannot ban IDs or modify today's topics. After one month of probation, submit an application along with work insights, and upon approval, the probationary moderator will become a formal moderator.

[Moderator Application Form Template] Instructions: The application form should be titled "[BBBB] xxx applies for YYY board moderator," where BBBB is the name of the major section, xxx is the applicant's ID, and YYY is the name of the applied-for board. Example: [Socks Forum] 'Bug' applies for [Nylon Charm] moderator, [Socks Forum] Bug applies for [Administrator Home] moderator. The moderator application form should be filled out in detail, and applications not filled out seriously will not be approved.

Any application that does not conform to the above format will be considered invalid, and the administrator will not process it.
