Internet users are expected to download a movie in 10 minutes

"We will invest 1 billion yuan to transform the CDMA network in Wuhan." Yesterday, Wuhan Telecom said that Wuhan, as one of the first 34 pilot cities in China Telecom's C network upgrade, invested 80 billion yuan in China Telecom's C network upgrade, Wuhan Telecom will invest another 1 billion yuan to optimize and upgrade the CDMA network. According to reports, the current CDMA expansion and upgrade project has been gradually launched. With the gradual installation of new and expanded equipment, some of the previous outdoor and indoor signal blind spots will expand the overall coverage of the signal, further improve the connection rate and reduce the call drop rate. It is revealed that the upgraded C network wireless mobile broadband will be nearly 20 times more bandwidth than the original, directly upgraded from more than 100 KB to 3M, and now the ADSL launched by Wuhan Telecom has a comparison. After upgrading the existing CDMA2.5G network directly to a 3G to 4G transition network, CDMA users may not have to spend a few hours to download a 700 Megabyte DVD movie through their mobile phones, but only 10 minutes. "Wuhan Telecom will complete the construction of more than 1,000 base stations and the upgrading of nearly 400 original C network equipment around November 20." According to the relevant person in charge of Wuhan Telecom, Wuhan Telecom's transformation and upgrading of the C network began as early as September this year, and it is expected that the comprehensive upgrading and transformation of the C network will be achieved before the end of the year.

Segment 189 is quasi-3G

After the completion of the comprehensive transformation of the C network, Wuhan Telecom will launch the "Tianyi" No. 189 segment to create a new generation of Internet mobile communication "Internet mobile phone". What are the features of the new "Internet phone"? How is it different from the phones we use today? Yesterday, the relevant person of Wuhan Telecom revealed that the "Internet mobile phone" is a combination of voice calls, PDAs and office tools, equivalent to access to high-speed mobile broadband PDAs, play the integration advantages of mobile Internet, maintain high-speed access anytime, anywhere, to ensure that through mobile phones, anytime, anywhere to enjoy high-speed Internet surfing. According to reports, "Tianyi" will integrate the relevant functions of mobile and fixed network to provide users with a real sense of Internet mobile phone services. It is understood that segment 189, which Telecom intends to launch within the year, has been approved by the relevant parties. People familiar with the matter said that segment 189 will be more powerful in bandwidth and data services and value-added services, closer to 3G.
