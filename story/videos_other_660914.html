A Letter to the Former Classmates of 07 Auto Class 6

Dear classmates,

How are you all? It's been over four years since we first met, and it's been two years since we graduated. How have you been during these two years?

Do you still remember how many classmates we had in our class? In the end, only about forty-plus of us graduated together. Can anyone get in touch with those who left before graduation? If so, please contact them and invite them back; after all, we were once 07 Auto Class 6.

Currently, several classmates have asked me when we will gather again. I've thought about it for a long time, and it's indeed time for us to meet up!

Dear friends, how are you all doing? We miss everyone very much. Therefore, we are organizing this reunion for the classmates of 07 Auto Class 6. The specific arrangements are as follows:

Meeting location: Maoming

Gathering location: Maoming

Initiator: Alumni Association

Date of gathering: To be determined

Contact person for registration: Songfei Li, Zongfu Chen

Duration: To be determined according to different activities...

Participants: Classmates, voluntary participation is encouraged. We really miss you all!

Costs: To be determined

Sponsorship: None at present

Activities: Group photos, etc.

Start date for registration: December 2011

End date for registration: January 21, 2012

This gathering is not the beginning, nor is the dispersal the end. The countless wonderful moments from our years as classmates will forever be etched in our memories...

No matter how wealthy or how difficult life becomes, we were once classmates.

We hope that on our journey through life, we can watch out for each other and maintain everlasting friendship!

~Classmates, if you see this message, please share it~

Let all our classmates see it~

We hope there will be more opportunities for us to gather together~
