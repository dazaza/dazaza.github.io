Villagers net more than 20,000 pounds of dead fish from their own pond, suspected of being poisoned by others

by iface001 on 2011-03-24 11:09:24

The owner is puzzled: was the fish pond poisoned?

(Editor: SN021)

According to Tang Chunhua's introduction: In 2002, he and his wife brought up three children, borrowed more than ten thousand yuan to contract a 67 mu fishpond of Yintang Village and Yan Tang Village. They worked hard for nine years. No matter it was spring, summer, autumn or winter, windy or rainy days, they lived in the wooden house by the fishpond. No matter how tired or how bitter, this man in his thirties said that as long as there was a dream and willing to work hard, life would be hopeful. Now seeing the happiness harvested by the family of five is coming, everything destroyed all their dreams. After the dead fish were fished ashore, there were fish vendors who came to buy these dead fish, used for smoking dried fish. Tang Chunhua rejected them. Others made me bankrupt. I would rather be poor, but I will not earn the black-hearted money. It is estimated that the economic loss reached more than 200,000 yuan. Currently, Tang Chunhua's family needs urgent assistance.

At 8 o'clock on the morning of March 19th, Tang Chunhua of Yantang Village, Yintian Town, Hunan Province found many small dead fish in the fishpond after getting up. When Tang Chunhua just finished fishing the dead fish, he saw big fish began to jump around. Instantly the water in the fishpond became muddy. Within two hours, the fish in the fishpond began to die in large areas. The dead fish floated on the water surface one after another. Tang Chunhua with red eyes said that the dead fish in the fishpond seemed to be endless. In the following few days, those bigger fish in the fishpond also died out one after another for unknown reasons. After taking stock of the fished dead fish, the total amount of dead fish reached more than 30,000 catties. The species of dead fish included common domestic fish such as grass carp, male fish, crucian carp, as well as precious species such as perch and mandarin fish.

The dead fish fished by Tang Chunhua

The whole family of Tang Chunhua felt painful when looking at the fished dead fish

According to Tang Chunhua: After the Spring Festival in 2010, someone reminded him to pay attention, saying that someone might poison his fish. Because of the mysterious mass death of fish in the fishpond, Tang Chunhua has reported the case to the police station of Yintian Town. Subsequently, the reporter learned from the town government and police station of Yintian that the case had been accepted, relevant departments have already sampled for testing, whether it was poisoned or not, currently waiting for the test results to determine.

More than 20,000 catties of dead fish floated on the water surface

[Voice of Hunan of China Network] Xiangyang Message: (Reporters Ge Qiwei and Li Zhong) On March 19th, Tang Chunhua of Yantang Village, Yintian Town, Changning City, Hunan Province found a large number of dead fish floating on the surface of his own fishpond. After carefully observing the situation of the fishpond, Tang Chunhua suspected that the fish in the fishpond were poisoned. Currently, Tang Chunhua has reported the case, and the local police have participated in the investigation. By this morning, Tang Chunhua has fished more than 20,000 catties of dead fish, with serious losses.
