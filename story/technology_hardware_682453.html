Emerson Network Power has launched a new 100W LED lighting product.

by ledlamp8 on 2012-02-28 22:31:08

November 18, 2011 - China News - Emerson Network Power (Emerson Network Power) is a business unit of Emerson Group (NYSE: EMR), which has been a global leader in the technology of full protection for critical business (Business-Critical Continuity™). The company announced the launch of a series of high-efficiency power supplies for LED lighting systems. The new model LDS100 LED lighting power supply offers three different output powers: 25W, 70W, and 100W to choose from. LED bulb lamp manufacturers, OEM manufacturers, and installation contractors can easily select the most suitable quality and reliable power supply according to the actual requirements of various applications.

The newly launched LDS100 series 100W LED power supply by Emerson Network Power provides several different product types to choose from. The H Model is for 120V-277V AC input voltage, while the U model applies to 100V-240V AC input voltage. The early launch models have an output DC voltage of 24V and 4.1A DC current, complying with Class 2 output regulations. Additionally, there are two modes to choose from: constant current output and constant voltage output, allowing customers to simply use one product to meet the requirements of different LED lighting systems, thereby helping reduce the inventory levels of customer companies. In constant-voltage mode, the operating range covers no-load to full load conditions. Taking the LDS100-24-H03 model as an example, this product has set a current limit function, whose limiting value can be set through the interface according to IEC 60929 regulations. In other words, controlling the output current using a standard signal of 0 to 10V regulates brightness. Furthermore, setting the current limit through an adjustable resistor facilitates the default value for system design engineers.

All models of the Emerson Network Power LDS100 power series have an active power factor correction function with a rating of not less than 0.9. Thus, they comply with the provisions of the U.S. Environmental Protection Agency and align with the harmonic limits of IEC 61000-3-2 Class C (Class C). The power conversion efficiency is also very high; for instance, under a nominal AC input voltage of 230V, the efficiency can reach up to 90% (typical). Such high efficiency further reduces the user’s electricity costs. If the rated power is considered, this power supply maintains the chassis temperature dropped to -40 degrees Celsius or increased to 90 degrees Celsius without the aid of a cooling fan to ensure that the power itself can still work within such a broad temperature range. Moreover, this series of power supplies complies with IP67 waterproof ratings, meaning it will not be damaged when immersed in shallow water, making it ideal for outdoor equipment and indoor working environment instrument installations.

The series of power supplies feature compact, slim designs measuring only 8.5 x 1.65 inches (215 x 42mm) with a thickness of 1.2 inches (30mm), enabling easy installation in extremely limited spaces for next-generation LED lighting systems. Besides meeting the electromagnetic interference requirements of the CISPR 15 standard, the LDS100 series also complies with section 15 of the Federal Communications Commission (FCC) conducted noise regulations to ensure these power systems do not experience short-circuit, overvoltage, or overheating situations. Additionally, the LDS100 series power supply has successfully passed tests proving its anti-surge interference meets the IEC 61000-4-5 standard installation related to Class 3 (U models) and Class 4 (H models) specifications.

The LDS100 series of power supplies meets more stringent requirements for product safety standards, including EN 61347-2-13 and UL 8750, among other LED lighting system safety requirements. Moreover, this series of products also meets the requirements of CSA C22.2 No. 107.1 guidelines and CE LVD. The LDS100 design platform offers high flexibility, enabling customers to quickly make appropriate design revisions according to individual applications. The Emerson Network Power LDS100 Series 100W LED power supply is now available in volume.

For further information, please visit the product information within the LDS100 series online database. Additionally, Emerson Network Power provides high-resolution images of the new LDS100 power supply related to this series for download.
