Godaddy launches a major New Year's Day promotional offensive, with both domain names and virtual hosting participating.

by xwkj0983 on 2012-02-22 14:37:58

Following the Christmas promotion, GoDaddy, the world's largest domain registrar and web hosting provider, launched a new round of aggressive discounts in preparation for the New Year. The prices for web hosting and domain services have been significantly reduced. Although GoDaddy has always been known for offering great value for money with frequent promotional activities, this is the first time they have implemented such large-scale and substantial price cuts.

### A Variety of Domain Products

GoDaddy’s current promotion features a wide range of domain products, including popular top-level domains (TLDs) and less mainstream ones. According to available information, the TLDs .COM, .ORG, and .CO are now priced at just $5.99, $6.99, and $13.99 respectively. Users can access these discounted prices through special links: www.gd5.org, www.gdoff.org, and www.gdoff.co. While all three domains offer significant discounts, the most impressive deal seems to be for the .ME domain. Through the link www.gdoff.me, users can purchase a .ME domain for only $7.99, saving $12 compared to previous prices. The .ME domain is both novel and unique, making it an excellent choice for companies looking to create personalized domain names. Additionally, GoDaddy has introduced the highly debated .XXX domain, specifically designed for adult websites. Users can enjoy a 10% discount by purchasing through the link www.gdxoff.com. This extensive range of domain promotions not only provides users with more choices but also enhances their overall purchasing experience.

### Up to 30% Off on Web Hosting Products

To ensure that web hosting users also benefit from the New Year festivities, GoDaddy has provided a special promotional link, www.gd30off.com, where users can enjoy up to 30% off when purchasing annual hosting plans. Among the various hosting options, the 4GH annual plan offers the best value. By using this promotional link, users purchasing the 4GH annual hosting plan can receive an additional 30% discount on top of the existing price reduction. After applying the discount, a deluxe hosting plan with 150GB of storage will only cost $338 per year. For business website users, GoDaddy also offers SSL certificate discounts. Users can purchase SSL certificates for only $12.99 via the link www.gdssl.org and will receive a free dedicated IP address as part of the deal. GoDaddy's hosting products have always been praised for their high cost-effectiveness, and this latest round of discounts is undoubtedly a significant reward for virtual hosting users.

### Preparing for the New Year Battle

As the New Year approaches, merchants are gearing up for another round of competitive pricing. The hosting and domain market is bound to see another fierce battle. During this period, some smaller hosting providers may take advantage of the situation by offering low prices to attract users to buy inferior products. To avoid being scammed, users should remain vigilant and preferably choose reputable, well-known virtual hosting providers. Doing so ensures that users don’t miss out on genuine opportunities to purchase quality services at great prices.
