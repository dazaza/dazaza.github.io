Plot Summary

Two worlds, one war - the ultimate battle begins. On the wild and primitive American continent where Columbus has yet to set foot, an epic war of love and hatred unfolds, a war that will change the fate of a man and even a nation.

Pathfinder tells the story of a survivor, the protagonist being a young pirate (Karl Urban). The dragon-headed pirate ship sails out of the mysterious Norwegian mainland, entering the virgin land untouched by outsiders - the American continent. To plunder slaves, the greedy Norwegian pirates attack a village near the coast of the American continent. However, during their triumphant return, the pirate ship encounters a disaster at sea, leaving the young pirate as the sole survivor.

Despite his golden hair and strange language, despite the suspicion that this 10-year-old boy was followed by devils wherever he went, the local Wampanoag people (a tribe of the Algonquian-speaking Native Americans, later relocated to southeastern Massachusetts) took him in and raised him into an exceptional hunter and warrior. Fifteen years later, the young pirate had grown into a strong youth, but this white-skinned young man was seen as a ghost by his tribe. To escape his awkward situation, he constantly sought ways to flee from his past. Unexpectedly, the fate of "Ghost" once again became intertwined with the pirates. Greedy and cruel pirates once more targeted the American continent, this time determined to carry out a savage attack, eliminating the tribe that "Ghost" deeply loved and abducting the woman he cherished (Moon Bloodgood).

In the pirates' savage operation, "Ghost" becomes a survivor again, but this time not losing his comrades, but rather the family who had raised him. If survival last time brought helplessness, then this time it brings only pain and hatred. Revenge has become deeply ingrained, entering every cell of his body.

By chance, "Ghost" becomes the guide for the Explorer (Russell Means). Perhaps fate intentionally arranged it so that this explorer happened to be a highly respected Shaman, who foresaw the unexpected destiny of the vengeful Ghost: This rare hero in the world would launch a war of one against the beast-like pirates, using his courage and blood to become the savior of his people...

Plot Summary:

A group of mutants born with special abilities, under the call of Professor X, formed a police force to protect humanity and Earth. Unexpectedly, humans began to harbor doubts about the mutants, leading to conflicts between them...

The X-Men team: Cyclops (the captain of the X-Men, able to shoot deadly laser beams from both eyes), Jean Grey (with telepathic, telekinetic, and teleportation abilities), Storm (the deputy captain of the X-Men, possessing climate control powers), and Rogue (with the ability to absorb memories and abilities through physical contact) are the core members of the X-Men.

Now, a dangerous figure has joined the X-Men - Wolverine (possessing super recovery abilities and having adamantium claws on both wrists), a volatile mutant almost uncontrollable, creating instability within the X-Men. The X-Men let him train new recruits and wanted to recruit him into their ranks. But is this possible? No one knows his origins, and soon they discover that most of his thoughts are controlled by outsiders. Worse still, a new mutant "Magneto" has emerged, capable of controlling magnetism and Earth's gravity. He was once exhibited as a monster by humans, suffering much humiliation. He hates humans, believing that mutants are superior to humans and should dominate them. He and Wolverine collaborate without scruples to achieve this goal. Can they be stopped in time? Will they lead to the mutual destruction of humans and the X-Men?

And the X-Men, sworn to defend humanity, will find that while they try their best to protect humans, humans have also developed great fear towards them, causing tensions in their relationship. Moreover, the X-Men must face Wolverine's explosive temper and Magneto's indiscriminate killings, plunging the X-Men into multiple crises with no way out...
