XQB60-6118A 2010-03-27 Qualified National Home Appliances Quality Supervision and Inspection Center

by fjk50kl8 on 2011-09-05 14:10:32

In this inspection, a total of 69 types of home electric washing machine products produced by 69 enterprises in 11 provinces and municipalities including Tianjin, Shanghai, Jiangsu, Zhejiang, Anhui, Shandong, Henan, Hunan, Guangdong, Sichuan, and Chongqing were randomly inspected.

This inspection was based on the requirements of the standards such as "General Requirements for Safety of Household and Similar Electrical Appliances" GB 4706.1-2005, "Special Requirements for Safety of Household and Similar Electrical Appliances - Washing Machines" GB 4706.24-2008, "Special Requirements for Safety of Household and Similar Electrical Appliances - Spin Dryers" GB 4706.26-2008, "Household and Similar Electric Washing Machines" GB/T 4288-2008, "Energy Consumption Limitation and Energy Efficiency Grade of Electric Washing Machines" GB 12021.4-2004, etc., to inspect 12 items of home electric washing machine products including protection against access to live parts, input power and current, heating, leakage current and electrical strength at operating temperature, structure, grounding measures, creepage distances, electrical clearances and through-insulation distances, cleaning ratio, energy consumption, water consumption, wide voltage design, and wide water pressure design limit values.

The inspection found that 8 products did not meet the relevant standard requirements, and the unqualified items involved heating, cleaning ratio, and energy consumption. The specific inspection results are as follows:
