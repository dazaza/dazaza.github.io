"Little West Heaven" - The Emotional Growing Pains of Post-90s Boarding Art Students

by jqrxass on 2009-10-25 22:52:09

"Little West Heaven" depicts a story of the constraints and struggles between dreams and reality. Xia Yu, with a broken family, comes to a boarding school with endless gloom in his heart and a yearning for happiness. There, he befriends Tan Xi, who is devoted to pursuing music and has been wandering, and Qiang Zi, who is cynical about the world. His father's remarriage worsens his relationship with his family. Later, he falls in love with Loulan, who has always wanted to bring Xia Yu out of his dark dreams, but ends up being trapped herself. Until Han's intervention shatters the relationship between Xia Yu and Loulan. Heartbroken, Loulan decides to leave to escape the pain, where she meets the ever-wandering Tan Xi and realizes what she truly wants is love. As time passes and the group of young people gradually grow up, they experience the growing pains and transformations, and when they look back on those past events, it feels like a nightmare that has already passed. And ahead of the dream, is the relentless pursuit of ideals.

Relevant Content of Little West Heaven

In the three hours since Yi Xi sent me the document of this novel, I changed my posture, didn't even drink water, until I finished reading the novel. It seems like it's been a long time since I've felt this way. The last time was three days ago when I finished reading "The Wind-Up Bird Chronicle."

From "Floating Clouds" to "Youth" and now to "Little West Heaven," from naivety to darkness and then to light...

Chapter One of Little West Heaven

I don't know when Qiang Zi had already stood behind me. The cigarette in his hand was lit, the sea breeze blowing, making it flicker on and off, also messing up his hair.

I said, why, why does she treat me like this?

Qiang Zi didn't say anything.

I said, she disappeared without a word. When I saw her again, she was with another man, and she looked at me so innocently. Tell me why, why should I endure this!

Chapter Two of Little West Heaven

I told Loulan, I want to see real snow, strong wind-driven snow.

Loulan smiled, handed me the paintbrush, leaned her body against my back, grabbed my hand, dipped it in paint, and started painting on the paper.

The wind is deep blue, the snowflakes are deep blue, the color of the summer sea, yet it feels bone-chillingly cold.

Only then did I realize how beautiful painting could be.

After finishing the painting, Loulan held my hand and signed the author's name in the bottom right corner of the painting.

-- Loulan loves Xia Yu.

Chapter Three of Little West Heaven

Han walked up to me and gently hugged me.

Han said, Xia Yu, can you hug me?

Han's body is very petite, small enough to curl up in my arms, as if melting instantly. My heart seemed ready to jump out, filled with guilt--but the person in front of me, that gentle breath, made me so captivated.

Han murmured, before we properly feel the climate of blooming snowflakes, we embrace together, only then will we understand what gentleness is.

Tighter, just a little tighter.
