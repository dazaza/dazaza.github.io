Man chops down college girlfriend and then attempts self-mutilation

by erh65f3dfen on 2012-03-01 13:15:44

A man and a woman lay on the ground with blood streaming from their necks. A large pool of blood soaked the floor, and a knife was still lodged in the man's neck. At around 8 PM the night before last, Ms. Liang, who resides in Xicun Village of Beijiang Town, was hanging her clothes out to dry when she suddenly heard a woman from the opposite household shouting "help." Running out of her home, she saw a woman collapsed on the ground at the entrance, with a gaping wound in her neck and blood gushing out. At that moment, a man ran out of the door and fell down, with a kitchen knife still stuck in the back of his neck, also bleeding profusely. Upon seeing this, Ms. Liang quickly called 110 to report the incident and asked her family to wait by the roadside for the ambulance. Mr. Chen, who lives next door, also informed the reporter that around 8 PM, he heard a man and a woman arguing, followed by someone rushing out of the door shouting for help.

After Ah Feng and Xiao Yu were injured, the police immediately launched an investigation. The motive behind Ah Feng being slashed remains unclear, but it is estimated to be related to emotions.

Yesterday morning, when the reporter arrived at Beijiang Hospital, the couple had temporarily escaped life-threatening danger after being rescued. After being pushed out of the operating room, they were respectively taken to the neurosurgery and orthopedics intensive care units for observation and treatment. A fellow townsman and coworker of Xiao Yu introduced that the 20-year-old Xiao Yu and the 21-year-old Ah Feng are from Shangrao, Jiangxi. They were high school classmates. Ah Feng was admitted to a university in Jiangxi and is set to graduate next year. "She came to Beijiang during the Spring Festival under the guise of an internship opportunity to join Xiao Yu," said the friend.

According to reports from Foshan (by reporter Lun Shaobin), due to an argument, the "post-90s" male swung a kitchen knife fiercely towards his girlfriend's throat, who is still in university. He slashed her neck four times and then turned the knife on himself, planning to die together. Last night, this horrifying scene occurred in Xicun Village, Beijiang Town, Shunde District, Foshan City. Currently, both are temporarily out of life-threatening danger.
