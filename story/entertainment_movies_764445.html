Synopsis:

Huang Bingyi (played by Sammo Hung), the village chief of Huangge Weicun, is a master chef who commands great respect from the villagers.

Ten years ago, his brother Huang Bingji (played by Leung Siu-lung) caused trouble while drunk, breaking one of his hands, and left the village in shame. He entrusted his son, Huang Jizu (played by Sean Lau), to be taken care of by Bingyi. However, young Jizu mistakenly believed that his uncle had driven away his father to seize the position of village chief and vowed to take revenge when he grew up. During a village wedding banquet, Jizu instructed his henchman Liang (played by Hong Tin-ming) to sabotage the food with laxatives to frame Bingyi, forcing him to leave the village.

Bingyi met Shen Qing (played by Charmaine Sheh), the owner of Si Hai Yi Pin, due to this misunderstanding. Without hesitation, Bingyi helped Si Hai Yi Pin innovate and create new dishes. His extraordinary culinary skills were showcased once again but soon attracted the challenge of a novice culinary genius, Long Jianyi (played by Vic Chou). In the end, Huang Bingyi's exquisite knife work created a dish of fresh snapper sashimi that outshone Long Jianyi's thinly sliced raw dolphin, leaving Long Jianyi impressed and eager to learn under Bingyi.

Later, upon learning that Bingyi was the head chef at Si Hai Yi Pin, Jizu began targeting the restaurant and sent people to cause repeated disturbances. To avoid involving others, Huang Bingyi decided to leave.

Soon after, a national divine chef competition was held. Long Jianyi decided to participate to restore the reputation of Si Hai Yi Pin. Huang Jizu's Yue Huang Xuan also entered the competition, sending their two-time culinary god chef, Tian Qiudao, to compete. The competition was extremely intense, showcasing the brilliance of Chinese cuisine. Before the competition, Huang Bingyi and Huang Jizu met privately, deciding to settle their grudge through martial arts.

In the final round, despite various disadvantages, Long Jianyi amazed everyone with a seemingly ordinary dish of boiled cabbage taught by Huang Bingyi, surpassing Yue Huang Xuan's Buddha Jumps Over the Wall. How will the two generations resolve their feud?
