Chapter 160: Tianyan Mountain Range

by fanyete on 2011-09-30 21:49:58

Yes, feeling the surging flow of primal energy coursing through his meridians, a deep sense of joy spread across Lin Dong's face. He could clearly feel that the primal energy condensed within the Yuan Dan in his dantian was now several times more powerful than before when he was at the Lesser Yuan Dan stage!

"Truly deserving of being at the Small Perfection stage."

Lin Dong sighed softly. Only by personally experiencing the fullness brought by the increase in his body's primal energy, would one understand the might of this realm. According to his estimation, even without deploying his mental energy, he now had more than a sixty percent chance of defeating Lin Chen.

Although this was merely the initial stage of Small Perfection, Lin Dong's Yuan Dan was an eight-star Yuan Dan. The purity of its primal energy far surpassed that of ordinary people. With this, it was not impossible for him to defeat Lin Chen who had only half-stepped into the Great Perfection stage.

"With my current strength, even if I were to encounter experts at the Great Perfection stage, I would be able to face them without any fear!"

When Lin Dong had yet to step into the Small Perfection stage, with the power of a three imprints spiritual master and the Lesser Yuan Dan stage, he was already able to contend against Yue Shan. Now that his primal energy had advanced further, his combat power naturally increased exponentially. In the future, should he encounter someone like Yue Shan again who had stepped into the Great Perfection stage, Lin Dong was confident that his opponent would no longer be able to gain any advantage over him.

He exhaled deeply, releasing a puff of white vapor as Lin Dong clenched his fists slowly. Now everything was ready, all they needed was to set off.

"This time, I'm curious to see what special qualities this ancient tomb has that have attracted so many powerful individuals!"

Ever since successfully breaking through to the Small Perfection stage, Lin Dong hadn't wandered around aimlessly anymore. Due to Qing Tan's increasingly frequent outbreaks of Yin Sha energy, most of his time was spent accompanying her, as she suffered daily from weakness caused by the Yin Sha energy.

In these few days, Lin Chen and the others who came from the Lin Clan had also stayed at the Lin family residence. However, because they had been humiliated by Lin Dong, they didn't dare to act arrogantly in front of the younger generation of the Lin family anymore. Thus, time passed peacefully and swiftly.
