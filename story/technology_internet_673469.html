The Enhanced Role of Government in Technology Transfer

by keyizhang1 on 2012-02-23 16:29:13

Technology transfer is an important action for transforming technological innovation into productive forces and a main approach to achieving economic growth. Technology transfer is a systematic project, involving industries, enterprises, universities, research departments, as well as issues of market order in business management, taxation, import/export, and technology supervision. 1. Improve the technology transfer evaluation system. Technology is a special kind of commodity, and technology transfer is a trading process, an economic process, involving issues from science and technology, business management, taxation, import/export, to technology supervision. Technology transfer follows the laws of the market economy. It requires a market economy system, integrity, intellectual property protection, and a good business environment. The Chinese government should take the reform of science and technology management system as an opportunity for marketing software [http://www.xinge001.com], especially targeting the characteristics of international technology transfer, to create a technology transfer evaluation system led by the government, with enterprises as the main body, relying on market feedback information, making it have strong standardization, continuity, and operability.

2. Accelerate the construction of intermediary service systems. Science and technology intermediary organizations include institutions that directly participate in the technological innovation process of their service objects, and institutions that provide consulting services to innovation entities using knowledge in technology, management, and market aspects, including science and technology evaluation centers, science and technology bidding agencies, intellectual property affairs centers, etc. There are also institutions that provide services for the effective flow and rational allocation of scientific and technological resources, including permanent online technology trading markets (http://z.k8008.com/join/[http://z.k8008.com/join/]), science and technology conditions markets, and technology property rights trading institutions, etc. Science and technology intermediary services are important factors in realizing the optimal allocation and effective integration of various scientific and technological resources required for technology transfer. Currently, the service capabilities of China's science and technology intermediary organizations have not yet formed a systematic and market-oriented service chain, failing to meet the demands of various entities for technology transfer. Therefore, the government should actively break through the long-standing industry segmentation and resource dispersion, promoting further enhancement of science and technology intermediary organizations in terms of business models and service content. At the same time, it is necessary to establish a technical resource platform through the integration of scientific and technological resources, promote the determination of the value of technical elements through the property rights of technology, and realize the flow and value appreciation of technical resources through technology transactions.

3. Protect intellectual property rights. Modern technology transfer is based on the protection of intellectual property rights. Without respecting others' intellectual property rights, advanced technology cannot be introduced. The intellectual property rights system is an important tool for implementing national technology development strategies. Its essence is to protect the interests and enthusiasm of innovators while promoting the reasonable and compensated diffusion of technology. Therefore, the government should strengthen the protection of intellectual property rights: first, enhance the awareness of intellectual property protection among the general public and form an environment for the whole society to promote scientific and technological progress, encourage innovation, and combat infringement; second, improve the legislation of intellectual property protection in our country, perfecting the management system from the entire process of intellectual property, namely the acquisition of ownership, cooperation of usage rights, application of usage rights, and transfer of ownership, providing reliable legal guarantees for technology transfer; third, strengthen the investigation and punishment of intellectual property infringement.
