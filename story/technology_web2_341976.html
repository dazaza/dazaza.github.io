Only by being content with what we have can we make ourselves happy-bm

by mal34jpl on 2010-04-08 09:47:07

Is there such a thing as eternal love? Definitely! Then why do the majority of people not believe it exists? Because they haven't found the one most suitable for them on their life journey - the one fated for them. Why can't they find this person? In the vast sea of humanity, life is fleeting like dew. To find the one most suitable for oneself, how simple is that?

Romance is originally a bet without a win-lose outcome. It accompanies us until the end of our lives, and only then does the conclusion of love come about. Regardless of what awaits us in the end, having love in our hearts is enough. I think cherishing the present should be more practical! Life is long, who can keep the one they love by their side forever? And who can say that losing them means losing all meaning? The outcome is not the value; the worth lies with the true masters of the romance themselves – no one has the right to judge! People! When will you wake up? When will you be content? When will you consider someone like me?

To live, one must be content! Only through contentment can satisfaction be achieved. Everything I've done is for a certain family, for all of you. But when have you ever considered me? I was very surprised. Three years ago, I watched them meet, get engaged, and marry. It seemed like everything between them was so natural. If it's really as she said, that none of that had love, then what were those moments before? They once stood proudly before everyone showing off their love. Could that not be considered love? I think that between them, it's not as she said, that there was no love. By respecting and understanding others, and always keeping a broad and grateful heart, forgiveness is a virtue, a wisdom. Just like the ocean becomes vast by accepting hundreds of rivers, thank your friends, because they have helped you; thank your enemies, because they make you stronger.

However, these years, the trivialities and pressures of life have dimmed the original brilliance of love, even making her blind to the dawn of love. Sometimes, I too feel lost about marriage, unable to see the light of love. People! As long as you pay attention every day, everything will be yours. Have you thought about it this way? No... Every day you accomplish nothing, only asking your family for money. Why don't you consider that even earning might be difficult? Everyone’s life oscillates between happiness and sorrow. If one is healthy without disease, the quality of life improves. If one doesn't encounter the dangers of war, the loneliness of prison, the torment of torture, or the taste of hunger, then one leads a happy and sorrowful life. If one enjoys four generations living together harmoniously, with a happy marriage, then one is enjoying worldly pleasures. If one possesses both spiritual richness and material abundance, life becomes even more beautiful.

People! Now, you have the responsibility to prepare yourself to be a boss. Why aren't you satisfied? Don't get married just to escape loneliness. Time is a wolf. Eternal love, the key to building a harmonious family - if you are an affectionate person, even if you don't initially love the other party, feelings may develop over time, leaving you wondering what to do in the end.

Friends are an eternal unsolvable mystery. We grow up, gain more friends, and expand our social circles. This undergoes both "qualitative" and "quantitative" changes. Some friends may be lifelong treasures, while others could become graves that bury us. Relationships must maintain a safe distance. Too far away and they become estranged or lost; too close and conflicts or scars may arise, leading to loss all the same.
