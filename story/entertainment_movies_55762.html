Meanwhile, the Japanese ronin Li Yunbao, hidden among the woodcutters, colluded with the Xia Shanhou bandit gang 500 li away and secretly plotted with the Kwantung Army's Captain Heben's squad to devise a plan against Yang Yunchang.

Along the way, to disrupt the pursuit of Yang Yunchang by Li Yunbao and Xia Shanhou, they fell into enemy hands. The woodcutter sacrificed his life falling off a cliff to save Yang Ren, while Yang Yunchang remained unaware after wiping out Captain Heben's squad.

Upon hearing the bad news, Yang Ren, Guai Wuyeh, and Yang Yunchang led all their brothers in a vengeful battle against Li Yunbao and Xia Shanhou, ultimately eliminating Li Yunbao and subduing Xia Shanhou. Once again, the Yang family brought temporary peace to the local people. However, soon after, the shocking September 18th Incident erupted, and the Japanese Kwantung Army's Shimada battalion stationed themselves in the area where the Yang family lived and operated. Major Shimada incorporated Xia Shanhou into his forces, placing the Yang family and their allies in a precarious position, vulnerable to annihilation.

Yang Ren united with his younger brother Yang Yi and together with Yang Yunchang, implemented a bold plan. Yang Yunchang pretended to be incorporated by Major Shimada, seizing the opportunity to use Shimada's hand to kill Xia Shanhou and gain control of the Chianan team. Through the joint efforts of everyone, Yang Yunchang cleverly devised a strategy, closely coordinating with Yang Ren and Yang Yi, eventually annihilating the entire Shimada battalion and openly embarking on the path of national liberation through armed resistance against the Japanese invaders.
