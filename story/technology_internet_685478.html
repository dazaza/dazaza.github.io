[Qingdao Wedding] Choosing a mainstream 50-point diamond ring for marriage, the smartest and most value-preserving choice

by qssqwe215 on 2012-03-01 15:30:58

 

This article is from Qingdao Total Search [http://www.qdqss.cn]

How big should an engagement ring be? A 50-point diamond ring has become the absolute favorite size that women hope to receive. Therefore, this week we will teach you how to choose the most mainstream 50-point diamond ring.

Smart Rule One: Do your 4C homework thoroughly to ensure efficiency.

Buying a 50-point diamond ring —— he not only needs to be mentally prepared but also have enough money in his pocket.

The price of 50-point diamond rings on the market ranges from thousands to tens of thousands of yuan. The reason for this disparity lies in the differences in the 4Cs. Diamond rings can be very expensive, but when it comes to marriage, a 50-point diamond ring with good color and overall quality generally costs around 12,000-18,000 yuan. Men might feel a bit nervous about this. If you want to buy a branded one, the price will be much higher. A regular 50-point diamond ring from Tiffany's costs around 70,000-80,000 yuan. Regardless of what style or brand you buy, you must know how to look at the 4Cs. This is extremely important!!!

Smart Rule Two: Choose affordability over brand

Blue box, white ribbon —— it's not a matter of whether you can afford it, but whether it's necessary.

Believe it or not, not all newlyweds are willing to spend nearly 100,000 yuan on a Tiffany diamond ring. For couples who still need to live on bread and butter, affordability is far more important than brand. And let me tell you, this is where the 4Cs come into play. For example, a half-carat F-color VVS2 3EX diamond ring with a simple platinum band and six-claw setting may cost several ten thousand yuan less if bought from a regular store instead of Cartier. The difference between them lies in the inner circle of the diamond ring —— which you cannot see unless it is taken off!

Smart Rule Three: Buy loose diamonds and set them —— there's no cheaper way

Unless you're a nouveau riche —— why not choose this most affordable method?

Some newlyweds may think buying finished products is more convenient. However, if I tell you the price difference between finished diamond rings and loose diamonds plus settings, I believe you won't want to waste money. Buying loose diamonds and settings is 20% or even more cheaper than buying finished diamond rings. Moreover, you can customize your own style. A unique diamond ring at an unbeatable price, why wouldn't you do it?

Smart Rule Four: Purchase in Hong Kong or overseas —— newer styles and better prices

Travel and buy a diamond ring at the same time —— absolutely a happy journey!

If you have the opportunity to go to Hong Kong or abroad before getting married, why not buy one there and bring it back? In fact, the styles of diamond rings from brands like Chow Tai Fook in mainland China have already been popular in Hong Kong. When you get to Hong Kong, you'll find that the styles there, whether it's diamond rings, platinum, or gold jewelry, are refreshing. You don't have to worry about them going out of fashion after buying them there, nor do you need to worry about cleaning and maintenance, as long as they are from chain stores. In terms of price, considering the exchange rate, Hong Kong is 20%-30% cheaper than the mainland. Recommended brands: Chow Tai Fook, Chow Sang Sang, TSL Jewellery, and Lukfook.
