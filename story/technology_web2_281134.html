Engineering pioneering

Tools used: Large vacuum cleaner, multi-functional floor cleaner, glass set tools, extension rod, ladder, bucket, duster, marble scraper, scraper, water applicator, etc.

Chemicals used: All-purpose cleaner, glass cleaner, tile cleaner, ceramic cleaner, adhesive remover, stain remover, acidic cleaner, toilet cleaner, stainless steel cleaner, stainless steel brightener, furniture wax, etc.

Pioneering procedure:

Pioneering is the first step in cleaning engineering. Since construction projects often leave behind a lot of garbage and dirt, such as cement blocks, paint, silicone sealant, water stains, rust marks on various ground stones and walls, all these must be cleaned during the pioneering work. Therefore, it is the most arduous, complex, and labor-intensive task. The quality of pioneering work directly affects the quality and class of future housekeeping tasks, so it has very important requirements.

Procedure as follows:

1. First, clear out the leftover construction waste from the site;

2. Conduct a comprehensive dusting from top to bottom;

3. Clean the glass: First, use a towel to wipe the glass frame clean, then use a water applicator with diluted glass cleaning solution to evenly coat the glass from top to bottom. Use a scraper to remove stubborn stains, repeat the process, and then scrape the glass clean from top to bottom with a squeegee. Wipe the remaining water marks on the frame with a dry towel, and use a microfiber cloth to polish the glass clean. (Qingdao Housekeeping)

4. Bathroom: Follow the principle of top-down. First, identify the material of the bathroom ceiling—whether it's PVC, aluminum plastic board, or paint—and then clean according to different materials using different cleaning methods, (Qingdao Cleaning). Use a scrub ball or brush to clean the bathroom walls, focusing on the gaps between tiles and any residual glue, paint spots, and cement stains on the tile surface. Use a towel to clean bathroom fixtures, and use stainless steel cleaning liquid for various faucets and pipe components. Finally, use a floor washer for the final cleaning of the floor, especially the edges, using a scrub ball and blade to specifically remove dirt and cement stains in areas that the floor washer cannot reach, (Qingdao Cleaning Company, our service item includes unit cleaning); After checking for any omissions, use a dry towel to wipe the faucet and other pipe components once more.

5. Kitchen: Same procedure as above. Note: Since there are more stainless steel components in the kitchen, they should be the focus of cleaning.

6. Bedroom and living room: Dust the walls with a duster or vacuum cleaner, and wipe the lights, switch boxes, exhaust vents, air conditioning outlets, exhaust vents, etc.

7. Doors and frames: Identify the material of the door, dilute professional cleaning agents, and use a towel to wipe. The procedure is also from top to bottom. Fold the towel into a square and start wiping from the top of the door from left to right without missing any spots. For places with glue stains, use a deglazing agent to handle them; the frame follows the same procedure as the door. It is essential to ensure no omissions or blind spots.

8. Floor cleaning: After completing all the previous steps, proceed with floor cleaning. Floors also need to be differentiated by material—wooden floors, tiles, or stone. Once identified, choose the appropriate specialized cleaning agent, dilute it, and begin cleaning. Floor glue stains can be removed with a blade, and stubborn ones can be handled with a deglazer, (Qingdao Cleaning, our service item includes marble renewal crystallization). After the final procedure, the supervisor should conduct a full inspection, confirm no omissions, and then withdraw from the site.

9. Baseboards: Wipe with a towel and use a blade to remove various glue stains and paint spots.

Standards:

Glass standard: Visually no water marks, handprints, stains, bright and clean.

Bathroom standard: Walls have no color difference, no obvious stains, no paint spots, no glue residue, sanitary ware is clean and shiny, stainless steel pipes are clean and shiny, floor has no blind spots, no omissions, no odors.

Kitchen standard: Walls have no color difference, no obvious stains, no paint spots, no glue residue, stainless steel pipes are clean and shiny, floor has no blind spots, no omissions.

Bedroom and living room standard: Walls have no dust, lights are clean, switch boxes are clean with no glue residue, exhaust vents, and air conditioning outlets have no dust or glue points.

Door and frame standard: No glue residue, no paint spots, smooth touch, luster, no dust on the edges.

Floor standard: Wooden floors have no glue residue, clean; Tiles have no dust, no paint spots, no cement stains, lustrous; Stones have no stains, no glue points, high gloss, (Qingdao Cleaning Company, our service item includes marble renewal crystallization).
