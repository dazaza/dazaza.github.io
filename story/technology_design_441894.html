Experts from the Laser Cosmetology Department of Huaxia Hospital in Jinan have summarized the key points as follows:

1. Stay far away from harmful cosmetics! Especially quick-effect whitening creams and fast-acting freckle-removal creams — choosing not to use these is the wisest decision! 2. Skin function exercise. It's actually very simple: wash your face alternately with cold and warm water (or use two towels dipped in cold and warm water respectively to apply them alternately to your face). Do this once in the morning and evening, each session lasting about 10 minutes, alternating around 10 times being ideal. The temperature of the cold water should generally be around 15°C, while the warm water should generally be around 45°C. This method has greatly benefited countless beauty-conscious women!

3. Regularly applying fruit face masks is not only beneficial for treating freckles but also very good for enhancing the beauty of the skin! In fact, many types of fruits are effective. Generally speaking, the richer the fruit is in vitamin C, the more potent its effect (of course, you must avoid overly stimulating fruits or those to which you're allergic). 4. Drink plenty of water (water is the best carrier for expelling toxins from the body). Eating more vegetables and fruits is of particular importance for expelling toxins from within the body, and is also very important for the care of freckles.

5. Avoid alcohol! Avoid spicy and stimulating foods! The reason is quite simple: these things easily generate internal toxins, exacerbating freckles and making them worsen dramatically!

6. Resolutely stay away from ultraviolet rays and ionizing radiation! These are among the most important reasons for worsening freckles! Because they stimulate the activity of tyrosinase, leading to excessive pigment deposition akin to a volcanic eruption! Therefore, ladies who are sensitive to UV rays should regularly use sunscreen to prevent UV radiation and care for red blood threads.

7. Supplement various vitamins. Especially vitamin C, vitamin E, and all kinds of B vitamins, along with abundant copper and zinc. These substances can promote the clearance of toxins and free radicals within the body, thus playing an active role in the care of freckles.

8. Stay away from severe environmental pollution and various harmful chemical substances!

9. Maintaining regular bowel movements is very important for the care of freckles! This is because constipation is one of the main culprits leading to the accumulation of toxins within the body!

10. Exercise more! Not only can it give you an enchanting figure, but it can also help expel various toxins through sweat, actively aiding in the care of freckles!

11. Maintain a pleasant mood, remain calm and patient, which is very important for the care of freckles.
