"Full Process Guide for Ancient Seal of Flames: Play the Game"

by akisscn on 2009-01-05 13:31:27

Chapter 1: The Gray Altar

At the beginning, you can familiarize yourself with the system controls through a tutorial. Click on the protagonist and then click where you want to go to move. If there is a chest, select しらべる (investigate) to obtain the items inside. After the tutorial ends, you can get a recovery potion from the chest above, then proceed to the left. At this point, monsters will appear along with a CG scene. Brunick, who was burned to ashes by the fire snake earlier, has resurrected. Brunick joins the party, and together they defeat the enemy. During battle, click たたかう (attack) and then click the enemy to attack them.

Mission: Obtain the Milinia Bracelet

Tip: In close-range combat, your hit rate will increase, as well as your attack power. However, the enemy's abilities will also increase, so be cautious. After moving upward for a distance, a white magician will appear (resurrect?) and join the party. He has healing magic; if you want to heal everyone during battle, just swipe over all of them. When you approach the bracelet, monsters will appear.

Mission: Defeat the Fire-Winged Beast

Pay attention to the white magician's recovery, as his HP is relatively low. Use water-element attacks for the rest. This is the first mini-boss, not very difficult.

After each chapter ends, the player's performance will be evaluated to determine a level rating, and items will be granted.

Note: The save function in this game does not clear after loading, so it can be used to save before facing bosses or strong enemies as a precaution.

After the evaluation ends, warriors, white magicians, and black magicians can be summoned later. On the world map, there are two options: one is Brunick's tutorial section, and the other is Chapter 2. Some tips when using magic: there will be a status indicator below the target character's portrait. A circle indicates high power status, a triangle-shaped exclamation mark represents slightly lower power, and a red cross means very low power. Although magic's long-range attack power decreases, the hit rate remains high (with some exceptions).

Chest Items: Ether, Potion, Potion

Chapter 2: Chasing the Flames

At the start, you need to perform summoning and team formation, which consumes ELE, obtained after previous battles. Press the summon bar on the left and select the desired class to summon. Drag the summoned class' avatar to the top of the previous screen to complete the team formation.

Mission: Defeat the Flame Snake

You can obtain 100 ELE by talking to the villager on the right. Another 100 ELE. You can get 200 ELE near the three people close to the Flame Snake. After speaking with the village chief, EX skills can be used. When the EX gauge is full, EX special moves can be used. Enemy reinforcements will also appear simultaneously.

Chest Items: Revival Medicine, Ether, Potion

It’s best to save the EX special move for use against the Flame Snake. After the EX animation ends, an "!" mark will appear above the enemy's head. Tapping it at this time will deal additional damage. The white magician's Shell magic can reduce some of the damage dealt by the boss. When the boss's HP reaches a certain level, he will use a cheating skill to fully restore his health, triggering a plot event.

After the plot, two ninjas, “Martial Artist” and “Item Specialist,” can also be summoned, and the store will be expanded. In the world map interface, the free mode of Chapter 2 is unlocked. After passing subsequent chapters, the free mode will automatically open, allowing for free leveling up.
