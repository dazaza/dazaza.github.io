Introduction to Ultimate Three Kingdoms:

Guan Yu and Zhang Fei are high school students, but due to their excessive love for chivalry, they have been expelled from twenty-four schools, known as the "twenty-four schools!" Just when the two feel desperate after being expelled again, a man with large earlobes suddenly appears. He introduces himself as Liu Bei, styled Xuande, a fallen nobleman who often changes schools because he cannot afford the tuition fees. Liu Bei proposes a plan: since he has noble bloodlines, he can attend the best noble school in the country, the "Donghan Academy," but it requires a very expensive tuition fee and only royal relatives can enroll. Zhang Fei, being wealthy, says he can pay everyone's tuition. Liu Bei says that once the money is settled, everything can be resolved. He suggests the three of them swear brotherhood so that all of them will become royal relatives and thus eligible to enroll. The three come to a beautiful and meaningful place to take the oath — but just as they finish the last sentence of the oath, "We don't seek to be born on the same day, but we wish to die on the same day," four people—Xiu, Wang Dongs, Ding Xiaoyu, and Wang Arthur—cross through a time portal from the Gold Space-Time to the Silver Space-Time. Due to Wang Dong accidentally dropping a coin, which causes a chain reaction leading to a falling stone, the rock strikes Liu Bei, killing him! Guan Yu, being loyal to his promise, wants to follow his elder brother to die on the same day. Zhang Fei tries to stop him but fails, so he knocks Guan Yu unconscious first. Then they discover that Liu Bei is not dead, just seriously injured and unconscious. After discussing with Dongs, Xiaoyu, and Arthur, they decide to leave Xiu in the Silver Space-Time as a substitute (since Xiu's counterpart in the Silver Space-Time is Liu Bei), and although Xiu is reluctant, he agrees to maintain the order of space-time. They send the other three back to the Gold Space-Time to treat the critically injured Liu Bei. When Guan Yu wakes up and finds out that the fake Liu Bei, played by Xiu, is alive, he is extremely happy. Zhang Fei is also glad that they don’t have to die together. Thus, the three finally enter the "Donghan Academy," where Guan Yu and Zhang Fei believe they can make a name for themselves!

The three hope to graduate from Donghan Academy smoothly, but due to its fame, it attracts attacks from the Yellow Turban High School and Hedong High School (where Dong Zhuo is the principal), putting Donghan Academy repeatedly in danger. Moreover, Zuo Ci Master even sets up an 'Eight Gates Golden Lock Formation' at the school gate to block students from attending classes. Naturally, the three take responsibility to break the formation, but the Eight Gates Golden Lock Formation is not easy to break. Even Liu Bei Xiu and Xiaoqiao get trapped in the formation, facing life-threatening situations. Therefore, Guan Yu and Zhang Fei, following the suggestion of the student president Cao Cao, find an incredibly resilient and cheerful young man named Ma Chao, as well as Ma Chao’s good friend, the infallible archer Huang Zhong, to help break the formation. Additionally, they involve Zhao Yun, a super handsome youth who has always been at odds with them. Together, the five break the formation to rescue Liu Bei Xiu and Xiaoqiao. During the process of breaking the formation, Guan Yu saves Zhao Yun's life by shielding him with his arm in a critical moment, resolving the previous conflicts between Zhao Yun and Guan and Zhang. From then on, Zhao Yun becomes brothers with the other four, standing shoulder to shoulder, and together they successfully break the Eight Gates Golden Lock Formation.

After breaking the formation, the group makes new brothers, thinking they can now smoothly complete high school. However, on one side, the Yellow Turban High School is eyeing the resources of Donghan Academy, while on the other side, Dong Zhuo, the principal of Hedong High School, Cao Cao, the student president of Donghan Academy, and Yuan Shao, the leader of the Guandong School Alliance, are all ambitious for the throne of the "Alliance Leader" of the entire school alliance. This dooms Liu Bei and the Five Tigers to live a peaceful campus life. A battle for world domination is about to begin…
