Changan CD101 is expected to be mass-produced and rolled off the production line at Changan Automobile's Beijing base on September 30th.

by jintaimqr on 2012-03-05 16:11:00

It was recently learned that Changan CD101 is expected to roll off the production line at the Beijing production base of Changan Automobile [http://www.jt077.cn/] on September 30, and it will be listed on the market in the same period. The new car is equipped with a jointly developed 2.0L engine [http://www.jt077.cn/index.php?m=qpsc&c=launch&a=lists&catid=11], and the top-end model is also equipped with a 1.8T turbocharged powertrain. As an independent mid-size sedan, Changan CD101 will also enter the official car market in the future.

Changan CD101 is Changan Automobile's first independently branded mid-size sedan, which has a more generous appearance, with a bit of Camry's shadow. The front face of the new car adopts a chrome hexagonal air intake grille, which is somewhat similar to the previously unveiled Yidong model. The logo on the grille will adopt the all-new V-shaped logo of the Changan brand. The xenon lens group and sickle-shaped LED daytime running lights of the headlights are eye-catching. The fog lamp design adjacent to the air intake grille is very three-dimensional. In terms of size, the length, width and height of the new car are 4920mm x 1825mm x 1500mm respectively, and the wheelbase reaches as high as 2800mm. The spacious body design will bring more comfortable rear seat space.

The rear spy photos show the advantages of the wheelbase of Changan CD101. It can be seen that the rear legroom is quite generous. Moreover, the central floor is not very prominent, and three people can sit down easily.

In terms of interior decoration, Changan CD101 also follows the generous style, with simple and generous design. It can be seen from the spy photos that the dashboard adopts the color matching of black and white, and appropriately adds wood panel decoration, greatly improving the interior grade. The new car is also equipped with multi-function steering wheel, large-sized driving computer screen, audio system integrated with DVD playback system, visual reversing and GPS navigation system, keyless one-button ignition system, electronic handbrake, independent dual-zone air conditioning, boneless wipers and electric sunroof and other high-tech configurations, all of which reflect the high configuration standard of Changan CD101 as a business car.

In terms of power, Changan CD101 will launch three power combinations, including 2.0L six-speed manual transmission models; 2.0L six-speed automatic transmission models, and top-end 1.8T turbocharged engine paired with six-speed automatic transmission models.

Among them, the 2.0L six-speed manual and automatic models will be equipped with JL486Q engine self-produced by Changan. This engine is a gasoline engine jointly developed by Changan Company and the famous German FEV engine design company. This engine is an inline 4-cylinder naturally aspirated engine, with unique technologies such as double overhead camshaft, electronically controlled multipoint fuel injection, mechanical tappet, forged crankshaft, etc., featuring high power, high torque, compact structure, low vibration, low noise, low fuel consumption, low emission, etc., and ranks among the best in the domestic and foreign similar engines.

For a long time, the intermediate car market not only brings richer profits, but also enhances brand reputation. Various independent brands have been striving to make efforts with prices comparable to joint venture compact cars and rich functional configurations, constantly making efforts to impact the market, and Changan CD101 represents the latest attempt of this effort.
