He had pale green skin, a broad forehead, and a blood-red mark in the middle of his eyebrows, like the third eye of the legendary Horse King Ma. He wore a dark green loose robe, revealing a black chest armor underneath, with a large round emblem on his left shoulder, engraved with strange patterns. His ink-green hair was braided into countless small braids, gathered at the top of his head. Other than that, he looked no different from ordinary people, with a moderate build and a smile on his face. Except for Li Qiang's astonishment, everyone else seemed unfazed. Qin Bantong quietly told Li Qiang: "This person should be a cultivator from Tanbang Star. Only Tanbang Star has this kind of green-skinned race." That person glanced at Qin Bantong and said, "You're right. I am indeed the envoy from Tanbang Star." Mei Youbing stepped forward to embrace him and touched foreheads. Li Qiang thought this ritual was quite fun and couldn't resist imitating it by also stepping forward to embrace and touch foreheads. That person was extremely surprised. After all, Mei Youbing and he were old friends, which is why they could use the highest courtesy of the Green Race. Li Qiang's unexpected move left him speechless as he tilted his head in confusion. Everyone understood the principle behind this situation; none of them expected Li Qiang to do such a thing. Seeing the dumbfounded Green Race envoy, everyone stood there awkwardly. Huamei couldn't help but burst out laughing, and Hou Pijin was amused by her laugh and roared with laughter. Everyone followed suit, and the Green Race envoy also realized what happened. Knowing that Li Qiang simply didn't understand the etiquette, he revealed a helpless smile. This smile immediately eased the atmosphere, and Mei Youbing laughed and said: "This is Kaben from Tanbang Star, an old friend of mine. This time, he specifically came to deliver several rare plants that are not found in the Celestial Court Star." After everyone greeted him, they entered the study. After sitting down, Kaben said, "Senior Mei, this time our Green Race leader specially instructed us to ask you to refine a furnace of Departure Elixir for our clan. Alas, recently, the Bang Qi Ning country where the Green Race resides may go to war with the Tante country." Hou Pijin was surprised and asked, "Is Tanbang Star going to have a war? Won't that drag your cultivators into it?" Kaben bitterly laughed and replied, "Not just us, cultivators from other planets will also get dragged in. This time, the scale is enormous!" Mei Youbing curiously asked, "What unsolvable issue requires such a large-scale war, even involving the cultivation world? The cultivation world generally doesn't meddle easily in secular matters." Kaben explained, "It's a newly discovered giant crystal stone mine, which we cultivators use as spirit stones. The reserves are astonishing, and the quality is excellent. Everyone knows that all developments on Tanbang Star depend on crystal mines. Unfortunately, the crystal mine is located exactly on the border between the two countries, originally a no-man's land. Now both countries claim ownership of that land, and the situation is getting increasingly tense." Upon hearing this, Li Qiang immediately understood. Just like the energy crisis on Earth, this was a war sparked by the competition for resources. The involvement of the cultivation world was based on the same reasoning. He silently sighed, human nature is inherently greedy, and interest-driven conflicts are the fundamental cause. However, Li Qiang wasn't particularly interested in this matter. Qin Bantong was restless, having received two important pieces of news within a day, making him both excited and fearful. Looking around, his head swaying like a metronome, Li Qiang quietly asked, "What's wrong with you? Are you unsettled? Is something the matter?" "Where did the Guardian Law go? Why can't I see him?" Immediately realizing his intention, Li Qiang wanted to find the sound transmission array. He was also curious about what this sound transmission array was and whether it was as convenient as Earth's telephone. He beckoned Mei Jingjing and whispered, "Sister, can you take us to the sound transmission array?" Mei Jingjing, who was eager to spend some time with Li Qiang, smiled upon hearing this and said, "Alright, Brother, follow me." The three arrived at Lingpan Gate's sound transmission array. It was Li Qiang's first time seeing a sound transmission array, and it was nothing like what he had imagined. It was made of an unknown material, about half a person's height, resembling a bronze drum supported by four legs. Its smooth surface was filled with swirling clouds inside. Qin Bantong skillfully took out five small spirit stones and embedded them into five indentations on the side. After spraying a mouthful of true essence onto it, the clouds inside began to swirl violently. A white mist rose and slowly coalesced into a humanoid shape. A voice asked, "What is the matter?" Li Qiang almost fainted. This device was better than a video phone, producing a three-dimensional image with sound transmission. After Qin Bantong finished reporting, seeing Li Qiang's stunned expression, he couldn't help but boast, "This is needed for long-distance sound transmission. There are also palm-sized sound transmission orbs produced on Fengyuan Star. Even Kaben from Tanbang Star has one, though its transmission distance isn't very far." Li Qiang suddenly realized that Celestial Court Star was merely a backward planet. Perhaps Fengyuan Star and Tanbang Star were advanced ones with technology comparable to Earth's. He thought that this would surely broaden his horizons. Mei Jingjing pulled on Li Qiang's arm and giggled, "Brother, let me show you the alchemy room. Would Qin sir like to come?" Qin Bantong's mind was entirely preoccupied with the impending war on Tanbang Star. He wanted to hear any new information, so he responded, "Oh, no, I'll return to the study to listen." "Alright, Qin sir, you go ahead. We'll be back soon." Mei Jingjing hoped he would leave early, and she pulled Li Qiang towards the back of the study. Mei Family's alchemy room enjoyed a high reputation in the cultivation world. The alchemy room was constructed nearly a hundred meters underground. The underground tunnels crisscrossed, and as soon as Li Qiang entered, he felt dizzy. He thought, "It seems the cultivators here like to dig tunnels. Last time there was Star Palace, and this time there's the alchemy room. It should be called the alchemy cave." "Be careful, Brother. Don't touch this. It's the Cold Purification Formation. Triggering it would be troublesome." "Don't step there; you'll fall in." Li Qiang didn't know what to do and awkwardly asked, "Sister, how come there are so many mechanisms and formations here?" Mei Jingjing giggled, "Brother, if it weren't for these numerous mechanisms and formations, the spiritual pills of the Mei family would have been robbed long ago." She continued, "We're almost at the entrance. Let's hurry up. We can't fly, okay?" Curious, Li Qiang asked, "Why can't we fly? Wouldn't it be faster?" "As soon as you fly, it will trigger the largest formation here, sealing off the entire tunnel. This is a secret of our Mei family. Don't tell anyone outside." Li Qiang was moved inside. Mei Jingjing regarded him as part of her own family, which was why she spoke thus. This trick was quite absolute. Any cultivator wanting to seize the pills would definitely enter the tunnel by flying—first, it's fast, second, it seems less likely to trigger any mechanism or formation—but they'd almost certainly fall for this trap. "We've arrived, Brother. Wait a moment." A roughly five-meter-square black hole appeared before their eyes. Mei Jingjing performed a series of hand seals that Li Qiang couldn't understand. With each change in hand seal, a wisp of blue smoke flew out of the black hole. After about the time it takes to drink a cup of tea, a black stone slab rose from the hole. Taking a deep breath, Mei Jingjing said, "Fortunately, I haven't forgotten. Brother, step up." The two stood on the black stone slab. Mei Jingjing performed another hand seal, and with a faint rumbling sound, the black stone slab began to descend slowly. Li Qiang had only one thought: the material conditions possessed by cultivators shouldn't be worse than those on Earth, if he chose to pursue them. Surrounded by swirling clouds and constantly flashing blinding light, after a while, Li Qiang felt a slight jolt underfoot, knowing they had arrived. Curiously, he asked, "Sister, why is it so dark around here?" Mei Jingjing performed the final hand seal, and instantly everything became bright. Staring intently, Li Qiang marveled and said, "Truly marvelous!" It was an eight-sided room, with each wall painted in a different color. When Mei Jingjing waved her hand, one of the walls silently revealed eight empty slots, each containing a different jade box. She smiled and said, "This is the room for storing spiritual pills. There are nine rooms in total, and one more is the alchemy room. Sister will take you to see it." Li Qiang noticed there were no other exits and asked, "Sister, there's no door." "It's here." Mei Jingjing walked towards a wall and disappeared directly into it. Li Qiang quickly followed, realizing it was actually an illusionary wall. Then he heard Mei Jingjing's surprised voice, "How are you here?" followed by a painful groan. Recognizing Mei Jingjing's cry of pain, Li Qiang was shocked and leapt forward with great force. In the alchemy room were three people: An Lang, the royal retainer of Litan Country, and Situ Yong, the War King, along with someone Li Qiang didn't recognize. Mei Jingjing was suspended in mid-air, struggling hard. Something seemed to have bound her, and she sobbed, "Seventh Uncle, you... you collude with outsiders. If Grandfather finds out, he will never forgive you..." The person called Seventh Uncle by Mei Jingjing showed a fleeting look of shame, then his expression turned cold, "Don't call me Uncle. I, Mei Qu, was long ago expelled as a disciple by Lingpan Gate. Who is he?" Li Qiang quickly responded, "I'm your grandpa!" and had already leapt beside Mei Jingjing, hugging her and flying backwards. Mei Qu shouted, "Stop him! He'll trigger the mechanism!" A massive force enveloped them, and Li Qiang knew things were bad. He yelled, "Sister, tell your big brother where..." Using all his strength, he threw her into the illusionary wall, triggering the defense formation of the alchemy room. Mei Jingjing cried out, "Brother..." Mei Qu panicked and said, "Quick, leave! The formation is powerful!" Situ Yong angrily cursed, "Little brat ruined our big plan! Anzi, activate the teleportation formation! Mei Qu, grab the Jade Pupil Scroll! Bastard, still want to run? Watch my skills!" Situ Yong waved his hand, grabbing into thin air, conjuring a golden giant hand that caught Li Qiang at lightning speed. Such immense power was something Li Qiang had never anticipated. It felt like being submerged in the deep sea, the pressure so great that it seemed his body might shatter. He deeply regretted not wearing the Lan Yun battle armor in time, and his training in the Ying Meng armor was still shallow, unable to withstand such tremendous pressure. He was completely grabbed by Situ Yong. Anzi had already activated the teleportation formation, and Mei Qu cheered, "I found it!" jumping into the formation, Li Qiang was pulled in at the exact same time. The Ying Meng armor resisted stubbornly, surprising Situ Yong. How could this disciple of the Mei family possess such resilience? He hadn't realized Li Qiang wasn't actually from the Mei family. Preparing to add more force, white light flashed, and the three were teleported away. Situ Yong was enraged. This teleportation point was painstakingly set up by them through Mei Qu, aiming to steal the Jade Pupil Scroll and refined spiritual pills from Lingpan Gate. Now, everything was disrupted by Li Qiang. Waves of heart-wrenching pain surged toward Li Qiang. Wanting to immerse his consciousness into the infant soul, he was shocked to find himself blocked outside. He had never felt so miserable. War King Situ Yong had no idea that Li Qiang's infant soul was different from other cultivators'. The ten Shattered Soul Golden Fingers could only temporarily seal him. Once an opportunity arose, he could break free. Had Situ Yong checked again, Li Qiang might have been in serious trouble. Watching Situ Yong stride away with a group of guards and listening to Anzi's continuous malicious laughter, Li Qiang suddenly found the situation absurd. From Earth to Celestial Court Star, it was like an inexplicable dream, where many unimaginable encounters actually occurred, blurring the line between reality and fantasy. "Haha, haha." Unbelievably touching his forehead and cheeks, he saw his hands covered in blood, tasting its saltiness. Four of the five streams of energy were blocked by his arms, while the remaining one slashed diagonally across his forehead and cheek. The power of the infant soul was sealed inside his body, preventing him from healing the wounds. This was the first time Li Qiang had truly been injured since arriving at Celestial Court Star. During this brutal beating, due to the presence of the infant soul, Li Qiang managed to protect his main vital areas. Despite blood flowing all over the floor and excruciating pain, he endured it. "Do you regret it now, ha ha, little boy, too late." Don't bother me." Anzi paused, a wave of unease rising within him. He said, "Good, good, soon you'll regret opposing us. Take him to the Black Camp. You'll have plenty of fun, ha ha." Neither Situ Yong nor Anzi realized he was Li Qiang. If Lei Tianxiao were present, Li Qiang would have been in real trouble.
