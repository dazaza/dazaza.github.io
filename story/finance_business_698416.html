Cement companies introduce concrete mixing plants with fiery production enthusiasm

by anonymous on 2012-03-09 09:26:05

Currently, cement enterprises extend industrial chain in different ways. Some enterprises begin to involve in concrete business, and some begin to involve in aggregate business, which is also beyond reproach, after all, they are all related products. But there is another point that a lot of enterprises also involve in real estate, chemical industry, smelting and so on, it can be said that they are colorful. However, marching into sand and gravel, concrete industry is still the mainstream. At present, China's cement enterprises introduce concrete mixing plant aggregate production line with great enthusiasm. As an indispensable raw material for commercial concrete, the production of artificial sand and gravel has been mentioned many times by some industry insiders. In the future, cement enterprises will become the main force of sand and gravel industry. It is indicated that cement enterprises develop aggregate sand and gravel production not only have natural unique advantages, but also have broad development prospects and considerable economic benefits. The reasons are as follows: 1. Existing large and medium-sized cement enterprises have strong capital operation ability. With sufficient financial support, compared with individual small aggregate mechanism sand and gravel processing industry, it has obvious capital support, and has strong competitiveness in participating in the production of mechanism sand and gravel. Because of its strong financial support, it can launch a larger production line of sand and gravel. 2. Mineral resources are fully guaranteed. Because large and medium-sized cement production enterprises have given priority to the guarantee coefficient of ore resources when selecting factory site, most of them are selected and built in mountainous areas and resource-rich geological belts. Therefore, when the sand and gravel production line project is launched, it can be said that it is the comprehensive utilization of waste rock and turning waste into treasure. Facing inexhaustible mining resources, the sand and gravel production line can produce at full load without worry, which is a unique advantage for cement enterprises to vigorously promote this project. 3. There is a relatively stable industrial chain (sand and gravel + cement + concrete + cement products) to achieve synergistic win-win benefits. Because cement enterprises have relatively stable supply and demand relations with construction, engineering, infrastructure, road traffic, municipal engineering and other departments, these units need cement mortar aggregate concrete components provided by other production enterprises. If cement enterprises have aggregate sand and gravel products, they can use their own cement products to directly synthesize concrete products and other cement products, create an all-round supply and demand channel for various customers with different needs, and form a one-stop service system. This also builds a fast and unified concrete mixing station platform for product quality, time guarantee and follow-up service, reduces the cost of many links such as exploring new ways, multi-supply, separate settlement, and undoubtedly produces ideal win-win benefits. 4. To start sand and gravel production projects, the investment is much less than a new dry-process cement production line, and the effect is much faster. For example, a 1 million ton/year dry-process cement production line, from design, project initiation, construction to production, requires an investment of 3 billion yuan and a construction period of 300 days; while a 5000 ton/day sand and gravel production line requires an investment of 15 million yuan. Moreover, from project initiation, construction to production, it only takes 100 days to obtain profits and generate benefits.
