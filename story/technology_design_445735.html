Cabinet brand selection

by f34ttr4t5ye on 2011-06-14 14:24:29

It is basically determined to sign a contract with the decoration company to start work. It is said that after the start, the kitchen cabinets need to be decided so as to carry out water and electricity transformation. But recently, I am too busy to know what brands of kitchen cabinets are available in the market. I would like to ask the classmates who have already decorated, which brands of kitchen cabinets have high cost performance.

I. Professionalism of Kitchen Cabinet Company:

First of all, it is the design ability of the kitchen cabinet company: the kitchen cabinet industry is full of fashionable elements. From materials, colors to accessories matching, all of them reflect the design ability of the kitchen cabinets. Therefore, the design principle of kitchen cabinets is the combination of usability, convenience and beauty. On the premise of meeting practicality, we should consider how to better realize the actions of material selection, washing, cutting and cooking, and how to make it more beautiful under the conditions of practicality and convenience, and has appreciability: there are priorities and main and secondary among these three, mainly depending on the role played by your kitchen.

As kitchen cabinet design personnel, they should have the ability to draw, read and explain drawings, and can spare no effort to formulate reasonable and scientific solutions for you, rather than promoting inferior, unsuitable and expensive materials and accessories to you

Secondly, the professional quality of the company's staff: as professional staff, they can communicate well with you. They should not only have sufficient understanding of their own products, but also have some understanding of the products of peers or competitors, but they must not maliciously attack and belittle the products of competitors in the market, but emphasize and introduce the characteristics of their own products and substitutes, understand and guide customers' actual needs, and be a good kitchen consultant for customers.

Whether the company's organization mechanism is perfect: if there are problems in any link of sales, design production, installation and after-sales service, it will affect the quality and delivery time of kitchen cabinets. Therefore, a perfect organization mechanism and an effective team are needed to fully guarantee the product quality. Some road guerrillas and irregular manufacturers are difficult to do this, and there is no way to ensure product quality to protect your rights and interests.

Machine configuration and material situation: if you want to do a good job, you must first sharpen your tools. The performance of production equipment and the variety of raw materials are the prerequisite conditions for making high-quality products. However, having advanced production equipment and high-quality raw materials does not necessarily produce high-quality products.

II. Customer satisfaction and social reputation:

Everyone can make kitchen cabinets. Especially now, some merchants on individual group-buying websites attract customers with low prices online with a small store. But it is difficult to make good kitchen cabinets. Because the threshold for making kitchen cabinets is not high, simple ones only need 3 sets of equipment, or even find someone to process without a factory. In addition, the market demand for kitchen cabinets is very large, resulting in many small workshops without the ability to make kitchen cabinets rushing to the market, and even decoration teams say that as long as they can see the shape, they can make exactly the same kitchen cabinets on site at your home. But it is not so simple. In addition, the kitchen cabinet consumption is not mature, lacks effective supervision and standardization, resulting in many shoddy kitchen cabinets without any aesthetic sense in the market, let alone complete functions and comfortable use. Moreover, there are subtle differences in technology, environmental protection and kitchen cabinet structure, melamine. As a result, many consumers need to replace kitchen cabinets in a short period of several years.

On the contrary, a kitchen cabinet brand that has occupied a certain share in the market and has no shortage of return customers actually shows that the brand has formed a good impression among customers, and deserves our choice.

III. The status and evaluation of the company in the same industry, and whether it is professional production?

Competition exists in any industry. Competitors are enemies. If they can get the approval and good comments from the industry, it is more valuable. The enterprises participating in the social group organization, their products are also subject to the supervision of the group organization. Once the product has problems, most consumers can rely on the strength of the organization to find the merchants and negotiate to solve the problems.
