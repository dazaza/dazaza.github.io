Introduction to the plot of "An Angel Will Love You Instead":

In 1994. Ten-year-old Xiao Yi lives with his single mother. At that time, his mother always took him to move from one city to another... Qingyuan was one of those places.

Most of the time, the mother would lock Xiao Yi at home and not allow him to go out. Xiao Yi felt very lonely, but fortunately, there were often beautiful piano sounds coming from the next door, keeping Xiao Yi company... Xiao Yi climbed up a high window and looked over, seeing a girl in a white dress sitting in front of a white piano teasingly tapping the keys with her index finger...

This became the most beautiful scene in Xiao Yi's childhood memory...

Until one day, the next door room echoed with cries of grief, Xiao Yi saw many adults in black clothes going in and out, each with a sorrowful face, and Xiao Yi learned that the girl in the white dress's mother had passed away due to illness...

Later, the girl's father found a stepmother for her, and that most beautiful scene never appeared again.

One morning, Xiao Yi was woken up by a noisy sound. He peeked out of the window and saw the stepmother disregarding the girl's desperate resistance, shouting at several workers to lift the piano onto a truck...

Not long after, the mother found a school for Pei Yi, and Xiao Yi was extremely excited, but he didn't expect that on the first day of school, he encountered playmates living on the same street. They bullied Xiao Yi, laughing at him for being a "kid without a father," and Xiao Yi fought back, getting his nose and face bruised.

The blood from his nose stained his new shirt, so Xiao Yi dared not go home and hid in the corner of the street crying. It happened to be the girl's birthday that day, but the stepmother punished her by making her kneel in the courtyard and threw her two angel dolls into the trash can. The girl broke free from the stepmother and cried as she ran out of the house...

As the lanterns lit up in the evening, it started to drizzle lightly. The two homeless children met in a dirty street corner...

Xiao Yi climbed into a high garbage truck and helped the girl find the two abandoned angel dolls. The girl wiped away her tears and smiled, saying that they were birthday gifts from her mother, who said her soul was inside the angel dolls, protecting her...

The girl handed one of them to Xiao Yi and said, "I'll ask my mom to bless you too!"

"My name is Mi Ai, call me Xiao Mi... And you?"

"My name is Yi."

Twelve years passed in the blink of an eye.

Xiao Mi was in her third year of college, and Yi was about to graduate soon. From that evening onward, the two have been inseparable, from elementary school to university... They warmed each other, envied by everyone in the academy as lovers.

Xiao Mi often threatened Yi: "If you do such and such again, I won't let my mom bless you!" Yi was very well-tempered and said, "I'm not afraid. Even if you don't like me anymore, even if I've disappeared from this world, I will still love you! I'll find an angel, to love you instead..."

Each time, Xiao Mi would cover her ears: "So cheesy!"

On the day the story begins, it was Xiao Mi's 20th birthday, but Yi went out of town to look for work. For so many years, Yi didn't accompany Xiao Mi on her birthday for the first time, leaving Xiao Mi feeling very disheartened. Yi hurried back from out of town and called Xiao Mi, but Xiao Mi missed the call... Yi, holding colorful balloons, breathlessly weaved through the bustling streets, catching sight of Xiao Mi flashing by on the opposite side of the street, and loudly called out to Xiao Mi...

Yi ran across the street, when a car sped past... After the sharp braking sound, Xiao Mi turned around and only saw the balloons floating up into the sky...

When Xiao Mi rushed to the hospital, Yi's heart had already stopped beating. The nurse handed over Yi's belongings to Xiao Mi, which included a bag full of chocolates and a blood-stained angel doll on the backpack...

Xiao Mi saw the last unanswered call from Yi in her phone's missed calls, and its timing was shockingly consistent with the time on Yi's wristwatch that had stopped. Tears fell from Xiao Mi's eyes: After the car accident, Yi still endured the pain to make one last call to her, but she missed her last chance to hear Yi's voice...
