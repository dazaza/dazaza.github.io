Wu Dong Qian Kun: Wife hides methamphetamine to frame and retaliate against her husband for his infidelity

by sznstejx3q3 on 2012-03-02 20:25:15

The husband had an extramarital affair, an urban young master, and the wife retaliated by framing him, even going so far as to set up a scheme to hide methamphetamine for false accusation. This bizarre case, prosecuted by the Nanshan Procuratorate, resulted in the defendant Jiang being sentenced to 7 years in prison for the crime of illegal possession of drugs in the first trial.

Upon discovering that her husband Lu was cohabiting with another person outside the marriage, the defendant Jiang suffered a great mental blow. Moreover, the 5 million yuan in funds at home were fully controlled by her husband, making her feel that her marriage and family were on the brink of collapse. In order to change this dire situation, in early 2011, Jiang met multiple times with her classmate Feng to discuss ways to frame and teach Lu a lesson while also recovering the 5 million yuan.

After several discussions, Feng introduced Cai to Jiang. The three conspired together, deciding to falsely accuse Lu of hiding drugs. Subsequently, Jiang let Cai use GPS positioning tracking to find out the residence and vehicle parking spot where her husband Lu lived with his lover, attempting to enter Lu's residence to place drugs but failing to succeed. Later, Jiang tried to have the K powder provided by Cai placed in water, hoping to cause Lu to become delirious and sign relevant promissory notes, but Lu detected it when drinking.

After several failed attempts, Cai brought in another defendant Zou, who discussed with Jiang placing drugs in Lu's car, with Jiang providing funding to purchase 200,000 RMB worth of methamphetamine. On June 24, 2011, after Jiang and Zou prepared the methamphetamine, they went to the underground garage of Lu's rented residence and secretly hid more than 180 grams of methamphetamine in the spare tire of Lu's car before fleeing the scene.

After hiding the drugs, Zou reported the detailed situation of the drug concealment to the public security organs, but the public security organs did not discover the facts he reported. On July 11 of the same year, Zou again reported Lu for drug trafficking, and the public security organs seized two packets of methamphetamine from Lu's car, leading to Lu being criminally detained on suspicion of illegal possession of drugs.

After Lu was apprehended, he claimed to have been falsely accused and framed. After the public security organs verified Zou's report and the source of the drugs, the truth was uncovered. Subsequently, defendant Zou turned himself in, and Feng, Cai, and Jiang were also arrested one after another. On January 12, 2012, Jiang and Zou were sentenced to 7 years and 4 years in prison respectively for the crime of illegal possession of drugs in the first trial. Cai and Feng were sentenced to 1 year and 10 months in prison respectively for the crime of false accusation in the first trial.
