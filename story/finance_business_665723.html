**Bidding Announcement for Air Source Heat Pump Air Conditioning Project**

**Registration Period:** March 31, 2011 - April 19, 2011

**Bidding Authority:** Yunlong Town People's Government of Yinzhou District

**Location:** Zhejiang Province

**Detailed Content:** Ningbo Yinzhou Public Resources Trading Center, on behalf of the Yunlong Town People's Government of Yinzhou District, is conducting an open bidding process for the air source heat pump air conditioning project. Suppliers who meet the qualification requirements are welcome to participate in the bidding.

---

### **1. Project Name:**

Air Source Heat Pump Air Conditioning

### **2. Procurement Number:**

YZZFCG (2011) 052

### **3. Procurement Method:**

Open Bidding

### **4. Procurement Content and Quantity:**

| Item | Procurement Content | Unit/Quantity |

|------|---------------------|---------------|

| 1 | Air source heat pump type chilled water unit | 1 set |

| | Air conditioning box | 3 sets |

**Note:** This project cannot be bid on separately.

---

### **5. Qualification Requirements for Qualified Bidders:**

**(a)** Must comply with Article 22 of the "Government Procurement Law of the People's Republic of China," which stipulates that suppliers participating in government procurement activities should meet the following conditions:

1. Have the ability to independently assume civil liability;

2. Possess a good business reputation and sound financial accounting system;

3. Have the necessary equipment and professional technical capabilities to perform contracts;

4. Maintain a good record of paying taxes and social security funds as required by law;

5. Have no major illegal records in business activities in the three years prior to participating in government procurement activities.

**(b)** Suppliers must have a business license issued by the industrial and commercial department.

---

### **6. Method of Obtaining Tender Documents:**

This tender document can be downloaded free of charge from the Ningbo Government Procurement Website and the Ningbo Yinzhou Public Resources Trading Center website. After downloading, fill out the "Bid Registration Letter" and fax it to this center before 9:00 AM on April 18, 2011. Otherwise, the bid will be rejected.

---

### **7. Bid Security Deposit:**

The bid security deposit for this project is RMB 5,000.

---

### **8. Bid Submission Deadline and Location:**

Bidders must submit their sealed tender documents to Room 226, Ningbo Yinzhou Public Resources Trading Center (No. 578 Qianhu South Road, Yinzhou Central Area, Ningbo, within the Yinzhou Administrative Service Center) before 9:00 AM (Beijing Time) on April 19, 2011. Late submissions or unsealed bids will be rejected (or treated as invalid bids).

---

### **9. Opening of Bids Time and Location:**

Time: 9:00 AM (Beijing Time) on April 19, 2011

Location: Ningbo Yinzhou Public Resources Trading Center
