Three Golden Periods for Slimming Legs:

Morning 8:00

Exercise on the bus

A busy day begins. A day's plan lies in the morning, and beautiful legs start in the morning too.

When walking to the bus stop, pay attention to the correct walking posture: head up, chest out, use the strength of your waist while walking, keep your back straight, tuck in your abdomen, tighten your buttocks, let your heel touch the ground first, and maintain a certain speed. By following these principles, you can beautify your legs while walking.

After boarding the bus, if you have a seat, you can easily do some exercises. Place your legs at a 90-degree angle, keep your heels fixed, and repeatedly move your toes up and down. This exercise strengthens the muscles of the calf, making the calf lines more even.

If there is no seat, you can practice small exercises: stand at attention, lift any foot, cross it inward, apply light pressure, hold for 10 seconds, then switch to the other leg. Note that the center of gravity should be placed in the center of the body. If it's hard to maintain balance, you can hold onto a pole with your hand.

Afternoon 3:00

Do some exercises while sitting

Office workers who sit at their desks for long periods of time are prone to having their legs get fat due to lack of movement. You can take advantage of the opportunity to sit and do some exercises, achieving both fatigue relief and leg slimming.

1. Sit on the chair, place your hands on both sides of the chair to stabilize your body, lift one foot and straighten your knee for 30 seconds. Then switch to the other foot and repeat the same action. Note that while straightening your knee, do not shift the position of your knee.

2. Sit on the chair, chest out, keep your legs crossed with your toes touching the ground, press down hard with the upper leg, and push up hard with the lower leg. After about 10 seconds, switch the positions of both legs and continue for another 10 seconds. Repeat this 2-3 times. There's no need to hold your breath while doing this.

Evening 8:00

Leg-beautifying exercises while watching TV

You can utilize the time at home resting or watching TV to do some leg-beautifying exercises.

1. A common ballet dancer's exercise: Lift one leg forward to 90 degrees, stretch your foot, then slowly move it to the side, repeating 20 times per leg. Persisting with this exercise can slim your legs and improve your overall posture.

2. An effective exercise for beautifying the calf curve: Lie flat on the ground, place your hands at your sides, stretch both legs straight and together, alternately move your feet 20-30 times, then rest briefly and repeat twice.
