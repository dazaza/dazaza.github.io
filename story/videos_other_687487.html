Beijing Yanjing Renrenda Hotel is located on the East Qianmen Street in Chongwen District, Beijing. The superior geographical location provides convenient and fast conditions for guests' sightseeing, tourism, and shopping.

The hotel has economically comfortable rooms with a magnificent hall. Each room is equipped with a bright and clean private bathroom, 24-hour hot water, air conditioning, an LCD TV, a telephone, broadband internet access, and reasonably priced accommodations with luxurious water purifiers. Adhering to the business philosophy of "people-oriented, customer first, honest treatment, and making guests feel at home," the hotel sincerely welcomes all friends to stay.

The hotel opened on April 13, 2004, and was recently renovated in November 2009. It has four floors with a total of 65 rooms.
