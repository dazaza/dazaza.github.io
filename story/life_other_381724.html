What is Blue Hour?

Blue Hour, also known as "blue time," refers to the time after work when friends gather together and watch the fiery red sunset gradually turn into shades of blue, enjoying the beauty of the moment. It's a time to unwind with some drinks, enjoy some snacks, and simply have a great time.

Lomography now invites everyone to our Blue Hour event! Every Thursday from 5:00 PM to 8:00 PM, we welcome you to visit the Lomography headquarters. We will provide free drinks and snacks for everyone to enjoy. You can also chat with fellow Lomographers, take some photos, and let Lomography become your second home after work or school.
