The blurred campus in the dream

It seems that the first year is very relaxing. There is no complicated examination in January, and I don't have to worry about whether my students have reviewed well or not.

I forgot what I was doing on this day last year, anyway, I think such a January is unprecedented. I will also dream that I am an elementary school student, a middle school student, and a university student who entered the normal school at sixteen... The location is vague, like real memories fading with time. Such dreams have never been interrupted, almost appearing in every snowy night, every night when I miss you.

Looking out of the window at the scenery of dawn, I feel that I used to be sunny and carefree. And now, everything is just fleeting clouds.

I experienced transferring schools in the third grade of elementary school. Occasionally, I dream that I am at the eve of graduation from elementary school, mustering up the courage to go back to my original class to take a look, muttering in my heart that after graduation, there will be no more chances to go back to see my classmates. I didn't seem to think so at the time, maybe the true thoughts will be verified in some unintentional dreams years later. The games of childhood, the cartoons of childhood, the busy January of childhood.

I also dream about some past events in junior high school, unwilling to mention the unforgettable experiences. Wearing blue school uniforms walking in No. 26 Middle School, suddenly realizing that I am the oldest among all the students, lost in finding my classmates. When I was in the second year of junior high school, once looking at the hurried figures of the third-year students, I couldn't help but sigh, poor seniors, they are about to leave this wonderful junior high school life. Later, the prediction came true.

When my classmates put on high school uniforms and went to high school, I had already stepped into the foreign language major, complaining every day that I couldn't study in high school with my classmates. In five years, I always dreamt that during the high school class division, my classmates and I were sitting in the classroom of No. 12 Middle School, until the teacher called the names and found that there was no me on the list, the sadness was frightening and trembling. Maybe not everyone can choose their own life path, family plays an important role in some degree. Life is like this. The person who belongs to Aquarius has never appeared, maybe we passed by each other, just with a faint smile.

I hate everything about the normal college, early higher education means getting involved in society earlier. I hate here where there are no boys to play basketball, I hate that the classmates here are not as pure as in high school, I hate the teachers here who prefer ease to work, and I even start to dislike myself a bit, except for English, what else do I know? Then is the night a demon at this moment? Not quite. It's still warm in the dream, feeling the tranquility of the teacher's college with classmates in snowy nights, unfortunately, this segment of the dream will always remain a dream, it will never appear in the real life of the teacher's college. Everything there is ineffable.

Now occasionally, I dream about the days when I first arrived among the children, thinking that I was a child then, taking classes and going on outings with the students, much warmer and happier than the real working scenes. More than ten years ago, it was a campus, now it's a campus, perhaps in the future, it will still be a campus. I cry in the dream, originally being with you is just a dream; I also smile in the dream, seeing my youthful vigor and charm in those days.
