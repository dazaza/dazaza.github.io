1st Place:

EILUN UK Dynasty Fabric (Evaluation: Originated from the UK, one of China's top ten curtain fabric brands) (Official Website: www.eilun.com[http://www.eilun.com/])

Editor’s Review: EILUN Dynasty Fabric was founded in the UK in 1980. It is a world 500 strong brand and a large chain enterprise in the home textile industry. It is also the largest and most successful curtain fabric company in the UK. In China, it is a well-known production and sales enterprise for curtain fabrics, wallpapers, and soft packages in the home textile industry, with total assets close to 2 billion pounds. The company has passed five certifications: quality, environment, standardization, measurement management system, and social responsibility. The company has independent import and export operating rights, exporting 80 million US dollars annually. Its products are exported to more than 60 countries and regions, including Europe, the United States, Germany, Canada, Italy, Turkey, and Hong Kong.

&；nbsp；

2nd Place:

French Auchan Curtains (Evaluation: Professional, specialized, internationalized styles, first-class design level, China's top curtain brand) (Official Website: www.eaushe.com[http://www.eaushe.com/])

Editor’s Review: French Auchan Curtains is undoubtedly the best curtain brand recognized worldwide, known as the "[Blue-blooded Aristocrat] of curtains." It is a key backbone production enterprise in China's curtain industry, integrating the research, development, design, production, and sales of curtains, wallpapers, soft packages, as well as textile printing, dyeing, and embroidery. Their design level is first-class, and Auchan Curtain styles have always led the international curtain design trend. Their proprietary processes include embroidery, jacquard, printing, and disk embroidery. Major styles include European style, country style, Chinese style, modern style, and cartoon style. They own thousands of proprietary copyrights for patterns and colors. Their products are mainly sold in various European countries and developed countries like the United States and Japan, while domestically they are sold nationwide. Their franchise specialty stores are spread all over the globe. The factory has successively passed ISO9001 Quality Management System Certification, ISO14001 Environmental Management System Certification, OHSAS18001 Occupational Health and Safety Management System Certification, and Ecological Textiles Quality Environmental Protection Certification.

&；nbsp；

3rd Place:

Molic Curtain Fabric (Evaluation: A long-standing industry brand, established authorized production relationships with factories in the US, Germany, etc.) (Official Website: www.fsmolik.com[http://www.fsmolik.com/])

Editor’s Review: Molic Curtain Fabric started in 1982, dedicated to the design and R&D of fabric curtains and the extensive dissemination of fabric culture. There are hundreds of specialty stores nationwide, with dozens of excellent fabric consultants and designers providing professional services to customers. As a leading domestic integrated decorative fabric enterprise that integrates R&D, design, production, and sales, Molic places great emphasis on the overall product development and design of materials, colors, craftsmanship, and styles. After thirty years of steady development, it has nurtured its unique Molic Fabric Culture and gradually formed a distinctive product style series within the industry.

&；nbsp；

4th Place:

Paris Fabric (France) (Evaluation: One of Europe's largest curtain fabric producers, one of the top brands in the fabric industry this century) (Official Website: www.pariscl.com[http://www.pariscl.com/])

Editor’s Review: Paris Fabric was created in Paris, France, in 1982. It mainly has equipment such as cotton embossing machines, automatic fabric printing machines, non-woven seamless splicing machines, three-in-one embossing machines, French imported edge trimming machines, and curtain fabric shaping machines, totaling more than 500 units, with total assets nearing 8 billion yuan. Through years of market experience and operation, Paris Fabric has gradually improved and formed a product line of over 100 categories and over 1,000 varieties of home decor products, with curtain fabric as the leader and wallpaper, soft packaging products as the main products. It has established and perfected a "one-stop" service capability for the design, processing, sales, and installation of home decor products, providing customers with diversified choices and meeting their personalized requirements. Paris Fabric has established a wholesale and sales network and franchise system centered in Guangzhou, covering the whole country, with various indicators ranking among the top in the domestic and international industries.

&；nbsp；

5th Place:

ITMILAN Italian Milan Curtain Fabric (Evaluation: A globally renowned curtain fabric brand, China's leading professional fabric enterprise) (Official Website: www.milancl.com[http://www.milancl.com/])

Editor’s Review: ITMILAN Italian Milan Curtain Fabric, established in China in 1995, mainly produces home and engineering curtain fabrics, researching, designing, producing, and selling wallpapers and wall fabrics, with total assets nearing 2 billion yuan. The company specializes in producing the "ITMILAN" brand curtain fabric series and other related products, possessing self-operated import and export operating rights, exporting nearly 8 billion US dollars annually, with products being sold extensively in Europe, America, and around the world.

&；nbsp；

6th Place:

British Royal Elizabeth EILISHA Curtain (Evaluation: World's top curtain fabric brand, China's top three fabric brands) (Official Website: www.roycl.com[http://www.roycl.com/])

Editor’s Review: British Royal Elizabeth EILISHA Curtain, a long-standing British royal brand since 1880, is dedicated to the design and R&D of curtains and wallpapers, as well as the extensive dissemination of fabric culture. Royal Elizabeth Curtain has a large, professional design team consisting of five major design groups: pattern design, style design, store design, advertising design, and space design, with over 600 professional designers. After decades of development, Royal Elizabeth Curtain has achieved remarkable results, winning the highest design awards multiple times at international home textile fabric exhibitions.

7th Place:

Italian Rome Fabric RUOME (Evaluation: International top fabric brand, reputed as the world's most environmentally friendly fabric, RUOME Fabric, Italy's largest fabric manufacturer, whose products are sold in 108 countries) (Official Website: www.luomac.com[http://www.luomac.com/])

Editor’s Review: Italian Rome Fabric RUOME is located in Guangzhou, the provincial capital of Guangdong Province, which ranks first in comprehensive economic strength. Italian Rome Fabric is currently the leading enterprise in the domestic and even Asian curtain industry. According to statistics from the China Fabric Industry Association, the national exempt inspection product "RUOME brand" series fabric production, sales, and exports have ranked first in the country for consecutive years. Italian Rome Fabric has specially built a digital fabric high-tech park in the Guangzhou High-Tech Development Zone, establishing a national-level key fabric R&D center and a trend release base, producing more than 20 million meters of various fabric fabrics annually. The company's products are exported to more than 108 countries and regions, including Europe, America, Australia, Southeast Asia, and the Middle East.

8th Place:

Grand Vision DIASIA (Evaluation: Home textile fabric brand designed in France, positioned as a top brand in China's home textile fabric industry) (Official Website: www.diasia.cn[http://www.diasia.cn/])

Editor’s Review: Headquarters located in Shantou, Guangdong, China. Accompanying the birth and growth of China's home textile fabric industry, Grand Vision Company has developed from a curtain retail store into the largest fabric mall in the country at the time, and from a wholesaler into one of the larger professional home textile fabric sales companies domestically that integrates design development and marketing. Grand Vision excels in uncovering the passion and joy in life, combining the product quality, unique design, terminal sales, and after-sales service of Grand Vision, allowing consumers to fully experience the infinite charm of the brand culture and products created by Grand Vision.

9th Place:

Ruyu Deshui Curtain Fabric (Evaluation: Ruyu Deshui Fabric Premium, one of the earliest curtain enterprises to implement standardized production nationwide) (Official Website: www.ruyudeshui.com[http://www.ruyudeshui.com/])

Editor’s Review: Ruyu Deshui headquarters is located in Zhejiang, China. With an enterprising spirit of innovation and diligent practicality, bold and novel marketing concepts, and the pursuit of perfect effect performances, based on superior fabric products and professional R&D and design, adhering to the corporate mission of "[Spread home textile culture, light up thousands of households]," every detail stands at the forefront of the industry; every step adheres to the brand cultural route, focusing on cultivating the brand's strengths.

10th Place:

Victory Office Curtain (Evaluation: Professional office curtain enterprise, international leader in office curtains) (Official Website: www.gzvic.com[http://www.gzvic.com/])

Editor’s Review: Victory Office Curtain was founded in 1990, mainly engaged in the manufacture and sale of window decoration products, and is a leading player in the domestic office curtain market. Victory Office Curtain's headquarters is in Guangdong, where it has established the largest office curtain R&D industrial base in the country. Its office curtain sales network covers the entire nation.
