No one belongs to anyone else!

by wangquan1986 on 2012-02-27 10:08:24

None of us belongs to each other! In one's lifetime, there are always too many memories and regrets. It is a good thing to learn to grow from regrets. But if one can only indulge in regrets and cannot let go, then happiness and joy will be far away from us! 2012 may also be the time for me to finally decide where I should go. ――Preface Time flows like water. This year, I have experienced too much, as if I have gone through all the ups and downs of my 30 years. I quarreled with my marriage for the sake of writing, disturbed my emotions for the sake of divorce, disrupted my work for the sake of emotions, and neglected my writing for the sake of work. Everything is cycling. The naivety of youth, the childhood sweethearts to the present changes, made me see clearly that people are so terrible, terrible beyond words. Friends don't seem like friends, family doesn't seem like family. I ask: who is worth trusting?

I used to firmly believe that wealth is not a lifelong friend, but friends are lifelong treasures. Perhaps everyone has such a friend around them. But actually, many times, the person who hurts us most is our best friend. It's hard to believe my own eyes. I would rather believe that what I saw was not done by my friend, but by a stranger. I have no words to say. Until today, I have always treated every friend around me carefully. I think a friend is a strong support that can give you spiritual support and encouragement when necessary. As long as possible, they will do their best within their ability to help friends get out of trouble and difficulties. However, the betrayal happened to me just like a drama.

But often, it ends up in disappointment. After paying, I realize that my efforts were just building a high bridge for others, while digging a deep pit for myself. Isn't this the big pit dug by so-called good friends? Thinking about myself, I've been walking on my own pace for so many years, step by step. I never depended on anyone, and everything was achieved through my own efforts. Every time I was in a difficult situation, I would remind myself: with the support and encouragement of so many friends, I must work hard, I must succeed! I won't let everyone down.

Perhaps due to the changing times or human manipulation, everyone is gradually changing. A person's IQ and EQ determine what they do. They forget themselves, forget the true friends who helped them and paid a lot. In a money-driven society, human relationships have become transactions. Who cares about each other's feelings? We must remember the friends who supported and encouraged us when we were in trouble, and never violate the principles and wishes of friendship. If someone can't even do that, then I'd rather not make friends with them. I give up. I gave up decades of friendship because my efforts only made others feel entitled. Now I understand, none of us belongs to each other!

As for my future beloved ones, how can I describe the fatigue? In the past year, I have been very tired. I hope I can completely break free from myself. But for the sake of the family and the children, I endured, I yielded, and I changed a lot. From the once strong-willed me to now being silent, it already shows that I don't want to continue arguing. Life is not something one person can persistently maintain alone. My silence does not mean I yield to you, but rather, I feel powerless to say more meaningless words. I don't want to involve too many people. Family and children are innocent. I only hope you deeply reflect on yourself. The straightforward me indeed has many flaws, but people change bit by bit. When someone realizes their own defects, it means that person can still improve. What's scary is when someone always thinks everything they do is completely correct; that person will never grow.

It's fine if life is tough, as long as one's heart is at peace, doing anything feels like practice makes perfect. Instead of panicking when things come up and being at a loss. I originally thought that family and children could double our confidence in life, but I was wrong. Some people can change, while others cannot change their whole lives. My efforts are silently paying off, but I am really tired. The burden of the family and the pressure of the children all fall on me. I am a woman too, and I need a strong spiritual and material guarantee. I don't want to endlessly dwell on trivial matters. Someone said: "A harmonious home leads to prosperity." This saying is very good. Indeed, a loving family is happy and joyful. The power of love can change everything that can be changed.

Spring comes and goes, year after year, day after day. Maybe it's time for some results. Everyone understands these truths, but relying on me alone, I can't keep going.

I can choose the responsibility of taking care of the family, and I can choose the conditions of working hard, but I can't bear such a heavy burden of the family.

In 2011, I was happy because I had a lovely daughter, she is my hope, the motivation for my hard work, and my spiritual encouragement.

I was comforted because my parents were all safe and sound, healthy and peaceful.

I was sad because I finally got rid of a hypocrite around me.

I was heartbroken because my future beloved one chose to believe others' slanders instead of trusting the person who loves him by his side.

I was persistent because my career began to take a new turn.

Therefore, I have thought deeply for a long time, and I have deeply realized: people cannot rely on anyone, not even their beloved ones. None of us belongs to each other! Women can only rely on themselves. Only with a career can women save themselves. In 2011, pack your luggage, pack yourself. In 2012, summarize yourself, perfect yourself, improve yourself, starting from your professional skills, and strive to learn to make yourself richer. Hope for a smooth year ahead, hope for my precious daughter's health, safety, and happiness. Hope for the family's good health. Hope for friends' success and smoothness in all matters.
