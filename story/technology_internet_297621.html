The director of the Great Wall Station in Antarctica, Mr. Pei, was moved by the sentiments of Tencent QQ users towards Antarctica!

by bjseeprof on 2009-12-07 11:34:36

Today, February 24th, is the highlight of the entire Antarctic trip - I am going to officially present a gift on behalf of Tencent to the Chinese Great Wall Station in Antarctica. But it's not an ordinary gift. Around noon, Director Pei of the Great Wall Station gathered all thirty-plus scientific research team members in the dining hall of the living quarters, which is also the largest meeting room here. Director Pei is about fifty years old and has many years of experience as a political commissar on large scientific research ships like the Xuelong. He is a very experienced and generous veteran of Antarctic exploration. Director Pei gave me a pleasant surprise with his opening remarks - he said that he himself is a loyal QQ user. This made me very excited, so I introduced myself to everyone by saying that it was a great honor to be specially invited by Director Li Weisen of the National Administration of Surveying, Mapping and Geoinformation, to come to the Great Wall Station with the慰问team. Not only am I bringing respect and blessings from Tencent Corporation to our scientific researchers, but I am also expressing gratitude to our loyal QQ users! The first thing I presented to Director Pei was a large crystal QQ penguin. The scientific researchers were all very surprised and said that although they have seen thousands or even millions of penguins in Antarctica, this was their first time seeing a crystal penguin. Next, I presented 100 eight-digit QQ numbers that we specially prepared for the Antarctic scientific research team. Some team members immediately asked if they could get two consecutive numbers - "lover numbers" - to use with their partners at home. Of course, this was possible and would be decided by Assistant Director Xiao Li. In addition, we also brought over twenty small QQ penguin plush toys for each scientific researcher, making everyone very happy.

In fact, Tencent has a unique relationship and sentiment with Antarctica. The image of the QQ penguin is deeply embedded in all of Tencent's products and services, and also deeply in the hearts of hundreds of millions of QQ users. Perhaps there is no other company or product in the world that has such an inseparable connection with Antarctic penguins. Because of this, when Tencent solicited electronic postcards from users through QQMail before the Spring Festival to send greetings and blessings to the Antarctic scientific research team, nearly half a million users sent personalized electronic postcards within just two weeks. Among these special gifts I brought to the Great Wall Station are one hundred representative postcards carefully selected from among the half a million postcards. We organized these postcards into a very exquisite PPT according to user categories, hoping that all the scientific researchers at the Great Wall Station can see and hear the blessings of hundreds of millions of QQ users. Among these QQMail electronic postcard users, there are children who have just turned one year old, seventy-four-year-old elders, the entire class of national flag guards, public security police, Tibetan teachers, young Hani girls, former Antarctic scientific research team members, young people hoping to realize their scientific research dreams, Olympic volunteers, earthquake disaster area children and cadres, acrylic manufacturers, newlyweds, elderly couples, and more. Some of these users are familiar to the scientific research team members present, but most resonate deeply with the scientific researchers.

One QQ user wrote a particularly good poem:

Faraway Antarctica,

Vast icy region

Away from home, hard to reunite with family

Hard to see fresh vegetables, come and go

Break the ice and cross the sea, full of ambition and spirit

Scientific research and exploration, for the country and the people

True heroes, forever remembered by the nation

Director Pei especially asked: how did he know that we don't have fresh vegetables here? Every time I read sincere greetings from QQ users to everyone, the team members applaud and cheer! They see and hear the wishes, expectations, and support from global Chinese people! Finally, Director Pei delivered a very touching speech on behalf of all the scientific research team members. He was very surprised that so many people from all walks of life, both at home and abroad, care about the scientific research team. So many QQ users are paying attention to and encouraging the scientific research team. He especially wants to represent the Great Wall Station scientific research team to express thanks and greetings to all QQ users!

In the afternoon, Director Pei specifically told me that he was deeply moved this noon, thanking Tencent for giving him and the entire scientific research team such a big spiritual gift!

Here, I want to share a little story with everyone. Before we set out for Antarctica, we thought the Great Wall Station must have a projector. However, when we reached Punta Arenas, Chile, the vanguard station before entering Antarctica, and prepared to enter Antarctica the next day, we confirmed via text message with one of the scientific research team members at the station that the Great Wall Station does not have a projector. Therefore, I decided to immediately buy one in Punta Arenas and give it to the Great Wall Station. Punta Arenas is a small city, and after searching everywhere, I found two electrical appliance stores. One of them had exactly one decent model, which was also made in China. However, the store clerk only spoke Spanish, couldn't understand English, let alone Chinese. After much effort, I finally negotiated a 95% discount, which came to about 280,000 pesos. Don't be scared, it's approximately equivalent to $500 USD. The next day, I heard that the Chilean peso had depreciated by 5%. Interestingly, after buying it, while leaving the store, I heard the store clerks discussing in Spanish and laughing heartily. They probably thought it was funny that a Chinese person traveled thousands of miles to Chile to buy a Chinese-made projector to take back to China to use! Must be expensive in China, or this person must be crazy. However, I think it is worth it to give it as another special gift to the Antarctic scientific research team.
