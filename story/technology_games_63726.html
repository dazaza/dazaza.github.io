Summarize the purposes of all cheats in Audition.

by fei19830305 on 2008-04-17 23:56:51

Stealth Mode: After turning it on, go to AU. Preferably with three people coordinating to help you go stealth mode - one in the lobby, one spectating, and one as DJ. Close all doors. Ideally, have a good F around, then play in AU! If there's a T prompt indicating being T'd, quickly open F - it must be that specific F. The person spectating will go stealth at this time. Then pull the person from the lobby inside. Once they enter, the spectating person clicks spectate again to pull you up. The DJ will fall into that person's control, then ask them to T you. At this point, you're in stealth mode...

Score Farming (Negative Scores): Nowadays, J cards aren't given out anymore. Farming is useless, but there's still some merit... When jumping, it's best to only jump twice; otherwise, you'll get disconnected while farming negative scores. After two jumps, select the 1677 option for farming negative scores.

Room Crashing: Crash rooms during gameplay. First, jump several times by 10000000 until reaching 2 billion. Wait for an explosion, then farm more points. Don’t farm 1677, as everyone will disconnect, including yourself.

Network Disruption Tool: Network disruption can either freeze indefinitely or crash rooms, starting with the DJ position. Room crashing process > Disconnect network > Confirm > Create room > Restore network. If you don't disconnect, try again. Additionally, if the stage is fully closed but spectating isn't, and the DJ isn't present, use network disruption to force the DJ down for preparation. Soon after, the DJ will be kicked out of the hall.

To crash all positions, one must have been at the DJ position before using network disruption to crash, which will kick them out of the hall.

Network Stealth: Two people coordinate. One uses network disruption to crash, . The other prepares in the lobby to enter.

When the person using network disruption feels like they’re about to disconnect, the person in the lobby quickly enters. This activates stealth. If King version 1.14 is enabled, once the DJ changes position, the room becomes yours. Play! T the person on the DJ position.

Unstealth: Similar to version 3.25. Break stealth during gameplay. Only this method works. Select a song with over 140 beats. When selecting FI songs and pressing F1, the person will get stuck in the game, causing them to disconnect...

Xiaofeng Room-Crashing Tool:

If there are issues like array subscripts, it might be a firewall problem. After resolving, when entering AU, it should display properly.

Free Sneezing: No need to buy paid cheats. Just lacks other functions.

Room Freezing:

1. Enter the room you want to crash. When others are ready, immediately prepare yourself!

2. After preparing, instantly freeze the game using input methods. At this point, the host can't start the game!

3. When the host is frozen, it means you've succeeded halfway. Next, wait for the DJ to fly you (it's better to have a friend inside misleading the DJ).

4. As soon as the DJ flies you, you’ve successfully crashed the room! At this point, you can restore the game and stop freezing the room.

Click confirm to return to the lobby (since you were T'd). In the lobby, find the room showing "In Game". Actually, the game hasn't started yet! It's just stuck there. The DJ inside cannot use any functions and can only click exit! Usually, good room DJs won't give up their rooms easily. But if crashed, there's nothing they can do but abandon it. Then comes room snatching!

Room Snatching: Due to the new lobby update, the button for creating rooms has changed position!

Many friends might think the old renaming method for room snatching no longer works.

I just tested with a friend and found that when you right-click to bring up room information, it covers the create room button. However, I discovered that you can transparently click the create room button through the room information dialog box.

You can judge by sound whether you clicked it or not! The location is slightly above the "Enter" button on the room information, almost like a semi-click.

This way, you can use the old method to snatch rooms!

Freeze Room Information: Freeze room information by setting F to 1/255. I'm sure many of you have seen this in AU! It's actually very simple. Have your friend enter the room, stand on top, then close all doors. Then exit to the lobby and re-enter. Your friend drops you as DJ, then exit and re-enter again. Make sure all doors are completely closed. Repeating this process several times will freeze the room information like this.
