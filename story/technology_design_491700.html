Online to kill time.

by tpvtaoirp on 2011-09-19 19:29:52

Even if I'm lonely, I would rather be alone.

Sometimes, liking a song is just for one person.

As night falls, with a tired heart, how can I fall asleep?

"There is a kind of love called closing one's heart; a kind of affection called forbidden romance; a kind of beauty called reminiscence!"

Suddenly, I feel like I am the extra person among you all.

"Can an angel with wings still be called an angel?"

I am trapped in your heart. There is no intersection at the class reunion.

Love makes people forget time, and time also makes people forget love.

I used to believe every word you said, so how could I trust your words now?

I really want to hide away, letting the whole world not find me!

The rain is so heavy, the thunder so loud, and the lonely person is wandering around!

"Past heartbreaks have left no trace, early autumn drizzle seems like spring..."

"As popularity rises, messages gradually decrease..."

Walking on this street, your smile was once so sweet.

A kind of disappointment, discarding hope, life's confusion, indescribable melancholy!

Every day I loved you because of a beautiful accident.

Looking at this symbol reminds me of you.

Always clinging to something that may never belong to me.

It's you who think too much, never considering the consequences.

Let me lose my memory alone and disappear from your world.

My longing for you is like a kite with its string cut, modern poems praising teachers.

Is there someone who can see through my pretense and protect my weakness?

Each twist and turn has been endured, who is secretly guarding?

Failure is the mother of success! Then who is the father of success?

The flowers on this shore have already bloomed. The person we wait for, why do they not come?

Don't press the video button as soon as you arrive. Do you think your TV will show people when pressed?

Already lacking the courage to speak, even a meeting glance is a luxury.

"Drowsy eyes, white daylight dreams..."

For earning money, sleepless nights...

Online to pass the time, invisible to avoid being a downer.

On rainy days, I dislike using an umbrella, preferring the feeling of being drenched.

When the sky changes, it's about to rain; when a person changes, it's about to break their heart.

Even if you don't belong to me, I don't want you to belong to anyone else.
