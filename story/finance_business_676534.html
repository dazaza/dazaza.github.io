The ultimate trick to double the whitening effect

by wswen156 on 2012-02-26 10:14:30

Oriental women generally have a pursuit for fair skin, and the idea of "being fair covers three ugliness" is deeply rooted in our minds. Especially in summer, whitening becomes a topic of concern for everyone. Changing some small habits or using medical beauty treatments for whitening can have a greater effect.

Daily Small Habits:

1. Washing face with a towel: The biggest obstacle to the absorption of skincare products is the stratum corneum. Just as your house will be cleaner if it's cleaned daily compared to a big clean-up once in a while, you can use a towel to splash water and wipe your face while washing off foam, which helps remove old dead skin cells.

2. Toner: If you have the habit of using toner, you can choose products containing exfoliating enzymes and organic acids, which help melanin metabolize upwards to the stratum corneum, preparing for whitening.

3. Post-sun exposure facial mask: Whitening masks containing exfoliating components are suitable for evenings. After applying, simply wipe off the remaining product without washing your face again, allowing the exfoliating components time to take effect. After sun exposure, you can also apply a mask, but it should contain soothing and antioxidant ingredients in addition to whitening components, to avoid inflammation accelerating melanin production.

4. Sunscreen: People without the habit of using sunscreen will find it hard to become fair even if they immerse themselves in whitening skincare products. The basic SPF should be at least 15, but to achieve the indicated level of protection, about 2 cm of sunscreen from a toothpaste tube is needed just for the face, which is difficult for most people to do, so it’s better to enhance the SPF.

Using external aids for whitening:

1. Whitening skincare products - continuous use is effective. Generally, the way these products work is by inhibiting tyrosinase activity to reduce melanin formation. Products must be used continuously, although the effects are slow, this is the gentlest way to whiten.

Precautions:

a. When choosing products, check the labels carefully. If purchasing L-ascorbic acid (vitamin C), it's best to choose dark bottles and use them up quickly after opening to prevent loss of efficacy.

b. When using whitening masks, the application time on the face should be around 15-20 minutes. If applied for too long, it may cause oxygen deficiency in the skin, leading to aging and counterproductive results.

2. Exfoliation Peels - changing overall skin tone. Using products that accelerate cell renewal and promote keratin shedding to remove epidermal melanocytes, common ingredients include fruit acids over 20% concentration and vitamin A acid over 0.1%. However, there are often redness and stinging sensations after use. Peeling may make the skin tone lighter than the original, but it is more harmful to the skin, requiring more thorough post-care; otherwise, it might lead to hyperpigmentation. Overuse of fruit acid peels may cause melanocyte necrosis, resulting in conditions similar to vitiligo. Suitable for those who want to lighten their overall skin tone or those with oily skin and thicker keratin layers.

Precautions:

a. It is recommended to undergo peeling during autumn and winter when sunlight is weaker. Avoid if there are open wounds, wait until the wound has healed before proceeding.

b. Do not overdo the peeling frequency. Use the product for one week, then let the skin rest for 2-3 weeks, allowing new skin growth.

3. Laser Surgery - Best Spot Removal Effect. In the field of medical aesthetics, laser treatment is widely recognized as the best method for spot removal. The principle is to mildly burn superficial skin with laser, stimulating faster skin cell renewal. This is an invasive treatment with good results, but a longer recovery period. After the laser treatment, the skin will scab and darken, and once the scab falls off, the skin will renew. Suitable for those with black spots and freckles on their faces.

Precautions:

a. After laser surgery, the skin may experience crusting, peeling, and increased sensitivity, making sunscreen very important.

b. Avoid if there are open wounds on the face.

4. Whitening Injections - Safety Requires Testing. Administered via intravenous drip, besides whitening components, additional ingredients can be added according to individual needs, such as fatigue elimination. Usually, injections are given 1-2 times per week, and it takes about 3 months to start seeing effects.

Precautions: The tranexamic acid component in whitening injections can whiten, but may lead to cardiovascular hardening and reduced menstrual flow side effects.
