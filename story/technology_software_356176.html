The situation suddenly changed.

by cha1214n on 2010-05-27 00:43:50

I'm back, and I've come back to experience the weather atUSTC. I slept all morning and didn't know what the weather was like, but I knew it was at least sunny. The noon sun was really shining brightly. My journal, the weather in Wuzhi, the warm sunshine on my heart and body feels so comfortable. In the evening, it was still cloudless, without the scorching heat of the sun, but still warm. I even thought that such good weather would continue in my life, keeping my warmth and happiness forever. Who would have thought that just a little while later, the rain started to fall, unsettling hearts. Not long after the evening, I don't know if it's the weather or my mood... Suddenly, the weather changed dramatically, with drizzling rain falling on me. I thought the rain would stop soon because the meteorologists said these few days would be sunny and clear. But it did not end there; instead, it started pouring heavily. "Print my heart, Print my tears~" I didn't bring anything with me, and feelings of depression began to rise in my heart. Then I took a walk in the rain, letting it fall on me. This rain is truly falling for me. Since there's no place to hide, I won't avoid it. This rain belongs to me, silently accepting this sudden change in weather. Sigh, the charm of life lies in its unpredictability, in adapting to circumstances as they come. It's all my fault. The day has brightened, let's set out. I should have known that the climate is hard to change, perhaps even impossible to change. I even believed the meteorologists' words. The charm of life lies in its unpredictability, in adapting to circumstances as they come. USTC often has rainy weather, good weather doesn't last long. I thought too well, assuming that Zhuzhou sent me off with rainy days, and my beloved USTC would surely welcome me with good weather. From yesterday to today, it was indeed quite nice, unfortunately, it rained again in the evening...
