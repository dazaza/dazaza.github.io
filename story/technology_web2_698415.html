Cement companies introduce concrete mixing plants with fiery production enthusiasm

by anonymous on 2012-03-09 09:24:39

Currently, cement enterprises have different ways to extend their industrial chains. Some companies begin to involve in the concrete business, while others start to engage in aggregate business, which is not wrong since they are all related products. However, there is another point that many companies also get involved in real estate, chemical industry, smelting and other industries, which can be said to be colorful. Nevertheless, entering the sand and gravel, concrete industry is still the mainstream. Currently, China's cement enterprises are fully introducing concrete mixing plants and aggregate production lines.

As an indispensable raw material for commercial concrete, the production of artificial sand and gravel has been mentioned several times by some industry professionals. In the future, cement enterprises will become the main force in the sand and gravel industry. It is indicated that cement enterprises developing aggregate sand and gravel production not only have natural unique advantages but also have a broad development prospect with considerable economic benefits. The reasons are:

1. Existing large and medium-sized cement enterprises have strong capital operation capabilities. With sufficient financial support, compared with individual small aggregate mechanism sand and gravel processing industries, they have obvious capital support, possessing strong competitiveness in participating in the production of mechanism sand and gravel. Due to their strong financial support, they can launch larger-scale sand and gravel production lines.

2. Mineral resources are fully guaranteed. Because large and medium-sized cement production enterprises, when selecting the location for building factories, have already prioritized the guarantee coefficient of mineral resources, most of them are located in mountainous areas and resource-rich geological belts. Therefore, when launching sand and gravel production line projects, it can be said to be the comprehensive utilization of waste rock and turning waste into treasure. Facing inexhaustible mining resources, the sand and gravel production line can produce at full capacity without worry. This is a unique advantage for cement enterprises to vigorously promote this project.

3. There is a relatively stable industrial chain (sand and gravel + cement + concrete + cement products) that can achieve synergistic win-win benefits. Because cement enterprises have relatively stable supply and demand relationships with construction, engineering, infrastructure, road traffic, municipal engineering departments, and the components used by these units such as cement mortar aggregates and concrete, etc., need to be supplied by other production enterprises. If cement enterprises have aggregate sand and gravel products, they can use their own cement products to directly synthesize concrete products and other cement products, creating a comprehensive supply and demand channel for various customers with different needs, forming a one-stop service system. This also builds a fast and unified concrete mixing station platform for product quality, time guarantee, and follow-up services, reducing the costs of exploring new paths, multiple supply, and separate settlement in many links, undoubtedly generating ideal win-win benefits.

4. Launching sand and gravel production projects requires much less investment and takes effect much faster than building a new dry-process cement production line. For example, building a 1 million-ton annual production dry-process cement production line, from design, project initiation, construction to commissioning, requires an investment of 3 billion yuan and a construction period of 300 days; while building a 5000-ton daily production sand and gravel production line only requires an investment of 15 million yuan. Moreover, from project initiation, construction to commissioning, it only takes 100 days to generate profits and benefits.
