Cold to the bone, cold to the depths of the soul

Second Online Submission

The room is vast, the room is pitch black, the room is empty and silent. There is only my crying in the room. Curling up in a corner, only this corner belongs to me.

You suddenly say you want to leave, and I stand there stunned. Panic spreads throughout my body, leaving me weak and powerless, collapsing on the ground. I want to hold you back, but I find that I can no longer speak. All that's left is my hand helplessly clutching my head, tears flowing endlessly, realizing how bitter they are, seeping deep into my heart and continuing to flow inward.

I clearly said I wouldn't cry, yet I can't stop; I clearly said I didn't care, yet I cling desperately; I clearly said I would walk alone, yet I fear loneliness. Only now do I realize how weak I am. How I hope you won't go, won't leave me alone on the street, won't abandon me in the rain, won't let me face all this coldness alone.

It's raining outside, so I ran out and soaked myself thoroughly. But no matter how much I try, I can't wash away those memories.

If only life were always like our first meeting, wouldn't that be wonderful? Then there wouldn't be any farewells; then there wouldn't be any sadness or pain; then we wouldn't care so much. Or perhaps we shouldn't have met at all. But we did meet, and it has already taken root in my heart.

I am searching, I am calling, I am yearning, I am waiting, but everything fades into darkness. Everything is an illusion, everything is fleeting, everything is an empty thought, everything is so sorrowful, and everything feels so powerless.

My phone remains on, still holding onto a sliver of hope that you might call or send a text message. It's always so quiet, always so cold. My heart has completely frozen. I should never have had these fantasies, I should never have had these expectations. The more hope I have, the more endless sorrow I receive. My hopes have never been fulfilled, and I should have known better. But I refused to believe, deceiving myself time and again, unwilling to wake up. In the end, it's all for nothing. Damn these expectations, damn this pain.

Bubbles, that's all. Ha, just bubbles.

So cold, why is it this cold? Cold to the bone, cold to the depths of the soul.

But who can warm me now?

Now, I am icy cold. Now, my heart is shattered. Tonight, I will not sleep.
