Vaguely, I can still recall a face, with such fair skin and delicate features, and deep cold in her eyes. Accompanied by that contemptuous, disdainful voice, echoing through my memory: "Tao Ying, you're not pretty, and your grades are bad too." "What can you do after graduation? Be a bus conductor?" "Or, you could go work for the sanitation bureau as a city cleaner?" It's a fragment of memory, yet clear and sharp. Like tiny shards of blades, small and unremarkable, flying past without leaving visible scars. After a short while, fresh blood gushes out, tendons are all severed, the hero falls, frightened by death - it is precisely because of its smallness that it can kill without form. Perhaps it is precisely because of these years buried deep in my memory, which I do not wish to recall, that I have sincere gratitude for everything I can own today.

"You Are On My Left Hand" Part 1

In the daytime, the street outside the school gate is always bustling with traffic, people rushing from one place to another. Many times, I stood here, watching the comings and goings of people, various faces, and colorful clothing. Due to their great number, I am dazzled. This is a famous university road in the city, besides one university after another, there are countless banks, bookstores, hair salons, snack shops... The road is not wide, yet the traffic is always congested. Those helpless and irritable drivers are accustomed to honking in an attempt to disperse the pedestrians, but the pedestrians always ignore them and recklessly cross through the traffic. Lin Ka said: on this road, two legs are more powerful than four wheels.

My friends, they stand around me, quietly watching my growth - in my room, all four walls are filled with books. The bookshelves were made by my dad, each with five layers. Dad carefully painted them white, making them look bright and exquisite. Then, we together placed thousands of books on them, tightly packed together like neat rows, orderly and well-arranged. Every morning when I wake up and see these books, I feel very happy: it's this simple happiness in this monotonous life; it's the most beautiful happiness I have, aside from my fantasies about princes.
