Beijing Animation and Game Industry Alliance was founded

by cbz77zkn on 2009-11-28 08:18:44

Nanjing Jingao Gaming Industry Alliance fails to sit & nbsp; To open up more international customers for "foreign production" When the alliance assists enterprises outside the industry in developing relevant works for the international market, Liu Qiugang, the first secretary-general of the alliance, told reporters that the alliance will make every effort to create opportunities for members to exchange with the international market. In particular, it is necessary to create more conditions for market personnel to "go out". The creative level of China's online animation picture books is gradually improving, and more and more international customers are beginning to be interested in "China-made". At this stage, it will be very necessary to take good works to the international market for preliminary market development. Among them, in addition to creating more contacts with the international animation industry, the alliance will also provide guidance to enterprises in terms of policy. For example, the "Management (Trial) Measures for the Development Fund of International Markets for Medium and Large Enterprises" formulated by the Ministry of Finance and the Foreign Economic and Trade Department, as well as the accompanying "Implementation Rules (Interim) for the Management Methods of the Development Fund of International Markets for Medium and Large Enterprises in Beijing", the alliance will start from the enterprise perspective, help relevant enterprises better understand and use policies, avoid the shortcomings caused by information asymmetry between enterprises and the government.

The link between the government and enterprises will promote the implementation of preferential policies Bai Yuzhu, Director of the Cultural Industry Department of the Ministry of Culture, said at the establishment conference of the alliance that in recent years, the party and the country's emphasis on animation can be said to be unprecedented. The Ministry of Culture, the Ministry of Finance, and the State Administration of Taxation have successively issued a series of preferential policies, including the recently announced tax preferential policies, which have provided the most real policy support for the development of the animation industry. It is believed that the spring soil for the development of animation enterprises in Beijing and even the whole country has arrived. But in reality, many small and medium-sized enterprises do not know about the specific application of policies. Regarding this, Liu Qiugang said that one of the important tasks of the current alliance during its establishment phase is to assist the government and enterprises in completing the certification work related to enterprises, and provide timely guidance on how small and medium-sized enterprises can better use the hardware and software facilities provided by the government and related institutions.

In serving member enterprises, there are two very important aspects: one is the incubation of large enterprises, and the other is services in the investment and financing aspect. Currently, including banks such as Bank of Beijing and long-term investment and financing institutions, guarantee institutions, and evaluation institutions have begun to notice the potential of Beijing's cultural and creative industries and are willing to contact cultural and creative enterprises. As an alliance itself, in addition to establishing an investment and financing department at this stage and using existing capital flows to help member enterprises obtain more financial support, it has also started to contact relevant institutions such as the Investment and Financing Promotion Center of Zhongguancun Science Park under the identity of the alliance, hoping to obtain more special funds support in the near future.

Members: Protect rights and self-discipline to be good owners

At the establishment conference of the alliance, Lu Gongguan, Director of the Beijing Bureau of Culture, repeatedly emphasized, "We hope that you (alliance members) clearly understand your rights and responsibilities when joining the association. You join the association to protect rights and self-discipline through the industrial alliance."

When talking about the issue of self-discipline, Hao Yaning stated that as an economic entity, although the alliance cannot directly provide financial assistance to members, it can use its influence in the industry to actively promote and organize enterprises to formulate industry standards and implement industry professional ethics principles, promote industry integrity construction, establish and improve the self-discipline governance constraint mechanism of the industry, standardize member behavior, coordinate member relations, and maintain a fair competitive market environment.

In terms of rights protection, the alliance will actively organize enterprises to learn intellectual property laws and regulations, assist members in carrying out intellectual property protection work, protect member interests, curb unfair competition, and promote the healthy development of the industry.
