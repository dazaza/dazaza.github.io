Wang Luodan and Li Guangjie mutually unfollow each other on Weibo, move on to new relationships, ending their secret affair.

In August last year, Wang Luodan and Li Guangjie continued the love of Lao San and Jingqiu in the drama "Love of the Hawthorn Tree" to real life. There was also a lot of talk about the previous female lead Jiang Yayan leaving the production. Yesterday, reporters received a tip-off that Wang Luodan and Li Guangjie, who have been dating for over a year, have already broken up, and Wang Luodan has started dating a new boyfriend outside of the entertainment circle. The reporter sought confirmation from Wang Luodan's agent Mr. Jian, who stated that he would not respond to the private affairs of artists, while Li Guangjie's agent Yuan Feiyu's phone was unanswered. However, it was found from their Weibo accounts that the two, who used to interact frequently on Weibo, have now unfollowed each other, which makes the breakup rumor seem plausible. (Click on the image to enter Wang Luodan's Weibo.)

Regarding the fact that I didn't act in "Love of the Hawthorn Tree," please don't feel regretful about it. It wasn't mine to take, I didn't fight for it, and I've enabled more harmonious collaborators. This was a Weibo post by the originally designated female lead, Jiang Yayan, before the TV version of "Love of the Hawthorn Tree" began filming last year. Afterward, Wang Luodan replaced her as the female lead, and that sentence about enabling more harmonious collaborators brought the romance between Wang Luodan and Li Guangjie to light.

An insider revealed at the time that the two sparked a connection while collaborating on the TV series "Du Lala's Promotion Diary." Their interactions on Weibo were incredibly intimate; Li Guangjie called Wang Luodan 'Wang Lala,' while Wang Luodan referred to him as 'Li Wangwei' or 'Uncle Li.' Those warm Weibo posts like 'Li Wangwei sent me a text' gave clear indications of their relationship. At the time, an insider confidently confirmed to the reporter that the two were together. Although neither Wang Luodan nor Li Guangjie admitted to being in a romantic relationship afterward, with Li Guangjie describing her as a good friend, the reporter learned that they had been photographed returning to an apartment together by a certain weekly magazine, entering and exiting together, and behaving very affectionately.

However, this relationship, which lasted over a year, recently ended in a split. Besides reliable sources revealing this information, there were also signs on Weibo. The reporter discovered that Wang Luodan and Li Guangjie, who originally followed each other and interacted frequently, had mutually unfollowed each other, almost completely disappearing from each other's Weibos without a trace. According to an informed Ms. X, the breakup is true, and it seems that Li Guangjie met someone new first, then they broke up. She revealed that not long after Li Guangjie's divorce last year, he collaborated on a new play with Wang Luodan, drawn to her straightforwardness and purity. However, they later quarreled and became unhappy due to work issues, during which time Li Guangjie met a girl who might not have strong career ambitions and was of the clingy type.

Although Ms. X revealed that Li Guangjie found a new lover first, she indicated that Wang Luodan also has a new boyfriend now, someone outside the entertainment circle. With her career development going smoothly, Wang Luodan, who is assertive in her work, wants to find a man who can make her look up to him in love.
