Advantages of M Series Street Light Fans

by 54szflfdj on 2012-03-01 09:05:48

Looking to the future, the utilization rate of wind energy will become higher and higher. Wind energy is an inexhaustible and renewable energy source, and using wind energy to generate electricity has been increasingly accepted and applied in real life by more and more people. Among these applications, street lamps are evidently a mode of transportation used by every country, province, or even town. Installing wind turbines on street lamps or achieving normal lighting through wind-solar complementary systems has become a beautiful aspiration in our minds. However, upon reviewing the entire nation, the distribution of wind resources is not equal. Some areas have abundant wind resources, while many other regions have relatively scarce wind resources. In order to make our wind turbines function well on street lamps across the country, we have developed and designed dedicated wind turbines for street lamps. After our tests and market tracking investigations over the past few years, it has been proven that the market share occupied by our street lamp wind turbines is entirely due to their strength. More notably, this type of wind turbine demonstrates excellent competitiveness in various fields such as landscape lighting, surveillance systems, highway warning signs, lighting and communication power supply in forest and pastoral areas, and lighting and communication on fishing boats. Through our long-term and unwavering efforts, our once beautiful aspiration has finally come true.

The street lamp-dedicated wind turbines we produce are mainly reflected in the M series, namely M-300 and M-400 models. The S series wind turbines, relying on their large power generation capacity, have also become popular in many areas with abundant wind energy. Many provinces and cities across the country, including Jiangsu, Guangdong, Xinjiang, Henan, Shandong, Inner Mongolia, Tianjin, Anhui, Guangxi, Hebei, etc., have seen our company's street lamp wind turbines!

The reason why so many people favor our street lamp wind turbines is that they must have unique characteristics and advantages.

First, micro-wind start-up and stable power generation. This is the greatest and most obvious advantage of our M series wind turbines. In some areas with good wind resources, this advantage may not be fully demonstrated, but for those areas with less wind, such as Yunnan Province and Chongqing Municipality, it is particularly evident. In areas with an annual average wind speed of 1 m/s, our street lamp wind turbines can still work normally, which cannot be matched by other wind turbines.

Second, DC output and built-in controller. The M series wind turbines have built-in controllers and rectifier bridges, providing DC output. In many wind-solar complementary street lamp projects, having a wind turbine with a built-in controller eliminates the need for an additional wind-solar complementary controller, saving a considerable amount of budget, which is quite appealing. Why not take advantage of it?

Third, simple and reliable structure, convenient installation, aesthetically pleasing appearance, and strong mobility. The entire M series wind turbine is made of high-quality fiberglass material through die-casting, weighing about 10 kg, making it easy to carry and giving this wind turbine a strong mobility feature that many others lack. Moreover, its attractive appearance allows the street lamp wind turbine to stand out in areas like landscape lighting, decorative brightening, and garden beautification.

Fourth, and very importantly, low failure rate and strong typhoon resistance. Our wind turbines all adopt unique body anti-vibration designs, ensuring smooth operation, high stability, and no noise. A series of objectively existing advantages have contributed to the long lifespan of the wind turbines (up to 15-20 years). Moreover, after several years of market research, it was found that the failure rate of this type of street lamp wind turbine is extremely low, unaffected by factors such as wind direction, and has strong typhoon resistance (typhoon resistance up to 35 m/s). For northern regions with strong winds, such robust wind resistance capabilities will certainly satisfy consumers and give them peace of mind.

The Skywing series' S series wind turbines are also highly favored by customers. This stems from the inherent advantages of the S series wind turbines. They use clamp-style connections, with a simple structure where only one screw fixes the three blades, making installation very convenient. Although the S series wind turbines have a slight disadvantage in starting wind speed compared to the M series, this is perfectly compensated for by their impressive power generation capacity. In terms of market share in the street lamp wind turbine sector, this series of wind turbines shows great potential to surpass the M series.

Undoubtedly, in the future, we will continue to strive hard in the field of street lamp wind turbines and achieve even greater accomplishments. We believe we will bring you more miracles and surprises!
