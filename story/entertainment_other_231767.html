QQ Sound Message is a wireless Internet value-added service launched by Tencent. You can also casually express yourself by publishing texts, voices, or picture messages on the web to record the wonderful moments of life.

How to activate the QQ Sound Message icon?

Users with an activity index greater than or equal to 10 can obtain the Sound Message icon through mutual gifting among users. After receiving the gift, the Sound Message icon will automatically light up. After lighting up the Sound Message icon, the system will also give 5 icons for free, which can be used to gift other QQ Sound Message users and help them light up their icons. After users light up the icon, the icon status will change according to the activity index. Users with an activity index greater than or equal to 10 will have their Sound Message icons in the lit state; users with an activity index less than 10 will have their Sound Message icons automatically turned off.

Sound Message Aristocrat Privilege: Opening QQ Sound Message Aristocrat will automatically light up the Sound Message icon. Open Sound Message Aristocrat immediately >>>

How to activate the QQ Sound Message icon for free?

Log in to QQ Sound Message, open the sending sound message; next, choose the free sound message, click the "casual talk" on the right side; after entering, casually send one (free), after succeeding, there will be 5 points of activity the next day, and lighting up requires 10 points of activity, so, you need to send it for two consecutive days, then ask a friend for a sound message icon seed, and it will automatically light up! If it doesn't light up, you can manually light it up at the Sound Message Icon Center!

How to turn off the QQ Sound Message icon?

After users light up the icon, the icon status will change according to the activity index. Users with an activity index less than 10 will have their Sound Message icons automatically turned off. As long as you send a sound message, increasing the activity index to over 10, the icon will automatically light up.

Of course, if you don't like it, you can also manually turn off the icon by logging into http://yinxin.qq.com/icon_index.shtml anytime.

How to check the status of the QQ Sound Message icon and the QQ Sound Message activity index?

Please log in to http://yinxin.qq.com/icon_index.shtml to check.

Calculation method for activity index:

Methods to increase the activity index:

1. Send sound messages:

* Each time you send a sound message to a phone or QQ, the activity index increases by 20.

* Send a sound message to the webpage (casual talk), the activity index increases by 5. The activity index increases on the second day when sending to the webpage.

* Sending multiple sound messages to the webpage on the same day does not cumulatively increase the activity index.

2. Gift icons: For every 1 sound message icon gifted to another user, helping other users light up their icons, the activity index increases by 5.

3. First-time lighting up of icons: Upon first-time lighting up of the sound message icon, you are given 20 points of activity index.

Sound Message Aristocrat Double Privilege: When Sound Message Aristocrats send sound messages, gift icons, and first-time light up icons, the activity index doubles.

Activity Index Explanation:

1. Regular users' activity index can reach up to 100, while Sound Message Aristocrats' activity index can reach up to 200.

2. All users' activity index decreases automatically by 1 each day.

3. If your activity index has not increased in real time, it may be due to network delay, and we will make up for it within 24 hours.

QQ Sound Message Introduction

1. What is QQ Sound Message?

QQ Sound Message is a wireless Internet value-added service launched by Tencent. Through QQ Sound Message, you can send voice blessings to the phones or QQ of your loved ones, friends, and colleagues. You can also casually express yourself by publishing texts, voices, or picture messages on the web to record the wonderful moments of life.

2. How do I send QQ Sound Messages to my friends?

Very simple. You just need to enter the recipient's phone number or QQ number on QQ Sound Message, write down what you want to say, QQ Sound Message will automatically convert it into voice and call the recipient's phone. He/she just needs to answer it like a regular incoming call.

3. Which phones can receive QQ Sound Messages?

All phones in mainland China can. Whether it's a fixed phone, mobile phone, or Xiaolingtong, they can all receive your voice messages.

Cost Information

1. How much does it cost to send sound messages to a friend's phone or QQ?

For each successful sound message sent to a friend's phone or QQ, it costs 1 Q-coin per message (for Q-coin paying users) or 1 yuan per message (for SMS paying users). If the recipient does not answer, or if the message fails to send due to system busyness, you can re-send it for free after logging into QQ Sound Message. This free re-sending opportunity will remain until you successfully send it.

2. Does it cost money to send messages to the webpage, i.e., casual talk?

Sending texts, voices, and picture messages to the webpage is completely free.

3. Does the recipient need to pay to receive the sound message via mobile phone?

Receiving your sound message is just like receiving a regular phone call, no information fee is charged.

4. How to use mobile phone SMS to pay for QQ Sound Message?

Cost:

SMS payment cost is 1 yuan per session.

Note: The recipient does not pay any information fee, receiving your voice blessing is just like receiving a regular phone call.

Activation instructions and regions

Mobile: Edit SMS 31 and send to 1066 6226 9

(Shandong Mobile users edit SMS 6 and send to 1066 6226 934)

Unicom: Edit SMS 3 and send to 1066 6226 791

Xiaolingtong: Edit SMS 811 and send to 1062 1700 46 (Not supported in Anhui, Hebei, Heilongjiang regions)

Chongqing, Shandong Xiaolingtong users please send instructions to 1062 5700 46

Jilin Xiaolingtong users please send instructions to 1062 8700 46

Liaoning, Qinghai, Ningxia, Gansu, Xinjiang, Tibet Xiaolingtong users please send instructions to 1066 1700 46.

5. In what situations will free sending opportunities be returned?

If the user pays to use QQ Sound Message but fails to send it successfully, the system will return one free sending opportunity. After logging in with your QQ number, the number of free sending opportunities will be displayed in the login area and payment process. If it's a free sending or paid by advertisers, the failure will not return a free opportunity.

6. How is the charge for sending sound messages through the QQ Sound Message robot?

Sending sound messages through the QQ Sound Message robot is the same as sending from the webpage, charging 1 yuan per message to a friend's phone or QQ, and completely free to send to the webpage, i.e., casual talk.

QQ Sound Message Icon

1. How to light up the QQ Sound Message icon?

Complete the following two steps to automatically light up:

1. Send QQ Sound Messages, raising the Sound Message activity index to over 10;

2. Find a user who has already lit up the QQ Sound Message icon, let him/her gift you one Sound Message icon. After receiving the gifted icon, the QQ Sound Message icon will automatically light up.

Sound Message Aristocrat Privilege: Activating Sound Message Aristocrat will automatically light up the Sound Message icon.

2. How to increase my Sound Message activity index?

You can increase the Sound Message activity index through the following methods:

1.

Send sound messages

* Each time you send a sound message to a phone or QQ, the activity index increases by 20.

* Send a sound message to the webpage (casual talk), the activity index increases by 5. The activity index increases on the second day when sending to the webpage.

* Sending multiple sound messages to the webpage on the same day does not cumulatively increase the activity index.

2.

Gift icons

For every 1 Sound Message icon gifted to another user, helping other users light up their icons, the activity index increases by 5.

3.

First-time lighting up of icons

Upon first-time lighting up of the Sound Message icon, you are given 20 points of activity index.

Sound Message Aristocrat Double Privilege:

Sound Message Aristocrats sending sound messages, gifting icons, and first-time lighting up icons, the activity index doubles.

3. Is there a maximum activity index limit?

The highest activity index for regular users is 100, and for Sound Message Aristocrats, it is 200. After reaching the maximum activity index, gifting icons and sending sound messages will not increase the activity index.

4. Will the activity index decrease?

All users' activity index decreases automatically by 1 each day.

5. Why didn't my activity index increase after sending sound messages?

Please check if you've reached the upper limit of the activity index; sending multiple sound messages to the webpage on the same day (casual talk) does not cumulatively increase the activity index; sending sound messages to the webpage increases the activity index on the second day; if sending to a phone or QQ, the activity index did not increase in real time, possibly due to system busyness, and we will make up for it within 24 hours.

6. I lit up the Sound Message icon before, why did it automatically turn off?

After users light up the icon, the icon status will change according to the activity index. Users with an activity index greater than or equal to 10 will have their Sound Message icons in the lit state; users with an activity index less than 10 will have their Sound Message icons automatically turned off.

7. My icon turned off, how do I light it up again?

Just send sound messages, raising the activity index to over 10, and the icon will automatically light up.

Sound Message Aristocrat

1. How to activate Sound Message Aristocrat?

Currently, you can activate Sound Message Aristocrat using mobile phones, Q-coins/Q-points, QQ cards, online banking, Tenpay, etc.,

Specific methods please click here to choose the corresponding payment method and follow the prompts to activate.

2. How is the free sending count calculated for Sound Message Aristocrat?

Starting from the date of activation, Sound Message Aristocrats can freely send 15 sound messages to phones or mobiles each month, and freely send 120 sound messages to QQ friends each month. These free sending counts are valid for the current month, and unused free counts will not accumulate to the next month. For users who continuously activate Sound Message Aristocrat for n months, each month an additional n×30 free sending counts to QQ will be rewarded.

3. How is QQ Sound Message Aristocrat charged?

QQ Sound Message Aristocrat fee: 10 yuan/month.

4. What functional privileges does QQ Sound Message Aristocrat have?

* Monthly free sending of 15 sound messages to phones/mobiles, and free sending of 120 sound messages to QQ friends.

For users who continuously activate Sound Message Aristocrat service, for each additional month of continuous activation, an additional reward of 30 free sound messages to QQ will be given.

* Sound Message Aristocrats can use the scheduled sending function (regular users cannot use it)

* Sound Message Aristocrats can use the custom song function (regular users cannot use it)

* Sound Message Aristocrats can automatically light up the Sound Message icon and automatically own the Sound Message badge

* Sound Message Aristocrats can prioritize experiencing the Sound Message phonograph function

For more details, please log in to the Sound Message Aristocrat website, address: http://yinxin.qq.com/vip/.

5. How to use the custom song function of QQ Sound Message Aristocrat?

Custom songs are one of the privileges of Sound Message Aristocrats. Operation steps are as follows:

1. During the process of sending sound messages, click the "pick a song" button, and "system songs" and "personal music library" will appear.

2. Sound Message Aristocrats can upload 10 songs in the personal music library, and select songs from the personal music library when sending sound messages to friends.

6. How to use the scheduled sending function of QQ Sound Message?

Scheduled sending function is one of the privileges of Sound Message Aristocrats, when Sound Message Aristocrats send sound messages, they can choose immediate sending,

Or choose scheduled sending, at a specified time after 1 hour, this way they won't forget to send greetings to friends.

7. How to cancel the monthly subscription of Sound Message Aristocrat activated by mobile phone?

Mobile users edit the message GBCQ and send it to 1066 6226 to deactivate the Sound Message Aristocrat service.

Note: Unicom and Xiaolingtong do not support activation currently.

Sound Message Phonograph

1. What is the Sound Message Phonograph?

The Sound Message Phonograph is a flash plugin on Qzone. Through the Sound Message Phonograph, you can place a personalized voice self-introduction on Qzone, and your friends can also leave voice messages for you on Qzone.

2. What conditions are needed to activate the Sound Message Phonograph?

* Currently only available to some users, Sound Message Aristocrats and Yellow Diamond users can prioritize experiencing it.

* Need to upgrade Qzone to version 5.0 or above.

3. How to activate the Sound Message Phonograph?

1. Add the Phonograph on Qzone. Log in to Qzone, choose Customize > Module > Sound Message Phonograph on the personal homepage, and it will immediately appear in the upper left corner of the space.

2. Configure my Phonograph. First-time users will see a prompt in the newly appeared Phonograph, clicking "Configure my Phonograph now" will open a new window to start configuration. Save after configuring.

Mobile Sound Message

1. What is Mobile Sound Message?

Sending sound messages by editing SMS on the mobile phone, expressing feelings anytime and anywhere.

2. How to send Mobile Sound Messages?

Mobile phone users edit SMS Q to 1066 1700 1266, experience immediately.

Quick experience channel: Edit SMS: recipient's phone number, message to 1066 1700 1266 1. For example: 13812345678, How have you been recently?

Note: Liaoning Mobile and Unicom, Xiaolingtong users are temporarily unsupported.

3. What is the cost for Mobile Sound Messages?

Sending one sound message costs 1 yuan as an information fee. If there are free opportunities, the free opportunity will be consumed first without deducting the information fee.

4. What if Mobile Sound Message sending fails?

If the information fee or QQ free opportunity has been deducted and the sound message fails to send, the system will return one free opportunity to your QQ, allowing you to use the free opportunity to send the sound message on your mobile phone.

Sound Message Robot

1. What is the Sound Message Robot?

Adding 77170088 as a friend allows you to receive and send sound messages on QQ, and you can consult and complain to it anytime.

Successfully adding the robot allows you to send sound messages, post suggestions, view unread messages, and understand the latest activities anytime and anywhere.

2. How is the charge for sending sound messages through the robot?

1 Q-coin/session.

3. Why am I always prompted that the data I sent is incorrect?

* Ensure the completeness of the data entered. Phone/QQ number, the recipient's nickname, your nickname, sound message content.

* The phone number should be filled with the recipient's 11-digit mobile number or "area code + landline number". For example: 13912345678 or 01088888888.

* Nickname should be limited to within 8 Chinese characters, and sensitive characters are prohibited.

* Wishes should be within 125 Chinese characters.

Other Common Issues

1. What to do if QQ Sound Message sending fails but fees were deducted?

If the paid QQ Sound Message fails to send, the system will return one free sending opportunity, which can be used during the next sending to send QQ Sound Messages.

2. How to check the number of my QQ Sound Message free opportunities?

Log in to the QQ Sound Message website (http://yinxin.qq.com) and check the number of free sending opportunities in the login status bar at the top right of the navigation bar.

3. Will QQ friends receive a notification on the client when I send them a sound message?

When sending QQ Sound Messages to QQ friends, they will receive a system message notification on the client.

4. Is there a time limit for the obtained free sending opportunities?

No.

5. Why does the system always prompt me that my wishes contain sensitive words when sending sound messages?

It is likely that your wishes contain content with the same pinyin as system-set sensitive words. For example, the pinyin qingse of the word "青涩" is the same as "情色", so it was not approved. It is suggested to appropriately modify the wishes and filter out sensitive sentences. Providing a safe, healthy, and active online platform for users is a consistent principle of Tencent, please understand and support.

6. Is there a character limit for QQ Sound Message self-written wishes?

The character limit for QQ Sound Message self-written wishes is 100 Chinese characters.

7. How can the phone receiver reply?

The phone receiver can press any key on the phone to record a reply during the process of answering QQ Sound Messages.

8. Why does it keep prompting "processing" after placing the order?

* After the first sound message call fails, the system will automatically call every 5 minutes, for a total of 3 times, so it will prompt "processing" within 10 minutes.

* Our system is busy (usually during holidays).
