How to choose a seller on Taobao, I won't elaborate on this issue, as it's a matter of personal judgment, right! Most people still look at the credit rating and reviews. Actually, the most important point is to see the ratio of the number of transactions for a certain model of phone to the number of reviews. I saw in one store that they sold 1371 units of the 5800 model, but there were only 13 reviews. What does this indicate? I have been thinking about it for a long time without finding an answer. I can only warn everyone to be extremely careful when encountering such sellers! It cannot be ruled out that some people returned the goods, or deliberately placed orders without payment, creating a false impression of high transaction volume!

Now that we have talked about how to choose a good seller, let me talk about what we should discuss with the seller (JS) after selecting a good seller, so that we can use it as evidence later.

The following suggestions are for those buying domestic models:

1. Guarantee the device, issue an invoice (a formal national warranty VAT invoice), ensure that the packaging box serial number matches, the body and all accessories are brand new and original, with no signs of use, including no scratches on the battery contact points and SIM card contact points (request the JS to test the machine without using the original battery), screws show no signs of being opened, and there are no call records or any other traces that may prove it has been used or refurbished.

2. After purchasing the device, before signing for it, if the buyer finds that the color of the device is wrong, the accessories are incomplete, or there are signs of use, the buyer has the right to refuse to sign and notify the seller, who must unconditionally refund the full purchase amount. After signing, within 7 days, if the buyer discovers through verification that the device is not a domestic model or for other reasons does not wish to purchase it, provided that the device has not been turned on or used and all accessories are intact, the device can be returned, and the seller must unconditionally refund or exchange it. Warranty period is 15 days, and warranty service lasts for one year.

For Hong Kong models, add the following:

3. During the warranty period, if the device suffers from any non-human-induced damage, the seller must be responsible for unconditional warranty service. If the Hong Kong Nokia cannot be repaired locally, the seller must handle the warranty on behalf of the buyer! The buyer bears 50% of the shipping cost!

One of the most important points: remember to remind the JS:

(Write the battery number, mobile phone serial number, memory card, earphone, and charger numbers on the front or back of the invoice and stamp it, this is very important)

Supplement: Some JS put their own sealing stickers, so be particularly careful with these. Request them not to put the sticker on the screws of the body, because some machines are refurbished, the screws have scratches, and the JS intentionally cover them with a seal, and we can't possibly remove the seal to check, right? Suning and Gome selling domestic models never put seals on the screws!
