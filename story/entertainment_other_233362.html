QQ Zone Happy Diary. Non-mainstream diary about happiness

by lwf225 on 2009-09-04 21:35:24

Tags: QQ Zone Sad Logs, QQ Zone Emotional Logs

Introduction:

Barely combing through the traces of the past under the faint moonlight, I, with my persistence, have already become physically and mentally exhausted! Walking with my head down, your figure lingers in my mind. Perhaps you are not the best, but I only love you...

[A man left, another man approached me]

The night was so quiet it felt cold, and my heart followed suit, feeling uneasy. Why is the noisy night so silent in my world? My heart feels heavy, as lonely as this night; my heart feels still, as dead silent as this night. Faced with my boyfriend's betrayal, my pain has nowhere to escape, slowly spreading in my heart...

I wander alone in the online world, reading the most touching chapters, listening to the most melancholic music. Tears quietly fall, silently, and the pain is tearing my heart apart...

My QQ avatar kept jumping, reading one message after another, I quietly closed the message channel, not replying to anyone’s messages. Only he continued to send concerned inquiries as usual. Maybe because there were too many grievances and too much pain, I started chatting with him. I told him that I am very distressed now. He asked why, and I told him that my boyfriend had found a new lover and shamelessly said, "I hope we can maintain this kind of ambiguous relationship." I love him, but I don’t know how to accept such a relationship...

He listened silently as I vented my frustrations. Unknowingly, it was already 3 AM. I told him, "Thank you for listening to all of this, I should go back now." He sent a message asking for my number. I don't know why, but I gave it to him...

That day happened to be Christmas Eve. Leaving the internet cafe, cold wind came rushing, I hugged my arms across my chest, wiped the tear marks from the corners of my eyes, and quickly walked towards home...

[He said he would give me happiness, I laughed it off]

When I got home, it was already 4 AM. He called me. He said he believed in love at first sight, and that was what he felt for me. He told me, "I like you, come over to me, I can give you happiness." After hearing his words, I laughed it off because too many men had said the same thing to me. Although where he was, there was the sea I liked...

Since that Christmas Eve, he called me every day. Gradually, I started getting used to waiting for his calls, accustomed to his gentle words. For the past relationship, I didn't want to mention it, nor did I want to remember it, but I knew I couldn't forget. I told him everything, but he still didn't care, continuing to call me as usual.

Maybe because I feared loneliness, I started having feelings for him, but I kept them hidden deep inside. I thought things could continue like this, far and light...

[His sudden arrival, deepening emotions]

One evening later, he suddenly told me: "I'm at Dongguan train station, come pick me up." Hearing his words, it really startled me. It was too sudden. I wasn't ready to meet him, and besides, I wasn't sure if I truly liked him or if it was just an emotional attachment. That night, I refused...

He stayed in Dongguan for a day. He said work was busy and the company was urging him to return. But he said he didn't want to go back, he couldn't bear to leave me, and asked me to go with him. But I couldn't. I had my job, my family. If I left, I'd lose everything. I didn't know what I was thinking, but my heart felt uneasy...

On the second evening, I wanted to see him off at the train station, but he lied and said he didn't have a ticket, then turned back. Later, we went out for a walk, each telling our own stories. I held nothing back, telling him everything, not for any other reason, but because I wanted to share the story I had kept buried in my heart for a long time. After telling him my story, I was in tears, looking quite haggard. He held my hand, interlocking our fingers, and said to me, "Yanyu, I will always be by your side, I won't leave you alone." Looking into his eyes, I saw deep into his soul and felt his sincerity without a trace of falsehood. At that moment, I firmly believed in the man in front of me, without any doubt...

On the third day, due to his work, he couldn't stay any longer. He said if he didn't go back, the consequences would be severe. When he left, he made a promise to me: "I am willing to settle down with you in Hunan." He was from Sichuan, and I was from Hunan. This statement further strengthened my trust in him.

After he left, I was like someone poisoned, my mind full of thoughts about him. All the memories were of the three days we spent together, the streets we walked, the snacks we ate.

Having been hurt in love before, I was persistent in matters of the heart!

[Happiness and Pain Often Coexist]

Thus, we continued this relationship, and I thought I could find happiness. Unconsciously, I deeply fell in love with him.

But, in July this year, a strange woman left a comment on my space: "We must be destined to meet, since we both know the same person." Seeing her comment, my heart was unsettled. A woman's intuition told me it might be him. I was restless all day but dared not ask him. So that night, I went to the internet cafe and stayed up all night. I hoped to encounter the woman who left the comment on my space. I just wanted to know what I should have known. Around eleven o'clock that night, he called me and asked what I was doing. Unable to hold back, I asked him, "Who is 'Autumn Wind'?" He paused for a moment on the phone and said, "A friend, what's wrong?" I didn't like keeping things bottled up, especially with him. I hoped we could be honest with each other. I continued questioning him, but he avoided answering directly. Later, the sister who left the comment told me everything: he also called and messaged her daily, their relationship being similar to mine, except they hadn't met yet. She told me: "He is not someone you can rely on."

After hearing everything from her, I felt the whole world turn gray. This deceitful man, why does he treat me this way? Why does he deceive my feelings? Why does he break his promises after making them? Why are all men like this? I thought, even if the whole world betrayed me, he wouldn't betray me. But before the whole world betrayed me, he already had...

He called me again, but he denied it, saying, "Trust me, I have no relationship with her, you are my only one." I shouted loudly, "I already know everything, why do you still want to deceive me..." After hanging up, I cried uncontrollably in a corner...

[What's Wrong with a Woman's True Love]

Things have come to this, I don't want to think about anything else. I know my heart has already cooled, and I've seen through it all.

In those days, he kept apologizing, but I didn't want to listen to any of his explanations. My heart, once pained, gradually became numb. What could I still believe?

On July 20th, perhaps he realized he might lose me. He quit his job and came to Dongguan from Liaoning, just to apologize, to prove that he loved only me. As for his connections with other women, he claimed they were meaningless. He said I didn't understand him, and maybe I really didn't. But is misunderstanding really the best excuse and reason for his betrayal?

He arrived in Dongguan at around six in the morning. Downstairs from where I lived, I saw him. He looked much more haggard than before, his face covered in stubble, no color at all, looking very worn out. Seeing him, I felt very sorry for him. He also saw me and without saying a word, tightly embraced me, "Yanyu, don't leave me, don't break up, please forgive me, I love you, please believe me." I stared blankly at him, unsure of what attitude I should take toward him. Seeing my silence, he continued, "Yanyu, you must believe me, I have no feelings for 'Autumn Wind.' You never wanted to come to me, so I acted like this. I know it was wrong, I will change, please believe me, okay?"

Listening to what he said, all the memories I had forgotten came flooding back: happiness, promises, betrayal, all rushing out at once. I broke down in tears... He kept comforting me, "Yanyu, don't cry, Yanyu, I'm sorry, I'm sorry... Yanyu, don't cry, say something."

Faced with this man, I helplessly lowered my guard, but I couldn't let go of my love for him. I forgave him under his earnest pleading. Perhaps, this is the weakest and most fatal point of women, but I really loved him...

After staying in Dongguan for two more days, he had to go back because of work. This time, I watched him leave, facing the wind, and said to the direction he was going, "Don't disappoint me."

It's laughable, because after that, I became friends with the sister who left the comment. Later, when I chatted with her about our current relationship, she cared a lot, though I didn't mean to stir up her wounds.

One day in September, she told me, "He called me yesterday, asking what I was doing, saying some very ambiguous things, saying how much he missed me..."

Hearing these words, I felt very uneasy inside.

[Love Overture, I Just Want Simple Happiness]

I'm not in good health, I have a genetic disease inherited from my family. I told him, but he said, "I don't care, even if you will lose your life tomorrow, I want to spend tonight with you."

Actually, he doesn't know that in July, when he came to Dongguan and then went back, I fell seriously ill after that, running a high fever, and then developing appendicitis. On July 31st, I underwent surgery in the hospital, but I didn't tell him because I was afraid to face him. Even though I said I trusted him, it wasn't true that I didn't care. The pain I felt during my illness wasn't as painful as the pain in my heart...

I've told him that my love cannot have any flaws, not even a little. But for this man full of stories, I love him thoroughly and helplessly. Until now, until today, he still calls me, still loves me, but I know this love carries too many layers of meaning.

Now he keeps asking me to go to him, but after all these things, how can I go to him with a balanced mindset?

I know his QQ password, but because I trust him, I don't want to look. However, I was afraid and ended up checking. Logging into his QQ, among his contacts, there were over four hundred people, and they were all girls, coincidentally all from Hunan...

I don't know how to explain all of this. Perhaps his love for me is real. Perhaps the sister is right when she says, "He is not someone you can rely on." QQ Zone Code

Who can tell me, is this seemingly contradictory man sincere or insincere?

If love means mutual destruction, I am willing to get hurt first, to let myself be injured before him..

Later, on his birthday, he drank a lot and called me. He didn't say anything, just cried, just shed tears, just like how I used to cry helplessly in front of him, and my heart ached...

Actually, I don't want anything. I just want simple happiness...

Postscript:

Here the story comes to an end, omitting some details. Xia Mo spent several days thinking about how to narrate Yanyu's story, and for a long time, had no clue. Regarding love, Xia Mo understands somewhat but also knows that loving someone involves going through many obstacles and difficulties, and those who ultimately make it to the end possess the most touching true love..

Xia Mo sincerely hopes that all truly loving couples can move forward hand in hand, blessings..
