【Dulü Refinement - Golden Armor Divine Ox】

The Golden Armor Divine Ox is the mount of Laozi, the Supreme Elder. The blue ox clad in battle armor appears in the "Ao Jian" world, bringing not only mythical attribute enhancements but also allowing players to acquire unparalleled mount skills in martial chaos, joining them in the struggle for dominance in the jianghu and providing the power to defeat enemies. Furthermore, it will open up an even stronger and more splendid path for mount evolution.

【Ultimate Northern Strength - Celestial Execution Wolf】

Forged with gold super armor, its snow domain glow cannot be obstructed. It is the Celestial Execution Wolf from the "Ao Jian" legend. The Celestial Execution Wolf from the far northern desert brings formidable attribute boosts and lightning-fast movement speed to players. Additionally, as the ultimate northern strength, the Celestial Execution Wolf can become a fierce warrior in chaotic battles, carving a bloody path for players' royal dominance.

【Buddhist War God - Xuanming Sacred Tiger】

Nurtured in the Buddhist sacred lotus beneath the Arhat Vajra, this is the majestic posture of the Xuanming Sacred Tiger in "Ao Jian." Under the effect of the Bone Strengthening Pill, the Celestial Execution Wolf transforms into the Xuanming Sacred Tiger, granting players the glory of being the Lord of Tigers and obtaining the earth-shaking power of the Xuanming Sacred Tiger. With powerful attribute enhancements, players will transform into war gods in "Ao Jian," taking enemy heads amidst thousands of soldiers!

【King of Beasts - Crimson Flame Lion】

With a mighty roar and a startling figure that disrupts the chaotic world, the King of Beasts, the Crimson Flame Lion, makes an appearance at the "Ao Jian" mount feast. It is a powerful existence that many players dream of possessing! To become its master, players need not only great strength but must also possess the demeanor to rule the world!
