Unit Opinion

In order to fully implement the "Agricultural Product Quality Safety Law" and the "Food Safety Law", comprehensively improve the technical level of grassroots agricultural product quality safety testing, and stimulate the enthusiasm of a wide range of grassroots testing technicians to learn professional theories and study technology, the Ministry of Agriculture has decided to host the first national grassroots agricultural product quality safety testing technician large-scale competition (hereinafter referred to as "large-scale competition"). The relevant matters are notified as follows:

(III) Scoring Method

The preliminary competition and the selection of participating teams for the final competition will be completed before the end of September 2011, organized independently by each provincial agricultural administrative department. The national final competition will be held in Beijing in November 2011. Representative teams will be formed by provinces, autonomous regions, municipalities, and the Xinjiang Production and Construction Corps. Each representative team consists of one team leader, five competitors (including vegetable, livestock, and aquatic product testers), and one liaison officer. The principal of the supervising hall (bureau, commission) is responsible for the team leader, and the principal section chief serves as the liaison officer. The five competitors must be official employees of municipal-level and county-level agricultural product quality safety inspection and testing institutions (for municipalities directly under the Central Government, competitors must be official employees of district-county level testing institutions). Among them, there are three personnel from municipal-level testing institutions and two from county-level testing institutions.

(IV) Each provincial agricultural administrative department should strengthen contact with the organizing committee and office of the large-scale competition of our department, promptly reflect the various situations of the large-scale competition activities in this region, exchange experiences, increase publicity, expand influence, and focus on effectiveness.

(II) Each provincial agricultural (animal husbandry) department (bureau, commission) is the leading unit for the large-scale competition activities, actively coordinating and cooperating with animal husbandry, veterinary medicine, fisheries departments in their province to form the organizing committee and office of the large-scale competition activities in their region, guiding the large-scale competition activities, jointly organizing technical drills, selecting representatives from their province, and recommending high-level competitors to form the provincial representative team to participate in the final competition.
