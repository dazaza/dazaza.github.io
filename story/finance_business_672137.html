For her own son, she knows in her heart.

by chao7wudi7 on 2012-02-22 15:52:56

Every school has more or less terrifying legends, such as the sinister dense forest, the terrifying morgue of the medical university, and the dormitory buildings of death, all of which spread stories that make people's hair stand on end. Who is the last lucky one and who is unlucky completely depends on their own ability to solve the mysteries and bring the truth back to light, unravel impossible puzzles, uncover old secrets and shocking truths, so that people can know that there was once such a story! Lin Heng, 1.8 meters tall, neither fat nor thin, with an above-average ordinary appearance, not very handsome, but he has an insatiable curiosity, especially for things that people think are difficult, he particularly likes them. In 1988, at just 18 years old, in the prime of his youth, having just graduated from high school, he received an admission notice from a certain university in Beijing (the story is purely fictional, so this university will not be named, perhaps it will be given a fake name). With an indescribably good mood, his mother sent him to the Nanning train station, ready to go to Beijing. Before departure, his mother said to him with great anticipation: "Xiao Heng, why did I give you this name? Mainly because mother hopes you to be happy forever. Mother doesn't want anything to happen to you, because your father fell from the construction building when I was five months pregnant with you and passed away. Besides..." The words were not fully spoken when Lin Heng's mother burst into tears. Lin Heng saw his mother crying in front of the platform, went up and hugged her, saying: "I know, child knows, mom, let the past be past, why remember again and feel sad?" "Mother knows, but I dream about him, sigh... How can the days we spent together be forgotten so easily? Although it's been 18 years, the past memories are vivid and clearer the more I think about it. You know how hard it was for me and your father to be together, but it's fate, no way! So you must strive, finish university, and become the first college student in our town. Do you have the determination?" Lin Heng then took his hands off his mother, stood in front of her, nodded firmly, and said: "Yes!" Hearing this, Lin's mother was overjoyed, because she knew her son well. Seeing her son's expression, how could she not be happy? So she wiped her tears excitedly, and they came out again, saying: "My dear child, mother believes in you, you can definitely do it, and return home with excellent results! "Lin Heng saw his mother crying again, and he reached out his hand to wipe the tears from his mother's face, saying: "Mom, why are you crying again? Don't cry, child will take care of myself!" She heard her mother say: "Mother is crying out of excitement, it's nothing, not sadness, because I have such a proud son, teacher teaches you how to scientifically raise dogs, mother is glad for you, good, mother won't cry anymore, it's not auspicious on the farewell road, child, take good care of yourself, wear more clothes when it's cold, pay attention to your diet!" Lin Heng replied: "Alright, child knows, don't worry, child is already grown up and will take good care of himself, the train has arrived, mom you go back, don't see me off, I will send letters back to you on time, don't worry, I will study hard!" Then Lin Heng walked into the station while looking back at his mother, his eyes red, his eyelids teary but not falling down, because he knew that men don't shed tears lightly! On the other side, Lin's mother was watching her son walk towards the train, shouting loudly: "That's good, son, get on the train, take care of everything, remember the most important point, curiosity killed the cat, don't try to know what you shouldn't, don't force it, not everything in the world can be known by you, do you understand?" Because only a mother knows her son better, knowing from childhood that he studies hard, has insight into science and technology, has independent ideas, and sometimes even opinions more powerful than her, a middle school teacher! Lin Heng didn't say anything, just made a gesture, a unique gesture of reassurance from his hometown, and then entered the train! His mother saw it and showed a worried expression, but finally shook her head, smiled bitterly, and said: "When children grow up, their world changes, let everything be!" Then she watched the train slowly start and drive away to the distance! What about Lin Heng? I am sitting in a window seat, lost in deep thought: "What kind of university will it be? How is the scenery? I seem to have heard someone say it's a university built against a mountain. Strange, why would a university be built against a mountain? There are too many things I can't figure out. I'll wait and see when I arrive. Let's stop thinking about it, let's read a book!" And time passed unnoticed! Since Lin Heng left in the morning, setting off from Nanning, and arriving at Wuchang the next day, after a sound sleep, the train arrived at Wuchang station, some people got on, among them a girl about Lin Heng's age, around 18, who gave him a bright impression. She had a head of shiny black hair, a heart-shaped face, big eyes, and looked very energetic! She scanned the carriage and paused on Lin Heng for a few seconds, then walked over and sat beside him!! Because the previous trains only had seats, not as advanced as now! (To be honest, I haven't even seen the trains from the 80s, everything is freely created, if there are any mistakes, please feel free to point them out, it's my first time writing, hope all readers don't mind, everything has a first time, that's all!) That beautiful girl walked over and said to Lin Heng, her voice pleasant and gentle: "You should be a student going to study in the capital!" Lin Heng was surprised and said: "How did you know!" The girl smiled gently, her smile beautiful, her voice still sweet, and said: "Stupid, I can tell by looking at you, always like a bookworm, giggles, look at the names of these books next to you, are they for people below university level?" Lin Heng wasn't embarrassed, but instead smiled like the sun and replied: "You're right, that's exactly correct, you've observed correctly!" That beautiful girl didn't answer him, but was stunned for a few seconds, then recovered and giggled: "Of course!" Actually, she was wondering, how could a boy who looks so ordinary have such a beautiful and sunny smile? Could he be lying about being a university student coming to the capital? No, that's impossible, why would he lie to me? Alright, decided, I'll chat with him! Then the beautiful girl extended her delicate tender hand to Lin Heng while introducing herself: "Hello, we're both going to study in the capital, meeting on the train is also a kind of fate, my name is Ye Zimei, nice to meet you, what's your name?" "Me? Lin Heng, forest's Lin, eternal's Heng, nice to meet you, what university are you from?" "Me? Capital China Qiaod University, the one built against the mountain!" Lin Heng replied: "Can't be serious, what a coincidence, originally we are classmates, I'm also going to that school!" Ye Zimei replied: "That's really a coincidence!" "Exactly, exactly!" Then they started chatting, getting familiar with each other, time passed quickly, finally reaching Beijing station, they walked out of the train station together, and took a tricycle to the university built against the mountain. The university is located on the west side of the Zijing city, relatively close. Lin Heng, with curiosity, went towards the school in his heart.
