ChinaMode Announces: 45 Startups/Products to Watch in 2010

by ybcz0519 on 2011-01-06 17:11:07

The 2010 ChinaMode award selection process lasted for over a month, progressing from online nominations through expert reviews to the final public voting stage. The ultimate list of winners for various categories in the 2010 ChinaMode awards was finally announced; the full list of shortlisted companies can be viewed here. Several companies introduced by Tianya Haige this year made the list, including Meituan, Shufei, Guokr, Qiangzuo, Huozhong,豌豆荚 (Wandoujia), Jiepang, 42Qu, Weiyun, Fansmi, and Qieke.

I. Innovation in Lifestyle Consumption Applications

Winners:

- Meituan

- Lashou

- Nuomi

- Shufei

- Taotaosou

Meituan offers a daily deal on premium consumption, including restaurants, bars, KTVs, SPAs, hair salons, etc., allowing users to purchase group-buying coupons at low prices. With one group buy per day, Meituan helps consumers discover the most trustworthy merchants, providing them with high-quality services at super discounts while offering merchants the greatest profit through internet promotion. Meituan is one of the earliest group-buying websites in China.

Link: http://www.meituan.com/

Lashou combines Groupon and Foursquare models to offer multiple super-discounted group buys daily. Users enjoy quality services at highly attractive discount prices. These super-discounted group buys have strong regional characteristics. Lashou's powerful market expansion team continuously networks and discovers high-quality dining and entertainment merchants that fit local tastes in first-tier cities like Beijing, Shanghai, Guangzhou, Shenzhen, and more than 100 second and third-tier cities.

Link: http://www.lashou.com/

Nuomi positions itself as a relatively high-end group-buying website, leveraging media resources such as Renren, Mop, and its own platform to effectively disseminate group-buying information. The launch of Nuomi represents a beneficial attempt at socialized e-commerce. The domain name for Nuomi was registered successfully by Renren on December 26, 2004, activated on June 9, 2010, and officially launched on June 23, 2010.

Link: http://www.nuomi.com/

Shufei operates on a membership model, renting books. Membership plans cater to various groups, including families, colleagues, and roommates. As a member of Shufei, users select books from the online library and place orders, which are then delivered via courier at specified times and locations. Shufei's collection includes literature, children’s books, management, fashion, society, animation, history, and even some English original children’s books. The library continues to expand, offering both classic and latest titles to meet the needs of most users.

Link: http://www.booksfly.net/

Taotaosou is an image-based shopping search engine based on visual computing technology, featuring over 500,000 merchants and 10 million product images. Simply upload a picture, and Taotaosou will quickly find the desired item among countless products, saving the trouble of selecting keyword descriptions. Additionally, Taotaosou aggregates related items in terms of style, color, and design for easy selection. Shopping becomes faster and easier with Taotaosou!

Link: http://www.taotaosou.com/

II. Innovation in Social Networking and Information Services

Winners:

- Sina Weibo

- Mtime

- Tencent Weibo

- Xian Guo Live Broadcast

- Guokr

Sina Weibo is a microblogging service provided by Sina Corporation similar to Twitter. Users can post messages or upload pictures via web pages, WAP pages, SMS/MMS. Sina Weibo can be understood as a "micro-blog" or "one-sentence blog." It is currently the most popular Weibo product in China, characterized by a large number of public celebrities. According to official data published in 2010, Sina Weibo posts exceed 25 million daily, with 38% coming from mobile terminals. The total number of Weibo posts exceeds 2 billion, making it the most influential and prominent Weibo operator in the country.

Link: http://t.sina.com.cn

Mtime is a platform for querying movie information, writing film reviews, and discussing movies. It is not a site for free watching or downloading movies. Similar to IMDb, Mtime provides authoritative movie information. Most of the registered users on Mtime are long-time cinephiles. After years of effort, Mtime has established a professional movie database covering tens of thousands of films, introducing global cinema and DVD news daily, gathering hundreds of thousands of filmmakers and cinephiles.

Mtime members enjoy functions such as browsing or searching for film information, browsing or searching for filmmaker information, viewing others' profiles, finding old friends, making new ones, sharing photos and blogs, and exchanging opinions through comments.

Link: http://www.mtime.com/

Tencent Weibo is a microblogging service offered by Tencent similar to Twitter. Tencent Weibo limits posts to 140 characters, features a "private message" function, supports web pages, client applications, mobile platforms, allows conversations and retweets, and includes picture uploads and video sharing functions. In the "retweet" design, forwarded content is independently restricted to 140 characters and adopts a reply type @ similar to Twitter, which is common among most domestic Weibos. An exception is Suixin Weibo, whose character limit is 333. Additionally, Tencent Weibo encourages users to create their own topics and allows direct account searches.

Link: http://t.qq.com/

Xian Guo is a well-known online RSS reader. Xian Guo Live Broadcast is a microblogging service akin to Twitter introduced by Xian Guo, aiming to introduce the Twitter interaction model into Xian Guo to create a better sharing and exchange atmosphere for reading enthusiasts. Xian Guo Live Broadcast can freely bind popular domestic BSP and universal API blog programs, pushing new articles from RSS feeds to Live Broadcast for sharing with more Live Broadcast users.

Link: http://bo.xianguo.com/

Guokr is a science-themed website with social networking attributes, composed of different themed sites, providing a large amount of responsible, intelligent, and life-related content. Here, you can:

- Follow different themed sites based on your interests, read selected content accurately, and interact with netizens.

- Follow people who interest you, read their recommendations, and share interesting content with those who follow you.

- Ask confusing scientific questions or provide reliable answers in "Guokr Q&A."

Guokr aims to awaken the public's interest in science: not cold knowledge, but practical wisdom in daily life. Ji Shisan, founder of the Science Loose Association, serves as CEO. Guokr Media aims to build a leading science communication institution domestically, currently owning two brands: Guokr (Guokr.com) and Guokr Reading.

Link: http://www.guokr.com/
