[Qingdao Taobao] Have you tried the practical money-saving strategy to compete with Starbucks?

by qssqwe215 on 2012-03-06 15:48:12

Starbucks has become a symbol of fashion. On bustling streets, whether it be the smartly dressed office ladies or high school students in uniform, they all have a cup of Starbucks coffee in hand. It's true that the coffee tastes good, but it's not cheap, and this year, prices have risen significantly. Recently, there's been a cost-saving guide for Starbucks circulating online. Have you tried it?

1. Starbucks paper cups come in three sizes: tall, grande, and venti. A tall is 12 ounces, a grande is 16 ounces, and a venti is 20 ounces (12 ounces is about 360 milliliters). Based on my experience, typically, the price difference between a grande and a tall is around 3 yuan, but the quantity increases significantly. For example, if a tall hot chocolate in Shanghai costs 24 yuan, that’s 2 yuan per ounce. To upgrade by 4 ounces only requires an extra payment of 3 yuan, saving 5 yuan instantly. So, without a doubt, choosing a grande is more cost-effective.

2. Order a small cup of the day's coffee, asking for only half a cup of coffee, then ask the server to add half a cup of hot milk, turning it into Starbucks' Coffee Mocha, saving 6 yuan.

3. Buy a French press from Starbucks along with a bag of coffee beans. You can get free coffee brewing at any Starbucks. One bag of coffee beans can make over ten cups of coffee, saving money.

4. Coffee lovers can pre-purchase Starbucks vouchers on various group-buying websites for future use. These opportunities are rare, so be sure to seize them.

5. Starbucks has collaborated with some credit cards to offer half-price discounts when using the card, which is great news for card users.

6. If you're not very thirsty but just tired after shopping and want to sit down with friends, order a large cup of Frappuccino, divide it into two small cups to share with your sister, saving over ten yuan.

7. Generally, Starbucks offers free samples of pastries in the afternoon. After shopping in the afternoon, grab a cup of coffee and try a few small pieces of dessert, saving quite a bit. The cheese cake is really rich and fragrant.

8. Try adding some vanilla, almond, or hazelnut syrup (for an additional 3 yuan) to your coffee for a different taste. You can also add some vanilla powder or cinnamon powder (which is free and according to personal preference).

9. Finally, this is a method that has gone viral online: bring your own cup and 12 yuan. First, order a 12-yuan espresso, then quickly fill your own cup with whole milk until it's three-quarters full, pour the small cup of espresso into the cup, go back to the counter and ask for a cup of ice cubes, and finally pour them into your own cup. Ta-da! A 12-yuan small cup of espresso transforms into a 25-yuan medium-sized iced latte.
