What is nipple reconstruction? The area around the base of the nipple on the breast skin, with a darker color, is called the areola. The diameter of the areola is mostly between 2.0-4.0 cm, and there is also a tendency to increase with age. Reconstruction methods for nipples and areolas:

● If one side of the nipple and areola is missing while the other healthy side's nipple and areola are intact and relatively large, half of it can be cut, pulled together, and sutured to rebuild the healthy side's nipple and areola and reconstruct the missing side's nipple and areola.

● When both sides of the nipples and areolas are missing, the labia minora or toe abdomen can be transplanted to reconstruct the nipple; alternatively, the earlobe, auricle skin with cartilage transplantation can be used to reconstruct the nipple. Using tattooing method, needles dipped in brown dye are inserted into the skin of the areola to form a similar areola, matching the color of the healthy side's nipple and areola; or thigh inner side skin grafts can be transplanted onto the areola area, combined with ultraviolet light exposure, which can further darken the skin graft for better results; the skin of the labia minora can also be transplanted onto the areola area; ear posterior skin transplantation can also be used for the areola.

● Flap method can also be used to reconstruct the nipple and areola. This method provides a larger amount of tissue, has good blood supply, and better sensory recovery. The nipple can maintain a certain height and appears more erect. Additionally, there are methods such as filling with dermal scar tissue or tissue substitutes to increase the protrusion of the nipple.
