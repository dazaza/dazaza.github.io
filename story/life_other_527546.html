A female section chief in Sichuan Peng'an was insulted by a county official after she got drunk, and the video before the humiliation was exposed.

by mlwang on 2011-10-31 11:01:48

The qualities of today's cadres are really not commendable, and all kinds of social scandals keep emerging. Recently, the director of a county Disabled Persons' Federation in Peng'an, Sichuan was accused of taking advantage of a female section chief when she was drunk, bringing her to a hotel and raping her. The ugly scene in the hotel corridor happened to be caught by the surveillance camera, becoming the most powerful evidence.

Recently, a real-name accusation post on the Internet caused turmoil in this small county of Peng'an in northeastern Sichuan. A female civil servant named Chen Jia (a pseudonym) from the Peng'an County Supervision Bureau made a real-name accusation online that the director of the county Disabled Persons' Federation raped her when she was drunk. The post said that at noon on October 19th, Chen Jia drank alcohol with Zhang Sen, the deputy director of the Peng'an County Supervision Bureau, and Liu Xi Quan, the director of the Peng'an County Disabled Persons' Federation. After Chen Jia got drunk, Zhang Sen and Liu Xi Quan carried her into a hotel, then Liu Xi Quan raped her.

Currently, Liu Xi Quan has been criminally detained on suspicion of rape, while Zhang Sen has been suspended by the Peng'an County Government and is under investigation by the Peng'an County Discipline Inspection Commission. Relevant leaders of Peng'an County stated that the attitude of the Peng'an County Committee and County Government is very clear. Regarding this incident, relevant departments will certainly handle it factually, swiftly, and strictly.

What is the truth behind this incident? Recently, reporters from Huaxi Metropolis Daily conducted an in-depth investigation.

Family Account

Wife "disappeared" mysteriously for 8 hours in broad daylight

By around 10 PM, when everyone was at a loss and extremely anxious, Chen Jia's father-in-law suddenly called to say that Chen Jia had returned home.

Lü Yulong (a pseudonym) is a physical education teacher at Peng'an Jinping Primary School. He said that the Chen Jia mentioned in the accusation post is his wife, who works as a cadre at the Peng'an County Supervision Bureau, and the person accused of raping her is Liu Xi Quan, the director of the Peng'an County Disabled Persons' Federation.

The incident can be traced back to the noon of October 19th. Lü Yulong said that he received a call from his wife Chen Jia that day at noon, saying that she would go out for lunch with Director Zhang (Deputy Director Zhang Sen) and other colleagues, and wouldn't return home for lunch. "I even reminded her on the phone to drink less alcohol."

Around 3 PM in the afternoon, Lü Yulong called Chen Jia, but no one answered. At 3:20 PM, Lü Yulong called his wife again, but still no one answered. Lü said, "My wife has never failed to answer my calls."

Lü Yulong took leave and returned home and to his father-in-law's house, but did not see his wife. Around 5:40 PM, he went to Chen Jia's workplace, where her colleague told him that Chen Jia didn't come to work in the afternoon.

He learned that besides Deputy Director Zhang Sen, there was another colleague of his wife present during lunch.

Lü Yulong then called Director Cheng Bo of his wife's unit and asked Cheng to help find his wife.

A few minutes later, Cheng Bo called back and said that only Liu Xi Quan's phone could be reached, but Liu Xi Quan said he didn't know when Chen Jia left after eating.

In a panic, Lü Yulong found another phone number for Zhang Sen. However, Zhang Sen replied, "I am outside with friends, I don't know where she went."

Around 9 PM, when everyone found Zhang Sen at a foot massage parlor, his attitude was the same as in the afternoon, insisting that he didn't know Chen Jia's whereabouts.

At around 9:50 PM, when everyone found Liu Xi Quan, his response was the same as Zhang Sen's: he didn't know Chen Jia's whereabouts.

By around 10 PM, when everyone was at a loss and extremely anxious, Chen Jia's father-in-law suddenly called to say that Chen Jia had returned home.

Where did Chen Jia go, why did she disappear for 8 hours, and what exactly happened during these 8 hours?

Account of the Victim

Chen Jia: Woke up to find herself violated

When Lü Yulong learned that his wife had returned home, he rushed back home on his motorcycle like a madman. When he got home, he saw his wife crying with her head down and vomiting continuously.

In a ward of Nanchong Second People's Hospital (Nanchong Mental Health Center), Chen Jia lay curled up in the blanket, looking haggard. She was diagnosed with "stress disorder" and "depression".

The 35-year-old Chen Jia used to be a teacher just like her husband. In September 2004, the Peng'an County Supervision Bureau recruited five safety supervision personnel publicly, and Chen Jia was among those who entered the bureau through the examination.

Recalling what happened on October 19th, Chen Jia felt "like having a nightmare".

Chen Jia said that on that morning, Directors Cheng and Wei of her unit both went to the countryside, and Deputy Director Zhang Sen was on duty at the office.

"About half past ten in the morning, Director Zhang called me to his office to talk about some important matters immediately. So I went over, and I saw there was another man in his office." Chen Jia said that she recognized the man, who was Liu Xi Quan, the director of the Peng'an County Disabled Persons' Federation.

"As soon as I arrived at his office, Director Zhang said, 'Let's play some landlord,' I said at the time, 'This is working hours, probably not good!' Zhang Sen replied, 'Director Cheng and Director Wei have gone to the countryside, close the door, what's there to worry about?' Under their strong request, I reluctantly agreed," Chen Jia recalled.

At noon, Zhang Sen said, "Let's go out for lunch today." Chen Jia said that due to her subordinate identity, she agreed. Besides Zhang Sen, Liu Xi Quan, and her, a colleague named Yu Ping also went along.

Their dining place was a restaurant in the city called "Old Huangyang." Chen Jia said that there were a total of nine people at their table, including Zhang Sen, Liu Xi Quan, Yu Ping, and her, plus Zhang Sen's classmate Li Yong, a deputy town mayor, and three town safety inspectors. They were arranged in a private room.

"We started drinking beer with earthen bowls, but I thought the taste was bad, and I saw others drinking liquor, so I switched to liquor." Chen Jia recalled. Later, she felt drunk, had a headache, and couldn't remember how much alcohol she drank, when she left, or where she went.

Chen Jia said that she woke up briefly and vaguely saw herself lying naked in bed, with Liu Xi Quan sleeping next to her. "I struggled a bit, but I had a terrible headache, then I fell asleep again vaguely."

Chen Jia slept until almost 10 o'clock in the evening, completely unaware that her family had been looking for her for several hours. When she woke up again, she realized that she had been violated.

Chen Jia told the reporter that she saw dozens of missed calls on her mobile phone, feeling ashamed and scared. She took a taxi and then drove her car back to the neighborhood from the restaurant entrance.

When Lü Yulong learned that his wife had returned home, he rushed back home on his motorcycle desperately. When he got home, he saw his wife crying with her head down and vomiting continuously.

Lü Yulong told the reporter that at the time, he asked his wife where she had gone and why she came back so late. His wife kept crying and eventually told him that when she woke up, she only knew she was at Mingyu Grand Hotel and had been raped.

That night, Lü Yulong and Chen Jia together collected evidence and then reported the case to the Peng'an County Criminal Investigation Brigade. The next afternoon, the police arrested Zhang Sen and Liu Xi Quan.

On October 24th, Lü Yulong and Chen Jia made a real-name accusation online, demanding that the relevant departments severely punish the perpetrator.

How did this "case" happen? Subsequently, reporters from Huaxi Metropolis Daily conducted an in-depth investigation.

Restaurant Owner: They indeed ate here that day

A colleague said that their table was in a private room, with a total of nine people. Except for him, another person didn't drink alcohol. Everyone started drinking beer with earthen bowls, then switched to liquor.

"Old Huangyang" is a mid-to-high-end restaurant in Peng'an County. On the 27th, the reporter found the female owner of the restaurant, but she refused to disclose more information. She only confirmed that Zhang Sen and others indeed ate there on the 19th.

According to a staff member of the Peng'an County Disabled Persons' Federation, on the morning of October 19th, the federation held a training session titled "'Tailor-made' personalized services" near the County Safety Supervision Bureau, which was presided over by Liu Xi Quan that day.

How did Liu Xi Quan end up playing cards in Zhang Sen's office during the training? This staff member smiled and said, "Leaders' affairs, where do we have any control?"

According to Yu Ping, a staff member of the Safety Supervision Bureau who participated in the meal that day, there were dozens of people who went to "Old Huangyang" for lunch, all of whom were participants in the training.

Yu Ping said that their table was in a private room, with a total of nine people. Except for him, another person didn't drink alcohol. Everyone started drinking beer with earthen bowls, then switched to liquor. He left at 1:50 PM in the afternoon. When he returned to get something, he saw Zhang Sen, Liu Xi Quan, and Zhang Sen's classmate Li Yong also preparing to leave. "Chen Jia walked last, she was standing but seemed a little drunk."

According to the reporter's investigation, Zhang Sen and Liu Xi Quan are friends who frequently interact. Chen Jia also recalled afterward that during the meal, the two of them referred to each other as sworn brothers.
