The Lotus L5 Sportback applies the most advanced "sport back" design from Europe, showcasing a strong sense of fashion and dynamism characteristic of European sports cars. The classic European two-door sports car waistline gives the L5 an elegant and aesthetically pleasing style. Stemming from the UK, the unique "edge rush power aesthetics" design philosophy of Lotus emphasizes the streamlined shape of the rear lines, endowing the Lotus L5 Sportback with pure Lotus sports car power aesthetics. The Lotus L5 Sportback features a stylish and dynamic appearance, with an elegant and lively design style that embodies the classic beauty of sports cars, undoubtedly appealing to the urban avant-garde crowd.

Equipped with the Lotus Campro 1.6L CPS engine designed by British Lotus, this is undoubtedly one of the biggest highlights of the Lotus L5 Sportback. This engine inherits the core technologies of Lotus sports cars: the "CPS variable valve lift system + VIM variable intake manifold length system". This globally leading technology enables the Lotus L5 Sportback to achieve three torque peaks, giving it a significant power advantage over its peers. It effortlessly delivers strong power and speed, especially excelling in high-end performance with a maximum power output of 93kW. The engine can reach up to 7000 rpm, with a top speed of 210 km/h. In addition to excellent power and response capabilities at high speeds, this engine effectively reduces fuel consumption and increases torque at low speeds, perfectly achieving both fuel economy at low speeds and superior power at high speeds in one engine. Moreover, the Lotus Campro 1.6L CPS engine with core technology offers smoother acceleration, less vibration, and lighter noise, providing a more comfortable driving experience.
