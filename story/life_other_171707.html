Company Profile:

KONILE products are researched, designed and manufactured by the Zhongshan Oubao KONILE Furniture Company in Guangdong. The Oubao KONILE Company is jointly invested by the famous Italian brand Oubao Furniture Company (English abbreviation: OBOO) and the Zhongshan KONILE Company in Guangdong.

The China headquarters is located in Zhongshan City, Guangdong Province. KONILE's main products include: overall kitchen cabinets, wardrobes, sliding doors, etc. They have participated in the Cologne Furniture Fair in Germany multiple times, and the company's designers have absorbed the most advanced design concepts in Europe, combined with traditional Chinese cultural elements, to design products that fully meet the Chinese preference for fashion, practicality, and multifunctionality.

KONILE has successively established production bases in cities such as Zhongshan, Beijing, Shanghai, and Xian. On the basis of optimizing product quality, they maximize the savings on product costs.

Custom-made integrated furniture maximizes the use of house space. KONILE's integrated home products, produced on standardized industrial production lines, provide high-quality home furnishings that ensure a perfect overall effect and excellent quality while maximizing the use of house space.

Healthy and Environmentally Friendly

KONILE's integrated wardrobes and kitchen cabinets are all made from 100% natural small-diameter original wood pulp, natural resin glue, linseed oil, and other raw materials to create truly healthy E1-level environmental protection home products.

KONILE's integrated home products rarely use paint processes, fundamentally eliminating defects such as surface cracking, aging, lack of wear resistance, and non-environmental friendliness.

Excellent Quality

Unique T-shaped back dragon bone design ensures that the cabinet body does not deform under load.

Modular design, versatile and customizable at will. KONILE's cabinet bodies adopt modular design, ensuring optimal product quality while freely adjustable. Shelves, hangers can be raised or lowered as needed and disassembled at will. Drawers, trouser racks, tie compartments, full-length mirrors, and other functional components can also be placed in any module according to human needs, truly achieving humanized smart home.

Mildew-proof and breathable, safe storage for clothes. KONILE's designers have specially added a 5mm ventilation gap between the inner shelves of the cabinet body, allowing air circulation within the wardrobe to keep it dry and breathable, preventing odors and mildew.

High-quality hardware, increased confidence. KONILE home products all use high-quality stainless steel or aluminum alloy connectors that do not rust, greatly extending the product's service life on the premise that the hardware does not rust.

Fifteen-year product quality guarantee. KONILE's integrated wardrobes and kitchen cabinets offer a fifteen-year quality warranty, giving you peace of mind when using them.
