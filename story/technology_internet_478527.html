Man does not walk down the stairs but slides down the steel wire. After falling and getting hurt, he keeps saying it's fun

by anonymous on 2011-08-17 10:46:08

Comic: Man slides down the steel wire to go downstairs. Morning Post Graphics by Sun Wei

Morning Post - Near a residential building No.7, 203 room in Fanchang Road market in Hefei city, a steel wire connects from the balcony to a big tree on the grass below. When 20-year-old Chen Jun (a pseudonym) wanted to go downstairs, he would slide down this wire. On the morning of September 9th, he failed while playing the role of "flying man" again and accidentally fell from mid-air.

The "flying man" had fans

At around 15:00 on September 9th, on the strongest tree under Building No.7 in the neighborhood, there was a deep groove clearly visible. The original wire that connected between the big tree and Room 203 had "disappeared".

"The wire was cut off by his family at noon," said Ms. Jiang, a resident of the community. Under Ms. Jiang's indication, a piece of wire was found on the grass. It looked like a steel cable, flexible but very resilient.

Ms. Jiang lived opposite in Building No.6. She was Chen Jun's "loyal audience". Waiting to see Chen Jun perform as the "flying man" was the most anticipated thing for Ms. Jiang every day, "Unfortunately, he didn't come down the wire every day."

The first person who noticed Chen Jun's "performance" was Ms. Jiang's 6-year-old son. "At 8:30 in the morning, we were having breakfast, and my son suddenly pointed at the window and shouted 'Mom, someone is flying down from the balcony!'" Therefore, she discovered that there was a "strange young man in the community, too lazy to walk down the stairs from the second floor."

A few days later, Ms. Jiang saw Chen Jun "fly" down again, "In a flash, he was already downstairs, looking pretty handsome." Ms. Jiang thus liked this "strange" young man and looked forward to his "performance."

Sliding down the wire and accidentally falling

"It should be a professional tool, like a pulley with a groove in the middle, hanging on the steel cable and sliding over. It's a bit like the zip line on TV." Mr. Wan, an elderly resident of Room 102, Building No.7, gestured while describing Chen Jun's tools.

Mr. Wan first saw Chen Jun go downstairs at the end of July this year. "I was drying clothes on the balcony, and I saw a shadow slide past me. I thought it was some big bird, but when I looked closely, it was a person, which really scared me." Mr. Wan did not approve of Chen Jun's actions, "He swings back and forth above the yard, which is not only unsafe for himself but also makes others feel unsafe."

A few days later, Mr. Wan no longer saw Chen Jun "fly" down, and this eliminated the unease in his heart.

On the morning of September 9th, hearing the commotion outside, he learned, "This young man swung down from upstairs again, and when he was about to reach the ground, he slipped suddenly and fell. Fortunately, it was grass below, so he shouldn't have fallen too hard."

After the fall, he kept saying "fun"

Short in stature, thin and small, this was the first impression upon meeting Chen Jun. Around 10:00 am, in the emergency department of the provincial hospital, after just being examined, Chen Jun sat on a long bench in the hall, unable to lift his head due to his mother's fiery scolding.

"Too undisciplined! You're such a grown-up and still fooling around, not walking the good stairs but choosing the wire, are you a circus actor? Is this what you've learned in school?" Chen Jun's mother got angrier and almost wanted to hit her son.

Chen's mother said that her son went to university in Nanjing and returned to Hefei in mid-July. "After starting university, he became interested in outdoor activities, calling himself a hiker, and bought quite a lot of equipment. We didn't notice the extra wire at home because we indulged him too much!"

"It's fun. From the second floor to the first floor, it's not high, and there's all grass below. For me, this is just a small matter!" Facing his furious mother, Chen Jun still looked relaxed.
