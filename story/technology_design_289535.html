Beauty expert Zhang Fengxiang and his Department of Medical Aesthetics

by fengxiang on 2009-12-02 21:07:01

The Medical Aesthetics Department of the People's Liberation Army No. 193 Land Force Hospital, which was established 10 years ago, has treated 100,000 patients and gained fame both domestically and internationally.

Zhang Fengxiang, a renowned medical aesthetics expert and head of the department at No. 193 Hospital, was sent to Shanghai in 1983 to learn dermabrasion cosmetic surgery. He was immediately captivated by this emerging technology. During this period, apart from studying diligently in Shanghai, Zhang Fengxiang also took advantage of his rest time to specially visit old experts in aesthetics in places like Suzhou for apprenticeship. In February 1984, upon his return, the hospital opened the Medical Aesthetics Department, with Zhang Fengxiang becoming the "Chief Beautician." His first specialty technique revealed was dermabrasion beauty treatment. Zhang Fengxiang deeply understood that as a cosmetic surgeon, performing surgery is akin to "embroidering" on the patient's face or "micromolding" on the skin; if done well, it can add luster to the patient, otherwise, it might ruin their appearance. To acquire solid skills, Zhang Fengxiang kept an eye on the latest international and domestic technologies, carefully studied them, and adapted them for his use; to ensure dermabrasion was foolproof, he conducted experiments hundreds and thousands of times on animals, honing his skills; to reduce the patient's pain during surgery, he boldly explored and invented an olive-shaped stainless steel grinding head, halving the surgical time; to prevent post-surgical infections, after repeated practice, he adopted multi-layer sterile gauze compression bandaging, no dressing changes post-operation, and the gauze would fall off automatically after ten days. After Zhang Fengxiang's innovation in dermabrasion beauty treatments, its application became widespread, including acne, chickenpox, smallpox, pyoderma, eczema, traumatic scars, dust discoloration, tattoos, freckles, sweat duct tumors, and more than 30 other diseases. After nearly 20,000 surgical practices, the results were satisfactory, earning him the title of Sculptor under the Shadowless Lamp from CCTV.

The dermabrasion beauty treatment at No. 193 Hospital became famous in Hubei Province and nationwide. Zhang Fengxiang and his colleagues in the Medical Aesthetics Department did not stop there; to meet various cosmetic needs, they added new beauty projects. From single beauty treatments, they expanded to comprehensive cosmetic treatments, such as double eyelid surgery, rhinoplasty, rosacea cutting surgery; correction of upper eyelid ptosis, bags under the eyes, strabismus, thinning thick lips, scar removal; breast augmentation for flat or small breasts, reduction for overly large breasts; for aesthetic-affecting skin conditions like melasma, freckles, and acne, their treatments had unique approaches, receiving praise from peers and patients alike. For high-tech innovations abroad, he actively introduced them. Recently, he purchased ultrasonic liposculpture equipment domestically, offering one-time treatments for obesity in various parts of the body, achieving good results and being favored by obese patients.

To promote and develop China's medical aesthetics industry and bring beauty and welcome to more people, Zhang Fengxiang and his colleagues organized 15 national beauty training sessions and one international beauty training session, along with accepting trainees, training over 2,000 medical personnel from 29 provinces, autonomous regions, and municipalities across the country, including Hong Kong and Macao. He authored two specialized books: "Medical Plastic Surgery and Beauty Health Care" and "Modern Medical Aesthetics," and recorded a set of plastic surgery videotapes. To facilitate visits, three medical aesthetics branches were established at addresses No. 885 Zhongshan Avenue in Hankou, No. 217 Wuluo Road in Wuchang, and No. 478 Jiefang Road.

From 1984 to the present, the Medical Aesthetics Department of No. 193 Hospital has performed plastic and cosmetic surgeries for over 100,000 patients both domestically and internationally, receiving widespread acclaim. The rapid development of medical plastic surgery and beauty at No. 193 Hospital has attracted extensive attention both domestically and internationally. Zhang Fengxiang was twice invited to participate in the International Medical Aesthetics Academic Seminar held in Hong Kong, was elected as a member of the conference presidium, and was hired as an advisor to the International Medical Aesthetics Association.

Source: Outlook, 1st Issue of Overseas Special Edition in 1995 (Hubei Special Edition).
