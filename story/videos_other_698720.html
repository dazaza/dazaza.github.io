Yan'er, you don't need to worry about me, I'm a stock trader.

by d4x4w0w3c3 on 2012-03-09 10:53:16

"Yan'er, you don't have to worry about me, I know my limits," said the stock trader gently and warmly to Yan'er. However, after saying this, when that 'Luoyu Brother' glanced at Lan Huoyun Tower, there was a hint of coldness in his eyes. Yan'er blinked her big eyes helplessly and sighed. Suddenly, Yan'er inadvertently spotted Qin Yu to the side front.

For a moment, the brilliance was dazzling, demonic energy surged into the sky, and war spread throughout the entire Xuan Yuan. Boom! A square-faced elder waved his left hand, and a huge fireball with a diameter of several feet shot out fiercely colliding with the building in front of him. This person was a cultivator in the middle stage of the Condensation Pill phase. Although he could not claim to possess the divine power to move mountains and overturn seas, his all-out attack could easily crush even rocks weighing tens of thousands of pounds into powder.

In the Qiu Fox Tribe, there was only one expert at the Great Luo Immortal level among stock traders — the tribal chief Tushan. There were also only more than ten elders and the talented Princess Nü Yao of the Qiu Fox Tribe who had reached the Great Luo True Immortal level. The number of Great Luo Scattered Immortals was slightly higher, but still did not exceed two hundred. Compared to the medium and small-sized races and powers of the primordial chaos, such strength was indeed very powerful. But if compared with the large races and major powers of the primordial chaos that were on par with the reputation of the Qiu Fox Tribe, the strength of the Qiu Fox Tribe appeared somewhat too weak.

Even though he had not yet revealed all his divine powers, Lin Chuan was clear in his mind that even if he risked his life, he would not be able to defeat this old monster. Therefore, Lin Chuan schemed against the Ten Thousand Beasts Honored One. A gentleman seeks revenge, and ten years is not too long for a cultivator who possesses an extended lifespan as a stock trader. There was no need to tangle with the old devil. To seek revenge, he needed to wait until he advanced to the Cave Mystic realm.

After being depressed for several days, he finally calmed down. He absolutely refused to give up his junior sister. Thus, he began to ponder if there were any other methods to rescue her. A direct confrontation would definitely not work, but it was not impossible that there might be another idea. Methods are devised by people... However, although it sounded easy, truly doing so proved extremely difficult. After countless twists and turns, and spending an enormous amount of crystal stones and treasures, he finally managed to invite the earlier mentioned Ye Cultivator to help.

Even now, he was still immersed in that great joy. Only, amidst all the excitement, he had some doubts. Why did the Northern Savage Spirit want to meet him alone? And why was the other party collecting so many spiritual herbs and medicines... This was not White Lingfeng's fault. It was just that although Li Danghui explained almost everything, there were some things that were not mentioned. For instance, what Xu Yu did to break through was something he dared not mention. If not seen in person, who would believe that Xu Yu could master five innate martial techniques within two short days? Moreover, he had to relearn alchemy to achieve a breakthrough. If such matters were spoken aloud, wouldn't they only make everyone within the island think it absurd and unbelievable, thus leading to suspicion about whether he was normal or if he had really found the Northern Savage Spirit?

Lin Chuan did not have a spirit root; he was just an ordinary stock trader and commoner. His cultivation progressed at an unbearably slow pace. Even though he exerted ten times more effort than others, he was still far behind his peers. However, Qin Yan's situation was exactly opposite to his. This woman had exceptionally excellent spirit roots. Any technique or secret method she learned, she grasped immediately and could apply it in different contexts. She was hailed as a genius unseen in their sect for hundreds of years and adorned with countless accolades.

The vengeful strike of the Yuan Ying wizard was naturally no trivial matter. However, Yue'er was not an ordinary cultivator either. Following Lin Chuan, the young lady had broadened her horizons and had even met the ancient demon Galo Gu Mi. Besides, she had already engaged in battle with Yuan Ying-level old monsters before. Seeing the ominous black strange hand approaching, there was not a trace of fear on the young girl's face. Rubbing her hands together, the ghost banner transformed into a massive cloud enveloping her body. Thunder roared loudly from the demon clouds.

At the same time, he himself turned into a streak of light, shooting backward. A strange laugh then entered his ears. "Haha, thinking of running? Too naive! My respected self has been trapped in this ancient demon's body for a hundred thousand years. I've waited so long for a sacrificial lamb, how could I possibly let you escape? Be sensible and willingly allow me to devour you!" That voice was hoarse and unpleasant, like the clashing of metal, causing the black-faced giant's heart to fill with increasing fear.
