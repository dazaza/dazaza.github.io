"Can't help but shed tears when loneliness kicks in."

by lwjv54gs on 2010-03-31 17:54:27

I like the character Mile, who has been steadfast in her loyalty to love from beginning to end. The simultaneous betrayal of both love and friendship could not break her down. She waits for any opportunity for him to turn back, ready to act at any moment. When I saw Mile singing "Left Side" to Lu Tao in the bar later on, I couldn't help but shed tears. Loving someone to this restrained degree, knowing clearly that it's a sorrow that can never be shaken off, yet still smiling and moving forward bravely. The lyrics are truly great. "Always can't help but shed tears out of loneliness, only then do you offer comfort, worrying that the brief clear sky may be reclaimed by gloom at any time, waiting for an opportunity, the worst yet sweetest, I am optimistic but weary, because I fear losing you so much, even happiness is filled with sadness. You've never realized, you always hold my hand with your right hand, but your heart beats on the left side, the distance between you and me is forever separated by intimacy, love is scarce, I stretch out my right hand, wanting to accompany you forward, feeling your love for my heartbeat on the left side, I who loves you so deeply believe you will understand! (You must see!) Always complaining about your coldness afterward, then hastily apologizing, as if begging for a little consideration from the estranged you, it's all my fault, the result might be the most beautiful, yet also the most tragic, I'm prepared, perhaps the too free you, the home in your heart, no one can return to. I've always believed that one day you will use your left hand to hold mine and walk towards tomorrow, the future is distant but will come true, hearing the same heartbeat means we hear each other, you say that sentence: I love you! Mutual willingness is true love, unilateral giving and longing can never be reciprocated by the other party, at most, it can only be pity. If that person doesn't even give pity, that's his real kindness to you. Lu Tao was like this with Mile, not giving opportunities, not having any more affection than friendship, persisting in being devoted to Xiaolin, persisting in being responsible to Mile. I like the final part where Xiaolin and Lu Tao stand on the beach shouting to each other "We will always be together!" then running towards the sea holding hands. That scene carries the leisure of ultimate happiness. And that sentence - "We will always be together." We, always, together. I always like similar words and sentences, every one of them, tinged with the taste of love, becomes incomparably beautiful. What is the ultimate goal of love? Crossing thousands of mountains and rivers, walking through the ends of the earth and sea, it's all for the deeply loved person, for wanting to be with him/her, for giving love its final destination. Happiness, in the heart. Watching "Spirited Away," Granny Qian said, "Past events cannot be forgotten, it's just that we temporarily can't recall them." Past events are like shells lying quietly at the bottom of the sea, no matter how the seawater surges or rolls, they always stay quietly in that corner, watching the changes of the world, watching the vicissitudes of life. But there will always be a moment when they suddenly want to surface to take a look at the vast world, thus even the calmest seabed, with just a ripple, can carry it close to the sunlit blue. Some things, we deliberately choose to forget, because of pain or because the splendor no longer exists. Ten years, twenty years, we won't remember, thinking we have successfully cast off the marks in our memories, but we will recall that fragment on some day or moment in old age, though broken and incomplete, we still remember. Originally, after circling around a big loop, we still return to the starting point. Love always has such magic. Therefore, you and I can't escape the shackles given by love.
