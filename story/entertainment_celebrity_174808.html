Kelly Chen gives birth to a baby boy by cesarean section

by kaxiya on 2009-07-11 10:56:02

Hong Kong media reported photos related to Kelly Chen's rush to the hospital for childbirth (on the left) and the wedding photos of Kelly Chen and Kevin Liu. Photos/CFP

Yesterday, according to Kelly Chen's agent Zhong Zhen, Kelly Chen gave birth by cesarean section at the Canossian Hospital on Old Peak Road in Central, Hong Kong, delivering a baby boy weighing about 5.5 pounds. Both mother and child are safe and sound. Kelly Chen's husband, Kevin Liu, stated that the baby has not yet been named and revealed that Kelly Chen will attempt breastfeeding.

Supported by her husband into the hospital

At 11:30 PM on the evening of September 9th, Kelly Chen's agent Zhong Zhen and several security personnel arrived at the hospital first. Ten minutes later, Kelly Chen and her husband Kevin Liu arrived at the hospital in a nanny car. On the way to the hospital, both Kelly Chen and her husband wore sunglasses, masks, and hats. According to the journalists present at the hospital, there were already security personnel guarding the scene. When Kelly Chen saw the journalists taking photos, she looked surprised but still maintained a smile. Her physical condition was also good, and after getting out of the car, she did not need a stretcher bed, as she was supported by her husband to walk into the hospital.

Agent Zhong Zhen revealed yesterday morning that Kelly Chen had given birth to a son by cesarean section, weighing about 5.5 pounds. Subsequently, the film production company where Kelly Chen works released a letter to the media, stating: "Kelly Chen has already given birth this morning (September 10th) via cesarean section to a six-pound (about 5.5 pounds) baby boy, and both mother and child are safe! The couple both feel overjoyed, and they think the baby sometimes looks like the father and sometimes like the mother. The baby was born a little earlier than the due date, and the name has not yet been decided."

The entire childbirth process was recorded with a DV

According to Kelly Chen's assistant, Tam Kam-ming, during the childbirth, Kelly Chen's husband, Kevin Liu, was by her side accompanying her. Not only did he take meticulous care of her throughout, but he also used a DV to record the entire process of the baby's birth. The new father praised his son's voice as having a magnetic quality and being very manly. Coincidentally, Kelly Chen's son shares the same birth month and day as the legendary singer Jacky Cheung.

The assistant also mentioned that Kelly Chen said she was extremely happy to finally see the baby who had been kicking her for so long, but she would not release the baby's photos in the short term. Previously, Kelly Chen's due date was estimated to be in early August this year. Due to this, many people suspected that Kelly Chen had given birth prematurely. However, Kelly Chen's assistant, Tam Kam-ming, explained: "It was actually the doctor's suggestion, no specific time was chosen." Regarding Kelly Chen's current mental state, Tam Kam-ming revealed: "She sleeps a bit, rests a bit, and seems a little dazed."

Kelly Chen plans to breastfeed her son

In October last year, Kelly Chen married Kevin Liu and announced her pregnancy by the end of the year. Despite her love for work, she continued to attend public events and even shot a mooncake advertisement until the fifth month of her pregnancy before officially pausing her work. It is understood that Kelly Chen had already prepared mentally for motherhood before giving birth, adhering to all the taboos for pregnant women, hoping to smoothly deliver a healthy baby boy. At the beginning of this month, she was spotted by reporters going to the hospital for a pre-natal checkup, and unexpectedly gave birth smoothly just a few weeks later.

Kevin Liu stated that the baby boy has not yet been named, and Kelly Chen will attempt to breastfeed the child.
