[Repost] Teach you how to deepen charming communication

by koucai4472 on 2012-03-08 16:03:10

Today, I would like to share a sentence with everyone: As long as you are willing and determined, as long as you can continuously recognize and accumulate knowledge, as long as you can persevere, then you will definitely be able to handle social interactions with ease. Interpersonal relationships is a long-term process; it cannot be accomplished overnight. I don't think anyone has been able to achieve that yet. From a young age, our teachers have always told us that we cannot expect to become fat by eating just one meal, nor can we reach the sky in one step. What we need to do is to advance step by step, gradually showcasing our strengths, and slowly accumulating advantages. In the end, we will be able to unleash our accumulated strength and turn our advantages into victories. Interpersonal relationships are actually like a war. Having good eloquence does not necessarily mean having good interpersonal relationships. Now, let's move on to sharing how to deepen charming social interactions.

1. Goal Management, Choose the Right Coordinates

Before doing anything, we must first consider what our ultimate purpose is, which is the goal we want to achieve. Only then can we make the necessary budgets and predictions. The emerging goal management in Chinese enterprises mainly refers to setting goals during management first, then analyzing the subjective and objective conditions, planning out how to overcome resistance and leverage advantages around the goal, in order to achieve it. For social interactions, this method has much to offer for reference. First, we should consider what kind of role we want to play in social interactions and choose the right social coordinates. This way, when you enter the social circle, you won't be blind and can act with clear objectives, ensuring that your measures serve your goal.

2. Continuous Recognition, Persistent Improvement

This is an extension of goal management and also one of the key elements to maintaining charming social interactions. Finally, it involves reviewing yourself based on others' opinions about you, making various corresponding improvement measures, and implementing them. No one is perfect, but if you can continue this way, you will tend towards perfection. If you can achieve what Lu Xun said, constantly dissecting yourself, you will possess a self that is forever fresh, and your social interactions will remain charming.

3. Continuous Accumulation, Enriching Experience

This is the objective aspect of maintaining the charm of social interactions. Practice reveals true knowledge. Only through practice can you see if your improvements are correct and appropriate. The world of social interactions is complex and varied, so it requires you to continuously practice. Social interactions, like other phenomena, more or less follow certain patterns. By continuously accumulating knowledge and enriching experience, you may be able to grasp the shape and size of these patterns, thereby reducing the possibility of taking detours. First, do not forget history, as it is the standard by which you measure the present. For example, if someone was usually quiet in the past, and today they suddenly talk a lot, there must be a reason. This makes it easier for you to respond. You might say, "Hey, buddy! You're really talkative today!" Whether it's a happy event or a sad one, you can get a general understanding, and this is the role of accumulation. Second, be good at capturing details. Actions are natural expressions of thoughts, and a small gesture reflects their current mood. By being good at capturing these, you can understand their innermost thoughts and thus take the initiative. Third, successful examples of social interactions in life are countless, and all of these are materials for you to accumulate knowledge and enrich your experience, so they should not be underestimated. Finally, don't forget to improve your cultivation, continuously expanding your knowledge base, cultivating your character, and raising your ideological awareness. This could also be considered the foundation of being a person.
