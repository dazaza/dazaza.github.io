Business license: An application for the recognition of a famous trademark in Guangzhou should provide the following materials.

by gddingtian7c on 2012-02-14 16:23:38

(1) The Application Form for the Recognition of Famous Trademarks in Guangzhou (the form is uniformly formulated by the Guangzhou Administration for Industry and Commerce);

(2) Qualification certificates of the applicant and trademark registrant, including business licenses, legal entity certificates for public institutions, registration certificates for social group legal entities, ID cards of natural persons, or other proof materials related to the subject qualifications;

(3) Authorization letter issued by the trademark registrant. If the authorized user of the applied-for trademark submits the application, they must provide an authorization letter from the trademark registrant approving the application for a famous city trademark, specifying the scope and duration of the authorization;

(4) The "Trademark Registration Certificate" for the applied-for trademark and proof materials showing the earliest use and continuous use of the trademark for at least three years;

(5) Actual trademark identification used on the goods being applied for, along with photographs of the physical product;

(6) Proof materials regarding the usage, management, and protection of the trademark, including the "Trademark Usage Management System," the name of the dedicated/part-time trademark management institution, explanations of trademark development, details of trademark infringement cases, etc.;

(7) Evidence showing the status of domestic and international trademark registrations obtained or applied for by the applicant;

(8) Proof materials for key economic indicators of the goods covered by the applied-for trademark over the past three years, such as annual sales volume, operating revenue, net profit, tax payments, etc.;

(9) Ranking certification within the industry for the goods covered by the trademark issued by municipal-level or higher industry associations or administrative departments;

(10) Proof materials indicating the sales regions of the goods covered by the applied-for trademark over the past three years;

(11) Details of advertising activities conducted for the applied-for trademark over the past three years;

(12) Proof materials showing awards received by the goods bearing the applied-for trademark;

(13) Presentation slides reflecting the operation status of the applicant and the usage status of the applied-for trademark. The presentation file must be created using PowerPoint software, not exceeding 1.5MB in size, and should include the following content:

1. Name of the applicant, name of the goods being applied for, and design;

2. One photo each of the company exterior, production line, and office premises;

3. Two photos of the physical goods bearing the applied-for trademark (clearly showing the trademark design);

4. One piece of evidence highlighting the strengths of the applicant;

(14) Other proof materials that the Recognition Committee deems necessary to submit.
