A man and a woman love each other for four years.

by vfxtaohto on 2011-09-14 14:12:34

(Old affairs touching)

A man and a woman have been in love for four years, deeply in love. However, the woman always doubted whether the man really loved her, so she refused to marry him. One day, the man could not help but bravely proposed to the woman. The woman said: Before I agree with you, you must answer several questions of mine, and absolutely no lying! The man nodded.

The woman asked the man: Do you love me?

The man said: Yes.

She asked again: If I die, will you die with me?

The man said: No.

The woman asked the man somewhat despairedly: Then if I die tomorrow, what will you do? No lying!

The man thought for a while and said: Forget you, continue living!

The woman was very disappointed, thinking that the man did not love her enough, so she left him. Two years passed quickly. During this time, the woman also tried to find a solid shoulder to lean on, but she never found one. She began to think about all the care he had given her and regretted not allowing him at that time. So she went to find him. But the man had been diagnosed by the hospital with an incurable disease and would soon leave the world. The woman looked at the dying man lying in bed and couldn't help crying loudly.

The woman asked the man: If you die, what should I do?

The faint sound of the man rang in her ear: Forget me, continue living.

When the woman heard this sentence, her heart trembled violently. She hugged the man in her arms and cried even harder. The man smiled in her arms and closed his eyes. Looking at the peaceful man lying in her arms who taught her how to forget, she wanted to tell him that forgetting love might be far away from a thing called pain, but at the same time, she would lose the most precious memories. Therefore, she would never forget.
