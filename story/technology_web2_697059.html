Maybe, we sometimes think that they have never loved themselves. But when we stay out all night in a sulk, they are so sad. Their teachings are just because of the fear of losing you, and they just hope that you will be fine! When you cry and lock yourself in the room, they walk back and forth outside the door worriedly. It's not that they don't comfort you, but they hope that you can face some problems alone, and they smile while crouching in the corner. They also have their moments of sadness, and they need your comfort too. Even if you may think that since they said to face it yourself, you let go and leave. They are old people, they cannot bear the hurt from their children. You will get angry, they will get upset, and they will be sad. Their bodies cannot withstand even a little setback, they are getting old. No matter how big you grow, no matter how much taller you become than them, you are still their child, their treasure. Their wish is for you to be happy and live a good life! On your birthday, they will prepare your favorite dishes, even though no one remembers their birthdays. They just hope that you can be happy with your friends. So please remember their birthdays, and make it an unforgettable day for them. How many more birthdays can you spend with them? Your youth has come at the cost of their aging, and they are without regrets. They only hope that their child can live a healthy and happy life. Give your mother a few carnations on Mother's Day, even though she might say that she doesn't celebrate foreign holidays, she will still feel gratified knowing her child is worth her pride. Do something within your means for your father, even though there isn't much conversation between you, he will happily talk about you, telling stories of your childhood and your mischief. They are getting old, and their lives are beginning to count down. They only hope that before they pass away, they can see you living independently, and then they can peacefully finish this life. We always complain that their love is not enough, far less than what they give to others. In fact, they love you so much, they can choose you in their lives, they can exchange their lives for your happiness. Please remember, their favorite one is always you. Also, don't forget those photos of you together, those past joys. Don't forget that she risked her life to bring you into this world. Don't forget that when he scolds you, he is hurting himself too, he only wants you to be fine. Don't forget that they are always by your side, even if they don't show it, even if their expressions when they teach you seem painful. Don't forget their anxiety when you were sick, how they got up in the middle of the night to rush you to the hospital, their anger and worry when they saw you injured and bleeding. They are getting old, and they hope you can give them some love. They will also look forward like children, hoping that you will accompany them through this journey. They will leave someday, they don't want to, it's not that they desire immortality, but they can't put down their worries for their children. So cherish the happiness you have now, don't argue over everything. We are in a rebellious phase now, but don't forget their anxious eyes, and don't forget your family portraits. Only be happy!
