Twitter's Rich List: Three Executives Worth Over $1 Billion

by anonymous on 2013-11-17 18:06:51

Twitter officially landed on the NYSE, opening at $45.1 per share, a 73% increase compared to its $26 IPO price. Calculated by the opening price, Twitter's market capitalization has exceeded $30 billion.

The surge in Twitter's stock price compared to the IPO price benefited the company's co-founders and early investors. The market value of shares held by Twitter executives, employees, and board members reached $5.62 billion. Currently, this group holds a total of 124,529,741 shares, accounting for 25.5% of the total shares outstanding. After the initial public offering is completed, this equity stake will be diluted to 22.3%.

As the largest individual shareholder of Twitter, co-founder William owns 10.4% of the company. Following Twitter's IPO, his net worth exceeded $3.2 billion; co-founder Dorsey's net worth exceeded $1.05 billion; early investor and board member Peter Fenton's net worth exceeded $1.42 billion; CEO Costolo's net worth is approximately $430 million.

Below are the five insiders whose net worth skyrocketed due to Twitter's initial public offering:

First: Evan Williams

As one of the co-founders, Williams served as Twitter's CEO from 2008 to 2010. By acquiring a company founded by Williams himself, Obvious, for an undisclosed amount, Williams became Twitter's largest shareholder. Williams currently holds approximately 10.4% of Twitter's shares, valued at about $3.2 billion.

Second: Peter Fenton

Silicon Valley venture capitalist Fenton made early investments in Twitter and currently serves as a board member. Fenton currently holds 31,568,740 shares of Twitter stock, valued at $1.42 billion.

Third: Jack Dorsey

As one of the co-founders, Dorsey is currently the chairman of the board. At the age of 36, he served as Twitter's CEO and president from May 2007 to October 2008. Since February 2009, as a co-founder of another popular innovative company, Square, Dorsey has served as the CEO of that company.

According to the latest revised S-1 filing, Dorsey currently holds 23,453,017 shares of Twitter stock. Valued at the IPO price, these shares are worth $1.05 billion.

There were also reports on Wednesday that mobile payment company Square is currently preparing for an initial public offering in 2014.

Fourth: Dick Costolo

Costolo has served as Twitter's CEO since 2010. He joined Twitter in 2009 and served as the company's chief operating officer. Costolo currently holds approximately 1.4% of Twitter's shares, valued at around $430 million.

Fifth: Biz Stone

As one of the co-founders of Twitter, although Stone still holds shares in Twitter, it may be due to the small quantity that it was not disclosed in Twitter's prospectus. By selling his Twitter shares in previous years, Stone might have already cashed out tens of millions of dollars.

In addition, Noah Glass, another co-founder of Twitter, was not mentioned in the company's prospectus, clearly indicating that Twitter's initial public offering did not make much money for this founder.
