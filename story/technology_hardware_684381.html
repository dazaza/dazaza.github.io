Qipolang Official Flagship Store: Rich kids buy luxury cars for rent, top-tier supercars 100,000 yuan a day

by hlti8014 on 2012-02-29 23:31:38

Mr. Sun told the reporter that many luxury car owners or large companies are actually very hesitant after purchasing luxury cars. Unless dealing with major affairs or attending important meetings, they normally do not easily take the car out of the garage. By affiliating with some car rental companies to make money, the rental company is responsible for taking orders, the driver of the luxury car owner is responsible for driving, and the rental company collects about 10% service fees on the side. According to reports, most of the luxury cars rented in the city are used for weddings, and some are used for business purposes, such as picking up people at the airport or taking clients out for meals, earning enough face for the company. A salesperson from Qingdao Ridar Car Rental Company told the reporter: "Our company's total annual income from renting out luxury cars for wedding cars is about 5 million to 10 million yuan, while the annual income from renting out luxury cars for business use is only about 2 million yuan."

"If you have a luxury car but don't use it often, instead of letting it gather dust in the parking lot, you might as well put it in a rental company to make money for yourself. 99% of the luxury cars rented out by Qingdao car rental companies are affiliate cars, which is already an open secret in the industry," Mr. Sun, who has worked in a Qingdao car rental company for many years, told the reporter.

Renting a top-tier sports car costs 100,000 yuan per day.

"The company currently has around 100 Mercedes-Benz cars, about 200 BMWs, over 50 Ferraris, and about 20 Jaguars on affiliate status. Generally, there are no problems handling larger weddings or business events," a staff member of Ridar Car Rental Company told the reporter: "The luxury cars affiliated with our company either belong to companies or large enterprises, or their owners are young rich second-generation heirs." Luxury car owners leave their name, car model, and contact information with the rental company, and when there is a need for rental, the rental company will call and contact the owner. Many owners, in order to make more money, affiliate their vehicles with five or six rental companies.

Most of the car owners are second-generation heirs.

Reporter Xu Yongguang

Then why are the rental prices of luxury cars so high? She told the reporter that first, luxury cars themselves are quite valuable; additionally, each luxury car has high annual maintenance costs, and each trip causes some wear and tear on the car itself, so if the price is too low, the car rental market won't be willing to rent them out; luxury cars themselves also require dedicated drivers, which requires choosing drivers with high quality and excellent driving skills, and the driver's expenses are also one of the reasons for the higher price of luxury car rentals.

Rolls-Royce, Bentley, Maserati, Ferrari, these luxury cars cost millions or even tens of millions, their high prices are astonishing. According to the reporter's investigation into the luxury car rental market in the island city, the rental prices of luxury cars are indeed not low. The daily rental price for self-driving sports cars like Ferrari and Lamborghini is around 100,000 yuan.

A salesperson from Ridar Car Rental also revealed to the reporter that most of the luxury car owners affiliated with their company are second-generation heirs, "These people are about twenty-two or twenty-three years old, mostly second-generation heirs, and it's quite strange, spending so much money to buy a luxury car, not driving it themselves but putting it in a rental company to earn such a small amount of rental fees."

Luxury car rentals are mostly used for weddings.

"Like Rolls-Royce Phantom, Bentley Azure, or Maserati, these luxury cars worth several million to tens of millions, generally the minimum half-day rental fee is 17,000 yuan; slightly cheaper ones like the Mercedes-Benz S600 worth over two million yuan, the daily rental fee is also about four or five thousand yuan." Miss Han from Qingdao Faji Car Rental Company told the reporter. Miss Han said that their store always provides a dedicated driver for the luxury cars they rent out. If the owner wants to drive themselves, the fee will be relatively more expensive. For example, the daily rental fee for self-driving sports cars like Ferrari and Lamborghini is around 100,000 yuan, with a minimum rental period of three days, and a deposit of 500,000 yuan must be paid in advance.
