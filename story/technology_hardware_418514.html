[Constellations That Won't Be Materialistic]

First Place: Pisces

Full of romantic fantasies, Pisces can be said to be the ones who least consider their partner's background or status. The main reason is that Pisces value feelings and their partner's emotions more than money and material enjoyment. For them, money isn't everything, but love is the most important. If they can share a romantic and beautiful relationship with someone they like, even if the person is poor, or even if they only have a short time together, they are willing to sacrifice for love. Therefore, if Pisces had to choose between love and bread, they would undoubtedly choose the love they dream of.

Second Place: Cancer

Cancer is always perceived as family-oriented. Their simple personalities and kind, agreeable attitudes won't make them argue much with their partners. Moreover, Cancer likes to take care of others, treating their partners like family members. They are responsible in relationships and never care about whether the partner has a high position or a powerful family background. They won't abandon the relationship for material enjoyment. Instead, if their partner needs help, Cancer will instinctively assist them, not only being a good companion but also an excellent assistant!

Third Place: Sagittarius

With a passionate personality, Sagittarius always gives their all to their partners, regardless of the partner's identity or whether they face difficulties. They are willing to sincerely pay attention to their partner, which represents the most genuine love. Loving freedom, Sagittarius values the joy in love with the person they like, not wanting their feelings to be blocked by any external factors. They hope for an intense romance with their partner, so it's truly a blessing to be Sagittarius's partner!

The Most Rebellious Girl Among the Twelve Constellations

First Place: Scorpio

The top-ranked Scorpio girls are seen as mysterious and seductive in men's eyes. They are sometimes obedient and sometimes rebellious, making them the hardest to grasp. This is because Scorpio girls naturally have a dramatic personality. Scorpio people never lack courage and adore dramatic situations, often directing scenes on your path forward.

Second Place: Gemini

Ranked second, Gemini girls are always lively and changeable, with complex personalities. Due to their quick thinking, intelligence, and some impulsiveness and neurotic tendencies, they easily captivate men but remain unpredictable. This is entirely due to Gemini's changeable nature. A single word may touch her heartstrings, and a small matter might make her walk away.

Third Place: Aquarius

Ranked third, Aquarius and Gemini have similar voting rates, showing how quirky Aquarius's personality makes men feel both delighted and confused yet hard to handle. This is mainly because Aquarius people are very individualistic and cannot tolerate any constraints, nor do they force themselves to follow any discipline. Therefore, Aquarius girls are sometimes imaginative and humorous, while other times cold and distant, making them seem rebellious and difficult to get along with. In emotional matters, they tend to extremes, possibly having the purest hearts and ideal love or becoming completely indifferent. Men often feel anxious because Aquarius girls merely fear losing their freedom, hoping to develop eternal love on the basis of friendship.

Fourth Place: Sagittarius

Sagittarius is only 100 votes away from the third-place Aquarius, showing how thought-provoking and cynical Sagittarius can also be rebellious and hard for men to handle. Sagittarius girls appear simple-minded, loving sports, but sometimes they are distracted and self-centered, disregarding others. Often, boys feel she is both near and far, sometimes seemingly compatible but ultimately incompatible. The reason lies in Sagittarius's unwillingness to let others threaten or interfere with their sacred freedom. They dislike obligations, can abandon gained benefits, and don't want to be constrained. Since they refuse to be emotionally bound, they often rebel, making men love and hate them simultaneously, and Sagittarius often appears scandal-prone.

Fifth Place: Libra

In fact, Libra girls are usually gentle and elegant. If we say Libra girls are rebellious, it's probably because they pursue perfect love, and their deep affection makes people unable to bear it. Thus, their emotions are hard to predict, and they pay great attention to their words and actions. Dealing with them requires neither impatience nor rudeness. They don't want to live in an environment lacking warmth and passion. They need opportunities to showcase their charm and grace and desire to live in an artistic and ideal world filled with beauty.

The Marital Destiny of the Twelve Constellations

Aries: Relationships are relatively complex, with both predestined karmic connections and fleeting misty affairs. If caught up in a misty affair without knowing when to retreat, there is a risk of falling out of love when the karmic connection appears. It's not difficult to distinguish between the two. When you realize a karmic connection appears, don't rush; patience will make the misty affair pass quickly. Thinking that mutual affection means immediate commitment is taboo for Aries, and one must control sudden romantic passions.

Taurus: Taurus people are the most likely among the twelve constellations to oppose fate. Clearly having feelings for someone, they prioritize external factors or circumstances over their true feelings. Many Taurus individuals experience a period of dating before returning to their initial love object. Fate often pulls Taurus towards those they perceive as too ordinary or plain.

Gemini: Fate meets its counterpart in romantic moments under the full moon. Without effort, the two can understand each other deeply. Unfortunately, Gemini people don't pay attention to this sense of destiny, realizing it only after the moment has passed. When fate appears, it should be cherished. Sweetness during interactions is the best proof of destiny.

Cancer: Cancer people are the easiest among the twelve constellations to be teased by fate. The destined partner often has many conflicting traits. There will be many frictions in interactions, with differing interests and life paths. Fate pulling two very different people together will inevitably bring many challenges and trials. Despite frequent arguments, Cancer's marital bond is the hardest to sever. No matter which side falls into difficulty, the other remains unwaveringly supportive.

Leo: Leo people, especially women, are particularly sensitive to fate. Once destiny appears, they can definitely feel it. Romantic relationships involve passionately engaging once the partner appears, quickly forming a pair, and progressing rapidly from meeting to discussing marriage. Leo people wait for fate to arrive, but if daily worries lower their sensitivity to destiny, their romantic relationships may stagnate.

Virgo: Virgo people may encounter long-distance fated relationships. These encounters are most likely to happen during travel, long journeys, or business trips. Long-distance fated relationships seem unstable and fraught with cultural and lifestyle differences, but in reality, they are often heart-to-heart, with strong emotional bonds. Some even benefit from their differences.

Libra: Libra's fated relationships are usually based on shared interests and similar personalities. They receive blessings and support from many people, and everyone feels happy when they unite. Occasional disagreements can be peacefully resolved, and even unresolved disputes often attract mediators. With similar personalities, both are overly optimistic about the future.

Scorpio: Scorpio's fated relationships are influenced by physical desires, where physical attraction outweighs spiritual connection. Such relationships become tense when bed harmony is lost but intensely inseparable when passionate. From the start, there's a strong attraction, and once physical contact occurs, they are controlled by their desires. Compared to other constellations, Scorpio's fated relationships have fewer disputes. For Scorpio people, when fate appears, it's best to embrace it fully, as trying to escape is futile.

Sagittarius: Sagittarius' fated relationships aren't destined pairs but rather combinations of contrasting traits—tall and short, fat and thin. Backgrounds differ greatly, but personalities less so. They could be childhood friends, classmates, or colleagues, people met frequently and known for a long time. Initially, they might bicker, but once destiny appears, the relationship develops quickly. The emotional development surprises those around them and leaves even the parties involved incredulous.

Capricorn: Capricorn people are more practical and don't fantasize about magical destinies. They consider love at first sight strange and destiny deceptive. However, when destiny strikes them personally, they find themselves powerless to resist. If you see a Capricorn woman, who usually doesn't wear makeup or doesn't care much about cosmetics, suddenly buying a lot of them, it indicates she has been ensnared by Cupid's red thread.

Aquarius: When destiny appears, it's quite exaggerated, akin to being possessed. Suddenly, they become infatuated with male-female relationships, feeling happiness whenever with the other person. Such happiness comes unexpectedly, possibly at first sight or unforgettable at first glance. If Aquarius mistakes temporary passion for destiny, the consequences could be severe.

Pisces: Destiny usually transforms Pisces' personalities because Pisces discovers true love and conforms to the other party. From the beginning of the acquaintance, Pisces tries new makeup and speech styles, even changing personal interests to accommodate the other. For example, a usually cheerful Pisces suddenly becomes reserved, covering their mouth while laughing and speaking elegantly, indicating they've found someone special.
