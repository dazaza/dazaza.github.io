Reporters in Xianyang (reporter Yuan Xiaofeng) "After that young man came, he unfolded the banner and stood in front of the Xingping Public Security Bureau for everyone to see. We can see that there are several words on it 'malfeasance and inaction'." On February 3, a young man handed over a banner of "inaction" to the staff of Xingping Public Security Bureau. Yesterday afternoon, the reporter learned from Xingping Public Security Bureau that the bureau had begun to investigate the matter. The police established a special investigation team to thoroughly investigate the fighting incident. Another merchant nearby told the reporter that they later also saw this incident online and in the bar, but no one knew why the young man did so. On the afternoon of February 5, a post appeared on Huashang.com Xianyang Forum. The content was only a set of pictures: a bespectacled young man holding a banner with the words "malfeasance and inaction". In the picture, many people surrounded the man who gave the banner, full of doubts. The Public Security Bureau received the "inaction" banner. Yesterday afternoon, when the reporter went to Xingping to understand the matter, a merchant near the Municipal Public Security Bureau said that on February 3, the Xingping Public Security Bureau cracked a criminal case, and someone set off firecrackers at the gate of the Public Security Bureau to express celebration and gratitude. "Just after the previous matter was over, a young man came, holding a banner standing at the gate of the Public Security Bureau. At that time, everyone still thought it might be another person giving a banner to express thanks. But when we approached and took a look, everyone stopped - the banner wrote 'malfeasance and inaction'. This young man has great courage. Later, a comrade from the Public Security Bureau took the banner." Relevant leaders of Xingping Public Security Bureau said yesterday that regarding the incident of the party concerned sending the "inaction" banner, the Municipal Bureau paid high attention, specially held a meeting to discuss the matter in the morning, and made a decision: led by the Discipline Inspection Secretary of Xingping Public Security Bureau, comprehensively investigate the complaint of the party concerned about the police's "inaction", understand whether there was "inaction" in this public security case; led by Deputy Director Zhang Zhicai, establish a special investigation team composed of officers from the Legal Department, Public Security Team, and Xiwu Police Station of the bureau to thoroughly investigate this fighting incident; inform the party concerned Yuan Mou about the handling situation of the case by the Municipal Public Security Bureau. According to the introduction of the responsible person, the person who sent the banner was named Yuan Mou, from Xingping City. The reason originated from a public security case last year. It was reported that around 8 o'clock on the morning of March 5, 2011, Yuan Mou was driving to Xianyang for work and had a conflict with a bus driver, and Yuan Mou was beaten and injured. The police from Xiwu Police Station of Xingping Public Security Bureau then arrived and handled the situation. Yuan Mou was hospitalized for treatment at that time. Relevant personnel of Xingping Public Security Bureau introduced the situation, saying that the police were not inactive in this matter. "After the police mediated, the other party had compensated for Yuan Mou's medical expenses. The reason he sent the banner was that the person who beat him had not yet been dealt with by the police. At that time, because there were many people involved in the fight, the scene was chaotic, and there was no other relevant evidence material on-site to determine the main responsible person. Meanwhile, Yuan Mou's injury appraisal had not concluded, which brought certain difficulties to the police's handling of the relevant responsible persons and the characterization of the case." Yesterday, Yuan Mou, who sent the banner, said he didn't want to say more about the matter, but he expressed understanding and satisfaction with the police's current active handling attitude. A relevant responsible person of Xingping Public Security Bureau told the reporter that due to the sudden incident, the staff subsequently took the young man who sent the banner to the petition office of the Public Security Bureau. After a simple inquiry about the relevant situation, they promptly fed back the situation to the Discipline Inspection Committee of Xingping Public Security Bureau.
