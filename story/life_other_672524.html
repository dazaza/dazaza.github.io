Innocent years, who fell in love with whom?

I remember that spring, there was a woman, beautiful beyond compare...

The first time I saw you was in the school art studio. That day, your class was holding an art exhibition. After lunch, I wandered around the school with nothing to do and heard that a sophomore class was hosting an art exhibition. Since I really like looking at paintings, I decided to check out the painting skills of the sophomores by casually browsing through the works.

There were many students from your class, so I deliberately looked for a girl, wondering if she was also in this class. While admiring the paintings, my eyes scanned every corner of the studio. Suddenly, I noticed a girl in a green short-sleeve shirt with a large bag on her back, helping her classmates put up paintings. You were standing there, looking incredibly cute with fair and tender skin. I moved closer to you, wanting to sneak a peek but not daring to look directly. However, I was deeply attracted by your adorable movements and enchanting eyes. I asked myself, could there be such a beautiful girl in this school? Wow, she is truly stunningly beautiful. I thought to myself, how wonderful it would be if I could get to know such a beautiful woman.

I wanted her to notice me, so I deliberately stood in a less crowded place where she might see me. But at that moment, it seemed like she was fully focused on hanging the paintings with her classmates and didn't pay attention to anything else. Not long after, she seemed to have gone out to wash her hands. At the time, I didn't realize she had left. Thinking I was tired, I decided to head back to the dormitory to rest. As soon as I stepped out of the studio and walked a couple of steps, I looked ahead and saw her walking towards me.

I thought, "This is it, she will definitely notice me." Step by step, she was slowly approaching me. For some unknown reason, my heart suddenly started beating fast. I pretended to act cool as I approached her. When she passed by me, my face suddenly turned red. I felt certain it must have been bright red. My heartbeat quickened, and I couldn't bring myself to look at her, quickly shifting my gaze to another direction. What's going on? I wanted to look at her but didn't dare. But I felt she must have noticed me. And just like that, I acted cool and passed by her. Walking to the staircase entrance, I quietly checked my reflection in the mirror-like door, realizing my face must have been very red. Sure enough, it was indeed very red. Why was my face so red? Because I was nervous, haha. So helplessly, I returned to the dormitory...

Time passed second by second, I don't know how many weeks had gone by, and I rarely saw her again.

I truly hoped to meet her somewhere. From then on, I shifted my focus to studying. During those days of diligent study, several times a good friend suddenly said to me: "Why don't we go to the sophomore grade to flirt with girls?" We excitedly agreed to go with him because at that time he was secretly in love with a freshman girl. Both of us were in the same boat of secretly loving someone. I said, "Let's go, the sooner the better." He replied, "Now? I'm too scared! There are only two of us, it's safer with more people."

I said, "What are you afraid of? Just go! Girls like guys who are bold." He was defeated by his own fear. In the end, our plan to flirt with girls failed. But sometimes he showed courage in other aspects, just not in the art of flirting. From then on, he became my partner, and I started hanging out with him.

Not long after, we made an appointment to dye our hair. I don't know what medicine I took, but I dyed my hair the brightest red in school, thinking it would make me stand out, catch her attention, and leave a cool impression. Every day after school, I waited with him at the school intersection for pretty girls to pass by. Actually, we sat waiting there early. I truly hoped to encounter that girl among the crowds of people coming and going. Waiting for several weeks, I had become accustomed to waiting every day, even though I rarely saw her. But my determination was endless; if I didn't see her today, I would continue waiting tomorrow. Several times I saw her, but I didn't dare to call out to her, just quietly watching her slip away. Being timid is indeed a problem, it's really frustrating.

One afternoon after school, we waited as usual for our goddess to appear. After a long wait, the target finally appeared not far away. My heart was still pounding. My friend said to me, "That girl is there, do you dare to ask for her phone number?" He was clearly challenging me. At that moment, there were many of my classmates around, giving me courage. In a burst of anger, I summoned all my courage, with a cigarette in my mouth, I ran up to the girl and said, "Hello, classmate."

Wow, before I could ask for her phone number, I scared her into running to the middle of the road. Her face turned red from being startled, but I could see a hint of a smile. She was scared off by me, making my classmates laugh. Back at school, my friend asked me if I got her phone number. I said, "I just said hello, she was scared and ran away."

Sigh, I ruined her impression of me. He said, "Your hair color is so red, who wouldn't be scared of you, hahaha." Sigh, it was another instance of helplessness. I said, "Well, if she notices me, that's fine."

From that point on, she became my target.

Much time passed, we were still waiting in the old place, smoking and laughing. Unconsciously, one of my classmates, Ah Fei, spotted the girl passing by in a car. Ah Fei said, "That girl is there, she just passed by in a car." How could I not have noticed? Sigh, another opportunity lost.

Fortunately, another classmate said, "Oh, it's her, I have her phone number." I asked, "Do you really have her phone number?" Yes. Do you want to pursue her? Why didn't you say earlier? I know her. Finally, that classmate gave me her phone number, which I immediately wrote down. Now, I have her number, there's a little bit of hope.

At around 12 o'clock that night, I picked up my phone and sent a text message to the girl. The content was: "Are you **? I've liked you for a long time, did you know?"

But it was so late, she might have turned off her phone, so she didn't reply. The next morning, she probably became curious about who I was and replied. She said, "Who are you?" Receiving her text message, I was extremely excited. Then I sent her another text saying, "My name is..., I often watch you, the guy who scared you yesterday at the school intersection, do you know who I am now?"

She should have known. By the afternoon, I sent a text asking for her QQ number, so we could chat on QQ.

But I never expected in my wildest dreams that she would actually call me. I went crazy telling Ah Fei and the others, "That girl called me!" Ah Fei said, "Is that true?" Then I hid in the restroom to answer the call, my heart pounding hard. I said, "Hello," pretending to ask, "Are you..." But she was very direct; she even invited me. She said, "Are you free tonight?" I said, "Yes, what's up?" She said, "Let's meet up tonight." I said, "Sure." "See you tonight..."

Not much conversation. It was that simple, she actively invited me. I proudly told Ah Fei and the others, "That girl invited me out tonight." They didn't believe me, "Really?" I said, "Yes." Haha, I'm going on a date tonight. I need to dress up coolly tonight.

Tonight, I arranged with my partner to go to the salon first, get a haircut, and style it. I remember that hairstyle was really handsome, and I can't even achieve that cool look now. After getting my hair done, I came out with my partner and waited for the girl in the city center. My partner said to me, "Will that girl really show up?" I said, "I don't know either, but she invited me out tonight."

He said, "Why have we been waiting for so long and she hasn't come yet? Did she stand you up?" I said, "Let me call her and check." I dialed her number, no answer. I tried again, still no answer.

By nearly 9 o'clock, I dialed again. This time, thankfully, the call connected, but no one answered after a long beep. I tried again, hearing the beep, "Sorry, the user you dialed is temporarily unavailable." Could she really have stood me up?

Not long after, it seemed like she called back. I answered, "Hey, are you out yet?" She said, "I'll be there soon."

Soon, she finally arrived, and I immediately greeted her. Seeing her appearance, she was truly adorable. We decided to take a walk by the West Lake and talk. We stayed by the West Lake until late at night before heading back. From then on, I officially met that girl.

Until one day, she sent me a text message saying, "I don't want to talk about this right now, because it's a bothersome thing." She wanted to recognize me as her elder brother. I was speechless.

I asked myself, "Is it because I'm not handsome that she doesn't like me?" I thought it must be the case. Later, she asked me, "Can you promise me something?" I asked her, "What is it?"

She said, "Can you dye your hair black?" I said, "I will follow your advice." Little servant obeys orders. That night, I immediately dyed my hair black and came back. But my classmates didn't know why, and I hadn't told them before. After dyeing my hair black, I told her, "I've dyed my hair black."

Finally, after overcoming numerous difficulties, she and I started dating. Afterwards, we experienced many unforgettable moments of love.
