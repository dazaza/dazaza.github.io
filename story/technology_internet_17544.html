Download Mobile QQ 2007 beta1 SP version

by killadm on 2007-04-25 22:19:56

The Windows Mobile SP version and QD of Mobile QQ 2007 were finally released at noon today. Many people have been looking forward to this for a long time, which can be seen from the number of people brushing posts on Tencent's mobile website forum. Some people were so eager that in many smartphone forums, you can find that many people stayed by their computers all night waiting for the release, almost reaching a fanatical level.

Today at noon, Tencent did not disappoint these many enthusiastic users as the SP and QD versions were released as scheduled. Many people downloaded and installed it at the first opportunity.

As for the SP version, its performance is acceptable. It supports cmwap, group chats, video, voice, and file sending. It does not have the additional functions like music and games found in the S60 version, which I am quite satisfied with, but there are also many people who are dissatisfied with this point. This is a matter of personal preference.

Currently, it seems that Tencent's web site has not been updated yet, and no download link has been found. You must download it from the Mobile Tencent website, and you need to sign up for testing to use it. However, according to some users' feedback, those who did not sign up can also use it.
