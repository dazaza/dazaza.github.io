Our relationship between an older woman and a younger man may indeed be absurd, but as long as this love makes us happier than before, who cares if it's absurd?

Life goes on, and our story continues to be written. Our sudden engagement did not change our respective career plans. Haidi will still go to Italy, and I will continue to work as a TV and column writer in Beijing. Where will our next absurd episode take place? Who knows? Maybe, it will be in St. Peter's Basilica in Rome.
