Increase the click price of Google Adsense ad units on the garbage station

by eleny on 2008-10-25 21:38:29

In the eyes of search engines, the value of spam sites is quite limited. However, due to the relatively low technical content and production cost of spam sites, more and more people are using them to place Google Adsense ads. In fact, things are far from being so pessimistic. As long as we look at the content of spam sites from the perspective of search engines, especially Google, it is easy for us to increase the price per click of Google Adsense ads on spam sites.

The parameters that directly affect the price per click of Google Adsense ads are ad relevance, conversion rate, and the importance (ranking) of the website.

Before we prepare to raise the price per click, we need to understand a rough estimate of the price per click so that we can define the concept of high and low. Generally speaking, an income per click between 0.01 and 1.00 is relatively normal. It's not surprising that some people encounter clicks earning more than 1.0 dollars because there are also reports of prices as high as ten dollars or even dozens of dollars. This is not the focus of our discussion, so we will not verify it.

Ad prices are set by advertisers based on bidding principles, while Google's Smart Pricing mechanism controls the actual cost of each click. Of course, after a click occurs, they will still make some adjustments based on the information they have.

Generally speaking, the higher the purchasing power of the market, the higher the overall level of ad prices. The ads we are involved with are mainly in English and Simplified Chinese. From the situation of foreign ordinary site owners (excluding professional publishers and cheating cases), the income per click on English sites is around 0.10 dollars, and the ads on Chinese sites vary greatly depending on the theme, with an overall level of around 0.04 dollars. Of course, many people will be much higher or lower than this value, which is an individual case.

What we want to talk about are spam sites. Making a successful spam site is a great skill, especially in terms of creation and promotion techniques. I know many people have very sophisticated methods. We can participate in different forums and read their articles to learn. Here we are talking about the price per click of ads on spam sites, regardless of how much traffic or clicks it gets. What we want to do is ensure that even if there is only one click, the income should be above 0.04 dollars.
