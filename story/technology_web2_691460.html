1. Selection of Tieba should have a focus. If you want to increase the traffic of your website, you must choose some popular Tiebas. However, although some Tiebas have very high popularity, the competition is also very fierce. A post may sink within a few minutes and fail to achieve the effect of online promotion, like anti-mildew powder at http://www.glechina.com.cn/. Therefore, you need to see if the posts in Tieba can include links. Generally, website promotion Tiebas can include links. Some strictly managed Tiebas seem not to allow links.

2. The content of the post needs attention. The promotional information must be soft. Everyone knows about soft-sell articles, right? I believe you also know about their survival rate and attention on the Internet. A good soft-sell article will not only win netizens' admiration but also make website editors or administrators like you, casually recommending your article or post to the homepage. Baidu Tieba promotion is the same; you cannot force it. Some people say that sometimes they just post vulgar advertisements and re-post them after deletion. I think this is a waste of time and meaningless. With this time, you can package your promotional posts. If hard methods don't work, try soft ones. If you can edit your promotional information perfectly and make it extremely soft, your post will be preserved in Tieba for a long time. Both the moderator and the system administrator are human beings, not as cold as you might imagine.

3. Learn to use Baidu hotspots. Use the hot events of the day to combine with your promotional information for promotion, such as glass mold remover at http://www.glechina.com.cn/. Such posts often have better promotional effects and attract a lot of clicks. You only need to reply in the corresponding hot event Tieba. It has been proven that posts below the 8th floor have a smaller probability of being deleted. Don't worry if no one will see the posts below the 8th floor. The heat of a hot event Tieba can last at least one day. The effect is quite good. If you can combine the second point of attention here, it will be even better.
