Principles that must be followed for the normal operation of a gyratory crusher

by hx515317 on 2012-03-02 15:06:01

Ball Mill Network: Operating Precautions for Gyratory Crushers

After the gyratory crusher enters the normal crushing work, the following items must be observed and noted during operation:

(1) Check the operating conditions. There should be no abnormal knocking sounds inside the crusher. In special cases, stop the operation immediately. The feed particle size must not exceed 0.8 times the size of the feed opening. The discharge chute and the lower part of the crushing cone must not be blocked by ore, otherwise the ore will be lifted by the crushing cone, causing ore powder to enter the eccentric shaft and leading to accidents.

(2) Pay attention to the operation of the oil pump, and frequently check the oil flow indicators, oil temperature, and oil pressure readings at various locations. The temperature of the oil after passing through the cooler should not exceed 45-53°C, and the return oil temperature should not exceed 60°C. Oil should be supplied regularly to the upper suspension device.

(3) Regularly check the tension of the V-belts. The tension can be tested by pressing down on the V-belt with your thumb. If the depression does not exceed 1.5 cm, then the tension is appropriate (this test must be done before starting or after stopping the machine). Check for any broken belts and ensure that the V-belts do not get contaminated with oil.

(4) Frequently check whether the connecting pins between the crossbeam, intermediate frame body, and machine base are loose, and whether the 15.0 mm gap around the circumference is equal.

(5) Check if there is any looseness, missing parts, or severe wear in the liners of all sections.

(6) Strictly inspect the feeding conditions and prevent metal objects such as hammerheads and drill bits from entering the crushing chamber.

(7) In case of unexpected accidents during operation, such as the chute being blocked by ore, oil supply stopping, oil temperature exceeding 60°C, or knocking sounds from the bevel gears, handle it immediately according to the specified procedure.
