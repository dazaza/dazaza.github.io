Sex is an innate desire of human beings, but sexual ability is like a sealed sword that must be honed. How can we simply nourish the kidneys in daily life? Below, I will introduce a good method to everyone.

Playing chess, using computers, or standing/sitting for a long time without moving can easily induce male diseases. It's best to do tiptoe exercises every hour. This is not only a good aerobic exercise that promotes blood return in the lower limbs and exercises the flexor muscles, but it also helps improve sexual ability and helps men "nourish their kidneys."

Tiptoeing can benefit the kidneys and strengthen yang energy, thereby improving sexual function. Especially during urination, tiptoeing has more prominent effects. From the perspective of meridians, tiptoeing helps to smooth the three yin meridians of the foot. These meridians are distributed on the inner side of the thigh: the upper side is the Spleen Meridian of Foot-Taiyin, the middle is the Liver Meridian of Foot-Jueyin, and the back is the Kidney Meridian of Foot-Shaoyin. The liver, kidney, and spleen all dominate ascending energy, and the three organs have the role of stimulating central qi, thus achieving the effect of nourishing the kidneys and consolidating the root.

When tiptoeing, the muscles at the back of both calves contract, squeezing out blood volume, which promotes blood return in the lower limbs, enhances the strength of the pelvic floor muscles, thereby improving the blood filling of the corpus cavernosum during erection and reducing blood return, enhancing the hardness of the penis and maintaining erection time. For women, enhanced pelvic floor muscles mean enhanced sexual sensations, which also helps them reach orgasm.

For men with chronic prostatitis and prostate enlargement, tiptoeing during urination can make urination smoother. From the perspective of traditional Chinese medicine, prostate diseases mostly belong to insufficient central qi, leading to dribbling after urination. Tiptoeing during urination can enhance central qi, making urination much smoother.

There are many variations of tiptoeing. You can walk on your tiptoes, each time walking 30-50 steps, then rest briefly, and repeat several sets according to your physical condition. The speed can be adjusted according to your comfort and ease. You can also sit and tiptoe, keeping your knees parallel to your thighs, and place two water-filled bottles on your thighs for weight training. Each time, tiptoe 30-50 times.
