Types of Car Beauty and Decoration

As the number of car owners increases, a new type of decoration industry - car beauty and decoration - is gradually coming into people's view. Recently, when some people washed their cars at several car beauty centers in a certain urban area, the operators all enthusiastically promoted car beauty products to them and persuaded them to apply for membership cards. "These new types of car beauty projects come in many varieties, costing from several thousand yuan to tens of thousands of yuan." What confuses car owners is whether it is necessary to spend "huge sums" on car decoration and beauty.

How many types of car beauty and decoration are there? Below is the answer from professionals.

Modern car beauty services can generally be divided into several parts: body beauty, interior beauty, paint surface treatment, car protection, and car accessories.

1. Paint Surface Treatment

Paint surface treatment services can be divided into oxide film treatment, spray paint treatment, acid rain treatment, paint surface scratch treatment, paint surface damage treatment, and whole-car spraying. Paint surface treatment not only keeps your beloved car looking "young," but also restores scratches and damages caused by carelessness. It better protects the body and maintains the value of the car.

2. Car Accessories

Car accessories are the finishing touches of car beauty and also a representation of car life culture. They aim to turn the car into a mobile living space, such as car perfumes, wax dusters, goggles, handle covers, seat pads, etc. Car accessories bring people a sense of close care.

3. Interior Beauty

Interior beauty mainly includes in-car beauty, engine beauty, and trunk cleaning. Among these, in-car beauty includes vacuuming and cleaning the dashboard, roof lining, carpet, floor mat, seats, seat covers, and door linings, as well as steam sterilization, odor removal from air vents, and in-car air purification projects. Engine beauty includes engine washing and cleaning, spraying gloss protector, refurbishing treatment, and cleaning the three filters (referring to fuel filter, oil filter, and air filter).

4. Body Beauty

Body beauty mainly includes high-pressure car washing, rust removal, removal of asphalt, tar, and other contaminants, waxing and enhancing brightness with mirror treatment, new car waxing, hub, tire, bumper refurbishment, and chassis corrosion protection coating treatment. Regular car washing can remove dirt, acid rain, asphalt, and other pollutants from the car surface, preventing corrosion and damage to the paint and other body components. Timely waxing not only gives the body a bright effect but also provides meticulous care for the car through multi-functional car wax, which can protect against ultraviolet rays, acid rain, high temperatures, and static electricity.

5. Car Protection

Car protection projects include applying anti-explosion sun film, installing anti-theft devices, installing static eliminators, and installing car voice alarm devices. Although car protection does not directly affect the appearance of the car, it can well protect your beloved car.
