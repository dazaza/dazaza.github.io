Submission for the 2012 Chinese Romance Novel Contest has started 2011-07-20 21:40:05 Romance Novel Bar

In order to promote the in-depth attention of the literary circle to the overall development level and trend of mainland romance novels, establish a systematic display platform and authoritative evaluation standard, and provide higher quality original works for female readers, the famous literary website Hongxiu Literature teamed up with Romance Novel Bar, as well as Changjiang Literature & Art Press, Spring Wind Literature & Art Press, Jushi Culture, Huawen World, Boji Tianjuan and dozens of other famous publishers in China to jointly launch the "Chinese Romance Novel Contest" as a professional online literature brand competition.

Since the first Chinese Romance Novel Contest in 2007, it has been committed to "realizing the new value of romance themes" and aims to "create globally popular Chinese romance masterpieces", promoting the creation, research, and commentary of mainland romance novels towards maturity and professionalism. So far, the Chinese Romance Novel Contest has held three sessions, with more than 50,000 submissions, over 10 billion total clicks on participating works, and over 5 billion total words. Participants come from Mainland China, Hong Kong, Taiwan, the UK, the US, Australia, Afghanistan, and other countries and regions.

After three editions of the contest, as of October 2010, the Chinese Romance Novel Contest - this literary arena themed on "love" - has given birth to 10 writers who have made it onto the list of top Chinese online literature authors, created nearly 500 writers in the digital publishing field with monthly royalties exceeding 10,000 yuan, published 266 physical books, and sold 5 film and television adaptation rights.

The Chinese Romance Novel Contest has launched many excellent works that meet reader's aesthetic tastes, market demands, and possess both literary and artistic qualities, such as "Naked Marriage - The New Era of Marriage for Post-80s", "Runaway Bride", "Air Hostess Diary", "Love Encounter", "Grass Marriage", "Writing Our Ending Together", "Snail Marriage", "Ex-Wife Strikes Back", etc. It has also cultivated nearly a hundred promising new stars in literary creation like Ji Yue Jiao Jiao, Nie Pan Hui, Tang Xin Tian, Ru Ruo, Xie Ai Zai Liu Pi, Mu Zi Miao Miao, Mo Wu Bi Ge, and others.

The TV series "Naked Marriage Era" adapted from the champion work of the 2010 Chinese Romance Novel Contest, "Naked Marriage - The New Era of Marriage for Post-80s", caused a strong reaction throughout society because it "appropriately stirred the marital nerves of Post-80s." "Naked marriage" became the most attention-grabbing marital phenomenon in 2011 and sparked a nationwide discussion among Post-80s young people about their beliefs in love.

Currently, the Chinese Romance Novel Contest has successfully integrated an operational chain consisting of digital publishing, traditional publishing, and film and television copyright operations, achieving numerous successful cases and accumulating rich copyright operation experience. Meanwhile, it provides a good and rapid communication platform for various publishing institutions, film and television production companies, mobile reading bases, and other partners. With the rapid development of the online literature industry, the current online creative writing model has become specialized and professionalized, and public attention has shifted from traditional publishing to the online creative writing field.

Facing the changes and developments in the industry, Hongxiu Literature and Romance Novel Bar will once again join hands to start the 2012 Chinese Romance Novel Contest. Currently, the submission backend has officially opened, and we hope everyone actively submits their works, wishing the Chinese Romance Novel Contest to become the best display stage for all those who believe in love, yearn for love, express love, share love, and still have literary dreams, creators.

Submission Time:

July 15, 2011, to January 20, 2012

Manuscript Types:

Novels within the scope of romance including content related to CEOs, time-travel, palace intrigue, high-ranking officials, marriage, fantasy romance, female dominance, immortal knights, farming life, etc.

Submission Method:

Users can log in to the Romance Novel Bar homepage and enter the "Personal Center" to submit manuscripts online. Specific steps are as follows:

1. Register as a user and log in to the writer center;

2. If it is a new submission, after entering the writer center, choose "Work Management" and enter "Publish Work";

3. Fill in the work information and check "Participate in the Contest" under the "2012 Romance Contest" option;

4. If it is a serialized work, enter "Work Management", click "Modify Work", and check "Participate in the Contest" under the "2012 Romance Contest" option;

For more contest information, please check the official contest website going live on July 20th.

The 2012 Chinese Romance Novel Contest looks forward to your participation!
