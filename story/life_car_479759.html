Analysis of the Major Issues Faced by China's Car Beauty Industry

The following is a brief analysis of the major issues faced by this industry based on my years of experience.

1. Lack of National Industry Standards and Management Regulations in This Industry

It is understood that the current chaos in the domestic car beauty decoration industry is mainly due to the lack of national management regulations and industry standards. The rapid development of the automobile industry and the increasing popularity of family cars have attracted more investors to join this industry. It is necessary to introduce corresponding management methods and industry standards to regulate the market. This can not only reduce and to some extent eliminate counterfeit and inferior products from entering the market, standardize the behavior of manufacturers and dealers, but also allow the market to develop towards professionalization and standardization, thereby ensuring the legitimate rights and interests of a wide range of car consumers. The continuous heating up of the car market indicates that the car beauty decoration industry has great market potential. It can be said that China's car beauty decoration industry is still in its primary stage of development. How to solve the above-mentioned problems in the industry requires serious consideration by relevant departments.

2. Uneven Quality of Beauty Products

The profit of car beauty decoration mainly comes from the price difference of materials and labor costs. Some merchants often pass off inferior products as good ones for more profit, and ordinary consumers are unable to identify them. Some consumers who use inferior sun protection films even experience the phenomenon of not being able to see outside at night. Some consumers who change to wide-view rearview mirrors feel dizzy and disoriented when looking in the mirror while driving, making it difficult to judge distances, which brings great safety hazards to driving. These situations lead to a lack of trust in car beauty products and car beauty shops among consumers.

3. Lack of Standard Procedures

Due to the lack of unified service standards, the price quotations for car beauty decoration services vary greatly between different merchants. The profit margin of car decoration in the market is generally around 40%~50%, but the profit margin of some individual merchants can reach 120%~200%. Take leather seats as an example, those sold for 4000 yuan may cost less than 1500 yuan; seat covers costing less than 100 yuan are sometimes sold for over 500 yuan. Industry insiders believe that it is precisely because the beauty decoration industry lacks unified service standards that lead to huge differences in service prices, making the car beauty market a highly profitable one, where service quality is hard to measure. If this continues, consumers will lose confidence in the beauty decoration market.

5. Severe Shortage of Skilled Workers

As everyone knows, the "threshold" for entering the car beauty decoration industry is low. With 3~5 people, a high-pressure water gun, a few bottles of car wax, a few rolls of sun protection film, and a few simple tools, a small-scale beauty decoration shop can open for business. Taking car decoration as an example, the reality is that there is a shortage of professional car decoration technicians, and currently, there are no evaluation standards for this industry. Even for the 4S stores of various car brands, which are generally considered to have relatively high technical and service standards, although they all have their own decoration departments and related staff members have undergone strict training from manufacturers, they basically only handle polishing, waxing, cleaning interiors, installing reversing radars, and other decorative tasks. For more technically demanding jobs like applying sun protection films or sealing glazes, they usually outsource these tasks or hire professional technicians to handle them.

Severe Shortage of Professional Technical Talent

As everyone knows, the "threshold" for entering the car beauty decoration industry is low. With 3~5 people, a high-pressure water gun, a few bottles of car wax, a few rolls of sun protection film, and a few simple tools, a small-scale beauty decoration shop can open for business. According to introductions, newly hired technicians generally can only perform basic car beauty tasks such as washing cars and waxing, which do not require high technical skills. More technically demanding professional tasks like sealing glazes or applying sun protection films require experienced skilled workers. Taking car decoration as an example, the reality is that there is a shortage of professional car decoration technicians, and currently, there are no evaluation standards for this industry. Even for the 4S stores of various car brands, which are generally considered to have relatively high technical and service standards, although they all have their own decoration departments and related staff members have undergone strict training from manufacturers, they basically only handle polishing, waxing, cleaning interiors, installing reversing radars, and other decorative tasks. For more technically demanding fine-detail work like applying sun protection films or sealing glazes, they still need to hire professional technicians. Due to the severe shortage of such professional technical talent, some skilled car decoration technicians are even rushing between different decoration shops.

Industry insiders believe that the work scope of qualified car decorators or car beauticians should include analyzing customer requirements, formulating corresponding car beauty decoration plans, and then performing different treatments such as car exterior beauty, car interior beauty, paint surface beauty, and car protection. However, most car beauty decoration services in China today fall far short of this level.
