Shenhuo Co., Ltd.: Steady Growth, Broad Prospects_stock basics

by xl0958870 on 2009-12-05 02:10:37

Shenhuo Co., Ltd. (000933) (Enter the stock bar, new version of market conditions): The company is mainly engaged in coal production, sales, washing and processing, as well as power generation and supply; among which aluminum and aluminum products account for 61.01% of the main business revenue, and coal products account for 32.12%. The company is one of the 34 key national enterprises in the coal industry, with excellent coal quality, and is one of China's six largest anthracite bases. By the first half of 2009, the company has nearly 1 billion tons of resources reserves in the exploration stage with prospecting rights, including the Shanxi Zuquan project fully owned by the company, the Tianhong Ligang Coal Mine project in which the company holds a 70% stake, and the Yongchang Shijing Mine project in which the company holds a 75% stake, etc. According to national regulations, prospectors with prospecting rights will have priority in obtaining mining rights after exploration is completed; once all the above-mentioned exploration projects are completed, the company's coal resource reserves are expected to increase from the current around 1 billion tons to nearly 2 billion tons, which will lay a resource foundation for the expansion of the company's coal business capacity. Continuous governance of industry supply is conducive to steady growth in the company's output, while economic recovery driving the gradual recovery of coal demand makes the sale of high-quality coal products smoother. For the company's refined processed commercial coal, the rebound in sales volume in the second half of the year is certain. The company has always maintained a relatively balanced production and sales, and in the next two years, the sales volume of the company's commercial coal will also basically keep pace with the growth in output. Galaxy Securities estimates that the company's coal sales in 2009-2011 will reach 6.12 million tons, 8.12 million tons, and 9.27 million tons respectively, with the company's coal prices maintained at a relatively high level. With the gradual recovery of domestic steel and cement industries, the prices of the company's main coal products - pulverized coal concentrate and anthracite lumps - will be strongly supported. It is expected that the sales gross profit margin of the company's coal business in 2009 and 2010 will be 51.32% and 53.44%, respectively, continuing to maintain a high gross profit margin level.

In addition, accelerated peripheral demand is prompting a rebound in aluminum prices; it is estimated that the demand for electrolytic aluminum in North America, Europe, and Asia markets will see a significant positive growth in 2010, with an annual year-on-year growth rate reaching 5-10%, while during the same period, China's demand maintains stable growth, thus boosting global aluminum demand back to 37.95 million tons, growing by 10% year-on-year. Improved demand leads to further narrowing of global supply surplus in 2010. It is expected that aluminum prices will show a slow upward trend in 2010, with an average price of 15,800 yuan/ton being a likely event. Moreover, the electrolytic aluminum sector, which once dragged down the company's performance, will make a brilliant turnaround and become a new driver of performance. In 2010, the annual output of electrolytic aluminum will increase to 600,000 tons/year, growing by 70% year-on-year. Under the assumption of a neutral aluminum price, the electrolytic aluminum business is expected to generate a profit of at least 400 million yuan, and the contribution of the electrolytic aluminum business to the company's profits will rise to more than 23%. The advantages of the coal-electricity-aluminum material industry chain will become more prominent, and there is still room for reducing the cost of electrolytic aluminum by improving the efficiency and power supply ratio of self-owned power plants.

In the secondary market, the stock has been steadily climbing recently. Trading volume has moderately expanded, with a good coordination between volume and price. Moreover, from the quarterly report, we can see that many institutional investors are among the top ten shareholders of the stock, indicating the recognition of institutional investors towards this stock, and also showing that the stock's chips are relatively concentrated. It is expected that the stock will continue to expand its space upwards in the future. Investors are advised to pay close attention.
