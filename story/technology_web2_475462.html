Huangshi Xisesi Centipede Breeding Base? The final centipede breeding agricultural science and technology popularization project in Xisesi District raises adult centipedes about 12 cm long, 0.5-1.1 cm wide, slightly flat on the back and abdomen, with a nearly round head plate protruding at the front end. The head plate and the first dorsal plate are golden yellow, so it is also called Golden Headed Centipede. They are distributed throughout the south and north of China, and the wild ones are mainly produced in the middle and lower reaches of the Yangtze River region.

Centipedes are omnivorous animals that feed on various insects, animal viscera, vegetables, fruits, and kitchen leftovers.

According to the records in "Changguo Dianyong", the local people of Moyinling breed centipedes as fields to supply the medicine shops, which indicates that this practice has a long history, but there are no reports of such practices today.

Moyinling is located within the territory of Daishan, Zhejiang Province, and is famous for its abundant production of medicinal centipedes. The locals make a living by operating medicinal centipedes and manage the suitable habitats for them, protect the population, and promote their reproduction or improve their growth environment by supplementing food, etc. After harvesting, they sell them to drug merchants to earn a living. This shows that there were people engaged in the cultivation of medicinal centipedes in ancient China.
