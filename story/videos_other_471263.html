Unemployed man kills cohabiting woman and hides her body in the wardrobe

by zxyasdu40 on 2011-08-05 13:20:58

Guiyang Online News - On October 9, I learned from the Tongren Public Security Bureau that a jobless man from Guangdong killed his cohabiting woman and hid the body in the wardrobe. The police of Tongren City captured the suspect after seven days of investigation in Guangdong and Guizhou Provinces.

At night on September 21, the Tongren Public Security Bureau's 110 Command Center received an alarm from the public that an unidentified female corpse was found in a rented house on Huanxi Road in Tongren City. The police found out that the renter Su had left Tongren and returned to his hometown Jieyang City of Guangdong Province 14 days after he rented the house. On September 27, the special investigating team got feedback information from Jieyang City that Su had secretly sneaked back to his mother-in-law’s home in Yinjiang Autonomous County. The police tracked him down to Yinjiang and finally successfully captured the criminal suspect Su at Baxi Town.

The police preliminarily found out that the criminal suspect Su was originally a jobless person from Puning City of Guangdong Province. In September this year, Su met a woman about twenty years old at Guangzhou Railway Station. They talked very well and came to Tongren through the housing intermediary company and rented a house on Huanxi Road in Tongren City for cohabitation. They went shopping together, stayed up all night surfing the Internet in the Internet bar, and were inseparable. On September 13, when the woman found out that he was married, she quarreled with him and threatened to report it to the police. Su lost his temper and hit her unconscious with an iron plate, then cruelly strangled her to death, and hid the woman's body in the wardrobe by the bed before fleeing.

Currently, the case is under further review.
