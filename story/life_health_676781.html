Can I have an abortion if I have a cold?

by betty2012 on 2012-02-26 15:11:40

Some women who have scheduled for painless abortion surgery, but before the abortion surgery, they developed symptoms of colds and cough. At this time, many women will question: Can I still have an abortion if I have a cold? In fact, there is an important precaution before the abortion surgery: to keep warm and prevent catching a cold. Therefore, it's best to take good care of yourself before the surgery and try not to have an abortion while having a cold.

Can I still have an abortion if I have a cold?

Experts from Xi'an Gynecology Hospital pointed out that women should not have an abortion after catching a cold, and the date of the abortion surgery should be postponed. The cold should be treated actively first, and then the abortion can be performed after recovery. If there are only mild cold symptoms such as sneezing, the doctor will check and confirm that it does not affect the abortion surgery, then the surgery can continue. However, if the cold is severe with symptoms like general weakness and fever, the abortion surgery must not be carried out. Generally, the abortion should be performed after the cold has recovered because a cold often comes with inflammation. If the cold is serious, the body is weak and the immunity is low. Under such circumstances, having an abortion surgery may easily lead to gynecological infections and hinder physical recovery.

In addition to colds, there are some other situations where women cannot have an abortion, such as during the acute phase of infectious diseases or chronic infectious diseases, severe systemic diseases (such as heart failure, symptomatic hypertension, pulmonary tuberculosis with high fever, anemia, vaginal fungal infections and other gynecological diseases) that cannot withstand surgery; acute reproductive organ inflammation, such as vaginitis, severe cervical erosion, pelvic inflammatory disease, etc., the surgery can only be performed after the inflammation has been cured; severe vomiting caused by pregnancy leading to uncorrected acidosis; within 4 hours before the surgery, if the body temperature is above 37.5℃ twice; those allergic to anesthetic drugs, etc., cannot undergo abortion surgery. Therefore, women must pay attention to the precautions before the abortion surgery, read carefully about what the precautions are, and avoid causing irreparable consequences due to their own negligence.
