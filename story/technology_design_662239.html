Common needs when copying sales targets

by phonics6168 on 2012-02-10 02:13:33

Good sales copy reaches and touches the needs of the reader. The ability of a copywriter to identify with their audience helps them build a connection with their readers. By doing so, it makes their copy work more effectively since people now become more curious about the copy in front of them. Being able to touch any trigger point and grab the attention of the reader allows a copywriter to deliver their information more effectively. Due to the limitations of online marketing in reaching people, effective copy is key. Being able to touch on the needs of the reader and establish a connection is the first step in delivering your sales message. Once the connection is established, it significantly increases any opportunity for making an online sale. Of course, the needs you target in your copy should align with the product and/or service you are offering! With that said... here are 5 common needs that most people have, and if targeted correctly, can enhance the effectiveness of your marketing copy.

Making money is a common need and/or interest for the majority of people, regardless of their current financial situation. People are naturally drawn to this area because money is commonly known as the "symbol" of success, it provides for clothing and food, and quite frankly, because we all have bills to pay.

Saving time seems to be a commodity that is always in increasing demand for all of us, especially when it feels like there's not enough of it. With associations today involving 60-hour workweeks and societal schedules that can be very demanding, finding ways to save time (or rather, find more time) allows many people to feel they can live a more fulfilling and happier life.

Whether it’s about getting into shape or learning how to better take care of one’s health, this is a massive area of interest for just about everyone. Good health is also seen as a sign of youth, and no one wants to give that up, regardless of how old they may be.

Different from making money, as we discussed above, this concern area is about how to properly manage what you already have. Generating a healthy income has no benefit if you can't hang onto it. Learning how to "grow" your savings not only increases the feeling of financial security but also helps alleviate stress and anxiety.

Boosting self-esteem is crucial because it affects how we conduct ourselves and the decisions we make. Having strong self-esteem also has a significant impact on our professional and social performance.

Any good sales copy begins with the ability to connect with the reader. Due to the limitations of online marketing in reaching your target audience, connecting through your copywriting is key to establishing this aspect. "Getting" the reader is the first step in gaining someone's attention so you can deliver the information you want. Knowing what most people want, a copywriter can use these "triggers" to capture their attention. The five common needs discussed above that most people have, if targeted correctly in your copy, can help enhance the effectiveness of your email marketing. By providing this message to an audience that already has an interest, it greatly improves your chances of making online sales.
