What do you need in a CMS?

There are many things we need in a Content Management System, but some of them are more important than others:

1. Quick and easy installation

2. A simple management interface

3. Quick and convenient CMS extension features

4. Easy template application

5. Practical user experience

Although this may not be very obvious, these are indeed what you need the most. An attractive design is the best factor, which includes a friendly interface. Therefore, before spending a lot of time and energy on the system, it's necessary to consider the interface issue.
