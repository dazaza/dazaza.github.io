Vigorously promote celebrity culture - accelerate the construction of a culturally renowned county

by xyxandy8 on 2012-03-08 09:35:01

Promote the culture of celebrities, speed up the construction of a famous cultural county. Yesterday, Pingyang County held the academic symposium of "Huang Gongwang and Pingyang" and the groundbreaking ceremony of Huang Gongwang Culture Square. Zhao Derun, a member of the Central Institute of History and Culture and editor-in-chief of Chinese Calligraphy and Painting Magazine, Xue Yongnian, a member of the Central Institute of History and Culture and chairman of the Theory Committee of China Artists Association, Qiu Yangjun, vice mayor of Wenzhou city, local leaders Wang Zhongyi, Huang Min, Sun Ruiqing, Hu Fangyong and others attended the event.

The academic symposium of "Huang Gongwang and Pingyang" is an important part of the activities for the grand exhibition of calligraphy and painting in China (Wenzhou) - "Huang Gongwang's paintings return to his hometown" and the 2011 National Famous Artists Invitation Exhibition of Chinese Painting. The Yuan Dynasty painter Huang Gongwang was born in 1269 and was the leader of the "Four Masters of the Yuan Dynasty". In the existing historical documents signed by Huang Gongwang that indicate his place of origin, all are named "Huang Gongwang from Pingyang", so it can be determined that he was from Pingyang. The representative work of Huang Gongwang's highest achievement in painting is "Dwelling in the Fuchun Mountains". It is a long scroll, with clear and moist brushwork and a simple and distant artistic conception, depicting the beautiful scenery of the banks of the Fuchun River in early autumn. The layout is sparse but well-arranged, with endless changes, vividly portraying the vast and continuous landscape of southern Jiangnan, reaching the realm of "mountains and rivers being thick, grass and trees flourishing". Later generations of painters such as Zou Zhilin praised it: "The wise discuss Huang Gongwang's paintings, they are like the calligraphy of Wang Xizhi, holy. As for 'Dwelling in the Fuchun Mountains', the changes and excitement in his brushwork are also like Wang Xizhi's 'Lanting Xu', holy and divine." This painting is compared to the 'Lanting Xu' of painting.

Qiu Yangjun delivered a speech at the academic symposium of "Huang Gongwang and Pingyang". He said that in recent years, while fully accelerating economic development, the Wenzhou Municipal Party Committee and Municipal Government have attached great importance to the construction of a major cultural city, especially regarding the research, publicity, and inheritance of historical and cultural figures as an important part of the construction of a major cultural city. Huang Gongwang, as an outstanding representative among the historical figures of Wenzhou, strengthening the research on his cultural history is of great significance for exploring the cultural origins of Wenzhou, showcasing the cultural heritage of Wenzhou, and promoting the great development and prosperity of Wenzhou's culture. He sincerely requests that all masters and experts share their valuable opinions and provide more precious suggestions on the excavation, research, and publicity campaign of Huang Gongwang's culture and traditional historical culture carried out this time.

Party Secretary Wang Zhongyi said in his speech that culture is the soul of a city and also an important support for the economic and social development of a place. Speeding up the construction of a famous cultural county and enhancing cultural soft power is currently a very important and urgent task for Pingyang County. During the "Twelfth Five-Year Plan" period, we will take the creation of a historic and cultural city as a starting point, deeply implement the "four twos" project, vigorously promote the 1700-year-old brilliant history of Pingyang, continuously improve the cultural connotation and taste, and promote Pingyang from a famous cultural county to a strong cultural county. As the hometown of Huang Gongwang, it is our duty to publicize, excavate, and carry forward the cultural heritage of Huang Gongwang well. This academic discussion will surely fill the blank in the academic research of "Huang Gongwang and Pingyang", and will surely have a positive impact on the future cultural development of Pingyang.

Zhao Derun introduced the basic situation of Huang Gongwang's paintings and a series of art seminars around "Dwelling in the Fuchun Mountains" conducted at home and abroad since the beginning of the year, highly praising the positive attitude of Wenzhou and Pingyang towards cultural inheritance.

At the meeting, participating experts discussed around the theme of "Huang Gongwang and Pingyang". It is reported that the results of this seminar will be compiled into a book. (Pingyang News Network reporter Huang Jianping)
