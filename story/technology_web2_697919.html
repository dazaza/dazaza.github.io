Provide a free mutual brushing place for everyone.

by bidetall050 on 2012-03-08 20:53:49

Once, someone helps you with a single brush, thousands of members buy from each other and give each other positive reviews (it's not one-on-one mutual brushing, but thousands or tens of thousands of people manually brushing each other, real and safe), the whole process only involves the flow of funds, while the credibility of both parties keeps increasing! This is the principle of free mutual brushing. For example: You can help other members improve their positive review by taking on a task, then you will get the right to post a task in the mutual brushing area, letting friends on the platform help you improve your positive review; you help others with the circulation of funds, there is actually no loss, and the credibility of both sides keeps increasing! Do you understand? Mutual brushing is free, so how does the platform survive? Mutual brushing is free, but some people are unwilling or don't have time to help others brush. So they can buy guns on the platform to post tasks, just let others brush positive reviews for them (they don't have to help others brush themselves, saving time, safe and convenient). The platform makes money by selling guns, providing everyone with a place for free mutual brushing.

For example, if you want to brush a 10 yuan product. Then the platform will hold your 10 yuan as a guarantee deposit. After the other party takes over the task, they use their own 10 yuan to really purchase your product on Taobao, then give you a positive review. In this way, your Taobao store gets a 10 yuan income and a positive review. And the other party gets your 10 yuan platform deposit (with platform deposit, he himself can post tasks for others to help him brush).
