What is the price of adult teeth straightening in Nanjing?

by 454kq on 2011-05-23 11:41:26

What is the price of adult teeth correction in Nanjing? Many friends have misaligned teeth in adulthood due to poor tooth development during childhood. At this point, adult orthodontic treatment is needed. So, what is the cost of adult teeth correction? Experts from the Stomatology Department of the 454th Hospital of the PLA in Nanjing introduce that the price of adult teeth correction is influenced by the following factors.

1. Regional differences affect the price of adult teeth correction. There are urban-rural disparities in different regions, and hospitals of different levels have different charging standards; in hospitals of different natures, the charging standards of for-profit hospitals may fluctuate slightly. Patients should inquire about the overall cost with their attending doctor before starting orthodontic treatment, so they can be prepared.

2. The price of adult teeth correction depends on the treatment goals the patient wants to achieve. This determines the difficulty and duration of orthodontic treatment. It's not hard to understand that the cost of adult teeth correction is positively correlated with the difficulty and duration of the treatment.

3. The price of adult teeth correction is related to the correction method and appliances used by the patient. Orthodontists will choose a reasonable correction method based on the patient's malocclusion condition, and different methods have different costs, with more technically demanding methods costing more. Then, doctors will select suitable appliances according to the patient's financial situation, such as domestically produced appliances being cost-effective and high-quality, while imported appliances, though expensive, are precise and efficient.

4. The patient's level of cooperation during the treatment process directly affects the length of the treatment course. If the patient cooperates well, the treatment time is shorter, and the cost will naturally decrease.
