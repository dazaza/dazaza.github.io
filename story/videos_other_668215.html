What are the causes of geriatric schizophrenia? Why does geriatric schizophrenia occur? Everyone knows that the physical and mental state of the elderly is often fragile. Moreover, there are many socio-psychological issues before the onset of geriatric schizophrenia, such as retirement, changes in social and family status, loss of a spouse, separation from children, sexual indifference, disharmony with neighbors, etc. Due to age and physical condition, the elderly's tolerance for mental setbacks decreases over time, while opportunities for encountering various psychological stressors increase, significantly affecting the progression of the disease.
