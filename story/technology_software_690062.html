The longest movie (the story keeps updating) 2012 movie

by hoouk686 on 2012-03-04 22:18:57

It's been over sixty days since we broke up, time really flies. I always feel like I'm still with him and we haven't broken up.

Actually, I've been thinking, when he asked me where I was and wanted to see me after the breakup, if I hadn't gone out of town and met him then, would the outcome have been different?

Being single really gives you a lot of choices. After the breakup, I did meet a few people who were good to me, but they were probably just passing by, or maybe not even that, because there was no feeling, so I stopped contacting them.

After the breakup, I tried to get back together with him, but he refused, saying "a good horse doesn't eat the grass it has already passed." Fine, if we can't get back together, can't I just forget about you? But why does it seem like people related to you keep showing up in my life? How am I supposed to forget you?

ZJ...you always remind me of YM, but unfortunately, fate tied us together.

After breaking up with him, you've always been by my side. Whenever I was upset, your shoulder was always my refuge. You didn't have to care about me getting drunk or being sad for him, letting me continue to fall into self-destruction.

When talking about you, should the time go back to the first day we met? I got to know both you and YM at the same time. It was YM who pursued me, so we've always been friends.

I don't know when it started, but every time YM and I had an argument and things weren't pleasant, I would turn to you first to vent. You would also occasionally send text messages asking me to introduce pretty girls to you.

Perhaps due to frequent contact, on the eve of my breakup with him, which was the night we had the car accident, you confessed to me after seeing the journal I shared with you. I was stunned, never expecting you to like me, given how high your standards usually were.

After that, we texted every day. I always felt like I was cheating on YM, but maybe fate was destined to make us break up. Our relationship grew increasingly distant, and he no longer came to see me every day like he did at the beginning of the school year.

In mid-October, my beloved grandfather passed away, and YM didn't come to see him, claiming "no identity, no reason to visit." Big brother, we were dating, and you've met all my relatives on my mother's side. Everyone knows that my grandfather has you as his "grandson-in-law." Are you telling me you have no identity or reason?

Later, ZJ was worried I might be upset, so he specially came from Wuhan to Anqing to see my deceased grandfather. So, who is my boyfriend, him or you?

Despite the sadness, during the week my grandfather passed away, I took ZJ to play around in the mountains and rivers. He said he'd never played like that before, making me realize he was a "child without a childhood."

After settling matters regarding my grandfather, it also marked the end of my relationship with YM. Breaking up with him wasn't because I had a new love or had fallen for someone else, but because of his attitude towards me and his family's coldness towards me. In other words, "our love was destroyed by family ties and attitudes."

He always said I had a bad temper and personality. How is my temper bad? Isn't it just that I don't like talking when I'm in a bad mood? Have I ever yelled at you or cursed you? No, right? Is my character bad? Hmph, it's not that my character is bad, but rather that your group of friends hasn't interacted much with me, and they just don't like me. Among my circle of friends, who has ever said I have a bad character?

Alright, let's not dwell on these irrelevant details; they're not the main theme of this diary.

As time passes after the breakup, I still think about YM, but not as intensely as I did at first.

It might be because of ZJ and FXW. Speaking of these two people, there are some long stories behind them.

After breaking up with YM, I fell into deep despair, often going to bars to drink until I forgot about him.

On the evening of December 11th, at Seven Bar, I met FXW. We could say we both had feelings for each other because he gave me a familiar feeling.

That night at the bar, I unexpectedly ran into a large group of friends. I moved from one booth to another, being forced to drink cup after cup until my brain went fuzzy. Finally, Fatso invited me to go to the pedestrian street with him, and it turned out we were riding in FXW's car.

The moment I sat in his car, it reminded me of the first time I saw YM outside Baidu, and sadness instantly welled up inside me.

Later, we ended up in front of Qingyun Building. FXW pulled out a bouquet of roses from the trunk and knelt down on one knee to ask me to be his girlfriend. Without hesitation, I agreed.

The next day, after sobering up, I remembered what I had done the previous night and felt an overwhelming urge to strangle myself.

But I decided to try to get along with him because I could find a sense of familiarity in him.

I told ZJ about me and FXW, and he said he was afraid of losing me, so he took leave and returned from Wuhan, bringing a surprise.

He came back suddenly, without my knowledge. Instead of being happy, I made him extremely angry.

During the days he was back in Hefei, I was heartbroken and troubled because two boys were asking me to choose between them, but I couldn't make a decision.

One night, I finally cleared things up with FXW at Muse Bar. I cried my heart out, not knowing why I felt so sad and reluctant to part with him after such a short relationship of only a few weeks. In the bar, he hugged me, and I knew he was crying too.

Later, ZJ came to pick me up. FXW walked me out of the bar and handed me over to ZJ, saying, "I'm entrusting her to you." At the time, I was so caught up in my own tears that I didn't pay attention to their conversation.

Sitting in ZJ's car, with the wind blowing fiercely on my face, ZJ said he had a surprise for me. I slightly raised the corner of my mouth and asked what it was, and he said I would know soon.

The location was below my house. The surprise was flowers and candles spelling out "I Love You." I never expected this to be his surprise because I had only seen such romantic gestures on TV.

A few days later, ZJ returned to Wuhan, and I continued my old life, hanging out with friends until midnight.

I don't know why, but whenever I casually mentioned something, FXW took it seriously and gave me big surprises.

First, at the intersection of the ring road, he opened my car door and knelt down again to profess his love. Then, that night at Seven Bar, I was pulled onto the stage by Fatso, and FXW knelt down again in front of everyone. The men and women in the bar cheered, pushing us to embrace.

Suddenly, I realized that the events happening to me were like movie plots.

"I lost everything overnight" - I've only seen such things on TV. I never imagined I would become someone who has nothing, unable to have whatever I want or buy whatever I desire anymore. My once-unhappy family has now become fragmented, and it feels like I've lost my father. I haven't seen him for months. I value face, so I don't want others to know about my family issues; I bear it alone.

Friends ask me why I'm not in school and why I've become so self-destructive, assuming it's because of my breakup with YM. To whom can I confide in everything? After the New Year, I can no longer live off my parents' support, spending money fearlessly. I need to earn my own living and take care of myself. This kind of life is something I've never experienced. Sometimes lying in bed, reminiscing about my past and present, I feel like a princess who used to worry about nothing has now become a commoner...

Just a few days ago, ZJ and I had a serious talk, and I knew who I wanted to be with. However, it seemed like he thought I was deceiving him, so he returned from Wuhan without taking leave.

I was sound asleep at home when my dear mom kept calling me to wake up. Still half-asleep, I asked her what was wrong, and she told me ZJ was at our house. I was startled and heard his voice.

But I didn't want to get up. Listening to ZJ's conversation with my mom, he always goes to my mom when he can't deal with me. I know he loves me, but I don't know why I don't want to be with him.

At noon, he and my mom went out to eat, and I got up, washed up, and joined them. Seeing the table full of dishes I liked, I later found out they were ordered by him. During the meal, I avoided looking at him; I didn't know how to face him.

After the meal, my mom and I argued because of him. I slammed the door and stormed out. He chased after me and asked if he would follow wherever I went. He said yes, and I felt annoyed.

Every time it's because of him and me, my mom scolds me. Whenever a boy likes me and sends me flowers or gifts, he talks endlessly to me, acting like we're already married.

I feel like the ZJ I know now is completely different from the one I used to know, so I gradually didn't want to have too much contact with him.

Walking on the pedestrian street, he followed me step by step, as if fearing I would be kidnapped. Later, I went to a sister's clothing store, and he waited outside the door, making the clothing seller say to me, "Wow, you're so lucky, having a boyfriend bodyguard following you around, worrying you'll run away?" I smiled.

I don't know how long he stayed, but when I didn't see him at the door, I grabbed my bag and left, wondering why he left suddenly. Was it because of the text message I sent?

On the road, he kept calling and texting me, but I ignored them, leisurely surfing the internet in the net cafe. However, I had an unusual feeling in my heart.

In the evening, he texted me, telling me that he was very sad when he got home, only to find out his parents were on a business trip. He slept for a long time. When he woke up, he felt empty, so he revisited all the places we had been to alone, sending me texts telling me where he was and how he felt until he reached the entrance of my house.

He asked me to come down and see him, to chat with him. I refused, but eventually, I couldn't resist and met him.

I said I would send him home, and he agreed. On the way, we didn't talk much. Once we arrived at his place, he showed me his photos, gave me a tour of his home, and told me about his day. Finally, in a song, he cried, and so did I. Mysteriously, I felt heartache. I hurt him, hurt a boy who loved me deeply, hurt a boy I tried hard to escape from his embrace...

Lately, I've been suffering from severe insomnia. I want to close my eyes and rest well, but when I close my eyes, I keep thinking about many things, unable to sleep... If I can't sleep, then so be it. My appetite has dropped significantly. I haven't eaten for two days and don't feel hungry. Am I trying to become immortal? Should I see a doctor?

I don't know when it started, but I've become someone who relies on others, clings to others, dislikes being alone, and fears loneliness. I always drag others to accompany me in whatever I do. However, the people around me are busy with their own affairs, and no one revolves around me. I can't selfishly demand anyone to stay by my side all the time. Recently, I've become a homebody, unwilling to go out. Besides my phone, computer, and TV, I have no one to keep me company. My former good friends rarely contact me now. Suddenly, I realize Zhang Jun was right: in the movie "2012," people grow lonelier as they grow older, and the number of good friends decreases. The only ones who will accompany us for life are our future partners...

I've realized that true friends should not be made recklessly. When choosing friends, some are hypocritical, some like to compare with each other, and some act differently in front of and behind others. Recently, I often seek you out because I consider you a true friend, not for any other reasons... A few months ago, we were good friends, playing together often. I don't know when it started, but you slowly distanced yourself from me. Was it because you were busy, or for other reasons? You know it in your heart, and so do I. I won't say more. I admit that at the time, there was a little misunderstanding between you and him, but when you told me that he criticized me in front of you, shouldn't I believe you instead? Sister, Xue, Yu, Qiu, you four are my best friends for life. Except for you, I currently have no close friends. Now that we've all gone our separate ways, our friendship still exists, right?

I can't describe my current mood. Today, I heard a song that I really like—"Don't Let You Cry." This song makes me think of someone. Right now, I believe he knows I'm talking about him because he understands me the most, and he is my safe haven, as he himself said. Ha ha, thinking about him makes me feel like I still have someone who understands me, and this feeling is great...

Right now, I may seem happy, but I'm actually not. I often cry secretly when it's quiet at night, and no one knows why. I don't know why I cry, I'm just sad... We agreed to change my phone number, but I hesitated and didn't want to. It's because I'm afraid that those who want to find me won't be able to. I want to go somewhere far away, start a new life, meet new friends, and say goodbye to the past...

Today is March 1st. It's been a year. Last year at this time, I went to a bar for the first time, broke up last year at this time, and met YM and ZJ last year at this time. Time flies. I don't know why I feel particularly sentimental today, recalling many memories. If time could rewind, I would definitely choose not to know either of you. That way, perhaps I wouldn't have changed, wouldn't have become so unfamiliar, and wouldn't have been told by friends that I'm not pure anymore. But what can be done? Now, I've learned to disguise myself and know how to pretend to be happy and laugh without being seen through...

On the way to the teacher's house today, I suddenly wanted to ride a motorcycle, remembering how comfortable it felt in the past. I'm not a pampered young lady. Although I've never done housework since birth, it doesn't mean I can't. I love spending money, but I've never spent a penny of a man's money... Xue told me today, "No matter whether you're happy or not, I have to say, you've changed. Maybe it's because I wasn't by your side, I don't know what happened to you, but you're not as pure as before." Ha ha, dear, I haven't changed. I've just disguised the real me. Do you know? I don't cry as easily as before, and I won't sob uncontrollably in your arms. After all these experiences, the only thing I've learned is to be strong, and I will slowly learn not to depend on anyone, to be independent. Don't worry...
