The deferred fee is two ten-thousandths, and both buyers and sellers need to pay on the same day.

The payment direction of the deferred fee is determined by comparing the delivery declaration quantity with the receipt declaration quantity. When the delivery declaration quantity is less than the receipt declaration quantity, the short position pays the deferred fee to the long position; when the delivery declaration quantity is greater than the receipt declaration quantity, the long position pays the deferred fee to the short position; when the delivery declaration quantity equals the receipt declaration quantity, no deferred fee payment occurs. Compared with other investment methods of gold and silver, Gold and Silver T+D [http://blog.sina.com.cn/u/2427482834] has greater advantages to attract investors.

Investors [http://blog.sina.com.cn/u/2427482834]. Compared with other investment methods, Gold and Silver T+D have evening trading hours from 21:00 to 02:30 on Monday to Thursday. Due to time differences, important economic events that affect international gold price trends often occur during this period. Since the T+D model is real-time trading, during the night trading hours, risks caused by drastic changes in the external market at night can be avoided in the first place, making the trading more flexible compared to gold and silver futures.

Secondly, Gold and Silver T+D adopt a margin mode, using the principle of leverage, requiring less capital input and lower investment thresholds. Compared with physical gold, silver bars, and paper gold, only 15% of the funds as margin are required for Gold and Silver T+D transactions. In physical gold or silver or paper gold transactions, if an investor invests 100,000 yuan, they would buy 100,000 yuan worth of gold bars or paper gold in full. However, if following a 15% margin ratio, a T+D mode investor only needs to put up 15,000 yuan to buy 100,000 yuan worth of gold and silver assets. The lower the margin ratio, the more obvious the leverage amplification effect, and the corresponding returns and risks are also higher.

Thirdly, unlike futures, there is no delivery time limit. In Gold and Silver T+D business, how long the position is held can be decided by the investor themselves, without having to deliver regardless of the price like in futures, reducing the operational costs for investors while allowing free delivery times, where delivery can be applied for each trading day. When investments are unfavorable, positions can be sold, and unlimited holding is possible when favorable.

Furthermore, there are diversified transactions, and Gold and Silver T+D have a short-selling mechanism. The short-selling mechanism of Gold and Silver T+D is that once a long position enters and the market reverses, the long position can be closed and reversed to short sell. This not only recovers lost money but also yields profits, making investors more active compared to passively waiting in stocks.

In this section, various banks' Gold and Silver T+D products are edited.

Currently, five banks have launched Gold and Silver T+D wealth management products, namely Industrial and Commercial Bank of China (ICBC), Industrial Bank, Minsheng Bank, Shenzhen Development Bank, and Everbright Bank. The operation process for Gold and Silver T+D is basically the same across these five banks. One can register and open an account for agency physical gold accounts through the designated counters of the corresponding banks. Personal online banking can also be opened to register and open an account for agency physical gold accounts. After the Gold Exchange returns the successful registration information of the gold account, one can sign the Precious Metal Deferred Trading Agreement of the corresponding bank on online banking and then operate the Au (T+D) business.

Among them, ICBC's telephone banking only provides Gold T+D business services, while Silver T+D business cannot yet be handled through telephone banking.

Currently, although none of the five banks forcibly require U-shield for online banking accounts for precious metal transactions, the daily payment amount for order card customers generally fails to meet the transaction needs of Gold and Silver T+D, so it is still necessary to handle U-shield for Gold and Silver T+D transactions.

The trading starting point for Gold T+D is the same across these five banks, with a minimum trading unit of 1000 grams of gold and silver. The minimum trading amount is the current price of 1000 grams of gold and silver multiplied by the margin ratio, and handling fees are charged according to a certain percentage of the actual price of gold and silver per transaction. The margins and handling fees vary among these five banks.

ICBC and Shenzhen Development Bank have the highest, with a trading margin of 15%, and handling fees of eighteen ten-thousandths; Industrial Bank's trading margin is 12%, and handling fees are seventeen ten-thousandths; Everbright Bank's trading margin is 15%, and handling fees are fifteen ten-thousandths; Minsheng Bank has the lowest margin and handling fees, with a trading margin of 10.5%, and handling fees of fourteen ten-thousandths.

Besides, although the Gold and Silver T+D businesses of CMB and SPDB are not yet open, both parties have indicated that they have passed the approval. SPDB stated that its Gold and Silver T+D business platform has been basically completed, but the launch time has not been announced.
