E8 Financial Management Software Enhanced Edition

by jupingliu on 2009-03-31 10:32:48

The enhanced version of E8 Financial Management Software complies with China's latest accounting system. After years of development and improvement, it has become one of the best financial software in the industry. The system adopts the design concept of "general program, user-specific", which can be personalized to meet the needs of various industries. It involves a large number of users in construction industry, industry, commerce, government agencies and institutions, fully demonstrating the extensive adaptability of the system.

E8 Financial Management Software includes the preparation, modification, review, query, and printing of vouchers. The software automatically posts, closes accounts, and calculates the summary balance of each account. The software automatically generates journals, subsidiary ledgers, general ledgers, and multiple-column accounts. Comprehensive queries and print outputs can be performed in different formats. In report preparation, various accounting tables can be edited and managed. Users can design and print various financial reports according to their needs. The report preparation function is extremely flexible. The system presets standard financial reports required by the latest accounting system, and users can also design various special reports according to actual needs. The cashier management function of E8 Financial Management Software greatly facilitates the account management for cashiers. The cashier-accountant reconciliation function reduces various inconveniences of manual reconciliation and provides convenience for the coordination work between cashiers and accountants. E8 Financial Management Software belongs to multi-set multi-ledger management software. Each set of books is independent and does not interfere with each other. It has the cross-month bookkeeping function. When a new month arrives and the previous month has not been closed, you can directly enter the new month. At the same time, it provides an interface for handling the accounts of the previous month, thus ensuring the timeliness and accuracy of account processing. The convenient maintenance function of accounting subjects and summary phrases allows you to add subjects and summary phrases at any time when entering vouchers. In addition, the unique multi-ledger bookkeeping management function of E8 Financial Management Software greatly meets the needs of accounting in various enterprises and institutions. The same voucher can be easily recorded in different ledgers at the same time, until finally automatically generating various accounting data and financial statements corresponding to each ledger, greatly reducing the workload of accountants. The unique connection to remote databases through ordinary broadband (such as ADSL) allows easy data sharing operations on wide area networks (Internet), saving users huge broadband rental costs.

Function features:

1. Voucher entry, review, posting, summarization, query, and printing functions;

2. Automatic posting function;

3. General ledger, arbitrary level and subject classification accounts, receivable and payable accounts, multiple-column accounts, fixed asset accounts, VAT dedicated accounts. All ledgers are automatically posted based on vouchers;

4. Fully automatic production of balance sheets, income statements, cash flow statements;

5. All accounting subject settings adopt tree-shaped directories, with clear hierarchy and structure;

6. You can directly select accounting subjects through the tree-shaped directory; or quickly input through the accounting subject code;

7. The system automatically identifies and preliminarily reviews bookkeeping vouchers, automatically prompts methods that do not conform to accounting bookkeeping methods, strictly adhering to the accounting bookkeeping principle: "There must be a debit for every credit, and debits must equal credits";

8. Decimal place self-definition function;

9. Independent cashier management module, convenient for comparison with finance;

10. Unique reconciliation function;

11. Customizable voucher type (receipt, payment, transfer, record) function;

12. Multi-ledger function within the same set of books;

13. The same voucher can be conveniently recorded in different ledgers (such as public accounts and internal accounts), greatly reducing workload;

14. The system provides an interface with EXCEL, allowing you to export vouchers, accounts receivable and payable, ledgers, reports, etc., to EXCEL. You can perform secondary processing on these exported data to generate various chart formats;

15. The network version uses three-layer structure programming, making the software run more stably and data safer.

16. Unique connection to remote databases through ordinary broadband (such as ADSL), easily achieving data sharing operations on wide area networks (Internet), saving users huge broadband rental costs.
