2010 China Leather Industry Association working conference was held in Beijing_1548

by qazwsx852 on 2012-02-18 19:14:09

The 2010 China Leather Association Working Conference was held in Beijing from March 1st to March 3rd, with all staff members of the China Leather Association in attendance. At the conference, Chairman Zhang Shuhua summarized the achievements and shortcomings of the association's work in 2009, proposed the working思路and priorities for 2010, and pointed out that as we enter the post-financial crisis period, the domestic situation will become more severe and complex. The association needs to further strengthen market research, provide forward-looking guidance, focus on promoting changes in industrial development methods and structural adjustments, achieve results in areas such as independent innovation, brand building, characteristic regions, specialized markets, and energy conservation and emissions reduction, and do a good job in preparing the industry's "12th Five-Year Plan" to promote steady and rapid development of the leather industry.

Each office of the association, professional committees, and various special projects also conducted summaries and discussions, and formulated detailed implementation progress schedules. During the conference, there were also training sessions on interpreting hot economic phenomena, traditional cultural education, and modern interpersonal communication laws. Additionally, this meeting made new breakthroughs in the focus of summarizing work, not listing specific work content, but focusing on summarizing and studying the rules and norms of the association's work, promoting the gradual maturity of all aspects of the association's work.

Vice President of the China National Light Industry Council and Honorary President of the China Leather Association, Xu Yong, gave high praise for the organization and arrangement of this working conference, as well as its activity content. He emphasized that training is the greatest benefit, and learning is the joy of life. He quoted Mao Zedong's speech at the Yan'an cadre education mobilization meeting in 1939, which mentioned: "There is a kind of panic in our ranks, it is not an economic panic or a political panic, but a panic of incompetence." "It's like a shop with little stock, once sold out, it becomes empty, and continuing without replenishing will not work." This was used to encourage everyone to continuously enhance their learning, strengthen their skills, improve their service abilities, better serve the industry and enterprises, and strive to achieve new超越in the association's work.
