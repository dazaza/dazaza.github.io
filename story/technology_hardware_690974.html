Quanzhou Huamei Editor's View: Due to differences in individual constitutions, pain and bruising may occur after breast augmentation surgery. The severity varies from person to person. Pain usually subsides within two to three days. Adequate rest is essential after surgery, and normal activities can resume within two to three days. During the first week post-surgery, high arm movements and carrying heavy objects should be avoided. Steam baths and swimming should be avoided for two weeks.

One week after breast augmentation surgery, breast massage can be performed. The method involves pushing the breasts upward, inward, and outward as much as possible, holding the position for ten seconds. This should be done for five minutes on each breast. In the first month, this should be done twice a day (morning and evening), and starting from the second month, once every night for a year.
