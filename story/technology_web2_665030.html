Fully digital control equipment, the main characteristics of this device: 1. Under precise digital control, frequency automatically tracks, ensuring the equipment works in the optimal resonant state. High-frequency quenching equipment is widely used by gear manufacturers.

Quenching technology has found widespread application in modern mechanical manufacturing industries. Important parts in machinery, especially steel components used in automobiles, airplanes, and rockets, almost all undergo quenching treatment. To meet a wide variety of technical requirements for different parts, various quenching processes have been developed. For instance, according to the treated part, there are overall quenching, partial quenching, and surface quenching; according to whether the phase change during heating is complete, there are complete quenching and incomplete quenching (for hypoeutectoid steel, this method is also called subcritical quenching); according to the content of phase change during cooling, there are graded quenching, isothermal quenching, and under-speed quenching, etc. Therefore, the equipment efficiency is high.

2. Protection and limitation functions are more comprehensive, reliable, and rapid, making the equipment more stable. The main components receive quick and reliable protection, reducing maintenance costs.

3. Chinese display is clear and comprehensive, with parameters easy to set.
