Last year, Tianhong Department Store's net profit increased by 18%, and same-store sales grew by 20%. Famous Brands Leather Network

by kecnlv88qx on 2012-03-05 23:13:09

News: On the evening of February 27, the joint-stock company released its annual report for 2011. During the reporting period, the company achieved a total operating revenue of RMB 13.035 billion, an increase of 28.12% year-on-year; net profit attributable to shareholders was RMB 573 million, up 18.33% year-on-year; basic earnings per share were RMB 0.72.

In addition, the company announced the profit distribution plan for 2011. It proposed to use the total share capital of 800 million shares at the end of 2011 as the base, distributing RMB 3.3 (including tax) in cash dividends for every 10 shares held by all shareholders. The total profit distribution would amount to RMB 264 million. After the profit distribution, the remaining undistributed profit of RMB 666 million would be carried over to the next year.

(1) Store Expansion

During the reporting period, the company opened 8 new stores including Yong'an Tianhong (franchise store), Changzhou Liyang Tianhong (which has ceased operations), Dongguan Humen Tianhong, Jiangxi Ganzhou Tianhong, Suzhou Mudu Tianhong, Fujian Quanzhou Tianhong, Loudi Tianhong in Hunan, and Weihai Tianhong in Shandong (franchise store). On January 6, 2012, Beijing Xin'ao Tianhong was newly opened.

While rapidly expanding its store network, the company also actively prepared for future development by signing multiple new projects in cities such as Shenzhen, Xiamen, Chengdu, and Dongguan. While consolidating its market position in advantageous regions, it gradually expanded into emerging areas, developing new markets, and further improving its national retail network.

In terms of real estate, the wholly-owned subsidiary Tianhong Real Estate Co., Ltd. purchased properties from the first to fourth floors of the Lianfa Plaza in Honggutan New District, Nanchang, Jiangxi Province for opening stores. Another wholly-owned subsidiary, Xiamen Junshang Century Investment Co., Ltd., acquired the Huiyan North plot in Siming District, Xiamen, Fujian Province for store construction. The company successfully bid for the land use rights of Shenzhen T107-0022(G) plot for the construction of parking facilities accompanying the headquarters building project.

(2) Optimizing Supply Chain and Breaking Through with High-end Brand Introduction

The company continued to promote direct procurement from origin and the construction of fresh produce bases. By adding new direct procurement categories, adopting the on-site purchasing model, and implementing single-item marketing strategies, the sales and gross profit from direct procurement increased by more than 50% year-on-year. Agreements were signed for 10 fresh produce bases in Shandong, Fujian, and other provinces. The company accelerated the development of private labels, improved the private label development system, and achieved a year-on-year increase of more than 20% in private label sales and gross profit. Unified packaging business developed, achieving unified packaging in multiple stores involving categories such as leather shoes, children's clothing, home goods, and sleepwear, achieving good performance. A nationwide commodity supply network was built, continuously promoting strategic cooperation, developing 39 strategic customers.

Using the development of high-end department stores - Junshang as a breakthrough point, international high-end brands such as CK formal wear, Max&Co, Shu Uemura, Clarins, and domestic high-end brands like Karlton (casual), BONI, Chow Sang Sang, among others, were introduced.

(3) Comprehensive Retail Information System Fully Online

After prior development, the integrated retail management information system (referred to as the R3 system) was first piloted in the Beijing area in May and fully launched across the company in November. The project mainly realized functions such as business-finance integration, full-process supply chain management, operation-procurement integration, and centralized data management for the entire company. The system managed resources such as customers, products, suppliers (brands), locations, and time; assisted in operations, supported operations, led operations, including planning resource utilization, planning actions, process follow-up monitoring, result evaluation and assessment, benchmark management (comparison, longitudinal and lateral comparisons), knowledge accumulation, etc.; continuously improved communication platforms, including contract negotiation approvals, process monitoring, budget execution, promotion preparation, execution, and results, etc. The launch of R3 promoted the optimization of company processes, improved accounting work methods, enhanced the efficiency of information production, and realized effective management and full utilization of various resources.

(4) Emerging Businesses Gradually Entering Development Track

Business operations have gradually entered a regular track. Through product optimization, the overall profitability level of the mall gradually increased; by establishing a customer behavior system, precise marketing was promoted; the online Tianhong sorting center was officially put into use, through the automated inventory allocation, wave planning, RF inbound picking outbound, and refined inventory management of the warehouse management system, effectively improving work efficiency and sorting accuracy; strengthened synergy marketing with physical stores, while expanding the online customer base for stores, enhancing the reputation of online Tianhong.

Brand agency business achieved significant breakthroughs. The newly established partnership brand agency company, Leti, successfully acted as agent for two international brands, one of which, PART TWO, currently has opened 8 counters and formed a cross-regional operating trend. The Commodity Center Agency Department currently acts as agent for 22 domestic brands, having opened 27 counters. The development of brand agency business enriched the company's product resources, beneficial for differentiated operations, and also cultivated new profit growth points.

(5) Expanding Category Management Pilots, Strengthening Product Operation Capabilities

Expanded the scope and scale of category management pilot programs, focusing on three aspects of work: building pilot store frameworks, enhancing marketing, and optimizing inventory. Achieved personnel division based on category structure in pilot stores, adjusting performance evaluation targets; adjusted the organizational structure of the operational site, establishing consistency in key indicators for operations and procurement, promoting the integration of operations and procurement, implemented a trial project for knitted category management, implemented category measures such as product selection, pricing, placement, marketing, etc., achieving a year-on-year increase of more than 20% in sales and profitability for pilot categories.

(6) Improving Service Processes, Enhancing Customer Shopping Experience

Organized the relationship between "emotion curve", "customer satisfaction" and "intimacy, convenience, professionalism" indicator databases, applying the emotion curve separately in key reception service projects, formulating corrective preventive measures for customer satisfaction, researching and updating the intimacy convenience professionalism indicator database, revising mystery shopper inspection forms, etc. Through the application of the "emotion curve" tool, continuously deepened understanding of customers' psychological feelings during the service process, identifying pain points and making targeted improvements, thereby enhancing the customer shopping experience. Customer satisfaction significantly improved in 2011.

(7) Promoting Best Practices, Strengthening Overall Marketing Level

Regarding themes such as online marketing, category marketing, marketing, revenue control, and typical mall activities, collected successful cases from each region and highlights of activities conducted by national competitors, summarized key control points involved in the four stages of promotional activity resource exploration, planning, implementation, and follow-up evaluation, extracted experiences and highlights of such activities, providing effective reference guidance for each region to conduct such activities. Shared excellent relevant cases in a timely manner, saving actual costs for planning and execution.

(8) Promoting Talent Cultivation and Localization, Perfecting Talent Succession Plan

Developed and enhanced leadership capabilities, initiating the "Elite Program" targeting mid-to-high-level management. Following the approach of "benchmarking mall general manager modeling - assessment center evaluation - individual training plan customization - supporting training project implementation - phased training effect review and adjustment," systematically trained nearly a hundred reserve talents within the company; promoted mentorship system, achieving one-on-one coaching between senior executives and reserve talents. The "Elite Program" won the Harvard Business Review Fifth Management Practice Award, the China Enterprise Business School Yearly Forum "Management Innovation" Award, and the HROOT "China Talent Management Award."

Implemented the "Talent Hunt Program," formulated operation standards for mid-to-high-level talent introduction measures, employee internal recommendation management measures, etc., perfected the talent introduction system, realizing the external recruitment of several mall general manager-level talents. Based on the "Two-way Learning Plan," optimized and summarized to form the "External Recruitment Talent Training Guidelines," achieving targeted training effects significantly, breaking through the introduction of high-quality external talents.

As of this reporting period, the company had entered 18 prefecture-level cities in Beijing, Guangdong, Fujian, Jiangxi, Hunan, Jiangsu, Zhejiang, Shandong, and other provinces and municipalities, owning 45 directly-operated "Tianhong" chain department stores with a total business area of 1.2045 million square meters, and 1 directly-operated "Junshang" store with a business area of 37,800 square meters. In addition, the company also manages 4 malls under franchise agreements.

During the reporting period, comparable store revenues grew by 20.01% year-on-year.
