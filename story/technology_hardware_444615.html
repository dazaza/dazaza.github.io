Edison Chen is the director of the post-sex photo scandal [Original]

By Fei Hua

The sex photo scandal is like a wildfire that never burns out, and every time there's an opportunity, it flares up again.

Recently, the internet has once again been stirred by so-called sex photo scandals. One involves Jolin Tsai allegedly exposed in sex photos, which were quickly identified as being digitally altered due to the lack of her iconic tattoo. The second involves intimate photos of Edison Chen with a certain woman, which were soon proven to be taken from the old MTV Channel program "Whatever Things." The third is a list titled "All Photos and Videos of the Edison Chen Sex Scandal," including mainland China's popular actresses Fan Bingbing and Zhao Wei, who are ranked second and third on the list. I can confidently say this list is fake. Objectively speaking, Jolin Tsai has offered a reward of 100 million NT dollars to clear her name regarding any sex photos, so she should be unrelated to the scandal; although Elva Hsiao was rumored to have sex photos circulating, causing a city-wide uproar, her name is not mentioned on the list. As for Fan Bingbing, her agent stated they have no time to deal with such trivial matters, implying that these so-called sex photos are fabricated. Subjectively, I believe Edison Chen does not have the capability to associate with Fan Bingbing and Zhao Wei. Not only do their living environments, lifestyles, and moral standards differ, but Fan Bingbing and Zhao Wei are exceptional individuals, intelligent and striving upwards. It would be unlikely for them to even consider someone like Edison Chen, let alone be tied down by him.

In my eyes, if at the beginning of the sex photo scandal there was some curiosity about it, it was because of the unimaginable possibilities; later, there was some dislike for it due to its toxic nature; then eventually, it became something we got used to because of how common it became. I call this stage of "getting used to it" the "post-sex photo scandal era."

In this post-sex photo scandal era, after Edison Chen fled Hong Kong, the reason why there have been repeated so-called celebrity sex photo leaks, I believe, is because Edison Chen is undoubtedly the "director." This is not me deliberately defaming Edison Chen. Haven't you noticed that while he announced his retirement from the Hong Kong entertainment industry, he also arrogantly claimed, "I will make a comeback, even more powerful, just wait and see!" These words are like seeds planted in the hearts of those who thrive on chaos. Thus, during the April 24 trial of the sex photo scandal, there were claims of more sex photos to be released. Originally, Chen planned to return to Hong Kong in May to testify in the sex photo scandal case and promote his own antique shop, but immediately afterward, the entire collection of photos and videos emerged. It feels like "troops haven't moved yet, but supplies have gone first," creating a big buzz for his "comeback" and "greater power." They produce fake sex photos to fulfill what Edison Chen "directed," essentially playing with Chen, female celebrities, the law, morality, the police, and the public. However, these "strange people" are truly "stupid and naive," mistaking counterfeit money for real, thinking the public is equally unintelligent, which is extremely laughable.

Paul Samuelson said economics is "like fishing in an undeveloped lake, where every cast brings fish, yet many still haven't tasted the fresh and tempting new varieties." The entertainment industry is like a lake, stars are like fish in the lake, and star sex photos are like newly developed species, albeit toxic. But humans are indeed funny creatures. Even things that are highly toxic, like opium, look beautiful, become addictive, and ultimately fatal, yet there are still those willing to sacrifice themselves one after another. Even when things are obviously fake, like "antiques" flooding the market stalls, there are still those who don't mind spending money to bring "garbage home." The sex photo scandal is no different. What is originally garbage must create some sort of commotion; what is originally a tragedy must end in farce.

Whether it is garbage or drama, the sex photo scandal should come to an end. The media should stop sensationalizing every little thing and focus more on our noble moral principles. The public should stop treating sex photos as treasures and instead admire the brilliant constellations above us. Those who create fake photos should also cease their actions, remembering that evil deeds bring evil consequences. Put down your sins and become a Buddha.
