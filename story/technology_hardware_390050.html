QQ Zone Section

QQ Zone is like your little nest. If it's decorated beautifully, it will surely make people linger;

The Paipai shop in QQ is similar to Taobao, where QQ friends can do some small trades while chatting.

However, netizens usually only like to browse QQ Zones and are not interested in others' Paipai shops. If we could move the online store to QQ Zone, wouldn't that be perfect? In fact, Tencent has already thought of this for everyone. Now, with simple settings, you can open a Paipai shop in your QQ Zone. Let's take a look:

Step one: Enter your QQ Zone, click on the "Customize" button at the top menu bar, and then click on the "Module Management" option in the pop-up "Customize" function list box.

Step two: In the subsequent dialog box, check the "My Paipai Shop" optional item, then choose "Large Window" or "Small Window" according to your needs, and click the "Submit" button. This will make the "My Paipai Shop" module appear in your QQ Zone. You can drag its frame to change its position in the space.

Step three: After all settings are completed, click the "Save" button in the "Customize" function list box to let the Paipai shop quietly settle in your QQ Zone.

This way, when friends browse your QQ Zone, they might also get some business through the Paipai shop in the space!

Taobao Section

Beginner's Taobao store tutorial, many friends who want to open an online store are not studying e-commerce, nor do they have much computer technology foundation, and some even have a low level of education, but these can't stop us, can they? Opening an online store and blocking our path to success? The following process is a foolproof Taobao store opening tutorial, follow it step by step and you will definitely learn.

What's the charm of opening an online store?

Nowadays, opening an online store is not just about starting a business, many people use it as a way to expand their capabilities and as a form of entertainment. What is the charm of opening an online store that attracts everyone? Summarized, there are mainly the following points:

1. Zero cost, low risk

Compared to traditional stores, opening an online store does not require renting storefronts, paying taxes or utility bills, it only charges a small fee for listing products and transactions, some are even free. Moreover, you can order goods according to demand, without worrying about overstocking.

2. Flexible operating methods

Not restricted by traditional business hours or locations. Operators can run full-time or part-time, without needing to invest a lot of time in managing the store.

3. Wide consumer base

Because online stores are on the Internet, they face all possible consumers who may see the products, this group can be internet users nationwide, even globally.

How exactly do you open an online store?

Online stores are truly diverse, cosmetics, clothing, digital products... Every project has great development potential and prospects. The key is how to do it, what suits you, this is the most important. Online stores also require skills, here's how to open one step by step.

One, what to sell online?

Like traditional stores, the first step in opening an online store is to consider what to sell. The choice of product should be based on your interests, abilities, conditions, as well as product attributes and consumer needs.

I like watching Korean dramas, and because Korean-style women's clothing has been very popular among Chinese women in recent years, naturally I started doing business in Korean-style women's clothing.

Show off your Taobao store

Two, preparations before opening a store

After choosing what to sell, before opening an online store, you need to choose a website that provides a personal store platform and register as a user. To ensure transaction security, corresponding identity and payment method verifications are also required.

The reason I chose to open a store on Taobao is because it's completely free. To open a store on Taobao, you need to meet three conditions: one, register as a member and pass verification; two, post more than 10 items (including 10); three, for convenient and secure transactions, it is recommended to activate online banking.

1. User registration

Log in to Taobao and click "Free Registration" at the top of the page. In the opened page, enter the username, password, email, etc., and click the "Agree to the following service terms, submit registration information" button. Then, the registered email will receive a confirmation email, open the link inside, confirm, and the user registration is complete.

Security tip: To ensure transaction security, don't set your password too simply, it is recommended to use a combination of "English letters + numbers + symbols".

2. Identity verification

Taobao stipulates that only after passing real-name verification can you sell items and open a store. Therefore, after registering as a user, you also need to undergo corresponding verifications (including real-name verification and Alipay verification). The specific steps are as follows:

Step one: Log in to Taobao, click "My Taobao" at the top of the page. In the opened page, click "Click here" next to the text "Want to sell items? First, undergo Alipay verification."

Step two: In the opened page, it will prompt that the Alipay account has not been activated yet, click "Click here to complete Alipay account activation." In the popped-up page, input your real name, ID type and number, Alipay password, etc., and click the "Save and immediately activate Alipay account" button.

Step three: After successfully activating the Alipay account, go back to the original page, press the F5 key to refresh the page. Click the "Apply for Alipay Personal Real Name Verification" button, read the Alipay verification service terms, then click the "I have read..." button to continue.

Step four: First, fill out your personal information according to the prompts, click "Next"; then, choose ID verification. You can choose "Online Upload" or "Mail" copies of your ID, click "Next"; then, input your bank card information, including the issuing bank, bank card number, province, city, etc., and wait for Alipay to transfer funds within one day.

Friendly reminder: If uploading ID copies online, the image file size should be controlled within 200KB; if it's an IC ID, you also need to provide the back picture.

Step five: One day later, reopen "My Taobao", click the corresponding link in the verification area to open the "Alipay Verification" page, click "Confirm deposit amount" in the "Bank Account Verification" area, then input the amount of funds Alipay injected into your bank account, and click the "Confirm" button.

3. Inventory and taking pictures

A key factor in the successful operation of an online store lies in the sourcing channel. For the same product, different sourcing channels result in different prices.

After passing identity verification, you need to organize the items you already have. To present the items for sale more intuitively to consumers, taking pictures is crucial, and it's best to use appropriate graphic/image processing tools for image format and size conversion, such as Photoshop, ACDSee, etc.

4. Posting items

To open a store online, in addition to meeting the membership certification requirements, you also need to post more than 10 items. Thus, after organizing the product information and images, you need to start posting your first item.

Friendly reminder: If you haven't passed personal real-name verification and Alipay verification, you can still post items, but they will only be posted under "Items in Warehouse," which buyers cannot see. Only after passing the verification can you put them up for sale.

Step one: Log in to Taobao, click "I Want to Sell" at the top of the page. In the opened page, you can choose between "Fixed Price" or "Auction" posting methods. Here, click "Fixed Price."

Friendly reminder: "Fixed Price" has a set price, allowing buyers to purchase immediately; "Auction" starts without a reserve price, letting buyers bid to buy.

Step two: Choose a category, select the appropriate category based on your product. For example, I chose details of women's shoes. Click the "Selected, Continue" button to proceed to the next step.

Step three: Fill in the item information, this step is very important. First, in the "Item Information" area, come up with a good title, click the "Browse" button to upload the item picture, input the item description information, quantity, start time, validity period, etc.; then, in the "Transaction Conditions" area, input the item's selling price, location, shipping cost, payment method, etc.; other information can remain default settings, such as default using Alipay for payment. Finally, click the "Confirm No Errors, Submit" button to post the item.

If the post is successful, a success page will appear below. Click "Here" to view the posted item page, and click "Continue Posting Items" to keep posting items.

Friendly reminder: If no bids have been made by buyers and you want to modify the posted item information, you can edit and modify in "My Taobao - I Am Seller - Items for Sale."

After the items are posted, it's best to regularly update and add new ones to prevent the store from being deleted by the system.

Three, Getting a Free Store

Taobao offers free store openings for certified members. As long as you post more than 10 items, you can own a store with your own independent URL. On this webpage, you can place all your items and decorate it according to your style.

In order to open my store as soon as possible, I originally posted 10 items at once, spending nearly half a day. After finishing, I couldn't wait to open "My Taobao - Free Store Opening." Then, in the opened page, I named the store, calling it "My Shoes My Show"; in the "Store Category," you can choose the item category; in the "Store Introduction," input the store's summary content. Finally, click the "Submit" button, and on the appeared page, it says "Congratulations! Your store has been successfully created..." along with providing the store address.

Four, Store Decoration is Important

After opening the free store, buyers can obtain their own space. Like traditional stores, to operate normally and attract customers, the store needs corresponding "decoration," mainly including store logo design, item categorization, recommended items, store style, etc.

1. Basic Settings

Log in to Taobao, open "My Taobao - I Am Seller - Manage My Store." In the "Store Management" on the left, click "Basic Settings." In the opened page, you can modify the store name, store category, store introduction; main projects need to be manually entered; in the "Logo" area, click the "Browse" button to choose the already designed store logo image; in the "Announcement" area, input the store announcement content, such as "Welcome to our store!" Click the "Preview" button to see the effect.

2. Item Categorization

Categorizing items makes it easier for buyers to search. In the opened "Manage My Store" page, you can click "Item Categories" on the left; then, input the new category name, for example, "Four Treasures of the Study," and input the sorting number (indicating the arrangement position), click the "Confirm" button to add. Click the "Item List" button corresponding to the category, and you can add posted items through keyword searches for categorized management.

3. Recommended Items

The "Recommended Items" feature provided by Taobao allows you to recommend your best 6 items, displaying them prominently in the store. Just open the "Manage My Store" page, click "Recommended Items" on the left; then, you can choose recommended items in the opened page and click the "Recommend" button.

4. Store Style

Different store styles suit different items and give buyers different feelings. Generally, choose a light-colored, comfortable style. I chose the "Green Wild Fairyland" style template, which shows a preview on the right side. Click the "Confirm" button to apply this style.

After decorating the store, a brand-new page appears before you.

Five, Promotion is the Key to Success

The online store is open, items are listed, features are present, but weeks have passed without any transactions, not even buyer comments, which is a common problem faced by many new sellers. At this point, you need to take the initiative.

I once encountered such troubles, so I decided to advertise my store through forum promotion, link exchange, showcase recommendation, and friend promotion.

1. Forum Promotion

The main method of forum promotion is through posting advertisements and utilizing signature lines.

The former can be done on forums of various provinces or cities. If there is a section that allows advertisement postings, you can post advertisements, ensuring the content is detailed, the product images are exquisite, and maintaining regular updates and top placements.

The latter involves changing your forum signature line to include your store's URL, logo, promotional phrases, and store name. Post some beautiful posts so interested friends can visit your store through your signature line.

2. Link Exchange

In the early stages of opening a store, to boost popularity, you can exchange links with popular stores, thus advertising your store for free. For example, Taobao provides up to 35 friendly links. The method to add them is simple:

First, use Taobao's search function to search for all stores, record the names of popular store owners.

Then, log in and download Taobao's buyer-seller communication tool - "Taobao Wangwang," add the names of these popular store owners and propose a link exchange request.

If they agree to exchange, finally, open "My Taobao - I Am Seller - Manage My Store," click "Friendly Links" on the left, then input the store owner's name, and click the "Add" button.

3. Showcase Recommendation

The "Showcase Recommendation" feature provided by Taobao is a special feature for sellers. When buyers choose to search or click "I Want to Buy" based on categories, the recommended showcase items will appear on the search results page. To set up the "Showcase Recommendation" feature, open "My Taobao - I Am Seller - Items for Sale," choose items to recommend to the showcase (items already recommended to the store homepage cannot be showcased again, i.e., those with "Recommended" marks), and click the "Showcase Recommendation" button.

Six, After Selling Items

After selling items, besides receiving corresponding sales reminders, you need to actively contact the buyer, request payment, ship the goods, and handle evaluations or complaints after the transaction is completed.

1. Check Sold Items

If a buyer purchases an item, Taobao will notify the seller through "Taobao Wangwang" or email. Sellers can also log in to Taobao, open "My Taobao - I Am Seller - Sold Items." In the "Contact Buyer" area, click "Leave a Message for Me" to leave a message via "Taobao Wangwang"; if the buyer doesn't use "Taobao Wangwang," you can also note down the buyer's ID and send an internal message.

2. Contact Transaction Details

After both parties communicate, they agree on payment and shipping methods. To ensure the interests of both parties, it is suggested to use Alipay for payment.

3. Payment and Shipping

To prevent situations where goods arrive but payments aren't made, sellers generally adopt a "Payment Received Before Shipping" method.

First, request the buyer to pay, usually through Alipay. After payment, the seller can open "My Taobao - I Am Seller - Sold Items" to check. If the transaction status shows "Buyer Has Paid, Waiting for Seller to Ship," it means Alipay has received the payment, and the seller can now safely ship the goods to the buyer.

Friendly reminder: For sellers using Alipay transactions, open "My Taobao - Alipay Zone - Transaction Management," Guangzhou VIP.com Information Technology Co., Ltd., manage ongoing transactions, such as transaction queries, refund management, etc.

After the buyer receives the goods, they confirm in the transaction status, and finally, Taobao will transfer the funds to the seller's Alipay account. This completes the transaction. Don't forget to stay in touch with the buyer to increase the chances of them revisiting your store and purchasing items.

4. Evaluation and Complaints

After completing the transaction, both buyers and sellers can open "My Taobao" for evaluation. Sellers can open "My Taobao - I Am Seller - Sold Items," click "Evaluate" on the sold items, choose positive, neutral, or negative reviews based on actual circumstances, and input text content.

Friendly reminder: How to become a Taobao seller with relatively high credit ratings?

To become a star-rated seller on Taobao, the credit rating must be at least 4 points. The basis for the seller's credit score is the buyer's evaluation after each successful Alipay transaction. If the evaluation is "Positive Review," it adds one point; "Neutral Review" doesn't add points; "Negative Review" deducts one point. Therefore, to become a star-rated seller, remember to provide honest services. If situations like online transactions without purchase or receipt without payment occur, sellers can open "My Taobao - Credit Management - Report" to file complaints and reports, but evidence such as shipping receipts, buyer sign-off receipts, Wangwang screenshots, etc., need to be collected.

Postscript: Although it is said that opening an online store has zero cost and low risk, examples of failed businesses and "closing shop" are abundant. To ensure the survival of your online store, the most important thing is "honesty." Only honesty can win the hearts of buyers and gain good credit evaluations, leading to a steady stream of wealth!
