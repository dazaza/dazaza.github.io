Jiangsu beat students to death

by qnx666 on 2010-11-30 19:00:33

15-Year-Old Middle School Student, Wang Zhenyu, after witnessing his head teacher being beaten, went to mediate but was unfortunately killed by three classmates surnamed Shen after evening study session. An eyewitness claimed that during the fight, the school principal and three teachers were in the school yard watching but did not step in to stop it. The deceased's mother stated that after the incident involving her child, the school gave no response whatsoever. Local government reported that the suspects have been detained. It is understood that one of the students surnamed Shen is commonly referred to as "Mayor Shen" due to his father holding an official position in Beijing.

The body of the deceased had multiple bruises.

"I heard from several students that both school teachers and students witnessed the beating, yet no one intervened, and the child was ultimately killed at the school gate," said the deceased’s mother.

On November 21st, a heatedly discussed post online emerged titled “Jiangsu Province Xinyi City Qipan Junior High School Grade 3 student Wang Zhenyu was beaten to death.” The post detailed how on the evening of November 17th, the student witnessed three fellow students assaulting his head teacher and went to intervene, only to be fatally beaten by his classmates. The post also included photos showing the extensive bruising on the student's body after the attack.

Netizens’ Post: Teachers Ignored as Students Were Beaten?

According to the online post, the deceased was Wang Zhenyu, a Grade 3 student at Xinyi City Qipan Middle School, known for his excellent academic performance and character. "Around 7 p.m. on the 17th, just after evening study ended and he was about to go home, Wang Zhenyu was cornered at the school gate by three classmates and beaten to death on the spot. The three students then fled. An eyewitness stated that during the fight, Principal Xu of Qipan Middle School and three teachers were in the schoolyard watching but did not move to stop it, allowing the fight to continue unchecked. Originally, there might have been a chance for the child to survive, but these teachers turned a blind eye. Do principals and teachers deserve the respect society gives them? Where has your conscience gone?"

"More than 20 minutes later, a kind-hearted teacher noticed the student was in bad shape and called 120 emergency services. The family immediately rushed to the scene. After asking around, the school authorities provided no answers. Where is justice? Should the child just be gone like this...”

The post also left contact information for the child’s family, sparking heated discussions online. Netizens expressed shock: How could such a thing happen? Isn't the school doing anything? What kind of students dare to kill openly?

Deceased’s Mother: No Response from the School After Incident
