In my youth, that sentimental story

This matter has been buried in my heart for a long time. For many years, apart from guilt and hatred, I feel more embarrassed and feel that I was once so insignificant as a man...

At that time, I was in my sophomore year. Not long after enrollment, Hua, who was in the same class as me, had a good feeling towards me. Maybe it was because I was relatively handsome, or maybe it was because I liked literature and often published articles in the community. Hua's affection towards me was completely unrestrained. She invited me to watch movies and found ways to talk to me. Almost all the classmates could sense it.

Maybe it's a common problem among all young literary enthusiasts, I pursued that kind of love at first sight. And Hua, she was just too ordinary in appearance, which couldn't arouse my interest at all. The reason why I didn't directly reject her and kept this ambiguous relationship with her was that there was still a bit of hypocrisy in my subconscious. I enjoyed the feeling of being pursued and admired.

When I entered my sophomore year, several buddies in the dormitory already had girlfriends. Every weekend, I was often left alone, bored. During the nightly bed meetings, they talked about the various tenderness and kindness of their girlfriends, making me feel itchy inside. The leader's girlfriend could make cold dishes, even though they were ordinary cold dishes, but the leader ate them with relish and endless aftertaste, saying they were much better than our cafeteria food. And the third brother's girlfriend, using the money saved from frugal living, bought him a windbreaker. The third brother said: "The weather in the north is windy and big, the girl's family is not rich either, she would rather not wear one herself but bought me a windbreaker, what else could I ask for?" Listening to their conversations, I couldn't interject, I could only listen. At this moment, the fifth brother spoke up: "What you guys said are not real. In high school, my girlfriend let me kiss her, and we've already done that." Everyone teased: "Which one? What does that mean?" The fifth brother loudly said: "We went to bed, are you satisfied now? But then the fifth brother cunningly laughed, that's impossible. How could I let my beloved take the risk! It turned out to be a false alarm.

At that moment, I also wanted to find a girlfriend for mutual comfort! Especially when I noticed that my parents' feelings were having problems, I was filled with distress.

Time continued to pass slowly, however, one day, a piece of news broke out in the college: A female student in her third year of the foreign language department got unexpectedly pregnant, and her boyfriend had fallen in love with someone else. The female student was so ashamed and angry that she jumped off the dormitory building!

As everyone was talking about the incident before going to sleep, some said that the boy was less than human, with no sense of responsibility at all; others said that the girl was too foolish, not knowing how to protect herself. The fifth brother sat up on his bed: "Today, I will give you all something. There are quite a few people in our dormitory who have girlfriends, remember, no matter when, you must pay attention to the other party's safety." We thought he was going to give us something, and everyone got up from their beds waiting, but it turned out to be condoms! "If the boys could take more responsibility, such things wouldn't happen like in the foreign language department. In the future, if any of us get into trouble in this aspect, I will never forgive him..." Originally, the brothers wanted to mock the fifth brother for the condoms, but after hearing his words, they all fell silent. The condom in their hands suddenly felt heavy.

Afterwards, the second brother also had a girlfriend, and I became a loner. Sometimes, I couldn't even find someone to talk to. That day was my birthday, and I didn't tell anyone. I went to class as usual. Suddenly, Hua stopped me: "Happy Birthday!" and handed me a gift, a beautiful diary book. I only remembered mentioning my birthday casually during our chats, but Hua remembered it, and a sense of moving overwhelmed me.

Perhaps, Hua's care came just in time, perhaps, my lonely feelings wanted to find a harbor to anchor. From then on, I seemed to change my view of Hua. Although she wasn't pretty, she was considerate. Even though she couldn't write poetic articles, she could sing songs that made people drunk. More importantly, Hua actively pursued me. I once read a sentence in a book: "If a girl takes the initiative, a boy can save a lot of trouble." I admit, a large part of the reason I accepted Hua was due to my emptiness. I was a weak hunter.

At that time, there was a "Couple Video Hall" not far from the school. Students often went there, and so did my roommate and I. The video hall was divided into individual "private rooms". Because the soundproofing effect was not very good, adjacent private rooms could hear each other's voices. Most of the time, the video hall played romantic films, sometimes some adult films. Such films would only be shown late at night when it was quiet and someone requested it. And at that time, the sounds of a couple in the neighboring private room always seemed to match the pictures, making people restless all over.

On weekends, when Hua and I passed by the video hall, looking at Hua's face full of infatuation, I suddenly wanted to watch a movie with her to test her feelings for me. Moreover, the condoms given by the fifth brother always didn't have a chance to be used. When I hesitantly asked Hua if she wanted to watch a movie, I didn't expect her to agree without hesitation, because almost all classmates thought that as long as you went there, the relationship must be special.

Next, we went to the video hall together. While watching the movie, I couldn't forget the importance of safety no matter what. Moreover, in my mind, even if we broke up in the future, there would be no aftermath, and no too much responsibility. On the way, she appeared bashful and cute, which further stimulated a certain desire in me. In the video hall, when the screen showed the scene of a naked couple embracing, we also couldn't control ourselves... Afterward, I wanted to throw away the condom, but she didn't allow it. She said it was a witness to her first time, and she was finally with the boy she admired.

However, real life is full of variables. At a gathering, I met a girl named Xin from another department. We shared similar interests, and I soon became unable to extricate myself. In front of Xin, I no longer had that primitive desire, but only respect for each other and the pursuit of literature...

But, what about her? We had done so many intimate things between us, how could Hua accept this reality all at once? But I truly loved Xin, that feeling was something I never experienced when I was with her. Young as I was, I felt the difficulty of choosing for the first time.

That night, when I brought her out and hesitatingly explained the situation, she first stood there in shock, then stared straight into my eyes: "Since you don't love me, why did you do that with me?" Her voice wasn't loud, but each word hit my heart like a heavy hammer. I didn't dare to meet her gaze, only feeling that I was a faithless person, unworthy of her sincere feelings.

Hua quickly became thinner. Classmates only knew that we had broken up, but they wouldn't think that Hua and I had already had that kind of relationship. Since we were in the same class, it was impossible not to see each other. Hua ignored me every time she saw me, and I never dared to look at her. But I deeply felt that under her seemingly strong exterior, there was an almost desperate sadness... Because of temporary loneliness, I hurt an innocent person, and I used the condom given by the fifth brother! I couldn't help but strongly blame myself deep inside. Thinking about how Hua once preserved our used condom so infatuatedly, there was an indescribable taste in my heart...

And my relationship with Xin, after experiencing a passionate romance, also came to a standstill as university life ended. Now, although it has been many years since graduation, I still can't forget her. She may not be the main theme of my life, but she is an indispensable interlude in my life. Even after I got married, had children, and became a real man, I still couldn't let go of that experience, harboring a deep hatred for my inner self and wanting to make up for it.

Originally, condoms represented sincerity in love, but I, thinking back to those despicable days when I used it as a tool to vent my troubles, outwardly claiming to protect the other party, under the guise of "love", actually had ulterior motives, treating sexual issues as a game, an irresponsible act. Hua's forgiveness towards me still makes me sigh and feel ashamed today. She and my love are unrelated, but she always tugs at my conscience.

In the youthful years, that sentimental condom, because of temporary loneliness, it has always been a knot in my heart, letting me understand how to love, cherish, care for, protect, and treasure.
