Related thematic articles: Untitled Document Zhao Rui felt his heart beating violently. He began to search around in this Immortal Devil Graveyard, hoping to find answers within the graveyard and even more so, a way back to the surface. If by chance he could find some treasures, like Qing Yao, that would be best of all, achieving three goals with one action. The Immortal Devil Graveyard was like a huge maze, with giant immortal tombs scattered throughout, like enormous icebergs blocking the way. Although the ancient immortals and devils were sealed in their tombs and had been dead for millions of years, their remaining majesty still commanded respect. Zhao Rui even felt that the gazes of these immortals and devils seemed to penetrate the limits of time and space, still watching him, making him feel immense pressure. The monotonous sound of footsteps echoed in the silent graveyard. The surrounding temperature became lower and lower, and the cold emanating from the immortal tombs grew stronger. Zhao Rui felt as if he was walking through a large freezer. Ice formed thickly on his clothes, his hands were frozen stiff, and his steps became heavier and heavier, like they were filled with lead. If he hadn't grown up in such a harsh environment, developing extraordinary perseverance, he might have already collapsed from exhaustion. He didn’t know how long he had been searching in the Immortal Devil Graveyard when he suddenly stumbled over something, almost falling down. Looking down, he found that it was a small jade tablet shaped like a long strip. Of course, there wouldn't be anything ordinary in the Immortal Devil Graveyard. This might even be a treasure that could help him get out. Zhao Rui's spirit lifted. He quickly picked up the jade tablet and examined it carefully. The tablet wasn't very big, about the size of a hand, with a faint flow of milky white light on its surface. The edges were engraved with intricate cloud patterns, and in the middle were four lines of ancient inscriptions. "On the night of the full moon" "The immortal tomb opens" "With this immortal token" "One can enter" "So this is the token to enter the graveyard. I wonder who dropped it." Zhao Rui gave a bitter laugh, slightly disappointed. "But now that I'm already inside the graveyard, my biggest problem is getting out, not getting in." Although this token didn't improve his situation, Zhao Rui still put it in his pocket. If he could really get out, he could use this token to come back in the future. After regaining his energy, he continued walking forward until he finally reached the center of the graveyard. In the exact center of the graveyard stood a peculiar immortal magic array, with misty immortal qi floating around and inscriptions drifting in the air. On the focal point of this array sat a ten-meter-high stone stele. When Zhao Rui approached to take a closer look, he discovered that the ancient characters on the stele actually described the origin of this graveyard: It turned out that during ancient times, when gods, demons, and immortals ruled the world, for some unknown reason, an unprecedented war broke out between the eastern and western gods and demons, which was both grand in scale and extremely brutal. Their terrifying power caused great disasters worldwide—mountains collapsed, the earth cracked, volcanoes erupted, and tsunamis raged. This was a war without winners. Both sides suffered heavy losses. Even though the eastern army of immortals and demons eventually gained the upper hand, countless powerful gods, demons, and immortals perished in battle. This war was so brutal that the eastern immortals and demons suffered heavy losses, while the western gods and demons lost many soldiers, leading to widespread suffering. As a result, the influence of the eastern and western gods and demons on the world gradually weakened. The surviving eastern immortals and demons built a graveyard for those who died in battle, sealing their remains with the ninefold ice from the hellish icy abyss, then sinking the entire graveyard thousands of meters below the sea. To prevent people from disturbing the rest of the deceased, the immortals and demons set up numerous prohibitions around the graveyard and laid down a powerful immortal magic array. Without a specially made token, even immortals and demons couldn't locate the graveyard, let alone enter it. After completing everything, the builders of the Immortal Devil Graveyard left quietly through the reserved immortal magic array at the center of the graveyard. "Whew...it's hard to believe," Zhao Rui exhaled heavily after reading the text on the stele. He felt that his doubts not only hadn't decreased but had increased instead. "What reason could have led to the eastern and western gods and demons being dragged into a mutually destructive war? Besides, the stele says that without an entrance token, even immortals and demons cannot enter the graveyard. So how did I manage to get in initially?" After speculating wildly for a while, he thought the reason he managed to accidentally enter the graveyard was probably due to the passage of time. Time's flow could cause celestial beings to decline, naturally causing subtle changes in these immortal magic prohibitions. As for why the war among immortals and demons broke out in ancient times, that was beyond what he could guess. Zhao Rui felt that his education of over a decade had been completely overturned. If he weren't currently in the Immortal Devil Graveyard, personally experiencing these incredible events, he would never have believed a single word on the stele! However, he now sincerely hoped that the content recorded on the stele was entirely accurate, because only in that case could he leave this Immortal Devil Graveyard. Stepping into the immortal magic array, Zhao Rui looked wistfully at the dense forest of Immortal Devil graves, feeling a bit regretful. These graves contained Immortal Devil treasures that could drive countless cultivators mad with desire. Forget the top-tier immortal treasures like Laozi’s Wind-Fire Fan and Golden Bell, even the treasures of lower-level immortals and demons could move mountains and seas, change the heavens and the earth if taken out. Because anyone buried here was a powerful ancient immortal or demon; the levels of strength were only relatively speaking. Unfortunately, he couldn't obtain any of them. Those immortal-reinforced graves were incredibly sturdy. No matter what method he used, he couldn't inflict even the slightest damage. Earlier, while wandering through the graveyard, he had seen the grave of an unnamed minor demon, probably the lowest level grave in the graveyard. Even the ninefold mystic ice used to build the tomb had been eroded by time, forming a deep crack. Despite this, no matter what he tried, he couldn't scrape off even a speck of ice from the tomb. "Entering a treasure mountain yet leaving empty-handed..." Zhao Rui clicked his tongue, feeling quite regretful. But soon he cheered up again because he had picked up an entrance token. With this treasure, as long as he survived and got out, he would naturally have the opportunity to return in the future. By then, he might have already found a way to open these Immortal Devil graves. Placing his hand on the stele, he closed his eyes and waited quietly for the immortal magic array to teleport him out. However, after waiting for a long time, the array showed no signs of movement, and he was still stuck in the Immortal Devil Graveyard. "Hmm? What's going on? Why am I still here?" Zhao Rui looked around bewilderedly, a wave of extreme disappointment rising from his heart and spreading throughout his body. He didn't want to stay another minute in the Immortal Devil Graveyard, even though countless immortal treasures were frozen inside. He was cold, tired, and almost at his physical limit. If he didn't leave soon, he would die here, and no matter how many treasures there were, they would be useless if he couldn't take them with him. "Damn! Why isn't this immortal magic array working? Could it also be due to the passage of too much time?" Zhao Rui angrily pondered, hoping to find a way to fix it. After thinking for a while, he suddenly realized that this immortal magic array might need special power to activate. Naturally, gods, demons, and immortals could easily do it, but he was just an ordinary person, possessing no power at all. Activating the array was obviously impossible for him! Zhao Rui angrily punched the stele, blood flowing from his hand. Surviving the shipwreck, entering the mysterious Immortal Devil Graveyard, and discovering this shocking secret could be considered incredibly lucky! However, his good luck ended here. He couldn't leave the graveyard and would ultimately die trapped here, burying all the secrets with him. It was like coming full circle and returning to the starting point, unable to escape this calamity. "No, I don't want to keep company with these ancient immortals and demons." Zhao Rui clenched his teeth, took a deep breath, and forced himself to calm down, seeking other ways to escape. According to his character, he would never give up until the last second. Lifting his head, he stared expressionlessly at an Immortal Devil tomb opposite him, towering like an iceberg. Inside this ancient tomb was a colossal, mighty ancient demon. The ancient demon had an indigo face, two large curved horns on his head, and a gaping mouth with four sharp fangs. His giant eyes emitted red light. His muscles were bulging, his back covered with mysterious tattoos, and a pair of black, massive wings extended from his back. In his left hand, he held a magical axe, and in his right hand, a giant shield. His hands were raised high, gazing at the sky, his face filled with arrogance and unwillingness. Even though he had been dead for millions of years, the overwhelming dignity and ferocity emanating from him were palpable! Zhao Rui suddenly felt that the look of reluctance on the ancient demon's face was very similar to his current mood. He walked to the tomb and carefully read the inscription on the tombstone. This ancient demon was named Ba Huang Mang Long, an ancient great demon born during the chaotic primeval era. Absorbing the most malevolent and evil qi of heaven and earth, he cultivated into his form, becoming the leader of all demons, commanding myriad demons and intimidating countless monsters. He transcended the three realms and was outside the six paths of reincarnation. He independently created the shocking cultivation technique called the Ba Huang Lu Xian Jue, capable of slaying immortals and annihilating deities, unmatched in ferocity. His fearsome reputation struck terror into the hearts of the immortals and deities of the three realms and the sentient beings of the six paths! During the war between the eastern and western gods and demons, Ba Huang Mang Long displayed astonishing strength, slaughtering countless western gods and demons with his magical axe. However, due to his overwhelming power and excessive arrogance, he fell into an ambush by the western gods and demons, was surrounded and attacked, and ultimately died fighting fiercely. "What a super tough guy!" Zhao Rui respectfully touched the surface of the tomb, thinking, "If I had even a fraction of his power, leaving this Immortal Devil Graveyard would be effortless." As his hand touched the tomb, the blood from his hand smeared onto the icy layer. Almost instantly, the blood transformed into thousands of fine threads, penetrating deep into the tomb as if strongly attracted by something. Immediately after, the mysterious tattoos on Ba Huang Mang Long's back suddenly lit up, emitting dazzling blue-green light, extraordinarily brilliant! "What's happening?" Zhao Rui squinted in surprise, looking at the tattoos, only to realize that they were composed of multiple unique patterns and texts combined together! "Ba... Huang... Lu... Xian... Jue..." he softly read aloud, his heart shaking, "Could this be the shocking technique mentioned on the stele, uniquely created by the ancient great demon Ba Huang Mang Long?" While he was guessing, the blue-green light emitted by the mysterious tattoo suddenly shot out from the tomb, forming a massive light diagram in the air, constantly changing and dazzling to the eyes. Before Zhao Rui could figure out what was happening, the blue-green light diagram exploded suddenly, breaking into fifteen smaller blue-green light diagrams that directly shot into his pupils! Zhao Rui felt a thunderous boom in his brain, his vision filled with blue-green light. He could see nothing but those fifteen mysterious light diagrams! After a while, when the blue-green light in his vision completely dissipated and his sight fully recovered, he realized that these fifteen blue-green light diagrams were deeply imprinted in his mind as if carved with a knife. At this moment, he finally confirmed that these fifteen blue-green light diagrams were precisely the shocking technique cultivated by the ancient demon Ba Huang Mang Long - the Ba Huang Lu Xian Jue!
