The jubilation of common people and the high-intelligence swindle shaking hands in reconciliation, this is my feeling after watching "Double Deceit". It has a plot that makes you laugh, feel emotional, and is worth savoring. From the film, the simple folk customs and the domineering town mayor of the Fengkou Town, I seem to see the true portrayal of the life of the most ordinary Chinese citizens. Everyone in the town harbors the dream of digging for gold, diligently living their lives. And this seemingly unattainable and absurd gold dream makes every character in the movie go all out. Who would have thought, all these efforts were later pocketed by the team of scammers led by Gui Lian, Shi Fo, Jiao Zi, Chen Shaoqing, and Lin Xiaodong.

Movies with hot openings, like "Saving Private Ryan", movies with cold openings, like "Forrest Gump", but "Double Deceit" has an opening that seems both hot and not cold, which gives me a bit of surprise. Let's talk about the simple townspeople of Fengkou Town, a seemingly absurd and incomprehensible case, a pair of men and women accusing each other of impropriety, directly sentenced to death by Wang Dashi. Later, when the mayor leads the townspeople to dig for gold, all sorts of desperation and conspiracies are calculated, yet they are caught by Lin Xiaodong. The townspeople still maintain a high level of jubilation and joy. This suddenly reminds one of China's current situation, making people feel sorrow. Perhaps successful comedies are like this, making people laugh until they can't straighten up, and also making people cry while laughing.
