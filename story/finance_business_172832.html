Hardware enterprises need to be down-to-earth and move forward in a balanced economic situation.

by baoxue552 on 2009-07-08 16:27:07

The Hardware Information Network (http://www.wujinxinxi.com/) broadcast a series of reports stating that hardware enterprises need to be down-to-earth and move forward in a balanced economic situation.

Eliminate outdated production capacity, accelerate mergers and reorganizations

The once-in-a-century global financial crisis has caused a sharp contraction in China's external demand, presenting a major challenge to the steady and relatively fast growth of China's economy. However, it also brings a rare opportunity for China to further implement the scientific concept of development, change its mode of economic growth, and promote the sustainable development of the economy and society. Hardware enterprises must, while fully ensuring growth and expanding domestic demand, seize the opportunity, attach great importance to eliminating backward production capacity, and persistently promote energy conservation and emissions reduction work, accelerating the pace of enterprise mergers and reorganizations.

Adjust industrial structure, promote economic development

Premier Wen Jiabao pointed out during his inspection that to deal with the financial crisis, structural adjustments must be promoted. Only when the economy grows does structural adjustment have momentum and support; only by adjusting the structure can the economy develop steadily, healthily, and sustainably. From this, we can see that economic growth and industrial structure are interrelated and mutually dependent. Therefore, if enterprises want to develop healthily and steadily, they must actively adjust their industrial structures to provide sufficient momentum and support for economic growth.

Rely on scientific and technological strength, develop new energy

Premier Wen Jiabao emphasized that currently, particular attention must be paid to relying on science and technology to develop new energy, new materials, biopharmaceuticals, environmental protection, energy saving and other emerging industries, cultivating new economic growth points. China's renewable energy resources are very abundant, so for China's economic development to break free from the constraints of insufficient energy supply, hardware enterprises must increase their research and development efforts in science and technology, make strategic deployments for new energy development, optimize the energy structure, and develop emerging energy sources.
