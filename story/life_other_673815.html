The Goodness That Has Passed

That online submission

The goodness that has passed, when can it return!

That night, during Chinese self-study, the teacher asked us to memorize classical Chinese text. I was just solving a Rubik's Cube (at that time, I was solving a Rubik's Cube, not feeling helpless). You asked me from behind to recite the text, and I said I would do it after finishing the cube. You gave me a 5-minute limit, and I agreed. In the end, I started reciting before 3 minutes were up (even though I had almost memorized it, I still tried my best to recite it!). That night, I was really happy!

Such happiness, when can it return? Can you tell me?

That morning, it drizzled slightly, but it was still very muggy! Between the third and fourth periods, I came into the classroom from outside. You probably saw that I was only wearing a sports tank top with my arms exposed, and they were all wet from the rain. You asked if I didn't bring an umbrella, and I confirmed it. Soon after, you wrote me a note asking me to bring an umbrella in the afternoon, and I was so moved, so moved! Because no one outside of my parents had ever treated me like that! However, I didn't bring an umbrella. The reason that my home is close to the school isn't an excuse; it's just that my younger sister had just started studying, and my parents hadn't realized that we needed another umbrella at home! I couldn't help but leave the umbrella for my sister! But now, there are two more umbrellas at home, yet I still don't use them. For whom am I supposed to use an umbrella? I'd rather be drenched by the heavy rain!

Your concern like that, when can it return? Can you tell me?

It was also a rainy day, but it was in the afternoon. The rain was light, but I instinctively wanted to "borrow" an umbrella. When I dried your umbrella and returned to school with my own umbrella, a certain idiotic guy wanted to go to the opposite teaching building to find someone. It was so close, yet he still insisted on staying under my umbrella, asking me to escort him, and even waiting for him to finish talking before returning together!!! At that moment, I was so angry... Coincidentally, you "rushed" from the dormitory building to the classroom in the rain that wasn't too big or too small. Actually, I knew it wasn't a coincidence because I had said I wanted to eat quickly and see you earlier in the classroom. And when you saw me arrive at school from the dormitory, you ran to the classroom without hesitation!!! At that moment, I almost cried out of emotion! But since I was holding an umbrella, I still shed tears, and you must have known that those weren't raindrops. I kept the emotion in my heart!

Your disregard for everything for me, when can it return? Can you tell me?

There's also a "trivial" "small" matter: that afternoon, when you asked me to put your black plastic bag in the classroom, I was so happy! You finally "acknowledged" me in front of others!

Regarding love, this short period of happy memories I have has already gone away! But I've been looking forward to the day it can return, it can return! But it's not up to me, it's in your hands, what you say counts!

You will definitely tell me that all of this can return, definitely, definitely...
