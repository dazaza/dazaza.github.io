Feelings after playing the game "Journey to the West 2"

by mirui3701 on 2010-03-30 21:16:10

Hey, old friends of DH2, I have been playing DH for three years... Nowadays, the level drops are not as good as before... So I put all the happiness and regrets from the past together to share with myself #1 (welcome Xiao Ye and Jie, the driver who has committed more than 130 violations in half a year without paying fines, please don't block the road or queue blindly #1 especially like me 1.2.3.....) 1. When I first started playing, I didn't know about buying regular cards, so when I ran out of regular time, I would apply again and start over, sometimes crying over it. 2. While playing, I always spent money, reliving the classic movie "DHXY", not afraid of crying. I once spent a whole night picking up money and medicine in Fish Village, feeling happy after getting 100,000 coins. There were also village chief tasks, catching flying fish, but not frogs because they were too hard to catch and the task was too far away. 3. We used to level up together in the tower, but for some reason, everyone else leveled up while I didn't, which made me very frustrated... Later I found out that others had partners while I didn't, so I wasted a whole night practicing alone... 4. There's one thing I regret the most... If I could have another chance, I would never make the same mistake again... A buddy challenged me to train a female character, but I felt too tired and ended up training a demon instead, directly leading to his killing incident (the demon character couldn't be trained well and became useless). 5. At that time, I trained a demon character... It was really tough... When I reached level 40, no one wanted to bring me along, so I practiced dual-sea by myself 1.2.3... I almost died of exhaustion... There was also a funny thing abroad, which was about hunting... We five people took turns forming teams, but none of us wanted to hunt... Later I found out... (especially despising one immortal, at this point, the relationship between immortals, demons, and humans was quite relaxed... It was just about whether to bring someone and whether to charge money). 6. When I reached level 60, I hunted big ghosts... Although I opened my mouth (made progress), I found a small problem... Babies at that time didn't have much growth rate or anything like that, as long as they were planted well... Everyone carried a Turtle Chancellor, and I also carried an anti-sleep one... Looking back now, it was all garbage... We carried them all the way to level 90... I regret dying... Why didn't I look later at Ye Nv Zhu? 7. When I reached level 70, I did the earth task... By sheer luck, I swear on the lamp, it was pure luck... I got a monkey girl with 168 ** growth 1.173, and at level 100, her magic power was 11730... How's that? There's an even scarier thing... To resist hundreds, I spent 135 yuan buying regular cards, just to resist hundreds... Let me tell you (secretly)... A 150-point card was only 1.3 million... 8. After resisting hundreds, I did the earth task every day (at this time, resisting hundreds was very prestigious, when no one shouted for iron immortals and iron demons outside, many people went crazy running around)... 9. There's still one thing I don't understand to this day... A friend said his divine turtle washed 100 times and got physical resistance... Is that true? Kneeling for the answer!!! 10. Now passing levels 11-14 doesn't cost much money... Back then, passing levels 9 and 10 cost me 5 million... Do you know how much 5 million is? Let me tell you = 45 RMB!!! Friends who play little games or don't play online games, talk about your real experiences and feelings in the games you play... Don't forget to leave comments hhhh!!!
