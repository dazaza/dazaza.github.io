Female worker died after falling into river following a quarrel with her lesbian girlfriend

by zzf000zxyr6 on 2011-07-27 14:50:26

In the early hours of August 11, a female corpse floated under the Da Zhong Bridge (for more details, see Express News B3 on August 12). As the identity of the woman was confirmed, a tragic lesbian love story came to light. Two young girls from out of town met, got to know each other, and fell in love while working in Nanjing. After maintaining their relationship for a period of time, inevitable conflicts arose. The result: one girl drowned after falling into the river, the other ran away from home, leaving behind two grieving, broken families.

Flower-like Girl Drowns in River

Ru Yan, a 20-something girl from Anhui province. Like most rural girls, she had been working alone in Nanjing for many years with the dream of integrating into the big city. But everything came to an abrupt end on August 10.

On the evening of August 10, Ru Meng, who was in Anhui, received a call from her younger sister Ru Yan's roommate and colleague Xiao Mei, "She said Ru Yan had some issues, it seemed unusual that she hadn't returned to the dormitory. She asked us to come to Nanjing."

What exactly happened? Unable to reach her sister by phone, Ru Meng had a premonition of something ominous. Xiao Mei mentioned a detail: Ru Yan had left after having an argument with her same-sex lover Qi Qi, and it seemed she had even been hit by the other party. Ru Meng wasn’t particularly clear about her sister’s feelings, but she vaguely knew that her sister did have a same-sex lover.

The next day, Ru Meng and her family rushed to Nanjing but still had no news of Ru Yan. On August 12, the family saw a message in the newspaper, "A female corpse found under Da Zhong Bridge." "The news said the woman had long hair, but my sister has short hair." Nevertheless, the family went to the police station to confirm.

The ominous premonition became reality.

Why did Ru Yan fall into the river? This mystery seems like only Qi Qi can solve it.

Lover Unable to Bear Pressure Leaves Home at Midnight

Yesterday morning, Ru Yan's father, uncle, and sister went to Qi Qi's house. Qi Qi wasn't at home. Her father, Mr. Wu, said that Qi Qi left home at around 1 a.m. yesterday, and he was already exhausted from searching for his daughter. However, Ru Yan's family was very suspicious of this explanation, "Why leave now? Or are they deliberately hiding the child? We just want to ask Qi Qi. After all, she was the last person Ru Yan saw before she died."

Mr. Wu told reporters that the previous day, his daughter Qi Qi went to the police station to cooperate with the investigation. It was during this process that he fully understood the same-sex romance between his daughter and Ru Yan. This made him very angry. When he returned home, he gave his daughter a good scolding. Qi Qi, being a stubborn girl, slammed the door and left.

From Friendship to Romance

Mr. Wu said that regarding the romance between his daughter Qi Qi and Ru Yan, even now he only knows the general outline.

Qi Qi is from Yangzhou and is not yet 20 years old. Last year, while working at a restaurant on Zhujiang Road, she met Ru Yan. The two girls shared common interests, got along well, and soon became close sisters who could talk about anything. Since Qi Qi's entire family was in Nanjing, she invited Ru Yan to visit her home.

In March this year, Ru Yan moved into the Wu family household and lived in the same room as Qi Qi. Later, the two referred to each other as sworn sisters, and Ru Yan even wanted to recognize Mr. Wu as her godfather.

More than a month later, the relationship between the two took a sharp turn for the worse. Mr. Wu noticed that they occasionally quarreled. Once, when he returned home, he found Ru Yan standing outside the door, unable to go in. His daughter Qi Qi and another girl were locked inside a small room in the house.

Mr. Wu called Ru Yan inside and asked his daughter to open the door. His daughter refused. Insisting, Qi Qi eventually made Ru Yan move out of the Wu family home. Thereafter, whenever Ru Yan came to look for Qi Qi, Qi Qi always avoided meeting her. Out of curiosity, Mr. Wu questioned his daughter about what had happened between them, "She wouldn't tell me, so I didn't ask anymore."

Around August 5, Ru Yan came to the Wu family home and took away Qi Qi's diary. Qi Qi was very upset when she returned. It wasn't until after the incident occurred that Mr. Wu saw several text messages sent by Ru Yan on his daughter's phone and finally understood. The two girls had fallen in love. One of the messages read, "If I return your diary, can we continue to be good?"

When making a statement at the police station, Qi Qi mentioned that on the afternoon of the day the incident occurred, the two had met. Ru Yan knelt down in front of her and begged her not to leave. However, such entreaties only made Qi Qi feel awkward, so she raised her hand and slapped Ru Yan twice.

That night, Ru Yan drowned in the river. Currently, Ru Yan's family is waiting in Nanjing for the results of the police investigation and also hoping to see the missing Qi Qi appear to uncover the truth.
