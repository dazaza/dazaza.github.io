1. Seeing kitchen or toilet first upon entering is a sign of bad luck

All houses should have the living room as the first sight upon entering. Modern architectural design sometimes considers space configuration, often leading to seeing the kitchen, dining area, or bathroom first upon entering. This is a major taboo for homes and doesn't make sense. Living in such a house will surely lead to a decline in family fortune.

2. Bedroom door facing the entrance leads to excessive desires

The bedroom door should not directly face the main door, as it can easily tempt residents to indulge in inappropriate desires.

3. Living room at the center of the house is very auspicious

In general residential houses, if the living room or living hall is set right in the middle of the entire house, this is a very auspicious sign that can make the family fortune flourish.

4. Irregular rooms unsuitable for kitchens

Irregular rooms used as kitchens can affect family health; irregular rooms are only suitable for use as storage.

5. Beams over head affect emotions and health

Beams should be avoided over beds, desks, and dining tables. If unavoidable, a ceiling design should be implemented to cover them, otherwise it could affect the occupant's mood and health, and also hinder career opportunities.

6. Irregular rooms unsuitable for bedrooms

Irregular rooms should not be used as the main bedroom for couples, as it may lead to long-term infertility after marriage.

7. Mirrors beside bed cause sleep problems

Apart from the bed not facing a large mirror, having large dressing mirrors on either side of the bed can cause restless sleep, insomnia, and nightmares.

8. Bedroom decoration should not be too extravagant

The bedroom color scheme should be elegant and warm, avoiding overly bright colors or being excessively decorated with luxurious items, especially shiny ornaments which are particularly unsuitable.

9. Bathroom facing the bed can cause serious illness

In the master bedroom, apart from the bed not directly facing the bathroom, even side-facing is inauspicious and can easily lead to serious diseases.

10. Evergreen potted plants benefit home fortune

Due to an improvement in living standards and to increase indoor greenery, evergreen potted plants are excellent indoor decorations, but one must choose plants that are always green, strong, hard to wither, and don't shed leaves easily.

11. Large paintings above the bed are undesirable

Having paintings above the bed can enhance the elegance of the bedroom, but they should be light and small, avoiding heavy large frames. Otherwise, if the hook falls, it could result in serious injury or death, so caution is advised.

12. Clean bathrooms retain wealth

Bathrooms are places for sewage discharge. Besides following proper directional rules, they should avoid being damp, unclean, or smelly. Keeping them clean and dry can instead help retain wealth.

13. Straight passage through the house causes trouble

Homes should avoid having a long corridor like hotels or restaurants connecting several rooms, as this can easily lead to extramarital affairs and elopements, making peace difficult.

14. Beds with legs should not have clutter underneath

For beds with legs, the space underneath should remain open and ventilated, avoiding clutter, especially important for newlyweds.

15. Door opening directions within the house should be consistent

Door opening directions inside the house should be consistent, determined by the doorknob. It is most taboo to have one door open left and another open right.

16. Bathrooms at the end of corridors are highly inauspicious

If there is a corridor in the house, bathrooms should be placed along the sides, not at the end, as this is highly inauspicious.

17. Avoid large windows at the head of the bed

Windows at the head of the bed are a major feng shui taboo, and should be remembered carefully.

18. Toilets should not face the main door

Toilets directly facing the main door indicate a leaking fortune and declining luck, which can be mitigated by using screens.

19. Pillar corners pointing towards sleeping areas harm relationships

Both male and female personal rooms should avoid pillar corners pointing towards sleeping areas, as this can affect mood and health, and also negatively impact romance and marriage, requiring early correction.

20. Warm light bulbs benefit relationships

Bedroom lighting is crucial for couples' feelings and should ideally use warm-colored bulbs, avoiding cold-colored bulbs or fluorescent lights.

21. Corner-sha impacts should be blocked by potted plants

To ensure safety and health at home, choose houses without corner-sha impacts from surrounding buildings. If your residence has a corner-sha impact, use potted plants, trees, or thick curtains to block it.

Key points of bedroom feng shui affecting marital relations:

(1) Flowers at the head of the bed can attract extramarital affairs: Both partners may have affairs, eventually leading to separation and family breakdown.

(2) Bed too close to glass windows: Lack of support and stability, affecting career development. In modern cities, buildings often surround each other, reducing privacy. Noise can disturb sleep, and proximity to windows can lead to frequent dreams and infidelity.

(3) Mirrors embedded at the head of the bed invite ghostly presence: Can cause frequent headaches and insomnia. Romantic principles should consider health effects.

(4) Bedroom furniture should avoid uneven surfaces: Uneven surfaces represent disharmony, potentially causing arguments.

(5) Clutter on the bed and floor affects the owner's luck: Many people don't understand how tidiness brings good fortune. Feng shui isn't complicated; it's simple and understandable. Keeping your environment tidy improves mood and invites good fortune naturally.

(6) Bedrooms prefer whole windows over split ones: Split windows complicate matters and hinder smooth progress. Choose wisely when buying a house.

(7) Plants suit living rooms more than bedrooms: Large-leafed plants enhance couple's relationship and improve financial luck. Small-leafed plants can lead to triviality and repetition in tasks.
