"Love is the Most Luxurious Thing" is a workplace romance novel. In the hustle and bustle of life, Pan Shu has become a "demon". Facing the deceit and intrigue of society, she no longer easily gives her true feelings. Yet, the casual interactions at drinking parties leave indelible marks. As time progresses, these feelings take root and sprout. When she looks back, it's like the flowers have already bloomed. She who dared not love, encounters love; her heart gets hurt but she can't distinguish between truth and falsehood. Why is it that the person who makes her give her heart is the one who brought her the deepest pain since middle school.

If life were just a game, how good would that be? If love only lingered in fleeting moments, wouldn’t that be better? The author vividly portrays a woman's hesitation and bewilderment when facing love—yearning yet timid, passionate yet helpless. We can't help but sigh: love is truly the most luxurious thing.
