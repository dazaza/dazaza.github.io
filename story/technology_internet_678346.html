&；nbsp； Nail decoration

&；nbsp；

Firstly, remove the old nail polish. Soak a cotton ball in nail polish remover and wipe from the base of the nail to the tip several times to ensure thorough removal. Then use a nail care tool to file the shape of the nails. Afterwards, soak your hands in soapy water to soften the cuticles. Use a towel to carefully dry your fingers and the spaces between them. If necessary, use a cuticle nipper and small tweezers to remove ingrown nails and uneven dead skin around the nail bed.

Choose the right color

&；nbsp；

&；nbsp；

&；nbsp；

The colors of nail polish include rose red, bright red, flesh tone, bean paste color, silver, etc. The most popular nail polish nowadays is semi-transparent pink and beige, with light-colored nail polish being particularly trendy. Nail polish can be mixed at will [Nanjing Nail School][http://www.njainisi.org]. You don't need to buy a large number of nail polishes to match your clothing colors. Just purchase a few main colors and mix them with existing colors using overlapping or zonal color matching methods to achieve unexpected results.

&；nbsp；

&；nbsp；

&；nbsp；

When overlapping colors, you can apply a high-pigment shiny color first, then add a layer of space-color with pearl texture for creative color effects.

&；nbsp；

&；nbsp；

&；nbsp；

For zonal color matching, you can apply contrasting colors on the tips of the nails or divide the ten fingernails into two zones [Nanjing Nail Training School][http://www.njainisi.org]. You can use two or more colors, alternating contrasting colors or harmonious colors.

&；nbsp；

French manicure involves applying a different color on the moon-shaped area at the top of the nail. For example, if the base color is white, you can apply a vivid contrasting color like red or black on the moon-shaped area; or apply pastel orange, light yellow, powder blue, or pink on a white base. Nail dyeing styles can be divided into full style and moon style.

Careful application

&；nbsp；

&；nbsp； &；nbsp； &；nbsp； &；nbsp； When applying nail polish, it's best not to let the brush pick up too much polish. Wipe it lightly on the bottle opening and adjust the bristles smoothly. When applying nail polish, note the following key points:

&；nbsp；

&；nbsp； &；nbsp； &；nbsp； &；nbsp； Typically, start by applying one stroke from the middle of the nail root forward, then apply one stroke along each side of the nail. That is, brush three times neatly and evenly in the order of center first, then sides to prevent the nail polish from peeling off quickly.

&；nbsp；

&；nbsp；

&；nbsp；

To increase the durability of the nail polish, you can apply a layer at the front of the nail first, then apply it over the entire nail. Or apply the polish in two batches: the first batch should be less to make it dry easily. The second batch should have more polish. After applying the polish, use a cotton swab to clean any polish that has gotten on the surrounding skin.
