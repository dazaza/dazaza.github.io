When the village director of Shangshui Village, Qian Dabao was holding a graduation banquet for his daughter, food poisoning occurred. Dozens of villagers were hospitalized at once. The culprit that caused the food poisoning was the Chinese cabbage irrigated with polluted water. It's a matter of life and death, but Qian Dabao didn't believe in this fact. Although there was only one factory opened by his younger brother Qian Erbao in Shangshui Village, he believed his younger brother's words that the factory had sewage treatment equipment. But Man Yihua, who contracted the reservoir to raise fish, kept an eye on Qian Erbao's factory because the pollution in the reservoir was most obvious. The fish she raised could not be sold at a good price in the market and eventually no one wanted them. Qian Dabao once saved Man Yihua's life, but Man Yihua did not appreciate it. Not only did she not show gratitude, but she thought Qian Dabao owed her something because she didn't want to live and Qian Dabao meddled too much. So Qian Dabao had to give way to her everywhere. The relationship between the two was very special, and naturally there were rumors in the village. When Man Yihua kept an eye on the discharge of sewage from Qian Erbao's factory, it made Qian Dabao very embarrassed. Man Yihua still sent the water samples of the reservoir to the Environmental Protection Bureau. Knowing that he was being watched, Qian Erbao often vented his dissatisfaction with Man Yihua on Qian Dabao.

After the poisoning incident, Tao Xiaohui, the newly arrived township mayor, was very dissatisfied with Qian Dabao's performance. But the two were old classmates twenty years ago. This not only didn't make Qian Dabao feel honored, but also verified the old saying "not enemies don't meet". Originally, Qian Dabao wrote a letter to pursue Tao Xiaohui, but Tao Xiaohui handed the letter over to the school, resulting in Qian Dabao's expulsion, which changed his life path. Therefore, when the two met again, they were basically like "enemies meeting each other, their eyes turned red." Qian Dabao was wary of Tao Xiaohui and couldn't distinguish whether Tao Xiaohui's identity was as a classmate or a township mayor. He often regarded criticism as harassment and concern as bait, so there was a big divergence between the two in work. Qian Erbao and Qian Dabao were half-brothers, but their brotherly affection was deep, and they lived in the same courtyard. In the poisoning incident, it was Qian Erbao who spent money to help solve the problem. However, under Man Yihua's relentless pressure, the discharge of sewage from Qian Erbao's factory became increasingly noticeable, and finally a piece of seal closed down the factory. Under the pressure of production contracts, Qian Erbao took desperate measures and secretly resumed operations after tearing off the seal.

For the current and future survival environment of all villagers, Qian Dabao decided to cut off his brotherly relationship with Qian Erbao and insisted on closing down Qian Erbao's factory. Qian Erbao jumped into the well hugging Qian Dabao, unexpectedly, Man Yihua threw Qian Erbao's wife Zhou Lingling into the well. With life and death at stake, Qian Erbao saw no retreat from Qian Dabao, and decided to cut off the brotherly relationship with Qian Dabao. The factory was completely shut down. Qian Erbao also went to jail for tax evasion, and his wife returned to her parents' home. The small yard that used to be lively suddenly became empty. The villagers all supported Qian Dabao. Tao Xiaohui also saw Qian Dabao's sense of responsibility for doing things for the villagers, and couldn't help but have some feelings for this old classmate. She took Qian Dabao to Beijing to visit the construction of new socialist countryside. This made Man Yihua, who had been "all ready except for the east wind" emotionally with Qian Dabao, feel lost. After Qian Erbao came out and knew that Man Yihua had paid his taxes for him, he still held Man Yihua accountable for his report and always went against Qian Dabao when he was destitute, creating problems everywhere. He felt that human relations are like paper. When he was capable, everyone looked up to him, but now they look down on him. So Qian Erbao gave up. At this time, Qian Dui'er got sick. Qian Erbao, who always thought he had no more glory and face in front of the whole village, saw friendship and love, which moved him. He realized that opening such a factory was wrong to everyone.

Under the guidance of the Party's enrichment policy and the joint efforts of everyone, Shangshui Village restored its former beauty and richness. In the conference of establishing a fishery company and an agro-tourism cooperative, Qian Erbao never expected that he would be heavily relied on...
