The bishop said, knowledge is one thing, virtue is another; good sense is not conscience; philosophy, no matter how profound, cannot control the feelings. Classroom can provide an understanding attitude towards right things, but it is not a good place to cultivate virtue, which requires practice. Students' social practice is essential and an important part of ideological and political theory teaching, as well as an extension of the ideological and political class. It is both a way to deepen the focus of ideological and political theory teaching and a necessity for university teaching reform. Strengthening students' social practice can promote the healthy and harmonious development of college students in all aspects of morality, intelligence, physical fitness, and more. To comprehensively improve the quality of education and teaching of ideological and political theory classes, train qualified builders and reliable successors for the community, and innovative high-quality personnel, social practice is indispensable in the work of ideological and political theory education.

An organization to carry out the significance of students' social practice:

1.1 Social Reality of Your Function and Role of Practice

Practice is a dynamic exploration and transformation of real-world social objective material activities, with special function and role in training all-round development of qualified people for socialist modernization. Social practice has a very broad extension and wide coverage. Here, social practice activities refer to the term for students during their studies to delve into society, conduct research on production, understand national conditions, and contribute their talents through a series of material and spiritual processes. In the process of social practice, students are not only objects receiving education and self-perfection but also use their initiative to serve the community and transform the world. Social practice is a comprehensive educational activity aimed at training students, and together with ideological and political theory courses, constitutes two parallel subsystems in the school education system. As an extension and supplementary form of ideological and political theory education, social practice has become an important way for young students.

1.2 Establish Human Resources as the First Resource

In a multi-level world amid twists and turns in economic globalization trends, it is therefore necessary to establish that human resources are the primary resource. Training personnel cannot be separated from practical education. Colleges and universities, with a high degree of awareness regarding the future of the motherland, the nation's revitalization, regional economic and social development, care about the development of higher education and the training of university students. Social practice is an important part of ideological and political education and should be included in the overall planning of education, teaching, and curricula. Marx pointed out: individual behavior improves the individual’s ability and promotes the formation of good quality. This process trains people to know, love, consciousness, and practice. Practice is the contact and uniform basis of various psychological factors and also promotes the formation and development of people's qualities. College students are the country's valuable human resources and effective strength in the cause of socialism construction. Knowledge comes from practice, capacity comes from practice, and quality develops in practice.

1.3 The Social Reality of Your Formation Through Various Forms of Social Practice

Guiding students to the grassroots among the masses, thoroughly practicing, combining the promotion of social practice and professional learning, combining with service to the community, work-study combination, career employment combination, innovation and entrepreneurship combination benefits students in understanding society and our country, increasing their abilities and contributions to society, exercising willpower, cultivating character; benefiting students in broadening their horizons, developing a good study style of integrating theory with practice; benefiting students in willpower cultivation, character development, correct world outlook, outlook on life, and values, going with the workers and peasants, and practicing the Road to Success; solving college students' career, employment, and entrepreneurship issues.

In short, strengthening and improving students' social practice has very important significance for enhancing college students' ideological and political quality, innovative spirit, and practical ability, and training socialist builders and successors.
