Our Changsha edition news: On both sides of Jianxiang Road in Kaifu District, there are many mobile phone specialty stores, with a relatively large volume of traffic. Last June 15th, this section began a full-day parking ban. The sides of the road also have obvious signs prohibiting parking and warning of "heavy penalties for illegal parking". However, the illegal act of random parking still persists despite repeated bans. Yesterday morning, the Kaifu Traffic Police Team conducted an operation on this section, carrying out special rectification for vehicles that violate the prohibition sign and park illegally. In order to crack down on the illegal act of random parking, recently, the Changsha Traffic Police added 500 new car lockers, distributed to various traffic police teams.

@Lawyer Yuan Yulai: The Changsha traffic police locked up the Shaanxi police car, one family doesn't recognize another family, impressive!

Reporter Ren Wenjing Intern Li Mingru

"Inside and outside the car locker each has a switch. The outer one can only be opened by a single key, while the inner one uses a universal key. After opening the outer lock, insert the key into the inner lock, turn right to tighten and left to loosen, adjustable according to the size of the wheel." Yang Wenhuan said, at the same time as locking the car, the traffic police department also took photos as evidence, even if some car owners really pry open the car locker, they can still find the locked vehicle. Yang Wenhuan reminds drivers that for illegally parked vehicles, the traffic police will impose strict penalties, hoping that drivers do not park illegally in prohibited areas.

At 10:40 yesterday morning, a Shaanxi A-licensed police car was illegally parked on the side of Jianxiang Road. The police immediately used a car locker to lock the illegally parked police car and attached a "illegal parking lock" reminder label. Ten minutes later, another black unlicensed car on the side of the road was also locked by the police due to illegal parking. "I know I can't park here, but I just went up to do something, only two minutes." Mr. Qiu rushed over to explain after seeing his car was locked.

At 11:36 AM, the photo of the locked police car was posted on Sina Weibo by a netizen. The Weibo post sparked heated discussions among many internet users. By 1 PM, more than 100 people had forwarded it, and over 70 had commented.

After the rectification action, Yang Wenhuan, captain of the Special Operations Team of the Kaifu District Traffic Police Brigade, introduced that in order to severely crack down on random parking, the municipal traffic police detachment newly purchased 500 dedicated car lockers, focusing on the difficult and hot spots of road parking bans within the key prevention and control area. "These newly added 500 car lockers are evenly distributed among five teams, with each team receiving 100 lockers. Now, there are more than 1,000 car lockers in the urban area of Changsha," Yang Wenhuan introduced. Traffic police will conduct regular inspections on sections where parking is prohibited and legally copy plates, lock cars, or tow away vehicles that do not follow traffic rules and park randomly.
