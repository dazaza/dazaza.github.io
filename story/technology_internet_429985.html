Which is the best place for breeding centipedes in Yichang, Hubei? The People's Livelihood Agricultural Science Centipede Breeding Base is a large professional breeding base integrating scientific research, breeding, reproduction, processing, and sales. It regularly offers free centipede breeding technology training classes. Our farm adopts a self-invented three-dimensional high-efficiency breeding method, which is the first of its kind domestically, integrating breeding and planting, and has been recognized by a wide range of breeders.

Centipedes are a traditional valuable Chinese medicine in our country, with functions of dispelling wind, relieving pain, promoting circulation, and detoxifying. In recent years, due to changes in the ecological environment, wild resources have decreased year by year, while the annual export volume has increased, leading to a very tight supply of centipedes, and prices have risen year by year. This provides a very good opportunity for artificial centipede breeding. Centipedes are carnivorous animals with a wide range of feed, strong adaptability, and relatively simple artificial breeding. Men, women, old, and young can breed them indoors or outdoors during their spare time without polluting the environment or affecting the relationship with neighbors. It is currently a good project for getting rich.

The People's Livelihood Agricultural Science Centipede Breeding Farm has gone through more than ten years of development, with an annual production of commercial centipedes exceeding ten million.

Supported by Huazhong Agricultural University: A key university under the national "211 Project" - Huazhong Agricultural University collaborates with Wuhan Agricultural Science and Technology People's Livelihood Bioengineering Co., Ltd. to jointly study and develop advanced centipede breeding technology for scientific agricultural development and technological enrichment of farmers: People's Livelihood No. 2 Golden Headed Centipede.

Advanced technical advantages: Through many years of breeding observation, with the use of pure selection technology and biological gene improvement engineering, Wuhan Agricultural Science and Technology People's Livelihood Bioengineering Co., Ltd. and several biological experts and professors of bioengineering from Huazhong Agricultural University have cultivated a new generation variety - People's Livelihood No. 2 Golden Headed Centipede. After observation, this variety is easy to raise, gentle-tempered, adaptable, grows quickly, reproduces at a high rate, has better medicinal value, and more significant economic benefits, making it an ideal investment breeding project for a wide range of farming friends.
