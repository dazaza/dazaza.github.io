The policemen guarded the supermarket with smashed windows for a whole night.

by xue94fwsh on 2012-03-05 10:13:27

Recently, a post on Xuzhou Pengke website titled "Young Policeman, Treatment for Hemorrhoids, Good Job" has sparked heated discussions among many netizens. The poster, ginger, said that she was reposting a thread from the netizen "candy820". After a "human flesh search," the main character in the post was "exposed" by netizens - he is Officer Wang Jian of the Xiyuan Police Station, born in 1977, who has been a police officer for 13 years. He just looks very young and was mistakenly thought to be a "young policeman" by netizens. A month ago, he kept watch all night for a supermarket in Xiyuan whose window had been smashed.

Eyewitness taxi driver: "Never seen such a straightforward policeman"

"candy820" is a taxi driver, his post recounted an incident that occurred at around 3 a.m. on July 15th: "That day I was driving around in the Xiyuan area. Around 3 a.m., with no more fares, I saw a police car flashing its lights near the Water Flood Bridge, so I drove over to see what was going on." It turned out that the glass storefront of a supermarket had been smashed open with a big hole. At this moment, he saw a young-looking policeman holding a flashlight, leading two security guards standing in front of the broken glass storefront.

"I want to see how this thief gets caught," so "candy820" parked his car behind the police car, prepared to observe for a long time. He said that the policeman led the security guards to search the entire supermarket, then said through the radio: "No thief inside, please help contact the supermarket's responsible person."

"candy820" lay in the taxi resting while marveling: How could such a large supermarket not even have a doorman? He saw the policeman constantly making phone calls but still couldn't get in touch with the supermarket owner, assuming the policeman would leave. At this point, he suddenly saw the officer laying down some newspapers with two security guards and sitting down at the entrance of the supermarket. "I've never seen such a straightforward policeman, actually guarding the door for others."

When "candy820" was half asleep, he was woken up by the walkie-talkie. At this time, the officer asked one security guard to stay and keep watch on the store door, preparing to take the other security guard out on patrol. When he reached the police car, the officer paused for a moment, then walked toward the taxi where "candy820" was parked. "Seeing me sitting inside, he saluted me and asked, Master, is your car broken? Do you need help?" "candy820" replied, "Nothing serious, I'm driving at night. No fares, expensive fuel, so I'll sleep here for a bit." After hearing that, the officer smiled and left.

After finishing the patrol, the officer returned with the security guards and continued to guard the supermarket door.

At around 8 a.m., "candy820" was awakened again by the noise outside. It turned out that the female boss of the supermarket had arrived, and many citizens passing by also gathered around. The female boss said that when she saw so many missed calls in the morning, she returned the call only to find out that the supermarket's glass storefront had been smashed.

When the officer asked her to check if there were any losses, the female boss's hands were trembling as she opened the door. She said that there were a lot of cigarettes and alcohol behind the smashed storefront, and there were also tens of thousands of yuan in the safe... After checking, it was found that nothing was missing. At this moment, the officer made a note in a small notebook and said that it was good that nothing was missing. Then he asked the female boss to carefully count everything again, and then go to the police station to make a record.

"If it weren't for the policeman and the two security guards who stayed from 3 a.m. until 8 a.m., it would be hard to say that nothing would have been 'stolen'. Although this is a small matter, a policeman being so conscientious and responsible, his work attitude really deserves everyone's praise!" "candy820" remarked.

The door-guarding policeman: "I am doing my own duty"

Yesterday, our reporter contacted Officer Wang Jian. Upon receiving the reporter's call, Wang Jian was quite surprised: "How did you know about this?"

Wang Jian told the reporter, "I can't remember which day it was. That day, it was my shift, and I received the alarm call at around 2 a.m." When he arrived at the scene, he saw that the supermarket's glass storefront had been smashed with a large hole, big enough for an adult to crawl through. "The supermarket installed a CK alarm system. It might be that someone wanted to steal something, smashed the storefront, heard the alarm, and got scared away." After carefully checking the scene and finding no thief, Officer Wang reported the situation back to the 110 command center and requested them to help contact the supermarket's responsible person.

"I made a lot of calls, either the old number of the boss, or out of service, or wrong number. Finally, I found the current boss's number, but it kept ringing unanswered," Officer Wang said. Behind the smashed storefront were shelves of liquor and cigarettes. To ensure the safety of the property, they could only squat by the roadside and keep watch.

"That night, we didn't just deal with this one case." So when other alarms came in, he could only let the security guards watch first, handle other cases, and then come back. This went on until early morning around seven or eight o'clock, until the supermarket boss arrived. Wang Jian claimed, "This is just part of my job, there's nothing worth praising."

Our reporter saw on the Pengke website that a gang had committed multiple indoor thefts in urban villages, specifically buying safes to store stolen goods. A netizen named "Big Afu" said: This policeman deserves the national emblem on his head, well done! Netizen "Living Freely" said: Such hardworking and conscientious people serving the common folks deserve our respect. Netizen "Lower Profile Again" said: Thousands upon thousands of ordinary good people are the backbone of China, reading this post makes me feel refreshed!
