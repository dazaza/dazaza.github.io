[Special Topic] Tianjin Women's Volleyball Team Competes in the 2011-2012 National Women's Volleyball League - Zhang Xiaotang's Right Eye "Severely Injured"

With a crucial match approaching, the Tianjin Women's Volleyball Team, which was preparing in Guangzhou, encountered an unexpected incident yesterday when their main setter, Zhang Xiaotang, was hit in her right eye by a ball during training. This resulted in a scratched eyeball with inflammation and eyelid congestion. Due to the team's need for players at this critical time, the injured Zhang Xiaotang resolutely stated that she would be able to continue playing.

Yesterday afternoon, the Tianjin Women's Volleyball Team conducted its first on-site training session at the Guangzhou Sports College Gymnasium. The recent sudden rise in temperature in Guangzhou saw the highest temperature reaching 26 degrees Celsius yesterday, making the players feel the stifling heat inside the venue. The plastic floor also became slippery due to the increased humidity in the air. Soon after the training began, head coach Liu Xiaoming noticed this issue and immediately reminded the players to be cautious of their footwork to avoid accidental injuries.

Despite the entire team being highly vigilant to prevent any unforeseen incidents before the major competition, the unwanted event still occurred. During a defensive drill, Zhang Xiaotang's right eye was struck diagonally by the ball. At the time, her contact lens was directly dislodged and lost, causing intense pain that made Xiaotang cry uncontrollably. Concerned about possible retinal damage, the coaching staff immediately decided to send team doctor Wang Xueyi with Zhang Xiaotang to the hospital for examination. After a detailed inspection, fortunately, there was no injury to the retina; only some eyelid congestion and slight abrasions on the cornea from the dislodging of the contact lens were found. The doctor prescribed some anti-inflammatory medication for Zhang Xiaotang. On the way back to the hotel, Zhang Xiaotang re-fitted another contact lens.

Upon returning to the hotel, it was already past the team’s mealtime, but team leader Chang Liangcai and coach Li Shan were still waiting in the restaurant. Upon understanding Zhang Xiaotang's situation, Li Shan joked: "Fitting a contact lens is no longer effective; you should quickly get a pair of large-framed glasses like those worn by former Dutch men's footballer Dennis Bergkamp, so cool when you go on the field!"

According to plans, this morning, the Tianjin team will conduct its second adaptive site training at the Guangzhou Sports Institute Gymnasium.

Preparation Mindsets Differ Between Tianjin and Guangdong Teams

The second leg of the semi-final between the Evergrande Women's Volleyball Team and the Tianjin team is about to begin. To allow the players more adequate rest time, Tianjin has put significant effort into the logistics support for this Guangzhou trip. Meanwhile, the importance placed on this semi-final by the Evergrande Group clearly cannot compare with that of the Super Cup in football, as the ticketing plan for this match has yet to be announced to the public even today.
