"Parting Song II" by Rao Xueman, from the Growing Pains Series.

by jqrxass on 2009-10-17 14:22:46

"Parting Song" tells the legendary experience of Ma Zhuo, a girl from Sichuan. In "Parting Song I", after her mother Lin Guoguo was mysteriously murdered, Ma Zhuo lived with her adoptive father A Nan. However, her peaceful life became turbulent and perilous again because of a boy named "Poison". In "Parting Song II", Ma Zhuo decided to end the entanglement with Poison, but she had to seek his help due to her good friend Yan Shushu's "sex photo" incident. Poison's girlfriend - school beauty Yu Anduo became more mysterious. She played a trick of self-mutilation in front of Ma Zhuo, warning her that if she wanted to enter Poison's life, she would pay a painful price.

When Poison's sister - Xia Hua, who looked like Lin Guoguo, appeared in front of A Nan, what kind of ripple would it cause in the lives of this father and daughter?

Raoyue Man's new work in 2008, "Parting Song II", continues the painful youth route, bringing readers thrilling emotions.

"Parting Song II" Part One

A deep blue button fell off my white shirt, and I sewed it for almost the whole afternoon. An old song by Faye Wong was on repeat in my MP3, listened to until my ears were almost numb. My black big schoolbag was drying on the windowsill, which I had washed three times, but I still felt that there was some annoying dirt on the shoulder strap. During this winter vacation, I had a fresh discovery: when you do something extremely slowly or repeatedly, you will have the illusion that time has been stuck. The wind does not move, the clouds do not move, and the clock on the wall seems to be dull too, everything in front of your eyes presents in slow motion, even your worries.

"Parting Song II" Part Two

On Monday, a notice was actually posted on the back door of our classroom. It was a large piece of white paper with bright red words. The poster was Yan Shushu, and the general idea of the notice was as follows: Due to financial difficulties, I urgently need five thousand yuan in cash. Please all friends in the world help me get through this difficult period, I am very grateful. The borrowed money will be repaid within one month, with an interest rate of twenty percent as a token of appreciation.

"Parting Song II" Part Three

I can't remember what time we went to sleep last night, maybe three o'clock, maybe four o'clock... In my lifetime memory, I have never said so many words like last night. I told him about Ya'an, the rain there, grandma, uncle, Lin Guoguo, A Nan, even the blueprint. He rarely asked questions, just listened to me, until I talked myself hoarse and fell asleep deeply in his arms. In a half-dream, half-awake state, I seemed to hear him calling my name: "Ma Zhuo, Little Ma Zhuo, Little Ma Yang..." Before I met him, I never had so many names. I thought it was funny, but I didn't have the strength to laugh. Actually, I also wanted to respond to him, but I didn't even have the strength to hum. It was like falling into some kind of nightmare, my mind was clear, but my body couldn't move at all.
