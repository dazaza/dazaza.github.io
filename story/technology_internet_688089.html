How to Deal with Frizzy Curly Hair - Key Insights

by king2f4j on 2012-03-03 00:23:35

Best Answer:

1. Regular hair care and the use of hair conditioner can improve the frizzy condition of your hair.

2. Avoid perming small curls.

3. The curl size should not be too large; natural bends are sufficient.

4. Apply a shine-enhancing weak styling product on wet or semi-dry hair to achieve the desired natural curls. If it still doesn't work, it is recommended to use a curling iron for styling.

Hair care should start from daily life. To make your hair healthy, internal care is the most fundamental. Early to bed and early to rise, regular meals, good living habits, and a cheerful mood can reduce hair loss and make your hair healthy and strong. Good eating habits can ensure that your hair gets enough nutrition. The secret to a good hair diet is to provide adequate protein because protein is the main component of hair. Foods such as eggs, meat, beans, and milk are rich in protein. Iodine can increase the luster of hair, so eating more seaweed and purple algae can enhance the brightness of hair and make it look healthy. Except for the hair follicle, other parts of the hair have no vitality. Once the hair that has grown out is damaged, it will be difficult to restore. Therefore, external hair care is very important: 1. Wash your hair in time and keep it clean. 2. Use hair care products regularly. 3. Use oil treatment products to repair hair quality damaged by perming, dyeing, and bleaching. Correct hair washing step 1: combing - combing your hair before washing can promote blood circulation in the scalp. Drying hair, using a wide-toothed comb to comb from the ends of the hair upward section by section, repeat several times, and smooth the hair. Step 2: Washing - use warm water around 40 degrees to wet your hair, pour an appropriate amount of shampoo into your palm, rub it to foam, and gently massage it on your hair. Step 3: Rinsing - rinse your hair thoroughly with clear water twice, trying not to leave any sticky residue. Step 4: Drying - use a dry large towel to absorb the water from your hair, don't rub hard to avoid damaging the fragile wet hair, then gently comb with a wide-toothed comb, best let the wet hair air dry naturally. If you need to use a hairdryer, choose the medium setting and blow until it's 80% dry.

How to take care of your hair in summer:

1. Follow the will of your hair - don't frequently subject your hair to extreme treatments like curling irons or straighteners as each operation reduces the moisture in your hair. Instead, get a haircut that allows your hair to grow healthily.

2. Protect your hair from the sun - sunlight is the enemy of hair shine as it takes away moisture and makes your hair dry and brittle. It's essential to use hair products with UV protection, or simply wear a wide-brimmed hat under the sun to protect your hair while adding a different charm.

3. Avoid salt and chlorine - hair is like a sponge absorbing whatever liquid it comes in contact with. Whether swimming in the ocean or a pool, soak your hair in fresh water beforehand to prevent it from being eroded by salt or chlorine. After swimming, wash your hair immediately with fresh water.

4. When unsure, keep your hair clean and tidy - this is the simplest way to style your hair. Dry your hair well and apply gel to the middle and ends of your hair (this avoids ugly marks left by the comb after applying gel).

How to take care of your hair in winter:

Winter dryness makes hair hardest to manage. After the heat of summer and the strong ultraviolet rays of autumn, your hair may fade, become yellow, split, tangle, or even produce static electricity. The ends of your hair are the most susceptible to damage, showing signs of weakness and lack of nutrients such as yellowing and splitting, which greatly affect your appearance. Therefore, regular maintenance is necessary, and the simplest and most effective method is to persistently use end-repairing nutritional products to provide ample nourishment to the ends of your hair. Nowadays, no-rinse end-repairing products contain balanced vitamins in addition to traditional nourishing oils, which can quickly penetrate deep into the roots and provide nutritional repair. There are also hair essences containing chitin and water-soluble silicone oil suitable for use on semi-dry hair after washing or before sleeping, just wear a sleep cap.

Color-locked dyed hair is already quite damaged, and the soaking in the summer pool water and the strong ultraviolet rays of autumn can further cause fading and yellowing. Fortunately, today's hair care products have introduced various color-locking and repairing products. In new color-locking products, there are magical color factors added to make the hair shine, as well as components that block ultraviolet rays and deeply nourish the hair. Especially this season, pearl ingredients are popularly added to post-dye care products, and the contained natural mineral crystals can give the hair a pearly sheen, reducing the trouble of re-dying.

Smooth and moisturize hair: Our hair contains about 15% water, but if it drops below 10%, it will immediately show various signs of dryness - static electricity, falling hair strands, and split ends, especially in autumn. Nowadays, the easiest way to hydrate your hair is to use a moisturizing spray, which can be lightly sprayed anytime and anywhere to moisturize the hair strands and reshape the hairstyle. The new trend is to add protein components in moisturizing sprays, which can replenish the lost proteins in the hair, especially suitable for dry hair.

Well, follow the correct methods to take care of your beautiful shiny hair. Curly hair can indeed be a headache; I had mine permed and then undone, which was frustrating. Use a conditioner suitable for your hair type, eat more nuts like melon seeds and peanuts, and regularly go to the hair salon for care.
