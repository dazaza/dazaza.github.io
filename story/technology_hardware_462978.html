Driver who returned an hour after hitting female beggar and ran her over again was arrested

by hj1718004 on 2011-07-14 19:43:51

Tian Houbo

Xinhua News Agency, Chongqing, May 12th (reported by Wang Xiaolei) - On May 12th, it was learned from the People's Procuratorate of Zhong County, Chongqing that the criminal suspect Tian Houbo, known as the "Chongqing Yao Jiaxin," was conditionally approved for arrest by the procuratorate on suspicion of intentional homicide. The prosecution determined that after driving over a female beggar and fleeing, he returned to the scene and ran over the victim, causing her death.

The prosecution stated that at around 10 PM on December 23rd last year, the suspect Tian Houbo was driving a Lifan brand truck along the Xingjiagou Road in Group 2, Helin Village, Maguan Town, Zhong County. Due to excessive speed while turning, he failed to avoid hitting a female beggar lying in the middle of the road, injuring her left leg before fleeing the scene.

At around 1 AM on December 24th, Tian Houbo, fearing that the injured beggar might be discovered, drove back to the scene and found the victim still lying in the same spot. He then positioned his vehicle in front of the victim, reversed, and used the left rear wheel to run over the victim’s head and face, causing severe cranial injuries leading to her death. Subsequently, he moved the body and concealed it in a nearby small ditch.

This case has drawn significant public attention. The investigative supervision department of the People's Procuratorate of Zhong County communicated with the public security bureau regarding the case details and closely tracked the progress of this case. On May 8th this year, the public security bureau requested the procuratorate to approve the arrest of Tian Houbo. On May 11th, the procuratorate approved the arrest of the criminal suspect Tian Houbo.

Currently, the case is under further investigation.
