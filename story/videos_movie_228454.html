"Queen Seondeok" tells the story of Princess Dae-Man (played by Lee Yo-Won), who was abandoned as a child due to an ominous prophecy, and through relentless effort and power struggles within the palace, grows up to become the first queen of Silla - Queen Seondeok.

Under the circumstances where Mi-Seok (played by Ko Hyun-Jung) wields great power in the palace, King Jinpyeong and Lady Maya gave birth to twin daughters. However, for the king, giving birth to twins is considered extremely unlucky, with the omen of "the end of the royal lineage." Inevitably, King Jinpyeong had to send away his second daughter, Dae-Man, but Mi-Seok secretly sent people to hunt her down. At a critical moment, Moon-Nu, a legendary Hwarang figure, used his wit to rescue Dae-Man and handed her over to be raised by Jiso-Hwa, one of King Jinpyeong's maids. Jiso-Hwa took Dae-Man to the desert regions of China and raised her in a small inn. Mi-Seok, Dae-Man's greatest rival, wielded her extraordinary beauty to gain the favor of King Jinheung and seize power in the palace. Ambitious, she established the young King Jinpyeong on the throne after King Jinheung’s death. When King Jinpyeong's wife gave birth to twin daughters, Mi-Seok intended to use their lives to threaten him and directly seize royal power. Unexpectedly, Moon-Nu and Jiso-Hwa saved Dae-Man, disrupting her plans. Despite this, Mi-Seok did not give up her quest for the throne and plotted to have her husband ascend it. As a result, she viewed Princess Cheon-Myeong (played by Park Yi-Jin), Dae-Man's twin sister, as a thorn in her side. Under Mi-Seok's rule, Princess Cheon-Myeong, possessing exceptional intelligence and quick judgment, did not easily reveal her true nature but always showcased her leadership qualities at crucial moments. Although Princess Cheon-Myeong married Kim Yongsu (played by Park Jung-Cheol) and had a son, Kim Chunchu (played by Yoo Seung-Ho), she developed feelings for Kim Yu-Shin (played by Um Tae-Woong), who once saved her life. Kim Yu-Shin, a renowned general of Silla, displayed remarkable martial arts skills. After accidentally rescuing Princess Cheon-Myeong from an assassination attempt by Mi-Seok's confidants, he met Princess Dae-Man under her arrangement. While helping Princess Dae-Man realize her grand dream of unifying the three kingdoms, Kim Yu-Shin developed feelings for her and became her loyal supporter. With the help of Kim Yu-Shin, Princess Dae-Man gradually dismantled Mi-Seok's influence and ascended to the throne as the first queen of Silla.
