Groaning without illness

How far is forever? My heart is already tired.

Forgive me for being this way, forgive me for not being brave, forgive me for not being strong, forgive me for being so stubborn.

I just want to live my life in confusion, without thinking too much, without being so sad, and just living in the past.

This is good enough, this is sufficient. I have already become numb, with no feeling left.

Numbness

Falling into disappointment time and again,

Accepting it all over again,

Drumming up courage once more,

Stirring up strength repeatedly,

Losing ground, living like a fool, leading a despondent life, that's it, that's fine, it's not bad either.

From now on, I will no longer force myself to forget, true forgetting requires no effort.

Since I can't forget, can't achieve it, even if I use all my strength, I still can't do it, even if I don't think about it, I can't control my emotions. Gradually, I am tired, time flows by, memories remain. With the passage of time, I gradually realize those past events, those memories I cannot forget, at least not now.

Occasionally, I still unintentionally remember them, and there's always a big void in my heart. I always burst into tears, always quickly escape. I used to try hard, very hard, I told myself I could definitely do it, I told myself those things didn't matter to me, they had nothing to do with me. But after saying it, tears still uncontrollably flowed, I was still sad, my heart still hurt.

Because it hurts too much, unbearable sorrow, I chose to avoid, no longer mention, no longer expose. I buried those wounds, that so-called past deep inside, I wrapped myself up tightly, no longer accepting anyone, no longer telling anyone, no longer letting anyone see, no longer letting anyone get close.

Recklessness

Sometimes, because of those who know my past, in that instant, I want to escape, I want to leave, I don't want to consider anything, I don't want to care about anything, I don't want to think about anything anymore, I even want to disappear, vanish forever, never meet those people again. But, I hesitated, I couldn't let go, I couldn't bear it. I began to waver, I started to feel lost, I stopped my steps, quietly standing alone, silent, no longer speaking.

Especially, those who clearly know my past but still incessantly ask me, constantly asking me questions I don't want to face or mention, at that moment, my heart hurts so much. At that moment, I am so disappointed, they don't understand me, they don't really get me...

I tell myself not to be sad, not to care, pretend as if nothing happened. But my heart hurts so much, so much.

Especially those things I've always been unwilling to face, unwilling to mention, they are so tactless, constantly bringing them up in front of me. Their words always pierce my heart like needles. I hurt so much, unable to hold back my tears, I feel so tormented, so uncomfortable. Those words make me see my helplessness and sadness once again, those so-called trivialities. Suddenly, tears fall like rain, making me inexplicably sad.

I say: Why touch upon the sadness in my eyes, why make me see my wounds unintentionally, why bring up the past, why sprinkle salt on my wounds, why don't the people I care about understand me and instead hurt me?

It hurts, it's unbearable, yet I still pretend like a fool, trying to hide it, not wanting, not hoping they find out, fearing they would feel guilty if they knew I was sad and worried. Helplessly, I laugh, helplessly, I endure, helplessly, I give a forced smile. Then I sigh, "Sad."

Promises, I dare not make promises easily. I'm afraid I won't be able to fulfill those promises, I'm afraid I'll let you down. Sorry, I can't give you any promises, not even one. To me, a promise isn't just words; it needs to be fulfilled through actions. A made promise is a debt owed, so I've always been cautious and dared not to make promises, I don't even have the courage to promise, I lack confidence when I speak, yet no one seems to notice my helplessness. I start hinting, but you don't catch on, which makes me so sad.

Explanation, explanations are always excessive, because those who understand you don't need them, and those who don't understand you need them even less. That's what I say.

Actually, I know I can't let go, I know I don't want to leave. But I'm scared, I'm fearful. I fear those wounds being exposed again, I fear those pasts being brought up again, every time they're mentioned, I can't help but cry, though I've grown numb, my emotions are still uncontrollable. Gradually, I've started to hate myself for being like this, so I want to quietly withdraw. I want to bury those pasts, not letting anyone discover them, or leave alone, hiding away from everyone, going somewhere where no one knows me, so I won't be scared, it won't hurt so much, so I don't have to face them again.

I didn't cry, but tears streamed down my face.

Dream

The dream woke, the heart broke.

My future is a dream. I said. Compared to anyone else, I'm just that unlucky, I'm just that sad.

Since it's a dream, then let's just sleep on forever, never wake up, just sink deeper. No more entanglement, no more thinking, no more pain.

I want to say nonchalantly: It doesn't matter, it's not a big deal.

But I can't say it, I instantly lose my ability to speak, I instantly feel the scent of death, I can't express my pain.

I call these helplessness sorrows, I call those pasts tragedies, I begin to hate my own existence.

I start endlessly cursing, endlessly blaming myself, endlessly grieving.

Regarding the future, I've never thought about it, nor do I wish to think about it. Future, what is the future, I don't know, I'm unclear, I don't understand, I don't comprehend. The future seems so distant to me, those things seem not to belong to me, I stubbornly say they have nothing to do with me.

The wounds I once desperately hid, desperately kept others from discovering, I now strangely and ruthlessly expose, laughing while looking at the scars.

Those wounds haven't healed yet, they're still there, obviously not healed, obviously still painful. Wounds are like me, stubborn children, refusing to heal because the heart is a warm and moist place suitable for anything to grow. I start mocking myself again.

Funny, absurd, speechless, sad. Muttering to oneself, reaping what one sows, flying moths toward fire.

If memory were as solid as steel, should I laugh or cry?

It can only be this way, hurting, aching, being sad, being sorrowful, at least it proves I'm still alive, originally I've always been alive.

Hypothesis

If I could, I wish I could be emotionless.

If I could, I hope I could be heartless,

If I could, I hope I could forget,

If I could, I wish I'd already lost my memory.
