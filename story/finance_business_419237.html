Hunan M-zone, what the hell are you? Do you know who I am?

by musicshow5 on 2011-04-21 09:46:45

A few days ago, when I was enthusiastically recommending this music website to friends and acquaintances around me, I received a lot of cold water being thrown my way. So here, I'll take some time to explain in detail what exactly Hunan M Zone is. However, before you listen to my explanation, please do me a favor and first open the Hunan M Zone website to see what kind of junk is inside. If you're going to criticize, please aim accurately before throwing your insults. I'll secretly rejoice and thank you for any well-placed critiques.

1. What does Hunan M Zone aim to become?

My plan for this music site isn't all that grandiose. The goal is simple: as long as Hunan musicians and local media know about this exchange platform, it will have achieved its thoroughly ambitious target. The road is rugged, and the task is heavy and long.

2. Who are the user groups for Hunan M Zone?

Upon entering our site, you won't find it hard to notice that everything revolves around Hunan music. This includes users related to Hunan musicians, music works, music institutions, music entertainment, musical instruments, and more. As for age groups, it ranges from young teenagers to seventy-year-old grandpas.

3. Why did you register this website?

This question is absolutely brilliant—it's pure genius! As the webmaster, I've been working tirelessly, running around in the early stages, actively integrating local music resources for Hunan music lovers. I've been begging people everywhere to support our platform—for instance, sponsoring KTV venues, recording studio usage, campus fan-meet tickets, discounts or even free vouchers for music bars and coffee shops. There’s only one truth: to give everyone a place to learn how to sing like others, a place to sing others' songs, a place to sing to others, a place to perform their own songs to others, and a place to sing their own songs just for themselves...and so on.

4. What benefits can we get from this?

You must either not have read the point above or didn't understand it clearly. Let me tell you straightforwardly, without any courtesy or restraint: in the future, we will frequently host free music events where everyone can participate. Music is loved by all, and who can resist free good things? Right?

5. You’ve explained it so well. How can I support you?

I may be shallow in knowledge and lacking in noble shame, but I sincerely appeal to all friends in Hunan who love music and see potential in Hunan M Zone to register on our website, actively communicate, and fully participate in our music activities. That would satisfy my heart greatly...

6. I don't have any other questions. Can you give me the website address again? I want to write it down!

Sure thing! www.hnmtv.net. Hopefully, you're wearing something unforgettable while jotting it down...
