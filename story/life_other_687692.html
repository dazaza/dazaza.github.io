Good night, dear loneliness

Tonight, the moonlight is very clear, quietly spilling onto the leaves. The night inscribes its history on the leaves, recording its own quiet thoughts.

Her, far away, I imagine she must be lonely too at this moment. But from the messages she sent, it seems that she's not alone. She said she has a strong ability to resist loneliness. The more lonely, the more one indulges in empty thoughts, but these thoughts only exacerbate the burning loneliness.

The bustling stands beside the football field are painted dark green, tier upon tier. On days with matches, I like to sit behind her, my feet resting on the stand behind her. This way, she can use my feet as a backrest, and most importantly, I can see the smile on her side face - an innocent, pure smile. Then I would also smile foolishly. I like carrying her bag and getting her snacks because I have plenty of strength, and most importantly, I feel it's what I should do.

Now, sitting quietly alone on the QVOD stand, the moonlight smiles beside me. I made a smiling face at it, but soon I was swallowed by the endless blackness. With headphones on, the volume almost deafening, my feet tapping to the drumbeat in the music, people far away could see the tiny dust particles dancing around me through the lamplight. Eventually, I stopped dancing. No matter how loud the sound was, it didn't stir even a ripple in my heart.

Squatting there, looking into the distance, I realized I was being enveloped by the spreading black loneliness. Suddenly, I felt like crying.

Half a bottle of cola consumed has released a lot of carbon dioxide gas, bubbling up and down in my stomach. The gas escaping from my mouth carries the taste of lunch. Suddenly, I remembered the wontons I ate alone in the afternoon, savoring the meat aroma and the soup with much vinegar.

Would Chang'e in the sky feel lonely? Since she lives in the Moon Palace, accompanied by moonlight, she must really like the delicate moonlight. From the color of her transparent brocade clothes, one can see that she isn't lonely. She likes this color of loneliness.

Suddenly, I heard Xu Fei's "The Left Wing" ... Dreams soar far away. You are my left wing. When I can't feel your breathing or heartbeat next to me, I'd rather not fly.

In this night, I lack my left wing, so I can only quietly imagine my QVOD flight.

When tired of flying, rest quietly. Lying in bed, I freeze my memory on this QVOD song "The Left Wing." Even in dreams, I want to fly.

Good night, dear loneliness.
