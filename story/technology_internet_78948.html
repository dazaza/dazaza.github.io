☆ Integrated three different styles of QQ skins: QQ2008 style, QQ2007 style, Olympic style. Each style of QQ skin has two types to choose from:

* Super slimmed-down version of QQ skin --- only retains the chat function of QQ, giving you a clean and simple chat interface

* Practical version of QQ skin --- under the premise of retaining all functions of QQ, the QQ interface is simplified and optimized, and some practical and fun small functions are added

You can choose any style and any type of QQ skin according to your needs

☆ Integrated mainstream QQ auxiliary software, and these auxiliary software can be directly invoked in QQ:

* One-click close all QQ --- close QQ without ending the process

* QQ Query Tool --- chat with any QQ, view any QQ's QQ space, QQ album, QQ pet, QQ space background music, etc.

* QQ Sound Changer --- a small tool that can change QQ prompt sounds

* Q Pet Nanny --- an auxiliary tool for Q Pet Penguin, automatically taking care of your Q Pet Penguin

* Q Pet Pig Companion --- an auxiliary tool for Q Pet Pig, automatically taking care of your Q Pet Pig
