What is a Logo?

A logo, emblem, or trademark is a product of modern economics. It is different from ancient seals; modern logos carry the intangible assets of a company and serve as a medium for comprehensive corporate information transmission. As the most important part of a company's CIS strategy, a logo is the most widely used, frequently appearing, and critical element in conveying a corporate image. A company's strong overall strength, perfect management mechanism, high-quality products and services are all encapsulated within the logo, which through continuous stimulation and repeated impressions, leaves a deep mark on the audience's mind. Common conceptual approaches in logo and emblem design. Contact us and let our design consultants serve you; we look forward to your information.

1. Representational Approach: Using an image that is directly associated with the characteristics of the logo object and has typical features, clearly stating the purpose of the logo. This approach is direct, clear, and immediately understandable, making it easy to quickly understand and remember.
