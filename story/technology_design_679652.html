Feasibility analysis report on the use of proceeds from the private placement of A shares of Anhui Energy Electric Power Co., Ltd. I. Background and objectives of this non-public offering (I) Background of this non-public offering 1. Achieving energy conservation and emission reduction is one of the important goals of China's electric power industry development China has become a major energy-consuming country in the world, with carbon emissions ranking first in the world. Before the Copenhagen Climate Summit, the Chinese government committed to reducing carbon emissions per unit of GDP by 40%-45% based on 2005 levels by 2020; On the other hand, China's electric power industry is currently still dominated by thermal power, and the main raw material for the thermal power industry is coal. Therefore, whether it is energy consumption or gas pollutant emissions, the electric power industry occupies an important position and is an important field for China's energy conservation and emission reduction. To achieve the energy conservation and emission reduction plan, China's electric power industry vigorously develops green and low-carbon energy and implements environmental protection policies such as "replacing small with large." Through this non-public offering, the company will effectively increase the proportion of large-capacity thermal power generation units and will have nuclear power investments, actively responding to the national call for energy conservation, emission reduction, and green environmental protection. 2. Improve fuel procurement channels and adapt to the requirements of the capital market for overall listing In recent years, the tight situation of electricity and coal supply and demand has shown a trend of spreading from local areas to the whole country. Currently, part of the coal used by the wholly-owned subsidiaries Anhui Tongling Company and Anhui Hefei Company of the company is entrusted to the related enterprise Electric Fuel Company for procurement and supply, and service fees are paid to the Electric Fuel Company. Through the acquisition behavior of raising funds through this non-public offering, the Electric Fuel Company will be included in the scope of listed companies, which can effectively ensure fuel procurement and help further control procurement costs. It also meets the requirements of the capital market for overall listing of enterprises. (II) Objectives of this non-public offering 1. Improve asset structure and enhance the operating strength of the company The company plans to use part of the funds raised from this issue to build the "Hefei Power Plant Unit 6 Expansion Project," which will effectively increase the proportion of large-capacity thermal power generation units in the company's total generating units, improve the company's unit structure, and enhance power generation capabilities; Another part will be used to acquire 80% equity of the Electric Fuel Company, 50% equity of Linhuan Zhongli, and 2% equity of Qinshan Phase II held by Anhui Energy Group, thereby further improving the company's fuel procurement channels, enhancing asset integrity, adding new nuclear power investments, forming a diversified industrial structure coexisting with traditional energy and highly efficient clean energy, improving the asset structure, reducing operating risks, and enhancing the company's profitability. 2. Alleviate funding pressure and promote long-term development In recent years, with the expansion of production scale, the company's debt-to-asset ratio has increased year by year. As of September 30, 2011, the company's debt-to-asset ratio (on a consolidated statement basis) was 63.72%, and the relatively high debt-to-asset ratio has brought certain financial cost pressures to the company. Through this non-public offering, on the one hand, it can alleviate funding pressure and directly promote faster and better development of the company's business; on the other hand, it can reduce the debt-to-asset ratio, lower debt repayment risks, provide good guarantees for subsequent debt financing of the company, and meet the funding needs of future new construction projects. Through the implementation of the fundraising investment project, the proportion of large-capacity, high-parameter generating units in the company will increase, and the structure of power generation assets will be further optimized, which is conducive to implementing the company's "replacing small with large" replacement plan, further reducing energy consumption levels; Through the implementation of the acquisition plan, the company will improve fuel procurement channels and asset structures, which is beneficial to enhancing profitability, core competitiveness, and the ability to resist cyclical fluctuations in the industry, and has important strategic significance for achieving the company's long-term sustainable development. II. Use Plan for Funds Raised This Time The total amount of funds expected to be raised from this non-public offering of shares by the company shall not exceed RMB 1.8 billion (including issuance expenses), which will be used for the following projects: Serial number Project name Main content of the project Hefei Power Plant Unit 6 Expansion Investment not exceeding RMB 840 million, used for the expansion project of Unit 6 (1×600MW) of Anhui Hefei Power Plant Acquisition of equity held by Anhui Energy Group in target companies Acquisition of 80% equity of the Electric Fuel Company, 50% equity of Linhuan Zhongli, and 2% equity of Qinshan Phase II held by Anhui Energy Group Note: The project of "acquisition of equity held by Anhui Energy Group in target companies" is expected to use no more than RMB 960 million of the funds raised, and the final funding requirement will be determined according to the asset appraisal results filed with the Anhui SASAC. If the funds raised from this non-public offering cannot meet the funding needs of the corresponding projects, the company will use self-raised funds to make up for the shortfall. III. Basic Situation of Fundraising Projects This Time (I) Hefei Power Plant Unit 6 Expansion Project 1. Project Overview The dynamic total investment of the "Hefei Power Plant Unit 6 Expansion Project" is RMB 2.13 billion, and the company plans to invest no more than RMB 840 million of the funds raised from this non-public offering. This project is located in Luyang District, Hefei City, Anhui Province, and plans to expand one 600MW supercritical extraction condensing coal-fired power generation unit to meet the growing demand for electricity load in Anhui Province, especially in central Anhui, and improve the power supply capability and quality in central Anhui. This project has obtained the following approval documents: "Approval of the Environmental Impact Report for the Expansion Project of Unit 6 (1×600 Megawatts) of Hefei Power Plant" (Approval [2010] No. 280); "Reply on the Preliminary Review Opinion of Construction Land for the Expansion Project of Unit 6 (1×600,000 kW) of Anhui Hefei Power Plant" (Preliminary Review Word [2009] No. 95) and "Letter Agreeing to Extend the Validity Period of the Preliminary Review Opinion of Construction Land for the Expansion Project of Unit 6 (1×600,000 kW) of Anhui Hefei Power Plant" (Preliminary Review Word [2011] No. 156); "Approval of the National Development and Reform Commission on the Approval of the Expansion Project of Unit 6 of Anhui Hefei Power Plant" (Development and Reform Energy [2011] No. 2457). 2. Project Implementation Content This project will be specifically implemented by Waneng Hefei Company, a controlled subsidiary of the company, and the project site is located in Luyang District, North Suburb of Hefei City. This phase of the expansion project plans to use the already built public facilities of Unit 5 (1×600MW) to expand one 600MW supercritical extraction condensing coal-fired power generation unit, synchronously equipped with flue gas desulfurization and denitrification facilities. The total dynamic investment of the project is RMB 2.13 billion. The expected construction period of this project is 18 months, and after Unit 6 is completed and put into operation, the total installed capacity of Waneng Hefei Company will reach 1,200MW. 3. Project Development Prospects This project plans to expand one 1×600MW supercritical unit, which is one of the more advanced units domestically and internationally. Its standard coal consumption for power generation is 284 grams/kWh, complying with the national industrial policy of constructing high-parameter, large-capacity, and high-efficiency coal-fired power stations, as well as the national requirements for energy conservation and circular economy development. This project is a renovation and expansion project utilizing the original plant site, allowing full utilization of existing technical resources and public facilities already built for Unit 5 at the original plant site; After the completion of the project and the shutdown of existing small units, it can improve energy utilization efficiency while increasing load, achieving "increased production with reduced emissions," both conforming to national industrial and environmental protection policies and having characteristics of less land occupation, short construction period, high safety, and economic efficiency. The project site is close to the Dafangying Reservoir auxiliary dam, which can reduce water resource consumption; it is near the Huainan Xinji Coal Mine Railway. Central Anhui region is an economically developed area in Anhui Province with relatively concentrated loads, but few power sources and insufficient installed capacity. The 1×600MW coal-fired power generation unit expanded by this project can be directly connected to the Hefei ring network for operation, which is beneficial for the rational layout of power sources throughout Anhui Province, reducing grid burden and lowering network losses. It also facilitates local power consumption, improving the power supply capability and quality in Central Anhui region, and enhancing the safety and reliability of grid operation. In summary, this project has good economic benefits and positive social effects, conforms to national industrial policies and local economic development needs, and helps improve the overall competitiveness of Waneng Hefei Company. Therefore, this project is feasible. (II) Acquisition Project of Equity Held by Anhui Energy Group in Target Companies 1.
