SEO optimization is not a mechanical and repetitive work.

by seo17511 on 2012-02-21 22:37:24

To most people, SEO is simply about updating content and building external links. This is merely the most basic work of SEO and also the common practice for those who have just started learning SEO. In fact, SEO is a scientific and systematic project rather than repetitive mechanical labor.

1. SEO should learn keyword analysis

Nowadays, many SEO practitioners jump into action without thinking twice. They randomly pick a keyword and start optimizing it. As a result, many websites may rank well with decent traffic, but they fail to receive any orders. This is because many people choose keywords based on assumptions, leading to situations where some keywords are too competitive and cannot be optimized effectively after a long period. Other times, due to incorrect keyword selection, the website receives traffic that cannot be retained. Therefore, a qualified SEO practitioner must learn to analyze various keyword data. The sources of keyword data include Baidu related searches, keyword tracking tools, Baidu promotion assistant, various Q&A platforms, etc. We accumulate these data and carefully select the most suitable keywords for optimization.

2. SEO should consider from the pre-construction stage

Many friends always think that SEO is just about ranking, similar to bidding, except it costs much less money. Indeed, as rumored online, adding external links and content can achieve a good ranking in a few ranking cycles when keyword competition is not intense. However, this ranking cannot be stable. Because our websites may not meet user needs. Therefore, we need to consider SEO from the beginning of website construction. Nowadays, many websites, especially medical sites, resemble small versions of 39 Health Network with plenty of content. But despite the abundance of content, much of it is not what users want. Moreover, numerous pop-up windows make it hard for users to stay. Such websites are useless no matter how good their rankings are.

3. Crazy pursuit of external links

Many external link personnel complain about their heavy workload, having to post a large number of links every day. Some even buy links for short-term benefits. Observing the workload of external link personnel and the prosperity of the link market, one can see how crazily SEOs pursue external links. However, have we ever thought about the original purpose of doing SEO? It's about optimizing the website to improve its search engine ranking, gain traffic, and convert traffic into orders. But if you only focus on external links, your website might still be terrible, which renders all efforts meaningless.

4. How to make SEO non-repetitive and non-mechanical

Step one: Do a good job of keyword mining and screening.

Step two: If it's a new site, first consider the layout of the website, including keyword layout, etc. If it's an old site, diagnose the website. Generally speaking, when taking over an old site, you need to do subtraction. Here, note that for medium and small-sized websites, the target keywords should not be too many, preferably not exceeding three. The number of long-tail keywords can be unlimited.

Step three: Add appropriate content to the website. What is appropriate content? Many websites, especially corporate websites, add articles like industry news or company news for optimization. Can such articles provide significant value to users? Definitely not! So we need to add appropriate content to our website. For example, if we are an underwear company website, there are two types of articles. One is "How to Choose Suitable Underwear," and the other is "Underwear Industry Sales Growth by 60%." Which article do you think would interest users more? Obviously, the former.

Step four: Perhaps this step will interest everyone - reasonable layout of the website's links. First is internal links. How should internal links be laid out? Currently, the most popular method might be adding internal link anchor texts at the keywords in the text. However, this approach greatly affects users' reading experience. We can add hot articles in the sidebar and related articles at the bottom of the article. This way, it doesn't affect readability while adding internal links.

Next is external links. Although with the changes in search engine algorithms, the importance of external links in ranking algorithms has gradually decreased, they are still an indispensable part. So how should we handle external links? Should we just sit in front of the computer and post links all day? Of course not!

External links can be divided into general weight external links and high-weight external links. General weight external links include forum signature anchor texts, blogs, and other common external link methods. Taking forum signatures as an example, many people say they are useless. Is this really true? Of course not! It's because we haven't mastered the techniques. We can't just go to a forum, add a personalized signature, and post without looking. After going to a forum, we first need to analyze what kind of posts attract user attention. For example, if a post has more than 150 clicks and more than 60 replies, we record it, find the common points, and extract the keywords. Then, we write posts around these keywords. Will our posts attract user attention? Will users click on the anchor text in our personalized signature? This way, aren't we increasing the value of our external links?

High-weight external links refer to cross-linking. The main purpose of cross-linking is to obtain one-way links, usually between the main site and our resource site. Cross-linking operations are too complex to detail here. Another type is friendship links. Friendship links are double-edged swords. Since they are mutual links, we must be cautious when handling them. How to get friendship links? You can use some friendship link platforms, such as GO9GO platform. We can directly look for suitable websites on this platform, ask the webmaster if they are willing to exchange, or post our friendship link information on the platform. From the author's experience, the quality of friendship links obtained from this platform is quite good. Those interested can try it. Also, note that if you have 10 friendship links and 4 of them lose authority, you shouldn't remove all of them at once, as this could cause excessive loss of external links, resulting in your website losing authority.

Step five: Do a good job of data segmentation and accumulation. This is very important for SEO, and I hope every SEO practitioner pays attention to it. During the SEO process, we need to record daily external link posting, content updates, inclusion changes, ranking changes, etc. In many companies, the person responsible for SEO is often alone. What should we do? We can use some webmaster tools to facilitate our work, such as Aizhan.com, which was the first to develop a Baidu weight query function. Many SEOs know this website. Through it, we can clearly see the changes in our Baidu weight, PR value changes, inclusion changes, etc., with quite detailed data, greatly facilitating our SEO work.
