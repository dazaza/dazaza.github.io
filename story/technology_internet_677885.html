[Qingdao Cuisine] Spicy and Sour Chinese Cabbage

by qssqwe215 on 2012-02-27 10:32:26

This is a sour and spicy cabbage dish that Miao Mom taught during the New Year [http://bbs.qdqss.cn/forum.php?mod=viewthread&tid=182532&page=1&extra=#pid1188516]. Even Miao Miao, who has always been afraid of eating cabbage ribs, happily finished it all. The method is extremely simple. Friends who have stock at home, give it a try!

Let's start with a picture of the finished product to whet your appetite.

After draining the water, use a spatula, then put cool water in the pot and bring it to a boil. Place the cabbage in the pot, and after 5-6 minutes, remove it and rinse thoroughly with cold water.

Ingredients: Cabbage hearts, sugar, rice vinegar, dried chili peppers, vegetable oil

Instructions:

1. Wash the cabbage hearts [http://bbs.qdqss.cn/forum.php?mod=viewthread&tid=182532&page=1&extra=#pid1188516]

2. After the cabbage hearts have cooled completely, drain the water, place them in a bowl, add white granulated sugar, pour in the rice vinegar, and sprinkle with dried chili peppers.

3. Pour oil into the pan, heat until smoking, then pour the hot oil over the prepared cabbage hearts, and you're done!

This sour and spicy cabbage heart dish [http://bbs.qdqss.cn/forum.php?mod=viewthread&tid=182532&page=1&extra=#pid1188516] is sour, sweet, spicy, crisp, and refreshing, making it especially suitable as a great dish to accompany drinks after having too much rich food!
