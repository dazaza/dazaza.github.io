Let him spoil you for a lifetime - Qzone Diary

"The most romantic thing I can think of is growing old with you slowly, until we are too old to go anywhere, and you still regard me as your treasure." Women often hum these lyrics from time to time. Yes, which woman doesn't want her man to love her for a lifetime? However, if you want him to spoil you, you need to learn some art of love first...

Being a Wife Like a Lover

Being a good wife is not difficult, and neither is being a good lover. But, making yourself like his lover while being his wife, making him miss and be addicted to you, does take some effort.

As Tender as a Lover

No man can escape the tender embrace of a woman. But don't just focus on being gentle; when he is behaving well, make sure to tell him about your recent thoughts at this moment, or ask for what you need right now. Men will never refuse such requests. Act cute and tell him that you want ice cream... But you also have to remember to bring him a cup of hot tea in his study when he's working overtime, or give him a bowl of noodles with a sunny-side-up egg. This will be more effective than doing any amount of housework.

Learning Some Good Dishes Just for Him

What we're talking about here isn't just general delicious food. You might not know how to make tomato and egg soup, but you must know how to make the braised pork that your husband likes. You don't need to know how to cook exotic delicacies, but you must know how to make a few home-style dishes that your husband enjoys. When he goes out, he will say that this is not as good as what his wife makes, and he will want to come back to you sooner. Even the way you learn recipes will leave an impression on him.

Having a Forever Youthful Heart for Beauty

It doesn't matter whether you are ugly or beautiful in appearance, but you should maintain the posture of a beautiful woman by his side. Remember, no matter how old you get, always dress up in front of the mirror and know the rules of dressing. Be clear about his style of clothing. Your pajamas and underwear should be carefully chosen because outerwear is for others to see, but sleepwear and underwear are specifically for your husband to see.

Being His Only Private Assistant

This doesn't mean you have to wash all the clothes in the house. Other clothes can be washed by a maid or someone else, but you must wash your husband's underwear and smelly socks, no matter how dirty or smelly they are. Make sure he changes into clean socks every day, and he should never wear torn socks. Polish each pair of his leather shoes until they shine. Ensure that the underwear he wears has been handled by you and carries your scent.

Satisfying His Desire to Conquer

There is much wisdom in the relationship between two people. A man hopes that his wife is a lady outside and passionate in bed, and this statement is almost true. Understand his nature for novelty, let him explore you for a lifetime, and always give him the feeling of conquest. Only then will he feel that you are a charming woman and he is a real man.

Giving Him a Reason to Love You

Regardless of how times change, pure and beautiful love still exists in this world. Although society provides many excuses for men's infidelity, I still believe that there are men who will always treat you as their treasured one. Believe that loving one person for a lifetime and lying next to one person to sleep is the happiest thing in the world. But the premise is that you give him a reason to deeply love you, learning to attract your man like a magnet.

Women are born to be loved and cherished by men. In the outside world, a man's vanity lies in possessing a beautiful and elegant woman.

There is no woman who doesn't yearn for a man's lifelong love, and no woman who doesn't wish for her family to be forever harmonious and happy. However, youth will fade, and younger women will always appear, especially if your man is mature and elegant, wealthy and gentle. It's inevitable that many women will swarm around him like butterflies. If you want a man to love you for a lifetime, you need to give him a reason to love you, even if it's as simple as the teasing messages suggest: either you are beautiful, or if you're not beautiful, then you must have charm; if you don't have charm, then you must have talent; if you don't have talent, then at least have a good character; if you don't have a good character, then at least be kind... In short, you must have one outstanding "brand" that keeps your husband forever infatuated with you.

Finally, I wish all the friends who read and reply to this post sweet love and happy marriage!
