Happiness is to spend a lifetime with the person you love

Happiness is to spend a lifetime with the person you love. Today, I suddenly want to tell everyone in the world: cherish! Cherish the person who loves you, the person who has always been protecting you and treating you like a treasure. That person is really precious. When he is around you, you don't even realize how much you actually love him. You might even think that all the care you get from him is something you deserve naturally, and rarely do you realize his importance. Sometimes you act willful and lose your temper with him, sometimes you feel tired, sometimes you feel lack of passion. But when there's no one around you nagging anymore, no one taking care of you, no one caring about your illness, you suddenly realize that you've lost the person you actually love the most.

Love is a long-lasting tug-of-war. From the time we first met to the time we got to know each other, then fell in love, we have embarked on a long-term tug-of-war. Two people who have had little or no intersection for 20 years or even longer inevitably argue and have differences. We are not originally one entity, but together because of love. However, love alone can't solve all the problems. Because our personalities are too strong, we love ourselves more, so we keep arguing, and after every argument, we find our own willfulness, yet it's hard to avoid being willful again next time.

If there were regret pills sold in this world, I believe everyone would want to have them, so that after each argument and cold war, we could take a pill and forget everything, start over again. It's such a pity that there are no such pills, and even if there were, taking regret pills all the time would also develop drug resistance. Every argument leaves a little hurt, which accumulates over time and becomes your burden and his烦恼. No one would remember a single small argument, but what about two? Three? Many? All the arguments eventually turn into grievances, and one day, they will explode.

We all have loved, I believe sincerely. Maybe some have been lost for a long time, but thinking about it now still causes pain. Because what he once gave you and what he finally took away from you are equally as much, surprisingly fair. No one owes anyone else, it's just that we didn't know how to cherish at that time. You might say, last time I didn't cherish, this time I will definitely do well, definitely cherish! But how to cherish?

Your lover, you need to understand and comprehend his heart, think about what he really needs. When you think about what he needs, you have already earned his respect. Your lover, you need to protect him wholeheartedly. Don't think that because he is stronger than you or a big man, you can't protect him. Your love is stronger than any encouragement, you are enough to protect the person you love. Your lover, you need to cherish him wholeheartedly. Remember in your heart what he does for you bit by bit, not only thank him, but often think about him. Being able to put aside his dignity to endure your bad temper, to get used to habits completely different from his, to take care of your time and routine, are all because of his love and sacrifice.

Before you lose your temper, please take a deep breath first, breathe deeply five times, and then exhale slowly five times. Then speak. When you have an opinion or problem with him, why not write a letter to tell him? Believe me, during the process of writing the letter, you will calm down first, and the originally depressed mood will be relieved. Sometimes you may not even be angry after finishing the letter. Because writing a letter requires wording and thought, making you calm down first. Communicating through letters reduces verbal conflicts and increases communication, allowing your problems to be solved in the most rational way, instead of replacing them with arguments or cold wars.

When you feel wronged, why not tell him directly what you think, let him know your real thoughts, what you actually want to do, and how you actually need him to do. Don't always let him guess your mind, because your thoughts are really hard to guess. Not all lovers have that sixth sense of mutual understanding. If he can't guess your thoughts, don't be angry, because you don't know how long he spent guessing your thoughts and how many brain cells he wasted.

When you're upset, why not tell him your troubles first? Maybe he doesn't have a good solution, but at least your lover will surely give you comfort. Sometimes he will bluntly tell you that you're wrong, don't be angry, he's doing it for your own good. He's just giving you guidance from the perspective of an observer. After you calm down, you may think his method is good. Even if it's not feasible, you should still empathize with his thoughts. On the contrary, when you notice some minor mistakes in him, care about him, pay attention to his eyebrows and mood. Understand him, and even if you can't understand, try to understand.

Before you make a decision, why not tell him your decision? This is a sign of respect for him. He may not agree, but he will surely be pleased because of your respect. If he happens to agree, congratulations, you have advanced your happiness a bit more. And when he makes a decision, don't interfere too much, give him as much space as possible. Believe me, although you two love each other, you are still complete individuals, so let him decide himself. Even if you don't agree, don't tell him when he looks excited, encourage him! Give him some advice later in a tactful way. When facing difficulties, don't underestimate your lover's ability, and don't blame him because of your own depression. He's not easy either, he faces difficulties just like you do, and he also has to consider your thoughts. Actually, he has it harder. Work hard together, believe that you two can solve anything together. Don't face difficulties alone, because no one wants their lover to face difficulties alone. It's equally important to go through hardships together as it is to enjoy happiness together. No matter what, believe that the two of you should be together.

When you feel that your love lacks passion, why not reflect on yourself first? What have you done for your relationship? Is the current dullness partly due to you? How long has it been since you last gave him a small gift? How long has it been since you kissed him without any reason? How long has it been since you chatted and talked with him? How long has it been since you invited him to dinner or to watch a movie? Don't always think that he doesn't love you enough or isn't as careful as before. Love matures, love grows up, unchanging love doesn't exist. Don't be too calculating, don't always calculate.

When you face temptation, why not calm down first and think about your past with him. He was once full of allure and attraction to you too. It's just that the passion has faded now. No one can stay passionate forever, no feeling can remain intense forever. The temptation you face now will eventually become calm one day. Just like you and your current lover. Moreover, if it were your lover facing such temptation, what would you hope he does? Do exactly what you hope he does.

Remember, take a deep breath first in any situation, don't rush, don't get excited. Matters between two people should be handled by the two people together.

From birth to now, we have never really learned how to love. We have gradually understood the meaning of love through reality and experience. I'm not writing these things to boast about myself. In fact, I am also a child who doesn't understand love. I just want to tell my brothers and sisters who have happiness now and are happy: cherish, protect, and care for your lover before you lose him. Also, I want to tell those who are looking for their own happiness and are on the path of finding it: after you find it, definitely cherish, protect, and care for your lover before you lose him...

Happiness is to spend a lifetime with a warm person. I sincerely hope that everyone has their own happiness!
