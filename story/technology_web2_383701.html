A Collection of 50 Interesting Websites Worth Bookmarking

by geekzhang on 2011-03-07 12:12:53

These 50 websites have been carefully selected for their interest. They can be entertaining when you're bored.

1、Worldwide Random Video Chat - Not Suitable for Minors

This site allows not only one-on-one chats but also video chats. After entering, wait a moment and click the Play button at the top of the webpage to connect randomly with an online user. Most are foreigners, though there are some Chinese users too. However, minors should be cautious as there are inappropriate individuals engaging in explicit content on camera. Of course, there are also many open-minded domestic and international beauties!

2、Creative Dominoes Online

Dominoes originated in China during the Song Dynasty and were later popularized by an Italian named Domino. As a child, I first played dominoes using military flags, just a straight line from start to finish, lacking creativity. Later, while neighbors built houses, we used bricks for more diverse and creative routes, though it wasted quite a few bricks...

3、Interactive Flash Sound-Controlled Ball

An interactive flash game where after entering, plug in your microphone because this animation is voice-controlled. When you speak or play music, the ball will jump around, creating a highly interactive experience!

4、Online Funny Video Editing - Hero Edition

This website, featured on the prestigious FWA, offers a video editing upgrade similar to previous image edits. Upload your photo to become the main character in a video featuring a female leader giving a speech: "What language truly deserves our trust? Who keeps our faith steadfast? Who ensures that weak voices are heard rather than silenced? I can tell you the answer! One person has done all this, deserving our gratitude."

5、Create Online GIF Animated Images

The site provides basic online GIF creation, allowing up to 10 images to be uploaded and combined into a GIF. I made a nice set of fireworks photos taken on Lantern Festival night into a GIF. The site also claims it can convert YouTube videos into GIFs, which is very powerful!

6、Web-Based Windows 3.1 Operating System

Windows 3.1 was familiar to those born in the 70s, remaining the preferred operating system until Windows 95 was released between 1992-1995. Programmer Michael Vincent recreated Windows 3.1 faithfully in web format, including common applications like Notepad, Calculator, Browser, and Media Player—all fully functional. Interestingly, the browser can even open modern web pages—amazing!

7、Speaking Custom Cartoon Avatars

This online avatar creator has a unique feature where avatars can produce sounds. Visitors clicking play will hear pre-set audio. These avatars can be placed on blogs, forums, or communities for personalization. Besides being avatars, they can also be used for introductions, confessions, pranks, etc., effectively speaking for you!

8、Re-edited Classic Bruce Lee Movie Clips - You Decide

Ah, Bruce Lee! This site features a flash titled "I Know Where Bruce Lee Lives," re-editing classic Bruce Lee movie clips and assigning them to keyboard keys. Pressing the keys displays corresponding clips, giving the feeling of being Bruce Lee yourself. Let's press the keys together while shouting "Ah!"

9、Simple and Practical Online Music Cards

Another highly practical site offering simple and effective music picture cards. Such card sites are available on major portals, but none match this site's distinct personality and simplicity. Cards made here exude sincerity!

10、Send Smoke Signals to Friends via Google Maps

Upon entering, the left menu offers options like map-based Tetris, custom smoke signals, Hollywood billboard pranks, building subtitles, alien fields, and Statue of Liberty pranks. Using the smoke signal option, enter an address (e.g., China Shanghai), find the exact location, input the smoke message, and obtain the link. Done!
