50 Attitudes of Cherishing Life for You to Bear in Mind

by jacklewman on 2008-02-14 08:48:51

1. Born for Love

Only love can make the world go round more smoothly; only love can create miracles.

Being able to see the good in others will enhance your own goodness;

being able to speak of the good in others will strengthen both them and yourself to be better.

Love is the original driving force of everything.

2. Be Your Own Heart Catcher

Prioritize realizing your own life’s purpose,

treat the inner child with kindness, giving it courage, confidence, and vitality,

miss yourself, be yourself, forgive yourself, take responsibility for yourself,

make good use of your feelings, act with passion, and live as your true self.

3. Simple Living

You don’t really need as much as you think,

anything extra that you have could be useful to someone else,

give it away or donate it for charity auctions so those who truly need it can benefit from it,

after getting used to a simple life, your existence will naturally feel less burdensome.

4. Hug Others, Let Others Hug You

A hug is a perfect gift, suitable for all ages,

and when you exchange it with others, no one will refuse it.

Practice using hugs instead of words to express your deepest emotions,

a timely hug conveys comfort and support, transmitting the vitality of life.

Hugging Therapy: For survival, four hugs per day; for maintenance, eight hugs per day; for healing, twelve hugs per day.
