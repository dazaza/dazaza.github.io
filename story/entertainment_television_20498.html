Meteor Garden Complete Series

by xiaopang on 2007-05-26 19:31:28

Xiazhi, a 24-year-old female fraudster, was granted parole. A woman who went to prison for love finally waited for this day to meet her lover Shisan after thirteen years. She even specially picked out gifts at a six-star hotel. Today was also the wedding of Ou Yaru, the former lover of E. SHINE Group's second young master Zhong Tianqi, and her brother Zhong Tianjun, as well as the auction of the mother's heirloom blue diamond "QUEEN MARY". To deliberately avoid the wedding, Zhong Tianqi was forcibly brought back from the airport by a monster and came to the stage. He sneaked into the backstage of the wedding and pulled Yaru into his arms. Tianqi kissed her while stealing QUEEN MARY... This was partly to take revenge on Yaru and partly because he didn't want his mother's legacy jewelry to become an auction item.

The loss of QUEEN MARY stopped the wedding from proceeding. By a strange twist of fate, the security guard mistook Yaru for a thief. Xiazhi caught Astar and handcuffed him, not wanting to involve the innocent Tianqi. Tianqi returned and pulled away Astar. In a moment of destiny, Astar took the blame for her lover and was just released on parole. Xiazhi and E-Shine Group's second young master "Zhong Tianqi", who operated a jewelry business, fled with their hands cuffed together, binding an unbreakable bond of affection.

On the run, Astar worried about Tianqi's disadvantage and, given her recent release from prison, feared being labeled as a jewel thief. She shouted at Tianqi: "Let me go right now..." and threatened Tianqi that she would call her boyfriend Shisan, thinking it would scare him off. Unexpectedly, the call was hung up in panic. In front of their old love nest, Astar couldn't find Shisan. Dragging the cuffed Tianqi along, they went to the landlord to get the address from the invitation. It turned out that Shisan had secretly planned to marry Meimei instead of Astar. Astar questioned Shisan while Shisan's wife Meimei gave birth prematurely. Kind-hearted Astar felt both embarrassed and angry, knowing that talking more would be futile as this man no longer had feelings for her. But she wouldn't lose face and said that Tianqi's friends were also about to get married, asking Shisan to bless them. On the same day, they both lost their loved ones and became companions in misfortune, drinking heavily.

Astar unlocked the handcuffs with a hairpin, a trick she learned in prison. Drunk as mud, Astar had to be half-carried and half-dragged into a hotel by Tianqi to rest. The hotel staff found it suspicious and suspected Tianqi of drugging and raping, so they reported him. Tianqi's family came to bail him out. Originally, Astar and Tianqi should have parted ways, but Tianqi was enlightened by Astar's words and decided to go to "Tomorrow Town" with her to seek ten thousand possibilities in life. Due to Tianqi's impulsiveness, the Zhong enterprise was in dire straits... and even caused a crisis where the board forced Tianjun to retrieve the lost blue diamond within a certain period... When the Zhong family was in chaos over the engagement banquet, a photo shocked Zhong Tianjun. Paparazzi took pictures of Yaru and his younger brother Tianqi kissing in the dressing room at the wedding banquet. The paparazzi demanded hush money, leaving Tianjun internally shaken as he never knew about the relationship between his younger brother and Yaru. Tianjun began to doubt who Yaru truly loved...

While doubting his fiancée Yaru, causing shock in Tianjun's heart, Tianqi and Astar boarded a bus heading to Tomorrow Town. These two strangers, tightly bound by chance, saw the seeds of love slowly sprouting. Ten thousand possibilities in life seemed about to unfold, but they didn't know that more tests were waiting ahead...
