Sanya Travel Beginner's Guide

If you just want to find a weekend time to bask in the sun for two days on the beach in Sanya, then you can skip this section and go straight to the following report. For first-time visitors to Sanya, or those who have previously traveled with a group and still remain confused about Sanya's situation after touring around, we will first outline an overall picture of Sanya travel for you.

Sanya City is located at the southernmost tip of Hainan Province (Hainan Island) in China. Here, it is summer all year round, and one could say that there is no frost or snow even in the coldest months, and flowers bloom all year round. It is known as the "Oriental Hawaii." Sanya tourism is mainly divided into three areas: Yalong Bay, Dadonghai, and Sanya Bay. Other bays include海棠Bay, Perfume Bay (at the boundary between Wanning and Lingshui).

Yalong Bay: Currently, it is the best holiday bay with the best quality of seawater and sand. It gathers the most top-end luxury hotels and is known as the "World's First Bay."

Dadonghai: The beach closest to the urban area of Sanya, with mostly four-star hotels. It is a good place to enjoy peace amidst the hustle and bustle.

Sanya Bay: Very close to the airport, convenient transportation, various star-rated hotels available, but not many top-end hotels.

海棠Bay, Perfume Bay, Shimei Bay: These bays are potential destinations for Sanya travel. Many luxury hotels are under construction. It is rumored that Li Ka-shing's investment in a 7-star hotel has already begun construction, but there is no need to check it out yet.

Wuzhizhou Island: A beautiful island that tourists must visit. You can go diving or camping here.

Xi Island: Another small island with a fishermen's village of over 4000 people who live by fishing. Although Xi Island may not be as beautiful as Wuzhizhou Island, it is more real and closer to nature.

Nantian Hot Spring, Garden Little Fish Hot Spring: Both are tropical rainforest hot springs, very close to Sanya City. Therefore, general tour groups usually include Nantian Hot Spring. In self-guided tour products, there are hot spring packages available, including tickets and round-trip transportation. Additionally, such hot spring travel packages can also be purchased at the hotel front desk.

Tianya Haijiao, Luhuitou, Nanshan Temple: Scenic spots with admission fees, which are quite expensive.
