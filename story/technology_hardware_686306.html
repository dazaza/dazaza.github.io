Emperor Yan: A filial son of the post-80s generation moves netizens by taking his octogenarian mother to work

by sznstejx3q3 on 2012-03-02 08:21:10

Yesterday, a video titled "80s-born Filial Son from Shanxi Works with His 80-year-old Mother" went viral on major forums and Weibo, sparking heated discussions among netizens. Many netizens said, "It almost brought tears to my eyes, this time I was truly moved," and praised, "It's rare for the post-80s generation to have such filial sons, who express sincere feelings in a simple manner, truly a role model for young people!"

The video: An 80s-born filial son works with his 80-year-old mother. Source: Sina Blog.

The filial son: "You must accept my mother if you want to hire me."

Reporters found out that the video was first posted by a netizen named "I'm on the road" at around 10 PM on the 20th on Sina Video. Initially, it did not attract much attention from netizens. Yesterday noon, after being forwarded by netizen @Paike Weishi (Weibo), it quickly triggered a large number of forwards and comments from netizens. By 5:30 PM yesterday, the video had been clicked 7,081 times, with 183 netizen comments.

Yesterday afternoon, Southern Daily reporters contacted the netizen "I'm on the road." He confirmed that the video was filmed by him on the 19th and uploaded to Sina.com on the evening of the 20th. He explained, "The reason for filming this video is also because I was deeply moved by his filial piety, uploading it online is hoping more netizens can share this kind of touching moment around them."

According to "I'm on the road," the "80s-born filial son from Shanxi" in the video is named Li Jie. After checking his ID, it was confirmed that Li Jie was born in 1981, "Although he looks quite old, he is indeed a standard 80s-born person." It is understood that Li Jie is from Guxiao Township, Lingchuan County, Shanxi Province, currently working as a chef in a stewed noodles restaurant in Huixian City, Henan Province. Currently, Li Jie lives with his over 80-year-old mother, working while taking care of his elderly mother.

Li Jie introduced that he is the youngest in the family, with seven older sisters. The fourth sister has mental problems, the fifth sister is mute, and the other sisters' families are not well off either. Naturally, the responsibility of taking care of his elderly mother fell on his shoulders. Since 2007, Li Jie wanted to go out and try his luck, but he couldn't bear leaving his sick mother behind. After careful consideration, he decided to take his mother to look for work. "You must accept my mother if you want to hire me!" This is Li Jie's only condition for finding a job.

Current situation: No economic income, wants to apply as a chef

Li Jie introduced that initially, he ran around with his mother to several recruitment units, but none of them were willing to hire him, thinking he had a "burden" and couldn't focus on work. However, Li Jie did not give up because of this. His filial piety finally moved his current boss, Li Qing Shun, who even let Li Jie and his mother live in his house. Li Qing Shun said in an interview with netizen "I'm on the road," "Li Jie is very filial, I admire him."

In the video, reporters saw that Li Jie and his mother lived in one room. He widened his mother's bed by over a foot, which became his sleeping place. Li Jie introduced, "This way, it won't cause any trouble to the boss's family, and also, if my mother needs anything at night, I can know immediately." It is understood that Li Jie used to earn 1,700 yuan per month, part of which was used to pay for his mother's medical treatment, and the rest was saved for getting married.

Netizen "I'm on the road" also introduced, "Li Jie's filial piety earned him good relationships. A customer, after learning about Li Jie's care for his mother, introduced him to a girlfriend, and now they are almost getting married." Li Jie's girlfriend, when interviewed by local media, said, "I am willing to be with him, not for anything else, but because of his filial piety. A person who is so filial to his mother will surely be good to me and my family in the future."

Southern Daily reporters latest learned that the restaurant where Li Jie currently works has been demolished, leaving him without any economic income, increasing Li Jie's pressure. Li Jie expressed that his ability is limited, unable to provide his mother with good food, clothing, and shelter, but he just wants to do his best to make her happy during her lifetime. Finally, Li Jie also released "job information" in the video: "If anyone needs a chef and allows me to bring my mother along, I will work hard."
