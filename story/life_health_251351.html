As an online dealer, Beiguo Shopping Network undoubtedly faces the greatest challenge with counterfeits running rampant on other websites! Moreover, the genuine Wenlishou has "MADE IN FRANCE" written on the bottom of the hard outer packaging.

After opening the hard packaging, looking at the soft packaging as a whole, the genuine "Wenlishou" packaging printing is much clearer, and the colors are more realistic. (Note: the genuine Wenlishou's outer packaging color leans towards yellow, while the fake Wenlishou's outer packaging color leans towards red, and the fake Wenlishou's outer packaging printing is relatively blurry, whereas the genuine Wenlishou is clear and straightforward.)
