Chen Sisi's Weibo post solicits names for Bo Jie's educational new book; netizens actively respond.

by edivista on 2011-12-16 21:39:24

Click to enter Chen Sisi's Weibo

Recently, famous young singer and deputy director of the PLA Second Artillery Corps Art Troupe, Chen Sisi, solicited names for her sister Hu Bo's new book on Weibo, sparking heated discussions among netizens.

It is reported that Chen Sisi's sister Hu Bo is an emotional psychology expert and also an outstanding educational psychological counselor. She has very unique insights into how to handle the relationship between parents and children. She spent more than twenty years deeply engaging with tens of thousands of parents and children, using all available time to communicate with them, conducting in-depth research, and using her original contract education theory to solve thousands of various family education cases. She has successfully held several educational lectures across the country, and is deeply loved by many parents. She is affectionately known as the first practical educator of family education in China by a wide range of parents and media. She is currently a professional psychological counselor for a cultural education group in Beijing. This new book is her painstaking masterpiece after years of research. The book almost covers all common problems and conflicts that worry and trouble both parents and children, and provides effective solutions. She is also speaking as a mother, imparting unique and innovative educational concepts to a wide range of parents. Therefore, taking into account the opinions of the publisher and others, the book title remains undecided between "Heart-wrenching Parents, Troubled Children" and "Good Parents Make Good Children." (Click on the picture to enter Chen Sisi's Weibo)

Chen Sisi expressed on Weibo that her sister Hu Bo is now troubled by the book title every day, and as a Weibo influencer, she initiated a name solicitation on Weibo, hoping to gather the wisdom of netizens and brainstorm a good name for her sister's new book. So far, netizens have been actively participating, and some enthusiastic netizens have also provided different book titles for reference.
