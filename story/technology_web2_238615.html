My Date with a Zombie III: Eternal Realm

by h9169755 on 2009-09-16 10:38:02

It is said that in modern society, one legend disappears every day. Some say it's because modern parents are too busy to tell stories to their children, while others say it's because modern people no longer believe in legends, nor do they believe that the heroes in these legends ever existed. However, regardless of whether people believe or not, and despite how fast legends may disappear, the legends about Ma Xiaoling, Kuang Tianyou, and others will continue to be passed down forever. Because they not only truly existed, but also left laughter, tears, and unforgettable touching stories in this world.

In a certain year, Ma Xiaoling, Kuang Tianyou, and their group of friends successfully prevented Nüwa from destroying the world. This was a legend that broke the shackles of fate, allowing the world to enjoy three years of peace. But would fate really just let things rest? Up until today, in another year, everyone thought this legend had disappeared. But fate once again arranged for the legend to be reborn, placing the responsibility of saving the world back on Ma Xiaoling and Kuang Tianyou. The Jade Pool Immortal Mother, the last goddess in the world, for the sake of her lover Fuxi, the King of Humans, has finally returned to earth with plans to launch a new world-ending action, hoping to use this to force Fuxi back into her embrace. But at this moment, the King of Humans no longer loves the Immortal Mother; he only wishes to spend his final days on earth with the woman he loves most. Consumed by jealousy, the Immortal Mother ignites a crisis that threatens the end of the world.

Is this the fate of the world, the fate of Xiaoling and Tianyou, or the fate of the gods? Regardless of the answer, it proves that everything is controlled by fate. After saving the world in a certain year, Tianyou and Xiaoling could have lived happily ever after in the Pangu Holy Land. But the Immortal Mother, in order to win back the King of Humans, used the Red Snow virus to wipe out the entire Pangu clan. In his dying moments, Tianyou used his last strength to send Xiaoling back to the human world, urging her to stop the Immortal Mother from destroying the world. Sent away with a heavy burden, Tianyou's survival remains uncertain. Xiaoling can only set off on her journey alone once more. During this time, Xiaoling never believed that Tianyou was dead. With this belief, she continued to move towards the goal of stopping the world's destruction, even traveling through time to return to the Song Dynasty to bring back Tianyou's past-life arrowhead to strengthen the power of salvation. Later, Xiaoling reunited with her childhood friend Mao You and trained together to catch ghosts. At this point, a mysterious young girl suddenly appeared, revealing herself to be Xiaoling's future daughter, Kuang Tianya. By chance, both mother and daughter also reunited with Xiaoling's parents, Ma Dalong and Tang Jinbao, and her brother Ma Xiaotiger, allowing Xiaoling to enjoy family happiness for the first time in her life.

At this moment, Tianyou unexpectedly reappeared and reunited with Xiaoling. Just as everything seemed to be improving, fate played tricks on Xiaoling once again. Chang'e, the old love of Tianyou and the King of Humans, returned, not only intensifying the resentment between the King of Humans and the Immortal Mother but also bringing an unknown virus that even the virus expert Immortal Mother couldn't handle. In the end, the Immortal Mother and the King of Humans worked together to solve the virus problem, painfully sending Chang'e far beyond the nine heavens. But they discovered that behind all this manipulation was actually fate. When everyone realized that fate was the greatest enemy, the Immortal Mother and the King of Humans suddenly decided to go to war. As they were about to destroy the world, Xiaoling, Tianyou, and others ultimately decided to join the battle to prevent the great war. Finally, the battle erupted. Before the world was destroyed, everyone finally managed to fight the Immortal Mother to near death. At this moment, fate possessed the Immortal Mother. The King of Humans then revealed that the mission of the Pangu clan was to eliminate fate, and everything done before was part of a trap to capture fate. In the end, everyone engaged in the final battle with the fate-possessed Immortal Mother. Can the combined efforts of humans and gods truly defeat fate, freeing the world from its control?
