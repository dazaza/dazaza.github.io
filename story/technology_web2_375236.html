Previously, I summarized the excellent start-ups of 2010 that I introduced on my blog this year, which was quite popular. Recently, an event organized by our Chinamode, where users nominate and expert judges review, has gone through three weeks, and the companies that have made it to the second stage of voting have now been announced. Among them are some very outstanding internet/mobile internet start-ups. You can vote for your favorite company in each category on the Chinamode homepage.

Chinamode official start-up summary page: http://chinamode.org/vote-list/

Voting page: http://chinamode.org/

One, Annual Application Innovation in Lifestyle Consumption

Meituan, Lashou, Nuomi.com, Taofang.com, Kuukie, Taotaosou, Booksfly.net, Mafengwo, Wacai365, Tucia

Meituan is an online group-buying service website. Its main feature is offering a daily deal on premium consumption (such as restaurants, bars, KTVs, SPAs, hair salons, yoga studios, etc.), then selling this service to users at a lower group-buying price. As long as the minimum number of group buyers is reached, they can enjoy the lower price, and obtain coupons via printing, downloading, or sending text messages, and use these coupons for consumption.

Link: http://www.meituan.com/

Lashou's group-buying service currently covers more than 100 cities, recommending high-quality local life services every day.

Link: http://www.lashou.com/

Nuomi.com is the group-buying website under Qianxiang Interactive, dedicated to recommending high-discount local premium lifestyle services to consumers through group-buying.

Link: http://www.nuomi.com/

Taofang.com is a second-hand housing website providing a Taobao-style home buying experience, belonging to Beijing Lexin Chuangzhan Technology Co., Ltd. Taofang.com mainly engages in the integration of second-hand house buying and leasing information services.

Link: http://www.taofang.com

Taotaosou (Sootougou), China's first visual-based product image search engine. Through picture uploads, picture viewing, and picture-to-picture searches, you can quickly and accurately find your desired target products.

Link: http://www.taotaosou.com/

BooksFly.net is an online platform providing an online book rental service (physical books). You can choose your favorite books on this platform, pay a certain fee, and read the book within a specified time period before returning it (they provide courier pick-up and delivery). Personally, I think this is a great service; it reduces the cost of reading on one hand, and better utilizes these book resources on the other.

Link: http://www.booksfly.net/

Mafengwo is a travel-sharing community where you can exchange information, share travels, help friends here, or get help. You can also exchange strategies, food, music, photography diaries, and various things related to travel.

Link: http://www.mafengwo.cn/

Wacai365 is a mass accounting platform developed by Hangzhou Caimei Technology Co., Ltd. The platform service is divided into two parts: mobile software and websites. Currently, it provides Android, Windows Mobile, and Java versions of the mobile application client.

Link: http://www.wacai365.com/

Kuukie is a website providing online customization services for card-type products (Mini cards [half the size of traditional business cards], personalized cards), with orders starting from one box. You can customize various personalized cards through Kuukie.

Link: http://www.kuukie.com/

Tucia is an online photo editing, professional photo compositing, free photo beautification community with over 6,000 real designers providing artificial online photo processing and photo beautification. You can also choose confidential, quick, advanced paid design services.

Link: http://cn.tucia.com/

Two, Annual Social, Communication, and Information Application Innovation

Sina Weibo, Wumii, Dongxi, Mtime, Guokr, Tencent Weibo, Caing, Xiangguo, Dajie, Umiwi

Sina Weibo is a microblogging service website launched by Sina, similar to Twitter. Users can post messages or upload pictures via web pages, WAP pages, mobile text/SMS.

Link: http://t.sina.com.cn

Dongxi is a community of translation and original enthusiasts, adhering to the concept of "building a bridge between East and West, presenting valuable content," introducing fresh views and values from foreign media to Chinese readers.

Link: http://dongxi.net

Mtime is China's most professional movie and TV drama database, including news, trailers, posters, stills, and popular reviews, while also providing movie information inquiries, blogs, albums, and group services, making it the best movie community for film fans.

Link: http://www.mtime.com/

Wumii is a socialized news sharing and recommendation website. Wumii uses artificial intelligence technology to find content you're interested in from numerous web pages based on the pages you like.

Link: http://www.wumii.com/

Guokr is a general science-themed website founded by Ji Shisan, known for the popular science blog "Science Squirrels." It carries light social elements, launching theme channels such as Fun Science, DIY Science, Health, Psychology, and Pet Love, covering a wide range of topics and interest points related to technology for urban youth.

Link: http://www.guokr.com/

Tencent's microblogging service website, where existing QQ numbers can be used directly to open accounts, providing iPhone, Android, and other mobile clients.

Link: http://t.qq.com/

Caing.com (Caing) and "The New Century" weekly ("New Century Weekly") both belong to Caixin Media. Caing.com is a financial news information website, integrating news, opinions, multimedia, interactive, and other rich media products of the information age.

Link: http://www.caing.com/

Xiangguo Live Broadcast is Xiangguo's microblogging service, integrating its RSS content and microblogging format.

Link: http://bo.xianguo.com/

Dajie Network is based on real-name systems, committed to creating a true, efficient, and interactive job-seeking/recruitment platform for users. Dajie innovatively applies traditional SNS to the recruitment field, providing Web2.0-based real, accurate, efficient, and reliable two-way对接撮合services, allowing users' time and relationships to precipitate on the Internet and create practical GDP, leading the entire recruitment industry towards a high-precision, high-efficiency value revolution.

Link: http://www.dajie.com/

Umiwi (umiwi.com) is a network television platform created by famous producer Wang Lifen, serving the country and knowledge groups. It mainly includes several columns such as On the Road, Network Television, Innovative China, Entrepreneurship Clinic, and Fen Enjoyment Moments.

Link: http://www.umiwi.com/
