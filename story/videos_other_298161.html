What kind of store should I open in 2010 to make money?

by bwmei5750 on 2009-12-07 18:34:21

Food is the first necessity for the people. The 1.4 billion Chinese "feel hungry if they don't eat for a day," and therefore, the catering industry remains the most promising popular entrepreneurial industry in 2009. In addition, due to its lowest investment threshold and strongest explosive power, it has always been very popular among entrepreneurs. Catering industry experts analyze that at this stage, the most lucrative projects in the catering business are breakfast shops, leisure beverage shops, and Chinese characteristic snack shops. Breakfast shops and leisure beverage shops have the advantages of low cost, high profit, and fast return. The average investment cost is between 20,000-100,000 yuan, with an average net profit margin of 25%-35%, and generally, the investment can be recovered within 6-9 months. With the steady development of China's economy and the acceleration of the pace of life, currently, in a few developed cities such as Shanghai, Beijing, and Shenzhen, the service-diversified convenience store industry has developed rapidly, and even some convenience stores have daily turnover reaching over ten thousand yuan. However, looking at China's entire convenience store industry, it is still only in its take-off stage. Various signs indicate that service-diversified convenience stores will be the focus of retail industry development in the next stage for most regions in China. Currently, in first-tier cities like Shanghai, Beijing, Guangzhou, and second-tier cities like Shenzhen, Suzhou, Wuxi, Hangzhou, where the population exceeds 5 million, the per capita GDP has already surpassed 3000 USD. This means that the above-mentioned cities have the large environment necessary for large-scale development of convenience stores, and the market space is very large.

As the knowledge level and income level of Chinese people improve simultaneously, everyone is paying more attention to the concept of health. Therefore, pharmacies and health food stores, which are closely related to people's health, are all good directions for entrepreneurship with promising financial prospects. From the perspective of investment thresholds, the investment threshold for comprehensive pharmacies or traditional Chinese medicine stores averages over 500,000 yuan, with a gross profit margin of about 35% and a longer investment recovery period, suitable for entrepreneurs with more substantial funds for long-term development. Health food stores are divided into natural health beverage stores and health food catering stores that emphasize health preservation and dietary therapy, with the latter having higher opening costs, averaging between 400,000-600,000 yuan. On the other hand, the investment threshold for health beverage stores is relatively lower, with average opening costs of approximately 100,000-150,000 yuan. Once successful, it will be the fastest money-making segment within the health concept industry.

"Beauty lies in the eyes of the beholder, and humans rely on clothing." Today's Chinese people all possess the desire for beauty, thus creating enormous business opportunities in the apparel industry across all cities in the country. From the perspective of personal investment and entrepreneurship, starting a business in the apparel industry can involve either establishing an apparel processing plant or studio for garment production, with the key point being that the design must keep up with trends; otherwise, it won't have strong competitiveness. Alternatively, one can engage in the market sales of clothing, either through wholesale or retail. For entrepreneurs with limited capital, opening a clothing retail store to accumulate initial funds is the first step toward success. Entrepreneurs with a certain amount of capital can open small sales companies to act as sales agents for clothing manufacturers or enter the clothing wholesale market.
