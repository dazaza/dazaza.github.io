Nowadays, modern online education has become the preferred method for in-service personnel in society to improve their academic qualifications and enhance their skills due to its flexible teaching methods, and no restrictions on learning time and location, which is convenient for in-service personnel to choose and study at any time. Currently, there are 17,000 students enrolled in correspondence courses and online tutoring in our region, making it an important avenue for adult education with the number of students increasing year by year. Our region has a total of 62 correspondence stations and off-campus learning centers of universities outside the region, relying on 32 host institutions. By 2020, the gross enrollment rate of higher education in the entire region will approach 40%, and adult education mainly based on correspondence and online learning will achieve faster development.

The characteristics of online education determine that distance learning is mainly self-study, with most of the students' learning time being loose from teachers and classmates, without a classroom or its atmosphere. These features can make many students who have just started distance learning encounter some difficulties or feel uncomfortable. Therefore, competing on educational quality and providing refined services have become the core competitiveness of online education.

The concept of "non-degree" has been around in the education industry for a long time. It not only refers to what people commonly call "vocational training," but also includes various learning methods and approaches involved in building a lifelong learning society. In an era where "everyone learns, learning can happen anywhere, and learning can occur anytime," online education methods will permeate into everyone's daily life.

Most students participating in online education are adults, whose motivations for obtaining an online education diploma vary widely, but generally include improving academic qualifications, enhancing skills, recharging while working, and expanding career opportunities.
