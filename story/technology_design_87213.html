Production and Installation Techniques for Large Outdoor Advertising Signs

by violet0807 on 2008-08-25 17:08:53

Production and Installation Techniques for Large Outdoor Advertising Signs - The production of outdoor advertising can be said to encompass a wide range of techniques, with increasingly specialized divisions. This article provides a detailed introduction to the production and installation techniques of large outdoor advertising signs (Bao Li cloth + external lighting).

I. Framework Section

1. Confirm design drawings, which should include:

- Structure and dimensions of the building where the advertisement is located

- Foundation structure

- Base position

- Material selection and dimensions

- Welding component methods

- Methods for binding the picture and spare parts

- Lighting bracket and installation instructions

- Rust-proofing and waterproofing instructions

- Construction instructions

- Name of the design unit

- Engineer's signature or design institute stamp

Matters needing attention during construction:

- Maintain contact with the construction unit and the advertising company to understand the progress and situation of the construction

- Visit the construction site as much as possible

- If differences between construction and design are found, immediately follow up to understand the reasons

- Notify the management department of the construction situation

2. Acceptance:

- Picture

- Completion dimensions of the board

- Whether the board is straight

- Base position

- Materials and dimensions used

- Welding quality

- Conduit wiring, connections, power supply, distribution

- Lighting installation

- Rust-proofing and waterproofing

- Problem report + completion report

- Photos

II. Picture Section

1. Characteristics of different picture productions:

Screen printing, Computer spray painting, Exposure technology

Adaptability: Indoor and outdoor / Outdoor / Indoor

Durability: Synthetic paper and instant stickers are suitable for short-term displays (used in short-term event promotions), film and flexible fabrics are more suitable for long-term displays / Suitable for long-term displays / Suitable for long-term displays

Color resolution: Better than general large computer spray paintings, but due to screen restrictions, not applicable for large advertisements / Low resolution, thus often used for large advertisements requiring a certain viewing distance (minimum 10 feet) / High fidelity, often used for indoor lightboxes

Time sensitivity: Faster production period, suitable for mass production of the same pattern, mass printing can also reduce costs / Generally longer production period / Longer production period

2. Picture quality requirements:

- Correct materials

- Correct colors

- Clear content

- Accurate dimensions

- Precise processing

- Smooth and secure installation

3. Improving the installation effect of externally lit pictures:

- All externally lit pictures have conduits sewn on for convenient installation

- When installing externally lit pictures, it is best not to use copper eyelets but iron pipes instead, making the binding force more even

- Replace nylon straps and ropes with tightening accessories that have a tightening function to effectively tighten the picture and open wrinkles in one go

III. Precautions for selecting advertising locations - Feasibility

- Overall quality of the building where the advertising location is located

- Whether the building where the advertising location is located can bear the self-weight of the framework and the load caused by the advertisement

- Age and structural materials of the building where the advertising location is located

- Whether the framework can be raised to avoid the advertisement being blocked and the normal installation of lights being restricted (e.g., the bottom line of the advertisement is higher than the parapet wall)

- Whether there is sufficient electricity

- Whether capacity can be increased

IV. Lighting section

1. Lighting quality requirements:

- Appropriate and sufficient brightness

- Evenly projected light onto the advertising surface

- Effectively showcasing the original color and content of the picture

- Lights turning on and off according to the scheduled time

2. Lighting material guidelines:

- Reliable and durable

- Not affected by climate changes

- Rust-proof and waterproof

- Voltage at 220 volts or 380 volts

- Equipped with a primary voltage stabilizer (-20%+20%)

- Convenient installation

- Spare parts available

3. Lighting installation guidelines:

- Refer to the manual provided by the manufacturer

- Obtain a lighting analysis report from the manufacturer or general agent

- Determine based on actual conditions and requirements

- Accumulate experience

4. Fluorescent lamp installation guidelines for lightboxes:

- Spacing between fluorescent tubes no more than 20 cm

- Distance between the surface of the fluorescent tube and the picture no less than 20 cm

- Overlapping length of fluorescent tubes no less than 15 cm

- Use electronic transformers

- Requirement for parallel straight-line arrangement of fluorescent tubes

5. Electricity requirements:

- For general signs under 15 meters high, average power consumption per square meter is estimated at 40 watts

- For wall paintings between 15 meters and 40 meters high, average power consumption per square meter is estimated at 45 watts

- Average power consumption per square meter for lightboxes

- Average power consumption per square meter for neon sign advertisements is estimated at 450 watts
