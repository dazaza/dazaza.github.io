Introduction to the 2008 MapQuest Edition of China Electronic Map:

===========================================================

With the support and help of many friends, I have finally obtained the latest version of "China Electronic Map 2007" by Lingtu. This software is the official release version, so it is recommended that after you experience it, please delete it immediately and purchase the genuine version for use (to combat piracy).

For those who support Lingtu Company, there's another good way to sponsor them: You don't need to spend money, but simply book hotels or flights through the new features provided by this software, which is the greatest support for Lingtu.

Alright, let's get back on topic. The new features of the "China Electronic Map 2008 Edition":

1. Public transportation data, navigation data, national map data, and train data are all from the latest version as of December 2007;

2. Improved functions such as public transport inquiry and driving travel;

3. Added the "Air Ticket and Hotel Inquiry" function;

4. Removed the "Comprehensive List of Chinese Attractions" function; (already added in the MapQuest edition)

5. Added a "Restricted Registration Number Common Function, Limited to 10 Uses," so you don't need to worry. If your registration number exceeds 10 uses, there will be a prompt saying "Your version serial number has exceeded the maximum installation quantity, please repurchase." After clicking OK, you can continue using it;

Usage Instructions:

1. After downloading, unzip and run directly: mapchina2008\bin\MapChina.exe (You can create a desktop shortcut)

2. If you want to use the "Comprehensive List of Chinese Attractions," copy the two folders: Travel1, Travel2 under the directory

mapchina2007\Data\template\special

to the directory mapchina2008\Data\template\special

Then start the program, and you will see the "Comprehensive List of Chinese Attractions" function. (Thanks to Major Hong Feng from [MapQuest] for his support!)

3. Strongly recommended: After downloading, do not close immediately. Keep the seed service running for a while.

Promote the MapQuest spirit: Help others, and others will help you!
