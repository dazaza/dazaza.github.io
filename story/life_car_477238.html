Analysis of Car Beauty Franchise Process

Franchise Diagram Explanation:

The general procedure for car beauty franchise is as follows:

1. Franchise Consultation: Inquire about franchise matters through phone, fax, or online messages to the headquarters and obtain relevant detailed materials;

2. Franchise Application: Fill out the franchise application form and confirm the franchise intention;

3. Field Investigation: Investors visit the headquarters for an on-site project investigation and engage in business exchanges with headquarters staff;

4. Qualification Review: The headquarters reviews various documents provided by the franchisee to confirm their franchise qualifications;

5. Contract Signing: After both parties confirm the results of the investigation without error, they officially sign the franchise contract after friendly negotiations;

6. Payment of Fees: The franchisee pays the corresponding franchise fee and other fees according to the chosen franchise type;

7. Skill Training: Investors undergo systematic technical, management, and marketing training at the headquarters, where accommodation is arranged by the headquarters. The training content includes theoretical learning and practical operations, and after passing the graduation examination, operation is approved;

8. Store Decoration: The headquarters assists the franchisee in selecting a store location locally and provides decoration plans and supervises the decoration;

9. Opening Preparation: Obtain authorization letters, opening gifts, marketing guidance manuals, and other materials for relevant opening guidance; after logistics distribution is completed, equipment installation and debugging are carried out, goods are shelved, and preparations for opening are made;

10. Operation Guidance: When the franchise store opens, the headquarters dispatches professional technicians and store managers to assist the franchise store in opening and tracks operational guidance and business planning to ensure normal operation of the franchise store.
