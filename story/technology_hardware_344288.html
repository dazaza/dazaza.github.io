Dunk High: A Unique Way of School Fundraising

by mh155544 on 2010-04-12 20:35:20

This organization makes it easy for teachers, administrators, or PTA members to create fundraisers for schools. Donors become community partners that can help teachers in various other ways. Most importantly though, they help teachers outfit the classroom with the adequate resources and materials for teaching. Not having these resources adversely affects their capacity to teach the students. Unfortunately, teachers spend an average of $1,200 of their own money every school year which adds up to $4 billion annually. These fundraisers for schools are a blessing to teachers and students alike. According to studies, having hands-on learning materials available for instruction improves students' learning abilities by increasing their enthusiasm for learning.

You can also take part by launching a program about fundraisers for schools. You can use Adopt-A-Classroom’s website to help schools you care about. When you launch a program, you will be given various resources to inform the local community about this program and help in creating awareness of fundraisers for schools. You can also work in teams with other people who have the same passion for helping teachers and students. By adopting a class, you become a community partner and are capable of building a relationship for future years to come. With each passing year, you will get to help a different class and can stay in touch with the same teacher thus creating a meaningful partnership. When you adopt a classroom, you can donate a certain amount as an individual and you can inquire about whether your company is willing to match your donation. The teacher has the authority to purchase whatever material is required for the classroom. However, this money cannot be used for any kind of field trips. You can find a classroom on the Adopt-A-Classroom website which allows you to donate to any classroom of your choice by geography, name of school, teacher’s name or other search criteria. If you cannot find a classroom to adopt, the website can always help you identify a classroom which has not been previously adopted by anyone else.

If you have ever been interested in helping out young children and students, there are several actions you can take. There are a number of fundraisers for schools but Adopt-A-Classroom has devised a unique way for fundraising. They invite donors to join the community by providing funds and support to the teachers and their students. Donors can also help to develop self-esteem amongst the students. A contribution in any amount creates a meaningful difference in a student’s life. To take part in various fundraisers for schools, Adopt-A-Classroom gives you the opportunity to interact personally with the teacher and the student. The founder of this organization is James Rosenberg, an attorney by profession who realized the need for provision of resources in classrooms to make the students more apt in learning.
