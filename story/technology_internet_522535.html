Anhui Chongyang armed minister suspected of fleeing after running over a girl (Picture)

by hj1718004 on 2011-10-26 18:59:59

○Incident site and the spherical panoramic camera above it. ○A netizen exposed this traffic accident on the Zongyang website. On May 21, in the "Zongyang Fax" forum of the Zongyang website, a netizen posted that the "official second generation" as a civil servant in the county drove over a girl on May 20 and fled, and attached his father's official title and name. After the post was sent out, it aroused the attention of many netizens. For a time, there were voices of condemnation and blame, and some people came to post to ease the situation, all of which were condemned by the world. The local netizens have been waiting for the relevant "processing results". The reporter immediately interviewed the much-anticipated traffic accident. Netizen exposure "Official second generation" drives over a girl and flees On the evening of May 21, the reporter saw on the Zongyang website forum that the netizen "Phoenix Hermit" said in the post: My home is near the Zongyang Theater. Yesterday afternoon, I arrived at the scene in the first place. The incident happened in the open space in front of the theater. When I arrived, the little girl was already lying on the ground, with blood flowing from her nostrils and covered in dust. After the family lifted the girl's clothes, they saw obvious tire marks from her left leg to her right shoulder, but the trouble-making car had disappeared. The little girl was immediately sent to the county hospital for rescue. About ten minutes later, the 110 police car arrived. The police questioned the boss, employees of the hotel operating in the theater, and the surrounding residents, all of whom claimed not to have noticed. Only one lady saw the incident process. It was a white SUV that ran over the girl, but the police did not take evidence from this lady. Later, someone suggested to the police to check the surveillance video of the theater, and the police said the video equipment had already failed. The post also said: Today, I learned that last night someone in the little girl's home found the suspect, who brought cash to the hospital. The little girl's family refused to accept it and wanted to pursue the suspect's fleeing responsibility. However, in the morning, a person who claimed to be the suspect's friend came to the theater and asked "Who saw him run over the person?" "He has been driving for more than ten years. He knows when he runs over small stones. How could he not know if he runs over a person?" He also said "We will invite relevant experts to conduct an investigation" "However, whether or not it was him who ran over the person, he will cure this girl and fulfill humanitarian spirit." At 3 p.m., a netizen named "Phoenix Mountain Man" also posted the official position and full name of this "official second generation" as promised. Reporter Investigation Twin sisters playing by the theater, the older sister got run over On May 22, the reporter went to the scene of the incident, No. 32 Zhengda Street, Zongyang Theater in the county. According to the owner of a hair salon in the middle, the injured girl is a pair of twin sisters, and the one who got run over is the older sister "Keke". Due to serious injuries, she was transferred to Anqing Municipal Hospital for treatment. The owner said that the incident happened around 5 p.m. that day. At the time, the girl's grandmother was taking care of her elder daughter's child in the hair salon, allowing the 4-year-old twin sisters of her younger daughter to play on the open space in front of the nearby theater. After the older sister "Keke" was run over by the car, the younger sister "Lele" saw her injured sister and hurriedly ran to call grandma, saying that her sister was run over by the car. Grandma, seeing the unconscious child with blood flowing from her nostrils, was frightened and quickly called the 120 emergency vehicle to send the child to the county hospital for rescue, and then transferred her to Anqing for treatment. When the reporter was looking for the girl's family locally, he met "Keke's" grandmother, who told the reporter that Keke was her youngest son's daughter. The couple both work in Beijing and just rushed back yesterday. The granddaughter is still not out of danger. She angrily said, my two sons also drive cars. You didn't intentionally run over the child, but you couldn't just flee. You should have sent the child to the hospital for rescue! Accident Investigation The driver of the suspected vehicle is a certain armed minister On that day, Deputy Captain Qian of the Accident Handling Team of the Zongyang County Traffic Police Brigade said in an interview with the reporter that after receiving the alarm at 5:07 p.m., they immediately went to the scene for investigation and examination. Upon investigation, in the afternoon, one of the twin sisters was playing upstairs in the theater, and the other was playing downstairs. The girl playing downstairs was squeezed and injured while reversing a parked car. The most suspicious vehicle was a white Dongfeng Nissan SUV parked here. They learned through interviews that the suspected vehicle involved in the accident was a second-hand car purchased by Wang, a member of the County Direct Work Committee and Armed Minister. Around 6 p.m., they called him, and Wang came to the accident team for investigation at around 7 p.m. According to Wang, from 1 p.m. to 5 p.m., his car was parked at the entrance of the theater to pick up his children after school. When he left with his children, he didn't know that his car had run over someone. Traffic Department Wait for the injury appraisal before making further handling Deputy Captain Qian said that at the time, the child was in the blind spot of the rearview mirror of the car. While reversing, the tires hit the child and squeezed her. There were tire marks on the child's back. After their inspection, the tread pattern on the tires of the SUV matched the marks on the child's body relatively well. Given the mention by netizens that the camera here should have captured the incident process. Deputy Captain Qian said that the spherical panoramic camera here is dedicated to the police network, and it only faces the west side of the road. This SUV entered from the east and left, so this camera never captured the SUV. "Around eight or nine o'clock in the evening, I accompanied Mr. Wang to visit the child at the municipal hospital, and Mr. Wang paid 10,000 yuan for medical expenses." Deputy Captain Qian said that from the investigation, after Mr. Wang arrived, he has been very cooperative and did not conceal the facts. Subjectively, no evidence of fleeing was found, nor was there any evidence of drunk driving. Currently, only Mr. Wang's driver's license and the suspected vehicle have been detained. The next step will be to appraise the injuries of the girl to make further handling. Municipal Hospital Injured child has not yet escaped life-threatening danger On the morning of May 23, the reporter went to the third floor pediatric surgery emergency room of the Anqing Municipal Hospital and saw "Keke", who still had oxygen tubes in her nose and was injured. Her parents and family were taking care of her nearby. The hospital diagnosed her with liver contusion, lung contusion, and large-scale severe abrasions on the right cheek, which would likely leave scars. Her father, Mr. Sun, worriedly said that he now only hopes that she can escape life-threatening danger. Keke's mother also lifted the child's clothes, and bruises from being squeezed by the car tire could still be seen on her back and legs. Keke's attending doctor, Dr. Hu, told the reporter that the child not only suffered severe liver contusion but also lung contusion and has not yet escaped life-threatening danger. Now, it is hoped that the capsule on her liver does not burst. If it bursts, it will cause massive bleeding and endanger life. Dr. Hu said that it will take about a week of observation to determine if the child has escaped life-threatening danger. □An Zi Text/Photo Welcome to comment I want to comment Weibo recommendation | Today's Weibo hot topics (Editor: SN034)
