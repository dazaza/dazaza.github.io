[Get Rid of Boring Leveling Up, Enjoy Easy Entertainment]

Many players may have had similar experiences: leveling up by continuously killing monsters every day or running errands all over the map for those meager rewards. Starting from this point, the development team of "Hunter Country" has designed reasonable daily quests and offline experience collection for the players. Not only does this allow players to enjoy the game experience in a healthy manner, but it also guides them to participate in high-yield quests and activities.

[Designed Reasonably According to Human Rest and Activity Patterns]

Based on human living habits, different quest activities are designed according to time nodes. For example, [Tasting Wine] at noon or evening, [Grinding Levels] during the day, and [Overseas Missions] in the evening. These activities allow players to participate in the game reasonably. They can grind levels effortlessly during the day, gain experience while sitting down during meal times, and enjoy exciting entertainment and relaxation in the evening.

[Goal System, Different Excitement Every Day]

Even though a reasonable schedule is set up, if players engage in the same game content every day, even the most exciting content can become monotonous and boring. Taking this into consideration, in addition to designing different daily activity schedules, the development team of "Hunter Country" has also created a unique goal system. Players can follow the target guidance provided by the goal system throughout their game experience, ensuring they enjoy different excitement every day within the game. Moreover, the goal system includes rare game rewards, allowing players to execute tasks easily and harvest happily.
