Advantages and Characteristics of Hong Kong Tax-Inclusive Express Import

For goods import without documents, the following method can be used: Express import declaration is a customs clearance method in which an express company submits an express customs declaration form (such as KJ1, KJ2, KJ3 type declaration forms) to the customs on behalf of the express company and clears the goods in the form of express.

What are the features of express customs declaration? The express customs declaration has the following characteristics:

1. Simple procedures

According to national regulations such as the Supervision Measures for Inbound and Outbound Expresses, compared with general trade customs declaration, the supervision conditions and customs clearance procedures are simplified, and the customs clearance is faster. Therefore, in most cases, the cargo owner does not need to provide special customs declaration forms, customs clearance documents, licenses/approvals (such as 3C national mandatory certification, environmental protection certificates, import quotas, etc.).

2. Lower costs

According to national regulations such as the Supervision Measures for Inbound and Outbound Expresses, express entry and exit can enjoy certain tax and fee reduction preferences. Plus, Shenzhen's proximity to Hong Kong, an international platform, makes operations convenient, with low tax rates, low operating costs, and naturally lower costs.

3. Faster speed

Due to fewer regulatory conditions and simpler procedures, plus the customs clearance and inspection during the customs clearance process are carried out in the form of express delivery, time is naturally saved.

What are the advantages of express import for personal items and small items? Advantages of express import:

1. Small scattered goods, small quantities of samples, and advertising materials, etc. Such as: IC, displays, adhesives, thinners, etc., difficult high-value products.

2. Various products lacking certain documents (such as no 3C certification). Such as: used molds, machinery, electrical appliances, etc.

3. Products that want to save costs and do not require VAT invoices.

4. If it is bulk cargo, it can also be imported through separate vehicles, batches, and times. Although the customs clearance speed may be affected to some extent, the two advantages of simple procedures and lower costs are still significant.

Note:

1. For tax-inclusive imports, you must know the dimensions of each piece of cargo, the weight of the cargo, and the value of the cargo.

2. Declare according to the actual value of the goods.

3. If it's a full container load, you need to know whether it will be transferred to another container in Hong Kong before entering the mainland or if the container will return to Hong Kong after unloading.

4. Full container loads take longer, about a week.

5. This method of import does not issue VAT invoices.
