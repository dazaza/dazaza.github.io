Internet exposes the middle school student Hao Zhipeng hiring people to chop Wu Tianhao to death in the Changchun hand-chopping incident.

Recently, the shocking news of the Changchun hand-chopping incident was exposed online. The party involved, Hao Zhipeng, is a senior three student at Jilin Experimental Middle School. He and several classmates met at the basketball court of the Baiyi Club in Changchun to practice basketball. About an hour later, several other students of similar age came to the basketball court and joined the game. However, what happened next was unexpected. (Photo of the deceased being rescued)

During the game, he collided physically with Wu Tianhao, who was later chopped to death. After an intense verbal argument, both parties started fighting. However, the dispute between the two groups did not last long and was quickly separated by onlookers. Later, one of the boys from the opposing group took out his phone from his bag and called his parents.

Not long after, Hao Zhipeng's parents and two men arrived at the scene and called Wu and several classmates outside the Baiyi Club to discuss solutions. But soon after, dozens of men suddenly rushed over from across the road, all around twenty years old, each holding a blade about thirty centimeters long. Unable to evade in time, Wu was slashed and fell into a pool of blood, eventually dying due to ineffective rescue.

After this incident, the background of Hao Zhipeng and his parents in the Changchun hand-chopping incident also became a focus of attention, sparking heated discussions among netizens. Since the party involved is still a senior three student but solved problems in a nearly mafia-like manner, leading to a loss of life, it incited anger among many netizens. Many people were investigating their information and exposing it. However, as of now, no detailed information has been disclosed.

According to the description of the deceased's parents, Wu fell into a coma after being sent to the hospital and remained in critical condition until the next morning. He suddenly regained consciousness at around 4 am, spoke a few sentences, then fell back into a coma, and passed away a few hours later. Currently, Hao and his father, Hao Shengshan, have surrendered to the public security authorities, and his mother, Du Xiajuan, has been taken to the police station for investigation. The case is still under further investigation, awaiting more details to be disclosed by the police.
