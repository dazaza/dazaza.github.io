Wuhan Oriental Express Children's Wear Design Concept: Generally in Wuhan, Oriental Express Children's Wear only cares about whether children can wear clothes, but ignores what kind of design is suitable for children to wear. Our designers base their design concepts on the following points:

Since children are lively and active, and their body temperature is higher than adults, they sweat relatively more. Clothes made from general synthetic fibers have poorer water absorption than cotton and are more likely to irritate children's tender skin, causing skin sensitivity. Therefore, we are very strict when selecting materials, mostly using cotton as the main material, and processing it to make it softer, allowing children to feel comfortable and natural when wearing it.

Breaking away from traditional Wuhan Oriental Express Children's Wear's stiff design, emphasizing the fashionable European and American style, but also retaining the lively and innocent side of children's wear. When designing styles, attention is paid to the size of the clothes, and whether they are easy to wear. Designs for toddlers aim to allow them to change clothes independently, helping them cultivate independence.

Points to note in www.dfkc88.com's design:

1. Fabric: Children's skin is delicate, non-irritating fabrics should be chosen.

2. Color: When buying clothes, color is looked at first. Children's clothes should be bright in color.

3. Style: Imaginative designs, such as mushroom-shaped pockets, flower-shaped collars. These are very attractive to children.
