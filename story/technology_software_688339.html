The passing car owner sent the hit father and daughter to the hospital and paid the medical expenses in advance

by fgh6dr4hhhy on 2012-03-03 09:44:36

Oriental Net, November 21st News: According to the "Morning News" report, at around 3 PM yesterday afternoon, a traffic accident occurred at the intersection of Wenshui Road and Hutai Road. A left-turning dump truck collided with a passenger-carrying motorized scooter, causing a father and daughter on the scooter to fall injured on the spot. In the accident, the 4-year-old girl's right leg was run over by a wheel, leaving her bloodied and mangled. Before the arrival of the 120 emergency vehicle, a passing "Camry" private car voluntarily stopped to send the girl to the hospital for emergency treatment and even prepaid more than 1000 yuan in medical expenses.

The man on the motorized scooter was thrown into the air on the spot. The accident happened at about 3 PM yesterday afternoon. Eyewitness Mr. Zhang told reporters that at the time, the motorized scooter was traveling straight across the intersection along Wenshui Road from east to west, while a dump truck with a "Shanghai B" license plate came from the north side of Hutai Road and was preparing to turn left at the intersection. "The dump truck crossed the intersection during the green light, so the motorized scooter should have run the red light," according to Mr. Zhang's description, the driver of the motorized scooter was thrown into the air on the spot, and the motorized scooter, along with the girl sitting behind, was "swallowed" under the dump truck. When the dump truck came to an emergency stop and passersby approached to check, they saw a 4-year-old girl lying in a pool of blood under the back bottom of the car. Her right foot was bloody and mangled, and the scene was heart-wrenching. The man who was thrown into the air was unconscious, and his life was unknown.

"The dump truck driver was already so scared that he lost direction." Mr. Zhang said he saw the driver pull the little girl out from under the car, with blood on his hands. He called the 110 and 120 phone numbers by the roadside and was urgently trying to stop cars on the road, apparently looking for a car to take the little girl to the hospital for emergency treatment, but he couldn't stop any cars. At this moment, a "Camry" sedan voluntarily parked by the roadside. The owner got out of the car and after saying a few words to the driver, he opened the rear door of the sedan and let the driver carrying the still bleeding little girl get in, driving towards the Tenth People's Hospital. About five minutes later, the 120 emergency vehicle arrived at the scene and took away the injured man.

If it had been delayed, the girl might not have been saved. Coincidentally, at 5 PM last night, the Morning Post hotline received a call from the emergency room of Xinhua Hospital. Nurse Cui from the hospital asked the reporter to definitely commend a good-hearted person. After asking, the reporter learned that the good-hearted person mentioned by the nurse was none other than Mr. Pan, the owner of the "Camry" who had escorted the little girl all the way from Wenshui Road, going through two hospitals to seek medical care for her.

"At the time, I just happened to be passing by after finishing some errands and saw the driver holding the little girl, trying to stop cars urgently." Mr. Pan, aged 35, said that when he got out of the car, he immediately felt that the adult's situation was urgent, but the little girl, although her leg was crushed, could still be saved. "It takes time for the 120 emergency vehicle to arrive, but the little girl was losing a lot of blood and couldn't wait anymore." Mr. Pan said he didn't care that the girl's leg was still bleeding and actively let the driver holding the little girl sit in his private car, then drove towards the Tenth People's Hospital on Yan Chang Road.

Upon arriving at the hospital, the emergency doctor stated that the girl's injuries had exceeded the capabilities of the hospital, suggesting that Mr. Pan and the driver take her to Xinhua Hospital, which is better equipped for pediatric emergencies. Thus, Mr. Pan followed the hospital's ambulance and the dump truck driver to Xinhua Hospital.

"He has already prepaid more than 1000 yuan for the little girl, and fortunately, she was sent in time, her wound was treated, otherwise there would have been a risk to her life." Nurse Cui from Xinhua Hospital said that upon diagnosis, the little girl's right leg needed amputation, and her left hand also had fractures. If she had waited a few more minutes at the accident scene, the consequences would have been unimaginable.

Nurse Cui indicated that people like Mr. Pan, who disregarded the damage to their private car, actively helped transport the injured for emergency treatment, and even prepaid medical expenses, are really rare these days. She hopes that Mr. Pan's actions can be commended. After efforts by the reporter, Mr. Pan revealed during a telephone interview last night that he is the head of a small advertising company. His rescue action was not for fame, so he declined the reporter's request to publicly commend him.

According to reports, the motorized scooter involved in this accident carried a father and daughter. The father riding the scooter has been confirmed dead.

At 11 PM last night, reporters learned from Mr. Pan and Xinhua Hospital that the hospital had completed all preparations for the girl's amputation surgery. However, since the girl's father had passed away and there were no direct clues to identify her identity nearby, they have been unable to contact the girl's immediate family members for the surgical consent.

"I stayed with the little girl at the hospital until 7 PM, but she is too young to say anything." The kind-hearted Mr. Pan hopes the Morning Post can help find the family of this unfortunate little girl as soon as possible. According to information collected by the reporter, the girl was wearing a pair of pink sneakers and a light blue mask at the time of the incident. Her mother may work in the Dahua area. Those with relevant information are urged to contact Baoshan Traffic Police, Xinhua Hospital, or our newspaper's news hotline as soon as possible.
