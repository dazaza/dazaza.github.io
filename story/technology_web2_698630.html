Boyfriend breaks into a house to steal money for his sick girlfriend

by yani914wxue on 2012-03-09 10:30:12

The young man is a person of sentiment and righteousness. He was very anxious when his girlfriend was sick and wanted to help her very much. Unfortunately, he chose the wrong method and ended up with regret. Our city is going to build the largest urban video surveillance and emergency system in the country. By the end of next year, 600,000 surveillance cameras across the city will give you a greater sense of security. When his girlfriend was sick, the young man went to visit her. Seeing how emaciated she was, his heart almost broke. Knowing that her family had spent a lot of money on her treatment, he actually came up with the idea of stealing money to treat his girlfriend. The result is temporary confusion, lifelong regret! The young man's name is Li Tuo (a pseudonym), 19 years old, from Pixian County, Sichuan Province. He was arrested by the Criminal Investigation Brigade of Dadukou District Public Security Bureau yesterday morning. "This matter, you must not tell my girlfriend." These were the most frequent words spoken by Li Tuo to the police these days. This matter happened at dawn on October 21st, and even now, every time Li Tuo thinks about it, he feels regretful. Startled awake in the dead of night: A thief entered the house! "Who are you?" Around 3 o'clock in the morning on October 21st, Mr. Zhang, who lived in Yuxin Garden, Dadukou District, suddenly woke up from a dream: a shadow sitting by the bed facing him scared him out of his wits. "What do you want to do?" Mr. Zhang shouted, and the "shadow" quickly got up and tried to escape from the room. Mr. Zhang reached out and grabbed him. The "shadow" struggled to push Mr. Zhang, and then they wrestled together. "Don't fight, don't fight, let's talk nicely." Less than a minute later, the "shadow" suddenly spoke and stopped struggling. Mr. Zhang then let go of him and turned on the bedroom light. Only then did he see clearly that this "shadow" was a young man, about 1.7 meters tall, looking somewhat thin and small, and he was none other than Li Tuo. After hearing the noise, Mr. Zhang's father rushed over from the adjacent bedroom. Upon seeing the scene, he immediately called the police. During the time waiting for the police to arrive, Mr. Zhang and his son kept asking Li Tuo questions. But Li Tuo just sat quietly by the bed, sending text messages on his phone. Finally, he lowered his head and said, "I'm sorry for her." After saying that, he turned off his phone and continued to sit quietly. Not long after, the police arrived at the scene. Girlfriend was sick, his heart was broken Li Tuo was taken back to the police station by the police and soon confessed to the crime. "I just thought of getting some money to treat my girlfriend," Li Tuo said softly. He has a girlfriend whom he has been in love with for more than a year, and their relationship has always been good. A few months ago, his girlfriend came to Chongqing with her parents to work, and the two of them have been constantly on the phone, sometimes chatting for more than an hour. Recently, Li Tuo heard that his girlfriend was hospitalized due to illness. On October 17th, he rushed from his hometown in Pixian to Chongqing and saw his girlfriend lying sick in bed. "She looked so haggard, my heart was broken at the time." Li Tuo said that his girlfriend's parents had already spent more than 10,000 yuan on her treatment. On the evening of October 20th, he stuffed the only 1,000 yuan cash he had into his girlfriend's mother's bag and left the ward to clear his mind, unknowingly walking to Yuxin Garden. By then, it was already past two o'clock in the morning on October 21st. "I felt like there weren't many people living in this neighborhood, and many windows were open." Li Tuo said that a bad idea suddenly flashed through his mind - to steal some money from someone else's home to treat his girlfriend. Li Tuo walked into a residential building, took the elevator directly to the top floor, the 24th floor. Then, through the stairs, he walked down floor by floor. When he reached the 20th floor, he found that a window in the corridor was open, so he quietly climbed into a residence through this window. It turned out that this residence was under renovation and no one was living there yet, and there was nothing valuable inside. He was about to leave when a rope inside caught his attention. He tied one end of the rope to the balcony railing and climbed down to the floor below. Unexpectedly, the living room window of this household was also open, allowing him to easily enter Mr. Zhang's home. Missing his girlfriend, regretting what he did Li Tuo quietly wandered around the living room of Mr. Zhang's house and Mr. Zhang's father's bedroom but found nothing. He then sneaked into Mr. Zhang's bedroom. "It was very dark in the room." So, Li Tuo slowly pulled open the curtains of Mr. Zhang's bedroom. At that moment, he finally saw clearly that there was a mobile phone and a wallet on the computer desk in the bedroom. Mr. Zhang's bedroom wasn't spacious, and the computer desk was very close to the bed. Thus, Li Tuo followed along the side of the bed and slowly approached the computer desk. "Bang!" Li Tuo accidentally kicked the bed frame, making a dull sound. Sensing the movement, Mr. Zhang suddenly woke up and caught Li Tuo red-handed. During the investigation, the police discovered that Li Tuo refused to disclose any information about his girlfriend to the authorities. "I hope she can recover well, I don't want her to worry." Li Tuo said that when he realized he couldn't escape, the first thing he thought of was his girlfriend. So he took out his phone and sent two text messages to his girlfriend, but soon realized that doing so would only make her more worried, so he turned off his phone. Until he was captured, he never turned it on again. "I know I've done wrong, I will repent properly, and strive to get out early to send another text message and make a call to my girlfriend..." Speaking here, Li Tuo choked up. With tears streaming down his face, he told the reporter that the content of the last two text messages he sent to his girlfriend was: "I got caught trying to find money." "Take care of yourself, stay healthy."
