When used in conjunction with core drill bits, the effect is even better; at the same time, it solves the problem of clearing waste residue from pipelines, achieving satisfactory results for overlapping steel plate drilling, and effectively addressing the issue of drilling stainless steel pipes and plates of a certain thickness.

Magnetic Drill [http://www.hanyue-sh.com/] Selection Tips:

One, If you are working in steel structures, wind power generation, nuclear power plants, and need to drill holes with diameters not exceeding 38MM (hollow drills), and depths not exceeding 50MM, I strongly recommend using the Mag MD38 model (twist drill capable of drilling up to 13MM). It has a small volume, weighs only 11KG, making it convenient for high-altitude work.

Two, If your drilling diameter does not exceed 50MM, depth does not exceed 50MM, and twist drill is less than 23MM, I recommend using the Mag MD50 machine. The machine itself has two mechanical speed settings, and its weight of 13KG is relatively light. If you need to drill inverted holes or 90-degree vertical holes with greater depth, the MD58 machine can meet your requirements. In addition to all the advantages of the MD50 machine, the MD58 has an enlarged magnetic base, increased adsorption force, and the drilling depth is increased from 50MM to 75MM, which can be said to be an enhanced version of the MD50 machine.

Three, Four-speed transmission, twist drill up to 32MM, hollow drill up to 100MM, maximum depth of 100MM, this refers to the Mag MD108 model. Some manufacturers cannot accept the cost of hollow drill bits and still use traditional twist drills, but occasionally need to drill hollow drills due to process requirements. In that case, please use the Mag MD108 machine. First, it can drill twist drills up to 32MM, and it also has the ability to drill large holes of 100*100MM hollow drills, saving consumable costs while possessing the capability to drill large holes.

Four, If you are drilling stainless steel,烙 material, manganese steel, etc., materials with such high hardness and viscosity, you must select the TAP30 machine. Not all machines are as practical as they claim. The TAP30 features stepless speed adjustment, torque protection, and dual insulation protection devices. Under load, the slowest speed of the TAP30 is 30 revolutions per minute, with adjustable torque. For drilling hard and viscous materials, the torque can be adjusted to the maximum, and the speed to the minimum. Everyone knows that the lower the speed, the greater the torque, and the higher the speed, the smaller the torque. The strongest feature of the TAP30 is its overload protection device. Due to manual operation leading to machine overload, the machine will automatically shut down, effectively protecting the motor from burning out, making your drilling more effortless. Its unique tapping function can tap M4-M30 internal threads, truly integrating hollow drilling, twist drilling, and tapping into one multi-functional magnetic base drill.
