Synopsis:

In the near future, a young cyber hacker (Keanu Reeves) discovers that the seemingly normal reality is actually controlled by a computer artificial intelligence system called "The Matrix," with people being like animals herded by it. Reeves searches online for everything about "The Matrix." Later, under the guidance of a mysterious woman (Carrie-Anne Moss), he meets the leader of a hacking organization (Laurence Fishburne), and the three embark on a journey to fight against "The Matrix."

"Neo, have you ever had this kind of dream? Are you sure the things you believe are real? Can you wake up from such dreams? Can you tell the difference between dreams and the real world?"

Regardless of everything, Neo wants to find out the truth about "The Matrix" - he always hears whispers about it in his dreams; it's something mysterious and unknowable, and he can definitely feel its ominous control over him.

"What is 'The Matrix'?" Neo doesn't know, but he believes that Morpheus, a legendary figure depicted as a very dangerous person, can give him answers.

"No one can clearly explain what 'The Matrix' is; you have to see it for yourself."

Neo faces another question: Is he it? Of course, though he doesn't know why, Neo's answer is "no." Morpheus's friend Cypher agrees with his view. Others aren't so sure.

Besides, there are those who protect "The Matrix," led by the cold-blooded Smith, and they hold shocking secrets.

Neo, Morpheus, and Trinity must risk their lives to fight powerful enemies in pursuit of answers and self-discovery.
