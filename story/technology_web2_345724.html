Related thematic articles: , Classic Untitled Document The scorching sun of June burned the earth, making the city W, known as the furnace, return to its famous reputation. In this season, all people have simplified their lives, completely separating themselves into their small squares. Some people are laughing and talking in air-conditioned rooms, while others are sweating on the streets; some are enjoying themselves in swimming pools, and others are envisioning the future in schools... At this time, everyone's belonging becomes so clear and distinct, almost no force can peel them off. The southern part of the eastern district of W City is a gathering place for university campuses, with more than twenty main and branch campuses combined. Due to the increasing prosperity of material life, the savvy landlords around universities also seized this market opportunity. Within a few years, restaurants, taverns, arcade game rooms, internet cafes sprang up like mushrooms after rain. College students also enjoyed the happy life brought to them by these landlords without any effort. Actually, the most savvy landlords were not these bosses, but those "developers" who provided rental housing for college students. These "developers" are just ordinary residents of the area, but because they had the conditions to build rental houses, many became newly rich. Su Su's father was one of the most successful individuals among them. Eight years ago, Su Jianjun used all his savings to buy this piece of vacant land and built a three-story building. At that time, the investment from start to finish only cost less than 100,000 yuan, but now it could bring his family an income of several thousand yuan per month. Su Su is currently a sophomore at the Science and Technology College, occasionally accompanying her father to collect rent. Her father wanted to teach her how to use this money-making tree, so that Su Su wouldn't need to look for work and could live off it for the rest of her life. After collecting rent, Su Su finally understood what a "shrewd merchant" was. The small building, which occupies no more than one acre, has five sets of houses on each floor, with living areas that can be imagined; the monthly rent for "single suite" is three hundred, and the monthly rent for "two-bedroom one-living room" is five hundred. Moreover, the houses built by Su Jianjun even have individual kitchens and bathrooms, which made his business always fully booked. Su Su initially couldn't understand why these college students would rent houses when there were dormitories available in school. Although Su Su's home was next to the small building, she still insisted on living in the dormitory. She thought that going to university should have the appearance of being a university student. Not wanting to go with the flow might be one of the reasons she insisted on staying in the school. Actually, the real reason was a boy named Ye Xinming in the opposite dormitory. Perhaps it was a karmic connection from a previous life. Not long after Su Su came to the Science and Technology College, she encountered the graduation competition of amateur boxing for college students. Elective courses are the best way for college students to increase their credits. Female college students generally choose courses such as dance and art, while male college students choose more passionate subjects like ball games and taekwondo. However, the boxing course was too specialized, and very few male students enrolled. Ye Xinming was one of the few males who did. In the graduation competition, students from surrounding junior colleges and even vocational martial arts colleges participated, so other students were eliminated before the official competition. Unexpectedly, the two boys who made it to the finals were both from the Science and Technology College. One was Fu Shengya, the prince charming recognized by the female students of the Science and Technology College, and the other was Ye Xinming, known as the Ice Prince. The referees almost couldn't believe that they could eliminate professional players, and many police stations and public security bureaus quietly investigated their employment motives. The names of the two young men spread throughout the campus. The final result of the competition showed Fu Shengya narrowly defeating the Ice Prince Ye Xinming by one point. However, if the rules of the competition were disregarded, Ye Xinming's multiple knockdowns of the opponent demonstrated his superior fighting skills. Compared to Fu Shengya's face that often carried an elegant smile, Ye Xinming's coldness and arrogance deeply imprinted in Su Su's heart. At the moment the match ended, Ming Xiaoxi, Su Su didn't care about the referee's conclusion. Her eyes were only on that tall and stern youth. From that moment on, Su Su began to notice him, admire him, and secretly liked him. Sometimes she wondered why, but she couldn't find an answer. Maybe liking someone is just that simple, without needing any reason. Ye Xinming is a senior, graduating this year. In these two years at the Science and Technology College, Su Su didn't have many opportunities to interact with him, silently bearing this affection alone. Actually, Ye Xinming wasn't an excellent student. Although he was tall and handsome, his temperament and personality were very cold. His academic performance was good, but his lifestyle wasn't strict. Strictly speaking, he wasn't the dream lover Su Su expected, and Su Su also knew that her parents definitely wouldn't accept someone like him, the so-called Ice Prince. Every time she thought about this, Su Su felt a sense of loss. Perhaps Ye Xinming's graduation will be the end of this unrequited love. Although he was the first boy she liked, this first love seemed to leave her only with a bitter taste. The hot weather made the atmosphere in the dormitory somewhat oppressive, with a few fans laboriously sending warm air to the girls. Su Su lay on the bed looking at her grade report, unsure if she could calmly enter and exit the campus as usual after this summer vacation. Although she and Ye Xinming hadn't truly dated, these two years had been bitter yet sweet. Subconsciously, she looked at the empty dormitory across from hers, feeling her heart become hollow and helpless. She knew where Ye Xinming might be now, and a sudden surge of courage made Su Su want to see him one last time. After all, this reason was quite sufficient; she would go to the rental house to collect rent from Xu Liang. If Xu Liang still wanted to live there after graduation, Su Su would still have the chance to see Ye Xinming. Thinking about this, Su Su suddenly felt some joy. Could Ye Xinming still be discussing life with his close friends in the rental house? Su Su took out her mirror to touch up her makeup. Actually, Su Su was a very pretty girl, and she herself knew this. From the admiring gazes of many boys, she realized she was a focal point of attention. However, Su Su could only sigh inwardly, lamenting why she was so infatuated with Ye Xinming and why she never had any confidence in front of this Ice Prince. With an umbrella shielding her from the sun, Su Su slowly walked towards her home. The temperature didn't make Su Su feel hot; instead, she felt a chilling coolness in her chest. How helpless I am... Su Su bitterly smiled and entered the rental building. Although Su Jianjun's building was built small, the layout was relatively neat. If it weren't for the overly miniature size, it could be considered a decent residential house. Su Su arrived at the second floor and walked to the door of Xu Liang's house. During the summer vacation, only here would there still be sounds coming out. She knocked on the slightly open door, and a hoarse male voice came from inside: "What the hell do you want?" Su Su disliked Xu Liang's coarse language and slightly furrowed her eyebrows: "It's me." For some reason, although Ye Xinming also frequently swore, Su Su didn't dislike it. Perhaps in this world, she could only tolerate two men swearing, one being her father Su Jianjun, and the other being Ye Xinming. Realizing he had spoken disrespectfully to the young mistress, Xu Liang hastily added: "Sorry, please wait a moment!" Then the sound of dressing came from inside the room. Su Su knew what these boys looked like in the summer, so she waited patiently outside the door. After a while, a short and fat boy opened the door, saying apologetically: "Su Su has come, please come in!" This short and fat boy was Xu Liang, wearing a pair of beach pants with his shirt only half tucked in. Inside the room, two other boys were sitting, one whom Su Su didn't recognize, and the other was Ye Xinming. Among the three boys, only Ye Xinming's clothing was the neatest, which made Su Su's heartbeat quicken a little. Xu Liang pulled out a chair for her to sit on and laughed, saying: "Can the rent collection be postponed for a couple more days? My mom is about to send me my allowance, so there's no rush, right..." Su Su nodded with a smile. Because her purpose of coming here wasn't to collect rent, looking at the thoughtful Ye Xinming, Su Su didn't know what to do. The boy she didn't recognize seemed not to have stopped their previous conversation due to Su Su's arrival. He merely greeted Su Su briefly and then asked Xu Liang and Ye Xinming: "Will you guys do it or not? Give us an answer!" Neither Xu Liang nor Ye Xinming responded. After a while, Xu Liang finally spoke: "This amount of money isn't a trivial matter, I have to discuss it with my family." That boy nodded and then said to Ye Xinming: "And you?" Ye Xinming sighed and said in a low tone: "You know my family situation, how can we possibly come up with that much money." That boy looked disappointed but still patted his shoulder and said: "Xinming, because you're my brother, I didn't forget about you this time. If you can raise the money, I'll still reserve a spot for you in this shareholding venture." After saying that, he patted Xu Liang's shoulder and slowly walked out of the room. Listening to his fading footsteps, neither Xu Liang nor Ye Xinming spoke again. Su Su understood that the graduates were discussing their futures, and what she should do now was leave. But curiosity drove her to want to know, to know about them and Ye Xinming. Because in Su Su's heart, she couldn't sever her admiration for Ye Xinming. "What were you discussing with that classmate?" Su Su asked Xu Liang in as calm a tone as possible, glancing at Ye Xinming who remained silent. Xu Liang shook his head and said: "That guy knows a stock trader and wants to pull me and Xinming into stock trading. He says he'll invest 100,000 yuan, and Xinming and I will each put in 50,000, allowing single-account trading. If it goes well, we can double our capital." Although Su Su didn't fully understand, she still said: "Stock trading seems to carry quite a bit of risk. Fifty thousand may not be much in the market, but it's still a significant amount for an average household. You must be careful!" After saying that, she quietly glanced at Ye Xinming, only to meet his eyes as he lifted his gaze. Their eyes crossed instantly! Su Su felt as if she'd been electrocuted and quickly turned her head away. Xu Liang said: "Who says otherwise, I'm troubled by this thing!" Suddenly, Ye Xinming stood up and coldly said: "I'll figure out the money when I get back. Once Zhang Hai arrives, we'll join the shares!" Xu Liang was stunned and said somewhat surprisedly: "Xinming, this is no joke! You don't really intend to... " Ye Xinming continued in a low tone: "If you're my brother, don't ask too many questions, just wait for my good news!" After finishing, he walked out of the small room with light steps. Su Su glanced back at his tall and upright figure, her expression becoming somewhat gloomy. This last meeting was even more hurried than she had imagined. "Sigh, this Xinming, he shouldn't sell his house for investment!" Xu Liang frowned deeply, using his hoarse voice to snap Su Su back to reality. Su Su was startled and asked worriedly: "Sell the house?" Xu Liang said: "Yeah, Xinming has always wanted to sell the old house and partner with Zhang Hai. But do you think stock trading is guaranteed to make money? If we get stuck, what will Xinming do later..." Perhaps Xu Liang was extremely worried about this brother and slowly poured out his inner thoughts to this young landlord. As Su Su listened to his words, her emotions gradually became tense. It turned out that Ye Xinming, the person she had been thinking about day and night, had such a tragic family background.
