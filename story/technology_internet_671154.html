Product Introduction: 38% Jinxiang Zhuyeqing Wine 500ml. Zhuyeqing Wine, as one of China's famous wines, can trace its history back to the Northern and Southern Dynasties. It uses high-quality Fen wine as the base wine, combined with more than ten kinds of precious herbs, processed with unique production technology. Its pure and sweet taste and significant health-care effects have been recognized by people since the Tang and Song dynasties, making it one of China's ancient traditional health-care wines. With a long history and its unique style of "three absolutes" in color, aroma, and taste, Zhuyeqing Wine has won the praise of drinkers at home and abroad from ancient times to the present.

Product Features: Jinxiang Zhuyeqing Wine uses high-quality aged Fen wine as the base wine, inherits the ancient formula of Zhuyeqing, and is carefully blended by master mixologists. In 2003, it won the International Green Health Golden Image Award in Thailand, receiving high praise from international friends.
