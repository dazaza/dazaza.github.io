One person walks well.

by axthaihait on 2011-09-11 18:53:05

Go well on your own!

A heart has belonged to you for a long time, and even after leaving, I still love you. Because of you, I have become so incomplete, torn apart. My love for you is a fusion of blood and tears.

From one city to another, from one emotion to another, while still licking my wounds, on the other side, I have already risked everything. Each time, I despair in waiting and flee in disappointment, not wanting to take away longing, nor wanting to take away the wounds!

Perhaps there was never any wound, just self-imposed shackles. What couldn't be bound was willingly carried as a heavy cross, unwilling to let go.

Life has always wandered on the edge of brokenness and perfection, yet the heart is still filled with hope. Maybe it's innate, always unwilling to submit, unwilling to be ordinary. Seeing those who let fate control them yet remain satisfied, I envy their ignorance while feeling the tragedy of their despair. Truly, I can't write such extravagant words to describe my feelings. No matter how deep these feelings are, when turned into words, they become as barren and desolate as a desert. In the end, my heart can only be understood by myself.

I once loved, suffered, and resented, but once I fell in, that love turned into torment, like termites eroding my internal organs, making my joys and sorrows no longer under my control.

Maybe, it's time to let go.

Each time, just when I am utterly disappointed, you give me a little hope, making me endure a new round of torment. Do you know my helplessness? Have you ever felt pain for me? Have you ever been sad or cried for me? Have you ever thought about running over and holding my hand when I turn away?

In fact, I have nothing left. At the moment I decided to give up, all those memories that once made me linger were blown away by the wind to the four corners of the earth! Everything catches me off guard. This life's destiny is that I cannot love you. You are my calamity, plunging me into endless suffering. Lost and confused, surrounded by stone walls, I am trapped inside, hurt so badly I can't breathe, and you have already abandoned me long ago. For the sake of so-called love, I struggle alone, thinking you will wait for me at the exit, together until the end. But in reality, our ending can only be divergent paths, unable to intersect.

Many people are destined to be just memories in your life. Perhaps some don’t even occupy a place in your memories. If it’s going to shatter, let it be more complete. Loving is enough, regardless of who gave more, who was more hurt, or who couldn’t let go more. Everything will pass.

Finally, we’ve come to this point! My heart hurts so much, I can't breathe for a moment, but as long as you are happy, I will smile through my tears and bless you!

In the end, I cannot withstand the reality of the world, and I must let go of your hand like this! Heartbreak is the most helpless conclusion of every story, and tears are the only language I can use to express myself. The one who turns and walks away first is relatively happier. When a person licks their wounds in the dark, or wakes up to find tears soaking their pillow, thinking of you causes such heartache.

Some say that parting is for reunion. Is that true? Afraid that when we meet again, everything will have changed beyond recognition!
