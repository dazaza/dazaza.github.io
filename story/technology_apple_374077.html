Apple announces the best iOS apps of 2010

by geekzhang on 2010-12-10 13:21:43

With the approach of New Year's Day, 2010 is about to end, and Apple has once again released its annual app rankings to showcase which software has been favored by users throughout the year. This year, apps and games are no longer categorized separately but are instead distinguished between iPhone and iPad, with separate lists for best free, best paid, and top ten overall.

Best Free Apps (iPhone):

1. Facebook

2. Angry Birds Lite (Angry Birds Trial Edition)

3. Words With Friends Free

4. Skype

5. Tap Tap Revenge 3

6. The Weather Channel

7. Paper Toss

8. Bing

9. Rock Band Free

10. Talking Tom Cat (Talking Tom Cat)

There is nothing particularly special about the best free app list; the popularity of Facebook has made it easy for its mobile client to win first place, while "Angry Birds" has shown some fatigue after reigning supreme for several months.

Best Paid Apps (iPhone):

1. Angry Birds (Angry Birds)

2. Doodle Jump - BE WARNED: Insanely Addictive! (Doodle Jump)

3. Skee-Ball

4. Bejeweled 2 + Blitz (Bejeweled)

5. Fruit Ninja (Fruit Ninja)

6. Cut the Rope (Cut the Rope)

7. ALL-IN-1 GAMEBOX (All-in-One Game Collection)

8. The Moron Test

9. Plants vs. Zombies (Plants vs. Zombies)

10. Pocket God (Pocket God)

In the paid app list, games still occupy the majority, and "Angry Birds" dominates both the free and paid rankings, also appearing in the overall rankings.
