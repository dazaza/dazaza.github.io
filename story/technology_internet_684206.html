Taiyuan Nail Fungus

Experts from the Dermatology Department of Shanxi People's Livelihood Hospital point out: Nail fungus is the source of fungal infections. The nail fungus can spread to other parts of the body through scratching, causing various types of skin fungus.

Be vigilant about the causes of nail changes; there are many reasons for such changes, and one cannot assume that all nail diseases are caused by nail fungus. However, it should be noted that nail fungus often starts with one finger or toe nail and then gradually spreads to others. If all ten fingernails or toenails are affected simultaneously, it may indicate a systemic disease, necessitating a comprehensive physical examination.

Pay attention to cross-infection among family members. After June, the weather becomes humid and hot, especially in the south where humidity is high, creating an ideal environment for mold proliferation and invasion of the human body! Special attention must be paid to bacterial cross-infections. For example, cross-infection caused by mold can lead to nail fungus, which easily results in a high rate of family transmission. According to statistics, approximately 30% of families with nail fungus patients also exhibit varying degrees of foot mold infection.

Timely treatment is best conducted between April and September. During spring and summer, when everything grows vigorously, including nails, choosing an effective treatment method suitable for oneself during this season can shorten the treatment time by one-third, minimizing the impact on the patient and reducing costs.

Do not self-treat after infection. Nail fungus is a chronic fungal infection that requires active treatment. Without active treatment, more than just the individual may be affected. It is not advisable to treat nail changes with old home remedies like ginger, tea water, essential oils, or vinegar soaks, as these methods waste money and time and increase the risk of severe complications.

Experts from the Dermatology Department of Shanxi People's Livelihood Hospital say that people often overlook the severity of nail fungus. Many patients only discover nail fungus while being treated for mold infections in other parts of their bodies, with many infection sources originating from family members.
