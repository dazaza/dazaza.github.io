Where there is love, there will be a promise.

by meimei234 on 2012-02-29 21:12:53

Because of love, there will be commitment.

The soft and sentimental melody played in my ears, recalling our love story, it was so happy and sweet. We wanted to keep loving each other forever...

I never thought that I, calm and persistent, would experience this online romance. I am a cold woman, but after meeting you, I completely melted. We met in the early summer of June. Two lonely hearts wandered in the virtual network. Because your online name was very poetic, among many netizens, I added you as a friend, which unexpectedly changed our lives.

Like all online love stories, at first we chatted about work, life, and family on the internet. Your extensive knowledge and literary flair deeply attracted me. My beauty, cuteness, and understanding nature also greatly attracted you. Thus, we walked from the internet into reality together. Although we were thousands of miles apart, our hearts were connected. Although we had never met, our love was deep. A warm greeting, an understanding look, and a sincere letter sent by mail - we knew we were in a long-distance relationship.

In five months, we experienced too much. We cried, laughed, and felt pain. Now when I look back, it all seems so sweet. All of this is because of love, because we are in love...

I never dreamed of actually meeting you in person. Since fate brought us into each other's lives, we were already grateful. We never expected fate to favor us so much, allowing us to meet and turning our online romance into a real-life one.

I still remember when I first heard that I would be traveling to your city for business. I called you, and we were both excited. Although the trip was still two months away, we saw hope and set our goal. We smiled happily every day. For those two months, we envisioned our meeting every day. Sometimes, I think the result doesn't matter as much as the process, which is worth savoring. As time approached, we were almost suffocated with nervousness. In our hearts, we yearned to see the person we loved, yet we were filled with fear. Because we loved each other so much, we were afraid, fearing even the slightest dissatisfaction from the other.

In the days leading up to our meeting, we planned the time and arrangements for our encounter. You bought new slippers, towels, and toothbrushes for me, busying yourself like preparing for a bride. You meticulously planned every meal and detail of our meeting. I was deeply moved, truly touched. I knew all of this was because of the love in our hearts.

When I boarded the first train to see you, my heart had already flown far away. Along the way, I recalled our journey of meeting, knowing, and falling in love, tears streaming down my face – they were tears of happiness. On the first night we were supposed to meet, when I heard your voice on the phone, you said everything was in chaos, completely out of control, too nervous to know what to wear or do to see me. You just wanted to hear my voice, calling me. Watching a man in his thirties confess like a child made me drunk with emotion, my heart aching.

As the plane circled in the sky, our hearts raced as the indicator light approached that city. We were so nervous, not knowing what kind of person the one we had been longing for would be in real life...

When you stood outside my room door and knocked on the doorbell, my heart leaped. The moment I opened the door, our love finally came true. We were shy and didn't know what to say. Although we had seen each other countless times on video chat, seeing the real person was still a bit strange. You had lost weight so much for fear I wouldn't be satisfied, making me almost unrecognizable.

Remember when you pinched my cheeks and kissed me lightly? You had said countless times that the first time you saw me, you would hold me tight. Although at the time, we both felt a slight difference between the real person and the video image (after all, the internet contains too much personal fantasy), I think everyone who meets someone online will notice some differences. But we didn't mention it because for us, appearance was no longer important. Our five-month relationship was real, and we were sure this was the person we loved.

People are strange. From initial awkwardness to being inseparable, the whole process didn't last more than thirty hours. During those twenty-seven hours, we happily did everything lovers should do, but it felt so short. You looked at me over and over again from head to toe, our eyes locking, silently gazing at each other, as if afraid that the other would disappear with a blink.

You tightly held my hand. We both knew that this time, letting go meant we might not hold hands again for a while, so we cherished every second together. You hugged me to the romantic place "Ends of the Earth" to take precious photos together. You joked, saying, "I've taken you to the ends of the earth, where else do you want to go?" Haha, when I was embraced in the crowd with you, I knew I hadn't loved the wrong person. I wanted to say that I loved you very much.

Do you remember the last night? Knowing that we would part the next day, we wished time would stop, but time doesn't pause for anyone or anything; we could only chase after it. Under the brilliant starry sky, by the boundless sea, you bought fireworks for me and let me light them. In your arms, watching the blooming fireworks in the sky, I thought that was a moment I would never forget in my lifetime, the happiest moment of my life.

We hugged and sang love songs, you fed me food, we embraced under the starry sky. You cupped my face and kissed my unstoppable tears because I couldn't bear to leave. I really didn't want to leave, never leave, always embrace you.

Our meeting was like a beautiful dream, hard to believe. Back in real life, our love continued. Sometimes we would have small conflicts, but slowly we realized we couldn't live without each other. Thinking about and loving each other has become a part of our lives. Don't force anything, don't demand anything, just let us love each other like this...forever...
