Towel Network and Roboo Win-win Development of 3G Mobile Network

At the beginning of 2011, when mobile internet was gaining momentum, Roboo Technology Co., Ltd. located in Suzhou Industrial Park launched its mobile top-advertising service first, which gave large, medium and small enterprises in all industries the opportunity to enter the mobile internet field and realize mobile commerce for their businesses. After Roboo Technology (http://www.roboo.com) launched the top-advertising business, many enterprises soon joined and signed top-advertising service contracts with Roboo Technology. "Beauty Hairdressing Towel" signed a top-advertising service contract with Roboo Technology at an early stage after Roboo launched the top-advertising business. Half a year later, now, the "Beauty Hairdressing Towel" (http://mrmfmj.roboo.com/) website has been continuously optimized by Roboo staff through various means, plus the unique user base of the mobile internet, and is now ranked at the forefront of Baidu search results.

"Beauty Hairdressing Towel", this term was registered in May 2011. The contract signed at that time stated that Roboo Technology Co., Ltd. would provide free 3G mobile websites and PC internet websites. Now, the dual-mode website has been successfully built, and when searching for this term on Baidu, the website is ranked on the first page of search results. The "Beauty Hairdressing Towel" website is constructed in a dual-mode way, with the PC website and 3G mobile website developing together, promoting in multiple layers. The "Beauty Hairdressing Towel" website mainly consists of five major sections: wholesale product display, industry information, supply and demand information, franchise columns, and contact us. The "Wholesale Product Display" section showcases a variety of towels, and "Beauty Hairdressing Towel" promotes its products through the product display section of the 3G website and gains certain sales volume. The "Industry Information" section mainly targets some news in this industry, allowing users to comprehensively understand the dynamics and product information of this industry. "Supply and Demand Information" mainly satisfies the buying and selling demands of the market, making it convenient for users to post information. The "Franchise Column" is used for some merchant users to cooperate with the "Beauty Hairdressing Towel" website and post industry commercial advertisements. "Contact Us" makes it convenient for users to get in touch and communicate with the "Beauty Hairdressing Towel" website. Based on its complete framework, the "Beauty Hairdressing Towel" website continuously optimizes itself, combining the advantages of the 3G website, constantly making the "Beauty Hairdressing Towel" website well-known in both PC websites and mobile internet.
