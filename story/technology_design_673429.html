Don't wait for me in this life.

by yayun21c45 on 2012-02-23 16:13:03

Do not wait for me in this life -- written for a boy named Wind

What a beautiful moon tonight! The gentle red clouds around it create a romantic atmosphere. On such nights, I always want to sit in meditation outside the mundane world, with no desires or demands. However, my heart is trembling now, because you are by my side tonight! The night of May always makes people recall that day under the brilliant sunshine when you, with a simple backpack and after a dusty journey, found my little house nestled deep in the mountains. When you first pushed the door open, I was waving a pair of scissors, cutting a wedding dress for my sister. Those delicate pieces of cloth seemed like a red thread predestined from a previous life, crossing thousands of mountains and rivers to bring you to me. Almost simultaneously, we called out each other's names without hesitation. Before I could even guard myself, your bright and penetrating gaze had already entered my heart.

Our acquaintance began with your article "Routine". Your description in that article was very similar to my real-life experiences, so I started writing letters to you. You and I were so compatible, but you kept blaming yourself. You stubbornly believed that you caused my accident. Later, every time you saw or heard on the radio the melancholy words I wrote, you would burst into tears. Back then, in your young heart, there grew a beautiful dream: to use your love in this life to fill the emotional void of a melancholy girl.

Finally, on this windless and rainless night, you held my delicate hands. When you sincerely told me those unforgettable words, I felt sad and couldn't face you. Because I still can't move on from my past experiences. I have lost the most beautiful health and beauty in life, and the love I once had has also died. For these irretrievable pasts, I returned from a bustling city to a remote countryside. I had repeatedly warned myself not to love again, not to touch on topics or people related to love. All the beautiful love stories in the world ultimately do not belong to me. Life has left me with nothing but endless coldness and boundless loneliness. Some things look beautiful but often break hearts. To protect my only remaining, pitiful, and preserved emotion from being hurt again, I enclosed myself in a cocoon. I once used a kind of chilling coldness to hurt many people who wanted to get close to my heart.

However, today, facing you, a boy four years younger than me, serious and persistent, I cannot remain cold. Facing your pure eyes, I feel as if you are as innocent as a child, with no traces of hardship in your life yet. How could I bear to impose my hardships and responsibilities of this life on your face that hasn't fully understood life? After all, in reality, love alone is far from enough!

"Because of the unfinished bond from our previous life, I reincarnated to this world. It's just that I arrived four years later than you. If you hadn't gone through that painful experience, perhaps in this life, you would have already been someone else's bride. In that case, would you let me wait another century for nothing? Anyway, in this life, I came just for you, regardless of what you look like now." That day, after drinking, you held my hand, looked into my eyes, and said these words to me. Panicked, I pulled my hands back almost desperately, avoiding your burning gaze and helplessly gazed at the distant night sky, unable to say a word.

At this moment, a meteor suddenly streaked across the sky and fell into the boundless night. Immediately afterward, another meteor followed behind, drawing a beautiful arc, creating a tragic and touching scene. You said, "Those two meteors are like us two. Wherever you go, I will follow!" Hearing this, I couldn't help but cry out loud. You came over, gently patting my shoulders to comfort me, "Although the life of a meteor is short, it has a moment of brilliance. To be by your side, I am willing to be a meteor with you." I trembled even more violently, and tears continuously welled up from the depths of my heart.

Tonight is the tenth day of the new moon, not full, but abundant. There are gentle red clouds surrounding it, enveloping the entire earth in a hazy silver-white glow. The moon moves slowly, as if it also cannot bear the heavy burdens and sorrows of the world. Once, I thought my love had died along with its first shattering. I thought a girl who lost her beauty and health could never find her true love in this life. But tonight, facing your pure eyes, I seem to return to the past. Actually, how much I wish I could stay by your side forever, letting my heart cease wandering; how much I wish I could lean on your shoulder forever, allowing you to listen to my accumulated words of affection spanning several lifetimes. But I cannot. Too much helplessness and sorrow in life have worn me down into a tree, made me wither into grass. I fear that your inexperienced shoulders cannot bear the strong winds of the secular world, blowing you back to where you started, leaving me with even more scars.

Therefore, Wind, for a better tomorrow for you, I can only let my yearning wither in your heart, stay briefly, and then leave forever, drinking all the bitterness and trials by myself. I really don't want, nor hope, to impose the hardships and responsibilities of my life onto you. Let me taste all the helplessness of my life alone throughout my lifetime!

Before I finished speaking, you interrupted my murmurs with a determined tone, painfully embracing my shoulders. Leaning on your warm chest and listening to your young heartbeat, I suddenly felt so helpless and fragile. For days, the emotions I had been holding up collapsed instantly. I long to not talk about the past life, nor the next, but to spend my entire life together with my beloved person without regrets in this life. But, does reality allow it? Does life allow it? Thinking about my past sufferings and experiences, I can only swallow my tears and tell you, "No!" Because I deeply understand your excellence in this life and my own sorrow.

When the dream wakes in reality, I find myself in my story with no way back, no shore to return to. I am too far away from the world, so far that no one can even see my face clearly. Wind, if there is really a next life, please wait for me by the river of the next life. Then, I will wear a long skirt, as beautiful as ever, and be the first to keep the promise I made with you in this life.

It is finally time for you to leave. You said, "Around winter, I will come back to see you. Sing a song for me." With tears in my eyes, I sang "Approximately in Winter" for you. But, Wind, I'm afraid I will be old before winter comes, and I'm afraid you will return in the snow when I am no longer here. So, Wind, do not wait for me in this life!
