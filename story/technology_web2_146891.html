by xjyzhenai on 2009-05-07 14:12:24

52PHOTO is an online fast image processing, 3D portrait composition, art painting processing website, providing users with one-click photo processing services, can quickly produce ordinary photos into outdoor posters, magazines, wall paintings, movie composition, sketch, oil painting and celebrity face change photos. Its service is similar to that of Toast.

image

52PHOTO is in private beta, and anyone can sign up for free to work with 52PHOTO templates for free.

After you select the image you want to synthesize, the ordinary image and painting type composite image synthesis takes only 5-10 seconds (depending on your network speed); For 3D portrait synthesis, due to the use of the latest virtual 3D technology, it only takes 10-15 seconds.

Although 52PHOTO is not yet stable for handling the private testing period, it uses advanced technology:

1. Face recognition technology

2. 3D face automatic modeling

3. Leading color matching and management

4. New image compositing format

5. A variety of painting effects processing

6. Unmanned automatic blue picking

Coupled with the gradual improvement, I believe 52PHOTO will become a good image online processing service.
