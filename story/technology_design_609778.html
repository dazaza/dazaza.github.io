Xu Jinglei directed the 2009 Kuai Nv MV, sparking controversy as Zeng Yike had the most screen time.

by zhlhmntk80 on 2011-12-08 04:16:59

Xu Jinglei - The MV of the 2009 Happy Female Sound, directed by Xu Jinglei, is about to be released. The theme of this MV is "musical fantasy, happy summer". Recently, the MV was exposed in advance through the Internet. Among the top 10 "Happy Girls", the most eye-catching one is Zeng Yike, who has the most controversy. In the short 3 and a half minute MV, her fixed-point shots are as many as 8. In addition, there have been rumors that Bao Xiaobai will return to the "Happy Girl" competition. Regarding these rumors, Bao Xiaobai's agency issued a statement to clarify the matter.

Xu Jinglei's first attempt at directing an MV, combined with the hottest topic of the summer, "Happy Girl", has attracted much public attention. On the 4th, the MV was prematurely exposed on the Internet. It was seen that all 10 "Happy Girls" wore white outfits, and the entire background was mainly white. After the MV was revealed, the overall evaluation from netizens was positive. However, the most noteworthy aspect is still Zeng Yike's performance inside: "Each person sang a line of lyrics, Zeng Yike sang 'accompanying smiles and being strong', still in her childlike voice, but the only improvement is that she hit the right pitch." Besides commenting on Zeng Yike's pitch accuracy, netizens also pointed out that Xu Jinglei must appreciate her very much. Zeng Yike had the most shots among everyone, with 8 fixed-point shots.

Recently, there were claims that Bao Xiaobai would return to the "Happy Girl" competition, and the "withdrawal incident" was just a joint publicity stunt. In response to these rumors, Bao Xiaobai's agency "Of Course Entertainment" broke its silence and issued a statement through the Internet: "After the withdrawal incident, Hunan TV and our company did have discussions regarding Bao Xiaobai returning to the Happy Girl competition as a judge, but no definitive agreement has been reached so far." Additionally, the statement strongly pointed out: "The widespread rumors of mutual hype between both parties are completely false."
