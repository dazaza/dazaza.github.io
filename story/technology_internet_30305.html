After more than a year of experience in clothing wholesale, the positioning of Trendsetter Clothing Wholesale Network has become very clear: to provide clothing wholesale services for clothing stores nationwide and even globally. Many clothing store owners have had the experience of going to the wholesale market to get goods. They usually go to various clothing wholesale markets to purchase clothing in bulk. Since the sources are not centralized, it's quite exhausting. Moreover, if you don't buy much, even manufacturers won't give you wholesale prices, nor will they be friendly. If there are quality issues, you have to return the goods yourself. The hardships involved are only known to those who have experienced it. As an advertising slogan says: "Only those who do it know."

On the Trendsetter Clothing Wholesale website, you can sit at home, click your mouse lightly, and easily place orders. Our clothing prices are set according to cost, and we treat every customer fairly. We won't offer different prices to different people. Our clothing is positioned as mid-to-low-end, so some customers who own high-end brand specialty stores may not find our wholesale suitable. Online ordering ensures that each order's goods are inspected before shipping, providing an additional layer of quality assurance compared to direct factory shipments. In fact, we are more like a team; we are responsible for the sources, and you are responsible for sales. Due to time constraints, I'll write so much for now and supplement later.

Wholesale Instructions

1. A minimum of 5 pieces per batch, no limit on color/style/size, reaching a total of 5 pieces is sufficient. Orders with fewer than 5 pieces will not be confirmed by us.

2. Discounted pricing: Unfortunately, we cannot offer more favorable discounts. We sell directly from the manufacturer, and our pricing is carefully studied. Between the options of high price with high discount and actual price with low discount, we chose the latter. Our wish is that regardless of how much you wholesale, you can gain a certain price advantage to enhance competitiveness. The online order system automatically adjusts the wholesale price based on your quantity.

3. Shipping costs for wholesale must be borne by the buyer. Please check the delivery fees under the transportation method. For small-batch customers, we recommend express delivery; for large-batch customers, we suggest freight transport. We do not recommend postal ordinary mail due to its slow speed and inability to track the package.

4. Regardless of the quantity you need, please do not negotiate prices with us at the beginning to avoid wasting valuable time. We price according to cost. We hope you first try a small batch of our products. If they suit you and after the first cooperation, if you want to place a large order, we can discuss further.

Product Out-of-Stock Handling Process

Although this site is designated as a spot goods area, with large wholesale volumes, there might still be out-of-stock or out-of-color situations. If some goods remain out of stock after your remittance, you can choose:

(1) To exchange for other colors or styles;

(2) To leave the balance in your account for next use;

(3) To refund the excess amount to the provided Industrial and Commercial Bank account or Alipay account, with the bank fee borne by you.

If you need to return the remaining money due to out-of-stock items to your bank account, please leave a message on the website: Please specify the order number, out-of-stock item number, amount, your Industrial and Commercial Bank account or Alipay account (we only have these two methods for online transfers, please understand). For security reasons, we only accept bank accounts provided through the website transaction account, not through QQ or SMS. We will refund your money within three working days after receiving your message.

Retail Group Buying Instructions

This site is a professional clothing wholesale website and does not provide retail services. If you are buying for personal use, please read the after-sales service terms carefully before purchasing to avoid inconvenience. The wholesale clothing may differ from what you see in clothing stores, as clothing stores usually tidy up the clothes before putting them on shelves, dealing with loose threads, ironing wrinkles, etc. Don't complain about poor workmanship just because of a few loose threads. Some minor issues need to be handled by yourself. Therefore, we recommend that retail members buy for personal use from clothing stores where they can try on clothes and feel the fabric, and enjoy good service. Our wholesale prices do not include these costs, so we cannot provide such services. Please understand.

Quality Issue Handling Process

Please refer to the After-Sales Service Agreement for the determination of quality issues. If the product you purchased on this site has quality issues confirmed by customer service, please follow the process below:

1. Please note your order number and the quality issue inside the clothing packaging so we can handle it promptly upon receipt. Unmarked ones will be treated as discarded.

2. Please pay the courier freight yourself when returning the goods. We do not accept any form of cash-on-delivery for couriers.

Supplementary Explanation: You might find it hard to understand why you have to pay the freight for quality issues. Think of it this way: if you go to the market to buy in bulk and there are quality issues, you still have to take them back yourself, and the manufacturer won't reimburse your transportation costs. Since the profit margin for wholesale is quite low, when quality issues occur, the freight often exceeds the product's profit. We can only bear the one-way freight. We hope you can handle some minor issues yourself, such as small openings that can be fixed, stains that can be washed away, or problems that can be solved by tailors in a few minutes for a few dollars. We cannot guarantee that all products are 100% flawless. No manufacturer or company can ensure their products are 100% problem-free.

3. We will send the exchanged goods back to you within three days of receiving the returned goods.
