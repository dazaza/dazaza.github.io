by fs56d546s on 2012-03-02 11:12:20

A 37-year-old man from Yunnan climbed onto the ninth-floor fire escape of a hotel in Luocun, Foshan, in a suicide attempt after being scammed out of 8,000 yuan by a female internet acquaintance. Six policemen pulled him back inside when he wasn't paying attention, narrowly saving his life. The man was administratively detained for 10 days.

● Time: 12:00~15:10, February 29th

(News excerpted from You News [http://www.ub-china.com/] : http://www.ub-china.com/[http://www.ub-china.com/])

● Location: Julong Hotel, Luocun, Nanhai

● Outcome: Saved

[If he wanted to jump, why was he holding an umbrella?]

[Exactly, it's been two hours and he still hasn't jumped. He's wasting everyone's time.]

On the afternoon of February 29th, at the top floor of Julong Hotel in Luocun, Nanhai, a sudden downpour began.

The man seemed to notice something and quickly bent down, took out a red umbrella, and opened it.

The onlookers began to jeer.

The voices reached the ninth floor, and the man hesitated for a moment before closing the umbrella and casually throwing down a glass cup.

Afterward, the man would sometimes crouch, sometimes hang in the air, occasionally looking down while always tightly gripping the railing.

In the rain, the standoff continued as the onlookers jeered, and the man angrily threw a glass cup.

[What do you want us to do? Get down on our knees and beg you? We've already told you, anything can be negotiated.] A SWAT officer shouted at the man outside the hotel, near the top of the fire escape.

By this point, a sudden downpour had begun, and it had been two and a half hours since the police were alerted at noon.

The man was wearing a dark green camouflage uniform, clinging to the outer side of the ninth-floor fire escape, with trembling legs.

He would occasionally look down; there were nearly forty onlookers on the ground. A 6m x 6m air cushion had been laid near the fire escape, and five rescue vehicles were on standby.

The man began moving his hands and feet but refused to let the police get closer.

The onlookers on the ground began to jeer.

[If he wants to jump, why is he using an umbrella?]

[Exactly, it's been two hours and he still hasn't jumped. He's wasting everyone's time.]

[Let's wait and see. If he doesn't jump soon, we'll leave.]

... ...

The man seemed to have heard the voices below and closed the umbrella, casually throwing down a glass cup.

The police quickly signaled for the onlookers on the ground to evacuate and demanded silence.

"At that moment, he was really angry, with a very difficult expression." Luo Liangde, a social worker from Luocun who helped communicate with the police, said that the man actually didn't want to die, but was stuck in a difficult situation and could only stay up there.

The man confessed that he fell in love with a female internet friend and was scammed out of 8,000 yuan.

Why did he climb if he wasn't truly seeking death?

"The boss of Julong Hotel owes the man 8,000 yuan, so the man climbed to demand repayment." Some onlookers speculated.

Hotel management immediately denied this claim. "How could we owe him money? Clearly, he saw the fire escape on the outside and climbed up himself. It must be due to too much pressure, but it has nothing to do with us!"

"Indeed, it's not that the hotel owes him money, but rather that a female internet friend scammed him out of 8,000 yuan." Li Dexian informed reporters afterward.

According to Li Dexian, the man's surname is Feng, he's from Yunnan, born in 1975, came to Foshan to work six months ago, and has two children, the oldest just eight years old. Because his wife left him many years ago, Xiao Feng has been depressed. In Foshan, Xiao Feng met an online friend named "Ah Ting," and after several conversations, they fell in love.

Li Dexian introduced that initially Xiao Feng was unwilling to communicate with anyone, repeatedly asking the police to bring "Ah Ting." However, based on the contact information provided, the police couldn't reach her, so they could only persuade Xiao Feng to "come down first and talk."

Xiao Feng wouldn't listen, continuing to call "Ah Ting," but all calls went unanswered.

"So, I proposed to make a phone call and chat with him." Li Dexian's suggestion was accepted by Xiao Feng. According to Li Dexian's call records, the conversation started at 1:20 PM and lasted about 17 minutes.

During the call, Li Dexian learned about Xiao Feng's basic situation and understood the root cause of his distress.

Yesterday, Xiao Feng and his online friend "Ah Ting" had agreed to meet at the Julong Hotel in Luocun. Prior to this, "Ah Ting" had asked Xiao Feng to transfer 8,000 yuan to her account. However, Xiao Feng did not see "Ah Ting" at the hotel and, after waiting for a long time and feeling tired, climbed up the building to attempt suicide.

"After saying these things, his emotions became agitated, and he quickly hung up the phone, saying he wanted to jump." Li Dexian was very nervous at the time but had no way out, so he kept repeating: "Don't jump, don't jump, I will be very sad if you jump."

Li Dexian's words touched Xiao Feng, and his emotions eased slightly, but he refused to come down, leading to a stalemate.

During this time, the police gave Xiao Feng water and an umbrella, but they were unable to take advantage of the opportunity to rescue him.

The hotel owner's offer of ten thousand yuan to buy the man's descent was rejected.

Time passed second by second, and it was almost three hours later. The rain got lighter then heavier, and heavier then lighter again. The onlookers grew weary, some leaving the scene, others returning after leaving. "Is he asking for money?" someone in the crowd said.

This caught the attention of the Julong Hotel owner, who proposed giving Xiao Feng ten thousand yuan to compensate for his loss.

"The key is to come down quickly, don't affect our business."

But when Li Dexian handed the ten thousand yuan to Xiao Feng, he firmly refused.

Li Dexian recalled: "He said he didn't want the money, he just couldn't figure it out, and he didn't want to live anymore."

The stalemate continued, and the man's physical condition clearly deteriorated.

The reporter observed that his legs were shaking constantly, sometimes squatting, sometimes hanging in the air. But his hands were tightly gripping the railing, continuously communicating with the police and Li Dexian, yet refusing to come down.

Special Police Rescue Without Using Tranquilizer Darts

"The situation was getting urgent, and the police decided to take action," Li Dexian said.

The police devised an emergency rescue plan, arranging for Li Dexian to go to the eighth-floor fire escape to attract the man's attention, then seize the opportunity to shoot tranquilizer darts and quickly move forward to save him.

The operation quickly commenced, and Xiao Feng also seemed to sense this, frequently looking down and making gestures as if preparing to jump.

Li Dexian and the police noticed these actions and began talking nonstop to him, drawing his attention. During this time, the special police took the opportunity to pass mineral water to the man, and at the instant he was about to grab it, several special police officers suddenly pounced, grabbing the man and pulling him upward forcefully. Xiao Feng struggled but was ultimately saved.

By this point, it was already 3:10 PM.

Li Dexian said, "The prepared tranquilizer gun wasn't used, but fortunately, we managed to save him."

Below, there was a chorus of screams. "That was close! He almost fell."

"Good job by the police this time, I wonder if it was recorded?"

"You know, that guy really caused a lot of trouble."

... ...

When Xiao Feng was led out of the hotel by the police, he burst into tears.

The reporter observed that he was wearing very little clothing—besides his coat, he only had a thin sweater underneath, and his hands were frozen red.

"Don't jump, don't jump, I will be very sad if you jump."

Part-time Negotiation Expert

Female Social Worker Rescues for the Third Time

This successful rescue owed much to Luo Village Social Worker Li Dexian, whose excellent communication skills provided the police with the opportunity and time to carry out the rescue.

Li Dexian is 27 years old, from Jiangmen, and one of the professional social workers in Luo Village. After graduating from the Department of Social Work at South China Agricultural University in 2007, she passed the exam and started working in Luo Village.

According to her introduction, the daily responsibilities of a social worker include conducting volunteer services, youth training, elderly care, and coordinating neighborhood relations, etc., which "[do not involve handling crisis events]."

This is her third time "part-time as a negotiation expert."

In 2009 and 2010, she assisted security personnel in rescuing a homeless girl and a man attempting suicide by drowning, respectively. "My main task is to communicate with them and persuade them to cherish their lives."

In Li Dexian's view, in grassroots crisis events such as communities and rural areas, few professional "negotiation experts" can intervene, which is precisely the breakthrough point of social worker work. "I think it's quite novel, requiring skills and courage."

According to Huang Yingshan, the Secretary of the Youth League Committee of Luo Village, not only Li Dexian, but all five professional social workers within the jurisdiction of Luo Village have received professional training in "crisis intervention." "We consciously guide social workers to develop towards 'generalists,' who can serve as both negotiation experts and 'trusted sisters,' thus better serving the community."

Latest Development

"Jumping Man" Detained for 10 Days

The police initially learned that the man's name is Feng Xiaobai, unemployed, temporarily residing in a rental house in Xiabai, Luo Village. Regarding the case where he was scammed out of 8,000 yuan by a female internet friend, the police stated that further investigation is needed.

As of the time of this report, the jumping man has been legally administratively detained for 10 days due to disrupting public order.
