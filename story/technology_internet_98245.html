WebSEO reminder: Some people like category directories, while others worry about them. However, submitting your site to a category directory can help with free website promotion and allow your website to gain more ratings from search engines, thereby enhancing the domain weight. Submitting to website directories is a time-consuming task. With thousands of directories available, you must manually submit and choose those that are most targeted and suitable for your industry in order to increase the success rate of submission. Choosing and submitting the most appropriate category for your website is crucial because search engines prefer links with strong industry relevance. Therefore, the selection of industry categories in classification directories is very important, such as Dmoz. Additionally, using a set of original and concise text to describe your website will help search engines understand your site. If you write a good description, your website will be more likely to pass the review of the classification directory, such as a summary of classification directories.
