Yesterday, Nandu reporters learned that Shenzhen Market Supervision Bureau had taken over and intervened in the investigation of this matter. Director Huang Gengbiao of the Nanwan Office had been suspended for inspection. The bureau stated that all seven people who participated in the Mahjong game would be dealt with.

On the afternoon of the previous day, the Longgang branch of the Shenzhen Market Supervision Bureau responded to Nandu reporters that after investigation, it was true that the leadership of the Nanwan Office led employees to play cards during working hours. They had already been publicly criticized, and the director was ordered to lead the written self-examination. The specific handling results would be announced to the public after they were determined.

The relevant person in charge of the bureau said that after investigation, the leadership of the Nanwan Office indeed played cards in the meeting room after lunch. The card game started around 12 o'clock and ended at about 2:15 PM. The end time of the card game had already exceeded 2 o'clock, so it was indeed playing cards during working hours. This matter has already drawn the attention of the branch leadership, and they would not tolerate those involved. They would be seriously handled.

Director of Mahjong: Admitting mistakes and making self-examinations.

On the afternoon of the previous day, the Longgang Market Supervision Branch admitted that the incident was true. Currently, the director who led the Mahjong game had been suspended, and other personnel were under investigation and being handled.

Nandu News - Reporters Guo Qiming and Wang Zirong - On the eighth day of the first lunar month, the first day of work. Some citizens complained to the Southern Metropolis Daily that when they went to the Nanwan Market Supervision Office in Longgang District to handle business, the staff said that it was currently unable to be processed. In the afternoon of the same day, when Nandu reporters visited the Nanwan Market Supervision Office, they found that many staff members were playing cards in the meeting room, including the director of the office and several deputy directors.

On the afternoon of the previous day, Director Huang Gengbiao of the Nanwan Market Supervision Office responded to this matter saying that playing Mahjong during working hours was indeed wrong. After the incident occurred, he led the self-examination and emphasized work discipline again, ensuring that such situations would not happen again. Regarding the claim that Mahjong was played from the eighth day of the first lunar month to the Lantern Festival, Huang Gengbiao denied the existence of such a tradition. He said it was impossible to play for that long. Everyone just simply entertained themselves during the noon break, got carried away and missed the start of work time, but they were already prepared to stop.

The Nanwan Office meeting room privately set up a Mahjong room, and the Longgang Branch of the Shenzhen Market Supervision Bureau claimed they were unaware. The person in charge said that during previous inspections, they found a table tennis room next to the meeting room and did not see any Mahjong table, otherwise, they would have warned the Nanwan Office. Regarding the claim that Mahjong was played from the eighth day of the first lunar month to the Lantern Festival as a tradition, the person in charge denied it again.
