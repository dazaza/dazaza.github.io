A laughable court judgment where the defendant could not provide any evidence, relying solely on empty talk.

The truth is:

I am the plaintiff, Zhao Yunhai, and the defendants are the Dao County People's Government and the Dao County Land Resources Bureau.

In July 2001, the plaintiff signed a land use rights transfer agreement with the Dao County Futang Fish Farm, stipulating that the farm would transfer its 250 square meter land use rights to the plaintiff. The Futang Fish Farm filled in the plaintiff's name on the planning map drawn by the Dao County Planning Bureau, stamped it with their official seal, and submitted it to the Planning Bureau. On January 12, 2007, the Dao County Planning and Construction Bureau legally issued the (0701120018) No. Construction Land Use Permit to the plaintiff. The plaintiff then applied to the Dao County Land Resources Bureau for a land use certificate with relevant documents. However, the Dao County Land Resources Bureau neither informed the plaintiff of the reasons nor fulfilled their legal obligations. In November 2010, the plaintiff learned that the Dao County Land Resources Bureau had confirmed the plaintiff’s land as being used by Xu Shanlin under the Dao Ji Jian (2001) No. 0814164 Collective Land Construction Use Certificate. The plaintiff believes that the issuance of the aforementioned land use certificate by the Dao County Land Resources Bureau violated legal procedures, was factually incorrect, lacked sufficient evidence, and violated the law, causing damage to the plaintiff's legitimate rights and interests. Therefore, the Dao County Land Resources Bureau's issuance of the Dao Ji Jian (2001) No. 0814164 Collective Land Construction Use Certificate should be legally revoked.

The first-instance and second-instance courts ruled that according to Article 63 of the "Land Management Law of the People's Republic of China," the right to use collectively-owned land cannot be transferred, assigned, or leased for non-agricultural construction. The Futang Fish Farm is an agricultural collective economic organization under the jurisdiction of the Futang Township Government of Dao County, and its land nature is collectively owned. The agreement signed between the Futang Fish Farm and the plaintiff without authorization is invalid and therefore not protected by law. The court dismissed the lawsuit on the grounds that the defendant's issuance of the certificate did not have a legal interest relationship with the plaintiff.

However, state-owned land use rights allocated by the state within the scope prescribed by law are exempted. According to the "Interim Measures for the Administration of Allocated Land Use Rights," after approval by the land management department of the city or county government and payment of the land use right transfer fee, the land user may transfer, lease, or mortgage the land use rights.
