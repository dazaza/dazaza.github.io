The results of the selection for outstanding enterprises and individuals in operational support in 2008 have been announced.

Believing that through the evaluation of excellent enterprises and individuals inside and outside in 2008, successful experiences will be shared and good ideas will be extended. The selection will witness the achievements made in the construction of IT support systems in the telecommunications industry in 2008, bringing new ideas and inspiration for further development, thus further promoting the rapid development of the telecommunications industry.

The judging process fully adhered to the principles of openness, fairness, and impartiality. In the end, Digital China Software (Beijing) Limited, Shenzhen Tianyu Information Development Co., Ltd., Accenture, CONVERGYS, AMDOCS were awarded the "Gold Enterprise in Operational Support in the Chinese Telecommunications Industry in 2008". Liu Chengliang, General Manager of the Group Business Support System Department of China Unicom, was awarded the "Outstanding Contribution Award in Operational Support in the Chinese Telecommunications Industry in 2008"; Li Jing, China Regional Marketing Director of AMDOCS, Wang Li, General Manager of the Operations Support Department of Hebei Mobile, Han Changmin, Director of the Enterprise Informationization Department and Support Center of Shaanxi Telecom, Liu Ganggang, Deputy General Manager of the Business Support Center of China Mobile Heilongjiang Branch, Gao Lingyun, Senior Manager of the Business Support System Department of China Unicom Nanjing Branch, etc., were awarded the "Personality of the Year in Operational Support in the Chinese Telecommunications Industry in 2008".

This conference received strong support and active participation from the leaders and experts of the Ministry of Industry and Information Technology's Science and Technology Department, MIIT Telecom Research Institute, Beijing University of Posts and Telecommunications, China Mobile, China Telecom, China Unicom, Digital China Software (Beijing) Limited, Shenzhen Tianyu Information Development Co., Ltd., IBM, Ericsson, AMDOCS, Accenture, CONVERGYS and other companies.

In 2008, the telecommunications industry underwent a new round of restructuring. During this process, telecommunications operating enterprises and operational support enterprises jointly worked hard for orderly data handover and stable system transition. Related operating enterprises such as China Telecom, China Mobile, and China Unicom actively responded to new changes, quickly responding from headquarters to branches, from systems to applications, carrying out a large number of effective integration and upgrade work. Various mainstream operational support enterprises also participated comprehensively, developing and promoting rich and colorful products and solutions. To commend enterprises and individuals who made outstanding contributions during this process and share best practices, the conference specially set up relevant awards.

On the evening of February 25, the much-anticipated "2009 Telecommunications Billing and Operations Support Conference" was held. The conference was hosted by the China Communications Society and organized by the editorial department of "China Communications." The theme of the conference was: Construction of Telecommunications Billing and Operations Support Systems in a Full-service Environment. At the same time, the conference also selected important award categories such as the "Gold Enterprise in Operational Support in the Chinese Telecommunications Industry in 2008" and the "Personality of the Year in Operational Support in the Chinese Telecommunications Industry in 2008."

To ensure the authority and exemplary significance of the awards, the selection process was divided into three stages. First, through preliminary research and enterprise recommendations, a candidate list was established. Thirty-one companies, including Accenture Company, entered the candidate list for the "Gold Enterprise in Operational Support in the Chinese Telecommunications Industry in 2008," and relevant personnel, including Director Han Changmin of the Shaanxi Telecom Operations Support Center, entered the candidate list for the "Personality of the Year in Operational Support in the Chinese Telecommunications Industry in 2008." The second step involved public online announcements and voting by industry experts to select shortlisted enterprises. Finally, through the organization of the organizing committee, experts and scholars from the China Communications Society, the Ministry's Communication Science and Technology Committee, the Ministry's Telecommunications Research Institute, Beijing University of Posts and Telecommunications, and other units formed a selection committee. After joint evaluations by the selection committee, the final winners were determined. At the same time, to reward individuals who made outstanding contributions in the field of operational support in 2008, with the approval of the selection committee, a special "Outstanding Contribution Award in Operational Support in the Chinese Telecommunications Industry in 2008" was established.

The representatives present unanimously believed that the full-service competitive landscape poses significant challenges to the operations support systems of telecommunications infrastructure operators. Meeting the complex requirements of tariff packages and multi-service integration is an urgent issue that needs to be addressed by telecommunications operators. New concepts and operational thinking need to be introduced.
