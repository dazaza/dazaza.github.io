Car beauty decoration and car beauty maintenance knowledge

Most car enthusiasts are very familiar with driving skills, but they know little about car beauty decoration and car beauty maintenance knowledge.

There was a car enthusiast who went to wash his car. He called a car beauty shop and asked a question about washing the car. They told this gentleman that he could use detergent or laundry powder water to clean it himself, no need to spend money, our charge is 10 yuan!

The driver was surprised and asked: "Will your boss cut your salary because of this lack of knowledge?" The shop assistant said: "In fact, this is our boss's idea." "Our boss approves of us, if we let customers wash their cars themselves first, it will generally cause bigger problems, earning much more money than just washing the car. Why not be happy?"

Car beauty decoration does not mean that cleaning it up is enough. You need to pay attention to what materials are used. This is very important. Misuse can damage the car's materials.

Here, I sincerely remind all car owners not to blindly understand the concept of "car beauty decoration" and cause great harm to your beloved car without knowing it.

For many reasons, car beauty in China lags behind developed countries abroad. Traditional single-handed maintenance methods have continued for decades in our country. In the eyes of many Chinese people, car beauty seems to be just washing the car and changing the interior decoration, but it is not so. "Car beauty" means both beautification and maintenance in English. It mainly includes exterior beautification (car washing, oil removal, stain removal, new car waxing, old car waxing, chrome restoration, and tire restoration), interior beautification (interior beautification care and luggage compartment cleaning), paint beautification (paint polishing treatment, paint scratch treatment, and spray painting), car protection (installation of anti-explosion sun film, theft alarm, voice warning system, and static discharger) and car accessories (car perfume, room purification, decorative stickers, and various mats). Therefore, real car beauty should achieve the effect of "making old cars look new, keeping new cars valuable, and making them always look new".

With the rapid development of the car beauty industry, the growing maturity of car consumption, and the increasing number of private car owners, the concept of maintenance instead of repair has become deeply rooted in the minds of car owners. More and more car owners have the habit of giving their cars a beauty treatment. Their concern for their beloved cars is no less than that of parents for their children. However, they do not know how to start. They want to make their cars look beautiful and tidy, but they use alkaline car wash liquid. Over time, they will find that the paint surface ages severely and loses its original luster. At this point, they still need to consult some professional car beauty maintenance shops after all, aren't they specialized in this field?

The car beauty industry did not appear in our country until the end of the twentieth century. With the development of the automobile industry, various brands of beauty maintenance products have sprung up like mushrooms after rain. Some well-known foreign car care product companies are far ahead of us. Renowned car care product companies from Europe and America, such as the US Pengzhe Quicksilver Company under the Shell brand which belongs to the largest automobile product company in the United States, and the Haot-Lowd Company under the UK Holleywell Group, have launched over eight hundred types of products just for car exterior beautification. These products are designed for different parts of the car and different seasons, and their advanced level is undeniable.

Gap between domestic and foreign car beauty industries

In some countries in Europe and America known as "nations built on wheels," although cars have long been a very common mode of transportation, car owners still take good care of their vehicles. They use specialized car shampoos for daily cleaning and maintenance, along with regular interior care and engine maintenance. As a result, when it's time to scrap the car, it still looks as good as new from the outside. In movies, we often see shiny new cars being smashed to pieces, and many people feel heartbroken. In fact, those cars are often only beautiful on the outside, while their internal parts are close to being scrapped, so there's nothing to regret. This shows how deep the work on car beauty is done by others.
