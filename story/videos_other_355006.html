Nanjing's real estate market sees a price-cutting trend, with the maximum drop nearing 13% (Part I)

by cscj010181 on 2010-05-12 16:17:35

,Ganjiakou second-hand house Nanjing Jiangbei real estate market suddenly saw a price cut yesterday. The super-large scale real estate market in Jiangbei has always taken the lead in Nanjing's development and sales volume. However, yesterday it suddenly acted! Following the fixed-price offer of 4500 yuan/sq m for more than 300 units by Xuri Shangcheng, Suning Properties launched another fixed-price strategy for its three residential projects in Qiaobei: Tianrun City offered a fixed price of 4088 yuan/sq m for 350 units, which is about 600 yuan/sq m cheaper than before, and Pingxixuan A10 second-hand house. The continuous discount promotions of Qiaobei residential projects make people wonder if the prices in Jiangbei can no longer hold up? Is this a sign of an overall drop in Nanjing housing prices?

[Price Drop] Three more residential projects launched fixed-price units

The super-large scale residential project Tianrun City, which surprisingly offered a fixed price of 4088 yuan/sq m, provided a total of 350 special-priced units distributed across different phases.

550 special-priced units account for 16% of the total available units.

Interestingly, two other residential projects in the same Jiangbei sector also implemented fixed prices: 150 units of Venetian Water City at a uniform price of 4480 yuan/sq m, and 50 units of Tianhua Green Valley at a uniform price of 4188 yuan/sq m. However, it was learned that these two projects have strictly limited the floors of the units, such as Project B targeting the first and top floors, and Project C targeting the first to sixth floors of high-rise buildings and the first and sixth floors of low-rise buildings.

In total, the 550 special-priced units account for what proportion of the available units on sale? According to data from Nanjing Online Real Estate, these three projects currently have a total of 3360 available units, which means the 550 special-priced units account for about 16% of the total available units, Linxiaobeili second-hand house.

An industry insider said that for a large player like Suning in Qiaobei, the 550 units spread across three projects are just a drop in the bucket, but considering the current total number of available units, the 16% ratio is still quite significant.

Sold to specific groups? Sales office says anyone can buy

Interestingly, Suning Properties' unified statement yesterday was: These 550 units from the three projects are specially selected collections to回馈 society and show social humanitarian care, targeting provincial and municipal model workers, advanced workers, military personnel, and others who have contributed to society.

However, when reporters separately asked these three projects yesterday, the sales staff all indicated there were no such restrictions, and buyers could purchase normally.

Why does this developer act so mysteriously? An industry insider speculated that the developer might be concerned that previous homebuyers might feel they bought at a loss and would demand refunds.

[Citizen Reactions] People who want to buy and those who don't both have their reasons.

Citizen Mr. Shi said that if the fixed-price discounts only apply to the first and top floors, it doesn't mean much. But he noticed that some fixed-price offers aren't exclusively for the first and top floors. For example, Project C also applies fixed-price discounts to the first to sixth floors of high-rise buildings, with units on the fourth and fifth floors priced at 4480 yuan/sq m, which is worth considering, Taipingqiaodongli community second-hand house.

Mr. Shi also believed that these fixed-price projects are generally hundreds of yuan per square meter cheaper than the prices he had been paying attention to, and with five or six hundred units involved, the scope of the price cut is quite extensive, Shuimutiancheng (Qingyuanxili) second-hand house. He decided to visit the site over the weekend.

However, some citizens are not impressed by the price cuts in Jiangbei projects. Citizen Ms. Lü said that she recently took a bus to look at houses in Jiangbei and got stuck in traffic for more than two hours on the bridge. By the time she arrived at the project, she had lost all enthusiasm. With such transportation conditions, living there in the future would be too troublesome. Even though the prices have dropped significantly, she is still hesitant and will not consider buying a house in Jiangbei for now.

[Industry Analysis] Qiaobei housing prices reaching five thousand yuan is a bubble burst.

Jiangbei projects last year aimed to break through five thousand yuan, but now it seems they've returned to their original state. Vice General Manager Shi Dong of Nanjing Wangshang Real Estate Research Institution analyzed that from a transaction average price of less than 3500 yuan/sq m in February last year, to struggling to maintain around 4500 yuan/sq m now, it's clear that breaking through 5000 yuan/sq m has failed.

This can also be seen from the comparison of sales volumes in the same period: In February this year, the sales volume of Pukou projects decreased by 74% month-on-month, putting pressure on developers.

According to the forecast of Wangshang Real Estate Research Institution, this year, Jiangbei will launch 3.313 million square meters of new commercial housing (slightly higher than 3.21 million square meters in 2007), including 2.865 million square meters of newly approved projects, leading among all sectors in Nanjing. This year, the 45 residential projects on sale in the Jiangbei sector will add 2.8 million square meters of new supply, and the concentrated and large-scale supply of commercial housing will inevitably intensify competition in the Qiaobei real estate market.
