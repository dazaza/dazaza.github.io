shwup - Online personal multimedia service to share photos, videos to the world

by smoa on 2008-12-22 11:47:45

shwup is a personal multimedia sharing service provided by muvee. After registering, users can share photos (jpg, bmp, png) or videos (avi, wmv, mpg, mov, gt, 3gp) in their own space, with a maximum of 100MB per file.

The site uses quite a bit of ajax functionality, the simple and elegant design really catches the eye of the user, and after uploading a photo or video, you can also share it with others through online bookmarking or Social networks, or display it to your own blog or website using embedded syntax.
