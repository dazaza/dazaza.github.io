How to ensure the quality and make money when you are buying things

by anonymous on 2011-04-01 12:09:07

From the day a person is born, he is destined to keep consuming. Everyone spends more when they earn more and less when they earn less. The daily consumption is limited, but what about a month, a year, ten years, or even a lifetime? What about the consumption of an entire family? All these add up to a huge number. In our daily lives, consumption is simply a relationship of buying and selling; you consume while the merchant gains profit. Now let's take a look at how a product reaches the consumer: Manufacturer - General Agent - Sub-Agent - Large/Medium/Small Wholesalers - Retailer - Consumer. In this sales process, every merchant needs to rent a store, pay for utilities and miscellaneous fees, and workers' wages, all of which increase the cost of the product, raising its price invisibly and harming the consumer's interests because, ultimately, the consumer always pays the bill.

Now let's examine direct selling. Guangzhou VIP Information Technology Co., Ltd. has only one intermediary between the manufacturer and the consumer - the direct seller. This greatly reduces the intermediate steps, not only lowering the price of the product but also increasing the profits for the manufacturer, direct seller, and consumer. At this point, all three parties benefit. Members of direct selling gain the qualification to sell products without a physical store by consuming the products. Each direct selling consumer transforms from a consumer to a "consumption merchant" who can sell all the products produced by the manufacturers without opening a store. In this way, the manufacturers lock in a group of consumers, and consumers can also benefit through the manufacturers. So, can friends who do direct selling make money? Companies like Perfect, Amway, SanSheng, New Era, etc., all share a common flaw: their direct selling products are single and somewhat expensive. Although the products are good, ordinary people neither recognize their brands nor their prices. Direct selling products are often series-based, and the singularity of the products makes direct selling difficult. Many direct selling products are health-related and quite expensive. Some people find them effective, while others don't, leading to potential negative social opinions. Additionally, some friends stockpile goods to advance their ranks, but stockpiling is a big no-no for merchants. Unsold products have expiration dates and end up as waste at home, causing losses and preventing direct sellers from making money. However, the concept of direct selling is indeed very good!

Joint online shopping e-commerce optimizes the difficulties of the direct selling industry. The products are diversified, all mass brand products recognized by ordinary people. Moreover, doing business online significantly reduces costs due to the widespread use of computers. More and more people are shopping online. According to reports on online shopping trends, China's internet users have exceeded 400 million, and more and more people are shopping online. It is evident that the trend of online shopping shows great prospects and sustainability. This can be said to be a family-wide beneficial entrepreneurial part-time project that everyone can participate in. However, competition among online shopping merchants is inevitable. In recent years, various models of online shopping e-commerce such as B2B, B2C, C2C, BMC have emerged, but no merchant has truly achieved the concept of "saving money + making money, entrepreneurship + employment." Many merchants merely attract consumers by offering a little savings through price wars. Ultimately, the consumer always pays the bill. Besides saving money, is there anything else? Even if some companies claim the slogan of "saving money + making money, entrepreneurship + employment," it is not integrated and can only be understood separately.

How can chain online shopping e-commerce carve out a bright path in the fiercely competitive online shopping market and become a dark horse among online shopping platforms? There is only one way: stand on the consumer's side to think about problems because the consumer is king. Whoever owns the consumer owns everything.
