When changing the home environment, designers often give us advice, such as using furniture arrangements to divide spaces or creating atmospheres through the placement of decorations. However, as the largest component of home area — walls, they are either fragmented or overly simplified. In fact, in today's world where decoration styles are diverse, we should pay more attention to walls because the color and pattern changes on walls can infinitely extend the meaning of life.

The appearance of wallpapers has changed the previous phenomenon of "bare-faced" home walls. As people's living interests and aesthetic qualities continue to improve, walls will become increasingly personalized, artistic, and humanized. In a new home, even if it is sparsely furnished, we can create beautiful scenery through unique style wallpapers. If you need to fix deficiencies after renovation or frequently change the home style, wallpapers can be an intimate helper for DIY enthusiasts.

◆ The Embodiment of Art and Personality

Wallpapers have high-quality textures, elegant colors, and soft materials. With various patterns and rich colors, they offer a sense of three-dimensionality and personality expression, making the home walls natural, lively, and vibrant. Whether it's digital compositions, cosmic space, green forests, or the blue Danube, different people can choose colors and patterns based on personal preferences and character, combined with corresponding paints. With a light brushstroke, all kinds of wonderful ideas and blueprints will be displayed on the wallpaper, thus creating infinite wonderful spaces and interpreting the new concept of wall decoration.

◆ Wide Range of Wallpaper Applications

As another form of wall expression, wallpapers have rich textures that bring a soft and gentle mood visually. The overall feeling is generous and luxurious. They are particularly suitable for home interior decoration and various high-demand occasions, and can also be used in hospitals, schools, offices, restaurants, shopping malls, and children's rooms.

Wallpapers are relatively high-end decoration materials, with prices higher than latex paint. They are calculated by the meter, varying depending on different widths, materials, and processes. In our city’s building material supermarket, fiberglass wallpapers are generally 1.2 meters wide, with lengths that can be cut at will. Prices usually range from 20 to 40 yuan per meter, while wool wallpapers are slightly more expensive, approximately 48 yuan per meter.

◆ Healthy and Eco-friendly, Easy to Change Colors

Updating wallpapers is simple and easy to implement. Wallpapers have rich textures and pattern combinations, with fine and delicate textures or elegant and generous appearances. With over two thousand color combinations and dozens of wallpaper patterns, they can create different decorative effects, fully showcasing personal styles.

In our city’s building material supermarkets, OBI and B&Q carry Swedish "Kleides" brand fiberglass wallpapers, while Homemate offers German "Auguter" brand wool fiber wallpapers. Although the types of wallpapers in our city are relatively limited, these two brands have multiple models and patterns to choose from. They use pure natural raw materials, ensuring environmental protection and health, with numerous patterns and bright colors. When used indoors, they do not fade or age, offering good fireproofing and moisture-proofing properties, being non-toxic, preventing microbial growth, anti-static, strong, durable, breathable, and washable. They also have sound insulation, sound absorption, and indoor temperature regulation functions, allowing the healthy living environment to breathe more warmth and joy.
