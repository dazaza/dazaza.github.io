City Bar City8 Beta is officially released

by lala on 2007-06-19 18:13:13

City8 beta major new features as follows, more detailed information can be found in http://www.city8.com/bulletin_full.html

1. Speed has been greatly optimized: users can walk smoothly in the city bar in City8, shopping, roaming

The test is better.

2. Reality annotation: Everyone can write in reality! You can write personalized messages or you can write public messages.

For example, mark a "thief here", or mark a public information "such and such a building"

3. City Bar Little C

Small C is the msn robot of the city bar, adding MSN "mm001@city8.com" can directly query the real scene in msn.

4. The channel of city navigation lists the life information of food, clothing, housing, transportation, amusement shopping in the city, which is convenient for locals and outsiders to understand the city.

5. 360-degree reality can be directly embedded in blogs, bbs and websites, and played directly in flash on the page.

6. Live maps of Guangzhou and Shenzhen are available online http://sz.city8.com, http://gz.city8.com
