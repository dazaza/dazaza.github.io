Introduction:

It goes without saying that when men grow up, they should get married, and women should marry. Men, besides doing business, their biggest wish is how to marry a good daughter-in-law. If you marry a good daughter-in-law, life will be more stable...

Mao Doudou, an emergency room nurse, and Yu Wei, a photographer at a portrait studio, went on a blind date, but encountered Mao Doudou's first love, Li Ruoqiu. The two blind dates ended unhappily.

In the evening, Mao Doudou's younger brother - avant-garde magician Mao Feng - brought his girlfriend Qin Susu home for dinner and announced that the two would soon get married. Mao Doudou's parents had no way to deal with their son, and the wedding was held as scheduled. However, the next day after the wedding, the groom announced that he was afraid of marriage and disappeared by himself, and proposed divorce. Helpless, Su Su proposed to leave her 7-year-old daughter with Mao Doudou's family for care as a condition for divorce. A day of marriage ended like this.

Mao Doudou started continuous blind dates. Incredibly, every time different people introduced the same person - Yu Wei. Resigned to fate, Mao Doudou and Yu Wei couldn't help but take a serious look at each other. Yu Wei frankly explained the purpose of consecutive blind dates. It turned out that he had been pretending to be his drowned childhood friend and sending his own photos to his grandmother for many years. Now, because of old age, the grandmother hoped to see her "grandson" find a wife soon. Yu Wei did it to fulfill the elder's wish. Moved by Mao Doudou's kindness, the two gradually developed feelings. Soon they reached the stage of meeting both sets of parents. Yu Wei told Mao Doudou in advance about his complicated family situation - "two fathers, two mothers, and a sister who lost her father on the second day of her wedding." He said that his father and mother divorced and remarried respectively, and the two mothers were irreconcilable. After much hesitation, Mao Doudou finally gathered the courage and asked Yu Wei to arrange a meeting between both sets of parents. The situation was predictable, and Mao Doudou was caught in the middle, struggling to cope.

Finally, Yu Wei and Mao Doudou got married. After the wedding, when they returned home, Yu Wei's sister who lost her father informed them that she would live with Yu Wei and his wife. Despite their complaints, the two had to force smiles and welcome her.

Life after marriage was relatively peaceful, although Yu Wei's sister, Yu Hao, constantly nitpicked and caused trouble, everything passed smoothly due to Mao Doudou's tolerance and carefree nature. However, the existence of two mothers-in-law truly troubled Mao Doudou. Even the number of visits and gifts she gave to each mother-in-law could be used as evidence of favoritism.

On one side, conflicts arose continuously, while on the other side, Mao Doudou's younger brother Mao Feng was preparing to marry a wealthy woman named Long Jin when he was abandoned by her. He was very disheartened because previously, only he had left women, not the other way around. In his despair, he met a rural girl named Pan Meili, whom he called an "original ecology" girl. Surprisingly, the two quickly got married amidst everyone's astonishment. Soon, Pan Meili's various "original ecology" qualities before marriage began to annoy Mao Feng. Being naturally fickle, he started entangling with Long Jin again. However, Pan Meili continued to treat him with simple affection.

Yu Wei's stepfather was suddenly diagnosed with cancer, and Yang Yifan, his son, rushed back from the island. He was a soldier guarding the island. Faced with the proud Yang Yifan, Yu Hao was somewhat moved. Because of Yang Yifan's important and busy work, he couldn't stay at home for long. Yu Hao and Mao Doudou took the initiative to take care of Yang's father. When Yang Yifan returned to the island, he didn't have time to get off the train and followed it to the next station. Yang Yifan was worried about Yu Hao taking the train back alone, so he accompanied her for a while. During the journey, the two exchanged more, and when they parted, there was something special in their eyes.

On the other side, Mao Feng, who re-entangled with Long Jin, believed in her get-rich-quick plan and encouraged friends and relatives around him to invest in Long Jin's so-called project. Pan Meili, who deeply loved and trusted him, even encouraged her parents and siblings to participate in the investment. While everyone was dreaming of getting rich, Long Jin disappeared. Mao Feng realized he had been tricked by Long Jin again and became extremely depressed. At this moment, Pan Meili stood by him and told him she was willing to start over with him. Mao Feng was deeply moved.

Although Yang's father's mental state remained good under everyone's care, the disease was merciless. Yang's father fell critically ill and hoped to see his son one more time. Yang Yifan hurried back, but he didn't get to see his father again. He only saw his father's will, which shocked everyone. Yang's father hoped to be buried with Yang Yifan's mother—Yang Shu's ex-wife after his death. No one dared to tell Yu Wei's mother about this.

Yang Yifan would soon return to his unit. Yu Hao went to see him off again. The train started moving, and Yu Hao still didn't get off the train, but this time she chose to stay behind herself. She mustered the courage to confess her feelings to Yang Yifan, expressing her willingness to become the wife of a soldier.

Yu Wei's mother soon learned about Yang's father's last wish. Initially, she couldn't accept it and became extremely upset, turning many unintentional actions of Mao Doudou and others into points of blame. But under the children's comfort and tolerance, she finally let go and fulfilled Yang's father's wish.

Yang Yifan didn't want to burden Yu Hao because he was stationed on the island for a long time, but Yu Hao was now exceptionally determined. The two met at the train station because Yang Yifan was going to military school. As the train started moving, Yu Hao jumped onto the train again. She had decided to marry this soldier.

At Yang Yifan and Yu Hao's wedding, Mao Doudou's two mothers-in-law also reconciled under the encouragement of a group of young people.

The three daughters-in-law faced the future life with confidence...
