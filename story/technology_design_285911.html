Winter color matching trend

by janydtfw98 on 2009-11-29 22:04:21

Women are always the forerunners of fashion, hanging happiness on themselves with various colors, like lively butterflies jumping above the trends, exuding unique colors and youthful audacity. In the winter of 08, optimistic and sweet makeup colors are back again. Pink, orange-red, apricot-brown color schemes are the main colors you can't miss this winter. Are they suitable for you? What color clothing should be paired with them to achieve the most harmonious effect? Let's learn now.

Pink matching pictures

One: Pink and peach color schemes

Color matching review: Sweet and approachable, you possess an adorable feminine charm and will become the focus of attention wherever you go. This season's pink color scheme is no longer just a color for little girls; what you will see are various elegant and trendy pink feelings, such as peach pink, honey pink, lilac pink...

Skin tone matching:

Fair skin: A pity not to try.

Yellowish skin: Very good.

Dark skin: Carefully match your clothing, wear fashionable personal styles, and pink will suit you too.

Makeup:

Foundation: A thin layer of foundation that closely resembles natural skin tone is sufficient.

Eyes: Pink-colored eyeshadow blended at the double eyelid area, slightly deeper color at the lash line and outer corner of the eye. Lower eyeliner can be drawn according to your preference. Mascara is indispensable.

Blush: Pink blush can be applied to the position you want. To show a cute quality, it can be applied to the laugh muscles.

Lips: Pink lip gloss complements this makeup look best.

Clothing coordination:

Large areas of pink, nude, beige, khaki can be used, and handbags can be black, bright red, navy blue, etc.

Orange-red matching picture

Two: Orange-red color scheme:

Color matching review: It makes you unconsciously reveal a look that men cannot resist. You have strong opinions, high control, enjoy a life full of fun, and are full of feminine charm and sensuality.

Skin tone matching:

Fair skin: Looks good, looks good

Yellowish skin: A very suitable color for you

Dark skin: You need to avoid bright red, try orange-red, it will make you look very individualistic.

Makeup:

Foundation: A naturally transparent tone is most effective.

Eyes: Try orange-red or powder green eyeshadow, note that the area should not be too large.

Blush: Apply orange-red blush however you want.

Lips: The lips are the focal point of this makeup style. Of course, it's also orange-red.

Clothing coordination:

Dark-colored clothing like black and gray best highlights the charm and brightness of orange, making it the preferred color scheme. If you feel it's too dull, bright yellow and yellow are also great choices.

Three: Apricot-brown color scheme:

Color matching review: Let you become the new aristocrat of high society, mature, steady, simple, and decisive, always actively facing the future, radiating elegance everywhere.

Skin tone matching:

Fair skin: Extremely stylish

Yellowish skin: Most suitable

Dark skin: Add a bit of pearl powder or pair with green eyeshadow, both will make you stand out.

Makeup:

Foundation: Natural principle is definitely correct.

Eyes: There are many eyeshadow colors that can be paired with apricot-brown makeup, brown, orange, yellow are all good. It is recommended to draw eyeliner or use mascara to brush your lashes long and curled for surprising results.

Blush: Apricot-brown blush suits sweeping diagonally from the ear root into the cheek, showing intellectual beauty.

Lips: Matte apricot-brown lipstick matches well with the makeup.

Clothing coordination:

Earth-tone clothing, or effects with strong color contrast are best. Brown jackets, hunting outfits, dark blue jeans are also very eye-catching.
