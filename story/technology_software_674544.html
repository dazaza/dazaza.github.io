"Order-based talents" directly supplied to enterprises

To address the labor needs within the zone, yesterday, the Development Zone Administrative Committee signed a talent development cooperation framework agreement with the Haihe Education Park Administrative Committee. In the future, the Development Zone will collaborate with the Haihe Education Park to "order-based" cultivate various types of talents for enterprises within the zone that have labor requirements. Additionally, the TEDA Sino-German Vocational and Technical Training Center co-sponsored by the Development Zone and the Haihe Education Park was officially inaugurated yesterday.

In recent years, with the establishment and production of a number of large projects in the Development Zone, the demand for talent in the entire region has been increasing day by day. Party Secretary and Director of the Administrative Committee of the Development Zone, He Shushan, told reporters at the signing ceremony that since the establishment of the Haihe Education Park, the Development Zone has built a platform for对接cooperation with the first batch of seven colleges that moved into the park. To date, the Changzheng Rocket Company of the Development Zone has consecutively opened a "Rocket Order Class" for two years at the Sino-German Vocational and Technical College. Other enterprises have also conducted various cooperations such as work-study alternation and top-post internships with the colleges within the education park. Last year, the seven colleges of the education park provided more than 4,000 graduates and interns to enterprises in the Development Zone.

Reporters learned that after the signing, the Development Zone and the Haihe Education Park will launch a series of measures on the basis of previous cooperation, including the "Integrated Talent Collaborative Development Plan" and the joint construction of a "High-skilled Talent Cultivation Base", making technical talent cultivation more targeted. Jin Runcheng, Director of the Municipal Education Commission and Director of the Haihe Education Park Administrative Committee, introduced that through cooperation, one can achieve that whatever kind of talent the enterprise needs, the college will set up corresponding majors; secondly, it can provide "accelerated" training for employees currently working in enterprises; thirdly, enterprises in the Development Zone can provide internship and training venues for students during their studies; fourthly, senior engineering technicians from enterprises can serve as teachers in colleges within the education park, teaching students with practical experience. This not only helps the Development Zone transform its economic development model but also facilitates the education park in transforming its talent cultivation model. "Currently, the first batch of 48 enterprises within the Development Zone have established对接relationships with seven colleges in the Haihe Education Park, cultivating talents in an order-based manner. The cultivation direction and curriculum settings of these order-based talents will be determined through negotiations between enterprises and universities within the park, enabling talents to start working immediately upon being supplied to the enterprise," said Deputy Director of the Development Zone Administrative Committee, Lang Dong.
