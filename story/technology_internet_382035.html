Top 10 WebOS App Downloads for HP Palm in 2010

by shootero on 2011-02-23 23:58:38

**Facebook for Palm**

Download Link: Facebook for Palm can sync your friends to your phone's contact list and automatically retrieve the latest status updates of your friends. This app also integrates seamlessly with the camera function, allowing you to conveniently upload photos and videos to Facebook.

**Tweed**

Download Link: Tweed is one of the best clients on WebOS. It fully supports search, trends, geolocation, and multi-account login. Its biggest feature is integration with WebOS push notifications.

**Weatherman 2.0**

Download Link: A weather app is indispensable, and Weatherman can display weather information on your lock screen. It can automatically determine your city based on GPS information, but you can also add cities via postal codes.

**Evernote**

Download Link: The famous note-taking software allows your WebOS device to take quick notes, create task lists, and capture web pages. It supports Evernote account synchronization and has been consistently recommended by long-term users. If you're a paid user, you'll get more cloud storage capacity.

**pReader**

Download Link: pReader is an excellent reading app that supports multiple e-book formats: Txt, HTML, PalmDoc, MobiPocket, ePub, and Amazon AZW documents. Its interface is clean and simple, supporting both portrait and landscape reading modes. The only downside might be Palm's small screen.

**FlightTrack**

Download Link: As its name suggests, this is a flight-tracking app. Its main function is to keep you informed about flight statuses, delays, and cancellations. After selecting a flight, it will send push notifications for status updates, making it very convenient.

**Trapster**

Download Link: Trapster is a driver's helper that can help you avoid traffic violations. It displays speed check cameras, police checkpoints, and other traps on a map. Recently, Trapster was acquired by Navteq, which has now become part of Nokia. So whether this great app will continue on WebOS remains uncertain.

**drPodder**

Download Link: drPodder is a podcasting app that downloads and plays popular audio and video content from the internet. It supports playlists and bookmarks and can be controlled with wired or wireless headphones.

**Grooveshark**

Download Link: Grooveshark lets you listen to popular online music. You can edit and save your playlists and then sync them. Unfortunately, due to copyright issues, this app doesn't receive frequent updates.

**mCraig**

Download Link: mCraig is a local service app that finds nearby ads based on your phone's GPS information. For example, it can help you find items for sale or job listings nearby.
