Secrets to Saving Money on Home Renovation for the Budget-Conscious: Part One

by longersing on 2009-07-26 00:26:46

In Shanghai, many of the new generation of consumers have begun to accept the buy-first pay-later method of purchasing homes. Mortgages have allowed these young people to own their own space, but it also brings many concerns: after buying a house, how can we set aside a sum of money for decoration under the pressure of loan repayment? The interior designers in Shanghai are also troubled by the design price and unable to design exquisite and practical works. So don't worry, there are ways to decorate even if you don't have much money. Let us teach you how to achieve the most luxurious effect with the least amount of money. In this issue, Shanghai Decoration Network will take you into the first series of poor man's decoration secrets.

Note: All price-related information mentioned in this article are from the three years between 2006-2009 and do not represent the average consumption level or prices in the future.

1. Wall base treatment costs 3 yuan per square meter.

Tip: If your base is quite good, you can save on this part. This part cannot be seen in the decoration quotation. In general, it will be included in the wall coating or wall base repair. The usual marking method is: wall leveling repair, calculated based on actual usage. However, overall construction is required under conditions such as electronic slotting or internal wall insulation.

2. Wall painting with plant-based Fuya six-in-one paint plus wall primer costs 27 yuan per square meter.

Tip: If you use primer mode, you can use 821 putty. If not, try not to use 821 putty because it easily bubbles and falls off, and has large particles. The wall size is generally calculated by multiplying the floor area by 3.5 to get the wall area, but it is usually a bit more, so multiply by 3. However, for accuracy, it is still best to handle it according to the actual situation on site.

Basic methods for selecting coatings: After opening the lid, truly environmentally friendly latex paint should be water-based, non-toxic, and odorless. After a period of time, a thick elastic oxide film will form on the surface of genuine latex paint, which is not easy to crack. Stir the latex paint evenly with a wooden stick, then lift it up with the wooden stick. High-quality latex paint will form a fan-shaped shape when it flows down. Touch with your fingers, genuine latex paint should feel smooth and delicate. When purchasing, check the components. High-quality coatings should contain copolymer resin or pure acrylic resin. Don't forget to check the product's expiration date.
