According to the records checked by the victim's family at the sports hall, on the day of the incident from 14:00 to 15:00, Zhang booked a court under her name at a sports hall on the west side of the Fourth Ring Road.

Reporters (reporter Zhang Yuan) Eight minutes after leaving work, the victim was found covered in blood under the Wukesong Bridge. On July 25 last year at noon, veteran music editor Jiao from the Central People's Broadcasting Station was murdered. Recently, defendant Huang has been prosecuted for intentional injury at the First Intermediate Court, claiming that he suspected his wife of having an improper relationship with Jiao.

The prosecution believes that Huang's actions constitute intentional injury. However, the family of victim Jiao believes that Huang left the injured Jiao by the roadside, knowingly causing the death of another person, and either hoped for or allowed such results to occur, and should be charged with intentional homicide. This week, the First Intermediate Court will publicly hear the case.

The defendant's wife denies infidelity.

Using a wine bottle to beat the victim.

According to autopsy reports, Jiao died from severe head injuries caused by being struck on the head and face with a blunt object.

The materials in the case file show that Huang, born in 1979, was the manager of the Event Operations Department of CCTV Sports Entertainment Co., Ltd. before the incident. He and his wife Zhang were university classmates, and they have a four-year-old son.

The victim was discovered by passersby who then reported it to the police.

At the same time, the families of Jiao, the security department of the Central People's Broadcasting Station, and the workplace of Zhang’s husband Huang received calls from Zhang saying, "Jiao was hit by my husband, we are surrendering." Subsequently, Huang was taken into custody by the police.

The defendant's wife denies infidelity.

On the issue of infidelity alleged by Huang, both Zhang and Jiao's families deny it. Zhang testified that Jiao worked in technology at the station, "Sometimes when Jiao repaired things for me, I would treat him to dinner, and we would also play badminton together."

According to the autopsy report, Jiao died from severe head injuries caused by being struck with a blunt object on the head and face.

Jiao was then sent to the PLA 301 Hospital, where tests revealed a skull contusion, bilateral pulmonary congestion, etc. Despite efforts to save him, he died three days later.

Case file materials show that at 12:30 on July 25, 2011, young woman Guo just exited the subway and saw the victim Jiao at the northeast corner of the Wukesong Bridge. "That man was lying in the accumulated water by the side of the road with his head towards the road and his face towards the sky. I saw blood on his right hand and forehead, so I quickly called 110 to report it."

"I felt that the two of them had an inappropriate relationship, and I was very angry, so I wanted to ask clearly." Huang said that he got out of the car and grabbed an empty wine bottle, "After Jiao got out of the car, I used the wine bottle in my right hand to strike his upper body and kicked his legs."

Huang said that he and his wife had been married for nine years, but they had been arguing nonstop since the beginning of last year, and "they often mentioned Jiao, so I suspected that Zhang might have had an affair."
