Task.fm: A Convenient Schedule Management and Reminder Service

by xjyzhenai on 2009-05-18 14:40:38

The pace of life is getting faster and faster, and people's time is getting tighter. The management and arrangement of time are becoming particularly important. When there are too many things to do, it's easy to forget this or that.

You can use some tools to help you manage your daily schedule. Once a task is due, it can automatically remind you, just like a little secretary helping you manage your time. So JarryJarry introduces Task.fm to you: a convenient schedule management and reminder service.

From the moment you log in to Task.fm, you no longer have to worry about always forgetting things. Task.fm provides a convenient "one sentence" operation method. When you need to add a task, just input a sentence in a fixed English format, and it's done. However, Task.fm has good support for Chinese.

The format is like this: e.g - meeting with bob tomorrow at 2pm. Therefore, we can write:

Visiting IT.Net from Mars today at 5pm

A new item will be added to the task list below:

For the tasks in the task list, you can disable, modify, and delete them. Of course, you can also use an advanced way to set up a reminder task:

And Task.fm can deliver reminders in the form of email and mobile text messaging services. It should be noted that using the text message method to deliver reminders requires payment. Registering for Task.fm gives you 10 credit points as a gift. One credit point can send one text message (including international text messages), and 1 dollar is equivalent to 10 credit points.

Task.fm's interface is simple and low-key, its functions are relatively single, making everything clear at a glance. Plus, the "one sentence" task addition method indeed saves us a lot of time. Registering gives you 10 credit points, enough for you to experience the schedule management and reminder services from abroad.
