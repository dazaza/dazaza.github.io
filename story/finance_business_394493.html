Together, let's explore traditional Chinese medicine beauty. Written by Whitening Delight in Intensive Care.

Today, we change our taste and return to our traditional treasure - Traditional Chinese Medicine (TCM) adjustment. TCM not only focuses on treating diseases and saving lives but also excels in beauty adjustments. Throughout history, countless TCM practitioners have spent their entire lives devising ways to enhance the beauty of palace women, leaving behind a wealth of experience that is absolutely worth our attention!

So, everyone, let's follow the editor and delve into TCM beauty together~

"TCM Beauty" carries profound Chinese cultural heritage. After thousands of years of beauty practice, the folk beauty and health methods passed down through generations embody infinite oriental wisdom.

Whether it is the ancient imperial court or modern cities, women have always been thoughtful about how to make themselves more beautiful.

And "TCM Beauty" with its internal and external adjustments, and the concept of achieving health and beauty from within, is increasingly revered by people who value beauty and health.

However, now TCM beauty products come in all shapes and sizes. What kind of method will be effective for you? What kind of product will benefit you? And how do you find a product suitable for yourself? I think this is what everyone is most concerned about, and also the most headache-inducing problem.

For this reason, I have visited supermarkets, malls, and beauty salons. Later, I found an issue: in supermarkets and malls, the products are mostly not TCM products, whereas some beauty salons do use TCM ones. When asking the merchants, without exception, they all introduce the merits of their products, telling me what can be achieved after using them, and that customers say they are very good. But these are ultimately just the merchants' opinions, and it's hard for us to find actual users to understand unless our friends around us are using them. I'm not saying their products are bad; each product that survives in the market must have a certain customer base. Personally, I still place more emphasis on our traditional TCM beauty products.

Because of my work, I often surf online, so I decided to use the internet to search. Taobao.com is the largest shopping platform in China. Usually, I often buy clothes and various international brand cosmetics on it. But I haven't yet understood our own TCM beauty products online. After searching, there are quite a few items here. I decided to ask a store with relatively high credibility.

I clicked into a TCM beauty store named "Green Woods." Honestly, what initially attracted me was the image of an ancient beauty on their products. Beauty is something that appeals to both men and women. After reading their introduction, it turned out they were from a family of TCM practitioners, and the products in the small shop were exclusively formulated by their old TCM doctor. This piqued my curiosity, imagining if they could also formulate a secret recipe for me, turning me into a youthful and invincible young girl overnight. After reading more about a few products, I discovered that the old TCM doctor in this shop, around 50 years old, looked like he was only 35 in his photo. He was truly impressive, making me admire and envy him. Thinking that if I look like this at 50, it would be great.

Thinking about this, I decided to consult the store as a customer to see how they introduced their products to me.

Editor: Shop owner, hello, are your products purely TCM?

Shop Owner: Hello, they are all pure TCM. Is there anything you need?

Editor: I want to understand first.

Shop Owner: Sure, no problem. What aspect would you like to know about?

Editor: Your products are said to be formulated by an old TCM doctor. How long will it take to see results?

Shop Owner: Yes, they are formulated by an old TCM doctor. TCM emphasizes internal and external adjustments. It differs from general chemical or hormone-containing beauty products. The effects won't be immediate, but they can address both symptoms and root causes, fundamentally solving the problems. Our products have a course of one month, and the speed of effectiveness varies from person to person. Some people can see results after one course, while others may need two courses or more to see effects. Generally, the longer you use it, the better the results.

Editor: Are there any customers who say they didn't see any effect?

Shop Owner: Yes, some customers are impatient. They stop using it after a few times, so they say it's ineffective. Our products are not magic pills, so it's impossible to get satisfactory results after just a few uses. For example, some customers have very yellowish complexions, are still young, and don't have other ailments. These customers generally suffer from qi and blood deficiency, so we adopt dual supplementation of qi and blood, along with internal and external adjustments. For such customers, the adjustment usually takes at least two months. Some customers give up halfway, claiming the product is ineffective. However, those who persist always see results.

Editor: It seems that beautifying through TCM cannot be rushed.

Shop Owner: Yes, because everyone's condition is different, the time needed for adjustment will vary. As long as customers can persist, their skin will significantly improve compared to before.

Editor: Okay, thank you, shop owner. I've learned a bit. I'll come back to buy when I need it next time. Wishing you booming business!

Shop Owner: Sure, no need to be polite. Welcome to visit anytime.

The editor believes that the quality of a product depends on its reputation. The store has decent credibility with many repeat customers. The editor decided to investigate the feedback from their customers to verify the authenticity. Among the numerous online buyers, I chose one with a high credit rating. After explaining my intentions, she was happy to chat with me. Below is a main segment of our conversation (to protect her privacy, I'll refer to her as Xiao A).

Editor: How long have you been using this store's TCM beauty products?

Xiao A: About 8 months ago. At that time, due to staying up late for a long time, my skin was in very bad condition, my complexion was dull, my skin was dry, I had some fine lines, and even developed spots. Since I have sensitive skin, I dared not use random spot-removal products and was looking for a gentle TCM spot-removal product. I stumbled upon this store unintentionally and started trying it out then. Seeing good results later, I continued using it.

Editor: Which products did you use?

Xiao A: The shop owner recommended a Whitening Spot Removal Jade Complexion Pill and a Comprehensive Whitening Spot Removal Mask for me based on my skin condition. She also recommended a Moisturizing Anti-Wrinkle Mask.

Editor: Did it really work?

Xiao A: Yes, after one month of use, my complexion became much more even, my face wasn't as dry, the spots faded slightly, and my skin became a little whiter. Although I wasn't super white, I wasn't as sallow as before.

Editor: How has your skin improved now?

Xiao A: Now, my skin is truly whiter and softer. The spots have become much lighter and are barely noticeable. With foundation on, they're completely invisible. The fine lines have also become much shallower, and my skin looks well-hydrated and radiant. My skin even appears rosy in the morning. Honestly, I am very satisfied with the current results. Moreover, I've introduced some friends to try it, and they also say the effects are good.

Editor: Were there any side effects?

Xiao A: No, since the shop owner inquired about my physical condition before recommending the products, I didn't notice any abnormalities. However, because the efficacy of TCM is slow, it does take longer.

Editor: Are you still using it now?

Xiao A: Yes. I've been taking the Whitening Complexion Pill for six months. I've been consistently using the Comprehensive Mask and the Moisturizing Anti-Wrinkle Mask twice a week for eight months. The shop owner suggests I take the Jade Complexion Pill every two months for another two months since it can also prevent aging. Women fear aging the most, haha.

Editor: It seems our long-standing beauty treasure is indeed excellent.

Xiao A: Yes, you can try it. Talk with the shop owner.

Eternal youth is a beautiful wish of humanity, but all natural things always go through birth to death, prosperity to decline. As a part of nature, humans cannot escape this objective law. Therefore, with age, the human body will also grow old.

In TCM, although it's impossible to stay forever young, by replenishing the deficiencies of the organs and blood and qi, maintaining the balance of yin and yang in the body, it can indeed delay the arrival of aging. In today's world where people advocate nature and green concepts, TCM, with its five-thousand-year accumulated strength, still has its unique charm in preventing aging and beauty health care.
