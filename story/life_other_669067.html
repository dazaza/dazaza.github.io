When you meet someone, you don't necessarily have to fall in love, or commit to each other.

by gege on 2012-02-18 20:43:54

When you encounter someone, it's not necessary to fall in love or stay together.

In this world, there are millions of leaves and millions of kinds of people. You may exchange greetings, shake hands, or pass by each other, but it's not necessary to fall in love or stay together. All you need to do is learn how to appreciate.

Having a good friend is much more down-to-earth than having a relationship. In one’s life, every heartfelt investment might cause harm. Friends are different. While having friends, you can savor the purity of humanity and the preciousness of true feelings. Friendship is also a kind of love, a higher and more sincere kind of love.

When you get to know and befriend someone with a calm heart, you will have fewer private thoughts and desires. Then you can interact freely and casually, and your hearts will gradually merge. A true friend will come to you through your appreciative eyes. Friendship is also an indispensable part of life. Only when you have many sincere friends will you feel the richness and happiness of life.

Everyone has flesh, blood, and a soul, and they all emit different kinds of beauty. Every beautiful quality is attractive. At any time, if you learn to view the world and the people around you with an appreciative eye, you will face everything more calmly. The greatest weakness of human nature is the desire to possess, to own everything you like. But humans also have minds that change constantly, and no emotion is eternally unchanged. Therefore, don't fantasize about owning too much. Learn to appreciate someone with a calm heart, just as you would enjoy a painting. You will be happy and at ease.

Different people with different tastes will have different feelings about the same scenery. The crystal-clear snow-capped mountains have a pure beauty, the babbling brooks have a graceful natural beauty, and the vast sea has a broad and unrestrained beauty. Each kind of beauty gives people different shocks and is pleasing to the eye.

Outstanding people radiate an irresistible charm. They not only attract you but also those who have the same appreciation ability as you. Just like beautiful scenery, its existence is not for a mountain or a wilderness, but for the entire nature, to embellish this beautiful world, to let more people admire, savor, and indulge in it.

When you fall in love with someone, you want to completely possess them, including their thoughts, emotions, and the whole person. But after each moment of being moved, can you really possess them for long? There are no forever unchanging thoughts, nor forever unique emotions. So what matters is not love, but appreciation.

Some say that the most important thing between men and women is to know and love each other. In fact, in your life, there will be far more than one person who truly touches you. Should you love them all? That's very unrealistic. Love is a narrow emotion that requires full dedication. It's beautiful but independent and exclusive.

We live in a colorful world. In this world, there are not only beautiful landscapes but also people with different personalities, different temperaments, and different personal charms. In the long journey of life, you will meet and get to know many people. Different people have different qualities and charms. Appreciation, liking, and love become the hardest measures for us to grasp. And we ourselves should learn to be wise in life.
