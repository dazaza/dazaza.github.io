George Lam Starts New Romance, Claims Incompatible Life with Sally Yeh Makes Them Only Nominal Couple

Recently, reports have surfaced indicating that the 12-year marriage between Sally Yeh and George Lam is once again under strain. The reason cited is George Lam's alleged secret affair with female artist Vivian Chien. In response to this rumor, Sally Yeh's camp has chosen not to comment. However, in an exclusive interview earlier this year, George Lam disclosed that he and Sally Yeh had incompatible lifestyles, making them only a nominal couple.

George Lam Meets New Love on the Golf Course

George Lam is known for his love of golf, and it is rumored that he met the alluring beauty Vivian Chien on the golf course—his current secret lover. It is said that this alluring beauty, Vivian Chien, comes from a wealthy family and starred in several films a few years ago. Due to her striking appearance and curvaceous figure, she has been linked romantically with various male artists in the industry. This time, however, she has reportedly started a clandestine relationship with veteran music icon George Lam, truly proving herself to be quite a formidable woman! Despite this, Sally Yeh's camp continues to remain silent about the matter, leaving everyone uncertain about her stance regarding Vivian Chien's involvement.

The Power Couple No Longer Exists in Recent Years

In a previous media interview, Sally Yeh revealed that she and her husband had been sleeping in separate rooms for several years after their marriage. She explained that when they first began living together, George Lam would rise early every morning to play golf, which she found difficult to adjust to. Later, her husband suggested that they could sleep separately if needed. At that time, she stated that separating was actually a comfortable arrangement. Regarding their incompatible living habits, George Lam admitted during a journalist interview earlier this year, saying, "I enjoy golf, but she isn't interested. She finds outdoor activities too sunny and prefers indoor sports, so I usually go to play golf alone."

George Lam and Sally Yeh fell in love through the song "Choice," and for many years afterward, regardless of whose concert it was, they would always sing this song. For several years after their marriage, they performed as a power couple during concerts. However, in recent years, this dynamic has ceased to exist. For instance, during George Lam's Guangzhou concert earlier this year, his wife did not appear as a guest performer. When journalists asked why he didn't invite his wife as a guest, he explained that she had already served as a guest before, and continuing to invite her would lack novelty. Yet, during those years when they appeared as a couple at every performance, there was no sense of lacking novelty, raising indeed intriguing questions.
