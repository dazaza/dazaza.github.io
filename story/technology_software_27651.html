Welcome to download and experience Yahoo Messenger version 8.3!

New version of efficiency manual plug-in, important matters in advance notice;

Chat record management, save all chat records of users by default;

Find friends function integration, simplify the search for known ID friends and real-time online users of the operation process;

Optimized the expression icon, the mood is more warm, cute, witty, humorous;

Spam report function, receive stranger information can choose to block as spam or add as a friend.
