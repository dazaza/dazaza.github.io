Upgrading Weapons: I have upgraded Wuji, Yin She, Li Bian, Magic Wand, Dragon Pattern, and Kitchen Knife. Overall, the success rate for upgrading weapons is high for the first two points, usually around 90%. The formula I use for upgrading our DS weapons is similar. For the first point, the formula for upgrading Wuji, Yin She, Li Bian, and Dragon Pattern is: Use black iron ore randomly (people upgrading weapons to level 4 dare not use much black iron ore, but I prefer to use it) 4-20 pieces are fine, the total number should not exceed 6 pieces, the total purity added together should be greater than the durability of the weapon you want to upgrade by 4 points, which is about right. For Taoist ornaments, use a pair of Taoist rings, a pair of Loulan hands, (or use a Si Nuo). Normally, I haven't failed to upgrade this point. For the second point, the total purity of black iron ore needs to exceed the weapon's 8 points, double the jewelry, (if originally 1 pair, use 2 pairs, if originally 2 pairs, use 4 pairs), immediately upgrade, immediately retrieve, success rate 80%. For the third point, the total purity of black iron ore needs to exceed the weapon's 10 points, double the jewelry, immediately upgrade, wait 5 minutes to retrieve, success rate 60%. As for the fourth point, I have succeeded relatively few times, so I won't elaborate here!
