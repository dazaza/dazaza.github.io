by topqrrufn on 2011-05-03 19:50:13

Leadership said: Happiness is a relatively long period of satisfaction with life and feeling that life is full of great fun and naturally wanting to continue the long-term happy mood. This is both the goal each person pursues, and the ultimate goal for all humanity.

Happiness is - when I'm hungry and see someone else holding a meat bun, he is happier than me; when I'm cold and see someone wearing a thick cotton-padded jacket, he is happier than me; when I want to go to the toilet and there's only one pit and you are squatting there, you are happier than me. This is a satire of happiness in a movie.

Happiness is having a grateful heart; possessing a healthy body; having a group of trustworthy friends; having a harmonious family and a hopeful future.

Happiness is a feeling, it does not depend on people's living conditions, but on their mindset. When you feel happy, everything seems so beautiful. A person working in the field, recalling how sweet it was like eating puff pastries, sweating all over, but he feels happy, then he is happy; another person walking in his own garden, but he feels himself very unfortunate, then he is not happy. In fact, if you feel happy, then you are happy, whether happy or not lies in your own heart...
