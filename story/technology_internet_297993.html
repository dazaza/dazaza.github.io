Zou Shenglong: Verbal spats always have positive and negative aspects, and there will definitely be an impact. However, it is difficult to evaluate the comprehensive effects right now. Taking Xunlei Kankan as an example, from the blockbuster movie "If You Are the One" at the beginning of this year to the current "Secret" and "The Founding of a Republic," all are legally purchased movies, which are contributions to the industry. Second, these companies, especially since entering 2009, whether it is Youku or Xunlei, have all reached annual revenues in the hundreds of millions scale. That is, real GDP has appeared in this niche market. Since you can make money here, you can take part of the earnings and distribute them to your upstream content providers, who can then produce better content, starting a virtuous cycle.
