Keke Tong "Calling Friends and Companions" Guess the Song Name Event Launch

by yinyin on 2007-05-29 13:43:14

All kinds of star-making activities have been heard of, but none of them are about making "listening stars". On May 28, the Keke Tong company launched a Big Ears music event called "Calling Friends" to guess song titles, which can be considered another innovation in discovering and creating "listening stars".

In fact, the Keke Tong (KKTONE) "Calling Friends" Guess the Song Title activity is a game where players listen to songs and guess their titles. During the game, the website automatically provides a link that users who are guessing the songs can send to their friends via a "help" function, allowing their friends to help identify the song. After logging in through www.kktone.cn, participants can join this activity.

It is understood that Keke Tong is a domestically produced free music tool software, lightweight, practical, and powerful. It includes functions such as music playback, automatic correction of music data, lyric search, and information search, meeting the various needs of music lovers. KKTONE is like a musical all-rounder that can easily manage your music, categorize it, and find relevant information. It features music acoustic fingerprint recognition technology, which identifies songs through melodies, matches lyrics more accurately, including singer, album, album cover, lyrics, and MVs. It even goes into finer details like a list of the singer's albums over the years and on-demand rankings.

Notably, the folder function of Keke Tong synchronizes local documents. Files already imported into KKTONE will be synchronized with any additions or deletions made locally. Under the KKTONE folder mode, moving or deleting files will also synchronize with local files. Moreover, playlists can be quickly created by directly dragging and dropping song folders or songs under the folder mode onto the playlist, reducing the hassle of repeated imports. The import action for songs is simplified into a single-layer folder, and all subfolders are synchronized, reducing lag time.

If you're a fan, if you feel that being close to a pop star is quite far, becoming a listening star might be your forte. Then why not start by listening to music with Keke Tong?
