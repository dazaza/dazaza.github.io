Hip hop clothing Fashion Lab is a fashion line.

by hiphoprqp on 2010-04-04 10:23:13

Fashion Lab is a unique fashion line. It doesn't just stop at creating its own designs like many other clothing lines; instead, Fashion Lab also manufactures and markets designs from other creators. The key point here is that they focus on quality brands and ensure the highest quality in their production. Confused, all you fashion-hungry divas? Don’t worry about which brand will work best for you! If you're struggling to choose or pick the right outfit to wear on the dance floor while showing off your perfect moves, head straight to Fashion Lab. Yes, it's not a brand with its own apparel line, but it produces and markets hip-hop designs from other makers. So, what's this all about? Being hip-hop is a bold style statement that sets you apart from the crowd. With P. Diddy, evisu, Jennifer Lopez, Beyoncé Knowles, Nelly, and other hip-hop moguls, urban fashion has made a big impact on the fashion scene. These hip-hop icons not only produce music that drives masses crazy but also wear eye-catching outfits. As a result, we see men wearing loose-fitting clothes, bold logo tees, baggy cargo pants, and funky jewelry. But what about women who love this urban style or want to impress their hip-hop fashion-conscious partners? Here's the answer to satisfy the female fantasy for urban fashion.

Although not as slick, most hip-hop attire for women is heavily influenced by Beyoncé and her line Dereon, DKNY, Kimora Lee Simmons (the inspiration behind Baby Phat), and brands like Apple Bottoms, Pastry, and many more. All of them create cool styles that reflect the attitude of liberated women. To achieve that enviable hip-hop look, all you need is to pick the right hip-hop attire and have the right attitude. You can wear anything from adventurous tees, hep hoodies, or even flirtatious low-waist jeans. Finding the right collection for your hip-hop wardrobe might be challenging, and that’s where Fashion Lab comes in. Fashion Lab offers all types of enticing clothes from around the world, giving you a wide selection of chic designs to choose from. For example, a black military tunic top with embossed yellow designs worn over wing-studded denim by Sour N’Cream can give you an irresistible look that guys won't be able to resist. When you walk into the hottest party in town dressed in your amazing Shimmer Bubble dress from ROCAWEAR, designed by Cheryl Creation, the guys are sure to go wild. Pairing this sparkling metallic outfit with adjustable spaghetti straps and a bubble hemline with the Judhar sandal from Apple Bottoms (a silver and rhinestone-embellished high-heeled sandal) will make a sizzling impact at the party. From the jean family to bold tees, you'll find all scintillating designs available in all sizes. Chic hoodies and dresses are just perfect for hanging out with your friends. So, Fashion Lab is the place where all you gals can go to get those absolutely stunning hip-hop outfits.
