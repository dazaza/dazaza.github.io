How are the transmission device and base of the sand making machine installed?

by hx515317 on 2012-02-24 15:15:29

The spindle assembly of the sand making machine is installed in the center of the space of the quadrangular prism on the base, which is used to transmit the power transmitted by the motor through the triangle belt and support the rotation movement of the impeller. The impeller is installed on the top of the spindle assembly. The vortex crushing cavity, spindle assembly, motor, and transmission device are all installed on the base. The base can be installed on the bracket or directly installed on the foundation.

The transmission device of the sand making machine can use single motor drive or dual motor drive. The motor is installed on both sides of the spindle assembly, and the motor is connected to the spindle pulley through the triangle belt pulley, so that the force on both sides of the spindle is balanced and no additional torque is generated. The distributor of the sand making machine is a container for diverting the material. It can make part of the material enter the impeller through the central feed pipe and gradually accelerate to a higher speed to be thrown out, and make another part of the material enter the vortex crushing cavity outside the central feed pipe and on the side of the impeller, and be impacted and broken by the high-speed material ejected from the impeller. The distributor is generally installed in the upper cylindrical section of the vortex crushing cavity.

The vortex crushing cavity of the sand making machine is composed of two segments of cylinders. Material can also reside in the vortex crushing cavity, forming a material lining layer. The crushing process of the material occurs inside the vortex crushing cavity. The material lining layer separates the crushing action from the wall of the vortex crushing cavity, limiting the crushing action to between the materials, thus serving as a wear-resistant self-lining function. There is also an observation hole installed in the vortex crushing cavity, which is mainly used to observe the wear condition of the wear-resistant block at the outlet of the impeller flow channel and the top liner of the vortex crushing cavity. When the rotary kiln is working, the observation hole must be sealed tightly.
