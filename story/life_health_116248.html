31. The ingenious use of wind essence (1): Sprinkle a few drops of wind essence on the blades of an electric fan. As the blades keep turning, it can make the room fragrant and has the effect of repelling mosquitoes.

32. Before painting, apply a layer of cream to your hands. After painting, apply the cream to the skin with paint on it, wipe it with a dry cloth, then wash it with soap, which can remove the paint adhering to the skin.

33. Tofu usually has a briny taste. If you soak the tofu in boiling water for more than 10 minutes before cooking, the briny taste can be removed. This way, the tofu made will not only have a good texture but also be deliciously sweet.

34. When boiling eggs, you can first soak the eggs in cold water for a while, then put them in boiling water to cook. This way, the eggshell won't crack after cooking, and it will be easier to peel.

35. A magnetized watch can affect its accuracy. The method to eliminate magnetism is very simple. Just find an unmagnetized iron ring, place the watch in the ring, and slowly move it back and forth. After a few minutes, the watch will demagnetize and return to normal.
