Watson's business allegedly exploited the fact that converting a semi-automatic rifle AR-15 to an automatic one isa surprisingly simple process with the right component. When an AR-15 is fired, the expanding gases inside its chamber propel the bullet out the barrel and push the bolt back to pick up another round from the gun's magazine.
