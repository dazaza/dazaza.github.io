The No. 2 Golden-headed Centipede of Nongke Humin, developed by the professional technical staff of our company in collaboration with several renowned biological experts and professors, is a new variety of golden-headed centipede improved and cultivated through years of breeding observation and selective hybridization technology. Through rearing observations, this variety is characterized by large individuals, fast growth, gentle temperament, strong adaptability, simple rearing, easy management, high reproduction rate, and significant economic value.

1. Simple to Rear and Manage: Indoors, idle houses or greenhouses can be used, such as abandoned factories, warehouses, schools, dormitories, or fruit and vegetable greenhouses; or pools, tanks, boxes, plastic buckets can be used for rearing. Outdoors, enclosures can be built in front or behind houses, on barren mountains or slopes for rearing. Additionally, isolated islands with water areas can be directly selected for three-dimensional breeding (no need for breeding soil, stones, or tiles), making it even more convenient.

2. Gentle Temperament, Safer: The No. 2 Golden-headed Centipede of Nongke Humin differs from the aggressive nature of wild centipedes. Even when hundreds or thousands are together, they can coexist peacefully and live in groups, making them safer to rear.

3. Low Food Consumption and Wide Range of Foods: The food consumption of the No. 2 Golden-headed Centipede of Nongke Humin is stable and low. For example, feeding 0 grams of food is enough for 00 centipedes at a time. According to Wuhan Nongke Humin Bioengineering Co., Ltd., one feeding can last 3-5 days. When food is scarce, vegetables, fruits, or kitchen scraps can also serve as the main food.

4. Fast Growth, Strong Reproduction, High Output Efficiency: Under the company's new technical conditions, they can be sold after just three months of rearing. They lay eggs twice a year, with about 100 eggs per batch.
