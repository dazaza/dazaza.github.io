This transaction constitutes a significant related party transaction, and the related directors avoided voting when the board of directors deliberated on the issuance and related party transaction-related motions this time. 3. This related party transaction still needs to be approved by the company's shareholders' meeting. Related shareholders will avoid voting on related motions at the shareholders' meeting and give up their voting rights on related motions. 4. The non-public issuance of shares plan and transaction still need to be approved by the Anhui Provincial People's Government State-owned Assets Supervision and Administration Commission (hereinafter referred to as "Anhui SASAC") and the China Securities Regulatory Commission (hereinafter referred to as "CSRC"). 5. This issuance will not lead to changes in the company's control, which will effectively improve the company's capital structure and reduce financial expenses. The company plans to use the funds raised from this private placement to invest in the "Expansion Project of Unit No. 6 of Hefei Power Plant" and acquire 80% equity held by Wan Neng Group, the controlling shareholder of the company, in Electric Fuel Company, 50% equity in Linhuan Zhongli, and 2% equity in Qinshan Phase II, which will help further optimize the company's power asset structure, ensure fuel procurement channels, reduce operating risks, and improve the company's profitability. 6. After the financial data of the proposed acquired assets or equity are completed with audits and assessments, the board of directors will convene again to deliberate on this related party transaction matter. The related party transaction situation involved in this issuance is as follows: I. Wan Neng Group subscribes to the shares of this private placement (I) Summary of related party transactions 1. Content of related party transactions The number of shares issued in this private placement shall not exceed 380 million shares, and the total amount of funds raised shall not exceed 1.8 billion yuan, of which Wan Neng Group plans to subscribe to no less than 10% (including 10%) of the total number of shares issued this time and no more than 30% (including 30%) of the total number of shares issued this time. According to the "Shenzhen Stock Exchange Stock Listing Rules," the controlling shareholder of the company, Wan Neng Group, plans to subscribe to the shares of this private placement in cash, which constitutes a related party transaction with the company. 2. Approval procedures for related party transactions This related party transaction matter has obtained the prior approval of the independent directors of the company before being submitted to the board of directors of the company for deliberation, and was reviewed and passed by the 25th temporary meeting of the sixth board of directors of the company on February 23, 2012. Related directors avoided voting, and non-related directors unanimously agreed to the aforementioned related party transactions. The independent directors of the company also issued an independent opinion on the aforementioned related party transactions. In addition, the following deliberation procedures are required for this related party transaction matter: (1) This related party transaction still needs to be approved by the shareholders' meeting of the company. After the implementation of this transaction, the company's total assets and net assets will increase, optimizing the company's asset structure; meanwhile, the company's net profit and earnings per share will increase. (3) Development status of main business Linhuan Zhongli is located in Suixi County, Huaibei City, Anhui Province, responsible for the construction and operation of phase one project of Huaibei Linhuan coal gangue power plant. The Huaibei Linhuan coal gangue power plant project is one of the key projects of Anhui Province's 861 projects. The project is planned and designed according to the concept of fully utilizing coal production waste. The units of the project are among the earlier CFB units in China and abroad that adopt the design of burning three fuels: coal gangue, coal slime, and medium coal. Coal gangue and coal slime are waste and by-products in the process of coal mining and washing. Because the prices of coal gangue and coal slime are lower than the prices of coal used as fuel for conventional thermal power units, the unit power generation cost of CFB units is lower than that of conventional thermal power units. Since the commissioning of Linhuan Zhongli's two 300WM CFB units, the benefits have been good. The two 300WM CFB units of phase one project of Huaibei Linhuan coal gangue power plant are subcritical, intermediate reheating, condensing steam turbine generator units. Phase one project started construction in January 2007 and was completed in February 2009. The two units officially entered commercial operation in March and May 2009 respectively
