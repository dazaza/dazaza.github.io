Young woman hugs her 3-year-old daughter and threatens to jump off a tower to force her boyfriend to show up (picture)

by zxyasdw90 on 2011-06-16 13:59:07

The mother and daughter climbed up to a tower that was 6 meters above the ground. After a standoff lasting three hours, the mother agreed to let the firefighters rescue her daughter first, and an hour later, she was also rescued.

After more than four hours of standoff with fire personnel, the mother and daughter were successively rescued.

According to reports (by reporter Xie Yingjun with photographs), it is suspected that due to being abandoned by her boyfriend, a young woman hugged her 3-year-old daughter and climbed onto a high-voltage electric tower 6 meters above the ground. This incident occurred at around 7:00 AM yesterday near Lixiang Wetland Park on Old Dongchang Road in Dalang Town.

This "tower-jumping spectacle" greatly disrupted the lives of nearby residents. The young woman's act of attempting suicide while holding her child drew criticism from many people: "She's too selfish."

The woman held her young daughter as they climbed the electric tower.

At 9:00 AM yesterday, the reporter arrived at the section of road in front of Lixiang Wetland Park on Old Dongchang Road. A cordon had been set up in front of a high-voltage electric tower. Police cars, fire trucks, and ambulances were all on standby, and rescue air cushions were laid out in front of the tower. About 6 meters above the ground, a young woman in a purple top hugged a girl of about 3 years old, sitting precariously on the steel frame of the tower.

"She climbed up there at around 7:00 AM," a resident told the reporter. The woman did not seem like someone who was "determined to die." She only climbed 6 meters and stopped, tightly hugging the steel frame, occasionally responding to the questions of rescue personnel.

Moved by the police, she allowed the child to be released first.

"Why don't you let the little one down first? The sun is so strong," said rescuers below. Seeing the little girl crying and fussing, the rescue personnel took the opportunity to climb the ladder and pass her bottled water. After receiving the water, the woman's attitude softened, and at around 10:42 AM, she agreed to let the rescue personnel take the little girl down from the high-voltage tower first. At 11:45 AM, under the scorching sun, the woman began to show signs of physical exhaustion. Rescue personnel climbed onto the steel frame, embraced her, and brought her down safely. The reporter saw that the woman was helped onto an ambulance and taken away.

Later, according to an informed person involved in the rescue, the woman and the child were mother and daughter. The woman reportedly told the rescuers that she had a daughter with her boyfriend, but her boyfriend had recently cut off contact with her. With no other options, she came to where her boyfriend worked and staged this "tower-jumping spectacle" in hopes of forcing him to appear.

Citizens: Demands Should Not Harm Children

"She used her child's life as a gamble to achieve her own purposes. I can only say that this mother is too selfish!" said Ms. Liu, a citizen at the scene to the reporter. "If her demands are reasonable, she should express them in a more rational way and shouldn't harm her child for it. The child doesn't understand anything; her young mind might think this is just a game that mom is taking her through."
