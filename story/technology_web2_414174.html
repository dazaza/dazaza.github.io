What is Korean-style mandibular angle facial reshaping? Experts describe it as follows: an incision is made inside the mouth along the lower edge of the mandibular ramus, extending forward along the buccal gingival groove to the second premolar, reaching the periosteum. The periosteum of the lateral plate of the mandibular ramus, especially the lower segment, and the mandible body, particularly the lower edge of the mandible, mandibular angle, and posterior edge of the mandibular angle, are carefully separated under the periosteum without exposing the mental nerve.
