2013 Q2 China Tablet Computer Survey Report: iPad Stands Alone

by anonymous on 2013-09-11 13:16:04

In the second quarter of this year, a total of 3.576 million tablets were sold in the domestic consumer market, marking a consecutive growth of 5.2% and an annual increase of 52.8%. The iPad sold 2.31 million units, accounting for 64.6% of total sales. Unfortunately, DigiTimes, which provided the report, did not have the sales figures for different versions of the iPad; otherwise, it would have been possible to see which screen size users prefer.

Microsoft's Surface captured 1.9% of total sales, selling fewer than 70,000 units in the second quarter of 2013 domestically, placing it sixth. All the rest were Android-based tablets.

In the commercial tablet sector, E-people E-book holds the largest market share with 33.6% of sales, followed by Asus at 8.1%, Microsoft at 7.8%, Dell at 6.6%, and Lenovo at 5.4%.
