Alternative photo sharing: theatre network

by cacard on 2007-08-19 09:21:11

Theater CNOW.cc is a global image mobilization program.

The theater was founded by the CNEX Project team of the Beijing Association for International Exchange. We are a team composed of Chinese from the mainland, Taiwan and three places.

It is expected to collect images from all over the world and record human's image civilization by means of collecting and mobilizing.

Features of the theatre include:

Aggregate image: A collection of images with the same theme, nature, and purpose, presenting the vision, contrast, statistics, and records conveyed by the images

Theme planning: Plan a point of view or vision that belongs to you, and let netizens from all over the world participate in it through mobilization and collection

Shared sharing: We encourage people from all over the world to share and contribute their video creations together, so the theater has no upload capacity limit, and open the theater images to re-post elsewhere

The theater went live in June 2007 and is still in beta testing.
