Nike Air Max Wright UEFA Super Cup - Messi scores and assists as Barcelona wins 2-0

by fwejgtu3f on 2011-08-27 11:44:26

At 2:45 AM Beijing time on August 27, in the 2011 UEFA Super Cup, Nike Air Max LTD, the 2010-11 season UEFA Champions League winners Barcelona defeated Porto, the Europa League champions, 2-0 to win their fourth UEFA Super Cup title. Messi scored with a one-on-one opportunity in the first half and assisted Fabregas to score in the second half. This was Barcelona's 74th championship in club history and their 15th UEFA competition title, surpassing Real Madrid in Spain and AC Milan in Europe in terms of the number of championships won.

---

After the kickoff, Barcelona quickly took control of the game. In the 2nd minute, Xavi’s through ball from the top of the arc was cleared by Porto's defender ahead of Villa. In the 4th minute, Guarin shot from distance; the ball hit Abidal’s arm, and Porto was awarded a free kick at the top of the arc. Hulk's direct free kick attempt went over the crossbar. In the 6th minute, Moutinho shot from distance, and Valdes made a diving save. In the 9th minute, Iniesta sent a through ball down the middle that was deflected by Porto's defender to Pedro, who had a one-on-one chance but his chip shot narrowly missed the target. Two minutes later, Pedro's cross found Messi, whose shot was blocked by a defender.

---

In the 18th minute, Xavi's lobbed pass was successfully offside-trapped by Porto's defense, leaving Villa offside. In the 24th minute, Hulk earned a corner kick for Porto on the right side. The corner was headed out by Barça's defenders, and Hulk's follow-up cross into the box saw Valdes make an error in coming out, but Mascherano headed the ball away from danger. In the 28th minute, Messi dribbled past two defenders in midfield before passing the ball, which was gathered by the goalkeeper ahead of Villa. There was a brief interruption as Messi ran to change his shoes. In the 30th minute, Rodriguez fouled Alves with a tackle, and the referee showed the first yellow card of the match.

---

In the 35th minute, Messi's through ball left Villa offside again, and he was verbally warned by the referee for dissent after protesting the decision, which was accurate upon slow-motion review. A minute later, Adriano crossed from the left flank, and Xavi's shot from the top of the arc was punched over the bar by Furtado.

---

In the 38th minute, Porto's player Guarin made a passing error, and Messi capitalized by going one-on-one with the goalkeeper, chipping the ball into the empty net! This was Messi's first goal in the UEFA Super Cup, completing his record of scoring in all six club competitions he has participated in!

---

In the 42nd minute, Messi's through ball saw Villa taken down by two Porto defenders, but the referee ignored the appeal. In the 45th minute, Messi's cross from the left flank earned a corner, and following a tactical corner, Iniesta's long-range shot was blocked by a defender. After one minute of added time, the first half ended with Barcelona leading Porto 1-0.

---

In the second half, neither side made substitutions initially. In the 49th minute, Iniesta lost possession to Guarin behind him, and the referee indicated play should continue. Iniesta protested the call and received a yellow card. In the 51st minute, Moutinho's long-range shot went high, and two minutes later, Guarin's shot was saved by Valdes. In the 55th minute, Iniesta passed left inside the box, and Villa's shot was gathered by Furtado. In the 59th minute, Barcelona counterattacked with Messi sending a through ball from midfield, but Pedro's one-on-one chance was cleared by the goalkeeper. Subsequently, Barcelona made substitutions, bringing on Sanchez for Villa and Busquets for Adriano.

---

In the 64th minute, Messi broke through, and Rolando was shown a yellow card for pulling him down. In the 66th minute, Xavi's through ball left Messi seemingly in a one-on-one situation, but the assistant referee flagged him offside. In the 71st minute, Iniesta's through ball almost connected with Pedro, who was slightly late to arrive. In the 72nd minute, Porto countered with a long ball, and Mascherano headed it clear, but the ball nearly flew over Valdes into the goal, with Abidal clearing it off the line. In the 74th minute, Sanchez cut into the box from the left flank, but his curling shot missed the far post.

---

In the 80th minute, Fabregas replaced Pedro. In the 85th minute, Rolando committed a tactical foul after being dribbled past by Messi, earning a second yellow card and subsequently being sent off with a red. In the 87th minute, Barcelona extended their lead to 2-0 when Messi's through ball found Fabregas, who successfully scored with a backheel finish inside the box! In the 89th minute, Guarin recklessly tackled Mascherano, and the referee directly issued a red card. In the second minute of stoppage time, Iniesta's long-range shot was blocked by a defender. The final whistle blew, and Barcelona won their fourth UEFA Super Cup title.
