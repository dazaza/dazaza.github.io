Li Bingbing shares her experience in nurturing hydrated skin

by onebelles on 2009-12-05 10:11:28

Reduce the burden on your skin: Zhong Liti, a clean face adds more friendliness.

Actresses shoot scenes every day, and thick makeup can clog pores, placing a heavy burden on the skin. Even for ordinary office ladies, sweating, heavy makeup, and environmental pollution place no small amount of pressure on the skin. Therefore, reducing the burden on the skin during autumn and winter is the primary task.

For me, the most important step in beauty care is cleansing the face. Carefully washing your face morning and evening removes excess sebum and metabolic waste from the surface of the skin, allowing newly generated cells in the deeper layers of the skin to smoothly reach the surface. Then gently pat some softening water to balance the skin's water and oil levels, enabling the day cream or night cream used afterward to be better absorbed by the skin. The use of highly nourishing essence serum should be determined according to the situation and does not necessarily need to be applied all over the face.

Many people have asked me if I need to frequently visit beauty salons for care. Everyone should know that actors' schedules are very unpredictable. So for me, making sure to do a face mask every day is very necessary. Out of 365 days a year, I will persist in applying face masks for at least 340 days. A fresh, clean, and moisturized face is very friendly.

Exercise makes the skin more energetic.

In addition to running and playing tennis, you can try more ways to exercise for skincare. Li Bingbing's secret is to persist in riding a stationary bike every day, listening to dynamic music, pedaling hard, and sweating profusely. During this time, the skin’s detoxification work quietly takes place. At the same time, exercising is a very motivating and healthy, refreshing activity. Gradually, you will feel more energetic, and your skin will look more radiant.

Choose skincare products based on comprehensive factors.

Should we choose our skincare products based on skin type or by season? I had doubts about this before as well. After discussing with beauty experts and makeup artists around me, I realized that it is very necessary to adjust our skincare product list based on comprehensive characteristics.

Our skin has a certain level of tolerance and needs, which are reflected differently depending on each skin type, region, and season. If we only rely on one specific need point, it is difficult to meet all of the skin's needs simultaneously. The key to choosing is first understanding your own skin, then carefully reading the product descriptions or instructions, and selecting the most suitable one based on its ingredients.

In autumn and winter, the most favored products are those with excellent hydration and moisturizing effects, low allergenicity, and unique skincare components. Here, I suggest using a moisturizing cream (with a slightly thicker texture) that can lock in moisture, thereby retaining the skin's natural moisture and that from cosmetics.

Recently, anti-aging skincare products targeted at young skin have also become very popular. In life, our skin endures wind and sun exposure, protecting us throughout the decades. Therefore, we should give it the most delicate care from an early age, just like being in love— the more you invest, the more hydrated and lustrous your skin will become.

TIPS: How to carry skincare products while traveling?

The climate varies greatly in different places, and there is a good chance you might fly from summer into winter. Before going on a business trip or vacation, it's best to learn about the local climate conditions in advance so you can customize an accurate skincare plan for your skin. Spending several hours on a plane may also be common. Li Bingbing's method is to pay attention to drinking an appropriate amount of water and apply a transparent hydrating mask. The enclosed cabin can easily cause fine lines around the eyes, so it's important to take special care of the eye area, using a highly moisturizing eye cream.
