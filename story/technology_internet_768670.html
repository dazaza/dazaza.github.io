Communication can be based on familiar relationships or common topics; the latter type of communication tends to be more enthusiastic - everyone knows this. However, topic-based social networking either relies on a strong community gene (like Douban) or a channel that continuously and accurately分流users (like Tieba). YiXin plans to use social networking to drive its product, but what will it use to drive its social networking?

On November 15 at noon, a former employee of NetEase, @Pure Silver V, exposed an internal email allegedly from NetEase CEO William Ding through his Sina Weibo account. In the email, Ding commented on the new feature product architecture of YiXin. He stated that among similar products now, WeChat scores 5 points, Momo 4 points, YiXin 0 points, and LaiWang has negative points. The next things YiXin does must score at least 6 points.

In the email, Ding also shared his understanding of social networking. Social networking is actually more about people who aren't so familiar with each other, for interests or cooperation. "On the contrary, communication between acquaintances mostly involves talking about specific matters when needed, and not talking when not needed. This is at most considered chatting, far from being real social networking. But recognizing strangers with common interests is everyone's social instinct," said Ding.

Ding remarked that the telecom people ask him various data questions every day, making him feel like he's working for someone else for the first time, "You shouldn't feel too pressured, my pressure is much greater than yours."

Below is the full text of the internal email:

Reply: 【Update】YiXin New Function Product Architecture

The overall idea is correct, but the term 'acquaintance social networking' is problematic.

Social networking is actually more about people who aren't so familiar with each other, for interests or cooperation. On YiXin, I've joined many groups. 200 girls in a sea shopping group chat four to five thousand messages daily. Do you even talk that much with your wives when you're dating? In Feng Shaofeng's fan group, there are thousands of messages without anyone live-streaming idol news for free. This kind of communication need is a strong one. People with common interests, under the same theme, start socializing as strangers or semi-acquaintances.

On the contrary, communication between acquaintances is mostly about discussing specific matters when necessary, and not communicating when unnecessary. This is at most considered chatting, far from being real social networking. With acquaintances, using YiXin, making phone calls, or sending text messages is all the same. But recognizing strangers with common interests is everyone's social instinct.

Among current similar products, WeChat scores 5 points, Momo 4 points, we score 0 points, and LaiWang has negative points. The next things you show me must score at least 6 points. Don't have perfectionist tendencies. I know you all don't like WeChat's shake feature, neither do I, but users do. This experience may be terrible, but the core demand it solves is worth our learning.

Social functions should be launched as soon as possible. I want to see them in the next version. Previously, you did a good job with the chat features, and social networking needs to be done even better. Keep me updated on project progress. You shouldn't feel too pressured, my pressure is much greater than yours. Telecom people ask me various data questions every day, making me feel like I'm working for someone else for the first time. We must significantly boost activity in the next version.

To create a great product, you need to have a broader perspective. If you define communication only as chatting, YiXin will end up in a dead end.

November 14, 2013

William Ding
