Plot Summary:

The Birth of the Black Knight

Mak Tai Cheung has been exceptionally gifted in business since he was young. Deeply understanding that only through business can one achieve success, he strives hard and completes his MBA at Stanford in the U.S. After graduation, Cheung sees potential in Ho Man Tin's company and proactively approaches him, clearly stating that he does not want to be an employee but rather a partner. Even with just 0.01% shares, he is willing to join. Impressed by Cheung's uniqueness, Ho allows him to join as a partner. Their relationship is both teacher and friend, working seamlessly together to specialize in acquiring companies and reselling them, becoming renowned snipers in the American business world.

Later, Cheung becomes known as the ruthless Black Knight in the business world, growing stronger over time. Similarly, the differences of opinion between him and Ho increase. To benefit the company, Cheung decides to fully acquire Ho's company. Unable to bear the blow, Ho gets drunk and drives, resulting in an accident where his car loses control and plunges into the sea. Cheung finds out about this and believes Ho is dead, feeling immense guilt. Is the value of a single deal really worth more than friendship and life?

Cheung reflects on everything and decides to stop being the Black Knight, leaving the business world. From the newspaper, Cheung learns that Ho's widow and child are living a difficult life in Hong Kong. Feeling responsible, Cheung decides to return to Hong Kong to take care of Ho's family.

Miu Miu Pursuing Debts

Cheung returns to Hong Kong and visits Ho's wife So Muk Lin, coincidentally staying at a hotel run by Tang Kit. He meets Tang Kit, who is troubled by hotel operation issues. Tang Kit is a middle-aged playboy born into a business family, notorious outside but very filial to his father, surrounded by beautiful women.

Coincidentally, Cheung meets Lam Miu Miu in a luxury store, creating animosity between them. Miu Miu is a compulsive shopper who owes a large amount of credit card debt and is forced to work as a debt collector. With a no-success-no-fee mentality, she becomes the top performer in the debt collection company.

Muk Lin owes a large debt to Miu Miu's financial company due to her grandmother Zhou Siu Siu being scammed. Miu Miu is responsible for collecting the debt and thus meets Cheung again. Cheung takes on Muk Lin's debt, making Miu Miu mistakenly believe that Cheung is Muk Lin's ex-husband who abandoned his family, forming a poor impression of Cheung.

Muk Lin is unaware that her husband's death was indirectly caused by Cheung and continues to believe her husband Ho Man Tin is still alive. Her grandmother even accuses Cheung of being the murderer of her son Ho Man Tin. Initially shocked, Cheung later discovers that the grandmother suffers from early-stage dementia, leading others to dismiss her accusations as nonsense.

Cheung Turns into a White Knight

Cheung is a regular customer at Chi Kee Noodle Shop. Seeing Muk Lin's difficulties, he introduces her to work at the noodle shop. Muk Lin grew up in the U.S., straightforward and unable to navigate social complexities but works diligently and passionately at the noodle shop, bringing new vitality, earning praise from Zhong Chi.

Chi Kee Noodle Shop faces threats from a newly opened cart noodle shop nearby. Cheung suggests the term CEO, and Miu Miu hires her brother Lin Muk San as the noodle shop's CEO. Muk San takes over the shop but performs poorly, causing chaos. Cheung advises Zhong Chi not to hand over the shop to Muk San, emphasizing not to favor relatives. Seeing Muk Lin's hardworking nature, he recommends her to take on important responsibilities, but Miu Miu doesn't take it seriously, thinking Cheung is just talking nonsense.

Until Cheung helps the Bo De Group acquire the old building where the noodle shop is located and convinces Sen and Kiu to sell the shop, Miu Miu doesn't want her father's hard work to be ruined and fights to protect the shop, removing Muk San and appointing Muk Lin as the CEO. As the CEO of the noodle shop, Muk Lin teams up with Miu Miu to negotiate with the Bo De Group. With Cheung's covert assistance, Tang Kit gains autonomy for the first time, allowing Chi Kee Noodle Shop to overcome the crisis and achieving a win-win situation for both parties. Since then, Miu Miu firmly believes Cheung is a business genius and decides to learn from him how to do business, hoping to escape poverty. Miu Miu keeps pestering Cheung, who, although not verbally agreeing, secretly teaches Miu Miu business strategies. Unexpectedly, Miu Miu meets her ex-boyfriend Tong Yi Ben on her entrepreneurial journey.

Miu Miu Gradually Develops Feelings for Cheung

Muk Lin reluctantly sends her grandmother Siu Siu to a nursing home for work reasons. Cheung consoles Siu Siu and requests to move in himself, only to find out that the nursing home is funded by Tang Kit for his girlfriend and is facing operational difficulties. While Tang Kit is having an affair at the nursing home, he meets Cheung and Miu Miu. Knowing Cheung's capabilities, Tang Kit hopes Cheung can solve the nursing home's problems. Miu Miu presses forward, hoping to learn business skills from Cheung, forcing Cheung to act.

After agreeing, Cheung seems indifferent, and Miu Miu sees that Cheung is relaxed about the nursing home reform, not taking it seriously. She decides to test him and proposes the "Planting Filial Piety, Winning Bonuses" plan, which unexpectedly causes trouble. Ultimately, Cheung steps in to resolve the disturbance at the nursing home and surprisingly merges it with the neighboring kindergarten, creating a new generation of elderly-child care center, solving all problems. After this battle, the master-apprentice relationship between Cheung and Miu Miu deepens.

Miu Miu gradually realizes her feelings for Cheung, but Cheung ignores them. Miu Miu assumes Cheung is a deeply emotional man with unresolved feelings for someone else. Later, she discovers that person is Cheung's mother Qing Qing. Cheung transfers his maternal affection onto Miu Miu, and she tries her best to resolve it. Seeing Cheung's refusal to accept her, she concludes that Cheung looks down on her and decides to strengthen herself, vowing to make Cheung see her in a different light and win the heart of the man she loves.
