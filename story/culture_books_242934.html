"Love Without Burden" - A Mature Woman's Game of Love

by jqrxass on 2009-09-26 11:38:29

At the age of thirty, Zhu Miao tried hard to conceal her checkered past and looked for a "clean" man to marry. However, the mysterious retreat of married man Fang Dongshu, the deception of young Cheng Xiaoqi, and the suspicion and insult from photographer Xu Zhiyuan, left Zhu Miao, who originally thought she had everything under control, severely wounded...

Part One of "No Love, No Burden"

Zhu Miao had been blossoming for N years, still attracting young, infatuated bees that buzzed around her, sticking and clinging like an open-air market. Thirty is usually the time when one's looks start to fade, causing worry, but Zhu Miao decided to enjoy it all, just as someone who indulges in fish and meat delicacies, only carefully removing the bones and not worrying about anything else. Zhu Miao always believed that love was like a menstrual period, nothing more than the abdominal pain and restlessness of the previous night, the fresh red blood during the process, feeling weak all over, followed by some lingering connections, repeating back and forth, until finally becoming completely clean. Love is like a physiological cycle, a natural law that cannot be reversed, usually beneficial to health, but obstructing people causes illness. Therefore, she began actively cooperating with young boys, making love look dazzling, creating some tears and vows, making time flow backward.

Part Two of "No Love, No Burden"

Cheng Xiaoqi's crying didn't seem fake, and his reaction was so strong that Zhu Miao didn't expect it. She instinctively thought that Cheng Xiaoqi had psychological issues or mental problems. But she quickly criticized herself for not seeing him as a devoted youth. In her view, this kind of emotion was like a faucet, once turned off, there would be no leakage, except Cheng Xiaoqi's faucet which didn't pass quality checks unlike hers which had been verified multiple times and was qualified. If seven and ten were compared normally, there wouldn't be much difference, but specifically placed in the context of Zhu Miao and Cheng Xiaoqi, it became significant. Just as a hundred pounds already presses a person down, unable to straighten their back, if three more pounds were added, the person would collapse entirely.

Part Three of "No Love, No Burden"

Zhu Miao was speechless. Her will started to waver. In this situation, any woman seemed unable to escape a fleeting fantasy: Cheng Xiaoqi, mature for his age, handsome, well-mannered, plus being affectionate, gentle, considerate, loyal, such a man, probably no one would want him to slip away. Zhu Miao yearned for what she fantasized about, compared to Fang Dongshu who was middle-aged, held a decent position, but was already married and had children, the imagined Cheng Xiaoqi was still quite comparable, even threatening. Moreover, the future with Fang Dongshu was uncertain, possibly ending up wasting her youth for nothing.

Part Four of "No Love, No Burden"

Xu Zhiyuan, unaware of Jin (Fang Dongshu), regardless of Wei Han (Cheng Xiaoqi, etc.), traveled and played with Zhu Miao, made love whenever they encountered a bed, could also cook, making delicious dishes like chrysanthemum fish, sweet and sour spare ribs, chestnut chicken, braised turtle, etc. After meals, he would brew tea for Zhu Miao, saying "rest while you write your novel, I'll wash the dishes," practically acting as a full-time male nanny. By principle, Xu Zhiyuan had impeccable character, appearance, and cooking skills, appreciating and liking Zhu Miao with a bit of admiration thrown in; as a husband, he would certainly be close to her heart and soul. But fate played tricks on him, Xu Zhiyuan was all show, his flaws fatal. He needed shoes size forty-two and above, wore large T-shirts, but used small condoms. Fortunately, he was good at compensating through effort, post-coital soothing work done properly, thus getting by.

Part Five of "No Love, No Burden"

Cheng Xiaoqi couldn't wait for her to unbutton, forcibly pulled her bra up three inches, like a thirsty person, sweeping away floating objects on the water surface, extending his mouth to drink deeply. At this moment, Zhu Miao had untied her bra, like a kind-hearted person bringing a bowl of tea to a thirsty person, if he drank it all at once, it would be a thank-you to the kind-hearted person. Cheng Xiaoqi took the big bowl of tea, too grateful to drink deeply, trembling hands, only licking the rim with his tongue, barely drinking a few sips, unsure how to swallow. An infant is born with the instinct to eat, how could a twenty-year-old youth not know? Zhu Miao thought Cheng Xiaoqi was faking it.

Part Six of "No Love, No Burden"

Standing by the seaside, hands empty. Why were her hands empty? Zhu Miao calculated her accounts, did a simple reflection, some reasons she knew, others were inexplicable, similar to spending money, part of the account can be traced, part remains unknown. Money spent can be earned back, lost lovers can be found again, these were trivial matters, but the wish to get married ended up in vain. Youth once lost, cannot be regained. Zhu Miao felt disheartened, after crossing the threshold of thirty, she felt everything going downhill rapidly, as if the clock speed had been adjusted, spinning fast, noon came in a flash, dusk suddenly arrived, but the night was long, tossing and turning without dawn.
