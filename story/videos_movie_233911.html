Introduction to Ultimate Three Kingdoms:

Guan Yu and Zhang Fei are high school students, but due to their excessive sense of justice, they have been expelled from twenty-four schools, known as the "twenty-four schools." Just when the two feel desperate after being expelled again, a man with large earlobes suddenly appears. He introduces himself as Liu Bei, styled Xuande, a member of a fallen noble family who often changes schools because he cannot afford the tuition fees. Liu Bei proposes a plan: since he has noble blood, he can attend the best noble school in the country, the Donghan Academy. However, it is extremely expensive and only open to royal relatives. Zhang Fei, wealthy, says he can pay the tuition for everyone. Liu Bei then suggests that the three of them swear brotherhood so that all of them would be considered royal relatives and thus eligible to attend the academy. After swearing brotherhood at a meaningful place, just as they finish saying, “We don’t seek to be born on the same day, month, and year, but we seek to die on the same day, month, and year,” four people—Xiu, Wang Dadi, Ding Xiaoyu, and Wang Yase—from the Golden Time Dimension accidentally pass through a time-space portal into the Silver Time Dimension. Due to Wang Dadi's carelessness, a coin he dropped causes a chain reaction leading to a falling stone that kills Liu Bei! Guan Yu, bound by his promise, wants to follow his elder brother and die on the same day. Zhang Fei tries to stop him and knocks him unconscious. They then discover that Liu Bei is not dead, just severely injured and unconscious. After consulting with Dadi, Xiaoyu, and Yase, they decide to leave Xiu in the Silver Time Dimension as a substitute (since Xiu’s counterpart in the Silver Time Dimension is Liu Bei). Although unwilling, Xiu agrees to maintain the order of the time-space dimensions. The other three take the seriously injured Liu Bei back to the Golden Time Dimension for treatment. When Guan Yu wakes up, he finds that Xiu pretending to be Liu Bei is still alive and becomes very happy. Zhang Fei is also glad that they don't have to die together. Thus, the three finally arrive at the Donghan Academy, where Guan Yu and Zhang Fei believe they can achieve great things.

The three hope to graduate from Donghan Academy, but because the academy is well-known, it attracts attacks from the Yellow Turban High School and River East High School (where Dong Zhuo is the principal), putting Donghan Academy in constant danger. Master Zuo Ci even sets up the 'Eight Gates Gold Lock Formation' at the school gate to block students from attending classes. Naturally, the three must break the formation, but the Eight Gates Gold Lock Formation is not easy to break. Moreover, Liu Bei Xiu and Xiao Qiao are trapped in the formation, facing life-threatening danger. Therefore, Guan Yu and Zhang Fei accept the suggestion of the student union president, Cao Cao, and find an extremely resilient and cheerful young man, Ma Chao, and Ma Chao's good friend, a perfect marksman, Huang Zhong, to help break the formation. They also involve Zhao Yun, a super handsome youth who has always been at odds with them. Together, the five break the camp and rescue Liu Bei Xiu and Xiao Qiao. During the process of breaking the formation, Guan Yu saves Zhao Yun's life by blocking an arrow with his arm in a critical moment. This action resolves the grudge between Zhao Yun and the others, and from then on, Zhao Yun calls the other four brothers, becoming close friends. Together, they successfully break the Eight Gates Gold Lock Formation.

After breaking the formation and making new brothers, they thought they could smoothly finish high school, but on one side, the Yellow Turban High School was eyeing the resources of the Donghan Academy, and on the other side, the principal of River East High School, Dong Zhuo, the student union president of Donghan Academy, Cao Cao, and the leader of the Guandong School Alliance, Yuan Shao, were all ambitious for the throne of the 'Alliance Leader'. This means that Liu Bei and the Five Tigers were destined not to live peaceful campus lives. A battle for supremacy is about to begin...
