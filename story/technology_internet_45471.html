1. Common forum terms:

1. BBS: ① Abbreviation of Bulletin Board System, referring to electronic bulletin board systems, commonly known as forums in China. ② Big-Breasted Sister, abbreviation of BBs.

2. Moderator: Also written as Ban Zhu, Ban Zhu, or Ban Zhu. A beautiful homophone caused by pinyin input. Deputy moderators are called "Ban Fu".

3. Alternate ID: Registered members who register other names, these names are collectively referred to as alternate IDs, in contrast to the main ID.

4. Newbie: Originally referred to people with low computer skills, later widely used in real life to refer to people who are not good at something. In contrast, there are old hands.

5. Expert: Homophonic for "Da Xia", referring to veteran netizens with long online tenure, or people who are particularly outstanding in a certain area (such as computer technology or writing). Generally, people with good interpersonal relationships and reputation will get such a title.

6. Flooding: Originally referred to posts on forums that have little reading value. Now, it is customary to call the majority of posting and replying as "flooding", without any derogatory meaning.

7. Pure water: Flooding without any substantial content, also known as water vapor.

8. Water hand: People who like flooding. Those with high levels are also called water buckets, water ghosts, water fairies. When referring to female flooding enthusiasts, there is a specific term: jellyfish.

9. Lurking: Staying in the forum every day but not posting, only reading posts and paying attention to the daily affairs of the forum.

10. Writing: Writing posts, generally referring to somewhat substantial posts.

11. Throwing bricks: Posting different opinions and understandings about someone or a post compared to others.

12. Screen sweeping: Opening a forum and finding that all the topic posts are from the same ID.

13. Floor sweeping: Also called wall brushing, opening a forum and finding that the last reply to all topic posts is from the same ID.

14. Topic poster: The person who posts the topic.

15. Building floors: Replying to the same topic post, fans usually like to build floors.

16. Upper floor: The person who replies to the same topic post one step ahead of you, in contrast is "lower floor".

17. Which floor: All people who reply to posts except the topic poster, can be called "2nd floor", "3rd floor"...

18. Sofa: SF, the first person to reply. Later, those who couldn't get the sofa claimed they sat on the "bed" or the topic poster's "thigh"~

19. Chair: The second person to reply.

20. Stool: The third person to reply.

21. Floor: People who don't even get a stool.

22. Pushing: Generally, once a post on the forum is replied to, it goes to the top of the topic list. This action of replying is called "pushing", in contrast to "sinking".

23. Zou Zhao Gong Sui: Super strong, usually used in replies to express admiration for the topic post.

24. Sweat: Expresses feelings of shame or helplessness. Derived words include: profuse sweating, big sweat, dying of sweat, waterfall sweat, torrential rain pear flower sweat, etc.
