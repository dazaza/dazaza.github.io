WeChat Partner Communication Meeting will be held on November 18th, registration is now open.

by anonymous on 2013-11-17 19:24:19

On November 12, the WeChat Official Accounts Platform website added an event registration entry for a WeChat Partner Communication Meeting themed "WeChat·Public." What will WeChat open up in the future? What new playstyles will partners gain through the already-opened capabilities? What fresh experiences will users obtain? What significant changes will occur in the industry? On November 18, these questions about WeChat's openness path and ecosystem blueprint will be revealed at the "WeChat·Public" Partner Communication Meeting in Guangzhou. The registration process for this meeting has already begun.

First use of official account registration mode

Unlike previous WeChat event registration methods, this partner communication meeting will utilize the WeChat Official Account internal registration method for the first time, which is also a new playstyle after the recent free opening of advanced technical interfaces. Users only need to log in to the WeChat Official Accounts Platform website page, enter the "WeChat · Public Partner Communication Meeting" event page, use WeChat's "Scan" feature to follow the official "WeChat·Public" WeChat account, and complete the registration application according to the menu prompt "Attend Registration > Conference Registration." This provides an opportunity to discuss WeChat's innovative playstyles with representatives from various enterprises and industry experts on-site.

This conference will present the latest overall openness capability conception of WeChat, focusing on multiple modules such as the WeChat Official Accounts Platform, WeChat Pay, and pattern recognition technology. The WeChat team will invite cooperative enterprises and industry experts from various fields such as public welfare, express delivery, health care, life services, government agencies, operators, aviation transportation, insurance, and e-commerce to attend. In addition to earlier benchmark enterprises like China Merchants Bank and China Southern Airlines that have grown on the WeChat Official Accounts Platform sharing their experiences, there will also be enterprises that have recently become popular due to WeChat Pay, who will share the new opportunities brought by WeChat's openness across industries.

WeChat + More New Opportunities

Since the new version of the WeChat Official Accounts Platform entered beta testing on October 29, its nine advanced interface functions freely opened—such as voice recognition, customer service interface, OAuth2.0 web authorization, generation of parameterized QR codes, user grouping interface, uploading/downloading multimedia files—have attracted considerable attention. What can companies do with these interfaces? How can they maximize the use of WeChat's power and choose service models suitable for themselves?

In fact, since the launch of WeChat Pay, various innovative integration cases such as WeChat + self-service vending machines, WeChat + e-commerce, and WeChat + smart homes have emerged, providing inspiration to various industries. "The internet plus traditional industries, such as telecommunications, entertainment, and retail, have undergone many disruptive changes," Tencent CEO Pony Ma proposed at the WE Conference, "the strength of each细分 field in traditional industries remains incredibly powerful, and with the internet, many new opportunities will emerge."

An industry insider believes that from the launch of WeChat Pay in version 5.0 to the recent update of advanced interfaces, WeChat's openness has taken another very important step forward. From the cases created by WeChat, such as China Merchants Bank, China Southern Airlines, Guangdong Unicom, and TicWatch, it can be seen that WeChat hopes the public platform becomes a stage for transforming modern commerce. For numerous enterprises, the continuous deepening of WeChat's openness and the pace represented by mobile internet + means the arrival of more new opportunities.

For example, through WeChat's voice recognition interface, companies can call upon WeChat's independently developed voice recognition technology to automatically convert user voice messages into text, allowing the system to quickly handle requests. With WeChat's powerful voice recognition technology, companies significantly reduce their operational time on the public platform, greatly improving operational efficiency on the public platform, and promptly responding to user feedback. Currently, Haier air conditioners and Future TV have both achieved "move your mouth on WeChat, and your home listens to you."

A function similar to account authorization on Weibo and QQ means that WeChat accounts will officially become an account system. Supported by the OAuth2.0 interface, it can maintain the status of WeChat users and retrieve information (nicknames, gender, region, etc.), truly achieving microsite user exemption from registration. Companies are equivalent to adding a mini-platform where they can straightforwardly understand various user information and conduct their own services. A responsible person from a well-known e-commerce company stated that the opening of the nine advanced interfaces allows small and medium-sized enterprises a low-threshold opportunity to embrace the mobile internet, not only saving funds and technical investment but also freely gaining access to interact with WeChat's hundreds of millions of users. The free opening of advanced technical interfaces is just one step in WeChat's openness.

A more innovative and regulated ecosystem

It is worth noting that while opening more valuable technical interfaces, WeChat has also introduced a safer and stricter certification system. An industry insider predicts that with the comprehensive systematization of WeChat's openness capabilities, WeChat will not only bring various services but also become increasingly standardized. In this regulated ecosystem, third-party enterprises operating regularly lower their technical thresholds while receiving WeChat's powerful personalized customization and precise service functions. Through these, enterprises can better understand users, thus providing users with more refined and valuable mobile internet services. In this ecosystem, service providers and consumers, manufacturers and consumers, can directly connect more effectively.

"The WeChat Official Accounts Platform was born out of a simple idea: the development of the internet today should provide a simpler way for individuals and enterprises to offer services to those in need." As early as July 3 this year, at the Tencent Partner Conference WeChat sub-forum, Senior Vice President of Tencent Group Allen Zhang expressed the hope that various valuable services would grow on the WeChat Official Accounts Platform. With the increasing clarity of WeChat's openness path, WeChat will help more enterprises find functional services suitable for their own characteristics and needs, and even give birth to more emerging ecological modes and emerging industries, activating the ecological industrial chain.
