Yuan Cheng Logistics team won the championship at the Panjin station of the truck race on June 5th.

by yuanchenwl1989 on 2012-03-08 15:18:49

The Shaanxi Auto Cup 2011 National Super Truck Race Owees Racing Club Panjin Liaobin station was successfully concluded on June 5, 2011. Yuancheng Logistics Team, Shaanxi Auto Changsha Huantong Team and Cooper Chengshan Tire Team ranked in the top three of the opening game. After an afternoon of fierce competition on June 5, six teams - Auto Observer Team, Cooper Chengshan Tire Team, Zero Kilometer Lubricant Team, Fast Tooth Pioneer Team, Yuancheng Logistics Team, and Shaanxi Auto Changsha Huantong Team - entered the semi-finals for the final two spots. Due to serious vehicle damage and tactical errors, the Shaanxi Huantong Team, which had a firm grip on victory, ultimately ceded the championship to the Yuancheng Logistics Team due to a delay in the last round. The teams that finished second and third were respectively the Shaanxi Changsha Huantong Team and the Cooper Chengshan Tire Team. The individuals who ranked in the top three were Gong Huan, Gong Bin, and Xin Kejun. The excitement of the race lies not only in the thrilling process but also in the unpredictable results that lead to speculation. With this station's regret, we look forward to the Shaanxi Changsha Huantong Team, represented by the outstanding domestic racers Gong Huan and Gong Bin, regrouping and setting off again at the next station in Wuhai Desert. In 2011, Panjin City and the race organizing committee jointly carried out large-scale renovations and upgrades to the track, adding new obstacles such as tire pits, single-side bridges, and side slope roads, laying the groundwork for new highlights in the new season. This year's new race cars are well-prepared, all being replaced with the upgraded version of the Shaanxi Auto De Long F3000 4-wheel drive off-road heavy-duty truck. The Weichai engine installed in the new race car uses a higher-powered model, while the Fast Tooth transmission has reduced its gears to six, making the operation more suitable for off-road competitions. The speed ratio of the Hande axle has also been matched and improved accordingly, and all shock absorbers have been upgraded. The tires use Cooper Chengshan Company's off-road dedicated tires, and even the lubricating oil has selected the "Iron Man" card race dedicated oil specially developed by Shandong Zero Kilometer Petrochemical Co., Ltd. It can be said that this year's super race car is both internally and externally refined, performing like a fish in water on the devilish track of Panjin wetlands, displaying its impressive Chinese Kung Fu to the audience. To meet the wishes of more truck enthusiasts and racing lovers, the organizing committee of this year's race has deliberately increased the number of competing teams to 10. Veteran truck race experts are still sharp, such as Li Wenli leading the Zero Kilometer Lubricants Team, Hou Hongning leading the Yuancheng Logistics Team, and Liang Xu leading the Cooper Chengshan Tire Team, all performing very stably; the new generation of truck racers who have grown up in recent years have become increasingly mature and have become the backbone force of the truck race, such as Li Wenbin leading the Liaoning Huading Team, Peng Qingguo leading the Auto Observer Team, Wu Changming leading the Shaanxi Rocket Team, Gong Huan leading the Shaanxi Changsha Huantong Team, and Ge Dongshen leading the Hande Tianya Four-Wheel Drive Team, all giving excellent performances; even more excitingly, 11 drivers including Captain Wu Fuxi of the Fast Tooth Pioneer Team and Captain Wei Hao of the Hanhai Oasis Team are participating in the truck race for the first time, bringing a fresh breeze to the race, and the rookie competition held among them has become a new highlight on the race track.
