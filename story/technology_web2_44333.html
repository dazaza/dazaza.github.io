Give me ten seconds, and I'll give you the whole Beijing - Beijing search

by my08 on 2008-01-22 16:23:27

"Beijing Search" (www.beijingso.com), which has the slogan "Give me ten seconds, and I will show you the whole Beijing", has received support from many enterprises, institutions, and government offices since it launched its self-management platform for businesses in September. Now, nearly a million pieces of enterprise information have been indexed, some of which were registered and submitted by the enterprises, institutions, and government offices themselves. Starting December 12th, the Beijing Search website began trial operations. Residents of Beijing and domestic and international visitors can now use the site to look up practical living information and more conveniently understand the entirety of Beijing.

Since its establishment, Beijing Search has received strong support from numerous enterprises, institutions, and government departments. In order to further improve the service functions of the website and provide stronger support to entities that join the site, Beijing Search today proposed the slogan "I participate, I benefit." The main purpose is to strengthen the service awareness for enterprises and institutions, and through various promotional activities on the website and multiple measures serving netizens, allow participating entities to benefit. Therefore, as an enterprise, the more complete the information you upload, the more opportunities for publicity you will receive, and the more opportunities to increase economic benefits you will gain. If you are a government agency, the more complete the information you fill out, the more comprehensive your promotion will be, not only enhancing the government's reputation but also improving efficiency and better connecting with and serving the people.

Beijing Search hopes that for the information already recorded about your entity, each unit will assist us in checking so we can make timely revisions; for those whose information has not yet been recorded, you may upload it personally. Beijing Search sincerely looks forward to receiving constructive feedback from all sectors.

"Beijing Search" was recently rated as one of the first batch of Class A creative excellent enterprises in 2007 and received the first batch of cultural and creative industry funds. Before the arrival of the 2008 Olympics, "Beijing Search" will develop into a comprehensive free public service platform, providing thoughtful services for domestic and international friends in Beijing and showcasing the modern face of Beijing while making due contributions to the citizens and city of Beijing. The website not only provides detailed information queries about Beijing’s public institutions and government agencies but also offers comprehensive information on dining, entertainment, shopping, and discounts.

Next May 1st, Beijing Search will launch Beijing's largest bilingual Chinese-English free search service platform. It is understood that all enterprises and institutions registered on the website before March 1st next year will have their database materials translated into English by experts from the Foreign Languages University commissioned by the website using special funds for the cultural and creative industries; enterprises registered after March 1st will no longer enjoy free English translation services.
