Determine the parting of hair according to face shape and become a beauty with a small pointed face.

by cshelive9520 on 2012-02-19 12:01:24

What hairstyles suit a long face? Generally, natural and voluminous hairstyles are suitable, which can appear gentle and lovely. Bangs can be left in front of the forehead, and the hair on both cheeks can be cut shorter to shorten the length of the face and enhance the width of the face. Ayumi Hamasaki: The fluffy and dreamy curly hairstyle is as cute as a girl, and the messy flat bangs modify the forehead.

What hairstyles suit a diamond-shaped face? For a diamond-shaped face with pointed forehead and chin, you can choose a perm hairstyle to soften the sharp angles of your face with large waves or small curls. You can use bangs to cover the pointed forehead.

What hairstyles suit an inverted triangle (pear-shaped face)? For a pear-shaped face with wider cheeks and narrower forehead, to conceal the defect, the pear-shaped face is most suitable for permed hair. The upper part of the hair should be fluffy and the lower part should be contracted to cover the cheeks and give a slim feeling. Girls with larger cheeks like Song Hye-kyo can try this hairstyle, using the inward curling shape at the bottom to slim down the large cheeks.

What hairstyles suit a square face? The inner contour of the hairstyle should partially obscure the face shape to reduce the square appearance. The hair on the forehead can slant down to cover a corner of the forehead, while the other side's hair can be waved to cover the other cheek. The top hair should be natural and soft, not too close to the scalp. Rina Fujii uses the soft lines of her hair to modify the rugged facial contours.

What hairstyles suit a round face? Round-faced girls may look younger. To appear more mature, fully expose the forehead to elongate the face shape. Parting the hair on both sides makes the face feel narrower and longer. Long bangs can be parted in the middle or to the side to cover the cheeks. Yoon Eun-hye has a slightly chubby face, and she uses long hair to cover her baby fat, and a center-parted hairstyle to make the visual effect symmetrical on both sides.

What hairstyles suit a regular triangle (almond-shaped face)? The face shape is wider on top and narrower at the bottom. When doing a hairstyle, note that the top of the head should not be too fluffy, and the exaggerated part should be the lower hair near the cheeks. Use expanded side hair to expand the chin and make the thin chin and cheeks stand out. You can side-part the hair, make the longer side into a wavy style over the forehead, and the hair length should reach the chin, letting the hair naturally fall and inward curl but covering the cheeks and chin to avoid looking sharper. Namie Amuro: When perming, avoid blowing and styling the top, as fluffy top hair will only make the upper half of the face look bigger. On the contrary, the smaller lower half of the face can be modified with fluffy curls.
