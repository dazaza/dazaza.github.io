Advantages of Registering a Hong Kong Company

Hong Kong is an extremely information-developed international metropolis and one of the world's most free trading ports. Coupled with its excellent infrastructure and sound legal system, it provides entrepreneurs and businessmen with an unparalleled business environment. Currently, more and more mainland businessmen are establishing their own companies in Hong Kong for various purposes, developing their businesses, enhancing their company images, and increasing the confidence of overseas customers.

(1) Free choice of company names: Company names can include regional and industry-specific terms, such as China, Beijing, Zhejiang, Hong Kong, United States, France, International, Asia, Academy, Publishing House, Association, Group, Holding, Industry, Development, Investment, Finance, Fellowship, Technology, Medicine, Fund, Promotion Association, etc.

(2) Minimal restrictions on business scope: Clothing, furniture, jewelry, electronics technology, finance, pharmaceuticals, shipping transportation, import-export trade, real estate, construction, decoration, information networks, tourism, academies, cultural publishing, associations, research institutes, and other high-tech industries can all become our businesses. A Hong Kong limited company can operate any business in Hong Kong except for illegal activities.

(3) Low-tax environment conducive to development: Hong Kong has low tax rates and few tax types. Many institutions around the world use Hong Kong's tax advantages to achieve reasonable tax avoidance. When we establish a company in Hong Kong, we generally only need to pay two taxes: one is a one-time capital registration stamp duty at a rate of 1/1000, which is determined based on your actual registered capital; the other is profits tax at a rate of 16.5%, calculated based on our actual profits (net profit). If the company does not make a profit, no tax is due. Apart from tobacco and alcohol or special cases, Hong Kong has no import-export taxes.

(4) Low registered capital and no need for capital verification: The Hong Kong government requires a minimum registered capital of HKD 10,000. You can increase the registered capital according to your actual situation. There is no limit on the amount of funds that need to be in place, and there is no requirement to transfer the funds to a Hong Kong bank, laying the foundation for us to establish an international group company.

(5) Hong Kong companies allow no operations and permit shell companies to exist. Establishing a Hong Kong shell company can turn into effective advertising promotion.

(6) A window for expanding the international market and a stepping stone for entering the Chinese mainland market: Due to Hong Kong's unique historical conditions and geographical location, it creates favorable conditions for mainland enterprises to develop outwardly. Therefore, establishing a company in Hong Kong can serve as a window to the outside world, easily gaining the trust and cooperation of foreign enterprises. On the other hand, since China has just opened up, overseas investors prefer to set up regional headquarters in Hong Kong as a stepping stone for investing in China.

(7) Easy access to international credit and loans: As is well known, Hong Kong is the economic and financial center of Asia, with almost every street having a bank. We can use the credit of Hong Kong banks, which is the foundation for developing international business. After obtaining credit, we can use Hong Kong as a financial center for financing, or directly issue letters of credit to overseas parties to conduct large transactions with minimal capital. If needed, we can also apply for small and medium-sized enterprise assistance loans from the Hong Kong government and reinvest this money into the market for profit.

(8) Free movement of people, goods, and capital: Hong Kong is one of the freest and most prosperous trading ports in the world, with good infrastructure. Not only is the movement of people and goods free, but so is the movement of capital. The freedom of people movement is reflected in Hong Kong's visa-free agreements with over 100 countries and the upcoming visa-free travel plan with the Pearl River Delta region. The freedom of goods movement mainly manifests in no tariffs on imports and exports, and extremely fast handling of sea, land, and air logistics. The freedom of capital movement reflects Hong Kong's lack of foreign exchange controls, allowing various currencies to be freely exchanged and transferred without restrictions. Businessmen like to use the convenience of Hong Kong banks to handle letters of credit.

(9) Hong Kong is one of the best places in the world because it has a complete intellectual property protection law and a world-recognized mechanism: Utilizing Hong Kong's reputation, creating a Hong Kong brand: Due to Hong Kong's significant international status, emerging enterprises often like to use Hong Kong's reputation to package themselves. For example, Hong Kong's clothing, toys, cultural publishing, and other industries have certain competitive advantages internationally. Establishing this type of company in Hong Kong allows you to immediately share the overall international image and status, enhancing your company's competitiveness to some extent.

(10) Striving for Hong Kong residency: If your company has already contributed to Hong Kong, you can apply for multiple business visas from the Hong Kong Immigration Department. After living there for seven years, you can obtain permanent residency in Hong Kong. Within this year, the Hong Kong government will launch an investment immigration plan. For more information, please stay updated on announcements from the Hong Kong government.

(11) Superb financial system: Hong Kong is a super financial center. Being part of it, you can freely conduct remittance transactions with various countries and enjoy over 200 types of financial services. Bank operations can be carried out through the internet and electronic transaction services, making it very convenient.

(12) Loans, mergers, and financing: Hong Kong limited companies can apply for any bank services, such as credit cards, bank loans, and letter of credit services, and can also apply for any trust funds from the government to assist in business development.

(13) Limited liability: A Hong Kong limited company is a legal and independent entity. Legally, you and your limited company are completely unrelated, and you do not need to bear any responsibility for the limited company.

(14) Reliable confidence: Hong Kong is a global super financial center with a sound legal system, giving your partners and any collaborators a trustworthy impression, undoubtedly greatly helping your business promotion and development.
