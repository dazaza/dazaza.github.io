Classification and Application Scope of Couplings

by zhangjintao3 on 2012-02-23 20:01:39

Types of Couplings: Commonly used precision couplings include: elastic couplings, diaphragm couplings, bellows couplings, slider couplings,梅花couplings, and rigid couplings.

Features of Couplings:

1. Elastic Coupling

(1) Monolithic metal elastomer;

(2) Zero backlash, can run synchronously;

(3) Elastic effect compensates for radial, angular, and axial deviations;

(4) High torque stiffness and excellent sensitivity;

(5) Clockwise and counterclockwise rotational characteristics are completely identical;

(6) Maintenance-free, oil-resistant, and corrosion-resistant;

(7) Available in aluminum alloy and stainless steel materials;

(8) Fixing methods mainly include set screws and clamping types.

2. Diaphragm Coupling

(1) High rigidity, high torque, low inertia;

(2) Uses circular or square elastic stainless steel plates to deform;

(3) Large torque capacity, high torque stiffness, and outstanding sensitivity;

(4) Zero rotational clearance, clockwise and counterclockwise rotational characteristics are the same;

(5) Maintenance-free, highly oil-resistant and corrosion-resistant;

(6) Double stainless steel diaphragms can compensate for radial, angular, and axial deviations, while single diaphragms cannot compensate for radial deviations.

3. Bellows Coupling

(1) No gear gap, torsional rigidity, reliable connection, corrosion resistance, heat resistance;

(2) Maintenance-free, highly oil-resistant, bellows-shaped structure compensates for radial, angular, and axial deviations, can maintain isokinetic motion even with deviations present;

(3) Clockwise and counterclockwise rotational characteristics are entirely the same;

(4) Bellows material options include phosphor bronze and stainless steel;

(5) Suitable for systems requiring high accuracy and stability.

4. Slider Coupling

(1) Gearless connection, simple structure for small torque measurement transmission;

(2) Easy to use, easy to install, time-saving, wide size range, low moment of inertia, convenient for visual inspection;

(3) Oil-resistant and electrically insulating, different material slider elastomers available;

(4) Sliding between the shaft sleeve and the middle piece allows for large radial and angular deviations. The special protruding design of the middle piece provides support, allowing larger angular deviations without generating bending moments and minimizing shaft load.

5.梅花Coupling

(1) Compact type, no gear gap, providing three different hardness elastomers, expansion bushings;

(2) Can absorb vibration, compensate for radial and angular deviations;

(3) Simple structure, easy maintenance, convenient inspection;

(4) Maintenance-free, oil-resistant, electrically insulating, working temperature 20°C-60°C;

(5)梅花elastomers come in four-petal, six-petal, eight-petal, and ten-petal versions;

(6) Fixing methods include set screws, clamping, and keyway fixing.

6. Rigid Coupling

(1) Lightweight, ultra-low inertia, and high sensitivity;

(2) Maintenance-free, highly oil-resistant and corrosion-resistant;

(3) Cannot accommodate eccentricity, should expose the shaft as much as possible during use;

(4) Main body material options include aluminum alloy/stainless steel;

(5) Fixing methods include clamping and set screw fixing.

Applications:

Elastic coupling: Suitable for rotary encoders, stepper motors;

Diaphragm coupling: Suitable for servo motors, stepper motors;

Bellows coupling: Suitable for servo motors;

Slider coupling: Suitable for general miniature motors;

梅花Coupling: Suitable for servo motors, stepper motors;

Rigid coupling: Suitable for servo motors, stepper motors.
