The puppy fell ill

One day, the little animals were playing games when the puppy suddenly felt very cold. The kitten gave the puppy more clothes, the lamb gave the puppy a towel and a hat, but the puppy was still hot. The pig brought a piece of silk and wrapped it around the puppy, but the puppy was still very cold. The monkey said: "The puppy must be sick, let's take him to the hospital!" Everyone took the puppy to Dr. Ma. After Dr. Ma examined the puppy, he said to everyone: "He has caught a cold and fevered, a shot and some medicine will make him better." After hearing the doctor's words, they understood that if you are sick, you should see a doctor so that you get better faster! A few days later, the puppy got well and happily played games with everyone again.

Question: If you are sick, can you bravely go to the doctor? Why should you go to the doctor?

Little Bear Doudou Finds Treasure

Little Bear Doudou found a treasure map. Following the map's record, Little Bear Doudou came to an ancient tomb where he discovered many artifacts from the Warring States period. There was a tripod, a cauldron, a teapot, a dagger, and a sand-filled funnel. The hourglass was used in ancient times to measure time, for example, Zi hour, You hour, which is what we now call o'clock. Doudou thought: These are all artifacts, they should be sent to the museum so that we can better understand history. Little Bear Doudou carried these artifacts to the museum and began a new adventure.

Little Rabbit and the Wolf

The little animals knew the big wolf would come to eat the little rabbit early in the morning, so everyone helped the little rabbit. The little dog closed the door and windows so the big wolf couldn't get in. But even though there was a roof with tiles and a chimney, if the big wolf climbed through the chimney, what would happen? The monkey had a good idea: dig a deep hole directly under the chimney and fill it with fire. It was getting dark, the big wolf came to eat the little rabbit, everyone hid under the bed. The big wolf noticed the door and windows were closed and couldn't get in, so he thought he could climb through the chimney. Just as the big wolf climbed onto the chimney, everyone heard a "splash," and sure enough, the big wolf fell into the hole they dug. The big wolf cried out: "Help, help!" But no one paid attention to him, and thus the big wolf drowned.

Question: If you were a little animal, what method would you use to defeat the big wolf?

Little Horse Gets Lost

Little Horse went to the forest alone to play, accidentally got lost, didn't know the way back, until noon still couldn't find the way home. At that moment, he found a house, but the owner wasn't there. Little Horse entered the house and found a fishing net and an umbrella on the wall, also a white flag pointing the way, and a wooden pestle and stone mortar for pounding rice upstairs. There was half a glass of milk and half a loaf of bread on the tea table. Little Horse was very hungry, but he didn't eat the food on the tea table because he didn't get the owner's permission. Little Horse saw the floor was dirty, so he picked up the broom and winnowing basket to sweep the house. It wasn't until evening that Little Horse saw someone holding a torch coming back. It turned out to be Grandma Sheep's house. Little Horse told his experience. Grandma Sheep thanked Little Horse for helping her clean and sent him home. Grandma Sheep told Little Horse not to go out alone in the future, because it was dangerous.
