In the movie, Liu Ye is quite lucky in love. He acts opposite Li Xiaoran and Gao Yuanyuan, who play the roles of his wife and first love respectively. Both relationships become entangled due to a one-night stand, resulting in eye-catching passionate scenes where they share the same bed. Liu Ye shares different feelings about the passionate scenes with these two beauties. He has already worked with Gao Yuanyuan three times, and they are like good buddies, so shooting passionate scenes doesn't make him uncomfortable. About Li Xiaoran, I think she's exceptionally beautiful, and we have great chemistry when working together. Liu Ye's preference seems to be well-received by Li Xiaoran.

China Film News reports that the movie "Unmanned" which will be released on July 2nd held its first trailer premiere yesterday (June 23rd) in Beijing. Director Zhang Yang appeared alongside the main cast including Liu Ye, Gao Yuanyuan, Li Xiaoran, Wang Luodan, etc. According to director Zhang Yang, this film integrates themes such as the seven-year itch, reunion with first love, the third party rising, age-gap romance, one-night stands, mid-life emotional crises, and the love stories of post-90s generation. The character relationships are quite intricate, and everyone who has loved will find resonance in the film.
