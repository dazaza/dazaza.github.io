When the object to be weighed or the loaded vehicle is placed on the load-bearing platform, under the action of gravity, the load-bearing device transmits the gravity to the weighing sensor, causing the elastic body of the weighing sensor to deform. The strain gauge bridge attached to the elastic body loses balance and outputs an electrical signal proportional to the weight value, which is amplified by a linear amplifier. Then, after A/D conversion into a digital signal, the microprocessor (CPU) of the instrument processes the weight signal and directly displays the weight data. If a printer is configured, the weighing data can be printed and recorded. If a computer is configured, the weighing data can be entered into the computer management system for comprehensive management.
