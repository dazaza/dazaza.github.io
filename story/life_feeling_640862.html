A journey, a lifetime......

by mimi456 on 2011-12-22 16:14:20

When she found out she was pregnant, it was a month after their breakup. At the moment she confirmed the result, Qin Xiaobai thought she was the unluckiest person in the world. The plot twists from TV shows, movies, and novels had actually happened to her. For a moment, she even had the impulse to die.

But then she thought, although this situation seemed terrible, there was only one path she could choose. Since he was determined to break up with her, there was no point in trying to win him back. Love, when it comes down to纠缠 (entanglement), loses all its previous beauty. She still had her parents, and in the future, she would have to get married and live her life. She wasn't foolish enough to keep the child.

In those days, Qin Xiaobai really wanted to contact him and tell him about this. He also bore responsibility for this outcome. However, Qin Xiaobai was a stubborn person. Sometimes, her good intentions were expressed in ways that were hard for others to understand. In dealing with this matter, Qin Xiaobai persisted in her own way as she always did, and kept this matter to herself.

After her friends found out about her breakup, they consoled her. Qin Xiaobai always smiled and told them that she was fine, and that breaking up was the best choice for both of them because two stubborn people together would never be happy. But her tears at night, her reluctance in her heart, and this entire event proved one thing: Qin Xiaobai was not doing well at all.

After quitting her job, Qin Xiaobai didn't immediately jump into the job-hunting crowd because she couldn't focus on work at this time. She needed to find a hospital and deal with this little life that shouldn't have come into this world at the most appropriate time. She didn't want to leave a blemish on the city where she was about to fight and live her life, so she chose another city, a city where no one knew her.

That day, the hospital was very busy. After finishing all the procedures, Qin Xiaobai sat on a bench waiting. Looking at the people around her, almost all of them were accompanied by someone else, and Qin Xiaobai's tears couldn't stop flowing. But she told herself that this was the karma she had created, and the method she had chosen, so she had no right to feel wronged.

When she went inside, her heart suddenly became calm. The process wasn't as painful as she had imagined, but everything around her was icy cold, chilling her to the core. That night, Qin Xiaobai had many dreams, fragmented and incomplete.

In the following days, Qin Xiaobai slowly recovered. Occasionally, she would receive his text messages asking about her current situation. Because he knew that Qin Xiaobai's menstrual period had been delayed, she thought she should let him feel some pain, make him feel guilty and self-blame. So Qin Xiaobai lied to him, telling him that her period hadn't come and that she wasn't pregnant after checking. But Qin Xiaobai forgot that one lie requires ten or even a hundred lies to cover it up.

By chance, Qin Xiaobai went to the city where they first met. She walked through the places where they had held hands for the first time, embraced for the first time, and kissed for the first time. The beauty of her memories contrasted with her present desolation. Qin Xiaobai squatted on the street where they had first held hands and cried, wiping her tears with all her strength, yet the more she wiped, the more tears came.

The day before Qin Xiaobai left, he also came.

Upon seeing each other, Qin Xiaobai pretended to be strong, acting tough. Both of them understood that even though they had broken up, upon seeing each other, neither could control themselves, so they got back together.

When they held hands again, Qin Xiaobai softened. All her strength crumbled in that moment, and all her resentment disappeared instantly. People say there is always that one person who, no matter how they treated you before, you can always find a reason to forgive them. So Qin Xiaobai closed her eyes and told herself to indulge just this once more. That night, while embracing each other, Qin Xiaobai said "I'm sorry" several times to him. But she never mentioned that event. In her heart, Qin Xiaobai said, "I'm sorry, I terminated our child without you knowing; I'm sorry, I lied to you; I'm sorry, please forgive my selfishness."

She left early the next morning. Before leaving, Qin Xiaobai told him "I love you" several times. That day, it rained heavily, matching Qin Xiaobai's mood perfectly.

Qin Xiaobai knew that this meeting meant it would be the last time they would ever meet in their lives. From now on, they would be strangers.

After returning home, Qin Xiaobai went to have her fortune told. The blind fortune-teller said that she had such a fate. Suddenly, Qin Xiaobai felt relieved. This was enough, worth it. Qin Xiaobai deleted everything she should delete and threw away everything she should throw away. She knew that even if she still had thoughts of him in the future, she wouldn't have any lingering affection.

After doing all this, Qin Xiaobai went to the seaside once. She said to the sea, "Qin Xiaobai, your life has started anew, cheer up!"

And so, Qin Xiaobai began her new life.

Trading this chapter for a lifetime.
