Jay Chou's Development History

by doudouqaj on 2011-10-17 08:18:56

Introduction to Jay Chou Music Network: Jay Chou Music Network (www.musicjay.com) is a professional Jie fan website mainly providing information related to Jay Chou. It was founded on March 8, 2011. It is an important fan exchange website with themes of Jay Chou's latest information and fan organizations. Members can fully showcase their talents in literary creation, music composition, and song singing on this professional Jie fan website.

Predecessor of Jay Chou Music Network: The predecessor of Jay Chou Music Network was the Jay Chou Tencent Fans Club, established in 2006 by Yan Wei. It developed mainly through QQ Space and QQ groups and had considerable influence within Tencent. Due to Yan Wei's academic commitments, the organization did not grow much. After four years, Yan Wei re-prepared the organization in 2010, and eventually, on March 8, 2011, together with Feng Juan, Zi Yun, and some Jie fans, they founded Jay Chou Music Network, registered the international top-level domain: www.musicjay.com, and successfully went online and opened for registration that evening.

Website Development History:

January 18, 2011: On the occasion of Jay Chou's birthday, the Jay Chou Music Network team began discussing domain names and website construction matters, starting work on the website.

February 21, 2011: The official YY channel of Jay Chou Music Network was officially launched, with the official YY channel number: 19793567.

March 6, 2011: Fans of Jay Chou Music Network started donating to purchase the domain name.

March 8, 2011: Jay Chou Music Network was officially established, registering the international domain name www.musicjay.com.

March 10, 2011: Jay Chou Music Network officially started collecting logos.

March 11, 2011: The homepage of Jay Chou Music Network was born.

March 12, 2011: The slogan of Jay Chou Music Network was born: "Music has no boundaries, loving Jie never tires!"

April 17, 2011: Jay Chou Music Network underwent its first redesign, with a Chinese style theme.

April 22, 2011: To successfully register Jay Chou Music Network, it began using a new domain name.

April 26, 2011: The official QQ Talk of Jay Chou Music Network was officially launched. Official QQ Talk: 679118

May 5, 2011: Jay Chou Music Network upgraded its interface again, with a cute Doudou Lun style.

May 16, 2011: Jay Chou Music Network was included in Bing.

May 18, 2011: Jay Chou Music Network successfully registered, restoring international domain access: www.musicjay.com.

June 3, 2011: Jay Chou Music Network's Sina Weibo official certification passed (as of July 5, the number of Weibo followers reached 71,920 people, the first certified Jie fan group on Sina Weibo).

June 14, 2011: Jay Chou Music Network successfully held a website event where participants could win prizes such as Jay Chou's autographed photo and QQ membership services.
