Newzan Network: It is a network that provides personalized recommended content, focusing on individualized reading. The Newzan Network was created to solve the problem of information overload. It is a news content reading filter that evolves into personalized recommendations through user behavior and data support. Link: http://www.newzan.com/

Meet - A social application based on nearby strangers: Meet is a social application based on nearby strangers, helping you communicate instantly with people near you who you do not know. Link: http://www.iaround.net/

Sina Weibo - Micro Show Launch: Female video live streaming, you can also apply for a room (if there's a private room, the market would be huge, haha). It seems that the demand in this area is very strong. Link: http://show.weibo.com/

LiiHo Handwritten Instant Messaging: By using LiiHo, you can chat by handwriting text on an iPad or doodling on photos, just like passing notes in person, very convenient! If you have an iPad, download it quickly and use it. Currently, it provides an iPad client. Link: http://www.liiho.com.tw/

Handmade Guest - Create Yourself with Both Hands: A community for handmade creative workers' work and life, currently in internal testing. Link: http://www.sogoke.com/

Yihu - Based on geographical location C2C supply and demand trading platform: Currently provides Android client. The biggest issue with this type of application lies in user density. Link: http://yihu.me/

Weishi Tong is a Social TV application. Through Weishi Tong, you can:

1. Learn about the featured programs broadcasted today by dozens of TV stations nationwide.

2. Query detailed information and broadcast times of featured programs by TV station classification.

3. Communicate with friends, audiences, and hosts, express opinions and vote on programs, participate in discussions.

4. Follow your favorite programs and receive notification reminders at designated times before the program airs.

5. Share the programs you are watching, following, or participating in discussions with friends on Weibo. Link: http://wst.ifeng.com/

Favspot - A tool to collect and share places you like. It has already completed several location-based SMS marketing campaigns with some merchants. Link: http://www.favspot.net/

QQ慧眼 (Huayan): It is a fast recognition software. Installing QQ Huayan on your phone can quickly capture Chinese, English, French, German texts, and translate them in real time. Main functions: Fast text capture and recognition function (Chinese, English, French, German); Multi-language real-time translation function (English-Chinese mutual translation, French to Chinese, German to Chinese); Subsequent support: QR code recognition, LOGO recognition, street view recognition. Link: http://labs.qq.com/product.php?id=5

Bale - Interactive entertainment content provider: Bale (bale.cn) is the first interactive entertainment content provider based on the Internet and mobile Internet in China, a high-end brand in China's interactive video industry, a leading new media content provider and operator in China, and China's first completely copyrighted self-produced entertainment information summary open platform. Link: http://www.bale.cn/

MOMO Weibo Fragment: A printing service based on Sina Weibo, DIY Weibo printing, print Weibo content into small cards (personal "memory fragments"). Link: http://www.momoka.cc/
