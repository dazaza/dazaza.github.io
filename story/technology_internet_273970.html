The standard setters are often the recognized industry leaders. A reporter learned from relevant departments that more than half of wealthy families on the mainland choose to travel to Hong Kong and Macao. Due to Hainan's special status in China's tourism industry, in the "International Demonstration Higher Vocational Colleges Construction Plan", Anqing hotels, Nanning • Southeast Asia International Tourism Food Festival attracted 600,000 food lovers in 9 days, specifically entrusting the professional standard of the "Tourism Management" major to the Tourism Department of Hainan Vocational and Technical College responsible for formulating detailed standards for this major.

This means that in the future, all higher vocational colleges across the country will implement the "Hainan Standard" for this major. This is the first time that the education sector in Hainan Province has formulated a national professional course standard.

Currently, there are already 1,460 higher vocational colleges in our country. But these higher vocational colleges have various majors without specific professional standards, which is extremely inconsistent with the national goal of vigorously developing higher vocational education. For this reason, the country plans to build 100 "National Demonstration Higher Vocational Colleges" nationwide. Hainan Vocational and Technical College was selected as a model institution for the 2008 fiscal year, simultaneously taking on the role of setting new professional standards for the "Tourism Management" major at higher vocational colleges.

Professor Yang Zhekun, Director of the Tourism Department of Hainan Vocational and Technical College and member of the Teaching Guidance Committee for Higher Vocational and Specialized Tourism Education under the Ministry of Education, believes that the reason why the Ministry of Education designated Hainan to formulate the "Tourism Management" professional standard is because the tourism industry is a pillar industry in Hainan, leading the nation in tourism, being the most capable region in China's tourism industry to align with international standards, and can fully represent China's tourism industry in participating in international competition. Additionally, the ongoing construction of the international tourism island in Hainan has a very significant impact domestically. Hainan's tourism industry is highly representative, encountering many cutting-edge issues, so it naturally became the choice for Hainan to set the professional standard for the "Tourism Management" major.
