Procedures:

1. The ski resort uses an all-in-one card system for consumption. Consumable items include skiing (boots, boards, poles), ski suits (jackets, pants) gloves, lockers (keys), snow rings, catering, hiring coaches, etc.

2. Guests process card collection at the cashier, each person pays a deposit of 200 RMB for ski equipment and consumption, receives one consumption card, which is used to collect and return ski equipment as well as for other consumptions.

3. When guests use their consumption cards for spending, the cost will automatically be deducted from the card. If the balance is insufficient, top up at the cashier to continue consumption.

4. One consumption card can only rent any equipment once, cannot rent again after returning.

5. Before checking out, guests must return all rented equipment. If there is any unreturned equipment, checkout will not be allowed.

6. Guests must use their own consumption cards to collect and return equipment, mixing is not allowed. Otherwise, unable to check out or discrepancies in consumption amounts will result in guest's responsibility.

7. Skiing starts timing when the first piece of ski equipment (boot or board) is rented until the last piece of ski equipment (boot or board) is returned. The ski resort gives guests 15 minutes of free consumption time. Guest’s skiing fees are automatically calculated based on the recorded consumption time on the consumption card. After exceeding one billing unit, it will charge according to the next billing unit.

8. Ski suits and lockers will not be charged if returned within 5 minutes of rental, after 5 minutes, charges will be automatically deducted.

9. Hiring coaches starts timing 5 minutes after appointing a coach.

10. Guests who damage ski resort equipment must compensate according to the ski resort compensation regulations.

11. Lost cards must be replaced. After replacement, all information from the original card will be transferred to the new card, with a replacement fee of 20 RMB. Please keep your card safe.
