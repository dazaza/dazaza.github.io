So how to open an online store, and how to open a store on Taobao? Below, we will introduce it to you! 1. Register an account on Taobao. First, log in to Taobao (2. Complete Alipay's real-name authentication. To open a store, you must complete Alipay's real-name authentication. Log in to the Taobao account you just registered, go to the Taobao homepage, find the "My Taobao" link and click to enter this page. You will see a prompt message: "To sell items, please complete real-name authentication first." Click it and follow the steps shown in the prompts to complete it step by step (illustrated tutorial: Alipay real-name authentication steps). Tip: Alipay is the financial intermediary for Taobao users. It is the basis for ensuring honest transactions between buyers and sellers. Alipay's real-name authentication confirms your true identity. This certification greatly increases the security of the entire Taobao transaction system. Now Taobao has cooperated with all banks nationwide. As long as you have a real-name registered bank card from some banks, Taobao can verify your identity through the bank system to complete the real-name authentication. 3. Pass Taobao's store-opening exam. Log in to your Taobao account, then go to "My Taobao" -> "I am a seller," and find the "I want to open a store" button. After clicking, there will be a prompt requiring you to take an exam. To pass Taobao's store-opening exam, you must carefully read and study the exam content "Taobao Rules." A score above 60 points is required to pass. After passing the exam, read the Integrity Operation Commitment Letter, then fill in the store name, store category, and store introduction according to the prompt information item by item. Check the agreement to "Product Release Rules" and "Consumer Protection Agreement," then confirm and submit. If everything goes smoothly, at this point, you will own your own Taobao store! Consumer Protection Plan Joining Taobao's Consumer Protection Program (referred to as 'Consumer Protection') has many benefits. For example, buyers will trust stores that join the consumer protection program more. If two stores offer the same product at the same price, one with consumer protection and one without, generally, buyers will choose to buy from the store with consumer protection; also, when searching for products on Taobao, stores with consumer protection always rank ahead of those without. Therefore, if you want your online store to be more competitive, joining the consumer protection program is also necessary. Of course, joining the consumer protection program requires paying a certain amount of deposit to Taobao, which will be frozen in Alipay. Don't worry, the money is still yours, and after you have been part of the consumer protection program for three months, you can apply for its unfreezing.
