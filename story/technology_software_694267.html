Apron fabric February 11-12 daily express of prices for various light textile materials at home and abroad

by wdkg3duan on 2012-03-07 00:38:36

From February 11 to 12, the PTA spot price was adjusted downward, MEG prices remained temporarily stable, and prices for semi-dull polyester chips, bright polyester chips continued to adjust weakly in the market. CDP chip prices were stable, and polyester bottle flake prices were also temporarily stable. Acrylic short fiber 1.5D×38mm, acrylic tow 3D×102mm prices were both adjusted, CPL, nylon 6 chip prices remained temporarily stable.

For the silk market prices, except for some individual factories which had sporadic increases on Saturday, others mainly maintained stable quotations. The trading atmosphere of silk improved compared to the previous weekend (February 4 to 10). However, the trends of various silk products still appeared fragmented. Currently, downstream weaving manufacturers and texturizing enterprises are not actively purchasing silk; they are first digesting their previously purchased inventory materials. Their procurement intention for silk spot goods is insufficient, and filament spinning manufacturers are under significant inventory pressure. On January 6, domestic prices of various light纺materials were reported daily.

From a cost perspective, most people believe that in the short term, the overall trend of silk will be temporarily stable with adjustments.

The price trend of pure cotton yarn shows steady growth. From the sales of varieties, the sales of 32s cotton yarn are acceptable, while other specifications are relatively quiet. In blended yarn products, prices for polyester-cotton yarn and polyester-viscose yarn remain stable. Rayon yarn market conditions are relatively stable, with better sales of 10s than other specifications, and prices of other specifications remain stable. The market central price for viscose short fibers is around 17,900 yuan/ton. It is expected that rayon yarn will continue to show an adjustment trend in the future. The pure polyester yarn market shows a weak performance, with individual varieties experiencing price reductions. In comparison, the sales volume of 45s is acceptable, but the transaction volume of other specifications is very low. The price trend of polyester short fibers shows weakness, with the market central price at 12,400-12,450 yuan/ton. It is expected that pure polyester yarn products will have a downward adjustment trend in the near future.
