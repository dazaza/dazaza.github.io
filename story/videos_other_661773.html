Having a team that is ready to fight at any time is extremely important, so developers are offering salary increases to attract talent.

by bhhgsyfk4143 on 2012-02-09 12:55:54

After the Evergrande Real Estate entered the Lanzhou real estate market, the Lanzhou property market has attracted many domestic real estate giants such as Wanda and China Overseas Holdings. Before this recruitment season began, the domestic real estate giants have already attracted a number of excellent talents at the end of last year by using their own broad platforms and influence. As for the local real estate developers, how to "retain people" has become an urgent issue, and salary increase has become one of the effective means.

The pattern of the real estate market in 2012 has become very clear. Looking at the whole year's market, due to the sufficient land reserves in the hands of various developers, there is an urgent need for talents to fill the gaps for new project developments. And for the domestic first-line "real estate tycoons", the problem of finding a team that can "immediately engage in battle" is even more pressing. "Therefore, the demand for usable talents is more urgent than ever," a real estate professional told reporters, "On the other hand, entering February, the sales process of the developers of the on-sale properties has been settled, and various real estate companies enter a standby state. Having a team that can fight at any time becomes extremely important, so developers attract talents by increasing salaries."

"This is a process of mutual selection," said the person in charge of the human resources department of Tianqing Group. Choosing talents mainly involves finding newcomers who match the company culture, "Selecting the most outstanding talents from those who suit us is our company's consistent practice." On the other hand, some talents also hold the attitude of "rather be the head of a chicken than the tail of a phoenix", believing that they may gain broader space in local real estate companies, thus choosing to serve local real estate enterprises. "For talents who can ultimately stay in the company, we will provide them with an interesting platform and give appropriate salary increases, making them the mainstay of the company."

Regarding the current concentration of real estate developers "grabbing people", many industry insiders believe that this is a signal of a new round of expansion for real estate developers. Once again, it's early spring and the talent war among various industries quietly begins. Real estate companies are no exception, actively recruiting talents to prepare for the Year of the Dragon. It is understood that different from the past, in the 2012 Lanzhou real estate talent war, there are quite a few new faces. The top domestic real estate tycoons are ambitious, following the principle of "provision must be secured before troops are set in motion", they are heavily investing in "grabbing people" to expand their territory in Lanzhou; while local real estate companies such as Yatai, Hongyun, and Tianqing are not hiding their purpose of "attracting talents and protecting their base". Right after the Spring Festival, they used large advertisements to gather all kinds of real estate talents, and offered significant salary increases to attract talents.
