&nbsp; Are you still troubled by the pimples on your face? Are you still worried about your dull, dark skin? Don't worry, we have some little secrets for you to throw all your troubles away.

&nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp;

　Skin care is very important for women, so female friends always try their best to take care of their skin. In fact, skin care is not just about using skincare products; it also involves paying attention to the details in daily life. Below, Anhui Beauty and Hairdressing Training [http://www.ahainisi.org] will introduce some daily skin care knowledge for everyone, let's learn together! &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp;

&nbsp;

1. Do not use a towel to wipe your face.

　After washing your face, you should use a towel or facial tissue to gently pat dry the water. This is much gentler and less harmful to the skin than wiping it dry with a towel.

　2. Do not pat toner with your hands.

　Toner must be applied with a cotton pad, moving from bottom to top to ensure that the skin truly absorbs the moisture. Especially for nose pores which are opposite in direction to the face, the application should also be opposite, moving from top to bottom.

　3. Do not apply a mask while doing housework.

　The best posture for applying a mask is lying down. When lying down, the body is in a relaxed state, the facial muscles are not tense, this helps the pores on the facial skin to open fully and absorb the nutrients from the mask.

　4. Use sunscreen all year round.

　No matter what season, whether spring, summer, autumn, or winter, any weather including cloudy, sunny, rainy, or snowy days, sunscreen should be used.

　5. Do not frequently do deep exfoliation.

　The frequency of exfoliation varies. Oily skin can do it once a week; for combination skin, it is recommended to exfoliate different areas separately, oily T-zone can be done once a week, dry V-zone can be done every three weeks; dry skin due to dryness and slow keratin metabolism should be done at most once a month; sensitive skin is advised not to exfoliate.

　6. If you wear makeup, you must remove it.

　No matter how tired you are from work or how late it is, if you haven't worn makeup, then there is no need to remove it. For sunscreens, those with SPF over 20 require removal, while those under SPF 20 can be cleaned off with facial cleanser.

　7. Spot-removal skincare products can only fade spots.

　Any beauty care, even fruit acid peeling, can only fade spots, not completely remove them. So, ladies should spend money on concealer or sunscreen.

　8. Use a towel to remove blackheads, whiteheads, and dead skin cells

　After bathing, when the stratum corneum is wet and soft, while the steam in the bathroom has not dissipated and is still warm, you can use medical gauze, but the denser the fabric, the better. Gently massage in circles on areas with blackheads and where exfoliation is needed, which will help remove blackheads and dead skin cells. Using a towel instead of hands during daily face washing will also have an exfoliating effect, but dry and sensitive skin should be cautious.

　9. Use sugar for massage, exfoliation, and skincare

　Wet your face beforehand, create foam with facial cleanser in your palm, add sugar (the granules should not be too large, otherwise they may scratch the skin), or finely ground mung bean powder on areas with blackheads and dead skin cells, and slowly massage in circles. The advantage of this method is that it can achieve the effect of exfoliation, and as the sugar melts, it also provides nourishment to the skin.

　10. Use frozen towels to shrink pores

　Place a completely dry towel in a sealed ziplock bag, then put it in the refrigerator. Before applying makeup in the morning, take it out and place it on your face, which can help shrink pores. The towel must not have any moisture, otherwise it will freeze and injure the skin.

　11. Massage to tighten skin and reduce swelling

　Drainage massage method: use the pads of your index and middle fingers, from the sides of the nostrils, cheeks to the earlobes, gradually pause and lightly press, each for about 5-10 seconds; then place both hands on either side of the cheeks extending to the ears for another 5-10 seconds. These are also the locations of acupuncture points known as Tinghui, Jiaochexue, and Dayingxue, which can help eliminate excess fat. Below the earlobes is the location of the lymph nodes responsible for metabolism. With the help of hand warmth, it aids in metabolizing water and waste from the lymph nodes. Clench your fists and slide them over the cheeks, through massage making the protruding masseter muscles smoother, and also lifting the skin that has become loose due to aging.

　12. Post-sunburn repair with watermelon

　Use watermelon rind to cool the skin. The effect of watermelon rind is similar to aloe vera, helping to soothe the skin after sun exposure.

　These twelve common methods for skin care mentioned above, do you understand them now? If you want to take care of your skin, you must start from the little things in daily life. Only then can you have good skin. So, what are you waiting for?

&nbsp; Many friends who want to learn beauty and hairdressing have seen some countermeasures online: Winter hair care

Introduction: As winter winds blow, hair inevitably encounters problems such as dryness, static electricity, and loss of moisture. Therefore, it's time to take action for hair care in winter. How should we care for our hair in winter? Like skin, hair is prone to dehydration, so we need to replenish moisture from root to tip. In winter, dry hair should choose weakly acidic shampoo because alkaline shampoo will take away too much oil, making the scalp drier. Let's look at more hair care strategies.

&middot; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; Problem 1 &nbsp; Dryness from top to bottom

　Countermeasure: Rehydration plan for roots and tips.

　Understanding the level of dehydration in your hair: Normally, hair contains about 15% water, but if it drops below 10%, it will immediately show signs of dryness. Now, please touch and gently rub your hair from root to tip. If it feels dry and makes a rustling sound when rubbed, your hair is severely dehydrated and needs immediate rehydration.

　Weakly acidic shampoo: In winter, dry hair should use weakly acidic shampoo because alkaline shampoo will take away too much oil, making the scalp drier. If you use moisturizing shampoo and wash your hair 4-7 times a week, you can ensure the moisture content of your hair.

&nbsp;

Problem 2 &nbsp; Static electricity caused by friction

　Hair care countermeasure: Complete anti-static.

　Frequent washing: Frequent washing prevents pollution particles from attaching to your hair, keeping your hair clean and reducing friction, thus preventing split ends and breakage.

　Post-wash conditioning: Cationic active substances in conditioners allow the molecules on the hair surface to align in one direction, reducing the charge and resistance on the hair, enhancing the anti-static effect of the protective film.

　Quality comb: Apply some smoothing agent or leave-in conditioner before combing to effectively improve static electricity. It is best to use wooden or horn combs, avoiding plastic or metal combs that can easily generate static electricity and harm your hair. &gt; &gt;

　Problem 3 &nbsp; Frizzy and unruly hair

　Hair care countermeasure: Add some conditioner to your hair

　Correct styling method: Many people's frizzy and unruly hair is caused by blowing it completely dry immediately after washing. After washing, when your hair is half-dry, use the low-temperature setting on your hairdryer to dry it to about 80% dry, then apply leave-in conditioner to the entire hair except the roots, and finally dry it while combing.

　Protein-based hair care: Adding a small amount of protein to your shampoo and gently massaging your scalp during washing yields great results.
