The woman who donated a kidney to save her husband unfortunately developed stomach cancer. Her husband wanted to give part of his stomach to save his beloved wife.

by xue94fwsh on 2012-03-01 14:41:44

Yesterday, before dawn, Zhang Deqian, as usual, put on a mask and hurriedly left home. Setting out from his home at the foot of Zipei Mountain, he transferred to a bus and then rode an electric scooter borrowed from his wife's younger sister's home. "Before 7:30, I can probably get to the hospital to pick up my test report," he said to himself. The cost of staying by the patient's bedside was five yuan per night, but he couldn't bear it. Due to the high cost of hospitalization, his wife had to go through the discharge procedures even though her stitches hadn't been removed yet.

Due to the sudden onset of Zhu Aiyun's illness, besides incessant vomiting, her stomach had swollen so much that she could not eat anything, and could only undergo surgery to "build a bridge" between her stomach and intestines. This "bridge-building" would allow her to eat. If Zhu Aiyun started chemotherapy, she would need treatment once a month, with each session costing nearly ten thousand yuan.

In the spring of 2009, Zhang Deqian, who had always been physically strong, felt something was wrong with his body. "I lost over ten kilograms all at once, the change was particularly significant. Look at my palms now, they are red, but back then they were as white as a wall, without a bit of blood color."

"She saved my life, but how come I can't help her?" Zhang Deqian felt extremely guilty. He said he was willing to give everything for his wife, but the enormous medical expenses left him no room to maneuver. Soon, Zhang Deqian was diagnosed with uremia, "either dialysis or kidney transplant." There were only two choices in front of him, and there was another possibility that Zhang Deqian didn't dare nor wanted to think about.

That day, Zhang Deqian quickly calmed down, holding back his tears, and calmly told his wife, "We will be going home soon. The doctor said you just have gastric ulcer, it's not serious."

Since November this year, Zhu Aiyun has been hospitalized in the Provincial Hospital for further physical examination. "About twenty years ago, she had gastric ulcer, but she never vomited like this before, she could hardly move."

Feeling his waist, inside was a kidney originally belonging to his wife, which had been functioning normally in his body for five months.

"Husband, my kidney is for you."

They looked forward to the last day of winter vacation after the operation, hoping to regain the happy family life of four people again. After getting sick, the young couple first divorced and then remarried just to register on Valentine's Day, dragging their pair of children into hardship, dropping out of school to work.

He urgently wanted to cut his stomach for his beloved.

"When we were matching, the doctor took her pictures, showing two kidneys, one big and one small, and she still smiled and told me, 'Husband, the bigger one is for you.'" Zhang Deqian reminisced about his wife's kindness.

During the operation period, Zhang Deqian accompanied her day and night. At night, he slept half-body beside her bed, legs resting on the nearby table.

"She doesn't mind our poverty, saying she is willing to stay and take care of our family." In Zhang Deqian's eyes, his wife Zhu Aiyun is the kindest person in the world. Not long after marriage, Zhu Aiyun gave him a son, and they also adopted a girl. A family of four lived happily together.

"I used to be a driver for a courier company, and she worked in housekeeping. We could earn three thousand yuan a month, not much, but we were very happy. She is especially kind and good, everyone who has interacted with her says so."

Her name is Zhu Aiyun, and she is 44 years old this year.

"I have even drunk stones, large green stones weighing several kilograms, just swallowed them down." To survive, Zhang Deqian tried every method. A barefoot doctor nearby told him that drinking stones could save the lives of those with uremia, and he decided to try.

When she cried, he drank stone powder for ten days, and Zhu Aiyun cried for ten days.

On December 2nd, Zhu Aiyun was admitted to the Provincial Hospital for surgical treatment.

That year

Zhang Deqian's older brother passed away due to uremia. At that time, Zhang Deqian was at the age of marrying, but couldn't find a spouse.

But now, the condition can no longer be concealed...

Upon hearing the diagnosis from the attending doctor, Zhang Deqian was overwhelmed with grief. But he made up his mind to hide the truth from his wife.

On December 1st, after completing the pathological analysis, the doctor called Zhang Deqian and told him the news he least wanted to hear.

"This cannot save her life, so I beg the doctor to take my stomach, give it all to her, okay?"

Zhang Deqian's elder brother died of uremia many years ago. By then, Zhang Deqian was already of marriageable age but couldn't find a bride.

Seeing her husband becoming thinner day by day, suffering unbearable abdominal pain, after the Spring Festival this year, Zhu Aiyun voluntarily proposed to go to the hospital for matching, to donate her kidney to her husband.

"The six indicators, three and a half matched, the doctors said it was rare." The matching went smoothly, but because of financial difficulties, the two didn't enter the hospital until late July for the kidney transplant surgery.

She is his wife now, but twenty years ago, they called each other uncle and niece-in-law.

"I feel something is not right, but neither of us dared to think the worst." Zhang Deqian said, this time his wife's illness seemed unusual.

The doctor told Zhang Deqian that besides needing chemotherapy, Zhu Aiyun also needed to undergo a "gastrojejunostomy" surgery.

She was willing to donate her kidney to him, saving his life. Now she is sick with advanced gastric cancer.

"Each chemotherapy costs at least eight to nine thousand yuan, where can I get this money?" The cruel and realistic problem was once again placed in front of Zhang Deqian, and this time, he felt completely helpless and desperate.

"You see her, under no circumstances should you let it slip. She does not know she has gastric cancer, she thinks it is just a gastric ulcer." On the way back to Zhang Deqian's house, he kept reminding the reporter.

He wove the lie of "just a gastric ulcer"

This day

After his wife was confirmed, Zhang Deqian actually considered telling her the truth. "All the family members, including neighbors, know except her and her mother now, just don't dare to tell her." Zhang Deqian said, afraid his wife would collapse upon hearing the truth.

"It was the surgery on July 29th, I remember clearly. From her chest to her waist, there was a wound so long, stitched 19 times."
