Repost your friends' photos, no need to "go through a lot of trouble"

by cyc44454ss on 2010-04-19 11:25:57

Previously, everyone gave feedback that they saw many photos taken together by their friends and wanted to put them in their own albums. However, they had to download the photos first and then upload them again, which was quite troublesome. So, the album team specially developed the "photo reprint" function, which allows you to easily transfer your friend's public photos to your own space in just one step. First, the owner can set whether to allow their friends to reprint photos from a certain album.

Entry point 1: Set album permissions on the right side of the album page.

Entry point 2: When creating an album.

Entry point 3: Click the permission icon below a single album.

Now go to your friend's space and reprint photos. (To provide better service, this function is currently in internal testing, during which only Yellow Diamond users can reprint.)

Reprint entry point: On the toolbar of individual photos (no such entry for encrypted albums).

When re-printing, you can choose which album to transfer the photo to, or even transfer it to a newly created album. You can also modify the photo name and description. Below the reprinted photo, there will be a red [Reprint] mark and the original author's address to protect the author's copyright.

Finally, please note that the reprinted photos will be saved in your album and will occupy your album space~ At the same time, don't worry about deleting your own photos inadvertently deleting your friend's photos, because your photos are not the same copy (*^__^*)...
