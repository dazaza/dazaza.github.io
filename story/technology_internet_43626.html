The 5th China (Guangzhou) International Machinery and Equipment Manufacturing Expo

by seosh on 2008-01-18 10:32:23

Original article from: Technifor http://www.technifor.cn/

Approval authority: Ministry of Science and Technology of the People's Republic of China

Organizer: Guangzhou Municipal People’s Government, China Machinery Industry Federation

Organizing unit: Guangzhou Economic and Trade Commission

Contracting unit: Guangdong Exhibition Promotion Co., Ltd. (Member of International Association of Exhibition Management IAEM)

GIMEE'2008 is an important exhibition in the field of machine tools, mechanical equipment, and related supporting industries in China and Asia. It has been successfully held for four editions with a cumulative exhibition area of 150,000 square meters and more than 2,500 exhibitors from home and abroad. This edition will cover an exhibition area of 40,000 square meters, with 2,000 booths pre-set. More than 600 domestic and foreign machine tool, mechanical equipment, and related supporting enterprises will jointly present new highlights in the industry. The strong support and participation of many domestic and foreign authoritative institutions and first-class professional buyers will stimulate business contacts between exhibitors and international markets. The exhibition will strive to achieve comprehensive breakthroughs in group exhibitions organized domestically and internationally, the organization of professional buyers at home and abroad, and the influence of the exhibition, creating cooperation opportunities for exhibitors.

- Highlighting the strong competitiveness and brand value of enterprises, tapping into potential unlimited business opportunities. China is the main driving force behind global economic development. In 2007, China's GDP is expected to rank third in the world. China has been the world's largest equipment consumption country for five consecutive years. This exhibition will create a platform for domestic and foreign equipment and related supporting enterprises to expand the market, showcase brand value, and maintain strong competitiveness.

- Entering the most dynamic market in China, opening the golden key to South China business opportunities. South China is China's largest manufacturing base, and Guangdong is China's first manufacturing province. The pace of industrial upgrading in the Pearl River Delta, a world-class manufacturing base, is accelerating, and heavy chemical industries are rising strongly. A large number of machine tools, metal processing, molds, and other mechanical equipment and supporting products have become urgent needs. Guangzhou is the strongest industrial base in Southeast Asia and the primary battleground for domestic and foreign manufacturers to open up the South China market. Guangzhou's automobile, shipbuilding, petrochemicals, mechanical equipment, steel, and other heavy chemical industries are developing rapidly. The output value of the mechanical equipment industry is expected to reach 550 billion yuan by 2010, with huge market potential!

- Total mobilization of advantageous resources, multi-dimensional promotional efforts to enhance your investment return on participation. The exhibition will use integrated marketing methods to actively coordinate social resources (producers, traders, dealers, media, experts, social celebrities, industry organizations, government agencies, overseas partners) and the resources of the organizing party (human resources, financial resources, and years of professional organization and planning experience). With a three-dimensional promotional strategy, it will focus on attracting merchants, buyers, and media attention, enhancing the influence of the exhibition and its participation effects.

- High-quality audiences gather, close contact with quality buyers. The organizing committee will promote the exhibition through various channels such as magazines, newspapers, the internet, television, mass SMS, direct mail advertising, emails, exhibition updates, and telephone communications. It will invite professionals and buyers from industries such as machinery, automobiles, steel, shipbuilding, military industry, metal processing, home appliances, electronics, IT, metallurgy, petrochemicals, toy manufacturing, etc., to visit and purchase. It is estimated that the number of visitors from all over the world will exceed 40,000.

- Major brands participating in previous editions include General Electric, Siemens, ABB, Hitachi, Mitsubishi, Emerson, Rockwell, Toshiba, Swiss Mikron, Itochu, EMAG, Hardinge, Haas, Charmilles, Sodick, RSF, Dazhan Laser, Zhen De, Zhen Jian, Yifu, NOK, GMG, SEW, OTC, THK, Sumitomo, Esaeca, Datong, Qiqihar No.2 Machine Tool Plant, Kia, Hongli, Guangshu, Guangji, Novex, Yellow River Precision Machinery, Phoenix Contact, Ingersoll Rand, Baoyuan, Bolie, Lifeng, Dow, Yi Li, Wafang Heavy Machinery, Mingpan, Minjia... Over 2,000 companies in total.

- Exhibit Categories:

◆ Machine tools and metalworking equipment:

Machining centers, milling machines, boring machines, broaching machines, pullers, shapers, electric spark machining machines, wire cutting machines, flame cutting equipment, coordinate grinding machines, various types of grinders, CNC lathes, engraving machines, mold closing machines, drilling machines, sawing machines, shearing machines, spinning machines, bending machines, CNC machine tools, stamping machine tools, forging machine tools, hydraulic presses, pressure machines, vacuum forming machines, rapid prototyping systems, etc.

◆ Processing and auxiliary device equipment:

Mold CAD/CAM/CAE technology, software, hardware, automatic programming devices, flexible processing systems, numerical control digital display systems, cold/hot working processing equipment, polishing machines, industrial marking machines, positioning and locking devices, various measuring instruments (meters), three-coordinate measuring machines, ultrasonic cleaning machines, motors, bearings, cylinders, mold frames, templates, hot runner elements, punches, springs, grinding wheels, etc.

◆ Molds/Tools:

Stamping, plastic, die-casting, forging, ceramic, drawing, hard alloy, rubber, and other types of molds, mold standard parts, mold materials, mold forming equipment and devices, mold repair equipment and materials, mold parts and components, manual, pneumatic, and electric tools, fixtures, cutters, grinding and polishing equipment, etc.

◆ Mechanical basic components:

Bearings: Bearings and their components, bearing production and processing equipment, related equipment and accessories, linear motion systems, etc.;

Transmission: Gears, chains, reducers, bearings, transmission shafts, couplings, clutches, brakes, belt chain transmission servo motors, etc.;

Hydraulic and pneumatic sealing: Hydraulic components, accessories, systems, technologies, devices, pneumatic three-piece sets, pneumatic components, accessories, pipes, valves, cylinders, etc., various sealing technologies, materials, products, etc.;

Air compressors: Air compressors, dryers, filters, and air compressor accessories;

Fasteners: Fastener production equipment, fasteners, accessories, and connection products, spring equipment, springs, and raw materials used for fastener and spring production.

Exhibition fees:

◆ Domestic exhibitors:

Area A: Standard booth: RMB 9,000 per booth; Net space: RMB 930 per sqm.

Area B: Standard booth: RMB 8,500 per booth; Net space: RMB 880 per sqm.

◆ International exhibitors: USD 2,000 per booth.

Note 1: Standard booth size is 3m x 3m, equipped with white panels, one table, two chairs, two lights, Chinese and English header boards, carpet covering the entire booth floor. Electrical outlets need to be rented separately.

Note 2: Minimum rental for net space inside the hall is 36sqm, non-standard booths do not come with any facilities and must be designed or decorated by the exhibitor themselves or commissioned.

Note 3: Free and value-added services provided by the exhibition include: publishing a 300-word company profile in the catalog, promoting the exhibition and organizing targeted audiences, exhibitor badges, building standard booths (excluding net space), cleaning and security at the exhibition site, providing two copies of the catalog, providing exhibition reports and selected audience lists.

Note 4: Related paid catalogs and outdoor advertisements can be consulted with the organizing committee or refer to the "Exhibitor Manual".
