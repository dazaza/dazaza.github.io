Green Bean Frog Anthology

Green Bean Frog is a detestable frog. He often says a lot of things. The following are some of his sayings.

Quotation One:

When I was young, I once loved a little fish.

But later on, she swam to the sea, and I grew legs, counting those, climbed onto the ocean.

This is the strange fate, you never know what your body hides.

But when they quietly grow out, you will suddenly realize, this is the real you.

All the previous ones were illusions, but they are so sorrowful illusions.

Quotation Two:

The bug crawled out of the cocoon and turned into a beautiful butterfly.

I crawled out of the water and became a strong Green Bean Frog.

You also crawled out of the water, although you look a bit like me,

but with a whole body of bumps. Life is so different, happiness is also so different.

Quotation Three:

Spiders are the pioneers of the Internet era,

flies and small flying insects are early investors, the handsome ones conquer Obama.

Quotation Four:

A toad that doesn't want to eat swan meat is not a good toad.

Quotation Five:

In summer, according to Phoenix News, frogs and cicadas are the two biggest karaoke kings.

Quotation Six:

For thirsty wild grasses, the arrival of a drop of rain is happiness.

And for water grass, even if you give it all the rain in the world,

it won't notice.

Quotation Seven:

For those who have better brains than us, we must overcome them physically.

For those who are physically stronger than us, we must defeat them with wisdom.

For those who are stronger and wiser than us,

we must cling to them shamelessly and bother them to death!!!

Quotation Eight:

I will fight for my ideals!

My ideal is to live a lazy life, sleeping in every day.

Quotation Nine:

People always say that difficult environments can better temper one's will.

But the water in the pot is really too hot.

Willpower hasn't been tempered, and I've been boiled.

Quotation Ten:

The brighter the sunshine, the darker the shadow.

Quotation Eleven:

Video games simplify the complex historical story of the struggle for power in the Three Kingdoms into a truth:

Chopping people is much more enjoyable than being chopped.

Quotation Twelve:

The generation that grew up playing video games is hard to take care of when they get old.

They won't feel that slowing down is a natural phenomenon,

but will keep shouting about needing more memory.

Quotation Thirteen:

Life is the most fun game, but unfortunately, you only have one life.

And there are no cheat codes!!!

Quotation Fourteen:

Don't limit your thoughts to one kind of game.

Although everyone tells you, you must play well your current role.

Otherwise, you won't play the game well. You must understand,

you have the right and ability to exit at any time and start another game.

There are no dead ends in the world, only trapped minds.

Quotation Fifteen:

The biggest disadvantage of being addicted to video games is, when you can't see the screen clearly,

your first reaction is not to get a pair of glasses, but to immediately replace the graphics card.

Quotation Sixteen:

In the showdown with computers, humans are the final winners.

Because they have the ultimate skill - shutting down.

Quotation Seventeen:

The most obvious characteristic of the information age is

you can't find a quiet place anywhere in the world.

Quotation Eighteen:

Although I jump with all my might, I still can't jump into your heart.
