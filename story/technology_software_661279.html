1. Canteen Contracting Method:

We will dispatch professional kitchen staff and managers to your company for professional kitchen management and operation, providing catering services to your employees. Employees enjoy three meals a day, with lunch and dinner offering three, four, or five dishes and one soup... The daily meal fee ranges from 6 to 20 yuan, depending on individual preference. Your company only needs to provide us with existing kitchen utensils, water and electricity, fuel, and accommodation. Other expenses will be fully covered by our company, ensuring standardized operation.

2. Canteen Management Plan:

- Maintain good kitchen hygiene, with comprehensive disinfection of tableware after each meal every day. All kitchen staff hold health certificates and comply with all rules of your company.

- Supervise the quantity, quality, and hygiene of items purchased for the next day by relevant management personnel from your company to ensure smooth operations.

- A dedicated representative will coordinate with your company regarding living improvement matters, constantly listening to employee feedback and making improvements accordingly.

- Vegetables, meats, and oils are monitored and approved by meat processing plants and health departments.

3. Advantages of Canteen Contracting:

- Every Friday, we publish the following week's menu for your employees and operate according to the specified dietary expenses, weekly menu, and portion sizes, focusing on the quality of meals, ensuring they are hygienic, delicious, and diverse.

- Flexible payment methods such as prepaid dining fees or pay-after-eating can be implemented, with settlement terms negotiable.

- During holidays, we can provide additional meals for your employees and offer free cool tea during hot weather.

- We employ chefs from different regions who are skilled in seasonal dish flavoring and preparation, offering a full range of dishes that are highly popular among employees.

4. Profit-making Methods in Contracting:

- To adapt to social development and market competition, we continuously expand our market network information, conduct group procurement of raw materials, and directly procure from major production areas and wholesale bases to obtain low-cost and high-quality goods, thus earning profits from the price difference.
