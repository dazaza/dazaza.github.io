To answer this question, one must first understand the main applications of different types and fineness levels of calcium carbonate powder.

The uses of calcium carbonate powder vary depending on the manufacturing process, but overall, they are very extensive. For example:

• Heavy calcium single flying powder (D95, 200 mesh): Used for producing anhydrous calcium chloride (CaCl2), auxiliary raw material for sodium dichromate production. It is also a primary raw material for glass and cement production. Additionally, it is used in building materials and poultry feed, etc.

• Heavy calcium double flying powder (D99, 325 mesh): Raw material for producing anhydrous calcium chloride and glass, white filler for rubber and paint, as well as building materials, etc.

• Heavy calcium triple flying powder (D99.9, 325 mesh): Used as a filler for plastics, coatings, putty, paints, plywood, and paint.

• Heavy calcium quadruple flying powder (D99.95, 400 mesh): Used as a filler for wire insulation layers, rubber mold-pressed products, and asphalt roofing felt fillers.

• Light calcium powder (above 325 mesh) Usage: Can be used as a filler in the rubber, plastic, papermaking, coatings, and ink industries. Widely used in organic synthesis, metallurgy, glass, and asbestos production. Also can be used as an industrial wastewater neutralizer, antacid for stomach and duodenal ulcer disease, antidote for acidosis, SO2 eliminator in SO2-containing exhaust gases, additive in dairy cow feed, and anti-sticking agent in oil-soaked felt.

• Colloidal calcium powder (above 800 mesh) Usage: Filler for rubber, which makes rubber have bright color, high elongation, high tensile strength, and good wear resistance. Also used as a filler in artificial leather, wires, PVC, coatings, inks, and papermaking industries. It makes the finished product have a certain tensile strength and a smooth appearance. When producing porous rubber, it allows uniform foaming.

• Crystal calcium powder Usage: Used in toothpaste and pharmaceuticals. Also can be used as thermal insulation material and other chemical raw materials.

Red Star Machinery's ultrafine grinder can process calcium carbonate powder between 200 to 3000 mesh, with adjustable finished product fineness. Common applications of calcium carbonate powder fineness are listed below.

1. Up to 200 mesh: Can be used as various feed additives.

4. 400 to 600 mesh: Can be used in toothpaste paste, soap.

5. 800 mesh: Used in rubber, plastic, cables, PVC.

6. 1250 mesh: PVC, PE, paint, coatings and products, paper bottom coating, paper surface coating. High purity, high whiteness, non-toxic, odorless, low fine oil content, low hardness. Can also be used as raw material for tooth powder, toothpaste, and other cosmetics.
