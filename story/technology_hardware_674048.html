Titleist Golfing Equipment Aimed at Lower Handicap Golfers

Titleist has a great reputation in the world of golf, particularly targeting lower handicap golfers. Titleist golf clubs combined with Titleist golf balls are aimed at the top level of golfers, including professionals and single-figure handicappers, reflecting their mission to serve the needs of both serious and recreational golfers by offering additional items and services that provide a competitive edge globally.

The Titleist clubs are of very high quality and are used by players like Adam Scott, Jordan Spieth, Steve Stricker, Geoff Ogilvy, and Rickie Fowler on the Tour. Titleist irons are extremely popular, especially the AP1 Irons Set and the AP2 Irons Set. Titleist putters are offered under the 'Scotty Cameron' brand and are widely used on the Tour by many golfers, including Padraig Harrington before he switched to the Nike Method Putter.

Titleist golf balls are number 1 on the Tour as well as in golf stores thanks to the incredible Titleist Pro V1 Golf Ball and the equally amazing Titleist Pro V1X Golf Ball. These golf balls are often used by winning golfers on both the PGA and European Tours and have achieved numerous major victories. Titleist golf balls are used by golfers such as Lee Westwood, Ian Poulter, Sergio Garcia, and Matteo Manassero, among many others.

If you're a Titleist golfer, there's a great range of accessories to match your golf clubs and balls—Titleist also produces golf bags, golf gloves, golf towels, and umbrellas, so you can look the part on the course.

Building on the success of the amazing Titleist 909 series, 2011 saw the launch of the Titleist 910 clubs. With their technologically advanced head designs, the 910 series delivers outstanding overall performance.

Titleist is part of the Acushnet Company, which also includes several other golf brands such as FootJoy and Pinnacle.
