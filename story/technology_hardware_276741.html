Introduction  

Skywash car washing services have sparked a wave of discussion in the UK and the US. After this service was introduced in Leicester, UK in 2008, it gained significant attention, but local councils received numerous complaints from residents. Although the police monitored the situation, they admitted there was no law to regulate it. In Idaho, USA, as early as 2002, three women living together offered skywash services on the roadside due to financial difficulties, surprising the quiet town and its council.

In Australia, due to severe drought, Opal Clean came up with the idea of topless car washes by naked beauties, which became extremely popular. In these topless car washes, how much clothing the beauties wear is decided by the customers.

Brisbane, the capital of Queensland, known as the "Sunshine Province" in eastern Australia, issued water restrictions that included residential car washing. As a result, the local car wash industry boomed. Armstrong, the owner of a local strip club, quickly seized the opportunity and launched a car wash service featuring semi-nude beauties.

These beauties will wash cars for customers; a topless wash costs 55 Australian dollars, while a fully nude wash costs 100 Australian dollars. Armstrong stated that his car wash service is legitimate. He said, "I just want to earn money honestly, that's all."

The police also noted that they had not received any complaints from the public. The car wash service was not conducted in public places, and no related cases had occurred.

Skywash Car Washing in Singapore  

In early October 2009, Singapore’s automobile website MotoringExchange organized an eye-catching car washing event to attract attention. They invited 20 members and their partners to attend at an abandoned warehouse in the eastern part of the country. Four sexy semi-naked models washed cars while stripping, eventually completing the task half-naked. However, the members could only watch and not participate. Afterward, the organizers published photos of the "topless car wash" online for registered members to view. As a result, one model was expelled from school after the school discovered the online photos, and another model revealed she was only 16 years old.
