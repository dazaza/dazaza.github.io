GoDaddy Promotion Continues: COM Domain Registration Discounts Unaffected by Cost Increase

by xwkj0983 on 2012-02-23 14:56:45

VeriSign once again increased the registration fee for .COM and .NET domain names at the beginning of the year. The market expects that the price of domain name registration from most domain name service providers will be significantly affected and increase substantially. However, GoDaddy, the largest domain name service provider, continues to push forward with its promotional offers for .COM domain names, and has not yet been impacted by the increase in domain name registration fees.

In January this year, VeriSign made some adjustments to the wholesale prices of .COM and .NET domain names. The registration fee for .COM domain names was raised from $7.34 to $7.85, a 6.9% increase. The registration fees from VeriSign do not include the annual ICANN tax of $0.18. After adding the tax, the wholesale price of a .COM domain name will rise to $8.03. Following the price increase of .COM domain names, the industry speculated that large domain name service providers would also raise the registration fees for .COM domain names.

As the world's largest domain name and hosting service provider, GoDaddy did not raise prices as the industry had guessed in response to the price increase of .COM domain names. Instead, it continued to promote its discount campaign for .COM domain names. As of now, the registration price for .COM domain names from GoDaddy has not been affected by the increase in wholesale prices. Users can still enjoy the special promotional price of $5.99 for registering a .COM domain through the discount website. This is very encouraging news for many domestic users who intend to register a .COM domain name.

Comparing the wholesale price of .COM domain names provided by VeriSign, GoDaddy's promotional price for .COM domain names is highly attractive in the market. Despite the increase in wholesale prices, GoDaddy continues to steadily advance its promotional discount policies for domain name registration. Such rare discount opportunities are sure to attract a wide range of users, and GoDaddy will likely trigger a wave of promotions for .COM domain names.

With the promotion of discounted .COM domain names, other related products from GoDaddy have also gained attention. The 30% discount on annual payment plans for shared virtual hosting continues. Purchasing their 4GH annual payment plan through the discount website allows users to enjoy an additional 30% discount on top of the original price. Additionally, the special offer from GoDaddy's virtual hosting service provider, where the Virtual Data Center requires only $1 for the first month, has attracted considerable attention. GoDaddy has captured the ever-changing market demand and places great importance on expanding and deepening cloud hosting services. The industry speculates that cloud products may become the focus of GoDaddy's future expansion and research and development.

From the perspective of the price fluctuations of .COM domain names, various factors could lead to changes in domain name registration prices. Even though domain name service providers continue to offer discounts despite the rise in registration costs, these offers are likely time-limited. It is recommended that users in the industry who have a need for .COM domain names take action early to register their domain names and seize the best opportunity for domain name discount promotions.
