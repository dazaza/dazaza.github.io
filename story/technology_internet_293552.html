At the beginning of the universe, chaos was broken and established, giving birth to the Three Thousand Great Dao. There are weak waters in Three Thousand Rivers, but one only needs a single scoop to drink. Yi Fengzhi obtained the ancient inheritance of "name" and the nine-layered Great Dream technique, so which cultivation path will he choose? Under the secret guidance of the inheritors, Yao Hua becomes a disciple of the great cultivation sect, the Heaven-Splitting Sword Sect. Due to cultivation reasons, she conceals her identity as an elder among the newly entered lower disciples and begins her legend with the Great Dream technique...
