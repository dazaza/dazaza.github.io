Main Text Chapter 245: Poison King

Novel: Super Mind | Author: Xingchen Yu | Category: Urban Romance

Qin Xiang and the other two women chatted with He Wenyen and the other three women until twelve o'clock before returning to their rooms to rest. They were clearly very much looking forward to their four future partners. At three o'clock, Qin Hua promptly opened his eyes. A flash of cold light passed through his eyes, then he got dressed and shoes on, walked out of the room, activated his domain, and his power and speed were greatly enhanced. His body also became more agile. In an instant, he had already appeared at the door without disturbing He Wenyen and the other four bodyguards.

After stepping outside, feeling the refreshing cool breeze outside, he stretched lazily. At this point, except for the night duty security guards who were mostly resting, someone as stealthy as Qin Hua wouldn't be foolish enough to get caught by a few security guards. Quietly, he made his way to Jiang Fu's villa. The radar was already monitoring him around the clock, but there had been no abnormalities so far. Swiftly, he jumped into his house. Carefully taking a few steps inside, after confirming there was no danger, he quietly approached the door of Jiang Fu's room.

Opening the door, Jiang Fu was lying face down on the bed sleeping soundly, obviously not noticing the intruder. Meanwhile, Qin Hua was walking around the room casually without touching any items. After deliberately leaving some traces behind, he headed towards Jiang Fu's study. Inside were various jade pieces and books, all rather simple items. He continued to deliberately leave traces before exiting the room. Although Jiang Fu increased his defenses after last night's unpleasant incident, it was still useless against Qin Hua.

After skillfully exiting Jiang Fu's room and returning to Qin Xiang's home, he saw He Wenyen sitting on the sofa waiting for him. Helplessly scratching his head, he said, "Still awake this late?"

"After I arrived, I have taken full defensive measures in the room. So, when that ancient palm covered with dark golden light roared again, I noticed someone left within my sight," He Wenyen calmly replied.

At this moment, Qin Hua looked around and helplessly nodded, saying, "No problem, just went out for a walk."

"Taking a walk at three in the morning, you sure are interesting." He Wenyen couldn't help but tease him, then continued, "I am now Miss Qin Xiang's personal bodyguard, and I do not want anyone's actions to threaten Miss Qin Xiang's safety, including yours."

"Dedicated!" Qin Hua praised her, saying, "Don't worry, what I do will not affect her safety."

He Wenyen merely nodded, then stood up and returned to her room. Qin Hua scratched his head and went back to sleep. The next morning, when Jiang Fu woke up, he was already sitting on the bed with a sneer on his face.

Jiang Fu is a shrewd person. After waking up and getting dressed, he immediately noticed that someone had been in his room! And they came in the middle of the night, which scared him cold sweat. If someone wanted to harm him, it would be effortless! Hurriedly, he rushed out of the room and called his subordinates over, angrily saying, "There was a thief at our house last night, what were you guys doing?"

The subordinates were shocked upon hearing this - how could there be a thief? We were all diligently on duty! Seeing their expressions, Jiang Fu scolded them a few more times. When he returned to his own room, he still noticed signs of being tampered with, making him even angrier. However, what annoyed Qin Hua was that this guy didn't seem concerned about the key's safety at all.

"Damn," Qin Hua cursed, watching as Jiang Fu ordered his subordinates to investigate further before leaving by car. "This old man must not be here, could he be at his wife's place? I'll check tonight."

After coming downstairs, breakfast was already prepared, and He Wenyen and the other three were eating together. Qin Xiang seemed to be chatting well with them, sitting across from Sister Long and yawning.

"What's wrong? Didn't sleep well?" Sister Long smiled and asked.

"Yeah," Qin Hua nodded, drinking a glass of milk and said, "Been busy lately."

"I'm curious, what have you been busy with all day?" Qin Xiang leaned closer curiously asking, "I haven't seen you go to class, could you be up to something unknown?"

"Nonsense," Qin Hua rolled his eyes. Hearing this, He Wenyen glanced at him, her eyes filled with a bit of caution, apparently believing that Qin Hua going out at night must have been up to no good, which could easily endanger Qin Xiang's safety. Once enemies find out, they might really use Qin Xiang as a target for revenge.

As for vigilance, Qin Hua didn't pay much attention. After finishing breakfast, he left. This time, instead of finding trouble unnecessarily, he went straight to Hong Kong University. Suddenly remembering that he was still an exchange student, it would be embarrassing if he didn't show up often. In fact, when this guy showed up, everyone remembered that there was actually a Qin Hua in their group!

"You're quite capable, aren't you? You've been missing for two days out of three since you came," Chu Han teased with a smile, "I'm curious, you were busy in Yanjing, why are you still so busy here?"

Qin Hua stretched lazily and said, "Too much luck, everyone wants me to do things."

After speaking, he fell asleep on the table. The people around him shook their heads helplessly. Is Qin Hua really here as an exchange student? It seems more like he's on vacation!

However, Zheng Qiyuan wasn't having a good time right now.

Originally, he thought that Qin Hua was just scaring him, but the next morning, after getting up and eating breakfast and preparing to go to work, he suddenly felt numbness and pain throughout his body. Instantly, he remembered the medicine he had left yesterday, hurriedly took a pill from his pocket, and only then did he feel better.

Sitting helplessly on the chair, Zheng Qiyuan hated Jiang Fu deeply! That damn bastard, sending people to harm him like this! Thinking about the poison in his body, Zheng Qiyuan suddenly remembered a certain big shot, giving him hope. He quickly called over the driver and requested to go to the coastal neighborhood in the east.

Poison!

Hong Kong has a Poison King! Ordinary people don't know, perhaps even the police don't know, but Zheng Qiyuan was clear. Because the idea of devouring the assets of three wealthy tycoons was proposed by the Poison King! Although the four of them wanted to get rid of the Poison King, after all, the person who came up with the plan was supposed to take away thirty percent of the profits! For these people, it was no small amount! But now that he was severely poisoned, he had no choice but to seek the Poison King's help. After all, this person known as one of the top ten killers in the world had deep expertise in poisons.

After parking at the entrance of the coastal neighborhood,

Zheng Qiyuan got out of the car and cautiously walked towards a courtyard nestled by the mountains and sea. It was just an ordinary residence, but its location was excellent. Upon reaching the gate, Zheng Qiyuan carefully knocked on the large wooden door. Only after hearing a voice from inside did he dare to push open the door. Each step he took was extremely cautious, fearing that he might come into contact with something he shouldn't. This was the lair of the Poison King, and probably every corner was filled with deadly toxins!

Even in one corner of the yard, he could see some disgusting venomous creatures: spiders, snakes, dart frogs, lizards, etc., all species capable of killing humans instantly! Even in the large aquarium beside him, there were box jellyfish from Australia and countless other species he had never seen before!

Although he hadn't seen them, during his previous visit, he vaguely heard that the poison of those unknown species was stronger than any poison in the world!

Walking to the front hall, he saw Pang Yu sitting nearby. Approaching with a forced smile, he said, "Mr. Pang, may I inquire where your esteemed teacher is?"

"In the room, wait," Pang Yu raised his eyelids slightly and said, then pointed to the chair beside him.

This Zheng Qiyuan carefully approached, hesitated for a moment before sitting down. Feeling no abnormalities, he secretly sighed in relief. Looking at Pang Yu, his skin seemed to have turned a dark red, and his lips had traces of purple. Even on his neck, he could see thin purple veins spreading, which was quite terrifying.

"One does not come to the Three Treasures Hall without a reason, speak, what is it?" At this moment, a very sinister voice came from the side. Hearing this, Zheng Qiyuan hurriedly stood up and turned around.
