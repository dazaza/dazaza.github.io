The most depressing online shopping experience

by anonymous on 2011-04-01 13:15:34

Recently, I have fallen in love with online shopping. However, before my interest reaches its peak, I have to put a full stop to it.

On March 2nd, I ordered three pieces of clothing online together with a colleague. The customer service told me that the goods would be shipped within 24 hours. I waited and waited, but after one and a half 24-hour cycles, there was still no movement from Guangzhou Vipshop Information Technology Co., Ltd. I asked the customer service again, who told me that the company had a large number of orders recently, please be patient. I waited again, urging them repeatedly, day by day. Finally, on the evening of the 7th, I saw that the goods had been shipped and had already passed through the airport scanner. Seeing the shipping information made me very excited, I was finally going to receive my wedding dress. Waiting and looking forward, I thought I would receive it on the 8th. On the 8th, my dream of receiving the goods was shattered again. I thought, well, I'll wait until the 9th then, maybe the transportation route is too long. By the end of the 10th, my goods still hadn't arrived. I was depressed, mentally tormented, it was simply torture! Nowadays, with such advanced transportation, even regular mail from Hangzhou to Jinzhou should have arrived, and mine was express delivery! Are airplanes really that slow? My online shopping was just getting started, but it was cut short in this express delivery process, it's really a pity. Well, if it's a pity, so be it. I can't bear the waiting, I don't have the patience. I still don't know whether what I'm waiting for after such a long torment will satisfy me or not. I hope my waiting hasn't been in vain, I hope... otherwise, I will completely lose faith in online shopping.
