Three Transformation Prospects for Taobao Affiliate Websites in 2011 by Peking University HSBC Business School

by zpeixun916 on 2012-02-23 11:27:09

In fact, the shopping assistant plugin is not dead now. If you go to the official website of 360 Browser, you can still find Youdao Shopping Assistant. Youdao Shopping Assistant seems to be more intense than the previous shopping assistant. It not only allows netizens to shop on Taobao and get cashback, but also allows netizens to shop on Dangdang and JD.com and get cashback. It is almost the ultimate tool for CPS. As long as the shopping assistant exists, the word CPS may become history. Of course, that is the most optimistic forecast, and we will never believe in such a result.

Later, after referring to other industry Taobao guest websites, we decided to start a local information website. While providing local news, it can also promote products at the same time. Of course, everyone must have patience and have long-term plans when starting! Our website has gradually shown some experience and foundation from the beginning to now. I believe that in the near future, it will definitely achieve rapid profit growth.

Before we know it, 2011 has already passed halfway. In the past six months, the life of Taobao guests is well known. The majority of Taobao guests are still silently working hard until the 360 Shopping Assistant suddenly appeared, and some Taobao guests began to stand up; although this is just a browser plugin, even not the default browser plugin, 360's promotion is really powerful. With just a few promotions, the word Taobao guest may become history. Therefore, Taobao guests rushed to advertisements, and soft articles were issued simultaneously, which made the momentum of the shopping assistant fade away.

Then, facing competition from peers, the bad temper of search engines, and the strong promotion of the 360 shopping plugin, it is necessary to think about where our future lies as Taobao guests. Do we need to transform?

If you can feel that you can achieve these, then transforming into an industry website is your inevitable choice.

Regarding the current predicament of Taobao guests, we humble ones only propose a few small suggestions. If you have any better opinions and suggestions, we welcome you to communicate with us! Please note: This original article is reprinted from Cool Dog Design (www.colddogs.cn).

2 In this industry, you have connections, or to put it bluntly, you can understand most of the companies in this industry.

For online stores, the key point is to have a source of goods. For the characteristics of the Taobao guest group, we suggest that everyone find a good supplier before transforming the website! If you plan to sell local resources, please make sure to contact and convince local merchants to provide you with goods. This is very important.

Second: Transform into an industry website. This sounds easy, but it is quite difficult to implement! To complete this transformation, you need to accomplish the following material foundations:

1 You have sufficient understanding and knowledge of the industry of the products you currently sell, and you can analyze the competition level of search engines, then decide accordingly.

First, transform into an information-based website, with advertising income as the basic starting point, and then promote Taobao single items based on the original content! For example, our previous 057366.com was specifically for Taobao guests. From 2008 to now, although we didn't make a lot of money in just two short years, we did make a little money! But since 360 launched the shopping plugin, in less than two months, profits have plummeted! It can be said that profits are trending towards zero-profit stage!

3 Have excellent insight into the future of this industry and understand the needs of future users!

Third: Transform into an online store. In 2011, the online store market continued to develop strongly. Whether it is a Taobao store or an independent online store, there are good development opportunities. For example, one of our sites, 0573rencai.com, used to develop a local talent website, but later found that the development opportunities for talent websites were very slim. In February this year, we transformed into an online store format, plus through the products sold by the original Taobao guest, the overall development trend is pretty good.

If your answer is yes, then I will talk about some transformation prospects for Taobao guests here!

Now, the life of Taobao guests is still not as good as before. Besides the sudden appearance of the shopping assistant, there are also the bad temper of search engines and the pressure of peer competition, all of which are reasons for the difficulties faced by Taobao guests.
