Synopsis:

Chev Chelios (Jason Statham), who fell from a helicopter, did not die. When he wakes up, he finds himself in a black-market clinic where a few incompetent Hong Kong doctors are performing a heart transplant surgery on him. During the procedure, Johnny Woon, a petty thug, keeps interrupting. After the surgery, Chev has an artificial heart made by Avicor and a belt battery. The doctor tells Chev that to keep his heart functioning, he must continuously charge his body. Chev believes Johnny Woon stole his heart and thus goes on a frenzied chase. In a sex club, he accidentally rescues a prostitute named Ria (Bai Ling).

In a nightclub, he encounters his girlfriend Eve (Amy Smart) dancing on a pole. The gunfight at the nightclub draws the police, but the taser only serves to charge Chev. At the racetrack, Chev, in need of friction to charge himself, ends up making a fool of himself in front of everyone. It is only when Tang Kim, the leader of the Hong Kong Triads appears, that Chev truly understands the real reason behind the theft of his heart...
