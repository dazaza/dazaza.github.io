Brand eco-bag show

by fashioninchina on 2008-05-01 20:56:26

In the era of underdeveloped economy, environmental protection was not in people's concept. Although people were also doing frugal things, it was not for the purpose of environmental protection. However, accompanying economic development are climate anomalies, tsunamis, floods, hurricanes and other natural hazards phenomena. Everyone is puzzled, what's wrong with the earth we live on? After reflection, protecting and saving the environment has become an urgent theme. Today, environmental issues have crossed national and industry boundaries, becoming a common hot topic among the political, business, academic, media circles. With the prevalence of the global environmental movement, the concept of environmental protection has gradually taken root in people's hearts.

The trend-setting fashion world has also caught the wind of environmental protection, closely linking fashion with environmental protection. For a time, the theme of "environmental protection can also be fashionable" spread through streets and alleys. Last year, well-known designer Marc Jacobs launched a trendy environmental-friendly handbag. Its simple shape and fresh white color, paired with the black "MARC JACOBS" and "NEW YORK SINCE 1984", truly showed the healthy and fashionable life brought by environmental protection. In his secondary brand Marc by Marc Jacobs, he designed a unique environmental-friendly handbag. The beige canvas with texture, printed with skyscrapers of varying heights, interwoven with light and shadow, brings the bustling urban night scene to life on the handbag, making it a collectible item for many fashion enthusiasts. This season, DKNY has launched an environmental-friendly handbag. On the beige canvas bag, the words "USE ME" and "REUSE ME" are printed, directly expressing the brand's environmental philosophy. With the infiltration of fashionable environmental concepts, young men and women who follow trends are all using environmental-friendly handbags without exception.

In order to make eco-bags more beautiful and fashionable, manufacturers and retailers have also made efforts on the unremarkable recyclable non-woven fabric or canvas. The size and style designs are constantly renewed, and more novelty is displayed in the patterns of the eco-bags. Many non-woven bags use different colors to show their characteristics. In addition to color matching aesthetics, cartoon or artistic letters are used in image selection to showcase. Disney's cute images, such as Mickey Mouse or Winnie the Pooh, have become common protagonists on non-woven fabrics. Other abstract patterns display unique personalities.

Even if you don't have money to own a bag designed by the famous MARC JACOBS. With so many concrete and abstract interpretations, ordinary eco-bags will not be inferior to leather bags or cloth bags. Now, walking down the street with a bag, you can also become very fashionable. Bring the awareness of environmental protection to the crowd, and you will definitely be the focus of everyone's admiration.
