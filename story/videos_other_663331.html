Before the reporter went to press, he learned from the hospital that the situation of the injured child was basically stable. "If it were a little later, it would be dangerous," the medical staff sighed: "This child has met a savior." It is reported that the management department of Suqian City has already initiated an initiative in the bus system to learn from bus driver Wang Meng. After pulling the woman out from under the car, out of professional habit, Wang Meng took another look at the scene and found a child lying face down more than ten meters away at the traffic lights. Terrible! Another child was injured. He hurried over, picked up the child, who was a boy about one year old, with blood all over his face, eyes tightly closed, hands stretched out flatly, and at first glance seemed to have no breath. Wang Meng was suddenly at a loss and instinctively carried the boy to the Suqian Central Hospital over a kilometer away. While running, he blew air into the child's mouth with his own mouth. "This road of more than a thousand meters is really long!" Wang Meng recalled, after running a few hundred meters, a black sedan stopped. Wang Meng immediately pulled open the door and got in. "Go, hurry up and save the person!" Wang Meng almost roared at the driver. "Until we get to the hospital, we can't say the child can't be saved!" Wang Meng told the reporter. When they arrived at the gate of the central hospital, Wang Meng rushed inside with the child and shouted, "Find a doctor!" But the more urgent the situation became, the more chaotic it got, and he temporarily couldn't find the emergency room. At this moment, an ambulance drove by, without hesitation, Wang Meng climbed aboard with the child and had the ambulance go straight to the emergency room entrance. The medical staff also came forward, Wang Meng carefully placed the child on the emergency bed, and a miracle happened: the boy's mouth moved, and he let out a cry. "There's still hope," the medical staff immediately began rescue efforts. At this point, Wang Meng finally let out a long sigh of relief and walked out of the emergency room door. He could no longer take a step and sat down on the ground, dry heaving violently. The bus driver and passengers together lifted the accident vehicle to rescue the injured. Bus driver Wang Meng was wiping the dashboard. Gao Feng took the photo. On February 8th, he and Shi Dong'en were driving along the route. At 10:45 AM, the bus departed from Suqian East Station. After leaving the east station and turning left, not far ahead was a traffic light. Shi Dong'en drove the car into the right-turn lane, preparing to turn onto Wutaishan Road. At this time, a traffic accident occurred at the traffic lights. A blue Guangzhou Honda CRV collided with a small minivan, causing the minivan to flip over, with a woman trapped underneath. "This road of more than a thousand meters is really long!" During the journey, seeing a traffic accident ahead at the intersection, a male child was thrown out of the car and fell more than ten meters away. Suqian bus No.309 driver Shi Dong'en quickly stopped the car and called on the passengers to get out of the car and help rescue people. Wang Meng carried the injured child and ran like crazy toward the hospital a kilometer away, racing against death... In the end, the child who was almost out of breath was saved. The bus driver carried the baby thrown out of the car and ran madly to the hospital. At this time, a power company's vehicle also arrived outside the emergency room. Inside the car, the driver of the minivan in the car accident was holding an injured woman. Wang Meng opened the car door and helped the injured woman out. "Don't worry, the child is fine," Wang Meng said softly to the woman. The woman unconsciously replied with a "Hmm."
