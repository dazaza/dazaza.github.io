Dear, please don't let go, I won't leave...

The harm you've done to me has already permeated every pore. I am still silently saying in my words that there is love between us.

Dear, can you tell me, which kind of patient woman is the most beautiful? I will try my best to learn.

You said you want to fly, I will be by your side. On that day, when you were hurt, I said our lips and teeth depend on each other, we won't part, our love can take us back to the blue sky and white clouds.

Do you remember? We lived frugally day after day, and I never complained. Because of you, I am the happiest person in the world. As long as you understand and care for me, it's worth suffering for love.

I am just an ordinary woman. In the world of feelings, I won't easily fall in love with someone. Once I fall in love, it's for a lifetime.

For those few days, you used words as weapons, piercing straight to my heart. I cried, really. The world is so big, it was so hard for me to find you. Please don't make me leave in this way, okay? I don't want the big house, big car, big garden... that you promised to give me in the future. I just want to stay by your side and quietly take care of you.

Dear, I only want a simple love. Quietly protect the home of our love. All these years, you forgot my birthdays, Valentine's Day... I don't need to celebrate those holidays because every day with you is a holiday, every day is precious. I am just a rule-abiding woman. I don't know romance, fashion, or novelty... I only know that in this love, with your affirmation, I am willing to do everything.

After walking many roads and meeting many people, I know that some love can only be forgotten by each other; some love can only be blessed for each other; some love can last until the end. I hope our love belongs to the third kind, where I become your most beautiful bride and stay with you until the end.

I stick to simplicity and keep away from materialism, just not to make you too tired. Someone said, use a sunny mood to grasp life, and heaven will give you everything you want. If this is true, I just want heaven to give me you. A you who knows me, understands me, and loves me. And I will be more positive, so you will cherish me and stop hurting me.

If hurting me makes you happy, please continue. I will strive to be a woman worthy of respect. Stick to patience, be understanding and considerate... I will learn one by one, just please don't make me leave, okay? I only hope you can minimize the harm, okay? Can you occasionally smile at me, okay?

I will feel upset, but I won't cry in front of you. I know, you love me, that's why you're trying your best to make me leave, to find a better place to settle down, right? I know, you love me, otherwise, how could you have once risked your life to protect me when I was in danger? In the hospital, you held my hand and said we wouldn't part.

I will choose my own way. Choose to protect this love with meticulous care forever. I will let the pain evaporate slowly.

You said when things stabilize, you will give me a home. I said, wherever it is, as long as you are there, it's fine. No matter how big the world is, it has nothing to do with me. In my world, having you means having the whole world. You kissed the tears on my cheeks and said you wouldn't betray me. In your arms, I feel a sense of peace. In your arms, I feel how deep your love is.

Dear, you promise me eternal love, and I will follow you to the ends of the earth. This sentence is permanently valid. Your委屈, your pain, your stubbornness, your persistence... I will accept them all. I will seal our love with a kiss, express love with words, and show my heart with actions.

Dear, the road ahead is still long, please walk with me to the end of happiness, okay? The song "Baby Heart Hurts" has been on repeat all day, my tears have fallen all over the floor, and the words have come to an end here. My heart, if only you could understand, it would be wonderful.

It's late at night, and I'm still muttering alone. Dear, please don't let go, I won't leave...
