by baiyasin2s on 2012-02-27 20:24:49

The importance of a man's waist and feet can be seen from the saying "men look at watches, women look at bags". Watching the waist mainly refers to observing a man's belt and its accessories, while watching the feet mainly refers to observing the shoes. Therefore, a belt and shoes, these essentials for men, are sufficient to reveal a man's taste and status. Today, Balenciaga will teach you how to interpret men from belts and shoes, and how business men can choose belts to enhance their image.

Modern urban life seems to put more and more pressure on men. From the belts and shoes around men's waists, one can feel their busyness. They always hang or clip mobile phones, pagers, key pouches, and even lighters, which makes one think of the bows, swords, sharpening stones, and abacuses that ancients carried on their waistbands, symbolizing high or low status. Although today's men's belts and shoes no longer carry the old meanings, their importance is still irreplaceable by other clothing accessories. Often, men who behave steadily and gracefully will deliberately decorate this detail around their waists.

A senior consultant of Balenciaga online shopping said that the quality, pattern, and buckle of belts and shoes determine their value and reflect a man's taste.

Keywords: Belt and shoe quality, pattern, trend

Common materials for belts and shoes include pigskin, cowhide, sheepskin, alligator skin, and casual canvas belts. Due to different processing and tanning processes, various materials present diverse styles. Pigskin and sheepskin become softer after being peeled and layered; cowhide has a hard and stiff feel; alligator skin is a higher-end choice. Moreover, the embossed patterns and textures on belts make them more charming and distinctive. The edges of sandblasted belts wrapped with hemp rope show the beauty of delicacy amidst ruggedness and boldness; cracked lacquer crocodile-patterned belts give men an elegant yet unforgettable charm; a casual outfit with a canvas belt tied around the waist is like a relaxed note jumping in the fast-paced city life.

The color of belts and shoes is closely related to the overall coordination of clothing. In formal occasions, when wearing a well-tailored suit, the color of the belt should match the shoes, generally black and dark brown. The leather must be good, usually made from calf leather, reflecting wealth and efficiency. When paired with casual wear, the choice of belts is relatively free. The color can be any color on your clothing, and the material choice is also relaxed. You can choose coarse-textured ones such as alligator skin, fish skin, snake skin, and ostrich skin, showing that leisure can also be high-quality.

The trend of belts and shoes changes largely due to the buckles. Gold buckles are often associated with noble and elegant words; copper buckles let people experience the masculinity and strength of men. Also, the shape and size of the buckle express the charm of men. Wide "Hui" shaped belt buckles fully reveal the firmness of men; elliptical buckles display the maturity of men; square represents taste. And these buckle brands also become symbols of male identity. Balenciaga belts and shoes, with exquisite craftsmanship, always showcase the luxurious quality of men in a low-key manner.

Keyword: How to choose

Business men should pay attention to several details when choosing belts and shoes to reflect a mature demeanor. First, the decorative nature of the belt is the first thing, so it cannot carry too many items, after all, it is not a utility belt - modern soldiers don't use utility belts either. Simplicity and efficiency are the characteristics of men. Secondly, the length of the belt should not be ignored. After tying it up, the end of the belt should be between the first and second belt loops. Any unsuitability will only make you quickly "divorce" your belt. Thirdly, the width of the belt should be kept at 3 centimeters. Too narrow will lose the masculine vigor; too wide is only suitable for casual, cowboy-style outfits.

In today's society, whether in daily leisure or business occasions, the meaning of belts and shoes far exceeds their functional significance. Though they are clothing, they are indispensable. The charm of men who are free, unrestrained, and full of vitality is irresistible, and they naturally turn their eyes to those accessories that highlight their bold personalities. Coarse denim belts and leather woven waistbands both demonstrate extraordinary masculinity.
