The most effective method for breast enlargement: new mothers urgently need life-saving blood; a friend's Weibo post leads to extensive forwarding.

by nim272dymc on 2012-02-10 00:49:01

■ Netizens help spread the Weibo post, showing gratitude to the owner of the post. "Friends are all trying to find ways, some through connections, others contacting blood banks. I feel that Weibo spreads information quickly, so I hurried online to post a Weibo appeal, which then caught the attention of many netizens." "Jingcheng XiaocuiV" told reporters that her appeal Weibo has been forwarded more than 6,000 times, and she and Mr. Wang, the person involved, have also received many calls from netizens, with multiple netizens expressing their willingness to donate blood.

■ Friends already left Beijing but heard the news via Weibo for help. Weibo recommendations | Today's Weibo hot topics (Editor: SN017).

■ Pregnant woman suddenly felt unwell after a miscarriage and had massive bleeding. Yesterday afternoon, the main person involved, Mr. Wang, told reporters that his wife had been transferred to Peking University Third Hospital, underwent surgery, and was admitted to the ICU for observation. Her condition has basically stabilized. In one day, he and his friends received numerous calls and comforting messages from kind-hearted people. They were very grateful. Although the hospital solved the blood needs, the goodwill of many kind-hearted people still made him feel warm. "On this winter-to-spring day, although the weather is still cold, our hearts are full of warmth. Thank you all." This is the gratitude expressed by Mr. Wang and his friends.

"At around 3 in the morning on the second day of the New Year, my wife suddenly had stomach pain, so we rushed to the hospital," Mr. Wang, the husband of the pregnant woman, told reporters. His wife was carrying twins and was already six months pregnant. Before the Spring Festival, there were signs of a possible miscarriage, and she was hospitalized for three days at Haidian Maternal and Child Hospital. On New Year's Eve, the hospital checked that the pregnant woman's condition was stable, so they discharged her. "The first two days at home were fine, but unexpectedly, an incident occurred on the second day. The situation was urgent when we arrived at the hospital. Not only did we lose the babies, but my wife also had massive bleeding. At that time, my wife was having difficulty breathing."

Netizen "Jingcheng XiaocuiV" is a friend of Mr. Wang and was already back in her hometown celebrating the New Year when she received Mr. Wang's call. "We are all working in Beijing, and we don't know much about his wife's specific situation. We only knew that his wife was carrying twins and seemed to have been hospitalized before the Spring Festival. When we received the call, we were also very worried." "Jingcheng XiaocuiV" said that since none of them were in Beijing, even if they wanted to donate blood, it would be difficult to do so, especially given the urgency of the situation.

"Massive bleeding, serious, life-threatening, praying for this sister, wishing you peace and your parents' hearts can rest. Unfortunately, I am not this blood type! If you see this, please spread it quickly!" Netizen "Wu Qing" sent blessings while forwarding the Weibo post. Another netizen commented, "This world is filled with love, this is love, hoping that everyone can lend a helping hand. Please forward this! Let this world be full of warmth! This winter will no longer be cold!" Netizen "Han DabheiV" responded, "Alright... Can only help to forward... Every drop of blood is warm, it is the continuation of life!" There were also netizens who mentioned that they had passed the appeal information to the China Rare Blood Type Alliance.

Mr. Wang said that the doctor mentioned during the rescue that the mother needed platelets, and the hospital might not have them. "Although it was just mentioned casually at the time, seeing my wife's condition, I was anxious and immediately called my friends for help, asking them to try to help me find blood."

According to the report, "A friend had massive bleeding after a miscarriage and is being rescued at Haidian Maternal and Child Hospital. Urgently needs B-type RH positive blood. Please help spread the rescue information in Beijing. Thank you, quickly learn English, listen and memorize words, total 7.10G. Kneeling down to beg everyone." Last night at 9 o'clock, netizen "Jingcheng XiaocuiV" posted an appeal for help on Weibo, and this Weibo post was quickly forwarded, reaching over 6,000 forwards by noon yesterday. Some netizens not only forwarded the Weibo post but also expressed their willingness to go to the hospital to donate blood.
