**Image** Kuba Network is an online social networking community based on personality and interest tests. Kuba Network will have you take small quizzes involving picture selections to determine your personality traits, after which it will recommend friends you might like and help you make new connections.

Kuba Network’s unique DNA dating community can automatically match your compatibility based on various social DNA profiles.

**Image**

You can also search for friends by gender, country, whether they have video profiles, or select certain personality traits such as outgoing or serious. You can adjust the degree of these traits using a bar. These personality parameters are assessed based on the test results. After finding these friends, you can view the tests they’ve participated in and see which of your own tests match with them—indicating similar interests, such as favorite movies or music. You can view their test results or take the same tests to check compatibility. The main user retention factor for SNS websites can be enhanced through these kinds of tests and interactions. In terms of revenue models, Kuba Network plays the role of a "matchmaker" and generates income accordingly. Its voice services are only available to users who bind their phone numbers, ensuring privacy as your number won't appear on the recipient's phone.

Currently, Kuba offers features such as "Moliao," "Voice Messaging," "Phone Love Messages"...

Leave a voice message for any Kuba friend via your phone (up to 3 minutes). Each message costs 1 Kuba coin. To leave a voice message, click here.

* **Call Invitation:**

Send a call invitation to a Kuba friend who is not yet in a dating relationship with you. If accepted, the call will connect immediately, paid by the inviter at a rate of 1 Kuba coin per minute.

* **Phone Love Messages:**

Limited to Kuba friends currently in a dating relationship. You can directly call the other person (provided they have a bound phone number), with the caller paying 1 Kuba coin every 2 minutes. To call a Kuba friend, click here.

* **Auto-Reply:**

A disturbance prevention feature. If you don’t want to answer calls from Kuba friends, you can set up an auto-reply with a recorded message. Each use costs 1 Kuba coin. To set up an auto-reply, click here.

* **Voice Monologue:**

Record a 3-minute voice monologue to place on your homepage. Each recording costs 1 Kuba coin. To record a voice monologue, click here.
