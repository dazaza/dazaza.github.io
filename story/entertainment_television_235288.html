Her brother Brewster was wandering due to the instability of the family, and when she got angry, Su-jin focused on studying, getting into a prestigious university, and later working at a famous law firm in the U.S. Later, she realized the limits of an Easterner living in the West, just as the best law firm in Korea invited her to develop in Korea.

Song Dae-pung - played by Lee Bi-mod

Second son of the Pine Pharmacy family, pediatrician, 180 cm tall, thick eyebrows, humorous, especially graceful towards women, graduated from the medical department of Korea's top university. Considering all these conditions together, there can only be one answer, and that is a playboy. Because he wants to fully utilize and enjoy these conditions, he is still busy dating various women. Unexpectedly, this romance expert has suffered consecutive failures recently, starting with pursuing the lawyer Lee Soo-jin who moved in front of their house, only to find out she likes the older brother Jin-pung. The artist Woo Eun-jee who lives next door also ended up liking his ugly younger brother Seon-pung. What is going on in this world?

Kim Bok-shil – played by Yu Sun

Nurse, as plain as her name suggests. In Song Dae-pung's clinic, there is only one doctor and one nurse, so she takes care of all the work, from registration to injections, cleaning, comforting crying children, and running errands when Dae-pung gets hungry. This is the all-around nurse Kim Bok-shil.

Song Seon-pung – played by Han Sang-jin

Third son, social affairs reporter at the TV station, knowledgeable about politics, economy, society, culture, and art, so others gave him a nickname, 'the walking internet'. Is it Bok-shil, his brother's girlfriend? Or the beautiful star Woo Eun-jee?

Woo Eun-jee – played by Yoo Na-ha

Artist, lost her parents in a traffic accident when she was young and grew up in her uncle's home who didn't have children. Beautiful and cute, she might know how to make others like her because she lost her parents at a young age, so everyone liked her as a child. This man isn't good-looking either, and she reluctantly agreed to a blind date arranged by her father, but unexpectedly, this guy named Seon-pung didn't show up. One day, Seon-pung gave her a big teddy bear and apologized, and Eun-jee found herself crying on the street holding the bear. Why did you buy me a bear? And thus, she fell in love with this man who resembles a clumsy bear.

Song Mi-pung --- played by Ji Chang-wook

Youngest son, objectively speaking, the most handsome among the four sons. Perhaps because he's the youngest in the family, his character is feminine, gentle, kind, and lacks decisiveness. His hobbies are also typically feminine, such as cooking, knitting, and cross-stitching. Suddenly one day, Mi-pung's best friend Yong-chul showed up with a child, left the child with him, and went off to serve in the army. Taking care of the child instead of his friend made Mi-pung feel happy, growing up just like his name suggests as the beautiful wind matured.
