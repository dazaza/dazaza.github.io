**Tanks Last Forever, Classics Endure**

When the familiar music of Tank Battle plays, all 80s and 90s gamers will smile knowingly. Today, *Tank Battle Online* carries on the legacy of the classic FC game, bringing the old battlefield onto the internet so that millions of former players can sit in front of their computers and relive childhood memories.

Do you want to meet your partners online again? Do you want to continue the unfinished battles of the past? Tank Battle is waiting for you to join!

**Retro Innovation with RPG Elements**

Although Tank Battle originated from the ancient FC game, the world of tanks has been restructured by the collective efforts of the planning team into a new world! Here, tanks are not just simple shapes as everyone knows them to be; they possess various imaginative and splendid appearances, each with characteristics matching their designs. The graceful and swift Spider-Man, the powerful and majestic Megatron, and the flexible and versatile Ghost Dancer – here, you can encounter many legendary heroes!

Compared to FC games, Tank Battle offers incomparable advantages in gameplay. In hundreds of campaigns within Tank Battle, there are various unique elite tanks and boss-level tanks, each often possessing their own special moves. To successfully clear levels, players must adopt targeted tactics and teamwork, fully utilizing the classic RPG gameplay.

**Ten Thousand Players on One Server, Competing in New Chapters**

Tank Battle uses the latest Flash technology and meticulously crafted artistic graphics to present on the web visuals no less impressive than those of large-scale games. At the same time, servers capable of accommodating ten thousand players allow players to always find compatible partners to fight alongside in the game.

Speaking of competition, what first comes to mind for everyone is undoubtedly world-class competitive events like WCG and ESWC. However, regrettably, for ordinary players, these events feel too distant. In Tank Battle, you don't need to possess extraordinary skills or endure long waits; every day, you can participate in ten-thousand-player online battles, progressing through layers of matches to select top-tier competitive players from among thousands, with excitement that rivals large competitions like WCG!

The grand competitive game of the year, *Tank Battle Online*, brings you to the forefront of fiery battlefields together!
