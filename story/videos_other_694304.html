Uniqlo, Sephora, and Starbucks sign contracts with Hefei Shushan "Heart City"

Recently, it was learned from the Shushan District of Hefei that in September next year, the Sanyuan business circle in the Shushan District of Hefei will see the first fourth-generation Shopping Mall (large shopping center) in the province.

Three-year-old "police officers" bravely capture "bad guys," five-year-old "doctors" urgently rescue "patients," and ten-year-old "firefighters" race against time to extinguish fires... In September next year, a large children's theme park within the first fourth-generation Shopping Mall will become a paradise for children's games. A so-called fourth-generation Shopping Mall is mainly positioned as an urban leisure center, emphasizing experiential shopping, integrating four major functions: shopping, catering, leisure, and entertainment. Among them, shopping is no longer the most important function of the fourth-generation business model but has significantly increased cultural and entertainment and life functions, adding social public activities such as press conferences and evening galas instead of commercial promotional events.

On that day, 15 well-known domestic and international brands, including Japan's Uniqlo, France's Sephora, and America's Starbucks, have already signed contracts with the "Heart City" project located in the Sanyuan business circle of Shushan District. Adding to this are three anchor stores that have previously signed contracts: Dazhong Department Store with more than 40,000 square meters of shopping area, a super five-star Poly cinema, and Hefei's first real ice skating rink. The Sanyuan business circle will add over 100 well-known domestic and international brands, most of which are entering Hefei for the first time. At that time, this large shopping center will bring great convenience to the daily lives, shopping, and leisure of Hefei residents. (Source: Tongling Daily) (View more "Heart City" news)
