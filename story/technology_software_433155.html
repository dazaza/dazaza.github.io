"Mr. Jiang recalls"

by tangtwuwu8 on 2011-05-19 09:00:13

People always say that animals are ferocious, but in this city, the dump trucks are more ferocious!

After being rescued, the driver was emotionally agitated. He told the rescuers that his wife and sister were sitting on the back seat, and his wife was holding a child under 2 years old.

The firefighters used tools to desperately dig out the soil covering the car. However, the car was still crushed by the dump truck.

Mr. Luo and Mr. Shen, the driver, are friends. He recalled that at around 4:20 PM, Mr. Shen drove his sister back to their residence in Zhen'an Village, Haicang, with his wife and child also in the car, making a total of five people.

Scene: The car was flattened, resulting in three deaths.

At 5:35 PM, the traffic police department brought in a large crane and two loaders. The crane finally separated the dump truck from the car, while the loaders cleared the soil at the scene. Firefighters then used shovels, hoes, and other tools to clear the soil piled on the car body.

Quick comment:

"Save me quickly!" At this moment, the car driver was half-lying in the driving seat, with an injured arm. With the help of the crowd, the firefighters successfully rescued the driver, and medical personnel provided on-site bandaging and treatment. It is understood that a man in the front seat of the car had already been saved by the crowd earlier, with relatively minor injuries.

Currently, another injured person, Mr. Shen, is unstable emotionally and has multiple fractures throughout his body. He is currently hospitalized for treatment.

Reported by the reporter Li Liyu, Huang Minjiang, and intern Cai Zhichao / text, Wu Fang / photo

Rescue scene

It's time to take it seriously, it's time to "brake". Both drivers and relevant organizations need a safety lesson; otherwise, tragedies will continue to occur.

"All the way, our car was driving very slowly. When we reached that intersection, a black BYD car drove out from the village, so we let this car go first. But before this car left, I felt something heavy suddenly press down on the back of the car. A large amount of sand and soil covered us completely," said Mr. Luo.

In fact, it's just a lack of safety awareness! Driving such a huge monster recklessly, frequently speeding, and once there is an emergency, how can they stop?

During this process, the Guangben car was pushed forward a short distance and collided with Mr. Jiang's car. Then the dump truck crushed the car, and the soil spilled out.

Eyewitness: "It's all because the dump truck went against traffic."

Around 5 PM, a large truck was called in. Rescue personnel tied one end of a steel rope to the bottom of the dump truck and tried to pull it with the large truck, but after several attempts, they were unable to move the dump truck.

"Guangben car was about to turn into the village, and the dump truck was driving in the adjacent lane. For some unknown reason, the dump truck crossed the double solid line, entered the opposite lane, and suddenly, a car came from the opposite direction. The dump truck sharply turned right to avoid it. Originally traveling fast and fully loaded with soil, it immediately lost control, followed by a rollover," Mr. Jiang recalled.

About 20 minutes later, the car body was exposed, but except for the driver's position, other parts were all crushed under the overturned dump truck, completely flattened.

Afterward, Mr. Luo and Mr. Shen called 119 and 110. "At that time, the crowd moved the sand and dragged me out of the car. I was the first one to be rescued."

Police, 119, 120, and other relevant departments have come to the rescue.

Correspondents Xia Houyan, Ye SenSen, Liao Honghui

At 8 PM, in the outpatient department of the Haicang Hospital, the reporter met the injured Mr. Luo. His cervical spine was slightly damaged, and he would stay in the hospital overnight for observation. If there are no other problems, he could be discharged.

Narration: Friends sending sister home involved in a car accident

"I witnessed the entire accident process, it was all because that dump truck went against traffic! Too terrible, too terrible!" said Mr. Jiang, the driver of the black car at the scene, who witnessed the entire accident process.

Before the accident, Mr. Luo was sitting in the passenger seat of the car. Recalling the car accident experience, his legs were still trembling uncontrollably.

Why are dump trucks so dangerous? Why do these drivers keep causing trouble?

It's not accidental that dump trucks are so dangerous. Such incidents frequently appear in newspapers, and our newspaper hotline often receives reports from readers that some roads have dump trucks speeding dangerously, making people anxious.

At the time, Mr. Jiang stopped at the intersection after driving out from the village.

Firefighters approached, some using demolition tools, others digging by hand, eventually opening a gap and successively lifting out the child and two women. Unfortunately, after confirmation by medical personnel, all three had no signs of life.

At the scene, the reporter saw that a dump truck had rolled over to the right side, next to a Guangben car. The spilled soil almost completely buried the car, only exposing a part of the front of the car body. Next to it was a black car with less damage.

Yesterday afternoon at around 4 PM, a tragedy occurred at the intersection of Lanwei New Village on Jian Gang Road in Haicang. Currently, the specific cause of the accident is under investigation.

On April 3rd, at the entrance of Tianyuan Bridge, a dump truck killed three children crossing the road. Just four days later, another incident involving a dump truck occurred, resulting in the unfortunate death of a family of three.
