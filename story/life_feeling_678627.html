My leading lady is you

A long, long time ago, he and she were in the same class.

He was interested in physics, mathematics, and chemistry, but unfortunately, he was very disappointed with English. No matter how hard he tried, he just couldn't remember it! And her physics, mathematics, and chemistry weren't that great either. Though he loved to talk, he always found himself tongue-tied when facing girls!! But she was lively and outgoing, always having endless things to say.

At the beginning of the school year, they were strangers, and she even called him by the wrong name.

Later, when they changed seats, even though they were far apart, her seatmate was his former seatmate. So, gradually, there were some simple conversations between him and her.

One ordinary morning, she said to him, "Can you help me with tutoring? Physics, math, chemistry... I'm dying!" (Such childish words!)

He was stunned by this unexpected request. It was the first time someone had asked him for tutoring, and he agreed. At that time, he didn't have any particular feelings for her.

Later, she learned from him that his English grades were terrible. So, she encouraged him to work hard on it and not give up. She even said she would tutor him in English. He was deeply moved because it was the first time at school that someone had encouraged him.

He tutored her for two days, and a few days later, in the final exam, she finally passed. Excitedly, she showed him her test paper. At that moment, he also smiled; his efforts had not been in vain. However, his English score was as usual—failing. Out of four subjects, he advanced in one but regressed in three. Feeling like a failure, he wondered why his English was so poor!!

After the tutoring incident, some classmates started saying that they were a couple!! Even though he tried not to let anyone know about his tutoring her, rumors spread like wildfire. He couldn't explain, nor did he bother to. The truth would clear itself up. After all, what's the big deal about helping someone with their studies? In fact, sometimes he liked these rumors, though he didn't know why.

Another ordinary morning, they were chatting casually. Then she asked if he wanted to go to McDonald's with her and his sister, AA system. He agreed.

It was also his first time going there. They decided on what to eat beforehand. He always accommodated her, saying he didn't mind what they ordered. That day, they ordered a little bit, and he ate very little, possibly because he wasn't used to the food! While they talked about their own stories, laughing and eating, the atmosphere felt wonderful. He just listened quietly, watching her. Looking at her complex and ever-changing expressions, he felt an indescribable sweetness in his heart.

Unconsciously, whenever he saw her laughing and playing with other classmates, he always felt an indescribable pang of jealousy. He realized that he had fallen for her, liking her chatter, her cute and childish smile. The feeling of liking someone was so strange. But he didn't know if she liked him too. He wanted to confess to her but hesitated, lacking the courage. What if she rejected him? Then he wouldn't be able to be friends with her anymore, and he feared returning to square one. So, he silently paid attention to her joys and sorrows.

Suddenly, a song came to mind, "My Leading Lady Is You." The lyrics perfectly described them.

"It was you who suddenly entered my world that day and changed everything about it, but I willingly left my past behind for you…." For her, he grew tired of online games. He, who used to go online every day, stopped doing so for her. Of course, during weekends, he would still go with his classmates. Compared to before, he had changed a lot for her.

A semester passed, and he never told her anything, nor did he hear anything from her. He was very disappointed.

In fact, he really wanted to tell her: "I love you, will you be my leading lady?"

He hoped so much that she could read this article and understand his heart! Maybe this is an ending without results.........
