Futian Lei Sa is confident about the future.

by ftleisa on 2011-11-24 14:48:38

According to the introduction, after the Chengde engine component manufacturing center of Beijing Auto Foton is built, it will have an annual production capacity of 150,000 tons of engine casting components and 150,000 tons of automobile chassis casting components. The engine casting components mainly meet the needs of Aoling engine, Leisa engine, Foton Cummins engine, Mercedes heavy truck engine, and Beijing Auto Group engine casting requirements.

Relevant person in charge of Foton Motor pointed out: "The scaled production of engine casting components can rapidly reduce costs, and the costs of engines and whole vehicles will also decrease accordingly, increasing the profit per vehicle and significantly improving the quality level of engine casting products, thereby enhancing the market competitiveness of whole vehicle products."

The establishment of the Chengde engine component manufacturing center of Beijing Auto Foton not only meets the current development needs of Foton Motors but also focuses on future market competition and serves the long-term development plan of Foton Motors. According to statistics, globally, 25% of pig iron components and 60% of cast aluminum components are used in the transportation industry, while in China, more than 30% of casting components are used in the automobile industry.

Having a high-end casting plant ensures the production of high-end engines; with the support of high-end power, it guarantees the improvement of technology and quality of Foton Motors. "From now on, Foton Motors will continue to promote the integrated layout of whole vehicles and components, enhance the optimization and adjustment of industrial location layout, and lay a solid foundation for promoting Foton Motors, this self-own brand automobile company, to become a world-class company." Foton Motors is full of confidence in the future.
