"It's a common practice in kindergartens for teachers to investigate when they smell something unpleasant in the classroom." Said Jing Wen, a teacher from the nursery of the kindergarten. She admitted that if children were asked to collectively remove their pants for inspection as they claimed, it was indeed inappropriate. However, some children might not care and think that the teacher was playing a game with them when the teacher pulled down their pants one by one.

Yesterday, the reporter contacted the parents of the senior class of Xingqing Kindergarten and found that most parents still thought that the kindergarten teachers cared about and cherished the children. "When I first heard about this from my child, I didn't believe it. After asking several other parents and getting confirmation, I was indeed a bit angry," said Ms. Zhang, a parent. "Isn't there a better way? How can we protect the children's self-esteem?" One parent even expressed anger.

In the afternoon yesterday, the reporter went to Xingqing Kindergarten. When the reporter talked with more than 30 children of the senior class, the children started chattering again. "The teacher asked who had soiled their pants, and everyone said no. Later, the teacher made us all stand at the front of the classroom and take off our pants," a little girl said softly while lowering her head. "I didn't take mine off." "You did, I saw it," said a boy sitting next to her. When the reporter asked why the girl lied, she replied, "There are more boys in my class, and I felt embarrassed when the teacher asked us to take off our pants."

However, more parents expressed helplessness. "This teacher has been with the children since they entered the kindergarten and has always communicated well with the parents. It wouldn't be good for the child to have a bad relationship with the teacher over this matter," one parent frankly stated.

To confirm whether what the children said was true, Ms. Tan contacted other parents in the class. The parents learned from their own children that the incident indeed happened.

Jing Wen said that since this incident occurred in the kindergarten, the school would conduct an investigation in the class and hold a parent meeting to explain the situation to the parents. If the situation described by the children is true, the school will seriously deal with the involved teacher, comfort and compensate the children whose self-esteem has been hurt, and sincerely apologize to the parents.
