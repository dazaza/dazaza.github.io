Nokia is not only a mobile phone, but also a kind of sentiment.

by anonymous on 2013-09-04 15:50:29

In the movie "The Five Stolen Years," He Man runs through every mall on the streets to buy a Nokia 5300 as an anniversary gift, presenting it with pride to her former lover and naively saying, "Didn't you always want this phone? It was really hard to find."

However, she, whose memory is still stuck five years ago, doesn't know that Nokia's glorious era, along with her former love, has already become a thing of the past.

In fact, there had been rumors earlier that Microsoft might acquire Nokia, but when Microsoft and Nokia officially announced the news, fans of Nokia couldn't hide their regret.

Instantly, the internet saw a wave of nostalgia discussions about "those years, the Nokia phones you've used."

Staff from Tencent Technology input keywords such as "Nokia" and "sentiment" on Tencent Weibo, searching out 16,700 related posts. The topic "Microsoft acquires Nokia, reminiscing about the old days' superphones" became a popular subject. On Sina Weibo, 28,042 related posts were found, placing the topic second on the popularity chart.

Netizen "Du Ni Dang Shen Wo" remarked that Microsoft acquired Nokia for $7.2 billion, meaning there will no longer be a pure Finnish company. Whose childhood didn't have a Nokia?

Another netizen, "JUNE Yi Sheng He Qiu," also said that despite having an iPhone 5 today, thoughts of the old monochrome screen Nokia still bring back fond memories.

Some people like brands, so they chose Apple; some like appearance, so they chose Sony Ericsson; some like powerful hardware, so they chose Samsung; some like high cost-performance, so they chose Xiaomi; some like conformity, so they chose HTC; some remain stubborn, so they chose Nokia.

Nokia fans reminisce about those years before smartphones were everywhere, accompanied by various old-style Nokias that looked plain but were sturdy and durable, inevitably evoking feelings of sadness.

Netizen "S_Chris_Lin" said, "We have too many emotions and sentiments towards Nokia, and too much helplessness and confusion towards its partner Windows Phone."

Netizen "Xihuan Fadai De Mutou" mentioned giving up Apple and returning to Nokia, "This is what kind of sentiment? I hope it can give me a new feeling, but I'm also afraid it will disappoint me."

Moreover, there are not a few netizens fantasizing about Nokia combining with Android, which now can only end in disappointment.

Netizen "-Qing Jiao Wo Mr_Yan" expressed wanting to buy a Nokia Android phone, but it seems impossible now.

Regarding this combination, netizen "Du Ni Dang Shen Wo" stated, "If Nokia produced Android devices, they would definitely be the best. No one would bother asking about Samsung, and Apple wouldn't be so arrogant."

When long-forgotten Nokia models like the 3210, 3320, 7610, and 5300 dust themselves off and reappear in people's sights as if bidding farewell, all we can do is remember.

Let's end with a sentence from a Nokia fan: "Remembering the times of those straight, slider, and flip phones - dedicated to our youth that will eventually fade away."
