Three Key Points of Safe, Environmentally-friendly, and Green Decoration

by longersing on 2009-10-28 14:45:39

The three key points of safe, environmental protection and green decoration: the design materials used in green environmental protection decoration must be green decoration materials, ventilation during the decoration process, etc. A more concise design can better reflect the modern people's home life following an orderly and non-chaotic way.

I. Green environmental protection decoration design

1. Select qualified decoration materials. Inorganic non-metallic materials should select Class A materials, and man-made wood panels and veneered man-made wood panels should select Class E1. Other materials should comply with the ten standards stipulated in the national standard "Limitation of Harmful Substances in Indoor Decoration and Renovation Materials" to ensure the safety of home decoration.

2. Control the amount of various materials used in decoration. During the decoration design before home decoration, it is necessary to consider using less materials that are prone to cause indoor environmental pollution, reducing laminate and paint processes to avoid potential safety hazards.

3. Follow the principles of simplicity and practicality. The more complex the decoration design and construction, the more functional spaces and shapes there are, the greater the workload, and the more main and auxiliary materials used. Even if all the materials that meet the standard for the limitation of harmful substances in indoor decorative materials are used, the finished product after construction may still cause indoor environmental pollution. Sometimes the hidden dangers are deep, slowly releasing years later, which is a major hidden danger in home decoration. Even if all qualified decoration materials are used, excessive usage can lead to excessive harmful substances in indoor air, causing unsafe factors.

4. Pay attention to "ventilation" during decoration. Through decoration design, make the indoor and outdoor space interconnected, creating an open and flowing space, integrating indoor and outdoor spaces, allowing residents to get more sunlight, fresh air, and scenery. This not only regulates mood but also brings safety.

5. Plants are the safety guardians of human health. Placing plants during decoration can not only absorb harmful substances indoors, create a relaxed and harmonious environment like being in nature, but also enhance the atmosphere of home decoration, cultivate life interests, and improve cultural taste.

II. Selection of green decoration materials

Many commonly used decoration materials, such as inorganic materials and metal materials, will not lead to environmental pollution or harm human health, so they can be safely used by consumers.
