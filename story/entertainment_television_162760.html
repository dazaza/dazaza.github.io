Hunan Economic TV's self-produced contemporary emotional drama "This Life This World" is a touching play filled with a strong 'Hunan spicy' flavor. It premiered on June 8th at ten o'clock in the evening on Hunan Economic TV's original theater.

Plot Summary:

The cast of "This Life This World" is quite unique, half are professional actors and the other half are hosts from various channels of Hunan Television, including Hunan Economic TV host KK, Jinying Cartoon Satellite TV's Ege (Wang Dongyue), Xiaodouzi (Jiang Yuchen), Hunan Economic TV's leading actress Bin Jie, and Urban Channel's Ya Li. The plot of "This Life This World" unfolds along two threads: one is the supermarket competition, which is reportedly rewritten based on the competition among several well-known supermarkets in Hunan; the other thread is the secret history of grievances of the Chen family, who became wealthy through selling lard, and this story is also based on a real-life incident that occurred in Hunan where a mother and daughter were both pregnant and their identities were swapped.

This is a story about a mother who keeps a promise for a lifetime; this represents "goodness."

Chen Jiahao, who once lived in Kuitan Village and made a living by collecting waste materials such as dead pigs and intestines to make lard, owns his own enterprise - the Jiayijia Supermarket Chain 24 years later. Chen Jiahao quickly becomes a well-known rich person in the area. Like most billionaires today, Chen Jiahao decides to venture into the real estate industry, partly to solve the identity of being a "peasant tycoon," and partly to choose an heir among his two sons. His eldest son, Chen Haofu, is unskilled, while his second son, Chen Haotian, is independent but born to his second wife, Mei Qing. Just as he is aggressively entering the southern city commercial district, Liang Jian appears. He is the man with whom Mei Qing's daughter from her previous marriage, Wang Xiaofan, had secretly fallen in love and bore a child. He insists to Chen Jiahao and Mei Qing that he wants his child with Xiaofan back, otherwise, he will fight Chen Jiahao in the business world until the end.

Chen Jiahao, forced to fight back, feels increasingly powerless and decides to pass his position to his second son, Chen Haotian, causing a great uproar. Mei Qing, who usually doesn't interfere in business operations, unexpectedly strongly opposes it. Mei Qing reveals the truth: Chen Haotian is not the biological son of Chen Jiahao and herself, but rather the child of Wang Xiaofan and Liang Jian! This revelation strikes Chen Jiahao like a thunderclap...

Twenty-three years ago, when Chen Jiahao's 16-year-old stepdaughter, Wang Xiaofan, was unmarried and seven months pregnant, it became impossible to hide. In anger, Chen Jiahao ignored the pleas of his nine-months-pregnant wife, Mei Qing, and physically abused Wang Xiaofan, forcing her to have an abortion. On a stormy night, Wang Xiaofan ran away from home, and Mei Qing followed her. Wang Xiaofan, suffering from congenital heart disease, gave birth prematurely and experienced severe postpartum bleeding, nearly dying. At this time, Mei Qing's child was also born prematurely. Knowing that Xiaofan couldn't return home with a child, and that Xiaofan's child also suffered from congenital heart disease, which would make it impossible for him to grow up healthy without proper conditions, Mei Qing tearfully swapped the children. She sent her own child to a farmer and left behind a token before deeply burying this secret in her heart. She lied to everyone, claiming that Xiaofan's child had already "died," hoping to return the child to Xiaofan when she grew up and could take care of herself. However, the family that adopted the child suddenly disappeared, and Wang Xiaofan blamed all her hatred on Chen Jiahao and Mei Qing, thus leaving home...
