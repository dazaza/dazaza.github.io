The WeChat Game Center has already launched three games: "Shooting Plane", "Everyday Puzzle", and "Everyday Chain". According to the latest news pushed yesterday, Tencent's game called "Rhythm Master" will also soon be available on the WeChat gaming platform.

"Rhythm Master" is a music game suitable for all ages. The gameplay builds on the traditional note-tapping method and adds a screen-swiping operation, featuring a large collection of Chinese music, diverse song types, innovative operational methods, and world rankings challenges.

The game adopts the traditional falling-note style of music gaming, allowing you to experience the classic VOS mode on your phone. It incorporates sound track sliding notes designed specifically for touch screens, giving you the sensation of your fingers dancing along with the melody. The song charts are crafted by professional music teams, ensuring top-notch quality in every chart!
