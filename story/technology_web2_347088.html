This article comes from Taobao 8848 http://www.8848.cm Chapter 031 The second young master's embarrassing story. The door was slightly ajar. Just as Yun Xiaoxiao's hand touched the door, it opened... Was it not locked? Could it be that there was no one in the room? Yun Xiaoxiao didn't know whether to enter or knock because she heard strange sounds.

Inside the room came a series of giggles from a woman... and a man's deep voice...

"Master... please don't do this. It will be troublesome if Madam sees us!"

"She won't be back until very late. We'll be quick, my darling..."

"You haven't bought me the ring you promised yet! Next time, you must buy it!" The woman cooed.

"When I become the president of Ouyang Group, whatever you want, you can have. Of course, on the premise that... you must carry my child!" The man seemed restless.

"If Madam finds out, she will go crazy!"

"She can't bear children, so she shouldn't hinder me. Whoever gives birth to a grandson first will be favored. Regardless of where it comes from, we need to produce one to win Grandpa's favor. Perhaps then, you can officially become my second wife! Who would have thought your face is so young..."

"Oh no... Master... did you lock the door?"

"I think I did..."

Yun Xiaoxiao's mouth dropped open as she looked up and saw the bed. There was an unexpected display of intimacy, so shameless, and the door wasn't even closed...

Could this... be the second young master of the Ou family? In broad daylight...

Yun Xiaoxiao had never seen such a scene before; her face turned red instantly. How embarrassing, accidentally witnessing the second young master and a woman flirting in broad daylight. Didn't they know how to show some restraint? Maybe they were too preoccupied and forgot to close the door?

The Ou family was not a simple household at all. What should she do with these documents? Should she deliver them to the second young master's bed? The third young master sure knew how to pick his timing.

Yun Xiaoxiao gathered the documents and ran off.

Due to her nervousness, she accidentally bumped into the door, making a sound. The man on the bed angrily raised his head, let go of the woman he was entangled with, and jumped off the bed.

Yun Xiaoxiao panicked, biting her finger, and fled like a streak of smoke, leaving only a slim back view behind. The second young master didn't know who had walked in on his private moment. How careless of him to forget locking the door. Annoyed, he shut the door again and returned to continue his sordid activity.

Yun Xiaoxiao felt wronged while running. Why was she so unlucky? She encountered such a situation. That maid obviously knew what was going on in the room but didn't warn her, letting her walk right into it. She might get eye infections for seeing such improper things.

Bad people! The entire Ou family were bad! The eldest young master, the second young master, and the third young master!

Tears almost welled up in Yun Xiaoxiao's eyes. She tried hard to suppress the urge to cry and headed towards the third young master's room. When she reached the door, the previous scene still lingered in her mind. What a licentious family.

However, Yun Xiaoxiao learned one thing: the second young master intended to gain favor with the Ou family patriarch by having a grandson and was also eyeing the position of president...
