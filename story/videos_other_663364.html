Man unable to perform during prostitution is ridiculed and strangles sex worker to unconsciousness

by daidi2mqy on 2012-02-11 15:28:42

Southeast Network - Strait Metropolis Daily, August 19th (Trainee Reporter Jian Weixiong, Reporter Liao Mingsheng, Correspondent Zhang Weiwen) A man from Longyan surnamed Huang was drunk and went to solicit prostitution but was unable to get "into the mood", leading to mockery from the prostitute Xiao Lan (pseudonym). In a fit of anger, he strangled Xiao Lan with a towel. Unexpectedly, Xiao Lan lost consciousness, and Huang fled home in panic before later surrendering himself to the police.

Yesterday, Huang was criminally detained by the Longyan police on suspicion of intentional homicide. The hotel owner Zheng who allowed Xiao Lan to engage in prostitution at his establishment was also criminally detained on suspicion of harboring prostitution. After the incident, Xiao Lan was rushed to the hospital for emergency treatment and is now out of danger.

He choked the prostitute unconscious after drinking

"I choked someone, I don't know if she's dead or not." At around 3 am the previous morning, officers from the Longmen Police Station received a call from a man and quickly arrived at his home, taking him back to the station for investigation.

It was understood that the caller was named Huang. He confessed that around 2 am that day, after drinking with friends, he checked into a hotel and asked the hotel owner to arrange a "escort" for relaxation.

During the act of solicitation, due to being too drunk, Huang was unable to perform, leading to verbal ridicule from the prostitute Xiao Lan. In anger, he used a towel to strangle Xiao Lan. Soon, Xiao Lan turned purple and lost consciousness. Afterwards, Huang checked out of the hotel and fled. Upon returning home, he became increasingly worried, fearing that he had killed Xiao Lan, so he surrendered to the police.

Parents unaware of daughter’s prostitution

Yesterday, when we saw Xiao Lan at the hospital, she was lying in bed, her eyes red, and the skin near her left eye darkened to a purplish hue. It was introduced that on the night of the incident, Xiao Lan's boyfriend Xiao Liu found her unconscious in the hotel room and immediately called the police and sent Xiao Lan to the hospital.

At the hospital, Xiao Lan did not continue with treatment. Her father said that when she was first admitted, she received treatment, but due to lack of funds, it was stopped the next day, “Hospitalization costs over 1000 yuan per day, my wife is also hospitalized, there's nothing we can do.”

Xiao Lan's father said, “I don’t know why she ended up like this; I always thought she worked in an electronics factory in Shanghang.” Even until yesterday afternoon, Xiao Lan's father still claimed that he didn’t know what kind of work his daughter was actually doing. Xiao Lan's mother similarly expressed that Xiao Lan never told her anything, and she only learned about the incident through her elder daughter, which prompted her to rush to the hospital.

Boyfriend tacitly approved of her prostitution

It was understood that Xiao Lan is 20 years old and is from Longyan. In 2006, after her parents divorced, her father opened a small shop in Jiangxi and remarried there. Xiao Lan's sister and younger brother stayed with their mother, while Xiao Lan was awarded to her father by court judgment, though she chose not to live with him but instead stayed with her grandparents in their hometown.

Xiao Lan's boyfriend Xiao Liu said that they had been together for over two years, and he knew that his girlfriend engaged in prostitution, “I know it's not good, but there's nothing I can do.”

When her parents were not present, Xiao Lan revealed that after graduating from junior high school, she originally apprenticed as a hairdresser. Later, the hair salon owner told her that working in a hair salon in Yong'an, Sanming would earn her more money. However, upon arriving in Yong'an, Sanming, she was forced into prostitution.

In May this year, after returning to Longyan, Xiao Lan rented a room long-term in a hotel where the hotel owner Zheng introduced clients to her for prostitution activities, earning a commission from it.
