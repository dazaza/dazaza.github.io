Answers to Questions Related to Double Eyelid Cosmetic Surgery

What kind of eyes are considered beautiful?

The aesthetics of the eyes is not solely measured by the size of the palpebral fissure or the presence of double eyelids. Instead, there needs to be a certain standard of beauty: the proportion between the eyeball and the palpebral fissure, the shape of the inner and outer corners, the length and height of the palpebral fissure, the distance between the upper eyelid margin and the eyebrows, the shape and position of the eyebrows, the arrangement and density of the eyelashes, as well as the harmony of the proportions between the eyes and other facial organs.

Why do double eyelids generally look more attractive than single eyelids?

Single eyelid eyes usually have smaller palpebral fissures, drooping eyelashes, hidden eyelash roots covered by the upper eyelid skin, and often come with an increase in upper eyelid fat. All these related symptoms can be resolved through double eyelid formation surgery. After double eyelid surgery, the excess skin on the upper eyelid can be removed, along with excess fat, exposing the eyelash roots, enlarging the palpebral fissure, and making the eyelashes curl upwards. As a result, the eyes appear lively, intelligent, expressive, and become beautiful large eyes.

What are the methods of double eyelid formation surgery (blepharoplasty), and what are their characteristics?

Double eyelid formation surgery (blepharoplasty) includes the suture method, the thread embedding method, and the incision method. The thread embedding method and the suture method are simple, non-surgical, and recover quickly, suitable for young people with thin and tight upper eyelid skin without excess fat. However, the permanence of double eyelid formation is questionable. The incision method is suitable for any type of single eyelid eyes. This method can simultaneously handle excess skin and fat on the upper eyelid, with permanent surgical effects suitable for any type of single eyelid, but it has a longer recovery time. Currently, there are methods that can shorten the recovery time of the surgery.

What are the recent advancements in double eyelid surgery?

Double eyelid surgery is one of the most numerous and earliest conducted cosmetic surgeries in our country. Although traditional incision double eyelid surgery suits anyone, the long sutures removal time and slower postoperative recovery cause inconvenience to patients. For this reason, many experts have researched various methods. Among them, the "three-day suture removal double eyelid" stands out. The postoperative double eyelids appear vivid and personalized, with quick recovery, deeply favored by beauty enthusiasts and highly regarded in the industry.

What are the precautions for double eyelid surgery?

After double eyelid surgery, take oral anti-inflammatory medication for three days, and use eye drops for three days starting from the second day after surgery.

Within a week after surgery, avoid bending over too much to prevent bleeding and hematoma.

Avoid tiring your eyes excessively within a week after surgery.

Avoid eating seafood for three days after surgery.

On the day of surgery, avoid doing heavy work.
