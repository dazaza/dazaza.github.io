"Women's Reality, Men's Madness" is a breakup memoir and marriage training camp for women.

by jqrxass on 2009-10-30 18:03:04

"Women Realistic, Men Crazy" is a breakup memoir, but more importantly, it is a happiness manual for all women. Don't blame women for being crazy, nor criticize men for being realistic. Women, if you don't know how to accumulate your own happiness experience in every relationship, then even if women become realistic and men become crazy, the ownership of happiness will still not be owned for life.

The protagonist of the novel - white-collar female manager Feng Xi is just such a typical example. Before she grasped the code of happiness, her emotional trajectory was roughly like this: first love, and deeply unforgettable - getting married hastily - post-marriage figure distortion, severe lack of confidence, subservience to men - husband proposes divorce - pain, self-reproach, self-questioning, confusion, persistence - ultimately divorce - continuing pain, self-reproach, self-questioning, confusion... until encountering another true love Meng Shi in her life.

During this period, the unexpected appearance of her first love, the numerous difficulties from Meng Shi's family, and the intrigue and rivalry in the workplace all forced Feng Xi to temporarily forget the pain of her failed marriage and regain confidence in facing life.

God closed a door for her, but also opened a window. But, what she saw was another disaster, or a breathtaking landscape?

Part One of "Women Realistic, Men Crazy"

Since that day when she had dinner with Fu Mingyi, it seemed that there was no intersection between her and Fu Mingyi. However, when they met in the office, he would always glance at her. That look was very special, as if he couldn't get enough of her. This look made Feng Xi have an impulse to escape from the company. She understood, because she was too fat, fat to the point where Fu Mingyi couldn't help but look at her each time he saw her, trying hard to find the shadow of the past Feng Xi on her. This gaze made Feng Xi feel like being cut by a knife.

Feng Xi took off her earphones, and the moonlight dyed the lake water with a flickering silver-white color, like a piece of white silk fluttering in the wind. She walked silently to a Taihu stone by the lake and sat down, suddenly bursting into tears. She buried her face in her knees and sobbed softly.

No one understands how it feels to gain weight. In fact, she used to love buying new clothes and dressing up, but now she has lost interest in shopping. That's fine, but the humiliation from Tian Dawei she could never forget; and Fu Mingyi, her former first love, every look from him felt like torturing her; and... someone actually wanted to use her overweight body shape to advertise for a gym. Her self-confidence was completely shattered.

Part Two of "Women Realistic, Men Crazy"

She quietly gazed at the ripples caused by the flowing water. The water resembled a piece of ice jade, seemingly clear and transparent, but upon closer inspection, it appeared hazy and unclear. It was like how she viewed her own life, seemingly very definite, able to clearly know where the future path lies. However, how to proceed in the future, she wasn't entirely sure.

A black-roofed boat was tied to a small pier, and the boat owner put down the bamboo pole and went to the rear cabin. There was a small square table at the bow of the boat, with some dishes for drinking, and a jar of Shaoxing old wine was opened.

There was only a crescent moon in the sky, its light bright and silver, like a thin hook. Meng Shi lightly tapped on the side of the boat, following the rhythm of the bamboo pole perfectly. With his tapping, the reflection of the moon was broken apart.

Meng Shi leisurely drank his wine. His gaze didn't fall on Feng Xi, but it didn't prevent him from observing her expressions and gestures clearly.

Part Three of "Women Realistic, Men Crazy"

"You have a piece of chili on your mouth." Meng Shi looked at her indifferently and said casually. He reached out his hand and wiped her corner of the mouth, then held her face and kissed her.

All her unconscious exclamations were swallowed by Meng Shi. Feng Xi widened her eyes and saw the red lantern swaying in front of his eyes, forming a patch of light and shadow. His palm already covered her eyes, and the warm breath followed the mountain wind and lingered between her lips. Feng Xi was so nervous that she didn't know whether to obey him or push him away. She heard Meng Shi say softly, "Don't refuse me..."

Feng Xi was stunned for a moment. His voice had already moved to her ear, "If you dare to push me away, I'll bite your neck!"

She instinctively pushed him, Meng Shi slightly increased the force on her cheek, forcing her to lean back, and his lips really moved to her neck. He gently ground his teeth, "Say, should I take a bite?"

Feng Xi screamed, "No!"
