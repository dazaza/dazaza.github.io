It was officially approved by the relevant higher authorities as a professional company. Established in 2003, the company has achieved certain results through continuous development and relentless efforts. We now possess top-of-the-line imported tools and equipment, along with a team of highly skilled professionals who provide quality services at competitive prices, earning us consistent praise from our broad customer base. Our mission is: "With sincerity, we'll do what you don't have time for! Your requirements are our pursuits! We hope to become your permanent friend through our genuine and thoughtful service! We also aim to serve all sectors of society and jointly create a better life and work environment."

Service Scope:

1. Installation/Repair of air conditioners, cold storage, refrigerators, water heaters, range hoods, as well as recycling of various household appliances, and sale of second-hand air conditioners.

3. Medium and long-distance freight transport, moving services (including relocation of factories and pianos).

4. Mechanical carpet cleaning, stone restoration, floor waxing, daily cleaning of malls, residences, and storefronts.

5. Water/electricity installation and repair, pipeline unblocking, drilling.

6. Wall painting and plastering, simple renovation updates, etc.
