How to choose the most suitable car insurance policy?

By Fang Bo

I often receive calls from friends asking about the types of car insurance they should buy without wasting money. Personally, I believe that insurance should be selected based on a comprehensive evaluation of the vehicle's condition and driving skills to choose an appropriate combination of insurance types to provide suitable coverage for your beloved car. Here, I will introduce a few common insurance package options for car owners' reference:

Plan 1: Minimum Guarantee

Insurance Combination: Compulsory Traffic Insurance (CTI)

Coverage Scope: Only liable for third-party losses.

Applicable Objects: Suitable for those who think insurance is useless or those who urgently need an insurance policy to register their license plates or for vehicle inspection, including short-term accidental insurance.

Pros and Cons: Can be used to meet the requirements for registration or inspection. In case of a traffic accident, the other party’s losses can be partially compensated by the insurance company, but the loss of one’s own vehicle needs to be borne by oneself.

Plan 2: Basic Guarantee

Insurance Combination: CTI + Third-Party Liability Insurance (100,000 yuan) + Occupant Liability Insurance.

Coverage Scope: Mainly avoids traffic accidents involving third-party fatalities.

Applicable Objects: Suitable for vehicles used for a longer time, with skilled drivers willing to bear most risks and reduce premium expenses.

Pros and Cons: This plan provides coverage for third-party personal injury and property damage as well as for occupants in the insured vehicle, but the loss of one’s own vehicle needs to be borne by oneself.

Plan 3: Economic Guarantee

Insurance Combination: CTI + Third-Party Liability Insurance (200,000 yuan) + Occupant Liability Insurance + Vehicle Damage Insurance + Theft Insurance + No Deductible Agreement.

Coverage Scope: Most insurance liability accidents.

Applicable Objects: Suitable for vehicles used for three to four years, with moderate value, experienced drivers who pay attention to vehicle maintenance and safety protection, economically not affluent, and willing to bear part of the risk, making it the best economical choice.

Pros and Cons: This is the most valuable insurance combination with high cost-effectiveness. The premium is economical, and the coverage is basically complete.

Plan 4: Best Guarantee

Insurance Combination: CTI + Third-Party Liability Insurance (500,000 yuan) + Occupant Liability Insurance + Vehicle Damage Insurance + Theft Insurance + No Deductible Agreement + Glass Separate Breakage Insurance + Rearview Mirror or Headlight Separate Breakage Insurance + Scratch Insurance.

Coverage Scope: Basically covers all insurance liabilities and minimizes losses. Especially provides security for easily damaged parts of the vehicle.

Applicable Objects: Generally applicable to companies or individuals.

Pros and Cons: High-value insurance, worth the investment; strong risk resistance ability, similar to China Life Accident Insurance.

Plan 5: Comprehensive Guarantee

Insurance Combination: CTI + Third-Party Liability Insurance (500,000 yuan) + Occupant Liability Insurance + Vehicle Damage Insurance + Theft Insurance + No Deductible Agreement + Glass Separate Breakage Insurance + Rearview Mirror or Headlight Separate Breakage Insurance + Scratch Insurance + Spontaneous Combustion Insurance + Other Supplementary Insurances.

Coverage Scope: Covers all insurance liability accidents, comprehensively covering all insurance liabilities and minimizing losses.

Applicable Objects: Suitable for new cars with novice drivers, good economic conditions, and a need for full coverage; also suitable for government agencies, public institutions, and large companies.

Pros and Cons: Almost all accident-related losses associated with cars can be compensated, aligning with the purpose of CTI.

Dudu Warm Reminder: When purchasing car insurance, choosing the one that suits you best is the most important. It is hoped that car owners will purchase insurance according to their actual situation and needs, ensuring the best possible protection for their beloved cars.
