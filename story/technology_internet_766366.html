Mates who want to change to a new iPhone, check out the 5S/5C purchasing guide.

by anonymous on 2013-09-11 14:36:04

On September 11, Beijing time, Apple officially released the iPhone 5C and 5S. All parameter details of these two phones had been leaked beforehand, and it seemed that Apple's best-kept secret was nothing more than the price...

Now that the release is over, our next task is figuring out how to buy these two products. With the domestic version of the iPhone 5C priced at a staggering 4488 yuan, it’s likely that many cash-strapped young people would prefer not to go hungry as much or sell fewer kidneys just to get their desired product. Don't worry, read on.

It is clear from this table that the official price given by Apple is the cheapest for the US version. If we include the US consumption tax (calculated at 1.085), the price of the US version of the iPhone 5S/5C is still cheaper than in Hong Kong. Therefore, the US becomes the cheapest area among the first batch of countries and regions where the 5S/5C is launched. Of course, some states in the US are exempt from consumption tax, so buying an iPhone in those places would be even cheaper. The second cheapest option is Hong Kong.

Key Point Two: Should I buy the 5S or the 5C?

Apple has unprecedentedly launched two phone products at once this time, with not much difference in price between the two. Added to this, the price-performance ratio of the now discounted iPhone 4S is also very attractive, inevitably making many people hesitate about which one to choose.

Taking the Chinese market as an example, let's simply analyze the currently available iPhones:

① iPhone 5S: Without a doubt, this is the best product in Apple's current iPhone lineup. Whether it's core hardware or software systems, the 5S is the best. The newly added deep gray and gold colors also cater well to certain consumer groups. Despite little change in appearance compared to the iPhone 5, this doesn't detract from its overall appeal.

Strongly recommended for: normal consumers who naturally upgrade their devices, die-hard Apple fans, nouveau riche who like gold, and kidney-selling youth looking to show off.

② iPhone 5C: Although it has a plastic shell, its bright and colorful design is a first for Apple's iPhone lineup. Its hardware is directly copied from the iPhone 5, maintaining strong performance.

Strongly recommended for: lovers of bright colors, fresh and lively teenagers, and uncles who have grown tired of black and white aesthetics.

③ iPhone 4S: With new iPhones out and yet last year's model still in production, this would have been unimaginable in the Jobs era. But now it's the Cook era, so everything is possible. Many people consider the 4S to be the perfect design of Apple's iPhones so far. Combined with its currently low price, it offers good value for money. Of course, the premise is that you can tolerate system operation delays.

Strongly recommended for: penny-pinching youths who want to use an iPhone, extremeists who admire perfect design, and guys who've already sold a kidney but lost their iPhone 5.

Key Point Three: How to buy with the least hassle?

The most important question is, how can we quickly and effortlessly buy the iPhone product we desire? Let's still take Chinese consumers as an example:

If you want to buy a cheaper iPhone 5S/5C, what should you do? If you have friends or relatives in the US or Hong Kong, ask them to help buy it and bring it back manually—it will definitely be the cheapest. However, few people are this lucky, so the second option is to go to Hong Kong or the US yourself to buy it, treating it as a trip. Assuming no cost for the trip, the 5S/5C bought this way will also be the cheapest. If you don’t have relatives or money to travel, then it's suggested that you wait a month for the gray market goods. Based on past trends, the first month sees inflated gray market prices, but after that, large quantities of iPhones hit the market, causing the gray market prices to drop significantly. Although this method is more expensive than the previous two, it's still cheaper than the official retail version, right?

If you want to buy the safest and most reliable iPhone 5S/5C, what should you do? The answer is to buy the domestic version! This time, China is one of the first-release regions, so domestic users no longer have to worry about watching others use gray market goods for a long time while they haven't even received their device. Moreover, the warranty for the domestic version is much more reliable than gray market goods, so it's worth choosing for peace of mind.
