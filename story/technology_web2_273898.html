A "Buddha's Light" spectacle appears at Huangyangjie in Jinggang Mountain

by syd17u123 on 2009-11-23 18:04:55

At around 9:20 AM on October 27th, a rare "Buddha's Light" appeared in the Huangyangjie scenic area of Jinggang Mountain, delighting many visitors.

At 9:20 AM on October 27th, a magnificent and colorful sea of clouds and solar halo landscape appeared in the Huangyangjie scenic area of Jinggang Mountain. The center of the light circle resembled a person's shadow, clear and bright like a mirror, while the outer part displayed a seven-color halo of red, orange, yellow, green, blue, indigo, and violet interwoven together. The entire light circle gradually became smaller from far to near, lasting for about 40 minutes.

According to reports, the solar halo landscape is also known as the "Buddha's Light". It is a diffraction phenomenon formed when strong sunlight shines on the surface of mist during overcast weather. The appearance of the "Buddha's Light" requires the combination of various natural factors such as sunlight, terrain, and sea of clouds, and can only be appreciated in extremely few places.
