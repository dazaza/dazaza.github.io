It's outdated for celebrities to act cute or sell fashion on Weibo. What people want now is juicy gossip and verbal sparring matches. After Yuan Li and Song Dandan had their verbal spat on Weibo, yesterday Huo Siyan also lost her cool, blasting a certain怨妇 (lit. woman who constantly complains/whines) type actress in the industry, calling her stupid and vain. Her final sentence revealed the real issue: "Don't bring up your fat, greasy ex-husband every time you have a movie coming out!" This seems to be directly targeting Huang Yi. (Click on the image to enter Huo Siyan's Weibo)

Yesterday, reporters from the Huaxi Metropolis Daily separately called Huo Siyan and Huang Yi's agents. Huo Siyan's agent confirmed that the Weibo post was made by Huo Siyan and warned: "We have a great sense of entertainment, but we hope some people will stop playing tricks because we've reached our limit."

Huo Siyan's cursing at a certain怨妇type actress has caused much speculation.

Yesterday morning, actress Huo Siyan suddenly went off on Weibo, directly cursing a certain actress in the entertainment industry, and her words were quite harsh: "I'd rather laugh on a second-hand bike than cry in a BMW, this is the most straightforward line from 'Windmill,' and I really want to give it to a certain怨妇type actress in the industry. I hope you can understand it and find the reason within yourself. You should actually be clear about what the reason is! Lying is the most stupid thing! Stop being vain and lying, happiness will come to you. Don't bring up your fat, greasy ex-husband every time you have a movie coming out! Okay?"

Onlooker netizens immediately started guessing: who is the actress being cursed? Based on "fat, greasy ex-husband," netizens directly associated it with Huang Yi.

In 2010, after Huang Yi quickly married and divorced her ex-husband Jiang Kai, there were reports that Huo Siyan's new boyfriend was named Jiang Kai, who was exactly Huang Yi's ex-husband. Afterwards, Huo Siyan repeatedly stated that she didn't know Jiang Kai at all.

Huang Yi's new movie is about to be released, and during the press conference, someone brought up the topic of marriage again, and Huang Yi directly said that she herself initially proposed the divorce, thus making the topic of Huo Siyan intervening in Huang Yi's marriage hot again.

Fans' complaints lead to various leaks

After this Weibo post was sent out, Huang Yi and Huo Siyan's fans immediately started cursing each other, and Huo Siyan immediately stopped talking. It wasn't until around 6 PM yesterday that Huo Siyan updated her Weibo, saying she was filming in Xinjiang, indicating that she was calm. As of the reporter's deadline, Huang Yi also did not respond to this matter.

Subsequently, Huang Yi's fans leaked that the actual conflict between the two did not involve Huang Yi's ex-husband Jiang Kai, but rather Huo Siyan took away Huang Yi's boyfriend before she got married.

Huang Yi and her ex-boyfriend dated for many years, even reaching the point of discussing marriage, and they also bought a marital home. However, in 2008, during the half month Huang Yi spent in Guangzhou filming "With You All The Way," due to Huo Siyan's intervention, Huang Yi broke up with her ex-boyfriend. The leaker also claimed to have chat records and screenshots with Huang Yi's ex-boyfriend as proof.

The person referred to as Huang Yi's ex-boyfriend has the Weibo name Yinming JK, and his Weibo verification shows he is Yang Yi, the executive director of a real estate company. Previously, when he was dating Huang Yi, he registered an account on Huang Yi's fan forum, and would often share his life with Huang Yi with the fans, thus winning the fans' favor. However, Yang Yi did not respond to the cursing incident.
