Leping Procuratorate: The traffic police urban area team's concentrated rectification of the traffic environment has obvious effects.

by shuiya111100 on 2012-02-06 13:26:45

In recent days, all the policemen of the urban team of Leping public security bureau traffic police detachment have taken the road as a battlefield and strictly checked and corrected all kinds of traffic violations. On the basis of duty in the booth to deal with traffic violations, the team arranged police force to strengthen the patrol of the main sections, key intersections, key parts and key periods of the urban area. Every day, they dealt with the overloading and unauthorized passage of detour trucks on Dongfeng Road and Phoenix Avenue, and the traffic environment in the urban area has been significantly improved. At the beginning of May, the urban traffic police team took the opportunity of the Leping city environmental remediation campaign and concentrated police force to carry out urban traffic order rectification activities. The team overcame the difficulties of insufficient police force and police cars after 10 policemen and one police car were dispatched to the city management office, formulated the "work plan for rectifying urban traffic order", divided the old city into four major blocks and 12 areas, reorganized all the team police forces into 12 groups, and each team leader led four groups to be responsible for the traffic order rectification work of one large block and four areas, severely investigated and dealt with the random parking and placing, no license and certificate, "running, overflowing and leaking" transportation vehicles, running red lights, not driving according to the prescribed route, drunk driving and other traffic violations in the area. Before the rectification, publicity should be carried out first. The urban traffic police team increased the publicity of traffic safety, hung nine publicity slogans in the urban area, and used various forms such as the electronic screen of the patrol car, shouting reminders and news media to publicize civilized travel and advocate harmonious traffic. At the same time, it adopted the way of "patrol on foot and education persuasion", gradually rectified the traffic order of streets and roads, invited reporters from Leping News Editing Department and TV station and other news media to follow up and report with the police, created a strong rectification atmosphere, and effectively deterred all kinds of traffic violations. "Since May 1, we have carried out three centralized rectification actions against traffic violations, temporarily detained and disintegrated 89 motorcycles without license plates and certificates, 17 freight tricycles, 11 tricycle passenger electric vehicles, 15 yellow package vehicles, and dismantled the power devices illegally installed on 117 tricycles; investigated 1316 cases of random parking and placement, 73 cases of running and overflowing transport vehicles, temporarily detained 16 overloaded trucks, 3 engineering vehicles and 2 agricultural vehicles without license plates and certificates, among which 1 agricultural vehicle was forcibly scrapped; warned more than 130 drivers with minor violations and persuaded and educated 262 cases of running red lights. Now, the traffic order in the urban area has significantly improved, and the traffic environment and urban appearance have been effectively improved." Captain Wang Shuihua of the urban traffic police team told reporters.
