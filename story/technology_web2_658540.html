Yesterday morning, in the courtyard behind a resident's house in Yongfu Community, Shiyong Subdistrict, Beicheng District, a pile of firewood caught fire. Before everyone could call the police, the community fire team arrived at the scene within a minute and promptly extinguished the fire.

The fire team that arrived in an electric fire truck was not part of the official fire department; they were volunteers from the community fire station.

Since the launch of the Yongfu Community Fire Station in Shiyong Subdistrict, Beicheng District in December 2011, this was the first time the fire station "responded to an emergency" and successfully handled the fire situation.

The timely and rapid fire extinguishing by the electric fire truck received approval from the fire department. This was the first time the Yongfu Community Fire Station in Shiyong Subdistrict, Beicheng District handled a fire situation. At around 10 o'clock yesterday morning, Xiao Wei, who was on duty in the monitoring room on the first floor of the Yongfu Community Fire Station in Shiyong Subdistrict, Beicheng District, noticed an anomaly in the footage returned by the No. 2 surveillance camera in the community.

"From the surveillance footage, we saw thick smoke rising in the area covered by the No. 2 surveillance camera." Xiao Lin immediately pressed the alarm button, initiating the emergency response plan. While notifying the fire station members to quickly deploy the electric fire truck, he also called 119 for help.

"The fire location was not far from the fire station. It took us more than a minute to arrive at the scene. It turned out to be a pile of burning firewood. Fortunately, there were no flammable materials nearby." Xiao Lin led his team to quickly extinguish the fire using the electric fire truck.

Subsequently, the firefighters from Beicheng arrived and completed the on-site handling. Due to the small size of the fire and the timely rescue, the fire did not spread and caused no property damage.

Xiao Lin said that although the fire station had conducted many drills, this was the first time it was "for real." This time, the fire station handled the situation in a timely manner and successfully extinguished the fire. The electric fire truck not only received praise from the residents but also gained recognition from the Xicheng Fire Department. WWW.BMWCHINA-EV.COM
