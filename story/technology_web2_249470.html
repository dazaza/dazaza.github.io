Samurai High School

by x101918460 on 2009-10-19 19:09:12

This month, the Japanese TV station announced that Haruma Miura will play the leading role in the new drama which is temporarily named "15-Year-Old Samurai" and will be broadcast every Saturday evening. In this drama, Haruma plays a useless high school student who pretends not to see anything. However, once he is cornered, he transforms into a samurai to solve problems. This is Haruma's first time playing the leading role in a continuous drama on the Japanese TV station. Wearing a samurai costume, Haruma looks handsome and energetic. He said that it was very rare to appear on TV as a samurai and hoped to become a hero in children's hearts through this opportunity. In the drama, Haruma plays the protagonist Mochizuki Kotaro, a typical herbivorous male. But one day, he found an ancient book in the library. After that, whenever he encounters difficulties, he transforms into a carnivorous samurai to solve various problems happening in the school. The script is written by Yumiko Inoue, who has written many popular productions such as "White Tower" and "15-Year-Old Mother". This time, it is not adapted from a comic but is an original script, which should attract women and children who are obsessed with history recently. The time slot of 9 pm every Saturday on the Japanese TV station has launched many popular campus dramas such as "The Kindaichi Case Files" and "Great Teacher Onizuka". This time is no exception. Haruma Miura expressed that himself transformed into a samurai and his normal self are the same character but have completely different inner qualities, and he is looking forward to seeing how he will perform. This drama will announce the birth of a new character - high school samurai.
