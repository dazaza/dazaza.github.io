8 Habits to Detox and Lose 1 Pound Daily

Weight loss is a lifestyle. When you embrace this lifestyle, how do you properly plan your daily diet? Take a look at the latest slimming research by scientists and apply them to your everyday life. By doing so, losing weight can become as natural and simple as eating and sleeping.

1. Drink Coffee Before Breakfast

A study conducted by Vanderbilt University in Nashville, USA found that drinking a cup of coffee 30 minutes before breakfast can effectively control appetite, making you feel full with only 75% of your usual intake. It also increases fat-burning speed by 5%! This is thanks to a thermogenic substance called xanthine in coffee, which also provides sufficient heat for your body.

2. Supplement Calcium During Breakfast

Consuming 600 milligrams of calcium daily (300 milligrams each during breakfast and lunch) can help your body burn fat faster. Scientists discovered in a study that women who followed this method lost 22% more weight, 61% more fat, and 81% more abdominal fat compared to those who did not consume calcium.

3. Drink a Cup of Plum Juice Before Using the Bathroom

Plums are rich in flavonoids - these plant chemicals help the body effectively expel fat and toxins. Drinking a cup of plum juice can significantly accelerate your body's "cleaning" process, allowing your liver to quickly and thoroughly eliminate substances in your body that may cause obesity.

4. Drink More Water

If you want to lose 2 kilograms this week, the amount of water you drink daily is related to your weight. Nutrition experts suggest consuming 31.3 milliliters of water per kilogram of body weight (for example, a 50-kilogram woman should drink 1.565 liters of water daily). Water acts as an automatic high-temperature regulator for metabolism. Once water intake is insufficient, body temperature drops, and the body begins to rely on fat.

5. Eat Peanuts Between Breakfast and Lunch

Scientists found in a survey that peanut-eating dieters lost twice as much weight as those who didn't eat peanuts! Studies show that eating a handful of peanuts daily reduces calorie intake by 333 calories.

6. Eat Fermented Foods at Noon

Researchers found that eating fermented foods like steamed buns and sandwiches makes you less likely to feel hungry, lasting at least one hour longer than eating regular food. This is because starches and sugars are broken down into harder-to-digest components during fermentation. The digestive system needs more time to digest them, so you don't feel hungry as easily.

7. Have a Spoonful of Honey in the Afternoon

Honey can adjust your blood sugar to normal levels within 20 minutes after consumption and maintain this normal state stably for two hours, reducing hunger and soothing emotions, keeping you in a good mood.

8. Relax with Oolong Tea

Developing the habit of drinking oolong tea makes your metabolic system work faster. It increases the heat burned by the body by 3% and fat consumption by 10%. Since this tea contains no calories, fat, or sodium, those wanting to lose weight can drink it without worry.
