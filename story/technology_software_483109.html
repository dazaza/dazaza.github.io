Opening a 2-yuan store:注意事项

by shunjiuzhou on 2011-08-30 09:39:29

With the emergence of 2-yuan supermarkets, some project speculators also view them as a new money-making tool. Currently, the 2-yuan supermarket chain institutions in the market are mixed, requiring operators to carefully identify and choose.

Profit is undoubtedly the factor that franchisees will first focus on when choosing a headquarters. Some recruitment agencies aggressively promote their profit margins. A 2-yuan supermarket franchise headquarters describes it this way on its website: The seemingly extremely low 2-yuan price actually has a huge profit margin. If calculated based on purchase prices of 40 cents or 50 cents, the profit can be as high as 400%, which is incomparable to the high profit margin of general large retail items! However, according to industry insiders, if 2-yuan supermarkets want to ensure product quality, the average wholesale price of single-item goods should be around 1.2 yuan. If one solely focuses on profit margins while ignoring product quality, the 2-yuan supermarket will struggle to remain competitive, which is also the main reason why most franchises in the current market fail.

The "fashion" label is another tactic used by some recruitment agencies to attract franchisees. Some franchise headquarters claim that their products have exquisite craftsmanship, fashionable styles, and follow the latest trends, attracting the most purchasing-powerful fashion crowd. However, whether the provided goods can truly meet such standards requires operators to conduct personal on-site inspections. A simple and effective method is to go directly to the headquarters, check if there are any directly-operated stores in other places, and understand their operating conditions. Recruitment agencies without directly-operated stores at their headquarters should be chosen with caution.

No risk is another temptation thrown out by some franchise headquarters to franchisees. The author found on a 2-yuan store's website: No franchise fees are charged, all goods can be returned or exchanged at the original price, completely risk-free operation. The author contacted this company as a potential franchisee and learned that the initial purchase amount must reach 20,000 yuan, and the franchisee must sign a distribution contract, completing a certain sales volume each month. When the author pressed further on whether all goods could be unconditionally returned or exchanged, the company's staff was vague, only indicating that those meeting regulations could be returned or exchanged. They then hung up on the author's call, citing lack of trust as unnecessary for cooperation.

Li Qiang, who once stumbled in this industry, told the author that as a small retail shop, product quality is always the foundation for survival and profitability. Therefore, when choosing a franchise headquarters, one must start from the products themselves and comprehensively examine the franchise headquarters.

How to join a 2-yuan store? Here are several key points to consider when examining a franchise headquarters: Key points for examining a 2-yuan supermarket franchise headquarters

1. Product categories. Check if the headquarters' product range is sufficiently diverse. Generally speaking, the number of products provided by the headquarters should ideally be five times more than the quantity operated by a single store.

2. Product quality. Check if the headquarters' product quality aligns with the consumption level of your location. Before going to the headquarters for an inspection, you should first understand the local market situation. You can select a few representative products for detailed comparison.

3. Product update speed. Check if the headquarters adjusts product types based on market demand. This is especially important if the business mainly deals with fashion items like accessories.

4. Understand the operating conditions of the headquarters' directly-operated stores and other franchises.
