Recommend a few music websites with unique features.

by geekzhang on 2008-06-05 19:18:21

1. [Micro Broadcast Music](http://www.vvpo.com/). A domestic music aggregation search site with quite excellent content. It also allows for convenient searches of non-mainstream rock music and folk songs.

![Micro Broadcast Music](http://www.vvpo.com/images/vvpo.gif)

2. [Audio Vision Sky](http://www.stsky.com/). Very comprehensive, with good speed, often able to find some non-mainstream singers within China.

3. [Giant Whale Music Network](http://www.top100.cn/). The reason it is liked by many is due to its wide range of music genres. Not only can various popular music be found, but non-mainstream rock music and folk songs can also be conveniently located.

![Giant Whale Music Network](http://image.top100.cn/webimg/414logo.gif)

4. [Sougua](http://www.sougua.com/). Besides having a powerful music search function, it also features many original singer's music discovered through its highly popular forum, making the website content even more attractive.

![Sougua](http://www.sougua.com/images/logo.jpg)

5. [Midomi](http://www.midomi.com/). In addition to regular keyboard input keyword searches, it also provides voice-based search functionality, meaning you can sing these songs with your voice, and the system will analyze and list the results you need.
