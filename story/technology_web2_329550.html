by xjyzhenai on 2010-02-21 12:02:11

Joy alone is better than joy alone. When we find some good resources, we want to share them with friends, even people we don't know. So on a regular basis, we share it on many social networks. If you use the copy and paste method, it is too backward. As a result, many blogs and websites use sharing tools to make it easier for everyone to share resources and improve clicks on the website. Such as proxy, and JiaThis and so on.

Today, I want to introduce you to a more functional sharing tool: bShare. Compared to the traditional sharing tools mentioned above, bShare is not just a simple share button. It can also provide data statistics and other functions, which can help you analyze user operation habits. With these features, you can always review what you've shared and repost it to more people. bShare Share history not only keeps track of what you've shared recently, but also tells you how many people have shared the same post across the bShare network around the world.

image

bShare supports 62 content platforms such as social networks and Digg-like sharing sites. That is to say, bShare basically includes the SNS applications that are popular at home and abroad. In this way, it is very convenient for various network roles to share resources. Whether it is a website visitor, or the webmaster itself.

image

bShare's statistical capabilities are not as powerful as professional access statistics systems. But it's great enough for individual webmasters and bloggers. It can provide the top ten viewed pages, the top ten bShare click pages, the top twenty share target sites, and can provide statistics on the number of shares.

SNS is also a marketing tool. When you have enough SNS friends, you only need to click the share button, and your resources can be shared by enough people. And it can be done by enough people twice, three times... Reshare many times. So many bloggers certainly haven't given up on this kind of marketing. So actively join various sharing networks and include enough friends. Whenever there is a new blog post, I take the initiative to share it to every SNS. This approach ultimately brings not only traffic, but also the many benefits associated with word-of-mouth marketing such as brand awareness.
