Carnival Plan One: Beauty + Beast

Carnival Index:

Features: Of course, among friends, there must be beauties, and the beast is the dinosaur on stage. Tonight, they are going to dance together.

Carnival Details: Different periods, different countries, different spaces make one feel as if they are traveling through paradise. Fortunately, the ticket in each person's hand tells you that you are still in this world. When the music starts, the beauty's long hair flows, many things become blurred. When celebrating with a carnival heart, you will find a brand new visual and auditory experience.

Suggestion: Beauties should dress like insects, such as cute little bees who are also spiky, or elegant dragonflies who are light and graceful. Of course, there cannot be a lack of butterflies with flowers fluttering around, letting her spread affection everywhere; she is destined to be the one getting drunk. The gentlemen, on the other hand, must withstand the bees' fervor.

Highlight: Beauties dressed in gauzy outfits as thin as cicada wings, weaving through the crowd, naturally create an atmosphere.

Carnival Plan Two: Beer + Devil

Carnival Index:

Features: In fairy tales, Harry Potter, Spiderman, and the devil fight together, creating a beautiful blend of East and West, ancient and modern.

Carnival Details: As the night falls, under the bright and colorful neon lights, stands a row of male waiters in black Tang costumes. Entering the bar, you see the bar managers also in black Tang costumes greeting you. Who is Harry Potter, who is Spiderman, and who is the devil is up to your own arrangement.

Suggestion: At this time, the bar's social charm can also be displayed (that is, when the service staff appears, if you fancy someone, you befriend them), and everyone parties together. The host interacts with the guests through playful banter, which can be quite outlandish, such as "Where do you most want to spend the night tonight?" or "Choose a man here to kiss."

Highlight: This method of carnival requires a friend with strong sacrificial spirit, he is the choice for the devil. Because in this scenario, the devil is one who persuades others to drink and drinks heavily himself.

Carnival Plan Three: Fairy Tale + Dice

Carnival Index:

Features: Dice are the elements that heat up the atmosphere and are indispensable props for a carnival. Additionally, hats, especially tall ones, are involved.

Carnival Details: This is a warm little house (the bar), with magicians (if possible, keep the costumes if borrowed, otherwise remove them) and charming brilliant decorations that sparkle and may momentarily confuse people, like entering a fairy tale world. All friends wear various colored tall hats, dressing up as cartoon characters, thus creating numerous handsome men and beautiful women dressed as cartoon characters. Exciting music plays, and feet move to the happy dance tunes, creating the liveliest atmosphere (you can discuss what kind of music is needed with the manager beforehand).

Suggestion: On such a carnival night, the boss must bring out all nearly 20 kinds of beer from all over the world, lined up neatly. The dice decide who picks up a bottle and drinks. However, the lucky one drinking gets to taste beer from over ten different countries in one night, where else can you find such an opportunity?

Highlight: Speaking of highlights, it actually means finding another person among this group willing to endure everyone's punishment. Here, a person skilled in controlling the dice is needed; whoever they want to drink, they make drink.
