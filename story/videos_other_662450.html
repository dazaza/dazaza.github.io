Market Research in the Leather Goods Industry in Late August

The interviews with dealers in late August have started again. Last time, many dealers predicted that business would improve in late August. However, after my thorough investigation, it seems that good news hasn't arrived as expected, and everyone is still in a tough period. But I want to say: "Although the road ahead is a bit confusing, we will bless you with smiles. Even though there's wind and rain, you must bravely move forward. Cheer up!"

Generally, the market starts to pick up in late August.

Mr. Yu Jin Kang, a leather industry expert from Wenzhou, has been in the leather business for six or seven years. He used to do lighting and computer businesses until he founded his leather factory and found the right path. When talking about the labor issue, he said that his factory doesn't have the problem of "labor shortage", and production is normal. Currently, he has two booths, one at the Yingfu Hardware Leather Market and the other around Jiahe Wanggang area. The store mainly deals in split leather, featuring animal patterns with bright colors, primarily used for women's bags. In terms of craftsmanship, embossed ones are more complicated in process, so they are more expensive than laminated ones. For example, if the same leather is laminated, it costs about 8 yuan per foot, while embossed ones can sell for 11 yuan per foot. Currently, the markets for handbags and bags are not doing well, directly affecting the leather industry. If it was last year, normally in July-August during the off-season, the market would start moving in late August. If the market does not improve in late August this year, then the leather industry as a whole will face some difficulties. So, if this happens, survival issues will become more serious. Many customers are currently in a wait-and-see state, so the customer base is unstable. My leather business includes both domestic and foreign sales, but domestic sales are relatively more this year. Guangzhou is the largest leather distribution center, which attracts customers from all over the country, so there is still room for development. There was also a rumor recently that Qianshi Market would undergo adjustments, seemingly converting the first and second floors of the leather section into a bag market, so many leather merchants have already moved to the Yingfu Hardware Leather Market.

Business is relatively slow, relying on old customers for support

— Mr. Li, Ocean Leather

Mr. Li, from Hebei, just came to Guangzhou last month. He frankly admitted that he doesn't know much about this industry, and the store mainly deals in split cowhide, with coffee and black being the best-selling colors, each color corresponding to different price levels. Mr. Li said: "Currently, the business is relatively slow, mainly supported by old customers, new customers just look around and leave. Besides, the shoe and luggage market conditions aren't good either, their orders have decreased, naturally making the leather business difficult."

Keep going and wait for the mall to get busier

— Mr. Li, Hao Lishi Hardware

"The business is so slow, I don't see a single stranger all day," when asked about how the business was going, the boss of Hao Lishi Hardware replied like this. Hao Lishi Hardware has its own factory, and the booth is mainly for customers to view samples, once an order comes in, it goes directly to the factory. The factory side has better business because there are customer supports. Mr. Li sighed: "If we rely on this booth, the business wouldn't be able to continue." He also revealed: "The booth has been operating here for two years, the business hasn't improved since the financial crisis. But the shop is self-purchased, so we will keep going, we will wait for the mall to get busier. Regarding economic forecasts, he said that the entire industry is currently in the off-season, and he cannot predict the economic trend. Anyway, things will improve when they do, but he doesn't know when."

Business will only improve in winter

— Mr. Li, Tai Xuan Leather Merchant

The owner of Tai Xuan Leather Merchant is from South Korea and has been in China doing leather business since 2004. The store mainly operates top-grain leather and split leather. Since there is no own factory, compared to booths with factories, the profit margin is smaller. Products are mainly sold to South Korea, of course, to other countries as well. Currently, the business is relatively dull, she estimates that business will only improve in winter. She also revealed that the business across the street belonging to people from Wenzhou is quite good, with many old customers, it seems that people from Wenzhou are really impressive, and we need to learn from them. In addition, besides Xin Hao Pan, there is also a booth at the Yingfu Hardware Market.

Hardware business is better before and after the New Year

— Miss Pan, Chun Rong Hardware

The overall industry situation is relatively sluggish, some stores closed earlier haven't reopened yet. Our store has been operating here for one year and eight months, mainly focusing on domestic sales, products are sold to cities within Guangdong province such as Guangzhou, Shenzhen, Dongguan, etc. Generally speaking, hardware business is better before and after the New Year. Now, merchants' confidence is insufficient, but leaving isn't good either, all are in a wait-and-see state. We rented here for four years, this mall still has advantages, such as convenient transportation. Now it's the off-season, some malls have held promotional activities to increase popularity. As a merchant, I hope our mall could have more promotional activities to boost sales. When asked whether they participated in trade fairs, Miss Pan said they haven't participated in such events.

In previous years, the market picked up in September

— Miss Liang, Ming Nuo Hardware

Ming Nuo Hardware mainly operates zinc alloy casting, hardware stamping, various furniture hardware, handbag hardware, decorative items, various hardware accessories. The booth mainly provides sample viewing, such as belt buckle, handbag hardware, and zipper head samples. She said the market situation isn't good, business in July-August is the slowest, the profit margin for a belt buckle is already very low, one could say "earning a few cents makes you smile." Now raw material prices are rising, labor costs have increased, and cash transactions are more common, directly increasing the factory's capital pressure, resulting in even smaller profit margins. Besides, she said that currently, the business is slow and relies on regular customers, there aren't many new customers. Regarding whether the market situation will improve, Miss Liang said it's hard to predict, according to past years, the market picks up in September. Miss Liang also introduced that there aren't many brands in the hardware industry, re-making molds requires significant investment, costing thousands of yuan, many factories can't bear the cost.

Journalist's Notes

Based on my observations during this period, although there are no obvious signs of widespread improvement in the leather goods industry, the precarious and difficult situation has eased compared to before. A leather boss said: The market has cycles, ups and downs alternate, so we should maintain a normal mindset, remain vigilant even in peace, and cultivate internal strength, which is the best weapon to deal with crises.
