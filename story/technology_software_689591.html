He worked so hard to send his son to go abroad to study, but the results were that his son ended up playing games and exporting them back to the domestic market. Originally, Miss Lu’s son had bad grades and no interest in studying, even stealing money from home, spending every day loitering in Internet cafes. It wasn’t until Miss Lu helped a neighbor look for their child in an Internet cafe that she found her own son looking pale-faced. Miss Lu's husband had passed away when their son was still in elementary school. When her son reached high school, Miss Lu heard about sending students abroad for studies and never expected that it would happen to her son too. But a few days before one afternoon, Ms. Lu was about to enter the living room when she saw her neighbor Mr. Zhang looking for his grandson. With a compassionate heart, she decided to help Mr. Zhang find his grandson. Since Mr. Zhang mentioned that his grandson loved going to Internet cafes, they decided to search there together. However, this almost left Miss Lu senseless when she unexpectedly spotted her own son in the corner of one of those cafes. After returning home, her son said nothing. It wasn't until a few days later, upon Miss Lu's insistence, that her son finally revealed the truth. It turned out that due to average academic performance and feeling inferior compared to other students who came from wealthier families, he sought solace in the virtual world of online gaming. Over time, his interest in studying waned, and his grades deteriorated into a mess. Facing impending graduation, Miss Lu’s son felt hopeless and decided to fake going abroad to study, only to return with a diploma. Unable to face his mother, he didn’t dare to go home and instead spent several days hiding in Internet cafes, indulging in all-night gaming sessions.
