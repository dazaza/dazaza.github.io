Recently, the town of Arongqi in Hulunbuir City, Inner Mongolia took measures to fund and pay in batches. They invested 2.4 million yuan to purchase a multi-functional aerial ladder fire truck with a maximum height of 32.5 meters and an operation radius reaching 19 meters, which has now been put into service.

The acquisition of the multi-functional aerial ladder fire truck effectively alleviated the pressure of insufficient equipment for fighting high-rise fires and rescuing trapped people, building a "ladder of life" for the people.

In recent years, Arongqi has actively promoted the socialization of fire prevention work, creating a good fire safety environment and promoting the healthy and orderly development of the economy and society throughout the region. The region focuses on serving the livelihood of the people. In response to the current reality of a sharp increase in the number of high-rise buildings in the jurisdiction and the existing fire-fighting equipment not being able to meet the needs of fighting high-rise fires, they purchased a multi-functional aerial ladder fire truck, filling the gap of having no "aerial ladder fire truck" in the region. This further optimizes the structure of fire-fighting equipment and plays a positive role in improving the technological content and modernization level of fire-fighting equipment to deal with high-rise building fires.

It is understood that the "multi-functional aerial ladder fire truck" uses an original imported Mercedes-Benz chassis, with a lifting arm reaching up to 32.5 meters, an operation radius reaching 19 meters, and an effective water cannon range of about 60 meters.

Hubei Jiangnan Dongfeng Special Purpose Vehicles Co., Ltd. is a designated production plant by the National Development and Reform Commission, the only factory in the "Capital of Special Purpose Vehicles" with qualifications from the Fire Bureau of the Ministry of Public Security to produce fire trucks, and a supporting modification plant of Dongfeng Motor Corporation. The main products produced and sold include fire trucks, tow trucks, aerial work platforms, wing-opening box transport vehicles, water sprinklers, fuel tankers, chemical liquid transport vehicles, flatbed transport vehicles, mobile power supply vehicles, garbage trucks, etc., with hundreds of varieties. All models have been listed in the national announcement directory, obtained ISO9001-2000 quality management system certification, and passed the CCC certification.
