"Secret War" is not just a pure espionage drama, more accurately, it should be considered a confidentiality-themed TV series. "Here, there is a comprehensive and in-depth display of the theft and counter-theft struggles in our current society, as well as a confidentiality awareness education for all citizens."

"Secret War" centers on the operation to destroy the attempt by foreign intelligence organizations to steal data from a synchronous orbit satellite under development in China - Starcom Six. Foreign intelligence organizations approach and threaten researchers, security personnel, and government officials related to the development of Starcom Six through means such as infiltration, subversion, inducement, deception, and kidnapping, setting up layer upon layer of puzzles and traps to steal satellite test data. Our side, composed of the Confidentiality Bureau, Security Bureau, and Public Security Bureau's joint investigation team, engages in thrilling confrontations with the foreign intelligence organization.

The use of modern high technology in espionage throughout the series will greatly impress the audience, such as stereoscopic real-time surveillance equipment, network tracking and monitoring, voice processing technology, mobile phone eavesdropping, various high-tech espionage devices, etc., making the audience feel the omnipresence and invisibility of modern espionage methods.

Plot Overview:

Captain Liu Zhijun of the Security Bureau's Reconnaissance Team, while leading his team in a sting operation against a spy codenamed "Leopard," unexpectedly discovers that what the other party intends to smuggle out are the design plans for the "Starcom Six" satellite. The Confidentiality Bureau receives instructions from the central authorities to cooperate with the Security Bureau and Public Security Bureau to form a special inspection team, with Wu Mei, Director of the Supervision Department of the Confidentiality Bureau, serving as the team leader. The inspection team conducts an investigation into the satellite research institution 322 Institute, but their work encounters numerous obstacles with little progress. Meanwhile, foreign spy organizations launch attacks on 322 Institute's network. With the help of Shen Weiguo and his son, the beleaguered inspection team successfully uncovers the spy Fang Yao hidden within the 322 Institute. Wu Mei originally intended to follow the leads to uncover the spy organization's behind-the-scenes commander "Silver Fox," but instead falls into "Silver Fox's" trap. Fang Yao is killed by the assassin "Butterfly," and the clues suddenly break off.

"Butterfly" escapes to Binyang, where the long-dormant spy Lin Xiaolian provides cover. Lin Xiaolian's mission is to infiltrate the Binyang Satellite Monitoring Experiment Base Station 727, but the arrival of the inspection team chasing "Butterfly" disrupts his plans. Spy Liu Peng dies in a car accident during the chase, and in a panic, Lin Xiaolian drags Secretary Tong Yifan of the Municipal Committee into the scheme. With Tong Yifan's help, "Butterfly" enters Station 727 but is discovered by Qin Feng of the Binyang Security Bureau. In a life-and-death confrontation, Qin Feng is unfortunately martyred, and "Butterfly" is also killed. Unable to bear the guilt, Tong Yifan voluntarily confesses. Lin Xiaolian, who was about to leave the country, is brought to justice.

With successive setbacks for the spy organization, "Silver Fox" has no choice but to make a final assault on Aerospace Institute No. 19. The security captain of Institute No. 19, Luo Bing, becomes a tool exploited by the spies. To capture "Silver Fox," Wu Mei sends Pang Jian undercover into Institute No. 19, quietly starting a covert war where each side infiltrates the other. After both of "Silver Fox's" sets of espionage plans fail, he kidnaps Wu Mei's daughter Tingting, threatening Chief Engineer Zhou Xu to exchange core secrets for her daughter's life. Faced with the threat to national interests and her daughter's safety, Wu Mei steps forward to engage "Silver Fox" in the final showdown...
