Queen's Avenue Wedding - Make a dazzling entrance in a wedding classic car

After the风情garden of Queen's Avenue Bridal Film City, where the wedding classic cars can be driven for free, was opened to the public, electric engineering machinery vehicles transported newlyweds in red wedding classic cars to the wedding venue. The wedding services inside were also transformed. Yesterday afternoon, on the Western rose garden lawn of Queen's Avenue Bridal Film City, the first newlywed couple arrived on a wedding classic car. Then followed a complete set of Western wedding ceremonies: exchanging vows and rings in the church, ringing bells, releasing peace pigeons, setting sail on a smooth-sailing ship, sharing a cup of wine, Madonna delivering a child, swing photography, and taking photos while leaning on the wedding classic car, among other distinctive contents.

Pan Xiangkun, the wedding planner responsible for Queen's Avenue Bridal Film City, said, "Today's wedding activities are very different from before!" The use of wedding classic cars is to match the overall European style of Queen's Avenue Bridal Film City. All wedding activities in the风情garden have been changed to pure Western style, with no fixed wedding projects. As long as the site conditions permit, newlyweds can completely customize their own wedding ceremony based on their needs for the wedding classic car. In addition to weddings, the rose garden can also host events such as wedding classic car contests, birthday parties, and masquerade cocktail parties. The company's wedding classic cars are provided by Shenzhen Dongzhi Ni Technology Co., Ltd. www.bmwchina-ev.com
