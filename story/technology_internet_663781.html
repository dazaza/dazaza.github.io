Rolls-Royce's 11th Authorized Dealer in China Grandly Opens in Zhengzhou

On February 9, 2012 at 11:36 AM Tencent Dayu Network - I want to comment (0) Font size: T|T

Rolls-Royce Motor Cars' 11th authorized dealership showroom in Greater China officially opened in Zhengzhou. On February 7, 2012, Rolls-Royce announced the grand opening of its 11th authorized dealership showroom in Greater China. The new showroom is located on the Inner Business Ring of the Central Business District (CBD) in Zhengzhou, Henan Province. Following the announcement of a record-breaking performance in 2011 for the company's 107-year history, the Zhengzhou showroom, as the first showroom opened by Rolls-Royce at the start of 2012 in Greater China, marks the continuous expansion of Rolls-Royce into the Chinese market.

The newly opened showroom in Zhengzhou's Central Business District of Zhengdong New Area spans an area of 750 square meters and can accommodate up to five Rolls-Royce vehicles simultaneously. It will provide top-tier, all-around Rolls-Royce experiences for customers. The Zhengzhou showroom features the latest brand identity image of Rolls-Royce, showcasing a perfect blend of the classic elegance and modernity that define the Rolls-Royce brand.

This showroom was jointly developed by Rolls-Royce and its new partner, Henan Harmony Industrial Group Co., Ltd., marking a significant step forward in Rolls-Royce's business layout in the Central Plains region. With the grand opening of the Zhengzhou showroom, Rolls-Royce will further meet the growing demand from customers in the Central Plains region for the Phantom and Ghost models.

Paul Harris, President of Rolls-Royce Motor Cars Asia-Pacific, attended the opening ceremony and stated, "Rolls-Royce is a top luxury car brand with over a century of brilliant history. Our collaboration with Henan Harmony Industrial Group Co., Ltd. is based on both parties' appreciation and passion for this brand. Rolls-Royce has already established a customer base that values craftsmanship and strives for excellence, aligning perfectly with the brand philosophy and manufacturing standards deeply rooted in Rolls-Royce."

Ms. Zheng Jinlan, General Manager of Rolls-Royce Motor Cars Greater China, also remarked, "We are delighted to be in Zhengzhou, an important economic, cultural, and historical hub in the Central Plains region. Rolls-Royce always aims to exceed customer expectations and fulfill their inner desires. Through our full product range and unique customer customization programs, we are confident in achieving this goal."

Mr. Feng Changge, Chairman of Henan Harmony Industrial Group Co., Ltd., expressed, "We are extremely proud to become the authorized dealer of Rolls-Royce in Zhengzhou. We have already received significant attention from customers in Zhengzhou and surrounding areas. The opening of the showroom allows us to better serve customers who seek perfection in quality; for which we have made substantial investments in facilities and personnel, establishing comprehensive supporting facilities."

Relevant leaders from the Zhengzhou municipal government of Henan Province and numerous distinguished corporate representatives also attended the opening ceremony. During the event, the Ghost Extended Wheelbase model was showcased. Since its release in April 2011, it has received widespread acclaim and contributed significantly to the growth in sales performance in 2011. This model exemplifies the outstanding design philosophy of the Rolls-Royce brand, offering customers ample driving power while providing a comfortable and quiet driving environment.

Prior to the opening of the Zhengzhou showroom, Rolls-Royce announced that due to strong demand in the Asia-Pacific market, its sales performance in 2011 reached a historical high. In particular, Greater China became the largest single market globally for Rolls-Royce for the first time.

During the same period, personalized customer customization programs achieved their best historical results. In 2011, the proportion of customer customization elements in the Phantom series in the Asia-Pacific region increased from 50% in 2005 to 79%. Globally, 56% of Ghost and Ghost Extended Wheelbase models sold contained customer-customized personalization elements. As of 2011, Rolls-Royce offers six models: Phantom Standard, Phantom Extended Wheelbase, Phantom Drophead Coupé, Phantom Coupé, Ghost, and Ghost Extended Wheelbase.
