Not long ago, Heilongjiang Province Yuquan Liquor Co., Ltd. got back three Chinese domains indicating the brand of the company, namely, China Yuquan Liquor.CC, China Yuquan Liquor.COM and China Yuquan.Company after arduous network domain name rights protection. Experts indicated that this reflected on one hand the awakening of brand protection consciousness of domestic enterprises, and on the other hand it also showed the unique identifiability and importance of Chinese domains, especially new top level domains such as .CC which were becoming hot spots for domain registration and enterprises should protect them in time.

It was understood that this was not the first case of domestic domain infringement. International large enterprises such as LG, Yahoo and Pfizer had taken legal rights protection actions because related domains had been squatted. Domains of well-known domestic brands such as Yellow Crane Tower and Huangshanghuang had also frequently been squatted. In respect to this, the person in charge of authoritative domestic domain registration service provider Times Interconnect (www.now.cn) indicated that according to international general rules, domain registration followed the principle of "whoever registers first gets it", once an enterprise domain was registered by someone else, the enterprise could only get back the domain through arbitration, litigation or high-priced repurchase. Industry insiders said that timely registration of domains related to their own brands by enterprises was the most effective way of protection. As the abbreviation of commercial company (Commercial Company) and Chinese company (China Company), .CC domain was deeply favored by all kinds of enterprises with its simple, easy-to-remember, eye-catching and very practical features and had become a new hot spot for enterprise domain registration.

According to investigation findings, network marketing had already become a promotion method very popular among enterprises these days. A textile company in Wuhan registered five categories of Chinese domains including "Asia Yarn.CC" at one time and took the lead in the industry. In addition, a Wenzhou company engaged in screws, nuts and other fasteners successfully revived the entire company through domain registration and product network promotion. Quite a lot of customers went straight to the enterprise website via the network and came voluntarily. Later, the company successively registered all product names of all categories produced by the company and the accumulated quantity exceeded 500, weaving a huge and comprehensive marketing net. The relevant leader of the company said: "Compared to four years ago, our business has increased fifty times now."

Website-based network marketing by small and medium-sized enterprises has already been very popular. However, small and medium-sized enterprises registering such a large quantity of domains triggered extensive attention and discussion within the industry because of such strong domain brand protection awareness and application awareness. The person in charge of Times Interconnect believed that domain was an important address resource on the Internet, having the characteristics of uniqueness and scarcity. On one hand, the "one-to-one" characteristic made a universal URL correspond to only one website or web page, enjoyed exclusively by registrants. Once a domain was registered, the enterprise would be unable to obtain it. On the other hand, due to the scarcity of domains, whoever preempted excellent resources first meant occupying the network initiative. It was learned that in order to encourage and advocate domestic small and medium-sized enterprises to build websites and protect brands, Times Interconnect recently implemented overall preferential treatment for .CC domains.

Authoritative persons said that network marketing consciousness of domestic small and medium-sized enterprises was gradually awakening, enterprise informationization process was continuously speeding up, preempting popular domain resources first and establishing websites to carry out marketing was conducive to preempting the network initiative.
