NO.2 Sweet Potato

Slimming Principle: The nutritional components of sweet potatoes are quite rich, with a very high content of dietary fiber. Eating them regularly can stimulate bowel movements. Additionally, sweet potatoes contain abundant oligosaccharides that help in the growth of probiotics, beneficial for the health of the large intestine and preventing weight regain after dieting. The water-soluble fiber they contain can maintain the elasticity and smoothness of blood vessels, ultimately leading to slimming effects.

Consumption Method:

1. Steamed Sweet Potato: If you want to eat sweet potatoes to increase bowel movements and reduce your waistline, it's best to steam them. Clean the skin thoroughly with a brush and steam them in an electric pot.

2. Sweet Potato Porridge: You can mix sweet potatoes with white rice and add some water, cooking it into sweet potato porridge. This is highly effective for healthy waist slimming and is one of the most effective weight loss products.

3. Waist Slimming Index: ★★ (Five ★ being the highest)

NO.3 Apple

Waist Slimming Principle: Apples contain a special type of pectin component. Pectin is a soluble fiber that swells when it meets water. Therefore, eating apples along with drinking water will give a strong sense of fullness. Plus, apples are rich in fiber, which can promote bowel movements and greatly assist in defecation, ultimately leading to waist slimming and weight loss effects.

Consumption Method:

1. Eating Whole: Eating the entire apple allows for better intake of pectin components, enhancing the effect of promoting bowel movements. Since apple peels contain a lot of coarse fiber, it's best to wash the peel thoroughly and eat the apple with the skin on.

2. Salad: Cut the apple into small cubes, add yogurt or buttermilk, and mix it into a salad. It's recommended to choose sugar-free or low-sugar yogurt or buttermilk to avoid consuming too many calories.
