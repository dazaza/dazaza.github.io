Benefits of Product Appearance Modeling Design to Enterprises:

1. Under original production conditions, by changing the product appearance modeling, new varieties can be developed and sold at good prices.

2. It helps sales significantly. In today's market, many competitive products exist in the same category. The first thing that catches the consumer's eye is the appearance design. According to estimates, for every dollar invested in industrial product appearance, it can bring a return of 1500 dollars. Hitachi's data from Japan is even more convincing; for every additional 1 billion yen in sales revenue, the role of industrial design accounts for 51%, while equipment transformation only accounts for 12%. Moreover, during economic crises, companies must develop new products to avoid elimination.

Many enterprises have realized the importance of product design and have established specialized development institutions, recruiting design talents with high salaries. Xinhua News conducted an employment survey and found that industrial designers are currently in great demand, with an annual salary of 200,000 RMB being the industry standard. For those with 5-8 years of professional experience, their annual salary often exceeds 500,000 RMB. As for industrial design directors, their annual salary could exceed one million RMB. Newly-employed graduates in industrial design also earn substantial incomes. According to our understanding, graduates from Donghua University's Design Art specialty start with significantly higher salaries than other majors, and after working for two or three years, their income increases further. The intense competition in the fashionable electronics industry has made design talent highly sought after.
