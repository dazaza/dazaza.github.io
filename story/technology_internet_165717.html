Web servers residing in Web browsers are taking the web into our own hands

by gnrsu on 2009-06-23 22:53:11

Opera Unite is an online collaboration technology that allows you to share your data - such as files, photos or music - directly with others using your computer. Opera Unite technology is based on simple and sophisticated server programs in the Opera browser to share data and services.

With Opera Unite, you no longer have to spend time uploading what you need to share. Share content directly on your own hard drive without the need for a third-party shared server.

Opera Unite radically expands the scope of your online activities. Opera Unite leverages today's advanced hardware and smooth connectivity to give everyone the power to define the future of the web.
