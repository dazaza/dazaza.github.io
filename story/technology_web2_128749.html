Tweetsum: Manage your Twitter Followers in bulk

by watsonxu on 2009-03-04 22:03:43

Twitter is a very good communication tool, it is very convenient to use a variety of ways to communicate with your friends, but the use of Twitter also has trouble, that is, after too many people Follow you, you do not know whether to Follow Back. Since Twitter itself does not provide bulk management for Followers, it is difficult to determine at one time whether these people deserve to be followed Back. Today's Tweetsum will solve this problem. The slogan of Tweetsum is "Digest your followers." With Tweetsum, you can organize your Followers on a single page and easily Follow Back or Unfollow them.
