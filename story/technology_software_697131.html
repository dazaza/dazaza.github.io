Latest注意事项 for Wedding Room Renovation - Suzhou Wedding Planning

by wang08073q2 on 2012-03-08 13:25:28

When it comes to the age of marriage, many post-80s choose to get married in 2012. Marriage is one of the happiest things. From getting to know each other to entering the hall of marriage, for young people who are about to enter this hall of happiness, the biggest challenge they face is how to work together to decorate a house that belongs to them as a matrimonial home. Below is a brief introduction to the matters needing attention in the design and decoration of the matrimonial home.

Save effort and money

Many couples-to-be have set their wedding date at the end of this year, so from the time perspective, it's now urgent to start the decoration. Because preparing for the wedding is a very complex and trivial matter, the couples hardly have time to run around the market, buy materials, and supervise the project themselves. Even if they take leave to decorate, all the hustle and bustle will inevitably make them look tired and feel exhausted. If the decoration project is handed over to the parents at home, it may tire out the elderly, so designer Tian Xiaoming suggests that everyone had better choose a reputable home decoration company to decorate the new house. As long as the responsibilities of both parties are clearly stated in the contract, then you can wait for the inspection.

Light decoration, heavy on embellishment

The overall design of the bridal chamber should reflect a festive atmosphere, which is best achieved through furniture placement and soft furnishings. Tian Xiaoming told reporters that compared with previous years, this year half of the consumers in the home decoration market who are preparing to get married prefer modern minimalist styles, among which European country style is deeply favored.

In the process of decorating the bridal chamber, it is best to focus lightly on construction and heavily on decoration. Materials containing harmful substances such as wood and glue should be used as little as possible to reduce toxic gas pollution in the new house. In general, soft furnishings can play a big role in decorating the bridal chamber. For example, red gauze curtains, crystal bead curtains, lace, flowers, checkered fabrics, etc., are all excellent tools for dressing up the bridal chamber and creating an atmosphere, and they are more convenient for future flexible changes.

Environmental protection and healthy bridal chamber

The pollution of the bridal chamber mainly manifests in air pollution, where formaldehyde and benzene exceedance are the most harmful. Although the environmental performance of various building materials has improved compared to the past, due to the fixed wedding date, newlyweds usually move into the new house quickly, making it hard for indoor polluted air to completely dissipate. Moreover, before the wedding, the bride and groom are busy preparing for the wedding, not resting well, and their physical condition is relatively poor, making them more susceptible to harmful substances.

According to the introduction, the construction period for general family decoration is two months, which is also the process of material releasing pollutants. However, after the decoration is completed, ventilation must be ensured to eliminate harmful gases. It’s best to frequently ventilate after the decoration. After a comprehensive treatment and inspection of indoor air by a testing agency, only then should one move in.

Showcasing individuality

The bridal chamber, as the house where the newlyweds get married, whether it's a new or second-hand house, decorating it into various styles has become an indispensable highlight of marriage. Traditionally, the colors of the bridal chamber often use warm tones like bright red. Bright red and purple are traditional celebratory colors in China. Even if the whole style does not include warm tones during the decoration process, the final accessories would still select corresponding large red carpets or red lampshades to create a strong newlywed atmosphere. Using cold tones like blue and black is a big taboo in bridal chamber decoration. This is what is traditionally known as the "bridal chamber."

However, in this era of showcasing individuality, how to break conventions and display individuality has gradually been integrated into the decoration of the bridal chamber. Nowadays, more and more young people like unique styles, and being unconventional has become a fashion in bridal chamber decoration. Many newlyweds have changed their previous thinking patterns, and their requirements for function and style are often unexpected. Some previously encountered owners of bridal chambers believed that the large chandeliers in traditional living rooms were rather vulgar. Every home without exception decorates with large chandeliers, unable to create a romantic atmosphere throughout the room. Thus, they bought several large floor lamps placed in different corners of the living room, and the originally banned black color became very popular, while the supposedly festive warm colors were not used at all.

Unconventional bridal chamber decoration, mostly for post-80s

Statistics show that most owners of unconventional bridal chamber decorations are from the post-80s generation. These mostly pampered single children born in the 1980s are more emotional than rational when it comes to decoration, and pursuing individuality has become the main theme. The requirements for the kitchen are not very important, and the distinction between room functions is relatively vague. They don't even consider baby rooms... But they will definitely choose the latest decorative materials, and they will definitely put effort into lighting, even if it's just buying a small ornament, they will carefully find the one that best suits their personality.

For the decoration of the bridal chamber, perhaps everyone's understanding is different, but do you have enough courage to try an "unconventional" bridal chamber decoration? Industry insiders remind consumers that bridal chamber decoration varies from person to person, and one cannot blindly follow trends nor purely pursue individuality. Practical functions still cannot be lost, and what suits oneself is the best.

The overall feeling of the room decoration is the most important

Firstly, the space size, light, and surrounding environment should be comprehensively considered, and the room should be designed as a whole. There should be an overall sense, the decoration and furniture styles should be unified, and the colors should be coordinated. To reflect the festive atmosphere of the new marriage, there can be some bright, vivid colors locally. Big red "double happiness" characters and wedding photos are indispensable and can be hung in prominent positions. But the newlywed room needs to be considered for long-term use, so generally, the tone should be fresh and elegant, making people feel comfortable and relaxed.

If you already have your own furniture and only need to purchase a few more items, you should adhere to the principle of harmony. Either modify the existing furniture, re-adjust its color to match the furniture you want to buy; or when purchasing new furniture, try to make it match the existing furniture as much as possible. Doing so well follows the principle of "diversity within unity" in form beauty.

Furniture that fits is the best

The new home must be decorated harmoniously, warmly, and uniformly in style to provide a good environment for married life. Furniture should not only have a unified style but must also be adapted to the conditions of the house itself to achieve the best effect. Therefore, when purchasing or making new home furniture, it is necessary to fully measure and analyze the housing conditions for selection. There are quite a few factors to consider, including the size of the room, lighting, and the owner's living habits.

Skillful color matching makes the bridal chamber lively and festive

The bedroom is also the bridal chamber, the center of the newlywed family, and a place highly valued by newlywed couples. Its decoration should reflect a festive, warm, and romantic atmosphere. Red is the main color of traditional Chinese weddings, so Chinese people also like to use celebratory red to adorn the new house. Red bedding, red curtains, etc., on the day of the wedding, rely on these decorations to create a festive atmosphere. Curtains can be double-layered, thin outside and thick inside, both blocking the view and maintaining the room temperature without affecting daytime rest. The pattern and color selection of curtains, bed covers, sofa covers, and other textiles should be coordinated. Adding appropriate personalized decorations makes the interior full of festive and romantic atmosphere, reflecting the owner's pursuit of a beautiful life.

The decoration of the newlywed home needs to have long-term consideration, be easy to replace, easy to clean, and consider the future living environment of the children, leaving enough activity space for the children. In addition, clutter in the room should be avoided. Many newlyweds prepare a lot of clothes and daily necessities, along with gifts from relatives and friends, various ornaments, tea sets, wine glasses, etc. These things should be placed in coordination with their home environment, and not all displayed at once, making the already crowded room seem even narrower. Not only will it fail to serve a decorative purpose, but it will also ruin the indoor atmosphere.
