The three smiles of the principal are easy to understand, but the three cries of the teachers are confusing.

by dongshan on 2008-11-01 14:35:28

Nowadays, the benefits in the education system are getting higher and higher, much better than other social classes. Recently, a satirical poem has caused a great stir, named "The Principal's Three Smiles and the Teachers' Three Cries". The following is an excerpt from the original text.

"The Email Poem" Original Text of "The Principal's Three Smiles and the Teachers' Three Cries"

Principal (Smile) No. 1 smiles at his high annual salary, with 200,000 to 300,000 yuan going into his pocket. Although he didn't get promoted, his money-making skills are extraordinary.

Principal (Smile) No. 2 smiles at his car allowance, with no less than 30,000 yuan every year. As long as there's money, he'll grab it, and the more he "laughs," the more energy he has.

Principal (Smile) No. 3 smiles without using his brain, with excellent skills in flattery. Despite losing tens of millions, he still keeps his position.

Due to forwarding a satirical poem related to teachers downloaded from the Internet to other teachers in the school, Mr. Zhang from a school in Shijingshan District received severe punishment from the school. Mr. Zhang believes that he cannot accept the school's punishment, so he sued the school in court.

In the complaint, Mr. Zhang stated that in mid-January this year, he saw a satirical poem titled "The Principal's Three Smiles and the Teachers' Three Cries" online. He then downloaded and forwarded the poem to some colleagues' emails. The school, through the internal security bureau investigation, learned about this matter and determined that Mr. Zhang's actions constituted misusing others' accounts to release inappropriate information, causing adverse effects on the school. After research, it was decided to give Mr. Zhang an administrative reprimand and withhold four months of performance-based wages, totaling RMB 14,164. Mr. Zhang不服 (does not accept) the school's handling and has filed a lawsuit with the court, requesting the court to revoke the administrative reprimand from the school and return the withheld salary of RMB 14,164.
