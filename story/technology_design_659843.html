by anonymous on 2012-02-06 21:42:00

Women's fashion information: from harem pants to khakis. By now, you should have bought a pair of harem pants. At first, everyone was uncomfortable with the balloon-like pant legs and abrupt gathered cuffs, but the thing is, whoever adapts first, is fashionable. Fortunately, harem pants can never become too widespread; only a few celebrities dare to try them out. Roisin Murphy is one of them, and she chose an extremely voluminous style, paired with a casually draped black top, and astutely used striking gold accessories (neckline, bracelet, sandals) to elevate her look.

Sienna Miller paired white harem pants with a black neutral suit and gray vest, a low-key yet sleek choice that taller girls can emulate. Amber Heard's taste needs no explanation, as she has never disappointed us every time she appears on camera. This time, she dressed in all-black wide-leg pants, especially the jumpsuit-style wide-leg trousers, which exuded a sense of calm elegance, looking very beautiful.

If you haven't tried harem pants yet, don't worry. As the weather gets even hotter, celebrities have already switched to loose and natural khaki pants. Unlike harem pants, khakis are originally a popular item that anyone can pull off, and it doesn't matter if they become widespread. Kylie Minogue was recently spotted wearing beige khaki pants and a striped T-shirt, still paired with golden fish-mouth high heels, revealing bright red toenails, making it impossible for people to accuse her of being unkempt.

Let time and warmth "add points" together. Enjoy the warm experience brought by INMAN women's cotton clothing, where your body feels free and relaxed.

From this, we can see that the key to wearing khaki pants is to roll up the pant legs below the knees and pair them with a stylish but not flashy pair of good shoes. Kristen Bell wore khaki pants to attend the opening ceremony of Armani Exchange, and with the help of a luxurious long necklace and avant-garde black toe-exposing shoe boots, she didn't lose any dignity.

And Rihanna’s outfit of fitted shiny fabric khaki pants paired with Gucci's ultra-high golden sandals and leopard-print shirt makes us believe that this old military uniform has completely transformed.
