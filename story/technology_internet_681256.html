Our country has comprehensively launched integrity management for infant milk powder enterprises

by childrenstory126 on 2012-02-28 12:10:57

At the recent national conference on the establishment of an integrity management system for infant formula manufacturers convened by the Ministry of Industry and Information Technology (MIIT), 124 infant milk powder companies across the country jointly signed an integrity declaration at the meeting, announcing that they would all establish integrity management systems.

According to the "2011 Work Implementation Plan for the Construction of Integrity System in Food Industrial Enterprises" led by MIIT, infant formula manufacturers must establish and implement an integrity management system at a rate of 100%, strictly controlling seven aspects of integrity: dairy sources, raw (auxiliary) materials, production, quality, circulation, finance, and personnel, integrating and permeating the principle of integrity into the company's business philosophy and all business activities.

Gao Fu, Deputy Inspector of the Consumer Goods Industry Department of MIIT, reported on the situation of the integrity construction work of food industrial enterprises since last year, pointing out that China's dairy industry as a whole shows a continuous development trend, but there are still some problems in terms of quality and safety. These issues include: some enterprises having a weak sense of integrity, adulteration, counterfeiting, and adding non-edible substances and other illegal acts still occur from time to time; the popularization rate of self-built dairy farms is relatively low, some areas have not strictly implemented industrial policies, enterprises develop blindly, resulting in relatively excessive capacity, and the momentum of disorderly market competition has yet to be curbed; consumers' confidence in domestic milk powder has not been restored, and they still have concerns about the quality and safety of milk powder, with many mainland tourists purchasing large quantities of infant milk powder abroad and in Hong Kong and Macau and bringing them back into the country.

More than a hundred participating companies made eight commitments to society: [adhere to lawful operation, promote the concept of integrity, practice integrity promises, ensure product quality, fulfill social responsibilities, maintain industry order, strengthen industry self-discipline, and accept social supervision]. A representative of Oushimengniu stated that as a newly established joint venture brand of Mengniu, the company not only introduced the technological strength of Oushi Arla Foods but also established a high-standard quality monitoring system. From dairy source management, raw milk procurement, ingredients, production, packaging, inspection to transportation, there are 217 monitoring points set up throughout the entire production and sales system, achieving 360-degree layer-by-layer control to ensure that Chinese babies truly enjoy international-quality infant formula.

It was learned that the food industry nationwide will accelerate the advancement of enterprise integrity system construction, expanding the pilot program from dairy products to enterprises in the seasoning, wine, canned food, and beverage industries based on the dairy product integrity pilot.
