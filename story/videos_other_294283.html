Energy Shortage: A Constant Warning Bell, Straw Gasifiers Step Up

The issue of energy shortage is no longer an empty statement; it is approaching us, and the warning bells are ringing incessantly. It is imperative to prepare for the future by substituting renewable energy sources for non-renewable ones. The central government has issued relevant documents, strongly supporting enterprises engaged in the research and development of new energy. Beijing Zhongke Jiamai Technology Development Co., Ltd. actively responded to the Party's call, collaborating with various prestigious universities and organizing a large number of professional scientific and technological personnel specializing in the research and development of new energy sources. After long-term market research and development, they successfully developed a series of gasifiers (http://www.zkjm168.com), including: "composite material gasifiers, direct combustion energy-saving stoves, general combustion type and infrared straw gasifiers, heating-type straw gasifiers, commercial multifunctional straw gasifiers," gaining praise from peers within the industry.

Zhongke Jiamai's straw gasifier (http://www.zkjm168.com) uses biomass as raw material, including: rice straw, cotton stalks, wheat straw, bran, corn stalks, sorghum stalks, rice husks, fruit shells, tree branches, sawdust, wood shavings, weeds, and various plant vines, stems, and leaves; waste residues from pharmaceutical factories, sugar factories, and tobacco factories. Straw used to produce biomass gas is a renewable resource, easily obtainable, with almost zero-cost raw materials. It can be used for boiling water, cooking, providing heat, and bathing, making it suitable for various gas needs such as restaurants and canteens.

The installation of the stove utilizes ordinary pipelines, without the need for steel bottles, ensuring safe and reliable combustion (no pressure). A unique secondary filtration device ensures that gas burns cleanly without blackening pots or emitting smoke, with no dust emissions, and CO emissions below 0.1. When in use, simply add agricultural crop straw directly into the furnace, and within 1 to 3 minutes, clean gas will be produced. Only 3-5 kilograms of straw per day are needed to meet daily requirements, saving over 70% compared to traditional wood-burning stoves.

In application, electronic ignition is used, allowing for instant lighting when needed, with fierce flames adjustable in size, producing blue flames and temperatures exceeding 900°C, comparable to liquefied gas. A cigarette butt or a piece of newspaper can ignite it, and once lit, it can be used continuously for up to 72 hours.

The straw gasifier adopts advanced gas purification technology and high-temperature pyrolysis gasification technology under no pressure, with scientifically sound gas production techniques leading domestically. The product is reliable in quality, technologically advanced, clean, hygienic, energy-saving, and environmentally friendly. Each new type of gas stove retails for 150-360 yuan, ready to use upon purchase, without the need for additional facilities, convenient and cost-effective, making it accessible to most families.

The awareness of the energy crisis has deeply penetrated everyone's mind, and conserving energy is now essential. The phenomenon of living off our ancestors' resources while cutting off future generations' livelihood no longer exists. Due to the heightened public awareness, since its launch on the market, the straw gasifier (http://www.zkjm168.com) has become a leader in the industry thanks to its safety, environmental friendliness, and ability to utilize renewable resources.
