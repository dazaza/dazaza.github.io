Who is right and who is wrong?

Mo Ran was arranged by the company to go on a business trip for a week, but due to an accident, her journey ended earlier. She wanted to give her boyfriend Gu Chen a surprise, so she did not tell him that she would return early. On the way home from the airport in a taxi, Mo Ran was imagining how surprised Gu Chen would be when he saw her back early.

Mo Ran always felt that she was happy. Gu Chen was her brother's high school classmate, five years older than her. The first time Mo Ran met Gu Chen, she was sixteen years old, in high school, and Gu Chen was in university. Because it was summer vacation, Gu Chen came to visit Mo Ran's brother, and Mo Ran fell in love with Gu Chen at first sight during that meeting.

After Mo Ran graduated from university, she entered Gu Chen's company through his connections, became his colleague, and also became Gu Chen's girlfriend.

The first time Mo Ran brought Gu Chen home as her boyfriend, her mother and brother tacitly approved, but her father strongly opposed it, saying that Gu Chen was unsuitable for her and was afraid she would be hurt in the future. But Mo Ran didn't care; she said she had loved Gu Chen for six years and wouldn't give up easily.

Seeing his daughter's persistence, Mo Ran's father eventually accepted it. After meeting both sets of parents, they planned to get engaged on Mo Ran's birthday in October.

That day, there was exactly one and a half months until their engagement. However, Mo Ran could never have dreamed that all her happiness would come to an abrupt end that day.

As the car drove into the residential area where Mo Ran and Gu Chen lived, Mo Ran's right eyelid started twitching when she got out of the car. Thinking about seeing her beloved boyfriend soon, her steps became lighter.

When Mo Ran quietly opened the door with her key, the room was pitch black. She tiptoed into the bedroom, and when she turned on the light, the scene before her eyes became a nightmare for her life.

There were clearly two people lying in the bed. When the light came on, the two people woke up from their sleep and immediately sat up in bed. These two people were the man Mo Ran had loved for six years, and her high school to university classmate and close friend.

Mo Ran couldn't believe what she was seeing, and the bag in her hand fell to the ground. When she bit her lips until they bled and felt the pain, she realized she wasn't having a nightmare, and she rushed out of the room.

Mo Ran didn't know how long she had run, and then she fell to the ground. She lay there crying. In the cold night of late autumn, it was colder outside, but not as cold as Mo Ran's heart inside. The happiness that existed in the previous second turned out to be an illusion in the next second.

Mo Ran spent the whole night in the nearby park, shedding tears all night. She didn't know how to face this sudden turn of events. She couldn't understand why the two people she trusted so much could betray her.

The next morning, Mo Ran returned to the house where she had lived with Gu Chen for more than a year. Gu Chen wasn't at home. Mo Ran silently packed her things, tears streaming down her face while she packed. The sweet days she had spent with Gu Chen flashed through her mind.

Afterward, Mo Ran went to resign from the company. Coming out of the company, the sunlight outside was blinding. The couples embracing each other on the street pierced Mo Ran's heart painfully.

Before Gu Chen returned, Mo Ran left a letter and took all her belongings away. In the letter, Mo Ran wrote the two words "breakup."

After walking out of that house, Mo Ran felt like she had nothing left—love, friendship, work, all gone. And her family, who had agreed to the engagement in one and a half months, now suddenly broke up. Mo Ran didn't know how to explain it to her family.

In the following days, Mo Ran stayed at a girlfriend's place. Every day, she locked herself in the room, crying herself to sleep and waking up crying again. In just a few short days, she lost weight, her complexion became pale, and she often didn't eat. Sometimes, her friends forced her to eat, but after eating, she would vomit. Mo Ran thought she would die like this.

On the fifteenth day after breaking up with Gu Chen, her friend went to work, and Mo Ran changed into a set of clothes, going to the supermarket to buy some daily necessities. While checking out, she heard someone call her name. Turning around, a bespectacled man smiled at her.

This man was Lu Siyang, Mo Ran's high school classmate and one of her suitors. At that time, Mo Ran only had eyes for Gu Chen and couldn't accommodate anyone else. Later, they went to different universities and lost contact. She didn't expect to meet him in the same city.

When Lu Siyang and Mo Ran were drinking coffee at Shangdao, Lu Siyang asked if Mo Ran had already married Gu Chen. This question made Mo Ran burst into uncontrollable tears. Seeing Mo Ran cry so bitterly, Lu Siyang was at a loss.

Lu Siyang was the first girl he had fallen in love with. Back then, Mo Ran was the school-acknowledged beauty queen. Even after entering university, Lu Siyang tried to find Mo Ran's contact information but failed.

After many years without seeing each other, the woman in front of him looked so haggard. Lu Siyang didn't know how to comfort the girl he once deeply loved.

Since that day, Lu Siyang frequently visited Mo Ran. He even took leave from work to accompany her on trips, trying every means to make the girl he loved happy. They went to Lijiang. In front of a stall selling small accessories, Lu Siyang bought a butterfly hair clip for Mo Ran. Mo Ran said, "If I die, will I turn into a butterfly?"

Lu Siyang replied, "I will warm you with my love; I won't let you die." As he spoke, his eyes were filled with deep affection.

After returning from Lijiang, Mo Ran's mood improved significantly, and she started a new job.

Every morning, Lu Siyang would drive to pick up Mo Ran, send her to work first, then go to his company. In the evening, he waited for her under her office building. Together, they had dinner, watched movies, shopped, and passed by a photo booth where Lu Siyang pulled Mo Ran in and had a blast taking pictures.

On Mo Ran's birthday, Lu Siyang bought flowers and a cake and waited for her at the agreed restaurant, but she never showed up. Her phone was off. When he found her, she was drunk in a bar, tears streaming down her face, repeatedly asking why.

The next morning, when Mo Ran woke up, she saw Lu Siyang holding a bouquet of roses, smiling at her and saying, "Let me love you."

Mo Ran smiled and nodded, and the two happily embraced.

In the cold winter, the sky began its first snowfall. In the snow, Lu Siyang knelt down on one knee and proposed to Mo Ran with a ring.

Their wedding date was set for the following spring. Mo Ran looked forward to becoming Lu Siyang's most beautiful bride on that warm, blooming day.

That day, Lu Siyang held Mo Ran's hand and went to a wedding photography studio called "True Love Forever" to take wedding photos. The shoulder-exposing white wedding dress looked like an angel on Mo Ran. Coming out of the dressing room, Lu Siyang looked at his beautiful bride-to-be and smiled foolishly.

The phone rang, Lu Siyang answered it, and his face immediately turned pale. Before Mo Ran knew what happened, Lu Siyang ran out.

The wedding photos weren't completed, and Mo Ran went home alone by taxi.

In the following days, Lu Siyang never came back. Mo Ran's calls to him were always unanswered, and those days, Mo Ran was disoriented, worried that something might have happened to Lu Siyang.

On the eighth day of Lu Siyang's disappearance, at nearly ten o'clock in the evening, Mo Ran's phone rang. Seeing "Siyang" displayed on her phone, Mo Ran's hands trembled with excitement as she picked up the call and kept asking where Lu Siyang had been these days. However, Lu Siyang remained silent on the other end of the line.

Lu Siyang asked Mo Ran to wait for him at home, saying he would be back soon and had something to tell her.

Half an hour later, Lu Siyang returned. In just a few days, he had lost a lot of weight and looked very tired.

Mo Ran poured him a glass of water, and the two sat facing each other. Lu Siyang began telling Mo Ran about the reason for his sudden departure and disappearance those few days.

Before this chance encounter with Mo Ran, Lu Siyang had a girlfriend with whom he had been in a relationship for two years. However, after meeting Mo Ran, Lu Siyang realized that he had actually never forgotten her over the years. So, he decided to break up with his girlfriend and pursue the girl he truly loved. But he didn't know how to bring up the topic with his girlfriend and procrastinated until the day before he decided to propose to Mo Ran.

However, unexpectedly, the girl hung herself at home on the morning they went to take wedding photos. She was later discovered by her neighbors, rescued, and sent to the hospital. Lu Siyang received the call at the hospital. The girl was saved, but the four-month-old life in her belly was lost. It was only when Lu Siyang went to the hospital that day that he learned she was pregnant.

Mo Ran listened quietly to everything Lu Siyang told her, and strangely, she shed no tears, only feeling a blankness in her mind.

Lu Siyang left to care for the girl in the hospital, while Mo Ran shut herself in her room for two days and nights without eating.

On the third day, Mo Ran bought some things and went to the hospital. Outside the ward, she saw Lu Siyang feeding the girl medicine. She put down the things she had brought and apologized to the girl lying in the bed. Taking off her ring, she prepared to leave the ward. As she reached the door, the girl cried out, "I will never forgive you!"

Mo Ran remembered that she had also once told Gu Chen, "I will never forgive you." It seemed that she was not only the one being hurt but also the one causing harm to others.

As Mo Ran walked out of the hospital gate, Lu Siyang chased after her. Mo Ran smiled faintly and said, "Marry her. She needs you more than I do."

In the season of blooming flowers, Lu Siyang's wedding proceeded as scheduled, but the bride was not Mo Ran. On the same day, Gu Chen also walked down the aisle with a beautiful bride. Mo Ran sat in front of her vanity table applying makeup meticulously, and then she laughed while crying.

While cutting her wrists with a razor blade, Mo Ran kept hearing the words that both Gu Chen and Lu Siyang had once said: "Dear, marry me and become my beautiful bride." Mo Ran knew that in heaven, there would no longer be any semblance of happiness.

As her consciousness gradually faded, Mo Ran felt as if she really flew like a butterfly. She wore a white wedding dress, surrounded by colorful butterflies and cute angels, holding an angel wand in her hand.
