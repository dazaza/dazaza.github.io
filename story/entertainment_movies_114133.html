Top Ten Free Movie Websites in China

Every day, a lot of netizens ask me where they can watch free movies. Today, I have compiled a few relatively stable sites to share with everyone, and I hope you all can bookmark them.

First: Extreme Audio-Video (http://www.17dow.cn)

This might be the pioneer among China's free movie websites. Although the domain name is a bit hard to remember, its good service has earned it a solid reputation among many free movie websites. You can find the fastest-updated and latest free movies here. However, you often need to "grab a line" as there is a limit on the number of people who can watch online at the same time.

Second: Truly Free Movies (http://www.456789.cn)

This is an excellent up-and-coming website. No registration is required to watch movies. The movie library grows rapidly and updates quickly (usually updating more than 10 movies per day), allowing you to watch the latest big-budget free movies. The site owner also pioneered the use of P2P software for online movie viewing, with a clean interface.

Third: New Fast Movies (http://www.xk88.com)

XK88 Movie Search Engine - a great movie search website. Some movies require downloading and do not offer online playback, but the inventory is substantial.

Fourth: Yu Ruyi (http://vod.ruyi.com)

A Unicom video website that requires higher system configurations to play videos properly, and registration is needed to watch movies.

Fifth: Century Frontline (http://www.avl.com.cn)

A comprehensive entertainment website operated by Guangzhou Telecom. It has been providing free movies for a long time with decent speed. However, registration is required, and you need to "grab a line" to watch. Registering also gets you a pretty good video email account!

Sixth: POCO Movies (http://movie.poco.cn)

POCO Free Movies - a website for free movie exchanges. The site offers free movie downloads, and you can also download movies from other people's computers via P2P, though you first need to install their dedicated software.

Seventh: KAN51 Online Short Movies (http://www.kan51.net)

China's largest free online short movie website. The movie playback speed is good. If you want to relax and watch short or variety movies online, KAN51 is your best choice!

Eighth: BTChina Alliance (http://www.btchina.net)

China's most comprehensive and largest free BT movie download website. There are many movie torrent links, and downloads are fast. Highly recommended as the top choice for free BT movie downloads.

Ninth: eMule · China (http://www.emule.org.cn)

eMule is a P2P software developed by foreigners. By connecting to different servers, you can download various movie files. Anyone can install and use it permanently for free. Moreover, you can download many movies that are unavailable domestically. Hehe!

Tenth: vv33 Free Movies (http://www.vv33.com)

A pretty good online movie website. No registration is required to watch. Most of the movies are short films, usually no longer than 10 minutes. The playback speed is decent.

Additionally, there is the Pop Online Movies site (www.bnb88.com), which mainly features animations and TV series.
