Chapter 986: Concealing Shape with the Wind

The moment the sword was unleashed, it transformed into a streak of golden light. The black-clad beautiful matron clearly intended to use this treasure to cut through and rescue the elder surnamed Fu first.

Han Li, however, frowned deeply!

Rescuing someone was naturally acceptable, but personally flying over and rashly increasing the distance between them wasn't a good thing at all.

Just as the golden light approached the giant cocoon, there was a "bang," and a green figure shot out from the ground. In a flash, it blocked in front of the giant cocoon, bare-handedly grabbed casually, and several claw beams shot out, instantly striking the golden scissors away.

Both Han Li and the black-clad beautiful matron initially assumed that this person was the Silver-winged Night Fork. However, when the light dimmed and they saw the face clearly, both were greatly shocked.

The green figure turned out to have a wolf's head on a human body, eyes blood-red, and was unmistakably the enormous zombie wolf they had seen earlier. It was unclear what secret technique had been used, but not only had it transformed into a humanoid state, but its size had also suddenly shrunk several times, its fierce face glaring at the black-clad beautiful matron.

But what concerned Han Li even more was that the Yin Zhi Ma that had originally been perched on the wolf's head was nowhere to be seen. Had this spiritual object remained underground, or was it hidden elsewhere?

Naturally reacting in such a manner, Han Li's eyes flashed with blue light as he instinctively scanned around.

Immediately, his complexion changed drastically, and he urgently shouted a warning to the black-clad beautiful matron:

"Watch out behind you!"

Then without hesitation, he flicked his ten fingers. More than a dozen streaks of green sword qi shot towards the direction of the black-clad beautiful matron.

Upon hearing Han Li's shout, the beautiful matron, being experienced in combat, immediately felt a chill in her heart.

Without thinking, she pointed at the green small shield in front of her. The shield shimmered and appeared behind her. Simultaneously, her entire magical power coalesced, causing the Dharma Wheel above her head to glow brightly. Seven-colored spiritual light merged into her protective spiritual light, swelling up by several times.

At the same time the beautiful matron made her defense, a faint golden figure resembling an invisible entity appeared behind her.

It was the Silver-winged Night Fork!

This flying corpse had undergone a significant transformation. All the green fur on its body had vanished, replaced by pale golden scales. The pair of silver wings on its back emitted a strange green-white light, and its silver eyes now bore a pair of pale golden pupils. More shockingly, the overwhelming corpse energy that usually surrounded the Silver-winged Night Fork had completely disappeared. There was no trace of any aura, and when one's divine sense swept over it, it seemed as if it were just an empty ethereal form.

The moment this creature appeared behind her, it raised a single hand without hesitation. Its body remained stationary, yet its arm suddenly elongated several times.

Several inches long golden light surged from its pitch-black claws, grabbing forward and landing directly on the green small shield that had just appeared behind the beautiful matron.

The shield, having just emerged, swelled in size, emitting brilliant light, appearing impregnable.

But an unbelievable scene unfolded!

The Silver-winged Night Fork treated the shield as if it didn’t exist. With a flash of golden light on its arm, its claws silently pierced through, grabbing onto the black-clad beautiful matron’s protective spiritual light.

A crisp cracking sound was heard. The claw beams merely paused for a moment before tearing through the spiritual light like paper.

With a "pu tsi," the claw beams pierced into the woman's chest, turning her heart into powder, and effortlessly pierced through her again.

Seeing this, Han Li felt a heavy weight in his heart.

Being close-ranged by this immensely powerful flying corpse was indeed a fatal matter.

At this point, the green sword qi arrived from afar. But with a flick of the Silver-winged Night Fork's double wings behind it, a green-white light swept over and dispersed the sword qi.

However, Han Li's attempt to rescue was not entirely wasted, as it gave the beautiful matron a bit more time.

At this opportunity, red light flashed from the top of her head, and a palm-sized Yuan Ying appeared there.

This Yuan Ying was naked, with features resembling the beautiful matron. However, it held a tiny inch-long flying sword. Almost simultaneously with its appearance, it flashed with red light in panic and teleported more than a dozen zhang away.

With a few more teleportations, it could escape.

At this moment, the Silver-winged Night Fork let out a sinister laugh and abruptly threw the corpse. It then dissolved into nothingness right where it stood.

Following this, a faint shadow appeared beside the Yuan Ying, opening its mouth to spit out a gray silk net, which perfectly enveloped the Yuan Ying just as it was about to teleport again.

"Han Dao You, save me!"

The Yuan Ying of the beautiful matron hurriedly called out loudly to Han Li for help while driving the flying sword to collide in all directions within the net. If her physical body were still intact, she might have had a chance to sever the silk net and escape. Now, with only her Yuan Ying remaining, not only was her magical power greatly diminished, but encountering the soul-absorbing sha-hun silk, she was naturally bounced back by the threads.

The Silver-winged Night Fork gave this woman no opportunity at all. As soon as the net was released, it made a single gesture, and a gray giant hand appeared in mid-air, catching both the net and the Yuan Ying.

Then with a flash of gray light, a foot-wide gray cocoon suddenly formed, floating in mid-air.

At this point, the body of the Silver-winged Night Fork became distinct. It glanced at the gray cocoon, then looked at Han Li, issuing a cold laugh.

"Hehe! I thought you would use Thunder Step to come and rescue her. Didn't expect you to know how to preserve your life wisely!"

"Go rescue her? If I could save her, I naturally would. But going there earlier would probably have been walking straight into a trap. The giant net you set up above can be retracted." Han Li's expression remained unchanged as he calmly spoke these words.

"You noticed? It seems you can see through my illusions, truly not by chance." A hint of surprise flashed across the Silver-winged Night Fork's face.

At this moment, more than a dozen zhang above its head, a huge gray net materialized, revealing no trace of its existence until now. It was unclear what technique this fiend used to conceal such a large net so thoroughly.

If it weren't for Han Li's Clear Mind Divine Eyes reaching a certain level of proficiency, he wouldn't have been able to notice the insidious trap.

Even without this net, Han Li likely wouldn't have acted recklessly.

The Silver-winged Night Fork that had transformed this time was indeed peculiar. Not only could it completely suppress its corpse energy without a trace, but its wind step technique was obviously much more profound than before, almost reaching the level of concealing shape with the wind.

If it weren't for his possession of the Divine Eyes, the opponent might have reached him without him noticing.

Moreover, the Silver-winged Night Fork's previous strike could easily penetrate through the defensive treasures of the black-clad beautiful matron, making Han Li feel a chill down his spine and extremely perplexed.

Seeing that Han Li hadn't fallen for the trick, the Silver-winged Night Fork let out a cold snort and gestured toward the humanoid zombie wolf with a wave of its hand.

Upon seeing this, the zombie wolf immediately flew over and in the blink of an eye was by the side of the flying corpse, twisting its wolf head to stare fiercely at Han Li.

But at this moment, a roar suddenly came from beneath Han Li, as the Ti Hun Beast began pounding its chest in a burst of ferocity.

In the previous battle, except for the beginning, it had been restrained by Han Li's mental commands from acting recklessly. This left the beast feeling extremely frustrated as it watched the Silver-winged Night Fork fly back and forth above its head without being able to do anything.

Now, seeing the zombie wolf approach, it took the opportunity to bare its fangs and glare menacingly.

Upon hearing this roar, the zombie wolf immediately showed signs of fear, its gaze toward the Ti Hun becoming quite timid.

On the side, the Silver-winged Night Fork saw this, frowned, but after a few turns of its eyes, it suddenly opened its mouth and spat out a gray bead. Grabbing the bead, it flipped its hand and tossed it into the zombie wolf's mouth.

After swallowing the bead, the zombie wolf immediately turned around and exhaled a surge of gray mist, its corpse energy greatly enhanced, and its timidity quickly dissipated.

Han Li paid no attention to all this, instead taking advantage of the opportunity to glance at the black-clad beautiful matron's green shield.

Without its owner, the treasure hovered motionlessly in mid-air, its surface glowing faintly green, smooth as ever.

Han Li furrowed his brows slightly, shifting his gaze to the pale golden scales on the Silver-winged Night Fork's body, his expression suddenly changing as if recalling something.

At this moment, the Silver-winged Night Fork suddenly tilted its head back and let out a long, piercing howl. Then its figure flickered and disappeared with the wind.

The zombie wolf roared once more, spewing streams of corpse fire from its mouth, charging straight toward the Ti Hun Beast below.

Without hesitation, the Ti Hun Beast swung its head, and yellow mist poured out from its nose.

Although the zombie wolf's fear of the Ti Hun had significantly decreased after exhaling the light ball, it still dared not approach the yellow mist in the slightest.

Its figure immediately blurred, transforming into several illusions surrounding the Ti Hun Beast chaotically due to its reduced size allowing for incredibly fast dodging speed. The yellow mist couldn't catch up for the moment.

However, the zombie wolf also dared not approach the Ti Hun easily. This beast was alert, continuously spraying divine light from its nose, forming a layer of light curtain encasing its massive body. Its two large fists continuously swung wildly around.

Given the Ti Hun Beast's ape-like massive body, its fists were naturally not small. Even if the zombie wolf, with its undead body, were struck by one, it would likely find it hard to bear.

Thus, the two were momentarily locked in a stalemate.

As the Ti Hun Beast was tied up, the Silver-winged Night Fork and Han Li were already flashing and retreating high in the sky, chasing and fleeing like ghosts.

Behind Han Li, electric arcs wrapped around his feathered wings, thundering incessantly. His figure appeared without any discernible pattern—appearing in the west moments ago, and then in the east shortly thereafter. One moment in the air, the next near the ground.

The Silver-winged Night Fork, however, used some unknown divine ability. Its flesh wings glowed with a green-white halo, making its figure seem intangible, yet it always managed to follow Han Li's lightning step closely.

Not long after, Han Li found himself struggling.

The other party's wind step technique was clearly far superior to his lightning step. The time it took to catch up was getting shorter and shorter. Now, it was almost as if every step Han Li took to transform into an electric arc was followed by the Silver-winged Night Fork arriving with the wind.

Suddenly, when Han Li's figure appeared in the air again, instead of immediately flapping his wings to leave, he suddenly tapped the snow crystal pearl in front of him. Instantly, the pearl spun, and large purple flames sprayed out in all directions. Meanwhile, dozens of golden small swords swirling around his body erupted in bursts of sword light, covering an area of more than ten zhang around him.

Just as he finished setting everything up, space rippled six or seven zhang away from Han Li, and a faint figure suddenly appeared there.

Instantly, the purple flames and sword lights converged on the person.

Han Li also raised his hand, and a loud crackling sound erupted as a thick golden arc appeared around his arm, transforming into an electric python rushing forward.

Upon seeing this momentum, the Silver-winged Night Fork was also startled. However, its scales immediately trembled, emitting a streak of golden light.

Subsequently, a golden light barrier appeared, enveloping the Silver-winged Night Fork inside.

An earth-shattering boom echoed as the sword lights, purple flames, and golden arcs arrived in succession, bursting with dazzling light, submerging the golden barrier in an instant.
