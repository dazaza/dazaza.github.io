Si Haijian_How to prepare for mall promotions?

by stonepx918 on 2012-02-29 09:46:55

How to carry out systematic promotional activities often can quickly increase product awareness. In the modern market competition, the key place for crucial publicity and sales effect is in the retail store. The layout around the store influences sales. The pre-promotion preparation should adopt a time-reversal system, clearly stating the nodes where various preparatory elements are in place. The content includes the rural location of the activity, time periods, product combinations, methods of the activity, personnel, promotional materials, gifts, props investment, corresponding support from the store, input-output ratio, estimated sales effects, analysis of store benefits, operational details, and contingency plans. Its purpose is to maximize the exchange of free or low-cost resource support from the store. (Complete one month before the activity) 1. Approval of the promotional activity application is finished. A complete promotional plan should be vivid and prominent. 2. Design and production of necessary promotional materials and gifts should be completed. Promotional content corresponds with the activity time, method, location, etc., but the critical point must not lose sight of the main product demand promotion point. (Complete twenty days before the activity).

Analyze gross profit amount, gross margin, and the proportion of special price activities; analyze the payment time for the store's activities and inventory turnover days; analyze all fees paid in the past; analyze the execution status of the annual contract; analyze records and effects of previous promotional activities at this store; historical data of competitive brands. 3. Confirm target promotional stores based on various data. Summarize the store's previous promotional sales data. Determine the highest and lowest promotional records of previous activities at that store. Provide a written explanation of the promotional policy to the store and sign agreements regarding stock preparation, pricing, and promotional areas. (Complete one month before the activity) Additionally, the aim is to catch the store’s DM schedule.

It is suggested that suppliers store and manage special inventories in advance. 4. Negotiate supply matters with the store. Ensure there is sufficient stock prepared at the store before the promotion starts. According to the promotional items, prioritize logistics delivery for the promotional store. (Complete three days before the activity) And prepare emergency response plans for the event. (Complete two days before the activity) 5. Reconfirm promotional matters with the store. The activity manager holds the signed promotional agreement and reconfirms with the store to prevent conflicts with sudden events at the store.

Require promoters to understand the store's promotional precautions and regulations (2 Targeted training: Psychological guidance before the promotion and how to answer rare questions from consumers). 6. Recruit and train promotional personnel. Based on the activity plan, recruit experienced promotional personnel and conduct pre-job training: 1 Basic training: Content covers company overview, product overview, production process, job responsibilities, and evaluation standards. Continuously enrich and refine more convincing statements. 3 Advanced training: Improve handling complaints, enhance communication skills, and achieve advisory-style promotions. (Complete two days before the activity)

The activity manager must personally conduct field research on the front lines of the store. Analyze customer walking patterns in the store, investigate consumer product purchasing habits, determine lively and advantageous display positions, understand the current situation of competitive brands, further grasp their future plans, and strengthen ties with relevant departments of the store.

7. Pre-activity mobilization meeting. Let every participant enter a warm-up state in advance. 1 Create a fighting atmosphere in the office. The content of the promotional banner echoes the company's sales slogan. The task comparison table is posted on the wall. Conduct task perception. 2 Build a "promote capable people, let average people step aside, demote incapable people" fair environment. Healthy competition, team collaboration, respect for everyone. Invent as much competition and comparison as possible (between stores, between team members, horizontal comparison, vertical comparison). 3 Break down tasks meticulously. Break down tasks by person, single item, hour. List them in a table, each participant signs off on it, post it on the office wall, stimulate everyone's competitiveness and fighting spirit, and provide sales skills training.
