Lao Li's lover shed tears, how to make a mask for acne scars by yourself. The most common phrase in Weibo comments is "May good people have peace all their lives". Worried about the second surgery of her husband, Lao Li's lover rushed back to the hospital after finishing things at home. In the afternoon of yesterday, when I just walked to the second floor of the ward building, I saw her standing in the corridor outside Lao Li's ward with her son and daughter far away. "He was sleeping, we were afraid to disturb him, so we stood outside first. The doctor said he would be operated on soon, I... felt a little nervous." The voice of Lao Li's lover was very low and a little hoarse. Hearing her mother's voice, son Li Wei slightly raised his head, his eyes which were red from lack of sleep kept staring at his mother's face for a long time. Evening Post reporter Chang Liang, Pei Lei / text, Chang Liang / photo. After receiving 20,000 yuan of charity funds, she burst into tears. The serious illness of "Water Delivery Brother" drew attention from all sectors of society. Zhengzhou Charity Federation acted immediately, joining the ranks of those who cared about "Water Delivery Brother". When the evening post reported on "Water Delivery Brother's" illness, President Wu Guorui of Zhengzhou Charity Federation specially called the chief editor of our newspaper at 5 p.m. that day, asking in detail about "Water Delivery Brother's" condition, and expressed that Zhengzhou Charity Federation would help "Water Delivery Brother" with the charity funds from all sectors of society, passing on the love of the whole society to him. At 3 p.m. yesterday, under the entrustment of Wu Guorui, Secretary General Zhou Peigong of Zhengzhou Charity Federation went to the People's Hospital of the province and, according to the relevant regulations of Zhengzhou Charity Federation, delivered the highest limit of 20,000 yuan of charity funds for major illness assistance. To avoid disturbing Lao Li resting in bed, everyone communicated with the hospital and arranged the donation event in the doctor's office. Secretary General Zhou said: "The touching deeds of Water Delivery Brother warm the whole society, his loving behavior is also a kind of charitable behavior, completely in line with the charity concept of our Charity Federation, so, when encountering difficulties, we should help him more." After receiving 20,000 yuan of charity funds, Lao Li's lover didn't say anything for a long time, the smile on her face gradually faded, and she burst into tears soon. Originally intended to initiate a donation for Lao Li, but both he and his wife disagreed. Knowing that all of Lao Li's medical expenses were borrowed from relatives, many colleagues in the newspaper company advocated initiating a donation for Lao Li. But this idea was quickly rejected by Lao Li and his lover. "We can't let others worry about us, money sent personally, we really can't accept it. I disagree, and Lao Li will not agree either." The attitude of Lao Li's lover was particularly resolute, "It's fine if familiar friends lend a helping hand, please don't bother others anymore. Everyone earns money hard." "Recently, the family's financial situation has been relatively difficult, but we really can't keep troubling others, otherwise, our whole family will feel indebted." Regarding how to solve Lao Li's upcoming medical expenses, Lao Li's lover insisted that she could definitely find a way to raise the money for treatment. At around 7 p.m. yesterday, Lao Li's lover called and said "At around 6 p.m., Lao Li had already entered the operating room."
