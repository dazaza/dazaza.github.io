On Women's Day, many tourist attractions offer special discounts, providing more options for traveling.

by anonymous on 2012-03-02 17:45:40

This year, my birthday coincides with International Women's Day, which has made me pay more attention to this special day. Many scenic spots are also launching promotional offers in celebration of the occasion, making this exclusive holiday for women even more exciting. Many sisters have been looking forward to the arrival of "March 8th Festival" for a long time.

At Jinxiangshan Ski Resort, there are endless coupons given out for International Women's Day. After the temperature rose following Start of Spring, many other ski resorts had to close down, leaving Jinxiangshan as the only operating ski resort within the province at this time. Located in the Southern Mountain Area, Jinxiangshan was carefully selected and planned by international renowned ski resort expert PALU. Its superior geographical location keeps Jinxiangshan 3-5℃ cooler than the city area all year round. On the occasion of "March 8th", Jinxiangshan Ski Resort specially launched a discount offer for women. All ladies skiing at Jinxiangshan can receive coupons worth between 20 to 50 yuan. This activity aims to encourage hardworking women to enjoy the excitement and beauty of spring skiing and enrich their festival experience.

At Qingdao Underwater World, color-changing magic adds to the fun on International Women's Day. The annual "March 8th" Women's Day is approaching, and Qingdao Underwater World has launched a special exhibition of "Baby Shark" color changes and discounted offers for female tourists. During the "March 8th" period, Qingdao Underwater World will hold a special exhibition of "Baby Shark" color changes, inviting female tourists to see these color-changing baby sharks during the festival. Additionally, from March 6th to 8th, Qingdao Underwater World will host a "March 8th" themed discount event. During the event, all female tourists purchasing tickets for the underwater world can enjoy an 80% discount (excluding special groups that already enjoy other ticket discount policies). Female teams from enterprises and institutions of 10 people or more, with a letter of introduction from their unit, can enjoy a 70% discount.

In Nanshan Scenic Area, multiple discounts are offered to celebrate International Women's Day. During the "March 8th" period, Longkou Nanshan Tourist Scenic Area specially launched a "Warm Nanshan, Heartfelt Gratitude and Blessings for Beautiful Ladies" thanksgiving and blessing trip for International Women's Day. It is understood that from March 7th to 8th, women's groups from government agencies and enterprises (10 people or more) can enjoy an 80% discount on the scenic area entrance fee; women who have won awards such as "March 8th Red Flag Bearer" at the county-city level and above, or were honored as "Advanced Worker" or "Outstanding Woman Cadre" in 2008, or those born on March 8th, can visit the park for free with their award certificates or personal ID cards. Moreover, during the festival period, female tourists whose ticket numbers end in "38" can receive exquisite souvenirs with their same-day tickets.

At Tianmu Hot Spring, water-like women shine differently. Another year of Beauty Festival is here, and to allow women to spend a meaningful festival, Tianmu • Northern Water City Hot Spring Resort has launched a Women's Theme Month, pushing the privileges of big and small beauties to the extreme.

During the Women's Theme Month, women can fully experience the Four Moisturizing Soups and SPA hydrotherapy of the scenic area; characteristic hot springs like milk baths and red wine baths will bring them new experiences. Female tourists visiting the scenic area during the theme month can also receive one beauty mask, one fruit acid steam therapy, and one sachet. In addition, in March, the scenic area continues to sell intimate couples package tickets: two hot spring tickets (market price 138 yuan per person) + two fish spa sessions (market price 38 yuan per person) + one night stay in a forest-view standard double room (market price 480 yuan), all for just 598 yuan to enjoy a sweet world for two.

At Jiudingtai, exciting activities continue throughout International Women's Day. During the "March 8th" period, Jiudingtai Chinese Folklore Happy Park will grandly host a large interactive entertainment event celebrating "March 8th", along with the "March Third" Mountain Song Festival, allowing brothers and sisters to spend a meaningful "March 8th".

The classic participatory drama "Tunnel War" and the Hollywood scene drama "Mountain Flood Bursting" will be splendidly performed; the "March Third" mountain song meeting of the scenic area allows tourists to watch and participate in the large situational drama "Liu Sanjie Seeking a Husband", learn to dance ethnic minority dances, tour each ethnic minority village, and learn different ethnic dances. In addition, tourists can participate in ethnic minority fun game competitions, watch the large horse battle live-action drama "Hero Qin Chong", and attend large bonfire party and song-dance evenings.

For International Women's Day, Yangzhou and Zhenjiang are great places for outings and flower appreciation. As "March 8th" approaches, three-day tours for outings and flower appreciation in Yangzhou and Zhenjiang are becoming increasingly popular. From the operators of Yangzhou and Zhenjiang direct tourist buses, the Mass Holiday Travel Agency and Full Shandong Travel Agency, it is learned that recently, there has been a particularly high number of female tourists consulting about and participating in Yangzhou and Zhenjiang travel routes, mostly group reward trips and welfare trips from units. The delicate and elegant beauty of Yangzhou's Slender West Lake and the deep historical significance of He Garden attract many tourists.

Another year of March has arrived, and many sisters have been eagerly awaiting the arrival of "March 8th". They plan to go out and have fun, and various scenic spots are also launching promotional offers, making this exclusive holiday for women even more exciting.
