Elva Hsiao wears a custom-made wedding dress to shoot an MV, transforms into a thief stealing diamonds - gfmeha's blog - Baidou.com - real online interaction brings us closer.

Elva Hsiao's new album "Miss Swagger" has been performing brilliantly since its release. Not only has it topped the G-Music and Five Major Record Stores charts for three consecutive weeks, but it has also received widespread acclaim from all quarters. In response, Gold Typhoon Music decided to increase the promotional budget to 25 million New Taiwan dollars and further invest 2 million New Taiwan dollars in shooting the fifth MV of the album, titled "Goddess with Two Faces." At the listening session held before the new album's release, the song "Goddess with Two Faces" had already garnered much praise from many professional music critics. After the album’s release, it won even more admiration from fans. Fans left numerous messages on Gold Typhoon Music's official discussion area, urging the record company to expedite and prioritize it as the fifth main single and quickly shoot the MV!

In the recording studio, Elva enjoyed herself immensely. Even though each costume change on set took six hours, she happily expressed, "The female spy is the 'Miss Swagger' who embodies the most swagger characteristics! Although I've tried various costumes in my music videos and even acted in movies, this time was the most exhilarating! Whether as a female spy or a pink thief, these are professions I would never have the chance to try in real life! So after filming this MV, I really felt an immense sense of satisfaction!"

In the retro wedding dress look revealed ahead of time, Elva wore a 10-meter-long wedding dress and used this charmingly vulnerable look to seduce the handsome male security guard guarding the diamond. Later, her beloved dog Gemini, who made a cameo appearance, successfully retrieved the large diamond. Gemini, dressed in a cool dog suit designed by ELVA herself and wearing an eye mask, transformed into the Green Hornet! Besides becoming the focus of everyone's attention and争相 taking photos with him, Gemini also prompted the director to decide on adding more scenes for him on the spot, elevating him from a supporting male role to the number one male lead! ELVA laughed and said happily, "Gemini has recently learned many new skills. If Hollywood movie producers want to make a sequel to 'Superdog Mission,' they can come talk to me! Because I am both his mother and his agent!"

In the MV, Elva played three different roles and finally succeeded in stealing the big diamond. She explained, "A diamond is the symbol of eternal love in every woman's heart. Like all women, I also yearn for eternal love the most. I now let things take their natural course in matters of the heart, but hearing Big S's wedding news made me admire her and feel that she is very elegant."
