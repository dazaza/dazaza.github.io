Soliloquies of the Main Characters in the "Tomb of God" Section

by fendan on 2008-05-28 01:28:52

Chen Nan: Ten thousand years ago, it was Yu Xin who showed me true love. When I saw her die in my arms, my heart went with her. For her, I died in the final battle! After ten thousand years, I woke up as if from a dream. How did I come back to life after death? Images of my father, mother, and Yu Xin reappeared. Gradually, I realized that I seemed to be someone else's pawn. I must find the answers. I want to return to my past happy life. I am myself. I won't rely on any external things; I will become stronger to change this world!

Meng MM: I originally thought I was the inheritor of Dantai, and I respected Dantai Xuan. But later, I realized that Dantai Xuan and I were one. Due to various reasons, I have a child from the Chen family, and this little guy has made me feel the glory of maternal love. I've decided to change myself, forget the past, although I have no feelings for Chen Nan, for Little Chenchen, I will be a good mother!

Dantai Xuan: Ten thousand years ago, I had no choice but to tamper with Chen Nan's body, reducing his power. After he died, I always felt guilty towards him. Ten thousand years later, he actually revived. I originally wanted to make up for something, but I understood my own destiny. Meng Ke'er was originally one with me, but because of Chen Nan, she changed, and he made me lose face. I hate...

Pizi Long (Rogue Dragon): "Howl..." I was once a dashing, carefree, handsome invincible dragon. Who would have thought that one day, a crazy guy would take a fancy to me and try to use me as a mount. I'd rather die than yield, but ultimately, I couldn’t resist. I was suppressed for a thousand years. Finally, one day, I saw the light again, but then I met an unlucky star, and since then, I've been following him around. No matter what happens in the future, he will always be my friend, and we fight side by side... a hundred times... a hundred times...

Baby Dragon: They always say I'm not ordinary, but I've forgotten the past. I only remember meeting an old man in the deep mountains. He insisted on riding me and took me to a great place where there was plenty of food. I decided to stay. Later, I saw someone, and at first sight, I liked him. I always felt he was special, maybe it was a gift from the gods. Since then, I've stuck to him, and through him, I met other dragons who shared my ideals. Together, we explore the future and face challenges!

Little Phoenix: When I opened my eyes, the first thing I saw was a little fat dragon. I thought he was my mom, but later I found out we weren't the same kind. However, the people around him were all very special, so I decided to give up looking for my mom and follow them. But then, I encountered a crazy dead bird who tortured me, but I knew it was for my own good. Now, I've left him and joined Baby Dragon and the others to explore... I've learned many useful things from them!

Chen Zhan: It is fate that I will become the legendary "Ninth One." The first eight were all my closest relatives. They were naturally talented but sacrificed themselves innocently for the sake of our legendary ancestors. I don't want to be like them. I believe I will surpass my ancestors. Ancestors are already dead; they should rest in peace. Why revive them to satisfy one's own ambition? The unfortunate departure of my son made me heartbroken, but I believe he will return, and I believe he will definitely do better than me!

Chen Dong: I know everyone is dissatisfied with me for updating too slowly. In fact, I've racked my brains to please readers, killing countless brain cells! I will work harder, and you all support me well. Actually, I also check Baidu to see you all. Let's work together to create good works!

Readers: Brother Dong, none of us have it easy. To read your works as soon as possible, we've given up our golden time. We know it's not easy for you either. We're just complaining a bit to pass the time. By the way, you must give "Shen Mu" a perfect ending, or it won't be worth it for everyone! Last sentence: Understanding forever! Everyone should understand each other!
