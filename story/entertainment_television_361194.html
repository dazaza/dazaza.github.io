Introduction to Detective Di Renjie 4:

"Detective Di Renjie 4" started filming on October 20, 2009, with a planned forty episodes divided into three stories: "The Mysterious Silver Carriage Case," "Snow Domain Celestial Fire," and "Lost in the Wilderness of Passion." The investment will reach 30 million RMB. On July 23, 2010, at 19:30, "Detective Di Renjie 4" will premiere nationwide on Shandong TV's Film and Television Channel under the "First Broadcast Theatre" starting at 18:40 every evening (the broadcast was delayed due to the airing of the conclusion of the new "Dream of the Red Chamber").

"The Mysterious Silver Carriage Case" mainly tells the story of how Commissioner Di is ordered to inspect the Jian Nan Dao region. He arrives incognito in Yizhou City with Li Yuanfang and Zeng Tai, just as a major case occurs in Yizhou - over a hundred silver artisans have gone missing. Commissioner Di delves deep into the community to understand the situation and finds the missing silver artisans in Wuxian County, dozens of miles away from Yizhou. As the case becomes clearer, a massive corruption ring entrenched in Yizhou also comes to light. In the end, Commissioner Di punishes the corrupt and eliminates evil.

"The Lost in the Wilderness of Passion" tells the story of how the King of Yue Shi celebrates his birthday. Empress Wu Zetian dispatches Commissioner Di as an envoy, leading Li Yuanfang and Zeng Tai to offer congratulations. From the mouth of the King's consort, Commissioner Di learns that King Cha Chi has suddenly died. After examining the body, Commissioner Di discovers that Cha Chi was poisoned by a potent, odorless, and colorless poison. Commissioner Di, along with Li Yuanfang and Zeng Tai, overcome numerous obstacles to completely dismantle a conspiratorial rebel organization.
