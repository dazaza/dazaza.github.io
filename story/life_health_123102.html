Be wary of contamination in American peanut butter products; over 1,100 products recalled

by john_7 on 2009-02-06 13:09:35

Yesterday, the company again announced the recall of nearly 20 products. Up to now, because of this contamination case, over 1100 products have been recalled in total. The main cause is Salmonella, which is a common source of food contamination in the United States. Patients infected will suffer from fever, diarrhea, abdominal cramps and other symptoms within 12 to 72 hours, and the illness often lasts for 4 to 7 days.
