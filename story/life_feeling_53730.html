Help us, which way should we go?

by tj970 on 2008-03-15 02:40:55

Yesterday I talked with you again, and I could hear from the other end of the phone that you were not happy. You told me that you still thought about me, and still remembered the days we spent together. Hearing this, I was really upset. I didn't know what to say to you, or how to express it. The breakup was your decision, and you hurt me three times last month. I've been healing myself ever since.

But why do you always come back to me just when I'm about to forget you? It's been less than a week since our real breakup. If you have changed your mind again about what you said on the day of our breakup, then why did you tell me to forget you completely that day? You said we couldn't be together anymore, and that you would slowly forget me too...

I hope I won't get hurt again, and I hope I can be happy. I am doing it. I am gradually forgetting you and making myself happy. But why do you tell me these things now? You say that you don't get along with the girl your father introduced you to. You say she is so bad. You say you don't want to keep in touch with her anymore because she's annoying.

(I also think that girl is really problematic. She asks him a lot of questions every day, even asking for his QQ password. Because this girl goes to my QQ space every day to read my diary and look at the photos of me and my ex-boyfriend. I think this girl is very annoying, always looking at my QQ space. So I set a login restriction, and she couldn't enter anymore. Then she tried to use his QQ to enter my space and read what I wrote. She wanted to know if I had broken up with him and if I was still in contact with him. That girl looked at it, and then told her father everything she saw, including some photos and diaries. Then her father found my boyfriend's father and said that if his son had any more contact with me, he wouldn't let his daughter be with his son. After knowing this, my boyfriend's father scolded him and said that if he had any more contact with me in the future, he would sever their father-son relationship. He doesn't know what to do now. He doesn't want to deal with that girl anymore. She sends messages to him, saying that if he doesn't reply, she will tell her father. My boyfriend ignores her and doesn't care. He feels that the girl mainly relies on her father to support her and protect their feelings. Whenever there is something wrong, she reports to her father. My boyfriend can't stand her, so he ignores her. But he doesn't know what to do next. He has already talked to his father, but his father refuses to listen to his explanation and doesn't want to communicate with him. He only tells him to be with that girl. My boyfriend is quite filial, so we broke up for his father's sake. Actually, we still love each other until now. But some reasons force us to separate. Now that girl is in Guangzhou, and my boyfriend is in Shenzhen. They met once during the Spring Festival in their hometown, and after he returned to Shenzhen, they communicated through text messages and phone calls. This is how it started.)

I don't know what to do, but when he told me that it was impossible for him to be with that girl, I suddenly felt much lighter inside. I don't know why, but I also felt much better. Is this the ending I want to see? No, I just want the person I loved to be happy. I don't want him to be unhappy. I don't hate him for the harm he caused me before. I don't know why, but I just can't hate him. Instead, I hope he can be happy. I think loving someone means blessing and sacrificing oneself. This is my love for him, a silly, simple, and pure love for him.

I know that it's impossible for us to be together, because there is a huge obstacle between us - his father. I think he won't break up with his father for me. I don't want that either. He is filial to his father, feeling that his father raised him, and he should listen to his father. But he said he wouldn't be with that girl, so I said that his father could find another one for him. Then he didn't say anything...

I don't know if our fate is over yet. We both feel that we will meet again someday. But I don't know when that day will be?

Last night, I talked a lot with him. It was the first time we had such a calm conversation after our breakup. When we talked about some things, I almost cried, but I held back the tears and didn't cry anymore... I feel that my love for him is not as strong as before, but I still hope to spend the rest of my life with him...

What should I do? What should I do? And what should he do?

We are secretly in contact now. I'm afraid that one day his father will find out. If one day his father finds me, what should I do, what should I say... (He doesn't live at home. His house is in Gongming, and he works in Nanshan, renting a place to live by himself, going home once a week.)

Orange: If he is no longer worth your love, then let go. Although it hurts, it's better than continuing. Maybe this sounds heartless, but he has already abandoned you, and you also said that you have started to heal yourself...

Alas, I don't know how to continue. You decide for yourself. As long as you feel it's worth it, and makes you happy...
