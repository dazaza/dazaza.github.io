Who polluted "You Fang Gou"?

by bjjdwxgs on 2009-12-03 09:41:29

Mr. Wang, who lives in the urban area, reported that there is a small river called "Youfang Ditch" west of the Century Huahua Supermarket on Yanhai Road. Garbage, feces and other dirty things are dumped into this river, causing serious pollution to the surrounding environment. When we arrived at the "Youfang Ditch" that Mr. Wang referred to, a strong smell hit us, and the river water was murky.

同期声: Citizen Mr. Wang - "When the stench is overwhelming, all the windows here are closed, they can't be opened, it's too smelly."

同期声: Resident - "The surrounding environment is terrible, the smell is too heavy."

We found that this river is connected to some of the main rivers in the urban area, it is not a stagnant water river. What is the reason for the deterioration of the river water?

同期声: Citizen Mr. Wang - "Some human waste from the sanitation department is directly discharged into the river, which is one of the factors. There are also other dirty things, garbage, and dirty water. Look, the sewage outlet is right here."

It turns out that the east bank of the river is a garbage processing station of the sanitation department, with sewage ditches leading straight into the river. Could it really be that the sanitation department disposes of garbage into the "Youfang Ditch"? So the reporter went to the processing station to understand the situation. However, the situation was not as the surrounding residents imagined. According to the person in charge of the processing station, the septic tank within the station had been abandoned two years ago. Therefore, there is currently no discharge of pollutants into the "Youfang Ditch". Mr. Wang confirmed this.

So where does the pollution of the river come from? We noticed that on the west bank of the river, there was a pile of garbage dumped by the surrounding residents.

同期声: Resident - "There is a trash can over here, it is placed here every day, and the sanitation workers clear it every night. (Then why is there still so much garbage in the river?) Definitely, some residents themselves are not conscious and dump garbage into the river. It's an issue of personal quality, some citizens have problems with their personal quality."

Conclusion: It seems that the pollution of this river is not a recent problem. Perhaps before this, the septic tank of the sanitation department caused some pollution to this river. Now, the nearby residents should take more responsibility for the pollution of the river.
