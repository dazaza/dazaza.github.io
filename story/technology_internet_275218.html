SEO needs to take a step back and reflect.

by fjs1d495 on 2009-11-24 12:47:21

The path to SEO success seems far from me, dear webmaster friends. But it is also very close. Learning optimization skills can benefit you for a lifetime. Everyone hopes to achieve good optimization results with minimal time and effort. Webmaster friends are increasingly valuing optimization to ultimately achieve their goals. Let's take an example of a client's enterprise site (www.sd59.cn) where the main keyword ranking "Fuzhou Website Construction" suddenly disappeared. It had consistently been ranked third on Baidu’s first page, but in the past few days, after chatting with a great SEO friend, I found out that the ranking suddenly vanished and the homepage dropped significantly, causing great anxiety. After reviewing the website, I analyzed that the site was over-optimized.

1. Frequently posting irrelevant threads on other websites and appending one's own URL to increase external links. This practice only temporarily boosts external link counts but lacks real value.

2. Excessive friendly link exchanges. SEO professionals know that too many friendly links are not beneficial; ideally, there should be around 50. Internal page links have less impact, and while they may briefly improve rankings, they will eventually result in search engine penalties.

3. Over-reliance on article collection tools like Huochetou. This makes the site appear as spam to search engines, leading to reduced rankings.

4. Insufficient external links. External links are crucial for increasing your site's weight. Do not overlook their importance. More external links will gradually improve your site's ranking.

I also want to tell all webmaster friends: How do we achieve the path to successful SEO optimization? Here are some points I analyzed:

1. Persist in original content creation. Keep updating your website's content. You can write more original articles on platforms like A5, which has high authority. Articles published here are easily indexed and reprinted, improving your visibility and increasing external links.

2. Learn language arts and promotional techniques. These skills will help your optimization work. Even without exceptional verbal expression talent, good writing skills will serve the same purpose.

3. Strengthen learning of SEO knowledge. You can visit sd59.cn or similar sites to learn many SEO techniques. My goal in Fuzhou website construction is to make it easier for more Fuzhou network companies to optimize their sites, catering to the general public.

4. Have perseverance and determination. Treat SEO as a lifelong pursuit. Since this is a career, you will continue to progress.

In summary, by adhering to these principles, you can pave the way to SEO success.
