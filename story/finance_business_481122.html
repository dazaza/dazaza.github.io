To reach the path to BanjoHero status.

To reach that place where you play bluegrass banjo, you will have to learn how to do these three things:

1) Take notes

2) Select the correct notes

3) Develop your timing correctly

The BanjoHero manual will help you understand what technology and banjo music skills you need for these three steps. As you enter this part of the general understanding, BanjoHero will make your first year of banjo playing as a beginner in context, and it will also let you know some of the broader issues that will play a big role in your musical journey (such as rhythm, song structure, musical timing, expectations, rhetoric). The indiscriminate appendix serves as an important knowledge resource, especially in the absence of an average DVD and Quick Start Guide explanations.

Feeling-wise, a DVD can show you things to learn, and teachers can show the right way to do the things you learn, but who needs to tell you what to learn so you can really play your banjo music? BanjoHero does not dwell on these things that a teacher could explain, but instead explains how to develop your musical ability, and ultimately how to combine them with a few banjo techniques, so you really have what you need to play the banjo well.
