Beiwai Perfume Girl Blog, Personal Profile of the Perfume Girl

by sisi1212 on 2009-03-18 10:11:27

Beiwai girl was forced to leave school because she criticized the Ministry of Education in her blog? This is yesterdays hottest news online. This "perfume girl" became an internet celebrity within a few hours, with countless netizens discussing it on hundreds and thousands of websites and forums. Some have even begun to criticize this prestigious foreign language university.

While public opinion online supports the expelled girl and condemns the school, many netizens also have doubts: how could a blog that has only been open for 10 days become so popular? Why did the girl post so many personal photos in her diary about her expulsion? How could such a thing happen in this day and age? A Beiwai teacher attacked the girl in her own blog as a "hype", but what made her helpless was that her clarification of the facts was drowned out by the tide of online criticism.
