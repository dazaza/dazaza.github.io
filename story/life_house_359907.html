Xixi International Information Technology Industrial Park officially moved into the innovation base

by dragonsoft on 2010-07-04 20:39:25

On the afternoon of April 15, 2010, a signing ceremony for the Xixi International Information Technology Industrial Park project led by Longruan Group to enter the Yuhang Innovation Base in Hangzhou was held by the beautiful West Lake. During the rapid enhancement phase of the construction of the innovation base platform, the signing of the Xixi International Information Technology Industrial Park project undoubtedly turned a new page for the innovation base. This project will strongly promote the construction of public service platforms, including the public data network, data center, and technical support center of the innovation base, thereby greatly promoting the great enhancement and development of high-tech industries in the innovation base. The location, transportation, environment, and industrial advantages of the innovation base will open up a whole new world for the development of the Xixi International Information Technology Industrial Park.

The signing ceremony was presided over by Sun Bingsong, member of the Standing Committee of the Yuhang District Party Committee and Secretary of the Party Working Committee of the Yuhang Group (Innovation Base). Assistant to the District Governor, Director of the Yuhang Innovation Base Management Committee, Shen Wannan, and President of the Shanghai Association of Young Talents in Informatization, Chairman of Longruan Group (China) Co., Ltd., Dai Jianbiao, respectively signed and confirmed the project investment agreement. Relevant leaders and department heads from Hangzhou and Yuhang districts, town and township leaders of the Yuhang group, and relevant enterprise leaders from the Shanghai Association of Young Talents in Informatization attended the signing ceremony, jointly witnessing this important moment in the history of the development of high-tech industries in Yuhang. President of the Shanghai Association of Young Talents in Informatization, Chairman of Longruan Group (China) Co., Ltd., Dai Jianbiao, and member of the Standing Committee of the District Party Committee, Executive Vice District Governor Shen Yu, respectively delivered passionate speeches at the ceremony.

The innovation base where Longruan Group is going to settle is currently the most promising area for the development of high-tech industries in Yuhang District and even Hangzhou city. The Xixi International Information Technology Industrial Park project, which has settled in this time, covers an area of about 150 mu and is positioned to create a gathering base for industries mainly including software development, information service outsourcing, internet commerce, data processing, ICT product R&D sales and services, with characteristics facing service outsourcing in Shanghai and overseas, business process outsourcing, and high-end knowledge service outsourcing. The park will host more than 3,000 various types of high-tech talents. A batch of 20 Shanghai IT enterprises led by Longruan Group will be the first to settle into the park.

At the signing ceremony, Chairman of Longruan Group, Mr. Dai Jianbiao, expressed his gratitude for the active support of Yuhang District on Longruan's project investment in the Yuhang Group Innovation Base. He introduced everyone to Longruan Group (China) Co., Ltd., a high-tech enterprise group co-founded by overseas returnees from Shanghai. Established in 2001, after nearly seven years of development, the company has gradually made achievements and progress in areas such as software outsourcing, financial industry software, network and data integration, software and foreign language education, science and technology park construction and management. In 2009, the total output value of the group enterprises was close to 1 billion yuan. Longruan Group also owns several foreign-funded and domestic enterprises. In the fields of software and IT services, Longruan has excellent experience in providing different levels of technical services to global software outsourcing customers, from high-end technical solutions and strategic decision-making support to basic project programming. As a localized IT service provider in Shanghai, Longruan has a deep understanding of the technical foundation of Shanghai software talents and the needs of European, American, and Japanese software customers. In the technical field, Longruan owns a unique system platform dedicated to software outsourcing project management domestically, which has been applied overseas and can effectively support software outsourcing enterprises to enhance their competitiveness. The construction of the Xixi International Information Technology Park in the Yuhang Innovation Base will play a very positive role in the development of industrial forms in this field in Hangzhou, forming an international industrial chain through the interaction of domestic and overseas markets and between Shanghai and Hangzhou in a "front store back factory" model. Mr. Dai Jianbiao indicated that the park economy would play a huge role in regional economic development. If the information service outsourcing industry chain in Hangzhou can continuously improve in terms of market, talent, and management, this park will play a good leading role in upgrading the industrial structure of Hangzhou and gathering the service industry. He hopes to create a legend in the IT industry with the support of the government. Signing the investment agreement is just the first step in a long journey, and there will be more detailed work waiting to be done. Mr. Dai Jianbiao hopes to have honest and tight cooperation with the Yuhang team in the future and jointly build the Hangzhou Information Service Base.
