Free reading of the latest novel "Ghost Blows Out the Light"

by mooxx on 2009-05-05 11:35:05

Ancient civilizations, lost treasures, and mysterious ancient tombs - nothing captures the audience's attention more than these elements. Now, a "tomb economy" is emerging around the world. American commercial blockbusters like *The Mummy* and *Indiana Jones*, as well as the classic video game *Tomb Raider*, have all achieved tremendous commercial success. These fictional "tombs" allow people to embark on thrilling and grand adventures from the comfort of movie theaters or their homes, following the protagonists in films or games for an unprecedented level of excitement.

*Ghost Blowing Out the Lamp* (*The Adventures of Grave Robbers*) is a series of text-based adventure stories. Three modern-day tomb raiders use secret feng shui techniques to interpret the pulse of mountains and rivers across the land, seeking out the dragon towers and treasure palaces lost in the wilderness. Deserts, snow-capped mountains, forests, canyons, rapids, grasslands, and rare mysterious flora and fauna create a perilous journey full of suspense and tension. Each step brings challenges that push the limits of imagination. In the bizarre underground world, layer by layer, they uncover the mysteries of ancient times. The story begins with a mysterious scroll passed down through the protagonist’s family, leading them on a journey spanning thousands of miles to seek out the hidden treasures. They face countless dangers and obstacles, as their actions—manipulating the forces of nature and defying convention—are far from ordinary. Highlights include the nine-story demon tower beneath the Kunlun Mountain glacier, the secret stronghold of the Kwantung Army in the Wild Man Gully on the China-Mongolia border, the lost city of Jingjue in the black desert of Taklamakan, the Corpse Fragrance Devil Lotus Flower in the bottomless cave of the sacred mountain, the demon coffin in the insect valley of the Yunnan jungle, the headless cave of the ancient Guge Kingdom in the Karakoram Mountains of Tibet, and the Dragon Ridge Mystery Cave in Shaanxi... Currently being serialized is the final volume: the headless cave ruins, the frozen maze of the evil deity.
