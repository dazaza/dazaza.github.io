Embarrassing Moment 1:

After a long wait, it finally rained! Although it wasn't heavy, he still took a small umbrella and waited for her on her way home from work. "You didn't bring an umbrella? Let me take you home!" "It's fine, the rain isn't heavy, I can go home by myself. The umbrella is so small, you keep it."

Embarrassing Moment 2:

When having dinner with guests, she was there too. After drinking and eating heartily, he shouted: "This round is on me!" He rushed to the front desk to pay, but came back with a waitress because he didn't have enough money.

Embarrassing Moment 3:

On their first meeting, they had walked for quite a while. He said: "I'm hungry, how about you?"

"A little."

"It's time for dinner, you should go home."

"Yesterday no one was at my place, I ate out."

"Oh, I know a pretty good spot, let's go together?"

Then they walked for two bus stops or so, and arrived at a food stall.

"Boss, give us two bowls of seafood noodles. Extra seafood. I don't want eggs, what about you? You probably don't want them either? Boss, no eggs in both bowls."

"I have something to do, I'll leave first."

"Hey, didn't we say we'd eat together? Why are you leaving? I'll see you off... Wait, boss, just one bowl is fine!"

Embarrassing Moment 4:

She often borrowed books from him, and there were always some small notes in the books. But one time, she accidentally took the wrong book and found many notes inside. Apparently, they weren't meant for her.

Embarrassing Moment 5:

Finally, it was her night shift. He called her.

"Hey, it's you? Is there anything?"

"Hehe, nothing much..."

"Oh, I've got something to do. Don't call when you have nothing to say."

"Beep... beep... ..."

Embarrassing Moment 6:

There were many people on the bus. He was originally inside, not very crowded. But suddenly he noticed a beautiful girl coming through the door! So he made great efforts to squeeze towards the bus door. Finally, he reached her side, but before he could take a good look, she couldn't hold it any longer, and threw up all over him, then said sorry and got off the bus.

Embarrassing Moment 7:

A godsend opportunity: It rained, and she didn't have an umbrella. So he bravely offered to ride his bike and send her home, with her sitting on the back seat holding the umbrella for him. How romantic! But after not going far, they fell into a puddle. Then she rode off first, and he had to carry the bike home by himself.

Embarrassing Moment 8:

He changed into a new set of clothes and waited for her downstairs. To give her a "surprise," he decided to jump out from behind a tree to scare her. But as he jumped, he accidentally stepped on a watermelon peel, not only landing himself in a puddle, but also splashing mud all over her.

Embarrassing Moment 9:

He was invited to a friend's house to play, and discovered many gifts that he had intended for his own girlfriend. Suppressing his anger, he asked, and his friend proudly said they were from his girlfriend.

Embarrassing Moment 10:

Feeling that their relationship had become quite close, she asked: "At the beginning, how did you think of writing me notes?"

"I wrote to every girl in the class, only you replied."
