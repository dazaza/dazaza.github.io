The cheap anti-acne mask made from Chinese cabbage leaves is currently popular online. This mask, originating from "Turkish folklore," uses very simple ingredients: the commonly eaten Chinese cabbage. It's said that Turkish women have fair and tender skin, with few occurrences of acne or other skin diseases, due to their frequent use of Chinese cabbage leaves on their faces. The method for making a Chinese cabbage mask involves taking a fresh cabbage leaf, washing it, laying it flat on a clean cutting board, and then rolling over it gently with a rolling pin or beer bottle for about 10 minutes until the leaf becomes mushy. Apply the mushy leaf to your face, changing it every 10 minutes, repeating three times, and doing it once daily. Dr. Yao, the director of dermatology at the China Academy of Chinese Medical Sciences, believes that this Chinese cabbage mask is indeed effective for treating acne and whitening the skin. This is because Chinese cabbage is rich in vitamins and other nutrients, can remove oils, and has unique heat-clearing and detoxifying effects. Those with acne might try this mask, but if any redness or small bumps appear as signs of an allergic reaction, usage should be immediately stopped.

As for carrot masks, the preparation method is also simple. The ingredients are 500 grams of carrots and 5 grams of flour. First, take fresh carrots, wash them, and crush them. Then, mix in the flour to form a paste, apply the carrot paste to your face, do it every other day, and leave it on for 10 minutes. This mask can help eliminate acne and reduce facial wrinkles. If the crushed carrots stick to your skin without adding flour, you can skip the flour. Washing your face with the juice squeezed from the carrots can achieve similar results.

Although vegetable masks are effective and can be self-administered by patients, the most effective are still traditional Chinese medicine (TCM) masks. Generally, this is done twice a week, for about 10 minutes each time, continuing for 1-3 months, which is highly effective for eliminating acne. However, different causes of acne require different combinations of TCM herbs, so patients are advised to consult a doctor before using them.
