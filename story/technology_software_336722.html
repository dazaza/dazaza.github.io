Wenzhou Ear, Nose and Throat Hospital

by wzaewgk2u0 on 2010-03-30 14:34:52

Wenzhou Aier Otorhinolaryngology Hospital, as the affiliated hospital of Shanghai Changgeng Rhinology and Laryngology Research Institute, is also the only affiliated hospital of the institute in Zhejiang Province. The hospital is located at the Shangjiang Building on Airport Avenue in Wenzhou Economic and Technological Development Zone (No. 1 Pujing Road). It has been approved by the Wenzhou Health Bureau to be a modern otorhinolaryngology specialty hospital integrating medical treatment, health care, and rehabilitation. The hospital has complete department facilities, strong technical forces, advanced medical equipment, and is the first prestigious professional otorhinolaryngology hospital in southern Zhejiang. The hospital gathers well-known experts and professors from Shanghai, scholars and experts who enjoy special allowances from the State Council, and has gradually formed a talent ladder in the medical discipline through a perfect talent cultivation system.

The hospital has three major professional departments: the Rhinology and Laryngology Center, Ophthalmology, and Stomatology. It has more than ten specialized outpatient clinics including Rhinology, Pharyngology, Otology, Ophthalmology Center, Orthodontics, Oral Internal Medicine, Oral Restoration, Oral and Maxillofacial Surgery, Oral Preventive Health Care, and a Snoring Disorder Research Institute.

Wenzhou Aier Otorhinolaryngology Hospital is the first in southern Zhejiang to introduce large and medium-sized medical equipment such as the German nasal endoscope system, the American DNR low-temperature plasma ablation system, laryngoscope microscopic system, Japanese fiber laryngoscope system, surgical microscope system, otorhinolaryngology microwave treatment system, otorhinolaryngology red light treatment system, micro-light treatment system, Alcon cataract ultrasonic emulsification from the United States, panoramic X-ray machine (Technic), computer-controlled painless dental anesthesia instrument, Italian dental chair comprehensive treatment instrument with oral endoscope. The hospital has built the first spacious surgical live broadcast center and hundred-level laminar flow purification operating room in southern Zhejiang.

The hospital's characteristic surgical projects include: nasal endoscopic surgery, sinus opening surgery, nasal septum correction surgery, nasal polyp removal surgery, prenasal cyst excision surgery, tonsillectomy, various snoring disorder surgeries, vocal cord polyp removal surgery, total laryngectomy, partial laryngectomy, tympanic membrane repair surgery, inner ear fenestration surgery, acoustic neuroma resection surgery, and other various difficult diseases.

After years of accumulation, Wenzhou Aier Otorhinolaryngology Hospital has established a complete set of effective diagnostic and therapeutic systems, effectively safeguarding patient rights and winning praise from a wide range of patients and society. It has multiple times won the "Most Popular Specialty Hospital" award in China's Annual Health Overall Evaluation List.

Aier Otorhinolaryngology Hospital strives to align with domestic medical new concepts and closely follows the latest trends in world medicine development. It adheres to the principle of "doing small specialties with precision and strength, serving the great demand for health." It pays particular attention to the diagnosis and treatment of difficult diseases, relieving patients' illnesses through surgery. Upholding the new Aier operation concept of "performing one surgery, producing one masterpiece, making one friend," Aier Otorhinolaryngology Hospital will benefit tens of millions of people in Zhejiang with its warm medical environment, advanced equipment, excellent skills, reasonable charges, and star-rated service.
