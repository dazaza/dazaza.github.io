1. Drive shaft: Instead of using belts and pulleys which require pulley covers and are not a new design, the universal joint is a clever new power transmission mechanism for drive shafts.

2. Rotational angle changes: The structure can be extended or retracted from the middle or single side to achieve this operation.

3. Substitute coupling: In some applications, couplings and protruding cores cannot achieve precise alignment. Universal joints can operate smoothly and reduce assembly time while considering "cost".

4. Substitute bevel gears: Unable to increase or decrease speed, adopting a universal joint results in a smaller size with processing accuracy comparable to gears. It can also connect pneumatic cylinders (AIR CYLINDER).

5. Cost consideration: When using universal joints from any manufacturer, the same conditions apply, and the total cost must include purchase fees, additional engineering costs, and installation time.
