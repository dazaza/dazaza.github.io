Post-80s, thus grow old - Playing with water and flirtation - Baidou.com - Real online interaction brings you and me closer.

by maobohe0905 on 2010-11-03 09:49:24

1. You don't send text messages as much anymore. It's better to make a call when there's something to talk about, and you prefer to keep conversations short.

2. You don't like spending too much time lost in thoughts about emotions. You feel those sentimental little feelings no longer belong to you.

3. Sometimes you enjoy being alone, but you no longer pity yourself for being alone. You like going online, listening to music, and enjoying yourself. Occasionally you play games, but you no longer play level-up games. You often play mini-games or competitive ones.

4. In the past, you only watched entertainment programs and waited on time for them. Now you watch legal channels, and you often pay attention to scientific exploration. Occasionally, when you see a handsome guy on TV, you always ask the people around you, "Who is this man? Is he new?" The people around you would say, "Don't you even know him?"

5. You take time to go to the cinema to watch movies. When you were a child, you thought it was a very romantic place for romance. Now you go for the directors you are familiar with or the actors you like.

6. You've joined many QQ groups, from elementary school to work, with common interests or the same zodiac signs, but you don't like to speak up in the group. You just occasionally open it to see what they've said.

7. You have a blog, sometimes you write things, and there are also some photos. You won't often visit other people's blogs because your blog is written for yourself to read.

8. Occasionally, you hum songs that are familiar melodies from several years ago. You may not remember the lyrics clearly, but you still hum along.

9. There will be at least one book by your bedside, maybe it's a book you haven't finished reading, but it's definitely a good book. You occasionally flip through it until you finish reading it.

10. You start to realize the seriousness of not eating breakfast. No matter what, you will buy some breakfast to fill your stomach.

11. You can never give an exact answer to your age, 24 or 25, half 24.

12. You have an account book, and you won't be like before, not knowing where your money went.

13. You spend a weekend shopping in the supermarket and buy your living necessities for a week or even half a month. When the fridge is empty, you will remember to fill it up.

14. When you were a child, you liked throwing things around everywhere. Now, when you see somewhere untidy, you naturally tidy it up. You're uncomfortable with messiness.

15. When you call your parents, you start to remind them to take care of their health. Sometimes you feel like you're the adult, and they're like your children.

16. You don't waste water or electricity.

17. Your clothes may not be many, but they will certainly be decent.

18. Occasionally you gather with friends and colleagues. You drink a bit of alcohol, but you won't easily let yourself get drunk.

19. When you see very young children, you feel childhood was truly beautiful.

20. You have a webpage that you browse frequently.
