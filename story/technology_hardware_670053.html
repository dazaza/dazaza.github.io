In order to promote the reform and development of China's capital market and strengthen the supervision of securities and futures, the CSRC released the "Announcement of Recruitment for Middle and High-level Professional Talents in 2012" today to recruit a group of middle and high-level professional talents from home and abroad. This recruitment will continue until the end of April. According to reports, the CSRC's talent introduction is aimed at the domestic and international financial sectors. The recruitment targets middle and high-level professional talents who have served as middle or senior management or professional technical positions in well-known financial institutions, managerial talents who have held positions equivalent to or above the level of a section chief in central government departments, experts and scholars with high academic attainments, fame and influence in economic and financial fields, Xiangyang Talent Network, Xiangfan Talent Network, Xiangyang Recruitment Network, Xiangfan Recruitment Network, Xiangyang Talent, Xiangfan Talent, Xiangyang Recruitment, Xiangfan Recruitment, Talent Network, Recruitment Network. This overseas talent introduction mainly focuses on Chinese overseas students, while also considering overseas Chinese and other foreign middle and high-level talents; the introduction area mainly focuses on developed market countries and regions, while also considering other countries and regions. The introduced personnel will work in the securities and futures regulatory system and a transition period of 1 to 2 years will be implemented. During the transition period, based on the individual conditions and professional direction of the introduced personnel, they will serve in the Planning and Development Committee (referred to as "Planning Committee") of the securities and futures regulatory system. After the transition period, according to the needs of regulatory work and personal wishes, they may continue to serve in the Planning Committee or enter relevant departments of the conference agency or relevant units of the regulatory system. The CSRC stated that it will provide corresponding working conditions and salary treatment for the introduced personnel according to national policies and combine them with actual situations. For talents who have entered the country's "Thousand Talents Program", they will enjoy corresponding treatment according to relevant regulations. For talents who meet the application conditions of the country's "Thousand Talents Program", the CSRC will assist in their application.
