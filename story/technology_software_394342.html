【Original】11 Types of People Suitable for Running an Online Store

by anonymous on 2011-03-28 10:05:41

Hello everyone, I am wangzi, which means prince in Chinese. Please don't take it literally, as it is just my nickname. I hope that in the future we can all learn from each other and exchange ideas and experiences about running online stores!

Alright, let's discuss who are the most suitable people to run an online store. If you're still a beginner, you can also see which type you belong to! (Purely personal opinion)

The first type of person: Good mentality

These people have a positive and optimistic attitude. Having a good mentality is a prerequisite for success. Although mentality is not the only condition for success, it is a necessary one. Otherwise, many large enterprises and companies would not be constantly emphasizing the issue of mentality. (Let me tell you a secret, when I was working before, I worked in a large shoe company. That company held morning meetings every day, and every day they emphasized the same issue - mentality. At the time, I didn't pay attention to these things, but after leaving, I had quite a few reflections. In fact, this is corporate culture. The company does this to improve employee quality. The company actually invests a lot of resources in this work, but very few truly understand it.) Hehe, sorry for going off-topic. I just wanted to give everyone an example to make you realize how important mentality is. As long as we adjust our mentality, no problem will be difficult, and all obstacles can be overcome. Have you adjusted your mentality?

The second type of person: Very smart people

These people are flexible-minded and quick-witted. They often act faster than others and do what they think of immediately. But this is not to belittle those who are less intelligent. I myself belong to the less intelligent type, but this does not mean we cannot succeed. I firmly believe that as long as we are not intellectually disabled, everything is possible, which leads me to the next type of person. I must remind those smart people not to underestimate others like in the "Tortoise and the Hare" story. There are probably quite a few such people in reality. This is just targeted at some people, don't get upset. Whether or not they are like that, only they themselves know clearly. For example, some people think their conditions are better than others, so they look down on them and think others are not as good as them. Such people do exist in large numbers. Reflect on yourself and strive to become a perfect smart person!

The third type of person: Diligent and active people

These people are willing to work hard, endure hardships, and have enthusiasm. Therefore, even if they are not very smart, as long as they are willing to study, think, observe, and put in more effort and time than others, they will eventually succeed. A sudden thought came to mind: the secret to success is to work a little harder every day than others! Is that right? Have you done it?

The fourth type of person: People with strong ability and efficiency

These people have strong execution capabilities. No matter how difficult the task is, they can always complete it in the end. This shows that they have a good plan before doing anything. Of course, this plan includes many things. Simply put, it is to analyze a matter thoroughly, formulate a plan, and implement it. Judging from this, we can also become such people, but we need to practice to reach that level. So we should start practicing now. Although this is a bit challenging, we must persevere and stick to it until the end! Can you do it?

The fifth type of person: People who are dissatisfied with the status quo and keep striving

These people are never satisfied with their current state and keep striving to do better. It's like when someone gets paid, the first time they receive 1000 yuan and see others getting 2000 yuan, they feel that others have much more money. But when they get 2000 yuan, they don't feel satisfied anymore and want even more. These are the people who are not content with the status quo. As the saying goes, people tend to climb higher, and water flows lower. Perhaps some might say, how greedy is this person? Yes, instead of calling it greed, let's call it ambition. And starting a business precisely requires such ambition, but it should be moderate, otherwise it may have the opposite effect. So, if you were the person receiving the salary, how would you feel?

The sixth type of person: People with life wealth goals

In fact, these people are similar to the previous type. They are full of expectations for their lives and dare to pursue them. Basically, they are also not satisfied with the status quo.

The seventh type of person: People who discard their pride

This means that if you want to get rich, you need a thick skin. Take distributing flyers for example, many people can't bring themselves to distribute flyers on the street because they might feel shy or fear encountering acquaintances. Hehe, I once had such an experience. It was the first time I did that, helping a friend. At first, I was really shy, afraid that people wouldn't accept the flyers and also worried about meeting acquaintances. My hand didn't even stretch out, definitely not asking. Later, when I saw my friend handing out flyers, I followed suit. Surprisingly, people accepted them. Hehe, Guangzhou VIP Information Technology Co., Ltd. Back then, I was young and naive, just trying to finish the task quickly, so I handed out several flyers at once. Thinking about it now, it was really interesting. So, if it were you, could you do it?

The eighth type of person: The most innovative people

These people have unique insights, always staying ahead of others, and letting others chase after them. This corresponds to the saying: "When others don't have it, I do; when others have it, I excel; when others excel, I change." Can you dare to innovate?

The ninth type of person: People with great confidence

These people, regardless of what they do, first have confidence in themselves, believing that they can achieve it, believing that they can definitely do it, believing that they are the best. With confidence, they tackle tasks with full confidence. Do you have confidence? Or are you indulging in self-pity and complaints?

The tenth type of person: People who can endure hardship

These people are not afraid of hard work, no matter how bitter or tired they are, they never give up. Perhaps many people can't endure hardship; as soon as they feel the bitterness, they have the idea of giving up. This only shows that their willpower is not strong enough, unable to endure for the sake of their goals. If you want to succeed, you must not be afraid of hard work and be able to withstand loneliness. Success doesn't come overnight. Can you withstand loneliness?

The eleventh type of person: People who cherish time

This is also very important. Efficiency in whatever you do is measured by time. Only those who know how to cherish time can succeed faster than others. People often say that cherishing time is the same as cherishing life, and time is money. Are you making good use of your time? Don't let others get up at 7 in the morning while you are still lying in bed having comfortable dreams. Don't say you have nothing to do and can sleep more. Things need to be sought out to be done, they won't come to you. If that happens, it's likely trouble. Troubles may find you, but good things may never come to you.

I've said so much without realizing it. These are all my personal views. If we apply these to online stores, which ones do you need to achieve? In short, remember that a good mentality is the most important thing. Combine it with actual action, and you won't be far from success. Go for it!

Alright, it's pretty late. I also hope everyone can share their own opinions and thoughts. Let's exchange and discuss together! Prince wishes everyone booming business and rolling wealth!
