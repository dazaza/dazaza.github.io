Prospect Analysis of Changchun Website Construction Market

by zhidian520 on 2011-07-06 14:07:27

The network analyzes the current market and prospects of website construction from multiple perspectives. 1, Overview of Changchun Website Construction Market: 1) Brief introduction to the development of Changchun website construction market. The first generation of Changchun website construction technology: using general web page production software, converting some flat page effects into web pages, then linking the web pages together to form an enterprise website. Websites made with "first-generation website construction technology" are troublesome to maintain as updates and modifications to the website content require professionals, and modifications or additions to the web pages require payment, which causes enterprises to often not update their website content, losing the significance of building a website; at the same time, due to the lack of interactivity in pure static pages, customers cannot communicate well with enterprises. The second generation of Changchun website construction technology: refers to adopting some database management modules (such as news release, product release, etc.) for a certain function of the website on the basis of the first generation of website construction technology, and managing these individual function modules through the backend of the website. Websites made with "second-generation website construction technology" to a certain extent get rid of the shortcomings of the first generation of website construction technology in terms of difficulties in updating website content, suitable for large enterprise websites that frequently update and maintain the website on a daily basis and have unique needs for each functional module of the website. However, since custom development is required for different website construction needs of enterprises, the price is generally high, making it difficult for small and medium-sized enterprises to afford. The third generation of Changchun website construction technology: Since the beginning of 2003, the third generation of website construction technology - intelligent website construction system has appeared. Website users can conveniently and quickly manage their own websites through the intelligent website construction system, define the framework content of the website by themselves, and upgrade the functions of the website at any time, and solve the demand for personalized front-end interface of each customer. Using the intelligent website construction system for the development and construction of enterprise websites can meet the general needs of most small and medium-sized enterprises for website construction, with a short development cycle, convenient updates and maintenance, it is the ideal choice for small and medium-sized enterprises to build websites. Intelligent website construction also has certain drawbacks, the more flexible the function, the higher the relative complexity of operation, many customers do not have professional knowledge, cannot use it well, thus the beauty of the generated website is relatively poor, and the later information release and maintenance are relatively troublesome. The fourth generation of Changchun website construction technology: our company now promotes standardized website customization development, which combines the advantages of the second and third generations, allowing customers to enjoy the quality and service of the second-generation customized development under the condition of trying to reduce costs.

2) Prospects of Changchun website construction market. In recent years, the website construction business has been rising rapidly, and the industry market is getting bigger and bigger. In terms of domestic situation, the market demand in coastal developed provinces and cities is greater than that in central and western provinces and cities, and the popularization work of business is also very well done, and many industries have formed a situation of competing to build enterprise websites and carry out online marketing. The application of new technologies will make enterprise website construction more charming. With the integration and development of technology, many technologies that are hotly applied in other industries, such as video, 3D animation, virtual reality, etc., have already been transplanted to the Internet, and they will soon appear on the websites of enterprise customers; new network programming languages (.net technology) and server CDN (content delivery network) technology will also make the website structure tighter, access smoother, and more adaptable to new requirements. Online marketing services will be integrated with website construction to provide integrated services. Enterprise customers are no longer satisfied with just building a website and then carrying out online marketing activities on their own. Network companies also need to formulate targeted online marketing strategies and implement them for enterprise customers, so that enterprise websites can truly play a role and bring practical results to customers. This puts higher demands on network companies, and practitioners in website construction must strengthen self-study and improvement to meet this requirement. Personalized personal websites, thematic websites facing individuals, and various forms of personal website construction services in Changchun website construction have just begun to show signs, and personal website construction services will become a new business growth point.
