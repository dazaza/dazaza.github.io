Keep your memories: Best-selling laptops with hundred-G hard drives recommended

by seosh on 2008-03-14 09:07:02

Have you ever experienced this: when you try to put your favorite stuff into your laptop, you find that the laptop's "stomach" is not big enough? This makes it hard for you to decide what to give up and what to keep. If you were to buy another hard drive, it would also be quite expensive. But now, with the development of technology, the capacity of laptops is getting larger and larger, allowing you to easily store your favorite things.

(Picture) Preserve Your Memories - Recommended Hot-Selling Laptops with 100GB Hard Drives

This summer, IT manufacturers are not only fighting price wars, but they are also heavily promoting the configuration upgrades of laptops. Laptops with over 100GB hard drives are everywhere, and with increased storage, speed naturally cannot be slow. Laptops equipped with dual-core processors, up to 1GB of RAM, and 100GB hard drives naturally attract everyone's attention. So now, let me introduce a few such laptops to you, hoping that the large-capacity hard drives can preserve more of your precious memories!
