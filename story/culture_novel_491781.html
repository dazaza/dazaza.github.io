Wu Dong Qian Kun Chapter 134: Ice玄Sword

by fanyete on 2011-09-19 23:48:16

Staring at the retreating figure of Lin Dong, after a long while, the crowd in the square finally came back to their senses, and waves of exclamation arose immediately.

"Who is he? He's so strong that even Cao Zhu isn't his rival."

"Should be a talisman master from our Flame City?"

"Lin Dong! I've seen him in the arena. It was him who killed Wei Tong of Blood Robe Sect."

"Oh, so he is Lin Dong. Indeed, hearsay doesn't compare with meeting in person..."

Hearing all the whispers around him, Cao Zhu looked extremely displeased. A talisman master from Heavenly Fire City wanted to help him up, but he shook him off harshly. He had lost face today big time, not only did he lose so badly, but also lost the Ice Mysterious Sword. It was truly a case of losing more than gaining!

"Ice Mysterious Sword..."

Thinking about his treasure, Cao Zhu's heart was bleeding. The Ice Mysterious Iron was quite rare, so it was very expensive. To forge the three Ice Mysterious Swords, he had spent nearly 30,000 Yang Yuan Stones. But now, the most expensive thing on his body had been taken away by Lin Dong as war spoils without hesitation. If he wasn't afraid of Lin Dong's strange abilities, he would have rushed forward just now despite getting seriously injured to snatch back the "Ice Mysterious Sword..."

"Let's go!"

Since the item had already been taken, no matter how much his heart bled, it wouldn't do any good. Cao Zhu could only suppress his anger, get up, and walk towards outside the square.

"Lin Dong, right? You wait for me. When my seniors from Heavenly Fire City arrive in a few days, I'll make you return everything!"

Looking at Cao Zhu who was walking away while gritting his teeth, the several Heavenly Fire City talisman masters behind him glanced at each other, none daring to offend him at this moment. They could only obediently follow behind him and left this square in a sorry state.

Seeing the dispirited backs of Cao Zhu and others, Zi Yue only slightly shook her head on the corridor. Looking far into the distance, she understood from Lin Dong's glance before he left that he didn't like her actions.

This kind of look made Zi Yue feel a bit astonished. From childhood till now, because of her special identity, outstanding spiritual talent, and beauty, the looks she received were either respectful or fiery admiration. This kind of look with some impatience was really the first time she encountered.

"Hmph, wanting to act tough in front of me, come back when you can show some performance in the tower fight."

Recalling that look, Zi Yue couldn't help but let out a light cold snort. With a wave of her jade-like hand, she turned around and left. With her cold and proud character, among her peers in Flame City, there was no one who could make her sincerely convinced.
