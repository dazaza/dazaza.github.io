Since the grand final, Hong Chen has frequently been rumored to be the hottest contender for the championship. Duan Linxi was spotted by the director team during the preliminary selection in Chengdu for her fresh self-accompanied singing style and was the first to enter the national top ten safety box. Born in Baoshan, Yunnan, she has an extraordinary family background and has almost become the motivational spokesperson for this year's Super Girl. Similar to Zeng Yike, her original music capabilities will lay a solid foundation for Duan Linxi's future development path.

Liu Xin once went to South Korea for professional artist training. Her experience in the Korean entertainment industry revealed her as an all-round artist excelling in singing, dancing, original music creation, and playing musical instruments even during the preliminary selection stage. The high popularity she gained after winning the championship in the Changsha district made her journey to the top three smoother. Many veteran musicians praised Liu Xin as the most marketable product, and with her rich experience, Liu Xin is undoubtedly the all-round artist that Tianyu will focus on packaging and promoting.
