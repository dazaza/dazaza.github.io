Love is not just love

He got married, and was happy ever after! The girl's parents have always disagreed with their marriage. However, the girl was relentless. As long as they had a house, they could get married! But the boy kept failing to provide a house! Until the girl's heart was broken! She left that city!

He felt that the days with her were the happiest. He liked to hold her gently, seeking a kind of sweetness, a kind of happiness. She felt that the days with him were the sweetest. She liked to put her hand on his chest, seeking a sense of security, a warmth. He once told her: no matter where in the world, we must not let go of each other's hands.

She once told him, no matter where you go, take me with you! Time passed slowly. They were still happy. In their happiness, they also had quarrels and separations. Fortunately, they always believed: nothing could separate them. However, bad news still came. She broke up with the boy, saying she fell in love with someone else.

In fact, it wasn't true. What the girl wanted was money and housing. Maybe from the day we met, this ending was destined. Life is very realistic! Family, money, education, identity, status... People say it's the 21st century, we should have free love, but how many people can really break through these constraints and be together?

The words of our elders are not without reason, it seems I understood too late. I've heard such a metaphor before, saying two people are like two parallel lines, they can only face each other forever, never intersect! Some people say parallel lines are the scariest, but I think intersecting lines are the scariest. Clearly, they had an intersection, yet they would eventually drift apart... just like us. I left, hoping you will be happy in the future. Two people who love each other don't necessarily have to be together, silently blessing each other is enough.

Some lyrics are written so well, saying as long as you remember he once loved you, the rest is not that important! But when it really happens to oneself, how can one let go so easily after truly loving? That subtle pain in your heart, you can deceive others, but ultimately you cannot deceive yourself!! He has decided to break up, and she agreed.

But she couldn't let go, she was still attached, her heart was knotted with sorrow! In the morning, he woke up from a dream, and found no one beside him! There was a note on the desk, simply saying, "I'm gone." When he opened the wardrobe, her clothes were already gone, but he could still faintly smell a delicate fragrance, reminiscent of yesterday's love!

They broke up, first proposed by him, finally she quietly left! A week after the breakup: she endured the intense pain in her heart, living alone, eating alone, walking and stopping alone. But she always thought of him, especially at night, it was like being pierced by ten thousand arrows. At that moment, she wished she could die, life was so painful. He was very relaxed, living alone, eating alone, walking and stopping alone. Occasionally he also thought of her, but he blamed her for leaving too hastily!

She started to adapt to this kind of life, but became more timid, afraid of the dark, the bedside lamp was always turned off at dawn! She also retained some aftermaths, unable to bear seeing couples being affectionate. Others' intimate actions, like holding hands or hugging waists, made her sentimental and tearful. At that moment, she wished she could become blind.

Looking at the new number, she always wanted to call him impulsively, but ultimately held back. Rationality and emotions tortured each other, leaving only her fragmented heart. He liked this kind of life, having free space, freely支配his own life, unrestrained. No longer did he have to make several calls or send dozens of text messages every day, nor endure her endless nagging. To be honest, her love made him feel suffocated. Occasionally seeing others in pairs, he even maliciously thought, "It's your turn to suffer!"

They once read a book together, which said: "As long as you really loved that person, there is no remedy for this pain, because the pain itself is the treatment." At that time, he thought, how foolish. She thought, it makes sense. "When we were young, we fell in love for love; when we grew up, we stayed together for marriage." When he saw this sentence, he thought, it makes sense. When she saw this sentence, she thought, how foolish!

Occasionally logging onto QQ, he never saw her online, but she always saw his presence. Her hidden avatar was like silent dialogue. He thought she didn't say anything, but she thought she had said everything. Once, his classmate asked him, "What happened? Did you break up?"

He was a bit shocked, "How do you know?" Then he received a piece of text: "Watching love drift away little by little, I am powerless, can only toast to memories in the quiet night, getting completely drunk!" This was her personal information on QQ. After logging online so many times, he never thought of checking certain things. Perhaps what he overlooked was not just these things, but perhaps also her love. Just sometimes, realization comes too late...

For her, time is the best doctor.

She gradually came out of it, but some things remained confused. What was so good about him that made me so heartbroken in love? When breaking up, she always wondered, but could never find an answer, because love is the best reason. Loving someone means treating them as a treasure, everything seems good, everything looks pleasing. Now that she doesn't love him anymore, she laughs at herself for being so foolish to want to die for him before. Now she has a boyfriend she loves, who has been by her side since her breakup, loving her deeply, she feels satisfied. Sometimes she also wonders, if she had stayed with him then, how would it have turned out? She doesn't know. Occasionally, she thinks of him, reminiscing about the days they spent together, but it feels like a century ago, separated by thick yellowed memories, already a thing of the past, like something that happened to someone else, no longer interesting.

For him, time is the worst doctor.

After the breakup, he dated a few girlfriends, all failed, always feeling incompatible... He needed distance and freedom. But he discovered that every woman in love was like a madman in the asylum, sensitive, clingy, hysterical.

Even on some issues, they were worse than she was. He began to regret! Troubled, he began to flip back through the pages of memory, those traces of their love were vividly clear! These things he once ignored now formed a line, straight into the depths of his vulnerable heart, like a whip lashing his remorseful heart. He finally realized why he gave her up at the time, it was because she loved too much, cared too much, paid too much attention. It was really laughable, there could be such a reason.

He was too young and ignorant back then, wanting freedom. He took her love for granted, like sunshine and air, enjoying it as a matter of course. Because of her indulgence, he only felt annoyed, rarely thinking for her. Sometimes he wonders, if he had stayed with her then, how would it have turned out?

One night he had a strange dream, dreaming that one day he saw her on the street. A slim boy was by her side, helping her carry her shopping, and holding her hand. It was obvious that the boy was considerate, just like she used to be to him. She was talking to the boy next to her, and when she turned around and saw him, she suddenly gave him a radiant smile. And then he woke up. After waking up, his heart was shattered with pain, but her smile lingered in his mind, impossible to shake off... Although dreams are dreams, he really missed the love of a lifetime!!

The boy was also very sad, and casually started a relationship with a much younger girl! Just because of sadness! Loneliness! The girl cried and married someone who loved her, the boy just pretended to smile and blessed them!

Friends, if you are in love, please look at yourself, have you unknowingly fallen into my story, playing different roles. If you accidentally play the role of that boy, please stop immediately; if you are the girl, please be patient a little longer, give him another chance, give yourself another chance, and reduce the painful memories... I also dedicate this to my friends, hoping you understand, this is my answer to your question, are you satisfied?
