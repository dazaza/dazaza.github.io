Love Confession Letter

Love

Literal explanation: The feeling of mutual affection between people. Love is attachment, intimacy, longing, and reliance.

My understanding: Selfless giving and dedication. Love is about giving, love is about missing, love is about going through experiences hand in hand, love is about mutual respect. Love is transparent, love is pure, love is sacred and inviolable. Love has taught me tolerance, inclusiveness, forbearance, and compromise.

Love brings happiness but also sorrow. Love in the heat of passion is full of joy, but after the passion fades, it's filled with tears. Love can make dignity seem cheap. Love is as gentle as water, as passionate as fire, and can plunge one into a state of extreme suffering. Love is so contradictory, yet despite knowing its potential to hurt, its allure is irresistible.

I don't know if my understanding of love is accurate, but what I do know is that in matters of love, I am a failure, a complete and utter failure.

Jing:

During the year we were together, you gave me many touching moments, much happiness, and joy. The time we spent together was always bright and sunny. I once told you, "You are my sunshine, my only sunshine." Do you remember? Coming out of depression, my world was dark and without light, but your appearance was the most beautiful memory of my life. But why did you give me fleeting happiness only to take it away so suddenly? I was completely unprepared.

I said I'm not someone who clings, but I apologize, at this moment I cannot just pack up my love and leave your world. I once said, "Don't leave me when I can't live without you," even though that sounds selfish, love itself is selfish.

Wife, do you remember the words you spoke, the promises you made, the vows you swore? If you've forgotten, let me remind you.

You said you couldn't live without me, you wouldn't leave me, you would always be by my side, you wouldn't abandon me, you wouldn't waver in our feelings for each other, the love you gave me was unmatched by anyone else, you loved me more than anyone, you said this relationship belonged to both of us and couldn't be decided by one person alone, you said you would definitely go with me under the Eiffel Tower and take pictures of me bare-bottomed while you collected money, you said... so many things... Have you forgotten all of these?

But why have you now given up on this relationship? I know I've made mistakes, I know the past pain has made my heart narrow, I have issues, but why won't you even give me one chance? I can change, really, I can. No one is perfect, everyone makes mistakes, and so do you and I. I've given you chances, why can't you give me one? Actually, everything you've said, I believe. Even though I repeatedly ask questions aloud, deep down I have no doubts. I just want to hear your explanation, but why is your attitude so indifferent, so cold?

There has been no betrayal between us, no principle-breaking issues. All of this happened because of a message from your ex-boyfriend. You told your friends you would leave me to marry him, and had them deceive me. Do you know how I felt at that moment? Dizzy, darkness everywhere. When we first got together, I told you that no matter what, don't lie to me. We should be open and honest with each other, transparent. You agreed, so why can I treat you this way, but you can't treat me the same?

I consider myself strong, brave. I rarely reveal my true feelings to my family and friends, but in front of you, I am so vulnerable. Do you know how many tears I shed every night holding you in my arms after finding out about your affair? How much pain I endured? Only I know.

You said no matter what, don't let you lose track of me, but why do you now make it impossible for me to find you? That time when I lay in the hospital bed reading your messages, I felt so heartbroken. I knew how much pain I caused you, I couldn't bear seeing you like that, so I came back to you. But now, my situation is exactly the same as yours was then. Why can you be so cold, ignoring me completely? Did you really love me? Can you really treat me this way? This must not be your true feelings, right?

You said you liked hearing me call you wife, you liked me hugging you to sleep, you liked being with me... Do you not want these things anymore? Are they all false? No matter what your thoughts or attitudes are, I don't want to give up on my efforts, but please don't keep pushing me away.

You told your friends your ex-boyfriend is better, but did I ever ask if you wanted to choose him? If so, I could let go and give you happiness. You told me those were jokes, so why do you think giving me a chance is a waste?

I know I was wrong, and I can change. Things I shouldn't say, I won't say; things I shouldn't mention, I won't bring up. Everything you want, I'll give you without reservation. Anything you don't like, I can abandon. They mean nothing to me because you are my whole world. Don't negate me in just two days. You yourself said love is between two people, you don't have the right to decide its fate alone.

Wife, give me one more chance to love you, care for you, look after you, protect you. I don't want to give you up, I can't lose you, I can't be without you. Believe in me, I will change. Believe in me, I will make you happy. Believe in me, I am the one who loves you the most.

Without you, I can't do anything, I have no interest in anything. Wife, if you come back, I'll be as obedient as before. You'll still be the boss, the queen, and I'll obediently go back to work at the TV station. I won't ask my parents for a single penny again. I'll work hard, strive forward, and make you happy. We will still go to Paris, the city of romance, Vienna, the city of music, California's coastline, and the Danube River that flows through nine countries. We'll leave our names and footprints there. I haven't forgotten any of this, I must achieve all of it with you.

Stupid wife, silly wife, piggy woman, come back. I will always be your Bai Xiaochi, your Doraemon. I haven't changed, I will never change. I love you, my love has never diminished. And I hope you will always be my Yutong, never changing. Please turn around and look at me. I'm right here waiting for you, watching over you. Don't give up on me. Once missed, it's forever missed. One turn is a lifetime. Please give me one more chance, a glimmer of hope!

Teamo Jing

In the days ahead, I resolve to study hard, work hard, and serve my wife wholeheartedly, actively responding to every request she makes, making myself worthy of the title of husband.

Now, I will briefly report my understanding of my relationship over the past year:

1. Too suspicious, listening to idle talk from others, failing to rationally find my place.

2. Using insulting language towards my wife, and collaborating with others in such behavior.

3. Failing to timely meet my wife's requests and not clearly explaining the reasons, due to my own face-saving issues, affecting normal marital life.

4. Often having a bad attitude, lacking patience, yelling at my wife, and behaving shamefully by biting her.

The above is my understanding. If there are any omissions or areas I haven't reflected on, I hope my wife forgives me. In the days ahead, I will be devoted to my wife. I firmly believe that under my wife's leadership, our family will become more harmonious and happy.

I eagerly await returning to my place in the family. Please approve my request and give me a chance to reform and start anew. If my wife thinks I am not yet ready to rejoin the family, I am willing to undergo further evaluation. I will continue to study hard, recognize my shortcomings, improve, and comprehensively enhance myself to become a worthy husband who serves my wife wholeheartedly. If you still insist on leaving after reading this, please answer these questions honestly.

If one day, no one gets angry with you, no one restrains you, no one finds an excuse to call you just to act childishly and make you say something nice before hanging up, and such a me disappears, will you be sad?

Even though I won't comfort you when you're unhappy, won't share your sadness, won't break my heart with you anymore, all the things I once did for you that you never noticed, when your memories, your life, your world no longer have me, I know clearly that you won't feel a bit of sadness, a bit of regret, a bit of missing me, or any memory of me. If that day comes and I'm no longer in your life, please remember my kindness, my stubbornness, my inclusiveness, my care, my crazy words, my foolish words, and the words I said in tears when I was sad. If that day comes, I still hope you feel a bit of sadness, a bit of regret, a bit of missing me. If that day comes, you will never find me again.

Even though I am someone who likes to be jealous, domineering, and unreasonable, and cannot tolerate the person I love loving someone else, I still hope you have a better life than me. I hope to see you happily living every day! Remember the fool who once loved you, willing to give up everything, including his life, for you. Remember that fool who once existed in your life. My silly woman, you must be happy, not for others, not for yourself, but for me.
