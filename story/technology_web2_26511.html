Regarding "flOw - the strange sea creatures raised in blogs" and its reviews

by xloong on 2007-08-04 19:34:02

flOw is a magical sea creature that lives in a blog and can freely swim around in any corner of it. Unlike some blog pets that evolve by consuming RSS Feeds, flOw's growth system evolves based on the blog's traffic to develop its body. There are currently 15 stages of evolution forms. Sounds interesting, doesn't it? You don't even need to register; you just need to place the JS Code they provide into your own blog.
