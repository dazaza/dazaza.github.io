Details of kitchen door selection

by qwy854627 on 2011-05-27 15:48:07

The kitchen is a sacred and wonderful place for Chinese people. For thousands of years, the Chinese civilization has been accompanied by the evolution of kitchens. As for doors, especially kitchen doors, I think only in an environment where material life is satisfied will there be spiritual choices. In a kitchen with cabinets, doors not only separate the kitchen layout but also serve as decorations for the cabinets.

Types and Materials of Doors

Currently, materials available for making sliding doors on the market include wood panels, glass, mirrors, and other materials. The natural texture and patterns of wood panels give a feeling of simplicity, warmth, and gentleness, suitable for those who pursue a return to nature. Some manufacturers even offer dual-color panels, similar to the colorful shells of fashionable phones. After using one side for a while, you can flip it over to use the other side without spending extra money to enjoy a new feel. Currently, nearly 20 such panels are available on the market, commonly known as color-changing panels, greatly facilitating consumer usage needs. Frosted or fabric-textured glass doors give a sense of mystery and subtlety, creating unique effects under indoor lighting, suitable for those who pursue fashion. Silver-plated glass gives a sense of high-end luxury. Transparent glass doors give a clear and refreshing feel. New types of cabinet door materials also include louvered doors. Louvered doors have novel designs, elegant and lightweight, widely recognized in the market.

Door Thickness and Solidity

For sliding doors made of wood panels, it's best to choose 10mm thick panels for sturdiness, stability, and durability; slightly inferior ones may use 8mm or even 6mm thick panels, which feel thinner, lighter, and more prone to deformation, with less stability.

Door Frame and Panel Patterns

For branded kitchen cabinets, both the door frames and panels come from the same manufacturer, ensuring consistent color and patterns, achieving perfect uniformity, including the cabinet body colors matching the door panels perfectly. On the other hand, generic brands often piece together components, using imported hardware for the frames and domestically produced panels with similar patterns to deceive uninformed consumers.

Whether the Wheels are Smooth, Durable, Pressure-resistant, Safe, and Reliable

Branded kitchen cabinets usually use carbon fiber wheels with internal ball bearings and non-drying lubricants, allowing smooth and flexible movement. They have high load-bearing capacity, pressure resistance, and wear resistance without deformation. Inferior wheels are often made from engineering plastics or organic plastics, wearing out quickly, initially usable but prone to deformation over time, affecting usability. Additionally, good wheels often have two anti-jump devices to ensure safe and reliable sliding of cabinet doors. Poorer wheels often have only one anti-jump device or none at all, easily derailing during use and failing to ensure safety. Box-type closed structure wheels have better dustproofing effects, allowing normal use even in sandy and windy northern regions.

Whether the Cabinet Design is Professional, Scientific, and Fashionable

Currently popular fashion kitchen cabinets follow the principle of dividing into smaller parts, developing several different sub-cabinets that can be freely combined when custom-made. Drawers and adjustable shelves can be freely added or removed, with height adjustable at will. Some cabinets can even adjust length to better adapt to different space conditions. Cabinet heights can reach up to the ceiling, fully utilizing space. They can cleverly handle special positions like indoor pillars, radiators, and gas pipes, deeply favored by DIY enthusiasts.

Whether the Kitchen Accessories are Complete and Provide Convenient and Applicable Supporting Functions

Currently, many kitchen manufacturers have introduced practical and aesthetically pleasing accessories, such as grid racks, pull-out baskets, L-shaped brackets, etc., actively advocating a quality lifestyle centered on human needs.

Whether Green Environmental Protection Materials are Used

According to inspections by relevant national departments, most products from European imports or large domestic artificial board manufacturers meet the E1 standard, whereas some generic domestic products often exceed formaldehyde content limits. How to judge whether the formaldehyde content of kitchen cabinets meets standards? First, open the cabinet door and drawers. If there is a strong irritating smell, even causing tears, it indicates excessive formaldehyde content. Second, check the material inspection certificate of the merchant to see if they hold an inspection qualified report from the National Artificial Board Quality Supervision and Inspection Center. Third, the store sales staff must clearly note on the agreement that the used panels meet the European E1 environmental protection standard.

Whether There is a Professional Factory

To ensure production, branded kitchen cabinets mostly have professional factories with modern machinery, assembly line production, on-site installation. This completely avoids the inconvenience brought to consumers by on-site production by decoration companies. Moreover, product dimensions are accurate, structures strict, giving an overall aesthetic appeal.

After-sales Warranty Card

Most kitchen brands adopt a system where warranty cards are packaged with the goods. Another method is direct provision by local specialty stores. It's difficult to prevent specialty stores from falsifying information. Currently, some large brand kitchen cabinets use unique methods: the warranty card is mailed directly to the customer by the manufacturer. Consumers who are unsure about the warranty card can verify the information via phone or online channels. Generally, large kitchen brands prevent dealers from locally processing counterfeit products by adopting direct mailing of warranty cards and clearly indicating customer purchase information to eliminate the possibility of counterfeits.
