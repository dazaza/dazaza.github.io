"Me too..." Yi Shui said softly. Zhao Feng smiled, very lightly, with a little relief and a little fatigue, and the hand that was hugging Yi Shui stroked her smooth long hair. "What are you going to do?" asked Yi Shui. Zhao Feng spoke; he had his eyes closed at this moment. Yi Shui didn't know whether he didn't want to say or didn't know how to say. Just as she was thinking of changing the subject, Zhao Feng spoke: "Hua Zi opened a place behind my back. He didn't find the right people. It was a local tyrant from Hebei who ran off with all three of our money. Hua Zi only came to me then. I had no choice but to go to Hebei. This Hua Zi is too bold." After finishing, Zhao Feng sighed wearily.

Yi Shui had heard something about these places, which meant opening a casino. But Zhao Feng didn't open a long-term casino. He just seized opportunities to open once in a while, each time in a different location, very hidden. Generally, they were some relatively wealthy people who didn't care about money. Their biggest hobby was gambling. They found people like Zhao Feng who had "resources", paid them to gather gamblers. The people Zhao Feng's team found were also rich and loved to gamble. These people generally had cooperated with Zhao Feng before. Each session lasted at least three days and at most no more than two weeks. During this period, the gamblers were not allowed to leave the specified area. After the session ended, if the person who gathered the gamblers won, they would split the profit 4-6 with Zhao Feng's team. If others won, the big winner would split it 3-7 with Zhao Feng's team, and the small winner would split it 2-8, depending on the actual situation. This kind of business was the most profitable, but the risk was also the greatest. Zhao Feng's team never opened small sessions. Each time, the amount involved was large, and the relative danger was also great.

"Are all issues resolved?" asked Yi Shui.

"Hmm! Hua Zi almost lost his life..." Zhao Feng said slowly. He was really tired, leaning back on the sofa, and sighed deeply.

"You should take a nap," Yi Shui said softly.

"No, let me stay with you for a while..." said Zhao Feng.

Yi Shui stopped talking, leaning on Zhao Feng's chest, listening to his weary yet strong heartbeat.

Zhao Feng didn't say a word, his chest rising and falling with his breathing. This was the first time Zhao Feng directly told Yi Shui about his own affairs. Except when Yi Shui heard that Hua Zi almost lost his life, she felt a bit nervous, she didn't think there was anything wrong with these things. For some reason, when these things happened to Zhao Feng, she thought it was so normal, just like workers needing to work, doctors needing to perform surgery. She was just a bit worried about Zhao Feng's safety and whether her parents could accept Zhao Feng. Perhaps, Yi Shui still did not fully understand the path Zhao Feng had chosen...

"This is really good... girl... forever like this... more new beginnings..." Zhao Feng's weary voice sounded.

Today, Zhao Feng woke up very late; he was too tired.

Yi Shui received the interview notification, which was at 2 PM. When she pushed the bedroom door open and saw Zhao Feng still sleeping, she quietly stepped out, called for takeaway, placed it on the coffee table in the living room, and got dressed to go out. It wasn't cold outside anymore. After the Spring Festival, the temperature rose. Yi Shui took a deep breath, sensing the arrival of spring.

The company where the interview was located was in Si Hui. Yi Shui planned to take the bus and transfer to the subway. Before the bus arrived, Zhao Feng called asking where Yi Shui was. Yi Shui said she went out for something and would be back in the afternoon. Yi Shui hadn't told Zhao Feng that she was looking for a job. Zhao Feng had once said that he didn't want Yi Shui to work so hard.
