Special Interview on Leysa Auto Listing

by ftleisa on 2011-12-12 10:43:12

The breakthrough of any product is guided by the company's strategic plan and supported by strong and substantial power. For this reason, after the launch of the new product, we interviewed Mr. Wu Yuejun, Vice President of Foton Motor Group, and Mr. Fan Xianjun, President of Resa Pumping Business Division, to conduct in-depth research and discussions on the success of Foton Resa, revealing a distinctive Foton and a business-rich Resa to ordinary people. The following is the transcript of this exclusive interview:

Reporter's question: First of all, congratulations on the successful launch of Resa's National IV 52-meter pump truck. In people's eyes, this is an event in the development of Foton Resa Pumping Business Division, as well as an event for the entire Foton Motor Group to promote the 2020 strategy and move towards a "world brand." Could you briefly introduce the 2020 strategy? What role does the Resa pumping business play on the road to becoming a world brand?

Response to the reporter's question: Since its establishment in 1996, Foton Motor has experienced more than ten years of arduous struggle in the initial establishment phase of the company and the full series development phase of commercial vehicles. In 2004, it successfully achieved first place in China for commercial vehicle production and sales for the first time and has maintained it until now, firmly establishing itself as the number one brand of commercial vehicles in China. According to the direction and goal of "cultivation improvement, structural adjustment, globalization," Foton Motor Group will focus all aspects of its business on the automotive-related industry, achieving vertical upgrades across the full series of commercial vehicles. While maintaining the number one position in China, it will steadily expand overseas markets, laying the foundation for globalization.

The "2020 Strategy" implemented since 2010 refers to Foton Motor's strategic plan for the future, with its core being the "5+3+1" strategy. The "5" means building five overseas plants in India, Russia, Brazil, Mexico, and Thailand; the "3" means breaking into the three highest-end automotive markets in the world—Japan-Korea, North America, and Western Europe—by producing high-end complete vehicles and engines; the "1" means using new energy as the key to transformation, achieving leapfrog development in passenger car business.

Under the "2020 Strategy," Foton Motor Group will invest a total of 28.8 billion yuan domestically, starting with hardware construction, to achieve the transformation of commercial vehicles from the largest to the strongest. It will focus on developing five pillar industries: automobiles, new energy vehicles, automotive Internet of Things, financial services, new energy, and high-end equipment manufacturing, transforming into a comprehensive general automobile company.

Foton Resa Business Division has developed based on the rapid development of Foton Motor and the swift growth of Foton Company. It mainly handles pump trucks, mixer trucks, truck-mounted pumps, and complete sets of cement facilities for mixing stations.

As the core of the equipment manufacturing business, Resa Pumping Business Division is one of the key businesses in Foton's international-oriented "2020 Strategy." The company plans to give full attention and support in the future development plan, sharing the high growth of the industry, creating profits to support the development of whole vehicles, and supporting technical upgrades and profit creation. So far, under the guidance of the management direction of "strengthening channels as the foundation, service building the future," both sales volume and market share of Resa Pumping Business Division have significantly increased.

Reporter's question: Under the overall "2020 Strategy" of Foton Motor Group, what are the specific plans of Resa Pumping Business Division? What new developments have been made so far?

Response to the reporter's question: Foton Resa has already established its strategic development goals for 2020, which is to become a global supplier of complete sets of cement machinery. Following the plan, by 2015, it aims to successfully achieve sales of 24,500 units, sales revenue of 15 billion yuan, and profit of 1 billion yuan, ranking third in the industry in terms of comprehensive strength, becoming a well-known domestic company. By 2020, it aims to successfully achieve sales of 45,000 units, sales revenue of 30 billion yuan, and profit of 2.5 billion yuan, ranking second in the industry in terms of comprehensive strength, becoming a globally renowned brand.

So far, under the guidance of the management direction of "strengthening channels as the foundation, service building the future," both sales volume and market share of Resa Pumping Business Division have significantly increased. Through the joint efforts of all employees, Foton Resa completed sales of 6,637 units in 2010, an increase of 56% year-on-year. All products have shown a good trend of rapid and continuous development.

Today's launch of Foton Resa's National IV 52-meter pump truck marks another significant step towards high technology, high environmental protection, and high service. This adds new momentum to Foton Resa's competition in the high-end pump truck market.
