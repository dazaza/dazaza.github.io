Introduction: Korean drama "Mr. Goodbye"

[Title] Mr. Goodbye (Farewell Mr.)

[Production] KBS TV Station, South Korea (following "Spring Waltz")

[Release] First aired on May 22, 2006 (aired in South Korea every Monday and Tuesday)

[Director] Huang Yijing (Marriage), Xian Yongxun (Episodes 18-29)

[Screenwriter] Xu Shuxiang (Return - Next)

[Producer] Lee Chankwe

[Cast] Ahn Jae-wook, Lee Bo-young, Oh Yoon-a, Heo Jung-min, Cho Dong-hyuk

[Genre] Romantic/Love

It tells the touching story of a hotel heir and a common woman. In the drama, Ahn Jae-wook plays the male lead who was adopted by a kind American couple as a child and became their foster son. After growing up, he returned to South Korea and took charge of his family's hotel business. There, he met Lee Bo-young, who worked as a guide at the hotel, and sparks flew between them...

In "Mr. Goodbye," Oh Yoon-a makes her debut portraying a single mother as Ahn Jea-wook's ex-wife. They met and married when she was 20 years old while studying in the U.S. They divorced three years later, and she returned to South Korea to become a cardiac surgeon. However, after their divorce, she realized she was pregnant but didn't tell Jea-wook. Later, when Jea-wook returned to South Korea and fell in love with the female lead, they accidentally met Oh Yoon-a again. She discovered that she still deeply loved Jea-wook and wanted to reunite, and it was only then that Jea-wook learned he had a child...
