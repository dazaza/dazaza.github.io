The Two Essentials of Natural Makeup The Two Essentials of Natural Makeup 　　If you wear myopic glasses for a long time and suddenly take them off, people will feel something is off. Similarly, if someone who usually wears makeup goes barefaced, people may feel that the tone of their entire face has become lighter, as if it were a piece of cloth washed out of color, lacking in vitality. The key to this issue lies in the base makeup and eye makeup. 　　No matter what kind of base makeup you apply, the triangular area under the eyes must be brightened. This area becoming brighter has an effect of one against a hundred. Choose a concealer with pearl luster; if your under-eye skin is particularly dry, then first dip some light-colored foundation liquid before spreading the concealer. Lifting the tail of the eye + false eyelashes Lifting the tail of the eye + false eyelashes 　　An appropriate elongation of the tail of the eye can make both eyes appear not droopy. However, do not deliberately draw lines, but instead use an eyeshadow pen to dot upwards. At this point, attach a small bunch of false eyelashes at the tail of the eye. Even when viewed up close, it's hard to tell that you've applied any makeup.
