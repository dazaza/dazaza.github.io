Class A Compressed Air Foam Fire Truck on duty at Taizhou Fire Brigade 18671079399

by xfxybk on 2012-02-18 16:28:43

On the morning of February 17, three newly purchased Class A Compressed Air Foam System (CAFS) fire trucks, funded by a special government fiscal allocation of 4.5 million yuan, were delivered to the detachments in Tiantai, Sanmen, and Xianju, three remote mountainous counties. This marked the end of the history where Tiantai and Sanmen detachments did not have imported chassis Class A CAFS fire trucks.

According to reports, the newly acquired Class A CAFS fire trucks have a water tank capacity of 4.8 tons, foam capacity of 200 liters, equipped with American Darley fire pumps and PL48 imported nozzles, with a range exceeding 55 meters, and a top speed reaching 100 km/h. The overall performance of this vehicle is very superior, and its addition will greatly enhance the firefighting and rescue combat effectiveness of grassroots fire detachments.

The purchase of new vehicles has alleviated the contradiction between the increasingly heavy task of fire safety protection under the new situation and the insufficient existing equipment, enhanced the overall combat strength and firefighting rescue capability of the fire department, ensuring that the team can "be mobilized at any time, rush forward, save lives, and extinguish fires quickly", safeguarding people's lives and property safety as well as local economic construction.

It is understood that the firefighters are currently familiarizing themselves with the performance of the vehicles and equipment to ensure that the new vehicles are put into service and standby as soon as possible, maximizing their effectiveness in emergency rescue operations.
