purify the network environment and trigger a new concept of space selection

by now on 2009-12-16 15:51:36

Recently, a new round of rectification actions has been carried out on the Internet in our country. In this rectification action, some data centers suspected of irregular operations have been sealed off, causing many websites to be inaccessible. The inability to open a website not only results in the loss of a large number of users but also brings immeasurable losses to enterprises or individuals. This event has sounded the alarm for IDC service providers and a wide range of website users.

When facing such matters, what should service providers do to ensure the safe and stable operation of their users' websites? According to the author's understanding from Now Telecom, one of the top five domestic virtual host providers, in terms of purifying the network environment, service providers should strengthen the investigation of illegal information and severely punish websites containing illegal information. Apart from strictly controlling illegal information, service providers should continue to carry out special filing activities, promptly submit information about unfilled websites, fully assist users in filing their websites, and strengthen supervision and management of websites. Only in this way can they create a harmonious and healthy network environment for their users.

It is understood that during the special campaign organized by relevant national departments this year to rectify the vulgar风气of the Internet, Now Telecom (www.now.cn) actively carried out an ICP filing activity themed "A near neighbor is better than a distant cousin", strictly investigating websites containing illegal information and those that have not submitted filings. They have consistently done a good job in related work, safeguarding the safe operation of user websites.

What should users pay attention to when choosing space? It is not difficult to see from this incident that service providers holding ISP and IDC licenses are more capable of providing security guarantees to users because ISP and IDC licenses are not something ordinary companies can apply for. Only those service providers with substantial strength and credibility can obtain them after strict reviews. Therefore, when choosing space, users should focus on the qualifications and honors of service providers, so the risks associated with the operation of the website will not be as great.

The IDC industry is different from traditional industries. In this era of the Internet, they directly affect the business of enterprises. Therefore, as service providers offering website infrastructure to a wide range of users, they should strengthen service standard management in daily operations, do a good job in all aspects of related work, eliminate the breeding of illegal websites, and make every effort to create a healthy network environment for users; while users, when choosing space, should also examine the related work of service providers from multiple aspects, whether they can manage strictly directly affects the environment in which their own websites operate.

Times are constantly evolving, and the ever-changing Internet is rapidly moving towards the right track. If one wants to develop long-term, when selecting space, one should not just consider the price aspect but should consider from multiple angles how to allow the website to develop healthily, thus avoiding risks and creating a good environment for the development of the website.
