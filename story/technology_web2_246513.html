"雪山惊魂" can be translated as "Horror on the Snow Mountain" or "Snow Mountain Nightmare". Both convey a sense of terror or suspense related to a snow mountain.

In the depths of winter, five young people went skiing on the Yserheim mountain in mid-southern Norway. On its slopes, one of the members, Morten, completed a terrible landing and broke his leg. They looked around but there was no one else in sight, and their mobile phones were out of service. Fortunately, they saw a mountain inn in the distance and decided to go there for help. The inn was deathly silent, clearly closed for many years, and the telephone lines were long dead. They had no choice but to spend the night at this inn.

The next morning, one of the members, Eric, went outside to look for a taxi. But before he had walked far, he saw the body of his female companion. Before he could figure out what was going on, he was struck down by someone with a pickaxe. Meanwhile, Jenny and Mikael unexpectedly discovered that the inn where they were staying was the notorious "Hell's Inn" that had closed earlier in the year. When they prepared to leave, a masked killer was already lying in wait. He killed Mikael and Morten one after another, then knocked Jenny unconscious.

When Jenny woke up, she found herself being dragged by the masked killer. It turned out that the masked killer intended to throw everyone into a crevasse in the glacier. At the right moment, Jenny managed to push the masked killer into the glacial crevasse. In the end, only Jenny survived this heart-stopping event.
