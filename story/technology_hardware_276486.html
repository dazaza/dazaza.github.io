Glasses made by Italian SAFILO choose Optyl (epoxy resin) as this kind of patent material is qualitative. It is 20~30% lighter than general plastic frames. This material polishes better than traditional thermoplastics, can produce peculiar unreal colors and luster, and is more durable and less prone to deformation. Optyl lenses are easily identifiable; their temple does not require the addition of metal staffs like general plastic frames do to increase strength and prevent deformation. Also, characters can be stamped on the end of the lens leg for Optyl models. Working with this simple material requires significant investment in equipment and polishing craftsmanship, making it difficult to copy and risky.
