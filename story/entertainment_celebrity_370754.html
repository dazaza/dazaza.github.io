According to the潇Xiang Morning Post report on November 4th, everyday drinking ten cups of coffee, using coffee as a hypnotic, not drinking icy water for more than 20 years. These are not the secret prescriptions from any health-care expert, but they are the living "eccentricities" of King Liu Dehua who has been active in the entertainment circle for 30 years. Recently, when Liu Dehua was invited to the interview site of "Very Close Distance", he spoke freely about these aspects of his life without any taboo. However, the only topic that he did not touch at all was his family life.

At the interview site, the host Li Jing had prepared Liu Dehua's favorite black coffee, and within a few minutes, this guy drank two cups. During the chat with Li Jing, Liu Dehua revealed with high interest that he used to drink more than ten cups of black coffee every day. Drinking coffee before sleep could induce hypnosis instead, so he slept very well, "and it is all right for me to drink like this." There is a reason why he only likes black coffee, "I am sensitive to all dairy products, that is to say, I will feel very bad after drinking them."

Still charming after 30 years since his debut, Liu Dehua at the age of 49 indicated that age was never an important thing to him, and he is undoubtedly the most proficient mature male star in terms of care and anti-aging. At the program site, Liu Dehua openly shared his private care methods and anti-aging secrets with Li Jing, and even held back nothing when talking about his personal living habits. Liu Dehua said that there were almost no mirrors at his home because he was afraid of mirrors. The host Li Jing thought it was incredible: "Is it because of the fear of bad feng shui?" King Liu replied: "There are not too many lights at home anyway, and when you walk around, the mirrors flash here and there. What is that? Especially in the middle of the night when you are half asleep, you will be scared by looking into the mirror."

In order to keep fit, he only eats oatmeal and porridge for breakfast and adds some vegetable salad. In fact, Liu Dehua's daily life is extremely simple, which is contrary to what ordinary people imagine - luxurious. When it comes to health care, even in daily water drinking, King Liu has a lot of concerns: "I have not drunk icy water for more than 20 years, nor have I drunk too hot water, I only drink warm water." As for the common sense of drinking more water every day, Liu Dehua also gave insights, "However, if you do not sweat a lot or do not go to the bathroom often, you really should not drink too much water, otherwise your kidney will be burdened."
