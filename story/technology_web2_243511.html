7 practical online small alarm clocks

by wodezzz on 2009-09-28 09:34:52

If you are boiling water, a good movie or TV series makes you forget the time, and the steam permeating the room makes you realize that, oh, the water is drying up. And today's online alarm clock website can make up for this gap, so that you don't have to worry about the Internet and delay other work. Need a friend may wish to choose a collection, in case of emergency.

1. E.ggTimer

Web site: http://e.ggtimer.com/. She is a simple and practical online countdown service, as long as you enter the countdown period on the home page, you can enter the corresponding countdown process, once the time is up, an alarm will sound. Page concise, no ads, the longest countdown for 28 years, I believe enough for you to use it, ha ha.

2. Kukuklok

Web site: http://kukuklok.com/. She is a simple online Alarm service, you can Set the alarm time, and then select the alarm type, can be rooster crow, ordinary alarm, electronic alarm, etc., set it and click Set Alarm.

3. Online Clock

Web site: http://onlineclock.net/. Her characteristic is simple and clear, the upper left corner can set the font size, the upper right corner set the background color, you need to do is to choose the time you need. A very real online alarm clock, and I personally like her alarm bell.

4. Alarmcocky

Website: http://www.alarmcocky.com/. She is basically the same as Kukuklok, setting her own Alarm time, selecting the alarm, and then setting alarm. There is a button in the center of the page that can listen to the alarm of your choice.

5. Nnaked Alarm Clock

Website: http://www.nakedalarmclock.com/. It's a little bit more complicated, a lot more technical. The top row of time is the current time, the upper and lower triangle symbols to adjust the time, several black and white fan areas are 15 minutes, 30 minutes, 45 minutes; And this alarm clock is different from the previous several models, it can set seconds, AM for morning, PM for afternoon; 1, 2, 3, and 4 Select the Alarm tone and Set the alarm.

6. Music Alarm Clock

Web site: http://www.music-alarm-clock.com/. This is different from the above, she can choose multiple alarm bells, and there is also a radio to provide the choice (I don't think there is any Chinese language), when the time comes, the radio can play directly. So this alarm clock can also be used as a fixed radio. In three steps, set the time, select the ringtone, and then Alarm ON.

7. Alarm

Website: http://www.avnoy.com/alarm/flash.swf. This is done directly in Flash, first Click on the right area Click here to set alarm, then you can find the familiar steps, set the time, when finished NEXT, select the sound, you are done.
