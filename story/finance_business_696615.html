Resonance of the Great Mountain - A Grand Event for the Inspiration Tour of Mount Hua by China's Renowned Painters and Photographers

The opening ceremony of the "Resonance of the Great Mountain" inspiration tour event on Mount Hua for China's famous painters and photographers was held grandly at the Lotus Villa in Mount Hua on April 8th, organized by the Mount Hua Scenic Area Management Committee. Attending the opening ceremony were Huo Wenjun, Deputy Secretary-General of the Weinan Municipal People's Government, Director of the Mount Hua Scenic Area Management Committee, and Chairman of the Mount Hua Tourism Group Co., Ltd.; Ma Jianping, Deputy Secretary of the Party Working Committee of the Huashan Scenic Area Management Committee who presided over the opening ceremony; Lu Genwen, Shu Feng, and Lu Jianming, Deputy Directors of the Huashan Scenic Area Management Committee; Zhang Xiaojian, General Manager of the Group Company; renowned painters Li Baolin, Xiao Yingchuan, Zhou Shunlai, and many others.

In his speech, Huo Wenjun expressed that the purpose of hosting the "Resonance of the Great Mountain" event for China's renowned painters and photographers is to showcase the captivating natural scenery and profound historical culture of Mount Hua through the artists' brilliant brushes and lens works, realizing a splendid transformation of Mount Hua's culture and elevating it comprehensively. He believed that through this event, the cultural connotation of Mount Hua will be further deepened, its external image enhanced, and the future of Mount Hua will be even more beautiful!

Representing all participating art masters, Li Baolin praised the magnificent natural landscape and the brilliant, long-standing human history of the Mount Hua scenic area. He stated that he would use his brush to discover the beauty of Mount Hua, continuously expanding its influence and allowing more people to understand and pay attention to Mount Hua.

Subsequently, the painters and photographers will ascend the main peak of Mount Hua, visit the Fairy Valley, pay homage at the Xiyue Temple, and experience the old腔 (a traditional form of local opera) of Mount Hua, engaging in painting and photography creation activities, presenting the beauty of Mount Hua to the world.

Culture is the soul of tourism, and tourism is the channel for the spread of culture. Closely integrating culture with tourism and further enhancing the cultural connotation of the scenic area has always been the direction of effort for the scenic area management committee. In recent years, the Mount Hua Scenic Area Management Committee has innovatively developed concepts, planned high-standard development blueprints for the scenic area, actively attracted investment, increased project construction efforts, gradually improved tourism infrastructure, sustained growth in tourist numbers, achieved remarkable results in all aspects of the scenic area's work, successfully created a national 5A-level tourist attraction in 2010, fully upgraded the scenic area’s image, and realized the healthy and rapid development of the Mount Hua tourism industry. Meanwhile, relying on its own rich cultural heritage, the Mount Hua Scenic Area has strengthened cultural construction, deeply explored the Taoist culture and folk customs of Mount Hua, and hosted multiple large-scale cultural activities to drive tourism industry development. This event is another large-scale series of cultural activities after last year's "Li Xiaochao Art Exhibition", "Mount Hua Sword Discussion Series Forum", "Experiencing Peril – Chinese Masters Enter Mount Hua", and "Mirror Beautiful China – Travel with Masters to Experience the Beauty of Mount Hua".

More than 100 employees from relevant units within the scenic area participated in the opening ceremony. The event was fully covered by well-known media nationwide including the China Tourism News, Hong Kong Evening Post, Shaanxi Daily, Shenzhen Evening News, Huashang Daily, Sanqin Metropolis Daily, Shaanxi Television Station, People's Network, etc.
