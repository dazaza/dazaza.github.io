Gas supply pipeline suddenly damaged, gas company urgently repairs

by cgccg001 on 2009-12-20 02:42:02

Voiceover: Yesterday evening, a gas pipeline in the urban area was broken by a construction team that was working nearby. After receiving the report, the XinAo Gas Company carried out an emergency repair operation overnight.

When reporters arrived at the scene of the broken pipeline, the gas company had already cordoned off the accident site. Emergency repair personnel and equipment dispatched by the company were arriving on-site one after another. Following the incident, the gas company immediately established an emergency response command center. Zhang Meng, Director of the Pipeline Network Operation Department, gave us a brief introduction to the incident.

同期声 (Synchronous sound): Zhang Meng, Director of the Pipeline Network Operation Department, XinAo Gas Company - "At 5:30 PM today, we received a maintenance call from the construction team. During sewage construction work, they broke one of our pipelines located beside a bridge at the northern section of Yingbin Road near Guofei Shangcheng. Our company is now organizing resources for full-scale repairs."

The incident occurred during the peak usage time for residents and commercial establishments. To ensure normal gas supply to five nearby residential areas and three commercial users, the gas company did not rashly cut off the gas supply. Instead, after making an emergency treatment of the pipeline break, they continued to supply gas at reduced pressure. Safety personnel were on-site continuously monitoring the concentration of leaking gas to prevent any mishaps. Repair personnel and equipment were also on standby ready to act at any moment. Users expressed deep gratitude for the gas company's handling of the situation.

同期声 (Synchronous sound): Hotel Manager - "Tonight, I would like to specially thank the XinAo Gas Company for their service that truly comes to our doorstep, considering our business needs and thinking about what we need. We sincerely thank them."

After the gas usage peak for nearby residents and businesses had passed, the repair crew of the gas company immediately began repairing the accident site overnight.

On-camera reporter (Zheng Yongshan) - "Friends, it is now 8:30 PM. Behind me, you can see that XinAo Gas has already cordoned off the scene. Their next step is to cut off the damaged pipeline and replace it with a new section of pipe, aiming to restore gas supply by tomorrow."

Editorial Note: Reporters learned early this morning from the gas company that around 6:00 AM today, the damaged gas pipeline had been fully repaired, and gas supply within the area has returned to normal.
