Telecom's Ace for the New iPhone Plan: Family Sharing with Primary and Secondary Cards

by anonymous on 2013-09-18 21:44:07

As the iPhone 5s/5c is about to be released on September 20th, industry insiders have revealed that China Telecom has prepared multiple selling points for the telecom version of the iPhone 5s/5c in order to differentiate it from other models. One significant selling point is that the new iPhone contract plan will support main and secondary card services, allowing family members to share package resources.

Family members can share one iPhone 5s/5c package.

Currently, some details of China Telecom's iPhone contract plans have been exposed, but it is reported that the full details of the contract plans will only be disclosed a few days later.

However, sources familiar with the situation revealed that, in response to users' actual usage needs, China Telecom will launch main and secondary card services for the iPhone 5s/5c: any user who purchases a telecom version of the iPhone 5s/5c under a contract plan, as long as they subscribe to a package above a certain price, can apply for a free secondary card for their relatives and friends, sharing all the duration and content within the main card package.

It is understood that the shared package between main and secondary cards is an exclusive service of China Telecom, considering the common usage needs of users and their relatives. Users of China Telecom's iPhone 5s/5c contract plans can apply for a free secondary card for their relatives, meaning that family members can share the same valuable package, including shared call fees, internet duration, and various discounts.

The advantage of this main and secondary card service is that it is practical for consumers who don't spend much on phone bills each month but still hope to purchase the telecom version of the iPhone 5s/5c at a more reasonable price, allowing them to save household expenses with a single person's package.

Launching a killer package

Previously, China Telecom had announced that it would fully release the iPhone 5S/5C on September 20th, at which time all the various discounts of the new version of China Telecom's iPhone would be fully revealed.

Currently, among operators in China offering main and secondary card services, only China Telecom provides this service. From past consumer feedback, the main and secondary card services have always received positive reviews. This is mainly because the main and secondary card services offer great benefits to users of the telecom version of the iPhone.

It was revealed that one of the main features of the soon-to-be-launched telecom version of the iPhone 5s/5c also lies in its "value for money." Apart from the main and secondary card services, other fee package designs are also consumer-oriented. For instance, to maximally demonstrate the excellent online performance of the telecom version of the iPhone 5s/5c, China Telecom has specifically designed longer durations for both internet use and calls within the packages for the iPhone 5s/5c.

At the same time, the telecom version of the iPhone 5s/5c offers substantial rewards to pre-order users. Users who pre-order the telecom version of the iPhone 5S/5C can enjoy a deposit of 200 yuan and receive a return of 500 yuan along with 4G data traffic.

The iPhone event will be held on September 10th.
