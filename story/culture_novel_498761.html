Chapter One Hundred Sixty-One: Song Knife

by fanyete on 2011-10-01 09:00:48

The next day, when dawn arrived, the area outside the Tianyan Mountain Range also became bustling. Many teams packed up their tents and one after another entered the mountain range. Quite a number of them were in a hurry, as if they believed that if they were late, all the treasures in the ancient tomb would be taken by others. This scene looked somewhat ridiculous in Lin Dong's eyes.

"Let's go."

Old Tao waved his hand, instructing the guards to pack up the tents. Then without saying much, he headed straight into the mountains. Lin Feng and others quickly followed.

As they entered the forest of the mountain range, the sky seemed to have darkened a bit. A faint sense of oppression enveloped everyone's hearts. Listening to the distant roars of beasts, the smiles on everyone's faces slightly faded. In such a place, carelessness could easily cost one their life.

Apparently, there were many teams entering the mountain range. Just in the direction of Lin Dong and his group, they saw more than ten teams. These teams had varying numbers of people, and were somewhat wary of each other. After entering the mountains, they separated.

Lin Dong and his group did not bother with these things. Old Tao and his companions were clearly well-prepared, possessing extremely detailed maps of the Tianyan Mountain Range. Thus, they saved themselves from unnecessary exploration. The group moved directly in a straight line towards the deeper parts of the mountain range.

As the team quickly advanced, the noise from behind gradually disappeared. At the same time, Old Tao and his companions' expressions became serious and alert. Inside the Tianyan Mountain Range, beast demons ran rampant, and there were no shortage of those with extremely strong capabilities. During this period, countless lives had been lost in these mountains.

Lin Dong sat calmly on the back of Little Flame Tiger, his expression peaceful and composed. However, his spiritual energy was slowly seeping out, observing every move around him.

"There is a team ahead. There are quite a number of them, and there is a smell of blood. It's not from beast demons." Suddenly, an experienced middle-aged guard stopped and whispered quietly while the team was cautiously moving forward.

"Go straight."

Old Tao nodded slightly, not at all surprised. Everyone here was for the ancient tomb. Apart from their own team, everyone else was a competitor. It was not uncommon for conflicts to break out in the Tianyan Mountain Range.

Seeing that Old Tao had no intention of detouring, the guards nodded. However, they slowly gripped their swords tighter.
