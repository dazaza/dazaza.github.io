A woman was awarded 5000 yuan in compensation after a server entered her hotel room when she was naked.

by whseoceo on 2011-08-24 18:30:02

Core tips:

Miss Zhang and her boyfriend rented an hourly room in a business hotel last night. But Miss Zhang never expected that someone would enter their room after opening the door. The key problem is that the people in the room at that time were naked.

West China Network (Shaanxi TV Station "Metropolis Express" reporter Yanfeng She, Xinglong Zhao) - Miss Zhang and her boyfriend rented an hourly room in a business hotel last night. But Miss Zhang never expected that someone would enter their room after opening the door. The key problem is that the people in the room at that time were naked.

The reporter came to Room 509 of Qintang No.1 Hotel located on Dongmutoushi Road in Xian. At eight o'clock yesterday evening, Miss Zhang and her boyfriend checked into this room. However, at around ten o'clock last night, while Miss Zhang and her boyfriend were resting in bed, there was suddenly a sound of opening door in the quiet room.

Miss Zhang: Around 10:20, we took a shower, then got out and lay down on the bed. We said that we would wear our clothes and leave soon. At this moment, the door suddenly made a sound and opened. From my lying angle, I just saw someone come in. I was so flustered and scared. Then I heard them say "there's someone", and they went out.

Through the surveillance video of the hotel, we saw the situation outside the corridor door at the time of the incident. It can be seen that the attendant opened the door of Room 509 with two customers. A female customer poked her head in but quickly retreated. The whole process only lasted for a few seconds.

Miss Zhang: I quickly grabbed a bath towel and ran out to take a look. There was a room attendant and two customers, one male and one female. I asked them what happened and who could explain it. They said they opened the wrong door, and then left.

Miss Zhang said that when the door was opened, she and her boyfriend were both naked. Therefore, she found it hard to accept that someone suddenly intruded.

Miss Zhang: I feel very uncomfortable because I have been seen by others. It's really indescribable.

The hotel manager explained that the wrong door was opened due to the attendant's mistake.

Hotel Manager: Because we are still in trial operation, the handover between the two attendants was not done properly.

However, did the customer who entered the wrong room see the situation inside the room after opening the door? On this point, Miss Zhang and the hotel manager had different opinions.

Hotel Manager: At that time, the female guest poked her head in and shouted "who's inside", and then she retreated.

Reporter: Your meaning is that they didn't see anything?

Hotel Manager: They couldn't see anything.

Miss Zhang: We just finished taking a shower and lay down on the bed, thinking about wearing our clothes after drying off. Reporter: Do you think they saw everything? Yes, there was a mirror at the door, which could completely show the situation on the bed.

In order to find out whether they could see the bed or not, let's do an experiment.

Miss Zhang: I paid for this room, so this space should be my private space. My spirit has been greatly hurt.

We learned that this hotel is still in trial operation, and unexpectedly, problems really occurred during the trial. However, Miss Zhang, the party involved, said that she did not want to be the guinea pig of the hotel, so she insisted on claiming compensation. Since both parties could not reach an agreement, the situation has remained deadlocked from last night until this afternoon. Apparently, after opening the wrong door, both parties still need to find a key to solve the problem.

In the afternoon, the store finally agreed to compensate Miss Zhang with 5000 yuan for mental damages.
