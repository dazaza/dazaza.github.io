How to Increase Online Store Traffic and Promote Your Store Better - 29 Methods for Store Promotion

Among the 29 methods you can consider adopting, you might have already used some of them. Some methods you may have forgotten to use, and there are still others that you've never heard of. In short, here you can find out about all the current effective website promotion methods. First, you need to understand that website promotion is a long-term and systematic process that requires setting clear goals and plans, and making the necessary preparations. The most important strategy in search engine marketing is to rank high with your website's main keywords on major search engines. Search engine bots automatically scan web page content, so search engine strategies start with optimizing web pages.

One, add webpage titles.

Write a descriptive title of 5 to 8 characters for each page's content. The title should be concise without unnecessary words like "the" or "and." It should explain what the most important content of this page and this website is. Webpage titles will appear as links on the search results page, so they can be slightly sensational to attract searchers to click on the link. At the same time, write your company name and your most important keywords in the homepage content, rather than just writing the company name. Every page of the website should have a title specific to that page.

Two, add descriptive META tags.

In addition to webpage titles, many search engines will also search for META tags. This is an explanatory sentence. It describes the content of the webpage body, and it should also include the keywords and phrases used on this page. Currently, META tags containing keywords have little effect on rankings, but sometimes META tags are used in paid submission techniques. And who knows when search engines might start valuing them again?

Three, fill in your keywords in bold text, generally article titles.

Search engines pay great attention to bold text, assuming it is very important content on the page. Therefore, ensure that your keywords are written in one or two bold text tags. Try to avoid turning important titles into images, and definitely do not make the entire homepage a single image.

Four, ensure that your keywords appear in the first paragraph of your main text.

Search engines hope to find your keywords in the first paragraph of text, but don't overfill with too many keywords. Google considers a keyword density of 1.5 to 2 keywords per 100 words as the optimal density for good rankings. Other places to consider placing keywords include ALT or COMMENT tags in the code. If you cannot ensure that your keywords appear in the first paragraph, you can consider some remedial measures, such as adding annotations to the webpage.

Five, navigation design should be easy for search engines to search.

Some people use frames in webpage creation, but this poses a serious problem for search engines. Even if search engines find your content pages, they may miss key navigation sections and thus fail to enter other pages. Navigation buttons made with JavaScript and Flash look beautiful and aesthetic, but search engines cannot find them. A remedy is to create another navigation bar at the bottom of the page using regular HTML links, ensuring that you can enter every page of the site through this navigation bar. You can also create a sitemap that links to every page. Moreover, some content management systems and e-commerce directories use dynamic webpages, which usually have a question mark followed by numbers in their URLs. Overworked search engines often stop at the question mark and do not continue searching. For this situation, URL changes and paid submissions can be used to solve the problem. Dynamic sites can generate static pages to facilitate search engine crawling. Sitemaps should try to link to all main pages and submit the sitemap page separately to search engines. Use more HTML static webpages, which increases workload but enhances search engine friendliness, thereby laying a foundation for better rankings.

Six, create several specialized pages for certain particularly important keywords.

Search engine optimization experts do not recommend using any deceptive transition pages targeted at search engines, as these almost duplicated pages could incur penalties from search engines. However, you can create a few pages, each focusing on different keywords or phrases. For example, instead of introducing all your services on one page, create a separate page for each service. This way, each page has corresponding keywords, and the content on these pages, due to its targeted keywords rather than general content, can improve rankings.

Seven, submit webpages to search engines.

Find the [ADD YOUR URL] website login link on the search engine. The search engine will automatically crawl the webpage you submit. The most famous search engines in the U.S. are: Google, Inktomi, AltaVista, Teoma. These search engines provide search content to other major search engines and portals. In Europe and other regions, you can post to regional search engines. As for spending money to have someone help you submit to thousands of search engines, it's a waste of money. Don't use FFA (Free For All Pages) websites, which supposedly submit your site to hundreds of search engines automatically. Such submissions not only have poor effects but also bring you a lot of spam and may result in penalties from search engines for your webpage. Don't use website submission software, which claims to submit your site to thousands of search engines at once; not only is it impossible, but it also lacks practical value. The most important thing is to optimize your website well. For major search engines, adopt individual manual submissions. For paid search engines, it's even less possible to rely on software submissions. In fact, an effective search engine marketing strategy doesn't require submitting your site to thousands of search engines because the top few search engines concentrate almost 98% of the traffic, leaving the remaining search engines with little practical significance. Additionally, note that submitting webpages shouldn't be a one-time event; with content updates, you should regularly resubmit webpages. Due to the increasing number of web pages, many search bots take a long time to revisit, so regular submissions allow search engines to crawl updated content.

Eight, adjust main content pages to improve rankings.

Adjust the pages you consider most important, perhaps the homepage, to improve their rankings. Some software allows you to check current rankings, compare the rankings of competitors' webpages with similar keywords, and obtain preferred statistical data from search engines, enabling you to adjust your own pages accordingly. You can use WebPositionGold to do this work yourself, though it takes a lot of time, or you can hire professional companies to help you. Links on other websites can bring more traffic. Since Google and other major search engines use the breadth of website links as an important factor in ranking, the more websites linking to you, the higher your website's ranking will be. Meanwhile, the quality of links is also an important factor considered by search engines. Links on high-traffic websites are more advantageous than those on low-traffic websites.

Nine, submit your website to major directories.

Ensure that your website logs into free directories like Open Directory (), which are manually reviewed for logins. This hierarchical directory provides directory content to all major search engines for searches. Google values whether your website has links on such important websites. Yahoo is another important directory where you need to log in. Tip: The number of words describing your website should be less than the maximum allowed, not exactly reaching the word limit, to avoid overly long descriptions causing website reviewers to delete some segments. Currently, commercial websites logging into Yahoo cost $299 annually, and it's best to get your website logged within 7 working days. Other directories you can consider logging into include About.com and Business.com. There are many website navigation sites domestically. Examples include:

Such sites can also bring significant traffic. Even if your online marketing budget is limited, it's still essential to register on major search engines like Sina and Sohu.

Ten, log your website onto industry sites and professional directories.

Some directories focus on specific industries, such as education or finance. If you belong to a trade association that aggregates numerous member sites, you can apply to join your site to the association website, even paying if necessary, because it will bring you many target visitors. Besides being able to log onto many yellow page-type websites, searching on search engines according to your industry will certainly find many related industry directory sites, which you should try to log onto.

Eleven, request reciprocal links.

Find complementary sites whose content complements yours and ask them for reciprocal links. The ideal linking targets are those with website traffic comparable to yours. Administrators of high-traffic websites have to deal with too many requests for reciprocal links, making it easy to overlook you. Smaller websites can also be considered. Place reciprocal link pages in relatively obscure parts of the website to avoid quickly directing your website visitors to others' sites. After finding websites for reciprocal links, send a personalized email to the administrator of the opposing website. If they don't reply, give them a call. Always focus on the quality of the opposing website, don't blindly pursue the number of links, and definitely don't send spam emails.

Twelve, publish free articles with a site signature.

Freely write professional articles for other websites' newsletters, attaching a brief description in the article about what you offer and requesting the other party to link to your website. This is an effective viral marketing method, as your article will be sent as information subscribed by thousands of users, allowing your website to gain hundreds of links at once. If you don't have your own original articles, always add your link when re-posting others' articles.

Traditional offline promotion methods remain effective, especially when combined with multiple approaches.

Thirteen, print your website address on letterheads, business cards, brochures, and printed materials.

This simple method is sometimes overlooked. Ensure the website address is spelled correctly. It's recommended to omit the http:// part and only write www.****.com. An easily memorable domain name aids in website promotion, so carefully consider it when choosing a domain name.

Fourteen, use traditional media advertising.

Traditional media advertising should not be abandoned. Whether newspaper or magazine ads, ensure your website address is displayed in them. Make browsing the website a supplementary part of the ad, reminding users to browse the website for more relevant information. Don't ignore advertising in relatively niche magazines or trade journals, as sometimes these advertisements are more accurate and effective, and cheaper than online ads. Other traditional ways to increase website traffic include direct mail, classified ads, postcards, etc. Television ads are probably more suitable for websites selling mass-market goods. Place the website address in a prominent position in the ad, as it's easier to remember than a phone number.

Fifteen, provide free services.

People love free things. Attracting people to visit your website through free informational content is more effective than simply inviting them to visit to learn about your business. Building free resources takes time and effort, but it's highly effective for increasing traffic. The free content you provide should closely relate to what you sell, so the visitors you attract are likely potential customers. While providing free services, your website should offer various linking methods to guide users obtaining free information to focus on the products you sell. Free resources include: free email, free website registration, free information posting, and offering distinctive free services is even better.

Sixteen, release news.

Look for newsworthy events, such as promoting the free services you offer, and publish the news in the print journals and website journals of your industry. You can use some online news release services. News promotion is always effective, which is why traditional media often features so many corporate advertisements that resemble news.

Email is an important method to increase traffic, but it's crucial not to send large amounts of unsolicited spam.

Seventeen, create a signature in your outgoing emails to connect with potential customers.

Most email systems have the ability to create signatures. Signatures automatically appear at the end of each email you send. Limit the signature to 6-8 lines. Include: company name, address, telephone, website, email, and a brief description of your business. Avoid designing the signature as an image for aesthetics; it should be easy for customers to copy and paste.

Eighteen, establish an email list and regularly send newsletters or other information to users.

This is one of the best ways to stay connected with customers, build trust, develop brands, and establish long-term relationships. You can invite website visitors to fill in their email addresses to receive your newsletters. Sending emails requires a bulk server, which is available online.

Nineteen, release product information to email list users, such as coupons, new products, and other promotional information.

If you personalize the email subject and body, you'll achieve better results. Five basic principles for designing email subjects are: 1. Reflect the essence of the email content. 2. Reflect information that cannot be included in the sender's details. 3. Reflect brand or product information. 4. The email subject contains rich keywords. 5. The email subject should neither be overly simple nor overly complex.

Twenty, rent target customer email lists.

These email list users agreed to receive certain types of commercial emails when registering on the email list provider's website, so sending your product information to these users is legal and does not constitute spam. Using email address search software to collect email addresses or purchasing email addresses both qualify as spam and not only bring no benefits but may even result in penalties.

Twenty-one, promote in email lists and newsgroups.

Many people with common interests in a particular professional field discuss and exchange ideas online, forming tens of thousands of newsgroups across different fields. Find newsgroups in your field—not to openly advertise your products or services—but to leave your email address and website in the discussion signature. As people gradually get to know and trust you, they will visit your website.

Newsgroups are not commonly used domestically, so they don't count as a common website promotion method. Forums and chat rooms were once used to advertise products, but now many websites do not welcome the publication of advertisement information in these online communities unless there are dedicated advertisement sections; otherwise, it's best to avoid this method.

Twenty-two, utilize competitions.

You can design a competition on your website with prizes that attract the type of clients you most want to acquire. For example, design the prize as your product or a certain purchase discount, etc. Utilizing competitions can effectively attract more traffic. If your visibility is low, how to let customers know about your competition is also a problem, so this method may not be applicable to some websites.

Twenty-three, encourage users to bookmark your website.

Our approach is to add a "Favorite" icon on the homepage. Truly interested customers can conveniently click the "Favorite" button on their browser. Definitely avoid forcibly setting your website as the user's homepage or frequently prompting users to add your site to their favorites while browsing your website.

Twenty-four, exchange advertisements with complementary websites.

Leverage the traffic from the other website to reach a new audience. Many websites have a "Friendly Link" section. Joining an ad exchange network is the most convenient, but some ad exchange networks carry codes that may pop up plugin prompts, potentially annoying visitors and affecting the website's loading speed. Exchanging ads directly between partners is ideal and worth the time spent.

Twenty-five, create viral marketing methods.

Viral marketing spreads information rapidly through various exchanges, such as oral transmission or press releases. Method 12 is viral marketing. The classic form of viral marketing is an email service provider briefly introducing their service at the end of every email sent by users. This information travels with the email, quickly reaching new users, who may then pass it on to other new users. Note that viral marketing is not about creating viruses; it's essentially a free service that will spread automatically among users if it has value.

Paid advertisements will more quickly promote your website. Online advertising prices generally fall into three calculation methods: Traditional CPM (cost per thousand impressions), PPC (pay-per-click), payment based on actual purchases, and affiliate program fees, etc.

Twenty-six, buy small text ads in email newsletters.

Choose target audiences suitable for your mailing list and place short text ads in the newsletters sent to that list. Compared to other online ads, this is a very effective and cost-efficient advertising method.

Twenty-seven, implement affiliate marketing.

Affiliate members place links to the main site on their own websites, and after sales are made through these links, the affiliate receives a commission. Factors to consider when implementing affiliate marketing include: the commission percentage given to affiliates, developing an affiliate program suitable for your needs, promoting the affiliate program to gain more affiliate links to your website. Affiliate management software can be used.

Twenty-eight, bid-based search engine ranking.

When purchasing pay-per-click ads, your ads will appear in the results pages of many search engines under certain keywords. Because the same keyword is often purchased by multiple clients, the specific ranking depends on the bidding price. Bid-based ranking pays based on the actual number of clicks, and it has precise targeting, making it a cost-effective and effective advertising method. Domestic providers like 3721, Sohu, Sina, NetEase, and Baidu all offer bid-based ranking services.

Twenty-nine, submit products to comparison shopping websites and auction websites.

eBay and the malls launched by major platforms can be tried. Additionally, utilizing online store platforms for online marketing is also an effective method.
