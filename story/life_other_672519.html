I'm sorry, it's my fault.

by lili1 on 2012-02-22 22:53:39

I'm sorry, I was wrong.

In a corner of the world,

the girl asked the boy, "Do you really love me?"

The boy replied, "Yes, very much."

The girl roared, "Then why did you abandon me after I gave you my first time? Why did you let me, at only 16 years old, endure all the insults and ridicule from everyone? Why didn't you visit me even once after I took sleeping pills? Why..."

"I have my reasons!" The boy interrupted the girl, grabbing his head in agony as he squatted against the wall: "I do have my reasons! Darling, I'm sorry, I'm so sorry, please forgive me...please..."

Looking at the boy's painful expression, the girl's sharp eyes gradually softened. Every beautiful moment between them flashed before her eyes, causing her heart that hated him to instantly collapse, and then it was beyond control.

Wiping away the tears at the corner of her eyes, the girl slowly squatted down, holding the boy's hand that was covering his head: "I forgive you."

Looking at the girl's innocent and serious eyes, the boy suddenly felt pain in his heart. "Maybe it was really my fault," the boy thought. Suddenly, another voice rang in the boy's mind: "Dare to fight with us? Kid, don't expect to live. Bring a girl to this bamboo grove tonight for our entertainment, and we'll settle this account. Otherwise, watch out for your hands!"

The boy suddenly woke up. He grabbed the girl's hand instead: "Dear, thank you for still forgiving me. I want to go to that little forest where we had our first date. Will you come with me?"

A trace of sadness passed through the girl's eyes but was quickly covered by her innocent smile. She hooked the boy's arm: "Okay, let's go!"

They held hands and arrived at the bamboo grove. Neither of them said anything on the way there. The atmosphere was eerily silent. The boy stopped, seemingly wanting to say something but hesitated. The girl looked at him questioningly. The boy didn't say anything; he just bent down and kissed her. The girl closed her eyes, and tears couldn't help but fall.

The boy noticed the girl's abnormality and was about to ask her what was wrong when a voice suddenly sounded: "Haha, kid, we knew you'd bring a woman. What else can you do besides rely on women? Coward!"

The boy didn't say anything. At this moment, he actually deeply regretted bringing the girl here. He anxiously looked at the girl, who appeared quite calm. However, in the dark, the girl let go of the boy's hand and whispered something only they could hear: "Dear, remember that I used my life to save your hands..."

The boy suddenly froze. It turned out the girl had known all along why he had come, yet she still followed him. What a fool! The boy realized how much his heart hurt. He understood that he truly couldn't lose her. When he tried to pull the girl to run away, she had already been taken away by those people.

"This girl looks pretty good" "Take good care of big brother, big brother will be nice to you" "Where did that kid find such a beautiful girl? Truly entertaining" "Tonight, let's enjoy ourselves thoroughly"...

The girl tightly bit her lower lip and didn't speak, letting them do as they pleased.

Suddenly, the boy went crazy and rushed over: "Fool, I won't let you do this! You're mine! I was wrong, I was really wrong! I'm an idiot, I actually brought you here to avoid getting beaten, I'm a complete bastard... I'm sorry!

But I can't lose you! Only now do I realize how much I truly love you! I'm sorry, I realized too late, but you absolutely cannot do this for me! Wife, I won't allow you to do this..." Those people became angry and started kicking and beating the boy: "So what if she's your woman? You brought her yourself, who told you to injure our brothers? Kid, don't you want your hands anymore?..."

"Stop!" The girl spoke, "As long as you don't touch him, you can do whatever you want to me!" They stopped. By this time, the boy had already been beaten so badly that he couldn't get up. He could only helplessly watch as the girl was defiled by those beasts. At this moment, he wanted to kill himself, tears flowed uncontrollably...

After their passion, those people left. The boy crawled to the girl's side. Surprisingly, the girl smiled at him. That smile, desolate and hopeless. The boy froze, slapping himself across the face: "I'm sorry, wife, I..." The girl shakily stood up, looking at the boy and saying, "I don't blame you, I only blame myself, for falling irrevocably in love with you."

After speaking, the girl left. The boy tearfully watched the girl's retreating figure but didn't chase after her. He no longer had the right.

Turning around, tears instantly spread. The girl loved so tiredly, truly so exhausted. Why is loving someone so tiring? Husband, I knew the reason you came to find me the moment you did. I didn't want to care about you, but my heart betrayed me. I couldn't help but care about you, willingly paying for you because I love you, I couldn't...

But dear, I am still happy about your final realization. I wish I could embrace you, never separating for a lifetime. But how can someone as dirty as me have the face to appear before you? Dear, I hope you won't be sad about my departure. If possible, in heaven, I would still be willing to protect you...

You will never understand how much I've been hurt. This time, I'm truly exhausted.

Perhaps, it's time for me to rest...

Closing her eyes, the girl jumped into the river.

When the boy heard that the girl had committed suicide, he went mad. Afterwards, whenever he saw someone, he would ask, "Have you seen my wife? Have you seen my wife?..."

And so, until he grew old...

"I've always been standing where you hurt me

You've always stayed far away where I cry

Love has always remained on the night you loved me

You were once so good to me

............................................."

Although the above is fictional, such tragic love stories do occasionally happen in real life. I write these things only to remind everyone to cherish the person who loves you. Don't always wait until you've lost them to realize their value. Perhaps, by then, it will only make you regret for a lifetime...
