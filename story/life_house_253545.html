Bathroom renovation integrated design

by longersing on 2009-10-29 16:54:53

The integrated design of bathroom decoration adheres to the principle of practical decoration. The bathroom: the unification of practicalism and multifunctionality; the washbasin: popular shape + practical function; the bathtub: materials adding line beauty. In terms of design, it emphasizes simple and smooth lines, which is a new fashion element in bathroom decoration, manifested by integrating functional details into the design. Relevant connection: How to carry out bathroom decoration changes.

Bathroom: The Unification of Practicalism and Multifunctionality

With the improvement of living standards, many people in Shunde are beginning to pay attention to bathroom decoration. A series of combinations such as toilets, bathtubs, faucets, and bathroom furniture, the combination of straight surfaces and curves, the distribution of functions and space, the response between details and the whole are gradually being valued by decorators, all of which have exceeded the basic functions originally required for life.

Bathroom decoration will no longer be limited to "design", practical performance is also a new highlight in the current home decoration market in Shunde, such as water saving, energy saving, easy cleaning, noise reduction, anti-blocking, etc.

Washbasin: Popular Shape + Practical Function

From the current home decoration market in Shunde, effective use of bathroom space in decoration design has become mainstream, among which wide-edged washbasins are one of the popular elements. There are various shapes like under-counter, integrated wall-hung, semi-above-counter, etc., which can be custom-made according to the bathroom decoration space.

The integrated wall-hung washbasin is suitable for smaller bathrooms, with a simple design where the wide edge can hold small items like mouthwash cups and soap dishes. If needed, towel hooks can be added around the basin, making it tidy and space-saving. If the bathroom space allows, it's very suitable to configure a semi-above-counter washbasin, which is extremely artistic, and more small items can be placed on top.

Bathtub: Materials Adding Line Beauty

Cast iron, acrylic sheets, steel enamel, and other materials made bathtubs have started appearing in the Shunde market. These materials give the bathtub characteristics of constant temperature, durability, and line beauty. According to Ms. Li, a salesperson at the Shunde store of Leka Sanitary Ware, the shape and practical performance are the biggest selling points.

In terms of design, it emphasizes simple and smooth lines, which is a new fashion element in bathroom decoration, manifested by integrating functional details into the design. Using line principles, the headrests, armrests, anti-slip lines, faucets, etc., in the bathtub are artistically combined to form a smooth visual effect.

In terms of materials, acrylic sheets have good water temperature retention; while cast iron bathtubs use titanium powder enamel, making the bathroom fixtures white and glossy, and they also have certain acid resistance and higher impact resistance.
