How are you?

by wenfqplove on 2011-09-20 22:23:25

In my childhood days, no one cared for me. Everyone thought that since I had no father or mother, I was good for nothing and would not amount to anything in the future. But you never gave up on me, you loved me! Whenever other children had something, you would try your best to make sure I had it too. When I craved snacks and my grandmother refused to give them to me, you would secretly buy them for me on the way and put them in my schoolbag. You often took me around, with me following you in my little shorts. You never tired of me, as long as I wasn't a burden to you!

When I returned to that familiar yet strange home and looked at your portrait, I could no longer hear your voice or see your figure. I could only sit in the chair you used to sit in and quietly recall your kindness from the past. My heart aches so much, but I have to endure it. I cannot show it in front of grandma. I know that when you left, you were most worried about grandma, so I have the responsibility to take care of her. Although I can't replace you in taking care of her, loving her, and cherishing her, as long as I have the ability, I will certainly honor grandma! Please forgive me, I really couldn't stay there any longer because I couldn't bear the pain of missing you! Therefore, I avoided paying respects to you during the Qingming Festival, only burning some paper money for you far away. Even on this 14th day of the seventh lunar month, I avoid facing it, unable to face it, friends' blessings! To this day, I cannot accept that you are gone forever, until now I feel like you've always been here! Every time I want to call you, when I open the phone, I realize that there is no line that can connect me to you!

To this day, I believe that the last time I saw you, you knew that your life was coming to an end. You urged me to go back and work hard, to cherish myself, and to live well. But I never imagined that this farewell would be eternal, it's all because I'm too stupid! When I heard the news of your passing, I collapsed! I was so heartbroken, feeling like I was dying. I rolled, crawled, cried, and shouted, but I couldn't see you one last time because my body was weak at that time, and the doctor said that if I moved, my life would be in danger. In those days, I ate desperately every day and took care of myself, just wanting to recover quickly and see you sooner, but it was too late! Only then did I realize how painful it is to lose someone you love!

In the following days, it was really quiet and peaceful! I was lazy, even skipping meals to satisfy my laziness, but you went to the market every day to buy vegetables for me. You also often cooked and delivered food to me. Whatever you thought was delicious, you saved a portion for me. Even when you made soup, you would pack it in a thermos and bring it to me! No matter what I wanted to eat, you would buy it for me! If you saw something beneficial for my health, no matter how far, you would look for it and ask about it! Whatever I wanted, whether it was a table, cabinet, or anything else from home, you let me take it! Back then, we quietly ate egg tarts in the room, and every time grandma wasn't paying attention, I would stuff good things into your pocket. I regret that I didn't have the ability to give you anything back then!

Whenever mom did something harmful to me, although you couldn't be there in person to solve it, you called her repeatedly and scolded her severely, urging me to leave and abandon them! I know that except for you, no one else cares about my feelings and future. When I was forced to self-harm, you received the call thinking it was mom who was committing suicide, and the phone was filled with curses, wishing she would die soon. But when you found out it was me who attempted suicide, your tone instantly softened and became gentle and sorrowful, even causing you to faint from high blood pressure immediately. It's my fault, and once again, I say sorry!

I deeply remember that when I was young, every time someone bullied me and hurt me, you would go to the school time and again to settle accounts with them, arguing with others! Watching your figure, listening to your teachings, allowed me to learn strength, to learn not to care about others' cold eyes, to prove myself with awards, and to walk proudly on my own path! To live in a way that doesn't embarrass you!

That year, when I was not yet one year old, I lived with you. Your heavy preference for boys over girls never appeared in me, I believe this is fate! Since I was young, I had poor health, cried a lot, acted spoiled and disobedient, giving you a lot of trouble!

Every time I did something wrong, you never scolded me, but instead educated me patiently. Even when you knew I was wrong, you still stood by me. You said you were my safe haven, where I could rest after being wronged or tired outside. With you, I always had a place in our home! Regardless of whether I was down or proud, you cared for and protected me differently!

Grandpa, are you doing well? Missing you is like a flood, I really miss you so much. Every time I think of you, my throat starts to choke up, and my heart begins to ache. Longing, guilt, and regret overwhelm me and don't dissipate easily. Although I know my clumsy writing cannot express everything, and words seem powerless in the face of true and deep emotions, I have no way to tell you in person. Please allow me to use my inferior words to express my longing and regret for you...

After failing in my business and shop, my mood was difficult to calm down. Upon learning the truth of my failure, my heart felt dead. Under your guidance, I moved back to live with you. Seeing your white hair and difficulty moving due to a stroke, I felt so guilty. In the blink of an eye, I had been away for so many years, leaving you lonely for so long! Seeing you drag out a pile of things you had saved for me from under the bed, my heart warmed so much. For the sake of being able to take care of you in the future and repay your love, at that moment, I vowed to start anew.

Later, I went to Hubei and Beijing, and you supported me while worrying about me. No matter how bad your health was, you always said everything was fine on the phone! By the time I returned circuitously from Beijing, you had added more white hair! I was too selfish, too ignorant of cherishing time, too unaware of spending good time with you. It's all my fault!

A tree wants to be still but the wind won't stop; a child wants to nurture but the parent is gone! Death is so cruel, there's no room for "if". There's no chance to correct mistakes! But if there is a next life, I would still be your granddaughter, living happily with you. Sitting by the bedside listening to your stories, walking with you on the street, playing chess together in our free time, laughing happily. Cherishing every moment with you, I wouldn't act capriciously and upset you anymore! Beloved grandpa, I will always remember you, miss you, and may you rest in peace!

It has been more than half a year since you left, but your kindness and love for me are becoming clearer in my mind. Especially in recent days, missing you has become an indispensable part of my life. Remembrance is no longer limited to every festival related to honoring you; it could be a plot in a TV drama, or even some words, that make me think of you, my heart surging like waves, regret mingling, tears streaming down my face.
