I don't know who my father is, and mother never mentions him to me! But I know mother is not happy. I have never seen mother smile! Aunt Qianqian came over. She is mother's best friend, but what are they doing in the yard? I didn't dare to go out because I was afraid that mother would be unhappy! She didn't call me out. "Have you really thought it over?" Aunt Qianqian said to mother! "Yes, I've thought it over!" Mother seemed very sad! Mother, why are you always sad? In fact, I know you always feel sad behind my back! Mother, I still don't understand why you don't like me! Other children's parents take them out to play everyday! But you don't take me! You only take me when you go to fight and help others! Mother, I want to go out and play! Mother, you said I was born with great potential and am a good material for learning martial arts. You said I am a child of Wuzhuang, and have the power of high-level killing, high-level sneak attack and Chiyou Martial Arts Decisiveness! Plus your assistance of universal salvation! No one dares to bully us two! Mother, I am very obedient, and my kung fu is very powerful! No other children can beat me! But mother, why don't you like me! "Piaoling, since you have thought it over, don't regret it in the future!" Aunt Qianqian is still talking to mother! Aunt Qianqian is very nice to me, she buys me tanghulu everyday. "But, I feel he is so innocent..." Mother cried! Mother, why are you crying? What's wrong with you? "Sigh... The child is raised by you, and now you want to give him up for adoption, I understand how you feel!" Aunt Qianqian stopped talking! Mother, why don't you want me anymore? Do you know? You said that we must become strong in order not to be bullied, so I practice everyday. In fact, I want to go out and play with other children. Mother, I am strong now, why don't you want me? Mother, why? "Then I will go and tell Xiao Shenling..." Aunt Qianqian came in and said, "Xiao Shenling, look what auntie bought for you... Tanghulu!" Auntie waved the tanghulu in front of me! But I couldn't see anything! "Auntie, why doesn't mother want me anymore?" Auntie paused for a moment, "Since you have heard it, Cheng Jihongsan, I will tell you everything!" Originally, I had a father. Before my parents got married, they loved each other very much, the kind of love that many people envied. After they got married, mother gave birth to me! Mother took out her dowry: high-level killing, high-level sneak attack, Chiyou Martial Arts Decisiveness to teach me! But I don't know why father stole these three skill books and ran away! Mother was so sad! In order to continue raising me, mother traveled all over the three realms and found these skill books for me to learn! But now I am growing up, and I am more and more like my father. When mother sees me, she thinks of her unfaithful father, and becomes sadder! Sadder!
