Fudan graduate student becomes a homeless person: his academic qualifications are questioned

by 54704780 on 2012-02-18 14:17:12

Wang Xiaoxu told the reporter of Oriental Outlook that after the operation, Xiao Wu had got out of danger and was undergoing rehabilitative treatment. "Xiao Wu has lost his courage to live originally, so I am really afraid that he would not be able to bear such exposure of his painful experience by the media," said Wang Xiaoxu. A staff member from Fudan University also told the reporter of Oriental Outlook that it is impossible for Xiao Wu to enter the school in 2007 and graduate in January 2009. If there were such experiences, he could only be a non-graduate or drop out during this period. The professional master's degree of Fudan University is all enrolled in September and graduated in June. Wang Xiaoxu said that he never expected that this incident would be reported by the media, which made him very angry. "We have become good friends now and he has trusted me very much. However, after the media reports, I feel embarrassed to see him," said Wang Xiaoxu. According to media reports, Xiao Wu, 33 years old and from Anhui Province, graduated from undergraduate at Anhui University of Finance and Economics. After graduation, he became a county civil servant, then he quit the "iron rice bowl" job and took the postgraduate examination of Fudan University. In 2007, Xiao Wu was admitted to the computer science major of Fudan University and graduated with a master's degree in January 2009. Since Xiao Wu could not find a job after graduation, he eventually became a homeless person. Wang Xiaoxu told the reporter that the accident happened on January 6th. When Xiao Wu was in the most destitute and helpless situation, Wang Xiaoxu always stayed by his side. Wang Xiaoxu said that it was him who informed Xiao Wu's family after the accident. Surprisingly, the family knew nothing about Xiao Wu's current situation. A few days ago, Xiao Wu's father even sent a banner and a thank-you letter to the police station. The reporter of Oriental Outlook contacted Officer Wang Xiaoxu of Qiuaai Police Station in Yinzhou District, Ningbo City, who mentioned in the report. Wang Xiaoxu said that he personally saw Xiao Wu's diploma. But regarding the school Xiao Wu graduated from and the major he studied, Wang Xiaoxu refused to disclose it to the reporter. So, who exactly is Xiao Wu? What is his real name? Wang Xiaoxu indicated that in order to protect his privacy, he firmly refuses to disclose it. Wang Xiaoxu said that before the telephone interview by Oriental Outlook reporter, a staff member from Fudan University asked him about Xiao Wu's name, but he did not disclose it. On February 17, Du Lihua of Oriental Outlook reported: Today, an article titled "Fudan Postgraduate Can Not Find A Job And Becomes A Homeless Person" has been widely reprinted by major websites. Fudan University is currently coordinating all parties to confirm the identity of "Xiao Wu" in the article. However, teachers and students of the school all question the authenticity of "Xiao Wu's" postgraduate degree from Fudan University. The reporter of Oriental Outlook saw that once the article was published online, more than a hundred websites rushed to reprint it. This news also became the focus of students' attention on the BBS of Fudan University, but many students doubted the content of the report. In the BBS section "Postgraduate in Computer Science", a classmate forwarded this article at noon today. "Which lab can graduate with a master's degree in one and a half years?" Some classmates replied expressing doubts, and other classmates also questioned the time nodes in the report.
