La Liga - Ibra's controversial goal secures Barcelona a 1-0 win over Real Madrid, reclaiming the top spot.

by djbgufgire on 2009-11-30 19:09:39

La Liga - Ibrahimović's controversial goal secures a last-minute win, Barcelona 1-0 Real Madrid reclaim top spot   At 02:00 Beijing time on November 30 (19:00 local Spanish time on November 29), in the 12th round of the 09-10 La Liga season, a world-renowned Clásico took place where Barcelona, down to ten men, triumphed 1-0 over Real Madrid at Camp Nou, reclaiming the top spot by surpassing their arch-rivals. In the 20th minute, Kaká delivered a beautiful pass but Cristiano Ronaldo missed a one-on-one chance. In the 56th minute, Alves crossed the ball and Ibrahimović, who had just come off the bench four minutes earlier, scored with a stunning volley from the far post. In the 62nd minute, Busquets was shown a second yellow card and sent off, but Real Madrid failed to capitalize on their numerical advantage. In the 89th minute, Lass Diarra also accumulated two yellow cards and was sent off.

Cristiano Ronaldo and Kaká formed a formidable partnership while Messi led the attack with Ibrahimović as substitute. This was the 236th El Clásico, with Barcelona winning 96 of the previous 235 encounters, according to historical records. The match promised to be one of the most dazzling Clásicos in history, featuring clashes between Messi and Ibrahimović against Kaká and Ronaldo, along with the battle of Spanish goalkeepers Casillas and Valdés, as well as the midfield duel between Alonso and Xavi capturing global attention.

For this game, Ronaldo returned to lead Real Madrid's star-studded lineup, with Kaká playing as the attacking midfielder, Marcelo on the left flank, Higuaín leading the attack alone, Benzema on the bench, Las Diarra and Alonso anchoring the midfield, Albiol and Pepe commanding the defense, and Casillas guarding the goal. On the Barcelona side, Messi formed a trident with Iniesta and Henry, with Ibrahimović as a substitute. Xavi controlled the midfield, Piqué and Puyol commanded the defense, and Valdés guarded the goal.

The century-old rivalry was set to ignite, but Alves made an early error due to nerves. Pre-match rain in Barcelona did little to dampen the fans' enthusiasm. Club presidents Laporta and Florentino embraced warmly in the VIP box. After the coin toss, Real Madrid kicked off first. In the 1st minute, Xavi delivered a brilliant through ball for Messi, but Casillas came out quickly to clear the danger. Playing at home, Barcelona showed great patience, searching for opportunities through continuous back passes. In the 3rd minute, under pressure from Marcelo, Messi delivered a clever heel pass, but Alves' cross flew directly into the stands.

In the 4th minute, Kaká, participating in his first Spanish Clásico, delivered a long through ball, but Higuaín mishandled it inside the box, allowing Valdés to secure possession. In the 6th minute, Kaká distributed the ball in midfield, Ronaldo used his speed to break past Puyol on the left wing and delivered a cross, but Marcelo's follow-up shot went high. In the 7th minute, Ramos broke forward, creating a 4v4 situation, but Kaká's pass was blocked by a defender, and Valdés secured the ball. In the 8th minute, Messi moved centrally, broke through the defense, and passed to Henry, whose low cross from the left side of the box was saved by Casillas. In the 9th minute, Xavi attempted a powerful long-range shot from 24 yards out that went high.

Ronaldo and Kaká demonstrated excellent chemistry, but Ronaldo missed a golden opportunity. In the 11th minute, Ronaldo broke through in midfield, prompting Busquets to commit a foul with a sliding tackle, drawing loud boos from the Barcelona fans in the stands. Ronaldo's free kick hit the wall and bounced out. In the 14th minute, Ronaldo delivered a through ball on the left flank, and Kaká surged forward but was brilliantly intercepted by Piqué. In the 17th minute, Higuaín forced his way through centrally, but Keita cut out the pass. In the 18th minute, Henry delivered a through ball, and Abidal's cross from the left was blocked by Ramos for a corner.

Real Madrid’s best chance came when Kaká skillfully maneuvered past Piqué in midfield and provided a perfect pass for Ronaldo, unmarked at the far post, but Valdés miraculously stopped the shot with his foot! In the 21st minute, Ronaldo delivered a clever backheel pass, and Kaká surged forward again, but Valdés was once again quick to defuse the danger. In the 24th minute, Piqué's long clearance found Keita breaking through, but Casillas gathered the ball safely. In the 25th minute, Ronaldo distributed the ball wide right, and Kaká quickly shifted it to Marcelo on the left, but his poor control allowed Puyol to make a crucial interception.

The tempo continued to rise, with both teams exchanging chances. In the 34th minute, Messi was brought down on the right flank by Arbeloa after persistent tugging, earning him the game's first yellow card. In the 36th minute, Xavi slipped a through ball for Henry, whose cross was cleared by Casillas for a corner. In the 38th minute, Barcelona executed a fine short passing sequence, with Xavi feeding Iniesta, whose low cross was saved by Casillas diving low. In the 40th minute, Iniesta's flick-on pass was handled by Albiol, earning him a yellow card. Tempers flared as Iniesta and Ronaldo exchanged words before calming down.

After halftime, the intensity remained high. In the 51st minute, Ibrahimović replaced Henry, greeted warmly by the fans. In the 53rd minute, Higuaín's powerful shot from the left side was blocked by Puyol. Barcelona finally broke the deadlock in the 56th minute. Keita distributed the ball, and Alves delivered a precise cross from the right. Pepe misjudged the run, leaving Ibrahimović, who had only been on the pitch for four minutes, to score with a stunning left-footed volley from close range. The goal was too close for Casillas to react! 1-0! This was Ibrahimović's eighth league goal of the season and his debut goal in the Spanish Clásico, although replays suggested he might have been offside.

In the 62nd minute, Busquets received a second yellow card for an unnecessary handball in defense, resulting in his red card dismissal. Barcelona were reduced to ten men. Guardiola vented his frustration by slamming the glass roof of the substitutes' bench. In the 65th minute, Marcelo's cross found Ronaldo, whose header went narrowly over the bar. Subsequently, Benzema replaced Ronaldo, and Yaya Touré replaced Keita.

Messi almost doubled the lead as Real Madrid pushed hard for an equalizer. In the 69th minute, Pepe fouled Messi during a dribble and was shown a yellow card. Xavi's free-kick found Piqué at the back post, whose header grazed the post. In the 70th minute, Benzema's powerful shot from the left side of the box was blocked by Puyol. In the 74th minute, Pellegrini substituted Ableroa with Raul. In the 76th minute, Xavi delivered a precise through ball for Abidal, whose shot went narrowly wide. In the 78th minute, Marcelo was cautioned for a foul on Puyol. In the 80th minute, from a corner, Raul's header found Benzema, who missed a golden chance.

In the dying moments, Barcelona countered, with Alves delivering a sublime through ball to Messi, who was denied by Casillas' heroic save. In the 89th minute, Diarra was shown a second yellow card for fouling Xavi, resulting in his dismissal. The match ended with Barcelona prevailing 1-0 at home over Real Madrid.

Starting lineups:

Barcelona (4-3-3):

1-Valdés; 2-Alves, 3-Piqué, 5-Puyol, 22-Abidal; 6-Xavi, 10-Busquets, 15-Keata (24-Yaya Touré 66'); 10-Messi, 14-Henry (9-Ibrahimović 51'), 8-Iniesta

Real Madrid (4-2-3-1):

1-Casillas; 4-Ramos, 18-Albiol, 3-Pepe, 2-Arbeloa (7-Raul 74'); 10-Las Diarra, 22-Alonso; 12-Marcelo, 8-Kaká, 9-Ronaldo (11-Benzema 64'); 20-Higuaín
