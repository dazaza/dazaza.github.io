【Basic Introduction】

The demo version of "Need for Speed: Underground 2," which boasts exotic cars, beautiful women, and wild underground street racing, has been released. The game features famous lingerie model and TV host Brooke Burke as its spokesperson. In the game, she appears in a unique 2D form and serves as an important guide for players. Brooke Burke's character is named Rachel, who is a close friend of the female race mentor from the previous game. At the very beginning of this game, Rachel will give you your first mission: to drive her car back to the garage. This task allows you to familiarize yourself with the overall structure of the game. You don't have to rush to return the car because there are many locations along the way that can trigger various side missions. Since the player's car is quite poor at the start, it might be better to explore more along the way because once you drive the car back to the garage, Rachel will take it back.

【System Requirements】

CPU: 1.4 GHz or higher

RAM: 256 MB or higher

Hard Drive: 2 GB or more free space

Graphics Card: Compatible with DirectX 9.0c

Sound Card: Compatible with DirectX 9.0c

Input Devices: Keyboard, mouse, steering wheel, or controller

Graphics card requires 32MB or more video memory: ATI Radeon 7500 or above; ATI Radeon Xpress 200; NVIDIA GeForce2 MX/GTS or above; Intel 950/i915g; S3 GammaChrome S18 Pro

Multiplayer mode requires each computer to have a game disc and a broadband (Cable, DSL or faster) network connection.
