Make new contributions to a new Kunming for ecological civilization

Since the 1972 United Nations Human Environment Conference determined to establish World Environment Day, every June 5th, according to different environmental issues, hotspots, and themes, various environmental protection activities are carried out around the world. This year, the theme of World Environment Day is "Forests: Nature at Your Service", the theme for China's Environment Day is "Building Ecological Civilization; Sharing a Green Future", and the theme for Kunming's Environment Day is: "New Ecological Kunming; A Green and Healthy Good Life". On the occasion of this year's June 5th "World Environment Day," I had an exclusive interview with Kunming’s Environmental Protection Bureau Director Liu Yajin regarding how to make wall-hung boilers more energy-efficient.

Reporter: This year, the theme of "World Environment Day" in Kunming is "Ecological New Kunming; Green and Healthy Good Life." What is the background and significance behind this theme?

Liu Yajin: Currently, from the global level down to the national level, and further to Kunming, the call for attention to the environment is growing louder day by day. In Kunming, environmental protection and ecological construction are not only strategic plans adhering to the scientific development perspective by the Kunming Municipal Committee and Government but also the glorious mission and persistent pursuit of all environmental protectors on Kunming's environmental frontlines. Environmental protection and ecology will inevitably become a keyword in the lives of all citizens. The theme of this year's World Environment Day in Kunming aims to advocate that citizens cultivate healthy living habits, participate in ecological civilization construction, and share the beauty of social green harmony.

Reporter: What is the current status of Kunming's environmental protection work? What are some of the main achievements?

Liu Yajin: During the "Eleventh Five-Year Plan" period, Kunming's environmental protection work achieved remarkable results. These are mainly reflected in: successfully completing the "Eleventh Five-Year Plan" pollution reduction tasks assigned by the provincial government to our city. During the "Eleventh Five-Year Plan" period, we completed a total of 163 projects listed in the emission reduction engineering construction, reducing chemical oxygen demand by 37,800 tons and sulfur dioxide by 55,100 tons.

The water environment remediation of key basins such as Dianchi Lake and Yangzonghai has achieved phased results. Compared with 2005, in 2010, the water quality of the Dianchi Lake Grass Sea showed a decrease in the average values of total nitrogen, total phosphorus, ammonia nitrogen, and chlorophyll a by 14.7%, 43.7%, 33.5%, and 26.8% respectively; the water quality of Yangzonghai recovered to Class IV. The water environment comprehensive remediation of “one lake and two rivers” within the city domain has achieved significant results.

Comprehensive environmental remediation has been deeply promoted, and the quality of the atmospheric environment continues to improve. In 2010, the number of days with excellent air quality in the urban area increased from 18 days in 2006 to 116 days, and the air quality excellence rate has remained at 100% for four consecutive years.

Model creation work is orderly carried out, and ecological creation achievements are evident. Completed the ecological creation planning and implementation organization of 14 counties (cities) districts, among the 26 model creation indicators, 23 indicators have reached the standard, passed the preliminary inspection organized by the province, and applied to the National Environmental Protection Department for assessment and acceptance. Over the past five years, the whole city has created a total of 6 national-level ecological towns, 23 provincial-level ecological towns, 215 green schools, 93 green communities, and 6 environmental education bases.

Innovative institutional work has yielded abundant results, and environmental law enforcement achievements are evident. During the "Eleventh Five-Year Plan" period, a total of 163 institutional innovations were completed, and pioneering explorations such as the "Environmental Police", "Environmental Court", and "Environmental Public Interest Litigation System" systems were launched nationwide. The city's environmental protection work has won the municipal system innovation special award for two consecutive years. Successfully completed the first pollution source census work.

Reporter: During the "Twelfth Five-Year Plan" period, how will Kunming's environmental protection work be carried out? What are the key construction areas?

Liu Yajin: The "Twelfth Five-Year Plan" period is a critical time for Kunming to comprehensively build a moderately prosperous society, a tough period to accelerate the transformation of economic development methods, and also a golden period for Kunming to build a regional international city facing Southwest China.

The entire environmental protection system must closely revolve around the key work of the Municipal Committee and Government, and comprehensively promote environmental protection and ecological civilization construction:

Continue to deeply implement the Seven Colorful Yunnan Kunming Protection Action, comprehensively promote seven major actions including environmental rule of law, environmental governance, environmental sunshine, green ecological protection, green creation, green dissemination, and energy conservation and consumption reduction, and strive to create an "ecological Kunming."

Implement a low-carbon development strategy and accelerate the construction of a low-carbon city. Establish green and low-carbon development concepts, take the low-carbonization of key areas as a breakthrough, promote clean production, develop a circular economy, carry out a "low-carbon life public action," actively guide the development of a circular economy and environmental protection industries, and strive to build a six-in-one low-carbon city comprising low-carbon industries, low-carbon buildings, low-carbon transportation, low-carbon living, low-carbon environment, and a low-carbon society.

Further strengthen the responsibility system for emission reduction targets and earnestly grasp pollution reduction. Pollution reduction indicators should be included in the comprehensive evaluation system of economic and social development, strictly implement the "dual responsibility" and "veto power system" systems of environmental protection.

Continue to increase efforts in the comprehensive prevention and treatment of water environments in the Dianchi Basin, Yangzonghai, and Niu Lanjiang River. Continue to deepen the "river (section) leader responsibility system," further promote the comprehensive remediation and inspection work of key basins such as the main inflow (outflow) Dian rivers, tributaries (channels), and Niu Lanjiang River.
