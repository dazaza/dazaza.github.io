Technological innovation, forge together

by ledlamp8 on 2012-03-07 20:23:27

Career hand in the LED industry pioneer compose blueprint for LED lighting. November 3, 2011 in Shanghai, China - The LED market leader in the field of Cree, Inc. (Nasdaq: CREE) unveiled at the 2011 China International Industrial Fair its advanced LED innovations and efficient lighting solutions, once again demonstrating Cree’s leading position in the LED field. Career and experts from the domestic semiconductor lighting field participated in the Fourth Urban Lighting Forum held on November 2, aiming to promote efficient LED lighting, implement information and experience sharing, and improve the application of LED lighting in cities.

The Chinese government attaches great importance to the development of the semiconductor lighting industry. In 2003, the National Semiconductor Lighting Project Coordination Leading Group was established, and the National Semiconductor Lighting Project was initiated. A series of related policies have been issued to develop the LED industry. The country's 12th Five-Year Plan energy-saving and emission reduction target has created a good opportunity for the development of the LED industry. According to the six ministries jointly issued document, President Hu Jintao, General Secretary, conducted special inspections of the LED lighting industry this year.

The 2011 China International Industry Fair is one of the important activities of the City Lighting Forum, bringing together experts from across the semiconductor lighting field to discuss and exchange LED technology development and promote the popularity of LED lighting.

In 2003-2011, China's LED pioneers were named for their outstanding contributions to the rise and development process of China’s LED industry in terms of research and development, application, promotion, and other aspects. A number of participants, including off-white Chen Yansheng, led spotlight Fangzhi Lie, Zhang Wansheng, Guo-Yi Zhang, Feng-Yi Jiang, Zhang Hai Cong Hao Losey, Lin Minghe Feng, and 20 others from leading organizations, academia, and industry experts in the semiconductor lighting field attended the award ceremony.

At the forum, experts in the audience unanimously expressed optimism about the prospects for the development of China's LED industry. Domestic governments at all levels continue to increase R&D efforts and investments in LED technology and industry to accelerate the development of the LED market, and have basically formed a complete LED industrial chain.

Currently, the semiconductor lighting industry has become one of the important green industries promoting China’s rapid economic development and has laid a solid foundation for realizing energy conservation and a green recycling economy. China has taken a big step towards the era of LED lighting.

Cree's general manager of China, Mr. Tang Guoqing, said: "From the perspective of global strategy, Cree has established chip plants and packaging plants in China. Cree is also the only internationally renowned LED company with chip production in China. More than 90% of the LED package devices by Chinese factories are Career’s core, which is an important strategy of Cree’s localization in China."

For a long time, Career has positively responded to national policy and, leveraging world-leading LED lighting technology, continuously developed high-quality products that meet different application areas and successfully completed a number of demonstration projects in the Beijing Olympics, Shanghai World Expo, and the Great Hall of the People. Cree's success in the Chinese market is bound to the support and cooperation of relevant governments, industry bodies, and partners.

Cree will continue to align with the concept of 'scientific and technological innovation, forging together' to further strengthen in-depth cooperation with colleagues in the industry, working alongside China's experts in the field of LED lighting to promote cooperation and contribute to the development of China's LED industry and energy-saving work.

Innovative products and LED lighting solutions in various application areas such as road lighting, landscape lighting, and displays, along with successful stories like the lighting renovation project of the Shanghai Donghai Bridge, Shenzhen 120 km highway LED lighting engineering, solar LED street lamp project in Tibet, and the LED display at the second terminal of Shanghai Hongqiao Airport, show Career's unprecedented creation of urban lighting effects, providing sleek and quality life for people.
