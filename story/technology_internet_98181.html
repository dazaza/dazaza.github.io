Huayu Logistics - Shanghai Huayu Logistics Co., Ltd. was established in 1998 and is one of the earlier founded logistics companies in Shanghai. Shanghai Huayu Logistics Co., Ltd. is a private enterprise specializing in logistics, transportation, and services. The business scope of Shanghai Huayu Logistics Co., Ltd. includes complex lifting and installation, moving, air conditioning equipment maintenance, pipeline installation and unblocking, as well as comprehensive property management services for numerous schools, factories, government agencies, enterprises, institutions, and residential communities. The company has continuously grown in scale and currently owns more than forty vehicles of various types and over eighty skilled workers, making it one of the larger logistics companies in Shanghai.

Shanghai Huayu Logistics adheres to the business philosophy of "customer first." Taking serving customers as its responsibility, Shanghai Huayu Logistics pursues safety, efficiency, punctuality, and dedication in service, while exploring a set of strict management systems and efficient, reasonable moving operation procedures, providing customers with peace of mind and confidence.

Huayu Logistics has proposed the slogan "Life is limited, but service is infinite," attracting tens of thousands of households and hundreds of government agencies and enterprises in the city. The company's business has expanded from within the city to provinces and regions outside, earning the trust and recognition of a wide range of customers.

In recent years, under the care and support of all sectors of society, Shanghai Huayu Logistics Co., Ltd. has seen significant improvement in economic and social benefits. Shanghai Huayu Logistics will continue to improve scientific management standards and quality service criteria. When conditions are ripe, with the guidance of a management consulting firm, the company will fully implement customer satisfaction systems and corporate image identification systems, comprehensively enhancing the company's image and brand.

Shanghai Huayu Logistics pursues "first-class service + reasonable charges = 100% satisfaction." Customer satisfaction is our greatest success!

Shanghai Huayu Logistics can undertake shipping according to customer requirements and specified needs, offering quick delivery and flexible methods.

The service creed of Shanghai Huayu Logistics Co., Ltd. is: high quality at low prices, mutual benefit, and common growth.
