Taste of Spring

by zhouzhou56 on 2011-12-31 00:52:19

Savoring the taste of spring

Time flies, and before we know it, spring has passed and summer has arrived. At this moment, I am still savoring the taste of spring. This year's Qingming Festival was particularly sunny, with an exceptionally clear sky, gentle breeze, and vibrant flowers. We, along with our niece Yan Yan, went to Poyang Lake for a spring outing to enjoy the scenery! Away from the hustle and bustle of the city, we enjoyed nature, felt the tranquility and ease of the fields, and experienced spring.

It is said that Poyang Lake is experiencing a severe water shortage this year, with drought conditions unprecedented in history. Although there was also a water shortage at Poyang Lake during the Qingming Festival, it was still very charming. Standing on the embankment of Poyang Lake, one could take in the vast lake view. Not only could we stand by the shores of Poyang Lake to feel the warmth of the spring sun and admire the beautiful Poyang Lake bathed in sunlight, but we also personally walked into the farmland to see the rape flowers and smell the earthy fragrance. We picked wild flowers, ate farm meals, and truly enjoyed ourselves!

Dreaming back to Poyang Lake, the shimmering lake water nourishes the bright April sun, and the beautiful lake scenery makes us drunk in it. Stepping into the fields, looking forward to the harvest fills our hearts with joy.

With warm spring sunshine and all things coming back to life, the sun shines on the earth. The grass grows lush, red flowers bloom vibrantly, and buds sprout on tree branches. It's spring, and you can smell the fragrance of flowers everywhere. Yellow rape flowers, golden Forsythia; crimson azaleas, and delicate peach blossoms? Those tender yellows, fresh greens, pale pinks, elegant whites... those verdant branches, warm sunshine, moist soil... the air is filled with the breath of spring, making one feel incredibly comfortable; intoxicated; deeply moved; in spring, we feel the power of life!

We immerse ourselves in the embrace of nature, breathing in the fresh air that invigorates the soul, listening to the sounds of all things awakening and the grass sprouting, greedily enjoying the taste of spring. Our bodies feel much lighter, and our moods are extraordinarily joyful. I love the warmth that ripples in the spring breeze; I love the feeling of vibrant colors impacting my vision in spring. Amidst the green mountains and clear waters, we savor various delicious flavors with the fragrance of the countryside... We search for the taste of spring, seek happiness, seek... sharing the spring outing, sharing joy!

The spring wind blows gently, softly caressing the hair on my forehead, filling my heart with indescribable joy! Glancing sideways at the graceful willow trees beside me, they seem to have taken on a hint of new green. The tender green tips of the willows seem to be telling me: the vibrant spring has arrived! Overflowing with the warmth of spring. Walking on the road, I see the resilient grass breaking through the soil with its tenacious perseverance, settling down under the walls, using its vivid green color to decorate the beautiful spring without reservation, and smelling a strong spring fragrance! Spring is indeed too beautiful! Spring stays forever in my heart, and even now, when I think about it, I still feel that beauty!
