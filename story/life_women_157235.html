Men love shoes as they love women - Jokes Stories

by longersing on 2009-06-07 21:10:20

One world, one dream, different women. A man's attitude towards women can be seen from his relationship with shoes, and the sincerity of his feelings for women can be seen from the shoes he wears.

1. Men wearing new shoes walk carefully, afraid that grass, trees, or mud will stain their shoes, even if they are not a pair of genuine sneakers.

Story imagination: Men treat women like shoes, not allowing others to touch their women, having a strong virgin complex, and requiring girls not to talk to strangers.

2. Never tie shoelaces or only tie them once without untying them again, directly stepping into the shoes.

Imagination of such men: Laziness is not inherent, nor is diligence expected. They treat life like tying shoelaces, getting by as long as possible. They have no requirements for women, as long as the lights are off, it's all the same. Their requirement for girls: being able to cook and bear children is enough. Once together with this kind of man, it's hard to break up because they are too stubborn.

3. Wearing branded shoes and deliberately crossing legs in crowded places.

Small tip: The rich can afford branded shoes, while the poor can wear counterfeit ones. Poor men with strong vanity, and rich men who are unreliable and overly showy. Regarding women: Rich men prefer beautiful women, considering face-saving as the top priority, other factors may not matter at all. For poor men, it reflects inferiority, intentionally showing off to stand out among the crowd and get noticed by the opposite sex but unable to put aside pride to pursue women. Therefore, these kinds of men will readily agree to any woman who initiates contact. Women's perspective: Such men are undesirable unless they are very wealthy.

4. Changing shoes every day.

Playboy, short-lived feelings. If it goes on long term, there will inevitably be heartaches due to the man's infidelity. For a healthy life, it's best to avoid them. It's like the Gubaiyou competition where adjustments are made daily to cater to algorithms, switching back and forth until eventually punished, leaving one to cry.

5. Never washing shoes until they are worn out.

Imagination: Which woman would want to spend her life with such a dirty person? Would you really wait until your home smells so bad after a few years that it becomes unbearable? Men’s treatment of women: Too casual, lacking affection, not caring about your clothing or makeup. You might live with him for ten years without truly integrating, and he might not even know what brand of "bread," lip gloss, or eyeshadow you use.

6. Playing basketball in shoes, using a new pair each week.

Such men never see you as a woman.
