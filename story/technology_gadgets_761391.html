162 yuan! Nokia's "legendary phone" 1050 is now officially on sale

by geekzhang on 2013-05-03 13:23:55

At 3 PM today, Nokia teamed up with China Post to release the iconic 1050 phone, and the first batch of users will be a group of rural mail carriers in Rugao, Jiangsu (Nokia donated 500 units). These mail carriers not only handle their postal duties but are also enthusiastic about providing assistance to elderly people living alone.From the feedback of on-site testers, the Nokia 1050 has an outer shell made of a rubber-like material, offering a very good grip. For users who have been using touch screen phones for a long time, the T9 keypad of the 1050 (splash-proof) is quite special, and of course, it also feels good.The 1050 also features a flashlight function, with an LED bulb on the top of the body. The phone also comes equipped with an FM radio and classic games like Snake. Additionally, the thickness of the phone is 14.3mm, weighing approximately 70g, with a built-in battery capacity of 800mAh. According to official claims, it can support 12.5 hours of continuous talk time or a standby time of up to 35 days.Nokia has announced that the 1050 is already available on the market, priced at 162 yuan, with users having the choice of blue or black colors. At the launch event, Nokia stated that this phone is already on sale, with the first batch being launched in collaboration with Tianyin Communication. It currently supports Mobile and Unicom networks, but does not support Telecom. Eager users should quickly check out physical stores.
