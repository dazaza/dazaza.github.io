[Book Introduction]

This is another masterpiece by Helen Palmer, the author of the international bestseller "The Enneagram". It helps you increase your insight into others for professional growth and romantic relationships! World-renowned Enneagram expert Helen Palmer focuses on the two most important aspects of life: work and love. She analyzes the mindset and behavior of the nine personality types in both the workplace and romance, and tells us how to deal with each type to achieve satisfying results. How to make a perfectionist boss stop being picky, how to calm down a dominant client, how to make a self-centered spouse more empathetic, and how to make a fun-loving partner more devoted. The Enneagram is a deep way of understanding people, dividing them into nine types according to their thoughts, emotions, and behaviors: perfectionists, givers, achievers, romantics, observers, skeptics, hedonists, protectors, and mediators. The greatest strength of the Enneagram lies in its ability to penetrate beyond surface-level joys and sorrows, reaching the deepest parts of the human heart to discover the truest, most basic needs and desires. The Enneagram has scientific, practical, and systematic characteristics. The Enneagram and leadership have been listed as part of the MBA curriculum at Stanford Business School, and it is also a widely adopted human capital training program in many multinational companies.

[Author Introduction]

Helen Palmer, a leading master of the Enneagram. She has been studying the Enneagram for over thirty years and is a world-renowned expert in Enneagram theory. She has authored several books on the Enneagram, including the globally bestselling "The Enneagram" and "The Enneagram in Love and Work", which have been translated into twenty-two languages.
