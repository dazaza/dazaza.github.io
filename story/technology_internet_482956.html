Wood-plastic flooring may become a new trend in the floor industry in the future

by jdhgsa078 on 2011-08-29 16:45:51

With the slogan of low-carbon environmental protection, more and more people's concepts have begun to change. In today's floor industry, the concept of wooden-plastic composite flooring is already familiar to many professionals in the industry. Wooden-plastic composite materials are a new type of environmental protection building material that has emerged in recent years. It is also just getting started abroad. The main raw materials for these products are usually waste wood, agricultural and forestry straw and other plant fibers, as well as recyclable waste plastics, with no other harmful components. Most importantly, they can be recycled, making them truly environmentally friendly, low-carbon, and renewable products. According to estimates by relevant industry experts, wooden-plastic floors may become a new trend in the industry in the coming years.

As our living standards continue to improve, people's attention to environmental resources is also increasing. The circular economic development model centered on waste material recycling and comprehensive utilization of resources has become a trend in world economic development. Developing resource recycling technology domestically is not only important now, but will also become a new international trend for the future development of China's national economy and sustainable environment. Due to the domestic market being still in its infancy, leather market conditions, wooden-plastic products have not yet been widely promoted in the domestic market. But precisely because of this, there is still considerable room for growth in wooden-plastic composite materials and finished products. It is expected that within a few years, the domestic production volume of wooden-plastic composite materials will exceed one million tons, with an output value exceeding 1 billion yuan, forming a new system of wooden-plastic composite products. Wooden-plastic floors will replace solid wood floors and become a new trend in the flooring industry.

What are wooden-plastic floors?

Many people who are unfamiliar may ask some questions about wooden-plastic floors, such as the price of wooden-plastic floors and their impact on users. In fact, wooden-plastic floors are also known in the industry as wooden-plastic composite panels. They are a high-tech green environmental protection material made primarily from cellulose, wood fiber combined with thermoplastic polymers and processing aids mixed uniformly and then formed through heating and extrusion by mold equipment. This is a new type of environmental protection material that can replace plastic and wood. It is marked in English as Wood Plastic Composites, which industry professionals abbreviate as WPC. It has processing characteristics similar to wood, can be sawed, drilled, and nailed using ordinary tools, and also possesses characteristics that wood cannot match, such as corrosion resistance and water resistance, highlighting a prominent wood-like texture. This makes it a very durable outdoor waterproof and anti-corrosion building material. To summarize, wooden-plastic floors have many advantages, such as insect resistance, termite resistance, water resistance, moisture resistance, multiple color options, strong formability, environmental friendliness, no pollution, high fire resistance, good processability, simple installation, no cracking or deformation, good sound absorption effects, etc. As a new type of industry material, it is believed to have great potential for development in the coming years.
