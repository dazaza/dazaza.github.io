Man commits suicide by hanging due to family trivialities, heart resumes beating one hour after stopping

by xue94fwsh on 2012-03-03 16:26:44

A Man Survives an Hour of Cardiac Arrest After Hanging Himself Due to Family Disputes

At around 2:00 AM, under normal circumstances, when a person's heart stops beating for about 10 minutes, the patient will die. However, in Hefei city, a man whose heart stopped beating for an hour after hanging himself was miraculously revived through emergency treatment. At approximately 12:10 AM today, the man was admitted to the ICU ward at the First People's Hospital of Hefei. But doctors indicated that even if the man survives, he would likely become a vegetative state patient. It was understood that this incident occurred merely due to family trivialities.

Family Members Lose Emotional Control

When the reporter arrived at the emergency department of the First People’s Hospital of Hefei, medical staff were doing their utmost to save the man who had hanged himself. Outside the emergency room, several women were crying together; they were relatives of the man who had attempted suicide. One woman screamed hoarsely, “It’s you who ruined my brother!” She directed the blame for the tragedy towards the man’s wife, who also burst into tears. To prevent the family members from losing emotional control, the hospital dispatched security personnel to maintain order.

After introduction, it was learned that the man was 42 years old, originally from Feidong County. Before the incident, he was doing business near Huaihe Road Pedestrian Street.

Miraculous Heartbeat Recovery

An external medicine doctor revealed that there were obvious marks on the man’s neck from the hanging, “When he was brought to the hospital, his heart had already stopped beating.” Nevertheless, the medical staff still attempted to resuscitate him. Time passed second by second, yet the man’s heartbeat did not return. “We were preparing to issue a death notice.”

About an hour later, something miraculous happened that thrilled all the medical staff involved in the rescue. The screen showed the man’s heart suddenly moved once, then began to beat rhythmically. Several medical workers were incredibly excited. They clearly knew that the man had just stepped back from the brink of death. Although the man regained his heartbeat, he could not breathe independently and had to rely on a ventilator to maintain his breathing. He was subsequently transferred to the ICU of the hospital.

The doctor disclosed that the man regaining his heartbeat was considered a miracle. Under normal circumstances, when a person’s heart stops beating for 5-10 minutes, and the brain lacks oxygen for 5-10 minutes, the patient will die. “His heart stopped for over an hour, but luckily, we persisted and managed to revive him.”

Ruining a Happy Family

At the ICU ward entrance, a doctor stated that although the man's heart was temporarily beating again, there was no guarantee that he could endure, "He might end up in a vegetative state." As soon as the doctor finished speaking, the man’s sister suddenly knelt down and cried out in grief, “He is still so young!”

It was understood that the man had a happy large family. They moved from their rural hometown in Feidong County to settle down in Hefei City. Relying on their small business near the pedestrian street, their lives gradually became prosperous. This sudden misfortune left several relatives present lamenting how unexpected the event was, making it unbearable for them to bear.

Argument Over Trivial Matters

According to relatives, the man was an extremely honest and straightforward person, usually speaking little and not good at expressing himself. Recently, he and his wife had disputes over family trivial matters. "During dinner, his wife even threw the plate," said the relatives after the incident, speculating, "He probably couldn't think straight at the moment." Some believed that before the incident, the man showed signs of depression, which might have been related to his decision to hang himself.

In fact, last night when the man hanged himself at home, his family didn’t realize what had happened until they saw the rope used for the hanging.

Doctors from the First People's Hospital of Hefei revealed that the man was discovered quite late, and additional time was needed to transport him to the hospital. "If he had been found earlier, it might have been better. The prolonged cardiac arrest led to massive death of brain cells, and the best outcome of the rescue would be that he becomes a vegetative state patient."

As of the time the reporter went to press, the man was still being treated in the hospital.
