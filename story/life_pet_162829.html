Teach children how to love

by qxing0001 on 2009-06-17 16:12:00

China Pet Network (http://www.dudupet.cn/) observes and analyzes that, for family members, the relationship between children and pets can be said to be the closest. Through interacting with pets, children learn how to respect others, cultivate their empathy and love, and will benefit from it for a lifetime. A child and a dog really make a great "image combination."

Whenever we talk about what is the greatest and most selfless,

Most people would think that the love of parents for their children is the most selfless and great,

Because its giving is unconditional and does not expect anything in return.

In fact, we as parents do not have such high realms,

It is the pet that truly gives the child unconditional attention.

It doesn't care about how well the child is doing in school, nor does it care about whether the child knows another foreign language,

And even less does it care about whether the child has done something foolish or whether they are mischievous.

No matter what setbacks the child encounters, it will give him unconditional attention.

In front of the pet, the child can appear as his true self and receive acceptance and recognition,

Which gives him confidence, and this is precisely the courage he needs to start anew.

Psychologists attribute the close relationship between pets and children to the "non-discriminatory" nature of pets,

This completely unconditional attention allows children to find comfort while accompanying and playing with pets,

And find security in their own world.

When carefully selected, healthy and gentle "doctor dogs" come to autistic children,

They have the ability to make those originally expressionless faces smile.

Everyone enjoys unconditional attention and company, and for parents,

As long as they pay more attention and invest in the interaction between their children and pets,

Such as choosing suitable pets for children, helping them build good relationships with pets, etc...

The child will have the most patient and confidential little listener,

Who gives him unconditional attention and company.
