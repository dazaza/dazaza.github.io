Why Choose Overseas Virtual Hosting

by anonymous on 2011-06-10 18:28:40

For foreign trade enterprise websites, it is generally recognized in the industry that only by adopting American web space can ideal promotional effects be achieved. Recognizing this fact, more and more foreign trade enterprises are choosing overseas virtual hosting. Indeed, overseas virtual hosting has incomparable advantages over domestic spaces. Here, let's discuss the advantages of overseas virtual hosting!

Advantages of Overseas Virtual Hosting Space:

1. Overcome the interconnection problems between Telecom and Unicom

The interconnection problem between Telecom and Unicom has always been troubling Chinese Internet users. From the following saying, we can get a glimpse of this issue: "The farthest distance in the world is not between China and the United States, but between Unicom and Telecom." By using overseas virtual hosting, such troubles can be easily avoided.

2. Avoid headaches like filing issues, ready to use immediately

In line with the special operation of ten ministries to crack down on obscene and pornographic activities, in order to solve some existing problems of untruthful and incomplete website filing information, the Ministry of Information Industry launched a website filing information verification work. However, due to the imperfection of the domestic filing system, the relatively insufficient human and material resources, and the current situation of low capital threshold and no technical threshold for opening websites on the Internet, the filing management department cannot cope with the increasing number of new websites. Therefore, the competent authority does not issue filing certificates for many newly opened websites now, but instead focuses on the management, closure, and shutdown of existing websites. However, since the current management of the Ministry of Information Industry on the Internet is limited to the forced regulation of domestic ISP access providers, there is no effective mechanism to impose strong constraints on overseas IDC service providers, which is why many websites purchase space abroad without filing.

3. Affordable price, stable server

The overseas hosting market is mature and standardized, with absolutely top-notch network facilities. Generally speaking, the prices of overseas virtual hosting services are relatively inexpensive. In this aspect, the domestic virtual hosting market cannot compare with overseas virtual hosting providers. Therefore, if you want to reduce your website operation costs, you should indeed consider cost-effective and high-quality overseas virtual hosting.

4. Solve the problem of slow speed for visitors from other countries

Especially for websites targeting foreign customers, they naturally hope that foreign customers can quickly open their websites. However, the development of China's network bandwidth, especially international outbound bandwidth, lags behind, directly resulting in extremely slow access speeds for domestic sites abroad. This is precisely the Achilles' heel of China's foreign trade enterprises. As a result, these foreign trade enterprises find it difficult to retain customers, seriously slowing down the pace of foreign trade enterprises expanding into overseas markets and becoming a bottleneck for the development of foreign trade enterprises! Therefore, it is very necessary for China's foreign trade enterprises to choose overseas hosting.
