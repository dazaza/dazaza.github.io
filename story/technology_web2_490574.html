Speaking of the incident of Sun Nan and Pan Wei... The divorce between Sun Nan and Mai Hongmei is no longer news, but the hidden details between Sun Nan and his new lover Pan Wei are probably little known. A close friend of Sun Nan once revealed to reporters that Sun Nan wasn't initially serious with Pan Wei; it was just a flirtation in his spare time, something he didn't expect to end this way. Sun Nan had never thought about divorce, but Pan Wei was a very cunning woman. She found Mai Hongmei and told her about her affair with Sun Nan. Mai Hongmei was extremely heartbroken at the time. She had been with Sun Nan since she was in her twenties and had taken great care of both the family and Sun Nan. She couldn't understand why this happened, so for a while, she often went out and didn't return home, and her personality changed. Later, they ended up living separately. Mai Hongmei's low spirits played right into Pan Wei's scheme. However, perhaps considering his status, Sun Nan hadn't yet decided to divorce. At this point, the child brought Pan Wei a new opportunity. After being pregnant for some time, Pan Wei told Sun Nan that there was a problem with the fetus's heart. She also said that her parents were constantly pressuring her, leaving her feeling helpless, even contemplating suicide. It was then that Sun Nan gave in. In the end, Pan Wei won this battle. Perhaps, some love requires a certain level of harm to obtain, but ultimately, how much damage does it take to finally prove love?

No matter what, don't use harm to prove love... Modern people seem indifferent to losing love, but from the way they pursue love, one can see that they are less confident about love compared to those holding traditional views on love, and even dare not dream of having lifelong love. When pursuing love, they appear quite pragmatic, like "seizing the moment" and only caring about having once possessed. Actually, this shows more psychological emptiness. So, what kind of psychological quality do we need to be able to navigate modern love freely?...

1. Believe in love, but don't idealize it... Believe that true love exists, but hoping it will surpass everything is unrealistic. Such understanding can make us not idolize love, thus not easily getting hurt or disappointed.

2. Be flexible in love... When committing, you can be selfless, but when results appear, let rationality step in, allowing you to maintain control over the direction of love.

3. Proactive and rational posture... Waiting for love like marking a boat for a lost sword will certainly miss opportunities, but blindly seizing love may harm others and yourself. With a proactive attitude, confidently pursue love without letting improper pursuit ruin it.

4. Have the ability to love... The ability to love includes the capacity to give, understand, forgive, and bear responsibility. Don't expect your partner to shoulder everything; many things still need to be faced alone. Giving is more beneficial to love than taking, making oneself happier. Forgiveness can have surprising effects on love, achieving goals that demanding, criticizing, or retreating cannot reach.

5. Have a bit of psychological flexibility... Enjoy the intimacy of love, accept the distance of your partner, and be able to handle looseness and tightness gracefully. Cherish what you have, and if you lose it, turn quickly without endlessly mourning the past.

6. Understand a bit of love psychology... The state of seeming attainable yet unattainable can easily heat up emotions, using this to enhance the atmosphere of love. When your partner encounters setbacks, they need the most comfort.

7. Have a bit of economic foundation... Although material wealth and love aren't necessarily proportional, having a bit of economic foundation definitely benefits the healthy growth of love. Love disconnected from worldly concerns is hard to last long.
