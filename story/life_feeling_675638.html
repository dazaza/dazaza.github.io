Untitled......

by malayo on 2012-02-25 11:13:21

I don't know how many untitled works have been written, so let's just use "untitled"... I don't know why I feel so sentimental tonight. I don't know what has influenced my mood, allowing sadness to spread recklessly and pain to slowly creep into my heart, overwhelming my whole body. Suddenly, I feel so tired... I take a cigarette, appreciating its loneliness. I don't know when it started, but I've gradually come to like the loneliness of smoking, liking the feeling of being anesthetized, liking the sensation of lightness after finishing a cigarette, liking the bitter taste in my mouth, liking letting sorrow overflow from my heart, liking the feeling that pain cannot be concealed. I take a gentle puff of smoke, wanting my face to become blurred and indifferent, hoarse and desperate, making it impossible for others to detect that I am still me, letting my soul fall alone, without anyone knowing, not even myself. Just like this, quietly, by myself... Give me the time of one song, the time of one song is enough. Death, hahaha, these two extreme words. But I prefer amnesia, I like goldfish, I like their way of life, maybe out of envy. Seven seconds of memory, a lifetime of guardianship, from meeting, getting acquainted, to knowing each other well and falling in love, in the end, we can't escape separation. Clouds gather and disperse, always someone consoles me with the saying that there is no banquet that never ends. Haha, laughable, but brother doesn't believe it's for the next reunion. Once lost, even if regained, will the original feeling still be there? Helpless, closing my eyes, listening to this beautiful song, hearing the breath of the wind, as if yesterday was right before my eyes, never leaving. Yearning escapes, memories are so clear... Standing in troubles and looking up at happiness, perhaps I am now being looked up to by others, but the rule of happiness is different. I strive to pursue my own happiness, but what is my happiness? Laughable, standing in blessing yet unaware of it, troubles, happiness, this world is quite absurd. Yes, suddenly I remember a sentence: Life is like rape. Since there is no power to resist, we might as well enjoy it... The most romantic thing in the world is being with you, a line from a song. I just want to say that early romance may lose you a friend. No matter what, in the end, whoever it is, those who stay with you, should cherish each other for a lifetime...
