Accompany him through the last journey of life with true love.

After graduating from university, Li Yuting went to work for a big company. She was particularly beautiful, 170 centimeters tall, with a head of black hair, eyebrows like a crescent moon, eyes like autumn water, face like peach blossoms, and mouth like a cherry. Her upper body was full, her waist slim, her hips round and her legs long, looking both sexy and dignified.

Suddenly, such a beautiful beauty came to the company, making the unmarried men flustered. Each of them tried their best to improve themselves in order to win the beauty's favor. Some worked overtime, some quit smoking, and some came to work early to clean up. They were all thinking that if they could marry the new girl, it would be worth coming to this world.

One day, it was raining heavily outside, and Li Yuting was walking on the road without rain gear. She happened to bump into an unmarried man from her company - Liu Xiange. Liu Xiange held a low position in the company, just an ordinary clerk. He only had a secondary vocational school diploma, and his appearance was average. Although he also admired the talented and beautiful girl in front of him, he knew he was not worthy of her.

Liu Xiange handed his umbrella to Li Yuting and said, "Take the umbrella, be careful not to catch a cold." After saying that, he was about to walk away in the heavy rain. Li Yuting shouted loudly, "Come under the umbrella quickly! Why didn't you come in when you gave me the umbrella? We can share one umbrella!" However, Liu Xiange said, "I'm a man, I'm not afraid of catching a cold, getting wet in the rain is nothing." Li Yuting said, "You look nervous when you talk, as if you're confessing your love to me." Liu Xiange replied, "In my heart, I've already confessed my love to you, but I felt I wasn't worthy, so I didn't dare to speak."

Li Yuting said, "Since you've already confessed your love to me in your heart, I've decided to marry you." Liu Xiange thought she was joking with him. He thought she knew his position, education, and appearance, and it was impossible. As they talked and walked, they arrived at the company and went to their respective offices.

That night after work, Li Yuting invited Liu Xiange to have dinner. During the meal, they formally discussed the engagement time. Liu Xiange asked, "Have you really decided to marry me?" Li Yuting said, "Yes, unless you don't want to marry me?" Liu Xiange said, "Of course I want to, I'm just afraid you'll regret it. My conditions are too poor."

Li Yuting said, "Conditions are just embellishments in marriage. Character is the soul of marriage. Just by your words on the road today, 'I'm a man,' I can see that you are a very responsible good man. Which girl doesn't want to find a warm and caring man! Don't worry, I won't look down on you. I believe you will definitely have the ability to make me love you wholeheartedly."

After hearing Li Yuting's words, Liu Xiange was as happy as a child. He suddenly threw himself into her arms, hugging the neck of the beautiful girl for a long time without letting go, as if he was afraid the beautiful woman in front of him would run away. Thus, the two of them finalized their lifelong commitment at the dining table.

They set their engagement date on Valentine's Day. They held a grand engagement ceremony at a hotel. At the wedding, someone asked the bride, "Why did you marry your groom?" The bride replied, "Because he was the only one who proposed to me."

Many of the gentlemen present regretted not having dared to propose to Li Yuting and congratulated the rather ordinary groom.

After Li Yuting and Liu Xiange got engaged, every Saturday and Sunday they went to the park or watched movies together. They were like married couples, always going out together. In the evening, the two of them lay on the grass, counting the stars in the sky, enjoying the gentle evening breeze, weaving golden dreams.

One day, Liu Xiange felt feverish, had chills but no shivering, followed by bleeding gums. Li Yuting took her fiancé to the hospital. After a preliminary diagnosis by the doctor, Liu Xiange was found to have leukemia.

The smart and clever Li Yuting knew what it meant for her fiancé to have leukemia, but she did not change her love for him one bit. She took leave from her company and stayed with her fiancé in the hospital. Every day, she went to the pharmacy to buy medicine. Sometimes she had to queue up at the pharmacy entrance. It was the hot summer, and Li Yuting often sweated profusely to buy medicine for her fiancé. After buying the medicine, she sat by her fiancé's side, trying every means to cheer him up.

One day, she said to her fiancé, "With such advanced science and technology now, your illness will soon be cured. You must not lose hope. You must cooperate well with the doctor's treatment. I believe there will be a miracle one day."

Liu Xiange said, "I hope heaven favors me and lets me get better soon because I haven't officially married you yet." Li Yuting said, "After you get better, we will go to the civil affairs office to register our marriage and hold a lively wedding, so all the people who know us can bless us."

Liu Xiange had been in the hospital for almost a month. He felt that his condition had not improved. His beloved fiancée had bloodshot eyes, lost the luster of her former face, became thinner, and looked somewhat weary.

He felt pain in his heart when he saw this. One day, he said to Li Yuting, "It seems I won't get better. You should go back to work tomorrow. I'll call home and ask my sister to take care of me. If there is a suitable young man, you should get along with him and don't miss the opportunity. Although I love you very much, it seems I have shallow blessings and a short life." Li Yuting extended her hand, covered her fiancé's mouth, and said, "Don't say nonsense. I won't leave you. Even if there is such a day, I will accompany you through the last journey. I will never abandon you."

After hearing the words of the woman he loved deeply, Liu Xiange burst into tears and hugged Li Yuting like a child. Li Yuting also cried like a teary person. Fortunately, they were in a single room, so it didn't affect other patients.

Three months passed quickly. In these three months, not only did they spend all of Liu Xiange's savings, but also the 50,000 yuan Li Yuting had saved was completely spent. To treat her fiancé, Li Yuting borrowed money from her colleagues and sold her necklace and ring. The money was still insufficient, so she secretly sold blood three times without her fiancé knowing.

She managed to gather a little money and stayed in the hospital for another month. Under Liu Xiange's repeated requests and with the consent of the hospital doctor, Li Yuting processed his discharge from the hospital. They both knew what kind of result awaited them after discharge. Li Yuting secretly shed tears as she accompanied her fiancé back to his hometown.

After sending Liu Xiange back to his hometown, Li Yuting said, "Stay at home and rest well. I will go back to the company to apply for a long leave, then I will come back to accompany you." He said, "The company business is busy, so you don't need to take leave to accompany me. Anyway, I also have family members to take care of me."

Li Yuting said, "I will be back soon. After I leave, remember to take your medicine on time." The next day, she left her fiancé and returned to the company in the city.

After Li Yuting left, Liu Xiange became silent and irritable, easily losing his temper and throwing things around. His family fully understood his pain and tried every way to guide him, but it didn't have much effect. On the second day after Li Yuting left, Liu Xiange refused to take medicine and wanted to die.

On the fifth day, Li Yuting finally returned from the city. She bought a wheelchair for her fiancé. During the day, Li Yuting pushed him in the wheelchair to see the changes in the outside world and appreciate the beauty of his hometown. While pushing him, she chatted with him, and gradually, Liu Xiange's temper improved, his thoughts of dying disappeared, and he started smiling again.

In the evening, Li Yuting used hot water to wash her fiancé's feet and wipe his body. She gave her fiancé the strength to fight against pain with pure kisses and reduced his suffering with her warm embrace. Liu Xiange said to his fiancée, "Yuting, it is your love that supports me to live on. I don't want to leave you either, nor do I want to leave the world prematurely, but it's my fate."

Despite Li Yuting's sincere efforts to keep her fiancé, the demon of illness did not show any mercy. Cruelly, it deprived Liu Xiange of his right to continue living in the world. He peacefully closed his eyes with reluctance and unwillingness. But he was also fortunate; such a good woman silently stood by him for 180 days, never leaving or abandoning him, warming him with sincerity and accompanying him through the last journey of life with true love.
