High-quality home life lies in maintenance.

by longersing on 2009-09-16 15:42:28

A high-quality home life is increasingly desired by urban dwellers. The elegance and comfort of a home environment, along with the convenience and speed of its maintenance, are becoming indispensable parts of a high-quality life. However, complicated work and limited time and energy make office workers struggle to balance everything. High-end furniture purchased at great expense and carefully decorated wooden floors lose their luster over time due to neglect, and may even crack from moisture, leading to waste and a significant reduction in living standards. So, what do we need to do?

Before maintenance, prepare carefully

Many traditional home care products on the market today are time-consuming and labor-intensive, and do not achieve ideal results. For example, some furniture care agents must first repeatedly clean the furniture before use, but some corners and stubborn stains can only be left untreated. Some products can make the surface of the furniture shine, but do not have a care function and may even corrode the material. Liquid floor wax has a relatively viscous texture and must be applied repeatedly during use, which not only causes waste but also easily leads to unevenness and spots. Traditional solid floor wax is even more inconvenient to use and requires a dedicated waxing machine for polishing. Moreover, well-waxed floors are prone to dust accumulation and slipping, creating safety hazards.

Care meticulously and frequently

Furniture care essential oil and wooden floor care wax effectively solve the above problems. Currently, the market mainly offers some care essential oil products specifically designed for mid-to-high-end solid wood furniture, suitable for various wooden surfaces such as redwood, teak, oak, and walnut. These products integrate cleaning, shining, and care into one. The natural orange oil they contain is absorbed by the wooden fibers, effectively nourishing the wood and locking in moisture to prevent cracking and deformation. Many new wooden floor care wax products are also suitable for laminate or solid wood floors. Simply dip a sponge with a little water into an appropriate amount of liquid wax and evenly brush it along the grain direction of the floor. After drying, a protective film naturally forms on the surface of the floor, effectively sealing the gaps between the boards, preventing water infiltration, and stopping the floor from warping due to moisture. Regular maintenance with floor care wax can effectively prevent wear and tear on the floor surface caused by long-term use, extending the life of the floor.

Choose professional maintenance services

In many home markets, you can often see free maintenance seminars and promotional activities. Some maintenance product companies even send specialized staff every weekend to provide free door-to-door services to residents.

Original wooden floors cost nearly 200 yuan per square meter, so regular maintenance is very important. However, if using floor wax, each application requires repeated wiping several times to ensure even coating, taking at least two and a half hours for the entire room, which is both tiring and troublesome. In contrast, professional maintenance personnel can apply floor wax to the master bedroom in just ten minutes. After cleaning and maintenance, the wooden floor shines like it was freshly varnished. Reports say that without counting the time spent on furniture care, applying floor wax to a house with over 50 square meters of wooden floors (total building area of 105 square meters) takes only about half an hour.
