The moon is like a hook, the gentle wind blows over the tall building, petals fall all over the mountain, but no leaf is seen to be thin.

Listening to the sound in the wind as it slowly spreads and then fades away far away, like a silent prayer in my heart, that indescribable shadow swirls in my thoughts, just like this scenery full of mountains disordered my mood, and this natural landscape moves freely and gracefully forming a habit of cycle, even if it's a scene of decay and prosperity on its last breath, it has already been predetermined as the next life, and all this seems like a reminder and hint from the world, life only passes once, and how we walk through it is not entirely up to us.

Seeking the realm of quiet rain in an empty mountain, tracing the Zen sounds of temples, yet still unable to escape worldly emotions.

Walking thousands of miles away, there are still countless more thousand-mile roads waiting afar. Hiding an unparalleled skill in one’s heart, there will still be higher skills hidden among the secular world yet to be revealed. Listening to ten thousand raindrops in the ear, there are still more ten thousand raindrops lingering outside the ear. If you want the lake water in your heart to be calm without ripples, what you need is not practice, but a touch of serene self-contentment immersed in the heart.

The Way is not the Way, thievery also has its way, still it is the Way, the road of humanity follows the Way.

In this vast world, each person is different. Who can reject wealth and power from afar, and ignore the seven emotions and six desires of humanity, living without partaking in worldly affairs is merely legend.

Chasing the season for admiring flowers in full bloom, using my heart to feel the flowing sounds of nature, like streams in the fields flowing over rice paddies or the reflection in a puddle lasting for how long, and how many people with similar hearts appreciate the loneliness and joy within? Isn't it just a fleeting and hasty withering dryness?

How much time does life have? When do we need to revel in joy? Timely enjoyment of life is not a mistake.

Using a mirror to adjust one's own spiritual world, external makeup is merely a form of action without substance, success outwardly cannot cover the barrenness and desolation in the heart, success inwardly won't let desolation show outwardly in the inner essence, integrity and character don't rely on the reflection of the mirror, but on the refraction of the heart.

Beautiful scenery often comes from distant deep mountains and old forests or uninhabited pure lands, the feeling of freshness and cleanliness from the air to every green leaf brings sighs and reluctant departures in silence. These are hurried sighs of praise, inevitably requiring a brief stay before returning, not to the city or densely populated urban artificial gardens and chaotic sculptures. The direction of human hearts isn't the ultimate attraction, the cohesion of groups is the attraction between groups, adapting for survival rather than pursuing the loneliness and solitude of uninhabited areas for one's survival purpose.

Desire remains desire, understanding remains understanding, and the bearer remains the bearer.

Letting others understand you is a mistake, and trying to understand others is also a mistake.

Looking at people through the air is not as good as looking through glass, which is not as comfortable as looking through walls; people should at least be separated by skin. Don't see through someone's mind completely, the pain of being utterly exposed is unbearable, the bloody unspeakable secrets, whether dissecting oneself or others, it is an unforgivable sin that cannot be relieved.
