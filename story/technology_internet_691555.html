How to Get Rid of Elephant Legs After Childbirth

by ciaifuke on 2012-03-05 16:38:25

Many women develop "elephant legs" after childbirth, while others become slim. So, how can you get rid of those dreaded elephant legs after giving birth? Thick calves can make skirts or boots look unflattering. How can we achieve a more balanced calf line?

Let's take a look at different ways to slim down various types of thick calves:

1. Slimming Plan for Fat Legs:

- Judging Criteria: Pinch your leg and you can easily grab a lump of fat. The legs appear chubby and are disproportionate to the actual body weight.

- Slimming Key Points: Besides exercise, avoiding sugary and oily foods is very effective. Eat more low-calorie seafood and vegetables, reduce snacking, and do more aerobic exercises like running, swimming, or cycling to eliminate excess fat from the legs.

Slimming Method One: Postpartum Leg Exercises

Start postpartum leg exercises from the fifth day after delivery until the end of the month. Appropriate leg movements can help strengthen leg muscles and improve the return flow of venous blood in the lower limbs. Sit on the ground with both legs stretched out and together, back straight, arms extended behind you, fingers spread supporting the ground. Inhale as you point your toes upward, exhale as you stretch them out. Then lie on your back, legs stretched slightly apart, arms by your sides. Inhale as you bend and stretch your left leg towards your chest with the toes pointed; exhale as you straighten your left leg, forming a right angle with your upper body and pointing your toes upward. Alternate with both legs.

These exercises are suitable for mothers who had normal deliveries. Since most new mothers tend to be weak, it's important to exercise according to your specific situation, without rushing things. Each set should last two to three minutes, twice a day, morning and evening. Pay special attention to coordinating your breathing with the movements. After the first month, you can start exercising all muscle groups to restore strength and elasticity in your thigh muscles. Suitable exercises include slow jogging, leg stretching exercises, and swimming.

Slimming Method Two: Gua Sha (Scraping) Leg Reduction

Every night after bathing, apply baby oil or olive oil on your calves for lubrication. Use a gua sha board (or the back of a wooden comb, a smooth spoon, etc.) to scrape your calves vigorously from the ankles up to the knees. Do this 100 times on each leg.

It’s best to perform this after bathing. Drink a little warm water afterward. If you can also do some air bicycle or scissor leg exercises (lying down, lifting your legs, extending them, opening them as wide as possible, pausing for a few seconds, then closing them like scissors), it will enhance the results. If you're not aiming for overall weight loss, there's no need for a special diet; just avoid greasy and high-sugar foods. Reducing dinner portions can speed up the slimming process.

Effect: Generally, people notice a significant reduction in leg circumference after consistently practicing for two weeks to one month.

Solution Three: Liposuction on Legs

To address thick calves caused by fat accumulation, you could consider leg liposuction, though it might be painful.

Typically, a small incision of about 0.5 cm is made either inside the ankle or at the back of the knee. Swelling anesthesia is administered, and ultrasound waves are used externally to break down and dissolve fat cells, which are then suctioned out using negative pressure. The duration of the surgery depends on the amount of fat in the calves, usually taking around 1.5 hours for both calves combined.

This method physically removes fat cells from the body, significantly reducing their number compared to methods like exercise, dieting, or medication. Therefore, it prevents rebound weight gain after the procedure.

Cost is approximately between 3000-4000 RMB.

2. Slimming Plan for Varicose Veins and "Edema" Legs:

- Judging Criteria: Press your calf muscle with your hand; if the skin takes a long time to bounce back, it falls under this category.

- Slimming Key Points: Avoid fast food, vacuum-packed food, pastries, and salty snacks. Don't drink carbonated beverages like cola or Sprite. Avoid staying in one position for over an hour. Doing simple muscle-strengthening exercises can improve swelling.

Solution One: 10-Minute Quick Slimming Method

Thick calves are often due to swelling. To perfect your leg shape quickly, massage your legs 10 minutes before a date. After massaging, allow your body to expel accumulated waste, then use a slimming cream. Elevate your legs for a short rest, which can reduce the size by 2-3 cm.

1. Alternate hand massages. Place both palms around your calf and alternately move them upwards. Do this 10 times.

2. Twist and knead with both hands. Grip your calf muscle tightly with both palms and twist and knead until the entire calf feels warm.

Solution Two: High-Leg Elevation Method

After sitting for a long time, place your legs higher than your heart. Before sleeping, you can rest your legs against a wall or elevate them 20-30 cm when sleeping. Lie on your side, propping your upper body with your hand. Bend one leg and keep the other straight. Then repeatedly lift the bent leg 10 times. Turn to the other side and repeat with the other leg.

Solution Three: Patting and Massage Method

Pat and massage along the lymph nodes of the leg to improve circulation and lymphatic drainage. Moderate massage can alleviate heaviness and swelling in the legs.

Start patting rhythmically from the ankle to the back of the knee with both hands. After finishing the right leg, do the same for the left leg.

If you choose this method, you should massage at least twice a week for an hour or more to see results. You can do this while watching TV in the evening.
