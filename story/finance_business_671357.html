The netizens of the "Public Network Board Game Group" can't help but feel lonely at home, and the price of underfloor heating nets is also a topic they discuss.

by rw3951o28 on 2012-02-21 21:03:01

After the first heavy snowfall of winter, the weather in Jinan finally warmed up on Sunday. The online friends of "Mass Network Board Game Group" naturally couldn't bear the loneliness at home anymore, and they were all busy calling their friends to come to "Let's Gou" for a lively and festive "Old and New Friends Great Reunion". Among the twenty-five netizens, Xia Yifan, who is only five years old, was particularly noticeable. Don't look down on him because of his young age; he is actually an expert at "Sanguosha"! Three netizens arrived an hour early at the scene and started small-scale PKs - one of them was playing chess with the staff, while the other two were playing "Sanguosha 1V1". Around half past two, the registered netizens had already checked in and taken their seats. Some came alone to spend their weekend time, some just finished shopping with their lovers and entered carrying large bags and small packages to play a hand of cards and rest their feet, while others were well-prepared for "real person combat" with their close-knit group of friends... Little Yifan came specially accompanied by his parents to participate in the event and improve his skills. It was learned that Yifan's mother is a loyal netizen of Mass Network. She said: "Yifan's father taught him how to play Sanguosha, and after he learned it, he taught me. Now I have to call him 'Master'. Through this game, our Yifan has learned a lot about the characters and history of the Three Kingdoms era. The books and DVDs of 'Romance of the Three Kingdoms' at our home are piled up in a box!" A table of girls came together to play cards. Upon inquiry, it was learned that they are all postgraduate students studying at Shandong Normal University. Miss Chang told the reporter: "We are sisters from the same dormitory, but we are usually busy with classes and projects, so we don't have much time to hang out together. When we saw that the event location was near our school, we decided to come and play a hand of cards and relax." It was learned that the "Board Game Group" of Mass Network has successfully held four events, each of which attracted many netizens to sign up enthusiastically. A total of twenty-five netizens participated in this event, ranging in age from 5 to 50 years old, with professions including kindergarten children, postgraduate students, office workers, and freelancers. Although the content of the board game activity mainly focuses on "Gou Ji," which is beloved by people in Shandong, games like "Sanguosha" and "Go" are also available. Netizens only need to register an account on the Mass Forum, sign up for the event, and they can immediately participate in this offline game for free, with no set end time, unlimited soft drinks, and fruit platters and new decks of cards provided by the store. This event is held every other week in Jinan, aiming to create a healthy and fashionable leisure and social platform for board game enthusiasts. All netizens are welcome to pay attention and participate. "Where should I go when I'm free on weekends?" The "Mass Network Board Game Group" will surely make your trip worthwhile.
