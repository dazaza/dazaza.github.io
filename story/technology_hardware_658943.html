Individual cool style 2012 fashion clothes

by xuezhen5556v on 2012-02-04 18:45:16

Individuality and coolness in the 2012 fashion scene - the favorite way for stylish women to dress up. As the weather turns slightly cooler, it's time for a light jacket to make an appearance. Tight pants with an ink painting style exude classical charm, paired with a clean white long T-shirt showcasing elegant girlish flair. This season’s hot fashion striped Roman high heels add points to your overall sense of fashion. The layered look with varying lengths has a very Japanese feel; the strong black and white combination adds dynamism, and ankle-high strapped buttoned high heels can increase your stylish edge. Add a purple skull-patterned scarf as an accent, and you're all set. Dressing: Standard suits are too conservative; a tight-fitting dress + uniform is the best choice, neither losing grandeur nor being too stiff. Metallic high heels become the highlight, instantly enhancing your sense of fashion. A simple T-shirt + seven-eighths distressed jeans looks cool, simple, and very British. Silver high heels elongate the leg lines, fully displaying a sexy S-shaped figure. Fashion clothing pictures: Oversized cotton batwing sleeve T-shirts paired with uniquely designed black cotton tight pants give a very comfortable feeling. A side-tied ponytail looks playful and cute, radiating youthfulness. Canvas shoes, tight pants, loose and simple oversized T-shirts are the best choices for outings, offering freedom and comfort. Add a short outerwear to guard against evening temperature drops, and a large handbag for storing all sorts of items. Military boots, vests, playing with a neutral style. The light brown vest worn over a shirt enriches the layers of the overall outfit, breaking the monotony of a single white shirt, and the dashing brown short boots are full of neutral flavor.
