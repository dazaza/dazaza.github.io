Huasheng Online - Hunan City Newspaper, December 14th: Today, the Litan detachment of Hunan Expressway Traffic Police announced a traffic violation that "broke the speed record of Hunan expressways." A BMW car with the license plate number Xiang B5055B was captured by a mobile speed detector traveling at a speed of 244 kilometers per hour when passing through the 998-kilometer section of the Shanghai-Kunming Expressway in Hunan.

Linked News: In recent years, the Litan detachment of Hunan Expressway Traffic Police has investigated multiple cases of speeding violations exceeding 200 kilometers per hour. Reporter Wang Tiewei, Correspondents Xian Ying and Cui Dan. The high-speed traffic police urge the vehicle that set a new speeding record in Hubei to voluntarily contact the Litan detachment and promptly return to the high-speed traffic police department for education and punishment.

This speeding violation occurred at the 998-kilometer mark on the Shanghai-Kunming Expressway from east to west. Monitoring showed that on the night of November 2nd at 9:09 PM, a gray BMW large car reached a speed of 244 kilometers per hour while driving through the monitored section. The driver was a young woman. After confirming that the instrument equipment was operating normally, the police were concerned about such reckless driving behavior. Upon checking the car's violation records, the police found that the car had multiple unresolved traffic violations. When the officer attempted to contact the car owner, the phone was out of service.
