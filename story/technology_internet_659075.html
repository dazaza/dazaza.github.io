Fang: You must have bribed her with a tip!

Fang: Your father knows the proctor, don't think I'm unaware of that!

A: When my father knew him, he was still working as a janitor at school!

Fang: I don't care, anyway, he knows him!

Fang: Why is the process on this scratch paper so neat! I couldn't write such a clean draft!

Fang: I don't believe it! Go get your parents!!! I need to have a good talk with them!
