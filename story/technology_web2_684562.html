The IT company of Jidao Xiuzhen formulates a weight loss plan for employees, and those who successfully lose weight will be rewarded 800 yuan per month.

by sznstejx3q3 on 2012-03-01 08:22:18

Employees actively exercise, weight loss leaderboard

Standing while working, eating vegetarian at noon, successful weight loss earns 800 yuan monthly reward

"2 months without losing weight, no one will chase you in March, fat accumulation in April, relationship blows up in May... This is a popular joke on Weibo. The winter of 'gaining weight' is about to pass, and losing weight has once again become a hot topic among white-collar workers.

Personal weight loss is no longer rare, have you heard of companies setting employee collective weight loss plans? In early February, to motivate employees who gained weight during the Spring Festival to lose weight, an internet company in Chengdu set a new rule: employees need to stand for 30 minutes in the morning and afternoon while working, with a maximum bonus of 400 yuan for the employee who loses the most weight...

The lunch for the little fatties is all vegetables...

At 10:40 am yesterday, I came to Chengdu Yongmeng Software Development Co., Ltd. located in Huaxi Meilu. The scene was quite astonishing: all employees were working standing in front of their desks, some stretching telephone lines talking, others raising their mice using computers...

This was exactly the time the company stipulated for "standing while working": from 10:30 am to 11:00 am, and from 3:30 pm to 4:00 pm. "Neck twist, butt twist." Miss Peng hummed the "Health Song" while moving her body. Although slim, she still had to "stand as punishment", "at least it can relieve cervical and lumbar pain."

Compared to Miss Peng, overweight Ah Jian didn't feel comfortable standing. Before half an hour was up, sweat had already seeped out on his forehead. But his hardship didn't stop there. At 12:00 pm, it was the company's lunch time, and the canteen aunt specially prepared a weight-loss vegetarian meal for the fat people who wanted to lose weight: vegetable soup, cold mixed cucumber, stir-fried bitter melon...

Looking at his own lunch, then looking at the meat on the plates of his thin colleagues, Ah Jian didn't even want to eat with them during meals - it was too tempting. "Actually, I could choose not to eat the vegetarian meal, but since I voluntarily signed up for the weight loss program, it's equivalent to committing to eating vegetarian at noon." Ah Jian laughed and said, "Otherwise, I would be looked down upon."

Losing weight for a month can earn up to 800 yuan...

In a prominent position in the office corridor, there is a "weight loss leaderboard". Besides providing a slightly chubby or chubby photo, each person on the list also shares a few sentences of "weight loss insights". Employees wrote on it: "My figure is finally starting to standardize... Thank everyone for not letting me eat meat these two weeks!" "For the sake of the bonus, I've really gone all out"...

The top of the list is employee Chen Yuan, who used to be 30% overweight. "I knew being too fat wasn't healthy," Chen Yuan said, "but I just couldn't make up my mind to do this thing." However, after losing 5 pounds in two weeks, Chen Yuan "finally started to feel what it's like to be light as a bird".

The "weight loss policies" such as standing for an hour, weight loss vegetarian meals, and the weight loss leaderboard will continue until the end of March, supervised by the company CEO throughout. During this period, participants will weigh themselves in the office every week, recorded by the administrative supervisor; the "weight loss leaderboard" will be updated every two weeks. Based on the number of pounds lost every two weeks, the top 4 employees on the list will receive weight loss bonuses ranging from 100 to 400 yuan. "Even if they don't make it onto the weight loss leaderboard, all employees participating in the collective weight loss plan will receive rewards." The company head said, however, the prerequisite for receiving rewards is that they must complete the entire plan.

Boss, how much do you want to attend weddings?

Why create a "collective weight loss plan"?

The company head told reporters: the company currently mainly operates the largest free domestic marriage and dating website, advocating energetic and fashionable employee images; and in the spring of each year, the website will have many outdoor dating activities. Considering that there are more singles among the employees, they hope they will have better images and be more confident in making friends.

In addition, standing for an hour every day is also aimed at helping employees stay away from office syndromes such as lumbar diseases.

Since the participation is voluntary, most employees expressed support for this plan. According to company statistics: 60% of employees voluntarily participated in the collective weight loss plan, including senior management (market director). Among the employees participating in the weight loss, more than half are female employees who want a better and slimmer figure. The rest are mostly truly overweight people, and a very small number are those who are not fat but participate in the weight loss for the bonus.
