Whitening: Nine Key Points

by wswen356 on 2012-02-26 10:11:48

Whitening is a permanent topic for MM. Every year, they try to whiten their skin, but every year it still gets dark. Where is the problem? Are there any points we haven't done or haven't done well? Take a look at the article that the editor brings you today, and maybe you will know the reason why your whitening has failed. Pay attention.

Every year, various methods of whitening are tried, but they all boil down to preventing ultraviolet rays from outside and resisting melanin from inside. This year's whitening theme also follows the trend of returning to the basics, re-emphasizing the whitening effect of vitamin C. Perhaps after last year's wave of analyzing ultraviolet rays and developing new products, the merchants realized that instead of continuously developing new products, it's better to use what we already have. As a result, various kinds of vitamin C products have emerged accordingly.

The main function of vitamin C is to inhibit the production of melanin and prevent skin damage caused by pollution and ultraviolet rays, thereby making the skin healthy and white. Most of the products launched by major skincare brands use plant-derived vitamin C. The benefits are self-evident; applying it directly on the skin is safer since it comes from natural sources.

Moreover, vitamin C extracted from plants is more stable, which helps it to work effectively on the skin for a longer period. Some products claim that pure vitamin C is the most effective, and use capsule technology to deliver concentrated vitamin C liquid for whitening purposes.

Another major feature of this year's whitening products is that many famous brands have launched complete sets of skin whitening care products. From makeup removal, cleaning, hydration, sun protection, deep repair to overnight nourishment, they are all provided in series, aiming to achieve a chain effect and carry out a long-term and comprehensive whitening project.

Speaking of "all-round", people now increasingly realize that only when the body and mind are in harmony can the skin truly reach health and whiteness from the inside out. In this regard, COSMO has the following suggestions:

1. Sunscreen is the first priority. Prevent UV damage to the skin and inhibit melanin.

2. Whitening should be started early. Don't wait until the peak of summer to take measures.

3. The intake of vitamins is crucial for the skin. Eat more fruits and vegetables to replenish the skin's vitality.

4. You might as well try the products of new technology research and development results, but be sure to choose reputable brands and pay attention to testing before use.

5. Pay attention to maintaining the air quality of the environment.

6. Whitening should be taken care of both day and night.

7. Drink more water, ensure sleep, and exercise appropriately - we don't need to tell you that, right?

8. Keep a happy mood.

9. Firmly believe in the goal of obtaining white skin. The power of intention cannot be underestimated.
