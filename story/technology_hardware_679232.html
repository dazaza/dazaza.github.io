Meanwhile, the company has strong manufacturing capabilities, providing reliable assurance for product quality and supply capacity. JDscan adopts the most advanced international 3D optical measurement technology, reaching the technical level of the latest foreign products. Compared to the traditional Gray code plus phase shift method, it offers higher measurement accuracy, larger single measurement area (from 50mm to 3m), strong anti-interference capability, and less influence by the brightness of the surface of the measured workpiece. The non-contact underwear scanner, mold cup scanner, and bra cup scanner use advanced structured light encoding technology, avoiding contact with the object surface, allowing the measurement of soft objects without any impact on the operator or the measured object. Fully automatic intelligent splicing, leading internationally in precision, seamless splicing, equipped with a global error control system; it can also be used in conjunction with the JDSACN-PH industrial photogrammetry system to measure extremely large objects with even higher precision. The scanning speed is fast, completing measurements within 4 seconds for a single area from 1200mm to 30mm, obtaining millions of scan points, with very regular measurement point data and basically no noise points in the measurement data. Low requirements for the measurement environment, can work in normal indoor environments or outdoors without a darkroom. Rich data interfaces: supports ASC, IGES, OBJ, STL, VRML, DXF, MDL, SMF data formats, compatible with Pro/E, UG, Imageware, Geomagic, AutoCAD, and various other design software interfaces.
