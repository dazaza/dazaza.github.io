We all know this truth: purchasing is a strategic link for a business. Practice has further proven that it is difficult to achieve a percentage point of profit margin in the sales环节, but relatively easier in the purchasing环节 with larger profit space. Therefore, the level of procurement and supply chain management can become the "cradle" of corporate profits, or it can also become the "graveyard" of corporate profits. After all, placing orders online is both convenient and fast. And these types of purchasers almost cover all industries in society, including clothing, instrumentation, pharmaceuticals, chemicals, etc., online procurement can be said to be omnipresent.

In recent years, we have seen such information on the internet: a company in Jinan, Shandong saves 8 million yuan annually through online procurement; a company in Anhui saves 5 million yuan annually through online procurement... However, from this, we can discover that companies' realization of online procurement as a new model has gradually matured. Some predict that in the next 10 years, one third of international trade will be conducted through the Internet. Companies like Ford and Daimler in the U.S. conduct all their annual $250 billion worth of parts procurement online. Domestic famous enterprises such as Haier, China National Petroleum Corporation, Sinopec, etc., are implementing online procurement and have achieved significant economic benefits. Experts indicate that companies conducting online procurement can effectively reduce costs.

Compared to traditional enterprise product procurement of previous years, today's enterprises tend to directly procure on specialized e-commerce platforms. Where there is demand, there is market, thus giving rise to a large number of comprehensive, professional, authoritative supply marketing platforms.
