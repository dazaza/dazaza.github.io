See beggar analysis market

by tuoqing1188 on 2011-08-03 12:44:09

Look at the beggar analyze the market, Google optimization

A blog post, laugh and think about it.

I came out from Maoye after buying a pair of Levi's, waiting for a friend at the door. A professional beggar found me and stopped in front of me very professionally. This stop led to a story that deeply shocked me, just like attending a vivid market research case class. In order to be faithful to the original meaning of this beggar, I repeated his original words as much as possible according to my memory.

"Sir... please help, give me something." I was bored so I found a coin in my pocket and threw it to him and started talking with him.

The beggar was talkative. "... I only beg around Huaqiangbei. Do you know? I saw you at a glance. Buying Levi's at Maoye means you are willing to spend money..."

"Oh? You know quite a lot!" I was surprised.

"As a beggar, I also use scientific methods," he said.

I was stunned and asked curiously, "What kind of scientific method?"

"Take a look first, what's different between me and other beggars?" I carefully observed him, his hair was messy, his clothes were torn, his hands were thin, but they were not dirty.

He interrupted my thoughts and said, "People are very repulsed by beggars, but I believe you don't feel that way about me, and I can see that. This is how I'm different from other beggars."

I nodded in agreement, indeed I didn't feel repulsed, otherwise how could I start a conversation with a beggar.

"I understand SWOT analysis, strengths, weaknesses, opportunities, and threats. For my competitors, my advantage is that I am not disgusting. Opportunities and threats are external factors, such as Shenzhen having a large population and Shenzhen will soon renovate its appearance."

"I have done precise calculations. There are tens of thousands of people here every day, more poor people, even more rich people. Theoretically speaking, if I ask each person for one yuan every day, then I can earn 300,000 yuan per month. But not everyone will give, and I can't ask so many people every day. So, I need to analyze who are the target customers and who are the potential customers." He continued after moistening his throat, "In the Huaqiangbei area, my target customers account for 30% of the total pedestrian traffic, with a success rate of 70%. Potential customers account for 20%, with a success rate of 50%; the remaining 50%, I choose to abandon because I don't have enough time to take chances on them."

"So how do you define your customers?" I followed up.

"First, target customers. Like you young sir, with an economic foundation, generous. Also, couples also belong to my target customers, as they will generously give alms to avoid losing face in front of the opposite sex. Second, I consider beautiful girls alone as potential customers, because they fear entanglement, so most of them would rather spend money to avoid trouble. These two groups, their age is controlled between 20-30 years old. Too young, no economic foundation; too old, probably married, financial power is in the wife's hands. These people, there's no hope, they might even ask me for money."

"How much can you beg for each day?" I continued to ask.

"From Monday to Friday, business is a bit worse, about 200 yuan. On weekends, I can even get four or five hundred yuan."

"So much?"

Seeing my doubt, he gave me an account. "Like you, I work 8 hours a day, from 11 in the morning to 7 in the evening, working normally on weekends. It takes me about 5 seconds to beg once, deducting the time spent walking back and forth and searching for targets, I can beg once every minute and get one yuan, which is 480 yuan in 8 hours, then multiply by the success rate of 60% [(70%+50%)÷2], which is nearly 300 yuan."

"Absolutely cannot chase customers all over the street. If begging fails, I will never cling stubbornly. Because if he was willing to give money, he would have given it already, so even if I shamelessly cling, the chance of success is still very small. Cannot waste limited time on customers who have no intention of giving alms, better to find the next target instead."

Impressive! This beggar really defies appearances, he sounds more like a seasoned marketing director.

"Go on." I became more interested, it seems I can learn something new today.

"Some people say being a beggar is eating by luck, I don't agree. Let me give you an example, in front of Women's World, there is a handsome boy and a pretty girl, who would you beg from?"

I thought about it and said I don't know.

"You should go to the boy. With a beautiful girl beside him, he wouldn't want to lose face by not giving. But if you went to the girl, she could easily pretend to be afraid and run away."

"Let me give you another example. That day at Coco Park entrance, there was a young girl holding a shopping bag, just finished shopping; there was also a couple eating ice cream; the third one was a well-dressed young man holding a laptop bag. I observe a person within three seconds, I walked straight to the girl without hesitation. The girl took out two coins from her bag and threw them to me, and wondered why I only begged from her. I answered that the couple was eating, it's inconvenient to take out money; that man is a high-level white-collar worker, possibly doesn't have loose change; you just came out from the supermarket, you must have loose change."

That makes sense! I found it more and more interesting.

"So I say, knowledge determines everything!" I've heard this sentence from more than a dozen presidents, it's the first time I hear it from a beggar.

"One must use scientific methods to beg. Lying on the overpass all day, how can you get money? People crossing the overpass are all in a hurry, who has nothing to do and climb up and down for fun, it's so tiring. One must arm oneself with knowledge, learning knowledge can make a person very smart, a smart person continuously learns knowledge and can become talented. What does the 21st century need the most? It's talent."

"Once, someone gave me 50 yuan, asking me to shout 'An Hong, I miss you' downstairs 100 times. I calculated, shouting once costs 5 seconds, equivalent to the time I spend begging once, the reward is only 0.5 yuan, so I refused him."

"In Shenzhen, generally a beggar can beg for a thousand or two yuan per month. When lucky, it's about two thousand yuan. Among all ten thousand beggars in Shenzhen, only ten beggars can beg more than ten thousand yuan per month. I am one of these rare ones.

And it's very stable, basically no big fluctuations."

Too impressive! I admire this beggar more and more.

"I often say I am a happy beggar. Other beggars say it's because I beg a lot of money, so I am happy. I tell them, you're wrong. It's because I have a happy and positive attitude, so I can beg more money."

How well said!

"Begging is my job, one must appreciate the joy brought by work. On rainy days when there are fewer people, other beggars complain or sleep. Don't be like that, take the time to enjoy the beauty of this city. After work in the evening, take your wife and children to stroll and play and enjoy the night view, it's a blissful family of three, worth living a lifetime. If you encounter a colleague, sometimes I throw a coin too, seeing them happily thank and walk away, it's like seeing my own shadow."

"Do you have a wife and children?" I couldn't help but exclaim loudly, drawing the attention of passers-by.

"My wife is a housewife, my child is in elementary school. I mortgaged a house in Futian District, ten-year installment, six years left to pay off. I need to work hard to earn money to support my son to study marketing in university, then carry on the family business and become a more outstanding beggar than me."

"I worked as a market planner in Microsoft China Greater Region five years ago, promoted to marketing manager two years ago, monthly salary of 5,000 yuan. At that time, I mortgaged a Samsung notebook computer costing more than 10,000 yuan, paying 2,000 yuan per month, struggling to death. Later, I realized that I would never make any progress this way, so I resigned and turned to begging, I want to be a high-quality beggar."

After listening, I excitedly said, "Are you interested in taking me as your apprentice?"
