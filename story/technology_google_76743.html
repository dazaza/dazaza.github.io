Google Maps recruits volunteers with a $100 reward

by fantexi on 2008-07-07 22:18:48

For users in the US and Canada, Google is launching a volunteer survey campaign. Users who help Google complete the survey report have the chance to earn a $100 reward.

Participating in this activity may require you to visit a Google office (if you can), allow a Google representative to visit you, or fill out an online survey form via the web. Google states that suitable participants can earn up to a $100 reward.

Unlike regular surveys, this one is relatively short. Google might ask users about their usage of software products, business habits, and more. The survey may take between 10 to 20 minutes. Finally, if Google deems the survey valuable, the user will receive their reward.

Readers abroad who are interested can visit https://survey.google.com/wix/p0822776.aspx?referral_code=MAPS1 to fill out the application form.
