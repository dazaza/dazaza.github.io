Car Beauty and Maintenance: Maintenance of Car Exhaust Pipes

The car exhaust pipe, as is well known, is an important component of a car. The car exhaust pipe, also known as the exhaust hose, is installed between the engine exhaust manifold and the muffler in the exhaust system, connecting the entire exhaust system. It plays a role in reducing vibration and noise and extending the life of the exhaust muffler system. The exhaust pipe requires more attention relatively speaking. For example, many people may notice the muffler changing color. Moreover, when driving through deep water, if water enters the exhaust pipe and the engine happens to stall, this kind of damage can be fatal for the car. Therefore, the maintenance of the exhaust pipe is very important in car maintenance.

Firstly, one should pay attention to preventing water from entering the inside of the muffler. When driving through deep water or washing the car, one should prevent water from entering the inside of the muffler. If water accidentally enters, the engine should be started and idled for at least 10 minutes to try to drain out the accumulated water. Also, rust-proof oil should be applied inside the muffler. Here, let me emphasize the specific method of applying oil:

First, remove the muffler and block the drainage hole at the lowest position of the muffler's middle section. Then add rust-proof oil from where the muffler connects with the engine. Shake the muffler so that the rust-proof oil evenly enters the cylinder. Afterward, reinstall the muffler. As the temperature of the muffler gradually rises, the heated rust-proof oil will evaporate, and blue smoke will come out from the tail of the muffler. However, this blue smoke will disappear after driving approximately 20 kilometers. After the blue smoke disappears, remove the plug from the drainage hole. Here, it is also recommended to perform an oil maintenance once after the new car gets its license plate and then every six months thereafter.

Secondly, there is the issue of the muffler changing color which many people are concerned about. When the car encounters difficulty starting, first close the choke valve to start the engine and remember to open the choke valve promptly. Absolutely do not drive for a long time at high speed or in low gear with the choke valve closed, as this will harm both the engine and the muffler. Secondly, avoid keeping the engine revving high while stationary for a long time because when the surface of the muffler has oil stains, starting the car will cause its surface color to turn yellow. Additionally, when the surface of the muffler is covered with a large amount of dirt or mud, it must be cleaned promptly to avoid affecting its heat dissipation function.

Although the exhaust pipe is not a precision part inside the car and is exposed externally, many people think it must be a durable component. Therefore, even if it is full of dirt, it is considered normal. However, a crucial point is that the exhaust pipe connects external substances with the inside of the car, and its main function is to expel waste gases from the car. But improper driving can allow harmful substances detrimental to car beauty to enter the car interior through the exhaust pipe. Therefore, maintaining the exhaust pipe means maintaining the internal parts of the car in various senses.
