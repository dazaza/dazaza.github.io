0323 Live Report: Incomplete Demolition Leaves Troubles, Neighbors Get Shocked

by wffnedv9f on 2010-01-25 05:23:39

[Voiceover] Currently, many areas in the city are undergoing intensive demolition work. However, due to varying progress rates of the demolition projects, safety issues at some construction sites deserve our attention. Yesterday afternoon, Mr. Cao from Xiaohaitan Alley No. 6, whose home was about to be demolished, encountered a nerve-wracking incident.

[Audio Clip] When the reporter arrived at the scene, we saw that the road in front of Mr. Cao's house was completely blocked by fallen walls, and cracks had appeared on the walls inside his home. The onlookers were discussing the situation.

[同期声] Mr. Cao: It almost hit a child, and my father almost tripped today (Bystander: Now the issue is the safety of the housing). Yes.

[Audio Clip] It was learned that Mr. Cao's neighbor had already moved according to the plan, and most of their house had been demolished. Unexpectedly, among the remaining ruins, some old steel attracted the interest of scrap collectors. At the time of the incident, his father was taking a nap at home when he suddenly heard a "bang." The elderly man was so frightened that he got up from bed immediately. Upon going outside, he discovered that a garbage collector was using a hammer to break down a wall adjacent to his house for the rebar inside. After being caught, the garbage collector dropped the demolition tools and fled. Mr. Cao then found the demolition personnel, who said they would clear the site as soon as possible to facilitate travel for Mr. Cao's family.

[同期声] Demolition Supervisor: We considered that demolishing the wall might affect this family. Originally, there was rebar in the wall. A garbage collector saw the demolition here and came to knock out the rebar to sell it, which caused the wall to collapse (general idea).

[同期声] Mr. Cao: How much can this rebar sell for? What if someone gets hurt?

[Audio Clip] In fact, the demolition office could put up warning signs at some demolition sites to explain the potential dangers and remind non-staff members not to enter, ensuring the safety of the demolition site.

[Ending Voiceover] This matter is still under further investigation by the police. You see, that garbage collector went to such lengths just for some rebar, completely disregarding that people still lived next door. Fortunately, no serious consequences occurred. If the neighboring wall had collapsed and injured someone, the results would have been unimaginable.
