Two, Believe in Customer Trusting Me Heart

1. Synchronization of values Purchase values: The important reference point for customers when purchasing products. To touch the customer, understanding the customer is more important than understanding the product. Liu Yisecond leadership wisdom Sales strategy in eight steps: 1. Do you want to see a certain product? 2. Have you seen some before? 3. That must have taken a lot of time and effort, right? 4. Our place may not be suitable for you either? 5. I've been in this industry for N years. Let me understand your needs or conditions now. If it's not suitable here, I'll directly introduce you to a suitable place. (When the customer doesn't trust you, everything you say is nonsense.) 6. Referring you to another place gives me no benefit. 7. List our product selling points in advance. 8. Let the customer choose what they care about most. Provide no more than three options. (No product can meet all the customer's needs, only sell key points.) Sales is: 1. Find out the customer's values 2. Change the customer's values 3. Plant new values.
