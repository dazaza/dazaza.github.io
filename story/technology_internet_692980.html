Two post-90s night robbers were identified and killed to silence them

by lovei0c1 on 2012-03-06 13:47:38

Suspect Zhong pointed out the crime scene.

During the crime, a teenage girl was found with her hands and feet bound, and her neck was stabbed three times. Her body was discovered in the washroom of a training school in Xinfeng County. After receiving the report, the Xinfeng County Public Security Bureau quickly identified the suspects through investigation and interviews. Within three days of the incident, the police arrested two suspects in a rented house in Dongguan, Guangdong Province.

According to suspect Zhong's confession, they were recognized by the victim during the robbery, so he stabbed the victim three times in the neck, killing her.

Incident

A teenage girl was stabbed three times in the washroom and died.

Zeng, 20 years old, was a student at a computer training school in Xinfeng County. Recalling what she saw on February 26 still makes her uncomfortable.

At around 9 a.m. that day, Zeng went to the washroom on the fourth floor of the school after getting up, and found her roommate Lu had been killed, with a lot of blood on the ground. Panicked, she immediately ran back to the dormitory to report it to the school and call the police.

After receiving the report, the Xinfeng County Public Security Bureau took it very seriously, assembling a special task force with experienced police officers who quickly arrived at the scene to start the investigation. Liu told the investigating police that the night before the incident, her roommate Lu went to the washroom to brush her teeth and never returned to the dormitory.

The police found that the school was located in a bustling area of the county. The school was a four-story office building, with classrooms on the second floor, student dormitories on the third floor, and a student washroom on the rooftop of the fourth floor. There were some splattered bloodstains on the walls at the crime scene. In addition to being stabbed three times in the neck with a sharp weapon, there were obvious signs of strangulation on the victim’s neck, and her hands and feet were tied with ropes. The victim's clothes were relatively neat.

According to the forensic examination, Lu died from mechanical asphyxiation and hemorrhagic shock, with the time of death estimated between 9:30 p.m. and 10:00 p.m. on February 25. Subsequently, after discussing the case, the members of the special task force believed that the suspects likely premeditated the crime and were familiar with the victim's daily routine.

Investigation

Two "post-90s" men were identified as suspects, ruling out sexual murder.

To solve the case as soon as possible, the special task force divided into two groups for the investigation. One group continued to investigate the scene, carefully examining every corner; the other group focused on investigating the social relationships of the victim.

During their investigations, the police learned that the victim was honest and kind, had no conflicts or disputes with the surrounding students, and had simple interpersonal interactions, basically ruling out the possibility of retaliatory murder.

The investigating police told reporters that the victim's mobile phone and cash were missing, leading the special task force to initially judge that the victim might have been robbed and then killed, or possibly sexually assaulted and killed.

The police received feedback from the forensic examination that the victim had not been sexually assaulted before her death. "What kind of person would choose a schoolgirl as a target? What kind of person would choose the washroom where the victim must go as the crime scene? What kind of person could be so cruel?" This question kept recurring in the mind of the investigating officer.

Through investigation, two men from Gan County eventually came under police scrutiny. It was learned that before the incident, these two men often visited the school and were familiar with the students' schedules. Following up on this lead, the police found that the two men lived in a rented house in the city of Xinfeng before the incident. According to the surrounding residents, both men were about 20 years old, usually slept during the day, went out at night, and idled away their time. Further investigation revealed that after the incident, the two men had not checked out of their room but had disappeared without a trace, significantly increasing their suspicion of involvement in the crime.

Arrest

Xinfeng police rushed to Dongguan to capture the suspects.

With the assistance of relevant departments of the Ganzhou Municipal Public Security Bureau, the special task force quickly identified the two suspects as Liu, born in 1991, and Zhong, born in 1992, both residing in Wangmudu Town, Gan County.

It was found that Liu and Zhong had known each other since childhood. Zhong's parents worked outside, so he lived with his grandparents. Since the elderly were too old to discipline him, he dropped out of school early and wandered around. Liu's father passed away early, and his mother could not manage him either, so he started wandering around society after finishing junior high school. Due to their similar life experiences, their relationship became even closer. Previously, Liu and Zhong had worked in Guangdong.

On February 27, after investigation and visits, the special task force learned that Liu and Zhong might be hiding in Dongguan, Guangdong Province. The police rushed to Dongguan that evening to arrest the suspects. With the cooperation of the local police, the special task force quickly determined that Liu and another individual were hiding in a certain neighborhood in Houjie District, Dongguan City. At 3 p.m. on February 29, the police captured Liu and Zhong while they were sound asleep in a rented house.

Confession

They were recognized during the robbery and killed the victim to cover their tracks.

Upon interrogation, the suspects Liu and Zhong confessed that in October 2011, after quitting his job in Gan County, Zhong met Liu who was unemployed at home. During this period, Zhong frequently invited Liu to visit his girlfriend Xiao Xiao at the involved training school. Over time, Liu became familiar with the environment and living habits of the female boarding students there. They had also committed theft twice.

On February 2nd this year, the two came to Xinfeng County and rented a house in Jiading Town. They slept during the day and surfed the internet at night, quickly spending all the money they had.

On February 25th, penniless, Liu and Zhong decided to rob the boarding students at a training school in Xinfeng County.

At around 4 p.m., the two carried tools such as a dagger and cloth strips and hid on the fourth floor of the school. At around 8:30 p.m., they heard the footsteps of two girls returning. Then, they saw them go back to the dormitory on the third floor. Liu suggested robbing them in the dormitory, but Zhong was too scared to go.

Half an hour later, they noticed one of the girls going downstairs. Liu suggested robbing her again, but Zhong was still too afraid to go. Around 9:30 p.m., Zhong and the other noticed a girl walking from the third floor to the fourth floor. Quickly, they used cloth strips and belts to restrain the girl. The girl shouted at Zhong, “I know you; you once treated me to dinner.” Zhong then stuffed a cotton pantyhose into the victim's mouth and searched her, taking 2510 yuan and a mobile phone. Fearing exposure, Zhong and Liu dragged the victim to the washroom, strangled her neck with their hands, and when Zhong noticed she was still breathing, he stabbed her neck three times with a knife. Subsequently, they fled the scene.

Zhong further confessed that at around 8 a.m. on February 26th, fearing capture by the police, they took multiple buses to reach Dongguan, Guangdong, but were still caught by the Xinfeng police within three days.

Currently, the two suspects, Zhong and Liu, have been criminally detained, and the case is under further review.
