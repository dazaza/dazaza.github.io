Hilarious Guide to Dealing with Rude Bank Service Attitudes

by xiaoxiao67 on 2011-12-22 16:59:08

I. Changing Money

My brother asked me to change 50 x 20 RMB notes for giving out as red envelopes at his wedding. I took 1k to the bank to change, but the bank clerk rudely said "No, we can't change it for you," with a terrible attitude. I fiercely countered, "Say that again." That person arrogantly replied, "There's just none." I said, "You wait and see." The bank clerk retorted, "Are you threatening me?" I then took out my ID and said, "Open an account, deposit 1k." After finishing, I said, "Withdraw 20 twice." The bank clerk asked, "What do you mean?" I responded, "Playing with money, withdrawing 50 times then closing the account, how about that?" In the end, the bank clerk reluctantly changed 50 crisp new 20-yuan notes for me. What a jerk!

II. Why is it called Construction Bank?

Just now, I went to the Construction Bank near the hospital to withdraw money. There were many people queuing up in the sun outside the ATM at the entrance. However, strangely, there was only one customer inside the business hall. So, I went to a counter and told the staff I wanted to withdraw 800 yuan. That person actually told me to use the ATM outside. I asked why the card couldn't be used on the counter? He said that any amount below 5000 should be withdrawn from the ATM, claiming it was to save time, improve efficiency, and分流 (divert the flow). I was stunned by this 'diversion'. I asked him, if the ATM queue outside is so long and there's no one inside, what kind of efficiency are you saving? Is there any document stating that amounts below 5000 cannot be withdrawn from the counter? Other banks can! That person said it was their bank's regulation. I demanded to see it. He couldn't produce it but still refused to let me withdraw. Looking at the people queuing up in the sun outside and the staff inside reading newspapers and enjoying air conditioning, I got extremely angry and decided to teach him a lesson. I said, "Alright, then let's withdraw 5000!" After he finished and handed me 5000, I took out 100 yuan and said, "Deposit 100!" Since ATMs can't deposit money, he had to handle it. After he finished with the 100 yuan, I took out another 100 and said, "Deposit 100!" That guy asked, "Can't you deposit all at once?" I replied, "The file I issued myself states that only 100 can be deposited at a time. What's wrong with that? I'll deposit 100 at a time." Gradually, many supporters gathered around, cheering me on. Immediately, someone nearby started doing the same as me. Finally, their leader came out, arrogantly saying, "You're disrupting financial order, I'll call the police." I replied, "I'm depositing money, isn't the bank supposed to be where money is deposited? Do you have a document stating that one cannot deposit 100 at a time? Show it to me!" Many people around supported me, loudly criticizing them. In the end, that leader apologized, saying they would definitely improve their working methods to provide convenience to everyone... When I turned to leave, I told him, "Now I know why your bank is called Construction Bank, because you're so despicable!"

III. Fake Bills at the Merchant Bank

A few days ago, I went to the Merchant Bank to withdraw money (for the company). When counting, I found a fake 100-yuan note. Because I am studying accounting and due to previous incidents at the company (fake bills found in salaries paid after withdrawing from the bank), I was particularly cautious. Afterwards, I told the counter lady, "Miss, there's something wrong with this note." Lady: "What's wrong?" Me: "It's fake." Lady: "Impossible, you've left the counter." I immediately got angry: "Are your eyes broken? Which eye saw me leave the counter?" Then, we started arguing. Her attitude was very bad, as if I deliberately swapped the money, and she was quite harsh with her words. I said, "Is there something wrong with your counterfeit detection machine? It didn't even alarm when a fake bill passed through. Let's report it to the police - isn't it supposed to be reported to 110 upon finding fake currency?" By then, the bank was already in an uproar. The bank manager came out. Without even understanding the situation, he first threw a question at me: "Did you leave the counter?" "If you left the counter, you can't prove that the fake bill came from us!" I exclaimed. At that moment, I thought it was funny: Was he here to solve the problem or just to shift responsibility? Without hesitation, I pushed all the blame away. I said, "Whether I left the counter or not, my word doesn't count. Let's check the surveillance footage. Otherwise, I'll report it to the police. You decide what to do." At this point, the bank manager began to soften a bit, improving his attitude and even smiling while asking me, "So, how do you think we should resolve this?" I suggested without much thought: "Punish tenfold!" The bank manager replied, "Compensating tenfold is impossible. How about a compromise, half off?" I thought about it and agreed. In the end, I withdrew the money, looking at the manager and the counter lady, saying, "You'd better remember my face next time, don't play any 'tricks'" (many banks have irregular operations, often issuing fake bills, mostly in large accounts where it's less likely to be noticed). In the end, the manager smiled and escorted me out.

IV. VIP Window at Agricultural Bank

Starting a few months ago, a certain Agricultural Bank set up a VIP window that only handles transactions above 50,000 yuan. Once, I wanted to withdraw 10,000 yuan. The queue at the small-amount window was super long, while the VIP window was empty. So, I went to the VIP window. The staff asked me how much I wanted to withdraw. I said 10,000 yuan, and she directly said, "Anything below 50,000 should go to the small-amount window." I then said, "Then I'll withdraw 60,000." She said, "60,000 is fine." After she gave me 60,000 cash, I took 10,000 and put the remaining 50,000 back, saying, "Deposit 50,000." The staff fainted.

V. Full Withdrawal

Another time at a certain Industrial and Commercial Bank, after a 1.4 million yuan payment from the client arrived, I went to the Industrial and Commercial Bank to handle the transaction. Since I didn't have an Industrial and Commercial Bank card, I planned to open an account and transfer the money. But the staff actually said, "To open an account, we need a copy of your ID, but our copier is broken, so you'll have to go copy it yourself." I exploded with anger, saying, "Forget it, no deposit. Withdraw all 1.4 million in cash! Submit your plan tonight, I'll come back tomorrow!" The face of the bank's business director immediately changed. Then, a young girl smiled broadly and said, "Please give me your ID, I'll copy it for you, please wait a moment..." Yeah, at that moment, I felt balanced psychologically...
