After carefully reading the excellent posts, I think my post is still helpful for newcomers. I just downloaded this game 5 days ago, and it's the version from here at Youxian Network. I've always played the Romance of the Three Kingdoms games starting from the orthodox era of Liu Bei in 191 AD (probably only I think it's orthodox! Haha), on the highest difficulty. All other options are default, and no modifications are used.

Upon starting again on the plains, it's still the three brothers. No money, little food, 12 empty plots of land. The graphics in Three Kingdoms 11 are quite comfortable to look at.

When I first started playing, I saved at the beginning of the first round, which is a good habit that should accompany your entire Three Kingdoms gaming experience. Personally, I don't use the SL strategy, as it's to prevent hours of gameplay from being wasted due to accidental mistakes. If you don't have this habit, you will suffer greatly. Due to unfamiliarity with the game rules, you will definitely make many mistakes. Just now, I sent out all my troops while defending (the default troop deployment is the maximum possible number of troops) resulting in no troops left in the city, causing the city to fall after being attacked... Upon checking the save file, it was from hours ago... This is called going back to square one.

I. Domestic Politics Chapter!

The first year, focus on farming, building markets, and recruiting people. Whether in the 2nd century or the 21st century, talent is always in short supply, so recruitment is very important. Generally speaking, action points are usually enough, but people are always insufficient. When hiring generals, follow the advice of your military advisor; if they say no, then it's probably not a good idea. Remember to check every ten-day period to see if there are any generals available for recruitment, which is the top priority. As long as they're within a 30-day journey, it's worth recruiting them, of course, under the approval of your military advisor. In the initial stage, even if some generals are only good for farming or merging territories (which takes a maximum of 20 days), those with higher numerical values can be assigned to other tasks.

1. Key stats for various development projects

Construction speed for development depends on the political ability of the selected general(s) (up to 3), recruitment depends on charm, production of weapons and armor depends on intelligence and the corresponding armory level, maintaining public order depends on leadership, and training depends on strength (training grounds can enhance training effects).
