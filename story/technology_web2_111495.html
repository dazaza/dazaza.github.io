Twitter is an Internet repository of information. There are already many variations of Twitter in China, such as talk, meal, tweet and so on more than ten websites. Its main advantages are good support for Chinese, as well as binding with domestic mobile communication service providers and instant chat tools. Chinese people also used to call it "short blog". But there are also many people who prefer to use the original Twitter with pure pedigree.

Due to the popularity of Twitter, many services have been launched specifically for Twitter. Twilert, for example.

image

Twilert is a service that delivers Twitter searches via email. Literally, Twilert is an original English combination, Twilert = Twitter+Alert. Twilert is kind of like Google Alerts. Through Twilert, you can develop several keywords or phrases that you are interested in, including brands, products, services, and other categories. You can then receive regular messages from Twitter. If you are a fan of Twitter, and if you are constantly following the news on Twitter, then Twilert is an indispensable tool for you.
