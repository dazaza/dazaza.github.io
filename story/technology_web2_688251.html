Husband stayed by the car after traffic accident and woke up his fainted wife 3 times

At the scene of the car accident, a red padded cotton jacket stained with blood and ash stood out in the sunlight.

Photo by Wang Chunsheng of Henan Business Daily

On a clear day, dense fog formed in patches, causing a chain collision involving 30 vehicles on the eastern side of the 107 National Road between Zhengzhou and Xinxiang County. This resulted in 2 fatalities and 7 injuries.

Drivers reported visibility of less than 5 meters. How can accidents be avoided in such patchy fog? Let's hear what the traffic police have to say.

Reported by Li Zheng of Henan Business Daily

Between 7:00 am and 8:30 am yesterday, 13 accidents occurred due to the patchy fog on the eastern side of the 107 National Road in the Xinxiang County section between Zhengzhou and Xinxiang. Captain Wang of the Xinxiang County Traffic Police Brigade stated that the accidents caused 2 deaths and 7 injuries. Drivers who experienced the crash said that visibility was less than 5 meters at the time of the incident.

Report

Chain collision on the 107 National Road

At 9:10 am yesterday, Weibo user @Pandongdong informed @Henan Business Daily that there had been a chain collision on the 107 National Road between Zhengzhou and Xinxiang, attaching multiple photos of the accident scene.

At 10:29 am, @Pandongdong updated their Weibo post, stating that 30 vehicles were involved in the collision, and the affected road section was four to five kilometers long.

Correspondents from Henan Business Daily immediately proceeded to the scene and interacted with readers and netizens through the Sina Weibo account of Henan Business Daily.

Scene

Four truck cabs crushed

At around 11:00 am, reporters walked northward along the eastern side of the 107 National Road for six to seven kilometers, where broken glass and vehicle debris were scattered everywhere. Along the way, at least four large truck cabs were found crushed, and another four vehicles sustained varying degrees of damage.

According to a gas station staff member, he saw 30 vehicles involved in a chain collision while passing through the accident site, spanning from Qiliying Town in Xinxiang County to the Xinxiang service area on the 107 National Road.

At approximately 12:40 pm, under the direction of the traffic police, the national road resumed normal passage.

Heartwarming Moment

Husband revives unconscious wife three times

Mr. Kong, a trailer truck driver, remained by his vehicle since the accident happened. He said the road was full of dense fog with visibility less than 5 meters.

At 12:40 pm, Room 8, Ward 11, Second People's Hospital of Xinxiang County.

"A big hole has been torn below my left eye," said Wei Bendong from Anhui Province, tears welling up in his eyes. His wife Chen Min was squeezed into the almost wrecked cab during the accident, suffering a fractured left leg and multiple injuries.

From the time the reporter entered the room until leaving, Wei Bendong never let go of his wife's left hand, holding it tightly. "Her hands are cold."

After the accident, Chen Min lay on the ground. Wei Bendong said he feared she wouldn't make it, so he shouted frantically to awaken her three times until rescue personnel arrived.

Cause

Patchy fog descended suddenly, reducing visibility to less than 5 meters

In the afternoon, Captain Wang of the Xinxiang County Traffic Police Brigade told reporters that between 7:00 am and 8:30 am, 13 accidents occurred on the eastern side of the 107 National Road in Xinxiang County, involving 30 vehicles in a chain collision, resulting in 2 deaths and 7 injuries.

Regarding the cause of the accidents, Captain Wang explained that sudden patchy fog descended at the incident location, reducing visibility to less than 5 meters.

The traffic police reminded drivers that before entering "patchy fog," if the distance and speed allow for lane changes, they should signal, reduce speed, and enter the far-right lane to quickly access the emergency lane to avoid being rear-ended by following vehicles. "Do not brake suddenly unless necessary."

Why does "patchy fog" favor highways?

According to meteorological experts, patchy fog is caused by localized water vapor cooling due to radiation, resembling a cloud resting on the ground. Visibility is good outside the fog but hazy inside. It generally appears in low-lying areas with high air humidity and can also occur in mountainous or rural regions on clear days.

Highway surfaces are warmer during the day with larger temperature differences between day and night, which is more conducive to the formation of patchy fog. Additionally, factors like burning straw in autumn and industrial dust pollution also contribute to the formation of patchy fog.
