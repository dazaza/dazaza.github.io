If three years ago, you asked me whether I believe in love at first sight, I would happily tell you, I believe.

------Preface

In November of my sophomore year, Hua Hua and I were watching a basketball game between our class and theirs on the basketball court in front of the boys' dormitory. It was also on that court where I saw him.

What about him caught my attention at the time? Was it his focused expression? Or was it his all-out commitment to basketball? Or perhaps the deep thought in his eyebrows? All I know is, from the moment I saw him, my eyes were entirely focused on him throughout the whole match. I just secretly told Hua Hua, "That person looks very familiar~". During halftime, I watched him drink water with sweat streaming down his back, nodding his head as he listened to what seemed like a strategy from a physical education teacher. I was still looking at him, continuously, continuously looking at him. Who does he resemble? Why does he look so familiar?

After that day, almost every day, I could bump into him, in Xiangxian Building, on Student Street, on the way to Zeguan Building. It seems that whenever I turned around, I always saw him.

At that time, I was doubting, is this fate? Why must I keep seeing him?

One day, I finally remembered who he looked like. Originally, he resembled Hong Kong actor Wang Xi, the same single eyelid, the same dark skin, the same height, only he was a bit more muscular, maybe because of his love for basketball. From then on, I called him Wang Xi, Hua Hua liked to call him Wang Changxi, and later, I called him Brother Xi.

But I didn't know him, I didn't even know his name. I even didn't know which major or class this boy who suddenly appeared in my line of sight belonged to? If we had classes in the same building, why did I have no impression of him at all during the previous year and a half?

He wasn't always on my mind, but every time I saw him walking towards me, I could always spot him. Or it was Hua Hua who spotted him for me, then she would tell me, your Brother Xi is here.

During that period, Hua and I often ate at Zhenwei, while he ate at Xingwei next door. Often, there were just a few tables separating us, and occasionally, I would sneak a few glances at him. Besides Xingwei, I discovered that he usually ate at Lebashi on weekends. Therefore, whenever someone suggested gathering for a meal, I always proposed going to Lebashi! For a few days, I worked at Liangji next to Lebashi. From the first day of work, I looked forward to seeing him. One day, I happened to see him sitting at the entrance of Lebashi, eating boiled fish with his roommates. At the time, I didn't want to go inside the restaurant; I just wanted to sit by the entrance and take a look at him. Even if it was just his back, it would be fine.

Sometimes at Brothers Fast Food, sometimes at Dongyuan Restaurant, sometimes at Nanyuan Restaurant, watching him walk towards me from the opposite side and pass by me.

Every time I saw him, I couldn't wait to write in my small notebook:

On a certain year, month, and day, Brother Xi sat across from me, today he wore a No. 21 jersey.

On a certain year, month, and day, Brother Xi walked past me, today he has a beard.

On a certain year, month, and day, Brother Xi carried a fast food box back to the boys' dormitory.

On a certain year, month, and day...

Since when? When I saw him, I felt my heart racing? I forgot. All I knew was that for a while, I started writing about him crazily on Renren.com, just a few short sentences, yet people laughed at me for being obsessed. But I just couldn't help but write a few lines. Why write on Renren? Because at the time, few people around me used Renren, I just didn't want people around me to know about this matter. My former classmates basically all knew about it.

I often asked Hua Hua, do you think Brother Xi has a girlfriend? Why is he always alone or with a group of guys? His conditions are pretty good! What if he has a girlfriend? Could his girlfriend be in another city? He has no reason not to have a girlfriend, right? And so on.

More than a year later, I found out his name, his class, his QQ number, and realized how close I was to him. The people around him gradually became known to me, including some of his dorm mates inadvertently. But I lacked the courage to get to know him, I kept this secret buried in my heart. Because I didn't understand what the feeling of a racing heart meant? I didn't understand what it meant to keep meeting him unexpectedly? I didn't know if there was any trace of me in his eyes each time I saw him?

During this over a year period, I noticed that his clothing was extremely simple. In spring and autumn, he only had two T-shirts, one white and one multicolored striped; in summer, besides his No. 21 jersey, he also had a golden yellow T-shirt; in winter, two coats, one light gray and one green windbreaker. At the time, I curiously asked Hua Hua, how come he only has those two pieces of clothing? He doesn't seem poor, right?

In the Chinese roll call competition of my junior year, I surprisingly saw his name on the published list. First came the surprise, then a smile. With so many boys in your class, you were chosen too? Haha. I began looking forward to the start of the roll call competition, so I could secretly cheer him on. But I was also worried, what if he forfeits?

The day of the roll call competition finally arrived. With a restless heart, I entered Room 103 of the old district Hechang Building, searching for his figure. He really came, sitting alone on the contestant seat near the front door, while I sat a few rows from the back, keeping my eyes on him. I discovered, originally he wears glasses, how refined! But why does everyone else have supporters cheering them on, while he sits there alone? His roommates are too inhumane, aren't they? I kept muttering under my breath. Watching him still holding the speech manuscript, reading it seriously. Actually, I was already happy that he didn't forfeit, at least he's not an irresponsible guy! He seemed to be one of the last few to appear, and I started to see the group of people who often walk with him come to cheer him on, great! However, he seemed to be reciting from memory and got stuck on his words, what should he do? I was anxious for him. Does he know that on that night, a girl was quietly cheering for him from a corner somewhere?

Time flies, my life continues. I just don't understand, is this what they call love at first sight? But I don't know him, I don't understand him, what makes me infatuated with him? I just occasionally, occasionally experience the feeling of a racing heart and blushing cheeks.

When I was at school, I would occasionally think:

Be braver? Tell him, I seem to like him a little. But I'm not sure if this is liking? This isn't a drama, where there's so much love at first sight?

Or just let it be, do nothing. When reminiscing about campus life in the future, consider it a beautiful fairy tale. In fairy tales, Cinderella only danced with the prince once, and when the clock struck midnight, didn't Cinderella return to her own life? Let everything settle down~~

If it were you, what would you choose to do?

Regardless of what choice you make, right or wrong, stop worrying about it. Life is long, we all make mistakes.

Now that I've graduated, I can share my past secrets with everyone very calmly, because I've let go.

Brother Xi, thank you for giving me the feeling of a racing heart.

Goodbye, my dark horse prince. You will find your Snow White, and I, am just a Cinderella.
