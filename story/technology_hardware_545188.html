"Can you help me take care of my daughter for a while?" the man asked. After some conversation, Chen Shuifeng learned that the man was named Tian Yaping. His daughter had just been born for over a month. Due to disputes with his wife about divorce, Tian Yaping planned to work in Guangdong. Someone told him that Chen Shuifeng was good at taking care of children, so he intended to leave his daughter with Chen Shuifeng.

In the blink of an eye, Tian Tian turned two years old, and Chen Shuifeng sent her to kindergarten. Not long after, Tian Tian started having repeated fevers and coughs. A hospital check revealed that Tian Tian had contracted bronchopneumonia.

Over the years, many people have advised Chen Shuifeng, saying this child was not hers, and with poor health, it would be better to send her away early. But she said that after four years of care, there might be no blood relation, but there is certainly affection.

The other day, in the ward of Hunan Children's Hospital, Chen Shuifeng was helping Tian Tian (a pseudonym) dress while blowing up balloons for her. Photo by reporter Hua Jian.

For Chen Shuifeng, Tian Yaping was grateful. "First, I need to find a way to repay the money she borrowed." He indicated that in the future, he would treat Chen Shuifeng as family, like a real mother.

Doctor Yang Ting from the Department of Respiratory Medicine at Hunan Children's Hospital said that on June 22nd, Tian Tian was hospitalized again due to bronchopneumonia. In fact, Tian Tian's condition was not severe, but due to long-term illness and malnutrition, her resistance was weak, resulting in poor physical condition.

That day, Chen Shuifeng took Tian Tian from Tian Yaping's hands and cared for her for four years. "For the first two years, except for her father not paying me wages many times, everything else was fine," Chen Shuifeng said. Tian Tian was very obedient, not crying or making a fuss, and did not eat much. Therefore, despite the lack of compensation, she continued to raise Tian Tian. Moreover, she believed that by the end of the year, Tian Yaping would always come back.

Relief: Tian Tian's biological father finally showed up.

"I really have no other options," Tian Yaping said softly. Both his parents passed away early, and he had no other relatives to turn to for help.

Tian Yaping said that currently, he and his wife have reconciled. "She also misses her daughter, but she gets carsick easily, so I didn't let her come along."

Upon hearing her children's opinions, Chen Shuifeng only sighed and said that she would continue to persevere. "The biggest difficulty lies in not being able to afford medical expenses anymore."

At 1 p.m., after finishing her meal, Tian Tian yawned several times before falling asleep in her grandma's arms for a nap. After putting Tian Tian to sleep, Chen Shuifeng picked up the leftover boxed meal and began her lunch.

Due to her frail body, Tian Tian often fell ill. Over these years, Chen Shuifeng took Tian Tian everywhere for treatment, spending all her more than 70,000 yuan retirement savings and borrowing another 30,000 yuan.

But Chen Shuifeng's children do not fully understand their mother's actions. Chen Shuifeng and her husband had three children. After her husband passed away, her children established their own families. For most of the time afterward, she lived alone. Therefore, during the initial period of caring for Tian Tian, her children were unaware that their mother had taken on such a task.

Tian Yaping said that he also had an 8-year-old son. After entrusting Tian Tian to Chen Shuifeng, he went to Guangdong. Two years later, he returned to Yongzhou and found a job, occasionally visiting his daughter at Chen Shuifeng's home. In March this year, he learned about his daughter's condition, but because of his "low salary," he was powerless to help.

Chen Shuifeng's care for Tian Tian could be described as meticulous. When Tian Tian had persistent high fever and needed to sleep on an ice bed, Chen Shuifeng also slept with her under the covers, "It was cold, but the baby needs grandma." Later, Tian Tian's fever subsided, but Chen Shuifeng fell ill - she had a cold and had to receive six days of injections.
