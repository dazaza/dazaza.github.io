The most precious gift from the Antarctic expedition team to QQ users!

by bjseeprof on 2009-12-07 11:33:36

On February 25th at noon, a certain message came from the Chilean Air Force airport: the plane coming from Punta to Antarctica would arrive at one o'clock in the afternoon and return at three o'clock in the afternoon. This means that I will leave Antarctica, the Great Wall Station, and the scientific research team members of the Great Wall Station in three hours. Although I have only stayed here for two days and three nights, I am already very familiar with everything about the Great Wall Station. Especially when I queue up with everyone to get food, living a communist-style collective life, it feels like going back to student times several decades ago. It is really hard to part with all these. Director Pei invited everyone to the VIP room, took out a beautiful large guestbook made of acrylic, and asked the members of this consoling delegation to leave some comments and blessings. I told Director Pei that I would write on this page and leave more space for other comrades. But as I was writing, I felt that there were many things I wanted to say, so I unconsciously wrote to the second page. My message to the Great Wall Station is: "May Tencent QQ go through every day together with all the staff of the Chinese Great Wall Station in Antarctica! Let QQ become the bridge for communication between scientific expedition members and their families in the motherland and an emotional support!" After everyone finished leaving messages, I specially went to Director Pei's office to ask him if he could sign the postcards we prepared for QQ users. He readily agreed. I reminded him that there were over a hundred postcards, and time was tight; he said no problem, let's hurry! So I quickly brought the postcards, asking the director to sign while I helped organize the signed ones. The director signed more than a hundred postcards at one breath, and I felt very apologetic, but I was more excited inside. Isn't this exactly the most precious gift from the Antarctic scientific expedition team to our hundreds of millions of QQ users! At 2 PM, we gathered and departed from the Great Wall Station to the Chilean Air Force Airport. Before leaving the Great Wall Station, I specially came to the VIP room to see our crystal QQ penguin. It has already been displayed in the most prominent place on the exhibition stand, behind which are three terracotta warriors. Although I am about to leave here and it is unlikely that I will come back, it comforts me that this crystal QQ penguin will permanently accompany our beloved Antarctic scientific expedition team under the guard of the terracotta warrior sentinels!
