The daughter is a beauty in the making.

by muoehtprer on 2012-03-08 09:35:01

My mother is still in good health, with all test indicators tending to normal, and she has an annual physical examination as scheduled. I deeply know that my mother's life was exchanged by my father's love, it is the result of my father's extreme dedication, the brilliance of thirty years of undiminished affection, and more importantly, the result of my father's consistent meticulous care, scientific diet, and reasonable exercise. Now, they are healthy, worry-free in their later years, cheerful, and loving for a hundred years.

When I was a child, I didn't understand the pain in my grandmother's heart. Mom, you have fewer books than her, but in terms of being a person and dealing with things, you are very sympathetic and reasonable, surpassing the general tolerance and greatness of a mother. Last spring, my mother sowed seeds in the flowerpot again, sowing new hopes. When encountering relatives or friends in trouble, grandpa would take out some money from his originally meager retirement salary to help others. At this time, grandma would cover up outside. Occasionally, I will think about the beautiful dreams of my childhood. The second time was in 1995, when my father sent my second sister to school and passed through Qufu, and had a meal in front of the Confucius Temple with his colleagues.

Especially for those health products that my father saw on TV advertisements or newspapers, which claimed to cure various cancers, I would buy them for my father without hesitation. Only when my father tried them and found them useless would I stop buying them. In those years, there was not enough ox power in the team, so my father carried the load on his shoulders with several strong laborers.

In my memory, tomorrow is my daughter's birthday. When I was a child, he was not brave and even weak, once being beaten until he bled from the nose during a fight and could only cry without saying anything. I proudly told her that my daughter is a beauty embryo and will surely grow up to be a beautiful woman. I hope my son lives a peaceful and happy life and becomes a pillar of the country with both virtue and talent.

I hung up the phone, loneliness, fear, sadness, and at a loss all surged into my heart, but it only lasted for a few seconds, and I finally came to my senses and went to the hospital. My heart was so painful, piercingly painful. Younger brother looked up and saw my face, not knowing what words were wrong, and became at a loss...

I am going to work. However, now seeing you reviewing homework at home every day brings some comfort to my parents' hearts. After all, our daughter is a obedient and sensible child.

But, in the hot summer fields, the tea water at the head of the field could be picked up and drunk by everyone else, but she always held back first. When she really couldn't hold back anymore, she poured the water in the teacup into her palm and drank it. He worked selflessly like this.

On September 13th at 6:00 AM, my father called to tell me that my second uncle had already passed away. He wants to see you on Sunday.

Until today, the girl is still waiting bitterly, hoping for a miracle to appear, hoping to see her best friend and closest confidant again.
