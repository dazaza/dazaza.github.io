Man climbs alone into the cliffside coffins and pitches tent to sleep with ancient corpses (pictures)

by aiipii12987 on 2012-02-28 10:29:57

"Gu Mu Ge" took a souvenir photo in front of the notice board. "Gu Mu Ge" wore a Taoist costume and danced with a sword.

Recently, a netizen from Chongqing named "Zhi Zhu Da Xia" has attracted the attention of netizens due to his crazy actions in Longhu Mountain, and was called "Gu Mu Ge". Originally, this "Gu Mu Ge" on the evening of August 8th this year, avoided the staff of the Longhu Mountain Management Committee, climbed alone into the hanging coffin on the cliff, set up a tent, and spent a night sleeping next to the thousand-year-old ancient corpse inside the coffin.

"Gu Mu Ge" sneaked into Longhu Mountain at night

Yesterday, I saw on "Gu Mu Ge's" Weibo that the man came from Chongqing, liked traveling, and was a backpacker. At around 9:00 PM on August 8th, "Gu Mu Ge" sent out his first Weibo post after arriving at the Longhu Mountain scenic area in Yingtan: "Tonight is bound to be an extraordinary night." Afterwards, taking advantage of the rest time of the Longhu Mountain scenic area staff, "Gu Mu Ge" wore a headlamp and followed the mountain path towards the destination he had planned - Feiyun Pavilion. At 10:40 PM, "Gu Mu Ge" took a photo in front of a notice board in the scenic area that read "Dangerous at night, prohibited passage." At 11:00 PM, "Gu Mu Ge" entered Feiyun Pavilion, then climbed along a steep wooden ladder into a stone coffin. After entering the stone coffin, he saw a cave above it, so he climbed further. Upon discovering a small side cave on the second level of the cave, "Gu Mu Ge" decided to camp there.

Spent a night sleeping next to a thousand-year-old ancient corpse

In the photos uploaded by "Gu Mu Ge" himself to Weibo, I saw that after climbing to the second level cave of the hanging coffin tomb, "Gu Mu Ge" pitched his camping tent on top of a stone coffin and used his own outdoor cooking equipment to make a dinner. After dinner, "Gu Mu Ge" put on a set of Taoist robes he brought with him and casually performed a set of "Residual Shadow Sword Technique." The next morning, "Gu Mu Ge" quickly packed up his luggage before the scenic area staff started work and descended from the stone coffin back down to the foot of the mountain.

This action may have damaged cultural relics

The crazy actions of "Gu Mu Ge" have attracted a large number of netizens' attention. A Weibo netizen commented: "To spend the night with a 2100-year-old ancient corpse, his courage is really too big." Some netizens questioned, "Hanging coffins are also considered cultural relics. This person sleeping on the cultural relics, will it cause damage to the ancient objects?"

Relevant persons in charge of the Longhu Mountain scenic area in Jiangxi introduced that the scenic area did not know about the incident where the man spent a night alone in the Longhu Mountain hanging coffin tomb cluster. This man's behavior seriously violated the management regulations of the scenic area. Moreover, the hanging coffin tomb cluster only exists in nine places domestically, which belongs to ancient cultural heritage. This man openly cooked a meal on the stone tomb, which might cause damage to the cultural relics.
