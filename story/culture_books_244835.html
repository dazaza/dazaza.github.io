Art college female student Jiang Tingting accidentally killed someone and hastily embarked on a path of escape. Her boyfriend Ning Zhe, who was a police officer, searched everywhere without success, and unexpectedly assisted the police in breaking up a criminal gang.

On her escape route, Jiang Tingting endured immense panic, unease, loneliness, and mental torment. She went through hardships unimaginable by ordinary people and unexpectedly found an abandoned infant. She overcame various difficulties and carefully raised the abandoned baby.

The mysterious man Wu Xie frequently approached her life, becoming her most trustworthy friend. His attitude towards the child made her suspect that he might be the child's biological father.

Ning Zhe judged with sensitivity and intuition that Wu Xie was no ordinary person...

Wu Xie also, with the keen sense of smell like a hound, sniffed out that Ning Zhe was the enemy who had once orchestrated the tragedy of his family...

These two men appeared simultaneously in her life.

On one side was her first love, on the other side was her best friend. Facing the battle between justice and evil, how should Jiang Tingting choose?

"The Flower is Burning" Part One

Ning Fen told Jiang Tingting that she just went to the mall to buy a pair of scissors because the ones at home were too short, so she specifically bought a long one. Jiang Tingting was shocked, "Do you want to kill that woman?" Ning Fen said, "Not killing that vixen, I want to use suicide as a way to scare her from being a disgraceful third party and return happiness to me." Jiang Tingting listened and broke out in cold sweat. She asked, "Does Ning Zhe know?" Ning Fen said tragically, "He doesn't know, can't let him know, he is a policeman, he definitely won't let me do it." Jiang Tingting said, "I think the main responsibility for this kind of thing lies with Xiao Bao. You'd better talk to Xiao Bao first. If his attitude is correct and he voluntarily breaks off relations with that woman, you don't need to find that woman, then there will be no problem." Ning Fen said, "No matter how good a man is, he can't resist a woman's seduction. That vixen is the root cause of calamity, the spark. As long as that side doesn't extinguish, Xiao Bao feels hot and restless, his mind will never settle down."

"The Flower is Burning" Part Two

In the dim light, the masked female singer gracefully walked from behind the stage. The fox-shaped mask, pink color, fine eyebrows and eyes, thin lips, carried a bit of demonic charm, extremely alluring. A special rubber mold, texture as delicate as porcelain. The singer's lips were inside the mouth hole of the fox mask, breathing sweetly, the singer's eyes inside the eye holes of the fox mask, exuding threads of enchantment. This woman had pure femininity, easily penetrating into men's vision and senses, and could captivate them fatally. Unfortunately, her face was hidden behind a mask, standing on the stage, the audience could only interpret her thoughts and expressions through her eyes and lips.

"The Flower is Burning" Part Three

Wu Xie brought his little piglet. Piglet was a small Japanese fragrant pig, looking clumsy but running as agile as a puppy. As he approached, he took out a box of chocolates. Feng Yun said, "Why spend this money? What a waste! Are you rich or something? Do you have more money than you can spend?" Wu Xie looked at her, "It's not for you!" Wu Xie turned to Tian Tian, "For you! Twelve flavors, you'll definitely like it!" Feng Yun recognized this chocolate; it was the signature product of the Western restaurant department of Holiday Hotel. When Tian Tian's birthday came, she bought a box. Each box had twelve pieces, each piece not much bigger than fruit candy, each box priced at 320 Hong Kong dollars. And Wu Xie wasn't a rich man, maybe even less wealthy than her. She didn't look down on him; his attire clearly reflected his financial strength. This made her feel somewhat uneasy. Feng Yun said, "I'm now controlling her sweets intake. Eating too much sugar has almost given her cavities!"

"The Flower is Burning" Part Four

Tian Tian was a lovely child, very smart, often in telepathic communication with Feng Yun. Tian Tian had a round apple face, tender and white skin, shoulder-length yellow hair, and every morning Feng Yun would tie her beautiful bows and braids. At this moment, her yellow hair was scattered over her shoulders, her teeth tightly biting her lower lip, and the expression on her small face was rapidly changing. She opened her black almond eyes, tears slowly welling up. There was a kind of fear on her small face that Feng Yun had never seen before. This expression frightened Feng Yun, tearing her heart apart. She immediately hugged the child.

"The Flower is Burning" Part Five

Wu Xie hadn't walked a few steps when Yang Meimei caught up from behind, panting and placing one hand on his shoulder. Wu Xie asked her why she didn't wait for her shoes? Yang Meimei said, the shoemaker wouldn't dare to keep her shoes, she didn't want to stay alone, she wanted to browse the pet market with him, she wanted to see if there were any Japanese fragrant pigs! Wu Xie said, truly a lover of dragons, having one at home and not treating it well, yet wanting to see others', agreed that when they reached the pet market, not to touch those dogs and pigs, those little animals weren't healthy, not to bring bacteria home and infect Piglet.

"The Flower is Burning" Part Six

Their relationship from the beginning was initiated by her. The one who was always attentive and pleasing was her, not him. He loved her deeply, but he was naturally not the type of man who speaks sweet words. She had long been accustomed to his reserve, his golden silence, and his silent love. Now, after the four-year itch, passion began to cool, daily life fell into a fixed pattern, and he started to stir. Since returning to Ling Shui, especially in recent months, he was indeed different, even contrary to usual, often mysteriously being attentive, unreasonably pleasing her, being exceptionally gentle and good to his wife. This was the typical behavior of a man having an affair, because he felt guilty, because he felt unworthy towards his wife.

"The Flower is Burning" Part Seven

Wu Jie's bodyguards were embroiled in fierce combat with the intruders. Wu Xie used his body to guard Wu Jie's door, fiercely fighting the bandits. He continuously cut down three people. Wu Jie and his young wife woke up from their sleep, not knowing who fired the first shot, then gunfire rang out, several figures collapsed. Wu Xie rushed into his elder brother's bedroom, demanding to protect Wu Jie's escape. But Wu Jie covered his blood-soaked chest and hopelessly shook his head. Struggling, Wu Jie picked up his still sleeping daughter from the nearby crib and handed her to Wu Xie. Wu Jie looked at his younger brother, tore a strip of cloth from his pajamas, and used his bloody fingers to record a string of numbers on it. Then he pulled out a key from under the bed, wrapped it in the cloth, and stuffed it into the baby's swaddling clothes. Finally, Wu Jie used his last ounce of strength to entrust Wu Xie with taking care of the daughter! After speaking, Wu Jie's head tilted, his eyes lost focus, but his other hand tightly held onto his wife who had already fallen into a pool of blood.
