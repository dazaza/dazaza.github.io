If only life could be like the first meeting, the beauty and sorrow of classical poetry

If only life could be as beautiful as the first encounter, this is a work that refuses to be lost in a vast sea of books. It seems to discuss poetry, yet also appears to talk about romance. It doesn't confine itself to a superficial understanding of classical poetry, nor is it a simple traditional appreciation, but rather a unique style of rich emotional prose and essays. With a clear and sentimental writing style, accompanied by elegant and free-spirited illustrations, it paints a series of beautiful and moving historical love pictures behind classical poetry, leading readers to listen to classic and shocking romantic stories from the past. Poets and lyricists highlight their extraordinary talents and true emotions. Talented scholars and beauties, with their faint smiles, resolute determination, and tacit understanding, make us suddenly realize amidst our mixed feelings of joy and sorrow...

If only life could be as beautiful as the first encounter Part One

Liu Lian didn't say anything more. How could she not want to become Jianghuai's girlfriend? However, she had already found out that this senior student, two years ahead of her, was an exceptionally outstanding person. He studied science but wrote excellent articles and served as the president of the literary society. Such an extraordinary and handsome person was very popular among girls, almost like a lady-killer who leaves no one untouched. She couldn't imagine what kind of excellent girl would have the qualifications to stand shoulder to shoulder with him under the sun.

If only life could be as beautiful as the first encounter Part Two

Even though the flower had its name, there were still people who remained infatuated with Han Jiuyue. Naturally, she continued to receive flowers, cards, various plush toys, snacks, etc. But she refused to accept them and pushed them back. Some boys went so far as to stand downstairs outside the dormitory, handing over the gifts to any girl preparing to go upstairs, saying: "Please help me give this to Han Jiuyue in room 305." Afraid the girl wouldn't agree, they quickly gave her a piece of chocolate.

If only life could be as beautiful as the first encounter Part Three

Through the window, she saw someone bring out a stool and sit on the balcony knitting sweaters or playing poker, presenting a scene of prosperous life. A classroom was showing TV, she walked over and stood at the open back door to watch—it was a period drama. A man in a bright red cape sneaked into the prime minister's residence. She chuckled: dressed up so flamboyantly to be an assassin?

If only life could be as beautiful as the first encounter Part Four

Chen Taixian helplessly said: "It's over, I've been teased by my bad friend." Then angrily added, "Hmph, if I pass all of them, I'll set fire to your mosquito nets!" She became much better-behaved, carrying her schoolbag every day to find classrooms to attend lectures. But it was too late, she couldn't focus or understand, so she decided to play pranks, sticking notes on the back of the boys in the front row, which read: "Don't stop me, let me die."
