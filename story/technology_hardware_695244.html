Can eating too many pomelos cause internal heat?

Pomelos do not cause internal heat; instead, they help reduce it.

Daily consumption should be limited to one large segment (approximately 50 grams).

Pomelos that are too bitter should not be consumed.

Pomelos are of a cold nature, so people with a weak constitution due to coldness should not eat too many.

Tangerines (written as "oranges" for a more formal tone) can be eaten regularly but should not be consumed excessively. It is recommended to eat 1-3 oranges daily. Eating too many at once may easily lead to internal heat, which could trigger mouth ulcers or gum inflammation, and may also result in yellowing of the skin.

Additionally, since pomelos contain physiologically active substances like pectin, they can lower blood viscosity and reduce the formation of blood clots, thus providing better preventive effects against cerebrovascular diseases such as cerebral thrombosis and stroke. Fresh pomelo flesh contains components similar to insulin, making it an ideal food for diabetic patients.

Traditional Chinese medicine also believes that pomelos have a sweet and sour taste, are of a cold nature, and possess functions such as regulating qi, resolving phlegm, moistening the lungs, clearing the intestines, replenishing blood, and strengthening the spleen. They can treat symptoms such as poor appetite, bland taste, and indigestion, and can aid digestion, eliminate phlegm, quench thirst, and resolve stagnation.

Regular consumption can strengthen the lungs.

The pomelo is the mature fruit of the plant Citrus maxima from the Rutaceae family, produced in southern regions of China such as Fujian, Jiangxi, Guangdong, and Guangxi. They are also considered by the medical community to be the fruit with the most therapeutic benefits. Both pomelo tea and pomelo peel have practical values. Pomelos are of a cold nature and have high calories, so eating too many can cause internal heat. It is suggested that you do not overconsume them. Eating them regularly is beneficial to your health, but excessive consumption can harm your body.
