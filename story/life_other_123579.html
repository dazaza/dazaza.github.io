Shijiazhuang Oriental Science and Technology Secondary Professional School

by xie11 on 2009-02-09 16:41:54

The school adopts a combination of work and study,勤工俭学办学 model, with first-class training facilities in the whole province, a simple, fast and accurate teaching model, fully closed quasi-military student management, perfect employment placement, and the lowest charging standard in the whole province.

Shijiazhuang Oriental Science and Technology Secondary Vocational School is a full-time comprehensive secondary vocational school approved by the Shijiazhuang Municipal Education Bureau and filed with the Hebei Provincial Department of Education. The school is located at No. 29, Shihuo South Road, Shijiazhuang City, Hebei Province, at the intersection of Xinhua Road and West Second Ring Road, with superior geographical conditions, convenient transportation, and beautiful environment.

Our school has always adhered to the development philosophy of "emancipating mind, being pragmatic and innovative, and thinking diligently for change", constantly carried out teaching and management reforms, firmly established the awareness of "market competition", taken the market as the guide, and taken the enterprise demand as the standard to explore new ideas and methods of theoretical and practical teaching, accumulating rich teaching and management experience. After employment, students have received widespread praise from employers.

1. With enterprise standards as guidance, first-class teaching facilities in the whole province have been configured.

Adequate and advanced teaching experimental equipment is the foundation and guarantee for doing a good job in vocational education. Our school has cumulatively invested more than 10 million yuan, and built a numerical control training workshop equipped with more than 50 numerical control lathes, numerical control milling machines, ordinary lathes, ordinary milling machines and other equipment, as well as an ordinary lathe training workshop. The school has also built communication and electronics laboratories, music rooms, electronic reading rooms, grinding rooms, body shaping rooms, and new teaching buildings and other supporting facilities.

2. With employment positions as guidance, an enterprise-style teaching model has been created.

Establishing a teaching model mainly based on practice, in the teaching process, all students are managed as enterprise employees. Through competitive job postings, students take on the roles of team leaders, line managers, and employees in their "work". And through regular evaluations and exams, outstanding team leaders and technical experts are selected among the students. This effectively promotes students' enthusiasm and initiative in learning.

Each day's "work" situation of the students is monitored by quality indicators, which are statistically analyzed monthly. According to the scores obtained each month, wages are calculated, which consist of: workload + performance evaluation + work discipline + appearance and demeanor + safe operation + bonuses, etc. This effectively stimulates students' initiative in learning and competitive awareness, enabling them to master the knowledge and skills they have learned faster and better.

Through enterprise-style teaching reform, students can gradually experience and adapt to enterprise management models during their schooling period, meet the needs of employment positions, and initially reach the enterprise employment standards. The goal of enterprise-style teaching is to gradually surpass enterprise employment standards.

3. With enterprise demand as guidance, a fully enclosed quasi-military management system for students has been created, and a daily standard for students and a practical manual for vocational quality training have been formulated.

Comprehensively learning from the enterprise management model for employees, cultivating qualified talents that enterprises like. Enterprises not only need skilled workers with strong technical skills but also focus more on the overall quality of employees, such as teamwork spirit, obedience, execution ability, competitive awareness, etc. The cultivation and improvement of overall quality require efforts and cooperation from all aspects of the school. Overall quality education runs through the entire learning process of students. To enhance the overall quality of our students, our school highlights two distinctive features in management:

Distinctive Management One: Strict Quasi-Military Management

Our school adheres to the educational concept of "becoming a responsible person first, then becoming a talented person," and hires retired military officers to specialize in quasi-military management of students. Starting from the daily life system, starting from dressing, behavior, etiquette, and internal affairs arrangement, we let students develop good living habits, cultivate students' excellent organizational discipline style, and make them quickly become disciplined talents with good military demeanor.

Distinctive Management Two: Vocational Quality Education Management

During the students' learning process, vocational quality courses are offered. These courses are divided into theoretical teaching and expansion practice, complementing each other and mutually enhancing. Vocational quality theoretical teaching mainly focuses on practical combat, doing the ideological transformation work for students, while expansion training uses the method of "personal experience" to consolidate teaching results, thereby making these vocational quality requirements become the voluntary actions of students.

Vocational quality expansion training: Our school signs agreements with enterprises and sends our on-campus students in batches to enterprise positions under the accompaniment of school leaders and teachers. After a period of quasi-employee life, students can understand society and enterprises before truly interning and taking up positions, enter the formal enterprise employee state in advance, and lay a good foundation for adapting to employment positions.

4. With employment as guidance, the employment placement work has been properly completed.

With the continuous improvement of our students' qualities and employment channels, student internship and employment bases have been widely established in the Pearl River Delta region, Beijing-Tianjin-Hebei-Shanxi area, and Jiaozhou Peninsula.

The employment direction of our school's CNC major is mainly automobile parts manufacturing. All student internships are in mechanical manufacturing enterprises ranked in the world's top 500. The professional alignment rate of student internships and employment reaches 90%, and the student employment satisfaction rate exceeds 95%.

5. Thoughtful后勤services, uniquely creating the "free meal system" for on-campus students in the whole province.

All on-campus students of our school implement the "free meal system" for three meals. On one hand, it reduces the burden on students' parents, and on the other hand, the school formulates scientific menus according to the physical development needs of students, fully ensuring the nutritional needs of students' physical development and adapting to intensive learning, effectively avoiding the adverse consequences caused by students eating outside the school.

The school's student dormitory building, canteen, supermarket, telephone bar, bathroom, and other service facilities are complete. The logistics management department always adheres to the principle of "service" and strives to be proactive, thoughtful, considerate, and convenient in its services.

6. Only miscellaneous fees are charged. The source of tuition and accommodation fees for three years for students.

Our school is a three-year secondary vocational school. With a strong sense of social responsibility derived from society and returned to society, relying on powerful educational strength and forward-looking educational concepts, we save costs to the maximum extent for students' parents. That is, students coming to our school only need to pay miscellaneous fees for three years, and the tuition and accommodation fees for three years are paid through the combination of work and study, i.e., vocational quality training. The school has also specially established a "Hongzhi Class," which fully or partially exempts all miscellaneous fees for academically excellent and highly competent students from humble backgrounds. Our students enjoy the national scholarship of 1500 yuan per year provided by the country.

In the process of running the school, our school continuously summarizes experiences and lessons learned and creatively refines our school culture.

The refinement of "school culture" outlines the grand blueprint and direction of struggle for our school. The practice process of school culture is also the process of shaping our brand and further enhancing our influence.
