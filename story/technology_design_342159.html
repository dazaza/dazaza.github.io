Spring is the perfect time for mountain climbing, and the Mountaining Challenge at the Southern Bamboo Sea of Tianmu Lake has become a classic event this spring. The winding mountain path follows along the stream, extending from the statue of the Longevity Elder at the southern foot of the mountain deep into the bamboo sea. The first peak of Wu and Yue in the Southern Bamboo Sea, with an elevation of 508 meters, serves as the finishing point of the mountain climbing competition. A trail of over a thousand meters suits friends, classmates, and colleagues to come together for a mountain race. It's not about who reaches the summit first, but rather the scenery along the way and the joyous mood of appreciating it.

At the top, the ZHUANGYUAN Pavilion allows visitors to admire the grace of literary talents from the past, inspiring future generations to strive for excellence. There are also thrilling high-altitude rope descents that bring excitement and the joy of spring. In the countryside, ancient mills and water wheels sway gently, and under the spring sunshine, one can operate these ancient water wheels, channeling the flowing creek water to irrigate fields and vegetable gardens, evoking memories of long-past times. On the streets of Jiangnan, various handicrafts dazzle the eyes, while delicious snacks open up one’s appetite. Additionally, there's a carnival filled with fun activities like riding a water bicycle which offers a unique pleasure. Other attractions such as the Butterfly Valley, Fun World, and high-altitude flying descents make for a distinctive and warm early spring journey.

The Fifth Tianmu Lake Southern Bamboo Sea Mountain Climbing Festival introduces the "Beautiful Lady Journey." In the misty rain of southern Jiangsu, Tianmu Lake resembles a newly awakened Jiangnan woman, adorned beautifully, charmingly captivating all who visit. Whether strolling by the lake or wandering deep in the bamboo forest, the scenery enhances the beauty of nature and emotion. With its superior natural resources and sincere service, Tianmu Lake has been awarded as the "Demonstration Site for the Shanghai Expo Theme Experience Journey in the Yangtze River Delta," warmly embracing the Shanghai Expo with a renewed charm. This spring, we gather at the edge of Tianmu Lake, whispering the beauty of Jiangnan.

Activities for the Tianmu Lake Southern Bamboo Sea Mountain Climbing Festival will last from March 6th to March 28th. Different from previous editions, this year's festival not only continues the beloved Spring Mountain Climbing Competition at the Southern Bamboo Sea but also incorporates health concepts such as hot spring wellness. Together with the Yu Shui Hot Spring, they create a healthy travel experience, allowing city dwellers to stretch their limbs in the early spring Southern Bamboo Sea and enjoy bathing in the Yu Shui Hot Spring, offering the most comfortable and fashionable spring health journey.

Ascending the Southern Bamboo Sea offers panoramic views of lush bamboo forests. Riding a fairytale-like little train along the mountain road, one transforms into a prince or princess, experiencing romance. The slow-moving cable car takes you through the air, showcasing the vastness of the bamboo sea, like rippling green waves moving with the wind, a masterpiece of nature that leaves one awestruck. Retracing the ancient official road, pondering the sentiments of predecessors, viewing bamboo carvings, and appreciating the exquisite craftsmanship. At the first peak of Wu and Yue, sipping a cup of Nan Shan寿mei tea, enjoying the beautiful scenery of three provinces with friends, such a moment feels truly divine.

As the ancients said, "A mountain does not need to be high, if it has immortals, it becomes spiritual." The bamboo of the Southern Mountains always makes one believe in the existence of immortals. Drifting on a bamboo raft, surrounded by verdant bamboo forests on both sides of the tranquil lake, the sound of bamboo leaves fills the ears, white egrets fly lightly, mandarin ducks play in the water, in such scenery, no words are needed, just quiet meditation is enough to understand the serene beauty of the Southern Mountains. Following the waterside corridor, winding upwards, every step reveals a different scene, full of emotions. Searching and seeking, suddenly an old immortal appears in the mountains singing...

Tianmu Lake Southern Bamboo Sea, exploring the early spring with companions by the lake, Tianmu Lake always inspires such spontaneous affection. The emerald lake stretches leisurely, willows sway gently, softly speaking of the tenderness of spring. Such a picturesque place is never short of lovers leaning against each other, whispering sweet nothings in the landscape painting of Tianmu Lake.

To celebrate Women's Day, the current Tianmu Lake Southern Bamboo Sea Mountain Climbing Festival grandly launches the "Beautiful Lady Journey." Online media from cities like Shanghai, Suzhou, Hangzhou, and Nanjing jointly recruit "Professional Beauties" for a self-driving tour of the Southern Bamboo Sea, igniting enthusiasm among numerous netizens to sign up. After careful selection, the outstanding "Professional Beauties" have become important guests at this mountain climbing festival. During the festival, they can freely tour the Tianmu Lake Southern Bamboo Sea scenic area and receive a free ticket to the Yu Shui Hot Spring. These youthful and attractive professional beauties, after climbing to the top of the Southern Mountain to admire the vast bamboo sea, then soak in the Yu Shui Hot Spring, enjoying the comfort of aromatic SPA treatments, eliminating fatigue and promoting health and well-being.
