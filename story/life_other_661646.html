My heart is yours for this lifetime.

by lili1 on 2012-02-09 11:23:16

This life, my heart belongs to you.

A boy fell in love with a girl. However, because of his own background, he didn't dare to confess his feelings to the girl for a long time. The girl's parents were wealthy and influential in the city.

Every day, the boy secretly followed the girl on her way home after school. After some time, the girl also noticed that the boy was following her every day. So, she scolded him in front of the whole class. "Are you a freak? You follow me every day. What do you want?" the girl angrily said. The boy didn't dare to look up and mumbled something. "Speak! Why do you follow me every day?" The girl continued unhappily. The boy lowered his head and softly said: "I-I-I, I-like-you." The girl was very surprised and shouted: "You..."

Then she walked away. The classmates around them burst into laughter: "Haha, a toad wants to eat swan meat? ... Haha, it's killing me. Go away." The boy lowered his head, and tears involuntarily streamed down his face.

The boy ran to the park where the girl often went. Suddenly, he saw a boy holding the girl's hand, and the girl was laughing non-stop, looking very happy - a happiness the boy had never seen before. He suddenly froze. Afraid that the girl would see him, he quickly ran away. When the boy got home, tears involuntarily streamed down his face again. That night, the boy cried very sadly. The boy knew that the girl already belonged to someone else.

The next day, the boy saw the girl constantly checking her watch. Then the bell rang... School was out. The girl quickly packed her bag and hurriedly left. The boy slowly packed his bag and walked like a lost soul. Suddenly, the boy saw the girl and that boy eating ice cream together. The boy's heart felt as if it were being cut by a knife, so he quickly walked away. From then on, the boy started to be late every day.

One day, the boy saw the girl leaning on the desk. Suddenly, the girl burst into tears. The boy wanted to comfort her but didn't dare to. However, the boy couldn't help himself anymore. He walked to the girl's side and gently hugged her. The girl pushed him away and shouted: "What do you want to do?" The boy didn't answer, but made some funny gestures to cheer her up. The girl understood the boy's intention and smiled unintentionally. The boy was very happy. Finally, the girl smiled. The boy and the girl eventually became good friends, and the boy was extremely happy.

After school, the boy sent the girl home. Suddenly, several people rushed out. One of them said: "Hand over all your money. If not, otherwise..." The boy had no money, so the girl took out her money and gave it to them. She said fearfully: "We've given you the money. Can we go now?" A sleazy man laughed lewdly: "Little sister, don't rush. We're bored. Let's play with us." Then he began to approach the girl. The girl hid behind the boy in fear. One of them pulled the boy aside, while others dragged the girl. The girl screamed in fear.

The boy became angry, pushed the man away, rushed to the girl's side, and fought with those thugs. Outnumbered, the boy was quickly beaten down. The thugs approached the girl. The boy struggled to get up and fought with the thugs again. The boy blocked the thugs and shouted: "Run! Run! Don't care about me, run!" The girl kept running until the boy saw her far away and smiled.

The thugs got angry. One of them drew a knife and stabbed at the boy. Still, the boy blocked the thugs and was stabbed again. The boy finally fell powerless. After a while, the girl arrived with several policemen. They immediately arrested the thugs. Seeing the boy lying in a pool of blood, the girl rushed to his side, shaking him constantly, and cried her heart out: "Wake up! Stop playing, wake up!" The boy weakly opened his eyes, smiled foolishly - this was the first time the girl cried for him. "I-love-you, truly. I-really-love-you." The boy finally confessed his love to the girl. "I accept you, you have to hold on," the girl cried. The boy smiled, smiling brilliantly, and then he passed away. The girl cried her heart out.

I hope you can cherish the person who deeply loves you, and don't realize it only when they're gone.

Wish everyone a happy ending with their loved ones.
