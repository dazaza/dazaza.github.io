9 Low-Calorie, High-Calcium Foods for Efficient Fat Burning

by cshelive9520 on 2012-02-20 11:57:39

1. Low-fat Yogurt

Calories: 1 cup / about 110 calories

Calcium content: 415 mg

Weight loss principle: Low-fat yogurt is rich in protein, which can stabilize blood sugar levels and delay the onset of hunger. Studies have shown that people on a low-calorie diet who include yogurt in their meal plans lose 61% more body fat compared to those who don't eat yogurt. This is closely related to the high calcium content in yogurt!

2. Radish Greens

Calories: 1 cup / about 10 calories

Calcium content: 252 mg

Weight loss principle: Radish greens are rich in dietary fiber, which not only helps increase your sense of fullness but also effectively relieves constipation. Additionally, one cup of radish greens contains only 10 calories but has a high calcium content of 252 mg, making it an excellent weight-loss food that keeps you full without gaining weight.

3. Sardines with Bones

Calories: 1 cup / about 100 calories

Calcium content: 372 mg

Weight loss principle: Sardines are rich in protein and omega-3 fatty acids, which help maintain muscle mass. Both protein and omega-3 fatty acids are important substances for increasing calorie expenditure. Moreover, the high calcium content in sardines is also a highlight of healthy weight loss!

4. Tofu

Calories: 1 cup / about 81 calories

Calcium content: 500 mg

Weight loss principle: Tofu is a very popular food among Japanese women because it promotes metabolism, prevents constipation, makes the skin more radiant, and helps accelerate fat burning. Therefore, tofu is undoubtedly one of the superfoods for weight loss and beauty.

5. Kale

Calories: 1 cup / about 11 calories

Calcium content: 355 mg

Weight loss principle: One cup of raw kale contains 11 calories and is rich in iron and calcium. This low-fat, high-calcium food is undoubtedly the most ideal food to keep you full without gaining weight. If you don't like the taste of kale, you can search online for some delicious weight-loss recipes to turn it into your favorite dish!

6. Skim Milk

Calories: 1 cup / about 90 calories

Calcium content: 306 mg

Weight loss principle: Studies have shown that drinking 2-3 cups of skim milk every day can achieve better weight-loss results. The abundant calcium in milk is a good helper for weight loss. Additionally, drinking more milk can improve your skin condition, making you look more hydrated and smooth!

7. Spinach

Calories: 1 cup / about 30 calories

Calcium content: 291 mg

Weight loss principle: Spinach is rich in vitamin C, and after cooking, it becomes soft and smooth, making it a very tasty and slimming delicacy. Moreover, the plant fiber in spinach can promote intestinal peristalsis, which is beneficial for detoxification and weight loss. More importantly, the low-calorie and high-calcium content in spinach are the biggest helpers for weight loss!

8. Fat-free Cheese Slices

Calories: 3 slices / about 90 calories

Calcium content: 450 mg

Weight loss principle: Cheese slices can increase metabolic rate, which is beneficial for fat burning. The popular online wine and cheese weight-loss method isn't just hearsay! What's the principle behind cheese's weight-loss effect? Of course, it's due to its rich calcium content and low calorie count.

9. Broccoli

Calories: 3 florets / about 31 calories

Calcium content: 158 mg

Weight loss principle: Broccoli is a high-fiber vegetable that can effectively reduce the absorption of glucose by the intestines, creating a feeling of fullness, which helps reduce food intake and control weight. Additionally, coarse fiber can lubricate the intestines and promote bowel movements, helping to reduce excess fat around the waist and abdomen. Moreover, one cup of broccoli contains only 31 calories but has a calcium content of 158 mg, making it absolutely the best choice for weight-loss enthusiasts!
