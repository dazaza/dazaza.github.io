At the Softstar booth in the Taipei Game Show, the exhibition area of "Sword and Fairy Online" was undoubtedly the most watched area by players and media. Although the game itself was not substantially presented, the reporter still gained a conceptual understanding of the game's content framework from Mr. Huang Wen, the General Manager Assistant and spokesperson of Softstar. "Sword and Fairy Online" is the online version of the single-player game "Sword and Fairy", which was released by Softstar in 1995. The unprecedented influence of "Sword and Fairy" in the PC single-player field also granted "Sword and Fairy Online" a special status in the hearts of Chinese online gamers. Around 1995, when PC games were not very popular, "Sword and Fairy" had an astonishing sales record of 180,000 copies, and in 1999, it was ported to the Sega Saturn platform. Later, in 2005, it was adapted into a TV series and widely broadcasted across global Chinese-speaking regions. Since the determination to develop "Sword and Fairy Online" in 2002, it has gone through four years of "long development". The developers have been continuously working hard to create a brand new online game world that includes the original story and style of "Sword and Fairy". The game's background will mainly be based on the world of Li Xiaoyao in Sword and Fairy. However, as an MMORPG rather than a single-player game, the characters that players encounter in "Sword and Fairy Online" will not be Li Xiaoyao and Zhao Ling'er, or their descendants. Players will create their own characters with individual personality traits, while the main characters from the single-player version will exist as NPCs, whose whereabouts can only be discovered through exploration in the game. Players' self-created characters will have six professions: Qi Practitioner, Swordsman, Five Elements Master, Snake Knife Warrior, Butterfly Dancer, and Fox Night Thief. The professions of Swordsman, Knife Warrior, and Fox Night Thief are relatively easy to understand. A Qi Practitioner is a combat type that uses martial arts barehanded, while a Five Elements Master is a long-range attack type that manipulates water, fire, thunder, wind, and earth elements. A Butterfly Dancer might be a healing and support class. Mr. Huang Wen explained that the current development progress is approximately 70%, and currently, Dahu is continuously conducting internal testing. It is expected that the ideal time for an open beta internal test would be during the summer vacation in July-August this year. In the exhibition area, apart from the video demonstration indicating some settings of the game part of "Sword and Fairy Online", there were no substantial representations of the game product content. Through these videos, we could still understand that "Sword and Fairy Online" indeed continued the orthodox storyline and game worldview of "Sword and Fairy". The game content settings also remained consistent with the previous single-player version. However, truly seeing the product itself would allow one to tangibly feel the characteristics and charm of "Sword and Fairy Online", and we hope that day comes sooner.
