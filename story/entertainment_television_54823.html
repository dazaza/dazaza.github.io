Introduction:

The hero Ji Cunxi (played by Johnny Yan) has the power to decide whether the island will be developed or not. However, the owner of the island only wants to keep this pure land and does not want it to be destroyed for profit. The owner came up with a crazy idea to "set a trap" for Cunxi in order to stop the development of the island... An ordinary office lady named Chen Xinyi (played by Ruby Lin), who is eager to tie down her handsome and upright boyfriend, finds herself on a love boat under unexpected circumstances. Her boyfriend invites her aboard, and she secretly plans to lose her virginity to him in order to bind him forever. But things don't go as planned - due to seasickness and excessive drinking, she ends up vomiting uncontrollably and mistakenly enters the wrong room... On that day, Cunxi was planning to propose to his girlfriend on the love boat. But the proposal failed, as his girlfriend didn't even board the ship. The owner of the island had drugged Cunxi's drink, making his mind unclear. He returned to his room, where Xinyi, already disoriented from seasickness, saw her boyfriend enter the room and decided to seize the opportunity... But fate has other plans, as Xinyi entered the wrong room, met the wrong person, and worse still, ended up pregnant...

This story begins with a certain type of girl that dominates all over Asia: the "sticky note girl". She is simple, unassuming, and content with small wishes. She has never been anyone special and doesn't expect to become one. Although her role is minor, she is indispensable, just like a sticky note that can be torn off at any time. She may not be noticeable or unique, but she is certainly around you! A "luxury cruise" trip allows such an ordinary girl to encounter the most extraordinary situation. Since childhood, Xinyi has always been afraid of others not liking her. So, she has always tried hard to be nice to others. Whenever someone gives her a little response, she easily likes them back. Everyone calls her a "sticky note girl" because when someone needs her, they can tear her off and use her. When no longer needed, she can be thrown away casually. But she is actually a sentimental fool, whose weakness makes her often soften when she should make a firm decision. One day, in order to win back her womanizing boyfriend Gucci, she spent a lot of travel expenses to take a romantic cruise. Unexpectedly, she mistakenly entered the wrong room and woke up to find herself having a one-night stand with a stranger. Moreover, she discovered that her boyfriend Gucci was cheating on her with a wealthy widow and left her behind, which dealt her a thunderous blow.

A month later, when Xinyi saw the result of the pregnancy test, she regretted not jumping into the sea on the ship. A 25-year-old law firm clerk, for a lousy boyfriend, is now 200,000 yuan in debt and carrying the child of an unknown man, making it the most ironic joke in the world. She took a week off from work and prepared to return to her hometown, Jiangmu Island, to figure out what to do.

That night, the stranger in bed was named Ji Cunxi. He is the ideal prince charming for all girls - rich, handsome, young, and talented. When he meets the ordinary office lady Chen Xinyi, it marks the start of either trouble or happiness.

Due to the genetic problems in the Ji family, each generation has had fewer male descendants. Up until now, there have been nine generations of single heirs. This has made Grandma Zhenzhu sleep restlessly every day, fearing that the only remaining spark of their lineage might accidentally extinguish! Grandma hopes that her grandson Ji Cunxi can be like other young entrepreneurs, sowing wild oats everywhere. Unfortunately, this grandson remains clean and self-disciplined. One day, Grandma accidentally discovers that Xinyi is carrying Cunxi's child. Perhaps blessed by the ancestors of the Ji family, heaven has truly bestowed upon her a "little golden grandson", leading Grandma to insist that Cunxi marry Xinyi. In order to complete Grandma's wish while she is still alive, Grandma threatens Cunxi by threatening to expose his three-year relationship with his girlfriend. Therefore, Cunxi reluctantly marries Xinyi, tricked into the marriage. However, Cunxi realizes that he cannot face the fact of getting married and is unsure whether he should run away or bravely bear the consequences. At this moment, they suddenly discover that they must start dating under Grandma's requirements.

In fact, Cunxi also has a girlfriend, Anna, a ballet dancer, with whom he has been in a relationship for three years. During this time, Cunxi proposed romantically multiple times, but Anna always gave him a soft rejection. Despite this, Cunxi quietly waited for Anna to let go of everything and agree to his proposal. Anna chose not to marry for her career, and at this time, Cunxi unexpectedly got Xinyi pregnant. Fortunately, Anna is on an international tour performance, allowing Cunxi to hide this awkward incident. However, Cunxi secretly signed a prenuptial agreement with Xinyi, clearly stating that after giving birth, they would divorce. Cunxi promised to give Xinyi a sum of money, and then they would part ways without owing each other anything. Xinyi, considering the future of her unborn child, seriously thought about it and agreed. Cunxi's plan was that after Xinyi gave birth to the child, when Anna's tour performances ended and she returned home, his contractual relationship with Xinyi would expire, and he could reunite with Anna.

Unaware of the situation, Xinyi moved into Cunxi's house and started learning how to live with Cunxi. The evolution of two strangers becoming accustomed to each other's presence is very subtle. Originally thinking they would just live under the same roof without much interaction, they gradually found themselves entangled in various daily trivial matters. Under Grandma's request, Xinyi moved into Cunxi's bedroom, so Cunxi found an excuse to sleep in the guest room. However, he began to dislike the loneliness of sleeping alone, and late at night, Cunxi always returned to the original room. Over time, having someone beside him while sleeping seemed to become a habit. Cunxi, who used to dislike eating at home, also started eating at home. Cunxi, who vowed not to help with anything, also started buying wallpapers for the baby's room and toys for children. Just when they thought this was the so-called happiness, the long-ignored and hidden bombshell, Anna, appeared. Cunxi never expected that his unclear relationship with Anna would hurt Xinyi. He left a divorce agreement and disappeared without a word. Thinking that this ending was the best conclusion, Xinyi had unknowingly occupied his heart. He swore that if he ever found Xinyi again, he would definitely chase her back, not as the father of the child chasing the mother, but as a man pursuing a woman...
