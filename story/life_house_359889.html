Xixi Tourism welcomes you.

by dragonsoft on 2010-07-04 13:07:58

The superiority of Xixi lies in its water. Water is the soul of Xixi, and about 70% of the park's area consists of river ports, ponds, lakes, marshes and other bodies of water. As the saying goes, "a winding stream with a wisp of smoke", the entire park features six rivers that cross each other. In between are distributed many tributaries and fish-scale shaped fish ponds that form the unique wetland scenery of Xixi.

The importance of Xixi lies in its ecology. To strengthen ecological protection, three major ecological reserves and restoration areas have been set up in the wetland - Feijia Tang, Xialong滩 Beach, Chaotianmu Yang. A wetland science popularization exhibition hall has been set up at the entrance, and there are three biological restoration pools and a wetland ecological viewing area within the park. Xixi is also a paradise for birds. There are many bird-watching pavilions set up in the park, presenting magnificent views of flocks of birds flying to visitors.

The culture of Xixi has a long history. Xixi has always been a place of seclusion, regarded by literati as a pure land on earth, a utopia beyond the mundane world. Qiuxue An, Bo'an, Meizhu Mountain Villa, and Xixi Cottage were all once private retreats established by numerous elegant scholars in history, who left behind a large number of poems and writings in Xixi. The ancient opera stage under the century-old camphor tree at Deep潭 Mouth is said to be the site of the first performance of artists from the northern school of Yue Opera.

The folk customs of Xixi are simple and honest. The dragon boat festival held every year at Deep潭 Mouth has a long history, with a unique form known as the "ornamental dragon boats". The "Xixi Family" and "Mulberry·Silkworm·Silk Story" near Yan Shui Fishing Village recreate the rural life and labor scenes of the original residents of Xixi, allowing more people to understand and learn about the typical folk customs of the waterside town.
