In recent days, a video titled "6-month-old Baby Cries Listening to 'Legend'" has become an internet sensation. This video records the expression changes of a little baby listening to the song "Legend" until the final legendary moment of quietly shedding tears. It has been frequently played on Weibo, Kaixin.com, and various video websites. A single repost on Tudou.com has already been viewed over 290,000 times. Netizens, besides being repeatedly amazed, are all filled with affection and blessings for the cute little one.

Baby becomes famous through "Legend"

This viral video was initially triggered by this year's hit song "Legend" and uploaded by a netizen named "Yoyo's Dad." In this 3-minute and 42-second video, a half-year-old baby named "Yoyo" is held facing the camera while the music of "Legend" sung by Wang Fei plays. The baby's facial expressions undergo fascinating changes as the melody progresses. Sometimes lost in thought, sometimes seemingly hurt, and finally shedding two clear tears.

Wang Fei herself forwarded this video on Sina Weibo, adding the comment "Nezha reincarnated, no doubt," which caused the video to be forwarded even more enthusiastically.

Many netizens left messages after watching the video:

"Dear baby, hope he grows up safe and happy."

"The little baby must have remembered past-life connections, that's why hearing this song evokes such deep resonance."

"I've watched it no less than five times. Seeing the baby like this really makes me feel it's incredible."

"I believe it's moved. Our baby has had moments like this too."

"Legend - our baby girl also listened very attentively and quietly, just didn't cry."

The original singer of "Legend," Li Jian, relatively showed much indifference in front of the enthusiastic netizens. "I've seen the 'Legend Baby' video; it was forwarded to me by Yao Chen. I think the video is quite interesting and brings a kind of joy, but that's all, don't take it too seriously. When I write songs, I've never thought about what reactions certain listeners might have; I just write according to my own wishes and aesthetics. I think the baby's reaction is just a coincidence, possibly moved by Wang Fei's voice, or possibly startled by the camera."

A netizen claimed to be the video uploader replied to everyone's attention: "I am the baby's mother, and my baby is a boy. We accidentally discovered his reaction to this song, so we filmed it and put it online so our relatives far away and grandpa could see. Unexpectedly, it caught the attention of so many friends. My Yoyo is actually a very happy baby who loves to laugh and rarely cries in life."
