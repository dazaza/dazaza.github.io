I'm so happy that my parents are with me.

by opde98169 on 2011-05-20 18:04:58

Today, my parents took me to visit the insect exhibition. I was so happy! Dad is really useless, he even got us lost. It's Armored Warrior炎Dragon侠's fault. We've been here before, but now many houses have been demolished and lots of new buildings are being constructed, so it's no wonder dad got lost.

There were so many insects in the exhibition area that I couldn't remember them all. I only knew there were bedbugs, spiders, rhinoceros beetles, butterflies, and they had many varieties. There was also a model of a big tiger. When I touched it, it let out an "ao ao" roar, which scared me! Unfortunately, I didn't bring a camera to take pictures! Dad bought me this butterfly specimen, which I picked myself. It glows at night, and I think it's very beautiful.

We also went to Li Lake New City to fly kites. It was very pretty there with lots of grasslands and such a huge Taihu Lake. The water column shooting up from the center of the lake was so high. Mom said it was a hundred meters!

It was time to fly kites. I chose an Ultraman kite. The uncle selling kites was very nice and gave me a bottle of bubbles too! Dad was responsible for getting the kite airborne. At first, dad wasn't doing well and couldn't get it up. Luckily, an old man nearby taught him, and finally, the kite soared into the sky. I insisted on holding the string, but dad was worried and kept telling me to hold it tightly. The wind was so strong that I almost couldn't hold it. I asked dad: Will I also fly into the sky with the kite? Both parents laughed, mom said I was so thin, I might actually be blown up into the sky. I was a bit scared and quickly handed the string back to dad. There were too many kites in the sky, and there was no more space. A black bat and a Teletubbies kite got tangled up with our Ultraman. When dad tried to untangle them, our Ultraman fell into Lake Taihu. What should we do? Parents said there was nothing we could do to retrieve it. I was very sad. Ultraman was alone in the lake, how lonely! I also saw some trees with kites stuck in them; they must have been dropped by other kids who were probably as sad as their kites. Later, mom told me that there would be uncles and aunts specially assigned to pick up the kites lost by the children at the end of the day and return them to the children. I told mom that next time, I will come again to find my Ultraman!

On the way home, mom said she would take me to Yuantouzhu next time. So I asked: Is it a pig with a round head? This made my parents laugh continuously. I was very curious, what's so funny? I even told my classmates like this!

When we got home, I gave the butterfly specimen to mom because mom said there is Mother's Day in May, and mom's birthday is also in May. I am going to prepare a surprise for mom, but I haven't decided what it will be yet!
