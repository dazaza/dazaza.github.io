ZHAO Peng emphasized that Dongtai City should rely on large enterprises as carriers and focus on major projects to promote the new round of economic development.

by cgccg001 on 2009-12-20 03:39:58

[Voiceover]: On March 17, Zhao Peng, Secretary of the Municipal Committee of the CPC and Director of the Standing Committee of the Municipal People's Congress, visited Dongtai City. Zhao Peng emphasized that Dongtai should further emancipate mind, give full play to its own advantages, seize opportunities, take large enterprises as carriers and big projects as handles, and promote the new round of economic development in Dongtai.

Mr. Zhao’s team first came to the Dongtai Economic Development Zone to inspect Jiangsu Desai Fiber Co., Ltd., Dongtai Leida Steel Cord Co., Ltd., and Shanghai Taisheng Electric Power Engineering Machinery Co., Ltd. He went deep into the front line of workshops to fully understand the relevant situations regarding production, sales, and new product development of these enterprises. When talking with the enterprise leaders, Mr. Zhao said that the fiber industry, wind power generation equipment markets are now changing unpredictably, with fierce competition both domestically and internationally. They need to accelerate progress, expand scale, grasp the timing, seize opportunities, use their own advantages to occupy similar industrial markets. Meanwhile, they also need to focus on talent cultivation, introduce more advanced technology, and promote project development.

During his stay in Dongtai, Mr. Zhao’s team also came to Shiyantown of Dongtai City, successively inspecting the construction site of the special steel industry gathering area under construction in Shiyantown and the metal cutting tool workshop of Dongtai Sublimation Tool Co., Ltd.

After listening to the work report from the main responsible comrades of Dongtai since November last year, Mr. Zhao pointed out that judging from the development momentum of large enterprises, major projects, development zone construction, city construction, and the development of characteristic industries in key towns this year in Dongtai City, the achievements in all aspects of work were remarkable. He emphasized that Dongtai should fully utilize its own advantages, take large enterprises as carriers, take major projects as handles, increase the intensity of work, broaden thinking, adopt more special measures, and increase the intensity of attracting investment. The work of key towns should focus on planning and form industrial characteristics. We must strongly promote the industrialization and urbanization of towns and villages. At the same time, we must earnestly think and find ways to increase farmers' income. Mr. Zhao also made requirements regarding city construction issues.

Dai Yuanhu, Member of the Standing Committee of the Municipal Committee and Secretary-General, accompanied the inspection.
