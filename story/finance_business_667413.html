The year 2012 will also be here soon, so what should the ladies do? In this slightly cool time, ladies can take inspiration from eight fashionable vest styling tricks, a great tool for trendy clothing combinations.

Pictures of clothing coordination: The softness or hardness of materials can convey different styles. Hard materials give off a strong and serious feel, while softer materials usually feel more casual and approachable.

Picture of clothing coordination: A strap-style badge vest brings out a naval style. The material mix of the straps and the main part of the vest adds dynamism, paired with khaki shorts to easily create a weekend leisure look.

Picture of clothing coordination: A yellow bubble-sleeve T-shirt with a standing-collar stud vest, youthful and fashionable. Pairing it with blue jeans is unremarkable, but the added belt feels like an over-accessorized touch. Actually, the look is already eye-catching enough without the belt.

Picture of clothing coordination: A khaki suit-style vest, sharp cuts and precise tailoring, mixed with a fringed silk scarf, cleverly blends femininity with a neutral style.

A luxurious black vest with gold-thread embroidery exudes a more extravagant air. Paired with a dark tank top and seven-eighths knot shorts, it brings a powerful presence. Even with a distracted expression, it cannot hide the efficiency and astuteness that radiates from within.

Fashion pictures: A rougher material vest with wooden decorative buttons and rounded hem designs add plenty of country charm, comfortable and pleasant. Mixed with darker narrow-brimmed straw hats, it’s likely a resort outfit favored by many street fashionistas. Jeans

Picture of clothing coordination: A loose long vest has a very stylish presence. For tall women, pairing it with a white T-shirt and a highly designed pant, such as pencil pants or harem pants, easily creates a high-turnaround neutral style look.

Picture of clothing coordination: A simple white T-shirt can easily exude elegance. In the days when floral prints dominate, when you're tired of looking at all the blossoms, a sudden burst of clean fabric always brings a good mood.
