The Maokanglin operating platform is becoming increasingly mature.

by maikaolin0111 on 2012-03-09 16:54:39

In the recent year, Mcullen has been continuously improving and upgrading the infrastructure services such as warehouse, logistics, customer service, etc., providing consumers with better and better online shopping experience. At present, Mcullen has established warehouses in Shanghai, Beijing, Guangzhou and Chengdu, and also built an operation center of more than 110,000 square meters in Wujiang, Jiangsu province, which is expected to be put into use in the spring of 2012. After the operation center starts up, Mcullen's warehouses will be able to accommodate 600,000 SKUs, and the maximum daily dispatch volume can reach 130,000 orders. Through strengthening management, the order fulfillment rate of Mcullen's warehouse reaches 99.98%, the complaint rate is less than 0.08%, the same-day shipping rate exceeds 99%, and the storage location accuracy exceeds 99.8%. In order to deliver goods to customers as soon as possible, Mcullen has made multiple speed-ups in 2011, launched measures such as air transport, land flight, and two deliveries per day. Currently, from placing an order to delivering to the customer's hand, it can reach the next day in Beijing, Shanghai, and Guangzhou, 2.3 days in provincial capital cities, and on average 2.8 days nationwide. In 2011, Mcullen realized cash-on-delivery and door-to-door return for cash in 1050 cities across the country. Through the online inquiry system and SMS notification system developed by Mcullen, customers can check the delivery progress of products on Mcullen's website, and Mcullen will also inform customers via SMS about the time goods arrive at each distribution node. Now Mcullen also offers night delivery, POS machine card swiping, and convenience store self-pickup services are also planned. In addition to the improvement of delivery speed, Mcullen has also formed a unique delivery culture. Customers can score delivery personnel to ensure the quality of the last mile delivery; Mcullen launches cartoon delivery personnel in Beijing, Shanghai, Guangzhou, and Shenzhen to bring surprises to customers while delivering goods; giving customers environmental protection bags, taking out the garbage for customers, calling for joint efforts to protect the earth, etc., fulfilling the company's social public welfare responsibilities. Mcullen's customer service answers customers' questions about products and services through various ways such as phone, email, and online replies. In order to improve service efficiency and quality, Mcullen has set up multiple evaluation standards to score service personnel. Key indicators include connection rate, effectiveness rate, single business processing duration, service attitude, service awareness, problem-solving ability, process execution accuracy rate, etc. Customers who shop online are most concerned about the inconsistency between pictures and actual items. To truly represent product information to the greatest extent, Mcullen has established a team including more than 20 top photographers, hundreds of Chinese and foreign models resources, as well as professional photo retouching, graphic design, planning, and copywriting teams. Providing multiple photos of real person shooting, detail pictures, fabric pictures, etc. of products, presenting the characteristics of products comprehensively. In the process of e-commerce transitioning from marketing wars and price wars to emphasizing products and services, Mcullen has taken the lead in this transition through investment and improvement in infrastructure.
