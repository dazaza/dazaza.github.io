The 2012 adult college entrance exam will be held on October 13-14.

by qindingdangx on 2012-03-06 15:38:56

According to the Hubei Education Examination Institute's [Hubei Education Examination Institute 2012 Examination Schedule], as reported by Zisheng Education [http://www.zsjykj.com/], the adult college entrance examination will take place on October 13-14, 2012.
