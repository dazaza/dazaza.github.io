Soaring against the trend, sector opportunities are coming.

by huaxun892 on 2010-04-20 16:54:47

In the repeated oscillation process at today's low point near 2950 points, except for the nonferrous metal and insurance sectors which showed some performance, most of the weighted stock sectors were in a state of oscillation adjustment throughout the day. However, after more than a week of oscillation adjustment, the previously highly active information technology sector probed the bottom during the session today and quickly rebounded collectively under the impetus of rapid rise or even direct limit-up of leading stocks such as Tsinghua Unisplendour, Tongfang Co., Ltd., and Huazhong Science & Technology. It eventually closed with a 4.30% increase, forming the largest bullish line in the daily chart in more than half a year. Does this surging bullish line indicate that the information technology sector has probed a phased bottom and will embark on a new round of upward trend? Below, we will briefly discuss this from aspects such as fundamentals and technical analysis.

From the fundamental perspective of the information technology industry, after getting through the relatively difficult year of 2009, the industry will welcome new development opportunities in 2010. First, the continuous recovery of domestic and foreign macroeconomic conditions provides a favorable development environment for the information technology industry. The increasing traditional informatization product demand from individuals and corporate consumers with continuously growing income offers a huge market space for the further recovery of the entire industry and even its progress towards a new prosperity peak. Second, the strategic position of the information technology industry in the future national emerging industry layout, industrial structure adjustment, and consumption structure adjustment has been further established, obtaining further strong support from the government in terms of policy and economy, thereby acquiring more social resources and development opportunities. Relevant government departments have continuously emphasized recently that the development of 3G should be accelerated, efforts should be made to achieve breakthroughs in sensor network construction and sensor technology development, thus deepening Internet applications, enhancing the level of socio-economic informatization, and applying electronic information and other high technologies and advanced applicable technologies to machinery, automobile, shipbuilding, rail transportation, CNC machine tools, aviation and other manufacturing industries, significantly improving the technological content and manufacturing level of industrial products. This not only greatly expands the market space for traditional information technology products but also extends their life cycle, which is conducive to the sustainable development of the entire industry. Moreover, since the Internet of Things, cloud computing, and the convergence of three networks have become universally recognized and highly pursued new technological trends worldwide, various policy preferences and support measures by the government will accelerate the development process of the information technology industry, a strategic emerging industry. New electronic information technologies and products will be continuously developed and promoted, providing enormous impetus and market space for the structural adjustment and industrial upgrading of the entire industry.

From the daily K-line chart of the information technology sector, since successfully probing the phased bottom on March 15th following the overall market, this sector has shown a sustained strong oscillatory upward trend. Although it has experienced certain oscillatory adjustments in the past two weeks due to market adjustments, its strong characteristics are still relatively prominent. Today, under the stimulation of relevant industry favorable news, it showed a strong oscillatory rising trend against the market trend. Although this sector saw a significant increase today, the trading volume during the rising process showed a certain degree of shrinkage, with less-than-ideal price-volume coordination. Considering factors such as the relatively limited short-term upward momentum of its leading stocks like Tongfang Co., Ltd. after continuous rises, the relatively high overall valuation of the sector, and the not-so-ideal medium-term technical patterns of most individual stocks within the sector, although this sector still has a certain amount of sustained upward momentum tomorrow, its short-term upward strength may be relatively weak. From the perspective of maximizing returns, prudent investors can mainly observe this sector tomorrow. If it first experiences significant oscillations tomorrow, aggressive investors can appropriately focus on and lightly accumulate the leading varieties within the sector, adopting quick-in quick-out short-term operations. Specifically, attention can be paid to the possible low-absorption opportunities during the short-term oscillations of individual stocks such as Tsinghua Unisplendour, Haihong Holding, Xinmao Technology, Hengdian Dongci, and Yushun Electronics.
