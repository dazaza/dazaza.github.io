Western Cowboy Game Machine Manual

Part One:

◆ Complete company table system, capable of remote telephone accounting, both the main unit and sub-units record accounts simultaneously, and power loss does not erase the accounts.

◆ The main unit displays real-time accounts, no need to stop the machine to check accounts. Just look at the account displayed on the main unit for shift handover.

◆ All music used is popular CD music, and can be selected whether to play through the main unit, convenient for different needs.

◆ Only one wire is needed to connect 8 machines, greatly improving the stability of the machine.

◆ New program combining 12-door animal connection and 12-door racing side line programs, eight people playing the fishing machine.

◆ This machine uses Chinese LCD display, all checks and adjustments have Chinese prompts, simple and easy to understand, convenient operation. Stable and reliable, fun to play, and with profit guarantee.

Part Two: Connection Usage Method

◆ After connecting the communication lines in order, start from machine number 8 and turn on the power one by one until the power of machine number 1 is turned on. The main unit is located in machine number 1. Alternatively, you can turn on the power of each of the 8 machines and the main unit simultaneously. If coin insertion and return are not required, please unplug the return coin key line on the control panel and seal the coin slot.

Part Three: Main Unit Usage Method

◆ For normal use, just connect the communication line and turn on the power. During normal use, the main unit displays the running status. In the betting process, the top row shows the remaining betting time before the lights start running, and the bottom row shows the total profit. During the running light process and winning process, the top row shows the remaining running time (i.e., how much time is left until coding, in minutes). It's best to code before the remaining 1000 minutes are up, to avoid blocking the machine during play. The bottom row also shows the total profit. During normal operation, if a shift change is needed, just record the total profit shown on the bottom row for clear settlement. Since this machine adopts the most advanced and reliable method currently available, verification is quite strict. Therefore, before changing shifts, ensure that there is no scoring or deduction for two rounds so that the main unit can accurately record all sub-unit accounts.

◆ To report and check accounts, first enter the menu state. There are two ways to enter the menu: ① While the machine is running, press the "function key", it will show "waiting to return". After one round ends, it will enter the menu. ② In case of a fault with the main unit, hold down the function key while turning on the power to directly enter the menu. After entering the menu, there are four options: "Check Accounts", "Report Accounts", "Parameters", "System". Press the "Select" key to move the cursor. When the cursor points to the function you need, press confirm to enter that function. (Be sure to note: To avoid unnecessary trouble, ensure all sub-units must have their power on and properly connected before entering the menu.)

◆ If the main unit's LCD has backlight but no display or shadow appears and the color is too dark to see clearly, open the back cover of the main unit. On the main unit board, there is a color adjustment switch. Clockwise is lighter, counterclockwise is darker.

◆ After entering the account checking, you can choose to check the main unit or sub-unit accounts. Use the "Move" key to select either the main unit or sub-unit accounts. After confirming, you can enter. After entering the main unit, you can check all accounts of the main unit. The first page shows the line number on top and the machine number below. Confirm to enter the total profit (the sum of all sub-units' profits), confirm again to enter the total coins inserted (all sub-units' coins inserted + last time), confirm again to enter the total coins returned (all sub-units' coins returned + deduction), confirm again to enter the current profit (profit from last barcode scanning to now, i.e., total profit on page 2 - total profit on page 6 from last time), confirm again to enter the previous total profit (total profit of all sub-units when the last barcode was scanned), confirm again to enter the previous actual profit (actual profit when the last barcode was scanned), confirm again to enter the operating time (cumulative operating time from last barcode scanning to now), confirm again to enter the number of times the barcode was scanned (total number of barcode scans), confirm again to enter the machine number (for convenient maintenance and reporting, each group of machines has a total payout, which is the total number of awards given by all sub-units, the next 6 pages are backup codes, not yet in use, press confirm twice to return to the total profit, ending the entire host account checking. Press return to exit the main unit account checking. Note: There is a lot of information recorded here to make it easier to settle accounts during cooperation. If you cannot fully understand everything, just recording the total profit is sufficient.

◆ Use the "Move" key to select sub-unit account checking. After confirming, you can enter. After entering the sub-unit, input the sub-unit number you want to check to view the corresponding sub-unit accounts, including the total profit, total coins inserted, and total coins returned for each sub-unit. Press return to exit.

◆ After entering the account reporting, it shows the total profit of all sub-units from the last reset on page 00, the actual profit of all sub-units from the last barcode scan on page 03, the total number of barcode scans on page 07, the machine number on page 08, and the verification code on page 15. Finally, it prompts to input the barcode. Inputting the correct barcode successfully reports the account. However, after entering the account reporting, all sub-units also enter the account reporting state, showing 76 on all sub-units. At this point, it is not allowed to press return to exit. All sub-units and the main unit must be turned off to exit the account reporting.

◆ After entering parameters, you can set: Running light card rate (first press delete to delete the original card rate, then input the desired card rate using the number keys. Minimum 00.00, maximum 99.9. Factory default is 94.0. Note: Decimal points must be displayed.) Card rate ratio (not used here), Coin ratio (choose by pressing the delete key. Options include 1 coin = 100 points, 1 coin = 1 point, 1 coin = 2 points, 1 coin = 5 points, 1 coin = 10 points, 1 coin = 20 points, 1 coin = 50 points, 1 coin = 80 points. Factory default is 1 coin = 10 points. Note: Scoring ratio is 10 times the coin ratio.) Maximum bet points (choose by pressing the delete key. Options include 99 points, 50 points, 95 points. Factory default is 99 points.) Bet points ratio (how many points per bet. Choose by pressing the delete key. Options include 1 point, 2 points, 5 points, 10 points. Factory default is 1 point.), Start-up music (whether to sing after winning. Choose by pressing the delete key. Options include on and off. Factory default is on. If singing after winning is not needed, choose off.), Size multiplier (choose between large multiplier 4-70x or small multiplier 4-46x. Choose by pressing the delete key. Factory default is small multiplier.) Bet time (minimum 15 seconds, maximum 60 seconds. First press delete to delete the original time, then input the desired time using the number keys. Factory default is 30 seconds.) Note: All default parameters are set according to the majority market needs. If there are no special requirements, please do not modify them. Because modifying any parameter requires the main unit to be connected to all sub-units, and the accounts must be reported twice to reset (refer to the account reporting instructions for resetting methods).

◆ Entering the system requires the manufacturer password, which will not be introduced here.

◆ Holding down the test key is inconvenient. Turn on the main unit power and input the sub-unit number to test the communication status between the main unit and sub-units. The top shows the total communication attempts, and the bottom shows the error count. If there are errors, check the communication connection lines.

Part Four: Fault Clearing

◆ Main unit fault: Internal error, report accounts twice and reset to eliminate most faults. Code 08 indicates reaching the highest account limit, report accounts twice and reset. Code 09 indicates reaching the time limit, report accounts once.

◆ Sub-unit fault: If a fault code starting with 4 appears, such as 40, 41, etc., it is a connection fault. First, check if there are duplicate sub-unit numbers, if the connection lines are disconnected, or if the communication port on the computer board is unplugged. If all above are normal, just turn on the power for this sub-unit and others, then report accounts twice and reset. If there are duplicate sub-unit numbers, hold down K3 of this sub-unit, turn on the power, press the start key to flip to page 8, then press the return coin key to modify the sub-unit number. Finally, reconnect all sub-units to the main unit, report accounts twice from the main unit and reset (sub-units should be normally connected and powered on). If a sub-unit or the main unit has been sent back to the factory for repair, after receiving it back, when re-connecting, the main unit must report accounts twice and reset (sub-units should be normally connected and powered on).

◆ Sub-unit faults can be handled in the following steps: ① Check coin insertion/return switches for codes 30, 31. ② If no problem, hold down K0+K3 or the score key+wash key, then turn on the power. A six-player push-coin machine will display 66, and the sub-unit resets. ③ If still not resolved, check if the fault code starts with 4. If so, check if the communication connection line is faulty. ④ If confirmed no connection issue, check for duplicate sub-unit numbers. ⑤ If none found, connect all sub-units to the main unit, then turn on the power and continuously report accounts twice from the main unit to reset. Note: For convenience in repairing sub-units, most sub-unit faults do not require power-off repairs. We specially designed a hot start switch K2. Under sub-unit fault conditions, just press K2 to restart the sub-unit. However, K2 has no effect if the sub-unit has no fault. Of course, you can also power off/on to resolve.

Part Five: Account Reporting Instructions

◆ First, press the function key to enter the menu, use the move key to select account reporting, confirm to enter account reporting. Report the complete short messages or phone calls for pages 00, 03, 07, 08 (panda machine), and 15. Finally, it prompts to "input barcode". After receiving the barcode, input the correct barcode to successfully report the account. Note: If the account is not correctly reported, the barcode cannot be calculated or entered.

◆ If a reset is needed, repeat the above account reporting process to reset. Note: During the reset, sub-units cannot score or deduct points.

◆ When the main unit cannot check accounts due to some reason, all sub-unit accounts can be checked. Sub-unit account checking method: Hold down the K0 key, or open the scoring lock and hold down the scoring key, then turn on the power. Page 0 shows total profit, page 1 shows total coins inserted, page 2 shows total coins returned, page 3 shows current actual profit, press the start key to flip pages. Then add up all sub-unit accounts, which equals the main unit account.

◆ Special Note: For sub-unit account checking, if the JP light is on, it represents a negative number. The JP light is located between the total score column and the score column.

◆ Additionally, when the main unit encounters faults like 06, 01, etc., the stored main unit and sub-unit accounts in the main unit may be incorrect. In this case, the accounts of each sub-unit must be taken as the standard.
