Ultra Electronics (000823) (Enter the stock bar, new version of market conditions): The company's main business is the research, development, production, and sales of printed circuit boards, LCD displays, ultrasonic electronic instruments, and ultra-thin and special copper-clad laminates.

Anticipating the added value from 3G mobile phone components and high-end communication products: Considering that the traditional products of the company are already at full production capacity, the growth in performance mainly comes from new technologies, new products, and product structure optimization. The gross margin of traditional PCBs will continue to rise. The release of production capacity for 3G product components and the increase in the proportion of high-end communication products means that the company currently has a production capacity of 1.2 million square meters for two-layer HDI boards used in 3G mobile phones and data cards. It is expected that two-layer HDI boards will be extensively applied this year in 3G mobile phones, smartphones, and data card products. Therefore, with the continuous increase in shipments in the high-end communication market, the utilization rate of two-layer HDI board production capacity will also rise, ensuring a stable profit margin and providing stable performance contributions to the company.

New patent technology supports future performance growth: The company’s capacitive touch screen technology is currently applying for a patent. The produced touch screens have a higher cost-performance ratio compared to similar products, with a promising market competitiveness outlook. Currently, the profitability of small-size LCD displays will significantly improve, and the downward price trend has basically ended; we await the acceleration of price recovery. A significant price increase in large-size LCD panels this year will gradually be passed on to small-size LCD displays. It is estimated that LCD displays will generate profits of at least 20 million yuan in the second half of the year. Meanwhile, the company is gradually expanding its touch screen production capacity, and small-scale shipments have already begun. With increased investment in touch screens, there is a strong possibility of rapid development in the next couple of years.

Earnings forecast: The EPS for 2009-2011 are 0.27 yuan, 0.36 yuan, and 0.45 yuan respectively. The corresponding dynamic P/E ratios are 28 times, 21 times, and 17 times, while the dynamic P/B ratios are 2.12, 1.98, and 1.83 times respectively.

In the secondary market, the stock has been in a long-term process of building a large momentum platform. In recent days, it rebounded along with the overall market, with ideal volume-price coordination and a well-maintained moving average system. It is expected that under the expectation of future good performance, the stock may experience an upward trend. We suggest investors pay close attention.

The procedures for buying and selling stocks are as follows: When trading stocks, you must specify whether you are buying or selling, including the name (or code), quantity, and price of the stock. This order is only valid on the day it is placed. The contents of the order include the abbreviation (code) of the stock you want to buy or sell, the quantity, and the price. The abbreviation of the stock usually consists of three to four Chinese characters, and the code is six digits. When placing an order, the code and abbreviation of the stock must be consistent. At the same time, there are certain regulations regarding the quantity of stocks being traded: The quantity of stocks ordered for purchase must be a multiple of 100, but the quantity for selling does not need to be a multiple of 100.

There are four methods of placing orders: Counter submission, telephone automatic order, computer automatic order, and remote terminal order.
