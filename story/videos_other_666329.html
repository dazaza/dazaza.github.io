Back in 2002, I had just started doing SEO for not too long when one day I posted on a famous IT community forum saying that SEO would become a rapidly growing industry. Not long ago, I saw this guy talk about this matter on Weibo, saying that he regretted it deeply after seeing the development of the SEO industry.

Two years ago, I wrote an article analyzing the Taobao SEO market, saying that this market has a very promising future. Then I formed a Taobao SEO team, introduced several Taobao SEO experts, trained hundreds of professional Taobao SEO personnel, and currently the Taobao SEO market is starting to develop, increasingly rapidly.

However, in my view, today's Taobao SEO is like search engine optimization ten years ago, still in its infancy, with super huge market opportunities. Why?

Let's look at the data:

A. Two years ago, when I wrote that article, there were only 2.58 million stores on Taobao, and at the time there wasn't a single professional company or organization specializing in Taobao SEO.

B. Now, Taobao already has 5.86 million stores, the number of stores has doubled. The number of companies professionally offering Taobao SEO services is still no more than 10, and most are still in the exploratory phase.

C. Ten years ago, there were only a few people in China working in the SEO industry. But now? There are at least tens of thousands of practitioners, and all companies are hiring SEO personnel.

D. Therefore, within three years, all Taobao stores will be hiring professional Taobao SEO personnel, and various Taobao SEO service organizations will spring up like mushrooms after a rain.

Choice is more important than effort! Choosing a fast-growing market naturally makes success easier. Remember Lei Jun's words: When the tornado comes, pigs can fly!

Since the Taobao SEO market has such huge space, how should a novice study the Taobao SEO algorithm?

1. Avoid falling into technical pitfalls

For search engine optimization, many people have fallen into technical pitfalls, learning more complex things makes their heads bigger; if you study the Taobao SEO algorithm, avoid falling into this pitfall from the start.

2. Study directly from the essence

In any field, simplicity is key. As long as we study from the essence, everything will become simple. My article two years ago already outlined the research approach for Taobao SEO. The principles are as follows:

Principle One: Must consider the buyer's confusion

Improvements to the Taobao internal search ranking algorithm are all aimed at satisfying shopping users, in order to give shopping users a better experience. Studying user needs and satisfying user needs is the core. Taobao also improves its search algorithm around this aspect. For example, incorporating service quality as a ranking factor stems from this consideration.

Principle Two: Must consider Taobao's revenue

Another purpose of improving the Taobao algorithm is to reasonably increase Taobao's income. Therefore, when studying Taobao SEO, we must consider Taobao's interests. For example, products with higher sales conversion rates gain relatively higher weight advantages because Taobao calculates its return on investment.

Principle Three: Reasonable optimization of Taobao shops

This is like optimizing a website. You need to consider keyword placement in over a dozen details such as shop settings and product descriptions in order to obtain more ranking opportunities.

Actually, the above approach is the same as my method for studying Baidu:

1. Study what kind of websites or web pages users like, and consider how search engines might judge them programmatically;

2. Must consider Baidu's interests in order to gain higher weight and stable rankings on Baidu;

3. Reasonably optimize the website, integrating SEO with user experience. Optimizing without leaving traces is the highest level.

Remember: Always stay true to the fundamentals!

Remember the above principles, analyze calmly, practice earnestly, summarize repeatedly, and your SEO skills will naturally improve.
