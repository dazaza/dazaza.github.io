At around 7 p.m. on February 5, a netizen from Jinan, Shandong Province named Li Xiaoya posted on Weibo that he found a band-aid in the Tangyuan (sweet glutinous rice balls) produced by Sientang, which made him feel very nauseous during the holiday! The black sesame + band-aid combination is as nauseating as it can get! From the pictures attached to the Weibo post, one can see a black strip-like object in the dark filling of the Tangyuan, forming a stark contrast with the white skin of the Tangyuan. (Click on the picture to enter Li Xiaoya's Weibo)

By 6 p.m. yesterday, this Weibo post had been forwarded more than 11,000 times and received over 1,000 comments. Yesterday was the Lantern Festival, and many netizens said that after reading this Weibo post, they lost their appetite and mood.

Yesterday afternoon, a Beijing netizen named Cartoon98 revealed through Weibo: In the fresh pork and cabbage dumplings produced by Sientang, bought at 4 p.m. on February 4 at the Easy Lotte supermarket in Beijing's Liuliqiao area, two pieces of foam of different sizes were found. The pictures attached to the Weibo post show a yellow and white block-like object wrapped in the dumpling filling. Cartoon98 hopes that companies producing problematic food will be severely punished.

Response

Investigations are ongoing and results will be announced

Regarding the incident of finding a band-aid in Sientang Tangyuan, Zhengzhou Sientang Food Company responded via Weibo yesterday that they have already contacted the consumer through their official Weibo account. Currently, the consumer has replied to us, and we have also gotten in touch with Mr. Li (the blogger's father). We will complete the investigation and give everyone an answer. The company stated through Weibo that they will definitely inform the public of the results and process, and if there are any issues, they will not shirk responsibility.

Regarding the incident of finding foam in Sientang dumplings, Sientang's official Weibo account replied to Tianfu Morning Post: We have just obtained the phone number of the consumer's representative and will immediately arrange for someone to handle this matter. Thank you all for your supervision.

No problem products found in Chengdu

Yesterday, reporters randomly visited several large shopping malls and supermarkets in Chengdu city, where many citizens were purchasing Sientang brand Tangyuan. On the shelves, there were numerous varieties of Sientang Tangyuan. However, in the few supermarkets visited by the reporter, no Sientang black sesame big Tangyuan products containing band-aids, as claimed online, were found.

In interviews, many citizens said they were unaware of the incident. "If encountered, there's nothing much we can do, after all, it's only a small number," said Ms. Li, a citizen.

A staff member of the frozen food section at Isetan department store said that if customers find problems with the quality and bring it to the mall's attention, after confirming the situation to be true, the mall will consider exchanging or removing the product from the shelves.

Party involved says

Neither side wants to let this go. We hope that everyone's efforts will push relevant departments to strengthen supervision of food enterprises, and severely punish enterprises producing problematic food.

Company response

We will certainly ensure good internal coordination and inform the public of the results and process. If there are any problems with Sientang, we will not shirk responsibility. Please believe in our sincerity.

Finding band-aids in Tangyuan and foam in dumplings follows the revelation last December that Sientang dumplings contained Staphylococcus aureus, which in severe cases could lead to sepsis. On February 5 and 6, netizens continuously exposed various issues with Sientang brand products on Weibo.

Yesterday, Zhengzhou Sientang Food Company responded that they would arrange personnel to handle the matter. Tianfu Morning Post reporters visiting some malls within Chengdu city found that the sales of Sientang Tangyuan were normal. Some malls indicated that after confirming the situation to be true, they would consider exchanging or removing the product from the shelves.
