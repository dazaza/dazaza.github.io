At 20 years old, there is usually no problem with dark spots, but prevention is very important. First, you need to understand the cause of pigmentation. Melanin can be absorbed by keratin protein cells and gradually rises to the surface of the skin during cell renewal. If it is not removed at this time, over time it will form dark spots. In addition, if your water and oil secretion becomes unbalanced, causing excessive oil secretion that clogs pores, it will make your face look dull and lifeless.

Although your skin has good metabolism, the old keratin and melanin accumulated on the surface of the skin need to be removed daily. The so-called removal refers to choosing appropriate cleansing products to target the removal of melanin and dead skin on the surface of the skin. When the dead skin is severe, you can also use a gentle scrub to improve the dull skin texture.

In addition, remember that only by doing sun protection every day is the most direct and lasting whitening solution. Besides regular sunscreen, some lotions containing SPF sun protection index can also make it more convenient for you to protect your skin every day.
