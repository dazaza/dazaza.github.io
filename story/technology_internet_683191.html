Chutian Jinchao (Weibo) [http://weibo.com/chutianjin?zw=news] News □ Text and photos by reporter Li Guangzheng, correspondent Liu Ling

At around 12:30 AM on February 23, a shocking scene unfolded at the Huaron Police Station in Huaron District, Ezhou City: three blood-covered men were tightly holding onto another man as they stumbled into the station. After understanding the situation, the on-duty officers were deeply moved. An old officer with over 30 years of experience excitedly handed over a towel and said emotionally: [I have never seen police officers who are so willing to risk their lives like you.]

The three men were detectives Tang Xingbo, Rao Haifeng, and Wen Xing from the Macheng Public Security Bureau. While treating their wounds, the doctor discovered that Tang Xingbo’s left cheek had been bitten through and there was a large gash on his forehead; Rao Haifeng's right wrist had been slashed by a dagger; and Wen Xing's left finger was fractured... The female nurse treating the wounds had tears in her eyes, imagining how intense the arrest must have been, how brutal the criminal was, and how courageous our police officers were.

A gunshot shocked the small town of Macheng

In Chengma Town, Macheng City, where people live peacefully with simple folk customs, however, the gunfire late at night on December 29 last year shattered the decades-long tranquility of this small town. At around 10 PM that night, a serious gun-related murder and robbery case occurred at a Hu family residence in Hujiadun Village. More than 20 men carrying several hunting rifles, machetes, and fish spears stormed into the Hu residence, injuring seven people and stealing over 70,000 yuan in cash.

The next morning, the Macheng Public Security Bureau received a report from the Wuhan police [several men injured by gunfire in Macheng were being treated in Wuhan]. Subsequently, the Macheng police quickly established an investigation team. After several days of arduous investigation, they learned that the case was actually due to two groups of organized crime factions from Xinzhou competing for control of a casino, inviting other smaller gangs to participate in the conflict.

On February 4, suspect Li Mou, who initiated the incident, surrendered to the Macheng Public Security Bureau. According to Li's confession, he had previously co-owned a casino with Guo Mou and Lei Mou. Later, Lei Mou independently set up a casino in Chengma Town. Li invited Jiang Mou and others to carry out retaliation against the Hu family. Subsequently, the police confiscated three homemade rifles used by Jiang Mou and others during the attack in Xinzhou. Gunman Jiang Mou fled and was listed as a key target for capture by the police.

Jiang Mou is 21 years old, originally from Huaron, Ezhou. His father passed away early, and his mother remarried shortly after, leaving him to be raised by his grandfather. From a young age, Jiang Mou exhibited a violent temper and had been dealt with multiple times by the police. He has a criminal record in Ezhou, Wuhan, Xiaogan, and other places. Jiang Mou's methods of committing crimes are cruel, and he possesses a certain degree of anti-investigation and anti-capture skills. In this case, Jiang Mou personally invited many others, armed himself with a five-shot rifle, led the group, and directly shot at people upon entering the scene, intending to kill them.

Tracing leads, the "gunman" comes to light

To capture Jiang Mou, on February 10, the special task force formed a dedicated team consisting of Rao Haifeng, head of the anti-organized crime unit of the criminal investigation team, deputy head Wen Xing, and deputy head of the network monitoring team Tang Xingbo. On February 21, the police determined through technical investigative means that Jiang Mou had appeared at someone surnamed Xia's home in Ezhou. The three police officers quickly arrived in Ezhou and, with the assistance of local police, found Mr. Xia. Xia stated that on the morning of February 19, Jiang Mou had driven a green Mitsubishi jeep and appeared in Huaron, Ezhou, and left the following afternoon.

The police officers decided to "track the vehicle and monitor the person," setting up ambushes in places Jiang Mou was most likely to go.

At around 11 PM the next day, the three police officers set up surveillance near Jiang Mou's home when they spotted a green Mitsubishi jeep parked in the corner of an alley. "Jiang Mou is very likely at home!" As the three police officers were about to approach the Mitsubishi jeep, Rao Haifeng suddenly had a premonition, "Is he sleeping in the car?" Rao Haifeng immediately signaled his teammates with gestures, and the three surrounded the Mitsubishi jeep in a combat formation from both sides. Looking up, they saw a person lying on the passenger seat playing with a mobile phone. With the dim light from the phone, the police officers identified the person as Jiang Mou.

Bloody Capture - Courage Prevails in a Close Encounter

At the same time, Jiang Mou also noticed the police outside the car. Courage prevails in close encounters, and the three police officers simultaneously pulled open the car door while shouting loudly, "Don't move! We are the police!" Enraged, Jiang Mou pulled out a dagger from his body and forcefully pushed open the passenger-side door. Tang Xingbo and Wen Xing quickly moved forward to intercept him. Jiang Mou stabbed fiercely toward Wen Xing's chest. Wen Xing dodged and, taking advantage of the momentum, threw himself and Tang Xingbo onto Jiang Mou, pinning him to the ground. However, Jiang Mou, with excellent physical fitness, rolled on the ground and immediately got up, fleeing aimlessly toward the fields beside the road. The three police officers chased closely behind.

"Absolutely cannot let the suspect escape!" Rao Haifeng and the other three tightly focused on Jiang Mou's shadow, relentlessly pursuing him. After chasing nearly one kilometer, the three police officers finally forced Jiang Mou into a dead-end corner behind a fence wall. Seeing no way out, Jiang Mou brandished the dagger and yelled wildly, "Whoever comes closer, I'll stab them to death!"

None of the special task force members showed fear. Tang Xingbo rushed forward and hugged Jiang Mou around the waist, while Rao Haifeng and Wen Xing moved forward to seize the dagger. The almost frenzied Jiang Mou resisted fiercely, stabbing furiously at the two officers in front of him. Rao Haifeng and Wen Xing's arms and wrists were successively slashed. The four wrestled and tumbled in the slippery mud. Unable to break free from the officers' control, Jiang Mou viciously bit Tang Xingbo's cheek and tore a brick from the fence wall to strike Tang Xingbo's head. Blood flowed profusely from Tang Xingbo's face and head, blurring his vision, but he endured the excruciating pain and held onto Jiang Mou tightly until Rao Haifeng and Wen Xing managed to handcuff Jiang Mou, subduing this ruthless criminal completely.
