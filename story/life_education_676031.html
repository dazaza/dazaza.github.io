Admission Prospectus

The accounting practitioner qualification certificate, also known as the "accountant上岗certificate," is a qualification certificate for individuals with certain accounting professional knowledge and skills to engage in accounting work. It is the basic minimum requirement and prerequisite condition for engaging in accounting work, the only legal proof that one can perform accounting work, the "access card" to enter an accounting position, and the mandatory path to engage in accounting work. It is a qualification certificate, the "上岗certificate" for accounting work, and it is not graded.

Nature of the accounting exam:

The accountant上岗qualification management follows the principle of territoriality. The Jiangsu Province accounting practitioner qualification examination implements a province-wide unified organization, unified examination time, unified examination questions, and unified pass standard examination system.

Accountant上岗certificate registration requirements:

Online registration is implemented. Candidates must fill out the "Accounting Practitioner Qualification Examination Tutoring Registration Form" in detail when registering and provide two recent unadorned 1-inch photos (same base version) and the original and copies of ID cards and other relevant documents.

Accountant上岗certificate examination subjects and times:

1. Accounting Basics; 2. Financial Regulations and Accounting Professional Ethics; 3. Primary Accounting Computerization.

All three subjects—Accounting Basics, Financial Regulations and Accounting Professional Ethics, and Accounting Computerization—are taken via computer-based exams. Candidates must simultaneously register for the three subject examinations: Accounting Basics, Financial Regulations and Accounting Professional Ethics, and Primary Accounting Computerization. Graduates of accounting-related majors within two years of graduation are exempt from taking the Accounting Basics and related courses exams.

By convention, Accounting Basics and Financial Regulations & Accounting Professional Ethics are usually held in mid-November each year, while Accounting Computerization is held in mid-September each year. Specific dates will be confirmed on the admission ticket!

The accounting practitioner qualification certificate is uniformly designed by the Ministry of Finance, with a unified numbering system. The provincial finance department is responsible for the printing, numbering, issuance, and management of the certificates, which are valid nationwide.
