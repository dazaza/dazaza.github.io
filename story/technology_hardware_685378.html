Captain who died while rescuing mother and child jumping into the river was posthumously honored as a martyr

by xue94fwsh on 2012-03-01 15:00:18

On November 23, the provincial Department of Civil Affairs approved and agreed to posthumously recognize Li Fugen as a martyr after a group in the restaurant was overheard by the police while discussing major offenses. Yesterday morning, Chen Bitian, member of the Standing Committee of the Sanshui District Party Committee and Minister of Social Work, delivered a "Revolutionary Martyr Certificate" issued by the Ministry of Civil Affairs to Li Fugen's family.

The next morning at around 11 o'clock, Chen Haoming, the Deputy Secretary of the Sanshui District Party Committee, on behalf of the Sanshui District Party Committee and government, visited Li Fugen's home for condolences. The Sanshui District Justice and Bravery Fund rewarded 100,000 yuan. On November 18, Li Yiwei, the Secretary of the Foshan Municipal Party Committee, and acting mayor Liu Yuelun gave 200,000 yuan in justice and bravery rewards to Li Fugen's family. Regarding Li Fugen's righteous actions, Li Yiwei repeatedly said three times "remarkable," and required that the Sanshui District and Lubei Town help this family through difficulties.

According to the Southern Metropolis Daily report, yesterday, the memorial service for Foshan's "Hero Captain" Li Fugen was held at the Sanshui Funeral Home, with more than 500 people attending to bid farewell. On November 15, the 54-year-old Li Fugen heroically saved a mother and child who jumped from a boat but unfortunately perished. The Guangdong Provincial Department of Civil Affairs has recently posthumously recognized Li Fugen as a martyr. It is understood that Li Fugen will be buried in the martyrs' cemetery plot in Sanshui District, and his dependents will receive martyr commendation funds and pensions.

At 1 p.m. on November 15, Li Fugen started the "Yue Sanshui Du 2009" ferry at the Lubei water gate ferry terminal in Sanshui. About ten minutes into the journey, just a hundred meters from the shore, a woman sitting at the front of the boat suddenly jumped into the Bei River with her four-year-old child.

Captain Li Fugen jumped into the river without even taking off his shoes to save them. However, the jumping woman struggled fiercely and pushed Li Fugen away multiple times. Passenger Lu Weiliang also jumped into the river to save them but was similarly pushed away by the woman. After struggling for some time, the woman and the child sank into the water. Captain Li Fugen disappeared while swimming back to the shore, and only Lu Weiliang managed to get ashore. That afternoon, the body of the jumping woman surfaced, and the next morning, the body of the four-year-old boy was found. It took 46 hours before Li Fugen's body was finally salvaged ashore.
