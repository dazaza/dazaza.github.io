Lip line and lip red effect

Currently, "medical aesthetics" indeed carries out this kind of work, mainly targeting the lip red or lip red line that is not neat due to congenital factors, damage, deficiency, too thick or too thin lip red, or other morphological or color defects. The lip line or lip red is used to make it less visible.

Suitable population for lip line

1. Lip red line is unclear or misplaced after trauma scars

2. Poor alignment or scarring of the lips after cleft lip repair surgery

3. People with indistinct, irregular, or misaligned red lips who want to enhance the lip line for a more three-dimensional and aesthetically pleasing effect

4. Unattractive shape wanting to correct thick, large, small lips, crying type lips, drooping lip shape, or indistinct lip peaks through lip line

Contraindications for lip line surgery:

1. Skin diseases, infectious diseases, allergic constitution, scar constitution

2. Infection on the lips

3. Abnormal mental state or psychiatric patients

4. Hesitant or overly demanding, relatives disagreeing with the patient

Principles for lip line:

1. The lip line should be smooth and neat, with distinct contours, so the lip contour line should be sketched before the surgery. First, five marking points should be marked: lip valley (center point), lip peak (two highest points on both sides), and lip slope (two over-marking points) to check if the heights and distances on both sides are equal. The thickness of the upper and lower lips is usually about 8:10 millimeters.

2. Lip line can change an unsatisfactory lip shape, making small lips bigger, big lips smaller, thick lips thinner, and thin lips thicker. This can be achieved by expanding or shrinking the existing red lip edge during the lip line procedure.

3. The principle of lip line should be lighter rather than darker, narrower rather than wider.

4. The shape and color choice of lip line should vary according to age, skin color, occupation, and temperament.

What precautions should be taken for lip line:

1. Instruments used should be strictly sterilized to avoid infection and other infectious diseases. Antibiotics should be paid attention to after surgery, while reducing various stimuli.

2. Rich in nerve endings and capillaries, highly sensitive, hence surface anesthesia is required during the surgery to prevent pain causing lip tremors affecting surgical operation.

3. Loose tissue in the lips, lip line liquid easily causes diffusion, leading to uneven color, forming patchy changes, once such situation occurs, it's hard to correct.

4. It has certain limitations, restricting changes in lip color, and lacks luster. Moreover, these lip line liquids contain certain harmful ingredients, long-term stimulation may cause adverse effects, leading to other lesions.

A hospital or beauty salon with complete medical equipment and technical conditions should be carefully selected for lip line procedures. To reshape a beautiful and charming lip.

Psychological care for lip line:

Some patients undergoing lip line procedures may experience abnormal psychological phenomena due to internal or external factors, such as being overly suspicious, depressed, or criticized by friends and family, which severely affects their physical and mental health. Therefore, doing a good job of consultation and explanation is very important.

Daily care for lip line:

1. Apply intermittent cold compress within 24 hours after surgery, use antibiotics, and apply locally.

2. Avoid hot, spicy, and scalding foods for 7 days after surgery. Eat more fruits and vegetables.

3. Rinse mouth with diluted salt water after meals, keep oral and lip cleanliness. Let the scabs fall off naturally, do not peel them off manually.

Local reactions to lip line:

After lip line procedures, some patients may experience burning, itching, and mild tension around the lips 3-5 days later. Subsequently, red spots appear, followed by clusters of small papules or small blisters the size of millet grains. Some may have a single blister the size of a soybean. After the blisters burst, they dry up and form scabs, healing in about a week. Patients should be advised not to scratch and drink more water and eat more fruit. Pay attention to oral and wound hygiene. When cleaning the wound, gentamicin and metronidazole solution can be alternately used every 2 hours, each time for 20 minutes; or take Niuhuang Jiedu tablets twice a day, 4-5 tablets each time, and Viral Spirit once a day, 3 times each time, for 3-5 days.

Infection treatment for lip line:

Prevention is key. If the infection is severe, 250ml of 10% glucose plus 100ml of metronidazole and 250ml of 10% glucose plus 5mg dexamethasone can be administered intravenously, or other effective antibiotics. For allergic reactions, in addition to local application or wet dressing with gentamicin solution mixed with dexamethason, antiallergic drugs and antibiotic drugs can be given appropriately. Eliminate allergens as soon as possible. For herpes simplex on the lips, antiviral treatment should be performed promptly, such as taking Viral Spirit, Vitamin C, Niuhuang Jiedu tablets, etc., or intravenous drip of acyclovir.

Dietary note:

Avoid spicy food, beef, mutton, fish, shrimp, crab, eggplant, mango and other foods. Milk, soy milk
