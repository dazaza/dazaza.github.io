Soft article blog mass distribution tool Ju Zi said, blog Weibo mass distribution software | Tianxie No.

by mutousan on 2012-02-10 17:39:06

"Juzi Said" is a blog offline publishing software. In addition to meeting your basic needs such as writing articles offline, inserting pictures, automatically uploading pictures to multiple online albums or the built-in space of blogs, it is also a professional microblog and online album management software. With one click, you can synchronize to multiple microblogs and online albums, saving the trouble of logging in one by one. You can share your posts and microblogs in more social circles for better sharing, marketing, promotion, and SEO effects. It supports offline writing and editing of blog posts like Windows Live Writer, updating and modifying already published blog posts, with a WYSIWYG (What You See Is What You Get) editing window to preview the effect after posting. In addition to blogs, "Juzi Said" also supports synchronizing microblogs and online albums, allowing easy management of multiple microblogs and online albums.

"Juzi Said" currently supports over 50 mainstream blogs and microblogs, as well as 26+ blog-building programs: My synchronized blogs and microblogs.

It supports multiple foreign blog hosting services:

Blogger, TypePad, WordPress.com, LiveJournal, WindowsLiveSpaces, Friendster, Blog.com, etc.

It supports multiple well-known Chinese blog hosting services domestically:

Sina Blog, Baidu Space, NetEase Blog, Sohu Blog, QQ Zone, Tianya Blog, Hexun Blog, Phoenix Bobao, Douban, BlogBus, YO2 Ubo, Seventy-two Pines, WordPress.com.cn, etc.

It supports multiple independent domestic and foreign blog systems:

WordPress, MovableType, Drupal, CommunityServer, ExpressionEngine, Bo-Blog, PJBlog, Z-Blog, Oblog, etc.

It supports multiple domestic and foreign online albums to facilitate the release and sharing of your photos:

Yupoo! (Up!), Flickr, Picasa, Bababian, etc.

It supports multiple domestic and foreign microblogging services:

Twitter, Sina Weibo, Tencent Weibo, Sohu Weibo, NetEase Weibo, Zuosha, Digu, Fanfou, etc.

"Juzi Said" has free users and paid users. Free users can bind unlimited commercial blogs, personal blogs, and microblogs, but sending commercial blogs is restricted for free users. These commercial blogs, such as CITIC Securities Blog and Alibaba Blog, are probably not necessary for us to synchronize. Free users can upload images to online albums or FTP space, but they cannot upload them to blog albums. Free users can synchronize an unlimited number of blogs at any time, but they can only synchronize three times a day, which is enough since it's unlikely that someone would write more than three articles in a day.
