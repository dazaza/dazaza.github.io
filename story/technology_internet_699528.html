Engineering marketing opens up multi-channel paths for curtain companies.

Nowadays, product sales still largely depend on traditional channels, relying on dealers at all levels to operate, making it difficult for each channel to achieve balance. Although e-commerce covers a wide range of areas, products can be listed infinitely, reducing shop rent, warehousing, and other expenses, giving it certain cost advantages. However, in Beijing, a few merchants only use it as a form of promotion, while engineering, home decoration, community, and retail channel construction remain indispensable channels.

However, competition in traditional sales channels in the curtain industry will become increasingly fierce, whereas competition in emerging channels such as e-commerce is relatively reduced. As market competition intensifies, brands themselves have become added value to products. At a new historical starting point, Chinese curtain brands have reached a certain level of emphasis on brand strategy and brand competition in recent years. With the rapid development of the Internet and the sharp increase in the number of Internet users in recent years, online marketing has become another important means for curtain companies to build their brands.

Currently, expanding new channels means investing in new fields, which requires fully considering whether the conditions for channel construction are already in place, whether the ability to sustain development can be guaranteed, and whether the risks in the operation process can be effectively resisted.

When you have a clear understanding and prevent problems before they happen, you can avoid the failure of rushing into things and hastily wrapping them up.

Finding a way out is urgent.

After several years of market integration, the network has basically taken shape, but some merchants have turned their attention to engineering channel sales. The engineering channel is in a thriving state of development, especially in the past two years, under the lure of repeated gains in the face of sluggish retail and saturated distribution, the construction of the engineering channel in the market has been highly promoted by merchants.

Establishing online marketing

For mainstream channels of purchasing curtain products, it is still in the business hall, but many consumers will understand the reputation of a brand online as a reference for offline purchases.

Nowadays, the engineering market has characteristics of relationship marketing, which seems unsuitable for online marketing. However, the Internet faces the entire national scope, and the effective conduct of online marketing can bring more business opportunities to enterprises. A company in Guangzhou providing production management project services has annual sales of tens of millions of yuan, with 100% of its customers coming from the Internet.

Therefore, the key to success is to consciously carry out online marketing based on the needs and characteristics of engineering clients. For example, corporate websites displaying engineering cases, cost-performance advantages, quality systems, etc., and conducting search engine marketing, online advertising, and soft article marketing based on the analysis of engineering client characteristics.

Also worth mentioning is that distributors, home decoration companies, designers, construction units, complementary manufacturers are not only themselves purchasers of curtain products but also influence consumer purchasing decisions to varying degrees. Therefore, each enterprise establishes cooperation with these institutions through various means. Online marketing can also play an important role in different ways.

Speaking of recruitment, whether it's traditional media, telephone visits, or online media, many ceramic enterprises recruiting do not even have an enterprise website, or it's too simple, not only affecting the enterprise image but also failing to fully leverage the enterprise's strengths. Therefore, first, importance should be placed on enterprise website construction, recruitment copywriting, etc. On this basis, recruitment can be carried out through traditional media, soft article marketing, search engine marketing, industry portal advertisements, etc., to improve recruitment effectiveness. If planning to increase online sales, developing online distributors is also necessary. For instance, Li Ning has nearly 20,000 sellers, generating annual sales of 200 million yuan.

Currently, in industries such as clothing, home appliances, and furniture, some distributors' online sales exceed physical stores. There are also many distributors in the curtain industry who attach great importance to online marketing, but due to limitations in experience, talent, and policy, they often feel inadequate. Therefore, if a set of online marketing systems can be established to help distributors increase sales, this is the key to attracting distributors and maintaining loyalty.

Planning and design are crucial

In engineering marketing, the roles of planning and design cannot be overlooked, as they will complete the effective matching of products. Some capable enterprises enhance their recognition among designers through design competitions while promoting new products. If held simultaneously online, it undoubtedly expands the scope of publicity for the event and produces more lasting effects. Alternatively, adopting purely online design competitions would significantly reduce costs, and the effect depends mainly on the planning.

Cooperation with designers and decoration companies is most importantly about achieving win-win outcomes. When promoting the company website and online promotions, certain cooperating companies can be recommended together to consumers, bringing benefits to designers in terms of brand recognition and orders, thereby strengthening the cooperation between both parties. Some companies have also established designer clubs to strengthen cooperation with designers.

Designers can be considered the beauticians of curtain products; under their hands, curtain products can be re-beautified, giving the products a new life. Designers, through their artistic processing and pairing of different products, provide consumers with a comprehensive spatial solution, creating a unique combination of human-made bricks and showcasing personalized lifestyles, achieving a second leap in the display effect of tile products. Due to their high sensitivity to design trends and styles, coupled with the severe homogenization of curtain industry products, designers can transform these homogenized products into personalized space displays, better reflecting the inherent value of curtain products.

Designers play a decisive role in increasing sales. Due to the lack of understanding of curtain products by consumers, designers have become trusted consumption guides for consumers. Curtain insiders also revealed that designers have more say in the terminal market than curtain companies, and consumers trust designers more, being more skeptical of curtain fabric companies and dealers. Therefore, designers guide consumers in choosing building materials. If curtain companies' products are favored by designers and recommended to consumers, the success rate of transactions will greatly increase.
