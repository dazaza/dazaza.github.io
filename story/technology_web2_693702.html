3. A Safe Firecracker

This invention relates to a safe firecracker made using an explosion drug with high combustion temperature and an ignition line. The firecracker's explosive drug consists of five materials: potassium perchlorate, sulfur, aluminum powder, aluminum-magnesium alloy powder, and charcoal. The high-temperature burning ignition line for the firecracker consists of three materials: potassium perchlorate, potassium chlorate, and charcoal. This invention's firecracker surpasses traditional potassium chlorate firecrackers in terms of explosion rate and sound level, solving the problem of high mechanical sensitivity due to impact and friction in firecracker explosives. Using aluminum-magnesium alloy powder as a combustible material lowers the ignition temperature of the explosive, while using potassium perchlorate as the drug raw material for the ignition line increases the burning temperature of the firecracker's ignition line, solving the issue of poor ignition rate for potassium perchlorate firecrackers. It enhances the sound value and explosion rate of the firecracker, significantly reducing the amount of explosive used and addressing the problem of high production costs for potassium perchlorate firecrackers. It has the advantages of stable performance, high explosion rate, crisp sound, strong radiation light, low production cost, etc.
