But no matter what, we found that the macro environment hasn't changed, and it's impossible for the market style transformation to be completed in just one day. Therefore, under such consolidation and capital inflow, the stock index will still be a process of consolidation next week. Because after the continuous volume increase in large-cap stocks, a sharp rise has become unrealistic, and the only thing left is further rest and brewing of new momentum.

After two trading days of consolidation, speculative stocks will inevitably trigger a technical rebound. Thus, the market eliminates the hidden dangers of upward pressure and the surge in volume from last week during the consolidation. Although we are not afraid of the market consolidation, it is also quite difficult for a new short-term direction to immediately appear.

Our judgment on next week's market situation remains the same - the consolidation will continue, and we firmly believe in the mid-term outlook.

Recently, overseas investment banks have all released their investment reports for the coming year, and we find that overseas hot money's hedging will further enter China. In that case, we cannot simply look at the reduction in credit and liquidity's impact on the market. This is something worth our deep consideration. We also need to consider the hotspots that hot money is willing to attack. As for the securities firms, medical reform, new energy, and banks they favor, we can discover two patterns from here: first, it is clear that the basis for the market's rise lies in the launch of stock index futures and the introduction of margin trading and short selling, which has solidified the foundation for the market's rise; second, it is the re-examination of our economic structure, where low-carbon economy stocks have segmental opportunities. Especially the stocks recommended by securities firms, I think this brings more opportunities with the listing of new shares.

What we need to study is not just to buy based on their recommendations, but to understand the opportunities and their starting point of thought. No matter what, we should correctly understand that thematic investment and value investment must proceed hand in hand, and there should be no one-sided situation. Overseas investment banks are optimistic about the first half of next year, and many securities firms are also optimistic. The industry holds this consistent view, but will the market once again go against expectations? Considering the year-on-year performance and the temporary non-withdrawal of economic stimulus policies, the first quarter may be the best investment period, and caution is needed as we approach the second quarter. Full positions should be avoided. Here, we give a rational reminder.

The increase in high-priced stocks does not indicate a complete style conversion. Suddenly, as if overnight spring breeze came, thousands of pear trees bloomed. Such a spectacle is very suitable to describe the current high-priced stocks in the market. Before June this year, the only true sense hundred-yuan high-priced stock in the market was Guizhou Maotai. After China Shipbuilding made two exploratory attempts to break through 90 yuan, it was sent back to its "original form." However, recently, with the resumption of IPOs and the rise of the Growth Enterprise Market (GEM), many secondary stocks and GEM stocks with high issuance prices and high P/E ratios have easily broken through the hundred-yuan mark. There are six stocks over a hundred yuan in the two markets, namely Guizhou Maotai, Shenzhou Taiyue, Hongri Pharmaceutical, Yanghe Shares, Orient Landscape, and Xinyi Tai. Stocks above 80 yuan are even more numerous, including old high-priced stocks like China Shipbuilding and Shandong Gold, as well as secondary stocks like Jifeng Agricultural Machinery, Robot, and Dinghan Technology, which have exceeded 80 yuan. There are nearly 100 stocks priced above 40 yuan.

Historically, high dividend-paying stocks depend on two factors: how much capital reserve there is and how high the undistributed profits are. Therefore, high-priced stocks without any bonus or transfer actions are most likely to generate high dividends. However, not all high-priced stocks will necessarily produce high dividend stocks. To effectively screen out the most likely high-dividend stocks from the constantly emerging high-priced stocks, one must also pay attention to the company's growth potential and national support policies.

Thus, among these high-priced stocks, the main driving force is the year-end high bonus share transfer, hence it does not possess the power to fully switch to blue-chip stocks. Investors please note, do not easily believe that blue-chip stocks will all soar.

It can be affirmed that small-cap stocks have more opportunities than large-cap stocks. Therefore, in the absence of a complete style conversion - certainly, today's strong move in large-cap stocks, with bank stocks surging, is due to an oversold situation leading to a capital game - considering the special nature of the year-end, it might instead be worthwhile to research the high bonus share transfer opportunities in small and medium-sized boards and small-cap stocks.

The significance of the launch of the Yellow River Delta and the multiple thoughts of the market

The Yellow River Delta sector is not just a short-term concept. With the recent approval by the State Council of the "Development Plan for the High-efficiency Ecological Economy Zone in the Yellow River Delta," up to now, the State Council has approved nine regional economic development plans elevated to the national strategy level this year. This number is the total of the past three to four years, with unprecedented speed and intensity in the release.

The central government's consecutive release of regional economic development plans mainly reflects the continuous deepening of our country's economic development. In the past, the national regional economy was divided into three major zones: east, central, and west. The 17th National Congress proposed the concept of subject functional areas, and this time further细分s were made according to the characteristics of different places, drawing intrinsic economic links to form a series of internally connected regions. This plays a very important role in further coordinating the development of China's regional economy, formulating policies for corresponding economic zones, and allowing the comparative advantages of each place to be brought into play.

According to the already approved regional economic development plans, they include the following economic regions: Yangtze River Delta, Pearl River Delta, Beibu Gulf, Bohai Rim, Strait West Coast, Northeast Three Provinces, Central and Western Regions, plus the newly approved Yellow River Delta. Gradually, China's new regional economic map is taking shape. China's economic development will transform from the previous outward-oriented growth structure to the common development of external demand and internal demand, which is entirely in line with the national decision to expand domestic demand. Next year, the country's credit policy will further support the Bohai Rim region, infrastructure construction in the central and western regions, and second-tier cities from the perspective of regional economy.

Regional rotation, the market has the foundation to develop in depth - based on past experience, the state's policy orientation often triggers the overall outbreak of regional bull stocks. The linkage of regional sectors often promotes the in-depth development of the market. Since the beginning of this year, the market has systematically speculated on regional concepts such as Haixi, Hainan, Changji Tu, and Shenzhen according to national policies. We believe that under the backdrop of the upcoming announcement of the "Development Plan for the High-efficiency Ecological Economy Zone in the Yellow River Delta," a new round of regional speculation waves has quietly begun. Among them, some are conceptual speculations, while others are indeed stocks with good performance.

This plan will accelerate the industrial upgrading of related companies. The "Plan" states that according to relevant management methods, a Yellow River Delta Industry (Equity) Investment Fund will be established in this area. At the same time, this area will also seek to establish a rural commercial bank. Previously, the Shandong Provincial Government issued an "Opinion," which will be jointly funded by the Shandong Provincial Government, provincial large enterprises, and relevant cities (counties, districts) and large enterprises within the region to set up a Yellow River Delta Development Fund. On this basis, financial capital, industrial investment capital, market liquidity funds, and foreign strategic investments will be absorbed, gradually expanding the scale to strive for the establishment of a Yellow River Delta Industry Investment Fund, providing financial support for key projects, key enterprises, and growth-oriented small and medium-sized enterprises.

This move will undoubtedly bring blessings to enterprises within the region. Currently, Shandong Haihua has created a new model for seawater utilization, achieving zero emissions and forming an ecological economic effect. Since China's new economic map has been established, the market has the foundation to develop in depth, and efforts in research and holding can be increased in this aspect.

The procedure for buying and selling stocks is as follows: When buying or selling stocks, you must specify whether you are buying or selling, the name (or code) of the stock, the quantity, and the price. Moreover, this order is only valid on the day it is placed. The content of the order includes the abbreviation (code) of the stock you want to buy or sell, the quantity, and the price at which you want to buy or sell the stock. The abbreviation of the stock usually consists of three to four Chinese characters, and the stock code is six digits. When placing an order, the stock code and abbreviation must match exactly. Additionally, there are certain regulations regarding the quantity of stocks being traded: the quantity of stocks ordered for purchase must be a whole multiple of 100, but the quantity ordered for sale does not have to be a whole multiple of 100.

There are four types of orders: counter submission, telephone automatic, computer automatic, and remote terminal orders.
