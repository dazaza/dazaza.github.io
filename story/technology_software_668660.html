The city of Wugang, where the municipal party and government offices have advanced alongside Wugang Steel, is rich in iron ore deposits and abundant water resources located upstream of the Huaihe River. As early as the Spring and Autumn Period, it was a metal fabrication powerhouse producing the famous Longquan, Hop Bo swords. According to historical records, these swords established Wuyang Iron and Steel as China’s special steel business card. Many domestic famous buildings constructed in recent years—such as the Olympic Stadium Bird’s Nest, Water Cube, National Grand Theater, new CCTV building, Oriental Pearl TV Tower, Soviet Union bridge—all use special steel from here.

In Wuyang Iron and Steel City, rapid economic development has led to progress in social undertakings. Since 2000, the city has won over 30 provincial-level honors including Henan Health City, Garden City, and Chinese Excellent Tourist City. With beautiful scenery blending forestry and mineral resources, it is resource-rich and economically developed. In 2004, the Wugang municipal government established goals for creating China’s Excellent Tourism City, National Garden City, National Sanitary City, and National Civilized City, aiming to fully improve the living environment and create a favorable investment climate. However, Wugang had limited visibility both domestically and internationally. In this era where branding is effective, in April 2004, according to the needs of economic and social development, the Wugang City E-Government Construction Leading Group was formally established with the mayor as the head and municipal party committee and municipal government leaders in charge as members of the related board committee responsible for the human crew.

In May 2004, Wugang City set up a unified platform for e-government in collaboration with Weale software to start constructing Wugang's e-government system. The network construction principles are overall design, safe and reliable, economical and practical, appropriate advancement step by step. The overall topology of Wugang City's informatization construction includes electronic Zhengwuzhuanwang (government intranet) and extranet, with private networks and external networks completely physically isolated. The city's unified e-government network infrastructure and platform use a three-tier network topology divided into the core layer, convergence layer, and access layer. The government designed a network central computer room core switch connecting seven main branch nodes, access layer switches, 8 Gigabit fiber as the core for the municipal people's congresses, governments, CPPCC four groups authority Gigabit backbone and 113 Mbps optical fiber connected to the municipal ministries, government departments, neighborhood offices, enterprises in the city, and above-scale enterprises. It is connected upward through Pingdingshan City's private network to the nationwide private network system, downward even to township offices, forming a public network platform for Wugang e-government cross-system, cross-sectoral, cross-cutting development of a stable, secure, manageable, scalable platform.

Collaborative office business resource integration network construction aims to break administrative barriers between different services, interoperability of information between different government agencies, collaborative applications as the goal, constructing various joint office business systems leading Wugang information resources integration and data sharing and collaborative progress. Collaborative office system unified platform includes four group common office systems, customizing four different interfaces. The system consists of office modules, email module, SMS module, and electronic signature module as the four main modules ensuring the timeliness and security of information transfer through the network platform and safety measures. Within the existing government processes of the four team organs, optimization, integration, interoperability, and sharing of information resources improve working efficiency, shorten the development cycle, reducing investment cost.

The city's various units of each committee, township and neighborhood offices, financial, corporate, and public four groups have a set of office information systems independently setting interface seamlessly realizing information resource sharing and parallel text wording, paperless document exchange, information collection, announcement notice, inspector supervision, electronic signature, official mail, mayor hotline and Mayor-mail and other functions improving document flow speed and office efficiency ensuring timeliness and good security.

Based on demand analysis reports, Wugang City's office automation system is divided into seven subsystems: personal office subsystem, document management subsystem, Chief transaction subsystem, document exchange subsystem, Public Information Sub-office subsystems, and systems management subsystem. Wugang collaborative office systems is a large-scale integrated general-purpose integrated office support system with functions including office portal, online process, document management, administrative services, public information, personal office, documentation centers, document exchange of information collection, supervision, meeting management, back-office, political research, management, file management, mayor hotline and Mayor-mail, leadership activities arrangements, electronic signatures, messaging services, system administration, more than 20 subsystems.
