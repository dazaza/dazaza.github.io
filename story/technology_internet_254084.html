Tencent's Soso search engine has minor technical flaws.

by longersing on 2009-10-30 21:19:05

By Long Ge

In many of the cases we've worked on, there are always some small details that leave a lasting impression. For instance, international logistics, foreign trade enterprises, and the sales of certain products have not only helped these businesses improve in their operations but also allowed us to accumulate knowledge on issues and their corresponding handling methods. When Tencent Soso was first launched, we specifically conducted promotions for some clients on the Soso platform. Even now, we still find that Soso has some flaws. Could this be due to the incomplete technical handover between Tencent and Google China before Tencent gained independence in search engine technology? It's possible.

The speed at which Tencent Soso includes websites in its index is not slow. Submitting a website to the official channel could result in inclusion within a few hours. However, after being indexed, something quite interesting occurred. For example: A website established in mid-September was just submitted and was included by Tencent Soso the next day, yet the search results displayed an incorrect date. Refer to the image below:

From the image, it can be seen that the latest page shown is from August 26th. If our domain had been resolved and in normal use on that day, we could understand this phenomenon. However, our new website was indeed put up many days later. This might be a small detail that wasn't perfected when Soso began its technical independence.

On the snapshot display page, the collection time shown is the latest time.

As seen in the image above, the actual snapshot differs from the previously viewed search page, showing the collection time with the latest snapshot being 13 hours ago. This still gives us some reassurance, as our site has indeed been normally indexed by Tencent Soso.

Although Tencent Soso has only recently become independent and still has many detailed issues, it remains a domestic search engine. Setting aside the previous technical support from Google China, the hundreds of millions of users from Tencent alone are enough to rival Baidu today. Therefore, we still place high hopes in Tencent. We wish them further success in their development.
