Who should a woman give her body to

by hesanqi2 on 2009-11-20 10:19:56

I think a woman can give her body to such a man.

One, a man who is full of love and loyalty from beginning to end

From the day he fell in love with you, you are his all. You are the earth, he is the moon, your smile is his good mood. With you, he will no longer shift his gaze to other women. Serving you is his lifelong career. If you meet such a man, don't care if he has money, status, or a good job. Being with him for life is happiness.

Two, a romantic, warm, elegant, and noble man

His cuffs are always neatly rolled up, his shoes will never be stained with dust, there isn't a single vulgar word, he is always calm, unrestrained, and smiling when you need him. Such a man is hard to come by. Maybe you only have the chance to drink coffee or admire the moon together, but his aristocratic charm and gentlemanly demeanor will make you miss him for a lifetime. So if you encounter such a man, if the atmosphere is suitable and the mood is right, don't refuse or hesitate.

Three, a straightforward, generous, and rugged man

Maybe he doesn't sweat the small stuff, loves to joke around, and often makes you angry, but when someone bullies you, he will use his strong arms to protect you. He works hard for you, does many foolish things for you without letting you know, gets hurt without wanting you to worry, loves to mingle among women, and even goes to bed with another woman when drunk. But the one he loves the most is still you. Under this protective love, women become more like little girls. If you like being a little girl, go with him.

Four, a weak, rigid, and awkward man

This kind of man is like an unpolished gem among men, requiring patient refinement. They believe in love, are obsessed with love, but cannot express it. Perhaps he doesn't quite understand life, often comparing reality to books, but he is sincere. He won't look for hidden meanings in every word you say. He can want just one woman for a lifetime. If you are poor, he is willing to change for you. If you are rich, he will be an even better man. Because whether it's family or career, he will face it with a positive attitude. Although he doesn't tell jokes, he might write some poems that move you. He fears interacting with other girls. Your embrace is the most comfortable and free place for him. Find such a man if you are a woman with a complex mindset.

Five, conceited and domineering

This kind of man will be self-righteous in every matter. He doesn't listen to anyone's advice, or you could say he has very insightful opinions. This kind of man is independent. Sacrificing yourself for him comes second; the important thing is to conquer him and make him yours. If successful, he will become a reformed prodigal son worth a thousand gold pieces. Such men usually have a 99-point percentage chance of becoming successful men. If you follow him, perhaps you won't have to worry about anything in the next life.

Six, a virgin

Actually, women are like men; women also have a "virgin" complex. In modern society, men are so dissolute. If there's a virgin in front of you, without a doubt, here are eight words for you: "Rather make a mistake than let go." It's said that men have an indelible and complex attachment to their first woman. If you can get such a man, maybe he will ignore all your flaws because of losing his virginity to you, then learn to take care of you and love you like an adult man, and occasionally act cute in front of you because he wasn't very experienced the first time, but only you know. If you want to be a big sister, find a virgin.
