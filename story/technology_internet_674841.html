Small knowledge of magnetic base drill selection

by dfasd98fh on 2012-02-24 16:09:05

Magnetic Drill [http://www.hanyue-sh.com/] Selection Tips:

First, if you are working in steel structure, wind power generation, or nuclear power plants and need to drill holes with a diameter not exceeding 38MM (hollow drill), and depth not exceeding 50MM, I strongly recommend using the Mag MD38 model (twist drill can drill up to 13MM). It is small in size, and the magnetic drill [http://www.hanyue-sh.com/] weighs only 11KG, making it convenient for high-altitude work.

Second, if your drilling diameter does not exceed 50MM, depth does not exceed 50MM, and twist drill is less than 23MM, I recommend using the Mag MD50 machine. The machine has two mechanical speed settings and weighs only 13KG. If you need reverse drilling or 90-degree vertical drilling, and the magnetic drill [http://www.hanyue-sh.com/] needs to be deeper, then the MD58 machine can meet your requirements. In addition to possessing all the advantages of the MD50 machine, the MD58 increases the magnet base, enhances the adsorption force, and increases the drilling depth from 50MM to 75MM. Therefore, the MD58 model can be considered as an enhanced version of the MD50 machine.

Third, four-speed transmission, the largest twist drill is 32MM, the largest hollow drill is 100MM, and the maximum depth of the magnetic drill [http://www.hanyue-sh.com/] is 100MM. This refers to the Mag MD108 model. Some manufacturers cannot accept the cost of hollow drill bits and still use traditional twist drills. However, sometimes they may also need to drill hollow drills due to process requirements. In this case, please use the Mag MD108 machine. First, it can handle twist drills up to 32MM. Moreover, it also has the capability to drill large holes of 100*100MM hollow drills. This not only saves consumable costs but also possesses the ability to drill large holes.

Fourth, if you are drilling stainless steel, hard materials, or manganese steel, which are high hardness and high viscosity materials, you must choose the TAP30 machine. Not all machines are as practical as advertised. The TAP30 features stepless speed adjustment, torque protection, and dual insulation protection devices for magnetic drills [http://www.hanyue-sh.com/]. When under load, the slowest speed of the TAP30 is 30 revolutions per minute, and the torque can be adjusted larger or smaller. For drilling hard and viscous material plates, adjust the torque to the maximum and the speed to the minimum. Everyone knows that the lower the speed, the greater the torque, and the higher the speed, the smaller the torque. The strongest feature of the TAP30 is its overload protection device. If the machine becomes overloaded due to human operation, it will automatically shut down, effectively protecting the motor from burning out, making your drilling more effortless. Its unique tapping function can tap internal threads from M4 to M30, making it a truly integrated multi-functional magnetic seat drill capable of hollow drilling, twist drilling, and tapping.
