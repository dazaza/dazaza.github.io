Louis Vuitton Galliera - Brand Vuitton

by swatk386 on 2012-02-25 08:55:32

The Louis Vuitton Galliera handbag stands within the classification of all-time best sellers in the extensive Louis Vuitton product assortment; other LV best-sellers alongside it currently include the likes of the LV Monogram Sprouse and Louis Vuitton Damier Canvas handbags. Personally, the LV Galliera handbag has proven to be one of the most enchanting products, at least in terms of handbags. One of the things I have come to appreciate most about the Louis Vuitton Galliera handbag is its compactness. Not being a fan of those large and seemingly (in my experience) unwieldy handbags—yet still wanting to have a handbag where I could fit all the items a woman needs—the LV Galliera turned out to be exactly what I wanted; in terms of a compromise between compactness and adequacy of carrying space in a handbag.

Measuring approximately 16 inches by 11 inches by 6 inches, the LV Galliera is evidently big enough to fit everything that is reasonably supposed to go into a handbag, yet compact enough to appear 'cute.' Another highly appealing feature of the Louis Vuitton Galliera, besides its beautiful compactness-adequate storage, one has to acknowledge, is the wide shoulder strap it comes with—which is also length adjustable, making it 'a bag for all seasons,' that is, a bag that you can carry whenever you are looking for either a low-hanging or high-hanging accessory equally well.

As a signature that it is a real LV, the LV Galliera comes with this Vuitton Inventeur plate—which is taken as the signature for LV's distinctive handbags. Due to this Louis Vuitton Inventeur plate, it becomes clear to everyone that what you are carrying is indeed an LV bag, with the admiration that comes from those who understand what the finer preferences as far as handbags go are.

Another prominently obvious feature of the LV Galliera should be the magnetic closure mechanism it comes with, which effectively does away with the demanding button-closing business. This is a great feature, many handbag users would acknowledge, since the buttons (where they are used) do tend to malfunction at some point as time goes on, while the magnetic closure mechanism on the LV Galliera is sure to last for as long as the bag is being used, and beyond.

Perhaps noting that the cell-phone has pretty much become part of everyday modern life, which usually needs to be carried in the handbag, and noting the annoying hassle that trying to fish out a cell-phone that has gotten mixed up with other handbag contents can be, the LV Galliera bag comes with a specially designed cell-phone compartment, so that one does not have to waste precious time and get stressed trying to fish out a cell-phone that has gotten mixed up with other handbag contents.

Yet another eye-catching aspect I have noted with the LV Galliera is the studs (especially those signature LV Inventeur studs) it comes with on its bottom, supposedly to protect the bag at its base. It is a fantastic feature, since as any expert handbag users will aver, the final damage that sees an otherwise terrific handbag having to be discarded is destruction to the handbag's base, especially for handbags that do not come with this type of base stud protection.
