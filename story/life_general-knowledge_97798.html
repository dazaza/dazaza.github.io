Teach you how to shop online safely

by tl43 on 2008-10-21 12:52:05

With the rapid development of domestic Internet, the popularization of network is gradually changing people's lives. Online shopping has become a fashionable and fast lifestyle, and is increasingly popular among consumers. However, there are still many friends who know little about online shopping and do not know how to correctly identify online fraud. Article source: Tang Lin Fashion (www.tlgood.com)

Step 1: Reasonably estimate the value of goods

Do not be blinded by ultra-low prices. Too cheap may indicate fake goods. Beware of traps set by scammers. The characteristics of online shopping are that it eliminates the expensive rent costs of traditional physical stores, so the sold goods are generally much cheaper than in physical stores. But no matter how costs are reduced, it is impossible for a mobile phone on the market priced at 3000 yuan to be listed at 500-700 yuan on some shopping websites. Carefully think about it, even if the profit margin of mobile phones is large, it cannot possibly differ this much. Normally, a difference of a few hundred yuan is already quite good. In addition to fraudulent practices, this could also be due to refurbished second-hand phones.

Step 2: How to safely conduct online transactions

1. Use Alipay tool for shopping

Alipay and Tenpay are important tools for ensuring secure transactions. They are designed payment and shipping methods for online safe transactions, thereby reducing the risk of transactions. Especially when purchasing large items, pay attention to whether the seller agrees to use Alipay. If they agree to use Alipay and support guaranteed payment, you can shop with peace of mind. Because Alipay is authenticated through identity and bank information, and supports cash-on-delivery. If you are not satisfied with the seller's goods, you do not have to make payment.

2. How to safely shop without Alipay

Try to choose postal money orders, because in this way the specific address of the other party exists, and if there are any issues, it would be easier to resolve. Postal money orders require identification to collect, and without a detailed address, the remittance receipt will not reach the hands of the seller. Therefore, many scammers online use bank accounts to defraud money, while remitting money to home addresses will prevent the situation where the money is received but the goods are not shipped.

3. Check if the order on the website is genuine

Many scam websites only collect payments and do not ship goods. To deal with this situation, shoppers can check if the shipping orders provided by the website are genuine by verifying them on relevant logistics websites.

4. Try to communicate with the website customer service

You can use tools such as QQ, MSN, or Taobao Wangwang to contact the website customer service and pay attention to saving chat records. Chat records can serve as evidence of transactions and have equal legal effect. Additionally, there is a small method to distinguish between real and fake. Pay attention to the level of the customer service QQ and the level of the Paipai online store. When you chat with the other party, you can know their online store level. The higher the QQ online store level, the more professional the merchant is, and it is absolutely impossible for them to be a scammer. If there are fraudulent behaviors, the seller would suffer significant losses due to the need to re-register QQ.

5. Find out the brand of the website

Check the brand of the website and the reviews from users on the site. If the website you encounter is messy or there are many negative comments online about the website or its products, then shoppers should be cautious.
