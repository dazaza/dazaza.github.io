On the 29th, the Shanghai Composite Index opened at 3076.05 points, up 0.53%. On the 29th, the Shenzhen Component Index opened at 12301.60 points, up 0.50%. On the 29th, the CSI 300 Index opened at 3295.76 points, up 0.63%. On the 29th, the SME Board Composite Index opened at 6054.78 points, up 0.27%. The Shenzhen market traded 444,312 hands, with a trading value of 533.26 million yuan, increasing by 25.38 million yuan compared to the previous trading day. The determination of the schedule for margin trading and short selling, as well as stock index futures, is expected to become an opportunity for blue-chip stocks to start moving, especially for broker stocks, as the launch of innovative businesses will have a significant impact on their performance improvement and are also expected to attract funds. However, last Friday, with the collective strength of weight-class stocks represented by banks, the index successfully held at 3000 points. It is expected that the strong vibration pattern of the short-term market will continue.
