Biometric industry leaders gather at the Internet of Things exhibition in Shenzhen

by gdshstyle on 2012-03-09 10:24:52

Leading Biometric Companies Gather at Shenzhen IoT Expo

The 4th China (Shenzhen) International Internet of Things Technology and Application Expo will be grandly held at the Shenzhen Convention and Exhibition Center from August 15 to 17, 2012. Reporters recently learned from the organizing committee of the expo that after the renewal of contracts by international giants Impinj and Alien, two leading domestic biometric companies, Shenzhen Feiris Technology Co., Ltd., and Beijing Tiancheng Shengye Technology Co., Ltd., will also sign up to participate in the exhibition. At that time, visitors will have the opportunity to experience the charm of these well-known IoT companies.

The China (Shenzhen) International Internet of Things Technology and Application Expo is a high-level international event showcasing comprehensive solutions and successful applications across the entire IoT industry chain, including RFID (Radio Frequency Identification) technology, sensor network technology, short-range communication technology, the latest mobile payment technology, electronic tag production solutions, the latest technology for reader development, precise control technology for middleware, as well as IoT technology applications in transportation, industrial automation, smart grids, smart homes, logistics, anti-counterfeiting, personnel, vehicles, military, asset management, clothing, books, home intelligence, urban management, environmental monitoring, and more.

Feiris Technology Co., Ltd., was a participant in the previous edition of this international event and has chosen to continue participating due to the high effectiveness of exhibiting. Meanwhile, Beijing Tiancheng Shengye Technology Co., Ltd., attracted by the excellent reputation and exhibition results of the expo, will make its debut at the event.

At the upcoming expo, we will see Feiris Technology's renowned facial recognition products both domestically and internationally. Additionally, IoT application schemes led by intelligent video analysis will be one of the major attractions capturing the audience's attention. Ding Xiaogang, Sales Director of Feiris Technology, stated: "Feiris' intelligent video analysis technology application is making IoT transition from concept to reality, such as electronic shoe cabinets. The annual IoT expo provides Feiris with an excellent display platform, allowing more people to know about our brand and technology. This year, our booth number is A40. Besides familiar facial recognition products, we will also showcase our solutions in smart transportation, smart commerce, and smart security. We look forward to everyone's participation!"

Beijing Tiancheng Shengye Technology Co., Ltd., has also made meticulous preparations. At this edition of the expo, Tiancheng Shengye will introduce the world's safest biometric product specifically designed for the IoT industry - the iris recognition device, including iris attendance recognition systems and iris access control recognition systems. Wang Houjin, Marketing Director of Tiancheng Shengye, expressed: "Tiancheng Shengye is committed to applying iris recognition technology across various IoT fields. Whether it's within the IoT or connecting IoT with other industries, users can enjoy safer, more convenient, and more thoughtful biometric service experiences. Moreover, for enterprises in the IoT industry with high information security requirements, we will offer precise VIP customer facial recognition services! By leveraging facial recognition technology, we aim to provide better services to VIP customers, thereby enhancing corporate brand benefits. Our booth this year is A4. Visitors will certainly not be disappointed!"

Chen, the recruitment manager of the Shenzhen IoT Expo Organizing Committee, said: "Tiancheng Shengye and Feiris are representative leading enterprises in the biometric industry. Their joint participation is a high recognition of the promotional effect of our exhibition, and it also signifies the confidence in the development prospects of IoT! The Shenzhen IoT Expo is growing annually. We look forward to more IoT companies showcasing their strengths at the exhibition, offering visitors even more exciting experiences. Based on the scale of the previous edition, we estimate that this year's exhibition area will exceed 15,000 square meters, with over 300 participating companies and more than 25,000 professional visitors! During the exhibition, events such as the 'Fifth International IoT Development Forum,' the '2012 IoT New Product Launch,' and the 'IoT Expo Exhibitors and Premium Purchasers Exchange Cocktail Party' will be held simultaneously. We strive to offer society all-round exhibitions and meetings featuring cutting-edge IoT technologies, innovative products, comprehensive solutions, and policy discussions."
