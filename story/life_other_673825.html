Hope to see you in my dream tonight

Time flies, it's been 14 days already. During these 14 days, I've been missing you every day, forcing myself to smile. Every night before sleeping, I would recall your appearance carefully: the white hair on both temples which grew out of worry for us, the wrinkles on your face left by hard work for us, and the bent back working hard for our better life. You are what I think during the day and dream at night, because the more I miss you, the more likely I will see you in my dreams. I wonder if you in heaven are also missing us and feeling what we feel...

I was insomnia again last night, tossing and turning all night just to meet you in my dream. But I couldn't see you in my dream anyway, so I woke up and continued to miss and recall you, but still couldn't see you in my dream. I wonder if you went traveling with dad and had so much fun that you forgot about us! Otherwise, why aren't you in your beloved daughter's dream...

I was restless and listless during the day today, lost interest in work. Your voice and laughter always flashed in my mind, the happiness of the past can't be erased. I can't forget how you raised me up, I can't forget the hardships of your education, I can't forget the reluctant look when I leave home every time, I can't forget the unique taste of your chicken soup. Too many unforgettable scenes kept flashing, until my eyes blurred with tears...

I went to the supermarket where we went together last time, looked at the food you like to eat, didn't want to put it down when I held it in my hand, always imagining that you are still with us and haven't gone to heaven yet. I still remember last year, I circled around to buy the ginger candy you liked, then I saw a kind of packaged ones which were more expensive than those we bought before, but you refused to buy them, saying that you didn't like eating them recently. At that time, I shouted at you, saying that you should eat whatever I bought for you, and finally I bought them for you. When I saw your expression being scolded by me, you didn't say a word. At that time, I regretted, but I knew I had a bad temper and didn't ask for your forgiveness for my rudeness. Now I realize that I am a daughter without filial piety, and I deserve to feel bad now...

I bought a lot of snacks and fruits, but my mood didn't get better because of the food I bought. I wandered aimlessly from noon until the neon lights flickered, eating snacks desperately, one bag after another, but your shadow was still in my mind. At this moment, I realized that no matter how I tried to shift my focus, the pain of missing you became more and more obvious. There was nothing I could do but call my sister, wanting to say something, but when the phone was answered, I realized how foolish I was, how could I disturb my sister who was also suffering from the pain. So I chatted idly and hung up the phone, and my heart was cut like a knife at that moment...

I squeezed on the bus alone with big and small packages, got home but felt no sense of familiarity. I turned on the computer just to look at your photos, the memories of the past appeared in my sight over and over again, never getting tired of looking at them. At this moment, I realized why I was restless and uneasy, it was because I didn't see you in my dream last night. Mom... Please make sure to appear in your daughter's dream tonight, because your daughter will be afraid if she doesn't see you in her dream, worried that you will leave us forever, really ignore us. Mom... Please make sure to appear in our dreams tonight...
