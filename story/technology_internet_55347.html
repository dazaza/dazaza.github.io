Instructions for the Selection of Function Classification of IC Card Canteen Consumption Machine

◆ A convenient and fast payment method has made the crowded, long-queue scenes in canteens a thing of the past.

◆ It forms a new model of hygienic and orderly canteen consumption.

◆ The report lists are accurate and detailed, allowing queries and printing at any time.

◆ Equipped with built-in voltage stabilizer and backup battery.

◆ Fixed amount consumption and free consumption.

◆ Supports one-card-for-all network.

◆ Blacklisted cards will be denied consumption.

◆ Can be used as a standalone machine or connected to a network.

◆ Different card types have different consumption unit prices.

◆ Different time periods have different consumption unit prices.

◆ Different card types have restricted consumption time periods.

◆ In the same time period, limited to one or multiple consumptions.

◆ In the same day, limited to one, multiple consumptions, or unlimited consumptions.

◆ For the same card, during the same time period, different pricing for the second and subsequent consumptions.

1. Standard consumption, fixed amount consumption, and free consumption.

2. Comprehensive consumption: Possesses both fixed amount and free consumption functions, plus an additional charge for the second consumption of the same card within the same time period; limits each meal and total daily consumption per card.

3. Limited meal consumption: Upgraded from the standard version, it features restrictions on single and multiple consumptions per card per meal.

4. Count-based consumption: Consumes based on the number of swipes rather than amounts.

5. Pre-order consumption: Adds the function of pre-ordering meals before consuming, based on standard consumption.

Consumption methods for the card machine include:

Pre-order consumption: First order meals then consume, IC cards must first perform a pre-order operation to swipe on the consumption machine, and only one consumption per meal is allowed. No pre-order means no consumption.

Free consumption: Free amount input consumption, can limit the maximum consumption amount per card per time period and per day, for example, if limited to 20 yuan per time period and 50 yuan per day, it means each card can only consume up to 20 yuan per meal type and up to 50 yuan per day, exceeding this limit will prevent consumption. Blacklisted cards will deny consumption and trigger an alarm to notify management, ensuring the safety of lost card funds;

Daily summary function of the card machine allows real-time querying of the number of consumers and total consumption amount per day, letting you keep track of consumption data at any time;

Supports one-card-for-all products and other devices networking, achieving universal card functionality;
