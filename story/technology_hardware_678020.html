National Gas Ultrasonic Flowmeter Technology Summary and Exchange Meeting was held

by testchamber4 on 2012-02-27 11:17:02

Summary Exchange Conference on Domestic Gas Ultrasonic Flowmeter Technology was held. Organized by Sinopec Natural Gas Branch, the Summary Exchange Conference on Domestic Gas Ultrasonic Flowmeter Technology of Zhejiang Wes Company was held in Xuancheng, Anhui from September 1st to September 2nd, 2011.

The exchange conference was presided over by Zhu Yong, director of the Production and Operation Department of Sinopec Natural Gas Branch. More than 40 leaders and experts from Sinopec Natural Gas Branch, Chuanqi East Send Pipeline Branch and other pipeline companies, natural gas companies and pipeline network companies from some provinces and cities attended the meeting.

At the exchange conference, Zhejiang Wes Company, as a equipment supplier, made a keynote speech and had technical exchanges with user units.

Chuanqi East Send Pipeline Branch and Shandong Natural Gas Pipeline Company respectively introduced the trial use situation of domestic gas ultrasonic flow meters. The attendees also conducted an on-site inspection of the operation status of two 300-caliber gas ultrasonic flow meters installed by Zhejiang Wes in the substation at Shizizhen, Xuancheng, Anhui.

Through special topic exchanges and on-site inspections, the attending experts expressed that: the product quality of domestic gas ultrasonic flow meters is reliable, performance is stable, service is timely, operation is safe, and measurement accuracy meets technical requirements. Experts unanimously believed that compared with imported similar products, domestic gas ultrasonic flow meters have advantages such as high cost performance, good after-sales service, and friendly operation interface, with great application potential. The trial use of domestic gas ultrasonic products in Sinopec has achieved phased results, providing good reference and demonstration for future batch promotion and application. The next step will continue to steadily promote and expand the application.

With the rapid expansion of the natural gas field, it provides a broad space for the gas ultrasonic flow meter of Zhejiang Wes. However, domestic gas ultrasonic flow meters need continuous improvement to fully catch up with or even surpass similar products of foreign well-known manufacturers. Currently, the gas ultrasonic products of Zhejiang Wes have started batch application in PetroChina, and have passed the industrial trial use of Sinopec this time. It is believed that domestic gas ultrasonic flow meters will play a greater role in the development of China's natural gas industry.
