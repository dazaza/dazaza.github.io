"Sniper" to be released at the end of the month: Shawn Yue breaks through to play a villain who actually conducts live experiments.

by mlwang on 2011-12-20 15:33:19

"Deadly Sniper" Poster

After a year of preparation, Yuwen Le's transformation masterpiece "Deadly Sniper" has been confirmed to hit nationwide on December 30. This time in "Deadly Sniper", Yuwen Le plays the big villain for the first time. For A Le who has always presented himself as a big boy, this is indeed a great challenge. Yu Wenle has high expectations for this new film and will tour the mainland before New Year's Day to promote this film.

Although the Spring Festival season already has two well-received Chinese blockbusters, "The Flowers of War" and "Dragon Gate Assassin", "Deadly Sniper" is the only action police thriller. Differentiated competition may be the only way to win in this season.

Whether from the story setting or overall effect, the film is completely in the style of Hollywood commercial blockbuster. Pursuit, conspiracy, gun battles, large-scale explosions and sniper scenes are frequent throughout the film. There is a turning point every three minutes, and the entire movie has no dull moments. The ending takes an unexpected turn. The character played by Yu Wenle is also the first anti-hero he has ever taken on since his career began. In the past, Yu Wenle's screen roles have mostly been positive, but this time his breakthrough is not just about playing a villain, but a deeply hidden, meticulous thinker. A Le said that he has been paying attention to seeking breakthroughs and transformations since he has been in the industry for quite some time, but mainly waiting for the right opportunity. "Deadly Sniper" happens to provide such an opportunity. "I hope everyone can seriously consider this character, although he is a villain, he is not a fool. His mind is very crazy but his thinking is clear. This is not an ordinary action police thriller. I think it is more like a game of wits, of course, the whole movie is very explosive."

Yu Wenle makes a sharp debut as a villain.

"Deadly Sniper" Plot Summary

The story tells of an award-winning journalist Cai Ni, who during the investigation of a news story, discovers a shocking secret. A chemical company is conducting a very terrible experiment. They use ordinary people as guinea pigs, looking for an antibody to a new lethal virus (X Plan).

Cai Ni begins to rescue innocent victims, but is discovered by the pharmaceutical company president Sang (Yu Wenle). Sang is cunning and deeply hidden, relentlessly pursuing Cai Ni to silence her; while the head of the government security bureau's action team, A Jie, becomes panicked by the consequences of X Plan. To cover up the news and prevent further spread, A Jie is ordered to find and eliminate all victims infected with the virus!

To ensure her daughter's safety, Cai Ni's father hires top-notch killer A Da as her bodyguard. A cruel experiment traps Cai Ni, Sang, A Jie, and A Da in the situation, and Sang pulls out all the stops, setting up layer upon layer of traps. Cai Ni finds herself unable to trust anyone with the mysterious bodyguard, Sang's pursuit, and the security bureau's secret actions! On the escape route, three groups of people, tanks and planes crazily intercept her. The continuous deaths of friends around Cai Ni, a series of assassination events and explosions, thick clouds of suspicion, relentless pursuits, she must survive against all odds.
