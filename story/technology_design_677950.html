The New Oath of AIKA Sets Sail

by xinsdyn56 on 2012-02-27 10:53:04

[Thousand-person National War, Grand and Magnificent] Currently, the management work of "New Oath" is proceeding in an orderly manner. With the deepening of the operational work, "New Oath" will definitely bring more surprises to the players. Players shape Fran's personality through interaction, and Fran will also adopt different stances based on the level of intimacy with the players. This truly realizes a two-way interaction between players and Fran. In addition to the thousand-person national war and Fran's cultivation, the diversified game system of "New Oath" is also very attractive. The coherent and tightly-paced mission system allows players to quickly immerse themselves in the game; the large team consisting of up to 24 people pioneers a new model of team combat; the highly humanized social system makes the game more convenient, allowing for both upgrading and socializing without interruption. In short, the diversified system can provide players with a more comfortable gaming experience.

C7game's 3D fantasy adventure cultivation masterpiece "AIKA" has officially announced its Chinese name as "New Oath," and the official website will be open to the public soon. "New Oath" is a reformative work of 3DMMORPG, featuring exquisite graphics and tight missions, and supports thousand-person city siege battles. Moreover, the game has uniquely created the Fran cultivation system, allowing players to relive thrilling memories in online games and continue the cultivation story.

[Fran Elf, Perfect Cultivation Plan] Cultivation has always been the exclusive domain of single-player games. This time, "New Oath" combines "cultivation" with "online games" for the first time, which is a groundbreaking move in the history of MMORPGs. Unlike the pet systems in general games, Fran's personality and growth direction are shaped through daily interactions with the players. The dialogues and interactions between Fran and the players will directly affect her personality and affection towards the players.

"New Oath" is the only game that can achieve the largest-scale battle in history. Various powers in the game compete against each other, and the city siege battle is one of the major features of "New Oath." Unlike other games where battles are based on guilds, "New Oath" supports large-scale battles of 1000 people VS 1000 people. The thousand-person national war, with its grand scene, brings unprecedented audio-visual shocks to the players.
