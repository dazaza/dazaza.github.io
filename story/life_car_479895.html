2011 Latest Research Report on China's Car Beauty Industry

I. Precise Analysis of China's Car Beauty Industry

The expansion of China's car beauty industry: In recent years, there have been schools established in China that specialize in car beauty and maintenance technology. Establishing a car maintenance training school has become a hot spot and one of the development strategies for many enterprises. Reasons why car product merchants start vocational schools: a) quick market benefits; b) huge market gap; c) enterprise strategy diversification.

II. Factors Affecting Industry Development - Pros and Cons

Positive factors: In 2004, the car beauty industry quietly underwent changes. On one hand, excessive low-level competition caused profit declines in some regions of the beauty industry, increasing consumer complaints, and small business bankruptcies during the reshuffling process. On the other hand, foreign car beauty companies increased their investment in China, with well-known North American car maintenance products such as Auto Brothers entering the Chinese market. A large amount of private capital also began to focus on the car beauty market. The concept of car beauty was upgraded: in terms of product technology, the product upgrades in the car beauty industry accelerated in 2004, with more extensive conceptual promotion. Domestic companies proposed the slogan of "star-rated beauty" and projects like "bubble bath" car washes, offering consumers an entirely new concept. For consumers, car beauty was no longer just a street-side term but had moved up in hierarchy.

New technologies spread quickly: New technologies appeared very rapidly in the car beauty field. In 2004, "crystallization" spread quickly in the car beauty industry, followed by the concept of "coating," which became extremely popular in the industry, with various coating products emerging. This indicated that domestic car beauty was rapidly narrowing the gap with international car beauty industries.

Businesses gradually became calm: In the large car beauty market, many businesses rushed in without much thought. By 2004, different voices began to appear. The era of high profits in car beauty was gradually passing, and how to seriously view this market showed that car beauty was gradually maturing.

Negative factors: Domestic car maintenance is still in its initial stage. Market operators vary greatly in quality, lacking relevant national management regulations, unified industry standards, product and service norms, complete distribution channels, professional technical personnel, and standardized technical operation standards, all of which are currently urgent problems to be solved. When an industry is in a high-speed development and expansion phase, it is inevitable that there will be a mix of good and bad players. An immature market and unregulated industry lead to various fraudulent behaviors and counterfeit inferior products rampant, harming consumers and causing various disputes.

1. Severe product homogenization issues.

2. Brands are messy, varying in quality, and brand strength is weak.

3. Low quality of employees, lack of standardized operations.

Due to the low technological content of car care products, once a new product comes out, it is easily imitated and plagiarized upon its release, leading to severe product homogenization in the market, making products lack competitiveness.

4. Strong dependence of maintenance companies on raw materials.

The car care industry is a fragile one, highly dependent on costs. Once raw material prices rise, small enterprises generally find it hard to continue. This restricts the survival of car care businesses. High oil and gas prices simultaneously increase energy, transportation, packaging costs, and chemical product prices, leading to rising raw material costs, causing a batch of small and medium-sized enterprises to go bankrupt. Meanwhile, as part of the after-sales car market, the car care industry is heavily influenced by the overall car market. Changes in the number of cars and their usage frequency will cause fluctuations in the care industry.

5. Imperfect related laws and regulations.

III. Estimation of the Market Size and Development Prospects of China's Car Beauty Industry

China's auto market is showing a rapid growth momentum, and that year was publicly recognized by many industry experts as the beginning of China's entry into an automobile society. In terms of car consumption structure, the proportion of family purchases is increasing, becoming the main demand subject. From now on, China will enter a stable growth period of a healthy car market. With fierce competition, car sales are increasingly moving towards a micro-profit era, while the after-sales car market shows great potential and a bright future due to changes in the car consumption structure and the maturity of consumer consumption concepts. Currently, the domestic after-sales car market is relatively chaotic and is still in the early stages of adjustment and expansion. However, the enormous market potential and rapid growth speed provide excellent market opportunities for merchants. After the market opens, multiple forces are competing in the domestic car repair and maintenance market. With the entry of foreign car service giants, competition in the after-sales car market becomes increasingly fierce. The substantial improvement in core technology, product quality, and service awareness in the automotive manufacturing industry forces car repair companies to invest in advanced maintenance and testing equipment, providing a rare development opportunity for the car protection industry. However, the actual threshold is low, and market competition is disorderly. Due to the large space in the low-price equipment market, car protection equipment manufacturers lower prices to gain market share, making price wars the main marketing method. Price reductions inevitably affect quality and indirectly harm brand establishment negatively. Foreign similar products feature stable quality, strong brand awareness, excellent processing technology, low return rates, aesthetically pleasing designs, and long lifespans, with some equipment even usable for life. For example, Japan's electronic paint mixing system is currently the most advanced in the world. In the past, countries' electronic paint mixing devices found car paint colors and formulas through car frame numbers. However, Japan's newly developed paint mixing device scans the car body with a scanner connected to a computer network. The computer quickly displays the car's brand, model, paint color, and formula and automatically prints out the results. Domestic product performance is unstable, fault rates are low, appearance design involves plagiarism, many companies lack their own brands, can only sell at low prices, and ultimately struggle to gain long-term market recognition. China's car protection equipment manufacturers must break geographical, industrial, and ownership limitations, achieve strong alliances, develop large-scale, high-quality, low-cost, and marketable equipment products, introduce foreign technology, produce their own quality products, and only then can they remain invincible in competition. Compared to the whole vehicle market, the car protection industry market has stronger growth potential because the growth of the car protection market mainly comes from the increase in car ownership. Even if the whole vehicle market sales growth stagnates, as long as the total number of vehicles in the after-sales market increases, the growth of the car protection industry is guaranteed; the industry's financial status is good, and companies' cost control capabilities are continuously strengthening. Even with an 8-10% price drop in whole vehicle enterprises, the profit margin of the entire car protection industry remains above 20%.

According to the profit calculation of a fully mature international car market, the total profit of China's after-sales car market is approximately 215.355 billion yuan. Apart from the 100 billion yuan market in the car electronics sector, the car protection industry still has a market exceeding 100 billion yuan. China's car protection industry has infinite broad development space in the next 5-10 years. As long as enterprises have perfect business decisions and adequate preparation, they can definitely secure a place in this market. Acceleration of product technology updates and replacements The widespread application of car electronics technology and products will inevitably bring about upgrades and replacements in car maintenance and care technology. Increasing detection instruments and equipment will appear in the car maintenance and care industry. At the same time, in car maintenance and care, model-specific and component-specific equipment will be more widely used. The more models manufacturers launch on the market, the greater the demand for specialized equipment will increase. Relying solely on imitation and replication will make it difficult to survive in the market in the future. Domestic car protection equipment manufacturers must increase their own technology and product development efforts and continuously launch new products. The trend of domestic brands strengthening and international brands localizing production Experts predict that car maintenance and care equipment, like cars and car parts, will also see a trend of international brands localizing production in the future. In the market, domestic brands and international brands will coexist and compete over the long term. In the competition, only those manufacturers whose products lead in technology, have solid quality, and focus on marketing and services will be the ultimate winners.

IV. Competitive Landscape and Future Trends of China's Car Beauty Industry

Foreign and private enterprises increase investment domestically: Following international giants like 3M entering the car beauty industry, a large amount of private capital also began to flow into the car beauty industry. Hong Kong's Golden Eagle Investment Company announced an investment of 3 billion yuan, using "Car's Journey" as a brand to enter the car beauty industry. As the car beauty industry welcomes its continuously expanding market, foreign and private capital also prepares to enter at this time. Broad prospects for professional beauty care: Car beauty is increasingly moving towards professionalism and standardization. Car beauty companies will strengthen services and management, and the standardization of car beauty operations becomes what consumers focus on. More intense competition, less obvious advantages: Unlike the high profits during the early stages of the car care industry, the gross margin space in the car aftermarket will further narrow in 2005, and competition in each project will become fiercer, with the competitive advantage reflected in market share becoming less prominent. A new pattern emerges in car beauty: Car beauty forms a new pattern of mutual competition, coexistence, and symbiosis across high, medium, and low consumption levels. Low-end is waxing, mid-range is crystallization, and high-end is silicon membrane coating. Waxing holds the largest market share, exceeding 70%; second is crystallization, accounting for about 20% of the market share; high-end is silicon membrane coating, holding less than 10% of the market share.

"One-stop solution" is the direction: Experts point out that to ensure the benign operation of the car beauty service industry, besides quickly introducing industry regulations for constraints, the key is to vigorously develop superstores. The "one-stop solution" consumption model allows superstores to further develop the market toward specialization and standardization. Car decoration and care centered around car beauty will form a one-stop comprehensive service.

Focus on brands and promotion: After the enhancement of the car interior care industry, companies pay more attention to shaping and promoting their image and brand, using various forms to build their brand.

V. Management System and Policy Analysis of China's Car Beauty Industry

The current government management model and legal system still have many imperfections. There are numerous policies and regulations, with significant gaps compared to internationally accepted management methods. Content overlaps and conflicts, which is not conducive to market development.

1. Some policy regulations are too general, and supporting laws and regulations have not caught up, lacking detailed implementation rules and specific operational norms.

2. Management functions are divided too finely, with multi-headed management and complex regulations.

3. Management functions overlap, with some policy regulations having overlapping and conflicting content.

4. Lack of social management policies and regulations.

5. Some regulations are not fully implemented, with discrepancies from reality.

6. Insufficient protection of consumer rights.
