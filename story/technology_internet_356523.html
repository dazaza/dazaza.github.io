Related thematic articles: Lanzhou hotel, Tianjin hotel, Tongcheng Network hotel reservation. People always say that small towns have many stories. Let's start with the old Jin city at the beginning of its establishment. They say beauty is in the water of one's hometown, and affection is in the soil of one's hometown. Twenty years ago, the map of Jin urban area was as small as a fingernail, and now it must be about half the size of a palm. Zhongjiazhuang, Xishangzhuang, the new development zone... etc., have already merged into the old urban area. The bustling Huanghua Street of the past, crowded with people, could be aptly described as bustling like a river full of carts and horses, but the impression of Huanghua Street was still somewhat bitter. Back then, the appearance of the city was "hardly bearable" to recall, not to mention being "ragged", but certainly "rustic" and more "rustic". Look at those thin alleys crisscrossing each other, and the uneven bungalows clinging to each other, their faces full of vicissitudes, looking like an elderly person in decline. In today's Jincheng people's eyes, the Hua Street of that time could be said to be bustling, but the word "rustic" took precedence. Nowadays, Hua Street is straight and wide, with beautiful buildings standing opposite each other, competing for brilliance, fully displaying their heroic nature. From Yaboli Mall to Fenghua Times Square, from Pacific Building to Jincheng Hotel, they are all vying with each other, none wanting to fall behind, each higher than the other, each more beautiful than the other, but every "person" maintains grace, neatly arranged. Especially when night falls, Hua Street is even more radiant, accompanied by streetlights, each "beauty" occasionally reveals a charming smile with twinkling eyes. From the smiling faces of passersby, you can get such an answer: wow! Today's Jincheng is really unrecognizable after 20 years, presenting a bright and youthful appearance. Walking through the night painting, I reached Fengtai Street. I have only seen the full view of Fengtai Street (night scene) in the album "Panoramic View of Jincheng". It resembles a giant dragon, spitting flames towards the end of the "Jin-Jiao Expressway". The picture of it can make people associate with a rising Jincheng, soaring into the sky and shooting off in all directions. Tonight, I am riding on this giant dragon, wandering on its most beautiful body. The vast earth welcomes a floating morning, slowly brushing past my face. With you every day for 20 years, counting the hundred-year-old hometowns, from Fengtai... Zezhou... enduring vicissitudes, today's Jincheng has arrived. If the hundred-year "Jincheng" is narrated by you, then today's Jincheng is explained by me. Jincheng Hotel Counting and remembering, countless memories lost and forgotten. Standing tall on the Taihang Mountains, you stand out uniquely, facing Central Plains, your face red and shining. You write development between the lines, using beauty to demonstrate the charm of speed, using charm to interpret history, forever written into a book, that is the history of Jincheng's development. History witnesses glory, beauty narrates fragments, seizing opportunities can welcome the future, jointly wishing for a youthful Jincheng, tomorrow will be better. Waving to stop, I got into a taxi. Boarding at the Pacific Building, the destination is Zezhou Park. Isn't this the botanical garden? Only people with memory like mine can remember you. A former scenic spot of Jincheng, now submerged among high-rise buildings, you represent the childhood memories of Jincheng. Looking to the right, a row of four or five-story buildings blocked my view, I saw a youthful Jincheng, and also remembered the childhood Jincheng. The past view full of bungalows stayed underfoot in memory. Moving forward, we reach the municipal government. The municipal government building hasn't changed much. Continuing forward, what came into sight was step-by-step memories. The former office building of the People's Bank of China could be called "high and mighty", but now, around it, none of them are inferior, look at the Jincheng Grand Hotel, see the Xiaoguang Building, and the Hengxin Building, all late bloomers, graceful and youthful. To the south of Xiaoguang Building is the Wumao Square. Thoughts paused, I must talk about the squares in Jincheng. For a person, youth highlights individuality, dyeing colorful hair is cool, attractive, and mesmerizing ten thousand people. For a modern metropolis, the square is a good place for citizens to relax and take a walk. Youthful Jincheng, the construction of squares attracts the attention of Jincheng people. Just in recent years, squares built include: Fengxi Square, Jincheng Hotel, Wumao Square, Huajie Square, Shuyuan Square, etc., which symbolize youth and mark the improvement of urban functions. Nowadays, Jincheng is a youthful Jincheng, a vibrant Jincheng, a burgeoning Jincheng, a rosy Jincheng. She has the gorgeous attire of beauties and the masculine charm of handsome men. Adorned Jincheng people, dressed in the aura of the times, integrating the speed of the times, taking bold steps on the red carpet of the times, carrying a steady posture and accelerating pace, walking out skyscrapers, walking out smooth roads. The elegant decoration demonstrates her exterior, paved asphalt represents her inner joy, all of which are her heartfelt expressions. Unconsciously, leaning forward, the car stopped, already at Zezhou Park. My heart also disembarked along with the car.
