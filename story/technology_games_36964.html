"Struggle for the World" Introduction of Four Classes:

1. General: Combat Mode: Possesses various close-combat skills against multiple enemies, can adapt to prolonged continuous combat, lacks ranged skills.

Specialized Weapons/Equipment: Spear, halberd, axe, shield, armor.

Advantages/Disadvantages: Extremely high health points, strong physical defense; weaker magic defense, no special advantage in one-on-one attack mode.

2. Swordsman: Combat Mode: Medium to long-range physical and magical attack skills, can cause significant damage to a single target in a short period.

Specialized Weapons/Equipment: Sword, blade, flying sword, armor.

Advantages/Disadvantages: Moderate health points, weak physical and magical defense, outstanding dodging ability, not good at prolonged combat.

3. Doctor: Combat Mode: Possesses various attack methods, powerful healing and individual auxiliary abilities, effective not only for oneself but also for the team.

Specialized Weapons/Equipment: Staff, needle, robe.

Advantages/Disadvantages: No obvious advantages or disadvantages in all kinds of battles, slower battle rhythm, belongs to a moderate class.

4. Taoist: Combat Mode: Long-range magical attack skills, possesses powerful attack capabilities whether facing multiple or single targets.

Specialized Weapons/Equipment: Magic sword, staff, magic tool, robe.

Advantages/Disadvantages: Extremely low health points, high magical defense, weak physical defense, at a disadvantage in close combat.

"Struggle for the World": Recommended Stat Distribution for Each Class

Due to personal preferences, you can freely distribute every upgrade point. Of course, I will also recommend a standard balanced stat distribution method for each class. The following recommended stat distribution is based on 20 upgrade points at level 4:

General: Strength 8, Constitution 8, Agility 4, always maintain the ratio of Strength:Constitution:Agility = 2:2:1 when leveling up.

Swordsman: Strength 5, Constitution 5, Agility 10, always maintain the ratio of Strength:Constitution:Agility = 1:1:2 when leveling up.

Taoist: Spirituality 8, Enlightenment 8, Agility 4, always maintain the ratio of Spirituality:Enlightenment:Agility = 2:2:1 when leveling up.

Doctor: Strength 4, Constitution 4, Spirituality 4, Enlightenment 4, Agility 4, all attributes maintain a 1:1:1:1:1 ratio.

Resetting Basic Character Attributes

Currently in the game, you can use the "Yijing Ximi Pill" to simultaneously complete job transfer and reset stats.

To obtain the "Yijing Ximi Pill", please refer to the "Change Profession" section in the class description.

After successfully using the "Yijing Ximi Pill", the character's basic attribute points will return to their state when the character was first created.
