Lu Chuan (Weibo) and Qin Lan (Weibo) have been in love for two years, and the two have always claimed that they are enjoying their relationship. This morning (October 25), Qin Lan showed off a diamond ring on Sina Weibo and said she was dazzled by the ring early in the morning. This has led many netizens to speculate whether Lu Chuan proposed to Qin Lan this morning, and if their happy event is approaching. (Click on the picture to enter Qin Lan's Weibo)

In 2009, after Lu Chuan and Qin Lan's romantic relationship was exposed, Lu Chuan admitted that they only started dating six months after the filming of "Nanjing! Nanjing!" was completed. Now, after two years of dating, both have previously stated that they were enjoying their relationship and had no plans to get married yet. During the shooting of Lu Chuan's new work, "The King's Feast," there were rumors that Lu Chuan and Qin Lan broke up due to Du Juan, who played Yu Ji. In response to these rumors, Lu Chuan called them baseless.

At 9:48 am today, Qin Lan suddenly showed off a diamond ring and a diamond necklace on her Sina Weibo, happily saying she was dazzled early in the morning, and mysteriously added that everyone would understand, waiting for the early start of work! Is it pretty? It dazzled me early in the morning. This is what I wanted in my heart, you know.
