1688 Authentic Mall teaches you how to start an online store

by anonymous on 2011-03-28 09:47:43

Nowadays, opening an online store is not only a way of starting a business; many people consider it as a way to enhance their skills and have fun. What exactly is the charm of running an online store that attracts so many people? Summarizing it, there are mainly the following points:

1. Zero cost, low risk

Compared with traditional stores, online stores do not require renting storefronts, paying taxes or utility bills. They only charge minimal fees for product listings and transactions, some even offer free services. Moreover, you can purchase goods on demand without worrying about inventory accumulation.

2. Flexible operation mode

Online stores are not restricted by traditional business hours or locations. Operators can run them full-time or part-time without having to invest large amounts of time in managing the store.

3. Broad consumer base

Since online stores are on the Internet, they face all possible netizens or consumers who might see the products. This group can be netizens from across the country, or even globally.

What exactly is involved in opening an online store?

The variety of online stores is vast, offering cosmetics, clothing, digital products, etc. Each category has great potential and a promising future. The key lies in how to proceed and what suits you best. Running an online store also requires certain techniques, which I will explain step-by-step below.

1. What to sell online?

Similar to traditional stores, the first step in opening an online store is deciding what to sell. The choice of products should be based on your interests, abilities, conditions, as well as product attributes and consumer needs.

Given my passion for calligraphy and paintings, naturally, I started a business selling calligraphy and paintings.

Tips for selecting items:

1. Preferably, items that are not easily available elsewhere, with unique features.

2. Avoid items from industries you're not familiar with.

3. Avoid bulky, heavy items with low prices.

2. Preparations before opening the store

After choosing what to sell, before opening the online store, you need to select a website that provides a personal store platform and register as a user. To ensure transaction security, corresponding identity and payment method verifications are required.

I chose Taobao because it's completely free. To open a store on Taobao, three conditions must be met: one, register as a member and pass verification; two, list at least 10 items for sale; three, to facilitate safe transactions, it is recommended to activate online banking.

1. User Registration

Log on to http://www.taobao.com and click "Free Registration" at the top of the page. In the opened page, input membership name, password, email, etc., then click the "Agree to the following service terms, submit registration information" button. Then, the registered email will receive a confirmation email. Open the link in the email, confirm, and complete the user registration.

Security tip: To ensure transaction safety, avoid overly simple passwords. It is recommended to use a combination of "English letters + numbers + symbols".

2. Identity Verification

Taobao stipulates that only after real-name verification can one sell items and open a store. Therefore, after registering as a user, corresponding verifications (including individual real-name verification and Alipay verification) are necessary. Specific steps are as follows:

Step One: Log in to Taobao, click "My Taobao" at the top of the page. In the opened page, click "Click here" next to "To sell items, first complete Alipay verification."

As shown in the figure.

Step Two: On the opened page, it will prompt that the Alipay account has not been activated. Click "Activate Alipay account here." In the popped-up page, input real name, ID type and number, Alipay password, etc., then click the "Save and immediately activate Alipay account" button.

Step Three: After successfully activating the Alipay account, go back to the original page, press F5 to refresh the page. Click the "Apply for Alipay Personal Real Name Verification" button, read the Alipay verification service terms, then click the "I have read..." button to continue.

Step Four: First, fill in personal information according to the prompts, click "Next"; then, choose ID verification. You can choose "Online Upload" or "Mail" copies of your ID, click "Next"; then, input bank card information, including bank, card number, province, city, etc., after completing the input, wait for Alipay to transfer funds within one day.

Friendly Tip: If uploading ID copies online, the image file size should be controlled within 200KB. If it's an IC ID, you also need to provide the back picture.

Step Five: After one day, reopen "My Taobao," click the corresponding link in the verification area to open the "Alipay Verification" page. In the "Bank Account Verification" area, click "Confirm Transfer Amount," then input the amount of funds Alipay injected into your bank account, click the "Confirm" button.

If the amount matches correctly, the entire Taobao identity verification process is completed.

3. Sourcing and photographing

A key factor in successfully opening an online store lies in the sourcing channels. For the same item, different sourcing channels result in different prices.

After passing the identity verification, you need to organize the items you already have. To display the items more intuitively to consumers, taking pictures is crucial. It's best to use appropriate graphic/image processing tools for format and size conversion, such as Photoshop, ACDSee, etc.

Tip: When taking pictures, it is recommended to use a simple background, like a white desk, highlight the main subject, ensure clarity, keep the picture size under 120KB, and prefer JPG format.

4. Posting Items

To open a store on Taobao, besides meeting the certification requirements, you also need to post over 10 items. Thus, after organizing the product information and pictures, you need to start posting your first item.

Friendly Tip: Without passing personal real-name verification and Alipay verification, you can post items, but they can only be listed in "Items in Warehouse," where buyers cannot see them. Only after passing the verification can you put them on shelves for sale.

Step One: Log in to Taobao, click "I Want to Sell" at the top of the page. On the opened page, you can choose between "Fixed Price" or "Auction" posting methods. Here, click "Fixed Price."

As shown in the figure.

Friendly Tip: "Fixed Price" has a set price, allowing immediate purchase by buyers; "Auction" starts without a reserve price, letting buyers bid to purchase.

Step Two: Choose the category, select a suitable category based on your product. For example, I chose details for stamps/antiques/calligraphy/paintings/collectibles/calligraphy/ink painting/beasts. Click the "Selected, Continue" button to proceed to the next step.

Step Three: Fill in the item information, this step is very important. First, in the "Item Information" area, come up with a good title, click the "Browse" button to upload the item picture, input the item description, quantity, start time, validity period, etc.; Next, in the "Transaction Conditions" area, input the item's selling price, location, shipping fee, payment method, etc. Other information can remain default settings, such as default use of Alipay payments, etc. Finally, click the "Confirm and Submit" button to post the item.

If posted successfully, a success page will appear below. Click "Here" to view the posted item page, click "Continue Posting Items" to post more items.

As shown in the figure.

Friendly Tip: If no bids have been placed yet and you need to modify the posted item information, you can do so in "My Taobao - I Am Seller - Selling Items."

After posting the items, it's best to regularly update and add new ones to prevent the store from being deleted by the system.

Item Posting Tips:

(1) Give the item a good title, adding appropriate keywords and symbols, such as ★●◆▲ characters;

(2) Use eye-catching and detailed item descriptions;

(3) Reasonable item pricing, taking a mid-low price among similar items;

(4) Reasonable shipping cost standards, referencing other similar items.

3. Getting a Free Store

Taobao offers certified members the opportunity to open a free store. As long as you post over 10 items, you can own your own store with an independent URL. On this webpage, you can place all your items and arrange them according to your style.

To get my store up and running sooner, I initially posted 10 items in one go, spending almost half a day. After finishing, I eagerly opened "My Taobao - Free Store Opening." Then, on the opened page, I named my store "Qi Bao Zhai"; in the "Store Category" section, I selected the category for my items; in the "Store Introduction" section, I input the brief content of the store introduction. Finally, I clicked the "Submit" button, and on the appeared page, it showed "Congratulations! Your store has been successfully created...," providing the store address.

4. Store Decoration is Important

After opening a free store, buyers can obtain their own space. Similar to traditional stores, to operate normally and attract customers, the store needs to be decorated accordingly, including designing a shop logo, categorizing items, recommending items, and setting the store style.

1. Basic Settings

Log in to Taobao, open "My Taobao - I am Seller - Manage My Store." In the "Store Management" section on the left, click "Basic Settings." In the opened page, you can modify the store name, store category, store introduction; manually input the main operating project; in the "Shop Logo" area, click the "Browse" button to select the pre-designed shop logo image; in the "Announcement" area, input the store announcement content, such as "Welcome to our store!", click the "Preview" button to check the effect.

2. Item Categorization

Categorizing items makes it convenient for buyers to search. In the "Manage My Store" page, you can click "Item Categories" on the left. Then, input the new category name, such as "Four Treasures of the Study," and enter the sorting number (indicating the arrangement position), click the "Confirm" button to add it.

Click the "Item List" button corresponding to the category, and through keyword searches, add the posted items for categorized management.

3. Recommended Items

Taobao's "Recommended Items" function allows you to showcase your top 6 items prominently in the store. Just open the "Manage My Store" page, click "Recommended Items" on the left. Then, in the opened page, select the items to recommend, and click the "Recommend" button.

4. Store Style

Different store styles suit different items, giving buyers different feelings. Generally, choose a light-colored, comfortable style. I chose the "Green Wild Fairy Tale" style template, which shows a preview on the right side, click the "Confirm" button to apply this style.

Friendly Tip: After successfully opening the store, if the number of items sold in your store is less than 10 for three consecutive weeks, you will receive a reminder from Taobao to increase the number of items. After another two weeks, if it still hasn't reached 10 items, the store will be released. After another week, if the number of items is still less than 10, the store will be deleted.

After decorating the store, a refreshed page appears before you.

5. Promotion is the Key to Success

The online store is opened, items are on the shelf, characteristics are present, but several weeks have passed without any sales, not even buyer messages. This is a common problem encountered by many novice sellers. At this point, you need to take the initiative.

I once faced such troubles, so I decided to promote the store through forum promotion, link exchange, window recommendation, and friend promotion.

1. Forum Promotion

The main method of forum promotion is through posting advertisements and using signature lines.

The former can be done on forums of various provinces or cities. If there is a section allowing advertisement posts, you can post advertisements, ensuring the content is detailed, the item images are beautiful, and keeping regular updates and top placement.

The latter involves changing the signature line on forums to include the URL, logo, slogan, and store name of your small store. Post some attractive threads to let interested friends visit your store through your signature line.

2. Link Exchange

In the early stages of opening a store, to boost popularity, you can exchange links with popular stores, utilizing free advertising to promote your small store. For example, Taobao provides up to 35 friendly links. Adding them is simple:

First, through Taobao's search function, search for all stores, and note down the names of the popular store owners.

Then, log in to http://www.taobao.com/help/wangwang/wangwang.php to download the Taobao buyer and seller communication tool - "Taobao Wangwang," add these popular store owner names, and request link exchanges.

If they agree to exchange, finally, open "My Taobao - I am Seller - Manage My Store," click "Friendly Links" on the left, then input the store owner's name, and click the "Add" button.

Friendly Tip: If you want to delete friendly links, check the store name at the bottom of the page, and click the "Delete" button.

3. Window Recommendation

Taobao's "Window Recommendation" function is a special feature provided for sellers. When buyers search or click "I Want to Buy" based on categories, the recommended items will appear on the search results page. To set the "Window Recommendation" function, open "My Taobao - I am Seller - Selling Items," choose the items to recommend in the window (items already recommended to the store homepage cannot be window-recommended again, i.e., items with a "Recommended" mark), and click the "Window Recommendation" button.

Friendly Tip: Window recommendation promotes good items to Taobao for more views and click rates, while store recommendations promote items to prominent spots on the store homepage. The number of window recommendation slots is determined by your item count, monthly credit rating, and transaction volume, defaulting to 10.

Recommendation Tip: To increase the chances of item searches, regularly check Taobao's popular search keywords. If related to your item categories, such as "Korean style" or "weight loss," you might consider modifying the item titles to include these popular keywords.

4. Friend Promotion

Once your online store opens, make sure to inform your friends and family. For instance, announce the store's opening via Alibaba or MSN Messenger, and change your personalized signature to include the store's URL. This way, word-of-mouth will spread, and more and more people will know about your store.

6. Items on Sale

When buyers purchase items, they generally inquire about some item-related information. They will ask questions online and wait for the seller's response before deciding whether to buy the item.

1. Checking Buyer Messages

After opening the store and promoting it, I would open "Taobao Wangwang" daily, waiting for buyer inquiries. If a buyer leaves a message for an item in the store, "Taobao Wangwang" will automatically pop up a message notification window.

Alternatively, log in to Taobao, sequentially open "My Taobao - I am Seller - Buyer Messages/Reply," and check the messages left by buyers in the opened page. Click "Awaiting My Reply" to reply to the messages.

Item Sales Tips:

(1) Discount Strategy. Set different discount standards based on the quantity purchased by the buyer to attract buyers.

(2) Promotion Strategy. Use holidays or store anniversaries to promote items at lower prices to increase store popularity.

(3) Auction Strategy. Use auctioned items to promote the store, such as adding links to other items in the auctioned items.

2. Checking if Items Are Sold

If an item has been sold, it can be checked through three ways:

One, log in to "Taobao Wangwang," the system will notify you that a buyer has purchased the item.

Two, check through "My Taobao" to see if the item has been sold. Simply log in to Taobao, sequentially open "My Taobao - I am Seller - Sold Items."

Three, log in to the email registered for Taobao, Taobao will send relevant notification emails.

7. After the Item is Sold

After an item is sold, in addition to receiving corresponding sale reminders, you also need to proactively contact the buyer, request payment, ship the item, and handle evaluations or complaints after the transaction is completed.

1. Check Sold Items

If a buyer purchases an item, Taobao will notify the seller through "Taobao Wangwang" or email. The seller can also log in to Taobao, open "My Taobao - I am Seller - Sold Items." In the "Contact Buyer" area, click "Leave Me a Message" to leave a message for the buyer via "Taobao Wangwang." If the buyer does not use "Taobao Wangwang," you can note down the buyer's ID and send an internal message.

2. Contact Transaction Details

After both parties communicate, agree on payment and shipping methods. To ensure the interests of both parties, it is recommended to use Alipay as the payment method.

3. Payment, Shipping

To prevent situations where the buyer receives the item but does not pay, sellers generally adopt the "payment received before shipping" method when selling items.

First, request the buyer to make the payment, usually through Alipay. After making the payment, the seller can open "My Taobao - I am Seller - Sold Items" to check. If the transaction status shows "Buyer Paid, Waiting for Seller to Ship," it indicates that Alipay has received the payment, at which point the seller can confidently ship the item to the buyer.

Friendly Tip: For sellers using Alipay transactions, you can open "My Taobao - Alipay Zone - Transaction Management" to manage ongoing transactions, such as transaction queries, refund management, etc.

After the buyer receives the item from the seller, they confirm in the transaction status, and finally, Taobao will transfer the payment to the seller's Alipay account. This completes the transaction. Don't forget to maintain contact with the buyer to increase the chances of them revisiting your store and purchasing items.

4. Evaluation, Complaints

After completing the transaction, both the buyer and the seller can open "My Taobao" for evaluations. Sellers can open "My Taobao - I am Seller - Sold Items," click "Evaluate" on the sold items, choose positive, neutral, or negative reviews based on actual situations, and input text content.

Friendly Tip: How to become a highly-creditable star seller on Taobao?

To become a star seller on Taobao, your credit score must be at least 4 points. The basis for a seller's credit score is the evaluation given by the buyer after each successful Alipay transaction. A "positive review" adds one point, a "neutral review" adds no points, and a "negative review" deducts one point. Therefore, to become a star seller, remember to provide honest service. For specific star and credit comparison relationships, refer to http://service.taobao.com/support/153-156-164/help-842.htm.

If there are cases of online orders not being fulfilled or non-payment upon receipt, sellers can open "My Taobao - Credit Management - Report Violations" to file complaints and reports, but evidence such as shipping receipts, buyer sign-off proofs, and Wangwang screenshots must be collected.
