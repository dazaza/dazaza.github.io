A lavish breakfast is prepared.

by nlqo9709 on 2010-05-31 18:16:52

Shinichi was staring at his breakfast, finding it hard to eat.

"What's wrong? Doesn't it taste good?"

"It's not that..."

"Then why do you look like it's so hard to eat? It's really disappointing!" Rachel pouted.

"... " Taking a deep breath, Shinichi decided it would be better to just get it over with. "Do you remember what I said yesterday..."

"Hmm? Yesterday? What about?" Rachel, who was biting her chopsticks, thought for a moment, "Are you talking about pretending not to know each other at school?"

"Ahhh!" Shinichi nodded.

Yesterday, when Rachel said she had transferred to Teitan and asked Shinichi to guide her at school, Shinichi, after much deliberation, stammered out the suggestion to pretend they didn't know each other at school to avoid any trouble. He thought Rachel would make things difficult for him, but unexpectedly, she readily agreed, making Shinichi suspect that Rachel's "guide me" might have been another way of teasing him.

Seeing Rachel remember, Shinichi quickly finished his meal and said, "I'm leaving first!" before rushing out the door as if he were afraid Rachel would change her mind.

"Hey!" Rachel tried to call him back, but Shinichi had already moved too fast, leaving Rachel feeling quite frustrated.

"I was just asking where the school is... Am I really that scary?" Rachel muttered to herself as she started cleaning up the dishes.

Unbelievably, the uniform for Teitan is quite nice. Thinking about the white and gold edged uniform Shinichi wore when he left, Rachel sighed in admiration. However, she hadn't received her uniform yet, so she could only wear casual clothes. With this thought, Rachel stepped out the door.

But soon enough, she stopped.

"Which way should I go..." Looking left and right without any clue, Rachel took out a piece of paper, folded it into a plane, and said, "Let's go whichever way the plane flies..."

The plane flew out, but a gust of wind blew it back to Rachel's feet.

"This is troublesome... I'm going to be late..." Checking her watch, the opening ceremony was starting in two minutes.

Rachel picked up the plane and tossed it into the trash can, then took a step forward in a random direction.

Not knowing how long she had walked, feeling a bit tired, Rachel entered a coffee shop to rest. Anyway, there was no way she could reach school today, so she might as well wander around. She had been here for one or two months, but except for the supermarket, she hadn't explored the area nearby.

Suddenly, a thought struck Rachel: Could she take a taxi since she didn't know the way...

Rejecting this thought immediately, Rachel shook her head. She needed to save money and not waste it.

Finally, she concluded: All this was that guy's fault! Everything was his mistake! Causing her to miss school on time, walking so much that now she even lost her way home. For the next few days, he'd be responsible for breakfast! And he could figure out dinner himself!

Hmph, such a stingy person!

"Ah-choo!" Shinichi sneezed just as he finished his speech at the end of the opening ceremony.

"What's wrong?" Vice President Aya Tenno asked concernedly. They hadn't seen each other from the end of the cram school until today because of the busy schedule for the opening ceremony, and she wasn't about to let this opportunity pass.

Shinichi smiled and said, "Nothing, maybe someone is talking about me."

--------------_____-------_____--------_____------------

"Didn't the principal say there would be a transfer student today? Why hasn't he come?" The homeroom teacher looked at his watch and questioned.

"Report!"

"Come in! Thank you for your hard work, Suzuki!" The homeroom teacher temporarily put aside his confusion as there were still many things to do on the first day of school, "Just place it here."

Yumi noticed the transfer application and file on the side and asked, "Teacher, is there a transfer student?"

"Yes, but I don't know why he hasn't arrived yet." The teacher frowned.

"Eh, it's fine. He'll come eventually. I'm off!"

"Okay."

--------Dividing Line---------

"Sorry, Sonoko, there were too many things today." The student council secretary, Hakuba Sousuke, hurried to Class 3-G as soon as the student council duties were done.

"It's alright, anyway, I have nothing to do at home."

"Let's go then!"

"By the way, we have a new classmate."

"Huh? There's a transfer student at this time? What kind of person is it?"

"I don't know!"

"Don't know?" Hakuba Sousuke asked in confusion.

"Because he didn't come today. I hope it's a handsome guy..." After saying this, Sonoko started dreaming.

"Stop daydreaming!" Giving Sonoko a glare, Hakuba Sousuke had an expression of "I can't handle you."

"Hehe, having dreams is better than not having any!"

"Yeah yeah! You win!"

As they talked, they walked and soon exited the school gate.

--------------------&&---------------

"Finally done! Everyone, thank you for your hard work!" Shinichi thanked the student council members with a steady smile.

"Thank you for your hard work!"

Everyone packed up their things and left, but Shinichi lingered.

"Chairman, is there something else?" Seeing Shinichi had no intention of leaving, Aya Tenno stayed behind.

"Eh?" Shinichi, who was spacing out, was slightly startled by Aya's sudden appearance, but he didn't show it on his face, "No, nothing."

There's definitely something wrong! This morning, it seemed like he heard that person calling him, but he ran away like a thief. Will he get punished when he goes back? Today, Shinichi truly liked the student council work he always disliked, even wishing there were more things to do.

But how could he show that? He was one of the best student council presidents in previous years.

"I've told you not to call me Chairman, it sounds weird, call me Kudo." Shinichi's smile was always perfect.

"Oh..." Calling his surname rather than his name... Though disappointed, being able to talk about anything other than work-related matters was enough.

"It's not early anymore, let's go together." There was no way not to return; he could only behave well and hope not to be bullied too much! Shinichi sighed countless times in his heart.

"Eh? Okay..." Because she was originally just planning to ask if he needed help, Rachel hadn't noticed everyone else had left, and her face turned red upon realizing she was alone with Shinichi.

Although Shinichi had a smiling face, his mind was wandering, completely unaware of his surroundings, so he didn't notice Aya's blushing cheeks.
