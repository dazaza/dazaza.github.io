Nokia 6760S gray market price, moving restart call fee exchange for fire cargo phone, 8 manufacturers.

by anonymous on 2011-08-12 17:11:52

On November 12th, it was reported that Beijing Mobile of China Mobile (hereinafter referred to as Beijing Mobile) launched a prepaid phone fee for grey market phones activity starting from November 9th, targeting Global Pass users with the Nokia 6760S grey market price. The activity is divided into three options: commitment periods of 12 months, 18 months, and 24 months. Users simply need to prepay their phone fees (or pay a deposit), and commit to spending a certain amount each month, to exchange for G3 grey market phones at a subsidized price, with the lowest option being a free phone.

For example, if a user participates in the "24-month commitment plan": by choosing the 128 yuan package, committing to an average monthly spend of 128 yuan (Nokia 6760S grey market price), and ensuring a minimum monthly spend of 102 yuan while staying connected for 24 months, they need to prepay/deposit 1560 yuan in phone fees and additionally pay 3170 yuan for the promotional offer, to receive one HTC T9188 phone. China Mobile will return 65 yuan in phone fees to the user each month.

If the user chooses the 388 yuan package, committing to an average monthly spend of 388 yuan, with a minimum monthly spend of 310 yuan, while staying connected for 24 months, they need to prepay/deposit 4560 yuan in phone fees to achieve a "0-yuan purchase" of the HTC (HTC) T9188 phone. China Mobile will return 190 yuan in phone fees to the user each month.

It is understood that a total of 21 models from 8 brands are participating in this event: Samsung, HTC, MOTO, Coolpad, Dopod, Dell, SonyEricsson, and Nokia.

Global Pass individual users participating in the event can go to designated service halls or the online store of Beijing Mobile to handle the procedures. Currently, only online payment and on-site cash payment methods are supported. In the last four days of each month, the website channel does not accept applications for this business. Global Pass VIP Diamond, Gold Card (including Gold Card Experience Card) customers, and customers who have been connected for ten years, can choose the 0-prepayment 0-deposit option when handling the procedures through the website. Regular corporate consignment customers and consignment-fulfilled-for-one-year regular corporate customers can also handle the grey market phone exchange event on the website, but customers can only choose the "deposit" method when processing.

Beijing Mobile reminds users that customers who have previously handled various phases of grey market phone exchanges for prepaid phone fees, various phases of prepaid return phone fees (return recharge card/exchange gift), international business travel packages, Global Pass Family Happiness Packages, centralized payment member services, etc., cannot participate in this event simultaneously. Customers cannot change their phone models or commitment plans during the event agreement period.

Customers handling this event need to activate the new book express package service for grey market phones (information fee 5 yuan/month). During the service activation period, the system will directly waive the 5 yuan monthly fee for the "new book express package" service each month, but any other costs incurred by the customer using the service will be borne by themselves.
