A post titled "Perverted civil servant beats his pregnant wife and father-in-law and repeatedly rapes his wife in public places" has been widely circulated online for nearly a month since it was posted on January 17. The poster is the "female victim", who claimed in the post that her husband is a civil servant at Zhongshan Intermediate People's Court, and he had repeatedly beaten her and her father, and forced her to have sexual intercourse in some public places. By last night, the post had received nearly 50,000 clicks and over 663 replies. Yesterday, Xin Kuai Daily reporters contacted the poster, Amei, and her husband, Wang Moumou. Amei said that there was absolutely no falsehood in her post, while Wang Moumou admitted that he is a clerk at Zhongshan Intermediate People's Court and a civil servant, but he denied the accusations in the post, repeatedly stating that "the truth will be revealed tomorrow by the court."

The female party disclosed details in an interview, and it was reportedly claimed that the court would conduct a closed-door trial today.

In a post on Tianya Community, the poster Amei claimed in a plea for help that her husband, Wang Moumou, is a clerk at Zhongshan Intermediate People's Court (hereinafter referred to as "Zhongshan Intermediate Court"). After marriage, her husband has continuously subjected her to domestic violence, sexual violence, and sexual abuse, and frequently insulted her parents. During her menstrual cycle and pregnancy, he never stopped having sex with her, causing her to bleed incessantly and requiring her to take medicine to preserve the fetus. When she was three months pregnant, he and her father beat her up. Amei posted that she could no longer endure this, so she reported it to the police. He then told her and her family, "I am from the court, you can sue wherever you want, the court is my home," "Is it really that impressive to be a civil servant in a law enforcement unit? He is too bullying."

On January 18, the day after Amei posted, a netizen named "Wang20121212" also replied, saying, Mei, as the saying goes, "One day of marriage equals a hundred days of kindness." If we cannot become lifelong partners, there is no need to become enemies. Others still keep family shame private, but now we have thrown our shame to the whole world. I did not give you a happy marriage or a joyful life, as a husband, I should reflect on myself. Hereby, I express my deep apology to you; due to not handling the relationship between both sets of parents well, our marriage fell into crisis, and because we did not calmly face each other, it led to today's situation, which pains me deeply, and I believe you feel the same way; when you were hospitalized for four days after an abortion, I specially took leave to accompany you. For these four days, I stayed with you all the time. During your hospitalization, not only did I stay by your side, but my father also cared for you for more than ten hours every day. Just a few days after the abortion, with you protecting your wound with a tissue, how dare I misbehave? How could I misbehave? Moreover, the toilet is less than two meters away from the room."

Narrating the Marriage

Meeting in the Morning and Proposing in the Evening

Yesterday, the reporter met the poster Amei at Zhongshan Langqingxuan. She told the reporter that she is from Jilin, 32 years old this year. After returning to China last July, she came to Zhongshan to take care of her parents. Before returning to China, she spent eight years in Singapore teaching at an international kindergarten. Due to the nature of her work, she rarely interacted with men, so she had never been in a romantic relationship. It wasn't until after returning to China that, through acquaintances, she met Wang Moumou. "That morning after meeting through a blind date, he asked me out in the evening and proposed to me. Although I thought highly of him during our first encounter, it was too rushed, so I didn't accept his proposal." Amei told the reporter. Unexpectedly, a month later, Wang Moumou's parents actually showed up at her place, asking her to marry Wang Moumou. They said that they had already sent out wedding invitations and booked tables in their hometown in Gansu, and if the wedding didn't happen, they wouldn't have the face to return to Gansu. However, her parents still refused. Amei also told the reporter that during their interactions, Wang Moumou often touched her inappropriately. "I mentioned this to my mother before, but she said that since he is an adult, it's not strange. Unbeknownst to us, after marriage, he became even crazier, frequently showing me naked pictures of his ex-girlfriend, telling me to learn from her."

It is understood that on August 30, 2011, less than two months after meeting, Amei and Wang Moumou officially registered their marriage in Zhongshan. This "lightning-fast" union seemed to set the stage for their painful marriage that lasted less than half a year.

Kneeling at the Doorstep Moved Two Elders' Hearts

"The daughter has been very obedient from childhood, with a rather weak character. Because her age isn't small anymore, my wife and I hope she finds a good home soon. Although Wang Moumou comes from a poor family, he initially gave a very good impression, speaking sweetly, so my wife particularly liked him. When we rejected this marriage proposal the next day, Wang Moumou knelt from the entrance of the neighborhood all the way to our doorstep, asking us to marry our daughter to him, and he provided us with a pre-marriage commitment letter: no divorce after marriage, mutual respect, trust, tolerance, and fidelity..." Amei's father told the reporter during an interview. The reporter noticed that this pre-marriage commitment letter was signed by both parties, their parents. Regarding Amei's marriage, her father regretfully stated: At the time, we mainly felt that Wang Moumou is a civil servant at Zhongshan Intermediate People's Court, certainly educated, and his character wouldn't be too bad. Plus, his parents are employees of a certain public institution, so we agreed to this marriage out of soft-heartedness. Unexpectedly, when we went to their hometown in Jinchang, Gansu, to handle the wedding, Wang Moumou turned against us and unreasonably demanded that the bride's side pay the groom's side 18,000 yuan as a gift. We disagreed, so he cursed at us. We were so angry that we wanted to go home and not hold the wedding. His family chased us to the train station and begged us to stay, signing another guarantee letter on the spot.

After Marriage, Claiming Constant Abuse by Her Husband

"Wang Moumou has a very suspicious personality, often suspecting others of luring me or me luring others. Remember last September, we were taking wedding photos at Jin夫人 in Zhongshan. A young man took our pictures, and during the process, we casually chatted, but he thought others were luring me and started cursing without any reason. Later, when my family and I went to pick the photos, that young man saw us from afar and hid, making us feel very embarrassed. Another time was when we were renovating the house, I was ironing clothes outside, but Wang Moumou suspected I was luring the workers, so he made me go back inside and not come out..." Amei told the reporter, thus leading to frequent arguments between them. Moreover, Wang Moumou often abused her physically, "During my menstrual cycle and while I was pregnant, he never stopped having sex with me." As a result, she bled incessantly and had to take medicine to preserve the fetus.

"To help get the birth certificate for the baby, I returned to my hometown in Jilin for more than a week. I don't know what happened during this period, but after I returned to Zhongshan from Jilin, he treated me very coldly. Especially his mother was very strange, whichever quilt we slept under, she took it, and when we got another quilt, she still took it. Whichever pillow we used, she took it too. Finally, his mother took the quilt and pillows to her room, and then Wang Moumou also went to sleep with his mother, locking the door behind him. On December 16, due to losing a thermos bottle, I had a conflict with his mother, and when he came back, without saying anything, he dragged me off the bed onto the ground and beat me up. Two days later, in broad daylight in the parking lot of Zhongshan No.1 Middle School, he forcibly pulled me into the car and sexually assaulted me. In his office within his workplace, when no one was around, he also forcibly had sex with me. I repeatedly knelt down and begged him to spare me and the poor child in my belly..." Amei cried as she told the reporter.

Event Progress

Unit Discipline Department Intervenes in Investigation

According to the telephone number provided by Amei, the reporter called Director Huang of Wang Moumou's department yesterday afternoon. Upon learning the reporter's identity, he stated that Wang Moumou is not in his department, and he doesn't know which department Wang Moumou belongs to. Regarding the relevant handling opinions of the Intermediate Court towards Wang Moumou, Director Huang said: "I don't know if he is currently working normally, and I'm unclear about the handling opinions. In short, the matter has now been handed over to the disciplinary inspection department of the Intermediate Court for handling, and I don't know anything else." He then hung up hurriedly. The reporter then called the director of the disciplinary inspection department of the Intermediate Court to inquire about the progress, and the reply was: "We just began accepting the case last week, and we are currently investigating. We will notify the female party and her family if there are any developments." Before the reporter could finish speaking, the director cut the call citing a meeting.

Domestic Violence

Police: Three People Injured on the Day of the Incident

Amei told the reporter that the most severe conflict between the two occurred on December 16 last year. Around 4 PM that day, because she lost a thermos bottle, her mother-in-law criticized her, leading to some quarrels. After Wang Moumou came home from work and learned about this, he threw all her luggage, including suitcases and clothes, out to the elevator area. She called her father for help. "After my father arrived, Wang Moumou punched his face and hit him with a chair," Amei said.

Mr. Zuo, Amei's father, introduced that around 6 PM that evening, he went to his daughter's home to mediate. "At the time, I thought young people arguing was just a momentary impulse, nothing serious. I smiled and told Wang to speak nicely and not get agitated. My daughter was moving the luggage back inside, but Wang Moumou didn't let her, and they started wrestling. He kicked Amei. Seeing my daughter being beaten, I quickly intervened, but unexpectedly, Wang Moumou turned around and hit me twice. My face went numb immediately," Mr. Zuo said. "Subsequently, my daughter called the police, and the two sides stopped wrestling." Mr. Zuo continued, "Later, I sat on the sofa smoking, and Wang Moumou kept cursing, forbidding smoking in his home, and then picked up a chair to hit me. At the time, I was completely unprepared."

The reporter verified the situation with the Shiqi District Public Security Bureau, and the police confirmed that on December 16 last year, they indeed received a call about domestic violence as described by Amei. After the police arrived at the scene, all three had varying degrees of injuries, so they suggested both parties go to the hospital for injury verification. Subsequently, the male party's unit, the Zhongshan Intermediate People's Court, intervened to mediate the matter.

Regarding Illegitimate Children

Amei: Saying My Nephew Is My Illegitimate Son

"After marriage, Wang Moumou never visited my parents' home, nor did he like me going back to my maternal home. Once, when I learned that my nephew had pneumonia and went to check on him, upon returning home, Wang Moumou got extremely angry with me. Furthermore, he maliciously defamed me, saying that my nephew is my illegitimate son. How could that be?" Speaking of this, Amei took out the household register from the room. From the household register, the reporter saw that the head of the household is Amei's father, the eldest son is Amei's brother, and on the last page is the nephew Amei mentioned, whose relationship with the head of the household is marked as 'grandson'.

"This is my younger brother and sister-in-law's wedding photo, this is my young nephew's photo, and here is their family portrait of three people. Since my younger brother and sister-in-law are busy abroad, they placed the child at home for a while. Unexpectedly, Wang Moumou suspected that this is my illegitimate child from abroad," Amei said.

Regarding Forgery of Official Seals

Claiming Her Husband Faked Official Seals

Amei revealed that Wang Moumou, as a court staff member, forged a community official seal. She said that shortly after getting married, she became pregnant. To transfer his collective household registration at Zhongshan Intermediate People's Court to a property under his mother's name in a certain neighborhood, and to obtain a birth planning certificate for the child, due to the cumbersome procedures, after failing multiple times, Wang Moumou decided to forge the official seal of the Lianxin Community Residents' Committee in Shiqi District, Zhongshan.

The reporter learned yesterday from the Lianxin Community Residents' Committee in Shiqi District, Zhongshan, that Wang Moumou indeed submitted some certificates to the community residents' committee at the end of last year. "However, at the time, he was handling household registration transfers, from Dong District to Shiqi District. If the procedures were complete, we would have processed it. But whether his birth planning certificate originated from our community, we currently cannot verify."

Subsequently, the reporter found Mr. Tang, who carved the official seal for Wang Moumou. He said that previously, a couple drove to find me to carve an official seal, the content being 'Lianxin Community Residents' Committee of Zhongshan City'. The man said he works in legal affairs, understands the law, and that these small official seals are fine, not to worry, as he needs them for procedures abroad and won't break the law. Later, I agreed to carve the official seal for him at a price of 138 yuan, without specifically asking what it would be used for. Soon, the man's wife came with a 'Lianxin Community Residents' Committee of Zhongshan City' official seal and asked me if it was carved by me. I recognized it as the one I carved last time. Then the woman said her husband is a civil servant at the Intermediate People's Court, planning to make another one, and asked for my phone number. 'The woman was very polite, very beautiful, so they left a deep impression on me, and I could recognize her as soon as she came,' Mr. Tang said.
