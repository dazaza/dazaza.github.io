Training Prospects

The second article of the Ministry of Labor and Social Security's Order No. 6, "Regulations on the Employment of Skilled Workers," points out that employers recruiting workers for technically complex jobs or those involving national property, people's lives, safety, and consumer interests must hire from individuals who have obtained the corresponding occupational qualification certificates. Therefore, Suzhou Electrician Training [http://www.bangyuanedu.com/] maintenance electricians must hold a national occupational qualification certificate. National occupational qualifications are divided into five levels, starting from the primary level up to the senior technician.

An occupational qualification certificate is proof that a worker possesses the necessary knowledge and skills for a particular occupation as provided by Suzhou Electrician Training [http://www.bangyuanedu.com]. It serves as a qualification voucher for job seekers, employees, and entrepreneurs. It is also the main basis for employers recruiting and hiring workers, and an effective document for skill level certification when working abroad or in international labor cooperation.

Training Content

1. Basic knowledge of safety technology and first aid; recognition of commonly used tools and materials;

2. Electrical diagram reading from Suzhou Electrician Training [http://www.bangyuanedu.com/]; installation electrician operation skills; use and repair of motors and transformers;

3. Installation and repair of common low-voltage electrical appliances and typical control circuits;

4. Installation and maintenance of three-phase asynchronous motor control circuits; identification and testing of electronic components; Suzhou Accounting Qualification Certificate Training [http://www.bangyuanedu.com];

5. Use and maintenance of electrician instruments and meters; use and repair of transformers and welding machines;

6. Use and repair of motors and special motors; installation and repair of complex control circuits for motors;

7. Maintenance of general machinery equipment electrical control circuits; application technology of programmable controllers;

8. Operation and maintenance of internal power supply systems in enterprises.

Qualification Certification

Upon passing the corresponding occupational qualification appraisal, successful candidates will simultaneously receive the intermediate-level "Maintenance Electrician" certificate issued by the Ministry of Human Resources and Social Security. This certificate is electronically registered and can be checked online (query website: http://www.osta.org.cn/[http://www.osta.org.cn/]). It is recognized nationwide. Additionally, one can apply for a special operations work permit (IC card) issued by the National Safety Quality Supervision Bureau.

Eligibility Requirements: (Candidates meeting any of the following conditions)

1) After obtaining the primary occupational qualification certificate of this trade, having worked continuously in this trade for more than 3 years, and having completed the standard number of hours of formal intermediate training in this trade, while obtaining a graduation (completion) certificate.

2) After obtaining the primary qualification certificate of this trade, having worked continuously in this trade for more than 5 years.

3) Having worked continuously in this trade for more than 7 years.

4) Obtaining a graduation certificate from a secondary vocational school or higher, which has been reviewed and recognized by the labor security administrative department with the goal of cultivating intermediate skills in this trade (profession).
