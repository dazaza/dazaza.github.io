Lianchengfa LED display screen serves the Guangzhou Military Region Office in Chenzhou, Hunan [Lianchengfa Group]

by lcfledcom on 2012-02-14 16:18:32

Following the completion of the P10 outdoor full-color LED display project in Lianshui, Huai'an, Jiangsu, another significant project by Lianchengfa Group (www.lcf-led.cn) has been successfully completed at the beginning of the year — the LED display project for the Guangzhou Military Region Office in Chenzhou, Hunan. The project involved a P10 outdoor full-color LED display with a total area of 49.76 square meters. The LED display features clear and soft images, excellent uniformity, high brightness, and has received high praise from the client for its display quality.

The LED display installed at the Guangzhou Military Region Office in Chenzhou, Hunan will be used to scroll military slogans and national defense promotional videos. It aims to educate the general public about basic knowledge of national defense construction and promote relevant laws and regulations. The location is advantageous due to its high foot traffic, which ensures an unexpected level of promotion effectiveness. Moreover, Lianchengfa Group has always been an outstanding manufacturer of outdoor full-color LED displays. Its P10 outdoor full-color LED display offers high-definition and high-quality images from any angle, providing a visual feast for citizens.
