Business operation embellishment: 3 essential common knowledge of website construction selected. Business website construction is gradually becoming a fashion under the sweeping tide of informatization. Establishing its own website by an enterprise is beneficial to enhance corporate image, make the company have network communication ability, use the huge export of the network to promote the company and products, and also can undertake the function of e-commerce. So, what common knowledge does website construction need to understand? Today, IDC Review Network has sorted out some small knowledge for your reference.

First, types of business website construction.

1. Basic Information Type: Mainly facing customers, industry people or ordinary browsers, mainly introducing the basic information of the enterprise to help establish corporate image; it can also appropriately provide news or knowledge information within the industry.

2. E-commerce Type: Mainly facing suppliers, customers or the consumer group of enterprise products (services), providing a certain service or transaction directly belonging to the enterprise's business scope or providing services or transactions for business services; such websites can be said to be in the middle stage of e-commerce, due to different industry characteristics and enterprise investment depth and breadth, its degree of e-commerce may be at one stage from relatively primary service support, product list to relatively advanced online payment.

3. Multimedia Advertising Type: Mainly facing customers or the consumer group of enterprise products (services), mainly promoting the core brand image or main products (services) of the enterprise. This type is more like a print advertisement or TV advertisement in terms of purpose and actual expression techniques compared to ordinary websites, so it is more appropriate to call this type of website "multimedia advertising".

4. Product Display Type: Mainly facing demanders, showing detailed information about their own products as well as the strength of the company. Make the most comprehensive introduction to the price, production, and detailed introduction of the products. The main purpose of this type of enterprise site is to display their products in the most direct and effective way. While paying attention to brands and images, you should also focus on the introduction of your products.

Second, choose a suitable website construction service provider.

1. Graphic design capabilities must be strong. The content of the website should be rich and professional, and the page design must reflect the enterprise's business philosophy and company strength, thereby enhancing the company's competitive advantage in the same industry.

2. Access speed must be fast, service stable, and support interconnection. According to surveys, people's tolerance for waiting for web pages to open is usually only 7 seconds, many visitors often turn to other websites because of slow webpage opening speeds, so the browsing speed of the website is crucial.

3. Try to choose professional website builders with strong strength, good reputation, and subsequent service guarantees. Professional network companies can provide professional websites that meet needs, choose quality rather than blindly pursue low prices, to ensure the benefit of the enterprise, careful consideration is required when making choices.

Third, matters needing attention after enterprise website construction.

1. Domain name ownership issues. The domain name ownership issue should be negotiated with the website construction service provider. Some unethical service providers hold the ownership when registering domains for enterprises, and if the enterprise wants to transfer virtual space, they will directly shut down the website. Therefore, enterprises should confirm with the service provider whether the domain name username and password can belong to the enterprise.

2. If renting a virtual host, then the space belongs to the enterprise itself. The enterprise needs to retrieve: 1. FTP username; 2. FTP password; 3. IP; 4. Control panel username; 5. Control panel password; 6. Control panel address; if the service provider does not intend to give the source code, these will not be disclosed.

3. If the website program has a management backend, then the management address, username, and password need to be requested from the website construction service provider.

Conclusion: If enterprises ignore their own website construction issues and only conduct exploratory operations with less website information and technical content, such a website cannot bring embellishment effects in the process of enterprise operation. Therefore, enterprises need to continuously update and enrich their own websites to play a greater role.
