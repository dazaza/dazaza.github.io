Miracle - Thousands of birds defecating together, cooperating to rescue a neighbor's moose

by crazyhr on 2008-04-25 11:53:50

In Scandinavia, there is a bird called Tiandong, and they are not friendly to predators.

A moose is quietly eating grass.

Tiandong birds are on guard for it.

Suddenly, the sentry discovers the situation.

At this time, the moose has no awareness of the impending danger at all.

The sentry keeps warning with its call.

Humans would not care about the calls of wild birds in the field.

However, the moose also did not hear this dangerous warning.

The hunter aims at the moose.

Now the surrounding Tiandong birds have received the signal and issued warnings together.

The moose is still leisurely eating grass.

The bullet is loaded into the chamber.

Tiandong can only take action now.

Aiming

At this moment, the hunter is only dozens of meters away from the target.

The moose didn't find the hunter at all.

The surrounding Tiandong birds all rushed to the hunter.

The first fighter plane arrived.

The hunter was ready to pull the trigger.

Tiandong birds actually defecated on the hunter.

It just fell on the hunter's hand.

The hunter's concentration was broken, and he missed the shot.

At this time, the scene was very chaotic, and many birds flew towards the hunter from different directions.

Their goal is to defecate on the hunter's head and try to drive away the invader.

The hunter is covered with bird droppings all over his head and body.

The hunter's hair and back are splashed with feces.

He helplessly looked up at these birds and gave up hunting this time.

The moose has withdrawn to a safe area.

Spectacle - ten thousand birds defecating and collectively collaborating to rescue the neighboring moose
