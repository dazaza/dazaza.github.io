After the age of 35 is the season of harvest. If you don't have the qualifications to say that, you will hate yourself. So it's best to do the following ten things before the age of 35!

First, learn all the knowledge required in this industry and develop further. Before the age of 35 is a period for primitive accumulation, and after the age of 35, one should flourish.

Second, cultivate a personal style. In your 20s and 30s, you can keep trying and changing, but by the time you're 35, you need to clearly establish your personal style.

Third, achieve peace and stability in emotional life. People whose private lives are already peaceful and stable before the age of 35 generally have more opportunities for success than those whose lives are unstable.

Fourth, understand your weaknesses. Admit that there are some things you really can't do well or don't want to do.

Fifth, know your strengths. You should know what you are good at and clearly understand what you like to do and do better than others.

Sixth, save money for quitting or finding another way to make a living. When you feel unable to perform, you may think about quitting or starting a second career. Having saved enough money beforehand gives you a safe backup.

Seventh, build a network of relationships. This network includes your friends and family, and at a minimum, includes everyone who can help each other. Success in one's career and life is actually similar to the success of a political party. You need many people spread out in appropriate places, and you can rely on them, and they can also rely on you.

Eighth, learn how to delegate authority to others. Delegating authority is half of success. A person who cannot delegate work to others due to micromanagement is destined to encounter great obstacles. In other words, you know how to pick the right people and trust them.

Ninth, learn when to remain silent. More people ruin their careers due to careless talk than for any other reason.

Tenth, be loyal to others. Before the age of 35, loyalty is just an investment; after the age of 35, you will receive the reward of loyalty as someone who can be trusted.
