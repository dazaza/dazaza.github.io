When the motor is powered on, touching the motor end cover will have an electric shock sensation. When tested with an electric tester pen, it shows electrification. The electrification of the motor end cover is generally caused by a decrease in the insulation performance of the motor. Use a megohmmeter (or multimeter) to measure the insulation resistance of the main and auxiliary windings of the motor to ground (that is, the motor end cover). During the test, connect the three lead wires of the motor together and connect them to one end of the megohmmeter, and connect the motor end cover to the other end of the megohmmeter. The normal value of the motor should be above 50MΩ (the standard stipulates 2MΩ), Beijing Samsung washing machine repair, if the value is several mega ohms or lower, all belong to abnormal conditions, which is a sign of reduced insulation performance; If the meter needle swings unstably, it indicates that the insulation has been basically broken down, there is only a very thin insulating layer left; and if the resistance approaches zero, it means that the motor winding lead wire and the end cover have already short-circuited, Beijing Samsung washing machine after-sales service phone. When touching the motor end cover gives an electric shock sensation, the insulation resistance of the motor has dropped below 0.1MΩ. Common reasons for the decrease in insulation resistance are motor dampness or excessive dust accumulation. For the former, the motor can be removed and placed in the sun to dry or use a hair dryer etc. to drive away moisture; for excessive dust accumulation, the motor can be disassembled and cleaned. If it is determined that the motor insulation has broken down, then the winding needs to be repaired, rewound, or the motor replaced. If it is determined that the motor has a grounding fault, check first whether the motor lead wire is worn, then see if any debris has fallen into the motor or if the motor assembly status is normal. Disassemble the motor to find if there are any grounding points on the surface of the winding. If it is determined that the grounding point of the winding is between the winding and the stator core, the winding should be rewound.
