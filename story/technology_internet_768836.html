Finally it's here! WeChat releases speech recognition open platform

by anonymous on 2013-11-25 11:32:26

Today, WeChat released a brand-new voice recognition platform called the WeChat Voice Recognition Open Platform at the WeChat Public Partner Communication Conference, which will be officially launched next month.

The WeChat Voice Recognition Open Platform is an open developer platform introduced by Tencent to provide developers with access to its voice recognition capabilities. It is not only available for internal products of Tencent but also open to tens of thousands of third-party application developers.

Not exclusive to WeChat, all Apps can call it

The technology behind the WeChat Voice Recognition Open Platform originates from the WeChat product. Developers using the open platform can achieve the same voice recognition effects and stable services as the WeChat product. The WeChat Voice Recognition Open Platform will continuously expand and improve its platform functions and product details based on the needs of a wide range of developers, providing a simpler and more convenient product development and operation support platform for developers.

The WeChat Voice Recognition Open Platform serves not only WeChat, Tencent Maps, and QQ Music but also focuses on opening up to a wide range of third-party developers. Products such as China Merchants Bank, China Southern Airlines, and Huaxia Fund have already integrated voice recognition functions to enhance their product interaction experiences. Most services within WeChat Official Accounts currently use WeChat's voice recognition service, while external APPs mostly use the open interface provided by iFlytek for voice recognition. After the launch of WeChat's voice platform, developers now have another choice. With the accumulation and development of WeChat's voice data and recognition over time, the accuracy rate has become quite high, which can be seen in the current built-in voice input function of WeChat.

Vertical细分 by domain

In addition to pure voice recognition, WeChat has also made vertical domain segmentation based on this. Currently, the WeChat Voice Recognition Open Platform has been fully integrated into the WeChat Official Account Platform system, comprehensively supporting and opening the voice recognition capabilities of official accounts. The vertical segmentation is based on the characteristics of the application scenarios of official accounts, where the WeChat Voice Recognition Open Platform offers 1+7 voice recognition services.

The so-called 1+7 refers to a general voice recognition engine at the center, simultaneously expanding seven specialized voice recognition services in major vertical domains. This not only facilitates the selection and use by official account developers but also significantly improves the accuracy of voice recognition in specific domains. Later, a voice recognition SDK developed for mobile clients will also be launched to a wide range of developers, ensuring that developers' voice recognition needs are met during iOS and Android mobile application development.

Challenge to iFlytek

When it comes to voice recognition, the domestic pioneer is iFlytek. Relying on its accumulation in the field of voice recognition, iFlytek launched the Voice Cloud Open Platform long ago, allowing developers to call its voice recognition library. Many domestic applications use iFlytek's database for the voice recognition feature. On iFlytek's Voice Cloud Platform, many excellent services have emerged, such as Smart 360 and Wormhole Voice Assistant. Many applications utilize iFlytek's cloud service to integrate voice input features, improving the product's interaction experience and user satisfaction.

Currently, iFlytek holds more than 70% of the Chinese voice technology market share, and its speech synthesis product market share is as high as over 80%. Through its own cloud platform, iFlytek provides cloud services to applications while collecting more linguistic material databases through the cloud platform, developing voice clouds and enriching the database. Currently, iFlytek's voice input method has improved the recognition accuracy rate for standard Mandarin to over 95%.

Zhang Xiaolong once mentioned that WeChat's voice recognition does not use iFlytek's technology but rather WeChat's independently developed voice recognition technology. As WeChat continued to update its versions, voice search and voice recognition were gradually added. The launch of this voice recognition open platform packages and outputs this technology, making it a platform-level service like iFlytek's Voice Cloud. In the future, there will inevitably be competition between WeChat and iFlytek.
