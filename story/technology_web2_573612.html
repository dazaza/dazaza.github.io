Quick Ask: A Social Q&A Application

by daodao on 2011-11-21 05:11:11

"Quick Ask" is a social lightweight Q&A application, providing both iPhone and Android versions, allowing users to make full use of their fragmented time to ask questions and answer others' questions. Defined as a social light Q&A app, Quick Ask offers two versions for iPhone and Android for user convenience. Users can take advantage of their fragmented time to answer questions posed by others, sharing their wisdom and insights with more friends. For those who post questions, they can harness the collective intelligence through Quick Ask, gaining reference opinions and auxiliary decision-making support. Besides iPhone and Android, Quick Ask also has a clear and complete deployment on the web.

From Zhihu to Mi Fan, after a relatively complete and fierce competition in the domestic social Q&A field, it has gradually become stable. Quora's influence on Zhihu in terms of knowledge, and Formspring's impact on Mi Fan regarding social aspects, can be considered a fairly good conclusion. In the Q&A field, there seems to be no other gold mine left to explore. However, foreign services like Loopt, LocalMind, and Dailyplaces have inspired a new way of asking questions - binding the content of the questions to real geographical locations! At this point, all valuable content becomes integrated into people's real-life living; thus, many applicable scenarios immediately come to mind: A traveler new to or preparing to visit Tianzifang can seek help from nearby people at a lower cost, learning which nearby shop can sell Letterpress small cards, etc.

Quick Ask enters the Q&A field via mobile terminals but fails to integrate well with LBS (Location-Based Services), which is indeed regrettable. I'm not saying that all services riding on mobile internet should necessarily be linked to geographical location information. What I want to express is that since the mobile phone is chosen as the entry point for social Q&A, the advantages of mobile terminals should be fully utilized to achieve a balance between cost investment and return benefits. Otherwise, how can this product compete with Zhihu and Mi Fan domestically?!
