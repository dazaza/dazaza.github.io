Expand the story - Deadly Greed

by chandadada on 2012-02-29 17:41:37

The greed and exploitation of mankind are bound to cause the constant emergence of war, disaster, and disease, all of which are caused by the disharmony within people's hearts. The ultimate harmony for humanity is the harmony within one's heart. As long as a person can correctly deal with difficulties, setbacks, and honors, they can easily achieve harmony. In other words, harmony is actually very simple; it only requires a calm heart.

It is said that after a great philanthropist who spent his life doing good deeds passed away, an angel specially came to escort him to heaven. The great philanthropist said to the angel: "Angel, I am about to ascend to heaven, and I thank you. But I heard that hell is very tragic. Can you take me there to have a look first?"

The angel replied: "No problem, since you are a great philanthropist, I can fulfill your request."

Then, the great philanthropist followed the angel to hell. He saw a large dining table there, filled with sumptuous delicacies. "Is this hell? Life here seems pretty good! Not tragic at all!" The great philanthropist asked the angel in confusion. "Don't rush, keep watching," the angel said.

After a while, it was time for the meal. A group of gaunt hungry ghosts quickly ran to the table. Each of them held a pair of chopsticks more than ten feet long. In order to eat more food, they pushed and fought each other, trying to pick up dishes with their chopsticks. However, because the chopsticks were too long, no one managed to eat anything.

"How tragic! How can you treat these people like this? You tempt them with food but don't let them eat." "Do you really think it's tragic? Let me show you heaven."

In heaven, the same scene appeared - a table full of delicious food, and everyone holding the same pair of chopsticks more than ten feet long. The difference was that the people sitting around the table were all smiling, plump, and adorable. They also used chopsticks to pick up food, but instead of feeding themselves, they fed each other, making everyone in heaven live happily.

What so-called heaven is, is just harmony. We need a harmonious environment to survive and develop. This kind of environmental harmony includes the harmony between humans and nature, between humans and society, and between people. But this is just external harmony. Without inner harmony, external harmony is destined to be superficial, formal, or low-level.
