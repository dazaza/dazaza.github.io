II. Quest Chapter

Without beating around the bush, let's get straight to the point. If this isn't well-written, please don't throw bricks at me, I'll accept golden ones.

For detailed quests, refer to |CFFFF0000|God|'s guide.

The Sad Che Ni quest - Are some of you searching the map carpet-style but still can't find the Wolf Lord?

Let me tell you responsibly, you need to activate the city defense mode for the Wolf Lord to appear in the bottom-left village.

Undead Wizard quest - After defeating the three elemental bosses, there will be a ring. Approach the Undead Wizard at the bottom-left corner of Yi Yun City to complete the quest and receive the Magic Storm Headband (PS: Little bird can go too).

Yuer quest - After defeating Spider King A, a vial of venom will drop. You can trade it with Yuer for a Holy Hood (PS: Yuer is just beside the Undead Wizard's right lower corner, little bird can also deliver the quest).

Rebellion quest: Approach the warrior marked with a yellow exclamation mark below Yi Yun City to trigger the quest. When fighting wild monsters, poisonous arrows will rain. The Water of Elements will drop from the first water element on Elemental Mountain (be careful not to kill it - - it's the only source of Water of Elements). After completion, go to the top left to find the Warden to trigger the rebellion quest (always suspected his thievery face, didn't expect him to really be the leader of the rebellion). Note: The soldiers on the left must be killed when the warden reaches the prison on the right, then quickly fly to the right. Completing the task will reward you with 200 honor points ~ Warden's Ring drops 100%.
