Sorry, let me introduce myself. My name is Fang Dong, male, 25 years old, engaged in external public relations work within the group.

Frowning, I replied: "You've sent it to the wrong person, there's no such person." He immediately replied: "Shit, don't mess with me, aren't you just someone who sells themselves?"

I imagine most people receiving such a harassing text message would have the same thoughts as me. Although mistaken messages like this are very common, receiving a text message with such content still left me quite upset.

The other end was very noisy, apparently outside. The other party didn't speak. I thought, here's another lunatic, just preparing to hang up when the other party spoke.

"That's correct, who are you?"

Baffled, I said, "You must have dialed the wrong number, there's no such person."

The other party fell silent again, after a while saying, "Oh, sorry, I guess I dialed the wrong number."

I thought, what bad luck, why do people keep dialing the wrong number today? But just after hanging up, the phone started vibrating again. My anger flared up, damn it, who is this? "Hey, I'm still a student, can you give me a discount? How about 500?"
