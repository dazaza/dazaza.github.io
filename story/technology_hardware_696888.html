Factors affecting the cost of skin grafting surgery in hospitals:

1. Surgical method

There are various methods for skin grafting surgery. Skin grafting surgery is suitable for larger areas of burn scars or tattoos that are difficult to clean locally. For smaller, less severe scars, it is recommended to use medications or surgical excision. For larger scars, skin grafting and flap transplantation surgeries are preferred as they yield more noticeable results.
