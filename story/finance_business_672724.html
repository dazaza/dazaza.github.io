Guilin tourism will make "big moves" this year with the reception target of 30 million personnels

by citscits5 on 2012-02-23 10:05:53

Vigorously promote the construction of Guilin World Tourist City; focus on supporting and creating unique cultural performance tourism products such as "Night Wangcheng"; innovate in the management system of the Lijiang River to solve problems related to its protection and development... This year, Guilin's tourism industry will see quite a few "big moves," which was learned by reporters from yesterday's 2012 city-wide tourism work conference. Municipal leaders Li Zhigang, Chen Lihua, Huang Tao, Huang Shuming, Yuan Xuxiang, etc., attended the meeting.

Key Term One: Projects - Guilin World Tourist City is the "Number One Project"

In October last year, Guilin World Tourist City was officially inaugurated. As the focal point of Guilin National Tourism Comprehensive Reform Pilot Zone and the pilot zone for Guangxi tourism reform and innovation, this year Guilin World Tourist City has become the "Number One Project" in the tourism industry. Many tourism projects surrounding it will gradually start construction or be completed.

These projects include the Wanfu Leisure Tourism Area, Taohua River Resort Area, Luoshan Lake Sports Leisure Industrial Park, Guilin National Forest Park, renovation and expansion of Guilin International Tourism Goods Wholesale City, Gaoxin Qixing District Jiata Xia Theatre, Yan Mountain Self-Entertainment Mediterranean Club, Guilin·Hong Kong 3D International City Animation Industrial Park, Guilin International Football Industrial Park, as well as China Guilin Aerospace City, Pingle Lijiang Town, Xing'an Lingqu Canal, Longsheng Longji Terrace, Gongcheng Mountain Village-Water Village, Xiangshan Scenic Area Sino-French Cooperation Project, Pengzu Ping Ecological Tourism Scenic Area, Yao Ethnic Thousand Caves Ancient Town, Guilin Impression Orchid Valley Tourism Ecological Leisure Resort Area, Guilin Guanyan Tourism Zeren Grand Hotel, Huixian Wetland Park, etc.

Key Term Two: Culture - Culture and Tourism Collaborate to "Travel"

Guilin is not only an international tourist city but also a historical and cultural city. This year, our city will further strengthen the integration of the tourism industry with the cultural industry, adhering to the interactive development of tourism and culture. We need to deeply explore Guilin's history, local characteristics, and ethnic folk cultures, focusing on supporting and creating distinctive cultural performance tourism products like "Impression·Liu Sanjie", large-scale fantasy acrobatic song and dance show "Between Mountains and Waters", and "Night Wangcheng". At the same time, we will try out new forms of visitor participation in the "Common People's Big Stage," striving to develop excellent mass cultural activities in Guilin into new tourist attractions. Additionally, we will advance the construction of Jingjiang Imperial Palace and Mausoleum, Zengpiyan Cave, ancient Lingqu Canal, various museums, Rujia Village folk tourism village in Xiufeng District, innovative creative cultural tourism park in Gaoxin District, Yan Mountain Cultural Tourism Park, strengthening and refining the cultural tourism brand.

Key Term Three: Lijiang River - Innovating Management System for Scientific Protection

The protection of the Lijiang River has always been closely watched by all sectors. Last year, our city established a leading group for the rectification of the Lijiang 5A-level scenic area, taking various measures to rectify the Lijiang scenic area. This year, our city will further innovate in management to scientifically protect the Lijiang River at its root. In terms of management systems, we will integrate the functions and administrative resources of municipal and county (district) departments involved in the Lijiang River, clarify the management subject, promote integrated management of planning, protection, development, utilization, and operation of Lijiang tourism resources, fundamentally solving the problem of fragmented management and unclear responsibilities, rights, and interests, constructing a Lijiang regulatory system that matches the scientific development concept. In terms of Lijiang scenic area management system reform, we will strengthen the operation and management of tourism in the Lijiang River Basin, adjust and improve the revenue distribution system, fully considering the interests of all parties. While considering how to standardize market order and ensure tourism safety, we will enable farmers along the Lijiang River to take the path to prosperity through tourism.

Key Term Four: Goals - Increase in Tourism Revenue by 25% This Year

Last year, our city's tourism achieved a historic breakthrough, receiving a total of 27.8817 million tourists, an increase of 24.12% compared to the previous year, among which there were 1.6439 million inbound tourists, an increase of 10.61%, accounting for 54.29% of the whole region; the total tourism revenue of the city exceeded 20 billion yuan for the first time, reaching 21.834 billion yuan, an increase of 29.74%, among which the inbound tourism revenue was 4.013 billion yuan, an increase of 17.6%. The meeting also proposed the goal of the city's tourism work in 2012: a 10% increase in the total number of tourists received, achieving a 25% increase in total tourism revenue. According to this requirement, the number of tourists received in our city this year will exceed 30 million people. To achieve the expected goals, Mayor Li Zhigang required that we should promote tourism development by seizing projects and transforming methods. Counties, districts, and various departments must find their entry points, seize breakthroughs, support the development of the tourism industry through different channels and levels, forming a good situation where all departments across the city cooperate and jointly manage the tourism industry, with a more pragmatic working style, firmly holding onto the goals without letting go, making new and greater contributions to the construction of Guilin National Tourism Comprehensive Reform Pilot Zone.

Guilin China International Travel Service looks forward to a brand-new mountain-water Guilin City together with a wide range of tourists.
