In the WEB2.0 era, network applications are constantly being updated.

by henguai on 2009-11-21 14:08:09

The marketing and promotion of a website also needs to keep pace with the development of the Internet and do a good job in brand marketing. By using attractive content for precise positioning, we can find interested users and use the powerful interactivity of the network to achieve the maximum effect of online marketing. Hengsou (a Chinese search engine). Yang Yang from the Chinese Webmaster Network hrzzz.com believes that only by learning to use various network-based methods and content to attract users, establishing a large-scale, effective, and diverse user circle for the website's brand, and forming a long-term positive cycle of marketing and promotion model, can the website adapt to the continuously developing and highly competitive Internet environment.

Today, I saw a piece of news: Tudou.com's Millionaire became the first case of video marketing. Tudou.com collaborated with the well-known mobile phone manufacturer Nokia to set up a million-dollar prize and launched the live-streamed "Internet Millionaire" reality show program through Tudou's network. The program attracted 49 million viewers during its live broadcast period. As the first large-scale marketing promotion activity entirely based on the Internet in China, it surpassed Hunan TV's "Happy Girl" this year and became the domestic reality show program with the most registration attempts. With planning skills no less than professional TV programs and a strong production team, the program not only effectively combined the interactive nature of the network with various popular network elements but also relied on Tudou's fame and user base to further promote Tudou, elevating its popularity and reputation to a new level. Based on such a transmission force, Nokia, the co-organizer, achieved both fame and profit in terms of brand enhancement and product promotion. The goodwill of netizens towards Nokia's brand and the program's sponsorship prize, Nokia N97, increased further, which greatly helped improve Nokia's recent mobile phone sales. Brand-named programs have always been a mutually beneficial marketing method. Tudou's efficient brand-customized marketing using network videos is worth learning for many webmasters.

Through this marketing event, webmasters should not only learn from Tudou's marketing methods and strategies but also understand an important truth: only by accurately targeting users, understanding their needs and preferences, and creating content that can attract users, can the promotional effects be maximized and brand marketing scaled. Tudou relied on its user base, drawing on the experience of television talent shows, attracting users with actual benefits by setting high participation rewards and generous online audience rewards, thus achieving such a powerful effect. Even the best strategy without attractive content is useless. Another example is the recently popular TV series "Snail House," which discusses issues that are currently of greatest concern to ordinary people - housing. The phenomenon of "house slaves" is widespread in China, so TV series reflecting such issues naturally attract the attention of most audiences because the content expressed reflects social phenomena closely related to ordinary people. This TV series caused a viewing craze when broadcast on Shanghai Dragon TV because Shanghai, as China's economic center, has the highest housing prices, and Shanghai citizens face the greatest pressure in buying houses. Therefore, such TV series easily attract Shanghai audiences, resonating and sparking heated discussions among Shanghai residents. Thus, it can be said that choosing to air this series during prime time was about using content to capture the psychology of the masses, achieving quite high ratings.

My small site Hengsou www.hengsou.com focuses on content essence, which revolves around users, because whether it's website content or promotion, if SEO optimization content does not meet user needs and fails to grasp the characteristics and preferences of users, success cannot be achieved. Therefore, webmasters need to learn how to create content that can attract users, such as small events with prizes or offline gatherings where people can exchange ideas. The key is not what form you use, but whether you can provide users with content they are interested in. Content is the key to guiding users.
