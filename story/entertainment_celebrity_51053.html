Depressed Zhang Bai Zhi returns to her parents' home for rest, and bursts into tears frequently while holding her son.

by pubsense on 2008-03-05 15:24:16

It was reported that:

After Zhang Bai Zhi got involved in the艳photo scandal, when Xie Ting Feng attended an endorsement promotional event in mainland China, he was found to be extremely thin, believed to be due to physical and mental exhaustion. However, he did not stop working because of this. He even collaborated with Joey Yung to perform and shoot the MV for the third round of Olympic theme songs invited by the Beijing Olympic Organizing Committee, "On the Same Blue Planet," without affecting his work. On the other hand, Zhang Bai Zhi has been emotionally low recently, with reports indicating that she hugged her son and cried.

According to Hong Kong's Sing Tao Daily, Xie Ting Feng previously collaborated with Joey Yung to perform and participate in the shooting of the MV for the third round of Olympic theme songs invited by the Beijing Olympic Organizing Committee, "On the Same Blue Planet." The MV for the theme song has already been broadcasted on a loop on CCTV, and various provincial and municipal television stations are also broadcasting it consecutively. In fact, Xie Ting Feng wasn't performing an Olympic song for the first time. Earlier, the Organizing Committee had specifically chosen Ting Feng to perform the only Guangdong solo version of "We Are Ready" globally. This song was also distributed by the Education Bureau to all primary and secondary schools under its jurisdiction, allowing all Hong Kong students to know this song and jointly feel the pride of the Beijing Olympics for the Chinese people.

It is known that Xie Ting Feng's Guangdong version of "We Are Ready" has been made into an official promotional video, broadcasted on government departments, television, and outdoor media, allowing overseas Chinese to feel the atmosphere of the Olympics together. Additionally, the only official Olympic theme promotional song of CCTV, "Believe in Love," is ready to be launched. This promotional song is a collaboration between singers from mainland China, Hong Kong, and Taiwan. Among them, Joey Yung, JJ Lin, Ashin, Angela Zhang, and Han Hong's collaboration on "Believe in Love" will become a key promotional project for the Beijing Olympics. CCTV has also specifically chosen Joey Yung and JJ Lin to participate in the performance of their respective solo versions.

Moreover, on the 3rd, there were reports that Zhang Bai Zhi, who had left home, took the opportunity when Xie Ting Feng was in Beijing attending an endorsement event to take away her son Lucas, and is now taking care of the child full-time at her sister's house. However, Zhang Bai Zhi's mood is very restless. When the child cries, she can't help but cry too.
