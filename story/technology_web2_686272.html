Against the backdrop of exaggerated prints,

by cunzhangeee on 2012-03-02 01:58:57

The inspiration for this season of Peter Pilotto comes from an Asian trip, chain sales, and at the same time borrows Japan's unique fluorescent effect - the lighting effect created by turning on thousands of car lights simultaneously. Combined with the elements of traditional Chinese comedic opera masks, it creates a dreamlike print of dense water ripples and water-dyed patterns with a futurist style. Even the traditional pilot jacket incorporates these dramatic elements, revealing a unique personality in its unrestrained and bold attitude under the exaggeration of prints. The most eye-catching items are undoubtedly the colorful furs. Besides keeping warm, fur’s most important function is its decorative effect. The skin-tight dresses are undoubtedly aimed to outline the graceful figures of women, exposing their sexy silhouettes. The dresses feature exaggerated 3D patterns spliced with other colors, while the contrasting fur squares below the collar are also a unique creation by the designer. We can still discover the elegance and charm of Eastern women beneath these highly exaggerated shapes. (Source: NetEase Women)

The designer also takes practicality into account amidst the exaggerated design. The winter and autumn jackets have been redesigned with a stand-up collar style, directly cut into three-quarter sleeves, and the fabric at the elbow is folded up. Meanwhile, the designer has pushed the use of prints to the extreme, using Chinese opera mask ink paintings and calm wave patterns to create a robust and strong female figure. Adding the design of a metallic waist skirt with decorative effects focuses all attention on the waistline, evoking a bit of the retro and refined feeling of women from the 50s and 60s.

For evening gown designs, the designer blends prints with water ripples and uses intricate cross designs to create a sensuous and twisted allure. With the waistline raised, it forms the golden ratio of a shorter top and longer bottom. The点缀of futurist fluorescent colors makes the prints lively and interesting. Although not shaped by mermaid skirts, this draping material allows women to find confidence and pride in their steps, making her a goddess of sensual and noble prints.

Additionally, the designer has prepared thick dresses for the cold season. If you don't look closely, you won’t notice any vulgarity. This is because the designer focuses on creating iconic female curves. The cinched waist design highlights the slim waist of women, integrating velvet material throughout. It continues with complex and dense water ripple patterns and Chinese-style blue-and-white porcelain patterns. The trefoil-style hollow-out design at the collar and the exposed neck give Western models more Oriental elegance and charm.
