Pulse-taking in 2010, the competition of Chinese sanitary ware brands presents four major changes

by fdkrkkgfem0 on 2012-02-08 11:22:17

Pulse-taking on 2010: Four Major Changes in the Competition among Chinese Bathroom Brands - Bathroom Fixtures - Industry News - Building Materials Network. In 2009, the kitchen and bathroom industry achieved an output value of 1.85 trillion yuan, a 12% increase over 2008. Looking forward to 2010, with the continuously strong real estate market, still rigid purchasing demands, and the momentum of high-speed development in 2009, home furnishing enterprises remain ambitious, naturally placing more expectations on the bathroom market.

From the beginning of the year's industry situation, well-known bathroom brands such as Hengjie, Ausman, and Gold Medal have already launched large-scale market offensives. Whether it is in the key promotion product combinations or brand building, the efforts invested are unprecedented, and changes have also appeared in brand competition, with several points worth mentioning.

Further diversification of competitive means

From basic channels and product competition to brand and service level competition, from relying on distribution channels, commercial fittings, malls, TV stations, outdoor advertising, DM sheets and other promotional channels to deeply mining the marketing value of the Internet, the competitive means of bathroom brands will become increasingly diversified. In terms of marketing methods, apart from making articles on price, promotion, and discount sales, all major brands are investing greater efforts in implementing "going out" strategies. Various kinds of group purchases, signings, community promotions, designer routes, etc., have already been put to great use.

Of course, new product technologies, designs, concepts, and ideas are naturally also important promotional means. For example, Hengjie Bathroom's proposed "green healthy bathroom" interprets a new bathroom lifestyle from the perspective of health and environmental protection; Ausman Bathroom was the first to propose the concept of "bathroom evolution theory" and advocate "overall bathroom space", and then promoted lifestyles and concepts such as "heart moves with water" and "easy life". These strategies not only meet consumer needs and provide convenience for consumers' lives but also ensure the creation of strong competitiveness for enterprises. It can be predicted that the competition in China's bathroom industry in 2010 will become more intense, and the strategies adopted will have more innovative connotations and values. Moreover, innovative marketing methods such as event marketing, topic marketing, activity marketing, interactive marketing, animation marketing, and FEA online integrated marketing communication will also become important marketing choices for enterprises.

Low-carbon living, energy saving, and environmental protection become important promotional themes

Before and after the Copenhagen Conference, the new lifestyle concept of "low-carbon living" began to become popular and became fashionable. Its core content is environmental protection, energy saving, greenness, and health. Water-saving and environmental protection are currently hot topics in the bathroom decoration field, which include two meanings: water conservation and safety and environmental protection. The rising water fees have made people begin to examine their own lifestyles, and bathrooms as major water users naturally become a place of focus. The root of water saving in bathrooms lies in choosing truly water-saving products at the decoration stage to block the source of waste. In the 2009 "National Construction Sanitary Ware Product Evaluation Report", the toilets of brands such as Hengjie Bathroom, Ausman Bathroom, and Gold Medal Bathroom received quite high scores, and their water-saving performance was highly recognized.

Green bathrooms and healthy bathrooms have always been the long-term adhered-to product route and lifestyle concept of Hengjie Bathroom. Water-saving and environmentally friendly bathroom products are the long-term technology route that Hengjie has adhered to; Gold Medal Bathroom was the first to use the "double S sewage pipe," which not only improved the product's lifespan but also enhanced its sewage discharge and water-saving functions. These technical advantages and lifestyle concepts are the key promotional themes of enterprises. It can be seen that many enterprises with a certain reputation and technical strength are exerting efforts in this area.

Domestic bathroom brands start to take the lead

Before 2003, Kohler dominated the high-end Chinese bathroom market. During the period of 2003-2007, TOTO rose suddenly, forming a tripartite situation with TOTO, Kohler, and American Standard. Currently, the main foreign brands active in the domestic market include Japan's TOTO, INAX, America's American Standard, Kohler, Moen, Delta, Germany's Hansgrohe, DuraVit, Lefroy Brooks, Grohe, Spain's Roca, etc. However, since 2008, domestic bathroom brands such as Hengjie, Ausman, Gold Medal, Arrow, Fasan, Huida, Siwei, Dongpeng Sanitary Ware, Meijiahua, etc., have started to form a noticeable advantage. In multiple industry lists such as "China's Top 100 Kitchen and Bathroom Brands", "Top 10 Bathroom Brands in China", "Top 10 Engineering Bathroom Brands in China", domestic brands like Hengjie Bathroom and Ausman Bathroom have obtained very advanced positions.

Taking WinDao Consulting's contact with more bathroom enterprises as an example, especially in 2009, leading domestic bathroom brands like Hengjie, Ausman, and Gold Medal Bathrooms achieved leapfrog development. For instance, Hengjie Bathrooms carried out a series of large-scale brand promotion actions in 2009. In the national sanitary ware authoritative evaluation, Hengjie products ranked in the top ten, and the overall sales increased by more than 30% compared to last year; Ausman Bathrooms caused explosive sales in dozens of cities such as Xuzhou, Inner Mongolia, Shanghai, Xi'an, Yangzhou, Zhuhai, Kaiyuan, Erdos, Baotou, Jining, Zhengzhou, Shenyang, Yangquan, Chengdu, Harbin, etc., starting from April 2009. There were scenes where single-day sales broke through 1 million yuan during holidays, attracting thousands of customers. And Gold Medal Bathrooms has gone through ten years of development, becoming one of the most scaled and professionalized bathroom brands in China, always regarded by insiders as a representative of technical enterprises among domestic ethnic bathroom brands. In the competition with foreign brands, it continues to maintain strong competitive advantages.

Recently, Hengjie Bathrooms has initiated the "Quality Service Hengjie Arrives Home: 3·15 Hengjie Star Service China Tour" consumer service upgrade activity. The star-quality preferential services mainly composed of "value arrives home", "service arrives home", and "quality arrives home" have attracted widespread attention from consumers; after initiating a large-scale "Quality Walks Ten Thousand Li" activity nationwide in 2009, Ausman Bathrooms launched a large-scale activity called "Good Quality Witness Together - Ausman Bathrooms 'Quality Supervisor' Recruitment and 3·15 Special Return" this year. Subsequently, many well-known domestic bathroom brands will spark a series of phased promotion climaxes, and their direction is no longer dominated by foreign brands.

Digital marketing becomes an important competitive means

With the popularization of the Internet, the growth in the consumption capacity of netizen groups, and the maturity of bathroom network promotion, the marketing value of the Internet has already been valued by many well-known bathroom brands. From online advertising placement, public relations deployment, to the emphasis on search engine transmission channels, gradually some bathroom brands have begun to elevate digital marketing to a strategic height. Well-known bathroom brands such as Hengjie Bathrooms, Ausman Bathrooms, and Gold Medal Bathrooms have successively introduced FEA online integrated marketing communication, conducting in-depth exploration in strategies such as bathroom and consumer topic marketing, online advertising placement, online forum reputation promotion, event dissemination, large-scale online activity marketing, online blog promotion, bathroom digital newspaper promotion, search engine marketing, e-commerce, etc.

Thus, these bathroom enterprises aiming to create global brands begin to establish such a pattern: taking national mainstream media and interactive communities as the main body, local mainstream media and interactive communities as support, and home furnishings, decoration, fashion, women, and life transmission channels as the backbone force, with terminal promotion activities and local theme market activities as traction, forming a nationwide joint marketing communication pattern, promoting brand marketing upgrades, and establishing more influential competitive advantages. It can be expected that in 2010, deeply mining the marketing value of the Internet will become a beautiful landscape in the bathroom industry.
