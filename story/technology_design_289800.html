(Social Wide Angle) Open Door to Solicit Advice, Collective Wisdom and Efforts

by bjjdwxgs on 2009-12-03 09:48:18

Open-door advice seeking, group brainstorming [Oral Guide] From November 6 to December 6 last year, our city carried out a special collection activity of people's suggestions titled "I contribute a suggestion for the construction of a new Yancheng". The activity received enthusiastic responses from the public throughout its duration. Numerous "golden ideas" and "good suggestions" were submitted to the organizing unit, providing beneficial assistance for the government to better serve the people and promote the scientification and democratization of government decision-making.

[Voiceover] Grandma Bao, who retired as the chairman of the trade union from a certain unit in the urban area, was an important participant in this event. Hearing that there are now many good people's suggestions, she decided to take a look today. Early in the morning, Grandma Bao came to the People's Suggestions Collection Office of the Municipal Letters and Calls Bureau.

[On-site Synchronous Sound] "I've made several suggestions to you, I wonder if they can be adopted."

"The grandma is here to check on this matter. Our city collected quite a few topics this time, altogether so many. Take a look."

"Many, indeed many. These are all golden ideas."

"It involves more than 20 aspects, such as mountain spirit power amplifier repair, coastal development, transportation, issues related to people's livelihood... (voice fading)"

[Voiceover] Seeing so many letters with people's suggestions, Grandma Bao was delighted.

[Concurrent Sound] "Mayor Li received a letter from me, it was about establishing a People's Suggestions Collection Office, and this activity. Mayor Li personally approved it, and it was implemented within a very short period of time. I'm especially coming to see today, and when I saw there were so many suggestions, I was very happy."

[Voiceover] While the People's Suggestions Collection Office does a good job of organizing the people's suggestions, they also collaborate with some main departments to invite some suggesters to the main department to exchange views with responsible persons in charge. Today, Mr. Yu from Dongtai was invited to the Municipal Urban Management Bureau to discuss issues regarding the construction of urban sanitation infrastructure.

[On-site Concurrent Sound] "Now, I bring the parties involved, the suggesters, to report to you, and then I would like you to introduce the implementation status after the reasonable suggestions have been adopted."

[Voiceover] After discussing the construction of urban sanitation infrastructure with Mr. Yu, the Municipal Urban Management Bureau also invited Mr. Yu to visit the already built modern public toilets and garbage transfer stations. Seeing such advanced infrastructure, Mr. Yu was deeply moved.

[Concurrent Sound] "Seeing my own suggestions being adopted by relevant departments of the city and having already achieved results, I feel very happy, and I am full of confidence in the work of people's suggestions. In the future, if I have any good suggestions or good ideas, I will promptly submit them through relevant channels to promote the development of various government works."

[Voiceover] The purpose of the government collecting good suggestions from the masses is to promote the progress and development of the city and improve the quality of life for the people, therefore it has received widespread attention and response from all sectors of society.

[Concurrent Sound] Deputy Secretary-General of the Yancheng Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China, Director of the Yancheng Municipal Letters and Calls Bureau, Hong Jianing: "Since the launch of the 'I contribute a suggestion for the construction of a new Yancheng' People's Suggestions Special Collection Activity, we have received XX people's suggestions. Among so many people's suggestions, not only do our citizens propose feasible suggestions, but also many Yancheng-born individuals outside the city and concerned individuals from other provinces provide us with many valuable suggestions."
