[Qingdao Wedding] 7 Little Tricks for Buying a Wedding Dress Online with Your Girlfriends

by qssqwe215 on 2012-03-08 11:32:23

Since clothes and electronic products can all be bought and sold online, why not wedding dresses? Being economical is the greatest advantage of purchasing a wedding dress online. However, the issues of quality and size are what worry brides-to-be the most. How to buy a satisfactory wedding dress online? Based on JM's experience in online shopping, I have summarized the following tips:

Tip 1: Browse shop websites, and select sellers

Most of the wedding dresses displayed on numerous websites are European styles, followed by Korean styles. Moreover, they are all worn by models. Therefore, choosing a suitable style probably becomes the hardest thing for brides-to-be. Before making a purchase, visit different Taobao sellers' shops (directly find the manufacturers), check out the styles and prices of the wedding dresses they display, so that you know what to expect.

Warm reminder: Make more comparisons —— you cannot fully tell the good or bad of a wedding dress pattern and material from pictures alone. Sellers generally showcase real-life shots. Comparing multiple stores can still reveal differences in photos. Also, you can compare prices.

Tip 2: Narrow down your seller options, search for your ideal wedding dress

After the first round of extensive searches, you should already have an idea of your ideal wedding dress. Don't rush to decide which one to buy yet. First, save the initially selected wedding dresses, then compare them carefully together. The quality of wedding dresses is mainly determined by their style, fabric, and craftsmanship.

Warm reminder: When you're indecisive, it's time for your bridesmaids to step in. Don't ignore their opinions! Let them thoroughly comment on each wedding dress you've chosen, inside and out. Then summarize and inform the seller. Most manufacturers can make minor adjustments according to your requirements.

Tip 3: Buy from well-known wedding dress production areas

Wedding dress hubs include Suzhou, Guangzhou, and Chaozhou, while Shanghai, Beijing, and Shenzhen have very few manufacturers and higher prices. Suzhou, due to its local sourcing of raw materials, offers cheaper ready-made garments compared to big cities like Shanghai. Additionally, Suzhou has a wide variety of wedding dress styles.

Warm reminder: When selecting a wedding dress online, it's best to go to larger, reputable websites or sites recommended by friends who purchased high-quality items. You can use third-party payment methods (Alipay, Tenpay) to avoid being scammed and losing both money and goods.

Tip 4: Pay attention to size and color

Since you cannot try on the dress in person, measuring your size accurately is crucial. Different styles require different measurements. Generally, height (barefoot), bust, waist, hips, and shoe height are required. Depending on the style, you may also need shoulder width, upper bust, lower bust, neck length, BP height and width, back length, and garment length. In short, the more detailed, the better.

Warm reminder: There’s room for adjustment in the final wedding dress to fit you perfectly. For colors, we suggest opting for white as it minimizes color discrepancies!

Tip 5: Gain more knowledge about wedding dresses, communicate with sellers

Sellers often provide brief introductions to wedding dresses in their product photos, but these descriptions are usually vague. For example, they might mention imported satin, imported tulle, imported sequins, and imported lace edging. For those unfamiliar with fabrics, it's hard to distinguish between domestic and imported materials. Ultimately, the saying "you get what you pay for" holds true.

Warm reminder: You can look up some information on wedding dress fabrics online beforehand. Sellers on website shops usually leave their phone numbers and QQ numbers as contact information. Whether you call or chat on QQ, build rapport by engaging in conversation with them.

Tip 6: Bargaining, persistence is key

You can get a less desirable wedding dress for 300-500 yuan, a decent one for 500-900 yuan. High-quality ones with better materials and more intricate handwork start at over 1000 yuan. Wedding dresses adorned with many sequins tend to be more expensive. Trailing wedding dresses cost around 50 yuan more than non-trailing ones. Generally, manufacturers will include accessories, though the quality won't be great. Personally, I think adding a little extra money will get you better accessories from the manufacturer. Be sure to be polite!

Warm reminder: When bargaining with sellers, persistence is key. Whoever sticks it out until the end wins. You can dangle the bait that you have several friends who are getting married and are involved in your wedding dress selection process. They’ll feel satisfied with your choice if they see it as a way to sell more items at a slight profit, thus gaining positive reviews to attract more buyers.

Tip 7: Secure transactions, third-party payments

It's best to try on the wedding dress upon receiving it before making the payment, but nine out of ten sellers won’t accept this. Those who do accept this method use what I mentioned earlier: third-party payment (Alipay, Tenpay).

Upon receiving the wedding dress, carefully inspect and try it on. Once you're satisfied, confirm the payment to the seller.

Warm reminder: This wedding dress, which goes through countless procedures, can be kept as a memento, witnessing that unforgettable moment. If it's a high-end dress, it can even be passed down to the next generation, which is quite popular abroad!
