Donation Fame Wall for the Sichuan Great Earthquake (Solar Level)

by cennatian on 2008-05-15 17:22:30

A sudden earthquake disaster took away tens of thousands of Chinese, and the country was in mourning; it was also this major earthquake that brought billions of admirable kind-hearted people. The unity and progress displayed by the citizens because of this disaster made the entire country feel proud.

Since the "May 12 Wenchuan Earthquake", all sectors of society, corporate celebrities, and friends of the state have reached out to show their love for the people in the disaster area who are in dire straits through practical actions. Below is Rologo's compiled list of "Donations from Enterprises in the Sichuan Earthquake", which records the generosity and social responsibility demonstrated by these corporate citizens during this great disaster.

Due to the countless number of donating enterprises, the list divides them into four levels based on the amount of donations: Double Sun Level: over one billion yuan; Sun Level: ten million or more, under one billion; Moon Level: five million or more, under ten million; Star Level: one million or more, under five million. Of course, as many companies continue to increase their donations, there may be cases where the moon level upgrades to the sun level, and this site will update accordingly. Meanwhile, due to the large number of donating enterprises, those under one million yuan will not be included for now.

However, regardless of the amount of the donation, whether from a company or an individual, as long as you contribute this love, it remains deeply touching!

The number of "Sun Level" enterprises continues to grow, please pay attention to the updates at the bottom of the "wall". You are also welcome to notify us via reply of any Sun Level enterprises not yet included on the list. Thank you!
