Disadvantages: self-showing off, complicated, depressed, overly restrained;

Characteristics: skill, virtue, intelligence, caution, ambition;

People born under the Rabbit sign never become tiresome. They are interested in history, art, music, drama, dance, fine food, etc., and never get tired of conversations and discussions with friends. People born under the Rabbit sign have a genuine interest in the beautiful things in life. They do not attend premieres but choose to stay at their simply decorated homes and listen to perfectly recorded broadcasts. People born under the Rabbit sign avoid all conflicts. They like peace and quietness, avoiding noise and clamor. Men read palmistry; they reject any situation or relationship that may cause storms.

Some people say, "A rabbit's tail doesn't last long." Wrong! A rabbit's short tail is exactly what shows its intelligence. Think about it, if rabbits always carried a long tail, just like tall trees attracting wind, wouldn't they easily become someone else's free lunch? Rabbits grow short tails for the purpose of always keeping a low profile while doing things, which aligns with the I Ching saying, "Gentlemen are diligent all day, cautious as if facing danger." Do you think rabbits are smart?

Some people say, rabbits are naturally envious, unable to bear seeing others better than themselves. Wrong! It's not jealousy; it's seeing others acting so foolishly that they get angry enough to make their eyes red. Haven't you seen how clever rabbits build three dens? In the cruel animal world, a person cannot possibly love only one person in their lifetime. Without sharp teeth and claws for self-defense, rabbits possess this survival wisdom, showing us how smart rabbits really are.

II. Naming babies should meet several requirements

1. The name characters should be phonetically, visually, and semantically appropriate. The characters used in the name should not be obscure but easy to write and recognize. There should also be no homophones (as of 2011-04-03) that could lead to inappropriate associations. A name should carry good implications, and positive names always give people a sense of vitality.

2. Names should be chosen based on personal character information, aiming to compensate for disadvantages in personality and highlight one's strengths.

3. The auspicious meanings hidden in the names according to the I Ching should be considered, avoiding conflicts between the preferred elements of the five phases.

4. The chosen name should avoid conflicts with family member names.

5. The name should conform to phonological and prosodic principles, meaning a good name should be bold, grand, and easy to pronounce. From a psychological perspective, a pleasant-sounding name can easily make others feel good towards oneself.

6. The chosen name should carry rich traditional cultural significance.

7. Naming should also consider the balance of the three talents (Heaven, Earth, Humanity). Integrating these favorable aspects into the name can enhance one's luck.
