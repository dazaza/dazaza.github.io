"Nine Days Fairy Dream" is an ARPG game that emphasizes group interaction. By forming teams reasonably, players can gain more advantages in battles. For the Eight Arrays, it is recommended that the team configuration be: two Pójūn, one Língxīng, and two Tiānshāng. The two Tiānshāng must ensure absolute high attack power, while the Língxīng should have a clear "nanny" consciousness. As for why two Pójūn are needed, we will explain later. After buying enough resurrection orders, you can officially enter the battle.

Xiu Gate Boss: Xiéguà Shāshén. Must-kill skill: Instant Death, Burst Fire Ring. Auxiliary skills: Dazzling Axe, Slow.

Before entering the Xiu Gate of the Eight Arrays, add all BUFFs first, including physical defense, magical defense, and magical attack. The boss has no difficulty in the early stages; everyone can gang up on it. When the boss's health bar turns yellow, the Kill God will release the Dazzling Axe. The damage from the Dazzling Axe is not significant, but it will continue to attack. At this point, one Pójūn needs to taunt and pull the monster, while the other continues to tank the boss, posing no threat.

However, the Kill God will also randomly cast Instant Death and Burst Fire Ring. Keeping a distance from these attack traps can ensure safety. Once the skills become ineffective, everyone can gang up on the boss and easily defeat it. Of course, during this period, various deaths are inevitable, so having enough resurrection orders is essential for passing through the Eight Arrays. After defeating the boss, items will drop; remember to manually pick them up!

After defeating the Xiu Gate boss in the Eight Arrays, you will enter the Xiu Gate Transfer area. There are four small monsters here - Sealed Demon Suppressing Swords - that need to be manually unsealed to summon Lei Li Floating Souls. Just remember one thing: "Defeat them one by one." There is no difficulty, allowing you to easily enter the Sheng Gate of the Eight Arrays.

The Sheng Gate battlefield is the ultimate magical battlefield in the dungeon, guarded by the Twin Yuan Stars and Twin Yi Stars. Here, you can see the traces left behind when demons descended upon the world. You can also admire the Huitian Platform built by interstellar demons. However, there is only 30 minutes of time in the battlefield. The Twin Star bosses have high defenses and thick hit points. The team must be divided into two parts, one attacking the Yuan Star and the other attacking the Yi Star. Both BOSSes must die simultaneously; otherwise, the Twin Stars will resurrect.
