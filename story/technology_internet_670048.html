Methods for pest control in sesame

by hdghu19056 on 2012-02-20 14:49:24

The pests of sesame include cutworm, stinging caterpillar, beaded caterpillar, June beetle and firefly, etc. You can use 100mL of 80% emulsion of DDVP or 150g of 90% crystalline trichlorfon mixed with 75kg water for spray control per mu.

The prevention and control of this disease should adopt comprehensive measures mainly based on agricultural control and supplemented by chemical control. The following aspects should be paid attention to:

1. Seed disinfection.

Hot drug soaking or warm water soaking can eliminate the pathogenic bacteria on seeds and reduce the initial infection source. You can choose warm water at 48-53℃ for seed soaking for 30 minutes, or soak seeds in 200 times diluted copper sulfate solution for 30 minutes, or soak seeds in 500 times diluted 5 million unit streptomycin sulfate constant temperature 48℃ hot drug for 15 minutes, or soak seeds in 1500 times diluted 20% quinoline wettable powder constant temperature 48℃ hot drug for 15 minutes. The effect of constant temperature hot drug soaking treatment is better than that of conventional warm water soaking treatment.

2. Pay attention to finding disease-resistant varieties or disease-resistant single plants, and select high-yield and disease-resistant good seeds.

3. Strengthen management and focus on cultivation disease prevention. Formula fertilization; scientific water management, timely clean ditches and drain water after rain to reduce humidity; clean the field, collect diseased leaves and burn them.

4. Timely spray medicine to prevent and control diseases.

In areas where this disease often occurs, you should spray medicine to prevent it 1-2 times before the disease appears, and continuously spray medicine to seal off the disease center in the early stage of the disease. You can choose 1:1:100 lime equivalent Bordeaux mixture, or 600 times diluted 12% copper green emulsion, or 600 times diluted 30% oxychloride copper suspension agent, or 800 times diluted 77% Keshade suspension agent, or 1000-1500 times diluted 20% quinoline wettable powder, or 4000 times diluted 12% agricultural streptomycin sulfate, or 800 times diluted 47% Jiarong wettable powder, spray 2-3 times, every 7-15 days once, dense first and sparse later, alternate application, spray evenly and sufficiently.
