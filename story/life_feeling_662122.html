The White Tea Flower I Know

by shulin730715 on 2012-02-09 18:12:21

People who drink tea understand it, but they don't necessarily know about the tea plant and the tea flower. I am a person who loves to drink tea and also loves tea flowers, especially white tea flowers. My love for white tea flowers is largely due to my early life spent in the land of white tea. For me, white tea flowers are not only about their flawless white appearance that gives me a sense of purity, but also about the memories associated with white tea flowers. Those are memories from the early days of my life, pure and beautiful.

Perhaps because I share the same roots as the white tea flower, I have a special affection for one of the camellia varieties - the white tea flower. White tea is just one of the many camellia varieties in China, and can even be said to be an ordinary wild camellia. China has many varieties of camellias, which are the oldest, most evolved, most typical, and most perfect ornamental plants cultivated in horticulture in the world. As early as the Three Kingdoms period, camellias were artificially cultivated. But until the Southern and Northern Dynasties and the Sui Dynasty, the camellias planted in imperial palaces and noble courtyards were still wild primitive camellias with single-petaled red flowers.

The characteristics of white tea flowers (also known as Snow Tower) are: leaves are long elliptical, 6.5-7.5 cm long, tapering at the apex, wedge-shaped at the base. The flowers are pure white, completely double-petaled, with petals arranged like tiles. Unlike other rare varieties that possess bright colors or peculiar shapes, they only have a clear white. This white is translucent, so white that it makes people reluctant to pick them. Therefore, when I revisited the old place and saw those touching white tea flowers, I couldn't help but think of my childhood when I always sucked the nectar from the stamens of the white tea flowers during dawn, feeling quite pleasant. But now, no matter how sweet the morning dew in the stamens is, I don't want to destroy the pure beauty of the white tea flowers.

Therefore, I would rather quietly admire the beauty of the white tea flowers. This kind of beauty cannot be found in the mundane world, like the original beauty of human nature, hidden deep in people's hearts, not easily discovered. Therefore, when I left the old place, I sealed the beauty of the white tea flowers in my memory, preserving it intact.
