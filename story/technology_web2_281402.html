Ms. Huang informed the reporter that in order to recover funds, even if there is no profit, she would sell. She pointed at the largest tea table outside the shop and said that such a tea table usually costs 200,000 yuan each, but now she is willing to sell it for 150,000-160,000 yuan. At noon today, in Yiwu International Flower Market, a customer compared three stores before entering Ms. Huang's root carving shop. The customer asked about the price of a two-meter long and wide chicken wing wood root carving tea table, and Ms. Huang replied: "16,000 yuan." The customer left without saying anything. "Since October, business has not been good," Ms. Huang said. Other operators in the market also said that due to the sluggish macroeconomic situation, their business was not as good as last year.

After retirement, Ms. Huang and her family opened a root carving store in Yiwu International Flower Market, named "Yiwu Longchang Root Art Shop". Business has not been good since opening. According to Ms. Huang, their root carving products have markets not only locally but also in Shanghai, Ningbo, Hangzhou and other places with many customers. However, in the first half of this year, they felt the impact of the economic slowdown. She said that their shop rent is more than 50,000 yuan per year. With poor business now, the high rent they need to pay at the end of the year may cause difficulties in capital turnover.

According to understanding, root carving crafts mainly target high-end consumers, mostly used by newly established companies and renovated villas. Many operators expressed that they will overcome the market downturn by lowering prices to break even and developing more sales channels.

The salesperson of Zhejiang Xinyang Fang Family Wood Carving Factory's Yiwu store also said that their business this year is not as good as last year. A salesperson from Hubei Yi Hui Root Carving Factory's Yiwu Office told the reporter that last year they could get several foreign trade orders from South Korea and Japan, but this year they haven't received any.
