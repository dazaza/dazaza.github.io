Reporters learned at the scene of the interview that the car was bringing the bride to Fuyang for makeup, and then planned to go home for marriage. Around 3 in the morning, after the bride finished her makeup and put on her wedding dress preparing to go home, an accident occurred on the way. Currently, the cause of the accident is still under further investigation.

The rescue firefighters told reporters that when they arrived at the scene, the small car was completely wrecked, with three people trapped inside. After the firefighters dismantled the vehicle, the driver and the man sitting in the front passenger seat were lifted out of the car. The two were confirmed dead by the on-site medical personnel. The bride sitting in the back seat of the car was seriously injured and unconscious. In addition, there were also multiple injuries on the bus, among whom two had serious injuries. All the injured were sent to the hospital for treatment.

"The accident happened around three o'clock. The collision between the two vehicles was very severe." Yesterday, Mr. Tian, a taxi driver in Fucheng, told reporters that it was a long-distance bus and a car that collided together. After the accident, four or five ambulances came, and nearby firefighters also came for rescue.

At 3:28 AM on December 12, a traffic accident occurred at the intersection of Yingzhou South Road and South Second Ring Road in Fuyang City. A large bus with a Henan license plate collided with a small car, causing two deaths on the spot and multiple injuries. The bride sitting in the small car was also seriously injured.

Yesterday, reporters came to the intersection of Yingzhou South Road and South Second Ring Road in Fuyang City and saw that the accident happened under the red light at the crossroads. Many stone pillars on the sidewalk were broken, and the ground was covered with shattered glass.

It is understood that the accident site was about 200 meters away from the Yingzhou District Fire Brigade of Fuyang City. Firefighters who participated in the rescue introduced that everyone was sleeping at that time, suddenly hearing the loud collision sound of the vehicles colliding, followed by the alarm bell ringing, and the firefighters quickly rushed to the accident scene.
