Wanbao Garden is an estate built in the style of classical garden architecture, located in the main park of the national AAAA-level scenic area - Anhui Tian Tangzhai National Forest Park. It is 100 meters to the southeast of the geological park stele, across the bridge.

Wanbao Garden is the garden within the Tian Tangzhai National Forest Park, a leading enterprise in the agricultural industrialization of Lu'an City, and an ecological tourism project aided by the Beijing Municipal Agricultural Commission. It is also the main scene of the movie "Weekend Village Official" filmed by the Bayi Film Studio.

Within Wanbao Garden: three sides bordering the river, standing alone amidst the water, pavilions and terraces, grand doors and buildings,

small bridges and winding paths, ancient roads and corridors, carved beams and painted rafters, ancient elegance,

strange flowers and exotic grasses, butterflies dancing and fragrant, green shade covering, red jujubes and yellow persimmons,

potted landscapes and sculptures, intertwining old trees, gurgling clear water, rippling green waves,

fish leaping in the river, birds singing joyfully, tea fragrance spreading, peaceful scenery.

All the views in Wanbao Garden are beautiful, eating, living, playing, and traveling in greenery. Our garden is not only picturesque but also has home-style villa buildings, staggered and elegant, with indoor layouts featuring three bedrooms and one living room, high-end guest rooms, business suites, large and small meeting rooms, card rooms, product exhibition halls, indoor and outdoor dance halls, restaurants, and fishing platforms all available. The dining experience is unique.

Wanbao Garden Farm owns nearly ten thousand mu of wild tea original bases, 600 mu of natural organic tea and white tea bases, more than 300 mu of large-leaf tea and small-leaf fragrant tea bases, over 5000 mu of wild mountain tea oil bases, and 1000 mu of natural pickled vegetable raw material bases. In the company plus farmer cooperative model, more than 1000 black local pigs are traditionally raised, with an annual output of more than 800 heads.

The farm has six departments and one factory and one garden, namely the "Natural Green Food Factory" and the "Potted Landscape Garden". In Wanbao Garden, you can eat various treasures, and choose precious wild flavors at will. Our self-collected and self-made mountain vegetables, wild mountain tea oil, wild fruits, and natural pickled vegetables come in many varieties and are precious gifts for respecting elders and giving to friends and relatives.

Our self-raised black local pigs, black goats, black earth dogs, country geese, country ducks, and country eggs, as well as wild river fish, wild eels, and wild crabs, make excellent dishes and medicinal diets with unmatched deliciousness.

Please come to Wanbao Garden for the long chopsticks hanging pot feast, drink homemade small hanging wine, and enjoy the farmhouse banquet.

Frequent toasts amidst the sound of firecrackers, folk songs and dances bring joy to guests.

Main businesses: Tangzhai Hotel, Tangzhai Inn, Tangzhai specialties
