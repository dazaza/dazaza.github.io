"One-day tour of 'New Views and New Experiences in Guilin'"

by wszdw142 on 2009-06-15 14:20:17

Trip Features: New Guilin scenery and new experience

Route: Guilin Wangcheng Duxiu Peak, Chuanshan Park, Chuanshan Cave, Underwater Guilin, Sun and Moon Pagodas

Scenic Spot Pictures:

Itinerary Arrangement:

In the morning, visit the currently best-preserved Ming Dynasty feudal prince's mansion in China - Jingjiang King City and the Duxiu Peak located within the King City: rising abruptly from the flat land, dignified and magnificent, steep and beautiful, with the reputation of "a pillar under the southern sky". Ascend to the Duxiu Pavilion, look around, peaks surround, green water winds, all the beauty of the city is visible at a glance. Visit Chuanshan Park, which is located at the confluence of Xiaodong River and Lijiang River, named after the legend that General Ma Yuan of the Han Dynasty "shot three mountains with one arrow". You can stroll through the pavilions, verdant lawns, and lush pines and emerald bamboos, and appreciate the famous "Taishan Qingying", which stands tall and reflects in the river, elegant and clear. The Chuanshan cave is characterized by crystal-clear stalactites, snow-white transparent crystals, stone flowers, and stone hair, known as a world-rare crystal treasure cave. After lunch, visit the underwater Guilin, where you can have an intimate encounter with marine life; visit the landmark attractions in the center of Guilin - Sun and Moon Pagodas, whose name comes from the combination of "Sun" and "Moon" forming "Ming", symbolizing a better tomorrow for Guilin. Visit Yu Mountain Park, the source of Guilin's historical culture, where flowing springs and waterfalls fill the park with a modern atmosphere. The Five Blessings Pagoda shines brilliantly, and the "Peace Bell" prays for national peace and prosperity, blending East and West, delighting visitors. Visit the classic Liu Sanjie Grand View Garden, where the outside mountains are green and the water is picturesque, and the inside has pavilions, terraces, towers, steep peaks, and secluded caves. There is also the most beautiful ecological paradise in Guilin - Hundred Birds Garden. You can also see some scenes and folk house models from the making of the Liu Sanjie movie, and have a close encounter with "Liu Sanjie". After dinner, the group disperses.
