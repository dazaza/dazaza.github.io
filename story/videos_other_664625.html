Shenyang Baidu snapshot 4: Links can be widely distributed but not excessively, and should be mainly based on relevance. When establishing links, Shenyang website construction considers whether the content has relevance as one of the most important evaluation standards. If external links can be related to the website's theme, it is naturally the best option. Sometimes, a certain page may not be relevant to the entire website’s theme, but if the theme of the page itself is related to the linked page, it will have a higher link weight than unrelated links. For example, a catering company saying how good our company’s website construction is, or how skilled the Shenyang online company is, might not necessarily be credible. However, if this comes from peers or design companies, then the credibility is much higher, which is also true for Shenyang website optimization.

How to optimize websites has always been a common topic among webmasters. Many people believe that a website with strong external links will have a good ranking in search engines. Facts also prove that all well-ranked websites are inseparable from the support of their external links. Therefore, it is evident that external links play an important role in the natural ranking of websites. Of course, external links are just one of the key factors in website optimization. Website optimization needs to combine various comprehensive factors. In the process of building a website, most customers will consider the promotion of the website later on. Our designers and programmers will also consider the optimization and promotion of the website later on while working. Here, I will briefly discuss how to lay the foundation for external links in new site optimization.

1: Website external links should also focus on naturally formed logic.

For search engines, they place more emphasis on natural laws. External link building is most taboo against sudden surges; obvious over-optimization is very easy to receive penalties from search engines. Once penalized, all previous efforts will be wasted. Some people add a large number of external links within a period of time, but then stop abruptly. This is harmful rather than beneficial to website optimization. Even if the website is not penalized by the search engine, the keyword rankings will still fluctuate. Building external links requires steady growth over time. Instead of adding hundreds of external links in a month and then doing nothing afterward, it would be better to distribute this time and effort over several months, slowly and steadily increasing them, persevering consistently.

Links are one of the essential characteristics of the Internet and also its core. A link refers to the connection relationship from one webpage pointing to a target. The target pointed to can be another webpage, different positions on the same webpage, pictures, email addresses, files, or even applications.

2: The quality of external links far outweighs quantity.

We often see websites in the same industry where sites with fewer external links rank better than those with more. From this, we can see that the quality of external links far outweighs quantity. One high-quality external link is more effective than dozens or even hundreds of junk external links. Search engines frequently update website rankings, and each update deletes part of what the search engine considers useless junk links. This is why when we check the external links of a website, we find that a large portion of the links have disappeared.

3: Strong external links require consistent accumulation over time.

External links are the foundation of a website, and website optimization does not take effect in ten days or half a month. A website's external links require daily accumulation. When you use SEO tools to query the well-ranked websites of competitors, and discover how powerful their external links are, can you think of "Without experiencing bone-chilling cold, how can one enjoy the fragrance of plum blossoms"?

5: Valuable content sometimes is the essence of high-quality external links.

Links cannot produce content for a website, but valuable content can bring high-quality links to the website. If a website can provide users with highly valuable content and meet user needs, when other webmasters cannot provide enough content on their own websites, and they believe your website can solve the user's needs and satisfy their demands, they will naturally direct to the website providing relevant content in the form of external links.
