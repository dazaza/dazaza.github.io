Hongan is developing red tourism. The roads leading to the villages, which go through mountains and hills, have opened a way for the old district people to access the world. Nowadays, the town of Qiliping has changed its former dirty, messy and substandard situation. It has added 5000 square meters of urban green space. From the town area to the Tai Mountain tourist road, the cement road can directly reach each administrative village. The unblocked main roads and the characteristic tourism projects with pleasant scenery have not only attracted tourists from all over to visit the mountain areas, but also promoted the development of services in the mountainous areas. At present, rural tourism in Hong'an County has developed a series of ancient and natural country-style tourism projects, including "farmhouse" food and accommodation, handicraft production and sales, natural landscape spot tours, summer resort, and field life experience. These projects are favored by many tourists and have significantly increased villagers' income. Rural tourism has also promoted the adjustment and optimization of Hong'an County's agricultural industry structure and pushed forward the development of characteristic agriculture. Many towns have vigorously developed characteristic agriculture, forming a series of agricultural sightseeing tourism products featured by "flower mountain fruit sea", becoming the characteristics and highlights of rural tourism. In 2008, Hong'an County realized an average increase in income of nearly 500 yuan per person through the development of red tourism. Hotels, scenic spots, concert bookings and inquiries can be made by calling the 7*24 hours free hotline 4007840076. "The revolutionary old district has extremely rich red tourism resources. Developing red tourism can effectively promote the pace of poverty alleviation for the old district people." Lei Dian told the reporter that the next step would be to promote market-oriented operation of scenic spots, promote intensive business development of tourism, extend the product chain, service chain, and operation chain of tourism, and make Dabie Mountain into a first-class red tourism base nationwide. On the Taimai Mountain scenic spot in Hong'an County, vehicles loaded with tourists are coming and going on the winding mountain roads. The charming and novel farmhouses in the hills are captivating. "When the scenic spot management office was established in 2005, the farmers did not have buildings, but now, one third of the households have built buildings." Talking about the great changes in the lives of the old district people, Hong'an County Party Secretary Xiong Liangxiao was deeply moved. For many years, due to poor traffic conditions in the scenic spot, the steep slopes and sharp bends on the roads have affected the development of the old district tourism. Now, the tourism industry brings nearly 2 billion yuan of tourism revenue to Hong'an County every year, and has also promoted the rapid development of the county's tourism service industry. "Based on the actual situation of Taimai Mountain, relying on the mountains and water, using the rich mountain and water resources, vigorously developing characteristic tourism, allowing tourism to boost the economic development of the scenic spot, can generate huge economic benefits." Xiong Liangxiao introduced that the Hong'an County Government had invested more than 80 million yuan in the planning and construction of the scenic spot, and continuously improved the infrastructure construction of the scenic spot. Currently, the scenic spot has formed a 52-kilometer-long tourism circulation route of "one vertical and one horizontal", and developed tourism attractions such as the Tonghe River drifting, the Taimai Mountain main peak attraction, the Ai River风情valley, and the Xiangshan Lake. "There are particularly many tourists on weekends, so we need to prepare for reception in advance. Running a catering business in the mountains can earn more than 100,000 yuan a year, which is no worse than working in the coastal areas." Early in the morning, Pan Tao, a villager from Caoxiedian Village in Qiliping Town, Hong'an County, was busy. Nowadays, holidays have become the days that local villagers look forward to. The Xiangshan Lake boating and Tonghe River drifting tourism attractions have attracted tourists from surrounding areas such as Wuhan and Xin County in Henan Province, bringing considerable income to the villagers. "In the first half of this year, the town received a total of 460,000 tourists, achieving a comprehensive tourism income of 800 million yuan." The Party Secretary of Qiliping Town, Hong'an County, Li Changyu, excitedly said, "We are currently accelerating the software and hardware construction of the town area, striving for the integration of the urban area and scenic spots, with red and green colors complementing each other." Qiliping Town has 37 national key protected cultural relics, rich red tourism resources, and unparalleled green ecological resources. Making full use of this advantage, Qiliping Town has established the development concept of building a famous red tourism town. "While brightening up the tourist attractions, we must coordinate the infrastructure construction of the scenic spot and the town area." The Director of the Huanggang City Tourism Bureau, Lei Dian, introduced that in order to create a red tourism economic circle in Dabie Mountain, Huanggang City has continuously increased its infrastructure construction efforts. It has invested 3 billion yuan to newly modify and construct more than 200 kilometers of red tourism roads in Dabie Mountain, connecting more than 10 red tourism attractions, and opening cross-regional red tourism channels with Xin County in Henan Province and Jinzhai and Yuxi in Anhui Province.
