Five Core Factors Affecting SEO Optimization

Date: 2010-09-04 15:42 Source: Shenzhen SEO Author: Shenzhen SEO Clicks: 119 times

The five core factors of SEO optimization are:

1. Website Quality

2. Website Structure and Three META Attributes

3. External Links of the Website

4. Keyword Density

5. Server Factors

1. Website Quality, also known as "User Experience". Remember, Robin Li, the head of Baidu, said on CCTV's "Dialogue" program that Baidu always ranks the websites that users want most at the top, meaning the sites with the best "user experience" come first. The quality of "user experience" can be judged by three words: "content, updates, originality." Imagine a website with little content, very hollow; would Baidu rank this site high? Or a site that is not updated for ten days or half a month, or even a year; would Baidu rank it high? Or a site that completely copies others' content; could its ranking on Baidu be high? This point alone could account for 50% of Baidu's rankings.

2. Website Structure and Three META Attributes. The most important thing about website structure is that it should not be entirely composed of images and flash. Even if the webpage is very beautiful, Baidu will still consider image-heavy websites to be mostly junk pages. The three META attributes are title, keyword, and description, which directly inform search engines of the main content and keywords of the website. This point can account for more than 10% of Baidu's rankings.

3. External Links of the Website. There are two theories here: one is that the more other sites link to a particular site, the more important that site is considered. Another theory is that the more a site's content is reprinted, the more important it is. So this provides an idea for building external links. For Baidu, the broader the range of external links a website has, the better. This way, your site's importance can be highlighted.

4. Keyword Density. Only when the keyword density is sufficient can Baidu notice your website regarding a specific keyword and feel that your website's content is highly relevant to this keyword. For example, if your website is about weight loss drugs, then the density of the term "immigration" within the content should be relatively concentrated. If the keyword density is too low, Baidu may think the theme of the website is not weight loss drugs or at least not closely related to immigration, so the ranking won't be too high. However, the keyword density shouldn't be too high either; otherwise, Baidu might think you're stuffing keywords and penalize your site for cheating. For instance, I have a single-page immigration website with the keyword "Immigration Study Abroad Network". This program was modified from the z-blog program. To increase the keyword density on the homepage, I basically added keywords to the titles of each article, thereby increasing the keyword density on the homepage.

5. Server Factors. Imagine a website that opens very slowly, sometimes not opening at all, or a website that is unstable and often inaccessible. Could such a site rank high on Baidu? Even if it did rank high, Baidu would likely demote it. Therefore, the website must be hosted on a fast and stable server to ensure quick loading speeds and stable operation, allowing the site to gain high scores from Baidu. The location of the server also matters. You may have noticed that different regions searching for the same keyword often yield different results. Local websites tend to rank higher, meaning search engines recognize IP addresses. Based on these two points, I do not recommend using foreign hosts, as they are slower and offer no regional advantage in searches.
