Introduction to the plot of Tang Boyu Dian Qiuxiang 2:

Yesterday afternoon, the movie "Tang Boyu Dian Qiuxiang 2" started filming in Beijing. The original director of "Tang Boyu Dian Qiuxiang", Li Lichi, along with the new main cast Huang Xiaoming, Zhang Jingchu, and Ren Xianqi, as well as a group of classic supporting actors formed the creative team. When asked about what story this sequel tells, they all remained tight-lipped. In response to the predecessors Zhou Xingchi and Gong Li, Huang Xiaoming said he feels "no pressure," while Zhang Jingchu responded: "Being a flower vase is also good." At the scene, Huang Xiaoming lifted Zhang Jingchu's veil.

Reporters learned that besides "Tang Boyu Dian Qiuxiang 2," not long ago, the movie "Three Smiles - A Story of Talented Scholars and Beautiful Ladies" starring Guo Degang just wrapped up, and at the end of last year, the National Film Bureau also approved the project application for the movie "Three Smiles Yuan." Within half a year, there are three movies based on the "Tang Boyu" theme that have collided.

Plot Changes:

Funny moments with added action scenes

From the plot published on the official website of the National Radio and Television Administration, the full name of "Tang Boyu Dian Qiuxiang 2" that received approval is "Tang Boyu Dian Qiuxiang - Four Talented Scholars Show Their Skills," and the plot has also changed accordingly. Unlike a sequel, it seems more like a prequel to "Tang Boyu Dian Qiuxiang," where Qiuxiang's "previous incarnation" is named Qianqian. The plot changes to Tang Boyu being fond of standing up for justice since childhood, befriending Zhu Zhishan, Wen Zhengming, and Zhou Wenbin. Tang Boyu was sent by his mother to Qingfeng Temple to study, which made him suffer greatly. The three friends brought Tang Boyu through a secret passage to play at the back mountain, where Tang Boyu met and fell in love with Qianqian.

In addition, the previous "Tang Boyu Dian Qiuxiang" focused mainly on comedy, but "Tang Boyu Dian Qiuxiang 2" will not only be funny but will also add many action scenes, making it "more martial arts-like." Therefore, the film invited Lin Di'an, who served as the action choreographer for "The Matrix" and "Spider-Man 2." He revealed that this time he volunteered himself.

Cast Changes:

Huang Xiaoming challenges Zhou Xingchi's classic

Almost every character played by Zhou Xingchi has become a classic, and "Tang Boyu Dian Qiuxiang" is one of his representative works. Therefore, who plays Tang Boyu in the sequel is everyone's most concerned question. Yesterday, the announcement of the "Four Talented Scholars" selection surprised everyone: Ren Xianqi plays Xu Zhenqing, Chen Baixiang plays Zhu Zhishan, the founder of "Shanghai-style talk show," Zhou Libo, plays Wen Zhengming, and the male lead Tang Boyu is played by Huang Xiaoming.

Huang Xiaoming hasn't played too many comedic roles; previously, his portrayal of "Wei Xiaobao" in the TV series "鹿鼎记" was questioned by audiences. Therefore, a reporter asked Huang Xiaoming, do you have a comedic flair? Huang Xiaoming responded: "Actually, in real life, Zhou Xingchi and Fan Wei aren't funny." He said he felt no pressure. Additionally, he felt confident because he had played young Tang Boyu in a TV series six years ago. A reporter mentioned that Zhou Xingchi is the "King of Comedy." Huang Xiaoming joked: "He is the big king, I am the little king." To express the feeling of an ancient great scholar, Huang Xiaoming said he would learn calligraphy and painting, and even prepared ink, brush, paper, and inkstone at home.

The female lead Zhang Jingchu expressed feeling a lot of pressure because she needed to practice dancing and kung fu, which was a huge challenge for her. A reporter mentioned that Gong Li was a flower vase in the original film and wondered if her role would have more room to develop. Zhang Jingchu said: "Being a flower vase is also good, isn't it beautiful?"

Supporting Roles Remain Unchanged:

Chen Baixiang and Zheng Peipei reunite again

In "Tang Boyu Dian Qiuxiang 2," the producer of the 1993 version of "Tang Boyu Dian Qiuxiang," Xiang Huasheng, will once again serve as the supervisor; director Li Lichi will also take the helm again. Besides Chen Baixiang, several golden supporting actors from the 1993 version of "Tang Boyu Dian Qiuxiang," such as Zheng Peipei ("Mrs. Hua"), Zhu Mimi ("Mother Tang"), Yuan Qiongdan ("Sister Shiliu"), and Liang Rongzhong (who competed with Zhou Xingchi in front of the Hua residence over who was more pitiful), will reunite with this movie. All four will play new characters, and the classic cockroach "Xiaoqiang" and the dog "Wangcai" from the old version will also regain vitality in the film.
