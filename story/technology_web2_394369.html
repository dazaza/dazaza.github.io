2. Essay Questions

(1) What form does the Alipay account take? (Page 31) What are the certification methods? (Page 33)

Answer: Alipay is founded by Aliwangwang Group, the largest independent third-party payment platform in China, specifically providing "simple, secure, fast" online payment solutions for China's e-commerce. Alipay accounts are represented in electronic email form. Certification: Alipay certification is divided into personal certification and merchant certification. Merchant certification requires submission of business licenses, company accounts, etc., while personal certification requires simpler documentation, just submitting an ID number and bank account to process, once passed, it gains buyer qualifications, specific certification procedures.

(2) How many types of Alipay transaction states are there? What does each transaction state represent in terms of transaction progress? (Page 35)

Answer: There are two types: "Buyer has paid, waiting for seller to ship," and "Seller has shipped, waiting for buyer confirmation." Transaction progress: After the buyer pays Alipay, the funds are frozen, and the system automatically notifies the seller via email. At the same time, it shows the transaction status as "Buyer has paid, waiting for seller to ship." In this state, the seller needs to promptly fulfill the shipping obligation. The seller should actively modify the transaction status, fill in the correct shipping information to confirm shipment, at which point the system shows the transaction status as "Seller has shipped, waiting for buyer confirmation."

(3) What are the three types of evaluations on Taobao? How are points calculated for each type? (Page 41)

Answer: Evaluations are divided into "Good Review," "Neutral Review," and "Bad Review," each corresponding to a point. Users gain one point for each "Good Review," no points for a "Neutral Review," and lose one point for a "Bad Review."

(4) How is it determined who should bear the risk of loss or damage to goods? (Page 46)

Answer: Before the buyer signs for receipt, the risk is borne by the buyer; after signing for receipt, the risk is borne by the buyer.

2. Essay Questions

(1) Understanding and application of product characteristics need to be improved through which three stages? Please explain in detail the capabilities of each stage. (Page 52)

Answer: 1. Nature of the product 2. Characteristics of the product 3. Benefits of the product. Features: At a minimum, you need to understand the material composition, size specifications, applicable range of the product, knowing these product characteristics allows you to answer simple customer inquiries, at least able to answer what fabric the clothes are made of, what basic raw materials the food uses, what people or situations the product is suitable for... Usually, the characteristics of the product to a certain extent represent the advantage compared to similar products, for example: the fabric is more breathable and sweat-absorbing, using additive-free food is safer, the product can be used in two or multiple ways, feels unique, very personalized, limited edition, very creative gift ideas... If the commercial advantage of the product cannot be effectively converted into customer benefits, then when selling, customers won't be easily swayed, because understanding the professional qualities and abilities required to sell is a must-have for sales experts.

(2) What steps usually compose the online reception process of an online store? (Pages 76-77)

Answer: Greeting: Polite and courteous, when Wangwang prompts that a customer is consulting, greet before asking and answering questions. Inquiry: Actively inquire about the customer's needs and see if you can provide assistance. Consultation: Based on the customer's inquiry, use industry knowledge, product knowledge, life experience, etc., to make objective and professional responses. Negotiation: Regarding pricing issues, handle appropriately based on specific circumstances. If unable to accept price negotiation, politely refuse or redirect the customer's attention to buy-gift activities or full-send discounts, encouraging more consumption and hinting that they can exchange higher order amounts for corresponding price discounts. Avoid being rude or forceful, avoid disputes. Recommendation: While answering a product inquiry, consciously recommend more products to the customer, such as recommending other products with pairing suggestions, promoting sale items, recommending latest styles or store shopping policies. Assistance: After the customer purchases a product, help modify freight or guide them through online payment operations. Verification: After confirming the customer's payment, use Aliwangwang to verify the shopping list and delivery address with the customer. If the customer has special requirements or needs to change the address, promptly note it in the memo. Farewell: Politely bid farewell. For customers who have already shopped, you can wish for pleasant cooperation and ask them to patiently wait for delivery. If there are any issues, they can contact you at any time. For customers who did not immediately close the deal, wish them pleasant shopping and sincerely express your willingness to serve them. If necessary, add them as Wangwang friends for future customer management and follow-up.

(3) What are the various transaction statuses of Alipay transactions? What do these transaction statuses mean? (Pages 85-86)

Answer: 1. Waiting for buyer to pay 2. Buyer has paid 3. Buyer has shipped 4. Transaction successful 5. Refund transaction. Timely verify order contents and delivery address with the customer. If the customer chooses courier shipping and the delivery address belongs to remote small towns, generally postal mail takes 30 days, courier takes 10 days, virtual items take 3 days.

(4) On which disk symbol should the chat software be installed? Why? (Page 77)

Answer: Installing chat software on a non-system disk is an effective way to preserve records. For example, installing Aliwangwang on the D, E, or F drive of your computer ensures that even if the operating system encounters issues requiring reinstallation, only the system disk C will be formatted, sparing the chat tools installed on other hard drives, thus preserving chat records. These chat record files can be shared with work partners.

(3) What in-store marketing tools does Taobao provide? (Pages 134-139)

Answer: The store sign is the first impression customers have of the store. Taobao places each store's sign at the golden ratio line of the first screen, aiming to convey the store's operating content, advantages, selling points, and brand value to browsers in the shortest time possible through visual design. Therefore, the sign plays a crucial role in visual marketing. Wangpu can set up to six customizable pages. These customizable page shortcuts are located below the store sign, essentially multiplying the valuable first screen space by six times. Thus, reasonably utilizing customizable pages allows conveying more marketing information to customers on the first screen, effectively promoting sales. The store's promotion area is the second visual focus that customers see after the store sign and customizable pages. Since the shortcuts of customizable pages are presented in text, if the design of the promotion area is sufficiently eye-catching, it might surpass the customizable pages above in terms of visual appeal and effectiveness. A store typically has hundreds or even thousands of products. Without reasonable product classification, customers may find it difficult to locate what they need, turning the shopping experience into a needle-in-a-haystack situation. Therefore, reasonably setting up and optimizing product classifications not only helps customers find products but also significantly increases product browsing and transaction volumes. Regular stores can display up to 20 products per page, with product sorting arranged by ending time from nearest to farthest, executed according to the system's predetermined design standards, unchangeable by anyone. However, the product display on the Wangpu homepage offers more autonomy, allowing settings according to merchant needs, providing ample design space and flexibility for in-store marketing, making product displays as flexible as physical store shelves, adaptable to merchant needs. Compared to regular stores, Wangpu has another important feature: expansion of store display areas according to needs, increasing more display space through adding customizable modules, releasing more promotional information, satisfying customers' desire to "browse" stores, ensuring online stores are no less competitive in marketing means than traditional storefronts.
