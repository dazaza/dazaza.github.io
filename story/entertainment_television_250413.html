The main plot of "Never Leave Never Abandon" is introduced as follows: The happy life of the couple, Jiang Lan and Lin Fangxi who are both doctors, was disrupted by an explosive event - Lin Lin, the son born ten years ago from Lin Fangxi's first love Bai Xiaou, inevitably had to enter the Lin family. It turned out that Lin Fangxi broke up with Bai Xiaou due to a misunderstanding back then, without knowing she was already pregnant. After giving birth to Lin Lin, Bai Xiaou raised him alone and never married. Before her death, she hoped that Lin Lin could return to his father's side. Thus, the drama unfolds the various conflicts, confusions, and tests faced by a happy family in front of unexpected ethical shocks, as well as their final steadfast adherence to beautiful emotions. Meanwhile, a mysterious woman named Bai Ling, claiming to come from overseas, entered the hospital where Lin Fangxi worked. Under the identity of Lin Fangxi's close colleague, she secretly monitored everything in the Lin family. She devoted herself to caring for and helping Lin Lin's life, becoming a red beauty confidante for Lin Fangxi to pour out his troubles, with sinister intentions. While striving to gain the favor of the Lin family, she constantly created traps of misfortune for them. Her incredible behavior made the audience full of doubts...

Lin Lin's sudden appearance caused a storm in the Lin family, with all kinds of unexpected events happening one after another. The first problem was whether to let Lin Lin into the house or not. Jiang Lan's mother Qian Sisu and her sister Jiang Ying firmly refused to let her be Lin Lin's stepmother. Lin Yuan, their daughter, after meeting Lin Lin, became even more capricious. Her rejection and extreme behavior made both families fall into extreme anxiety and restlessness. Lin's mother, driven by the desire for a grandson, surprisingly took a liking to Lin Lin whom she had never met before, showing immense affection towards him like an old hen wrapping a frightened little bird under her wing. This way, the family conflict escalated further, creating more branches and leaves. The originally harmonious relationships between husband and wife, in-laws, mother-in-law and daughter-in-law, daughters and parents, mother-in-law and son-in-law became tangled in complex emotions, tense and subtle confrontations. Facing an uncontrollable daughter, a stubborn husband, a resentful mother, a doting grandmother on her grandson, and a troublesome younger sister who didn't know any better, Jiang Lan also had to face the inner conflict and torment of her feelings. Lin's mother was extremely indignant, believing that this was the mother-in-law's intentional revenge. After careful reflection, Jiang Lan and Lin Fangxi both felt that they must responsibly face the family, eliminate misunderstandings, and repair their feelings. With the greatest tolerance and patience, Jiang Lan strived to ease the tense relations between her mother-in-law and mother, Lin Yuan and Lin Lin. Lin Fangxi, on the other hand, voluntarily went to the TV station for an interview, taking full responsibility for the family turmoil. Realizing the great trouble he had caused, Lin Lin deeply regretted and simply ran away from home for several days without returning, making the whole family anxiously search for him and feel heartbroken. In fact, Lin Lin did not genuinely run away; he just wanted to use the only money he had to buy Lin Yuan a similar trophy to express his apology. During the process of finding a trophy for Lin Yuan, Lin Lin wandered around and endured many hardships. At this time, Lin Fangxi finally found clues and came, fighting with the hooligans and getting injured. However, Lin Fangxi eventually brought Lin Lin safely back home.

Lin Lin was a child who valued righteousness and understood things. Although there were continuous upheavals at home due to him, Lin Fangxi and Jiang Lan had developed deep feelings for him out of pity. As Lin Lin spent more time with him, Lin Fangxi increasingly felt that he owed a lot to his deceased first love Bai Xiaou. Therefore, under Lin Lin's request, Lin Fangxi agreed to visit Bai Xiaou's grave in a southern ancient town with Lin Lin, and later secretly erected a tombstone for Bai Xiaou in the Beijiang City where they lived. And Jiang Lan's persistence in marital feelings and family responsibilities indeed showed Bai Ling her strength of gentleness and rational determination. However, Bai Ling was no ordinary woman either. Since the day she returned to China, she consciously used her grace and talent to tempt Lin Fangxi, preparing to pull him to her side step by step, giving outsiders the impression of Lin Fangxi having an extramarital affair. Taking advantage of Jiang Lan going on academic activities, she stayed in the Lin family under the name of taking care of Lin Lin and Lin Yuan, participating in the daily life of the Lin family. Subsequently, she sent photos of herself and Lin Fangxi at a bar to Jiang Lan...

But Bai Ling also paid a heavy price, and she soon tasted the bitterness of failure. To lure Lin Fangxi into a trap, she had to endure repeatedly distancing herself from her beloved boyfriend Lao Li, whose constant disappointment, anger, and sarcasm toward Bai Ling in turn inflicted repeated blows to her soul. At this point, no matter what, Jiang Lan would hold on to her family and that true love.

Bai Ling, heartbroken, drove recklessly to chase him and was seriously injured in a car accident. Bai Ling was timely rescued by Lin Fangxi and Jiang Lan. Although they fully knew that Bai Ling had been causing trouble behind the scenes for the Lin family, they still forgave her thoroughly. Bai Ling's love shattered, leaving her hopeless. She attempted suicide but failed. Jiang Lan and Lin Fangxi decided to sincerely rescue Bai Ling from both spiritual and physical aspects. Subsequently, he and Jiang Lan spontaneously decided to go abroad to replace Lao Li, saving Bai Ling's love.

Half a year later, Bai Ling and Lao Li were going to the United States again. They wanted to take Lin Lin with them. Lin Lin declined this wonderful invitation and turned to call Jiang Lan "Mom." Jiang Lan movedly embraced him, as she had long regarded this child as her own son.
