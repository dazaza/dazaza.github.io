New features in Foxmail6.0

In addition to email reading management, you can read RSS

RSS is a Simple information aggregation (RSS: Really Simple Syndication) abbreviation, for the respected user you, RSS is a subscription consultation function, just like offline subscription newspapers, magazines, and more convenient. With this subscription feature, you can subscribe to your favorite content, such as portal news, personal blogs, RSS-enabled forums, and more.

Starting from Foxmail 6.0, we will be happy to provide you with Chinese RSS reading function. Foxmail 6.0 can automatically collect the latest information for you to subscribe to, maintain the timeliness of news content, do not need you to visit the website one by one, the goal is strong, save you valuable time. In this software, the use of RSS function and the use of mail function is very similar, you do not need to learn to use freely.

Note that Foxmail 6.0 already has a default subscription to some content, you can also subscribe to the content you like. RSS feeds are easy to use and do not require an Email address or any personal information. If you see a logo on a page like this:,,,,, it means that these sites can be subscribed. See the help information for details.

The first automatic intelligent learning, easy to obtain the ideal anti-garbage effect

Foxmail 6.0 followed Foxmail 5.0 with another leap forward in anti-spam functionality. The new version of the system has automatic learning, reducing the application and configuration complexity of the anti-garbage feature to zero. Users no longer need to manually formulate and learn, they can accurately identify spam.

Foxmail research and development team developed an automatic intelligent learning algorithm, which can automatically analyze and determine the user's daily mail operation and management at the same time, so that the user can use Foxmail software for a period of time (depending on the frequency of your sending and receiving emails). It may take a few weeks of learning to accumulate time) to achieve the ideal anti-spam effect, and the effect will be better and better, so that you are handy.

The improvement of anti-spam performance in this process is continuously optimized with the increase of use time, you do not need to do anything extra, you can enjoy the excellent anti-spam effect brought by this innovative technology, and gradually get better.

Enhanced security, viewing HTML mail is no longer risky

Bad HTML mail may carry a destructive ActiveX control or Script malicious code, now, Foxmail 6.0 can display HTML mail beautiful original appearance at the same time, effectively prevent these controls and code running, so as to avoid automatic activation of attachment viruses or trojans, to protect your mailbox and computer security.

Provides 4 display layouts for an updated visual experience

In the traditional layout of the main screen, the content preview window is placed at the bottom of the screen. You can also place it on the right side of the screen, which will reduce the information displayed in the mail or article list, but it will make it easier for you to read more emails or RSS articles at once. In addition, the content preview window can be hidden or enlarged to meet your different needs.

Tips: You can find four small ICONS representing different layouts in the status bar at the bottom of the main interface. You can easily switch to the corresponding layout by clicking the corresponding ICONS.

Group management, more clear

In order to facilitate your daily management and reading of mail, Foxmail 6.0 provides mail grouping function. All messages are automatically organized by a certain group (for example, the time group includes "today", "yesterday", "day of the week", "last week", "earlier", and other elements such as the sender can be grouped, so that you can browse the message at a glance.
