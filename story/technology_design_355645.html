3. CCTV Weather Forecast Theme Song - "Fishing Boat Evening Song"

　　It is said to be the only background music that has never changed on Chinese television so far. The Weather Forecast program has consistently used it. "Fishing Boat Evening Song" (the Weather Forecast background music), was adapted and performed by the then famous electronic keyboardist Pu Qizhang in Shanghai based on the homonymous folk melody. She was originally an accompaniment actor in the Shanghai Orchestra and graduated from the "Little Star" Art Troupe. In the art world, she achieved significant success and collaborated with the Shanghai Light Music Orchestra on many popular tunes like "Happy Dai City." Musician Tu Bahai often worked with her. After this piece was completed, Pu Qizhang retired from the music scene. However, the piece became widely loved by the Chinese public due to its adoption as the background music for the prime-time CCTV Weather Forecast program. When Pu Qizhang used the Yamaha three-row keyboard concert electronic organ to adapt and perform this piece, she did not expect it to become the golden background music for a prime-time program, nor did she anticipate its impact on so many people.
