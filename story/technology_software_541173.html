Office Gossip: November Peach Blossom Fortune Forecast for 12 Chinese Zodiac Signs

The Cold Dew has passed and the Frost’s Descent is gradually approaching; the weather slowly turns cold. Entering November, the influence of the Earthly Branch Xū (戌土) is slowly subsiding, while the influence of Hài (亥水) is quickly taking over. This means we are entering the winter season where water thrives. The prosperity of water affects the 12 zodiac signs differently. Today, we will focus on the romantic fortune of the 12 zodiac signs, as per Hohhot Recruitment Network.

Rat: According to the five elements theory, the Rat belongs to water. Entering the month of Hài where water thrives, there is an excess of water which leads to competition among peers. The emotional fortune this month is average. Although you may encounter some peach blossoms, spending money and losing wealth due to emotional issues is likely. Also, conflicts with family members due to differing opinions on emotions can arise, leading to arguments. For single individuals, the emotional period remains stable, so it's best not to rush things and instead focus on building a solid foundation.

Ox: According to the five elements theory, the Ox belongs to damp earth. Entering November where water thrives, the dampness increases. Compared to last month, the emotional fortune improves slightly. However, due to the lingering negative fortune from last month, caution is still necessary. This month, one should increase contact with relatives and friends to strengthen emotional bonds, thereby enhancing your peach blossom luck, as noted by Ulanqab Recruitment Network.

Tiger: According to the five elements theory, the Tiger belongs to wood. Entering November where water thrives and nourishes wood, and with the harmonious relationship between Yín (寅) and Hài (亥), the good peach blossom fortune is very strong. These two months are the most prosperous periods of the year in terms of fortune. Both peach blossom fortune and interpersonal relationships will be excellent, presenting opportunities for noble cooperation and enriching emotional life. It is also a time when love fortune surges, avoiding bad peach blossoms, as highlighted by Manzhouli Recruitment Network.

Rabbit: According to the five elements theory, the Rabbit belongs to wood, which is nourished by water. The Rabbit finally dispels the gloom of the birth year and gradually moves from the shadows into the sunlight. This month brings good peach blossom fortune, providing excellent opportunities to meet desirable members of the opposite sex. You can also go out with someone you like, strengthening intimacy. Remember to forget past troubles and welcome a better future.

Dragon: According to the five elements theory, the Dragon belongs to damp earth. Entering November where water thrives, the damp earth does not exert much force. This month, the peach blossom fortune for the Dragon is average, and care must be taken to avoid bad peach blossoms. This is a relatively ordinary month for emotional life. However, the Dragon should treat friends and family around them with integrity, laying a good foundation for their future.

Snake: According to the five elements theory, the Snake belongs to fire. This is a month where the Snake encounters its conflicting element, resulting in declining fortune. Emotional disputes increase, leading to constant arguments with others. It is important to adjust one’s mindset and practice more tolerance to prevent conflicts from escalating due to temporary impulses. For singles, this month is mainly about laying a foundation, not a good time for romance. Even if there is someone you like, this month is not the time for aggressive declarations.
