Common weight loss methods include: ① preventive weight loss; ② exercise weight loss; ③ behavior weight loss; ④ mechanical weight loss; ⑤ sauna bath weight loss; ⑥ plaster weight loss; ⑦ paraffin weight loss; ⑧ surgical weight loss; ⑨ massage weight loss; ⑩ electronic weight loss.

Among these, drug-induced weight loss should not be overused. Drug-induced weight loss is a method of losing weight that is only adopted when the above physical and chemical weight loss methods are ineffective. There are two types of drug-induced weight loss: appetite suppression and metabolic promotion. Drug-induced weight loss has many side effects and should be avoided as much as possible.

Obesity not only affects body beauty but also brings inconvenience to life. More importantly, it can easily cause multiple diseases, accelerate aging and death. How to lose weight scientifically? Medical experts believe that the scientific way to lose weight is to control calorie intake and increase exercise, while maintaining a balanced diet. Weight loss is a systematic project that requires persistence over a long period of time, and there is no quick fix.

However, if you can master some dietary weight loss tricks, it will be very helpful for your weight loss. You might want to give them a try:

1. Set a weight loss goal (ideal or standard weight). Write it down on paper and post it where you can see it every day.

2. Keep a weight loss diary. Create cards or charts to mark out your planned weight loss numbers and achievement status.

3. Drink more water. You should drink seven to eight glasses of plain water every day. Water is the most basic element for bodily function and contains no calories, making it the most suitable beverage for dieting.

4. Have perseverance and determination. During moderate dieting, don't "try" but "persist." Control your appetite in front of delicious food, eat in moderation.

5. Control calories and fat. Always be aware of the calories in food. Reduce fatty meat in your diet and increase fish and poultry.

6. Eat blandly. Eat less salt. The more salty things you eat, the more you want to eat. Avoid processed foods with sauces, which contain abundant sugar, salt, and flour, adding to your caloric intake.

7. Eat more fruits and vegetables. Consume an appropriate amount of fiber-rich fruits, vegetables, and whole wheat bread.

8. Maintain a balanced diet. Plan your diet daily and follow it accordingly. Pay attention to eating on schedule and avoid overeating. Slow down your eating time, spend at least 20 minutes per meal.

9. Negative caloric balance. Remember the principle of weight loss: caloric intake must be less than your consumption.

10. Establish a good lifestyle. Remember that you are learning a "way of life," correcting previous bad eating habits and lifestyles.

Weight loss requires patience and perseverance. Persistence is success!

① Continuously eat only apples for three days, avoiding other fruits and foods.

Dietary tips after three days

After three days of apple dieting, since you've been away from stimulating foods, your gastrointestinal tract will be delicate, your taste buds sensitive, and your stomach smaller.

Starting from the fourth day, your diet should gradually return to normal. Don't suddenly eat a lot of food, especially snacks. During the first three days of resuming your diet, it's best to start with porridge and tofu.

In short, when resuming your diet after weight loss, the food should be bland and not excessive. This way, the effect of weight loss will last.
