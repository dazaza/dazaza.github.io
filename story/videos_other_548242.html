Gemini's philosophy of love is "not caring about longevity, but only seeking to have had it," which even the paternalistic Capricorn finds hard to resist.

Love for Capricorn Men:

Capricorn men are conservative, but their ambition makes it hard to believe they are so. However, he usually won't let you see his ambition. His ability to conceal is quite remarkable, just like how easily he blends into a crowd without being noticed. Yet, most Capricorn men don't waste much time on emotions unless your family background can help his career. Nonetheless, he is honest, whether in his career or in love and life. To achieve success earlier and reach the peak, he seizes any opportunity that might lead him to success. In fact, his pragmatism absolutely wouldn't allow him to indulge in romance when his career is not yet established. Even if you help his career, you'll find he expects you to get into shape early and stop wasting your life. Capricorn men are indeed romantic, not just for comfort! Many great lovers come from this sign. Only you need to understand, his romance is often restrained by his discipline, so most of the time, he appears serious and calm. Though he seems steady, serious, and practical, deep inside he awaits praise. He expects you to be a good wife and mother, and also to get along well with his large family. Of course, you must win the favor of all his relatives; any criticism of his family will sound to him like you're asking for a breakup. He loves everything, including you, remember: though he's the kind whose flowers bloom late, once they do, they last a very long time!

Love for Capricorn Women:

Regardless of what impression a Capricorn woman gives you on the surface, you should never think she is weak. She may be soft fundamentally, but definitely not weak. And under no circumstances should you consider her from a male perspective as part of a weaker group, because that could make you lose faith in continuing to be a man. Moreover, even if you stake your bet on a man's ambition, you'd still lose. She is a woman who knows exactly what she can do and what she's doing. She always handles her own affairs vigilantly and isn't uncaring about yours; she simply understands her position too well, especially since you're a man. She might place feelings second and work first; this doesn't mean she doesn't need feelings, just that she's uninterested in wasting time. She's more interested in people with good backgrounds or families, but the key isn't money. She probably earns faster and more than you, rather, she cares more about social status. She's quite traditional and holds firm opinions. She may be conservative, but she's also feminine; she may be quiet, but she can coldly accept challenges. She has unique elegance, conservatism, tradition, grace, etiquette, stubbornness, melancholy, optimism, and despair. She's really versatile, only she restrains her own nature because she knows the road ahead is long. Before finding a man she can rely on, there's much she needs to do herself; moreover, which man can see the future clearer than her?

Aquarius:

Their relationship is both like lovers and friends, balancing emotion and reason, making them a perfect pair.

Least Compatible Zodiac Sign: Cancer. Compatibility Index - 40 points [Aquarius is at a disadvantage]. Cancer's rational, timid, and lack of confidence attitude truly makes Aquarians very impatient. Aquarius is an overly emotional sign, lacking commonality between the two.

Aquarius men always give the impression of being curious, and what's most frustrating is: he often makes you feel like he's interested in you, only to discover that he's equally curious about everyone. He prefers women who attract him with intelligence rather than those with beautiful hair and pretty faces but nothing beneath. Sometimes, if you intentionally seem indifferent to him or ignore his presence, his interest in you will increase significantly. His feelings always start from friendship, and even after marriage, you should maintain a friendly relationship with him, which is very important. You need to know that in his mind, friends are very important; and when you're dating him, you should notice that he always treats his friends better than you because he views your relationship as close, like family. He likes women with attitudes but not those moralists or overly passionate feminists. He tends to marry late mainly because he can't find a woman intellectually matched with him, or he loves freedom too much. An Aquarius man can make your life colorful, and just being his friend won't make you bored!

Aquarius women have a special kind of devotion, but you wouldn't be able to tell from her outward appearance. She is everyone's friend, but some people imagine her as a flirt, because they can't distinguish between friends and lovers, while she does clearly. At least she can sense who makes her heart beat faster. There is no Aquarius woman who doesn't act mischievously; her speed and methods of changing clothes can break Guinness records, especially in styling her head. Most Aquarius women love hats or unusual hair accessories, which is a rather subtle hidden behavior. Mainly, she doesn't want ordinary people to discover her sharp, sensitive, and intelligent mind. The best way to pursue her is definitely not through "flirting", sometimes she can be an excellent insulator. Start as friends, then present yourself as a mystery; she's interested in mysterious people, but she doesn't like you probing into her secrets, and even less does she like you testing her warmth, which is a trick kids play, go try it with someone else! Her interests change quite a bit. She tries various things, so you need to be prepared mentally, and you need a brain that matches hers; you can even challenge her, and your chances of winning her favor will be several times higher than others. She loves to test people's motives, especially checking your sincerity; even though she looks so carefree on the outside, she's still afraid of getting hurt.

Least Compatible Zodiac Sign: Leo. Pisces men often make you think they are like dreams, then you discover, what they breathe isn't air, but dreams. In his view, dreams don't represent rich imagination, dreams are what allow him to continue living in reality. If you deprive him of his right to dream, you'll find this man becomes completely devoid of anything lovable. Pisces men are indeed adorable, and you rarely find one who looks older than his actual age; most appear younger, one of his favorite protective colors; but sometimes he's troubled by this trait many people yearn for, because it makes him seem unreliable, especially in front of the opposite sex. All Pisces seek false security, therefore, he often makes you feel he's not what you imagined, and he always takes your imagination for a ride. Fishes always have the desire to act in the opposite direction and enjoy hiding their true thoughts, letting others know only the negative answers. Though Pisces like to hedge, this is also a form of imagination. Pisces men are indeed romantic, thanks to their rich imagination, but sometimes their avoidance mentality troubles those who want to like him. Older Pisces handle responsibility better, and all Pisces need confirmation, which helps them bravely face problems. He won't tell you his innermost thoughts, you can only feel them with your heart. Ah, fishing!!

Love for Pisces Women:

Pisces women are the dream lovers of many men, but these mermaids aren't easy to find. The key is that she always knows how to make her man protect her and do things for her. Of course, you might encounter some very strong female fish, the kind that makes men pale; and you might also meet the clumsy type, the ones who want to outdo men in everything. However, regardless of the type of female fish, they always give off the impression of needing protection, being shy, occasionally careless, charming, wise, confused, neurotic, etc.; this is a protective color, didn't I mention? Pursuing false security. It's an instinct, and this semi-disclosed attitude is often the main reason why many men willingly do things for her. Pisces women often disarm men, making them talk about their feelings, and magically, after chatting with her, they relax as if hypnotized and may fall madly in love with her. They are absolutely loved by everyone, though there are some overly arrogant female fish that make people hesitate; "beloved by all" certainly doesn't describe this type. She is very feminine, so she also has the emotional troubles that women face. She needs security, she needs protection; even if she's better than men, why press down on a man's self-esteem? She's a mystic, her secrets may outnumber your friends, but her clever mind always knows how to hide them. Do you think you're fishing? Don't know who's fishing whom!

Aries:

Most Compatible Zodiac Sign: Sagittarius. Compatibility Index - 100 points [Aries has the upper hand]. Both belong to the Fire element, both are passionate; their romance develops quickly and intensely, their characters and views are similar, so they are a dazzling pair.

Least Harmonious Zodiac Sign: Virgo. Compatibility Index - 40 points [Aries is at a disadvantage]. The generous Aries and the sensitive Virgo find it hard to find common ground; if they want a lasting love, they need heaven's help!!

Love for Aries Men:

Aries men are relatively passionate, but they have a fairy-tale-like emotional pattern; imagining themselves as princes or knights, and their partners as princesses or daughters of lords. From another perspective, since you have such a captivating identity in his imagination, you need to maintain that image. He's a child, and all children dislike disillusionment. He enters love quickly and can also detach from it quickly; sometimes, you really shouldn't fear his passion, but rather dread his icy responses - that indicates: everything to him is meaningless or uninteresting! Although we mentioned the word "child" to describe him, some words used for children apply to him, like jealousy, strong possessiveness, and not trusting partners easily. However, he's quite trustworthy; he's sincere, dedicated, and passionately gives himself to love. Many people don't believe in reconciliation, but he does, and he hopes for it. His temper isn't great, and he gets angry easily. In relationships, he needs to be in charge; otherwise, he gets upset, sometimes acting childish and irrational. He won't shirk responsibility, but he loses interest quickly in many things, so maintaining his interest and passion in the relationship is crucial; keep your ladylike demeanor, take care of him like a child, listen to him, let him lead, don't damage his masculinity.

Love for Aries Women:

Aries women walk their own path; if you want to chase her, you need to run faster than her and set up a carefully designed net in front of her without her noticing, but who can outrun her? She has a dream lover, she even awaits the "Prince Charming" or the knight who saves her from the thorny castle tower, but the person who appears in reality must surpass her! Although she's domineering and rough, she expects to be tamed by a real strongman who kneels before her knees; looking at her lover with eyes full of tenderness, depth, and pride. Don't expect this woman to abandon her pride and confidence, don't force her, and don't put her behind the scenes; if you really want to do this, you'd better learn not to think about her, because you might lose her anytime, and every time you think about her, it hurts. I can't continue mentioning her innocent, cruel smile, her passion, and her sincere loving eyes; I already see you collapsing in pain. Don't think that having this woman's love or being married to her gives you the right to put a collar around her neck with your name on it. You'd better not even think about it; she would divorce you for this. She demands freedom, absolutely! And you must trust her as she trusts you. She doesn't deceive, and her love at first sight still feels sweet to her now, especially since she's so bold.

Two members of the Earth element family, their temperaments and ways of thinking are quite close, so it's no surprise they attract each other; but their romance is of the slow-flowing type, without breath-taking dramatic scenes.

Least Compatible Zodiac Sign: Libra. Compatibility Index - 40 points [Taurus is at a disadvantage]. Although both belong to Venus's camp, Taurus is yin, and Libra is yang, so there's no intersection point!!

Taurus men take a lot of time. He's romantic, this isn't a joke; it just takes practice to recognize his romance, and he's the coach, a slow-coach. He understands how enchanting romantic sensations can be, just like him. I hope you have ladylike manners and a bit of intelligence, like knowing etiquette, understanding when to talk and when to stay silent; and lowering your head in front of gentlemen, not making strange expressions that make other gentlemen think you fancy them. Of course, gentlemen, if a woman shows interest in you, I hope you check the birthday of her protector first, preferably not in May; although his temperament is quite gentle, sometimes he's not very friendly, like trampling over your body... He's a nice guy, just a bit stubborn, but his love always feels simple and plain; besides giving flowers, he writes poems, and he has another charming talent—earning money. I see your eyes lighting up, but I have to tell you: don't push him, no bull likes being pushed.

Taurus women are mostly quiet and strong. They quietly endure life's tests and setbacks, rarely complaining. When other zodiac women complain, she continues walking forward without wasting energy. She has the ability to handle everything, don't underestimate her; she wants to find a real man to pass the burden to, so don't underestimate yourself, at least she won't underestimate you if you can make her feel secure for life. She may be stubborn and has strong self-control, so in general, unless it affects her value judgment, she won't argue strongly with you and will endure your different viewpoints using her self-control. Don't anger her, it does you no good; if you want to be boneless, this might be a good way. Taurus women are quite pragmatic and romantic, but she doesn't like emotions interfering with reality. However, she really enjoys romance; her idea of romance isn't counting stone slabs at Chiang Kai-shek Memorial Hall, that's too exhausting, something more static! Every cow has possessiveness, she really dislikes losing what she owns, even though she's resigned, she strives to keep everything. She has quite sensitive senses; only real things attract her, she has no interest in plastic flowers. Her home is undoubtedly incredibly comfortable; don't send fake flowers, otherwise you have no chance at all—not even sitting on her doorstep!

Gemini:

Most Compatible Zodiac Sign: Aquarius. Compatibility Index - 100 points [Gemini has the advantage]. Both belong to the intellectual, avant-garde type, their shared personality and thinking make them blend seamlessly; their penchant for good discussions and debates suits each other well, so love for them is colorful!!!

Least Compatible Zodiac Sign: Scorpio. Compatibility Index - 40 points [Gemini is at a disadvantage]. Active and ever-changing Gemini meets the intense and sharp Scorpio, and you lose miserably; to maintain the relationship, you have to try harder! And harder!!!

Love for Gemini Men:

Gemini men need two kinds of love, or you could say two types of love; anyway, he likes everything in pairs. Although this doesn't necessarily mean he requires two women to provide him with two loves, if you can offer him both types of love at once, he's probably already eyeing you as his chosen one. Gemini men are quite magical; he can say "I love you" in a hundred ways, and he can always find another hundred ways at any time, so you need enough mental agility to keep up with him; but sometimes you shouldn't follow too closely, smart as you are, you should know when to keep a distance.

Gemini's emotions require time and space to intertwine. "Change" is one of his nicknames; Gemini people practically have several names. He changes quickly, whether in feelings, positions, moods, ideas, or clothing—don't forget, he's two people. It's not that you're dizzy, but seeing him as two people makes everything more interesting, especially when he's sarcastic towards you.

Don't delve too deeply into his inner world; there's a place in his heart that no one is allowed to approach. You'd better conform to his rules and not violate his taboos. Whether before or after marriage, he won't settle down, including in his way of speaking. You must always retain your imagination, even if you're together until you're old, you must maintain it, otherwise, your life will be boring.

Gemini women often dazzle you, sometimes even make you confused. This woman has many times more changes than regular women; she's made up of a bunch of different personalities, so she always keeps you guessing, and she always does. Her speed is incredible; I'm not asking you to race or jog with her. If she wanted to compete with you in speed, you'd lose even before reaching the starting line. Dating a Gemini woman requires imagination; you really need to work hard to figure out which one is the real her, and by the end, you'll realize: none of them were her, the real her never showed up, and if you realize this, then you've missed the chance to truly know her, and she's gone; wasn't it said at the beginning? Her speed is incredible!

Be smart, respond quickly, trust her, although... it's a bit of a stretch, but you absolutely must trust her. She's always worried about this and that, but it's not obvious to others.
