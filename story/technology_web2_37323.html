Make love once, and you'll know.

by rockxie on 2007-11-30 20:50:05

This is a search site based on the Google personalized search engine. To put it plainly, it involves searching for results within a specified few websites.

Google keeps improving, and I really like this feature. Moreover, I believe more and more people will come to appreciate it.

Website navigation portals have always been popular in China. However, I believe that Chinese internet users are gradually upgrading their habits, transitioning from "website navigation" to "search engines."

Search engines have a significant problem: the internet is filled with numerous junk sites, cluttered with useless information, and even many viruses. This provides those who are accustomed to using website navigation portals with a reason not to "evolve."

The emergence of personalized search can address this issue.

Personalized search can be considered something between website navigation and the current general search.

The only hurdle: typing.

I speculate that among the large number of internet users in China, a considerable proportion do not know how to type. Therefore, these individuals are staunch supporters of website navigation portals.

Recently, I created XXOO.com.cn, which is a search website based on Google's personalized search. By manually adding websites that are trusted by the public and contain large amounts of data, users can significantly improve their efficiency when searching for information within these results.

Additionally, taking inspiration from traditional website navigation portals, XXOO.com.cn will gradually incorporate some commonly used features.
