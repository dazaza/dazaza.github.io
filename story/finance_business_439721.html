Young man picks up a wallet, starts to dating with the female owner, and successfully proposes on the bus

by zxyhszzfp on 2011-05-31 09:53:54

Wang Fei knelt down to propose, not with flowers or chocolate, but with his salary card - Photo by reporter Ding Jie of Henan Business Daily.

Reporters Xiong Yuwei and Zhang Linan of Henan Business Daily: A man unexpectedly found a wallet on a bus containing cash, ID card, bank cards and other items. After returning it intact, he won the heart of the beautiful owner of the lost wallet.

Yesterday morning, the man proposed to his girlfriend on the bus and was successful. Although it was Valentine's Day, the young man did not give roses or chocolates but instead handed over his salary card.

The proposal on the bus won the heart of the beautiful woman.

At 10 a.m. yesterday, a B11 bus left Liu Zhuang bus station.

This particular bus was decorated with balloons and ribbons, looking like a flower car. When the vehicle reached the Luge Town stop on Garden Road in Zhengzhou, a young woman boarded the bus.

At that moment, Wang Fei, standing at the front of the bus, immediately went up to her, pulled her to the middle of the bus, and knelt down on one knee: "Marry me!"

The woman blushed deeply. Seeing the cameras all around the bus, she was a bit at a loss, urging Wang Fei: "Get up quickly, what are you doing?"

"Please say yes! Although I don't have a house yet, I hope you can be my wife." As he spoke, Wang Fei took out a mysterious gift box from his chest and thrust it into her hands.

"Open it and see what the mysterious gift is." Encouraged by the crowd on the bus, she opened the box to find a bank card inside.

"This is my salary card, you know the password!" With these words from Wang Fei, there was a burst of laughter in the bus.

"Say yes, say yes..."

Encouraged by everyone on the bus, the woman accepted Wang Fei's proposal.

Returning the wallet intact led to their meeting

The proposer, Wang Fei, 30 years old, works in Zhengzhou. His acquaintance with the woman Ma Xiaojing was purely accidental.

Seven or eight months ago, Wang Fei found a wallet on the B11 bus by chance. Inside were Ma Xiaojing's ID card, bank cards, and more than 800 yuan in cash.

Wang Fei handed the wallet over to the bus driver and exchanged contact numbers with him.

Driver Hu Shengjun then went through various channels to locate the owner, Ma Xiaojing, and returned the wallet while explaining the situation.

"I was very upset when I lost my wallet, thinking I would never find it again. There are too few people like this nowadays," Ma Xiaojing said after retrieving her wallet and decided to thank Wang Fei.

After they met and felt good about each other, they began to interact frequently. Three months later, they became a couple.

"Our relationship started through the bus, and I wanted to propose to her on Valentine's Day," Wang Fei said, adding that if it could happen on the bus, wouldn't that be better?

A few days ago, Wang Fei contacted Zhengzhou Rapid Bus, which gave strong support and even funded the decoration of the bus.

"I've seen special proposals in the media before, but never thought it would happen to me. I was so nervous," Ma Xiaojing said, adding that as soon as she got on the bus, she didn't know what to say.
