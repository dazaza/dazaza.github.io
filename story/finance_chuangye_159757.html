Situated at the core of the Guanyinqiao business district, it boasts prominent geographical advantages and convenient transportation conditions.

Government Strongly Supports the Emergence of the "Industrial Port"

The economic concept of "production services" was first proposed in 1975 by American economist Browning during his classification of the service sector, distinguishing it from living services. It refers to the service industry that provides security services for maintaining the continuity of industrial production processes, promoting industrial technological progress, upgrading industries, and improving production efficiency. It is directly related to the supporting services of manufacturing.

Production services are the fastest-growing industry in the world economy. Currently, the service industry output of some developed countries accounts for about 55% of their GDP, and in individual countries, it even exceeds 75%. The growth of their production services far surpasses the average growth level of the service sector, developing at a very remarkable speed.

In recent years, China has increasingly valued the development of production services. The State Council and Chongqing Municipality have successively issued a series of policy documents such as "Several Opinions on Accelerating the Development of Services," "Several Opinions on Promoting the Comprehensive Urban-Rural Reform and Development of Chongqing," and "Opinions of the Chongqing Municipal People's Government on Developing Services," clearly defining the requirement for actively developing production services. According to the head of the Industrial Park Department of the Municipal Economic Commission, the commission conducted special research on areas within China that had better-developed production services, such as Shanghai and Shenzhen, starting in the second half of 2007. By the end of that year, they reached a consensus with the Jiangbei District Government to initiate the construction of the western region's first production service cluster area in Jiangbei District. The Jiangbei District Government attached great importance to this project and specifically established a project construction leading group headed by the main leaders of the district government, effectively advancing the construction process. According to the planning of the Jiangbei District Government, the production service cluster area will take the Chongqing Industrial Service Port as its core, initially incorporating 1 million square feet of commercial office buildings into the Guanyinqiao business district. Through the aggregation, radiation, and driving effects of the Chongqing Industrial Service Port, a large number of well-known institutions in the domestic and international production service fields will be introduced to settle in the Guanyinqiao business district, constructing the most influential modern high-end service base in the city and one of the most important service outsourcing bases in the west.

Comprehensive Industrial Services, Innovative Service Methods

To build the Chongqing Industrial Service Port into a uniquely characteristic modern industrial service base domestically, after over a year of exploration and preparation, the builders of the Chongqing Industrial Service Port boldly innovated in functional layout, service platforms, service methods, and management mechanisms, making it more adaptable to the needs of industrial enterprises and service institutions under new circumstances, truly becoming a bridge and link between the supply and demand sides of production services. The second floor of the Chongqing Industrial Service Port is the production factor information market and business salon, the third floor is the production service supermarket, the fourth floor is the exhibition hall and meeting activity area of the Chongqing Industrial Park Recruitment Center, and the fifth to sixth floors are business office areas. The entire building possesses various functions such as display, negotiation, meetings, and offices, fully satisfying the communication and connection needs of industrial enterprises and service institutions. In addition to relatively complete hardware facilities, the Chongqing Industrial Service Port has also made great efforts in software construction. The person in charge of the Chongqing Industrial Park Recruitment Service Center, responsible for the operation of the Chongqing Industrial Service Port, introduced that the software construction of the Chongqing Industrial Service Port has three major characteristics: comprehensive service platforms, diverse service methods, and innovative management mechanisms.
