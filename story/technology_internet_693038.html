(1) **First, Time Control**

- Once investors enter the market for trading, they are constantly exposed to varying levels of risk. Additionally, as deferred delivery businesses, Silver TD and Gold TD incur certain deferred costs. Therefore, it is crucial for investors to control the timing of their entry into trades. The shorter the holding period, the smaller the risk; conversely, the longer the holding period, the greater the risk. To reduce risks, investors should aim to shorten their holding periods.

(2) **Second, Timely Stop-Loss and Position Control**

- In margin trading, position control is also very important for investors. Both Silver TD and Gold TD involve leverage, so even slight price fluctuations can lead to forced liquidation. Investors should implement timely stop-loss strategies and avoid blind operations. This is especially true for newcomers who lack experience, have insufficient market sensitivity, and are prone to being emotionally influenced by market fluctuations. Therefore, we recommend operating with a light position.
