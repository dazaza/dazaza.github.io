2011 Happy Girl Voice national singing area Weibo address - Impressions of Girls' Voice voting entrance

by edivista on 2011-04-02 18:54:59

(Weibo Navigation Network) The registration for the 2011 Happy China Happy Female Voice started on April 1st. Unlike previous years, Weibo has become a new highlight. According to official information:

The official Weibo of each sub-singing area will release the video of the impression female voice of each participant in the preliminary competition. From the start of the preliminary competition to 12 noon on the second day of its end, the participant whose Weibo is effectively forwarded the most times can directly enter the top 50 of that sub-singing area, with one participant in each singing area advancing directly to the top 50.

To participate in any 2011 Happy Female Voice Weibo activity, you must first register for four major Weibo accounts. Click on the corresponding image below to complete the registration:

According to the official website of Hunan TV's Happy Female Voice, in 2011, exclusive Weibo accounts will be set up for the participants on Sina Weibo, Tencent Weibo (QQ Weibo), Sohu Weibo, and NetEase Weibo, and the impression female voice of each participant will be promoted on the official Weibo of the 2011 Happy Female Voice.
