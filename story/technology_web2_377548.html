Meilishuo is a fashion shopping community buzzing with the thrill of bargain hunting. Fashionable young women who love online shopping and beauty gather here to share shopping links and exchange tips on dressing and online shopping. Essentially, it is a community that combines e-commerce and SNS. http://www.meilishuo.com/. Masar (www.masar.cn) is a tool for synchronized updates across multiple microblogs and SNS platforms based on API. By updating through the website interface, the system automatically pushes messages to each microblog and SNS site via API. It can also obtain the latest status information from various microblogs and SNS platforms in real time. http://www.masar.cn/. Beva aims at "discovering children's interests and talents, cultivating their hobbies and skills, and sharing the joy in their growth," as its business philosophy. It creates beloved cartoon characters for children, builds a cyber world where they can chase their dreams, and strives to become China's most contributing children's growth company. http://www.beva.com/. Duitang is a user-sharing shopping community. On Duitang, everyone shares and discovers great stuff together. Here, you can: 1. Discover treasures shared by others 2. Join treasure circles and find friends with similar tastes. http://www.duitang.com/. NetEase has launched NetEase Docs, which helps you quickly preview local and online documents online.
