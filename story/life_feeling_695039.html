Please give me another chance to love you

I can't forget your goodness, and I can't forget the hurt I've done to you. You always come to my mind when my heartbeat is calm, after one o'clock. What's wrong? Why can't I forget you? It's so annoying. It was me who ruthlessly broke up with you at that time, but in the end, I still can't let go. I'm suffering from a love that should have ended but hasn't. It's all my fault, I'm torturing myself. It was supposed to be a perfect ending, but in the end it turned into a tragedy... Alas. My promise made you believe in me, made you follow me unconditionally. But in the face of reality, I retreated and used my promise as an excuse to abandon you. So heartless, so cruel.

I regret it now. I realize my mistake and appreciate your value. Love isn't something that can be easily let go of. My repeated ruthlessness has made you lose faith in true love. I've made you numb, turning you into an "eclipse" without sunlight. It's so cold, I'm so cold. Can you still hold me? Can you still let me lean on you when I'm sad? It's too late now, there's no turning back. One misstep leads to eternal regret. I hate myself so much, I wish I could bury myself, disappear from your sight and the world, as if I never existed. Maybe then I would feel better.

So, Mr. Huatuo, do you have any regret pills?

Huatuo: Yes, but I only have one left. I won't give it to people like you anymore. Even if you take it, you will still forget your original promise, I won't give it to you. You will just repeat your mistakes!

He said: "No", No, I won't leave without getting the pill. I've endured so much torment, all I want is to go back to her. I will not betray her again.

Huatuo: Really? Can you really do it?

He: Yes, I swear by my life.

Huatuo: Alright, but this last pill is not ordinary. Unlike the previous ones, this pill is made from the 999 tears shed by couples after breaking up. Its power is immense. If you betray your promise, you will die the moment you separate again. Do you still want it?

He: ???

He: Yes!

After eating the "999 Drops of Love", he returned to her side. So happy, he lay beside her, gently stroking her long hair, a fresh scent filled his heart, so familiar, so warm.

She woke up: What are you doing? Haven't you had enough yet?

He: No, seeing you still asleep, I didn't want to wake you.

She: Have I been sleeping for a long time?

He: No, maybe you're just too tired.

She: Oh!

He: Are you hungry? What do you want me to cook for you?

She: Are you new here? Don't you know what I like to eat?

"Oh", haha, I'll go cook it, wait for me, it will be ready soon. Almost exposed...

He: "Here it comes. Your favorite instant noodles."

He leaned close to her face and asked: Has my cooking skill improved?

She: Yes, it has improved. Look, I've trained you into a great chef, ha ha.

He: Are you mocking me? Let me see how I deal with you.

She: I'm not! Dear husband. Hmm.

He: What else do you want, my lady? 'Your servant' will immediately prepare it for you.

She: Give me an apple.

He: Coming right up...

She: Haha, you've been busy making food for me since early morning. Have you eaten yet?

He: No problem, I'm willing to serve my lady.

She: Come, have some of our love dan dan noodles.

He: As you command, my lord, wu wu!

She: What's wrong? Did you choke? Here, drink some water!

He: No, my mouth is too small.

She: Haha...

They lived a sweet and happy life once more.

Can I be like them? How I wish I could go back to our past, back to the world that belongs to you and me. Can we go back?

I can't forget you because we truly loved each other. It's as if I was born for you. Without you, I don't know where to go. The train without a locomotive has lost its direction. I've gone off the tracks, waiting for you to come back and reconnect me. I yearn to return to the track that belongs to you and me and drive our happiness together. When you're tired, I'll push you; when I can't push anymore, you pull me. That's what I look forward to.

Please give me another chance to love you. I will love you well, very well. Dan
