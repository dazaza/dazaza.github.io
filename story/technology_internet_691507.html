Registration Guidelines [http://www.bangyuanedu.com]

2012 Annual Jiangsu Province Second-level Constructor [http://www.bangyuanedu.com/wzny.asp?id=1085] Professional Examination Registration Guidelines

Respected Candidates:

Hello, and welcome to the Suzhou Bangyuan Education [http://www.bangyuanedu.com] website!!!

Before registering, we suggest you carefully read the following content; otherwise, you may not be able to complete the online registration process properly.

I. Registration Process

First-time registrants - Fill out information online - Upload electronic photo (requirements detailed in Attachment 1) - Wait for photo review - After one working day, check the review results (input ID number and password into the original registration system to access Suzhou Human Resources Training [http://www.bangyuanedu.com], then check the review status) - Pay online - After payment is confirmed, print the registration form and attend on-site preliminary qualification review at the specified time (on-site preliminary qualification review locations are detailed in Attachment 2).

II. Time Arrangement

1. Online registration time: March 5, 2012, 9:00 AM – March 26, 2012, 4:00 PM;

2. Online payment time: March 5, 2012, 9:00 AM – March 26, 2012, 4:00 PM;

3. Information modification time: March 5, 2012, 9:00 AM – March 26, 2012, 3:00 PM;

4. On-site confirmation (preliminary qualification review) time: March 28, 2012 – April 1, 2012;

5. Online printing of admission tickets: Starting October 17, 2012 (Suzhou Bachelor's Degree Training [http://www.bangyuanedu.com]);

6. Examination time: October 27-28, 2012.

III. Materials Required for On-Site Confirmation by Candidates

(1) Original and photocopy of the corresponding academic degree certificate;

(2) Original and photocopy of personal ID card (or officer’s ID);

(3) Valid work experience proof (can be downloaded from the download center of the website);

(4) Online-downloaded and printed 2012 annual registration form (truthfully filled out and stamped with the seal of the employer);

(5) One recent 2-inch bareheaded photo (same as the uploaded electronic photo), to be attached to the designated position on the registration form.

Candidates who have previously participated in the exam (only referring to those whose level and major match the previous registration) do not need to submit additional certification materials on-site after successful online payment; they will have completed their registration.

Attachment 1: Photo Upload Requirements

1. The uploaded electronic photo must be a recent two-inch bareheaded identification photo, in JPG format; file size should not exceed 30K (width 158 pixels, height 210 pixels); it must be in color, with a white, red, or blue background; no white borders or tilts allowed.

2. When scanning photos, set the scanner resolution to 200 dpi.

3. If the uploaded photo is unclear or does not reflect the candidate’s features, it may affect the exam or post-exam certificate usage; the candidate is solely responsible for this.

4. Do not upload photos that are scanned from film or taken with a life camera or an electronic camera.

7. Photos uploaded online will be used for future certificates; the photo review requirements this year are strict, so please do not neglect the photo upload.

IV. Information Correction Methods

1. If the candidate’s online registration information has not been reviewed or was not approved, the candidate can modify the relevant information themselves.

2. If the candidate’s online registration information has been reviewed (but before payment), please download the attachment "Online Registration Information Modification Application Form", fill it out carefully and attach an ID card copy, then fax it to 0512-65109690 requesting the reviewer to fail the review, allowing the candidate to modify the relevant information and re-submit for review. Candidates should log back into the online registration system one hour after faxing for confirmation.

3. During on-site preliminary qualification review, all information modifications will not be accepted.

4. After the candidate's registration data has been submitted to the national authorities, all information modifications will not be accepted.

V. Special Reminder

The [registration point] selected by the candidate must be consistent with the [review point]; otherwise, the review point will reject the candidate’s review application. For example, candidates who select the registration point as [Changshu City] cannot go to the [City Area] review point for review. Candidates who select the wrong registration point can handle it according to the information correction method.

Old candidates refer only to those who have passed the qualification review in previous registrations, and if the current registration maintains the same level and major as before, simply verify the previous archive number during online registration, then proceed with online registration - uploading photos - online payment. Candidates who arbitrarily change levels and majors will bear the responsibility for any resulting disqualification of qualified subjects.

This year's registration still includes two portals: the second-level constructor professional qualification examination and the second-level constructor corresponding (additional item) examination. Please ensure you enter the correct portal during registration, and for specific requirements, please refer to the relevant notices for both examinations on the website.
