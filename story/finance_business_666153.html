E-commerce domain names encounter embarrassing situations: Netizens list eight hard-to-remember domain names. Recently, e-commerce domain names have become a hot topic of attention. The entry of 38 e-commerce websites into Taobao Mall's Tmall.com has drawn industry attention. Although e-commerce websites are experiencing good fortune, there are also "flaws" that cannot be ignored. Recently, netizens have compiled a ranking of the worst domestic e-commerce website domain names. These domains have caused many embarrassing situations for e-commerce sites due to their difficulty to remember!

Overseas enterprises' Chinese station domain names face embarrassing situations. When overseas companies enter the domestic market, they often choose domain names that fit local conditions, such as the famous Groupon China station Gaopeng.com. The value of this domain name is quite good, and it was rumored that the transaction price of this domain name reached 980,000 yuan. The results brought by Gaopeng choosing a phonetic domain name are evident. Unfortunately, Amazon China’s excellent network domain amazon.cn, although "amazon" and the phonetic spelling of Amazon "yamaxun" sound similar, many people still can't remember this "amazon.cn".

Vancl.com - Can't distinguish between Vancl and Vancle. Vanke independently established the "VANCL" brand domestically and has had a significant impact in the domestic e-commerce market. However, many new users don't know whether Vanke Chengpin is "vancl" or "vancle", so they often unconsciously type in the phonetic spelling "fanke". Fanke.com is not Vanke Chengpin's official website domain, resulting in much traffic going to fanke.com.

Yixun Network - icson and 51buy.com. Yixun Network's previous official website domain was icson.com. I couldn’t understand what it represented either—it wasn’t a phonetic domain name, nor was it an English one. It was a creative domain name that left its meaning unclear, which was somewhat of a failure. It seems that Yixun realized the shortcomings of the icson.com domain. Recently, it changed the domain to 51buy.com. At first glance, with "buy," it seems related to shopping. However, while others are shifting to phonetic domain names, you're still opting for a creative one.

Chuke Network - crucco.com Creative Domain Name. If there were a Chuke Network with the domain chuke.com online, no doubt, many people would unintentionally be directed to this website instead of crucco.com. Compared to crucco.com, this creative domain name is truly difficult to remember, even for experts who shake their heads and keep mixing up "crucc." Crucco.com, you need to learn from this lesson quickly.

Masamaso.com - Too unconventional. In today's society, if you dare to be unconventional and unique, fame won't be far away. However, whether it will be "fragrant for a thousand years" or "stink for ten thousand years" remains to be seen. Among e-commerce websites, the unconventional domain name for men's clothing brand Masamaso is masamaso.com. Personally, I wouldn't praise this domain name. The Chinese name "Masamaso" sounds like a tongue twister? Is the website domain "masamasa.com" or "masamaso.com"? Can't tell them apart.

Currently, there are many more hard-to-remember domain names among e-commerce websites, such as Moonbasa.com. "Moonbasa" is a well-named brand, dreamlike and beautiful, but remembering the domain Moonbasa.com is quite challenging—always forgetting whether it's "moon" or "meng." For Redbaby.com.cn, it's an English domain name. Translated directly, "redbaby" means "Red Baby," correct. But why choose a .com.cn suffix? Wouldn't using just .cn be simpler? And then there's Vcotton.com, another young e-commerce website with a confusing domain name.

Winning the hearts of the people means winning the world. Websites on the Internet must also follow this principle. A good domain name can be easily remembered and instantly gain user recognition, showing the influence a domain name can represent. This is also the key for e-commerce websites to "win the hearts of the people." With the current fierce competition in the e-commerce industry, the competitive battlefield has shifted from products and service quality to domain names. The role of domain names in the development of e-commerce websites is becoming more prominent, making the choice of a good domain name an indispensable step for e-commerce websites.
