Procedures:

1. The ski resort uses an all-in-one card system for consumption, including skiing (boots, boards, poles), ski suits (jackets, pants), gloves, lockers (keys), snow circles, catering, hiring coaches, etc.

2. Guests need to register for card collection at the cashier, pay a deposit of 200 yuan per person for ski equipment and consumption. Each guest will receive one consumption card, which can be used to collect and return ski equipment and make other purchases.

3. When guests use the consumption card, fees are automatically deducted from the card. If the balance is insufficient, please recharge at the cashier to continue consumption.

4. A consumption card can only rent equipment once. After returning, it cannot be rented again.

5. Before checking out, guests must return all rented equipment. If any equipment is not returned, checkout will not be allowed.

6. Guests must use their own consumption card to collect and return equipment, mixing cards is not allowed. Otherwise, settlement issues or discrepancies in consumption amounts may occur, for which the guest is responsible.

7. Skiing starts timing when the first piece of ski equipment (ski boots or board) is rented, ending when the last piece is returned. The ski resort offers a free 15-minute consumption time, and the cost is automatically calculated based on the recorded consumption time on the consumption card. After exceeding one billing unit, the next billing unit is charged.

8. Ski suits and lockers do not charge if returned within 5 minutes of rental. After 5 minutes, fees are automatically deducted.

9. Hiring a coach starts timing 5 minutes after the designated coach.

10. Guests who damage ski resort equipment must compensate according to the ski resort's compensation rules.

11. Lost cards must be replaced. After replacement, all information from the original card is transferred to the new card, with a replacement fee of 20 yuan. Please keep your card safe.
