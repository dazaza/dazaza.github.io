How to talk so kids will listen, and how to listen so kids will talk

by creat on 2007-12-14 09:24:50

The experience of reading this book will be a process of learning the skills of love and receiving training in accepting love. It gives you the key to enter the inner world of children and guides you to personally experience the feelings inside a child's heart. Through a series of practical and effective methods, it helps you and your child establish a platform for joyful communication, making the child extraordinarily willing to cooperate with their parents. By mastering the skills of "how to say?" and "how to listen?", you can open up the child's inner world and achieve wonderful communication with them, allowing the child to develop mentally and physically in a healthy way under your guidance.
