Generally speaking: Older children: XL: 58-60CM, Middle children: XL: 50CM, Small-middle children: XL: 45CM, Smaller children: XL: 35CM. How to choose a well-fitting piece of children's clothing? A convenient method is to let the child try it on directly or take precise measurements. However, if the child is not present or cannot be measured, knowing the child's height can still help select appropriate clothing through calculations.

Generally, the head accounts for 20% of the total height, and the body height accounts for 80%. When choosing clothes for children, the body height is usually used as a standard.

The length of children's shorts is about 30% of the body height;

The length of children's shirts is about 50% of the body height;

The length of children's long pants is about 75% of the body height (Wendy's House prospect);

The length of children's jackets is about 49% of the body height;

The length of children's suits is about 53% of the body height;

The length of children's long coats is about 70% of the body height;

The length of girls' dresses is about 78% of the body height.

For example, a girl who is 100 centimeters tall has a body height of 80% of 100 centimeters, which is approximately 80 centimeters. If selecting a dress, the length would be 70% of 80 centimeters, which is 56 centimeters long (Wendy's House). If selecting a shirt, the length would be 50% of 80 centimeters, which is 40 centimeters. If you are out shopping for your child, you might consider calculating using this formula (Wendy's House business opportunity) to choose a satisfactory and well-fitting outfit for your child.
