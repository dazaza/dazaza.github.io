On February 16, Beijing time, Shandong Luneng's (Weibo) recruitment finally came into focus. Today, multiple Danish media outlets, including the Danish National Television, reported on the transfer of Brazilian striker Gilberto Machena from the Danish Superliga team Horsens to Luneng. The report revealed that Machena's transfer fee reached 1 million euros, with a three-year contract worth 4 million USD in salary plus bonuses.

Recently, the Danish media "sporten" first reported that Machena would join a team in Guangzhou, China. However, it turned out he was set to transfer to Shandong Luneng. Today, the Danish media "EkstraBladet.dk" published an article titled "Gilberto Joins Shandong Luneng Taishan" about this transfer news. The article mentioned that AC Horsens traded Brazilian forward Gilberto Machena to Shandong Luneng Taishan in China. It briefly introduced the club: located in Jinan, Shandong Province, with nearly 7 million residents, it is one of the larger cities in eastern Shandong Province. Shandong Luneng is one of the most successful clubs in Chinese history, having won numerous national championships. In recent seasons, they have experienced ups and downs, finishing fifth last season. Machena has already traveled with AC Horsens administrative official Gaspar Hansen to Valencia, where Shandong Luneng is training, to sign the transfer contract. The contract is expected to be announced Thursday evening or Friday, with a term of 2-3 years. However, this media outlet did not disclose the specific transfer fee.

Another Danish media outlet "BOLD.DK" reported that according to the national television channel TV2, the top scorer of the Danish league, Gilberto Machena, has signed a high-paying contract with Shandong Luneng in Spain after a week of negotiations. Originally, Machena had received interest from Guangzhou Evergrande (Weibo), but he will now join Shandong Luneng instead. AC Horsens will receive a transfer fee of 1 million euros, and Luneng will offer Machena a salary of approximately 4 million USD over three years, along with bonus income.

Gilberto Machena was born on April 1, 1984, and stands at 1.73 meters tall. In the 2005 season, Machena transferred for free to Holbæk in Denmark, scoring 16 goals in 22 matches. In 2006, he returned to Horsens from Holbæk. Over 178 appearances for Horsens, he scored 81 goals, achieving an average goal efficiency of nearly 0.5 per game, making him a highly efficient striker. In the 2011/2012 Danish Superliga, Machena has already scored 11 goals in 18 games, currently leading the scoring chart. Due to his outstanding performance in the Danish Superliga, Machena once attracted the attention of Bundesliga teams (Weibo), though the move ultimately did not happen. Machena is a speed-oriented player with excellent technique and quickness.

Another Danish media outlet "tipsbladet.dk" also reported on Machena's move to Luneng, extensively introducing the Shandong Luneng team and praising this Chinese powerhouse and its renowned current coach Ten Kate. According to the evaluation by Transfermarkt Germany, Machena's value is around 2 million euros. Shandong Luneng acquiring this Brazilian striker at half the price represents a cost-effective deal.
