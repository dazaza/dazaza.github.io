The proportion of Guangdong women buying houses is increasing.

by comeccoo81 on 2010-05-27 17:28:27

In today's world where there are more and more powerful women, it is obviously out of date to think that men are the only ones who can make decisions about buying a house. Reporters have noticed that the proportion of women buying houses in Zhaoqing, Guangdong has been increasing year by year. They see the characteristics of local women's housing purchase areas and living habits. For example, the second-hand houses in Zone Three of Meteor Garden.

Moonlight Tribe: Romantic Love Before Marriage

The term "moonlight tribe" refers to women before marriage because of the romance during the passionate courtship period. According to local surveys, moonlight tribe women account for 18% of all female homebuyers locally. The age of the moonlight tribe is mostly post-80s, generally senior white-collar workers who have worked in enterprises or institutions for over a year. Among the moonlight tribe, the reason for buying a house is experiencing the troubles of moving. They believe they are young and do not want to get married yet, but they want to have their own space. Daily living expenses and rental fees already take up most of the monthly salary of moonlight tribe women. Therefore, their home purchases mainly rely on parental support for the down payment, treating previous rental fees as monthly mortgage payments.

Baby Tribe: Welcoming the Birth of a Baby

Surveys show that the proportion of baby tribe women (married women with children) purchasing homes accounts for around 42% of all female homebuyers locally.

These homebuying women have limited working years after getting married and start learning to be meticulous. They have their own requirements for housing for the happiness of the next generation. The baby tribe believes that the reason for buying a house is to welcome the birth of a baby and expand their living space.

Investment-Oriented: Pursuing Stable Returns

Investment-oriented women refer to middle-aged women. Local statistics show that they account for approximately 16% of all female homebuyers. Investment-oriented individuals have a certain economic foundation and thus consider real estate trading as their main investment direction.

They believe that doing other businesses is too tiring, or stock trading involves high risks, while property investment offers high and stable returns. In terms of home purchasing methods, they either pay in full or take out small loans, repaying early when there is cash flow available.

The goal of investment-oriented homebuyers is to reduce investment risks and pursue medium-to-long-term, stable investment returns.

Retirement-Oriented: Seeking Peaceful Later Years

According to local statistics, elderly retirees account for 24% of female homebuyers. After spending most of their lives in bustling cities, at retirement age, they hope to choose a peaceful place with good environment, fresh air, and convenient transportation to enjoy their later years.

The reason for retirement-oriented homebuyers to purchase houses is simple: enjoying a peaceful later life and desiring a living environment beneficial to physical and mental health. Their home purchasing method is always full payment. When choosing a house, they prefer places with excellent air quality, fewer residents, relatively convenient transportation, and very smooth access to hospitals.
