Beijing Sport University's Master's Degree Program in Sports Operation Management for working professionals offers dual certificates.

by shuoshi_b on 2011-08-17 17:17:53

Beijing Sport University and China Youth Education have formed a strong alliance to jointly establish a part-time graduate course program in sports management, aimed at cultivating high-level talent in sports management in China. The purpose of this project is "to provide students with cutting-edge systematic knowledge and practical experience in international sports management, and to cultivate a group of urgently needed sports management talents for the development of China's sports industry and major national sporting events."

This project has received strong support from the Ministry of Education, Beijing Organizing Committee for the Olympic Games, General Administration of Sport of China, and local sports bureaus in terms of content and format.

1. Program Name

Sports Humanities and Social Sciences (Sports Management Direction)

2. Training Objectives

With an international perspective, through systematic and professional learning, students will master modern sports management knowledge, understand advanced concepts and operational models of the sports industry, and become visionary, talented, and culturally sophisticated sports elites.

3. Teaching Features

Systematic lectures, case analysis, seminars, thematic salons, skills training, visits

4. Faculty Strengths

Senior professors from Beijing Sport University, Peking University, Renmin University of China, Tsinghua University, China University of Political Science and Law; government officials from the General Administration of Sport of China, the Olympic Committee, and renowned experts in the domestic and international sports industry.

5. Target Audience

National and local sports system management cadres;

Athletes and coaches who have contributed to the sports cause;

Main staff involved in organizing and preparing for major national sporting events;

Senior management personnel of sports clubs and sports goods enterprises;

Individuals from the sports education field and non-sports professionals aspiring to enter the sports industry.

6. Enrollment Requirements

Graduates with a bachelor’s degree; individuals with a college diploma and more than two years of work experience.

7. Core Courses

- Management

- Sports and Media

- Dialectics of Nature

- Sports Event Management

- Venue Management

- Sports Marketing

- Applied Research Methods

- Sports Industry Operation Management

- Sports and Law

- Human Resource Management

- Capital Operation and Management

- Series Lectures on Sports Business Management

8. Teaching Method and Location

A combination of face-to-face instruction and correspondence courses, weekend classes and winter/summer vacation classes are offered in Beijing.

9. Duration and Fee Standards

Two-year program. Students pay a one-time tuition fee of 19,800 yuan, textbook fees of 700 yuan, and an enrollment fee of 100 yuan, after which they are registered. According to relevant regulations, after the start of classes, those who cannot continue due to personal reasons will be considered to have automatically abandoned their studies, and no refunds will be given.

10. Certificate Issuance

Those who complete all coursework and pass the exams will submit a research report and receive a graduation certificate equivalent to a Master's degree in Sports Business Management from Beijing Sport University.

11. Degree Application

Graduates with a bachelor’s degree and a minimum of three years of work experience (including three years), and who have obtained the "Graduate Course Completion Certificate in Sports Business Management," can apply for a Master's degree from Beijing Sport University by passing relevant exams. After writing and defending a thesis, they will receive a Master's degree from Beijing Sport University. Fees during this stage will follow the regulations set by the Graduate School of Beijing Sport University.

12. Qualification Certification

To effectively apply the successful experiences and methods of project management in the operation of sports projects and to provide students with more employment opportunities, there will be special arrangements for training and exams for national professional qualification project managers. Those who pass the exam will receive a certificate issued by the Ministry of Labor and Social Security of the People's Republic of China. Additional fees apply.

13. Study Tours

Graduates may participate in study tours to the University of California, Los Angeles (UCLA) and New York University in the United States, with costs borne by the participants.

14. Enrollment Process

(1) Fill out the part-time graduate student application form; submit original and copies of your ID card, diploma, and degree certificate.

(2) After qualification review, qualified applicants will be admitted based on enrollment order and available spots, and a letter of admission will be issued.

(3) Submit two 2-inch and three 1-inch recent identical color photos.
