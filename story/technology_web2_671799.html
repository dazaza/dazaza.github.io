School principal beats parent with steel pipe and claims self-defense

by zsh0827601 on 2012-02-22 13:46:33

Our staff reporter (intern reporter Zhang Jian, reporter Wang Penghao) Yesterday morning, in the Baolu School of Nanhuoda Village, Zhangjiawan Town, Tongzhou District, a conflict occurred when parents discussed the school bus pick-up issue with the school's responsible person. During the conflict, the principal injured two parents with a steel pipe. The principal himself claimed that it was for the reason of protecting students and family members that he used the steel pipe to injure one person. Currently, the police are further handling this matter.

Parents said the principal injured people with a steel pipe. Ms. Zhang introduced that her two children study at Baolu School in Nanhuoda Village. They are picked up by the school bus every day. Yesterday early morning she sent the two children to the pick-up location. But until around 7:20 am, the school bus still did not arrive, so Ms. Zhang could only ride her bike to send the children to school herself.

After arriving at the school, Ms. Zhang found the school's responsible person Mr. Gao and asked why the school bus didn't come to pick up the children, but his attitude was not good. This made Ms. Zhang very unhappy, and their discussion quickly turned into an argument, which led to physical conflict. During this period, Ms. Zhang called her husband Mr. Zeng.

Subsequently, Mr. Zeng and another male relative came to the school and continued to discuss the matter. "At that time, we were definitely upset, and with the previous conflict, both sides argued again," Mr. Zeng said. The argument escalated into physical conflict again, but unexpectedly, the school principal Mr. Gao hit his head with a steel pipe, and the accompanying male relative's head was also hit. Blood flowed from their heads. Other people in the school quickly stepped in to separate the two parties.

The principal claimed he acted after being beaten first. At around 12:00 pm yesterday, Mr. Zeng and a relative were still receiving treatment at Luhe Hospital, where their heads had been wrapped in bandages. The hospital doctor stated that both of their heads had wounds, one requiring three stitches, the other one stitch.

Baolu School Principal Mr. Gao and his wife also came to Luhe Hospital. Mr. Gao claimed that during the conflict, he and his wife were also injured. Regarding the issue of the school bus not picking up the children, after his inquiry to the school bus driver, yesterday morning the school bus went to pick up the children according to the normal schedule, but at the agreed time and place, they did not see the two children from Mr. Zeng's family. After the children's mother Ms. Zhang sent them to the school, she questioned him about why they didn't go to pick up the children. He prepared to calmly discuss this matter with Ms. Zhang, but her emotions were agitated, leading to arguments and conflicts.

Subsequently, Mr. Zeng and another male relative came to the school, and once began attacking people indiscriminately, even threatening to smash the school and damaged some glass windows of the houses. Another conflict arose between the two sides, and someone from Mr. Zeng's side also knocked him down. Mr. Gao expressed that for the purpose of protecting the children, he fetched a steel pipe used for security. In the conflict, one person from the other side hit his lower back with a stick, and he retaliated with the steel pipe, hitting the other party on the head. This conflict was subsequently separated by school staff, but he was unaware of how the other parent's head was injured.

Mr. Zeng stated that they did not attack people indiscriminately or damage the glass. The situation was chaotic at the time, and the accompanying male relative indeed hit someone from Mr. Gao's family in the melee.

Local police have already intervened in the investigation.

Some staff members of the school confirmed that there was a conflict between parents and the principal in the morning. It was class time then, and someone separated the two parties, but they were not clear about the specific circumstances of the incident.

In the afternoon, the staff of the Legal Safety Department of the Tongzhou District Education Commission stated that Baolu School is a self-run school in the township. According to local principles, it should be managed by the education department of Zhangjiawan Town Government. The Tongzhou police confirmed that this incident did indeed occur, and someone was injured in this conflict. After receiving the alarm, they went to the scene for disposal. Currently, they are further handling this matter.
