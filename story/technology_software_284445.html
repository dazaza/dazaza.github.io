Home cleaning in Qingdao is similar to project cleaning. Carpet cleaning in Qingdao, and large exhaust fan duct cleaning, are also due to the fact that construction often leaves behind a lot of garbage and dirt. Various floor stones, cement slurry blocks, paint, glass glue, water stains, rust marks, etc., will be left on the walls. All these must be cleaned during the cleaning process. Therefore, it is the hardest, most complex, and most labor-intensive work. The quality of the cleaning project directly affects the quality and level of future housekeeping work. Hence, doing a good job in cleaning is extremely important.

The service process of Qingdao Liangxin Cleaning Company is as follows:

1. First, clear the decoration waste left at the site;

2. Conduct comprehensive dusting from top to bottom;

3. Clean the glass: First, wipe the glass frame clean with a towel, then use a water applicator dipped in diluted glass water solution, and evenly apply it on the glass from top to bottom. Use a scraper to thoroughly remove any stubborn stains, repeat the above steps, and finally scrape the glass clean from top to bottom with a squeegee. Wipe the water marks left on the frame with a dry towel, and use a chamois leather to wipe off the water marks on the glass.

4. Bathroom: Adhere to the principle of starting from the top and moving down. First, identify the material of the bathroom ceiling - whether it's PVC, aluminum-plastic board, or paint, and then use different cleaning methods according to different materials; Clean the bathroom walls with a cleaning ball or a brush, focusing on cleaning the gaps between tiles and removing adhesive marks, paint spots, and cement stains on the tile surface; Clean the bathroom fixtures with a towel, and use stainless steel cleaner to clean various faucets and pipes; Finally, use a floor washer for the last cleaning of the floor, especially the edges and corners. For areas where the floor washer cannot reach, use a cleaning ball and a blade for targeted stain removal and removal of cement stains. After checking that nothing is missed, wipe the faucets and other pipe components again with a dry towel.

5. Kitchen: Follow the same procedure as above. Note: Since there are more stainless steel components in the kitchen, they should be the focus of cleaning.

6. Bedroom and living room: Use a duster or vacuum cleaner for dust removal on the walls, and wipe lamps, switch boxes, smoke outlets, air conditioner outlets, and exhaust vents.

7. Doors and frames: Identify the material of the door. Dilute professional cleaning agents and use a towel to wipe them. The procedure is also from top to bottom. Fold the towel into a square and start wiping from the top of the door from left to right without missing anything. Areas with adhesive stains can be treated with a degumming agent; The procedure for the frame is the same as for the door. It is essential to ensure no omissions or blind spots.

8. Floor cleaning: After all the above tasks are completed, it’s time to clean the floor. Floors also need to be divided by material - whether they are wooden floors, tiles, or stones. After identifying the type, choose a dedicated cleaning agent, dilute it, and start cleaning. Floor adhesive stains can be removed with a blade, and stubborn ones can be handled with a degumming agent. After the final step is completed, the team leader should conduct a full inspection, confirm that nothing is left behind, and then withdraw from the site.

9. Baseboard: Wipe with a towel, use a blade to remove various adhesive marks and paint spots.
