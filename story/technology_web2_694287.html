Wang Haibing doesn't rely on his father's influence, but on himself!

by chongwuyin5r on 2012-03-07 06:00:59

On the evening of October 10, Wang Haibing, an alumnus of Huazhong University of Science and Technology (HUST), returned to his alma mater to give a lecture about his personal growth journey. Wang Haibing, who led his company to list on the New York Stock Exchange in just four years of entrepreneurship, was born into a rural family in the former Chaohu City of Anhui Province. Due to poverty, he was sent to live with his grandmother in Jiangxi during junior high school. His mother's greatest wish was for him to attend a teacher training college. However, through his own efforts, he first entered a key high school and then achieved excellent results to enroll at Huazhong University of Science and Technology (formerly known as Huazhong Institute of Technology). Eventually, he became a "star" among HUST's young entrepreneur alumni.

When talking about his success, Wang Haibing clearly pointed out: "I don't rely on my father's influence; I rely on myself! My success comes from my own efforts, intelligence, diligence, and perseverance!"

The most important significance of university is itself.

"Generally speaking, university is the first environment where students have autonomy and a voice. You can encounter many opportunities and experience various life stories, so there is no need to approach university with too much purpose, such as planning to take postgraduate exams or study abroad right from the start of your freshman year. The most important meaning of university is the experience of being there."

Wang Haibing used his personal experiences to illustrate this point. As a student of the Electrical Engineering Department, he enthusiastically joined the school newspaper reporter group, taking on the tasks of interviewing and writing. Through continuous effort, he performed better than many of his peers from journalism and Chinese literature departments and eventually became the team leader. With a desire to achieve live text and image broadcasts for the school anniversary, he and his team explored the creation of the Huazhong University News website, which laid the foundation for the "Ice Rock Workshop." Upon graduation, due to a teacher's suggestion that he should stay in school longer, he pursued a master's degree in computer science, laying the groundwork for his future career in the internet industry. It was through these constant attempts and seizing opportunities that he eventually found the direction suitable for himself.

However, while living "recklessly," he maintained a rational attitude of doing every task to the best of his ability. He said, "When you do what you are supposed to do now to the best of your ability, you will also receive rich rewards." After a bonfire party organized by the reporter group ended, he alone cleaned the garbage blocking the toilet drainage pipe despite being scolded by security guards. To create the news website, he spent the entire seven-day May Day holiday in the school computer lab, working day and night with those monotonous programs.

"Guts" are the most important.

In this process of finding a suitable career, Wang Haibing emphasized that "guts" are very important. Only by having the courage to challenge, compete, and take risks will opportunities not slip away.

"When I proposed the promotion plan for 'QQ Pet' at Tencent, my boss asked me to guarantee an income target of 2 million yuan within six months. Despite not having full confidence, I boldly made the commitment. Later, 'QQ Pet' earned 2 million yuan in its first month of market promotion, and the profits grew even larger afterward." He pointed out that the most terrifying thing is to scare oneself before even trying, and the most precious quality is the spirit and courage to move forward when nothing is foreseeable.

With extraordinary courage, the young Wang Haibing has already had three "entrepreneurial" experiences: founding the "Ice Rock Workshop" in university, developing "QQ Pet" at Tencent, and establishing the Taomi company. He encouraged students not to mystify entrepreneurship but to dare to explore: "Entrepreneurship means walking a path that others haven't walked before. It is actually a profession like being a civil servant or an engineer. Only by constantly exploring and finding the most suitable career for yourself is the first step towards success."

With extraordinary "guts" and a spirit of moving forward, Wang Haibing's entrepreneurial journey achieved great success: becoming the "father of QQ Pet" within half a year at Tencent, and successfully listing the Shanghai Taomi Network Technology Co., Ltd. on the NYSE within four years of its establishment.

Practicing social responsibility in business competition

"People work not only for money but more importantly to fulfill the responsibilities society assigns to us." At the end of the lecture, when discussing the relationship between learning and society, Wang Haibing expressed deep feelings. He believed that society provides us with many valuable resources and growth experiences, whether successful or failed. When we have learned enough, we should take on our due social responsibilities and give back to society.

Taomi Network has a well-known concept: "Mom feels reassured, children feel happy." Its vision is to become the most beloved children's entertainment company by Chinese families, creating happiness and dreams for Chinese children. Wang Haibing introduced that children have a natural love for play and freedom. By developing games, books, films, and TV shows suitable for children, we understand and influence their psychology, thereby achieving satisfaction for children, families, and society. This is our simplest concept and way of giving back to society.

Facing various doubts in society, such as "only thinking about making money from children" or "making children addicted to online games and affecting their studies," Wang Haibing hit the nail on the head: "Our company can survive under the supervision of the entire internet system, and many of our products quickly become things that many children like and parents trust, proving that what we do is beneficial to families and society. Of course, besides having certain social value and user value, it must also have investment value; otherwise, it's not called business but charity."

Brief introduction of Wang Haibing:

Wang Haibing, male, born in 1980, undergraduate student of the Electrical Engineering Department of Huazhong University of Science and Technology in 1997, graduated with a master's degree in Computer Science in 2003. During his time at school, he founded the Ice Rock Workshop and served as the 21st team leader of the reporter group. He is currently the CEO of Shanghai Taomi Network Technology Co., Ltd.

In 2003, he started working at Tencent, independently designing and promoting QQ Pet, which became widely popular, earning him the title of "Father of QQ Pet." In October 2007, he started his own business and founded Shanghai Taomi Network Technology Co., Ltd., serving as the company's CEO. The company owns five children's SNS games: Sai'er Hao, Haqi Town, Xiaohuaxian, Mole Farm, and Dawanguo. In the 2008-2009 Baidu Storm Rankings, "Mole Farm" won the title of Best Web Game of the Year. In 2011, Shanghai Taomi Network Technology Co., Ltd. was listed in the United States, with a company market value reaching 300 million US dollars.
