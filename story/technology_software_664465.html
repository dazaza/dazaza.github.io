by d8eks7dj on 2012-02-13 15:22:32

Performance advantages and application scope of xenon lamp weathering test chamber: The xenon lamp weathering test chamber has six environmental factors including sunlight, rain spray, humidity, condensation, and military standard 150, which can be independently adjusted. It uses a xenon lamp as the light source, which has a spectral distribution similar to that of sunlight, so the xenon lamp is used to simulate sunlight. At the same time, it provides a wide range of temperature and humidity adjustments. The rain spray adopts an independent circulation system, so harmful gas substances can be added to the water supply source when needed. By changing the cooling water temperature of the sample rack, the amount of condensation on the sample surface and the sample surface temperature can be adjusted. Therefore, the xenon lamp weathering test chamber can perform multi-factor weather combination tests.

Climate conditions have a significant impact on the aging of organic materials, pigment fading, and metal corrosion. Therefore, aging tests are increasingly valued by industries such as rubber, plastics, paints, petrochemicals, automobiles, and textiles. There are many climate environmental factors, such as solar radiation, temperature, humidity, rain, condensation; harmful gases, dust, etc. Evaluating material performance through a single environmental factor test is not comprehensive enough, often requiring multi-factor combination tests to simulate natural conditions as closely as possible for more accurate evaluations.

Although outdoor exposure tests offer better authenticity, the climatic conditions vary greatly in different regions and seasons. Therefore, the importance of the test is relatively low. Moreover, with the continuous improvement of sample material quality, the cycle of exposure tests becomes longer and longer, unable to meet the needs of constantly growing scientific research and production.
