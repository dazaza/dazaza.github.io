Innocent man abducted for 40 hours, saved by secretly sending text message to his wife

by xue94fwsh on 2012-02-27 12:12:37

Currently, the three criminal suspects have been placed under criminal detention. Ten minutes later, Liu was spotted being escorted by two young men near the station. The policemen immediately apprehended the two criminal suspects. Through further on-site investigation, another suspect was captured at a certain inn in Zhuyeshan Third Village, Jiang'an District.

An innocent man was held hostage for 40 hours and was rescued after secretly sending a text message to his wife. Liu works at a bar in Hankou's riverside area. No matter how much time has passed, he still remembers... On the night of the 11th, he didn't return home. His wife called him but there was no answer. A man met with a car accident and had a 12-centimeter long tree branch pierced into his eye. On the morning of the 13th, Liu's wife suddenly received a call from her husband asking her to quickly deliver ten thousand yuan to Dazhi Road in Jiang'an District for urgent use. When she asked where her husband was, the call was disconnected. Liu's wife immediately suspected that her husband was in danger and reported it to the water police. Sure enough, she then received a secretly sent text message from her husband saying he was kidnapped.

According to reports (reporter: Wan Qin, correspondent: Yang Fei), a gambler owed money and went missing, prompting the creditor to kidnap his friend and illegally detain him for 40 hours. The victim secretly sent a text message to his wife, and the water police, upon receiving the news, rescued the victim and arrested the three suspects.

After being rescued, Liu revealed that at around 11 PM on the 11th, he was suddenly forced into a small car by three strangers and taken to a certain inn in Zhuyeshan, Jiang'an District. Originally, his friend Wang owed the other party 50,000 yuan in gambling debts and disappeared when it was time to repay. Since the two were often together, the creditors found Liu to "assist" in debt collection. During the 40 hours at the inn, unable to contact Wang, Liu was repeatedly slapped and threatened with a knife by the three criminal suspects. Unable to bear the torture, Liu proposed that he would pay 10,000 yuan himself. Subsequently, Liu called and texted his wife, hinting for her to report the incident to the police.

A friend owing money and going missing affected the "innocent bystander". The water police formulated an on-site arrest plan and had Liu's wife call her husband, saying that the money would be delivered to Dazhi Road at 6 PM. At 6 PM on the 13th, 10 officers from the water police rushed to Dazhi Road to set up surveillance and had Liu's wife say that she would deliver the money to the light rail station at Dazhi Road.
