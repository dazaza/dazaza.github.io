Fei Mo's original 24-episode TV series screenplay "Promise" Episode 1 (3)

by qaswr378 on 2011-06-10 17:53:54

Chapter Eighteen: Outside of Yu Lanlan's house.

It is a bungalow. In 2009-2012, the Chinese combine harvester market speculation and investment prospects analysis report shows that the furniture arrangement appears to be relatively low-end and outdated.

Yu Mother, a woman in her sixties, is struggling to stand in front of a cabinet, rummaging through the drawers inside in a disordered manner. Suddenly, she closes her eyes, clutches her chest, and her legs feel weak. Instinctively, she grabs the cabinet with another hand. The cabinet shakes, her hand slips off the cabinet, and her body stumbles, almost falling to the ground. Fortunately, there is a sofa armrest supporting her bottom, preventing her from falling immediately. Instead, she slowly slides down towards the ground.

The sound of keys turning in the lock, Yu Lanlan enters the room. She sees her mother falling to the ground, so she throws down the medicine bag, runs to her mother, and shouts: "Mom!"

Yu Mother painfully clutches her chest without any response.

Yu Lanlan quickly embraces her mother, panicking.

Yu Mother shakily extends one hand and mutters to Yu Lanlan: "Lanlan, give me the medicine..."

Yu Lanlan suddenly wakes up as if from a dream. She lets her mother lean against the sofa on the spot, then rushes to the door in three quick steps, picks up the rapid heart-saving pills, finds a cup, pours water, takes the medicine, and runs back to her mother's side to feed her the medicine.

When Yu Lanlan does these things, she seems nervous and clumsy.

With her eyes closed, Yu Mother swallows the medicine.

While drinking water, Yu Mother chokes and coughs. Yu Lanlan immediately supports her mother and pats her back.

Chapter Nineteen: Inside Yu Lanlan's house.

Yu Lanlan helps her mother lie down on the bed.

After taking the medicine, Yu Mother's painful expression gradually subsides.

Sitting at the bedside, Yu Lanlan worriedly asks her mother: "Mom, are you feeling better?"

Yu Mother still clutches her chest and softly says to Yu Lanlan: "I still feel my heart is blocked! Every time I think about him, I... I... feel chest tightness, can't even breathe..."

Yu Mother clutches her chest, breathing heavily.

Yu Lanlan hugs her mother and comforts her: "Mom, you have heart disease, don't get angry anymore. This person loves gambling and never changes, it's not something you just discovered now. When I was little, you two fought every day because of this! So many times, I dreamed that he lost money gambling, sold our furniture, or even sold me and my sister to human traffickers..."

Yu Mother's face shows pain. She sighs to the sky: "This cursed person! What kind of sin did I commit in this life to suffer such retribution!"

Yu Lanlan tightly hugs her mother and continues to comfort her: "Mom, aren't you going to divorce him? After the divorce, we won't have to live in fear like this anymore."

Yu Mother painfully shakes her head, looks blankly at the opposite pink wall, and mutters: "But he doesn't want to divorce! Since you came back these few days, he has disappeared completely! What should we do?!"

Yu Lanlan firmly tells her mother: "We must get divorced, otherwise, we will never have peace!"

Yu Mother turns around helplessly and says to Yu Lanlan: "Lanlan, he disappeared..."

Chapter Twenty: On the highway.

Among the moving vehicles, Li Yaming's car is easily recognizable because it is quite dirty.

Li Yaming drives not very fast, appearing cautious. He picks up his phone again and dials the number with "Lan" in it. We hear the same voice: "Sorry, the number you dialed cannot be connected temporarily, please try again later."

Li Yaming's expression is not pleasant, looking serious. He irritably taps the steering wheel, causing the car to sway left and right. The flowers and crystal box on the passenger seat almost fall off. Li Yaming notices this and immediately moves them further into the seat with his right hand.

At this moment, Li Yaming's phone rings.

He happily picks up the phone but upon seeing the number, his face shows disappointment.

Li Yaming answers the call: "Hello, Old Cao... Speak... A gallery owner wants to meet me... Alright... I'm on the road... Almost there (at this point, Li Yaming's phone makes two 'beep' sounds)... Hey, my phone is almost out of power... I'll call you when I arrive... That's all... Bye!"
