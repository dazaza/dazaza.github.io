The conversion rate is directly proportional to your execution!

1. Compile a list of information portal sites in various regions. This can actually be done in one or two hours, don't ask me how. Pay attention to the sites you usually follow, and you'll understand.

2. Each portal site has an information publishing platform. Don't search for it on search engines because the biggest one is the one on the local portal site.

3. Select several fields such as tutoring, recruitment, cooperation, and selling houses.

4. Post a tutoring-seeking message. There are so many tutors in each city, and most of them are college students. If you need traffic from poor college students, you can choose to use the method of finding tutors. Generally, a single piece of information can bring more than 100 IPs per day.

At the same time, you can post recruitment information, for example, China Unicom is hiring salespeople, part-time or full-time positions available, basic salary 2000 yuan, commission calculated separately. You can call 10011 for inquiries or send resumes to the email address, and set up automatic replies in the email.

6. If you want to meet some wealthy people and attract traffic from them, you can post: "I've just arrived in this city and am planning to invest 500,000~1,000,000 yuan. I'm looking for promising projects to cooperate on, requiring a minimum return rate of 10%. Friends who are interested can contact QQ: XXXX."

7. You can post super cheap house-selling information, such as houses at 1000 yuan per square meter, due to urgent family matters, I need to sell quickly, money received and the deal is done. Contact QQ: XXXX.

8. Persist in posting one province's worth of information per day, and within a month, you can cover all provinces. It takes only half an hour to post for one province, only posting on municipal-level portal sites. If you continue to post for 10 days, you can easily get tens of thousands of IPs per day.
