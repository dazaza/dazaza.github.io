Selection of high and low temperature test chamber, high and low temperature humidity test chamber, and high and low temperature impact test chamber!

Dear user, in order to ensure that the product you purchase is as economical and practical as possible, please confirm the following matters with our sales engineer when selecting the product:

1. The size of the working chamber (generally, the volume of the sample should not exceed 1/5 of the overall volume of the working chamber for the best and most realistic effect).

2. Temperature range, whether there is a requirement for temperature program change or rapid temperature change. If so, please provide the reference temperature change speed.

3. Humidity range, whether there is a requirement for low-temperature and low-humidity, and whether there is a requirement for humidity program change. If so, please provide the corresponding relationship diagram.

4. Whether there is a load, and whether the load generates heat. If it generates heat, please provide the reference power.

5. Selection of the cooling method for the refrigeration machine should be combined with the actual situation of the laboratory and the local area. If a test chamber with a smaller refrigeration system is selected, air cooling can be given priority; if a test chamber with a larger refrigeration system is selected, water cooling can be given priority under permissible conditions.

6. Whether there are requirements for the external dimensions, which are related to the entry and exit of the product in the laboratory, placement position, working conditions, and maintenance conditions, in order to avoid unnecessary trouble.

7. Whether there is a requirement for the carrying capacity of the test rack. If so, please provide the reference weight.

8. Power condition and installation power, whether they are available and have sufficient margin.

9. Whether there are optional functions, accessories, or spare parts required (the series of standard products of this unit can work normally under standard configuration and meet general test requirements).

10. Due to the limited space, the standards listed in the sample are general references. In actual application, they may be part of the standards or some clauses of some standards. Therefore, when placing an order, users should specify the specific standards and clauses as much as possible, and provide detailed temperature and humidity points or other special indicators.

11. Other related parameters and matters.

12. When selecting a model, users should choose standard models as much as possible to obtain the shortest delivery period and the most economical quality guarantee. However, due to the diversity of test requirements, we have the ability and willingness to manufacture various non-standard products or OEM products.
