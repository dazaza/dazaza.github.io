Developing e-commerce and carrying out informatization to the end

by now on 2009-02-13 12:25:16

With the development of the Internet, information globalization has increasingly become a major force driving economic development that cannot be ignored. Facing such a macro-environment, developing e-commerce, this low-investment and high-return business model, is undoubtedly an effective way for small and medium-sized enterprises to enhance their competitiveness. However, according to survey results, among more than 3 million industrial and commercial enterprises in Guangdong, the informatization of small and medium-sized enterprises is still at a stage of non-popularization. Only twenty percent of enterprises have fully realized the system construction of office automation, while more than forty percent of small and medium-sized enterprises have not established websites or do not regularly apply them. Therefore, the awareness of utilizing informatization for online marketing needs to be improved for small and medium-sized enterprises.

To carry out e-commerce, establishing a website is crucial. Users first need to choose a virtual host that suits them and is of good quality. Virtual hosts are service-oriented products. When users choose, they are not just making a simple selection, but more importantly, choosing a hosting provider that can offer excellent services. This better ensures the smooth operation of the website and facilitates communication between enterprises and customers. As is well known, when selecting a virtual host suitable for an enterprise, one can start by considering factors such as the size of the host space, functions, website speed, and product services. But with the continuous development of technology and increasing market demands, during the process of selecting a host, users should also pay attention to whether the supplier offers support for multiple sub-sites, multi-domain binding, and Chinese multi-level subdomain binding.
