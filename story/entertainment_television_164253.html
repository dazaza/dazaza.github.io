[Profile of the Witness]

Young detective Qin Tong accidentally injured his colleague Jianyu during a mission and left the police force overwhelmed with guilt. A few years later, Qin Tong has become an excellent lawyer, assigned to provide legal aid to suspect Han XinYu. Han XinYu is a hospital doctor accused of killing her adoptive father, a radical woman filled with a vengeful mindset. Beneath her cold and alluring exterior lies a heart that is despondent and icy towards the world.

Twenty years ago, her father, Professor Han Jian of Donghai City's Traditional Chinese Medicine College, fell to his death under mysterious circumstances. Before dying, he handed a broken spectacle leg to his only daughter, Han XinYu, and kept muttering the words "west." After losing her father, Han XinYu moved in with her mother who remarried, but soon her mother also passed away due to illness. The young Han XinYu endured not only the pain of losing both parents but also suffered sexual abuse from her stepfather Wei Zhiqiang. These misfortunes tormented her sensitive teenage heart. The flames of hatred grew in her consciousness, and her father's tragic death haunted her sleepless nights. Her tragic childhood transformed her into a vengeful angel with a heart as cold as ice. By chance, Han XinYu found a broken spectacle leg which proved that Liu Puming, who had adopted her, was the murderer of her father. Using her position as a doctor, she prepared a lethal injection for Liu Puming.

When Qin Tong met Han XinYu at the detention center, he realized that this seemingly frail girl had already given up on her will to survive. During their conversation, Han XinYu's despair and hatred for the world sparked Qin Tong's interest. Because of his past as a police officer, Qin Tong sensed that the matter was more complicated than it seemed. He decided to investigate this case full of doubts. In the course of his investigation, Qin Tong discovered that the drugs carefully prepared by Han XinYu were never injected into Liu Puming’s body. Therefore, it was very likely that someone else had killed Liu Puming. The determination and spirit of inquiry that Qin Tong once possessed as a policeman resurfaced. He believed that there was always a way to uncover the truth, find evidence, catch the real culprit, and prove Han XinYu's innocence. Thus, this idealistic and humane lawyer embarked on a challenging and perilous journey to solve an old case and warm Han XinYu's frozen heart, reigniting her desire to live.
