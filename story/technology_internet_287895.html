E Jing Net message (Chutian Golden Newspaper) special reporter Jing Wen report: Due to emotional entanglements, a 71-year-old grandfather from Jianli County shot and killed his 36-year-old cohabiting girlfriend Zhang. A neighbor who came to dissuade him was also injured by him. The Jianli police announced the day before yesterday that the suspect Wu committed suicide with a gun after fleeing for a day.

It is reported that Wu is from the fourth group of Changjiang Village, Hongcheng Township, Jianli County. He was once dealt with by the public security organs in his early years and is a released criminal. For many years, Wu has been doing odd jobs everywhere and has never had a fixed legitimate occupation. The deceased Zhang is from Xinshi Village, Rongcheng Town, Jianli County, and is unemployed. The two have lived together for six or seven years.

According to the investigation, Wu's left hand held a mobile phone, and his right hand held a仿six four pistol, with five bullets still in the gun. He had been dead for a long time. The police initially judged that this gun had been kept by Wu for many years, and its source is currently under investigation.

After the incident, nearby residents sent Zhang, who was shot in the head and chest, to the hospital for emergency treatment, but she eventually died due to ineffective rescue.

Considering that the suspect is too old, the Jianli police believed that he could not run too far, so they carried out a search nearby. That evening, Wu's eldest son told the police that his father had just called to say that he wanted to commit suicide. Early the next morning, the Jianli police received a call from a water plant saying that a male corpse was found on a ship at the riverside, and his physical characteristics were similar to those of the suspect. The policemen confirmed on site that it was exactly the suspect Wu.

At around 11 o'clock on the morning of November 28th, the two went to Mr. Li's house to play," Principal Yin happily told the reporter, during which Wu and Zhang had a quarrel over trivial matters of life, and the argument became more and more fierce. Zhang cited the lack of feelings between the two as the reason and said that she did not want to continue living with Wu. In a fit of anger, Wu pulled out a gun and shot Zhang. Mr. Li, who was washing his hands in the room, heard the gunshot outside and ran out. He saw Zhang lying on the ground covered in blood. At this moment, Wu was running out of the door with a handgun. Mr. Li immediately shouted: "What are you doing? You killed someone and want to run away?" Saying this, he chased after him. Wu then fired a shot at Mr. Li, hitting his left calf, and Wu took the opportunity to escape.
