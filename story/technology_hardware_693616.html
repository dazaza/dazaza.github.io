www.bailaohuihk.com| A man was detained for 10 days for lying about being robbed in order to embezzle 80,000 yuan of public funds.

by wan1jincai on 2012-03-06 17:04:32

China News Service, Yulin, March 2 (Yang Zhi Xiong, Li Yong, Tang Lin Lin) - Li Mou from Rong County in Yulin City, Guangxi, falsely reported to the police that he was robbed by two thugs with sticks in order to embezzle 80,000 yuan of public funds. After the case was uncovered, Li was administratively detained for 10 days by the police.

On March 2, the police introduced that at 16:30 on February 28, www.bailaohuihk.com, the Mingshan Police Station of the Yulin Public Security Bureau received an emergency call instruction that someone had been robbed near Mingshan Primary School. The on-duty police officers quickly arrived at the scene and saw a black women's motorcycle lying on the ground. The complainant, Li Mou (male, 26 years old, from Rong County, Yulin), claimed that he was collecting payment for a logistics company and had placed more than 80,000 yuan into a hanging bag inside the seat compartment of his motorcycle. When passing near Mingshan Primary School, he was hit by two men riding a 125C motorcycle wielding iron rods, who then robbed him of over 80,000 yuan in cash and hundreds of thousands of yuan worth of freight documents, according to Hong Kong Broadway Official Website.

The police officers found that the situation described by Li did not match the "injuries" on his body. The next day, after two hours of questioning, Li finally confessed to the police that he had reported a false case in order to embezzle the company's payment for himself. After the interrogation ended, Li signed the record to confirm, but his hands were shaking continuously.

According to relevant provisions of the "Law of the People's Republic of China on Public Security Administration Punishments," the Yulin police imposed an administrative detention of 10 days on Li and fined him 200 yuan.
