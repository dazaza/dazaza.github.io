Conditions for popularizing Chinese domain names are ripe, 2008 may trigger large-scale application

by now on 2008-01-04 09:57:24

Recently, the universal attention in the industry is the adjustment of domain name price by CNNIC. On one hand, the 1 yuan price for .CN domain name in English will continue until December 31, 2008; on the other hand, however, the price for Chinese domain name has increased from 280 yuan per year to 320 yuan. It is not difficult for us to see the significance from the adjustment of domain name price. There will be a breakthrough in the registration amount of the English .CN domain name in 2008; and the Chinese domain name will welcome its true spring time. Its popularization and application are inevitable tendency.

The Chinese domain name is the new generation domain name containing Chinese characters. Just like the English domain name, it is also the house number on the Internet. Since the formal introduction of the Chinese domain name in 2003, all sizes of domain name service providers have been promoting actively, but often they receive good comments but few buyers. Most registrants are concentrated in large enterprises and domain name investors. One is to protect their own network brands, and the other is to grab others' brands and then sell them out. Although the Chinese domain name has accumulated some registration amount, it is still limited in protection. Not many people realize its significant importance and use the Chinese domain name.

From the development in 2007: First, mainstream browsers represented by IE7.0, Firefox2.0, Netscape8.0 were upgraded successively, and full support for Chinese domain names was realized. Experts predict that by the end of 2007, more than 90% of global netizens can input Chinese domain names to visit websites directly when surfing online.

Second, according to relevant regulations of the State Council of China on government websites using "Chinese.CN", the activation rate of Chinese domain names of central ministries and commissions including the Ministry of Foreign Affairs and the Ministry of Finance and other government institutions as well as provincial government institutions in Shanghai, Sichuan, etc., has exceeded 90%.

Third, according to the Investigation on Internet Brand Marketing Status of China's Automobile Industry, 62.5% of the top 50 automobile manufacturing enterprises in terms of sales volume in China in the first half of 2007 had already registered or activated Chinese domain names.

Fourth, according to an investigation on how Chinese netizens access the Internet, more than 90% of interviewees said that they would rather access the Internet in their mother tongue, thinking that it is more convenient to visit websites with Chinese domain names. After four years of development, Chinese domain names have possessed the conditions for general use and promotion.

"The ducks know the river water is warming in spring" Of course, the most sensitive domain name service providers are the first to perceive the market potential and developing tendency of Chinese domain names. The price increase of Chinese domain names by CNNIC will be formally implemented on January 1. And as the owner of 14 types of Chinese domain names, Now Interconnection (www.now.cn) launched the activity of adding 10 yuan to purchase virtual host when buying Chinese domain names on January 1. It is not difficult for us to perceive from this activity that the competition of Chinese domain names in the domain name market in 2008 will become extraordinarily fierce, filled with gunpowder smell from the first day at the beginning of the year.

If enterprises register Chinese domain names to protect brands in the past, Chinese domain names will enter the true stage of large-scale application in 2008.
