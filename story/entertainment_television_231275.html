Plot Summary of "Ultimate Three Kingdoms":

Guan Yu and Zhang Fei are high school students, but due to their excessive love for upholding justice, they have been expelled from twenty-four schools and are known as the "twenty-four schools"! Just as the two feel desperate after being expelled again, a man with large earlobes suddenly appears. He introduces himself as Liu Bei, courtesy name Xuande, a fallen noble who often changes schools because he can't afford the tuition. Liu Bei proposes a plan: since he has noble bloodlines, he could attend the best national noble school, "Donghan Academy." However, the tuition is extremely expensive, and only members of the royal family can enroll. Zhang Fei, being wealthy, says he can help pay everyone's tuition. Liu Bei says: "As long as the money is settled, everything can be solved. I suggest that we three swear brotherhood, so that we all become members of the royal family and can enroll!" Thus, the three come to a beautiful and meaningful place to swear brotherhood. But just as the three finish saying the last sentence: "We do not seek to be born on the same day, but we wish to die on the same day," four people—Xiu, Wang Daxiong, Ding Xiaoyu, and Wang Yaser—from the Iron Space-time dimension accidentally travel through a time-space portal to Silver Space-time. Due to Wang Daxiong accidentally dropping a coin, which knocks over an empty can, causing a chain reaction leading to a falling rock, the rock hits Liu Bei, killing him! Guan Yu, valuing promises, is determined to follow his elder brother and die on the same day. Zhang Fei tries to stop him but fails, so he knocks Guan Yu unconscious first. They then discover that Liu Bei is not dead, just severely injured and unconscious. After discussing with Daxiong, Xiaoyu, and Yaser, they decide to leave Xiu, who looks strikingly similar to Liu Bei, in Silver Space-time as a double, while the other three take the seriously injured Liu Bei back to Iron Space-time for treatment. When Guan Yu wakes up and finds out that the fake Liu Bei (Xiu) is still alive, he is very happy. Zhang Fei is also glad that they don't have to die together. The three finally arrive at "Donghan Academy," where Guan Yu and Zhang Fei believe they can make a name for themselves! Their hope is to graduate well from Donghan Academy. However, due to its prominence, Donghan Academy attracts attacks from Gold High School and Hedong High School (where Dong Zhuo is the principal), putting Donghan Academy in constant danger. Master Zuo Ci even sets up the "Eight Gates Golden Lock Array" at the school gate to block the students from attending classes. Naturally, the three cannot sit idly by and must break the array. However, breaking the Eight Gates Golden Lock Array is not easy. Even Liu Bei (Xiu) and Xiao Qiao get trapped in it, facing life-threatening situations. Therefore, Guan Yu and Zhang Fei accept the suggestion of Student Union President Cao Cao and find Ma Chao, an extremely resilient and cheerful young man, and Huang Zhong, Ma Chao's good friend and an infallible archer, to help break the array. They also involve Zhao Yun, a super handsome youth who has always been at odds with them, to rescue Liu Bei (Xiu) and Xiao Qiao. During the process of breaking the array, Guan Yu saves Zhao Yun's life by shielding him with his arm in a critical moment, resolving the previous conflicts between Zhao Yun and the other two. From then on, Zhao Yun becomes brothers with the other four, showing loyalty and courage, and together they successfully break the Eight Gates Golden Lock Array.

After breaking the array and making new brothers, they thought they could smoothly finish high school, but on one side, Gold High School was eyeing Donghan Academy's resources greedily, and on the other side, Dong Zhuo, the principal of Hedong High School, had ambitions for the position of the principal of Donghan Academy, which owns the "Same School Alliance" leader, the Young Emperor. This means that the Five Tigers and Liu Bei (Xiu) are destined for an unpeaceful campus life. A battle for world domination is about to begin...
