"Night Store" focuses on the lives of lottery players, with Xu Zheng's performance as a lottery enthusiast receiving much attention.

by zhlnjxvr80 on 2012-01-05 14:27:19

Xu Zheng

The film "Night Store" starring Xu Zheng, Li Xiaolu, Qiao Renliang, Zhang Jia yi and others is scheduled to be released in major theaters on July 24. "Night Store" received many pleasant applauses when it was screened in some industry venues, and the audience's evaluation of the film is quite high. Some media commentators also expressed that this is a movie focusing on lottery fans, among whom He San Shui played by Xu Zheng has drawn great attention from the audience.

It is reported that He San Shui (played by Xu Zheng) is a math genius and also a lottery enthusiast who calculated the probability of a set of numbers winning. His buddy only bought one set of numbers, and one day luck finally came to He San Shui - he won 9500 yuan. However, worse than not winning, the boss of the lottery shop made a mistake in printing his winning numbers because he was busy watching TV, thus He San Shui narrowly missed the 9500 yuan prize. The incident did not end there. He San Shui blamed the mistake on the boss of the lottery shop, and repeatedly asked the boss for the prize money. Under the circumstances where he kept getting no results, this math genius eventually resorted to robbery one night.

When a reporter asked Xu Zheng during an interview whether it was worth it for He San Shui to resort to robbery for 9500 yuan, Xu Zheng also stated that in real life, it is absolutely not worth robbing for 9500 yuan. He said: "Buying lottery tickets still involves more random and lucky elements, but in life we cannot always rely on luck; we need to create our own lives." Fortunately, the ending of the movie "Night Store" is pretty good. After the disaster, He San Shui ended up with the supermarket boss lady, which can be considered as another kind of life he created for himself.
