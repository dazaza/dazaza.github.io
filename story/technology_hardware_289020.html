The woman who is seeking a marriage partner is Ms. Kim.

by h0945616 on 2009-12-02 13:45:20

He Xiaomei said that some people with good conditions have put out marriage advertisements but are unwilling to appear in person for a blind date, which is relatively common in Yiwu. She gave Jin a suggestion: "You can show up at the scene and play interactive games with others, to understand and feel each other, and only then can you decide if you are suitable." Original report correspondent Qian Guanjian.

Some people believe that Ms. Jin's actions may be due to a lack of money. "With tens of millions of assets, why does she still need someone from a matching family background? Why does she require a monthly income of 200,000 yuan? The international financial crisis has arrived, and cash is the most valuable. Ms. Jin's actions might also be due to financial pressure, wanting someone to help her out."

Our reporter stationed in Jinhua, Zhao Dao.

But her requirements are not low - the opposite party must be a wealthy person from a matching family background: not only should they have a car and a house, but they must also have a shop in the trading city. Moreover, their monthly income must exceed 200,000 yuan.

Don't think this is just a young woman seeking a rich partner; the 42-year-old Ms. Jin from Yiwu herself is a wealthy person, claiming to have tens of millions of yuan in assets. Her high-priced search for a husband while demanding a matching family background has caused much discussion locally.

"My future son-in-law must not only have a car and a house, but also a shop in the trading city. Moreover, his monthly income must exceed 200,000 yuan."

The main character remains partially hidden.

Although the main character did not appear, the advertisement still attracted a lot of attention. Someone calculated, "An annual income of 2.4 million yuan. This is already considered a rich person in Yiwu."

Today, I interviewed by phone the intermediary agency handling Ms. Jin's matrimonial advertisement. The staff informed me that this lady surnamed Jin comes from a wealthy family. "She has been doing clothing business in Yiwu for more than 20 years, with a solid foundation. Her liquid assets must exceed tens of millions of yuan." We also learned that this lady has been divorced for six years and has two children studying in Yiwu. This staff member indicated that it would be inappropriate to disclose more details.

On that day, many people applied. However, meeting the main character was not easy - everyone was required to fill out personal information first, and suitable candidates would receive a phone call notification.

Today, I contacted Ms. Jin. "This matrimonial advertisement was posted under the encouragement of friends." However, she indicated that in Yiwu, those who meet her criteria are often unmarried, so finding someone from a matching family background is actually not easy.

Ms. He, who has been engaged in matchmaking in Yiwu for many years, introduced that based on her experience, there are about three types of people in Yiwu who can meet the condition of a monthly salary of 200,000 yuan for matrimonial purposes: those who have done business on a certain scale, some store renters in prosperous areas, and securities or investment managers.

On June 13th, a matchmaking event called "Love in Yiwu Fashion Friendship Meeting" was held in Jiangbin Park in Yiwu.

There were not many people participating in this event. A white carpet stretching for 300 meters was crowded with "leftover men and women" greeting each other. Among them, an eye-catching matrimonial advertisement caught many people's attention - the lady advertising for a spouse is 42 years old and claims to have substantial assets.

After the high-priced matrimonial advertisement appeared, people started discussing it. Many said that she had the suspicion of showing off her wealth, or even intentionally creating a hype to attract attention, thinking that few people in Yiwu would come forward to answer the advertisement.

Ms. Jin also mentioned that so far, two or three people have contacted her, all entrepreneurs, but she was unwilling to disclose too much information.
