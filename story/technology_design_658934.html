1 Sweet Date Outfit

Don't waste the single items worn in summer, a pink chiffon short sleeve with ruffles is very cute, paired with a pair of denim shorts, it feels very fresh and sweet. Just add a long wool coat, the embellishments on the cuffs and hem make the originally plain coat look cuter.

2 Casual Shopping Outfit

Polka dots are this year's trend element, black and white polka dots or like this pinkish polka dots are all very nice, and also very versatile single items, they go well with jeans and mini skirts.

3 Fashionable Sporty Outfit

Casual shopping outfits don't have to be too stiff, wear a sporty style jacket with leopard print accents, layered rose red wavy skirt, very casual but still fashionable.

4 Individual Fashionista Outfit

Fashion clothes matching review: Black and white stripe knitted sweater is very practical, especially after a red heart accent, the cuteness has greatly increased, faded light blue jeans are very relaxed and casual. The short edged white hat as an accessory is very stylish, greatly increasing the fashion level.

5 Neutral Mix-and-Match Outfit

Neutral style motorcycle jacket paired with a powder green fringed mini skirt, neutral with a bit of womanly taste, shiny fabric T-shirts are also very eye-catching. Use a dome hat as an accessory, feeling much warmer, and there's also the wardrobe essential chain bag.

6 European and American Designer Outfit

Very European and American street outfit, a must-have suit for autumn, the special thing is the rose red lining rolled out from the cuffs, making the originally dull clothing look much brighter. This season animal prints are also very popular, simple black and white zebra prints. Wearing a woolen cap makes the cuteness degree increase greatly.

7 Girl Next Door Outfit

Pink leopard print wavy dress is extremely sweet, it's the best outfit for dating. If you're afraid of cold, you can add such a furry coat, without destroying the overall sweet and cute feeling.

8 Elegant Ladylike Outfit

Printed dress is one of the hottest items this year, small flower prints add vitality to the originally dull black knitted sweater, sexy black stockings can increase maturity. Wearing this kind of mature yet approachable style is most suitable for work.
