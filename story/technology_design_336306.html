The scientific research topic of "Epidemiological Investigation of Diabetes" was appraised in our hospital.

by zbfirst5f on 2010-03-30 08:58:36

"The Epidemiological Survey Study of Diabetes among the Population Aged 30-70 in Zibo City" passed the appraisal by the Municipal Science and Technology Bureau at our hospital, reaching a domestic leading level.

Professor Chen Li, Director of the Department of Endocrinology of Qilu Hospital of Shandong University, Chairman of the Provincial Endocrine Society, and doctoral supervisor, served as the chairman of the appraisal committee, with six other well-known endocrinology experts and professors from the province and city forming the appraisal committee. Director Li Huanmei of the Achievements Division of the Municipal Science and Technology Bureau presided over the appraisal meeting, attended by Vice President Ma Jinqiang of the First Hospital and Director Li Juan of the Department of Science, Education, and Technology of the Municipal Health Bureau.

This topic took 2 years, with the investigation targeting people aged 30 to 50 who have resided in the urban area of Zibo City for more than 5 years. In Zhangdian District, Zichuan District, Boshan District, Linzi District, Zhoucun District, and Huantai County, 1000 people were respectively selected, totaling 6000 diabetic patients. Through blood glucose testing and oral glucose tolerance (OGTT) tests screening, the First Hospital of Zibo City statistically organized and analyzed the epidemiological data of diabetes in Zibo City, deriving the prevalence data of diabetes and abnormal blood glucose regulation in Zibo City. This achievement has important guiding significance for directing the prevention and treatment work of diabetes in Zibo City, filling the gap in the epidemiological data of diabetes in Zibo City. The research was unanimously recognized and highly evaluated by the topic appraisal committee. After appraisal, this achievement reached a domestic leading level, with very high clinical application value. It is recommended to further improve the data and conduct in-depth research.

In recent years, the hospital leadership team has continuously strengthened the concept of "science and technology revitalizing the hospital", taking preferential measures, vigorously encouraging medical staff to conduct scientific research and innovation, constantly improving the professional technical level and hierarchy of the hospital. One investment, one harvest, the enthusiasm of medical staff for scientific research continues to increase, and the achievements obtained are abundant.
