"Dance District" play: surprisingly got a girlfriend

by 56963tg on 2008-05-14 19:17:47

This thing has developed in an absolutely magical way. I've been single for 24 years, and never thought that playing a game would help me find a girlfriend as beautiful as a flower. Happiness came too fast and suddenly, it was like a dream. The tortuous and strange stories in between could even be made into a movie. Now everything has settled down, and I've successfully gotten my girlfriend. I'd like to share this dream-like experience with you all, hoping that you can also find your own beloved one. It really proves the saying "Nothing is impossible if you can think of it." This story needs to be told from one and a half months ago.

I run my own business and have a studio. To save costs, I took the computer from home to the company for use. As a result, after work, I went home and had nothing to do. The TV programs were terrible. So, I went to a nearby internet cafe to watch movies online. Unexpectedly, this led to a romantic encounter.

That day, I remember clearly. After dinner, I went to the internet cafe to watch movies. It started raining just as I entered the internet cafe. I thought, "Finished. I didn't bring an umbrella. I might not be able to go home tonight." I lost interest in watching movies and didn't know what to do. Suddenly, I saw that the internet cafe was holding an event. A game called Dance Street (Official Website of China Games http://5jq.chinagames.net) was holding an event where registration gave free internet time. I thought, "If it's free, not playing would be a waste. Anyway, I can't leave, and I've played Audition before. It should be similar, so I'll just pass the time."

I registered with the network administrator and then got a free internet card and found a corner in the activity area to sit. The Dance Street client was already open, so I directly registered a China Games account and logged into the game. Once inside, I felt it was quite good. The graphics were bright and pretty, and the characters were 3D, which looked much better than many cartoon dance characters pretending to be cute. I tried out the operations and felt the interface was okay. I liked how the characters looked more sexy and mature. Hehe, I like it. Sorry for digressing.

After entering the game, I started wandering around. This game is a bit different from Audition. To start the game, you need to enter a certain scene and pay the entrance fee. Inside, there are some NPCs you can interact with, and then you can find a place to start. It felt quite fresh. Since I had played similar games before, I found a corner and started playing by myself (I don't like being social, I always like finding corners, haha, it was precisely this excellent characteristic that let me meet my girlfriend). I played for about half an hour and felt it was quite interesting. At this point, I suddenly noticed someone next to me, a female ID named "Cat Baby," wearing a beginner outfit, repeatedly bumping into the wall near me... -_- I awkwardly watched this person, forgetting to play, and noticed she slowly turned a direction and continued bumping into another wall. Awkward rz, I believe my expression at that moment must have been complicated. I thought, "This person is either disconnected or a super newbie, and probably even mentally challenged" (Ah!!! Wife, don't hit me, I'm just stating my true inner thoughts at the time!). Then, I watched with great interest as this "Cat Baby" bumped into walls, flower stands, billboards, and various things, laughing so hard I was rolling on the floor. Finally, I couldn't resist sending her/him a private message (at that time, I didn't think it would be a girl, I thought it was a transvestite kid, and definitely didn't expect...) "What's up? It's only 7 o'clock, already drunk and here? Can't walk straight, hahaha." Then, I saw "Cat Baby" stop moving, and I thought they were probably typing a reply. I have to admit, I guessed the beginning, but not the ending!

The ending was that I waited for two whole minutes to see four words replied: "I don't know how to play." When I saw these four words, I was completely dumbfounded. It turns out she/he was a super newbie, typing so slow that it took two minutes to type four words. Chatting with her/him would probably require the lifespan of a turtle. "Brother...you're too slow, it took you two minutes to type four words. If I talk to you a little more, spiders will probably weave webs on me..." "No, I didn't find how to reply to others' messages, it took me a while!" Oh, this time the reply was quick, and then another line flashed out "Also, don't call me randomly, I'm a girl!!!!!!" "Not believing! In this kind of game, nine out of ten female characters are transvestites, and the only one who isn't basically looks like something that ran out of Jurassic Park!" Next, the other side was silent, seeing no movement, I guessed I was right, and continued playing by myself.

About seven or eight minutes later, the most dizzying thing happened to me that night. I have to say, I had absolutely no psychological preparation. I saw a video request appear in the lower left corner, looking at the name "Cat Baby", my heart tightened | "Really? Could she really be a girl?" Hesitating for a moment, I clicked accept, and the image that appeared became the trigger for my sleepless night afterward.

On the other side of the screen, a figure appeared. At first, it was a bit blurry, and I could see two people on the other side, one debugging, probably the network administrator, and behind him, a girl sitting on the sofa with long straight hair falling to her chest, only showing half her face. Finally, the picture became clear, the network administrator stood up and left, and at this moment, the girl behind (now my LP) revealed her true identity: a head of smooth long hair, a little fringe on the forehead, a clip on the left side, a delicate face with big eyes, eyebrows like fine willows, a small nose, and slightly pouting lips. I saw her turn her head to thank the network administrator, then turn back and smile shyly, her eyebrows and eyes bending into crescents. At that instant, I felt like I was electrocuted, my eyeballs almost falling out. This girl wasn't just beautiful, she had a classical beauty, looking somewhat like Sun Li!!! Especially when her eyes smiled, emitting a very sweet feeling, and her skin looked very delicate and tender, her face white and rosy, wearing a tight white tank top exposing her snow-white shoulders and delicate neck. Little Brother admitted that he swallowed a mouthful of saliva at that moment. Then, I saw her typing on the keyboard, a line of text came over "See now?! I told you I'm a girl, now you believe? Hmm hmm" I hadn't recovered yet, and I saw her send another line "I'm not very good at this game, it's so boring alone, you teach me to play, consider it punishment for guessing the wrong gender of this young lady!"

Unfortunately, I'm such an unambitious guy, lived half a lifetime without having a girlfriend, even rarely talked to girls (as mentioned before, I like to hide in corners...), suddenly a beautiful girl asking me to teach her how to play the game, it really made me at a loss. "What's wrong? Don't want to?" She sent another line, and in the video, I saw her slightly pout and look at the camera with a smile. It was like someone had pressed a button, and I immediately surrendered "Willing! Of course willing!!" Hehe, I'm hungry, let me eat something, I'll continue telling you later.

Supplement: My free internet activity had already ended, but I see that Shenzhen still has internet cafe events going on, with raffles and such. Friends who are interested can check it out, maybe you'll have a wonderful encounter too.
