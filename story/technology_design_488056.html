The summer storms always come so suddenly.

by wenngtlove on 2011-09-11 17:22:42

The summer storms always come so suddenly, with great authority. After they pass, everything returns to silence.

Yes, who would truly love these sudden downpours from the sky?

When two people in love find themselves in such situations, it's hard for either of them to face or accept it. These conflicts are like the summer storms—frightening when they arrive. Today, we had an argument. It wasn't just any ordinary quarrel. Usually, when we have conflicts or misunderstandings, we don’t argue; instead, we ignore each other unhappily, stubbornly stating our own reasons. We both want the other to understand and tolerate us. But when two people in love are separated by distance, can they really explain things clearly? You feel misunderstood, she feels unloved. With this mindset, conflicts linger between the two of you who love each other. In truth, neither of you can change the other. It’s simply about whether one is willing to change for the other. I love him recklessly, fearing that my lack of understanding, my intolerance, my childishness, and my unwillingness to forgive might bring him all sorts of troubles and feelings he cannot accept. I want to change for him. I've started learning how not to be childish, how not to constantly cause him trouble. I've begun to learn tolerance, not disturbing him when he's with friends. When he can't reply to my messages, I tell myself that he must be busy. Yes, I always find various excuses to soothe the results I desire. But allowing myself to do this constantly can be very painful and makes me feel wronged. Do you know, when a woman spoiled by love gets used to others selflessly indulging her without valuing their feelings, today, because of loving you, she has to get used to all of this again. Yes, aside from love, I can't think of anything else that could make her do this. However, giving herself reasons but not receiving the response she wants makes her very unhappy and feel wronged. She wants to talk to you about it. When she approaches you, if you don't comfort her, her already wronged heart will feel even worse. This way, your conflict begins. Her reasons are simple: if you take time to comfort her and spend more time with her, and when she reaches out to you, you don't give your own excuses but comfort her more, there wouldn't be any conflict. But...

Yes, you are also proud, you also feel wronged, and you're not used to comforting anyone because you've never done such things. You believe that love is about understanding each other through actions, and expressing feelings with words every day isn't realistic. However, since you're not together all the time, your relationship depends on words, not physical presence. A simple greeting, a word of care, a sentence saying "I miss you" can soothe all her discomforts and sorrows. But you didn't do that. Instead, you chose to reason with her, standing against her. Both of you, having been spoiled by love, this kind of persistence only brings tears to your relationship.

As night deepens, when she sees you still online, she knows you're upset, you're sad, yet you're still worried about whether she's sleeping. You gently called her at first, but you didn't realize what you said afterward. You were drunk, but you clearly knew what you wanted to express. Yes, you feel wronged, you feel sad, you feel heartbroken because of my misunderstanding. Your tears made her heart ache even more. Your words made her wish she was dead. You wouldn't listen to her explanations, you let loose and vented your grievances recklessly. You hurt her, using the harshest words you ever could. You both held onto your principles, and all those sweet promises became meaningless. The vows of eternal love disappeared with the wind. You left her with a careless remark and logged off. That's how you walked away.

Her sorrow has nowhere to go. Her tears mix with the rain. Everything she had is abandoned because of your tears. She lets herself go, standing in the rain, in the dark night, in a lonely bar. Her heart, her soul, her thoughts have all gone with you. Do you know, you've pushed her onto an unknown path. Don't you feel heartbroken for someone you once loved? Are you really at peace with doing this?

Thinking that this is really the end, that after the storm, there won't be any more pain, but then you tell her you were drunk last night. Yes, you were drunk. She shouldn't hold it against you, but can her heart not be hurt? Can her emotions not ache? She doesn't want to burden you, she wants to let go. She's tired, she doesn't want to love anymore. You've said you no longer love her, yet you tell her you do. Such contradictory words can make her collapse under reality. Her decision to stop loving is conquered by your declaration of love. She gives you and herself another chance, a reality where she still loves you. After the storm, you indeed gave her the rainbow after the storm. You've changed, becoming gentle, considerate, and clingy. Aren't you always telling her you miss her? This is what she wants, but it makes her feel insecure, as if it's all fleeting. The you of before and the you now seem like two different people. Such a change happened overnight. She worries if this change won't last long. If so, she'd rather have the old you who didn't care too much about her than this new version that makes her feel unstable. Can you tell her, are you really changing because you love her, or are you pretending because you pity her love for you? I don't want your charity; I want your sincerity. Because she truly loves you, don't leave room for harm. The heart can only endure so much. Once it breaks, it's hard to mend. Once love leaves, it never comes back. Love well this time, love thoroughly this one time. Perhaps this life, this is the only chance. True love isn't something you encounter anytime. Some feelings are rare and hard to seek. Fate is predetermined, but happiness relies on yourself. Hold onto your happiness, don't let it slip through your fingers. Use your happiest smile to hold the hand of the person you most want to give to. Love courageously and walk towards your direction!
