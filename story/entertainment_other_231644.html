Warm Reminder: You need QQ 2008 or a higher version of QQ to display the Xunxian icon. Due to inconsistent QQ versions, some QQ versions may temporarily not support icon upgrades, so it will uniformly display as a red gourd icon.

Precautions:

If the player's level meets the requirements to light up the subsequent icons, it will automatically update to the corresponding icon within 3 days. The icon display rule is: it takes the information of the highest-level character within the current QQ number to display the icon. The process of lighting up the icon is irreversible, meaning once a higher-level icon has been lit, it cannot be reverted to a lower-level icon or turned off.

FAQ:

Q: Why should I light up the Xunxian icon?

A: Lighting up the Xunxian icon is the right of every Xunxian user. It can showcase that you are a Xunxian player on the Xunxian-related data panel on QQ, highlighting your uniqueness!

Q: How do I light up the Xunxian icon?

A: You just need to have a character in Xunxian and reach level 25, and the system will directly light up the icon for you.

Q: Why does it require level 25 to light up the icon?

A: This is to make the Xunxian icon more valuable and represent a higher pursuit! At the same time, it's also a test!

Q: How do I upgrade the Xunxian icon?

A: Reaching level 40 will upgrade the Xunxian icon to a Level 2 icon, and reaching level 60 will upgrade it to a Level 3 icon. The icon upgrade requires no user operation, as the system will automatically help players complete the upgrade.

Q: Why hasn't my icon been lit up/ upgraded even though I've reached the required level?

A: Please use the QQ version provided by the official website (Chuanmei QQ, Piaoyun QQ, or Shanhu Bug are non-official versions), and upgrade your version to QQ 2008 Official Version or higher. Versions below QQ 2008 Official Version will not be able to display the Xunxian icon.

Q: Why can't I light up/upgrade the icon even though I've reached the required level and meet the version requirement (not less than QQ2007II Beta1)?

A: First, we apologize for any inconvenience caused. Automatic icon upgrades will be completed within 3 days. If it still doesn't light up/upgrade after 3 days, we suggest you contact Xunxian online customer service and choose the category "Other - Game Complaint" to report your issue. We apologize for any inconvenience caused.

Q: Why does my icon remain as the red gourd icon in the QQ version even though I've reached the upgrade requirement and meet the version requirement?

A: Due to inconsistent QQ versions, some QQ versions temporarily do not support icon upgrades, so it will uniformly display as a red gourd icon.
