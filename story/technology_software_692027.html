Regardless of cold or heat, wind or sun, as long as she guessed that her daughter Liu Ruoying's dialysis money was almost gone, Grandma Wang would go out to beg. The hardships of begging were something Grandma Wang never wanted to talk about to others. She always said gratefully: "I've met too many good people." For example, because she often went out begging, bus drivers knew about her daughter's misfortune and never charged her for rides; for example, on cold nights sleeping outdoors, there would be kind-hearted people covering her with a cotton curtain; when thirsty or hungry, there would often be someone giving her a piece of bread or a cup of water. The hospital where her daughter was treated also tried every means to lower the costs. The pain in the child's body, the pain in the mother's heart. Zhang Shuzhen suffered from uremia, and to sustain life, regular hemodialysis was necessary. Initially, Wang Guifang and her daughter almost borrowed from all relatives and friends. By the end of 2008, they had accumulated debts exceeding 130,000 yuan, and even Wang Guifang herself felt reluctant to ask others for help again. After careful consideration, Grandma Wang made an astonishing decision: to go out begging to raise money for her daughter's dialysis fees. When news came that no one was taking care of her daughter after she fell ill, Grandma Wang Gui Fang, who was already eighty years old, was heartbroken. Since her daughter brought her granddaughter to remarry in Zhouzhuang Village, the elder felt that Zhang Shuzhen's husband might never take care of this family again. To take care of her daughter who had lost her ability to work and her young granddaughter, the elder resolutely moved from her son's house to her daughter's house. From then on, the heavy burden of her daughter's home was placed on the frail shoulders of Tuan Gou. Grandma Wang Gui Fang's strength and perseverance also influenced her nine-year-old granddaughter Jingjing. Seeing her grandmother working so hard to support the family and treat her mother, Jingjing not only studied hard at school but was also very sensible at home. I am a legend, studying in the second grade at Zhouzhuang Primary School, who returned several times with the first place award certificate in the grade exams. Sometimes, when grandma went to a faraway place to beg and couldn't return on the same day, Jingjing had to cook rice and take care of her mother herself. At the beginning, Grandma Wang Gui Fang, who hadn't traveled far before, just begged for money along the streets in the county town of Mancheng. Later, in order to beg for more money, she started traveling between Baoding City and surrounding areas such as Xushui, Qingyuan, Shunping, Yixian, Anxin, Rongcheng, Anguo, etc.
