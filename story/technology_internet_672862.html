Brief Content: Carpets [http://www.elidt.com/] have many advantages, such as good elasticity, beauty, sound absorption, etc. Although the biggest disadvantage of carpets is that they are a bit troublesome to maintain, as long as dust is cleaned up in time and proper maintenance is carried out, the service life of the carpet can be quite long.

In daily life, attention should be paid to the care and maintenance of carpets. For example, padding should be placed where the legs of furniture contact the carpet or the furniture should be moved frequently; for areas with severe wear, in addition to using coverings for protection, the position of the carpet can also be exchanged for use. Since dust easily accumulates in the surface fibers of carpets, the best method for high-end carpets [http://www.elidt.com/] is to regularly vacuum along the direction of the nap, which will keep the carpet clean. It's best to vacuum the carpet once a week. Currently, there are three types of vacuum cleaners: pure suction, beating rod, and bristle brush rotation, all of which are suitable for carpets with tightly packed pile heads; while loop pile carpets can only use vacuum cleaners with suction functions. Additionally, the last push when using a vacuum cleaner should be made in the direction of the nap. It's important to note that tools with teeth or rough edges should not be used during cleaning, as this can damage the surface fibers of the carpet. If the carpet has nap inversion, it should be wiped with a clean towel soaked in hot water, combed straight, and ironed with a damp cloth in the direction of the nap, which can restore its original condition.
