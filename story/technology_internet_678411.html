Post-80s starts a business, becomes a CEO with over 10 million yuan in assets in 5 years

by gskfk5x6 on 2012-02-27 14:12:31

With more and more successful people from the post-80s generation, new entrepreneurs are beginning to jump into the market. So what is the most profitable venture to start? He is a post-80s CEO who has been stuck on a difficult problem for 5 years. He is a university entrepreneur who did not obtain a degree. As a boss, he finds inspiration by riding his bike around the loop road - how many people can actually do that? This is how assets worth millions are built. What is the most profitable venture to start? Let's take a look at the business philosophy of a 28-year-old general manager.

The 28-year-old general manager achieves free stage changes

Yang Zhen was born in 1983, he is not tall, and when he thinks, he adjusts his glasses. On his office desk are "Capital" and "Wolf-like Management at Huawei", next to a notebook where he wrote "Rain Bell" and "Farewell Again to Cambridge Bridge" with a soft brush pen, and his bookshelf is filled with professional books.

The company is located in the East Second Section of Chengdu's Third Ring Road in a university entrepreneurship park, his home is nearby, Yang Zhen's life goes back and forth between these two points, rarely entering the inner ring, spending most of his time pondering technical issues and company operations.

[There are many devices in the theater, such as the stage needing to rise and fall, which requires motor drive, and the motor needs to be operated by a computer. It looks simple, but large theaters have hundreds of devices, and there are also hundreds of motors. To connect them all together for operation is not so simple.] Yang Zhen said that Yanxing Technology's main product is the stage mechanical control system. This system is specifically developed for theaters, clubs, and other venues. Users just need to input operational information on the control panel to control the corresponding motor, completing the elevation, speed adjustment, positioning, and other debugging of stage equipment.

[Using this system, whatever effect you want, the stage can present it.] Yang Zhen said. Nowadays, Jincheng Art Palace, Southwest Theatre, Gansu Grand Theatre, and others are using this system.

On the basis of the mature stage mechanical control system, Yang Zhen's company began to extend into the industry, currently mainly focusing on lubrication systems and engineering machinery. He said, although the company has only been established for a few years, its annual output value has already reached the million level.

Campus romance turns girl into wife

Once, while walking with a few classmates, Yang Zhen was walking ahead and said: [Let's hurry up.] This sentence was heard by an out-of-school girl looking for her classmate, and like a TV drama plot, she developed a liking for him. This girl later became his wife.

In 2006, Yang Zhen, then 23 years old and a student at the University of Electronic Science and Technology, along with his partners, developed a system that not only solved the trouble of door-to-door meter reading for meter readers but also brought him his first pot of gold.

After that, Yang Zhen and his classmates founded a company and developed a habit —— if there were no projects, they would ride their bicycles around the inner loop road to find inspiration.

In 2011, the 28-year-old Yang Zhen had not ridden his bicycle around the inner loop road for over a year. At that point, he was the boss of a company with 60 employees, and the stage control system he and his partners developed was successfully used in Jincheng Art Palace, Southwest Theatre, Gansu Grand Theatre, and many other famous theaters.

Nowadays, he spends his days thinking about how to apply this core technology to more fields. Meanwhile, he still occasionally ponders a math problem that has troubled him for many years, even causing him to suffer from neurasthenia.

Changing long-standing meter reading habits during university

In 2006, near the University of Electronic Science and Technology, in an office of over 60 square meters, with a few desks, a few computers, and a few people who liked research and development, this was the predecessor of Yanxing Technology.

In his sophomore year, Yang Zhen participated in the National College Students' Electronic Design Competition with two classmates and won an award. Afterwards, they formed a team named ADT, meaning "Advanced Design Team," and these three letters later became the company's logo.

They rented a house near the school, called a few senior brothers, and started taking jobs. To buy equipment, they even contributed their tuition fees, infuriating their teachers, but the teachers admired their courage and paid their tuition fees for them. Speaking of this, Yang Zhen was quite moved; a teacher named Ding Qingsheng, after retiring from the University of Electronic Science and Technology earlier this year, came to Yanxing Technology and served as the R&D department director, often communicating and exchanging ideas with them, providing guidance.

In the second half of 2006, a power meter reading company found them through the school's teaching and research office, hoping they could develop a wireless meter reading system. After six months of developing this project, they earned 300,000 yuan. Yang Zhen said that before this, meter readers had to go door-to-door to read the electric meters. Their system changed this situation by installing a data collector on the electric meter and then installing a wireless signal relay station downstairs, transmitting the data collected from each meter to the computer through the relay station.

In April 2007, holding the first large sum of money in their lives, the group didn't know how to distribute it. After discussing it, they decided to simply establish a company, naming it "Yanxing Technology." In the team, Yang Zhen has always been the key figure, naturally assuming the role of company general manager.

When the company was just established, everyone didn't know what to do well, sometimes riding bikes around the inner loop road to hope to find some inspiration. Not long after, through the recommendation of a friend they met while working on previous projects, the company finally welcomed its first project —— creating a set of stage mechanical operating systems for Jia Yu Pass Grand Theatre in Gansu.

This project, worth over 2 million yuan, excited them greatly, but progress was fraught with difficulties. The project required the use of controllers, and when Yang Zhen went to purchase Siemens PLC controllers, the other party required full payment of over 700,000 yuan. The company couldn't afford it, and they were frustrated for a long time, [deciding in anger to do it themselves].

No money to buy equipment forced self-research and development

After two months, the new controller was developed, but the results were not optimistic.

[Trying to connect the stage equipment in Gansu, hundreds of devices were moving chaotically, completely out of control, so we had to dismantle it and redesign it.] Yang Zhen laughed as he recalled, failing two or three times before they finally succeeded in making a usable controller.

It took a total of one year to complete the project satisfactorily, and the client was very satisfied. [But we lost money, didn't make any profit.] Yang Zhen said that on the train back from Gansu, he asked the partner responsible for finance how much money was left in the account? The partner said: [Boss, we still have enough money for the bus fare, but we can't afford a taxi.]

However, it was precisely this "money-losing" project that brought a new situation to the company. What is the most profitable venture to start? Since this industry is relatively "narrow", the reputation of Yanxing Technology's stage operating system for Jia Yu Pass Grand Theatre being "very useful" spread quickly. Two months later, they cooperated with a Gansu stage machinery factory. Subsequently, through word-of-mouth, several engineering merchants needing this system gradually approached, allowing the company to slowly overcome difficulties and get back on track.

Yang Zhen and his team carefully considered that this project was relatively specialized, few people were doing it, fewer competitors meant relatively larger market potential, so they decided to make it the company's main project. Over the past three years, their business has covered Sichuan, Shaanxi, Guangdong, Chongqing, and many other places, orders have become increasingly larger, and the number of employees has grown from a few to about 60 now.

What conclusion has the math problem that has puzzled him for many years come to?

Yang Zhen regrets not obtaining his university degree certificate.

Actually, Yang Zhen's grades were not bad, and Teacher Ding Qingsheng always praised him for being "smart" and "capable". But since his sophomore year, he focused his energy on projects, neglecting exams, and by graduation, he hadn't accumulated enough credits, so he didn't obtain a degree certificate.

However, he did harvest love in university. Once, while walking with a few classmates, Yang Zhen was walking ahead and said: [Hurry up.] This sentence was heard by an out-of-school girl visiting her classmate, and like a TV drama plot, she developed a liking for him. This girl later became his wife.

Although the company's profits are already considerable, and his family is very happy, Yang Zhen is still troubled: how to find better talent? And also, what is the conclusion of the math problem that has plagued him for many years?

Speaking of this math problem, Yang Zhen's voice suddenly rose significantly. He said that he had been thinking about it during his studies but could not solve it, which made him neurotic, and he had to take medicine every day. Even now, he continues to explore this issue from time to time. He even hopes to return to school someday, obtain a degree certificate, study mathematics, and research this question.

The problem is: because the positive and negative infinity points of the electric field lines are the same point, can the XY axes of the Cartesian coordinate system be bent into a circle in both the X and Y directions like electric field lines? In this case, the plane under infinitely large conditions will bend into a curved surface. Establishing many analytical geometry methods tending towards infinity in such a bent plane coordinate system, would it also be possible to establish the algebraic method of Riemannian geometry space? Would it also be possible to analytically determine that the sum of the interior angles of a triangle is greater than or less than 180 degrees? Could it also explain that two parallel lines intersect at the same point at infinity?
