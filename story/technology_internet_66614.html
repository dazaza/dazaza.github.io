Nanjing urban management beating video

by diglog2008 on 2008-05-03 17:16:42

Modern Express reported on May 2nd that in recent days, the Express continuously tracked and reported on the incident of urban management law enforcement officers in the Maigaoqiao Subdistrict beating people. Relevant departments have reached a preliminary conclusion regarding this assault case, and the main participants and responsible leaders have all been dealt with accordingly. Meanwhile, the rectification work of the urban appearance system by the Qixia District Party Committee and District Government has also commenced during the May Day holiday.

"Abandoning our vacation this time is to focus internally on disciplinary conduct rectification. In the future, anyone who tarnishes the image of Qixia District or the urban management team will be dismissed from their position." Yesterday morning, at the Qixia District Urban Appearance System's Conduct and Discipline Rectification Conference, District Party Secretary Liang Xuezhong required the urban appearance department to thoroughly rectify itself and proposed the establishment of an "adverse record" system for urban appearance law enforcement personnel to severely punish those who enforce laws barbarically.

All the leading members of the Qixia District Party Committee and District Government abandoned their May Day vacation and organized the district's urban appearance system conduct and discipline rectification conference. All urban appearance law enforcement personnel in the entire district were required to attend this conference.

At the meeting, District Party Secretary Liang Xuezhong expressed his deep pain and regret, stating that this incident of assaulting people has caused extremely negative effects, damaging the image of the urban management team. He required all urban appearance staff members to fully recognize the severity of this matter, make profound reflections; in the future, they must greatly improve the quality of the urban management team, further clarify and implement the leadership responsibility system; urban appearance staff members must enforce laws civilly and resolutely eliminate barbaric law enforcement.

Meanwhile, Liang Xuezhong also indicated that especially some urban appearance assistants, whose qualities are uneven, need to be determined to clean out the "bad apples" within the next month during the urban appearance system's conduct and discipline rectification month. In the future, there should be more emphasis on human-oriented management to resolve confrontations.

To better supervise the law enforcement of urban appearance personnel, at this conference, Liang Xuezhong also proposed to establish an "adverse record" system for all urban appearance personnel in the district. That is, if urban appearance personnel engage in barbaric or brutal law enforcement during the enforcement process, and it is verified, it will be recorded. This will not only be included in daily evaluations but also penalties will be given based on adverse evaluation results. Regarding issues of enforcement discipline and conduct, anyone found problematic will be severely punished without exception.

The District Party Secretary visited and consoled hospitalized victims

At the rectification conference, Liang Xuezhong also required urban appearance personnel to fully consider the livelihood needs of ordinary people when enforcing laws and guide operators to suitable places for operation. "It is essential to strictly distinguish between public power and private rights; the enforcement subject cannot turn public power into private power."

Yesterday afternoon, after concluding the urban appearance system conduct and discipline rectification conference, District Party Secretary Liang Xuezhong, along with the primary leaders of the district government and the urban appearance department, visited and consoled the injured Wang Qi and his family at the Jiangsu Integrated Traditional Chinese and Western Medicine Hospital.
