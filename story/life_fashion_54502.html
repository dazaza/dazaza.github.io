Shinzi Katoh

SHINZI KATOH - General goods filled with fairy tale innocence.

Created by the famous Japanese illustrator Shinzi Katoh with the Italian word "Sereno" as the theme, these are hand-drawn lifestyle general goods. They often present life themes in a fresh and smooth style. Whether depicting nature and trees, or cute animals and lively people, they can all make one feel the innocence of a fairy tale world and a sense of warmth full of vitality. "Sereno" series: In Italian, it means calm weather, mood, or feeling. If every morning when you open the window, you can see a clear blue sky, our mood will also feel full of hope, able to dream come true.
