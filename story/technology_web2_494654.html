**The Evolution of Mobile Phones**

Since the 1970s, mobile phones have undergone significant developments:

- In 1973, Martin Cooper invented the first mobile phone.

- In 1983, Motorola Dynatac 8000X was released, weighing about two pounds and priced at $4000.

- In 1992, the world's first text message was sent, reading "Merry Christmas."

- In 1996, the world's first flip phone, Motorola StarTAC, hit the market.

**Application Comparison Across Mobile Platforms**

According to DamnDigital, there are a total of 826,200 applications globally, with 375,000 on iPhone and 225,000 on Android. In terms of operating systems, Android holds a 36% share (41% of apps are paid), iOS has 26% (71% of apps are paid), and BlackBerry accounts for 23% (75% of apps are paid).

**Data About App Store**

From July 2008 to May 2011, the number of applications in the App Store increased significantly:

- July 2008: 500 apps

- November 2009: 10,000 apps

- April 2009: 100 million app downloads

- October 2009: 100,000 apps

- April 2010: 200,000 apps

- June 2010: 500 million downloads

- February 2011: 1 billion downloads
