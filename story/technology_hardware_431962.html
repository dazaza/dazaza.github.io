After Returning from Hongqiao Vehicle Inspection - It was Smooth!

At 8:30 AM, I entered the inspection compound. It seemed like they had just set off firecrackers. I asked if vehicles could be inspected today.

I went home to get the documents needed for inspection.

At 9:10 AM, I returned right on time. The whole compound had only my car ready for inspection. Haha.

1. First, go to the hall and swipe the card (probably checking for violations).

2. Go for external inspection (they check headlights and brake lights).

3. Number stamping (costs 5 yuan).

4. Return to the external inspection room to fill out the inspection form, then pay the inspection fee of 100 yuan. They will tell you to come back after the online test to collect the invoice.

5. Online testing: hand over the car. They first check the exhaust, front and rear brakes, and headlights.

6. After passing smoothly, take the invoice.

7. Before returning to the hall to collect the qualification certificate, you must first check the vehicle and vessel tax. I paid 420 yuan for the 2009 tax and 11 yuan in late fees (from January 1, 2010), and also paid the 2010 vehicle and vessel tax.

8. At the certification window, they checked the compulsory insurance policy again. Then, I successfully received the vehicle inspection qualification certificate. Checking the clock, it was 9:35 AM. The entire process took 25 minutes, smooth and quick.

I especially want to tell everyone that there was no mention of the 55 yuan fee. Fortunately, I didn't pay it either. The total cost for the vehicle inspection was 105 yuan. Before inspection, all violations must be cleared first.

I hope this can provide some reference for everyone. Cheers!
