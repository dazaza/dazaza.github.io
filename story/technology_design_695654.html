Squirrel crosses the track, collides with a speeding Lamborghini, loses its tail to survive (Picture)

by yann287xxue on 2012-03-07 16:38:26

The video screenshots show that, in California of the United States recently, while a Lamborghini was speeding on the track, a squirrel suddenly crossed the track. When people thought that this squirrel had unfortunately been killed by the car, it reappeared miraculously but its tail seemed to be broken off. On July 3rd, according to foreign media reports on July 2nd, a lucky little squirrel was captured on video crossing the track and unfortunately encountered a racing car, breaking its tail to save its life. This thrilling video has received an extremely high click rate online. According to the report, a group of Lamborghini enthusiasts were practicing "speeding" at a racetrack in California, USA. The racing cars circled the track again and again at a speed of over one hundred kilometers per hour. However, a moving "obstacle" suddenly appeared on the track, and the people beside the track quickly filmed the video. Just as a Lamborghini LP670-4 SV was traveling at a speed of 100 miles per hour (approximately 160 kilometers per hour), a little squirrel jumped across the track and just rushed under the car. The videographer thought that this poor squirrel had died, but unexpectedly, the figure of the squirrel reappeared on the other side of the track after the car passed, proving that it had survived crossing the dangerous track, which could be called a "miracle". However, from the video, it can be seen that this "lucky" squirrel's tail seemed to be broken, and the previously raised big tail disappeared, but the fact that the squirrel wasn't run over or crushed to death could also be considered "a blessing in disguise". This video has garnered 365,000 views in just three or four days since it was uploaded online. Netizens commented, "It looks like the little guy lost part of its tail. I hope this little squirrel is okay." It is currently unknown whether the driver of this Lamborghini was a professional race car driver or an amateur enthusiast. Comments are welcome.
