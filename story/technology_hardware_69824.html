NOKIA N78 is officially launched!!

by clickme on 2008-05-21 09:13:26

Starting from May 20th, the NOKIA N78 has been stocked in major overseas online malls! Prices vary from $210 to $699.99. It seems that the online marketing environment is similar both domestically and internationally, with a mix of good and bad products and sellers.

The Nokia n78 Black US NAM (unlocked) is sold for $499.99 on mobilecityonline, which seems to be a fairly credible price point for its release.

At the same time, the sales price of the official version of Nokia N78 was revealed for the first time on the club page of a domestic bank. Although it is still unclear whether this model can be purchased from their supplier, the listed price of 4388 yuan does give an impression of being quite expensive.

The NOKIA Station will continue to monitor the situation after the release of the N78 and share updates with everyone at the first opportunity!
