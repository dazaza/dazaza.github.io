Wu Qilong and Ma Yashu's marriage has always been a good story in the entertainment circle. Why did this marriage end after only five years? This has left many people puzzled. So what are the hidden reasons behind Wu Qilong and Ma Yashu's divorce?

1. Ma Yashu being flirtatious

According to informed sources: "They've had problems for a long time now, they haven't been together for quite some time. Haven't you seen Wu Qilong return to Kunming in nearly a year? Didn't he used to go there often? In fact, the main reason for their marital change is Ma Yashu. She has several boyfriends, and she also has some ambiguous relationship with one of the male stars from their company. Wu Qilong couldn't bear her flirtatiousness, so he left."

2. Refusal to have children

In fact, Wu Qilong and Ma Yashu's romance was not favored from the start. However, since Ma Yashu wasn't very famous, this wedding where the man was stronger than the woman didn't cause much of a stir in the entertainment industry. After marriage, besides the topic of "when will they have a child," it didn't help much with either of their careers. Wu Qilong was very good to Ma Yashu, but Ma Yashu kept delaying and refusing to have children, which made Wu Qilong very helpless. He's reached an age where he can afford to have a family, but his wife just wouldn't give birth. How many men would want to marry a wife who doesn't bear children? Regardless of the concept of carrying on the family line, the pressure of working in the entertainment industry is immense, having a child would add a lot more joy and motivation to life.

3. Poor mother-in-law and daughter-in-law relationship

The mother-in-law and daughter-in-law relationship is also an important factor affecting the couple's relationship. Wu's mother once privately revealed that Ma Yashu was too high-profile, while Wu Qilong's ex-girlfriend Cai Shaoqin was more elegant, and she still preferred Cai Shaoqin. The mother-in-law didn't like the daughter-in-law to begin with, and the daughter-in-law refused to have children. A mother-in-law who wanted to hold her grandchild would definitely have complaints. Wu Qilong is known as a filial son, so the conflict continued to escalate.

4. Blind admiration before marriage, post-marriage fantasy shattered

Ma Yashu once said: Wu Qilong was her idol, and she blindly admired him. It's normal for young girls to admire idols and then develop affection. But blind admiration often easily leads to confusion and falling into the trap of "false love." Ma Yashu married Wu Qilong just because of idol worship, but life is cruel, idols are human beings, and they also have flaws. Post-marriage life shattered Ma Yashu's perfect image of her idol husband, extinguishing the flame of love.

5. The female side being too dominant, not giving the male side face

The two have vastly different personalities and concepts. Straightforward Ma Yashu speaks her mind quickly, often making Wu Qilong feel hurt and unable to hold his head up in front of friends.

6. Too little time spent together

Too little time spent together is a common problem in entertainment industry marriages, and it's not surprising that it led to divorce.

7. No wedding ceremony held, damaging the female side's dignity

Wu Qilong didn't even stage a lavish wedding, and he didn't even get out of the car when registering their marriage, letting relatives do it on his behalf, which was shockingly low-key. He stated on his blog that he just wanted to quietly enjoy the happiness of the two of them. This fits Wu Qilong's Scorpio personality, but it might not align with Ma Yashu's Aries wishes. But women are women, weddings are more important than marriage. Even though Wu Qilong later gave her a million-dollar diamond ring, it still couldn't make up for this regret.

A fortune-teller once predicted: Over time, there would be some disharmony in Wu and Ma's post-marriage life. One prefers quietness, the other prefers activity. But Wu Qilong is rushing down the path of becoming a successful man, and Ma Yashu naturally enjoys being a small woman reaping the benefits. As long as she doesn't become restless, everything will be fine. But Ma Yashu isn't a submissive person, and this good story has finally become an illusion, truly regrettable.
