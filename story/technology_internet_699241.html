Minister of Housing and Urban-Rural Development Jiang Weixin: The housing price in big cities will definitely go down.

by loveh1o7 on 2012-03-09 14:10:54

Minister of Housing and Urban-Rural Development Jiang Weixin indicated on the 8th that the real estate regulation in 2012 will be implemented in a totally different and resolute manner. He stated very clearly that the purchase restriction order will not be relaxed in the short term, and he is very confident about the decline in housing prices in first-tier cities.

Video: Minister of Housing and Urban-Rural Development says that housing prices in big cities will definitely fall.

Media Source: Ningxia TV "Finance Early Bus"

Jiang Weixin also clearly indicated that the personal housing information system will definitely be launched within this year, and according to the plan, the first step is to complete the network connection of the personal housing information system of 40 key cities with the Ministry of Housing and Urban-Rural Development. Authoritative experts pointed out that the implementation of this system will lay the foundation for accelerating the promotion of long-term mechanisms such as property tax.

Before and after the two sessions, signals that real estate regulation will not be relaxed have repeatedly become clear bra[http://www.tingfeier.com].

Just before the convening of the two sessions, it was exclusively confirmed from the Ministry of Housing and Urban-Rural Development that local governments are absolutely not allowed to shake the overall situation of the central government's real estate regulation for their own interests. In response to the recent [small moves] by some cities, the Ministry of Housing and Urban-Rural Development will take action without hesitation, suppressing any attempts immediately. www.syzc020.com

Subsequently, Director of the National Development and Reform Commission Zhang Ping stated at the meeting regarding the [small moves] by some cities that the attitude of the central government is very clear, which is to maintain the various policies of the real estate market macro-control unwaveringly; meanwhile, they will expedite research on promoting the long-term healthy development mechanism of the real estate industry.

Following closely, Minister of Finance Xie Xuren stated on the 6th that based on the pilot projects in Guangzhou and Chongqing, further research and planning for the reform of property tax will be conducted, and the pilot scope will be appropriately expanded, and actively and steadily promoted. www.tingfeier.com

The most significant statement came from Minister of Housing and Urban-Rural Development Jiang Weixin on the 8th: The real estate regulation in 2012 will be implemented in a totally different and resolute manner, [the purchase restriction order will not be relaxed in the short term, and I am very confident about the decline in housing prices in first-tier cities.]

Just the day before, Jiang Weixin emphasized that if the housing prices in seven or eight large cities continue to increase at an annual rate of even dozens of percentage points, once the bubble bursts in the future, the consequences will be unimaginable. [This will have a very significant impact on the entire economy and even society.] Jiang Weixin said, [Now we have made up our minds, at least the prices in these seven or eight large cities must come down.]
