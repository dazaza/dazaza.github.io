Five words to summarize Battier

The 'Happy Camp' production team used five English words to summarize this "Batman": high, air, papa, perfect, and Yao. High refers to Battier's height of 2 meters and 3 centimeters; air refers to Battier's indispensable role in the Rockets. Papa is one of Battier's current identities—"Tomorrow is my son's 15-month birthday." A normally ordinary sentence from Battier revealed the tender side of this tough court hero to all the viewers—"My son is now my heart and soul. No matter what happens on the court, seeing my son when I get home makes me feel warm. Any honor on the court brings me joy that can hardly compare to my son." Battier also revealed that he cried for ten minutes when his son was born. Perfect is the affirmation people have for Battier, but he said, "It's too far away."

Yao, of course, refers to Yao Ming. As Yao Ming's teammate, Battier praised him endlessly—"He is an excellent person. He represents China's image well, and he deserves all Chinese people to be proud of him." However, Battier still revealed a small embarrassing moment involving Yao Ming. Before the annual American college basketball league, players in the Rockets' locker room always bet $100 as entertainment. When Battier collected money from Yao Ming, the Chinese giant always hesitated and said, "Can I not participate... $100 is too much." Battier joked, "Every time at this point, we would tease him, 'Yao, you are the one who earns the most among us, yet you hold onto your money so tightly it seems like water could be squeezed out of it.'" It seems that Yao Ming's "frugality" has become one of the jokes in the Rockets' locker room.

Professional Basketball vs Street Basketball, Battier barely wins

In each round, one side could shoot in any posture, while the other side had to score using the same method; otherwise, they would be declared the loser.
