Taste of Love Episode 7 Watch Online

Introduction to the Separate Episodes of Taste of Love

Shooting Ads, Competing for Clothes

The employees of the Japanese restaurant "Ichi-go Ichi-e" are preparing for the dinner service. Among them, Master Rei is personally making a sushi box for Yumi, the daughter of the owner Takamori. Takamori's sister Gaokui insists that she will not take Yumi's order, but Rei indicates that he will settle the bill. Rei personally rides his motorcycle to the ad shooting site to find Yumi and witnesses Yumi competing for clothes with another female model, Suki. Yumi gives the sushi brought by Rei to the staff to taste. Rei presents specially prepared sashimi for Yumi, but Yumi gives it to the director to gain his favor, eventually earning the chance to act as the lead actress. The director asks Suki to push Yumi into the pool, and despite not knowing how to swim, Yumi does not refuse. Unexpectedly, Yumi drowns during the shoot, and Rei jumps into the pool without hesitation to save her. On the other hand, the boss of a food group, Masaiyi, invites Takamori to endorse chicken soup with fish balls, offering a generous remuneration, but Takamori remains unmoved. As the dinner service at "Ichi-go Ichi-e" approaches, the staff is busy preparing, and Takamori personally oversees the scene, causing tension among the employees. Since the ad aired, Yumi has become very popular, and her altered life-size cardboard cutout is being snatched up by men, much to Takamori's dissatisfaction. Yumi's manager asks her to undergo breast enhancement surgery in Japan, which makes Yumi hesitant. After hearing this news, Takamori strongly opposes it, but Yumi insists on going to Japan alone for the surgery...

Playing Around, Losing Money

Yumi ultimately fails to convince herself to undergo breast enhancement and escapes from the plastic surgery center to play around, unaware that Rei is searching for her in Japan. However, due to playing with Japanese children, Yumi loses her wallet. In great panic, Yumi immediately calls back to Hong Kong for help. Although Takamori claims that he no longer cares about her, he still tells Rei about the situation, allowing Rei to search for Yumi.

Rei rushes to Kumamoto and finds Yumi through the scent of "orange-flavored red bean soup." This allows Rei to discover that Yumi saved an elderly man named Takano who wanted to starve himself to death after his wife passed away. Rei observes that Takano's wife used to cook eel rice for her husband before she passed away, so Rei decides to prepare a meal of eel rice for Takano. As expected by Rei, this meal of eel rice reignites Takano's zest for life. It turns out that Takano is a renowned food critic in Japan. Because he mistakenly thought the eel rice was made by Yumi, Japanese media crowned Yumi as the "Beauty Chef Goddess," and her interview clips even spread back to Hong Kong... Encouraged by Takano, Rei proposes to Yumi, who surprisingly accepts right away and immediately returns to Hong Kong to announce their engagement. This brings great joy to Takamori, who then cancels Yumi's contract with her manager Wendy. But in fact, this is Yumi's scheme: on one hand, to pacify Takamori so he stops bothering her, and on the other hand, to use the name of the "Beauty Chef Goddess" to switch companies under Saiyi...

Father-Daughter Discord, Affecting Rei

When Takamori learns that Yumi lied about marrying Rei to further her career, he becomes furious and accidentally injures his left hand while scolding Yumi, angrily going home to drink alone.

When everyone returns home, the father and daughter argue again. Rei feels responsible for injuring Takamori and starts blaming Yumi on behalf of Takamori. Yumi immediately blames Rei as well, and the three of them part ways unhappily...

Yumi wants Rei to go with her to Japan for filming, but Rei refuses because Takamori injured his hand. However, Rei worries all day that Yumi might expose the truth, becoming distracted. In anger, Takamori forbids Rei from working. When Yumi suggests again that Rei go to Japan with her, Rei still refuses.

Yumi goes to Japan alone for filming but is constantly teased by the producer Yanli, who devises ways to make her look foolish every day. Fortunately, Yumi manages to resolve these issues with her cleverness. But Yanli doesn't let Yumi off easily, arranging for her to compete in a dumpling-making contest against the Ramen King, which greatly troubles Yumi. Fortunately, Rei suddenly appears and teaches Yumi the secrets of making dumplings... When everyone inspects the ramen shop where the competition will take place, a Hong Kong apprentice named Gang suddenly causes trouble, accusing the boss of refusing to teach him dumpling-making techniques. Yumi informs Rei about this, and Rei comes up with the idea of bringing Gang's mother to Japan to meet him at the competition site, which helps Gang regain his motivation to stay in Japan and learn the craft, also sparing Yumi from the embarrassment of the competition.

Yumi Advances, Master-Apprentice Reconciliation

Saiyi, after watching the clips of the dumpling competition, highly praises Yumi and publicly announces that Yumi will host a brand-new food program, much to Yanli's disdain. Despite being replaced, Yumi doesn't care much. Upon returning home to celebrate, she learns that Rei is troubled about how to reconcile with Takamori. Yumi then advises Rei to persistently pester Takamori until he forgives Rei, which eventually works, leading Takamori to forgive Rei.

Suddenly, the principal of Yumi's former school calls asking for her help. It turns out that the students at the school have serious picky eating habits, resulting in either lunchboxes that don't suit the students' tastes or "junk food" that makes them obese. Principal Fan hopes that Yumi can design lunchboxes that are both nutritious and appealing to the students.

After hearing this, Yumi readily agrees and pitches her concept to Saiyi. Delighted, Saiyi accepts her suggestion and appoints Yumi as the host, which irritates Yanli. Taking advantage of Saiyi's absence on a trip, Yanli secretly replaces Yumi. Feeling dejected after being replaced, Yumi gets drunk at "Ichi-go Ichi-e," causing Takamori deep heartache...

Although Yumi has been replaced, Rei continues to work on designing student lunchboxes. During this time, Rei meets Jiale, who has just lost his father, inspiring him to let the students personally make their own nutritious lunches. Yumi forces Tianxin to secretly film a program for her without the company's knowledge. Suddenly, Yanli appears and forcibly stops everyone. Amidst the argument, Yanli reveals her own background...
