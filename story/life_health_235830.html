PLA 254 Hospital in Tianjin 1940~2009 Legendary Hospital History

by ddd150 on 2009-09-10 13:46:13

1. Sent a voluntary medical team to Korea for the War to Resist US Aggression and Aid Korea. In November 1950, a voluntary medical team composed of 99 medical staff was sent to the front line in Korea to carry out battlefield rescue missions, lasting for 7 months.

2. Participated in the Northwest Bandit Suppression. From April 1960 to January 1961, five military doctors were dispatched to the Qilian area in Qinghai Province's front-line command cavalry regiment to execute bandit suppression combat and medical tasks.

3. Participated in the Xingtai Earthquake Rescue. In March 1966, a medical team composed of 60 medical staff was sent to the Xingtai disaster area to carry out medical rescue missions, lasting for 40 days.

4. Participated in the "Three Supports and Two Armies" work. In March 1967, a total of 284 people were successively dispatched to regions such as Tianjin, Hebei, Shanxi, and Inner Mongolia to participate in the "Three Supports and Two Armies" (supporting agriculture, supporting industry, supporting left-wing forces, military training, and military management) work, all returning to the hospital by 1973.

5. Participated in rural mobile medical care. In June 1969, 126 medical staff were organized and sent to Qinglong County in Hebei Province's countryside for fixed-point mobile medical care. They returned to the hospital in May 1970. In March 1974, a second group of 89 medical personnel was dispatched for mobile medical care in the area, concluding at the end of 1976.

6. Participated in Tangshan Earthquake Rescue. In July 1976, 26 medical staff were dispatched to Tangshan and Ninghe to participate in earthquake relief medical missions, lasting for 70 days and successfully completing the mission.

7. Participated in the Defensive Operation Against Vietnam. In September 1986, five military doctors were dispatched to join the Third Field Medical Unit, executing field medical rescue missions in Chouxian, Yunnan, completing the mission and returning to the hospital in April 1988.

8. Executed medical rescue missions on the Laoshan Frontline. In March 1987, 11 military doctors were dispatched to the Laoshan Frontline in Yunnan to execute battlefield medical rescue missions, returning to their units in 1988.

9. Participated in the medical task to quell the counter-revolutionary riot in the capital. In June 1989, 10 medical staff were dispatched to participate in the medical task to quell the counter-revolutionary riot in the capital, returning to the hospital on August 31 of the same year.

10. Participated in the Hangu Anti-Sea Tide Disaster Relief. On September 2, 1992, 80 medical staff and six vehicles were dispatched to Hangu for disaster relief, continuously fighting for 17 hours, rescuing 116 patients, and successfully completing the task.

11. In 1997, a 24-person medical team was dispatched to participate in the opening medical security task of the Pingjin Campaign Memorial Hall in Tianjin.

12. In September 1999, eight medical staff were dispatched to participate in the medical security for the National Day 50th Anniversary Parade.

13. In May 2003, our hospital achieved complete victory in the fight against SARS, with no one infected.

14. In October 2003, the new outpatient building was completed.

15. In August 2007, 43 medical staff from our hospital were dispatched to Liberia to complete peacekeeping missions.
