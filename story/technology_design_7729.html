by commencement06927 on 2006-10-20 17:36:29

"Why is it always the same line in your mouth, why doesn't my heart die?"

In the more than 10 years since the telecommunications industry broke its monopoly, one of the changes brought about by competition has been the shaping of operators' brands and the evolution of advertising. Now, many slogans of telecommunications operators have even become popular phrases in society, becoming part of popular culture. Let's look back at the classic advertising "golden lines" of the six major operators and take a look at the opening and competitive path of the telecommunications industry from this side.
