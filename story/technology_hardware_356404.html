29 social teams compete for promotion in Shanghai selection competition

by kvidi2510 on 2010-05-27 17:25:05

On April 24, Shenyang hosted the preliminary competition of the affiliated region for the 2010 ChinaJoy Cosplay Carnival Northeast Region, specifically the Shenyang area selection contest. Thirty groups registered to compete and after fierce competition, six outstanding groups were selected to advance to the Changchun Northeast regional finals. They are: Manshuang Animation Group's "Naruto"; Yunyu Animation Group's "Saint Seiya"; Ziteng Animation Group's "X Record"; Zhuoyan Group's "Hitman Reborn"; Infinite Set Animation Group's "Higurashi When They Cry", Naruto Episode 256; We're Animation Group's "Rich Man, Poor Man".

On May 1, Harbin hosted the preliminary competition of the affiliated region for the 2010 ChinaJoy Cosplay Carnival Northeast Region, specifically the Harbin area selection contest. Thirty groups registered to compete and after fierce competition, eight outstanding groups were selected to advance to the Changchun Northeast regional finals. They are: Yinhun Tongmeng's "Gintama"; Meiyin Ge's "Lovers' Double"; Manying Buluo's "Pokémon"; Beiju Zuofang's "God Cook"; No.13 Cemetery's "Samurai Warriors 3"; Rococo Era's "Angel Sanctuary"; Baihe Hui's "Higurashi When They Cry"; Qianmo Animation Group's "Shugo Chara".

From May 28 to 29, the Changchun area selection contest will be held at the Ouya Superstore Exhibition Center. So far, 50 groups have registered to compete and it is expected that after fierce competition, 15 outstanding groups will be selected to advance to the Changchun Northeast regional finals.

From May 30 to June 2, SpongeBob Comics Network, the six outstanding groups from the Shenyang region, the eight outstanding groups from the Harbin region, and the 15 outstanding groups from the Changchun region will gather at the Ouya Superstore Exhibition Center in Changchun for an exciting competition.

Who will emerge as the top performer in the Northeast regional finals? Who will represent the Northeast region to go to the Shanghai finals? All comic fans are eagerly waiting to see.
