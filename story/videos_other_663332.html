A 14-year-old "dishwashing younger brother" works to raise money. Weibo recommendation | Today's Weibo hotspot (editor: SN017) Washed 1500 dishes in 16 hours, daily wage 13 yuan. This is the labor income of the 14-year-old dishwashing boy Mr Gao every day, and his hands have been full of chilblains. Mr Gao's mother Mrs Ma was disfigured by severe burns and has received the first phase of repair treatment in Zhongda Hospital. However, the huge medical expenses made this family from Ningxia feel very difficult. Helplessly, the 14-year-old Mr Gao had to drop out of school and work in a noodle shop to wash dishes and raise money for his mother's treatment.

The relay of love is still going on.

The story of "dishwashing younger brother" Mr Gao moved many people. A Mr Sun from Beijing proposed that he would sponsor the dishwashing boy to complete his studies. Mr Sun said that he particularly sympathized with young children, "For children of this age, they should be sitting in bright classrooms, but now they have to bear the heavy burden of the family." So he decided that if Mr Gao could continue his studies, he would be willing to bear all the tuition fees. A Ms Chen, a reader from Shandong, also expressed that Mr Gao could work in her own specialty store, "This will definitely earn more than washing dishes."

After the misfortune of a 39-year-old woman Ms Ma from Gansu suffering burn disfigurement was reported in our newspaper, many unnamed good-hearted people came to donate. And the story of Ms Ma's 14-year-old son Mr Gao dropping out of school to wash dishes moved many people, and the donations from good-hearted people have reached more than 40,000 yuan. It was learned from the Department of Plastic Surgery of Zhongda Hospital Affiliated to Southeast University that experts have successfully performed surgery on Ms Ma, and she can be discharged from the hospital in about a week.

Mr Tang Shizhong, assistant to the president of Zhongda Hospital and director of the Medical Affairs Department, said that Ms Ma has already undergone the first phase of surgery, followed by the second and third phases of surgery. In order to help the patient, the hospital leadership finally decided to reduce part of the anesthesia surgery and other treatment costs for the patient Ms Ma. It was learned that before the reporter finished the article, Ms Ma had received more than 40,000 yuan of love donations from all over the country, and there is still a funding gap of 60,000 yuan for the current treatment costs.
