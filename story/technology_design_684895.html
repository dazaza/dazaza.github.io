Emphasizing Comfort with People-Oriented Approach

With consumers being more rational about decoration, decoration has shifted from "I like this look, that form" passive acceptance, to "my favorite lifestyle leads me to choose this decoration style." Experts indicate that for a long time in the future, emphasizing comfort, more humane and culturally rich home decoration designs will become mainstream.

Elegant Rustic Style

Elegantism stems from a return to the modern minimalist style, while the simplified lines and natural materials in the rustic style are a strong response to pseudo-minimalist styles. Elegance can be simple, but without the dullness and monotony of pseudo-simplicity; elegance can be classical, but without the complexity and seriousness of classical styles, instead giving people a sense of dignity and tranquility, allowing one to relax spiritually in the space.

Fusion Style, Healthy Life

Health and nature will become an enduring theme. More and more designers are starting to tailor home designs according to the specific requirements of clients, creating designs that meet their living needs. Incorporating modern elements into classical styles to make the entire space more vibrant; or adding European elements to Chinese-style environments to create a more comfortable life. The fusion of styles not only reflects the diversity of design but also emphasizes people's understanding of life from another aspect.

Dining, Kitchen, Bathrooms - Suitability is Key

Since most families have relatively small bathroom spaces, the style orientation should be determined by the space, scale, or based on one's own economic ability. It's hard for any single trend to dominate in terms of style.
