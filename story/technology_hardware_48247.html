The shooting king in mobile phones, Sony Ericsson SO905iCS launched in Japan

by athw2002 on 2008-02-18 20:00:48

The Cyber-shot flagship product customized by Sony Ericsson for NTT, the 5-megapixel phone SO905iCS with a 3x optical zoom lens, garnered attention no less intense than the current launch of the Xperia X1 when it was unveiled last November. Even before its release, it drew significant interest from fans of camera phones, especially those who favor Sony Ericsson's Cyber-shot series. Recently, Japanese carrier NTT DoCoMo officially announced that this exceptional Cyber-shot camera phone will be released in Japan on February 15th, along with the commencement of a series of promotional activities.
