Plot Summary of "Killer・Wedding Road":

Translated: Despite always messing things up, everything seems to be going well for Haruko. Fate appears not to have abandoned her, as she has found a handsome and wealthy fiancé, much to the envy of her colleagues at work.

However, who would have known that on the day before her wedding, Haruko would commit the biggest mistake of her life - she accidentally kills someone in her own apartment. What should she do? Remembering her late adoptive grandfather who raised her, Haruko decides she must wear her wedding dress and become a bride. She begins taking action, determined to hide the body no matter what, at least until after the wedding ceremony so that it remains undiscovered.

Haruko stuffs the body into a suitcase and drives towards Mount Fuji. Suddenly, an unfamiliar woman jumps out in front of her car.

This beauty, who wants to be run over by a car, is named Fuko Kobayashi. She's never had any luck with men, always getting dumped. Each time she gets dumped, she tries different methods to kill herself but somehow always survives. Fuko proposes a deal to Haruko: "I will help you dispose of the body if, in return, you kill me."

Under the whimsical twists of fate, "the woman who wants to get married no matter what" and "the woman who wants to die no matter what" end up together. They team up to evade the police, facing numerous close calls. In the process, two people from completely opposite worlds form a strange bond of friendship. Will Haruko make it to her wedding ceremony? And will Fuko find a way to live again and regain hope?
