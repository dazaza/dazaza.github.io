Synopsis:

A group of problem students, who are constant headaches for the school, accidentally discover an unknown organism in the campus. When they take it to the lab for testing, they find out that it's a very strange species that becomes highly active when exposed to water and continuously divides.

Soon after, strange occurrences start happening around the campus. The teachers begin acting very differently from their usual selves. Eventually, the students discover that many teachers and so-called model students have been taken over by alien parasites. Naturally, this theory is dismissed by others as nonsense, forcing them to investigate on their own. At the same time, these students must evade the pursuit of the alien-infested teachers.

As the crisis intensifies, the alien infestation spreads throughout the entire school. The most terrifying realization is that they can no longer be sure whether their closest classmates are still human. They eventually discover that the lethal weapon against the aliens is the drugs they made themselves. How will they eliminate these terrifying aliens that have invaded humanity and plan to control Earth?
