Half of Weibo users are single, and among the post-80s generation, those who are born in the year of Tiger and have blood type O are the most numerous.

by geekzhang on 2013-05-09 12:19:17

At the beginning of this year, Alipay had launched an annual activity of showing off account statements, which made quite a lot of netizens exclaim that Alipay knew them better than their own partners. Now it seems that Sina Weibo also knows you well.Yesterday, the 2013 Hubei Weibo Marketing Conference hosted by Sina and Sina Weibo was held in Han, where a reporter got hold of a "Sina Weibo User Development Report", which could be said to be a portrait of the 500 million Sina Weibo users.In terms of age, people born in the 80s accounted for 55%, while those born in the 90s and 00s accounted for 37%, adding up to more than 90% in total. People born in the 70s and before only accounted for 6% and 2%. In terms of education, 80% of users have received higher education, with bachelor's degree or above accounting for 55.9%.Even zodiac signs, constellations, and blood types are included in the statistics. The most populous zodiac sign is Tiger, accounting for 13%, while Snake is the least; the most populous constellation is Scorpio, accounting for 9.5%, while Taurus is the least; the most populous blood type is O, accounting for 36.5%, while AB is the least.In terms of gender, men and women each account for 50%, but the content they focus on differs greatly. The top three topics that women are most interested in are food, entertainment gossip, and fashion, while men are most interested in social news, technology IT, and travel. More interestingly, 55.1% of Weibo users are single, 20.3% are in love, and 14% are married without children.In terms of Internet access devices, smartphones account for 83%, indicating that mobile Internet has penetrated into people's lives. Eight o'clock pm to midnight is the peak time for brushing Weibo, and Weibo has become a sleep aid tool.The report shows that about 40% of users are peers with their friends, and their consumption level and income level are also very close. 27.6% of users buy products through short links on Weibo, and books/audiovisual, catering/entertainment group buying, and clothing/shoes/bags are the three largest categories of products purchased through Weibo traffic diversion.
