You're a lucky man to have a wife like that.

by meimei234 on 2012-03-06 15:15:47

If you have such a wife, then you are truly happy.

If you have such a wife, every morning when you get up, she will quietly watch you wash your face, brush your teeth, and get dressed. Before you go to work every morning, you must eat the fruit and milk she prepares for you. Even if you occasionally forget, she will always remind you.

Before you go home in the evening, she always asks what you want to eat. Even if she finishes work hours later than you, she will still cook for you by hand. If you want to cook, she will be your assistant, standing by your side - you stir-fry, she adds seasoning... There is never a lack of your favorite drinks in the fridge - there's milk, coffee, bubble tea, sago pudding, cold water... Never missing.

The house is always neat and clean. When you open the door after dragging your tired body home, you always feel an indescribable sense of ease, and the feeling of fatigue instantly disappears without a trace. Although it's just a small single room, it makes you feel a sense of satisfaction you've never felt before. The moment she opens the door after work, your heart is filled with happiness and contentment. Life is actually like this...

She loves you and also loves your family. Perhaps women's emotions are so delicate. She cares more about your family than you do. When you haven't gone back to see them for a few days, she always urges you to go back and take a look. Sometimes things you haven't thought of, she has already considered much earlier. ***When she goes back to her hometown, she will tell you to make more phone calls home because she knows that the elders actually want you to call home more often. This shows that you're still thinking of them and caring for them.

Occasionally, when you come home drunk, she will help you wash your face, brush your teeth, and even give you a bath. She will massage you.

She never gives you a bad attitude, even if you make mistakes. She won't lose her temper but will gently talk to you, making you feel an urge to change your bad habits.

The clothes, pants, and even underwear she buys for you are all carefully selected by her.

No matter how late it is at night, as long as you say you're thirsty or hungry, she will get up to pour you water or prepare food.

When you go shopping, she will dress beautifully, tightly holding your hand, making you feel both happy and gentlemanly.

You two will never argue over trivial matters in life, but only over emotional issues because she cares about you and values you deeply. Each argument never lasts overnight. As long as you comfort her a little, she won't be angry anymore and will jump into your arms like a rabbit.

......

......

If you have such a wife, would you leave her? Could you bear to give her up? Could you bear to leave her alone?
