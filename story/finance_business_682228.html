Ministry of Land and Resources: The national land data at a unified time point will be aggregated by the end of the year.

by z3bsoa45119 on 2012-02-28 18:28:26

China News Service, July 24 - According to the website of the Ministry of Land and Resources, regarding the next steps in the second national land survey, the television teleconference on the notification of land resources management work requires that all efforts be made to ensure the successful completion of all tasks of the second survey. By the end of this year, a unified update of the change survey data will be carried out, and a unified national land data at the same time point will be summarized.

Currently, the external field investigation of rural land across the country has been fully completed, and the urban land survey is being actively promoted. The next step is to do six tasks:

First, accelerate progress and continue to promote the overall advancement of the second survey work. Continue to strengthen various guarantee measures, increase the intensity of supervision and inspection, and ensure the timely completion of phased tasks. Fully promote the construction of basic databases such as land registration and the "one map" project. It is necessary to not only investigate the changes in land use since the launch of the second survey, but also investigate the changes in land use in 2009, and report the data results of both aspects to the National Land Survey Office to form a survey result report.

Second, increase efforts to ensure the quality of the survey results. The National Land Survey Office should organize forces to further increase the intensity of internal and external field verification, strictly review the data results reported by local areas, and focus on checking provinces with significant increases and decreases in arable land.

Third, in accordance with requirements, standardize the completion of the basic farmland survey and mapping. It is necessary to effectively improve the understanding of the importance of the basic farmland survey and mapping, strengthen leadership, and have the main leaders personally take charge to form coordination and linkage among departments such as planning, cultivated land protection, and land registry. Adhere to standardized mapping, focusing on collecting and organizing basic materials, mapping according to the current protection status, writing analysis and inspection reports, adjusting basic farmland, and accepting mapping results, ensuring consistency between maps, databases, and actual sites. Accelerate the progress of mapping work, adjust and improve the mapping work plan, and advance the mapping work. The ministry has clearly stated that for regions that fail to complete the mapping task as required, the results of the second survey will not be accepted, and the revision of the land use总体规划will not be approved.

Fourth, strengthen mediation and effectively carry out the work of confirming and registering rural land ownership to ensure the basic completion of the tasks of registering collective land ownership and state-owned land use rights within the scope of rural areas, serving the needs of rural reform and development. During the process of work, focus on coordinating the resolution of ownership disputes, resolving contradictions, and clarifying property rights.

Fifth, strengthen overall planning and accelerate the construction of the national land database. According to the construction plan of the national database management system, summarize the data results of the second survey; strengthen land data analysis, and propose policy recommendations for strengthening various management. Focusing on the "one map" project construction, integrate and consolidate the data and results of the second survey, establish a national land database integrating remote sensing imagery maps, land use status maps, basic farmland data, and land ownership data, providing support services for building a unified supervision platform for land and resources.

Sixth, create an atmosphere and further effectively carry out publicity and public opinion work, focusing on reflecting the overall progress of the work, summarizing and reflecting a number of good examples, and investigating and exposing a batch of cases of falsification. (Reporters Liu Zhen Guo and Lyu Yuan Juan)
