Unemployed man scams both money and sexual favors in 49 cases, involving over 560,000 yuan

by lovei0c1 on 2012-03-06 14:00:37

Our agency news (reporter Li Sheng, correspondent Lan Xuan) - With his good looks and eloquence, a 39-year-old unemployed man, Zheng Moumou, deceived the trust of several women by pretending to date them or suggesting doing business together. He was recently arrested by the Xigu police after a report was made. The investigation uncovered 49 fraud cases involving 12 verified female victims, with a total amount of defrauded money reaching over 560,000 yuan.

Report: Man deceiving people everywhere

In early February this year, the Criminal Investigation Team of the Xigu Public Security Bureau received three reports from women who had met a man while driving unlicensed taxis. They were deceived by his sweet talk into lending him tens of thousands of yuan for his supposed business ventures. Afterward, the man disappeared, only occasionally answering their calls with various excuses. Through further investigation, the police learned that these three women all owned private cars and drove unlicensed taxis in Xigu. Their encounters with the man were almost identical: after getting into the car, the man would introduce himself as being in the oil business and frequently needing rides. After exchanging contact information, he would call for rides every few days, often giving gifts or treating them to meals. Once they became familiar, the man would start borrowing money under the pretense of doing business together, with each woman losing at least 20,000-30,000 yuan.

Arrest: Deceiver from Jingning finally caught

Based on the clues provided by the complainants, the police conducted extensive investigations and arrested Zheng Moumou on February 26th. Upon interrogation, it was revealed that Zheng Moumou is 39 years old, from Jingning County, and rented a house in Xigu District. Relying on his good looks and eloquence, he observed and manipulated the psychology of women, gaining their trust through small favors.

The investigating officers told reporters that Zheng Moumou, based on his accumulated experience, had been deceiving women for many years both financially and sexually. Due to the strong self-esteem of most of the deceived women, they were reluctant to speak out, allowing him to remain free for many years. Investigations showed that apart from the already verified 12 women, there are other female victims who have not reported the incidents.

Confession: Embezzled 560,000 yuan, lost it all gambling

In October last year, Zheng Moumou met Liu Ting (a pseudonym), sister of Liu Mou who worked in a net bar. Liu Ting, from a rural area in Pingliang, believed she had found true love after dating Zheng Moumou. During the Spring Festival this year, Liu Ting introduced her "fiancé" to her parents. Taking advantage of the situation, Zheng Moumou extended his greedy hands towards Liu Ting's family. After staying at Liu Ting’s home for a few days, Zheng Moumou claimed that he needed funds for his business and persuaded Liu Ting to borrow money from her family. Her family managed to gather 50,000 yuan for him. Unexpectedly, upon returning to Lanzhou, Zheng Moumou vanished without a trace.

A similar incident involved Wang Juan (a pseudonym), a teacher from a school in Lin洮. After meeting Zheng Moumou online, she fell deeply for his charm. On a weekend in September last year, she specially came to Lanzhou to meet him. After dinner, at the hotel where they checked in, Wang Juan went to take a shower. When she came out, she found that Zheng Moumou had left the room, taking her mobile phone and over 2,000 yuan in cash with him.

According to the police, among the women deceived by Zheng Moumou, there were government officials, teachers, and enterprise employees, but more commonly, they were migrant women workers. In the 49 cases initially confessed by Zheng Moumou, the police have confirmed 12 female victims, with a total defrauded amount exceeding 560,000 yuan, the largest single loss being 140,000 yuan. All the money was gambled away in electronic game halls by Zheng Moumou, who was addicted to gambling.
