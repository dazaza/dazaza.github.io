The ripples on the lake reflect the sky and water in dazzling colors; in the Year of the Dragon, the screen opens for prosperity - Lianchengfa Group.

by lcfledcom on 2012-02-08 15:06:15

In the prosperous year of the dragon and at the beginning of the new year, Lianchengfa launched its grand 2012 opening screen in Lianshui, Jiangsu Huai'an. As one of the key large-scale screen projects for the start of 2012, the outdoor P10 full-color LED display screen project in Lianshui, Jiangsu Huai'an, undoubtedly marks the opening work of Lianchengfa Technology (www.lcf-led.cn). This project follows the successful installation of Lianchengfa's P4 high-definition full-color LED display screen at Hubei TV station, making it another landmark project. According to information from the customer service center, this LED display screen is located in Lianshui County, Jiangsu Huai'an, and is a P10 outdoor full-color LED display screen produced by Lianchengfa Technology, with a total area of 274.5 square meters. Before the press release, the project has been successfully accepted. During the acceptance process, its high clarity and high quality were highly praised and recognized by the customers.

After the completion of the LED display screen project, it will mainly be used to release the latest news and timely advertising information in the region. The high-definition display effect will attract significant attention and add brilliance to Lianshui County in Jiangsu Huai'an.

According to the staff of Lianchengfa Technology who visited Lianshui city, if we talk about cities, and then specifically about water within those cities, Lianshui county boasts a charming ripple lake that is certainly worth boasting about. Like many famous lakes, Ripple Lake is renowned for its water and sky scenery. Especially during sunrise and sunset, and under clear winds and bright moons, the views change with each season, presenting unique characteristics. Hence, it also has the nickname "Sun Moon Lake."

In the winter mornings, the sun appears purplish-red on the frozen lake surface, resembling a large ripe tomato; during winter nights, the pure white moon casts a chilling glow over the lake, blending moonlight and water color into a cold, unified world. If viewed from above, the thousand-acre mirror-like lake surface looks like a shining pearl embedded in the heart of Lianshui City, sparkling under the sunlight. At night, when seen from above, the elliptical lake surface resembles a moon, the seemingly connected yet separated five islands look like the cassia tree in the moon, and the entire Five Islands Park becomes a hazy poem, an ancient story, and a light painting.
