Any time an individual refers to a polo tank top or golf attire, they are essentially referring to a tennis shirt. This type of shirt features a collar, a few buttons at the front placket, and side slits at the bottom. It may or may not have a pocket. The most commonly used knitted material for making a tennis tank top is pique cotton. Sometimes polyester, merino wool, or synthetic fibers are also used. It was a Frenchman named Rene Lacoste, a 7-time tennis Grand Slam champion, who first introduced tennis clothing. He thought of making changes in tennis attire because, during the 19th to early 20th century, tennis players wore long-sleeved shirts, trousers, and ties to the court. These outfits were more suited for office workers since the stiff collars and ties were too rigid for comfort. Lacoste's design, named after himself, consisted of a thick collar, a loose non-starched pique cotton shirt with side slits at the bottom and a longer shirt-tail at the back. The reason for the easily upturnable collar, which was heavier than the shirt, was to protect the tennis player's neck from sunburns. Interestingly, polo players had already been using this design since the late 19th century. Before Lacoste's tennis clothing design made its debut in 1929, polo players wore heavy Oxford-cloth cotton long-sleeved shirts with button-down collars. Although the collar design kept the wind from constantly flapping it down, the polo shirt back then was generally inconvenient to use. Therefore, when they discovered Lacoste's line of tennis shirts in the 1930s, they didn't hesitate to adopt his design. It wasn't long before the term "polo shirt" became synonymous with a golf top. In 1967, the popularity of men's polo shirts skyrocketed when Ralph Lauren included men's polo shirts as a key product in his original clothing line called Polo. Although these shirts weren't meant to be worn for polo games, Laurens' men's polo shirts were an excellent replica of the then official polo attire. In the 1990s, the tennis shirt became the standard business casual/casual attire for the high-tech industry and then spread to other sectors. Business logos are often printed on these golf shirts to be used as custom corporate and business apparel. Some government officials in India wear tennis shirts as regular work attire. Because golf shirts are closely associated with sports, they come in all sorts of light shades. The most common color is white. The modern golf shirt has a specific feature called a tennis butt. This design is derived from Lacoste's original, where the back is cut a few centimeters longer than the front so that when the tennis player leans forward, the shirt still remains tucked properly into their pants. Moreover, the tennis shirt collar can be easily turned up to protect the player's neck from the sun. The tennis tank top or polo tank top will continue to occupy a distinct niche in the fashion industry, regardless of changing styles and times. It has become an essential wear in our lives.
