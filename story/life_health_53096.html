Reporters follow up on women's entrepreneurship, starting with franchises!

by shouyaocha on 2008-03-12 23:58:24

Hand-shaken for nine years

Reporter's tracking report: Women's entrepreneurship, starting with franchising!

Ms. Liu was unfortunately laid off at first, and with children to take care of, she suddenly lost confidence in life. Later, under the guidance of her friends, she franchised a tea drink shop and worked hard for nine years, deeply embedding her brand in the hearts of consumers. Yes, this is Youzi Hand-shaken Tea, a tea drink store famous for its exquisite hand-shaking process.

According to her account, in the early days, through the learning method of franchising, she was exposed to more tea drinks that surpassed traditional ones. The master leads you to the door, but cultivation depends on the individual. In the service industry, there are no formulas to follow. Food culture is also defined by personal taste preferences. In the fiercely competitive tea drink market, where there is a small shop every three steps and a large one every five, only by always keeping fresh and enthusiastic can you better win the hearts of consumers. Moreover, choosing good tea, knowing how to brew it, and shaking it are just basic skills. You also need to understand the nature of tea, how to shake it, and how to adjust the taste that each consumer likes. This still requires mastering the art of tea.

Indeed, it is true!

The starting point of Youzi Hand-shaken Tea lies in novelty and skill. The full combination of creative ideas has elevated tea drinks to a new level, no longer confined to rigid production processes. With dynamism as its feature, it satisfies consumers' needs from different angles, not only in terms of taste but also providing corresponding visual impact.

Finally, Ms. Liu said that the path of entrepreneurship is difficult. The market is filled with cutthroat price competition and inferior substitutes. However, as long as one holds firm beliefs and shakes the tea with passionate hands, it will surely capture consumers while improving oneself, making the business increasingly prosperous.
