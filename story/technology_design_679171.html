Principles and taboos of men's clothing coordination in different occasions

Men's clothing coordination is directly related to one's temperament, age, skin color, figure, and the occasion they attend. Below are several occasions introduced for all gentlemen.

Location: Company affairs: Meeting with clients

Dressing rules to follow: Simplicity and tidiness are the primary principles of work attire, especially important for men. No one would willingly deal with a man who dresses sloppily. Moreover, the quality of the clothes is equally important for men; the difference between a high-quality suit and a low-quality one is very obvious.

Coordination plan: A suit is undoubtedly the best choice, and darker-colored suits are more appropriate as they make you look more serious and reliable. You need to pay attention to coordinating shirts, ties, and shoes. Accessories such as stationery, business card holders, belts, etc., are also important details that reflect your taste.

Trendy choices: 1. This season's men's fashion trend embraces tradition, with various brands offering authentic suits, providing you with a wide range of choices. 2. Shirts should be chosen in solid colors or fine patterns, fully demonstrating an understated discipline. 3. Ties are a more personalized choice, allowing you to coordinate based on your own preferences. 4. Stationery, business card holders, and similar accessories are best chosen from well-known brands, NTNY being a great option.

Taboos: 1. Unless you are the owner of the company, do not wear printed shirts. 2. Do not wear the same pair of shoes for three consecutive days. 3. Do not wear white-colored suits. 4. Within three days, your suit, shirt, and tie should not repeat. 5. Do not spray strong perfume.

Location: A coffee shop affair: Dating with her

Dressing rules to follow: This is a time when you need to carefully groom yourself, but do not dress too formally or overly flashy. Keeping your style is most important. Adjust your clothing according to different dating locations.

Coordination plan: If the dating location is a high-end coffee shop, you can dress more meticulously, such as wearing silk-textured shirts and formal leather shoes. If the dating location is a regular coffee shop, you can dress more casually.

Trendy choices: 1. Printed shirts are a good choice and can be paired with casual pants, but the prints should not be too bizarre. 2. Denim jeans can also be considered as they have become increasingly fashionable. 3. Lightly spraying a wooden fragrance perfume is acceptable.

Taboos: 1. Do not wear a full suit, as it may scare the other party, because this is not a blind date. 2. In a high-end coffee shop, do not wear sportswear or athletic shoes.

Location: Luxury restaurant affair: Attending a banquet

Dressing rules to follow: For a grand occasion, naturally, you should dress more formally. Generally speaking, the suit you wear to work will suffice, as men ultimately cannot compare with women who have dazzling evening gowns to choose from (accept it!).

Coordination plan: If it's just a regular dinner, a well-fitted suit is enough. If it's a more formal event, you can wear an "NTNY" suit and insert a pocket square into your chest pocket, becoming a graceful British gentleman.

Trendy choices: 1. The safest choice is a pure black suit paired with a pure white shirt; if the banquet is not overly formal, there's no need to wear a tie, just unbutton one button at the collar. 2. If you have enough confidence in yourself, you can boldly wear a pure white suit, which will make you stand out. 3. You must wear formal leather shoes.

Taboos: 1. Do not wear a coat to the banquet; it should be left in the cloakroom. 2. Do not appear carrying a briefcase. 3. Try not to go straight from the company to the banquet; if time allows, take a shower and change into clean clothes to avoid looking dusty.

Location: Fitness club affair: Exercise and Keep Fit

Dressing rules to follow: Comfort is still the top priority for exercise attire. If you don't like tight-fitting workout clothes, you can choose looser ones as long as they facilitate your movement.

Coordination plan: Workout clothes should be paired with athletic shoes; do not wear other types of shoes. If you're not too confident about your body shape, you should choose darker-colored workout clothes.

Trendy choices: 1. The sports series of sports brands is naturally your choice, as they have strict classifications that can suit your different exercise needs. 2. Many famous men's clothing brands also offer sports series clothing, with thoughtful designs suitable for those who care about their image. 3. Appropriately spraying sports-type perfume can help you avoid embarrassing body odor and boost your confidence.

Taboos: 1. Do not wear any non-sports clothing, as it will make you look outdated. 2. Do not wear your school sports shirts, as faded patterned sports shirts will make you look silly. 3. Do not wear any jewelry. 4. Do not wear clothes that are not breathable or sweat-absorbent.

Location: Popular club affair: Attending a friend's party

Dressing rules to follow: Tight-fitting clothes will always be your first choice. These outfits will make you appear extremely sexy and become the focal point of women's attention. Additionally, some high-class accessories will be indispensable.

Coordination plan: Exaggerated and eye-catching clothes are worth recommending. A noisy night club is the space where you can release yourself. You can deliberately dress up a bit more beautifully and spray strong-smelling perfume.

Trendy choices: 1. This season's popular low-waist jeans are a hot choice, paired with high-quality silk tops, making you irresistibly attractive. 2. If the weather gets colder, a simple leather jacket is a great choice, paired with a pair of boots, making you stylish. 3. Accessories such as necklaces, bracelets, and rings are now the time to show up, but the craftsmanship must be exquisite, otherwise, you'll look like a trendy teenager.

Taboos: 1. Absolutely do not wear a suit, otherwise, you'll find yourself in the wrong place. 2. Try not to appear carrying a handbag, and briefcases are strictly prohibited. 3. Do not dress too flashy, otherwise, it may cause misunderstandings.
