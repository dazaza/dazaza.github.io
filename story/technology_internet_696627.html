### Best Free Open Source Tax Preparation Software Download Reviews

Open Tax Solver is free and open source tax preparation software that runs on Microsoft Windows, Linux, and Apple Mac operating systems. It can help a taxpayer easily calculate tax form entries and prepare income tax returns. This system includes versions for US 1040 and Schedules A, B, C, D, and provides forms for several U.S. states, Canada, and the UK.

To download the Windows version of Open Tax Solver, just click the "Download Now!" button on its website and download the Zip file. Then unzip it to your hard disk and run payroll_deduction.exe. Input numbers such as total federal or state taxes and taxes deduction into the pop-up window. A user can easily calculate the payroll reduction.
