Relying on the World Expo to Build a Wave of Station Construction, com Domain Names Spark Another Investment Boom

by now on 2010-04-14 11:52:43

On December 3, 2002, China was awarded the right to host the 2010 World Expo after voting by the General Assembly of the International Exhibition Bureau. After eight years of preparation, the Shanghai World Expo will begin on May 1, 2010 and end on October 31, lasting for a total of 184 days, offering the world a grand feast of human culture.

With the completion of the Shanghai Pavilion on April 11, the Shanghai Expo officially enters the final 20-day countdown, further fueling the already heated tourism around the Expo. For those who cannot be present due to various reasons, the online Expo becomes their best choice to experience the world from home. Led by the official Expo website expo2010.cn, a wave of Expo website creation has swept across the internet. On the 30th day countdown to the opening of the Shanghai Expo, nearly 40 Chinese news websites simultaneously launched "Expo Channels" or "Expo Specials," collectively sounding the "call to arms" for welcoming the Expo.

China's total number of internet users has reached 360 million, and with global attention, it is foreseeable that starting from April 30, the Expo will bring enormous benefits to China's internet economy. Investors have already focused on internet site building, seeking appropriate opportunities to gain maximum economic benefits in this cultural feast.

In such a bustling wave of website construction, the crucial role of domain names cannot be overlooked. Whether for individuals or enterprises, choosing a domain name requires particular caution. A domain name is the eye of a website; through it, the website’s features, products, and services can be perfectly presented to users. In the vast and varied internet, a domain name is like the title of an article. If the title is not attractive, it will be difficult to capture readers' interest. Without readers, no matter how good the content of the article is, it cannot reflect the value it should possess.

As is well known, the .com domain, as the most widely used international top-level domain, has always held the leading position in domain usage and is the most internationally recognized domain. Many website builders choose it as their first option when building a site, and during this wave of website construction, the .com domain also holds a dominant position. By purchasing related products from Now Networks, adding one yuan allows you to own a .com domain. Additionally, Now Networks offers the most comprehensive range of domain products, meeting the different needs of website builders.

This year also marks the 25th anniversary of the .com domain. With its high adoption rate among large websites and widespread recognition globally, the .com domain remains in strong demand among foreign trade enterprises and enjoys immense popularity among numerous personal websites. Among China's 3.3 million websites, 550,000 use the .com domain. Relying on such a world-renowned event, the value of the .com domain continues to rise. Based on past experience, it is entirely possible for one or more high-priced domains to emerge during this period, which requires domain investors to keep a keen eye and carefully uncover domains with appreciation potential.
