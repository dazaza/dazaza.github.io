Yesterday he said

by ydjfeifeib on 2011-09-23 18:42:51

I said, my sister is not your sister. My sister has never said a bad word about him in front of me. On the contrary, she always speaks well of him. I said that last year was such a hard time for us, but his sister and her husband still resented the hard life here and felt uncomfortable. They also said that if I were at home, they wouldn't come over. They accused me of bullying her husband.

I have never smiled, recounting my views on his sister's family, just like the latest lines from Guo Degang's crosstalk shows. To sum it up, she doesn't treat me as family, so why should I treat her as family? Moreover, regarding the matter of coming to our house, she hasn't mentioned a single word to me as usual. What does she regard me as? He might be helping me, but I feel he's in a bad mood.

I said I find it hard to understand your unhappiness.

Later, he didn't say anything, but seemed a bit more relaxed. I ignored him. He knows his sister's character and can't ask me to do any more.

I asked him if he would be upset if there were conflicts between him and my sister.

He said yes. Upon hearing this, I became even more unhappy. I said it's up to you. There's nothing I can do to change your state of unhappiness. However, I won't mention anything about your sister in the future since you two are blood-related. At that moment, I was very angry.

He asked if I would be upset if there were conflicts between him and my sister.

He said he regards me as his closest person and reveals his emotions to me.

I got angry, I went crazy, I truly hope I never see them again in my life. But I am helpless and uncomfortable because it's impossible to never see them again. Look, we'll see them soon.

Yesterday, he said his sister would come to our place for a week in August. My mood immediately dropped, and I had no smile on my face. Sensitive as he is, he immediately noticed the change: "I feel you're suddenly unhappy." I said, "Yes, how could I be happy?"

I didn't give in all night. I told him I found him strange. I said that given his sister's character, I can manage not to show any displeasure when she comes, and he should consider himself lucky to have married a kind wife. His sister's character is hard to change, so don't expect me to get along with her like sisters. We can just get by on the surface. They are siblings with blood ties, and I don't care how good their relationship is, but don't involve me. I can't pretend to be indifferent.
