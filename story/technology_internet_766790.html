BesTV Microsoft establish joint venture, create family gaming and entertainment center

by anonymous on 2013-10-01 21:31:51

BesTV New Media announced that it has reached a cooperation agreement with Microsoft. Both parties have agreed to jointly invest $79 million to establish a joint venture company. In the joint venture, BesTV will hold 51% of the shares and Microsoft will hold 49%.

According to the announcement, the joint venture is tentatively named Shanghai Baijiahe Information Technology Development Co., Ltd. It is planned to be registered in the Shanghai Free Trade Zone. The main business scope includes designing, developing, and producing game and entertainment application software and derivative products; selling, licensing, marketing self-produced and third-party games and entertainment applications; providing technical consulting and services related to gaming consoles. Both parties will contribute their core resources relevant to their businesses to conduct comprehensive cooperation in areas such as new generation home gaming and entertainment technology, terminals, content, and services.

Microsoft owns the well-known gaming console, Xbox, which has sold over 24 million units globally. Additionally, they are currently developing or operating several popular games such as Ryse, Forza Motorsport (Extreme Speed), and Halo.

BesTV New Media is a new media company controlled by SMG. Its current market value exceeds 40 billion yuan, and it has over 18 million IPTV users. As the world's largest IPTV operator, BesTV also boasts a content copyright library of 450,000 hours. BesTV is committed to building a new media industry cluster, with its IPTV, smart TV, OTT, mobile TV, and other new media businesses covering more than 20 provinces and cities in mainland China as well as Southeast Asia.

The announcement stated that this cooperation will promote a comprehensive enhancement of BesTV’s "one cloud, multiple screens" business-related technologies, platforms, terminals, content, networks, and service capabilities both domestically and internationally, and will advance its layout in the gaming industry field.
