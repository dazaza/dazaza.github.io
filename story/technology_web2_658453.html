Liaoning Province's opening of the province-wide fitness games: leaders run the "first lap"_2026

by eawdqrxbh on 2012-02-04 00:59:37

Liaoning Province's opening ceremony of the province-wide fitness games held: leaders run "the first lap". Yesterday, the inaugural Liaoning Province Fitness Games was opened at the Shenyang Olympic Sports Center. In the men's 4x100m relay race event, leaders from each city ran the first lap.

At around 10 a.m. on August 19th, with the sound of the starting pistol in the competition venue of the Shenyang Olympic Sports Center, the men's 4x100m relay officially commenced the first edition of Liaoning Province's Fitness Games and Sports Congress.

The first two groups of the race immediately sparked a climax in the sports field, as the runners for the first leg were almost all deputy mayors or other leaders from various cities in Liaoning Province.

Yesterday, the municipal leaders on the field swapped their usual business suits for sportswear. Before the start of the race, the leaders appeared on the track, stretching their arms and legs, practicing their starts on the spot. "Not nervous, quite excited," said Cheng Xiaolong, Secretary-General of the Shenyang Municipal Government, when asked by reporters if he was nervous about participating in this race. "Although work has been busy, I still found time to practice over the past month. Friendship first, competition second, but I will still give it my all, leading the way for citizens, hoping everyone will participate in fitness activities."

When the race officially began, with the command "take your marks", and the sound of the gun, "the first lap" took off in big strides. In just 100 meters, all the leaders gave it their best shot.

It is understood that this sports congress has set up 30 competitive events, which will be held across the 14 cities of the province, lasting approximately two months.

The province-wide fitness games were established against the backdrop of implementing the "National Fitness Program" and welcoming the National Games to be held in our province in 2013.

At the opening ceremony of the games, a thousand employees from Shenyang Dongji Group, who have long adhered to doing radio calisthenics and have continuously received national mass sports advanced units for 24 years, showcased the newly released ninth set of radio calisthenics.
