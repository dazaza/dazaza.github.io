First step: Burn incense to remove false thoughts

As the saying goes: "Drinking tea can cultivate one's character, tasting tea is like chewing on life." Ancient and modern tea tasting both emphasize tranquility. "Burn incense to remove false thoughts" is to create a peaceful and solemn atmosphere by lighting this incense.

Second step: Purify the heart with ice water

Tea, extremely clear and pure, is a divine product nurtured by heaven and earth. The utensils used for brewing tea must also be extremely clean and pure. "Purify the heart with ice water" is to rinse the originally clean glass cup with boiling water again, making sure the tea cup remains undisturbed and free of dust.

Third step: Nurture harmony in the jade pot

Green tea belongs to the category of bud teas. Since the tea leaves are tender, if brewed directly with scalding hot water, it will destroy the vitamin content in the tea buds and cause the tea to lose flavor. It is suitable to use water at 80℃. "Nurture harmony in the jade pot" is to pour the water from the kettle into a porcelain pot beforehand to let it cool down to about 80℃.

Fourth step: Welcome the beauty to a clean palace

Su Dongpo wrote a poem: "Don't laugh at my playful small poem, healthy topic personality shaping disease!, never has a fine tea been like a fine lady." "Welcome the genius to a clean palace" is to put the tea into the crystal-clear glass cup using a tea spoon.
