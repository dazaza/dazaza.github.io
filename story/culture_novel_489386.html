Wu Dong Qian Kun Chapter 124: Displaying Strength

by fanyete on 2011-09-14 18:41:40

Linzhaotian and others came to Yancheng, and Lin Dong naturally had to accompany them. So after a brief stay at the Ten Thousand Gold Commerce Guild, they left and returned to a large courtyard owned by the Lin family in Yancheng. Although the Lin family was considered a force in Qingyang Town, they also had some small businesses in Yancheng, and there were certainly no shortage of places to stay.

Now that Linzhaotian and his group were staying in Yancheng, Lin Dong had nothing to worry about. Nowadays, he was no longer concerned about the Blood Robe Sect, and he had also established a good relationship with the Ten Thousand Gold Commerce Guild. The Lin family could stand firmly in Yancheng.

After returning to the courtyard, they closed the gates tightly and then looked seriously at Lin Dong.

"Little Dong, our younger generation of the Lin family has already been secretly transferred away. Even if the Blood Robe Sect comes in full force, we can still preserve some of our bloodline."

Linzhaotian stared seriously at the young man in front of him and said gravely: "In four days' time, it will be your life-and-death battle with Wei Tong. If you have no confidence, leave now. It's better to keep the green mountains than to run out of firewood!"

"Wei Tong is ruthless. In this life-and-death battle, as soon as he gets the chance, he will take your life. Moreover, at that time, I believe he won't show any mercy to the Lin family either."

Looking at the serious expression on Linzhaotian's face, Lin Dong nodded silently. He knew that at this moment, all the hopes of the Lin family rested on his shoulders.

"Grandfather, this battle has already been widely discussed in Yancheng. If we win, it will be an excellent opportunity for publicity for our Lin family. In the future, our Lin family will gradually move our businesses to Yancheng, and with the care of the Ten Thousand Gold Commerce Guild, our achievements will surely far surpass what we had in Qingyang Town." Lin Dong lightly murmured.

"I know that if we win, it will bring great benefits to our Lin family, but... what if we lose?" Linzhaotian slowly asked.

"Grandfather, we won't lose." The young man shook his head slightly and looked up, his face still carrying a hint of naivety, yet extraordinarily determined.

Seeing the resolute expression on Lin Dong's face, Linzhaotian and others looked at each other in bewilderment, seemingly not understanding why Lin Dong was so confident. After all, Wei Tong was a powerful warrior at the Small Full Yuan Dan Realm.

"Take my one finger strike!"

Staring at Lin Dong, Linzhaotian suddenly roared after half a moment of silence. At the same time, an exceptionally sharp white energy quickly enveloped his arm. Then, he bent his two fingers together, and a fierce gust of wind tore through the air as he heavily pointed towards Lin Dong.

From the look of it, he intended to personally test Lin Dong's current strength!

And this single strike from Linzhaotian was indeed without any holding back. Not only did he directly mobilize the Pure Yuan Gang Qi which could only be used by Yuan Dan Realm warriors, but he also unleashed the Lin family's "Pure Yuan Finger."

Seeing Linzhaotian's fierce attack, Lin Xiao, Linken, and others nearby also changed their expressions. They wanted to intercept Linzhaotian's attack, but they believed that Lin Dong would have to use his deeply hidden mental power.

However, their thoughts were completely different from reality. Facing Linzhaotian's fierce strike, Lin Dong remained calm, and there was no fluctuation of mental power around him. He quietly watched Linzhaotian's incoming strike, as if taking no action at all.

This reaction from Lin Dong also surprised Lin Xiao and others greatly. But based on their understanding of him, Lin Dong obviously wouldn't truly offer no resistance.

Indeed, as expected, when Linzhaotian's fierce strike was just a few feet away from Lin Dong, the latter suddenly raised his hand, bent his two fingers into a sword-like shape, and then, a series of similarly fierce gusts of wind quickly wrapped around his two fingers. That fierce wind carried a faint golden hue.

"Pure Yuan Gang Qi!"

Upon seeing the familiar pale golden energy emanating from Lin Dong's fingertips, Lin Xiao and others were startled. Their hearts trembled, and they couldn't help but let out cries of shock.

"Bang!"

The two fingers collided lightning-fast, and the fierce gusts of wind swept out like a storm, directly shaking Lin Xiao and others back several steps before they managed to stabilize themselves rather clumsily.

The storm of energy overflowed, and the soil in the yard was peeled off in layers. As mud chips flew everywhere, Lin Xiao and others who had just stabilized themselves were shocked to see that Linzhaotian had actually been thrown back, leaving a long trail on the ground with his foot until he finally stopped after stepping on a stone staircase.

"Have you advanced to the Small Yuan Dan Realm?!"

As soon as Linzhaotian stopped, he immediately turned his head and looked at Lin Dong with disbelief, who hadn't taken even half a step back. When Lin Dong made his move earlier, he had clearly relied entirely on his own yuan energy to counter, without using any mental power at all!

Under identical moves and identical confrontations, Linzhaotian was instead forcefully pushed back. There was only one possibility: Lin Dong had also advanced to the Small Yuan Dan Realm!

This result made Linzhaotian and Lin Xiao and others unable to suppress a sharp intake of breath. When Lin Dong left Qingyang Town, he was merely at the Middle Tian Yuan Realm. However, in less than two months, he had directly leaped into the Small Yuan Dan Realm. This speed was simply terrifying!

And now, they finally fully understood why Lin Dong was so confident. With his own strength entering the Small Yuan Dan Realm, plus mental power comparable to that of a second-rank seal master, even facing Wei Tong who had entered the Small Full Yuan Dan Realm, Lin Dong still possessed the ability to fight!

"Advanced a few days ago."

Looking at the astonished gazes, Lin Dong smiled faintly. He knew that if he couldn't demonstrate the strength to match Wei Tong, Linzhaotian and others would absolutely not allow him to participate in this life-and-death battle!

"Good! Good!"

Linzhaotian's old face flushed red at this moment, and the disbelief in his eyes instantly turned into joy. His excitement made him repeatedly exclaim in approval.

Such a young Yuan Dan Realm warrior, not only in Yancheng but even across the entire Great Flame Dynasty, was exceedingly rare. Even Lin Langtian, the dazzling star within the Lin clan, entered the Yuan Dan Realm at the age of twenty. And now, Lin Dong was two years younger than him at the time!

Lin Xiao, Linken, and others were also filled with overwhelming joy. During these two months, the Blood Robe Sect and Wei Tong had been pressing them down, making it hard for them to breathe. Although Lin Dong's delaying tactics allowed the Lin family to survive for two more months, during this period, they still couldn't come up with any effective ways to deal with the Blood Robe Sect. Even coming to Yancheng this time, they had resolved to fight the Blood Robe Sect to the death. However, before fighting to the death, they must first ensure Lin Dong's safety.

In the courtyard, after being excited for a while, Linzhaotian gradually calmed down. Looking at Lin Dong, he sighed with emotion, but his expression quickly became serious and fierce again: "Although your strength has greatly increased now, Wei Tong is not someone to be underestimated. You must never be careless. Since you've decided to engage in this life-and-death battle with Wei Tong, we will accompany you. If you win, that's fine, but if you lose, I will risk my life to bite off a piece of the Blood Robe Sect!"

A life-and-death battle, one life, one death. If Lin Dong lost, with Wei Tong's methods, he would certainly show no mercy. Since that was the case, Linzhaotian and the others were determined to fight the Blood Robe Sect to the death!

Lin Dong nodded slightly, looking at the killing intent surging in Linzhaotian's eyes. He knew that if he lost, Linzhaotian and the others probably didn't intend to walk out of Yancheng alive.

So, this time, he could only win, not lose!

"Wei Tong, I'm going to take your dog life!"

For four days, Linzhaotian and others stayed in Yancheng. This time, they brought many capable hands from the Lin family. It was evident that perhaps it was indeed as he said; if Lin Dong lost, they would spare no effort to fight the Blood Robe Sect to the death.

Given the number of people Linzhaotian and others brought to Yancheng, the Blood Robe Sect, with its capabilities, likely had early suspicions. However, they took no special actions. In Wei Tong's view, the Lin family was merely meat on his chopping board. Once he dealt with Lin Dong in the arena, the Lin family would no longer pose any threat, and whether to kill or destroy them would all depend on his word.

As both sides harbored different thoughts, the four days passed quietly.

When the fourth morning arrived, Yancheng, as usual, quickly became bustling. However, today, quite a number of curious individuals flocked to the arena located in the city center. The Blood Wolf Gang's publicity efforts over this period were now gradually paying off. Many people were interested in this life-and-death battle whose main characters were relatively unknown. They wanted to know whether the young man from the Lin family was arrogant and ignorant or really possessed the strength to look down upon someone like Wei Tong, who was well-known in Yancheng.

While the outside world was buzzing with anticipation, in the courtyard where the Lin family was stationed, a door opened, and Lin Dong slowly stepped out. He looked up at the warm sunlight in the sky, and a cold smile slowly spread across his lips.

"Let's go."

Lowering his head, Lin Dong looked at Linzhaotian and others who had already been waiting in the courtyard. Without saying much, he waved his hand and walked straight toward the gate outside. Behind him, Linzhaotian and others also waved their hands, leading the capable hands of the Lin family closely following behind. Their figures seemed to carry a sense of 'the wind howls cold over the Yi River.'

This time, if they win, the Lin family will be able to firmly establish itself in Yancheng. But if they lose, they will add another stain to the notorious name of the Blood Robe Sect.

Failure is not something Lin Dong allows, so this time, he must claim Wei Tong's life!
