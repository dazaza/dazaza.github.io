Profit Analysis:

Using semi-automatic pen core ink filling equipment, the operation is simple, with an average daily output of around 2000 units. Hiring three workers and choosing to rent a 30 square meter factory in a second-tier city or rural area (or assembling at home if conditions allow), the rent is about 900 RMB/month, worker wages about 60 RMB/day. Each pen sold to distributors is priced at 0.38 RMB (much lower than the market purchase price, or negotiated pricing for greater profit) or the company pays processors 0.15-0.25 RMB per pen. The pen manufacturing industry has a low threshold, is relatively easy to learn, and does not require specialized technical knowledge or diplomas. After simple learning and practice, anyone can master basic pen-making techniques. Elderly people, children, and women can also assemble them. Currently, family-style processing and assembly of ballpoint pens and water-based pens indeed have considerable development potential.
