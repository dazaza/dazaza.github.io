Introduction:

A light comedy reflecting the love, marriage, career, and life of young people in rural areas, it is a bright rural youth idol drama. Through the stories of several young couples such as college student Xie Yongqiang and rural girl Wang Xiaomeng, the drama portrays a group of lively and modern young people in the countryside, presenting us with a multi-angle picture of contemporary rural youth's love life, filled with ease, celebration, humor, twists, hope, and strength. Love is not just for the young; Village Chief Zhang Gui and Xie Dajiao, this middle-aged couple also like the young people, have played out their own dramatic and passionate love story.

This time, both the male lead and female lead are newcomers. Zhao Benshan and Fan Wei do not play leading roles but guest-star as father and son, allowing new actors to take the lead for the first time. The female lead comes from the Beijing Dance Academy, Wang Yabin, who once served as Zhang Ziyi's dance double in the movie "Ten Faces of Ambush". The male lead He Shufeng is a student at the Ben Shan Art Institute, selected due to his outstanding performance when Zhao Benshan visited the school. He plays Xie Yongqiang in the drama. The romance between the male and female leads will experience difficulties and trials, but the ending is happy for all.

Story outline: Forestry University graduate Xie Yongqiang returns to his village while waiting for job placement. During this time, he falls deeply in love with young woman Wang Xiaomeng. Xie Yongqiang's father, Xie Guangkun, has always hoped for his son to become successful and strongly opposes his son's relationship with Wang Xiaomeng. He personally seeks out Wang Xiaomeng's father, Wang Laoqi, to intervene in this marriage proposal.

Wang Laoqi, who was already concerned about Wang Xiaomeng reaching too high, stated under pressure that Wang Xiaomeng would not marry Xie Yongqiang. Xie Yongqiang and Wang Xiaomeng’s love suddenly enters a dangerous situation.

Village Chief Zhang Gui's daughter, Xiangxiu, has always harbored feelings for Xie Yongqiang and fantasizes about his future job. For Xiangxiu's happiness, Zhang Gui personally finds the village chief Qi Santai, who is also Xie Yongqiang's uncle, to act as a matchmaker. The Xie family had pinned their hopes on finding a job through Qi Santai, and Xiangxiu's involvement causes a great stir in the Xie family. To secure Xie Yongqiang's job, Wang Xiaomeng persuades Xie Yongqiang to outwardly agree to marry Xiangxiu. Xie Guangkun turns the pretense into reality, deceiving everyone and gradually forcing Xie Yongqiang closer to Xiangxiu. After a series of incidents, Wang Xiaomeng realizes that there is indeed a certain distance between her and university graduate Xie Yongqiang, prompting her to re-evaluate her relationship with Yongqiang.

Meanwhile, the Wang family asks their neighbor Zhao Yutian to sell grain in the city, but an accident occurs, injuring Zhao Yutian's leg. Zhao Yutian's future father-in-law Liu Neng, worried about the severity of the injury, sends Xie Dajiao to the hospital to investigate. Misjudging the situation, Xie Dajiao tells Liu Neng that Zhao Yutian may be disabled, causing Liu Neng to forcibly break off the engagement between his daughter Liu Ying and Zhao Yutian.

By a twist of fate, Xie Yongqiang becomes engaged to Xiangxiu. The head of the village livestock farm, Liu Yishui, begins secretly pursuing the heartbroken Wang Xiaomeng. Wang Xiaomeng remains indifferent. City girl Xie Xiaomei, who resigned to work at the livestock farm for Liu Yishui, feels secretly sorrowful.

Zhao Yutian's leg recovers fully, leaving Liu Neng regretful. When Liu Ying visits Zhao Yutian, Yutian demands an apology from Liu Neng. Embarrassed by the situation, Liu Neng refuses and instead hopes the Zhao family will come begging him. A psychological battle ensues between the Zhao and Liu families.

Wang Laoqi has always felt guilty about Zhao Yutian's failed engagement and asks matchmaker Xie Dajiao to arrange another marriage for Yutian. Liu Neng knows his daughter Liu Ying and Zhao Yutian are a suitable pair and urgently pleads with Xie Dajiao not to arrange any other marriage for Yutian. Xie Dajiao, feeling remorseful for misjudging the situation earlier, secretly helps Liu Neng. Several matchmaking attempts for Zhao Yutian end in failure, leaving his family in despair. In a desperate situation, Wang Laoqi states that if Zhao Yutian cannot find a wife, Wang Xiaomeng will marry him. Zhao Yutian even pretends to be lame to win Wang Xiaomeng, but is exposed by Liu Yishui.

Xie Yongqiang unexpectedly ends up being assigned as a primary school teacher in the village rather than in the city. Feeling immense disappointment, Xiangxiu regrets the engagement and considers breaking it off. Zhang Gui fears angering the village chief Qi Santai and asks Xiangxiu to endure for a while. However, Xiangxiu develops feelings for Li Daguo, a city driver working at the livestock farm, and distances herself from Xie Yongqiang. At this point, Xie Guangkun finally sees the charm in Wang Xiaomeng and sets out to propose to Wang Laoqi, only to be refused.

Xie Yongqiang solves a math problem on the blackboard: 3+2=Wang Xiaomeng, which results in ridicule from the teachers. Enraged, Xie Yongqiang leaves the school and focuses solely on studying to pass the county cadre exam. However, no admission notice arrives, leaving Xie Yongqiang in anguish. In this pain, Xie Yongqiang becomes more pragmatic and decides to start anew, utilizing his expertise to develop barren mountains and plant fruit trees. The down-to-earth Xie Yongqiang makes Wang Xiaomeng feel the same excitement she felt during their initial romance, regaining her admiration. Wang Xiaomeng also helps him develop the barren mountain. When Xie Yongqiang's fruit tree base reaches a considerable scale, the county admission notice suddenly arrives. Faced with a large orchard and his hard-won love with Wang Xiaomeng, Xie Yongqiang tears up the acceptance letter... Zhao Yutian's matchmaking attempt fails again...

Wang Laoqi hopes Wang Xiaomeng can marry Zhao Yutian and begins a hunger strike at the hospital. Xie Dajiao, protecting Wang Xiaomeng, reveals Liu Neng's sabotage of Zhao Yutian's matchmaking. Realizing the truth, Zhao Yutian takes the initiative to reconcile with Liu Ying.
