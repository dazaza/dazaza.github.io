[Recommendation] The seven-year legendary entrepreneurial road to wealth of a post-80s girl

by jacklewman on 2008-02-04 18:53:23

At the very beginning, I had no experience in negotiation. I decided to use the method of provocation -- sending a letter to the CEO of company A with rather tough words, which generally expressed the following meaning: In technology, we are absolutely the global leader and you can't find a second team like us in your country. If you don't accept our offer, this cooperation will not be achieved because we are also negotiating with company B.

Author's note: The "I" in the article is an outstanding post-80s girl and a typical entrepreneurial strike force member. In seven years, she has created her own wealth world. What does she rely on?

I am an entrepreneur and also a foreign trade person. I am currently the chairman of RT Co., Ltd., engaged in foreign trade and cooperation in the toy field. In seven years, I have experienced setbacks and recovery in this industry and have gained the following insight: Entrepreneurship and foreign trade are like jumping off a cliff. Either you will be smashed to pieces or you will force yourself to grow wings during the fall!

Seven years ago, I was a 21-year-old girl who had just graduated from university and was assigned to a very good research institute in Beijing. Seeing my colleagues content with the status quo and lazy, I spent a sleepless night thinking about it. The next day, I submitted my resignation letter to my boss and left the unit that everyone envied with a backpack, starting my journey south. In Guangzhou, I met my first partner in entrepreneurship, Zheng, and we rented a civilian house and started the difficult entrepreneurial road. Like many entrepreneurs, it was hard but full of hope. Fearless, we chose to open up the international market and carried out technical and market breakthroughs targeting the European and American markets.
