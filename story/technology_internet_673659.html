"Mission: Impossible III" Tom Cruise secretly infiltrates to rescue

by dy5200 on 2012-02-23 18:14:37

Secret agent Ethan (Tom Cruise) concealed his real identity and was ready to live peacefully with his girlfriend. At this time, he suddenly received a call from his superior. During the supermarket rendezvous, his colleague conveyed a new mission order to him.

In the mission, he fought alongside his black veteran partner and two female agents Lindsay (Keri Russell) and Jane (Maggie Q). While breaking into a certain building to find an evidence called "Rabbit's Leg," they unfortunately fell into an ambush, resulting in Lindsay's immediate death on the scene. Accused of dereliction of duty, Tom suffered immense mental and physical pain. After a falling out with his superior, he became obsessed with investigating the cause of the accident, only to uncover a shocking secret in the deceased's secret video. To avenge the dead and bring the mastermind to justice, Tom embarked on a perilous journey to Shanghai to search for key evidence, but ended up trapped in a scheme set by the crime syndicate leader Owen (Philip Seymour Hoffman), and even his girlfriend became a hostage...

Since ancient times, heroes have had a hard time resisting the allure of beauties. The same goes for the super-agent Ethan Hunt who has gone through countless life-and-death situations. Since meeting his beautiful fiancée Julia, he decided to retire from the dangerous front-line agent work, step away from IMF's original duties, and lead a quiet life with his fiancée. The change in work brought new opportunities to Ethan’s life. He began trying things he never dared to imagine before, including marriage. However, facing his bride Julia, Ethan could only conceal his true identity.

However, sweet life had not lasted long when IMF delivered news — Ethan's previous protégé Lindsay was held hostage by the evil arms dealer and intelligence merchant Owen Davien. Persuaded by the director of the special operations department Brassel, Ethan agreed to reassemble his special operations team. This team naturally included some old friends: the computer genius Luther Stickell, who had cooperated multiple times; Declan, the Irish descendant and language expert, who is a transportation expert in the action group. Regardless of flying or diving, Declan's skills were unparalleled. Moreover, Declan excelled at disguise, transforming from an Italian deliveryman to an American tourist, then to a Vatican guard in the blink of an eye. Due to his love for adventure and talent, Ethan decided to recruit Declan as the second-generation IMF agent; there was also a strong and efficient female agent Jane, mainly responsible for backstage work, providing support for Ethan's actions together with other members. Together, they will face off against the ruthless "devil" Davien.

This time, Ethan faces an unprecedented powerful opponent, Owen Davien. This villain not only engages in international arms and intelligence transactions but is also cold-blooded and nearly insane, and knows Ethan's background like the back of his hand. Before one wave subsides, another rises. Ethan never expected that his wife would also become Davien's target, and the situation begins to spiral out of control. The enemy in front of him is unprecedentedly powerful. In this impossible mission, Ethan must traverse the world, overcome numerous challenges, rescue his beloved woman while once again saving the world in crisis, teetering on the brink of destruction. Re-armed, Ethan must collaborate with his action team to start another global rescue mission.
