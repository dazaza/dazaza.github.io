Man Vanishes After Throwing Sulfuric Acid on Wife and Child, Boy Hopes Father is Brought to Case Soon

by sznstejx3q3 on 2012-03-02 08:25:09

One morning three months ago, the husband threw prepared sulfuric acid toward his wife and son, Binbin (alias), who was about to go to school for enrollment. When talking about his father, 14-year-old Binbin's eyes were filled with deep hatred.

The 16-year marriage was like a nightmare. On the afternoon of December 15th, under the guidance of Zhang Haiqin from the Shaanxi Women's Research Association, I met Zhang Ping in a rented house shared by three families. What a face it was: the skin on the entire left side of her face seemed to be torn off, revealing pink flesh that didn't resemble a human being’s at all. From her forehead, eyes to her neck, it was completely unrecognizable as its original form. Looking at her ear from the side, it had already shrunk into a small meat ball due to the corrosion of sulfuric acid.

Zhang Ping told me, "You must think I look very terrifying, right? My two arms are even worse than my face. All these were caused by that person (her husband). Now I have to rely on applying medicine every day to live. I never dare to look in the mirror because I'm afraid I'll be scared of myself." In 1995, she met her husband, who worked as a lab technician in a hospital, through an introduction. Due to lack of emotional foundation, her husband often worked outside, they spent little time together. After two years of marriage, Zhang Ping had her son, Binbin. At the time when she most needed care and comfort, her husband had another woman outside.

"Son has always been my concern since he was born, it's as if this child wasn't his (husband's). I remember clearly, when I wasn't home, he would leave 10 yuan daily for food and then go gamble. He rarely stayed home. Every time he came back, without considering our son doing homework, he turned up the TV volume loud. If I said a word to him, he would immediately hit me. If it weren't for having a son, if we weren't in the countryside where traditions are strong, and if my parents hadn't strongly opposed divorce, I wouldn't have been able to continue long ago. But now looking back, not divorcing him is the biggest mistake I've made in my life. It harmed me and also harmed my son."

After throwing sulfuric acid, the husband disappeared

On the morning of August 31st at around 7 o'clock, while she and her son were washing up in the washroom, her husband rushed towards them with a bottle from beside the bed, splashing it onto her head, shoulders, and arms. Even more shocking was that her husband, as if gone mad, even splashed her son. In a panic, she used her right hand to protect her son, but intense pain spread throughout her body wherever she was splashed. Zhang Ping thought it was boiling water and quickly pulled her son out of the rental room, shouting for help. However, the more she ran, the more she realized something was wrong. The burning sensation on her face was so painful it almost made her faint.

Binbin still vividly remembers the situation that day. Although his mother protected him, there were permanent scars left on his arm. Since then, Zhang Ping's husband has seemingly vanished, and the police are actively searching, but so far there is no relevant information.

When talking about his father, Binbin's face suddenly became cold, expressionless, and silent. But from his eyes, the reporter saw deep hatred.

Son hopes dad will soon be brought to justice

Before the reporter left, Teacher Zhang Haiqin handed over 3000 yuan in treatment fees donated by kind-hearted people to Binbin, encouraging Zhang Ping to live and treat herself with strength. And after discussing with Zhang Ping, plans were made to provide psychological counseling for Binbin every week in the future.

When talking about wishes, Binbin didn't want to say much, but Teacher Zhang Haiqin said that the child's greatest wish was for the perpetrator who hurt his mother and himself to be brought to justice soon and receive legal punishment.

Currently, Zhang Ping's total burn area reaches 15%, her face is completely disfigured, and she urgently needs corneal transplant surgery. Her arms also need further treatment, with all treatment costs reaching as high as 800,000 yuan.
