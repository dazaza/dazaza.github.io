Love desire, power desire, women, the harem... Men have a bloody official career, and women have a harem battlefield filled with gunpowder smoke. These women are very special. They are so beautiful that they can make the moon hide its face in shame and make fish sink to the bottom of the river. They live within high walls, amidst intrigue and conspiracy. They deceive each other, survival of the fittest... So who will be the ultimate victor?

An official's career requires bloodshed, and roses require madness. These enchanting beauties are like gorgeous roses vying for attention. In front of love, many women go mad, let alone when their goal is to win the emperor's love! Once they enter the palace gates, their lifelong mission is to vie for the emperor's affection. Men are destined to shed blood on the battlefield of their official careers; while in the harem battlefield, these alluring roses are also stained red with blood.

Love desire, power desire, women, the harem... How do these women become the most favored by the emperor? Where will their loves and hatreds lead? Some are straightforward, some resort to any means necessary, some are virtuous and set examples for the world, and others are cruelly cunning and infamous... The joys and sorrows of these women, their lives filled with blood and tears, form an indispensable page in grand history.

The Rose Goes Mad

Emperor Gaozu Liu Bang of the Western Han Dynasty founded his empire on horseback. He knew how hard it was to gain the empire, so he cherished both beauty and the empire, not indulging freely in lust. The two women he favored - his wife through shared hardships, Empress Lu Zhi, and the unexpected arrival, Consort Qi - each had their own misfortunes and fortunes. While Liu Bang gave them love, he also brought them endless pain, even an unescapable tragic fate. In a polygamous palace, the emperor could only guarantee the wealth and honor of the women he favored during his lifetime, but couldn't ensure their wealth and honor after his death. This irreconcilable contradiction led to endless struggles between the empress and the favored concubines, resulting in one tragedy of love after another.

The Rose Goes Mad Part Two

Rising from a female slave to become the Empress Mother of the Great Han Dynasty was something Wei Zifu and the Wei family could never have imagined.

Wei Zifu's legendary experiences were certainly due to her own efforts. But more were uncontrollable factors. When the Wei family was at its peak and Wei Zifu was about to ascend to the rank of Empress Dowager, the Wei family met a tragic end with the entire clan being wiped out. More than twenty years of brilliant love, over forty years of wealth and honor, disappeared in an instant. Truly, fate played a cruel joke, causing one to lament. If life could start over, if Wei Zifu were given another choice, would she still choose to meet Emperor Wu of Han?
