"Founding Ceremony" VS TV series "Liberation"

"Watch the movie 'Founding Ceremony', watch the drama 'Liberation'", this is the slogan proposed by netizens. The key tribute drama for the 60th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China, "Liberation", aired on CCTV-1 on September 7. This 50-episode TV series cost 50 million RMB, had a cast of 2400 actors, and gathered many special actors such as Tang Guoqiang, Liu Jin, Wang Wufu, Ma Xiaowei, etc., and was considered a heavy hitter among the tribute dramas for the 60th anniversary of National Day.

But compared to past tribute dramas, "Liberation" is quite different.

Difference 1

War scenes cleverly borrowed from movies

The first episode of "Liberation" was the Battle of Siping Defense, with cruel and shocking scenes. Besides the three major campaigns - Liaoshen, Huaihai, and Pingjin Campaigns, there were also famous battles like the defense of Yan'an and the Menglianggu Campaign. And "Liberation" did not just talk about the victories of the People's Liberation Army; the first episode directly mentioned the repeated defeats at the beginning of the civil war, especially in the Northeast battlefield, which was a less selected topic in past TV dramas.

For the display of large-scale war scenes, "Liberation" differed from previous works in terms of grandeur. Director and lead actor Tang Guoqiang revealed that many war scenes were cleverly borrowed from the movie "The Great Decisive Battle": "The grand scenes are basically similar, but they only have three campaigns, while we have much more comprehensive ones, with many scenes and details they don't have."

Difference 2

Leader characters no longer high above

In past tribute dramas, leader characters either commanded wars or discussed strategies and tactics in meetings, seemingly without private lives. "Liberation" is different; it tries to add ordinary people's lives, making leader characters no longer high above.

For example, at that time, Mao Zedong was waiting for frontline news in the cave house in Yan'an, insomnia set in, so Zhu De invited people to treat him, and the method was a familiar activity for Sichuan people - playing mahjong, so in the cave house in Yan'an, the founders of the republic played mahjong. To highlight Mao Zedong's emotional life, there were frequent exchanges with Jiang Qing and harmonious pictures of playing with children.

And meeting scenes, besides in the smoke-filled meeting room, there were also scenes of meetings in bathhouses and on horsebacks, appearing very vivid.

In the past, Zhou Enlai on screen was always a refined image, but in "Liberation", it showed the fiery temper of Zhou Enlai. At that time, Zhou Enlai's rare anger and criticism against democratic figures' compromise highlighted another side of Zhou Enlai's character.

Difference 3

Leader looks different from the past

Tang Guoqiang plays Mao Zedong, Liu Jin plays Zhou Enlai, this seems to have become a rule, but "Liberation" differs from the past in the looks of leaders.

"The Liberation is one of the best feelings I've had playing Mao Zedong." Tang Guoqiang said, he has played Mao Zedong more than 10 times. The feeling of playing Mao Zedong again in "Liberation" is, "To portray Chairman Mao more intimately and approachably, first as a person, then as a great man."
