Synopsis:

On the small island of Swallow Falls, a young man named Flint Lockwood (voiced by Bill Hader) lives. Since childhood, he has been brimming with ideas and passionate about various scientific inventions, such as shoes without laces, TVs that can walk automatically, flying cars, and monkey language translators. Although his ideas are good, they usually end in failure and disaster. The encouragement from his mother before she passed away keeps Flint from losing heart; he repeatedly overcomes setbacks and finds joy in the world of scientific invention. However, his father can no longer tolerate his son's endless折腾 (trials and tribulations), so he asks Flint to help out in their family store. Meanwhile, to solve the local food shortage problem, Flint secretly works on the research for a super food machine. In his latest attempt, Flint destroys the newly unveiled Sardine Paradise, but his experiment also proves successful. Right before everyone's eyes, it begins to rain unbelievable hamburger buns!
