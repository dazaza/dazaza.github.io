Pug refuses to eat or drink and lies in wait after being given away due to owner's job transfer

by fazhen999 on 2011-09-04 21:53:30

"Pangpang" has been lying at the crossroads all day, and it won't eat anything. Photo by reporter Zha Zhuo of Henan Business Daily.

By Yanyang Wang, Henan Business Daily,

"Pangpang" is a brown and yellow Pekingese dog. A month ago, its owner sent it to a stranger's house. It doesn't know why its owner abandoned it. On July 11th, it ran away from home again for the third time to find its master, but it didn't see its master anywhere.

It lay at the door, waiting for its master all day without eating or drinking.

At 7 p.m. on July 11th, in front of Henan Provincial Salt Company on Weisi Road in Zhengzhou City, a brown and yellow Pekingese dog was lying on the steps, making "wuu wuu" sounds.

The sound was a bit scary, so many passers-by dared not go through here. A citizen stopped the patrol guard Zhu Lianhe and others who were patrolling here, and told them about this matter.

"It's looking for its master," said Grandpa Zhang who has been watching cars here. The dog has lain on the steps for a whole day without eating, drinking, or leaving. "A security guard who used to work here called it 'Pangpang'."

"Pangpang" has just had its hair cut. Its eyes looked a little soulless. When you approach it and touch its neck, it stops barking immediately, very gentle and enjoying the touch.

Grandpa Zhang told the patrol guards that "Pangpang" was raised by a security guard who once worked at Henan Provincial Salt Company. A while ago, the security guard was transferred to another place for work and couldn't take it with him, so he gave it away.

"Who would have thought that soon after it was given away, it came back by itself." Later, the security guard took it away again, but early this morning, it came back again.

The third time it has come back within a month

"A security guard at Henan Provincial Salt Company said that it has come back twice before," said the security guard who used to raise "Pangpang", his name is Xiao Tian.

"When Xiao Tian was here, this dog was always with him," said the security guard. About ten days ago one morning, he saw "Pangpang" run back once, it was lying here motionlessly, unmoved by anyone's call.

"This time is already the third time it has come back," said the security guard. Xiao Tian was here the first two times, but this time, Xiao Tian has already been transferred.

Some citizens passing by bought it sausage, some bought it buns, but it didn't even glance at them.

At 8 p.m., the patrol guards decided to take it to their team first. However, it took them more than an hour to move "Pangpang" over a distance of about 200 meters because it almost refused to move a step.

Currently, "Pangpang" is at the Garden Road Patrol Team. The patrol guards are also trying to contact the security guard who went elsewhere, hoping that the patiently waiting "Pangpang" can return to its master's side. (Tipster Mr. Zhai received a manuscript fee of 50 yuan, tip line 0371-86088666)
