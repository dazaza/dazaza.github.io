1. iFanr (爱范儿) Shanghai Station Small Gathering Event

On December 26, I was honored to be invited by iFanr to participate in a small fan meet-up (#ifanrevent#) held at Kathleen's 5 on the 5th floor of the Shanghai Art Museum (Live Streaming Thread). At the request of the organizers, I gave a presentation titled "Beginner's Guide to Syncing" for the attendees.

Since this was an introductory-level sharing session, the content wasn't particularly complex, mainly focusing on industry analysis and tool recommendations, which could largely be traced back to related articles previously published.

The PPT used during the live demonstration has been uploaded slide-by-slide to Flickr (I'm glad that some friends at the scene and on Weibo liked my PPT; I spent over half an hour the night before agonizing over the placement of just a few pixels...). You can view it directly as an album or in slideshow format. Additionally, there is a cloud storage download link available.

Lastly, I would like to extend special thanks to HP for sponsoring this event. As a memento, I received a very powerful 360GB external hard drive.

2. Sync Control 2010 X'MAS Christmas Prize Giveaway Event

The Christmas prize giveaway event released on Christmas Eve received enthusiastic support and forwarding from netizens, with a large number of participants. Codes were handed out until my hands cramped. By the end of the event, a total of 527 friends participated. Except for a few individuals who provided invalid email addresses, gifts were basically sent out to everyone else.

Due to the high demand for some invitation codes, we even specially applied to increase the number of prizes, but there were still shortages. Fortunately, we had prepared a primary/backup gift distribution plan.

Some netizens reported that after registering using the VIP qualification invitation code they received from TAP, their account was still a regular one. Regarding this issue, we have contacted TAP to confirm, and it may be due to a bug in the invitation system. You can contact me or TAP to exchange invitations (additional invitations are being sent out as compensation).

Currently, there are still a few remaining invitations available. If you're interested, you can apply via email. Additionally, I will also be distributing some on Weibo soon.
