One Year Anniversary of Melamine - The Quality Brand Behind Wei Quan Milk Powder

by dzsw on 2009-10-19 20:47:57

It can be said that the melamine incident in last September was the most serious crisis ever happened in China's milk powder industry. In the past year, not a few milk powder enterprises lost honors such as tax-free brand and China famous brand because of the melamine incident. Many enterprises re-packaged to change image, striving to save consumers' confidence. Now a year has passed, major media have held anniversaries respectively, beginning to look back and reflect. The ups and downs in the past year in milk powder industry can't help people feel sentimental.

Looking at the present milk powder industry in China, as one of the few famous enterprises whose products were proved free from melamine after inspection (in September 2008, the National Quality Inspection Bureau urgently carried out special inspection on melamine in infant formula nationwide, involving 109 enterprises, among which products from 87 enterprises were not detected melamine), Wei Quan milk powder always maintained a low-key style. San Lu fell, all brands were implicated. How did Wei Quan insist and keep "clean" under such circumstances? What do we see from Wei Quan? And what enlightenment does this bring to the re-emergence of China's milk powder industry?

For this, the author specially made an investigation on Wei Quan milk powder, also visited Wei Quan Company, and drew such a conclusion: for food industry, especially milk powder industry, good faith and high quality are always the first thing.

Responsibility awareness builds up gold medal quality

Any enterprise with conscience should consider consumers' interests, public influence and social responsibility other than commercial benefits. Enterprises and products are the same as people. To gain market recognition and consumer favor, they need trustworthy character or personality. Su Shoubin, general manager of Wei Quan introduced that Wei Quan is doing business with a strong sense of mission.

When talking about the melamine incident causing earthquake in China's dairy industry, Su Shoubin said: "Melamine brought us many warnings in dairy product industry, it was also a very good learning opportunity for Wei Quan."

Wei Quan withstood the test of melamine incident because Wei Quan always treated quality problems as the first basic task of the enterprise.

If you cannot reach the quality standard, don't mention the brand. If an enterprise wants to grow and become big, if it cannot pass the quality barrier, then this enterprise should not be run. Wei Quan uses 100% effort to control quality. For this, we are always very cautious. For an entrepreneur with conscience, product quality is more important than their lives, Su Shoubin said so.

Quality is guaranteed by management and controlled from the source

Wei Quan talks like this and does like this. In controlling product quality, Wei Quan not only inspects according to national quality inspection standards, but also expands the scope and items of inspection.

Aiming at the long-term existing problem in China's dairy product industry that processing is strong while milk source and after-sales service are weak, Wei Quan made breakthroughs. Especially in milk source which is the key to dairy product quality, Wei Quan chose better milk source to guarantee product quality.

Su Shoubin introduced that, analyzing from geographical advantage, regions between latitude 30 degrees and 40 degrees are usually the most ideal milk producing areas. In southern hemisphere, Australia and New Zealand in this latitude have very natural conditions conducive to producing good milk sources. In order to provide better milk products to consumers, Wei Quan targets at New Zealand, Australia and Netherlands in northern hemisphere in the same latitude, chooses these most ideal milk producing areas to import high-quality milk sources.

It is not difficult to see that the choice of milk source enables Wei Quan milk powder to avoid melamine pollution. But Wei Quan always treats high-quality milk source as something that goes without saying, doesn't make a great fuss over propaganda, which helps build a clean and low-key brand.

Quality is the first step to build up a brand, and building quality absolutely is not enough just by lip service. Wei Quan milk powder example can be used as a warning. When milk powder enterprises talk about social responsibilities, are they really running enterprises with a sense of responsibility? When talking about confidence guarantee, do they strictly manage every link in the industrial chain? When talking about high-quality milk products, are products really worthy of consumer trust! These are questions we need to think about.

Melamine exposes a large number of enterprises to consumers, which also means there are more problems behind melamine waiting to be exposed. Repeating this cycle, consumers will not get really good products for a long time. And now at this moment of reflection, apart from making every effort to fill the void brought by melamine, shouldn't we think more about how an enterprise can take social responsible attitude to shoulder the responsibility of this industry concerning national economy and people's livelihood, and build up brands worthy of consumer trust from the grassroots?!
