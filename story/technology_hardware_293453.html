A Few Demonic Men at Home - In a world of ancient fantasy and alternate history, the female protagonists each possess incredible abilities and thrive like fish in water; but how do antique men from the past survive in the modern world? One word – Chaos! Just look, three of them accidentally end up in front of me. With their wandering lustful eyes, their hostile yet charming gazes, and their gentle azure pupils, my heart beats wildly, and my legs tremble uncontrollably. But, faced with such beauty, why do I feel an icy chill under the bright June sun? Disaster strikes, it's cold...

"Ah! How could you use up all my shower gel! The house is full of bubbles!"

"You shameless woman! How dare you spy on me while I bathe and touch me inappropriately!"

"..."

Never mind, let's enjoy the view for a bit longer...

"Damn! Who put the vegetables and fruits into the washing machine?"

"..." A figure quickly flashes away at the door.

"Ah! Don't put the whole eggs inside!!"

With a loud bang, the microwave door explodes, and the poor man crouches down shaking his head, his beautiful eyes innocently looking at me. Looking at the mess, my heart aches as if bleeding... Heaven! Send a thunderbolt to zap me back to ancient times... Rather than letting them come here to wreak havoc, I'd rather go back and change history... But the result —

Did I really time-travel? And brought those three demonic men back with me? Moreover, the master who raised me like a monster since I was young — turns out to be my father? Allegedly, he's also a great personage? He... because he competed with the emperor for a woman, thus was chased and fled to the modern world with me to take refuge?

Then —

Am I the daughter of the emperor or the daughter of my master? And where is my mother from ancient times?

[The above is an excerpt from part of the article, below is the main introduction] After several people returned to ancient times, they were separated. Piayun and Qin Feng unfortunately got sold to a brothel by human traffickers while unconscious. The madam treated Qin Feng well to get a good price, but she alone abolished his internal strength and martial arts. Finally... Piayun escaped from the brothel and inexplicably became a wanted criminal by the court. Fortunately, she met Ruhai who came to look for her. Under the escort of two gentlemen from the Song family, they returned together to Hangzhou, only to discover that someone was deliberately causing them trouble. And who is the mysterious black hand behind it all...

Ling Wenyu also found his wife, but discovered something strange about the two daughters his wife had adopted. More troubling was that both Qin Feng and Wen Ruhai proposed to Piayun simultaneously. One was arranged by their parents, the other was mutual affection. Among them, various types of women got involved, confusing their situation even more, making their family's Piayun unsure what to do. On the day of their wedding, he married another woman, and she married another man. When will these unfortunate lovers have a peaceful life!
