If contraception is not adhered to, pregnancy will occur again soon. Some women get pregnant again in the same month they had an abortion, which has a greater impact on their bodies. Therefore, reliable contraceptive measures should be chosen, and abortion should only be used as a remedial surgery when contraception fails.
