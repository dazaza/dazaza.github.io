How to increase the viewership of a blog?

by dstoyboy on 2008-04-30 16:07:52

Many friends write blogs with the aim of getting more people to browse them. However, newly established blogs often struggle to quickly increase their viewership. The main reasons for this lie in several aspects. If you are planning to keep writing your blog long-term, you need to make more friends and establish link exchanges. This can directly bring traffic and comments, but the effect is slow and falls under the category of long-term cultivation. So, how can one quickly achieve short-term views?

1. If your blog has its own domain name and independent space, it is recommended to choose a suitable blogging system. For Chinese users, z-blog, Wordpress, and MovableType are highly recommended. The first one is an excellent domestic system known for its speed and good Chinese support, widely used by many bloggers in China. The latter two are foreign systems that have gained popularity due to their good plugins and Chinese versions, occupying a significant share of the blogging system market. A good blogging system will help authors use it easily, allow user comments, and provide a good interactive interface. If some friends don't know how to install it or don't have their own space, they can go to Sina Blog, Baidu Space, 51 Blog, Blogbus, MSN Space. I also recommend our site's Australian blog on Liuliuwang.

2. Good content is the best way to increase views, but that alone is not enough. You need to get more people involved and must increase your blog's exposure. How do you increase it? There is now a great technology called RSS output. Taking the Australian blog on Liuliuwang as an example, here is the link: http://www.6aoo.cn/?action-blog. You will see an RSS output as shown in the figure:

20080430094225265

Right-click, select "Properties", and you will see an address: http://www.6aoo.cn/?uid-913-action-rss-type-blog. This is the RSS output address. How exactly do you use it?

Choose a good RSS output website, commonly known as the "HeShao" plan, such as Feedsky. You can directly add the above output address. After submission, you will receive an address that is easier to use like a reader, such as Xian Guo or Zha Xia.

3. Submit your Chinese blog to search engines. Below are some tested and effective submission options:

Baidu http://utility.baidu.com/blogsearch/submit.php

Youdao Login http://tellbot.yodao.com/report?type=BLOG

Blogcn Search http://www.blogcn.com/search/newsite.asp

Hubro Aggregation Submission http://feed.hubro.net/subblog.aspx

Kantianxia RSS Directory http://rss.com.tv/rssindex

Bolaa Blog Inclusion Center http://www5.bolaa.com/CommendBlog

RSS Expo http://rss.bokee.com

Zhou Botong http://www.potu.com/index

4. Besides increasing RSS subscriptions, some friends can further expand their blog's exposure through bookmarking services. Here are some projects tested by Liuliuwang:

365key Tian Tian Wenzhai http://www.365key.com/member/reg.aspx

Sina Aiwen ViVi Bookmarks http://vivi.sina.com.cn/user/register.php

Yesky Wenzhai http://passport.yesky.com/customer/agreement.jsp

Liuliuwang Bookmarks http://www.6aoo.cn/?action-link

Bookmarking services can effectively increase your blog's exposure, but direct reading effects are not obvious. They should only be used as auxiliary tools.

5. Close comments because new blogs often have no comments. When people see zero comments, they may think the blog is neglected or not viewed, making it seem lifeless. Therefore, the best approach is to close comments until your blog has 50-100 subscribers, at which point you can reopen comments.

6. Keep updating regularly. No one wants to follow a blog that isn't updated. Even if you update just once a day, you should stick to it. Bring new content every day. This content should not be simple copy and paste; write about topics you understand well, professionally. Spend some time reading related articles of interest before creating content. Alternatively, if you feel confident in your writing skills, start writing a long novel, fantasy, etc. This will help capture the attention of readers and eventually build a loyal readership.

7. Cleverly increase blog links by participating in gatherings where blog enthusiasts like to congregate, such as blog alliances and forums. Include your blog link in your signature to increase exposure. Interest is key; if you like something, visit more places and leave your mark. Avoid posting advertisements as many people strongly resist ads.
