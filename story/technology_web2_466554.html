The National Procurement Center's elevator procurement exceeds 70 million yuan in 3 years

by xiaoling4430 on 2011-07-27 11:12:21

From 2008 to 2010, the Central State Organs Government Procurement Center (GPC) completed a total of 158 elevator定点 procurement projects, purchasing 209 elevators with a total procurement amount of 70.25 million yuan.

According to relevant responsible persons from the GPC, since July 1, 2007, the GPC has been the first in the country to implement fixed-point procurement for elevator projects below the public tendering limit. In recent years, whether it is the number of fixed-point procurement projects or the total procurement amount, both have shown a relatively fast upward trend.

Looking at the procurement situation of first-level budgetary units, the procurement amounts of 11 units including the State-owned Assets Supervision and Administration Commission of the State Council account for 71% of the total procurement amount, among which the procurement amount of the State-owned Assets Supervision and Administration Commission of the State Council accounted for 29%, ranking first in terms of elevator procurement scale by the GPC. The Chinese Academy of Sciences and the Ministry of Education both ranked second with an 11% share each.

In the central state organs' elevator fixed-point procurement, nine elevator companies including Shanghai Mitsubishi, Xizi Otis, Hitachi, and Shanghai Yongda became designated suppliers, having the opportunity to share this market. Among them, Shanghai Mitsubishi Elevator Co., Ltd. performed particularly well. I believe that the GPC values quality requirements for elevator products the most. Therefore, Mitsubishi's high proportion in the GPC's elevator fixed-point procurement also stems from our excellent quality and good service. Wang Yong, General Manager of the Sales Department of Shanghai Mitsubishi Elevator Co., Ltd., told reporters that the company has always attached great importance to the GPC's elevator fixed-point procurement projects. After the execution period of this fixed-point procurement was extended, Shanghai Mitsubishi Elevator will continue to do related work well, insisting on providing high-quality products and good services to various ministries.

Volume increase and price decrease: significant results after three years of practice

In terms of elevator fixed-point procurement, the Central State Organs Government Procurement Center (hereinafter referred to as the GPC) can be considered the first in the country to try it out. Since July 1, 2007, the GPC has been the first in the country to implement fixed-point procurement for elevator projects below the public tendering limit.

After more than three years of practice, in 2010, the GPC doubled the number of completed elevator fixed-point procurement projects, the number of elevators procured, and the procurement amount compared to 2008. However, the average procurement price per elevator dropped from 362,500 yuan in 2008 to 321,100 yuan in 2010, a decrease of 11.42%. It can be seen that the GPC's method of elevator fixed-point procurement has been recognized by the purchasers and indeed reduced the procurement cost.

In terms of sales, although there are nine qualified suppliers, the sales amount mainly concentrates in three companies: Shanghai Mitsubishi Elevator Co., Ltd., Xizi Otis Elevator Co., Ltd., and Beijing Hitachi Elevator Marketing Co., Ltd. These three companies split 78% of the total sales amount.
