Why would I still visit your space? Why do I feel a bit sad when I see this post of yours? Did I act too decisively?

But do you know, my heart grew cold first.

Maybe I asked for too much. You said the process is more important than the result, and I know that. But when I trusted you so much, placed all my hopes on you, and saw your efforts... but the outcome wasn't what I wanted; and when you couldn't help at all, did you know how disappointed I was? If you hadn't exaggerated to give me hope, maybe I wouldn't have been so disappointed. Disappointment after disappointment eventually turned into despair - my despair with you. I could only make myself stop trusting you, stop relying on you, and not see you anymore.

I don't want to be the one who foolishly waits for you alone every time. You're not the person I should wait for.

Last time, you said you wouldn't let me wait again. I told you, that was the last time I waited for you. I meant it. I won't give you the chance to make me wait again.

You said, "those things from the past." What past do we have? We were just classmates.

There's something you might not remember, but I will always remember.

During the graduation defense, my girlfriend and I were scheduled in the same session. She defended first, I was fourth, and Yang was third. And you? You just came to watch her defense and left. There were only two people in between, yet you didn't stay. Ironically, I thought you would stay, I thought you were my friend.

That day, I questioned myself: Are you my friend?

Between us, it was only after something happened during the farewell dinner that you started to care about me. You began asking me to go back to your hometown with you; you wanted me to be your confidante; you wanted me to come to Xiamen; you said you would protect me; later when you moved to Fuzhou, you asked me to come to Fuzhou. Sometimes I ask myself, why do I revolve around you? We are nothing to each other. If nothing had happened that night, there would be no connection between us.

Maybe this time, I really hurt you. But I don't want to deceive you or deceive myself. I really don't want to see you again. Between truth and falsehood, reality and illusion, I don't understand you, I don't know what kind of person you are. I don't know which of your words are true and which are false.

Those two movie tickets shouldn't have been for you and me to watch together in the first place.

Sorry, maybe my directness hurt you. But let's just live our own lives. Maybe time will help me let go, but not now.
