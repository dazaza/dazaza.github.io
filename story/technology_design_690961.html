**What to Pay Attention to After Accessory Breast Removal:**

2. **Follow your doctor’s advice** and take medication on time to reduce post-operative discomfort.

3. **Apply ice packs** to the surgical area for cold compression for 3-4 days after the surgery to help reduce swelling.

4. **The effects of the anesthetic will not disappear immediately**, so you may feel drowsy after the surgery. After being discharged, make sure to get plenty of rest. Avoid driving or working at heights until fully recovered. When sleeping, you can elevate your head with more pillows.

5. **Avoid getting the surgical site wet** within one week after the surgery. You can wash your hair four days after the surgery but avoid excessive rubbing of the scalp.

6. **Best to avoid physical activity** within four days after the surgery. Light exercise can be resumed after four days, and the intensity can be gradually increased ten days after the surgery. However, avoid intense physical activities involving bodily collisions for at least three weeks.

7. **Avoid spicy and stimulating foods** that could interfere with healing.
