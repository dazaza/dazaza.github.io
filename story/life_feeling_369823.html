After graduation, I spent every day reading newspapers and attending interviews, and eventually got hired by an insurance company as a customer service representative. Everyone knows that there are many beautiful women among customer service staff. As a newly graduated college student, no matter how I dress up, I can't get rid of the rustic air. So I was mocked and bullied by those glamorous women. I swore that one day I would take revenge on these women. I didn't quite understand, is appearance really that important?
