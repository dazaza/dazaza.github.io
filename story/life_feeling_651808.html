A Person, in the Silent Time

A person, in the quiet time, opens up my heart, letting the tenderness flow. Listening to those gentle love songs by the ear, it's like a sense of time illusion, you cross the time and space, standing silently with a smile before me.

I look up, capturing that bit of mischief at the corner of your mouth, hiding the gentleness I desire, spreading around if there or not. Then, my thoughts suddenly move, then, my heart beats strangely. Reaching out, catching the warmth you throw, a feeling is transmitted through the fingertips, unintentionally rippling in the heart lake, making the clear heart sky full of birds' chirping and flowers' fragrance, with the breeze blowing everywhere.

The music is soft, the atmosphere is ambiguous and easy to fall into. When no words are spoken, can another way be used to express? Such as missing, such as hugging, such as the tacit understanding of looking at each other and smiling. It was once a distant journey across mountains and rivers, all the way through wind and rain, but ultimately couldn't resist the yearning for peace, seeking a belonging.

Smiling silently, in this quiet moment. Thinking and missing is a kind of poison that erodes the heart, yet wrapped in a layer of sweetness. To you, it's a distant appointment, a legend of the earth's old age. Amazingly, it's fate's arrangement, encountering an unknown romance, following your steps, walking into the painting of the twilight horizon.

Slightly tilting my head, closing my eyes, listening to the melody by the ear, entwining a sentimental heart. Clouds flying and wind caressing, the gentleness is just like your whispers. The song turns gently, involuntarily thinking of you, it's like that continuous flower rain, moistening the longing, like ivy spreading endlessly, sleepless and soundless.

What is love? It's the sound of a flower blooming, the mystery of a sudden breeze, the shadow of a drifting cloud. Quietly listening to the fragrant petals, the aroma overflows the blue sky, the clear heart lake, it's the ripples you create when treading the waves, layer after layer. Every corner has your figure, every breath has your scent. Love is the flower surrounding you, blossoming in the light of time. You're the touch of peach red in my spring, a thread of concern in the autumn wind, a series of tinkling sounds in the summer rain, a ray of warm sunshine in the winter snow, flowing with time, gracefully entering my four seasons, watching the sun, moon, and mountains together, carefree and intimate.
