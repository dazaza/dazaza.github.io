Beijing Criminal Lawyer Consultation: Case Analysis of Li Moumou's Robbery, Snatching, and Provoking Troubles Case

by lilan2333 on 2009-11-30 12:27:03

Case Summary:

1. On the evening of November 3, 2003, at around 10 p.m., the defendant Li Moumou colluded with others to assault five individuals who did not wish to renew their contracts as security guards in front of the security guard dormitory at the Fangjia Alley Group Four residential area in the Chaoyang Inner Renovation Zone in Dongcheng District, Beijing. They also forcibly extorted over 100 RMB from these five victims.

2. On the morning of November 16, 2003, at around 9 a.m., the defendant Li Moumou colluded with others to assault Geng Moumou and Qiu Moumou, who were collecting scrap materials, inside the security guard dormitory at No. 59 Fangjia Alley in the Chaoyang Inner Renovation Zone in Dongcheng District, Beijing. They also forcibly extorted over 100 RMB from the two victims.

3. At around 11 p.m. on October 16, 2003, in the vicinity south of the Mai Lixiang Bakery on Fadou Zhong Street in Chaoyang District, Beijing, the defendant Li Moumou used violent means to forcibly rob the victim Ye Moumou of a cloth bag containing 640 RMB, a Samsung T508 mobile phone, a mobile phone battery, a Guangda quartz watch, and a wallet (the total value of the items was 2950 RMB).

4. At around 8 p.m. on November 13, 2003, near the moon gate east of Building 6 in Area One of Fadou in Chaoyang District, Beijing, the defendant Li Moumou used violence and verbal threats to forcibly rob the victim Zang Mou of a shoulder bag containing over 1000 RMB and 14 transfer checks (one of which had already been filled out for 500 RMB).

5. On the evening of October 23, 2003, at around 10 p.m., near the Second Middle School of Fadou in Chaoyang District, Beijing, taking advantage of the victim Yu Moumou's unawareness, the defendant Li Moumou colluded with others to forcibly snatch a shoulder bag containing approximately 300 RMB, a backpack, a wallet, and a Panasonic GD55 mobile phone (the total value of the items was 780 RMB).

Defense Notes:

After accepting the designation from the Dongcheng District People's Court of Beijing and being assigned by the Dongcheng District Legal Aid Center and Huading Law Firm of Beijing, I propose the following defense opinions:

1. The defendant Li Moumou is a minor.

According to various pieces of evidence presented during the court investigation, the defendant Li Moumou was born on October 17 of the lunar calendar in 1987, which corresponds to December 7, 1987, in the Gregorian calendar. Therefore, the defendant Li Moumou was under 16 years old at the time of his last crime on November 16, 2003. Please consider this fact during deliberations. According to Article 17, Paragraph 2 of the Criminal Law, the defendant Li Moumou in this case is only criminally responsible for robbery. Furthermore, according to Article 17, Paragraph 3 of the Criminal Law, please appropriately reduce or mitigate the punishment for the defendant Li Moumou.

2. The defendant Li Moumou has confessed voluntarily.

After being summoned by the police on November 20, 2003, the defendant Li Moumou not only truthfully confessed to the criminal facts of provoking trouble that the public security authorities had already uncovered but also voluntarily admitted to the crimes of robbery and snatching. According to Article 67 of the Criminal Law, this constitutes voluntary confession. The indictment and the prosecution opinion have already recognized this fact, which I will not repeat here. Please consider this during deliberations and, according to Article 67 of the Criminal Law, appropriately reduce or mitigate the punishment for the defendant Li Moumou.

3. The defendant Li Moumou is a first-time offender.

The defendant Li Moumou has never been subject to administrative penalties, let alone criminal penalties. This offense is his first, making him easier to educate and reform compared to recidivists or those with a history of misconduct. Please consider this circumstance during deliberations and appropriately impose a lighter punishment on the defendant Li Moumou.
