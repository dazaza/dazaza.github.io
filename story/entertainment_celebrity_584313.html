Wu Junru, who loves to play in the movies, is still very mischievous outside of them. She has actually had a Sina Weibo account for a long time but has been lurking and enjoying herself. She occasionally uploads rare glamorous photos and even refers to herself as sisters with Zheng Zhongji in his cheongsam outfit. Wu Junru's husband, Peter Chan, is also a lurking expert. He has opened a Weibo account named "Pete the Uncle" and showers his wife with affection.

This comedic star, Wu Junru, has been anonymously lurking on Sina Weibo under the name "Kind Simqua" for three months. Recently, she uploaded several rare glamorous photos on Weibo, completely changing her usual image as a comedic star in films and TV shows.

On November 10th, Wu Junru uploaded a photo on her Weibo. In the photo, Wu Junru has traded her usual short hair for long gentle waves, adorned with a large colorful flower on her head, exuding charm. Behind her stands a portrait of Zheng Zhongji wearing a floral cheongsam. Wu Junru happily wrote on Weibo: "@Ronald Zheng Zhongji, the two sisters are deeply in love, is this the one? Every picture makes me laugh until I cry." (Click on the image to enter Wu Junru's Weibo)

At 12:25 PM today, Wu Junru also revealed her long-haired look from the movie "Eight Stars Report Good News." With big earrings and bangs, the netizens hardly recognized Wu Junru, all praising her increasing allure. The playful Louis Koo wore a wig to impersonate Peter Chan during a visit to Wu Junru's set, making her unable to stop laughing.

Additionally, at 10:44 AM today, Wu Junru uploaded a makeup photo on Weibo. Her husband, Peter Chan, reposted it and flatteringly commented: "You're always the most beautiful without makeup," which made many netizens burst into laughter.
