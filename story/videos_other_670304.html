Punch hard to protect people's livelihood

The latest results of the "crack down on four black and eliminate four harm" special operation conducted by the Ministry of Public Security (MPS) revealed that over 24,000 tons of feed involved in "lean meat powder" cases were investigated, more than 120 criminal cases involving the production and sale of edible oil using "gutter oil" were solved, over 5,000 tons of "sick dead pigs" and their meat products were seized, and more than 260 cases involving the production and sale of toxic and harmful vinegar and other condiments were cracked...

Since the beginning of this year, in order to strengthen and innovate social governance, public security organs across the country have carried out a "large visit" activity to evaluate police performance openly. They opened their doors to understand public sentiment, listened sincerely to public voices, delved into the masses to grasp real situations, and listened to people's opinions privately. Following the standard of public satisfaction, they continuously strengthened and improved public security work, actively responding to social concerns and effectively safeguarding the interests of the people.

"Bright Sword" against infringement and counterfeiting, launching an arrest storm, "crack down on four black and eliminate four harm"... In recent times, multiple special operations deeply carried out by public security organs have become people-oriented projects practicing the solemn commitment of "placing the people at the highest position in our hearts" and "the people's public security for the people."

Curb the rampant trend of infringement and counterfeiting

In a certain auto parts market in Fuzhou City, Fujian Province, dozens of boxes of counterfeit and inferior car parts were searched out from warehouses by public security officers. Facing undeniable sales records, the sales personnel admitted to selling fake goods without reservation. This was a video clip shown at the command and scheduling meeting for the network operation closure action of the "Bright Sword" operation against intellectual property rights infringement and the production and sale of substandard goods conducted by the MPS recently.

According to the unified deployment of the "Bright Sword" office of the MPS, starting from 13:00 on October 8th, led by the public security organs of Fujian Province and with the participation of the public security organs of Jiangsu, Anhui, Zhejiang, and Henan provinces, a network operation closure action was simultaneously launched, attacking a fake auto parts production and sales network and a fake battery production and sales network. This action dismantled these two production and sales networks.

At the same time, the "Bright Sword" office of the MPS used information technology platforms to command and schedule the action process in real-time at the main venue in Beijing and various relevant venues of public security organs. This was the first cluster battle in the "Bright Sword" operation where national public security organs utilized information technology means for command and scheduling. The characteristics of multi-location coordination, multi-police force synthesis, live video transmission from action sites, and flat command were fully demonstrated during the operation.

In the "Bright Sword" operation, public security organs across the country took extraordinary measures and efforts, ensuring significant achievements in the "Bright Sword" operation. According to the latest statistics, as of the end of October, public security organs across the country had solved over 28,000 cases of intellectual property rights infringement and the production and sale of substandard goods, destroyed over 22,000 dens of counterfeit and substandard goods production and sales, dismantled over 6,700 groups involved in the wholesale and sale of infringing and substandard goods, with a total case value of 18 billion yuan. If calculated based on genuine product prices, the total case value exceeded 5 trillion yuan, effectively curbing the rampant trend of intellectual property rights infringement and counterfeiting crimes.

Spreading a tight net for fugitive apprehension

Since May 26th, a wave of fugitive pursuit has swept across the country. Nationwide mobilization, full police force mobilization, and full public mobilization quickly spread a dense net for fugitive capture; "emotional cards", "legal cards", "information cards", a series of effective fugitive pursuit tactics were deployed.

168 - this is the number of fugitive suspects captured so far by the Yunnan Public Security Frontier Corps since the launch of the "Clear Net Operation". After the MPS deployed the "Clear Net Operation", the Yunnan Public Security Frontier Corps immediately launched its net-clearing battle. This battle was filled with the regret and tears of fugitives reuniting with their families, but more importantly, it was filled with the blood, sweat, and even the ultimate sacrifice of many fugitive pursuit officers...

During the "Clear Net Operation", various regions and police forces cooperated with each other, jointly tackling challenges, turning the entire nation into a large battlefield, forming a three-dimensional overall war and synthetic war. The Traffic Management Bureau of the MPS initiated regional cooperation in the "Clear Net Operation" among eight provinces surrounding Beijing, making all-out efforts to apprehend traffic accident fugitives. The Cybersecurity and Technical Investigation departments fully leveraged their unique advantages, assisting case-handling units in promptly capturing a large number of fugitives.

For the people, eliminating harm, ensuring safety

For a long time, the existence of "black workshops", "black factories", "black markets", and "black dens" has seriously endangered the lives and health of the people, harmed the physical and mental health of teenagers, and jeopardized public safety and social integrity.

Since August 22nd, according to the unified deployment of the MPS, public security organs across the country have deeply carried out the "crack down on four black and eliminate four harm" special operation, strictly cracking down on and rectifying "black workshops", "black factories", "black markets", and "black dens" engaged in the production and sale of toxic and harmful food, counterfeit and inferior drugs and agricultural supplies, building materials, and other living and production materials, as well as those involved in receiving stolen goods, gambling, prostitution, and drug-related criminal activities.

Since the launch of the special operation, public security organs in various places have swiftly taken action, prioritizing the crackdown on food, drug, and agricultural supply crimes, focusing on solving major cases, smashing gangs, destroying networks, and dismantling sources. They comprehensively intensified investigation and handling of "four black four harm" criminal activities and enhanced measures for investigation and rectification.

"We must continue to deepen the investigation of clues, forcefully tackle cases, and fully advance the special operation to ensure we resolutely crack down on criminal activities that endanger the lives and health of the people, and resolutely see this struggle through to the end to safeguard the food safety and medication safety of the common people," said relevant officials from the MPS.
