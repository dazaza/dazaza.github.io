Wang Yan's life in a rich and powerful family is so luxurious that it's unimaginable.

by cdz25979 on 2012-03-08 10:56:24

According to the Chongqing Times, recently, the ambiguous relationship between Zhou Xun and Wang Yue's stepson, Wang Sha, has drawn particular attention. This has once again brought focus onto Wang Yue's extravagant life. In most cases, we would think that actresses who marry into wealthy families are like going into a deep sea, but recently, a reporter in Beijing learned from an actor that Wang Yue's life is actually very happy and not lonely at all. Recently, the TV series "Regrets of the Southern City" starring Wang Yue and Li Zonghan wrapped up in Hengdian. A reporter learned from an actor in the show that due to the relatively tough shooting conditions, Wang Yue's husband specially sent a Mercedes-Benz G-class car from their home in Beijing to the Hengdian set for Wang Yue's use. Every time the director called cut, Wang Yue would immediately get into the car to rest, making other actors envious. When an actor asked Wang Yue about the price of the car, she casually said, "It's just around 1 million yuan." The actor told the reporter that Wang Yue actually doesn't know much about cars. When some actors admired her good car, Wang Yue laughed and said, "We have 12 cars at my house. Come over if you have time, you can take a look around." When asked what kind of good cars Wang Yue's family owns, the actor said he hasn't been to Wang Yue's house yet, but heard that the best cars are worth tens of millions, and the ones costing over a million are just for getting around. An actor from "Regrets of the Southern City" said, "Wang Yue often invites the cast and crew out to eat. Usually at one of the better restaurants in Hengdian, several tables are set up, costing tens of thousands of yuan per meal." The actor said that Wang Yue isn't too particular about anything else, but she does care about food. On the set, she doesn't really like eating boxed lunches. Before wrapping up, someone invited Wang Yue to a newly opened high-end seafood restaurant in Hengdian. As soon as they went in, Wang Yue said, "If I had known it was this clean, I should have come here every day." It's understood that the average person spends around 500 yuan at this restaurant.
