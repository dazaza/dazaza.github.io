Beloved for a lifetime, Xi Chen and Fan watch comedy movies.

by hoouk260 on 2012-03-03 19:53:33

After finally finishing dinner, Ye Fan wanted to find an excuse not to go see a movie. However, Lin Xichen seemed determined about this matter, and drove directly to the cinema, which had night screenings. This left Ye Fan with no way to say that she could skip it. If she didn't like one film, they could switch to another; if the timing wasn't suitable, they could change to another time. In short, she would have to watch it, whether she liked it or not.

They bought drinks and popcorn, then chose a foreign blockbuster – an intense disaster film, selected by Ye Fan. She certainly didn't want to watch any romantic love story with Lin Xichen, who knows what he might do during the movie. In the dark cinema, the two sat in adjacent seats, their breathing audible, shoulders touching. It was safer to watch this kind of action-packed film that focused on special effects and visuals rather than emotions.

Lin Xichen made no objections, seemingly only caring about watching the movie together, regardless of the genre. This allowed Ye Fan to quietly relax. If he insisted on watching a romance, she really wouldn’t know how to refuse him since he always managed to twist things his way.

When they entered, there were still three or four minutes left before the movie started, so their row was empty. They sat down comfortably in the middle, a great spot for viewing. Lin Xichen leaned back casually, showing no intention of disturbing Ye Fan as they waited for the movie to start. Yet, Ye Fan felt tense, unsure of what this person might do.

"Am I scarier than a criminal?" Lin Xichen teased suddenly.

"I'm not sure. A criminal is an enemy I face, but you are neither friend nor foe, it feels strange, and I can't predict your next move," Ye Fan replied honestly.

"The next move?" Lin Xichen chuckled, "Of course, we just wait for the movie to start."

Ye Fan was momentarily speechless, finding this man truly insufferable!

As more people came in and took their seats, most were couples snuggling and whispering playfully.

"Are you mad?" Just as the movie began, the lights dimmed, and everyone stopped talking to focus on the screen. Lin Xichen gently put his arm around Ye Fan's shoulder, sitting almost seamlessly close to her. He whispered in her ear, startling her.

Turning to look at him, Ye Fan found herself too close to him. Her cheek brushed against his lips, sending a current-like sensation through her. Hot and soft, her face flushed instantly. She wanted to pull away, but Lin Xichen's arm around her shoulder prevented her from doing so. In the cinema setting, making a scene wasn’t an option, especially surrounded by other affectionate couples.

"Lin Xichen, don't take advantage of this situation," Ye Fan said softly but angrily, "Take your hand off me."

"I'm not. How can I ask you loudly here?" Lin Xichen teased with a mischievous grin, though barely visible in the dim light. Occasionally, flashes of brightness from the screen revealed the smile on his lips. "It was you who accidentally touched my lips. I didn't intentionally take advantage of you, though you smell really nice."

"Lin Xichen, I’m getting angry!" Ye Fan’s tone carried a hint of irritation.

"Alright." Lin Xichen withdrew his arm, sat up properly, and stared at the screen without looking at Ye Fan again.

Relieved, Ye Fan let out a sigh, though a cold sweat broke out across her body. The air conditioning should be moderate, but she felt a chill. She glanced around and saw that no one had noticed their interaction. Here in the cinema, all the couples were absorbed in each other, paying no attention to others.

Focusing on the screen, the images were vivid and realistic. Gradually, Ye Fan got into the movie and ignored Lin Xichen who remained silent beside her.

The movie was indeed thrilling, quickly drawing her into its plot. But just as she was engrossed, a scene appeared that immediately tensed her up. The protagonist fell into a hole filled with countless rats. Though she knew it was just a movie, and the protagonist would be fine, the lifelike and terrifying rats caused her entire body to stiffen. A scream almost escaped her throat.

She wasn’t the only one startled. Everyone around her, including those in front and behind, jumped at the sight of the realistic scene, especially when a rat crawled over the protagonist. Suddenly, the hall erupted with many screams. Already rigid from the scene, Ye Fan shivered at the high-pitched scream of the girl sitting next to her. Instinctively, she leaned towards Lin Xichen, tightly gripping his hand. She felt as if the rat crawling over her foot was right there again. She gasped lightly, instinctively looking down at her feet, but there was nothing except her own tension.

"What's wrong?" Lin Xichen felt Ye Fan's cold, trembling hand. The screen showed frighteningly real images, yet this was just a movie. Why was Ye Fan so scared? "Are you okay? We can stop if you're uncomfortable."

Ye Fan felt her breathing difficult. Closing her eyes, she refused to let go of Lin Xichen's hand, unaware that her whole body was leaning on him. Lin Xichen naturally wrapped his other arm around her shoulders.

Refusing to open her eyes, Ye Fan listened to the constant squeaking of the rats. These fat, ugly creatures dominated her nightmares for a long time.

"It's okay, nothing happened, Xiao Fan, it's alright," Lin Xichen held her, softly comforting her repeatedly. In the occasional flashes of light, Ye Fan's pale face was visible, with tears at the corners of her closed eyes, resembling a frightened child, helpless and sad.

Until the movie ended, Ye Fan refused to open her eyes. She quietly stayed in Lin Xichen's arms, listening to his steady heartbeat. His warm breath caressed her face, making her feel comfortable despite her icy body.

The lights suddenly turned on, revealing it was a comedy movie. Ye Fan opened her eyes, feeling disoriented. After some time, once others had left, she reluctantly stood up and followed Lin Xichen out of the cinema. She felt as if the rat was still around her, its small green eyes glaring at her. She was genuinely afraid, so much so that now she sleeps with the light on, ensuring her room is clean, with minimal furniture, and avoiding clutter. She wished to live in a place with a wide-open view, never wanting to encounter filth or confined spaces again.

Lin Xichen supported Ye Fan's waist as she went home. Although he was reluctant to half-carry her out of the cinema and into the car, it was late outside. He helped Ye Fan sit in the car seat, her body leaning uncomfortably against the chairback, seemingly avoiding something. She watched the slowly departing crowd, new viewers entering the cinema, neon lights flashing. Suddenly, she felt like crying.

"What's wrong? Are you not feeling well? Should I take you to see a doctor?" Lin Xichen asked gently, careful not to scare Ye Fan, who looked genuinely terrified.

Ye Fan shook her head, remaining silent, her face still pale.

"Then I'll take you home, get some rest early," Lin Xichen said softly.

Ye Fan nodded absentmindedly, still not speaking.

The car smoothly parked in the rental neighborhood where Ye Fan lived. Everything was pitch black. "Xiao Fan, did the power go out where you live? All the buildings are dark, and there are many people outside cooling off. Let me go check."

Lin Xichen got out of the car and quickly returned, smiling. "Sure enough, the power has been out since noon today and will continue until tomorrow afternoon."

Ye Fan felt like she was about to break down. How unlucky could she get? No lights, no illumination, she couldn’t sleep alone!

"Is there a place open all night where I can stay?" she asked somewhat unclearly.

Lin Xichen sensed that Ye Fan must have something on her mind, something she remembered. Sitting closer to her in the car, he looked at her, "Is something wrong? Can you tell me?"

"I don't want to be in the dark," Ye Fan pitifully looked at Lin Xichen. Though she didn't want to say it, she unconsciously blurted out, "I'm very afraid of the dark, and also of rats."

Lin Xichen thought for a moment, looking at the hot and restless night sky outside. Then he said, "If you don't mind, I'll take you to my place. I promise I won't do anything. I just hope you can rest well. It's too hot outside, and apart from bars, there doesn't seem to be anywhere else open all night."

Ye Fan nodded absentmindedly, not quite hearing what Lin Xichen said. She just wanted to quickly escape the fear brought on by the movie. Only when the car parked in the garage, and Lin Xichen got out to lead her into a large house, did she vaguely ask, "Where are we?"

"My home," Lin Xichen smiled warmly, turning on the lights. The spacious living room lit up instantly, bright and clear. The furniture was sleek and simple, clearly designed by a master.

Ye Fan slightly frowned, sitting on the sofa. It was very comfortable, allowing her to hide herself completely while seated. Taking a deep breath, she looked at Lin Xichen and said, "Can you give me something to keep me awake?"

Lin Xichen smiled, "Had I known you'd come, I would have prepared some tea. Here, rarely anyone comes besides me, so we only have coffee. Or, I can heat you a cup of milk?"

"No, I don't want to sleep," Ye Fan quickly said. She was afraid that if she closed her eyes, the rats would appear in her mind, causing her to relive the terror of that night.

"Alright, no sleeping then. I'll make you a coffee, adding extra sugar," Lin Xichen immediately laughed, getting up to prepare the coffee. The room temperature was comfortable, the air conditioning already set via his phone before he arrived home. Entering, there was an immediate sense of coolness, with a faint fragrance in the air, probably from the jasmine blooming on the windowsill.

Taking the coffee Lin Xichen handed her, she sipped it, wrinkling her nose in confusion, "No wonder Lu Rui said your coffee tastes worse than Chinese medicine, it's really bitter."

"Haha, I haven't added sugar yet. There's sugar on the plate, add it yourself. I don't know your taste," Lin Xichen laughed, sitting beside Ye Fan instead of across from her. He could tell Ye Fan was tense, but it wasn't because of him. Her past experiences had left her feeling fearful.

"Am I being too out of sorts?" Ye Fan leaned tiredly against the sofa, not letting Lin Xichen move away from her side. She knew Lin Xichen wouldn't do anything inappropriate. Though he was flirtatious, he wasn't vulgar or despicable, just confident and spoiled.

"Not at all," Lin Xichen grinned, "This makes me feel like I still have a chance. At least you trust me enough to follow me here and be alone with me."

"I'm a police officer, rest assured, if you try anything, you might not even be a match for me," Ye Fan casually smiled, "My martial arts in the major case squad are pretty good. Handling someone like you, three or four at a time would be no problem."

"I believe you," Lin Xichen smiled warmly, taking the coffee back from Ye Fan, "Too bitter, don't drink it. I'll buy some good new tea tomorrow."

"No, it's fine. Drinking it slowly will help me stay awake," Ye Fan sighed lightly, saying slowly, "I'm really sorry, but I was truly scared. Those scenes keep replaying in my mind. Today's movie recreated these scenes. I really don't want to fall asleep, fearing that if I do, those scenes will reappear before my eyes."

"What's wrong?" Lin Xichen looked at Ye Fan gently, his voice soft, "Keeping it bottled up isn't good. Talking about it might make it less of an issue."

Ye Fan was silent for a long time before slowly saying, "It was ten years ago. I fell off a cliff and was saved by Uncle Jiang. After dinner one night, he and Aunt Qian sent me home. My parents, if they can be called that, took your money and were counting the sixteen thousand yuan. They didn't want to return the money to your family because of my coming back. But please rest assured, although they greedily kept the money and never returned it to your family after I came back alive, after I started working, I've monthly donated part of my salary under your family's name to support some elderly people and children. By now, I’ve paid everything back. I've recorded who received each donation and when, considering it as repaying it on their behalf."

Lin Xichen paused, softly saying, "That way, you've reduced your own expenses."

"I don't need much money," Ye Fan smiled lightly, "In my daily life, I only have basic expenses, like rent. When I was in the major case squad, I didn't need many clothes, and my salary was decent, allowing me to live comfortably. Now, with no such issues, I plan to buy my own apartment later, so I don't have to live in someone else's house anymore."

Lin Xichen remained silent, surprised by this revelation.

"Well, where was I? Oh, yes, they weren't happy I came back alive," Ye Fan continued calmly, "They said they would marry me off to someone named Li Doutu. My brother disagreed, as I mentioned before. He said that person was a widower over fifty, and absolutely, I shouldn't marry him. But they said I couldn't return to school. However, they were a bit afraid of my brother, who became a construction worker at an early age, working hard to support our family. He was strong and fiery-tempered. At that moment, my younger brother suddenly attacked my brother from behind, and they locked me in a tiny room."

Ye Fan paused, as if gathering courage to face this memory. Lin Xichen didn't interrupt, sensing Ye Fan's unease was definitely related to this small room.

"Then, they locked me in a tiny room, so small, filled with junk—things that couldn't be used or temporarily unnecessary. You know, for people living in such conditions, usable and unusable items are basically all trash that can be thrown away," Ye Fan exhaled deeply, continuing slowly, "There was no light, it was very dark, with only a weak beam of light seeping through the cracks. Above was a small window, no glass, just some randomly nailed wooden strips. Streetlights cast a faint glow inside, very dim. Then, I felt something crawling on my foot. Looking down, I saw a fat rat on my foot, staring at me with its green eyes, blinking. And suddenly, I realized there were many, many rats—"

Ye Fan's speech quickened suddenly, as if seeing those disgusting rats again! Lin Xichen's heart tightened immediately, and he reached out to hold her nervous hand. Her hand was ice-cold, trembling, and her whole body was stiff, her eyes full of fear.

"No one cared about me, they left me alone. My brother had been knocked unconscious by my younger brother. All I could do was jump constantly, but the space was too small. So small that there was only a little standing room among the junk. I was too scared to shout, my brain went blank, and all I could hear were the squeaks of the rats, the fattest ones I had ever seen. I don't know how I survived that night. I learned that sometimes a minute can feel as long as a year. My memory seemed frozen in that moment, unable to escape it. Even now, I still can't stay alone in a dark room or sleep with the lights off, and often suffer from nightmares. Luckily, just before dawn, my brother woke up. He secretly took me away from my parents' house and sent me to Uncle Jiang's home. Uncle Jiang and Aunt Qian took me in and arranged for me to study in another city. I lived with them ever since, and they treated me well."

Ye Fan tried to calm herself but couldn't control her trembling body, "The first time I saw Uncle Jiang being held hostage, I thought the person looked like a rat, sneaky and rat-like. He threatened hostages with a weapon. Uncle Jiang exchanged himself for the hostage, but the man actually attacked Uncle Jiang with the weapon. I don't know where I got the courage, but I thought he was a rat, and I had to kill him, make him disappear from my sight. So, I fired the gun, but I hesitated at the last moment. Back then, I was studying medicine, and I knew the location of the heart. At the last second, I thought, he's still a human, not just a rat, so he survived, was saved. But from that moment on, I decided to become a police officer because, for many people, some are just like rats."

Lin Xichen continuously warmed Ye Fan's body with his own, her pale and helpless appearance reflecting an indelible sadness in her eyes. All of this was caused by him. If he hadn't played games back then, Ye Fan might be leading a normal, happy life as an ordinary woman. Now, although she was doing well, she carried terrible memories that would never fade. He swore to take good care of Ye Fan and ensure she would never be hurt again!
