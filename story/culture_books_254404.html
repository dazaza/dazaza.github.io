During school days, classmates who pronounced "English" as "ying ge li xi" (should be given interest) became bank managers; those who pronounced it as "yin gou li xi" (wash in the sewer) became small vegetable vendors; those who pronounced it as "yin guo lian xi" (cause and effect relationship) became philosophers; those who pronounced it as "ying gai li shi" (hard change history) became politicians; those who pronounced it as "ying guo li qu" (go to England) became overseas Chinese; those who pronounced it as "ying gai lei si" (should be worked to death) became IT employees...

Yu Minhong's Legend - Chapter 1: The Grassroots Becomes Elite

2006 was a year of explosive growth for China's capital markets. In this year, numerous stunning capital events continued to occur: new stock issues attracted the crazy pursuit of trillions of yuan; open-end funds set a new record of 419 billion shares in a single day; venture capital funds grew like snowballs in China, getting better each year...

In 2006, there was another commercial miracle that must be mentioned in the capital market: On September 8th, New Oriental Education Group successfully listed on the New York Stock Exchange. Within just 50 minutes of listing, the stock price soared to $22 per share, naturally —— President of New Oriental, Yu Minhong's personal assets surged by 600 million yuan.

Earning 6 billion yuan in an hour, even the world's richest man, Bill Gates, would have to look up in admiration. And this miracle was created by a local private education institution in China, created by Yu Minhong, a seemingly ordinary teacher of peasant origin, making the legend of the miracle itself even more vivid.

Without a doubt, Teacher Yu Minhong has already become an idol and role model for young entrepreneurs, becoming the best case study for motivational books. This scene makes motivation even more motivational, legends even more legendary.

The world is bustling, coming and going. In a person's lifetime, one meets countless people, from childhood playmates and youthful classmates to colleagues after work and friends made for various reasons... Among this infinite number of people, only a few can become close friends, as emotions need nurturing, which requires time and energy.

After experiencing the ups and downs of life, when people reach middle age and look back on their youth, there are always a few main characters from those awkward years, unforgettable lifelong figures. Yu Minhong is no exception. The people and events in those memories remain so clear in Yu Minhong's mind.

Yu Minhong's Legend - Chapter 3: Golden Age: The People and Events of Peking University

Those youthful, reckless years, those years of trials and tribulations, that sudden quest for seeking and exploration... All those past moments, in the changing seasons, slowly washed away into old photographs, recording fragmented years deeply cared about and cherished by Yu Minhong.

The young Yu Minhong, the pre-fame Yu Minhong, spent the best years of his life at Peking University. The atmosphere here, the scholarly air, shaped a completely new Yu Minhong; the troubles of work here, the disappointments of life here, equally forged a strong Yu Minhong.

In Yu Minhong's fragmented youth, Peking University left a deep impression. Many years later, when he achieved success and fame, he always remembered himself as a PKU person. Because the style there could not be erased, the traces there could not be washed away, just like the elderly Hemingway recalling Paris:

If you are lucky enough to have lived in Paris as a young man,

Then wherever you go for the rest of your life,

It stays with you,

Because Paris is a moveable feast.

Yu Minhong's Legend - Chapter 4: Starting from Zero: The Sun Rises from New Oriental

God closes a window, He will surely open another one. Finding hope in despair, days with hope are always happy.

Zero means nothing, yet it also signifies renewal, therefore it can be unrestricted, giving rise to infinite possibilities, infinite changes. Like the symbol "zero", without a beginning or end, starting from any point, as long as you persist, you can walk a complete path. Getting a start from zero is a wonderful opportunity.

A person can be knocked down, but never defeated. Light the candle, pass on light and hope. The condition for transforming despair into hope is never giving up.

Yu Minhong's Legend - Chapter 5: Rapid Progress: The "Oriental Express" Roars

New Oriental is not only a cause, but also a place to realize dreams. Dreams attract "sea turtles" to return home, while also attracting many "extraordinary people" and "strange people" to co-create dreams.

However, competitors abound both domestically and internationally, such as Wall Street and Li Yang’s "Crazy English." To make the New Oriental train move faster and more steadily, Yu Minhong and his partners didn't spare any effort.

They "don't follow the usual path," seeking new directions for development, exploring new markets, finding a better future for New Oriental.

Yu Minhong's Legend - Chapter 6: Secret of Advantage: Why is New Oriental So Popular

When New Oriental first started, it wasn't much different from other foreign language training institutions.

In "Swordsman," swordsmanship master Feng Qingyang told Linghu Chong: "The sword is dead, but the person is alive. It is the person who wields the sword, not the sword that controls the person."

If Yu Minhong had only blindly posted ads every day, perhaps today's New Oriental would have been lost among many similar institutions. However, New Oriental, with its innovative thinking, sought differences within similarities, grasped the characteristics of the era to operate education, and thus stood out.

Smooth sailing is merely a beautiful wish in people's hearts. Which cause, which life, does not grow through twists and turns, moving forward amidst difficulties?
