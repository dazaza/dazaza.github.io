The girl is pregnant! And there are more than 20 mice in her belly!

by internet8 on 2009-07-21 23:06:48

The incident took place in Heilongjiang, Northeast China. A girl met a major misfortune at the age of 17. At this age, little girls are usually doted on by their parents. However, due to her family's poor condition, she was forced to drop out of school and work in a factory. Her family ran a small grocery store in the suburbs. Her parents were ordinary farmers whose income could only sustain daily life. Though they were not extremely poor, they couldn't save much money throughout the year. Thus, they lived frugally.

However, fate did not allow them to live peacefully. One day, the girl's father went into the house to sort out leftover goods. Since there was no warehouse at home, all the groceries were piled up in a small room behind the store. Some of the items had been stored for a long time and had already become moldy. On that day, while looking for something, the father noticed several packs of sanitary napkins at the bottom that had been damaged. Upon inspection, he found that a group of rats had made it their home. The father became furious and searched for the rats but couldn't find them. He then placed rat poison on the ground. But what should be done with these few packs of sanitary napkins? They couldn't be sold, and it was a pity to throw them away as they cost over ten yuan. After some thought, he called his wife over and said, "These sanitary napkins are damaged. You and the child can use them. It would be a waste to throw them away." Initially, the girl's mother refused, but eventually, she kept them. Time passed day by day, and during their menstrual period, the mother and daughter used these sanitary napkins contaminated by the rats. Two months later, one day, the girl felt unwell, weak all over, accompanied by nausea and vomiting. Under the support of her manager and colleagues, she went to a nearby hospital for some examinations. However, the examination results shocked everyone - the doctor said she was pregnant. The girl burst into tears. Her colleagues quickly informed her parents of the situation. The girl's parents firmly refused to believe that their daughter would do such a thing. The girl also insisted it was impossible. She had never dated anyone at school or at the factory where she worked. How could she possibly be pregnant? The hospital said this was the result of the examination. If they didn't believe it, they could go to a larger hospital for confirmation. That day, the girl's parents, along with the manager and colleagues, went to a well-known hospital nearby, but the examination result was still pregnancy. This left everyone very puzzled. The chief of the hospital said they would conduct a detailed examination.

In front of the ultrasound machine, the girl's mother fainted. The manager and colleagues vomited and cried, and even the nurses in the hospital shed tears and couldn't bear to look. Under the ultrasound examination, we saw more than twenty 3-centimeter-long baby mice in the girl's uterus. This scene shocked everyone present, including the experienced chief physician who was stunned for quite a while. After a while, the doctor told the girl's parents that surgery must be performed immediately, or the girl's life would be in danger. These baby mice grew very fast. They had already developed teeth and were slowly gnawing around. If delayed, the girl wouldn't survive. Later, after consultation between the hospital and the girl's parents, it was decided to perform an abdominal operation to remove the mice. During the surgery, none of the nurses dared to face it. It was all handled by the hospital doctors. Although the surgery was successful and the girl was no longer in danger, deep shadows had already been left in her mind, which would never fade away. She was still very young; how would her future life be? Later, the doctor informed that whether she could have children in the future was unknown. What a pitiful child! Should things continue like this? Let's pray for her and hope for a miracle so that she can become a happy girl again.
