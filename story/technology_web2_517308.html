Beijing Benz new C-Class, fully upgraded

by rabiads652 on 2011-10-24 10:20:18

The new Mercedes-Benz product design style with increasingly strong family characteristics, especially after the announcement of new models such as the gull-wing supercar SLS and the all-new CLS, has gradually revealed the full picture of the new generation family design to the public. On the new C-Class with code W204/S204, Beijing Benz officially launched a facelift product, pushing the new C-Class sedan to the Chinese market with more than 2,000 shape and configuration upgrades.

In 1982, the Mercedes-Benz C-Class car with code W201 platform was born, and its cumulative global sales have reached 8.5 million units to date. Since the complete redesign of the W204 three-box sedan and the S204 Estate wagon in 2007, their global sales have exceeded 1 million units. However, in the Chinese market, due to the difficulty in changing consumer perceptions, the sales of the new C-Class cannot be said to have been smooth sailing. Beijing Benz hopes that the all-new facelift C-Class sedan will become a new benchmark among products in the same class, equipped with more advanced internal and external technologies to meet the lifestyle of young elites.

In terms of appearance, the most obvious changes in the new facelift C-Class are in the headlight assembly, bumper, and air intake, which in turn affect the design language of the front lines and fog lights. Observers can easily see the similarities in design between the new C-Class and the second-generation CLS. This is the current family DNA design style of Mercedes-Benz.

The original relatively steady headlight design of the old C-Class has been integrated with more lines in the new C-Class, creating a sharper effect, moving the world forward. The addition of LED daytime running lights above the fog lights also adds a touch of elegance to the new C-Class. Mercedes-Benz claims that the use of LED daytime running lights not only enhances the sense of technology but also helps pedestrians determine whether the vehicle's engine is running, providing assistance for driving safety and pedestrian protection.

On the side, the new C-Class intentionally creates a diagonal upward stretching effect, exuding a sports car aura, while also achieving a wind resistance coefficient of only 0.26Cd for the vehicle. In the rear, there are fewer changes in the lines, but the tail light design also uses LED light groups, making them more translucent and adding ventilation holes to maintain the transparency of the tail light cover after long-term use.

Compared to its same-class competitors, the performance of the C200 tends to be stable, leading in power, slightly weaker in handling than BMW, but fully leading in quality and safety equipment. The new ride crafted by Beijing Benz for the new generation of young elites should not be difficult to gain consumer recognition.
