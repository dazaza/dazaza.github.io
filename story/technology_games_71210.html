The latest stunning screenshots of "Red Alert 3"

by keteo2008 on 2008-05-27 11:28:18

"Red Alert 3" has been very active these days: first, the HD trailer was released, then it was confirmed to be released in October, and then a large number of screenshots leaked.

Today, EA released eight more screenshots. From the visuals, they look quite stunning. Not only do they show intense battle scenes, but also display meticulously crafted towns (i.e., battlefields). Moreover, the overall style is different from the previous works; it remains to be seen whether the majority of players will accept it. Of course, such stunning graphics come at a cost, potentially causing many machines to struggle. EA has not yet disclosed the specific configuration requirements, but it is estimated that they should be similar to "Command & Conquer 3," or slightly higher.
