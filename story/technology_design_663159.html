The vice president of the driving school drove under the influence of alcohol and hit two old people, then fled the scene.

by haojing12 on 2012-02-11 11:05:33

By Li Jiawei, our correspondent in Cangzhou, and communicator Sun Mingshan:

On the evening of January 29th, in front of the沧 County Procuratorate on East Huanghe Road in Cangzhou City, a small car hit three people and fled. The accident resulted in two elderly people in their sixties being injured. On the morning of February 8th, the Second Traffic Police Detachment of Cangzhou announced that the case had been successfully solved. Yu, the vice-principal of a driving school in Cangzhou, was legally detained on suspicion of fleeing the scene of a traffic accident.

■ A Car Hits Two Elderly People

At 20:20 on the evening of January 29th, the Second Traffic Police Detachment of Cangzhou received an order from the command center that an accident occurred in front of the沧 County Procuratorate on Huanghe Road. Three pedestrians were hit, and the offending vehicle fled. The on-duty police officers and detachment leaders quickly arrived at the scene. At the accident site, it was learned that a family of six, including elderly people and children, were crossing the road to go to the theater across the street. When they were in the middle of the road waiting for vehicles to pass, three of them were hit by a car, which then fled. Among those hit, two were injured and both were over sixty years old. They were sent to the hospital for treatment, later it was found out that both elders suffered fractures and other injuries.

According to the families of the victims, the offending vehicle was a blue taxi, and no remnants of the offending vehicle were left at the accident site. The Second Traffic Police Detachment of Cangzhou organized police forces overnight to investigate all taxis with targeted and specific goals. After checking more than 1,000 taxis throughout the night, they found nothing.

■ Locking Down the Offending Vehicle

Because the hit-and-run case was particularly heinous, the Second Traffic Police Detachment of Cangzhou quickly established a special task force named "1.29". However, after further investigation, the police learned that the offending vehicle was not a taxi but rather what the family mistakenly saw as one. After extensive verification, the police determined that the offending vehicle should be a blue-gray compact car. On the morning of February 7th, the investigating police used technical means to lock down the suspected vehicle —— a "Honda Civic" small car.

■ "Two Drunken Bouts" Led to the Disaster

After obtaining sufficient evidence, on the morning of February 8th, the police of the Second Traffic Police Detachment of Cangzhou took action and captured the suspect Yu at his workplace. Upon investigation, it was found that Yu was the vice-principal of a driving school in Cangzhou, and he confessed to the hit-and-run incident. According to Yu, on the night of the incident, he first drank alcohol at a restaurant, then went to another restaurant where he drank two glasses of baijiu (Chinese liquor). While driving through the incident location, he hit the pedestrians and fled. The day after the incident, he placed the offending vehicle at his workplace. On the third day, he drove the car to Huanghua and had it repaired. He told the police officer, "After the incident, I couldn't sleep every day, afraid of hearing the phone ring. I deeply regret my hit-and-run behavior. Now, a stone has fallen off my heart."

It is understood that Yu has been administratively detained for 15 days according to law, and the case is currently under further handling.
