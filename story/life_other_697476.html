The car rental industry is an asset-heavy business.

by wodingche on 2012-03-08 15:50:43

As is well-known, the car rental industry is one of heavy assets, low profits and requiring a great deal of "burning money". Its operating cost is high, one is depreciation, two is daily operation, three is the cost of market promotion. Just the automobile depreciation accounts for more than half of the operating cost. The gross profit margin is quite low, and with the expansion of the scale of car rental companies comes the constantly growing loss. Perhaps in the future, when the renting habit of domestic consumers has been cultivated and the price of car rental can be at an appropriate range, China's car rental industry can get out of loss.

The heavy debt, consecutive losses, the not yet mature car rental market, and the financial report which was questioned as soon as it came out, make the road of China's major car rental companies full of thorns. But the person in charge of the car booking website said that the industry firmly believes that China's car rental industry can bravely break through the chaos and gradually improve.

Whether buying a car, taking a taxi, or renting a car, which is more cost-effective?

Suppose we now buy a mid-low range car for about 100,000 yuan, and need about 10,000 yuan annually for road maintenance fees, insurance fees, parking fees, oil fees, etc. Calculating by using it for 10 years, the annual depreciation is 10,000 yuan. So a car costs 20,000 yuan per year to buy and maintain.

Similarly, for 20,000 yuan per year domestically, there is another way to solve the need for a car: spend 1,000 yuan per month on taking taxis, totaling 12,000 yuan per year. The remaining 8,000 yuan is more than enough for renting a car.

To sum up, taking a taxi has its advantages, buying a car also has its advantages, but if you rent a car, you can combine both, neither bearing the high cost of maintaining a car, nor having the trouble of not being able to catch a taxi during peak hours and being late as a result. Why not do it? Moreover, many domestic car rental companies offer very low prices for economy cars, and some companies have even introduced hourly car rental information, making it even more convenient for many white-collar workers who go to and from work.
