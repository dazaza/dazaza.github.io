Online post exposes woman being molested and beaten by bank manager, plea to deputy county magistrate for help goes unanswered.

by gvpnbvents on 2010-04-10 15:13:02

Scene of the incident

□ Chief Reporter Yu Chao of Today's Newspaper

Yesterday, a post in the "People's Messages" section of the People's Network titled "Bank Manager Molests and Brutally Beats Woman After Drinking, Deputy County Magistrate Watches Indifferently" caught the attention of netizens.

The post by "Netizen Ms. Liu" claimed that at around 10 p.m. on March 25 this year, an entrepreneur from Gushi County in Xinyang City hosted a dinner for Min Mou, the manager of the Gushi branch of Bank of China, and Zhang Mou, the deputy county magistrate of Gushi County. Inside a teahouse in the county, after drinking, Min Mou "suddenly had evil thoughts" and under the guise of toasting, he "grabbed and groped three women present." When his behavior was strongly opposed, Min Mou "became enraged, grabbed Chen Mou (one of the women) and threw her to the ground, violently grabbing her chest and lower body, and repeatedly slammed her head against the floor by pulling her hair." The post reflected that during the incident, Deputy County Magistrate Zhang Mou was present but did not intervene, instead "leaving without concern."

Beaten woman kneels and begs the deputy county magistrate for justice

In the "People's Messages" section of the People's Network, the netizen signing as Ms. Liu stated that at around 10 p.m. on March 25, 2010, an entrepreneur from Gushi County in Xinyang City hosted a dinner for Zhang Mou, the deputy county magistrate in charge of agriculture in the county, and Min Mou, the manager of the Gushi branch of Bank of China, among others.

After having enough food and drink, at the request of the two esteemed guests, the boss invited four ladies, including Xu Mou and Chen Mou from the Guobin Grand Hotel in Gushi County, to the "Yantianxue Green Tea House" in the urban area of Gushi County for tea. Since the four ladies were marketing personnel from the Guobin Grand Hotel, to maintain and develop customer relationships, they accepted the invitation as friends, knowing beforehand that the deputy county magistrate would be present and assuming nothing untoward would happen, so they willingly came to the teahouse.

During tea time, under the influence of alcohol, Manager Min suddenly had evil thoughts and started touching Chen Mou and two other women under the guise of toasting. When Xu Mou tried to stop him, she was also grabbed and groped by Manager Min. Min’s vulgar behavior was strongly resisted by the four women, who then decided to leave.

Min Mou, unwilling to let them go, followed Chen Mou to the restroom and attempted to molest her again. When his actions were rejected once more, Manager Min became furious, grabbed Chen Mou and threw her to the ground, violently grabbing her chest and lower body, and repeatedly slammed her head against the floor by pulling her hair. He then dragged Chen Mou from the second floor to the first floor of the teahouse. When Xu Mou and the other three women tried to rescue her, they were also beaten by Min Mou and thrown into the parking lot of the teahouse (later determined by medical examination that both Chen and Xu suffered minor injuries). After beating people, Min Mou left without concern.

Throughout the entire incident, Deputy County Magistrate Zhang Mou did not offer any advice or intervention. More strangely, when Chen Mou knelt down and pleaded with Deputy County Magistrate Zhang Mou for justice, this Zhang Deputy County Magistrate coldly said in her ear, "Young lady, don't make a fuss. Manager Min has connections." Then he left without concern. Later, after concerned onlookers at the scene reported the incident, the four women were sent to the hospital.

Several days have passed since the incident, and not only has Manager Min not apologized sincerely, but he has continuously used his connections to threaten and intimidate the victims, causing Chen, Xu, and the other four ladies to live in fear and remain indoors. Faced with "well-connected" Manager Min, even the respected deputy county magistrate dared not uphold justice, and even helped absolve him of responsibility. Who else will dare to help them? Who can uphold justice for them?

Teahouse Manager: All because of alcohol

The "Yantianxue Green Tea House" mentioned in the post is quite famous in Gushi County.

When asked about the events of the evening of March 25, the on-duty manager of the teahouse said that it was indeed an enterprise owner from Gushi County who hosted a dinner for Min Zhitao, the manager of the Gushi branch of the Bank of China, and Zhang Jiancheng, the deputy county magistrate in charge of agriculture in Gushi County. "They drank wine outside at a hotel before coming to the teahouse. Initially, they sang in the multi-functional hall of the teahouse, drinking red wine while singing. Some men got drunk, and later for some reason, they started quarreling with several women. Manager Min did hit two of the women, making quite a commotion. One of the women surnamed Chen had her head injured." The on-duty manager of the teahouse said, "It was all because of alcohol. Actually, Manager Min is usually a decent person."

Ms. Xu: No one has apologized

After much effort, the reporter contacted one of the women present that evening, Ms. Xu, who is a customer director at the Guobin Grand Hotel in Gushi County. When asked about the events of that night, she appeared very cautious but still confirmed the events of that evening. "Currently, no one has come forward to apologize. The matter is still being handled, but it's hard to say what the outcome will be."

Ms. Chen: Too much pressure, recuperating at home

Yesterday afternoon, the reporter also contacted Ms. Chen, mentioned in the post, who was chased and beaten by Manager Min. However, over the phone, she said, "I don't want to give interviews, I don't want to talk about this matter." Ms. Chen said, "I'm under too much pressure right now, please don't ask me about this matter, I'm still recuperating at home."

The reporter asked her where the pressure was coming from? "It has greatly affected my family, as well as various other aspects. The parties involved have not yet apologized, but I believe there should be a resolution eventually."

Taxi driver: Scene was chaotic, couldn't bear to watch

On the evening of March 25, a taxi driver witnessed the scene in front of the teahouse: "At the time, I happened to be driving past there and saw a man kicking and hitting a woman, the woman fell to the ground, crying loudly. There were a few other women trying to stop him, the scene was very chaotic. A man was beating a woman, I really couldn't bear to watch, so I called the police."

Police station chief: Manager molested the women and was refused, then beat them

The officers who responded to the call that evening were from the Xiguan Police Station of the Gushi County Public Security Bureau. We learned that the case was handled by a political instructor surnamed He and a police officer surnamed Chai from the station.

A responsible person from the station revealed to the reporter that the person who hosted the dinner for Manager Min and Deputy County Magistrate Zhang that evening was Zhao Mou, the factory manager of a printing factory in Gushi County. The several women were all customer managers from the Guobin Grand Hotel, invited to accompany the drinking session. "Min Zhitao was very drunk, went up to molest the women, and after being rejected, he assaulted them. Among them, the girl surnamed Chen was the most severely injured, and the other three women were also injured to varying degrees. The case has been transferred to the Gushi County Public Security Bureau."

It was understood that this printing factory is called "Quanyi Color Printing."

Parties Involved

Manager claims "at the police station", deputy county magistrate plays "hide and seek"

Regarding the situation reflected in the post, as the main party involved, what does Min Zhitao, the manager of the Gushi County branch of the Bank of China, have to say about the matter? At 5:45 PM yesterday, the reporter called Min Zhitao and asked about the situation that evening. He only said, "The public security authorities are currently investigating." The reporter repeatedly asked about the situation during the incident, and he emphasized twice, "I am at the police station now, it's inconvenient to talk."

Another party mentioned in the post, Deputy County Magistrate Zhang Jiancheng of Gushi County, did he really stand idly by as described by Netizen Ms. Liu? Starting at 5:20 PM yesterday, the reporter began calling Deputy County Magistrate Zhang's phone, dialing more than ten times without anyone answering. At 6:00 PM, Zhang Deputy County Magistrate's mobile phone showed a call state, and a few minutes later the call was connected again, but still no one answered. As of the time the reporter finished the article, Zhang Deputy County Magistrate's mobile phone remained unanswered.

■ Information capture

Yesterday, the reporter saw such a message on the Gushi County Government website: Bank of China grants a credit line of 150 million yuan to enterprises in Gushi County. The message concluded: The Gushi County branch of the Bank of China signed credit intention contracts worth a total of 1.5 billion yuan with four enterprises including Quanyi Color Printing and Xinhe Real Estate, and promoted its main financial products.
