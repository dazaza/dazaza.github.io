Network pyramid scheme "sugar-coated bullets" are running rampant, six departments jointly launch a heavy blow

by nuo01 on 2011-07-05 09:39:51

The Internet continues to grow and develop, and in this warm cradle, a pair of twins "online pyramid selling" have been conceived from the traditional offline pyramid selling. They have moved from the ground to the web, from the real to the virtual, and have a set of skills for weaving lies. They create a beautiful future on the Internet, planting a tree of desire in the hearts of those who want "success" and "money", and in the end, they "burn their bridges" and "disappear".

According to relevant reports, starting from July 12th, in order to punish online pyramid selling organizations, purify the network environment, and ensure the safety of netizens' online behavior, six departments including the State Administration for Industry and Commerce, the Ministry of Public Security, the Ministry of Industry and Information Technology, the National Internet Office, the People's Bank of China, and the China Banking Regulatory Commission will jointly launch a nationwide special operation to crack down on online pyramid selling crimes and expose the inside story of online pyramid selling scams. Due to the openness of the network, pyramid selling organizations play a game of "hide and seek", spreading quickly and involving a wider range, making it necessary to stop them in time and cut off this tumor, which will be a protracted battle. This operation will continue until December 2011.

In the 2010 China Online Pyramid Selling Report, it was pointed out that in recent years, online pyramid selling has developed rapidly domestically, with more than 40 million people participating in 2010, and the amount involved in cases investigated and announced by the industry and commerce authorities reaching as high as 8 billion yuan. These figures show that online pyramid selling is still growing day by day, devouring people's wealth, and must be stopped urgently.

Responsible persons of related websites expressed firm support and cooperation in cracking down on online pyramid selling criminal activities, adopting relevant technical means to strengthen website management and monitoring, clean up the network, jointly uncover the tails of criminals, establish a "blacklist", report pyramid selling "nests" in time, and "protect the network home, everyone's responsibility".

Online pyramid selling changes its tactics frequently, mainly using e-commerce, network capital operations, personal finance, online sales entrepreneurship, online training, distance education, etc., to weave a series of wealth and get-rich-quick "black holes", beautify them with beautiful words, lure people with low cost and high returns, and deceive those with weak willpower, entrepreneurs at home, those who want to get rich quickly, and unemployed individuals into stepping into the minefield, then swallowing their money like "zombies". Shaking the bait, it's just like Jiang Taigong fishing - those who are willing to bite. There are many fishing nets on the Internet, without keen eyesight, one might easily become someone else's bait.

Since the start of traditional pyramid selling to online pyramid selling, how many pyramid selling incidents have occurred. The "World Tong" incident, under the banner of "click advertisements, make money", claims that spending more than 20,000 yuan to become a registered member, clicking related software every day can earn more than 2,000 yuan per day, clicking for 5 years can easily earn 130,000 yuan. The "myth of getting rich" has made countless people fall into the abyss, unable to extricate themselves; In March, an online news reported that Mr. Hu, a billionaire who had worked hard in the Wenzhou business circle for decades, was deceived by a network pyramid selling organization that relied on developing downlines and paying membership fees through virtual shop products on the network, lured by the slogan "sit at home part-time and make big money", falling into the pyramid selling trap and losing millions of yuan. Such incidents occur frequently on the Internet, causing netizens great pain.

As the saying goes, "where there is demand, there is a market", the reason so many pyramid selling organizations succeed is also because there are such gullible netizens on the network. In recent years, more and more netizens have joined the online shopping army, establishing websites, online sales, online group buying, e-commerce, online payment, online Taobao are all common topics among netizens. The "prosperous" development of the network has led to the proliferation of pyramid selling, attracting those who lack proper employment and have plenty of side jobs, grabbing the psychology of the masses, setting one trap after another, changing tactics, turning illusory wealth into magic-like overnight riches, monthly earnings of hundreds of thousands, "honestly recruiting typists, wages over ten thousand, work at home", "earn 200 yuan a day", etc., after being misled by these words, next comes collecting deposits, handling fees, membership fees, etc.

China's famous e-commerce expert Nuowang www.nuo.cn reminds all netizens that pyramid selling organizations generally use overseas servers to build websites, with all information chains concentrated in the network environment. When conducting online communication and transactions, be extremely cautious of the "sugar-coated bullets" of pyramid selling organizations, such as "recruiting members", "collecting high entry fees", "team compensation", do not easily fall for them, beware of clicking on "landmines" and blowing yourself up. We look forward to this six-month concentrated crackdown on online pyramid selling by the six departments, hoping that after the storm, a clean network environment will emerge.
