The fantasy world of "The Lord of the Rings" brought to players the eternal tale of conflict between different races and factions, while the dark world of "Diablo" introduced a game system that continues to influence games today. On April 8, 2011, "Contract," an exclusive ARPG masterpiece by Game5's web game platform, will undergo its first test. It perfectly continues the style of "Diablo," and its profound plot and vast world view are truly breathtaking.

In ancient times, the continent where civilizations existed was once a single entity, known as Mu Continent by wizards and historians. This land was home to various civilized races, including humans, elves, orcs, dwarves, and gnomes. Suddenly one day, ominous signs appeared in the sky, revealing a power that made even the gods tremble.

Eight heroes formed contracts with deities and spirits, combining divine and human strength seamlessly. After arduous battles, they finally emerged victorious.

However, afterward, the heroes were required to sacrifice their flesh and soul for the sealing ritual. But when the noble sacrificers plunged their weapons into their own chests, their expressions twisted upon seeing the hesitation of their comrades...

This was betrayal!! Betrayal not only among companions but also among the deities, spirits, and even the souls of all things who had formed these contracts. Consequently, the entire world split apart.

The surviving human hero led his kin to the beautiful continent of Freyaniya. There, summer never ends, the Moor Estate is abundant with life, and seven great powers vie for dominance. Civilization developed rapidly here; the people were morally upright, culturally prosperous, and followed the teachings of the deities. Meanwhile, the dark continent isolated by a vast strait became a gathering place of terror.

Rabiria is filled with fallen beings, dangerous monsters, and never sees sunlight, leaving it barren. Creatures fight each other endlessly for survival, knowing no peace. In this harsh environment filled with hatred and darkness, a new race of elves was born—they are called the Night Elves.

In elven legends, the betrayal of the opposite continent is often repeated. They do not know the truth of history, but they understand that their race has been abandoned. Through countless long nights, much of the true history has been lost. Players entering the game "Contract" start as a minor character within a tribe. War is about to reignite due to hatred, with the two sides now being humans and Night Elves. Will you choose revenge? Or defense? What lies ahead for the destinies of the two continents, the future of the human, Night Elf, dwarf, orc, and gnome races? Can the secrets behind the betrayal of the contract be uncovered? The splendid plot awaits your guidance to unfold, everything is in "Contract"!

The First Dark Fantasy Fully Real-Time Combat Epic "Contract"

The epic dark fantasy "Contract" is the latest ARPG masterpiece exclusively distributed by Game5's web game platform in 2011. The game uses Western cultural themes as its backdrop, presenting a grand epic storyline, integrating the essence of excellent domestic and international games, and perfectly continuing the philosophy of "Diablo."

The game employs the most advanced Flash technology to create exquisite 3D-rendered visuals. With fully real-time free combat, rich battlefield dungeons, it offers users an incredibly thrilling gaming experience. Featuring two major factions—humans and Night Elves—and three professions—warriors, mages, and divine messengers—with numerous dazzling skills, hundreds of labyrinthine maps, and invincible reproducible and transformable magical beasts, users can enjoy unprecedented innovative gameplay and entertainment excitement. One contract, one imminent fierce battle between humans and Night Elves. Warriors, what are you waiting for?
