53% ABV 10-Year Red Flower Lang Wine 500ml

The bottle shape of the 10-Year Red Flower Lang Wine has been meticulously crafted through 105 processes, with a special introduction of British low-temperature red technology, 24K sand gold burnishing, and 24K bright gold edge pressing. The bottle design features smooth and graceful lines, balanced yet expressive. Its pure red color is both reserved and bold, perfectly showcasing the exceptional taste of the 10-Year Red Flower Lang Wine.

The 10-Year Red Flower Lang Wine is one of the key series launched by Sichuan Lang Wine Group Co., Ltd. It is characterized by its rich aroma, full-bodied flavor, aged taste, sweet and refreshing aftertaste, clean finish, and elegant style. The newly developed product innovates boldly in packaging while ensuring the original quality and style characteristics. With its novel and unique packaging design, it breaks away from tradition and sets a new trend for ultra-fashionable liquor packaging.

The ingredients of the 10-Year Red Flower Lang Wine include rare glutinous red sorghum grown at altitudes above 1000 meters on the Yunnan-Guizhou Plateau, and natural spring water drawn from deep underground wells over 1000 meters below the mountains. Adhering to ancient brewing methods passed down for thousands of years, each bottle of Red Flower Lang Wine undergoes 9 rounds of distillation, 8 rounds of fermentation, and 7 rounds of extraction, with a production cycle lasting a full year. Afterward, the wine is stored in caves for more than 10 or even 15 years. Through countless cycles of fermentation involving billions of microorganisms, this process gives birth to a precious sauce-flavor liquor that contains over 400 aromatic components, green and healthy, and extremely valuable. The 10-Year Red Flower Lang Wine is notable for its prominent sauce fragrance, full-bodied and clean taste, refined elegance, long-lasting aftertaste, and lingering aroma in an empty glass.
