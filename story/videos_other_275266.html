The flight which was supposed to take off at 11:15 am back to Beijing, the Chongqing-Guangzhou air ticket, but it took off an hour later due to some reasons. I was bored sitting on the plane and suddenly found a strange phenomenon. The important notice on the Hainan Airlines ticket was actually on the barcode of the ticket. I put on my glasses and looked carefully. The content was roughly as follows: "Important Notice: The boarding gate will close X (really unclear) minutes before departure. The expected boarding time is for reference only." This important notice has two problems: 1. The content is unclear, especially how many minutes before departure the gate will close, which is really unclear. 2. The expected boarding time is for reference only, meaning that delays are already a normal occurrence. Now let's take a look at the tickets of Eastern Airlines and Air China. The important notices are all at the bottom, clear and legible. Although there are no issues with the important notices of Eastern Airlines and Air China, their delay situations are not much better than Hainan Airlines. The delay times for these three tickets are as follows: Hainan Airlines delayed by one hour due to air traffic control; Eastern Airlines delayed by four hours without any reason given, and the staff attitude was terrible; Air China delayed by one hour because the original aircraft had a fault and another plane was used instead.
