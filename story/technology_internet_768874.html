Alibaba Plans to Gift 7 Million Sellers with Cloud OS Phones, Pushing Wireless Services

On the morning of November 19th, news came that Alibaba Group announced it would invest funds and resources to help 7 million Taobao sellers adapt to new development trends. The Alibaba Group will launch a mobile e-commerce service platform, with the first phase being implemented on smartphones specially customized by AliCloud OS for sellers (mjj.taobao.com). To this end, Alibaba will offer these phones free of charge to all Taobao sellers.

According to official information from Alibaba, the preliminary investment for this plan alone will reach 5 billion yuan by the end of this year. Taobao will also open a dedicated customer service line specifically to provide mobile e-commerce services for sellers.

Alibaba Group CEO Jonathan Lu expressed: "We hope all sellers can fully participate, quickly entering the mobile wireless space, jointly creating a new internet miracle, driving the economic transformation and upgrading of China, boosting consumption, and creating jobs." Alibaba Group stated that this is just the first step in helping a large number of sellers go all-in on wireless services. In the future, the group will continue to invest in the mobile e-commerce service platform, fully assisting all Taobao sellers to more quickly and effectively transition into the mobile internet era, keeping pace with the trend of mobile internet development, and better serving consumers.

Senior Vice President of Alibaba Group, Joseph Wu, pointed out that mobile shopping differs significantly from PC-based online shopping. Mobile users exhibit typical characteristics such as personalization, fragmentation, and information push notifications. He believes that Chinese online shoppers are undergoing a complete migration path from computers to mobile devices. "Currently, the total number of users of the mobile Taobao app has exceeded 320 million, and in the future, mobile phones will undoubtedly become one of the main channels for people's consumption."

It was learned that Alibaba Group will adopt a policy of "discounted subscription to Taobao services, free gift of seller phones." A service package discount bundle will be launched, including various essential services needed by Taobao sellers, such as Taobao旺铺Professional Edition, Data Cube Professional Edition, An Cun Recordings, Transaction Wizard, among others. President of AliCloud OS, Yu Ce, revealed: "Not only will we offer these service packages at historically unprecedented discount prices, but on top of that, we will also give away a seller phone worth the same value as the discounted service package to each store that subscribes to the service package."

Additionally, according to information disclosed by Alibaba, the detailed rules for gifting seller phones will be announced later this week. The first booking period is set for 10:00 AM on November 25th. Due to limited quantities in the first batch, the booking period will only be open for one day, following a first-come, first-served basis.
