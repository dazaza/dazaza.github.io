I. How to Open an Online Store on Taobao: Steps for Opening a Store

With the increasing popularity of the internet, more and more people are shopping online. If you're wondering how to open a store on Taobao, what preparations are needed, and what precautions should be taken, please read on.

1. Register Your Account on Taobao

First, go to the Taobao homepage, click "Free Registration," and choose between registering with a phone number or email. Typically, we choose "Email Registration." Fill out all the required information and click "Agree to Terms and Submit Registration Information." If everything goes smoothly, the website will confirm successful registration.

Next, log into your email to receive the confirmation email from Taobao. Click the confirmation link to activate your account, completing the first step in opening an online store. Note: If you've already shopped on Taobao, there's no need to register again. On Taobao, one account can serve both as a buyer and seller.

2. Complete Alipay Real-name Verification

How to open a store on Taobao? What steps are necessary? The first step is successfully registering a Taobao account. Next, complete the Alipay real-name verification, which is mandatory. After clicking "My Taobao," you'll see a prompt saying "Please complete real-name verification to sell items." Follow the prompts to proceed.

Alipay serves as the money bag for Taobao users and is the foundation for ensuring honest transactions. Real-name verification confirms your true identity. This process adds complexity to opening an online store but significantly enhances the security of Taobao transactions. In the past, uploading an ID was necessary for manual verification by Taobao. Now, Taobao has partnered with various banks across the country. If you have a bank card registered under your name, Taobao can verify your identity through the banking system, making it much more convenient than before.

3. Post More Than 10 Items

Post at least 10 different items (and keep them listed for sale), and you can open your store for free. To post items, navigate sequentially to "My Taobao" - "I am a Seller" - "I Want to Sell." Taobao considers having more than 10 items for sale as qualifying for opening a store. Posting fewer than 10 items only counts as personal idle item trading, so posting less than 10 items disqualifies you from applying to open a store.

4. Pass the Taobao Store Opening Exam

Navigate sequentially to "My Taobao" - "I am a Seller" and find the "Open Store" button. After clicking, a prompt will appear requiring you to take an exam. Passing the Taobao store opening exam is mandatory; this new rule was implemented for new sellers at the end of 2010. The exam primarily covers the "Taobao Rules," and you must score at least 60 points to pass. The basic questions section must have 100% accuracy. After passing, read the Honest Business Commitment Letter, then follow the prompts to fill out your store name, category, and introduction. Check and agree to the "Product Posting Rules" and "Consumer Protection Agreement," then confirm submission. If everything goes smoothly, you now own your own Taobao store! Congratulations on successfully opening your store!

5. Install Aliwangwang Software

On Taobao, communication with buyers isn't done through QQ, phone calls, or other methods, but through Aliwangwang. Aliwangwang is the essential tool for communication between Taobao sellers and buyers, integrating many seller functions. It's very practical. In the event of any disputes during transactions, the chat records from Aliwangwang serve as crucial evidence for resolving disputes later. Please note that the official Taobao platform does not recognize QQ chat records. Therefore, Aliwangwang is indispensable.

Opening an online store on Taobao is simple. However, running a successful store on Taobao is not easy!

II. Supplementary Notes and Precautions

Store Naming: A shorter and easier-to-remember store name isn't always better because it relates to Taobao search rankings. It's best to include the name of the items you sell, their purpose, brand relevance, current promotions, etc., which improves Taobao's search hit rate. Additionally, consistently listing new items daily helps improve search ranking.

2. Real-name Verification Process

To open a store on Taobao, real-name verification is required. The store owner must be a Chinese citizen aged eighteen or older and possess a bank account associated with Alipay. During real-name verification, Alipay will deposit a small amount (around 0.01-1 RMB) into your account. After checking via online banking, input the exact transaction amount into the Alipay webpage. If correct, the verification will pass.

3. What to Do if No One Visits Due to Lack of Reputation?

A newly opened store may lose sales opportunities due to low credibility, causing potential customers to mistrust the seller. Even good products might be overlooked without reputation. Virtual investments are small, offer quick returns, and help build credibility.
