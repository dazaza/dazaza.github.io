With the rapid growth of collection techniques, network marketing has become a trend for the marketing development of small and medium-sized enterprises (SMEs). It runs through the entire process of SMEs' online business activities and is a major component of their overall marketing strategy. Unlike other media, network marketing is not restricted by time and space. Through the Internet, companies can promote their products non-stop, 24 hours a day, to the whole country or even the world, offering unparalleled advantages for brand promotion.

Currently, the goal of SMEs in carrying out market marketing is to promote their corporate brands, and the emergence of network marketing has accelerated the pace of brand promotion. Establishing a corporate brand on the Internet and effectively promoting it extends and expands the physical brand of the enterprise onto the Internet. Network marketing provides convenient conditions for enterprises to use the Internet to build a brand image. Regardless of size or amount of funds, SMEs can use network marketing to promote development methods suitable for their own enterprises and showcase their brand images.

For SMEs, due to limitations in operating costs, the promotion and publicity of their brands are relatively weak. Marketing methods commonly used by large enterprises, such as issuing news, placing advertisements, and conducting large-scale promotional activities, offer fewer opportunities for SMEs. Therefore, choosing a method that requires less capital investment and conserves resources for brand promotion becomes crucial. For SMEs, using the Internet for network marketing is very necessary for promoting their online brands.

Strong Online Interaction

The network is an interactive information transmission channel that breaks the one-way nature of traditional marketing methods and has extremely strong interactivity. Precisely because of this strong interactivity, while enterprises are engaging in network marketing, consumer groups are also paying attention to and discussing the company and its products through the Internet. This word-of-mouth spread generated by consumer feedback is spontaneous among the consumer group. The enterprise's image and brand spread like a virus, reaching widespread recognition in a short period, making the promotion of the enterprise's brand highly efficient.

Long Duration

Network marketing allows enterprises to present their brands globally for 24 hours a day without interruption. Enterprises release their products on the Internet through network marketing, transforming instant product displays into permanence. Unlike the brief promotion methods of newspapers, magazines, television, and radio, brand promotion through network marketing can remain on the Internet for a long time, avoiding situations where consumer groups overlook the entire brand due to temporary inattention.

Wide Promotion Scope

The Internet is a globally pervasive network platform that is not limited to a specific region or country but rather extends its promotion scope worldwide, enabling the dissemination of corporate brands beyond spatial constraints. In traditional marketing, corporate brands are mostly focused on a specific region, creating limitations in brand promotion. Choosing network marketing methods effectively solves the problem of regional limitations in brand promotion since Internet promotion is borderless and unrestricted by regions.

Fast Dissemination Speed

According to surveys by a foreign authoritative institution: under conditions of equal returns, the investment in network marketing is 1/10 of that in traditional marketing, yet the speed of information transmission is 5 to 8 times faster than traditional marketing! Such a rapid marketing method is why numerous SMEs choose network marketing as the main force in their enterprise marketing strategies.
