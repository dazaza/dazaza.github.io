Churchman University: American Centennial Business School Corporate Internship

In order to meet the requirements of practical work abilities that employers have for graduates, more than 20 prestigious American universities with over a century of history, including Churchman University and Stanford University, have reformed their current educational systems. These universities provide and encourage students to participate in cooperative internship programs with globally renowned enterprises during their studies. While earning an income, students also earn academic credits which can be counted towards their total credits.

Founded in 1911, Churchman University is one of the top business schools in the United States, offering undergraduate, master's, and doctoral degrees. The university has trained many high-end financial talents in the U.S., who mainly work in accounting, auditing, securities investment, insurance, futures, and other high-end financial industries.

Students from Churchman University can integrate into work faster than general university graduates. This is because since its establishment, Churchman University has always advocated cultivating students' practical abilities. Close alumni networks and employment recommendations are important factors for the success of the school's students. Therefore, the university highly values cooperation with many famous large enterprises in the U.S., providing valuable internship opportunities for students.

Churchman University offers various tuition assistance programs for students with outstanding potential in business, such as scholarships and corporate internships.

The remote commerce degree program at Churchman University allows middle and senior-level managers worldwide to study online courses, take online exams, and submit papers online at any time and place without affecting their original work. The degree obtained is exactly the same as studying on campus and is the preferred educational model for middle and senior-level managers who are employed in Europe, America, and China.

Churchman University's bilingual courses in Spanish-English and Chinese-English are very popular among multinational corporations. The university offers MBA, undergraduate, master's, and doctoral degrees. The low-income scholarships offered by the school require only a few thousand dollars to complete the degree once successfully applied.

Churchman University campuses are located in Pennsylvania, California, Texas, and other places in the U.S. The earliest established branch, Churchman Business School, is a well-known American church university with a rigorous academic atmosphere, founded in 1911, having nearly a hundred years of history. It is also one of the oldest business schools in the U.S., specifically cultivating high-end talents for the American financial industry. In the "National Ranking of Comprehensive MBA Programs," this centennial prestigious university ranks 17th.

Besides Churchman University, the teaching quality of other centennial prestigious universities in the U.S., such as Boston University and Brown University, is also consistently recognized by white-collar professionals in China.
