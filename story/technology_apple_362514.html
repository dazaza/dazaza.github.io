Fresh! Five cool Apple concept laptops

by geekzhang on 2010-08-18 10:59:19

1, water concept Apple notebook

The Greeks had proposed centuries earlier that the world was made up of four elements: air, earth, water and fire. Now that the Air Series is available, who's to say there won't be land series, water series, and fire series in the future? With these three elements, Dvice has produced an outstanding vision. We like the flow of Apple's Laptop Water series because it takes even less startup time than a phone. Pour some water on the plane to start the water series concept, and shut down just wipe the water on the plane, and then store it in a space below 0°. Unfortunately, it is not possible to make such a computer now, so let's wait for it to arrive in the next century.

2, 3D concept Apple notebook

We have entered the age of 3D. Imagine spinning around in a 3D world! A hingeless spin design makes your macbook look 3D.

3, Apple triangle screen concept

Apple's triangle-screen notebook is designed to solve the thorny problem many traditional users have with screen real estate. The triangular-screen notebook has an additional side screen on each side to assist the main screen, so the screen can be enlarged to 21 inches. The two foldable side screens provide users with more options when performing multiple tasks. According to some information feedback on the Internet, the initial proof that the game players are all applauded for this design!

4. Foldable touch Apple concept book

Let's take a look at the first flexible OLED touchscreen Apple laptop, the first laptop you can do anything with.

5, air concept virtual Apple notebook

Imagine an air-thin macbook with a virtual keyboard instead of a real one. The use of two touch screens has rendered the mouse useless. There is no longer even a real keyboard. Of course, the old Apple laptop would have looked clunky in front of it.
