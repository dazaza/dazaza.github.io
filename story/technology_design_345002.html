Wig Q&A:

You may have many doubts if you've never tried a wig before. YOKA's small Q&A helps clear up misconceptions and reveals the truth about wigs in a friendly and natural way.

Q: Can people with long hair also wear wigs?

A: Before wearing a wig, you tie up your own hair and use hairpins to closely attach it flat to your scalp. Then, you put on the wig. Therefore, whether your own hair is long or short doesn't matter.

Q: Will wearing a wig in summer be very hot?

A: The top of the wig is designed with a breathable mesh cover, so it won't feel too hot. You can wear it all summer without any issues.

Q: Can you wear a wig during the day? Will it look fake?

A: Nowadays, wig strands are very realistic, fine, and soft, with good luster and texture. As long as you pay attention to matching the color tone with your real hair when choosing a wig piece, it will look authentic.

Q: Is wearing a wig time-consuming?

A: When trying a wig for the first time, it may take some time to figure out. However, this is just like putting on contact lenses; once you get used to it, it only takes two minutes, which is not more time-consuming than styling your own hair.

1. After wearing a wig, there is one more step that can help you score extra points: trimming the wig according to your face shape and style. Everyone's face shape and style is different, and further trimming can make the wig fit you better.

2. Wearing a wig can strongly modify your face shape. When you fix your own hair underneath, you can cleverly adjust it: if your face is rounder, gather your hair towards the center of the top of your head; if your face is longer, fix it symmetrically on both sides. This way, the modification effect after wearing the wig will be seamless.

3. If you're worried about being caught out with a fake fringe, there’s a great prop that can help: wear a beautiful hairband at the connection of the wig piece. It can cleverly cover the dividing line and also enhance the fresh quality of the fringe.

Transformation Appreciation: Do you have wide jaw angles or baby fat? This voluminous long curly wig is absolutely a blessing. The abundant curls loosely falling on both sides of your cheeks can immediately create a small-face effect.

Transformation Trap: For wigs that expose your forehead, you must especially pay attention to ensuring that the wig scalp color matches your forehead skin tone, otherwise, it's easy to be caught out.

Step 1. Divide your hair into three sections. Twist a few braids at the top section and secure them with hairpins as close to the scalp as possible, especially near the hairline, ensuring the hair lies smoothly and neatly.

Step 2. Divide the back section of your hair along the midline into two sections, tie them up, and braid them into two braids. Then secure the braids upward with hairpins.

Step 3. Wear the wig and use a fine-toothed comb to backcomb, creating a lush and charming romantic curl.
