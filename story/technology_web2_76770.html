PlanBus: Share your creations, sync + interact - Fresh Sweet IT.Net

by xjyzhenai on 2008-07-08 08:24:36

image PlanBus! is a synchronized + interactive online platform that can update the latest web resources created by you and of interest to you, and share them with friends in the first time. It allows the high-quality web resources such as videos, blogs, pictures, news, music, etc. created or synchronized by you to be transmitted instantly and efficiently among friends, colleagues, classmates, and provides you with rich and vivid interaction functions such as reply, discussion, and resharing. It can make your "online achievements" become topics for friends to talk about, answers to questions, precious collections...; and also allow you to feel their "online pulse" from the synchronized resources of friends; thus realizing the online interaction with rich content integrating various Internet resources. Your original creation and synchronization may even be used as search resources for search engines to be shared and consulted across the entire Internet.

image

First, planbus! is a completely open interactive platform. It has the ability and technology to gather various web resources from the entire Internet and connect relevant websites, thereby realizing a new type of interconnected network model. This is completely different from general social networking sites: just limiting the content and scope of communication with friends within a certain website. planbus! allows you to use planbus! as the basis, with categories, websites, interests as indexes, and continuously expand your social circle.

Secondly, the operation and use of planbus! are very convenient and user-friendly. You can freely set and adjust the content, sharing range, and presentation mode of your synchronized resources; and can participate in friends' spaces in various forms.

The most important thing is the technical advancement of planbus!: Although terms like synchronization and subscription on the network may not be unfamiliar to you, not everyone knows how to synchronize network resources. In fact, it is true that: the existing synchronization technologies are too complicated and cumbersome: professional terms like URL and RSS are not so easy for you, the vast majority of users, to accept and apply skillfully, so they are not universal, and it is also difficult to develop big and well; and due to the lack of unified standards, some resources cannot even be synchronized. All these problems have been solved in planbus!: We first set up synchronization operation boxes for 9 relatively popular websites in China. You only need to input the corresponding website's username, account name, nickname, or copy and paste the URL, which is very convenient. With the development of the website, the increase in users, and the changes in "network fashion", we will continue to add "Synchronization Express". For some web page contents that were once thought to be unsynchronizable in the past, planbus! also has solutions: this is "Universal Sync".
