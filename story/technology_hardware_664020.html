Chapter 1 It was already time to go home, and most of the students had already left. The vast campus of Ruo Yi Academy suddenly became quiet. However, Class F of Grade 1 was particularly lively. "Damn A class! What's so great about them? They just have a bit of money!" "Shit! A bunch of parasites! Without their family background, they are nothing!" "How dare they mock us like that!" The boys' anger grew in the midst of their discussions. "But... what they said is true, our class... indeed does not..." a bespectacled boy said timidly. The boys discussing sighed in unison, it was a characteristic of this type of reasoning! Ruo Yi Academy was a school that divided students into different levels based on their situations. There were a total of 6 classes from A to F in each grade. From A to F, it was from excellent to poor, from wealthy to impoverished. "Bang!" Suddenly, there was a sound, and the boys turned their heads. They saw a petite girl lying on the ground. "Ow... it hurts..." came a soft voice. She struggled to lift her head, revealing her delicate little face. She was exquisite like the finest porcelain doll. Her fair skin faintly glowed with a rosy hue, under two fine willow-leaf eyebrows, a pair of long eyelashes gently closed. Her amber-colored pupils faintly shimmered with tears, her cherry lips full like flowers slightly pouted. A beautiful head of golden curls, due to the fall, the hair tie in her hair had slipped off, making her even more enchanting. It seemed as if the air froze at the moment she lifted her head.

Chapter 2 She blinked her big eyes, at a loss. After a while, one of the boys reacted: "Is there someone like this in our class?" The boys shook their heads. "I seem to have an impression..." the bespectacled boy spoke up, staring at her for a long time, suddenly realizing, "Ah! Xiao Feifei!" "Do you know her, class monitor?" asked Boy A. "She is our English representative," the class monitor adjusted his glasses. English representative? The faces of many boys involuntarily conjured up an image. That person wore thick glasses, had two braids, and always stayed in the corner of the classroom. "Xiao Feifei?!" The boys all shouted in unison with perfect timing. Xiao Feifei still lay on the ground, feeling very uncomfortable under the gaze of the boys. "Our class actually has such a beautiful beauty!" "Isn't F class saved then?" "Haha! Let's see how the A class group goes wild!" After hesitating for a long time, Xiao Feifei finally opened her red face and spoke: "Can someone help me up? I twisted my ankle..." "Whoosh!" Xiao Feifei only felt as if a gust of wind passed by. When she realized, the boys were already in front of her. "Feifei, let me support you!" "Get out of the way! Feifei princess is mine to support!" "Look at yourself! A toad wanting to eat swan meat!" The crowd gradually became chaotic, Xiao Feifei looked at her glasses being stepped on beyond recognition, and felt like crying!
