Liu Yuhan briefly discusses online marketing strategies - Integrated Online Marketing Communication

by yuhanwangluo on 2009-06-06 16:14:46

The speed of online information dissemination has become the main media for the contemporary mainstream population. Events like Sister Furong,艳men photos, human flesh search, and Sanlu milk powder incident have all fully demonstrated the fast speed of online information dissemination. The Internet drowns us in information, makes news transparent, and makes shopping cheaper and more convenient. The diversified ways of online information dissemination can replace all traditional media forms with sounds, texts, and pictures. People have gradually gotten used to acquiring knowledge and information through the Internet, and online media has already had a strong impact on traditional media! Thus, various marketing integration and dissemination models based on the network have begun to attract corporate attention. Yuhan Network suggests that the network has become the main channel for contemporary enterprises to attract and capture customers, spread marketing concepts, display product features, communicate market information, and drive market development. With the development of integrated online marketing, more and more companies hope to understand some current situations and knowledge about integrated online marketing communication. Therefore, many companies will never miss the network when conducting marketing communication. A suitable integrated online marketing communication plan is even an enterprise's surefire winning strategy.

I. E-commerce

E-commerce is receiving increasing attention from more and more merchants. It's a large trading market that gathers merchants from all walks of life. Choosing B2B platforms for trade has become the first choice for many merchants. The popularization of e-commerce has greatly improved. Choosing to start a business online will also become a major trend during a period of rapid development.

II. Search engines

Search engines can help you quickly find the information you need. Let your customers find you in the shortest time possible. How to make the company's website attract customers is the first step, while how to retain customers is the key. Mastering the two focuses of search engine marketing allows you to grasp and retain customers who actively seek you out at the right time. Let the company's website become the company's salesperson, allowing customers to find you instantly and fall in love with you! Trust from customers is very important in the virtual world!

III. Community marketing

E-commerce and search engines are the basic skills of a company's online marketing, while community marketing is the best platform to showcase a company's soft power. Through a large number of interactive online activities, the exposure of products is greatly increased, and the awareness of netizens about the company is deepened. Yuhan Network suggests that a good idea and activity can play a pivotal role akin to using a small force to move a great weight.

IV. Blog interaction

Blogs themselves are excellent sources of information, and the content of blogs determines whether they have widespread attention and popularity. Through blog sharing, readers interact well with them. Exchanging ideas, it can be said to be a personal electronic business card.

V. Event marketing

In the Internet era, also known as the era of eyeball economy, how to conduct event marketing. How to create events to help companies enhance their brand image once again triggers the thoughts of Internet advertising insiders and companies. Public welfare marketing is the highest realm of corporate marketing, which is easier for society and the public to accept and recognize. For corporate marketing, it is absolutely innovative. The most successful combination of public welfare and online interactive communication undoubtedly requires carefully planned event marketing, and the biggest driving force behind this is the Internet. An organic combination of a typical event and the Internet.

VI. Video marketing

The Internet has evolved from text and images to video. As the needs of netizens continue to increase, the Internet industry has rapidly developed into a sunrise industry in just over a decade. Video marketing starts with creativity, including visual design creativity, interactive participation creativity, word-of-mouth topic creativity, and media application creativity. Video marketing will evolve from product-centered to people-centered new marketing methods. From the perspective of corporate marketing, showcasing the characteristics of individuals, experience sharing, legendary entrepreneurship, or unique business models to commercial users, demonstrating the wisdom of experts. Through online transmission, multiple transmissions occur. This is the unique charm of the new concept of video marketing!

VII. Online public relations

Online public relations means: Enterprises use various forms of information release on the Internet (such as new product releases and leader awards, etc.) to take various activity approaches related to topics of interest to target groups to achieve interaction with online audiences, thereby maintaining good relationships with the public and enhancing specific understanding, thus strengthening the influence of brands or products, promoting the promotion of brands or products. It uses high-tech expression methods of the Internet to create corporate images, providing new thinking, planning ideas, and communication media for modern public relations.

Yuhan Network reminds: Online reputation (online public opinion), it is forming a kind of civilian mainstream. We are more familiar with Carrefour this year, the network once pushed him to the forefront of the storm, and the milk powder incident that happened not long ago. Due to online public opinion, a national brand enterprise disappeared instantly. Snow disasters, earthquakes, and the Olympics, these series of major events have allowed the power of online public opinion to be fully utilized and released, and online public opinion is gradually guiding social public opinion, even changing the rules of public opinion. In addition to actively spreading correct messages about enterprises and products, dealing with online public relations crises has become an important part of online public relations. How to communicate with the media and how to handle it timely and effectively is the core of online public relations. The basic model of online public relations is such: media relations, news releases, including rich media, and public opinion guidance.

Integrated online marketing communication is the advanced stage of online marketing development. The core strategy of integrated online marketing is: "Internet + Creativity + Strategy + Integration + Interaction + Execution + Service". Yuhan reminds that "deep communication", "multiple communications", and "interactive communication" will surely protect your enterprise, making customers come automatically.
