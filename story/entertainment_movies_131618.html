The movie tells of a new police officer, played by Li Yuanba, who is honest and impulsive. He meets the experienced detective Chow Yun-fat, who is carefree and womanizing, through a misunderstanding. They are assigned to work together on a drug trafficking case. They start investigating the gang clues from Maggie Cheung's character, a fitness center coach, which leads to a series of life-and-death crises.

The young police officer Mike Cao (played by Li Yuanba) does not get along with the arrogant senior police officer Francis Lee (played by Chow Yun-fat), but they are mistakenly assigned to investigate a Thai drug lord's murder case together, leading to countless jokes. The big drug dealer Luo Maosen (played by Xu Shaoqiang), due to Snake Bing taking all the drugs, brings a killer Hui (played by Liu Jiahui) to nail Bing to death. Bing's death makes Donna (played by Maggie Cheung) decide to cooperate with the police and reveal the drug delivery location. The police set up a dragnet, and after a fierce battle, Luo Maosen is captured, but the killer Hui escapes. To save Luo, Hui first kills Donna, then takes Lee's sister as a hostage, forcing the police to exchange Luo for her...
