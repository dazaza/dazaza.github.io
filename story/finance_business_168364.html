Packaging equipment

by youngsunmachine on 2009-06-30 15:43:24

Hangzhou Yongchuang Machinery Co., Ltd.'s main product series include: vacuum packaging machines, packing machines, bundling machines, case sealers, shrink wrapping machines, carton forming machines, boxing machines, stretch wrappers, labeling machines, palletizers, and the design of various conveyor production lines; while also producing and selling strapping bands, stretch films, and various other packaging materials. The products are exported to more than fifty countries and regions. The company has established branches in Beijing, Shanghai, Suzhou, Xiamen, Hefei, Guangzhou, Wuhan, Qingdao, Kunming, Chongqing, and other places. By leveraging its favorable geographical advantages and strong production technology capabilities, the company adheres to the business philosophy of "Professionalism, Quality, Service, Integrity", is committed to the development of finished product packaging machinery, focuses on the planning and design of automated packaging processes in the final stages, and coordinates related product materials.

After years of development, Hangzhou Yongchuang Machinery Co., Ltd. has become a professional company that owns core technologies in the finished product packaging industry and has established a service network covering most parts of the country, providing customers with comprehensive, reasonable, high-quality, and efficient products and services at any time and place. Since its establishment, the company has successfully developed and manufactured packaging machinery and supporting equipment across multiple series and dozens of varieties. It has accumulated rich professional knowledge and practical experience, and can design solutions based on enterprises' investment status, actual production needs, and customer requirements, truly delivering turnkey projects to customers.

Main Honors:

1. Possesses 40 Chinese invention and utility model patents, passed CE, TUV, ISO9001 (2000) certification;

2. Provides OEM orders for many world-renowned packaging machine factories;

3. Sole drafting unit of China's mechanical industry standards for "Mechanical Bundling Machines" and "Case Sealers";

4. Designated production enterprise of China's packaging products, honored with the highest quality product award in the packaging industry;

5. Holds an absolute leading position in China's packaging machinery industry, with market share ranging from 20%-70% for eight series of products in the domestic packaging machinery market.

Main Business:

1: Planning, design, and coordination of finished product packaging solutions;

2: Design, manufacture, sales, and maintenance of automated packaging production lines, assembly production lines, and in-plant conveying and handling equipment;

3: Production and sales of packaging machinery;

4: Production and sales of packaging materials.
