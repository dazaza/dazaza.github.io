To My Future Wife (Repost)

by xue94fwsh on 2012-03-02 10:42:18

Share an article, indicating that I am not old yet!!!

1. Marry me and be my wife. Although you are not a pretty girl, I can guarantee that in my eyes, you will be the most beautiful bride in the world. And after marriage, in my eyes, you will still be the most beautiful woman in the world. I hope when you are eighty years old, I can lean over to your ear and tell you: "Thank God, he gave me the most beautiful, lovely toothless old lady in the world."

2. After marriage, if you still want to pursue your career, I will do my best to support you and take care of the housework I should do. I confess that I cannot distinguish between mung bean sprouts and soybean sprouts, and I will make a mess in the kitchen, but I will try my best to follow the instructions in the "Kitchen Gourmet" and strive to become a great cook.

3. If you yearn to escape from the noise and troubles of the world and want to be a quiet little housewife, then I will do my best to work hard and earn more money to support the family. Just, just my smelly socks will be washed by you.

4. I promise that I will decline some unnecessary social engagements and come home earlier because I know you will be lonely at home and worried about me. Moreover, I promise that no matter how tired I am when I come home, I will listen carefully to your stories about what happened during your day because I know you need understanding and support. I am your husband, it is my duty.

5. Have a child with me. I hope we can celebrate your fortieth birthday together with only thirty-six candles on the birthday cake. Moreover, I believe our children will change their mischievous behavior and solemnly tell you like a little adult: "My goodness, mom, you look only thirty years old, my classmates all say you look more like my sister!"

6. I will be loyal to you and our family. Because I know this is the responsibility a man should fulfill. Moreover, I know that if I betray you and have improper relations with other women, I will feel guilty. Guilt will make me self-reproachful, self-reproach will make me ashamed to face you, and being ashamed to face you will make me want to avoid your still pure eyes, wanting to leave you. However, leaving you is the thing I least want to do in my life.

7. If one day you get tired and long to leave me to fly freely, I will see you off. Although I stubbornly think that the sweetness in your arms far surpasses any freedom in the world, I have no right to expect you to think so too. I think I would say to you: when you're tired, come home; when you're weary, come home; when you're hurt, come home. I am your husband, I will wait for your return, this home is your home, the light of this home will always burn for you.

8. If one day you fall in love with another man and want to leave me, I admit that I will be jealous, angry, sad, and painful, but eventually, I will still choose to break up. I just hope that man can treat you as well as I do, take care of you, and take good care of your present and future life. I will still bless you to God as before, and because you are not by my side, I cannot take care of you personally, so I will pray to God to bless you a thousand times.

Maybe a few years later I will have a new wife, or maybe I will be alone until the end of my life. In either case, if you encounter difficulties, please be sure to tell me. Under the premise of not violating morality, I will do my best to help you.

9. I don't know if we were together in our past lives, but that's already a thing of the past and doesn't matter anymore; I don't know if we will be together in our next lives, but that's too far away for me to grasp. I only care about this life. I hope that within my lifetime, I can make my greatest effort to keep you in my arms, protected from wind and rain, healthy, safe, happy. (I have just opened a space, hoping everyone will visit often.)

10. I love you.
