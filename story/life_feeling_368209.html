9 Real Reasons for Divorce Nowdays

by ruosi6 on 2010-10-16 13:31:23

The divorce rate is getting higher and higher, but after all, there are reasons for that. What are the reasons? Here we have整理ed the top 9 most realistic reasons for you:

NO.1 The topmost realistic reason for divorce now: House

The newlyweds of the post-80s generation divorced just after marriage because the property certificate did not have the wife's name on it. 40.72% of men think they need to have a house before marrying a wife, while 22.63% of women choose to marry only if the man has a house. As early as in 2008, the results of an online survey showed this result. Mr. Qian Zhongshu said that marriage is like a walled city, but he probably didn't expect that a house would become the ticket to enter this city. Those who bought expensive tickets and entered the walled city have a new name: mortgage slaves. Therefore, NO.1 is the most realistic reason: House.

NO.2 The most realistic reason for divorce now: Dowry

If a house is a new obstacle produced by the new era, then dowry money starting at 50,000 yuan and increasing by 10,000 yuan each year is unbearable for prospective grooms, and the dowry has been a long-standing problem in our country for thousands of years. According to Chinese wedding customs, when a man marries a wife, he should give a dowry or bride price to the woman's family. The amount of the dowry depends on local conditions and the economic status of the parties involved. With the passage of time, the dowry money keeps rising. In recent years, it has been observed that the average increase per year is 10,000 yuan, and it has gradually become a bargaining chip for marriage. Under such circumstances, both families may even have conflicts over this issue, which in turn affects the feelings of the couple-to-be. The dowry is also a headache.

NO.3 The most realistic reason for divorce now: Work

Nowadays, if the husband has to work elsewhere, the countdown for divorce begins. For the post-80s generation, it's hard to get married, but even harder to find a job. A job opportunity is hard-won, so many people value it greatly. Thus, when career development conflicts with personal rights being infringed upon, many people would rather tolerate the unreasonable demands of their careers and neglect the pursuit of legitimate rights. This kind of submission reflects a servile mindset, which, in the era of awakening rights awareness and happening among the younger generation, makes people feel powerless and want to cry. A sigh for the most realistic reason for divorce now: Work.

NO.4 The most helpless reason for divorce now: Mother-in-law

Nowadays, divorce happens because the mother-in-law dislikes her son-in-law and persuades her daughter to abort and divorce. In the environment of only children, every parent fears that their child will be wronged. Post-80s poor sons-in-law hate materialistic mothers-in-law, and post-80s girls always say they can marry men without houses, but their mothers don't agree. Experts say that high housing prices are due to the "mother-in-law economy." These groups of mothers-in-law from the 1950s, who seem like kind aunties in the eyes of neighbors, are extremely strict and uncompromising when it comes to their daughters' marriages. A sigh for the most helpless reason for divorce now: Mother-in-law.

NO.5 The most helpless reason for divorce now: Mother-in-law (another perspective)

Nowadays, the mother-in-law causes increasingly fierce battles between mother-in-law and daughter-in-law, making her a victim.

If the mother-in-law makes the men suffer, then the mother-in-law is the nightmare of countless women. For thousands of years, the relationship between mother-in-law and daughter-in-law has been one of the biggest problems in family life. In ancient society, which emphasized filial piety and the three obediences and four virtues, the daughter-in-law was undoubtedly the absolute weaker party. But in contemporary times, traditional shackles have been broken, and the post-80s generation is the most self-centered generation in history. The daughter-in-law wants to take charge, but the mother-in-law won't let her. Thus, the conflict between mother-in-law and daughter-in-law becomes more and more intense. A sigh for the most helpless reason for divorce now: Mother-in-law.

NO.6 The most helpless reason for divorce now: Breaking up couples

Nowadays, she is put under house arrest by her father for marrying a thrice-divorced man.

Breaking up couples is something more common in ancient times and modern novels or films, but it is rarely seen in contemporary times, especially for the fearless post-80s generation who wouldn't dare to break up others' relationships. However, nothing is absolute. Sometimes, encountering extreme parents or elders who insist on breaking up young couples, the post-80s generation still cannot disobey. A sigh for the most helpless reason for divorce now: Breaking up couples.

NO.7 The most bizarre reason for divorce now: Astrological compatibility

Nowadays, post-80s urban youth believe in fortune-telling and divorce just because their astrological signs do not match.

Divorcing because of unmatched astrological signs seems like something that could only happen in ancient times. But nowadays, many post-80s youth are really into fortune-telling. Holding the results calculated by some unknown great master, they rashly decide on major life events such as work, love, marriage, and divorce. If breaking up couples leads to tragedy, then divorcing due to fortune-telling is a complete farce. The most bizarre reason for divorce now: Astrological compatibility.

NO.8 The most bizarre reason for divorce now: Wedding pranks

Nowadays, the bride asks the groom's father to kneel down to receive her, angering the groom: "I won't get married."

Making things difficult for the groom during the wedding is originally for fun. Pranking at the wedding is also a traditional wedding custom. In the past, it was mostly about having some fun, asking for red envelopes, or perhaps secretly listening to the honeymoon sounds from the corner of the wall. But now, it seems to be getting more and more out of control. Throwing flour, changing wedding clothes, barking like a dog, parading through the streets, guessing underwear, and even involving elders in pranks. When the pranks go too far, the farce turns into a disaster. Pranking at weddings is too bizarre.

NO.9 The most bizarre reason for divorce now: Toilet fights, snoring, and underwear scandals

Netizens share bizarre divorce reasons of the post-80s generation: snoring and constipation make the list.

Recently, there was a post titled "Seemingly absurd divorce reasons of the post-80s generation," where the poster listed some divorce cases of post-80s people around them. As netizens say, there's no limit to how embarrassing these reasons can be, leading to sighs over the most bizarre reasons for divorce now: toilet fights, snoring, and underwear scandals.

After reading this, aren't you left with a bitter smile? It's truly unbelievable that there are all sorts of reasons, even the most bizarre ones.
