The Amazing Elderly Man Gives an Interview

Recently, a reporter from the Southern Metropolis Daily met with the amazing elderly man Wu Shengming at the Bao Hua Haijing Grand Hotel in Haikou. The old man was kind and friendly, well-dressed in a green plaid suit, with beige stockings and crystal heel high heels. His skin was white and despite being 77 years old, he looked only about 60. He warmly invited the reporter to sit down.

"I lived in Zhengzhou, even though I was sweeping toilets, I still dressed very neatly. The neighbors all said I loved dressing up," Wu humorously told the reporter. Through his conversation, one could feel his resilience and optimistic spirit. After a few simple sentences, Wu recounted his life's tumultuous and touching story.

Running Away With Her Young Boyfriend Led To A Life Of Turmoil

Wu Shengming was born in Zhejiang Province's Shengzhou City. The Wu family had been wealthy merchants for generations, and her family arranged a marriage for her when she was young. She, with a stubborn personality, believed that her fate should not be decided by others. On the day of her wedding at the age of 16, Wu Shengming pretended to go out to buy something, but instead fled alone to Shanghai. To survive, at the age of 16, she became a maid for a family.

After wandering in Shanghai for four years, Wu Shengming returned to her hometown and used her savings of more than 2,000 yuan to open a small grocery store. Wu Shengming fell in love with Zhang Lin, who worked in the opposite repair shop. Zhang Lin was seven years younger than Wu Shengming, and they quickly started dating. The Wu family disapproved of the match due to Zhang Lin's poor family background, but with her stubborn character, she chose to elope with her boyfriend and escaped to Qinghai, where they opened a small electrical repair shop to make a living.

In the second year in Qinghai, Wu Shengming became pregnant, but after lifting heavy objects, she suddenly fell and miscarried. Two months later, she became pregnant again and miscarried once more. The doctor said she had habitual miscarriages, suspecting it might be related to the high-altitude reaction. After discussing with her husband, they moved back to the plains to have children. Later, they moved to Zhengzhou, Henan, but she continued to miscarry naturally.

After experiencing ten miscarriages, Wu Shengming became pregnant for the eleventh time at the age of 42. She lay in the hospital for over six months and finally gave birth to her daughter Zhang Yan smoothly. From the moment she saw her daughter, Wu Shengming secretly vowed to give her daughter the happiest life possible!

Reaching The Peak Of Her Career And Suddenly Becoming A Prisoner

Having lost her mother at a young age, Wu Shengming understood the great significance of a mother for a child. Wu Shengming valued her daughter Zhang Yan more than her own life. To provide a better life for her daughter, Wu Shengming and her husband decided to return to their hometown of Shengzhou to develop. At the time, China had just begun its reform and opening-up policy, gradually transitioning towards a market economy. Wu Shengming seized the opportunity and opened a company in Shengzhou. To express their deep love for their daughter, they named the company: Ai Yan Trading Company.

Wu Shengming started with radios and nylon clothing, and her business quickly took off. She traveled between Zhengzhou, Fujian, and Shanghai, and by 1984, Wu Shengming's career reached its peak, earning millions each month. "At that time, my life could be described as luxurious; I didn't need to consider how much money I spent; I could spend however I wanted," Wu Shengming told the reporter. By the 1990s, her net worth had already reached over twenty million yuan.

"The desire for money is endless, and money is the root of all evil, which has been verified in my case." Wu Shengming told the reporter that in 1985, through connections, she obtained a plan to import 48 luxury cars into Nanjing. Importing under the name of a military retirement home and reselling them at a higher price earned her several million. Since she did not have the qualifications to operate car businesses, on November 1, 1986, the Intermediate People's Court of Shanghai sentenced Wu Shengming to death for smuggling and contract fraud. After appealing, the Higher Court reduced her sentence to life imprisonment.

Her Husband Betrayed Her, And Her Daughter Committed Suicide

The phrase "all hope lost" accurately describes Wu Shengming's mood when she first entered prison. Initially, her daughter Zhang Yan did not know where her mother had gone, as her father Zhang Lin kept it hidden from her, lying that her mother was away doing business. Over time, Zhang Yan began to suspect, and reluctantly, Zhang Lin revealed the truth to Zhang Yan.

"My daughter's letters were the pillar that kept me alive." Talking about her daughter, the strong Wu Shengming shed tears. Just a year after entering prison, her husband Zhang Lin started living with her daughter's nanny and proposed divorce to Wu Shengming. After completing the divorce procedures, Zhang Lin remarried, while Zhang Yan was sent to live with her uncle in Yangshe, a small town in Zhangjiagang, Jiangsu Province. Her understanding daughter wrote letters to console her mother: "Mom, no matter what, there's still your daughter who loves you in this world. I will never change. For me, take care of yourself, live well, and reform well." Thereafter, mother and daughter communicated warmth through letters.

"As long as I received a letter from my daughter, that day I felt that my meals tasted better than usual, and I felt energized doing anything." Wu Shengming said.

Misfortunes Never Come Alone, Another Tragedy Struck Wu Shengming

On the day of her daughter Zhang Yan's 16th birthday, she invited a few friends to celebrate at home. Due to her uncle finding the noise unbearable, during an argument with him, Zhang Yan learned that her mother had been sentenced to life imprisonment. Desperate, Zhang Yan chose to end her life by taking pesticide. Before ingesting the poison, Zhang Yan asked a classmate to write a letter to her mother on her behalf.

Two years after her daughter's passing, the prison informed Wu Shengming of her daughter's death.

Serving An 18-Year Sentence And Cleaning Toilets Upon Release

"At that moment, I only wanted to follow my daughter, there was no longer any meaning to continue living," Wu Shengming wiped her tears repeatedly with tissues. The prison guards patiently counseled Wu Shengming and showed her her daughter Zhang Yan's last letter: "Try to do something beneficial for society. You can take in those who are homeless or without families. If you don't agree, I won't be able to rest in peace...". These words deeply moved Wu Shengming and gave her the courage to continue living.

Wu Shengming became more proactive in her rehabilitation, and until 2003, she received eight commutations. On July 28, 2003, Wu Shengming walked out of the prison gates, then already 71 years old. She was assigned to Ming Gong Road in Zhengzhou, where the police station and street office arranged for her to work cleaning public toilets, with a monthly salary of 400 yuan. The community provided her with an 18-square-meter small house.

With guilt towards her daughter, Wu Shengming repeatedly told herself that she must persevere; her goal was still far away. In 2004, Wu Shengming was rated as an outstanding cleaner by her street office.

Starting A Business Again At Age 70 And Now Worth Millions

In 2004, national media vied to report on Wu Shengming's story, attracting widespread attention from all sectors of society. Kind-hearted people frequently contacted Wu Shengming. From then on, Wu Shengming realized that her wish was not just hers alone; many kind-hearted people cared about her wish and wanted to help her fulfill it.

In September 2005, a dramatic turn of events occurred when a swindler surnamed Zhang appeared, inviting her to jointly plant a grape orchard. As a result, Zhang not only defrauded Wu Shengming of over 300,000 yuan borrowed but also deceived several of Wu Shengming's friends out of over 300,000 yuan, totaling nearly 700,000 yuan.

"Since we've already started planting grapes, let's continue. Without him, I can still manage well," Wu Shengming said. To fulfill her daughter's wish, in 2006, she planted 170 mu (approx. 28 acres) of grapes in Yangling, Shaanxi. Now, she has founded Yangling Hongyang Fruit Industry Technology Development Co., Ltd., Grandma Wu Xingnong Technology Development Co., Ltd., Yangling Health Chicken Breeding Farm, Grandma Wu Chain Chat Bars, and restaurants. These projects are currently operational, and two of them will soon start making profits.

Her latest project involves raising funds to build an ecological park in the Yangling area, integrating tourism, sightseeing, and planting. It aims to become a highlight of local ecological agriculture development.

Wu Shengming mentioned that she visited Hainan in the 1990s. Now, with Hainan constructing an international tourist island, there are numerous opportunities. Wu Shengming stated that developing in Hainan is inevitable, and as long as the funds are sufficient, she will come to Hainan to develop the tourism industry.

Before turning 50, she had assets worth 20 million; at 52, she was sentenced to life imprisonment, her husband betrayed her, her daughter committed suicide, and after being released from prison, Grandma Wu Shengming from Zhejiang restarted her business.

An Extraordinary Old Woman Starts A Business At Age 70 And Amasses Assets Worth Millions

In the 1990s, she was less than 50 years old, with assets worth 20 million, and a happy family; because of reselling imported cars, at 52, she was sentenced to life imprisonment and became a prisoner. After being imprisoned, her husband betrayed her, her daughter committed suicide, and after receiving eight commutations, she served 18 years and 2 months in prison. When she came out, she was over 70 and had no choice but to clean toilets in a poor neighborhood. Filled with guilt for her daughter and to fulfill her daughter's final wish, three years later, she started from scratch again. Today, she has amassed assets worth millions. She is Wu Shengming, an extraordinary woman in her 70s who created a legendary life.

As the Women's Day approaches, Wu Shengming came to Hainan. She said that taking advantage of the construction of the International Tourist Island, she intends to examine relevant projects and plans to develop in Hainan in the future. Therefore, the Southern Metropolis Daily reporter exclusively interviewed this amazing elder.
