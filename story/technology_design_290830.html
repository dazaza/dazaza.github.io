Guoyuan Securities: Shan Ying Paper Industry, a packaging paper producer with obvious regional advantages＿stock

by dx0966632 on 2009-12-03 22:44:20

Investment highlights:

1. The company's main business is packaging paper. Its leading products are corrugated cardboard, kraft lining paper, corrugated cardboard boxes and other packaging papers as well as newsprint, offset printing paper for books and periodicals and other cultural papers. The company is currently a national large-scale enterprise of the first rank, one of the 30 key papermaking enterprises nationwide. In 2008, the company realized operating revenue of RMB 2.859 billion yuan and net profit of RMB 13 million yuan.

2. Significant recovery in performance, stock. In the third quarter this year, the company realized operating revenue of RMB 769 million yuan, increasing by 5.78% year-on-year, and the single-quarter operating revenue level has reached the level of the second quarter of 2008; net profit was RMB 41.32 million yuan, increasing by 318.64% year-on-year; single-quarter earnings per share realized were 0.09 yuan. Compared with the second quarter this year, the operating revenue in the third quarter increased by 10.97% month-on-month, and net profit increased by 165.72% month-on-month.

3. Gross margin improvement is the main reason. The gross margin for the first three quarters of the company was 16.15%, reaching the highest historical level. The main reasons for the increase in gross margin are on the one hand adjustment of product structure, stock forum, and on the other hand benefiting from the rise in paper prices. Due to the fourth quarter being the traditional sales peak season, and the recovery of the macro economy will drive the rebound in paper demand, it is expected that there will still be room for further increases in paper prices in the future.

4. Over-supply pressure in the corrugated cardboard market is relatively large, expecting the recovery of exports. After experiencing significant expansion in 2007 and 2008, China’s corrugated cardboard production capacity showed an obvious phenomenon of over-supply. Only the newly added production capacity of several core enterprises within the industry in 2007 and 2008 reached 5.55 million tons, while the domestic annual demand increment is only around 1.5 million tons, making the phenomenon of over-capacity very obvious. We predict that in 2010, China's corrugated cardboard market will still be in the stage of digesting over-capacity.

5. Maintain "Recommend" investment rating for the company. It is estimated that the earnings per share of the company in 2009-2010 will be 0.14 yuan and 0.25 yuan respectively, stock knowledge. The recovery of the domestic economic situation will drive the market demand for corrugated cardboard and kraft lining paper, and the company's profitability will gradually improve. The current stock price of the company corresponds to a dynamic P/E ratio of 21.2 times for 2010, which is relatively reasonable valuation. Therefore, we maintain the "Recommend" investment rating for the company.
