Things remain the same but people have changed, yet my heart remains sweet.

by lili1 on 2012-01-28 22:00:49

Things have changed, but my heart remains sweet.

Late at night, I know I have to go to the Western Philosophy class early tomorrow morning, and I understand how important it is. But these few days, I've been hurt. I'm really hurt. Sometimes, I even feel like this heart no longer belongs to me, so light, so light. I wish you would just fall down and never come back.

That day, I enthusiastically called Mom. I chatted with her a lot, but from childhood until now, I've never told her about my bitterness and difficulties. I always secretly bleed in my heart, letting my tears flow back into the past, just like Brother Dong. Maybe I am what you said, a cold-blooded animal with hot blood. I never shed a tear easily, no matter what. That day was as usual, I only told Mom how good the school is, how well I am doing, and the thing about going to Beijing. Mom asked if I needed money. I said no, since it's an exercise... Mom paused for a moment, didn't say anything, but I knew what she wanted to say. Then I quickly said, "Mom, don't worry, your son is great." The next day, Dad sent me money. Suddenly, I realized how unsuccessful I am. Every time I call home, the first thing my parents ask is, "Do you need money again?"

Mom asked me, "Have you called Grandpa?" I said, "No." "Grandma just passed away, you should remember to call him often and ask about your grandpa," Mom said. I just nodded slightly and said yes. At that moment, my eyes were already wet with tears, but they still couldn't fall. It turns out I am so unfilial. And I have always been like this. I am really so cold-blooded.

That day, I also talked a lot with Sister. I felt that Sister has changed, really changed, changed a lot. Some things are really not dare to think about, so I can only let myself become more cold-blooded, not caring about so many things. Sister, really, brother found that you have changed. I want to have a chance to talk to you, the two of us sitting quietly on the mountain, looking at the stars, talking about our past, your present, my present, and our future. But suddenly I realized how far that is from me. Since you got married and had a child, you have changed. A trench that cannot be crossed has been dug between you and me.

Sister, I just want to say to you, when you enter a circle, hope you can appropriately step out, look at it from different perspectives and horizons. As the saying goes, "The one involved is confused while the onlooker sees clearly." Only by looking at things from different angles can we see them more clearly and better display your talent and ability in this field.

But the comforting thing is that my younger brother has grown up. In the past, I really didn't quite look forward to you, but I know you are the most emotional among our siblings. Whenever you see those touching stories, your eyes redden instantly, and you might even cry. But I don't like you neglecting your main business and only playing games and watching TV. But that day, when you chatted with me, I found that you have grown up, no longer the ignorant you from before. You have learned to "breathe" and "inhale". Keep it up, I will be your strong backing, always supporting you.

Brother Dong, there's no need for your younger brother to say more. You understand. From childhood to 20 years old, we played together until the day the college entrance exam ended. We are forever real brothers, not by blood.

But wind and rain are wind and rain, these few days, I have also received sunshine. I really like Yutian, a graceful and elegant you. I believe you also like me. Whether complete or fragmented, I will always be with you.

Still that sentence, every day the sun is new. Things may change, but my heart remains sweet.
