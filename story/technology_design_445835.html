From Passive to Active – The Ultimate Guide for IT Women to Conquer Men

In this world, there are countless men and women. With the development of the world, more and more women are entering the IT industry.

The vast majority of women who enter the IT field tend to have outgoing and cheerful personalities. They prefer direct communication and act decisively, with fewer feminine charms but more independence.

As the saying goes, "It's like crossing a mountain for a man to pursue a woman, but just a layer of gauze for a woman to pursue a man."

However, for most men, things that come too easily are often not cherished. Relationships initiated by women are hard to maintain long-term.

Therefore, our IT women must turn passivity into activity and practice stillness as a form of motion.

Why chase him when you can simply reel him in?

First, in daily life, you can blend in with him, removing any barriers between you. Show thoughtfulness through small actions, making him realize that despite your openness, you are still gentle.

Next, at an appropriate time, you can show some coquettish behavior, letting him know that even though you're open-minded, you still have a feminine side.

Over time, friendship can naturally evolve into love.

But be cautious: At the beginning, avoid being too direct, as it may create awkwardness.

If he is interested in you, he will definitely understand your intentions and respond positively, gradually... haha, and everything will be fine.

If he isn't interested, he will distance himself from you. Even if you tell him directly, he might ignore you.

As IT women, we all possess an inherent spirit of striving and progress. Of course, in matters of pursuit, this spirit should be fully utilized.

If you like him, go ahead and pursue him bravely! Let's start here! It’s now or never—express your feelings, state your intentions, seize the opportunity, and grab the happiness that belongs to you!

But when women pursue men, they need more delicate techniques...

1. If you now have a clear target and decide to take action, let me share a trick. I believe the best way to win a man's heart is to be good to him. As the saying goes, softness can overcome hardness. But remember, don’t always be good to him in every situation. For instance, only take care of him when he's sick. Remember: Never spoil a man too much. Don’t let him think it's something you're obligated to do or enjoy doing. Instead, let him be moved by your attentiveness and warmth, making him accustomed to your kindness, understanding your heart, and gradually becoming unable to live without your goodness.

Emotional truth: Habit is the highest level of love.

2. However, I believe women pursuing men must also bear the following points in mind:

1. Avoid relentless pursuit.

If you invite him multiple times and he keeps refusing, it means he doesn't have romantic feelings for you. In such cases, it's better to stop. If you don't want to give up and wish to reveal your feelings, you can try using special props or occasions and jokingly say, "I really like you." If he has feelings for you, he'll take it seriously; if not, he'll just treat it as a joke, saving both of you from embarrassment.

2. Avoid prolonged pursuit.

During the process of a girl pursuing a boy, it shouldn't last longer than three months. During this period, if the boy hasn't introduced you separately to his boss, female friends, or female colleagues, hasn't taken the initiative to call or text you, and hasn't accepted you as you imagine, or hasn't treated you as well as you treat him, hasn't publicly acknowledged you, and hasn't said "I love you," it means he has indirectly rejected you while enjoying your care. At this point, you should learn to let go. If you can't bear to give up and still deeply love him, then try to change your approach to make him fall in love with you.

3. Avoid being overly calculating.

Some girls mistakenly believe that influencing the boy's father or relatives, or creating an impression among his friends and colleagues that the two of you are dating, will work. In reality, concern from his father or playful teasing from friends and colleagues might create a reverse psychology in him, making him wonder, "How does everyone in the world know except the main characters?" Your sincere intentions might make him feel that you're scheming and manipulative, leading him to think you have ulterior motives.

Dear IT women, men are creatures that can easily mislead you. At first, you might think he's a prince, but later you find out he's just a frog. But there are excellent men in this world. He is gentle, kind, considerate, full of charm, highly responsible, and ideally has some assets. But most importantly, he loves you! At this moment, dear ones, remember to seize every opportunity and find ways to marry the man you love!
