Typically, surgical removal is the primary method for treating kidney tumors, mainly divided into radical surgery and palliative surgery. To create a higher quality of life, patients need to take scientifically effective maintenance measures after kidney tumor surgery. If done reasonably and appropriately, it can effectively improve the patient's physical condition and immunity. Additionally, kidney cancer patients need to protect their kidney function after surgery. Experts from the Biological Treatment Center of the Guangxi Armed Police Corps Hospital introduce specific postoperative care methods as follows:

1. Diversity of food: Eat a variety of vegetables, fruits, and coarse-processed staple foods, with plant-based foods as the main focus. Ensure the intake of 3-5 types of vegetables and 2-4 types of fruits every day, paying special attention to consuming dark-colored vegetables rich in vitamin A precursors and fruits rich in vitamin C.

2. Maintain an appropriate weight, avoiding being too light or overweight.

3. Keep suitable physical activity and actively participate in appropriate sports activities.

4. Strictly quit smoking and it is recommended not to drink alcohol.

5. Daily intake of red meat (referring to beef, lamb, pork, and its products) should be below 80 grams. Choose poultry and fish whenever possible and reduce the intake of soy products.

6. Limit animal fat intake, choose plant oils but also limit them. Total fat and oil energy should account for 15%-30% of total energy.

7. Diet should be bland rather than salty, daily salt usage should not exceed 6 grams; and limit sugar intake.

8. Do not eat moldy or spoiled food. When cooking fish and meat, keep the temperature low, do not eat burnt food, grilled meat, or preserved meats.

9. Monitor the safe limits of food additives, pesticides, and other chemical pollutants.

10. Avoid using nephrotoxic drugs such as non-steroidal anti-inflammatory drugs and certain antibiotics.

11. Actively monitor and control blood pressure within the normal range; control blood sugar normally.
