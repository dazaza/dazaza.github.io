Online earnings support the investigation station for nationals.

by netgirl on 2008-01-08 04:19:08

Currently, there are still many survey stations that support Chinese nationals. However, some of them don't pay cash, while others offer points that can be exchanged for gifts instead of money.

Let's start with the exceptions:

1. Gift-giving but no cash: Your Voice (Your Heart's Voice)

As a leading global market research company under ACNielsen, you can accumulate electronic points after completing each questionnaire to exchange for different products, coupons, or donate to charity organizations online (note: not in cash). Even if you do not meet the criteria to become an interviewee for certain surveys, every time you attempt to participate in a survey, you have the chance to win prizes worth RMB 250 to 3,500 in our grand lottery.

2. Paying cash but not for surveys:

Tiaoke.com - Very popular online, earning money through recommendations.

Below is a summary of some highly recommended survey websites that support Chinese nationals:

1. Zhongzhiku Survey Network (HOT) - The most extensive range of surveys among all available. It is a must-do project for domestic online survey companies (first choice).

2. Chinese Version Ciao Reward Surveys (HOT) - Ciao is the Chinese survey station opened by Ciao-surveysavvy, Europe's largest survey company. Reward points are virtual online currency used on the Ciao website to measure the rewards users receive. Users can apply for cash redemption once they reach 3000 points. The exchange rate is 100 reward points = 1 RMB. Membership is free for all users of all age groups with no special requirements. Members earn points by answering questions, and the points vary depending on factors such as question length. You can also invite friends through "Recommend Friends" and earn 500 points (5 RMB) for each referral! Payments are made monthly.

3. Xin Qin Survey - One of the fastest and highest-earning sites. It is also one of China's oldest reputable survey websites with excellent credibility, powerful site functionality, and rich content. There are multiple commercial surveys each month and daily free surveys available. After logging in successfully, make sure to complete all the "Edit Information" options next to your avatar to gain 50 points and qualify for future surveys. If you skip this step, you won’t be eligible for surveys. Surveys are tailored based on the information you provide.

4. Chinese Survey Network - A Chinese survey station opened by a treasure-hunting webmaster. Even if your account has only 1 RMB, you can always request your cash rewards from the redemption center at any time.

5. Datashuo Survey Zone - The portal community of InterfaceAsia, a world-renowned survey company in China. Each survey offers compensation worth 5-100 RMB, with chances to win merchants' valuable dollar prizes.

6. EZYPOINT Survey (Chinese) - An emerging survey company in Shanghai. One of its humanized features is sending survey reminders over the weekend. 10 points = 1 RMB.

7. 100 Survey Network - Points can be exchanged for gifts, and coins can be redeemed 1:1 for cash. There are numerous surveys, but sometimes you may miss out due to not meeting the eligibility criteria.

8. First Survey - A rising star and one of China’s most promising survey websites. Although you cannot earn points by referring someone, you will always get 15% of your downline’s survey earnings. Additionally, people referred by your downlines can earn 10% commissions, continuing up to five levels with percentages of 15%, 10%, 5%, 5%, and 5%.

9. 51Point - Many members have earned significant amounts from this platform, which was quite popular for some time.

10. YiPai Survey Network (Epanel) - A survey company in Shanghai with very simple surveys that usually take about 2 minutes to complete. 100 points = 1 RMB, and payments via Alipay require 5000 points... There is also a daily login bonus (lucky ones can earn 100 points).

11. QTICK Questionnaire Network - Members receive 500 points upon registration and verification and can enter lotteries. For each friend you refer who becomes a verified member, you earn 20 points. Participating in one survey can earn you up to 10,000 points = 100 RMB.

12. Planet Pulse (Chinese) - A survey company spanning over 20 countries with a simplified Chinese option available. No worries if your English isn't good. Each survey can offer rewards as high as $50.

13. OpinionWorld (Jisi Network): http://www.opinionworld.com/ow/ With over 2 million members, it is likely familiar to everyone.

14. GlobalTestMarket (Chinese): http://www.globaltestmarket.com

Of course, there are other platforms, but they are less reliable and harder to earn money from. Friends are welcome to recommend more here.
