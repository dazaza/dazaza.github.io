Self-regulation methods for patients with depression:

1. Try to be more extroverted and communicate with friends as much as possible.

2. When sleeping, think about others' good qualities and learn to tolerate their flaws.

3. Participate in more physical exercise to improve physical fitness, which is very helpful for regulating depression.

4. Ensure good sleep and maintain a regular lifestyle. If sleep is really poor, you can take some Chinese herbal medicine, which will help regulate depression.

5. Do good deeds. The appearance of schizophrenia in the elderly is often closely related to abnormal psychological conditions. Don't expect others to return the favor because helping others is also helping yourself.

6. Discuss your worries with your family and seek their help. Trust that your family won't make mistakes. This is a psychological adjustment for regulating depression.

7. Throw all the unhappiness into the trash can.

8. Don't gossip about others behind their backs, nor care too much about others' opinions of you. Sometimes losing out is actually gaining. Don't overthink, otherwise, it's hard to regulate depression.

9. If conditions allow, increase nutrition, but don't eat too many snacks. Overeating may lead to obesity, which is not good for the stomach.

10. If it's due to romantic issues, you must be open-minded, www.syzhiliao.com. There will be plenty of opportunities in the future.
