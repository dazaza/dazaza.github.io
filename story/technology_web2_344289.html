Teacher Kicks Student's Phone Video. 　　Zhuhai Message (reported by Chen Zhi Jia) An education director at a Zhuhai middle school first slapped a student and then kicked the student in the office. Since the night before last, a video of "teacher beating student" from Beishida Zhuhai attached middle school has gone viral on the campus forum. Yesterday afternoon, Zhuhai Education Bureau and the school issued a statement about the situation and the handling of the involved teacher, who was suspended. The school authorities said that after the incident, the school leaders and the involved teacher have apologized to the parents of the beaten student and obtained their forgiveness. The teacher slapped the student while being recorded. Yesterday, students from Beishida Zhuhai attached middle school tipped off the reporter that at around 9 o'clock in the evening of the 9th, an education director surnamed Ma slapped a rule-breaking student in the office, and after the student fell down, the teacher kicked the student's body. Some students secretly recorded the scene with their mobile phones. After the incident, the beaten student went to the hospital for relevant examinations and it was found that his ears were bleeding. The next day at noon, the parents reported to the police, and the police have intervened in the investigation. Yesterday, many students from the school introduced to the reporter that Director Ma teaches Chinese to the second year high school students and mainly takes charge of student management work. "He treats good students well, but sometimes he can be rough with disobedient students," a student said. Yesterday, the reporter tried to interview the involved teacher, but according to the relevant person in charge, Director Ma was sick and running a high fever during the incident and is still receiving intravenous therapy in the hospital. The school has already apologized to the parents. Yesterday afternoon, Zhuhai Education Bureau and the school jointly announced the results of the school's handling of the incident, and the involved teacher was suspended. According to the school authorities, before the incident, there had been several incidents of mass fights among students outside the school, and Director Ma from the student affairs office had been dealing with these matters. The beaten student was one of the participants in the fight. The school and Director Ma have personally apologized to the parents of the beaten student.
