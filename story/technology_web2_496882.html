Yesterday, Guangzhou Daily teamed up with Guangzhou New TV Tower to specially invite one hundred readers to experience the "World New High Tower Low Carbon High Technology Journey" in advance.

The scope of readers who climbed the tower was extensive

Among the participating citizens, there were teachers, students, doctors, lawyers, civil servants, waiters, housewives, freelancers, and retired engineers, covering a very wide range. After a brief ceremony, the readers started climbing the tower. When the sightseeing elevator ascended rapidly at a speed of 5 meters per second, the broad and magnificent scenery on both sides of the Pearl River quickly unfolded like a long scroll, eliciting continuous cheers inside the elevator. Everyone took out their "long guns and short cannons" (cameras) to take pictures competitively. After 1 minute and 20 seconds, the elevator steadily stopped at the 84th floor, which is 433.2 meters high. The readers rushed to the windows as soon as they got off the elevator, closely experiencing the beauty of Guangzhou from all angles.

Gong Guihua, a "mother holding her child" from Jinsha Zhou, and her son Liu Zhiyong were even happier. Gong Guihua pushed her son in a wheelchair around the viewing platform again and again: "Thank you to the newspaper for letting us climb the tower to enjoy the view!" Just as everyone was having fun, several orange-clad paint workers descended from above, causing the citizens to exclaim in surprise: "Spiderman!"

Looking outside, the Zhujiang New Town directly north of the TV tower, Ersha Island to the west, Hunted Bridge and South China Bridge to the east, and even further places came into view one by one. From the TV tower looking down, everything seemed like sand table models.

The suspended glass house is exciting

The suspended glass house on the viewing platform was extremely popular. "Looking down through the glass floor, it feels like being in the air, it's so exciting!" Miss Li, who rushed in first, said she was scared while asking her friend to take photos for her. The tour guide told everyone: "These are all tempered glasses, absolutely safe, Guangzhou New TV Tower ticket reservation, and in the future, telescopes and other equipment will be installed inside the viewing platform for everyone to look far away."

Revealing the high technology of the new TV tower

Highlight 1 High-speed elevators Only 80 seconds to the 84th floor

According to the introduction, the new TV tower is equipped with 6 high-speed elevators, all of which have the world's fastest lifting speeds and are currently the elevators with the highest single lifting height in the world. Two of them are sightseeing elevators with a lifting speed of 5 meters/second, two passenger elevators with a lifting speed of 6 meters/second, and two fire elevators that can lift 10 meters per second. Sightseeing elevators and passenger elevators can accommodate 48 passengers each time. Vice President Huang Jiatai of Guangzhou Urban Investment Group said that the six high-speed elevators can transport 1,400 people per hour. "There is no problem receiving 20,000 people per day."

Highlight 2 A thousand-ton water tank Vibration reduction and fire extinguishing

The TV tower is tall and slim, belonging to the "wind-sensitive" structure in engineering. The new TV tower globally innovates the active-passive hybrid control system for vibration reduction, using two fire water tanks with a total weight of 1,200 tons installed on floors 85 and 86 as tuned masses. When the tower sways, the water tank can slide in the opposite direction through sensors, reducing the swaying amplitude of the tower, decreasing the swaying displacement by 40%.

In normal winds, the main tower uses the passive tuning system to reduce shaking or vibrations; in rare strong winds, the passive tuning system works together with the active tuning system to ensure optimal vibration reduction. Guangzhou New TV Tower can withstand an earthquake of magnitude 8 and a typhoon that occurs once every hundred years. Moreover, the mesh leak-proof cavity design of the TV tower can greatly reduce the weight and wind load of the tower body, with a design service life exceeding 100 years.

In the future, the TV tower also plans to open a science museum at the top floor, where tourists can visit these two stabilizing water tanks of the TV tower and see the real-time swaying data of the tower body through a computer simulation system. For fire protection considerations, once a fire occurs, the water in the water tank can also be used to extinguish the fire.

Highlight 3 280 sensors Monitoring 24 hours a day

Guangzhou New TV Tower has installed a structural health monitoring system, as if a person had a 24-hour dynamic ECG scanner attached to their body. 280 sensors and devices monitor the environmental parameters (temperature, humidity, rainfall, etc.), loads (wind and earthquakes), and responses (stress, horizontal displacement, acceleration, inclination, settlement, corrosion, etc.) of the TV tower in real time.

Highlight 4 Photovoltaic curtain wall generating 12,660 kWh annually

Semi-transparent amorphous silicon BIPV photovoltaic battery components are installed in the E functional section (438.4 meters to 448.8 meters) of the new TV tower, with an expected annual power generation of 12,660 kWh.

Highlight 5 Wind turbines saving 41,000 kWh annually

Two propeller-type wind turbines are installed on the roof of the C functional section (168 meters) of the new TV tower, with each turbine having an installed capacity of 3~5Kw, saving 41,000 kWh annually.

Highlight 6 Rainwater recycling system irrigating green spaces

The new TV tower adopts a rainwater recycling system, with a rainwater collection area of 9,300 square meters and an annual collection of tens of thousands of tons of rainwater, which can be used to irrigate green spaces and clean squares.
