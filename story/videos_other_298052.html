Sina Sports reports that Gago is extremely dissatisfied with his current situation at Real Madrid. He is worried that his marginal status at Bernabeu will make his dream of participating in next year's South Africa World Cup far away from him. For this reason, Marca revealed that Gago has already expressed his desire to leave to Real Madrid Club, and he also urged his agent to quickly find a buyer. However, due to the reality that Mohammed Diarra will participate in the African Cup in January, unless a decent offer is received, the club will not let him go in principle, and Pellegrini also stated that he does not want Gago to leave.

Marca disclosed that Gago can no longer tolerate his current situation at Real Madrid. He asked his agent to quickly find a buyer so that he could get continuous playing opportunities and ensure his participation in the World Cup. The reality is that Gago not only often sits on the cold bench but is also occasionally excluded from the big list. This is unacceptable for a player who has played as a mainstay at Real Madrid and still has dreams of participating in the World Cup. Gago's agent, Lombardia, revealed that Gago's failure in competition was related to the reality of "a new emperor means new ministers". "Alonso is indeed a very good player, but more importantly, he is also Chairman Florentino's bet, so he can get more playing time than Gago."

Gago decided to find a way to make Real Madrid let him go. He hopes his agent can solve his transfer issue in the winter transfer market. "I must get playing time in the second half of the season, otherwise Diego will not take me to South Africa," Gago said to one of his friends. However, Real Madrid does not want Gago to leave in January because Muhammad Diarra will leave to participate in the African Cup in January. Real Madrid must ensure its bench strength, but Gago urged his agent to negotiate with two senior executives of Real Madrid, Valdano and Pardeza.

Marca said that regardless, Real Madrid now regrets not selling him to Everton for 15 million euros last summer because such a price is hard to reach today. Some agents said that Gago's value is currently between 8 million to 10 million euros, which means while Higuain's value skyrocketed several times, Gago's value depreciated significantly during the same period.

Gago's agent also realized that it is not easy to let Gago leave Real Madrid now. Lombardia has already talked with the senior management of Real Madrid. He asked Valdano to let Gago go to help the young man realize his dream of participating in the World Cup. But the answer from the senior management of Real Madrid was very straightforward: bring an attractive quote from other clubs first. On one hand, Real Madrid may need Gago in the second half of the season, and on the other hand, Gago is only 23 years old, and they spent over 20 million euros to introduce him initially, so selling him at a steep discount is reluctant.

The senior management of Real Madrid set a price for Gago: 15 million euros, which was exactly the offer from Everton that Real Madrid rejected last summer. Lombardia then suggested that Real Madrid accept a scheme where other clubs lease first and then acquire later. Lombardia contacted multiple clubs, and now the information he got is that in the winter transfer market, under the financial strain of all clubs, none are willing to pay 15 million euros for Gago. Additionally, an unfavorable point for Gago is that because he participated in the Champions League representing Real Madrid, he cannot play in the Champions League for another team this season.

Currently, the clubs interested in Gago are basically all from Serie A. His situation is somewhat similar to Cambiasso, both joined Real Madrid at a young age but failed to succeed. Cambiasso achieved success in Serie A after leaving Real Madrid, so some Serie A clubs believe that Gago can also find his footing in Serie A. Lombardia also denied the possibility of Gago returning to Boca Juniors, "Boca never called me." He revealed that Gago's future might be in Fiorentina. Real Madrid is interested in Vargas from Fiorentina, and he hopes to promote a player exchange agreement.

Pellegrini hopes to keep Gago, as the team's bench depth is always better when thicker. Moreover, within Real Madrid, Muhammad will participate in the African Cup in January, Guti is constantly injured, Granero is not trusted, and Lass and Alonso need rest. Pellegrini explained why Gago was put on the substitute bench: "Gago first had an injury, then some of his performances made me realize that some players in the team were more efficient than him. But Gago is still an important player. I have no intention of letting him go during the winter break. I believe he will soon regain his position and contribute to the team."

However, Marca revealed that Gago has made up his mind to leave. In training, he performed very negatively. Last week, he even did not attend the team dinner. Although Pellegrini comfortingly included Gago in the game roster, Gago felt that he would not get real heavy use, so he must leave anyway. For personal dignity and for the dream of the World Cup.
