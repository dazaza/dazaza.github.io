Common SEO strategies and operation specifications for portal websites in Beijing website construction

by bjwanzjs on 2010-04-08 10:38:55

Characteristics of Beijing website construction: abundant information, plus more categories, each category has a lot of popular terms and long-tail keywords, so the search engine traffic improvement space is large. Overall principle: focus on key words, solve inclusion with long-tail keywords. Here Fenglin analyzes the commonly used SEO strategies for portal websites:

1. Use of secondary or tertiary domains

1) Portal websites have many categories, so multiple secondary or tertiary domains can be used to independently establish sites, which after cultivation, can become resources for SEO in the future, such as exchanging friendly links including cross-linking.

2) If secondary or tertiary domains are not used and directory structures continue to be used, two-layer or three-layer directories are not conducive to accumulating SEO resources. Others are very reluctant to exchange links with your directories but are willing to exchange links with independent sites.

3) From the perspective of URL weight, independent domains have advantages over directories or single pages.

4) It is relatively easy to improve the PR of an independent domain.

5) It helps solve inclusion issues. Binding subdomains can effectively reduce directory levels, making it convenient for crawling. The internal pages of the subdomain can be placed on the homepage of that category for display, effectively solving webpage inclusion.

6) If the traffic of a separate category is large, it is also very convenient for the server to operate independently.

7) Assuming SEO is overused or cheating methods are used, thus being penalized by search engines, it will not affect the development of other sites (many examples exist where subdomains are penalized, but the main site and other subdomains perform normally).

2. URL standardization

After the domain name is determined, next comes URL standardization, title standardization, etc. Since doing products, Fenglin has developed the habit of integrating SEO. At the beginning of each product's planning, absolute URL and tag standards were established. These standards cannot be changed in the future, especially URL standards, because changing a URL once means re-indexing tens of thousands, hundreds of thousands, or even millions of pages for portal websites. Commonly used URL forms include static URLs and directory structure URLs. Dynamic URLs can now be crawled, but Fenglin still recommends static URLs or directory structure URLs.

3. Page code standardization

The page template design of general portal sites has strict design standards, and code or CSS writing will not be chaotic. Most follow W3C web design standards, with fewer traditional Table structures and more DIV+CSS structures. Of course, the code will be simplified, naturally speeding up webpage loading and reducing Spider (search engine spider) crawling burden.

4. Page tag standardization

This is an old topic, not considered an SEO technique, but only an element slightly helpful for SEO. Tag standardization includes the use of Title, Meta, Alt, Nofollow, and other tag attributes. Regarding specific content, word count, and special characters to highlight the page or category displayed on the search results page, these are all details that Fenglin finds unnecessary to elaborate on here.

5. Keyword analysis standardization

From the homepage to the column and then to the inner page, from popular terms to long-tail keywords, categorize them according to the descending order of search volume. When implementing, shuffle the order according to the index and assign batches to subordinate personnel.

6. Page keyword layout

Regarding keyword layout strategy, Fenglin still adheres to his argument - keywords scattered across various corners of the page. This scattering refers to dispersed layout; various corners should not be understood as the entire page but rather evenly distributed. The purpose is to prevent keyword stacking and create a suspicion of cheating. However, most websites' handling remains Title full of keywords, Keywords written full, Description repeated several times. (This leads to numerous cases of being penalized, one of the older search engine cheating tricks.)

7. Internal link (inside link) construction

Some portal sites do not have dedicated personnel to build external links, yet their keyword rankings remain good. On one hand, the history and authority of the domain make it a news source for search engines; on the other hand, most of this is due to reasonable and standardized internal linking. There are many ways to build internal links. Based on several years of SEO and product experience, Fenglin briefly discusses a few points:

1) Related categories, articles, or popular recommendations - Do not underestimate this. It is very useful for increasing internal links. On one hand, an article appearing as a hot recommendation on multiple pages or within related articles can effectively increase page exposure, providing multiple entry points for search engine spiders, which is beneficial for inclusion. On the other hand, it can effectively balance the page's PR value. Fenglin always believes that a high PR on a site's homepage is not truly meaningful in terms of weight; the average PR of pages or the average PR of category URLs is valuable. Unfortunately, some Blogs or portal sites use JS calls for related articles or popular recommendations, making it difficult for certain search engine spiders to crawl, rendering them ineffective and merely decorative.
