Memory of Love

by sjpfeifeiz on 2011-09-18 20:38:33

Everyone has his own destiny, so why bother others.

For the memory of love, we should cherish it well. But in the future, we need to seek happiness respectively.

When you should laugh, you don't feel happy; when you should cry, you have no tears; when you should trust, there is no promise.

People who say they are realists often have the fantasy deeply rooted in reality with a particular appearance

The greatest fault for a person is not selfishness, passion, arbitrariness or capriciousness, but loving a person who doesn't love him obsessively.

We can love each other, but we are doomed unable to stay together. It's not that I don't love you enough, it's just that I'm not sure whether this love is the most accurate.

Regrets in this life, before it begins it already stops, and thus passes by him. He becomes the most familiar stranger to me.

Only you can remember me, even if it's in the form of hatred. But when I try to hate you, I remember your smile.

I asked tears: why do you always expose my lies? Tears answered: you shouldn't pretend to be strong. I asked tears: why do you always show up at night? Tears answered: inadvertently lighting up the loneliness of midnight. I asked tears: why don't you ever accompany solitude? Tears answered: when you're lonely, you're not thinking of me.

We keep searching all the time, and often return from the end point back to the starting point. Then we find out it was never absent from our emotional world. Love comes from our hearts. Love is giving without counting the cost, it is the wealth of the soul. We lose it while searching. When you understand it clearly, how far is love from us?

When our growth filled with joy and sorrow becomes something that can be told, we discover we have never followed any promise, but we really truly loved each other.

Loneliness is indeed different from being alone. Being alone just means there's no one around, but loneliness is a psychological state where you cannot communicate or share your feelings with others. And true loneliness is when you even forget the feeling of liking someone.

It may take a day to fall in love with someone, but it takes a lifetime to forget a person.

I smile. In any moment when I am sad or happy, I am left with only smiles.

I step closer to my dream bit by bit, even though it's broken beyond recognition. I will try my best to salvage it, exchanging everything I have for it.

Those beautiful little fish, they sleep with their eyes open. They don't need love, nor do they ever cry. They are my role models.

You sacrifice a little of your own feelings, but bring great comfort to those around you.

See that? Even when I get home, I'm still alone. I still want to leave, at least I won't feel as lonely as I do now.

Salt is destined to dissolve, perhaps in the form of tears.

I can't explain what love really is. Maybe it's just the simplest and most natural expression of emotion between a man and a woman; it's our understanding of happiness. Love and loneliness always go hand in hand. We yearn for love strongly in our loneliness.

Beauty is an illusion we maintain ourselves, wounds are the realities given by others. Everything makes me feel that there isn't enough happiness in this world. When you gain happiness, someone in this world loses it. You never know she wanders aimlessly in the bright daylight, waiting alone in the gathering dusk. You hold hands smiling in a world she doesn't know, and she has walked through countless seasons in a world you've never thought of. You never look at me straight. Actually, let me tell you, I am the one who loves you the most.

It will pass, it will all pass. Our pain, our sorrow, our guilt.

I don't want to hang myself on your tree either, because it would cause you concern, but I can't help it. Is it wrong to like someone?

When you think you've been abandoned, the one who actually suffers the loss is the other party: because he lost someone who truly likes him, and you just lost someone who didn't like you.

Forgetfulness is our unchangeable fate. Everything is like paper that hasn't been aligned properly. The past cannot return to the past, slowly stretching out, bit by bit moving apart. Perhaps we really should forget the things that have moved apart!
