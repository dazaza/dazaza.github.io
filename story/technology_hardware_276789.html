Breakthrough in nuclear power pumps

by tfbgfnbv on 2009-11-25 11:36:11

Zibo Vacuum Equipment Factory Co., Ltd.'s new products launched in 2008, including Roots vacuum pumps, liquid ring vacuum pumps, conical liquid ring pumps, double-stage conical liquid ring vacuum pumps, conical liquid ring vacuum pumps, and water pump priming device units, have all reached the advanced level of domestic and international counterparts.

The pump industry has also made breakthrough progress in technological innovation for nuclear power station pumps. Shenyang Drum Group's Shenyang Pump Co., Ltd.'s implementation of the R&D platform project for nuclear secondary and tertiary pumps and conventional island pumps has been included in the National Development and Reform Commission's national technology center innovation capability construction plan. Based on the 1 million kilowatt nuclear power plant project at Dalian Hongyanhe, they have completed the development of prototypes for four types of nuclear secondary pumps used in China Guangdong Nuclear Power Corporation's 1 million kilowatt nuclear power plants, including vacuum pumps, spray pumps, and makeup pumps, and have successfully passed performance tests, durability tests, thermal shock tests, and other factory tests. The four types of nuclear secondary pump prototypes have passed expert appraisals, with all technical indicators and performance parameters reaching international advanced levels, realizing the autonomy of major equipment for China's nuclear secondary pumps. They have also developed two types of nuclear tertiary vacuum pumps for the Qinshan Phase II 600,000 kilowatt nuclear power station units; completed one type of nuclear tertiary important plant water pump for the Pakistan C2 nuclear motor unit project; and developed one type of nuclear tertiary safety plant water pump for the Qinshan Phase II nuclear power project.
