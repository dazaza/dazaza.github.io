Cinderella Meets Her Match: Author: Nuan Nuan Feng Qing

by jqrxass on 2009-10-12 11:56:43

Ruolin replaced her good friend, Xin Yu, in a blind date. Unexpectedly, she met an extraordinary man named Han Haoxuan. By a twist of fate, Han Haoxuan actually took a liking to the fake Xin Yu.

The truth will eventually come to light. Upon learning that Ruolin is not Xin Yu, Han Haoxuan still finds himself captivated by Ruolin.

Just at this moment, Ruolin's mother falls ill, and it is Mu Jingyan, her long-time secret admirer, who comes to her aid.

If Ruolin is Cinderella, then Han Haoxuan is undoubtedly the epitome of a prince on a white horse. Later on, Mu Jingyan makes an appearance with charm that rivals Han Haoxuan's, making him a dark horse. "Dark horse" Mu Jingyan is the man Ruolin admired when she first started to develop feelings for someone. However, due to the long time this emotion has been buried, it's unclear whether it is still love, even to Ruolin herself. On the other hand, "White Horse" Han Haoxuan, the man who abruptly entered Ruolin's world, made her experience tangible feelings: longing, attachment, as well as pain and tears.

In the end, who will win Cinderella's heart? Author Nuan Feng Qing uses delicate and warm words to tell us: whether it's a dark horse prince or a white horse prince, as long as they love us and are worth entrusting our lives to, they are good princes.

Waiting for Eternity in a Moment Chapter 1: A Twist of Fate Brings Us Together

This was Ruolin's first time dining at such a high-end place like "Yuelai Grand Hotel," and its splendid decoration left her feeling dazzled.

She originally planned to be intentionally late to leave a bad impression on the blind date, but since she usually didn't come here and was unfamiliar with the area, plus fearing traffic jams, she ended up leaving early.

When Ruolin arrived at the hotel's private room number 6, it was only half past five.

Looking at the vast private room, Ruolin felt somewhat uneasy. She couldn't understand why two people needed to meet in such a luxurious and spacious private room for a blind date. Was the man's family really so rich that they had money to burn?

Waiting for Eternity in a Moment Chapter 2: When Lies Are Exposed

"Ruolin...," Xin Yu suddenly furrowed her brow and asked, "Has that guy named Han Haoxuan contacted you these days?"

"No." Ruolin was actually quite surprised that Xin Yu would bring up Han Haoxuan. This man named Han Haoxuan was truly haunting her! It had been a long time since they last communicated, yet his name kept coming up through others. Was heaven deliberately picking on her?

"Wow, my trick worked perfectly," Xin Yu relaxed her brows and smiled.

Waiting for Eternity in a Moment Chapter 3: Who Is By Your Side When You're Helpless

Ruolin's strategy turned out to be very effective; Han Haoxuan did not call her for several consecutive days. At first, there was a bit of disappointment, but gradually she got used to it and stopped thinking about it.

However, life is always so interesting. Just as she was about to forget Han Haoxuan, he popped up again at the right moment.

It was unclear whether Qin Tianyi proactively recommended Han Haoxuan to the magazine or if the editor included interviewing Han Haoxuan in their plans, but the next character featured in "Fang Ling's Appointment" turned out to be Han Haoxuan!

Waiting for Eternity in a Moment Chapter 4: Waxing Crescent Moon, Waning Crescent Moon

"Since we're all friends, why can't we meet?" Xin Yu said nonchalantly, "Besides, it's natural for him to inquire about your situation. After all, you've met three times, and he has a good impression of you. He can't just forget you as if you were a stranger. More importantly, you're my best friend, so he won't forget you easily. Whenever he sees me, he naturally thinks of you. Moreover, this time he wants to specially thank you, so he invites you to dinner. You shouldn't reject his goodwill, right?"

"But..." Ruolin stammered, unsure what to say. Perhaps she thought too much. Han Haoxuan calling her might just be out of friendship...
