Mobile Phone World Alipay Taobao Developer Contest ccmobi

by jokeonyou on 2011-05-26 17:18:24

Mobile World (bbs.ccmobi.com) Guide:

Alipay and Taobao.com jointly announced the launch of the "2011 Mobile E-commerce Application Development Competition" today, encouraging mobile application developers to create innovative applications based on Alipay's "secure payment" system and Taobao Wireless Open Platform. A reward of hundreds of thousands of yuan will be given to outstanding developers. Meanwhile, the well-known domestic venture capital fund IDG will invest 5 million US dollars in strategic investment and support for excellent teams.

It is reported that this development competition is jointly organized by Taobao.com and Alipay, and hosted by CSDN. Developers can participate as long as they integrate Taobao Wireless Open Platform API or Alipay Wireless "secure payment" API into their applications. The platforms are not limited, including Android, iOS, Symbian, Windows Phone, Java, BlackBerry, etc. After the start of the competition on May 25th, there will be stages such as work submission, voting, expert review, preliminary competition, and final competition, with the winners to be announced on November 25th. Alipay and Taobao.com have allocated 1 million yuan in prizes for this competition, setting up various awards such as first, second, and third prizes to reward the winners of the competition, while IDG will invest 5 million US dollars in strategic investment and support for outstanding teams emerging from the competition.

"The innovation of application scenarios will be a great opportunity, and entrepreneurs should have the ability to find细分value points." As a member of the expert jury, Zhu Yinjia, General Manager of Alipay Wireless Division, told reporters that holding this mobile development competition jointly with Taobao.com, he hopes to see creative applications most. "Bluetooth, GPS, camera, electronic compass, 3D acceleration, barometric altitude measurement and more sensors make the wireless market more fun. We specialize in providing underlying payment solutions and leave more application space for entrepreneurs."

"Mobile e-commerce can surpass the accumulation of traditional e-commerce in ten years within three years." Vice President of Taobao.com and General Manager of Wireless Division Qiu Changheng said: "On Taobao's wireless platform, we provide basic mobile e-commerce services, welcome third-party service providers, entrepreneurial teams to provide users with rich, segmented, professional services, and do very vertical细分fields."

In fact, the current external environment, including hardware and software, has matured. Just like the previous PC Internet process, the focus of mobile Internet has also shifted from shallow applications such as news and entertainment to the e-commerce level, with more and more developers entering the wireless field.

Alipay has specifically developed a "secure payment" solution for the mobile Internet environment. Developers can call this plug-in in the APP to directly connect to the Alipay system, building a "sterile" environment without redirection, achieving a fast and secure payment process. Currently, clients such as Alipay Mobile, Taobao Mobile, UC Browser, and Xinhua Travel have all adopted the "secure payment" solution, quickly entering the mobile market.

"Taobao.com will cooperate with developers to build personalized product libraries, making applications more valuable and competitive." Qiu Changheng stated that the Taobao Wireless Open Platform provides partners with a base of massive Taobao products and services, such as products, stores, transactions, logistics, distribution, warehousing, etc., offering over 18 types of services and more than 100 APIs for third parties to access and use.

"This is the most complete resource platform that domestic developers can encounter. Developers can focus on creativity and development, saving precious entrepreneurial resources, and have the opportunity to obtain IDG investment opportunities." A relevant person in charge of CSDN suggested developers, indicating that mature applications such as weather, games, reading, e-commerce, tourism, and news can be integrated with Taobao and Alipay to achieve value monetization.

It is understood that this competition has also attracted operators, mobile terminal manufacturers, various platform providers, and investors. "There will be a big outbreak of mobile e-commerce in the next two years. Only on truly open platforms can various types of cross-industry collaborators find suitable ways for survival and development, enjoying opportunities in entrepreneurship," industry experts said.
