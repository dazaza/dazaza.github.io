Apple Releases iOS 6.1.5/7.0.4! Attached with Download Links

by anonymous on 2013-11-17 20:25:00

The iPhone 5S

Earlier today, Apple officially released iOS 7.0.4. At the same time, they also brought iOS 6.1.5 to the iPod touch 4.

iOS 7.0.4 (version number 11B554a) is just a small update. In their release notes, Apple mentioned that it mainly fixes FaceTime call failures for some users. You can update through OTA.

Meanwhile, Apple also specifically released iOS 6.1.5 for the iPod touch 4, which also addresses the issue of FaceTime call failures.

In addition, users who have already upgraded to the new system have indicated that iOS 7.0.4 does not block the exploit that allows jailbroken Hong Kong versions of the iPhone 5S/5C to use China Mobile's 3G/4G networks. This means that after upgrading to the new system, you can simply jailbreak again to restore normal functionality.
