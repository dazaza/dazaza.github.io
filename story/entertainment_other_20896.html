Essential items for self-driving travel and GPS related introduction

by zeeulin on 2007-05-31 11:03:34

    Avoid the bustling crowd and go on a self-driving trip! This has become an increasingly popular way to travel among those who own cars. However, it is also very necessary to prepare adequately before embarking on the journey. For example, equipping yourself with some camping gear such as tents, sleeping bags, anti-moisture pads, camp lights, headlamps, stoves, fuel, tableware, etc., will not only add fun to your trip but also give you a different experience.  ●  Choose a three-season tent

    A tent is the most important equipment for outdoor camping. Without a tent, there would be no place to stay. Some friends have experienced sleeping in their cars, but I personally believe that this method should only be chosen when absolutely necessary or when camping conditions are not met. The space inside a car is small and its thermal insulation performance is poor, making the activity space very restricted.

    When buying a tent, choose one with strong wind and rain resistance for the three seasons rather than a single-layer beach tent. Professional high-altitude tents, due to their small volume and high cost, are not suitable for self-driving use.

    Currently, there are multiple manufacturers domestically producing reliable tents. A two-person three-season tent costs around 300-500 yuan. Pay attention to checking the waterproof index.

    Brand recommendations: MOGAODI, Eurohike, Pureland, ANE, or MAQEN, etc.●  Sleeping bag selection by season

    Self-driving trips mostly occur in spring, summer, and autumn, so when choosing a sleeping bag, consider the different temperatures of these three seasons. In summer, when the temperature is very high, one can sleep without a sleeping bag even in a three-season tent. However, in areas where nighttime temperatures are slightly lower, a lightweight fleece sleeping bag can be chosen, which is very inexpensive at about 100 yuan.

    In spring, nighttime temperatures in general areas are roughly not below 5-10 degrees Celsius, so a cotton sleeping bag with a temperature rating of 0-15 degrees Celsius can be selected. Common materials include four-hole cotton or newer materials, which are sufficient to meet the needs of spring camping. The price of a reliable cotton sleeping bag generally ranges from 200-500 yuan.

       Climb into the sleeping bag if it gets cold at night to avoid catching a cold

    In autumn, nighttime temperatures are lower, and cotton sleeping bags may not be sufficient. The high insulation properties of down sleeping bags come into play, helping you drive away the chill of cold nights and sleep comfortably. Choose a down sleeping bag with moderate fill power and high loft, with a temperature rating of -10 to 0 degrees Celsius, with a fill weight of approximately 500-700 grams, priced around 500-1000 yuan.

    Brand recommendations: Camp, HighRock, Pureland, VAUDE, etc.

    Weather during self-driving tours is hard to predict, and camping locations may not always be ideal, possibly encountering uneven ground or rocky terrain.

    The advantage of driving is that you don't need to worry too much about the weight of your gear, allowing you to choose more comfortable equipment. Here, I recommend self-inflating anti-moisture pads or even inflatable mattresses to ensure a comfortable sleep.

 Look at this guy lying leisurely on his inflatable pad!

    Self-inflating pads are small in volume and easy to inflate, providing greater comfort than foam anti-moisture pads due to their self-inflating system. Currently, AIRWAVES offers various sizes of self-inflating anti-moisture pads domestically, which can be chosen based on your body size. Prices range from 200-400 yuan.

    Inflatable mattresses are large in volume and require an electric air pump to inflate, making them impractical for hikers. However, they can naturally be enjoyed by those driving. Note that inflatable mattresses are not standard camping equipment, so manufacturers do not design them specifically for outdoor use. Be extra careful not to let stones under the tent puncture the mattress.

●  Headlamp

    Since the main function of pathfinding in the wild has been replaced by automobiles, the function of headlamps has largely been replaced by camp lights. Halogen bulbs with high luminosity have fewer applications, while energy-saving LED bulbs are perfectly suitable. Prices range from 200-300 yuan.

       Evening lighting must not be neglected

    There are numerous LED headlamps available on the market, but their quality varies greatly. Products ranging from 20-500 yuan are available. Generally, headlamps with low prices tend to have subpar circuitry and water resistance, so they are not recommended.

    Reasonably designed LED and halogen mixed light source headlamps in the 200-300 yuan range have ideal power consumption.

    Recommended brands: BlackDiamond, Petzl, Princeton, Blackyak, etc.Essential items for self-driving tours

● Documents

    Having complete documents ensures that you won't encounter unnecessary trouble. It's especially important to bring credit cards and vehicle insurance documents when going out of province for a self-driving tour. Before departure, it's also essential to check if permits are required for the destination to avoid affecting your itinerary.

● Daily necessities

    Clothing: appropriate outdoor clothing, sun hats, sunglasses, gloves, soft-soled shoes suitable for driving, rain gear, lighting equipment, thermos flasks, and utensils, photographic equipment, washing supplies, sewing kits, etc.; Food: easily preserved food, fruits, vegetables, drinking water, daily water, and instant food, etc.; Others: plastic garbage bags, matches, flashlights, various batteries, applicable knives, etc.

    Suggestion: Garbage should be packed in garbage bags and taken away for disposal. Used batteries should not be discarded indiscriminately.

● Communication and electronic devices

    Vehicle-mounted radio: In areas where mobile phones have no signal, especially when traveling with multiple vehicles, the vehicle-mounted wireless radio is the only effective communication tool during the journey.

     Vehicle-mounted radios are very necessary in areas without mobile phone signals

    Vehicle-mounted power inverter: During travel and camping, the only source of 220V household electricity is the vehicle power inverter.

    Vehicle navigation: The minimum utility of GPS can guide you back along the same route, and track records can permanently document your journey. For touring, durable GPS devices should be chosen.

● Photography equipment

    Camera, DV, spare batteries, film or storage cards, etc.

    Suggestion: Battery levels should be checked daily before departure to avoid missing beautiful scenery. Also, pay attention to dust protection for the equipment.

● Spare parts

    Including inspected spare tires, various easily damaged parts, various oils, coolant, various sealants (exhaust pipe, radiator, etc.), tape, binding straps, tow ropes, jumper cables, jack, electric air pump, tire pressure gauge, oil drums, shovels, etc.

Introduction to Car-mounted GPS Navigation SystemOne, Electronic maps are the soul of GPS:

    Electronic maps are as significant to vehicle-mounted GPS as Windows is to computers. In China, companies producing electronic maps must obtain relevant permits from the National Administration of Surveying, Mapping, and Geoinformation and must comply with current regulations regarding publication management. This makes the threshold for electronic map manufacturers quite high, preventing many companies interested in electronic map products from directly engaging in development and sales.

   

 

     Some common navigation software on the domestic market

    In the market, genuine electronic maps generally sell for over ten thousand yuan, while some pirated electronic maps sell for just 15 yuan, greatly reducing the credibility of GPS products equipped with such maps.

    These electronic maps simply combine pirated original electronic maps, sometimes even expired ones, with products in a simplistic manner. Such products have poor accuracy and after-sales service. Consumers should understand clearly before deciding to purchase. Additionally, with the rapid development of urban construction, whether electronic map upgrades are guaranteed is another issue consumers should pay attention to. Two, Don't use GPS as a vehicle anti-theft device?

    The application of GPS technology in vehicle tracking and scheduling began in the late 1980s in developed countries in Europe and America, and has a history of over a decade. From practical applications, using GPS technology to track and monitor moving targets can bring management and economic benefits to vehicle users. The governments of these countries also provide policy and financial support for the application of GPS in vehicles, thus making GPS increasingly popular in vehicle management. In China, the application of GPS technology in vehicle management has been sporadic for nearly ten years, but the results have been far less successful than abroad.

    Most consumers know "GPS", but their understanding of "GPS" is merely at the conceptual level. Frequent use of terms like "GPS tracking", "GPS anti-theft", "GPS anti-hijacking", "remote fuel cut-off", "remote door opening" gives people the impression that GPS is a vehicle tracking and anti-theft device. Unknown to many, in the event of vehicle theft, the GPS vehicle tracking system can determine the location of the alarm vehicle based on the spatiotemporal attributes contained in the alarm information provided by GPS, thereby responding accordingly. However, this does not mean that GPS equals a vehicle anti-theft device. The functions of GPS in vehicles develop in two directions: one is monitoring and management, and the other is navigation. GPS technology applied to vehicle monitoring and management is what people commonly know as vehicle GPS technology + anti-theft device; while navigation products are the best companions for guiding drivers on their journeys.Three, Car-mounted GPS can only serve as an auxiliary tool for driving:

    As mentioned earlier, GPS technology initially appeared as military technology, and now civilian-level GPS tools, whether car-mounted GPS or other products, still use the American 24-satellite positioning system. The U.S. strictly restricts the use of civilian-level GPS and adds many shields and interferences in the signal, making the positioning accuracy not as high as claimed by GPS manufacturers. Sometimes, the error could reach tens of kilometers.

    Additionally, the usage conditions of car-mounted GPS can affect positioning accuracy. We know that the working principle of GPS is to determine the position through satellite signal transmission and reception. Naturally, the more and stronger the received satellite signals within the allowed angle, the higher the positioning accuracy. However, in today's conditions, whether in urban concrete structures or rural mountains and forests, part of the signals will be affected and absorbed, leading to positioning deviations. Therefore, the positioning accuracy of car-mounted GPS is not as described in advertisements but is constrained by many objective factors.

Four, Differences exist between various brand GPS products:

    From a design and principle perspective, GPS car products may not seem very complex, but not any technician or company with electronic engineering background knowledge can design and manufacture GPS car products.

    According to experts, GPS vehicle application products are high-tech products integrating GPS technology, CIS technology, communication technology, and computer network technology. Products applied to cars require technical and quality adaptations to the harsh environment of vehicles. International renowned car equipment manufacturers' experiences show that any new type of GPS car equipment usually goes through a process of "use —— improve —— reuse —— re-improve" for two to three years from production to stability. This process of continuously improving product design through use until the product becomes stable cannot be skipped by any manufacturer, and this experience itself is a valuable asset.

   

 

     There are certain differences in car navigation products under various brands

    Many domestic GPS car equipment manufacturers often overlook the importance of this experience accumulation process. To capture the market, they often start large-scale marketing right after the product comes out of the laboratory, resulting in the inability to use the product normally after installation on the car. They have to send technicians for on-site debugging or return in batches. Currently, there are no standards for GPS car equipment domestically. Some companies are eager for quick success and ignore the true understanding of installing GPS equipment on vehicles, purchasing GPS receiver chips for assembly. Therefore, there are many problems with insufficient development of car equipment, leading to very high repair rates. Manufacturers are busy dealing with equipment repairs, how to improve the quality and technological content of the product?

Five, How much should you spend on GPS?

    With the continuous expansion of GPS application fields, people's enthusiasm for GPS car products has also increased. However, domestic users have quite strict requirements for the price of GPS car equipment. In recent years, with the gradual development of the market, users' expected price for car GPS has dropped sharply, from an initial price of around 10,000 yuan to now expecting to get fully functional products for hundreds of yuan.

    Of course, we are not saying that only high-priced products are fully functional and reliable in quality. However, this price requirement causes many car GPS manufacturers to constantly change product designs and components to meet user price expectations, leaving insufficient time and funds to perfect the technical design of the product and improve product quality.
