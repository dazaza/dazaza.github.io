HR Management World announced the official launch of HRoot Search Engine, the world's first professional search engine for human resources. Following the development of RSS function based on Web2.0, support of Internet Explorer and Firefox ToolBar --HRoot ToolBar, WAP version of mobile website, PODCAST service, HR management world once again launched a new heavyweight product leading the market.

HRoot Search Engine can help users to solve the problem of using common search engines (such as Google, Yahoo, Live, Baidu, etc.) to search for information, often encounter the problem of unexplained results. When users retrieve information today, "massive" is no longer important, and "accurate" results are really needed. As a well-known saying goes in search: a user cannot describe what he is looking for unless he can see what he is looking for. For human resource managers, HRoot website launched a human resource professional management search engine in a timely manner, so that users can enjoy accurate and efficient search experience!
