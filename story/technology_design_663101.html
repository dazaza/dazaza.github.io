Fuyao Glass achieves its ERP system dream with the help of Oracle software.

Fuyao Glass achieved its ERP system dream with the help of Oracle software - Corporate Dynamics - Industry News - Building Materials Network. After China's accession to the WTO, Chinese enterprises are facing global competition based on international rules. Seeking benefits and competitiveness through management has become a consensus among enterprises. How can we effectively improve enterprise management efficiency? Without a doubt, an efficient IT system has become an important foundation. Fuyao Group fully recognizes this. Chairman Cao Dewang of Fuyao Group believes that the key to enterprise takeoff lies in aligning with international standards and advanced operational management. In response to this situation, Fuyao Group decided in 2001 to implement a new ERP system, considering the entire supply chain management and e-commerce platform integration to build a framework and environment suitable for the overall future development of Fuyao Group. After a six-month selection process of mainstream ERP systems in the market, they finally decided to choose Oracle ERP. In October 2001, Fuyao officially signed a contract with Oracle and hired Hande Company, an Oracle partner, as a project implementation partner.

Mr. Wu Dongcai, the manager of Fuyao Group's Information Technology Department, said: "The main reason for choosing Oracle is that Oracle has a good database platform and provides a complete set of enterprise informatization system architecture and solutions from the underlying database platform to comprehensive e-commerce solutions, all based on the Internet architecture, with good scalability and extensibility, which can meet our needs for continuously improving enterprise information system construction. At the same time, Oracle has a good technical development prospect, which gives us users confidence. Oracle's standard-based, user-friendly development environment allows us to retain Fuyao Group's production manufacturing characteristics of both process-oriented and discrete types during the migration of the original system. On the other hand, Oracle ERP has been successfully applied both domestically and internationally, and its partners have practical experience in many successful ERP implementations. These are the keys to Fuyao Group's final successful promotion of the ERP system."

Using Oracle software, Fuyao implemented a perfect combination of standardization and personalization. During the implementation of Oracle ERP, Fuyao Group optimized its business processes on one hand to make them more effective, and on the other hand, fully utilized Oracle ERP's good development environment to perform secondary development tailored to Fuyao Group's special business operation and management model, thus effectively combining the general enterprise management principles contained in ERP with the specific conditions of the enterprise, achieving a perfect combination of standardization and personalization. Fuyao Group used Oracle database and ERP system to secondarily develop a production tracking system, production process database system, and OEM online inventory management system suitable for the mixed discrete and process production mode of the automotive glass industry, well meeting the special needs of Fuyao Group's operation and management.

Application Benefits

The success of ERP implementation brings more implicit benefits to enterprises, including business process optimization and improved management efficiency triggered by ERP. Despite this, the benefits gained from Fuyao Group's successful ERP implementation can still be explained with some very specific data, mainly reflected in several aspects:

Firstly, it solved the problem of two companies having two different systems within the group in the past, completing the structuring and standardization of the company's management system, ensuring the stability of business processes after organizational adjustments.

Secondly, by utilizing the management efficiency of ERP, it realized the reduction of management personnel. Despite a 40% reduction in enterprise managers, it can still support a 35%~50% annual profit growth of the enterprise.

Thirdly, the process database management system ensures the accuracy and timeliness of data through a unified platform. After adopting the process database management system, it eliminated the annual scrapping of 300,000 yuan worth of glass due to process data errors, accelerated market response speed, improved inter-departmental cooperation effects, increased new product development speed by 35%, and raised data accuracy from 95% to 100%.

Fourthly, through the national OEM market inventory management system, it ensures the timeliness of inventory information and analyzes and aggregates inventory status, reducing inventory volume by 25%. It also greatly accelerates the correct circulation of inventory data; the original inventory status required one month to aggregate, now shortened to 5 minutes, providing strong support for corporate decision-making with dynamic data.

Fifthly, through the transformation of the order issuance process in the sales system and manufacturing system, and the process transformation of the Oracle production manufacturing system and production tracking system, it smoothed out the enterprise's management processes and accelerated the information processing flow. Compared to the original process, work efficiency was improved by 100%; through the optimization of the basic data maintenance process, the time for basic data maintenance was reduced from 3 days to 0.5 days, and the completeness rate of basic data was increased from 95% to 100%.

Future Development

The informatization of any enterprise is never perfect and requires continuous improvement and keeping pace with the times. Using Oracle software, Fuyao Group can easily expand the current system and continuously improve the enterprise information system. After completing the implementation of the Oracle ERP system, Fuyao Group is moving toward the next goal of informatization. First, extending the ERP system to Fuyao Changchun branch and Chongqing branch to further expand the results of the Fuyao ERP system; secondly, using Oracle's mature data mining technology to integrate all the data of the group into a data warehouse so that the entire Fuyao Group can promptly obtain analysis results derived from complete data; thirdly, using Oracle database and e-commerce platform to build an e-commerce platform with Fuyao characteristics.
