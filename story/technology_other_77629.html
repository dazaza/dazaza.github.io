The story of angels and dolphins

by taoling11 on 2008-07-14 09:13:18

The angel wanted to give the dolphin a kiss,

but the sea was too deep.

The dolphin wanted to give the angel a hug,

but the sky was too high...

"Angel, how can I get your love's gift..." The dolphin moaned in pain.

"I can't abandon my identity as an angel to meet you in the sea, unless you can give up your dolphin form and become a small fish in the celestial palace pond, then we can be together..." The angel hesitated, because she knew what her words meant. For the dolphin to transform from a beautiful sea spirit into a freshwater fish would not be easy, both in appearance and customs. Moreover, the dolphin had his family ties.

"I am willing!" The dolphin firmly replied, taking one last look at the ocean he loved so much.

And thus, a free-spirited creature of the sea became a fish kept in the celestial palace.

The dolphin could finally live in the same place as the angel he admired, even though his freedom seemed pitiful, spending all day in shallow water, drinking fresh water that was hard for him to swallow, eating tasteless aquatic plants, but seeing the angel smile every day made the dolphin feel satisfied.

The angel and the dolphin could only meet every night, because he was just a fish, and she was an angel...

The angel could accept the dolphin's love, but she couldn't accept it —— she was an angel, and he was just a fish. A fish cannot provide honor or wealth to an angel, nor can it give an angel a home or the security and sense of belonging that a woman longs for.

Like many loves in this world, pure feelings are so fragile when faced with reality and aspirations, and love without bread will eventually be extinguished into ashes by the trials of reality.

One day, the angel threw the dolphin high up into the air from the heavens, and the dolphin fell heavily onto the earth. Covered in wounds, the dolphin didn't even have time to examine its injuries before sinking into despair over lost love.

When he was willing to give up his form, even his family, willing to change his preferences and interests, willing to sacrifice everything, what he got in return wasn't love, but endless heartbreak and despair.

The sky remained beautiful, the angel remained beautiful, but the dolphin was no longer a dolphin.

After all, a dolphin is still a dolphin, not just any ordinary fish. After overcoming near-despairing sadness, it still survived tenaciously, adapting to its new environment until it became the strongest fish in the river!

The failure of love did not affect its love for life. It ultimately wanted to prove to the angel that it would never be defeated so easily, it was the spirit of the sea, and it could also become the spirit of the river, but... it was after all just a fish!

One day, it arrived at a place called the Dragon Gate. Fellow fish told it that if it could leap over the Dragon Gate, it might become a dragon, the king of rivers and seas, able to summon wind and rain!

But for millions of years, this was just a dream for fish, very few could succeed! Without hesitation, the dolphin had already decided, it wanted to become a dragon! It wanted to become strong!

To become a dragon, one must persistently jump, hold on to their dreams, the dolphin had no confidence in success, but it knew it would never give up, just like its love for the angel. It couldn't give the angel the bread and reality she desired, all it had was its unwillingness to give up...

Whether it was its sorrow or courage, it continued jumping, jumping nonstop, despite being battered and bruised, it never stopped.

Opportunity is such a thing, you may never grasp it in your lifetime, but perhaps your efforts and persistence will make you achieve all your hopes in an instant!

A heavy rain made the previously unreachable Dragon Gate lower. Already almost out of strength, the dolphin didn't miss this final opportunity. As it leapt into the air, it felt its strength double and it soared into the sky. It succeeded! Now it was a dragon...

It could now fly into the sky, receiving blessings from the angels. No one knew it was once an ordinary fish, no one knew it was once a spirit of the sea.

Now it was a dragon, the pride of the heavens, with the Dragon Palace in its possession, its power enough to make people envious, countless fairy maidens came and went like schools of fish, but it chose none. Now, it wanted to see the angel who betrayed its love...

It longed for her to kneel before it, to let her see the dolphin it once was, the choice she gave up... Only then could its raging anger subside.

But it searched throughout the heavens and found no trace of the angel...

It suddenly realized it didn't truly hate the angel, it just wanted the angel to see it, to see it proudly stand before her, making her feel a hint of regret. But the angel seemed to have vanished from the horizon.

It rested at the Dragon Gate, where accompanying angels asked, "Did you leap from here to become a dragon?"

"Yes," it answered somewhat proudly.

"You're so lucky, if only my friend's lover were as lucky as you!" The angels envied.

"Your friend? I haven't heard about it."

"It's like this, my friend is also an angel, but she fell in love with a dolphin. To make the dolphin become a dragon, my friend cruelly threw it into the water, hoping it would one day become a dragon."

"But don't you know how impossible it is for a dolphin to become a dragon... Only my friend, she's so foolish, waiting here every day for her boyfriend to become a dragon, and she believes so firmly!"

"Why didn't she tell the dolphin..." The dolphin felt its eyes start to moisten.

"That's impossible, if she told it, then it would always think of the angel and wouldn't strive to realize its ideal..."

"So... where is the angel now?" The dolphin asked anxiously.

"It's too late, she waited and refused to leave. You know, angels can't stay in the human world for too long. She waited too long, and the dolphin never appeared. She has already turned into a giant stone in the mountains." The angels looked pityingly at the dolphin.

Then they witnessed something they least expected: a dragon ramming itself to death against the rock. "She's gone, what use is it for me to become a dragon, what meaning does it have for me to become strong, let my blood mix with hers, never to separate!"

That night, in heaven and on earth, there was a downpour...
