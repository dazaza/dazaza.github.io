Description:

The X968 Fingerprint Attendance Machine, part of the TocoTech Q520 Series Network Access Control Machines, utilizes proprietary technology with a parallel high-speed chip ZEM300 and the ZK6001 multimedia platform. It employs the latest recognition algorithm technology in its high-speed mixed-recognition engine Biokey8.0, which increases recognition speed by more than 5 times compared to the original version. The machine can store over 1,500 fingerprints and record up to 5 million entries. It complies with the U.S. fingerprint device standard in its new version, comprehensively enhancing the quality of fingerprint identification. Its unique input status ensures precise attendance management without requiring real-time computer connection, making it particularly suitable for enterprises that need long-distance offline fingerprint attendance. Simultaneously, it is applicable to various scenarios requiring accurate identification. The fingerprint attendance machine communicates with computers via RS232, RS485, Face Recognition, or Standard TCP/IP methods and supports multiple network configurations, making it ideal for large office environments or factory attendance systems.
