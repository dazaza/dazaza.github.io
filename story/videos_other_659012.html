For the rapidly emerging Xiang'an business district, another milestone has been added - Xiamen Yiting Express Hotel, Xiang'an's first business economy hotel, begins trial operations today. This marks a significant step towards making Huijing Shopping Plaza the first urban complex in Xiang'an. The Xiamen Yiting Express Hotel is located in Building 8 of the Huijing New City Center, built on top of the 60,000 square meter Huijing Shopping Plaza.

As a wholly-owned subsidiary of Xiamen Yisha Group, one of China's top 500 enterprises and one of the ten largest state-owned enterprises directly managed by Xiamen, Xiamen Yiting Express Hotel offers excellent value for money to ordinary business and leisure travelers. Grand opening promotion: For stays before July 31, 2011 at the Xiangzun branch, enjoy room rates as low as 50-60% off!

Xiamen Yiting Business Hotel Xiangzun branch is carefully crafted by Xiamen Tourism Group as part of the Yiting Business brand. It is also Xiang'an District's first business economy hotel, located in Building 8 of Huijing Shopping Plaza, adjacent to the Xiang'an District Government, court, procuratorate, and Torch Park. With extremely convenient transportation and complete surrounding facilities, the hotel rooms are all decorated according to four-star standards. It features 66 rooms including luxury suites, executive VIP rooms, business standard rooms, and characteristic king-size beds. Comfortable beds, soft cotton quilts, hot water showers available 24 hours a day, central air conditioning, 32-inch high-definition LCD TVs, in-room safes, princess chairs, free long-distance and local calls, and 24-hour high-speed broadband internet are all provided. Its personalized, fashionable, and distinctive decoration style, quick and enthusiastic service, clean and warm accommodation environment fully meet the needs of business travelers and high-end tourists, allowing you to enjoy every segment of your journey freely.

Buildings 8 and 9 of Huijing New City Center are built above the shopping plaza. Project Introduction:

Huijing Shopping Plaza is located at the intersection of Maxin Road and District Government Avenue in Xiang'an, occupying the most core geographical location of "Xiang'an New City". It is in the core area of Minnan's "Golden Quadrangle" consisting of Xiamen, Zhangzhou, Quanzhou, and Jinmen, and will become Xiamen's new commercial center in the future. As R&L Supermarket, Suning Appliances, Outback Steakhouse, Ping An Bank, Donghai Securities, Dadi Kindergarten, China Telecom, China Tobacco, and others have already opened, the commercial atmosphere of Huijing Shopping Plaza is increasingly mature, with an increasing number of consumers. The good investment prospects have prompted domestic and international brand merchants and astute shop investors to accelerate their pace of entering Xiang'an, seizing the last opportunity to secure a place in Huijing Shopping Plaza and along the street shops.

It is reported that China Film International Cinema, Xinshishang Food Square, E Cool Animation Park, Xiamen Yiting Hotel, Binghuo Workshop, KFC, Watsons, and Dafengshou Fish Restaurant will soon open, with some merchants already undergoing interior renovations. It can be foreseen that within the next 1-2 months, Huijing Shopping Plaza will inevitably experience a peak in brand merchants rushing to move in. At that time, a large business district covering supermarkets, cinemas, electrical appliance malls, famous brands, food squares, Western restaurants, brand clothing, video game entertainment, computer digital products, youth goods, banks, telecommunications, securities, tobacco, community convenience stores, etc., will fully open.
