See how Shanghai housewives decorate the living room

by longersing on 2009-08-02 00:07:09

Housewives who enjoy a high-end lifestyle often decorate their homes in their spare time. Many old house decorations are proposed by these housewives. Today, let's take a look at how Shanghai housewives decorate their living rooms. The living room is the first view that a family presents to friends and guests, reflecting the taste and appreciation level of the family. However, designing and decorating it is not that easy.

How to maximize the functionality of the living room in a small space is something every housewife considers. Below are some experiences in living room decoration that may be helpful! We have also discussed many space decorations before, such as: office decoration cannot do without plant decoration.

Rational purchase of decorative items: Shopping is loved by most women. Seeing beautiful and inexpensive decorations can lead to impulsive buying. Most housewives start to consider saving on decorations, which contrasts sharply with their usual spending on themselves. After all, the competition in Shanghai is too fierce, and money doesn't come easily. Therefore, when shopping, one should use their brain, avoid impulsive buying for small discounts, and not buy too many seasonal decorations. Occasionally, leaving some blank spaces can better highlight the main atmosphere.

Reusing old items: Develop the habit of paying attention to existing household items. If old furniture can still be used and there is no need to add new items, it’s best not to buy new ones. This will save some display space. At appropriate times, you can consider going to second-hand markets to exchange decorations with others, allowing you to find what you like and enabling your unused items to be appreciated or collected by others. However, harmful furniture should be discarded if possible, as we mentioned before: villa decoration involves both giving up and keeping - please discard harmful furniture.

Flexible decoration space: Purchase more storage cabinets to enhance the space's storage function. This makes the decorated space seem larger.

Restrained use of large furniture in decoration: Use fewer large wine cabinets, TV cabinets, etc., to simplify the division of space. If large furniture is necessary, place it in the corner. Choose furniture that uses green building materials whenever possible.

Utilize three-dimensional space during decoration: Nail wooden boards onto the wall to store CDs and other items. Only when truly using these small items do you realize:乱放乱摆很难找，真正想保存却没地方放。At this moment, the advantage of three-dimensional space becomes evident.

Make the living and dining areas semi-open. Knock down the partition walls and use storage cabinets to divide the space. Note that the knocked-down walls must not be load-bearing walls.

Fully utilize track sliding doors for decoration: Increase the functionality of the space and free the hallway from door opening and closing constraints.

Properly use home decor fabrics to create a warm and cozy living space. For example, hanging fabric from the ceiling can effectively separate spaces and can be pulled up when not in use to expand the space.

Create a simple work area in the corner of the living room with a table and chairs, then use a screen to block the view.

Maximize the storage capacity of boxes. Attach consistent wrapping paper to the boxes and flexibly place them in various corners of the living room.

Mirrors or monochromatic color schemes can make the living room feel larger. Although this decoration method may seem outdated, carefully decorated results can still reach a high standard.

We must also consider some coatings used in re-decorating. If funds allow, try to use environmentally friendly and harmless decoration materials, such as: environmental protection decoration - teach you the correct way to remove new house odors. Living room decoration may seem simple, but it can actually be quite complicated. The key is whether you have the patience to choose decoration materials and how to match the entire living room space.
