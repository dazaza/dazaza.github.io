eReading of China Telecom has launched the official electronic version of "Steve Jobs"

by szjqd510 on 2011-11-15 09:41:03

If you find it difficult to get the book "Steve Jobs Biography of iPhone 4" in the bookstore, or if the price is too high to bear for the iPhone 4, then you might as well turn to e-reading.

The Nanfang Daily reported that China Telecom's e-reading platform recently released the official electronic version of "Steve·Jobs Biography", and also opened some free trial reading chapters. Book enthusiasts can purchase any chapter of the book according to their personal interests for selective reading.

The pricing is also quite affordable. It is understood that from November 3 to November 30, China Telecom's e-reading platform will host a new reading format of "1 yuan per chapter" to read "Steve·Jobs Biography". This allows netizens to subscribe to the chapters they are interested in based on their needs. However, this new wireless release is temporarily only open to users of the China Telecom e-reading mobile client. Users can obtain it by sending the text message "3102" or "YDKHD" to 10001 with their China Telecom mobile phone. Android phone users can also download the "China Telecom Reading" client for reading.
