Bad Love, Toxic Love Episode Summary

Episode 12

Injeong agrees to work at the company, proposing that the Hope Home should not be demolished before spring. Although Yonggi makes things difficult for Injeong, he eventually agrees. The next day, Yonggi anxiously waits for Injeong's arrival early in the morning. Seeing that Injeong has kept her word, Yonggi smiles with satisfaction but also realizes that although Injeong is by his side, it will not be easy to win her back. Indeed, Injeong only hopes that Yonggi will forget her as soon as possible.

Suhwan and Kim legislator cut off the funding sources of Daehan Construction, putting the company at risk of bankruptcy. Finding Suhwan to return to the company becomes the only emergency solution, but Yonggi refuses to comply. Injeong learns that one of the reasons for the company's difficulties is Yonggi's agreement to stop the demolition due to her, making her feel complex emotions. After careful consideration, she decides to ask Suhwan to return to help Yonggi. Meanwhile, Yonggi has thought through and is ready to accept Suhwan's return. Yonggi is very angry about Injeong going to see Suhwan. Injeong says that even if Suhwan returns to the company, it will have nothing to do with her. Suhwan wants to regain Injeong's love, so he arranges for Lee Shin-yeong, who resembles Jo-an, to approach Yonggi...

Episode 13

Suhwan sits solemnly in the car watching Yonggi and Injeong outside. Injeong refuses to ride in Yonggi's car due to stomach pain when Shin-yeong walks over... Yonggi is shocked the moment he sees Shin-yeong.

Julan, who is determined to break up with Secretary Yi, drowns her sorrows in alcohol, feeling disheartened; while Shin-yeong reminds Yonggi of Jo-an, causing him to have nightmares about Jo-an again at night. Injeong opens the door and sees Yonggi's back, impulsively wanting to chase after him but restrains herself instantly.

On Sunday, Yonggi takes Injeong to see the new address selected for the elderly. Injeong can't help but praise Yonggi like she used to, making both of them nostalgic. However, Injeong insists they should no longer be related to each other now.

Shin-yeong moves to the house next to Injeong to get closer to her. The next day, Shin-yeong comes to work at the company arranged by President Han. Yonggi notices there’s something suspicious about Shin-yeong's appearance. During lunch, Injeong learns from Suhwan that Shin-yeong looks a lot like the deceased Jo-an, shocking her. Recalling Yonggi staring at Shin-yeong, Injeong starts to worry about Yonggi.

During the company meeting, Yonggi and Suhwan have a fierce conflict of opinions. Angry, Yonggi goes to the rooftop alone, followed by Shin-yeong. Injeong goes up to persuade Yonggi to come down for the meeting, only to see Shin-yeong kissing Yonggi...

Episode 14

When Injeong pushes open the door to the rooftop, she sees Shin-yeong kissing Yonggi. She slams the door and leaves angrily. Yonggi chases after her to explain, but hearing Injeong say they are no longer related, he cruelly admits Injeong is right and walks downstairs. Injeong stands still, too angry to move for a long time.

Suhwan tells Shin-yeong that he only wants to verify how much Yonggi loves Injeong because Injeong loves Yonggi. The next day at work, Yonggi continues to show intimacy with Shin-yeong in front of Injeong, who tries hard to stay calm. Seeing Injeong's fatigue, Suhwan feels conflicted. Shin-yeong arrogantly announces to Injeong that she will pursue Yonggi.

The four go to the construction site. Yonggi takes care of the injured Shin-yeong, leaving Suhwan and Injeong behind. Suhwan tells Injeong that once the company stabilizes, he will leave the country as promised, hoping she will smile often after he's gone. After sending Injeong off, Suhwan falls ill, and Injeong takes him to the hospital. Suhwan's illness turns out to be minor. From Suhwan's mother, Injeong learns the reason why Suhwan broke up with her back then.

A call from Jo-an's mother makes Yonggi realize that Shin-yeong's appearance was deliberately arranged by Suhwan. Enraged, Yonggi tells Suhwan he will continue the game. As Yonggi is about to leave after sending Shin-yeong to Injeong's doorstep, Injeong suddenly opens the door...

Episode 15

Seeing Injeong come out, Shin-yeong deliberately hugs Yonggi from behind and invites him to her house for tea. Yonggi, who was cold towards Shin-yeong, intentionally cooperates with her this time. Injeong, seeing this, angrily turns around and walks away.

It turns out Shin-yeong also has a tough past. Shin-yeong genuinely likes Yonggi and expresses her willingness to let Yonggi continue using her to test Injeong's sincerity. In reality, she hopes that Injeong will make Yonggi completely give up on her. The next morning, seeing Yonggi spend the night at Shin-yeong's house, Injeong is truly furious.

Suhwan meets with Shin-yeong and offers to pay her now, asking her to stop further provoking Injeong. Shin-yeong refuses, openly admitting her love for Yonggi. Later, Shin-yeong deliberately mentions the third party to Injeong, deeply hurting her. Unable to bear Injeong's inner torment, Suhwan reveals to Injeong the truth that Yonggi's closeness to Shin-yeong is merely to provoke her. He also confesses that he still loves Injeong and suggests they start anew if Injeong gives up on Yonggi.

After learning the truth, Injeong storms into Yonggi's office and angrily slaps him... Injeong tearfully declares that she only loves Yonggi, and they part ways in tears. Yonggi is in great pain. Yonggi's friend finds Injeong and advises her that if she wants to leave Yonggi, she should give him some time to adjust...
