"Introduction of the 8th Episode of 'Bullfight': The Crossroads of Love"

The night's restaurant was far from peaceful. Prince of Ice Shen Ruohé had carefully arranged a romantic candlelight dinner for Princess Yi Shengxue, but it was ruined by a series of rude and clumsy individuals! In another corner of the restaurant, the group’s heiress He Qianna also had a date with someone her mother had arranged! Outside, the devoted knight Jin Zicong followed closely behind to protect the princess. Originally, everyone occupied their own "tug-of-war" position in love, coexisting without conflict. However, an unexpected event caused these complicated relationships to collide directly within the restaurant!

Shen Ruohé took Yi Shengxue away, even though the meticulously planned date was ruined, the real two-person world had just begun! By the fountain, Shen Ruohé finally couldn't help but reveal his feelings. A discarded monkey unexpectedly stirred up Shen Ruohé's emotions. The warmth of the thirteenth street made him reluctant to put an end to this relationship! But how should he face the impending crisis? Shen Ruohé's heart wavered!

On the other hand, the super movie enthusiast He Qianna was also attracted by Zicong's dominance. Looking at the Zicong before her eyes, she recalled that long-buried memory...the "dream chaser" she lost forever because she let go...facing this black society big brother cooler than Godzilla! Regardless, Qianna decided to stick with him that night!

When Shengxue was deeply immersed in the love illusion Ruohé had woven, Ruohé didn't realize that another conspiracy had already taken shape. Both sides carefully laid out their plans, and once they acted, they would deliver a fatal blow leaving no room for breath...

"Introduction of the 9th Episode of 'Bullfight': Shen Ruohé, See You There!"

Shengxue, full of hope for a smooth-sailing love life, didn't expect her father's doubts about Shen Ruohé. Shengxue was confident in Ruohé, but others didn’t see it the same way. Even at school, the girls who supported Ruohé expressed doubt about Shengxue and Ruohé's dates, which angered Shengxue enough to declare she must obtain proof of Ruohé's love.

Before starting a relationship with Qianna, President He began trying to control Ruohé's life, asking Ruohé to attend a public cocktail party and announce his engagement to Qianna, creating the impression that they were already dating. This move triggered Ruohé's dissatisfaction, and Ruohé resisted by refusing to attend. But when his father pleaded bitterly, would Ruohé soften and give in?

Shengxue happily waited for Ruohé outside the gymnasium, but Ruohé never showed up. All the doubts about Shengxue and Ruohé's relationship started to surface in Shengxue's mind. Had she really misjudged Ruohé?

Zicong, standing by Shengxue, hoped Miss could find happiness, while Ruohé, eager to win the bullfighting arena to surprise Shengxue, disregarded his old injuries and fought desperately to win over Blue Earth in this bullfight. As the time for the date approached second by second, Shengxue's heart gradually sank. At this moment, a rose appeared in front of Shengxue. Did this rose come from the devoted knight or the prince of ice?

"Introduction of the 10th Episode of 'Bullfight': Heartbroken Party"

Shengxue rushed anxiously to the bullfighting arena and unexpectedly discovered that the Ruohé before her eyes was no longer yesterday's Shen Ruohé who had dined with her, dated her, watched fireworks with her, and ran holding hands together. After only one night, Shen Ruohé's eyes had changed!

If change is necessary, why did he retrieve the Bullfighting Arena on the thirteenth street for her? If change is necessary, why did he still give her one last hug before leaving? His eyes conveyed pain, yet he left cruelly. Shengxue couldn't accept such an ending...

At this time, Zicong unexpectedly learned the truth about his origins, plunging him into despair. He liked Shengxue, but Shengxue liked Ruohé. He respected Wu Ye, but Wu Ye had killed his father. His reasons for staying in the Yi family were all gone. Meanwhile, President He extended an invitation to Zicong, asking him to work for He Feng... For Ruohé, giving up Shengxue and dating He Qianna was already an extremely difficult decision, so he fortified his defenses. He must not falter again due to weakness, causing his father's painstakingly established Qingtian Group to collapse. But now that Shengxue appeared before him, could he be sure he wouldn't waver?
