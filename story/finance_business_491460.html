What are the attractions in the southern mountainous area?

by doudouqaj on 2011-09-19 13:03:21

■ Jiuruisan Waterfall Group Scenic Area

The Jiuruisan Waterfall Group Scenic Area is mainly characterized by its natural mountain and water scenery. Due to the temperatures in March and April this year being lower than the same period in previous years, the water volume of the waterfalls within the scenic area has been more abundant than it has been in recent years, attracting a large number of tourists from within and outside the province.

Liu Zheng, Vice General Manager of the Market Department of the Jiuruisan Waterfall Group Scenic Area, introduced that as of 4 PM on May 1st, the scenic area's visitor traffic reached 26,000 people, with nearly 2 million yuan in ticket revenue. "The overall situation is more bustling than last year's Labor Day, with a 30% increase in visitor traffic," he said. He indicated that based on the parking lot vehicle conditions, many visitors came from places like Hebei and Tianjin.

■ Hongye Valley Ecological Civilization Tourism Area

With recent clear weather and rising temperatures, the crocuses in the Hongye Valley Ecological Cultural Tourism Area are in full bloom, attracting many tourists for flower appreciation and spring outings.

Staff informed reporters that on the first day of the "May Day" mini-holiday, the tourist reception volume increased by 13% compared to the same period last year, with local tourists exceeding 40%, an increase of 10 percentage points over last year.

■ Shui Lian Xia Scenic Area

During this short holiday, the Shui Lian Xia Scenic Area launched the "Happy May Day Week." Tourists can not only enjoy the boundless beauty of spring but also take advantage of discounts such as free gas coupons and complimentary lunches. This satisfies both the desire for spring outings and achieves the goal of saving money while traveling. According to the staff of the Shui Lian Xia Scenic Area, on May 1st, winners of the municipal-level or above "May Day Labor Medal" and district-level or above model workers enjoyed free admission. On May 4th, visitors under 18 years old received a 50% discount on admission tickets. During the "Happy May Day Week," the scenic area will continue to introduce water and land entertainment projects such as "rafting," "watercraft battles," and "maze walking," and host activities including the "Jinan University Students Campus Image Competition and Shui Lian Xia Ambassador Selection Contest," "Fruit and Vegetable Crisps Exhibition Festival," and the "First Self-Driving Tour Festival."

■ Jinan World Garden Scenic Area

This scenic area is the first in Jinan to participate in entertainment competition activities like "Happy Move Forward." On May 1st, the scenic area's ticket revenue doubled compared to last year, with a daily visitor count of over ten thousand setting a record since the park's opening. By 9 AM that morning, the first parking lot was completely full, forcing the scenic area to activate the second parking lot. At 4 PM, the scenic area was still crowded with tourists.

■ Jinan Maoling Wild Animal World

On May 1st, the "stars" of the Wildlife World took the stage for competitions: the bear "Dou Dou" jumping over obstacles, "Wo Wo" walking upside down on parallel bars, golden tiger rolling drums... The performances were spectacular. On that day, the Jinan Wildlife World received more than 7,500 tourists from within and outside the province, setting a record for the highest visitor count during the same period in the history of the scenic area.

(Reporters Yang Yongqing and Zhang Dingye)
