"Million Sunflower Garden" is an ecological park themed around sunflowers and ranks first in the world in two categories:

1. Largest scale at 66.7 hectares, winning the title of "the largest sunflower garden in the country."

[Scenic Spot Introduction]

The Million Sunflower Garden has introduced 18 ornamental sunflower varieties from Europe, Japan, and other places, and has cultivated a batch of varieties suitable for subtropical planting and growth through optimal selection. By introducing genes, they have cultivated red, purple, and other special color sunflowers with a strong fragrance. Through scientific planning and planting, the Million Sunflower Garden keeps a million sunflowers blooming all year round.

With the development of tourism and people's preference for eco-tourism, the Sunflower Garden has evolved from a single sunflower garden into a theme flower city featuring famous flowers from all over the world. French lavender, Japanese Hokkaido lavender, Japanese romantic cherry blossoms, European tree-shaped roses, Korean peacocks, ornamental "poppies," and China's fragrant jasmine have all become famous attractions in the Sunflower Garden and places where visitors linger.

Lavender Base

Lavender is a fragrant purple-blue small flower. Also known as "tranquil perfume plant." Originally from the Mediterranean region, it prefers dry conditions. Its flowers resemble wheat ears, with long stems covered in star-shaped fine hair. Tiny purple-blue flowers bloom at the tips, with narrow gray-green leaves. When grown to full size, it can reach up to 80 cm in height. Whenever the wind blows, the entire lavender field ripples like deep purple waves, extremely beautiful.

Appreciate "a hundred flowers in full bloom" - South African paradise birds, Japanese water orchids, French peonies, South American lavender, Dutch tulips, American purple rice, European trumpet flowers, Mediterranean violets, Argentine morning glories...

Local specialties:

Sunflower Chicken - The feed for sunflower chickens is 95% fresh sunflower ingredients. It tastes fragrant and has crispy bones, with vitamin E levels ten times higher than market chickens. In June 2004, the only chicken selected for the provincial governor-level banquet at the White Swan Hotel was the sunflower chicken. This chicken has won numerous culinary awards and is praised by food critics as the top chicken in Panyu.

Newken Lotus Root - Panyu is a well-known land of fish and rice, with a rich variety of agricultural products. The delicious food made from fresh local agricultural products carries a strong regional flavor. The Newken lotus root in Panyu is large and starchy, making it a major local specialty. Many restaurants use it to create signature dishes, attracting many diners from Guangzhou, Hong Kong, and Macao. Among these, braised pork belly with lotus root, braised duck meat with lotus root, and fermented tofu-fried lotus slices are local delicacies. The most special dish is green bean-stuffed lotus root. The chef first cooks the green beans until they are skinless, then stuffs them into the lotus root holes, adding special ingredients before cooking and serving. This dish is powdery and fragrant, delicious and tasty.

Shaowan Ginger Milk Pudding: Sweet and beneficial

When searching for delicacies in Panyu, be sure to try Shaowan's ginger milk pudding, a traditional snack passed down since the 1930s, suitable for modern tastes.

At Shaowan Qinfangyuan Dessert Shop, according to Zeng Huizhuang, the inheritor of the famous ginger milk pudding master "Niunai Dang", making ginger milk pudding requires selecting yellow, starchy ginger and using concentrated fresh buffalo milk.

When making it, place the ginger juice at the bottom of the bowl, pour milk heated to around 80°C into the bowl and mix evenly. If a spoon can stand steadily on the surface of the milk without sinking, the ginger milk pudding is considered successful.
