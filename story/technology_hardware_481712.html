Guangdong Province holds a training class for the interpretation of agricultural machinery purchase subsidy policy in 2011 -- Industry Information -

by maling29 on 2011-08-26 09:10:04

This morning (April 1), an interpretation training class for the agricultural machinery purchase subsidy policy of Guangdong Province in 2011 jointly held by the Mechanization Office of Guangdong Provincial Department of Agriculture and the Agricultural Machinery Appraisal Station of Guangdong Province was grandly held in Guangzhou. This training class implemented the spirit of the central and provincial relevant agricultural machinery purchase subsidy policy documents, summarized the achievements and experience of the agricultural machinery purchase subsidy work in Guangdong Province in recent years, arranged and deployed the implementation of the agricultural machinery purchase subsidy in 2011, and conducted training on the implementation of this year's central and provincial agricultural machinery purchase subsidy policies and projects. Deputy Director of the Department of Agriculture of Guangdong Province Zheng Huidian attended the training class and delivered a speech. A total of five hundred leading person in charge of domestic and foreign agricultural machinery production enterprises and dealers participated in the training.

Zheng Huidian pointed out that in the past year, Guangdong effectively promoted the implementation of the agricultural machinery purchase subsidy policy, and successfully completed the agricultural machinery purchase subsidy funds arranged by the central and provincial finance amounting to 299.8 million yuan. It is estimated that the whole province will subsidize nearly 150,000 units (sets) of various machinery, benefiting more than 96,000 households, achieving remarkable results. Regarding the conduct of the new year's agricultural machinery subsidy work, Zheng Huidian made three requests to agricultural machinery enterprises and dealers: First, enhance independent intellectual property rights and core competitiveness, strictly control the quality and safety barriers, grass cutting knife series. Agricultural machinery products are tools for agricultural production, the market is in the countryside, the target is farmers, agricultural machinery enterprises should be based in the countryside, develop agriculture, adhere to the direction of serving agriculture, rural areas and farmers, which is the glorious social responsibility of the enterprise. Second, strengthen service awareness, build a technical service guarantee line together. Agricultural machinery dealers are an important link in implementing the national agricultural machinery subsidy policy, and are a bond connecting farmers and agricultural machinery production enterprises. They must do a good job in promotion and publicity information services, as well as perfecting the technical services of agricultural machinery operations, in order to play a role in implementing the agricultural machinery subsidy policy. Third, strengthen supervision, operate legally, create a harmonious industry environment. Fully implement the strong agricultural and beneficial agricultural policies and industrial promotion policies of agricultural machinery purchase subsidies.

Director of the Mechanization Office of Guangdong Province Zheng Hongxuan, researcher Huang Hanfan, station master of the agricultural machinery appraisal station Yao Yunshi, and deputy station master Xiong Yuanfang respectively gave profound interpretations of the goals and tasks, key trends, main policies and measures, and relevant policy requirements in the agricultural machinery purchase subsidy implementation plan regarding the development of agricultural mechanization in Guangdong Province at the training class. At the same time, through the analysis of the quality situation of agricultural machinery purchase subsidies in Guangdong Province in the past two years, they proposed the procedures and requirements for production enterprises to apply for agricultural machinery product testing and appraisal and to declare the directory.

Under the chairmanship of Vice Director of the Provincial Agricultural Machinery Office Li Yingchi, the training class carried out an interactive session of on-site questioning and answering. The participating agricultural machinery production enterprises and dealers actively spoke up, combining the problems encountered in actual work, and put forward many valuable opinions and suggestions for the agricultural machinery purchase subsidy work in Guangdong.

The training class was successfully concluded in a lively and orderly atmosphere.

(Yuan Rong)
