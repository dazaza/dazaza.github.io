12th December Guangdong TV Public Channel 22:00 Mysterious Underground Path in the Desolate Village

By chance, Li Shengtian, a villager from Diantou village, accidentally broke through his own floor. To his surprise, there was a winding and extensive tunnel hidden beneath the surface. How could there be tunnels under his own cave dwelling? Full of doubt, Li Shengtian decided to investigate further. However, he never expected that this discovery would bring a secret buried for a thousand years back to the eyes of the world. Who built these tunnels? And why were they abandoned?

13th December Guangdong TV Public Channel 22:00 Mystery of the Elderly Woman Ascending to Immortality

An unusual event occurred in Shangrao, Jiangxi. A Mrs. Wang left behind a will before her death, stating that her body would not decay after her death. If she was worshipped three years later, she would ascend to immortality and gain eternal life. Three years later, when her descendants retrieved her remains, they were greatly shocked - her body was indeed intact. Since then, Mrs. Wang's name as an immortal spread far and wide. People worshipped her even more, calling her the "Saint Mother of Ling Mountain". What secrets lie behind the preservation of her body? Did Mrs. Wang really "ascend to immortality"?

14th December Guangdong TV Public Channel 22:00 Secret Language to Summon Snakes

It is said that in Changle Village, Taoxi Town, Anhua County, there is a profound master who understands "snake magic" and speaks "snake language". He can cure snake bites with just a bowl of water and heal with a few spells. Even more incredibly, he claims to have inherited a family secret, able to summon snakes from the mountains and send them away with just a few incense sticks and talismans. Is this true? Behind the mystery, is it a rare form of "snake magic", or are there other secrets hidden?

15th December Guangdong TV Public Channel 22:00 Master of Incredible Skills in the Miao Village

A Miao man from deep within the mountains had once given up his studies due to poverty and wandered the streets. Over the past ten years, he has diligently honed his skills, creating unique Chinese folk skills such as drilling his nostrils and pulling a cart with his eye sockets. One by one, seemingly impossible extreme challenges have been miraculously achieved by him. However, when he became famous and successful, his sudden retirement sparked many speculations. What kind of legendary experiences does he have? How many unknown unique skills does he possess?

16th December Guangdong TV Public Channel 22:00 Strange Hero of Foshan

Shadowless kick, Wing Chun, Bruce Lee, Yip Man, Wong Fei-hung - these names that ignite passion all come from Foshan. Are the incredible martial arts that once terrified enemies and gained fame overseas now lost? Are they truly as amazing as the legends say? Can these astonishing martial arts move from legends into the common people's daily lives? This episode takes you to a martial arts gathering, searching for extraordinary individuals hidden in bustling cities, uncovering the secrets behind incredible skills like blowing arrows with the stomach and attracting objects with the palm.
