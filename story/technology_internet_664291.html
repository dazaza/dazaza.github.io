3: Building powerful backlinks requires persistent accumulation over time. Backlinks are the foundation of a website, and website optimization is not something that shows results in just ten days or half a month. The external links of a website need to be accumulated day by day. When you use SEO tools to query websites with good rankings from your competitors and find their backlinks to be so strong, can you think of "Without the bitter cold, how could the plum blossoms smell so sweet!"?

5: Valuable content sometimes is the root of high-quality backlinks. Links cannot generate content for a website, but valuable content can bring high-quality links to the website. If a website can provide users with very valuable content and meet user needs, when other webmasters cannot provide sufficient content on their own websites, but they believe that your website can solve user needs and satisfy user demands, they will naturally direct traffic to the relevant content-providing website in the form of external links.

4: Links should be broad, not excessive, and relevance should be prioritized. When building links, whether the content has relevance is one of the most important considerations. If the external link can be related to the website's theme, it is of course the best. Sometimes, a page may not be related to the overall theme of the site, but if the theme of the page itself is related to the linked page, it carries more weight than unrelated links. For example, if a restaurant says how good our company’s website construction is and how skilled we are, it may not be entirely credible. However, if it comes from peers or design companies, then the credibility is much higher.

Links are one of the essential characteristics of the Internet, and they are also at its core. A link refers to the connection relationship from one webpage pointing to a target. The target pointed to can be another webpage, different positions on the same webpage (like in Shenyang website construction), or even images, email addresses, files, or applications.

How to optimize a website has always been a common topic among webmasters. Many people believe that a website with powerful backlinks will have a good ranking in search engines, and facts have proven that all websites with good rankings rely on their backlinks. Therefore, it is evident that website backlinks indeed play an important role in natural rankings. Of course, backlinks are only one of the key factors in website optimization; website optimization must consider various comprehensive factors. In the process of building a website, most customers will consider the promotion of the website after its launch. Our designers and programmers will take into account the later optimization and promotion of the website during their work. Here, I would like to briefly discuss how to do well in the foundation of backlinks for new site optimization.

2: The quality of backlinks far outweighs the quantity. We often see websites in the same industry where websites with fewer backlinks rank better than those with more backlinks. This shows that the quality of backlinks far outweighs the quantity. One high-quality backlink is more effective than dozens or even hundreds of spammy backlinks. Search engines frequently update website rankings, and each update deletes part of the links deemed useless by the search engine, which is why we sometimes find a significant reduction in links when checking the website's backlinks.

1: Website backlinks should also focus on naturally formed logic. For search engines, they tend to emphasize natural laws. The establishment of backlinks is most taboo against sudden surges. Obvious over-optimization is very likely to result in punishment from search engines. Once punished, all previous efforts will be wasted. Some people add a large number of backlinks within a short period, and then stop afterward. This is harmful rather than beneficial to website optimization. Even if the website is not penalized by the search engine, the keyword rankings will still fluctuate. Building backlinks should steadily increase over time. Instead of adding hundreds of backlinks in a month and then doing nothing afterward, it is better to distribute this time and effort over several months, gradually and steadily increasing, maintaining persistence.
