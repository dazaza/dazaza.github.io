On the day of the opening ceremony of Yinhai City's Hope Avenue, the special vehicles of provincial and municipal leaders who participated in the ribbon-cutting ceremony were intercepted by angry citizens near the Golden Sunshine Garden residential area. The Public Security Bureau took emergency measures, and the ringleaders were arrested... The very next day, Du Yumeng, the procurator of Jinshan City Procuratorate, along with a group of people, was dispatched by the Provincial High Procuratorate on a mission to quietly enter Yinhai City. Starting from the Golden Sunshine Garden incident, they soon discovered that the person they were looking for was one of those arrested by the Public Security Bureau. And before they even had a chance to meet him, he had already met his end under the wheels of a car...

1 First Part

A newly built main traffic artery emerged from the depths of the mountains, like a white silk ribbon, extending from the entrance to the urban area of Yinhai City towards the city center. All the branches of the decades-old giant ginkgo trees newly transplanted on both sides of the road were cut off, resembling monks who have just undergone a solemn tonsure.

2 Second Part

Guan Yanan's mood was low, and seeing Tang Zhaodi did not change his mood in the slightest. At this moment, he felt a bit lost, his mind was in chaos. He felt there were still many things left unsaid to Tang Zhaodi, despite having spoken quite thoroughly that evening. However, those words seemed to have not drawn much attention from Tang Zhaodi, nor caused her too much shock.

3 Third Part

That afternoon, Jin Weidong and Zhang Meran continued to investigate the related situations about several houses under the names of Guan Yanan's relatives. Du Yumeng, along with Shui Oceango and He Zhiqiang, went to Guan Yanan's office. After arriving there, they tore off the seal posted on the door of Guan Yanan's office a few days ago and had someone open the door.
