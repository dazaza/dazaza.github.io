DMP9051 Multi-Parameter Transmitter: A new type of transmitter integrating differential pressure transmitter, temperature transmitter, pressure transmitter, and flow integrator. It can display working pressure, temperature, instantaneous flow, and accumulated flow. It can also perform automatic temperature and pressure compensation for gases and steam, achieving the function of directly displaying standard condition flow and mass flow on-site. When connected to a 24V external power source, it can also provide current, frequency, and 485 remote transmission output. Furthermore, it can operate on a single battery for 2-3 years and can be directly paired with differential pressure flow meters.

Suitable for V-cone, orifice plate, elbow, Annubar, wedge, AK balance, nozzle, Venturi, and Pitot tube flow meters. The DMP9051 offers integrated field display, temperature and pressure compensation, and battery-powered operation for differential pressure flow meters. The second generation of DMP9051 on-site display differential pressure flow temperature and pressure transmitter has been launched. This revolutionary battery-powered multi-parameter transmitter for differential pressure flow offers an ultra-wide range ratio of 1:400, high precision of 0.1 grade, and integrated temperature and pressure compensation.

Key Features:

- Applicable to all differential pressure flow sensors.

- Battery-powered on-site display without external power supply.

- Fully isolated output with strong anti-interference design, shielding all interferences.

- Can output pulse, current (4-20mA), and 485 (Modbus-RTU) signals (external power supply required).

- Built-in temperature and pressure sensors.

- Automatic temperature and pressure compensation for gases and steam.

- Range ratios available: 1:60, 1:100, 1:400.

- Accuracy levels: 0.1 grade, 0.2 grade, 0.5 grade.

- For gases, it can display temperature, pressure, operating condition flow, standard condition flow, etc.

- For steam, it can display temperature, pressure, density, mass flow, etc.
