The grains of rice turn the cooked rice red. The rice shop owner claims that the rice may have been soaked and then dried again.

by letaoehv on 2011-05-25 12:33:11

From the appearance, the rice was a little bit similar to ordinary fragrant rice. The rice was relatively long and translucent. The reporter smelled it carefully and did not find any strange smell.

The rice might have been soaked and then dried.

Aunt Li rinsed the rice in the basin with tap water, poured it into the rice cooker, added more than half a pot of water, and plugged in the power. Half an hour later, the fragrant aroma wafted through the air, and the rice was cooked.

Aunt Li said that the 20-jin bag of "Xiangheyuan" rice was bought last Saturday, and by the time the problem was truly discovered, nearly five jin had already been eaten.

Yesterday, holding a large bowl of red-and-white rice, Aunt Li was very puzzled. Having lived for more than half her life, she still saw such a situation for the first time.

Boss Ji mentioned that not long ago, a dealer of this brand of rice conducted sales at the wholesale market. After inspecting the packaging and checking the quality of the rice, she felt that the rice was quite good, so she ordered about 100 bags. By yesterday, about 30 bags had been wholesaled out, and no retailer had reported any issues with this brand of rice, let alone the strange phenomenon of the rice turning red after cooking.

Cause remains a mystery

Cooking on site

Upon receiving the call yesterday morning, the reporter immediately rushed to Aunt Li's home. The cooked rice from the previous day was placed in a white enamel bowl. Among the white rice, there were dark red grains scattered throughout. When stirred with a rice spoon, even more could be seen at the bottom. Picking up one grain with hand, it felt sticky. The reporter was greatly surprised and quickly requested Aunt Li to cook another batch of rice.

Boss Luo was also troubled. The rice was indeed delivered to Aunt Li's house last Saturday. A total of 10 bags were purchased, and only this one bag had been sold to Aunt Li's family.

No certificate of conformity found in the rice bag

No problems with the same batch of rice

Wholesaler experiment

With the information provided by Boss Luo, the reporter tracked down to the Panxi Grain and Oil Wholesale Market and found Boss Ji. Boss Ji's shop was filled with stacks of rice, and among them was the red-packaged "Xiangheyuan" rice.

Boss Ji, who was thoroughly confused, wanted to know if there was a problem with this batch of rice, so she voluntarily proposed to open a bag of rice and cook it to see. She randomly opened a bag of "Xiangheyuan" rice from the neatly stacked pile, washed it, and placed it in the rice cooker. "Ah, strange," the rice cooked this time had no issues at all—white and fragrant, with a pleasant aroma.

Proof that the red color of the rice was not accidental, Aunt Li, holding the "problematic" rice, went to the rice seller located nearby.

Rice store owner

Aunt Li's son said that actually from the very first day they started eating this type of rice, he noticed the red grains but assumed it was because the pot wasn't cleaned properly, so he didn't pay much attention. As time went on, the red grains increased significantly.

Lifting the lid, a mist of steam rose. Fanning the steam away with his hand, the peculiar red grains appeared again, mixed with the normal white rice. At a glance, it looked as though a layer of wolfberries had been sprinkled on top. Using chopsticks to poke deeper, there were quite a few red grains.

Fragrant rice with red and white grains

News report (by reporter Chen Yingcong): Last night, Aunt Li, who lives at No. 11 Lingshi Alley, Qixiagang, Yuzhong District, encountered a strange occurrence while cooking dinner. Originally silver-white rice turned into grains of dark red when cooked at high temperature, scaring Aunt Li enough to stop preparing dinner.

Boss Luo said that this type of rice was the first time her family sold it, sourced from the Panxi Grain and Oil Wholesale Market. Before purchasing, she verified the various certificates of the merchant and checked whether the rice was yellow or moldy. Just from the appearance, there was nothing unusual. As for why so many red grains appeared after cooking, it might be that the rice had been soaked in water and then dried, resulting in this phenomenon. But since the issue has arisen, Boss Luo could only refund Aunt Li the 50 yuan paid for the rice and provided the phone number of the rice seller at Panxi Market. "If the rice really has a problem, we are victims too!" Boss Luo complained. However, she stated that before the final conclusion is reached, they would not sell the remaining few bags of "Xiangheyuan" rice.

For the same brand of rice, Aunt Li consistently cooked it and found strange red grains each time, whereas the wholesaler's cooking showed no problems, which is indeed perplexing. After being informed of the situation, the industrial and commercial department has intervened in the investigation.

And these past few days, she and her son, daughter-in-law, and daughter have all experienced varying degrees of diarrhea, unsure if it's related to eating this strange red rice. Even her grandson, who is less than seven months old, ate porridge made from this rice, "I don't know if it will affect the child's health?" Aunt Li was very worried, and she quickly took photos as evidence for the rice. "I couldn't find the qualification certificate that is usually present in common rice bags, nor did I find the production date on the outer packaging."

The rice was completely normal before being put into the pot.

After cooking, red grains were mixed in among the rice. Photographed by Wang Hai.

Aunt Li rolled up her sleeves, scooped out the rice cooked the previous day from the rice cooker, washed the pot and lid thoroughly, and then scooped three cups of rice from the red plastic bag marked with the "Xiangheyuan" brand, placing it into the stainless steel basin.

"It's impossible." Looking at the rice, the grain and oil shop owner, Ms. Luo, was also amazed. Having done business here for many years, Ms. Luo enjoys a good reputation in the area. Many residents in the vicinity are her regular customers. Whenever there's a need for rice or oil, they even offer free delivery. Aunt Li's family is also their long-time customer. Over the years, they've never encountered such a strange incident.
