The police caught the robber through the saliva residue on the victim's nipples.

by xue15jwsh on 2012-03-07 15:55:06

Cartoon/Feng Xiaopeng - Not long ago, the police of Xinchang County extracted the saliva left on the victim's nipple by the suspect according to the victim's description and conducted DNA testing, thereby locking and arresting the suspect. Meanwhile, based on this clue, two other people involved in this case were arrested, and several thefts and robberies related to these three individuals were solved.

【Incident】Two men broke into a house under the pretext of collecting gambling debts

At around 11 a.m. on June 10, Zhu, a woman from Xinchang County, heard someone knocking on her door and went to check through the peephole. She saw two strange men standing outside; one was wearing black, the other beige. She then opened the security door. As soon as she opened it, the man in black asked if she was Zhu. Because the name mentioned by the man differed from hers by only one character, Zhu remained silent. The man told Zhu that they were all cellmates with her husband who was still serving time in prison and that her husband had asked her to visit him there. Zhu immediately became suspicious because her husband had not asked her to go for a visit. Zhu thus prepared to close the door, but the two men stood at the entrance refusing to leave. Subsequently, Zhu let them into the living room.

After entering the room, Zhu tentatively asked whether they knew her husband's name. The two only gave an alias, saying, "In prison, he is always referred to like this." Then, they claimed that Zhu's husband had lost 8000 yuan to them gambling inside the prison and instructed them to collect the money from Zhu after their release. At this point, Zhu quickly denied being the person they were looking for. Then, the two men proposed to see her ID card. Reluctantly, Zhu had to go into the bedroom to fetch her ID card.

As soon as Zhu entered the bedroom, the man in black followed her in, suddenly covering her mouth and pushing her down onto the bed. The man in beige immediately rushed into the bedroom, grabbing Zhu's hands, flipping her over, and pressing her back down, stealing a gold ring from her right hand.

Zhu began struggling non-stop, causing her platinum necklace hanging around her neck to fall onto the bed, whereupon it was taken by the man in beige, who also slapped her. The man in black rummaged through things inside the bedroom and found a mobile phone charger cable, which he used to tie her hands behind her back.

At this moment, the man in beige who was holding Zhu down suddenly kissed her right nipple, asking her to be his lover. Seeing no other valuables, the man in black took her mobile phone from the bedside table and put it into his pocket.

Before leaving, the man in black tied her feet with her stockings, while the man in beige gagged her with a towel. After struggling for a bit, Zhu managed to break free from the stockings, went to the kitchen, and cut the charger cable binding her hands with a kitchen knife. She then immediately reported the incident to the police station.

【Investigation】Residual saliva on the nipple locks down the robber

Based on Zhu's report, the police of Xinchang County launched an investigation and obtained relevant clues from Zhu's husband, identifying the suspects.

During the investigation of this case, according to what Zhu claimed, the suspect had kissed her right nipple and smoked at the crime scene. The police used cotton swabs to wipe her right nipple, extracting residual saliva left by the suspect, and collected cigarette butts left at the scene. After forensic examination, human DNA was detected on the cotton swab wiped from Zhu's nipple and on the cigarette butts found at the scene. Analysis revealed that the DNA belonged to Wan, the man in beige.

After capturing Wan, based on his confession, the police successively arrested co-conspirators Wan (another individual) and Li.

【Inside Story】Planned robbery for the sake of ex-wife's "face"

Why did only two of the three individuals involved in this case go to Zhu's home to rob her? How was the plan devised? According to Wan's confession, last October, shortly after his release from labor reform, Zhu, a colleague of his ex-wife, had her house burglarized. At the time, Zhu suspected his ex-wife, leading the public security department to summon his ex-wife for questioning, which made her lose face and stop working.

Upon learning of this matter, Wan became very angry and decided to retaliate against Zhu. In early May this year, Wan gathered Wan and Li, telling them that Zhu had items such as gold necklaces and rings, urging them to join him in robbing her. He specifically informed them that Zhu's husband was imprisoned for tomb raiding, leaving no man at home, making it easier to succeed. Afterwards, Wan repeatedly led Li and Wan to Zhu's residence, pointing out its location. Wan, Li, and Wan agreed to act.

On the morning of June 10, Wan and Li arrived at Zhu's house, but when they called Wan, they found his phone turned off. Consequently, the two proceeded with the robbery alone. Afterward, they specially used the proceeds from selling the jewelry to buy two packs of cigarettes for Wan.

【Progress】Solved multiple theft and robbery cases

After solving this case, the police discovered during interrogation that the three individuals plotting this case were also involved in other crimes.

One evening in March, Wan and Wan sneaked into a small shop in Guantian Village, Luojia Town, using pliers to damage the aluminum alloy door and stole more than 100 yuan in cash and two Nokia 1208 phones.

On the evening of March 13, Wan climbed into the second-floor office area of a university in Nanchang to steal, while Li kept watch outside. They stole a Lenovo Qitian M6900 computer worth 3572 yuan, 280 yuan in cash, and a jade Buddha statue.

One evening in early April, Wan and Wan sneaked into a resident's home in Area A of Xiayalu New Village and stole an electric scooter worth more than 2400 yuan.

On the evening of July 3, Wan and Li lured massage worker Xu to a culvert under an overpass under the guise of soliciting prostitution. Using threats and violence, they robbed Xu of a gold necklace worth more than 5300 yuan and a platinum necklace worth more than 1700 yuan.

The reporter learned that Wan, Li, and Wan's actions are suspected of constituting robbery and theft charges. Additionally, Wan's actions are suspected of lewd conduct against women. Recently, the People's Procuratorate of Xinchang County will review and prosecute this case.
