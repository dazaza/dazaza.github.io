The 40-episode family ethical emotional drama "After Thirty Years of Marriage" is directed by renowned director Wang Xiaokang and stars Li Zonghan, Juanzi, Yue Hong, Wu Mian, Da Juanzi, He Jianze, among others.

The TV series "After Thirty Years of Marriage" simultaneously tells the stories of three families. Relying on China's brilliant changes over the past 30 years, it narrates the development history of China's yellow wine industry over the past 30 years, while reflecting the marital status of three reconstituted families and the joys and sorrows of each family during the 30-year transformation.
