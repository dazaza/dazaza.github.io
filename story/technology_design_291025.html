The loans for animal husbandry, sideline production, and fishery amount to 1.5 billion yuan, the loans for rural infrastructure construction amount to 2.4 billion yuan, and the loans for farmland capital construction amount to 0.99 billion yuan. In order to provide financial support for the new countryside construction, the municipal credit union cooperated with the Youth League Municipal Committee to launch the "Youth Entrepreneurship Card" loan business, providing young people who are interested in starting a business with a maximum of 50,000 yuan in small loans, and giving priority loans, quality services, and preferential interest rates known as the "three advantages" service. From January to October this year, the balance of farmer loans of the municipal credit union was 1.866 billion yuan, and a total of 2.28 billion yuan in agricultural financial organization loans were issued, including 237.5 million yuan for leading enterprises, effectively promoting their development and expansion. The municipal credit union and the poverty alleviation office jointly launched poverty alleviation micro loans, which significantly promoted the adjustment of household business structure and income growth. Under the joint efforts of the Municipal Party Committee, Municipal Government, and financial departments, the People's Bank of China Nantong Central Branch listed our city as a pilot county (city) for financial support of the new countryside construction. Financial institutions such as Rugao Rural Credit Cooperative Union and Agricultural Development Bank of China have indeed increased their support for agriculture. By the end of October, loans of 97.45 million yuan were provided to five municipal-level or above leading enterprises such as Rugao Banxin肠衣, and 23.75 million yuan were lent to five related enterprises of leading enterprises, effectively promoting the development and strengthening of leading enterprises. Leading enterprises play a powerful driving role in agricultural industrialized operation, shouldering multiple functions such as expanding the market, guiding production, and deepening processing. The strength of leading enterprises determines the rise and fall of the industrial chain. The municipal credit union continuously innovates financial products and enhances financial support. Farmers can directly handle loan procedures at the municipal credit union counter within the approved credit limit by holding a loan certificate and valid ID. This greatly improves the level of agricultural services and work efficiency. As of the end of September, 329,500 farmer credit files have been established in the whole city, among which 148,167 have been rated. The effectiveness of supporting the new countryside construction is remarkable.
