"Disaster of Wife" is another story told by the best-selling author of "Director of Reception Office". Worker He Tianliang finds out that his beautiful wife has an affair with Bai Guoguang, the Party Secretary of the factory. In anger, he beats up Bai and is sentenced to 10 years in prison. After being released from prison, He Tianliang searches for his daughter everywhere and wants to live a normal life through hard work. However, Bai Guoguang, who is rich and powerful, does not let him go. The two people with unequal strength start a thrilling open and hidden struggle. He Tianliang takes some abnormal means to carve out a place for himself. When he succeeds in his career, finds his daughter, and falls in love with Xiaocao, misfortune strikes again, and he has to fight to the death. However, the result of the fight shocks him and makes him feel unbearable pain...

The author, with a deep understanding of life and a calm creative attitude, vividly and movingly tells us the story of a group of small characters at the bottom of society using simple and fluent language. At the same time, it reveals the inside story of some hot social phenomena and raises profound questions with practical significance, making readers unable to put it down, while thinking in bitterness.
