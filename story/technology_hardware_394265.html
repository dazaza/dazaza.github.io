Industry Analysis

Market Analysis of Gulou (Marketability of products, current market and potential market conditions)

Macromarket Overview:

Current Status and Market Prospects of China's Toy Market:

China's toy market has great development potential. Currently, the per capita toy consumption level in China is relatively low, but with sustained economic growth and significant increases in urban and rural residents' income, China's toy market will gradually showcase its amazing potential.

According to surveys by the Chinese Toy Association and the Chinese Social Investigation Office, there are currently 286 million children and teenagers under the age of 14 in China. Their average annual toy consumption is only 20 yuan (2.4 USD) to 30 yuan (3.6 USD), far below the average annual toy consumption of 13 USD for Asian children and 34 USD for children worldwide. If China's toy consumption reaches the average level of Asia, the market scale is expected to exceed 30 billion yuan. Additionally, a comprehensive survey report published by the Hong Kong Trade Development Council predicts that the domestic toy market will grow at an annual rate of 40%, reaching sales exceeding 100 billion yuan by 2010.

Moreover, according to estimates by relevant experts, the adult toy market in our country alone can reach around 50 billion yuan annually for the youth demand. This group of people in their twenties and thirties, limited by economic conditions during their childhood, now embrace a "compensation" mentality and have a preference for adult toys. Furthermore, with the acceleration of work and life rhythms, many people experience excessive psychological and physiological stress, and adult toys can alleviate or even help forget external pressures, thus becoming a huge market.

Market Prospects for Gulou on Taobao:

Retail channels for toys in mainland China mainly include department stores, warehouse-style malls, specialty stores, individual retailers, and emerging children's product specialty stores. Among these, department stores and warehouse-style malls are the main places where consumers buy toys. Manufacturers generally supply retailers through product agents or through toy wholesale markets. According to data from the International Toy Association in 1998: In Europe, toy specialty stores, supermarkets, and department stores account for 23%, 19%, and 6% respectively of total toy sales. In China, most toy sales are completed by department stores, while toy wholesale markets have started to handle a small portion of sales in recent years. The singularity of business formats makes it difficult for toy sales to meet customers' understanding and demands well, and is not conducive to creating new consumer demands. Changes in consumer lifestyles and concepts create space for new marketing ideas and new retail formats. With the rapid development of e-commerce, consumer shopping channels are gradually shifting to the internet. Surveys by the Hong Kong Trade Development Council indicate that China is expected to become the second-largest toy retail market after the United States and Europe. Therefore, the retail industry must possess a keen market sense, dig into its inherent business creation functions, and provide toy products using new methods based on consumer lifestyles, consumption awareness, and shopping habits. By closely integrating advanced foreign business formats with the Chinese market, traditional sales models can be changed, and online retail can break regional restrictions and reduce consumer shopping costs while meeting consumers' needs for convenience, choice, and cultural aspects, tightly connecting the toy market with holiday economy and entertainment economy; the integration and flexible use of new business formats create a large toy sales market.

Psychological research shows that each person has corresponding psychological needs at different age stages, and whether a person's psychological needs in childhood are met will affect their entire life's intellectual shaping; modern people live fast-paced lives with high work pressure, and relationships between people are increasingly tense. To eliminate the pressures they face, along with increased demand for leisure and entertainment, the consumer base for adult toys is also rapidly expanding.

For the creative dolls, unconventional gadgets, trendy accessories, and distinctive home decor sold by Gulou Diary, all of these are uniquely featured among toy products, having both entertainment value and practical utility. This aligns with the needs of our customer demographic.

While meeting the needs of our store's customers, it also influences their overall mental development; alleviates issues such as fast-paced modern living, high work pressure, and increasingly strained interpersonal relationships. Moreover, it forms our unique sales style and sales culture, which is beneficial for the development and expansion of our unique market.

Gulou's Pricing Strategy:

The prices of products in Gulou Diary are determined based on the purchasing power and needs of different consumer age groups. Ordinary products are generally priced within the range that various consumer segments can afford.

According to a survey by the Chinese Social Investigation Office: 64% of consumers indicated that if conditions allow, they would consider buying toys suitable for themselves. Among them, 33% of adults believe they like and are willing to purchase toys. However, domestic consumers' demand for toys mainly focuses on mid-range or lower-mid-range products, which account for more than 80% of total toy sales. Although low-end toys have an absolute price advantage, most of their products are of poor quality, very unsafe, and infringe on the patent rights of original products. However, the culture of Gulou Diary enables consumers to find high-quality, reasonably priced creative items, satisfying the demand for high-quality low-end toys. Additionally, our mid-to-high-end products are rare and unique in the market, not only meeting purchasing needs but also being priced lower than similar products, giving us a cost advantage.

By investigating and understanding the pricing of similar products from current competitors, we gain insight into the general price range. We set up a pricing system for product marketing according to low, medium, and high strategies. Low-priced products aim to attract attention, sometimes breaking even or even at a loss just to gain popularity and credibility. Mid-priced products target the mainstream consumer group, aiming to achieve both transaction rates and some profit. High-priced products target the high-end group, not seeking high volume but aiming to compensate for losses incurred by selling low-priced products at a loss, pursuing balance.

Choose sources for products with relatively higher cost-performance ratios, ensuring product quality based on purchase price. Use competition-oriented pricing: in the early stages of operation, adopt a strategy of thin profit margins and high sales volumes to quickly improve credit ratings or open up the market and increase sales. As the online store's reputation improves, appropriately increase profits. But product prices should be as cheap as possible while including reasonable profits, enhancing competitiveness in search price rankings. Prices including shipping should be lower than market prices. Fashionable goods unavailable offline can be priced slightly higher, as too low might negatively impact customer impressions. Clearly state whether the price includes shipping to avoid confusion, which could harm one's reputation. Ambiguous pricing might cause potential customers to abandon purchases.

Gulou Payment Methods:

Currently, online stores mainly offer several payment methods: online payment, postal remittance, bank transfer, cash on delivery. To facilitate customer payments, multiple options should be provided instead of just one method, as this could lose sales opportunities due to customer inconvenience. Generally, cash on delivery is not accepted to reduce operational risks. Use Alipay transactions.

Product Packaging: Pay attention to packaging. Spend some effort, and when necessary, spend money to buy some pretty packaging boxes for our customers' purchased items. Homemade cardboard boxes have their unique advantages: low cost, fully utilizing the recycling value of old cardboard boxes and boards, saving money for oneself and conserving resources for society. The packaging box for mailing items should be as small as possible to reduce weight; do not fill the box with newspapers, use plastic foam instead to reduce weight and save costs. Also, homemade boxes can be made to fit any size, breaking the restriction of fixed sizes from postal boxes.

Gulou Logistics:

1. Keep shipping receipts properly.

2. Specify service hours in the store.

3. Respond to emails within 8 hours.

4. Avoid customers querying orders, proactively send bottom sheets/transaction numbers to customers.

5. For cash on delivery, try to call customers first.

6. After receiving customer complaints, give reassuring responses to customers.

Key Points:

Ship promptly. Use recommended logistics services for online ordering (including YTO Express, STO E-logistics, Yunda Express, Fenghuo Tian Di). If problems occur with the logistics service or goods due to logistics reasons, Taobao will file a claim with the logistics company. If the logistics company does not accept the claim, Taobao will first compensate the seller, prioritizing the seller's rights.

Gulou After-Sales Service:

Mainly regarding wear and tear, loss of items during mailing, and issues caused by packaging problems. Neither the buyer nor the seller wants these situations to happen. However, when problems arise, the seller should first identify the cause of the accident. If it truly is due to the seller’s or the postal service’s fault, the seller should promptly provide a solution to the buyer. This is a principle sellers must follow. How could buyers not trust such a seller? This inadvertently boosts one's popularity. Sometimes, even if one loses profit, long-term profit accumulates precisely because of this.

Do follow-up services after shipping, understand the transportation situation of express deliveries, and timely feedback information to buyers. Let buyers feel that we are sincerely serving them. This not only allows real-time tracking of shipping conditions but also shortens the distance between us and buyers. Even if problems occur during the process, buyers will likely overlook them due to our service attitude.

Gulou Customer Management:

1. Exchange Wangwang IDs with customers before or after each transaction and establish related after-sales service groups for later service and new product promotion, which helps develop old customers and bring in new ones.

2. Establish groups for easier management.

3. Set data items, i.e., understand the customer's "information items" (such as name, ethnicity, age, gender, purchase time, price range, product category, city of residence, etc.) for future classification and searching.

4. Place data items in the first row of Excel (add numbering for future management convenience) and then add customer information line by line.

5. Analyze the customer's purchase (order) time and previous interactions to determine the customer's online time period (for the fastest service).

6. Conduct highly targeted promotional projects on each holiday, birthday, purchase anniversary, etc., and send them in card form (preferably when the customer is online; email may be off-putting).

7. Communicate with customers at specific times identified in the above statistics, inquire about product usage (making them feel VIP all the time), and recommend specific information considering the family members of customers.

8. Learn to cater to their interests, write articles that interest customers but also carry advertising messages, and send them to customers.

9. Propose purchase requests to customers based on weather and time periods.

10. Help customers solve problems (such as financial advice, psychological counseling, etc., as gift products).

11. Send discount cards for "Gulou Diary" before the customer's birthday.

12. Consult customers about their expertise, return with discount cards, allowing them to feel a sense of self-actualization, prompting them to quickly use their "spoils."

13. Establish a "Gulou Diary" points system.

Gulou Risk Prediction:

Small capital costs: Due to the initial stage of store establishment and the owner being a college student, funds are relatively tight.

Insufficient experience: Compared to most Taobao toy sellers, "Gulou Diary" is still in its infancy, with weak competitiveness and relatively insufficient experience compared to professional Taobao sellers of similar products.

Part-time sellers: As college students, Sons and Daughters of the Rivers and Lakes have limited online time, causing a loss of customers.

Relevant Measures:

1) Try to keep the computer on, keep Wangwang online, and set automatic replies. Automatic replies should include: main business scope, recent promotional activities, contact information, telephone, mobile phone, QQ, email, MSN, etc.

2) Try to stay online during peak customer traffic times. According to statistics, the peak customer traffic times on Taobao are 14:00-17:00 and 19:00-22:00. Staying online during these golden hours means higher chances of actual purchases.

3) Change the status on Wangwang to reflect promotional activities and contact information.

4) For capital shortage issues, establish long-term cooperative relationships with relevant physical stores.

5) Use low-price promotions to open up initial sales channels, intensify promotion, provide the best service, and create our unique "Gulou Diary" brand culture.

6) Humbly seek advice, pay attention to the strengths of other similar stores nearby, and learn from experienced sellers' lessons to forge a path suited to ourselves.
