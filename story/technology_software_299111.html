Legend of Mir 3 Private Server, numerous events

by htl2344312 on 2009-12-08 15:54:59

"Haotianlong" will officially start testing at 14:00 on December 4th. We have prepared a variety of exciting online activities for every player who joins the game, and we believe that these activities will surely make players enjoy themselves and be reluctant to leave. Now, the client of "Haotianlong" is available for download. Download it immediately and enter the vast world of "Haotianlong" to create an epic tale of group battles with your comrades-in-arms. We will definitely see each other there!

1. Gift for Meeting Heroes

Upon first login, you will receive a gift from the sky in "Haotianlong". Although pies don't fall from the sky, gifts do! The greeting gift for heroes in "Haotianlong" is waiting for you to log in!

2. Theme Days for Heroes

For every player joining the game, "Haotianlong" has carefully prepared different theme day events with gifts. If you want to celebrate every day, come to "Haotianlong"!

3. Collect Characters to Win Membership

Collect characters to win great gifts —— Supreme VIP privileges, daily gifts, and be the coolest among crowds! During the internal test, monsters in the game will drop special props for the "Collect Characters Event". By collecting the corresponding props and exchanging them with the event NPC, you can win various precious game items and massive experience rewards. You also have the chance to enjoy the prestigious VIP membership privilege for free —— "Dragon Tiger Pavilion Member".

4. Activate Supreme Account to Receive Epic Equipment

The "Supreme Account" is a rare account issued in limited quantities during the internal test of "Haotianlong". Any account activated using a limited gold internal test activation code has the chance to be upgraded to a "Supreme Account". The list of upgraded accounts will be announced on the official website news and official forum!

Note: After reaching a certain level, the "Supreme Account" can claim valuable equipment from a designated NPC.

5. Salary Packages Awaiting Your Claim

Playing "Haotianlong" allows you to earn a salary, with a large amount of game currency always accompanying your growth journey. The total value of the salary package reaches several thousand yuan, letting you experience the life of a white-collar gamer!

6. Level Up Heroes to Win Gifts

After "Haotianlong" starts its test, players can register for the Hero Level-Up Competition at the NPC "Hero Instructor". Players who reach the designated level during the event will receive a beautiful gift and have the chance to win massive experience, epic items, and other rewards. They can also upgrade their account to a "Supreme Account". If the account is already a "Supreme Account", they will additionally receive a mysterious grand prize.

7. Challenge the GM

Mysterious Encounter with GM Activity: During the event period, the first player to encounter the GM in the designated map of the game will receive a mysterious gift.

Battle the GM Activity: During the event period, the GM will announce a hero challenge. Players can be teleported to the event map via an NPC to compete with the GM. There are no limits on the number of participants or levels. The Battle of Heroes against the GM awaits your participation!
