"The Large-scale Charity Bazaar Event '365 Love in the Spring' was a Complete Success"

by childrenstory126 on 2012-02-28 12:10:18

At 2 PM on February 26, 2012, hundreds of volunteers gathered at the first floor of Xinhua Lian International Mall near the Third Ring Road in Beijing to hold a large charity sale and performance event titled "[365 Love in Spring]." This event was jointly initiated by the 365 Children Rescue Fund and volunteers Liu Zhuoling and Sun Shiting. During the process of securing the venue, they encountered difficulties due to the tight schedule and were unable to find an appropriate location. When the leadership of CapitalLand MALL Cuiwei Road Company learned about this matter, they canceled other work engagements and provided free venue sponsorship for the 365 Children Rescue Fund. The company's leadership said: "Our CapitalLand MALL Cuiwei Road Company strongly supports the development of public welfare causes. It is our responsibility as a company to contribute to public welfare, and we also hope that more loving enterprises will participate in public welfare activities."

The purpose of this event was to raise living expenses and medical fees for the children of the 365 Chen Guang Baby Home. Chen Guang Baby Home is a civilian organization currently fostering 14 orphaned disabled children from various welfare institutes across the country. Due to congenital diseases, these children were abandoned by their parents. The Baby Home not only provides the children with a warm home but also offers them a good rehabilitation and educational environment. After learning about the story of the children of the 365 Chen Guang Baby Home online, volunteer Liu Zhuoling organized netizens to bring daily necessities to visit the children. The innocence and happiness of the children deeply moved Liu Zhuoling. She said she wanted to organize netizens to do some loving activities for the children, allowing everyone to personally participate in acts of kindness.

From organizing and planning the event to its official start, only ten days were used. Volunteers Liu Zhuoling and Sun Shiting had not slept well for several days and nights. Although very tired, they remained optimistic. They said: "To help these children, working hard is worth it. I give, I am happy."

At the event site, nearly 300 volunteers participated in the charity sale. Some grassroots stars and child celebrities came to the scene to support the event. Other volunteers brought their children to the scene to show their love. On the stage, the actors' wonderful performances attracted passersby, who also joined the team of love. When the volunteers performed programs together with the orphans of the 365 Chen Guang Baby Home, many kind-hearted people in the audience were moved to tears.

The charity sale area was the liveliest part of the entire event. The finance personnel of the China Social Welfare Foundation were busy collecting donations. Some volunteers not only donated idle items from their homes but also bought love gifts from other charity sale booths. Everyone was surrounded by, moved by, and happy with this deep sense of love! This event raised a total of 22,910.75 yuan, all of which will be used for the living expenses and medical costs of the children of the 365 Chen Guang Baby Home.

The large-scale charity sale public welfare activity "[365 Love in Spring]" concluded with the song "[A Grateful Heart]." Many volunteers gained a close-up understanding of public welfare at the event site and personally participated in public welfare activities. While giving, they also received happiness!
