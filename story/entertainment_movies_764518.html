Synopsis:

Due to exposure to cosmic rays, Reed Richards and his friend Ben Grimm, along with Sue and Johnny Storm, have their DNA restructured, giving them superhuman abilities... After overcoming the discomfort and disagreements caused by their physical transformations, Reed, as "Mr. Fantastic," gathers the other three together to form the Fantastic Four (F4). They use their unique and powerful abilities to protect the citizens of New York, also dealing with the ambitious Doctor Doom along the way.

Having already adapted to their new selves, the F4 continues to explore their new abilities, aiming to elevate these changes into a genetic evolution. Moreover, their lives have returned to normal, becoming enjoyable. Johnny remains carefree and always on the lookout for hot women, which is his favorite pursuit. However, watching Reed and sister Sue share affection, and the once-bullied Ben having a stable relationship with his blind sculptor lover Alicia, Johnny suddenly feels a sense of loneliness he has never experienced before. Should he also find a good girl to settle down?

Reed and Sue are finally about to tie the knot... However, during the wedding ceremony, it is interrupted by an eerie wind that disrupts the vows at the altar and scatters the entire banquet. Reed sees a silver shadow flash across the sky, and a bad feeling arises in him. He asks Johnny to transform into the "Human Torch" and chase after it, but the Human Torch proves no match for the opponent. Not only does he lose, but his body undergoes temporary subtle changes—this silver shadow turns out to be the Fantastic Four's new enemy, the "Silver Surfer," who hails from another planet.

Reed's premonition proves correct; since the appearance of the Silver Surfer, devastating disasters have occurred around the world. General Hager requests the Fantastic Four to team up with the military to capture the Silver Surfer, who has caused widespread panic worldwide. They must uncover the mysterious veil of this enigmatic galactic messenger, the Silver Surfer, before all hope is lost. As the investigation deepens, Sue, through her female intuition, perceives the nobility and sorrow within the Silver Surfer's heart... It turns out that the Silver Surfer is also a pitiable person. Originally an astronomer, he voluntarily became a puppet of his enemy to save his home planet from being devoured by the "Planet Eater" Galactus. Riding his surfboard, he travels the galaxy searching for planets for Galactus to consume, and his next target is Earth. The strong team spirit of the Fantastic Four reignites long-dormant emotions within the Silver Surfer. He betrays Galactus and assists the Fantastic Four in forcing him to abandon Earth. However, the Silver Surfer is punished for his actions, being forcibly confined to Earth by Galactus.
