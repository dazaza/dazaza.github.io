The Wound in the Splendor

In dazzling years, beautiful times, as if in a brilliant heaven. However, beneath the quiet heaven, there always hides much sorrow. Perhaps, I once possessed a lot of warmth and then lost it; perhaps, I never owned too much. Everything is undeniable.

I swear not to let myself be saddened by some trivial matters, but sometimes, sadness arises spontaneously. I lose my mind like a madman, singing wildly until I feel torn apart inside, yet strangely, my heart doesn't ache. I don't care who's around me anymore; all I know is that I need to calm down, and only this impulsive kind of singing can make me feel at ease.

After singing, my throat feels hoarse. Then I log out of QQ, Renren, Feixin, and even turn off my mobile phone. I take out my notebook from my bag and start furiously taking notes under the light. Word by word, I suddenly think of Lu Xun, who spent his silent decade copying books day by day "instead of indulging in alcohol and women." I can't empathize with Lu Xun's pain, but probably no one can understand my pain either. I keep writing until my eyes hurt and become sore. When I look up, I feel much better.

I don't like when others repeatedly break their promises to me. Every time I say "it doesn't matter" just because I don't want people to feel guilty. I don't like when others treat my words as jokes. Every time I laugh "hehe," it just means I have nothing to say, not that I'm telling jokes. I don't like when others are arrogant in front of me. A person can be proud of themselves, but why should I appear so humble? Do they know how tiring it is to feel inferior for too long and how much you want to leave? And I don't like when others敷衍me. Either don't respond or give me more attention. What does "恩恩啊啊" mean after a long message from me? All these things, I don't like. I am sad because I've never been important. The word "important" in their mouths is merely two characters, not something cherished in their hearts. I never want to tell others about my sorrows because it's meaningless. So those people always think I won't get hurt, making me uncomfortable again and again without realizing it, how ironic. Blame them? Blame me? Who knows? No one is to blame, this is just a kind of growth. I see it clearly. I'm sad not because of anything else, just because I've invested too much emotion. Probably, people need to learn self-protection. I shouldn't be so serious. That's it. It's already like this. The worst has already happened. When everything becomes a habit, it will all be fine.

The brighter my smile, the more splendid I am. The more splendid you see me, the more wounded I am.

By the end of March, the sun begins to shine brightly, and I will follow its brightness. Bright enough to see myself shining, bright enough not to see the darkness hidden in the splendor, although, the brighter the place, the deeper the darkness it conceals.

Tired, goodnight.
