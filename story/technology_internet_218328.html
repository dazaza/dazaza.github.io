Mobile email integrates into mobile commerce, making business marketing faster and more efficient.

by now on 2009-08-05 12:10:35

Not long ago, relevant reports showed that mobile Internet users in our country were currently experiencing explosive growth. It was predicted that the number of mobile Internet users would exceed 200 million this year, with more than 600 million potential users. These figures all indicate that China's mobile Internet has a broad market, which is precisely the great driving force for the development of mobile e-commerce by enterprises in our country.

Under the efforts of the Chinese government and major operators, coupled with continuous reductions in mobile Internet access fees, these conditions have created a good environment for the development of China's mobile Internet. Now, 3G portals have started to become profitable, and the development of China's mobile Internet market has already taken shape. In the near future, mobile e-commerce will become an indispensable field of development for enterprises.

Taking advantage of the vigorous development of the mobile Internet, many Internet service providers have also joined this wave of development. At the beginning of this year, NetSun Business declared that it would move e-commerce search onto 3G phones. This move proves that the development of e-commerce has already penetrated into the mobile Internet. The enterprise mailbox launched by Now.cn (www.now.cn/email), a well-known Internet service provider in our country, has also been integrated into the mobile Internet. Their enterprise mailbox comes with mobile email functionality. The research and application of mobile email just meet the needs of enterprises developing mobile e-commerce.

So how is mobile email applied in the development of mobile e-commerce? According to relevant responsible persons at Now.cn, mobile email is an extension of the functions of enterprise email. It can be used simultaneously on computers and mobile phones. Users can not only send and receive emails on their computers but also read, reply, forward, and write emails through their mobile phones. Most importantly, users can use email services anytime and anywhere via mobile email. Regardless of location, they can promptly send and receive emails and handle important business matters, eliminating the obstacles of time and place restrictions that ordinary email encounters. The application of these functions exactly fills the need for email marketing in the development of mobile e-commerce by enterprises. From this perspective, mobile email can not only help users easily conduct mobile office work but also enable enterprises to expand the market for mobile e-commerce, perfectly aligning with the current trend of mobile Internet development.

China's mobile Internet is developing vigorously, and more and more enterprises will delve into the development of mobile e-commerce. The mobile office, email marketing, speed, and convenience functions of mobile email will also be applied in this area. As the number of mobile Internet users grows rapidly, enterprises should accelerate the pace of developing mobile e-commerce because the business opportunities contained within more than 600 million potential users are not insignificant.
