The Love Saved by a Bra

Xiaomu occasionally didn't wear a bra, but this time was different. She remembered dressing neatly early in the morning before leaving home. However, when she returned and wanted to take a shower, she discovered that her bra had vanished. Naked from the waist up, she stood in front of the mirror in a daze, finding it increasingly strange. Could it be him? Xiaomu thought of Ada, who had been pursuing her recently. However, Xiaomu found him short and not handsome, so she ignored him. That afternoon, Ada took her out for a meal, and she only drank a bottle of beer. Then they went to the karaoke bar to sing, with everything still vividly imprinted in her memory. Ada hadn't even touched her...

It was impossible for Ada to have stolen the bra.

After washing herself, Xiaomu sat on the sofa in her pajamas, lost in deep thought about the matter, unable to come up with any answers. After searching through her wardrobe again, she ultimately couldn't find the bra she was looking for. Xiaomu began to feel truly alarmed.

Suddenly, she noticed the wallet placed on the table and frowned, thinking, "Could I have encountered a more skilled pickpocket than me on the '315' bus? No way! If someone had noticed, why wouldn't they have taken my wallet? Why didn't they report me? This is strange. Who could have such great skills, able to steal my... right through my clothes?"

By the next day, Xiaomu still hadn't figured out how her bra had gone missing. Since then, she dared not take the '315' bus anymore.

In order to uncover the truth, Xiaomu tried every method to test Ada, but nothing ever went missing again. Xiaomu concluded that Ada couldn't possibly have such incredible skills.

That day, after eating at a restaurant, Ada sent her home, taking the '315' bus again. Xiaomu hesitated as she got on, which was her first time riding this bus since losing her bra. After getting on, she cautiously observed the people around her, pretending to scratch her armpit while feeling that her bra was still there. She then laughed cheerfully and chatted with Ada. The bus became increasingly crowded, and a well-dressed woman carrying a handbag boarded the bus. When she squeezed past Xiaomu, Xiaomu's handbag already contained an extra wallet. Xiaomu glanced at Ada, who was looking out the window, seemingly admiring the beautiful scenery outside.

"There's a thief on the bus, my wallet is gone," the woman shouted. Chaos erupted among the passengers. Upon hearing this, the driver immediately stopped the bus and dialed '110'. Within just a few minutes, '110' arrived, boarding the bus and calling all the passengers down one by one for inspection. But the search yielded no results. After checking all the passengers, no thief was found. Just as the '110' staff were questioning the female victim, the driver standing at the bus door suddenly shouted loudly: "There's a bag on the bus, check if it's yours!" Hearing this, the female victim rushed onto the bus, and indeed, it was her lost bag. Excitedly, she called out to those below: "I've found it, I've found it."

"Be more careful next time, don't make noise unnecessarily!" Someone among the passengers said angrily.

"My wallet is inside my tightly closed bag; it must be that the thief, upon hearing '110', threw the bag onto the bus..."

After the false alarm, Xiaomu and Ada reboarded the bus. They looked at each other, neither saying a word.

On the evening of the second day, Ada came to find Xiaomu. As they approached a crossroads, they saw a large crowd gathered in front of the supermarket, making a lot of noise, with a police car parked nearby. There must be a thief. Thinking this, Ada quickened his steps to get closer. Unexpectedly, he saw Xiaomu surrounded by two policemen. Ada squeezed into the crowd and asked Xiaomu, whose expression seemed slightly panicked, "What happened?"

"What happened?" A policeman glanced at Ada and asked, "Who are you to her?"

"Oh, she's my girlfriend," Ada replied calmly.

"The security guard says she stole something, but your girlfriend has been stubbornly denying it. Here's the deal: if she admits it, we can go easy on her. If she doesn't admit it—well, the security guard says they have evidence."

"Evidence?" Ada stared intently at the security guard's eyes and confidently declared, "My girlfriend always behaves properly. You mustn't falsely accuse her!"

"We haven't falsely accused her. The surveillance clearly recorded everything. The item is on her person. If she continues to deny it, we'll review the footage."

"I won't go, I didn't steal anything." Xiaomu turned red in the face and insisted.

"You'll know soon enough whether you stole or not."

Just then, a woman loudly proclaimed that her wallet was missing! This drew everyone's attention towards Xiaomu once again.

Ada moved closer to Xiaomu, gripping her hand and said, "Go with them. If you didn't steal, then you didn't steal." Pushed along, they entered the manager's office. The security guard pressed the button on the video recorder and gave Xiaomu one last look before asking, "Here's the evidence. Are you going to talk?"

"Search her clothes, what's the point of watching the footage? Find the wallet, and we'll beat her until she confesses!"

Xiaomu's face flushed red. Seeing this, Ada angrily said, "Watch the footage then, I don't believe my girlfriend is a thief!"

After the video recorder flashed with static, the shoppers in the supermarket appeared one by one. The security guard watched carefully for a moment, then suddenly furrowed his brow and said, "Something's wrong. This isn't today's footage."

"Let's watch today's footage then," a policeman said seriously.

The security guard searched for a long time but couldn't find today's footage tape. He turned to the policeman and said, "This is strange. I clearly saw her hide the socks from the shelf inside her clothes."

"In that case, let's all step outside. Come here," the policeman called the woman who lost her wallet over and said to her, "Check if her body has your wallet."

"I didn't steal."

"Let her check. If she doesn't find anything, we'll naturally clear your name!"

"Let her check." Ada confidently glanced at Xiaomu and told the others, "All of you go outside."

Not long after the group left, the woman who lost money walked out of the manager's office dejectedly and told the policeman, "It wasn't her. I didn't find anything on her."

At this moment, the manager also returned from outside. After hearing the security guard's explanation, he too found the situation peculiar. However, without finding any evidence, he smiled apologetically at the policeman and Xiaomu, claiming it was a misunderstanding, perhaps the security guard had made a mistake. The security guard tried to defend himself but was stopped by the manager.

"My wallet!" The woman suddenly pulled a wallet out of her pocket and joyfully exclaimed. The policeman looked at her and said, "Are you deliberately fooling us?"

The woman kept shaking her head, repeatedly saying that her wallet had indeed been missing just now.

"Can you guarantee my girlfriend stole your stuff?" Ada looked at several security guards, lifted his shoe, and asked, "Look at the socks I'm wearing now. Are these the ones my girlfriend stole earlier?"

The manager quickly stepped forward to mediate. Several security guards hung their heads dejectedly and remained silent...

As Ada and Xiaomu walked home together, Xiaomu's excitement hadn't diminished. Coming to a small bridge, she tugged at Ada and asked, "Tell me, what exactly do you do?"

"I'm just an ordinary person."

"Don't fool me. Tell me quickly, what do you do? The socks on your feet are the ones I stole from the supermarket. What do you do?"

"I'm just, just Ada who likes Xiaomu."

Xiaomu grabbed Ada urgently and said, "If you don't tell me, I'll strangle you."

"Don't, wait." Ada stopped Xiaomu, frowning and said, "Why do we keep playing around while walking fine? Look, your bra fell under the bridge!"

"Nonsense!" Xiaomu said, suspiciously reaching into her clothes. Suddenly, she shouted loudly, "Ada, this time I've caught you. It turns out you stole my bra again."

Ada pointed to under the bridge, following his finger Xiaomu saw a light blue bra lying on the grass under the bridge, shocking Xiaomu speechless. Watching Ada walk down to pick up the bra, a look of unprecedented astonishment appeared on her face.

"Ada, what exactly do you do? Why did you help me?"

Ada slowly said, "Actually, I've also taken the wrong path before, but later I corrected myself. Now I'm a magician. As for why I helped you, I can only say, liking someone doesn't need a reason. Do you remember that time you were caught stealing a bag on the bus and got beaten badly? I was there too. At that time, I decided that I would save you."

Hearing Ada's heartfelt words, Xiaomu cried softly. Sobbing, she said to Ada, "I'll change. I will never steal again..."

Two years later, a supermarket security guard was watching TV at home. On the TV, there was a report promoting a pair of magicians who contributed to charity to help poor students attend university. At the end of the program, there was a wonderful performance by the magicians. The security guard praised endlessly while watching, suddenly realizing how familiar the two people were. Looking closely, he was shocked speechless for half a day.
