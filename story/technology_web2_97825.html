Online image editing software based on Flash technology: Pixlr

by xjyzhenai on 2008-10-21 15:11:36

We often encounter the following problems:

When you need to create a file in a certain format, but don't have the corresponding software.

When you need to immediately submit a completed file, but suddenly remember that you don't have the required software installed on your computer.

When you're at an internet cafe and can't download files or install software...

The most common type of demand is for images. Image processing software can be bulky. How can you quickly and easily process the images you want? The web2.0 era has given us a satisfying answer - online image processing software: Pixlr.

Pixlr is based on Flash technology, which means it can be easily embedded into web pages. So as long as your computer has internet access, you don't need to waste time downloading hundreds of megabytes of software to get things done.

The biggest feature of Pixlr is its power. It's just like the powerful Photoshop. Even the interface looks very similar to PS. Pixlr is still in development, but it's already sufficient for basic image processing. Pixlr supports multiple languages, including simplified Chinese.

Pixlr even offers an open API, allowing you to integrate it into your blog, web application, or website.
