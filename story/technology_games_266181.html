Ghost-Stories Board Game: Premium Chinese Edition

by nickamanda on 2009-11-21 00:10:47

This is a cooperative board game designed by Antoine Bauza for 1-4 players aged 12 and above, full of creativity and challenges!

Players need to send the ghosts to their afterlife before they take over the village. Each player takes on the role of a Taoist priest (yes, the kind from China), working together to withstand wave after wave of ghost attacks and finally send their boss back to where it came from.

Background story: At the end of the Ming Dynasty and the beginning of the Qing Dynasty, there was an inconspicuous small village on the edge of Xiangxi, home to about a hundred households. The villagers worked from sunrise to sunset, farming and fishing every day, living a primitive life, and the whole village presented a scene of prosperity. But the village's tranquility was soon broken by some strange phenomena. The river water on the west side of the village became increasingly murky, the bamboo forest on the east side occasionally emitted strange cries at night, smoke and dust rolled on the southern hillside, and the northern marsh gave off foul odors.

At this time, four Taoist priests came from all directions of the village and told the villagers the source of these strange phenomena. Thousands of years ago, the Nine Netherworld King was naturally cruel and warlike, leading a horde of ghosts from hell to the human world, stirring up wars everywhere. At that time, four Taoist priests who had become immortals set up a magical array at the current location of the village to lure the Nine Netherworld King. After a fierce battle, they finally sent the Nine Netherworld King back to hell and sealed its power in this place. Recently, the power of the Nine Netherworld King has somewhat recovered. Although it is not enough to break the seal, it has summoned a horde of ghosts and its incarnations to attack the village, intending to destroy the seal. If the Nine Netherworld King reappears in the human world, the consequences will be unimaginable.

The four people in front of you are the descendants of the Taoist priests who sealed the Nine Netherworld King at that time, and you are one of them. The task of driving away the ghosts, protecting the village, and preventing the Nine Netherworld King from reappearing in the human world falls on you and your companions. Can you use your own skills and the help of the villagers to complete the mission?
