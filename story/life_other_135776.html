Lemon Green Tea [Spark] is looking for partners, aiming to work with the most excellent people to achieve the greatest cause.

by loveruly on 2009-03-30 11:01:33

In the movie "No Thieves", Uncle Li has a classic line: "What's the most valuable in the 21st century? Talents!"

Nowadays, the competition for talents in all fields has become increasingly fierce. As the saying goes, those who have the talents will win the world. Whether a country or a company can gather truly excellent talents determines its rise and fall.

Zhou Yu's words in his blog hit the nail on the head and revealed the essence of this issue: "If building the Great Wall, talents are the foundation stones; if constructing a skyscraper, talents are the pillars; if running a business, talents are the guarantee of success. If you want to expand your business instead of being a small workshop owner, you must value talents. No matter what kind of career you pursue, talents are the guarantee of success."

With the continuous development and expansion of the business, the people of Lemon Green Tea aim to transform a two-person cause into a common cause for everyone. Talents are highly sought after, and seeking more outstanding talents to support the business is the hard truth of development! Building a core leadership team that integrates wisdom and strength is the key to development. A great leadership team means half of the success in the business.

Lemon Green Tea eagerly seeks talents and respects them. Not only has it hired a personnel manager from a foreign-funded enterprise as the company's human resources director with high salaries, but it has also invited a seasoned market manager from a well-known home appliance company to serve as the company's vice president after repeated efforts. A group of senior professional managers from traditional industries, who share the same ideals and aspirations, have joined the entrepreneurs of Lemon Green Tea to build an excellent management team together.

Niu Niu, an employee of the company, was once a regional manager in a traditional industry with a long history! After joining the Lemon Green Tea team, he did not slack off at all because of his previous outstanding work achievements. Instead, he became even more proactive in adapting to this new environment. Working overtime voluntarily became a daily routine, and he often worked until after twelve o'clock! Undoubtedly, his excellent performance proved his extraordinary strength. Whether in company management, retail department assessment, or store management, he made outstanding contributions. He received consistent recognition from both leaders and colleagues and had a very high evaluation!

Another employee, Xiao Qi, has been actively striving since the day she joined the company. She diligently works and provides many valuable constructive suggestions regarding various issues discovered during her work, greatly improving the operational efficiency of the clothing department and enhancing benefits! Within a short period, she was unofficially recognized by her colleagues in the clothing department as the head of the clothing department!

An excellent enterprise is like an excellent person, having a healthy body, flexible limbs, and more importantly, a wise brain. This batch of outstanding talents from traditional industries has injected fresh blood into the company and built the framework of the enterprise together with the entrepreneurs of Lemon Green Tea. Good thoughts and concepts have been introduced, and an excellent set of management standards has been redefined.

The operating philosophy of Alaiwu Company: "Operate people! Cultivate corporate talents and social elites! Provide a stage for every hardworking person, help them unleash their potential, and realize their dreams!"

Internal cultivation and selection of talents have always been the fundamental work that Lemon Green Tea consistently focuses on. "As the company grows, we will never forget the old employees who have fought with us to establish the company. The company will continue to invest more manpower and resources in training you. We hope that every old employee can grow together with the company!" How to cultivate talents from the grassroots level has always been the top priority of the company's work.

Lemon Green Tea's standard for selecting talents is simple: appointing people based solely on merit! Regardless of one's background, education level, whether they have work experience, whether they were previously a leader, or whether they are smart... as long as they are diligent, pragmatic, and strive for progress, the company will recognize them and give them enough space to showcase their abilities.

Many middle and senior-level cadres, such as the managers of the retail department, wholesale department, various sales supervisors, and the after-sales department manager, started from the most basic work and were selected layer by layer. These grassroots cadres are very familiar with the details of the company's start-up, have a good grasp of the company's business processes and work details, co-founded the company with Lemon Green Tea, and deeply understand the hardships of entrepreneurship. They can treat the company's affairs as their own, making them the most outstanding talents of the company and the most precious wealth of the company.
