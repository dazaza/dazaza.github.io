Aibang.com mobile life search is officially online

by yinyin on 2008-03-01 10:37:03

Aibang.com has launched a WAP mobile search service. Through Aibang's mobile service, any mobile user with GPRS or CDMA1x can log in to wap.aibang.com via their mobile browser and obtain local information on 15 categories of lifestyle services including dining, entertainment, clothing, food, accommodation, transportation, education, and healthcare anytime and anywhere. Behind this move, Aibang.com, as the leader in lifestyle search, officially extends its reach into the wireless search field.

Users logging into Aibang's mobile interface can first select a city from the mobile directory, then input a specific location within that city, and subsequently choose or enter the type of lifestyle service they are looking for. Aibang will then find relevant nearby lifestyle service providers. Aibang.com also specifically provides a "call" function after the merchant's phone number for the convenience of direct dialing by users.

Currently, there are 520 million mobile users in China. According to statistics, as of December 31, 2007, the number of mobile internet users in China was 50.4 million. However, no service provider has been able to offer good wireless lifestyle search services. With the launch of Aibang's mobile search service, this situation will change. As the "most comprehensive merchant information", "covering the broadest range of lifestyle areas", and "having the most reviews" lifestyle search engine, Aibang will bring better experiences to users.
