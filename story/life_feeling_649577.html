Can we be so happy one day?

by zhouzhou56 on 2011-12-31 00:52:48

One day, can we be this happy?

One day, can we be this happy - going to see the beautiful scenery of the places we want to go together, eating the snacks we want to eat and savoring them again and again, leaving our footprints and memories in every place.

One day, can we be this happy - climbing the mountain we once said we would go together, leaning on each other to watch the bright stars in the sky, and making a promise to be together forever to the meteor.

One day, can we be this happy - buying a house not too big or too small, designing and decorating it together, preparing all the things in the house together, and permeating warmth into every drop.

One day, can we be this happy - having your warm arms at night, occasionally quarreling like a child asking you to sing, then you helplessly but indulgently pat my head, letting me fall asleep listening to your voice.

One day, can we be this happy - eating the delicious food you make every day, eating it without worrying about the image, watching you smile and help me wipe off the residue on the corner of my mouth.

One day, can we be this happy - I am unreasonable sometimes, and you are always tolerant and understanding. Sometimes we have a cold war, and finally compromise together.

One day, can we be this happy - when the other is busy, step back appropriately, don't disturb and miss silently, and bring a cup of hot tea when necessary, waiting quietly for the other to finish.

One day, can we be this happy - when I come home late, there is always your figure under the dim yellow light, walk up quietly and hug you lightly, and then go back to our home together.

One day, can we be this happy - shopping for clothes together, with you matching for me and me picking for you, even the coldest winter has each other's heart to keep warm.

One day, can we be this happy - knowing that the other is not the best, knowing the other's shortcomings, but still loving stubbornly, and still willing to enter the "siege" for the other.

One day, can we be this happy - not caring about what the other gives and what I get, only caring whether the other is happy or not, when love slowly becomes a habit, plainness is also unforgettable.

If one day we can be so happy, I am willing to take your happiness as my happiness, and I am willing to hold you gently in my arms and tell you that I will always be with you no matter what when you are sad and crying.

If one day we can be so happy, I am willing to mark every memorable day on the calendar, recalling those past events together, and keeping the sweetness and beauty in my heart forever.

If one day we can be so happy, I am willing to guard you for a lifetime, holding your hand and growing old with you, never leaving or abandoning. My love is not luxurious, but real, and I am willing to risk my life for you if necessary.
