Lusong Garment and Apparel City: One of the Top Ten Specialized Markets

by shenli474a on 2012-02-06 14:09:45

The Lusong clothing business circle has gathered more than 7,000 general agents of Taobao.com clothing brands in China. Their products radiate to more than 230 counties and cities in 15 provinces and cities, with an annual turnover of nearly 30 billion yuan. In order to enhance the overall quality of Lusong Garment City, Lusong District comprehensively launched the quality improvement and transformation project of Lusong Garment City on August 5th. The project mainly includes five items: the civil air defense project of Renmin South Road, the underground pipeline renovation project, the creation project of national "3A" tourist attraction, the pedestrian traffic diversion planning project of garment city, and the beautification and brightening project of garment city. The district committee and district government of Lusong District formed a project command composed of strong forces drawn from relevant departments such as the district urban construction bureau and the district commerce bureau, which was commanded by Liu Haibin, standing committee member of the district committee and deputy district governor. At the same time, a special矛盾纠纷coordination group was established to increase attention to all kinds of problems emerging inside the market and maximize the resolution of various contradictions and disputes in the bud. Currently, the comprehensive quality improvement and transformation project of Lusong Garment City has successfully achieved the expected goals. Through the overall transformation of roads and building facades within the garment city, as well as the comprehensive upgrading of greening, tourism supporting facilities and services, Lusong Taobao.com garment city will welcome visitors from all over with a brand-new look. Following the release of the "Lusong trillion yuan clothing industry cluster" plan by Zhuzhou Municipal Government in 2010, Lusong set up the garment city command this year at the beginning; in June, Zhuzhou Guotou and Lusong Guotou jointly invested to establish Zhuzhou Garment Industry Development Co., Ltd., determined to build the capital of garment in southern China; in August, the partition planning of Lusong garment city and aviation city passed the examination. According to the plan, the capital of garment in southern China includes a modern trade center, a headquarters creative base, and a garment industry new city, which will further boost the formation of a complete industrial chain in Lusong garment city to continue to grow stronger.
