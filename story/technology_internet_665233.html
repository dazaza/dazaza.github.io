Only some qualified people can be recruited and then trained in professional knowledge and other skills.

by bhhgsyfk4143 on 2012-02-14 15:02:56

A headhunter said, "The main channel for recruiting customer managers in the private banking department is other banks, especially foreign-funded banks. But due to the lack of talent, we are also broadening our recruitment channels and will go to the asset management departments of fund, trust, insurance companies and asset management companies to recruit sales personnel and investment research department staff. Generally speaking, the retail business of foreign-funded banks has higher requirements for personal net assets. Therefore, if the customer manager of a foreign-funded bank jumps to a private bank, it can directly bring customer resources to the bank. However, if recruited from securities companies and other financial institutions, it is difficult to recruit top talents, and only some qualified talents can be recruited for professional knowledge and other skills training."

It seems that internal cultivation is still the first means for the private banking department to obtain talent, but the cultivation speed is far lower than the industry's demand for talent. A headhunter revealed that recently, many customer managers in the private banking department frequently changed their "bosses". Some customer managers from the private banking department of state-owned commercial banks went to joint-stock commercial banks, while some customer managers from the private banking department of joint-stock commercial banks jumped to securities companies or fund companies. Even more people joined private equity investment.

A person from a commercial bank recently told reporters that a customer manager in the personal finance department of his bank had an interview opportunity with the private banking department of the bank. If successful, his salary would increase significantly. Such recruitment is held once a year by the bank, and many capable employees actively participate. There is a big difference in salary.

It is understood that most of these talents have a background in asset management and mostly left when the private bank was established, then returned to their original bank as "returning" personnel. After returning, they mainly still engage in asset management business, but their positions and benefits have improved.

According to the data investigated by Taihe Consultant in 2011, the median basic salary (which can be understood as middle-income) of entry-level PBs in the private banking department was 110,000 yuan, with bonuses of about 50% of the basic salary; the median basic salary of junior PBs was 280,000 yuan, with bonuses of about 50% of the basic salary; the median basic salary of intermediate PBs was 476,000 yuan, with bonuses of about 50% of the basic salary; the median income of senior PBs was 750,000 yuan, with bonuses of around 40% of the basic salary. Generally, the total annual income of entry-level PBs is about 165,000 yuan, and the total annual income of senior PBs is about 1 million yuan. High recruitment requirements.

However, insiders believe that although many banks hope to establish an internal cultivation mechanism and a talent delivery channel, the talents in the individual finance department and corporate finance department do not fully meet the requirements of the private banking department. The latter has higher comprehensive ability requirements, including sales ability, communication ability, professional ability, international perspective, etc., and not just developing customer resources. The "2011 China Wealth Management Report" also shows that the ability and professional quality of service and sales personnel are one of the important reasons for high-net-worth individuals to choose private banks. When customers choose customer managers, they consider "professional financial knowledge" and "good communication skills" the most.

In addition, bank insiders revealed that the recruitment requirements for customer managers in the private banking department include: postgraduate degree or above, studying finance, economics, law, mathematics or related majors, having at least one year of work experience in the individual finance department of the bank, having experience in customer maintenance and contact, having certain work achievements and service capabilities, and having good image and temperament.

It is understood that since last year, the talent war in the private banking sector has gradually escalated, and the turnover rate of customer managers in the private banking department has increased. Five large banks, most joint-stock banks, and foreign-funded banks have successively established specialized institutions and built their own professional teams to join this competition. In addition, more potential competitors plan to establish private banking departments. According to the investigation data of Taihe Consultant, the personnel turnover rate in the private banking department is between 20% and 30%.

The reason why many people jump from other departments to the private banking department lies in the high salary. According to insiders, the customer managers (PB) in the private banking department are roughly divided into entry-level, junior, intermediate, and senior levels. In 2007 and 2008, there was no obvious difference in income between the private banking department and other departments of the bank, but in recent years, a large number of external talents, especially overseas and pan-financial field talents entering the private banking system, have pushed up the salary level in the industry.

It is understood that generally, the customer managers of the private banking department are mainly selected from the excellent customer managers of the bank's wealth management center. Although the ideal development path is from corporate finance department customer manager to financial planner, and then to private banking customer manager, few corporate finance department customer managers are willing to go to the individual finance department. The service content of individual finance department customer managers and private banking customer managers is closer, and the gap in salary income makes it easier for the private banking department of various banks to recruit employees from the individual finance department.

High salaries make the recruitment requirements of the private banking department very high, and the workload and pressure are also quite heavy.
