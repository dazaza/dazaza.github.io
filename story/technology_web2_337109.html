Today, it's already 10:30, but because I was out these past few days, none of my regular work has been done. So, I will stay late to catch up on the work that wasn't done! Today, we will continue explaining practical experience and techniques regarding Taobao affiliates, hoping to give everyone a good start.

In the middle of 2008, the main source of advertising revenue was GG. Later, I learned about the existence of Taobao affiliates and thus created a specialized Taobao affiliate website, which opened on February 1st, 2009. In the first few months after the website opened, the money earned through Taobao affiliates was 0, but there was an average daily income of about 2.5 yuan from Ali Mama advertisements. Below, I will summarize my own experiences and techniques for promotion and earning money, sharing them with all Taobao affiliates:

1. Personally, I think that for Taobao affiliate promotion, channel promotion is enough. These two methods not only serve as promotion but can also beautify the website. Placing them increases content and enhances the website's aesthetics. However, one disadvantage is that currently many people already know these two promotion methods, so you can see identical things on many personal sites. But up until now, I have not yet earned any income from Taobao affiliate promotion.

2. Ad promotion is a fairly good method. Currently, netizens have high praise for GOOGLE's ad strategy, but GOOGLE requires an application, and their website review process is quite strict. Personally, I think Ali Mama is also good since they share the same parent company as Taobao affiliates, ensuring credibility, and you can see your daily income.

3. Another way to earn money is through alliances. However, after summarizing the experiences of many netizens, I find that the credibility of alliances is too low. Most are just white promotions, or when you promote and finally make money, they say your website doesn't meet the requirements. Therefore, I do not recommend this method.

4. The website needs to be attractive. To freely share practical techniques and methods of being a Taobao affiliate, it needs good content. Additionally, I think a crucial phrase from sales applies here: selling without selling. If you want to place ads, it's best if they blend seamlessly with the website, making visitors unaware that they're ads. This way, you will get clicks and generate income.

All of the above techniques were tested and explored bit by bit. Success always comes after countless failures and sighs. So, we should approach this unique and lucrative industry of online earnings with a positive mindset.
