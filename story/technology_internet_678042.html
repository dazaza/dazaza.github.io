According to the "China Papermaking Industry Annual Report 2009" released by the China Paper Association, the annual average growth rate of China's corrugated cardboard box industry has reached 18%-20% in recent years. Zhang Yaoquan, an authoritative expert in China's packaging industry, vice president of the China Packaging Federation, and president of the Zhejiang Provincial Packaging Technology Association, said that in 2010, the total output value of the national packaging industry reached more than 1.2 trillion yuan, among which the paper packaging output value was over 400 billion yuan. There are more than 4,500 corrugated paper production lines nationwide, with an annual output of over 40 billion square meters, making it the world's largest producer.

At the same time as production and consumption rapidly increase, the corrugated part of the corrugated cardboard box is becoming increasingly [fine]. [Traditional corrugated types include A, B, C, D, mainly used for paper boxes with higher strength requirements. In recent years, E, F, G type corrugated have appeared on the market. The micro-fine corrugated cardboard has attracted increasing attention from insiders.] Zhang Yaoquan said, [Micro-fine corrugated cardboard boxes save more materials, have stronger cushioning power, lighter weight, better protect goods, and also reduce packaging costs and logistics costs, now starting to replace white cardboard, traditional fiber thick paperboards, and other materials.]

Fanke Chengpin, which started selling men's shirts, uses E-type corrugated cardboard boxes to ensure that clothes are flat when delivered to customers. Zhang Yaoquan introduced that in the packaging market for small products such as mobile phones, cosmetics, medical equipment, small appliances, and even food, the application of micro-fine corrugated cardboard boxes is increasing.
