The response from the Beijing Haier Washing Machine Repair Center is as follows:

Single Cylinder: This refers to a washing machine that only has one cylinder where washing, rinsing, and spinning are all completed in one cylinder.

Article source: Beijing Haier Washing Machine Repair Center http://blog.tianya.cn/blogger/blog_main.asp?BlogID=3427273.

Three-in-one: Refers to a washing machine that integrates washing, spin-drying, and drying functions into one unit.

Frequency Conversion: Refers to washing machines that use frequency conversion motors. During operation, the computer control system automatically senses the balance of the clothes to determine the optimal motor speed, thereby reducing vibration and noise of the washing machine.

Health Disinfection: Refers to washing machines with ionization, ozone or other disinfection functions.

Dual Power: Refers to washing machines with heating wash function that have two heating elements. Users can choose single or dual element heating for washing.

Fuzzy Control: Refers to washing machines that can judge the texture, weight, and degree of dirtiness of clothes through sensors, automatically determining the water level, detergent dosage, and optimal washing program.

Ultra-thin Type: Refers to washing machines that are 5~10cm thinner than regular washing machines but have larger capacity and occupy less space.

Double Cylinder: Refers to washing machines that have two cylinders, namely the washing drum and the spin-dry drum. After washing, clothes need to be manually transferred from the washing drum to the spin-dry drum.

Spray Waterfall: Refers to washing machines that can produce a circulating three-dimensional water flow that impacts clothes like a waterfall, increasing tumbling, reducing tangling, and improving cleaning efficiency (Beijing Bosch Washing Machine Repair Center).

Intelligent Type: Refers to washing machines that can continuously adjust the water intake based on the weight and fiber category of the clothes. This ensures the optimal water volume for complete dissolution of detergent and achieves the best concentration of washing liquid, allowing for thorough soaking of clothes.

What are the common terms used in washing machines and what do they mean?

There are several common terms used in washing machines:

Heated Wash: Refers to washing machines equipped with electric heating devices, commonly found in fully automatic washing machines.

Cashmere Wash: Refers to gentle washing, a feature available in all drum-type washing machines.
