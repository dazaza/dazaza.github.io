The Trend of Soft Decoration Design Spreads: A New Lifestyle for Designers - Technical Information - Design Group Network

by anonymous on 2011-06-28 09:04:11

Home decoration and soft furnishing design has reshaped the development of designers in the direction of hard interior construction, pioneering a completely new path that is more fashionable, more culturally focused, and better able to understand the lifestyle inspired by design—a new way of life for designers!

To varying degrees, home decoration has changed the development status of contemporary designers. It has shifted their lifestyle from one involving steel, concrete, construction workers, and traveling between construction sites and office spaces, towards a task-oriented and developmental state filled with fashion and creativity.

The job characteristics of the home decoration and soft furnishing design industry have attracted an increasing number of people from the hard construction and other sectors. They are beginning to broaden their horizons through professional learning, integrate industry resources, and continuously narrow the gap with the domestic home soft furnishing industry. Whether it's opening a design company, a home accessory store, or a design studio, any lifestyle choice can appropriately facilitate the designers' transformation into a new way of life.

The home decoration and soft furnishing design industry incorporates more soft elements: instantly changing international trend information, information speed on par with fashion releases, and an industry feature that offers a quality life beyond fashion. These include detailed craftsmanship of various styles in home soft furnishing fabrics such as lace, linen, tassels, prints, jacquard, wool, handwoven textiles, etc. The intricate details attract the full creativity of designers. Furniture products integrate many historical imprints in their designs: aged appearances, lacquer techniques, oak and cedar materials, carved metal designs, regional historical style traces, rich cultural connotations, all bringing the most abundant creativity and lively design temperament to home decoration and soft furnishing design!

Accessories, lighting, decorative items, wallpapers...

Through home decoration, more and more people are finding the direction for their career development—a way to beautify life and fulfill the higher aspirations of others' homes and living environments. In design, they enhance people's sense of aesthetics, create cultural green architecture like the San Ying Art Village, and elevate the quality of life. Designers themselves undergo a revolutionary evolution, moving from hard structural designs to leading ideal lifestyles through soft design, ultimately achieving their own and others' dreams and constructing a new way of life for designers. Armeftis & Associates designed the Stavrides House in Cyprus!

People coming to learn about home decoration and soft furnishing design are first and foremost those who have higher demands for life. They may come from different cities, fields, and specialties, carrying different developmental aspirations—to become signed soft furnishing designers at design companies, home brand product display artists, independent operators of home brand stores (companies), independent design companies, or open their own design studios...

Despite differing needs and dream directions, through learning in the classroom of home decoration and soft furnishing design, they absorb information from broader fields, extending the scope of what they can handle, broadening their horizons, making their careers wider and their knowledge richer. This spiritual elevation will reshape their designer qualities, giving them clearer development plans, broader career thinking, and enabling them to achieve farther and broader levels than they imagined!
