Make the switch spring of the lighting lamp just touch the inner frame of the door when the door is closed. OH

by ogz59nt15 on 2011-08-30 14:07:53

Data source: Beijing Hisense refrigerator after-sales service telephone http://blog.tianya.cn/blogger/blog_main.asp?BlogID=3658737.

Analysis and maintenance: The refrigerator lighting lamp is realized by pressing the door switch spring frame of the box when closing the door, which turns on the light when opening the door and turns off the light when closing the door. If the lighting lamp fails, there are two situations: the light does not go out when the door is closed, and the light does not turn on when the door is opened.

(1) Light does not go out when the door is closed; this is often a fault caused by severe vibration during transportation of the refrigerator. The method to check if the bulb is still on after closing the door is very simple, just press the magnetic door seal lightly with your finger or a small screwdriver while closing the door, and peek through the gap to see inside the box. When repairing, open the refrigerator door and press the door switch spring with your hand. If the light goes out, it is due to the side frame not being able to push the spring after closing the door. You can use a screwdriver to loosen the fixing screw of the temperature controller, move the temperature controller forward slightly so that the switch spring of the lighting lamp is exactly in contact with the inner side frame of the door when closing the door, then tighten the fixing screw of the temperature controller. Alternatively, you can take a piece of cardboard about 2 square centimeters in size, stick it with adhesive tape at the corresponding position of the switch spring on the inner side frame of the door, to cut off the power supply when closing the door.

(2) Light does not turn on when the door is opened: If there is no power outage, poor contact of the power plug, or broken filament in the bulb, it may be due to poor contact between the bulb connector and the socket spring. During repair, you can gently pry up the spring in the socket with a small screwdriver. For lighting lamps in imported refrigerators, because the standard of the bulb screw mouth is different from that of domestic ones, it is difficult to buy replacement bulbs if damaged. Replacing the lamp holder would be troublesome and could cause other problems due to size differences. In this case, according to the difference between the threads of imported and domestic bulbs, an aluminum-made adapter can be machined, where the outer thread matches the thread of the imported bulb and the inner thread matches the thread of the domestic bulb.
