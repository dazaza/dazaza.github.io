The Smiles in Memories

Spring departs and autumn comes, flowers wither and bloom again. Memories are buried deep in the sea of heart...

In one's life, there are so many people and things to encounter. But the story between you and you all is the hardest for me to let go... Who is the transient in whose life? Who is the reincarnation in whose life? The dust from the previous life, the wind from this life, and an endless sorrowful soul!

Who will it be? The only one in your past; and who will be the only one in your future? After tasting the unripe fruit, you realize its bitterness. She knows that she hasn't forgotten and can't let go. Otherwise, she wouldn't have been silently paying attention to you and blessing you; otherwise, when you send her a message, her heartbeat wouldn't accelerate... She cannot deceive herself; you have always been in her heart. But she also knows that you are a bird while she is a fish. You two are doomed not to be together. Facing everything, she remains silent, keeping silent with her mouth... but how can her heart be silent? And you will never see what she looks like at her loneliest moments because she is the loneliest when you are not by her side. This loneliness accompanies her pain and tears flowing into her heart, and those tears, that pain, may accompany her for her entire life...

Xiaofei, a sentimental girl, hides a fragile heart under her strong appearance. She responds to every moment with a smile, masking her true feelings. No one can see through her strong exterior to understand how shattered her fragile heart has become, no one understands how much she has paid on her journey, no one knows why she makes selfless contributions, and even more, no one supports her as she struggles along... She doesn't remember what the sky looked like back then, what color the trees were, or whether a gentle breeze had ever brushed past her face. She only vaguely remembers that summer...

He, a sunny, handsome, sporty, yet steady big boy, suddenly entered Xiaofei's life, stirring up emotions in her still immature world of feelings, quietly taking root and beginning to spread... Gradually, Xiaofei felt as if she had already known him well, and could clearly imagine his outline with her eyes closed. The sudden arrival of emotions was beyond anyone's control, and Xiaofei could do nothing about it. All she could do was allow her gaze and thoughts to quietly revolve around him every day, gradually forming a habit! Without any defense, Xiaofei realized that she had fallen in love with this irresistible boy who had once helped her - Xiaomo!

Buddha said: Everything is an illusion! I think, perhaps all those repressed sadnesses and pains, those flying happinesses and youths, all the stories are just a beautiful dream. Those sorrows quietly diffuse in the air, feeling the coolness of tears sliding down with a burning face, in that naive season, he changed Xiaofei's originally peaceful life...

The dust from the previous life, the wind from this life, you come like a gust of wind and leave like a gust of wind. The waiting her has her hair blown away, her skin blown old, her body blown dissolved, leaving only a posture of waiting, forever waiting... still cherishing that mood at that time: crying for you, laughing for you, being happy for you, fantasizing for you, making unremittingly sincere sacrifices for you! Let the youthful dream be like a flower that never wilts, staying in her memory! After all, he is the boy who made her laugh the most sincerely in the few years she has lived through. Despite all the harm he caused to the girl, she still thanks him very much, because he eventually gave her so many beautiful memories, letting her understand the growth process of a girl!

Only as we grow up gradually do we find out that some fates are just wrong encounters, some people are destined to part, and some roads still have to be walked alone... Some feelings are not love...
