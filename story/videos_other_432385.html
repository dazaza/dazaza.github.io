Changhong W3 smartphone makes an appearance at ccmobi

by romantic0689 on 2011-05-17 14:54:27

Mobile Phone World (www.ccmobi.com) Guide:

Changhong Group has always maintained a rigorous and responsible attitude in the research and development of mobile phone products. The phones produced under its brand have consistently performed well and have enjoyed a good reputation among domestic brands. After comparing some domestic mobile phone products on the market, I found that most of them focus on price but neglect the quality and user experience of the product; or when they improve the quality and functionality of the product, they often significantly increase the selling price. As a domestic mobile phone, whether there is a perfect balance point for the product is something we consumers are very concerned about and value. Moreover, as a "perfectly frugalist" like me, I want to find a product that is cheap, feature-rich and powerful, with a beautiful appearance that can be shown off. Recently, I learned that Changhong has released a new model smartphone - the W3 Fengshang, which has attracted the attention and affection of many consumers.

According to my understanding, this Changhong W3 achieved excellent sales results upon its release. The W3 Fengshang is an Android smartphone. Not only is it reasonably priced, but it also performs comprehensively in terms of functionality and application. Its appearance design is also very appealing, fully embodying the concept of being at the forefront of fashion trends as implied by the term "Fengshang".

The Changhong W3 adopts a handsome straight-board shape, complemented by a stylish mix of dazzling white and black tones, making its unconventional appearance design very attractive and endearing. Moreover, this W3 inherits the noble style of another flagship model, the Z1, from Changhong. The overall craftsmanship and design materials are very meticulous, and it is undoubtedly a representative model among mid-range products. The front of the W3 Fengshang body is designed in black, while the back is white, making the overall combination natural and aesthetically pleasing. At the same time, the transition part of the frame uses dual-color injection molding technology, emitting a crystal-clear texture from within. A 3.2-inch capacitive touch screen and a 5-megapixel camera configuration provide good support for the application experience.

Since it is an Android device, W3 naturally handles functions and applications with ease. W3 uses the Android 2.1 system, and based on this, it has undergone independent innovation and UI beautification. Not only does it add some plug-ins unique to Changhong, but it also adds mobile AP functionality that the native Android 2.1 lacks, surprising users. W3 also supports customizable Widget desktops, allowing dynamic wallpaper display during standby mode, as well as free switching between seven different split-screen interfaces. In other aspects, W3 also supports WLAN wireless networks, GPS, mini maps, video playback, mobile assistants, SNS social networks, Baidu on the go, Changhong's own software store, and other rich features, covering all bases.

One of the most attractive points of W3 is that it is also a "cloud smart" phone, which is still rare among mid-range products. The advantages of cloud computing and cloud intelligence are self-evident. The resources in the "cloud" appear to users as infinitely expandable, and can be obtained at any time, used on demand, and expanded at any time. Currently, W3's cloud services are mainly reflected in applications such as Hong News, Hong Email, network time synchronization, and weather updates. Information acquisition and sharing are quite convenient and fast. Moreover, Changhong has begun to gradually improve its cloud service system, so in the future, we will be able to experience more cloud applications and cloud support using W3.

As an Android phone targeting mid-range users, the current price of the Changhong W3 Fengshang is 2399 yuan. It provides abundant and practical functions, fully optimizes and improves the Android 2.1 system, and offers cloud intelligence support. It can be said to be a highly cost-effective model and a rare good product in the current market.
