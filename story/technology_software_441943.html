Introduction to the Knowledge Review Newspaper:

Our newspaper was first published on February 27, 1993, originally named Military Review Newspaper; it was officially renamed Knowledge Review Newspaper in October 2005. The newspaper has always been committed to "creating a newspaper with taste, thought-provoking, and engaging", focusing mainly on military and political content. It features sections such as hot topics, focus, military, society, global affairs, historical reviews, and lifestyle. Published every Monday, the newspaper is a four-page, 16-edition issue that integrates news, knowledge, and readability, with each edition being outstanding.

Currently, the national circulation of the Knowledge Review Newspaper has reached 480,000 copies per issue. It has printing points in Beijing, Changsha, Chengdu, Nanning, Lanzhou, Zhengzhou, Nanjing, and other places. Its sales network covers more than 40,000 newspaper kiosks across all provinces, municipalities, and autonomous regions in China. In 2008, it was honored as one of the four key recommended newspapers by the postal service, with an actual circulation of 150,000 copies, making it the most circulated and best-selling weekly newspaper in Hunan Province.
