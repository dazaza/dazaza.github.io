They successfully established the world's largest immortalized cell library for schizophrenia and the DNA library for Chinese people with schizophrenia in cooperation with the State Key Laboratory of Medical Genetics. Functional studies of schizophrenia, manic-depressive gene mapping, cloning, and disease genes have filled the blank in psychiatric genetic research for the Chinese population.
