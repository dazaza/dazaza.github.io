Brushing credit Introduction www.yingkeduo.com, which in the Taobao shopping website, buyers and sellers take the goal of increasing credit, or both sides make "satisfactory" and good reviews without actual transactions. Process The detailed process of brushing is basically as follows: First, the buyer reaches a purchase agreement with you through QQ chat, then the buyer takes a screenshot of the item you request for transaction, the buyer pays the goods money, you send the goods on Taobao or Paipai, the buyer receives the goods and gives a positive review, you reply to the review, and the transaction ends. The Taobao brushing platform runs synchronously with the above steps. Methods Currently, there are several methods of brushing popular on the Internet: Proxy brushing of Taobao credit. Proxy brushing of Taobao credit refers to entrusting the store to the proxy brush party, then it does not need to be managed, the proxy brush party will brush to the required level, and pay after brushing. The cost required is Alipay guarantee transaction. The characteristics of this method are time-saving, labor-saving, and safe, no need for the store owner to operate, avoiding risks caused by insufficient experience. Free group brushing A group of people in a group brush each other's credit. The problem is low efficiency, because there are not many people, the authenticity is not very strong, and the repetition rate of IP is not low. It is easy to be sealed, there are more scammers, and rights protection fraud often occurs! It is recommended to choose some super groups of 500 people. The best is a chargeable group that can compensate. If it is a three-person mutual brushing mode, it will basically not reduce the weight. Risk level three to four stars! Platform brushing credit is an upgraded version of Taobao credit brushing platform. It is free, has many people, high efficiency, safety, and relatively strong authenticity. There are many credit brushing platforms in the current market. Some large platforms usually recruit professional brushes to take tasks. These people use small accounts to take hundreds of tasks a day. If the small account is sealed, your Taobao will also be affected. Therefore, it is necessary to find a safe platform. Risk level one star. Brushing preparation multiple small accounts Mutual brushing So-called small accounts are unverified Taobao accounts. Each account registered on Taobao can only open a store after verification, and the verified one is your main account. In order to ensure the safety and interests of everyone, please follow the rule that the main account sells and the small account buys. When members receive tasks, please use the small account to take pictures of the products and make payments. The most direct way for buyers to identify credit brushing 1. Look at the "credit evaluation" after "virtual commodity transaction". If this item is about 30%, then 90% of the credit is brushed. If it is selling recharge cards and game coins, it can be ruled out as brushed. If the seller sells physical goods and this item is about 30%, it must be brushed credit. 2. For Taobao credit brushing, look at the transaction evaluation. Generally, the amount behind the credit brushed is 0.01 yuan, and the product name is QQ number, QQ coin and other information. These do not need to be guessed, they must be brushed credit. 3. Some careful buyers can see that if the product is taken at the same time, the evaluation record behind is evaluated a few minutes later, this must be brushed credit. Among the 80% of defrauded buyers, they were deceived when buying goods from stores that brush credit. Some are serious mismatches of goods, some just received an empty box, and the buyers saw the diamond credit or crown credit of the seller, so they lowered their guard and got defrauded. Here I remind all Taobao friends, sellers and buyers, credit is built on the trust of both parties, not by brushing credit. At the same time, buyers should not choose shopping stores based solely on credit ratings. Some newly opened stores may have little credit, but the quality and price of their products are better than those crown stores. Let's work together to completely eliminate "credit brushing". Status Analysis If netizens buy goods on Taobao and give a positive review of the goods, they contribute a credit to the seller. Most users on Taobao shopping will take how much credit the seller has as an important indicator of whether to buy or not. For sellers, not brushing credit, they might not get an order in a month. This is a paradox of which came first, the chicken or the egg. Because Taobao is already huge, newly registered store owners will find that all the goods are already being sold by others, and their newly registered stores have no competitive advantage. When users search for similar goods, sellers with higher credit values rank higher, while newly registered store owners, due to their short stay, have not accumulated enough credit, and their goods often rank after one or two hundred pages, and no buyers have the patience to flip over one hundred pages. Credit brushing (a colloquial term for credit brushing) is the only means to solve this paradox. First, create false transactions to brush the online store into three diamonds, four diamonds, or even a shop with a crown, and then really start doing business. At this point, the store ranks higher and is easier to attract customers. The phenomenon of credit brushing is deeply entrenched.
