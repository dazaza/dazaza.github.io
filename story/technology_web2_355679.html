If it's love! Then what else could be the reason?

by dufuren93 on 2010-05-24 22:36:36

Since it's love, what reasons does borrowing need? So-called reasons! Are they intended for the other party, or are you looking for them for yourself? Love is just love! There is no reason. Each love only has the people involved who understand each other most clearly. Love needs tolerance and understanding - more so, the ability to put oneself in the other's shoes and consider things from their perspective. If love needs a reason, there is no most accurate answer either.

It's easy to be calm, but hard to be deeply infatuated! It's hard to retain feelings, but easy to hurt others! Understand how to cherish, and learn how to treasure!

The simplest sign of loving someone is that all your emotions are moved by her. When she's happy, you're also happy; when she's sad, you're also sad; when you make her angry, you feel extremely guilty; when she ignores you, you become anxious and restless.

When you love someone, you can't help but look at her. When she leaves your line of sight, you will look around aimlessly.

When you love someone, you'll keep your phone within reach because you're afraid of missing her messages, or replying too late.

When you love someone, you'll often miss her. When you can't see her, you'll think: What is she doing? Is she happy? Is she lonely?

When you love someone, you won't constantly calculate what you've given. As long as you see her smile, you're willing to do anything.

When you love someone, you'll share everything with her, tell her your news, and share every bit of happiness you have.

When you love someone, you'll be a loyal listener, share her news, help her relieve worries and difficulties, and give her comfort.

When you love someone, you'll always try to stay by her side because you're afraid she might be lonely, because you like the feeling of being with her.

When you love someone, you'll remember every word she says, every thing she wants to do, not deliberately memorizing it, but indeed keeping it in your heart.

When you love someone, you'll believe every word she says, whether seriously or jokingly, whether happily or sorrowfully.

When you love someone, you won't always ask what you want, but rather what she wants.

When you love someone, you'll pay attention to everything about her, think of all her needs, bring water when it's cold for fear she might be thirsty, bring hot water bottles and continuously replace the warm water when it's cold, think of buying her a scarf when you see her wearing gloves, think of buying her a hat when she wears a scarf, want to carry her when she walks slowly, warn her not to fall when she runs fast, leave the best food for her when eating, and when shopping, you'll spend without hesitation on her (even though you're very frugal yourself).

When you love someone, just one sweet smile from her can make your day full of energy and confidence, making you feel incredibly happy.

When you love someone, just one caring word from her can make you get up smiling every day, full of hope for life.

When you love someone, just a little encouragement from her can give you the courage to face all difficulties, full of vitality and perseverance.

When you love someone, you'll want to cook meals for her. Even if you're apart for two days, you'll think of her at meal times, wondering if she's hungry.

When you love someone, you'll value her health more than your own. When she's sick, you'll be anxious and restless, prepare various medications for her, search for various precautions, instruct her on what to eat and what not to eat, and what to pay attention to.

When you love someone, you'll never say anything that makes her angry because you don't want to hurt her.

When you love someone, you'll treat her with great tolerance, even if she says something that hurts you, you'll still smile at her...
