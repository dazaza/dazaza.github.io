On the Leader of the Wulin - The Later Development Trend of Shaolin

by lk0gameh on 2009-07-18 21:34:03

The term "Master of Wulin" refers to the idea that "all martial arts under heaven originate from Shaolin", and nothing else!

The test server has been played by everyone, and everyone has their own interpretation and opinions after experiencing it. The final right of interpretation belongs to Kingsoft, not you.

Shaolin - less staff, less knife

In the future, the trend will still be that the staff is stronger than the knife. This applies to sects, Song-Jin battles, and group fights alike!

Don't rush to criticize before finishing reading.

1: On the test server, the 90-level skill of the knife disciple indeed involves a straight line with two linear attacks. Its characteristics are speed, which is the only commendable aspect. However, its weakness remains the same - linear attack. This means a straight line, and it ignores those who turn corners! The manifestation of the skill depends on the strength of the weapon. On the test server, the 90-level BOSS's knife has an injury effect, incapacitating the opponent with one hit and preventing retaliation, thus facilitating subsequent attacks!

To put it simply, the rise of the knife disciple can only be discussed when the skills are changed to include tracking!

(The knife disciple's skill tracking from the closed beta won't be reverted. Although Kingsoft makes everything possible, the collective power of the knife disciple is still weak.)

2: On the test server, the 90-level skill of the staff disciple is neither strong nor weak, but mediocre. Official server positioning: the staff disciple is an auxiliary player in group battles, capable of knocking down lone opponents (which the knife disciple cannot do). The advantages of the staff disciple need not be elaborated, but its disadvantages include lack of running speed and average attack power. Most cases involve stunning without killing!

The official server hasn't truly experienced this yet. After level 99, in territorial battles and guild occupations, the staff disciple plays a significant role, but I won't go into detail about that.

Here is the key point: putting aside the technically uncomplicated Song-Jin battles, the main focus now is on group battles and sect competitions.

Regardless of the sect, players all want to enter the top 16 or top 8, or achieve even higher rankings. It's up to you.

Sect: I can responsibly say that if the ratio of staff disciples to knife disciples in your server is inversely proportional (more staff), and if the general equipment of the staff disciples is better, with some even having +12 gear sets, then considering numbers alone, the knife disciples have no chance of turning the situation around. A group of staff disciples collectively stunning you means that no matter how fast you cast spells or how far you ride, you'll still have to stand there and take it!

If in your server, after level 99 opens, most choose to reset as knife disciples, unless there is strong collective awareness and many friends cooperate to concentrate firepower and bombard the staff disciples, only a few knife disciples might luckily advance. The official server's sect competition isn't very regular either. Some kill first, some brush with acquaintances, and some stand still at the end. There are too many factors involved, varying from person to person and server to server!
