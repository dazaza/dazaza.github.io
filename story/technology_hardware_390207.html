Many friends who want to open a store often think about this question: what should I sell?

Here, I can't give a definite answer, but I will tell you what I think.

When a new store opens, it faces a severe problem: no one cares. Why does this happen? The reason is simple. After all, it's an online store, not as real as reality. You don't have credibility, so how would anyone care! If you were shopping online, would you choose a store with low credibility or high credibility? The answer is self-evident!

Therefore, a process that a new store must go through when opening is: improving credibility. Of course, doing the recharge business is a way to improve credibility.

Here I want to declare: many people mistakenly think that improving credibility means more transactions and higher evaluations from others. I don't deny that this can be used as a standard for evaluating the credibility of a store, but there is another important factor: authenticity.

As a new store, first of all, your influence and publicity cannot match that of established stores. Therefore, we can take a shortcut, which is to make customers feel the authenticity. When we search on Baidu, the things we see are enough to dazzle us, but how many make us feel real? I think very few.

Therefore, our advertisements must be more realistic. I often see ads like "If you find me to teach you, I guarantee you'll earn...". I don't know if the person themselves believes in such guarantees; I remain skeptical. A friend who wanted to open a store came to me and asked: what if I lose money buying software from you? I said: if you lose, then you lose. I can only teach you what you should learn, I can only teach you my experience in making money, I can't guarantee that you'll make money. Then she said something unexpected: good, if I do the recharge store, I'll find you! I'll go get my online banking done tomorrow. This might be the effect of making customers feel real.

In fact, many people want to open an online store, and many people want to know which online store makes the most money. In fact, everyone should know that there's no free lunch in this world. Opening an online store is the same as opening a physical store; some people make money, and some don't! The key lies in individual actions: good execution + the right method + persistent persistence equals making money.

At the beginning, I also hesitated about what to do. I looked at many projects and even planned to purchase goods on Alibaba's distribution platform. But after doing it for a while, I found that I couldn't continue. Why? First, I didn't know Taobao's operation process. Second, I didn't know how to promote. Third, the goods source wasn't in my hands; relying solely on my friends to buy was unrealistic. Considering these factors, I started moving towards the recharge industry.

Now I've been doing the virtual recharge business all along. To be honest, I treat this as my career! The money from selling software and recharges is quite substantial. Of course, the most important thing is that Guangzhou VIP Information Technology Co., Ltd. has made many good friends. Life is just like this, isn't it? For new sellers, doing virtual products is the most suitable option when starting without capital and goods sources.

Virtual items refer to selling mobile phone fees, point cards, QQ value-added services, Q points/Q coins, game cards, etc. All they need is a computer and a software program, no need to stock up on goods. The advantage of virtual items is that they require less cost, reducing investment risks. Fast capital turnover, no inventory, no logistics, most are automatically shipped, making it an ideal choice for friends who don't have enough time to sit by their computers. More importantly, selling virtual items is the best way to quickly enhance your store's credibility and popularity. For online stores, the importance of credibility doesn't need further explanation... New sellers always worry about losing money when starting a store, but think about it from another angle: where there's no investment, there's no return?! Without trying, how do you know you'll fail?! There's no such thing as getting something for nothing; all results are proportional to your efforts, so friends who want to open a store should prepare for a long-term struggle...
