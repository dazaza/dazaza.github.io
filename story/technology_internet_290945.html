Today's investment: Zoomlion's main business benefits from economic recovery and grows steadily_stock basics

by dx0966632 on 2009-12-03 22:59:44

Zoomlion (000157) (enter the stock bar, new version of market conditions) mainly engages in the development, production, sales and leasing of concrete machinery equipment, lifting equipment, road machinery, horizontal directional drilling equipment and other products and accessories. Among them, manufacturing revenue accounts for 100% of the main business revenue. The company is a high-tech listed company, a leading enterprise in China's construction machinery equipment manufacturing industry, and one of the largest research and manufacturing bases for major infrastructure equipment in the country. The company's comprehensive earnings per share forecast values from 2009 to 2011 are 1.28 yuan, 1.59 yuan, and 1.95 yuan respectively, corresponding to dynamic price-earnings ratios of 21, 17, and 14 times; currently, there are 25 analysts tracking the company, with 13 analysts recommending "strong buy", 10 analysts recommending "buy", and 2 analysts suggesting "hold". The comprehensive rating coefficient is 1.56.

Unlike other domestic construction machinery companies, the company has achieved high growth through mergers and acquisitions. This growth model may seem risky, but it is actually steady growth. Three factors support the company's successful acquisitions: (1) a humanized acquisition approach; (2) an incentive-based business unit system; (3) the industry being in a growth phase. As the market increasingly recognizes the company's acquisitions, Guotai Junan expects the company's valuation level to rank among the top tier domestically, matching its strength.

After realizing the acquisition of CIFA, the company has become the world's largest concrete equipment enterprise. The integration has initially shown results, with CIFA expected to turn a profit within the year. The concrete business is developing towards "cost localization in China and high-end technology branding." From a domestic business perspective, under the impetus of real estate, urbanization in central and western regions of China has significantly accelerated. Based on the growth space of real estate and ready-mixed concrete, Guotai Junan quantitatively calculates that the CAGR of concrete machinery will reach 23.4% over the next three years.

Since its acquisition in 2003, PuYuan crane has been in a rapid growth phase and now accounts for nearly half of the company's revenue. Under the impetus of the 4 trillion yuan investment, cranes have become one of the first construction machinery products to recover. From January to September this year, the company's sales volume increased by 14% year-on-year. Based on optimistic expectations for investments in large-scale infrastructure projects, petrochemicals, wind power, and nuclear power, Guotai Junan estimates that the CAGR of the crane business will be 28.9% over the next three years.

After acquiring Xin Huanggong, leveraging its technical platform, combined with Zoomlion's management and capital advantages, the excavator launched into the market this year has received good feedback. Next April, the production capacity will increase to 4,000 units, and its market performance is worth looking forward to.

Tianxiang Investment predicts that the company's EPS for 2009 and 2010 will be 1.42 yuan and 1.95 yuan respectively, taking into account the dilution impact of issuing an additional 300 million shares in the future, the EPS for 2009-2010 will be 1.20 yuan and 1.66 yuan respectively. With the closing price on October 26 at 26.61 yuan, the corresponding price-earnings ratios based on diluted EPS are 22 times and 16 times respectively. It maintains an "increase" investment rating.

The procedure for buying and selling stocks is as follows: When buying or selling stocks, you must specify whether you are buying or selling, the name (or code) of the stock, the quantity, and the price. And this order is only valid for the day it is placed. The content of the order includes the abbreviation (code) of the stock you want to buy or sell, the quantity, and the price of the stock. The abbreviation of the stock is usually three to four Chinese characters, and the code of the stock is six digits. The code and abbreviation of the stock must be consistent when placing an order. At the same time, there are certain regulations regarding the quantity of stocks traded: that is, the quantity of stocks ordered for purchase must be a whole multiple of 100, but the quantity of stocks ordered for sale does not have to be a whole multiple of 100.

There are four ways to place an order: counter submission, telephone automatic order, computer automatic order, and remote terminal order.
