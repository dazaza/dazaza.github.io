Olympic Gold Medal Ranking vs. China's Best Roast Wings

by sam000 on 2008-08-22 20:05:37

With the continuous improvement of people's living standards, a new round of fierce competition has begun in the catering market. An increasing number of foreign food brands are entering the domestic market and local brands are emerging like mushrooms after spring rain. The food industry is gradually transforming from an individual operation model with roadside stalls that are rudimentary and unsanitary with no brand influence to a direction of branding, scaling, and chain operations. In the barbecue sector, a young and energetic brand has gradually entered the market and established itself, it is - "Golden Medal Grilled Wings".

The Olympic gold medal shines, and "Golden Medal Grilled Wings" is no exception. To further enhance the atmosphere of the Olympics, Golden Medal Grilled Wings launched a series of new products with Olympic features. As soon as they hit the market, they immediately caught everyone's attention.

Among the new products, the "National Ball Gold Medal Grilled Wings" is the most eye-catching "Signature Wing". This secret recipe integrates the腌brining techniques of both the south and north, and uses a specially formulated secret barbecue seasoning for meticulous grilling. The result is an unmatched golden flavor that is wonderfully fragrant, juicy and delicious, with a lingering aftertaste. Golden Medal Grilled Wings claims the championship in the wing world with this signature wing, dominating globally like table tennis, thus being revered by countless diners as the "National Ball Golden Wings".

In addition to the "National Ball Gold Medal Grilled Wings", Golden Medal Grilled Wings also introduced the "Diving Gold Wings" with a savory flavor. These wings must be braised for several hours so that the flavors of the spices penetrate deep into the bones, then slowly cooked over low heat to become a top-grade crispy-on-the-outside-and-tender-on-the-inside wing; the "Track and Field Gold Wings" which have nourishing effects is a supreme tonic. It is carefully marinated with more than a dozen Chinese herbs such as yuzhu, radix scrophulariae, radix ophiopogonis, lily bulb, radix asparagi, and wolfberry that benefit qi and kidney health. Besides enjoying the delicious taste, customers can also gain nourishing and health benefits; the lemon-flavored "Gymnastics Gold Wings" uses premium tender wings mixed with lemon, grapefruit, apple juices, and others. After marinating until fully flavored, they are lightly grilled on the rack, retaining the tenderness of the chicken wings while infusing a refreshing fruity aroma, akin to gymnastics being graceful yet highly skilled.

To cater to the tastes of the young and dynamic crowd, Golden Medal Grilled Wings also launched the fashionable and healthy "Coke Trampoline Gold Wings". The "Trampoline Gold Wings" breaks through the barriers of the barbecue world, actively innovating. In addition to using ancient methods of preparation, it adds a modern element - cola, giving it a light and elegant taste, fresh and pleasant, adding a rare touch of the times to the century-old barbecue wings craftsmanship, much like trampolining being exhilarating.

Through launching the "Olympic Heroes List" of grilled wings, Golden Medal Grilled Wings has gained greater popularity among the masses. The grilled wings specialty stores are bustling with people from morning till night without interruption. A man dining with his family at the Golden Medal Grilled Wings store said: "Not just the kids, even we adults have a strong interest in the Olympics, but Olympic tickets aren't that easy to get, which has always been a regret for our entire family. But unexpectedly today, we found out here that even eating barbecue can immerse us in the culture of the Olympics, so this barbecue meal was worth it!"
