On October 10, Wan Chunsheng, the Director of the Justice Bureau of Hengyang City in Hunan Province, and his Deputy Director Liao Yaozhong engaged in physical violence during a party committee meeting. On October 12, an online reconciliation statement signed by both parties appeared. Subsequently, Liao Yaozhong opened a Weibo account, stating that the reconciliation was not voluntary, but he was soon silenced.

The previous evening, his wife Hua Yaping opened a Tencent Weibo account to support her husband. She recounted the events, demanding severe punishment for the aggressor, and stated that the Discipline Inspection Commission said that a handling opinion would be issued soon, and one should trust the organization.

Everyone doubts that it is a struggle for interests. We hope relevant departments will investigate this. We are not afraid of investigations; we are only afraid they won't investigate. Our lives are not affluent. He has been a deputy department-level official for nine years, and we still have to pay the mortgage on our house until we retire. I await the outcome of the handling process. (Click on the image to enter Liao Yaozhong's wife's Weibo.)

The wife narrates her husband's deep mental anguish.

On the evening of October 12, Hua Yaping, the wife of Deputy Director Liao Yaozhong, opened a real-name certified Tencent Weibo account under the name "Liao Yaozhong's wife." She posted four consecutive tweets to support her husband, stating that her remarks only represent her personal opinions and have nothing to do with her husband.

She said that her husband initially concealed the fact that he had been beaten and the extent of his injuries, and the event was not first disclosed online by her husband. It wasn’t until around 7 a.m. on the 11th when Wan Chunsheng's wife came over demanding a private reconciliation with her husband that she learned about what happened the day before.

She described the events as she understood them. At the time, Wan slammed the table, followed by Liao doing the same. Then, as Liao picked up his cup and got up to leave past Wan's back, Wan prepared to hit him, so Liao pushed him slightly, causing Wan to fall. Immediately afterward, people pulled Liao away, and then several of Wan's associates held Liao down while Wan beat him, possibly delivering more than ten punches. She mentioned that this incident caused her husband immense mental pain and disrupted her peaceful life.

She revealed the process of her husband signing the statement: at around 9 a.m. on the 12th, her husband was called to the Organization Department and was required not to give interviews or post anything online. If discovered, he would face dismissal. If the situation escalated, he would also be dismissed. She demanded severe punishment for Wan Chunsheng and other attackers, requiring them to publicly apologize, and expressed anger towards relevant departments.

Media Interview:

Deputy Director Reports Director's Corruption

In media interviews with Liao Yaozhong, Liao accused Wan Chunsheng of being extremely corrupt; whether to expose Wan, he has considered many times. He said: there are several people in the bureau reporting him.

He believed that it was impossible for him and Wan to continue working together. If the Organization Department did not transfer Wan Chunsheng, he decided to continue exposing the issue through petitions, as he was a doctoral student specializing in studying petition rights.

Liao believed that since he had criticized Wan for illegal and disciplinary violations, he has been viewed as a thorn in their side. This conflict has elements of retaliation. Regarding Wan's corruption issues, Liao claimed to have evidence. He said that Wan took advantage of the bureau buying air conditioners to buy two units for himself, which he placed at home; he had two official cars, one of which was an SUV; he introduced relatives into the bureau; and accepted bribes for promoting officials.

What started as a brawl between the director and deputy director turned into a real-name report by the deputy director against the director. The reporter from Tianfu Morning Post interviewed Director Xie from the Propaganda Room of the Hengyang Municipal Discipline Inspection Commission, who stated that the matter has entered the investigation stage. The other party involved, Wan Chunsheng, currently has no public response.
