Deep Processing Technology of Northeastern China Monkey Head Mushroom:

I. Ingredients

Fresh Northeastern China monkey head mushroom 100-125g, salt 12g, wheat flour 10g, vinegar 5g, pepper powder 1g, monosodium glutamate (MSG) 5g, sugar 20g, green onion 5g, vegetable oil 25g, fresh ginger 2g, soy sauce 30g, organic cornmeal price, star anise powder 0.3g, rice wine 15g, water 240g, cornstarch 12g.

II. Preparation of Northeastern China Monkey Head Mushroom

1. Material Selection: Choose whole Northeastern China monkey head mushrooms of uniform size, remove impurities, soak in alkaline water for 2 hours, then rinse twice with clear water.

2. Coloring and Washing: Soak in a 0.03% sodium metabisulfite solution for 1-2 minutes, then wash with clear water, squeeze out the water, and then place in a freshly prepared 0.03% sodium metabisulfite solution to bleach and set color, then wash repeatedly with tap water.

3. Blanching: Soak in a 1% sodium chloride solution for 15 minutes, drain, then boil until the mushrooms are thoroughly cooked, and then drain again.

4. Making Soup: ① Weigh cornstarch, wheat flour, salt, and sugar according to the above formula, add 40ml of water, mix evenly, stir into a paste, then while stirring continuously, pour about 200ml of boiling water to gelatinize the starch, making a thick soup. ② Add refined vegetable oil, soy sauce, pepper powder, and starch, stirring while adding, and finally add MSG. ③ Mix rice wine and vinegar, add ground ginger and green onion, filter twice, take 30ml of the filtrate, and add it to the soup ingredients.

5. Canning: Place the processed monkey head mushrooms weighing 100-125g into a pre-cleaned 500ml canning jar, then add 500ml of soup. Seal the lid, sterilize at 121°C for 17 minutes, pass quality inspection, label, and package for factory release.
