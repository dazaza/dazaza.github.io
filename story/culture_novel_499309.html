Chapter One Hundred Sixty-Two: Spiritual Treasure

by fanyete on 2011-10-01 13:55:02

When Lin Dong's figure darted from the forest behind them and finally landed steadily on Little Yan’s tiger back, Tao Lao and others merely glanced at him and did not inquire about what he had done behind. On that day when Lin Dong defeated Lin Chen, he showcased his strength, so even Lin Chen and his group didn't dare to provoke him easily.

"Tao Lao, those guys were too overbearing! How could they just walk away like that!" After Lin Dong returned to the group, Lin Chen obviously still harbored some anger due to the collision with those people earlier, and spoke up.

As members of the Lin Clan, they were always the ones bullying others. In situations like this, if it weren’t for Tao Lao insisting on leaving earlier, there would probably have been a fight on the spot.

"That group has some skills. If we were to engage them in combat, we might win, but we would also pay a certain price," Tao Lao shook his head and said, "Our top priority now is to reach the Ancient Tomb Estate. Once the clan's stronger warriors arrive, you can deal with them as you wish."

There was also a hint of anger in Tao Lao's voice, clearly showing dissatisfaction with the earlier group.

"At that time, we mustn't let them off easily!" Lin Chen gritted his teeth and said.

Beside him, Lin Ke'er blinked her beautiful eyes, a cold stream surging deep within her gaze. Then she suddenly turned her head, looking towards Lin Dong behind her, and smiled, saying, "Those guys won't end well, right?"

Upon hearing this, Lin Dong was startled, ambiguously replying, "Perhaps."

Lin Ke'er gave a meaningful smile and didn't ask any more questions.

The journey ahead wasn't lonely. Even though they were gradually delving deeper into the Tianyan Mountain Range, Lin Dong and his group still encountered several teams who had made their way here. These teams were not weak either. Although they were equally wary of other teams, their behavior was far less reckless compared to the first group. Lin Dong's team also appeared to possess formidable strength, so both sides coexisted peacefully without conflict.

Apart from these teams, Lin Dong and his companions rarely encountered the indigenous beastly demons, which were the fiercest in the Tianyan Mountain Range. It wasn't because the beastly demons didn't attack them, but rather Tao Lao and his group were well-prepared, bringing along some special medicinal powders. These powders had some suppressive effects on ordinary beastly demons. As for the stronger ones, they were often detected by Lin Ke'er from a distance beforehand, allowing the group to make slight detours. Thus, the journey was unexpectedly smooth.

Occasionally encountering beastly demons did not cause significant hindrance.

During the journey, Lin Dong discovered that Lin Ke'er was also a Symbol Master. Though he hadn't seen her display any spiritual attacks, based on Lin Dong's perception, this girl's strength was certainly not weak.

Most of the day was spent rushing on the road. When nightfall gradually enveloped the vast Tianyan Mountain Range, Lin Dong and his group finally came to a halt. The night was when beastly demons roamed freely, making it unsuitable to continue traveling.

As usual, they found a place to set up camp. After a brief conversation with Tao Lao and others, Lin Dong returned to his tent to sit in meditation.

Pale moonlight poured down from the sky, draping this mountain range in a faint silver gauze. Occasionally, low growls of beasts could be heard.

In the quiet encampment, one tent suddenly trembled slightly. A man and a beast transformed into two shadows, quietly slipping out and disappearing into the forest in a flash.

Once inside the forest, Lin Dong looked back at the tents on the open ground behind him, letting out a sigh of relief. He then patted Xiao Yan's head, who remained unusually quiet, refraining from roaring.
