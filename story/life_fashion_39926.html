Clinique Three-Step System

by toyess on 2007-12-20 14:54:50

The Clinique Three-Step System comes in three varieties: one for dry skin, one for combination skin, and the last for oily skin. The function involves three steps: cleansing, exfoliating the skin layer, and moisturizing. It is suitable for young skin up to around 35 years old. The specific products are as follows:

1. Cleansing Soaps

This product is divided into Gentle Cleansing Soap, Extra Strength Cleansing Soap, and Ultra-Gentle Cleansing Soap. These are mainly chosen based on different skin types. The gentle type is suitable for normal and combination skin, while the extra strength type is suitable for oily skin and those prone to acne. Please pay attention to your selection and do not choose incorrectly.

2. Toner

Clinique's toner comes in both new and old versions, each with Number 1, 2, 3, and 4 options, targeting dry sensitive, combination, oily, and extremely oily skin respectively. The biggest difference between the new and old versions of the toner lies in the presence or absence of alcohol.

The old version toner, all contain medicinal alcohol, which can provide further cleansing and anti-inflammatory effects. The old version's Number 2 toner is the most commonly used type, mainly targeting combination and slightly oily skin prone to small pimples. Due to its unique cleansing and anti-inflammatory properties, it is the first choice for many who are prone to acne, and once selected, they generally do not switch to other products. This Number 2 toner is the flagship product among Clinique's toners. When choosing, please pay attention and select Numbers 1, 2, 3, or 4 based on your skin type (Number 1 for dry, Number 3 for oily, Number 4 for extremely oily skin). The old version toner is mainly sold abroad.

3. Moisturizer ("Yellow Oil")

The moisturizer comes in two types: with oil and oil-free (Gel Version).

The moisturizer with oil is the first choice for autumn and winter, providing excellent hydration and temperature retention. Additionally, for those with relatively dry or combination skin, this can be used all year round.

The oil-free moisturizer (Gel Version) is a newly launched product, suitable for those with slightly oily skin. It is very refreshing and does not contain oil. It can be the first choice for oily-skinned individuals during the summer. Of course, for those with particularly oily skin, this can be used throughout the year.
