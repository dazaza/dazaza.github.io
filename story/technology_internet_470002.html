Ranking of the Best Wives Among the 12 Zodiac Signs

by tuoqing1188 on 2011-08-03 13:18:04

1. Libra:

Girls of Libra possess an elegant feminine charm and smooth social skills. The smart ones among them usually know how to hide their intelligence behind their feminine gentleness so as not to hurt men's self-esteem.

So, how to win the heart of a Libra girl? Of course, you must be a very logical person first! A man too emotional, too stubborn or too radical is not her ideal partner. Girls of Libra need "companions" most. Usually, finding a lifelong partner is their biggest wish in life.

Secondly, if you don't like talkative girls, she might not suit you. Because she has a natural talent for debate. She is good at talking and likes discussing with others. Most of them speak elegantly and behave transcendentally. If you can appreciate it, you will find listening to her is a kind of enjoyment. She often likes to analyze things from various angles and find out a fair and objective conclusion.

For food, clothing, shelter and transportation, she has noble and extraordinary taste. If you want her to accompany you to create a career, she will prioritize her husband. She will be a good helper, reliable and modest, giving you the best advice without stealing your limelight.

2. Taurus:

If you have decided to marry a Taurus girl, I salute you immediately - you are really wise. First, she will never be a moody pampered young lady; second, she will never be a spendthrift young madam; third, when you invite your boss or business friends home, she will definitely leave them with a great impression; fourth, when you encounter setbacks, she will give you strong support. She will be a loyal friend for life, but "absolute loyalty" is also her greatest requirement for you. Of course, she won't sentence you guilty of infidelity just because you talked more with another girl. She usually has a broad mind, but there is a certain limit, you'd better figure out what her limits are, otherwise stepping over the line might trigger a landmine. To marry such a good wife, you need to make some efforts. Practical Taurus girls pursue solid feelings. They want something visible, audible, smellable, eatable, and even touchable. When you are with her, don't let your mouth stink of garlic (if possible! brush your teeth first and keep your breath fresh); wear a set of clothes with coordinated colors (she likes materials with good texture); take her to a concert and have a dinner with authentic flavors. You should respect her and not criticize everything about her in front of others. You don't have to boast about your booming business in front of her, showing off or pretending to be rich is not a way to attract her. She would rather marry a substantial, idealistic small businessman than choose a nouveau riche who spends lavishly. She will always patiently accompany you, working hard for your future.

3. Cancer:

The gentle and considerate Cancer girl easily gives people the feeling of "wife"; if you are a man pursuing stability, choosing her, you will definitely not be disappointed. However, if you are someone who treats dating as a game, please do not bother her, because, first, it is too cruel for the sincere Cancer girl; second, if she really falls in love with you, saying "bye-bye" will not be that easy.

Her gentle consideration and wholehearted love will make you feel incredibly moved, but her emotional fluctuations may drive you to mental breakdown. She lacks security, is sensitive and easily hurt. She always worries that she is not good enough, and that you don't love her enough. You might unintentionally say something that makes her burst into tears on the spot. I think apart from loving and cherishing her, there is no other way to deal with her unpredictable mood swings.

After marriage, she will gently take care of your daily life. She always makes you feel that she is fragile and helpless, unable to live without you (this will make you feel quite accomplished). But, when you two really encounter problems and even you feel hopeless and helpless, she will be strong in a way that surprises you! You can depend on her like a child throwing himself into his mother's arms. She will carefully heal your wounds. Most Cancer girls also have a good sense of money management and understand the principle of living within one's means. Usually, they only overspend when they are in low spirits to balance their unstable emotions.

4. Leo:

Are you pursuing a Leo girl? Then presumably you are a very confident man. She is noble, cheerful, and dazzling, really attractive, but the royal pride makes many admirers hesitate to approach. In fact, under the proud appearance, she hides a pure and sentimental heart. Enjoying compliments is a common weakness of Leo girls, but don't think all sweet words work. She despises vulgar flattery, your rhetoric needs some creativity. She will like you to indulge her, pamper her. When she feels treated like a princess, she will be the cutest and most understanding girl.

But remember: she wants a prince who loves her, not a sycophant who only knows how to flatter. Her charming smile may attract the admiration of many opposite sexes, but her warm smile is just the courtesy of the royal family of Leo. However, you need to be careful, the Leo girl's jealousy is terrible!

Leo princesses usually lack financial awareness! Moreover, her taste will never be cheap. Spending money is a kind of enjoyment for her, poverty will make her depressed. But rest assured, she usually performs excellently at work. She is a born hostess! Finally, let me remind you one thing: a Leo wife may forgive a husband who occasionally plays around, but will absolutely not forgive a man who betrays her emotionally.

5. Virgo:

Want to have a perfect Virgo wife? First, please pat the dandruff off your shoulders, comb your hair neatly, trim your nails cleanly, change into a fresh, slightly fragrant outfit, then slowly approach her. Although Virgo women may not all be as beautiful as flowers, they are 100% clean and fresh, and she also requires the same from you. If you don't want to scare her away, it's best not to pursue her in an overly enthusiastic way, being reserved will give you more hope. Her nature is shy and restrained, always pursuing a refined feeling, striving for perfection in everything; you don't need to tell her about a bunch of unattainable dreams, she wants a practical life; her excellent analytical and critical ability can always find flaws in what you think is perfect, and she also believes that you should have the grace to accept criticism. However, she herself dislikes being criticized the most, making her admit her mistakes is difficult. You might think it's unfair, but please understand that her strict self-demand has already put a lot of pressure on herself. Naturally, she can't bear criticism from others, although she doesn't admit it verbally, she understands clearly in her heart. Marrying her, your home will be neat every day; your accounts will be clear, not a cent off; after work, you will have a hot meal waiting for you; all your troubles, she will be happy to share with you, she is a perfectionist, she will strive to be a good wife. As for her tendency to nitpick, her obsession with cleanliness, and her habit of taking two hours to shower, you should certainly tolerate these quirks!

6. Scorpio:

A Scorpio girl with mysterious charm is often seen by other women as a threat to love. She may not necessarily be very beautiful, but usually, her every smile and frown make men's hearts race. She has a power that stimulates the primitive desires deep inside the opposite sex. However, if you therefore have the thought of having a little romance and it doesn't matter, then you've made a big mistake! Her sharp eyes see right through your intentions. Even if you cunningly deceive her love, men who play with the feelings of a Scorpio girl will surely get punished. Scorpio girls mostly have a determined attitude of preferring death to dishonor.

When you argue, each of her words may be like a knife, cutting you to pieces. That's her instinct to protect herself and attack her enemies. After the war ends, she will wrap you up tightly like a soft blanket again.

As a wife, she will give her all to help you walk towards success. Her tenacity and bravery will make you feel as if you own a million brave soldiers. Her suspicious nature may cause you some distress, I advise you to be honest, why bother yourself unnecessarily?! You should be very clear and grateful that once she truly loves you and decides to stay with you forever, no matter how big the resistance and difficulties are, they will not make her retreat. "Until death do us part," she can absolutely do it.

7. Sagittarius:

Sagittarius girls are always full of curiosity about the world, constantly trying new and exciting things. She sincerely seeks a soulmate, often impulsively jumping into love. Her optimistic personality makes her not be defeated by the setbacks of love, quickly gathering the courage to try again. Therefore, everyone mistakenly thinks she plays with love. She is independent and pursues freedom. She doesn't like, nor does she need too much restriction. Her straightforwardness might be well-known near and far, please don't criticize her with a worldly eye, you should cherish her precious innocence.

As for her refusal of marriage! In fact, that is just her fear of losing freedom. If you really let her understand that you won't be like a dorm supervisor disciplining her, you just want to be her eternal friend, sharing the joy of life together. I believe she will happily marry you. She loves freedom and will demand economic independence. As for housework, honestly speaking, she is not particularly enthusiastic. However, when she realizes that this is her responsibility, she will do it both quickly and well. She is a smart and cute, somewhat careless, yet not suspicious or limiting your freedom wife.

8. Capricorn:

Her appearance is mostly conventional and quiet. Her inner self is always somewhat melancholic: anxious about herself, insecure about the environment, but conservative her, she won't easily reveal her feelings. If you fall in love with her, you need to empathetically understand her feelings.

Don't joke with her casually, she is not someone to be mocked, your jokes can easily hurt her most vulnerable inner place. Don't criticize her family, she might break up with you because of that. Don't be a lazy bum, otherwise she will definitely not choose you. Capricorn girls' love always arises under circumstances where everything is secure. Even if you don't have a good economic foundation, you must have a clearly bright prospect. Before she finds a husband who can ensure her future happiness, she will be a professional woman sticking to her post. Please don't misunderstand her as being realistic and cold because of this. When she meets the man who gives her confidence and security, she will become an extremely gentle and charming woman.

Marrying a Capricorn wife is actually also a kind of security for men. You will have a wonderful home. Besides diligently managing household chores, she will tirelessly accompany you in striving for your ideals. She knows how to use money wisely, unlike many wives who buy a house full of cheap goods, she always budgetedly brings brand-name products home. She is neither nagging nor emotional. Of course, her stubbornness is something you clearly know, don't try to change her established thoughts, it's like kicking a big rock with your foot.

9. Aquarius:

To an Aquarius woman, whether a man possesses wealth and an appealing status is not very important. What she needs is a man with wisdom and breadth of vision, who can accompany her in seeking the "path of humanity". Life to her is an endless exploration. Her broad-loving heart believes that people from all walks of life should be cared for and understood.

She, who advocates freedom, has many friends, please don't criticize her friends with a patriarchal attitude. In her eyes, only narrow-minded men talk bad about others behind their backs. She pursues friendship, yet paradoxically enjoys solitude. When she needs a bit of personal space, she chooses to be a lone female knight. She won't stick together all the time because she believes everyone needs their own space. Don't investigate here and there like a detective. She respects herself and will respect you too, so please don't disturb her.

In fact, Aquarius women are not girls who randomly send electric signals everywhere. Once she genuinely chooses you, she will be very faithful. Don't expect her to report her every move and all her thoughts to you word by word; she dislikes lying, but also dislikes explaining. She firmly believes: anyone has the right to "keep a little to themselves".

Aquarius women are the most suitable modern wives, they are neither feminists nor traditional women. They know how to preserve their selfhood in marriage and respect others.

10. Pisces:

A typical Pisces girl is simply the embodiment of a man's dream lover! Her every move is full of femininity, like the little mermaid walking out of a fairy tale. She always looks at you with gentle eyes, seriously listening to you talk, whether it's about ideals or boasting! She will never pour cold water on you. She won't ask you a bunch of worldly and realistic questions, or force you to earn more money.

She always says gentle and romantic words. Because of her trust, you are filled with confidence! Pisces girls never want to control men, they are willing to hide in your arms, protected by you. Letting you cherish them. Although she has so many advantages, most Pisces girls always feel that they are not strong enough, not ambitious enough. Therefore, they often feel inferior and depressed. She needs a lot of encouragement and comfort from you, your infidelity or affair will give her a great blow. Pisces girls have a strong self-exile mentality. When suffering setbacks, they easily self-abuse, self-harm, or even harm their own lives. Dreamy Pisces have a glass-like heart, since she has given it to you, you must carefully cherish it. As a wife, she is gentle and lovely, unless you are too lazy, she almost won't urge you to work hard in your career. She often maintains a romantic atmosphere in marriage, if not forced by reality, she would rather be a simple housewife. "Strong women" are rarely the goals of Pisces women. Lack of financial awareness may be a common shortcoming of most Pisces wives, it may take her a long time to learn about "household budgeting." But such a gentle and lovely wife, how could you bear not to spoil her?

11. Gemini:

Gemini girls are like kaleidoscopes, changing patterns every now and then, you can never figure out what they are like. Some say, a girl likes men of a certain type; not necessarily for Gemini girls, due to their dual or even multiple personalities, they may like men of various types, naturally increasing your rivals! It's hard to find another girl who can talk with you about astronomy, geography, and all sorts of things, go crazy with you in mountains and seas, and be tender and affectionate with you under the moonlight. She is indeed interesting and indeed changeable; what fascinates her today may turn into something she hates tomorrow. The only way to avoid getting hurt and get along well with her is to keep changing yourself. Diverse life and atmosphere will make her full of vitality, and keep her passionate about you. Don't stay away from her for too long, otherwise her speed of forgetting you will surely break your heart. After marriage, most Gemini girls will restrain their desire for change and focus on being a versatile wife. If you don't strictly require her to be a standard housewife, you will feel too happy. On the contrary, when you worry about earning too little money, she will come up with a bunch of ideas for extra income. Don't restrict her freedom too much! Of course, she won't excessively restrict you either. She may often enthusiastically tell you about her new plans! Sometimes, she might feel down, give her some comfort. Hold her hand, follow her changing rhythm, and you will find that life is like a kaleidoscope, colorful, stimulating, and interesting.

12. Aries:

Under the protection of Mars, Aries girls are usually positive and strong. Words like delicate and pitiful are unthinkable for her!

She is absolutely a heroist, she usually admires a man who impresses her, she wants to marry a husband she can be proud of. If you fall in love with an Aries girl, don't start a passionate pursuit right away. Men like lapdogs will make her both dislike and fear. It's best to let her understand your talents and charm first, arouse her curiosity, then interest, and when she truly shows affection for you, express your love sincerely; then the prospects will be very promising.

On the surface, they won't let others (especially men) take advantage of them. Because of this, they often suffer silently and face setbacks. In fact, their inner selves are mostly upright, kind-hearted, and fragile. As long as you genuinely care for her, give her a warm embrace when she is wronged, she will become your most loyal companion. After marriage, let her fully utilize her competitiveness and conquest desire at work, and she will have more chances to be a gentle lamb at home. Rest assured, she won't be clueless about housework. Although she is not keen on being a full-time housewife, her competitive nature will prevent her from becoming an incompetent wife.
