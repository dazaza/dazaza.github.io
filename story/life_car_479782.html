Analysis Report of China Automobile Accessories Industry in 2011

Analysis Report of China Automobile Accessories Industry

According to the official prediction of automobile, in 2011, the sales of the automobile products market which is mainly based on automobile beauty will reach RMB 113 billion. In 2012, it will double and reach RMB 50 billion. If the relevant supporting services are also taken into consideration, the market space will further expand. According to the official speculation, by 2012, the scale of the whole automobile aftermarket will reach RMB 880 billion, only second to Japan in Asia, ranking the second in Asia.

Through the calm review of the lively scene that many automobile manufacturers devote themselves to the automobile products industry, we find that although China's automobile products industry has developed beyond before, there are still objective problems that cannot be ignored.

The Analysis Report of China Automobile Products Industry considers:

1. The price of China automobile products market is very transparent and open, and this may be another defect existing in the current automobile products industry. You can easily find if you walk around the market: the same brand of car audio sells for more than RMB 300 in the wholesale market, but when it comes to the high-end stores, it sells for at least more than RMB 1000 or even thousands yuan. The same brand seat cushion only sells for dozens of yuan in the wholesale market, but it costs over RMB 100 in the high-end brand store; such a huge price difference confuses not only the consumers but also the professionals.

2. The threshold of the automobile products industry is relatively low while the profit margin is very large, so many investors join the production and sales army of the automobile products industry without standardized guidance. There is a certain scale in sales, but there are not many well-known brands, the popularity of automobile products is not very high, there are too much counterfeit products and they are prevailing in the market.

3. Most enterprises in the industry ignore the enterprise culture, resulting in the lack of soul in enterprises. Nowadays, the competition between enterprises is ultimately the competition of enterprise culture. Compared with the successful enterprise culture of the whole vehicle enterprises, automobile accessories enterprises lack strength in shaping the enterprise culture, leading to the lack of soul in enterprises.

4. The homogenization of automobile products is very serious. Without innovation, they only talk about profits. At present, there are not many products produced and sold in the market that reflect obvious personality. The design is simple and the production is not delicate enough. Monotonous products obviously can hardly meet the needs of consumers. With the sharp increase in the number of private cars, consumers' demand for automobile products shows a strong personalized trend, and they need more automobile ornaments that can reflect the personality of car owners.

5. The automobile beauty industry is not professional enough, and beauticians have become the bottleneck restricting the development of the industry. Take automobile beauty as an example. The automobile beauty technology has experienced the development of early technologies such as waxing, coating and glazing.

After several years of development, the pattern and development trend of the automobile products industry have changed significantly. "Intensive" service and "one-stop" sales chain operation system will become the mainstream of the domestic automobile products market. Chinese and foreign-funded enterprises are all around this point, taking Shanghai, Guangzhou, Beijing and other medium and large-sized cities as bases, accelerating the integration of resources and domestic layout. In fact, in addition to the above-mentioned factors, there will be new products launched in the markets such as automobile decoration and automobile anti-theft devices, but the whole industry will not be booming. Car navigation is more in cultivating consumers and waiting for more merchants to participate and jointly promote the industrial development with national support.

Business opportunities hidden in automobile products industry. According to the analysis of the profit situation of the world's top 11 automobile companies in the past 11 years, in a completely mature international automobile market, the sales profit of automobiles accounts for about 32% of the total profit of the automobile industry, the profit of parts supply accounts for about 31%, and 71% of the profit is generated in its service field.

With the continuous fulfillment of China's commitment to joining the World Trade Organization, China's automobile aftermarket has achieved remarkable development, presenting ten business opportunities.

Automobile maintenance

Parts and premium retail. Automobile insurance. Automobile financing. Automobile information.

The newly issued Article 33 of the "Automobile Industry Development Policy" clearly states: "The state encourages automobile, motorcycle, parts manufacturing enterprises and financial and service trade enterprises to learn from the mature automobile marketing methods, management experience and service trade concepts internationally, and actively develop automobile service trade".

The next two years are important years for the development of the automobile products industry, and the automobile products industry is transforming from the previous "dirty, messy, poor" to scaling, standardization and specialization. Opportunities and challenges coexist. Only by recognizing the situation can we seize opportunities in development and achieve greater success.
