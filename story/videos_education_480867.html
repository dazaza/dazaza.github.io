2011 Enterprise Theory and Business Management Professional Working Postgraduate Enrollment

by shuoshi on 2011-08-23 17:33:45

(Exempt from entrance exams, eligible candidates may apply for a Master of Economics degree)

To meet the needs of reform, opening up, and economic development for high-level specialized talents and to improve the professional quality of employees, with the approval of the Beijing Degree Committee Office, the School of Economics at Renmin University of China has decided to offer a postgraduate course advancement program in Western Economics in Beijing.

Eligibility:

1. Support the Constitution of the People's Republic of China, abide by laws and regulations, have good moral character, be an outstanding professional backbone, be in good health, and able to persist in on-the-job study.

2. Those who have obtained a junior college degree or above, aiming to improve their professional quality, can all enroll in the advancement program.

3. Candidates applying for a master’s degree must have obtained a bachelor’s degree and worked for more than three years with certain research achievements; if working less than three years, they can first participate in the postgraduate course advancement program and its examination, then apply for the degree course examination after reaching three years of work experience.

Enrollment Procedures:

1. Enrollment time: Enrollment begins from now.

2. Enrollment location: Renmin University of China

3. Enrollment formalities:

(1) Fill out the enrollment registration form for the graduate course advancement program.

(2) Submit one copy each of your highest academic qualification certificate, degree certificate, and ID card.

(3) Provide one one-inch photo.

Certificate Issuance:

Participants who complete the required study items of the advancement program and pass the exams will be issued a completion certificate reviewed by the Graduate School of Renmin University of China. Those who have obtained a bachelor’s degree for more than three years may apply for a master’s degree.

Tuition Fees:

The tuition fee for the advancement program is 22,000 yuan. Book fees, material fees, and certificate production costs are borne by the student. Tuition must be paid in full at once; no refunds will be given if a student cannot continue due to personal reasons after the official start of classes. For those applying for a master’s degree, relevant fees will be charged according to Renmin University of China's standards.

Steps to Apply for a Master's Degree:

1. After the People's University Degree Office reviews and agrees, obtain the "Exam Eligibility Card".

2. Take the exam after receiving the "Exam Eligibility Card" (including question bank exams and non-question bank exams) and national-level exams organized by the state (foreign language proficiency test, comprehensive economics subject level test).

3. Within one year after passing all course exams, submit a master’s thesis and apply for the thesis defense.

4. Conduct the thesis defense within six months after submitting the thesis.

5. After passing the thesis defense, upon review and approval by the university's degree evaluation committee, the "Economics" master's degree will be conferred.
