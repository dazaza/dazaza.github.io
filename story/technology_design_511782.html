Aquarium: There are 20 fish display tanks with different themes, breeding more than 600 kinds of rare fish and over ten thousand tails. In the Shenzhen Ocean World, the underwater theater breeds various marine fish. There are performances such as "Passionate Underwater Ballet", "Mysterious Mermaid", "Romantic Underwater Wedding", and "Deep-sea Diving" scheduled daily. The underwater tunnel is magnificent, filled with numerous fish, making people feel as if they were strolling under the sea. The spectacular "Ten Thousand Fish Feeding Performance" takes turns to be staged here every day. The "Seven-color Tank" and "Treasure Tank" breed some strange and interesting marine fish.

Shell Museum: There are five exhibition rooms in total, showcasing more than 600 kinds of rare marine shells, corals, ancient biological fossils, and precious dinosaur egg fossils.

Model Ship Museum: It is currently the largest scale, most complete variety, most quantity, and richest theme model ship museum in the country, also the only large-scale model ship exhibition hall in Shenzhen at present. It mainly divides into five major themes: "Ancient Navigators", "Sailing Ship Era", "World Famous Ships", "Sea Storms", and "Future Era". There are hundreds of exquisite pictures and ship models displayed, ranging from the most primitive dugout canoes and grass rafts to modern high-tech aircraft carriers and nuclear submarines.

1) Family fun tickets for two adults and one child (scatter guests).

7. Students with valid student IDs or middle/high school exam certificates are charged 33 yuan per person for groups of 10 or more.

Shenzhen Xiaomeisha

The Shenzhen Ocean World is less than a hundred meters away from the Xiaomeisha beach, just across the road from the Xiaomeisha Grand Hotel. With the opening of the second channel of Shenzhen-Yanbu Expressway (free and fast from Luosha Overpass to Dameisha), and the convenient transportation network formed by Yantian-Exhibition Expressway and Yan-Dam Expressway, it has become even more convenient to visit Dameisha and Xiaomeisha. The beaches of Dameisha and Xiaomeisha + Shenzhen Ocean World will be jointly developed into a unique marine-themed cool resort water world.

Polar Hall: Currently, nearly 100 million yuan is being invested to build the first large-scale "Polar Animal World" in southern China. The preliminary beluga whales, polar bears, arctic foxes, and penguins have already welcomed visitors. The three "beluga whales" from the Arctic ice sea are multi-talented, able to sing and dance, spray water, and perform circles. The majestic king of the poles, the "polar bear", lives in a polar bear house equipped with cooling systems and swimming pools. Six "Ice Mountain Snow Foxes - Arctic Foxes", backed by ice mountains and stepping on ice blocks, have snow-white fur and charming eyes that shimmer with waves. Five penguins leisurely groom their feathers, showing off the elegance of Antarctic gentlemen to tourists.

The first phase project covers an area of about 200,000 square meters, featuring "Ten Halls and Three Parks" (i.e., Beluga Whale Hall, Polar Bear Hall, Arctic Fox Hall, Penguin Hall, Aquarium, Fantasy Ocean Hall, Whale Shark Hall, Shark Hall, Shell Hall, Science Popularization Hall, Model Ship Hall, Ocean Park, Water Fun Park, Sea God Garden), and "Fourteen Special Programs" (i.e., Extreme High-altitude Diving Show, Foreign Clown Humorous Diving Show, Adorable Seal Naive Performance, Funny Sea Lion Humorous Performance, Intelligent Dolphin Skills Performance, Passionate Ukrainian Underwater Ballet Performance, Dangerous Human-Shark Dance Performance, Astonishing Bull Shark Hunting Performance, Magical Ukrainian Mermaid Performance, Romantic Underwater Dream Wedding Performance, Underwater Ten Thousand Fish Feeding Performance, Leisure Deep-sea Diving Performance, Wonderful Ukrainian Water Ballet Performance, Frog Fairy Animation Performance) as the main display subjects. The two unique selling points: First, the ocean park in southern China that displays the most polar animals; Second, the largest scale "Sea-Land-Air" stunt variety show in the Asia-Pacific region lasting nearly 2 hours.

Whale Shark Hall: It was specially built for a mysterious visitor from the ocean - the whale shark, which is 8.34 meters long and weighs 3 tons, the largest whale shark found domestically so far. There are also nearly a hundred kinds of rare fish specimens here.

Traffic Tips

Ocean Theater: Equipped with nearly 3000 seats, there are currently six dolphins, eight sea lions, and six seals. Every day, there are three rounds of nearly one-hour-long grand performances at the Ocean Theater. Russian brave warriors bring extreme high-altitude diving stunts, Ukrainian beauties perform passionate water ballet, international clowns bring hilarious water programs, and there are wonderful performances by marine animal stars like dolphins, sea lions, and seals, winning nearly 100 rounds of cheers and applause from the audience!

Fantasy Ocean Hall: It is currently the largest indoor track-style exhibition hall in the country. Through the scenes of thirteen themed marine stories (such as The Fisherman and the Golden Fish, Robinson Crusoe, Nezha Creates Havoc in the Sea, etc.), it simulates and establishes a condensed time-space tunnel reflecting the history of human interaction with the ocean.

Science Popularization Hall: Through film and television small theaters, models, and exhibition boards, it showcases the vastness and profundity of marine culture. Divided into themes such as "Ocean - The Cradle of Life", "Oldest Marine Organisms", "Mysteries of the Ocean", "Ocean and Humanity", etc.

You may refer to the map to choose the appropriate mode of transportation.

Ocean Fun Park: Features thrilling and exciting projects such as "Hurricane Ship", "Surfing Ship", "Diving Chamber", "Dolphin Slide", and some children's entertainment projects, allowing you to fully experience the mighty power of nature's ocean and demonstrate the courage to fight against the ocean.

Shark Hall: It is currently the largest shark hall in the country, breeding over thirty sharks including the astonishing bull shark, fierce lemon shark, blacktip shark, whitetip shark, agile leopard shark, and massive nurse shark. Both the quantity and variety are considered the best among similar domestic projects. There is also a daily "Man and Shark Dancing Together" performance inside the shark hall.
