Women, pregnant, what are the signs of ectopic pregnancy in the uterus, Zhengzhou Gynecology. What are the signs of Zhengzhou female ectopic pregnancy? Experts from the Zhengzhou Anti-Aircraft Hospital Gynecology Department introduce that ectopic pregnancy is also known as extrauterine pregnancy. Simply put, it means being pregnant outside the uterus. The early symptoms of ectopic pregnancy are not obvious, usually requiring a B-ultrasound for diagnosis. Therefore, some women who already have an ectopic pregnancy only find out later when serious bleeding occurs, which is very dangerous. Below, our hospital experts will discuss this issue.

The most important symptom of ectopic pregnancy is pain on the side of the fallopian tube. This symptom usually starts one to two weeks after the overdue period and becomes increasingly severe. Pregnancy symptoms will also appear, and the pregnancy test will be positive. When the fertilized egg develops for a long time in the fallopian tube, there may be vaginal bleeding. If heavy vaginal bleeding occurs, it could mean the fallopian tube has ruptured. At this point, the pregnant woman may become very pale and may even faint due to low blood pressure.

Lower abdominal pain during early pregnancy is quite common, but it is more severe and continuous than in ectopic pregnancies. Any woman experiencing lower abdominal pain on one side during early pregnancy should seek medical attention immediately. Ectopic pregnancy can be diagnosed through a simple vaginal examination.

Symptoms: missed period, abdominal pain, vaginal bleeding.

Firstly, women of childbearing age, with missed periods accompanied by symptoms such as loss of appetite and nausea indicative of early pregnancy. This suggests pregnancy.

Secondly, sudden lower abdominal pain, either continuous or recurring, sometimes accompanied by nausea, vomiting, anal discomfort, and in severe cases, pallor, cold sweats, cold extremities, or even fainting and shock.

Thirdly, irregular vaginal bleeding.

If a woman experiences any of the above symptoms, it is highly likely to be an ectopic pregnancy. Immediate medical examination and confirmation are necessary. If it is an ectopic pregnancy, timely rescue is required to reduce or prevent intra-abdominal bleeding and avoid serious consequences due to excessive bleeding.
