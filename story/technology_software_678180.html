Police station chief jumps into an icy river in the rain and successfully rescues a woman attempting suicide

A card game enthusiast became a vegetable after a car accident, and her family called out "three缺one" every day to awaken her. Gradually sinking, Liu Qingguo's police uniform, soaked through, had become unbearably heavy. With the low temperature, it accelerated his heat consumption, and he also experienced physical exhaustion. At this moment, the people on the shore noticed his condition, and he heard someone shouting, "He is almost out of energy."

The family of the drowning woman later told Liu Qingguo that she had a conflict with her boyfriend of many years. Her boyfriend proposed a breakup, which she refused, leading her to attempt suicide in a moment of despair. When the rescued woman woke up, lying in bed, she sighed repeatedly. Liu Qingguo and her family quickly comforted her emotions. On that day, before leaving the hospital, the rescued woman expressed that she would never have any suicidal thoughts again.

(According to Ms. Jingjia) According to the recollection of witness Ms. Wang, at around 8 o'clock on the morning of the 5th, after eating breakfast, while walking by the pond outside the village, she saw a yellow-clad woman pacing back and forth by the pond. Suddenly, the woman jumped into the pond, scaring her into screaming. "Not good! Someone is jumping into the water! Come quick!" The scene by the pond quickly drew the attention of nearby people. Several villagers ran over upon hearing the shouts. Meanwhile, someone quickly took out their phone to call for help, while the drowning woman struggled and rose and fell in the pond, clearly not knowing how to swim.

Without much thought, he approached and forcefully dragged the woman ashore. The entire rescue process took more than ten minutes. After the woman was loaded onto the ambulance, Liu Qingguo was frozen to the point where his whole body was trembling. "Nothing, it's all part of the job," said Liu Qingguo.

Liu Qingguo recalled the details of rescuing the woman from the water: Nothing, it's all part of the job.

Due to the rain at the time, Liu Qingguo's vision was obstructed. After entering the water, it was difficult for him to determine the location of the drowning woman. "Swim forward... turn left..." "Almost there! Add some effort!" The crowd anxiously shouted from the shore. Liu Qingguo said that after several attempts to approach the woman, he tried to pull her towards himself, but due to the woman's extreme tension, her hands were wildly grabbing.

After much difficulty, Liu Qingguo managed to drag the drowning woman to the shore, and the onlookers all lent a hand, lifting the woman onto the 120 emergency vehicle. Subsequently, the woman was rushed to the nearby hospital for treatment. Thanks to timely rescue, the woman escaped life-threatening danger.

At this moment, Chief Liu Qingguo of the Danni Police Station in Juancheng County happened to be passing by. Upon hearing the cries for help from the crowd, without even taking off his police uniform, he bravely jumped into the icy water to save the person. Meanwhile, the people on the scene offered suggestions and guidance from the shore.

Our newspaper reports from Juancheng on December 6 (by reporter Jingjia, correspondent Tian Xiaocun, Liu Jia): Dissatisfied with her boyfriend's proposal to break up, a young woman from Danni Township in Juancheng jumped into a pond more than 2 meters deep. Fortunately, the local police chief happened to pass by, hearing the cries for help from the crowd, he immediately jumped into the icy river and saved her. Thanks to timely rescue, the woman escaped life-threatening danger.
