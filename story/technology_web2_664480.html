Good mood this morning

by tiany4676 on 2012-02-13 15:30:08

I got up very early today, which was the first day I got up so early these days (8:07, hehe... The feeling of waking up naturally is good, but I can't help it that I have been opening my eyes at 9 or 10 o'clock these days. I can't imagine how to live if this continues!). After finishing washing and combing quickly, the talented doctor put on the "Jordan" sneakers I bought yesterday and planned to show off on the street, ha.... As soon as I left the community, I felt a little uncomfortable. People on the street were still wearing cotton clothes, while I was wearing spring sportswear. I thought: I am young, not for showing off, but to be at the forefront of fashion. Regardless of everything, I started running, and there was no one exercising at this time. It seems that it's not early anymore, hey... After running for a while, I decided to take a small road to buy some fried dough sticks to eat breakfast at home, but unexpectedly an old tree caught my attention. I wanted to take a picture of it, but realized I didn't bring my mobile phone. So, I had to leave reluctantly. Originally, I planned to eat fried dough sticks with milk, combining Chinese and Western styles, but they didn't make fried dough sticks, so I could only buy buns. Holding the buns, I walked leisurely on the street and suddenly felt that this posture was too foolish, which didn't match my petite bourgeoisie identity, hehe... So I hurried back home. Without buying fried dough sticks, I could only have pure Chinese-style buns and soybean milk. The nutrition should not be inferior to fried dough sticks and milk! "A year's plan starts with spring, a day's plan starts with morning", a good mood accompanies me. Even I, such a low-key person, can't help but share the good mood this morning and let everyone share!
