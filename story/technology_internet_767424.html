Microsoft Ventures Accelerator launches the fourth phase of startup recruitment

by anonymous on 2013-10-15 21:19:15

On the evening of October 14th, it was announced that Microsoft Ventures Accelerator (formerly known as "Microsoft Cloud Accelerator") has officially launched its fourth round of recruitment and will begin accepting online applications from startup teams in the TMT industry. Ultimately, 15-20 companies will be selected to join the accelerator in January 2014.

Microsoft Ventures will provide free support to the selected enterprises in multiple areas such as world-class office space, technical and operational guidance, mentorship, entrepreneurial training, early user connections, financing, recruitment, and legal assistance, fully helping startups achieve success.

The selected startup teams will undergo a 6-month acceleration program, during which they will make varying degrees of breakthroughs and progress in technology, product and market development, management, and talent.

By the end of September, more than 90% of the 47 startups selected in the first three rounds had received market funding.

Besides the comprehensive entrepreneurial support services provided by Microsoft for free, another reason attracting these startup teams is Microsoft's positive attitude towards open-source entrepreneurship. Regardless of whether the enterprise uses Microsoft or non-Microsoft technology, or various open-source platforms and development languages, they can apply. Once they become members of the accelerator, they will all receive cloud services worth $60,000, and Microsoft will not require the selected enterprises to migrate their original technology platforms to the Microsoft platform.

Gao Xinxin, the China-based Executive Officer of Microsoft Ventures Accelerator, stated: "With the accumulated experience from the first three rounds and the mature development of the operational model, Microsoft Ventures Accelerator plans to expand to other cities in China, assisting more entrepreneurs in realizing their dreams."

The recruitment process will end on November 15th. For more details and registration, please visit: http://www.yunjiasuqi.cn/zh-CN/Recruitment/Content
