Suitable for: Golf friends and family vacations

Itinerary features: Direct flight from Beijing to Singapore, the island has the top-ranked golf course with the most beautiful sea view in Asia.

Local temperature: The annual temperature remains between 27~31 degrees Celsius, with a refreshing sea breeze.

Preparation time: Seven working days

Day 1 Beijing/Singapore 2330/0545+1

Depart from the Capital International Airport at night, flying to Singapore.

Accommodation: On the plane

Day 2 Singapore - Bintan

Arrive early morning, proceed to Tanah Merah Ferry Terminal, take the ferry at 0910 (about 45 minutes) to Bintan Island. After arrival, hotel staff will pick up from the ferry and transfer to the hotel.

Accommodation: Nirwana Garden Hotel

Day 3 Bintan

After breakfast, head to the TOP100 world-class golf course, Ria Bintan Golf Club. The feature of the course is the surrounding old woodlands and breezy sandy beaches. The course was nominated as one of the "Golf Digest's" 100 best courses, and also selected as the "Best Golf Course in Indonesia 2005" by "Asian Golf Monthly." The inland and scenic design hides many surprises and challenges, admired by golfers of all levels.

Accommodation: Nirwana Garden Hotel

Day 4 Bintan

After breakfast, play at Laguna Bintan course. This 18-hole championship course always gives golfers a pleasant surprise and exciting cheers, especially admiration for the designer's rich imagination. The course covers natural forests and swamps, beautiful beaches and abandoned quarries; the surroundings are ever-changing, constantly challenging the adaptability and judgment of golfers, providing a challenging, stimulating, and enjoyable venue for golfers of any technical level! Or you can participate in various water activities in Bintan or relax on the beach.

Accommodation: Nirwana Garden Hotel

Day 5 Bintan - Singapore

After breakfast, play again at Ria Bintan course for 18 holes.

In the afternoon, take a vehicle to the pier and board the 1735 ship back to Singapore, arriving at the Singapore pier at 1935, then head to Changi Airport.

Night stay: On the plane

Day 6 Singapore/Beijing 0015/0620

Take the flight back to Beijing, end of the journey.

Course Introduction

Ria Bintan Golf Club 27 holes

Introduction: This 27-hole championship course features old woodlands around the course and breezy sandy beaches. The course was nominated as one of the "Golf Digest's" 100 best courses and also selected as the "Best Golf Course in Indonesia 2005" by "Asian Golf Monthly." The inland and scenic design hides many surprises and challenges, admired by golfers of all levels.

Laguna Golf Club Bintan Reef 18 holes

Introduction: This 18-hole championship course always gives golfers a pleasant surprise and exciting cheers, especially admiration for the designer's rich imagination. The course covers natural forests and swamps, beautiful beaches and abandoned quarries; the surroundings are ever-changing, constantly challenging the adaptability and judgment of golfers, providing a challenging, stimulating, and enjoyable venue for golfers of any technical level!

Hotel Introduction

Nirwana Garden Hotel

Nirwana Gardens boasts 330 hectares of lush tropical scenery and pristine white sandy beaches, offering 4 different types of hotel accommodations. The hotel has a total of 245 superior and luxury rooms and newly renovated Nirwana suites. Each room is equipped with a color TV, satellite programs, air conditioning, international direct dial telephone, mini-fridge, private bathroom and shower, and free coffee and tea making facilities.
