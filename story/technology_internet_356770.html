Industry News:

1. The Fourth APEC E-commerce Business Alliance Forum was held in Chengdu.

The APEC E-commerce Business Alliance Forum is co-organized by the Asia-Pacific Economic Cooperation (APEC) and the Ministry of Commerce of the People's Republic of China. Under the theme of "Opening up the New Future of the E-era", this forum discussed 7 topics, including: policy environment and development trends of e-commerce in the Asia-Pacific region; convergence of three networks, digital entertainment, and commercial value; women's "E-consumption" era, commercial opportunities in the wireless field in the 3G era; e-commerce, foreign trade breakthroughs; building an e-commerce ecological industry chain; competition patterns and investment transformation in e-commerce, etc.

2. The Chengdu station of Baidu Marketing China Tour was successfully held.

On the afternoon of May 14th, the Chengdu station of Baidu Marketing China Tour was successfully held at the Shangri-La Hotel. More than 700 representatives of small and medium-sized enterprises attended and discussed how to conduct search engine marketing. Subsequently, under the assistance of IT Teahouse, the Baidu Union sub-forum attracted more than a hundred Sichuan webmasters to participate. Relevant responsible persons of Baidu Union introduced the development ideas of Baidu Union in the future and revealed that the requirements for website review would be gradually relaxed in 2010, allowing more long-tail websites to join the Baidu Union and share growth. At the same time, Baidu also increased its technical investment.

3. Tencent builds a hub for southwestern development in Chengdu.

In the Tianfu Software Park in Chengdu, thousands of miles away from Tencent Tower in Shenzhen, there is also a Chengdu Tencent Tower that has gathered more than a thousand Tencent employees. Almost all of Tencent's businesses have been implemented and expanded here, showing a good development trend. In Chen Yidan's words, Chengdu has now become an important support for Tencent's national layout of "north-south axis, east-west wings".

4. Perfect World invests in Ye Wang Technology in Chengdu with 80% equity.

Perfect World Co., Ltd. (Nasdaq: PWRD) (hereinafter referred to as "Perfect World" or "the Company"), a leading online game developer and operator headquartered in China, today announced its unaudited financial results for the first quarter ended March 31, 2010. It disclosed that it holds 80% equity in Ye Wang Technology in Chengdu.

5. The High-tech Internet Association upgrades to Chengdu Internet Association.

The founding conference of the Chengdu Internet Association and the first member representative conference were held in Chengdu. Relevant leaders from the Chengdu Information Office and the Chengdu Civil Affairs Bureau attended the meeting to congratulate.

3. Xinhua Wenchuan plans to cooperate with Jin Suandan to enter the online sales market.

Xinhua Wenchuan (00811.HK) announced on the 18th that the company and its parent company, Sichuan Xinhua Distribution Group, plan to establish a joint venture with software development company Jin Suandan to engage in online trading business of publications and cultural products. The registered capital of the joint venture is 30 million yuan, and the company, parent company, and Jin Suandan own 75%, 15%, and 10% of the rights respectively.
