Even if it's just a small cleaning tip, attention should be paid to the standardized operation of the cleaning method; otherwise, the expected results will not be achieved.

True carpet cleaning involves the use of tools such as steam saunas, multi-functional cleaning machines, and vacuum water suction machines, combined with specialized disinfection and stain removal agents, to kill mites, sterilize, clean, remove stains, suck up dirty water, dry, and comb the carpet. Finally, it is treated with an anti-stain spray to form a waterproof film on the carpet surface, achieving a waterproof and dustproof effect. Below are two standardized cleaning methods introduced.

Water extraction method suitable for synthetic fiber carpets.

Tools: Carpet brush, water extractor, carpet washing machine, carpet shampoo.

Operation process:

1. Dusting: Use a vacuum cleaner to thoroughly vacuum.

2. Dilute cleaning agent: It can also be injected into the water tank.

3. Spray cleaning agent: Fully spray it on the carpet.

4. Extraction: After spraying the cleaning agent for 10 to 15 minutes, the stains detach from the fibers. Then use the floor washer to extract the dirt. When operating, walk backward, ensuring some overlap in each operation, and perform at least two extractions.

5. Rinse: While cleaning the carpet, use the water extractor to suck up the already cleaned carpet.

6. Drying: Let the carpet dry completely. To speed up drying, you can turn on the carpet dryer.

Dry foam cleaning method suitable for pure wool carpets.

Tools: Single-disc scrubber with carpet brush and foamer, carpet comb or rake, vacuum cleaner, high-foam carpet cleaner.

Shanghai Carpet Cleaning Company carpet cleaning operation process:

1. Dusting: Same as the water extraction method, the first step is to thoroughly vacuum using a vacuum cleaner.

2. Local treatment: Use specialized cleaning agents to separately handle oil stains, fruit stains, coffee stains on the carpet.

3. Dilute carpet foam cleaner: Inject the cleaner into the foaming box for dilution.

4. Hand brushing: Manually brush the edges, corners, and areas that the machine cannot reach.

5. Dry foam brushing: Use a single-disc sweeper equipped with a foamer and carpet brush to dry foam wash the carpet. After the cleaner acts on the carpet for a while, repeat the brushing process.

6. Combing: Use a carpet comb or rake to comb the carpet fibers, which is very important for the appearance of the carpet, especially for longer fibered cotton velvet carpets, and it also helps accelerate drying.

7. Stain removal: After the carpet fibers are completely dry, use a vacuum cleaner to suck up the dirt and dry foam crystals.
