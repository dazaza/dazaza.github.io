The provincial passenger transport has reached its peak. Haikou bus stations are adding 398 extra trips daily.

66 tourist buses are supporting the provincial line passenger transport.

省内客运迎来高峰 海口各汽车站日加班398个

Under the surge of passengers from all directions to the bus stations, the peak of passenger flow for the provincial bus transport continued today. Hainan Auto Transport Group Co., Ltd. increased the frequency of departures and coordinated through Haikou transportation management department, mobilizing a total of 50 tourist buses and 16 public buses to support the provincial line passenger transport. By 18:00 this afternoon, Haikou region had dispatched a total of 1900 trips, which was an increase of 232 compared to yesterday, with a passenger volume of 36,000 people, including 398 extra trips, an increase of 98 compared to January 23rd.

A relevant person in charge of Hainan Auto Transport Group Co., Ltd. stated that during the last two days, the provincial bus passenger transport has experienced a pre-holiday peak in passenger flow. On January 23rd, a total of 7029 trips were dispatched across the province, transporting 115,600 people, including 680 extra trips. Only in the Haikou region, a total of 1966 trips were dispatched, transporting 46,000 people, with 436 extra trips.

Facing the sudden peak in passenger flow, Hainan Auto Group scientifically allocated transport capacity, increased the frequency of departures, and implemented continuous dispatching at the square in front of the station for short-distance lines. Due to the significant increase in passenger flow, Hainan Auto Transport Group Co., Ltd.'s transport capacity became strained today. In order to timely transport passengers, the company coordinated through Haikou City's transport management office, mobilizing 66 tourist buses and public buses from various Haikou tourist bus companies and public bus companies to support the line passenger transport. Extra trips were added to routes with high passenger flow to ensure passengers can return home for the New Year in time.

Author: Hou Xiaojian, Mai Shixiao

Source: Hainan Daily
