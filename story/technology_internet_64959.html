HP Pavilion dv3000 Notebook - HP Digital Mix Video Contest

by xloong on 2008-04-23 20:23:14

With the improvement of people's living standards, people are no longer limited to the pursuit of single professional functions. People prefer to "mix and match" related things together, which is not only convenient but also saves time, and can better adapt to the fast-paced life of modern society.

The HP Pavilion dv3000 is a series of laptops that claim to lead the trend of the "new digital mix-and-matchism." It integrates leading technologies such as a 13.3-inch high-brightness widescreen, NVIDIA® GeForce™ 8400M GS high-performance graphics card, smart remote control, QuickPlay 3.6 entertainment box, biometric fingerprint recognition technology, high-sensitivity camera, and 3D DriveGuard mobile protection into one system. It is a comprehensive example of the "mix-and-match" philosophy. Whether for entertainment or work, having such a powerful "mix-and-match" laptop will surely make you feel confident and efficient.

HP has collaborated with Youku, a top domestic video website, to jointly host the "My Digital Mix-and-Match Life Video Contest" in order to better promote the "mix-and-matchism" of the HP Pavilion dv3000 series laptops.

From the picture on the left, you can see the award settings for the "My Digital Mix-and-Match Life Video Contest."

The first prize, "Best 'Digital Mix-and-Match' Storm Award," has two slots, each winning a HP Pavilion dv3000 worth 15,000 yuan, which is quite tempting.

The second prize, "Best Work Award," has eight slots for the iPod Nano3 (4G).

The third prize, "Spectator Struggle Award," has 15 winners who will receive luxury thumb-sized laptop desks.

All these prizes are very attractive... Unfortunately, I don't know how to make videos, otherwise, I would definitely participate.

Moreover, the requirements for participating in this event are very low. The official statement says: As long as you become a registered user of Youku, as long as you are interested in the HP Pavilion dv3000, and as long as you like it, you can participate.
