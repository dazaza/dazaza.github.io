Big Exposure of Stars’ Gossip: On January 18, NetEase Entertainment reported that the audience who had watched the TV version of "My Own Swordsman" must know that Yan Ni's performance as Miss Tong was undoubtedly the most charming beauty in the world. In the upcoming movie version, apart from her usual charm, Miss Tong has transformed into a professional ancient career manager, carefully calculating and leading her team to dominate the world.

The Dignity of the Shopkeeper is Indisputable

As the head of the Tongfu Inn, being charming is not enough. Yan Ni displays her bossy demeanor in the movie version, portraying a decisive and authoritative shopkeeper vividly. Although these heroes have a certain status in the world, they all become subordinates of "Miss Tong" when they enter the inn. A leader naturally needs to have authority, so everyone can see in the new play that whether it is running the inn or leading the heroes to intelligently repel real estate villains, Yan Ni is always full of presence and in charge. Everyone listens to her orders and supports her like stars surrounding the moon.

Team Spirit with Justice as the Priority

As a manager, to successfully develop one's career, naturally cannot be separated from a loyal team, so team awareness is particularly important. On the one hand, "Miss Tong" needs to be the backbone, but on the other hand, she also needs to care about her team. In life, they are friends who share happiness and difficulties together. She takes care of everything from their daily lives to their inner emotions. In the movie, "Miss Tong" displays the chivalrous spirit of righteousness: sincerely inviting "Big Mouth" back to the team, and after driving away "Guo Fu Rong", quietly caring about everything about her behind the scenes, which deeply moves people. The sisterhood sentiment is very touching. "Wu Shuang" may be doing things outside, but "Miss Tong" has always been concerned about her marriage. It is with this sincerity that she moves everyone around her, so no matter big or small matters, how bumpy the road is, everyone will stay by Miss Tong's side, never leaving.

Bravery and Strategy in Business

As is well known, the new version of "My Own Swordsman" uses the current hottest topic of real estate speculation to tell a financial crisis story of the Tongfu Inn. To intelligently repel the real estate developers and protect the Seven Heroes Town, making everyone clearly aware of the real estate bubble problem, "Miss Tong" leads everyone to plan strategies. In front of the real estate developers, she uses various tactics such as the "Beauty Plan", the "Bitter Flesh Plan", and the "Empty City Plan". Facing the main villain "Ji Wu Li", Miss Tong remains calm in danger, like a military strategist, impeaching and arranging formations, fighting with wisdom and courage. Finally, under everyone's united efforts, they not only win the victory but also help the Tongfu Inn get out of the economic crisis.

Although it is a costume comedy, the movie version of "My Own Swordsman" depicts a storm in the modern business world, integrating the topic of real estate speculation into the film as one of its highlights. The main actors also reflect various roles in the modern workplace, especially Miss Tong as a career manager. Apart from bringing joy to everyone, imparting the way of career managers and survival rules in the workplace has also become a hidden theme of the movie.
