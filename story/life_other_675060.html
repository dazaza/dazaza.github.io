Write to yourself, those words that belong to you

That's you, with eyebrows aligned bangs, black curly hair, the pear blossom he accompanied you to perm in July. But now you say, you want to straighten your hair. Time moves forward unknowingly, you've started high school. You've left there, that place of lost sorrow. You are the fallen leaves of autumn.

Once again, it's a full moon on the Mid-Autumn Festival.

You've fallen in love with reading, the slightly affected words of An Yiru. You remember a sentence: "The moon is full but people are not." You cried, this Mid-Autumn Festival, truly, a solitary one, eating mooncakes alone.

You've fallen in love with drawing hearts, sad and desolate tunes, perhaps they don't belong to you. Red beauty, cold fireworks, yet you've forgotten the faint gardenia behind them, silently blooming. "The morning wind, piercing and cool, at the lonely station I am alone. I just want to leave this city, slowly forgetting you. Forgetting all memories of you in this city. Forgetting all memories of me in this city." A short monologue. It pierces through your already fragile heart. On single loop, when the song ends, will the people disperse? You don't know, and there's no way to know.

You've fallen in love with her, those things we both wish for. A phrase "to my true love". How much you want to promise her, to stop drowning sorrows in alcohol. In that cherry blossom blooming, gentle evening breeze, holding her hand until the end of time. How much you want her to understand, even as time passes, she will always be by your side. How much you want to give her everything you can. Calm and content, adaptable, that's you, that's her. For a thousand years, ten thousand years, you and she are still like the first meeting.

You've fallen in love with classical things, those dusts that have slept for thousands of years, destined to be forgotten in the past. Beauty fades quickly, youth easily slips away. Like Rongruo's line, if only life were always as at the first meeting, how good it would be. He remains his world-famous master, she remains her peerless beauty, kingdom and beauty do not invade each other, no beginning, no end. But life, after all, is filled with regrets. Death is selfish and fair, it won't let you accompany. Thus, there was the not entirely tragic love between Sanlang and Yuhuan. Seeing this, you cry once more. Yes, you suddenly become particularly prone to tears. Even though the ending isn't entirely perfect, it's still spectacular. "Mountains without ridges, winter thunder rumbling, summer snowfall, heaven and earth unite, only then dare I sever ties with you."

You've fallen in love with being busy, no longer going to bed early, no longer staring blankly at the same night sky alone. You seem to fear, fearing the starry night's descent, fearing the lights of ten thousand homes. You always say, perhaps, only that inconspicuous corner suits you. Untouched, unmentioned. You like curling up in your own arms, quietly gazing at the long night's shadow. You say, you won't betray yourself. But aren't you always deceiving yourself? Just a habit, a habit of loneliness. Yet you always believe that only when the wind stops and the dust settles, flowers exhausted, can you see clear winds, bright moons, flowers in full bloom under the moonlight. You're afraid to imagine, next year's Mid-Autumn Festival, will it still be a full moon but incomplete people?

You've fallen in love with dancing, the bustling prosperity, if it were all just a dream, leaving no trace, how good that would be. Long dances, falling into half a lifetime of prosperity. Melodious tunes, spanning an entire life of leisure. Perhaps, only this fleeting peace suits you. As your body moves busily, your mind becomes blank. Fluttering and falling, you love it.

Sometimes, you deceive yourself, after the bustle, nothing has passed. "Flowers fall and water flows on its own, one kind of longing, two places of idle worry." Chanting these few lines, just a light spring that doesn't return has already expressed the intention, striking the heartstring. Yes, after the bustle, what has changed is your exclusive memory, slowly withering, rotting in deep meaning. You also fantasize, when can you lift his face, carefully examining his features. Ultimately, you wake up, because fantasy is ultimately just a fantasy.

You use small notebooks to record the bits and pieces of daily life, laughter, sadness, gratitude. Your life, elegant and clear like water, you'll raise your head and smile, this, is good. Words, for you, may be a kind of addiction, a form of寄托寄托. Yearning for life, reminiscing about the past, and missing loved ones. You like expressing yourself through words. But this is called cowardice by him. Perhaps you are indeed timid, during long nights, you're always irrationally afraid, always overthinking. You'll like very quiet music, in the silent dark night, savoring the color of each note, simply to feel the little happiness brought by the long night.

You don't have a stunning beauty or a sharp mind, you only have yourself. The most ordinary self in the crowd, you always try to love yourself, saying, only by loving yourself can you have the qualifications to love others. So, you indulge in deep love, yet subtly bitter in fate. You comfort yourself, life is still sweet.

The bitter wind blows, the Mid-Autumn Festival quietly flows by in an instant, bathed in a romantic orange-yellow hue. Blowing open the curtains, awakening the mind. On the full moon of the Mid-Autumn Festival, can you also delicately experience this festival praised by countless ancients? Once, those partings and reunions made you gradually forget life's vibrancy. Perhaps you haven't forgotten, just unwilling to mention. Some things look complete, but when flipped over, you see a kind of pain, tearing the heart apart.

On the Mid-Autumn night, you also need someone to light up the darkness for you. But he has flown to the ends of the earth, and you cannot bring him back.

A mournful song, two streams of clear tears, the story reaches here, does it also need an excuse to conclude? Stop here, just for a second, and you'll hold back your tears.
