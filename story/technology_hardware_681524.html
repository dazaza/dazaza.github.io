Yangcheng Traffic Radio launches a special event for the Lantern Festival

by yubmhzky on 2012-02-28 14:04:02

On February 6th, the Lantern Festival, Yangcheng Traffic Radio Station, together with Tencent Weibo, Southern Television, Lung Feng Food, and Auto Magazine, specially launched the "Lung Feng Presents Auspicious Lantern Festival Wishes, Love Overflowing Throughout Yangcheng" event. Gifts of "love-filled glutinous rice balls" will be presented to enthusiastic listeners and dedicated workers on the front lines of transportation, making this Lantern Festival in the Year of the Dragon especially warm-hearted.

During every holiday, traffic police, railway, civil aviation, and public transportation employees working on the front lines often work overtime selflessly. To express our admiration, reporters from Yangcheng Traffic Radio Station also brought batches of "love-filled glutinous rice balls" to various front-line transportation stations on the day of the Lantern Festival, delivering festival greetings to those working in the railway, civil aviation, and public transportation sectors.

At 1:30 PM, well-known hosts, journalists, and editors from Yangcheng Traffic Radio Station, led by the station's leadership team, arrived at the front gate of the Guangdong Broadcast Center on Renmin North Road to distribute Lantern Festival "love-filled glutinous rice balls" to passing cultural driving drivers. Many drivers, hearing the announcement, waited eagerly in front of the radio station on Renmin North Road to support Yangcheng Traffic Radio Station's event. Due to the immense popularity of the gifts, a long line of cars formed in front of the radio station after 1:30 PM. Drivers all expressed, "If Yangcheng Traffic Radio Station has an event, we must support it! Not only can we receive Lantern Festival gifts, but we can also meet famous broadcasters; there’s no way we wouldn’t come!"

The Lantern Festival is a time for family reunions, but around us, there are also some low-income households, widowed elderly individuals, and those living in hardship who particularly need society's care. Yangcheng Traffic Radio Station's "love-filled glutinous rice balls" naturally did not forget them. After distributing gifts to drivers on Renmin North Road in the afternoon, Director Zhang Jun and Deputy Director Zhou Bing of Yangcheng Traffic Radio Station immediately led a love team to Shuangjing Street, delivering large and small packages of Lantern Festival gifts to the community members in Shuangjing who were in special need of assistance. A widowed elder who received the gift emotionally said, "Yangcheng Traffic Radio Station feels like my family, always thinking of me. Every time I see you, I am deeply moved!"

Every year during the Spring Festival travel rush, the traffic conditions on the Jingzhu North Expressway have always been a focal point of Guangdong's road traffic. This year, during the Spring Festival travel rush, Yangcheng Traffic Radio Station, together with Southern Television and Tencent Weibo, set up a live broadcast studio for the first time on the Jingzhu North Expressway, reporting daily updates on the latest traffic conditions on the Jingzhu North Expressway to the many car owners. To thank the traffic police officers of the Shaoguan Highway Police Brigade and the colleagues of Shaoguan Radio Station for their strong support of the work at the Jingzhu North live broadcast studio, Deputy Director Liang Zhu led a team that specifically traveled to Shaoguan on the day of the Lantern Festival, delivering festival greetings and gifts to the Jingzhu North Highway Police Brigade and the Jingzhu North live broadcast studio, allowing the staff steadfastly holding their positions on the front lines of the Spring Festival travel rush to experience the unique warmth of the 2012 Year of the Dragon Lantern Festival!
