Seek the way of art and embrace the strengths of all schools.

by xyxgyp1998 on 2012-02-11 19:02:51

Seeking the Way of Art, Embracing the Best of All Schools: A Pilgrimage to Mount Emei, Collecting Treasures in the Golden Autumn, "Shi Bi Jia" Raises the Golden Hammer Again, Setting Up an Altar on Mount Emei. Another aesthetic collision and a legendary collection will leave lingering memories.

In this auction, excellent works [calligraphy and painting wholesale] are abundant, with masterpieces emerging one after another. More than 300 fine works from collectors across over 20 provinces and cities in the country are vying for attention, each with distinct styles and vibrant personalities. Looking at the selected masterpieces for auction, the ink and brushwork of landscapes, the vivid portrayal of figures, the taste and interest of flowers and birds, and the linear philosophy of calligraphy all rank among the best, truly rare treasures that are hard to find in the market and difficult for collectors to part with. Works such as Cheng Shifa's super large-character figure paintings, Feng Jianwu's broad-stroke landscapes, and Zhao Yunhe's freehand flower-and-bird paintings can all be seen in this auction.

This "Emei Autumn Auction" encompasses three levels of contemporary painting and calligraphy ecology and artistic expression from across the country, the Bashu school, and the Jiazhou school. The lineup of these three levels presents a "three peaks reflecting each other" stereoscopic momentum, with brilliant and unique aesthetic reflections. Observing them harmoniously, one can fully grasp the profound mysteries of the great way and embrace the best of all schools.

Wang Xueqiao's flower-and-bird works integrate traditional and modern knowledge, with mature techniques and free-spirited brushwork. His ability to capture the essence is evident in his strokes.

Zhang Hai's calligraphy has reached the realm of Tai Chi, containing strength in softness and revealing the Dao in elegance. His achievements directly draw from the Jin and Han dynasties, reaching simplicity and authenticity, achieving excellence.

Moreover, works by masters such as Li Kuchan, Sun Qifeng, Jia Guangjian, Ouyang Zhongshi, and others represent classic works from different periods.

As a leading figure of the Bashu school, Feng Jianwu's landscapes feature bold and vigorous brushstrokes, with powerful ink and free-flowing writing. The grandeur of hundred-zhang mountains breaking through the air showcases immense energy and tension.

Chen Zizhuang captures the small scale of Sichuan villages with sparse, simple, and desolate brushwork, creating a timeless masterpiece in contemporary painting.

He Yinghui's calligraphy deeply roots in Qin and Han, striving for the styles of Jin and Tang. His style is majestic and ancient, belonging to the category of divine works.

Zhao Yunyu's figures, Sun Zhili's flowers and birds, Wu Yifeng's landscapes, as well as works by Cen Xuegong, Huang Chunyao, Yan Songfu, Li Wenxin, Dai Wei, Peng Xiancheng, Wu Xujing, Qin Tianzhu, Shen Daohong, Wang Shengyong, Zhang Jian, Wan Yibin, Guo Ruyu, Tan Changrong, Ni Ma Zeren, Liu Yunquan, and others, are considered pinnacles of art in Sichuan.

The founder of the "Jiazhou School," Li Qiongjiu, creates groundbreaking works with free and unrestrained brushwork. His integration of calligraphy into painting and vice versa reveals a grand master’s presence. His works harmonize dryness and moisture, with lofty and distant realms, being a stroke of genius in contemporary art.

Yang Tiankai's calligraphy is elegant and refined, with cultural sophistication. It reveals character within rules and cultivation within measures, featuring full lines and rich spirit, belonging to the capable works of contemporary art.

As main players of the Jiazhou School, works by Li Daoxi, Yang Feng, Xiong Shisen, Li Zhongchun, Yu Long, Chen Lie, Yang Xun, Dong Ming, and others are gradually being discovered by collectors, with very optimistic future prospects.

The era shapes the realm of painting and calligraphy, and painting and calligraphy present the style of the times. The second "Emei Autumn Auction" once again presents the spiritual state and heart colors of half a century to the public, an art invitation worth looking forward to.
