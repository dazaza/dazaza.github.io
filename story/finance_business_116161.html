Against the backdrop of the crisis, online stores are defying the market trend and gaining popularity.

by sky110 on 2009-01-01 20:30:15

In recent years, the development of electronic commerce has shown a vigorous trend. "Online shopping and opening online stores" have become popular ways of consumption and entrepreneurship. The change in consumption methods has provided favorable conditions for the development of online stores in recent years. By developing online stores, IT dealers in a depressed market environment have begun to find new profit points. However, reporters learned from the market that the development of online stores is not always smooth.

Online Store Sales Rising

The IT market has been sluggish for a long time this year. In areas such as channels, industries, and retail markets, the demand for IT products has sharply contracted. Due to the sluggishness of the terminal retail market, many channel merchants face the risk of inventory accumulation at the end of the year. However, after recently interviewing several IT merchants, reporters found that although the sluggish market environment has caused setbacks for many IT-related consumptions, the demand in the e-commerce field is increasing day by day.

"From the initial few thousand yuan per month to a current monthly turnover of 800,000 yuan, Top Choice Online Mall has seen growth in sales every month since its operation," Manager Zhang Haidong of Guangxi Top Choice Computer Information Co., Ltd. told reporters. It is understood that from product transactions to after-sales services, the sales support services of Top Choice Online Mall are very complete. For online consumers within the city, Top Choice also provides free delivery. With the company's rich product structure, a perfect sales service system, and the experience and influence accumulated from operating physical stores with integrity for over a decade, Top Choice Online Mall is currently developing very strongly.
