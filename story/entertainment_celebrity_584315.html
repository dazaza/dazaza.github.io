Chen Peisi rejected the invitation of the Spring Festival Gala, and frankly said that he would not go back.

by edivista on 2011-11-24 19:50:28

Click to enter Chen Pesi's Weibo

Recently, Chen Pesi (Weibo: Yanqing Orchard Owner) attended the opening ceremony of the 2011 Beijing Comedy Arts Festival and was once again asked about the old topic of whether he would appear on the Spring Festival Gala. Unlike before, this time Chen Pesi received a text message invitation from director Ha Wen, who wrote on Weibo: Thank you to all the artists who have contributed to the Spring Festival Gala.

At the opening ceremony of the comedy festival, Chen Pesi was asked if he would return to the Spring Festival Gala stage. "I can't go back, I can't return to the cradle, time cannot flow backwards," he said. He revealed that he had indeed received an invitation via text message from the Spring Festival Gala director, but declined due to scheduling conflicts. After Chen Pesi and Zhu Shimao left the Spring Festival Gala stage, there were rumors that they had been banned by the production team. Does Chen Pesi's invitation from the production team mean that their past grievances have been resolved? In response, the Spring Festival Gala publicity group only answered to look at Director Ha's Weibo. However, the reporter did not find a definitive answer in Director Ha Wen's Weibo, where she wrote: Thank you to all the artists who have contributed to the Spring Festival Gala! Thank you to all friends who bless the Spring Festival Gala! We will work hard! (Click on the picture to enter Chen Pesi's Weibo)

Chen Pesi gathers eight plays for another comedy festival

The 2011 Beijing Comedy Arts Festival is already the third time it has been held, with all the plays personally selected by Chen Pesi. Eight different styles of comedies will be staged from December 21st to January 9th next year at the Century Theatre.

This year's comedy arts festival focuses on recommending Fujian Quanzhou's newly compiled Pear Garden Opera "Dongsheng and Mrs. Li", which not only positions Chen Pesi as improving the taste and style of the comedy arts festival, but also features him making a cameo appearance as a minor role, the storyteller. Chen Pesi said: "Dongsheng and Mrs. Li" has given me great harvest, it can be said that we have found the root of Chinese comedy from our ancestors. This is a very good opportunity for us to learn from traditional excellent plays. Fujian Pear Garden Opera has been listed as a national intangible cultural heritage, its performance form originates from the Song and Yuan Dynasties, and has a history of more than 800 years, truly a living fossil. This 20-day comedy festival is exactly a feast of joy for the audience.
