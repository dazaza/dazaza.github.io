Mobispine helps you use Facebook better.

by gaopengbo on 2008-06-20 06:37:07

Facebook should be the most innovative invention in IT- Internet after Microsoft and Google. With the popularity of social networks, people can get in touch with, share with, and taste the life of different culture and regions among friends all over the world without going out.

Mobispine also develops a third party application specially for Facebook along with the developing trend of Internet. From now on, you can bind your Facebook account with your mobile phone to share your happiness in reading via mobile phone and know the status update of your friends.

How to use:

1. Add Mobispine third party application on the computer first.

2. Make sure that the free Mobispine client program has been downloaded in the mobile phone (you can get it by entering m.mobispine.com in the mobile browser).

3. After installation, find the user's unique id through options(选项)--> settings(设置)--> my account(我的帐户).

4. Fill this id in the place where the Mobispine application on the Facebook page requires. Finish setting!
