Huangcun Inn - Zhou Xun and Wang Zhonglei absent from the premiere of 'Homeland' - no discussion on whether to split with Huayi Brothers - China Film

by liteemjt on 2011-05-26 23:55:52

After ending her relationship with Xiao Wang Luo, Zhou Xun stayed out of the media spotlight for a long time. Recently, rumors about Zhou Xun leaving Huayi Brothers have been swirling again. However, yesterday, Huayi boss Wang Zhonglei specially attended to support Zhou Xun, seemingly to prove that their cooperation will continue. When speaking on stage, Wang Zhonglei defined Zhou Xun with three identities at the beginning: cooperative partner, good friend, and environmental teacher.

Zhou Xun (right) with "Homeland" director Yang Ardis-Bertrand attended the premiere of the environmental documentary "Homeland" in Shanghai.

During the interview, Zhou Xun stated that the environmental concept promoted in the film "Homeland" is precisely the voice of herself and the OUR PART team. Since serving as the first Chinese goodwill ambassador of the United Nations Development Programme in 2008, Zhou Xun has always been personally involved and led her team to spread environmental awareness through various forms of cooperation. It is reported that the movie "Homeland" is also the first international project launched this year by Zhou Xun's environmental project OUR PART Our Contribution.

When asked during the interview, Zhou Xun smiled upon hearing the media's question and said: I don't even know what happened, suddenly such news came out... After finishing, Zhou Xun looked at her boss Wang Zhonglei. As if they had built up默契, Wang Zhonglei replied cooperatively: I know there are many rumors, and they often appear. I think we should choose not to respond, not to clarify!

Wang Zhonglei: No response, no clarification

It is reported that Zhou Xun's next movie has been confirmed to be Xu Ke's new work "Dragon Gate Inn." Yesterday, when talking about the next phase of work plans, Zhou Xun said: I'm about to start shooting soon.

Zhou Xun shed tears several times

Is Zhou Xun going to leave Huayi?

In addition to the Chinese version, "HOME" has been translated into 14 languages and released in 87 countries. Director Yang Ardis-Bertrand said that "Homeland" is a copyright-free movie because he hopes everyone can see it through any channel. The narrator of the Chinese version of the film, Zhou Xun, sighed and said: This is a touching documentary, and I cried several times while dubbing. After watching this documentary for the first time, I thought I must do something for this movie, and I must make sure more people see this documentary.

Dubbing for "Homeland"

The film "Homeland" (Home), directed by French famous director Luc Besson and Yang Ardis-Bertrand, is called the largest investment environmental film in history. Yesterday, the film was screened for the first time at the United Nations Pavilion within the Shanghai Expo Park. Zhou Xun, who provided the Chinese narration for the film, also attended yesterday's event. At the same time, Zhou Xun's boss, President of Huayi Brothers Brokerage Company, Wang Zhonglei, also came to show his support. When talking about the recent rumors of Zhou Xun leaving Huayi, Wang Zhonglei and Zhou Xun handled the media with great默契 by saying there were too many rumors and they chose not to respond.
