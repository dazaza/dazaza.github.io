83-Year-Old Man with 100,000 Yuan Annual Salary Applies for Position as "Granddaughter" (Photo)

by iface001 on 2011-05-10 14:10:59

When the two elderly people talked about their deceased relatives, they could not help crying. Commercial Journal reporter Zhongyu Tian/photograph

Citizen Grandpa Liu asked: "Your recruitment says that you give priority to those in power in the health enforcement department, and then you also say that your granddaughter-in-law died in the hospital. Is this a way of attracting well-intentioned people to solve this matter?" The two elderly people said: "If there are kind-hearted people, please help us. We do not care about the hospital's compensation." Seeing the elders crying painfully, a citizen surnamed Li handed over a bag of cookies: "It is so cold today, and no one takes care of them. They are really pitiful."

Yesterday morning, near the intersection of Zhijing Mountain Road and Jinshui Road in Zhengzhou City, an old man wearing a blue cotton-padded jacket was riding a tricycle with another white-haired elder. On the tricycle, there was an advertising board that read "Hiring Filial Granddaughter-in-law". It said that they were looking for women aged between 30~45 years old, and the hiring period would be 5 years (those in power in the health enforcement department will be given priority).

Zhao Ping emphasized that although the family was poor, they were willing to offer an annual salary of 100,000 yuan, which would definitely be honored after the hospital compensates.

"She was very filial." The 83-year-old elder sitting on the tricycle, Zhao Ping (phonetic), cried while recounting her story. She mentioned that due to her mobility issues, she was mainly taken care of by her granddaughter-in-law, Lei Ge Li. Because the family was poor, at the beginning of this year, Lei Ge Li came to Zhengzhou to work together with her husband and she was also pregnant. On November 2nd at 4 o'clock in the morning, she gave birth in a hospital in Zhengzhou. Later, due to heavy bleeding, she needed to be transferred to another hospital but unfortunately passed away. She left behind a son who is around seven or eight years old and a newly born baby girl.

The elder wearing a blue cotton-padded jacket said that her surname was Wen, and she was 63 years old and lived in Zhumadian. The advertisement was made for her mother-in-law, the 83-year-old Zhao Ping (phonetic). When talking about the reason for the recruitment, the two elders burst into tears. It turned out that Zhao Ping's granddaughter-in-law, Lei Ge Li, had died due to heavy bleeding during childbirth on November 2nd. Usually, Lei Ge Li took care of the two elders. After her death, acquaintances helped create this advertisement.

Promise: Once the hospital compensates, the annual salary of 100,000 yuan will definitely be honored.

In the wind, the 83-year-old elder is recruiting a filial granddaughter.

Yesterday morning, the cold wind was biting. Two elders were recruiting a filial granddaughter on the streets of Zhengzhou with generous conditions: an annual salary of 100,000 yuan.

■ Commercial Journal reporters Yinting Yin, Wenkai Wang, and intern Ruifeng Fan
