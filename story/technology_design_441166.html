UK woman loses weight magically during pregnancy (picture)

by iface002 on 2011-06-03 14:20:42

International Online Exclusive: For women, gaining weight during pregnancy is already a common occurrence. If one could lose weight during pregnancy, that would be extremely rare. Recently, a British woman has "invented" this "myth": 30-year-old Zoe Bissgrave actually lost half her weight during pregnancy. Before becoming pregnant, she was a woman weighing over 200 pounds, but after giving birth, she transformed into a slim lady weighing around 90 pounds.

According to British media on the 27th, Zoe from Exmouth, Devonshire, UK, has always been overweight from childhood to adulthood. For many years, being obese has been a pain in her heart. Since her childhood, with the help of her family, she started dieting, but it never seemed to work. By the age of 20, her weight had reached 200 pounds. Compared to girls her age, Zoe looked more like an "auntie."

Zoe said: "When I found out I was pregnant, I was particularly frustrated. I thought my diet plan was ruined again, but unbelievably, my weight kept decreasing during pregnancy. Now, it feels like I've been reborn. In the past, all I had in my wardrobe were clothes in the largest sizes. Wearing those clothes, I was far from attractive. Now, I can wear all kinds of clothing and flaunt my curves. Walking down the street, many people don't even recognize me. My friends are amazed at how much I've changed!"

According to media reports, Zoe, who successfully lost weight, is now a weight loss consultant. Because she herself is a living example, Zoe has many customers. During an interview, Zoe said: "Since I managed to lose weight during pregnancy, we have good reason to believe other women can do the same. Now I'm helping them cultivate healthy eating and lifestyle habits."

After trying various methods without success, Zoe gave up on the idea of losing weight. It wasn’t until she was 27 that, due to her obesity making it difficult for her to see clearly, she fell down her own staircase and broke her tailbone. After that incident, Zoe made up her mind to shed the excess fat. This went on for two years. Eighteen months ago, Zoe was astonished to discover that she had lost over three pounds. For Zoe, who had never succeeded in losing weight before, this was undoubtedly a huge surprise. Just as she was reveling in the joy of losing weight, the unexpected arrival of a baby boy dampened her spirits. Having already given birth to two sons, Zoe knew that pregnancy meant gaining weight. However, during her third pregnancy, Zoe did not gain weight. On the contrary, during the nine months of pregnancy, her weight quickly decreased. By the time her son Seth was born, Zoe, who used to weigh over 200 pounds, had transformed into a beautiful mother weighing only around 90 pounds.
