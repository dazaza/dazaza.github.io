Chapter One [13] The Hand that Steals the Spirit

by lpoihjkmkk on 2012-03-01 14:09:36

"Seven points, little!" Judging from the mysterious man's expression, he had given the answer without even thinking about it. The assistant, full of suspicion, uncovered the dice cup, and the three dice inside showed two, two, and three points respectively - not too much, not too little, exactly seven points.

Michael's heart felt as if it had been struck by a hammer. This mysterious person really did know how to listen to the dice. Could he possibly deal with someone who was so skilled at listening to the dice? He didn't know, or perhaps he could only hope that this person was only exceptionally good with dice.

With a slightly pale face, Michael approached the gambling table. The assistant, his face completely red, left the table. Originally, he had looked down on the mysterious person, but he never expected that this mysterious person would make him lose so thoroughly upon arrival. However, he couldn't help but admit defeat; if it were him to guess, he definitely wouldn't have guessed correctly. He didn't even know how many points he himself had shaken out. In this regard, at least, he still wasn't as good as Michael.

"Twenty-one!" The mysterious person threw a sentence at Michael, then crossed his arms with a sharp gaze, watching Michael with great interest. Michael mustered up his strength and began shuffling the deck of cards taken from the card box on the table.

However, just the act of shuffling the cards amazed the gamblers around. In their eyes, they only heard a few "pa pa pa" sounds, and all they saw was Michael's hands moving a few times on the table, completing the shuffle without seeing any detailed movements.

"Please cut the cards!" Michael showed no trace of looking down on the situation, instead very respectfully taking a white card and handing it over to the mysterious person. In the gambling world, those with superb gambling skills are always admired as mysterious figures and deserve respect.

This is a rule in casino poker games. To prevent gamblers from calculating ways to win, years ago Macau's gambling king Ho Yan came up with this method: increasing a single deck of cards to four decks, then allowing players to randomly insert a white card at any position. When the cards are dealt to the position of the white card, the remaining cards are discarded, and the deck needs to be reshuffled. Nowadays, regular casinos generally use eight decks of cards.

Among the gamblers present, some had seen Michael before, and others knew his identity well. Seeing him show such respect to this unknown stranger made them deeply suspicious. Upon further thought, they quickly guessed that this person in front of them was likely an expert, making them even more delighted.

The corner of the mysterious person's mouth curled upwards as he casually took the white card and tossed it. The white card drew a beautiful arc in the air and gracefully inserted itself into the middle of the deck of cards. Seeing this move, Michael's heart immediately sank. Unconsciously, he suddenly thought of a legend, a legend spread between various casinos...

The mysterious person casually placed several thousand dollars on the bet, smiled revealingly, and gestured for the cards to be dealt. This smile appeared quite strange and sinister in anyone's eyes. Michael barely calmed himself, quickly composing his thoughts. If he didn't have this ability, how could he become today's technical director!

The cards were dealt! First were the mysterious person's cards. As the playing cards flipped in Michael's hand, a Queen of Diamonds was revealed and handed over. Michael then dealt himself a card from the card box, flipping it open to find it was the smallest possible card—a two!

Before dealing the second round, Michael's heart had already sunk entirely. Just looking at the first card, he knew he had lost. During the shuffle earlier, he had memorized the order of the cards and set up the sequence. Even though the mysterious person had cut the deck, the card he should have received was supposed to be a Queen, but now it had turned into a two.

He absolutely didn't believe in so-called mistakes. There could only be one explanation: the mysterious person opposite him, exuding an eerie aura, had tampered with something. Yet, he couldn't even guess what move the other person had made, only vaguely guessing that the white card was the key!

A wise person knows their own limitations. Michael knew he had lost, and lost cleanly and decisively. However, he still had to finish dealing this hand. Closing his eyes, he knew the next card in the opponent's hand would absolutely be an Ace, because that Ace was originally prepared for himself.

It's hard to describe whether it was disappointment or happiness—or maybe both. Disappointed that thirty years of hard training in gambling meant nothing in front of this person, yet excited to have encountered a true master, perhaps after this battle, his gambling skills might break through.

Feeling the next card in the card box, since Michael was sure he had lost, his mood quickly settled. Calmly opening the card, he immediately caused a wave of astonishment among the surrounding gamblers. With this Ace, the mysterious person achieved a blackjack. Even if Michael managed to get twenty-one, he would still lose.

"I lost, you're stronger than me!" Michael frankly told the young mysterious person. He had always longed for a breakthrough in himself, and now he had found an opportunity. Regardless of the aspects, he should respect such a master, even though his age could make him the other person's father!

"You're very honest, I like that." The mysterious person laughed loudly, saying with pleasure, "Now, hand over the 'Gambler God Card'."

The entire venue erupted in commotion.

The Gambler God Card was created in the 1980s by the first-generation Gambler God, Gao Jin, and granted to intercontinental gambling experts across six continents. Possessing the Gambler God Card allows the holder to enjoy VIP treatment in any casino within their continent and grants the right to open a casino at any location within the same continent. Simply put, the holder of the Gambler God Card is a gambling titan in their region. And the holder of the North American Gambler God Card was none other than the previous World Cup champion—Michael Doton.

"Yes, sir." Michael stared at the mysterious person, thinking he had guessed the true identity of the mysterious person and the purpose of his visit. He quickly added, "To amend the Gambler God Law, we must..."

"Must achieve the [Six Gods Union], right?" Pulling out five pure gold spade A cards from his pocket, the corner of the mysterious person's mouth curled again, revealing a sharp mockery: "Here, this is my last stop."

Without saying anything more, he respectfully handed over the last golden spade A. Michael and his subordinates sent the mysterious person out of the casino in the most honorable way.

"Indeed... gambling skills still belong to Easterners..." For some reason, at the moment Michael handed over the Gambler God Card, he suddenly felt an inexplicable sense of relief.

Beside him, the assistant finally couldn't help but ask, "Director Michael, who is that person? It seems like you know?"

"Have you heard of the Intercontinental Challenge two years ago?"

"You mean, the freak who took on the top 100 experts from the six continents alone?" The assistant's face instantly turned pale, "It... was that person just now?"

"Yes... no mistake..." Michael nodded, "Two years ago, no, I think even now, the undisputed [King of Gambling]—God Hand Bao Yunfeng!"
