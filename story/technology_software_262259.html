**Birthday Code for October 18th: Born Leaders**

Position: Libra 24°-26°

Sub-sign: Third part of Libra

Element: Native Air Sign

People born on October 18th are destined to play key roles in the "drama of life." They are not only excellent actors but can also take on the role of director within their families or social circles. Their ambition to be leaders is very strong, and they strive tirelessly without hesitation. Interestingly, if the situation demands it, they are willing to maintain a low profile. However, even so, they often find it difficult to become the most exemplary leaders because their interests are too diverse! For example, they tend to leave their positions early to focus on other more interesting work. When promoted to managerial roles, they often eagerly await the day when they can "redeem" their freedom and return to their original areas of interest. If they do leave, their colleagues will miss their upright, honest attitudes, as well as their impeccable integrity.

Even if those born on this day aren't particularly skilled, they still have the potential to achieve fame or wealth through their talents, social connections, or luck. However, sitting in such high positions doesn't feel comfortable. The pressure that comes with handling numerous responsibilities may lead them to face the risk of collapse. Therefore, they must ultimately decide whether to voluntarily "downgrade" themselves or risk being overwhelmed by the pressure. Although they can be leaders, they shouldn’t force themselves into it. After all, their own work and hobbies are what they love most. Asking them to put their own affairs second would make them unhappy.

They enjoy playing with others, but since they aren't particularly adept at socializing, they sometimes face challenges in relationships. They like participating in family and friends' activities, but if ignored and left out, they may become moody. Despite their outward flamboyance, people born on this day possess introspective abilities. Moreover, because they despise self-pity, they will never reveal emotional wounds. In such situations, their most common method of release is returning to their personal work and interests rather than lingering like a Cinderella unable to attend a ball. This self-soothing method is admirable, but they shouldn’t take things too seriously and cut off communication channels with loved ones.

It’s best for them to stay in touch with others, converse regularly, and display appropriate humility to avoid being misunderstood as arrogant or omnipotent. Of course, there's no need to overly grovel either, as noble character and dignity remain their most precious qualities.

**Lucky Numbers and Guardian Stars**

People born on October 18th are influenced by the number 9 (1+8=9) and Mars. The number 9 has a strong influence on other numbers (any number added to 9 results in the original number, e.g., 5+9=14, 4+1=5; any number multiplied by 9 results in 9, e.g., 9*5=45, 4+5=9). Similarly, people born on this day strongly influence those around them, though the intense Martian energy is somewhat mitigated by the calm Venusian temperament (the ruling planet of Libra). The combination of Venus and Mars gives them charming and sensual qualities, but in times of frustration, it could harm both themselves and others involved.

**Health**

People born on October 18th must avoid collapsing under excessive responsibilities or stress. When their physical limits are reached, they should learn to say "no." It's important to consider what they can control versus what they cannot, allowing them to clearly understand their limits. Regular sleep and eating habits greatly help stabilize their emotions. Pay attention to balanced nutrition during meals, including grains and fresh vegetables. Stimulants like caffeine and nicotine should be controlled, while drugs or alcohol that cause numbness, depression, or shock should be avoided. Spending time on fitness routines like massage, yoga, or physical therapy can alleviate pain (especially in the mid and lower back) and promote relaxation.

**Advice**

Learn to say "no"—not just to others but also to yourself. Remember: outcomes are just a part of life. If circumstances allow, occasionally indulge in the joy of "uncertainty."

**Tarot Card**

The 18th Major Arcana card in the Tarot is "The Moon," representing dreams, emotions, and the subconscious world. Traditionally, the moon symbolizes feminine power and emotional embodiment. Thus, the upright meaning signifies sensitivity, empathy, and understanding. The reversed meaning represents emotional submission, passivity, and lack of self.

**Beauty Tip**

Throughout history, people have used skin bleaching methods to improve dullness and whiten their complexion. These methods primarily involve using potent chemical ingredients like Vitamin A acid and hydroquinone, which effectively lighten dark spots. However, using bleaching agents can damage the skin's natural lipid barrier. If exposed to UV radiation or hormonal imbalances during this period, the skin's protective function intensifies, causing melanin cells to release more melanin, leading to severe hyperpigmentation. With advancements in modern technology, people now use gentler and more effective whitening skincare products. By inhibiting tyrosinase activity, these products reduce melanin production, preventing new pigmentation and improving dullness caused by sun exposure or hormonal imbalance, enhancing skin clarity and uniformity while gradually fading dark spots.
