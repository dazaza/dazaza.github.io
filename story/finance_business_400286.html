"Taobao Fearless" Heart Sutra!

Huo Yan Jin Jing Debates the Real Goods

For online shopping novices, buying cosmetics on the internet is like playing a mine-sweeping game. Every step is taken with caution for fear of accidentally purchasing counterfeit goods! Developing a set of "Huo Yan Jin Jing" (sharp eyes) skills is the most important prerequisite for smooth online shopping!

1. Beware of goods with excessively low prices:

The biggest worry for many people is undoubtedly buying counterfeit goods online. However, if you always prioritize price over quality, the risk of buying fake goods significantly increases. For example, when you search for Olay cream on Taobao and sort the results by price from low to high, you might find some priced at only 1 yuan per bottle! Think about it, which merchant would willingly sell at a loss? Products with excessively low prices often have questionable quality. Therefore, when purchasing cosmetics online, one should not solely choose based on the lowest price. It's recommended to first establish a reasonable price expectation for the product you wish to buy, then compare and select within that price range.

2. "Credit Rating" ≠ Absolute Trust:

Those who are just learning how to shop online will undoubtedly pay close attention to each store's credit rating. Crown stores are naturally the best choice, but things aren't always so straightforward. Remember, in some cases, the credibility ratings on Taobao may involve artificial manipulation! Blind trust should be avoided.

For many newly developed medium and small sellers, they don't have extra funds for advertising and their credit ratings are still accumulating. In this case, more attention should be paid to the "product match degree." For many super buyers, they wouldn't completely exclude sellers with only 2 or 3 hearts, but if the "product match degree" is below 4.7 points, they wouldn't consider purchasing from them!

Additionally, whether a Taobao store has "consumer protection (accurate description)" is also very crucial. If you're dissatisfied with your purchase, you have refund protection! If the store also offers "unconditional return within 7 days" (represented by a small green hand holding up a red 7), the credibility is further enhanced.

3. Distinguish Right from Wrong by Reading "Reviews":

Since we've mentioned that credit ratings cannot be fully trusted, carefully reading other buyers' reviews becomes very important. Interestingly, some people may give a "positive review" out of politeness even if the product isn't entirely suitable for them. However, they might express some grievances or complaints in their written reviews.

As for "negative reviews," one shouldn't dismiss them outright. We hope everyone can develop some judgment abilities. It's possible that some competitors might intentionally sabotage each other. For example, some sellers may have a large number of sales and "positive reviews," but the reasons for "negative reviews" could simply be "the delivery was too slow!" or "it's not what I imagined" or "it's not genuine." For such vague reasons, one should consider whether it's malicious attack against the seller.

4. Smartly Choose Overseas Purchasing Services:

In life, we often encounter situations where our favorite brands don't have physical stores or counters domestically, or new products aren't available in the domestic market. In such cases, choosing overseas purchasing services can solve the problem! Even if the product is available domestically, purchasing it overseas can save a lot on taxes, making it very cost-effective! Just remember to pay attention to the following points when selecting a purchasing service to ensure authenticity!

▲ Preferably choose merchants who can provide shopping receipts.

▲ Preferably choose merchants whose products all originate from the same region or country. For instance, some purchasing services only sell Japanese, American, or Hong Kong duty-free products. If the regions are too mixed, there's a possibility of counterfeit goods being sourced from wholesale markets.

▲ Preferably choose stores that offer real-life photos of the products. Stores that only provide official website photos have lower credibility. If the store also provides on-site photos from foreign counters, it's even better.

▲ Some stores meticulously label product descriptions, including methods for identifying batch numbers. Such stores can be trusted.

Tips: Decoding Batch Numbers

To many people, product batch numbers seem like optional letters and numbers or incomprehensible texts. But these batch numbers actually contain significant identity information about the product! Here, we'll take L'Oreal and Estee Lauder as examples.

● Brands under L'Oreal include L’Oréal Paris, Lancôme, Biotherm, HR, etc., all with five-digit batch numbers. Typically, the first letter represents the country of origin, the second letter represents the year, and the last three digits represent the day of that year.

● Brands under Estée Lauder include Estée Lauder, Clinique, Bobbi Brown, M.A.C., etc., with batch numbers consisting of three parts: letters, numbers, or a combination of both. The letters represent the place of origin, the second digit represents the month, and the third represents the year.

Save Money, That's the Hard Truth

Buying the best items at the lowest price is the goal of savvy shoppers when it comes to online purchases. But extracting a few extra dollars from what seems to be already profitable online transactions involves some tricks!

1. Compare Shipping Fees:

If you've selected several reliable merchants with similar products after comparison, you'd naturally choose the one with the relatively lower price, right? Be careful here because some merchants play tricks with shipping fees. Merchant A's product might be 5 yuan cheaper than B's, but A's shipping fee could be 7-8 yuan more expensive. At this point, you need to weigh your options since no one's money grows on trees! Also, try to concentrate your purchases at one store, even if it's slightly more expensive. Otherwise, the accumulated shipping costs from multiple stores can be quite alarming!

2. Skillfully Bargain by Observing Cues:

The online shopping world is also a competitive arena. To avoid being overly exploited in the battle between buyers and sellers, sometimes you need to be a bit "ruthless." Although cosmetics sold online are generally discounted, it doesn't mean you should abandon the possibility of bargaining with the shop owner...

3. Find Secret Discount Information:

Even if you've been shopping on Taobao for years, you might not know this little secret... Given the massive amount of goods on Taobao, sometimes Taobao filters out some merchants from search results due to certain reasons! However, if you want to see a more comprehensive search result and truly "compare thousands of items," here's what you should do: Enter keywords for search → Choose the sorting method (price, credit, sales volume, etc.) → Scroll down the search results page to the bottom, and you'll find a line of gray text saying "Taobao has omitted some non-standard or irrelevant items." Click on "View All Items," and you'll be pleasantly surprised by more complete and affordable information!

Additionally, if you're not too busy, it's recommended to frequently browse Taobao's "Jianghu" (community) and "Search Stores" sections. You can discover more discount information through interactions with netizens.

4. Official Websites Can Also Offer Discounts:

Are brand official websites always stuck with fixed prices? Not necessarily! Due to tariffs and other reasons, many brands are cheaper abroad, so shopping on their foreign official websites is also a smart move! Of course, regardless of whether it's domestic or international, if you register as a user on a brand's official website, you can get first-hand information on new products and special offers, which is also very cost-effective!

Some Procedures Must Not Be Missed

Regardless of whether you're an experienced online shopper or a novice, there are some procedures that absolutely must not be skipped when conducting transactions in the virtual network. After all, wherever there are people, there's a competitive arena, and no one wants to end up as a sucker due to poor communication.

1. Communicate with the Shop Owner Before Placing an Order:

When you see a product you've long desired, everyone has the impulse to immediately place an order. Stay calm; there are some issues that need to be communicated with the shop owner beforehand.

▲ First, ask the shop owner if the item is still in stock?

▲ If it's not a small item, ask the shop owner if returns/exchanges are possible in case of quality issues?

▲ For cosmetics, make sure to ask about the product's origin, texture, shade, scent, packaging, authenticity, expiration date, and production date. Chat records can serve as evidence in case of problems.

▲ Also, note that the store's actual product photos must be their own. If the images still bear watermarks from another store, don't buy!

2. If You Buy Counterfeit Goods:

If, unfortunately, you end up buying counterfeit goods, remember to immediately file a complaint with the relevant department! For example, Taobao's customer service hotline is 0571-88157858. Although it's hard to get through, persistence pays off. Most importantly, apply for a return and refund from the store! Also, take good photos of the product and keep them on file as strong evidence.
