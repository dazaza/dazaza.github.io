Man who killed someone and fled for 6 years to avoid punishment claims he would turn himself in if he had a son

by xue94fwsh on 2012-03-01 17:11:01

Nandu News, reporter Feng Lei - Six years ago, pickpocket Wang killed thief He with a gunshot. After the case broke out, he fled with his pregnant wife and daughter. By the time he was captured, Wang already had three daughters and had been living under an assumed name in Yibin, Sichuan, making a living by driving unlicensed taxis. Yesterday, the Nanshan police introduced that the fugitive-hunting officers went to Yibin and arrested Wang. Why didn't he surrender? Wang claimed that since all three of his children were daughters, he wanted to have a son to carry on the family name before surrendering.

On September 25, 2005, gunshots were heard from a rental house in Nantou Yi Jia Village, Nanshan District. Thief He was shot in the stomach by a scattergun and died on the spot. The man who fired the gun, Wang, disappeared. Deputy Captain Qi Youjuan of the Nanshan Criminal Investigation Team introduced that Wang is from Zigong, Sichuan. After the incident, Wang fled with his wife, who was five months pregnant, and their three-year-old daughter. However, Wang's group did not return to their hometown in Zigong. Wang's wife is from Yibin, but after the incident, they also did not return to Yibin, causing the police to lose direction in their pursuit.

After the "Clear Web" operation began, the Nanshan police combed through clues and found that Wang's wife had once appeared in Yibin. On November 18th, the fugitive-hunting officers rushed to Yibin to search for Wang. Since Wang had been caught for theft in Shenzhen before the incident, his fingerprints were on record. The police checked the local information system in Yibin and found that Wang, who had changed his name to Li, had been sentenced to six months in prison for theft in Yibin in July 2009. After being released, he made a living by driving unlicensed taxis near the Yibin bus station. On November 21st, the officers disguised as lovers went to the Yibin bus station specifically to rent Wang's car. After finding Wang's vehicle, the officers questioned other drivers and learned that Wang was sleeping in his dormitory. Eventually, the officers captured Wang, the fugitive of this murder case who had been on the run for nearly six years.

After being escorted back to Shenzhen, Wang confessed that due to disputes over territory with He, He had once threatened to retaliate against him, so he shot and killed He. After the incident, he discarded the gun in the sea and fled with his wife and daughter. At the time of fleeing, he had a three-year-old daughter, and his wife was pregnant. Knowing that killing someone would cost him his life and that he had no way out, he only wanted to have a son to leave behind a descendant before surrendering. However, his wife gave birth to another daughter, so he did not surrender and continued to flee in the hope of having a son. But before he was captured, his wife gave birth to yet another daughter, so he still did not surrender. His wife, currently breastfeeding, has gone to her relatives' home to sit out her confinement period.
