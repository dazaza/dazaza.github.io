"The Temptation of a Wife" (Zhang Ruixi's Latest Korean Drama 2008)

Synopsis:

This is a story about the transformation of the world's most virtuous housewife into the most terrifying seductress. The drama unfolds as a woman betrayed by deceit and conspiracy experiences her first death and becomes a goddess of vengeance. Kind-hearted housewife Eun-joo has her husband stolen away by her best friend. To take revenge, she transforms herself into another woman who re-tempts her ex-husband, ultimately destroying his new family.

Character Introduction:

Zhang Ruixi as Gu Eun-joo

33 years old, fashion designer, and daughter-in-law of a real estate tycoon.

Highly responsible, she dreams of becoming a successful fashion designer. She is filial to her parents and willing to sacrifice for her family.

A graduate of a prestigious university's fashion design program, she won a major award in the Q Fashion Shopping Network design competition. Just as she was taking steps toward success, she was sexually assaulted by Cho Bin and became pregnant, so she gave up the opportunity to study abroad in Paris.

Although she chose a loveless marriage, she poured all her efforts into her husband and his family, spending seven years as a capable homemaker.

But when she discovers that her chosen life was filled with lies and betrayal, she transforms into another woman. The betrayal of her most trusted husband and her close friend-like sister leaves her deeply scarred, and she is reborn as a seductive femme fatale.

Bian Yumin as Jung Cho-bin

35 years old, Eun-joo's husband, and Kang Jae's friend.

Famous as a playboy since his school days, he was naturally disinterested in academics and barely graduated from university with money. He learned the real estate business under his father. The person he feared most in the world was his father; in front of him, he didn't dare to breathe loudly, but in terms of business, he achieved greater success than his father, expanding the company through domestic and international real estate ventures.

He liked Eun-joo since high school, but Eun-joo always ignored him. Eventually, he forcibly assaulted Eun-joo, leading to their marriage. However, soon after the wedding, he lost interest in Eun-joo and began seeking other women outside the marriage. He had a one-night stand with Eun-joo's best friend and also his brother-in-law's girlfriend, Ae-ri. Seeing Ae-ri's ambitious approach, he hastily sent her abroad under the guise of studying abroad.

Five years later, when Ae-ri returned as a mature and competent woman, Cho-bin was once again captivated by her. Following Ae-ri's plan, he divorced Eun-joo, leaving her with an irreparable wound.

Jin Shuhen as Shin Ae-ri

33 years old, Eun-joo's friend, Kang Jae's lover, and Eun-joo's husband's mistress.

Daughter of an old family friend of Gu Yeong-su, the same age as Eun-joo. Gu Yeong-su and Ae-ri's father came to Seoul together and became neighbors. Later, after Ae-ri's father passed away and her mother remarried, Gu Yeong-su raised her.

Helping with household chores at Gu Yeong-su's place, she learned to read people's expressions due to living in others' homes since childhood. She knew how to win people's favor and had a strong desire for success. She gained the love of Kang Jae, whom she grew up with, and was regarded as a future daughter-in-law by the Gu couple.

She treated Eun-joo's parents like her own and was as close as a sister to Eun-joo, but deep down, she was a frightening woman dreaming of entering high society. Dissatisfied with the poor Kang Jae, she set her sights on Cho-bin as a way out of poverty.

Unexpectedly, Cho-bin forcibly took Eun-joo and married her, making Ae-ri envious and jealous. Growing up under the same roof as Eun-joo, she had always been competitive with her. Due to her impoverished background, despite having similar academic performance as Eun-joo, she could only attend a professional university. Her long-prepared design competition dream was shattered when she lost to Eun-joo, breaking her hopes for studying abroad. Envious of Eun-joo marrying into wealth and receiving the love of a wealthy son, she harbored resentment.

By chance, she had a sexual encounter with Cho-bin and, with his financial support, went to study in Paris, a place she had always dreamed of. After returning as a successful fashion designer, she destroyed Eun-joo's family.

Li Zaihuang as Cao Jianyu

31 years old, architectural designer, adopted son of Ms. Min.

An orphan since childhood, he was adopted by Ms. Min and grew up healthily under her care. He treated Min's daughter Xiao Xi as his own younger sister, believing this was the best way to repay Ms. Min. However, upon learning that Xiao Xi had fallen in love with him, he began to distance himself from her. After Xiao Xi disappeared, he felt guilty, and his relationship with Ms. Min became distant. It was then that he met Eun-joo and fell deeply in love.

He works as an architectural designer in Cho-bin's company and enjoys Cho-bin's absolute trust.

He is the charming man all women hope to have—gentle, meticulous, sometimes calm, and sometimes impulsive in defending his love for Eun-joo.
