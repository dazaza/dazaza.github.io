E8 Inventory Management Software

by jupingliu on 2009-03-31 10:26:59

The E8 Inventory Management Software integrates purchase, sales, inventory, and finance management into one system. Specifically designed for small and medium-sized enterprises (SMEs), its functionalities include purchase management, sales management, returns and exchanges management, accounts receivable and payable management, and inventory management. It also comes with a comprehensive business reporting system (including income and expenditure details, purchase details, sales details, inventory overview, goods purchase and sales overview, top-selling goods rankings, business condition reports, etc.). The software offers a powerful query function that allows you to trace any transaction that has occurred. After installation, the software is ready to use immediately, making it an out-of-the-box solution. Its features cover all aspects of financial management and goods inventory management for SMEs, providing a highly practical tool.

Additionally, the software includes a minimum selling price limit function, a maximum customer credit limit mechanism, automatic switching between negative and non-negative inventory outflows, and tracking prices for both inbound and outbound transactions. A unique feature is the ability to connect to remote databases via standard broadband connections (such as ADSL), enabling easy data sharing over a wide area network (WAN) or the Internet, significantly reducing the cost of broadband rental for users.

2. Simplifies operational processes with intelligent and efficient design, achieving transparent and low-cost enterprise management.

3. Comprehensive business functions covering procurement, sales, production, and various cash flows. Automatically generates vouchers that link directly to accounts receivable, payable, cash/bank accounts, and general ledgers, fully integrating all processes with the company’s financial systems.

4. Diverse warehouse management supporting multiple warehouses for inbound, outbound, transfer, and inventory operations.

6. A simple one-click closing process that takes only a minute to automatically close the books at the end of the fiscal period, transferring balances, calculating costs, and generating sales reports, inventory reports, and financial statements.

7. Smart tracking of company and individual profitability, sales volumes, return quantities, and profit margin comparisons. Provides various product reports, performance reports, sales reports, collection reports, and overall enterprise reports.

8. Full support for standard and customizable document formats, along with flexible printing options: standard carbon copy printing, laser multi-part printing, printed output, and print prohibition settings.

9. Powerful data import functionality supporting the import of goods and client information from Excel files, reducing the workload for initial data entry.

10. Inventory warning system for maximum and minimum stock levels.

11. Automatic management of customer overdue payment alerts.

12. Customizable warning settings for product outflow quantities.

13. System-based automatic management of product pricing limits.

14. Tracking of inbound and outbound product pricing.

15. User-defined software interface functionality for a more user-friendly experience.

16. Three-tier architecture in the network version ensures stable software operation and secure data management.

17. Unique connectivity via standard broadband (like ADSL) to remote databases, enabling easy data sharing over WAN (Internet), saving significant costs on broadband rentals.
