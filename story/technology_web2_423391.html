The Superb has dimensions of 4,838 mm in length, 1,817 mm in width, and 1,462 mm in height. Its exterior and interior design style is both elegant and fashionable. Many lines inside and outside the vehicle clearly show the passionate and energetic side of the Superb. The wooden trim on the dashboard reflects the owner's nobility and elegance. With Skoda's consistently excellent craftsmanship, it can meet the needs of consumers of different age groups.

In terms of power, the Superb is equipped with three engines: 1.4TTSI, 1.8TSI, and 2.0TSI, featuring high torque output at low RPMs and high power characteristics. Except for the 1.8TSI manual intelligent edition which uses a 5-speed manual transmission, all other models use the Tiptronic 6-speed automatic/manual integrated transmission. Additionally, the Superb is generously equipped with standard features such as driver and passenger airbags, seatbelt reminder, ABS+EBD, etc. Higher-end models also come with front-row side airbags, curtain airbags, ESP, and other high-end safety configurations.
