Introduction of highlights

Jiang Wen's fourth directorial work "Let the Bullets Fly" recently started filming in Huairou, Beijing. Although the film has been under closed-door shooting, the media still pursued it relentlessly yesterday, leaking the situation on the set.

After the previous shooting in the deep mountains, "Let the Bullets Fly" recently moved to a certain film base in Huairou to continue shooting. At the shooting site, the vehicles of the crew were marked with the words "Fire Clouds". However, according to the explanation of the production team later, "Fire Clouds" was the film name when it was first reported to the Film Bureau, and the current film name is still "Let the Bullets Fly".

In the film, the character played by Jiang Wen and the character played by Zhou Runfa will be two different extremes, while the swindler played by Ge You will be the balancing role between these two.

"Let the Bullets Fly" tells an interesting story about bandits buying official positions during the Beiyang warlord era. Therefore, it is not a revenge battle as previously rumored by outsiders, but a story full of black humor and absurdity.

As for another super star in the film, Zhou Runfa, he plays a local tyrant who returned from overseas and is very influential locally.

Bandit VS Swindler VS Local Tyrant

At the shooting site, the media first waited for the lead actor and director Jiang Wen. He had short hair stubble, and two thick little mustaches added a rugged and heroic feel to him. In the film, Jiang Wen will play the role of a bandit leader who kills people, sets fires, and actively recruits others.

After changing into a long robe and mandarin jacket, Ge You vividly presented the image of a landlord. However, according to the production team, the character played by Ge You in the film is not a landlord who has a grudge against the bandit chief Jiang Wen, but a swindler.
