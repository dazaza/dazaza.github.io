Tissot Couturier Series Mike Owen 2011 World Time Limited Edition

by longtime9t on 2012-03-05 14:45:46

The 2011 Tissot Couturier Mike Owen World Time Limited Edition watch serves as a testament that every man of taste should own a timepiece that combines unique style with performance. This watch features an inspired design, high-quality materials with exceptional texture, and is crafted using the most precise watchmaking techniques. The design incorporates black, white, and red – the lucky colors of Manchester United's home and away kits. On the back cover of the watch, it bears the signature of this super striker, Mike Owen, along with his lucky number "7". The Tissot Couturier Mike Owen 2011 World Time Limited Edition is globally limited to 4,999 pieces, featuring a rubber strap for men.

Every second on the pitch is precious, and every detail of this high-precision product matches the demands of football. One of the chronograph dials is outlined in bold black, resembling a fast-spinning football. Another dial represents corresponding time zones, marked in red, symbolizing the global lifestyle attitude of Tissot’s ambassador. Every scale on the Tissot Couturier Mike Owen 2011 World Time Limited Edition highlights the pursuit of ultimate details. The screen-printed back cover, the second hand, and the stitching on the elegant black leather strap all feature a dynamic and generous red color. This watch will become the best personal accessory for followers of Tissot ambassador Mike Owen, showcasing each individual's distinct style.

The back cover of the Tissot Couturier Mike Owen 2011 World Time Limited Edition has a unique design. The new movement inside is full of dynamic design elements. The innovative world-time movement, Swiss-made G10.961 caliber, can time a second time zone with ease in setting and reading. At the ten o'clock position on the dial, there is a disk with 24-hour graduations closely linked to the hour and minute hands on the dial. According to the design of the G10.211 caliber, the chronograph disk at the two o'clock position has two hands representing the hours and minutes elapsed by the chronograph, capable of continuous timing up to 12 hours with a precision of one second. People like Mike Owen, who have a global consciousness, can set and reset the GMT function by pressing the button in the clockwise or counterclockwise direction to meet their personal timing needs. Each brief press represents 10 minutes, while a long press represents an hour. With this chronograph product, extra time will no longer be exclusive to football.

**Technical Specifications:**

- Scratch-resistant sapphire crystal

- 316L stainless steel case

- Water-resistant to 100 meters (10 bar)

- Leather strap with push-button folding clasp

**Suggested Retail Price:** RMB 4,050

Tissot Ambassador Mike Owen wears this custom-designed limited edition watch.
