Traditional Chinese medicine has long used Huai yam as a medicinal ingredient with the reputation of being one of the finest herbs. Huai yam, also known as Shan Yao or Huai Shan Yao, has a neutral nature and a sweet taste. Its tubers are rich in various essential amino acids, proteins, and starches, containing mucus, allantoin, choline, fiber, fats, vitamins A, B2, C, and minerals such as calcium, phosphorus, iron, and iodine, providing the body with many necessary nutrients. Its most distinctive feature is its ability to supply the body with a large amount of mucus protein. This is a polysaccharide protein that has special health benefits for the human body, preventing fat deposition in the cardiovascular system, maintaining vascular elasticity, preventing premature occurrence of arteriosclerosis, reducing subcutaneous fat deposition, and avoiding obesity. Therefore, Huai yam is an ideal food for weight loss and slimming.

Below are several Huai yam recipes that allow beauty-conscious ladies to enjoy delicious flavors while reducing weight and increasing bust size! Huai Yam Apple Juice

1. Huai Yam Apple Juice

Ingredients: 15g Huai yam powder, 1 apple, 250cc fresh milk

Method: Peel and cut the apple into small pieces; mix Huai yam powder, apple pieces, and fresh milk in a blender and blend until smooth before drinking.

2. Huai Yam Sesame Paste

Ingredients: 20g Huai yam powder, 120g sesame powder, 250g fresh milk, appropriate amount of rock sugar

Method: Mix Huai yam powder with black sesame powder and fresh milk, cook on low heat until thickened, then add rock sugar before consuming.

3. Huai Yam Honey Tea

Ingredients: 20g Huai yam powder, honey (amount according to personal preference)

Method: Cook 20g Huai yam powder on low heat for about 3 minutes, turn off the heat and let it sit for 5 minutes, then add honey before drinking.

4. Fresh Huai Yam Stir-fried Chicken Slices

Ingredients: 100g chicken breast meat, 50g Huai yam, cooking wine, salt, monosodium glutamate, egg white, dry cornstarch (appropriate amount)

Method: Clean the chicken breast and remove tendons, slice thinly on the bias, marinate with cooking wine, salt, monosodium glutamate, egg white, and dry cornstarch. Peel and cut Huai yam into rhombus-shaped slices. Heat a wok, coat with oil, add more oil until it reaches three-tenths hot, gently stir in chicken slices, then add Huai yam slices until cooked, strain out. Leave a little oil in the pot, stir-fry scallion sections briefly, add chicken slices and Huai yam slices, water, seasonings, adjust flavor, thicken with wet cornstarch, drizzle with bright oil, toss a few times, and serve.

5. Burdock Huai Yam Red Date Soup

Ingredients: 30g burdock root, 30g Huai yam, 20g red dates

Method: Wash Huai yam, peel and cut into chunks; clean burdock root, scrape off the outer skin with the back of a knife, and cut into thick diagonal slices; wash red dates, flatten and remove seeds; bring clear water in a clay pot to a boil, add all ingredients, boil on high heat for 20 minutes, then simmer for an hour and a half, season with salt, and serve.

6. Arm-sculpting and Bust-enhancing Porridge

Ingredients: 50g Job's tears, 15g wolfberry, 50g Huai yam, 10g kudzu root

Method: Place all ingredients in the inner pot of an electric rice cooker, add two bowls of water, place two bowls of water in the outer pot, and cook until the rice cooker switches off, ready to eat.

7. Beautiful Bust Huai Yam Milk

Ingredients: One bottle of yogurt, appropriate amount of Huai yam powder

Method: Mix Huai yam powder with yogurt in a cup and stir evenly before eating.
