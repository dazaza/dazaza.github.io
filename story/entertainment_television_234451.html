Sixty years ago, a mountain girl Huang Chunxiang (Tan Aizhen) accidentally saved Yan Wangcai (Ding Qiang), who had gone up the mountain to collect herbs and fainted after mistakenly triggering a trap, with Eucommia leaves. Chunxiang kept the watch that Wangcai left her, guarding it for six decades, like a dream, always hoping that one day Wangcai would come back to find her. This deep affection moved her granddaughter Xie Fu'an (Ruby Lin) immensely...

「If only I could have such a beautiful first love, it would be wonderful...」

The chairman of the world's largest Chinese medicine group 'Babao Tang Pharmaceutical Group', Yan Yungao, was in a coma due to a car accident, lying weakly in an ambulance rushing through the country roads to the hospital. Soon after, his descendants, who were like wolves waiting for him to die so they could inherit his fortune, arrived one by one; seeing Yungao in critical condition, only his grandson Yan Yang (Xiu Jieqie), who had no ambition, genuinely felt sad, while the others were all happy. Even when the doctor tried to save Yungao, they strongly opposed it. "Let father (grandfather) go peacefully!" With a strong sense of justice, Fu'an decided not to let Yungao's descendants succeed, so she sneaked into the emergency room and saved Yungao with Eucommia leaves. Smelling the familiar scent, Yungao faintly woke up, vaguely seeing Fu'an's face, recalling a familiar face, he weakly called out:

"Chunxiang?"
