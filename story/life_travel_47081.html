Everyone is talking | From taking transportation to eating and drinking, the top ten Chinese New Year "tortures" for Chinese people

by easyba on 2008-02-04 16:50:04

Torture No. 1: Taking transportation. Riding the train is highly recommended. After standing in line for an entire morning, the ticket agent might tell you that there are no more tickets available; unexpectedly, you may "successfully" buy a ticket from a scalper at twice or even multiple times the original price; to catch this train, you have come from a remote mountain village hundreds of miles away, waited in the cold rain and wind, without even having time to go to the bathroom or eat a meal; finally, the most feared moment has arrived - you are "luckily" only squeezed out of one shoe (some people get injured, some even die), but manage to squeeze into the carriage, only to find that breathing is even harder than finding a place to stand...

Riding the bus or car is not much better. At this point, you must not still consider yourself a cultured or civilized person, or respond to Premier Wen's call to "look up at the stars." Being able to squeeze on is already considered lucky.

As for taking a taxi, you should be prepared for three possibilities. First, there are no taxis available, as they are all full of passengers; second, being ripped off, where the price is determined by the driver, and if you want to complain, go ahead, as long as you have the patience to make the call, or don't mind paying the higher phone bill; third, being refused service, sorry, who asked you to want to go somewhere the driver doesn't want to go?

Torture No. 2: Gatherings. Class reunions, hometown gatherings, family reunions - don't forget, even if you had a bad year, this is not the time to talk about it, and you still need to bring out your best cigarettes. Of course, those suddenly wealthy strange rich uncles and officials, the psychological gap you feel is a test of your mental strength; as for the once beautiful girls who are now dark, fat, and vulgar, you don't need to feel sad, just check if you brought a mirror?
