Yuping Scenic Area

by jj0505 on 2012-02-20 17:11:20

The Yubing scenic area of Mount Huangshan is centered on the Yubing Building, with the Lianhua Peak and Tiandu Peak as its main body. What people usually refer to as the "front mountain" refers to this scenic area. Along the way, there are marvelous sights such as the "Penglai Three Islands," "Hundred Steps Cloudy Ladder," "A Line in the Sky," "New A Line in the Sky," and the "Ao Fish Cave."

The Yubing Building is located between Tiandu and Lianhua peaks. Here, almost all the wonders of Mount Huangshan are gathered, hence it's known as the best place in Mount Huangshan, famous both at home and abroad. The renowned guest-welcoming pine stands on the left side of the Yubing Building, while the guest-seeing-off pine is on the right. In front of the building are the guest-accompanying pine and Wenshu Platform, and behind the building is the Yubing Peak. The famous "Yubing Reclining Buddha" lies on top of the peak, with its head on the left and feet on the right, vividly lifelike. On the peak stone, there is Mao Zedong's calligraphy, "The country's scenery is so enchanting." On the eastern stone wall of the building, there are inscriptions of Zhu De's "scenery like a painting" and Liu Bocheng's poem "Traveling Mount Huangshan with Old Comrades-in-Arms in Southern Anhui during the Anti-Japanese War": "The anti-Japanese army once went north, how much they longed for rainbows in the drought. Mount Huang has always been covered by clouds, since then there have been more rains."

The Tiandu Peak is located south of the Yubing Peak, about one kilometer away. It is the most perilous place among the three main peaks of Mount Huangshan, with an altitude of 1830 meters. An ancient poem praised it: "No matter how many of the five sacred mountains you've visited, seeing Tiandu still makes you marvel." The road up Tiandu is extremely dangerous. In recent years, after continuous development by the constructors, the road to climb the peak has become thrilling but not dangerous. There is a stone carving at the top of Tiandu Peak saying "Reached the summit," making people feel like "When the sea ends, the sky is the shore; when I climb to the top of the mountain, I am the peak."

The Lianhua Peak, located north of the Yubing Building, is the highest peak of Mount Huangshan, with an altitude of 1864.8 meters. It is steep and towering, majestic and grand, resembling a blooming lotus flower, hence its name. From Lianhua Ridge to the top of Lianhua Peak is about 1.5 kilometers. This section of the road is called Lianhua Geng. Along the way, there are famous pines of Mount Huangshan such as Feilong Pine and Hanging Pine, as well as Mount Huangshan azaleas. The top of Lianhua Peak is about ten square meters. In the middle, there is a Xiangsha Well. Standing on the peak and looking around, thousands of peaks compete in beauty, and tens of thousands of valleys are filled with mist. On a clear day, you can see Mount Tianmu to the east, Mount Lu to the west, and Mount Jiuhua to the north. After the rain, looking out over the eight sides of the sea of clouds is even more spectacular.

Going down from Lianhua Peak, passing through the Turtle and Snake rocks, Hundred Steps Cloudy Ladder, and through the Ao Fish Cave, you arrive at the Ao Fish Peak. This peak is 1780 meters high, named after its shape. That huge and lofty whale head seems to have the momentum of swallowing heaven and earth. Below the Ao Fish Peak is the Tianhai. Tianhai is located among the front, back, east, and west seas of Mount Huangshan, at the center of Mount Huangshan. In this high-altitude basin of 1750 meters, there grow many plant species rare both domestically and internationally. The Mount Huangshan garden department has used the unique climate conditions of Tianhai to create the Tianhai Alpine Botanical Garden. Near Tianhai, there are famous scenic spots such as the Heart of the Sea Pavilion and Phoenix Pine.
