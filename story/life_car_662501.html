Autumn has arrived, and it's time to give your car a tune-up. Just like people, cars need regular "check-ups". Every seasonal change is a good opportunity for vehicle inspection and maintenance. As we enter early autumn in October, after a summer of sun exposure and rain, it's also time to give your car a comprehensive maintenance check. So, what are the general items checked during an autumn car inspection? And how should maintenance be carried out? A. Check the oil, water, and electricity in the engine compartment

The oils in the engine compartment include brake fluid, power steering pump oil, transmission oil, etc. Here, special attention must be given to owners who use their cars less frequently. They should regularly check the condition of each type of oil based on usage time to ensure timely replacement. At the same time, you should also check the water levels in the radiator, windshield washer fluid, antifreeze, etc., to prevent them from deteriorating. Additionally, pay attention to checking the waterproof sealing effects and tightness of each connector to ensure normal waterproof sealing functions and proper contact of connectors. On the other hand, to ensure the safety of the vehicle, any connectors that come into contact with water should be unplugged and treated for rust prevention.

Special reminder: This check is indispensable and must not be overlooked.

B. Car paint maintenance

In summer, the sunlight is intense and temperatures are high. If the car is exposed to the sun for long periods, the body and paint will be damaged. Furthermore, acidic substances in the rain can also harm the car's paint surface. If left untreated over time, the paint on your beloved car will lose its luster. Meanwhile, dust and mud from the air and roads can easily stick to the car body along with the rain, making the car body dirty. Therefore, as we enter autumn, the first step in maintenance is to polish and wax the car, restoring its new and shiny appearance.

Special reminder: Many car owners neglect the maintenance of rubber products on the doors when maintaining the exterior of the car. After enduring the scorching summer sun and rain, the rubber parts on the exterior of the car will also age to varying degrees. Therefore, while maintaining the car body, attention should also be paid to the maintenance and replacement of rubber parts.

It is necessary to carry out paint maintenance during seasonal changes.

C. Clean the radiator condenser net

Summer is hot and prone to heavy rain. Not only does the use of air conditioning increase, but many damp dust particles, cotton fibers, leaves, and other impurities also accumulate on the radiator condenser net. This affects two aspects: one is the easy breeding of bacteria; the second is the impact on the radiator and condenser's heat dissipation function. To eliminate these effects, thorough cleaning is all that's needed. Here, we remind all car owners that radiator fins are very easy to deform, so there are certain requirements for the force and angle used during cleaning. It is recommended to have professionals at car maintenance and repair shops clean it. To thoroughly remove bacteria and odors inside the car, it is best to replace the car's air conditioning filter at the end of summer to increase the cooling efficiency and improve riding comfort.

Special reminder: Make sure to have professionals clean it.

D. Pay attention to sterilization

In the hot summer, car owners generally use air conditioning and rarely open the windows. In this case, the car's interior will have fewer opportunities to come into contact with fresh air. At this point, bacteria produced by the air conditioner will gather and accumulate inside the car cabin and breed mold, unable to be discharged. Therefore, during autumn car maintenance, it is suggested to clean all hygienic dead corners and perform ozone sterilization and photocatalytic mildew removal for thorough cleaning.

Notably, if sweat comes into contact with leather seats, it is very easy for bacteria to grow. Owners should take their cars to specialized stores for sterilization treatment. If it's a velvet seat cover, dry cleaning is sufficient.

Special reminder: Try to go to reputable stores for cleaning to ensure quality.

E. Chassis rust removal and tire checks

For autumn car chassis maintenance, the focus is on rust removal and tire checks. On one hand, metal materials in the chassis, after being exposed to sunlight and coming into contact with rainwater, are prone to rust, affecting driving safety. Therefore, it is necessary to treat the chassis for rust and check for scratches, missing screws, and other issues. Additionally, the braking system should be comprehensively inspected and undergo corresponding cleaning, lubrication, rust removal, and other treatments to ensure system performance remains normal.

Moreover, tire inspections and replacements are also very important. After a season of use, tires may experience wear, leaks, reduced tire pressure, and other problems. At this point, the car should be taken to professional automotive service stores or repair stations for testing, repairs, or replacements.

By following the above points, your beloved car will stay youthful and never fade.
