Web2.0Share Weekly: Ffan.com, Jimi, Yike, Shuaji Spirit, etc.

by ybcz0519 on 2011-08-22 05:21:19

Web2.0Share Weekly is a featured column of Tianya Haige. Every week, it selects the latest excellent startups in China to share with everyone. Startups can submit their information through Weibo: @web20share or email: watsonxu{at}web20share.com.

Ao Ao Tang - Entertainment Q&A Platform: Ao Ao Tang provides rapid, comprehensive, and authoritative discussions on entertainment topics, including celebrities, movies, TV shows, music, variety shows, reviews, and more.

Link: http://www.ootang.cn/

Jimi: Your portable shopping and leisure guide. Whether it's a mall sale, a discount event from your favorite brand, or the latest news from nearby specialty stores, you can find everything at a glance in the Jimi app. Currently available for iPhone and Android.

Link: http://www.jimi.com/

Kuai Tuo: A foolproof data collection and download tool developed for Windows. It helps you collect various URLs, regular webpages, encrypted pages, locked images, songs, FLASH files, software, articles, novels, comics, and more with just a drag-and-drop action. All content can be permanently saved on your personal private cloud storage.

Link: http://www.iuse.com.cn/index.html

Yikuer Micro Market: Unlike general group-buying platforms, Yikuer allows users to pay a portion of the price upfront and settle the remaining amount after enjoying the service. Yikuer Micro Market is a subsidiary of specialdeals.com in China.

Link: http://www.yikuair.com/

YY Database Backup: Users registered on the YY Backup platform can manage their mobile phones by storing contacts, calendars, text messages, call records, and browser bookmarks into a secure account provided by the YY Backup platform. Currently available for Android.

Link: http://www.yybackup.com/

Shua Ji Spirit: Shua Ji Spirit is the debut work of the Ours team. Our goal is to make everyone understand how to flash and install systems easily with truly one-click worry-free installation.

Link: http://shuame.com/

We Collect: A free online bookmarking application based on Sina Weibo APP that allows users to quickly store their favorite webpages. It offers content extraction for easy mobile reading. The feature has been integrated into the Sina Weibo iPhone client.

Link: http://mark.appsina.com/

I Start: Wokaishi.com is an innovative website promoting a healthy lifestyle concept. It customizes fitness and healthy eating plans for users. "I Start" aims to deepen users' understanding of health and lead the public towards a healthier life. The main site includes three parts: social network, health information, and fitness database.

Link: http://www.wokaishi.com/

Fanfan recently launched its official website. Fanfan is a product guidance platform dedicated to providing the most comprehensive product information, the latest discount information, and valuable reviews, shares, and purchase confirmations. According to its founder @bian (former vice president of Shanda Online, Bian Jiang), this is "just a bare version, merely running through the entire process," with iterations and improvements planned over the next three months.

Link: http://www.fanfan.com/

Saturday Date - A dating website helping people in the same city who mutually like each other go on dates: To date someone you like, first mark them on the map. If the other person also chooses you, the website will send a notification to both parties. For more stranger dating services, check here.

Link: http://www.lovesat.com/

Desktoday is a practical free Mac application that organizes the files on your Mac desktop daily into folders named by date. It can exclude certain items that need to remain on the desktop without being organized into folders.

Link: http://www.hanken.co.uk/desktoday/
