by fkfykpllj on 2012-02-22 15:04:25

**November 15 News**: After Italy's sovereign debt auction, the country's borrowing costs rose. The market is concerned about whether European leaders have the ability to control the debt crisis. On Monday, the three major US stock indexes fell slightly.

At 5:00 AM Beijing Time on November 15, 2011 (4:00 PM Eastern Time on November 14), the Dow Jones Industrial Average fell 74.62 points, or 0.61%, closing at 12,079.06; the Nasdaq Composite Index fell 21.53 points, or 0.80%, closing at 2,657.22; and the S&P 500 Index fell 12.04 points, or 0.95%, closing at 1,251.81.
