Henan Mine Machinery Equipment Manufacturing Co., Ltd. (formerly known as the State-owned Henan Mine Machinery Factory) is a joint-stock enterprise, established in 1958 and reorganized in September 2003. It is located in Zhengzhou City, Henan Province, to the north of the picturesque Yellow River Geological Park, a national 5A-level scenic area - Yellow River Tourist Area, with two factory areas on Nanyang Road and in the Development Zone, covering an area of 120,000 square meters. There are currently 1,132 employees, including 266 college graduates and professional technical personnel. The company owns large-scale processing equipment such as a 6.3m vertical lathe, an 8m gear hobbing machine, a C6050 cylinder lathe, a Y9650 gear milling machine, a WD160 floor-type boring and milling machine, a T6216 boring machine, a CNC-CG4000A numerical control cutting machine, and an HJ-5 welding center. Henan Mine Machinery Equipment Manufacturing Co., Ltd. has three subsidiaries: Zhengzhou Zhongbang Machine Engineering Installation Branch, Zhengzhou Zhongbang Machine Engineering Spare Parts Branch, and Zhengzhou Zhongbang Machine Engineering Casting Branch, forming a diversified large company mainly based on "cement, mineral selection, metallurgy, coal washing, hoist, winch". The company produces: a complete set of mineral selection equipment with a capacity of 3 million tons/year; cement complete sets of equipment below 3,000 tons/day; a complete set of coal washing equipment with a capacity of 3 million tons/year; 800,000 tons of pelletized ore; metal magnesium complete sets of equipment. The "Gongnong" brand crusher series produced by our company has won the high-quality product award of the Ministry of Machinery Industry, the first prize in the national industry evaluation, and was rated as an excellent product by the Provincial Quality Supervision Bureau Information Center in 2005. The company as a whole passed the ISO9001:2000 quality system certification. It is an excellent trustworthy enterprise in Henan Province and a key industrial enterprise in Zhengzhou City. The company's goal is to achieve a production value of 250 million yuan in 2008. The company's philosophy is "Casting Gongnong Brand, Creating a Century-old Enterprise". The business philosophy is "People-oriented, Honest Service, Science and Technology for Enterprise". In future development, our company will fully play its technical advantages according to market demand, establish good technical cooperation relationships with domestic research institutes and colleges and universities, continuously introduce new technologies and processes, always maintain strong market competitiveness, sincerely cooperate with friends at home and abroad, recruit talents, and jointly create a bright future for the company. Our factory has complete modern mechanical processing equipment, advanced design, process outfitting, and R&D technical forces for new products, and can also manufacture various non-standard equipment according to user requirements.
