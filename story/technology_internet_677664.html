Analysis of the Current Situation of Foreign Trade Export and Overseas Promotion

by yahoobing on 2012-02-27 08:54:23

Most of China's foreign trade enterprises are still in a state of blindly following trends when it comes to overseas promotion. Without the reference of objective and scientific opinions from professionals, they impulsively make investment decisions. Therefore, our foreign trade companies should consider choosing appropriate methods to carry out online marketing.

I believe that the foreign trade salespeople, managers, or decision-makers of foreign trade companies have all received promotional calls for online promotion, online marketing, website construction, overseas promotion, trade platform promotion, search engine promotion, yellow page promotion, and industry website promotion, whether paid or free. While being deeply [harassed] by these telemarketers, they also worry about the lack of companies that provide professional foreign trade online marketing services from the perspective of corporate strategic development and offer full-service support for the overseas online promotion of export enterprises. The following points are what a professional-level foreign trade online marketing consultant should do. Companies can also compare which aspects the e-commerce company providing internet services at the time has achieved for them, and whether they should consider replacing it with a more professional service provider.

The common problems in the online marketing process of domestic export enterprises mainly include the following three:

First, over-reliance on B2B trading platforms leads to a single channel.

Nowadays, domestic export enterprises have become quite serious in their over-reliance on B2B platforms. Some enterprises invest as much as 500,000 yuan in a B2B platform, putting all their promotion costs and hopes on this platform, hoping to open up their overseas sales channels through B2B websites. This is indeed an unwise approach. Because many domestic B2B platforms now have insufficient product classification and lack obvious professionalism.

Have our export enterprises analyzed who would look for products on B2B platforms like Alibaba, and what kind of products they are looking for? It can be said with certainty that individual consumers generally will not go to B2B platforms like Alibaba to look for products. Therefore, customers visiting Alibaba are all business customers. However, large enterprises probably won't come because they all have relatively fixed supplier systems, and it's difficult for general small and medium-sized enterprises to enter their supplier lists.

Therefore, most of the buyers looking for products on B2B websites are small and medium-sized buyers abroad. What kind of products do these small and medium-sized buyers hope to find? Clearly, because they are at a disadvantage in competition, these small and medium-sized buyers come to B2B platforms like Alibaba to find low-priced products provided by small and medium-sized enterprises, take them back to their own countries to compete with other companies, bypass importers or agents, and sell products at low prices.

Although we can sell our products abroad through these small and medium-sized buyers, their loyalty is very low, and they can easily be taken away by other manufacturers, failing to become a stable overseas sales channel. Moreover, there are countless factories on B2B websites, and customers will negotiate with multiple suppliers, repeatedly haggle, and even if a deal is made, the profit is pitifully low. But those B2B platforms keep raising prices, leaving our enterprises in a dilemma of doing or not doing, which will eventually cause Chinese export enterprises to lose their core competitiveness in online marketing in the long run.

Second, many export enterprises do not pay enough attention to the construction and promotion of their own websites.

An enterprise's own website is the center of its online marketing, while search engines, trading platforms, and other promotions are carried out around the enterprise website. However, many enterprises neglect the construction and promotion of their own websites, causing them to fail to play any role.

Third, lack of effective monitoring of promotion effects.

Many enterprises evaluate the effectiveness of promotions based on how many orders have been completed. We know that online marketing is a long-term project, and the evaluation of promotion effectiveness needs to be assessed from a more comprehensive perspective, including the situation of website construction, search engine performance, buyer visits, valid inquiries, and transaction volumes. Some enterprises may abandon or stop promotions due to incorrect promotion methods, resulting in no orders or very few orders in the short term, leading to the loss of original promotion resources.

The Role of Professional Foreign Trade Online Marketing Consultants

What kind of online marketing consultants do export enterprises need, and what aspects should online marketing companies help enterprises improve their online marketing level?

First, foreign trade online marketing consultants can help foreign trade enterprises analyze and summarize their existing online marketing strategies. Understand the customer's past understanding and practices regarding online marketing, the invested funds, manpower, and returns obtained. Specifically, it includes the starting point of the enterprise's website construction, what considerations were taken into account when building the website, whether there are dedicated personnel responsible for online promotion, what network advertisements or marketing software the enterprise has purchased, what services from which service providers the enterprise uses, as well as their specific service models and cost situations.

Second, a good foreign trade online marketing consultant can help foreign trade enterprises diagnose and analyze their websites. Currently, more than 90% of enterprise websites have significant problems in marketing functions, such as a considerable number of website production companies adding a large amount of Flash animation to please clients, which is actually undesirable. Although Flash appears visually appealing, it provides a poor user experience for visitors.

Currently, the servers of the vast majority of small and medium-sized enterprise websites are located domestically, so when foreign users visit, the speed will be affected. Professional-level foreign trade online marketing consultants need to help enterprises analyze their websites, including analyzing issues related to both customer friendliness and search engine friendliness, and analyzing the impact of these issues to help enterprises improve the operational level and utilization rate of their websites.

In addition, professional foreign trade marketing consultants can also help foreign trade enterprises conduct competitor analysis, helping enterprises analyze the online marketing methods of specific industry competitors, estimate their advertising investments and results, and compare them to identify areas worth learning from and problems to avoid. Competitor analysis also includes comparisons and analyses of both parties' websites as well as comparative analyses of promotion channels.

Foreign trade online marketing consultants can also help foreign trade enterprises formulate marketing strategies and select advertisements or service providers. Based on the analysis of the enterprise's product situation, discuss suitable online marketing strategies with the client, including search engine optimization, online advertising placement, online information release, and registration on trading platforms. During discussions, it is necessary to help clients analyze the advantages and disadvantages of various online marketing tools and provide corresponding suggestions for the client to decide. At the same time, based on their own experience, help enterprises choose service providers, even including hints about discount prices for certain services.

Besides, foreign trade marketing consultants should also do the following four tasks:

Guide and train relevant employees of foreign trade enterprises. Understand the work methods of employees related to the enterprise's online marketing and conduct targeted analyses. Provide them with professional training to enhance work efficiency. For enterprises, theoretical discussions should not be emphasized; instead, practical operations should be focused on, including how to choose good online marketing platforms, what methods can be used for online promotion, how to post information, and simple search engine optimization techniques.

Help foreign trade enterprises maintain their websites. Many enterprises do not have professional network administrators, or their staff do not know how to maintain websites, so they need guidance from online marketing consultants, even regular checks and maintenance of websites, including simple content updates and information modifications.

Track and maintain the online marketing effects of foreign trade enterprises, including helping enterprises conduct traffic statistics analysis and answering daily online promotion questions.

According to the technical development of foreign trade online marketing, recommend new tools and methods to customers, passing on the latest concepts or tools and methods to ensure that the enterprise's online marketing stays ahead of the industry and leads among peers.

At this point, a professional advisory team undoubtedly can provide many practical experiences and help to export enterprises, allowing enterprises to achieve the best results with the smallest promotion budget, improve return on investment and overseas market share, and stand out from competing peers.

Foreign trade companies cannot simply follow trends when doing online marketing anymore, whether promoting B2B websites, bidding rankings, or building websites, without fully utilizing the true intelligence of online marketing. Online marketing is just part of traditional enterprise marketing, and one must learn to effectively utilize various means of online marketing. Each method must be carefully chosen based on the company’s specific situation, using one or several comprehensively to achieve good results.

At this point, a professional advisory team undoubtedly can provide many practical experiences and help to export enterprises, allowing enterprises to achieve the best results with the smallest promotion budget, improve return on investment and overseas market share, and stand out from competing peers.
