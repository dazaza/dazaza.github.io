Ghost Eye Bride - A Perfect Tribute to Humanity and Ghostliness

"The Ghost Eye Bride" is written in a narrative style that is as expansive as the landscape paintings of Jiangnan. The beauty of its language not only brings tears but also much reflection. The content is rich and peculiar, and the author vividly creates Lan Ruoxi, a character born with ghost vision who is kind and well-behaved. Good people always hope for all things in the world to be good and kind, hoping their spiritual powers can save others.

——Xie Fei (a well-known horror novelist)

"Does an angel redeem the devil, or does the devil corrupt the angel? Is it love or lust?" I greatly admire how Qingniao analyzes sex; it's very real and natural. I believe many other writers would agree. It's like a girl living long-term in a bustling city being suddenly taken to a field full of green grass, becoming so entranced she forgets to return, following the protagonist into strange realms time and again...

——Ziwei Zhujin (Member of Shandong Writers Association, Director of Shandong Essay Society)

"I enjoy 'The Ghost Eye Bride' not just because of its allure, but also due to Qingniao’s intricate yet textured language and the pure heart behind it."

——Xiaoshou Bingliang (Author of "Loving My Child's Father")

"The Ghost Eye Bride" is a novel that one cannot put down. Unconsciously captivated by the plot, you follow the emotions and feelings of the protagonist, perceiving the unknown world and guessing what will happen next. The infinite imaginative space provided by this novel is the greatest enjoyment for readers. Qingniao is someone who knows how to discover life and beauty. Having seen her photographic works, where her unique perspective captures various aspects of life, I believe she will bring us even more wonders.

——Piao Ruochen (Radio Host)

"The Ghost Eye Bride" is Qingniao's first suspenseful long-form work. With a tightly woven and thrilling plot, it draws the reader in as if they were experiencing it firsthand, filled with eerie sensations. Her writing is delicate yet warm, showing her keen observation of life's details, truly making it a rare masterpiece. The character Lan Ruoxi, a kind yet mysterious girl, is depicted vividly, serving as a perfect tribute to humanity and ghostliness, indeed a rare masterpiece.

——Ye Xiaotang (Author of "The End of Flower Affairs")
