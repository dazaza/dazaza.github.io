I. The current situation of home water treatment equipment:

Currently in the world, the home water treatment equipment system generally needs to install at least: pre-filter, central softener and central purifier.

1. In order to completely remove water alkali and reduce water hardness, a softener must be installed. The softener is a water treatment device that removes calcium and magnesium ions (i.e., water alkali) from water through ion exchange resin and reduces water hardness. Softened water belongs to domestic water, which can extend the service life of equipment such as water heaters, showers, and underfloor heating; make clothes fluffy and soft after washing; avoid stains, marks, and dirt on sanitary ware, bathtubs, and showers.

2. In order to make tap water clean and healthy, and remove residual chlorine and various harmful substances, a purifier must be installed. Moreover, softened water cannot be drunk directly - it cannot be used for cooking, washing vegetables, or making hot water, and further purification is required. Therefore, if a softener is installed, a water purification device must also be installed. More importantly, in order to avoid the harm of residual chlorine and harmful substances to the skin during showering and washing, shower water also needs to be purified. Therefore, it is best to use a central purifier for the whole house.

3. In order to ensure the normal operation and service life of the softener and purifier, a "pre-filter" must be installed in front of the softener, which can remove iron rust, mud sand, suspended particles and other large particle harmful substances.

Therefore, to achieve full-house water purification, at least three devices need to be installed: pre-filter, central softener, and central purifier.
