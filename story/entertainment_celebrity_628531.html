The movie *Eight Stars to a Happy Reunion* tells the story of several men coming to the aid of women who are either in a career slump or suffering from emotional trauma, all under the premise of receiving a sincere hug as their reward. Among them, lawyer Eric Tsang whose family has failed and spoiled rich girl Yang Mi, never-popular pop singer Donnie Yen and washed-up idol Sandra Ng who was kicked out of her house, construction site worker Louis Koo who moonlights as a model and female photographer Kelly Chen who seeks male charm, love-novel writer Wong Cho-wan who doesn't believe in love and blind woman Jill Xin who has never been in love. The process of these four pairs of vastly different men and women "help-help-hug" is filled with unexpected twists and bursts of humor.

As a new member of the "Happy Event" series, mainland China's popular actress Yang Mi plays a "quasi" rich girl in this film who is expected to inherit a 6 billion yuan fortune. In order to obtain this huge wealth, this "fake heiress" has to team up with her cheap dad Eric Tsang, whom she found on the "Happy Reunion" website, and use extremely unreliable methods to find an extremely reliable "true-hearted person". "To create a comedic feel, Yang Mi dares to try any costume, whether it be dressing as a dancer, a masked character, or even a mummy." Regarding Yang Mi's performance in the film, her co-star Eric Tsang said: "She is so beautiful, of course she can't hide it. Every father hopes that their daughter is beautiful. Most importantly, this 'daughter' is especially good at making me happy. Working with her really stimulates my desire to perform. On the set, she has already made me laugh uncontrollably. After the audience sees it, they will surely be laughing until they fall over."

Eric Tsang expressed that this time, *Eight Stars to a Happy Reunion* has spent a large amount of money.
