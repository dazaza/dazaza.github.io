Group buying should return to local services

by beetgk2958 on 2012-03-02 13:23:31

Because of the low gross profit margin and high market promotion cost, coupled with strong competition in the market and high labor costs, all group buying websites across the country are currently at a loss. Nuomi.com is also still incurring losses, but compared to other group-buying sites, Nuomi's operations are already in good condition. Our advertisements are not bombarding, but rather a steady stream; we have less than 1,000 employees nationwide, while other similar group-buying sites have several thousand employees. However, this year as the entire group-buying industry moves towards rationality, some large group-buying websites will announce profits in the second half of the year, including Nuomi.com.

Although the group-buying industry has entered the winter, no matter how much people criticize group-buying, it helps local merchants promote their e-commerce business and truly saves users money, which will never go out of style. As long as the quality is good, it will continue to develop. Although the entire industry has entered the winter, individual companies will welcome spring. Despite the fact that group-buying websites are still in a state of full-industry loss and none of them are making money, if the market does not become as crazy as last year but instead returns to rationality, then by the second half of the year, there may be some profitable ones, and Nuomi.com could be one of them. Facing the so-called "Battle of a Thousand Group-Buying Sites" in the cold winter, we did not feel much difficulty. In my view, there were never more than 20 serious group-buying sites that received financing and were really working hard to establish nationwide group-buying websites. So, the "Battle of a Thousand Group-Buying Sites" is more like a false proposition. Now, after the contraction, fewer than 10 of these 20 sites remain. I always say that group-buying is a marathon, it just burst too quickly in a short period of time, and the number of companies doing similar things slightly increased. Looking at it now, the competition is far less intense than imagined. Currently, for Nuomi.com, it is actually a better opportunity. Because advertising costs have decreased, and it’s easier to hire people. Currently, Nuomi.com has set up sub-stations in 58 cities across the country, and is the only large domestic group-buying website that has not violently laid off employees or reduced stations due to performance reasons. It is still continuously opening new stations and expanding orderly.

Group-buying should return to local services. At present, the group-buying winter has spread throughout the entire e-commerce industry, and those small and medium-sized group-buying websites are not the only ones facing closure. In the future, except for regional and specialized group-buying websites that can survive, such as professional group-buying websites focused on wedding dresses and photography equipment, most national comprehensive websites will close down. The number of future national comprehensive group-buying websites will not exceed five. In 2012, group-buying websites need to return to the essence of corporate operations and make a profit. Meanwhile, consumers will no longer go crazy over group-buying, and the frequency of each person's consumption will decrease, but the number of people participating in group-buying will increase.

Group-buying websites that want to make a profit must innovate, but so-called innovation is not about modifying the old model that couldn't work before. I believe that localized services are the core competitiveness of group-buying. This model not only saves consumers money, but also meets the promotion needs of local merchants on the Internet, while allowing group-buying websites to earn service fees. This is a win-win-win model where all three links are closely connected, none of which can be missing, and this model will never go out of style. Currently, some group-buying websites in the market have transformed into selling goods. I think they cannot be called "group-buying." These websites that rely on "selling goods" require a large logistics team to ensure express delivery, and they face competition from JD and Taobao, and it is difficult for them to surpass the latter. However, the group-buying services of local merchants cannot be done by Taobao or JD. Why? This requires a large street-sweeping team, knocking on doors, talking to one merchant at a time, which is very hard work. Currently, the proportion of "selling goods" on Nuomi.com is controlled at less than 10%, and the rest of the group-buying products are local catering and entertainment services. This is what group-buying websites should do.

"Group-buying websites, after experiencing enlightenment in 2010 and savage growth in 2011, will return to rationality in 2012." Said Shen Boyang, General Manager of Nuomi.com: "In 2011, in order to quickly occupy the market, many group-buying websites were not just burning money, but wasting money, advertisement wars, high salaries poaching talent, losing money to gain popularity... This madness quickly cultivated China's group-buying market, but also caused the group-buying industry to enter the winter."

Profitable group-buying websites will appear in the second half of the year.
