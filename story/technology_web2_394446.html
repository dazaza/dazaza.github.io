1. Purchase from wholesale markets

Whether it is a physical store or an online store, most sellers purchase goods from wholesale markets. Although manufacturers offer first-hand goods with larger profit margins in the price, most manufacturers usually have major clients and generally do not cooperate with small sellers. So, when you haven't yet decided what to sell, go to the wholesale market and take a look, there might be unexpected surprises.

1. Wholesale markets have a large number of products, with ample varieties and quantities, giving everyone a wide selection. It's also easy to achieve "compare three shops".

2. Wholesale markets are very suitable for part-time sellers, with flexible purchase times and quantities.

3. Prices in wholesale markets are relatively low, making it easy for online stores to achieve thin-margin but high-volume sales, which also helps accumulate the credibility of online store transactions.

Comparatively speaking, wholesale markets are indeed a good choice for novice sellers. If you happen to live in a big city with large wholesale markets, why not go check them out? They won't disappoint you. If you live in a smaller city without wholesale markets, don't worry. All roads lead to Rome. There will be a path suitable for you as long as you patiently continue reading.

2. Wholesale on Alibaba Website

The largest wholesale markets in the country are mainly concentrated in a few cities, and many sellers do not have the conditions to travel thousands of miles to these wholesale markets. At this time, Alibaba, as a platform for online wholesale, fully demonstrates its superiority. It provides a large selection space for many sellers in small places. It is not only convenient to search for information, but also specifically provides services for small sellers, with a small starting bid.

1. Alibaba not only offers wholesale purchases but also small-scale auction purchases, both of which are popular ways of purchasing goods among Taobao sellers.

2. When purchasing goods on the website, it is best to choose products that support Alipay or TrustPass members. Alibaba has been promoting 'TrustPass' for four years. Generally, if a member has been a TrustPass member for two or three years or more, and their TrustPass index reaches nearly 100 or more, they are relatively trustworthy. However, this can only be used as a reference, and communication is still key.

3. Alibaba has a powerful search function, allowing for maximum comparison of goods when purchasing.

4. When negotiating with merchants, try to use Trade Manager. If any disputes arise, it can serve as evidence.

5. For the first purchase, you can also choose local manufacturers or companies, which makes it convenient to pick up goods in person.

Online purchasing is not like wholesale markets, as there is a certain degree of virtuality. Therefore, when choosing merchants, one must be extremely cautious and transact with relatively reliable companies.

3. Online Consignment

Online consignment (http://www.haodp.com) involves displaying product information provided by merchants online, collecting order funds from buyers, then transferring a certain amount of funds to the merchant for them to ship the goods, and earning the difference as profit. Two types of novices are suitable for choosing consignment. One is those who do not have much capital, and the other is those who fear risks and just want to try it out.

1. Online consignment requires almost no capital investment, making it very suitable for new and small sellers.

2. Online consignment does not require a warehouse or personal responsibility for logistics. Merchants will directly ship the goods to buyers after receiving the deposit and information, saving the hassle of mailing.

3. Online consignment eliminates the trouble of taking pictures of products and writing product descriptions. Product images obtained from merchants are generally of good quality and easier to attract buyers.

4. Since online consignment cannot directly contact the products, it is difficult to have full confidence in product quality, inventory, and after-sales service. Therefore, when selecting, find some relatively formal companies and choose the most suitable ones based on your own requirements.

Because of so many advantages of online consignment, I am now mainly doing online consignment. I currently handle the goods supply for the brand clothing mall of Agent Shop Leader 123, which includes dozens of brands, men's and women's clothing, shoes, bags, children's clothing, etc., all available for simultaneous agency, which is very convenient. This is my reason for choosing it.

4. Find Merchant Surplus Goods

Here, the term "merchant" refers to the previous one or two levels of sellers of the product, such as foreign trade clothing processing plants or wholesalers.

So-called "A goods" that we often talk about are usually surplus goods produced by foreign trade companies, order returns, or cancellations causing inventory.

Also, larger wholesalers generally have a certain amount of inventory backlog, sometimes even including branded goods. Of course, the styles may not be the latest, but their brand effect is still present. Therefore, finding good merchant surplus goods can actually be a great source.

1. Merchant surplus goods generally have a large market demand, with a certain guarantee of product quality, belonging to mid-to-high-end items, making it easy to gain positive reviews from buyers in online transactions.

2. Merchant surplus goods have relatively limited sources, so there is less competition and stronger competitiveness. Moreover, you can take advantage of regional differences in online stores to enhance the quality of backlogged products and increase prices. That is, if the market for these products is poor in one place causing backlog, they may become best-sellers in another place.

Compared to other sources, merchant surplus goods are relatively hard to find. Although many factories may have inventory backlog, the difficulty of finding them is also quite high. However, if the city where you live has many factories, it's a different story.

7. Techniques for Purchasing Goods on Alibaba

1. Always use Alipay for purchasing goods. I've helped my classmates purchase goods on Alibaba over 20 times, and there were never any problems because I always insisted on using Alipay payment. Do not let the cheapness of the goods sway your decision to use Alipay. This is our basic protection as small sellers.

2. Be extra careful when making payments. Many Alibaba merchants do not conduct transactions directly on Alibaba. They will give you a photo album of their company. Then, after you place an order, they will provide a link to their Taobao store, asking you to make a purchase there. At this point, we must be extra careful. First, check if the Taobao shop owner is the same person you negotiated with on Alibaba. Second, carefully examine the shop's evaluation content. That is, check if there are evaluations similar to yours, made through links using Alipay. Also, check how they evaluate the goods. Third, check the price changes when you make the payment.

3. Find fixed sellers. If you don't have good wholesale markets nearby, Alibaba is indeed your best choice for small-scale purchases. If you have decided to purchase goods on Alibaba, it is recommended that you first buy a small amount of goods for testing. If the quality is satisfactory, you can continue to purchase from this company. This way, you get more discounts, and the risk is greatly reduced.

4. Compare and study more. I once spent two days comparing and studying for a 300 yuan purchase on Taobao and Alibaba. Not rushing to purchase is something we should all pay attention to. There are many factors to consider. For example, is the seller a TrustPass member? How does their price compare with competitors? Remember, cheaper doesn't necessarily mean better. This depends on your insight.

5. Special reminder: Some goods on Taobao may even be cheaper than on Alibaba. Some Alibaba merchants also have Taobao stores. If you encounter goods you want to purchase, it's best to search for prices on Taobao.

6. Do not casually act as an agent. Many people choose to act as agents, which is a risk-free good thing. However, I do not recommend acting as an agent. You cannot hold the goods in your hands, nor can you control shipping. You are just a pawn for them. If there are problems with the goods, you will suffer the consequences. You will be caught between both sides. Therefore, I do not recommend it. I am someone who must hold the goods in my hands before releasing them (this point may be somewhat subjective, so take it as a reference).

7. Find Alibaba merchants who offer consignment sales. This approach has no capital pressure and is worth trying (also a very important point).

8. How to find the lowest price for a single product on Alibaba. When searching for each product, click the price in the upper right corner, and it will sort from low to high, which is very convenient.

8. Techniques for Purchasing Goods at Yiwu Commercial City

1. You need to follow a principle: prioritize the most important products and take them first. Put the ones you need less of or those located farther away last. If there's no time, just skip them (the commercial city closes at 5 PM). This ensures the quality of your goods. It is recommended that you record the distribution map of the shops you need during your first and second visits (this will save time and effort later), and also note down whether there are passages between areas, which can save time and energy.

2. Always collect business cards from the most important shops after each purchase, and determine the shops you frequently visit based on the market feedback of the products.

3. Don't forget to buy a luggage handcart when purchasing goods. Stainless steel ones are best, as they are durable and won't break easily. Also, buy packaging bags (woven bags). These are available at the entrance of the second phase on the first floor, priced at 45-50 yuan per cart, and woven bags cost 3-5 yuan each (in different sizes). Don't skimp on this expense, as cheap ones are not good quality.

4. The CDE zones of the first phase of the commercial city are suitable for small-scale purchases, mostly concentrated in zones C and D. When you go to purchase goods, first check if the store has a large amount of stock placed aside. If yes, you can make small-scale purchases. However, many stores specialize in foreign trade. If you see attractive goods with only one sample per style, they are usually for large orders (occasionally, they also sell in small quantities). Stock goods are mostly headwear. Small-scale customers can directly look at some headwear and jewelry parts, such as small pendants, alloy diamond-studded jewelry, and silver jewelry. Some larger shops are better, where you don't need to take whole packages or pieces, but can take scattered items. Usually, there won't be restrictions on the amount or quantity. It's necessary to supplement here with professional terms regarding purchasing and bargaining. For example, the term "whole piece" refers to goods packed into a box for shipping, abbreviated as "piece". Don't let the seller know you're a novice (otherwise, they might raise the price or refuse to sell in bulk).

5. If you have fixed cooperative merchants, you can have them send express delivery or freight, so you don't have to go in person, which is very convenient!

6. When purchasing goods at the commercial city, the first principle is to save wherever possible. Therefore, you must budget in advance for the total costs of food, accommodation, and transportation, and then roughly determine the purchase amount based on the types and quantities of goods needed. Of course, don't be too hard on yourself, and don't skimp on things you shouldn't.

7. Loading Principle: Heavy, large-volume, and crush-resistant goods should be placed at the bottom of the packaging bag, while fragile and valuable items like jewelry should be placed on top or carried with you.

8. Layout of the Commercial City Area: The second floor E zone north side of Phase I and Phase II are connected by corridors, so you don't need to exit the north gate of Phase I, cross the motor vehicle lane, and climb the steps to enter Phase II, which is both troublesome and dangerous! The second floors of G and H zones in Phase II are also connected, so you don't need to cross the motor vehicle lane on the first floor to enter the H zone.

9. Regarding Banks: Construction Bank and Postal Savings are located on the first and second floors of the east gate of Phase I of the Commercial City. Zhouzhou Commercial Bank and Credit Union are in Zone D, Bank of China and ICBC are in Zone B, and Agricultural Bank is in Zone A. Zhejiang Merchant Bank is near the south gate of Phase II, and banks are available at the entrances of F, G, and H zones!
