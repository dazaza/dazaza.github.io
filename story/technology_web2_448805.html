Woman hit claims 3 people in the car smelled of alcohol, police say the offender did not drive under the influence

by zxyasdk60 on 2011-06-20 10:51:25

The victim lay painfully on the hospital bed. Kuang女士's husband, Mr. Zi, was brutally beaten by three men in the conflict, and his glasses were also broken.

The incident occurred in Shenzhen, where three men got out of a car reeking of alcohol, surrounded and assaulted Mr. Zi before fleeing. The traffic police stated that they have already taken control of the "hit-and-run driver," but tests showed no evidence of drunk driving.

According to the Yangcheng Evening News, at around 9 PM on the 13th, near the Shenzhen University intersection, a Buick car while reversing struck Kuang女士 and her 7-year-old daughter, throwing them several meters away. Kuang女士 claimed that three men smelling strongly of alcohol got out of the Buick car and severely beat her husband who had just arrived on the scene, after which they fled. According to the traffic police department, they have currently controlled the "hit-and-run driver," but after an alcohol test, it was found he was not driving under the influence. Kuang女士 insists that the drunk driver has been replaced by someone else.

The abnormal Buick car caused the mother and daughter to "fly."

The incident occurred at the entrance of Xuelin Yawen Community, Gui Miao New Village in Nanshan District, Shenzhen. At around 1 AM on the 14th, I received a tip-off and rushed to the scene of the incident; the offending vehicle had already driven away, leaving only the injured party's shoe on the ground. From the 120 command center, I learned that the ambulance arrived at the scene at 9:14 PM.

At around 8:30 AM on the 14th, I visited the injured Kuang女士 at the orthopedics department of Nanshan People's Hospital. According to her account, at around 9 PM on the 13th, Kuang女士 was riding an electric scooter with her daughter nearby in Gui Miao New Village. "The Buick car in front of me seemed to be malfunctioning; it suddenly stopped while driving. I immediately braked, but then the car first blocked the road sideways and then reversed quickly into us." In the accident, the rear of the car hit Kuang女士's spine. Her daughter, tightly held by her mother, only suffered a head impact. Mother and daughter were thrown several meters away.

Kuang女士 said that at the time, three men and one woman got out of the car, and the man who got out from the driver's seat had a completely different accent from the other three. Students from Shenzhen University dining nearby gradually gathered around. About ten minutes later, Kuang女士's husband, Mr. Zi, arrived from their nearby home, and the three men immediately began to brutally beat him before fleeing afterward.

Mother and daughter were sent to Nanshan People's Hospital. From the hospital side, we learned that Kuang女士 suffered a fracture of the spinous process of the L3 vertebra, and her daughter suffered intracranial bleeding, requiring hospital observation.

A self-claimed district government official intervened in mediation.

At around 10 PM on the 13th, a Li surnamed man claiming to be from the Nanshan District Government of Shenzhen came to Kuang女士's ward. Kuang女士 said, "He kept telling me to make big issues small and small issues disappear. He said he was a person from the district committee and government." Until around 2 AM on the 14th, this Li surnamed man left Kuang女士's ward and left his contact information.

At around 9 AM on the 14th, I called the contact number of this Li surnamed man as a friend of Kuang女士. In the conversation, the man insisted that he was from the Nanshan District Government. As for the identities of the people suspected of assaulting others while drunk, he refused to disclose them. The Li surnamed man said that he was just a friend of the Buick car owner. Whether this man is indeed a staff member of the Nanshan District Government remains unverified.

Shenzhen University female student's Weibo live report receives praise.

Among the onlookers at the car accident scene was an 18-year-old girl from Shenzhen University named Xiao Wang (a pseudonym). She witnessed the entire event and exposed it through Sina Weibo, immediately causing tens of thousands of forwards. By 10 AM on the 14th, a total of 2,629 netizens had added comments.

One netizen said, "You're very brave! Very righteous! Support you!"

Traffic Police: Driver has been controlled, no drunk driving

According to Xiao Wang's description in her Weibo post, the Buick car driver was driving under the influence, and many onlookers could testify to this, which is basically consistent with Kuang女士's statement.

However, Kuang女士 told the reporter that at around 11 PM last night, Officer Zhou from the Shekou Traffic Police Station of Nanshan District brought three men to Nan Shan People's Hospital for alcohol testing and brought these three people to Kuang女士's ward, asking her to identify which one was the offending driver. "None of these three people were the driver, and I told the traffic police on the spot."

At around 10 AM on the 14th, I called the Shekou Traffic Police Station of Nanshan District. A duty captain who did not wish to reveal his name told me that one of the three men was the offending driver, and the other two were his friends, who were sitting in the backseat of the car at the time. After the two friends finished their statements, they left on their own, while this "offending driver" is still within the traffic police station. However, after alcohol testing, this "offending driver" was not found to be driving under the influence.

At 10:30 AM on the 14th, I contacted the poster Xiao Wang, who was on her way to the Shekou Traffic Police Station. She told me, "The driver who got out of the car definitely drank alcohol, reeking of it, and he was the first to flee." Xiao Wang publicized this event online, drawing attention from the Nanshan District Traffic Police Brigade. The officer handling the case on the 13th evening also left Xiao Wang's phone number, and this morning, the traffic police contacted Xiao Wang, hoping to obtain relevant photos and videos from her.
