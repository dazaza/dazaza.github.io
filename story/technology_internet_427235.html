Phase 3 of the Erhai International Eco-city Grand Opening

Tags: Erhai International Eco-city

On the morning of May 7, 2011, Phase 3 of the Erhai International Eco-city was launched.

The first bay of Phase 3 of the Erhai International Eco-city offered multiple discounts on the opening day.

More than a thousand customers rushed to buy properties in Phase 3 of the Dali Erhai International City, with tourism and vacation products receiving great popularity among homebuyers!

Nearly a thousand buyers attended the opening day of the first bay of the Erhai International Eco-city. In January 2010, Phase 1 of the Erhai International Eco-city was launched in Kunming, attracting 2,000 people, and all 600 units were sold out on the same day, creating a miracle for regional property sales in Kunming. On January 22, 2011, Phase 2, Peninsula No.1 of the Erhai International Eco-city, was launched at Yunnan Grand Theatre, selling 80% of the units on the same day, once again creating a legend for regional property sales in Kunming. On May 7, 2011, the first bay of the Erhai International Eco-city saw a rush purchase by over a thousand people, creating a myth of strong sales in a weak market, continuing the legend of the Erhai International Eco-city.

Under the pressure of real estate market regulation, regional properties, tourism properties, and commercial properties have become investment hotspots. The Erhai International Eco-city has gained favor from savvy investors due to its unique and irreplicable resources!

Going to Dali to see the sea after work, spending weekends on vacation at Erhai

"Going to Dali to see the sea after work, spending weekends on vacation at Erhai" is an advertising slogan conceived by the CEO of the Erhai International Eco-city, and also the most fitting description of this project. Phase 3 of the Erhai International Eco-city is just a step away from the future administrative center of Dali, located in the central area of the future Dali city. The 400-mu original ecological peninsula of the Erhai International Eco-city is the only gold peninsula in Dali's Erhai with three sides facing the sea, offering a panoramic view of approximately 250 square kilometers of magnificent Erhai Lake, as well as the sight of the 19 peaks and 18 streams of Cangshan Mountain. Phase 3 of the Erhai International Eco-city is heavily invested in by Dali Erhai Jinsha Tourism Resort Real Estate Co., Ltd., integrating cultural experience, leisure vacations, and ecological sightseeing into a comprehensive real estate project. Combining modern wisdom, it vividly interprets technological ecological culture. Dali's climate, with an average annual temperature of 17.8°C, is like spring all year round, making it a top choice for tourism and leisure vacations. "Going to Dali to see the sea after work, spending weekends on vacation at Erhai" is no longer just a slogan or a luxury, but a fashion and the preferred choice for modern people's leisure vacations. With the gradual construction of the Greater Kunming urban circle and the opening of high-speed rail, the vision of Kunming residents going to Dali to see the sea after work is becoming a reality, and investing in the Erhai International Eco-city will become the choice of investors.

Phase 3 of the Erhai International Eco-city, the first bay, was launched at the Kunming sales office of Dali Jinsha Real Estate. On the opening day of Phase 3 of the Erhai International Eco-city, mainly residential units and shops were offered for sale. The unit sizes range from 30 square meters to 120 square meters. The main unit type of the first bay of Phase 3 of the Erhai International Eco-city is a 38-square-meter apartment, starting at 200,000 yuan per unit. On the opening day of the first bay of Phase 3 of the Erhai International Eco-city, residential units including holiday homes starting at 200,000 yuan per unit and sea-view apartments starting at only 300,000 yuan per unit were offered for sale. According to Wang Zhiwei, the executive general manager of Dali Jinsha Tourism Resort Real Estate Co., Ltd., the holiday homes and sea-view apartments launched on the opening day of May 8, 2011, are exclusive rare units of the project. After the opening, there will be no more holiday homes for 200,000 yuan or sea-view apartments for 300,000 yuan available for sale. On the opening day, purchasing a sea-view apartment will also come with a gift of 1,000 yuan per square meter for high-quality renovation. Manager Wang Zhuanwei of Dali Jinsha Tourism Resort Real Estate also stated that the first bay of Phase 3 of the Erhai International Eco-city has 10 specially designed large sea-view apartments with balconies that can be converted into two separate units through renovation, priced between 350,000 yuan and 400,000 yuan per unit. Additionally, on the opening day, more than 50 shops were offered for sale, ranging in size from 10 square meters to 300 square meters, starting at 300,000 yuan per unit, with a down payment of only 150,000 yuan. The shop return rate for the first bay of Phase 3 of the Erhai International Eco-city reaches 8%, with unlimited future appreciation potential.

With a subscription card, buyers at the opening of the first bay of the Erhai International Eco-city can enjoy a 2% discount, an additional 3% discount for full payment, an additional 1% discount for mortgage, and an extra 1.8% discount for existing owners. Buyers who successfully purchase a property on the opening day can receive multiple major gifts, including special-priced houses with a total price of 90,000 yuan, gold bars, free accompanying fees, high-quality renovations, etc.
