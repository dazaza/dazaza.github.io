Life is a process of searching for love, and everyone needs to find four people in their life.

by lwf225 on 2009-09-16 22:48:10

Life is a journey to search for love, and everyone needs to find four people in their life.

The first is yourself,

The second is the person you love the most,

The third is the person who loves you the most,

And the fourth is the person with whom you will spend your entire life.

First, you will encounter the person you love the most, then experience the feeling of love;

Because you understand the feeling of being loved, you can discover the person who loves you the most;

After experiencing loving and being loved, you learn about love, and only then do you know what you need,

And thus, find the person most suitable for you, someone with whom you can spend your entire life.

Sadly, in real life, these three people are usually not the same person;

The one you love the most often does not choose you;

The one who loves you the most is often not the one you love the most;

And the one who stays the longest is neither the one you love nor the one who loves you,

but rather the person who appears at the right time.

Which of these four people are you in someone else's life?

No one intentionally changes their heart; when they love you, they truly love you,

But when they no longer love you, it is also genuinely over,

When they love you, they cannot pretend not to love you;

Similarly, when they don't love you, they cannot pretend to love you.

When someone doesn't love you anymore and wants to leave,

You should ask yourself if you still love them,

If you no longer love them, never stay just for a pitiful sense of self-respect;

If you still love them, you should hope for their happiness and joy,

Wish that they be with the person who truly loves them, and never stop them,

If you prevent them from finding true happiness, it means you no longer love them,

And if you don't love them, on what grounds do you criticize their change of heart?

Love is not possession,

If you like the moon, you cannot take the moon down and put it in a basin,

But the moon's light can still shine into your room.

In other words, you can love someone in another way,

Letting your loved one become an eternal memory in your life,

If you truly love someone, love them as they are - love their goodness and flaws:

Love their strengths and weaknesses,

Absolutely do not try to change them because of your love, hoping they become someone different,

Or you might end up not loving them once they fail to change.

Truly loving someone defies explanation,

You simply know that no matter when or where, whether you're happy or sad, you want this person by your side;

True affection is two people standing together through the toughest times without any demands.

After all, relationships require giving, not just taking;

Separation is an inevitable test,

If your relationship isn't strong enough, you must accept it,

True love does not turn into resentment.

When lovers are exchanging sweet words,

They love to make each other swear and promise.

Why do we ask our partners to swear?

It's because we don't trust them completely; we fundamentally don't trust our lovers,

And those vows of eternal love sound unrealistic:

Even if seas dry up, rocks crumble, heaven and earth perish, my love for you will never change!

We know well that seas won't dry, rocks won't crumble, heaven won't fall, and earth won't perish;

Even if they did, we wouldn't live long enough to see it.

When making promises, be cautious not to vow something achievable,

It's best to promise things that are impossible,

Since what cannot be achieved can be spoken of lightly,

Remember: "Impossible promises are the most touching."

In love, what is said and what is done are often contradictory;

The speaker doesn't believe, and neither does the listener.

And you? Which of these four people have you found yet?

In the vast sea of humanity, who have you met? And who has met you?
