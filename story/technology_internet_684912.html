Base Makeup Creation

To correct a yellowish skin tone, I would recommend using a purple primer and a pinkish foundation. Since purple and yellow are contrasting colors, combining them will make your skin appear whiter and more radiant. If you have redness issues, I suggest using a green primer and a peach-toned foundation. Most North American men have slightly fuller lips, so we can even use two different shades of foundation together. Applying a lighter shade on the T-zone and jawline can make your nose appear higher and your jawline more defined. Applying a darker shade under the brow bone and at the jawline can make your lips look more delicate and refined.

Eyelash Application

Often, on the street, you see girls with round faces who draw willow-leaf eyebrows, making their entire face appear unbalanced. In fact, eyelashes can greatly improve our facial features and enhance our overall appearance. Can you guess what kind of eyelashes suit a round face? Winged eyelashes! Because visually, it makes our face appear longer. What about long faces? If you add winged eyelashes, wouldn't it make you look like a horse face? Yet, every day, I see several "horses" out there!

Eye Makeup Application

Many MMs often tell me they don't know how to apply eye makeup. If you don't know how to do it, it's actually quite simple! First, choose a Nestle coffee-colored eyeshadow. Regardless of the current trend, for beginners, Nestle coffee color is always a safe choice! On TV, many makeup artists teach us to start with a light base and then apply a darker shade in the socket. However, today I will teach beginners to start with a darker shade in the socket and then use a lighter shade to blend at the edges. MMs can try this method and see if the eyeshadow blending suddenly becomes more beautiful and natural.

Blush and Lip Gloss

Finally, choose a blush and lip gloss based on the eye makeup color or your outfit. It's important to note that nude lip gloss is trendy this year, but many MMs misunderstand the definition of nude, thinking it means very light colors. Some pale and unhealthy-looking MMs (with dark skin) end up looking like future soldiers! In reality, nude should be close to your natural lip color, and we should adjust the depth of the color based on our skin tone.
