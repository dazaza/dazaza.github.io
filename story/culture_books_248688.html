"Why Bother with Marriage" - the emotional entanglement of marital failure and reignited first love.

by jqrxass on 2009-10-17 14:29:52

Xu Ruoxing, after a failed first love, married Ni Zhiqing out of spite. However, four years later, she meets her first love Liu Junhao again and discovers that the child she bore is actually Liu Junhao's. Ni Zhiqing cannot accept this reality and resorts to domestic violence against Xu Ruoxing. After Xu Ruoxing divorces, Liu Junhao begins to pursue her again, along with his younger brother Hua Zhuotian纠缠 (Hua Zhuotian). Faced with these two men, Xu Ruoxing feels at a loss. In the end, who will Xu Ruoxing choose? Will Xu Ruoxing enter into marriage again?

Why Bother with Marriage Part One

It wasn't until Liu Junhao stood in front of her that she realized it really was him! How ironic fate can be, how laughable! This person she hated to the core, someone she never wanted to see for the rest of her life, but now she needed to ask this person to save her daughter. The city isn't too big, nor too small, but meeting one particular person is still difficult, especially if that person is someone you want to avoid deep down.

After she got married, she never saw him again, except occasionally spotting his figure on TV or in newspapers and magazines. It seemed as though Liu Junhao had vanished from her life.

He remained as popular as ever, always surrounded by beautiful women, with scandals year-round without interruption. Whenever she turned on the TV, there would be news about him. Xu Ruoxing could recite all information about him backwards. And her? For Liu Junhao, she might as well have disappeared into the vast ocean long ago.

Why Bother with Marriage Part Two

Ni Zhiqing's partner left the country because his girlfriend went abroad, leaving the company solely in Ni Zhiqing's hands. Due to being busy, also because of the great temptations outside, and more so because their living conditions improved later on, Ni Zhiqing began to demand more from Xu Ruoxing. Actually, Ni Zhiqing thought Xu Ruoxing was pretty good in every way. In others' eyes, she was gentle, virtuous, poised, and graceful - in short, a perfect woman. But for him, unfortunately, Xu Ruoxing became increasingly cold towards sex, even reaching the point of aversion. Yet, she forced herself to satisfy him, which made him dislike her even more.

A life without sex is unbearable, and Ni Zhiqing increasingly desired Xu Ruoxing to satisfy him in this aspect, thus leading to constant conflicts between them. In the past, when their economic situation was poor, they were too busy to notice anything amiss. Once material wealth increased, they pursued such physical pleasures even more than before.

Why Bother with Marriage Part Three

Xu Ruoxing lay on the bed, laughing lightly while propping up her head in a seductive manner, asking, "Could it be that you want to get into bed with me?"

Liu Junhao looked at her eyes warningly, "Don't pose like that to entice people!"

Xu Ruoxing stroked the soft blanket on the bed teasingly, "Yes, this bed has probably seen countless women, countless affairs!"

Liu Junhao grabbed her face, kissed her fiercely as if biting, "Your voice sounds so jealous, looks like you really miss me!"
