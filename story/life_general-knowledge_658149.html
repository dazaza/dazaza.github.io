Unfulfilled Love

For a very long time, I haven't been online. Perhaps it's because I don't want to see your QQ status as "online", but I am unwilling to delete you either. I fear that I can't control my emotions. I know that at this moment, you should be very happy. But, I still want to tell you: I love you!

We met on that sleepless night. That unusual night brought us together unexpectedly, yet it filled me with joy. Usually, I like to wander aimlessly online. The dazzling web pages make me no longer feel the pain of loneliness. Every night at midnight, I log into my QQ, because at that moment, I can feel how quiet the night is, almost terrifyingly so. But I like this feeling and atmosphere. Except for the weak light source from the computer, everything is pitch black, as if my existence is the source of life.

That day, just like any other, I finished all my tasks before 12 o'clock, silently waiting for the sound of the midnight bell. I turned on my computer, suddenly feeling an indescribable touch in my heart, but I didn't pay much attention to it. My fingers skillfully tapped on the keyboard, bringing a little sound to this quiet night, like the joyous leap of piano keys.

I like to visit many forums, understanding some distant stories, leaving behind traces of my solitary journey. That night, I found a Tieba named Xiaoxiang Garden. It recorded the moods of many people. A few words here and there revealed simple and deep feelings in their hearts. Here, I rarely believe in so-called feelings. In a way, I might be a complete loser, but I lack warmth more than anything else. While longing for it, I also hate my own contradictions. It turns out I'm still a child. I quickly browsed through the posts of netizens who were far away yet close to me, feeling their hearts near to mine. Yet, I couldn't help but feel pain because I feared such tender affection. Maybe it's because I'm not brave enough to take on such responsibility.

An article titled "A Whiff of Fragrance Hidden in the Mist" caught my attention. It was accompanied by several beautiful flowers which I didn't recognize. There was a faint mist over the pictures, making one involuntarily feel the fragrance slowly seeping into the heart, as if refreshing the soul. Perhaps it was the elegance of this post that attracted me, leading me to click on the author's avatar and enter her space. The content inside was simple, mostly related to flowers, but it made people feel particularly comfortable. For the first time ever, I felt a wave of emotion. Maybe my heart had been empty for too long. I added her as a friend, without thinking too much about it.

However, I don't know why, but after that, every night I would unconsciously go to her space to check if she had any new updates. This might be a way to understand her, or maybe I didn't want anything. Later, we started chatting on QQ. We hit it off, and gradually, I got used to chatting with her every day. When I didn't see her online one day, I felt an emptiness in my heart. Maybe this is the terrible habit.

We went from simple greetings to talking about everything under the sun. Such intimacy may not be considered complete love, but it made me so dependent. Many people think I was lonely, but my heart was true. Once, unable to suppress the pressure in my heart, I told her: Let's meet up! Although I had imagined such a scene countless times, I was afraid of her rejection. However, she agreed, leaving me at a loss, perhaps too happy and surprised.

Although we were in the same city, to preserve a bit of mystery, neither of us had seen each other's photos before. We arranged to meet on that beautiful evening at a quiet coffee shop because she told me she liked that kind of style, that kind of solitude. That night, I arrived early, maybe wanting to leave a good impression on her. I sat at our pre-arranged seat, looking out the window, lost in thought but not knowing what I was thinking about. Later, I smelled a faint aroma. I turned around and saw a slim girl. Her hair was slightly yellow, and her complexion wasn't great, but she was still pretty, and her shallow dimples were especially adorable. She also had a faint fragrance. I invited her to sit down. We talked a lot. She seemed talkative, which was good. I liked this girl who gave me a different feeling.

That night, when I sent her back, she suddenly said she didn't want to go home and wanted to come to my place. So, we went to my messy little apartment. That night, I wanted to hold her, but she didn't let me touch her, which made me uncomfortable. That night passed simply. The next day, I sent her away.

Perhaps, from a certain perspective, men's and women's ways of thinking are different. Now I suddenly realize that men are hard to understand, and women are hard to understand too, just with different targets. I think, if I really loved her, I should give her at least respect, but I couldn't control my physical reaction. Maybe I'm not a good man. As a bad man, I think I'm not very qualified. Well, maybe I need to catch up on some sleep.

After that, we seldom contacted each other. I don't know why, but it seemed like she disappeared from my world, and it seemed like I left no trace in her world. Maybe her world was too cold to accommodate me, a person who loves sunshine. Gradually, I put her aside. Maybe I'm a person who fears loneliness. Because of this, I want to live a full life and continue my previous activities, wandering in the cyber world just to achieve a certain psychological pleasure.

Another rainy day, now I don't know why, every rainy day makes me particularly restless. I feel even lonelier, as if I'm a discarded child living alone in the corner of the world. To make others think I'm living well, I went to a large mall and started a crazy shopping spree. After spending the whole day shopping, I suddenly felt tired and walked into a coffee shop, ordering something casually. Unconsciously, I looked out the window, seeing the dim sky and drizzling rain. I became inexplicably sentimental, remembering her who has been buried deep in my heart for a long time. It turns out she was still there, just quietly settling in my heart, never disappearing. Suddenly, I felt the pain, the heart-wrenching pain, but after a while, I was fine.

Disappointed, I picked up my today's spoils and returned to my small dwelling place. I was a bit tired. I thought I should go to sleep. Unconsciously, I watched things far away getting further and further until my vision blurred. In that beautiful dream, I dreamed that we met again, but this time, there was a man more handsome than me by her side. This made me extremely uncomfortable, but I still smiled at her silently. However, she seemed to ignore my existence and walked past me quietly, holding hands with that handsome man, walking closely by my side. At that moment, it felt like my heart was about to stop beating, and breathing was so difficult, as if I was falling into hell. Without any sensation, suddenly, my brain went blank. Weakly, I opened my tired eyes. Before my consciousness returned, I don't know why I suddenly jumped up, turned on the computer, quickly logged into QQ, and searched for her trail. However, after searching for a long time, I couldn't find her. Cold sweat slowly dripped down from my forehead. I remember, I didn't delete her QQ number, did I?

It took me a lot of effort to find her QQ number that I once noted down beside my computer desk, and then I couldn't help but add her again. Perhaps, this is another beginning, thus starting a different life. We met a few times later, and gradually, the frequency of our meetings increased. I especially liked going out for walks with her. Maybe due to our personalities, there was always an unbridgeable boundary between us, but I was willing to cater to her, only because I realized I fell in love with her, and under unknown circumstances, I loved her deeply.

On a very ordinary day, we started living together, beginning our own small life. It felt like my place finally became a home. She decorated the room warmly and cared for me specially. During the day, we were busy with work, and in the evening, we went out for dinner and took walks because she liked walking. She said she liked this slow pace, as if we could keep holding hands forever. Later, I lost my job because of conflicts with my boss, so I resigned.

She still went to work on time every day, and I began looking for a new job. Sometimes her work would make her come home very late, and I would pick her up. When she had no time, I would clean the house and wash her clothes, etc. I don't know why, but I always looked forward to her coming back. I don't know if it's because of my recent bad luck or something else, but I couldn't find a job, and we lived like this. Sometimes, I unintentionally made her cry, but she liked my embrace. She always said: "You should be grateful for your embrace, it brings me a lot of warmth and makes me feel safe!" Each time I heard this sentence, I felt inexplicable warmth.

During her holidays, we liked to get up late, then go out to eat something, and stroll around nearby. This was her preferred lifestyle. Like me, although I wanted to go out for exercise, I still chose to walk with her. This might be what people often call love, because except for her holidays, the rest of the time I could freely arrange. She never asked what I did during the day, not because she didn't care about me, but because she didn't want to put pressure on me. I understood that.

Even though we weren't rich, we lived happily. Occasionally, we would have minor quarrels, but we would reconcile as usual. This is normal life, I guess. I think our first home was pretty good! Later, she came home later and sometimes scolded me. I knew it was because of me that she suffered a lot. I had thought many times of leaving her resolutely, but I couldn't bear to part with her. Seeing her so thin, I really couldn't let her go. She also said many times that she would leave, but she said she didn't have the courage, she couldn't bear to leave me alone, so we still lived in that small room. I spoiled her a lot, maybe because I loved her too much!

If you love her, please love her sincerely. This is what a good friend once told me, and I have remembered it ever since. Later, we moved because we couldn't afford the rent there anymore. Relying solely on her salary was far from enough for both of us. We packed our stuff and moved to a smaller house, starting a new life! When we left, she said that was our home, and she was afraid we would lose those youthful dreams. Just like the protagonists in "Vocal Exercises of Love," because of love, I believed we could live better.

But, maybe good times don't last long, or happiness is always envied by others. One day, she said she was tired. She still hadn't forgotten her ex-boyfriend. She simply packed her luggage and left, quietly departing from my life. All I did was tightly hug her and say, "Take care of yourself outside, I'll wait for you to come back!" Smiling and saying goodbye to her. But after she left, I couldn't suppress the heart-wrenching pain, tears flowed silently. It turned out it hurt so much. I hurriedly ran out and shouted towards her retreating figure, "Take care of yourself!" Tears blurred her vision, and she forced a smile, saying, "Yes, I will!" Then she turned and left.

Next, I started a single life, but the room was still kept clean because I was still waiting for her to return. She was the firework in my life, very beautiful, but I couldn't possess her. I also started a normal life, working and going home every day. Sometimes I thought, if I could have found a job earlier, maybe she wouldn't have left. But I understood, if I couldn't bring her happiness, then I would bless her! When I had free time, I would often sit on the benches in the park we visited, feeling if she had left any traces there. It felt beautiful, breathing fresh air, as if my heart was drunk. Only now do I realize how much she enjoyed the natural fragrance!

Later, one day, I was aimlessly strolling on the street, maybe influenced by her. Suddenly, I saw a scene where a man was arguing with two women. The man was hugging a beautiful woman, while the other woman appeared to be pregnant. Many people were watching, as if it was a wonderful drama. Out of curiosity, I approached to take a look. When I saw the pregnant woman, my heart ached again. It was her. What happened to her? I asked myself. From everyone's discussion, I learned that her ex-boyfriend had abandoned her. It hurt so much to see her so gaunt. Silently, I helped her up and took her away from that cold place. We returned to my dormitory, and she didn't say a word the entire time. I helped her take a bath, and at night, we lay down to rest. She fell asleep leaning on my shoulder, while I stayed awake all night. I told myself: I will take care of her for the rest of my life!

I learned from others that when she was with me, her ex-boyfriend had come to bother her many times, even threatening her and wanting to harm me, forcing her to do things she didn't want to do. She always kept it hidden from me. Later, when she discovered she was pregnant, she knew the child wasn't mine, so she left me and went to live in a remote place. And the scene I saw that day was because she wanted to ask her ex-boyfriend for a little money to save for the soon-to-be-born child. She couldn't work now, so she could only beg her ex-boyfriend. I listened to all the details patiently, and my heart ached again! Tears blurred my vision. That day, I walked that road for a long, long time. When I returned, I didn't say anything, and she still didn't say a word. But we started a happy life. Soon, her stomach started to ache severely, and I quickly took her to the hospital. I insisted on accompanying her during childbirth. She tightly held my hand, and I thought, at that moment, our hearts were tightly connected. Not long after, she gave birth to a boy. As the baby cried loudly, she smiled happily and weakly said, "Our child..."
