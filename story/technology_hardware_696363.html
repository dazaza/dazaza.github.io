Several players introduced by Juventus in the winter transfer window have proven to be quite practical. Caceres is a versatile defender, Padoin has both good offensive and defensive abilities, Borrello has had shining moments at Milan and Roma but hasn't had consistent starting opportunities. However, in the past month, Caceres has only come off the bench twice in the league for a total of 29 minutes. Padoin also has only made two appearances in the league, while Borrello's situation is slightly better with four appearances, though without any goals (Sohu Sports, Diamo).

Five, Adjusting the players' mentality

Recently, Conte has frequently used the 3-5-2 formation. In the midweek match against Bologna, due to the absence of two main center-backs, Conte will revert to the 4-3-3 formation. La Gazzetta dello Sport believes that 4-3-3 is more suitable for Juventus. The 3-5-2 formation requires extreme mobility from the players, and its disadvantages were fully exposed in the match against Milan. In the last game against Chievo, Juventus was clearly worn down in the second half.

Four, Trying multiple offensive combinations

In the match against Chievo, Juventus lost two absolute mainstays, Barzagli and Chiellini. It may seem accidental, but it is also inevitable. This season, in 25 league matches, Barzagli has played every game, and Chiellini has missed only one. Although Juventus does not have European competitions this season, some players' physical endurance has already reached its limit. Lichtsteiner has played 25 games, Marchisio and Pirlo have played 24 games, Pepe and Matri have played 23 games... If Conte doesn't want to lose more key players at the critical moment of chasing the championship, he must make appropriate rotations. And having a deep squad is actually an advantage for the Old Lady.

One, Reasonably allocating players' stamina

In the midweek, Juventus will have a postponed match against Bologna. If the Bianconeri can win, they will catch up with Milan in points. However, in the recent five rounds of the league, Juventus has drawn four times. They couldn't secure all three points even against Siena, Parma, and Chievo, who are ranked in the bottom ten of the league. The edition of La Gazzetta dello Sport on March 7th believed that although Juventus has been undefeated in 25 consecutive rounds, there have been 12 draws. Juventus finished seventh in the last two seasons, and the initial goal for this season was just to finish in the top three and gain Champions League qualification. But as the team performed well, including players like Del Piero, the title has become their target, which has added a significant burden to them. Before the match against Milan, Juventus manager Marotta said: "The result of this match is not important. I'm happy that Juventus has once again become a team with the ability to compete for the title." Correcting their mindset is very important for the current Juventus.

Del Piero, Vucinic, Borrello, Quagliarella, Matri, and Giaccherini, these six Juventus forwards each have their own characteristics. So far, apart from Matri scoring 10 league goals, among the others, Vucinic has the most goals with only three, and Del Piero and Borrello haven't scored yet. La Gazzetta dello Sport suggests that Conte try various attacking combinations and consider giving Del Piero a chance when it matters. Although the prince is aging, his championship experience is unmatched by others.
