Puyang County Traditional Chinese Medicine Hospital Test Report

Zhongxin Henan News Network, Puyang, November 18th (reporter Yu Yan, Yang Fan) In response to the national call and actively signing up for military service, Mao Jingbiao, a college student from Puyang County, was found to carry the hepatitis B virus during a physical examination. This test result shattered his dream of joining the army and caused almost a collapse in his mental state. To confirm whether he is a carrier of the hepatitis B virus, he underwent blood tests six times in four hospitals within 11 days. The two physical examinations at the designated county traditional Chinese medicine hospital by the county armed forces department and health bureau both showed that he was a carrier of the hepatitis B virus. However, the results of the checks at three municipal hospitals in Puyang City all indicated that he was not a carrier of the hepatitis B virus. More puzzlingly, when the college student used an alias for the same check at the county traditional Chinese medicine hospital, the test results showed normal.

Mao Jingbiao, 23 years old, is from Wuzhuang Village, Wangcheng Township, Puyang County, and is about to graduate from Puyang Vocational and Technical College. After seeing the winter recruitment propaganda this year, he actively signed up for military service. Although he is the only son in the family, his parents firmly supported his idea. On November 5th, he went to Puyang County Traditional Chinese Medicine Hospital for a military recruitment physical examination, and the unexpected result came out: positive for hepatitis B surface antigen (hepatitis B virus). He has always been healthy, and this sudden physical examination result stunned him. Is he really carrying the hepatitis B virus, or did the physical examination result make a mistake? That day, he and his father went to Puyang Third People's Hospital again for a single-item test of hepatitis B surface antigen, and the test result was negative for hepatitis B surface antigen, which means he is not a carrier of the hepatitis B virus. Holding this test result, his heart finally settled down. Dissatisfied with the initial result of the military recruitment physical examination, he requested a re-examination. According to the notification requirements, at 9 o'clock on the morning of November 14th, he was required to go to Puyang County Traditional Chinese Medicine Hospital for a blood test. Worried that the re-examination would be the same as the initial examination, he arrived 30 minutes earlier at Puyang County Traditional Chinese Medicine Hospital and asked under the pseudonym "Jia Xiaozhu" to examine the "two pairs and half" of hepatitis B (hepatitis B surface antigen, hepatitis B surface antibody, hepatitis B e antigen, hepatitis B e antibody, hepatitis B c antibody). The test results for "two pairs and half" of hepatitis B under the pseudonym "Jia Xiaozhu" were all normal. However, 30 minutes later, at the same Puyang County Traditional Chinese Medicine Hospital, another blood test was performed on Mao Jingbiao, and the result was still positive for hepatitis B surface antigen. Because this was the re-examination result for military recruitment, Mao Jingbiao was rejected from the military camp with a test report.

Mao Jingbiao and his family could not accept such a re-examination result. On November 15th, Mao Jingbiao went to the Affiliated Hospital of Puyang Medical School to check the "two pairs and half" of hepatitis B, and the result was still normal. Looking at the test reports in Mao Jingbiao's hand, all marked as negative for hepatitis B surface antigen, the reporter still could not believe it. Therefore, the reporter decided to personally accompany Mao Jingbiao to Puyang People's Hospital for a physical examination. At 4 p.m. on November 16th, the test results came out, and his "two pairs and half" of hepatitis B were all negative. For this test result, Mao Jingbiao's father was particularly excited: "The test results reported by you journalists show that my child does not have hepatitis B. Why did the physical examination result for military recruitment at Puyang County Traditional Chinese Medicine Hospital say my child has hepatitis B!"

The reporter went to Puyang County Traditional Chinese Medicine Hospital for investigation. The hospital director Mu Huapu said that the county armed forces department and health bureau had designated their hospital to conduct physical examinations for this year's winter military recruitment. To do a good job in this work, they required all staff participating in the military recruitment physical examination to strictly, seriously, and cleanly complete this political task and send high-quality recruits to the army. Regarding the physical examination results of Mao Jingbiao, Mu Huapu said that he is not familiar with laboratory testing business, and this year's military recruitment physical examination work was handled by Deputy Director Wang Changpu.

Regarding the physical examination results of Mao Jingbiao, Deputy Director Wang Changpu said that the specific work was handled by the laboratory department, and why such a situation occurred, he also could not explain clearly.

Mao Jingbiao, using the pseudonym "Jia Xiaozhu", underwent a hepatitis B "two pairs and half" examination at Puyang County Traditional Chinese Medicine Hospital. The attending physician was Liu Hongkui, who is also the deputy director of the hospital. Liu Hongkui said that around 8 a.m. on the 14th, indeed, a young man requested a hepatitis B "two pairs and half" test. Upon identification, "Jia Xiaozhu" was Mao Jingbiao. Mao Jingbiao's family informed Liu Hongkui about this matter, regarding Mao Jingbiao's two blood tests within 30 minutes, conducted in the same laboratory department of Puyang County Traditional Chinese Medicine Hospital but under different names, with completely different test results. Liu Hongkui went to the laboratory department to inquire about the reason, and the staff said that since Liu Hongkui does not oversee the laboratory department, they should not provide him with an explanation.

Deputy Director Gao of Puyang County Health Bureau is the group leader of the Puyang County military recruitment physical examination team. Regarding Mao Jingbiao's situation, he said that it is a good thing for college students to actively join the military and support national defense construction. They are more willing to send excellent recruits to the army. Given Mao Jingbiao's situation, the health bureau held a meeting to discuss and decided to conduct a re-examination for him. The re-examination will no longer take place at Puyang County Traditional Chinese Medicine Hospital but will be transferred to Puyang County People's Hospital or Puyang County Second People's Hospital. If the physical examination is qualified, the political review process can proceed. If the physical examination is still unqualified, there will be no further physical examinations, just one chance.

After the reporter conveyed the opinion of the Puyang County military recruitment physical examination team to Mao Jingbiao and his father, his father strongly opposed it. His father said, Puyang city is so small, if someone manipulates the situation and still determines that Mao Jingbiao carries the hepatitis B virus, what should they do? If a re-examination is conducted at a municipal hospital in Puyang, any hospital they agree with, all expenses will be borne by themselves. If a re-examination is conducted at a municipal hospital in Puyang, even if it is determined that Mao Jingbiao carries the hepatitis B virus, they will accept it.

At around 6 p.m. on the 17th, Mao Jingbiao's relatives received a notice from a staff member of the Puyang County government office, asking Mao Jingbiao to undergo another physical examination at Puyang County Traditional Chinese Medicine Hospital that day. In response, Mao Jingbiao and his family did not agree.

Mao Jingbiao's father said: "If the conclusion of the test at Puyang County Traditional Chinese Medicine Hospital that the child carries the hepatitis B virus is true, it should withstand the inspection of various levels of hospitals. Otherwise, it means that someone manipulated the military recruitment physical examination."

Regarding this, the reporter will continue to follow up. (End)
