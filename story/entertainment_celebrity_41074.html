The Latest Zhang Xiaoyu Diary - Art is a Very Simple Thing

Zhang Xiaoyu Diary. Many people are unfriendly to me, thinking I am cheap, shameless, etc. I know all these. Even if many people don't say it out loud, they still think this way in their hearts. People always have the mentality of belittling others, which is the sad inferiority of human beings.

In fact, I am just an ordinary girl. I laugh when I am happy, cry when I am sad, get lost in music, and yearn for a perfect love.

I love art. It's a kind of indescribable beauty. In the experience of music and the pleasure of appreciating prose, there is a kind of inexplicable happiness. And artistic nudes have made me experience the beauty of the human body and my own beauty for the first time. I do not regret it.

It was the Internet that made me famous. I don't like being famous. Even though many people rack their brains every day to figure out how... it has nothing to do with me. In fact, I just want to quietly savor the feeling of art by myself, alone.

Now, it is meaningless to investigate who put up the first picture on the Internet. The situation has now gone beyond the control of everyone.

I admit that I was indeed at a loss for a moment. Although I had once prepared for the worst scenario where my pictures would be all over the Internet, I was still very confused when it actually happened. For a long time at the beginning, I didn't dare to go out, I didn't even dare to answer caring calls from close friends, yet our home was still harassed by someone every day. Later, I had no choice but to move to a place where no one knew one night, until now.

...

In fact, art is a very simple thing. Please give me some space to breathe, okay?
