Perpetrator: Does Not Deny "Harassment" After examination by the Procuratorate, around 1 a.m. on November 12, 2011, He Wei, after drinking at the Chaoshan Big Restaurant in the Anju area of Nanzhao Town, Zhao'an County, drove a white Audi car (with front and rear license plates covered by camouflage cloth). While passing through the Ancient Street section of Nanzhao Town, Zhao'an County, he encountered Chen Daying and his wife driving an electric scooter. When He Wei failed to get Chen Daying to stop, he followed them until near the old Public Security Bureau gate of Nanzhao Town, Zhao'an County, where he forced their electric scooter to fall over.

He Wei claimed that he did not know Chen Daying and his wife.

When most factors of the incident are still unclear, there is a risk of the situation getting out of control. The snowball of confusing facts will continue to grow, inevitably damaging the authority of the government and society as a whole.

After getting out of the car, He Wei assaulted Chen Daying. When Chen Daying's wife, Shen Shuna, tried to mediate, she was also attacked by He Wei. After being beaten, the couple abandoned their scooter and ran towards home, with He Wei following them in his car. When Chen Daying reached his own doorstep, He Wei soon arrived. Chen Daying used a brick to smash He Wei's white Audi car, after which He Wei turned left, accelerated towards Chen Daying, then reversed and fled the scene. Chen Daying was hit and later died despite rescue efforts.

However, according to other sources from the Daobao reporter, after surrendering, He Wei claimed that he could not remember the details of the incident due to excessive drinking. He said that before the incident, he drank two bottles of liquor with two friends, felt that a motorcycle had scratched his car, so he continued to follow the other vehicle intending to force it to stop. He Wei stated that after forcing the electric scooter to fall over, he got out of the car and strangled the man riding the scooter (the deceased), and was later persuaded to stop by people nearby. Later, realizing that his car's windshield had been smashed, he turned the car around, stepped on the gas, and collided with the deceased.

All of this urgently awaits investigation and clarification by relevant authorities, yet we have not received any information so far.

Before the event, He Wei reportedly followed Chen Daying and his wife while calling Shen Shuna "beautiful lady." Moreover, it was rumored that before the incident, He Wei drank with two government officials, one of whom was sitting in the passenger seat during the incident.
