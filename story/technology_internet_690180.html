Yiwu Jewelry is almost the hottest line in Yiwu commodity city. However, since 2010, labor and raw materials costs have increased substantially, which has become another major problem for the operation of jewelry businesses. In the face of the ferocious momentum of this "stumbling block," business owners have no choice but to learn new coping tricks to make transitions and break the current crisis. They change from being passive to active, from part to the whole industry. Although many companies proceed cautiously, with the utmost propulsion from all parties, the transformation is like a lit torch, lighting up the whole of Yiwu, the jewelry industry base. The concept of change strengthens the transformation dynamics.

A few years ago, the jewelry industry had quite a good market, and entrepreneurs could just "wait for windfalls." Very few companies attached importance to brand building and marketing. But now it's different; if an enterprise has no own brand, there is no advantage in marketing, making it difficult to gain a foothold in the market, especially in the domestic market. "If you want to do the domestic market, necessarily you have to do the brand," this is the consensus of the operators in the jewelry industry. The brand contains a series of product development, standardization of consumer management, precision of marketing, and personalized customer service content, so the operator essentially gains the new concept of "brand."

I understand from the Chinese jewelry network and advertising companies that for the past year, enterprises doing brand advertising have increased significantly, and more and more enterprises begin to bear the image speak and model shoot. "Electronic commerce," "network marketing" are the hot words for the past two years in the jewelry industry. Many business owners don't know much about computer operations, but they also set up a special department, instructing staff to learn the operating. Even some companies incur "determined to grow, flower hair" due to the lack of experiences, but it caused little changes to the old form.

In addition, the internal change is the most obvious: employees' benefits and wage progress, improvement and establishment of various systems, the technology upgrade — all these new thoughts bring a turning point for jewelry enterprises.
