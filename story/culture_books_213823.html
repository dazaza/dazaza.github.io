Property Management Company's Autumn Carnival Event Planning Proposal

by lk0fwdd on 2009-07-24 15:44:51

Dynamic and Colorful New Homeland - *Plaza Autumn Carnival Event Planning New Strategy

Event Time: September 24, 2005 (tentative)

Event Location: No. 238 Changfu Road, Dalang Town, Dongguan City

Organizer: Dongguan Changhe Property Investment Co., Ltd.

Planning and Execution: Shenzhen Tangshengren Etiquette Promotion Institution

Participants: Several owners and potential property buyers

I. Event Theme

Changfu Plaza, the core of a new economic mutation, is located in the top business circle of Dalang Town's commercial center. Successfully developed by one of Dongguan's famous developers, Dongguan Changhe Property Investment Co., Ltd., Changfu Plaza integrates shopping malls and residences, embodying the urban efficiency, convenience, and noble heritage, making it a brand-new landmark architectural building in the city. It is also a win-win platform suitable for merchants, entrepreneurs, and investors to gallop across the world. Through a colorful, dynamic, and European-style autumn carnival event covering the entire second phase of Changfu Plaza, we aim to gather popularity, encourage enthusiastic visits from property buyers in Shenzhen and Huizhou, and trigger the hottest commercial atmosphere! Let the participants be reluctant to leave Changfu Plaza, thereby showcasing the strength of Changhe Property, achieving the purpose of promoting sales strategies, and increasing the sense of belonging for the owners of Changfu Plaza.

II. Main Event Line

A grand and jubilant parade, where the smiles of the artists are our salvoes ringing in a new chapter; diverse and multi-styled cultural performances bringing each guest a brand-new visual enjoyment; dynamic and fun-filled carnival interactive games bringing new vitality to Changfu Plaza!

III. Scene Arrangement

To express welcome to the guests while showcasing the luxurious charm of Changfu Plaza, it is suggested that the environmental arrangement starts from the outer environment so that guests can already feel the joyful atmosphere of the carnival event during their ride to Changfu Plaza. Moreover, extensive peripheral arrangements can also serve as good publicity and provide direction guidance for the guests.

Peripheral Scene Arrangement:

- Flags Specifications: 1.5 meters X 0.5 meters, made of satin fabric, various colors

Quantity: 100 flags

Position: Placed on one side of the road surrounding Changfu Plaza

- Floating Lantern Balls Specifications: Ball skin diameter 3 meters, made of PVC material, lantern shape

Quantity: 8 balls

Position: Suspended above Changfu Plaza's periphery

- Festive Lantern Pillars Specifications: Height 6 meters, including large characters on pillars

Quantity: 6 pillars

Position: Placed on both sides of all entrances to Changfu Plaza

- Dragon Archway Specifications: Span 15 meters, including double-sided printed words

Quantity: 1 archway

Position: Placed at the main entrance of Changfu Plaza

Square Scene Arrangement:

- Dynamic Stage Specifications: 8 meters X 3.6 meters X 0.5 meters, constructed with steel frames and 18mm stage boards, red satin fabric laid on the stage surface, surrounded by curtains, steps on the front side, decorated with colored plants

Quantity: 1 stage

Position: Placed in the center of the front square

- Background Sign Specifications: 8 meters X 3.6 meters, composed of steel frames, combination boards, and prints

Quantity: 1 sign

Position: Placed behind the stage

- Event Guide Signs Specifications: 1.5 meters X 0.6 meters, made of stainless steel with prints

Quantity: 4 signs

Content: Summary of event content, site area distribution map

Position: Placed at designated locations within the event site

- Performance Sound System Specifications: Professional performance sound system, with multi-channel mixer and tuning equipment, total of 6 speakers, including DJ adjustment

Quantity: 1 set

Position: Placed on both sides of the stage

- Lighting Specifications: Professional lighting equipment, including light stands, spotlights, computer-controlled rotating lights, smoke machines, bubble machines, and lighting technicians

Quantity: Full set

Inner Courtyard Scene Arrangement:

- Curtain Decorations Specifications: Golden curtains, width 0.8 meters

Quantity: 100 meters

Position: Hung along the eaves on both sides of the inner courtyard

- Colorful Ribbons Specifications: Various colors

Quantity: 500 meters

Position: Stretched across the inner courtyard

- Column Packaging Specifications: Packaged with golden cloth

Quantity: 30 columns
