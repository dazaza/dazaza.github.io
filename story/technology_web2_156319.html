9911 Small Character post: microblogging service launched by Myspace China

by kissfire on 2009-06-04 16:42:05

Myspace China launched a micro-blogging service called 9911 micro-blogging service today. 9911 Small character post is a micro-blog tool that can be easily recorded and quickly shared by myspace. Through it, users can share their feelings, findings and observations with friends in the form of a sentence anytime and anywhere. At present, users can use computers, desktop clients, mobile phones, SMS, MSN bots and other ways to send and receive their status information.
