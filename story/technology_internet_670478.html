[Chongqing] Successfully Completed the Spring Festival Travel Rush Work_4

by wzcx2v5z on 2012-02-20 22:11:48

Under the strong leadership of the Municipal Committee and the Municipal Government, with the joint efforts of relevant departments such as transportation, public security, work safety supervision, and maritime affairs, there has been close cooperation among highways, waterways, civil aviation, railways, and urban public transportation. By February 16th, our city has successfully completed this year's Spring Festival travel rush work, achieving the goal of "safety, orderliness, and convenience". During the 40-day Spring Festival travel season, a total of 67.4029 million passengers were transported across the entire city, an increase of 13.56% compared to last year. Among them: road transport completed passenger volume of 62.1835 million, an increase of 13.80% over the same period last year; railway transport completed passenger volume of 28.938 million, an increase of 6.83%; civil aviation transport completed passenger volume of 11.850 million, an increase of 19.38%; waterway transport completed passenger volume of 11.406 million, an increase of 13.12%.
