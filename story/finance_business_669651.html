The short mop... Teacher Xiao has been busy handling cases recently, so diligent Xiao stayed at home like a guard chicken, neither going out to dance and exercise, nor playing mahjong with the aunts in the yard for entertainment. For the former, she had lost interest, and for the latter, she had always detested it deeply. Thus, while the teacher was away, she took the opportunity to repair and clean the household items, determined to keep her small family spotless and fresh. One reason was to do something for this home and enjoy her own happiness, killing time and exercising her body; the other was to give the teacher returning home in a few days a completely refreshed feeling. Family warmth and happiness would thus enter into a virtuous cycle...... After dismantling, washing, folding, selecting, wiping, and mopping, unexpectedly due to her great mood, her strength increased compared to usual, plus the life tools that looked handsome nowadays were often as shoddy as paper-made, looking good but not practical! Hehe, after a few rounds, one elegant mop "cracked" and broke its spine, startling Xiao in the quiet atmosphere. The sentimental Xiao still washed it and hung it in the corner of the bathroom. This could also reflect her characteristic of being all talk and no action. Due to the many affairs of working professionals, and letting it stay in its place, waiting for some time to "renew", there was nothing more to say.

Unexpectedly, this morning, the teacher returned fully loaded, sympathizing with Xiao's hard work these past few days, and personally cooked a few home-style dishes to reward Xiao. Just when they needed this "handsome mop," it was seen curled up in the corner of the wall. Clearly knowing it was broken by his loving wife while cleaning, he, known for his humor, used all his comedic skills to tease her with a cold yet warm joke: "Old woman, did you buy a short mop or what?" Heart-to-heart communication! With just one sentence, both Xiao and the teacher burst into laughter. The generous Xiao immediately adjusted the joy between them: "LX, I'm incompetent, today I discovered how truly funny you are, once you start joking, you become instantly famous! High, truly high! But your jokes might make people laugh themselves to death!" The gentlemanly teacher even made coquettish eyes while hugging the petite Xiao on his shoulder: "You've worked hard at home! Let's have fun!" Both laughed until they couldn't catch their breath! Especially Xiao, who cried from laughing so hard!
