The Development Trend of Automotive Products

The development of the economy and the increase in the number of cars have opened up a market for automotive beauty products. After purchasing a new car, doing some simple decoration has become the choice of many car owners. However, due to the relatively short development time of the automotive products market and its immaturity in various aspects, this has to some extent restrained the development of automotive beauty products and car maintenance products. According to the person in charge of the Guipai Automotive Products Wholesale Network, automotive beauty products will develop in the following directions:

Firstly, automotive beauty products need to improve product quality. The profit from automotive beauty product decoration mainly comes from the price difference of materials and labor costs. Some merchants often substitute inferior products for better ones in order to gain profits, which affects product quality and also damages the overall image of automotive beauty products. Inferior explosion-proof films and rearview mirrors bring great potential hazards to drivers, leading consumers to lose trust in automotive beauty products and car maintenance products.

Secondly, automotive beauty products should establish industry standards. Due to the lack of unified service standards, the quotations for automotive beauty product decoration services vary greatly among different merchants. Industry insiders believe that only by establishing unified service standards can the automotive beauty products industry unify service quotations. If the automotive beauty products market becomes a market of excessive profits and service quality is difficult to measure, consumers will lose confidence in the beauty products decoration market in the long run.

Finally, improving the skills of personnel providing automotive beauty products services. It is difficult to distinguish quality and effect immediately after the decoration of automotive beauty products. Therefore, there are currently quite a few "four-no" beauty products on the market - those without proper training, without professional brand products, without professional machinery and equipment, and without service quality guarantees. According to reports, there are 9,000 enterprises specializing or concurrently operating in automotive beauty product decoration in China, with about 150,000 employees, many of whom are unlicensed operators.
