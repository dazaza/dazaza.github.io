Love Paves the Way to Happiness: Liu Yong's Family, the First "Safe Production Civilized Family" of the Group Company in 2011

"Holding hands and looking at each other with tearful eyes, we are speechless with sorrow." The famous poet of the Northern Song Dynasty, Liu Yong, described a touching love in his poem "Rain Bell". In the ten-mile mine area, there is such a loving couple: the husband, Liu Yong, is an electrician of the preparatory team of the mining district of the third mine, and the wife, Yin Aibin, is a teacher at the No.2 Vocational School of the group company. For more than ten years since their marriage, the wife has silently contributed, paving the way to happiness with true love and family affection. Liu Yong has been awarded the title of "Five Goods" Party member of the group company for three consecutive years, and has been rated as the "Model Worker" and "Advanced Producer" of the mine many times. In 2011, Liu Yong's family was honored with the title of the first "Safe Production Civilized Family" of the group company.

Being the Backbone of Her Husband's Safety

"When I chose to marry Liu Yong, it was because of his sincerity and hard work..." said Yin Aibin. When they got married, some relatives and friends opposed their union, telling Yin Aibin that frontline workers were hard, tiring, and dangerous, and marrying one would mean more sacrifices. However, Yin Aibin resolutely married Liu Yong in 2000.

After marriage, apart from her normal working hours, Yin Aibin spent most of her time doing housework. Regardless of the cold or heat, she insisted on getting up at five o'clock every morning to cook breakfast for Liu Yong before waking him up. Every day when she saw him off to work, she gave him some reminders. Sometimes when Liu Yong wanted to help with the housework, Yin Aibin laughed and pushed him away, saying, "Your job is to rest well and ensure you have enough energy to focus on your work underground. Go to work happily and come home safely."

Once, Liu Yong had a four o'clock shift, but due to a fault in the underground roadheader, he had to work overtime. After finishing the task and taking a shower, it was already noon the next day. When Liu Yong called his wife, she burst into tears. Liu Yong realized that he hadn't informed her through the dispatch station about the overtime, making her worry all night. Although Liu Yong felt very guilty, when he returned home with a tired body, his wife had already prepared a meal for him without uttering a single word of blame.

As a husband, Liu Yong did not辜负the good intentions of his wife. In his work, he always prioritized safety. He often told his colleagues, "We must work under safe conditions; otherwise, it would be too unfair to our wives and children." In over ten years of work, Liu Yong never had any violation records during production, let alone minor injuries. Liu Yong also voluntarily identified potential safety hazards in production. Last year alone, he discovered and handled more than thirty various safety hazards, making positive contributions to the safe production of his team and earning praise from both leaders and colleagues.

Working Hard to Repay His Wife

Since joining the workforce, Liu Yong has diligently studied business knowledge and obtained certificates for multiple job operations. He once won first place in the anchor drill operator competition of the Yangmei Group. Later, due to work needs, Liu Yong became an electrician.

In recent years, with the strong development of the group company, new coal mining technologies and equipment have been continuously put into use. To quickly master underground electrical knowledge, on one hand, Liu Yong persisted in organizing what he encountered in daily operations that he didn't understand, then sought advice from senior technicians until he fully understood them. On the other hand, he bought a large number of professional books and used his spare time to study. As soon as he returned home, he would immerse himself in circuit diagrams and mechanical books. His wife, Yin Aibin, often joked that Liu Yong was living in a hotel. Even their child frequently complained to grandparents, saying, "Dad comes home only to eat and read books; he doesn't do anything else." With his diligent learning spirit, Liu Yong quickly mastered various techniques for maintaining and inspecting underground excavation machinery.

Last year, during the installation process of K8302, there was a problem where the belt intelligent switch could not start. The electrician on duty worked for an entire shift but failed to restore power. At this critical moment of the installation, the fault could affect the entire project progress and directly relate to the connection of the working face. That day, while resting at home, Liu Yong received a call from the dispatch office and rushed to the unit without hesitation. After analyzing, Liu Yong confirmed that the frequency converter in the intelligent switch was damaged and unable to supply power normally, requiring replacement of the frequency converter. However, there was no backup frequency converter available underground, and it would take about seven to eight hours to organize one from above ground. After studying and analysis, Liu Yong used his own technical expertise to solve the problem, winning valuable time for production organization.

The preparatory team one of the excavation district is responsible for the installation tasks of all connecting working faces in the mine. Last year alone, they installed and dismantled more than twenty working faces. Together with his colleagues, Liu Yong overcame numerous difficulties such as poor conditions, long transportation lines, and serving multiple teams. From the installation of large equipment like transfer machines and roadheaders to the laying of lines, they successfully completed all installation tasks with excellence and safety.

In last year's mine electrician competition, Liu Yong achieved the second place. Talking about his achievements, he said, "I can only repay my wife's sacrifices by working hard."
