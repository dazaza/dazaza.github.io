If she's not a virgin, would you want her?

by zxyhszzfp on 2011-06-13 09:36:17

In a remote mountain village, there were two sisters who got along very well since childhood.

In the year the elder sister took the college entrance exam, due to the heavy study burden, she had to take night classes.

That night, she told her younger sister that she was going for class. The younger sister said, "Come back early, be careful on the way."

Time passed minute by minute, the younger sister estimated that her elder sister should have come back, but why hasn't she returned yet?

The younger sister waited anxiously and couldn't wait any longer, so she woke up their parents, and the whole family went out to look for the elder sister. They searched all over the village,

but they couldn't find any trace of the elder sister. The whole family was worried and anxious.

Finally, they found the elder sister in a small woods at the edge of the village. She was lying there unconscious. When their parents saw her, they were shocked,

because the elder sister's clothes were disheveled, lying there motionless. The younger sister fainted on the spot.

The next day, when the younger sister woke up, she knew that her elder sister had been raped the previous day. The younger sister was silent for a long time.

The parents told the younger sister: Don't tell anyone about this matter, because it concerns your elder sister's future and life. The younger sister remained silent...

Day after day, both the elder sister and the younger sister grew up. Only their family knew about that incident, and it became the shadow of their family.

The parents were always thinking about finding a partner for the elder sister. Finally, one day, the elder sister met a suitable partner.

The elder sister and her partner got along very well. At that time, the elder sister seemed to forget that she was no longer a virgin. She felt like a princess with a prince by her side, the happiest girl in the world. The boy also liked the elder sister very much and obeyed her every wish.

The younger sister was also very comforted. She silently prayed that her elder sister had found a good place to settle down. She hoped that her elder sister's life would always be like this, like an angel living in heaven, forever living a happy, peaceful, and stable life. This is how the younger sister prayed.

However, things didn't go as planned.

Just before the elder sister and her partner were about to get engaged, the elder sister told him that she was not a virgin and had been raped when she was young.

The partner was stunned. After a while, when the boy came to his senses, he told the girl: "I can't accept you, we... we should break up. Even if I could accept you, my family might not be able to accept this cruel fact."

The girl didn't say anything and just left.

When she got home, the younger sister noticed that the elder sister was in low spirits and asked what happened. The elder sister said nothing was wrong, telling her to sleep because she still had school the next day. So, the younger sister didn't ask anymore.

The next day, when the younger sister came home from school, she saw her parents crying. When she went into the room, she saw her elder sister lying on the bed in a white wedding dress.

The younger sister was stunned. On the table, there was an empty bottle of sleeping pills. Tears fell from the younger sister's eyes. She shouted loudly: "Why are you so foolish? Why did you leave me?" She fainted several times, cried again when she woke up, and her parents were also crying beside her.

Her father shouted loudly: "Heaven! What sins did I commit in my previous life? Why do you treat me and my family like this?"

After the family dealt with the elder sister's funeral, they found a letter written by the elder sister for the younger sister. The letter read:

Younger Sister,

Forgive me, because I really couldn't bear the blows of reality. I told my fiancé that I am not a virgin.

He said that he couldn't accept such a fact, and neither could his family, because they couldn't accept the fact that I am not a virgin.

Younger Sister, forgive me. In the future, you must take good care of our parents. Don't forget your promise to me.

I can only wear my pure white wedding dress, take my tainted body, and go to heaven to find someone who can accept me.

Forgive me, my foolish elder sister...!!

After reading the letter, the younger sister made a firm resolution to fulfill her promise to her elder sister - to go to college.

Time flies, and the younger sister indeed fulfilled her promise to her elder sister and was admitted to college. On the day she was admitted, she went to her elder sister's grave and cried, saying: "Sister, I have fulfilled my promise to you. I will find a boyfriend in my own way.

If you are watching over me from above, watch how I find a boyfriend in your way."

During her university years, the younger sister underwent a great transformation. She became a tall, graceful, and very beautiful girl.

Girls like her are often pursued by many boys because they are the ideal partners in the hearts of many boys.

Every time she dated a boy for a period of time, she would tell the boy: "I am not a virgin, do you still want me?"

And each boy's first reaction was always shock and disbelief, standing there dumbfounded.

Whenever the girl saw such a situation, she would turn around and leave without looking back.

After graduating from university, she applied for a job at a network company. There too, she was not short of admirers because beauty is loved by all.

But every time she told the boy that she was not a virgin, their reactions were the same as those of the boys in school.

One day, a tall, handsome, and sunny boy joined the company. He was promoted quickly.

The boy was young and successful, tall and handsome. Many girls showed their affection towards him, but this girl seemed indifferent to him. Later, he discovered that this girl not only had grace but was also full of womanly charm. Why wasn't anyone pursuing her?

So, he began to pursue her. Seeing such a handsome man pursuing her, and knowing that this handsome man was the prince in the hearts of all the girls in the company, now starting to pursue her, she thought: Why should I refuse?

She dated the boy for some time, and during that time, she was very happy, almost forgetting the words she had to tell the boy.

Later, one day, when they were together, the girl told the boy: "Sorry, I have hidden this from you for so long. Now I must tell you... I am not a virgin, do you still want me?"

The boy reacted the same way as the other boys before him—shocked and dumbfounded. And as usual, the girl turned around and left without looking back.

Back in the dormitory, the girl shed tears for the boy for the first time because she truly loved him.

But why was he just like the other boys before? He was raised in the city; why was his thinking still so...

The girl kept pondering, tears streaming down her face until she fell asleep crying.

A few days later, when they met at the company, they acted like strangers.

On a rainy night, the girl was drinking coffee in a cafe, looking at the rain outside the window, feeling heavy-hearted, and reminiscing about him.

Not long after, she saw a familiar figure—it was him, holding flowers, drenched in the heavy rain.

He shouted loudly: "Rou, I love you!

I don't love your body, but your pure and clean soul.

I've thought about it for so many days. How could I be like those ordinary people?

I come from an educated family, my thoughts shouldn't be the same as theirs.

I can't be confined by traditional thoughts, I should break free from feudal ideas.

I... I... I know I was wrong, please forgive me.

I... I love you... Marry me..."

Unable to control her emotions any longer, the girl ran out and threw herself into the boy's arms. They embraced under the rain, as if time had stopped at that moment, comforting each other. At that moment, the girl said to her elder sister:

"Sister, I have found my true love, thank you."

On the night of their honeymoon, the boy was surprised to discover that the girl was still a virgin.

He asked why, and the girl completely explained everything to the boy. The boy was amazed and said: "You... Your actions are right.

To be able to marry you as my wife, I must have accumulated virtue for eight lifetimes."

The girl smiled happily in the boy's arms, her smile so sweet, so happy......!! End......

May all lovers be faithfully devoted to each other~
