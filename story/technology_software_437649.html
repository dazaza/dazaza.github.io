Centipedes have been used as traditional animal-based Chinese medicine for thousands of years. The government has always called for vigorous efforts to catch centipedes every year. In some main producing areas of centipedes, the original seasonal catching and seasonal purchasing has gradually changed to cross-seasonal catching and all-year-round purchasing. The purchase price per centipede in major Chinese herbal medicine markets has soared from a few cents to over 1.5 yuan, with even higher export prices, and the demand exceeds supply, causing prices to rise year by year. However, due to deforestation, pesticide and fertilizer pollution, and other ecological damage, wild resources are becoming increasingly scarce, leading to a severe shortage of centipede resources and rising prices. Therefore, a once-in-a-century opportunity has unexpectedly presented itself. In the coming years, the centipede farming industry will surely become a shining highlight in the special animal husbandry sector, with significant economic and social benefits. With the development of modern medicine, especially after China's accession to the WTO, traditional Chinese medicine faces unprecedented opportunities. International pharmaceutical institutions no longer require Chinese medicine products to be pure products with known structures but can be mixtures with fixed components, stable efficacy, and reliable safety, providing favorable conditions for Chinese medicine to go global. In terms of health care and tonics, centipede-based health products with health benefits are gaining increasing attention and popularity. Studies on its toxicology, nutrition, and efficacy show that Shennong Wine is highly nutritious, containing more than 15 types of amino acids and trace elements needed by the human body, with evident analgesic and detoxifying effects. Moreover, centipede health products have various functions such as kidney-nourishing, yang-enhancing, and boosting the immune function of the body. Therefore, vigorously developing centipede farming and developing a series of centipede products to gradually move towards industrial development and utilization is a contemporary production need. ●Policy Support:

After the 16th National Congress of the Party, especially in the Eleventh Five-Year Plan, the Central Committee of the Party has paid high attention to the three rural issues centered on increasing farmers' income. Developing centipede farming, entering rural areas, and getting close to farmers can not only earn foreign exchange for the country but also meet the material life needs of the people. Therefore, it is the preferred project for adjusting the agricultural structure at the grassroots level in rural areas, providing farmers with another practical and feasible way to increase their income and help them get rid of poverty. At the same time, it opens up a rare path to wealth for laid-off workers and those seeking re-employment.
