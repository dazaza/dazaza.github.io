Guomao Plaza Office Building: From a marketing perspective

by cffc20127 on 2012-02-19 22:48:46

Commercial property, a new driving force for China's economy. The development and construction of commercial real estate has a tremendous pull on the entire economy, not only basic industries but also manufacturing, design, consulting, management and other intermediary industries. During the construction process, significant productivity is formed. More importantly, after the completion of commercial real estate, it will affect the country's tax revenue, employment, and citizens' welfare, involving all aspects of family life. Especially in the current financial crisis period, the development of commercial real estate can drive the development of the entire industrial chain, including the growth of the consumer market, which has positive significance for enhancing socio-economic activities.

Commercial real estate involves many sciences, such as economics, cultural science, and many other fields. Our commerce, including our commercial real estate, itself is a culture. From planning and design, it should have culture. We Chinese people should have Chinese cultural elements in our projects; we cannot blindly plagiarize others. We should learn from the West, but we must not lose our own things. When doing commercial real estate, it should be regarded as a cultural cause, starting from design and planning.

From a marketing perspective, China's commercial real estate needs to cater to the demands and tastes of the Chinese people. This requires us, the developers of commercial real estate, to first understand the consumption psychology of the Chinese people. Developers and operators need to work together to create Chinese-characteristic commercial real estate and its operations.

In recent years, more and more people have started engaging in commercial real estate, especially in the Yangtze River Delta region, where economic development provides external conditions for the rapid development of commercial real estate. However, the development of commercial real estate in China is still in its infancy. The quantity may be increasing, but there are not many with truly good quality. In the Yangtze River Delta region, some projects enter the vacancy period immediately after completion, some even though they have opened, their operating status has never been ideal. The problem of project homogenization still exists.

In fact, this special real estate product of commercial property itself contains a complex relational system. First, there is a relationship between the government, enterprises, and the market; among the business community itself, there is a relationship between developers and operators; this also includes the relationship between the economy and culture. Taking the ongoing financial crisis as an example, the role of commercial real estate in reviving the economy becomes evident.

The financial crisis is both a bad thing and a good thing, a grand review for all industries. Under the backdrop of the financial crisis, commercial real estate will definitely face more difficulties. This requires our entire industry to unite and deal with it together. For commercial real estate to develop faster and better, it is necessary to improve communication and exchange between commerce and real estate, commercial planning and urban planning, investors and operators, enterprises and government departments. Regarding the Yangtze River Delta region, this is precisely the responsibility and mission of the regional city commercial real estate alliance.
