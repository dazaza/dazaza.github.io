Detailed Explanation of Wedding Photo Album Knowledge

by zzzfwaa17o on 2010-06-01 14:00:04

Detailed Explanation of Wedding Photo Album Knowledge: Oil painting frames, laminas, raymonas, bible albums, cross-page, digital design, Tianjin photography studio. With so many professional terms, do you get confused when negotiating orders at the photo studio? Now let me briefly explain these terms to avoid being misled by the studio salespeople.

Traditional wedding photo album:

Generally made by the studio itself, where photos are simply stuck on the inner pages with double-sided tape, resulting in low production costs. There are obvious edges and corners, which can easily come off or deform over time, especially in humid areas with a lot of plum rain in the south, where albums can easily deform.

Full-page wedding photo album:

The photos are printed out exactly as they are, for example, if the album is 18 inches, the printed photo will also be 18 inches. This is called full-page.

Digital design wedding photo album:

Using Photoshop to combine several photos with attractive background templates to achieve good visual effects.

Cross-page wedding photo album:

After digital design, on an 18-inch album, a photo twice as large (18*2) is created across two pages. This is called a cross-page. There is usually a seam in the middle of the album, while seamless digital cross-page does not have this seam, but it is said that seamless ones are more prone to bubbling.

Lamina Crystal Frame:

The photo is pressed onto a density board and then coated with crystal liquid, which cools and solidifies into crystal. The picture backboard and crystal are combined together. The crystal surface is smooth, reflective, very clear, and becomes very bright when enlarged. It's easy to clean and there is no frame. Lamina crystal boards are slightly better than lamina prints, with a different tactile sensation. Crystals may contain some toxins, so it's best not to make large pieces as it could be harmful to health.

Oil Painting Frame:

An oil painting with a frame, elegant, generally up to about 60 inches in size. When enlarging photos significantly, oil frames are usually used. They have a long preservation period, do not deform, and provide the most stable effect.

After studying the above knowledge about wedding photo albums, I believe you will definitely find the most suitable wedding package for yourself.

Lamina Print Wedding Photo Album:

One additional process compared to crystal, where the photo is thermally or cold-mounted and machine-pressed onto a board. It has a matte finish. You can select some fashionable photos to enlarge and make them look nice. Lamina prints are compressed materials with a rough surface and some small pits.

Raymona Frame:

A new type of mounting form, using advanced technology and equipment to go through multiple processes to directly mount the photo on the backing board, creating a seamless combination of the picture and the backing board. Since the paper is completely isolated from air, it has high clarity, vibrant colors, never fades, is waterproof, sunproof, and can be wiped clean. The material of the board is thicker than Lamina.

Bible Wedding Photo Album:

The entire page of photos is pressed together with thickened inner pages using thermal mounting and gilding techniques. Between the photos, some are PVC and others are cardstock. The edges are gilded, and a cover is added. These albums are relatively expensive.
