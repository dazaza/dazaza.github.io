How to ask your boss for a salary increase? Well! It is possible, but you need to apply effective tricks to get your salary raised. How to Ask Your Boss for a Salary Increase - Tactics and Strategies

The art of negotiation: If you are a good shopper, you will surely know all strategies and tactics to bargain. You need to haggle down the price close to the actual rate and pay what you feel is right without any loss to the seller or you. The same tactic is the first step in how to ask your boss for a salary increase. It can be applied while you ask for a salary increase from your boss. But you need to be positive and a little more sophisticated than in market negotiations. Be authentic and talk about facts. Calculate your overall expenses and match them with your salary and present it to your boss. Explain to him why you feel it justifiable to ask for a better salary. The reasons you give should be acceptable and such that they can be considered. Have a smiling face to give a positive impact.

Direct conversation with your Boss: The second step in how to ask your boss for a salary increase is to have direct communication and discussion with your boss rather than discussing this with your colleagues and managers. Avoid sending an email or indirect approaches. Take an appointment and have the conversation across the table. Have a formal talk, and let your boss know why you are asking for a salary hike. Do not overrule the rules of the company, talk according to the rules.
