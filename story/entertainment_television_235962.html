Plot summary of "Sword and Fairy 3":

The story of "Sword and Fairy 3" takes place fifty years before the first generation of "Sword and Fairy." At that time, Li Sansi had just let out his first cry as a newborn; the witch queen was still an infant in swaddling clothes, and the sword saint had yet to join the Shu Mountain sect to practice Taoist magic... Suddenly, the top of the Demon Locking Tower collapsed, breaking the seal, and demons began to wreak havoc on the mortal world. The root cause of this chaos can be traced back a thousand years to the divine realm. General Feipeng from the divine realm and General Chonglou from the demon realm frequently clashed due to conflicts between their two realms, always evenly matched. Over time, they developed mutual respect for each other's abilities. Chonglou proposed a duel with Feipeng in a newly discovered fairy realm to determine who was superior. Feipeng hesitated due to the heavenly rules of the divine realm but eventually succumbed to Chonglou's persuasion and agreed. The evenly matched duel lasted for days, ultimately drawing the attention of the celestial court. Emperor Tian sent troops to capture Feipeng and hold him accountable. Sensing the approaching soldiers, Feipeng became distracted, causing his sword to be knocked down to the mortal world by Chonglou, resulting in his defeat... Time passed, and Feipeng was punished by the divine realm and reborn as a mortal, reincarnated multiple times until he became Jing Tian, a shop assistant at Xin'an Pawnshop in Yuzhou under the Tang Sect. Meanwhile, Chonglou peeked into the mortal world from the demon realm and discovered Feipeng's current lowly and ordinary reincarnation. Moved by this, he decided to help Jing Tian and thus descended into the mortal world, disturbing Mount Shu in an attempt to retrieve Feipeng's lost sword. Inadvertently, he damaged the Demon Locking Tower and accidentally obtained the Magic Sword forged by Longyang, the crown prince of the Jiang Kingdom during Feipeng's first incarnation in the mortal world.

When Jing Tian acquired the Magic Sword, he met Xuejian, the daughter of the Tang Sect. From then on, his life changed. Initially caught up in internal struggles within Xuejian's family and the toxic person incident involving Pili Hall, he later met Changqing, a renegade disciple of Mount Shu, and his fiancée Zixuan, embarking on a long journey to assist Mount Shu in finding the Five Spirit Pearls and resealing the Demon Locking Tower...
