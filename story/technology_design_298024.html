Having a stable job and comfortable home is the traditional life aspiration of Chinese people. Having a good living environment is an important manifestation of the Chinese people entering a well-off society. Wuhan Harvard liquid wallpaper, beautifying the living environment and improving the quality of life have become the most basic material requirements for people. Then, from the perspective of current domestic interior wall decoration materials in China, wall paints (latex paint) and wallpapers are the main materials. Latex paint is favored by a large number of urban and rural residents for its green environmental protection, economic durability, simplicity and generosity. Wallpapers are also welcomed by a wide range of mid-to-high-end consumers for their luxurious richness, noble elegance, and diverse styles. Although they have the same function, their effects are very different. Despite the gradual popularization and application of latex paint in urban and rural areas of our country, due to its special decorative effect, the amount of wallpaper used in hotels, restaurants, and high-end residences is much higher than that of latex paint. Faced with this huge market for wallpaper products, can we use advanced technical means to develop a kind of liquid wallpaper that not only has the gorgeous effect of wallpaper but also overcomes its various drawbacks, while being low-cost and suitable for all consumer groups in our country?
