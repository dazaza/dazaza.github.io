It's great to have a home!

by impark on 2007-05-27 19:26:17

Home, speaking of home, my first home was in the countryside. It was a thatched cottage, where I spent a happy childhood. Since the second year of junior high school, I left home and rented a house with my grandmother. Because our home was in the countryside while the school was in the town, it took too much time to go to school back and forth every day. At that time, my grandma got up early everyday to cook for me and wash clothes, etc., which was very hard!

My parents have been outside all the time, so we don't have a home. After I went to high school and college, I have been living in the dormitory. During holidays, I would stay at relatives' home. Although they were good to me, I still thought that there was no place like home, even if it was just a shabby thatched cottage. The feeling of being together with family is great. Of course, I've moved to Dalian now. My current home is far away from the company. I have to take a 1 hour 40 minutes bus ride or so to get to the company. Many friends asked why bother so much, why not rent a house? In fact, I also rented a house outside for more than half a year. But I always miss home. Even though at that time I lived in Dalian home for less than a month in total.

I always think that there is nothing better than family in this world, really~~ Like my sister who always said I messed up the house and nagged me nonstop, but she cared about me from her heart. She cooked for me everyday and cleaned the house, let alone my parents. This is the love between relatives.

I've always wanted to buy a house of my own, but it's so hard to buy a house in Dalian. Although I'm still a poor guy and a moonlight clan now, I am still very happy. At least I have my family. We encourage each other and care for each other, which feels great! I will repay my parents well in the future!

Mom and Dad, I love you! Forever~~
