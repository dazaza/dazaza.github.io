Summer sunflower fields, fall into the afternoon with you

by sunwaylv on 2011-09-08 17:16:56

All along, I have admitted that I am a playful child. I just want to spend 1/4 of my life earning enough money to visit tourist attractions around the world, then spend half of my life traveling with someone I like. When I expressed this wish, you pointed at me and said I was too naive.

Is that so? I was disdainful, always thinking that as long as we work together, our shadows will be engraved in every place, and our footprints will be on every road.

Is it because you are older than me? Or because you realized the reality earlier than me? My wish was cut off too early. I once thought about rushing to the city where you live and visiting some unknown scenic spots in the beautiful town you told me about.

Another summer with sunflowers in full bloom, I think the sunflowers in the university should now be blooming splendidly. Do you still remember your graduation that year? You called me and asked me to come to Hangzhou. After preparing everything in a hurry, I finally waited for the time to see you. Yes, you were the image I imagined, wearing a white T-shirt, faded jeans, and a pair of glasses on your nose. Among the sighs of your classmates, you pulled me to the sunflower garden of your school!

Do you know? That was the farthest I had ever been, and also the first time I visited such a beautiful scenic spot. But you insisted that it was not a scenic spot, but just an ordinary sunflower field. Because of this issue, we both fell silent. Do you know, my definition of a scenic spot is not how famous it is, but whether this place has left me with many memories.

Big sunflowers blossomed in the afternoon of summer, it was really beautiful at that time, and I have always hoped that this beautiful flower would bloom between us. You gently turned my head and rested it on your shoulder, with a faint taste of sunshine and flowers. You asked me what happiness tastes like? I shook my head, and then a sweet feeling rose in my heart. Maybe, that's the taste of happiness, but I didn't say it. I didn't want happiness to suffocate me too soon. I still wanted to finish visiting all the scenic spots happily with you.

You said that you have already arranged to teach in the west, and said that when I graduate, you will take me to the west of our country to see the blue sky and grassland there. I've heard that there are a lot of scenic spots there, and I have always been looking forward to it~

But after you went there, how did you gradually lose contact with me. Later, I heard from your classmates that you have actually returned to your hometown.

Following your memory, I chose to further my studies at the university. Walking through the former sunflower fields, this scenic spot must bear too many of my memories...
