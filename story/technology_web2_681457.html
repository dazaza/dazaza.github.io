There is a kind of existence called happiness.

by wangquan1986 on 2012-02-28 13:43:49

There is something called happiness. As the Spring Festival approaches, look back and think about what you have done in this year. Maybe, I am very happy at this moment. Happiness can be the small flowers and grass by the road, or it can be somewhere far away in the south or north. Anyway, I think my current life is very full and fresh. Maybe, people nowadays are all running according to the rules of this world. If there is no accident, life is a perfect blank sheet of paper. Unfortunately, there are too many unknowns in this world. Often at some point, your life becomes a vivid red sheet of paper. Perhaps, everyone has their own advantages, and they survive based on these advantages. When I think about my own advantages, I always remain calm. I started reading books since I was young, and until now I have read a lot of books. The more books I read, the broader my vision and perspective become. In my understanding, every book reflects a kind of reality. The past quarrels and disturbances disappear like clouds when I close the book, and become like dust underfoot. Maybe, reading a lot of books makes one called a bookworm. But this is the nobility and meaning of being a bookworm. Walking among the crowd, never infected by the smell of smoke and alcohol, others' disdain turns into our unique taste. In the vast sea of people, making a few bosom friends, choosing greatness in love and being loved. Maybe, I have a natural sensitivity to pop music. When I have sufficient time and energy, I have no retreat. Thus, the former bookworm is completely liberated, like a frog at the bottom of a well. During my school days, my best subject was liberal arts, but this world always has something better. Gradually, I became a supporting role, slowly, those who don't work hard become minions. Just because we lost at the starting line. But my heart is strong, and my only remaining advantage is this strength. Therefore, it's just like a basketball game, only chasing. Basketball is a spirit, in fierce competitive sports, I learned relaxation and struggle, and more importantly, how to face life calmly. The essence passed down by ancient sages that I've learned before are all embodied in sweat and passion. Sometimes, the subtle changes of life happen without us noticing. Many things we ignore accomplish their missions subtly. A person's beauty is such, and so is a person's soul. The power of time is great, and time is heavy. Early in the morning, when I open the window, I find the snowflakes covering the earth, or the garbage left from last night has been cleaned up. All of this is love, all of this is happiness. It's a surprise, it's a gift life rewards us with. Without winter, snowflakes would lose their luster, without sanitation workers, there would be no clean mornings. Originally, the terminator of hate is our love.
