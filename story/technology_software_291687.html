Chery QQ Cruise Edition hits the market, priced from 47,800 to 69,000 yuan

by 3rwf900z5y on 2009-12-04 10:37:15

Chery officially launched the "Cruise Edition" of QQ on December 2nd. Meanwhile, the 6th Annual QQ Festival was grandly held at Beijing’s Fenghua Auto Cinema.

NetEase Auto, December 2nd - The launch ceremony of the 6th Annual QQ Festival and the release event for the QQ Cruise Edition was grandly held at Beijing’s Fenghua Auto Cinema! Chery officially launched the QQ "Cruise Edition" model on December 2nd. It's reported that two new automatic models were introduced for QQme: the 1.3 AMT Freedom Cruise Edition (65,000 RMB) and the 1.3 AMT Happy Cruise Edition (69,000 RMB). Two new automatic models were also introduced for QQ3: the 1.0 AMT Freedom Cruise Edition (47,800 RMB) and the 1.0 AMT Happy Cruise Edition (50,800 RMB). QQ6 added the 1.3 AMT Cruise Edition (54,800 RMB). All new vehicles will be equipped with a 5-speed AMT automatic transmission.

Pictures from the Launch Event

At the launch event, Yang Hongze, Vice President of Chery Automobile Sales Co., Ltd., stated that the introduction of these five QQ Cruise Edition models not only completes the lineup of QQ family models, allowing all three models under its umbrella—QQ3, QQ6, and QQme—to offer both manual and automatic driving modes; simultaneously, the AMT electronic automatic transmission equipped in the Chery QQ Cruise Edition has advantages such as easier driving, lower power loss, lower fuel consumption, and lower maintenance costs. Moreover, the QQ Cruise Edition offers better cost-performance among its peers and is the only model with an automatic transmission available for around 50,000 RMB. These advantages make the QQ Cruise Edition even more competitive.

6 Years, 7 Million Units, Champion Quality in Small Cars

Event Pictures

In 2009, Chery QQ has reaped numerous rewards, selling over 150,000 units from January to November, creating a cumulative sales volume of 700,000 units for the QQ brand, with the QQ3 alone breaking 600,000 units sold—a very impressive performance.

Additionally, in October just past, both the QQ3 and QQ6 were honored with the JD Power "2009 China New Vehicle Quality Study SM (IQS) Report" quality champions in the compact car and high-end compact car sub-segments respectively.

Leading a New Blueprint for the Small Car Market

Event Pictures

With the successful launch of the Chery MZ small car QQme in 2009, the QQ brand's family lineup continues to grow. Adding to this, the introduction of the Chery QQ Cruise Edition has enabled Chery QQ to fully develop into a massive lineup encompassing 3 major products and over 10 models. In another sense, consumers' demand for automatic transmissions in small cars is becoming increasingly strong, yet there are almost no automatic transmission options available in the 50,000 RMB level small car market. The launch of the Chery QQ Cruise Edition provides Chery QQ with richer product offerings and greater market competitiveness.

7 Million Q Clan Members, United by One Brand Culture

Event Pictures

With six years of cultural heritage, the current Chery QQ has become a fashionable brand, and its innovative cultural marketing model has made the QQ brand even more well-known. Chery Automobile set a historical record for the first time surpassing 50,000 units in a single month for domestic brands in September, and accumulated sales of 430,000 units in the first 11 months, firmly holding the top position in domestic brand automobile sales, with Chery QQ making up the main share.

Automatic Transmissions Attract More Consumers

A significant portion of QQ's target audience consists of female consumers who have traditionally shown a preference for automatic transmission models. Concealed shoe boxes, CD+MP3/USB interfaces, reverse parking radars, and other intelligent E-trend devices all hold a strong appeal for women. Additionally, the AMT five-speed automatic transmission equipped in the new QQ family models offers an even simpler operation mode compared to conventional automatic transmissions, providing greater convenience for female drivers. The introduction of the AMT version will help Chery QQ series expand into new markets.
