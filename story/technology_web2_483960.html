Web2.0Share Weekly: Renting Treasure, Jig, Buy Friends, Lezhi, etc.

by ybcz0519 on 2011-08-31 18:42:32

Web2.0Share Weekly is a featured column from Tianya Haige, where every week we carefully select the latest excellent startups domestically and share them with everyone. Startups can submit their information through Weibo: @web20share or email: watsonxu{at}web20share.com.

Renting Treasure: A rental payment platform developed by Hangzhou Fangtu Information Technology. This platform is a global first and China's first service product to achieve rental loan financial functionality. Through this platform, you can pay your rent monthly with "zero down payment," meaning you pay the rent for the month after living there for one month.

Brief Review: Integration of renting and payment, establishing a rental credit system, and zero down payment seems quite attractive ;)

Link: http://www.zufangbao.com/

Jig: Joshua Schachter, the founder of Delicious, launched a new website called Jig. It’s a place to share what you need, and others can respond with suggestions or provide products that meet your needs. It's somewhat similar to a Q&A platform but focuses on solving user needs. Other users can give advice or solutions for those needs.

Link: https://www.jig.com/

Secret: Qiu Shi Baike released an iPhone app named "Secret." The Secret app provides a completely anonymous network where secrets you can't tell your family and friends, or post on QQ and Weibo, can be shared here. You can also gain help from others via the network and truly relieve the pressure on your mind.

Link: http://thesecretapp.com/

Lazy Baba: A microblogging platform for interactive exchanges between buyers and sellers starting from the buyer issuing their own demands. It is also a leading and well-known online shopping microblogging platform.

Link: http://lanrenbaba.com/

Google Phone Gallery: Similar to a Google Android phone shopping guide website, it allows you to compare different phones and check stores where you can purchase them online.

Link: http://www.google.com/phone/

GoPoYou - Your Social Shopping Microblog: A platform for friends to share shopping information. You can share the information of products you want to buy or recommend via any website using the GoPoYou bookmarklet tool, which can be simultaneously shared to your Facebook and Weibo. It is a social shopping site.

Link: http://www.gopoyou.com/

UnionPay - National Level Electronic Payment Platform: UnionPay Information Technology Co., Ltd. was initiated and established based on China International E-Commerce Center, specifically designed for government and enterprise needs as well as the development of e-commerce. It is a state-owned background independent third-party payment platform.

Link: https://www.gopay.com.cn/

Shanda Tongtong: A multi-person voice call software based on the contact list. It not only provides all functions of the contact list but also supports free multi-person voice calls. Currently in beta testing for Android clients. Such services are crucial in negotiating with operators.

Link: http://17et.com/

Dong Dong Xi Xi Shopping Assistant: A shopping bookmark that allows you to collect products from different shopping websites like Taobao, JD.com, Dangdang, and Joyo together. It includes features such as price drop alerts, following friends' collections, setting favorite items, making it a useful Taobao assistant, JD assistant, and Dangdang assistant.

Link: http://www.dongdongxixi.com/

Lezhi: bShare developed a smart reading software tightly connected to Sina Weibo. Lezhi recommends the freshest and hottest internet news based on your preferences and aims to create a personalized reading experience for you. Lezhi will sync your article comments or forwards to Weibo if allowed, and you can log in with your Weibo account.

Link: http://www.bshare.cn/lezhi

Gezbox: Focuses on designing an entirely new online shopping method. It helps people find their favorite products more easily. Sharing and discovering products becomes fun. An iPhone client is currently available.

Link: http://www.gezbox.com/

Rainbow Traffic | Your Mobile Data Expert: Rainbow Traffic can effectively help you save mobile data, thereby reducing your mobile charges. It is your mobile data expert.

Link: http://chzip.com/

Internet Influence - Evaluate Your Weibo Influence: μ Index Analysis mainly considers data such as Weibo content, activity status, whether verified, number/quality of followers, number/quality of retweets, number/quality of mentions (@), number/quality of comments, mutual follow ratio, audience range, etc., calculating an individual's comprehensive influence index through a series of algorithms. Currently in beta testing.

Link: http://www.miujia.com/
