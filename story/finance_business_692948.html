Life is Struggle

In the most sorrowful moments, one must not lose confidence. In the happiest times, one must not forget the ruggedness of life. Life is not a path filled with roses; every day is a struggle.

Each person's life journey is continuously in a state of struggle. The goal of life is to strive for success and glory. From the moment of birth to old age, we are always in a state of struggle.

Looking at the biographies of famous people, their success in their careers has all come from struggle. Even ordinary, mundane lives are without exception gained through struggle.

During youth, besides being protected by parents and guided by teachers and friends, one must also struggle against the cold and heat, fight against illness. If the family situation is poor, one must also struggle against life itself.

In young adulthood, one must struggle even more with oneself. This is the preparation period for the great struggles of life.

All the strength needed for establishing a career during one’s prime years is learned, completed, and stored during this period. It is one of the greatest keys in life.

One should cultivate good physical health, nurture good character, conduct solid academic research. Moreover, in order to secure a perfect marriage, one must also greatly struggle in the field of love.

The prime years are the most intense and brilliant period of struggle in life. Whether you can win applause from others and perform excellently depends entirely on how much effort you put in.

When you first step into society, what you see on the surface may be dazzling and radiant, and you may feel full of temptation. On the other hand, traps are laid everywhere, thorns line every step, and obstacles stand in every place.

If you want to become an upright person, or a popular figure, a famous person, or a giant in your field, you must put forth your entire energy to struggle with society, to fight for your career, and carve out a bloody path.

Once you seize a foothold and stand firmly on your feet, then use your position, power, economy, and means to various forces to develop your ideals, display your talents, grow progressively from small to large, and maintain continuous improvement without interruption. Always improve realistically, enrich, expand, and achieve success.

So does the struggle of life have an end?

No.

We live one day, we must struggle one day. We live one minute, one second, and we must struggle one minute, one second. Therefore, life is struggle.
