Fiction: Female bus conductor stabs male passenger with a fruit knife

by sznstejx3q3 on 2012-02-28 10:08:49

● A female ticket collector on bus route 8, operated by Shilong Bus Company, stabbed a male passenger in the abdomen with a fruit knife.

● Surveillance footage showed that after boarding the bus, the male passenger had thrown away his ticket casually which led to an argument. The male passenger "quarrelled" all the way.

● The female ticket collector stated that the passenger had insulted her by calling her a "little slut" and made an offensive gesture after getting off the bus. Unable to endure the humiliation, she stabbed him once.

Yesterday morning, Liu Jun (a pseudonym), aged 46, underwent a second surgery at Shilong Boai Hospital. On February 12, Liu Jun's left abdomen was stabbed by a female ticket collector on bus route 8, operated by Shilong Bus Company. For the past half month since that day, Liu Jun has been receiving treatment.

The conflict between Liu Jun and the female ticket collector started when Liu Jun threw away his ticket casually.

The reporter reviewed the surveillance video inside the bus at the Operations Department of Shilong Bus Company. The surveillance showed that Liu Jun had been "quarrelling" for nearly 20 minutes from when he boarded until he got off the bus. After getting off the bus, he made a gesture with his finger which the female ticket collector considered as an insulting action.

Miss Xu, the involved female ticket collector, told the reporter that being scolded all the way, Liu Jun's actions after getting off the bus ignited the situation, leading her to act so irrationally. "I'm divorced, yet he called me a little slut and scolded me all the way..."

Mr. Li, the head of the Operations Department of Shilong Bus Company, stated, "It is definitely wrong for the female ticket collector to stab someone with a knife, but they also need to be respected."

Currently, the biggest contradiction between both parties lies in the issue of compensation.

By reporter Wang Wanli

Injured party: The female ticket collector's bad service attitude made people angry.

At around 4 PM on February 12, Liu Jun boarded the No. 8 bus with the license plate number "Yue S73029" at Shilong Railway Station. His destination was Yuwu in Dongcheng. He was holding vegetables and leaned against the railing near the door playing with his phone... Yesterday, lying on the hospital bed at Shilong Boai Hospital, Liu Jun vividly recalled everything that happened.

Liu Jun told the reporter that he just underwent a second surgery yesterday morning. "Because the ultrasound found some adhesions after the original surgery, this surgery separated the adhesions." Liu Jun said that the female ticket collector's stab fortunately did not injure any internal organs. "At that time, after I boarded the bus, I paid and received the ticket without paying attention and discarded it casually." Liu Jun recalled, "After the female ticket collector noticed, she asked me to pick up the ticket, and that's how the conflict began."

He believed that "the female ticket collector first used insulting words" was the reason that made him angry. "She said if you have money, don't take the bus, drive your own car." Liu Jun said, "Why can't I take the bus? With such a bad service attitude, what's wrong with me saying a few words?"

Progress: The injured party demands 100,000 yuan in compensation, but the gap is too large to reach an agreement.

However, after half a month since the incident, the matter has still not been properly resolved. Mr. Li from the Operations Department of Shilong Bus Company stated, "Mainly because both parties cannot agree on the compensation amount." Liu Jun said that after the first surgery, he initially requested 80,000 yuan in compensation, "Now that I've had a second surgery, 80,000 yuan is definitely insufficient; at least 100,000 yuan in compensation is required."

But Mr. Li pointed to the ticket collector Miss Xu and asked the reporter, "You tell me, she's just a working woman, how much can she compensate? She can only pay 2,000 yuan." Mr. Li said, "This conflict is entirely a personal one, and the company has already covered the medical expenses; it's impossible for the company to bear this compensation."

Miss Xu spoke in a low tone. She told the reporter that she is 30 years old and due to emotional issues, she got divorced. She detests others cursing or insulting her the most, "Yet he kept calling me a little slut." She couldn't help feeling sad.

Mr. Li told the reporter that ticket collectors like Miss Xu work 20 days a month, from 6 AM to 10 PM every day, working 16 hours a day, with a monthly salary of just over 2,000 yuan. They are very hardworking. "This matter is definitely the fault of the ticket collector, but passengers should also understand them and respect them." Mr. Li said.

What exactly happened on bus route 8?

Surveillance: Male passenger throwing the ticket upon boarding caused an argument.

On the 5th floor of the Operations Department office of Shilong Bus Company, Mr. Li, the person in charge, retrieved the surveillance footage taken at the front and rear doors of the involved bus. He said that as the matter developed to this point, he hoped everyone could see the truth.

In the surveillance footage taken at the rear door, the reporter saw that at "17:59:26," Liu Jun boarded the No. 8 bus. Mr. Li said that because the surveillance system's time was not adjusted, the recorded time was not accurate.

In the surveillance footage, the reporter saw that after Liu Jun received the ticket, he threw it away casually. The female ticket collector returned and said something, causing Liu Jun to start "arguing". The surveillance could only show the appearance of the people, unable to capture sound. Liu Jun occasionally pointed his finger at the female ticket collector. Until he got off the bus, during the nearly 20 minutes he was on the bus, he kept "arguing." Liu Jun's actions drew the attention of other passengers who turned to look at him. During this period, there was merely verbal conflict or pointing gestures between Liu Jun and the female ticket collector, no physical contact occurred.

Conflict: Actions after getting off the bus further enraged the ticket collector.

"If he got off the bus and I ignored him, then none of this would have happened." In front of the reporter, the involved female ticket collector, Miss Xu, deeply regretted.

After the incident, Miss Xu was detained for a day by the Dongcheng Sangyuan police station. The next day, Shilong Bus Company bailed her out. For these half months, the bus company has not arranged her to work, "fearing retaliation."

Staff played the recording from the front door of the involved bus. When Liu Jun got off the bus, Miss Xu was leaning against the railing at the front door looking at her phone angrily and glanced at Liu Jun as he got off.

The surveillance showed that after Liu Jun got off the bus and walked to the side of the road, he lifted his left hand and made a movement towards the departing bus, which was somewhat blurry in the recording. However, Miss Xu said, "After getting off the bus, he continued shouting insults and made that offensive gesture." This completely infuriated her.

The surveillance showed that the bus stopped, and Miss Xu took a fruit knife about 5 cm long from her bag with her left hand and rushed off the bus. Upon noticing the bus had stopped, Liu Jun also rushed over. When the two met, Miss Xu stabbed once. In the surveillance footage, Liu Jun lifted his shirt, revealing blood on his left abdomen.

Upon seeing the blood on Liu Jun's abdomen, Miss Xu stood motionlessly at the bus door. Recalling this moment in the surveillance footage, Miss Xu said that at that time, she never expected things to escalate this far, only thinking "it's all over." Police officers arrived quickly, and the injured party was sent to the hospital. Based on the conclusion of "minor injury," the Sangyuan police station detained Miss Xu.
