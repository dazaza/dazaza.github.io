Man slashes 4 passers-by with an axe and turns himself in, citing frustration as the motive

by lovei0c1 on 2012-03-06 14:02:24

A man wielding a sharp axe ran to the traffic lights near Wuzhou City in the Jiangbei district of Meizhou. After brutally injuring four strangers, he turned himself in at the police station. Yesterday, local police informed reporters that the four injured individuals were out of danger after receiving emergency treatment at the hospital. The man who committed the attack has been criminally detained by the local police on suspicion of intentional injury.

While going out for dinner, an innocent person was attacked

At around 7 p.m. on the evening of March 2nd, Xiao Zheng and his girlfriend, Xiao Liu, were heading out for dinner. When they reached the traffic light intersection near the Wuzhou City bus station on Meizhou Avenue, Xiao Zheng suddenly felt a massive blow to the back of his head, followed by intense pain and dizziness. Blood streamed down his neck before he could even turn around to see what had happened. Another blow struck him from behind. It turned out that an unknown man had wielded a sharp axe and knocked him to the ground.

Next, the middle-aged man swung his axe at Grandma Liu, who was walking home. "I was also hit on the back of my head," said Grandma Liu, still shaken but stable yesterday as she recounted the incident to reporters. After being slashed, Grandma Liu quickly covered her bleeding head and ran away. Subsequently, Mr. Li, who was riding his bike past the scene, was also struck on the back of his head by the middle-aged man who had suddenly turned around. Mr. Li could only drag his bike and run towards the direction of the Meizhou People's Hospital. The most severely injured was Uncle Huang, who was in his fifties; his head and face were struck several times with the axe.

A woman bravely fought off the attacker and escaped

When Xiao Zheng's girlfriend, Xiao Liu, saw her boyfriend being attacked, she didn't have time to react before the middle-aged man, about 1.6 meters tall, came swinging his axe at her while muttering unintelligibly.

Instinctively defending herself, Xiao Liu grabbed the man's hands tightly, holding them for nearly a minute until she couldn't hold on any longer and fell to the ground. At this point, the man stopped attacking her and turned his attention to others. Once the man had moved far enough away, the terrified Xiao Liu quickly helped Xiao Zheng up, and the two fled several hundred meters before taking a vehicle to the hospital for treatment.

The man surrendered after committing the crime

After the incident, the axe-wielding man voluntarily surrendered at the Wuzhou police station. Upon investigation by the officers, it was found that the man's name is Zhang, he is 41 years old, and he rented a room in a residential building in the Jiangbei district of Meizhou. He confessed to the act of injuring the passersby.

Why did Zhang ruthlessly attack complete strangers? According to the police, Zhang claimed that he attacked people simply because he was feeling frustrated. As a result, the police will conduct a psychiatric evaluation on Zhang.
