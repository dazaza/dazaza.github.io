March 7, 2011

I changed the layout and continued writing my baby's diary because there were too many words... The weather today is really nice. I wish it could be like this every day. My husband had tonsillitis these past two days. This silly child still complained about wanting me to accompany him for an IV when it wasn't that serious yet. Hey, such a silly child. When you like a song, you can listen to it over and over again, just like when you fall in love with someone, you will tolerate a lot of their quirks, get used to their habits, and like what they like... slowly overlapping his shadow... There are not many days left until the baby is born. My stomach feels swollen and a bit strange. I can't even imagine what I look like with a big belly. It has been difficult to eat recently. Although my appetite has increased, after eating a full meal, my stomach hurts so much, and I feel like throwing up. Being a mother is really not easy... Can fathers share some of the burden? No way.

March 8, 2011

What Women's Day, I realized it. In the afternoon, a group of old women were chatting at my good friend's store. I didn't feel like I was already among the ranks of women... My waist has been sore, and I've been very tired since getting pregnant. Luckily, it won't be long now; I'll be free soon... I just hope I can eat more every day so the baby grows strong. Chunxiang and Lin Haiqin always say it's weird that I write diaries... Damn... I want to buy myself some clothes...

March 9, 2011

"A lifetime is short, life itself is a mistake, but I am willing to make mistakes with you," I passed on my favorite saying to my friends again. It's true; many times we know something is wrong, but we still do it. We meet the wrong person, but we still continue... Right and wrong can only be understood by ourselves. Hey... Time flies. When I took out the photos from 2008, I really felt that I had aged a lot. Time waits for no one... Recently, I've been crazy about online shopping. Even the old man downstairs said, "Is the internet really that attractive?"... Hehe, it's not easy to buy something I truly like. Baby, you have to be good, Mommy will love you very much...

March 11, 2011

I like the recent weather. During the day, the warm weather makes me feel cozy while leaning by the window, drinking tea, and chatting happily. Tomorrow Miss Mao is getting married, and I'll see my good sisters again. I hope they will be very happy. Thinking back to my own wedding, I was so excited... Last night, I quarreled with Zhang Dabao unconsciously. My chest was scratched by my claws a little, which made me a bit heartbroken, but I endured it. I kept telling myself, although I love you so much, living together means I have the duty to manage you... Not because I need your obedience or want you to listen, but I hope we live together out of responsibility and love. Anyway, seeing him hugging me last night and saying, "I'm sorry," I also felt a bit tender-hearted and a bit sour. Actually, Zhang Dabao doesn't have other hobbies or messy friends. Since we got married, he has been pretty good. Women are always soft-hearted...

March 13, 2011

Even though I was very tired yesterday and didn't sleep well, I was very happy looking back on it. Miss Mao's wedding was very touching... In the ordinary, it shows that they are really strong for love. I like the feeling of gathering with childhood friends. But I don't like them saying I'm fat. Another boring weekend. Zhang Dabao is still shouting nearby. The renovation downstairs is driving me crazy... Hey... Thinking about Zhang Dabao's appearance when he drank last night, thinking about his foolish face singing 'Ten Years,' and thinking about our first complete gathering together. Even though my stomach feels uncomfortable, I'm still very happy. Anyway, I bless every friend around me to be happy. Happiness is simple, but it's not easy to come by...

March 16, 2011

I saw the long-awaited sun again. It's a bit windy. I don't remember what clothes I wore at this time last year. I feel that this spring will be colder. Enjoying a life without playing mahjong but knitting sweaters instead, spending an hour or two walking around every day, and leaving the time except for eating and sleeping to knit sweaters for the baby. I vaguely feel that the baby can hear me talking. I told him that this beige cardigan plus suspenders would make Pipi look very handsome... Hehe, absurd imagination. Yesterday, Dabao played cards with his sister-in-law's husbands. Seeing his smile, sometimes I feel that some things weigh heavily on him, making him unable to breathe. Only then does he seem a bit happier. Maybe I shouldn't always tie him to my side. I should give him some free space... (Not necessarily, depending on his performance, hehe). There are only a few days left before the physical examination. God bless everything will be fine.

March 17, 2011

These days all sorts of strange things happen. The cheapest salt has risen to three yuan and fifty cents and is out of stock. The ignorant old ladies downstairs are even fighting over buying salt, buying them by the box. I can only lament that the poor Japanese people have dragged us, these ignorant Chinese women and children... Shameful, despise. The baby's clothes are almost finalized, trendy and stylish. I plan to soak and wash them tomorrow if the weather is good... Hehe.

March 19, 2011

I woke up early this morning, not knowing why, and I don't want to eat anything. I didn't sleep well last night, and my head feels heavy and dizzy. Guangzhou VIP Information Technology Co., Ltd. Zhang Dabao stayed up all night again last night, making me very tired. The baby's little cardigan has become very long, which makes me happy. Originally, I could also do something decent. Today, let's be happy and rest more at home.

March 21, 2011

The first time I wrote a diary at dawn during pregnancy, because of my recent sleep, I haven't been in a good mood or state. Moreover, it's raining again, which I hate the most. Cold and damp. My bad mood isn't because Zhang Dabao lost money gambling. It's because I'm confused about what I'm entangled in and what kind of life I want to live. I clearly know I'm living for my husband and child, so I don't have time and space for myself, or even freedom. I'm annoyed... Hey, I envy the friends around me who can live so easily and naturally. Why can't I? Or am I just unable to let go... Depressed. I always bark nonsense on rainy days when there's nothing to do. What's there to complain about? I haven't experienced greater pain, it's just small things, right? Be calmer. If the baby is fine, everything is fine...

March 23, 2011

March has quietly passed another half. I'm very nervous. The due date is getting closer. They all say fear comes first, but once the moment arrives, it's not so scary anymore. The baby is fine. The doctor said the baby is quite big, with long hands and big feet... Very happy. However, as the baby gets bigger, it becomes restless inside me, making me unable to sit or stand comfortably... The lower back pain continues, I want to be free soon. It's dawn again, I've been going to bed at this time all week, and it seems I've adapted to it. But this isn't the life I want. It's not raining anymore, so I washed all the baby's little clothes. That feeling is really quite wonderful, looking at these cute clothes, it feels like being a mother is really not easy... Hey... Looks like I need to work hard to be a good mother. Go to sleep early, stop thinking about other things.

March 24, 2011

I've already met two expectant mothers online, and I enjoy listening to their birth experiences. I can't help but marvel at the magic of the internet... It was windy today, and I went home after lunch. I lay down and watched dumbly as the baby occasionally became restless. Pretty good... The baby's sweater is starting to take shape. I admire my patience. Perhaps becoming a mother changes one's mindset. I think I will persist forever. I look forward to the baby wearing my knitted sweater, looking handsome and adorable. Hehe, endless fantasies. The days are boring to the point of panic, even online shopping has become tedious. I really don't know what to do... The countdown to delivery has started, and I'm a bit scared...

March 25, 2011

Today's weather is super good, warm and bright, making my eyes squint. I don't know if it's because I'm single again, but I seem to have caught a cold. Catching a cold on such a day is not worth it. It's better to go home and lie in bed. I've imagined countless times how I will lose weight after giving birth and how I will shop like crazy. At least I can't miss a perfect summer, but it seems I'll have no chance... I keep staring blankly at Taobao, wanting countless things, and constantly sighing at how money slips through my fingers like sand... So I can just watch from afar without spending money. I've become obsessed with canvas lace this year, and I love searching for these things, even if I don't buy them, just looking makes me happy. My stomach has been slightly painful since last night until today. I'm so scared. I'd better take good care of myself...

It's the weekend. Walking on the street, everyone seems very busy, shopping and strolling back and forth. It seems like I'm the most bored one. When I worked, I looked forward to weekends, but now that I spend every day like a weekend, I wish I had a busy day... I slept awkwardly last night, and now I can't turn my head. It's like fate, I can't turn back... Hehe. Now, it's really hard to take a step. Walking a little makes me feel tired. How will I survive the next two months? Hey... March 31, 2011

It's the last day of March. The sun is powerful, marking a pleasant ending. As April approaches, I'm getting more nervous. The closer I get to my due date, the more scared I become. I've imagined countless times the scene of giving birth, how much pain, how much hardship. No matter how much my friends around me console me and tell me their experiences, I can't absorb them. When it's my turn, it's bound to be painful. Yesterday, when the baby moved, I could feel its little feet. I pushed gently, and it retracted. It was so interesting. Yesterday, my mood wasn't great because I witnessed a severe car accident. I shouldn't have looked, but that old lady was so pitiful. So, I remind all my friends with cars to drive slowly and follow traffic rules. Hey... May heaven bless that old lady.
