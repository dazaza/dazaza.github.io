On December 14th, according to informed sources, the first provincial physical store network mall of the National Farmers' Professional Cooperatives United Association, "Huirun Ten Thousand Families", officially went online for operation. Citizens can go online and select characteristic agricultural products from all over Anhui, with the convenience of home delivery services. The launch of "Huirun Ten Thousand Families (www.huirun365.com)" Network Mall fills the domestic market gap of single varieties, uneven quality, and lack of brand certification for special agricultural products.

1. Complete variety, covering all well-known and high-quality special agricultural products in Anhui.

2. Government support for every step of "Huirun Ten Thousand Families."

Notably, the Anhui Huirun Agricultural Products Marketing Farmers' Professional Cooperative United Association (the operating body of the Huirun Ten Thousand Families Mall) was established under the strong support of relevant government departments such as the Anhui Provincial Agriculture Commission, the Anhui Provincial Department of Commerce, the Hefei Municipal Agriculture Commission, and the Hefei Municipal Department of Commerce. It is currently the first provincial farmers' professional cooperative united association registered by the industrial and commercial department nationwide (with a registered capital of 9.2 million yuan), and also the first provincial farmers' professional cooperative united association in Anhui Province. It will become the "green aircraft carrier" for direct supply and direct sales of agricultural products, carrying the expectations of the government and the heavy entrustment of farmers, bringing green and fresh quality life to a wide range of consumers.
