Where will the cloud drift to?

I am a cloud in the sky, occasionally casting my shadow on the surface of your waves.

You need not be glad nor surprised, for I will vanish in an instant.

We met on the dark sea, you with your direction and me with mine.

You may remember, or better yet, forget the brightness we exchanged during our encounter.

My hair had just been washed, long and wet, hanging over my shoulders. I paused by the window, watching the pedestrians outside under the gloomy sky, while the melancholic tune of "Old Love Song" seemed to faintly reach my ears.

I once read this line: there is no real coincidence in this world, only inevitability disguised as coincidence. Just like how we met and came to know each other. Thinking quietly, then smiling faintly, though I don't even know what I'm smiling about - perhaps at my own obsession? That's not a good excuse, never has been.

A cloud cannot stay in one place; it must travel far, so its life is destined to be drifting aimlessly. Even if the end result is having nowhere to return to, it does not regret. I don't want to be a cloud because it never belongs to just one person. It merely drifts from one person's hands, side, or embrace to another. I can't choose who I stay by, just as I can't choose my future - that's my fate.

I've forgotten how we met, whether the weather was fine or not, what we said, happy or unhappy moments alike. All I remember is that your name found its way into my heart, regardless of whether it's buried deep within. I just want you to know how much I care. Yes, I care about you, I have since the beginning, otherwise I wouldn't have held your hand so tightly, unwilling to let go for even a moment.

If meeting is only for the sake of parting, then I'd rather we were forever apart. Do you remember our words? Darling, wherever we are, our hearts are always together! If you ever think of me, then look up at the sky, maybe on that day, I am flying right within your sight. Even if I leave, my eyes will still linger lovingly upon you. Darling, if you have already moved beyond my reach, let me follow in your footsteps... until the day I can no longer chase after you, which will be my end.

Clouds cannot be sad, for their droplets are not tears but cold rain.

My darling, have you ever heard of a cloud's love? It doesn't want to keep wandering anymore. It wants to stay for the one it loves, but it can't stop its own journey. Every time it looks back with deep affection, it's searching for your figure, again and again, refusing to continue its predestined path.

The cloud wonders, if one day it could only stay by your side, how wonderful would that be - softly encircling you, without any more farewells. But alas, we are so far apart, even if you spent your entire life trying, you could never reach me. So, stop your steps. Since one of us is destined to suffer, let me take that burden. You just stand there, waiting for me. No matter where I am, I will strive to traverse towards you until one day, when I grow weary, I'll remain by your side and never leave. Or, perhaps, dissipate before your eyes like smoke...

I am a cloud, the sky is my home. Greeting the sunrise in the morning, bidding farewell to the sunset in the evening...
