Looking Forward to the Development Trend of Virtual Hosting in 2008

by now on 2008-01-12 14:57:13

In 2007, China's economy developed vigorously, and the development of the Internet economy was especially magnificent. According to statistical data from the authoritative institution IDC, the market growth rate of the Internet industry in 2007 reached over 50%, ranking first among all industries. Reviewing the application trajectory of the Internet in commerce, in 2007, a large number of enterprises began their informatization construction. Enterprise website building drove the demand for Internet basic products such as domain names, virtual hosts, and servers. Among these, virtual hosting is one of the essential products for enterprise website building and also the most core part of a website. The author looks ahead to the trends of the virtual hosting development in 2008 based on the battles fought in the virtual hosting market in 2007.

Quality Still Occupies the "High Ground"

Carrying the online marketing resources of enterprises, there is no doubt that virtual hosting requires quality. The stability and speed of virtual hosting directly affect the normal operation of a website. Currently, the virtual hosting market is not yet standardized, with providers offering prices at various levels and quality varying greatly. Therefore, many problems have gradually emerged. A server host can only support a certain number of virtual hosts; when this number is exceeded, users will experience a sharp decline in performance. For those small-scale companies that overload the number of virtual hosts on a single server, this not only affects the speed of your website; if other customers' virtual hosts contain illegal information, the server will be shut down, implicating you and causing your website to temporarily become inaccessible. Virtual hosting is a network technology product, and users of technology products gradually realize that the performance of the product is fundamental. The security, stability, and speed of virtual hosting remain the greatest concerns of users. The strength of service providers, including hardware, software, professional technical personnel, etc., determines the quality of host usage. In 2008, service providers with professional, stable, sufficient bandwidth, and good service data centers will continue to perform well.

Tailor-Made "Fit Clothes" Trend Continues

In traditional virtual hosting use, some users waste part of the cost on unnecessary space resources. Considering both cost and management, virtual hosting isn't necessarily better when it's larger. Larger capacity or more functions inevitably come with costs, so there's no need to blindly pursue a large space. The best and largest things aren't necessarily what users need; the best is what suits them the most. As for space capacity, having enough is sufficient. When the website develops and the space becomes insufficient, upgrading later is not too late. Additionally, Era Interconnect (www.now.cn), relying on its R&D strength, pioneered customizable hosting where users can freely combine factors like space size, script type, database type, database size, data center type, operating system, and purchase duration according to their own situation. This is a technological innovation adapted to user needs, receiving excellent market feedback and drawing attention and imitation within the industry, driving virtual machines towards more personalized and humanized directions.

Users' Consideration of Price Becomes More Rational

There are many low-priced virtual hosts on the market. As the saying goes, "a penny saved is a penny earned." Prices that are too low generally mean the provider is hiding some situations from you, which could result in frequent abnormalities in virtual hosting, affecting far more than just a few hundred or thousand yuan. Facing many low-price promotions, reputable and well-known service providers can still stand firm in the virtual machine market competition.

Service Returns "Voice Rights" to Customers

Providers with weak strength either lack professional technical support teams or have technicians whose skills are not solid. When problems occur, they are difficult to handle and take longer to resolve. Even worse, some individuals manage your virtual hosting casually, leaving you unsure of whom to contact when your virtual hosting experiences abnormalities. Since your website operates around the clock, you need a 7x24-hour guarantee. Virtual machine users should not only focus on high-quality products and reasonable prices but also choose service providers with good after-sales service and high customer satisfaction.
