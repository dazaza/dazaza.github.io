Lu Xun said: It is enough in life to have one bosom friend. The latest conclusion drawn by anthropologists is that each of us has about 30 friends, of whom six are considered close friends. During our lifetime, we may make as many as 400 friends, but less than 10% of them remain in contact with us. Although 60% of people claim that friendship is more important than career, money or family, other studies show that men have fewer friends than women, and the middle class has more friends than the working class. In fact, our understanding of friendship is based on speculation. We don't know what our friends see in us, and although we may like them very much, we don't know if they are willing to establish a closer relationship with us. Therefore, sometimes we are greatly surprised by friendship. -psytopic.com
