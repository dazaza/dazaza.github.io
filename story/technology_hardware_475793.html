A career with Liushen Flower Water, more insurance and comfort.

by logitech888 on 2011-08-13 01:50:06

From the source point of view, the 1980s was a turning point for the luck of Hualewater. Liushen added a kind of Liushen original liquid mixed with peppermint camphor, borneol and Phellodendron to the Hualewater. In addition to maintaining the fragrant characteristics of Liushen Hualewater, it also added many functions such as antibacterial detoxification, heat rash relief, itching relief, and mosquito repellent.

In terms of formation, the reason why Liushen Hualewater is antibacterial lies in the alcohol and Liushen original liquid it contains. The borneol, peppermint camphor, pearl, and Phellodendron in Liushen Original Liquid are all top-class Chinese herbal medicines for detoxification and heat-clearing. Borneol can disperse stagnant fire and reduce swelling and relieve pain. Phellodendron can detoxify, remove dampness, and purge fire. There are many places in life where Hualewater can be used for sterilization, for example: soak towels and masks. Fill half a basin of clear water in a washbasin, put towels, masks and other items in it, sprinkle 4-5 drops of Hualewater, and soak for about 15 minutes. The components of borneol, Phellodendron, Borneol and alcohol in Hualewater can remove bacteria from the mask. Then boil the mask in boiling water, rub and rinse it. Before using, spray 1-2 drops of Hualewater to prevent bacterial contamination in the air.

When going out, Liushen Hualewater can be used for hand sterilization. When taking buses, subways, shopping or hiking, both hands cannot avoid touching public railings. Bring a small bottle of Liushen spray Hualewater, which can clean the bacteria on your hands at any time. Spray Hualewater into your palm, rub both hands, remember to take care of the palms, backs of hands and wrists.

Liushen Hualewater can be added when washing hair and bathing. Hair is the best place for bacteria to live. In special times, fill half a basin of warm water, drop in 5-6 drops of Hualewater, put your hair in it first, then enter the normal hair washing procedure. Do this every day, and the bacteria in your hair will be removed cleanly. If you are used to soaking in a bathtub, especially for children, you can add 8-10 drops of Hualewater in the bathtub according to the amount of water, which also has the functions of refreshing, sterilizing, and relieving heat rash and itching.

Wipe telephone and mobile phone. Especially office telephones are an important way for bacteria to spread, so we must pay special attention to cleaning and sterilization. Use a tissue dipped in a little Hualewater, carefully wipe the telephone receiver, buttons, mobile phone keyboard and body, and keep the telephone and mobile phone clean at any time.

Air sterilization. Put water and Hualewater into a sprayer in a ratio of 40:1, close the doors and windows tightly, and evenly spray them on the ground and in the air, especially in the corners of the room. Keep the doors and windows closed for about 10 minutes, then open the window for ventilation. Do it once in the morning and evening, and if possible, spray several more times.

Wash underwear. Underwear is a close-fitting item that directly contacts the skin, so it cannot be mixed with other clothes and must be washed separately. Add 4-5 drops of Hualewater in half a basin of clean water, sterilize first, spread out the underwear and put it there, soak for about 15 minutes, repeatedly knead with soap, rinse clean with clear water, and dry it outdoors, preferably in a sunny place. Spraying Hualewater can make the dried underwear fragrant and comfortable.

Summer wiping sweat stains on bamboo mats. Sweat stains on bamboo mats will make the body feel uncomfortable. Wiping the bamboo mat with clear water added with Hualewater every day can keep the bamboo mat fresh and clean. Wipe repeatedly along the texture of the bamboo mat to make the Hualewater enter the corners and gaps of the bamboo mat, which can remove dirt and bacteria hidden in the cracks, and at the same time, make the skin feel cooler and more comfortable.

If you pay more attention, you can find more new uses for Hualewater. Keeping a bottle of Hualewater at home can make life safer and more comfortable.
