Middle-aged female cashier embezzles 900,000 yuan of public funds for her lover's extravagance

by aia7892c on 2012-02-10 16:42:32

She used to be the cashier of a company with a happy family. In order to indulge her lover's extravagance, she embezzled 900,000 yuan of public funds and eventually fled with her lover. Six years later, she was captured by the police. She lived in a foreign place as a cleaner, with only 20 yuan on her, and her lover had long abandoned her. On November 28, a reporter came to Yangqu County Public Security Bureau and interviewed Zhang Meijuan (a pseudonym), the suspect who had been on the run for six years.

Zhang Meijuan, 43 years old this year, is from Yangqu. In 2003, she entered a large magnesium industry company locally to serve as a cashier. That year, she was 35 years old, married, and had a son and a daughter. Her monthly salary was more than one thousand yuan, not much, but enough to cover daily expenses. Plus, Zhang Meijuan was very good at managing her finances and never spent money recklessly on a daily basis. Therefore, when the news of her embezzling more than 900,000 yuan of public funds came out, many people did not believe it.

It was not until six years later, when the police captured her from Shandong, that people learned the truth. It turned out that she had given all the money to her lover, Zhang Wei (a pseudonym). "No legitimate occupation, has been in jail, complex background, and irascible temperament." Some people familiar with Zhang Wei's situation said so. Zhang Wei is four years older than Zhang Meijuan, a two-time labor-released person. In 2005, he delivered goods to the magnesium industry company, and cashier Zhang Meijuan was responsible for paying him his freight. The two met under such circumstances and quickly developed into a lover relationship.

Perhaps blinded by love, Zhang Meijuan followed her lover's every word. Zhang Wei said he wanted to buy a car and claimed he had ways to make money through usury, asking Zhang Meijuan to find ways to gather money, and solemnly promising to pay back soon. However, Zhang Meijuan herself did not have any money, so she had no choice but to reach out to the company account, subsequently embezzling more than 700,000 yuan. Zhang Wei used this money to purchase a Hyundai car, and indulged in extravagance, gambling everywhere, yet failed to earn a single penny.

As the company's accounts deficit grew larger, Zhang Meijuan became increasingly anxious and afraid, seeking Zhang Wei's help, but Zhang Wei replied, "I can't do anything either, or we should run away."

In June 2005, Zhang Meijuan went to her workplace for the last time, withdrawing 200,000 yuan in cash from the company account, and then quietly left with Zhang Wei. Before leaving, she made a phone call to her daughter, giving some instructions before hanging up. For the next six years, she never met her children again, not even making a phone call.

After the money was spent, the lover abandoned her

On the first day of their escape, Zhang Meijuan handed over the remaining 200,000 yuan in cash to Zhang Wei. She thought that from now on they could stay together day and night, and finally she waited for true love. At the beginning, the two indeed lived well relying on this money. Zhang Wei took Zhang Meijuan to travel to many places like Kunming and Dalian, and even took her to gamble in Myanmar.

However, the good days lasted only three years. In 2009, Zhang Meijuan unexpectedly found that there was only 10,000 yuan left in her bank card, while Zhang Wei still gambled day and night as before. Zhang Meijuan asked him for money, but her once gentle lover seemed to have changed completely, loudly scolding her and even hitting her. The most serious time, he actually beat her so badly that she had to be hospitalized. When she returned to their rented place, she discovered that Zhang Wei had already run away with the money. Only then did she realize that Zhang Wei did not love her, but just loved her money. Even so, until she was arrested, she still missed this man, saying, "He treated me pretty well."

After the lover left, Zhang Meijuan changed her name to "Zhang Jie" and started doing odd jobs, mainly as a cleaner and housekeeper. She originally wanted to work in her old profession to earn more money, but because the cashier position was important, employers would always verify her identity, which scared Zhang Meijuan from daring to apply. By chance, she saw online that a company in Weihai was hiring cleaners with a monthly salary of 900 yuan, so she took the little money she had and boarded a train to Shandong.

On November 22 this year, plainclothes police officers came to the canteen of the enterprise where Zhang Meijuan worked. At that moment, Zhang Meijuan was having dinner. She had a mole beside her eye, fearing that this feature was too obvious, she specially wore a pair of wide-rimmed glasses to cover it. Seeing these people who just came in, she felt inexplicably nervous and unnaturally adjusted her glasses several times. It was precisely this action that drew the attention of the police officers. When the police officers asked her what her name was and where she was from, she calmly answered, "Zhang Jie, from Lüliang, Shanxi." But when the police officers asked her to say a few sentences in Lüliang dialect, she collapsed on the spot. She was caught with only 20 yuan on her.

On the evening of November 24, the police brought Zhang Meijuan back to Yangqu County, which was the first time she had returned home in six years.

At Yangqu County Public Security Bureau, Zhang Meijuan sat in the interrogation room, lowering her head quickly when she saw the journalist come in. Her rough long hair was simply tied into a ponytail, with an old-fashioned jacket and pants, and a pair of leather shoes that showed her feet looked somewhat inappropriate. At 43 years old, she looked much older than her actual age.

During the interview, Zhang Meijuan kept her head down all the time, speaking in a very low voice, but talking clearly about her own affairs, almost without any avoidance. However, when mentioning her children, she suddenly burst into tears and cried on the table.

Speaking of Zhang Meijuan, the investigating police officers all said, "This woman is stupid," and when she was finally arrested, she only had 20 yuan on her. Of the more than 900,000 yuan of public funds she embezzled, very little was spent on herself, all given to her lover Zhang Wei. And when she ran out of money, the first person to cruelly abandon her was her own lover. Such blind and muddled feelings made Zhang Meijuan pay a high price. Would she regret committing a crime for such love? Faced with the reporter's question, she thought for a long time and finally whispered "regret."

She said that in the second year of her escape, she wanted to return and surrender herself, but Zhang Wei didn't allow it. Later, when she wanted to come back, she never had the courage.
