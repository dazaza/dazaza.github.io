It’s the fifth day since the launch of Hacker Applications, and we haven’t released a single software yet. In the future, I’ll organize the tools on my hard drive and then release those that I find useful.

Today, I'll release an analysis tool similar to HiJackThis—ESET SysInspector, which cnbeta has covered before. This falls under the category of security software, developed by ESET, the company behind NOD32.

ESET SysInspector is an analytical tool akin to HiJackThis, serving as one of the most advanced and easy-to-use diagnostic tools based on the Windows system. It can deeply analyze various aspects of your operating system, including running processes, registry contents, startup items, and network connections, making your system completely transparent for further operations.

Official website: http://www.eset.sk/eset-vydal-sysinspector
