Weibo's "Awakening Style" goes viral, various industry professions are parodied

by edivista on 2011-12-29 10:49:06

Click to participate in the "Awakening Style" Weibo

Recently, a joke about a person becoming a vegetable after a car accident and being awakened has made the "Awakening Style" a popular internet trend. The "Awakening Style" originates from a news report about Gan Xuemei, a woman from Jiangxi who became a vegetable after a car accident, with little hope of survival. However, her family did not give up on her, and based on her love for mahjong, they called out to her every day: "Xuemei, wake up and play mahjong! Three are waiting, come quick!" After two months, Gan Xuemei miraculously woke up. This news made the "Awakening Style" explode in popularity on websites like Tencent Weibo, with many netizens parodying it into different versions for various professions. Even Liu Chun, Vice President of Sohu, happily participated, not only forwarding the parodied version of the website's "Awakening Style" but also replying, "Too touching, Chen Tong (Executive Vice President of Sina) and I cried our eyes out." It must be said that there is a strong sense of self-deprecating humor. Below are some versions of the "Awakening Style":

Website Version: An editor at Sohu, Mr. Chen, became a vegetable after a car accident. The doctor said his chances of survival were one in ten thousand, and waking him up was even more unlikely. His leader and family didn't give up on him, and based on his workaholic nature, they recited by his side every day: "Sina made another mistake!" A miracle happened, and Chen woke up, his first words being: "Did you take a screenshot?"

Programmer Version: A programmer at a company became a vegetable after a car accident. The doctor said his chances of survival were one in ten thousand, and waking him up was even more unlikely. His colleagues and family didn't give up on him, and based on his habit of viewing bugs as mortal enemies, they repeated by his ear every day: "Your code has a bug again!" A miracle happened, and he woke up, his first words being: "Is the platform acting up again?"

National Football Team Version: The national football team became vegetables after a car accident. The doctor said their chances of survival were one in ten thousand, and waking them up was even more unlikely. Their families and friends didn't give up on them, and based on their spirit of trying to score goals, they recited by their side every day: "The ball is in, come quick!" A miracle finally happened, and the national football team woke up, their first words being: "Are we qualified?"

Stockholder Version: A stock market crash caused an elderly man to have a stroke and become a vegetable. His family continuously called out to him: "The stocks are rising, get up and operate!" Such continuous summoning resulted in the elderly man miraculously waking up one day, getting up and asking: "How much has it risen?"

Mobile Phone Version: A sales manager at a mobile phone company became a vegetable after a car accident. His family tried everything to wake him up but failed. A colleague visited and whispered in his ear: "Apple has gone bankrupt." The manager immediately jumped up, saying: "Quick, let's do a big year-end marketing campaign."

Commentary: In 2011, Apple iPhone stood out uniquely, constantly breaking sales records. The iPhone took away the territories of traditional mobile phone giants, and almost all peers envied and hated it.

iPhone Fanatic Version: An iPhone user became a vegetable after a car accident. Based on his anticipation for a perfect jailbreak, his family and friends recited by his side every day: "It's out, it's out, the iOS5 jailbreak is released!" A miracle finally happened, and he woke up, his first words being: "Is it perfect?"

Commentary: The iPhone 4S sold well across the country, but due to China's vast gray market, Apple's iOS5 had yet to be jailbroken, causing many users with mobile cards to experience incompatibility and instability issues. Apple enthusiasts eagerly awaited the jailbreak.

Real Estate Sales Version: A real estate sales manager became a vegetable after a car accident. A colleague visited and whispered in his ear: "The reserve requirement ratio has been reduced by five percentage points." The manager immediately jumped up, saying: "Quick, increase the price by 10,000 yuan per square meter!"

Commentary: Real estate regulation this year was strict, with some cities' real estate markets falling into depression. The lives of real estate salespeople have seen dramatic ups and downs.

Stockholder Version: A stock market crash caused an elderly man to have a stroke and become a vegetable. His family continuously called out to him: "The stocks are rising, get up and operate!" Such continuous summoning resulted in the elderly man miraculously waking up one day, getting up and asking: "How much has it risen?"

Commentary: The stock market in 2011 brought new and old stockholders' moods to a low point. Some lamented that behind a decade of high economic growth, the Shanghai Composite Index gasped its way back to where it started.

E-commerce Version: A books market manager at an e-commerce website became a vegetable after a car accident. His family tried everything to wake him up but failed. A colleague visited and whispered in his ear: "We've gone bankrupt." The manager immediately jumped up, saying: "Quick, let's do a big year-end marketing campaign!"

Commentary: The price wars in the e-commerce sector were so intense they were speechless.

Weibo Version: A business manager at a microblogging site became a vegetable after a car accident. His family tried everything to wake him up but failed. A colleague visited and whispered in his ear: "We've gone bankrupt." The manager immediately jumped up, saying: "Quick, attract celebrities!"
