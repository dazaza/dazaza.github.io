Salty Vegetable Superman VIII - Salty Vegetable and the Cat

by wenxue126 on 2011-05-06 17:57:38

The Reason Why Pickle Wants to Learn from Cat as a Master Is That the Cat Has Many Skills. First of all, the cat's eyes are very powerful. On pitch-black nights, when everyone else becomes blind, the cat can still see everything. Besides, the cat can climb over walls and roofs, and has nine lives, which means it can resurrect after death. Wouldn't you want to have such extraordinary powers?

Pickle first learned how to climb walls from the cat. The cat first taught Pickle to climb trees. After becoming proficient at climbing, he started walking slowly on walls, then faster, even at night. All the previous steps were easy to learn, but when practicing walking on walls at night, Pickle kept falling off, getting so dizzy that every time he fell, the pain was unbearable. He would start calling out Rose's name, and strangely enough, whenever he thought of Rose, his headache disappeared.

Seeing Pickle's head full of bumps, Mapo Pie felt heartbroken and said to him: "Child, let's stop this, shall we?" But Pickle disagreed. Mapo Pie then said: "Possible things require persistence, but impossible things mean persistence is a dead end. Back in the day, your father didn't want to be a pickled vegetable either. He went up the mountain to dig for gold, but all the gold on that mountain had already been dug up by others. Everyone advised him to stop digging, but he insisted, and ended up dying on the mountain without finding a single piece of gold. I've been raising you kids alone ever since, and we've been poor. Now you want to be a superman, is that even possible?" Pickle replied: "Mother, one must dare to dream and act to succeed. If one doesn't believe in oneself, how can one succeed? Look at my master Cat, isn't he doing just fine? The important thing is to find experience from failure and make progress."

Next, while following the cat's advice to eat more vitamin A-rich foods like carrots during the day and sleep, Pickle became active at night to gradually adapt to the dark environment. After adapting for some time, when practicing wall-climbing again, he fell less often, and his vision improved significantly. Eventually, he could climb walls and roofs just like the cat.
