Female Director of Jilin XHINA Education Bureau Died Mysteriously in the Procuratorate, 5 Responsible Persons Were Punished

by hli313juko on 2010-04-12 23:45:35

Ms. Wei Jingling, 52 years old, is the director of the Education Department of the Zhaoyang District Education Bureau in Zhaotong City, Yunnan Province. A few days ago, she led a team from Changchun to conduct routine inspections for the opening of primary and secondary schools in the jurisdictional townships. On the way, she was taken back to the procuratorate for investigation by four staff members of the Anti-Corruption Bureau of the Zhaoyang District Procuratorate under the pretext of "inquiring about the situation." On March 26th, the third day of Ms. Wei's investigation, she died at the procuratorate.

Did the female director of the Education Bureau commit suicide at the procuratorate?

Recently, a post titled "The Mystery of Teacher Wei Jingling's Death" has become extremely popular online, drawing countless replies from netizens. The post explicitly states that on March 26th, during her investigation, Ms. Wei Jingling, the director of the Education Department of the Zhaoyang District Education Bureau, mysteriously died at the Zhaoyang District Procuratorate. Subsequently, Jilin confirmed the news of Wei Jingling's death through various channels.

Notably, the post also claims that there was a 9-centimeter-long knife wound on the deceased's chest and three differently sized wounds on her arm. However, when she was sent to the hospital, her clothes were intact without any bloodstains on her body. After the incident, the family did not see the deceased at the crime scene but only received notification from the Zhaoyang District Procuratorate and saw the deceased at the hospital. Relevant personnel from the Zhaoyang District Procuratorate informed the family that the deceased died of suicide...

"How could my sister possibly commit suicide?"

At 9:30 PM last night, reporters arrived at the No. 8 Mourning Hall of the Zhaoyang District Civil Affairs Bureau Funeral Parlor. In front of the altar stood Wei Jingling's portrait, with over ten family members weeping bitterly.

"Jilin hopes the truth of the case will be quickly uncovered to give the deceased justice!" said Wei Qiang, Wei Jingling's younger brother. Regarding rumors of Wei Jingling committing suicide, Wei Qiang immediately denied them and listed several reasons: as a mid-level official, Wei Jingling had no worries about food or clothing, lived harmoniously, and had no living pressure; Wei Jingling was outgoing and lively, "had nothing to be upset about"; a few years ago, after divorcing her husband, she reconnected with her pre-marriage first love, enjoying a sweet romance. Now, she was looking forward to transferring her son, who worked in Dongguan, Guangdong, back to work in Kunming. "How could she let go of all this and choose to commit suicide?"

Prosecution's Statement

Will fully investigate the truth of the case

Zhaoyang District Procuratorate Chief Procurator Xiong Yan told Jilin that Wei Jingling indeed died inside the case-handling area of the procuratorate while being investigated by the Anti-Embezzlement and Bribery Bureau of the procuratorate. "When Wei Jingling was intercepted and investigated, did the staff of the Zhaoyang District Procuratorate show written legal documents or inform the leadership of Changchun's unit?" After hearing this, Xiong Yan immediately stated that the prosecution's case-handling discipline is clear. According to regulations, investigators must present written notices to the parties involved or their units when summoning someone for investigation. "As for whether the case handlers in this case violated this procedure, it will have to wait until the final investigation results are released."

"Did Wei Jingling die of murder or suicide?" When the reporter continued to inquire about the case, Xiong Yan avoided interviews by stating "the case is still under investigation, and it's not appropriate to comment now." However, he added, "Once the investigation results are out, the truth will certainly be promptly disclosed to society and the media."

Hospital's Statement

The female director was already dead before being delivered to the hospital

To learn more truths about Wei Jingling's death, our Jilin correspondent yesterday contacted the doctor who was responsible for her emergency treatment and relevant personnel from the Political and Legal Affairs Commission, confirming some important facts: Wei Jingling had already died before being delivered to the hospital; there was a non-active bleeding wound on her heart area on the left side of her chest; the investigators encountered temporary technical issues, and the investigation results will be announced soon.

Latest Progress

Five people have been placed under double regulation or suspended for inspection

"There must be surveillance footage of the incident," revealed Xiong Yan. The Zhaoyang District Procuratorate has severely dealt with five related responsible persons in this case: one deputy procurator of the procuratorate was suspended for inspection and placed under investigation by the Zhaoyang District Discipline Inspection Commission. Additionally, three anti-corruption bureau case handlers and one judicial police officer were placed under double regulation.

When Jilin attempted to enter the incident site, which Xiong Yan referred to as the case handling work area, Xiong Yan replied straightforwardly: "This case handling work area has now been urgently sealed off." Subsequently, Jilin observed on-site that the entrance to the work area was tightly locked, and its "true face" was not visible.
