Hu Jing Before-and-After Photos

Case No. 3: Hu Jing

Distorted Features Index: ****

Affected Area: Cheeks

In terms of facial features, Hu Jing's face is definitely one that represents good fortune. I like her straight nose, eyes, and lips; her beautiful features are complemented by a rounded chin, making her appear gentle and charming.

Intelligent, pretty, and talented in acting, I always felt that Hu Jing possessed many wonderful qualities that women admire.

Although her face was a bit round when she was younger, her substantial talent made me appreciate her round, youthful face. Her portrayal of Suzma La姑 in "Secret History of Empress Xiaozhuang," the passionate character in "Detective Fang Miao," and Ming Ji in "Tigers of the Yang Family" left countless stunning impressions and heart-wrenching roles.

By the time of "Tigers of the Yang Family," she had already become much slimmer, with her features standing out even more. She was truly a beauty!

During the "Chu Liuxiang" period,

However, one day while watching the new adaptation of "Chu Liuxiang" featuring Yu Chaochao, I suddenly found Hu Jing's face very strange. I couldn't quite put my finger on it, but I felt that she looked older all of a sudden. Later, in "Secret History of Emperor Kangxi" and "Women Without Regret"... I realized Hu Jing could no longer do without her thick bangs covering her forehead.

The reason being, she had undergone surgery to reduce her cheeks, removing the roundness and fortune-bringing look from her face. Suddenly, her entire face appeared sharp, harsh, aged, and thin.

Hu Jing now cannot live without her bangs, akin to a chronic and addictive poison. Her high cheekbones lack support, causing her right cheek to appear sunken with loose skin, adding years to her appearance.

Tragedy.

Li Xiaolu Before-and-After Photos

Case No. 5: Li Xiaolu

Distorted Features Index: ****

Affected Area: Nose Bridge

Li Xiaolu, who starred in "Mysterious Swordsman," "Eight Great Heroes," "Chi Zi Cheng Long," and "Young Zhang Sanfeng," has always portrayed herself as a lively and spirited little sister next door. Winning the youngest Golden Horse Award for Best Actress through "The Bath," gaining recognition via "My Naughty Angel," and leaving a deep impression on audiences with her role as Yang Xiaoyun in the recent drama "Struggle," one could say that Li Xiaolu's acting career has been smooth sailing.

However, I knew there was an issue that had long troubled her: her slightly flat nose bridge.

If she were just an ordinary person, Li Xiaolu would already be considered a great beauty. Unfortunately, TV directors often have a peculiar habit of filming close-ups from the side, which put pressure on Li Xiaolu due to her less prominent nose bridge.

Thus, she underwent nose augmentation. It was done using liquid injections, possibly hyaluronic acid, which always made me feel like injecting water into pork.

Li Xiaolu's early nose was flatter, similar to Avatar's.

The key tragedy lies in the fact that solid implants can shift positions, while liquid ones tend to spread. As a result, Li Xiaolu's nose bridge didn't appear much higher from the side, but from the front, it looked wider, somewhat resembling Avatar's...

Li Xiaolu began to look unnatural after undergoing eye corner surgeries, nose augmentation, and chin padding. Then her face stiffened...

Yang Danchen's Plastic Surgery

Listing Yang Danchen as Case No. 1 is not because her distortion is particularly severe, but rather due to the surprise factor. Many people said she had undergone plastic surgery, something I initially didn't believe, because I thought her naturally beautiful face didn't resemble any artificial enhancements. Yang Danchen's nose had some issues.

This is Yang Danchen when her face was still slightly round.

Looking at your past photos, your nose wasn't collapsed either. Why pad your nose? The implant seems to have shifted poorly. I feel sorry for you. You might as well redo your nose.

Yang Mi
