The recent popularity of "Naked Marriage Era" has sparked a hot topic: Would you be willing to have a naked marriage? Are you prepared for a naked marriage? Regarding the topic of naked marriage, people hold different views. How do you approach it? For women, have you really thought through the concept of naked marriage? Is a bare marriage comfortable?

Naked marriage refers to couples who do not buy a house, do not buy a car, do not hold a wedding ceremony, and may not even have a wedding ring, but directly register their marriage. This term emerged as a new online word in 2008. However, I believe it needs further clarification. Nowadays, many couples choosing naked marriage are not necessarily against buying a house or a car; rather, they cannot afford them with their current financial capabilities. Even the cost of a wedding can overwhelm them. This is likely the true meaning of the naked marriage group.

At first glance, naked marriage seems great!

With today's high housing prices, young people stepping into society and immediately purchasing their own house and car is quite challenging without relying on their parents. Therefore, refusing to be part of the "parasitic youth" generation and building one's life independently is an admirable life view. Moreover, when there are no ulterior motives in the relationship between two individuals, that love becomes relatively pure. In this complex society, finding someone to create such simplicity with you makes the relationship crystal clear and allows you to see yourself with a sense of courage and greatness. Naked marriage showcases your unconventional and unbound personality in a practical way—pretty cool!

Is naked marriage good? Hard to say!

Marriage involves two mature individuals of opposite sexes becoming husband and wife, forming a family. Besides physiological needs like sexual desires, having children, supporting elders, etc., marriage also serves two important functions: economic functionality and emotional exchange. According to stress theory, if a person constantly faces things beyond their ability, they will experience anxiety. Anxiety leads to dissatisfaction with the current situation, higher expectations, and possibly more pressure. Over time, this forms a negative cycle, ultimately leading to high levels of anxiety. At this point, emotions might lose control, become uncontrollable, and even lead to interpersonal problems. For lovers, this could easily trigger conflicts.

Is naked marriage good? It has both advantages and disadvantages. Life is diverse after all. Some people think it’s good, while others don’t, which depends on individuals' problem-solving abilities and attitudes towards difficulties and setbacks. However, regardless of whether it’s seen as good or bad, many realistic issues brought by naked marriage objectively exist and cannot be entirely or forever overshadowed by love. Marriage isn't about material wealth but rather about mutual support, joint efforts, and the warmth of dependence. Without a calm mindset and relatively stable environment, it’s easy to destroy the belief in joint effort and the intimate, warm state of being together.

Naked marriage: men are happy, women worry

Regarding the topic of naked marriage, surveys show that men accept it much more than women. Preliminary statistics online indicate that 80% of men agree with naked marriage, whereas 70% of women consider it unreliable. The attitudes of parents differ based on gender. Surveys reveal that the concerns of male parents are far less than those of female parents.

This is understandable. Parents hope their children can live a stable and happy life. For male parents, a man's struggle is a necessary process. If during this process, there is a loving partner accompanying their beloved son, why refuse?

On the other hand, the parents of daughters might feel more bitter. Women face greater competition and have less time capital compared to men. Thus, the struggles for men and women indeed differ due to gender differences. When parents see their daughters entering happiness with a sense of sacrifice, how can they happily accept it?

From the perspective of young people, societal expectations for men and women differ in our culture. Men bear heavier responsibilities for the family. Although we are in an era of gender equality, even where feminism is somewhat prevalent, people often still resist gender equality within the family context. Buying a house and marrying a wife seems to remain a fixed mindset for many. Men wanting to marry risk becoming "mortgage slaves," and once burdened with this identity, they might carry this financial pressure for many years. Therefore, when a woman willingly enters marriage out of love without imposing such heavy pressure, why not joyfully accept it?

Innocent girls in love often overlook the influence of this pressure on men's decision-making, preferring to believe that the decision stems from deep love rather than avoiding something.

Facing naked marriage, women need more rationality and thought

Naked marriage, since it implies having nothing, indicates that the economic foundation is relatively weak. Therefore, selecting a partner for naked marriage requires stricter scrutiny of their character and personality. Naked marriage is not blind marriage!

A person's current success may not equate to true success, but their character and personality determine their future potential for a happy and successful life! Choosing a naked marriage is fine. It is far better than those who oppress their parents for their own happiness. However, marriage is ultimately realistic and cannot truly rely solely on love to survive. Therefore, this decision should not be made impulsively but requires careful consideration. Success in a career might be fleeting, but recognizing a person's character and understanding their personality is the foundation for achieving a happy life. For friends considering naked marriage without economic factors as references, at this point, you should carefully review the basic qualities and overall condition of your partner. This way, your choice will have more certainty and make your "naked" marriage more valuable!
