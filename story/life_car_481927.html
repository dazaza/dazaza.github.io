Car Beauty Knowledge: Car Maintenance Q&A

Car beauty knowledge related to car maintenance reveals that just like humans, cars need care and attention. They require regular upkeep as well. To achieve both the beautification and maintenance of a car, one must understand relevant maintenance knowledge, as well as the commonly seen car beauty products and car maintenance products on the market. Only with this understanding can comparisons be made and the quality and authenticity of products be determined. Some car accessories may seem very cheap, such as car wax, which costs around 5 yuan per box. We all know about cold cream; twenty years ago, young girls used it for 2 yuan a bag, now people use 200-yuan-a-bottle creams. However, these seemingly inexpensive products offer no real benefits for car maintenance or beautification and may even damage the vehicle. Below are answers to several common issues car owners encounter during car maintenance or washing.

A. What to do if ice forms while washing your car?

Answer: Use warm water. In cold weather, water often freezes as soon as it touches the car, so the biggest taboo when washing your car is allowing water droplets to freeze on the surface, which could lead to paint layer cracking. Therefore, it's best to wash your car with warm water rather than cold water. If possible, wash indoors. Let the vehicle sit in the work area for 5 to 10 minutes after entering, allowing the engine to cool down before cleaning. Sudden temperature changes are harmful to the paint surface.

B. What’s the difference between polymers and silicones?

Answer: Car waxes contain different types of polymers. Polymers usually enhance the durability of the product and/or seal the car paint to protect it from environmental pollution that could corrode the paint. Silicon components are used to increase the brightness of the paint finish.

C. How to wash your car during snowy winter days?

Answer: If it snows continuously for several days, Mr. Li might delay washing his car, only to find later that the car paint has lost its previous luster. It’s important to clean the car promptly. Even if the weather forecast predicts more snow in the next few days, don't delay washing your car. As soon as the car is covered with snow, rinse it off with clear water immediately. Snow contains corrosive components that can erode the paint, undercarriage, tires, and rims if left on the car for too long. This is why the car may lose its shine.

D. Are the dashboard and car doors made of genuine leather?

Answer: No (very expensive cars might have genuine leather interiors). They are leather-like products. Thus, they only need surface polishing and do not require deep-nourishing liquids.

E. What's the difference between genuine leather and artificial leather?

Answer: Artificial leather lacks pores and is merely shiny on the surface. Genuine leather resembles human skin; its internal structure is much like a dense bundle of silk threads, connected by protein chains. Once these protein chains shrink or dry out, genuine leather begins to crack.
