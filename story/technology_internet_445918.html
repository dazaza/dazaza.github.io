Tocotech MU Fingerprint Device

by anonymous on 2011-06-14 21:37:56

Mu Technology Co., Ltd. in Chengdu produces fingerprint attendance machines, ID verification systems, patrol systems, and other related products. For more fingerprint products, please visit our website.

The universal fingerprint attendance machine uses the Otsuki 2005 single-engine recognition algorithm, capable of storing 1500-3000 fingerprints and 30,000 records. It does not require a real-time computer connection and is specifically designed for enterprises using long-distance offline fingerprint attendance. It is suitable for various occasions requiring accurate identification. The fingerprint attendance machine communicates with the computer via RS232 or RS485, and can support more than one network; it is ideal for businesses with fewer than 100 employees.

Features:

- Equipped with a high-speed embedded CPU from Intel USA, processing speed is five times faster at 800-3000 rounds/sec (depending on the quality of the fingerprints).

- Utilizes the Otsuki 2005 single-engine recognition algorithm, which is faster while enhancing the processing of difficult fingerprints and mixing ratios of 1:1 and 1:N. It also supports 360-degree rotation, making usage easier.

- Fingerprint matching regardless of angle, allowing for arbitrary 360-degree rotation.

- Includes automatic fault detection and automatic turning, sleep function, ensuring reliable operation of the X638-type fingerprint attendance machine over the long term.

- Displays the name of the person attending, ensuring accurate and reliable time and attendance data.

- Uses special data compression technology, making raw data transfer rate 3-5 times faster than previous versions.

- The number of stored fingerprints (expandable up to 3000) and the number of records (expandable up to 120,000) can be easily selected according to customer demand.

- Offers an open development agreement with a wide selection of hardware, featuring multi-level classification of up to 64 kinds of products to meet multi-level customer needs.

- Encourages ODM/OEM cooperation from system requirements analysis, mold making, circuit design, production and assembly, to packaging design, providing a full range of services.

Technical Parameters:

- Fingerprint capacity: 1500/2200/2800

- Recording capacity: 50000/80000/120000

- Communication: RS232, RS485

- Attendance rate: <= 2 seconds

- Error rate: <= 0.0001%

- Rejection rate: <= 1%

- Temperature: 0°C - 45°C

- Operating Humidity: 20% - 80%

- Collector: Mu fingerprint device

- Language: Simplified Chinese, Traditional Chinese, English
