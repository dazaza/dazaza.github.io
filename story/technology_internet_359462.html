Introduction to Coin-Operated Computers

by toubidiannao on 2010-06-29 14:41:53

The coin-operated internet computer is a new product developed byTaizhou Golden Apple Information Technology Co., Ltd. in response to market feedback. It aims to solve the problems of difficult internet access in public places and downloading network resources, achieving massive sharing of online resources over a wider range. The biggest feature of this product is to provide consumers with maximum convenience, speed, reliability, and extensive resource availability. One can imagine that during a long journey, consumers may need to send and receive emails, follow the news, gather information, play online games, update songs for their MP3 player, update video materials for their MP4 video player, or add software, data, and information to personal computers or laptops, etc. In general, stations, airports, docks, shopping malls, hotels, schools, hospitals, parks, subways, inns etc are suitable locations for placing coin-operated internet computers. Some special areas, such as closed schools, government service windows, and office buildings where white-collar workers gather, are also very good placement sites.

II. Operation Instructions

After turning on the machine, insert one or more coins and start using the computer. Each 1 yuan coin provides 20 minutes of internet access, and 2 yuan coins provide 40 minutes of internet access. You can insert several coins at once, and the time will accumulate automatically. This machine is equipped with a USB port (on the side) and a multi-function card reader (on the front), which can upload and download various types of data.

Always close unused browser windows promptly. After finishing internet browsing, close the Internet Explorer browser. Please do not modify system settings, do not use virus-infected data, and do not visit unhealthy, unsafe, or virus-infested websites.

III. Benefits of the Machine

1.　 Small footprint, especially the coin-operated machine requires no human management, just collecting money, and the cash box can store thousands of coins without any problem.

2.　 No fixed storefront required, it can be moved around at any time, saving time, effort, and expenses.

3.　 Guaranteed after-sales service, automatic system.

4.　 Most importantly, small investment, no risk.

a)　 Can be placed in other people's stores or supermarkets to share profits with the shop owner.

b)　 Can be rented out to others for business purposes.

c)　 Can be sold to others for profit.

d)　 Can be kept for personal use in your own physical store.
