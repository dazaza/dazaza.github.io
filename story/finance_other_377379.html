September 16th is China's annual Brain Health Day. Nutricia (China) Co., Ltd. has recently launched a new brain nutrition upgraded formula milk powder, and appealed to the public through relevant experts to pay attention to brain health starting from babies.

It is reported that Nutricia's newly upgraded formula milk powder has been on the shelves in China recently. The new product has comprehensively upgraded the brain nutrition formula, specifically targeting children over 1 year old, and scientifically adding at least 4 times more DHA during their critical period of brain development.

In recent years, brain health issues have increasingly attracted people's attention and importance, and as early as 2000, China designated September each year as "Brain Health Month." However, society's focus on brain health has always concentrated on the middle-aged and elderly groups, while infants' brain health and its foundational role in lifelong brain health has been neglected. In fact, during infancy, the synapses between brain neurons rapidly increase and accumulate, so it can also be called the "golden period" of brain development. If the brain lacks sufficient nutrition at this time, supplementing later may be half as effective. "Therefore, adequate and appropriate nutrition supply during the critical period of infant brain development partly determines their future brain health," said Associate Professor Craig Jensen of Baylor College of Medicine, Texas, USA.

Associate Professor Craig Jensen further explained that the human brain is like an "Internet," where the number, size, and connections and communication between brain cells are the key to brain development. As one of the most important nutrients for brain development, DHA is an important component of phospholipids in brain cell membranes, but often infants do not consume enough levels in their daily diet. Therefore, by supplementing with formula milk powder scientifically added with sufficient DHA, it will greatly improve the growth and transmission efficiency of synapses in brain neurons, maintain efficient contact between brain cells, and thereby promote the growth and development of infants in terms of vision, memory, etc., allowing babies to learn faster and better.

"As the recognized 'brain nutrition expert,' Nutricia has been continuously striving to provide babies with the best nutritional support at different developmental stages based on the constantly deepening scientific research results, providing them with the best start for their brilliant lives," said Ms. Wang Yuehuan, Vice President of the Marketing Department of Nutricia (China) Co., Ltd. "The launch of the fully upgraded formula milk powder and the A+ series boxed milk powder once again proves Nutricia's leading position in the professional field of infant nutrition."

"The fully upgraded products are another world-class product provided by Nutricia to celebrate its 15th anniversary of entering China," said Mr. Matthew Chapple, Vice President of Greater China Area and General Manager of Nutricia (China) Co., Ltd. in his speech. "China is one of the most important markets globally for Nutricia. Providing innovative and high-quality products to Chinese babies is the best expression of Nutricia's care for China, and also our core strategy. Nutricia will continue to pay attention to infant brain nutrition and healthy development, dedicated to providing care and concern from world-class nutrition experts to Chinese babies and families." (People's Daily Online Beijing report on September 11th)
