Approaching 30, what should we post-80s do?

by yangtai3986 on 2012-02-08 10:54:10

"Crunch, crunch," after dinner, walking alone on the snowy ground is so comforting and pleasant. At this moment, everything in time, everything in life seems so distant!

We 80s generation, nearing 30 years old, should no longer be the children who immersed themselves in the fictional world of online games to avoid reality! In the face of various tests in life, we need to learn to live with a smile - although this is not an easy thing.

Regarding love and desire, we should have a clear and accurate awareness. Don't be too indifferent or indulgent! Wake up: give yourself a position. Don't let your thoughts get stuck too deeply in the world of desires, "tranquility clarifies will, peace leads far". Life should be simple, clean, fresh, and warm! Don't let it get tangled with desire, that would make life a heavy experience!

As for the meaning of life, don't be too ambiguous. Life is just a simple process, coming from nothingness and going back to nothingness. However, we must learn to take good care of it, don't let this simple process become heavy and complicated! More importantly, we hope our lives are comfortable and pleasant!

Alright, after thinking clearly about these issues above, let's seriously consider what we should do at our age:

If you work in a big city, don't let the sky-high housing prices scare you - even though they have exceeded the承受能力 (bearing capacity) of both your parents and yourself. It is not a healthy entity, learn to shift your focus away from it! It's a thoroughly rotten piece of meat, full of all kinds of crawling creatures, despite their dazzling appearances. But because it has disrupted personal lives and corrupted the entire society's economic life, making originally simple house prices complex. If you have to buy one due to marriage issues but can't afford it, take a break first! Focus your attention on your work, even if you're not entirely satisfied with it!

If you're muddling through in a unit, cough, unfortunate 80s generation, this era does not fully belong to you yet. We should have realized long ago that unit life is relatively not an ideal place to stay, in more cases, it's not suitable for personal growth! We still need to improve it, rather than submit to it. Looking across the country, we almost can't find a place where we can develop according to our own wishes - formalism, bureaucratic habits, various unwritten rules... countless complexities have already damaged the system and human nature! 80s generation, if we are proactive and progressive young people, don't lose ourselves in front of these phenomena, don't think life should be like this. Between people, we need to establish a relationship of unity, mutual trust, and mutual assistance, rather than competing for interests. We must have our unique pursuits and beliefs - a simple and clear pursuit of life belief, even if this belief may temporarily cause setbacks in life due to lack of understanding! Please believe, the meaning of life does not lie in greed or debauchery!

What is your understanding of belief? What is your belief? Hey, us 80s generation, don't let ourselves become "practical" in the face of all sorts of harsh realities. The belief rooted deep in our hearts will help us correctly identify our life goals during the vast journey of life! Think about those unimaginable ideas you had as a child, which ones can be achieved through effort? Our responsibility is not to endure silently in life - enduring everything life imposes on us; not to rot in the world of dreams; not to waste time in the river of wasted years. We must have our unique pursuits!"Don't be idle, don't be tired," this pursuit will make our lives not too monotonous, and more importantly, it won't let the flower of life wither all the time!

Don't be completely a person in life, that way, life will be heavy! Learn to "look up at the stars", see which ideas in your inner thought world are not too difficult for you. Pursue them freely without delaying your work! In life, some things are unavoidable and require patience. At the same time, we must have our own things, only then can life be colorful and not dry!

80s generation, we should have long left behind the difficult period when we first graduated from school! Care about your loved ones, maybe they cannot meet all your needs in life, but they have paid too much for us. It's our turn to do something for them - even though we ourselves are caught in a very difficult vicious circle of life!

In short, we should have long seen the spots in the details of life, let's try to find our own way of living! The responsibility of the 80s generation is not to submit to the past, not to get lost in difficult times, but to actively and bravely explore and open up a new world of our own!

The night view is beautiful, accompanied by gentle breeze, the continuous "crunch, crunch" sound of stepping on snow! I think, in most cases, we need to learn to dig out those simple "beautiful" factors in life!
