WDQK is willing to let go, willing to laugh, and willing to throw away.

by anonymous on 2012-02-27 16:40:43

Who would know just how strong I am until one day we have no choice but to be strong. Everyone knows that even if life is monotonous and boring every day, it's still something we have to go through. Everyone has moments of pain they can't express, helplessly unable to find someone to confide in. In "The King's Avatar", denying oneself as just an individual, whose promises are truly fulfilled? Reality forces us to trade rumors for vows. Who wasn't happy in the past? It's just that the sweetness of the past was too profound. Who always claims they've let go, yet shed tears because of a word, a song, or a familiar scene? Who has many friends, yet when their heart is about to give in to its own pressure, they can't find anyone to confide in? Who feels they've given a lot, only to realize in the end they've never truly opened up to anyone? Who thinks time is the most terrifying thing, and then is grateful that time helps them forget what shouldn't be remembered? Dear who, find yourself, tell me who you are. Then, please remember: I am who I am, the one no one should ever give up on. Please don't make strength a habit, it will tire both body and mind. Please smile after being hurt, letting yourself mature. Even if tears well up in your eyes, keep smiling. Please seriously live each day as you should, willing to let go, willing to laugh, willing to discard. When you have nothing to do, try to find reasons or excuses to make yourself happier. Please don't fully believe in vows, or believe in half of them. Please treat yourself as a friend, a friend who understands everything about you. If you can't find someone to open up to, tell yourself to take good care of yourself. No one is lonely for a lifetime. Beloved self, there's always the right person waiting in the heart to give me the right place.
