Office online----ThinkFree

by cisir on 2006-04-25 13:05:20

Office online is still out of reach for those who are eager for mobile collaborative work, but after 11 a.m. on the 24th, this group has another option,That's Thinkfree!

After filling out a simple application form, we can immediately log in, and we can see the three ICONS in the Webtop "Create a new Document", "Create a new Spreadsheet", "Create a new" "Presentation", these three are corresponding to the creation of different files used, in addition to uploading, sharing, public, delete and other options used in collaborative work.

Thinkfree itself provides a 1G size of the network hard drive space for you to store files, try "Create a new Document", there will be two editing modes for you to choose, choose "Power" Edit ", after a Java program running warning box, after running about 1 minute you can enter a very similar to word editing interface, the use of Java Applet technology, in addition to a bit of resources, there is no problem.

I tried to input Chinese in the editing interface, and found that it could be supported (later I found that the Chinese file could not be edited again, and the Chinese characters would all become question marks, so it is best to make it Public after saving). When saving, I was prompted to wait 30 seconds to connect to the server, and then ok(Quick Edit mode does not have this prompt, but it does have the word Saving in the lower right corner). In general, if the domestic speed can be accelerated a little, to solve the problem that Chinese files can not be edited after saving, this website is still very good!
