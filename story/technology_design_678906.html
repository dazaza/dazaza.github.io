Dear Friends who are Suffering from Anorectal Diseases,

Hello! First of all, we sincerely thank you for your continuous attention and support to Nanchang Dongda Anorectal Hospital, the first professional anorectal hospital in Jiangxi, over the years. We express our heartfelt gratitude to each of you! You trust us and come to our hospital, entrusting your health to us. We fully understand the weight of our responsibilities, so we have always been doing our utmost. The greatest joy for medical practitioners is to see the smile on the face of every patient when they leave the hospital healthy. Because your health and your smile are what we strive for. Gold cups and silver cups are not as valuable as your reputation, and we have both the confidence and the ability to do better!

Doing better is not just a slogan; it's a direction. The American PPH technology is something you can witness firsthand!

We know that you may be very afraid of the pain associated with traditional anorectal surgery, so we have introduced various advanced devices and multiple minimally invasive, pain-free technologies to quickly alleviate your suffering. Advanced anorectal examination equipment such as the American ZZ electronic anal scope and the Japanese Olympus electronic colonoscope ensures the rapid and painless diagnosis of anorectal diseases like hemorrhoids and fistulas. Additionally, the internationally renowned American PPH technology and the "golden combination" of minimally invasive, pain-free techniques have been widely praised by patients. It is worth mentioning that Dongda exclusively owns the Beijing "An Shi Therapy for Anorectal Diseases," which is highly recommended by the Ministry of Health and represents the highest level of combined Chinese and Western medicine treatment for anorectal diseases in China. This technique is minimally invasive, pain-free, allows for immediate treatment and departure, and offers affordable pricing, providing more scientific and humanized diagnostic and therapeutic options.

We know that when you seek medical care, you would prefer doctors with profound expertise to feel more reassured. Therefore, we have built a team of authoritative experts led by Dean Wen Wangen, who has nearly 40 years of clinical experience in anorectal diseases, including directors Li Qicai, Wang Naimin, Zuo Liming, Deng Xianghong, Zeng Guibao, Zhong Junyuan, and others. Moreover, we regularly invite the "An Shi Therapy for Anorectal Diseases" expert group from Beijing's Sino-Japanese Friendship Hospital to provide consultations at Dongda, allowing you to enjoy advanced diagnostic and therapeutic technologies from Beijing right at your doorstep.

We know that due to the special nature of anorectal disease locations, you might feel embarrassed about seeking treatment at the hospital, especially female patients who are more influenced by traditional concepts. Therefore, we established the "Women's Anorectal Disease Treatment Center" first in the province, implementing a gender-segregated consultation system where men consult male experts and women consult female experts, fundamentally eliminating concerns for male and female hemorrhoid patients.

We know that you might delay treatment due to financial difficulties, leading to worsening conditions. In July 2011, Mr. Yu Rongguang, the image ambassador of Nanchang Dongda Anorectal Hospital, established the "Charity Surgery Fund" at the hospital to provide medical assistance to impoverished, complex cases, disabled individuals, and those who have made outstanding contributions to society. This charitable act has received high praise from the people of Jiangxi. To date, thousands of impoverished patients have benefited from the assistance of the "Charity Surgery Fund."

We know that deep down, you hope to spend less money while getting cured, and it would be great if medical expenses could be reimbursed. Therefore, Nanchang Dongda Anorectal Hospital became the first designated unit for employee, resident, and new rural cooperative medical insurance in Jiangxi Province, with up to 90% reimbursement available! Additionally, for the convenience of patients, online appointment registration is available. Patients with an appointment number can visit the hospital without paying the expert registration fee, receive priority treatment, and choose between male or female experts for their consultation. The online registration address is: http://www.0791gc.com/zixun/.

We know that upon admission, you might wonder whether you should give a red envelope (bribe) before surgery. We are committed to building a "No Red Envelope" hospital, strictly prohibiting any healthcare staff from accepting or soliciting red envelopes, thus avoiding adding to your financial burden or psychological concerns. Your continued "insistence" will make our doctors and nurses "lose their jobs"!

Health is the pursuit of every one of us, and it is also what we most want for every friend who comes to Dongda. Currently, more than 60,000 anorectal disease patients have recovered at Nanchang Dongda Anorectal Hospital, with 80% of patients able to treat and go immediately, not affecting normal life and work, and services available even during holidays. We adhere to the principles of high-tech medical care and high-standard services, placing patients at the center, strengthening medical quality, optimizing medical services, creating a harmonious medical environment, with top-tier talents, top-tier equipment, top-tier technology, top-tier management, and top-tier service, ensuring that you truly feel the professional care from Dongda.

All medical staff of Jiangxi's first professional anorectal hospital (Nanchang Dongda Anorectal Hospital)
