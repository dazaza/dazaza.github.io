by xiaochouyutaozi on 2009-10-27 12:57:48

Xiong Jiaqing, female, known as Sister Xiong, Violent Bear, is a student at the second branch school of Nanhubei. On October 23rd, a video titled "Shanghai Xuhui girls fighting, it's infuriating just to look at it" went viral online. In the video, a girl stands in the middle of an alley, and another girl rushes from behind and kicks her lower back with force. When the first girl falls to the ground, the other continues to stomp on her abdomen... This was later revealed by netizens to be Xiong Jiaqing. The incident sparked heated discussions on well-known Shanghai forums, with many expressing anger towards the girl who did the beating.

The Incident Involving Xiong Jiaqing's Assault

A video of a Shanghai girl assaulting a classmate lasted more than 5 minutes (Online Video [5 minutes 20 seconds]: http://www.wwe88.com/xjp/2135).

On the evening of October 23rd, a netizen on a well-known Shanghai forum Broadband Mountain (KDS) forwarded a video about a fight involving Shanghai girls that was over 5 minutes and 20 seconds long. The video showed a girl wearing a purple T-shirt, looking like a middle school student, punching and kicking another girl in an alley. Bystanders in the video occasionally spoke in the Shanghai dialect and referred to the attacker as "Sister Xiong."

In the video, "Sister Xiong" continuously slapped the other girl's face, pulled her hair, and even charged from behind to assault her... During this time, some troublemakers handed cigarettes to "Sister Xiong," saying "just make a show." The victim did not retaliate throughout the entire incident, allowing "Sister Xiong" to beat and scold her.

Violent Girl Sparks Public Outrage; Netizens Initiate Human Flesh Search

This video caused extensive discussion among netizens, with many criticizing "Sister Xiong" for being too violent. Comments included "too lacking in family education," "how can kids today be like this," "why didn't they report it to the police"... Many netizens expressed sympathy for the beaten girl while condemning "Sister Xiong's" brutal actions. One netizen analyzed: "Young, impulsive, likes to show off, imitates gangsters, and thinks it's prestigious to make others fear you. These thoughts are easily amplified in group situations, and when incited and cheered on by others, irrational behavior occurs." He believed that the beaten girl could sue "Sister Xiong" for intentional injury.

Reason for Xiong Jiaqing's Attack: The assaulted girl had taken away Xiong Jiaqing's boyfriend, prompting Xiong Jiaqing to seek revenge.
