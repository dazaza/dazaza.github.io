Common sense you need to know when buying a branded computer, it is recommended that those who want to buy a computer take a look.

by cqe on 2009-08-03 17:34:43

In the computer market, the question of whether to buy a "branded machine" or a "custom-built machine" has been debated for a long time. For users purchasing a "custom-built machine," they should first determine which components they want to use, and then go to the market to make their selections. As for whether they will encounter counterfeit goods, it completely depends on the buyer's "discerning eye." There are quite a number of relevant introductions in many professional newspapers and magazines; so how do we select pre-designed branded machines?

Tailor-made Pricing

Frequently, friends who want to buy a computer ask me: "I want to buy a computer, about how much will it cost?" When encountering such situations, I usually counter-question the other party: "How much are you prepared to spend?" Indeed, the price difference for computers nowadays is very large, with two or three thousand yuan being enough to assemble one, and over ten thousand yuan also sufficient to assemble one. The same applies in the branded machine market. There are popular models costing less than 5000 yuan, as well as luxurious models costing over 10,000 yuan. Therefore, when selecting, one should first determine a price range, leaving around 500 yuan of floating space above and below.

Shop Around

After determining the price range, you can visit the computer market once to understand what brands and models are available within your set price range, selecting a certain number of models as candidates. Salespeople of branded machines generally have advertising brochures printed with great precision, which you can take some for reference. Don't worry about getting disapproving looks from merchants when taking these materials, as the current competition in the computer market has become extremely fierce, and taking their materials is exactly what they desire.

Picking One Among Many

This step is the most critical and complex part of the entire selection process. Facing a dazzling array of candidate machines and colorful advertising brochures, I suggest making comparisons from the following aspects.

1. Compare the performance of main components

Generally speaking, manufacturers list the performance of main components in their promotional materials: such as the CPU frequency, memory size, hard disk capacity, display screen size, etc. These component performances can be compared, and under the same price, naturally choose the one with higher performance.

2. Focus on understanding the specific situation of components not listed

There will be some components' models and performances not listed or vaguely described in the promotional materials, commonly including: motherboard, sound card, graphics card, etc. For these components, consumers should pay particular attention to understanding their exact models, because these components might sometimes be the "weak points" of branded machines, such as some models using high-frequency CPUs but paired with integrated sound cards and graphics cards on the motherboard. Of course, it’s not that branded machines cannot use integrated sound cards and graphics cards, but at least consumers should have the right to know, so they can make comparisons, make choices, and reach final decisions.

If all other components are similar, but a certain model has an especially outstanding component, even if there is a slight price difference, it should still be given serious consideration. For example, if both models have the same configurations except that one uses a TNT2 M64-32MB video memory graphics card, while the other uses a GeForce2 MX400-32MB video memory graphics card with a price difference of 200 yuan, it is recommended to choose the latter. Because replacing a component in a branded machine can be very troublesome, and after changing the hardware configuration, there may be some issues regarding after-sales service guarantees. It would be better to consider thoroughly during purchase.

3. Pay attention to the mouse and keyboard

Although the price of mice and keyboards is a very small proportion of the total computer price, they cannot be ignored, as they are parts that directly contact the human body and are among the most frequently used components. If improperly selected, they can directly affect your usage comfort, and in severe cases, even impact your health. Therefore, during selection, you must try out the mouse and keyboard of the sample machine.

4. Compare overall appearance

One important reason for buying a branded machine is that most branded machines have a beautiful or distinctive appearance. Therefore, when selecting, consumers should compare the appearances of various brands and models, combining personal preferences and the overall atmosphere of the usage environment (of course, ensuring internal quality is the primary condition). Especially for home users, if the shape and color of your computer can blend seamlessly with your home environment, it will definitely enhance your living space.

5. Look at the pre-installed software

Branded machines generally come pre-installed with some software, in addition to the operating system, there will be word processing, email, antivirus, entertainment games, computer education, etc. Therefore, under similar hardware conditions, consumers can compare the software, seeing which model’s installed software suits them more. At the same time, you should also understand whether these softwares are only pre-installed in the machine, or whether they provide installation disks and recovery disks besides pre-installation, as the latter's cost is higher than the former, and under the same price, the latter is more economical and convenient for consumers.

6. Compare special functions of various branded machines

All major branded machine manufacturers will endow their products with some unique functions to increase selling points. Consumers can compare these functions, seeing whether they indeed bring convenience during use or merely add costs. Of course, due to different personal usage habits and starting points, the final choice may vary.

7. Compare after-sales service conditions

The biggest advantage of branded machines lies in their excellent after-sales service. Therefore, comparing the after-sales services of various manufacturers is also very important when selecting a branded machine. For instance, some manufacturers offer free replacements for products within the warranty period, while others offer free repairs; some offer free on-site repairs within the warranty period, and beyond the warranty period, only charge for component costs, while others also charge for on-site service fees.

On-site Trial Use

After a series of comparisons, believe that consumers must have their favored models. Don’t rush to place orders and pay money, you should also personally trial-use it on-site, because for computers, "seeing is deceiving, using is believing." No matter how good the promotional materials describe, it's not as effective as personally operating it. Only after personally operating and testing can you make the final decision.

Preparation for Future Needs

After payment, please ask the merchant to provide an unopened machine for unpacking inspection. Generally speaking, branded machines come with packing lists, listing the items inside the box, and consumers should check each item against the list. If any item is missing, immediately request the merchant to replace the set. At the same time, consumers should also obtain all necessary receipts, most importantly the invoice and warranty card. Besides these, various branded machines may also include other items, such as service gold cards, service manuals, etc.

Finally, I would like to remind everyone that branded machine manufacturers often carry out promotional activities. During these promotional activities, it is often possible to purchase some computer peripherals, such as printers, scanners, etc., at relatively discounted prices along with the branded machine, sometimes even as free gifts. Therefore, when purchasing, you should clearly understand your rights and not give them up!

Hope that through this article, everyone will have a certain understanding of how to select branded machines, wishing everyone can pick the most suitable "favorite machine" for themselves.
