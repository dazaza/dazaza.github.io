What is car beauty?

So-called car beauty refers to the intuitive perception of aesthetics, practicality, and comfort of a car's appearance and interior.

The authoritative definition of car beauty (Auto Beauty) refers to the use of different types of car beauty care products and construction techniques based on the maintenance requirements of different materials in various parts of the car, providing comprehensive maintenance and care for the car. The concept of "car beauty" first appeared in our country in 1994. Nowadays, this concept has been widely accepted by the public, and car beauty centers have spread all over the country.

Simply put, it's about exterior maintenance of the car.

"Car beauty" mainly includes five aspects: car surface beauty (car washing, removing oily stains, car beauty glazing, new car dewaxing, old car dewaxing, chrome part renewal, and tire renewal), car decoration beauty (interior beauty care, engine beauty care, and trunk cleaning), paint beauty (paint fading treatment, paint scratch treatment, and spray painting), car protection (applying anti-explosion sun film, installing alarms, installing voice alarm systems, and installing static dischargers), and car accessories (car perfumes, room purification, decorative stickers, and various pads).

"Car beauty" originated from Western developed countries, with English names expressed as "Car Beauty", "Car Care", or "CarDitill". Due to the development of the automotive industry, the popularity of social consumption trends, and the influence of people's curiosity and pursuit of novelty, the pace of new car model updates in these countries is very fast. Followers want new cars without wanting their old cars to depreciate, leading to the emergence of the car beauty and decoration industry between the automobile consumption and second-hand car markets. In other words, car beauty is the inevitable product of the rapid development of industrial economy, the progress of consumer concepts, and the increasing penetration of car culture.

With the continuous progress of society and the improvement of human civilization, cars are entering the lives of ordinary people as mass consumer goods. Therefore, the style, performance, and cleanliness of cars all reflect the personality, cultivation, lifestyle, and preferences of the car owner. As a result, many people want their "ride" to look clean and beautiful, offering a comfortable experience. All the work carried out around this purpose is what many people generally refer to as "car beauty".

Today's car beauty, inspired by the basic idea of human "beauty and skin care", has been given new connotations of bionics and is gradually forming modern car beauty. The new concept of car beauty is not just simple car waxing, stain removal, odor removal, dust suction, and cleaning services inside and outside the car for regular beauty care. It also includes the use of professional beauty series products and high-tech equipment, adopting special processes and methods, such as enhancing paint luster, waxing, polishing, coating, handling light and deep scratches, full car paint beauty, underbody anti-corrosion coating self-care, and engine surface renewal, among a series of car maintenance technologies, achieving the effect of "making old cars look new, preserving the value of new cars, extending life, and increasing benefits".
