Yunnan Ruibao Biotechnology Co., Ltd. Profile

by rf67w0cfhj on 2012-02-27 16:45:55

Yunnan Ruibao Biotechnology Co., Ltd. (hereinafter referred to as Yunnan Ruibao Company) is a newly established high-tech specialized enterprise (state-owned enterprise) in 1996, under the Yunnan Industrial Investment Holding Group Co., Ltd. (state-owned enterprise). The company's registered capital is 80 million yuan, mainly producing natural pigments (food additives), and it is located in the Kunming National Economic and Technological Development Zone.

Yunnan Ruibao Company specializes in using natural plants as raw materials to extract natural pigments, pharmaceutical raw materials, and functional food additives and other plant chemical products. Among them, the natural pigment products include: radish red pigment, red cabbage pigment, perilla pigment, red rice pigment, lac pigment, beet pigment, gardenia yellow pigment, safflower yellow pigment, purple sweet potato pigment. Other plant chemical products include: Tara gum, tannic acid, gallic acid, Panax Notoginseng extract, molting hormone, etc. 70% of the products are exported to Europe, America, Japan, South Korea and other countries, while the rest are sold domestically. Yunnan Ruibao Company has first-class domestic equipment and product testing instruments that keep pace with international standards, advanced processes, standardized and scientific management. With rapid development in recent years, the company now has the ability to produce hundreds of tons of natural pigments annually, becoming one of the main natural pigment enterprises in China. Currently, it is one of the largest production and sales enterprises for radish red pigment, red cabbage pigment, and red rice pigment in China.
