by yinding999 on 2009-12-18 12:45:18

Italian Cup - Sneijder's free kick seals victory, Inter Milan hits two posts, wins 1-0 to advance to the quarterfinals.

Sina Sports report -- At 3:00 AM Beijing time (9:00 PM local time in Italy on December 16), the first match of the Italian Cup Round of 16 began, with Inter Milan defeating Livorno 1-0 at home. In the first half, Stanisovć’s long-range shot and Sneijder's free kick both hit the post. In the second half, Sneijder scored from a free kick, and substitute Milito missed a one-on-one chance. In the quarterfinals, Inter will face the winner between Juventus and Napoli.

This was the first cup encounter between the two teams. In their previous 19 Serie A matches, Inter had a record of 14 wins, 3 draws, and 2 losses. Livorno eliminated Sampdoria in the last round. Mourinho fielded a semi-reserve lineup, with "Coppa Italia goalkeeper" Toldo making his first start of the season, Chivu playing as center-back, young Donati starting at right-back, which was also his debut in an official senior game, while Montari played as left-back. Vieira got a chance to play midfield, Suazo partnered Balotelli after scoring twice in the last meeting between the two teams in Milan. Livorno's central defender Rivas faced his former club.

The temperature at San Siro Stadium was close to 0 degrees Celsius. Only three minutes into the game, Motta combined with Stanisovć in the middle of the field, then Stanisovć passed to Motta who was running forward, but the defender deflected it with his heel. Stanisovć struck a powerful right-footed shot from 19 meters out that hit the right post! On the fifth minute, Suazo broke into the penalty area on the right side and fell after contact with Cnerjevic, but no penalty was awarded. On the 18th minute, Candreva's low shot from outside the box was blocked out for a corner. Then Stanisovć combined with Sneijder before breaking into the penalty area on the right side and crossing, but Suazo could not get enough power on his shot from eight meters out under the pressure of defenders, and Benussi collected the ball.

Suazo collided knees with his former teammate Rivas and fell, but he was fine. On the 22nd minute, Montari fouled Daniilevicius, and C. Lucarelli took the free kick from 22 meters out that hit the wall, then his follow-up shot went wide of the left post. On the 25th minute, Daniilevicius attempted a super long-distance shot that Toldo easily gathered. On the 28th minute, Stanisovć made a through pass after breaking through the center, and Balotelli's chip shot from just inside the penalty area on the left was caught by Benussi.

Inter hit the post again on the 33rd minute! Balotelli was brought down by Perticione in the center of the field, and Sneijder took the free kick from 25 meters out that Benussi touched with his fingertips, hitting the right post. On the 36th minute, Sneijder played a through ball, and Stanisovć swept a right-footed shot from 13 meters out that went wide of the left post. On the 42nd minute, Mozart fouled Sneijder, and Balotelli's free kick from 27 meters out curved wide of the right post. In stoppage time of the first half, Stanisovć was cautioned for a tactical foul on Mozart.

In the second half, Dionisi replaced Cnerjevic, immediately picking up a yellow card for a foul on Suazo. On the 49th minute, Marchini cut into the penalty area from the right flank and struck an outside-of-the-foot shot from 11 meters out that Vieira blocked; he claimed handball, but the referee ignored it. On the 52nd minute, Suazo combined with Stanisović, who then sent a diagonal pass, and Suazo's shot in the center of the penalty area was blocked by Perticione for a corner. On the 59th minute, Sneijder's shot from 25 meters out went high.

Inter broke the deadlock on the 60th minute when Rivas was penalized for handball while defending Balotelli closely. Sneijder took the free kick from 22 meters out on the left, curling a right-footed shot over the wall into the near corner, 1-0. Then Sneijder played a one-two before sending a through ball, and Suazo dribbled into the penalty area on the right, but Benussi came out to clear the danger. Dionisi replaced Daniilevicius. On the 67th minute, Inter launched a long ball from the backfield, and Balotelli's acrobatic hook shot from 12 meters out was gathered by Benussi. Then Sneijder delivered a free kick from the left, and Balotelli's header went over the bar.

Marchini was booked for a foul on Sneijder. Cosmi substituted Pulzetti for Bellavald. On the 72nd minute, Sneijder played a through ball, and Suazo's powerful shot from the edge of the penalty area on the right was blocked by Benussi for a corner. Inter's ensuing corner was cleared, and Motta's follow-up shot from distance went over. On the 75th minute, Dionisi's low shot was gathered by Toldo diving to his left. Milito replaced Suazo. On the 81st minute, after combining with Sneijder, Balotelli's low shot was gathered by Benussi. Maicon replaced Donati. Inter missed a golden opportunity on the 84th minute when Balotelli passed to Milito, who broke into the penalty area alone on the left side and shot for the upper corner, but Benussi came out quickly and made a correct judgment to block it for a corner. Inter ultimately won 1-0.

Inter Milan (4-3-1-2): 1-Toldo / 29-Donati (83'Maicon), 6-Lucio, 26-Chivu, 11-Montari / 14-Vieira, 5-Stanisovć, 8-Thiago Motta / 10-Sneijder / 45-Balotelli, 18-Suazo (76'Milito).

Livorno (3-5-2): 11-Benussi / 2-Perticione, 4-Rivas, 13-Cnerjevic (46'Dionisi) / 5-Marchini, 8-Bellavald (72'Pulzetti), 24-Mozart, 83-Candreva, 46-Pieri / 9-Daniilevicius (63'Dionisi), 99-C. Lucarelli.
