Five, Red Clay Sentiment - Lu Quanzhi Red Clay Art Masterpiece Exhibition

Master of arts and crafts Lu Quanzhi has been engaged in the arts for more than thirty years, achieving abundant results. His paintings, ceramics, and art papers have been published in dozens of domestic and international newspapers and magazines. Many of his red clay art masterpieces have been collected by official institutions and individuals in Beijing Olympic Organizing Committee, the National Museum of China, the National Art Museum of China, as well as in the UK, the US, Japan, and many other countries and regions.

Lu Quanzhi's red clay art works are innovative in their themes and subjects, and his techniques of "large" and "empty" are unparalleled. His large-scale red clay art work "Hundred Blessings" won the title of the largest ceramic art piece in the world by Shanghai Guinness World Records. With solid creative achievements, Mr. Lu Quanzhi has established himself as the "First Person of Guangxi Red Clay Art". Meanwhile, Guangxi's red clay art has entered a brand-new artistic journey marked by Mr. Lu Quanzhi's series of works.

In order to promote the long-standing Chinese pottery art culture, create a distinctive Guangxi pottery art cultural brand, and further promote the development of Nanning's red clay art, during the folk song festival, there will be an exhibition of Mr. Lu Quanzhi's red clay art masterpieces at the Nanning People's Hall to appreciate the master's style and witness the charm of red clay. This exhibition is hosted by the Autonomous Region Department of Culture and the Nanning Municipal Government, and organized by the Nanning Culture Bureau and the Guangxi Quanzhi Red Clay Art Culture Exchange Center.
