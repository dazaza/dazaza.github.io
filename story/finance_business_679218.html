It is certainly important to wear men's clothing.

by baiyasin2s on 2012-02-27 20:38:31

However, accessories should not be overlooked. Below are some basic etiquettes of men's accessories: 1. Belts The importance of belts to men is irreplaceable by other clothing accessories. Any elegant man will deliberately decorate this detail at the waist. 1. When wearing a well-tailored suit, the color of the belt should match the leather shoes. The belt should not carry too many items, and its length should be kept between the first and second trouser loops. The width should be around 3 centimeters. Too narrow will lose the masculine vigor; too wide is only suitable for casual, jeans style outfits. 2. Calm, composed, and subtle men can choose simple, pure, and tough belts. A noble calf leather belt with gold or silver shiny or matte metal buckle is undoubtedly the best choice. The classic combination of black leather belt and silver-white buckle can show the concise and clear nature of men. 3. Men with romantic temperament can choose belts made of crocodile skin, lizard skin, ostrich skin, or snake skin, but remember that the color should not be too gaudy, and do not easily use belts with novel styles and huge buckles. 4. Free-spirited, unrestrained, and energetic men can choose coarse cowboy belts or leather woven belts. Flax and leather, two different materials woven together seamlessly, do not seek luxury, only seek bravery. Wearing a leisure outfit with a canvas belt around the waist makes people feel the beauty full of youthful vitality in ruggedness and generosity. 2. Leather bags Generally speaking, men's leather bags can be divided into briefcases and clutches. White-collar class men can use square cowhide briefcases. This bag is reasonably designed, with all kinds of objects set up, even the most trivial things can find their place. Going out on business or meeting clients with such a high-quality appropriate briefcase will bring men a sense of confidence and reliability. In addition to briefcases, men also need a convenient clutch to store various cards, checks, cash, and other important items for easy carrying and use. Men's clutches mostly use cowhide, brown or black cowhide clutches make men appear noble and elegant without being luxurious and ostentatious. 3. Watches Wearing a watch usually means strong time consciousness and strict style. In formal social occasions, men's watches often indicate their identity, while those who don't wear watches and use mobile phones to check time or ask others about time frequently will always make people feel low-class and lack of time consciousness. And many women also believe that men naturally like mechanics, so watches of course prefer thick and sturdy mechanical watches. The crisp ticking sound, exquisite watch type, and craftsmanship reveal the charm of male maturity and stability everywhere. The thickness of the watch is actually the embodiment of men's rigidity, and this attraction is irresistible. 4. Lighters Different lighters can reflect different personality tastes. If you are an inveterate smoker and also want to emphasize your cultural connotation in the pursuit of life quality, then stop using those cheap disposable lighters, especially in formal social occasions, using disposable lighters is a loss of identity, and you should choose suitable lighters according to your status. 5. Glasses Glasses can appropriately highlight one's temperament. For example, a pair of high-quality and well-crafted glasses can well display your elegant and noble temperament as a white-collar aristocrat; a pair of stylish glasses is enough to give people the impression that you are knowledgeable. Therefore, it's better not to use those glasses that have been used for too long, unsuitable for your face shape, and of poor quality. 6. Perfume Men can also use some perfumes, such as cologne, etc. Clean and decent clothes with a faint fragrance can show the elegance of men. Of course, perfume is not necessarily required, which should be combined with the nature of work and the occasion attended. 7. Tie clips Tie clips should be used when wearing suits, meaning that there is no need to use tie clips when only wearing long-sleeved shirts, and definitely not when wearing jackets. When wearing a suit and using a tie clip, it should be pinned in a specific position, namely, between the fourth and fifth buttons of the shirt from top to bottom. After pinning the tie clip, button the jacket, and generally, the tie clip should not be visible from the outside.
