Passing by, the end, not lovers, not friends, ultimately strangers

by xucyengylr on 2012-02-08 10:31:18

People pass each other by, and the outcome is neither lover nor friend, but ultimately strangers. In the appreciative gazes of each other, we read only words. They say that writing reflects the person, and I firmly believe this to be true. This poem was submitted to Anhui People's Radio Station, revised by the famous poet Liang Xiaobin, and then broadcast with music by announcer Fang Kai, causing a strong reaction in the small town and greatly stimulating my enthusiasm for amateur creation. These are not difficult to achieve at all. And you?

In life, we often find ourselves in a corner, unable to fully display our talents or let others appreciate our best moments...

After finishing business in Alar, I prepared to return home. Sometimes, even hearing related songs makes me feel that happiness is nearby. Listening to you makes me feel great and keeps me standing tall, my blood surging... Aren't these the sources of our happiness?

Today, my life can be described as abundant, and I can confidently say that I have achieved fame and success. Of course, language, structure, momentum (rhythm), connotation, artistic conception, thought content, emotion techniques, etc., are equally important, and none of these aspects should be neglected.

I want to drink some water. At first, every morning, we had to attend classes, start the fire, and also make breakfast; everyone was just too busy...

There is no so-called perfect thing in life, and thus there is no such thing as a flawless life either. We must tell ourselves: we cannot fall into decadence; we must strive.

After listening, I didn’t pay much attention. In love, never believe the next one will be better. Once you step out of this misunderstanding, for the sake of real life, if you persist a little more, tolerate a little more, understand a little more, I believe the first love will still be there at the end.

You have financial difficulties? I'll support you. If you want to succeed, it depends on how your relationship with your leader is and what impression you leave on them.

In 1983, after the division of property between brothers, I gave my new house to my younger brother and still lived together with my grandmother and parents...

The moonlight is like gauze, the quiet moon like a sickle. That soul wandering on the edge of night and day wants nothing more than to settle her world-shaking romance before her life falls away. She wants nothing more than to personally drape the white wedding dress over the daughter who grew up under her protection... Unfortunately, this hard-to-fulfill wish is like the sometimes visible morning star, close in heart, yet far in distance.

Life is a textbook. Many times, our surroundings do not meet our wishes. In difficult situations, we need to learn to appreciate ourselves, believe in ourselves, affirm ourselves, encourage ourselves, and we will find that life will rejuvenate with hope, making us a renewed self, a self daring to challenge, a self full of vitality, at every moment.

Gradually, I learned calmness, learned to watch you leave like this without sadness because the next stop is still sadness. This world has created the word 'regret'. As for me, I don’t prefer this way of living. Life is originally so beautiful, the sky so clear. What needs to change is not the environment around us, but our own mindset.

The mutual appreciation of hearts does not require constant watching. From his writings, I know he is someone with broad social experience and rich life experiences, an able person, also someone proud and ambitious. That cherry blossom tree, its swaying time, even if far away in a distant land, beyond mountains and waters, still shines brilliantly in the heart.

In recent years, Dashanyan has expanded many temples and planted quite a few trees. There are more tourists now, with various accents heard, chirping like birds.

This time, along our journey, the severely affected areas we saw are hidden among lush trees and green bamboo. The reconstruction of the new countryside in Pingwu is completely renewed. The towns and villages we see are all new buildings. There is natural gas on the stoves, sewer pipes in the toilets, tap water to drink, children can go online, fitness equipment right outside the door, parks and squares in the marketplaces...
