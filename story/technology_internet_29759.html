The public beta test of Wangshang Magazine has officially started. Enjoy ERM services for free.

by haihun on 2007-09-13 17:01:09

Recently, a reporter conducted an interview regarding the public beta test of the enterprise rich media product "Wangshang Kan" by Shenzhen Vanston Technology Co., Ltd. Mr. Qiu Qiping, the company's Chief Operating Officer, accepted the interview.

According to Mr. Qiu Qiping, this public beta test will last for half a month. During the testing period, users can not only enjoy free upgrades for e-magazines but also receive a one-year ERM service as a gift. Additionally, during this period, customizing Wangshang Kan will come with an 80% discount (the detailed information about the public beta test will be available on their official website and corporate blog).

During the conversation, the reporter learned that even before the start of the public beta test, two government institutions had shown strong interest in cooperating with Wangshang Kan after learning about it through other channels. They signed cooperation agreements during the development process of Wangshang Kan. This situation prompted the reporter to want to learn more about the background of Wangshang Kan. Subsequently, Mr. Qiu introduced the background of Wangshang Kan to the reporter.

The emergence of Wangshang Kan is due to market demand. As we all know, enterprises are most concerned with how to promote their products. Every enterprise is trying its best to package its external image and products. Similarly, every enterprise hopes to achieve maximum benefits with minimal investment. In promoting around these needs, enterprises choose various methods for promotion. In the process of self-promotion, advertising is the most effective form of publicity within a short time.

In the past, advertisements were mostly confined to the four traditional media. These four traditional media have been carrying out their own promotions with their unique advantages. Even in today's Internet information age, they still play a powerful role in publicity. However, from the moment they were born, the four media were destined to have inherent defects. Their coverage range has restricted their development, especially in the Internet era, where this defect becomes increasingly obvious. Wangshang Kan was born under such circumstances.

Wangshang Kan, as a brand new enterprise marketing method, integrates the advantages of the four traditional media, being intuitive and full of sound and color. Most importantly, it uses the Internet, which is fast and widely covered, as a promotional channel. It combines multimedia applications to effectively promote and publicize enterprises. At the same time, Wangshang Kan utilizes a powerful ERM management system, allowing enterprises to understand the effectiveness of their publicity at any time and facilitate interactive information between enterprises and customers. Its appearance plays a substantial role in establishing the image of enterprises and saving on publicity costs. Finally, the reporter tried reading Wangshang Kan and, after practical inspection, felt its novelty, practicality, and the effectiveness it could bring to enterprise promotion.
