Zhengzhou Mining Machinery Group Co., Ltd. is a large-scale mining machinery and equipment manufacturer integrating R&D, production, and sales. It owns fixed assets of 300 million yuan. Within the company, there is a feeding and grinding machinery equipment research institute and 10 departments. The company has more than 1900 employees, including 11 employees with senior technical titles and 38 employees with intermediate technical titles. The company has two production plants, eight workshops including cast steel, cast iron, machining, riveting and welding, precision assembly, etc., covering a total area of 112,000 square meters. It also has 302 units of production equipment, 12 units of lifting equipment, with a maximum lifting capacity of 50 tons, and an annual industrial output value of 300 million yuan.

The main products produced by Zhengzhou Mining Machinery Group Co., Ltd. are as follows:

(1) Feeding machinery: 1. Plate feeder 2. Disc feeder 3. Trough feeder 4. Pendulum feeder

(2) Conveying machinery: 1. Belt conveyor 2. Screw conveyor 3. Underground quantitative conveyor

(3) Crushing machinery: 1. Jaw crusher 2. Hammer crusher 3. Cone crusher 4. Vertical shaft crusher 5. Double roll crusher 6. Impact crusher

(4) Ball mill, rod mill (new energy-saving type)

(5) Spiral classifier

(6) Magnetic separator, flotation machine

(7) Filter press: 1. Internal filtration vacuum filter 2. External filtration vacuum filter 3. Permanent magnet vacuum filter 4. Belt filter

(8) Water treatment equipment: 1. Thickener 2. Mixer 3. Sludge scraper, etc., nearly 60 varieties, more than 500 specifications. Products are sold all over the country, and some products are exported overseas.
