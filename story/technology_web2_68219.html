Q: What is Tu Square?

A: Tu Square is an online visual interactive platform that integrates various services such as picture competition, heat analysis, and homepage promotion, with the core concept of the Olympic Games.

Q: Who made Tu Square?

A: More than ten students from Jiangnan University. They named their team "Weishi" and even came up with an English name "Vport." They said V stands for victory, and port is a "harbor," this "harbor of victory" also plans to develop an English version, go beyond China, and reach the world.

They were very "disobedient." Some people said that for this kind of competition, they could just modify existing site source codes, but they insisted on creating their own original picture competition program. They also said that if time wasn't limited, they would have developed the program for the image forum too. They truly are a group of self-inflicted individuals.

You can't deny that these college students are indeed simple-minded. Have you ever seen such a simple university observation station?

Q: What is the relationship between Tu Square and the Olympics?

A: Tu Square promotes the three major concepts of "green, technology, and humanities" like the Olympics, where you can deeply reflect through visuals.

Tu Square is a competitive arena like the Olympics, where you can place pictures you admire to participate in competitions.

Tu Square is a bridge for communication and sharing like the Olympics, where "friendship first," and you can meet many like-minded friends here.

Tu Square is a stage for showcasing individuality like the Olympics, where you can use your pictures and comments to display your individuality.

Tu Square is a platform for gaining popularity like the Olympics, where the personal homepage address is linked to the pictures, bringing higher popularity to your website.

Tu Square has participants, judges, and audiences like the Olympics, but in Tu Square, you can play all three roles at the same time.

Tu Square has a group of news observers like the Olympics, who will provide in-depth analysis and reports on the latest competition status, and you are also one of them.

Tu Square can help you cultivate a group of fans like the Olympics, as long as your pictures are powerful enough.

Compared to the Olympics, Tu Square's advantages:

You can be present at the scene without buying tickets because everything is free.

If you're itchy, you can participate anytime without going through multiple selections.

Q: What benefits can Tu Square bring me?

A: You can support the Olympics, upload pictures, vote, promote yourself, and gain deep insights through Tu Square...

1. Popularity: The traffic of personal websites (blogs, podcasts, personal homepages, albums) UP! UP! UP! Pictures and websites are synchronized and bound. Comprehensive promotion on the homepage, rankings, image forums, picture display pages... Good pictures bring good popularity!

2. Democracy: Internet democracy, come and vote! Deep Olympics, public selection by all citizens!

3. Deep thinking: PK? It's more than just PK. Picture heat reflects public psychology. Everyone is an observer, writing in-depth phase analysis reports on pictures, reporting on social depth issues from multiple angles and comprehensively!

4. New network experience: Picture competition, heat analysis, homepage promotion, interactive friendship all-in-one! Focusing on user experience, more functions are under development...
