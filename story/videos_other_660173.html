Leping Procuratorate: Skillfully Combining Strengths and Resources

by chen18888302 on 2012-02-07 14:05:29

Looking at Leping's urban and rural areas, there is an energetic scene everywhere: in the production workshops of companies such as Chenhang Light Source and Tianxin Pharmaceutical, machines are running at full speed; in Zhongyuan Modern Agriculture Company's Baila Green Vegetable Base, truckloads of green pollution-free vegetables are being transported to large and medium-sized cities nationwide... This is the result of the city leveraging its resource advantages, adhering to scientific development, and cleverly writing the "low-carbon rise" article.

The concept of a green economy has now taken root in Leping; green environmental protection has gradually become a common pursuit for farmers and enterprises alike. On the basis of developing a "green economy," Leping City has also successfully written articles on agriculture and industry, green and golden "low-carbon rise," achieving seamless integration and mutual enhancement. The city is committed to building technologically advanced ecological industrial parks, implementing a "green stamp" policy for new enterprises and a regular "health check" system for enterprise production. A series of ecological "beautification" measures have improved the "output per mu" of the industrial park. The city promotes the circular economic development model of "resources-products-waste-regenerated resources," encouraging enterprises to actively upgrade their technology and convert their production, focusing on enhancing the technological content of industries and increasing product added value. As a result, the city has formed more than ten types of green circular economy chains, including ceramic machinery, building materials, biopharmaceuticals, agricultural product processing, and eco-tourism, generating over 10 billion yuan in value.

Using waste heat for power generation is another highlight of Leping's "low-carbon economy." Jinxi Cement Co., Ltd.'s waste heat power generation project is a key national supported and prioritized ecological environmental protection project, using pure low-temperature waste heat power generation of 9MW, which is a key project for the city’s energy conservation, emission reduction, and comprehensive utilization. In recent years, the city has closed down more than 360 small coal mines, and what used to be mountainous piles of coal gangue polluting farmland is now being fully utilized by former individual mine owners as internal combustion auxiliary materials for building material production, effectively conserving coal energy. At the same time, red bricks produced by burning coal are gradually being replaced by cement bricks, with the city newly developing 78 cement brick and other new building material enterprises. White charcoal and coal cakes for heating are being replaced by hot water bags and biogas heating, with 40% of urban residents and 20% of farmers using solar water heaters.

In addition, the city takes reducing pollutants as a handle to improve the ecological environment and develop the "low-carbon economy," implementing a "high threshold" access system for polluting enterprises. An environmental monitoring center has been established in the industrial park, implementing a total pollutant reduction plan and tracking monitoring, and a sewage treatment plant with a daily capacity of 20,000 tons has been constructed. Chemical oxygen demand, sulfur dioxide, and carbon dioxide emissions have decreased by 9%, 7.3%, and 8.8% respectively compared to 2005, reducing carbon dioxide emissions by more than 8 million tons.

"We grow all green pollution-free vegetables." Recently, in the 20,000-mu vegetable base of Zhongyuan Modern Agriculture Co., Ltd., Huang Xiaolin, a farmer from Baila Village in Zhenqiao Town, was leveling his ridges in his greenhouse while telling us: "How to plant, how to exterminate pests, how to manage, are all uniformly guided by technical personnel, not only saving effort but also yielding high economic benefits. My greenhouse can generate an annual income of two or three ten thousand yuan without any problem!"

In the process of new rural construction, Leping City accelerates the development of "low-carbon agriculture" through the implementation of the biogas project, allowing more farmers to bid farewell to the traditional way of life filled with smoke and fire. The large-scale biogas pool of 1,000 cubic meters at Jinfu Ecological Farm in Le Gang Town can provide living gas for 400 households. There are 16 gas stations like Jinfu Ecological Farm in Leping City that supply gas to entire villages using pig and cattle dung as raw materials. Thirty-five large-scale farm biogas projects have been built, benefiting over 8,000 households across 298 villages in 19 towns.

Biogas not only changes the traditional production and lifestyle of farmers but also extends the industrial chain, realizing the transformation of agricultural growth methods and promoting ecological agricultural construction. The city has promoted more than ten integrated utilization models such as "pig-biogas-fruit," "pig-biogas-vegetable," and "pig-biogas-fish." Biogas generates an annual increase in income and savings of 28 million yuan for the city's farmers, driving the construction of over 120,000 mu of green standardized vegetable bases and 78,000 mu of green fruit bases. Currently, the city has certified 48 agricultural products and 30 bases through the "three quality" certification system.
