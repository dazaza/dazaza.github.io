Van Gogh's sunflowers and cafes, oil paintings with bright colors and full of artistic atmosphere... Don't you think they should all come from the hands of famous artists? In fact, it's not the case. Nowadays, even if you don't know color coordination or how to adjust brightness and darkness, you can still paint a "world-famous painting." Following cross-stitching, another new DIY product called digital oil painting has appeared in Yiwu, becoming the new favorite of fashionistas.

Everyone can be an artist.

In front of several booths at the International Business City, there were a few easels and some oil paintings on display. Four or five young girls were gathered together discussing which type of oil painting to buy. The reporter saw that the products were not colored; the oil cloth was only filled with numbers. Originally, dealers decomposed these paintings into color blocks, dividing them into small sections like puzzles. As long as you fill in the colors, you can complete a masterpiece.

"This is really interesting. It turns out that being a painter isn't such a difficult thing. After completing a painting, I feel a great sense of accomplishment, and compared to cross-stitching, this is more suitable for people of all ages," said Miss Wu, who is 27 years old and already a regular customer at the store. This time, she brought her girlfriends to select oil paintings.

According to the shop owner, Mr. Liu Jianping, the entire painting process requires extreme focus and care, but even those without any painting foundation can complete large-scale oil paintings. After finishing a work, it's impossible to tell that it was drawn using digital lines, so everyone can become an "oil painter."

It is understood that although both belong to DIY products, the operational demographic for digital oil paintings is much broader than that of cross-stitching. Plus, with the advantage of moderate prices, since its debut six months ago, the sales of digital oil paintings have been increasing day by day, gaining popularity among more and more fashionable individuals.
