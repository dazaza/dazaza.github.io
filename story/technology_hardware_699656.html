Cotton 2012 Spring/Summer Yarn Exhibition interprets the theme of "fashion, technology, and low carbon".

by uyahd9mjik on 2012-03-09 16:11:18

This year's yarn exhibition will be held concurrently with the China International Textile Fabrics & Accessories Fair and the China International Apparel & Accessories Expo. The three exhibitions will jointly showcase their strengths, presenting a dazzling array of attractions that will undoubtedly offer visitors an all-new experience in technology, fashion, and environmental protection.

Pakistan's Opulent Company, which plans to expand its market in China through blended yarns, will also participate in this year's exhibition. They will display various colored blended yarns, including acrylic and cotton blends, as well as modal and tencel blends. Most of the raw materials for these products come from countries like Pakistan, the United States, and Australia, ensuring high-quality goods. Over the years, Opulent has established offices in Malaysia and Canada, and in 2010, they opened an office in China to better develop their business in the country.

Bailong Oriental Co., Ltd., a leading company in China's color-spun industry, has participated in every yarn exhibition since its inception. Their booth size continues to expand each year. In recent years, Bailong has focused on the research and development of environmentally-friendly products. This year, their main exhibits will be innovative eco-friendly yarns, all of which are their latest R&D achievements and represent significant innovations in the entire industry. These eco-friendly yarns not only meet the demand for high-quality products from downstream enterprises but also enable resource recycling.

This year's exhibition will comprehensively present the theme of "Fashion + Technology + Low Carbon," showcasing the latest trends and developments in the domestic and international yarn industries with a brand-new concept and posture. To promote product innovation within the industry, enhance corporate competitiveness, and increase the influence of Chinese fiber brands, the organizers have specially invited the Nylon Professional Committee of the China Chemical Fiber Association and the Textile Chemical Fiber Product Development Center to collaborate with nylon enterprises in creating a "Colorful Life, Fashionable Nylon" section and activities. The exhibition will focus on new fiber results independently developed by nylon and related enterprises, such as moisture-wicking, anti-static, antibacterial, far-infrared, negative ion, conductive, UV-resistant, flame-retardant, high-strength, composite ultra-fine, fine denier irregular, high elasticity, fully matte, whitening, and eco-friendly color-spun fibers and products.

After years of accumulation and growth, the China International Textile Yarn Exhibition has continuously enriched and perfected its exhibition functions. Based on gathering comprehensive industry information and authentic market trends, this year's exhibition will announce the popular trends of yarn products for 2012/2013, guiding enterprises in technological upgrades and product innovation. High-tech, fashionable yarns, fibers, knitted products, and knitted fabrics will be showcased to industry professionals, promoting the development of new technologies and products in downstream industries and enhancing the fashion level of downstream products. The exhibition will continue to provide a fair, interactive, and flexible trading platform for enterprises, where nearly a hundred renowned domestic and international yarn and fiber companies will not only display their latest products but also seek more strategic partners to jointly enrich the variety of yarns.

At this year's exhibition, visitors can also see innovations in traditional products, such as filament short fiber composite core-spun yarn series. Filament short fiber core-spun yarn is a type of functional filament core sheath composite yarn. The short fibers in the sheath layer can provide softness, fullness, skin-friendliness, and excellent moisture absorption and breathability, while the long filament core can offer strength, elasticity, shape retention, drape, stiffness, and other special functions.

Baoding Zhongfang Yimian Textile Co., Ltd. is one of the earliest companies in China to produce large-scale lyocell, lenzing modal, and bamboo fiber yarns. At this year's yarn exhibition, Zhongfang Yimian will continue to showcase differentiated fibers and multi-component blended yarn series, mainly including lyocell, bamboo fiber, hemp fiber, conductive fiber, water-soluble vinylon, fine denier acrylic, and other mixed yarns.

Participation brings opportunities, exchanges foster cooperation.

Dezhou Huayuan Ecological Technology Co., Ltd. is a well-known multi-fiber blended yarn manufacturer in the industry, specializing in the development of new fiber yarns. Their main products include new cellulose fibers, fine denier fibers, flame-retardant fiber yarns, cashmere blended yarns, silk blended yarns, hemp fiber blended yarns, differentiated functional fiber yarns, and organic cotton yarns. Dezhou Huayuan uses over 50 types of fibers and nearly a hundred specifications. At this year's yarn exhibition, Dezhou Huayuan will launch moisture-absorbing heat-generating yarns, moisture-absorbing quick-drying composite yarns, fine denier high-strength viscose yarns, and vortex-spun functional yarns. These new yarns have obtained patents domestically and have broad market prospects. Additionally, Dezhou Huayuan will also showcase textile yarn system solutions at the exhibition, designing different products for customers with personalized needs.

In the spring of 2012, the China International Textile Yarn (Spring/Summer) Expo made a grand appearance again. To meet the demands of numerous domestic and international textile enterprises and professional buyers, the Spring/Summer Yarn Exhibition will be held from March 29 to March 31, 2012, as one of the three major events of the "March Textile Spring Market Action." It will continue to take place at Pavilion 1 and 2 of the Beijing China International Trade Center, with an exhibition area of 5,500 square meters. This exhibition will feature the theme of "Fashion + Technology + Low Carbon," offering the industry a brand-new experience!

Wuxi Yimian has always been a participant in the yarn exhibition, consistently earning a great reputation for its top-notch management and product quality. They believe that participating in the exhibition allows them to better understand the market, gather extensive information, strengthen exchanges and cooperation with peers, and effectively guide the company's production and operation. This year's yarn exhibition will feature Wuxi Yimian's full-cotton compact-spun yarns, polyester-cotton CVC compact-spun yarns, viscose compact ring-spun yarns, and new fiber precision-spun yarns represented by Tencel and Modal. All these yarn varieties use the finest cottons, such as Xinjiang long-staple cotton, American PIMA cotton, and arterial cotton, ensuring the superior quality of the yarns.

It is understood that India's R M INTERNATIONAL Company, which has participated in two consecutive yarn exhibitions, will continue to join this year's exhibition. They will introduce traditional premium pure cotton yarn products at this year's exhibition. The company's manager, Rajesh Mittal, introduced, "The demand for pure cotton yarn in the Chinese market is very large, so the market prospect is extremely optimistic. Our sales performance in the Chinese market is also relatively ideal, so we attach great importance to this large market." It is reported that India's R M INTERNATIONAL Company is a textile trading company mainly engaged in yarns, fibers, fabrics, and garments. Their products are mainly sold to foreign countries such as China, Bangladesh, Turkey, and Italy, and they have established good cooperative relationships with some local textile enterprises.

In addition, the exhibition will simultaneously hold events such as announcements of innovative product achievements, publication and analysis of the 2012 fiber trend, where industry experts will deeply interpret the latest developments and applications of fiber products.

Through the joint efforts of various professional associations under the China National Textile and Apparel Council and textile fiber and yarn enterprises, the China International Textile Yarn Exhibition has played an active and important role in enhancing the overall level of China's textile industry chain and strengthening international and domestic textile cooperation and exchange.

Exhibitions are barometers of the industry, providing insights into phenomena, essence, and trends. Foreign enterprises will strongly enter the Chinese market at this year's exhibition, with textile yarn enterprises from cotton-producing giants such as India, Pakistan, and Turkey participating in groups. At that time, many overseas renowned enterprises will gather in Beijing. Apart from hoping to find agents in China through participation, they also hope to attract more professional buyers from around the world through one of the few professional yarn and fiber exhibitions in the world. Here, yarn producers and sellers from various countries can exchange and communicate with professionals in the yarn and fiber kingdom, finding more new products, new technologies, and new friends through the exhibition.

At this year's yarn exhibition, visitors will be able to closely experience functional fibers such as coffee charcoal, cool jade, and Qilishun series yarns. Coffee charcoal has warming properties, cool jade offers cooling effects, and Qilishun features environmental protection, strong anti-pilling, soft hand feel, smooth texture, and good breathability. Attendees can experience ATPDP fiber-based energy-saving and environmental protection differentiated fibers. ATPDP fibers are the latest eco-friendly products that can be dyed and printed under normal temperature and pressure conditions, featuring excellent normal temperature and pressure dyeing characteristics and high uptake rates. They can be interwoven with any natural fiber to achieve cotton-like, silk-like, and wool-like effects. The products have bright colors, high absorption rates, and good lightfastness. The successful launch of ATPDP fibers creates conditions for further energy conservation, emission reduction, and environmental protection in textiles. During the printing and dyeing and subsequent finishing processes, ATPDP can significantly reduce water usage, with preliminary estimates showing savings of 25%-30% in electricity and dyeing chemicals.

Foreign enterprises see potential in the Chinese market and make a strong entry.

The exhibits at this year's yarn exhibition will cover widely-concerned functional fibers, differentiated fibers, high-performance fibers, special fibers, and new chemical fibers, such as low-melting-point fibers, solution-dyed ultrafine polyester fibers, slub yarns, special cloud-patterned yarns, filament short fiber core-spun yarns, compact-spun yarns, Tencel, Modal, water-soluble yarns, no-twist yarns, hollow yarns, etc. These products fully reflect the current yarn R&D trends and public consumption demands.

Fashion leads trends; technology brings enjoyment.
