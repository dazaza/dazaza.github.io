How to deal with frizzy curls

by king2f4j on 2012-03-03 00:23:56

1. Regular hair care, using hair conditioner, to improve the frizzy condition of hair.

2. Avoid perming small curls.

3. The curl size should not be too large, natural bends are enough.

4. Apply a shine-enhancing weak fixing product on wet or semi-dry hair, allowing the hair to naturally curl into the desired effect. If it doesn't work, consider using a curling iron for styling.

Hair Care:

Hair care should start from daily life. To keep hair healthy, internal care is fundamental. Early to bed and early to rise, regular meals, good living habits, maintaining a pleasant mood can reduce hair loss and make hair healthy and strong. Good eating habits can ensure sufficient nutrition for your hair. The secret of a beauty diet is to provide adequate protein, as protein is the main component of hair. Foods like eggs, meat, beans, and milk are rich in protein. Iodine can increase the luster of hair, so eating more seaweed and purple algae can enhance the brightness of hair, making it look healthy. Except for the hair follicles, other parts of the hair have no life. Once the grown hair is damaged, it will be difficult to restore. Therefore, external hair care is very important:

1. Wash your hair in time and keep it clean.

2. Use hair care products regularly.

3. Use oil treatment products to repair hair damaged by perming, dyeing, or bleaching.

Correct Hair Washing Steps:

Step 1: Comb - Combing hair before washing can promote blood circulation in the scalp, reduce broken hair during washing, which is a good way to maintain hair. Use a wide-tooth comb to comb from the ends of the hair upward section by section, repeat several times until the hair is smooth.

Step 2: Wash - Wet your hair with water around 40 degrees Celsius, pour an appropriate amount of shampoo into your palm, rub to produce foam, and gently massage your hair.

Step 3: Rinse - Rinse your hair thoroughly twice with clear water, trying to leave no sticky residue.

Step 4: Dry - Use a dry large towel to absorb the water from your hair, do not rub forcefully to avoid damaging wet hair. Then use a wide-tooth comb to comb gently, preferably let the hair air dry. If you need to use a hairdryer, choose the medium setting and blow until the hair is about 80% dry.

How to Care for Hair in Summer:

1. Follow the nature of your hair - Don't frequently subject your hair to high-temperature treatments such as curling irons or straighteners, each operation reduces the moisture in your hair. It's better to get a haircut that lets your hair grow healthily.

2. Protect hair from the sun - Sunlight is the enemy of hair luster, it takes away moisture and makes hair dry and brittle. Therefore, choosing hair products with UV protection is essential, and another simple and effective method is wearing a wide-brimmed hat under the sun, protecting your hair while adding style.

3. Avoid salt and chlorine - Hair is like a sponge, absorbing liquids it comes into contact with. Whether swimming in the ocean or in a pool, wet your hair with fresh water beforehand to prevent salt or chlorine from eroding your hair. After swimming, rinse your hair thoroughly with fresh water immediately.

4. When in doubt, keep your hair clean and tidy - This is the simplest hair styling method. Dry your hair well and apply styling gel to the middle and ends of the hair (this avoids unsightly marks left by combs after using gel).

How to Care for Hair in Winter:

Winter is dry, and the hardest part to manage is that head of hair. After the summer heat and autumn's intense ultraviolet rays, the hair inevitably fades, turns yellow, splits, tangles, and at the least, static electricity increases. The ends of the hair are the most prone to damage, fragile and lacking nutrients, showing symptoms like yellowing and splitting, greatly affecting one's appearance. Therefore, regular maintenance is necessary. The simplest and most effective way is to consistently use end-repairing nutritional products to provide sufficient nourishment to the hair ends. Nowadays, no-rinse end-repairing products, in addition to traditional nourishing oils, also contain balanced vitamins, which can quickly penetrate deep into the roots and provide nutritional repair. There are also hair essences containing chitosan and water-soluble silicone oil, suitable for use on half-dry hair after washing or before sleeping, simply wear a sleep cap.

Color-locking for Dyed Hair:

Dyed hair is already quite damaged, and the soaking in summer pool water and autumn's strong UV rays exacerbate the fading and yellowing. Fortunately, today's hair care products have introduced various color-locking and repairing products. In new color-locking products, besides magical color factors that make hair shine, there are also components that block UV rays and deeply nourish the hair. Especially this season, pearl's precious ingredients are popularly added to post-dye care products, the natural mineral crystals they contain can add pearl-like luster to the hair, reducing the trouble of touch-ups.

Moisturizing and Smoothing Hair:

Our hair contains approximately 15% water, but if it drops below 10%, it will immediately show signs of dryness - static electricity, falling strands, split ends, especially in autumn. The simplest way to hydrate hair now is to use a moisturizing spray, lightly spraying it anytime, anywhere, to moisturize the hair and reshape the hairstyle. A new trend is adding protein components to the moisturizing spray, which can replenish the lost protein in the hair, particularly suitable for dry hair.

Well, follow the correct methods to take care of your beautiful shiny hair. Curly hair can be really troublesome, I had mine permed and then straightened back, frustrating. Use a suitable conditioner for your hair type, eat more nuts like melon seeds and peanuts, and go to the salon for regular care.
