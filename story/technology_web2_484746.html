The court's verdict is laughable. The defendant could not submit any evidence, only relying on his baseless claims.

It’s like the two mouths of "official" saying you're illegal, then you're illegal!

Facts and reasons:

I am the plaintiff Zhao Yunhai, the defendants are Dao County People's Government and Dao County Land and Resources Bureau.

In July 2001, the plaintiff signed a land use rights transfer agreement with Dao County Fugang Fish Farm. According to the agreement, Dao County Fugang Fish Farm would transfer its 250 square meters of land use rights for the plaintiff's use. Fugang Fish Farm filled in the plaintiff's name on the planning map drawn by Dao County Planning Bureau and submitted it after stamping it with the official seal. On January 12, 2007, Dao County Planning and Construction Bureau legally issued (0701120018) No. Construction Land Use Permit to the plaintiff. With relevant procedures, the plaintiff applied to Dao County Land and Resources Bureau for a land use certificate, but until now, Dao County Land and Resources Bureau has neither informed the reasons nor fulfilled relevant obligations according to law. In November 2010, the plaintiff learned that Dao County Land and Resources Bureau had confirmed the plaintiff's land to Xu Shanlin for use through Dao Jijian (2001) No. 0814164 Collective Land Construction Land Use Certificate. The plaintiff believes that Dao County Land and Resources Bureau's issuance of the above-mentioned land use certificate is procedurally illegal, factually erroneous, and lacks sufficient evidence, violating legal provisions, resulting in damage to the plaintiff's legitimate rights and interests, and should be revoked according to law Dao County Land and Resources Bureau issued Dao Jijian (2001) No. 0814164 Collective Land Construction Land Use Certificate.

The first instance and second instance courts believed that according to Article 63 of the "Land Management Law of the People's Republic of China": The right to use collectively-owned land shall not be transferred, transferred or leased for non-agricultural construction. Fugang Fish Farm is an agricultural collective economic organization under the jurisdiction of the Fugang Township People's Government of Dao County, and its land nature belongs to collective ownership. The agreement signed between Dao County Fugang Fish Farm and the plaintiff without authorization is invalid, and therefore not protected by law. For this reason, the lawsuit was dismissed.

The truth:

1. Look at the evidence documents submitted by Dao County Government - Dao County Revolutionary Committee Document (1978) No. 037

In 1978, Dao County Government organized the scattered individual fishermen into a unit, which was a collective unit, but its land source was government-granted land, clearly stated in the proof document. Base issue: After organizing the scattered individual fishermen, a production and living base must be established, so Fugang Commune allocated Dafugang (more than 200 mu) to the fish farm for building ponds and raising fish... ...

How can granted land be called collective land? It's simple to prove the nature of the land, just provide the original land certificate. Why doesn't the government provide it but instead provides a 1978 document? Is it easier to find a 1978 document than a land certificate, or dare they not provide it? The (1978) No. 037 document clearly states that it was granted land from Fugang Commune. Are they trying to deceive us as if we don't understand the difference between state-owned land, collective land, and granted land?

According to Article 2 of the "Land Management Law of the People's Republic of China", the state implements a paid use system for state-owned land according to law. However, the granting of state-owned land use rights within the scope prescribed by law is exempted. According to the "Provisional Measures for the Administration of Granted Land Use Rights", the user of the land approved by the land management department of the municipal or county people's government and who pays the land use right transfer fee may transfer, lease, or mortgage the land use right.

2. Look at the land transfer fee receipts and issued state-owned land use certificates of Dao County Government

Dao County Fugang Fish Farm did not sign a transfer agreement with me alone, but signed transfer agreements with many others. Please look at the names on the planning map, others who signed transfer agreements with Dao County Fugang Fish Farm in the same way were issued state-owned land use certificates and receipts by Dao County Land and Resources Bureau.

Evidence One: Internal Settlement Receipt of Administrative and Public Institutions in Hunan Province, No. (2002) 3339768, Paying Unit: Fugang Fish Farm, Date: December 8, 2002, Payment Item: Advance Payment for Land Transfer Fee etc., 30,000 yuan, stamped with the official seal of Dao County Land and Resources Bureau.

Evidence Two: Internal Settlement Receipt of Administrative and Public Institutions in Hunan Province, No. (2002) 0140677, Paying Unit: Fugang Fish Farm, Date: September 25, 2002, Payment Item: Advance Payment for Land Use Procedures, 60,000 yuan, stamped with the official seal of Dao County Land and Resources Bureau.

Evidence Three: Dao County Land and Resources Bureau issued Dao Guoyong (2007) No. 01031421 State-Owned Land Use Certificate on March 22, 2007. The holder of this certificate is my right neighbor, consistent with the list on the planning map. In addition, many others have also obtained state-owned land use certificates, I have evidence for this.

The above evidence fully proves that Dao County Fugang Fish Farm's land transfer was approved by the land management department of the county people's government and the land use right transfer fee was paid. Why is it legal for them to obtain state-owned land use certificates, while it becomes illegal collective land transfer when I try to obtain one?

3. Look at the falsification by some individuals in government departments

The temporary project department of Line 1863, such a temporary engineering department, has no authority to expropriate or transfer land. Such a department completes its mission once the project ends. If such a department really intervenes in administrative licensing matters, how much corruption and crimes would result? Strangely, the area of expropriation does not match the quantity of land transferred. According to the plan, there should be at least 60 households on both sides of the street in the Fugang section of Line 1863, each household being 4.5 meters wide. If these 60 households' land was really expropriated by the temporary project department of Line 1863, logically all 60 transferees (people buying land) should pay money and get land from Line 1863. But in reality, all the transferees who built houses on site signed transfer agreements with Dao County Fugang Fish Farm. Only Xu Shanlin signed a transfer agreement with Line 1863 and obtained a collective land use certificate. Was the temporary project department of Line 1863 established solely for Xu Shanlin?

4. Look at Xu Shanlin's laughable evidence

Xu Shanlin signed a land transfer contract with the temporary project department of Line 1863 on December 23, 2000, and made payment on December 26, 2000. However, the receipt for his planning permit was dated December 11, 2000, obtaining the planning permit 15 days before the transfer contract was even signed. This is truly amazing. The judge asked: How could the four boundaries of Xu Shanlin's collective land use certificate be empty plots? The government official replied: There was no planning at the time. Absurd! How could a planning permit be issued without planning? And how could land be transferred? And how could a collective land use certificate be issued? Looking at the date of issuance of the collective land use certificate, it is the same day as the receipt, proving that the government did not conduct a review, handing over the certificate upon receiving payment, making the procedure illegal.

5. Look at the government's confusion

Line 1863 highway occupied 12 mu of land from Fugang Fish Farm, but the farm leaders illegally sold another 140 mu of land. Liu Qicheng, Chen Baoping, Li Qingzhu were sentenced for embezzlement and illegal sale of land, exceeding the land power authorized by Dao County Government, which has nothing to do with me. The land I transferred is within these 12 mu, authorized by the government and with land transfer fees paid. Does Dao County Government go back on its word? Is it neglecting its duties to harm the country and its people?

6. Look at whether administrative litigation truly serves the people or is a new platform for local governments to deceive the masses

During the court trial, the judge asked the government to show expropriation permission documents, original vouchers for pond ownership, and expropriation payment vouchers, but Dao County Government could not provide them. Why not provide them? Is it contempt of court, or disregard for ordinary people like us; is it lack of evidence, or unwillingness to provide. When the judge asked why the land became collective land after expropriation and transfer, Dao County Government officials were speechless.

In administrative litigation, the burden of proof lies with the government. Dao County Government cannot provide any evidence to prove its innocence, nor any evidence to prove that my land transfer was illegal. Yet it keeps accusing me of illegality. The law is about evidence, not baseless talk.

However, despite such simple facts, Yongzhou Intermediate People's Court still protects Dao County Government, failing to review administrative facts but instead reviewing the plaintiff's civil behavior (and providing no evidence), deviating from the spirit of the Administrative Litigation Law. It's unclear whether local government is applying pressure or if it's simply universal corruption. If the court wants to rule me illegal, the government must provide Dao County Fugang Fish Farm's collective land certificate, original basis for pond ownership, expropriation permission documents, and expropriation payment receipts. Without these evidences, what basis is there to rule me illegally bought land? This is a wrongful judgment! It's breaking the law knowingly! It's deceiving the people! It's trampling on Chinese law!
