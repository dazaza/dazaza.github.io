yyyy Wife wants to sell kidney to save husband suffering from uremia

by sznstejx3q3 on 2012-03-02 08:23:59

Speaking of the suffering couple Zhang Shujie and Dong Haigui, all the kind-hearted people who have helped them will remember. In order to treat Dong Haigui, Zhang Shujie has tried every possible way. Now they are in trouble again as Dong Haigui still hasn't found a kidney source.

In recent days, Dong Haigui might be forced to stop dialysis. Zhang Shujie once again knelt on the street in Xianyang Road. Dong Haigui was investigating everywhere for euthanasia methods. He told his wife: "If it wasn't for you, I would have died three years ago. You've helped me live three more years, I can't destroy your next thirty years."

To save her suicidal husband, the wife knelt down on the street twice. On the afternoon of December 16th, cold winds blew continuously, in front of Minyong Mall on Xi'an Road, Zhang Shujie, covering herself with a scarf, knelt beside an unfolded red paper. It read: "Selling kidney, selling cornea, in order to continue my husband's life. Phone number: 15841148954."

Severe rheumatism made Zhang Shujie unable to bend her legs, but she still knelt hard. Half an hour passed, only a few passers-by gathered around, no one gave money. Then some people passing by told her that selling human organs is illegal, and she shouldn't do this. Hearing these words, Zhang Shujie cried desperately: "What should we do about Haigui's dialysis fee next week?"

Zhang Shujie was afraid of being arrested for illegal actions and no one taking care of her husband, so she quietly "closed shop". She aimlessly walked in the bustling area, crying while calling the newspaper office: "Please help Haigui one more time!"

"Today he investigated everywhere for where to buy euthanasia medicine." Zhang Shujie said. This is Dong Haigui's second attempt at suicide, the first was on the night when Zhang Shujie knelt down for the first time begging...

In May 2010, Dong Haigui's condition worsened, and Zhang Shujie had no money left. But the doctor said if Dong Haigui stopped dialysis even once, he could possibly die from suffocation. Zhang Shujie bought a piece of red cloth and wrote on it, then knelt down on the busy Xi'an Road with her severely ill husband... That night, Dong Haigui disappeared, leaving a note: "Sujie, don't look for me, I can't drag you down anymore." Later, Zhang Shujie found her husband at Fushun Bay beach and persuaded him to return.

This time, the two were facing the same dilemma again, and Zhang Shujie decided to use all her strength to let her husband live.

If her husband passes away, she will still guard their "home".

Losing her son, getting divorced, returning to her ex-husband with uremic poisoning... At the age of 42, Zhang Shujie married Dong Haigui 18 years ago. She rented a house and earned money cleaning out cesspools, suffering from malnutrition for a long time, her child lived only 41 days before dying, and she lost her right to be a mother. To let Dong Haigui have offspring, Zhang Shujie chose divorce. When she learned that Haigui suffered from uremia, she resolutely returned to his side and took care of him without hesitation.

Over the past three years, the couple have endured various hardships together. Many kind-hearted people were moved by their true feelings and donated money to them successively. Zhang Shujie recorded each donation: A boss from Liaoyang donated over 30,000 yuan, Tang Jie from Dalian Lions Club donated 5000 yuan, last time she knelt down begging and raised over 4000 yuan, after media reports citizens donated over 10,000 yuan... All the money was used for Dong Haigui's dialysis and treatment fees. Meanwhile, Zhang Shujie also operated a small shoe store named "Chasing Happiness" to sustain their living.

However, the shoe store business was very poor, and Zhang Shujie couldn't sell a single pair of shoes for several days. "If I can't raise enough money, Haigui will lose his life." Now Dong Haigui is waiting for a kidney source, Zhang Shujie prayed that the "kidney source" could come sooner, and before that, what she must do is earn money for her husband's dialysis, which means the end of Dong Haigui's life if not done.

The reporter once asked Zhang Shujie: "What will you do if Dong Haigui leaves?" Zhang Shujie calmly replied: "I will continue guarding our home and strive to pay off the debts incurred from his medical treatment." Zhang Shujie will spend her whole life repaying the kind-hearted people who helped them.
