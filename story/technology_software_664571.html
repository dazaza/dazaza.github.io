To have a good understanding of the calculation of premium of various kinds of vehicle loss insurance

by xuntian8746 on 2012-02-13 16:25:46

Vehicle Loss Insurance:

This mainly refers to the insurance that covers damage to the insured vehicle due to internal or external factors while the vehicle is in operation. The scope of coverage and claims for this type of insurance is relatively broad, and regardless of whether the loss was caused by the driver’s own responsibility or others' or external factors, the insured can receive corresponding compensation. The premium for this type of insurance = 1.2% of the car price + basic premium (600 yuan for imported cars, 140 yuan for domestic cars).

Deep dive into the calculation of premiums for various types of insurance:

A. Compulsory Traffic Insurance (CTI):

Compulsory Traffic Insurance (shortened as "CTI") is China's first mandatory insurance system established by national law. According to the Regulations on Compulsory Traffic Insurance, CTI is a compulsory liability insurance where the insurance company compensates for personal injuries or property losses suffered by victims (excluding occupants of the insured vehicle and the insured person) caused by road traffic accidents involving the insured motor vehicle within the limits of liability.

CTI Final Premium = CTI Base Premium × (1 + Floating Rate Linked to Road Traffic Accidents) (See details)

B. Vehicle Loss Insurance:

This covers compensation for damage to the vehicle itself caused by natural disasters or accidents. This is the main type of vehicle insurance. Whether or not to purchase this type of insurance needs to be weighed against its impact. If not insured, all repair costs after a collision must be borne by the owner themselves.

Premium Calculation: Vehicle Loss Insurance Premium = Basic Insurance Premium + Insurance Amount Rate for this Type of Insurance (See details)

C. Third-Party Liability Insurance:

This covers the compensation responsibility for personal injury or direct property damage caused to others (third parties) due to unexpected accidents involving the insured vehicle during its use.

Premium Calculation: Third-Party Liability Insurance Premium equals the fixed premium corresponding to the selected compensation limit tier. (See details)

D. Comprehensive Theft Insurance:

This covers compensation for total loss of the insured vehicle due to theft, robbery, or plundering, as well as losses caused by vehicle damage or the loss of parts or accessories during such incidents.

Premium Calculation: Comprehensive Theft Insurance Premium = Insurance Amount Rate for this Type of Insurance (See details)

E. Occupant Liability Insurance:

This covers the compensation responsibility for personal injury to occupants and direct damage to cargo carried on the vehicle caused by unexpected accidents involving the insured vehicle. The compensation responsibility for personal injury to occupants is equivalent to the previous driver/passenger accidental injury insurance.

Premium Calculation: Occupant Liability Insurance Premium = Compensation Limit Rate for this Type of Insurance (See details)

F. No-Fault Liability Insurance:

This covers situations where the insured vehicle causes personal injury or direct property damage to non-motorized vehicles or pedestrians during use, but the insured vehicle is not responsible for compensation.

Premium Calculation: No-Fault Liability Insurance Premium = Rate of the Same Tier Third-Party Liability Insurance Premium (See details)

G. Cargo Drop Responsibility Insurance:

This covers economic compensation responsibility for third-party personal injury or property damage caused by cargo falling from the insured vehicle during its use.

Premium Calculation: Cargo Drop Insurance Premium = Compensation Limit Rate × (1 + Floating Proportion of Compensation Limit) (See details)

H. Glass Breakage Insurance:

This covers the loss of windshield glass if it breaks alone while the vehicle is parked or in use, with no other parts damaged. The loss of the windshield glass will be compensated by the insurance company.

Premium Calculation: Glass Breakage Insurance Premium = New Car Purchase Price Rate (See details)

I. Vehicle Downtime Loss Insurance:

This covers losses caused by body damage resulting from an insurance accident covered under the Vehicle Loss Insurance policy, leading to downtime. The insurance company will compensate according to regulations.

Premium Calculation: Vehicle Loss Insurance Premium = Basic Insurance Premium + Insurance Amount Rate for this Type of Insurance (See details)

J. Spontaneous Combustion Loss Insurance:

This covers compensation for losses caused by spontaneous combustion due to faults in the vehicle's electrical system, wiring, fuel system, or the nature of the cargo being transported during its use.

Spontaneous Combustion Loss Insurance Premium = Insurance Amount Rate for this Type of Insurance (See details)

K. Additional Equipment Loss Insurance:

If an insurance accident occurs within the scope of Vehicle Loss Insurance, causing direct damage to newly added equipment on the vehicle, the insurance company will compensate based on actual losses. If this type of insurance is not purchased, the insurance company will not cover the losses of newly added equipment.

Premium Calculation: Additional Equipment Loss Insurance Premium = Insurance Amount Rate for this Type of Insurance (See details)

L. Waiver of Deductible Special Insurance:

This can only be purchased simultaneously with Vehicle Loss Insurance and Third-Party Liability Insurance.

Premium Calculation: Waiver of Deductible Special Insurance Premium = (Vehicle Loss Insurance Premium + Third-Party Liability Insurance Premium) Rate
