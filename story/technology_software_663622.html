What is Hong Kong express import? Which type of goods is suitable for import? Why import through Hong Kong? 1. Hong Kong is a real free trade port! The full name of "free port" is "free trade port". Simply put, it is a port or seaport city where goods can enter and exit freely without paying tariffs. The content of the free port policy is mainly reflected in three aspects: free trade, free business operation, and free exchange. For example, free trade does not provide any preferences or privileges for the export of Hong Kong products; no tariffs are generally levied on imported goods; import and export trade procedures are very simple, usually only need to declare within 14 days after the import and export of goods, without prior approval. Therefore, the development of Hong Kong follows such a logical relationship: the free port policy reduces transaction costs, low transaction costs develop trade, trade promotes openness, and attracts foreign investment gathering, which drives the rise of shipping, shipbuilding, warehouse, hotel, catering, commerce and banking industries. The rise of these industries also provides sales channels and market for industrial development, promoting Hong Kong's transformation from entrepot trade to "processing trade".

What is express customs declaration (also known as express customs declaration) import expert? Express customs declaration refers to the submission of express quotation forms (KJ1, KJ2, KJ3 customs declaration forms, etc.) to the customs in the name of an express company, and the clearance of goods in the form of express.

Why choose customs declaration, what are the characteristics of express customs declaration? 1. Extremely simple procedures. According to national regulations such as the Supervision Measures for Inbound and Outbound Expresses, in order to adapt to the customs declaration of expresses as a "rapid commercial operation mode for carrying and transporting inbound and outbound goods and items", a series of special arrangements have been made in terms of supervision conditions and customs clearance (customs declaration and customs clearance) procedures to simplify procedures and speed up the process. Therefore, in general, there is no need for the cargo owner to specifically provide customs declaration forms, customs clearance documents, licenses/approval documents (such as: 3C mandatory certification, environmental protection certificate, import quota, etc.). 2. Extremely low fees. According to national regulations such as the Supervision Measures for Inbound and Outbound Expresses, expresses entering and leaving the country can enjoy certain tax and fee reduction benefits. In addition, due to the geographical advantage of Shenzhen being close to Hong Kong, an international free port, the operation is convenient, the tax rate is low, the operating cost is low, and the cost is naturally lower. 3. Extremely fast speed. On the one hand, due to fewer regulatory conditions and simpler procedures, the procedures are simplified, which naturally saves time; on the other hand, during the process and customs clearance, the speed of inspection reflects the "fast" nature of express. Also, the express customs supervision center works on evenings and Saturdays.

In which cases and for which goods is express customs declaration (express customs declaration) the most suitable import method? 1. Small pieces of scattered goods, small quantities of samples and advertisements, etc.; 2. Various products lacking certain units (such as without 3C certification); 3. Urgent goods that do not have time to handle trade customs clearance procedures; 4. Products that want to save costs but do not need VAT invoices; 5. Personal use items.

If it is large-scale and bulky goods, can they also be imported through express customs declaration? Is there still an advantage? As mentioned above, large-scale goods in tons or tens of tons can be declared in batches and phases, of course, this will affect the speed of the whole batch of goods; however, the advantages of extremely simple procedures and extremely low fees remain the same. So its advantages should not be underestimated.
