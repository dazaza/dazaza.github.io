International high-end clothing brand merchants come to Wenzhou

by mttasod13 on 2009-12-06 02:05:15

Wenzhou garment merchants have firmly established their position in the domestic garment industry through concerted efforts and continuous innovation. Yesterday, Wenzhou merchants introduced another new initiative with the opening of a three-day event titled "2009 International (Wenzhou) Procurement Trade Week and the First High-end Men's Wear Brand Joint Ordering Fair" at the Wenzhou Garment Development Tower. Over 50 international buyers and more than 200 domestic high-end men's wear brands gathered in Wenzhou for a "seamless" connection with local enterprises.

The "Wenzhou Manufacturing" was impressive. During this three-day ordering fair, the Italian Northwest Chamber of Commerce led more than 20 renowned Italian men's wear brand enterprises to bring cutting-edge fashion information and business opportunities to Wenzhou garment manufacturing enterprises. Meanwhile, over 200 high-end men's wear brand suppliers from across the country will conduct "seamless" trade connections with more than 300 Wenzhou private label enterprises right at their doorstep.

Before the exhibition opened, Mr. Cai, an Indonesian buyer, had already taken some time to make a preliminary tour. He stopped at Wenzhou Zhongse Clothing Co., Ltd. (shirts), clearly showing interest in the shirts produced by this enterprise. This company provides private label production for high-end men's wear brands both domestically and internationally, producing over 300,000 shirts annually. The company's general manager, Xie Qinwang, said that they had hardly participated in similar exhibitions before. They decided to take part this time mainly because of the high platform targeting high-end men's wear, which fits the company's characteristics.

"This exhibition has allowed me to see the overall strength of Wenzhou men's wear manufacturing," said Wang Tingjun, a buyer who came from Inner Mongolia. He used to conduct purchases in the north but came to Wenzhou this time on a friend's recommendation, with a mindset of trying it out. To his surprise, Wenzhou gave him a pleasant shock. "I didn't expect Wenzhou's men's wear manufacturing technology to be so advanced," Wang Tingjun said, planning to stay a few more days in Wenzhou for further investigation.

The Garment Development Tower acted as a "matchmaker." This event was organized by the Municipal Foreign Economic Relations and Trade Bureau, co-organized by the Garment Chamber of Commerce, and undertaken by the Wenzhou Garment Development Company. Known as the "Smiling Tower," the Wenzhou Garment Development Tower became the "matchmaker" for this "marriage" activity.

The Wenzhou Garment Development Tower, which welcomed guests in August this year, integrates the "Wenzhou SME Garment Enterprise Service Platform" and the "Wenzhou Foreign Trade Garment Enterprise Export Service Platform." Currently, nearly 200 Wenzhou enterprises have moved into the tower, with 150 companies setting up display centers there.

Xu Yunxu, vice president of the Wenzhou Garment Chamber of Commerce and general manager of the Wenzhou Garment Development Company, stated that taking this high-end men's wear ordering fair as an opportunity to establish Wenzhou's reputation as a high-end gathering place and seek matching partners for its world-class manufacturing capability is a very significant first move for the industry.

Today, within the Garment Development Tower, there will be activities such as lectures by Italian designers, negotiation meetings for Italian brand agency and acquisition, and exchanges of Italian fabric technology and information. These negotiations aim to allow more excellent Italian brands to enter the Chinese market through Wenzhou's network, with some Italian brands even being operated by Wenzhou people through acquisition and transfer methods; fabric exchanges hope that Wenzhou enterprises can access the latest international popular fabric trend information.

Wenzhou men's wear needs to cultivate both internal and external strengths. Data shows that in 2008, Wenzhou's total garment output value reached over 50 billion yuan, with men's wear accounting for more than 30 billion yuan, and the market transaction amount of men's wear reaching over 200 billion yuan. Meanwhile, Wenzhou men's wear manufacturing has captured over 50% of the domestic first and second-tier men's wear manufacturing markets. That means currently, out of every two pieces of high-end men's wear nationwide, one is produced in Wenzhou.

"Wenzhou will spend the next five years building itself into the 'Chinese High-end Men's Wear Aggregation Base,'" said Zheng Chen'ai, president of the Wenzhou Garment Chamber of Commerce. She believes that Wenzhou's early development of the men's wear industry, ample talent resources, and internationally leading production technology give it the capital to become a base. Currently, Wenzhou has over 100 high-end men's wear manufacturing enterprises. To establish Wenzhou's leading position in the high-end men's wear industry and gain the initiative in supply chain cooperation, Zheng Chen'ai said that Wenzhou men's wear must cultivate both internal and external strengths.

Internally, it should rely on overseas Wenzhounese resources to widely promote the image of Wenzhou men's wear and improve and perfect the global market network, relying on the "Wenzhou Famous Purchases" plan advocated by the municipal Party committee and government to convert the current industrial total output value of men's wear into market sales totals; externally, it should further increase cooperation with the international men's wear industry. "Next, Wenzhou enterprises are expected to engage in deep cooperation in various fields of men's wear with relevant institutions from Italy, France, and other countries," said Zheng Chen'ai, who indicated that related plans are currently being formulated.
