Web2.0Share Weekly: 15 Startup Projects Recommended by AngelBay DemoDay

by ybcz0519 on 2011-11-20 19:10:27

#AngelBay Fusion# Season 1 events were held nationwide in June this year, and ultimately selected and invested in 15 internet teams from across China and North America. These 15 projects held their final grand event - Demo Day on November 5th, 2011, in Hangzhou. I'm sharing these 15 projects with everyone right away.

Huiyun Network is an online platform for managing meetings and events.

Link: http://confone.com

Lebang is a mobile help application based on LBS (Location-Based Services) aimed at solving problems within a 3km radius around you. Whether your cat goes missing, you're caught in the rain without an umbrella, your computer crashes, or you forget toilet paper, if you have good karma or offer sufficient rewards, someone will be there to help! Lebang offers downloads for both iPhone and Android.

Link: http://www.whohelp.me

Muerr is a completely user-defined web whiteboard. It makes it effortless for any user to publish articles online, upload photos, and download documents. You can turn this web whiteboard into anything you want—Blog? Photo album? Universal bookmark? Instant discussion area?... All are possible! What Muerr is, you decide.

Link: http://muerr.com/

Bailty is a personal shopping decision assistant. When faced with countless products in the mall and unsure of what to choose, simply take out your phone and snap a picture of the product barcode to instantly obtain reviews about the product. Good or bad, buy or not, listen to what others say!

Link: http://www.bailty.com/

Cuoluo is a website/application that provides random solutions for daily life. Our lives are full of unnecessary choices, Cuoluo simplifies chaotic life and makes it more interesting by offering features like randomly listening to music, reading jokes, deciding what to eat, learning words, raffles, generating passwords... The possibilities are endless, just try it yourself.

Link: http://www.cuoluo.com/

Youyu is a virtual employment and online work platform. On Youyu, individuals can apply to multiple companies, sign virtual employment contracts, establish long-term relationships, with high and stable income; companies can hire and store large numbers of virtual employees, only needing to pay when work transactions occur. Youyu allows the working class to be freer and happier!

Link: http://www.youyur.com

Miihuu is a personal search engine.

Link: http://www.miihuu.com

Jupoo is a local activity gathering application based on LBS. By posting an activity on Jupoo, it spreads faster, allowing users to quickly discover and participate in nearby activities. Fun living starts with Jupoo.

Link: http://www.juupoo.com/

52Hz is a webpage recommendation tool based on user preferences. According to user evaluations of webpages, it recommends other unknown pages that users might be interested in, providing a new way to explore the internet.

Link: http://www.52hz.com/

Timap is a smart schedule management tool based on LBS. We encounter many related tasks every day, at different times and places. Timap helps users intelligently coordinate schedules in terms of time and space, making time more efficient and life simpler.

Link: http://timap.me/

Xiaoenai is a website specifically designed for couples and newlyweds. Through various fun communication methods, it makes the emotional life between couples more interesting and understanding each other better. Xiaoenai's mission is to make couples and married people love each other more!

Link: http://www.xiaoenai.com

Hupo is a campus social mobile application based on LBS. You can send anonymous love letters to the handsome guys or beautiful girls you meet on campus, spread the top ten campus gossips anytime, or use AR augmented reality technology to digitize everyone around you. And all of this can be done without following anyone, as PR algorithm automatically builds relationship maps. Hupo makes meeting strangers simple.

Link: Not available yet

Fanyor is a social dining platform. Chinese dinners are filled with attraction because they bring together business contacts, likeminded friends, and singles' dates. Fanyor naturally connects all of this, bringing people from online to offline gatherings.

Link: http://www.fanyor.com

Tongxing is a free iPhone application. Install it in your iPhone, and when you're ready to go out, find the most suitable travel companion nearby conveniently and pleasantly. You can also choose your preferred mode of transportation: share a taxi or drive.

Link: http://itongxing.com/

Matii is a website/mobile application that provides "live" guides for independent travelers. It helps independent travel enthusiasts plan trips more easily and reasonably, and recommends appropriate attractions and routes according to changes in the itinerary. Matii makes independent travel simpler and fills the journey with surprises!

Link: http://maatii.com/ (Not yet online)

Content Source: Angel Bay Venture Capital

Official Sina Weibo: http://t.sina.com.cn/web20share

Watsonxu says: The increasing number of start-ups domestically indicates a growing entrepreneurial atmosphere. Institutions like Innovation Works and Angel Bay Venture Capital play an active role in this. A quick look at the 15 projects shows that many are mobile-oriented, and quite a few combine with geographical locations. However, for projects combining with geographical locations, entrepreneurs may face several issues: how to solve the problem of low user density in the initial stage; projects may involve offline-related businesses which they may not be adept at; business models for location-based services are more combined with offline operations, requiring a deep understanding of the targeted offline businesses.
