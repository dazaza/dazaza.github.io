How to Increase Online Store Traffic and Promote Your Store Better - 29 Methods for Store Promotion

Among the 29 methods that you can consider adopting, perhaps you have already used some. Some methods you may have forgotten to use, and there are still others that you have never heard of. In short, here you can learn about all kinds of effective website promotion methods currently available. First, you need to understand that website promotion is a long-term and systematic process that requires setting clear goals and plans and making corresponding preparations. The most important strategy in search engine promotion is to rank leading in the main search engines with the most important keywords of your site. Search engine robots automatically search web page content, so search engine strategies start with optimizing web pages.

1. Add Web Page Titles.

Write a descriptive title of 5 to 8 words for each page content. Keep titles concise by avoiding unimportant words like "of" or "and." The title should clearly state what the most important content on this page and site is. Web page titles will appear as links on the search results page, so they can be slightly provocative to attract searchers to click on the link. Also, include your company name and most important keywords in the homepage content, not just the company name. Every page of the website should have a title specific to that page.

2. Add Descriptive META Tags.

Besides the web page title, many search engines will also search META tags. These are explanatory sentences describing the content of the web page, which should also contain keywords and phrases used on this page. Currently, META tags containing keywords have little impact on rankings, but sometimes META tags are used in paid submission techniques. And who knows when search engines might start valuing them again?

3. Add Your Keywords to Bold Text, Generally Article Titles.

Search engines place great importance on bold text, assuming it contains crucial information on the page. Therefore, ensure that your keywords are included in one or two bold text tags. Avoid turning important titles into images, and definitely do not make the entire homepage a single image.

4. Ensure Your Keywords Appear in the First Paragraph of Your Content.

Search engines hope to find your keywords in the first paragraph of text, but avoid overloading with too many keywords. Google considers approximately 1.5 to 2 keywords per 100 words to be the optimal keyword density for good rankings. Other places where keywords can be placed include ALT or COMMENT tags in the code. If it's impossible to ensure the first paragraph includes keywords, consider alternative measures such as adding an annotation to the webpage.

5. Navigation Design Should Be Easy for Search Engines to Crawl.

Some people use frames in web design, but this poses serious problems for search engines. Even if search engines find your content pages, they may miss key navigation sections, thus preventing access to other pages. Navigation buttons made with JavaScript and Flash look aesthetically pleasing, but search engines cannot find them. A remedy is to add another navigation bar at the bottom of the page using regular HTML links, ensuring access to every page of the site through this navigation bar. You can also create a sitemap that links to every page. Moreover, some content management systems and e-commerce directories use dynamic web pages; these pages' URLs typically end with a question mark followed by numbers. Overworked search engines often stop at the question mark without continuing to crawl further. This situation can be resolved through URL modification, paid submission, etc. Dynamic sites can facilitate search engine crawling by generating static pages. Sitemaps should aim to link to all major pages and submit the sitemap page separately to search engines. Use more HTML static web pages, even though it increases workload, to enhance search engine friendliness and lay the foundation for obtaining good rankings.

6. Create Specific Pages Targeting Certain Important Keywords.

SEO experts do not recommend creating any deceptive transitional pages for search engines, as these almost identical pages could lead to penalties from search engines. However, you can create a few pages, each concentrating on different keywords or phrases. For example, instead of introducing all your services on one page, create a separate page for each service. This way, each page has its corresponding keywords, and the content of these pages, due to containing targeted keywords rather than general content, can improve rankings.

7. Submit Web Pages to Search Engines.

Find the [ADD YOUR URL] website login link on the search engine. Search engines will automatically crawl the web pages you submit. The most famous search engines in the U.S. are Google, Inktomi, AltaVista, Teoma. These search engines provide search content to other major search engines and portals. In Europe and other regions, you can publish to regional search engines. Spending money to have someone submit your site to thousands of search engines is a waste. Do not use FFA (Free For All Pages) websites that claim to automatically submit your site to hundreds of search engines. Such submissions not only have poor effects but also bring large amounts of spam emails and may result in penalties from search engines. Do not use website submission software that claims to submit your site to thousands of search engines at once, as it is both impossible and of no practical value. The most important thing is to optimize your website design well. For major search engines, adopt a manual submission method. For paid search engines, software submission is even less possible. In fact, an effective search engine marketing strategy does not require submitting your site to thousands of search engines because the top few search engines concentrate almost 98% of the traffic, and the remaining search engines hold little practical significance. Additionally, note that submitting web pages should not be a one-time event. As content updates, regularly resubmit your web pages. Due to the increasing number of web pages, many search bots have long revisit times, and regular submissions allow search engines to fetch the latest updated content.

8. Adjust Main Content Pages to Improve Rankings.

Adjust pages you consider most important, perhaps the homepage, to improve their rankings. Some software allows you to check current rankings, compare the rankings of competitors' web pages with similar keywords, and obtain preferred statistical data from search engines regarding your web page, allowing you to adjust your own pages accordingly. You can use WebPositionGold to do this work yourself, but since it takes time, you can also hire professional companies to help. Links from other websites can bring more traffic. Since Google and other major search engines use the breadth of a website's links as an important factor in ranking, the more websites linking to you, the higher your website's ranking will be. Additionally, the quality of links is an important factor considered by search engines. Links from high-traffic websites are more advantageous than those from low-traffic websites.

9. Submit Your Website to Major Directories.

Ensure your website is logged into free directories like Open Directory (), which are manually reviewed for logins. This hierarchical directory provides directory content for searches by all major search engines. Google values whether your website has links on important sites. Yahoo is another important directory that you need to log into. Tip: The number of words describing your website should ideally be fewer than the maximum allowed, not exactly reaching the word limit, to avoid overly long descriptions causing review personnel to delete some segments. Currently, commercial website logins to Yahoo cost $299 annually, and it's best to have them log your website within 7 working days. Other directories you can consider logging into include About.com and Business.com. There are many website navigation sites domestically, such as ... etc., which can also bring significant traffic. Even if your online marketing budget is limited, registering with major search engines like Sina and Sohu is still very necessary.

10. Log Your Website onto Industry Sites and Professional Directories.

Some directories focus on specific industries, such as education or finance. If you belong to a trade association that aggregates numerous member sites, you can apply to join your site to the association's website, even if it costs money, as it will bring you many target visitors. Besides being able to log onto many yellow-page type websites, searching on search engines according to your industry will certainly find many related industry directory sites, which you should try to log onto as much as possible.

11. Request Link Exchanges.

Look for complementary sites to your website content and request link exchanges. The ideal link partners are those with website traffic comparable to yours. Administrators of high-traffic websites have to handle too many requests for link exchanges, making it easy for them to overlook you. Smaller websites can also be considered. Place the link exchange page in a relatively obscure part of the site to avoid quickly directing your site visitors to others' sites. After finding potential link exchange websites, send a personalized email to the opposing site administrator. If the other party does not respond, try calling them. Always pay attention to the quality of the opposing site, do not blindly pursue the quantity of links, and do not send spam emails.

12. Publish Free Articles with Site Signatures.

Write professional articles for other websites' newsletters for free, briefly describing what you offer in the article and requesting the other party to link to your website. This is an effective viral marketing method, as your article will be sent out as subscription information to thousands of users, instantly gaining hundreds of links to your website. When re-posting others' articles, be sure to add your own link.

Traditional offline promotion methods remain effective. Using multiple methods simultaneously is especially effective.

13. Print Your Website Address on Letterheads, Business Cards, Brochures, and Printed Materials.

This simple method is sometimes overlooked. Ensure the website address is spelled correctly. It is recommended to omit the http:// part and only write www.****.com. An easily memorable domain name facilitates website promotion, so carefully consider your choice of domain name.

14. Use Traditional Media Advertising.

Traditional media advertising should not be abandoned. Whether newspaper or magazine ads, ensure your website address is displayed in them. Present visiting the website as supplementary content in the ad, reminding users that browsing the website will provide more relevant information. Do not ignore advertising in some relatively narrow magazines or trade journals, as sometimes these advertisements are more accurate and effective. They are also cheaper than online ads. Other traditional ways to increase website traffic include direct mail, classified ads, postcards, etc. Television ads are probably more suitable for websites selling mass-market products. Make the website address appear in a prominent position in the ad, as it is easier to remember than a phone number.

15. Provide Free Services.

People love free things. Attracting people to visit your website through free information content is more effective than simply inviting them to visit and learn about your business. Establishing free resources requires time and effort, but it is highly effective in increasing traffic. The free content you provide should closely relate to what you sell, so the visitors you attract are likely to be potential target customers. While providing free services, the website should offer various linking methods to guide users acquiring free information to the product sales section. Examples of free resources include: free email, free website registration, free posting of information, and offering unique free services if possible.

16. Issue News Releases.

Find newsworthy events, such as promoting the free services you offer, and issue news releases to print journals and website journals in your industry. You can use some online news release services. News promotion is always effective, which is why traditional media often feature so many corporate ads that resemble news.

Email is an important method to increase traffic, but the premise is not to send large amounts of unsolicited spam.

17. Create a Signature in Your Outgoing Emails to Connect with Potential Clients.

Most email systems have the ability to create signatures. Signatures automatically appear at the end of every email you send. Limit signatures to 6-8 lines. Include: company name, address, phone, website, email, and a brief description of your business. Do not design signatures as pictures for aesthetic reasons, as they should be easy for clients to copy.

18. Build Email Lists and Regularly Send Newsletters or Other Information to Users.

This is one of the best ways to maintain contact with customers, build trust, develop brands, and establish long-term relationships. Ask website visitors to fill out their email addresses to receive your newsletters. Sending emails requires a bulk server, which are available on the network.

19. Send Product Information to Email List Users, Such as Coupons, New Products, and Other Promotional Information.

If the email subject and body are personalized, better results will be achieved. The five basic principles for designing email subjects are: 1. Reflect the essence of the email content. 2. Reflect information that cannot be contained in the sender's details. 3. Reflect brand or product information. 4. The email subject contains rich keywords. 5. The email subject should neither be too simple nor too complex.

20. Rent Target Customer Email Lists.

These email list customers agree to receive certain category commercial emails when registering on the email list provider's website, so sending your product information to these users is legal and not spam. Using email address search software to collect email addresses or buying email addresses belongs to spam and not only brings no benefits but may even result in penalties.

21. Promote in Email Lists and News Groups.

Many people with common interests in a particular professional field discuss and exchange ideas online, forming tens of thousands of news groups in different fields. Find news groups in your field, not to openly promote your products or services, but to leave your email address and website in the signature during exchanges. As people gradually get to know and trust you, they will visit your website.

News groups are not commonly used domestically as a website promotion method. Forums and chat rooms were once used to promote products, but now many websites do not welcome advertising information being posted in these network communities unless there are dedicated advertisement sections, so it is best not to adopt this method unless necessary.

22. Utilize Competitions.

You can design a competition on your website, with prizes that can attract the type of customers you hope to gain. For example, design the prize as your product or a certain purchase discount, etc. Utilizing competitions can effectively attract more traffic. If your visibility is not high, how to let customers know about your competition is also a problem, so this method is not applicable to some websites.

23. Encourage Users to Bookmark Your Website.

Our practice is to add a bookmark icon on the homepage, making it convenient for truly interested customers to click the browser's bookmark. Definitely do not force the website to be set as the customer's homepage or frequently automatically prompt to add the site to bookmarks or set it as the homepage while browsing.

24. Exchange Ads with Complementary Websites.

Utilize the traffic from the other website to reach a new audience. Many websites have a friendly link column, joining an ad exchange network is the most convenient, but some ad exchange networks come with codes that pop up plugins, possibly annoying viewers and affecting website loading speed. Ad exchange between partners is ideal and worth the time spent.

25. Create Viral Marketing Methods.

Viral marketing spreads information quickly through various communication methods, such as oral transmission, news releases, etc. The 12th promotion method is viral marketing. The classic viral marketing method is the email service provider briefly introducing their service at the end of every email sent by users. This information spreads with the email, quickly reaching new users, who may continue to pass it on to other new users. Note that viral marketing is not about creating viruses; it is essentially a free service, and as long as this service is valuable, it will automatically spread among users.

Paid advertisements will more quickly promote your website. Online advertising prices generally have three calculation methods: traditional CPM (cost per thousand impressions), PPC (pay per click), payment based on actual purchase quantities, and affiliate program fee methods, etc.

26. Purchase Short Text Ads in Email Newsletters.

Choose a mail list suitable for your target positioning and publish short text ads in the newsletters sent to the series of users. Compared with other online ads, this is a very effective and cost-effective advertising method.

27. Implement Membership Marketing.

Affiliate members add the master website's link on their own website, and after sales are realized through this link, a certain commission will be given to the member. To implement membership marketing, consider the following factors: the commission percentage given to members, developing an affiliate program suitable for yourself, promoting the membership program to get more members linking to your website. You can use some affiliate management software.

28. Bid for Search Engine Rankings.

Purchase pay-per-click ads and keyword ads on search engines, and your ads will appear on the result pages under certain keywords on many search engines. Since the same keyword is often purchased by multiple clients, the specific ranking order depends on the bid price. Bid-based ranking pays according to the actual number of ad clicks, and it is an advertising method with accurate target positioning, low cost, and good effect. Domestically, 3721, Sohu, Sina, NetEase, Baidu all provide bidding ranking services.

29. Submit Goods to Comparison Shopping Websites and Auction Websites.

eBay and malls launched by major websites can be tried. Additionally, utilizing online store platforms for online marketing is also one of the effective methods.
