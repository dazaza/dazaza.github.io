Cost accounting for pursuing a girl

by the9th on 2007-08-17 16:58:39

Good Girl Type

Good girls are generally more refined and family-oriented. They don't often go out shopping, so their entertainment expenses won't be too high. They also won't ask you to frequently buy them branded jewelry or clothes. However, they love romance, so it's best if you save money for phone bills, haha. During your free time, you can go shopping, give her small surprises on holidays, such as roses, chocolates, little trinkets, crystals. Occasionally, watch movies, ballets, listen to concerts, and travel less often.

Also, these girls usually trust close friends and family, so you must implement a "rural encirclement" strategy and never neglect this important force. I've seen boys succeed many times through this method. This expense is necessary.

Average monthly expenses:

Phone bill: (based on local calls, domestic and international long-distance cannot be calculated) 200 yuan

Shopping: 100 yuan per week, 450 yuan per month;

Small holiday gifts: an average of 100~300 yuan per month

Entertainment consumption: an average of 100 yuan per month

Cost of winning hearts: an average of 100 yuan per month;

Total: 600~1150 yuan/month

Lively Girl Type

Lively girls hate staying at home the most. They chatter non-stop and there will never be a dull moment with them around. These girls dislike fancy skirts and accessories, they don't like boring performances, and they certainly won't be content just imagining things over the phone. But, they love to play! Wildly play! Always hanging out on the streets, they like sports attire, wearing basketball shoes endorsed by celebrities, knowing which brand of mobile phone is the latest trend, where the hottest disco is this weekend, what events are happening at which bars. They actively participate in socially popular activities, especially sports like rock climbing and karting. They are loyal fans of celebrity concerts and are even more enthusiastic about short and long-distance trips. They do have quiet moments, and that's when they're surfing the internet in front of a computer, haha. They are untamable little wild cats.

Average monthly expenses:

Entertainment consumption: Since these activities generally involve a group of friends going out to play, and the boys take turns paying, the average weekly cost is 200 yuan, which amounts to 900 yuan per month.

Shopping and gaming machine fees: 100 yuan per week, 450 yuan per month

Holiday gifts: an average of 100~300 yuan per month

Travel expenses: an average of 300~700 yuan per month

Total: 1300~2350 yuan/month

Pretty Girl Type

Pretty girls are usually quite lively and very popular among boys. However, not everyone can handle pursuing these girls. They've already been spoiled by boys: they like beautiful clothes and accessories, use high-end skincare products, spray expensive perfumes, and prefer not to take public transportation like buses or subways. They often dine at upscale Western restaurants, shop at high-end malls, and watch movies at premium cinemas. Or maybe they don't ask for anything, but would you really let such an ethereal beauty sweat alongside you on a crowded bus and eat noodles at a street-side stall? Besides, even if she doesn't ask for expensive gifts, who can say others haven't secretly sent them?

Moreover, spending money alone isn't enough when dealing with these girls; you need to think carefully. Otherwise, you'll just get lost in the sea of stars chasing her. For example: one call every day, one rose, and persistence is key to leaving a deep impression.

Additionally, the parents and close friends of these girls should not be overlooked. Even if you don't expect them to help you succeed, at least don't ruin your chances.

Average monthly expenses:

Expenses for creativity: 150~300 yuan per month

Date expenses: an average of 200 yuan per week, 900 yuan per month

Shopping expenses: an average of 200 yuan per week, 900 yuan per month

Holiday gifts: an average of 300~500 yuan per month

Cost of winning hearts: an average of 100~200 yuan per month

Total: 2350~2800 yuan/month

The above data is based on average income levels. The higher the income, the greater the possible expenses. Of course, these are all matters during the initial pursuit period, and it's not necessarily true that the more money spent, the higher the success rate. Once this girl falls in love with you, she will naturally consider your wallet. If not, then you yourself need to reflect.
