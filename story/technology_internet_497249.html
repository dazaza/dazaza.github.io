Video: iPhone 5 or iPod Touch 3G Case?

by diglog on 2011-09-30 03:41:22

Not long ago, micgadget released a video filmed by a Shenzhen contract manufacturer showing an iPhone5 case display. The video showcased multiple products and compared them. Is it the iPhone 5 or the iPod Touch 3G? Even though Apple has not yet announced the name of the latest version of the iPhone, it seems that Chinese accessory stores already have quite a few "iPhone5" cases for you to choose from. These cases all share the same three-dimensional parameters (see video at 2:39). According to micgadget, this is likely due to the loss of an iPhone5 prototype at Foxconn in Shenzhen recently. Chinese case manufacturers, even when driven by small profits, tend to be very active. They are also well aware of the serious consequences of obtaining Apple prototypes through illegal means. In June this year, three speculators were imprisoned for conspiring to sell a prototype of the iPad 2 to a case manufacturer for 20,000 yuan. At the 2:39 mark, there is a specific parameter measurement of the case for those who are interested: Youku, Youtube. Micgadget claims that this case is prepared for the iPhone 5, but readers quickly countered saying that it is actually the shell for the new iPod Touch 3G. "The back cover design of the iPod Touch has always been rounded corners. I dare say the shape of the iPhone 5 is different from any previous device, with its hardware configuration definitely being the A5 processor, 8-megapixel camera, and borderless large screen." Another long-circulated rumored image is as follows; please note the thickness of the body, which really matches the back cover in the video. What is the truth?
