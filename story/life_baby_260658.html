How to Ensure the Health and Environmental Friendliness of a Baby's Room

by longersing on 2009-11-17 15:34:03

How to ensure the health and environmental protection of a nursery? Infants aged 0-3 years spend most of their time indoors. During this stage, infants have relatively low physical resistance. Therefore, arranging an environmentally friendly, safe, and comfortable indoor environment for infants is crucial for their healthy development and growth.

To provide a green and environmentally friendly nursery for children, it is essential to choose green and eco-friendly building and decorating materials during renovation. However, using green materials does not necessarily guarantee green decoration. Any type of decorative material may meet environmental standards and be considered green individually, but when multiple materials are combined, the situation can change. The release of harmful substances is also closely related to changes in temperature and humidity. Therefore, to create a green and environmentally friendly nursery, it is necessary to strictly follow the "four steps": green building materials, green construction, testing, and treatment.

Ensuring the environmental friendliness of a nursery starts with selecting green decorative materials

In the renovation of a nursery, it is advisable to use natural and eco-friendly materials as much as possible, such as wooden materials. Specifically, in house decoration, flooring, walls, and lighting fixtures are the three main projects, so it is important to pay attention to these three categories when choosing materials for the nursery.

Flooring: The flooring in a nursery should preferably be made of solid wood or eco-friendly carpets. These materials are natural and environmentally friendly, and they are soft and warm, suitable for infants to play and learn crawling.

Walls: For the decoration of a nursery, it is better to use eco-friendly fabric wallpapers for the walls, as they are resistant to drawings and easy to clean.

Lighting: The lighting in a baby's room should vary depending on its position. Ceiling lights should be bright; wall lights should be soft; table lamps should not hurt the eyes. It is best to use several small spotlights for the ceiling light, which can be adjusted at any angle. This is beneficial for lighting and protecting the baby's eyes.

Environmental considerations must be taken into account during the construction phase

After processing and construction, decorative materials undergo changes in form, and the amount of harmful substances released from these materials will inevitably change as well. Therefore, in the decoration of a nursery, it is necessary to choose materials that require fewer processing steps, adhering to the principle of "no pollution, easy cleaning," and opting for natural materials whenever possible. The fewer intermediate processing steps, the better. Some imported baby-specific wallpapers or high-quality wall paints conform to this principle, containing fewer harmful substances and being easy to wipe clean.

There are standards to follow for environmental protection

According to relevant national regulations, the main environmental indicators in a nursery include: carbon monoxide levels less than 5 mg per cubic meter; humidity should be maintained between 30% and 70%... Other indoor environmental indicators include: the formaldehyde emission limit value in artificial boards used in decoration projects should be less than 1.5 mg/L; the maximum allowable concentration of harmful substances in residential area air, the standard for ammonia in the air is that the control concentration of ammonia gas in each cubic meter of air should not exceed 0.2 mg.

As mentioned earlier, after the nursery is renovated, it is essential to pay attention to ventilation. According to tests by indoor environmental experts, the frequency of indoor air exchange directly affects the content of harmful substances in indoor air. The more frequent the indoor air exchange or the use of air filters and exchangers, the fewer harmful substances there will be in the air, possibly even none.
