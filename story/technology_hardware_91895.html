China Migrant Workers Technical Work Network

by nmgjggz on 2008-09-14 22:49:57

The first website providing employment services for migrant workers - I am someone who studied hard to come from a rural area to the city, and I know how important it is for rural families to find work. I hope to repay the nurturing grace of the countryside through this platform. The newly launched website provides free registration for job seekers and employers to post recruitment and job-seeking information.

Individuals can register for free and post job-seeking information to have jobs come looking for them, without the need to worry about finding work. They can also see recruitment information from other provinces and choose based on treatment and working conditions. Individuals can also post recruitment information, such as looking for nannies or cleaners. [Register]

Employers can register for free and post recruitment information, making it convenient for job seekers to browse and contact them. They can search for qualified individual job seeker information and recruitment information posted by employment agencies and village organizations, easily obtaining cross-provincial recruitment information. [Register]

Employment agencies can register for free and connect with employers and other employment agencies nationwide, sharing information and achieving cross-regional information exchange. [Register]

Villages can register for free and post recruitment introduction information under the collective's name to be searched by employers. They can also see recruitment information from various provinces and provide services to villagers. [Register]

Town governments can register for free and post recruitment information under the collective's name or guide villages to post recruitment information. They can also timely provide information to people who cannot or do not know how to access the internet, truly realizing the government's function of serving the people and becoming a government that serves the people. [Register]

Training schools can register for free and post introduction information, allowing people who want to learn a skill to choose suitable schools and majors in advance, which is also a form of self-promotion. Schools can also post job-seeking information to help students quickly find jobs and establish cooperation with employers. [Register]

Law firms can register for free and post introduction information, enabling those in need of legal assistance to receive timely help and promote themselves. [Register]

The purpose of this website is to provide an employment information platform for a wide range of workers and enterprises. With the support of the masses and enterprises, its role in serving the people will become increasingly significant.

Through this website, individuals can realize having jobs come looking for them. They can find a job for themselves, relatives can find a job for each other, friends can find a job for each other, and students can find a job for their parents online. Each registration is an opportunity.
