Man raises over 100 million yuan through illegal fundraising as police confiscate tens of millions of yuan in dirty money and receive awards

by zsh0827245 on 2012-02-22 13:46:32

The cash and goods police seized By Gong Xuanchu and Xu Yuanbin BEIJING, October 25 - According to news yesterday from the Yuzhong District Public Security Bureau, Chongqing police acted swiftly on a mutual assistance request from Henan police, successfully capturing two fugitives from Henan province. On the spot, they seized over 8.8 million yuan in cash, 1000 grams of gold bars, and a newly purchased Cadillac SUV. The total value of the confiscated illicit proceeds and goods is close to 10 million yuan. Deputy Minister of Public Security Liu Jingguo commended the Chongqing police and rewarded them for their efforts. Urgent call for help from Henan police late at night Chongqing police seize nearly 10 million yuan in illicit proceeds and goods It was reported that at around midnight on October 24, the Municipal Public Security Bureau received a report from Henan police requesting assistance in capturing two online fugitives who had fled to the Yuzhong District of Chongqing. The Chongqing police immediately instructed the Yuzhong District Public Security Bureau to organize and implement the arrest operation. Through rapid and intense work, they quickly ascertained the movement trails of the two fugitives and successfully locked down their hiding place. Nearby police forces were mobilized, and within three minutes, case investigation officers from Dàyánggōu Police Station arrived at the location where the fugitives were hiding. At 00:30 AM on the same day, the police apprehended the sleeping suspects Li Yan and Li Shuang at a five-star hotel in Jiefangbei, seizing on the spot over 8.8 million yuan in cash and 1000 grams of gold bars. Subsequently, they also discovered in the hotel garage a Cadillac SUV that the two had just purchased. The total value of the cash and illicit goods seized on-site amounted to nearly 10 million yuan. Police warn about high-return investment projects Li Yan, aged 29, is from Anyang City in Henan Province. In February 2010, his father established Henan Anyang Anran New Energy Co., Ltd., which lured the public with high interest rates to deposit more than 100 million yuan. In mid-September this year, both the company's office and construction site could not be found, and the legal representative was unreachable. Numerous victims filed reports, prompting the Henan police to begin investigating the case and placing relevant suspects under online manhunt. On October 22, Li Yan used illegally raised funds to purchase a luxury car and bought 1000 grams of gold bars using cash. He then fled to Chongqing with 8.8 million yuan in cash, assisted by his girlfriend Li Shuang. Officers reminded the public that when faced with various fundraising and financing activities, investors should inquire about the business operations and legal representatives from departments such as the industry and commerce bureau before investing. Especially for those "high returns" and "quick enrichment" investment projects, one should calmly analyze and distinguish between truth and falsehood to avoid being deceived.
