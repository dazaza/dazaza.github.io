The picture shows Yin Jian (center) on trial at the Intermediate People's Court of Hengyang City, Hunan Province.

On April 19, 2011, at the Intermediate People's Court of Hengyang City, Hunan Province, more than 160 armed police and court bailiffs were present with loaded weapons. In the criminal courtroom, 34 defendants from the once rampant Hengyang "gang" known as "Heaven and Earth" were on trial.

The reporter of the Legal Daily noted that the indictment for this case was 71 pages long, nearly 40,000 words. Five years ago, it took Yang Hancheng, the deputy procurator of Hengyang City People's Procuratorate, over three hours to read it out in full.

"Jianghu Training" made a gang boss

In the courtroom, wearing prisoner uniform No. 01 was Yin Jian, the leader of this group. During the trial, Yin Jian looked around frequently and yawned repeatedly. When the presiding judge called his name to verify his identity, he didn't even want to stand up unless prompted by the bailiff, continuing to show off his demeanor as the "big boss."

Yin Jian, nicknamed "Northeasterner," was born in October 1969 in Changning, Hengyang City, Hunan Province. He was the chairman of Hengyang Heaven and Earth Catering Culture Entertainment Co., Ltd.

Fifteen years ago, Yin Jian was an unknown small-time thug. In 1996, Yin Jian was arrested by the Changning Public Security Bureau for the crimes of illegal detention and extortion but was later released without prosecution. In 1997, Yin Jian began to move around in Hengyang City, opening clothing stores and restaurants. While running the restaurant, Yin Jian was bullied by some street thugs in Hengyang, which led him to conclude an "experience lesson": to survive in Hengyang, one must have a group of followers. Later, when Yin Jian sent his followers to collect protection fees from a hotel, they were arrested and detained by the police, leading him to another "experience lesson": besides having followers, one must also maintain good relations with government departments so that issues involving subordinates can be resolved through connections.

After going through such "jianghu training," Yin Jian gradually established his reputation in the underworld of Hengyang City.

In 2002, Yin Jian became a shareholder in a karaoke entertainment club operated by Yuan, a fellow townsman from Changning. Initially, the venue mainly focused on dance performances and standard karaoke entertainment, while Yin Jian only handled general external affairs. Yin Jian saw a business opportunity here and used various means to force Yuan out. The other shareholders, fearing Yin Jian, had no choice but to withdraw from the company successively. In May 2004, Yin Jian started operating "Heaven and Earth" exclusively.

In January 2005, after Ma, one of Yin Jian's underlings, was injured by a group of people who came to disrupt the venue,

due to insufficient manpower, Yin Jian felt that his own strength was still not enough. Without a strong force, it would not only affect the business but also make him lose face. Therefore, Yin Jian began to continuously recruit idle social individuals to guard the premises. By the end of 2005, Liu Fei, who was well-known in the underworld of Hengyang, along with his "little brothers" Chen Hongjun and others, joined Yin Jian. From then on, the organized crime group headed by Yin Jian was officially formed.

"Internal Protection" and "External Protection" Establish Authority

"In the 'underworld,' you don't necessarily need people to love you, but you must make them fear you!" This is a phrase that Yin Jian often says.

To turn this phrase into reality, Yin Jian implemented hierarchical management within the "Heaven and Earth" organization: the only leader within the organization was Yin Jian himself, with all others being his "little brothers," directly responsible to him.

Yin Jian controlled all major decisions and economic lifelines of the entire "Heaven and Earth," clarified the responsibilities of his subordinates, established organizational rules and discipline, covered all expenses for criminal activities, and provided welfare benefits to the members of the organization.

Yin Jian had numerous "little brothers," mainly divided into "internal protection" and "external protection." The "internal protection" consisted of the internal management and security personnel of the "Heaven and Earth" company, primarily responsible for accumulating wealth for the organization; the "external protection" were the so-called "firefighters," i.e., "little brothers," whose main task was to protect the safety of "Heaven and Earth," engage in violent conflicts outside, and establish the dominance of the gang.

According to Yin Jian's regulations, the "little brothers" must respect Yin Jian as their "elder brother" and serve him devotedly. For example, when Yin Jian was "high," the "little brothers" should proactively wipe his sweat; when Yin Jian smoked, the "little brothers" should light his cigarettes; when Yin Jian got into the car, the "little brothers" should open the door for him; when walking, Yin Jian must walk first, and the "little brothers" must follow behind. The "little brothers" must absolutely obey Yin Jian's commands and follow his instructions in all actions. If they do not obey, they will either be punished or expelled from the organization.

On the other hand, "Elder Brother" Yin Jian genuinely protected the safety of his "little brothers." Regardless of whether it was before or after an incident, as long as the "little brothers" promptly reported the situation to "Elder Brother," Yin Jian would step in to help resolve the issue. In 2007, Liu lent a handgun to Zhou, who shot and injured Wang. Afterward, Liu reported the matter to Yin Jian, who spent over 100,000 yuan to smooth things over, resulting in Liu receiving a lighter punishment.

One day in 2006, Li Bing's friend "Monkey" had an argument with Wang, the supervisor of the KTV department at Crystal Square in Hengyang, and sought help from gang member Chen Hongjun. Chen Hongjun immediately notified ten gang members to rush to the Crystal Square KTV room where they got into an argument with the security guards. Chen Hongjun and his group did not win. Later, when Chen Hongjun and others prepared to gather more people to cause trouble, the security guards at Crystal Square were extremely afraid upon learning that the opposing side was from "Heaven and Earth." The security captain Ren urgently sought mediation from intermediaries and went to apologize to Yin Jian and his group in a private room at "Heaven and Earth" that night. The next day, the security guards at Crystal Square held a banquet at a high-end restaurant to apologize and paid 3,000 yuan in cash to Chen Hongjun.

Even more astonishingly, on October 9, 2009, when Hengyang police deployed forces to seal off "Heaven and Earth," they could not mobilize a single truck in Hengyang city to collect evidence and seize property, nor could they find a locksmith, because they were afraid of Yin Jian and dared not come forward.

During the investigation of the case, many victims also refused to cooperate with the investigation out of fear of retaliation since the name "Northeasterner" instilled fear in the hearts of the people of Hengyang.

Model Team Openly Engages in Prostitution

To ensure the prosperity of "Heaven and Earth," Yin Jian specially consulted the head of the Beijing "Heaven and Earth." According to financial records, just one meal cost Yin Jian 360,000 yuan.

After learning from Beijing and Shenzhen, Yin Jian immediately put his knowledge into practice. The renovated "Heaven and Earth" not only provided facilities such as turntables for drug users and offered tools like scrapers and straws but also set up a model team openly engaging in prostitution.

Upon entering the company, models had 10 days' salary deducted as a deposit. They were assigned unified employee numbers, wore uniforms, stayed in centralized accommodation, and managed collectively. They were required to ask for leave if leaving for more than 20 minutes and had to pay a 100-yuan deposit. Every evening at 7:30 PM, the models were called to work in the company, waiting in the rest area for customers. Photos of all models were displayed on TVs in private rooms for guests to choose from. According to seized financial records, from April 2005 to December 2007, there were 159 "model fee statistics tables" and 88 daily revenue reports showing that 74 models appeared 310 times.

Among the group, Duan Lijuan, aged 26, was the only one with a university degree and was also Yin Jian's mistress. It was understood that the two met at an entertainment venue in Shenzhen. To expand "Heaven and Earth," Yin Jian recruited her. And Duan Lijuan brought four "top courtesans" to become the pillars of "Heaven and Earth."

Drug trafficking and sex transactions at "Heaven and Earth" had become an undeniable reality. When law enforcement officers and prosecutors questioned about "Heaven and Earth," many gang members reflexively responded, "Heaven and Earth, Paradise on Earth."

Setting up secret lookouts to monitor the movements of the police chief

To protect his "business," Yin Jian lured Chen Xiaoping, then Deputy Director of the Shigu Branch of the Hengyang Public Security Bureau, and Captain He Hangguo of the Narcotics Division into corruption. He bribed each of them with 60,000 yuan and 17,500 yuan respectively, providing free access to "Heaven and Earth" VIP rooms for over 40 times and more than 10 times, with waived charges amounting to over 150,000 yuan.

It turned out that Yin Jian's expenditures were not wasted.

In April 2009, Xu Fakē was appointed as the Chief of the Hengyang Public Security Bureau. Soon after taking office, Xu learned about a place called "Heaven and Earth" in Hengyang, which was involved in organized crime, drugs, and prostitution, causing strong public dissatisfaction. Moreover, previous bureau chiefs had tried multiple times to crack down on the establishment but failed to eliminate this cancer.

After some undercover investigations, Xu was shocked to discover that selling drugs and accommodating women for prostitution were done openly and brazenly. Members of the organization even invited him to use drugs. At that moment, Xu resolved to take down "Heaven and Earth," using it as a breakthrough to purify Hengyang's public security environment.

On the evening of June 3, Chen Xiaoping, who knew the news in advance, informed Yin Jian that the Hengyang Public Security Bureau would investigate "Heaven and Earth," advising Yin Jian to prepare a show for others. On the morning of June 4, Chen Xiaoping personally led the raid on two specially arranged private rooms and 11 drug users. Afterwards, Yin Jian arranged for items like blankets to be delivered to those detained as part of the cooperation, and issued 800 yuan compensation to each person.

On June 6, hundreds of police officers suddenly surrounded "Heaven and Earth," only to find it had been cleared out. Thus, Xu realized that Yin Jian had "protection umbrellas" within the public security system.

Subsequently, Yin Jian set up secret lookouts at various intersections two kilometers away from "Heaven and Earth," extending even to the entrance of the Hengyang County Public Security Bureau.

On July 9, the second arrest attempt failed again.

Later, the Chief of the Hengyang County Public Security Bureau and the Chief of the Municipal Public Security Bureau, Xu Fakē, were followed. Yin Jian even bribed the person delivering boxed meals to the Public Security Bureau, asking him to report their movements.

On the afternoon of October 8, Xu adjusted his strategy, temporarily notifying the action personnel to gather in the suburbs, and only informing the target of the operation at 1 AM the next morning. This time, Yin Jian did not get the message. The police captured 132 suspects on the spot and seized large quantities of new drugs like ketamine and ecstasy, firearms, ammunition, etc., finally apprehending Yin Jian and other members of the mafia-like criminal group.

The indictment charged that the mafia-like criminal organization led by Yin Jian relied on "Heaven and Earth" as a base to carry out extensive illegal activities,涉嫌组织、领导、参加黑社会性质组织罪；故意杀人罪；组织卖淫罪、协助组织卖淫罪；嫖宿幼女罪；介绍卖淫罪；贩卖毒品罪；非法持有枪支罪；容留他人吸毒罪；故意伤害罪；敲诈勒索罪；寻衅滋事罪；非法拘禁罪；赌博罪；包庇黑社会性质组织罪；受贿罪、行贿罪等 15项罪名。

This criminal organization illegally amassed over 21 million yuan in profits, with Yin Jian personally extracting 19.65 million yuan. The criminal group committed 15 organized crimes, resulting in 1 death, 2 severely injured, and countless lightly injured victims; in 2008 alone, they trafficked nearly 30 kilograms of ketamine and accommodated hundreds of thousands of drug users, among whom one overdosed and died, and more than ten were induced to commit crimes due to drug addiction; a local police station recorded 32 major emergency calls related to "Heaven and Earth" between 2005 and 2009; the criminal organization possessed fourteen guns including Type-54, Type-64, imitation German Walther PPK, rifles, and homemade revolvers, with over a hundred bullets; they engaged in dozens of incidents of provoking troubles and extorted over 5 million yuan...

The above is only part of what the indictment accused them of committing. There are still quite a number of criminal acts that cannot be testified or proven because the parties feared Yin Jian's ruthlessness.

Precisely for this reason, the resolution of the "Heaven and Earth" case won the support and praise of the local population. At 7:30 AM on April 19, many citizens of Hengyang who heard about the trial spontaneously gathered at the gate of the Intermediate People's Court of Hengyang City. Ultimately, the entire criminal courtroom was packed.

Due to the complexity of the case and the large number of people involved, it is estimated that the court proceedings will last five to six days.
