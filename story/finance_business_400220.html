You can try opening a store online and do consignment sales without inventory pressure. How could I open an online store? Regardless! First, I went to the bank and activated online banking, after a series of procedures, I was finally able to buy things. So, I quickly bought a pair of shoes, the money was paid into the intermediary Alipay account, not directly into the seller's account! Only after I received the goods and confirmed payment would the money enter the seller's account! This is the most important link and one of the reasons why so many people choose to shop online, truly realizing the principle of cash on delivery. The process of waiting for the package was a kind of enjoyment, like waiting for a gift to arrive, feeling very excited, imagining what the items I bought would look like. Expecting! Since I bought from a local seller, the goods were delivered to my door in two days. With great passion, I opened the package, and before me was a pair of quality shoes at a good price, fitting perfectly when I tried them on, then I confirmed receipt and gave the seller a positive review. This was my first shopping experience, and having had the first, there would be a second, third... until I couldn't stop.

With the pleasant shopping experience last time, I became more determined to open my own online store. At that time, I didn't choose consignment because I knew that credibility was very important in online stores. Because I couldn't see the physical items, I worried about the quality of the goods I sold to customers, fearing they wouldn't be satisfied. So, I chose to bear the inventory pressure myself. The store was opened, giving me a great sense of accomplishment. I immediately sent messages to my friends asking them to support me, but being a novice back then, I knew nothing, didn't know to attend classes on Taobao University, just thought that once the store was opened, business would come automatically, waiting for manna from heaven! Eventually, the urban management officers kept finding fault with us, so we had to give up the street stall business.
