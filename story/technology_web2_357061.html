Adhere to the "four starting points" and identify existing problems

by tanfuwo0r on 2010-05-31 08:26:05

Our News Agency reports that during the phase of analysis and self-examination in the campaign for studying the scientific development concept, the Yandu health system closely links with the actual situation of the health system. It focuses on four aspects: reality, detail, accuracy, and depth to find out the prominent problems existing in the system.

Firstly, focusing on "reality", it collects opinions face-to-face. The system tightly combines with the functions and business work of this unit, targeting the main and prominent issues that need to be solved. It conducts a "prominent issue face-to-face" research activity, directly collecting suggestions from service objects about this unit, frankly finding out the specific problems existing in this system and unit.

Secondly, focusing on "detail", it conducts extensive and in-depth research. Each unit of the system mainly revolves around changing functions, simplifying procedures, and improving service levels. They adopt methods such as holding seminars, visiting the masses, conducting questionnaire surveys, opening hotlines, online surveys, media interaction, and setting up suggestion boxes and email boxes to extensively and deeply conduct research, carefully finding every specific problem affecting the development of this system and unit. At the same time, for the issues reflected by the people during the research, they do not overlook them due to their smallness or complexity but actively and seriously sort them out, categorize them, and use them as important content for formulating rectification plans, carefully implementing them.

Thirdly, focusing on "accuracy", it fully discusses. Within the entire system, especially among the leadership and party cadre leaders, they adopt methods such as self-assessment and mutual evaluation. They seriously listen to the suggestions and opinions of the masses, especially service objects, about issues affecting the work of the health system, accurately finding the poor service attitude of individuals in some units strongly reflected by the people. The District Health Bureau also separately held seminars with the responsible persons of town (center, street) health centers and district direct health units such as City Third Hospital, Disease Control Center, Health Supervision Office, Women's Health Care Office, Health School, Mental Prevention Hospital, etc., really finding out the prominent issues affecting the development of the medical and health cause in the whole district, laying a good foundation for clarifying the development思路of the health cause in the whole district.

Fourthly, focusing on "depth", it comprehensively analyzes. Based on the serious study of required reading materials by the majority of party members in the system, it deeply analyzes the causes of the problems found during the in-depth research, comprehensively analyzes them, deepens the rational understanding of typical cases, so that the majority of party cadres receive profound education. At the same time, closely surrounding the contents of health law enforcement, supervision activities, and medical-patient disputes, starting from the prominent specific problems, seeing people, events, and thoughts, it really achieves "not letting go if the understanding is not deep, not letting go if the problems are not found, and not letting go if the reasons are not found deep."
