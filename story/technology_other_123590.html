Shijiazhuang Oriental Science and Technology Secondary Professional School

by xie11 on 2009-02-09 16:46:43

The school adopts a combination of work and study, a frugal schooling model, first-class training facilities in the province, a simple, fast, and accurate teaching model, fully enclosed quasi-military student management, perfect employment placement, and the lowest tuition fee in the province.

Shijiazhuang Oriental Science and Technology Secondary Vocational School is a full-time comprehensive secondary vocational school approved by the Shijiazhuang Municipal Education Bureau and filed with the Hebei Provincial Department of Education. The school is located at No. 29 Shihe Road South, the intersection of Xinhua Road and West Second Ring Road, Shijiazhuang City, Hebei Province. It has superior geographical conditions, convenient transportation, and a beautiful environment.

Our school always adheres to the development philosophy of "liberating thinking, being pragmatic, and innovative, and diligent in seeking change." We continuously carry out teaching and management reforms, firmly establish the "market competition" awareness, take the market as the orientation, take enterprise demand as the standard, explore new ideas and methods for theoretical and practical teaching, accumulate rich teaching and management experience, and students have received general praise from employers after employment.

1. With enterprise standards as guidance, we have equipped first-class teaching facilities in the province.

Adequate and advanced teaching experimental equipment is the foundation and guarantee for doing a good job in vocational education. Our school has cumulatively invested more than 10 million yuan, built a numerical control (NC) training workshop equipped with more than 50 NC lathes, NC milling machines, conventional lathes, conventional milling machines, etc., and also built communication electronics laboratories, music rooms, electronic reading rooms, grinding rooms, body shape rooms, and new teaching buildings and other supporting facilities.

2. With employment positions as guidance, we have created an enterprise-style teaching model.

We have established a teaching model mainly based on practice. In the teaching process, all students are managed as enterprise employees. Through competitive job postings, students take on roles such as team leaders, line managers, and employees in their "work." They are evaluated through regular assessments and exams, and outstanding team leaders and technical experts are selected among the students. This effectively promotes students' enthusiasm and initiative for learning.

Each day's "work" situation is monitored by quality indicators, which are statistically analyzed monthly. Wages are calculated based on the scores obtained each month. The wage composition includes: workload + performance evaluation + work discipline + appearance + safe operation + bonuses, etc. This effectively motivates students' initiative for learning and competitive awareness, enabling them to master knowledge and skills faster and better.

Through enterprise-style teaching reform, students can gradually experience and adapt to enterprise management models during their schooling, meet the needs of employment positions, initially reach enterprise employment standards, and the goal of enterprise-style teaching is to gradually surpass enterprise employment standards.

3. With enterprise demands as guidance, we have created a fully enclosed quasi-military student management system and formulated a daily norm for students and a practical manual for vocational quality training.

Comprehensively learning the enterprise employee management model, we cultivate qualified talents that enterprises welcome. Enterprises not only need skilled workers but also emphasize employees' overall qualities, such as teamwork spirit, compliance, execution ability, and competitive awareness. The cultivation and improvement of overall qualities require efforts and cooperation from all aspects of the school, and overall quality education runs through the entire learning process of students. To enhance our students' overall qualities, our school focuses on two distinctive management features:

Distinctive Management Feature One: Strict Quasi-Military Management

Our school adheres to the educational concept of "first becoming a person, then becoming talented," and hires retired military officers dedicated to quasi-military management of students. Starting from the daily life system, wearing uniforms, behavior, manners, tidying up the quarters, we let students develop good living habits, cultivate excellent organizational discipline, and quickly become disciplined talents with good military demeanor.

Distinctive Management Feature Two: Vocational Quality Education Management

During the learning process, we offer vocational quality courses, divided into theoretical lectures and practical training. These two aspects complement each other and improve each other. The theoretical lecture mainly focuses on practical combat, guiding the transformation of students' thoughts, while practical training consolidates the teaching results through students' "personal experiences," thus turning these vocational quality requirements into students' voluntary behaviors.

Vocational quality expansion training: Our school signs agreements with enterprises and sends our students in batches to enterprise positions under the accompaniment of school leaders and teachers. After a period of quasi-employee life, students can understand society and enterprises before actual internship employment, enter the formal enterprise employee state in advance, and lay a good foundation for adapting to employment positions.

4. With employment as guidance, we have successfully completed the employment placement work.

With the continuous improvement of our students' qualities and employment channels, we have widely established student internship and employment bases in the Pearl River Delta region, Beijing-Tianjin-Hebei-Shanxi area, and Jiaozhou Peninsula.

The employment direction of our CNC major is mainly in the automobile parts manufacturing industry. All student internship units are top 500 mechanical manufacturing enterprises in the world. The professional alignment rate of student internships and employment reaches 90%, and the student employment satisfaction rate exceeds 95%.

5. Thoughtful后勤service, uniquely creating a "free meal system" for students in the province.

All students of our school implement the "free meal system" for three meals. On one hand, it reduces the burden on students' parents, and on the other hand, the school formulates scientific menus according to the students' physical development needs, fully ensuring the nutritional needs for students' physical development and adapting to intensive learning, effectively avoiding adverse consequences caused by dining outside the school.

The school's student dormitory building, canteen, supermarket, phone booth, bathhouse, and other service facilities are complete. The logistics management department always adheres to the principle of "service," striving to make the service proactive, thoughtful, intimate, and convenient.

6. Only miscellaneous fees are charged. The source of three years' tuition and accommodation fees.

Our school is a three-year secondary vocational school. With a strong sense of social responsibility originating from society and giving back to society, relying on powerful educational capabilities and forward-thinking educational concepts, we maximize cost savings for students' parents. Students who come to our school only need to pay three years of miscellaneous fees, and the three years of tuition and accommodation fees are paid through a combination of work and study, i.e., vocational quality training. The school has also specially established a "Hongzhi Class," fully or partially exempting all miscellaneous fees for academically excellent and highly competent students from humble backgrounds. Our students enjoy a national scholarship of 1500 yuan per year provided by the government.

In the process of running the school, we continuously summarize experiences and lessons learned, innovatively distilling our school culture.

The refinement of "school culture" outlines the grand blueprint and fighting direction for our school. The practice process of school culture is also the process of shaping our brand and further enhancing our influence.
