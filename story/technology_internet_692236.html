1. Low cost: the total annual tuition, accommodation fees, etc., amount to approximately 36,250 yuan.

- Tuition is quite cheap, at 4.4 million KRW/year, equivalent to about 26,000 RMB/year.

- Accommodation fees are also very low, at 1.1264 million KRW/year, equivalent to about 6,700 RMB/year.

- Meal expenses are 0.264 million KRW/semester (one meal per day), equivalent to about 1,550 RMB/semester.

- Enrollment fee is 0.1 million KRW, insurance fee is 0.24 million KRW/year, totaling approximately 2,000 RMB.

2. Simple application materials: only a bank deposit certificate of 30,000 RMB with a three-month term is required;

- Admission requirements: diploma from a vocational high school, regular high school, or technical secondary school, with final academic qualifications obtained within the last two years.

3. The university has two long holidays each year, lasting around four months, during which students have free time to manage on their own.

4. One month after enrollment, assistance will be provided to arrange part-time work-study opportunities, where students can work at designated factories after classes. At Konkuk University, the monthly salary is approximately 1.4 million KRW.
