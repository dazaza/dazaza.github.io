When "bullet" has become a trend

by coors01 on 2011-10-15 13:19:43

In today's urban areas, fashion trends and popular elements change every day. Are you left behind in the trend? According to a survey, 65% of young people choose to meet at bars, KTVs, or large shake bars. As an important force leading the trend, their choices for leisure living have been specified as these places. Gatherings are meant to bring everyone together for some fun and excitement, which often involves drinking to liven up the atmosphere. However, excessive drinking can easily lead to farces during gatherings. During the National Day holiday, our old classmates rarely had time to gather, but some female classmates couldn't handle alcohol well, and some even had special conditions. We couldn't force them to drink, so the male classmates took on the role of being the main force to liven up the atmosphere and enjoy the drinks. But one buddy couldn't hold his liquor, and after a few bottles of beer, he started acting out. Seeing this, the server quickly approached us: "Sir, Miss, we all come out for a gathering to have fun, but getting drunk is neither safe nor healthy. The alcohol content in the drinks you ordered is too high. Why don't we switch to a different type of beer?" We were not happy upon hearing this. Who likes to be told they're not drinking enough? Besides, isn't it normal to get a little tipsy when drinking? One friend then retorted: "All beers taste the same anyway. We've been drinking for several years. Do you look down on us because we can't hold our liquor?" Seeing the situation, the front desk manager immediately arrived and presented us with five bottles of a beer called "Silver Bullet" to help liven up the atmosphere, claiming it was the most trendy beer right now. At first, we didn't think much of it, but after opening and taking a few sips, we found that this beer was indeed quite good. Even our female colleagues tried it and said it was acceptable. We soon noticed that the young people at nearby tables were also drinking this beer, so we asked the server to bring more of it. The server who brought the beer was the same one from earlier, who smiled at us and said: "I wanted to recommend it earlier, but you wouldn't listen." We responded: "You should have recommended it when we first ordered." The "Silver Bullet" beer has a crystal-clear quality and a refreshing taste, making you feel as if you've heard celestial music after drinking it, enlightening your mind. We sang songs for the entire afternoon without being affected by the alcohol. It seems that the "Silver Bullet" beer has become a trend. Looks like we really are "out"! Are you keeping up with the latest trends? When "Bullet" has become a trend, where is your "Silver" (person)? "Silver Bullet" beer keeps you shining at the forefront of trends!
