Top 10 IT Products of 07: Windows XP Edges Out Vista - CSDN News

by cacard on 2007-12-07 12:12:17

December 7 news, according to foreign media reports, famous channel website CRN recently selected the top 10 IT products of 2007. As a result, the Windows XP system, which has been on the market for 6 years, edged out Vista to make the list.

In addition, the listed products also include AMD Quad-Core Opteron processors, HP desktops, Toshiba laptops, and RIM BlackBerry smartphones. The following are the top 10 IT products of 2007 as selected by CRN:

1. SonicWall TZ 180 hardware firewall (security category)

2. VMWare Infrastructure 3 (virtualization software)

3. Microsoft Office Communications Server 2007 (VoIP product)

4. Extricom EXWO-404 wireless LAN switch (wireless product)

5. Microsoft Windows XP (operating system)

6. Tableau v3.0 data analysis tool (application)

7. HP Compaq dc7800 desktop (desktop)

8. Toshiba Portege R400 laptop (laptop)

9. AMD Quad-Core Opteron processor (processor)

10. RIM BlackBerry Curve 8310 (smartphone)
