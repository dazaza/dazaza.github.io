Jeju Island Travel Guide: Food

by anonymous on 2012-03-02 10:04:09

One of our travel purposes is to enjoy the scenery, and the other is to savor delicious food. Of course, when visiting Jeju, one should not miss out on a culinary journey filled with fresh seafood and local specialties.

The seafood in Jeju Island is not only fresh and tasty but also rich in nutrients that are beneficial for health. Famous seafood dishes in Jeju include sashimi, abalone porridge, grilled tilefish, and seafood hot pot. The variety of agricultural products in Jeju also results in unique and exquisite flavors. Grilled pork made from Jeju black pig meat is very popular, along with five-flavor soda, Hallasan soju, buckwheat noodles, and more. These delicacies can only be enjoyed in Jeju. Moreover, the warm hospitality of Jeju people enhances the flavor of the meals, making them even more fragrant and richer.

Introduction to Entertainment in Jeju Island, South Korea

Beautiful Jeju Island is a kingdom of leisure and sports. There, the abundant entertainment activities free modern people from their busy work and allow them to fully rest while getting close to nature. Tourists who love the sea can go to the Chinese Seawater Bathing Beach for fun or take a sightseeing boat tour in Seogwipo to explore underwater diving and experience the mystery of the underwater world. Alternatively, you could try windsurfing to find excitement and pleasure in your battle with the sea. If none of these interest you, you can try your luck at the casinos of Jeju Shinsegae Hotel, Hyatt Hotel, and Jeju KAL Hotel; relax at the Jungmun Golf Club; or visit the Seogwipo racecourse, which is the only place in the world where you can watch dwarf horse races. It is surrounded by large parking lots, local restaurants, children's horse-riding areas, artificial ponds, and other facilities, gradually becoming a family leisure and entertainment center.

Recommendations for Entertainment in Jeju Island, South Korea:

Jeju KAL Hotel Casino

The casino is located in the Jeju KAL Hotel, which is a special first-class tourist hotel with 2 underground floors, 18 above-ground floors, and 282 guest rooms, making it an international hotel. The Jeju KAL Hotel Casino offers table games such as BLACKJACK, ROULETTE, BACCARAT, BIG WHEEL, TAI-SAI, and is exclusively for foreigners. With an area of 550 pyeong, it is the largest casino on Jeju Island, featuring the highest level of facilities on the island.

Transportation:

It takes about 10 minutes by taxi from Jeju Airport.

Address: 1691-9 Eodo-dong, Jeju-si, Jeju-do

Shinsegae Hotel Casino in Jeju Island, South Korea

The casino is located in the Jeju Shilla Hotel, which is a top-grade hotel with 429 guest rooms, 6 restaurants and bars, 7 banquet halls, indoor and outdoor swimming pools, male and female saunas, fitness centers, bowling alleys, billiard rooms, tennis courts, game plazas, travel agencies, car rental stations, photo studios, souvenir shops, duty-free stores, and more. The casino has a traditional Western style with an area of 260 pyeong, offering various games such as Roulette, Blackjack, Baccarat, and Slot Machines. You can enjoy yourself in the unique European-style atmosphere. The casino is exclusively for foreigners.

Transportation:

Take the direct airport shuttle bus between the airport and the hotel (every 15 minutes, approximately 50 minutes).

Address: 3039-3 Seodat-dong, Seogwipo-si, Jeju-do

Hyatt Hotel Casino in Jeju Island, South Korea

What Hyatt Hotel is most proud of is its casino, which features the latest Las Vegas-style facilities. The casino is exclusively for foreigners and offers various games such as ROULETTE, BLACKJACK, BACCARAT, SLOT MACHINE, and more.

Transportation:

Take the airport bus (route 600/every 15 minutes) or taxi from Jeju International Airport. The casino is only a 40-minute drive from Jeju City and the airport, and a 20-minute drive from Seogwipo City.

Address: 3039-1 Seodat-dong, Seogwipo-si, Jeju-do

Chinese Golf Club in Jeju Island, South Korea

The Chinese Golf Club is divided into the Hallasan route and the coast route. The Hallasan route is the external route where you can enjoy the beautiful scenery of Hallasan Mountain while playing golf. The coast route is the internal route, where the holes are laid out along the Chinese coast, allowing you to enjoy the sea view while playing golf, which is quite unique. Here, you can also visit the botanical garden, Royal Marin Park, Chinese seaside bathing beach, and Cheonjiyeon Waterfall within the resort.

Transportation:

Take the airport bus from Jeju International Airport to the entrance of the golf club (takes 45 minutes), then enter the Chinese Resort to find it.

Address: Inside the Chinese Resort, Seodat-dong, Seogwipo-si, Jeju-do
