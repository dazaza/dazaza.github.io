Men take children, a question of eternal words. People often say that foolish women and benevolent women have been explained very clearly today. It's easy to be foolish. A man's infatuation with love and his gentle emotions towards feelings. A woman can hate fewer men, while a man can love several women at the same time. However, few people realize that a man only loves one woman!

A woman's purest love only happens once. When this great love comes, she will give everything without hesitation. Once this love happens, it won't happen again. That love might seem too light, but it also hurts too deeply.

Therefore, after that, a man's love becomes numb. Men may love other girls in the future, but that kind of love is no longer pure. It includes desire, sympathy, and care...

All women's hearts are made of fire, but the most venomous is the heart of a woman. So the ones who get hurt are women, and the ones who get hurt the deepest are also women. A woman's love can happen many times, but a man's love only happens once. When a man meets the girl he loves the most, he gives her all his love. But how many young girls know to cherish it?

Thus, men cry, men shed tears, tears of sorrow and disappointment. Gradually, men come to bury their only love, sealing it deep in the darkest abyss of their hearts.

When someone stirs up these memories, they will crazily jump into this abyss, trying to find traces of that great love, even just a tiny bit. But the result is the same as before - nothing gained. They are left alone in the dark corner, smoking cigarettes in solitude, shedding tears in loneliness.

When women complain about men being fickle, do they ever think about the things men cherish most being cruelly destroyed by you? When men shyly say "I love you," do you cherish it? When men cry for you, do you wipe away their tears? When men beg you not to leave, do you turn back? Everyone says men are fickle, but women don't know that men's fickleness stems from their obsession. Extreme sadness leads to obsession, extreme obsession leads to pain, extreme pain leads to death. After that love turns to ashes, men become indifferent. They won't give their best for anyone anymore. Without motivation, without passion, they no longer believe in true love. They approach love with cynicism. Some things only happen once in a lifetime.

When a man truly loves someone, when he has given everything, tried his best to hold on, attempted to give her everything, even his life, but receives indifference in return, then in those lonely waits, his blood and sweat drip away drop by drop until he loses all hope. When belief in love is lost, when emotions lose their loyal haven, for men, love becomes meaningless! When a woman's heart gets cold, it's hard to warm it up again because after loving someone, she loses the ability to love again.

The person a man truly loves is only one person, and men know this in their hearts. If it's not you, can you feel it? Will you cherish it?
