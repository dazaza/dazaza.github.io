I. Tongji University

1. Academician

Li Guohao: Academician of the Chinese Academy of Sciences and the Chinese Academy of Engineering, a famous expert in bridge structures, structural mechanics, honorary president of Tongji University, honorary chairman of the China Civil Engineering Society, and honorary chairman of the Chinese Association of Engineering Societies.

Sun Jun: Academician of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, a renowned expert in underground construction structures and geotechnical mechanics, convener of the Degree Evaluation Group for Civil Construction, Water Conservancy, and Surveying under the State Council's Academic Degrees Committee, evaluator for the National Natural Science Fund, and vice chairman of the China Civil Engineering Society.

Xiang Haifan: Academician of the Chinese Academy of Engineering, a distinguished expert in bridges and structural engineering, pioneer in wind resistance research for large-span bridges in China, and one of the main academic leaders in the field of wind engineering in China. Over the past decade, he has undertaken more than ten national climbing plan projects; his work on the "Nanpu Bridge Project in Shanghai" received the First Prize for National Scientific and Technological Progress. He currently serves as Vice President of the International Association for Bridge and Structural Engineering (IABSE).

Fan Lichu: Academician of the Chinese Academy of Engineering, a renowned expert in bridges and structural engineering, current Chairman of the Chinese National Group of the International Association for Bridge and Structural Engineering (IABSE), standing director of the China Civil Engineering Society, and chairman of the Bridge and Structural Engineering Branch of the China Civil Engineering Society.

Lu Yaoru: Elected as an academician of the Chinese Academy of Engineering in 1997, he is a famous geologist. Currently a professor in the Department of Underground Architecture and Engineering at the College of Civil Engineering, Tongji University. He has been engaged in karst geological scientific research and engineering practice for a long time, participating in and guiding a series of surveys and studies related to water conservancy, hydropower, railways, mines, and urban engineering. Known as "Karst Lu" both domestically and internationally due to his achievements in karst research, he was awarded the National Science Conference Award in 1998, the second prize for geological science and technology, and the second prize for excellent scientific and technological books nationwide. In 1999, he received the Sixth Li Siguang Geological Science Honor Award.

II. Tsinghua University

1. Academician

Long Yuchiu: Academician of the Chinese Academy of Engineering, a famous civil engineer and structural mechanics expert, chairman of the Teaching Steering Committee for Mechanical Courses in Engineering Colleges under the Ministry of Education, and editor-in-chief of the Journal of Engineering Mechanics published by the Chinese Society of Theoretical and Applied Mechanics. His research focuses on the finite element method, energy principles, and structural analysis. He has achieved five pioneering scientific research results: generalized conforming elements, domain mixed elements, new shell elements, partitioned energy principles, and optional parameter energy principles. He participated in formulating the "Design Code for Thin Shell Structures."

Chen Zhaoyuan: Academician of the Chinese Academy of Engineering, a famous structural engineer, concurrently serving as standing director of the China Civil Engineering Society and chairman of the Education Work Committee, deputy chairman of the Protective Engineering Sub-discipline Society, and editor-in-chief of the Journal of Civil Engineering. He has been engaged in research on protective engineering design theory, blast-resistant structures, reinforced concrete structures, and evaluation of the actual working performance of buildings. He has achieved remarkable results in protective engineering and high-strength concrete structure research. He leads key national scientific research projects in the field of high-strength, high-performance concrete and is responsible for editing and publishing the Design and Construction Code for High-Strength Concrete Structures.

2. National Key Discipline

Structural Engineering

III. Southeast University

1. Academician

Lü Zhitao: Academician of the Chinese Academy of Engineering, a famous structural engineering expert. He is currently a member of the board of directors of the China Civil Engineering Society, deputy chairman of the national prestressed concrete society, and the academic leader of the prestressed discipline in China. He has made outstanding contributions to the development of concrete and prestressed concrete structural science and technology in China. He improved the calculation theory and design methods of concrete structures, proposed two types of oblique crack theories, shear design methods, and calculation formulas for bidirectional eccentric tension, developed the system, calculation theory, and design methods of prestressed concrete structures, and pioneered new fields of application for prestress technology.

2. National Key Disciplines

Structural Engineering

Transportation Planning and Management

IV. Harbin Institute of Technology

1. Academician

Wang Guangyuan: Academician of the Chinese Academy of Engineering, a famous seismic engineering expert, and member of the International Association for Structural Safety and Reliability, former vice chairman of the Chinese Society of Theoretical and Applied Mechanics.

Xie Lilie: Academician of the Chinese Academy of Engineering, a famous earthquake and seismic engineering expert, currently honorary director of the Institute of Engineering Mechanics of the China Earthquake Administration, deputy director of the Science and Technology Committee of the China Earthquake Administration, vice chairman of the China Earthquake Society, editor-in-chief of the editorial boards of the Journal of Natural Disasters and Earthquake Engineering and Engineering Vibration, chairman of the International Strong Motion Seismology Association, executive director of the International Association of Seismology and Physics of the Earth's Interior, member of the editorial board of the International Journal of Earthquake Engineering, and executive director of the International Association of Earthquake Engineering.

Shen Shizhao: Academician of the Chinese Academy of Engineering, a famous space structure expert, and vice chairman of the Chinese Architectural Society. He has been engaged in research and teaching in the field of structural engineering for a long time. Since the 1980s, he has been committed to opening up the emerging discipline of large-span space structures. He has achieved important results in frontier research such as "Cable Structure System and Its Analytical Theory", "Nonlinear Stability of Reticulated Shell Structures", and "Wind Vibration Response of Large-Span Flexible Roofs". His research has made important contributions to the development of large-span space structures in China. His research results have won the Second Prize for National Scientific and Technological Progress and the First Prize for Scientific and Technological Progress of the Ministry of Construction.

Ou Jinping: Academician of the Chinese Academy of Engineering (elected at the age of 44 in 2003)

2. National Key Disciplines

Structural Engineering

Municipal Engineering

V. Zhejiang University

1. Academician

Dong Shilin: Academician of the Chinese Academy of Engineering, a famous space structure expert. He systematically established the calculation theory, methods, and charts of the quasi-laminated plate method for various truss structures in large-span space structures, which were included in the "Design and Construction Regulations for Truss Structures" in China. He created a new method for calculating honeycomb triangular pyramid trusses - the lower chord internal force method; for the first time, he proposed the quasi-triangular layer calculation theory method for combined reticulated shell structures; and internationally and domestically, he established the theoretical basis, calculation formulas, and multi-layer large-span building combined truss "floor within a floor" complete set of design and construction application technologies.

2. National Key Discipline

Geotechnical Engineering

VI. Tianjin University

1. Academician

Xie Shileng: Academician of the Chinese Academy of Engineering, who has been engaged in port and coastal engineering research and design for a long time, winning three national awards for scientific and technological progress, including second and third prizes.

2. National Key Discipline

Port, Coastal, and Offshore Engineering
