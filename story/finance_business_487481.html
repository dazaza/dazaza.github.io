A note of dreams

by krdxiaoyand on 2011-09-09 23:38:18

(Compulsory compilation: Absolute Love in the Mortal World), Classic Insults

A dream note, how much revelry? Plain moon and red maple leaves, shadows dance with melancholy. Beauty's tears, breaking hearts. The bright Milky Way, within reach yet so far. Regret lingers, affection flows endlessly. She is emaciated, he weeps. A thousand autumns of parting, a magpie bridge reunion. Waning moon and falling flowers, forever wishing to be together. Alas, yearning gently ripples, only for my hair to be prematurely grayed by starlight frost. Pluck a bright moon to cleanse the dust, carry a cold heart to drift. Count the years, bow your head and accept fate. A moment of cowardice, a moment of melancholy, aging my lonely heart. Lonely figure intertwined with weary emotions, on a desolate evening, facing bleak cold winds, wandering, drifting.

Carrying sorrowful memories, bearing heartaches. In the cold night, repeatedly enduring a monotonous life accompanied only by solitary lamplight and cold moonlight. I once thought that by going far away, I could forget wounds not yet healed. I stopped my steps, paused my breath, but couldn't help but pause my thoughts. Without your moon to admire, without your tea to savor, without writing words about you, it would be such a sorrowful thing. So, I borrow the soft hazy moonlight, savoring tea that has yet to develop its fragrance, ceaselessly writing words thinking of you. One word: miss. Two words: really miss. Three words: extremely miss. Then an infinite amount of longing spreads recklessly in the night sky, long and heartfelt. From then on, I have become accustomed to the outline of the moon, the shape of the night. I don’t care about the differences in space and time because all my time and space are occupied by you.

Perhaps distance slows down the anticipation of a feeling, but I believe time will make up for my lateness. Those lost yearnings in the flow of years were not because I didn’t send them, but because the moon didn’t deliver them for me on rainy nights. Therefore, moonlit nights are always so crystal clear, as I miss you even more, making up for the blessings and admiration the moon didn't carry. I believe that in my night sky, there will be a moon that you can understand. Because only you would tirelessly enter my heart during my melancholic nights.

I only like the dim half-moon; such light is the most tranquil. All the light you emit belongs to me, not to others. I am very concerned about the full moon, as more people would share you. I am so selfish, wanting all your pure light, because I have deeply fallen in love with you. You are like a pure tear about to fall. Vaguely, you give me a faint longing, such ancient tenderness, deep and unforgettable, soothing the wounds carved into past memories. In the serene moonlight, I cherish your crystalline tears, quietly entering my dreams, dreaming of you again. The moon is so enchanting, tears so sentimental, and I am so passionate. Let me feel that all this is a gift from heaven, the moon is, and you even more so. Therefore, I treasure it even more. Even if the end result is just a prelude, it still has a conclusion, a beautiful memory worth collecting and loving. Wind erodes appearances, rain harms memories, yet I obtain the sadness that has already passed. You, the warmth in my palm, me, your forgotten presence upon turning around. Perhaps this is why beauties throughout history have been remembered, and gentlemen recall their deep sentiments! We only cherish after losing, making everything precious. Neither of us regrets, because you gave wings to my dreams.

I believe in the ancient and beautiful legend that everyone has a star guarding them. It may currently be hidden in some corner, but one day it will shine brightly for itself, brilliantly illuminating the way forward without any complaints or regrets, only inevitability. Now, my night sky will not lack your moon. The shadow curled up in the corner will be accompanied by a few lamps, even if thin, emaciated, and wounded, I will not be lonely or melancholy.

Year after year on Qixi Festival, what changes are only time and space, what doesn't change is the never-fading yearning, and a heart waiting only for the half-moon filled with loneliness.
