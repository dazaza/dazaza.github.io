Girl ran away from home and spent three consecutive days in an Internet cafe; her legs were frozen and rotting, and doctors considered amputation.

by mlwang on 2011-12-15 10:45:59

After staying in the internet cafe for three days and three nights, not only were her feet severely swollen and unable to walk, but they also emitted a foul odor. Eventually, some kind-hearted netizens sent her to the hospital... "Is there anything wrong with my feet?" On the morning of December 12th, after the doctor finished examining her, she kept asking. It was learned that the 21-year-old girl's name is An Hongfeng, and she is from Dai County, Shanxi Province. The doctor said that because she wore tight pants, sitting for a long time caused poor blood circulation, and due to the cold weather, she developed chilblains. On the same day, her sister in Shanxi heard about this and told the reporter that her younger sister An Hongfeng came to Qingdao in February this year, but lost contact a month later. "You must help me keep an eye on her, don't let her run away again. We will go to Qingdao right now."

After spending three days in the internet cafe, she developed chilblains.

"There was a girl who stayed in the internet cafe for three days, her feet swelled up like radishes, and she couldn't walk anymore..." On the morning of December 12th, citizen Mr. Li called our newspaper hotline and said that the girl was receiving treatment at the No. 401 Hospital.

The reporter immediately went to the hospital and saw the girl receiving intravenous fluids without any family members accompanying her. Her lower body was covered with a quilt, and her upper body was only wearing a thin coat. When the doctor lifted the quilt for examination, the reporter saw that her feet were slightly purple, severely swollen, and some places had even cracked open. A foul smell could be smelled upon approaching.

"She was sent here last night and hasn't spoken a word," the emergency room medical staff introduced, according to her ID card, her name is An Hongfeng, 21 years old, and she lives in Dongduanjing Village, Nieying Town, Dai County, Shanxi Province.

"Does your foot hurt?" the reporter asked her. "No pain." After a long while, the girl replied. "No feeling?" "None." Subsequently, no matter what was asked, she answered "none."

It was learned that her bilateral veins and arteries are currently normal. "She has chilblains," the doctor told the reporter that because she sat for a long time, and the pants she wore were tight, causing poor blood circulation, combined with the cold weather, this situation occurred. If it gets worse, it might lead to frostbite.

Kind-hearted netizens took her to the hospital

Subsequently, the reporter found the internet cafe where An Hongfeng once stayed on Xuzhou Road. A Wang surnamed network administrator said that the girl originally came for an interview, but since the internet cafe didn't need more people, she left, but soon returned.

"After returning, she sat in this place," Wang pointed to the second seat in the penultimate row of the internet cafe and said that An Hongfeng sat there for three days and three nights. "When she first came, she looked fine, but had no money on her, so she just sat in front of the computer without turning it on," Wang said that they had asked her to leave, but seeing her condition, they allowed her to stay in the internet cafe.

"Yesterday afternoon, I heard that she was washing her feet in the restroom, and the smell was very strong, many netizens went over there," Wang said that he had already finished his shift, but heard from another network administrator that the smell in the internet cafe was quite strong at the time. After An Hongfeng washed her feet and returned to her seat, she didn't wear shoes. By the evening of December 11th, her feet were red and swollen even more severely, and six or seven netizens took her to the police station.
