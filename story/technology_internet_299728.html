AWS-1W1SJ1J-1 Single Channel Temperature and Humidity Controller

I. Main Functions

The AWS-1W1SJ1J-1 single channel temperature and humidity controller is an automated industrial instrument that can preset the control range of environmental temperature. It features one-channel temperature monitoring. The AWS-1W1SJ1J-1 has characteristics such as small size, high precision, long life, easy installation, and simple usage. It can be used in various scenarios within the power sector, including high and low voltage switch cabinets, terminal boxes, transformer substations, disconnect switches, circuit breaker mechanism boxes, and other situations where controlling the environmental temperature of equipment is necessary. This ensures the safety of power equipment operation, protects it, reduces costs, and enhances economic benefits. The AWS-1W1SJ1J-1 uses internationally advanced sensors to automatically monitor and sample the temperature parameters of the working environment over a long period. When the working environment temperature falls (or rises) below (or above) the set temperature value, the corresponding heating (or cooling) load will instantly activate until the environmental temperature reaches the required setting, at which point it will stop working and re-enter the monitoring state, repeating this automatic cycle.
