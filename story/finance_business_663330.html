About Details

Today, our newspaper's 1 Yuan Care Plan continues to gather loving merchants, providing a variety of cares for lonely elderly people over 80 years old, such as "1 yuan for medicine," "1 yuan for meals," "1 yuan for haircuts," "1 yuan for laundry," "1 yuan for taxi rides," etc. Participating catering enterprises can launch nutritious and delicious meal packages for lonely elderly people over 80 years old to enjoy; pharmacies can choose drugs priced within 15 yuan as 1 yuan love medicine, and can also limit the number of purchases per time...
