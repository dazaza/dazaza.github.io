Norm for writing the title of a website for SEO

by officialcc on 2009-11-23 00:52:54

The norm for writing the title of a website in SEO is not merely about having good rankings through backlink construction. Even if you have an excellent variety of backlinks, the relevance of your homepage and keywords must also be reflected in the title. The way to reflect this is skillful. Here's an article discussing ten common and puzzling aspects of SEO encountered in the past, including some points related to title writing. Today, we will mainly discuss how to write titles that allow for optimal keyword placement.

The components of a page’s SEO can be divided into several parts: The title, keywords, and descriptions should be relevantly recommended within the content of the title. Distribute the keywords across these elements to form an overall keyword arrangement for the entire page. It should be understood here that the most critical part of the overall keyword arrangement is the writing of the title, while other parts are all auxiliary. This is because search engines index based on the title first, and then rank according to the relevance of the content's keywords. Knowing this, we can understand how to write effective website titles.

The title of the content page is not discussed here because each article’s title is different, and the weight of the content page is lower. Typically, the title is set as the content title + column name + website name. However, the homepage title is key among keys. You should let the search engine know what your website is about by writing a good title first. Generally, search engines assign more weight to the left side of the title. Better rankings are achieved when the keyword is placed further left. Therefore, if your website deals with suitcases, putting the word "suitcase" at the end would be very wrong.

Suppose our website specializes in SEO optimization, and the name of our company is Qingdao Fluent Innovative Science Technologies Co., Ltd. If we consider this prominent SEO term, how should we write the title for this website? As someone who has developed as a network head for a long time, I would say the company's name is the most crucial. If your company grows, others may create websites with "Qingdao Fluent Innovative Science Technologies Co., Ltd." as their title. If your title places the company name further down, when customers search for the company name, your ranking could be lower than others, which would be counterproductive.

Some friends might ask, "If I think SEO is outstanding, don't I want the company name to rank higher?" Unfortunately, there's no way around it. After all, you need to consider future things. While SEO has high value, the primary thought behind a website serving a company should be clear. Writing resentful titles doesn't require too much nesting of keywords stiffly. Repeating "SEO" three times in the title might not only fail to improve rankings but could even result in punishment from the search engine. Even without quoting out of context, adding keywords excessively will be considered cheating. Thus, ensuring smooth readability of the title is more critical. Placing the keyword intelligently within a smooth sentence will be the best method for long-term optimization.

We can design the title like this for that company. Search engines generally treat the first 32 characters (Chinese) of the title as the part with supreme weight for the website; anything beyond that is nearly nonsensical. Therefore, we only need to optimize these 32 words. For example, the title of this professional SEO network popularization company can evolve several main keywords: Qingdao network company, Qingdao Fluent Innovative Scientific & Technical Corporation, professional SEO, specialized network popularization, network popularization company, etc. The weight of professional SEO is slightly higher. Designing such a title wouldn't involve any cheating behavior, and the evolved keyword rankings would all be good.

Many friends like to make keywords hard and go beyond limits, for instance: Qingdao Fluent Innovative Science Technology | Professional SEO | Professional Website Construction | Specialized Network Popularization | Replica Watches | Replica Watches | IWC Replica Watch | Replica Audemars Piguet Watch | Replica Richard Mille Watch | Replica Rolex Watches | Replica Longines Watches | Zenith Replica Watches | Replica Panerai Watches | Replica Chanel Watches | Qingdao Network Company. Not only does this exceed the standard word count seriously, but it also writes potential keywords directly, wasting resources in vain. Such practices could lead to severe penalties, resulting in direct entry into the sandbox, which is absolutely inadvisable.

Certainly, how gracefully and smoothly you write the title, transforming more keywords into needs, requires careful pondering. Believe that everyone will achieve a good ranking by completing the title for one's own website.
