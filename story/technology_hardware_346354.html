Blue chips remain strong, and the overall market still shows resilience.

by xw0972406 on 2010-04-15 22:37:33

The Shanghai and Shenzhen stock markets experienced severe fluctuations on Thursday. The two markets opened steadily in the morning, but property stocks fell collectively under the impact of news about the State Council's measures to strongly suppress high housing prices. With the disclosure of macroeconomic data, PetroChina and Sinopec both surged strongly, followed by coal and securities sectors, which quickly drove up the indexes of the two markets. However, other small and medium-sized capitalization stocks fell one after another, releasing selling pressure, causing the market to rise and then fall back. In the afternoon, the Shanghai Composite Index once approached the six-month line. Small games. At the close, bargain hunters intervened, continuing to chase blue-chip stocks, and the Shanghai Composite Index closed at 3164.97 points. From the Thursday trading situation, the divergence between bulls and bears was relatively obvious, with evident selling of real estate and small and medium-sized capitalization stocks. Given the valuation advantage of blue-chip stocks and the launch of stock index futures on Friday, mainstream funds are accelerating their focus on blue chips, which will continue to support the market maintaining strength in the short term.

1. According to reports from Sina Finance, the Federal Reserve Chairman Ben Bernanke said on Wednesday that the U.S. would still maintain a moderate pace of economic growth because the country's economy is facing weak construction spending and persistently high unemployment rates. Bernanke stated in response to questions from the Joint Economic Committee that the Federal Open Market Committee has "clearly declared" its commitment to keep benchmark interest rates at extremely low levels for a "longer period," based on the current reality of high unemployment and low inflation levels. Currently, members of the Federal Reserve Board are debating how and when to withdraw record monetary stimulus measures as the U.S. economy recovers from the most severe recession since the Great Depression.

2. Premier Wen Jiabao presided over the State Council executive meeting on April 14th, analyzing the economic situation in the first quarter and studying and deploying economic work for the next phase. Among the topics mentioned: (1) Greater efforts should be made to improve agricultural production. (2) Financial control work should be done well. (3) Efforts should be made to stabilize the overall price level. (4) Housing price increases must be resolutely curbed. Local governments should earnestly take responsibility for maintaining the healthy development of the real estate market. Strengthen housing security work and increase the effective supply of housing construction land. Implement a more stringent differentiated housing credit policy to curb speculative home purchases. Accelerate research and formulation of reasonable tax policies to guide personal housing consumption. (5) Maintain reasonable and stable growth of domestic demand. (6) Actively promote structural adjustment. Continue to implement the adjustment and revitalization plans of key industries. Vigorously develop strategic emerging industries. Ensure the implementation of various policies to promote the development of small and medium enterprises. Improve policies to promote regional coordinated development. (7) Increase efforts on energy conservation and emissions reduction. (8) Enhance the level of opening up to the outside world. (9) Accelerate the advancement of reform in key areas and critical links. (10) Vigorously ensure and improve people's livelihood.

3. On April 15th, the National Bureau of Statistics held a press conference where spokesperson Li Xiaochao announced economic data for the first quarter. In the first quarter, the gross domestic product was 8,057.7 billion yuan, increasing by 11.9% year-on-year at comparable prices, accelerating by 5.7 percentage points compared to the same period last year. The consumer price index rose by 2.2% year-on-year.

4. On April 15th, the Ministry of Land and Resources reported on the national housing land supply plan for 2010, mainly including the total planned supply of housing land, the amount of land for affordable housing, the amount of land for slum renovation, the amount of land for small and medium-sized unit commercial housing, and the proportion of these three types of housing land out of the total housing land supply, as well as the Ministry of Land and Resources' considerations for promoting the implementation of the plan. According to statistics, the planned housing land supply volume for 2010 in 30 provinces, municipalities, and regions (excluding Tibet) is approximately 180,000 hectares. Compared to the actual housing land supply of 76,461 hectares last year and the average annual actual supply of 54,650 hectares over the past five years, there is a significant increase.

5. According to reports from China Securities News, Xu Quan, deputy director of the Shanghai Municipal Financial Services Office, revealed that the Shanghai Stock Exchange is conducting institutional design for the launch of the international board, and the CSRC is also discussing the draft IPO system for this sector. Once the system is perfected, the international board will be launched. Xu Quan did not provide a specific timetable for the launch of the international board but indicated that it "is expected to be launched within the year."

6. According to reports from 21st Century Business Herald, on April 14th, Jiang Yi, Secretary of the Shanghai Municipal Party Committee and District Chief of Pudong New Area, stated at a Shanghai municipal government press conference that centered around the Disney theme park project, there will also be the creation of tourism zones such as Sanjia Port and Lingang Coastal Tourism Resort. Jiang Yi noted that in the next three years, 100 billion yuan will be invested to strengthen road connections and infrastructure construction in southern and northern Pudong. Previously, this reporter learned that the 100 billion yuan investment is largely used for infrastructure services surrounding Disney.

The Fed reiterated its commitment to maintaining low interest rates for an extended period, and the impressive earnings reports from listed U.S. companies have enhanced the market's optimistic sentiment regarding the economic recovery outlook, stimulating continued gains in U.S. stocks and international commodities prices, thereby creating a favorable external environment for A-shares. Domestic policies will further adopt stricter measures to control housing prices, forming a certain short-term impact on the A-share market. However, real estate stocks have been adjusting continuously for several days, gradually digesting the negative impacts of policy. Meanwhile, the macroeconomic data for the first quarter and March indicate that the micro-adjustment policies are taking effect, reducing the possibility of a short-term interest rate hike, thus enhancing market confidence in buying. Considering the listing of stock index futures, blue-chip stocks continue to receive attention, and they will continue to support the market's upward trend in the short term. However, the volatility of the market will increase.
