Who polluted "You Fang Gou"?

by bjjdwxgs on 2009-12-06 07:32:39

Mr. Wang, who lives in the urban area, reported that there is a small river called "Youfang Ditch" west of the Century联Hua Supermarket on Yanma Road. Garbage, feces and other dirty things are dumped into this river, causing serious pollution to the surrounding environment. Upon arriving at the "Youfang Ditch" Mr. Wang pointed out, a strong odor hit our noses, and the river water was turbid.

Simultaneous voice: Citizen Mr. Wang - When the stench is overwhelming, all the windows here are closed, they can't be opened, it's too smelly.

Simultaneous voice: Resident - The surrounding environment is too bad, the smell is too heavy.

We found that this river is connected to some of the main rivers in the urban area, it is not a stagnant water river. What is the reason for the deterioration of the river water?

Simultaneous voice: Citizen Mr. Wang - Some of the feces from the sanitation department are directly discharged into the river, which is one of the factors. There are also other dirty things, garbage, and dirty water. You can see the sewage outlet is right here.

It turns out that the east bank of the river is a garbage treatment station of the sanitation department. A number of sewage ditches are directly connected to the river. Is it really the case that the sanitation department disposes of garbage in the "Youfang Ditch"? So the reporter went to the treatment station to find out. However, the situation is not what the surrounding residents imagined. According to the person in charge of the treatment station, the septic tank in the treatment station has been abandoned for two years, so currently, there is no situation of discharging pollutants into the "Youfang Ditch". Mr. Wang also confirmed this.

So, where does the pollution of the river come from? We noticed that on the west bank of the river, there was a pile of garbage dumped by the surrounding residents.

Simultaneous voice: Resident - There is a trash can here, it is placed here every day. Every evening (the sanitation workers) will clean it up. (Then why is there still so much garbage in the river?) Definitely some residents themselves are not conscious, they dump the garbage into the river. Different personal qualities, individual quality problems of some citizens.

Postscript: It seems that the pollution of this river water is not a problem of recent times. Perhaps before this, the septic tank of the sanitation department caused a certain degree of pollution to this river. And now, the nearby residents should take more responsibility for the pollution of the river water.
