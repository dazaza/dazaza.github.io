How to open an online store on Taobao

I. How to Open an Online Store on Taobao: Steps for Opening a Shop

Nowadays, with the growing prevalence of the internet, online shopping has become increasingly popular. If you're wondering how to open a store on Taobao, what preparations are needed, and what precautions should be taken, read on.

1. Register Your Account on Taobao

First, go to Taobao's homepage. After opening the website, click "Free Registration." You can choose to register using your phone number or email address; generally, we recommend "Email Registration." Fill out all the required information and click "Agree to Terms and Submit Registration Information." If everything goes smoothly, the website will confirm your successful registration.

Next, log into your email to retrieve the confirmation email from Taobao. Click the confirmation link to activate your account, completing the first step in opening your online store. Note that if you've already shopped on Taobao, there’s no need to re-register. On Taobao, one account can serve as both a buyer and seller.

2. Complete Alipay Real Name Verification

How do you open a store on Taobao? What steps are necessary? The first step is successfully registering a Taobao account. Next, you need to complete Alipay real name verification, which is mandatory. After clicking “My Taobao,” you’ll see a prompt saying “Sell Items, Please Complete Real Name Verification.” Follow the instructions provided.

Alipay acts as the money pouch for Taobao users and ensures honest transactions between both parties. Real name verification confirms your true identity. This process increases the complexity of opening a store but significantly enhances the security of Taobao transactions. In the past, uploading your ID was required for manual verification by Taobao. Now, Taobao has partnered with banks nationwide, allowing verification through bank systems if you have a real-name registered bank card, making the process much easier.

3. List More Than 10 Items

List more than 10 different items (and keep them listed for sale), and you can open your store for free. To list items, go to “My Taobao” - “I am a Seller” - “I Want to Sell.” Taobao considers having more than 10 items for sale as a requirement for opening a store. Listing fewer than 10 items is considered personal trading, so it's impossible to apply for a store without listing at least 10 items.

4. Pass the Taobao Store Exam

Go to “My Taobao” - “I am a Seller,” and find the “Open Store” button. Clicking it will prompt you to take an exam. Passing the Taobao store exam is mandatory for new sellers since this rule was implemented by Taobao at the end of 2010. The exam primarily covers the “Taobao Rules,” and you must score at least 60 points to pass, with 100% accuracy on basic questions. After passing, read the integrity operation commitment letter, then follow the prompts to fill in the shop name, category, and introduction. Check the boxes agreeing to the “Product Release Rules” and “Consumer Protection Agreement,” then confirm and submit. If everything goes well, you now own your own Taobao store!

5. Install Aliwangwang Software

On Taobao, communication with buyers is not done through QQ, phone, or other methods, but through Aliwangwang. Aliwangwang is the essential tool for communication between Taobao sellers and buyers, integrating many seller functions and being highly practical. In case of any disputes during transactions, Aliwangwang chat records serve as crucial evidence for dispute resolution. Note that Taobao does not recognize QQ chat records. Thus, Aliwangwang is irreplaceable.

Opening an online store on Taobao is simple, but running a successful store on Taobao is not easy!

II. Supplementary Notes and Precautions

Shop Naming: A shorter shop name isn't necessarily better, as it relates to Taobao search rankings. It's best to include keywords related to the items you sell, their purpose, brand, and any ongoing promotions. This improves the likelihood of being found through Taobao searches. Additionally, regularly listing new items helps improve search rankings.

2. Real Name Verification Process

To open a store on Taobao, real name verification is required. The shop owner must be a legal citizen of China aged eighteen or older and possess a bank account linked to Alipay. During real name verification, Alipay will deposit a small amount (approximately 0.01-1 RMB) into your account. After checking via online banking, input the exact transaction amount into Alipay's webpage. Correct entry will verify your identity.

3. No Credit, No Visitors?

New stores often struggle due to low credibility, leading to lost sales opportunities. Even excellent products may be overlooked without trust. Virtual investments are small, offer quick returns, and help build credit.

III. Necessity of Decorating a New Store

1. As a new seller with zero or near-zero credit, how can buyers trust you aren’t a scammer? Decorate your store to make it attractive and professional.

2. Give buyers a good impression. Ensure the store is tidy and preferably unique, making it memorable and encouraging repeat visits.

3. Pay attention to overall design consistency. Many beginners seek free resources and patch together various elements. While cost-effective, ensure the final result looks cohesive. Store decoration is crucial and should not be neglected. Properly designed pages reflect the seller's dedication, enhancing the store's image and aiding brand formation, increasing traffic.

Beautiful and appropriate store decoration increases customer dwell time, offering visual pleasure and reducing browsing fatigue. Attractive displays enhance product appeal, promoting sales.

IV. Preparations Before Becoming a Store Owner

1. Adjust your mindset and commit fully! Persistence and honesty are key.

2. Develop a detailed store plan (important before opening). Consider what to sell, sourcing, promotion, costs, etc., beforehand.

3. Choose suitable products but understand them thoroughly. Start with virtual goods. Keep learning! Engage with forums and discuss with others.

V. Additional Notes

1. Sourcing: Note that sourcing can be divided into virtual and physical sources. Methods include self-sourcing or dropshipping. Virtual sourcing doesn't require logistics, while physical sourcing does. You can start with virtual and move to physical or handle both simultaneously.

2. Prepare a second-generation ID card and a bank card with online banking enabled. Ensure clear photos of both sides of the ID are available on your computer.

3. Both membership registration and store opening are free, with no fees involved. Beware of scammers posing as registrars.

4. After registering, activate the associated Alipay account, which guarantees secure online transactions.

5. Real name verification requires your ID and a bank card under your ID's name with online banking enabled. Second-generation IDs require clear photos of both sides. Verification typically takes 2-3 days but may be faster.

6. After verification, upload items for sale.

7. For agents, whether physical or virtual, your supplier will provide data packages. Using Taobao Assistant, upload all data, representing the information of the items you sell. For virtual software agency, no logistics are required. If self-sourcing, contact logistics companies, take item photos, describe the items, and upload them to your store. With Taobao, listing 10 or more items allows you to open your store for free.

8. Beginners, beware of scams!

9. On Taobao, sellers with one-diamond reputation or below can upgrade to the supported version of Wangpu for free.

10. Understand the transaction process: Buyer places order — Buyer pays — "Sold Items" shows "Buyer Paid" — Seller ships — Buyer confirms receipt — Evaluates buyer.

Finally, I wish everyone success in opening their stores and thriving businesses. Welcome everyone, hoping to gain friendships while doing business.
