"Time Capsule" knock-offs sell well on Taobao, can be buried for 50 years

by anonymous on 2013-10-02 10:35:46

If you plan to bury a time capsule like Steve Jobs and reminisce about today's times in a few decades, what would you and your friends prepare to bury? With the recent excavation of the "time capsule" that Jobs buried 30 years ago, the business of selling time capsules on Taobao has become increasingly prosperous. Paying tribute to the future, the time capsule has become a wondrous experience of crossing time and space.

Oval-shaped Time Capsules are Hot Sellers on Taobao

Jobs' brilliant idea of burying a time capsule has sparked a new wave of enthusiasm among young people for digging and burying capsules. Many people are asking themselves what items they should bury to represent their present selves. Some couples plan to bury their QQ chat records from their dating days underground to dig up in their old age as a love memorial. Other netizens say they will bury their Taobao account and records underground to reminisce about their past consumption records and use them as a reference for future expenses. Someone reminds those preparing to dig: "Don't forget to put things in a plastic bag before burying them, otherwise, they may be ruined when you dig them up later. Moreover, it's essential to choose a good location and reference point; don't let your time capsule be like Jobs', which took 10 more years to excavate."

A Time Capsule is an internationally recognized method of commemorating important events. It involves sealing important items, documents, and physical objects into a secure container, burying it deep underground for a long time, and then retrieving it. Due to the need for long-term burial, the material of the time capsule becomes particularly important.

On Taobao, there are very few merchants selling time capsules. Among them, an e-commerce company from Zigong, Sichuan stands out due to its patent certificate granted by the National Intellectual Property Administration. Unlike Jobs' tube-shaped "time capsule," this team designed a more vivid egg-shaped capsule. With the recent surge in popularity of time capsules, the shop's sales have doubled, with around 10 transactions per day.

Various Materials Claim to Last 50 Years

"What can dozens of yuan do? Why not plant a hope under a wishing tree." To ensure that this hope isn't corroded by soil, this online store offers time capsules in two versions: a fifty-year version made of imported industrial plastic and a hundred-year version made of stainless steel. The plastic version, priced at around a hundred yuan, comes in ten colors including green, blue, pink, and silver, where green symbolizing youth and silver giving a technological feel are already sold out.

"The size is quite appropriate, but it looks shiny, somewhat like a monochrome egg-shaped trash bin," one buyer commented. The regular egg-shaped capsule is 31 cm high and weighs 1 catty and 2 liang (about 650 grams). It can hold items such as a mouse, CDs, books, and diaries. In addition to providing imported sealing glue, the store also thoughtfully provides a folding spade for buyers to dig and bury easily.

Additionally, another professional version of the time capsule costing 290 yuan uses high-strength, corrosion-resistant stainless steel material, and its shape resembles a segment of a steel pipe. Despite being three times the price of the regular version, it has received five orders recently. A buyer commented, "The item is a bit expensive, but the craftsmanship of pure stainless steel is worth it. The white gloves provided by the seller are very warm and touching, I give it a thumbs up!"

How to Ensure the Capsule Remains Unchanged Decades Later?

Mr. He from the online store stated that they currently cannot guarantee their promise. "The capsule's material has been experimentally tested. We use professional anti-aging materials imported from the United States, which can withstand strong light exposure and at least 100°C high temperatures."

Young People Become the Main Consumers of Time Capsules

Beijing Youth Daily reporter found that most buyers of time capsules are students during graduation season, young people burying love letters, and some parents who seal their children's most memorable items in capsules to be opened by them after several years. Notably, Li Ka-shing, as the honorary chairman of the Shantou University Board of Trustees, placed a time capsule last year during the unveiling of the university's new administrative building.

Buyers of time capsules expressed, "Seal it first, then open it again in 50 years." Another buyer frankly said, "I just want to bury it for a year, and if it remains intact, I'll be very happy." "The seller sells creativity, I put all my old diaries inside and buried them." "It was my friend's birthday yesterday, and I finally gave her the time capsule. She was moved to tears, and this gift is really meaningful as a witness to our friendship." A teacher mentioned, "After receiving the time capsule, the students were so happy. They filled it with letters from over 20 classmates, and the capacity is not small."

Mr. He revealed that most buyers of time capsules are students who bury their graduation reflections and classmate friendships in the capsules to be opened during reunions decades later. There are also buyers purchasing for their 8-year-old sister because this little girl saw it mentioned on TV.
