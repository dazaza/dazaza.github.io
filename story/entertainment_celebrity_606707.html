Famous blogger Changchun Guomao published a self-claimed exclusive interview with Fang Hongjin's daughter Fang Zhen on his blog. In the interview, Fang Zhen frankly admitted that the Weibo account "Mengsha Zhi" is indeed hers and that Fang Hongjin had tricked her into deleting the Weibo posts. Fang Zhen also stated that she had witnessed domestic violence by Fang Hongjin at home. In response, Fang Hongjin claimed he would not comment and criticized it as hype. Today at noon, Fang Zhen updated the content of her Weibo account "Mengsha Zhi" again, uploading photos of herself with her father and her student ID to confirm her identity as Fang Hongjin's daughter Fang Zhen. She posted more than thirty Weibo updates condemning various actions of Fang Hongjin, stating that due to the hardship of life and her father not paying child support, she resorted to Weibo, denying any intention to create hype. Fang Zhen accuses her father in an interview

Fang Hongjin responds claiming it is hype

From Changchun Guomao's interview with Fang Zhen, Fang Zhen refutes many aspects of what Fang Hongjin previously said in interviews. First, regarding the authenticity of the Weibo account "Mengsha Zhi," Fang Hongjin once pointed out in an interview that this Weibo was not her daughter's, otherwise why would all the Weibo posts be deleted. Fang Zhen explained that since emails and phone calls failed to reach her father, she eventually decided to use Weibo to contact him. She also mentioned that Fang Hongjin knew her Weibo account, had repeatedly blocked her, and even tricked her into deleting the Weibo posts. Regarding her father's claim of leaving everything behind and leaving millions of assets, Fang Zhen stated that her father not only has no assets but is heavily in debt. Fang Zhen said that now living with her mother is very difficult, so they could only resort to legal means to claim child support.

Sina Entertainment contacted Fang Hongjin to verify the interview content. Fang Hongjin criticized Changchun Guomao and stated that he would not respond to this interview, hoping not to make it a hype or an entertainment topic.

Fang Zhen denies the hype on Weibo

Enumerates multiple inappropriate behaviors of her father

At 12:04 PM today, Fang Zhen updated the content of her Weibo account "Mengsha Zhi" again. To prove that she is Fang Hongjin's daughter Fang Zhen, netizens uploaded two photos with her father Fang Hongjin and her student ID on Weibo. She also updated more than thirty Weibo posts recounting various inappropriate behaviors of Fang Hongjin.

Fang Zhen stated on Weibo that Fang Hongjin left home four years ago and on December 1st, during the court hearing, Fang Hongjin could not even recognize his own daughter. Regarding Fang Hongjin's previous statement in an interview that he left because his wife crossed moral boundaries, Fang Zhen exposed on Weibo that Fang Hongjin had several mistresses, including having a room with a female undergraduate student from Fudan University in June 2006 at Shanghai Jing'an Ruaitai Hotel, which was discovered by her mother who made him write a repentance letter. Her mother forgave him, but later found out that he was supporting a woman in Beijing, as seen in text messages on his phone. In 2007, when her mother refused to give him money (which was the only money for her education), he used domestic violence. When she wanted to call the police, he knelt down and begged her mother, who forgave him again considering his face, but unexpectedly, by the end of 2007, he abandoned her and her mother. She also personally witnessed him confronting her mother with that woman in a Starbucks.

Regarding Fang Hongjin's claim that his daughter attended an elite school and his ex-wife lived in a luxury villa and drove a luxury car, Fang Zhen responded on Weibo that it was not an elite school, and textbooks, uniforms, etc., were borrowed from senior students. The so-called luxury villa did not exist; the rented house owner was just a subway commuter. There was no luxury car either; she had to wake up early every day and take the subway to school.

Fang Zhen also mentioned on Weibo that she was deceived by Fang Hongjin into deleting the Weibo posts she had previously released.
