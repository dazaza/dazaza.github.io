**Synopsis of "Underworld 4: Awakening"**:

During the decades that Selene was in a coma, humans discovered the existence of vampires and werewolves in the world and implemented a "minor purge" policy against them. While humans believed they had nearly exterminated both species, a secret organization used biotechnology and research on werewolves to create a new species: the super werewolf. It possesses incomparable strength, speed, size, and destructive power. For any species, this is a potential threat.

Upon awakening, Selene discovers that the world has changed. To survive, she must once again take up arms and fight for her life. She also learns that she has a 15-year-old daughter named Eve, who is a hybrid of vampire and werewolf. Her existence could be key to finding the super werewolf's weakness. However, Eve is locked in a human research facility. To ensure the survival of her race and to save her daughter, Selene embarks on another bloody journey.
