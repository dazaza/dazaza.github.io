Hollow drill bit, core drill bit, hole opener, rail drill bit, high-speed steel drill bit, carbide drill bit.

Magnetic drill [http://www.hanyue-sh.com/]-is a movable and highly efficient tool that uses electromagnetic principles through a magnetic base to adsorb the drill frame and rotary drilling mechanism onto the surface of a steel workpiece for drilling processing. After being powered on, the magnetic drill generates thousands of kilograms of magnetic suction force vertically to the bottom surface of the magnetic base through electromagnetic effects, firmly adsorbing it onto the surface of the steel plate or structural component to be processed, serving to fix the machine. The stronger the magnetic suction force, the more stable the body of the magnetic drill [http://www.hanyue-sh.com/] will be during operation, and the higher the precision of the drilled holes will be. When the motor start switch is turned on, the rotor inside the machine motor rotates at high speed, driving the rotation of the drive shaft through the gear set, which in turn drives the drill bit to rotate and cut holes in the required drilling area through the drill bit. Compared with flame cutting holes, etc., the holes drilled by the magnetic drill have higher precision, belong to physical cutting, do not produce chemical reactions, and will not cause material deformation.

The magnetic drill [http://www.hanyue-sh.com/] is suitable for various complex environmental steel structure drilling operations, especially applicable to: nuclear power plants, shipbuilding industry, papermaking industry, bridge engineering, mechanical engineering, coal mine machinery and mining machinery, machinery equipment installation, steel structure engineering, railway and subway construction, large equipment maintenance, power plant maintenance, power construction, oilfield construction, etc., and is an indispensable special equipment for drilling, tapping, reaming, and other operations.

The characteristics of the magnetic drill are small size, light weight (such as only 9.9 kg for the German Boden30), high power, high efficiency, and accurate positioning. It can also use the adsorption force of the magnetic base to perform drilling operations from different angles. When used in conjunction with a core drill bit, the effect is even better; it also solves the problem of pipeline waste residue cleaning, and satisfactory results can be achieved for overlapping steel plate drilling. It can also easily solve the problem of drilling stainless steel pipes and stainless steel of a certain thickness.

The FE32 magnetic base drill is a compact multi-functional drilling machine. Suitable for various drilling situations, hollow drill bits or twist drills can be used. The machine is equipped with a powerful 900W motor. This machine can choose internal cooling device.
