Super Bowl: How much will the NFL fine Chad Ochocinco for the latest touchdown celebration?

by alasjtom on 2009-12-08 16:39:30

Ochocinco had promised a celebration that would draw the attention of the NFL's disciplinarians. He tweeted on Sunday: "What I've planned for today will get me fined when I score, but it's so worth it." As promised, Chad Ochocinco delivered an odd touchdown celebration after scoring a 36-yard TD in Cincinnati's 23-13 win against the Lions.
