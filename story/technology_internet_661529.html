Overview of Recent Operations:

From this survey, we learned that the company's recent product sales have been good, and it is still in a state of full-load production. According to the company's introduction, the inventory backlog problem of air conditioning products is not as severe as reported by the media. In fact, the largest inventory situation in the past was only about one month's worth of sales volume. Therefore, the booming sales during the National Day holiday caused some products to sell out. It was introduced that the workers took only two days off during the National Day holiday, and the workers have not left the production line for 31 consecutive days and are still continuing at full load production.

For this reason, under the accompaniment of relevant company officials, we also visited the production workshop; the workers work in shifts daily, operating at full capacity with high equipment utilization. It was introduced that the daily output of split-type wall-mounted air conditioners is approximately 2,000 units, and there are not many holidays throughout the year, so the annual production capacity exceeds the designed capacity of the company. Additionally, when discussing the peak and off-peak seasons of air conditioning products, the company believes that its air conditioning products no longer have distinct peak and off-peak seasons, especially this year, where the off-season trend did not appear.

Benefiting from Consumption Stimulus Policies:

At the beginning of this year, the government increased the implementation strength of the rural electrification policy and included air conditioners as one of the rural electrification products. The consumption stimulation effect of rural electrification has performed well.

As a leading domestic home air conditioning enterprise, the company's air conditioning products rank among the top in the industry in terms of rural electrification sales share. Data from the rural electrification information management system shows that in September of this year, the company's rural electrification air conditioner sales reached 164,100 sets, accounting for 47.15% of all rural electrification air conditioner total sales; the rural electrification sales amount reached 450 million yuan, accounting for 47.87% of all rural electrification air conditioner total sales. From these figures, which are nearly half of the shares, it is clear that the company is the most significantly benefited air conditioner manufacturing enterprise from the rural electrification policy.

Compared to urban areas, the household appliance penetration rate in rural areas is very low, especially the air conditioner penetration rate, which is less than 10%. The demand space for air conditioners is large, and the conditions for using household appliances in rural areas are continuously improving. These factors determine that the rural market will become the most worthwhile territory for domestic air conditioner enterprises to develop. The continued implementation of the rural electrification policy becomes an opportunity for air conditioner enterprises to expand their rural markets. As the first domestic home air conditioner brand, the company will continue to be the most significantly benefited air conditioner manufacturing enterprise from the rural electrification policy.

In addition to the rural electrification policy, the government has also launched a "trade-in" consumption stimulation policy for household appliances. This policy has not been implemented for long, so there is currently no obvious implementation effect. However, with the acceleration of the upgrade and replacement of air conditioning products and the pace of consumption upgrades for urban residents, the policy effects of "trade-in" for household appliances will gradually become apparent.

Furthermore, the implementation and promotion of energy-saving subsidy policies will accelerate the speed of upgrading and replacing air conditioning products. The pace of air conditioning upgrades has accelerated this year, and environmental protection and energy saving have become the mainstream development trends. Energy-saving frequency conversion air conditioners have begun to gradually become the best choice for mid-to-high-end consumers to upgrade and replace their products. Frequency conversion air conditioners began to gain momentum in 2008, and the trend of air conditioner frequency conversion after 2009 has become increasingly evident. It is expected that in the next three to five years, frequency conversion products will completely replace fixed-frequency products. Additionally, from the survey, it was learned that the lowest frequency currently mastered by the company in the frequency conversion technology field has reached 6HZ, ranking among the top in the industry. From the sales perspective, the company's frequency conversion air conditioner sales share for the whole year of 2009 is expected to reach 15% to 20%. The proportion of products with level 2 energy efficiency or higher has exceeded 80%. The company actively responds to the national energy-saving people's livelihood policy call, actively adjusts product structure, and vigorously promotes energy-saving products.

From the survey, it was learned that currently, among all the company's air conditioning products, the proportion of products with level 2 energy efficiency or higher has exceeded 80%, and level 3 energy efficiency products are only for rural electrification products. Other lower energy efficiency grade products are no longer produced. Additionally, up to now, the company has submitted three batches of energy-saving subsidy applications to the energy-saving department, with a total subsidy amount reaching 1.08 billion yuan, and there may still be further applications before the end of the year.

Air Conditioner Gross Profit Margin Can Be Maintained Around 24%

It is expected that in the future one to two years, with the improvement of exports and the gradual growth of commercial air conditioner sales, the company's revenue growth will hopefully recover to double digits, and it is estimated that the sales growth rate in 2010 will return to around 16%. In addition, given the company's view on high gross profit margins and the significant promotional efforts during the National Day holiday, along with the gradual recovery of raw material prices, the gross profit margin in the fourth quarter will further experience a sequential decline. However, the gross profit margin for the whole year of 2009 will still reach around 24.5%, which is a historical high point. In the coming years, the further rise in raw material prices will lead to a decline in the gross profit margin of air conditioner products. However, with the increase in new product research and development and launches, the gross profit margin of air conditioner products will still remain within the reasonable range of 18%-23%; it is expected that the gross profit margin of the company's air conditioner products in 2010 will still be around 22%.

We estimate that the revenue growth rates of the company in 2009 and 2010 will reach -4.2% and 16.8%, respectively, with gross profit margins being 24.6% and 22.2%, respectively. The corresponding EPS of the company in 2009 and 2010 will be 1.39 yuan and 1.66 yuan, respectively.
