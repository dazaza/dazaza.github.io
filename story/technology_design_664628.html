Wind drill bit welding equipment

Zhengzhou Guoyun Electronics Co., Ltd. was established in 1990 and is a high-tech enterprise in Henan Province. It has successively obtained six national patents for its achievements, focusing exclusively on the induction heating field for twenty years. For the welding part in mechanical manufacturing, we have developed various professional welding equipment, among which the wind drill bit welding equipment is one. Common problems in welding include: (1) weak welding, de-welding, cold overlap; (2) ripples appear on both sides of the weld seam; (3) deep pits and pinholes in the weld seam; (4) too high burrs on the weld seam; (5) slag inclusion. Our company's welding equipment has continuously adjustable power with digital display, making it convenient for operators to observe and adjust. It also has remote control functions, making operation more convenient and easier to control the melting point during welding, thus easily solving the above problems and greatly improving the quality of welding. By carefully making the key part of heating (the inductor), it makes it easier for operators to grasp the melting point during welding and improves efficiency. Compared with similar products, our product has the following advantages: 1. Faster speed and higher efficiency. 2. More energy-saving. 3. Multiple workpieces can be welded at once, greatly improving welding efficiency. 4. Smaller oxidation area. 5. More uniform heating, no missing or leaking weld points. 6. The weld seam of the welded workpiece is firm and full. 7. Load continuous rate: 100%. 8. More convenient daily maintenance. By replacing the inductor, it can be used for multiple purposes, achieving multi-functionality with one machine. Specifically, as follows: 1. Welding of diamond tool heads, hard alloy saw blades, and welding of diamonds, abrasives, and drills. 2. Welding of hard alloys used in machining. Such as welding of cutting tools like turning tools, planing tools, milling cutters, reamers, etc. 3. Welding of mining tools, such as "one" type chisel head, column tooth chisel head, swallowtail coal drill head, rivet rod drill head, various coal mining machine teeth, various tunneling machine teeth. 4. Various woodworking welding, such as welding of various woodworking planer knives, milling cutters, and various woodworking drill bits, brazing equipment.
