The little cat Mimi was born with a limp.

by rwmfeifei on 2011-09-12 12:49:43

Mi Mi and Ben Ben were walking on the street. A woman passed by them, looked at Ben Ben, who friendly wagged his tail. Then, Ben Ben and Mi Mi secretly followed her until she reached her doorstep. The woman looked at Ben Ben and asked: "Can you guard the door?" Ben Ben nodded and wagged his tail, then barked twice as if showing her an example.

Soon after, Ben Ben got cleaned up. He shook off the water after getting out of the river and basked in the sun. Mi Mi looked at Ben Ben and said: "Ben Ben, you've really become beautiful."

The woman thought Ben Ben was very likable, so she decided to keep him at her home.

Little cat Mi Mi had a limp, while big dog Ben Ben was blind in one eye. Mi Mi and Ben Ben met each other while wandering on Yongfeng Street. In no time, they had known each other for half a year, becoming inseparable friends who relied on each other for survival.

When Mi Mi and Ben Ben didn't find anything good to eat in the market, they returned home dejectedly, thinking that their fate was bitter. As dusk fell, Mi Mi and Ben Ben arrived at their nest. It got colder again, so Ben Ben gathered some wheat straw and soft cloth nearby, using them as a soft blanket to lay in their nest. Ben Ben held Mi Mi in his arms, noticing that Mi Mi had been down since returning from the market. He consoled Mi Mi, saying: "Mi Mi, don't be sad. We will definitely find our own owner in the future and live a happy life without worrying about food."

One day, Mi Mi and Ben Ben went to the vegetable market on Yongfeng Street to scavenge bones, vegetable leaves, and garbage to fill their stomachs. At this time, Aunt Wang came with her pet Beibei, who was also a big white dog. Beibei seemed to smell the delicious meat in the market and started barking toward Aunt Wang. Aunt Wang then bought Beibei a sausage. Seeing this, Mi Mi and Ben Ben were so envious that they licked their lips.

The next morning, Mi Mi and Ben Ben got up early. They went to a small river. Ben Ben said: "We haven't taken a bath for a long time, so we are dirty. That's probably why no one wants us. Let's clean ourselves in the river first." Mi Mi replied: "But I can't swim. You go first, and I'll wait for you by the riverside."
