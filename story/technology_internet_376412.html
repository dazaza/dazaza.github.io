1. Wumii http://www.wumii.com/

Wumii is a social news sharing and recommendation website. By using artificial intelligence technology, Wumii finds content that interests you among numerous web pages based on the pages you like. You just need to click "Like on Wumii" to share your favorite articles on the site. Wumii will record your preferences; the more you like, the more accurate its recommendations will be.

2. Qing6 Network http://www.qing6.com/

Qing6 is a classic online resource collection and sharing platform where users can share and discuss webpages, images, videos they love with other netizens.

3. IT Elite Network http://www.itniuren.com/

IT Elite Network is an online exchange platform specifically built for IT professionals. Through features such as news, forums, circles, and SNS, it creates a good environment for IT-related personnel to communicate and learn. IT Elite Network is committed to building personal influence for IT professionals and provides comprehensive personal development and improvement space for registered members.

4. Guokr http://www.guokr.com/

Guokr is a science-themed website offering responsible, intelligent, and interesting content close to daily life. Here, you can read, share, communicate, and ask questions. Guokr is dedicated to making scientific interest an important part of people's cultural and entertainment lives.

5. iKeepu http://ikeepu.com/

iKeepu is an online service used for collecting, organizing, and sharing any resources from the internet, including websites, images, and videos, and is also committed to helping users discover more interesting internet resources.

6. Bengxin Network http://www.bengxin.com/

Bengxin Network is a community for sharing life experiences and information about dining, entertainment, shopping, etc. It has developed into a cross-platform LBS service site where users can find merchant information nearby, check in at visited places, post photos, and write reviews.

7. MyMova http://www.mymova.com/

MyMova is a professional conference and event management system that improves the efficiency of organizing events and reduces costs. Through MyMova-built thematic websites, it attracts high-quality participants, making your conferences and events more efficient and professional!

8. Jamguo Music http://www.jamguo.com/

Jamguo Music is China's first original music trading platform that offers buying and selling of original music. It gathers a large number of original music tracks and provides massive downloads of original music. Its unique MBC cooperative promotion model allows original musicians and promoters to make money together.

9. Qiangzuo Network http://www.51qiangzuo.com/

With Qiangzuo Network, you can create, publish, collect, and manage guest information online. You can promote your events through emails, blogs, microblogs, and various social networks, and even sell tickets directly online. Suitable for performances, training, lectures, clubs, public welfare, and other types of activities.

10. K.ai http://k.ai/

K.ai is a new generation of location network and also a real white-collar social network. By checking in on K.ai, you can find friends, share tips, and explore the city around you in a brand-new way.
