Du Wenhui sang loudly and "shook" the entire scene, as his teammates joked that he should develop in the entertainment circle.

by qinglai888 on 2009-12-15 12:13:29

Du Wenhui's powerful singing "shook" the whole audience; teammates joked that he should develop in the entertainment circle. "I didn't expect that the players of Guoan could sing so well!" Last night, when the entire Guoan team led all the fans to sing "A True Hero," all the fans were deeply moved.

In the Guoan team, Du Wenhui is universally recognized as the "KTV king." He took the stage as the lead singer last night and immediately "shocked" the entire audience with his impressive voice, surprising all the fans: "It's a pity that Du Wenhui has such a good voice but doesn't pursue singing." The Yu & Quan duo couldn't help but praise him from below the stage. Wang Changqing, Yan Xiangchuang, and Xu Yulong then each sang a line, making the song incredibly stirring.

When the whole team joined in the chorus, a touching scene occurred, with all the fans spontaneously joining in the singing. "In my heart, there was once a dream, to use my songs to make you forget all the pain. In this brilliant starry sky, who is the real hero? Ordinary people give me the most moving feelings." Everyone waved their glow sticks continuously, turning the entire National Stadium into a sea of green.

After the event, the teammates teased Du Wenhui: "Hui Zi, with such great talent, you really should develop in the entertainment industry. Otherwise, tomorrow you can retire, shoot idol dramas, or release albums—whatever it is, we will definitely support you." Du Wenhui innocently replied: "I'll still stay in the football world for now; I haven't thought about the entertainment industry."

"Who among Guoan has the greatest influence? Fans."

"Who among Guoan has the greatest influence?" During the evening party, Jiang Kun and Dai Zhicheng discussed the topic of Guoan winning the championship in the form of a crosstalk. Notably, when the two mentioned "Jin Zhiyang" and "Lee Chang-soo," the reactions of the on-site fans grew more intense. Hong Yuanshu, who took charge in a critical situation and ensured Guoan's championship win, also received applause. Ultimately, the conclusion of their discussion was that Guoan fans have the greatest influence.

The honorary chairman of the club, Luo Ning, emotionally said: "For 16 years, the ones we need to thank most are the fans who have never abandoned us. Without their support, it would have been difficult for us to achieve our goals. The club thanks the fans for growing together with us. All experiences—whether the joy of winning the championship or the pain of narrowly missing it before—are beautiful memories. In these memories, what is most precious is the support of the fans. On behalf of the club, I thank you, thank you for your companionship through wind and rain for 16 years!"

It is understood that to thank the fans for their support, the season tickets for next year's Guoan will not increase in price.

This article was written by reporter Zheng Xiaolong.
