K Gold Polished Crystal Tiles, Renowned in Shandong

Editor: Italian Roma Carpet [http://www.luomab.com/]     Website: www.luomab.com[http://www.luomab.com/] 

Article Summary: K gold polished crystal tiles are made using existing ceramic tiles as the main body. Through a scientific and special process, various artistic patterns are engraved on them, and various gold lines are hand-painted for embellishment, followed by a second high-temperature firing.

On March 6th, Oujiak Group's Ousike Ceramic Company in Zibo, Shandong Province, officially began production of the K gold polished crystal tile, hailed as the [ceramic carpet].

The K gold polished crystal tile uses existing ceramic tiles as the main body. Through a scientifically special process conducted by the carpet company [http://www.luomab.com/], various artistic patterns are engraved on them, and various gold lines are hand-painted for embellishment, followed by a second high-temperature firing. When these tiles are pieced together, they resemble a [ceramic carpet]. Not only are they beautiful and luxurious, but they are also durable, transforming the monotonous colors of traditional ceramics into a rich variety. They can be widely used for floor and wall decorations in high-end residential living rooms, corridors, star-rated hotels, and entertainment venues.

It is understood that this enterprise invested over 10 million yuan, and after one and a half years of research and development, successfully developed eight major series with over sixty varieties including Noble, Elegant, and Supreme series.
