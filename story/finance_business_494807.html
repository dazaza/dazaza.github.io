Weihai sea cucumber makes a collective appearance at the "2011 Quality Aquatic Products Exhibition and Trading Fair"_ Finance_ Tencent Network

by prettybaby on 2011-09-25 11:10:45

Since the brand advertising slogan of "Good people? Good sea? Good sea cucumber Weihai Stichopus" was grandly launched on Channel 1 of CCTV and the Life Channel of Shandong TV on May 1st, the "Weihai Sea Cucumber" brand has gradually developed into a city card with distinct regional characteristics, and at the same time, it has gradually become widely known and accepted by the whole country. In order to further improve the market awareness and credibility of "Weihai Sea Cucumber", promote the rooting of "Weihai Sea Cucumber" in the main sales market, and realize the brand communication of "Weihai Sea Cucumber", the major brands of Weihai sea cucumber represented by Haodangjia Sea Cucumber (www.haishen888.com), Gongpin Sea Cucumber, and Shuangju Sea Cucumber will once again join hands strongly and participate in the "2011 National Quality Aquatic Products (Beijing) Exhibition and Trading Fair" held at the "National Agricultural Exhibition Hall" in Beijing from October 1st to October 4th, 2011.

Exhibition preheats the Beijing sea cucumber market

The exhibition is jointly organized by the China Aquatic Products Circulation and Processing Association and the National Agricultural Exhibition Hall. It is a large-scale aquatic products exhibition and trading fair that will face the vast consumers, various restaurants, canteens, government cafeterias, and large and medium-sized purchasers in Beijing. It will focus on displaying high-quality and distinctive aquatic products from all over the country and strive to create the most influential aquatic products display and sales platform. As the main representative of high-end aquatic products, sea cucumbers will also become one of the main参展 products of this exhibition. At the same time, the Beijing sea cucumber market has always been the largest sea cucumber consumption market in the country, so the Beijing sea cucumber market has also become a must-contest place for major sea cucumber brands across the country. According to incomplete statistics, the number of Beijing sea cucumber specialty stores and mall counters has doubled in the past 2-3 years, and almost all well-known sea cucumber brands have specialty stores or franchise stores in Beijing. Participating in such an exhibition can not only greatly publicize their own brands but also obtain some orders, so this exhibition has also become an opportunity for many well-known sea cucumber brands to make a collective appearance.
