I. Event Introduction

II. Theme of This Event

Event Theme: The New Landscape of Mobile Terminals Through Developers' Eyes: The Impact and Variables of Nokia-Microsoft Cooperation (Shanghai)

Recently, Nokia announced that it would form an alliance with Microsoft to jointly promote the Windows Phone platform, adopting the WP system for its mainstream smartphones. This adds new variables and uncertainties to the increasingly fierce operating system competition. A significant impact will be the formation of a tripartite situation among smart device systems: iOS + Android + WP. Opportunities for HP's WebOS, BlackBerry's BBOS, and Samsung Bada will be very limited. Meanwhile, the fate of Symbian and MeeGo becomes even more intriguing.

Although it may seem like a "weak combination," Google's boss straightforwardly stated that "the combination of two chickens won't turn into an eagle," the strength generated by the combination of Microsoft's technology and enterprise application integration capabilities with Nokia's brand and hardware experience should not be underestimated.

For numerous mobile application development teams, challenges are also opportunities. How to balance resource allocation and market preparation under the current landscape; how to view development opportunities and costs on different operating system terminals; and how to effectively improve development efficiency across different operating system platforms—these questions are fundamental issues concerning the survival of mobile application developers and basic drivers determining the future direction of the industry.

The 10th NTalks event will invite active developers from different development platforms to discuss the impact of the Nokia-Microsoft cooperation and share their understanding and predictions about the future development prospects of each operating system platform.

This discussion will focus on the following aspects:

- The challenges faced by other platforms, such as Symbian and BlackBerry, and the way out for developers.

- Predictions about the weight and role of each platform in the future wireless industry landscape.

- The impact on various manufacturers and other aspects of the market.
