Fatal Mistakes Easily Made in Website Construction

by hebei56cn02c on 2012-02-12 17:25:00

Website, in terms of website appearance, content, column planning, and promotion, there are several details that people tend to overlook and some incorrect operations. Below are four main aspects:

1. Website Appearance

Many webmasters think that for a website design company, the most important thing is the content, while the appearance is not so important. In fact, this is not true. I am not suggesting that the website appearance should be overly flashy. Regarding Shijiazhuang website construction, these two points can reflect the lack of effort from webmasters. If visitors find that the webmaster does not care about the website appearance, they will not bother to look at the content either. If I see such websites, I would just take a quick glance at the content and then close it without visiting again. I believe many people share the same feeling.

2. Website Content

Content is the core part of a website. A website with rich content, in terms of Shijiazhuang website optimization, is beneficial in all aspects. Although we cannot make all our content original, we should at least have some original articles. We can also post some good articles to enrich the website content. Some people worry that copying articles may cause search engines to penalize them, so they carry out a large number of pseudo-originals. I think this is unnecessary and not very good. First, it shows disrespect to your visitors unless you can make the pseudo-original better than the original. Second, the more you do this, the more you will be led by the nose by search engines. Therefore, reduce pseudo-originals and increase original content.

3. Column Planning

Here, column planning does not refer to website navigation columns but rather efforts in other positions on the website according to user needs and细分栏目 etc. Here, I offer you a 16-character mantra: "What others don't have, I have; what others have, I have more; what others have more, I have better; what others have better, I specialize." Visitors should be able to understand the content of your website at a glance, without spending too much time figuring out the final content, giving them a good impression. This way, people will remember your site. I see some sites only plan navigation columns, which makes it difficult to retain visitors even if the website content is rich enough and good enough. The column planning of the homepage is very important and cannot be neglected.

4. Website Promotion

Nowadays, there are many ways to promote a website design company's website. First, many start promoting using various methods without doing well in the above three aspects. Actually, this is ineffective, although it brings traffic, but if it cannot retain users, it is not very meaningful. Second, when using soft article promotion, the traffic is just a fleeting phenomenon. Before long, your soft articles will be pushed down by others. I suggest you keep writing and posting soft articles to improve the exposure of your website. Each carefully planned soft article should also be reposted by yourself, so you need to prepare as many promotional places as possible. This allows your soft articles to exert their maximum power.
