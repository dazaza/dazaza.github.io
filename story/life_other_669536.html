How much love can start again

How many nights have I wandered alone with a glass of wine, quietly letting go of my lost heart as the darkness comes. One after another, heartaches flow straight down, and the worries I can't put down fill the bottles of liquor. Gradually, I've become accustomed to this way of life and this feeling of helplessness.

I often blame myself for not being able to blow away the memories of the past like the wind into your dreams, holding you gently in my arms. I shouldn't have given up my persistence for you, leaving behind this pain of longing now. I often wrestle with regret in my heart, but my hands still let you go, allowing you to fly freely and pursue the sky you desire. I regret not keeping you, thinking about those words that are now useless, feeling helpless about these emotional ups and downs. Writing this essay on the weight of love, I weigh everything from the past.

Sorrowfully, I wanted to love, but ended up so uneasy. Why does it always end in disappointment? I lament the bits and pieces of the past, like an unceasing stream. Regretfully carrying these painful memories, I wonder if this passionate heart will always wander outside the door of my heart, rejecting this restless loneliness. Yet, we meet in a sea of people, who knows if it's fate or just a confusing arrangement.

Destiny seems to deliberately play its hand, making us feel powerless. These years haven't been too bad, but it feels like something is missing inside, like a soul. Lamenting the present doesn't compare to the past, I gradually realize you're still the constant care in my heart.

How much love can start again? How many people are quietly waiting, hoping for its return? But the past has already become an unchanging landscape. Lingering only makes one breathless, causing endless sorrow like "Thoughts in the Silent Night" under the moonlight. When I understand the value of cherishing, it's already someone else's story. Should I pretend not to care and end it here, or continue to indulge in the dream-like beauty? But do I know if that love is still waiting there? How much love can start again? How many people are willing to wait silently?

When love has reached a point where it cannot be salvaged, do we still have the courage to keep loving? When I understand the value of cherishing and come back, all I see is emptiness at the edge. Believe in the feeling, believe in the existence of love. Missing opportunities will write our own sad stories in this song, chasing the sentiments of the past.

May love stay, sharing the details of life and emotions, lasting forever.

How much love can start again? How many people are willing to wait patiently?
