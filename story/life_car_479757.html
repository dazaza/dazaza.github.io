2011 Analysis Report of China's Automobile Accessories Industry

Analysis Report of China's Automobile Accessories Industry

According to the official prediction of the automobile industry, in 2011 alone, the sales revenue of the multi-category automobile accessory market dominated by automobile beauty will reach RMB 113 billion. By 2012, it will double again to reach RMB 500 billion. If the related supporting services are also taken into account, the market space will further expand. According to the official speculation, by 2012, the scale of the entire automobile aftermarket will reach RMB 880 billion, second only to Japan in Asia, ranking second in Asia.

Through a calm review of the lively scene of many car manufacturers entering the automobile accessories industry, we found that although China's automobile accessories industry has developed beyond its previous state, there are still objective problems that cannot be ignored.

The analysis report of China's automobile accessories industry believes:

1. The price of China's automobile accessories market is very transparent and open, which may also be another defect currently existing in the automobile accessories industry. You don't have to walk around the market much to find: the same brand of car audio sells for more than 300 yuan in the wholesale market, but when it reaches a high-end store, it costs at least over a thousand yuan and sometimes even thousands of yuan. The same brand of seat cushion only sells for dozens of yuan in the wholesale market, but in a high-end brand store, it can cost over a hundred yuan. Such a huge price difference not only confuses consumers but also baffles professionals.

2. The threshold for the automobile accessories industry is relatively low, yet the profit margin is extremely large. Numerous investors join the production and sales army of automobile accessories without proper guidance. Although there is a certain scale in sales, there aren't many well-known brands, and the recognition of automobile accessories isn't high. There are too many counterfeit products flooding the market.

3. Most companies in the industry neglect corporate culture, resulting in companies lacking soul. In today's competition among enterprises, the ultimate competition is the competition of corporate culture. Compared with the successful corporate culture of whole vehicle enterprises, automobile accessory enterprises lack strength in shaping corporate culture, leading to the lack of soul in enterprises.

4. The homogenization of automobile accessories is very serious. Without innovation, they only focus on profits. Currently, there are not many products in the market that clearly reflect individuality, and their designs are simple, and the craftsmanship is not delicate enough. Monotonous products obviously find it hard to meet the needs of consumers. With the rapid increase in private cars, consumers' demand for automobile accessories shows a strong trend towards personalization, requiring more personalized automobile decorations that reflect the individuality of car owners.

5. The automobile beauty industry is not professional enough, and beauticians have become a bottleneck constraining the development of the industry. Taking automobile beauty as an example, automobile beauty technology has already experienced the development of early technologies such as waxing, coating, and glazing.

After several years of development, the pattern and development trend of the automobile accessories industry have undergone significant changes. The "integrated" service and "one-stop" sales chain operation system will become the mainstream of China's automobile accessories market. Chinese and foreign-funded enterprises are all focusing on this point, using large and medium-sized cities like Shanghai, Guangzhou, and Beijing as bases to accelerate the integration of resources and domestic layout. The automobile accessories industry is transitioning from the previous 'dirty, messy, poor' to scaling up, standardizing, and professionalizing. Opportunities and challenges coexist, and only by recognizing the situation can one seize opportunities in development and achieve greater success.
