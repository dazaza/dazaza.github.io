Viewbar Latest News: Coming Soon for Download!! (April 30, 2007)

We estimate that everyone should have already received this email. We’re posting it here for you. If you haven’t checked your email yet, please do so immediately!

Dear ***,

We are about to release the AGLOCO Viewbar software and would like to inform all members of its features once released.

Every member will receive an email notifying them when they can download and use the Viewbar software. The email will provide detailed instructions on how to download the software from agloco.com. Over a two-week period, we will schedule 50,000 members per day to download the software in the order they registered.

Before the first batch of download invitations is sent out, you will receive an email notifying you that the AGLOCO site will be temporarily unavailable for a few hours as we transfer the Viewbar download system from the test server to the main server.

Please note the following points:

**AGLOCO Hours:** The number of hours in your AGLOCO account will continue to accumulate (currently capped at five hours per month). Members earn hours by active internet usage. The Viewbar has an indicator light that turns green when your hours are being accumulated. The effective online time of the members you refer will be credited to your account at a 25% ratio. For example, if one of your referred members spends five hours online, your total referral hours will increase by 1.25 hours. You can only receive referral hours based on your own online hours (i.e., if you spend three hours online and your referral spends four hours online, you will only receive credit for the first three hours proportionally). AGLOCO will generate these hours monthly and cumulatively track them. Payments and other distributions to members will be calculated based on these hours. The Viewbar includes a deactivation button which allows you to turn off and hide the Viewbar at any time.

**Initial Release Supports Windows Only:** The initial release of the Viewbar software supports only Windows Vista, XP, 2000 Pro, and 2000 Server systems. Subsequently, we will release versions compatible with Mac and Linux systems. Until then, Mac/Linux users can still log into any Windows computer to accumulate their five hours. The Viewbar is compatible with browsers IE 5.5 and above, as well as Firefox 1.5 and above.

**Cash Distribution:** To become a long-term sustainable entity, AGLOCO distributes cash from its effective capital flow (revenue minus costs). Therefore, don't expect cash payments in the first few months, as AGLOCO needs time to collect fees from advertisers, and collected fees must exceed operating costs before cash can be distributed to members. We will periodically update members on our financial status.

**Multiple Users Can Use One Viewbar:** As mentioned earlier, multiple users can use the same Viewbar software on one computer. Only one download of Viewbar is necessary per computer, as each member can log in and out using their respective member ID and password (obviously, only one member ID can accumulate Viewbar hours at a time). Please note that only one instance of the Viewbar can run at a time.

**One-Time Download:** Version 1.0 of the Viewbar focuses on simplicity and ease of use but also includes automatic update functionality. Once downloaded, the Viewbar will automatically update itself when new features are available. Future features will include direct cashback functionality, where a portion of purchases made through sponsor websites will be returned to the member's AGLOCO account (as described in the official AGLOCO blog post: http://blog.agloco.com/index.php/post35/).

We look forward to your active participation in the AGLOCO community.

Dan Jorgensen

Member Coordinator
