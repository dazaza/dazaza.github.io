The moment a meteor streaked across the sky

Times Online Submission

I have the ability to catch a meteor and turn an instant into eternity. But I don't know if you will give me the chance to catch the meteor and make it eternal. You are the most beautiful meteor in my dreams, and I caught you. But I failed to hold onto you forever, and after seven years, I might have been forgotten by you! But you are deeply rooted in my memory. Your figure is still so unforgettable, just like yesterday.

All the memories of the past have disappeared. Do you know how sad I was at first? Every time I wanted to confess my feelings to you, I didn't have the courage. Why is loving someone so difficult? What is love? Is it about loving each other forever? Is it about spending one's entire life together? Or does it just mean putting my ring on your finger and being with you forever? I think all these things will come true in the near future. I once asked myself whether I should wait for this late-coming love, but I never gave up in the end. I believe that I truly love you.

My love for you is no longer what it used to be. Now, I love you even more, loving you until the seas dry up and the rocks crumble. Although we are far apart, the memories from our childhood are vividly close. Where are you now? And where is the place where my love for you takes root and sprouts? Loving someone requires giving. Haven't I given enough? For these seven years, I've been waiting painfully, waiting for the love I hope to harvest. But what I've harvested is only an empty dream. Even if just a small part of it is real, I would already be very satisfied.

Now I really want to see you and find the dream we left behind, a dream that will never be completed. But I don't know if this dream is just an empty one. In the dream, there's only you and me. But I'm already very happy about this. Could this be what people often refer to as "having fate but no destiny"? Why does heaven play such jokes on me? I can't bear this pain anymore. Are we really impossible? I don't believe it. I want you to tell me yourself. I don't believe that fate would toy with people like this. But why hasn't Cupid's arrow of love come yet? Why has the red thread of destiny from Yue Lao come so late? Is it because they don't want to show kindness, or don't want to give me this one opportunity? Or do they think I don't deserve it? But none of these matter to me. All I care about is my promise to you. I don't have sweet words like Zhou Xingchi, but I will use my own way to make a promise to you, letting you understand how much I love you. During these years of waiting for you, I've thought a lot. There were moments of despair and discouragement, but I never gave up. Whenever I feel lonely, the person who appears in my mind is always you. I can't forget you anymore. No matter what your final response is, I believe that these seven years of waiting were worth it because I am waiting for the person I have always deeply loved. Some people might say I'm foolish, but I don't care. I think it's worth being foolish for you!!

Now you have become my spiritual support. Without you, it's like fish leaving the sea; without you, it's like the soul leaving the body; without you, the earth wouldn't even rotate. Is loving someone wrong? Some people say it is, and I agree, because I fell in love with someone who could never love me in return, but I will love her forever!

I firmly believe that from the moment I fell in love with you, it was already destined that you would be my love for a lifetime, and it will never change. If it's wrong, let it keep being wrong!
