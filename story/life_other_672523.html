If there is a next life,

Wife, are you alright? I miss you so much. I don't know how long a lifetime is, but I do know that every second without you is a torment for me.

I can no longer count the number of times I've shed tears or how many times I've whispered your name in my heart, but I truly miss you so much. I eagerly await the end of this life and the arrival of the next.

Wife, without you by my side, I have forgotten what it feels like to smile. Can I ever experience that happy smile again? I'm so afraid to look at your pictures, especially the one where I'm holding you, but I can't help but open the album and look at them. I stare at them for hours, and when I come back to reality, my eyes are always wet.

Every time I close my eyes, I always hope that when I open them, you will appear in front of me and call me "husband" with a pout. Haha! I know that's impossible. I'm so afraid, I'm afraid of every day, each day is a torment for me. In the past few days, I keep having nightmares, and in those dreams, I always see the scene of you leaving me. Do you know how real it feels even in my dreams?

Stupid, you used to say that you always felt I was melancholy and wanted to be close to me. Do you know that after you got close to me, my world completely changed. Since then, whenever you were by my side, I always smiled happily. You said I looked foolish when I smiled, and cold when I didn't. I wish I could continue being foolish towards you forever. I don't like spicy food, vinegar, or cilantro, but you love them very much.

Every time I take you out, you insist on eating these things, and I feel incredibly happy watching you eat. When you can't bear the spiciness, I find it amusing, and then you force me to eat as well. I reluctantly eat with you, and in that moment, I feel completely satisfied.

When we go to the park, you lie down on the grass with your eyes closed enjoying the sun, and you make me lie down too, but I refuse.

Do you know that while you had your eyes closed, I was actually looking at you all the time! At that moment, I thought if I could be with you for a lifetime, I would be willing to do anything. Later, a fortune-teller came, and I lied to him saying that we were already a couple. The fortune-teller said we were really compatible, and after hearing that, I felt extremely happy. You also laughed foolishly, and I knew you must have been ecstatic inside. Haha!

During the conference in Suzhou, I was always distracted, thinking about you all the time. I kept turning around to look at you, and if I couldn't see you, I would feel very anxious. I remember I was slightly upset with you during the time in Suzhou. Do you know why?

It was because I saw you chatting with other men, and since I didn't know you were familiar with them, I wondered why you could chat so enthusiastically with people you weren't close to. I was just a little jealous. Haha! Actually, I get jealous too. That night, our training went very late, and we returned to the hotel quite late. You could have gone back earlier, but you insisted on staying with me. It was so cold, and you were hungry, tired, and freezing. At that moment, I was deeply moved and wanted to hold you tightly to warm you up, but there were too many people around, and I couldn't do that. Seeing you shiver from the cold made me feel terrible.

I asked if you were cold, and you pretended to be strong saying it was okay. What a complete fool. After returning to the hotel, I kept complaining that I was hungry and asked you to go out and eat with me. You kept asking how many people were going, and I listed a lot of names, making you lose interest and say you wanted to sleep. Later, I realized that you wanted to go out alone with me. I was so stupid at that time.

Every time we ate, you always tried to secure the best seat for me, not letting anyone else sit there. I was so happy. Although those few days were tiring, I have never been this happy before.

After coming back from Suzhou, you eventually couldn't maintain the ambiguous relationship between us and confessed to me. Perhaps if you hadn't confessed, we might still be together now, happily. But when you hugged me, I couldn't refuse you. Maybe I had been waiting for this result for a long time.

Later, our love became so exhausting. Facing the challenges of reality and gossip, you eventually gave in, but how could I let you go like that? I knew if I let go, we would have no future, not even the previous ambiguity. I simply couldn't do that. I worked very hard, ready to give up everything, even fight against the whole world. You have become my entire world, do you know that?

For a period of time, we finally managed to be together, and during that time, I was so happy. I instantly felt like I had everything, and all the previous pain and effort were worth it. You said you wanted to have a child with me and asked what the child's name should be. I said "Zhao Shi Orphan," and you foolishly agreed. You said the child's features should look like mine, and the skin like yours. At that moment, I was truly so happy. But who knew happiness would be so fleeting?

Wife, I miss you so much! We agreed on the next life; in the next life, I will find you first. I will hold onto you tightly and never let go.

In this life, we loved each other so exhaustingly. In the next life, no matter how bitter or tiring it may be, we must stay together, alright? I will definitely recognize you. You told me not to drink the Mengpo soup, fearing that I might forget you. Foolish, some things, some people, can never be forgotten no matter what. Wife, I am eagerly awaiting the next life.

In the next life, you must wait for me, wait for me to be the first person in your life, wait for me to take you away. This life, what you owe me, you must repay fully in the next life.

In the next life, if you don't leave, I won't abandon either! Your loving husband...
