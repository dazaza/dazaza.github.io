Introduction:

Detective Ratch's wife and daughter died in an accident. For years he has been living in his memories, even seeing the figure of his daughter frequently. Ratch blames himself for the death of his wife and daughter, and this self-reproach makes him more reclusive. Ratch accepts a mission to investigate a mysterious woman named Joanna. Joanna often appears in various cities under different aliases, and those men who have evil intentions towards her ultimately die at her hands. Ratch tracks Joanna through several cities. But at this moment, Ratch is not in a hurry to capture Joanna, instead, he is very curious about Joanna's motive for killing, and he is also deeply attracted by Joanna's cold beauty, as if there is an invisible force pulling Ratch. Whether they are street scammers or millionaires, even police officers, any man who is attracted by Joanna's beauty and tries to take advantage of her is mercilessly killed by Joanna. Ratch hides in the dark, monitoring and peeping on Joanna all the way, but he unknowingly gets deeply entangled in his infatuation with Joanna. In a motel, Joanna was held hostage by a man and almost lost her life. Fortunately, Ratch arrived in time to save Joanna, but at this moment Joanna was injected with drugs and became unconscious, and she didn't even know that she was pregnant. Joanna finally had a miscarriage due to exhaustion from running around, and Ratch took care of Joanna like a wife. But when Joanna woke up, she knew nothing about it. Ratch changed Joanna's helpless fate through his efforts, and for Ratch, it was as if his wish to find back his wife and daughter had come true.

Joanna met Alex at the airport. Although Alex, a billionaire, was blind, he liked Joanna and proposed to her. In order to prevent Joanna from killing Alex, Ratch did not hesitate to shoot the car tire of Alex, but unexpectedly the car went off course and caused an accident, and Alex was killed, while Joanna survived. Joanna left the city where Alex was and came to Alaska after many turns. Several people killed by Joanna before were discovered, and the police were looking for her everywhere. Ratch felt that he could no longer sit idly by, watching more people be killed by Joanna until she was eventually arrested by the police. Ratch began to get close to Joanna, trying to understand her inner world and lead her out of her current life. Before the police arrived, Ratch saved Joanna and took her to his temporary residence. But Joanna mistakenly thought that Ratch was the same kind of man who wanted to take advantage of her, and she raised her gun to him...
