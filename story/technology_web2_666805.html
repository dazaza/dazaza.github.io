For a long time, our country has adopted the economic growth mode of sacrificing resources and environment, and dazzling GDP at the cost of high emissions and inefficiency. The "Eleventh Five-Year" plan for national environmental protection aims to thoroughly implement the scientific development concept, change the way of economic development, phase out backward production capacity with high consumption and pollution as soon as possible, actively develop circular economy and promote resource conservation, strengthen research and practice of circular economy, strictly control the development of high energy-consuming, high material-consuming and high water-consuming industries, close down enterprises that destroy resources, pollute the environment and do not meet safety production conditions according to law, take technological innovation as power, take industrial development as support, strive to improve the level of environmental protection technology, and focus on supporting enterprises with low input, high output, low consumption, less emission, recyclable and sustainable development.

As the first batch in medical industry responding to government's energy-saving renovation incentive policy, Chaoyang Hospital in Beijing, Beijing Children's Hospital, People's Hospital of Henan Province and others have chosen central air conditioning electricity saving renovation to save energy and reduce enterprise costs. Take Chaoyang Hospital in Beijing as an example, the hospital consumes nearly ten million kilowatt-hours of electricity each year, and its annual electricity bill is about 5 million yuan, which is the second largest expenditure of the hospital. In February 2008, the hospital launched the innovation project of the old building when it was opened, focusing on energy saving renovation in the operating room central air conditioning project, introducing polluted air conditioning unit, purified fresh air unit, ultrasonic air conditioning humidifier and other fully automatic control equipment. The original central air conditioning humidifier used electrode-type and dry-steam humidifier, and only this item costs 600,000 yuan in electricity each year. The new humidification system adopts Himeju ultrasonic humidifier, saving 70% electricity, and saving 540,000 yuan in enterprise costs only in this item each year.

Electricity saving and energy consumption reduction is the key to achieve the energy consumption reduction target during the "Eleventh Five-Year" period, and the market demand for electricity saving products increases. Beijing Himeju Electric Co., Ltd. follows the national policies, perceives development opportunities, closely meets customer needs, continuously innovates science and technology, and successively launches multiple energy-saving products, using high-tech means together with users to make traditional industries achieve the effect of energy saving, emission reduction and energy consumption reduction. Himeju Company will take central air conditioning energy-saving humidifier as one of the main products of future industry in 2009.

Beijing Himeju Electric Co., Ltd. is a domestic new high-tech technology company. After receiving the humidification technology energy-saving transformation requirement from Chaoyang Hospital in Beijing, the company cooperated multiple times to break through, research and repeatedly experiment. Considering the cleanliness requirement of the hospital operating room and the electricity-saving requirement of the hospital party, the company developed an ultrasonic humidifier for the clean electricity-saving of the hospital operating room central air conditioning. This humidifier does not produce standing water, does not need water-blocking board, and prevents the reproduction of bacteria in the unit duct. This humidifier completely replaces the old-style humidifiers such as electrode type and dry steam type, and has advantages that the old-style humidifiers do not have, such as product stability, fast humidification speed, clean and environmental protection, and electricity consumption is only one-tenth of the old-style.

From the perspective of Beijing's electricity energy consumption, Beijing's summer electricity load gap reaches 1.3 million kilowatts. Beijing Development and Reform Commission has introduced ten measures to adjust electricity use, mainly including opening up sources to increase supply, power grid transformation, price adjustment, technical power restriction, etc. In order to control electricity quantity, reduce loss, and save cost, various enterprises in Beijing have chosen electricity saving transformation, achieving control of power loss through advanced technology.
