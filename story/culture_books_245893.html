After an accident, modern girl Xiao Meier travels through time to the ancient era, transforming into Empress Mu - Nalan Muruo.

Facing the concubines in the harem vying for favor and power, the shadowy Empress Dowager whose intentions are hard to discern, the mysterious Queen who can disappear at will, and the passionate and distinct female knight... In the face of these women each harboring their own thoughts, how should she respond? Should she submit humbly, or confront them head-on? Or perhaps...

Facing the power-hungry and cold monarch Long Yan, the world-renowned martial general Nalan Zhenyun, the handsome and icy guard Lichong, and the cunning and flirtatious Nalan Zhansnow... In the face of these outstanding men, which path should she choose? To be the favored consort of the emperor, or the beloved wife of the general? Or perhaps...
