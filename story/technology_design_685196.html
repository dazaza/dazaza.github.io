Nie Meisheng: Real estate industry urgently needs repositioning

by w9pzjf58625 on 2012-03-01 14:05:31

News Translation: [Reporter An Yuan] Yesterday, Nie Mei, the president of the Real Estate Chamber of Commerce of the All-China Federation of Industry and Commerce, participated in the "Urbanization and Regional Economic Development" forum in the Jinnan District and expressed that expanding the concept of real estate will be an important way for future development enterprises to survive. She also pointed out that the current housing market's sequential data has already shown signs of market stabilization.

Regarding the overall situation of the real estate market, President Nie Meisheng reiterated her four-word maxim: "The market shows confusion, regulation enters deep water, the industry repositions, and enterprises face adjustments," www.qqzajinhua.com. "Real estate sequential data is very important because there are such international conventions. If a certain price's sequential change is within ±0.5%, whether it rises 0.5% or falls 0.5%, it indicates that the overall trend of the market is stable. From the various statistical figures released by the National Bureau of Statistics in June, it can be seen that all indicators of real estate have fallen below the same period indicators." While pointing out that the indicators of the housing market are trending towards stability, President Nie Meisheng also stated, "From the perspective of the first half of the year's housing market situation, the overall momentum of real estate sales this year has not declined. Therefore, the regulation of the real estate market will not be relaxed. So, one important issue for the real estate market this year is affordable housing."

For the future development of the real estate industry, President Nie Meisheng frankly stated: "The real estate industry urgently needs to be repositioned." President Nie emphasized: "Under the background of real estate market regulation and industry integration, the concept of the real estate market needs to be expanded, and enterprises also face the trend of growth and strong alliances."

"Our economy is moving towards domestic demand-driven development, and the investment-driven force is declining." Combining with the situation of the Tianjin market, President Nie indicated that the five distinctive towns planned in Jinnan District—Jinnan New City and the four new towns of Shuanggang, Geju, BaliTai, and Xiaozhan—through the overall development of these towns, introducing outstanding domestic and international development enterprises, taking on the responsibility of trend-driven development, represents the trend of a new real estate development model.

The transformation of the real estate industry requires extending its connotations, and President Nie believed that shopping, medical care, elderly care, and other multiple urban and social functions will all become the connotations that the real estate industry needs to carry in the future. "In the future, the real estate industry will play a carrying role, needing to integrate and coexist with the development of emerging urban industries."

President Nie Meisheng frankly stated that the single model of commercial residential properties could develop for at most another 20 years, and the direction of urbanization would accelerate the development of urbanization models along with changes in infrastructure and urban layouts.
