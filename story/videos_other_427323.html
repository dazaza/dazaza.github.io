Male sentenced to 6 and a half years for stealing 27,000 yuan from captain when released from labor camp

by dehua900 on 2011-05-08 09:42:04

The police quickly investigated and gathered evidence, soon locking onto the suspect Yu Peng. At 23 years old, Yu Peng had been sentenced to six months in prison by the Dongchang District Court for theft in 2008. In November of the same year, he was sentenced to one year of re-education through labor for fraudulent behavior. During his re-education period, Yu Peng was responsible for cleaning Captain Wang Lin's office. On November 7th, the police arrested Yu Peng at an internet cafe in Laozhan. After interrogation, Yu Peng confessed to stealing 27,000 yuan from Wang Lin.

After being released from the labor camp, Yu Peng extravagantly spent Wang Lin’s private savings, buying branded phones, wearing designer clothes, and staying in luxury hotels, flaunting his wealth for over a month. When caught, all that remained for Wang Lin was 210 yuan in cash and an old used phone.

On January 25th, the Dongchang District Court announced its first-instance verdict: Yu Peng was found guilty of theft, as a repeat offender with significant amounts involved, and was sentenced to six and a half years in prison.

Xinhua News Agency reports from Tonghua (by reporter Lu Hong, correspondent Yu Lijuan): "I've been secretly saving this money for nearly 20 years without my wife knowing, and I kept it in the drawer. I never expected someone would take it all..." On November 4th last year, Captain Wang Lin (a pseudonym) of a team at the Tonghua Labor Camp opened the drawer and found that the 27,000 yuan inside had disappeared, immediately reporting the incident to the police.

Back in the dormitory, taking advantage when no one was around, Yu Peng took the money out of the bag and hid it. Then he worked with Hua to make a pillowcase. Using the opportunity to deliver the pillowcase, Yu Peng secretly returned the bag to the drawer in Wang Lin's office.

On the evening of September 29th last year, before dinner, Yu Peng found Hua, who had taken over cleaning Wang Lin's office, and said: "Tomorrow, I will be released. I want to make a pillowcase for Captain Wang, can you open the door of his office for me?" After Hua opened the door to Wang Lin's office, while Hua was fetching fabric from under the fish tank cabinet, Yu Peng secretly took out the black briefcase containing the money and hid it in his clothes.

Yu Peng confessed that during his time cleaning Wang Lin's office, he discovered money in the drawer but did not dare to touch it at the time.
