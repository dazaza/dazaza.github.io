Today, I learned from Hainan Impression Company that since the grand premiere of "Impression·Hainan Island," a large-scale live performance directed by Zhang Yimou and his "Iron Triangle Director" team on the evening of March 16th at the Sea Urchin Theatre on the West Coast, the box office sales have been continuously booming.

Recently, many enthusiastic citizens have called our newspaper's news hotline to inquire about the ticketing hotline for "Impression·Hainan Island" and how to purchase tickets for the performance. "Zhang Yimou is the director of the Olympics, and just based on the box office guarantee of a renowned director, my family must see 'Impression·Hainan Island,'" said Ms. Wang, a resident of Haikou. She also mentioned that the appearance and design of the Sea Urchin Theatre are very distinctive and worth seeing. Mr. Chen believes that he has already gained a general understanding of the large-scale performance "Impression·Hainan Island" through various media, but still feels unsatisfied. "I must personally experience this visual feast on-site."

According to the introduction, the Impression series of live performances is an art form pioneered by Zhang Yimou, Wang Chaoge, and Fan Yue's "Iron Triangle Team," blending culture with real-life scenery. "Impression·Hainan Island" features more than 300 actors, creating the vast momentum of the sea within a limited space. Through a unique sense of time-space crossover and enjoyable interactivity, it conveys to the audience the concept of getting close to the sea, relaxing the mind, and fully embracing the beauty of life. The Sea Urchin Theatre has over 1500 seats for the audience. One-third of the "Sea Urchin" shell is "missing," allowing the audience inside the theatre to maintain an open view: the beach, coconut trees, boundless sea, and ships on the sea are all in sight. "Impression·Hainan Island" is the first work of the Impression series created by Zhang Yimou, Wang Chaoge, and Fan Yue after the Beijing Olympic Games and Paralympic Games opening and closing ceremonies, and it is the fourth work of the Impression series following "Impression·Liu Sanjie," "Impression·Lijiang" Snow Mountain Edition, and "Impression·West Lake." "Impression·Hainan Island" will undergo a one-month trial performance, followed by modifications and improvements, before its official premiere during the Boao Forum for Asia in mid-April.

Relevant officials from Hainan Impression Company told reporters that the large-scale live performance "Impression·Hainan Island" is staged every night at 8:30 PM at the Sea Urchin Theatre. Citizens can buy tickets directly at the Impression Theatre ticket office or order tickets by calling the ticket hotline at 60898888. When purchasing tickets and watching the performance, citizens can take bus routes 35, 37, 40, or 50 and get off at Holiday Beach Station. Regarding ticket prices, the tickets for "Impression·Hainan Island" are divided into three categories: regular seats, guest seats, and VIP seats. Regular seats cost 238 yuan per ticket, guest seats cost 288 yuan, and VIP seats cost 688 yuan each. Author: Zhang Yi Wu Liang
