The neighborhood committee lists the New Year cracker as office supply for reimbursement

by wjwf8179 on 2012-02-22 12:44:43

□ By reporter You Jie After the Spring Festival, on the first day back to work, the Nanxu Community Residents' Committee in Tianqiao District set off about 20 boxes of "King Cannon" fireworks and strings of firecrackers with extended arms. On January 30th, after our newspaper reported on this event, it drew strong reactions from all sectors of society. In response, relevant departments of the Jinan Municipal Discipline Inspection Commission, the Civilized Office, and the Tianqiao District Party Committee and People's Government all paid attention.

Relevant responsible persons all expressed that it is inappropriate for public service institutions, organizations, and government departments to set off a large amount of "opening cannon" fireworks, and they should not use public funds for such activities. How can we talk about building a resource-saving and environment-friendly society amidst the loud sounds and swirling smoke of "luxurious opening cannons"?

Pursuing Funding: Fireworks were purchased, firecrackers were collected

On January 30th, the reporter went to the Nanxu Residents' Committee. Vice Secretary Xing informed the reporter that setting off "opening cannons" on the first day back to work every year is for good luck, starting the new year’s work with a lively atmosphere, which has become a routine at the committee.

"Our village has been an advanced village every year. In recent years, the enterprises in the village have done well, much better than before, so setting off some fireworks for celebration is quite normal," Mr. Xing said. The fireworks were bought by the residents' committee before the New Year, and some were set off during the Spring Festival. The remaining ones were feared to be dangerous if left in the warehouse, so they were all set off on the first day back to work. The firecrackers were brought by everyone from their homes, and since the courtyard of the residents' committee is relatively spacious, it was safer to set them off there.

When asked how these fireworks would be reimbursed, Mr. Xing said they would be reimbursed as office supplies, "The fireworks were purchased from a related unit, and we still haven't paid them."

Financial Pursuit: Nanxu Residents' Committee claims self-financing

During the interview, when asked about the financial situation of the Nanxu Residents' Committee, relevant responsible persons stated that they are self-financed and receive no subsidies from higher-ups.

"In the past few years, there were many enterprises in the village. In recent years, due to demolition, many have moved away, but in the last two years, the village's income has increased significantly, much better than before," Mr. Xing said, "There are four or five hundred people in the village, and each resident received more than 8,000 yuan in dividends last year, which does not include the rice, flour, and other welfare distributed during festivals."

From this, the reporter learned from other residents' committees that due to urban development needs, newly established residents' committees in recent years are entirely funded by higher-ups, with activity funds allocated based on the number of staff, but often insufficient; while some older residents' committees located in the urban-rural fringe have multiple collective incomes, such as collective enterprises, house rentals, tax returns, various collective rewards, etc., making them relatively wealthy. The Nanxu Residents' Committee belongs to the latter category.

Relevant Departments: Setting off "opening cannons" should be moderate, and public funds should not be used

In the afternoon of the 30th, a relevant person in charge of the Jinan City Civilization Office told reporters that it is inappropriate for grassroots party and government organizations to set off a large amount of "opening cannons." Under the trend of "luxurious opening cannons," how can we talk about building a resource-saving and environment-friendly society? How can ordinary people trust or like such public service institutions or government departments that seek good luck and auspicious signs, and how can they willingly accept services provided by such institutions?

This person in charge stated that as a folk tradition, setting off fireworks during the Spring Festival can be understood. In the absence of relevant regulations, banning "opening cannons" for government and party departments will require a gradual process. "Shenyang spent five years formulating relevant regulations to eliminate the traditional practice of using incense during memorial ceremonies. Now, Shenyang citizens basically use flowers for ancestor worship. Their practices are worth learning from."

A relevant person in charge of the Jinan City Discipline Inspection Commission stated that the phenomenon of excessive setting off of "opening cannons" not only exists in residents' committees but also in some government departments. The phenomenon of setting off "luxurious opening cannons" reflects, to a certain extent, the extravagant and wasteful style of some departments. If this continues, it will corrupt social morals, hurt public virtue, and lose public support; the phenomenon of using public funds to set off "opening cannons" must be banned.

In response, relevant departments of the Tianqiao District stated that they respect the custom of setting off "opening cannons" to seek good fortune, but standardization and guidance are needed.
