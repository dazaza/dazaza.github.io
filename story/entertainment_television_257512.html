Synopsis:

"Falcon Squadron" tells the story of Ma He, a test pilot who witnesses his comrade Xiao Bang's heroic sacrifice as he protects a village on the ground during an aircraft malfunction. Subsequently, Ma He is transferred to the Falcon Squadron. Despite his extraordinary flying skills, Ma He repeatedly experiences hallucinations of the final moments of Xiao Bang’s sacrifice during various exercises. After a series of setbacks, Ma He finally becomes a strong pilot in the battle to defend the country's islands and reefs and in the joint military exercises with foreign countries...

Since the beginning of the 21st century, the Chinese Air Force's strategic campaign has shifted from "territorial air defense" to "offensive and defensive capabilities," extending national security concepts beyond domestic borders. To adapt to this trend, the Air Force decided to establish a multi-model aircraft unit known as the Falcon Detachment, tasked with independently winning local wars, responding to emergencies, and counter-terrorism situations. The detachment consists of various advanced aircraft models, including J-10 fighter jets, Su-30 bombers, J-8 electronic warfare aircraft, airborne early warning aircraft, electronic interference aircraft, reconnaissance aircraft, transport aircraft, aerial refueling aircraft, and more. The best pilots from across the army are selected, and the Aviation Industry Corporation of China service team provides comprehensive support to ensure rapid combat readiness for national security missions.

Ma He, a test pilot with exceptional flying skills, is eager to join this detachment but is also tempted by the lucrative material benefits offered by a local airline company. When the division commander decides to send Xiao Bang instead of Ma He to the detachment, Ma He feels that the Air Force does not value him and angrily requests retirement. During a flight confrontation, Xiao Bang's aircraft malfunctions, forcing him to parachute out. In desperation, Ma He shoots down the out-of-control plane that was about to cross the national border, leaving him with psychological scars. Seeing Ma He's calmness under pressure, Captain Chen Ning of the Falcon Detachment decides to transfer Ma He into the team. Feeling disheartened, Ma He only agrees to replace Xiao Bang after being scolded by him.

After joining the Falcon Detachment, Ma He quickly demonstrates superior tactical qualities, competing with another elite pilot, Wu Weitao. Captain Chen Ning emphasizes the team's philosophy: "One for all, all for one." At no time should anyone act heroically; they must follow the bigger picture and work collaboratively. Soon, the detachment receives a mission: a terrorist group in Central Asia causes unrest, severely threatening regional security in Central Asian countries, including China. The Shanghai Cooperation Organization decides to jointly strike against the terrorist organization, assigning the air strike task to the Falcon Detachment. Initially, Ma He faces psychological barriers during the battle, constantly experiencing hallucinations of shooting down Xiao Bang's plane. Under Captain Chen Ning's severe criticism, Ma He exhibits another extreme, disregarding the combat plan and launching an attack with Wu Weitao without authorization. Although the attack succeeds, Ma He is still disciplined by Captain Chen Ning for violating battlefield discipline and demoted to the reserve team, losing his combat qualification. Later in the battle, the detachment encounters a major problem; the enemy's ammunition depot is unusually sturdy, and regular bombs cannot destroy it. Ma He rises to the challenge, attacking successfully using methods beyond human limits, but his aircraft sustains damage and makes an emergency landing at a small airport. The Aviation Industry Corporation of China service team rushes to the small airport day and night, repairing Ma He's aircraft, during which Ma He develops feelings for engineer Jiang Jingchao. Through various signs, Ma He begins to doubt the authenticity of the war and eventually discovers that it was just a highly realistic exercise, completely fooling the pilots. Unable to tolerate being deceived, Ma He exposes the truth, causing the exercise to be forcibly terminated. As a result, Ma He is dismissed by Captain Chen Ning, who plans to expel him from the Falcon Detachment. Back at his old unit, Ma He is inspired by Xiao Bang and educated by the heroes of the Korean War, helping him understand the true meaning of "one for all, all for one." After returning to the team, Ma He is only allowed to conduct research with the Aviation Industry Corporation of China service team and is forbidden from participating in combat. At this time, the Falcon Detachment receives a real war mission, and Ma He can only serve as a porter for the missile battalion. During the attack on foreign forces occupying China's islands and reefs, Ma He strongly requests to participate in the battle, reclaiming the islands with his comrades. However, Wu Weitao sacrifices himself to protect the airborne early warning aircraft by blocking the enemy missiles with his own aircraft, elevating their friendship to an extremely high level. In the subsequent Sino-foreign joint military exercises, the detachment faces serious challenges when confronting foreign troops. The detachment devises a creative attack plan where paratrooper Wu Weitao parachutes down to laser-designate the target, guiding the detachment's attack. Ma He then pilots the high-performance stealth aircraft modified by the Aviation Industry Corporation of China service team, perfectly completing the mission. After growing mentally and spiritually, Ma He becomes a true invincible warrior.

The support period of the Aviation Industry Corporation of China service team has ended, and engineer Jiang Jingchao is leaving. To focus on flying, Ma He ends his emotional relationship with Jiang Jingchao, but both reach a new spiritual height. Their mutual affection embodies a higher ideal.

"One for all, all for one," the Falcon Detachment rapidly enhances from technical equipment to fighting spirit within a short time. They fulfill their mission and truly become a valiant team worthy of the nation's trust.
