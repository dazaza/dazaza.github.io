Help you manage social media easily: Secondbrain

by xjyzhenai on 2008-11-02 15:39:05

Social media may be a strange word to us. What is social media?

Let's start with the definition of the concept.

Social media is a new type of online media that gives users great space to participate in. It has the following characteristics:

#183; Engagement: Social media can inspire active contributions and feedback from interested people, and it blurs the line between media and audience

#183; Open: Most social media are free to participate in, and they estimate people commenting, giving feedback, and sharing information. There are virtually no barriers to participating in and using content in social media - except for protected content.

#183; Communication: Traditional media take the form of "broadcast", the content is transmitted from the media to the user, one-way flow. The advantage of social media is that content spreads bidirectional between media and users, which forms a kind of communication.

Dialogue: Traditional media deliver content one-way to the audience in the form of "broadcast". Social media, on the other hand, is often considered to have the characteristics of two-way dialogue.

#183; Community: In social media, people can quickly form a community and communicate fully on topics of common interest such as photography, political topics or TV dramas.

#183; Connectivity: Most social media have strong connectivity, integrating multiple media through links.

The most common forms of social media are blogs, wikis, podcasts, forums, social networks, and content communities.

Secondbrain: SecondBrain. It can organize your social media in the same place, so that you can unified and convenient management.

image

Secondbrain's main idea has three parts: organizing your library, sharing the Web with you, and discovering great resources.

image

Signing up for Secondbrain gives you your own library and a secondary domain name of "yours.secondbrain.com", along with its corresponding console. Secondbrain uses your library as the hub of a collection of social media resources you care about and can share them with your friends.

image

After you share your resources, you can see which followers you have from all over the world. The concept of SNS connects you with friends who have the same interests as you. In Secondbrain, which is all about sharing, you can also share other people's collections and visit other people's libraries.

Secondbrain supports resource import for most social media services. For example: bookmarking services, blog resources import, photos, videos and other multimedia resources import.

image

These resources from different services are imported and integrated into Secondbrain's personal library. After the experience, Secondbrain's service is pretty good. More emphasis on user experience, close to the concept of socialization. It's all good, except Secondbrain's official short name is too bitchy.
