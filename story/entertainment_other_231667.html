QQ Pet is a casual养成 (breeding) game where the pet you adopt will accompany you on your desktop, grow with you, share in your happiness, bear your sorrow, and can even be shown off to your friends. Currently, QQ Pets come in three varieties: QQ Penguin, QQ Pig, and QQ Bear.

Below, we'll introduce QQ Penguin first. If you wish to adopt a QQ Pig, please refer to: How to Adopt QQ Pig; if you wish to adopt a QQ Bear, please see: How to Adopt QQ Bear.

You need to download QQ 2005 Spring Festival Edition or a higher version (download the latest QQ software immediately). After installation, click the small dog circled in the picture on the right to launch QQ Pet. However, before this, you need to go to the QQ Pet official website and participate in some activities on the official adoption channel to obtain a pet egg. Sometimes the official site offers free QQ pets; check out Several Ways to Adopt QQ Pets for Free. Of course, if you want to adopt a QQ pet for free, you can also ask QQ friends who have already raised pet eggs to gift you one.

QQ Pets have been released for quite some time now, yet many cash-strapped friends still haven't been able to get a pet. In fact, we can still get these cute QQ Pets for free. QQ users without pets can obtain them through two channels:

1. Weekly Q Pets

This means that every QQ account that hasn't received a pet this week can try again next week by smashing eggs.

Egg-smashing address: http://client.pet.qq.com/pet.htm

2. Friends Sending QQ Pets

Many QQ pets have already "married" and had adorable baby pets. Friends who haven't received a pet can ask those who already have baby pets to give one away. The method is relatively simple and won't be elaborated here.

Through the above methods of obtaining QQ Pets for free, everyone should be able to successfully claim their own baby pet. If you successfully claim a baby pet, the system will notify you via QQ system messages.

If you are unable to log in to QQ Penguin smoothly, please try the following operations:

1. Download the latest QQ Penguin client installation package from the address: http://www.qqjia.com/learn/pet.htm

2. Double-click the downloaded file to install it. Please make sure to use the default path for installation.

3. After installation, click the little dog head at the bottom of the QQ panel to log in.
