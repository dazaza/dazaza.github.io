Revealing the Workaholic Index of the 12 Zodiac Signs_8096

by fiqdhjrm on 2011-11-17 08:42:50

Aries (3.21-4.20)

Workaholic Index: 80%

When Aries gets interested in work, it can be a bit frightening. They seem to have boundless energy and enthusiasm. Whatever they do, they throw themselves into it with great passion. However, this can be somewhat worrying - will they make mistakes? In fact, for Aries, making mistakes is forgivable. But most people believe that their crazy attitude towards work won't last long.

Taurus (4.21-5.21)

Workaholic Index: 40%

Although Taurus is always hardworking and industrious, calculating carefully about work, their laziness makes it difficult for them to have intense work enthusiasm. Even if they need to work overtime, they would think twice. If Taurus becomes a workaholic, there's only one reason: "If you take someone's money, you should do the job well."

Gemini (5.22-6.21)

Workaholic Index: 30%

To be honest, Geminis are not very focused. Therefore, we can hardly expect them to become workaholics. If they have too much work, their smart brains will start working faster than anyone else’s, aiming to reduce the workload as much as possible. However, if you meet a workaholic who can multitask, they are probably a Gemini.

Cancer (6.22-7.22)

Workaholic Index: 30%

Cancers are not born to be career-oriented people. Don’t expect them to voluntarily work overtime. After all, family is more important than work. If they have to work, there are only two possibilities: either they really fear you or they owe you a favor.

Leo (7.23-8.23)

Workaholic Index: 80%

Compared to Aries, although both are fire signs, Leos are more action-oriented in their work. When they work, they are energetic and dynamic. Although they may lack attention to detail, their macro-management skills are unmatched. As leaders, they are almost the best workaholics among the 12 zodiac signs.

Virgo (8.24-9.23)

Workaholic Index: 70%

Virgos are always busy, and work seems like their daily life. Every day, they are occupied with many trivial tasks. Even if others or even they themselves don't know whether these tasks will lead to the final goal, they still feel that these tasks are necessary. Others naturally consider Virgos to be diligent.

Libra (9.24-10.23)

Workaholic Index: 60%

We cannot say that Libras dislike work or refuse to become workaholics. For Libras, who are easily influenced by others, we can only judge based on their circumstances. If their colleagues are like Capricorns, Libras will likely put in a lot of effort, though they might work slowly.

Scorpio (10.24-11.22)

Workaholic Index: 80%

For Scorpios, who have clear likes and dislikes, becoming a workaholic means they have an extremely high passion for their work. With passion, there is nothing Scorpios can't achieve. The more difficult the task, the more they can demonstrate their persistent spirit. Working late into the night is a common occurrence for them.

Sagittarius (11.23-12.21)

Workaholic Index: 50%

Sagittarians are people who fight for ideals. Under the support of great ideals, they always have endless energy to work hard. However, their biggest problem is that once the gap between ideals and reality becomes too large, they might lose motivation. For them, being a workaholic is mostly a phase.

Capricorn (12.22-1.20)

Workaholic Index: 100%

Capricorns are undoubtedly the top workaholics. Struggling day and night for the ultimate goal is a unique spirit of Capricorns. For them, work is the greatest responsibility in life. No matter how hard they work, it is for the final success. However, note that Capricorns won't do meaningless work.

Aquarius (1.21-2.19)

Workaholic Index: 90%

In others' eyes, Aquarians may seem a bit cynical, so it's hard to associate them with being workaholics. In fact, they are more skillful and methodical in their work than anyone else, making them one of the most efficient groups. Rational Aquarians' work results are no worse than Capricorns'.

Pisces (2.20-3.20)

Workaholic Index: 40%

For Pisceans, work might just be a burden, so being a workaholic doesn't suit them. If you see them working frantically, there's only one explanation: they are usually soft-hearted.
