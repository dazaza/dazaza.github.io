When it comes to building websites and writing blogs, no webmaster is indifferent to the traffic of their own website. Without traffic, for whom is the website built? Therefore, traffic has become the core parameter of website value.

In order to gain more traffic, webmasters have racked their brains and tried all kinds of methods. However, every webmaster must surely consider a technically demanding yet effective way: SEO. SEO (Search Engine Optimization), also known as search engine optimization, is a popular online marketing method in recent years. Its main purpose is to increase the exposure rate of specific keywords to enhance the visibility of the website, thereby increasing sales opportunities. Website SEO refers to making website content more easily accessible and acceptable by search engines, so that after receiving the website's data, search engines can compare and calculate it, placing websites with higher PR values (Page Rank) in priority positions when other users search online, thus providing searchers with accurate and helpful information.
