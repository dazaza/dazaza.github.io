Secretary of the Hunan Provincial Committee: The rule of law must highlight the supervision and restraint of power vrma

by esdkqr603 on 2012-03-02 16:49:40

CHINA News, Changsha, February 29 -- "Power without restraint and supervision will inevitably lead to corruption," said Zhou Qiang, Secretary of the Hunan Provincial Committee of the CPC on February 29. He added that this year, the construction of Rule-of-Law Hunan should focus on the restriction and supervision of power, improve the supervision system, establish an accountability mechanism, strengthen the restriction and supervision of the operation of power in reality, and ensure the standardized operation of power.

On the same day, the leadership organization for the construction of Rule-of-Law Hunan was officially established and began its operations. Zhou Qiang proposed at the first plenary meeting of the Leading Group for the Construction of Rule-of-Law Hunan that this year, the construction of Rule-of-Law Hunan should focus on ruling according to law, administering according to law, ensuring judicial justice, restricting and supervising power, strengthening grassroots democratic political construction, innovating social management, and strengthening legal education and publicity.

On July 26, 2011, the Thirteenth Plenary Session of the Ninth Term of the Hunan Provincial Committee of the CPC passed the Outline for the Construction of Rule-of-Law Hunan (referred to as the Outline), which became the guiding document for the rule-of-law construction in Hunan over the next ten years. It highlighted the new highlights of the ruling according to law by the Hunan Party Committee and opened a new era of rule-of-law construction in Hunan.

In the past six months, Hunan has strengthened organizational leadership, improved working mechanisms, consolidated grassroots foundations, created a strong atmosphere, and enhanced research and inspections to promote the implementation of the Outline. For example, it actively carried out the integrated creation activities of democratic legal construction at the county, township, and village levels to promote grassroots democratic legal construction.

Zhou Qiang said that this year is a crucial year for fully implementing the Outline, with arduous tasks and significant meaning. "Through focusing on grassroots development, strengthening the foundation, benefiting people's livelihood, we must establish and improve the working mechanism for the construction of Rule-of-Law Hunan. By improving legislation, strengthening law enforcement, deepening legal education, and reinforcing supervision, we can gradually achieve ruling according to law, administering according to law, ensuring judicial fairness, and everyone abiding by the law, creating a good legal environment to promote the rapid and sound development of Hunan's economy and society and the 'four modernizations and two types' construction."

"Administering according to law is the key point of building Rule-of-Law Hunan." Zhou Qiang emphasized that administrative management system reform should be deepened, administrative decision-making mechanisms and law enforcement systems should be improved, government transparency should be deepened, government services should be strengthened, and the comprehensive administration according to law should be promoted to accelerate the construction of a rule-of-law government. The focus of constructing Rule-of-Law Hunan lies in the grassroots, and the difficulty also lies in the grassroots. We must take grassroots democratic political construction as the breakthrough point to comprehensively enhance the legalization level of all grassroots undertakings.

Zhou Qiang proposed that efforts should be made to further increase the intensity of legislation, law enforcement, and supervision in the field of people's livelihood, skillfully use legal means to resolve social contradictions, safeguard and improve people's livelihood, and solve the prominent problems reflected by the masses and the hot and difficult issues involving the vital interests of the masses such as employment, social security, education, medical care, production safety, etc., in accordance with the law.

It is understood that in order to promote the implementation of various tasks for the construction of Rule-of-Law Hunan, Hunan will conduct a comprehensive evaluation of the implementation of the Outline by various places and departments at the end of the year. The evaluation results will be used as an important part of the term target assessment and annual report of leading cadres at all levels and as an important basis for promoting and using cadres.
