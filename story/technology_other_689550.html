Committee member Wang Guoxin accepted an exclusive interview with Tencent Weibo.

by wangguoxin on 2012-03-04 11:02:30

Mr. Wang Guoxin, member of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference (CPPCC), member of the Central Committee of the Agricultural Workers' Party Cultural Committee, president of the Three Gorges Painting Academy, researcher at the Central Institute of Literature and History Calligraphy and Painting Research Institute, and a first-class national artist enjoying government allowances, was online at Tencent Weibo at 8:00 PM on March 3rd to have an online exchange with netizens about hot topics such as the dual application for World Heritage status for Guan Yu culture and the development of illustrated books.

The following is an excerpt from the text transcript:

Wang Guoxin: I would like to thank Tencent.com and Tencent Weibo for their attention to me, and hope to become a good friend in spirit with everyone!

Tencent Netizen: Should the cultural industry open its doors and go global? Are there any corresponding action plans in this regard? Is it necessary for the state to provide corresponding support?

Wang Guoxin: Culture must rely on the wings of industry to transcend time and space and reach the masses to be passed down. Artworks should be healthy, unique, and unparalleled throughout history. Artists should have high standards and strive to surpass their predecessors, such as Xu Beihong painting horses. When I paint Guan Yu, I depict both his divine and human aspects. If one keeps imitating, it will be difficult to go global. The state should invest significant resources in providing corresponding support.

Tencent Netizen: Is my understanding of Guan Yu culture as a philosophical thought and worldview culture about Guan Yu correct? How significant is this culture for the modern society's ideological and moral system?

Wang Guoxin: Guan Yu culture represents the core traditional values that have supported the ethnic cohesion and centripetal force for thousands of years and will continue for many more years. Chinese traditional culture is the culture of loyalty, whether the country is prosperous or in crisis, loyalty, righteousness, benevolence, and courage are needed.

Tencent Netizen: Have you encountered any memorable events during the process of applying for World Heritage status for Guan Yu culture?

Wang Guoxin: Last year during the two sessions, I had a proposal suggesting the establishment of a statue of Guan Yu next to the National Grand Theater. The suggestion was originally accepted but then faced some netizens' doubts, which led to the matter being put on hold. This made me sigh deeply: we must start with popularizing Guan Yu culture! Over the past year, I have been doing relevant investigations and planning, writing materials and submitting them to the government, and actively promoting the application for World Heritage status for Guan Yu culture!

Tencent Netizen: Yichang has beautiful mountains and clear waters, talented people, and convenient transportation. What suggestions do you have for establishing a cultural tourism circle in Yichang?

Wang Guoxin: Guan Yu Cultural Tourism Circle? Haha. China Yichang, the land of great righteousness. Lead with the dragon's head: Guan Yu, the Martial Saint of Dangyang, set up nine tripods, establish nine platforms, and build a 58-meter bronze statue of Guan Yu. Strengthen the dragon's waist: develop industrial, pharmaceutical, and tourism products related to Guan Yu culture in Xiangting, Yichang, benefiting the country and enriching the people. Dance with the dragon's tail: along Guan Yu's army slope, develop leisure and cultural tourism areas.

Tencent Netizen: I support the application for World Heritage status for Guan Yu culture, but I think it should be expanded to include Three Kingdoms culture. Does applying for Guan Yu culture heritage have the suspicion of a deity-making movement?

Wang Guoxin: All nations in the world revere their own gods in their hearts. The word "god" consists of "revelation" on the left and three horizontal lines and one vertical line on the right, connecting heaven, earth, and man, telling others what a god is. Exceeding human power is divine power. A god is meant to instill reverence in people; a nation without reverence has no hope.

Tencent Netizen: Some underground societies in China, especially in Hong Kong, worship Guan Yu. As a symbol of justice, loyalty, martial saint, and many positive images, it seems ironic that he is worshipped by criminal organizations. Do you think this is ironic? Does this have an adverse effect on your promotion of Guan Yu culture?

Wang Guoxin: People from all walks of life hope that what they believe in is the "righteous god." The spirit of Guan Yu's loyalty, righteousness, benevolence, and bravery knows no distinction between black and white societies.

Tencent Netizen: What do you think is the biggest problem with applying for World Heritage status?

Wang Guoxin: Personally, I believe that as time goes by, more and more people from all sectors will believe in and support Guan Yu culture. Guan Yu culture will become mainstream culture!

Tencent Netizen: Nowadays, many hotel lobbies place statues of Guan Yu. What does this generally represent in terms of good intentions?

Wang Guoxin: Personally, I think it means putting "loyalty and trustworthiness" in one's heart, not making ill-gotten gains, and not using gutter oil!!

Tencent Netizen: Guan Yu has always appeared as a loyal and righteous figure in Chinese folk culture, representing the essence of excellent traditional Chinese culture. In the application for World Heritage status for Guan Yu culture, what specific material relics and cultural forms of Guan Yu culture have we mainly established? As cultural relics, what related activities have been held and will be held in our country?

Wang Guoxin: Forms include calligraphy and painting, sculpture, drama, folklore, place names, temples, scenic spots, etc. Activities include the grand sacrifices at the Guanling Tomb in Dangyang, the Guanlin Tomb in Luoyang, and the Guandi Temple in Yuncheng.

Wang Guoxin: The end of this interview marks the beginning of my friendship with a wide range of netizens! I hope Tencent Weibo can become our spiritual teahouse. I will take time every week to talk with a wide range of netizens. That’s all for today's interview. Thank you to all the netizens, and I hope you continue to pay attention to me.

Wang Guoxin is an outstanding contemporary painter, calligrapher, and poet. Since childhood, he has been fascinated by poetry, calligraphy, and painting. He has widely read Chinese history, philosophy, classical literature, and Confucianism, Taoism, and Buddhism. At the age of 16, his works were selected for the Central South Five Provinces Art Exhibition, and in 1969, his oil paintings were published and featured in the movie "News Briefing". He is currently a researcher at the Central Institute of Literature and History Calligraphy and Painting Research Institute, a member of the Art Committee of the Chinese Artists Association, a member of the Central Cultural Committee of the Agricultural Workers' Democratic Party, a first-class national artist enjoying government allowances, a member of the Chinese Artists Association, president of the Three Gorges Painting Academy, guest professor at Valenciennes University in France, member of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference (CPPCC), and executive vice president of the Beijing Oriental Calligraphy and Painting Research Society. He has published more than 70 poetry, calligraphy, and painting monographs, with over 15 million copies distributed. His representative works include the 10-volume Yangtze River Trilogy series, 240 illustrations for the Romance of the Three Kingdoms, and a 158-meter silk scroll depicting the scenery of the Yangtze River. He has also composed more than 7,000 poems.
