This is a joke told by my home treasure: There are three kinds of silly birds. The first kind is the early flying silly bird; the second kind is the silly bird that doesn't fly; the third kind is the egg-laying silly bird. It's an attitude,兔子舞, it's also a method, it's quite interesting. Each has its own way, each seems to have its own characteristics and future.

Anyone must have their own future path,兔子舞, any silly bird must have its own future direction. This super big silly bird of Chinese football, stumbling on the thorny road of green shade, after experiencing three consecutive draws, welcomes two consecutive losses (possibly three consecutive losses), creating a new bad record for Chinese football's failure to walk out of the World Cup.

In fact, Chinese football sometimes flies earlier, very early. But sometimes it just won't fly even if it's beaten to death. For example, when the national team selects a coach, each time it's like the early flying silly bird, planned very early, circling around Europe, finding a mediocrity, the cycle and results are basically the same. And at times when the whole world of football is in motion, Chinese football just remains unmoved, such as the league connection, establishment of a professional alliance, we don't know why it can't connect with their seasons, what's so scary? Without connecting, it's hard to have better talents entering and exiting, making exchanges more difficult. For many excellent experiences in international football, or the success of Japanese and Korean football, we need to learn humbly.

This two consecutive losses at Tianjin Shuidrop indicate that our attitude towards football matches is problematic, our players' understanding and reading of football matches is problematic, our coach's deployment of troops is problematic, our coach's treatment of players and the culture of Chinese football (if there is still one) all have problems. Players have problems, coaches have problems, it's normal for such games not to win. A more important question is that previous failures of Chinese football still had some bright spots, but this team has no redeeming qualities at all, and we need to reflect instead of forgiving ourselves again as before.

Not forgiving oneself is an attitude, and we need to be tough on ourselves. This means identifying problems and solving them in ways that can promote the progress of Chinese football. According to the view of a fan like me, learning from Brazilian and Argentinian football, we are not yet up to par, learning from Dutch and German football, we don't have the ability yet, so learning from Japanese and Korean football is feasible. In the past, Japanese and Korean teams were willing to play against us for practice, but in recent years, they are not very enthusiastic about Chinese football, the problem is our level and ability are too low, they don't want to play with us anymore. Truly, regrettable, pitiful, lamentable.

What should we do! Since Chinese football is a silly bird, it needs to take measures suitable for a silly bird to solve its own problems. Early flying silly bird is not working, not only does it not produce much result, but it also produces a lot of flaws. Not flying at all is even worse, after all, this is equivalent to avoidance, football needs to face challenges head-on to have a future. Let's let this silly bird of Chinese football lay an egg, perhaps this egg is the hope for the future of Chinese football.

Knowing where the problems lie, and knowing how to solve them. The next step is to implement a ten-year plan for Chinese football after the Olympics. In these ten years, we don't need any achievements. Using national football bases at all levels as football focal points, playing the role of football cities and relatively developed areas in football, planning more for youth football cultivation, eliminating false ages and other malpractices, and earnestly doing a good job in cultivating and promoting football talents. The Football Association should not do anything else, just seek alms, seek funds for football, provide wings for football to take off. Do not turn football schools into aristocratic football schools, do not miss any good football seedlings, increase the base number of football talents for national football. In this regard, rabbits dance, many domestic football schools have failed, the decline of talents is very common. Successful ones are not many, but there are some, one is Xu Genbao's Chongming base, which has cultivated a very competitive team, very commendable; another is Gao Fengwen's earlier football school, which has trained a few people for Chinese football. If China had ten, twenty, a hundred such football schools, how could we have only a couple of hundred football players available in one age group? Countries that silently succeed are Japanese football, whether it's men's or women's football, they spent ten years sharpening their knives, caught up with and surpassed us, laying down a continuous flow of talent chains, focusing on one learning target and benchmark without letting go. Now, their men's and women's football can be said to be successful.

There is one more point, while making football foundation the focus of Chinese football, cancel or dilute the CSL, the CSL does not play a role in the foundation of Chinese football, but becomes a negative example in football and even the sports world, highest income, worst performance. Eating four hundred yuan a day, achieving nothing all year round, going back to make big money, blushing when going out. This attitude towards the CSL is not angry words, but a very practical approach to the reality of Chinese football. Breaking and establishing anew, rebirth through fire, Chinese football is at a time when it needs to be rebuilt from scratch. After the Olympics, there is a great opportunity, it cannot be missed. Ten years of honing a sword, destined for success.

I beg Chinese football, just lay an egg!
