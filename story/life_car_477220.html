Car beauty should be cautious, teach you注意事项 for washing the car

Whether you go to the car wash or wash your car yourself, you need to understand the requirements of washing a car. There are ten taboos about washing cars. Have you noticed them?

One, avoid washing the car when the engine has not completely cooled down. This will cause the engine to age prematurely.

Two, avoid washing the car under intense sunlight. This will leave dry water droplet marks on the body of the car.

Three, avoid washing the car in extremely cold weather. Once the water freezes, it can cause the paint coating to crack.

Four, avoid using anything other than soft water for cleaning, including hot water, alkaline water, and hard water. These substances can damage the paint. Using hard water for cleaning will leave traces and a film on the surface of the body after drying.

Five, avoid using high-pressure water flow to rinse the body. Too much water pressure will damage the paint on the body. If there is tough dust or mud on the body, soak it with water first and then rinse it with water. Use dispersed water flow to spray.

Six, avoid adding random cleaning agents. When cleaning, do not use laundry powder, detergent powder, or inferior soap. Because these cleaning agents contain alkali, which may wash away the oil in the paint and accelerate the aging of the paint. Generally, no cleaning agent is added when washing the car.

Seven, avoid random wiping. After rinsing with clear water, many people like to wipe it. The main problem with wiping lies in two aspects: unqualified wiping cloth used and incorrect wiping method. If you want to wipe, the wiping cloth should be made of soft material or a clean sponge. Wipe in the direction of the water flow, from top to bottom, gently. Do not draw circles or wipe horizontally.

Eight, avoid random use of stain removers. There might be stains on the car body that are highly corrosive, such as asphalt, oil stains, bird and insect feces, etc., which are difficult to clean and require the right method. When cleaning oil stains on the car body, use a sponge dipped in a little kerosene or gasoline to gently wipe, then apply polishing paste on the wiped area to restore its original luster. If there are dead insects or other animal/plant juices on the car body, soak them with soapy water first; then clean with a sponge soaked in water. For oil stains on the steering wheel, lights, plastic and rubber parts, only ordinary soapy water can be used for cleaning, not gasoline, stain remover, or diluent.

Nine, avoid using hard cleaning tools to remove dirt. For example, using plastic brushes or blades to scrape off stains, this can easily damage the paint.

Ten, avoid touching the body surface with oily hands or placing oily tools or organic solvent-containing cloths on the car body. This can easily leave marks on the paint surface or cause the paint to fade prematurely.
