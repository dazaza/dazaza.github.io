BYD expects to receive some feedbacks internationally through this auto show.

by g8aq8r6jnjj on 2011-05-28 11:44:03

Reporters learned yesterday that BYD Auto will participate in the 78th Geneva International Motor Show held in Geneva, Switzerland on March 6 with five models.

On the exhibition hall arrangement map of the upcoming 78th Geneva International Motor Show, the three words "BYD (BYD Auto)" on booth No. 6350 caught the reporter's attention. The reporter confirmed from Xia Zhibing, General Manager of BYD Auto Sales Company, that the company will make its first appearance at the Geneva Motor Show this year and will bring five models including F3, F3R, F6, F1, and F3DM dual-mode electric vehicle to participate. Among them, the F3DM dual-mode electric vehicle as the latest model will make its debut in a public venue.

It is understood that BYD may be the only Chinese whole vehicle manufacturing enterprise participating in the Geneva International Motor Show. Unlike some domestic enterprises participating in international motor shows in recent years, this time not only did BYD Auto Sales Company directly organize the participation, but also got a pretty good booth. The reporter noticed that although the area of the BYD booth is not large, it is located right in the middle of Hall 6. Hall 6 not only has international first-line manufacturers such as Mercedes-Benz, BMW, Chrysler, PSA, etc., but the layout of the entire exhibition hall is relatively neat among the total of 5 whole vehicle exhibition halls. Opposite the BYD booth is the Mercedes-Benz brand booth, and it is also adjacent to the booths of brands like Jeep and Rolls-Royce.

Xia Zhibing said in an interview with our reporter that BYD hopes to get some feedback internationally through this motor show to further expand BYD's internationalization strategy. "Participating in the Geneva Motor Show also considers showcasing BYD's core strength in the electric vehicle field," so "it's time to show off."
