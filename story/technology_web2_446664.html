Resolutely follow the correct path of combining football with entertainment.

by j639c681 on 2011-06-15 21:17:09

Related thematic articles:

I have been living in Jingmen for six years.

Recently, there are two events that are very encouraging and worth applauding and celebrating! This is the beginning of the high combination of Chinese football and Chinese entertainment, and also the beginning of the profound integration of football and entertainment. Because of the combination, more fans begin to care about entertainment, and more entertainment fans begin to care about football.

One of these two major combinations is automatic and belongs to the practical field. That is the high degree and zero distance combination of Zhao Benshan and Liaoning Football. Such a combination result directly shocked the Anshan fans who shouted loudly, and the stadium was overcrowded; it directly attracted various stars and football bosses from all over the country, Taiwan, Hong Kong and Macao to gather in the Liaoshen area, with one goal, to cheer for such an intimate combination. Another combination is passive and belongs to a certain scope. This is the strong combination of Emperor Li Yi of Chinese football and Sister Furong who swept the network. Their combination enriched the way of celebrities' combination and enriched the trajectory of football activities. The World Cup made football more entertaining after entertainment, and also made football not only have outsiders but also relatives-in-law. Practice has proved that such a combination is a success. The overcrowding of the Anshan Stadium is a good example. After the combination, Li Yi's "ball protection is more like Henry", so even the visiting Henry dare not say that he surpasses Li Yi in awareness.

China is an ancient cultural country with endless entertainment items, bizarre entertainment means, and successive entertainment figures, while Chinese football has been in a failed state for a long time and has been in a state of no one caring for a long time. At this time, combining entertainment and football is to give Chinese football a "wedding blessing". Once the method is appropriate, it will bring back the dying Chinese football. The success of Liaoning football in the return of fans is a very representative example; the overwhelming reputation of Emperor Li Yi and Sister Furong is also a very successful example.

There are many places in China with entertainment significance, which is a cultural choice of the masses for hundreds of years, and more a rational understanding of the people on entertainment. Local football can be more based on local realities, according to local customs and humanistic preferences, and highly integrate local football and entertainment. Zhao Benshan's "Er Ren Zhuan" has already bloomed together with Liaoning football, so other local football teams should not hesitate. They should criticize Xu Ming's obvious argument that Dalian football does not need entertainment, and firmly take the road of combining football and entertainment. In the Shanghai and Jiangsu-Zhejiang areas, "Kunqu Opera", "Yueju Opera", "Shanghai Opera" can be combined with football; "Luju Opera", "Qinqiang Opera", "Sichuan Opera" are very local characteristic operas that can be utilized; "Peking Opera", "Pingju Opera", "Beijing Drum Music" can show entertainment ability in northern football, etc. Not only that, operas can be developed, and characters can also be combined. The spiritual combination of Li Yi and Furong is very meaningful, and Hao Haidong has already started to develop with Zhang Heng. This is a very good pioneering practice of the combination of football and entertainment.

The practice of Chinese football has proved that not taking the road of combining with entertainment football is a dead end. Taking the road of combining with entertainment has a bright future. Milu is very international in terms of entertainment. His unique fascinated eyes have once charmed a large number of people in Chinese football. His "happy football" is a specific experience of entertainment in football.

To take the road of combining football and entertainment, guidance and leadership are needed. Developed football regions should play a leading role, and less developed football regions should catch up quickly. One-to-one or group-to-one methods can be adopted. Gradual methods can also be used in specific operations. By taking the road of combining football and entertainment, the structure of Chinese football fans can be changed, the entertainment quality of fans can be improved, and the attitude towards football can be changed.

Chinese football has truly become a big stage. With Zhao Benshan and "Er Ren Zhuan" as the beginning, and Li Yi and Hao Haidong as the vanguard, the curtain of the close integration of football and entertainment has been opened. It seems that we have vaguely seen that black-and-white football already has the signs of pink entertainment, and we seem to see that entertainment has also put on the wings of football to take off. Practice will surely prove that taking the road of combining football and entertainment suits the development needs of Chinese football, and suits the today and tomorrow of Chinese football.

Nanshan Woodcutter 2005-7-6
