Introduction to The Sun in My Heart

Yuan Ning, in order to escape the shadows of life and free herself from idle gossip, comes alone to a coastal city to work. Here, she makes the best friend of her life; similarly, it is here where she experiences the warmth and coldness of human relationships, the treachery of human nature, enduring humiliation and living in disgrace. Finally, after achieving impressive glory and brilliance, she takes revenge for herself and eliminates harm for the people...
