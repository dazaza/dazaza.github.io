Be a responsible citizen of the earth

by hunanshida on 2012-02-14 13:46:23

Human beings are the children of the earth, nature is the mother of mankind, and the earth is the home of mankind. So far, human beings have not yet discovered a celestial body other than the earth suitable for human reproduction and habitation. In the vast universe, the earth is only a drop in the ocean, but it provides an ideal life support system and living space for human beings and even all living things: air, sunshine, water, energy, etc. It is precisely because of these precious resources that human beings can survive and develop, create brilliant civilizations, and splendid cultures.

For a long time, Hunan Normal University's self-study examination teachers found that our attitude towards nature has been to utilize, conquer, and transform nature, which has caused the relationship between man and nature to be misplaced. As a result, humans have been punished by nature, and the survival environment of human beings is facing enormous crises. The environment has increasingly become the focus of human attention.

The teachers from Hunan Normal University Self-examination Network know that the World Environment and Development Commission interprets "sustainable development" as: "a kind of development that meets the needs of the present generation without impairing the ability of future generations to meet their own needs." Human beings should be in harmony with nature, develop sustainably, and provide good living and development spaces for future generations. Today's development and construction must consider not only the use of resources and wealth among countries in the present era but also lay a foundation for the development of future generations. We should not only consider the lives of the current generation and recklessly exploit limited resources and wealth. Each generation should think about the next generation. The earth does not only belong to the current generation; it is also the home where our descendants will survive.

A green lifestyle requires us to educate our children to start from small things in daily life, starting from ourselves, and cultivate correct environmental protection habits. Educational suggestions from Hunan Normal University's self-study teachers are:

1. Saving is a virtue.

2. Teach children some "green behaviors."

3. Leave 5 more minutes when going out.

4. Teach children to sort garbage.

5. Pay attention to the power of role models.
