**Moisture-proof, Antibacterial, and Environmentally Safe**

Wuhan Harvard Liquid Wall Paint is a new type of artistic coating, also known as wallpaper paint. It combines the features of wallpapers and latex paints into an eco-friendly water-based coating. Regarding its environmental performance, it starts with the raw materials. According to reports, the main ingredient of this Wuhan Harvard Liquid Wall Paint comes from the surface layer of natural shellfish shells, processed at high temperatures. Some versions use acrylic emulsion, titanium dioxide, pigments, and other additives, avoiding pollutants in the internal composition of the material.

Additionally, as we know, although the primary raw materials of wallpapers—such as pure natural paper and micro-suspension PVC resin—are less polluting and free from lead, benzene, and other harmful substances, they have minimal toxicity compared to other chemical decorative materials. However, during wallpaper installation, wall adhesives may contain lead, mercury, and other heavy metals, as well as aldehydes, indirectly causing indoor environmental pollution. Wuhan Harvard Liquid Wall Paint, being a water-based coating, eliminates the use of wall adhesives and their associated odors, ensuring the product's environmental safety during use. Moreover, as a water-based coating, it offers excellent moisture-proof and antibacterial properties, resisting insect infestations and aging.
