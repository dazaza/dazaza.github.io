The total number of tourists in Anji during the Spring Festival reached 210,000 - Anji Farm Stay

by super2733 on 2012-02-21 10:56:51

During the long Spring Festival holiday, tourist attractions were bustling with activity. Yesterday, the reporter learned from the Anji County Tourism Committee that from the first day of the lunar new year to the sixth day, the total number of tourists in Anji reached 2.1 million, generating a tourism revenue of 1.58 billion yuan, with ticket sales reaching 2.8 million yuan. In particular, the Jiangnan Tianchi Scenic Area received an average of 8,000 visitors per day. During the third and fourth days of the lunar new year, the visitor numbers at Zhongnan Baiyaoyuan and Bamboo Museum Park also gradually increased, reaching up to 6,000 visitors per day at its peak.

**Jiangnan Tianchi: A Bustling Paradise**

"Car after car drove into the Jiangnan Tianchi scenic area, sometimes even forming long queues." This has been a common sight on the main roads of Tiangang Town since the Spring Festival. On the first day of the lunar new year, many self-driving tourists came here to enjoy skiing and hot springs.

To prepare for the Spring Festival tourist reception, Jiangnan Tianchi had made ample preparations before the festival. Red balloons floated in the air, festive couplets were hung, and staff greeted visitors with smiles, saying "Happy New Year, welcome to the Jiangnan Tianchi scenic area."

The staff informed reporters that it snowed in Jiangnan Tianchi during the first month of the lunar calendar, which improved the skiing conditions and atmosphere. Many tour groups had been booked in advance, while a significant portion of self-driving tourists arrived after seeing promotional advertisements. "These tourists account for more than 70% of the total number," said the staff member.

**Baiyaoyuan and Bamboo Museum Park: Showcasing Their Best Attractions**

Traditionally quieter in winter, attractions like Zhongnan Baiyaoyuan and Bamboo Museum Park also unveiled their signature performances this Spring Festival. On the first day of the lunar new year, over a hundred private cars were parked at the entrance of Zhongnan Baiyaoyuan, all families coming together.

The staff explained: "The scenic area has been particularly lively these days. There are daily ethnic performances, such as bears riding bicycles and tiger shows, which visitors love very much. Many children also enjoy coming to Baiyaoyuan to see animals. Even though it's winter, there is still plenty to do here."

At the Bamboo Museum Park, the newly launched 4D cinema has been well-received by many tourists. Although each movie lasts only 15 minutes, visitors still like to go inside for an experience. Additionally, many tourists from places like Shanghai came to visit the bamboo forest during the Spring Festival long holiday. The staff introduced: "It snowed a little during the first month, and the snowy bamboo forest scenery was exceptionally beautiful."

**Rural Tourism Becomes Fashionable**

On the third day of the lunar new year, Shangshu Tugan Village in Guishan Township welcomed many tourists from Hangzhou. "We learned about the beautiful scenery of Shangshu Tugan Village in Anji during a tourism recommendation event in Anji. Since we had time during the Spring Festival holiday, we brought our family here. It's really great," Mr. Wang from Hangzhou introduced.

Shangshu Tugan Village's Party Secretary Li Xiliang introduced: "During the Spring Festival, the village's tourism service center remained open for business, receiving more than 500 individual tourists from the first to the sixth day of the lunar new year."

In the White Tea Film Base in Huangdu Village, Xilong Township, tourists visited every day during the first month of the lunar calendar. A tourist from Shanghai introduced: "The tea gardens here stretch endlessly, lush and green, very beautiful. The film base is located amidst the tea gardens, simple yet elegant. Walking around here is very comfortable."

Huangdu Village's Party Secretary Sheng Awei told reporters: "During the first month, many villagers brought their friends and relatives to the film base for photos, introducing them to the development of Huangdu Village. Now, villagers' concepts have changed, and so have their ways of playing."

In places like Ligou Village, Xiaoyun Village, and Xigang Village in Liangpeng Town, the creation of beautiful countryside last year gave the villages a brand-new look. During the Spring Festival, many nearby villagers came here to stroll and admire the new rural landscapes.

A relevant person in charge of the county tourism committee introduced: "With the rise of rural tourism, the development of winter tourism projects such as hot springs and skiing in Anji, as well as the active creation of new projects by Zhongnan Baiyaoyuan and Bamboo Museum Park, Anji's tourism experienced another small peak during the Spring Festival. In the new year, the county tourism committee will continue to strengthen external publicity for Anji tourism, further standardize the Anji tourism market, and enhance the quality of Anji tourism."
