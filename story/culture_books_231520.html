Traversing the Nonsensical and Thunderous Jianghu: A Journey Through "Heavenly Thunder" by Shu Ke

by jqrxass on 2009-09-01 10:52:34

If women can be described as red and white roses, then so can men. It's just that they are not characterized by being gorgeous or thorny. Even archangel Michael and Satan, who are completely opposed to each other, have their own advantages, making you involuntarily approach them again and again.

It is probably to prove this that Lei Lei, a modern woman, was deceived by an unscrupulous穿越group and resurrected in the body of Hua Xiaorui... Without knowing the cause and effect of Hua Xiaorui's suicide, Lei Lei got involved in the martial arts world affairs, and also met the heir of Fengming Knife, the perfect and righteous young master, Hua Xiaorui's nominal husband - Xiao Bai.

The martial arts world is in chaos, Yi Xian's medical fanatic "Don't want money" died at home for the sake of the Longevity Fruit. The three major sects were already suspicious of each other, and after the death of Leng Ying, the leader of the South Sea Sect, it caused even more enmity between the sects. As the martial artists were investigating the real culprit and looking for the whereabouts of the Longevity Fruit, the lord of the Devil Sect, Shangguan Qiuyue, who wanted to reap the benefits, set up numerous traps, making them fall into the maze and unable to extricate themselves...

Part 1

Lei Lei realized that her soul had already possessed someone else's body when she noticed her hands weren't the usual healthy color but were instead pale, tender, delicate, with long nails. She immediately became interested in her new identity: "Then what's my name?"

The aunt smiled kindly, "Silly girl, of course you're called Chunhua."

Lei Lei almost fainted, "Chunhua!"

"Yes, you're Chunhua, you've been in our Gushan Village for only three months. You said you came here from Jinjiang City," the aunt consoled her, "You're still young, your road is still long, why do such foolish things? Thank goodness for that tree on the cliff..."

Part 2

How similar to a morgue! Had she been murdered?

Her hair stood on end. Just as Lei Lei was filled with fear, suddenly a cold wind swept by, making the surrounding gauze curtains flutter silently, eerie and ghostly.

Unlike ordinary autumn cool breezes, this wind carried countless icy chills, stinging her skin painfully, the cold piercing straight to the bone. Lei Lei felt her whole body was about to freeze numb, and when she opened her mouth, white mist came out. It was like being inside a large freezer. She almost suspected that this place was the legendary Yama's Hell.

Had she really become a ghost? Lei Lei forced herself to calm down, rubbing her legs, struggling to stand up, moving her limbs. Feeling slightly better, she began to carefully analyze her current situation.

Part 3

Lei Lei suddenly understood. What prosperity? She heard that the Xiao family started declining since Master Xiao Min, passing down only one line, and those who were born later were all daughters. There would only be one son, no more children once a boy was born. This almost became a rule, and in this generation, there was only "Little White", without even sisters. If something happened to him now, the Xiao family would truly be extinct. In this place, "no descendants" was considered the greatest sin. It seemed that Xiao Min indeed had foresight. She heard that in the past battle, blood flowed like rivers, and countless people died. Even though the Devil Sect had done bad things, not everyone deserved to die. No wonder he was uneasy and conducted such superstitious activities to seek psychological balance. He probably didn't expect "prosperity", only hoping for "peace" would be lucky enough.
