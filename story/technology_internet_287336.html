Netizens expose photos of the thinnest dog, which is as thin as a rail (picture group)

by mingdaolin on 2009-12-01 10:40:47

The photo of the skinniest dog was exposed, with its bones showing through its skin. The truly emaciated dog has visible ribs, and a watch-style messaging device that looks like a real bone structure. The dog's bones are all exposed, just like the general agent for wireless headphones in Tianjin.

Rednet Changsha, November 1st (Xiangchen Rolling News Correspondent Xie Xiangzuo) "What a pitiful dog!" Following the case of a dog named "Newman" in the United States, whose face full of wrinkles and sad expression made his photo go viral online and quickly became a sensation, capturing the hearts of millions of netizens. A small dog named "Lelè" in Yueyang, Hunan, which followed its owner who worked as a traffic warden on duty every day, was called by netizens as "the most dedicated dog". Recently, netizens have also exposed a "skinniest dog ever" in Changzhou, Jiangsu, attracting widespread attention from many netizens.

Encountering the "skinniest dog ever" during a walk

According to a post by netizen "Rouyuan" on the Zhongwu Community: "This afternoon, while strolling after eating at the side of Lantian Garden with my colleagues, suddenly a person and a dog appeared. I don't know why, but this dog is so thin, looking like it’s addicted to drugs, with clearly visible ribs. Its face looks like it’s been flattened by a tire! It’s terrifying... Is there a dog association in Changzhou? Who can save this dog?" Such a shocking exposure triggered numerous forwards on various forums such as Sohu News Community and Rednet Forum, thus drawing strong attention from netizens.

The photos show, near the tactile paving on a pedestrian path, a man wearing a white jacket, dark waist pants, and athletic leisure shoes teasing a dog. This dog is completely black, with a long, skinny head, wide eyes without any luster, and a body so thin it looks like skin and bones, as if it has been sick for a very long time. Its body curled up, with each rib exposed, making it seem like it could be blown over by a gentle breeze. Its tail is tucked between its legs, and a long iron chain is around its neck, making it look quite pitiful.

Netizens have different opinions: Too Pitiful

"If you can't afford to keep it, then don't keep it! Truly!" Many netizens believe that this dog suffers from malnutrition and suspect that the owner may be abusing it. Netizen "zark_00" said, "Perhaps this person abuses dogs. It looks like a greyhound, but it shouldn’t be this thin." Netizen "weisheng0213" also blamed the dog's owner, "It's too excessive. How can someone treat a dog like this? His owner lacks humanity!" After careful observation, netizen "wanghaoran85" said, "This puppy indeed suffers from malnutrition. Has anyone noticed that there's no meat on its buttocks?" Netizen "Yuzui Shenzhen" specifically mentioned, "The dog's tail being tucked means it is either scared or sick!"

More netizens believe that dogs of this breed are naturally thin. If they get too fat, they won't be able to run or catch rabbits, becoming less valuable.

"What's going on! This dog is a greyhound, naturally this thin! I have one at home! This dog is about three months old. When it grows up, it will become slightly more solid, absolutely impossible to fatten up. Once fat, it becomes worthless because it cannot run anymore." Netizen "Yu Xiaoyu" continued to comment, "My greyhound is six months old. When we take it out for a walk, others always say, 'Why don't you feed your dog? It's so thin!' Wrongfully accused! His daily food cost is 15 yuan." Netizens "Proud Wolf" and "Lqkokjl" also stated, "This dog belongs to the hound type. It's not abused; it's just the owner preventing it from eating too much to maintain its shape and retain the essence of a hunting dog!" "This dog is like this. I also have one at home. It can catch rabbits, and no matter how much it eats, it doesn't get fat. We call it a 'line dog' locally."

Some netizens are clearly joking around. "It must have drunk weight-loss tea! Even if the owner doesn't feed it, it would still run around to find something to eat." "Impossible! Could it be Chen Luyu's dog?" "Maybe the dog has worms in its stomach, let the nurse give it some artificial respiration." "This dog must be a drug addict."
