Step up support for the building of enterprises' self-own brands

by bhhgsyfk4143 on 2012-02-07 10:46:22

On December 30th last year, Rugao Software Park was officially rated as a national-level science and technology business incubator center. As a public welfare science and technology entrepreneurship service institution, the center will provide services for the incubation of enterprises entering the park, including cooperation in industry-university-research, administrative affairs, business affairs, information, consultation, market training, financing, human resources, and scientific and technological project application. Last year, our city made new progress in scientific and technological innovation. The achievements in project application were abundant, the cooperation in industry-university-research deepened continuously, the innovation carriers achieved efficient breakthroughs, the innovation ability significantly enhanced, and the results of energy conservation and emissions reduction were evident.

This year, our city will strengthen the supporting role of talent more, increase the recruitment of talent and wisdom, and establish a special fund for talent development. We will deeply implement the "222" talent introduction project and other talent recruitment plans to accelerate the cultivation and gathering of a group of high-level scientific and technological talents, high-quality management talents, and highly skilled practical talents; continue to promote the "EMBA Plan" for outstanding entrepreneurs, focusing on cultivating a group of leading entrepreneur talents with global strategic vision, market pioneering spirit, and continuous innovation capabilities; deepen the implementation of the "talent accommodation" project, adding 200,000 square meters of talent apartments throughout the year.

The creation of a national-level technological innovation demonstration city has built a new platform for our city to promote the construction of innovative enterprises, parks, towns (districts), and continuously enhance independent innovation capabilities. This year, our city will ensure the addition of 25 new high-tech enterprises through measures such as strengthening the construction of independent innovation platforms, accelerating the industrialization of patent technologies, and promoting the deep integration of industry-university-research.

Enterprises are the main body for achieving economic takeoff. This year, our city will actively guide innovation elements to gather towards enterprises, pushing enterprises to truly become the main bodies for innovation demand, R&D investment, technology development, and application of results.

The main theme of scientific and technological innovation focuses more on digital design, intelligent equipment, automated production, networked management, and electronic commerce. Our city will actively promote the informatization transformation of enterprises with these goals in mind. Throughout the year, we aim to newly cultivate one provincial two-in-one integration demonstration enterprise, five Nantong demonstration pilot enterprises, and ten Rugao demonstration pilot enterprises; comprehensively enhance the enterprise's ability to register, cultivate, utilize, protect, and manage trademarks, creating independent brands. Encourage enterprises to conduct international trademark registration and use independent brands to expand international markets. Strengthen support for the construction of enterprise independent brands, encourage and support enterprises with conditions to actively participate in the formulation of industry standards, aiming to add one China Renowned Trademark, three provincial famous trademarks, and two provincial brand products throughout the year; encourage enterprises to conduct technological innovation, resolutely eliminate outdated production processes and equipment, and accelerate the promotion and application of new technologies, new products, new processes, and new materials, enhancing the enterprise's energy utilization efficiency. Strengthen supervision of existing energy conservation and emissions reduction projects to ensure normal operation.

The role of talent support is strengthened further. This year, our city will promote enterprises with taxable sales of 10 billion yuan, 1 billion yuan, 100 million yuan, and 20 million yuan to build national, provincial, Nantong, and Rugao municipal engineering centers respectively, building 35 innovation platforms of various levels and types throughout the year. Enlarge and strengthen all kinds of enterprise incubators, promote the construction of the entrepreneurship center in the Economic Development Zone (Chaowan Town) and Yangtze River Town (Rugao Port Area), aiming to add 320,000 square meters of incubator building area throughout the year. Actively cultivate the shipbuilding and offshore engineering, green new material parks to be built into provincial-level science and technology industrial parks; actively support enterprises to strengthen core technology research and development, apply for 6000 patents throughout the year, among which 1200 are invention patents, authorize 1500 patents, and authorize 110 invention patents. Increase the intensity of scientific and technological plan applications, organize and apply for more than 280 scientific and technological projects at or above the Nantong municipal level; fully leverage the role of the science and technology town mayor team, vigorously promote the construction of "university-enterprise alliances", encourage and support more enterprises to conduct industry-university-research cooperation with universities and scientific research institutions. Actively apply for the national-level shipbuilding and supporting industry technological innovation strategic alliance, give full play to the role of the provincial flower wood industry technological innovation alliance, fiberglass composite new material industry technological innovation alliance, and poultry waste comprehensive utilization industry technology alliance, actively promoting the transformation of innovation results into real productive forces.

This year, our city will continue to vigorously implement the strategy of revitalizing the city through science, education, and talent, accelerate the advancement of the scientific and technological innovation project, and effectively promote economic growth shifting mainly to innovation-driven.

The most important aspect of today's development is talent, and the competition for talent has become the top priority for competition between enterprises and even regions. Only by paying more attention to identifying, attracting, and utilizing talent can one remain invincible in competition.

The position of enterprises as the main body of innovation is more prominent.
