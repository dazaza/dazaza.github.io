FOTON LeiSa's three magic weapons

by ftleisa on 2011-12-30 10:20:01

China's first National IV standard self-owning brand 52-meter Foton LeiSa pump truck was launched in Beijing a few days ago and drew high attention from the industry. Experts believe that this marks another step for China's pump truck towards the world level of high technology, high environmental protection, and high quality. In recent years, relying on breakthroughs in core self-owning technologies, Foton LeiSa has quickly established its professional and reliable market image. The newly launched National IV 52-meter pump truck has also created the "three most" in China's pump truck industry: the most environmentally friendly pump truck, the highest pumping height domestic special chassis pump truck, and the most advanced performance pump truck. Integrated development, integrated manufacturing, and integrated service are Foton LeiSa's three treasures. In the LeiSa pump truck "integrated manufacturing" plant, it can be seen that the production process especially introduced Toyota's TPS lean production model, adopting an integrated production process for the chassis and upper structure, ensuring product quality reaches a leading level. Moreover, LeiSa pump trucks stand internationally, using Foton Motor's global component procurement system, and appropriately use imported parts in key components and assemblies. This not only significantly increases the lifespan and reliability of the whole vehicle but also enhances the market competitiveness of core components, successfully achieving efficient integration of global specialized resources. Foton LeiSa's emphasis on "integrated service" lets many people feel LeiSa's commitment to establishing product value with international standards and concepts. The company owns a nationwide network of 1,100 integrated service stations for chassis and upper structures, capable of solving vehicle problems in one stop. From vehicle financing to vehicle guarantees and parts financing, LeiSa provides users with one-stop financial solutions. Financial services offer low down payments, long loan terms, low interest rates, simple procedures, flexible repayments, and minimal burden. This customer-needs-oriented integrated service allows consumers to truly experience the feeling of "saving money and less worry." Foton Motor Group Vice President Wu Yuejun said that starting from the previous year, Foton Motor has unveiled the prelude to its 2020 strategy. In the next ten years, Foton Motor will follow the idea of "world standards, mid-range products," focusing on expanding five pillar industries - new energy vehicles, automotive Internet of Things, financial services, new energy, and high-end equipment manufacturing - while continuing to develop existing businesses, transforming into a comprehensive transportation tool company. Senior experts expressed that in international market competition, the "cost advantage" that Chinese companies have long relied on is gradually diminishing, while the "technical barriers" and "environmental barriers" established by Western developed countries are becoming stumbling blocks for Chinese companies to expand the global market and create world brands. The launch of Foton LeiSa's new pump truck with higher environmental standards and higher technical levels has let many people see the determined efforts of Chinese companies to reshape the global industrial landscape.
