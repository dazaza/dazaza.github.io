Renovation Launch: October Edition - From a Hands-off Owner to DIY

by kfjro779 on 2011-06-14 09:56:23

The keys to our new house were not obtained until November 30th. Taking advantage of the National Day celebration marking the 60th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China, major merchants offered discounts and promotions, so we entered the pre-decorating phase in advance. The process of starting practical work once again confirmed the old saying: "Example is better than precept." During our first visit to Red Star Macalline in August, we immediately fell in love with Kebao's overall home decoration. However, after much deliberation, we were concerned that our budget would not be sufficient, so we had to abandon it. On September 27th, during our second visit to the building materials city, the vast expanse of the Shilihe Building Materials Market left us dazzled. Impulsively and excitedly, without batting an eye, we spent 16,800 yuan to order a three-meter-long Boss integrated kitchen cabinet. After the excitement of spending money wore off, I began to regret it. Nearly twenty thousand yuan, just like that, handed over to a kitchen of more than four square meters? It was truly difficult to accept. When I couldn't help but complain to my husband, we once again confirmed how well-matched we are as a couple. Hee hee. Without hesitation, my husband wholeheartedly agreed with my suggestion to return the goods. The price of Boss products was indeed too high, so in order to control the budget, we had to endure the pain of giving up on them. After making three trips between Leroy Merlin and Jiaru Home, we finally decided on Deyi's range hood and stove. We were quite satisfied with ourselves for negotiating a relatively appropriate price and securing some very good gifts. Here are the results of our labor up to this point: Deyi CXW-200-1897 Range Hood According to the introductions by the salesperson and the manual, I have summarized the following features: 1. Practical: No need to disassemble, no need to wash, no need to replace the net; 2. Beautiful: Heaven-round earth-square design, black glass panel, titanium gold protective cover; 3. Advanced: Touch-sensitive switch; Deyi Junhui Series Gas Stove 2517 Similarly featuring a tempered glass panel, elegant and refined. The sink was a special gift paired with the range hood and gas stove for the National Day promotion, and we also bought Deyi's faucet to complete the set of kitchen appliances. When choosing the cabinets, the entire family was involved, including our son, and we ultimately selected the Sunshine Sakura integrated kitchen cabinets from Lantai Home. We also purchased the paint, Dulux Anti-formaldehyde All-in-one Wall Paint. For the bedrooms, living room, and dining room, we chose the Impressions series flooring from Life Home * Baroque. The kitchen and bathroom feature tiles and wall bricks selected by my husband from Boloni Ceramics. For the sanitary ware, we chose the Emperor Colored Series: We will leave the washbasin photo as a suspense... Once the renovation is complete, I will take photos. All these items are things we have long desired, and the prices are still reasonable. Up to now, we are glad that we did not choose an overall home decoration package. That way, we would have missed out on the fun of decorating our home for the first time. Now we are almost completely DIY-ing it, even coming close to ordering the waterproofing ourselves. Enjoying the fun of decorating our own home, we look forward to the official start of the renovation project.
