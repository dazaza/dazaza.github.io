Park Si Ho's Weibo account is set up on Sina; the Korean Smile Prince never forgets to promote his new play.

by edivista on 2011-12-15 20:59:13

Click to enter朴施厚 Weibo

On December 13, popular South Korean male star Park Si-hoo officially launched his Weibo account and greeted fans in China right away. He also posted the latest stills from his new drama for promotion.

Born in 1978, Park Si-hoo made his debut in 2005. Although he played roles in many hit TV dramas such as "Hero Spring Fragrance" and "Which Star Are You From," he failed to leave a deep impression on the audience. It was not until "The Way of Meeting Perfect Neighbors" in 2007 that Park Si-hoo, who portrayed an icy prince as the second male lead, was originally intended to play out a love story without results with the female lead Byeo Doo-na according to the storyline. Unexpectedly, Park Si-hoo's performance of being cold on the outside but conflicted inside won countless praises from the audience. Therefore, Park Si-hoo's role in "Neighbors" became one of the most classic cases in Korean drama history where the second male lead turned into the first male lead. (Click on the image to enter Park Si-hoo's Weibo)

After "The Way of Meeting Perfect Neighbors," Park Si-hoo's popularity soared, and he began to continuously star in popular Korean dramas, each receiving good reviews. He could be said to be a late bloomer actor and also a guarantee for Korean drama ratings. He is the amorous young master in "One Branch Plum;" his sorrowful eyes make people feel sorry for him; he is the naughty rich second generation in "Family Glory," yet his charm is impossible to escape from; he is the mysterious man in "Prosecutor Princess," meticulously seeking revenge but falling in love unintentionally; he is the elegant gentleman in "Reversal Queen," caring for older women down to the smallest detail; he is also the Romeo of Korea in "Princess' Man," turning the love story into an eternal masterpiece. Despite portraying characters with different personalities in previous works, they share a common feature: gentle eyes and warm smiles.

Park Si-hoo, known as the "Prince of Smiles," has extremely high popularity among Chinese drama fans. Therefore, when Park Si-hoo wrote in his first Weibo post: "I am Park Si-hoo, greeting everyone for the first time. Fans in China, let's meet here from now on~," he received enthusiastic responses from fans in a short period of time. Meanwhile, Park Si-hoo did not forget to mention his currently filming movie "I Am A Murderer" in his Weibo. Although he has rich experience in TV dramas, "I Am A Murderer" is Park Si-hoo's screen debut. It is worth mentioning that Park Si-hoo will play the role of a hidden murderer in the film, which can be said to be a special experience for him who usually plays positive characters. Through the film, the audience will see Park Si-hoo's new side. As for more information about the film's shooting, it is believed that Park Si-hoo will share it with everyone on Weibo at the first time!
