19-Year-Old Boy Dies After Falling Ill During Military Training

by anonymous on 2012-02-05 22:39:53

Yesterday at noon, Wang Xiaoying showed reporters Jojo's health examination report and the agreement signed with the college. Chief reporter Wang Di took the photo. A 19-year-old boy suddenly fell ill during military training at school. After being saved in the hospital for 10 days, this freshman died of cerebral hemorrhage.

The parents of the student want to know whether the school has any responsibility for their child's death, or how much responsibility it bears.

Even though the incident happened a month ago, mom Wang Xiaoying still cannot believe that her 19-year-old son Jojo (a pseudonym) is gone. This year, Jojo was admitted to Shenyang Normal University International Business College and started military training after registration. On the night of September 18, Wang Xiaoying suddenly received a call saying that her son "had no heartbeat and no breathing" at school. When she arrived at the hospital, she saw the diagnosis provided by the 120 emergency service: "sudden cardiac arrest for 45 minutes."

After being rescued in the hospital for 10 days, Jojo was ultimately declared dead due to cerebral hemorrhage.

For Wang Xiaoying, her son's death has thrown her entire life off balance. But more level-headed family members reminded her that she should find out how her child actually died.

Yesterday, she presented a physical examination report from September 9 during Jojo's military training period. The report showed that Jojo's blood pressure was found to be 146/74 mmHg during the physical examination.

Wang Xiaoying, who is also a medical worker, stated that from this physical examination report, it can be seen that this blood pressure has exceeded the normal range and should be diagnosed as hypertension. Rest and drug treatment should have been given.

Wang Xiaoying believes that "the school hospital did not provide necessary blood pressure monitoring and drug treatment, and failed to fulfill the duty of notification. At the same time, there was excessive physical training, which led to a continuous increase in blood pressure causing Jojo's cerebral hemorrhage. The school bears inescapable responsibility."

Had he drunk alcohol with his dormitory mates before the incident?

Yesterday afternoon, the reporter accompanied Wang Xiaoying to Shenyang Normal University as a family member.

A responsible person from the International Business College of Shenyang Normal University said that regarding Jojo's matter, they were also very saddened. After the incident, the school conducted an investigation. During the investigation, some of Jojo's classmates said that "Jojo claimed that his birthday was on September 26th, and planned to celebrate early with his classmates. That evening, after the counselor checked the dormitories, he gathered seven classmates to drink alcohol in the dormitory." "This child had drunk alcohol before."

A staff member of Shenyang Normal University said that only when the relevant department's examination report comes out can a conclusion be drawn about what caused Jojo's cerebral hemorrhage.

Due to significant differences, both parties failed to reach a consensus.

After the incident, the International Business College of Shenyang Normal University signed an agreement with Wang Xiaoying. The agreement states (Party A Wang Xiaoying, Party B International Business College of Shenyang Normal University): Party B carried out the entrance education work in a standardized and orderly manner, without any mistakes or improper actions. Jojo died of cerebral hemorrhage on September 28, 2010, in the hospital, and the responsibility does not lie with Party B. Party B refunded tuition fees and prepaid medical expenses of 7182 RMB, organized all teachers and students of the college to donate 22558 RMB for Jojo, and covered his funeral expenses of 20000 RMB, providing Party A with a one-time compensation of 26000 RMB. From the date of signing, Party A shall not request Party B to bear any responsibility in any way. Both parties signed the agreement, and the unit seal was from the International Business College of Shenyang Normal University.

However, Wang Xiaoying believed that the agreement signed with the International Business College of Shenyang Normal University does not represent Shenyang Normal University. The former is a subordinate branch institution of the latter, and proposed that the school should compensate for various expenses amounting to 800,000 RMB.

The school side stated that the Business College is co-sponsored by Shenyang Normal University and other aspects. Currently, the school's handling result is to follow the opinions signed in the agreement. Due to significant differences between both parties, no consensus was reached during this negotiation.

Lawyer Zhang Mingchun from Liaoning Mingxi Law Firm said that regarding the agreement itself, if the International Business College has independent legal person status, Shenyang Normal University has no legal responsibility in this matter. The International Business College needs to independently bear civil rights and responsibilities externally. If the International Business College does not have independent legal person status, Shenyang Normal University can entrust the International Business College to sign an agreement with the parents under the name of the college regarding the student's death issue, and this agreement will be binding on both the parents and the school. Reporter Li Ying
