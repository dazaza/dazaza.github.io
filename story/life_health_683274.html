Which gynecological inflammations that women with them cannot have painless artificial abortion? Many women who have had painless artificial abortion know that a necessary examination before the operation is gynecological examination, which is very important. The main purpose of gynecological examination is to check if women have gynecological inflammation. Many women may not understand this and just want to solve the unexpected trouble as soon as possible. But usually these girls do not know that if they have gynecological inflammation, they cannot have an abortion but need to anti-inflammation first and control infection. They can have an abortion only when the inflammation is under control so as to reduce postoperative complications and adverse effects. So which gynecological inflammations that women with them cannot have painless artificial abortion? If they have an abortion without treatment, the surgical instruments need to pass through vagina, cervix into uterine cavity during the operation. Although strict surgical operation rules are followed, the cervical canal and internal orifice of cervix are expanded during the operation, providing opportunities for bacterial ascending infection; if the operation is performed in an unregular hospital, the surgical instruments are not strictly sterilized and the bacteria may enter uterine cavity directly along with the surgical instruments, making it easier to cause postoperative infection. 2.When women have cervical erosion, their resistance will decrease and vagina will secrete a lot under the stimulation of inflammation. At this time, both body and mind of women will be damaged. If there is no serious infection, they generally can have an abortion. 3.Pelvic inflammatory disease can be divided into acute pelvic inflammatory disease and chronic pelvic inflammatory disease. Chronic pelvic inflammatory disease is mostly caused by the extension of acute pelvic inflammatory disease. Women with mild pelvic inflammatory disease can have an abortion. However, if they have acute pelvic inflammatory disease or acute exacerbation period of chronic pelvic inflammatory disease, they'd better not have the operation.
