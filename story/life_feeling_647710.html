Happiness is to spend a lifetime with the person you love

Happiness is to spend a lifetime with a warm person. Today, I suddenly wanted to tell everyone in the world, cherish, cherish the people who love you, cherish those who are always protecting you and treating you as a treasure beside you. That person is really precious. When he is by your side, you do not realize how much you really love him. You may even think that the care you receive is something you deserve, rarely realizing his importance. Sometimes you are capricious and get angry with him. Sometimes you feel tired, sometimes you feel lack of passion. But when you no longer have him around to nag at you, no one to take care of you, no one to care about your pain, you suddenly realize that you have lost the person you actually loved the most.

Love is a long-lasting tug-of-war. From the time we met to the time we got to know each other, then fell in love, there has been a long line of tug-of-war. Two people who had no intersection for 20 years or even longer inevitably argue and have differences. We were originally not one, just because of love we came together, but love alone cannot solve all problems. Because our personalities are too strong, we love ourselves more, so we are always arguing non-stop, and after each argument, we realize our own capriciousness, yet we can't help being capricious again next time.

If there were regret pills sold in this world, I believe everyone would want to have them, so after each argument and cold war, we could take a pill and forget everything, start over. Unfortunately, there is no such medicine, and even if there was, taking regret pills all the time would develop resistance. Each argument leaves a little hurt, which accumulates over time and becomes your burden and his烦恼. No one would remember a single small argument, but what about two, three? Many times? All the arguments eventually turn into grievances, and one day, they will burst.

We all have loved, and I believe we have all done it seriously. Maybe some have lost for a long time, but when thinking back now, there is still a lingering pain, isn't there? Because what he once gave you and what he finally took away from you were equally as much, surprisingly fair. No one owes anyone, it's just that we didn't know how to cherish back then. You might say, last time I didn't cherish, this time I will definitely do well, definitely cherish! But how to cherish?

For your beloved one, you need to understand and comprehend his heart, think about what he really needs. When you think about what he needs, you have already gained his respect. For your beloved one, you need to protect him with all your heart. Don't think that because he is stronger than you or a big man, you can't protect him. Your love is more powerful than any encouragement, you are enough to protect the person you love. For your beloved one, you need to cherish him with all your heart. Every little thing he does for you, don't just thank him, but keep it in mind, often think about him. To lower his pride and endure your bad temper for love, to get used to habits completely different from yours, to take care of your time and routines, are all his sacrifices out of love.

Before you lose your temper, please take a deep breath first, breathe deeply five times, and then exhale slowly five times. Then speak. When you have an opinion or problem with him, you might as well write a letter to tell him. Believe that during the process of writing the letter, you have already calmed down, and the originally depressed mood has been relieved. Sometimes after finishing the letter, you may not be angry anymore. Writing a letter requires careful wording and thought, which makes you calm down first. Communicating through letters reduces verbal conflicts and increases communication, allowing your issues to be resolved in the most rational way, without resorting to arguments or cold wars.

When you feel wronged, you might as well tell him your thoughts directly, let him know your real thoughts, what you actually want to do, what you actually need him to do. Don't always make him guess your mind, because your thoughts are really hard to guess. Not all lovers have that sixth sense of mutual understanding. If he can't guess your mind, don't be angry, because you don't know how long he spent guessing and how many brain cells he wasted.

When you're sad, you might as well tell him your troubles first. He may not have a good solution, but at least your beloved one will surely give you comfort. Sometimes he will bluntly tell you that you are wrong, don't be angry, he is doing it for your good. He is just giving you guidance from an observer's perspective. After you calm down, you may find his method good, even if it's not feasible, you should understand his intentions. Conversely, when you notice something slightly wrong with him, care for him, pay attention to his brows, his mood. Try to understand him, even if you can't fully understand, attempt to learn about him.

Before making a decision, you might as well tell him your decision. This shows respect for him. He may not agree, but he will definitely be pleased because of your respect. If he happens to agree, congratulations, you have advanced your happiness. And when he makes a decision, don't interfere too much, give him as much space as possible. Believe me, although you love each other, you are still complete individuals, so let him decide on his own. Even if you don't agree, don't tell him when he is excited, encourage him! Give him some advice later in a gentle manner. When facing difficulties, don't underestimate your beloved one's ability, and don't blame him because of your own depression. It's not easy for him either. He faces difficulties just like you do, and he also has to consider your thoughts. In fact, it's harder for him. Work hard together, believe that you can solve anything together. Don't face difficulties alone, because no one wants their beloved one to face difficulties alone. It's equally important to go through hardships together as it is to enjoy happiness together. No matter what you face, believe that the two of you should be together.

When you feel that your love has lost its passion, you might as well reflect on yourself first. What have you done for your relationship? Is the current dullness partly due to you? How long has it been since you last gave him a small gift? How long has it been since you last kissed him without any reason? How long has it been since you last chatted and shared hearts with him? How long has it been since you last invited him to dinner or to watch a movie? Don't always think that he doesn't love you enough, or that he is not as thoughtful as before. Love matures, love grows, unchanging love does not exist. Don't be too calculative, don't always calculate.

When you face temptation, you might as well calm down and think about your past with him. The former him was also full of temptation and attraction to you. It's just that the passion has faded now. No one can maintain passion forever, no feelings can stay intense forever. The temptation you are facing now will one day return to calmness. Just like you and your current beloved one. Moreover, if your beloved one were facing such temptation, how would you hope he would act? Do what you hope he would do.

Remember, whatever happens, take a deep breath first, don't rush, don't get excited. Matters between two people should be handled by the two of you.

From birth until now, we have never really learned how to love. We have been experiencing the meaning of love bit by bit from reality and experience. I wrote these words not to boast about myself, in fact, I am also a child who doesn't understand love. Actually, I want to tell my brothers and sisters who have happiness around them and are happy now, cherish, protect, and care for your beloved one before you lose him. And I also want to tell those who are looking for their own happiness and are on the path of searching, after finding it, definitely cherish, protect, and care for your beloved one before you lose him...

Happiness is to spend a lifetime with a warm person. I sincerely hope that everyone has their own happiness!
