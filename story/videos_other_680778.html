1. Whitening + DNA Repair

The latest research found that DNA damage is one of the most fundamental reasons for dull skin tone and aging phenomena. By repairing DNA, it can not only prevent the formation of melanin in the skin but also help delay signs of aging such as wrinkles. When the skin is exposed to the sun, to protect DNA from ultraviolet radiation, the melanin in the skin begins to increase. After this temporary protection ends, the melanocytes will return to normal function. However, due to continuous UV exposure or slowing down of skin regeneration speed, the skin's elasticity and hydration functions weaken, affecting the melanin recovery process, thereby causing pigmentation. Skin cells under external pressure and stimulation will experience cell DNA damage, which also happens to melanocytes that affect skin tone. In response to this phenomenon, Dior discovered six specific types of DNA damage after cellular injury and found ways to repair these six types of DNA damage: the revolutionary patent S.N.O.W. complex (new oligonucleotide whitening factor). This cutting-edge technology derived from Nobel Prize research integrates the unique reverse transcription technology (Anti-Sense) in the beauty industry, accurately acting on the true source of spots and uneven skin tone - DNA, deeply nourishing the skin, repairing DNA to prevent its damage, thereby making the skin white and youthful.

2. Whitening + Detoxification and Antioxidation

In the process of resisting external harm and metabolism, our bodies produce toxic oxidants, and these toxic oxidants are the culprits behind wrinkles and spots known as reactive oxygen species. Reactive oxygen species are one of the main causes of wrinkle and spot formation and are seen as toxins that destroy the skin. Harmful substances entering the skin increase the oxidative stress of keratinocytes on the epidermis, which then gradually signal deeper into the melanocytes, ultimately leading to rapid melanin deposition and a dull complexion. Environmental pollution has significantly increased the content of reactive oxygen species in the air, which can cause mutations in skin cells, thereby leading to excessive melanin production. Now, under the shadow of the global crisis of increasingly serious environmental pollution, purification and detoxification, along with antioxidant effects, have become another important additional feature of whitening products! The GIVENCHY Brightening Intensive Care Serum first incorporated the potent anti-melanin compound Dermo-Melannin Break into its formula, effectively detoxifying through intensive care over 21 days, promoting cell regeneration and differentiation, thus restoring the skin's brightness; HR’s newly launched Hydrating Whitening Emulsion contains spot-deep removal factors and Vitamin C, effectively purifying the skin while hydrating and providing a secondary cleansing effect, balancing the skin's pH value, making the skin fresh and transparent; CLINIQUE added the patented plant-extracted CL-301 complex to its Crystal Radiance Essence, focusing on simultaneous whitening and antioxidation, gently preventing skin dullness. LANCME gathered extracts from five natural plants including ginkgo and licorice, comprehensively purifying the skin's internal environment, enhancing the skin's self-regeneration ability, making the skin whiter and finer from within.

Surveys show that over 30% of whitening product users worry about insufficient hydration and other visible signs of aging while using whitening products. From LAUDER's intensive brightening series, which adjusts collagen disorder, reduces dry lines and fine lines, and removes yellowish complexion, to la prairie's caviar essence pure brightness series, which contains precious imperial-grade gold caviar extract combined with anti-wrinkle peptide components, effectively enhancing skin elasticity and brightness, to sisley's plant-based whitening luminous serum, which forms a "lifting film" on the surface of the skin, tightening facial contours and lightening fine lines, women who want both whiteness and worry about wrinkles, looseness, and other signs of aging can now easily combat multiple signs of aging in one step!

5. Whitening + Fresh Oil Control

Many users of whitening products always complain about product comfort, either being too dry or too heavy. Especially as the weather gets warmer, the skin yearns for free breathing. Even Korean actresses, known for their "dedicated" whitening, have complaints about this. Korean actress Yoon Eun-hye once talked about her beauty secrets, lamenting the stickiness and heaviness of whitening essence products, sometimes waking up with an oily face. Therefore, while pursuing whitening effects, she pays the most attention to the thinness and freshness of whitening products. Indeed, lightweight and comfortable textures that absorb quickly are becoming the demand of more and more women. Brands receiving this information have developed a series of lightweight whitening products targeting oily and combination skin. Artistry's Pure White whitening series offers two different textures of whitening toner and lotion: moisturizing formula and lightweight formula, for different skin types to choose from. Aqua Sprina's newly developed Timeless Whitening Tightening Oil-Control Essence focuses on the prominent pores and surrounding skin in the T-zone, adjusting the obvious pores and dark areas around them caused by excessive sebum secretion. Meanwhile, effective whitening ingredients like wheat germ extract inhibit melanin formation and pigmentation, creating a clean T-zone that is fresh, radiant, and oil-free.
