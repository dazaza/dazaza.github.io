"Discipline Inspection Archives" Author: Wu Yuyang

by jqrxass on 2009-09-22 13:55:03

The newly appointed deputy secretary of the provincial Discipline Inspection Commission, Gu Zi Qiang, uncovered corruption within Luo Yuan's political circle. In order to expose this dark underbelly, he withstood immense pressure and maneuvered around the corrupt forces in Luo Yuan led by Party Secretary Bai Xiang Nan. As the investigation progressed further, Gu Zi Qiang discovered that the mistress of the corrupt official was actually a pure young girl he had secretly admired in the past... A fierce "disciplinary inspection battle" unfolded between the disciplinary inspection officers and the corrupt officials, revealing the conspiracy beneath the political power struggles.

"The Disciplinary Inspection Files" Part One Directory

Due to its inherent uniqueness, the disciplinary inspection unit has always been quite mysterious to ordinary people. The way disciplinary inspectors work and solve cases remains as elusive as seeing through a fog. The publication of "The Disciplinary Inspection Files" not only truthfully describes how the disciplinary personnel tenaciously fought against corrupt forces but also vividly portrays the real work and life conditions of these inspectors, making it a rare work of realistic fiction.

"The Disciplinary Inspection Files" Part Two Midnight Horror

Xiao Yun Pu once said she was like the rose in his palm, cold yet unpredictable. Bai Xiang Nan had also described her beauty as being unrestrained like a rose when she first entered Luo Yuan University for her studies. Back then, he was just an powerless director of the Elderly Cadres Bureau, yet she almost instantly fell inexplicably in love with this middle-aged man who was twenty years older than her, despite having no shortage of princely suitors chasing after her.

"The Disciplinary Inspection Files" Part Three Shocking Emotions by the River

Xiao Mei's mouth was tightly stuffed with a dirty handkerchief by those ruffians. Her hands and feet were also bound, her whole body pressed tightly into the corner by the window at the back row, her face filled with a look of utter despair, wanting to cry but unable to do so. "Xiang Nan," she silently called out Bai Xiang Nan's name in her heart. Could this be his way of repaying all those years of her meticulous care for him and the deep affection she once held for him?

Part Four - Storm over Luojiang Dam: "Is that Xiao Bai?" In the early morning, Bai Xiang Nan was still asleep when the phone in the bedroom woke him up. Stretching lazily, Bai Xiang Nan grabbed the receiver from the bedside phone and nonchalantly placed it to his ear, sleepily responding, "Hello, this is Bai Xiang Nan. May I ask who's calling?"
