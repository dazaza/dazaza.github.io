How was Dora A Rabbit trained?

by ofree on 2007-08-30 17:57:54

This is a crazy age, where heaven and hell are just a step apart, and thus angels easily stumble into falling. If you understand this principle, then you won't be surprised why the Angel Rabbit has turned into Dora A Rabbit.

Dora A Rabbit was not originally called Dora A Rabbit. It once had an angelic name - Super Rabbit. This name accompanied it for 9 years. In the past, it was full of ideals, with noble character, using its own magic to clean up system trash and maintain system security. It was famous for being easy to set up and having a friendly interface. More importantly, all its services were free, so many people loved it.

However, purity is also a kind of poverty. The more obscure it remains, the more it yearns for attention. The rabbit was no different. Relying on donations from friends clearly wasn't enough to solve its livelihood issues. So, it thought about taking on other jobs. Thus, bundled services came about. Just earning some advertising fees, what's wrong with that? It's better than you paying that so-called Optimization Master, the rabbit thought.

But energy is limited while desires are infinite. When life is too comfortable, one forgets their ideals; when life isn't comfortable, one's beliefs wear away. Sometimes, the more you get, the less satisfied you feel. The rabbit craved to have more magic and skills in the shortest time possible, to become even more eye-catching, but didn't have the energy or interest to delve into technology. Then there was the "take what you need" approach, eliminating the unnecessary and making use of others' work.

Thus, there came Rabbit Knows, Upgrade Angel, Rabbit Xunlei, Rabbit QQ, Rabbit Guard, Rabbit Audio-Video, Rabbit IE, Rabbit WMP, Rabbit Cleanup Assistant, Rabbit Desktop Show, Rabbit Desktop Search Sprite, Rabbit Installation CD, etc. The business grew larger and larger, the variety increased, while the cost decreased and the technical content lowered. And that's how the rabbit got lost. But the rabbit didn't admit it was falling. It thought this was just a lifestyle and shouldn't be criticized.

Unfortunately, some mistakes aren't worth forgiving. People mocked the rabbit, saying, "How come you're like Doraemon, having everything you want? Might as well call you Dora A Rabbit from now on." The rabbit got very angry, but it refused to back down. It thought it needed to persevere at this point and didn't need to heed others' criticism. So it continued recklessly. Therefore, if one day you see Rabbit Optimization Master or Rabbit WIN XP, don't be surprised...
