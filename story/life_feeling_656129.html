Lonely winter, the cold neglected the soul

The lonely winter has neglected the heart.

Online submission.

The white snowflakes fall, carrying away the ups and downs of human life; deep feelings shatter the vows of eternal love. Gazing at the snowflakes, like spirits dancing on earth, my heart becomes moist with emotion but cannot carry the beauty of each flake as it slips through my fingers and vanishes. Watching your quietly retreating figure, tears well up in my eyes, slowly falling, chilling my cheeks, and grieving my heart.

Snow and rain fall profusely, freezing the earth. Intense longing pierces the heart. The train moving forward has already disappeared into the distance, and you have gradually faded from my sight. I stand alone, gazing at the bleak land, my heart growing older, abandoning once cherished dreams, and shattering beautiful memories.

Coldness cuts like a sharp blade against the body, while loneliness devours the soul like a ravenous wolf. Ahead, all I see is an endless expanse of snow, its foggy shadows obscuring the path. White stretches endlessly before me. I want to forget, but in the distance lies a dim night, cold and desolate, swallowing the spirit. Standing forlornly in a corner, my thin and slightly emaciated figure trembles against the biting wind without a word, only ripples of sorrow echoing in my heart.

The snow continues to fall gently. Stretching out my hands, I grasp the pristine and delicate snowflakes, and waves of coldness penetrate my body. Yet, I no longer feel the warmth that once held promise. Those tender moments can now only be sought in dreams. The snowflakes bloom beautifully, and the fragrance of plum blossoms spreads resolutely. In the distance, children joyfully play, building snowmen, having snowball fights, writing, drawing, bouncing, and jumping. In front of houses, adults sweep the snow with brooms, one stroke at a time, revealing muddy ground beneath. They breathe heavily in the thick air, looking toward the vast expanse of snow far away, their faces beaming with happiness. There lie their crops, their source of livelihood, and their hope for the future. Auspicious snow signifies a bountiful harvest. Beneath the snow, vegetables accumulate energy, destined to grow more vigorously next year.

Each scene resembles the past so vividly, yet memory cannot hold your steps as you journey far away. Familiar scenarios remain, leaving behind only the innocent joys of childhood. One by one, the curtains fall, and the audience disperses, leaving me alone to wander aimlessly. Laughter and liveliness surround me, but I no longer feel genuine emotions. A sense of helplessness turns into countless snowflakes falling from the sky, dampening my broken heart.

In this icy and snowy land, affection falls upon the winter kingdom. Each tree dons a pure white coat, like holy angels guarding our sacred place. A small stream flows quietly around here, not shimmering with light, but clad in a thick white armor, steadfastly guarding. Guarding, when the holy angels arrive with their mission, will I still see your captivating figure? Will we be able to hold hands and walk towards our dream destination, fulfilling our vows of eternal love? Beneath the sky, tear drops accompany the snowflakes, falling gently.

Snowflakes are pure and divine, plum blossoms exude a subtle fragrance. Can my heart find solace within you, escaping loneliness?
