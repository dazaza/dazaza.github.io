Poor enterprise management, debts reaching over 18 million yuan, company mergers and acquisitions encountering obstacles, decoration projects unable to continue normally... At around 3 PM yesterday afternoon, the company's Sina verified official microblog suddenly announced its bankruptcy, being called by real estate professionals as "the first nationwide case of an official microblog announcing the company's bankruptcy." However, after just over an hour, this microblog post was deleted, citing "account hacked, message not officially released."

News Follow-up

Relevant personnel from Nanjing Yaguangya stated that their company was originally a well-performing decoration company in Nanjing. Due to poor management and excessively high operating costs, internal capital chains broke, causing severe losses to the company, with debts reaching over 18 million yuan. Additionally, the company borrowed 2 million yuan from the bank, and because Mr. Xu, the boss, couldn't repay the loan, his BMW car has been seized. Currently, all the company's accounts have been frozen by the bank.

At around 5 PM yesterday afternoon, Chen Lijun, General Manager of Weile Decoration Company, told reporters that currently, the police, Baimaxian District Housing and Urban-Rural Development Bureau, and other departments have intervened in handling the situation and have conducted multiple negotiations. However, due to the involvement of two companies, the situation is complicated, and there is currently no complete solution plan. Under the coordination of relevant departments, the two companies will further negotiate.
