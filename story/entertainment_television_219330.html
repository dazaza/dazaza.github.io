Original Plot of the TV series "Canghai":

The defeat in the Kinmen Sea Battle caught the attention of the top levels of the Republic, leading the Chinese Navy onto the path of self-strengthening. Wang ShanKui, originally from the Army, transferred to the Navy after his comrade-in-arms fell in Kinmen, vowing to brave the waves and dominate the seas. Despite countless hardships and setbacks, he persevered, fighting and sacrificing alongside a group of patriots like Ma Jiancheng to build up the Navy. The standoff between enemies, the clash of old and new ideas, and the entanglements of family emotions constantly tested him. Decades passed swiftly, as the Chinese Navy grew stronger. An aging Wang Shankui nurtured a new generation of outstanding Chinese naval talents represented by Wang Haijun, who will lead the Chinese Navy to victory in modern warfare and defend national security.
