Is your spouse an alien? 5 million aliens hiding on Earth

by joepue on 2008-08-30 12:42:34

According to American UFO expert and physicist Dr. James, as reported by the World News Paper, there are currently 5 million aliens in the United States. They look no different from Earthlings, have a balanced gender ratio, most of them are married, but they do not have children.

Dr. James has been studying the marriages between Earthlings and aliens over the past decade and has discovered at least 1,000 cases of marriage between humans and aliens. He said that every American has a 50% chance of marrying an alien.

Dr. James said it is still unclear why aliens come to the United States. "Perhaps they are here to conquer the world, or to experience strange lives on this strange planet called Earth, or because their own planets are too crowded, so they come to Earth to seize territory."

"When aliens first visited Earth, they were worried that it would be difficult for them to realize their dream of marrying Earthlings. But later, they found out that not everyone on Earth could find a suitable partner to marry and have children with, so they eliminated all concerns, doing their best to charm Earthlings," said Dr. James.

Dr. James also made a surprising discovery during his research: among all Earth marriages, those involving aliens are the most stable. Although the divorce rate in the U.S. has always been as high as 50%, the divorce rate in marriages between Earthlings and aliens is only 10%. Dr. James explained that aliens take better care of their marriages with Earthlings than Earthlings themselves.

To help Americans determine whether their spouses are aliens, Dr. James listed several criteria:

If your husband is an alien, he will:

- Like fixing things around the house because they naturally have strong hands-on skills;

- Unlike Earthlings, they don't like holding the TV remote control and frequently changing channels. They always patiently absorb information from each TV program;

- After making love, they like to keep communicating with their partners because they want to understand more about Earthlings' views on **;

- Have poor sense of direction (one might suspect that they stay on Earth because they can't find their way home).

If your wife is an alien, she will:

- Be obsessed with sports events. When watching sports programs, she remains unusually quiet and doesn't need to consult her husband about sports-related issues;

- Handle household chores in a unique way and does not allow men to interfere. They believe that men's active help with housework is an insult to them;

- To maintain a moderate figure and extraordinary charm, they insist on daily exercise and pay attention to a balanced diet;

- Have extremely strong logic when arguing with their husbands.
