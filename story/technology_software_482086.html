A passionate person must be philic, but a philic person is not necessarily a passionate one. (Qing Dynasty - Zhang Chao)

One eye sparkles with the light of love, while the other burns with the vulgar fire of selfishness. (Dickens)

Falling in love is natural for people, and so is indulgence. (Plautus)

Love is but a madness. (Shakespeare)

Love is a tyrant in heart; it makes reason unclear, judgment confused; it doesn't listen to advice, and rushes straight to madness. (John Ford)

Love is a kind of mental illness, there is no good treatment for it. (Theophrastus)

Love is the nature of life, like the sun to shine; it is the most pleasant and natural enjoyment of human soul; without it, man is ignorant and pitiful. Those who have not enjoyed its joy are equivalent to living in vain, suffering in vain. (Traherne)

Love has a thousand touching strings and yet each note is different. (George Crabbe)

Cupid can conquer all, let's yield to Cupid. (Virgil)

Love, it is love that propels the development of the world.

No rope can compare with the double strand line twisted by love in terms of tensile strength. (Robert Burton)

I tell you, Cupid is the second sun of all things, he shines where, there will be spring. (Chapman)

Absence makes love grow hotter, and meeting makes it more solid. (Thomas Fuller)

Love is not subject to constraints; once the system wants to exert power, Cupid will fly away far and high; Cupid, like other gods, is also free. (Chaucer)

Short-term separation promotes love, while long-term separation strangles it. (Charles de Saint-Evremond)
