Nine things not suitable after meals

by huiming on 2011-12-22 16:40:04

Remind women not to do the following things after meals, otherwise it will affect your health and the luster of your skin. Pay attention to the following points, which will make you look younger for many years.

1. Do not eat sugar immediately after a meal.

Sugar is easily converted into fat, causing obesity. Sugar also reduces the secretion function of the pancreas, promoting the occurrence of diabetes.

2. Do not engage in strenuous exercise immediately after a meal.

During strenuous exercise, blood flow to the limbs increases, affecting the blood supply to the gastrointestinal tract, which affects gastric juice secretion and leads to poor digestion of food. At the same time, after a meal, the stomach volume increases, and movement can cause ptosis of the stomach.

3. Do not read immediately after a meal.

Reading immediately after a meal can reduce the relative amount of blood in the gastrointestinal tract, affecting gastric juice secretion. Over time, this can lead to symptoms such as indigestion, bloating, and stomach pain.

4. Do not take a shower immediately after a meal.

When taking a shower, the skin capillaries dilate and fill with blood, reducing the blood flow to the digestive tract and affecting the digestion and absorption of food.

So, how long after a meal should one wait before engaging in the above activities? The retention time of food in the stomach is about 1 hour for carbohydrates, 2-3 hours for proteins, and 5-6 hours for fats. Therefore, at least 1 hour after a meal should pass before engaging in the above activities. However, flexibility is also important. For example, if you feel thirsty, you should drink some water after a meal instead of rigidly sticking to rules.

5. Do not eat fruit immediately after a meal.

Fruits contain flavonoid compounds that are converted into dihydroxybenzoic acid by intestinal bacteria after ingestion. Vegetables consumed contain thiocyanate salts, and under the influence of these two chemical substances, thyroid function is interfered with, potentially leading to non-iodine-induced goiter.

6. Do not drink tea immediately after a meal.

Tannins in tea can bind with protein and iron in food, producing colloids or precipitates that are difficult to absorb. Long-term effects may result in iron-deficiency anemia and protein deficiency diseases.

7. Do not smoke immediately after a meal.

After a meal, gastric motility accelerates, blood circulation increases, and capillaries expand, promoting the absorption of harmful substances in cigarettes. Smoking after a meal absorbs harmful substances in cigarettes more than ten times compared to regular smoking. Smokers, please pay attention to your health.

8. Do not drink a large amount of water immediately after a meal.

Drinking water immediately after a meal increases the pressure inside the stomach, causing food that hasn't been digested yet to enter the small intestine. Additionally, drinking water dilutes gastric juice, weakening its digestive power and making it less effective for gastric acid to kill bacteria, which can easily lead to gastrointestinal diseases.

9. Do not drink carbonated beverages immediately after a meal.

Carbonated beverages entering the stomach dilute gastric juice, affecting digestion, reducing appetite, producing carbon dioxide, increasing gastric pressure, and leading to acute gastric dilation.
