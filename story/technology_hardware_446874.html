Man posts online to look for a girl he met on a bus by chance

by zzf000zxyn7 on 2011-06-16 11:29:23

"Only because I took an extra glance at you in the crowd, I couldn't forget your looks ever since..." On the night of New Year's Eve, a song named "The Legend" by Faye Wong, the "Songstress Queen," could be described as "lingering melody." Unbeknownst to all, before the melody faded away, a real-life version of "The Legend" unfolded in Zhenjiang: on the first day of the New Year, a man from Zhenjiang encountered a "beautiful big-eyed girl" on bus No. 2. When the girl smiled and looked back just before getting off the bus, the man couldn't help but remember her. Subsequently, the man, under the online name "longlonghh," posted on the MY0511 forum, a popular website in Zhenjiang, eagerly hoping for a reunion. Currently, this post titled "The Beautiful Girl I Met on the First Day of the New Year" has been viewed by nearly ten thousand netizens. While wishing the young man good luck in finding his destined match, netizens collectively cheered him on with "加油" (which means "come on" or "go for it").

The Devoted Young Man Posts Looking for the "Big-Eyed Girl"

"Today around 2 pm, while riding bus No. 2, I saw a beautiful big-eyed girl on the bus, which made my heart flutter and left an indelible impression. Just before she got off the bus, we exchanged glances. Her smile was truly captivating! Although I know the chances of meeting again are almost non-existent, I still hope for that slim possibility."

This post on the Zhenjiang MY0511 website was put up by "longlonghh" at 2:38 pm on the first day of the New Year. By noon the next day, over 8,000 netizens had viewed the post, with 60 netizens responding in support or cheering on the poster.

Netizens Post Romantic English Songs for Encouragement

I noticed that in response to the poster's courage and romance, several netizens quickly responded with praise and encouragement: Netizen "Zao Yi Mei MM" replied within a minute of "longlonghh's" post: "Wow, today is the rare occasion when New Year's Day coincides with Valentine's Day, your romantic destiny has arrived; hurry up and pursue her!" Netizen "Bei Xiao Yu Chi Le" remarked after reading the post: "Although the snow blankets the ground, spring is already in the air!" "Jingcai Zai Wo" encouraged: "It takes five hundred lifetimes of glances to exchange one passing encounter in this life;" "Bernie Sun" said: "What good fortune (destiny)! Who would have thought that knocking so many prayer beads in a past life would result in such a meeting!"

Encouraged by everyone, "longlonghh" suddenly realized: "I completely forgot, today isn't just the first day of the New Year, it's also Valentine's Day. Could this really be a sign that I should find a partner?"

A netizen even uploaded a classic romantic English love song along with the post. As netizens browsed through the page, this yet-to-be-named English love song played softly and automatically.

Awaiting the Real-Life Version of "The Legend"

Perhaps "longlonghh's" story coincides too perfectly with Faye Wong's "The Legend," making it seem like "The Legend" was written specifically for "longlonghh." Thus, netizen "Xia Xue" bluntly stated: "The Legend - simply because I took an extra glance at her in the crowd." Many netizens directly quoted excerpts from "The Legend's" lyrics in their replies: "Just because I took an extra glance at you in the crowd," and netizen "VV99" went to great lengths to not only record the entire lyrics of "The Legend" but also linked the audio file of the song.

Moreover, a large number of netizens used icons or words to cheer on "longlonghh": "Ping An Shi Shi Jiu Shi Fu" said: "Strongly support the original poster, come on! May your dreams come true and perform a real-life version of 'The Legend' in Zhenjiang." Netizen "17 Degrees" commented: "With fate, you'll meet again. Of course, you need to make efforts yourself. Come on, wish you good luck!"

Netizens Suggest Ingenious Ways to Find the Person

Netizen "zjxing" came up with ingenious suggestions for "longlonghh" to find the "beautiful big-eyed girl," listing no less than "five methods": "I think some methods can be tried: 1. Ask the bus company leader how much it costs to hang a missing person notice on bus No. 2; 2. The local evening paper classifieds only cost 60 yuan per day to post two lines (looking for contact information of the beautiful girl who exchanged glances on bus No. 2, include your phone number); 3. Through connections, ask the Public Security Bureau's household registration department to check related photo records on the computer; 4. Find a computer expert to simulate a portrait based on your description; 5. Focus on the area around the bus stop where she got off, mobilize compassionate or loving people to help search."
