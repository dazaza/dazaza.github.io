The direction of history is irreversible. Reform and opening-up has led China into a new spring since then, during which countless model villages have emerged. This book records the development changes of 5 villages at different periods in the east, west, south, north and central regions after reform and opening-up, describes the transformation and innovation of contemporary Chinese farmers' thoughts on the road to wealth, reviews the reform process with an economic perspective and historical vision, shows the development of the 30 years of reform and opening-up, and explores the future trend of rural China.

Within 5 kilometers to the north lies the Huai River, historically plagued by natural disasters nine out of ten years. Every year, many people come here from all over the country and even the world. However, most of them do not come to see the former residence of Emperor Taizu of Ming or listen to the Fengyang flower drum, but rather to take a look at a remote small village 30 kilometers east of the county town.
