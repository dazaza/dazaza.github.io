Users who supply power with a generator should note: Power plug must be unplugged when stopping work. Voltage cannot exceed 240V!

Do not perform welding on the steel plate while the magnetic drill is working! Welding generates strong currents that can damage the magnetic base and motor of the magnetic drill!

Managers please note: Prohibit operators from rough handling, such as pressing down hard for faster speed, which damages both the drill bit and the machine; not using coolant; hitting forcefully when the drill bit gets stuck; failing to clean iron filings in time, leading to excessive filings wrapping around the drill bit and possibly jumping into the machine to burn out the circuit board.
