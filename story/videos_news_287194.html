14-Year-Old Girl Co-habits with Man, Refuses to Return Home, Says She Wants to Marry Him (Photo)

by yyusk on 2009-12-01 09:37:45

The mother was extremely upset: Who can help me persuade my daughter to go home?

"My daughter used to be very sensible. I shouldn't have sent her to school alone in another place. In the first year of junior high school, she picked up the bad habit of smoking and became addicted to surfing the Internet, often skipping classes. Now she doesn't want to go to school or go home. She is cohabiting with her boyfriend and says she wants to get married." Yesterday morning, in a rented apartment in Xiuying District of Haikou City, Ms. Li from Hunan Province, who works in Haikou, said sorrowfully as she looked at the text messages her daughter had sent her. Ms. Li urgently told the reporter that her daughter is only 14 years old. She hopes her daughter will return to her side soon and go back to school, but she does not know how to win her daughter's heart... Reporter Su Zhong wrote and photographed.

A girl in the first year of junior high school learned to smoke and often skipped class

Yesterday morning, I met Ms. Li in a rented house on Shuchang Street in Xiuying District of Haikou City.

Ms. Li told the reporter that she is 38 years old and is from Hunan Province. She came to Hainan to work in 1994 and developed feelings for a local man. Due to family opposition, they did not get married, but they had a daughter named Xiaoyu (a pseudonym) in April 1995.

"Later, due to differences in feelings, we separated, and Xiaoyu has been living with me ever since." Ms. Li said that when Xiaoyu was young, she was very obedient. During elementary school, she insisted on writing a diary every day, recording her daily mood, and her academic performance was also excellent. But after Xiaoyu entered the first year of junior high school in 2007, everything quietly changed.

Ms. Li said that at the time, she sent Xiaoyu to a school in Lingao County to study in the first year of junior high school. Perhaps because there was no family member around to discipline her, Xiaoyu became addicted to surfing the Internet and even learned to smoke, later gradually leading to frequent truancy.

Living with her boyfriend, refusing to go home, claiming to get married

Ms. Li told the reporter that during this year's National Day holiday, she brought Xiaoyu back to Haikou and sent her to a middle school in Haikou to study in the third year of junior high school, but after just a few days, Xiaoyu left school and ran away. A few days later, she received a text message from Xiaoyu: "I've already found a boyfriend working in a factory. He treats me very well. We are already living together. That man said he wants to marry me, and I want to marry him." Ms. Li asked her daughter to come home, but she was refused. "Xiaoyu is only 14 years old. She doesn't understand what love is, and her body hasn't fully developed yet. How can she get married at such a young age?" Ms. Li said sorrowfully. She strongly opposed it. However, Xiaoyu seemed determined and even said she wanted to introduce the man to her.
