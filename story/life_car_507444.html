With the development of the automobile industry, the apprentice of car beauty has become the hottest position nowadays. What are the main job responsibilities for a new apprentice in the car beauty industry? What matters need to be paid attention to? The editor of China Auto Beauty Talent Network summarizes the following points for all prospective apprentices. What matters should car beauty apprentices pay attention to?

A. What does a car beauty apprentice mainly do?

Car beauty apprentices usually start with decoration, assisting the decoration technicians and beauty technicians in various tasks. The focus is on "beauty", with an emphasis on mastering the knowledge and skills of car beauty. Most car beauty apprentices are newcomers or newly graduated students. As long as they work hard enough, pay enough attention to details, and are sincere enough, anyone can become the best car beautician within a year!

B. How to handle interpersonal relationships as a car beauty apprentice?

As the saying goes, learn to be a person before learning to do things. Apprentices must learn to handle interpersonal relationships. First of all, they need to get along well with their masters, be humble in learning from others, and be grateful to those who teach them. Only by lowering one's own identity and status can more knowledge be absorbed. In addition, after work, spend more time building relationships with experienced colleagues. Sincere treatment of people will make your learning environment smoother.

In fact, learning car beauty technology doesn't involve high-end content. As long as you work hard enough, pay enough attention to details, and are sincere enough, anyone can become the best car beautician within a year!

C. Why do beauty apprentices keep washing cars?

It is quite normal to wash cars for half a year while learning at the store. Don't think that washing cars is just physical labor with nothing to learn. Actually, there are huge commercial secrets hidden in washing cars. Through washing cars, we discover issues with the car's paint surface, tires, glass, leather, seat covers, floor mats, dashboards, etc. And these discovered issues, after communicating with the car owner, can turn into sales. Without washing cars, these opportunities would be lost.

Additionally, the process of washing cars allows for the accumulation of a large amount of car paint analysis experience. A mature car beauty master should be able to tell at a glance whether the car paint is original factory paint, and what grade of polishing agent should be used to remove scratches. This makes customers feel trusted. These things cannot be achieved overnight, but through daily washing of cars, they can become reality in the shortest possible time.

D. Is it better to go to school to learn or to become an apprentice at a store if you want to be a car beauty apprentice?

If you have no concept of cars at all, you can first go to school to receive some basic education. However, most schools do not provide practical operation opportunities. Therefore, the best shortcut to becoming a car beauty apprentice is to become an apprentice at a real store. Generally speaking, the salary for apprentices is not high, and some stores may not even offer wages, only providing accommodation. Use this time to strive to learn as much knowledge as possible, utilizing the technical resources and customer resources available at the store to improve the proficiency of front-line operations and exercise the ability to communicate face-to-face with car owners.
