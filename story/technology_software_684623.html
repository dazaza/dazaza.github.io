Man causes fatal car accident while driving drunk, resulting in the death of his daughter and friend

by sehetdn3hur on 2012-03-01 09:24:39

A good friend claimed to be the hit-and-run driver. On the morning after the incident, Wen admitted to the act of impersonation: "I know that what I did was wrong. At the time, I didn't think too much about it or the consequences, and now I deeply regret it." After the truth came out, the police found the real hit-and-run driver Xu, and placed Xu under criminal detention. On February 21st, Xu was released on bail due to suspicion of traffic manslaughter. After responsibility determination, it was decided that Xu bore the main responsibility for this accident.

After the incident, Xu reached a civil agreement with the close relatives of Wang. Xu compensated them with RMB 230,000 yuan, and Wang's close relatives earnestly requested to reduce or exempt Xu from criminal responsibility. The procuratorate believed that Xu violated road traffic safety regulations, drove in violation of rules, caused a major accident resulting in 2 deaths, which constituted an especially heinous situation. He should be held criminally responsible for traffic manslaughter. Currently, the case is still under review for prosecution.

At the scene of the car accident, Wen saw a shaken Xu. Xu asked Wen to exchange clothes with him and let Wen impersonate the hit-and-run driver. "At the time, I saw him crying, and we usually got along well. He was afraid of being punished for drunk driving, so I felt sorry for him and exchanged clothes with him," said Wen.

After exchanging clothes, the traffic police arrived at the scene and asked who was the driver of the minivan. Wen stepped forward and said that he was the driver. "At the time, I received a call, took out my mobile phone to answer it, and the car veered to the right. Due to high speed, I couldn't react in time and collided with the rear of a semi-truck parked on the side of the road. The speed at the time was about 90 kilometers per hour, and I hadn't been drinking," Wen lied deliberately when questioned by the traffic police, trying to get away with it.

After the accident, the injured were sent to the hospital for emergency treatment. However, Xu's 2-year-old daughter and his friend Wang ultimately died after unsuccessful rescue attempts. The police immediately began investigating the accident and actively sought eyewitnesses. After careful investigation, the police quickly found discrepancies.

"I was shopping near May Day Square when Xu called me and said he had been drinking, asking me to drive for him," Wen said. He and Xu were good friends, so upon receiving Xu's call, he immediately hailed a taxi and rushed to the scene of the accident. "I saw that Xu's car had suffered a serious accident, his wife was trapped in the passenger seat, and the firefighters were carrying out rescue operations. The scene was chaotic."

After the accident, seeing someone trapped in the minivan, a person on the semi-truck dialed 119 for help. Xu, seeing his daughter severely injured, squatted by the roadside and cried uncontrollably. Since he knew he was driving under the influence of alcohol, in order to evade responsibility, he decided to have a friend impersonate him, and thus called his friend Wen.

Xu's 2-year-old daughter passed away after unsuccessful rescue attempts.

Around 20:40 that day, after the birthday banquet ended, Xu drove his minivan north along Northwest Road. There were some of his relatives and friends in the car, with his wife holding their daughter in the passenger seat. "I saw a semi-truck about 30 meters ahead. At first, I couldn't tell if the semi-truck was moving or parked. When it was about 10 meters away, I realized the semi-truck was parked on the side of the road!" Seeing that he was about to collide with the rear of the semi-truck, Xu hurriedly turned the steering wheel to the left, but it was already too late. The front-right corner of the minivan collided with the semi-truck.

The pain of losing his daughter plunged Xu into deep self-reproach. However, besides mental torment, he may also face criminal punishment.

"I drank about 1 liang (50g) of white liquor and one bottle of beer." Around 18:30 on January 6th this year, in a private room of a restaurant in Ganjingzi District, more than 20 people gathered together, raising their glasses, creating a joyful atmosphere. That day was Xu's 36th birthday, and family and friends gathered to celebrate his birthday. Xu was very happy at the dinner table, even though he drove to the banquet that day, he still drank some alcohol.
