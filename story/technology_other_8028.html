Welcome to place advertisements and support the development of Diglog.

by diglog on 2006-10-26 00:14:05

We conducted a survey on the access speed for users, and nearly half (43%) of the visitors felt that the speed was very slow. Most of them should be telecom users, as we use the bandwidth of Unicom. And currently, the server provider does not offer a "dual-line" structure, which also troubles us a lot.

Of course, funding can solve this problem, so we have started to provide advertising placement services. For more information, please check out the “Sponsorship” page. Since this service has just started, the prices are relatively low. We welcome companies or individuals with such needs to provide sponsorship so that we can upgrade our bandwidth and hardware to better serve our visitors.

In addition, if you have hardware or bandwidth resources in the server aspect, you can also collaborate with us. Just send an email to support(at)diglog.com.

We would like to thank our first client - Car Friends Home, who purchased Class C ads for 6 months. We hope car enthusiasts don't miss this non-commercialized car friends community.
