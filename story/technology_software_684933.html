Uniqlo's official Taobao store

by wgyuvs8a on 2012-03-01 11:37:13

After repeated trials, Fast Retailing Co., Ltd. finally established the SPA business operation model, reorganizing the entire process from planning, production, logistics to sales by directly listening to customer needs. "We take on 100% of the risks ourselves and control the whole process ourselves," eliminating unnecessary middlemen such as agents and intermediaries, thoroughly implementing low-cost operations. The stores also adopt a self-service warehouse supermarket style, but their pursuit of improving product quality and service is endless.

In order to manage each production link more specifically, Fast Retailing hired many senior technical consultants in the fiber clothing production industry to provide technical guidance for every step from raw materials to clothing production, ensuring product quality through improving the production technology level of the factory, which they call the "Master Engineer" project. Almost all clothing items are personally tried on by senior management during the development stage, and all developers are required to focus on creating the most exquisite single items.

They do not relax their standards for service levels either. To provide customers with a relaxed and pleasant shopping environment, they avoid aggressive sales tactics and instead focus on ensuring a clean and comfortable mall environment along with an abundant and easy-to-select product display. When customers need help, store staff will promptly appear. Stores that often exceed thousands of square meters in size, spacious like large warehouses, have products displayed and arranged like a supermarket that can be visited daily - all goods are neatly placed on metal shelves that reach up to two people's height, with one side piled with baskets identical to those in supermarkets. Goods almost cover all types of clothing needed for daily life, including children's socks, umbrellas, wallets, hats, gloves, and other small accessories, all easily found in clearly divided sections of the store. From the customer demographics of the specialty stores, it covers various consumer groups, truly reflecting the operating goal that "casual wear is clothing that transcends age, gender, nationality, occupation, education, and other boundaries." The combination of China's garment production with Fast Retailing's unique marketing philosophy and operational model has created today's casual wear giant.

Company Origin

Fast Retailing Co., Ltd., which operates the UNIQLO brand, was established in 1953 and originally started as a small clothing store selling suits. Later, it aimed to develop using the latest product in clothing - casual wear. The current chairman and general manager, Tadashi Yanai, graduated from Waseda University's economics department early in his career, entered Fast Retailing in August 1972, and became the company's chairman and general manager in September 1984. In 1982, when Yanai, who was already a dedicated director of the company, was on an inspection trip in the United States, he was inspired by the warehouse-style sales method used on American university campuses. Upon returning to Japan, he proposed trying out a warehouse-type self-service shopping method in clothing stores and introduced the large-scale shopping mall-style clothing sales method for the first time in Japan, achieving low-cost shop operations through a unique product planning, development, and sales system.
