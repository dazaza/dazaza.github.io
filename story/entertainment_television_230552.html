PK reasons have improved significantly

This season of Happy Girl Voice places great emphasis on selecting opponents for PK matches. In previous competitions, various reasons for choosing opponents became hot topics among netizens. In this match, the director group seemed to deliberately arrange it, requiring the four Happy Girls to choose their challengers in the first round. Yuke Wei chose Li Xiaoyun, her "enemy" from the last match, because "I fell where I got up again when I chose her last time," while Li Xiaoyun chose a new opponent, "I choose Jiang Yingrong, I want to choose someone with comprehensive strength"; Jiang Yingrong and Li Xiaoyun shared a telepathic connection, "The Ballad Prince will have a big challenge." Anyway, these PK reasons seem to have been carefully thought out this time, unlike the previous two matches where the reasons were too ridiculous, leaving behind talk handles for netizens to discuss.

Jiang Yingrong wins three cover voices

The first round of PK matches was extraordinarily intense. In previous competitions, Huang Ying, Yuke Wei, and Li Xiaoyun were all types who quietly sang, but they all challenged dancing this time. Jiang Yingrong is originally a dance master, but she still chose a higher difficulty Indian song "JIA HO", and although her collaboration with the Latin dance master had slight flaws, she still defeated Li Xiaoyun, who challenged rap music again, by a large margin. In this match, Jiang Yingrong won the cover voice again and was the first to enter the finals. Whether it was the jazz dance at the beginning or the extremely cool stool dance, it seemed as if a new generation of dancing queen was born from this stage.

Enemies meet Yuke Wei is eliminated

The biggest highlight of this match was the repeated PK between Yuke Wei and Li Xiaoyun, "Enemies do not meet unless they are destined." In the first round of dance PK, the three judges chose Li Xiaoyun, and Yuke Wei then turned the tables, winning in the second round of duet singing. In the third round of the "Killer Move" segment, Yuke Wei's song "Itch" was heart-wrenching, defeating Li Xiaoyun by a large margin again. However, Yuke Wei did not laugh until the end. The six eliminated Happy Girl Voice contestants chose one person to directly advance between them. The decisive vote was cast by Liu Xi Jun, who was personally eliminated by Yuke Wei last period. Without hesitation, Liu Xi Jun raised Li Xiaoyun's hand, sending her directly into the top three. In the PK against Huang Ying, Yuke Wei was surprisingly overtaken by Huang Ying in terms of votes, regretfully stopping short of the top three. She deeply bowed to all the audiences who loved her and disappeared from this stage she adored.

We cannot evaluate the social value and influence created by talent shows, but we recognize a batch of overnight-famous talent show stars such as Li Yu Chun, Zhang Liang Ying, Zhang Jie, Ji Jie, etc. From Super Girl, Happy Boy to Absolute Singing, talent shows emerged suddenly, dominating the ratings king throne for several years. In 2009, "Happy Girl Voice" made a strong comeback with rule reforms, shortened competition periods, cancellation of text message voting... Hunan TV calmly faced all these changes.

The 2009 Happy Girl Voice expanded from the previous five Super Girl regions to 18 ground-based singing zones nationwide, integrating 18 cities including Changsha, Nanjing, Chengdu, Shenyang, Wuhan, Zhengzhou, Lanzhou, Kunming, Hangzhou, etc., forming a Happy Alliance. In addition, six online direct zones led by the official website Golden Eagle Network were opened for carpet-style search of contestants.

The "Happy Alliance" is considered a countermeasure to deal with the regulations set by the State Administration of Radio and Television regarding talent shows. The "Happy Girl" regional competitions will not be broadcast live via satellite but will cooperate with local stations. Each local station will launch publicity campaigns for their respective regions or registration points effectively solving the problem of the "Happy Girl" preliminary rounds not being able to air on satellite channels, compensating for the lack of national impact. Under tight time constraints, "broad sowing and wide harvesting" is also a good choice.

In fact, from the current effects, the "Happy Alliance" has made significant contributions to the "Happy Girl" competition. From the start of registration, local TV stations specifically created a program to promote the "Happy Girl". Afterward, the 18 cooperative TV stations and six online media outlets all provided full support, effectively spreading the "Happy Girl" competition. After the top 300 gathered in Changsha, under the "restriction on satellite broadcasting", the 18 ground media and six online media collectively exerted effort, not only broadcasting the situation of the eight "breakthrough matches" over the widest possible range, but each media outlet also sent personnel to Changsha for interviews, triggering a media craze, reflecting a powerful dissemination effect. And even before going on satellite, "Happy Girl Voice" had already firmly captured the attention of a large number of viewers.
