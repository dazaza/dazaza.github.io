What is the best method for removing spots? Unsightly spots are always far from a beautiful face. What is the best way to remove spots? Laser spot removal restores your elegant appearance. Below, please listen to the introduction by experts from Wuhan Boai Hospital, hoping it will be helpful to everyone.

Laser spot removal can deliver light beams of specific wavelengths through the epidermis without damaging normal skin, precisely reaching the root of the spots. It destroys pigment cells and pigment particles, which are then absorbed by macrophages in the body. Wuhan Boai Aesthetic Hospital uses Q-switched lasers, targeting different types of spots with different laser wavelengths. This method provides good spot-removal results, is safe, and leaves no scars. The spot removal process is fast, and there is no special post-treatment care required.

The principle of intense pulsed light (IPL) spot removal is that it acts on superficial skin pigments and smaller pigment molecules, gently decomposing and dissolving them, reducing superficial skin spots. Then, it targets deeper pigments and larger pigment molecules, causing the pigments and pigment cells within the spots to heat up, coagulate, and wither before being metabolized and expelled from the body. The Royal IPL Skin Rejuvenation at Wuhan Boai Medical Aesthetics Hospital uses strong pulse light sources and unique broad-spectrum treatment light to penetrate the skin, making the spot removal effect more thorough, cleaner, and more comprehensive. It is especially suitable for Asians and other pigmented skin types.

Both IPL and laser spot removal are relatively good methods for removing spots. However, due to individual differences in constitution and skin reactions to lasers or IPL, the best method varies from person to person. It is recommended to visit a plastic surgery hospital for a personal consultation to develop a detailed spot removal plan.
