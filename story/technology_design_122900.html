Wealth, power, Gol D Roger, the Pirate King who once had it all, left behind a single sentence before his execution: "Want my wealth? Then come and find it! Everything I have is there, on the Grand Line!" Thus, more and more people rushed to the sea, sailing into the Grand Line, and the world welcomed the Great Pirate Era!

Many years later, a child swore to a one-armed pirate: "I will definitely become the Pirate King!" And so, 10 years later, a great legend began.

This is a story about a group of children who love to dream. They have passion, perseverance, and companions...

Main Characters

MONKEY D LUFFY

The protagonist of this animated series. 17 years old, 172 cm tall, known as "Straw Hat Luffy." As a child, he ate the Gum-Gum Devil Fruit and became a rubber man, with the ability to freely stretch his entire body. After ten years of training, he has mastered the abilities of the Gum-Gum Devil Fruit. His motto is: "I want to become the Pirate King."

His signature, the straw hat on his head, was given to him by the pirate Shanks as a token for their next meeting!

Personality: Very straightforward, does whatever comes to mind without considering the consequences (occasionally becomes smart).

Also, don't forget his terrifying appetite. For him, no matter what happens, eating is the most important thing, and the more the better. As long as it's food (especially meat), he can eat it, and at an incredibly fast speed.

RORONOA ZORO

Luffy's first partner. 19 years old, 178 cm tall.

Known as the Pirate Hunter in the East Blue.

As a child, he made a promise with his then-friend Kuina to become the world's greatest swordsman.

His mark is the light green cloth wrapped around his waist and the dark green cloth on his left shoulder. During battle, he often ties the one on his left shoulder around his head. He has no sense of direction, practices hard frequently, has great wrist strength, loves to sleep, and is also the only swordsman in the world who can use three swords simultaneously, calling himself a "Three-Sword Style" user.

NAMI

Luffy's second partner, 18 years old, 169 cm tall. Measurements: B86, W57, H86 (estimated by Sanji).

Previously a professional pirate thief, she is the navigator of the Luffy Pirates. She knows everything about meteorology, has excellent speaking skills, and is a negotiation expert. Her dream is to draw a complete world map on her own.

After her foster parent Bellemere was killed, she spent 8 years drawing navigation charts for the Dragon Pirates. She made an exchange deal with the fish-man Dragon: she would earn 100 million beris to buy back the village where she grew up —— Cocomes.

Her personality is very open, she drinks a lot, pays attention to her appearance, and often changes clothes. In battle, although she is skilled in staff techniques, if she gets angry, even Luffy can't beat her. She likes money and oranges, and has an orange and windmill carved on her left arm. But as long as she's happy, that's all that matters to her. She hides a combination three-section staff in her full thighs, which was modified by Usopp during the voyage into a Weather Staff.

USOPP

Luffy's third partner, 17 years old, 174 cm tall.

The gunner of the Luffy Pirates, the son of the legendary pirate Yasop. A young man who wants to become a brave warrior of the sea. Lying is his daily routine, he lies to make Miss Kaya and his mother happy.

His mark is his very long nose, and he wears goggles during battle. Additionally, he is multi-talented, able to draw, repair things, invent weapons, etc. By nature, he is timid, but his mind is quick, and he often comes up with good ideas. His weapon is a slingshot, and his offensive tactics involve deception and trickery.

SANJI

Luffy's fourth partner, 19 years old, 177 cm tall, born in the NORTH SEA (North Sea).

The chef of the Luffy Pirates, originally the deputy chef of a floating restaurant. His dream is to see the mysterious sea ALL BLUE (All Blue, referring to the sea that has fish from all four seas), he is lecherous and likes all kinds of women, and is very strict about the quality of food.

His mark is the cigarette in his mouth, his suit, and his unique eyebrows.

To repay Chef Herutsume for raising him, he became the successor to Chef's invincible kick technique.

TONY TONY CHOPPER

Height varies, 15 years old, originally the beloved reindeer of Dr. Medico, who passed down his unparalleled medical skills entirely to him. Later, he became the ship doctor and translator (Animal Language) of the Luffy Pirates.

Since birth, he has had a blue nose different from other reindeers, with a strong sense of smell. Because he ate the Human-Human Devil Fruit, he gained human abilities (speech, standing, intellect, etc.). When he eats the Rumble Ball he developed himself, he gains seven transformation abilities, but the effects only last three minutes. Although he doesn't hide his feelings when speaking or acting, his heart has actually been deeply hurt.

NICO ROBIN

Former vice-commander of Baroque Works, 28 years old, actually an archaeologist. At just 8 years old, she sank 8 Navy warships on her own, and because of this incident, the World Government labeled her as a dangerous person and placed a bounty of 79 million beris on her. However, for 20 years, she disappeared without a trace until she reappeared with Baroque Works.

She is a fruit ability user who ate the Flower-Flower Fruit. Any enemy she can see cannot escape her.
