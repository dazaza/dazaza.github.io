12 million is too wasteful, 5 million pixel cost-effective camera recommendation

by dj11441l on 2009-12-07 02:52:38

Since mobile phones have been equipped with cameras, so far almost 95% of mobile phones have a photography function. As a standard feature of mobile phones, the camera provides great convenience for casual shooting and recording the bits and pieces of daily life, and has become a basic factor for users to consider when purchasing a mobile phone. In recent years, the pixel count of mobile phone cameras has greatly improved, and several models with 12 million pixels have already been launched. However, for ordinary users, such high pixel counts are not really necessary; a 5 million pixel model is already very good. Below, the author recommends some cost-effective 5 million pixel camera phones on the market for friends who like casual shooting.

Nokia 6600is (Picture Forum) (The smallest 5 million pixel metal mobile phone)

Reference price: 1700 yuan (revised version)

The Nokia 6600is is a small and cute metal phone, and is also one of the smallest 5 million pixel camera phones, making it suitable for those who like the metallic feel. Similar to its predecessor, the 6600s, it uses Nokia's S40 sixth edition system, and is a mid-range positioned phone.

The Nokia 6600is features a fashionable slider design, with a metal surface that has undergone a brushed treatment, giving it a very high-end appearance. Its dimensions are 93x45x14mm, and it weighs only 110g, making it small and delicate with a fine texture when held in hand. The 6600is is equipped with a 2.2-inch QVGA display screen. Due to its small size, the image display is more refined, with better handling of details.

The Nokia 6600is, like the 6600s, uses Nokia's commonly used non-smart S40 system, which is simple and mature, allowing quick and easy use. The 6600is supports playback of various video and audio formats, offering strong entertainment functions, making it suitable for young and fashionable people. It comes with a built-in 5 million pixel lens, supporting LED flash and auto-focus.

Editor's review: Metal phones still receive consumer favor. The simple slider design, combined with the user-friendly S40 system, positions the phone as mid-range. However, it is fully made with high-quality materials, featuring a wide screen, a textured shell, and an affordable price. These factors will likely lead to its rapid popularization.
