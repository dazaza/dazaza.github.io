Mother really aged.

by sqrfeifeic on 2011-09-19 19:56:31

Seeing that I was not annoyed, my mother immediately became happy. She excitedly told me: the pomegranates in the backyard have blossomed, and the watermelons will be ripe soon. Come back home. I awkwardly said: I am so busy, how can I ask for leave! She hastily said: Just say mom got cancer, and she only has half a year to live! I immediately blamed her for making it up, and she chuckled. When I was little, every time it was windy or rainy, if I didn't want to go to school, I would pretend to have a stomachache, and was found out by mother, then got scolded severely. Now that she is old, she instead teaches her daughter to lie, I felt both angry and amused. Such questions and answers kept repeating, I finally couldn't bear it, and told her I would definitely come back next month. My mother was so pleased that she choked up.

My mother really got old, becoming as clingy as a child. Every time she called, she always asked enthusiastically: when are you coming home? Not to mention that we are more than a thousand miles away, need to transfer three times, just work and children already make me unable to take care of everything, where do I have time to go home. My mother's hearing is not good, I explained for a long time, she still eagerly asked: when can you come back? After several times, I finally lost my patience, and shouted loudly on the phone. She finally heard clearly, and silently hung up the phone. A few days later, my mother asked the same question again, but this time her tone was timid, without confidence. Like a reluctant child, knowing that asking is useless, but just can't help it. My heart softened, and I hesitated.

On Saturday, the temperature was exceptionally high, I didn't dare to go out, and stayed at home with the air conditioner on. The children complained that there was no more ice cream, so I had to go downstairs to buy some. On the street steamed by the heat, I suddenly saw my mother's figure. It seemed that she had just got off the car, with a basket on her arm and a heavy bag on her back. She bent over, dodging left and right, afraid that others would bump into her things. In the crowded crowd, my mother took every step with great difficulty. I called her loudly, she hurriedly lifted her sweaty face, looking around, and when she saw me coming, she was so surprised that she couldn't speak. As soon as we got home, my mother happily took out those things. Her hands were full of veins, all ten fingers were wrapped with tape, and there were scabbed wounds on her hands. My mother, who had never been far away, just because of my one word, traveled a long distance to come here. She took the cheapest bus without air conditioning, it was hot and crowded inside, but those juicy grapes and pears were all intact. I can't imagine how she managed to get here, I only know that in this world, wherever there is a mother, there is a wonder. My mother only stayed for three days, she said I was too busy, getting up early and working late, taking care of the children, she could only worry helplessly and couldn't help.

No matter what I said, she faithfully half-opened her mouth, tilted her ears and listened carefully, even during her nap, she sat on the edge of the bed, smiling at me. I said: since you love me so much, why don't you live with me? She said she wasn't used to city life. After a few days, I was eager to go back, my mother begged me to stay one more day. She said that she had already asked someone to go to the city to buy vegetables this morning, they would be back soon, she must cook a good meal for me. The county town is more than ninety miles away from here, my mother wanted to bring all the things she thought were delicious back, let me eat them, then she would feel at ease.

Only now did I realize that a person needs their mother even when they are eighty years old. The car finally arrived at the village entrance, my mother ran over, with a full face of smiles. I hugged her, wanting to cry and laugh at the same time, and reproached her: you could have said anything, why say you were sick, how could you think of such an excuse!

The criticized mother was still infinitely happy, she just wanted to see me.

Auntie told me, when she called me, mother was already gone, she passed away peacefully. Six months ago, my mother was diagnosed with cancer, but she didn't tell anyone, still cheerfully busy until she closed her eyes. And she had arranged all her posthumous affairs properly. Auntie also told me, my mother had suffered from eye disease for a long time, it was very difficult for her to see things. I tightly held the bag of oil cake to my chest, feeling like my heart was being dug out. Originally, my mother knew that her remaining days were limited, so she kept calling me to come home, she wanted to see me more often, talk to me more.

In the kitchen facilities, she didn't dare to touch anything, for fear of breaking it. She quietly booked the ticket herself, and quietly went alone. Just a week after returning home, my mother said she missed me again, and kept urging me to come home. I laughed bitterly: Mom, please be patient! The next day, I received Auntie's call: your mother is sick, come back quickly. I was so anxious that my vision turned dark, and I tearfully rushed to the station, catching the last bus. All the way, I prayed silently in my heart.

I cooked for my mother, chatted with her, my mother looked at me for a long time, with infinite pity in her eyes.

This time, my mother seemed satisfied, she didn't urge me to come home again, but kept telling me happy things: a very obedient calf was added to the family; next spring, she planned to plant a lot of flowers in the yard. Listening to her, I felt a warmth in my heart. By the end of the year, I received another call from Auntie. She said: your mother is sick, come back quickly. How could I believe it, we just talked the other day, and she said she was fine, telling me not to worry. Auntie kept urging me, so I returned with a bag of oil cakes that my mother loved. When the car reached the village entrance, I stretched my neck to look around, and my mother didn't come to meet me, my heart trembled with an ominous foreboding.

Originally, the food that I criticized and refused to eat was made by her with blurred vision, how careless I was! That night I left, how did she explore her way home alone, did she fall, I would never know. Mother, in your final moments, you happily told me that morning glories covered the old chimney, and the hyacinth beans bloomed like the purple clothes I wore as a child. You left behind all your love, all the warmth, and then departed peacefully.

When I came back from Auntie's house, the dishes carefully prepared by my mother were finally served on the table, I couldn't help but be amazed: the fish scales were not cleaned thoroughly, there were fine chicken feathers on the chicken pieces, and there were hair strands in the fragrant mushroom soup. Whether meat or vegetable, none of them could be eaten. My mother used to be so clean when she was young, now that she was old, she became so dirty. Seeing me picking and choosing without eating, she lovingly gave in, sending me to the night bus. It was very dark, my mother held my arm. She said, you are not used to the country road. She accompanied me onto the bus, and kept giving instructions. The bus started, she hurriedly got down, her clothes corner was caught by the door, almost falling down. I sobbed, shouting at the bus window: Mom, Mom, be careful! She didn't hear clearly, chasing the bus while shouting: child, I am not angry with you, I know you are busy!

But somehow, there was always something urgent to do, every task was more important than going home, in the end, I didn't return. The mother on the other end of the phone seemed to have no strength to say another word, I was filled with guilt: Mom, are you mad? This time, my mother heard clearly, she immediately said: child, I am not angry with you, I know you are busy. But in a few days, my mother's calls became more frequent. She said, the grapes were ripe, the pears were ripe, come back and eat them. I said, what's special, they are everywhere here, spending ten or eight yuan can make you eat enough. My mother was unhappy, I patiently coaxed her: but, these things are fed with fertilizer and pesticide, how can they compare with what you grow. My mother proudly laughed.

My mother bustled about happily, setting a table full of delicious food, waiting for my praise. I mercilessly criticized: the red bean porridge was burnt; the pan-fried buns' skin was too thick; the braised pork was too salty. My mother's smile immediately became embarrassed, and she helplessly scratched her head. I secretly laughed in my heart, I knew that once I said something was delicious, my mother would force me to eat a lot, and take some with me when I left. This way, I was fed fat and white, and couldn't lose weight no matter what. Moreover, if I didn't criticize her, how could I have the chance to take over the stove?

I hope my mother was lying to me, I hope she is well. I am willing to listen to her nagging, willing to eat all the food she makes for me, willingly find time to visit her.

I know, you are the only person in this world who won't be angry with me, the only one who will wait for me forever, it's precisely because of this indulgence that I dared to let you wait so long. But, mother, am I really that busy?
