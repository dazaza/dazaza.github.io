According to recent news from China Shandong Network, the editor learned from Shandong Guangyuan 4S Store that the third generation of Fuli can enjoy a 3000 yuan government subsidy, with an additional gift of 2500 yuan car insurance. Interested friends can visit the store for more information.

Third Generation Fuli 7.68--9.88 7.68--9.88 3000 yuan government subsidy + 2500 yuan car insurance

The breakthrough in appearance makes the new third generation Fuli very eye-catching. Compared with previous Fuli products, the design of the new third generation Fuli has a strong sense of fashion. Whether it is the strong lines on the engine cover or the integration of the straight waterfall front grille with the hawk logo, one can strongly feel that the shape of the new third generation Fuli is gradually becoming younger, overturning the previously stable and mature image of Fuli. It is believed that this will also win the hearts of more young consumers.

As a classic brand that has been on the market for 8 years, how to break through its original image and gain more support from young consumers is not only a topic for the new third generation Fuli, but also a major challenge for all classic brands. It is not difficult to see from these changes that the third generation Fuli is trying hard to get closer to young consumers, and its market performance after its launch is worth looking forward to.
