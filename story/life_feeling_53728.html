by tj970 on 2008-03-15 02:38:08

At the invitation of her boyfriend, she went to his house for an overnight stay. Unexpectedly, her boyfriend's husband sneaked into her room in the middle of the night... She was filled with shame and anger but didn't dare to tell anyone. A month later, she discovered that she was pregnant. In a state of utter despair, she sought help from our newspaper's emotional hotline under the pseudonym "Chen Meng." The confessor: Chen Meng, 27 years old, from Zizhong, currently working at a tourism department in Chengdu.

Date: Lantern Festival Night

Location: Baiguolin Neighborhood

Compiled by: Reporter Li Yaling

Working — Coming to Chengdu Alone and Meeting an Older Sister

I am from Zizhong and graduated from junior high school. In 2001, I started working at an insurance company where I met my colleague, an older sister who took very good care of me. She was in her early thirties, smart, capable, and had a happy family. Her husband, Ou Ge, is a postgraduate and now serves as the head of the East China region for a pharmaceutical company. They have a ten-year-old son. The older sister often invited me to her house to play. Ou Ge appears well-mannered and treats people warmly. Unable to find a place to rent, he introduced me to a male colleague with whom I shared a two-bedroom apartment. Over time, I became like family with the older sister and them; whenever I went to her house, I would wash the dishes while she cooked. In 2004, they bought a large house of over 160 square meters with four bedrooms and two living rooms. Several times, Ou Ge invited me to live with them in front of the older sister, but I felt it would be too much trouble for them, so I declined.

One day in October last year, I needed to write a resume, so I sought the older sister's help. She let Ou Ge teach me how to write it and left to play mahjong. After teaching me for a while, Ou Ge suggested we go check out their newly decorated house. As soon as we entered, he suddenly hugged me and started touching me inappropriately. I was shocked. I pushed him away forcefully and said, "Ou Ge, I am your wife's friend. Even rabbits don’t eat the grass near their burrows, how could you do this?" He didn't force me further. After going home, I hesitated about whether I should tell the older sister about this incident. She looked so happy, and I couldn't bear to ruin it. I told the guy I rented with about this matter, and only then did he reveal that Ou Ge has always been inappropriate in that aspect and frequently goes to nightclubs to watch erotic dances.

Staying Overnight — Ou Ge Breaks Into My Room At Night

The older sister had no idea that Ou Ge had developed improper intentions towards me and still frequently invited me to her house. I didn't want to go, but I was also afraid she might suspect something. After years of interaction, I had developed a sense of attachment to her family. So I continued to visit, just not staying overnight anymore. Ou Ge acted as if nothing had happened and treated both the older sister and me the same as before. On January 20, 2005, the older sister once again invited me to her house. In the afternoon, taking advantage of Ou Ge’s absence, the older sister and I chatted casually. It was then that she vaguely mentioned that her relationship with Ou Ge was "not very good." The older sister had poor health, and Ou Ge was a man with strong desires that she couldn't satisfy. Their affection was all pretense. That day, we chatted until late, missing the bus, so the older sister asked me to stay. Thinking that the older sister was there, I agreed. I slept in the guest room next to the master bedroom and carefully locked the door from the inside.

Unexpectedly, in the middle of the night, the door was somehow opened, and Ou Ge slipped in. I originally wanted to call for the older sister, but at that moment, a sudden question crossed my mind: If the older sister and Ou Ge were sleeping together, how could Ou Ge be so bold? Could it be that they had planned it together? In the end, I didn't dare to shout and kept resisting. However, he was tall and strong, and my struggles were futile...
