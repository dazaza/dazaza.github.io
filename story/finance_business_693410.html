With the rapid development of the internet, more and more companies are beginning to focus on online marketing. However, due to a lack of professional talent and unclear understanding of online marketing, many companies invest money in online marketing but fail to achieve ideal results. Later, they only complain that online promotion doesn't work. Where do these companies go wrong? In fact, it's because they haven't taken the first step in online marketing - marketing positioning.

Currently, China already has 200 million internet users, and this number is growing rapidly. If the first step of positioning isn't done well in online marketing, it will inevitably waste huge amounts of money and fail to achieve ideal results.

There are many such companies that have no positioning for their own marketing. They just require that their website's traffic be as high as possible. For example, there was once a company that sold computers, and they insisted I help them with movie keyword search engine promotions. They said that while people watch movies, they might casually look at their computers. It seemed like a funny issue. Promoting this way might randomly get some effect, but what are the chances? Another time, a friend told me that his website had just been made, and he needed me to quickly generate some traffic so that the counter would run faster. I asked him why he was so eager for traffic. He said that with traffic, he could make money quickly. I replied, can you really make money just by having traffic? How many websites have closed down because they had traffic but no profits? This friend could only laugh awkwardly.

In general people's understanding, online marketing is about getting popularity and traffic for one's website. But they always overlook a fundamental question: does your website want traffic or profit?

The positioning in online marketing mainly involves positioning in search engine promotion and email marketing, as well as media positioning when placing online advertisements.

With the arrival of the online marketing era, the economy of search power has become another economic hotspot after the economy of attention and the economy of eyeballs. One can say that the main method of online marketing is search engine marketing. Below, we will first discuss the marketing positioning in search engine promotion. Search engine promotion is actually target customer promotion based on keyword searches. It is currently the most effective and targeted form of online marketing. The key to marketing positioning in search engine promotion is finding the keywords that target customers are most likely to use. This way, customers can find you, making your promotion most effective. There was once a lighting company that asked us to help with search engine promotion. The set keywords were as follows: the best lighting manufacturer, the top lighting production enterprise, the best lighting provider. Imagine, if such keywords were used for search engine promotion, the effect would be predictable. In keyword selection, the following points should be noted:

1. Avoid blindly pursuing popular keywords; use the Email search client method.

2. Avoid choosing keywords with too many modifiers; the more modifiers, the less likely they are to be used.

3. Conduct market research to find the keywords that customers are most likely to use. In this aspect, it is suggested that search engines provide relevant data, but this is all confidential information of the search engines, so the hope is slim. We still need to make our own efforts. Baidu's more search results include some usage rate rankings, but they are not very accurate and can only be used as a reference.
