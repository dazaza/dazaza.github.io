7th: American KAS Carpet

The famous KAS carpets in the United States, headquartered in New Jersey, USA, is a well-known hand-made and machine-woven carpet importer and also one of the main suppliers in the industry. 8th: German INFLOOR Carpet

Modern interior design factors are increasingly restricted by the size of the indoor space. How to decorate the limited space properly without making it crowded? The pattern design and color matching of carpets can greatly change the spatial visual effect indoors. Germany's infloor carpets have always focused on carpet design, making the full-lay carpet patterns fashionable. Infloor was created by Ulrich Dresing in 1977, initially as a small family business with only three employees and one tufted carpet production machine. After years of effort, infloor has now become well-known in the European market for its experience and knowledge in carpets and sells 300 million square meters of full-lay carpets worldwide each year. (Official website: www.infloor.de)

Ryalux is the leader of the UK carpet production reform. Ryalux carpets are located in England and were created by William Lomas in 1970, mainly producing high-quality full-lay carpets.
