So, how should people correctly choose high-quality car floor mats? · Measure the "safety factor" of car floor mats. More and more cases of car floor mats causing trouble have made people pay attention to the safety factor of these unassuming car floor mats. Industry experts point out that car floor mats mainly affect normal driving safety in several aspects. When choosing, the quality of car floor mats should be evaluated from these aspects. One, whether the car floor mat is easy to slide. If the size of the car floor mat is too large or too small, it will cause the car floor mat to shift during braking. Once your pedal is stuck or hooked by the car floor mat, it may cause a traffic accident. It is not difficult to imagine that in a high-speed moving vehicle, if an emergency event occurs, the driver can avoid an accident by braking. But at this critical moment, if the car floor mat slides and shifts, resulting in the driver failing to brake in time, the probability of an accident occurring will undoubtedly be very high. Generally, the reasons for car floor mat sliding are twofold: one is that the size of the car floor mat is unsuitable, so it easily shifts position; another reason is that the bottom of ordinary car floor mats is generally flat, which can easily cause sliding when stepped on. Some car floor mats add "anti-slip nails" at the bottom, but due to process reasons, the so-called "anti-slip nails" are hard objects that do not possess good grip, so they may also cause sliding during use. Therefore, when selecting foot pads, it is recommended to choose anti-slip car floor mats with special designed soft anti-slip nails (the nails have good grip) that will not slip even when forcefully stepped on. If the car floor mat has buckle anti-slip, it undoubtedly has dual anti-slip effects. You can observe the adhesive on the back of the car floor mat. Ordinary car floor mat products mostly have foam adhesive at the bottom, which is prone to tearing, cracking, and slipping. In such cases, besides easily damaging the product, it also poses a great safety hazard while driving. On the other hand, the adhesive on the back of high-quality car floor mats is made of specially designed environmentally friendly rubber to form anti-slip soft nails, making them safer and more durable. Two, whether the car floor mat contains toxic substances. Among various types of car floor mats, some contain cheap materials and have added chemicals like chlorinated paraffin. Therefore, as soon as you get the product, it will immediately emit a pungent rubber odor, especially in high-temperature environments, where the smell is particularly strong. Long-term inhalation of these harmful odors can have significant impacts on the human body. By choosing high-quality car floor mats, you can safely drive yourself.
