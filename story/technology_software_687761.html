Winter: Card games combine chess, comics, and dungeons, divided into four types of gameplay: card collection, synthesis, special equipment exchange, card dungeon, and mysterious card quests. Meanwhile, all the card comics in the game are created by the famous "World of Warcraft" comic artist Yiyue Mao, making even just collecting them very interesting. The core of this gameplay lies in the exchange of special equipment, card upgrading mechanics, and event pairings. It's a very enjoyable gameplay system that players must try. [About Donghai Qitan] Winter: I think the biggest feature of Donghai is the comics and the fact that there are many original gameplay systems, relatively new ones. We were the first to attempt using a large amount of comics in the game, whether it's plot guidance, beginner instructions, or worldview descriptions, or even the core card game system. I always had the belief that a game must be fun to be considered a quality product. Therefore, our other core concept was to create a fun game, so we innovated a lot in terms of player experiences, creating four original gameplay systems: card games, divine edges, encounters, and spirit battles.
