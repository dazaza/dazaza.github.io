Sina Entertainment News - The legendary drama "Iron Pear Blossom", adapted from the namesake novel, will be rebroadcast on January 19, 2011, on four satellite channels: Shanxi, Henan, Jilin, and Guangdong. "Iron Pear Blossom" was produced by Dalian Tiange Media, jointly created by renowned screenwriter Yan Geling and director Guo Jingyu, starring Chen Shu and Wei Zi, with excellent actors Shi Ke, Zhang Shaohua, Yang Zhigang, and others passionately performing. It tells the story of Tie Lihua, the daughter of a tomb raider born at the border of Shanxi and Shaanxi, who, during the turbulent years spanning from the warlord strife to the Anti-Japanese War, weaves a legendary tale together with her family, lovers, friends, and even enemies.

Boyfriend's soup conveys affection to Chen Shu; eager to get married and refuses to hide marriage

The popularity of "Iron Pear Blossom" quickly elevated Chen Shu to the ranks of top-tier actresses in China, with renowned screenwriter Yan Geling promoting Chen Shu like Zhao Wei, leaving Chen Shu extremely grateful. Although the emotional fate of Tie Lihua, portrayed by Chen Shu in "Iron Pear Blossom," is tumultuous as she navigates between her first love Liu Tienci, her husband Zhao Yuangeng, her benefactor Liang Feihu, and Zhang Ji'an throughout her life, in real life, Chen Shu is very devoted in her feelings. She fell in love at first sight with her boyfriend Zhao Yinjin, and just 15 days after meeting, her boyfriend once proposed to her. However, Chen Shu belongs to the conservative type of women and did not accept it at the time. Instead, "Iron Pear Blossom" became the matchmaker for the two. Chen Shu once expressed to the media that if the ratings of "Iron Pear Blossom" were good, she would agree to marry Zhao Yinjin, and as the ratings of "Iron Pear Blossom" soared, it smoothly made Chen Shu's promise a reality. Chen Shu frankly stated: Of course, I hope to get married as soon as possible, but because of my busy work schedule, I haven't specifically considered it yet, but I absolutely will not hide my marriage. I will share my happiness with everyone. As for whether Chen Shu will wear a cheongsam at her wedding to reflect her status as the "Cheongsam Queen," she clearly stated that she would not, because reality and roles need to be separated. That beautiful title was given to her by the character, not because she herself is that beautiful.
