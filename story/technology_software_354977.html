Several methods of injectable wrinkle removal & nbsp; Injection for wrinkle removal (biological wrinkle removal) has the advantages of being highly effective, safe, fast, and minimally invasive. Therefore, many people hope to choose this method of wrinkle removal. The injectable methods for wrinkle removal include botulinum toxin injection, Evolence injection, and autologous cell injection for wrinkle removal, among others.

(1) Botulinum Toxin Wrinkle Removal: Botulinum toxin injection for wrinkle removal is minimally invasive, shows results quickly, is easy to operate, and does not affect work. Compared with traditional methods such as chemical peeling, facelifts, and fat grafting, it only requires injecting a certain dose of botulinum toxin into the forehead or between the eyebrows. The entire process takes just a few minutes. Botulinum toxin wrinkle removal is suitable for eliminating crow's feet, forehead lines, frown lines, nasolabial folds, and neck wrinkles, among others.

(2) Evolence Wrinkle Removal: Evolence is the latest non-surgical wrinkle removal product in the world and is the latest third-generation injectable wrinkle removal technology derivative. Once injected, its gentle positive electrons attract negatively charged extracellular matrix cells. One of the most important molecules that combine is hyaluronic acid, and other formed substances include glucosamine bases and equally important collagen precursors. About two months after injection, the charge becomes balanced between positive and negative. Hyaluronic acid and other glucosamine bases are attracted into the interior of Evolence, forming large molecular groups that are soft and sponge-like. This structure improves the texture of the skin and gives the skin a brand-new natural feel. Evolence can be used to fill related deep wrinkles, creases, forehead lines, crow's feet at the corners of the eyes, nasolabial folds, cheek stripes, lip folds, frown lines, etc., without any allergic reactions to body cells, making it very safe.

(3) Autologous Cell Wrinkle Removal: Autologous cell wrinkle removal uses the patient's own skin tissue. Through tissue engineering technology, skin stem cells are separated from it and then placed in a specialized sterile culture solution for stem cells, where they differentiate into proliferative fibroblast precursor cells, also known as youth cells, which are then reinjected into the dermis of facial wrinkles and depressed scars. After entering the mother body, these physiologically active skin youth cells grow again into fibroblasts, which can continuously secrete autologous collagen protein, increasing the density and thickness of the dermal layer of collagen protein, restoring skin elasticity, and smoothing out wrinkles and scars.

Autologous cell wrinkle removal has achieved very stable results in clinical applications, with no rejection reaction and no significant disadvantages. In terms of wrinkle removal, it effectively improves skin elasticity, luster, and removes wrinkles. It is suitable for treating crow's feet, periorbital lines, nasolabial folds, forehead lines, neck lines, frown lines, stretch marks, and more. & nbsp;
