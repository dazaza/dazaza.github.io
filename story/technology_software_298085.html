by hartyqekb on 2009-12-07 17:43:32

In Saturday's match against Sampdoria, AC Milan sealed the victory within just 24 minutes by tearing through the opponent’s defense. Meanwhile, in the same round of matches, Inter Milan and Juventus were also associated with victories, narrowing the gap between themselves and the leaders to four points. This excellent performance has made AC Milan club president Silvio Berlusconi very proud. With winds of a winning streak blowing for AC Milan, Berlusconi is now happy and hopes that the team can continue this good momentum into the next game and be ready to capitalize on any further mistakes made by Inter Milan for greater achievements. In this round of competition, during the home game against Sampdoria, after the start of the match, Berlusconi unexpectedly appeared at the scene and went to the locker room after the game to congratulate the players publicly. Since the 10th round of the league, after defeating Naples, AC Milan has maintained their winning streak. In response, Berlusconi said: "AC Milan is doing something very exciting; they have played very well and returned to their own recognition. Now AC Milan is very strong. We haven't previously won against Naples, unfortunately, otherwise we would be able to go even further. To become second in the Italian league is a very satisfying achievement, and we must maintain it. If Inter Milan faces setbacks, then we have to seize the opportunity."

During the game, Ronaldinho put on a great performance in the first half but suffered a slight thigh muscle strain and had to be substituted. However, after the game, he personally assured everyone that his condition was not serious, saying: "I can play Tuesday against Zurich." Talking about Ronaldinho's rebirth, Berlusconi said: "I have always been very optimistic about Ronaldinho's ability, and now I have seen his performance prove me wrong. Seedorf and Pato are also exceptional—unparalleled, charming, and extraordinary. I think it is not unreasonable to describe them as such. AC Milan's primary goal is the UEFA Champions League trophy. Coming second in Serie A has already become a very good result, and we need to maintain it. If Inter Milan faces difficulties or setbacks, then we should be prepared to take advantage of this opportunity."

Regarding Gattuso, Berlusconi said: "We will try our best to keep him, after all, he is a banner for AC Milan."
