A low temperature steam formaldehyde sterilization uses low temperature steam to help formaldehyde penetrate and kill microorganisms with a sterilization temperature of 60 ~ 80 ℃. This is one of eleven kinds of low-temperature sterilization methods, particularly suitable for heat-sensitive materials. It has been widely used in many countries and regions: Sweden, Finland, Germany, Britain and France.

1 Materials and Methods

1.1 Materials: Formaldehyde low-temperature sterilizer provided by the Swedish Getinge company, its volume of 564L, can accommodate up to nine dedicated stainless steel mesh baskets, three-layer emission, sterilization items placed in a basket, sterile packaging all dedicated by the Finnish company WIPAK transparent bag packaging; 35% - 40% aqueous solution of formaldehyde using a commercially available.

1.2 Method: The sterilization process is divided into six steps:

(1) Sterilization chamber warm-up: When the sterilizer door is closed, through the jacket of the heating chamber so that the cavity wall achieves the desired temperature, the beginning of the process.

(2) Pre-vacuum: By repeating several times, the injection of steam and vacuum, pulsation vacuum, to achieve the pre-vacuum sterilization chamber body, its purpose is to exclude the air of the sterilization chamber body, packing bags due to the folding within the air gap, air items to be sterilized hollow points, and the treatment of sterilized items to warm up.

(3) Sterilization: Formaldehyde liquid heated by a steam jacket heating turns to mist to be inhaled into formaldehyde vapor, according to the formaldehyde vapor pressure values set to inject a specific amount of formaldehyde vapor to the sterilization chamber, and then adding steam to the internal cavity temperature reaches the preset sterilization temperature. Formaldehyde vapor and water vapor gas mixture in the case of negative pressure to fully penetrate to the inside of the article to be sterilized.

(4) To exclude gas: After sterilization of the default break after the removal of sterilized items deep within the body cavity through the change process of liquefaction and vaporization of the state of 20 to 50 times pulsating steam formaldehyde to make sterilization residues of the items inside reduce to a minimum.

(5) Drying: The pulse of air, outside air into the sterilization chamber body, through the high efficiency filter to remove the moisture on the sterilization items.

(6) Pressure process: The injection of fresh air to the cavity of the sterilizer of units: 518 026 Children’s Hospital of Shenzhen City, Guangdong Province pressure equal. The whole sterilization process run-time break is about 5h.

1.3 Sterilizing effect of monitoring:

(1) Monitoring of the process: In the sterilization process, the computer automatically drawing record the various stages of temperature and pressure;

(2) Chemical monitoring: Formaldehyde-low-temperature steam sterilization provided by the Swedish Getinge chemical indicator card is put in the center of the sterilization package WIPAK transparent bags, set up a chemical indicator, color case to determine whether the sterilization effect observed after sterilization;

(3) Biological monitoring: Each pot sterilization in the autoclave under before the next, the next, the middle five points placed on the experimental bag experiments bag installed a 3M self-food biological indicators, biological indicators agent according to the German industrial standard (D) 58 948 requirements thermophilic fat Bacillus (A1 °C C7953), provided by the 3M Company.
