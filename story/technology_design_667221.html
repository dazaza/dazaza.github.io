Hyaluronic acid wrinkle removal: Beauty tourism on the rise, tourists from other regions favor coming to Thailand for plastic surgery

by li8l9fdfg on 2012-02-16 16:14:45

Thailand is a world-renowned destination for medical tourism. Tourists from many countries travel to Thailand during their leisure time to enjoy its high-quality medical services. It is said that dozens of Thai hospitals with international reputations receive a large number of visitors from all over the world every year.

People from Western countries such as the United States, the United Kingdom, and Australia go to Thailand for plastic surgery, as do people from Asian countries like the UAE and Bangladesh. The wide range of foreigners choosing Thailand for plastic surgery is closely related to the country's high-quality medical standards and relatively low costs. Many beauty enthusiasts who are not yet familiar with domestic plastic surgery technology believe that they should go abroad for plastic surgery. However, this is not necessarily true, as having plastic surgery abroad does not guarantee more security than undergoing procedures domestically. Currently, China's plastic surgery technology is on par with countries like South Korea and Thailand. Individuals considering plastic surgery should think carefully before making any decisions and avoid acting impulsively.

Among Thailand's medical tourism projects, plastic surgery-related services form an important component. When it comes to plastic surgery in Thailand, gender reassignment surgery is perhaps the most famous. In fact, aside from gender reassignment surgery, other plastic surgery procedures such as breast augmentation, liposuction, double eyelid surgery, and rhinoplasty are also specialties of Thai plastic surgeons.

However, while Thailand’s medical standards are relatively high in Asia, there are still issues with inconsistent quality among doctors. Making an uninformed choice when seeking medical care can lead to many troubles. Not long ago, the Chongqing Evening News reported a story about a woman from Chongqing who underwent facial injection beauty treatment in Thailand several years ago. Later, problems arose in the injected areas, causing discoloration and severe itching of the local skin. After diagnosis by a plastic surgeon in Chongqing, it was found that the substance injected into her face was Osmiderm, which had been banned in China since 2006. Once injected into the body, this substance is difficult to completely remove. This shows that going abroad for plastic surgery is not always reliable. Beauty enthusiasts can choose authoritative domestic plastic surgery institutions and experienced plastic surgeons in specific fields for cosmetic procedures. Currently, China's plastic surgery level has reached the forefront of the world.
