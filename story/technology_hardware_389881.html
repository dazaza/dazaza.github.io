Let's Explore Traditional Chinese Medicine (TCM) Beauty Written by Taste of Whitening in Intensive Care

Today, let's switch gears and return to our traditional treasure - TCM conditioning. TCM not only focuses on treating diseases but also excels in beauty conditioning. Throughout history, countless TCM practitioners have devoted their lives to finding ways to enhance the beauty of palace women, leaving behind a wealth of experience that is absolutely worth our attention!

So, let's follow the editor together into the world of TCM beauty~

"TCM Beauty" has deep roots in Chinese culture, refined over thousands of years of beauty practices. These time-tested methods passed down through generations embody boundless Eastern wisdom.

Whether it was the ancient imperial court or modern cities, women have always been thoughtful about how to make themselves more beautiful.

And "TCM Beauty", with its holistic approach to internal and external conditioning, and the concept of achieving health and beauty from within, is increasingly favored by those who value beauty and health.

However, now there are many kinds of TCM beauty products on the market. What kind of method will be effective for you? What kind of product will benefit you? And how can you find the right product for yourself? I believe these are the issues everyone cares most about and finds most perplexing.

For this reason, I visited supermarkets, malls, and beauty salons. Later, I found that most of what is sold in supermarkets and malls aren't TCM products, while some beauty salons do use them. When I asked the merchants, without exception, they all introduced me to their products, telling me how good they were, what results I could expect after using them, and how customers said they were great. But these are ultimately just the merchants' claims, and it's hard for us to find actual users to understand the truth unless we have friends around us who are using them. I'm not saying their products are bad; every product that survives in the market must have a certain customer base. Personally, I still place more emphasis on our traditional TCM beauty products.

Because of work, I often surf online, so I decided to use the internet to search. Taobao is China's largest shopping and trading platform. Normally, I often buy clothes and various international brand cosmetics there. But I haven't yet explored our own TCM beauty-related things online. After searching, I found quite a few items related to Guangzhou VIP Information Technology Co., Ltd. I decided to choose a store with relatively high credibility to inquire.

I clicked into a TCM beauty shop named "Green Forest." Honestly, what initially attracted me to click in was the image of an ancient beauty on their products—both men and women share a love for beauty. After reading their introduction, it turns out they come from a family of TCM practitioners, and all the products in the small shop are specially formulated by the elderly TCM doctor in the family. This piqued my curiosity—I wondered if he could also formulate a secret recipe for me, transforming me overnight into a youthful and invincible young girl. After checking a few more product introductions, I discovered that the old TCM doctor is around 50 years old but looks like he’s only 35 in the photo! Truly impressive, making me both admire and envy him. I thought to myself, if I look like this at 50, that would be pretty good.

With that thought, I consulted the shopkeeper as a customer to see how she would introduce their products to me.

Editor: Shopkeeper, hello, are all your products pure TCM?

Shopkeeper: Hello, yes, they are all pure TCM. Is there anything specific you need?

Editor: I want to learn more first.

Shopkeeper: Sure, no problem. Which aspect would you like to know more about?

Editor: Your products are said to be formulated by an old TCM doctor. How long does it take to see results?

Shopkeeper: Yes, they are formulated by the old TCM doctor. TCM emphasizes internal and external conditioning. It differs from general beauty products containing chemicals or hormones which may show immediate effects but cannot address the root cause. Our products focus on fundamental solutions. One course lasts for a month, and the speed of effectiveness varies from person to person. Some people may see results after one course, while others may need two courses or more. Generally, the longer you use them, the better the results.

Editor: Are there any customers who say they didn’t see any effect?

Shopkeeper: Yes, some customers are impatient and stop using the products after a few tries, then claim they don’t work. Our products are not miraculous cures, so it’s impossible to achieve satisfactory results after just a few uses. For example, some customers have very yellowish complexions despite being young and free from other illnesses. These customers generally suffer from qi and blood deficiency, so we adopt a dual supplementation of qi and blood along with internal and external conditioning. Such customers usually require at least two months of conditioning. Some customers give up halfway, thus claiming the product is ineffective. However, those who persist will definitely see results.

Editor: It seems that beautifying through TCM methods requires patience.

Shopkeeper: Yes, because each person's condition is different, the required conditioning time varies. As long as customers can persist, their skin will significantly improve compared to before.

Editor: Alright, thank you, shopkeeper. I've gained some understanding. If I need anything next time, I'll come back to purchase. Wishing you booming business!

Shopkeeper: Sure, no need to be polite, feel free to visit anytime.

An editor believes that the quality of a product depends on its reputation. The shop has a decent credit rating and many repeat customers. The editor decided to investigate the feedback from their customers to verify the authenticity. Among the numerous online buyers, I chose one with a Yellow Diamond credit rating. After explaining my purpose, she was happy to chat with me. Below is a segment of our conversation (for her privacy, she is referred to as A).

Editor: How long have you been using the TCM beauty products from this shop?

A: About 8 months ago, due to prolonged sleep deprivation, my skin was in poor condition. My complexion was dull, my skin was dry, I had fine lines, and even developed spots. Since I have sensitive skin, I dared not use random spot-removal products. I wanted a gentle TCM spot-removal product, and I stumbled upon this shop by chance. So, I started trying it out then. Seeing good results, I continued using it.

Editor: What products did you use?

A: The shopkeeper recommended a Whitening and Spot Removal Jade Complexion Pill and a Full Effect Whitening Spot Removal Mask tailored to my skin type, along with a Softening Hydrating Wrinkle-Removing Mask.

Editor: Did it really work?

A: Yes, after one month, my complexion became much more even, my face wasn't as dry anymore, the spots faded slightly, and my skin became a bit whiter. Although it wasn't extremely white, it was no longer as sallow as before.

Editor: How is your skin now?

A: Now, my skin is truly whiter and smoother. The spots have become very faint and are almost unnoticeable. With foundation applied, they disappear completely. The fine lines have also become much shallower, and my skin looks moisturized and radiant. Even when I wake up in the morning, my skin has a rosy glow. Honestly, I am very satisfied with the current results. Moreover, I have introduced some friends to use it, and they also say the results are good.

Editor: Were there any side effects?

A: No, the shopkeeper inquired about my physical condition before recommending the products, so I didn't notice any abnormalities. However, since TCM works slowly, it takes a longer time.

Editor: Are you still using them now?

A: Yes. I've been taking the Whitening and Spot Removal Jade Complexion Pill for six months, and I've been consistently using the Full Effect Mask and the Softening Hydrating Wrinkle-Removing Mask twice a week each, for eight months now. The shopkeeper suggested that I take the Jade Complexion Pill every two months for another two months, as it can also prevent aging. Women fear aging the most, haha.

Editor: It seems that our historically rich beauty treasure is truly excellent.

A: Yes, you can try it. Talk to the shopkeeper.

Eternal youth is humanity's beautiful wish, but all natural things inevitably go through life and death, prosperity and decline. As part of nature, humans cannot escape this objective rule. Therefore, with age, the human body will also age.

TCM believes that although humans cannot stay forever young, by replenishing the deficiencies of the organs and blood and qi, maintaining balance between yin and yang in the body, it is indeed possible to delay the arrival of aging. In today's world where people value nature and green concepts, TCM with its five thousand years of accumulated strength, still has its unique charm in anti-aging, beauty, and health care.
