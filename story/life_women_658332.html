Key points: In most cases, people with spots at the corner of their eyes have one thing in common: they are prone to feeling depressed and tend to react negatively to external influences. This is because the area around the temple is controlled by what Traditional Chinese Medicine calls the gallbladder meridian, which is a channel for relieving accumulated worries. If there are spots here, it indicates that the gallbladder meridian is blocked. And precisely because they feel low-spirited, easily depressed, and find it hard to relieve stress, over time this leads to spots forming at the corner of the eyes.

Once you know the cause of the spots, it's easier to deal with them. So, if you're someone who cares about beauty, what issues should you pay attention to? What should you do? How can you face life with positivity, live happily, and stay beautiful?

Here are three suggestions specifically for women showing signs of spots to consider. Other causes of spots are related to personal habits, and as long as you take note of them, you can avoid them.

One, Be a Happy Little Witch

In life, don't worry unnecessarily. Stay happy and don't always imagine bad things happening to you. Don't be overly anxious about gains and losses. Be a cheerful little witch. But even so, you will encounter some unhappy events. What to do then?

Give yourself five minutes. You can vent during these five minutes, but after five minutes, you need to calm down and face everything with joy.

Two, Always Eat Breakfast

Don't oversleep. Many girls like to sleep late and skip breakfast, but this isn't a good habit and doesn't help your body relax. You should go to bed by 10 o'clock every night and wake up no later than 8:30 in the morning. Drink a little plain water on an empty stomach, and make sure to eat breakfast. No matter how much or how little, you should eat something for breakfast as it benefits your health.

Three, Develop Good Habits

A large part of the reason for developing spots is due to poor living habits. Developing good habits is essential.

1. Do a good job with sun protection.

2. Avoid heavy makeup, as it can greatly irritate the skin and make it more likely to develop spots.

3. Whiten moderately. Some people excessively pursue beauty, which can backfire.

4. Stay away from computers. Don't sit in front of a computer all day.

5. Love exercising. A healthy body means all parts function normally, resulting in fewer problems. Fewer problems mean spots won't randomly appear on your face.

Actually, having spots at the corner of your eyes isn't scary. Being a happy and loving woman is the most important thing. Even the most beautiful woman without love won't be liked by others.
