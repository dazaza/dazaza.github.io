Acer 8-inch Windows 8.1 Tablet Makes an Appearance

by anonymous on 2013-10-02 17:15:02

Acer's Iconia W3 tablet, launched three months ago, was the first Windows 8 tablet with an 8-inch display. Its successor - the Iconia W4 - has been revealed ahead of its official launch today. Although Acer has not officially announced this tablet yet, Dutch media Bouweenpc got their hands on it early at an Intel media event, and the specifications have also been leaked: Intel Atom Z3740 processor, clocked at 1.33GHz 2GB RAM, 32GB internal storage 5-megapixel rear camera, 2-megapixel front camera 8-inch IPS display, resolution of 1280x800 Runs on Windows 8.1 system, pre-installed with Office 2013 Home & Student Edition Retail price is approximately 330 Euros, equivalent to about 2730 RMB Other features: 802.11n Wi-Fi, Bluetooth 4.0, GPS, Micro-HDMI

When the W3 was released, it came with a wireless external keyboard. It’s uncertain whether the W4 will come with the same accessory. Engadget was impressed by the W3's battery life during their review: one charge could last for nine and a half hours. Hopefully, the excellent battery life of the W3 can be carried over to the W4.
