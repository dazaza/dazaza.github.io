How to find cheap things in Shanghai

I saw it in a magazine, and typed it out myself. My hands are so sore. Everyone must support this. All these things are very practical, let's share them together.

1. New low price for Shanghai fashion - Light Textile Market (No. 1618 Ca'an Road)

In fact, the origin of Shanghai's fashion wholesale is not Qipu Road, but the Light Textile Market. You will find that the price of the same type of clothing may be about 70% cheaper than Qipu Road, and many bosses on Qipu Road go directly to the Light Textile Market for goods.

2. Free online financial accounting book - Caike Online Accounting Website (www.coko365.com) is a free online accounting software. Registered members can use it for free. You can record the money spent every day, and record the monthly income. The powerful online accounting function and rich reporting system can make you clearly know how your money is spent and where it is spent.

3. No cost to go home - RT-Mart shuttle bus

From Yangpu to Baoshan? It sounds far away. Now the transportation cost is quite high. You can pay attention to the RT-Mart shuttle bus. It provides one-stop non-stop service from the starting point to the end point, and it is free. You just need to prepare a supermarket plastic bag, put some things inside, and you can take it.

4. Affordable women's clothing - (Intersection of Huaihai Road and Baoqing Road)

The prices of some small shops on Huaihai Road are quite high. However, in the nameless small shop on Baoqing Road, women's clothing has a variety of styles and excellent quality. You need to slowly search. But the price there is much cheaper than other places.

5. Must visit for saving money - Discount Network (www.zhequ.com)

Do you know where there are discounts in Shanghai? A relatively authoritative website about Shanghai discount information can be recommended.

6. Cheap cars gather - Lancao Bridge (Lancao Bridge Second-hand Car Market)

7. Traffic AA system - Carpooling Network (www.pinchela.com)

Meet every day to take the same taxi to the company, monthly subscription taxi is also very cheap, four people share. Save money and time.

8. Shield currency - Barter Network (www.comhuan.com)

What you don't need, he needs, his useless things are treasures to you. Sort out all the useless things in your house, don't throw them away. Go to the barter network to see who needs them. If everyone advocates win-win, right??

9. Low-price electronic paradise - Qiujiang Road (No. 500-526 Qiujiang Road)

10. Super cheap books - No. 1 Yan'an East Road

This is an ultra-large discount bookstore. The biggest feature is that various types of books are arranged very clearly. In addition, there are three large bookshelves on the left after entering the bookstore. These shelves generally display newer books and complete sets of books, with fast updates. So, every time you go there, you should look at the books on these three bookshelves first.

11. Second-hand trendy stalls - Jiaozhou Road (5th floor LAB laboratory platform, No. 343 Jiaozhou Road)

This market is on the rooftop of a building on Jiaozhou Road. On weekends, there are always a large number of rock and non-mainstream culture enthusiasts here. They set up stalls and place all the things they want to sell on the ground. You can often find clothes, bags, and small items brought by foreign students from their own countries here, and the prices are also reasonable. In addition, buying things here is not just a simple buyer-seller relationship. You can also talk about your favorite music. If you get excited, you can even do two segments of HIP HOP, very relaxed and casual.

12. Super cheap hot pot restaurant - Dai Zhuang (No. 498 Haining Road)

It's Dai Zhuang, not Dai Mei. It's much cheaper than Dai Mei. Vegetables are all 50 cents each, meatballs are 1-2 yuan per skewer, mutton and beef are 5 yuan per bowl. The most expensive seaweed is only 6 yuan per serving. Four people, no more than 80 yuan, and drinks are free.

13. Six yuan unlimited drink - IKEA (No. 126 Caoxi Road)

Spend 6 yuan at IKEA's café and drink as much as you want. Browse the furniture, drink coffee, and spend a pleasant afternoon.

14. Be a millionaire without spending money - Tomorrow Plaza (No. 399 West Nanjing Road)

Experience the luxury tea drinking style for free in the waiting area of the banquet hall on the 5th floor of Tomorrow Plaza. Not only are there various tea bags to choose from, but there are also very luxurious sofas.

15. Millionaire enjoyment also discounted - Yidian Hong Coffee (Inside the Binjiang Garden, Fudou Section of Binjiang Avenue)

Sitting in Starbucks for hours pretending to be a petty bourgeoisie is actually very boring. It's better to drink coffee at Yidian Hong on the Bund Binjiang Avenue. Now there is a promotion for the second cup of coffee at a discount. The level of enjoyment does not shrink, and the price is half of the original.

16. Cheap fresh vegetables - Dinghai Bridge Market (No. 2802 Yangshupu Road)

If you don't eat dinner regularly, you can go to the market before it closes. There are fewer people, and the prices are low and easy to bargain. Most of the vegetables in this market are transported directly from Pudong by ferry by farmers, super fresh, and cheap. It's really different from other markets.

17. Nightclub at a discount - C’S Bar (Basement Level, No. 679 Dingxi Road)

The main body of the entire bar is a basement renovation. The dim lighting gives a rebellious feeling. The maze-like corridor, wooden round table, and high stool make the bar style appear very primitive. The music here is mainly HIP HOP, with more young people and many international students. The bar does not set a minimum consumption, and you can even bring your own drinks inside. The average consumption is 10 yuan.

18. Independent travel headquarters - Lvyou Commune (Lvyou Commune www.69life.com Youzai Travel Network www.uzai.com)

On average, each independent traveler is about 80% cheaper than joining a group tour.

19. Free movie den - Cat Rain (No. 1221 Chang乐e Road)

As one of countless small bars in Shanghai, Cat Rain has found its own way out. Film lovers like you have also found your organization. The movies shown inside are diverse, including famous works by big directors and small-scale independent films. Your only expense may be around 10 yuan for drinks.

20. Free art exhibition - M50 (No. 50 Moganshan Road)

As the currently best-performing art exhibition park in Shanghai, there are not only regular exhibitions held by artists here, but also many artists set up their studios there, available for visits anytime. Friends with similar interests are also welcome to come in and chat. The most important thing is that everything is free, reminiscent of Greenwich Village in those days.

21. Cheap "cheap" body - Fitness Annual Card Transfer Network (http://shanghai.flxz.com/8002.htm)

An annual card costs at least 2,000 to 3,000 yuan, but now you can enjoy the fitness club with even half the price of others. Among the transfer cards, there is an ultimate kind, which is the welfare card issued by many units to employees. Since the cardholder did not pay anything for this card originally, getting this card from him won't cost too much either.

22. Good news for ball players - Free venues (Shanghai Free Basketball Venue Inquiry www.outbbs.com.cn)

For fans of three-on-three basketball and outdoor football, they are much happier. Currently, there are many free outdoor venues open to the public in Shanghai. Some only charge a small management fee. Worth recommending is the three-on-three basketball court opposite Baoshan Station of Line Three. There are many hoops, lighting, and you can play all night. The venue fee is only 5 yuan.

23. Cheap ticket distribution center - Travel Network (http://travelsh.web20.bootchina.com)

Want to buy discounted tickets, remember the following rules - buy tickets more than 15 days in advance, usually you can buy tickets at a discount of less than 80%. Southern Airlines can offer 40% off 45 days in advance, 45% off 30 days in advance, and 50% off 15 days in advance. The regulations of each airline are not exactly the same. Buying connecting flights is cheaper than direct flights. Morning and evening flights are cheaper than daytime flights; when buying round-trip tickets for the same route from one airline, you can enjoy more discounts ranging from half to full discount.
