Taobao Taobao online shopping report: what can it tell us

by e2hjjhg9e on 2010-04-19 16:16:41

By Zhan Peng The Cnnic 2007 Internet Users Investigation Report has just been released, and the number of internet users in China is 210 million. Taobao and iResearch have also published online shopping reports.

One, China has 55 million online shoppers. The report indicates that the quality of Chinese internet users is still generally low.

Two, the online shopping transaction volume on Taobao has broken through the 50 billion yuan mark. The report suggests: it's time to tax!

Three, the average annual online shopping transaction amount per person on Taobao is 1080 yuan. The report suggests: high-end internet users have been lazy for about a month without going to the market, they buy shoes and bags on Taobao, their exercise has decreased, they need to make up for it.

Four, Beijing has the highest per capita online shopping consumption at 1121 yuan, and also the most number of online shoppers. The report suggests: 1. People in Beijing are the laziest, but have the highest level of computer skills. 2. Traffic in Beijing is too congested, forcing many people to learn online shopping.

Five, Taobao sold 2.28 billion yuan worth of mobile phone recharge cards last year, sales close to China Unicom's net profit for half a year last year. The report suggests: 1. Unicom's performance is much worse than Mobile. 2. Some people who spend a lot on phone bills love to shop online, some people love to shop online and thus love to talk on the phone.

Six, Taobao has become China's largest maternal and infant products market, half of the baby formula in big cities comes from Taobao. The report suggests: I'm not quite sure if I believe this, can we trust it?

Seven, clothing transactions on Taobao in 2007 were 5.78 billion yuan, exceeding Beijing's total annual clothing sales, making clothing the champion of online shopping sales. The report suggests: You should go to Taobao to buy new clothes for the Spring Festival this year, no wonder my friends always say they want to use their Taobao store to change my image.

Eight, cosmetics sales in 2007: Taobao exceeds Avon's 6000 stores. The report suggests: Women are still the majority in online shopping.
