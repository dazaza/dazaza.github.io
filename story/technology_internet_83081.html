IE8 Release Coincides with ".China" Domain Name System Launch Date?

by now on 2008-08-08 12:23:28

The ".中国" domain name has recently attracted much attention because it has been incorporated into the global root domain name system. Notably, IE8 was released at the end of 2008, while the ".中国" domain system officially began operation in 2009. These two events can be said to coincide. It is well known that IE8 supports Chinese domain names, and industry insiders have analyzed that this will further promote the activation of the ".中国" domain system.

At a recent Microsoft annual financial analysts meeting, Bill Veghte, vice president of Microsoft, revealed that the official version of IE8 would be released by the end of this year. It is reported that IE8 will continue to support international internet standard protocols such as RFC3743 and RFC4713 (two independently established Chinese domain name-related international internet standards in our country), enabling "seamless" support for Chinese CN domains. Its address bar can correctly add, display, and access Chinese CN domains in the "Favorites," show and correctly access visited Chinese CN domains in the "History," and support hyperlink access to Chinese CN domains on web pages.

It is understood that domestic authoritative institutions have actively advocated and promoted the equivalent handling of simplified and traditional Chinese variants for Chinese domain names, as well as researched the steps and methods for registering Chinese domain names to avoid potential conflicts between different Chinese character variants. They achieved significant breakthroughs with RFC3743 and RFC4713, making them IETF (Internet Engineering Task Force) international technical standards, contributing greatly to the application of "Chinese.中国" domain names.

"The '.中国' domain entering the global root domain system will enjoy almost the same treatment as CN domains," said a responsible person from Now.cn (www.now.cn), a domestic domain registration service provider. IE8 will enable "Chinese.中国" and "Chinese.CN" to achieve no differentiation in access, and the former can better reflect Chinese characteristics, providing convenient webpage browsing methods for Chinese-speaking internet users. For Chinese enterprise users, Chinese domain names are undoubtedly their best online brand.

Observers noted that the direct conversion of "Chinese.CN" to "Chinese.中国" has been basically determined. However, if customers have not yet registered any Chinese domain names, they should register in advance to ensure they do not encounter cybersquatting. It is reported that some domestic registration service providers remind customers to fully protect the ".中国" domain brand when registering "Chinese.CN." For example, registering a simplified "Chinese.CN" domain name now on Now.cn will grant you free traditional and simplified "Chinese.中国" domain names as well as a traditional "Chinese.CN" domain name.

It is understood that ".中国" and ".cn" domains will complement each other and develop together, and ".cn" domains will still be able to be normally registered and used in our country.
