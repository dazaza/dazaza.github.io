Qianqian Jingting is about to say goodbye: officially renamed Baidu Music

by anonymous on 2013-07-29 12:10:28

July 19 news, TTPlayer, one of the oldest music players in China, is now officially leaving the stage. Baidu announced today that its TTPlayer will be renamed to Baidu Music, including all versions for PC and mobile devices, which will gradually be replaced by Baidu Music.

Currently, the domain qianqian.baidu.com has automatically redirected to http://music.baidu.com/pc/. The official slogan is "A decade journey, TTPlayer has accompanied you through, a new beginning, Baidu Music will go with you."

Data shows that TTPlayer once dominated the music player field. In recent years, QQ Music, Kuwo Music, and other players have taken over, online music listening and providing high-quality music have become the trend. TTPlayer's main focus on local playback mode can no longer meet user demands, and after being acquired by Baidu, it did not adjust its product direction in time, thus failing to continue its former glory.

TTPlayer carries the memories of a generation, and its decline makes countless people feel regretful, as one line from a script said: "In memory of those youthful years gone by, TTPlayer, thank you for being there!"

With this renaming, Baidu Music launched version V7.1.0 based on the original version, which is also the starting version of Baidu Music on PC software. The new version of Baidu Music upgrades the existing chart, artist, category, and special sections. Besides synchronizing the smart sound effect enhancement function of the mobile client, it also adds high-quality content such as MV videos and playlist channels.

Baidu Music claims that it already includes millions of legitimate MV resources. Compared to the previous "TTPlayer," Baidu Music has upgraded from a "playback tool" to an "internet music product." Through cooperation with copyright holders like Universal, Warner, Sony, Rock Records, and more than a hundred international and domestic record companies, it offers legal online music listening.

The person in charge of Baidu Music stated that this renaming allows Baidu Music to have a unified identity, integrating product resources and advantageous functions. In the future, Baidu Music will enhance cross-platform, cross-terminal, cloud distribution, real-time migration, and other service capabilities, while also increasing the introduction of legitimate, high-quality digital music resources.
