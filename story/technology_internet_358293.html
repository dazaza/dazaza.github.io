Platysolar Technology Holdings Co., Ltd. signs a cooperation agreement with Hanergy

by ditan360 on 2010-06-17 17:02:38

Exclusive to Low Carbon Network - Brite Solar Technology Holdings Limited ("Brite" or "the Group") announces that the Group has recently signed a sales contract with Hanergy Holding Group ("Hanergy") to sell thin-film solar module production equipment with a total contract value of $2.55 billion (equivalent to approximately HK$19.84 billion). The Group has applied to the Stock Exchange for the resumption of trading in its shares at 9:30 AM on June 17, 2010.

Brite also announces with pleasure the introduction of Hanergy Holding Group as a strategic investor in the Group and one of its major shareholders. On May 20, 2010, the Group entered into a share subscription agreement with Hanergy. Hanergy agreed to subscribe for 4,911,528,960 shares of Brite at a subscription price of HK$0.239 per share.

Mr. Peng Libin, Executive Director of the Group, said: "Hanergy is one of the largest private clean energy enterprises in mainland China today and a leader in the clean energy power generation industry. The acquisition of Hanergy as a strategic investor and one of the Group's major shareholders fully demonstrates Hanergy's recognition of Brite's leading technology and R&D capabilities. This substantial order not only brings a large amount of cash flow to Brite but also significantly increases capital investment in the Group's R&D team and enhances R&D capabilities. It also greatly increases Brite's market share in the production equipment and turnkey production solutions for amorphous silicon thin-film solar modules in China and globally, further consolidating the Group's leading position in the global industry."

Details of the Sales Contract

Brite will provide thin-film solar cell production equipment to Hanergy under the sales contract. Brite will produce and deliver three batches of production lines in three stages. Each batch of production lines will include (among others) 140 plasma-enhanced chemical vapor deposition furnaces (PECVD equipment) and 30 physical vapor deposition furnaces (PVD equipment), which are core devices for new assembly complete equipment, tools, and machinery in solar photovoltaic component production systems. Brite shall complete the delivery of all equipment for each batch within six months after the first prepayment for that batch (equivalent to 50% of the equipment cost for each batch).

The total cost of the equipment is $2.55 billion (equivalent to approximately HK$19.84 billion), determined by reference to the past selling prices of similar equipment with comparable thin-film solar photovoltaic component production capacity. The equipment cost for each batch of production lines is $850 million (equivalent to HK$6.613 billion).

Details of the Subscription Shares

Hanergy will subscribe for a total of 4,911,528,960 subscription shares in three tranches, representing (i) approximately 106.49% of Brite's existing issued share capital, and (ii) 51.57% of Brite's enlarged issued share capital after the issuance of these subscription shares. Hanergy will complete the subscription of the corresponding tranche of subscription shares on the third business day after the payment of the prepayment for each batch of production lines (equivalent to 50% of the equipment cost for each batch). A six-month lock-up period will apply to the first tranche of subscription shares. Hanergy will also appoint three directors to Brite, two of whom will be executive directors.

The total proceeds from the subscription will be approximately HK$1.17 billion. Brite intends to use the net proceeds from the subscription to (i) increase the Group's production capacity to fulfill the sales orders placed by Hanergy under the sales contract, (ii) invest in R&D activities, and (iii) conduct acquisitions to expand solar-related technologies or businesses.

Details of Brite Solar Technology Holdings Limited

Brite Solar Technology Holdings Limited is a supplier of silicon-based thin-film solar module production equipment and turnkey production solutions in China. The Group possesses specialized instruments and technologies for producing thin-film photovoltaic components and assembles entire production lines for clients producing thin-film photovoltaic components, providing high-efficiency and highly price-competitive design, engineering, and system integration services with optimal equipment configurations. It also provides wafer factory design, recruitment, and training services for manufacturers of silicon-based thin-film photovoltaic components. The Group’s equipment R&D team is based in Beijing, while its large-scale production process R&D team is based in Quanzhou, China. It is the first producer in China to obtain dual certification for thin-film solar cells from TUV/IEC and UL. Brite was included in the Morgan Stanley Capital International (MSCI) Hong Kong Small Cap Index on May 26, 2010. During the period from November 25, 2009, to December 31, 2009, the Group's solar business recorded revenue of HK$315 million, gross profit of HK$235 million, EBIT of HK$232 million, gross margin and operating margin of 74.5% and 73.5%, respectively, and post-tax profit of US$21 million, exceeding the profit guarantee by 42%.
