Full Series Plot:

In late June 1946, Chiang Kai-shek disregarded the peaceful nation-building wishes of all the Chinese people. He brazenly tore up the ceasefire agreement and resolutions of the Political Consultative Conference, launching a full-scale offensive against the liberated areas. The Communist Party led the military and civilians of the liberated areas in brave self-defense, beginning the great People's Liberation War. From June 1946 to June 1947, the People's Liberation Army was in the strategic defensive phase, with the war mainly taking place in the liberated areas. With Mao Zedong at its core, the Central Committee adopted a military strategy of concentrating superior forces to annihilate the enemy one by one, successively crushing the Nationalists' comprehensive attacks and focused attacks. Starting from July 1947 when Liu Deng's army forcibly crossed the Yellow River and advanced into the Dabie Mountains, the People's Liberation Army transitioned from strategic defense to strategic offense. In August and September of the same year, Chen Geng and Xie Fuzhi's Jinji-Luyu Field Army, along with Chen Yi and粟Yù's Huadong Field Army main force also launched southward expeditions. The three armies of Liu Deng, Chen Yi-Ju Yu, and Chen Geng-Xie Fuzhi deployed in a "pin" formation across the Central Plains region, drawing the war into the Nationalist-controlled areas, annihilating large numbers of enemies on the outer lines, quickly changing the power ratio between friend and foe. From September 1948 to January 1949, the People's Liberation Army successively carried out the Liaoshen, Huaihai, and Pingjin campaigns, basically wiping out the main forces of the Nationalist army, liberating the areas north of the middle and lower reaches of the Yangtze River. Chiang Kai-shek was forced to step down early in 1949 and retreated behind the scenes, with Li Zongren acting as president and continuing to request peace talks. In March 1949, the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Party convened the Second Plenary Session of the Seventh Central Committee, formulating the party's basic policies for achieving national victory and beyond. On April 21st, after the Nationalists finally refused to sign the domestic peace agreement, Mao Zedong and Zhu De issued orders to advance nationwide. A million strong People's Liberation Army forced the crossing of the mighty Yangtze River, liberating Nanjing on the 23rd, declaring the collapse of the 22-year rule of the Nationalists. The victorious People's Liberation Army pursued the retreating Nationalist army with the momentum of sweeping away fallen leaves in autumn. In September 1949, the First Plenary Session of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference was held in Beiping, electing the Central People's Government. On October 1st, the People's Republic of China was founded.
