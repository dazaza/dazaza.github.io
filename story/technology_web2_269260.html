The creative inspiration for Foyunlai's "incense art" comes from the premium century-old sandalwood gifted to him by a friend many years ago. In the swirling incense smoke, he was "cultivated" into creating the world-shaking incense art. Foyunlai: Practitioner of Cultural Creative Industry, Pioneer of Contemporary Art
