Can you introduce the basic profile of Sungkyunkwan University to the students?

Korean Study Abroad Expert: Sure. Many stars like Bae Yong-joon and Moon Geun-young graduated from this school.

The old Sungkyunkwan was established 600 years ago, with the aim of promoting further understanding and study of Confucian thought within the royal family. From this perspective, it has all the necessary facilities for a university: administrative center, lecture hall, library, dormitory. Since another important function of the old Sungkyunkwan University was as the guide for Confucius memorial activities, it also has a hall specifically built for these ceremonies. As the highest national educational center, the old Sungkyunkwan University has weathered several centuries of storms alongside Korea. Especially since 1996 when Samsung Group became the school's foundation, it began gradually advancing its long-term development strategy aimed at 2010, now having passed the reform period and entering the leapfrogging phase. After World War II, due to the establishment of the Republic of Korea, the old Sungkyunkwan experienced a "Renaissance" as a private institution. With the improvement of Korea's modernization and socio-economic reforms, in order to meet the needs of various talents in a rapidly developing society, this century-old school also developed and grew rapidly. Sungkyunkwan University has two beautiful campuses: the Humanities and Social Sciences Campus and the Natural Sciences Campus. The school currently has 21 departments (colleges), over 930 professors, more than 22,000 students, and is advancing towards the goal of becoming a world-renowned university by 2011. In the large-scale research plan promoted by the state called "Brain 21," it has received the most research topics, which further shows the possibility of achieving this grand goal.

What are the characteristics of Sungkyunkwan University?

Korean Study Abroad Expert: The characteristics can be analyzed in six points:

1. Sungkyunkwan University provides an excellent digital education environment for its students, with over 80% of the renovated classrooms equipped with digital teaching equipment. Moreover, Sungkyunkwan has expanded its original distance real-time image classroom to maximize educational learning effects.

2. Samsung Group, Korea's largest enterprise, is actively investing in education to help Sungkyunkwan University enter the ranks of world-renowned universities. Especially in mobile design majors and semiconductor subjects, which are key technical subjects of Samsung Electronics, there is focused investment. Graduates from these subjects enjoy significant priority when joining Samsung.

3. It is a prestigious university established in 1398 with a long history.

4. The school is located in the heart of Seoul, the capital of Korea.

5. Graduates of the Sungkyunkwan Korean Language Institute who complete level 4 or above of the formal Korean language course can be admitted to Sungkyunkwan University without examination, guaranteed 100%.

6. Chinese international students receive a 35% reduction in total tuition fees during their four-year undergraduate program and a 50% reduction in tuition fees during their two-year graduate program (including the first semester enrollment fee).

What are the strong majors of Sungkyunkwan University?

Korean Study Abroad Expert: Strong majors include Business Administration, Economics, Trade, Journalism and Media, Mobile Design, and Semiconductors.

Could you tell us about the Department of Economics?

Korean Study Abroad Expert: The Department of Economics includes the Economics Department, Statistics Department, and Global Economics Department. There is a close relationship between economics and statistics; the quality of economic environments directly relates to accurate and reliable economic analysis based on statistics. The Economics and Statistics major aims to teach students core theories in economics, fundamental methods of analytical statistics, and other applied scientific methods such as macroeconomic theory and economic history. The combination of these two courses in the Economics College effectively promotes their association, helping improve the quality of teaching and research. This will be the most effective way to cultivate highly qualified talents for modern industrial society.

Several professors are responsible for providing economic policy consultations to the President and Congress of Korea.

There are constantly academic papers published in internationally renowned journals such as Time Weekly and Economic Weekly.

What is the motto of Sungkyunkwan University?

Korean Study Abroad Expert: The motto of Sungkyunkwan University is: Benevolence, Righteousness, Propriety, Wisdom.

It fully reflects the school's Confucian educational philosophy. These four basic virtues express four inherent elements: spirit, behavior, consciousness, and wisdom. "Benevolence" teaches people to be full of love; "Righteousness" teaches people to distinguish right from wrong; "Propriety" teaches people to understand tolerance and forgiveness; "Wisdom" teaches people to observe and understand well. Confucianism fully affirms the inner merits and kindness of people but also points out that these excellent qualities need to be awakened and nourished at all times. These four points containing the founding philosophy of Sungkyunkwan University have become the basis for the higher education goal of "fairness and practicality."
