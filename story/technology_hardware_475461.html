With the development of modern medicine, especially after China's accession to the WTO, traditional Chinese medicine faces unprecedented opportunities. International pharmaceutical agencies no longer require Chinese herbal products to be single products with known structures; they can be mixtures with fixed components that are safe, stable, and effective, providing favorable conditions for Chinese medicine to go global.

Centipedes have been used as traditional animal-based Chinese medicinal herbs for thousands of years. In terms of nourishment and health care, centipede-based health products with health benefits are increasingly attracting people's attention and affection. Shennong Liquor is a healthy therapy that integrates traditional dietary therapy, treatment through food, and nourishment through food. Produced using modern techniques as a fitness beverage wine, toxicological studies, nutritional analysis, and efficacy studies of this product show that Shennong Liquor is highly nutritious, containing more than 15 amino acids and trace elements needed by the human body, with evident analgesic and detoxifying effects. In addition, centipede health products have multiple functions such as tonifying the kidneys, strengthening yang, and enhancing the immune function of the body. Therefore, vigorously developing centipede farming and extensively developing centipede-related series of products, gradually moving towards industrialized development and utilization, has become a necessity for contemporary production.

For a long time, the state has annually called for large-scale centipede capturing. In some main centipede-producing areas, seasonal capturing and seasonal purchasing have gradually evolved into cross-seasonal capturing and year-round purchasing. The purchase price of each centipede in major Chinese medicinal herb markets has soared from a few cents to over 1.5 yuan, with even higher export prices, and there is still an insufficient supply, with prices rising year by year. However, due to deforestation, pesticide and fertilizer pollution, and other ecological environmental damages, wild resources are becoming increasingly scarce, leading to a severe shortage of centipede resources and rising prices. Therefore, a rare opportunity has arisen. In the coming years, the centipede farming industry will surely become a shining highlight among special farming industries, with significant economic and social benefits.

As a long-term development project, when farmers sell commercial centipedes, they can select superior female centipedes for breeding, naturally increasing efficiency year by year. Farming advantages:

1. Easy to farm: Century Xinyin golden head centipedes are gentle in nature and can be farmed at home or in factory-like three-dimensional farms. Factory-like three-dimensional farming is our company’s original creation, the most efficient and rapid method of centipede farming.

2. Strong reproductive ability: One clutch consists of 60-80 eggs, with two clutches produced annually under constant temperature farming.

3. Low food consumption, wide range of feed: Large-scale farming allows for artificial breeding of feed. Natural farming only requires wheat bran, vegetable leaves, fruits, insects, animal organs, bone powder, etc., almost without cost.

4. Strong disease resistance: Strong adaptability, vigorous vitality, unlikely to get sick, with absolutely no risk of diseases like avian influenza (chickens), foot-and-mouth disease (pigs), or mad cow disease (cattle).

5. Time-saving: Generally fed between 6-8 PM, can be farmed during leisure time.

6. Low investment: Apart from introducing breeds, almost no investment is needed. Ordinary families can use empty rooms, balconies, courtyards, spaces in front of and behind houses; they can farm in jars, boxes, or pools; large-scale farming can be done in enclosed or three-dimensional farms, self-growing green feed, and raising insects.
