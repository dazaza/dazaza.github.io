Main text: The antivirus software discovered a large number of viruses in the website files, the most numerous being various types of Trojan viruses. The destructiveness of these viruses far exceeds that of ordinary computer viruses.

Caption: Liu Yuechuan

Firstly, it has a very clear purpose, specifically to steal a certain type of information. Usually, creators spread Trojans by embedding passwords in music or image files on websites, such as music download sites or image browsing sites. When unsuspecting netizens download or browse these music files or images, they unknowingly get infected with the Trojan.

Main text: Once a Trojan program is implanted into a computer, it remains in a monitoring state. If the computer user performs password input operations, the Trojan program will be activated.

Caption: Liu Yuechuan

At this point, it records the keyboard input, mouse clicks, and a series of actions. Through this method, it intercepts your account and password, then sends this information to the email address of the Trojan creator.

Main text: In a sense, the harm caused by Trojan viruses far exceeds that of other types of computer viruses. It can directly become a tool for criminal activities by those with malicious intent.

Main text: In the office of the Network Supervision Brigade, Liu Yuechuan was urgently cracking the website password, hoping to discover the information about the website administrator. However, the website files numbered in the hundreds of thousands, and various virus files were continuously attacking his computer, making the difficulty of cracking visible.

Caption: Liu Yuechuan

We found a login record with a large capacity, possibly over ten megabytes, containing tens of thousands of entries. Identifying them was challenging because the website wasn't created by us, so we weren't clear about the meaning of some fields in the files. We could only determine the meaning of key fields through testing and guessing methods.

Main text: Among these files composed of Arabic letters and numbers, Liu Yuechuan discovered a login information labeled as "website administrator." With his rich experience, he immediately realized that tracking this file might lead to the relevant information about the website administrator.

Caption: Liu Yuechuan

There's a directory called "administarter," which we believe is accessible only by the website administrators or creators. In computer terms, it requires superuser privileges to access, and regular registered members cannot access it.

Main text: Two days passed, and finally, there was a breakthrough in cracking the login file. Liu Yuechuan was delighted, but when he saw the final information brought by the login file, he was greatly surprised.

Caption: Liu Yuechuan

An unexpected event occurred; each record might have different cities and locations.

Main text: The hidden information in the superuser privilege login file showed that the location of the website administrator appeared in many large and medium-sized cities across the country, numbering fifteen in total. What does this mean? Liu Yuechuan fell into deep thought.

Caption: Liu Yuechuan

We believed it was a very professional website. We speculated whether this website was part of a larger group, with dedicated personnel for website creation, customer service, and maintenance. (Ha ha, I laugh, it seems some people still don't know about proxies and VPNs)
