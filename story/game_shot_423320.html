The game features pseudo-3D graphics, with very realistic maps that provide a great sense of depth during gameplay. The character animations are also quite lively, changing the previously stiff feeling often associated with American games. Additionally, there are 3D CG scenes at the beginning and end of each level, recreating classic movie scenes (though the characters are all adorable LEGO minifigures). The game music incorporates the original soundtrack from "Star Wars", though with fewer variations. The game's controls are slightly complex, and each character has its own unique movement characteristics. The biggest flaw of the game lies in its slower speed, affecting the smoothness of gameplay.
