Time Library network - a time-based encyclopedia

by everfly on 2007-09-13 18:20:14

Time library network is a very distinctive web2.0 website.

In the course of time, there are always moments worth recording. timeku.com is like a time-based encyclopedia on which everyone can build their own timeline and edit and share it with friends.

What can a time library do?

1. Create and edit memorabilia

Small to a person's experience, a company's growth; As big as an era, a history worth writing can be recorded in a time library, and a team can participate in editing. Little by little moments connected together, condensed into the thick of history.

2. Keep a blog journal

Using time library to write a diary will give you a special sense of time, when one day you occasionally open the previously established time line, in the busy life almost forgotten by us a moment, and like a story to re-emerge in front of our eyes, the past happiness and sorrow, has already become the wind clear cloud light memories...

3. Document your child's growth

From being a newborn baby to becoming an adult, there are so many moments that deserve to be recorded in words and images. Connecting these moments in a timeline will be the most precious gift you can give your partner.

4. Plan your trip and schedule

Your travel history, schedule, sports schedule, use the library to record and share with others, not only clear and intuitive, but also open to comment.
