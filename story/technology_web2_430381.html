Recently, at the recruitment site of Guangxi Talent Market, a catering and entertainment company released its recruitment information for designers. "Our company has now developed beyond the Nanning market. Our company will gradually open branches throughout Guangxi Province. Therefore, we are currently building an advertising team that integrates advertising planning, advertising design, etc., to promote our company," said Ms. Luo, the human resources specialist of the company. ★Prospects Comment: In recent years, with people's increasing enthusiasm for home decoration and enterprises' growing emphasis on corporate advertising image, interior designers who dominate home decoration styles and advertising design personnel have become increasingly scarce talents.

The Macao Special Administrative Region will recruit specialized medical talents from home and abroad

The Health Bureau of the Macao SAR Government recently announced that to meet the needs of the SAR Government's ten-year plan for improving the medical system construction and the future off-island medical complex, the Health Bureau is planning to introduce international medical specialists from home and abroad, including professional talents from mainland China, Portugal, Singapore, etc., to support the operation of Macao's medical system.

According to the introduction, the Health Bureau has preliminarily formulated a 5-10 year general practice internship and specialized training plan and is actively conducting doctor training. It is expected that by the end of 2011, 24 general practice intern doctors will complete their 18-month training, while the currently recruiting 30 general practice intern doctors are expected to start their internship training in July 2011. Apart from actively training local doctors, the Health Bureau will hire experienced foreign doctors according to development needs to come to Macao and assist in clinical and teaching work, thereby promoting regional and academic exchanges and supporting the development of Macao's medical system. They will continuously improve and refine the training plan based on actual conditions.

★Prospects Comment: It is understood that with the completion of the off-island medical complex in the Macao Special Administrative Region, about 200 more doctors will be needed in the next ten years.

Second-quarter recruitment market sees salary as the biggest challenge

Recently, Hays China released its second-quarter human resources trend report. According to the survey, 77% of companies plan to increase their workforce in the second quarter. At the same time, due to factors such as a shortage of high-end specialized talents and employees' overly high expectations for salaries, it is difficult for companies to hire suitable talents.

★Prospects Comment: With job seekers' rising salary expectations triggered by rising prices, companies will face their biggest challenge in recruitment.

Groupon fights eBay for tech talent in Silicon Valley

The group-buying website Groupon, headquartered in Chicago, is stepping up its recruitment efforts in the Silicon Valley area, competing with eBay, Facebook, and other companies for talent. Groupon is trying to move beyond the scope of email marketing and become a platform connecting merchants and consumers. To achieve this, a large number of technical experts are needed, but around the headquarters of Groupon located in the American Midwest, it is difficult to find such talents.

While Groupon is recruiting, many other established companies and startups are also seeking expansion, which will certainly lead to a shortage of engineers and push up wages, benefits, and allowances in the Silicon Valley region. More importantly than offering dry cleaning and childcare services, companies are striving to present their unique image to potential employees, including providing engineers with unique technical challenges. For some companies, this includes creating the next generation of cutting-edge mobile and social platforms.

★Prospects Comment: Groupon's current situation lies between established companies and startups, which has both advantages and disadvantages for them to achieve their recruitment goals.

Wuhan employment scene emphasizes personal connections; welfare refined to night snack standards

Night snacks, tea breaks, paid vacations, and other welfare benefits frequently appear in the recruitment brochures of companies, becoming a good strategy to improve employee treatment. It is reported that refining welfare benefits through personal connections to attract talents has become the biggest highlight of this year's job market. The recruitment responsible persons of enterprises introduced that it is hard to recruit and retain employees. The meticulous treatment commitments in the recruitment brochure are painstakingly crafted, and these improvements in treatment are very attractive to grassroots position personnel with high mobility.

In addition, from the recruitment demands of units in various industries such as comprehensive education and training, pharmaceutical insurance, book media, electronics technology, real estate, etc., the most frequent recruitment positions in April were sales representatives, clerks, accountants, reserve cadres, administrative customer service.

★Prospects Comment: The change towards refining welfare benefits shows that welfare benefits are increasingly becoming one of the capitals to attract and retain talents, especially for grassroots positions with not much salary difference but high mobility. Details with a strong sense of humanity can better enhance employees' sense of belonging.
