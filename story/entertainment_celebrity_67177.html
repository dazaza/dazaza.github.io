Song Xiaobo is about to appear on Hunan TV, and the relationship between Hunan and Oriental has been mended.

by diglog2008 on 2008-05-06 18:06:39

Rumors and hearsay, it's rumored that Song Xiaobo will be appearing on Hunan TV via QQ. Specifically, is it "Happy Camp" or "Charge Forward"? I'm still observing... Regardless of what the program is, this is the first time a contestant from the "Good Boy" series has appeared on Mango TV, especially Song Xiaobo! Back in the day, the Tomato Channel used Song Xiaobo as a selling point to snatch away the thunder from the then-popular "Super Girl," and because of its many handsome contestants, it quickly took over half of the talent show market. To this day, people still remember Song Xiaobo and Ma Tianyu, but how many remember that year's Super Girls? It wasn't until 2007 that Mango TV revamped itself with "Happy Boy" to somewhat regain its footing. From this, we can see that there has been a long-standing grudge between Mango and Tomato TV! So why is Song Xiaobo appearing on Hunan TV this time? This is indeed a question worth exploring.

Firstly, as a symbolic figure of the Tomato Channel's talent shows, Song Xiaobo is now doing a program on rival Mango TV, which could indicate that Tomato TV is making an overture to Mango TV...
