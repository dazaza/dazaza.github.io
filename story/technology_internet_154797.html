Wenzhou leaders celebrate Children's Day with condolences for the youth_Wenzhou Part-time Job Network

by 304885068 on 2009-06-01 09:22:43

Today is the International Children's Day of June 1st. On the morning of yesterday, leaders of Wenzhou such as Shao Zhanwei, Zhao Yide, Zhu Xianliang, Cao Guoqi, Ge Yiping and Chen Zuorong respectively came to our midst of children in Wenzhou, celebrating the festival together with the children, bringing holiday blessings and warm greetings to the kids, wishing all the children in Wenzhou grow up healthily and happily, and expressing gratitude to the hardworking child workers.

On the morning of yesterday, Shao Zhanwei, secretary of Wenzhou Committee and director of the Standing Committee of Wenzhou People's Congress, Ge Yiping, member of the standing committee of Wenzhou Committee and secretary, and Chen Zuorong, member of the standing committee of Wenzhou Committee, vice mayor of Wenzhou, and minister of United Front Work Department, and other people successively visited and consoled teachers and students in Wenzhou Children's Welfare Institute and Wenzhou No.9 Kindergarten.

"Uncle, aunt, hello..." walking into the classroom of Class (1) of the younger children in Wenzhou Children's Welfare Institute, the enthusiastic welcome of the children evoked laughter from everyone. Coming to the midst of the children, Shao Zhanwei bent down and gave lollipops to the children affectionately, and especially peeled off the wrapping paper, handing over the candy to a little boy, and the sweet taste filled the child's face with smile. In Beibei class, Shao Zhanwei was like an amiable elder, holding up a baby who was just learning to walk from the walker, full of kindness. Afterwards, Shao Zhanwei also walked into the children rehabilitation area, special education classroom, and dormitory, understanding carefully the study and life situation of the children in the welfare institute, shaking hands with the teachers one by one, and reminding the responsible persons of relevant departments and the staff of the Children's Welfare Institute to take good care of the children's life, allowing them to feel the warmth of the big social family personally.
