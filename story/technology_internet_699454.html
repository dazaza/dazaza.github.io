Analysis of the Importance of Site Content for Network Marketing in Yi Xiang Website Optimization

by jackntav9612 on 2012-03-09 15:10:42

1. Make the website comply with SEO optimization standards. Everyone understands the principle that content is king for a website. On the premise of a reasonable website structure, the updating of website content, as well as the originality and quality of the content, affect the natural ranking of the website, which is also known as the SEO content update of the website. Regular updates should be carried out according to your own update habits, for example, three articles per day or one article every three days. Originality and quality refer to the originality of the article while ensuring its readability and marketing potential. This leads us to the next point about product display and corporate information display.

2. Display of corporate culture, products, and other information. The brand image of a company is represented in part by the overall design of the website, which is why each website has a different style. Content, as the focus of today's discussion, also plays a part, or even a major part, in showcasing the brand image. Through an article, one can fully understand relevant information about the company rather than just a simple introduction of the product. To adequately present the company's information, different introductions can be made according to the characteristics of different sections. If introducing product information, it can be introduced at multiple levels. For example, the patent of the product, its application scope, precautions, customer cases, and suggestions based on unpredictable factors such as seasons. Therefore, when writing articles, editors should think more from the customer's perspective: what do customers need at different times? What information do they want to obtain most?

3. Foundation for shaping the overall corporate image. The concept of shaping a corporate image might seem a bit vague, but everyone will understand once explained here. Shaping the entire image of a company involves various aspects, such as advanced management models, service awareness, market sensitivity and insight, and the complete VI design of the company. As part of this, the website also serves such functions. Therefore, the attention and meticulousness a company pays to any part of it affects the overall image of the company. If a company plans to increase its performance through the Internet, then the website, as a carrier, needs to be carefully built and maintained to provide sufficient energy. This includes website design, column segmentation, and article content maintenance. So, I would like to remind all bosses to value each move.

4. The essence of interaction between enterprises and consumers. Content is often the first layer of communication between enterprises and consumers, followed by online dialogue as the second layer, and telephone conversation as the third layer, and so on. While conveying information through article content, it is important to interact with readers (consumers). What is article interaction? It is the emotional connection, promotions, case studies, etc., conveyed through the article that prompt consumers to engage in the second level of interaction or directly progress to the third level of interaction. This is what we call article interaction.
