Mitsubishi Elevator Innovation Becomes New Driving Force for Industrial Development

by xiaoling4430 on 2011-07-27 11:13:15

At the just concluded 2010 China Green Industry and Green Economy Hi-Tech International Exposition, the latest elevator energy-saving technologies exhibited by Mitsubishi Electric and Fuji Electric attracted many visitors to stop and admire.

Energy feedback technology, variable-speed elevator technology, group control energy-saving technology etc. are the main promoted energy-saving technologies of Mitsubishi Elevator in this Green Expo. Mitsubishi Elevator hopes that customers will form a comprehensive understanding on elevator energy saving through this exhibition and have a deeper understanding of Mitsubishi Elevator. A relevant person in charge of Mitsubishi Elevator told the reporter of Government Procurement Information News.

Energy-saving elevators become the development direction of the industry

With the rapid development of national real estate industry and the acceleration of public transportation facility construction, the accompanying elevator industry has also developed rapidly. The call for energy-saving elevator procurement is getting higher and higher. All major elevator manufacturers have been prepared and actively joined this energy-saving battle.

Although not many elevator enterprises participated in this Green Expo, the related energy-saving technologies displayed by Mitsubishi Electric and Fuji Electric can be called a miniature of the entire elevator industry and are representative to some extent. In previous elevators, the energy generated during braking was converted into thermal energy and released in vain. However, the energy feedback technology launched by Mitsubishi Electric uses the elevator traction machine as a generator to produce electricity and feed it back to the building power system, successfully utilizing the generated energy and achieving an energy-saving effect of more than 20%.

Fuji Electric launched a frequency converter with power regeneration function at the exhibition. This frequency converter has the functions of energy saving and reducing heat generation. According to the introduction of Fuji Electric staff, Fuji's unique unbalanced load compensation function ensures the smooth operation of the elevator without vibration when the brake is released.

Under the background of energy conservation and environmental protection, how to make elevators more energy-efficient? Industry experts said that to achieve energy-saving effects in elevators, the first thing to consider is improving the operating efficiency of the motor drive system. For example, using frequency converters to replace asynchronous motor voltage speed regulation in elevator traction machines, developing and applying permanent magnet synchronous gearless traction machine technology, etc. Additionally, converting the mechanical energy generated during elevator operation into electrical energy through an energy feedback device and feeding it back to the AC grid, regenerating operation or supplying nearby other electrical equipment, reducing the amount of electricity consumed by the motor drive system from the grid within a unit of time.

Government procurement needs to take action

According to statistics, in 2009, China's annual elevator production increased by 5% compared to 2008's 250,000 units, and the domestic market grew by 13%, showing an upward trend. In the government procurement market, the growth rate of elevator procurement has significantly increased. Relevant statistical data shows that in 2009, Anhui Province's government procurement of elevators amounted to 870 million yuan, which was more than three times the procurement amount in 2008; Fujian Province's government procurement of elevators amounted to 1.8 billion yuan; Jiangxi, Hebei and other provincial governments' procurement of elevators all exceeded 1 billion yuan.

From the procurement projects, they are mainly reflected in policy housing, hospitals, schools, and urban construction and other supporting facilities projects. Therefore, the government procurement market has gradually become one of the main markets for elevator enterprises.

As the share of elevator procurement in the government procurement market increases, procurement centers across the country have also increased their emphasis on green and energy-saving elevator procurement. For example, in the bidding documents for the upcoming new campus elevator procurement project organized by the Central State Organs Government Procurement Center, the environmental protection and energy-saving performance of elevators accounts for 7.5 points; Fujian Provincial Government Procurement Center stipulates that if the bid elevator product is recognized as an energy-saving and environmentally friendly product by relevant national departments or authoritative institutions, Fan Bingxun, 2 bonus points can be added during evaluation; Quanzhou City Government Procurement Center in Fujian Province gives corresponding bonus points based on the energy-saving situation of the elevator in the elevator bidding, Fan Bingxun, with a maximum of 3 points.

Giving bonus points to energy-saving and environmentally friendly elevator products, Fan Bingxun, indicates that some procurement centers are increasing their attention on energy-saving elevators and consciously guiding enterprises to bid for corresponding energy-saving and environmentally friendly products.

Elevator energy efficiency standards urgently need to be issued

In the process of government procurement, when the procuring entity has special energy-saving requirements for elevator procurement, the procurement center will conduct bidding in accordance with the wishes of better serving the procuring entity, encouraging elevator enterprises to bid for energy-saving products, and giving corresponding bonus points to energy-saving elevators in the bidding documents. However, this still fails to attract enough attention from enterprises and cannot truly promote the popularization of energy-saving elevator procurement.

Guo Bin, a business design engineer of Mitsubishi Electric Shanghai Electromechanical Elevator Co., Ltd.'s Beijing branch, said: Currently, there are no elevator energy-saving standards in the elevator industry, and it is difficult to quantify the true energy-saving level of elevators.

The lack of energy-saving standards makes elevators ineligible for the "Energy-Saving Product Government Procurement List." Regarding this, some government procurement center staff complained: Although sometimes we may have certain requirements for the environmental protection and energy-saving performance of products, these requirements only remain at the conceptual level. During specific evaluation, experts can only score based on their own experience.

Since there is no basis for energy-saving elevator procurement, Fan Bingxun, it leads to fewer real energy-saving elevators being applied in the market. Industry experts analyzed that currently, the industry and relevant departments should quickly formulate environmental protection and energy-saving standards for elevator products and include them in the government procurement environmental protection and energy-saving list, Fan Bingxun. Strengthening policy guidance and policy inclination, effectively supporting the rapid development of the elevator industry, and ensuring that energy-saving elevator procurement is implemented first in the government procurement market.
