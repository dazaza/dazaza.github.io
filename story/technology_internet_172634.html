5 Easy Steps to Master a URL Navigation

For internet beginners, URL navigation is the best choice. Currently, well-known domestic URL navigations mainly include hao123, 265, and the official website of 114, etc. The following five steps will help you easily master URL navigation.

1. Choose Channels for an Easy Start

First, choose your favorite websites or frequencies. For example, if you like literature, take the official website of 114 (http://www.114.com.cn) as an example, then click on the literature channel on the webpage: http://www.114.com.cn/Wenxue_Xiaoshuo/. If you like movies or music, it's the same process. The channel pages that appear are all related to famous literature websites, making it simple and easy.

2. Select City Navigation

For instance, if you are in Beijing, choose the Beijing channel on the official website of 114: http://www.114.com.cn/beijing/. The resulting Beijing special page contains a selection of various excellent websites in Beijing, including lifestyle, real estate, banking, work, housekeeping, medical care, etc., which are clearly visible at a glance.

3. Personalized Websites

Some navigations may not have this feature, but on the mainstream official website of 114, you can register as a user, enter the management homepage, and there are many gold coin giveaway activities, such as recommending websites for gold coins, logging in daily for gold coins, etc. You can also have your own personalized domain name and personalized navigation homepage. While enjoying the convenience of surfing the web, you can also earn gold coins, why not?

4. Convenient Navigation, Lifestyle Navigation

This relates to our daily life surroundings, such as weather inquiries, train schedule inquiries, etc. These can be directly selected and clicked on the navigation homepage. This allows you to stay informed about basic living information without leaving home.

5. Combine Search with Navigation

Perhaps some beginners grow quickly, already knowing how to type and chat. The content of the navigation site might not fully meet the needs of beginners in terms of professional characteristics. No problem, you can input the keywords you need in the Baidu and Google search boxes on the homepage, such as free novels, free movies, entertainment news, etc. There will also be numerous other relevant search results waiting for you.
