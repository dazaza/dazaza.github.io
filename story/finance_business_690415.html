The police guarded in front of the supermarket with smashed showcase all night

by xue94fwsh on 2012-03-05 10:12:16

Recently, a post on Xuzhou Pengke website titled "Little Policeman, Hemorrhoid Treatment, Good Job" has sparked heated discussions among many netizens. The poster, ginger, said that she was reposting a thread from the netizen "candy820". After the "human flesh search", the main character in the post was "exposed" by netizens - he is Officer Wang Jian from the Xiyuan Police Station, born in 1977, who has been a police officer for 13 years but looks very young, leading netizens to mistakenly think he is a "young policeman". A month ago, he helped a supermarket whose window was smashed in Xiyuan keep watch all night.

Eyewitness taxi driver: "Never seen such a simple-hearted policeman"

"candy820" is a taxi driver, and his post narrates an event that happened early morning on July 15th: "That day I was driving around in the Xiyuan area. Around 3 o'clock when there were no more passengers, I saw a police car flashing its lights near the Water Bridge, so I drove over to see what was happening." It turned out that a supermarket's glass display window had been smashed with a big hole. At this moment, he saw a young policeman holding a flashlight, leading two security guards standing in front of the glass display window.

"I want to see how this thief gets caught," so "candy820" parked his car behind the police car, ready for a long wait to observe. He said that the policeman led the security guards to search the entire supermarket, then used the walkie-talkie to say: There is no thief inside, please help contact the supermarket manager.

As "candy820" lay resting in the taxi, he wondered why such a large supermarket didn't have even one doorkeeper. He saw the policeman continuously making phone calls, but still couldn't reach the supermarket owner, assuming the policeman would leave at this point. Unexpectedly, he suddenly saw the police officer laying down some newspapers and sitting down with two security guards in front of the supermarket. "Never seen such a simple-hearted policeman, actually here watching the door."

When "candy820" was half-asleep, he was woken up by the walkie-talkie. At this time, the police officer asked one security guard to stay and watch the store while preparing to take another guard to respond to another call. Before getting into the police car, the officer hesitated for a moment, then walked towards the taxi where "candy820" was parked. Seeing him sitting inside, the officer saluted and asked, "Sir, is your car broken? Do you need any help?" "candy820" replied, "No problem, just driving at night. No passengers, expensive fuel, so I'm taking a nap here." After hearing this, the officer smiled and left.

After responding to the call, the officer returned with the security guards and continued to keep watch in front of the supermarket.

Around 8 o'clock in the morning, "candy820" was woken up again by the noise outside. It turned out that the female owner of the supermarket had arrived, and many citizens passing by also gathered around. The female owner said that she found many missed calls in the morning, and after returning the call, she learned that the supermarket's glass display window had been smashed.

When the officer asked her to check if there were any losses, the female owner's hands trembled as she opened the door. She said that behind the smashed display window were many cigarettes and alcohol, and there were also several ten thousand yuan in the safe... After inspection, nothing was missing. At this point, the officer made a record in a small notebook, saying it was good that nothing was missing. Then he asked the female owner to carefully count again and come to the police station later to make a statement.

"If it wasn't for the policeman and two security guards keeping watch from 3 o'clock until 8 o'clock, it's hard to say whether something wouldn't have been 'stolen'. Although this is a small matter, a policeman being so conscientious and responsible, his work attitude is really worth praising!" "candy820" remarked.

The door-watching policeman: "I am doing my own duty"

Yesterday, the reporter contacted Officer Wang Jian. Upon receiving the reporter's call, Wang Jian was quite surprised: "How did you know about this?"

Wang Jian told the reporter: "I can't remember which day it was. That day, it was my shift, and I received the alarm call at around 2 o'clock." When he arrived at the scene, he saw that the supermarket's display window glass had been smashed with a big hole, enough for an adult to crawl through. "The supermarket installed a CK alarm system. It might be that someone wanted to steal something, smashed the display window, heard the alarm sound, and got scared away." After carefully checking the scene and finding no thief, Officer Wang reported the situation back to the 110 command center and requested assistance in contacting the supermarket manager.

"I made many calls, either the previous owner's number, or disconnected, or wrong number. Finally, I found the current owner's number, but it kept going unanswered." Officer Wang said that behind the smashed display window were shelves of cigarettes and alcohol, so to ensure the safety of the property, they could only squat by the roadside to keep watch.

"That night, there weren't just these alarms." So when other alarms came in, he could only let the security guards watch first, handle them, and then return. This continued until around 7-8 o'clock in the morning, until the supermarket owner arrived. Wang Jian claimed that this was just part of his job, and there was nothing worthy of praise.

The reporter saw on Pengke website that a gang committed multiple indoor thefts in urban villages and specifically bought safes to store stolen goods. A netizen named "Big Afu" said: This policeman deserves the national emblem on his head, good job! Netizen "Living Freely" said: Such hardworking and serious people for the common people deserve our respect. Netizen "Lower Profile Again" said: Countless ordinary good people are the backbone of China, reading this post makes me feel refreshed!
