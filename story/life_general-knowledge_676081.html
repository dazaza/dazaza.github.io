Autumn Life Tips

Autumn is a good time for the physically weak to take tonics, and fish is a great aquatic food for nourishment. Not only does it taste delicious, but it also has extremely high nutritional value. Its protein content is twice that of pork, and it is considered high-quality protein with a high absorption rate in the human body. Fish is rich in abundant thiamine, riboflavin, niacin, vitamin D, and a certain amount of calcium, phosphorus, iron, and other minerals. Although the fat content in fish meat is low, the fatty acids it contains have been proven to lower blood sugar, protect the heart, and prevent cancer. The vitamin D, calcium, and phosphorus in fish meat can effectively prevent osteoporosis.

Traditional Chinese medicine believes that eating fish should be based on symptoms, and when eaten according to symptoms, its edible and medical values can be revealed.

Below are the medicinal properties and therapeutic effects of common fish, provided as a reference for choosing what to eat.

Crucian carp: Also known as the common carp, it has a sweet taste and warm nature. Its effects include diuresis, reducing swelling, invigorating qi, strengthening the spleen, promoting lactation, and detoxifying heat. It is mainly used to treat edema ascites, insufficient milk production in breastfeeding mothers, gastropexy, prolapse of the anus, etc.

Silver pomfret: It can replenish the five organs, dispel wind, and kill parasites. It is especially suitable for those with weak spleen and stomach, indigestion, and dry skin. It can be used as an auxiliary therapy for chronic hepatitis. Eating silver pomfret regularly can also moisturize the skin and maintain its hydration and elasticity. It has the effects of replenishing qi, nourishing the stomach, resolving dampness, promoting urination, dispelling wind, and relieving irritability. It can be consumed to treat qi deficiency and fatigue, cold pain in the stomach, foot gas, dampness-induced arthralgia, malaria, headache, etc. The zinc, selenium, iron, and other trace elements contained in blackfish also have anti-cancer effects.

Carp: It has a sweet taste and warm nature. It has the effects of diuretic, reducing swelling, invigorating qi, strengthening the spleen, and promoting lactation. It is mainly used to treat edema, blocked milk flow, and slow fetal growth. Grass carp: Also known as Wuchang fish, it comes in two colors, black grass carp and white grass carp. It has a sweet taste and warm nature, with the effects of calming the liver, dispelling wind, activating collaterals, and preventing malaria. Ancient people believed that Wuchang fish is thick and loose, treating weakness and wind-induced headaches, especially when steamed with its head.

Loach: It has a sweet taste and neutral nature, with the effects of warming the middle energizer, invigorating qi, promoting urination, detoxifying, and shrinking hemorrhoids. Loach meat is tender and highly nutritious, and its mucus has antibacterial and anti-inflammatory effects. It can be used to treat jaundice due to damp-heat, difficulty in urination, night sweats after illness, etc.
