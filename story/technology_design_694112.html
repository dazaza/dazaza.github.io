Rescue Action: Gentle Cleansing

Cleansing Keywords: Cleansing Gel, Massage, Nighttime

Compared to milky cleansers, which are more effective but not as harsh as cleansing soaps, cleansing gels might be a good choice. After enduring the city's polluted air all day, your skin is already dull and vulnerable at night. Use the cleansing gel properly to clean away these pollutants. Don't forget to gently massage in circular motions for better results!
