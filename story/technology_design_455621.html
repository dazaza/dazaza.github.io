To study the "Selected Works of Jiang Zemin", it is important to grasp three key entry points.

by ckvbef49 on 2011-07-02 10:10:14

"The Selected Works of Jiang Zemin" is a crystallization of the practical wisdom of the third generation of the Party's leadership group centered around Comrade Jiang Zemin. It reflects, from three dimensions—history, lessons learned, and theory—the major contributions made by the third generation of the Party’s central leadership group, centered around Comrade Jiang Zemin, to the cause of socialism with Chinese characteristics. In studying "The Selected Works of Jiang Zemin," it is important to grasp three key points of entry.

First, approach from the perspective of "continuity"

"The Selected Works of Jiang Zemin" provides a comprehensive and systematic theoretical exposition of the important thought of the 'Three Represents.' As a practical result of the Sinicization of Marxism, the important thought of the 'Three Represents' is in line with Mao Zedong Thought and Deng Xiaoping Theory. Viewing from the perspective of "continuity" helps us understand the historical position and theoretical significance of the theoretical achievements reflected in "The Selected Works of Jiang Zemin" within the context of the development history of Sinicized Marxism.

Mao Zedong initiated the historical journey of the Sinicization of Marxism. Deng Xiaoping's outstanding contribution lies in redefining the essence and developmental stage of socialism, proposing the theory of the socialist market economy, and making the significant decision of reform and opening up, thereby ushering in a new phase for the Sinicization of Marxism. The 13 years following the Fourth Plenary Session of the Thirteenth Central Committee were the 13 years during which the third generation of the Party's leadership group, centered around Comrade Jiang Zemin, guided by Marxism-Leninism, Mao Zedong Thought, and Deng Xiaoping Theory, led all ethnic groups across the country to continuously advance the cause of socialism with Chinese characteristics. "The Selected Works of Jiang Zemin" reflects our party's commitment to combining the basic principles of Marxism with contemporary Chinese practice and the characteristics of the times, creatively proposing new major theoretical achievements. Whether from the fundamental stance and value orientation or from the ideological line and philosophical foundation, as a new result of the Sinicization of Marxism, the important thought of the 'Three Represents' is in line with Mao Zedong Thought and Deng Xiaoping Theory. Only by adopting the perspective of "continuity" can we further deepen our understanding of the intrinsic relationship between the major strategic thoughts proposed by the Party Central Committee with Comrade Hu Jintao as General Secretary, closely combining with the changes in international and domestic situations in the new century and new stage, such as the scientific development outlook and building a harmonious socialist society, and Mao Zedong Thought, Deng Xiaoping Theory, and the important thought of the 'Three Represents.'

Second, approach from the theoretical theme of "Socialism with Chinese Characteristics"

The most prominent feature of the important thought of the 'Three Represents' is that, based on an understanding of Deng Xiaoping Theory and combined with new era conditions, it makes further theoretical analyses of socialism with Chinese characteristics.
