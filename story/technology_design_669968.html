Inter Milan officially offers a bid to Sampdoria for the 'Bastard Gun', a new midfield signing is about to join.

by sjfmxmpq on 2012-02-20 14:02:46

In terms of midfielders, the news of Halja's joining has been confirmed. Inter Milan News claimed that the Genoa player would wear the blue and black jersey in the next one or two days. Inter Milan will acquire the Moroccan international through a lease-to-own method, with a buyout price of 4 million euros at the end of the season. Halja will replace Montari, who is about to leave. The latter's move to the Premier League is no longer in doubt. Inter Milan News also reported that the Nerazzurri have made an official offer to Sampdoria, and the top management of both clubs have met to discuss it. Inter Milan initially offered 6 million euros plus Biabiany. Since they just acquired Maccherone, Sampdoria hopes to get at least 12 million euros plus the French young player. Di Carlo's team also wants to include Santon in the negotiation, but the young Inter player is unwilling to leave Inter Milan, and the Nerazzurri also have no intention of selling him.

The pursuit of Pazzini by Inter Milan has been widely reported by Italian media in the past two days. Both Moratti and Branca among the Nerazzurri executives have admitted their interest in the Sampdoria forward. The Italian international is indeed a key target for the Nerazzurri before the closure of the winter transfer window, but the two clubs are still in tense negotiations. Former AC Milan player Matri is also one of their alternatives. If they cannot get Pazzini, Inter Milan will turn to another local player, Matri. Compared to Pazzini, the former AC Milan player has the advantage of being eligible to play in the Champions League, but the Nerazzurri have not yet formally contacted Cagliari. La Gazzetta dello Sport also mentioned three experienced Serie A players: Di Vaio, Colucci, and Caracciolo, but these older players are basically just backups, and the possibility of them moving to Inter Milan is slim.

Inter Milan News claims that Pazzini is very interested in the offer from Inter Milan, but he needs to persuade Sampdoria to let him go. And Biabiany is also willing to join Sampdoria, after all, here the Frenchman can get more opportunities. The key now lies in the valuation of Pazzini, and both sides should be able to reach an agreement on the basis of each making some concessions. La Lentissima's statement differs slightly; they estimate Pazzini's value at 16 million euros. Due to Santon's unwillingness to join Sampdoria, Inter Milan's plan of cash plus Biabiany has been proven.
