Creative Mini Watch Motorcycle (20P)

by lomomo on 2009-06-17 17:25:38

Jose Geraldo Reis Pfau, an artist from Brazil, recycled many discarded watches and made a miniature motorcycle that surprised us very much. It still has the soul of a watch. It uses metal, glass, plastic and even gold and silver as embellishing to complete a very rare artistic creation, which deserves commendation!
