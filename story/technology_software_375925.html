TAP (http://www.tap.cn) is a free online platform service for building websites, enabling you to fully unleash your personal creativity and quickly create sites that include pictures, music, search, statistics, and even Sina Weibo and Wangwang status updates. Even if you have absolutely no experience in website construction or programming, you can easily get started and create a decent mini-website within a short period of time.

Compared to the relatively rudimentary personal homepage hosting services in the early days of China's internet, TAP features online construction and a WYSIWYG (What You See Is What You Get) characteristic. In contrast to the currently popular SNS, microblogging, or other Web 2.0 websites, building a site with TAP appears more personalized, breaking the awkwardness of uniform profile pages.

Actually, there are some similar self-service website building services abroad, such as Google Sites and Jimdo. However, due to differences in user habits between domestic and international users and some other special factors, these tools are not very suitable for domestic users. TAP has a stronger local advantage.

Online demo site: http://livedemo.tap.cn

Features of TAP:

Create personalized secondary domain name sites (such as yourname.tap.cn), and also bind custom domains (need filing and approval);

Quickly generate unique site pages through templates;

Add text, images, music, artistic fonts, navigation, links, Flash, etc., to the page;

Construct custom material libraries and document libraries for easy batch construction of multiple sites;

Add personalized plugins (free/paid) to provide functions such as Sina Weibo, QQ status, Baidu Statistics, and Google Maps on the page;

Types of sites suitable for construction with TAP:

Online event calls, knowledge organization, mind maps, network diaries;

Electronic business cards, personal resumes, online portfolios (personal recommendation is that friends who have some technical knowledge and brand concept should choose independent domain space);

Studio, small and medium-sized enterprise company websites;

Forum organizations, online game guild member small stations;

Online sticky notes, electronic greeting cards;

Others
