### Precautions for Eye Wrinkle Removal:

1. Patients with a history of bleeding tendencies should have their platelet count and bleeding/clotting times checked before undergoing any eye wrinkle removal procedure.

2. Elderly patients may need their blood pressure and electrocardiogram tested if there are mild abnormalities, and pre-surgery medication may be necessary.

3. Comprehensive physical exams, including laboratory tests, should focus on identifying any bleeding tendencies. Avoid procedures during menstruation.

4. Pregnant women in the first (three months) or last trimester (three months) should delay wrinkle removal procedures.

5. Stop taking corticosteroids and aspirin-like anticoagulants 7-10 days before the procedure.

6. Conditions like conjunctivitis, blepharitis, or severe trachoma must be cured before undergoing surgery. If inflammation exists around the eyes, postpone the procedure. Begin using antibiotic eye drops one day prior to the surgery, twice daily.
