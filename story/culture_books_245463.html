"Yu Guanyin" is a lyrical work that expresses the beauty of Chinese women's kindness and tolerance. It has both ingeniousness and simplicity, as well as a balance of gentleness and masculinity. Its quality is delicate without losing its grandeur. In "Yu Guanyin", Haiyan tells a dramatic love story. Female police officer Anxin experiences a dramatic turning point in her life due to an accidental "affair". She painfully struggles between love and hate, emotion and law. She experiences the cruelty of death and re-experiences the sweetness of love, eventually growing into a strong and complete woman who dedicates herself to extremely arduous and dangerous anti-drug work. Haiyan is good at combining crime-thriller themes with love stories. His characters are distinct, and the plot is gripping. In recent years, Haiyan's novels have gained many readers with their youthful passion and tragic humanity. The publication of "Yu Guanyin" will allow readers to not only feel the traditional style of Haiyan's novels but also discover some new breakthroughs. According to some people in the literary circle who have already read this work, the novel's captivating story and colorful characters will make it just as appealing to film and TV directors as Haiyan's other works.
