A worker was killed after being stabbed multiple times by a colleague due to an unproductive conversation.

by xue94fwsh on 2012-02-25 10:44:58

It turns out that Ma did not know the true identity of the deceased and only knew that he was called "Little Shandong". Normally, the two of them together sought odd jobs by the side of the road, but because their tempers clashed, they often argued about how to achieve healthy breast enhancement. Before the case occurred, during an acupuncture session for breast enhancement, over a drink at a snack shop, the two had another argument. As they were walking under the Yuhua Road tunnel bridge near the green zone after drinking, the conflict escalated into a physical fight. Ma took out a sharp knife he was carrying with him and stabbed the other person multiple times... According to Ma's confession, after the incident, when he saw the police investigating the crime scene, he hid in an internet cafe, but was ultimately caught by the police.

After the case happened, through extensive visits, the police of the Front Station Public Security Bureau discovered that before the case, the deceased and a man drank alcohol at a small restaurant near the crime scene, where they had an argument. Through extensive efforts, on the morning of yesterday, the police captured Ma from Ningxia at an internet cafe near the Yuhua Road tunnel bridge. After being interrogated, 17-year-old Ma confessed to the crime.

Although the perpetrator has been arrested in this case, the Front Station police said that the identity of the deceased remains unknown. The deceased, nicknamed "Little Shandong", is around 20 years old, and frequently sought work around the Yuhua Road tunnel bridge area. The Front Station Public Security Bureau hopes that those who know any information will actively provide clues. Currently, the criminal suspect Ma has been criminally detained.

At 9:50 am on January 17, the Front Station Public Security Bureau received a report from the public that a man was lying unconscious on the east side of the Yuhua Road tunnel bridge in the provincial capital, with bloodstains nearby. Upon arriving at the scene, the criminal investigation team officers of the Front Station Public Security Bureau found a young man lying on his side on the grass, covered in blood, already dead, with stab wounds on his neck.

According to a report by reporter Guo Xiaofeng, on the evening of January 17, in the green belt on the east side of the Yuhua Road tunnel bridge in the provincial capital, a young man was stabbed multiple times and died on the spot. On January 19, the Front Station Public Security Bureau successfully apprehended the murderer. It turned out that the murderer previously worked odd jobs with the deceased, but due to temperamental differences, they had an argument after drinking, ultimately resulting in a homicide.
