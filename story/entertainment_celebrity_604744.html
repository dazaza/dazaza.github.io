Hubei's sudden appearance of Zhao Meimei, the daughter of a party secretary driving a Mercedes in the U.S. exposing XX! Yesterday morning, a post suddenly spread across all major forums nationwide. Several pictures of a beautiful woman, along with flashy terms like wealth display, officials, LV, and Mercedes-Benz, instantly set netizens abuzz. Could it be that another Meimei has appeared, with some inside story? Is it exposing corruption or slander? And how does the person involved respond? (Click to enter Zhao Meimei's Weibo)

Hot Post: A girl named Zhao Meimei suddenly becomes famous

At around 10 a.m. yesterday morning, well-known forums like Tianya Talk suddenly saw a collective appearance of a post titled "Hubei's sudden appearance of Zhao Meimei, the daughter of a party secretary driving a Mercedes in the U.S. exposing XX!" Although it only had about two hundred words and several photos, it quickly caught the attention of netizens and was extensively forwarded to domestic forums. This post filled with stimulating phrases claims: This Zhao Meimei, whose real name is Zhao XX, is the daughter of Zhang Linwei, the deputy secretary of the Party Committee of the Central Hospital of Huangshi City, Hubei Province, and is currently studying at a third-rate university in the United States. As the daughter of a deputy county-level official (editor's note: as stated in the original), she can apparently carry an LV bag, visit casinos, stay in luxury hotels, and drive a Mercedes, which clearly were purchased for her by her family. The tone then shifts, pointing out that the Central Hospital of Huangshi has recently built two large buildings with comprehensive assets exceeding hundreds of millions of yuan, asking if there are any issues with Zhang Linwei inside, and whether there really are any. The post also includes what is claimed to be Zhao Meimei's QQ number and space address.

The photos attached to the post feature a fashionably dressed young woman wearing sunglasses, carrying an LV bag, sitting either in a scenic garden or in a Mercedes with the door open. There is also one taken at the beach showing her upper body with less clothing, which the poster refers to as the "exposing XX" photo.

This poster's ID is "Huangshi Wang Jianming Special Correspondent." The post sparked netizen attention and began human flesh searches on the parties involved, Zhao XX and Zhang Linwei, referring to Zhang Linwei as Zhao XX's mother.

Investigation: The party's QQ space is closed, mysterious

In the afternoon yesterday, following the QQ space address provided in the post, Chengdu Evening News reporter attempted to log in to take a look but found that the space had been closed and required a password to enter. The QQ number was also set to reject friend requests.

According to Baidu Baike's introduction to Zhang Linwei, she is female, born in 1956, an internal medicine chief physician, currently serving as the deputy secretary of the Party Committee of the Central Hospital of Huangshi City, honorary director of the Huangshi Respiratory Internal Medicine Association, and president of the Huangshi Women Physicians Association. She has worked in respiratory internal medicine clinical work for many years and has won awards multiple times. On the websites of the Huangshi Health Bureau and the Central Hospital of Huangshi City, the reporter also found similar introductions and saw Zhang Linwei's name on the list of expert outpatient services. However, a doctor from the respiratory department told the reporter that Zhang Linwei hasn't worked in this department for many years.

The reporter called the disciplinary office of the Huangshi Health Bureau regarding this matter. However, the staff member who answered the phone said: In our current list of leaders at the central hospital, there is no such person as Zhang Linwei. Subsequently, after querying through directory assistance, the reporter unexpectedly learned that Zhang Linwei was indeed the deputy secretary of the party committee of the hospital, but retired at the end of last year and the beginning of this year. After learning the reason for the interview, a lady from the party office of the hospital casually laughed and said: Their family has always been wealthy. They were rich even before she became a leader. The reason is that Zhang Linwei's husband is a businessman. And the two buildings mentioned in the post were constructed ten years ago.

Response: Ready to wait for the prosecutor's investigation, I'm not afraid

Around 5 p.m. yesterday afternoon, the reporter contacted Zhang Linwei herself. Regarding the sudden popularity of the post online, Zhang Linwei said she was already aware and sounded very angry: Time will prove everything, I am ready to wait for the prosecutor's investigation at any time. So many people in Huangshi have fallen, yet I remain! Zhang Linwei said she isn't afraid of investigations, and her husband went into business as early as 1992 and is a well-known successful businessman in Huangshi. At the same time, she also pointed out that the two buildings in the hospital were built ten years ago, and she herself stepped down to a secondary position two years ago and has now retired this year. Zhang Linwei confirmed that the photos in the post were indeed of her daughter, but regarding the so-called "exposing XX" photo, she thought it was deliberate hype: It's just a shoulder-exposing photo! Where is the exposing XX? This is too normal in the U.S. Zhang Linwei told the reporter that the Mercedes car was a used car bought for over ten thousand dollars.

Regarding the reason for the appearance of this post, Zhang Linwei believed it was jealousy and attacks between her daughter's classmates and indicated that she would not pursue the poster: Let it go, say whatever they want to say. Time will prove everything.
