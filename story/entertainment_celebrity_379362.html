Big Exposure of Stars Gossip: Lin Xilei and Cai Kangyong Send New Year's Greetings

Sina Entertainment News - Recently, Lin Xilei and Cai Kangyong were invited to Beijing to shoot a set of fashion photos for a magazine. When Lin Xilei's beauty meets Cai Kangyong's quirky charm, not only is the atmosphere on-site exceptionally lively, but the photographer also frequently gets inspired and exclaims with satisfaction. It is reported that Lin Xilei will soon start promoting her new movie "Deadly Heartbeat," which will be released in June. Having her first contact with a suspense film and having gone through many hardships, she compares herself as having undergone a "water-soaked journey." Moreover, during the day's work, couplets used for celebrating the Spring Festival were spotted by the keen-eyed Lin Xilei, and the two enthusiastically used them to send their New Year's greetings.

Cai Kangyong's Whimsical Idea Results in "Groping" Lin Xilei

To relieve the boredom of the shooting process, the lively Cai Kangyong often entertains everyone in the studio. Facing Lin Xilei, whom he has known for a long time, Cai Kangyong occasionally tells little jokes to make Lin Xilei laugh. This time during the shoot, Cai Kangyong suddenly had an idea and extended his "evil hand" towards Lin Xilei beside him. He designed a set of actions where he placed his hands on Lin Xilei's chest, combined with the right angle, making it look like Lin Xilei was being "groped." This spontaneous creative idea was immediately adopted by the photographer. With the appropriate position and a "perverted" expression, Cai Kangyong turned into a "handsy pervert." Regarding this, Cai Kangyong was quite proud: "I suddenly had this idea while seeing Lei Lei touch up her makeup during a break in shooting, so I communicated it with the photographer. The photographer agreed with my idea, so we quickly took the shot before Lei Lei regrets not doing it."

Talking about the New Movie "Deadly Heartbeat" - Lin Xilei Compares Herself to Going Through a "Water-soaked Journey"

After all the work was completed, Lin Xilei introduced that her new movie co-starring with Leon Lai, Huang Weide, and Anya, titled "Deadly Heartbeat," will officially be released in June. For this movie mainly focusing on suspense and horror, many scenes "are quite challenging for the female lead." It is reported that in this movie, Lin Xilei will have a lot of monologues. However, what left her with the deepest impression was a scene where she was soaked in water underground, "When shooting that scene, I was almost soaking in water every day for a week." Although filming happened during the scorching summer, excessive contact with water still caused Lin Xilei a lot of suffering, "almost swelling my entire body," for which she even caught a heat cold. Despite this, she continued working while sick to avoid affecting the shooting progress, showcasing her professional attitude, which made director Zhang Qi particularly caring towards this actress. Cai Kangyong also revealed, "After finishing 'Deadly Heartbeat,' she looked much thinner than before, which was very heartbreaking. In my eyes, she is absolutely a serious and professional excellent artist."

Encountering Couplets Becomes a "Weapon" - Lin Xilei Wishes While Cai Kangyong Identifies Characters

Besides Cai Kangyong's impromptu entertainment, Lin Xilei didn't miss any opportunity to relax. After one group of shots, the sharp-eyed Lin Xilei discovered the couplets placed in the corner by the staff, so she discussed with Cai Kangyong taking some photos to celebrate the New Year. Indeed, the two immediately agreed, and under the photographer's direction, each held a couplet to wish everyone an early Happy New Year, praying for everyone to "sail smoothly and achieve everything they wish." Meanwhile, Lin Xilei also expressed her rabbit year wishes: "Hope my family is safe and happy, and hope my new movie 'Deadly Heartbeat' can be a big hit." On the side, Cai Kangyong took the opportunity to study the characters on the couplets, "'Hao' character is written excellently! I can't remember where I've seen something similar before." As he spoke, he murmured to the staff nearby, looking extremely adorable with his furrowed brows.
