Favorite literary works (Participating in the Feedsky- "Blog" Challenge)

by onehedge on 2007-10-23 20:32:25

The most interesting website I saw today, my favorite literary works (books/movies/music), show my treasures, my daydreams. The blogger just needs to choose one of the four major themes and write an article every day for a month.

Then I saw the introduction of "Blogger Divine Turtle", which might be designed by those friends who can't stick to writing the same theme every day. Starting from the day the competition begins, the organizer will come up with a theme for the blogger every day, and the blogger just needs to write a blog post around this theme, of course, also need to persist for a month.

In order to promote the event, Feedsky not only has many sponsors and collaborators, but also takes out a large sum of money to give gifts and prizes. ① As long as you stick to it for a month, you will get gifts worth 300 yuan, ② Recommend friends to participate in the competition and you will get gifts, ③ Every week there will be lucky blogs that win gifts, ③ Finally, according to popularity, three blogs will be selected for both the "Turtle and Hare Race" and the "Blogger Divine Turtle" categories, and Sony DV cameras, Nokia 5700 mobile phones, and Anna Sui cosmetic gift boxes will be awarded. ④ Finally, according to the judges' evaluation, two winners will receive an Apple iBook. I won't say more about the relevant marketing here. This is more or less an incentive for participants.
