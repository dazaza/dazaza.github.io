Australian customers visit Hongchang Chemical for examination and cooperation negotiation

by hongchang81 on 2012-03-08 15:01:41

Australian Business Guests Visit Hongchang Chemical Industry for Investigation and Cooperation Discussion

According to the Coating Network of HC360.com, recently, Alan, an Australian potential customer, specially visited Guangdong Hongchang Chemical Industry Co., Ltd. for on-site investigation. Ms. Liu Fang, Executive Director of the Decoration Paint Division of Hongchang Chemical Industry, warmly received the foreign guests. She led the foreign guests to visit the production workshop and R&D center of Hongchang Chemical Industry. Through the on-site visit of the company, Alan from Australia had a further understanding of Hongchang Chemical Industry's product R&D technology, corporate culture and future development prospects. During the visit, Alan was highly impressed by Hongchang Chemical Industry's scientific and technological innovation strength and enterprise scale.

Hongchang Chemical Industry is a high-tech private enterprise in the coating field. Whether it is in undertaking national and regional major science and technology projects, tackling key industrial technologies, or in the organic combination of industry-university-research, converting scientific research results into actual productivity, it occupies an important position in the industry and even nationwide. It is understood that at the end of 2000, Hongchang Chemical Industry cooperated with the Institute of Chemistry of the Chinese Academy of Sciences. The Institute of Chemistry provided comprehensive technical support for Hongchang Chemical Industry's scientific and technological innovation, carried out technological innovation in the coating field, especially made remarkable achievements in the research and development of architectural coatings. For example, in April 2002, among the 33 topics organized by the Ministry of Construction for key technology breakthroughs in building coatings, Hongchang Chemical Industry alone accounted for two of them. Its demonstrated scientific research strength earned praise from academicians and experts who hailed Hongchang Chemical Industry as "an important mainstay of the national team for scientific research in building coatings"; "Large-scale automatic mechanical coating polyurethane paint" and "Anti-contaminant super weather-resistant exterior latex paint" were listed in the 2002 "Key New Product Plan Project of Guangdong Province" and the 2002 "National Key New Product Plan Project" list. In recent years, Hongchang Chemical Industry has been committed to scientific and technological research and development in the field of architectural coatings, achieving remarkable results. Its products and brands have also gained widespread recognition in the market.

"When I was online, I found that your company exports wall latex paint to the United States, which immediately sparked my great interest. Later, I learned more about your company through the internet. I understand that your company was the first to pioneer the entry of Chinese coating products into the United States, we know that the United States is a country with extremely high environmental protection requirements. Your ability to open up the US market precisely demonstrates the strength of your company. This time I specially visited your company for inspection and investigation, hoping to discuss cooperation with your company," said Alan from Australia after the visit and investigation. "Your company owns three national-level technology research platforms including the National Postdoctoral Scientific Research Workstation, National Key High-Tech Enterprise, National 863 Program Experimental Base, and ten core patent technologies, which are very rare in China's coating industry. Therefore, I believe in and look forward to your company's future development prospects."

Ms. Liu Fang introduced in detail to Alan from Australia that after three years of arduous scientific research and development, five varieties of wall paint from Hongchang Chemical Industry passed the ASTM test of the U.S. Environmental Protection Agency in August 2005 and were finally approved to enter the U.S. market.

It is introduced that the American ASTM standard includes strict requirements for the environmental protection, product performance, and technical indicators of coating products. In order to overcome the environmental protection technology challenges, Hongchang Group, which owns a postdoctoral scientific research workstation and strong scientific research strength, specially gathered eight coating experts in 2002 to concentrate on solving the problem. In addition, Academician Wang Fusong, former standing vice president of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, and Academician Zhou Qifeng, current president of Peking University, both polymer chemistry experts, separately provided guidance to the project multiple times. Meanwhile, the Institute of Chemistry of the Chinese Academy of Sciences even provided national-level laboratories for some related key projects of Hongchang Chemical Industry. They eventually succeeded in the project. "The successful entry of Hongchang's coatings into the U.S. market marks another major breakthrough in our country's chemical industry and also represents significant progress in our country's environmental coating R&D field. So this good news is not only the good news of an enterprise but also the good news of China's chemical industry!" Academician Wang Fusong of the Chinese Academy of Sciences once commented like this when interviewed by the media.

After listening to the introduction, Alan from Australia also introduced some relevant situations of his own company and expressed: "The reason why your company can achieve such remarkable achievements is that it has grasped the core advantage of scientific and technological innovation, which is exactly what gives me confidence to specially visit your company for inspection and investigation. At the same time, through your detailed introduction and on-site inspection, I am more determined to cooperate with your company. I believe our cooperation will be pleasant."
