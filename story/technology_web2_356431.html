Seven) The background music most favored by large award ceremonies, known by everyone on Earth. Composed by Bernstein.

2. Hometown Scenery

Repeatedly quoted in "The Return of the Condor Heroes", this touching and sorrowful piece comes from composer Sojiro's 1991 album "Mudo".

3. CCTV Weather Forecast Theme

Reportedly the only background music that has never changed on CCTV, used consistently for the "Weather Forecast". This piece is called "Evening Fishing Boats", adapted and performed by electronic organist Pu Qizhang based on a traditional folk tune.

4. Simple Gifts

The background music for the VOA radio program (***), SPECIAL ENGLISH (Slow Music). Extremely familiar, though the radio version is faster.

5. Snow Dreams

From Bandari's album "Spring Field", this classic instrumental piece has been used countless times on radio and TV, often as background music for emotional (especially romantic, with a hint of melancholy) broadcasts.

6. Childhood Memory

From Bandari's album "Sun Coast", this piece indeed lives up to its name, evoking memories of the past and bringing tears to the eyes...

7. The Song Dynasty Sisters

Composed by Kitaro, this piece feels both grand and poignant, often used as background music in Hong Kong and Taiwan TV dramas, such as the version of "The Return of the Condor Heroes" starring Gu Tianle and Li Ruotong.

8. Your Smile

From Bandari's album "Fantasy Land", often used as background music for late-night radio programs. Compared to other emotional background music, this piece isn't overly sad or mournful but still touches the nerves.

9. One Day In Spring

From Bandari's album "Spring Field", often used at the beginning of programs or as opening background music for news and wake-up calls. A very soothing and beautiful classic piece.

10. Annie’s Wonderland

From Bandari's album "Fantasy Land", soft yet powerful, suitable for recitation accompaniment.

11. Phoenix Satellite Weather Forecast Theme

"This Orchid" from Yanni's album "If I Could Tell You", used as background music for weather forecasts on Phoenix Satellite.

12. Song From A Secret Garden

From the first album of Secret Garden, "Songs From A Secret Garden". It's said this song can kill with sadness, making one feel lost in a mysterious forest, dark and without an exit.

13. Windancer

From Secret Garden's album "White Stone", a melodious and smooth piece favored by bookstores with taste and Flash animations.

14. Chariots Of Fire

A very majestic and powerful song, which should be familiar to everyone.

15. Tears

A pure and beautiful piano piece, frequently heard, but unfortunately, I don't have further information about it.

Years later, if we meet again, how will I face you, with silence or with tears... In the sorrowful keys, I find myself consoled, my tears crystallizing into perfect cherry blossoms under the sunlight, even when they wither, there's still warmth. At that moment, I was truly afraid of having any resentment or darkness left in my heart, even a little bit would prevent me from encountering the beauty in the music.

16. Goodbye Pol.ice

Seems to be an Italian song, widely quoted in domestic films and TV series, most notably the background music "Goodbye Pol.ice" in "Infernal Affairs".

17. Adagio

"Adagio" is very soft and beautiful, just like its name suggests, it's a great work that can evoke deep feelings. Listening to it can't help but bring tears, showing its strong感染力infectivity. Some advertisements use it as background music, and some emotional programs on the radio also use it as background music. Moreover, this piece is also an excellent choice for recitation accompaniment (the music comes from the album "Songs From A Secret Garden").

18. Dawn of the Hero

A grand piece that appeared in the animated series "Romance of the Three Kingdoms" and in the 1995 version of "The Return of the Condor Heroes".

19. Burning Capital

Used in "New Legend of White Snake", especially during the scene where Xu Xian meets Bai Suzhen at Broken Bridge after the flooding of Jinshan Temple.

20. Romance De Amor

A must-play piece for guitarists, often appearing in romance and youth TV series.

21. Returning to Innocence

A classic track from Enigma's album "The Cross of Changes", used as background music in advertisements by Taichao Life Insurance on CCTV. It was also used in the Sydney Olympics.

22. Lone Wanderer

From "Eastern Evil Western Poison - Lone Wanderer", the background music for the mountain-splitting scene at the start of the movie, extremely desolate and majestic. When this music played during Zi Xia's death in "Journey to the West", it brought tears to my eyes.

23. Bandari - The Song Dynasty Sisters

A background music from the 1997 "Demigods and Half-Devils", a deeply cleansing and sorrowful piece, especially the latter half, which is moving.

24. Forrest Gump - Suite Forrest Gump

A warm symphonic piece from "Forrest Gump", deeply buried in our souls along with the movie. Whenever I hear this piece, I think of Forrest, who taught us through his hardships how to cherish everything before us and understand that adversity cannot break us; we should strive to find our own happiness.

25. Canon (Pachelbel's Canon)

In the movie "My Sassy Girl", when Jun Ji-hyun's piano sound rang out in the university lecture hall, Cha Tae-hyun couldn't help but cross the crowd and give her the long-prepared rose. The audience cheered and clapped, filling us with endless fantasies about love.

26. Irish Flute

I consider this a very classic piece, often used in many radio and TV programs. The Irish flute carries a light sadness, softly and gently swaying in the wind, stirring your heartstrings.

27. Idle Cloud Solitary Crane

As the name suggests, it creates a very ancient atmosphere. Without twists and turns, ups and downs, low returns, not hurried or anxious, the casual plucking lays a foundation of peace and tranquility in one's heart, which doesn't leave. In such music, there are endless imaginations, barren plains, and women playing the qin, with Xiaomi flowers falling from the sky, leading to flights of fancy.

28. True Love

This was a background music I searched for a long time, later found out it was from my favorite director Wong Kar-wai's work "Eastern Evil Western Poison". Their movies are made for people with delicate souls, perhaps off-topic. This piece is used as background music in radio stories and literature, touching the heartstrings and causing slight pain.

29. Autumn Whisper

Frequently used background music, like a gentle autumn breeze brushing your face, with a faintly sweet scent in the air. Richard Clayderman's music flows quietly, and you can hear the whispering of leaves and the sound of chrysanthemums blooming.

30. Hinata vs Neji

A very sad piece of music, filled with sorrow from start to finish. Often appears in programs as a melancholic background music.

31. Theme of Yesseniya - Eternal Memory

When listening to this piece, I always think of kites, wind, strings, and kite flyers. It's also commonly used as background music.

32. Background Music from "Journey to the West": "Long Journey to the West"

The final piece of music from "Journey to the West: Demon Subduing Monk's Wedding", when Sun Wukong possesses the warrior and kisses the girl on top of a high wall, is classic.

33. Ave Maria - Celine Dion, Franz Schubert Classical Christmas Song

A very gentle piece of music accompanied by a warm voice, softly speaking and telling something. Is it telling the story of the Virgin Mary? Or is it praying for something? Listening quietly, tears fall unknowingly, warm. Is it because of being moved? Or is it because of something else?

34. Classicriver (Gray Space)

Initially listened to the song Gray Space, then learned about the music Classicriver. A beautiful and melancholic melody, slowly rising, loneliness and solitude follow, life, love, friendship, money, and those painful memories are slowly awakened, like slowly rising smoke, lingering and winding.

35. 30 Minutes - Tatu

A clear voice, gradually pulling at your heartstrings, giving you an indescribable feeling, elusive and strange, like standing in the rain, watching the rain hit the ground, listening to the sound of the rain. A rather sentimental song, also commonly used as background music.

36. "Sorrow" from "A Chinese Ghost Story" Television Series

Soft plucking, a sorrowful melody, slightly painful tremors, a slow release of heartache, a light sadness. Deep grievances, intense emotions, outstanding, deeply captivating, willingly indulging in it, willingly so.

37. The Secret of Rain

About entanglement, about parting, about longing, about subtlety, about loneliness, about desire, it's your secret, but actually, it's also the secret of the rain. Delicate things have already seen through your mind, but you can't see through the heart of the rain, the endless drizzle. Thus, listening becomes the most important path.

38. I Believe - Piano Piece from "My Sassy Girl"

39. Coffee Fragrance Morning

Many of you should have heard this, it's quite classic too. Listening to such music feels like being in a secret garden surrounded by mountains, a river, green grass, the pervasive coffee fragrance, and the bird perched in front of the window. A beautiful life, a life everyone yearns for. Very beautiful, enjoying it fully in the world of music. "Achieving unintended meaning, doing nothing while achieving everything," such magic.

Tea is a good thing, naturally accompanied by good music, leisurely listening to tea, tasting tea.
