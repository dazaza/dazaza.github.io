Lingshan Yiyuan Lixing Hot Spring

by lingxi69 on 2011-12-29 15:09:31

Lingshan Yuan Yi Lixing Hot Spring Resort is an Asian-level leisure and health resort created by Yuan Yi Group with an investment of 300 million yuan. The hotel is located on the west side of the Lingshan Scenic Area in Wuxi Taihu National Resort, backed by Maji Mountain and overlooking 36,000 hectares of Taihu Lake scenery. It covers an area of 100 mu (about 16 acres), with a total construction area of 25,000 square meters. The first phase has a construction area of 15,000 square meters, and the second phase of the hotel has an area of 10,000 square meters. It owns various themed hot spring houses, 72 themed hot spring pools, and is managed by the well-known domestic professional management team, Lich Group.

The outdoor landscape area of the first phase of Lingshan Yuan Yi Lixing Hot Spring reaches 58,000 square meters, with 72 characteristic soaking pools and hot spring houses divided into six major features: Dynamic Hot Spring Area, Characteristic Hot Spring Area, Dense Forest Hot Spring Area, Business Lake View Hot Spring Area, Outdoor Swimming Pool, and Children's Water Play Area. The hot spring originates from deep within the earth's crustal fault zones, where underground thermal magma undergoes decades of evolution and infiltration before finally surfacing as Lingshan Yuan Yi Lixing Hot Spring. The bottom temperature of the hot spring well is 70°C, and the outlet temperature is 50°C. The pH of the spring water is 7.49. The minerals, trace elements, and special chemical components contained in the hot spring not only have therapeutic effects on various diseases but also offer health benefits, beauty care, skin care, and recuperation effects. Visitors are embraced by lush green forests while soaking in the pools, facing the vast Taihu Lake, naturally fostering a sense of harmony between humans and nature.

As winter approaches, soaking in hot springs becomes a popular choice for many people to relax during their "New Year's Day" holidays. For people in the Yangtze River Delta region, there are numerous places nearby to enjoy hot springs, such as Zui Hot Spring in Huangshan, Tangfeng in Wuyi, and Tangshan in Nanjing. However, due to high concentrations of visitors, some hot spring clubs have seen a decline in their experience reputation. Now, within this Yangtze River Delta "Hot Spring Circle," there is a new star member - Wuxi Lingshan Yuan Yi Lixing Hot Spring. In August, it hosted the contestants of the 61st Miss World China Final, where the beauties took swimsuit photo shoots.

The first phase of the club includes functional areas such as the largest scale Noble Ladies Club, Noble Gentleman Club, Lixpa, Health Preservation Hall, Imperial Foot Palace, Lifu Lijia, Sinology Hall, Yoga Hall, and premium luxury guest rooms in Asia; the second phase includes 135 luxurious specialty guest rooms and hotel ancillary facilities. Lingshan Yuan Yi Lixing Hot Spring integrates hot spring culture, Zen culture, health preservation culture, and leisure resort culture to create a harmonious atmosphere between humans and nature, reflecting its high-end positioning of "Ultimate Life, Lingshan Yuan Yi Lixing Hot Spring, Made in China."
