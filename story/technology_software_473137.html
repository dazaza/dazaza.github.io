Analysis and Repair of VW

by zmw26cx59 on 2011-08-09 13:08:24

Panasonic refrigerator, after a period of use, experiences a fault of continuous operation.

Fault Phenomenon: A Panasonic frost-free refrigerator, after being used for a while, experiences a fault of continuous operation without stopping.

Analysis and Maintenance: After checking the condition of the machine, the following was found:

(1) The refrigerator can cool, but the compressor runs continuously without stopping.

(2) When the auto-control knob is adjusted to weak cooling, it can stop automatically, but when adjusted to medium cooling, it does not stop automatically. (Beijing Panasonic Refrigerator Repair).

(3) By touching the condenser, the heat dissipation at the top and bottom is normal, but due to prolonged operation, the temperature of the compressor is relatively high (about 90°C).

(4) After carefully checking the temperature controller, no faults were found.

(5) Further inspection revealed that the air intake of the air vent is relatively weak, and the fan stops immediately when the door is opened.

(6) When the door is closed, the fan motor can be heard starting, but the speed is slow and weak.

(7) When placing your hand at the air outlet, there is a noticeable lack of wind force. Therefore, it was judged that the continuous operation of the refrigerator's self-control is related to the smaller air volume. During the repair, the refrigerator was gently moved, and the screws on the back cover of the upper fan were unscrewed and removed. It was found that the front shaft of the fan motor and the blades were covered with dirt. The fan motor was removed, and the blades and the front bearing of the motor were cleaned with a degreaser. Lubricating oil was dripped into the bearing clearance, and the blades were constantly rotated until there was no resistance before power could be applied for testing (the speed should reach 50 revolutions per second). After half an hour, the motor temperature did not exceed 40°C, and there were no abnormal phenomena. The fan motor was repositioned to check if the cooling was normal. At this point, the auto-control knob was adjusted to medium cooling, and if the auto-control was normal, it was then adjusted to the coldest and weakest cooling positions respectively for testing. After everything was normal, the temperature control knob was then adjusted to the required position according to the ambient temperature at the time. For double-door, dual-control frost-free refrigerators, when the ambient temperature is between 15~25°C, the upper and lower temperature controllers are generally adjusted to the weak cooling position; in the range of 25~30°C, they are adjusted to the medium cooling position; and above 30°C, they are adjusted to the coldest position. Frost-free refrigerator fans having weak airflow faults are easily misjudged as temperature controller failures or poor performance of the refrigeration system.

Data provided by Beijing Panasonic Refrigerator Repair Center.
