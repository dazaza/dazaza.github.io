Preparation of ingredients:

100 grams of leeks, 100 grams of red peppers, 2 eggs, 1 piece of white lan melon peel, 1 piece of Hami melon peel, 5 grams of salt.

Now let's start making it:

1. Beat the eggs, add a little salt and mix evenly; chop the leeks finely, preferably with a little minced ginger mixed evenly.

2. Heat the oil in the pan, pour part of the egg liquid, spread it into an omelette, then put it in the Hami melon peel.

3. Cut the red pepper into cubes, sprinkle with salt, squeeze out the water. Heat the oil in another pan, stir-fry the diced red pepper and minced leek, then evenly pour the remaining egg liquid over the bottom of the pan, spread it evenly, cook until done, then add the white lan melon peel and stir-fry. Eat the omelette with it, it tastes great!
