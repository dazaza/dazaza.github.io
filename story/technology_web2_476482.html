Web2.0Share Weekly: Add a little, Mutual Invitation Network, PiaoPiao Network, Here, etc.

by ybcz0519 on 2011-08-14 15:22:10

Web2.0Share Weekly is a featured column of Tianya Haige, where we carefully select the latest outstanding start-ups in China every week to share with you. Start-up companies can submit their information via Weibo: @web20share or email: watsonxu{at}web20share.com.

Add a bit more, get closer: Beijing/Shanghai/Guangzhou, an integrated mobile social networking app for making friends, location services, and entertainment. It discovers intersections between people in real life through algorithms. With continuous intersection index generation, you can find strangers who share similar life trajectories and interests, allowing you to connect with like-minded individuals in bustling cities whom you've never met before. Currently, it offers an iPhone client.

Link: http://www.jiayi.me/

LeWen: LeWen is the first video Q&A social network in China. However, from a personal perspective, I'm not particularly optimistic about video Q&A. In terms of Q&A needs, text can solve most requirements, and video is just one way to present answers. If this is used as the feature of Q&A, it might not be a good choice.

Link: http://hahask.com/

WhoYao Network - Precise, efficient, and instant event organizing platform: A core product under Beijing Ruilang Information Technology Co., Ltd., as a new type of network architecture and service platform, it aims to meet the entertainment and cultural life needs of users, enhancing their quality of life by integrating harmony, immediacy, and convenience into an efficient service website.

Link: http://www.whoyao.com/

Foncr - Free instant messaging service: Another instant messaging application on mobile devices. Is there still potential in this direction domestically?

Link: http://www.foncr.com/

I Offer Price - Super low-price hotel booking: "I Offer Price" uses the "I Offer Booking Model." Under this service model, members may receive larger price discounts, with the highest possible discount being 40% or even more below the normal other online public selling prices.

Link: http://www.wokaijia.com/

Gujie Network: The world's first social search platform: This positioning is quite bold!! Through it, you can find information and interest circles. By searching, you can interact socially with users who share common interests and hobbies, and also get more genuine, timely, and humanized opinions and suggestions from netizens and friends regarding certain individuals, products, or news. This site seems to have been around for quite some time, but it appears to be undergoing a transformation recently.

Brief Review: The concept of search + social is good, but achieving success isn't easy because it resembles what many platforms are trying to do!

Link: http://2011.goojje.com/

KuKid Fun Shopping - Making shopping and socializing more fun!: A location-based fashion shopping and social sharing mobile application that discovers trendy topics and fashion trends. Pick up your iPhone and GPhone, go shopping and socialize with friends, and become the King and Queen of fashion! Coming soon.

Link: http://www.kukid.net/

Weijing: Based on user behavior learning and semantic analysis, it helps users find the most important information content in the Weibo information flow through data mining and intelligent filtering, reorganizes and sorts it for convenient reading and sharing, improving the quality of user reading while saving valuable time. The commercial value behind this application lies in future precise marketing!

Link: http://www.51weijing.com/

PiaoPiao Network - Discuss beauty and fashion with trusted people: A social Q&A website focused on beauty and fashion themes, composed of a professional thematic term system, providing trustworthy, high-quality, and practical content. You can: follow different thematic terms based on your interests, easily obtain content you like, conveniently share and communicate; follow people you're interested in, learn and share makeup tips.

Link: http://www.skinpp.com/

Tencent launches LBS service QQ Travel Around: (Accessible via lbs.qq.com, mobile-friendly) QQ Travel Around is a mobile software service that allows you to check surroundings and leave messages anytime and anywhere. In the future, it will introduce task systems, merchant discounts, etc. Currently, the iPhone client is already downloadable.

Link: http://itunes.apple.com/us/app/id449522077?mt=8

Buying and selling treasures around you - Baibu Tao: Conveniently buy and sell idle items via your phone. The mobile client of Baixing.com seems to have similar functions!

Link: http://baibutao.com/

MoGuo: Meet strangers around you: MoGuo is a location-based mobile social application for strangers. Open it to see photos and background information of every MoGuo user around you, quickly find common topics, and instantly interact through your phone. However, WeChat introduced a similar function yesterday, so if there isn't significant differentiation, such applications may face great challenges...

Link: http://moguo.cc/

Here: Discover potential connections around you. Main functions include exchanging electronic business cards, discovering connections nearby, maintaining relationships, etc.

Link: http://zaizher.im/
