Man was beaten to残for having an appointment with the wife of the black society leader

by yanq412exue on 2012-03-07 19:40:06

All 7 suspects of the criminal gang have been arrested. According to the Xian Evening Post, the husband suspected his wife of having an affair and immediately gathered his brothers to teach the other person a lesson, resulting in the other person's left leg being disabled. Yesterday, the Yanta police reported that this criminal gang has been successfully dismantled and all 7 suspects have been arrested.

The male netizen was slashed when meeting a female netizen.

"Something's wrong, there's a man lying on the ground not moving, with a large pool of blood." At around 1:00 AM on October 9th, a member of the public reported to the Changyanbao police station of the Xian Public Security Bureau. The police arrived at the scene and saw a man lying in a pool of blood at the entrance of a parking lot on Dongyi Road, not moving. The police immediately called 120 to urgently send him to the hospital. After a preliminary diagnosis by medical personnel, the man's left leg artery was cut, and due to serious injuries, he had no consciousness, no breathing, and no heartbeat. The situation was critical, so medical staff immediately began emergency treatment.

The Yanta police formed a special task force to visit the crime scene and retrieved surveillance footage from nearby areas, but found no information useful for solving the case. The police found an ID card, a mobile phone, and 2000 yuan in cash on the victim. The ID showed that the injured person surnamed Xu, 35 years old, from Zichang County. After a preliminary investigation, the police ruled out robbery as the cause of the injury.

At around 4:00 AM that day, after emergency treatment by medical staff, Xu regained consciousness. The police learned that Xu met a female netizen online and they had an ambiguous relationship. That day, Xu specially came from northern Shaanxi to Xi'an to meet the female netizen and stayed temporarily in a hotel in Xi'an. Two hours before the incident, the female netizen Wang asked him to meet her in the southern suburbs. Unexpectedly, Xu was attacked by a group of strangers without seeing the female netizen, and his left leg was slashed.

The black society leader set up a scheme to punish the "rival".

According to the information obtained by the police, this female netizen Wang had significant suspicion of committing the crime. After more than two months of police investigation and visits, the police finally discovered the whereabouts of the female netizen Wang. On December 13th, the police captured Wang in the northern suburbs. Investigations revealed that Wang is from Yan'an, 29 years old. Wang often spent time online at home due to boredom. During this period, she met the victim Xu online and their relationship became ambiguous.

In early October, Wang's husband Li discovered his wife's ambiguous relationship with Xu and immediately became furious, deciding immediately to punish Xu. Li immediately found Zhao, Chen, and others to prepare for retaliation, then he let his wife Wang invite Xu out. At around midnight on October 9th, Xu unknowingly arrived at the entrance of a parking lot on Dongyi Road in the southern suburbs according to his agreement with the female netizen Wang. Suddenly, five men with knives rushed out from the surroundings, saying nothing and started punching and kicking him. One of the men stabbed his left thigh, and then the five men scattered and fled.

The criminal gang was dismantled.

After Wang was captured, by yesterday, the police had arrested her husband Li, Zhao, Chen, Yao, Shi, and six others. In further investigations, the police discovered that Wang's husband Li had a group of brothers under him who frequently collected debts for others, forcibly took over projects, acted as underground police squads, and occasionally undertook small projects. Li was the "boss" among them. This group was a crazy criminal gang. On the day of the incident, all five suspects who stabbed and disabled Xu were Li's subordinates. That night, in order to thank the five suspects who carried out the stabbing, Li gave each participant 500 yuan in cash and a pack of Furongwang cigarettes as a reward.

The police introduced that the criminal gang led by Li had, between September and November this year, multiple times acted as an underground police squad and used illegal means such as collecting debts for others and participating in forcibly taking over projects to make profits. Among them, Yao and Shi were previously dealt with by the Gaoxin police for acting as an underground police squad and being suspected of provoking trouble. This criminal gang is also the second criminal gang dismantled by the Xian police since the anti-gang action began. Currently, the seven suspects are criminally detained on suspicion of intentional injury, and the case is still under further review.

By reporter Wang Tao
