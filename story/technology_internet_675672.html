Young couples show affection in the restaurant living room after drinking

by xue94fwsh on 2012-02-25 11:31:52

On December 26, a man drove under the influence and caused a car accident. The vehicle caught fire, resulting in the death of four people across three generations of a family. Under the mediation of the police officers at Xiajie Police Station, the three parties involved reached an agreement where Guo Da would compensate Wang Wei's family with 600 yuan as "incense money."

In the locals' view, outsiders engaging in sexual activities at one's home is considered a disruption of feng shui. After getting dressed, Wang Wei wouldn't let them leave and demanded an explanation from the two individuals. In a state of panic, Guo Da disregarded Wang Wei's obstruction, went to the second floor of the house, and jumped out of the window to escape, injuring his foot in the process.

Sun Wenjie, Feng Wei, Peng Yunhua

"Good news doesn't travel far, but bad news spreads quickly." Xiajie Township was not large, and just over ten minutes later, a crowd of onlookers had gathered at Wang Wei's house, along with the families of Guo Da and Yang Hui who had come to demand an explanation.

The two young individuals were Guo Da (a pseudonym), aged 16, from Xiajie Township, and his 19-year-old girlfriend, Yang Hui (also a pseudonym). It turned out that this couple had been drinking at a restaurant, and under the influence of alcohol, both became somewhat impulsive. The restaurant was converted from the owner's home, and seeing that the living room of the owner's house was empty, they engaged in inappropriate behavior there.

The incident occurred on the evening of December 25th, during a trip to Hubei Province. Around 9 PM, after Wang Wei had finished attending to the guests in his store, he heard strange noises coming from the adjacent living room. Curious, Wang Wei opened the door and was shocked by what he saw: a naked couple on the sofa in his living room being intimate... Upon closer inspection, Wang Wei realized that these two were not members of his own family, but rather the young couple who had been drinking at his place earlier that evening.

When they noticed Wang Wei's sudden appearance, Guo Da and Yang Hui sobered up a bit from their drunken state and hastily got dressed.

Around 10 PM, after receiving the alarm, the police station immediately rushed to the scene for investigation and carried out persuasion and education work. After more than two hours of mediation, the three parties agreed to meet at the police station for further mediation on the 26th.

These days, Wang Wei, a villager running a restaurant in Xiajie Township of Lincang, has been feeling uneasy and angry due to an embarrassing situation he encountered.

Villagers had differing opinions and discussions were widespread. Wang Wei insisted that Guo Da and Yang Hui provide an explanation before leaving; Guo Da’s family admitted their prior mistakes but argued that the children were young and didn’t know better, willing to take responsibility, yet due to Wang Wei's improper handling which led to Guo falling and getting injured, Wang's family should bear the costs for Guo's medical examination and treatment; Yang Hui's family believed that both Guo Da and Wang Wei damaged their family reputation and demanded compensation for reputational damages...
