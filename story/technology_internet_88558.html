I heard that there is a new game called "Smiling Proudly in the Three Kingdoms". Have you played it?

by ufo on 2008-09-01 17:40:33

1. Registration: Please visit the official website of Laughing傲at Three Kingdoms (http://sanguo.game.com.cn/) to register for a One-Card account and activate the game. The registration process involves filling out basic information such as account name, login password, ID number, email address, and selecting game-related data including city name, ruler's name, ruler's avatar, and national flag.

2. Login: After registering and activating the game, please go to the Laughing傲at Three Kingdoms game website (sanguo.game.com.cn), click on the "Game Login" option, select the server, and enter the game.

3. Character Creation: When logging in for the first time, pay attention to the prompt in the top left corner of the page that says "Please click to set your ruler's ability values." After clicking, you will see that the ruler's strength, intelligence, and political abilities are all initially set at 50 points, with 70 additional points available for free allocation. Players can allocate these points according to their preferences and needs.

4. Recruitment: Recruitment is the option for hiring generals. Almost all famous figures from the Three Kingdoms era and special hidden generals unique to Laughing傲at Three Kingdoms can be purchased here. When recruiting, pay attention to the general's strength, intelligence, and political abilities, and choose carefully based on your needs. PS: There are two ways to recruit generals. Purchasing generals through the "Recruitment" option requires the use of Yuanbao bought with RMB, with prices determined by the general's ability values. Besides this, you can also use the "Search" and "Visit" options to recruit generals. This method may uncover special generals not included in the "Recruitment" option. After a successful search, use the "Visit" option to increase goodwill until it reaches 100%, at which point the general can be successfully recruited. Each visit increases goodwill by 10-30 points depending on the intelligence value of the general responsible for the visit.

5. City Development:

a. Click on your city on the page to display detailed information about the city on the right side. Here, you can upgrade the levels of agriculture, commerce, and defense. Upgrading agriculture increases maximum grain storage, upgrading commerce increases maximum silver storage, and upgrading defense increases maximum defense value. Upgrade times depend on the LV value.

b. Both the internal affairs and military sectors allow for city development. In the internal affairs sector, the "Patrol" option can increase civilian loyalty and population limit, requiring political ability from the general executing the task, with each task taking 5 minutes; the "Cultivation" option can increase hourly grain production, requiring political ability from the general executing the task, with each task taking 5 minutes; the "Commerce" option can increase hourly silver income, requiring intelligence from the general executing the task, with each task taking 5 minutes; the "Population" option can increase hourly population growth speed, requiring intelligence from the general executing the task, with each task taking 5 minutes; the "Market" option allows for the exchange of grain and silver within the city, with an exchange rate of 100:80. In the military sector, the "Recruit" option can increase city troops without needing a task general or ability requirements, with time depending on the number of recruits; the "Training" option can increase army morale, requiring strength from the general executing the task, with each task taking 5 minutes; the "Defense" option can increase city defense values, requiring strength from the general executing the task, with each task taking 5 minutes; the "Transportation" option can transport grain and silver from the city to other cities in the same country, with transportation time depending on the distance between cities.

6. Appointment and Removal: This includes appointing a governor and appointing a strategist. The type of general appointed as governor will determine the attack and defense types of the city, and the appointed governor cannot participate in combat missions. The strategist appointed will have his intelligence value increased by 10 points free of charge.

7. Search: The "Search" option allows for searching cities to find treasures, grain, money, and talent, increasing the city's grain or silver reserves or increasing the output of grain or silver. It may also uncover special generals not included in the "Recruitment" option and unique Three Kingdoms storyline tasks specific to Laughing傲at Three Kingdoms.
