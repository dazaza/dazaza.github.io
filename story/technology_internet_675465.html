Truck driver backs up and crashes into a sedan to take another look at toll collector

by anonymous on 2012-02-25 09:31:27

Our reporter (Li Hongxun) - In order to get a few more looks at the pretty toll collector at the highway toll station, a large truck driver actually reversed his vehicle to take a better look. Unfortunately, he crashed into a BMW car that was waiting in line behind him, causing direct economic losses amounting to several ten-thousand yuan. Yesterday, this incident occurred at the Gongyi toll station on the Lian-Ho Expressway. Upon realizing he would have to compensate the BMW owner for a loss of several ten-thousand yuan, the driver Zhang regretted his actions immensely.

Yesterday morning, while officers Huang Wei and Bai Jianfeng from the Gongyi Highway Police Team were on duty at the Gongyi East Station on the Lian-Ho Expressway, they noticed that the driver of a semi-trailer truck, Zhang, kept staring at a female toll collector. After paying the toll, the driver suddenly reversed his truck. Before he had gone far, there was a "bang" as he collided with a BMW car that was waiting in line behind him to pay the toll. The truck driver Zhang quickly stopped his vehicle to check the situation and realized that reversing had caused a major problem. The BMW owner, seeing his less-than-a-month-old new car damaged, was so heartbroken that he burst into tears. At this point, Zhang continuously apologized to the BMW owner, showing obvious regret on his face.

The police officers quickly approached and asked Zhang about what had happened. Zhang explained, "I was worried that the weighing information might be wrong, so I reversed to check." When asked why he kept looking at the toll collector, Zhang's face turned red. He admitted that it was simply because he wanted to steal a few more glances at the pretty toll collector.

According to the regulations of the Road Traffic Safety Law Implementation Rules: "When reversing a motor vehicle, one should observe the conditions behind the vehicle and reverse only after confirming safety," the truck driver Zhang must bear full responsibility for the accident, compensating the BMW owner for tens of thousands of yuan in vehicle damage. Additionally, he was fined 100 yuan according to the law.
