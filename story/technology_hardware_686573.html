The meaning of spring is the spiritual revival, and the emotions that have been pent up all winter begin to be released. Many people like the romantic scene of admiring hundreds of flowers in bloom in spring; many others enjoy looking for the gentleness of willow branches; but I like the freshest colors of spring. These colors include not only the brilliance of flowers and the greenness of willows, but also the fresh and delicious taste of fruits.

In early spring, when it's still chilly despite the warmth, the soil begins to emit a lazy fragrance. Most fruits have yet to hang on the branches, but strawberries are the main attraction of this season. This red and adorable fruit awaits your visit at the fields: Charlie, Zhang Ji, Tian Huang Yu Yong, and Hong Yan, these beautiful names allure both your eyes and stomach. There’s no other fruit as visually appealing and sweet. Come, let's meet the most beautiful strawberries together.

Tianrun Garden

The three-dimensional cultivation of strawberries actually means lifting the strawberries off the flat ground and planting them on adjustable height platforms. The taste remains unchanged, but it looks more straightforward and easier to pick. Walking into the greenhouse, several parallel placed adjustable height racks are quite spectacular. Large strawberries hang down, with lush strawberry branches, making it more eye-catching than those planted in the ground. The owner of the garden says that the strawberry garden here is a garden-style picking experience, providing visitors with the joy of picking while achieving an aesthetically pleasing visual effect. Moreover, the garden has considered the needs of disabled visitors and arranged barrier-free channels specifically for them, showing thoughtful consideration.

When picking strawberries at Tianrun Garden, you can eat the freshly picked strawberries directly without any problem. The strawberries in the greenhouse are grown using organic fertilizers, free from any pollution. If you don't mind the slight dust, you can taste the strawberries' freshness right away.

From early December to the end of May the next year, it is the time for strawberry picking. Currently, Tianrun Garden mainly focuses on strawberry cultivation, with varieties such as Zhang Ji and Hong Yan. More than 70 greenhouses can accommodate 2000 people picking simultaneously. By April, when the weather warms up, the restaurant in the garden will make fresh strawberry juice from the newly picked strawberries. A sip of freshly squeezed juice feels incredibly refreshing, and it only costs 5 yuan per cup, much cheaper and tastier compared to street-side beverage shops.

Recommendation Reason:

A garden-style picking experience brings a unique sensory enjoyment, and the three-dimensional cultivation is something you've never heard of before. The adjustable cultivation platform allows you to get closer to the strawberries.

Address: East side of Xingshou Town Government, Changping District

Driving Route: From Daliu Tree Roundabout on Litang Road, turn right onto Ansi Road to Xingshou South Entrance, then go straight to Xingshou Town Government.
