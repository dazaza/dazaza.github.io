At noon, I downloaded and listened to an album named "Tango Lyrico: The Fantastic Tango", and it felt extremely comfortable. All tracks on the album are 320K bitrate, so the loss of audio quality is minimal. The rich saxophone combined with the clear harp makes one feel extremely peaceful and content after listening. Therefore, I recommend it for everyone to enjoy. There will be a download link at the end of the article.

Let's listen to the second track of the album: 02. El Choclo 03:20

Chinese Name: Saxophone and Harp

Version: SACD

Release Time: 2002

Album Artist: Mulo Francel & Tango Lyrico

Region: Hong Kong

This album was discovered by the audiophile circle in Hong Kong and has recently become very popular there. Among familiar albums to general audiophiles, there aren't many that feature duets between saxophone and harp. Renowned saxophonist Mulo Francel teams up with harpist Evelyn Huber to interpret several enchanting melodies. The vivid saxophone playing is accompanied by the crisp and delicate sound of the harp. In CASAR, the opening piece, the lively tune dances through every music cell you have. Following that, EL CHOCLO offers delightful tunes and a brisk rhythm, exuding the passion of tango. A romantic and captivating rendition of Iwans Lied adapted for the harp surely brings a fresh surprise.

The album uses SACD technology, and just the excellent recording quality alone is enough to recommend it to music lovers. With its cheerful and pleasant melodies, the rare combination of saxophone and harp, high-level performances full of harmony, this album deserves recommendation.
