Will you be my angel?

by uins9126 on 2010-04-19 13:52:14

"Will you be my angel?" the boy said to the girl.

"Can I? You're so good, am I worthy enough?" The girl lowered her proud head.

"You are worthy. Your heart is so beautiful, you're the most suitable!" The boy lifted the girl's head and pressed his lips down...

Year after year, the boy's farm gained harvests time and again, the girl repeatedly sought hope for him, containing the boy with her love.

The boy became successful, starting from coming home late and leaving early, then eventually sleeping at the company. The girl cared about his health, bought a cabinet full of food books, and cleverly prepared various dishes delivered to his office, feeding him bite by bite. Sometimes, when the boy was too tired to even chew, the girl would use her own mouth to transfer the food into his. The boy silently vowed to himself that he would definitely treat the girl well in the future...

Ten years passed in the days of poverty but happiness. The girl gave birth to a lovely daughter for the boy, and the company grew from a small enterprise to one with over ten thousand employees. The boy turned into a man, and the girl became a woman. Farming became busier, life less regular. The boy handed all his money to the girl without keeping a cent, bought N villas for her, hired the best nannies to take care of her. He felt that what he did was the best way to thank the girl. But the girl didn't stop cooking meals for the boy. Although the nannies could cook much better than she could, she still cooked every day and sent the food to the chairman's office for him to eat.

The boy changed from being grateful to becoming impatient and resistant. In his view, now that they had a good life, the girl should enjoy it as his wife rather than doing a nanny's job. The first major argument since their marriage occurred:

"Can you please stop bringing food?"

"No, this has become my habit!" The girl insisted.

"Now we have everything. I want you to be my real angel, protected by me, made happy by me. You don't need to work for me anymore," the boy said softly, his eyes as sincere as ever!

The girl lowered her head silently, protecting her habit. She turned around and slammed the door. Outside, tears streamed down her face soundlessly... Her heart hurt, but she didn't tell the boy that some habits couldn't be changed forever...

In the bar, the boy drank alone, reminiscing about the past, thinking about the girl's figure coming and going in the rain. He thought about how he was already a chairman and no longer needed her to do such humble things to take care of him. His mind struggled between returning home or not.

A fashionably dressed beautiful woman stood next to the boy, flirtatiously crying on his shoulder, ambiguously exhaling towards him. The boy looked up and saw a beautiful and radiant face. Somehow, he felt an urge to vomit. He threw the money for his drink, crazily ran out of the bar.

Standing outside his door, the boy finally realized how deeply he loved his angel, loved to the point that he automatically guarded against any other woman. He faintly heard the girl soothing the child singing inside. This peaceful atmosphere made the boy sigh gently, feeling as if he returned to the small office where he used to feed the girl bite by bite. His eyes moistened, and he sat down in the small garden behind the door. On this cold summer night, he made an important decision...

The next day, when the girl opened the door, she found the boy sleeping in the garden, sleeping soundly like a child who had done something right. The girl happily dismissed any wild thoughts from the previous night, helped the boy inside, and thus the big storm stopped. They no longer mentioned the unhappiness from last night. The girl continued loving the boy in her own way, while the boy said nothing...

The boy quietly sold half of his shares to others, and he also changed from being a chairman to an investor. The boy took off his expensive suit and quietly bought several sets of casual clothes for himself. Then, he silently dismissed the nannies. All these things were done quietly...

The boy's birthday arrived, another grand occasion. The boy told the girl to dress beautifully to make him look good. The girl secretly smiled, mischievously winking at the boy, putting on the dress he had prepared for her, attending the gathering. For the girl, she was the most beautiful tonight.

When she arrived, the girl discovered that there wasn't a single person in the small banquet venue. When she was about to turn around, her daughter appeared from the corner, wearing a mother's dress and holding a bouquet of yellow tulips for the girl.

"Mom, please accept Dad's tulips," the daughter's childish voice made the girl confusedly accept the flowers. The boy dressed in formal attire walked towards her, unchanged after ten years, at least in the girl's eyes.

"My angel, please forgive me for neglecting your feelings these past few years. People forget that money cannot compensate for the lack of love. Now, please let me be your prince," the boy knelt down before the girl, held her hand, and kissed it deeply.

The girl's eyes moistened, moved to only tightly embrace the boy, feeling like an 18-year-old girl again, happier than ever. No matter how much money there was, she had never been so happy. What she cared about was only the boy...

That night, the lights in their home didn't go out, and they remained lit forever...
