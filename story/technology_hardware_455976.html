moviesoon.com has already reported that the movie promotional website "SectorSeven" for "Transformers" provided a free download of a reinterpreted animated version of the opening theme of "Transformers" as a rock theme. Since the official source did not reveal which band created this theme song, it led to various speculations among fans. Currently, Linkin Park, who recently released a new album "Minutes to Midnight," is definitely a top choice among Transformer fans. However, according to the information we received, the actual band that contributed the theme song for "Transformers" is a less well-known independent band.

This highly sought-after band behind the scenes is black lab from Los Angeles. They have previously released several successful pop-rock albums, and the new album "technologie", which includes "transformers theme," marks the band's first attempt at electronic rock style. Guitarist Andy Ellis himself is also a super fan of Transformers, so they created this quite explosive rock version of the "Transformers" theme based on the animated version of the opening theme. In addition, they also used materials from the "Transformers" trailer to create a fan-made music video.
