China Agriculture Business Network Officially Goes Live - Announcement

by cnagr on 2009-12-05 09:34:44

China Agriculture Business Network (http://www.cnagr.com.cn) was established in December 2009. It is a comprehensive platform for providing rural internet information services, integrating agricultural information, investment consulting, business planning, transaction services, and user interaction related to agriculture.

China Agriculture Business Network is committed to the service tenet of "supporting agriculture, revitalizing agriculture, enriching farmers, and innovating agriculture." It has launched various distinctive channels such as news, market conditions, technology, cooperatives, supply and demand, forums, etc., with over a hundred subcategories. It updates more than 5,000 pieces of information daily. It basically covers all relevant groups, including ordinary farmers, large-scale farming and breeding operators, farmer brokers, agricultural dealers, farmers' cooperative economic organizations, and agricultural enterprises. With professional and abundant segmented agricultural product information, it has attracted widespread attention from farmers and migrant workers with certain investment capabilities.

China Agriculture Business Network boasts strong financial resources and a professional operational team, complete hardware facilities, and powerful technical strength. Through the team's relentless efforts and the extensive support of users, within half a year of its launch, China Agriculture Business Network ranked steadily at the forefront among vertical portals in rural areas. The increasing number of new users further reinforces its position as China's leading rural interactive media.

As a leading comprehensive provider of rural internet information services in China, China Agriculture Business Network has established close cooperative relationships with several domestic authoritative media outlets. It became the special network media for the Fourth Inner Mongolia Agricultural Expo and the chief co-organizer network media for the First Zhejiang Agricultural Foreign Investment Expo. It has numerous traditional agricultural media strategic partners such as "Rural Hundred Affairs Express," "Southern Rural Newspaper," and "Common People Wealth Creation Guide."

From the core perspective of the "three rurals" issues, rural areas are the environment and background for the development of agriculture and farmers, and also the convergence point of the "three rurals" issues as well as the geographical concept representation. China Agriculture Business Network differs from other agricultural media mainly in its focus on the development mode of agriculture and its competitiveness. What we strive to do is to view the development of various aspects of rural areas (such as informatization of agricultural product production, processing, and sales, rural informatization, rural ecological tourism, modern urban agriculture) as a whole. We sincerely hope that more partners will join us in jointly enhancing the overall competitiveness of rural areas, promoting the prosperity of the rural economy, and ensuring social harmony and stability!
