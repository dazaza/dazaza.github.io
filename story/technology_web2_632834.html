Futian LeiSa triggers the integrated effect of pumping machinery

by ftleisa on 2011-12-19 10:09:28

At the end of the year, reviewing the concrete machinery market in 2011, despite being severely impacted by unfavorable factors such as a tight financial credit policy, real estate regulation, and high oil prices, it still maintained high-speed growth. When summarizing the standout events of the year, the most eye-catching was undoubtedly the joint venture between Foton Motor, the world's largest commercial vehicle producer, and Daimler, the world-renowned car manufacturer. Foton Raizon launched China's first National IV 52-meter pump truck product; simultaneously, Foton Raizon led the industry in releasing an "Integrated Overall Solution," providing customers with a full set of concrete machinery equipment covering "vehicles, pumps, and stations" as well as integrated financial, training, maintenance, and after-sales services to solve all customer problems at once, further sparking deep discussions within the industry about the value of pumping machinery.

The connotation of integration nurtures excellence. How could a new brand be so well-regarded and attract significant attention in its early stages? Industry experts commented that it "stands on the shoulders of giants." Backed by Foton Motor, the world's largest commercial vehicle enterprise, Foton Raizon shares the group's powerful R&D, production, and service resources, exploring an integrated product strategy that fully leverages its own advantages to achieve value leadership. Through this, Foton Raizon developed a series of products, establishing a professional, efficient, and reliable product image, achieving significant breakthroughs in the market quickly.

Integration includes integrated R&D, integrated manufacturing, and integrated services, covering different stages of value injection for products. As is well known, in the current concrete machinery market, the chassis and superstructure R&D and manufacturing of most products are conducted separately, making it impossible for their technical parameters to be shared synchronously. This often leads to matching errors among various components, affecting the reliability and performance of the entire vehicle. However, the product chassis and superstructure of Foton Raizon are completed during the process of integrated R&D and production.

Integrated R&D ensures perfect design of product performance, while integrated manufacturing is the necessary guarantee for presenting the design. The integrated production process of Foton Raizon's chassis and superstructure achieves seamless connection of the entire vehicle and optimal matching of the whole vehicle's performance. In the production phase, Toyota's TPS lean production method is especially introduced to ensure that the product quality reaches the leading level in the same industry. It is reported that both the chassis and superstructure of Foton Raizon come from Foton's world-class digital factory. Advanced equipment in the digital factory, such as computer-controlled automatic submerged arc welding, roller track overall processing technology, CNC underwater plasma machines, large CNC linked bending machines, etc., also guarantees the accuracy and quality of the products from a hardware perspective.

Integrated R&D and production have realized high configuration, high craftsmanship, and high matching of Raizon products, satisfying users' value demands for product performance and quality. Integrated services extend the value connotation to a new height. From vehicle financing to vehicle insurance and parts financing, Raizon provides one-stop financial solutions, offering multiple modes such as bank mortgages, installment payments, leasing, etc., flexible financial solutions meeting various user needs. Meanwhile, rooted in Foton's integrated advantage, Foton Raizon is one of the few domestic enterprises capable of realizing integrated service for chassis and superstructure. A nationwide network of 1100 integrated service stations for chassis and superstructure truly enables users to quickly resolve any issues, whether originating from the chassis or the superstructure, by contacting just one service station.

Not long ago, Foton Raizon also launched the "43210 Service Commitment," allowing Foton Raizon users to enjoy the content of Foton Automobile's "Worry-Free All the Way" service brand, including "three comprehensive services, four constructions, five worry-frees, six unifications," while simultaneously experiencing unique one-stop services, 0-time difference service support, and a series of added-value contents from Foton Raizon, creating service standards in China's engineering machinery field comparable to those in Europe and America.

In modern enterprise construction, strategy is actually a form of competitiveness; strategic leadership ensures that enterprises seize the initiative in competition. Foton Raizon's integrated strategy fundamentally helps it realize competitive advantages in cost, technology, and the entire value system.

In the pumping machinery industry, due to the high requirements for equipment reliability, imported chassis and components were previously used, resulting in high prices and usage costs, which became a heavy burden for customers. Foton Raizon, through its integrated strategy, equipped vehicles with domestically produced high-end Ouman chassis recognized by the market. In key components such as hydraulic systems, electrical systems, mixing systems, and boom systems, it uses imported high-configuration products, successfully achieving high quality and high configuration at reasonable prices, helping users realize their ideal pumping machinery sooner.

Foton Raizon's integrated strategy represents an advanced form of R&D and production organization. Currently, it encompasses Foton's headquarters research institute, two major R&D centers in Germany and Japan, and supporting facilities across Europe and America. In the future, with close cooperation among various R&D centers, the integrated advantage will give birth to more scientific and technological achievements, highlighting Raizon's technological superiority.

Rooted in the integrated advantage, deeply exploring customer value demands, Foton Raizon has launched an "Integrated Overall Solution," which, based on product performance and the actual situation of customers, not only provides a complete set of equipment including mixing stations, mixing trucks, and pump trucks but also offers integrated after-sales services, maintenance, use training, finance, and other series of services. With the best full-system matching solution and the optimal cost-performance ratio of a complete set of equipment, it improves customers' selection efficiency, solving all customer problems at once and comprehensively upgrading the customer value experience.

Relying on the technological, quality, and service advantages created by integrated R&D, integrated manufacturing, and integrated services, Raizon is comprehensively enhancing its brand height. In 2011, Foton Raizon's mixer truck sales grew by 47%, ranking third in the industry, and pump truck sales increased by 91%, entering the top four in the market, writing a new legend for Foton Automobile in the pumping machinery field. It is understood that as one of the important pillars for Foton Automobile to achieve its 2020 international strategy, the next stage will see Foton Raizon leading domestic brands to actively enter the global pumping machinery market, advancing towards becoming a world-class high-end equipment manufacturing enterprise.
