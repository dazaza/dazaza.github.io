Couple Jokes Chapter One

by tstsfywis on 2010-05-26 11:37:59

Yearning for His Wife

Husband: "Dear, I've been missing you so much since you went on this business trip that I'm almost going crazy."

Wife: "Look at you, I've only been gone for four days."

Husband: "But for the whole four days, I couldn't find the key to the money box."

Start and End

Dorn met his wedding pastor on the street.

Dorn: "A few months ago, when my wife and I got married, you proclaimed in the name of God that all troubles between the bridegroom and the bride would come to an end, right? But now I am so troubled!"

Pastor: "Yes, I did say that. Troubles have a start and an end, but at that time, I didn't specify whether your troubles were at the beginning or the end."

There's Also an Uncle

"Oh, if life continues like this, I really feel ashamed." The wife complained to her husband, "Look, letting mom pay our rent, aunt buying us clothes, sister buying us food, living like this makes me uncomfortable and ashamed..."

"Humph, you should feel ashamed," the husband said discontentedly, "you have two uncles, yet they haven't given us anything..."

Clever Metaphor

The wife complained to her husband: "Before, you used to send me a bouquet of roses every day. Why don't you even send me one now?"

The husband said: "I ask you, does a fisherman continue feeding the bait after catching the fish?"

Moonlit Proposal

Wife: "Some people say young people lose their minds under the enchanting moonlight. Do you think it's true?"

Husband: "It might have some truth to it. Don't you remember? I proposed to you under the moonlight."

No Need to Spend Too Much

Female customer: "I want to buy something for my husband. What do you think is most suitable for men?"

Female clerk: "Madam, how many years have you been married?"

Female customer: "About ten or more years."

Female clerk: "Then please go to the discount section downstairs."

Walking

An elderly couple over seventy still had strong bodies and loved each other very much. On the day of their fiftieth wedding anniversary, someone asked the old man about the secret to his health, and he said: "Whenever my wife argues with me, I go out for a walk. My good health today is because for fifty years, I have mostly been outdoors."

Tense Relationship

The manager in charge of recruitment said to his new employee: "You filled out this form well. There's just one point; in the 'relationship' column with your mother-in-law, you should have written 'wife' instead of 'tense.'"

Who's Disappointed

Wife: "If I married you, imagine how many men must be disappointed?"

Husband: "Hum! That probably means only I am disappointed!"

Disaster of Overspeeding

Person A: "One time, I hit her with my bike, and that's how I got to know her. After she recovered, we got married."

Person B: "Ah, the traffic sign really says it well, 'Overspeeding can cause disasters!'"

More Pleasant

Someone wrote on the rock wall of Mount Huang: "My wife and I came here for a visit, it was very pleasant, so I left these words as a memento." A few days later, another line appeared beside it: "I came here alone, without my wife, it was even more pleasant, so I left these words as a memento."

Honeymoon Chat

Husband: "Sigh, marrying a wife costs tens of thousands, it's really hard!"

Wife: "Dear, giving birth to a precious son for you isn't enough?"

Husband: "How about having a daughter? Maybe she could help recover the money spent on marrying you."

Going to the Tax Office

The wife always gets angry at her husband, shouting at him and complaining that he earns too little money. Every time this happens, the husband stands up and leaves home.

"You're going where?" the wife asks.

"I'm going to the tax office," the husband replies.

"Why are you in such a hurry?"

"Because I don't like listening to you. At the tax office, I'll hear the opposite."

Wedding Suit

On the wedding day, the groom's clothes were both loose and big. The bride unhappily asked: "Isn't this suit tailored to your size?"

"Yes! But recently, running around trying to meet your demands has made me lose weight in two places!"

Absolutely Worth It

A husband sent his wife off to take the train back to her hometown. The wife said: "You don't need to see me off on the platform. That would cost two pence for the platform ticket."

"It's no problem," the husband replied, "spending so little to send you away is absolutely worth it."

Tracing the Roots

Person A: "I dislike my mother-in-law the most."

Person B: "You should know, if it weren't for your mother-in-law, where would your wife come from?"

Person A: "Exactly, it's because of this reason that I dislike my mother-in-law. Who made her give birth to such a daughter?"

Opening and Closing Mouth

Doctor: "Please open your mouth."

Patient: "Doctor, thank you."

Doctor: "Why are you thanking me?"

Patient: "My husband always tells me to shut up!"

Thanks to Different Beliefs

Wife: "I go to the church to pray, but you burn incense and worship Buddha. So, after we die, we won't be able to meet in heaven."

Husband: "Sigh, isn't being together for a lifetime enough?"

Anniversary

Grandma mentioned the upcoming wedding anniversary to her daughter-in-law and said: "I guess you probably don't know how many years I've been married to your dad, right?"

Before the daughter-in-law could answer, the five-year-old grandson interjected: "I know, forty years."

"How do you know?" the old grandmother asked.

"I often hear grandpa tell you: 'I've endured all this for forty years.'"

Not As Good As the Maid

After watching the maid get scolded by his wife, the husband said to the maid: "Don't be upset, you and I share the same fate!"

The maid smiled and said: "Sir, how can you be like me? I'm not afraid of her at all, and I've already told her I won't work here from tomorrow. Would you dare to say that?"

Three "People"

On Sunday off, the wife couldn't find her husband. When the husband returned home after dinner, she asked: "Where did you go?"

"I went to see three people."

"Three people?" The wife was a bit nervous.

"Don't worry, they are all decent people: one is 'The Silent Man,' one is 'The Angry Man,' and one is 'The Man Who Deals With the Devil.'"

Limited Patience

Woman: "Why did you obey me completely before marriage, but now, three days after marriage, you've argued with me for two and a half days?"

Man: "Because my patience has limits."

Back to Normal

Wife: "Before marriage, didn't you use to call me an angel?"

Husband: "Yes."

Wife: "Why don't you call me that now?"

Husband: "Dear, you should be happy that I'm thinking more clearly now."

Free Love

At a wedding party, everyone wanted the newlyweds to talk about how they met and got married. The bride shyly said: "We fell in love freely."

The groom quickly added: "That's right, it was free love. I handed over all my savings of more than ten years to your mother, which made you free."

Just for the Wife

Person A: "Why have you been looking so worried these past few days?"

Person B: "I don't know myself."

Person A: "Then just think about your wife, and you will feel happy."

Person B: "Right! I think my worries are exactly because of my wife."

Reason for Worrying

The salesman's wife cried and said: "Every time you go out, I worry."

The husband consoled her: "Dear, don't worry about me, I will always come back."

"I know, that's why I worry."

Good Friend

Two gentlemen talked about women.

Person A: "Women must live frugally, behave properly, and keep silent. That's what makes a good woman."

Person B: "My wife is like that: she's extremely frugal, the towel hung there hasn't been used for six weeks; she stays put, sitting steadily on the sofa all day; she also keeps silent, until today she hasn't told me who the father of our child Jiajia is."

Bachelor Club

A certain gentleman was originally a member of the bachelor club, but after getting married, he left the club.

One day, after a quarrel with his wife, the gentleman angrily said: "If I knew leaving the bachelor club was the start of restriction, I would rather stay in the bachelor club."

His wife retorted: "If your father was a permanent member of that club, it would be great!"

Retorting Back

A couple was arguing. The husband cursed: "You should remember, whatever time it is, men's thoughts are always right, and their judgments are never wrong. Women are just the opposite."

"That's right, you choosing me as your wife was absolutely correct, but me choosing you as my husband was a huge mistake!"

Unparalleled

Ten years later, C returned to his hometown and met his old friend K. They talked about the past.

"What happened to Miss A from our class? She insulted me a lot back then."

"Oh, me too... I had it worse than you."

"Not likely, once I said to her 'You're so beautiful!', she spat on my face. Is there anything worse than that in the world?"

"Yes! She married me!" K firmly answered.

Chattering Wife

That night, the wife kept talking non-stop. Then, she turned off the light and asked: "Dear, are all the windows and doors closed?"

In the darkness, her husband replied: "Dear, everything except your chatterbox is closed."

Switch Roles

A wife frequently lost her temper and nagged her husband endlessly. One day, the husband said to her: "I heard that a wife's nagging affects her husband's lifespan."

The wife confidently rebutted: "Nonsense, a wife's nagging is very useful in changing her husband's character."

"Then, let's switch roles. Let me nag at you to change your character, okay?"

Using the Topic

The couple was fishing, and the wife kept talking while fishing. Suddenly, a large fish was hooked.

Wife: "This big fish is pitiful."

Husband: "Yes! If it had closed its mouth, nothing would have happened."

Stupid Idiot

The wife, while flipping through the book "Exploring World Folklore," told her husband: "It says here that on a certain island in the Atlantic Ocean, there is a magical custom: after the wedding, if the husband doesn't speak first, the wife will never speak."

"Really?" the husband said, "I think the husband who speaks first must be an idiot, a stupid idiot."

Nothing Strange

The teacher wrote in Fangfang's gradebook under "Teacher's Comments": "Talks too much during class."

Fangfang's father, after reading it, wrote beside the "Teacher's Comments": "If you knew her mother, you wouldn't find it strange."

Confession

One midnight, the female host received a phone call from a strange woman: "I hate my husband so much!"

"You dialed the wrong number," the female host told her.

She seemed not to have heard and continued talking non-stop: "I take care of five kids all day, and he thinks I'm enjoying myself. Sometimes I want to go out for a break, but he refuses. He goes out every night himself, saying he has social engagements. Hmph, who would believe that?"

"Sorry," the female host interrupted her, "I don't know you."

"You shouldn't know me!" she said, "If I told this to friends and family, it would cause a scandal. Now that I've said it, I feel much better, thank you!" After finishing, she hung up.

For Others

The father told his daughter: "Your boyfriend told me he wants to marry you, and I agreed."

The daughter said: "But I don't want to leave Mom."

"I understand. I won't ruin your happiness. You can take Mom with you," the father said.

Sad Prayer

After a woman remarried after her first husband's death, her second husband often beat her.

One day, the husband came home and found the wife praying: "Oh, omnipresent God! Please don't let my husband die, don't let me marry a third husband!"

The husband, hearing this, was quite surprised and questioned the wife why she prayed like that.

The wife answered: "My first husband only scolded me; you already hit me. If you die, my third husband will surely kill me!"

Different Feelings

The husband arrived at the airport to pick up his wife.

Wife: "Why do you look so gloomy? Look at that couple over there, they are laughing and chatting happily."

Husband: "He is seeing her off."

Wrong Marriage

A woman said to her companion: "Your wedding ring is on the wrong finger."

"Who cares! Anyway, my husband and I are wrongly married."

Inventor

Wife: "Gas stoves are very convenient. Do you know who invented them?"

Husband: "It was invented by a man who was called by his wife every morning to light the stove."

Think Positive

At the funeral, a girlfriend consoled the widow: "Don't think about the bad side, think about the good side."

The widow thought for a moment and said: "Right, this is the first time in twenty years I know where he spends his nights."

My Fault

Person A: "Who is that woman who talks incessantly?"

Person B: "My wife."

Person A: "Ah, sorry, forgive me."

Person B: "No, it's not your fault, it's my fault."

Cause and Effect

Wife: "When did your tympanitis get cured?"

Husband: "It was the day your throat started hurting."

Transfer of Trouble

A man attended his friend's wedding and saw the bride thanking her parents for raising her, with both of them in tears. This moved the man greatly. He said to the person beside him: "When I got married, my mother-in-law didn't shed a single tear, nor did she show any reluctance to part with her daughter. It's clear my wife caused her a lot of trouble before she married me. But now, my mother-in-law has transferred this trouble to me."

What to Eat Now

A newlywed couple was on their honeymoon and entered an underground restaurant from their hotel. The waiter brought the menu and waited for them to order.

The bride coquettishly said to her husband: "You should know very well what I like to eat, right?"

Her husband replied: "Of course, but what should we eat now?"

Seven Years Like One Day

Person A: "Since I married my husband, he has treated me the same way as on the day we got married seven years ago."

Person B: "That's not right, I heard you arguing last night!"

Person A: "Yes! We started arguing on the day we got married."

Make Up for Shortcomings

Wife: "I know you married me because I'm rich."

Husband: "No, it's because I'm poor."

Praise

Wife: "Since I married you, I often hear others praising me..."

Husband: "Of course, look at me, wealthy and powerful, no wonder others praise your fortune!"

Wife: "No! They don't praise me for that."

Husband: "Then why?"

Wife: "They first praised me: 'What a heavenly beauty!' Then sighed: 'Unfortunately, she married such a stupid and ugly man!'"

Wife's Freedom

Husband: "Next time, you should bear a boy."

Wife: "How can that be 'free'?"

Husband: "You're free in everything else, why not in this?"

Made a Vow

Husband: "Didn't you make a vow during the wedding ceremony to obey your husband?"

Wife: "Yes, but at that time, there was a pastor present!"

Mood

Since getting married, Xiao Li never took his wife to a restaurant or for coffee. One day, the wife teasingly said: "You really lack romantic mood. When will we go to the café again?"

Xiao Li readily agreed: "No problem, I fully support it." Then he asked, "But what shall we talk about at the café?"

Bride's Complaint

Just after the honeymoon, the bride began complaining about the lack of money: "Before we got married," she nagged, "you said you were very rich."

"True," the husband sighed, "back then, I was indeed very rich."

Good Woman

Zhang San's daughter-in-law was making a fuss at home. Zhang San, having no other choice, went to the matchmaker Li Si and said: "When you introduced my son to his partner, you repeatedly said she was a good woman. Who knew she would turn out like this..."

Li Si hurriedly explained: "Back then, I only said she was a good woman, but I didn't say she was a good daughter-in-law!"

Headache

A lady told the doctor she had a headache. The doctor suggested she get married. A year later, the doctor accidentally met the lady: "Hey, did you get married?"

"Thank you, yes, I did."

"Do you still have headaches?"

"No, but my husband started having headaches."

Between Husband and Wife

Person A: "Oh my god! I forgot my wallet under the pillow!"

Person B: "Oh, aren't your servants very honest?"

Person A: "Yes, but they would hand the wallet to my wife!"

Promise or Threat

"Listen, friend, I've encountered misfortune. Yesterday, my wife and I had an argument, and she stormed out angrily, declaring she would live with her mother. Tell me, is this a promise or a threat?"

"For you, what's the difference?!"

"Hey, there's a big difference! If it's a promise, it means my wife will definitely go to her mother's place; if it's a threat, it means my mother-in-law will move into my house."

Parting Words

Person A: "Every time my wife goes back to her parents' house, she always leaves me with a message."

Person B: "What does she say?"

Person A: "Twenty years ago, she would have said: 'Dear, I'll be back soon.'"

Person B: "And now?"

Person A: "Now, she says: 'Hey! Don't forget to water the flowers, feed the cat, clean the room, and watch the door!'"

Exchange Again

Alcoholic: "I really hope my wife comes back."

Friend: "Where is she?"

Alcoholic: "I traded her for a bottle of alcohol with a man."

Friend: "Do you finally realize you love her? You fool!"

Alcoholic: "No, my alcohol craving came back, but now there's no wife left to trade."

She Knows You Too Well

Person A: "I've been unlucky since I got married!"

Person B: "How unlucky?"

Person A: "In front of my wife, I look stern, and she says I'm cold and heartless; when I smile, she says I'm hiding daggers behind smiles; when I take care of her, she says I'm good at flattering; when I stop working, she says I'm putting on airs. What do you think?"

Person B: "Your wife is truly talented!"

Person A: "Do you think she's using words improperly?"

Person B: "No, she knows you too well!"
