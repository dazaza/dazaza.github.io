[Information] YYeTs (Everyone's Subtitles) receives data bank cloud storage service support

by xdash on 2010-09-21 17:58:27

# This sponsorship cooperation was initiated by Data Bank (DBank), led by Sync Control XDash, and ultimately DBank is willing to provide YYeTs (Yiren Yingshi) with unlimited capacity and bandwidth for file external link services. Here, on behalf of the vast readers of Sync Control and the enthusiastic fans of YYeTs, I would like to thank DBank for its strong support, hoping that YYeTs will continue to produce more excellent subtitle translation works in the future (by the way, my American drama enlightenment work "Heroes" and my favorite "The Big Bang Theory" are all brought by YYeTs).

Below is the full text of the press release:

According to Liang Liang, the person in charge of the YYeTs subtitle group, recently the subtitle group has received cloud storage service support provided by the well-known domestic online disk Data Bank (DBank).

At noon on August 20, YYeTs announced that the website had been shut down due to some reasons. "At 3 pm on August 19, because of some reasons, the website was shut down, and the server was confiscated. We are actively cooperating with the handling." YYeTs posted an announcement on the website to explain why the website could not be accessed normally. At the same time, the announcement estimated that the website would return to normal in 3-5 days.

On the temporary homepage of YYeTs, there was also information about fundraising donations: "Now a stable 100M dedicated server, the annual cost is over 30,000 yuan, such a huge expense completely exceeds the承受range of our non-profit organization. We now hope that netizens who have the ability can provide financial support for our server upgrade project, so that we can better translate and spread open course projects and benefit more enthusiasts." Donations can be completed through Alipay, and YYeTs stated that all donations and later expenses will be regularly published on an XLS table (expected to be updated every half month) to ensure the transparency of the funds.

After the incident, some server sponsors even withdrew a large number of分流servers, making it even more painful for YYeTs. This matter has attracted widespread attention and heated discussions from the Internet users. Netizens expressed their support for YYeTs, and many netizens generously donated through various means to purchase servers for YYeTs. A few days later, YYeTs' website resumed access, but netizens still worried about the current situation and future prospects of domestic subtitle groups.

At this moment, Data Bank (DBank) took the initiative to contact YYeTs and expressed its willingness to provide unlimited space and unlimited traffic file external link services as a long-term auxiliary publishing and downloading channel for YYeTs' open courses.

After a week of testing and use, YYeTs decided to abandon other transfer methods and online disks, and will fully switch to Data Bank as the only designated online disk publishing channel in the future: First, Data Bank's upload and download are stable and fast; Second, YYeTs does not have more manpower to maintain multiple online disks at the same time, and can only choose the best one, other online disks can only rely on netizens to update.

This move not only solved YYeTs' urgent needs, but also provided a guarantee for the continuous and healthy stable development of YYeTs, and gave a shot in the arm to the enthusiastic netizens who love American dramas and open courses.

According to relevant responsible persons of Data Bank, the main focus of sponsoring YYeTs is to fully support the development of excellent original domestic Internet teams like YYeTs with its own advantages in cloud storage resources and data storage sharing services, benefiting netizens and realizing the complementary advantages and maximization of value for both parties.

Data Bank's file external link service has the characteristics of no advertisements on the page, one external link with multiple files, and the external link never expires.

It is reported that the Data Bank team has strong brand support behind it.
