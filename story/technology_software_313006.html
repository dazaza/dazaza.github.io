Inside Sheffield United: Chairman Xu's words from Shenyang! Everyone should focus on their own responsibilities and do their work well. In the Chengdu Sheffield United management team, there is a saying: "Chairman Xu has spoken from Shenyang, everyone needs to focus on their own responsibilities and do their work well." Common sense tells us this isn't possible. However, the management seems to think that creating such a story could better motivate the employees and reduce their insecurity. This club, as well as the football park and sports park standing in Shuangliu, were almost all built by Xu Hongtao. With his gentle personality and strong sense of humanity, he is a person who has been deified in many people's hearts. Every employee that the reporter met would refer to "Chairman Xu" with great respect. The reporter joked with Ma Mingyu, "Will we need Chairman Xu to remote control in the future?" Ma Mingyu became serious, "I really haven't thought that far ahead." Currently, all arrangements for the club and team are being carried out according to Xu Hongtao's established policies. A director specially sent by the Sheffield United Holding Company announced the new personnel arrangements at 11 am on the 14th: in order to better manage the club and team, a General Manager's Office was set up, with Ma Mingyu and Wang Maojun among others entering this General Manager's Office as part of the management team. Yao Xia was appointed as vice president of the club, also serving as the team leader and player of Chengdu Sheffield United Football Team. Subsequently, at 3 pm in the afternoon, the club held a press briefing to officially announce this news. In fact, this personnel arrangement was also decided by Xu Hongtao beforehand. Now, the personnel layout of the club is completely following the plan determined by Xu Hongtao before. It is said that Xu Hongtao once boldly proposed to the British side that next year's goal is to qualify for the AFC Champions League. Now, of course, the target is still unclear. However, the club has stated that the list of players for sale will be published within this week. As for the incoming players, negotiations are still ongoing. According to Ma Mingyu, head coach Wang Baoshan and others have already identified several players during their trip to Brazil to evaluate foreign players, and negotiations are currently underway.
