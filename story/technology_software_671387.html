Guangzhou Game Machine and Anime Wholesale Market

by andersonmfb on 2012-02-21 22:00:49

Our staff reporter Zuo Shangming reports: I recently accompanied the Lanzhou home appliance chamber of commerce investigation team on a trip to Guangdong Zhongshan, Foshan small home appliance production enterprises and Guangzhou digital products specialized market for an investigative study. We found that No. 25 Xinji Road in the Zhujiang Xi Di electronic digital product business circle was leased and invested in by Guangdong Jinda Industrial Development Co., Ltd. and Hong Kong's Wanfang International Technology Co., Ltd. It has been renovated into a professional wholesale and retail market for the distribution of animated video games and other digital products nationwide - the Guangzhou Lingnan International Electronic Digital Plaza. Close to 400 merchants dealing in mainland and Hong Kong-Macau video game digital products have already moved in - filling the gap in domestic animated professional markets.

Due to the concentration of various upgraded domestic and international animation video game consoles and their accessories, the plaza can provide "one-stop" services to video game product procurement merchants. Since its opening half a year ago, it has seen booming business, filling the gap in the domestic wholesale and retail distribution market for all kinds of video game software and hardware digital products. At the same time, it has effectively promoted the rectification of the current disorderly, uneven quality, unremarkable brand, blocked supply channels, unclear target markets, and consumer selection difficulties in the domestic video game product market.

During my visit and interview, I found that the newly decorated Guangzhou Lingnan International Electronic Digital Plaza, mainly focused on the centralized exhibition and sales of video game products, is six stories tall with over 450 shops and offices of various specifications, covering tens of thousands of square meters of operating space. The surrounding financial, parking lot, and numerous logistics company facilities are complete.

The Xidi digital business circle formed by the "Southern Tower International Electronic Digital City" has developed over many years, with increasingly hot transactions, becoming a well-known domestic and international hub for mobile phone accessories and digital products, ranking among the top in annual transaction volume nationwide. The business circle effect is very prominent. The Guangzhou Lingnan International Electronic Digital Plaza enters this mature business circle as a differentiated professional wholesale market at a high starting point, providing a good wholesale and retail distribution trading platform for numerous domestic animated video game manufacturers.

To develop online trading services, the investors have not only improved service facilities but also selected professional management teams, adhering to the principle of "releasing water to raise fish, promoting prosperity," formulating generous recruitment policies. Additionally, efforts have been made to optimize market management, attracting and optimizing commodity structures and merchant structures, promoting the market's rapid advancement towards an e-commerce platform closely linking physical stores with warehousing and logistics. This allows each operating merchant to not only become a base for physical transactions but also use information technology as a means, integrity as a guarantee, and mutual benefit and win-win as a lever, quickly enabling every merchant to partner with numerous logistics companies as rapid and secure delivery service collaborators, using warehousing pickup as the external base for market exhibitions and sales, developing B-B and B-C combined online remote trading information windows and service windows, thereby driving the continuous expansion of the transaction scale of each merchant stall. Thus realizing the informatization management and networked development of professional markets, truly achieving a wholesale service operation mechanism of Internet of Things (IoT) supporting virtual operations through modern logistics distribution services and e-commerce greater than physical store operations, creating typical demonstrations for the transformation and upgrading of specialized factor markets in southern China and even nationwide, playing a guiding role.
