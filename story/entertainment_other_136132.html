Wenzhou Public Security Police College begins enrollment for admissions

by 304885068 on 2009-03-31 08:50:42

The enrollment for the 2009 provincial public security police college entrance examination has begun. Before April 12, candidates can bring their college entrance examination registration certificate and ID card to directly handle registration procedures at Zhejiang Police College, or register through remittance mail.

Candidates must have already registered for the college entrance examination in their place of permanent residence, and their age must not exceed 22 years old (born after September 1, 1987).

Male candidates must be over 1.70 meters tall (including 1.70 meters), with a weight of no less than 50 kilograms; female candidates must be over 1.60 meters tall (including 1.60 meters), with a weight of no less than 45 kilograms. Art, sports, and public security volunteering cannot be reported simultaneously.

All candidates must participate in interviews, psychological tests, and physical fitness tests, with physical fitness test scores counted towards the comprehensive score.

Zhejiang Police College is enrolling 64 students from our Wenzhou area. For more details, log on to the Zhejiang Education Examination Network.
