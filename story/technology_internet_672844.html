A carpet doesn't necessarily have to be bought; making your own DIY modular faux leather carpet at home is also an excellent choice that will warm up your house. &；nbsp；&；nbsp；&；nbsp； Artificial leather usually undergoes a surface coating process, ensuring the texture does not deform or peel off. It can be cut into various shapes according to need or preference for direct use. This advantage makes creating a modular carpet quite achievable. After purchasing the desired amount of artificial leather, you can trace circles on the leather using a dinner bowl, then cut them out following the traced lines to form individual circular leather pieces. Use a compass to mark four symmetrical edge points on each small piece of leather, and cut a small slit at each point with scissors. Then, connect these circular leather pieces using 1-centimeter wide leather strips. Finally, secure the ends of the leather connecting strips on the backside with a thinner strip to complete the project. Such a modular carpet can refresh the entire living space and make it much cozier. &；nbsp；
