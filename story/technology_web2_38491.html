Good news for students & the mobile version of Xiaonei campus network is officially released: m.xiaonei.com

by turbo0612 on 2007-12-08 17:08:13

Now, students can access m.xiaonei.com via mobile phone, log in to Xiaonei, and keep in touch with friends on the network at any time.

What is the mobile version of Xiaonei?

The mobile version of Xiaonei is the mobile website of Xiaonei. It is a free wireless WAP site that can be accessed at will when surfing the Internet on a mobile phone. The mobile version of Xiaonei moves your Xiaonei account to your phone, allowing you to communicate with your friends on the network anytime and anywhere.

How to log in?

To log in to the mobile version of Xiaonei, you need a mobile phone with an Internet browser installed.
