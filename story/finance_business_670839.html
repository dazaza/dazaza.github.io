Man gets 10 years in prison for helping others transport drugs for free out of brotherhood

by anonymous on 2012-02-21 11:53:54

Now there are quite a few farcical stories presented in court, and also many heartrending cases. "Wen Qiang's Case Stories" mainly tells those stories inside and outside the court. Readers are welcome to call 15954530929, and give Wen Qiang some suggestions or share feelings. This 39-year-old man is seen as a "successful gentleman" in his friends' eyes. He owns a shop in Yantai urban area, and his family is very happy and harmonious. However, he was sentenced to 10 years and 5 months of fixed-term imprisonment and fined 20,000 yuan for helping with drug trafficking out of consideration for friendship without taking any money. This information was obtained by the reporter from Zhifu District Procuratorate recently. On July 16 last year, Yantai police intercepted a drug user Liu Mou at the gate of a hotel. During the routine trial, Liu Mou actually revealed a clue of a big drug trafficking case - 50 grams of crystal methamphetamine would be traded at the Civil Aviation Building at noon on that day. Liu Mou then confessed that a fellow countryman from Northeast China had contacted him half a month ago, asking him to help find a buyer and plan to sell crystal methamphetamine in Yantai. The other party had already arrived in Yantai with 50 grams of drugs yesterday. Under his contact, the seller Zhang Mou and the buyer Gao Mou agreed to trade around noon on July 16, and the location was the Civil Aviation Building. The police immediately set up control at the Civil Aviation Building, opened a room, and let Liu Mou call both parties to come and trade crystal methamphetamine. At noon, Zhang Mou and Gao Mou came to the trading place one after another. The police officers went forward to arrest both parties and searched a cigarette box on Zhang Mou's body, which contained 50 grams of crystal methamphetamine prepared for trading. It was understood that these 50 grams of crystal methamphetamine were planned to be traded at a price of 28,000 yuan. Unexpectedly, the arrested Zhang Mou was not the seller, but the liaison responsible for connecting the two sides. The real seller was waiting outside the Civil Aviation Building for Zhang Mou to return after the transaction. The police immediately searched around the Civil Aviation Building and eventually caught the seller who rushed from Northeast China. Zhang Mou said that he and the seller were good friends. In early July, the other party suddenly called him, asking him to help ask if it was possible to sell crystal methamphetamine in Yantai. Zhang Mou helped contact Liu Mou, and Liu Mou then found the buyer Gao Mou. Out of consideration for friendship, Zhang Mou did not ask for any commission and had once told the seller not to give him any money, just give Liu Mou a little benefit. Both Zhang Mou and the seller's confessions confirmed this statement. The prosecutor in charge of the case at Zhifu District Procuratorate said that Zhang Mou, 39 years old, was seen as a successful gentleman in his friends' eyes. He was at the rising stage of his career, owning a good shop in the urban area, and his family was also very happy and harmonious. After the trial, the police verified that Liu Mou had gathered a crowd to fight in his hometown in Northeast China and seriously injured one person. Gao Mou had repeatedly sold drugs in Zhifu District. The prosecutor said that although Zhang Mou did not gain any benefits, he personally participated in drug trafficking and provided contact for both parties of the transaction. They still prosecuted Zhang Mou for drug trafficking. The court finally sentenced Zhang Mou, who was "righteous", to 10 years and 5 months of fixed-term imprisonment and fined 20,000 yuan for drug trafficking; sentenced Liu Mou to 14 years of fixed-term imprisonment and fined 20,000 yuan for drug trafficking and intentional injury; and since Gao Mou was caught before the 50-gram crystal methamphetamine transaction, he was sentenced to 3 years and 9 months of fixed-term imprisonment and fined 10,000 yuan for drug trafficking.
