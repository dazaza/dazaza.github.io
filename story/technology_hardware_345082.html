Businessman donates 650,000 yuan to help female college student with uremia have kidney transplant (Picture)

by bm80com2 on 2010-04-13 22:05:43

Mai Tingting has already undergone a kidney transplant surgery.

Sanya, April 11th - "The surgery was very successful! We sincerely thank the kind-hearted Mr. Zeng for his selfless help. Without his continuous care, Mai Tingting might not have made it through this ordeal. Here, we would like to once again use this corner of Nanguo Metropolis Daily to say - Thank you!" Just after 8 am on the 10th, upon hearing the resolute confirmation from the chief surgeon that the surgery was a success, Ms. Long, Mai Tingting's mother, finally unclenched her fists which had been tightly clenched due to tension. Unable to contain her joy, Ms. Long, who was at the Third Affiliated Hospital of Guangzhou Medical College accompanying her daughter during the surgery, immediately reported the good news to the Nanguo Metropolis Daily reporter and her family in Sanya.

Thus, Mai Tingting, a female college student from Sanya who has suffered from uremia for nearly two and a half years, successfully underwent a kidney transplant surgery with the help of Mr. Zeng, a philanthropist who donated a total of 650,000 yuan in three separate occasions.

To save the female college student suffering from uremia, the kind-hearted person donates twice, totaling 350,000 yuan.

On March 2nd this year, after learning about the situation where Mai Tingting, a female college student from Sanya, suffered from severe renal failure (uremia) and pulmonary erosion, a Mr. Zeng, whom she had never met, donated 350,000 yuan in two separate instances. In response, our newspaper published a major news story titled "Where are you, Good Samaritan who donated 350,000 yuan? The girl's family entrusts the reporter to convey: Mr. Zeng, we just want to thank you face-to-face!" This report sparked a strong reaction in society.

In March 2008, Mr. Zeng came to Hainan on a business trip by plane. After seeing a report about Mai Tingting, a female college student from Sanya, who was severely ill and lacked funds for treatment, he did not hesitate to donate 300,000 yuan. Meanwhile, people from all walks of life full of love also extended their helping hands.

However, doctors told Mai Tingting that before undergoing a kidney transplant surgery, she must first treat her already serious pulmonary erosion and continue hemodialysis three times a week. In April 2009, after receiving treatment, Mai Tingting could undergo a kidney transplant surgery, and a donor kidney was found in Guangzhou. However, the challenge facing Mai Tingting was that out of the nearly 400,000 yuan donated by socially-minded individuals, after repaying borrowed money amounting to 70,000-80,000 yuan and daily high medical expenses, only 100,000 yuan remained. Yet, the cost of the kidney transplant surgery alone amounted to 200,000-300,000 yuan, not including post-operative care costs. At that time, after learning the situation, Mr. Zeng expressed his willingness to donate another 400,000 yuan to Mai Tingting. Unexpectedly, affected by the global financial crisis, his business suffered greatly, forcing him to delay the donation.

Nevertheless, Mr. Zeng did not abandon his concern for Mai Tingting and frequently inquired about her condition through friends. On February 24th this year, after learning through his friend that Ms. Long, Mai Tingting's mother, had pawned her salary card to raise her daughter's medical fees, he immediately transferred 50,000 yuan of charitable funds to Mai Tingting's bank account to address the urgent needs of Mai Tingting's family, and promised to cover all expenses while contacting a kidney donor for Mai Tingting...

Kidney source found for girl’s transplant, good-hearted person donates another 300,000 yuan for surgery

On April 9th, the Third Affiliated Hospital of Guangzhou Medical College called Ms. Long, informing her that a kidney source had been found. If they wanted to proceed with the transplant, they needed to arrive in Guangzhou as soon as possible. Upon hearing this, Ms. Long was so delighted that she almost jumped up, immediately informing her husband who was at work. However, the 50,000 yuan Mr. Zeng donated in February had been mostly spent on over a month's worth of dialysis and medication fees, leaving only more than 30,000 yuan. Subsequently, she borrowed money from relatives and friends, but unfortunately, they were also unable to assist. The joy on Ms. Long's face quickly disappeared.

At noon, Ms. Long tentatively called a friend of Mr. Zeng in Sanya who had been in contact with her. After hearing the situation, the friend immediately called Mr. Zeng. However, he couldn't be reached at the moment, so the friend sent him a message instead. Around 3 pm, Mr. Zeng called his friend in Sanya, instructing him to tell Ms. Long to immediately bring Mai Tingting to Guangzhou for the surgery, with all surgical expenses covered by him. Upon hearing this news, Ms. Long and her husband immediately booked air tickets and took Mai Tingting to the Third Affiliated Hospital of Guangzhou Medical College.

Around 6:30 pm, upon arriving in Guangzhou, they immediately headed to the hospital to prepare for the surgery. Unexpectedly, since Ms. Long only had more than 30,000 yuan on her, it wasn’t enough to pay the deposit, and the hospital indicated they could do nothing to help.

What to do? Should they just go back? In a panic, Ms. Long thought of Mai Tingting's doctor at the People's Hospital of Hainan Province, who might be able to help by explaining the situation to the hospital and promising to make up the payment once Mr. Zeng's donation arrived. Thus, she immediately called Mai Tingting's doctor at the People's Hospital of Hainan Province. Upon hearing that Mai Tingting had received donations from the benevolent Mr. Zeng and other loving individuals in society, the chief surgeon at the Third Affiliated Hospital of Guangzhou Medical College immediately went online to search for relevant reports about Mai Tingting. After seeing the reports from our newspaper and others about Mr. Zeng donating 350,000 yuan for Mai Tingting, he decided to perform the surgery on Mai Tingting.

After arrangements were made, at 00:20 am on April 10th, Mai Tingting finally lay on the operating table. After anxiously waiting for nearly three hours, at 03:28 am, Mai Tingting was wheeled out of the operating room. Ms. Long and her husband approached, gently touching their daughter's head and softly calling her name. A while later, when Mai Tingting passed some light red urine, the doctor informed Ms. Long that this signified the newly transplanted kidney had started functioning within her daughter's body.

Ms. Long said that around 15:30 pm on April 10th, a friend of Mr. Zeng called her, informing her that after hearing Mai Tingting didn't have enough money for the surgery, Mr. Zeng had already transferred 300,000 yuan for the surgery fee to Mai Tingting's bank account. She quickly checked the bank, and indeed, the 300,000 yuan had arrived. Up to this point, Mr. Zeng had donated a total of 650,000 yuan to Mai Tingting in three separate instances.

Ms. Long said that currently, her daughter Tingting had been transferred to the intensive care unit and would need to be observed there for one to two days before being moved to a regular ward. If no rejection reactions occur, she could return home to recuperate after half a month.

Unable to know the benefactor's name, the family can only bless their benefactor

Even more moving is that until now, Mr. Zeng, who donated 650,000 yuan to Mai Tingting on three separate occasions, has never met Mai Tingting's family, even the usual greetings or text messages were relayed through a friend of Mr. Zeng. Mai Tingting's family does not know Mr. Zeng's real name, where he is from, or what he does.

Ms. Long said that since Mr. Zeng donated to help her daughter, she had repeatedly asked his friend to tell her Mr. Zeng's name, place of origin, phone number, etc., so that her entire family could personally express their gratitude to Mr. Zeng, but she was never able to fulfill this wish. She hopes the journalist can help inquire about Mr. Zeng's name, phone number, and other details.

Tonight, when the journalist told Mr. Zeng's friend that since Mr. Zeng's spirit of helping others is so admirable, Mai Tingting's family also hoped to express their thanks face-to-face, even if it were over the phone, Mr. Zeng's friend stated that over the past two years, despite Mai Tingting's family repeatedly asking him for Mr. Zeng's phone number, Mr. Zeng clearly told him that he did not do this for anything in return, merely doing his best to do good deeds, and there was no need to inform Mai Tingting's family of his real name, phone number, workplace, etc. Therefore, he also told Mr. Zeng that since he did not want to disclose any specific information, he would not reveal any information about him to Mai Tingting's family. Mr. Zeng's friend said that in fact, Mr. Zeng had helped not only Mai Tingting, but according to his understanding, in life, Mr. Zeng always enjoyed helping those in need, he was just low-key about it. Regarding this, he also felt powerless.

At 9:30 pm, when the journalist informed Ms. Long that Mr. Zeng's friend still refused to disclose any specific information about Mr. Zeng, Ms. Long said that it is often said, "If someone gives you a drop of water, you should repay them with a gushing spring." Since Mr. Zeng helped her family so much without even letting them know his name, they could only silently make 650,000 wishes: wishing Mr. Zeng success in his career and happiness in his life...
