**Steps You Need to Know for Changing Wallpaper**

&；nbsp；

**Article Summary:** If the walls in your home already have wallpaper, most of the previously used wallpaper [http://www.itmilan.com/], belongs to PVC wallpaper, also known as plastic wallpaper. If you are replacing it with new wallpaper, this old wallpaper must be removed. What should you pay attention to when replacing the new wallpaper? Plastic wallpaper generally consists of two layers: the surface is a plastic layer, and the base material is a paper substrate. The specific method for replacing wallpaper is as follows:

1. Use a sprayer to spray warm water onto the paper substrate of the plastic wallpaper, soaking the wallpaper and softening the adhesive. This usually takes more than 2 hours.

2. Use a toothed scraper to remove the plastic layer on the surface of the plastic wallpaper. The purpose is to allow water to penetrate into the inner layer smoothly.

3. Check whether the plastic wallpaper substrate can be easily peeled off from the wall. If so, it can be removed.

4. Use sandpaper to clear any remaining plastic wallpaper debris from the wall.

5. Check if there are any cracks or depressions on the wall, and remove any attached objects from the plastic wallpaper. The treatment method is the same as for painted walls.

**Tip:** If there are damp areas on part of the wall, do not apply wallpaper until you find and remove the root cause of the dampness, then address it accordingly.
