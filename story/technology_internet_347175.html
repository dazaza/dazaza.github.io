10 Small Secrets for Women's Health and Beauty

As everyone knows, staying healthy and beautiful, maintaining youthful looks, is the lifelong pursuit of all women. Therefore, so many women willingly choose expensive, varied skincare products to maintain their skin. In fact, in daily life, besides skincare products, there are many seemingly minor details that can be considered proper maintenance secrets. Today's blog post brings 10 such small secrets for women. Let's take a look!

1. Take multivitamins after breakfast:

Studies show that taking appropriate multivitamins after breakfast yields the best results. Firstly, it provides what the body needs for the day, giving you energy to focus on work or study. Secondly, it doesn't overly burden the kidneys. Therefore, taking suitable multivitamins after breakfast is a good choice for women's health and beauty.

2. Drink a large glass of water after eating fast food:

The calories and salt content in fast food generally exceed standards severely. Although we can't do much about the fat we've eaten, a large glass of water can help dilute the sodium concentration in your body, keeping you further from hypertension and aging.

3. Eat the "white threads" on oranges:

Many people peel off the "white threads" on oranges when eating them. In fact, these contain abundant flavonoids, greatly benefiting women's health.

4. Drink two glasses of water before each meal:

Drinking two glasses of water before each meal keeps your body hydrated and controls your appetite. A Dutch study shows that drinking two glasses of water before meals reduces hunger and food intake, helping with weight loss.

5. Don't miss any chance to eat onions:

Many people carefully pick out the onions when eating vegetables, trying to avoid them at all costs. This is a big mistake. Onions contain a large amount of heart-protecting flavonoids, so eating onions should be our responsibility. Especially when eating less healthy foods like grilled meat, the onion inside is your "life-saving grass."

6. Prefer dark-colored fruits:

Although fruits come in various appearances, to measure health benefits, dark-colored fruits definitely surpass others because they contain more antioxidants. Therefore, women can appropriately favor plums, prunes, and other black fruits when choosing fruit.

7. Snack at three o'clock in the afternoon:

Perhaps sleeping in can be your excuse for skipping breakfast, but the snack at three o'clock in the afternoon cannot be excused. Supplementing nutrients between lunch and dinner helps you get through the most tired part of the day. Yogurt, fruit, and cookies are great choices.

8. Drink coffee with milk:

The first thing in the morning is to fill a cup with skim milk. Then drink 1/5 of it and fill it with coffee. This way, you can consume 25% of the daily required vitamin D and 30% of calcium.

9. Frequently drink cold-water steeped black tea:

Recent research from the U.S. Department of Agriculture found that compared to greens or carrots, a serving of black tea contains more antioxidants, effectively helping you resist wrinkles or cancer. Cold water allows the beneficial substances in tea to slowly dissolve without being destroyed. Drinking cold-water steeped black tea simply requires waiting a little longer.

10. Drink water in fixed quantities:

A busy job may make you only think of drinking water when your mouth feels dry, realizing you haven't drunk water all morning. Prepare a 1.5-liter large bottle on your desk, pour the water you need to drink in a day into it, and set a rule to finish it before leaving work.
