Fault Phenomenon and Repair Method of IR6 York Air Conditioning Filter Caused by Dirt Blockage

by ndx76ol70 on 2011-11-15 13:27:15

Fault Phenomenon and Repair Method of York Air Conditioning Filter Blockage: Cause Analysis: On-site repair, no cooling effect, outdoor unit starts and stops frequently, indoor unit can be remotely controlled to run normally, but the outdoor unit starts and stops within about three minutes, and during this time, the air blown out is not cold. Thus, it was initially judged that there was a fault in the refrigeration system. The low-pressure side pressure was tested with a pressure gauge. Since the balanced pressure at shutdown was 1.1 MPa, after startup, it gradually dropped to 0.1 MPa until shutdown, then returned to the balanced pressure. Moreover, during the operation of the outdoor unit, frost was found from the filter to the capillary tube to the high-pressure pipe, thus confirming the blockage of the filter.

Solution Measures: After replacing the new filter, the test machine ran normally.

Data Source: http://blog.163.com/york_kongt/.

Experience Summary: For faults where the outdoor unit starts frequently, first confirm whether it is an electrical fault or a refrigeration system fault. Generally, if the filter is blocked, the following phenomena will occur: frosting at the capillary tube outlet, partial frosting on the evaporator, detection of low-pressure levels below normal values, slightly lower high-pressure levels than normal pressures, and the balanced pressure at shutdown being close to the saturated pressure under ambient temperature. The exhaust temperature and casing temperature of the compressor increase. If there is a phenomenon of excessive current causing shutdown, it does not necessarily mean a compressor fault. The Beijing York Air Conditioning Repair Center suggests comprehensively considering the fault phenomena. During general air conditioning repairs, check the current and maintenance pressure. High current and low pressure indicate a system blockage, focusing on checking the filter and capillary tube.

Fault Phenomenon: No cooling, frequent start-stop of the outdoor unit.
