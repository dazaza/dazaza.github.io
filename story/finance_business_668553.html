Nike Footwear - Making Thuds on the Globe of Fashion

The year 1987 witnessed the launch of a new line of Nike shoes. This innovative type of footwear, which shook the world with its advanced air sole technology, was the Nike Air Max. Since its introduction, numerous variations have been made to the original design. The campaign started with the slogan: "A little less damage." The marketing campaign created a strong appeal in the minds of consumers by featuring athletes in the promotional campaigns.

In 1985, Nike launched the Dunk as the shoe for basketball players. It was initially named Nike Dunk SB and specifically targeted at basketball players. The embedded technology was designed to support the athletic capabilities of the shoe. In 1998, the Dunk series of shoes was re-launched as a fashion icon among the youth. Compared to Nike's original shoes, the Dunk series was launched as a lower-profile shoe. However, the sole was lighter than the parent shoe.

To popularize the Dunk series, Nike even launched a program called "College Colors" Program. The Dunk series was further expanded to include Nike Dunks Premium, Nike Dunks Low, and Nike Dunks Mid. The Nike Dunk Highs are quite impressive to mention and undoubtedly captivating to look at. Compared to the Dunk High Shoe line, the Nike Dunks Low and Nike Dunks Mid range shoes have a street style but certainly with higher performance quality.

As a special treat for women, Nike released an exceptional collection of shoes that looked entirely sporty, trendy, and inspiring. If you are a fashion lover and wish to have an ultra-stylish appearance, then Nike sneakers can meet your expectations. The designs appear inspiring, but they come with reasonable prices so everyone can sizzle with a dashing look.

Air Jordans - Air Jordan is also known as "The Best Shoe Ever" or "MJs." This line of shoes was specifically designed for Jordan and named after him. After Jordan left professional basketball, his name became the ultimate brand endorsement. Even after Jordan retired from the game, the shoes bearing his name remain very popular. Now, Jordan is sold under the Jordan brand. So, it is jokingly said that the Jordan Brand has immortalized a basketball player. A signature shoe product, a variant of the same, has also been recently launched.

Women’s Jordans: With the first-time release of the Air Jordan Women's collection, Nike promised to bring the same quality and commitment to this line that it had shown previously in the men's shoe line. The Women's Air Jordan shoes were no exception to that promise. Female fans, much like their male counterparts, were passionate about owning Air Jordan sneakers. The Women's Air Jordans come with a wide-ranging collection, and you can spend some time browsing the internet to find your choice of collections.
