Preliminary review under the president's real-name recommendation system at Peking University: 90 students passed

by 3ryo950p3u on 2009-12-01 15:25:48

Key tips: At 18:00 on November 30th, the enrollment office of Peking University published the list of students who have passed the preliminary examination and were recommended by the "High School Principal Real-name Recommendation System" in 2010. The list includes 90 students from 39 high schools, with a public announcement period of 5 days. If they pass the interview, they will ultimately receive up to 30 bonus points for the college entrance examination.

According to China News Service on December 1st, at 6 PM yesterday, the enrollment office of Peking University announced the list of students who passed the preliminary review and were recommended by the "High School Principal Real-name Recommendation System" in 2010. This began a public announcement period lasting 5 days. According to statistics, the list includes 90 students from 39 high schools.

The announcement made by the enrollment office of Peking University stated that relevant discipline expert groups arranged by the PKU admissions office reviewed the registration materials of the recommended students. If there are any issues with the individuals being announced, the public can report them in writing to the Peking University Admissions Office.

The Peking University Admissions Office also disclosed their contact information:

Fax: 010-62554332

Address: Room 129, Old Chemistry Building, Peking University, Peking University Admissions Office

Postal Code: 100871

It is understood that except for Zhengzhou No.1 High School which abandoned two spots, most other high schools fully utilized their recommendation quotas. The PKU admissions office said that they would take a trusting attitude towards the principal recommendations, so as long as the recommended students do not have special circumstances or obvious disadvantages, they basically can pass the preliminary review, skip the written test, and directly enter the independent recruitment interview stage. If they pass the interview, they will ultimately receive up to 30 bonus points for the college entrance examination.
