Weirdo steals iPhone, sends photos of himself to original owner without wiping device

by anonymous on 2013-08-08 10:55:42

Nowadays, thieves who steal iPhones generally know the basic steps of turning off the phone first, removing the SIM card, and then resetting it. But there are exceptions, like the thief in the story below. Recently, an anonymous female netizen set up a blog on Tumblr, constantly updating and uploading photos of the life of the person who stole her iPhone, as well as videos.

This woman, no, she is actually the victim, was on a trip one evening to Ibiza Island in Spain. She and her three friends were all quite drunk, so much so that they took off all their clothes and ran naked towards the sea. But when they came back, they found that their belongings were gone.

Normally, the story would have ended here, but the person who took her iPhone was a bit special. This "taker" of the iPhone is named "Hafid," and he continued using it without performing any reset operations, nor did he disable the photo upload function on Dropbox. As a result, all of the thief's personal photos and videos were sent to the victim.

In fact, Hafid intentionally did not reset the phone. He even logged into the victim’s Facebook and Skype accounts to flirt with her friends... Currently, this "taker" of the iPhone, Hafid, is in Dubai. Perhaps because he thought he was far away and safe from being found, here are some of his personal photos.
