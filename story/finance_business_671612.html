by jsr86918 on 2012-02-22 11:01:23

"Molly is here, thieves please step aside!" In recent days, Miss Wang, a Shanghai citizen with the web name "Molly," has become the hotly debated "female knight" among netizens. On the evening of the 18th, two thieves were caught stealing at Yishan Road Station in Shanghai. Miss Wang used her iPad to fully document the entire crime process and bravely intervened. Taking advantage of everyone boarding the subway, one woman stole a citizen's phone. After seeing the thief succeed and attempt to leave, Miss Wang courageously stopped her. She was hit on the back of the head, and her iPad was knocked to the ground.
