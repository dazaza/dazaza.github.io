Luxurious leather goods arrive in full summer swing; the quality of luggage and bags cannot be overlooked_207

by wangquan1986 on 2012-02-18 18:36:53

Luxury leather goods arrive in the height of summer - bag quality cannot be overlooked. In early June, as the hot summer quietly approaches our lives, with the weather continuously warming up, the consumer market welcomes a new shopping craze. Currently, malls and supermarkets across the country are taking the opportunity to launch new brand advertisements. Among the numerous brand advertisements, footwear, bags, and leather goods have innovatively introduced a series of summer products that leave a stunning impression.

However, just as leather goods are entering a great opportunity for sales recovery, there have been countless reports on the internet exposing quality issues with luxury leather goods. This has led many consumers to adopt a cold attitude towards purchasing luxury leather goods this summer, resulting in a chorus of criticism and complaints.

Leather Goods Quality Exposed

On the afternoon of June 4 at around 6 PM, Ms. Xu accompanied her husband to buy a men's leather bag at a boutique in Shenzhen. However, the next day, the bag developed a problem. The receipt provided by Ms. Xu shows that the bag was priced at 6,470 yuan, but when she touched the right side of the bag with her hand, black fragments fell off. Ms. Xu told reporters that when she brought the bag back to the merchant, they denied responsibility.

On May 13, in a professional leather goods store in Changsha, Hunan, a customer bought a pair of leather shoes originally priced at 839 yuan for 571 yuan. After wearing them for less than two weeks, the shoes started showing multiple instances of glue separation and began fading, making them unwearable. Since the customer had personal matters and could not address the issue immediately, it was delayed until June 9. When the customer approached Friendship Apollo requesting a return or exchange, they unexpectedly faced refusal from the staff.

Production Quality Concerns

Currently, during the peak shopping season, many leather goods brands are rushing to get their products to market first. While everyone is focused on the new products, they fail to notice that the quality of these so-called new designs does not match the sturdiness of older bags. This greatly affects consumers' confidence in newly launched luxury items.

Today, leather goods not only serve the function of holding items but also emphasize aesthetics and individuality in matching. This has shifted the focus of leather goods development from production quality to manufacturing techniques. To cater to more vain consumers, companies produce "face-saving" products, which explains why leather goods frequently encounter quality issues in various places.

Leather goods directly connect with consumers, and any production quality problems will significantly impact the perception of leather goods among consumers. Although today's world emphasizes beautification, people pursue various image-enhancing products. However, product quality remains the hidden selection foundation. Therefore, ultimately, the distinction between good and bad leather goods still comes down to product quality.

Sales Quality Needs Improvement

Currently, sales quality is also a shortcoming in the leather goods distribution market. In supermarkets or specialized leather goods stores across the country, low-quality sales service frequently occurs, becoming one of the major factors affecting the perception of leather goods among consumers. Many times, merchants make grand promises when selling, but when problems arise, they procrastinate and hesitate. After-sales services often appear "discounted."

A sheepskin branded handbag stored in the wardrobe for a year without use developed some white spots the size of rice grains, lost a screw, and the metal parts lost their former luster. Thus, Ms. Lu decided to find a specialized handbag cleaning and maintenance shop to take care of her bag. "I didn't know until I asked, and once I did, I was shocked. Two leather goods maintenance shops quoted over a thousand yuan each. Although my bag originally cost over ten thousand yuan, after discounts, it was still about five thousand yuan. But I am hesitant—do I really need to spend enough money to buy several new bags just for handbag maintenance? What is the actual cost of maintaining a handbag?" Ms. Lu expressed her doubts.

As the saying goes, "Market demand always brings business opportunities." It is worth discussing that even though "one willing to hit and one willing to take it" is an implicit rule in business, except for real rich people, ordinary young white-collar workers who spend a month's salary on a designer bag have never existed before. Faced with maintenance costs easily exceeding a thousand yuan, these young white-collar workers feel both hatred and confusion: if they pay, the money would be enough to buy several bags; if they don't pay, the bag they spent a month's salary on would be wasted. But even if they pay and entrust the bag to a maintenance shop, no one can guarantee the results!
