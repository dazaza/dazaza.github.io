A promise of love under the five rings

by tq04fsj on 2008-04-01 11:43:40

Are you looking forward to an eternal keepsake from the year 2008 to mark your wedding in this Olympic year? The Beijing Organizing Committee has released a series of platinum wedding rings with the theme "An eternal century of Olympics". This Olympic-licensed product is for the newlyweds who are getting married as China steps onto the Olympic stage for the first time, and will be an everlasting blessing for their marriage. Featuring the ultimate Olympic achievement alongside the treasured platinum, it will celebrate a century of joy and stand as an eternal commemoration for every marriage.
