The woman shows disdain and contempt.

by dayeainic on 2012-02-08 23:05:04

Xiao Sheng was trying his best to hold her back: "Erica, how can you bear to leave?" She looked down on him: "I won't play for real with you. Only a fool like you would believe in forever." Erica picked up her luggage and left without any room for negotiation. Xiao Sheng fell to the ground, he couldn't figure out what the problem was. Xiao Xiaonan's remote control plane flew over his head, and he came out shouting: "Daddy, play a game with me!" Xiao Sheng had no mood, and bluntly refused: "My son, you play by yourself. I'm too busy dealing with this situation!" This made Xiao Xiaonan angry: "No!" Before Xiao Sheng could react, the little boy burst into tears: "I want mom! I want mom!" "Be good, Auntie Erica will be back soon." Xiao Sheng was completely lying, which is typical behavior to deceive a child. The mess that resulted was his own fault.

Xiao Sheng originally had a happy family: his wife Xu Xiumin was gentle and charming, and his son Xiao Xiaonan was clever and intelligent. But he wasn't satisfied. Ever since he met Erica, his mind has been wandering. Erica was his administrative secretary, and they had many opportunities to interact. Xiao Sheng's first sexual innuendo was not rejected by Erica - she let his hand roam freely around her waist. This lecherous boss thought he could secretly keep this subordinate as a mistress without anyone knowing.

There's nothing new under the sun. Everyone was jealous, why should Xiao Sheng enjoy all the pleasures? Based on reliable intelligence, Xu Xiumin successfully caught Xiao Sheng and Erica in bed together. What else could be said? After knowing each other for seven years and being married for six, the so-called "one night of husband and wife equals a hundred nights of grace" couldn't match Erica's allure. Divorce was inevitable. There wouldn't be a lack of female masters in this house. Xu Xiumin left, and Erica moved in.

However, things did not go as smoothly as Xiao Sheng had imagined. It's unclear if Erica was too naive or if Xiao Xiaonan was truly difficult to deal with. Conflicts between them kept escalating, causing Xiao Sheng immense headaches. On one side was his son, on the other was his lover. Both were close to him. Xiao Xiaonan treated Erica like a servant, while Erica saw Xiao Xiaonan as a big nuisance. Small arguments happened every three days, and big ones every five - Xiao Sheng was almost at his wits' end!

One day, Erica slapped Xiao Xiaonan hard. Xiao Sheng saw it, and it pained him: "You're an adult, why argue with a child?" "Do you know what your precious son did? He poured my French perfume all into the toilet!" "I'll buy you another one, it's not something crucial!" Xiao Sheng's obvious favoritism was more than Erica could bear. She regretted deeply in her heart: driving away Xu Xiumin, but what was the result?

Xiao Xiaonan, emboldened by his father's support, made faces at Erica. Wiping his tears and snot, he played with his remote control plane by himself. Flying back and forth. Erica was annoyed - being a stepmother was not easy! Xiao Sheng wouldn't blame his son because of her. So what was the point of staying? The more Erica thought about it, the less willing she became: Peter was interested in her. She was young and beautiful, why hang herself on Xiao Sheng's tree?

Leaving! Erica rushed into the room to pack up. Xiao Sheng tried to persuade her earnestly: "Dear, what's wrong?" "From today on, I'm not your dear anymore." "I love you, otherwise why would I get a divorce?" "Wrong, you love your son more." Erica didn't bother to waste words on Xiao Sheng, and left the house resolutely with her luggage. Before leaving, she confirmed: "Hey, Peter. It's me, Erica. I have nowhere to go, can I come to your place? Good!"
