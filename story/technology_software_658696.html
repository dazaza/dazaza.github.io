How beginners should optimize their websites

by hebei56cn02c on 2012-02-04 12:18:55

How can beginners optimize their websites? We can consider optimizing keywords from two major aspects: on-site optimization and off-site optimization.

1. On-site optimization mainly includes title, keywords, description, and article updates.

- Title: Generally, the relevant keywords you want to target are placed in the title, so do not alter the title position.

- Keywords: Determine the keywords based on the type of website. You can use webmaster tools to check the keyword density, backlinks, and user awareness.

- Description: Usually refers to a company introduction where you naturally embed the keywords. Keywords should appear 2-3 times in the article.

- If the website is new, it's best to create original content during the early stages of optimization and update daily. This can increase the website's weight and also improve the inclusion of articles. For older sites, you can create some pseudo-original content as the domain has already been indexed and the site will have corresponding weight.

2. Off-site optimization mainly involves posting high-quality articles on forums, blogs, and Q&A platforms. The main purpose is to allow simulated spiders to crawl these forums and blogs while also crawling your own website, which benefits your site.

How can off-site optimization be effective?

- First, register on forums, blogs, and Q&A platforms with relatively higher weight, such as Sina forum, blog, Tianya forum, blog, and Baidu Q&A, SoSo, etc.

- Secondly, write some original content for friends browsing the articles to reference or learn from. This can increase click-through rates and demonstrate the value of the article.

- Finally, establish friendly links. You can find websites with higher weight than yours to create friendly links. Remember: try to create friendly links with websites in the same industry. This is beneficial to your website and is also a way to optimize keywords. Generally, creating 2-3 friendly links per day is most appropriate.
