Top 10 Excellent App Recommendations from the First 10 Issues of PeaPod Design Awards

by ybcz0519 on 2011-11-20 19:10:27

I. Wandoujia Design Award

The Wandoujia Design Award is an initiative by Wandoujia aimed at discovering and recommending outstanding Android applications. Every week, one category of Android apps is selected, and then evaluated by well-known domestic tech blogs based on UI, creativity, usability, etc. Each week, one excellent Android application is chosen. So far, 10 excellent Android applications have been selected. The results of each selection can be viewed here: http://award.wandoujia.com/

II. Outstanding Application Recommendations

Below, I will briefly share with you the 10 excellent Android applications selected in these 10 sessions (the ranking order does not matter):

1. Youlu Contact Book

Youlu Contact Book is a free communication enhancement tool that provides smart dialing, contact grouping, group text messaging, contact synchronization, and other thoughtful features.

2. NetEase Open Courses

Integrated with over 500 courses from top universities around the world, including 2000 episodes of Chinese-translated videos. You can conveniently watch courses online on your phone or download them to your device for offline viewing, realizing your dream of "attending prestigious university open courses anytime and anywhere."

3. Dolphin Browser

Dolphin Browser HD is custom-made for the browsing experience of the smartphone 3G era. With gestures, sharing, plugins, themes, bookmark management, and multi-touch functions, it offers the perfect smartphone internet experience! New features include Dolphin Reader and web apps that will surprise your eyes.

4. On-Time Alarm Clock

Wake-up alarms, birthday reminders, game reminders, shift reminders, meeting reminders, credit card repayments, on-time notifications, countdowns, count-back days... personalized alarm templates, sweet real-person notification ringtones, various useful functions, all in the On-Time Alarm Clock!

5. Kitchen Assistant

This recipe collection isn't the most comprehensive — dog meat, cat meat... absolutely prohibited here; this isn't the most popular either — still young, take it slow; designed with love, serving loving you.

6. Cobo Input Method

Equipped with intelligent correction, intelligent full pinyin whole sentence input, handwriting filtering, patented T+ double letter layout, symbol and number input without switching, powerful intelligent English input, and more innovative designs, Cobo Input Method is undoubtedly a must-have for your phone!

7. Ji Yue

Ji Yue delivers fresh news and special stories. Convenient and simple design, truly an information powerhouse for mobile life.

8. Puding Love Life

Let Puding light up your urban life with movie information, food recommendations, premium group buying, local events, all at your fingertips.

9. GNotes

A notebook for the mobile internet era. Record bits and pieces, record life. Let life be easier, Make life easier!

10. Xinghui

Xinghui is a mobile social network based on gatherings and activities, helping you meet more friends through themed online and offline gatherings, and discover valuable people around you.

III. Voting

Which of these 10 applications do you like best? You can go here: http://award.wandoujia.com/10th/, to vote for your favorite Android application among these 10. Participants in the voting will have a chance to win prizes such as pillows and badges.
