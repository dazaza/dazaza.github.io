Souyo Reader: Read your Feed on the Couch

by loooooveu on 2007-07-14 19:55:36

Souyo is a unique and novel Chinese search engine. In short, it can help you find interesting blog posts, blog sites, and tag topics (Tags) from Chinese blogs on the Internet. In the next step, it will also collect various relevant reviews of products you care about from blogs and aggregate more valuable information for you.

Its homepage claims to instantly search 2,171,378 Chinese blogs and 32,244,835 blog posts.

Interesting features of Souyo:

# Search Posts: Blog posts are articles published daily on blogs. This search helps you find content that interests you within the text of various blogs' posts. It is also the default search option for Souyo. The following are special features that appear in the search results for blog posts:

- Link Statistics: Clicking on this allows you to see which other blogs link to or recommend the current blog post.

- Mini Reader: Try clicking it! A small window will automatically open, allowing you to preview the content of the current blog post. Ajax-based reading technology provides a smoother reading experience.

- Deep Search: Click to perform a deeper search for your current keywords within all the posts of the current blog.

- RSS Address: If you have an RSS reader installed, you can subscribe to the blog through this address.

# Search Blogs: Search personal blogs created by Chinese users on the Internet. By entering keywords, you can find blogs that match your interests based on their names, descriptions, and tags.

# Search Tags: Tags are words used by bloggers to summarize the content of their posts. Through tags, we can quickly understand the theme of a blog post. Souyo aggregates posts with the same tags to make it easier for you to search and read.

- Related Tags: Other tags related to the one you are currently searching, helping you browse more similar thematic tag posts.

# Automatic Excerpt: "Encounter Their Thoughts" and "1 Hour Fresh" on the homepage are interesting features based on Souyo's intelligent article structure analysis technology. It automatically extracts sentences from blog posts according to people's reading habits, allowing us to see more excellent sentences from Chinese blogs.

# Review Search: A feature currently under secret testing in the Souyo Lab, which may soon be available for testing. It will collect many valuable reviews for various products that interest you!
