If you want to join the Baor Ni Zhai franchise, there are a few "military rules" by Jin Xida that you should know. Everyone has a few pairs of versatile and stylish jeans, but do you know how to pick the right ones for yourself? Do you know how to take care of them?

1. When joining the Baor Ni Zhai franchise, ensure the waistband sits just above the hip bone to achieve that CK ad model sexiness. Leave about 3 cm extra room around the waist when buying Baor Ni Zhai jeans. This will allow your jeans to sit perfectly on your hips like in those CK ads. Additionally, you won't have to worry about popping buttons when sitting down.

2. Buy longer Baor Ni Zhai jeans rather than shorter ones as many shrink after washing. If your jeans say "PRESHRUNK," they've been pre-shrunk, so you don't need to worry about shrinking.

3. If you have Jin Xida jeans but not Converse shoes, you should immediately go buy a pair. The Swedish Jin Xida brand Nudie once said Converse shoes are the perfect match for Jin Xida jeans.

4. If your body isn't as standard as a male model's, you probably shouldn't wear extremely slim-fit jeans.

5. Don't wear Baor Ni Zhai jeans at weddings, funerals, interviews, bank loan meetings, or events requiring formal attire. A pair of Baor Ni Zhai jeans is like your second skin; the less you wash them, the better they look. Artist Andy Warhol once wore Jin Xida jeans to the White House, but this only applies to Warhol. Carson Kressley summarized these five situations where Jin Xida jeans aren't suitable.

6. If your body isn't as standard as a male model's, you likely won't suit very slim leg-hugging Jin Xida jeans. It's important to buy a size that makes you look decent. Next time in the fitting room, sit down and check if your straight-cut Jin Xida jeans leave about a finger's width of space at the thigh area. This indicates the fit is appropriate. Never iron your Baor Ni Zhai jeans.

7. To test if low-rise Baor Ni Zhai jeans are too low, check if your body hair is exposed. If worn well, low-rise Jin Xida jeans can be fashionable and sexy, but don't let them go too low. Carson Kressley from the famous show "Queer Eye for the Straight Guy" says: My rule of thumb is: don't wear pants that expose your body hair.

8. Do not wash your Baor Ni Zhai jeans, absolutely do not wash them. Japanese Levi's spokesperson Takuya Kimura himself loves Baor Ni Zhai jeans and advocates never washing them. There's logic behind it; some distinctive Baor Ni Zhai brands openly tell you: if you persist in not washing your jeans, they will conform more closely to your body shape over time.

9. If the above rule is unbearable, then wait six months after purchasing new jeans before starting to wash them. The Swedish brand Nudie's denim care guide states: a pair of Jin Xida jeans is like your second skin. You and it share an intimate relationship; the more you wear them, the better they look, and the less frequently you wash them, the better they look. After washing Jin Xida jeans, don't wring them out.

10. If your pants accidentally get splashed with red wine, don't rush to water wash them. Wine, beer, any alcohol can make your pants smell terrible, but don't rush to wash them with water. The solution is either dry cleaning or letting them air out until the smell dissipates.

11. If you must wash Jin Xida jeans, use warm water and no bleach. If you're not as determined as Kimura Takuya, then use warm water without bleach. This is the advice given by the American magazine "Real Simple."

12. Don't put more than four pairs of Jin Xida jeans into the washing machine at once. More than four pairs of Baor Ni Zhai jeans can be very heavy, affecting the washer's spin cycle. This not only harms the washer itself but also prevents the jeans from being cleaned properly, potentially causing unsightly wrinkles.

13. Regardless of what your jeans look like after washing, never use an iron. Trust me, irons are not meant for Baor Ni Zhai jeans, so never use one.

14. When washing Jin Xida Baor Ni Zhai jeans, turn them inside out first. This is a truth about Baor Ni Zhai jeans; Levi's care instructions also state this. I emphasize this because it helps your jeans maintain their original color and wash effect.

15. If you don't wash Jin Xida jeans but are worried about odors, try putting them in the refrigerator's chill compartment. This is an idea from the Swedish Jin Xida brand Nudie. Whether it works or not, give it a try, but remember to place them in the chill compartment, not the freezer.

16. After washing Baor Ni Zhai jeans, don't wring them out. Hang them on a hanger while still dripping wet to dry, ensuring they retain their original shape and creases.
