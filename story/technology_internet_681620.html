Basic knowledge of hairdressing, makeup, and basic care

by king5c5t on 2012-02-28 14:30:55

Learn basic nursing well, let you have a more confident appearance, no need to panic, show the best state, and welcome every new day!

Basic nursing refers to the care preparation that the bride makes in order to welcome the arrival of the wedding. Clothes are of course important, but hairdressing, makeup, and nursing should also be valued so that you can truly become a beautiful bride in your wedding dress.

Hairdressing, Makeup, and Basic Nursing Implementation Plan

In order to appear in the best condition on the wedding day, you should make a plan and start changing yourself. Don't get caught up in preparations close to the wedding and end up rushing around. You should schedule everything in advance.

Almost all brides have experts do their hair and makeup. Generally, the hairdressing and makeup for the bride at the wedding are entrusted to experts. This can be done at the beauty room of the venue or by hiring a hairdressing salon with door-to-door service.

Facial and Body Care

Generally, this involves beautifying the skin on the face, chest, back, and hands. Some salons even offer special wedding beauty services.

Face Shaving

Use a razor to shave the sweat hairs on the back of the neck, chest, and face, which makes it easier to apply makeup. It's quite troublesome to handle it yourself, so you can request help from a beauty salon.

Nail Polish

The majority of wedding photos include the scene of exchanging rings, so nails must be well maintained. If you apply nail polish and add some patterns, it will look even more splendid.

6~4 Months Before the Wedding

Decide on the desired salon and make an appointment.

Start proper basic care. If there are any skin issues, it's best to see a dermatologist. Select a salon, consult after making an appointment.

3~2 Months Before the Wedding

After deciding on the wedding dress, consider what kind of hairstyle and makeup would be suitable.

Make an appointment for a pre-wedding rehearsal and conduct detailed negotiations.

Start going to the beauty salon. Acne on the back should be handled early.

Start taking care of your nails so they will be very beautiful by the time of the wedding.

1 Month Before the Wedding

If possible, bring along your wedding dress and accessories for the rehearsal.

Start facial care, including facial massage and mask care for smooth and white skin.

Shave sweat hairs carefully, including the back of the neck, as this will make the updo more striking.

Use a file to shape the nails, cut off the hangnails, apply nail polish, and finally draw patterns.

One Month Before the Wedding

Confirm the check-in time and specific content with the makeup artist on the day of the wedding. Also confirm with the venue at the same time.

Hand care should be done before the wedding.

Sweat hair trimming.

Apply nail polish and draw patterns ideally the day before the wedding.
