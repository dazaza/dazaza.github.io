The protagonist Wang Luodan in the film was once rumored to be a chatterbox, but in the film "Unmanned Driving", she played a deaf-mute girl named Li Xin. She met Li Jia, played by Huang Xuan, by chance. From then on, they started a stimulating, funny, and sincere love story. When Wang Luodan talked about the character Li Xin on the scene, she showed that although this character had no lines, her emotional expression was very special, more straightforward and sincere.

China Film News: On the afternoon of June 24th, the modern urban love film "Unmanned Driving" directed by Zhang Yang held a grand premiere ceremony in Beijing. The main actors such as Wang Luodan, Liu Ye, Li Xiaoran, Huang Xuan, Lin Xinru, and Zhang Yan were all present. Wang Luodan made an elegant appearance in a light pink dress. She not only talked about her own view of love on the scene, but also sang the theme song of the film "The Love of Unmanned Driving", which became one of the highlights of the day's celebration.

In the following self-disclosure of the love concept, Wang Luodan, as a representative of the younger generation of post-80s, said: The love of post-80s seems to be simpler, dare to love and hate, although there are more impetuous things and less realistic things, but if you really fall in love with someone, you will express it fearlessly.
