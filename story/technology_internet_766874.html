Baidu technology VP Wang Mengqiu reported to leave, Liu Jun might take over

by anonymous on 2013-10-01 22:26:51

Baidu's Vice President of Technology, Wang Mengqiu, Resigns

On September 25th, it was reported that Baidu’s Vice President of Technology, Wang Mengqiu, has recently resigned. Liu Jun, the founder of Yunyun.com, is likely to take over Wang Mengqiu's position. Tencent Technology contacted Wang Mengqiu, who stated that she cannot comment on this matter at the moment and everything will be clarified once Baidu officially announces the news. Baidu's official side refused to comment on this matter, and there was no internal email sent within Baidu to announce this news.

This is not the first time rumors about Wang Mengqiu's resignation have surfaced. As early as December last year, there were reports suggesting that Wang Mengqiu might leave her post. Coincidentally, Liu Jun has recently officially joined Baidu as the company's Vice President of Technology, responsible for the operations of Baidu's Technical Strategy Committee (TSC), supporting the development and innovation of key Baidu businesses.

In terms of business responsibilities, Wang Mengqiu mainly managed the Search Development Department, Vertical & Community Search Product Marketing Department, User Experience Department, Community Search Department, Web Search Product Marketing Department, Tieba Business Division, Baidu Music Business Division, and Operations Department at Baidu. Wang Mengqiu's work was closely related to social search.

Meanwhile, Liu Jun has been a strong advocate of social search. The website he founded, Yunyun.com, is dedicated to combining social networks with search engines. Yunyun's microblog search can simultaneously search through the four major domestic microblogging platforms, filling the gap in social information in internet searches. In 2013, Yunyun launched a mobile advertising platform that integrates social information, achieving precise targeting of mobile ads using social information.

External observers believe that Liu Jun's joining may imply taking over Wang Mengqiu's role, and they expect Liu Jun to help Baidu further strengthen search technology innovation in the era of mobile internet, providing more beneficial ideas for the commercialization of Baidu's mobile search. When asked by Tencent Technology, Liu Jun avoided discussing the matter but mentioned that the public would soon know about his work at Baidu.

Wang Mengqiu's Resume:

Wang Mengqiu, female, born in August 1975, holds a master's degree and has worked in research and management in the internet industry for many years. She joined Baidu in November 2002 and successively served as the Manager of the Baidu Portal Search Department, Baidu Technology Director, Senior Technology Director, and Vice President of Technology at Baidu.

In July 1997, she graduated from Peking University's Computer Science Department with a bachelor's degree.

In 2001, she graduated from the Computer Science Department of the University of California, Los Angeles (UCLA) with a master's degree.

From June 2001 to October 2002, Wang Mengqiu worked as a software engineer at ABAQOS Communications in California, USA, engaged in research and development work related to routers, LAN switches, and routing protocols.

In November 2002, she was invited back to China to join Baidu as a senior engineer specializing in search and network technologies. She participated in the design and development of numerous projects, serving as project manager, technical manager, senior technical manager, technology director, and senior technology director. She also took charge of a large amount of management work related to user product marketing.

In January 2011, she was promoted to Vice President of Baidu, responsible for overseeing the Search Development Department, Vertical & Community Search Product Marketing Department, User Experience Department, Community Search Department, Web Search Product Marketing Department, Tieba Business Division, Baidu Music Business Division, and Operations Department.
