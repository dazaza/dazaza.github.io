Google Glass Appears on eBay Auction: Bidding Exceeds $90,000

by geekzhang on 2013-05-03 13:21:23

Someone is auctioning Google Glass on eBay, and the bid has exceeded $90,000April 24th morning Beijing time - According to a report by American tech blog Engadget, even though Google's wearable smart device, Google Glass (GoogleGlass), may not be officially released for another year, someone has already started an auction for it on eBay, with bids surpassing $90,000.It’s clear that Google Glass has already generated a lot of anticipation among many people. However, unless they are developers or winners of the Google Glass event, they will have to wait until the official release to experience the endless fun of this product. Nevertheless, if one is wealthy enough, there is a chance to bid for Google Glass on the eBay website, but even if successful, they still cannot obtain it immediately.Currently, the bidding price for this pair of Google Glasses has surpassed $90,000, but the seller claims they are just lucky participants in the Google Glass event, needing a few weeks to receive their prize. There is no reason to doubt the seller's behavior because they have been an eBay member since 2004, and all buyers give them 100% positive feedback regarding their service. Therefore, they would never take any risk that could ruin their reputation.However, Google might be unhappy about this move. Clearly, the company does not welcome developers reselling or gifting the Google Glass Explorer Edition. It is expected that the Glass event should also have such regulations. Thus, some believe that Google will intervene.Sure enough. According to the latest news, this pair of Google Glasses has already been removed from eBay. Some speculate that this was the result of negotiations between Google and the seller. However, the seller gave their own reasons: they were unsettled by the massive attention caused by the auction and were dissatisfied with those who kept raising the bid just for fun.
