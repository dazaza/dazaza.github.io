Guoxin Securities: Growth of the sewage business of Bluestar Cleaning is expected to accelerate＿Stock Introduction

by dx0957095 on 2009-12-03 18:34:34

Item: Stock Basics

December 1, 2009, after review by the Mergers and Acquisitions Examination Committee of the China Securities Regulatory Commission (CSRC), the major asset swap and issuance of shares to purchase assets related to BlueStar Cleaning were conditionally approved. A separate announcement will be made once the company receives relevant approval documents from the CSRC.

Comment:

The reorganization is basically complete. From the release of the reorganization draft in June to the approval on December 1, the entire reorganization process has proceeded smoothly. Currently, there are still two steps remaining:

(1) Exemption from tender offer obligations. Since Xingrong Company holds 52.3% of the company's equity, exceeding 30%, it still requires CSRC approval for exemption from its tender offer obligation.

(2) Asset delivery. We maintain the "optimistic estimate that asset delivery will be completed within the year," based on (1) both parties' determination to restructure and support from all sides, as it took less than a month from the release of the reorganization draft to the submission of materials; (2) Based on experience, under normal circumstances, the exemption from tender offer obligations will be approved within one month after passing the review, and the asset delivery matters will be confirmed. At that time, the entire reorganization process will be completed.

Taking a key step forward, the growth of the sewage business is expected to accelerate. The company will become the leading sewage treatment company in Southwest China, owning eight sewage plants of Chengdu Drainage Company (with approximately 1.3 million cubic meters/day capacity), responsible for sewage treatment in Chengdu's central urban area, with clear assets. With the basic completion of the reorganization, we believe that the company will shift its focus to increasing the growth of the sewage business, which will significantly promote the resolution of pipeline bottlenecks and the intensification of external expansion. The probability of achieving a "10%-15% annual growth" in sewage treatment volume increases, and the performance growth rate in the next two years is expected to exceed expectations, reaching about 20% growth.

Maintain the "Recommend" rating

Basically, the end of the year can be taken as a node, and almost all the profits in 2010 will come from newly injected assets. Without considering subsequent asset injections and sludge projects, the diluted EPS for 2010 and 2011 will be 0.50 and 0.60 yuan respectively, with the current stock price at 12.3 yuan, placing the valuation at the bottom of the entire industry (the PE level in 2010 is close to 40), providing a high safety margin. Meanwhile, Xingrong Company's development is both internal and external (1) located in Chengdu, with an undeniable promoting effect on water usage and sewage treatment due to the accelerated urbanization speed in recent years; (2) government support, which can be seen somewhat throughout the entire reorganization process; (3) operational strength and leadership position in the Chengdu waterworks market, whether it's sewage or tap water, facing significant expansion space. Maintain the company's "Recommend" rating.

The procedures for buying and selling stocks are as follows: When buying or selling stocks, you must specify whether you are buying or selling, the name (or code) of the stock, quantity, and price. Furthermore, this order is only valid for the day on which the order is placed. The content of the order includes the abbreviation (code) of the stock you want to buy or sell, the quantity, and the price at which you want to buy or sell the stock. The abbreviation of the stock usually consists of three to four Chinese characters, while the stock code is six digits long. When entrusting a trade, the stock code and abbreviation must match. Additionally, there are certain regulations regarding the quantity of stocks being traded: the quantity for entrusting to buy stocks must be a whole multiple of 100, but the quantity for entrusting to sell stocks does not have to be a whole multiple of 100.

There are four methods of entrustment: counter submission entrustment, telephone automatic entrustment, computer automatic entrustment, and remote terminal entrustment.
