Love on the Iceberg

A long time ago, there was a man named Liu Jun and a woman named Bingru.

They lived in an ice village. They loved each other dearly, had been married for 20 years, and led a very happy life.

Liu Jun would go hunting during the day while Bingru stayed home to do housework. In the evening when Liu Jun returned from hunting, Bingru would have dinner ready for him... Every night, Liu Jun would drink the soup that his beloved wife Bingru made for him... As he drank the soup every night, he would look at Bingru and say: "Thank you, Bingru, you've worked hard! The soup is delicious, it's really a happiness to drink the soup you make..." Bingru would smile at Liu Jun and say: "I'm also happy being with you..."

And so, they continued to live this simple life, without suspicion, without arguments, leading a happy life. People in the village were all envious...

One day, as Liu Jun prepared to go hunting, Bingru also wanted to follow along to collect medicinal herbs. However, Liu Jun didn't allow Bingru to go because it was dangerous; there might be a blizzard.

Liu Jun said: "I'll collect the herbs for you."

Bingru said to Liu Jun: "Do you even know what the herbs look like? With you around, I don't need to be afraid of anything."

Liu Jun helplessly said: "Alright, alright, let's go. Wear more clothes."

Liu Jun took his beloved wife Bingru up the icy mountain. Liu Jun went hunting while Bingru collected medicinal herbs nearby. Suddenly, a bear attacked Liu Jun. Liu Jun quickly dragged his wife away, running into a small cave entrance. The bear kept guarding the entrance, and Liu Jun's left hand was injured by the bear's claws. Seeing the blood flowing, Bingru cried and tore off a small piece of her clothing to bandage Liu Jun. It seemed as if heaven was testing them; the weather changed, and a heavy blizzard started. The bear slowly left. Liu Jun saw the bear leave and was about to take Bingru away, but it was already dark, and the blizzard was too strong. They decided to stay in the cave for the night.

Liu Jun's friend, Wei, wanted to find Liu Jun and Bingru to have dinner at his house.

When Wei arrived at Liu Jun's home, the lights were off. Wei knocked on the door for a long time outside. Seeing such a heavy blizzard, Wei wondered... Could something have happened to Liu Jun on the icy mountain?

Wei quickly gathered all the villagers, asking if anyone had seen Liu Jun. The villagers said they hadn't, but suddenly an old man who had never been seen before said: "I saw the couple climb onto the ice today."

Wei and the villagers became worried and all climbed up the icy mountain together to search for Liu Jun and Bingru. By then, the blizzard was extremely strong. Liu Jun and Bingru were still in the cave. Liu Jun saw Bingru shivering and falling asleep, so he took off his coat and gave it to Bingru. Despite his pain, he tightly hugged Bingru with his body. Soon, Bingru stopped shivering and slowly fell asleep. Liu Jun smiled as he looked at the sleeping Bingru, kissed her, and said to her as she slept: "Is this fate? If so, I hope we can be husband and wife again in the next life, Bingru, I love you!"

Tears fell from Liu Jun's eyes, freezing instantly. He also fell asleep. All the villagers from the ice village were searching for the couple. Wei noticed the traces of a bear attack near a small cave and ran up with a few villagers. They found Liu Jun wearing only one layer of clothing, tightly hugging Bingru even in death. Wei brought the couple back to the village and rushed to save Liu Jun, but unfortunately...

When Bingru woke up and saw herself wearing Liu Jun's clothes, she asked Wei where Liu Jun was. Wei pointed to the frozen Liu Jun, and Bingru was stunned. She hugged Liu Jun's body tightly and cried, hoping to warm him with her own body. Bingru cried as she held Liu Jun's corpse, saying it wasn't real. Why did you leave me? Why does heaven do this? Wake up, please! You haven't tasted the soup I made today!

Wei and the villagers standing beside them also cried. Such a loving couple was now separated by heaven and earth.

Wei said to Bingru: "When we found you in the cave, Liu Jun had given you his clothes and was hugging you tightly..."

Bingru cried and said: "I'd rather go with Liu Jun than have him leave me behind."

Wei said to Bingru: "Don't think about doing something foolish. Brother Liu Jun is gone, that's a fact."

He wanted you to live well, otherwise, he wouldn't have used his body to keep you warm.

That night, the entire village was quiet. Bingru waited outside the door every day, making soup every night, hoping to see Liu Jun's smile when he came home. This wait lasted for 10 years... 20 years... until the moment of death...

Bingru never loved anyone else and waited outside the door until she grew old and passed away.

Let us linger at the edge of the cycle of rebirth, waiting for a ray of light. In 2008, Liu Jun and Bingru met again. This time, Liu Jun was named Liu Fulong, and Bingru was named Lai Wenjing. But they had forgotten who each other was, just colleagues in the same company, yet they ended up together again.

Our love has followed for half a century. I am still waiting outside the house, watching the snow, waiting for you to come back. Finally, I've waited for you!

Do you believe in reincarnation? Perhaps the person you're with now is the lover from the last century who sacrificed their life for you. Cherish this happiness well!
