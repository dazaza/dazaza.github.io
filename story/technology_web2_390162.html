Step One: Register as a Taobao Member

(1) Log in to Taobao, click on "Free Registration" at the top of the page;

(2) Enter the registration page, fill in the username and password;

(3) Input an email address you use frequently to activate your membership name;

(4) Enter the verification code in the input box on the right;

(5) Carefully read the Taobao service agreement, agree to the terms, and click submit;

(6) At this point, Taobao will send a confirmation email to the email address you just entered;

(7) Log in to that email account to complete your Taobao membership registration.

(8) Remember your username and password. This is equivalent to the key to your physical store.

Step Two: Apply for Alipay Real-name Certification

To open a store, you must pass Taobao's Alipay real-name certification, linking your Alipay, store, and identity together, giving your store and Alipay legitimacy.

First, register an Alipay account. When you register as a Taobao member, Taobao automatically gives you an Alipay account. In the lower-right corner of the successful registration page, the system provides you with a linked Alipay account, which uses the email address you registered with Taobao as the account name (you can also bind your own email as the Alipay account).

Second, apply for Alipay real-name certification. Log in to Taobao, click on "My Taobao" at the top, under "Welcome XXX, humble servant awaits!" click on "Want to sell items? First, perform Alipay certification, please click here," enter the certification page, or log into your Alipay account, click on the "Unverified" prompt on the "My Alipay" homepage, and follow the certification process. First, the steps to open an online store: Step One: Preparations for opening an online store

1. Find a good source. Note: Sources are divided into virtual sources and physical sources. Finding sources is divided into self-purchasing for resale and consignment sales. Among these, virtual sources do not require logistics, generally being your own source. Physical sources require logistics distribution, and sources can either be self-purchased for resale or consignment sales. You can start with virtual and then physical, or do both simultaneously, or just do physical, depending on your source situation, with different investments for different sources. For example, with virtual sources, around 300 to 600 yuan should suffice.

2. Prepare a second-generation ID card and a bank card with online banking enabled. Note: Take clear photos of the front and back of your ID card and upload them to your computer. Install the matching certificate driver for online banking on your computer.

3. Different sources require different startup funds. For example, with virtual sources, approximately 300 to 600 yuan is enough.

Step Two, find an online store platform.

There are many online store platforms available, with three of the most well-known being Taobao, Youa, and Paipai. One is under Alibaba, one under Baidu, and one under Tencent. The ranking of registered member numbers is Taobao > Youa > Paipai. Personally, I suggest doing Taobao. You can also consider Paipai and Youa.

Step Three, after finding a suitable online store platform, you can register as a member on the platform.

Note: Registering as a member and opening a store are free and do not incur any fees. Beware of scammers using registration to defraud money, always be cautious. Taking Taobao as an example, after registering as a member, you also need to activate the associated Alipay account, which is a payment platform ensuring online transactions.

Step Four, after registering as a member, you can perform real-name certification.

Note: During real-name certification, you will need your ID card and a bank card with online banking enabled under your ID card name. A second-generation ID card has two sides, both needing clear photos. Taking Taobao as an example, the certification time is approximately 2-3 days.

Step Five: After passing certification, you can upload items.

Note: If you're doing consignment sales, whether physical or virtual, your merchant or manufacturer will provide you with a data package. Taking Taobao stores as an example, you only need to use Taobao Assistant to upload all the data. This data is the information about the items you are selling. Professional virtual software agents. Virtual software agents do not require logistics and are relatively easy. If you purchase items yourself, you will need to contact logistics companies, take photos of the items, describe the items, and then upload them to your store.

Taking Taobao stores as an example, if the number of uploaded items reaches more than 10, you can open a free store.

Step Six, store setup and decoration.

After the store is open, you can proceed with setup and decoration. Sellers of virtual goods need to be vigilant against fraud at this point because I do virtual goods and was scammed out of 1300 yuan when I didn't know better. Don't rush into transactions until the store is decorated properly.

Note: Taking Taobao as an example, sellers with credits below one diamond can upgrade to become users of the supported version of Wangpu for free, so there's no need to waste money buying the standard version of Wangpu. After all, costs need to be reduced! There are many ways to decorate, and I have many tutorials and materials prepared for my agents. A well-decorated store shows that you care and are professional.

Step Seven, understand the transaction process.

Note: Virtual sellers especially need to understand this part. Many virtual sellers ship goods before the buyer pays, resulting in losing both money and goods.

Taking Taobao as an example, from the seller's side: Buyer places order -- Buyer pays -- "Sold Items" shows "Buyer Has Paid" -- Seller ships goods -- Buyer confirms receipt -- Evaluates buyer.
