Sword Network 3 fastest level up — 70th level newbie account in 11 hours

by 173xsk on 2011-03-06 08:18:11

First of all, I want to clarify that this is not me. I can't level up so fast. I reached level 70 with someone else's help. Occasionally, I came across a good article and wanted to share it with old players who are preparing to level up their secondary characters. Beginners should still follow the tasks (173 Newbie Card Home).

Main character is Wanhua, two internal Chunyaang, and a friend's Qixiu account. Dual-boxing throughout: one main character, one secondary character to be leveled up. Single-person operation.

Preparations: Xuanjiu Pills, Panlong Dumplings, double experience books, experience books, and an on-call account for pushing QuGu.

Levels 1-10: Daoxiang Village, following a high-level character takes about 15 minutes, then head to Yangzhou.

Levels 10-12: The main character trades 1WG to the secondary character, instantly completed.

Levels 12-15: Newbie village quests, around half an hour (but recently I had another idea, which is for the main character to prepare experience books and let the secondary character read them directly in the newbie village. However, I have been busy with battlegrounds lately and haven't had time to try it out, so I don't know if it will work).

Levels 15-22: CJ map, there is a repeatable quest below Jianzhong. Brush those monsters, if no one is brushing, push QuGu, then eat Xuanjiu Pills. My two internal Chunyaang can complete it in less than an hour.

Levels 22-28: This is my least favorite stage. Every time, I go to brush all the small monsters in the ordinary Sancai area to complete the quests. It takes about one and a half to two hours. I always feel there is a faster way, but I haven't thought of it yet.

Levels 28-36: Ordinary Lingxiaoxia is very fast. On average, you can level up by two levels each time, clearing all the small monsters in about ten minutes. My route every time starts from the entrance and pulls all the way to Boss 1 for AoE, then pulls all the monsters before Boss 2 together for AoE, kill Boss 2 alone, then pull all the small monsters after that including Boss 3 together for AoE. Using Chunyaang is very efficient.

Levels 36-44: Ordinary Wuyan Island, the secondary character waits at the entrance while the main character walks all the way to Boss 1, pulling about 50 monsters including Boss 1 to the entrance for AoE, taking about three minutes. Each time, you can gain a little more than one level. Then teleport out, leave the group, if the main character was grouped with the secondary character before, then switch roles this time, enter again, and repeat the cycle. PS: Chunyaang might take some damage, it's recommended to bring some red potions and activate Baihui just in case. If using Qixiu, it would be much easier.

Levels 44-48: Ordinary Dihua Palace, because my two internals are Chunyaang and I don't have ZhenShanHe, I gave up on Boss 2 (actually got defeated several times). My route is to clear the small monsters and boss from the entrance to Boss 1 in one go, then pull all the monsters from the mountain path including the madmen on the mountaintop and the small monsters guarding the pillars down to the platform below Boss 2 for AoE once, then kill the small monsters on the walkway before Boss 2 alone (here I want to explain why I didn't pull them directly to Boss 2, if pulled directly, the small monsters would lose aggro).

Pull the small monsters between Boss 2 and Boss 3 together with Boss 3 and the nine monsters in front for AoE. Then jump off the mountain and reset the dungeon to re-enter. If using three doubles, only twice is needed. Each time takes about 12 minutes.

Levels 48-55: Ordinary Daoxiang Village, first go to Jing Shi Tang to claim the daily half-hour double experience, then enter the dungeon. In Daoxiang Village, Chunyaang can hardly carry, it's easy to get killed by small monsters, so I switched to my friend's Hero Warbao graduated Qixiu to carry. At the entrance, the secondary character hides behind a tree behind Old Wang, people who have cleared Daoxiang Village at level 50 should know this spot, we used to card monsters here. Starting from the left side of the road to Old 1, pull all the cavalry to abandon the infantry because the infantry easily loses aggro here, pull the three waves of cavalry at Old 1, then walk back. There's a quest NPC in the middle, whose monsters can also be pulled.

After pulling, open acceleration, pull the cavalry at the entrance of the terraced fields and the first layer of cavalry together, then run towards Old Wang. Along the way, there are several waves of infantry that can also be pulled without losing aggro. Remember, stay far away from the secondary character, otherwise they will be instantly killed by the monsters' AoE. Basically, just within the distance where you can activate your array. Then AoE them. I don't know how many monsters can be pulled at once, but my sword spirit Huanyu's DPS is around 300k. Each time takes about four minutes, the secondary character can basically ensure leveling up by one level each time. After AoE, exit the dungeon, switch leaders, and re-enter like in Wuyan Island.

Levels 55-60: Longmen Loulan Ancient City, if you meet Old Xie, follow him to massacre the city, if not, brush the 80k HP monsters yourself. But there's a trick here. First, group the main and secondary characters, the secondary character hits first to confirm hitting, reduce monster's HP, then the main character immediately grabs the hate and leaves the group. Solo the monster, this way the experience is double what you would get if grouped with two people. Four doubles are about 15k per monster. I use Wanhua to fight, basically able to hold until the end, each monster takes 40 seconds to 1 minute.

Levels 60-70: Kunlun Changsheng Cave, there's an elite monster with 30k HP. If possible, it's best to meditate for two hours of double experience. School double, QuGu, double experience book, dumplings, pills, this is five kinds of double. I use Wanhua to fight, averaging less than 20 seconds per monster, each monster gives 6000 experience. The trick here is that there are two stationary monsters at the entrance, repeatedly killing these two, after brushing dozens of times, the spawn rate will be faster than your attack speed. Basically, if you persist without resting in the middle, two hours is enough to bring your secondary character to level 70.

Thank you to the friend who shared this. Those interested can try it out when bored. Selling accounts can also make money, ^_^
