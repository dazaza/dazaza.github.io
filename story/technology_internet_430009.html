I have a client who is in his sixties. He is now the president of a real estate company with assets exceeding hundreds of millions. Because I've known him since he wasn't wealthy, we speak very casually. Every time I see him, he says, "You're getting more beautiful," and I respond, "You're getting more handsome." One day, he said that my car was low-end and I worked too hard. If I wanted to change my situation, I could immediately have a nice car and house. I told him, "Although the four wheels are old, they can still protect me from wind and rain. Although making money is hard work, it's all sweat and blood. I like this kind of simple life." Another day, he said that although I am beautiful, I'm not approachable. I replied, "Although you're good-looking, you're a bit old."

I have another client who is in his thirties. He is a top figure in his industry, spending millions on advertising each year. He is very arrogant. I know both him and his wife. When I do business with him, he often hints at me. After seeing that I don't respond, his friend tells me: "His relationship with his wife isn't good, and they are divorcing. Now there's a long queue of girls chasing after him. If you want, you don't need to wait in line." I told his friend, "No matter what, there should be an order. I am willing to wait in line. If someone cuts in front of me, I will move further back." This rich man, after the contract ended, owed me for over a year without settling the account. I got so angry that I removed his advertisement. Later, when there was no other way, he finally paid me. There were several similar situations where they were initially very nice but later refused to settle the account. I kept thinking, if I had followed their wishes, would my money have been easier to collect?

The other day, while I was walking on the street, a man driving a BMW stopped beside me and rolled down the window to offer to drive me home. I've encountered such situations many times before, which always puzzled me. If I were a seventeen or eighteen-year-old girl, this would be easy to understand, but I'm already thirty years old. Furthermore, familiar people expressing affection means they know you. You walk on the street, and suddenly someone expresses concern; who knows what they really intend. After refusing, I asked him, "Do I look like a bad person?" What I meant was whether he thought I looked like a prostitute, which is why he dared to express himself so boldly. He didn't answer but instead asked me if I thought he looked like a bad person. I said, "You absolutely look like a good person, but I like bad ones." He then asked if I was married. I replied, "Not married, just an old maid." He muttered, "Old? But are you still a girl?" I said, "Oh, you think I'm old and can't walk, so you're offering to drive me home? Thank you, Comrade Lei Feng, but I can still walk." Then I walked away.

There's another client who is also in his thirties. When signing the contract, his conditions were extremely harsh and unacceptable. After giving one condition, he asked for another. When he made another request, I was about to get angry but couldn't. So, I sulkily said, "Then I'll just give you a wife." Everyone laughed, and the contract was signed. However, when I went to settle the account after completing the project, he said I owed him a wife, and wouldn't pay. No matter how many times I asked, he used the same excuse. One day, right after their meeting, with many people around, I again asked to settle the account. He again said I owed him a wife. I said, "Alright, I'll introduce you to two women." He said one was enough, and he didn't need that many. I said, "One older woman bringing a younger one, buy one get one free. If you don't accept, it's fine." He said, "Then you might as well introduce me to a pockmarked woman." I said, "A pockmarked woman is better than a pretty fifty-year-old." He said, "Then you might as well introduce me to an eighty-year-old." I said, "If you marry an eighty-year-old, you'll immediately have a bunch of grandchildren, becoming a grandfather without effort. Moreover, every night you'll hug an eighty-year-old grandma, drinking expired milk. If you get poisoned, how will I explain it?" Everyone laughed, and he finally issued the transfer check.

There is a boy in his early twenties, much younger than me, not very familiar. He sent me text messages every day. Seeing that I ignored him, he finally told me he was a virgin. If I was willing, we could meet immediately. This put me in a difficult position. After hesitating for a while, I told him, "Be careful these days. Don't lose your innocence yet. I'll discuss it with my husband and reply later."

Recently, I bought a new Honda Accord with the highest configuration. Although it's inferior compared to Mercedes-Benz and BMW, compared to my previous car, it's a significant upgrade. I feel great driving it, and others who ride in it also say it's nice. One day, I went out with two colleagues. When they got into the car, one colleague sighed, "Riding in this car makes me want to marry you." After traveling a long distance and finishing our errands, the two insisted on treating me to dinner. Unable to refuse, I agreed. During the meal, they told me that they had discussed it and were willing for one to be my second husband and the other to be my third. They promised they would never argue and asked for my opinion. I was speechless, staring at them for a long time before saying, "The tall one is too tall, and the short one is too short. Starting today, go home and cultivate yourselves. When the tall one shrinks to 1.7 meters and the short one grows to 1.8 meters, I'll take you in." The tall one was dissatisfied and said, "It's fine for the short one to grow taller, but why does the tall one have to shrink?"

I said, "The short one is too fat, so growing to 1.8 meters will be more proportionate. The tall one is too thin, so shrinking to 1.7 meters will be more appropriate."

The tall one said, "You're being picky." I said, "Things that come easily generally aren't good, so of course, I have to choose."

The tall one said, "So things that don't come easily are good?"

I said, "No, things that come by chance are the best."

The other night, I placed a glass of water on the bedside table, intending to drink it if I got thirsty in the middle of the night. Just before dawn, my husband picked up the cup and took a sip, saying, "Why does this water taste strange?" I said, "Nothing's wrong, it's the water I poured." My husband took another sip and said, "What tea bag did you use? Why is it so swollen?" I was surprised and said, "I didn't put any tea bags in." "Then what is this?" My husband handed me the cup.
