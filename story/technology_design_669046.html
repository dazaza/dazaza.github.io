Star 97 troubleshooting

by gzqldz3b5y on 2012-02-18 20:32:19

Star 97 Fault Handling:

1. No image output, but still operable:

(1) U17 fault.

(2) U18 fault.

(3) U48 fault.

2. No beauty image, otherwise normal:

(1) U17 fault.

(2) C945 transistor fault.

3. No sound:

(1) 8910 fault.

(2) Audio amplifier fault.

4. Unable to connect:

(1) Edge card fault.

(2) U49 74LS245 fault.

(3) Connection wire broken.

5. Connector card fault:

(1) Common faulty components on the connector card are: 1) U1 8031; 2) U6 27128; 3) E2 PC817.

6. Keys not functioning:

(1) 82C255.

(2) Integrator circuit fault.

7. Unable to reset:

(1) Usually due to users not knowing that the reset switch must be operated in the powered-on state to reset.

(2) 82C255 fault.

(3) Reset switch fault.

8. Wrinkling on the screen:

(1) 74LS283 fault.

(2) 6116 fault.

(3) XC2064 fault.

9. DIP SWITCH setting failure:

(1) 8910 fault.

(2) 82C255 fault.

10. Screen jitter during scrolling:

(1) 6116 fault.

(2) 74LS245 fault.
