Fu Xinbo went to Korea to record "Let's Get Married", Korean-style wedding photos exposed

by zcs1272 on 2012-02-21 11:24:00

"We Got Married" is one of the most popular variety shows in South Korea in 2011, and it has a certain amount of popularity both domestically and internationally. It's a celebrity imaginary marriage show. Several pairs of celebrities pretend to be newlyweds, moving their luggage over to decorate their "newlywed room". First season guests: Andy and Solbi, He Xiaowang, Alex and Shin Ae, Jung Hyung-don and Saori, Crown J and Seo Yoon-young, Lee Hwi-jae and Cho Yeon-joo, Kim Hyun-jung and Huang Fu, Fany and Hua Yao Fei, Marco and Sun Dan Fei, Kang Si and Li Yun Zhi, Jung Hyung-don and Kim Tae Yeon, Jun Jin and Lee Si Young, Shenyang Wedding Network, Shin Sung Rok and Kim Shin Young. Second season guests: Kim Yong Jun and Hwang Jung-eum. Cho Gwon and Sun Ga Ren, Jung Yong Hwa and Su Hyun, Nichkhun and Victoria. Third season new guests: Han Eun Jung and Lee Jang Woo, Kim Won Joon and Park Shi Hyeon, Shenyang Marriage Network, Leeteuk and Kang So Ra. Among them, the most familiar to Chinese audiences are the international couples from the third season, the "Vini couple" (2PM's Nichkhun and f(x)'s Song Qian). Recently, Fu Xin Bo, who went solo from the Bobo group, traveled to South Korea to join the popular Korean program "We Got Married", forming a "couple" with Park Hyo Min from the Korean T-ara group. This pretend couple recently took a set of beautiful Korean-style wedding photos outdoors after dating for only a short time. What twists or romantic events will happen next for this language-barrier-crossing "international couple"? It's truly something to look forward to.
