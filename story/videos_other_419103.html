How do these internet experts learn from a teacher? At the beginning, they were probably novices just like everyone else. How did they learn when they started?

Let me share the four-step learning process. This four-step method applies to any industry, and these secrets have been used to the extreme by successful people or even reached a high level. Everyone is eager to know what they are: First, full acceptance; Second, full denial; Third, optimized absorption; Fourth, enhanced thinking. This is a general explanation, and each step contains deep truths that not everyone can easily understand. I will briefly explain them here. First, full acceptance: Whatever the teacher says, don't doubt it, just follow what the teacher says. If the teacher tells you to go east, you go east; if west, you go west. Don't ask if there are wild beasts ahead or if it's dangerous. Just do it, and you'll understand why the teacher told you to do it.

Second, full denial: Some might say, isn't this contradictory to the previous step? Not at all. Why? Because only when you truly follow what the teacher says will you have your own judgment and know whether what the teacher said is correct. So, knowing whether the teacher is right or wrong, why fully deny rather than optimize and absorb? If you don't think critically and don't deny, how can you distinguish between right and wrong? Remember, it's a full denial, every word the teacher says must be fully denied.

Third, optimized absorption: After going through the above two steps, you will naturally move on to the third step, optimized absorption. But you must have done the previous two steps well.

Fourth, enhanced thinking: Do you know why they are experts and why they are so successful? It's because they have their own thinking, innovation, and ideas, not just limited to what the teacher taught. They have elevated this thinking and expanded it to many aspects, which leads to such success.

These four steps hope everyone will think deeply about them and not stay on the surface.
