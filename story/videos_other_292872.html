(11) Before you kiss me —— The rich son falls in love with me, a young widow. She is a young girl, no more than twenty-three or twenty-four years old, with fine and long eyebrows, and her phoenix eyes are full of charm. Her lips are thin, and although her skin is not very white, it is very tender. Half of her melon seed-shaped face has already swollen up, marked by five fiery finger prints.

It seems that my slap was not light. My own palm is a bit sore.

I am still sitting on the grass, trembling all over. I say hoarsely: "We almost got hit by a train, you crazy person! Why do you walk on the railway tracks when there's a good road to take?"

The girl sniffs and flips her hand: "Because this leads to heaven!"

I have already confirmed that this girl is not normal. Since I have no strength, I can only grab her pants for support to stand up, saying: "Where do you live? I'll send you home."

She glares at me: "Don't meddle in other people's affairs. I have no home. I've already decided to die. No one can stop me."

I look at her once, this girl indeed has problems, she is definitely trying to commit suicide. How should I persuade her? Looking at her lightly powdered face and the clothes she wears, this girl also follows fashion trends.

I say coldly: "Do you want to imitate others committing suicide by lying on the railway tracks? Have you thought about how your body will be torn apart when the train passes, dying as ugly as possible? Not even dogs would want to look at you."

Girls all love beauty, so I have to attack her weakness first.

Sure enough, her face turns pale. I point at the surrounding weeds and say: "Your broken body will be casually thrown here, after the blood drains out, someone might just bury you... Poor thing, you will become a wandering ghost..."

She screams and covers her face: "Don't say anymore! It's too scary!"

I sigh, thinking that she realizes it's terrifying now. What was she thinking before? This head is filled with porridge!
