The leaders of Beijing municipality received the officers and men of the Public Security, Party Secretary Li Qi gave four instructions

by qinglai888 on 2009-12-15 19:48:41

Beijing Municipal Leaders Meet with National Security Officers; CPC Beijing Committee Secretary Liu Qi Issues Four Instructions

According to Sina Sports, on December 14, the Beijing Guoan team, which had won the Chinese Super League title, was warmly received by Politburo member and CPC Beijing Committee Secretary Liu Qi. During the meeting, Liu Qi issued four instructions, encouraging the Beijing Guoan team to continue their efforts, uphold the spirit of always striving for first place, and promote the development of sports in Beijing.

After the Beijing Guoan team won the championship, the Beijing Municipal Party Committee and Municipal Government sent congratulatory telegrams celebrating the team's victory in the Chinese Super League. On the morning of December 14 at 11:00, while the team was preparing for the evening's championship celebration banquet, the club received a call from the Beijing Sports Bureau informing them that the city's leaders were prepared to meet with the Beijing Guoan players.

Subsequently, all players and coaches of the Beijing Guoan team were warmly received by Politburo member and CPC Beijing Committee Secretary Liu Qi. Other leaders who joined the reception included Beijing Mayor Guo Jinlong, Standing Vice-Mayor Ji Lin, Propaganda Department Minister Cai Futao, Vice-Mayors Liu Jingmin and Huang Wei, among others.

During the reception, Liu Qi issued four instructions to the entire Beijing Guoan team. First, Liu Qi required the Guoan team to continue upholding the spirit of always striving for first place and maintain this positive corporate spirit. Second, while Guoan strives to uphold this spirit and improve its results, it should also focus on the cultivation of young talents and strengthen youth team construction. Third, moving forward, the government, enterprises, and clubs must work together. It is not just about the improvement of the Beijing Guoan Football Club but also about the rapid growth and enhancement of other sports projects in Beijing. Fourth, all sectors should learn from Beijing Guoan, reflecting the spirit of an international metropolis, promoting mass sports activities, and contributing to the accelerated construction of a "Humanistic Beijing, Technological Beijing, Green Beijing."

During the meeting, Beijing Mayor Guo Jinlong also shared his attention and love for sports with the Beijing Guoan team. Moreover, Guo Jinlong mentioned that he had once worked as a coach at the Physical Education Commission. Originally, when Guo Jinlong worked in Zhong County, Sichuan, he served as a Physical Education Commission coach and was the启蒙教练 (initiation coach) of Zhou Jian'an, the current head coach of the Chinese men's volleyball team. Guo Jinlong expressed to the Guoan team that he had only watched two of their matches this year: one was the home game against Chongqing, which they won 3-1, and the other was the final match against Zhejiang where Guoan triumphed 4-0. He hoped that next year, the Guoan team would continue their efforts, uphold the spirit of unity and hard work, and strive for good results.
