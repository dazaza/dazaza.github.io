How to tell if someone loves you or not, the "fourth sentence" is very accurate!

by motglove60 on 2010-04-10 22:06:38

1. If someone calls or sends you text messages everyday, no doubt he loves you, at least you are in his mind. If every time it's you calling him, then forget it, you are not in his mind at all. Never believe the lies that he doesn't have time or is too busy, if a person doesn't even have 3-5 minutes to call you, how busy could he be? Would he still be alive?

2. If a person takes you to some private places when meeting with you, then he doesn't love you. On the contrary, if he takes you to parks and other public places, then he enjoys being with you.

3. If a person only calls or texts you for some small talk without any hints of intimacy, don't be silly, he has no feelings for you anymore. He may just greet you out of courtesy.

4. If a person loves you, he will tell you he misses you without your asking.

5. If a person gets angry at you because of some trivial things, then it shows he cares about you. But if he still ignores you after you admitted your mistakes, let me tell you, don't bother him. If he loves you, he will take the initiative to talk to you. If he doesn't love you, he will use this as an excuse to leave you.

6. If a person loves you, he will tolerate all your mistakes. If a person doesn't love you, no matter how well you behave, he will eventually find fault with you and leave.

7. If a person loves you, he will accept all of you instead of making you change this or that.

Therefore, be yourself, don't compromise yourself because of love. Do you know, whether you are in a relationship or already broken up, after reading this, you must forward it, we will be happy...
