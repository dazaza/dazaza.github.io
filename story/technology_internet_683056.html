How is a "Chinese Master of Engineering Supervision" made?

by yibadou13 on 2012-02-29 11:31:28

He has worked hard in the construction supervision industry of Hunan Province for nearly 20 years, successively obtaining professional qualifications as a nationally registered supervising engineer, a nationally registered cost engineer, and a transportation department registered supervising engineer. He was also rated as a professor-level senior engineer.

In 2008, to honor his outstanding contributions to the supervising cause, at the summary and commendation meeting celebrating 20 years of innovation and development in China's engineering supervision, the China Construction Supervision Association awarded him the prestigious title of "China Engineering Supervision Master" in its inaugural ceremony.

He is Pan Xiangming, Chairman and General Manager of Hunan Changshun Engineering Construction Supervision Co., Ltd.

Bearing Heavy Responsibilities

Pan Xiangming is one of the founders of Changshun Supervision Company. When the company was established, he was already the leader in the engineering cost industry at China National Light Industry Changsha Engineering Co., Ltd. (formerly China National Light Industry Changsha Design Institute), serving as Director of the Engineering Cost Department. In its early days, Changshun Supervision Company consecutively undertook the supervision work for two projects: the Guangzhou Weida High Paper Industry Co., Ltd. project (producing 100,000 tons of coated whiteboard paper annually, with an imported production line, a Sino-foreign joint venture, total investment of 1.4 billion yuan) and the Suzhou Industrial Park Asia-Pacific Paper Products Co., Ltd. (producing 350,000 tons of cultural paper annually, solely funded by Indonesia, total investment of 550 million US dollars). The key equipment for these industrial projects were all imported from abroad, and personnel involved in the construction and management came from more than twenty countries and regions. Changshun Supervision Company was the only domestic supervising unit participating in the construction supervision of these projects. For a newly established supervising company, this was an extremely challenging task, with immense difficulties. At a critical moment, Pan Xiangming was entrusted with a heavy responsibility, successively serving as the representative of the chief supervising engineer for these two projects. Leading by example, he meticulously studied business issues, endured hardships ahead of others, and led the project supervising department to conscientiously and orderly carry out supervisory work. The meticulous and precise work of the project supervising department earned high praise from the sole proprietorship enterprises. Subsequently, Pan Xiangming served as the chief supervising engineer for the expansion project of the terminal building at Huanghua Airport in Changsha, Hunan. With his outstanding professional ability and organizational work, he led the project supervising department to successfully complete the supervisory tasks. After the completion of the project, it received the [Luban Award] in 2001.

Striving for Governance

Due to his strong business capabilities and innovative pioneering spirit, Pan Xiangming was quickly promoted to Vice President of Changshun Supervision Company, responsible for the company's production and operation. He keenly realized that for the company to open up in fierce market competition, establish a foothold, and grow stronger, it could not do without its own powerful strength and scientific management. One of the characteristics of a supervising company's work is that the supervised engineering projects are scattered, and the company's employees are stationed at various construction sites. To ensure the effective operation and supervision of the company, Pan Xiangming strongly advocated establishing a modern enterprise management system. In 1999, Changshun Supervision Company became one of the first in the national supervising industry to establish an ISO9001 quality management system, making the responsibilities of each department clear and the duties distinct, ensuring that the company's management had rules to follow and achieved proceduralization and standardization, forming a complete scientific quality assurance system in practice. Pan Xiangming required that Changshun Supervision Company strictly adhere to the established management system, avoiding formalism and superficiality. Due to the company's down-to-earth, meticulous adherence to the corporate philosophy of "scientific fairness, integrity and law-abidingness, diligent dedication, and top-notch service," one after another project supervision gained good reputations from the owners, causing Changshun Supervision Company's reputation to rise rapidly. Its supervising business steadily developed, successively obtaining Class A qualifications in engineering supervision, highway engineering supervision, and engineering bidding agency. Its supervising business covered most provinces across the country, encompassing supervision of mechanical and electrical installation projects, municipal public works projects, housing construction projects, forestry and ecological projects, and power projects; class one, two, and three highway projects, bridge projects, and class two and three traffic engineering construction supervision; engineering bidding agency, and technical consulting for construction projects. In October 2008, Changshun Supervision Company obtained the highest national qualification in engineering supervision—the comprehensive qualification for engineering supervision.

Specialized Expertise

Pan Xiangming pays great attention to theoretical learning in his daily life and has a tenacious drive for studying business. In his spare time, he persists in studying management knowledge, participating in the first batch of cost engineer training [http://www.otje.com.cn/html/jianshegongcheng_sheji/zhucezaojiashi/] and cost engineer exams [http://www.otje.com.cn/html/jianshegongcheng_sheji/zhucezaojiashi/], obtaining a national registration certificate for cost engineers. Pan Xiangming also published articles such as "Reflections and Thoughts on Supervision Practice", "Strict Supervision, Fully Achieving Engineering Construction Goals", and "Enhancing Corporate Management, Building Renowned Brands in the Industry" in the Hunan Construction Supervision Journal, expounding on strict, standardized, and refined management ideas. In 2007, after Comrade Pan Xiangming was promoted to General Manager of Changshun Supervision Company, he set an example, diligently working, always busy with hardly any holidays. Under his guidance of strict and refined management, Changshun Supervision Company continuously strengthened its own construction, especially increasing employee training efforts in human resource construction, formulating a series of incentive policies and corresponding assessment systems, and organizing core staff to study abroad. In terms of organizational construction, each management department of Changshun Supervision Company continuously strengthened their forces. To reinforce the company's guidance and supervision over on-site project departments, experienced excellent chief supervising engineers were transferred from the frontlines to supplement the technical quality department. In terms of corporate culture construction, various cultural and sports activities were conducted, enhancing employees' sense of identity and cohesion, and stimulating their work enthusiasm. Under his leadership, Changshun Supervision Company annually won the titles of Advanced Supervision Unit in Hunan Province and National Advanced Engineering Construction Supervision Unit, and in 2008, it received the title of Advanced Unit in the 20th Anniversary of Innovation and Development in National Engineering Supervision, which is the highest commendation for supervising enterprises.
