521218 Commemorative Liquor (Naked Bottle) 500ml On December 18, 1978, the Third Plenary Session of the 11th Central Committee of the Communist Party was held, marking the beginning of a new era of reform and opening up in China. --10 years: from 1978 to 1988, the 10th anniversary of reform and opening up, Wuliangye initially developed into a large modern brewing enterprise. --20 years: from 1978 to 1998, the 20th anniversary of reform and opening up, Wuliangye successfully achieved restructuring and listing, becoming a benchmark in Chinese liquor. --30 years: from 1978 to 2008, the 30th anniversary of reform and opening up, Wuliangye reached an unprecedented new height in production and benefits scale, truly becoming the king of Chinese liquor. To commemorate this historic great moment, Wuliangye Group launched the Wuliangye 1218 commemorative liquor. Wuliangye is a strong-aroma type of liquor made with five grains: wheat, rice, corn, sorghum, and glutinous rice, fermented and brewed in Yibin City, Sichuan Province. It stands out among strong-aroma type liquors in China for its long-lasting aroma, rich flavor, sweet taste, clean throat sensation, harmonious flavors, and perfect balance. Renowned worldwide, Wuliangye selects the essence fermented from ancient cellars since the Ming Dynasty, brewed with unique blending techniques. The 1218 commemorative liquor inherits the true and pure flavor of a grand style, allowing one to appreciate the natural changes over time. The Wuliangye 1218 commemorative liquor is a genuine internal-use liquor of Wuliangye, definitely a fine wine. Every December 18th at the Wuliangye distributor conference, distributors from all over the country drink this wine. Therefore, it is not only a fine wine recognized by insiders but also a wine with perceptible and appreciable quality and taste for people of discernment, making it a wine for sharing and enjoying. This wine has excellent quality and taste without question.

Honors Received:

Since winning the gold award at the "Panama International Exposition" in 1915 as the first Chinese product, Wuliangye won another gold award at the "13th Panama International Food Exposition" in 1995, creating the brilliance of "eighty years of unshaken gold medal". It was also rated as the "King of China's Liquor Industry" at the 50th World Statistical Congress. In June 2002, at the "20th International Trade Fair" in Panama, it once again won the only gold award in the liquor category, continuing the century-long honor of Wuliangye. Meanwhile, Wuliangye liquor has four times been awarded the title of "National Famous Liquor"; four times received the golden medal for national quality products; its trademark "Wuliangye" was rated as one of the first ten well-known trademarks in China in 1991; the sub-brand "Wuliangchun" was rated as a well-known trademark in China in 2008, and "Wuliang Chun" was rated as a well-known trademark in China in 2009. In 2009, Wuliangye was rated as one of the top 100 units in China for trademark registration, the most influential well-known trademark in China, and the best geographical indication product in China. In 2010, Wuliangye brand liquor ranked first in sales nationwide among similar products, with a brand value reaching 52.616 billion yuan, continuously ranking first in the industry for 16 years, and ranking fourth among the most valuable brands nationwide.
