2. I don't want a fleeting affection, just your company for a lifetime

3. Only because you are too wonderful that I cannot frankly say I love you

4. I think that after fifty years, I will still love you as much as now

5. I hope the last thing I see before sleeping is you...

6. Please be my treasure in my hand

7. I wonder if loving you counts as a reason for being considerate?

11. I think in the future I will definitely go to the market with you every day..

12. Since you appeared, I have known how wonderful it is to be loved..

13. In the once bewildered heart, it was you who led me out of loneliness

14. My love for you will never change until the end of the world

15. For you, no matter whether it is cloudy or clear, full or waning, it will not change

16. Imagine the joy of watching sunrise and sunset together with me

17. I am willing to wait for ten million years for your smile like the warm sunshine of early spring.

18. Only you understand my world

19. Hide you deeply in my heart

20. I know that love needs freedom to be happy, but I would rather stay by your side, accompany you, accompany you through everything

21. I don't know when it started, but I have learned to depend on you

22. Only you know my emotions, and only you can bring me emotions

23. When love gets hot, I freeze it with ice; when love gets cold, I surround it with a fireplace; when love gets thirsty, I soak it in water; I take good care of my love, and bring it to you on the seventh day of the seventh month, it will be as fresh as ever!

24. When you see the mountain streams flowing, and when you see the fire burning fiercely, please do not look down on it, scoop it up, get close to it, cherish it! Do you know? That is all the power of my thoughts flying over thousands of mountains and rivers for you!

25. Your heart, my heart, I fell in love with you, and I am crazy about you for life; Your heart, my heart, without seeing you, there is only sorrow; Your heart, his heart, regardless of others, please be assured; Exchange hearts, even if pierced by ten thousand arrows, I will not change my heart!

26. Qixi has arrived, magpies line up in teams, crows form rows, eagles come to pay respects, even frogs come to help, croaking to make music, we live happily and healthily, forever and ever!

30. Our delicious life is in vinegar, sesame oil, and soy sauce, our plain true feelings are in oil, salt, soy sauce, vinegar, and tea, our simple happiness is in pots, pans, ladles, and plates, except for the illusionary romance, we own the truth of love!

31. My yearning is woven into a net, my sincerity builds a bridge, my promise turns into wind: no matter how wide the Milky Way is, it cannot stop the integration of our spirits!

32. Magpies witnessed our love and came to build a bridge; grapes witnessed our love and hurriedly spread the message, the Milky Way witnessed our love and turned into a meteor to bring Zhinu's wishes: Niulang, remember to meet on the magpie bridge today!

33. The meteor fell, it was the sky crying; the rain fell, it was my thoughts swelling; the moon rose, it was our eyes crossing; the earthly magpies flew, they were paying tribute to the Qixi Festival of love!

34. You are Niulang, I am Zhinu; you cook, I wash rice; you plow the field, I pull the plow; I wish to be branches, entwined in heaven; I wish to be mandarin ducks, playing in the water, carefree and happy, just for having you!
