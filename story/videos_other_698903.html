What kinds of fitness equipment are there?

by anonymous on 2012-03-09 11:50:14

Yesterday, in front of the Shenyang Bird Island, the reporter met two "bicycle friends", Zhang Bo and Dong Chenglin. Their homes are in different directions. Every day, Zhang Bo rides from Changqing Bridge to Lvlng Sports Park and then to Bird Island. Dong Chenglin rides even further, covering 44 kilometers from Sitaizi to meet Zhang Bo in front of Bird Island. They are just two of many bicycle enthusiasts. Many bicycle enthusiasts in Shenyang like to ride to Qipanshan for fitness because the air here is fresh, the roads are neat, and there are many fitness equipment along the roadside.

Nowadays, there are many fitness enthusiasts who use bicycles as a means of fitness, and most of them choose to ride to Qipanshan to breathe fresh air and exercise for good health. The 64-year-old Zhang Bo said to the reporter: "Fitness guides in the media often tell us that middle-aged and elderly people are not suitable for long outdoor fitness activities when it's cold outside. So, I only come out when the weather warms up, and my first choice is still riding a bicycle."

Zhang Bo rides to Bird Island every day. "The air here at Qipanshan is fresh and the roads are neat. Coming here every day to breathe fresh air naturally refreshes me." It was at Bird Island that Zhang Bo met Dong Chenglin. The 56-year-old Dong Chenglin has been riding for less than a year, but his equipment is quite professional. "Although I haven't been riding for long, I cover a long distance every day. A round trip is more than 80 kilometers."

Zhang Bo and Dong Chenglin became cycling friends. After reaching Bird Island, they put down their bicycles and exercised on the fitness path in Lvlng Sports Park, chatting. "Riding a bicycle is a lower body workout, and using the fitness equipment here exercises the upper body." Zhang Bo told the reporter: "May and June are the peak periods for bicycle sports. On weekends, you can see at least five or six thousand people riding past here."

Physical and mental pleasure is found here.

At Lvlng Sports Park, the reporter also enjoyed a delightful Tai Chi performance by 40 participants, all members of the Tai Chi team from the No. 75 coaching station of the city's Tai Chi Association.

Gao Ronglan, 68 years old, is a coach of the Tai Chi team. She has been practicing Tai Chi for 18 years. Gao Ronglan said that the Tai Chi team was founded 6 years ago and has more than 90 members. For these 6 years, the main activity site has been the cultural square of Qianlingbaozi. They exercise for more than an hour each morning and evening. Gao Ronglan said: "Our Tai Chi team often represents Qipanshan in city events. We want to convey the concept of 'fitness benefits' to more people." Occasionally, the Tai Chi team also performs at Lvlng Sports Park. "There are many types of fitness equipment here, which we can use for warm-ups before practicing Tai Chi."

Gao Ronglan has another special task - to translate for the captain and head coach of the Tai Chi team. Captain and head coach Yu Jingzhu is deaf-mute. Before practicing Tai Chi, she practiced long boxing for many years, so she is very experienced in fitness related to boxing projects. In her own words: "I am disabled myself, but I have achieved some success in Tai Chi, and both my physical and mental condition is getting better every year. Therefore, I believe that through my vivid example, more people can be encouraged to participate in fitness activities, truly realizing 'national fitness'!"

There are also many fitness activities and competitions held annually at Qipanshan, such as triathlon competitions and Huixiu Lake Bicycle Open Competitions.
