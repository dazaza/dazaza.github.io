A. Special provisions regarding the structure of the company name:

(1) If the company name uses the trade name from a holding company's name and that holding company's name does not contain an administrative division, then the administrative division can be placed after the trade name and before the organizational form. For example, "Baila Group Beijing Economic Development Co., Ltd."

(2) If the company name does not use an industry description, such as "Beijing Huahui Company Development Co., Ltd.", it should meet the following conditions:

1. The economic activities of the company belong to five or more major categories of the national economy;

2. The registered capital (or registered funds) of the company is over 1 billion RMB or is the parent company of a corporate group;

3. The trade name is not the same as any other company name approved or registered by the same industrial and commercial administrative authority.

Structure of the company name for application inquiries: The company name should consist of an administrative division, trade name, industry, and organizational form in sequence, except where otherwise provided by laws and regulations. Taking the company name "Beijing Huangshan Catering Co., Ltd." as an example:

(1) The administrative division refers to the name or place name of the administrative division at or above the county level where the company is located, such as "Beijing";

(2) The trade name in the company name should consist of two or more Chinese characters. The administrative division cannot be used as the trade name, unless the place name of an administrative division at or above the county level has other meanings, such as "Huangshan";

(3) The industry description in the company name should reflect the economic activity nature of the company under the national economy or the operating characteristics of the company, such as "catering". The content of the industry term in the company name should be consistent with the business scope of the company;

(4) The company name can only contain one organizational form, such as "limited company";

C. Another company name cannot be included in the applied-for company name;

D. The applied-for company name cannot be the same or similar to the following company names:

(1) Same-industry company names approved or registered by the registration authority in this city, except those with investment relationships;

(2) Names of companies in this city whose business licenses have been revoked within the last three years;

(3) Names of companies in this city that have changed their names or been deregistered within the last year;
