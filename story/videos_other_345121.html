Betrayal with full knowledge

by gfrw0738 on 2010-04-13 23:18:11

There was a girl who gave him a real name, "Hongcheng," which meant the hope of being able to become a person of great achievement... In August of 2006, he failed the college entrance exam and went to a remedial school in Kunming. There, he bore the hopes of all those who cared about him, striving hard to get into his dream university. He had no vast fortune, but fortunately, his sister and brother treated him very well, which was one of the few blessings in his life! On this campus for college exam preparation, he was ambitious; it should be the place where he would realize his dreams.

However, reality was not as simple as imagined. He met a girl, and from the first time he saw her, he felt a faint affection. Finally, Hongcheng expressed his "love" for this girl... They started dating. At that time, Hongcheng almost forgot everything until one day, he discovered something about the girl... Thus, he decided to break up with her. Even though she was the girl he loved most, Hongcheng still broke up with her.

Because of the complicated circumstances Hongcheng faced, his only choice was to separate from her. Perhaps this lifetime, he might never find such a dear girl again, but they parted ways. He didn't know if giving up the girl he loved most was painful or happy. At that moment, his heart was filled with conflict.

Some days passed, and Hongcheng wasn't happy. His longing for her drove him nearly insane. Every day, he thought of her countless times, sometimes crying painfully when thinking of her. But what could he do? Facing various pressures, he had no right to love. He also forgot the lightness of their past romance, and Hongcheng dared not let others know he was in love...

Since everyone who cared about him did not allow him to fall in love at this time, none of his actions were approved by anyone. Yet, Hongcheng still betrayed them to love this girl...

In phone calls with his close ones, he claimed he wasn't in love, yet there was a girl by his side. He clearly knew all of this was wrong, yet he still did it. He knew he had betrayed the girl, his siblings, even his friends... He had betrayed everyone who cared about him. His heart ached, but he couldn't give up the girl he loved most. Perhaps, Hongcheng really wasn't a good person! He had no "conscience"! For "love," he betrayed so many people who cared about him! He really wasn't anything special!!

If loving someone lightly isn't wrong, then is betraying those who care about him right? Hongcheng, betraying his family to love the girl he cherished most, left his soul restless. He didn't know where to put his conscience; breaking up with the girl he loved, the kind of unforgettable, heart-wrenching pain almost destroyed him. He was truly going mad...

Love, especially for the person you cherish most, what choice would you make in such a dilemma? Love can make a person, but it can also utterly destroy one! Dear reader, if you were Hongcheng, what choice would you make?
