Man and woman survived for 6 days in grass after car accident O(∩_∩)O~

by mienglin on 2010-04-16 23:40:35

Foshan News - (Reporters Yang Bo and Pang Xiao Ming) Mr. Liang, a motorcycle taxi driver, never expected to witness such a scene in the grass: next to a rusted and deformed black car lay two injured individuals, a man and a woman. Due to severe mosquito bites, the woman's face was already somewhat distorted... Even more astonishingly, after doctors examined the two victims, they discovered that they had not eaten or drunk anything for six days but were still alive, creating a miracle of survival. When firefighters arrived at the scene, they suspected that there might be two more people missing in the car, but their search proved fruitless.

After the accident, the two were "thrown" into the grass.

According to the on-site firefighters, at 3:25 PM yesterday, when they arrived at the scene, they found that the entire car was hidden in the grass, making it very hard to spot. A weak 20-something-year-old woman lay in a small pit about three meters away from the car, her foot covered by grass. She couldn't speak or move. About six meters away from the car, another young man was found lying in a ditch. His feet were soaked white from the water in the ditch, his body covered with red spots from mosquito bites, and his condition appeared even worse than the woman's—only his eyes could blink.

The firefighters said: "We heard that there were four people in the car at the time of the incident, so we immediately started searching." Twenty firefighters then conducted a thorough search over an area of two to three hundred square meters. An hour later, no one else was found. They estimated that the car's speed at the time of the accident was at least 100 kilometers per hour. It first crashed off the bridge and then flew into the grass.

At the scene, reporters observed that above the accident car was the lead-in bridge of the Guangsan Expressway. The iron guardrail of the bridge was broken for six to seven meters. Below the bridge was a patch of reeds, with clear signs of where the car had crashed through. From the point where the guardrail was broken to the location of the accident car was approximately 50 meters. The exact time of the accident is still uncertain.

A girl has been missing for 6 days, and her phone has been unreachable.

Later, the male and female victims were sent to the People's Hospital of Sanshui District. According to the attending doctor in the emergency department, both victims suffered from severe dehydration and had obvious rashes due to mosquito bites. Upon examination, it was found that the female victim had a fractured thigh bone, while the male had brain injuries including cerebral contusion and concussion. After receiving extensive fluid replacement and comprehensive examinations at the hospital, both victims' vital signs became temporarily stable.

At the hospital, the relatives of the female victim waited outside the CT examination room. The relatives mentioned that the female victim was from Sanshui and had been missing for several days, with her phone consistently turned off.

The father of the female victim stated that the male victim was the girl's friend. The girl had been missing for six days, and both had not eaten or drunk anything for six days. The attending doctor expressed amazement, stating that surviving for six days without food or water in the car was already nothing short of a miracle of life.
