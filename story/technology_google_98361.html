Top 10 Apps for Google Android Phones

by yinyin on 2008-10-23 17:21:20

According to Techcrunch, the first Google Android phone, G1, was launched in the United States on Wednesday. Perhaps G1 is not particularly attractive in hardware design or software interface, but its applications are excellent.

Frequently used applications by users include Gmail, Web browser, or Google Maps, and MP3 can also be downloaded from Amazon. Most importantly, applications can be downloaded from the "Android Market" online application store. Accompanying the launch of G1, today Android Market will add 50 new applications, most of which are free.

The following are the top ten Android applications that you cannot miss:

1. imeem Mobile: Provides a large amount of copyright-protected music and free music radio, cumulative download mode ensures that users are not affected even in an elevator;

2. ShopSavvy: Turns the G1 phone into a barcode scanner. While scanning the product barcode, it searches for prices of this product across various stores online, so users don't have to worry about overspending;

3. Pac-Man: A classic game from Namco, provided for free. It costs $7.99 on iPhone;

4. BreadCrumbz: Uses photos or voice to create vivid photo maps that can be shared with others;

5. Wikitude: A travel-specific search tool that allows users to quickly find desired attractions in unfamiliar cities, supplemented by relevant introductions from Wikipedia, letting users fully enjoy the fun of traveling;

6. Shazam: Music recognition software. Whether it's a song being played on the radio or a speaker, just point the phone at the sound source to identify the name of the music track and the singer;

7. SplashPlay: Guitar learning software where users can get interactive guitar learning results through SplashPlay;

8. iSkoot for Skype: Allows users to contact friends using their Skype account and make Skype calls via the internet;

9. MyCloset: Takes photos of each piece of clothing in the wardrobe, classifies them, marks which season they are suitable for wearing, then freely matches outfits;

10. Cab4Me Light: A simple application that provides taxi company telephone numbers based on the user's geographical location.
