It includes US dollar uncut sheets (1 dollar, 2 dollars, 5 dollars, 10 dollars), Iraqi uncut sheets, Macedonian uncut sheets, Thai uncut sheets, North Korean uncut sheets, South Korean uncut sheets, and Ukrainian uncut sheets. The US dollar, the premier world currency, can be circulated and exchanged globally.

Collecting US dollars has always been the most stable wealth reserve globally. The "King of Dollar Uncut Sheets" represents rare US dollar uncut sheets, with a face value several times higher than Renminbi uncut sheets, making it a true king among banknotes; the "United States of America 50 States Coin" is the only set of circulating coins issued by the US Treasury under an order signed by President Clinton, featuring all 50 states of the United States. Each coin has a denomination of 25 cents. It took 12 years to complete the entire issue. After 12 years of waiting, the first four coins issued have almost disappeared from the international coin trading market. The appreciation potential of the entire set of American state coins is enormous, and they are hailed in the collecting community as the "First Treasure of American Circulating Coin Collectibles"; compared to Renminbi uncut sheets of equivalent face value, US dollar uncut sheets are currently at a relatively low price, leaving significant room for appreciation. With decreasing quantities and increasing market demand, substantial price increases are inevitable.

China is the country with the largest US dollar foreign exchange reserves in the world. The strong national reserves provide the most reliable and stable guarantee for US dollar uncut sheets. The 2002 Iraqi 10,000 dinar uncut sheet is a rare collectible item, extremely scarce. Given that the Saddam era will not return, this set of uncut sheets has great collection value! The uncut sheets contain two anti-counterfeiting lines, as well as white metallic reflective lines. However, due to Iraq's less advanced printing technology at the time, parts of the white reflective lines may peel off slightly in some areas, and there may be some residual ink on the back.
