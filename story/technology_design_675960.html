His wife saw him return and heated up a plate of dumplings for him, letting him take a shower and changing his clothes. After eating and drinking to his fill, he went to stroll at the east end of the village and met the village chief. The chief pulled him aside and said, "Da Chun, you're back? Don't be too upset, unexpected things happen. Your wife is gone, but you can always marry again." Da Chun got angry and impatiently said, "Nonsense! I just ate the dumplings my wife warmed up and changed into new clothes!" The chief gave him a strange look and said, "How did you put on the mourning clothes the villagers collected money to buy for your wife's funeral?" Da Chun looked and indeed, it was mourning clothes. The chief said, "Tonight, come to my house and hide. I'll prepare a room for you. Wait for me." The chief rushed home. As soon as he entered, before he could speak, his wife said, "Husband, Da Chun's bus overturned, and he died. What a tragic fate for his family!" Now, it was the chief's turn to be dumbfounded.

16. Hospital: Doctor Wang was about to finish work when a patient in urgent need of rescue was brought in, so Doctor Wang worked until midnight. By this time, it was almost dawn. After changing clothes, Doctor Wang, exhausted, took the elevator downstairs. There was another doctor in the elevator, so Doctor Wang stepped in. As the elevator descended slowly, the other doctor asked Doctor Wang, "Wasn't the surgery proceeding smoothly earlier? How did it fail?" Doctor Wang glanced at him, recognizing the masked face, but didn't think much of it and casually replied, "It was fine at first, but there was massive bleeding during the operation. The other doctor broke the silence, muttering, "So this is how I died..." After saying this, he removed his mask, and Doctor Wang recognized the face - it was the patient who had just died.
