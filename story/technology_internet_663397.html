Radiant floor heating uses the entire floor as a radiator, evenly heating the whole floor through the heat medium in the floor's radiant layer. It applies the principle of the floor's own heat storage and upward radiation to conduct heat from bottom to top to achieve the goal of heating, which is known as underfloor heating installation. Because it creates a temperature gradient that decreases gradually from the feet to the head inside the room, it provides people with a comfortable sensation of warm feet and cool head. Radiant floor heating aligns with the traditional Chinese medical practice of "warming the feet while keeping the top of the head cool," making it currently the most comfortable heating method and a symbol of modern living quality.
