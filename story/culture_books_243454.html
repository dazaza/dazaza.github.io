"Art of Love" - A Premium Handsome Guy's Deceptive Love Story

by jqrxass on 2009-09-27 22:35:59

Dong! Dong! Dong! The Chi Ling Mi Deception Academy is officially open for business!

Specializing in teaching how to scam food, drink, and bed space, even more rare are the professional heart-capturing tactics courses!

Of course, heart-capturing tactics are extremely difficult to learn; ordinary people can't master them!

Condition one: You must find a surname stranger than Chi Ling Mi to use as your alias;

Condition two: You must be as stunningly handsome as Chi Ling Mi;

Condition three: You also need to find an extremely gullible super good person as your target for capturing their heart.

If you've achieved all of these points, congratulations, you're basically ready to start capturing hearts. But under no circumstances should you fall genuinely in love, otherwise tears will dampen your collar, and your heart will be severely injured by the heart-capturing tactics. So...

The Art of Love Heart-Capturing Tactics, Begin! Please proceed with caution.

The Art of Love Heart-Capturing Tactics Part One

"Dead A Che, scoot further inside, this bed isn't just yours alone, I've drawn the boundary line, don't cross it! Although you're my sister... Wow! Your pajamas are so cute, lend them to me! Lend them to me now, or I won't let you sleep!"

"Che, I'm using the computer, go draw your pictures! You won't get to use the computer this month, there are still 15 family members waiting to use it, why didn't you sign up earlier, ha ha!"

"Wowaaaah—Young Che Chen, you actually watched romance DVDs behind everyone's back? There were even kissing scenes? Oh, my God! I'm going to tell Mom and Dad right now! What do you mean invading your privacy?"

The Art of Love Heart-Capturing Tactics Part Two

My face turned red, I quickly retreated in panic, looking down at my own feet and saying: "You... you should have heard already, right? I, I don't want to repeat myself... I... I... I don't have the courage to say it again..."

"Chuckling," Chi Ling Lei gently smiled, pulling me into his arms and softly whispering in my ear: "Hmm, I understand your feelings. Thank you for accepting me."

I buried my head deeply in his embrace, feeling that my face was still burning hot, as if it could scorch his clothes, but suddenly felt that this was the warmth of happiness, so I sweetly smiled...

Then, Chi Ling Lei gently pulled away from me, holding my shoulders tightly and staring at me intently asking: "So, will you reject Wei Tai? Will you make things clear with Wei Tai? Will you keep your distance from him? And you won't give him any more chances, right?"

The Art of Love Heart-Capturing Tactics Part Three

Chi Ling Lei stood frozen in place, silent for a long time without speaking, his expression grave and somber.

Then, he suddenly sneered and spoke to me: "Huh, what if Wei Tai is good? What if I am bad? In the end, you fell in love with me, this bad person, and are unable to extricate yourself. You even said that your favorite thing to hear is the music from this bad person's hands, and your favorite sight is watching this bad person play the piano..."

As he spoke, he slowly approached me, his deep, mesmerizing gaze like poison enveloping me, reaching out with his pale, long fingers toward my tear-streaked cheeks. Was he going to wipe my tears?

No! No! I couldn't be charmed by him again! Panicking, I retreated desperately, screaming hysterically: "Don't touch me! From this moment on, I won't let you have another chance to hurt me, because—I want to break up with you! I won't be the girlfriend of someone who doesn't truly love me and is just using me!"
