"To Those Who Once Loved Me and Whom I Once Loved" - a youth romance competition among two women and one man.

by jqrxass on 2009-10-02 21:50:00

The novel "To Those Who Once Loved Me and Whom I Once Loved" mainly tells the love story of two girls, Xu Anli and Shen Yingchun, and a boy, Tang Liyu.

Xu Anli secretly loves her senior Tang Liyu and gets into his university, only to find out that Tang Liyu has a girlfriend, Shen Yingchun. Qin Ge heals Xu Anli's heartbreak. After graduation, Tang Liyu and Shen Yingchun have been cohabiting, and Shen Yingchun gives up returning to the United States for him. However, due to Xu Anli being in between them, conflicts arise continuously. Shen Yingchun wants to take a break and return to the United States. Tang Liyu chases after her at the airport and becomes a vegetative state as her plane takes off. Xu Anli returns from Beijing to Qingdao to take care of Tang Liyu. The amnesic Tang Liyu regards Xu Anli as his girlfriend. When Shen Yingchun returns to China, she discovers that Tang Liyu no longer recognizes her... The romance between Xu and Tang reignites, and Qin Ge leaves dispiritedly...

If one day, your old man cheats on you and likes another girl, will you cripple me? As if everything he does is because I told him to behind your back!"

Part 2: To Those Who Once Loved Me and Whom I Once Loved

Innocent are always the men, and the ones who get hurt are always the women. This is the theorem of love breakups, whether you admit it or not, this is how most realities play out.

Extreme joy turns to sorrow; these four words fit Shen Yingchun tonight perfectly. But why does extreme joy lead to tragedy?

If there's no joy, would there be no tragedy? God really knows how to play games, making you laugh and cry, love and hate. Perhaps, this is what extreme joy turning to sorrow means.

People can't outplay fate, and fate can't outplay God.

Part 3: To Those Who Once Loved Me and Whom I Once Loved

Is that John really a thousand times, ten thousand times better than me? Obsessively trying to crack Shen Yingchun's QQ password and MSN password, but finding nothing.

Tang Liyu realizes that he isn't a person with a magnanimous heart capable of embracing everything. He wants to know the QQ chat records between Shen Yingchun and John, wanting to know if they have already... From now on, she no longer belongs to him, Tang Liyu. She's going back to her America. Without her, will he be happy?

"Hey! Mickey Mouse!"

It was still the gentle, jade-like voice, like three years ago, unchanged.

Xu Anli's body stiffened momentarily, continuing forward, but behind her were chaotic footsteps rushing towards her. Finally, his tall figure blocked her path.

She looked up, cracked a dry, foolish smile. If it weren't for a short circuit in intelligence, there could only be one explanation - such a foolish smile could be the best cover-up, covering up her heart that aches like needles, covering up her exhausted face, covering up her disordered gaze.
