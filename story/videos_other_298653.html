Love begins with a smile.

by liuyjqba on 2009-12-08 08:36:04

Love begins with a smile, light-hearted and refreshing... Loving someone who doesn't love you is painful; loving someone but lacking the courage to let them know your feelings is even more painful. Perhaps heaven intentionally lets us encounter a few people with whom we have no destiny before meeting our true soulmate, so we can learn to cherish this late-coming gift. As all the excitement, passion, and romance dissipate, your care and concern for that person remain unchanged. That's love.

The most sorrowful thing in life is giving up on pursuing the one you love, watching them walk away. Their importance to you cannot make them give anything back to you. No matter how long you chase, you still have to let them go, powerless...

We tend to immerse ourselves in present happiness, drunk and unable to extricate ourselves. When this happiness suddenly disappears, we are at a loss, falling into the abyss of melancholy over lost happiness, without realizing there is still much happiness waiting for us elsewhere in life. The best friends don't need any verbal communication; when they pass by, you just sit on the porch and wave lightly, yet it feels like the best communication you've ever had.

We only understand what we once possessed when we lose it, but we also discover what we lacked when we gain something.

When giving love, no one can be sure of getting reciprocation. Don't wait to receive love; slowly wait for your love to take root and sprout in their heart. Even if it doesn't, you should still feel satisfied because you already have an oasis of love in your heart.

Perhaps there are many words you will never hear from the person you hope for, which will be the greatest sadness and pain! If your heart has not found peace, then please never say give up. If you still love them, why say you don't? Love belongs to those who have been disappointed but continue to wait, love belongs to those who have been betrayed but still believe in beauty, love belongs to those who, despite being scarred, still yearn for love. Don't let the person you like become overly sad and painful! If you love her, tell her!!!

It takes only a minute to turn against someone, an hour to fall in love with someone, but a lifetime to forget someone. Don't set out for beautiful appearances; they may just be illusions. Don't set out for wealth; it will eventually fade. Head towards the person who can make you smile genuinely, because a smile can illuminate darkness. I hope you find the person who illuminates your life. When the person you deeply miss appears in your dreams, you truly wish to feel their real embrace. I hope there's someone in your life worth thinking about and dreaming of.

Dream the dreams you want to dream, do the things you want to do, go to the places you want to go, and be with the people you like, becoming the person you want to be, loving the people you like! Because you only have one life to fulfill your desires.

What makes the happiest people? They are the ones who have cried, been hurt, sought, tried, filled with gratitude, and started living happily again! They are the ones who truly understand happiness!

Love begins with a smile, extends in happiness, but fades with tears...
