Software park construction

by dragonsoft on 2010-07-15 15:07:41

In ancient Chinese legend, the dragon is the rain god, and Chinese dragons can gather auspicious clouds from the heavens to moisten the earth! In modern China, the dragon represents a spirit, and the dragon of DragonSoft can bring together an elite team with super strength to realize the dream of the dragon!

As a leading high-end enterprise in software outsourcing, DragonSoft Group has united upstream and downstream software enterprises to jointly create a software export base facing overseas - DragonSoft United Software Outsourcing Park (DUSOP).

Purpose - With DragonSoft as the leader, relying on relevant regions, standing on the technical team of Shanghai, facing the global software market, building a cluster of outsourcing service enterprises at all levels! Operation strategy - DUSOP will attract well-known enterprises' R&D departments and talent elites to enter the park and create a software R&D base. Promote the construction of self-owned brands. Create a first-class vocational education environment and establish a software training base, providing integrated services of talent training, internship, and employment for society and enterprises within the zone. Focus on software exports, organize and unite software export enterprises, and provide professional comprehensive services for software export enterprises.

DragonSoft Park is a comprehensive service platform built for software export enterprises, which realizes service sharing and knowledge sharing while making up for the inability of a single enterprise or a single educational institution to meet the multi-level needs of users in multiple fields, and can provide more professional skills, longer time, and larger domain services for internal members and external customers.

R&D Base - DUSOP will unite backbone software outsourcing enterprises/R&D institutions and talent elites to enter the park and create a software R&D base. Promote the construction of software outsourcing teams.

Training Base - DragonSoft Park creates a first-class software and foreign language talent education environment and establishes a truly suitable talent training base for outsourcing services. Provide integrated services of talent training, internship, and employment for society and enterprises within the zone.

Export Base - We will focus on software exports, organize and unite software export enterprises, and provide internationalized and professional comprehensive services for software export enterprises.

DragonPark, DragonSoft United Software Park, is a professional park serving software and information service outsourcing enterprises. Moreover, we are an alliance, an international software enterprise alliance; a platform, a platform providing services for IT enterprise clusters; a community, a community where IT talents of all levels gather and grow; and hope to become a symbol of the development model of China's information service outsourcing industry! The DragonSoft Base is a professional park for Shanghai's information service outsourcing industry, fully built by the Shanghai Municipal Government, Zhabei District Government, and DragonSoft Enterprise Group. It is located east of Wanrong Road and south of Wenshui Road Middle Ring Line, with a total construction area of 38,281 square meters. Nearly 30 software enterprises related to upstream and downstream software outsourcing have already moved in, covering ITO, BPO, and KPO three fields. Enterprises entering the park can not only receive various preferential policy supports but also enjoy good services and support from public service platforms, public data platforms, and public project management platforms, presenting a good international image to overseas customers. By 2011, after the completion of the third phase of the Shanghai base, it is expected that there will be 120-150 enterprises engaged in service outsourcing in the base, with 8,000 relevant practitioners.
