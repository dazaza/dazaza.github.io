What to do if spots grow at the corner of eyes?

Time: 2012-2-3 16:55:29 Hits: 225

Key tips: In general, people with spots growing at the corner of eyes have one thing in common: they are prone to be depressed and tend to react negatively to external influences. This is because the area near the temples is controlled by what Traditional Chinese Medicine (TCM) calls the gallbladder meridian, which is a channel for relieving accumulated worries. If spots appear here, it indicates that the gallbladder meridian is blocked. Moreover, due to their low mood, tendency towards depression, and difficulty in relieving stress, these issues accumulate over time, leading to spots forming at the corner of the eyes.

Once we understand the cause of the spots, it becomes easier to address. So, what should you, who love beauty, pay attention to? What should you do? How can you face life with positivity, live happily, and stay beautiful!

Below are three suggestions for women showing signs of spot formation for your consideration. Other causes of spots are related to oneself; as long as you take note, they can all be avoided.

1. Be a happy little witch

In daily life, don't worry unnecessarily. Stay cheerful and don't always assume bad things will happen to you. Don't be overly anxious about gains and losses, and become a happy little witch. But inevitably, there will be some unpleasant things that occur. What should you do then?

Give yourself five minutes to vent within those five minutes. Afterward, calm down and face everything cheerfully.

2. Always eat breakfast

Don't sleep in. Many girls like to sleep late and skip breakfast, but this isn't a good habit, nor is it relaxing for the body. It's recommended to go to bed by 10 o'clock every night and wake up no later than 8:30 in the morning. Drink a little plain water on an empty stomach, and always eat breakfast, no matter how little. Doing so benefits your health.

3. Develop good habits

A large portion of the reasons for spots forming are due to poor living habits. Developing good habits is essential.

1. Do proper sun protection

2. Avoid heavy makeup, as it can significantly irritate the skin and more easily lead to spots forming.

3. Whiten moderately. Some people excessively pursue beauty, which can backfire.

4. Stay away from computers and avoid sitting in front of them all day.

5. Exercise regularly. A healthy body ensures that all parts function properly, reducing problems. Fewer problems mean fewer spots appearing on your face without reason.

Actually, having spots at the corner of your eyes isn't scary. The most important thing is to be a happy and loving woman. Even the most beautiful woman won't be liked if she lacks kindness.
