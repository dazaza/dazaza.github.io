Love is always beautiful in the memory when parting

Six years ago, Nuo’er was playing shuttlecock downstairs. While playing, she kicked the shuttlecock away. "Ouch!" A groan came from not far away. Nuo’er looked up and saw that the shuttlecock fell on a boy of her age. The boy was struggling to carry a travel bag. "It's bad, he must be the new neighbor, run!" Nuo'er wanted to run away when the boy caught up with her: "Don't run, I don't blame you. What's your name?" Nuo'er told him her name because of his good attitude. The boy also told her: "My name is Cheng Xiangyu, I am your new neighbor." That's how they met each other and became close friends.

One day, Nuo'er and Xiangyu lay on the grass, squinted at the horizon. "When I grow up, I will definitely marry a pretty girl," Xiangyu said slowly. After hearing this, Nuo'er giggled and replied: "When I grow up, I will definitely marry a handsome man."

Later, because of Nuo'er father's job change, their family would move to a distant city. After knowing this, Nuo'er cried alone in the corner. That day, when she told Xiangyu this news, Xiangyu didn't say anything, just looked at her and ran away.

The day of departure arrived. When Nuo'er was about to get on the car, Xiangyu came panting and handed her a painting. Nuo'er opened the painting and saw a boy holding a girl's hand walking towards the distance in the painting. The sunset shone on her face, tears glistened. Nuo'er understood that the boy in the painting was Xiangyu and the girl was herself. She got on the bus with the painting. The bus slowly drove away. Nuo'er looked at the increasingly blurred Xiangyu and shouted in her heart: If there is a destiny, we will meet again!

As time passed, six years later, due to a temporary change in Nuo'er father's work, they returned to their hometown. Father transferred Nuo'er to a school in their hometown. That day, after being encouraged by the teacher, Nuo'er dared to walk timidly into the classroom and introduced herself shyly: "Hello everyone, my name is Lin Nuo'er..." "Nuo'er, it's really you!" A boy exclaimed in surprise. Nuo'er suddenly looked up, her expression froze for a moment. The boy in front of her was exactly Xiangyu. Although they had been separated for six years, his appearance was only more handsome and mature than before. Nuo'er would never forget this face. She looked at Xiangyu and instinctively lowered her head. Vaguely, she heard Xiangyu muttering softly: "I made a mistake." Tears poured down, her heart filled with sorrow.

The teacher looked around and smiled, letting Nuo'er sit in the empty seat next to Xiangyu. As soon as she sat down, Xiangyu asked her: "Do you know a girl with the same name as you? Her name is also Nuo'er." Nuo'er shook her head lightly. Xiangyu sighed and said: "I miss her so much." Blood dripped drop by drop in Nuo'er's heart. She shouted in her heart again and again: Why am I right in front of you, but you don't recognize me?

Time flowed like water. Although the father always called Nuo'er to integrate into the group, Nuo'er could never do it. In the class, only Xiangyu was nice to her. Xiangyu always told Nuo'er stories about his childhood and his neighbor girl during his free time, laughing while telling the story. And Nuo'er just silently watched him, listening quietly.

"Xiangyu, come on, we support you." A bunch of girls were cheering on the playground. Xiangyu was the captain of the basketball team and naturally surrounded by girls. Although Nuo'er had no interest in basketball, every time Xiangyu played basketball, she would sit quietly watching him. And every time Xiangyu finished playing basketball, he would smile at Nuo'er.

Soon, rumors spread in the class that Xiangyu liked Siyu, the beauty queen of the neighboring class. This couldn't help but make Nuo'er feel a bit disappointed. However, she blessed Xiangyu because Xiangyu had once said that he would marry a beautiful and kind girl, wasn't Siyu such a girl? Nuo'er often thought this way.

One day, while passing through the stairs, Nuo'er happened to see Xiangyu and Siyu walking side by side upstairs, talking and laughing all the way. Nuo'er's heart broke instantly, tears blurred her vision. She turned and left. Although she warned herself countless times to bless Xiangyu, she still couldn't do it when she actually saw them together.

Is it true that people always like to deceive themselves and dare not accept the truth? When the truth comes, it breaks the dam instantly.

That day, Xiangyu faced an important basketball game, but Nuo'er didn't go to watch it. Instead, she sat in her seat thinking about things. She didn't know how long it had been until she felt someone patting her from behind. She turned her head and saw Xiangyu. Xiangyu asked her: "Why didn't you come to watch my game today? Is it because of me and Siyu? Actually, there is nothing between us..." "Enough, Xiangyu, your kindness to me all along was just treating me as your childhood playmate. I'm just her shadow, right?" Nuo'er shouted at Xiangyu for the first time. Xiangyu didn't know what to say for a long time and finally said: "Sorry, I miss her too much." Nuo'er left her seat sadly, not understanding why she felt this way.

"Do you know? Xiangyu lost this basketball game." "Of course I know, I didn't expect Xiangyu to lose." "In fact, it was expected. During this game, Xiangyu was always absent-minded. I don't know what's wrong with him." Girls gathered together discussing. Nuo'er wanted to block out the sound automatically, but every word pierced her heart like needles.

On Valentine's Day, the campus was full of warm love, but Nuo'er could only sit alone on the grass, looking at the painting given by Xiangyu when she was a child. "Nuo'er, how did you get this painting? You are exactly the Nuo'er I knew as a child, right?" Xiangyu stood beside Nuo'er without her noticing. Nuo'er looked at him in surprise and ran away. Xiangyu's mind went blank, standing there dumbly.

At night, all the girls in the dormitory went to enjoy their Valentine's date, leaving only Nuo'er in the dormitory. "Knock knock knock..." Suddenly, knocking sounds came from outside the door. Nuo'er jumped off the bed and opened the door, seeing Xiangyu standing outside with a bunch of roses in his hands. Nuo'er was terrified and wanted to close the door, but Xiangyu pushed it open, "Nuo'er, please don't be like this." Xiangyu begged. At that moment, Nuo'er burst into tears and let Xiangyu hug her tightly. She knew it was the first time and probably the last time.

The next day, there was a letter on Xiangyu's seat. Xiangyu opened the letter and saw the following words:

Xiangyu,

I'm really glad to see you. Thank you for always caring about me. Please forgive my sudden departure. I will move to another city with my father again.

I hope in your memory, I will always be the beautiful Nuo'er from our childhood.

Nuo'er

Suddenly, drizzle began to fall, lingering and melancholic. Xiangyu stood there for a long time.

Four years ago, due to a car accident, Nuo'er's face was disfigured. Although her father found the best hospital for plastic surgery, the treatment still failed, making her lose her original appearance. After the plastic surgery, she was not only ugly but even terrifying.

Perhaps, love is always present when saying goodbye.
