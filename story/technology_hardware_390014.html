Taobao Section

Beginner's Taobao Store Tutorial - Many friends who want to open an online store are not studying e-commerce, nor do they have any computer technology foundation, and some don't even have a high level of education. But can these things stop us from opening an online store and block our path to success? The following process is a step-by-step Taobao store opening tutorial, and you can definitely learn it step by step.

Why is online store so attractive?

Nowadays, opening an online store is not just about entrepreneurship; many people use it as a way to expand their capabilities and for leisure. What attracts everyone to online stores? Summarized, there are mainly the following points:

1. Zero cost, low risk

Compared to traditional stores, online stores do not require renting storefronts, paying taxes or utility bills, only charging minimal product listing fees and transaction fees, some are even free. And you can purchase goods as needed, without worrying about overstocking.

2. Flexible business methods

Not restricted by traditional business hours or locations, operators can run full-time or part-time businesses, without spending a lot of time watching the store.

3. Wide consumer base:

Because online stores are opened on the Internet, they face all possible consumers who might see the products, this group can be all the netizens in the country, or even globally.

How exactly do you open an online store?

Online stores offer a wide variety of items such as cosmetics, clothing, digital products... Each project has great development potential and prospects. The key is how to go about it, what suits you, which is the most important. Opening an online store also requires skills, here I will teach you step by step how to open an online store.

One, What to sell online?

Just like traditional stores, the first step in opening an online store is to consider what to sell. The choice of products should be based on your interests, abilities, conditions, as well as product attributes, consumer demands, etc.

Since I like watching Korean dramas, and due to the popularity of Korean-style women's clothing among Chinese women in recent years, I naturally started doing business in Korean-style women's clothing.

Two, Preparations before opening a store

After choosing what to sell, before opening an online store, you need to choose a website that provides a personal store platform and register as a user. To ensure transaction security, corresponding identity and payment method verifications are also required.

I chose to open a store on Taobao because it's completely free. To open a store on Taobao, you need to meet three conditions: one, register as a member and pass the verification; two, post more than 10 (including 10) items; three, for convenient and secure transactions, it is recommended to activate online banking.

1. User Registration

Log in to Taobao and click the "Free Registration" at the top of the page. On the opened page, input membership name, password, email, etc., and click the "Agree to the following service terms, submit registration information" button. Then, the registered email will receive a confirmation information email, open the link inside, confirm after, and the user registration is completed.

Security tip: To ensure transaction security, note that the password should not be set too simply, it is recommended to use a combination of "English letters + numbers + symbols".

2. Identity Verification

Taobao stipulates that only after real-name verification can you sell items and open a store. Therefore, after registering as a user, corresponding verifications (including real-name verification and Alipay verification) are also required. Specific operation steps are as follows:

Step one, log in to Taobao, click "My Taobao" at the top of the page. On the opened page, click "Click here" next to the text "Want to sell items? First, complete Alipay verification."

Step two, on the opened page, it will prompt that the Alipay account has not been activated, click "Click here to complete Alipay account activation." In the popped-out page, input real name, ID type and number, Alipay password, etc., and click the "Save and immediately activate Alipay account" button.

Step three, after successfully activating the Alipay account, return to the original page, press F5 to refresh the page. Click the "Apply for Alipay individual real-name verification" button, read the Alipay verification service terms, and click the "I have read..." button to continue.

Step four, first fill in personal information according to the prompts, click "Next"; then, choose ID verification. You can choose "Online upload" or "Mail" copies of ID documents, click "Next"; then, input bank card information including the issuing bank, bank card number, province, city, etc., after completing the input, wait for Alipay to transfer funds within one day.

Friendly tip: If you upload copies of ID documents online, the image file size should be controlled within 200KB; if it's an IC ID, you also need to provide a backside picture.

Step five, Guangzhou VIPshop Information Technology Co., Ltd., one day later, re-open "My Taobao", click the corresponding link in the verification area to open the "Alipay Verification" page, click "Confirm deposit amount" in the "Bank Account Verification" area, then input the amount of funds injected by Alipay into your bank account, and click the "Confirm" button.

3. Purchasing and Taking Pictures

A key factor in the successful operation of an online store lies in the purchasing channel. For the same item, prices vary depending on different purchasing channels.

After passing the identity verification, you need to organize the items you already have. To display the items for sale more intuitively in front of consumers, taking pictures is crucial, and it's best to use corresponding graphic/image processing tools for image format and size conversion, such as Photoshop, ACDSee, etc.

4. Posting Items

To open a store, besides meeting the certification membership conditions, you also need to post more than 10 items. Thus, after organizing the product information and images, you need to start posting your first item.

Friendly tip: Without passing the individual real-name verification and Alipay verification, you can post items, but the items can only be posted to "Items in Warehouse," which buyers cannot see. Only after passing the verification can you put them on the shelf for sale.

Step one, log in to Taobao, click "I Want to Sell" at the top of the page. On the opened page, you can choose between "Fixed Price" or "Auction" posting methods. Here, click "Fixed Price."

Friendly tip: "Fixed Price" has a set price, allowing buyers to purchase immediately; "Auction" starts without a reserve price, letting buyers bid to purchase.

Step two, choose category, select an appropriate category according to your product. For example, I selected details of women's shoes. Click the "Selected, Continue" button to proceed to the next step.

Step three, fill in item information, this step is very important. First, in the "Item Information" area, create a good title, click the "Browse" button to upload item pictures, input item description information, quantity, start time, validity period, etc.; then, in the "Transaction Conditions" area, input the item's selling price, location, shipping fee, payment method, etc.; other information can remain default settings, such as using Alipay by default, etc. Finally, click the "Confirm No Errors, Submit" button to post the item.

If the post is successful, a success page will appear below. Click "Here" to view the posted item page, and click "Continue Posting Items" to continue posting items.

Friendly tip: If no bids have been placed by buyers, you can modify the posted item information in "My Taobao - I am Seller - Selling Items."

After the item is posted, it's best to regularly update and add to avoid the store being deleted by the system.

Three, Getting a Free Store

Taobao provides certified members with the opportunity to open a free store. As long as you post more than 10 items, you can own a store with your own independent URL. On this webpage, you can place all your items and arrange them according to your style.

In order to open my store early, I initially posted 10 items at once, spending nearly half a day. After finishing, I couldn't wait to open "My Taobao - Free Store Opening." Then, in the opened page, I named my store "My Shoes, My Show;" in the "Store Category," I could choose the category of the items; in the "Store Introduction," I input the store's brief introduction content. Finally, click the "Submit" button, and on the appeared page, there was a message saying "Congratulations! Your store has been successfully created..." along with the store's address.

Four, Store Decoration is Important

After opening the free store, buyers can obtain a space of their own. Just like traditional stores, to operate normally and attract customers, the store needs corresponding "decorations," mainly including store logo design, item classification, recommended items, store style, etc.

1. Basic Settings

Log in to Taobao, open "My Taobao - I am Seller - Manage My Store." In the "Store Management" on the left, click "Basic Settings," and in the opened page, you can modify the store name, store category, store introduction; main projects need to be manually input; in the "Logo" area, click the "Browse" button to select the pre-designed store logo image; in the "Announcement" area, input the store announcement content, such as "Welcome to our store!" Click the "Preview" button to check the effect.

2. Item Classification

Classifying items makes it easier for buyers to search. In the opened "Manage My Store" page, you can click "Item Classification" on the left; then, input the new classification name, such as "Four Treasures of the Study," and input the sorting number (indicating the arrangement position), and click the "Confirm" button to add. Click the "Item List" button corresponding to the classification, and you can add posted items by searching keywords, managing classifications.

3. Recommended Items

The "Recommended Items" feature provided by Taobao allows you to recommend your best 6 items, displaying them prominently in the store. Simply open the "Manage My Store" page, click "Recommended Items" on the left; then, in the opened page, select the recommended items and click the "Recommend" button.

4. Store Style

Different store styles suit different items and give different impressions to buyers. Generally, choosing a light-colored, comfortable style works well. I chose the "Green Wild Fairyland" style template, and the preview image appears on the right side. Click the "Confirm" button to apply this style.

After decorating the store, a refreshed page appears before you.

Five, Promotion is Key to Success

The online store is open, the items are on the shelf, characteristics are present, but weeks have passed without any sales, not even buyer comments, a problem often encountered by many new sellers. At this point, you need to take the initiative.

I once faced such troubles, so I decided to advertise my store through forum promotion, link exchange, showcase recommendations, and friend promotions.

1. Forum Promotion

The main method for promoting on forums is through posting ads and using signature lines.

The former can be done on forums in various provinces or large cities. If there are sections allowing ad postings, you can post ads with detailed content, beautiful product pictures, and keep them regularly updated and pinned.

The latter involves changing your forum signature line to include your store's URL, logo, promotional phrases, and store name. Post some beautiful threads to interest friends who can visit your store via your signature line.

2. Link Exchange

In the initial stage of opening a store, to boost popularity, you can exchange links with popular stores, utilizing free advertising to promote your store. For instance, Taobao offers up to 35 friendly links, and adding them is simple:

First, use Taobao's search function to find all stores, and record the names of popular store owners.

Then, log in and download Taobao's buyer and seller communication tool - "Taobao Wangwang," add these popular store owner names, and propose a link exchange request.

If they agree to exchange, finally, open "My Taobao - I am Seller - Manage My Store," click "Friendly Links" on the left, then input the store owner's name, and click the "Add" button.

3. Showcase Recommendation

The "Showcase Recommendation" feature provided by Taobao is a special feature for sellers. When buyers search or click "I Want to Buy" and search by category, the showcase-recommended items will appear on the search results page. To set the "Showcase Recommendation" feature, open "My Taobao - I am Seller - Selling Items," select the items you want to recommend to the showcase (items already recommended to the homepage cannot be showcased again, marked with "Recommended"), and click the "Showcase Recommendation" button.

Six, After Selling Items

After selling an item, besides receiving corresponding sales reminders, you need to actively contact the buyer, requesting payment, shipping, and evaluations or complaints after the transaction is completed.

1. Check Sold Items

If a buyer purchases an item, Taobao will notify the seller via "Taobao Wangwang" or email. The seller can also log in to Taobao and open "My Taobao - I am Seller - Sold Items." In the "Contact Buyer" area, click "Leave Me a Message" to leave a message via "Taobao Wangwang;" if the buyer does not use "Taobao Wangwang," you can also record the buyer's ID and send a private message.

2. Contact Transaction Details

After contacting each other, the buyer and seller agree on payment and shipping methods. To ensure the interests of both parties, it is suggested to use Alipay for payment.

3. Payment and Shipping

To prevent situations where the goods arrive but the payment isn't made, sellers generally adopt the "Payment Received Before Shipping" method.

First, request the buyer to pay, usually through Alipay. After making the payment, the seller can open "My Taobao - I am Seller - Sold Items" to query, and if the transaction status shows "Buyer Has Paid, Waiting for Seller to Ship," it means Alipay has received the payment, and at this point, the seller can confidently ship the goods to the buyer.

Friendly Tip: For sellers using Alipay transactions, you can open "My Taobao - Alipay Zone - Transaction Management" to manage ongoing transactions, such as transaction queries, refund management, etc.

After the buyer receives the goods from the seller, they confirm in the transaction status, and finally, Taobao transfers the payment to the seller's Alipay account. This completes the transaction. Don't forget to stay in touch with the buyer, increasing the chances of them visiting your store again and buying items.

4. Evaluation and Complaints

After completing the transaction, both buyers and sellers can open "My Taobao" for evaluation. Sellers can open "My Taobao - I am Seller - Sold Items," click "Evaluate" on sold items, and choose positive, neutral, or negative reviews based on actual conditions, and can also input text content.

Friendly Tip: How to become a highly reputable star-level store owner on Taobao?

To become a star-level store owner on Taobao, your credit rating must be at least 4 points. The basis for the seller's credit score is the buyer's evaluation after each successful Alipay transaction. If it's "Positive Review," add one point; "Neutral Review" adds no points; "Negative Review" deducts one point. Therefore, to become a star-level store owner, remember to provide honest services. If situations like online transactions not being completed or receiving goods without payment occur, sellers can open "My Taobao - Credit Management - I Want to Report" for complaints and reports, but evidence such as shipping receipts, buyer's receipt proof, and Wangwang screenshots needs to be collected.

Postscript: Although it's said that opening an online store has zero cost and low risk, examples of failing to make a single sale and "closing down" are also common. To let your small store survive online, the most important thing is "integrity." Only integrity can win the trust of buyers and gain good credit evaluations, leading to a steady flow of wealth!

Many bloggers have established a fixed readership through writing blogs, varying in number, but all painstakingly built interpersonal clusters through one article after another. This group often shares consistent ideas, concepts, emotions, and styles. Loving the house extends to loving the crows, products favored by bloggers often become favorites of their fans, and products upgraded by bloggers often attract attention from their fans.

The textual power of blogs effectively helps products gain impact. Product descriptions in stores, no matter how good the content, are always seen as product instructions. However, on blogs, product descriptions are considered literature. Bloggers fully display their personalities and expertise in describing products, not limited to direct descriptions, often writing stories, novels, essays, and poems to evoke resonance with appreciators. Blogs also become a new way for bloggers and friends to establish emotional connections through product sales.

Moreover, the interactivity of blogs is more prominent than online stores. The tagging function of blogs is better than general online stores, not only keywords in products but also many keywords in articles effectively help products appear in online search systems. Additionally, blog links expand the influence of products. Friends can make more people see the products through links.
