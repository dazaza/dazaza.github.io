Chinese Title: "Amazing Three Shots"

Production Company: New Image Film Company

Producer: Zhang Wei Ping

Director: Zhang Yimou

Screenplay: Shi Jian Quan, Shang Jing, Xu Zheng Chao

Main Cast: Sun Hong Lei, Xiao Shen Yang, Yan Ni, Ni Da Hong, Ya Dan, Cheng Ye

Release Date: Planned for the New Year's release schedule.

Plot Introduction: Xiao Shen Yang will not be performing his most skilled Er Ren Zhuan (folk art performance). However, in order to let Xiao Shen Yang fully demonstrate his comedic talent, Zhang Yimou specially tailored a role for him, providing him with ample space for performance. Thus, as the male lead of the comedy section in "Amazing Three Shots," Xiao Shen Yang must rely on his personal charm to entertain the audience. "Xiao Shen Yang is funny just by standing there; the story is full of jokes, leading the audience deeper into the film." Zhang Wei Ping said.

Since Xiao Shen Yang is the main force for humor, who is responsible for scaring the audience? The answer is: Sun Hong Lei. The most shocking part of Zhang Yimou's new film comes from the oppression and despair of human hearts. Sun Hong Lei will be the main character creating a suspenseful atmosphere in the film, as he accidentally revealed, "I will kill some other important characters, including Xiao Shen Yang."

Detailed Introduction: The story takes place in an ancient desert, where the owner of a noodle shop named Wang Wu Ma Zi is treacherous and stingy. With such a character, it's natural that his wife doesn't like him. Fortunately, the helper Li Si is handsome and humorous, and they quickly form a secret relationship. After Wang Wu discovers their affair, instead of making a fuss, he hires Zhang San to quietly eliminate them. Unexpectedly, Zhang San lacks professionalism; seeing that Wang Wu has money, he kills Wang Wu first. Later, due to a series of misunderstandings, Zhang San feels that someone has discovered him, so he continues to kill, first eliminating two other helpers at the noodle shop, Xiao Liu and Qi Mei, then chasing after Li Si and the boss's wife. However, evil cannot prevail over justice; Li Si and the boss's wife eventually manage to accidentally kill Zhang San, become a couple, and live happily ever after.

In the stills, Yan Ni is dressed in green, adorned with jewels, resembling an upgraded version of "Tong Xiang Yu". The image of Xiao Shen Yang running across the Gobi Desert in a red belly band is filled with humor, and his disheveled appearance resembles that of a fleeing groom.

Additionally, the stills of Sun Hong Lei killing the boss's wife have been exposed. At the same time, stills of Ni Da Hong and Xiao Shen Yang staring at each other have also been released, with the characters appearing particularly terrified under the backlighting.

"Amazing Three Shots" has maintained a low profile since filming began. Recently, it was announced that the film will be released on December 18, on the same day as "The Siege".
