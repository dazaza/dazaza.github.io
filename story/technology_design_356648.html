"Click to consume, rest assured to be cost-effective" grand opening

by golf8279 on 2010-05-28 16:33:28

On the morning of May 28th, Zhongxin Golf Equipment and Supplies Mall (www.golf08.com) attended the opening ceremony of the third “Click to Consume, Golf Supplies, Reliable and Cost-effective” event. This event was hosted by the Beijing E-commerce Association with the core theme of “Happy Online Shopping, Low-carbon Living.” Deputy Director of the Department of Trade and Service under the Ministry of Commerce, Wang Xiaochuan, participated in this event and delivered an important speech.

Nearly 50 representatives from well-known brand e-commerce operating enterprises in Beijing signed the Click-to-Consumption Service Convention at the event. These companies included Joyo Amazon, Dangdang.com, Vancl, and other influential entities in the national e-commerce sector, as well as Zhongxin Golf Equipment and Supplies Mall, JD.com, Jinxiang.com, Letao.com, Shiji Electrical Appliances Network, etc., which have achieved remarkable results in their respective specialized fields. All these companies firmly expressed their commitment to customer integrity and consistency. Representatives from multiple companies such as Newman, Zhongxin Golf, 18900 Mobile Phone Network, Yongle Ticketing, etc., also jointly launched the “Happy Online Shopping Guru, Low-carbon Environmental Protection Pioneer” campaign.

Finally, Vice Chairman of the Beijing Municipal Commission of Commerce, Li Weiwei, and the attending guests jointly searched for the event website (www.clibuy.cn), officially launching the event.

The “Click to Consume, Reliable and Cost-effective” activity will continue until August 31st, 2010, featuring a variety of rich contents and diverse forms. While promoting the concept of low-carbon environmental protection pioneers, it will conduct specific activities such as flash sale zones, hot promotions, online shopping guru competitions, registration lotteries, etc. Consumers can also participate in related activities by logging into the website via their mobile phones.
