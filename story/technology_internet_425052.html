Mid-Autumn - Things - Words

by topqrrufn on 2011-05-05 16:46:28

As the Mid-Autumn Festival approaches, the following blessings are sent to all dear friends and family, as well as all Chinese people!

Mid-Autumn Festival Greetings - Collection of Wishes 1-1: Though the bright moon is far away and hard to touch, my longing for you has nowhere to rest. Raising a cup to invite the moon, please let her tell you: more blessings! More money! More happiness!

Mid-Autumn Festival Greetings - Collection of Wishes 1-2: The sky is vast, the wilderness boundless, this year's bonus seems so distant! The water bends, the road is long, the days without money seem endless! The buildings are tall, people busy, will we team up to rob a bank tonight... Password: Happy Mid-Autumn Festival!

Mid-Autumn Festival Greetings - Collection of Wishes 1-3: In the cool and refreshing autumn days that coincide with the double festivals, under the beautiful full moon, watch the wisps of clouds in the moonlight. Perhaps it carries my countless greetings, deep longing, and wishes for your happiness and success.

Mid-Autumn Festival Greetings - Collection of Wishes 1-4: I wanted to give you the most special blessing on this most beautiful festival. Searching for the richest words of blessing, but I couldn't find them. Or maybe just a simple wish: Happy Mid-Autumn Festival, may your family be happy together.

Mid-Autumn Festival Greetings - Collection of Wishes 1-5: My longing for you grows day by day, yet my loneliness remains unchanged. How I long to see you again! But I can't find where you're being kept... in which pigpen!

Mid-Autumn Festival Greetings - Collection of Wishes 1-6: Thoughts grow like green leaves, rushing forth, and now it's another Mid-Autumn moonlit night. Gazing at the moon and stars with drunken eyes, my thoughts of you turn into murmurs. Blessings to you!

Mid-Autumn Festival Greetings - Collection of Wishes 1-7: Stars streak across the horizon, I missed making a wish; waves crash against rocks, I missed giving blessings; stories have been told, I missed listening. Life only comes once, I'm glad I didn't miss having you as a friend! Wishing you a happy Mid-Autumn Festival!

And so on...

Each message conveys different sentiments, from heartfelt wishes of happiness, prosperity, reunion, and good fortune, to playful jokes and teasing remarks, capturing the spirit of the Mid-Autumn Festival. These messages express love, gratitude, and friendship, reminding everyone to cherish their relationships and celebrate the joyous occasion together.

Happy Mid-Autumn Festival to all!
