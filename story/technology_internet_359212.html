 Since Ma No became famous on TV, netizens have also started a war of words around her. Actually, the real-life Ma No is not as materialistic as everyone sees her to be. On the contrary, Ma No's past experience as a courier proves that she is a woman who doesn't care about money.

 Recently, a girl who claims to have been Ma No's colleague in the past revealed this information to a well-known website.

 The girl revealing this information is named Fang Yu-jiao. She said that in 2008, she and Ma No worked at the same courier company, and both were ordinary couriers at the time. Their monthly salary was around 1000 yuan, barely enough to support their lives. At that time, Ma No left an impression on people as having a good figure but being very simple otherwise.

 Fang Yu-jiao said that during the six months she worked with Ma No, one event left a deep impression on her, an event that Ma No only told her about. It was in April 2008. In the early summer days when summer clothes were in fashion, one day Ma No rushed to take packages from the courier station as usual and began delivering them according to the addresses. Unexpectedly, an accident occurred. When Ma No passed through a crossroads, a light truck caught the rope on the rear seat of her electric bike, pulling her down. Fortunately, she was not injured, and the bike only had a broken piece of plastic. The only thing that made her heartache was that one package was torn open. Upon closer inspection, she found inside was a beige wide-skirted dress. The delivery slip clearly printed the online store name and address: Lisa's Showroom (http://24juan.taobao.com/), along with the owner's phone number (186 2710 8891). Ma No realized that if the package was damaged, the customer would definitely not sign for it. To check if the dress was damaged, she took out the dress and, upon careful inspection, discovered what she feared - there was a small tear in the waist area of the dress. What could she do? Ma No was very worried. According to the rules, she would have to compensate for the loss. Carefully examining the dress, she couldn't help but admire the customer's taste. The soft and comfortable fabric, fresh and beautiful colors, low square neckline, exuding sensuality; the bow-tied waistband, adorable indeed. Ma No could imagine that the female customer must have a good figure, and wearing it would certainly be sexy and alluring. At that moment, Ma No felt a deep sense of guilt, stemming from a strong sense of responsibility as a courier. Eventually, Ma No decided to take the dress herself, considering it as something she bought, and then help the customer buy another one from Lisa's Women's Clothing Store, while explaining the situation to the customer and asking them to wait two more days. Thus, Ma No found the female customer, explained the entire incident clearly, and the customer understood her, agreeing with Ma No's decision to pay for another dress for her.

 That evening, Ma No turned on her computer, following the store address on the delivery slip (http://24juan.taobao.com/), and found the online store on Taobao. She chatted with the shopkeeper, explaining the entire incident. Unexpectedly, the shopkeeper was deeply moved by Ma No's dedication and immediately offered to waive the shipping fee. Simultaneously, he asked Ma No if she would like to work as a customer service representative in his store, offering a monthly salary of 1500 yuan, which also deeply touched Ma No. In June 2008, Ma No resigned from her job as a courier and became a full-time customer service representative at Lisa's Women's Clothing Store. Thus, due to an accidental encounter, turning misfortune into fortune, Ma No achieved her first glamorous transformation in life.

 Fang Yu-jiao said that Ma No worked as a customer service representative at Lisa's Embroidery Shop for three months and later became the store's model (and still works part-time as a model at Lisa's Showroom), accumulating rich knowledge of women's clothing coordination, laying the foundation for her participation in television talent shows later on.

 From this perspective, Ma No's rise to fame was not accidental. Her experience as a courier allowed us to see an honest and dedicated Ma No, without any trace of a gold-digging image. And her experience at Lisa's Women's Clothing Store prompted her to embark on a path as a grassroots star, leveraging her beauty advantage.
