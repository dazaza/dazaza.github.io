Technical data for Beijing Philips TV repair hotline.

Symptom of the fault: No raster, no audio, but the power indicator light is on.

Philips TV has no raster and no sound, but the power indicator light is on.

Analysis and treatment: This fault symptom indicates that there is an abnormality in the switching power supply circuit or the line output circuit, and the power supply circuit controlled by the microprocessor. After turning on the set, it was measured that the +B voltage output of the switch power supply was only +50V, and the switch power supply was in standby mode. It was found that the power control pin 31 of microprocessor DQA1 was always at a high level of 4.6V whether the operation panel button was pressed or the remote control was used to turn on the machine again, and could not jump to a low level to issue a start-up command. The voltages of the power supply, reset, and 4MHz oscillation pins of DQA1 were checked and all normal. Try to weld off key pulse pins 18~24 completely, at this time, the Philips TV can successfully be turned on again with the remote control, and other control functions also return to normal. Thus, the fault is narrowed down to the peripheral components of the key pulse pin. Re-weld pins 18~24 well, measure the voltage across micro switches SA01~SA08 one by one, and it was indeed found that both ends of SA07 were 1.7V (normally 1.6V and 3.2V respectively), which was obviously abnormal. Replace this switch, and the fault of the color TV is eliminated.
