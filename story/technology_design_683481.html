Woman returns child born from adulterous affair after flash marriage: court orders reimbursement of child support to the man

by xue94fwsh on 2012-02-29 14:38:44

Liu Xin is a person who believes in feelings. By intuition, Han Yu is not the kind of woman he likes. However, Han Yu made a good impression on Liu Xin, and she also won the favor of Liu Xin's parents. Thus, a month later, Liu Xin and Han Yu got engaged according to local customs. In November of that year, under the strong order of their parents, Liu Xin unwillingly walked up the red carpet of marriage with Han Yu. Gradually, some meticulous and sharp-sighted neighbors discovered that Liu Xiaoxiao, Liu Xin's son, looked extremely similar to Qin Hai... The "secret" spread and became the topic of conversation for the neighbors after meals. An intangible pressure surrounded Han Yu from all sides.

Divorce

In early 2012, the west wind wrapped with snowflakes brought a chill. As Liu Xin walked out of the court gate, his mood was as sad as the cold wind. Although he won the legal battle against his unfaithful ex-wife, and the court ordered her to return the child support he had paid for his illegitimate child, he did not taste the joy of victory. The court's ruling gave him a fair explanation, but it could not soothe the psychological trauma caused by his flash-marriage wife's infidelity and having a child with another man...

Flash Marriage and Childbirth

Under the care of Han Yu, Liu Xiaoxiao grew up. This cute and obedient little guy brought endless happiness to his mother while also bringing endless troubles: Liu Xiaoxiao looked more and more like Qin Hai.

These discussions soon caused a great uproar in Liu Xin's family. To verify the rumors, Liu Xin's father and mother-in-law often lay in wait near Han Yu's residence, and finally saw their grandson Liu Xiaoxiao and Qin Hai. After carefully comparing them, they indeed found that Liu Xiaoxiao had no resemblance to Liu Xin, but many similarities with Qin Hai. Considering the deteriorating relationship between their son and daughter-in-law and the fact that their daughter-in-law lived long-term at her parents' house after marriage, the two elders suddenly realized something. That night, Liu Xin's father and mother-in-law called him.

After the divorce, Han Yu had fewer concerns, and Qin Hai even boldly visited Han Yu regularly, officially becoming her boyfriend.

During the honeymoon period, Han Yu was very considerate and full of tenderness, but she could not win her husband's affection. On January 8, 2009, disregarding his new wife's firm opposition, Liu Xin embarked on an out-of-town work trip. With her husband gone, Han Yu, living alone in an empty house, felt lonely and returned to her parents' home. One evening, Han Yu met her high school classmate Qin Hai on the street.

Unexpectedly meeting each other, both Han Yu and Qin Hai were very happy. Han Yu invited Qin Hai to her home as a guest. During their conversation, she learned that Qin Hai was still single, and Qin Hai also read the unhappiness of her marriage from her melancholy eyes. They talked more and more harmoniously, and before they knew it, their hands were holding each other. That night, they spent a sweet night together...

Upon hearing the news, Liu Xin's father and mother-in-law were overjoyed and immediately informed their son Liu Xin, who was working far away in Fujian province.

On November 2, 2009, the child in Han Yu's belly was finally born.

Liu Xin's parents were very happy when they heard that their daughter-in-law was pregnant, and they took extra care of her. Under everyone's attention, Han Yu's belly gradually grew bigger, and her in-laws and parents were full of anticipation. Only Han Yu herself was constantly shrouded by an inexplicable sorrow...

After receiving the call, Liu Xin eagerly boarded the train back home. On November 4, after a dusty journey, he returned to his hometown and rushed straight to the hospital to visit his long-separated wife and newborn son. Seeing his wife's weary face, Liu Xin instantly felt somewhat guilty towards her, and quickly showed his concern by asking about her warmth and coldness. However, Han Yu ignored him completely. In the following days, despite Liu Xin's efforts, Han Yu remained indifferent. Liu Xin's initial joy as a father slowly disappeared in the continuous setbacks. On November 12, with his mood at rock bottom, Liu Xin quarreled fiercely with his wife and kissed his adorable son goodbye before embarking on his journey to a distant place again.

Truth Revealed

Since the birth of his son, Han Yu had repeatedly scrutinized her son's appearance, trying to guess his real father from his looks, but because her son was too young, she couldn't find the answer.

In May 2011, Han Yu went to the residents' committee as usual to collect remittance slips, but came up empty-handed. For the next two months, it was the same. Soon, this news spread again, and the discussion that Liu Xiaoxiao was an illegitimate child resurfaced. "Who is Xiaoxiao's real father?" Unable to bear the public opinion pressure, her parents questioned their daughter, but Han Yu dared not tell the truth and insisted that Xiaoxiao was Liu Xin's son. Therefore, Han Yu's parents asked her to sue Liu Xin, demanding the child support he should pay for Xiaoxiao and proving Han Yu's innocence.

On September 29, 2011, the court held a trial for this case. During the trial, Liu Xin argued that Xiaoxiao was not his biological son, and Han Yu denied it outright. Liu Xin applied for a paternity test and stated: if Xiaoxiao was his son, he would guarantee to pay all child support. His ex-husband's application for a paternity test hit Han Yu's weak point, but she had no way out and could only wait for the test results.

After receiving the call from his parents, Liu Xin's heart was like a pot boiling over, feeling extremely uncomfortable. Although he had already divorced Han Yu, and although judging solely by appearance could not completely rule out the possibility that Xiaoxiao was his own flesh and blood, he still could not suppress his anger. "If Xiaoxiao is not my flesh and blood, why should I raise him?" Starting in May 2011, Liu Xin decisively stopped paying Xiaoxiao's child support.

Three months later, the conclusion of the paternity test indicated: there was no parent-child relationship between Liu Xin and Liu Xiaoxiao. Everything finally came to light. Faced with this test result, Han Yu's defense instantly became powerless, and Liu Xin, who had been full of suspicion, though mentally prepared, could not help but feel angry. When the court reopened the trial, he angrily filed a counterclaim, requesting the court to rule that he stop paying Xiaoxiao's child support and demand that Han Yu return the child support he had paid for over a year, amounting to 8400 yuan.

In March 2010, while working far away in Fujian, Liu Xin heard rumors about Han Yu and Qin Hai from his friends, which made him furious. He traveled a long distance back home to confront his wife. Facing her husband's fierce accusations, Han Yu dared not admit it, but their marriage boat finally reached its end. That month, without seeing the face of his "rival," Liu Xin and Han Yu entered the civil affairs bureau's door, agreeing on the terms: "both parties voluntarily divorce; their son Liu Xiaoxiao will live with his mother Han Yu, and his father Liu Xin will pay 600 yuan per month for Xiaoxiao's child support." Afterward, they parted ways. After the divorce, Han Yu returned to her parents' home with her son, and Liu Xin continued to send 600 yuan monthly through the post office to his ex-wife as child support.

With no way out, Han Yu had to go to court. On August 9, 2011, she sued Liu Xin, demanding the court to order Liu Xin to pay the overdue child support of 2400 yuan.

Rumors

Savoring the joy of her rendezvous with Qin Hai and recalling the pain brought by her husband's indifference, Han Yu strayed further down the path of infidelity. After that night, she indulged in illicit affairs with Qin Hai multiple times. Soon, Han Yu discovered that she was pregnant. A worry crept into her heart: Was it Liu Xin's? Or Qin Hai's?

Living in a certain county of Hunan Province, southwest China, Liu Xin is the only son of his family. Three years ago, although he had passed thirty, he was still single. His aging parents, eager for a grandchild, entrusted matchmakers to arrange marriages for him. In June 2008, through the introduction of a matchmaker, Liu Xin's parents took him to meet Han Yu, a young woman from their town.
