Tian Zhen was reported to retire from the music circle due to a blood disease, and her condition worsened due to her sense of guilt towards her father.

by addintime on 2009-05-27 01:09:20

Singer Tian Zhen, who became famous all over China with the song "Devotion", revealed yesterday (the 19th) that she has suffered from a blood disease for many years. She is now out of the danger zone and is actively receiving treatment. It is known that Tian Zhen has joined the Chinese Celebrity Badminton Team and will gradually retire from the music scene.

Affected by Blood Disease

Qiang Yi, secretary general of the Celebrities Badminton Team, disclosed in his recent blog post titled "After Recovering from Serious Illness - Two Aspired Lifestyles of Tian Zhen," that "after a serious illness, the famous singer Tian Zhen aspires to two kinds of lifestyles: one is the excitement of badminton sports, and the other is returning to simplicity and nature... In the past two years, Tian Zhen went through a serious illness, but fortunately, it was not life-threatening. She has passed the dangerous stage, and after more than a year of traditional Chinese medicine treatment, Tian Zhen has finally regained her 'vitality'."

Qiang Yi revealed that more than a year ago, doctors found during an examination that Tian Zhen had a relatively low platelet count. Through a series of scientific treatments via traditional Chinese medicine, along with frequently playing badminton, Tian Zhen's platelet count has now returned to normal.
