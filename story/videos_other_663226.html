Recruitment Prospectus for Level 3 Remote Training Class of National Vocational Qualification Certification for Psychological Counselors

Huaxia Psychology - the first brand of psychological vocational education in China, the only designated technical support unit of the remote training project for psychological counselors by the National Labor Department, the largest national vocational qualification certification training institution for psychological counselors in the country, and the only psychological counselor training institution that issues dual certificates nationwide: CETTIC certificate and national vocational qualification certificate, both with national-level validity. To date, it has trained more than 50,000 students and owns more than a hundred cooperative institutions.

Shenzhen Jin Yu Psychological Consulting Co., Ltd. was authorized in March 2006 as the office for remote training of national vocational qualifications for psychological counselors, the only counseling station of Huaxia Psychological Education Center in Shenzhen (exclusively authorized in October 2010); the teaching counseling station of the Continuing Education Center and Correspondence University of the Institute of Psychology, Chinese Academy of Sciences; the only recruitment agency in Shenzhen for the graduate class of the Institute of Psychology, Chinese Academy of Sciences; the recruitment and school-running agency in Shenzhen for the school psychological counseling teacher training class; and the recruitment and school-running agency in Shenzhen for the psychological measurement qualification certificate training class. In 2008, it was rated as an "Excellent Branch Nationwide" and in 2009, it received the Outstanding Contribution Award from Huaxia Psychology. In April 2009, it officially obtained the qualification to cooperate with the Shenzhen Labor Bureau to conduct face-to-face training. So far, Jin Yu Psychology has cultivated more than 600 psychological counselors with qualification certificates and psychological service workers with training qualification certificates, some of whom have become outstanding practitioners in this industry.

Remote Course Accompaniment Introduction: The first part includes ten learning function modules: basic course study (mainly lectured by professors who wrote each chapter of the textbook) + discussion and answer questions + homework practice + skills training + pre-exam tutoring + graduation exam (national dual certificate system) + consultation practice + resource center and weekly live interactive courses and online mock exams before the exam.

Module One: Recruitment Object: Medical workers, educators, primary and secondary school head teachers and psychological counselors, university counselors and psychology teachers, journalists, social workers, ideological and political (party, government, labor union, youth, women) workers, enterprise human resources managers, public security and judicial police officers, public relations, insurance, salespeople, etc., and people from all walks of life who are interested in pursuing a career in psychological counseling.

Enrollment conditions refer to the provisions of the National Standard: 1. Psychological Counselor Level Two (meets one of the following conditions):

(1) Those with a doctoral degree in psychology, education, or medicine;

(2) Those with a master's degree in psychology, education, or medicine, who have undergone formal training for psychological counselors level two and reached the specified standard number of hours, and obtained a graduation certificate;

(3) Those who have obtained the level three occupational qualification certificate for psychological counselors, undergone formal training for psychological counselors level two and reached the specified standard number of hours, obtained a graduation certificate, and have been continuously engaged in psychological counseling for more than three years;

(4) Those with intermediate or higher professional technical job qualifications in psychology, education, or medicine, who have undergone formal training for psychological counselors level two and reached the specified standard number of hours, obtained a graduation certificate, and have been continuously engaged in psychological counseling for more than three years.

Five: Certificates (Dual Certificate Education)

After successful completion of the psychological counselor training program, students can obtain the "Psychological Counseling Position Skill Certificate" (CETTIC certificate), issued by the Employment Training Technical Guidance Center of the Ministry of Labor and Social Security of the People's Republic of China, recognized nationwide, and serves as proof of vocational skills. After successful completion of the training, students can participate in the national unified appraisal examination, and upon passing, they will obtain the "National Vocational Qualification Certificate for Psychological Counselors" (OSTA certificate), issued by the National Vocational Skills Appraisal Center, serving as proof of qualification for individuals engaged in psychological counseling work.
