Women's Fashion Fabric Trends for Spring/Summer 2012

by shenzhen123f on 2012-02-08 14:27:16

In terms of raw material application, the women's fashion fabrics for spring and summer 2012 will see a major breakthrough, emphasizing natural comfort, functionality, and ecology.

Firstly, the knitted clothing fabrics for spring and summer 2012 still emphasize natural comfort, balancing functionality and ecology. The T-shirt knitted fabric products for spring and summer 2012 emphasize natural comfort, functionality, and ecology. From the yarn form, fabric structure to post-finishing, they will guide the development of fabrics, making the popularity of knitted T-shirt fabrics for spring and summer 2012 more diversified. Multi-component blended yarns such as silk cotton yarn, cotton linen yarn, high-count segment dyeing, flower gray yarn, delicate high-count mercerized cotton fabric, colorful and elegant jacquard, inlaid flower fabric, imitation woven hanging lines, three-dimensional structured printed fabric are favored. The main categories of materials for T-shirts in spring and summer 2012 include: Mercerized cotton remains the main material for high-end business casual brands due to its advantages of comfort, breathability, softness, wrinkle resistance, and excellent luster; fine cotton products are suitable for young casual brands because of their soft comfort and style that fits the masses; half-flat knitted cotton cloth returns to the original color of cotton canvas, forming a distinct double-sided elasticity, plus bamboo weaving and spot small ribbing. Lightweight cotton linen yarn and unexpectedly light flat knitted fabric formed, cotton polyester products have become the first choice for sports casual brands due to their functional properties such as moisture-wicking, sweat-wicking, anti-ultraviolet, antibacterial, etc.; recycled再生materials have become the darling of clothing brands under the large environment where consumers pursue environmental protection and low carbon. Many domestic and international sports brands have started to apply these materials on a large scale. At the same time, through investigation, it can be seen that multi-component fiber fabrics occupy an increasing share in the T-shirt market due to the complementarity of their performance. In the knitted T-shirt fabrics for spring and summer 2012 in China Light Textile City, T/C polyester-cotton blended yarn 32S knitted dyed varieties, T/C polyester-cotton blended yarn 32S knitted colored varieties, and pure cotton pure cotton combed yarn 32S knitted dyed four-corner pearl ground, knitted dyed six-corner pearl ground, some front-store back-factory-style textile companies and industrial trade integrated professional operating households will be popular after positioning printing and positioning embroidery. Special structured yarns also shine brightly, such as high-count yarn, strong-twist yarn, weak-twist yarn, AB yarn, segment-dyed yarn, flower-gray yarn, pearl yarn, etc., all of which are welcomed by the market.

Secondly, fashionable fabrics increasingly focus on color and creative design. In the new year, everything is constantly changing, and fashionable fabrics will pay more attention to color and creative design. Different from the bland or continuation of previous seasons, the spring and summer 2012 brings us a strong seasonal trend orientation, especially noteworthy is the optimistic spirit running through it, whose core is the minimalist outline paired with bold and vivid tones after discarding complicated details. Boldness is not only reflected in intense single colors, but more so in combinations and Taobao women's wear matching in spring and summer 2012: different colors are combined in a confrontational way, yet create a perfect whole. For example, orange combined with pink and apple green; watermelon red combined with purple, emerald green, chrome yellow, and magenta are mixed randomly without any relation. Hot pink and bright red are combined together in bold color blocks or lively stripes. Yellow continues as a key color for spring and summer 2012, presented in less glaring tones, a series of soft sunshine yellows are fresh and pleasant, combined with white even more presenting a perfect early spring style. Blue continues to show its strong momentum in spring and summer 2012, presented as vivid cobalt blue and ultramarine blue, and can also form conflicting combinations with other bright colors like light green and orange. Emphasizing deeper, richer tones, such as deep ultramarine and ink blue perfectly combined with white linen or denim, presented in a low-key simple form before our eyes. Orange or coral color systems are always the darlings of spring and summer: delicious citrus colors, lemon acid fruit prints; or under the theme of contrasting colors, combining orange-yellow with hot pink. Presenting a warmer tone, combined with warm ginger yellow and chili red, and adding a little cinnamon brown to reduce overall purity. Although this has been a strong trend for several seasons, the performance in spring and summer 2012 will be particularly prominent: designers bring a series of rich saturated peacock blues based on turquoise green and light water green, which can be combined with navy blue and white; or mixed with summer warm brown and khaki to form a soft contrasting effect.

Different from the strong and bold color series, this is another important color series for spring and summer. Inspired by traditional British textiles and interior decoration items, the "freedom" theme includes floral prints, natural linen, and pale-colored suit fabrics, combined with somewhat quirky and neat or elegant shapes. As the new neutral color for spring and summer 2012, here the pale washed-out colors almost lack color, mainly focusing on nude powder, and watercolor-like algae green, light blue, etc. A series of washed white and light gray systems bring a fresh, ethereal color system. In China Light Textile City for spring and summer 2012, products such as dyeing, printing, color weaving, jacquard, cut flowers, colored grids, colored stripes, misty colored grids, misty colored stripes lead sales, reflecting romantic leisure characteristics through the combination of embroidery and computer embroidery, showing new fabric styles through tie-dye products. Fabric colors will be paid more attention to, accompanied by the further embodiment of network advantages, stylishly catering to basic international and domestic trends, thus extending their sales cycle to attract more merchants from all over.
