"Lin Daiyu" - the beauty with an unfortunate fate; Chen Xiaoxu dies of breast cancer

by hanhai1314 on 2007-05-18 10:11:50

After repeated confirmation, it was learned that Chen Xiaoxu had passed away. According to Wang Yuanxi's statement, Chen Xiaoxu started to experience some obvious physical discomfort symptoms more than half a year ago.

"Breast cancer?" Last night, when one of our female reporters tentatively asked this question, Wang Yuanxi hesitated for a moment and then confirmed this answer.

According to Wang Yuanxi's recollection, over the past six months, Chen Xiaoxu's mental state was still okay, but her physical condition became increasingly worse. Two months ago, after shaving her head with her husband, Chen Xiaoxu stayed on the 18th floor of a building on Nanhai Avenue in Nanshan District, Shenzhen. This place is known as the Shenzhen Buddhist Hall.

The entire Buddhist hall covers an area of more than 800 square meters, with 20 beds inside. Only those who are "willing to retreat for more than three months" can enter this place. Sometimes, it also accepts lay practitioners who wish to shave their heads and become monks. The monastery has two entrances: one next to the elevator and another hidden fire escape staircase entrance, providing quite good privacy.

After "May Day," Wang Yuanfeng's daughter and sister-in-law successively arrived in Shenzhen but did not inform Wang Yuanfeng, who was far away in Anshan, about Chen Xiaoxu's recent condition. Yesterday afternoon, someone posted online that Chen Xiaoxu had already passed away. Around 4 PM, a local media reporter sought confirmation from Wang Yuanfeng, who hurriedly made a phone call to Chen Xiaoxu's home. The maid answering the phone said she did not know about Chen Xiaoxu's situation. Under Wang Yuanfeng's almost angry tone, the maid reluctantly reminded Wang Yuanfeng, "Call Xiaolu (Chen Xiaolu)."

Wang Yuanfeng hastily dialed her daughter's mobile phone to inquire about Chen Xiaoxu's situation. Upon receiving the call from her mother, the daughter did not immediately respond but only told her mother, "If the internet wants to stir things up, let them; just don't say anything!"

After hanging up, Wang Yuanfeng became even more worried and sent a text message to her daughter. After a while, her daughter replied: "Mom, my elder cousin passed away on the evening of the 13th. Please keep it confidential for my cousin first."

Thus, the death of Lin Daiyu —— Chen Xiaoxu —— was completely confirmed.
