Don't assume that only men suffer from kidney deficiency and need to replenish their kidneys; women with kidney deficiency are also at great risk, such as accelerated aging, sagging breasts, hair loss, and sexual indifference. If the woman is pregnant, kidney deficiency can also affect the next generation! Beware! These bad habits can ruin your kidneys! Not drinking enough water Many women don't like to drink water because they find it troublesome to go to the bathroom frequently. Remember, the kidneys are responsible for metabolizing waste products from physiological activities and excreting them in urine. During these activities, the kidneys require sufficient water to function properly. Therefore, ladies must develop the good habit of drinking more water. Drinking more beverages than plain water The caffeine contained in beverages often leads to increased blood pressure, and high blood pressure can harm the kidneys. So it's best to avoid drinking too many beverages. Eating too much salt Consuming too much salt increases the burden on the kidneys, leading to reduced kidney function, so it's important to minimize salt intake. Loving to drink beer If you already have kidney problems and frequently drink large amounts of beer, uric acid deposition can cause tubular obstruction, leading to kidney failure. Eating too much meat Eating too much meat can harm the kidneys. Meat intake per meal should be controlled to about the size of your palm and approximately 0.5 cm thick. Difficulty sleeping at night, and even when asleep, you're constantly dreaming. 4. Frequent nighttime urination and frequent urination during the day. 5. Low immunity, always being the first to catch the flu. 6. Sexual indifference. 7. Irregular menstruation and restlessness. 8. Often feeling inexplicable anger but lacking the energy to express it. 9. Loss of appetite. 10. A haggard appearance, often having dark circles under the eyes in the morning, with loose and dry skin. If you experience six or more of these symptoms, be careful about your kidneys and start replenishing them. How to replenish your kidneys? Eating more yams is a good choice As a food and medicinal ingredient with kidney-replenishing effects, yam holds a top position. You can cook yams according to your personal taste preference through stir-frying, boiling, or stewing, consuming 50-200 grams per day. Besides replenishing the kidneys, yams also significantly benefit the lungs and spleen. Snack on wolfberries occasionally Wolfberries have a relatively strong kidney-replenishing effect. The key is to control the amount; generally, a handful per day is sufficient, which can be eaten like raisins. Drink polygonum tea while replenishing your kidneys Use 25 grams of Cornus officinalis, 25 grams of Cuscuta seed, 25 grams of wolfberry, and 25 grams of Polygonum multiflorum. These herbs can be steeped in water as tea or used in soups, providing excellent kidney-replenishing effects. Black beans for kidney replenishment and beauty Use 50 grams of black beans, 15 grams of longan flesh, and 50 grams of red dates.
