A Random Talk about What Happiness Is from the College Entrance Exam Essay Topic in Liaoning Province

by dianxin8 on 2011-04-28 19:25:34

This year, the Liaoning Province college entrance examination essay topic was "What is happiness?" Combining the provided materials, students were required to write an article exceeding 800 words. The following is the prompt, contributed by ks5u College Entrance Exam Resources Network:

Happiness is a timeless topic that truly fills our lives.

Material 1: A philosopher accidentally fell into the water. After being rescued, his first words were: "Breathing is such a happy thing." Being alive is happiness, but why do so many people sacrifice themselves for what they call happiness?

Material 2: An online survey conducted by the China Youth Daily on November 19, 2009, about the relationship between happiness and housing showed that 80% of Chinese respondents believed there was a connection between happiness and housing. However, Japanese netizens had vastly different opinions, with over 90% believing that happiness has no relation to housing.

Material 3: Some believe that the most superficial level of happiness comes from desires and material wealth, but a life driven by desire and materialism can never be satisfied.

Material 4: Others believe that while everyone is pursuing happiness, one should not harm others or even the country in pursuit of personal happiness.

Requirements: Choose an appropriate angle, clarify your stance, select your own writing style, and use "Happiness is" as the title (fill in the blank line with the word or phrase you choose). Do not stray from the content and meaning of the provided materials. Avoid formulaic writing and plagiarism.

The author of ks5u College Entrance Exam Resources Network combines the understanding of a wide range of netizens to discuss everyone's interpretation of happiness.

Is happiness simplicity? Is it the love and care between family members? Is it a false inner world? Is money happiness? In the harsh reality of society, is money still happiness? Can we be happy without money?? Happiness: It’s a feeling! One must experience it with heart, listen to it, and feel it...

Happiness is the comfort of lying in your mother's warm embrace;

Happiness is the sweetness of leaning on your lover's broad shoulders;

Happiness is the kindness of caressing your child's tender skin;

Happiness is the respect of gazing at your parents' weathered faces.

What is happiness?

Happiness is a riddle; ask a thousand people, and you will get a thousand answers.

Someone once said: True happiness cannot be described; it can only be experienced. The deeper the experience, the harder it is to describe, because true happiness is not a collection of facts, but a continuous state. Happiness is not something to show others; it has nothing to do with what others say. What matters is having joy filling your heart, meaning that happiness is in your own hands, not in others' eyes. Happiness is a feeling, and this feeling should bring ease and make one's mood sweet and joyful.

Happiness is when I can't see you, I can console myself like this: Just thinking of you quietly is already very good. Happiness is that I am always connected to you, even if you're not by my side.

Happiness is whenever I think of you, the feeling of spring spreads through the air. Yearning itself is speechless...

Happiness: No matter how big the storm outside may be, you will know that at home, there is always a steaming cup of coffee waiting for you.

Happiness is when lovers grow old together and still look at each other without tiring. Happiness is being able to stay together forever, spending a lifetime together, from this world to the next.
