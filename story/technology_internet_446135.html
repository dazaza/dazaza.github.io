CSN captain burst coarse, CSN captain burst coarse passengers were refused to be loaded and Weibo war broke out!

by shootero on 2011-06-15 10:32:01

On June 9, three passengers including Wang Ziqi boarded the China Southern Airlines flight CZ6800 to fly from Kunming back to Shanghai. Their seats were located at the rear of the cabin. After boarding, they saw there were still three empty seats on the left side of the first row in the economy class and wanted to change seats. The crew asked them to return to their original seats, but no agreement was reached after negotiation. Soon after, the captain of the flight came out and required the three people to go back to their original seats, saying that if these three people do not cooperate with us, we will not fly to Shanghai. The three returned to their original seats, but about five minutes later, the ground police boarded the plane and took them away. After mediation, the three agreed to apologize, but the captain still refused their return to the plane. Wang Ziqi and the other two had to take another flight back to Shanghai. The captain of the China Southern Airlines cursed: "You can't play with state-owned enterprises."

On June 9, a passenger of China Southern Airlines got into a conflict with the captain of the flight due to seat replacement and was forced off the plane. On June 11, the passenger and someone suspected to be an employee of China Southern Airlines engaged in a heated debate on Weibo. The passenger said on Weibo that he would file a legal lawsuit against the China Southern Airlines employee for insult. In response, a netizen named Zeng Ming CSN commented on his Weibo: "You can't play with state-owned enterprises, you media people are nothing compared to the entire aviation industry." This comment quickly spread on Weibo and triggered a large number of comments from netizens. The captain of China Southern Airlines cursing drew criticism from celebrities.

The focus of discussion on Weibo gradually shifted from whether the captain has the right to refuse carriage to dissatisfaction with Zeng Ming's statement. Regarding the phrase "you can't play with state-owned enterprises," famous screenwriter Ning Caishen posted on Weibo: "Indeed, you can't play with state-owned enterprises, long live state-owned enterprises!" Shen Tao, the General Manager of the Board of Directors of Shengshi, posted on Weibo: "Look at the comments made by this China Southern Airlines captain, it turns out that you are all playing with state-owned enterprises, so you are nothing!"

According to reports, the identity authentication of Zeng Ming CSN is Captain Zeng Ming of the A320 fleet of Guangzhou Flight Department of China Southern Airlines. However, there is currently no evidence proving that Zeng Ming is the captain of the involved flight. Yesterday, Zeng Ming expressed on Weibo that his comments have led the issue to an irrelevant direction, hoping for the understanding of netizens.

Subsequently, Zeng Ming again issued an apology letter on Weibo, stating that he has self-examined his mistakes to the company and applied for leave. He would use some time to reflect and educate himself, and once again solemnly apologized to netizens for his impulsive and irresponsible remarks.

A Weibo netizen commented that there have been multiple incidents where flight attendants of state-owned large airlines debated with passengers on Weibo and insulted passengers, released photos of first-class passengers for human flesh search and other malicious events, which seriously affected the brand image of airlines.
