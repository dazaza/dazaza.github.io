Media Revenue Sharing in the Midst of Operating Dilemmas

by zrq827871vxe on 2012-02-08 10:26:44

2009 is the winter for media advertising business. The objective fact is that, media volume itself is constantly growing in orders of magnitude, the emergence of various new media is continuously diverting media audiences, and the reach rate and audience size of a single medium are gradually declining. The decrease in the product of media loyalty and media audience is the shrinkage of media value. The economic cold wave has led to a reduction in advertisers' advertising budgets, especially this year, the advertising volume of consumer goods brands will fall into a low point. In 2009, when consumer behavior is sluggish, the difficulty of market promotion and exploration will certainly increase. However, due to the poor market response, advertisers are forced to cautiously invest in advertising. This will form a triangle relationship of mutual restriction leading to an adverse cycle among enterprises (advertisers), media, and consumers. Ultimately, the core reason for the decline in advertisers' advertising expenditure leading to the decline in media advertising revenue is the decrease in consumption activity. In short, the unprosperous big market leads to difficulties in media advertising operation.

The cost of the media objectively exists, but the value of the media fluctuates, resulting in a decrease in the input-output ratio, which causes the value to be devalued. As the foundation for media survival, the cost of content part (reporting, editing, printing, distribution, and release belong to the basic cost of media products) is objectively existent. However, its commercial value needs to be embodied by the actual income brought by the media on the market scale. Under the heavy pressure of fewer advertisers and reduced advertising investment, media advertising operations face the arduous task of finding more advertisers and larger advertising investment amounts. In terms of advertising input-output ratio, there is also a force that can balance the scale, which is the breakthrough in marketing. The core manifestation is to use marketing means and innovative planning to enhance the input-output ratio of media advertising, namely, to develop the maximum potential of marketing ability to explore the market growth rate of advertising. This is manifested under the premise of comprehensive innovation in enterprise products, business models, advertising creative expressions, etc.

The call for precise advertising placement and monitorable advertising effects is getting higher and higher. The most direct method is media profit-sharing. Profit-sharing marketing is a new model where media (advertising carriers) and enterprises (advertisers) share risks. Enterprises (B)'s products and services become commodities that must spread information (M) to consumers (C) through the media (intermediaries). Consumers' consumption supports enterprises, and enterprises have the energy to support media. The key is the match between consumers and media audiences. The higher the matching degree, the clearer the media's value becomes.

The primary condition for media profit-sharing is to have media. Enterprises can only disseminate information about their products and services through media and attract and absorb consumers to create more revenue. In this process, media is the key. Therefore, after enterprises gain profits, they must return the maximum benefits to the media so that the media can provide more resources to support enterprises in conducting more media communication activities to bring market returns.

Why do media trust enterprises and are willing to pre-provide resources with objective value, such as advertising time slots, pages, and positions, for enterprises to use? There must be a good contractual mechanism and a precise monitoring system that both parties understand clearly. If these can be broken through, cooperation is feasible. Guanglian Era (www.bjglsd.com) has technically fully realized the BIZPROUD Media Profit-Sharing System.

The guarantee of media revenue is the driving force for media profit-sharing to operate sustainably. The principle is simple: while media can make money, enterprises must also earn what they deserve. However, this kind of cooperation requires integrity to last.

A conclusion: In terms of advertising formats, sales-oriented advertisements with the ability to achieve direct sales will gradually replace the share of brand advertisements that only scratch the surface this year.

In addition, to create a higher advertising input-output ratio with unit advertising resources, enterprises need to do more than just simply plan an advertisement. Naturally, many details such as products, operations, management, and marketing planning put relatively high demands on enterprises.
