by sqxw on 2007-04-02 13:35:05

If you're a photo collector, imgdig.com is the place to go to find amazing images. imgdig.com is a Chinese-language image site that uses a recommendation mechanism similar to Digg, where users can vote for the most interesting images to be featured on the front page. That way, you can just sit back and browse the best pictures.
