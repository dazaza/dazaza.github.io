Be the silly one for only Ben Ben

by wwxxq8408 on 2009-12-04 00:54:01

After I don't know how many days, he logged on again and I was so happy. He told me that he still couldn't bear to give up Dreamland, so he came back to play. I gave the baby back to him, and we were just like before. Only I had changed a little, it seemed that I liked to scold people. He didn't like me scolding people, every time he saw me cursing, he got angry, but I just loved seeing him get angry. On my birthday, he sent me a very cute gift - a bubble, a very ordinary bubble, its name is Silly. He also has a bubble, its name is Stupid. He said: "The bubble is a present from me, you are not allowed to give it to anyone else, you must always carry it." Before, I used to run around with my daughter all over the place. After that, I started running around with the bubble named Silly every day. My friends teased me when they saw me, saying: "Only low-level people run around with bubbles, you're already at such a high level, why do you still have a bubble?" I just smiled and said: "I like bubbles."

I had an argument with my boyfriend, in real life and also in Dreamland, so I didn't want to log in anymore. I wanted a few days of peace, so I told him: "I don't have much time to play recently, can you play for me for a while?" He agreed, but after a few days, he read my diary on QQ space. He thought that I didn't want to play, rather than not having time to play, and he got really angry. He told me: "If you don't play anymore, then what am I playing for?" Then he started selling his equipment and pets. I was scared, so I told him: "Can I come back and play? Don't leave! I promise I will never curse or lose my temper again." He told me: "I know this game has made you sad. Since you choose to leave, I won't keep you, and you don't keep me either. Let's both go. One last thing to say to you - I like you." When I saw that he really sold his equipment and pets, my heart turned cold. This time, I really didn't want to play anymore. I gave away all the equipment that my friends could use.

In these few days, I haven't touched Dreamland, but every time I turn on my computer, I habitually open Dreamland first, then bitterly smile, and then close it. Once, I couldn't resist and logged on. Just as I saw him online, he told me: "I want to go play in another zone, will you come with me?" Without thinking, I said: "I'll go." He asked me to go to Chongqing Zone. I said: "I'll go now, you come pick me up."

In this zone, I didn't know what name to choose, so I called myself Silly, the bubble he gave me, I am his Silly. I chose Flying Swallow and named myself Silly because I knew that with this name, he would definitely be able to find me. But I stood in the birthplace for a while without anyone talking to me, so I cried: "Stupid, where are you?" Then a little dragon beside me laughed: "I'm here, silly." It turned out that he had changed his name. He had been standing next to me without saying a word, which annoyed me. He asked me: "Why did you choose such an ugly name? Silly girl?" I said: "From now on, I am Silly, only for Stupid." Although he has a name, I always call him Stupid, and he likes me calling him Stupid.
