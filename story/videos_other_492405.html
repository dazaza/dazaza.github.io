Kindergarten requires to buy 27 uniforms at one time, continuation: majority of parents don't intend to reduce the amount

by iface011 on 2011-09-21 09:44:34

Tracking the Xicun Kindergarten Uniform Incident

Suggestions brought up again after many years

At around 8 in the morning, parents of Xicun Kindergarten had already started bringing their children to school. Most parents interviewed randomly by reporters expressed that they were aware of the controversy caused by the uniform incident but showed a certain degree of understanding towards the kindergarten's actions. Mr. Sun, a parent, said: "Children's clothes are generally expensive these days, and the price of uniforms provided by the school is still acceptable." He added that buying uniforms could even reduce the burden on parents to some extent, "In the past, I was worried that children would compare the quality of their clothes, now with uniforms, this problem can be avoided."

According to the detailed list of uniforms for new students and operational tips provided by Xicun Kindergarten, the quantity and price of the kindergarten uniforms are as follows: four sets of summer uniforms, totaling 232 yuan; three sets of spring uniforms plus two windbreakers, totaling 113 yuan; three sets of autumn uniforms plus one vest, totaling 107 yuan; two winter padded jackets, totaling 200 yuan; one backpack, 33 yuan; total 1005 yuan. If enrolling in the nursery class, two coveralls also need to be purchased, totaling 60 yuan.

Huang Dagu, deputy director of the Education Bureau of Liwan District, also introduced, "School uniforms and kindergarten uniforms help create a good spirit appearance, strengthen unity among students, cultivate collective consciousness, and reduce possible mutual comparison due to clothing among students."

Reporters from the Yangcheng Evening News, Li Chunyu and He Weijie

Reporters also learned that the kindergarten and the manufacturer will arrange for returns and refunds on the 7th. However, this move sparked quite a bit of debate online, with many netizens believing that no parent would return the uniforms, "Adults are afraid that their children will be affected, so if they have paid, they just accept it."

Merchant Explanation: Bidding Required Prior to Sale, Profit Approximately 10%

Reporters from the Yangcheng Evening News, Li Chunyu and He Weijie reported: The controversy over the "Xicun Kindergarten Uniform Incident" is gradually being resolved. At around 10 in the morning on the 7th, reporters from the Yangcheng Evening News learned that the first case of "returning and reducing uniforms" appeared. Parents also hope that the controversy will quickly subside, giving the children a quiet learning environment.

The company's responsible person strongly denied such speculation, stating that providing uniforms for schools "makes very little profit." Taking a summer knitted top as an example, the unit price published to parents is 30 yuan, while the cost price is about 27 yuan, leaving a profit margin of only around 10%.

With so many items, does it involve any interest transfer? In response, the kindergarten's responsible person stated that the kindergarten is merely responsible for inviting specific merchants to the kindergarten, and whether to purchase is decided by the parents and students. On the 6th, reporters also checked and interviewed Longgepai Clothing Co., Ltd., the supplier of this batch of uniforms.

From every middle and primary school having uniforms to every kindergarten having uniforms. Recently, this topic of suspected "forced selling" of uniforms has been continuously fermenting due to Xicun Kindergarten in Liwan District. Can uniforms and kindergarten uniforms be canceled? Almost all education authorities have given negative responses. Some educational scholars have proposed that if they cannot be completely canceled, can they be unified?

Some parents indicated that this time the main issue was the lack of humanization in the kindergarten's approach. "Buying clothes for the whole year at once, anyone would have questions," Ms. Duan, a parent, said, "Children are growing rapidly, usually needing to change clothes every half year. Buying 27 pieces of uniforms at once, if the size doesn't fit, it will be very troublesome to exchange." In addition, what parents complained most about to reporters was the constantly rising sponsorship fees of kindergartens in recent years: "Last year it was more than ten thousand, this year it's already thirty to forty thousand." They pointed out that this is the core reason triggering the "uniform controversy".

Interestingly, according to media reports, as early as 2005, Guo Xiling, then Vice Chairman of the Guangzhou Municipal Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference and then representative of the Guangzhou Municipal People's Congress, also proposed hoping to "unify into one type of clothing." Guo Xiling stated in an interview that each school could use "different school emblems or badges to signify different identities." Guo Xiling also pointed out that from the perspective of parents and students, customizing school uniforms every year means increased expenditure. Once students transfer schools, the original uniforms become useless, which is a waste.

Can school uniforms be canceled?

Is unification possible?

"The simple unification of clothing cannot stop students from comparing with each other," Guangdong Provincial Government Supervisor Li Weicheng refuted the claim that "school uniforms can prevent comparison." He stated that with the changing times, the content of comparisons among students also changes, "Nowadays, students compare digital products and the houses they live in. Would unifying clothing be useful?"

Li Weicheng introduced that school uniforms are now causing more problems. "The country originally did not encourage rural schools to unify school uniforms, but now many rural schools have more than one set of school uniforms." Li Weicheng believed that if school uniforms cannot be abolished, it is suggested that all of Guangzhou unify student uniforms, "The entire process should fully consult public opinion, ensuring openness and transparency."

Education departments emphasize the role of school uniforms, experts suggest second-best solutions—

The reporter from the Yangcheng Evening News also noticed that actually, almost all parents expressed no intention to return or reduce the uniforms. Therefore, until 10:30 AM today, Xicun Kindergarten saw its first case of returning and reducing uniforms. A parent from the nursery class requested to return two sets each of spring, summer, and autumn uniforms, one each of windbreaker, vest, and padded jacket, with a returned amount of 571 yuan, accounting for half of the total amount.

To facilitate parents, the kindergarten has arranged for the manufacturer to add a return and reduction point. The address is No. 27 Guangya Qian Street, Xibay Road, with the contact number 81262016.

However, whether school uniforms should be unified remains controversial in society. Hainan Province recently issued regulations stipulating that starting next year, all primary and secondary schools in the province will have unified school uniforms. After media coverage, this regulation sparked doubts about whether the education department was doing it for the sake of selling school uniforms.

"Since the school has no interest involved, and parents and students have such strong opinions, can kindergarten uniforms be canceled?" On the 6th, at the situation briefing held by the Education Bureau of Liwan District regarding Xicun Kindergarten, this reporter asked relevant responsible persons. Although both the deputy director of the Education Bureau of Liwan District and Principal Zhou Zhifen of Xicun Kindergarten emphasized that whether to buy uniforms is up to the parents, they also mentioned the importance of uniforms without exception.
