The spot price of Q345B thick-walled straight seam steel pipe fell on the 7th day.

by sggotl38 on 2012-03-05 10:24:19

On April 7, the average spot price of domestic Q345B heavy-walled straight seam steel pipe was RMB 4,737/ton, a decrease of RMB 5/ton from the previous trading day. The average price of P20mm Q345B heavy-walled straight seam steel pipe in 25 major cities across the country was RMB 4,734/ton, a decrease of RMB 4/ton from the previous day's price. The average price of P20mm was RMB 4,887/ton, a decrease of RMB 3/ton from the previous trading day. Regarding the steel mill price adjustment, Shagang announced its pricing policy for the first ten days of April, maintaining the prices of rebar and wire rod unchanged. The current Ф16-25mm HRB335 rebar ex-factory price is RMB 4,750/ton, and the Ф16-25mm HRB400 rebar ex-factory price is RMB 4,870/ton. In the Shanghai building materials market, the Xiben New Line member transaction guidance price for Q345B heavy-walled straight seam steel pipe remained stable, with the steel index at RMB 4,630. The selling price of high-quality rebar (HRB335) in the 16-25mm size was reported at RMB 4,620-4,650/ton; the grade three rebar (HRB400) in the 18-25mm size remained at RMB 4,750-4,800/ton. The mainstream market quotations were flat, with the bottom-line transaction price basically stable. On April 7, Jolin Tsai's ["Agent J"] super dance video was released, featuring gravity-spinning colorful ribbon steel pipes. The selling price of high-quality rebar (HRB335) in the 16-25mm size was reported at RMB 4,620-4,650/ton.
