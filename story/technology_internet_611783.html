The Jelly Fractional Radio Frequency (Jelly Fraxel) features a three-stage focused wave design that delivers bipolar radiofrequency energy to the skin in a matrix pattern. The radiofrequency energy penetrates deep into the dermis via microdots, effectively stimulating collagen and elastic fiber production, promoting skin renewal, and significantly improving issues such as enlarged pores, acne scars, fine wrinkles, and uneven skin tone, resulting in smoother and tighter skin.

**Jelly Fraxel Benefits:**

- Smoothes fine wrinkles

- Tightens skin

- Minimizes pores

- Evens out skin tone

- Treats acne and acne scars

**Pre-Treatment Considerations:**

- Stop using retinoids or photosensitive medications 2-3 weeks before treatment.

- Avoid excessive sun exposure one month prior to treatment.

- Inform your doctor if you are pregnant or have other medical conditions.

**Post-Treatment Skin Effects:**

- After treatment, the skin may appear slightly red or feel warm, which usually subsides within a few hours. If redness or discomfort persists, apply cold compresses and contact your clinic immediately. Do not apply medication without consultation to avoid skin irritation.

- In the first 1-2 days after treatment, slight crusting may occur. Crusts will naturally fall off between days 5-7. Avoid picking at the crusts or peeled areas and minimize touching the treated area.

**Treatment Course and Intervals:**

- The Jelly Fraxel treatment is progressive, typically requiring 3-5 sessions with intervals of about 3-4 weeks between each session.

**Post-Treatment Care:**

- Wash your face with lukewarm water (avoid hot water) 3-5 days after treatment. Avoid exfoliation or using harsh cleansers for three weeks. Use gentle cleansers once the crust has fallen off.

- Enhance hydration and sunscreen use. Morning and evening care can include applying brightening essence. Use sunscreen with SPF30+ and wear hats or umbrellas during the day.

- Avoid spicy foods like hot pot for two weeks, but increase vitamin C intake.

- Do not use acidic products (like AHA, BHA, or L-ascorbic acid) or visit spas, hot springs, or saunas post-treatment. Normal skincare routines can resume after recovery, and recommended products from the clinic can be used.

- Test unfamiliar skincare products on the ear area for an hour before use to ensure no redness or allergic reactions.

**Q&A:**

Q: What exactly does Jelly Fraxel do?

A: Accurate bipolar radiofrequency energy penetrates deeply into the dermis, effectively stimulating collagen and elastin production, promoting skin regeneration. It improves issues like pores, scars, fine lines, and uneven skin tone, making the skin smoother, firmer, and more elastic.

Q: Is Jelly Fraxel suitable for everyone?

A: According to medical reports, starting at age 20, skin begins to show signs of micro-aging, with collagen gradually decreasing over time. This treatment targets skin aging and repairs it, suitable for all skin types and tones, making it accessible to anyone.

Q: How long after Jelly Fraxel treatment will results be visible?

A: Results can be felt after just one session, but significant improvements are seen after completing the full course (typically 3 sessions), subject to professional physician evaluation.

Q: Will Jelly Fraxel treatment cause pain?

A: During the procedure, patients may feel mild heat and slight stinging. Temporary redness may occur afterward.

Q: How long does recovery take after Jelly Fraxel treatment?

A: Jelly Fraxel causes minimal damage to the skin, allowing for quick recovery. Makeup can be applied normally after 3 days, and crusts will fall off around day 7.
