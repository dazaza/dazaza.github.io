More than 100 descendants wish a happy 110th birthday (photo)

by mingdaolin on 2009-11-30 11:17:22

Shen Xiuyuan, a 110-year-old birthday star, celebrated her birthday with 305 pairs of headphones and 230 banquet tables. There was also an 11-layer big cake, each layer representing ten years of her life. More than 100 descendants in the family came together to celebrate her birthday. Recently, Shen Xiuyuan, the 110-year-old birthday star from Yuejiang Town, Gao County, Yibin City, had a lively birthday celebration. Many strangers on the road, hearing that the birthday star was 110 years old, also voluntarily came to the banquet to ask for gifts from the birthday star. Yesterday, "super birthday star" Shen Xiuyuan said that she had never eaten health supplements. Her longevity mainly relied on good living habits. In 2009, she received guidance for university level four and six exams.

No dietary restrictions, she loves eating fruits and coarse grains. Shen Xiuyuan was born on August 16th of the lunar calendar in 1900. Despite reaching the ripe old age of 110, she still appears spirited. Yesterday, Shen Xiuyuan wore the red satin clothes she wore during the birthday banquet and sat in the small courtyard in front of her house eating snacks. This rosy-cheeked, energetic "super birthday star" said that she likes eating cookies but doesn't like drinking milk because she's not used to the taste of milk. Her granddaughter-in-law, Gao Shengli, takes care of her daily diet and living arrangements. Gao Shengli said: "In terms of diet, the elder has no dietary restrictions, so her nutrition in all aspects is relatively sufficient."

"Grandma loves eating porridge in the morning and especially enjoys drinking rice gruel," Gao Shengli said. Since the family now cooks using an electric rice cooker, there is no more rice gruel. Once, Grandma really wanted to drink rice gruel but didn't tell anyone, so she walked alone from the city to a rural relative's home to find some rice gruel to drink. "Grandma has very regular meals three times a day, eats very early in the evening, and consumes little food. She particularly loves twice-cooked pork, fatty meat without the skin," Gao Shengli said. Grandma eats without dietary restrictions, consuming plenty of oily foods, vegetables, and coarse grains. She usually eats easily digestible fruits like grapes and bananas, preferring to steam pears and apples before eating them.

Early to bed and early to rise, doing some household chores every day. Shen Xiuyuan said that she goes to bed around 8 PM every night and gets up after 6 AM. After getting up, she tidies up her bedroom first, then goes out for a walk after breakfast. Her grandson, Wu Changjin, said that Grandma insists on exercising every day and also helps with some small household chores, such as sweeping the floor and wiping the table.

Wu Changjin said that Grandma is quite active and enjoys chatting with her friends and going out to play. Wu Changjin said that due to concerns about Grandma's safety, he disagrees with her going to places that are too far away, so later Grandma stopped telling him where she was going. "That year, Grandma was already 105 years old. She went out early in the morning and returned at night. Later, I found out that she walked over 20 kilometers with her old friends to visit a temple." Wu Changjin said that Grandma persists in exercising every day. For many years, she has never been hospitalized. Even if she occasionally catches a cold, taking medicine once is enough to make her better. "And because she chats with people regularly, her thinking remains quite clear."

Wu Changjin said that now there are five generations living together in the family, and Shen Xiuyuan's youngest daughter is already 80 years old, which makes many families envious. He said that Grandma herself pays great attention to her health. He hopes to take good care of Grandma so that she can live well and have another grand birthday celebration when she turns 120.

By reporter He Dong with photographic reporting.
