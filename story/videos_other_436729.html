I. Super Simple VC Whitening Mask

First, let's talk about the ingredients: one compressed mask, a small bowl of mineral water, and an appropriate amount of Vitamin C. The most common white granules can be used, 100mg each. Niuer said on the show that if you are young, 500mg is enough.

Method: Simply crush the Vitamin C, dissolve it in mineral water, then soak the prepared compressed mask in the water until it expands.

Revised Version: You can also use soaked lemon water, as lemons contain a lot of Vitamin C and have whitening effects. Replacing mineral water with lemon water should be better. Then just add two 100mg tablets.

Of course, if you don't have lemon water, it's completely fine. Just add more Vitamin C tablets appropriately.

Effect: After using it and looking in the mirror, you'll be surprised to see how white and tender your skin becomes. Your skin will feel hydrated. And when you remove the mask, you may notice that the area around your eyes feels darker compared to the rest of your face.

Special Note:

This mask needs to be washed off after use because the Vitamin C may not have completely dissolved. If left on the skin, it could cause yellowing and be counterproductive. So don't forget to wash it off after use.

II. Magical Aspirin Mask

On the American MUAmakeupalley beauty forum, there is a popular Aspirin mask. Here is the method for making the Aspirin mask:

Ingredients: 5-6 aspirin tablets, water

Method: Crush 5-6 aspirin tablets into a fine powder using a rolling pin. Add a spoonful of water to mix, then use a cotton pad to apply the aspirin solution onto your face. Leave it on for 20-30 minutes before rinsing off.

Experience after use: Your face will feel exceptionally smooth and fine after washing. Your complexion will become whiter and more even, and blemish marks will fade significantly. The next day after using this mask, your skin will look radiant and feel like you've had a facial treatment.

Notes:

1. The aspirin tablets need to be ground very finely, the finer the better, otherwise it might cause pain when applied.

2. To prevent any allergic reactions, test it first behind your ear to see if you're suitable for it.

3. After using the Aspirin mask, pay attention to moisturizing and use hydrating skincare products to achieve better results.

III. Sugar Washing Method for Acne Scars

Wash your face three times daily with sugar and a little bit of water. Within a week, you'll feel smooth and tender skin, and it's highly effective for acne scars. Try it if you're interested, but consistency is key, and it's one of the cheapest cosmetics!

Usage: After washing your face with cleanser, take a little white sugar. In medical terms, sugar aids in wound healing. Place it in your palm, add a little water and rub gently to avoid irritation and allergies. Then rub it on your face for about a minute before washing it off with clear water.

Experience after use: You'll feel the tenderness and smoothness. Keep doing it for a while, and your acne scars will disappear. Give it a try, it works well!

IV. Silver Ear Soup Method for Fine Lines

Easily prone to fine lines around the eyes? Give this a try; it's quite useful. Of course, prepare it in advance for quick application.

Ingredients: Silver ear (Tremella), water

Method: Cook silver ear with a little water for about an hour, preferably until it becomes a thick soup. Apply it around the eyes. Any leftover can be stored in the fridge, and over time, it will yield good results.

V. Potato Slice Method for Dark Circles

Ingredients: Potato slices

Method: Peel the potato, wash it, and cut it into thick slices about 2 cm. Lie down and place the potato slices on your eyes. Wait for about 5 minutes, then rinse with clear water.

Tip: Applying at night helps eliminate eye fatigue. Larger potatoes are preferred as they cover a larger area. Avoid using sprouted potatoes as they are toxic.

VI. Sweet Rice Wine Whitening Mask

Ingredients: Sweet rice wine, compressed mask

Method: Pour sweet rice wine into a clean container, along with the rice. Crush the rice slightly, then soak the mask paper in the container to absorb sufficient rice wine. Apply for 8-10 minutes.

Then wash your face with clear water and follow your normal skincare routine.

VII. Cucumber Slice Whitening Method

Before sleeping, place cucumber slices on your face for a few minutes. Within a month, your face will become white and tender. Remember to peel the cucumber!

VIII. Pure Water Hydration Method

A bottle of pure water (600cc) available on the market. Use pure water to wash your face 3-4 times a week, and frequently apply it on your face with a cotton pad. Your skin will naturally become smooth and beautiful!

IX. Mineral Water Anti-Aging Method

Pour mineral water into a small spray bottle, spray directly onto your face, and gently pat it with your hands. While hydrating your skin, allow the silica in the mineral water to be fully absorbed, increasing skin elasticity, smoothing out tiny facial wrinkles, and delaying skin aging.
