Let's Discover Traditional Chinese Medicine (TCM) Beauty Written by Whitening Tastes in Intensive Care

Today, let's switch flavors and return to our traditional treasure - TCM Adjustment. Chinese medicine not only focuses on treating diseases and saving lives but also excels in beauty adjustments. Throughout history, countless Chinese doctors have spent their entire lives devising methods to enhance the beauty of palace women, leaving behind a wealth of experience that we must value!

So, everyone, let's join the editor and delve into TCM beauty together~

"TCM Beauty" carries deep Chinese cultural heritage, refined through thousands of years of beauty practices, and passed down through generations. These folk beauty and health preservation methods embody infinite Eastern wisdom.

Whether it's the ancient imperial court or modern cities, women have always thoughtfully considered how to make themselves more beautiful.

And "TCM Beauty", with its comprehensive internal and external adjustments, and its concept of achieving health and beauty from within, is increasingly favored by those who value beauty and health.

However, now with a myriad of TCM beauty products available, what kind of method would be effective for oneself? Which product would benefit oneself? And how should one go about finding the right product? I believe these are the issues everyone cares most about and finds most troubling.

To address this, I visited supermarkets, malls, and beauty salons. I later discovered that supermarkets and malls mostly do not sell TCM products, while some beauty salons do use them. When asking merchants, without exception, they all introduced me to their products, explaining their benefits, customer testimonials, etc. However, these are ultimately one-sided opinions from merchants, making it difficult to find actual users to consult unless it's a friend nearby using the product. I'm not saying their products are bad; each product surviving in the market must have a certain customer base. Personally, I still place more emphasis on our traditional TCM beauty products.

Due to work reasons, I often surf online, so I decided to use the internet to search. Taobao is China's largest shopping and trading platform. Normally, I frequently buy clothes and various international brand cosmetics there. But I've never explored our own TCM beauty products online before. After searching, I found quite a few items here. I decided to inquire at a shop with relatively high credibility.

I clicked into a TCM beauty store named "Green Forest." Frankly, what initially attracted me was the image of an ancient beauty on their products—funny how the love for beauty transcends gender. After reading their introduction, they claimed to be from a family of TCM practitioners, with the store's products being uniquely formulated by their old TCM doctor. This piqued my curiosity—I wondered if they could also create a secret formula for me to instantly become a youthful and invincible young girl! Guangzhou VIP Information Technology Co., Ltd. After reading several product introductions, I discovered that the elderly TCM doctor in the store, around 50 years old, looked like he was 35 in the photo; truly impressive, making me admire and envy him. If I could look like this at 50, I'd be content.

Hmm, with that thought, I consulted the shopkeeper as a customer to see how she would introduce her products to me.

Editor: Shopkeeper, hello. Are your products purely made from TCM?

Shopkeeper: Hello, yes, they are purely TCM. What do you need?

Editor: I want to understand first.

Shopkeeper: Sure, no problem. What aspect would you like to know about?

Editor: Your products claim to be formulated by an old TCM doctor. How long will it take to see results?

Shopkeeper: Yes, they are formulated by an old TCM doctor. TCM emphasizes internal and external adjustment. Unlike general chemical or hormone-based beauty products, the effects won't be immediate, but they can treat both symptoms and root causes, fundamentally resolving problems. Our product course lasts a month, and the speed of effectiveness varies from person to person. Some people see results after one course, while others need more than two courses to see results. Generally, the longer you use them, the better the effect.

Editor: Are there any customers who say they didn't see any results?

Shopkeeper: Yes, some customers get impatient, use it a few times, and then stop using it, claiming it's ineffective. Our products aren't miracle drugs, so it's impossible to achieve satisfactory results after just a few uses. For example, some customers have very yellowish skin but are otherwise young and healthy. These customers generally have qi and blood deficiency, so we adopt dual supplementation of qi and blood and internal and external adjustments. For such customers, adjustments usually take over two months. Some customers give up halfway, saying the product is ineffective. However, those who persist will see results.

Editor: It seems that beautifying through TCM methods requires patience.

Shopkeeper: Yes, because everyone's situation is different, the time needed for adjustments varies. As long as customers persist, their skin improves significantly compared to before.

Editor: Okay, thank you, shopkeeper. I've learned a bit. I'll come back to buy when needed. Wishing you booming business!

Shopkeeper: Sure, no need to be polite. Welcome back anytime.

I believe a product's quality depends on its reputation. The shop's credibility is decent, with many repeat customers. I decided to probe the opinions of their customers to verify the claims. Among numerous online buyers, I chose one with a Yellow Diamond credit rating. After explaining my intentions, she was happy to chat with me. Below is a main segment of our conversation (to protect her privacy, I'll refer to her as Xiao A).

Editor: How long have you been using this store's TCM beauty products?

Xiao A: About eight months ago. At the time, due to prolonged staying up late, my skin condition was terrible, my complexion was dull, my skin was dry, I had fine lines, and even spots. Since I have sensitive skin, I dared not use random spot-removal products and wanted a gentle TCM spot-removal product. I accidentally found this store and started trying it out then. Seeing good results, I continued using it.

Editor: What products did you use?

Xiao A: The shopkeeper recommended a whitening and spot-removal jade complexion pill tailored to my skin, along with an all-in-one whitening and spot-removal mask and a moisturizing anti-wrinkle mask.

Editor: Did it really work?

Xiao A: Yes, after one month, my complexion became more even, my face wasn't as dry, the spots faded slightly, and my skin became a little whiter. Although I wasn't extremely white yet, it wasn't as sallow as before.

Editor: How has your skin improved now?

Xiao A: Now my skin is truly whiter and softer, the spots are much lighter and less noticeable. With foundation, they're almost invisible. The fine lines have also become very faint, and my skin looks hydrated and radiant. My skin still looks rosy in the morning. Honestly, I'm very satisfied with the current results. Moreover, I've introduced some friends to use it, and they've said the effects are great too.

Editor: Have there been any side effects?

Xiao A: No, since the shopkeeper inquired about my physical condition before recommending the products, I haven't noticed any abnormalities. However, because the efficacy of TCM is slow, it takes a longer time.

Editor: Are you still using them consistently?

Xiao A: Yes. I've taken the whitening and spot-removal jade complexion pills for six months, and I've consistently used the all-in-one mask and the moisturizing anti-wrinkle mask twice a week each for eight months. The shopkeeper suggested that I now take the jade complexion pills every two months for two months since they also prevent aging. Women fear aging the most, haha.

Editor: It seems that our long-standing beauty treasure is indeed excellent.

Xiao A: Yes, you can try it out. Talk to the shopkeeper.

Eternal youth is a beautiful wish for humanity, but natural things always go from birth to death, from prosperity to decline. As a part of nature, humans cannot escape this objective rule. Therefore, as age increases, the human body also ages.

TCM believes that although people cannot remain young forever, by replenishing the deficiencies of organs and blood and qi, maintaining the balance of yin and yang in the human body, it is indeed possible to delay the arrival of aging. Believing that in today's era where people advocate nature and green concepts, TCM, with its strength accumulated over five thousand years, still has its unique charm in preventing aging, beautification, and health preservation.
