Hu Guangchuan, a soldier of the Armed Police who was on duty here, faced with the high-speed approaching train, showed no fear in the face of danger, quickly jumped down from the platform, picked up the little girl and handed her over to his comrades on the platform. Almost simultaneously, Hu Guangchuan, with the help of his comrades, quickly leapt back onto the platform.

Xinhua News Agency, Nanchang, January 31st - In the face of the roaring incoming train, Hu Guangchuan, a soldier from the 7th Detachment of the 1st Brigade of the Jiangxi Armed Police Corps, showed no fear and bravely jumped off the platform to save the fallen little girl, successfully preventing an accident.

Before boarding, Ms. Zhao held Hu Guangchuan's hand gratefully and said: "You have given my daughter a second chance at life, our whole family thanks you, and thanks the Armed Police Force."

With the help of Hu Guangchuan and others, the little girl who had escaped danger smoothly boarded the K8716 train with her mother.

When this mother and daughter walked to the right side of Platform No. 3, due to the large number of passengers, Ms. Zhao was pushed by the crowd, lost her balance for a moment, and her luggage hit her daughter, causing the little girl to lose her balance and fall off the platform. At this time, a train was roaring towards them. Both Ms. Zhao and the train attendant were shocked by the sudden situation and stood there at a loss.

At around 20:00 on January 29th, the K8716 train from Nanchang to Fuzhou was checking tickets for boarding. Ms. Zhao, a passenger from Fuzhou, was carrying luggage and leading her 5-year-old daughter, preparing to return to her hometown in Fuzhou for the New Year.

Just as Hu Guangchuan had steadied himself, the train roared past behind him.
