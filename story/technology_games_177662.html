It all started when I followed my elder brother, whom I've recognized as such since three years ago, to this district. This place can be described as relatively chaotic due to the large number of wealthy people here. It's not about who's the richest, but who's richer. Where there are people, there is a江湖 (jianghu - a term for the martial arts world or any competitive environment), and where there is a jianghu, there are disputes. Especially in Hongzhou City, a place concentrated with rich people.

Each district will have an unbalanced development among sects, and Hongzhou City is no exception. PT, MW, and PS seem to have only a few strong individuals. Although there are flyers, they aren't very powerful.

FC, a sect that Hongzhou City takes pride in. Liu Shen Wu Zhu represents them, and has consistently been at the top in terms of strength. Although he hasn't joined any factions, the others still haven't reached the maximum level. Moreover, his skills are probably already fully developed now. However, after observing him PK a few times, I don't think it's just one or two people playing. Sometimes his skills are poor, other times he performs well. Anyway, I'm not sure if he himself is truly skilled. In the early stages, I saw him PK several times in the wild, and he performed quite well. However, he has become more low-key lately and hasn't participated in PK or rankings. Ai Wo ★ Hui Hen Lei is also quite formidable and very wealthy. One night, he dug up over 100 sets of five treasures, which were selling for 1.6 million per set in our district. Anyway, on Sanjie, most of the impressive equipment belonging to FC seems to be in his possession. His attributes are drool-worthy. He is quite competitive, and it's common to see him cursing or being cursed on the world chat. However, the game is like a jianghu, and this is normal. Whether you're bullied or bullying others, it's common. Not expressing opinions here. Ao Qi Feng Wang is also quite capable. In the early stages, he was quite arrogant but also very strong. When I visited his house, it was filled with level 3 and 4 furniture. Sweat! People comparing with him would die of envy. However, it seems like he hasn't been advancing much lately. On the arena platform, I saw their practice before F station, and his magic baby pet burst down over 2000 blood points. I'm not sure how he's doing now. FC also has a few other formidable individuals, but their glory has been overshadowed by others, which is regrettable.
