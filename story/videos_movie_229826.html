Introduction of "Genius Investigator B":

Following the events of the previous season, under Lang's leadership, DIE (Detection Investigation Elite) successfully solved numerous bizarre cases and was ultimately "honorably disbanded." Apart from Crystal taking a temporary maternity leave and Detective Fee retiring back to his hometown in Scotland, the rest of the team returned to their previous workplaces. However, their quirky personalities and unorthodox styles remained unchanged, continuing to cause headaches for their superiors and leading to their marginalization at work.

During a large-scale official event, the former members of DIE gathered on-site for various tasks. Coincidentally, Crystal, who was passing by with her baby, instinctively apprehended her long-time nemesis Big Mouth Ying, mistakenly entering a restricted area. This caused a chain reaction, resulting in chaos and embarrassment for the top police officer... As a result, the senior police management held an emergency meeting. Luo Youheng, Lang's immediate superior, seeing this as an opportunity to get rid of troublesome subordinates, proactively suggested re-establishing DIE. The group of troublemakers, who couldn't be fired due to various reasons, were once again brought together, left to fend for themselves. Since the original head of DIE, Detective Fee, had retired, Heng was reluctantly appointed as the new supervisor of DIE.

Upon hearing about the reformation of DIE, Crystal became very interested and proposed to Lang that she wanted to return to work early from her maternity leave. However, Lang, driven by his traditional male mindset, used some tricks to convince Crystal to take a full year off, focusing on being a full-time mom and taking care of the baby. Later, when Crystal discovered Lang's schemes, she decided not to be manipulated by him and applied to return to work independently. Back at the Serious Crime Unit, Crystal worked alongside a newly arrived high-achieving graduate whose personality and style clashed with hers like Mars colliding with Earth. Moreover, having previously offended the top police officer, Crystal’s superiors feared she would jeopardize the Serious Crime Unit, so they transferred her back to DIE under the pretense of needing her expertise there. Returning to DIE, Crystal entrusted her baby to her extended family for care. Unexpectedly, this led to disagreements between Crystal's mother and grandmother over how to take care of the baby, causing constant arguments day and night. To avoid family disputes, Crystal's father ordered her to personally take care of the baby herself. Thus, Crystal brought the baby to work, where the DIE team took turns looking after it, leading to a series of hilarious situations...

Heng, desperate to quickly resolve these difficulties, continuously overworked the team and deliberately attempted to split DIE, forcing Lang and Crystal to lead separate teams in competing to solve cases. Initially, both Lang and Crystal just did their jobs diligently. However, Lang, with his unique empathic abilities, solved case after case, putting his team far ahead in performance... Meanwhile, Crystal inadvertently discovered that her baby seemed to have inherited some extraordinary abilities from Lang, often able to predict answers to certain questions, helping Crystal make correct decisions and repeatedly escape danger. Doubtful yet intrigued, Crystal temporarily kept this matter secret from Lang. Wanting to protect her subordinates from unjust dismissals, Crystal secretly utilized the baby's abilities to assist in investigations, impressing everyone around her. Eventually, Lang found out about this, feeling deeply dissatisfied. Concerned that such actions might negatively affect the baby's growth, Lang strongly opposed Crystal's exploitation of the baby's abilities, further straining their relationship. At this time, Lang encountered his first love, Wendy Choo. His efforts to help clear her name in a murder case made Crystal extremely jealous, plunging the couple into a cold war... Just then, Xiao Yi reappeared, but this time it wasn’t the real Xiao Yi... Perhaps influenced by his thoughts, Lang dreamed of Xiao Yi, who warned him in the dream that the life-and-death fate between him and Crystal was still unresolved. Although the birth of their baby had temporarily averted disaster, as the baby grew older, misfortune would return, and Crystal would face mortal danger once again. While Lang was half-believing this, the major villain from the previous season, Zhen Xiangrong, reappeared before Lang and Crystal… It turned out that this person was actually Zhen Xiangrong's twin brother, Zhen Xianghua, who had been secretly hospitalized in a foreign psychiatric hospital due to mental issues. He had now returned to carry out his revenge plan…

As for Qing, seeing Lang and Crystal get married and start a family, she felt it was time to pursue her own dreams and went abroad to study. Yi didn’t take it seriously at first, waiting patiently for Qing to return and marry him. However, their feelings had unknowingly changed… Since Qing left Hong Kong, the business of Yiexianqian relied solely on Mei and Pei to keep it running. When Pei encountered difficulties at work, she sought Yi's help. Yi, considering himself a co-owner of Yiexianqian, helped without hesitation. Through this process, Pei came to appreciate Yi's kindness, positivity, and optimism, becoming good friends with him. Over time, while living abroad, Qing realized a change in her life goals and subtly revealed her intentions to Lang and Crystal, hoping they would persuade Yi not to wait for her anymore. By accident, Pei learned about this and thought it was unfair for Yi. Unable to bear seeing Yi continue preparing for an impossible wedding, Pei taught Yi various romantic methods to express his feelings to Qing overseas, hoping to save this precious relationship…
