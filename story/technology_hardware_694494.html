Maternity Evening Dresses Pregnancy does not mean that date nights or special occasions come to an end, and it certainly doesn't mean the end of flattering, attractive, and beautiful evening dresses. For those unique nights out, every expectant mom needs her go-to dress. Whether you choose long or short, or perhaps an LBD (little black dress) or a vibrant jewel tone, there is sure to be the perfect gown for you. Find an evening maternity dress that flatters your shape and is unique to your personal style so you are guaranteed to look and feel great all night long.

One wonderful option for your maternity evening gown choice could be the maxi dress. A maxi gown is incredibly flattering as it creates a longer and leaner appearance. While there are many casual maxi dresses, the evening options exemplify such features as richer fabrics, intricate necklines, and a distinctive gown quality. New for the season is the Gold Band Maxi Dress from Maternal America. This Grecian-inspired design combines rich gold and stylish black for a true evening look. The gold band emphasizes a beautiful v-neckline and empire waist, creating a flattering form that allows the fabric to drape beautifully over your baby bump. Paired with gold strappy heels, you will surely be a showstopper.

The Maxi Maternity Gown from Olian Maternity is another beautiful option. This smooth material stretches exactly where you need it and creates a slimming appearance. The most breathtaking aspect of this gown is its neckline, with criss-crossing straps creating a stunning style. Combined with heels or flats, this maxi gown is sure to impress!

Another popular maternity evening dress style, especially during the fall and winter months, is a dress accented with a jeweled neckline. Jeweled accents provide a rich, warm feel and create a stunning frame for your face. They add unique detailing to any gown they adorn, elevating your look to the next level. One gorgeous option for an evening out with your special someone or a dinner extravaganza with your office is the Embellished Gown from Maternal America. Vintage and elegant, the empire waist creates a beautiful silhouette, and the stretchy fabric ensures your utmost comfort. The neckline is the focal point of the dress, composed of beautiful beading in a gentle scoop-neck design. The jewels, layered on top of the deep navy blue fabric wedding dress, truly exemplify elegance.

Another beautiful beaded option is the Beaded Neck Cocktail Gown from Olian Maternity. The stunning scoop-neck beading against the black creates a high-fashion feel, and the cut accentuates both the neck and shoulders beautifully.

A little black dress, more affectionately known as the LBD, is another fantastic option for maternity evening wear. LBDs are classic, captivating, and flattering. Perfect for cocktail parties, romantic dinners out, and even corporate events, they are versatile, and you are sure to get a lot of use out of them. Though an all-black costume, each LBD has its own unique characteristics that allow you to choose the right gown for your personality and style. The Black Diamond Beaded Gown is a wonderful option from Maternal America. With long sleeves that are great for fall weddings, it has a deep v-neckline that culminates with a beaded diamond pattern at the bust.

Another stylish LBD is the Satin Border Dress, also from Maternal America. Easily dressed up with a beautiful pair of heels, it features a scoop neck and flattering empire waist. The delicate pleating of the bodice and satin band at the hem provide a lovely feminine touch. Wherever you choose to wear your gown, an LBD is the perfect go-to staple for a multitude of events.

An evening gown is a crucial staple to have in one’s maternity wardrobe, and there are plenty of options available for your unique style. Choose the style that works best for your body and fashion sense, and you are sure to look and feel great all night long.
