She appears pure on the surface, but in critical moments, she can unleash tremendous energy. Personally, I really like her.

Chen Sicheng: Actually, from the perspective of an actor, choosing to become a director and screenwriter for "Beijing Love Story" was a major decision. Because everyone has different perceptions of the industry, but I decided to become a director because I had something to say. So I chose to make movies. In fact, the person who influences oneself the most is oneself. Different choices stem from different worldviews. But just like Huang Shengyi, I have never regretted my decision.
