Summary of Windows 7 holiday themes and wallpapers

by geekzhang on 2010-12-24 13:01:30

In December, Microsoft released several Windows 7 holiday themes, including "Winter Snow," "Snow Angels," "Harvest Time," "Holiday Lights," and "Halloween." As Christmas approaches, Microsoft has compiled these themes into one place. Win7 users who want to add some holiday atmosphere to their desktops might consider downloading and using them.

 

Here's a look at the various Windows 7 holiday themes:

"Halloween" provides 15 eerie wallpapers, each unsettling—staring owls, upside-down bats, pale moonlight, tangled branches, and spooky forests that make you shiver. Notably, the Halloween theme pack also includes a series of scary sound effects. Combined with the eerie wallpapers, it can create an excellent Halloween atmosphere.

Official download:http://download.microsoft.com/download/c/f/6/CF64A244-76F5-4AFA-98D9-AE7165201543/EerieAutumn.themepack

"Holiday Lights" contains as many as 17 exquisite wallpapers filled with Christmas ambiance, featuring Christmas trees, colored lights, candles, pine cones, snowflakes, little boats, even coconut trees and cacti. After installing this theme, the system windows will all turn white.

Official download:http://download.microsoft.com/download/B/7/8/B7813136-95FD-4DA2-A399-6E445DDC8C33/HolidayLights.themepack

"Harvest Time" features 18 wallpapers showing golden wheat, bountiful peaches, and purple grapes... seemingly exuding fruit fragrance, allowing you to feel the joy of harvest.

Official download:http://download.microsoft.com/download/7/F/D/7FD44708-0F10-4F9C-B341-8337EC6887CE/HarvestTime.themepack

"Snow Angels" is full of romantic charm, containing 6 wallpapers and sound effects, bringing adorable snow angels to everyone's side.

Official download:http://download.microsoft.com/download/5/7/E/57ED0A09-998D-4A09-AB75-82FD46AFD7BF/SnowAngels.themepack 

"Winter Snow" includes 13 beautiful snowy landscapes, from snow-covered pine forests to birds perched on branches, and shining snow grains... everything is serenely peaceful, crafted by the white snow. If you're someone who loves snow, don't miss this theme.

Official download:http://download.microsoft.com/download/E/6/7/E676F0DD-AD9F-4BCD-AAEE-D9872BF4C9BF/Winter.themepack

If you're a movie enthusiast, you can try out Microsoft's releases this month, "The Chronicles of Narnia 3: The Voyage of the Dawn Treader" and "Harry Potter 7."

Harry Potter:http://download.microsoft.com/download/C/D/2/CD2EF4A8-855C-4286-AC04-BF7713FAEA99/HarryPotter.themepack

The Chronicles of Narnia 3:http://download.microsoft.com/download/E/8/B/E8BFC043-DA49-41B5-AB15-E75BD2612A60/NarniaDawnTreader.themepack
