The usual concept of using a business jet

by shi8x0r2 on 2009-11-20 01:54:29

The typical concept of using a business jet is as follows:

a· You can independently determine your flight route and departure time, effectively flying from any airport to any airport at any time;

· After the plan is confirmed, it can be changed or canceled, with the actual take-off time completely under your control, subject to minimal restrictions;

· When you arrive at the airport, the plane can depart immediately without waiting. At most airports, you can drive directly to the tarmac, board the plane without going through security checks. At most airports, upon arrival, vehicles can meet you at the tarmac, allowing you to leave the airport in just a few minutes;

· On the plane, you can usually make phone calls, send faxes, even access the internet, hold meetings, sleep continuously, or smoke.

b· For businesses, business flights are first and foremost a strategic tool. If a company has access to business aviation services:

· The company can establish branches in any city with an airport, allowing both management and clients to use the company's business jet to travel between locations effortlessly, eliminating geographical barriers for the enterprise;

· Entrepreneurs no longer need to worry about difficulties in arranging business trips. In fact, we believe that business jets can, in a sense, eliminate the concept of business travel because entrepreneurs can return to their headquarters anytime and anywhere. Moreover, the plane itself can serve as an office or a home for the entrepreneur.

· From a long-term strategic perspective for the company or the entrepreneur, a plane can last at least 30 years. Over this period, if an entrepreneur owns a plane, they undoubtedly can accomplish more and do so more comfortably.

c· Secondly, business aviation also serves tactical purposes for enterprises:

· Visiting a city previously required staying overnight, but now business jets allow same-day round trips, or even visits to several cities in one day;

· Before, you had to travel according to scheduled flights, but a business jet allows you to decide your itinerary entirely based on your needs;

· Previously, you had to arrive at the airport at least half an hour before departure, often more than an hour regardless of whether your business was concluded. Now, with the take-off time determined by you, you can complete your tasks calmly before heading to the airport, where the plane will depart immediately upon arrival;

· Before, after boarding, you had to follow the constant instructions of the flight attendants. Now, the plane is your private space. For smokers, one benefit of flying on a business jet is the ability to smoke;

· If you're exhausted from travel, you can almost sleep throughout the entire flight. Business jet seats typically recline like those in first class, allowing you to rest undisturbed without anyone asking you to adjust your seat;

· Your favorite food and drinks are always available on your plane;

· On the plane, you can relax because you're surrounded by your most trusted people;

· Holding meetings on commercial flights seems awkward, but business jets are ideal for meetings—private and uninterrupted. If there’s important work to prepare after landing, you can do it on the plane, which is equipped with computer power sources, internet, and telephones just like an office.

d· If an important client needs to visit your company but struggles to arrange their schedule, you can send your plane to pick them up from their city and ensure they are returned whenever they wish;

· If someone invites you to play golf in Lijiang on your way to work in the morning, you can go for lunch, play golf in the afternoon, and return in the evening;

· Every weekend can be spent in your favorite place, flying there Friday night and even returning Monday morning;

· If you promised to spend the evening with your children, no matter how late, you can fly back.
