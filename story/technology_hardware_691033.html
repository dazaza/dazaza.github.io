Preface to Wu-Wei Health Preservation

Wu-Wei Energy Life Information Health Preservation, abbreviated as "Wu-Wei Health Preservation"

Founder: Ding Zhiming

"Wu-Wei Health Preservation" inherits traditional health preservation culture and combines with my own practical experience. It has been promoted among many students. It is a key to remove difficult and miscellaneous diseases, keep the body and mind healthy, and improve the wisdom of spiritual cultivation. It is a scientific health preservation method that renews itself and is suitable for men and women of all ages to practice health preservation. As long as students who love traditional health preservation can sign up to learn. Traditional health preservation culture is the soul treasure of Chinese traditional health preservation culture, which is the summary of the health preservation experience of the descendants of China.

The theory of Wu-Wei Health Preservation follows the idea of the unity of heaven and man. It also includes the basic theory of traditional Chinese medicine. More importantly, it inherits the essence of Taoist health preservation, reflecting Laozi's "Tao Te Ching" thoughts on Wu-Wei health preservation, Wu-Wei Dan Dao (internal alchemy), Wu-Wei management, and Wu-Wei while doing. The author discarded many theories and practice methods in compiling this book of Wu-Wei health preservation, so that students can benefit from practicing and understand after learning, reflecting the thought of simplicity and ease in the way of health preservation:

Yin and Yang are called the Way; fate is in your hands, not determined by heaven;

Every thought and intention is a method; every movement and action is nurturing;

There is no fixed method in health preservation, just a clear heart nourishes the root of life;

All kinds of health preservation return to the heart, originating from the vast universe; Wu-Wei while cultivating life and spirit;

Combining movement and stillness in harmony with nature, keeping the body and mind healthy is a wonderful way to nurture the heart!

At the same time, Laozi said in the "Tao Te Ching": "In pursuing learning, every day something is added. In pursuing the Way, every day something is dropped. Less and less do until non-doing, where non-doing leads to nothing being left undone." This reflects the spirit of the Way, like water benefiting all things without contention, thus water approaches the spirit of the Way, achieving its goals with a selfless heart, genuine giving and letting go, naturally receiving the rich blessings of nature, allowing people to discard excessive delusions, distinctions, and attachments, having more subtraction and less addition, entering the wisdom of great kindness and virtue.

The "Huangdi Neijing" also says: "Tranquility and emptiness allow true energy to follow; inner guarding of spirit prevents disease." This indicates that if a person maintains a Wu-Wei mindset, actively works yet does not cling to delusions and distinctions, the whole body’s meridians will naturally flow unimpeded, the heart calm and the spirit at peace, the vital energy abundant, righteousness present in the body, naturally leading to a state of health preservation with fewer or no illnesses.

Furthermore, "When disorder has already formed and then it is governed; when disease has already developed and then it is medicated. It is like digging a well when one is thirsty, or casting a weapon when one is fighting – isn't it too late?" This shows that if one does not understand Wu-Wei health preservation and prevent diseases in the future, it is like digging a well for water when thirsty or casting weapons during a fight – it is already too late. If one can mend the fence after the sheep are lost, although it is not too late, the process of recovery is slow and equally uncomfortable. Therefore, preventing problems before they occur is a kind of wisdom in the way of health preservation and dealing with matters.

Looking at the world, modern industry is developed, material abundance exists, but at the same time, humanity has also entered a period of increasing physical and mental stress. According to statistics from the World Health Organization, the number of sub-healthy people is constantly increasing, and the population with difficult and miscellaneous diseases, as well as those with five high conditions, is generally growing. Thus, promoting health preservation projects is a trend for future development and will continuously lead to the formation of a large-scale industry, driving the stable popularization of real industries to meet the needs of people wanting to maintain healthy bodies and minds after becoming materially affluent. People have truly realized that health is the first element of happiness in life, hence, the health industry will be the third major emerging industry after the financial industry and the information industry.

The research and promotion of "Wu-Wei Health Preservation" not only popularizes health preservation methods but also promotes and complements the wisdom of modern health preservation science. Wu-Wei Health Preservation will make its due contribution to the development of human health.
