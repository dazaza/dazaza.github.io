Pioneer cleaning service

Pioneer cleaning after decoration is the first of cleaning projects. Since there are often a lot of garbage and dirt left in construction projects, various ground stones, cement mortar blocks, paint, glass glue, water stains, rust stains, etc., will be left on the walls. All these must be cleaned up in the pioneer work, so it is the most arduous, complex and exhausting work. The quality of the pioneer project directly affects the quality and level of daily indoor cleaning work in the future, so it is quite important to do a good job in pioneering.

(I) Tools used:

Large vacuum cleaner, multi-functional floor cleaner, glass set tools, extension rod, ladder, bucket, duster, marble scraper, scraper, water applicator, etc.

(II) Chemicals used:

All-purpose cleaner, glass cleaner, tile cleaner, ceramic cleaner, degumming agent, stain remover, acidic cleaner, toilet cleaner, stainless steel cleaner, stainless steel brightener, furniture wax, etc.

(III) Pioneer procedure:

The difference between pioneer cleaning and refined cleaning

1. First, clean up the decoration waste left at the scene;

2. Dust off from top to bottom;

3. Wipe the glass: first wipe the glass frame with a towel, then use the water applicator dipped in diluted glass water solution, evenly coat the glass from top to bottom, remove stubborn stains with a scraper, repeat the above process, then scrape from top to bottom with a scraper, wipe off the water marks left on the frame with a dry towel, and wipe off the water marks on the glass with a leather cleaner.

4. Bathroom: adhere to the principle from top to bottom, first recognize the material of the bathroom ceiling, whether it is PVC, aluminum plastic board or coating, then clean according to different materials with different cleaning methods; clean the bathroom walls with a cleaning ball or brush, focus on the gaps between tiles, and the remaining adhesive traces, paint points, and cement stains on the surface of the tiles; clean the sanitary ware in the bathroom with a towel, use stainless steel cleaning liquid for various faucets and pipe fittings; finally clean the floor with a floor washer, especially the corners of the floor, use a cleaning ball and blade to target the corners that the floor washer cannot reach for targeted decontamination and removal of cement stains; finally, check that nothing is missed, then wipe the faucet and other pipe fittings with a dry towel again.

5. Kitchen: same as above. Note: There are more stainless steel components in the kitchen, which should be the focus of cleaning.

6. Bedroom and living room: use a duster or vacuum cleaner to dust the walls, wipe the lamps, switch boxes, exhaust devices, air conditioning outlets, exhaust vents, etc.

7. Door and frame: distinguish the material of the door, dilute the professional cleaner and wipe it with a towel, also from top to bottom, fold the towel into a square block, start wiping from the top of the door from left to right, can't miss anything, places with adhesive stains can be treated with a degumming agent; the process of the frame is the same as the door; make sure there is no omission, no blind spot.

8. Floor cleaning: After all the above are done, it's time to clean the floor. The floor also needs to be divided by material, whether it is wooden floor, tile or stone. After distinguishing, choose a special cleaner and start cleaning. The adhesive stains on the floor can be removed with a blade, stubborn ones can be handled with a degumming agent; after the last process is finished, the team leader should check everything again, confirm that nothing is left behind, and then withdraw from the scene.

9. Baseboard: wipe with a towel, use a blade to remove various adhesive traces, paint points, etc.

(IV) Standards for pioneer cleaning:

Glass standards: No water marks, fingerprints, stains, shiny and clean when viewed.

Bathroom standards: Walls have no color difference, obvious stains, paint spots, adhesive traces, sanitary ware is clean and shiny, stainless steel components are clean and shiny, floor has no blind spots, no omissions, no odor.

Kitchen standards: Walls have no color difference, obvious stains, paint spots, adhesive traces, stainless steel components are clean and shiny, floor has no blind spots, no omissions.

Bedroom and living room standards: Walls have no dust, lamps are clean, switch boxes are clean without adhesive residue, exhaust vents, air conditioning outlets have no dust, no adhesive points.

Door and frame standards: No adhesive residue, no paint spots, smooth to the touch, glossy, no dust on the edges.

Floor standards: Wooden floors have no adhesive residue, clean; tiles have no dust, no paint spots, no cement stains, glossy; stones have no stains, no adhesive points, high gloss.

Carpet cleaning method

Carpets always give people an elegant, luxurious, and impressive feeling. The texture, pattern, and color of carpets indirectly reflect the cultural heritage, aesthetic level, and appreciation taste of the user. However, a dirty carpet is like a dirty piece of clothing, making people very frustrated. How to clean and maintain heavy carpets to make them clean, fresh, soft, and elastic? This requires us to have high-quality dedicated cleaning agents, advanced and efficient cleaning machines, experienced professional workers, and a long-term, sustainable cleaning and maintenance plan. We not only have these basic necessary conditions, but also many techniques and tricks to deal with various special stains, and we believe we can definitely help you.

Carpet cleaning specifications and operations:

Including various genuine leather, pure wool, synthetic fiber carpets, tapestries, bed rugs, decorative rugs, etc. Carpet treatment uses steam saunas, multi-functional cleaning machines, vacuum cleaners, combined with special disinfectants and stain removers for steam mite-killing, sterilization, foam cleaning, removing stains, sucking sewage with vacuum cleaners, drying, combing, and finally treating with anti-stain spray, forming a waterproof film on the surface of the carpet to achieve waterproof and dustproof effects.

Tools and materials used

One, water extraction carpet cleaning (suitable for synthetic fiber carpets)

Equipment used: carpet brush, sprayer, water extractor, carpet washing machine.

Chemical agents used: carpet shampoo.

Operation method:

1. Use a vacuum cleaner to fully vacuum the carpet

2. Dilute the cleaner, or inject it into the water tank

3. Fully spray the cleaner on the carpet

4. After 10-15 minutes, the stains detach from the fibers

5. Use a floor washer to extract and wash, walk backward during operation so that each row overlaps partially, at least go through two extractions

6. While cleaning the carpet, use a water extractor to suck up the already washed carpet

7. Let the carpet dry completely, to speed up drying, you can turn on the carpet dryer.

Two, Dry foam carpet cleaning (suitable for pure wool carpets)

Equipment, tools: single disc floor scrubber with carpet brush and foaming device, carpet comb or rake, vacuum cleaner.

Chemical agents used: high-foam carpet cleaner.

Operation method:

1. Use a vacuum cleaner to fully vacuum the carpet

2. Local treatment is using special cleaner to separately handle oil stains, fruit stains, coffee stains on the carpet

3. Dilute the carpet foam cleaner and inject it into the foaming box

4. Use a hand brush to treat the edges, corners, and areas where the machine cannot reach

5. Use a single-disc sweeper with foaming device and carpet brush to dry foam wash the carpet

6. Act for a while, then repeat

7. Comb the carpet fibers with a carpet comb or rake, this is very important for the appearance of the carpet, especially for cotton velvet carpets with longer fibers, and it also helps to dry the carpet faster

8. Let the carpet fibers dry completely

9. Use a vacuum cleaner to suck up dirt and dry foam crystals.
