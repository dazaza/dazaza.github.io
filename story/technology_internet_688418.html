Company installs fingerprint punch machine, employees buy fingerprint film online to skip attendance

by aiipii12987 on 2012-03-03 10:33:12

Making fingerprint materials on the network, reported by this station's reporter Zhang Jie. "What should I do? I was late for work yesterday morning and didn't get to punch the fingerprint time clock at the office, it's really troubling." Xiao Huang who works in the southern part of the city was originally at a loss for what to do, but a colleague gave him an idea: buy a fingerprint mold online to deal with the time clock, and said it was very effective.

New year, new rules - the company installed fingerprint time clocks. Xiao Huang lives in the eastern part of the city and works in a company in the south, his daily commute takes nearly an hour, and he is often late due to traffic jams. Just before the Spring Festival this year, the company bought fingerprint time clocks for each department and requested that fingerprint time clock attendance be implemented after the Spring Festival. Being late three times in a month would affect performance evaluations, which made Xiao Huang quite worried. On the first day back to work after the holiday on February 9th, Xiao Huang was already 20 minutes late.

A colleague of Xiao Huang's said that there are "fingerprints" sold online, very convenient. If one has something urgent and comes late, an early-coming colleague can help punch the time clock.

Xiao Huang found out online that this kind of "fingerprint mold" is quite popular and has received a lot of positive reviews. Xiao Huang said that if everyone deals with it this way, then the company's attendance measures will be meaningless.

Very popular online, fingerprint molds clone fingers to deal with time clocks. Yesterday, our reporter found several sellers online selling fingerprint molds. A seller from Shandong said that their product series have two packages: A package and B package. The A package includes the production materials and tools provided by them as well as the manual, and the buyer makes it themselves, guaranteed to be taught. This method does not involve leaking personal fingerprint information. The B package is where the seller makes the fingerprint mold for the buyer.

A seller from Jiangxi has already sold 65 products, and on the webpage advertisement says, "One fingerprint mold can avoid attendance checks, greatly benefiting many white-collar workers." In the buyers' comments, one buyer said: "Already bought three times, very useful. Bought again for a friend." But some buyers also said "did not pass."

It is introduced that the so-called fingerprint mold is made from silicone gel imitating human skin, prices vary, and the pass rate on fingerprint time clocks is almost 100%, generally priced around 100 yuan.

Lawyer's opinion: Don't seek temporary convenience and leave behind hidden dangers. Although many people choose to buy "fingerprint molds" and "fingerprint sleeves" online to deal with the attendance punching system, many people worry whether letting others use their fingerprints to punch in might pose security risks.

Director lawyer Wang Liping from Shaanxi Shangwen Law Firm said that fingerprint information, as a form of personal biological information, indeed should raise enough awareness for protection. Legally, there is no explicit prohibition against trading fingerprint information, but it is clearly stipulated that human organs cannot be traded; in the much-anticipated "Personal Information Protection Law", people pay more attention to basic personal information. As society develops, biometric recognition technology will be increasingly utilized, such as access control systems, bank internal authorization systems, etc. Originally, many banks used password input for internal authorization, but now there are those using fingerprint recognition for authorization. Therefore, protecting fingerprint information should raise people's attention, don't seek temporary convenience and leave hidden dangers for oneself, be careful not to let personal information be misused by illegal individuals.

Company stance: No need to walk on both sides of the fence. Mr. Zhang, who works in a public institution, said that last year at the beginning of the year, their unit also implemented fingerprint punching. Because he sends his son to school in the morning, he arrives at the unit relatively early, so colleagues entrust him with the punching task. During the first collection of fingerprint information, six of his fingers were "borrowed" by colleagues, later making the punching system only a formality, eventually being canceled.

The office director of a real estate company said that he specifically handles attendance. After entering the unit in 2005, he found that employees lacked discipline, so he introduced the fingerprint punching system, with very evident results. Hearing that there are fingerprint molds sold online, he was quite surprised. He said that he had even done experiments, pressing fingerprints onto white paper and scanning them, but they could not be recorded because the fingerprint time clock not only identifies fingerprints but also senses a certain body temperature. However, he didn't expect that someone had developed fingerprint molds. Regarding this, he expressed that the attendance system aims to enhance employees' discipline awareness and time consciousness, forming self-restraint. If one cheats like this, it is really unnecessary.

Reported by this station's reporter Zhang Li.
