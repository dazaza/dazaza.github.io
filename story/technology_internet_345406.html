The owner of the foot wouldn't care about Su Yixuan's thoughts, quickly got off the car, and then stumbled towards the roadside. Soon after, the sound of vomiting could be heard. Oh, it seems to be a drunkard, no wonder the car was driving fast at one moment and slow at another. Su Yixuan nodded in understanding. Since this person wasn't there to settle accounts with her, she naturally didn't bother with such a drunkard. Dragging her suitcase, she walked past the half-bent man as if nothing happened. Just as she was about to pass by, her arm was tightly gripped by someone. Instinctively, Su Yixuan let go of her suitcase and executed a beautiful spin. Her hand turned defense into offense. Not only did she free herself from the restraint, but she also grabbed the other person’s hand. At the same time, she kicked out with her long legs. Surprisingly, the other person used an even faster movement and caught Su Yixuan's ankle with their hand. It was a very beautiful hand, with long and strong fingers, round and smooth fingertips. Su Yixuan could clearly feel the warm temperature transmitted from her ankle. Following the hand, she slowly looked up at the man. His clear and elegant features, his amber-colored eyes were as clear as mountain springs, making people involuntarily drown in that coolness. His slim figure exuded a faint scholarly air, but there was a hint of sharpness between his eyebrows. Mixed with his gentle demeanor, it created a contradictory sense of impact. Su Yixuan secretly admired him for a while. If the timing wasn't inappropriate, she would definitely have whistled. "Hey, how long do you plan to hold onto my foot?" "Burp!" Ou Jingze let out a loud belch, opening his somewhat bewildered eyes and carefully looking at the woman in front of him. A face shaped like a melon seed, willow leaf eyebrows, big eyes, a slightly upturned nose tip, and those alluring honey lips. "Who are you?" He murmured, his expression suddenly becoming confused. "Hey, don't care who I am, just let go of my foot." Su Yixuan forcefully moved her foot, but she couldn't break free from his grip. This made her quite frustrated. After all, she was a black belt expert, yet now she was being restrained by a drunk scholar, which naturally made her angry. Ou Jingze nodded, and his long fingers suddenly loosened. Not expecting him to let go so suddenly, Su Yixuan lost her balance, and her heel made contact with the ground. A tingling sensation spread from her heel. "Ouch!" She exclaimed, hurriedly squatting down and gently rubbing her heel. "Hey, why didn't you say anything before letting go? What if my foot gets injured?" While rubbing her foot, she looked up at Ou Jingze, her bright eyes flashing with anger and complaint. This face, so familiar... Ou Jingze stared blankly at Su Yixuan who was squatting on the ground and looking up at him. The originally bewildered look in his eyes sparkled with clarity as he slowly moved aside, but his eyes remained fixed on Su Yixuan's face. This profile seemed even more familiar... But, it's just similar, you're not her. Xia You... "Hey, what are you staring at! Haven't you seen a beauty before!" Su Yixuan stood up angrily when she noticed his eyes still fixed on her. "Keep staring, and I'll beat you until your head is full of lumps!" Such rude words, such messy appearance, made Ou Jingze's eyes filled with increasing disappointment. He looked at Su Yixuan dejectedly and then turned to walk towards the red sports car parked nearby. "Hmph! What kind of look is that!" Su Yixuan snorted softly, reached out to grab her suitcase, and prepared to continue on her way. Suddenly, her gaze fell on that sports car, and an idea flashed through her mind. "Hey!
