An internal email written by Jack Ma - The merger of Taobao and Ali Mama - Age1983

by hexiaoyao on 2008-09-05 12:34:24

Two companies under the Alibaba Group, Taobao and Alimama, will merge to meet user needs. Alibaba will later announce the details of the merger and make personnel appointments for the new company after the merger. According to insider information from cnbeta, Jack Ma sent an email to Ali employees this afternoon. The content of the email is as follows:

Dear Ali people,

Today, I am very pleased to announce to everyone: Alibaba Group has officially launched the first step of the "Big Taobao Strategy" today - Taobao and Alimama will merge and develop from today, and jointly build the world's largest e-commerce ecosystem!

After nearly 5 years of efforts, Taobao has developed into Asia's largest shopping website with daily transactions exceeding 300 million RMB and more than 80 million users. With the efforts of Internet users, online merchants, and peers in the e-commerce industry, Taobao has shown great advantages and vitality in online shopping, entrepreneurship, and employment, and has become an important part of the social economy.

Since its establishment a year ago, Alimama has also become one of the fastest-growing companies in the group, with the mission of helping countless small and medium-sized websites survive, grow, and develop. Relying on its unique accurate, simple, and efficient online promotion service model, Alimama has not only established an excellent network technology and service team in just one year, but also covered 400,000 small and medium-sized websites with a daily network traffic of 3 billion and 80 million Internet users.

Faced with the huge opportunities for the future development of global e-commerce, in order to provide network consumers with a better shopping experience, the "Big Taobao Strategy" will surpass itself based on today's Taobao, and only by taking an unconventional development route can we create a unique business model in the world that will enable us to achieve our set goal of "Taobao's transaction volume exceeding Walmart's global transaction volume in ten years."

We will continue to support all entrepreneurs who open stores on Taobao C2C, and we will help countless small and medium-sized enterprises that need e-commerce to open up the market under the current economic situation. We will always place the creation of job opportunities as the top priority of Taobao!

I believe that the merger of Taobao and Alimama will bring new vitality to the development of Chinese e-commerce and even global e-commerce. But this is just the first step of our "Big Taobao Strategy". There will be a second step, a third step... Let's embrace all changes with an attitude of "Embracing Change"!!

Dear Ali people, I would also like to proudly announce here: After insisting on being free for 5 years, relying solely on advertising revenue, we achieved break-even for Taobao last week!!!!!

In this era, e-commerce is needed, and e-commerce needs the passion, innovation, and dreams of young people like us...
