Hainan, moving forward bravely at a new starting point

Date: January 21, 2006, 12:31:40 Source: Hainan Daily

Moving forward bravely at a new starting point —— Warm congratulations on the successful conclusion of the provincial People's Congress and Political Consultative Conference "two sessions"

Haikou Evening News reports on January 21 that the fourth session of the Third People's Congress of Hainan Province and the fourth session of the Fourth Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference of Hainan Province have successfully completed all scheduled agendas and concluded victoriously. We warmly congratulate on the complete success of the "two sessions"!

This year's "two sessions" is a conference of carrying forward the past and ushering in the future, a conference mobilizing the entire population of Hainan to achieve better and faster development at a new starting point. Deputies attending the meeting conscientiously performed the sacred duties conferred by the Constitution and law, deliberated and approved the "Outline of the Eleventh Five-Year Plan for National Economy and Social Development of Hainan Province". During the meeting, deputies focused on implementing the scientific outlook on development and promoting the theme of better and faster development in Hainan, deeply discussed comprehensive issues, strategic issues in the economic and social development during our province's "Eleventh Five-Year Plan", as well as a series of hot and difficult issues generally concerned by the masses, putting forward many excellent motions, suggestions, criticisms, and opinions. Members of the CPPCC with a high sense of mission and responsibility conscientiously performed their functions of political consultation, democratic supervision, and participation in government affairs, providing many valuable proposals and suggestions to the Provincial Party Committee, Provincial Government, and relevant departments regarding strengthening social unity and gathering various forces to actively invest in our province's "Eleventh Five-Year Plan" construction cause. These important achievements obtained from the "two sessions" will strongly promote the process of our province's reform, opening up, and modernization construction cause.

The "Eleventh Five-Year Plan" period is a critical time for our province to accelerate development. These five years are a crucial time for Hainan to implement the scientific outlook on development, coordinate urban and rural economic and social development, take the construction of civilized ecological villages as a carrier, and steadily advance the construction of socialist new countryside; it is also a crucial time to further consolidate the economic development foundation and significantly increase the total economic volume; a crucial time to expedite industrial structure adjustment and develop pillar industries; a crucial time to quicken the pace of reform and opening up and form distinctive characteristics of the special economic zone system and new advantages of opening up to the outside world; a crucial time to promote the "four-in-one" approach of economic, political, cultural, and social construction, striving to build a harmonious Hainan.

Currently, our province's economy has achieved a historic transformation from restorative growth to accelerated growth, various social causes have made new progress, and a strong consensus has formed throughout the province to not argue, not fuss, and wholeheartedly plan for development, finding a development path more in line with the province's conditions, laying a good material and ideological foundation for the healthy, stable, and relatively fast development of the economy and society. Starting from this new historical starting point, we must spare no effort to overcome the main contradiction of "three lows and one small", and a future Hainan with a prosperous economy, wealthy people, civilized society, and beautiful environment will surely make the world look at us with new eyes!

To outstandingly complete the annual tasks determined by this meeting and the long-term goals set for the "Eleventh Five-Year Plan", we must adhere to the important guiding principles and major policies proposed by the Provincial Party Committee. We must strive to change the mode of economic growth, cultivate internal driving forces for economic development, and realize the continuous and rapid healthy development of the national economy; we must uphold the harmonious development of humans and nature, striving to create environmental characteristics; we must vigorously adjust the economic structure, striving to create industrial characteristics; we must deepen reforms, expand openness, striving to create institutional characteristics. We must adhere to using the scientific outlook on development to lead the overall situation of economic and social development, persist in solving problems in progress with developmental methods and reform-minded thinking, promoting Hainan’s better and faster development. For this reason, we must further play the positive role of the People's Congress in democratic legal system construction and governing the province according to law, using legislation to promote institutional innovation and the development of advantageous industries and characteristic economies in our province; further increase the intensity of People's Congress supervision, focusing on supervisory effectiveness, promoting law-based administration and fair justice, creating a good legal environment for accelerating Hainan's development. We must fully utilize the advantages of the CPPCC in having a wide range of contacts and talented individuals, striving to make new progress in enhancing unity, consultative supervision, offering advice, and reflecting public opinion, making new and greater contributions to optimizing Hainan's development environment and promoting the comprehensive development of our province's economy and society.

This year is the opening year of the "Eleventh Five-Year Plan". Getting off to a good start and beginning well is extremely important for the work of the next five years. Major responsibilities are urging us, sacred missions are inspiring us, and bright prospects are calling us. Let us join hands, be confident, face difficulties head-on, forge ahead, work solidly, and strive hard for a brighter tomorrow of Hainan! The price of 97-octane gasoline in Jinan!
