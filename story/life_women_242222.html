Because it is a very beautiful emotion, a kind of emotional communication far from ambiguous feelings. Both parties must have rational thinking and hold a line of feeling, not too loose or too tight. I admire true male or female confidants! Because blue is the mixed color of sky and sea, requiring a broad and generous chest; red is bright and eye-catching, the tears of blood, requiring generosity and selflessness! If you don't have determination, a selfless chest, noble qualities, and a spirit willing to sacrifice for others, please stay away from this door, and don't step into this door!

May true male and female confidants walk lightly, leisurely and distantly! May their feelings be more pure, noble and heart-to-heart! Blue gets deeper, red gets more proud and gorgeous!
