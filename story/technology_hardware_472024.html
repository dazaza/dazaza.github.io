Classic simplicity, the epitome of precision: ZENITH El Primero Tourbillon Chronograph Watch

In 2010, the all-new Zenith El Primero Tourbillon watch presents a minimalist dial configuration but boasts top-tier exquisite craftsmanship and the strongest heart - the El Primero movement. In addition to having chronograph functionality, it also features a unique date display surrounding the tourbillon, a 50-hour power reserve, stable high-frequency accuracy, presenting an impeccable model of a top-tier watch. The watch is available in rose gold and stainless steel options, making it an invaluable addition to your collection.

The El Primero Tourbillon Chronograph Tourbillon, one of the greatest complex mechanical inventions in the world, uses a rotating frame to encase the movement's balance wheel and escapement mechanism, boldly countering the effects of gravity on mechanical movements. This reduces time discrepancies caused by gravity, ensuring unmatched precision. Installing the tourbillon device into the high-frequency El Primero movement, which operates at 36,000 vibrations per hour, seemed like an impossible mission. However, the skilled engineers at the ZENITH factory achieved this daunting task, creating the world's fastest oscillating tourbillon movement.

- El Primero 4035

- 36,000 vibrations per hour

- 50-hour power reserve

- Tourbillon at eleven o'clock

- Patented date display (surrounding the tourbillon)

- Sapphire crystal glass

- Diameter 44 mm

- Rose gold or stainless steel case

- Stainless steel suggested price NT$1,550,000

- Rose gold suggested price NT$2,050,000

Swiss professional mechanical watch brand Zenith, with its 145 years of exquisite watchmaking tradition, has returned this year to classic and simple watch designs. It has abandoned the previously complex dial settings, showcasing the multifunctionality and high precision of Zenith watches through a simple appearance. Although it has embraced "simplicity," the meticulous production processes and precise movement remain consistent with the highest quality maintained for over a century.
