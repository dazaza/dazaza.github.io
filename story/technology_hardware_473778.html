Globally, beautiful women with both beauty and intelligence.

by doudouqaj on 2011-08-10 11:46:15

The Miss Switzerland runner-up knows five languages and will intern at JPMorgan

The description of possessing both beauty and intelligence fits 22-year-old Swiss model Senya Te Homich perfectly! This runner-up of "Miss Switzerland," who can speak Italian, Russian, English, and three other languages, has been hired by JPMorgan. She will work as an intern this summer at the renowned American multinational financial services institution. The titleholder of "Best Bikini Miss" not only has a thriving acting career but also excels academically, soon to obtain a degree in economics. She has also had internship experiences at Merrill Lynch and the British hedge fund Duet Group.

A woman dominates the European Poker Tour, embodying both beauty and intelligence

As a model, TV host, and astrophysics graduate student, Liv Boeree has a colorful career. But now there is a new label to add—"Poker Queen." In April 2010, this then 25-year-old brunette defeated 1,240 competitors at the "European Poker Tour" and won a cool £1.1 million prize with ease.

An 80s-born beauty becomes the youngest mayor in the UK, leaving her husband in awe

Words like blonde, blue-eyed, devilish figure, and cute face are generally associated with dazzling stars or models, rarely used to describe iron ladies of politics. However, in April 2010, Catherine Bursnall, then just elected as the youngest mayor in the UK from the post-80s generation, was exactly such a brilliant political talent. Her husband Tom said: "It looks like I'll have to be the man behind her from now on."

A 26-year-old female architect from Xinjiang, "talented and beautiful," receives enthusiastic praise from netizens

In December 2010, Paziletin was 26 years old and worked as an architect at Wang Xiaodong's Architectural Creation Research Studio under the Xinjiang Architectural Design Institute. She and her colleagues worked on the Kashgar Old Town Ahe Street renovation project, which was shortlisted for the Residential Architecture Special Award at the Chinese Architectural Media Awards. The result was posted on Sina Weibo, where the tweet was shared and commented on by many netizens.

Russian beauty creates a spicy word website, winning millions of fans

Russian woman Marina Orlova, 30 years old, after years of trying various jobs and failing, became one of the most popular online English teachers in America. In 2007, Orlova started her online teacher journey with a two-minute English teaching video on YouTube. The same year, YouTube created a video column for Orlova, naming the program "Spicy Words." American media rated her as one of the "most influential bigwigs" on YouTube. Today, Orlova has nearly ten million "academic fans" in the U.S.

A 19-year-old college beauty becomes the champion of the 2010 Miss Korea, proficient in four languages

In July 2010, the then 19-year-old Jeong So-ra became the champion of the 2010 Miss Korea pageant. College beauty Jeong So-ra stands 171 cm tall, weighs 49.2 kg, and has an alluring golden body ratio of 33-24-36. At the time, Jeong So-ra was studying at the University of California, Riverside, and was proficient in four languages: English, Chinese, Japanese, etc.

Oxford beauty master's student becomes a hot favorite in the "Miss United Kingdom" beauty pageant

In May 2007, during the "Miss United Kingdom" beauty pageant, a beautiful master's student from Oxford University became a strong contender, overturning people's conventional views about beauty contests. This 23-year-old beauty named Lois Day, from St Anne's College at Oxford, was a social economics master's student. She, along with another 49 girls, entered the final round of the "Miss United Kingdom" beauty pageant. Unlike others, Lois is a highly educated beauty from academia who had never participated in any beauty pageants before. Many of the other girls had experience in large-scale beauty contests.

The youngest 25-year-old billionaire beauty possesses a master's degree

In December 2010, the second batch of Growth Enterprise Market companies were collectively listed. Among them, the most attention-grabbing should be Jin Mei'ou, the 25-year-old director and vice president of Jinlong Machinery. According to the prospectus, this "post-80s" billionaire born on January 16, 1984, is the daughter of Jin Shaoping, the actual controller of Jinlong Machinery. This "post-80s" female shareholder also holds a master's degree and serves as the director and vice president of Jinlong Machinery while concurrently holding the position of chairman of Wenzhou Jinlong Shipping Co., Ltd.

Russian beauty spy holds a master's degree in economics and speaks four languages

In July 2010, British and American media reported that Russian archival documents showed that the 28-year-old Anna Chapman's real surname is "Kushchenko." She was born in Ukraine and grew up in Volgograd (formerly Stalingrad). She obtained a master's degree in economics from the Faculty of Economics at Moscow People's Friendship University. After graduation, she left Russia and traveled around multiple countries worldwide. She speaks fluent Russian and English and can communicate proficiently in German and French. She owns a luxury mansion in London. Besides working in banks and asset companies, she also served at NetJets Europe, the largest business jet operator in Europe, where her clients were all wealthy individuals and celebrities.

An 18-year-old beauty establishes a social networking site and becomes the youngest billionaire

In August 2008, an 18-year-old American girl, Katherine Cook, created a network legend. The website MyYearbook.com, founded by her three years ago, became the third-largest social networking site in the U.S. and was the most popular among teenagers in American schools. It is estimated that MyYearbook.com is now worth hundreds of millions of dollars, with annual advertising revenue alone reaching $10 million. Cook has become one of the youngest billionaires in the world.
