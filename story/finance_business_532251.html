Sina Entertainment reported on June 5th that the TV drama "The Four Young Lords of the Capital" held a press conference in Shanghai. The main cast, including Meng Guangmei, Zhou Jie, and Yang Mi, gathered at the scene to promote the new film. On that day, Yang Mi wore a white long chiffon dress with a fitted top and gracefully descended the stairs. Her petite figure resembled a princess, successfully capturing the attention of many media outlets. Meanwhile, Meng Guangmei was dressed seductively and exuded a sense of worldliness that matched her character's persona.

When asked by the media about her experience acting in this TV series, Meng Guangmei mentioned that she doesn't often act but found this experience incredibly insightful. Guangmei also revealed that her role in the drama wasn't about being surrounded by men every day but rather assisting Yang Mi's character Bai Xue in arranging entertainment for wealthy and powerful guests who came for pleasure.

In this new version of "The Four Young Lords of the Capital," Yang Mi plays Bai Xue, who is the stunning dancer Yin Xiaochai from the old version. When asked if she felt pressured by comparisons, Yang Mi stated that she had put a lot of effort into portraying this character and believed that everyone would like it.
