Moisture-proof, Antibacterial, Environmentally Safe

Wuhan Harvard Liquid Wallpaper is a new type of artistic coating, also known as wallpaper paint, which combines the features of wallpaper and latex paint into an environmentally friendly water-based coating. Regarding its environmental performance, it starts with the raw materials. According to reports, the main raw material of this Wuhan Harvard Liquid Wallpaper comes from the surface layer of natural shellfish shells treated at high temperatures, while others are made from acrylic emulsion, titanium dioxide, pigments, and other additives, thus avoiding pollutants in the internal raw material components.

Additionally, as we know, although the main raw materials of wallpaper are pure natural, less-polluting paper and micro-suspended PVC resin, which do not contain harmful substances like lead and benzene, compared with other chemical decorative materials, toxicity is almost negligible. However, when decorating with wallpaper, some wall adhesives need to be used, which may contain lead, mercury, and other heavy metals as well as aldehyde substances, indirectly causing indoor environmental pollution. Wuhan Harvard Liquid Wallpaper, being a water-based coating, can eliminate wall adhesive pollution and strong odors during use, thereby ensuring the environmental safety of product usage externally. At the same time, because it is a water-based coating, it also has excellent moisture-proof and antibacterial properties, making it resistant to pests and aging.
