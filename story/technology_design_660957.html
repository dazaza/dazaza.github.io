Wallpaper Effect: Half Depends on the Product, Half on Installation

In the minds of many consumers, when using wallpaper, they primarily focus on whether the quality of the wallpaper is environmentally friendly and durable, and whether the product's design matches their aesthetic and taste. The installation process of wallpaper, however, often gets overlooked by many wallpaper brands and consumers. Since wallpaper is a semi-finished product, its installation directly affects the decorative effect and usability. Neglecting the installation greatly impacts the healthy and orderly development of the industry.

As wallpaper production technology continues to evolve, design patterns become more contemporary, materials more environmentally friendly, and varieties constantly innovate and increase, greatly satisfying consumer choices. At the same time, this places high demands on wallpaper installation techniques.

The Importance of Wallpaper Installation Gets Overlooked

Within the wallpaper industry, there has long been a saying: "half product, half installation," which sufficiently proves the importance of wallpaper installation in the entire consumer experience. However, many wallpaper brands still prioritize product sales without adequately following up with relevant installation services. This has led to inconsistent construction teams within the wallpaper industry. Without professional personnel to inspect the wall base before installation, and without professional construction materials and tools, issues frequently arise after wallpaper installation that leave consumers highly dissatisfied. Due to the lack of an integrated sales and installation service system, when problems occur with wallpaper products, it is easy for the sales party and the installation party to shirk responsibility, leaving no one to resolve the issue. This not only affects the mood but also damages the economic interests of consumers, while simultaneously causing disorder in the current home decoration market due to a lack of effective supervision.

Experts remind that not all walls can be directly pasted with wallpaper. The wall must be dry, clean, smooth, have aligned angles, and possess a certain load-bearing capacity with moderate water absorption. If the wall is not simply inspected or treated before construction, it may affect the decorative effect of the wallpaper and reduce its lifespan. Besides being closely related to wall quality, the presentation of wallpaper's decorative effect and the guarantee of its use quality also depend significantly on professional installation tools. As the saying goes, "To do a job well, one must first sharpen his tools." Professional tools not only enhance installation efficiency but are also a necessary guarantee for installation quality. The crude tools commonly used by wandering construction teams in the market easily cause unnecessary damage and contamination of the wallpaper, failing to ensure the quality of the construction.
