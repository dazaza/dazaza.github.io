Fashion Women's Clothing Online Store Agent Talks about Online Store Decoration

by anonymous on 2011-03-25 14:18:08

Fashion women's clothing online store agents, especially professional fashion women's clothing online store agents, are definitely willing to spend time and money on their online store decoration. It can be said that a good fashion women's clothing online store agent must have a beautiful fashion women's clothing online store, which is the thought derived from continuously observing store decoration. To put it plainly, life is full of attractions everywhere, and decoration is not ordinary. Fashion women's clothing online store agents must create a fashion women's clothing online store with their own style to attract customers. Below, the editor will introduce the experience of "Fashion Women's Clothing Agents Talking about Online Store Decoration."

For fashion women's clothing online stores, the key lies in specific representation. Simply put, what is usually referred to as store decoration includes: store name, logo, bulletin board, store classification, product name, product image, product template, personal space, and store introduction. (With these personalized store solutions, the personality and style of fashion women's clothing agents are fully revealed in the decoration.)

Some people will tell you that your store name should be distinctive, putting the category of the products you sell in the store name and choosing a clever name to leave a deep impression. Others will tell you that there are many people online who can help you design logos, product description templates, bulletin board images, etc., making your online store more beautiful. However, what I want to say here is that just like now when women buy clothes to highlight individuality, they don't buy expensive ones but only buy suitable ones. The basic principle of fashion women's clothing store decoration is also: not to choose pretty ones, but to choose suitable ones.

What is suitable? On the premise of establishing a decoration theme, all things that can highlight your theme, accompany, reflect, emphasize, and bring out the essence of your theme are suitable.

Therefore, please establish your decoration theme before decorating your store.

The principle is very simple, but many people take detours. Some fashion women's clothing agents see a pretty doll logo today and use it, then find a leaf heart ring for the bulletin board picture tomorrow. They hang a lotus-shaped counter in the store, use a landscape painting in the store introduction, and use comic-style small icons in the product description template. They still think it's beautiful... A pile of beautiful things patched together without a theme makes the overall look out of place.

How to achieve consistent styling, let's share some experiences from fashion women's clothing online store agents:

1. Store Name: A distinctive store name can also add recent mood or activity themes. Since Taobao store names can be modified at will and accommodate a long character limit, you can freely add some mood themes or activity themes to the store name. For example, in August: ada51 Fashion Apparel Mall Summer Clearance Old Customers Free Shipping, and in September it can become ada51 Fashion Apparel Mall Autumn Welcomes National Day Partially Discounted, and in winter, it can say ada51 Fashion Apparel Mall Cold Wave Coming Beware of Colds. Every month, every quarter can change, letting your old customers feel your recent changes and allowing new customers entering the store to clearly see your current new discounts. This is something many people overlook. Someone says to write something in the Wangwang note, but frankly, this thing doesn't get much attention. Writing in the Wangwang note is not as effective as writing in your store title, straightforward and with high visibility. (Similarly, there is Wangwang status, a clever method is to post a product name on your status every 3 days or so. With so many items, posting a different one every few days won't repeat, and it will give the impression that your store is constantly restocking. New buyers will know your signature products when they chat with you, and old buyers will know what new goods you have.)

2. Logo: As the image of a fashion women's clothing store, it must not be ugly. When designing a logo, you must have your own ideas and incorporate the essence of your shop. Generally, fashion women's clothing LOGOs should be fashionable, warm, healthy, and feminine.

3. Bulletin Board: Some people like to make it dazzling, no problem, as long as you don't forget to add some words, such as "Welcome to our little shop" or add information about your recent activities, these are okay. Just one point, many bulletin board pictures are not transparent GIF formats and are quite large, either too long or covering the items or recommended spots. So here's a tip: convert the picture to a transparent GIF format, so it won't obstruct people from viewing the fashion women's clothing in your store.

4. Counter: Nothing much to say, plenty available online, go find one. Don't pick large picture ones; delicate and compact designs are better. Beautiful counter pictures and beautiful bulletin board pictures appearing together may shift focus. Ask yourself, do you want viewers' attention on your counter and bulletin board pictures or on your products?

5. Store Classification: This is a big part. Seeing an interesting store name leads to entering, seeing beautiful counters and bulletin boards lets you know recent activities, next is looking at products and classifications. Store classifications generally come in two types: text and pictures. Many big sellers don't use gimmicks, plain text records for classifications work well, and some incorporate skillful symbols, giving the impression of organized categories, which is also good.

Suggestions:

(1) After one category ends, you can separate it from the new category by leaving a classification position. This prevents congestion. Some might ask, how to separate? New classifications must have text, otherwise, a new classification cannot be created. Simple, just type a symbol '.' to create a new classification.

(2) Category names should be concise, hitting the mark. You can even inspire a change, making it feel fresh yet relevant to the product theme. This way, your store and products are half successful. This requires inspiration entirely. Now I understand why those sudden ideas are so precious. It has a lot to do with your knowledge saturation, sensitivity, even culture and literary cultivation.

(3) Certain prominent categories can be presented differently from other categories. For instance, if this month's highlight is the half-price discount category, then the design of this category must differ from others. If the others are plain text, then the half-price discount could use a floating effect. Or make the simplest graphic, using red letters to mark 'Half-Price Discount' and place the image in the category section. Exaggerated graphics are fine as long as they highlight the main idea. However, don't overdo the gimmicks. The editor believes that if your category sections are less than 10, then the fancy sections should not exceed 3. More would dilute the quality.

(4) Those categories with red crosses can simply be replaced with Chinese characters.

6. Product Name: My friend also runs a store, she asked me to help her check. I told her that many people sell the same products as you, so attracting attention depends on the product name. This topic is profound. I know a seller whose name I can't mention here, it's really unfortunate. Each of her products takes a line from classical poetry, incredibly graceful. After visiting her fashion women's clothing store, you'll feel the strong cultural atmosphere within. This isn't something achieved by casually searching for poems online; it's closely related to personal cultivation. Unfortunately, her women's clothing is relatively expensive, with similar clothes sold cheaper elsewhere. Another approach is to use titles like 'Must-Have for Elegant Ladies' or 'Essential for Trendy Girls', attracting customers to click and view products involuntarily. Technically speaking, analyzing the naming characteristics of these two individuals, the former’s features include: store name, brand, fabric, material, characteristics, clothing type, personalized description. The latter includes brand, color, eye-catching decorations, original order evidence, preferential price/occasion attended. Of course, logically, if you want your product to be searched by more people, you need to include keywords in the search. Combining keywords with your personal style would be perfect.

Imagine if all the results of your search are repetitive brands and products, descriptions that stand out will gain your favor, leading you to click.

Special Tip: In Taobao stores, product names can only display the first 18 Chinese characters (if searched, the full name is displayed, but people generally focus on these first 18 characters). Therefore, the essence of your product name must be included within these 18 characters (including some symbols). Personal suggestion: Please include your store name or abbreviation in the product name, generally not exceeding 4 characters, symbols less than or equal to 2, English or Chinese brands consider the arrangement order. After all, you need to place the elements that attract attention within the first 18 characters.

7. Product Pictures: This is another significant part. I won’t elaborate much here as there are many resources teaching shooting techniques, watermarks to prevent theft, etc. But now I generally avoid buying items with heavily PS-ed pictures; they appear fake and uncomfortable. Real items might not be great either. So my own photos aren't PS-ed (though some were before, and I'm gradually replacing them). Achieving this isn't difficult: ensure a clean background paper or cloth, sufficient light source (sun, lamp, mirror), and a not-too-outdated camera. The photos might not be as beautiful as PS-ed ones, but they never mislead customers with discrepancies between pictures and actual items. Personally, I believe this is crucial. I once saw someone saying that it's best to maintain a consistent background when taking photos; otherwise, customers might lose interest when they see chaotic backgrounds. This statement is correct, and those who realize this are usually already on the right track as sellers or buyers. Of course, some might say that their store is chaotic but still attracts many buyers. Remember, e-commerce is a trend, more and more people will buy online, and experienced buyers will develop discerning eyes over time. Skilled buyers can decide whether to stay and browse after just one glance at your store. Such buyers will increase.

8. Product Description Template: First, I admit my template is unsuccessful. I've seen many sellers without templates doing well, but if you use the right template, it makes buyers feel you're dedicated to running your store. Over time, your style will resonate deeply with them, creating special affection. However, many templates online are generic, just a frame: welcome message on top, other categories on the left or right, and a large space in the middle for the item picture. These look extremely uncoordinated because many item pictures have non-pure backgrounds, some placed on beds, others on flower fabrics. Colors, combinations, styles, patterns, and templates are completely mismatched, making them awkward and unattractive. That's the truth, and if you don't want to hear it, I can't help it. This aligns with the reason for LOGO design mentioned earlier: a unified decoration theme must exist; otherwise, it's just a mess, getting uglier the more you decorate.

Are you trying to show off your beautiful template or draw attention to your self-taken pictures that might look bad? Many notice their pictures don't fit the template, so they simply remove the pictures, leaving blanks, forcing buyers to painstakingly search for detailed information (length, width, height, chest/waist/shoulder/diameter size) among various unrelated content. Ultimately, you might only see a bunch of bank accounts and close the page.

Suggestions:

(1) When your template doesn't suit you, don't use it.

(2) To highlight your product pictures, other contents of your template must be concise and clear, avoiding flashy large images. The goal is one: highlighting your own product pictures.

(3) Basic elements of product descriptions: specific product name, dimensions, price, and optionally shipping cost information, discount information, etc. If possible, set up a server to upload pictures, then add the picture addresses to the product description. Visitors will immediately see real pictures and details.

9. Personal Space and Store Introduction: Like chicken ribs, tasteless to eat but hard to discard. Writing something is always better than nothing. Usually, write trading principles, such as fixed discount measures, shipping cost standards, and professional knowledge related to the products you sell. More personalized approaches depend on your individual creativity.

10. Others: Including customized transaction messages, monthly electronic newsletters, these are all good ideas, but one person might not handle them all. Actually, websites targeting personal business in mainland China form a tripod, each with personalized services. You can fully utilize their advantages to comprehensively expand your business.

After saying so much and providing examples, I hope it helps sellers identify areas in their stores worth improving. In general, all decorative elements should align with the theme. For instance, if your theme is kawaii, Japanese cute style, then in the store name, product names, descriptions, and category names, you can lean towards cuteness. Use candy colors, sweet, cute-related words in product names, choose fonts and materials in watermarks, bulletin boards, or category pictures that match your product style, such as Hua Kang Shao Nv Ti (font), and use more descriptions favored by young girls in the descriptions, letting buyers feel your coherent theme at all times.

This is truly one part of the fun in online transactions. I feel these things are particularly suitable for highly emotional sellers and buyers. When they become your regular customers, guessing whether a particular store has new items today becomes a fun event.

As long as you clearly define the service class and age income of your target audience, you can have a rough idea of the decoration style. Design, change, integrate, and highlight your personalized style according to the theme, which will double your business efficiency.

These things comprehensively reflect your products, store, mindset, etc. Even if you didn't make a sale today, you can't be sure if someone hasn't already added you to their favorites.

Doing clothing business online, especially fashion women's clothing business, requires a lot of effort in store decoration. This is akin to physical store facade decoration, attracting business through appeal.
