"Plants vs. Zombies" dolls become popular New Year goods

by blogthishaha on 2011-01-30 11:14:56

In front of the New World Department Store, on the Spring Festival goods street, seven or eight people were selecting sunflower and chili dolls at a booth. Within minutes, four or five of them were sold. A woman picking out a rabbit doll curiously asked: "Why do you all buy this? What is it?"

"Plants vs Zombies."

"What 'master'?"

"If you don't know Plants vs Zombies, you're out of date! This is a computer game where zombies are going to invade your home, and you use plants to arrange troops and resist the attack." The stall owner said, picking up a green doll with a round mouth, "This is Peashooter, the rest are sunflowers, peppers, corns, nuts, cherries, and a zombie. They are all characters in the game. Nuts, sunflowers, and peas sell the best."

"I want one nut and one pea." Before the words were finished, a girl who looked like a student bought two.

"This is the best-selling doll this year, selling more than rabbits." The stall owner said that customers range from children aged three or four to elderly people aged fifty or sixty, but mainly children and young people.

The computer game Plants vs Zombies has swept the world, and the game dolls have become a popular Spring Festival good this year. Not only do they sell well in various toy markets, but online sales are even more vigorous. If you enter "Plants vs Zombies dolls" on Taobao, you can find 4058 items. Judging from the sales records, the store that sells the most has sold nearly 2000 pieces within 30 days. Miaomiao, the owner of a doll shop, said that Plants vs Zombies dolls sell the best, already selling more than 5000 pieces. Most buyers are children and young people aged 20 to 28.
