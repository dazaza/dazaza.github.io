365 Menshen (WeSec) uses technologies such as page analysis, access monitoring, and a malicious page database to identify web URLs, mark malicious links, and block access to malicious pages. This helps defend against the threat posed by malicious pages.

Comprehensive Protection: Warns when encountering malicious links, blocks access to malicious pages.

Simple to Use: No learning required, and even no user involvement needed to provide internet safety protection.

Environmentally Friendly: Absolutely no bundling of other software during installation or use.

Low Power Consumption: No impact on system performance.

Automatic Updates: Always maintained in optimal condition to deal with malicious pages.

Early Warning:

The best security strategy is to issue a warning before the threat actually arrives. I quickly scan and analyze the content of the pages being browsed, monitoring the included URLs. Once a malicious link is detected, it is immediately marked.

Blocking:

I monitor all page accesses. Once a malicious page is discovered, it is immediately blocked, and you are given a prompt to choose your operation: test it yourself? Understand more details?

WEB Mail Security:

Secure Chatting:

In QQ and MSN chats, eye-catching titles and links to malicious websites frequently appear. These malicious pages lure you into visiting them for ulterior motives. I help you identify them.

Secure Searching:

When searching for songs, movies, or keywords via Baidu or Yahoo, search results often include links to malicious websites. I will mark these links for you to avoid accessing such malicious sites.

Forum and Blog Safety:

Often, people post malicious page links in forums to steal your sensitive information. The same situation exists with blogs. When you browse posts and blog articles, I provide you with protection.

More:
