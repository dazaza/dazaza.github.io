The snow is drifting, and I am walking.

by zhouzhou56 on 2012-02-04 17:14:44

The snow was falling, and I was walking.

No, I don't hate, I love. My comfort and fulfillment are him, him, and still him.

It snowed.

In the morning, I walked slowly alone on the street. The snow kept falling gently, not snowflakes, but snow grains, small and crystal clear. Occasionally, they would fly into my eyes. I squinted and walked slowly.

The New Year is over. I need to keep walking, whether it snows or shines. I can't stop. As long as life continues, I can only move forward.

The New Year is over. Suddenly, I feel much older. I don't like dressing up anymore. I go out without makeup, just like the snowy morning after, empty and vast. I wore an old cotton coat from many years ago, black, with a plain beige scarf. I am a woman without luster or charm, walking on the street, no one will pay attention to me, and I don't care about others either. I just like to be obstinate, monotonous and dull. But I like it. No one can understand. Really, no one can understand. At such an empty moment, my heart is very full, as if there is a kind of tension that supports me happily.

I like such snowy days. I like being alone on the street. I like walking slowly. Especially this morning, I am alone, which is really good. I went to the park, which is really good. There was only me in the park, walking slowly, which is really good. I am mine.

On the snowy ground, there were only my footprints, alone, my footprints were lonely, just like me, a very lonely woman. In this world, the only person who can enter my heart is him. But at this moment, I feel he is so far away, so far away. He is the man who has already merged into my bone marrow, but this morning, I feel he is so distant and strange to me. Despite this, I am still very happy because at this moment, he is still mine, belonging to my inner self.

In fact, he is always present, everywhere. Standing under the long grape trellis, it is called a grape trellis, but I have never seen grapes. However, in summer and autumn, the corridor sides will be covered with green leaves. I don't care if it's grapes or if they are sour. As long as I like it, these things are not important.

I stood under the leafless long corridor. Above me were some old red lanterns, making me feel as if I was in a very old time, not just after the holiday. My vision became blurred. I thought of him, the two of us, one in front and one behind, whispering secrets. Actually, they weren't secrets, but we could only whisper. Sometimes, we didn't even talk, just a glance, a strange smile, and we understood each other perfectly, infinitely happy.

The snow didn't stop, it kept falling. Walking out of the empty corridor, passing by the temple, I couldn't go in because people who are not clean enough should not enter the pure land of the temple. Yes, above the main hall hung: "Buddha Light Illuminates All, Dignified Pure Land." This is the Pure Land, and I am a person filled with selfish thoughts, how could I be pure? I don't know if Buddha cares about the purity of one's heart or body. To me, the heart is more important than the body. Regardless of which, neither belongs to me because at this moment, my heart is filled with him. Our relationship is secret, noble and pure to me. Would Buddha think the same way? I've always been confused, is Heaven the same as Buddha? Is Buddha the same as Heaven? Buddha is Buddha, how could he be human? What exactly is Buddha? Sometimes I know, sometimes I get confused. But last night, when I saw the sky full of floating wishing lamps, I secretly made a wish. I hope that he and I can be together forever, for a lifetime. How long is my lifetime? 10 years? 20 years? Or 50 years? These are not important. With him, 100 years wouldn't be enough, without him, 10 years feels too long. This morning, everywhere were the remnants of the wishing lamps, reminding me that last night was the Lantern Festival, once noisy, but now I am alone in the park, very quiet. Last night, the noise was in my heart, huge. The biggest thing in my life is him. Even if his voice is weak, it will cover the whole world, shocking me.

The temple door is still closed, locked. Inside came the sound of chanting scriptures, not chanted by monks, but played from a CD. The temple was very cold, probably because the monks stayed up too late last night and are still sleeping. I didn't go in, I looked at the bulletin posted outside the wall of the temple. It said that in order to deter global disasters, the temple needs to expand and requires donations. After donating, calamities will be eliminated. Such is the title. I thought of my father. He recited scriptures for nearly 30 years, but he passed away, and I can no longer perceive his existence.

The snow kept falling. Step by step, I climbed the martyr's monument. In the past, mornings were always noisy in front of the monument. Many people exercised around it, stretching their legs, practicing boxing. Then someone wrote on the lime railing around the monument: "Solemn, Quiet, Do Not Make Noise Here, Exercise Elsewhere." But no one paid attention, they still stretched their legs on the railing. I did too. Later, someone splashed oil on the monument, making it dirty and greasy, so fewer people came. I know, I am disrespectful, but I don't care. Sometimes, I know, I am a person with low quality, lacking in self-reflection and self-control, numb and vulgar. Every time I make funny faces at home, my son points at me and says, "You look just like Aunt Two." I laugh, I know what Aunt Two is like, and I understand what my son means by "look alike". He means I am too vulgar, because Aunt Two sometimes jokes around with everyone in a vulgar manner. My son is not looking down on me, he is reminding me to take care of my image. When I argue with my husband, he scolds me, "You are just like your sister." I say, "I am just like my sister, and I am just like my mother." My sister looks fierce when she is angry, her face twisted. My mother also gets angry and jumps around when mad. It's genetic! I know how ugly I am, but I haven't changed, because I lack self-reflection and restraint. I am spoiled, and I often throw tantrums. But I never thought this way, because I never thought of myself as a miss, rather like a maid, or even a nanny, a hardworking nanny. People are really incredible. At this moment, I think, maybe I am like the White Bone Demon in Journey to the West, transform, transform, transform, because he once said that in his eyes, I am the White Lady in Legend of the White Snake, gentle and kind. Is that me? Who am I? I don't know either.

Walking around the monument, I ran down the hill along the slope. I thought of him: he was walking ahead, I rushed down, brushing past him, ignoring him, but deliberately bumping into him, then turning around and smiling at him, and he smiled back. But now, he is not here, only me, only me, I also rush, rushing down and then rushing up the little hill ahead.

Standing on top of the hill, everything before my eyes was hazy. The lake was frozen, all white, an ice lake. The trees in the distance were misty, like a painting, very beautiful. At that moment, I suddenly missed my father. I vaguely saw him coming from the stone bridge ahead, thin but determined, swaying, carrying his black bag, head held high. Tears blurred my eyes, I will never see my father again, he has left us forever. If there really is a soul, I think my father can sense my thoughts at this moment. Then my father is the only person in the world who knows about our hidden feelings. Will my father agree for me to be with him? Will he? I don't know.

I wiped my eyes, shook off the snow grains from my hair and scarf, went down the hill, facing the direction where my father came from, I am going home. My father and I are separated by the boundary between life and death, we walk on the same road, but in different directions. He must be going to Lingyuan Temple, I am going home, I can only go home.

I looked at my phone. This morning, it quietly slept in my pocket, silent, as if it had forgotten me, got rid of me. I suddenly felt a bit uneasy. What if he really forgets me, leaves me, what should I do? I shook my head, it won't happen, but I still feel insecure, because I dare not face the reality of losing him.
