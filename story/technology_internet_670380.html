Chongqing's public security bureau chief Wang Lijun

by m123456789 on 2012-02-20 19:15:08

From the public security bureau director to the vice mayor, Wang Lijun's role has changed, but his essence remains unchanged. Professor Xue Lan, Dean of Tsinghua University's School of Public Policy and Management and an expert in public policy and management, stated that in his personal view, from a rationally designed government system perspective, different levels of government departments should not have cross-level part-time jobs to avoid confusing leadership responsibilities and administrative responsibilities between different levels. However, under the background of China's transformation period, public safety and social stability are increasingly becoming major issues affecting the overall work of a place, and must be given high attention in all local works. Meanwhile, during the transformation period, public safety and social stability involve all aspects of government work, requiring strengthened comprehensive coordination between the public security department and various departments. In such circumstances, having the head of the public security department (director) as part of the leadership team is beneficial for comprehensively and timely understanding the special needs of public safety and social stability and promptly making corresponding decisions. (Global People)

Under Wang Lijun's strong promotion, the "9・19" anti-crime operation achieved victory, capturing 110 main criminals and gang members, among whom seven were sentenced to death, seizing over 2 million yuan in ill-gotten gains, and completely eliminating 19 corrupt officials within the government.

One day, a mafia group sent someone to give him a "book." Upon opening the package, Wang Lijun saw it was all hundred-yuan denomination cash, with dozens of bundles. Wang Lijun stopped him and said, "You take the money back quickly, or I will arrest you too!" The mafia boss, seeing Wang Lijun wouldn't take bribes, resorted to threats by calling and saying, "Do you want your life if you don't want hundreds of thousands?" Wang Lijun firmly replied, "Since the day I became a police officer, I haven't cared about my own life! If you let me catch you, I will definitely eliminate you!" Having no other options, the mafia boss hired a hitman for 200,000 yuan to ambush and bomb Wang Lijun's car. Fortunately, thanks to Wang Lijun's precise gun skills, he repelled the hidden thugs.

One night, Wang Lijun was on duty when suddenly the phone rang. A sinister voice asked, "Are you the new Wang Lijun? Do you know Wang Tao?" Wang Lijun answered, "Yes." The person then said, "Good, his fate will be yours. Do you dare come to the train station?" Without hesitation, Wang Lijun grabbed his gun and rushed to the train station, searching for the thug in the freezing cold wind. As a result, until dawn, the thugs didn't dare show their faces, but Wang Lijun's reputation as "fearless" spread widely.

However, Wang Lijun's recent public statements seem to already indicate his attitude towards this matter.

"It's your child who saved you"

It is understood that Wang Lijun still concurrently holds the positions of Party Secretary and Director of the Chongqing Municipal Public Security Bureau, along with other roles such as Director of the Research Center for Countermeasures Against Organized Crime, Director of the Chinese On-site Psychology Research Center, Researcher at the Criminal Law Institute of Peking University Law School, and Professor at the Li Chang-ju Forensic Science Research Institute in the United States.

In 1987, at the age of 28, Wang Lijun served as the chief of the Xiaonan Town Police Station in Tiefashi City. At that time, Xiaonan Town had very poor public security, with thugs being extremely rampant, even setting up traps to kill young police officer Wang Tao from the police station.

Not using keys to open doors at home

The 52-year-old Public Security Bureau Director Wang Lijun is locally known as the "modern-day Bao Qingtian." Since transferring from Liaoning to Chongqing in 2008, he has led the city's public security officers in a large-scale crackdown on organized crime, investigating 377 criminal organizations, solving 4,990 criminal cases related to organized crime, arresting 5,983 suspects, achieving the political removal, organizational elimination, and economic destruction of organized crime.

During the most intense period of the anti-crime campaign, he would carry a black bag larger than a newspaper at meetings, containing a foldable submachine gun.

This battle made Wang Lijun famous throughout the national police force, earning him the title of "one of the top ten outstanding police officers nationwide." Former Mayor of Tieling, Li Shiweng, called him "Tieling's treasure" and said, "Tieling can do without me, Li Shiweng, but absolutely cannot do without Wang Lijun."

Additionally, when asked about his views on numerous official duties and work pressure, he expressed, "Apart from the expectations of the people, I feel no pressure. I will do everything possible to handle the relationship between work and research." It can be foreseen that regarding this promotion, he might still be "undoubtedly confident and full of confidence."

One night, Wang Lijun went to arrest an infamous fugitive. As soon as he entered the house, the ruthless thug swung a knife toward him. He quickly aimed his gun at the fugitive's head but did not pull the trigger; instead, risking his life, he rushed forward to wrestle with the fugitive. Originally, he noticed a child on the bed and was afraid the gunshot would scare the child. After subduing the fugitive, he said, "Do you know? It's your child who saved you!" The fugitive's wife, hearing this, fell to her knees and repeatedly bowed to Wang Lijun.

In the second year after arriving in Tieling, Wang Lijun personally commanded the shocking "9・19" anti-crime operation in Liaoning. At that time, Tieling had four major gangs of thugs, employing many henchmen, dividing territories, oppressing the people, and even infiltrating government agencies. With protection from patrons, local officials gave them three points of respect.

Regarding Wang Lijun's election, some citizens of Chongqing expressed, "His appointment as deputy mayor is a great thing." Many outsiders also appealed, "When will Wang Lijun come to our place?"

As for the reasons behind Wang Lijun's election, relevant experts summarized three main points. First, his work capability is indeed outstanding. Besides taking decisive actions against organized crime, he has also achieved remarkable results in police reform, food and drug safety construction, etc.; Second, moving from Northeast to Southwest, he has consistently upheld a strict and impartial style and a sharp method of eliminating evil, gaining high recognition within the government; Third, he pays attention to people's livelihood and is committed to maintaining social stability, receiving unanimous praise from the citizens of Chongqing.

At the voting meeting on that day, all 54 members of the standing committee unanimously voted for him to serve as deputy mayor. An attendee remarked, "It is rare for an official to be elected with such a high support rate domestically."

In early 2011, Chongqing Mayor Huang Qifan mentioned in his government report that Chongqing aims to become the safest city in China. Wang Lijun's election as deputy mayor will help achieve this goal sooner.

Wang Lijun's election as Deputy Mayor of Chongqing and entry into the municipal government leadership team has attracted significant attention. In fact, this is not an isolated case. Statistics show that currently, the heads of public security bureaus in 26 provinces, municipalities, and autonomous regions across the country have joined the local party and government leadership teams.

There was also a girl named Sun Fang who excelled academically and was admitted to junior high school, but her family couldn't afford her education. She felt deep sorrow and contemplated suicide. At this time, she read about Wang Lijun's deeds in the China Youth Daily and thought Wang Lijun was a good person, so she wrote him a letter. From then on, Wang Lijun started sending Sun Fang 50 yuan every month under the name of "Party-Crowd," until Sun Fang graduated from high school three years later.

For ordinary people, Wang Lijun showed great compassion. Once, while on duty, he picked up a little boy. The boy said his name was Li Tao, but he couldn't say where he came from. Wang Lijun could have handed the boy over to the local civil affairs department, but he didn't feel at ease. He took care of the boy himself and paid out of pocket for his schooling. Li Tao developed a deep bond with Wang Lijun, calling him "Second Dad," and didn't want to leave even after his family came to claim him three months later.

In the following days, Wang Lijun took the initiative to deal with the troublemakers one by one, thoroughly suppressing their arrogance. During his three years at the Xiaonan Town Police Station, Wang Lijun arrested more than 1,600 lawbreakers, transforming what was once the town with the worst public security into a civilized town in the province.

Wang Lijun, this "anti-crime hero" and "iron-fisted director," after undergoing the evaluation and testing of the people of Chongqing, ascended to a higher leadership position. On May 27, 2011, Wang Lijun was unanimously elected as Vice Mayor of Chongqing by the Standing Committee of the Municipal People's Congress. But this ordinary personnel appointment caused a nationwide sensation. The entry of the Public Security Bureau Director into the leadership team has become a hot topic of discussion.

But Wang Lijun was relentless and began the anti-crime operation targeting these four groups on September 19, 1994. At this time, many people also targeted Wang Lijun. He received many calls every day asking for leniency for the suspects.

"The treasure of Tieling"

Typically, newly elected officials always make some remarks or respond to media questions. However, regarding his "promotion," Wang Lijun chose to be low-key. It is said that after being elected, he did not make any remarks and avoided answering media questions.

"Anti-crime hero" Wang Lijun was unanimously elected as Vice Mayor of Chongqing in 2011.

In 1977, at the age of 18, Wang Lijun joined the army. After being discharged from the military, Wang Lijun entered the Public Security Bureau. In 1984, Wang Lijun worked at the Traffic Police Brigade of the Tiefashi Public Security Bureau in Liaoning Province.

After being elected as a National People's Congress representative, he once said, "That is the trust the masses and society have placed in me. I first think of national interests and prioritize the people, discussing issues related to people's livelihood and fundamental rights."

Wang Lijun's daughter has now graduated from university and works in Beijing. When she attended elementary school in Tieling, she was forced to transfer schools due to safety concerns. His wife, Xiao Suli, was originally a comrade-in-arms with Wang Lijun in the same military unit. She now works at a city traffic police detachment, characterized by introversion, dignity, and steadiness, but shares her husband's decisive and efficient personality. When Wang Lijun was hospitalized due to illness in Tieling, she disapproved of the unclean hospital floor and immediately knelt down to clean it. Over the years, Wang Lijun developed a habit: he doesn't like using keys to open doors at home. He always believed: using keys to open doors is like staying at a hotel. Ringing the doorbell and having someone inside open the door makes it feel like home. That feeling, that atmosphere, is absolutely different.

Huge achievements also made Wang Lijun a target for criminal organizations. The mafia once offered 5 million yuan for his head. Wang Lijun has over 20 scars from knife wounds and fractures, with the most serious injury being a head wound that left him unconscious for more than ten days. His unit even prepared wreaths and eulogies. Miraculously, he survived again.

Various dangers did not make Wang Lijun retreat. On the night of the arrest operation, to prevent information leakage, he decided to only bring one police officer and secretly wrote a will without informing his family. During the fight with the mafia boss, the inside of his right cheek was bitten off, his eye corner split open, and his neck was choked almost leading to suffocation. It was the "first time he felt so close to death." However, his strong willpower helped him "laugh last," successfully capturing the mafia boss despite being covered in blood.

Wang Lijun frankly said, "Sometimes when I am quiet or with my family, I think, oh, I almost sacrificed myself for something before, which is too dangerous. Everyone has selfish thoughts. But when you step out of the house; when you enter a big working environment; when you face bloody crime scenes and helpless victims, possibly elderly people or women and children; when you see the brutality and defiance of criminals; as a police officer, can you stand idly by? When danger comes towards you, what choices do you have? You only have one choice: go forward and overcome it. There is no other option."

Wang Lijun often joked with others that the police directors in charge of criminal investigations usually don't live long, "because their lives lack regularity. You never know when a case will happen, so you keep the phone next to your pillow, and once it rings, you have to go." Years of criminal investigation work have also prevented Wang Lijun from living a peaceful life.
