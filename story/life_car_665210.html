The difficulty in finding qualified beauticians has always been one of the biggest concerns for the automotive beauty industry over the years. So, what kind of beautician is needed by China's automotive beauty industry?

As everyone knows, the threshold for entering the automotive beauty and decoration industry is low. With 3 to 5 people, a high-pressure water gun, a few bottles of car wax, a few rolls of sun film, and a few simple tools, a small-scale beauty and decoration shop can open for business. Taking car decoration as an example, the reality is that there is a lack of professional car decorators, and the standards for evaluation in this industry are still blank. Even the 4S stores of various car brands, which are considered to have relatively high technical and service standards, although they all have their own decoration departments and related staff who have undergone strict training from the manufacturers, basically only do polishing, waxing, cleaning interiors, and installing reverse radars for decoration work. For tasks such as applying sun film or glazing, they are generally outsourced or handled by professional technicians.

Cars are durable goods. When sales reach relative saturation, later-stage maintenance becomes more enduring and important, making the prospects of the car beauty and decoration industry exceptionally broad. Some insiders revealed that current car beauticians are mostly trained through oral and manual transmission, and the industry is filled with many practitioners who only have elementary or middle school education levels. They only know simple washing methods and cannot truly understand the intrinsic requirements of car beauty.

Currently, there is no news about car beauticians needing certification to work, but as the industry gradually "reshuffles," increasingly higher demands will certainly be placed on employees. Only "all-round" car beauticians who can analyze customers' requirements for car beauty and decoration, and provide corresponding and appropriate car beauty solutions, can hope to become "evergreens" in the industry. Car beauty talents need to combine theory with practice to be considered excellent beauticians. Oral and manual transmission alone, without understanding the theory, is not enough. Therefore, companies should provide short-term theoretical knowledge training and guidance for employees when they start working, lasting for a month or a week.
