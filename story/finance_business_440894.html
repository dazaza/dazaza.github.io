The theme song of the movie "Autumn White Flowers" was released. Dou Xiao and Dong Jie made their first vocal appearance on the big screen - China Film

by letaoeoe on 2011-06-03 00:47:35

The romance in the movie "Autumn Chrysanthemum" is noble, lively, romantic and sorrowful. Dou Xiao, a new and popular actor who is well-known and loved by a wide audience for his leading role in the movie "Love of Hawthorn Tree", plays the role of Qu Qiubai in this movie. Dong Jie, Dou Xiao's schoolmate senior to him, plays Yang Zhihua. The two not only interpret the couple in the movie with tacit understanding but also present their first big screen duet, singing the theme song "Together".

Due to deep insight

Director He Jianqi said that the images and narratives in "Autumn Chrysanthemum" are relatively romantic and poetic. A movie like this would be more atmospheric with a theme song that has exquisite lyrics and melody. The two lead actors, Dou Xiao and Dong Jie, have been together on set for several months and have a deeper understanding of their characters. Therefore, they decided to invite Dou Xiao and Dong Jie to sing the song.

Main creative team photo at the scene

According to China Film News Network, on April 11, the launch event for the theme song and MV of the movie "Autumn Chrysanthemum" was held in Beijing. Director He Jianqi and the lead actors Dou Xiao and Guo Jiaming attended the launch event. At the scene, Dou Xiao, who plays Qu Qiubai in the movie, sang the theme song of the film and was greatly praised by director He Jianqi for his clear eyes and literati temperament.
