Serious study and implementation of the important speech spirit of General Secretary Hu

by guogaiyanxiu on 2010-02-25 15:33:59

Recently, the Lao District Office of Sanming City consecutively held cadres meetings to study and convey the spirit of Hu Jintao's important speech during his inspection in Fujian and the meetings' spirit of the province and city. In connection with the actual situation of this unit, they are determined to seriously study and implement the important speech spirit of General Secretary Hu, and take practical actions to do a good job in the old district work and accelerate the development of the old district, so as not to disappoint the care and expectations of the CPC Central Committee and General Secretary Hu for the old district.

One, strengthen learning and strive to improve the quality of the cadre team. All cadres and Party members must make serious study and implementation of General Secretary Hu's important speech spirit as an important political task for the current and future period, and seize it tightly. Focus on improving six abilities of cadres in the old district office: first, improve the ability to understand policies. Currently, the National Development and Reform Commission has clearly defined the scope of the original central Soviet counties and revolutionary old districts in Fujian enjoying the central and western region policies. The Provincial Party Committee and the provincial government will introduce support policies for revolutionary old districts not included in the policy enjoyment scope. We must certainly strengthen communication with the province, grasp the opportunity well, strengthen policy docking, and use the policies of the seven original central Soviet counties in our city and three old district counties supported by the province (Datian, Yong'an, Youxi) well, fully and flexibly; second, improve execution ability, especially the ability to independently handle work; third, improve the ability to serve the masses, taking the development of the old district as a mission, being concerned about the old district, rooting in the old district, serving the old district; fourth, improve the ability to operate projects, using the limited old district funds well and flexibly, improving the economic, social, and ecological benefits of assistance construction projects; fifth, improve the ability to overcome difficulties, striving to overcome difficulties in funding, manpower, institutions, and project promotion,

enhancing the determination and confidence to do a good job in the old district work; sixth, improve the ability to unite and cooperate. Look at the old district from outside the old district, firmly establish the concept of "a large old district", strengthen unity and cooperation, strengthen coordination and cooperation with departments, and strengthen communication and contact with higher levels.

Two, emancipate mind and constantly update concepts. First, be good at seizing opportunities. Firmly seize the opportunity that the seven original central Soviet counties enjoy the national western policies, and the provincial party committee and provincial government introduce policies to support the old district counties, increase support, increase help, increase inclination, do a good job in project research, actively think, actively do, actively research, actively dock, actively implement. Second, dare to pilot and experiment. From the perspective of benefiting the development of the old district, improving the production and living conditions of the people in the old district, and benefiting the work, carefully consider the fields and channels for the old district work to pilot and experiment. With an innovative concept, promote the old district work to a new level, produce new results, and achieve new effects. Third, be brave to take responsibility. Adhere to the system of collective research on major matters. Enhance execution ability and implementation ability, ensure "zero" obstacles in work advancement, truly achieve "four less and four more": less requests, more thinking about ways to solve problems; less explanations, more solving actual difficulties; less shirking, more doing pragmatic work; less formalism, more doing practical and effective things.

Three, link with reality and focus on implementing work. According to the deployment of the province and city, linking with the actual work of the old district, currently focus on grasping the "five works": first, strengthen publicity and solidify the ideological foundation of the old district. Through various channels, publicize the important speech spirit of General Secretary Hu during his inspection in Fujian, especially the important speech spirit regarding the construction of the old district, combining the study of General Secretary Hu's important speeches with the series of important discourses on the development of the old district in recent years, further understand the requirements of the CPC Central Committee and General Secretary Hu for the construction of the old district, and solidify the ideological foundation of the old district construction; second, strengthen research and play the role of advisor and assistant of the old district office. Enhance the foresight and forward-looking nature of the work, focusing on the central work of the municipal Party committee and municipal government, focusing on the hot and difficult issues and deep-seated contradictions in the construction and development of the old district, strengthen research, and provide references for the decision-making of the municipal Party committee and municipal government; third, strengthen guidance and effectively implement assistance construction work. With deep feelings for the people in the old district, do more work in terms of policy level, atmosphere creation, and seeking support, implement the various support policies of the central, provincial, and municipal governments; fourth, strengthen services and do all the work of the five "old" revolutionary personnel well. Seriously implement the life and medical subsidy policies for the five "old" personnel, and deliver the care of the Party and government to the five "old" personnel; fifth, strengthen learning and earnestly strengthen the construction of the old district cadre team. Build a learning-type agency, improve the level of thought and policy and the ability to solve practical problems; strengthen self-construction, promote work development, and open up a new situation for the old district work.

(Sanming Old District Office)
