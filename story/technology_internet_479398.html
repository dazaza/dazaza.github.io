With the advancement of social material civilization, Hubei Agricultural Science and Technology's People-Oriented No. 2 centipede farming has gained significant attention as an excellent choice for treatment and health preservation. Products such as centipede wine and centipede Fen health supplements have subsequently emerged. Additionally, with the development of medicine, the role of centipede venom has been widely recognized. Centipede venom is more valuable than gold, priced at approximately $150,000 per kilogram. About 500 grams of venom can be extracted annually from 10,000 centipedes; thus, the medicinal value of centipede venom far exceeds that of the centipede itself.

The People-Oriented No. 2 Golden Headed Centipede breeding base in rural areas vigorously develops farming, which is an excellent way for Hubei Agricultural Science and Technology's People-Oriented No. 2 centipede farming to rely on rural resource advantages. The cultivation of animal-based medicinal herbs like centipedes, if good techniques are mastered, will become a great option among them.

As a traditional and valuable Chinese herb, centipedes have a long history of being used in medicine, recognized and utilized by people over 2,000 years ago. Many medical treatises, including "Compendium of Materia Medica," provide detailed records.

Hubei Agricultural Science and Technology's People-Oriented No. 2 centipede farming has very high medicinal value, with over 100 Chinese herbal prescriptions containing centipedes. Centipedes are crucial ingredients in more than 30 types of traditional Chinese medicines, such as Ginseng Regeneration Pills, Great Meridian Pills, and Traction Correcting Powder, showcasing remarkable therapeutic effects. For example, centipedes can treat phlebitis, blood clot blockages, trigeminal neuralgia, anti-cancer detoxification, tumor resolution, wind-dispelling sedation, facial paralysis, tetanus, etc. These applications have significantly increased the demand for centipedes. Besides medicinal use, centipedes can also be made into nourishing foods.

Centipede farming? Centipede breeding bases? Hubei Agricultural Science and Technology's People-Oriented No. 2 Golden Headed Centipede Breeding Base is vigorously developing centipede farming.

For many years, the market supply of centipedes has mainly relied on wild capture. However, with the extensive use of fertilizers and pesticides, the natural habitat of wild centipedes has been damaged, causing a sharp decline in their natural population, leading to market shortages. Currently, the national production can only meet about 30% of the demand, causing a rapid increase in market prices. Artificial centipede farming has broad prospects for development, and there will be a wave of wealth creation through centipede farming across the country.
