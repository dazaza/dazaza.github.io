The incident occurred at Yongguang Village, Huadong Town, Huadu District, in an alley called Yonglong South Lane. It was a one-and-a-half-story brick house covering nearly 100 square meters including the yard. On the first floor, there were four rooms and one kitchen. The room that caught fire was located on the west side of the yard. Villagers said that the house was usually occupied by a sixty-year-old couple, Mr. and Mrs. Hou, and their 15-year-old granddaughter Xiaoqing. At around 11 PM on January 28th, while watching TV, a neighbor suddenly smelled smoke and burnt odor and hurriedly went out to check. They found flames and thick smoke coming from the neighbor's house. Several neighbors went forward to knock on the door, but no one answered.

Correspondents: Hua Gongxuan, Gongxuan

The Huadu police in Guangzhou informed the media yesterday that at 11:48 PM on January 28th, the police received a public report about a residential fire in Yongguang Village, Huadong Town. After receiving the report, police officers and firefighters quickly arrived at the scene to handle the situation. At 12:30 AM on January 29th, the fire was extinguished, and the police discovered the body of the female homeowner, Mrs. Hou, inside the house. There were signs of arson at the scene. After thorough investigation, the police found that the homeowner's granddaughter, Wang (Xiaoqing), had disappeared and was a major suspect in the crime. At around 3 AM on January 29th, the police arrested the suspect Wang in Xiawen Street, Xiuzquan Road.

After receiving the alarm, the firefighters carried her out, and the 120 emergency doctor confirmed that she had no vital signs. The neighbors discovered that it was Grandma Hou, who had multiple knife wounds on her body.

Since the semester had not yet started, the reporter was unable to interview the teachers at Datan Middle School. However, the reporter managed to find Xiaoyong (a pseudonym) from the village, who claimed to be Xiaoqing's classmate. He confirmed to the reporter that Xiaoqing indeed had good grades and could rank among the top 10 in her class, but she was very introverted.

According to Wang's confession: Her grandmother, Mrs. Hou, was quite talkative and often had conflicts with her. On the day of the incident, after being scolded by her grandmother, they got into an argument and physical altercation. Wang pushed Mrs. Hou down and strangled her until she died. To cover up the crime, Wang set fire to the house and fled.

Analysis: Suspected to be related to pocket money.

Mr. Wang said that about five or six years ago, Xiaoqing's parents divorced, and she was raised by her father. Later, her father remarried and had two more children with his second wife. Therefore, Xiaoqing had been living with her grandmother for many years. Mr. Wang said that Xiaoqing's living expenses were covered by her father, who visited her once or twice a month along with his second wife. Xiaoqing's biological mother had never been seen, and her life was not affluent.

Nearby residents introduced that soon after the incident, Xiaoqing's father took her grandfather away and then locked the door, and they never appeared again. Through the window, the reporter saw that the room on fire had been completely destroyed, and there was still a burnt smell inside. However, the other rooms did not show any excessive damage.

Parents divorced, introverted, polite.

Xiaoyong said that Xiaoqing rarely talked to other classmates, so everyone was not familiar with her situation. He said that Xiaoqing's life was not hard, and he had not heard of any conflicts with her family over anything. He found it hard to imagine that such an event would occur.

Many villagers were shocked by Xiaoqing's actions. Some villagers said that although Xiaoqing was introverted, she was still polite and greeted people she met in the village. Xiaoqing's parents had been divorced for many years and had little contact with the village.

From Xiaoqing's room, one could see the charred remains of her grandmother's room. Nanfang Daily journalist Liang Wei Pei took the photo.

Some villagers said that normally, Grandma kept a tight rein on Xiaoqing, and it was suspected that Xiaoqing asked her grandmother for pocket money, but her grandmother refused, leading to a dispute. Villagers who arrived at the scene said that there were many coins scattered in the kitchen at the time of the incident.

Neighboring Mr. Wang said that Xiaoqing was usually very introverted and led a simple life. He had never seen her associating with young people from society, nor had he seen classmates come to play. Occasionally, she interacted with some peers in the village. He said that even though he was a neighbor, he rarely interacted with Xiaoqing's family and only knew that her father lived in Huadu Xinhua, but they had no contact.

Fire: A fire broke out late at night, and the elderly woman died inside the house.

About two hours later, the firefighters put out the fire. Fortunately, the fire did not spread, and only one room was destroyed. The police did not find any trace of Xiaoqing at the scene. It was not until the next day that the neighbors learned that Xiaoqing was suspected of killing her grandmother and setting fire to the house.

According to the villagers, when the neighbors broke into the house, they found a large pool of blood in front of the kitchen door. The room that caught fire belonged to Grandma Hou, and several people tried to put out the fire, but the flames were too big to enter. Some neighbors noticed that there was a person lying in the burning room, suspected to be Grandma Hou.

Yesterday, Nanfang Daily revisited the scene, and this incident had already spread throughout the village, but most of the surrounding residents were unwilling to talk about it. However, neighboring Mr. Wang told the reporter that Xiaoqing's parents had been divorced for many years and did not live there.

In the villagers' impression, Guangzhou Huadu District's fifteen-year-old junior three girl Xiaoqing (a pseudonym) was introverted but polite, with good academic performance, a smart girl next door. But the fire that occurred in her home on the night of January 28th destroyed all of this. Xiaoqing's grandmother, Mrs. Hou, died in the fire, and Xiaoqing was subsequently arrested by the police on suspicion of murder and arson.

Currently, Wang has been criminally detained by the police according to law, and the case is still under further review.
