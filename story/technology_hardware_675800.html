1. Protective and Decorative Convenient Shoe Polishing Wipes

A convenient shoe polishing wipe for protecting and decorating leather shoes, consisting of crumpled paper base and a coating solution. This coating solution is made up of nitrocellulose lacquer (film-forming agent), dibutyl phthalate (toughening agent), butyl acetate (diluent), and white oil (fatting agent). The weight percentage composition is as follows: nitrocellulose lacquer 25-40%, dibutyl phthalate 25-60%, butyl acetate 20-35%, and white oil 10-20%. The coating solution is absorbed by the paper base and used to polish leather shoes, resulting in a bright and durable film that makes the leather surface wear-resistant, water-resistant, oil-resistant, and crease-resistant, providing both protective and decorative functions.

This practical design has a simple structure and is easy to implement.
