With the steady rise in the popularity of the MP3 market, MP4 has also been accepted by consumers and is moving towards mainstream. Both are media players, so what are the differences between MP4 and MP3? In fact, these two are products from different eras. The emergence of MP3 was to meet the needs of portability and sound playback. Most MP3s, especially early models, only came with a small screen. Some later MP3 players, although equipped with larger color screens such as OLED and TFT, were mainly for music playback, while the screen only provided better display effects and extended some simple functions like e-books and browsing pictures. However, MP4 players are different; they have changed the relative weaknesses of MP3. In the measurement conditions of MP4 players, video playback accounts for a considerable part, so it can be said that the appearance of MP4 was to meet the needs of video playback. Currently, MP4 does not have a unified standard. Due to insufficient understanding of MP4 by people, how to correctly choose MP4 products has become the focus of what everyone hopes to understand. Many manufacturers on the market are releasing their own standards, which can easily confuse consumers when choosing. Moreover, there are too many and too varied MP4 brands on the current market. Although the entertainment functions are rich, the prices are also quite high. Therefore, before spending money to keep up with the trend, you still need to have an understanding of MP4 products.

First: Look at the appearance. We first look at their appearance. Most MP4 products on the market are rectangular, with large screens for users to watch movies well. The volume, appearance, and button distribution of MP4 have a very critical role in consumer use. A compact body will make it convenient for you to carry. The design of the appearance depends on personal preference. If you buy an MP4 that you hate because of certain reasons, you will definitely regret it after buying it. The distribution of buttons is also important. The left hand is suitable for operating infrequently used buttons, while the right hand is suitable for operating functions that require force and must be used during the process. Therefore, manufacturers will reasonably arrange the button layout. The editor hopes that users will not only focus on one aspect when choosing MP4 but also ignore other aspects.

Second: Look at the screen. The screens available on the market now are divided into 2.5 inches and 3.5 inches, all using 260,000 colors. Only a few products, due to the LCD screen, lack sufficient color saturation. Therefore, pay attention to the clarity of the screen's colors when choosing.

Third: Look at the supported formats. The most decisive factor in whether something is an MP4 is the supported format issue. Everyone knows that a product is called MP4 because it supports professional audio and video playback formats, so this is also an area that consumers need to focus on. If it only supports one audio and video playback format, calling this product an MP4 would be somewhat far-fetched. However, everyone knows that the more formats supported, the more convenient it is to watch movies. And you should also know if this MP4 requires conversion for many formats to be viewed, as everyone knows, converting a certain format takes a long time, which causes great inconvenience to users. For example, the PISA power cinema can directly play MPEG-4, DVD, VCD format movies without needing to convert DVDs or VCDs into MPEG-4 before playing; it also comes with a mini remote control. It supports various video formats such as AVI, MPG, DAT, VOB.

Fourth: Playback time. Choosing an MP4 is mainly for watching movies, and movie times are generally around 2 hours or even longer. Besides, consumers like to carry MP4s when traveling. If it runs out of power after watching for a while and you can't find a place to charge it in time, that would be very frustrating. The nominal playback time of MP4 players seen now is mostly around 4 hours, but usually, they can only be used for 2-3 hours. The longest playback time currently available on the market is 6 hours.

Fifth: Consider the cost-performance ratio. When selecting, look around and compare. Check the brand and after-sales service of the product to avoid regrets from making a hasty decision.

Sixth: Functional requirements. Before purchasing an MP4, you need to be clear about which functions you need. "There's no such thing as a free lunch," every function is something you pay for. Among many digital products, MP4 belongs to the category with numerous functions. Correctly choosing an MP4 not only allows you to obtain more practical functions but also prevents you from wasting money. Currently, the functions of MP4 start with basic ones: video playback, video output, MP3 playback, voice recorder, and mobile hard drive. These are the most basic functions of MP4, and if a product lacks these functions, then it shouldn't be considered. Additionally, some have digital companion functions, which are quite attractive to users who own digital cameras. Products like Archos AV420 and Yundi PVP Video Companion have this function. Through a transfer slot, these MP4s can support various types of memory cards, making them convenient to use. For example, Archos AV420 supports CF, SD, MMC, SM cards, and Sony Memory Sticks.

After introducing so much, you probably have a certain understanding of MP4. Many MP4 products have additional functions, and consumers can see which functions are most important to themselves. Sharpen your eyes and learn more about MP4 knowledge, which will help you buy satisfactory products with confidence.
