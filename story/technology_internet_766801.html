Microsoft held a press conference at 10:30 PM tonight to release the second generation of Surface tablets. Today marks another attempt by Microsoft in the tablet market. Through the Surface, Microsoft officially entered this competitive arena; however, the first generation of Surface products undoubtedly dealt a significant blow to Microsoft—resulting in a loss of $900 million (approximately 5.5 billion RMB). Although Microsoft ventured into the tablet market with the Surface line, it lagged behind due to its performance. The success of the next generation of Surface hinges on whether it can open up the tablet market and improve upon the previous model's underperformance.
