Outside the palace, while in disguise, Ling Xuewei meets the emperor who is also disguised. His charming and passionate demeanor causes her to feel a confusing sense of hesitation and attachment.

How will Ling Xuewei deal with this seemingly endless situation?

In the struggle for imperial favor, Consort Liu, Noble Consort An, and others, driven by jealousy, instigate a series of complex and intricate power struggles. Amidst laughter and anger, life and death are separated.

In the battle for court power, the emperor finally secretly eliminates Ling Xuewei's father, but continues to show her great affection. However, she has already learned the truth...
