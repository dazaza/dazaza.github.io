Industry insider: A reporter was injured after interviewing about the gas leakage accident in a steel company

by level2tzt on 2012-02-24 14:26:33

Nanjing, February 23 (reporter: Shuai Jie) - Around 11:50 AM on the 23rd, a gas leak occurred at the Shanghai Meishan Iron and Steel Company located in Nanjing's Yuhuatai District. However, when several journalists from Jiangsu went to cover the incident, they were physically prevented by the factory’s security guards.

At 8 PM on the 23rd, this reporter telephoned one of the injured journalists, Xu Xin, who works for a media outlet in Beijing. He stated that he and another injured journalist, Cao Lujie, are currently receiving treatment at Mingji Hospital. Xu Xin mentioned feeling dizzy with some bleeding from his mouth, while Cao Lujie, having been severely beaten, is currently undergoing intravenous therapy.

Subsequently, Xu Xin recounted the events leading up to the attack to the reporter from China News Service.

"Today around 2:30 PM, after hearing about the leak near Gate No. 25 of Meishan Steel Plant, I went over to check it out. When I saw that the gate was closed off by the police, I entered through a middle gate. But once inside, I wasn't sure of the exact location, so I got down to ask some workers about the situation. At that moment, two security guards on motorcycles came over. Seeing our photographer with his camera, they rushed over and grabbed him, saying we couldn't conduct interviews. I told them we're from a newspaper and just wanted to understand the situation. Before I could finish speaking, one of the guards grabbed the photographer by the neck from behind. As they started hitting the photographer's head, I tried to intervene asking what he was doing, then another guard came over to help seize the camera. I said you can't grab the camera."

"After they finished seizing the camera, the two stood in front of the car to prevent us from leaving, so I called the police," Xu Xin continued recounting. "While waiting for the police, I was looking at my phone inside the car, and journalist Cao Lujie had stepped out for a cigarette. Suddenly, a security guard without a hat hit Cao Lujie on the back of the head. Then, I saw another security guard without a hat rush to open the car door and punch me twice in the face."

Xu Xin indicated that as of 8 AM the next morning, the local police authorities under whose jurisdiction the Meishan Steel Company falls have already intervened in the investigation. However, as of the same time, Meishan Steel has yet to provide any explanation regarding the assault. (End)
