Other Common Questions for Online Store Agents:

1. Can you pay per order without paying a deposit?

Answer: No. All orders placed by agents are done through the company website, with automatic settlement, a highly standardized process that reduces manual intervention, improves efficiency, and lowers the possibility of errors. In our eyes, efficiency equals benefits, and you are the same. We survive and develop through efficiency, so we no longer conduct business where payments are made per order.

2. Are there any other fees for being our agent?

Answer: There are no other fees. The prepayment is used for deducting goods and shipping costs when placing an order, and you can use it at any time. It is not a fee, and there are no miscellaneous charges. On the contrary, qualified agents with certain annual performance can receive rebates based on their sales.

3. As mentioned above, how is the rebate calculated?

Answer: The rebate is returned based on the actual sales benefits generated for the company by the agent. Sometimes, to control the actual selling price within a reasonable range, part of the profit is retained in the rebate and returned to the agent. Because the profit margins of different categories of goods vary greatly, there is no direct relationship with sales volume. You cannot calculate how much rebate you will receive based on your own sales volume. This can, to some extent, prevent agents from engaging in price-cutting competition to boost sales. Rest assured, what belongs to you, you will definitely get, and what does not belong to you, you will not be able to obtain...

We will provide exclusive pictures and other services to excellent agents in the future.

5. Can I have you ship on my behalf as your agent?

Answer: Of course!
