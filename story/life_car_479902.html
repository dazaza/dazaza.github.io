The difficulty in finding qualified beauticians has always been one of the main concerns in the automotive beauty industry. So, what kind of beautician is needed by China's automotive beauty industry?

As we all know, the threshold for entering the automotive beauty and decoration industry is low. With 3 to 5 people, a high-pressure water gun, a few bottles of car wax, a few rolls of sun film, and some simple tools, a small-scale beauty and decoration shop can open for business. Taking car decoration as an example, the reality is that there is a lack of professional car decorators, and currently, this industry has no standards for evaluation. Even taking the relatively technical and service-standardized 4S stores of various car brands as an example, although they have their own decoration departments and related staff who have undergone strict training from manufacturers, they generally only do polishing, waxing, cleaning interiors, and installing reversing radars.

For tasks such as applying sun films or glazing, these are usually outsourced or handled by professional technicians.

Private car sales have been steadily increasing, and the concept of car maintenance has gradually taken root in people's minds. The phrase "take care of your beloved car" has become a catchphrase for many car owners. According to experts' estimates, a car worth 100,000 yuan generally lasts for 10 years, with an annual mileage of 20,000-30,000 kilometers, resulting in maintenance costs of over 4,000 yuan per year. Cars are durable goods, and after sales reach relative saturation, later-stage maintenance becomes even more lasting and important, making the prospects of the car beauty and decoration industry exceptionally broad. According to insiders, today's car beauticians are mostly trained through word-of-mouth and hands-on experience, and many people entering this profession have only primary school or middle school education levels. They only know simple washing methods and cannot truly understand the intrinsic requirements of car beauty.

Currently, there have been no reports about the need for car beauticians to hold certificates before starting work, but as the industry gradually "reshuffles," it will undoubtedly place increasingly higher demands on its workers. Only those "multi-skilled" car beauticians who can analyze customers' needs regarding car beauty and decoration and come up with corresponding, appropriate car beauty solutions have the potential to become "evergreen trees" in the industry. Car beauty talent requires a combination of theory and practice to be considered excellent. Just relying on word-of-mouth and practical knowledge without understanding theory is insufficient. Therefore, companies should provide short-term theoretical knowledge training and guidance for employees when they start their jobs, whether it's for a month or a week.
