Kentucky Fried Chicken sex scandal: KFC female employee takes nude photos in a washing-up bowl

by teman on 2008-12-15 11:20:52

According to the British "Daily Mail" reported that three female employees of a Kentucky Fried Chicken shop in California, the United States were fired for bathing in the kitchen sink and uploading bikini pictures.

The three girls from Anderson, California wore bikinis to relax in the kitchen sink. They had worked in the store for a long time before this. They then took selfies, one of the girls decided to put these photos on her MySpace page, with captions including "Haha, KFC shower" and "Haha, we turned on the tap."

KFC store manager Cherry said: "These girls have been dealt with for this. I learned about it during the dinner rush hour. I was very shocked when I saw these pictures. There may be no manager present when the girls took these pictures, and the three girls closed the KFC store themselves. I have never seen such a situation during the more than a year I have worked in the store." Cherry did not disclose the names of these girls.
