An elderly man in his seventies who fell down was helped up half an hour later and said that he would not blackmail the helper.

by zxyasdw90 on 2011-07-15 14:28:41

According to the reporter Yang Kuang, on the afternoon of November 15th, a 70-year-old man fell down on Lianfang Road in Handan City. For half an hour, pedestrians walked by and did not help. Wang Tiejun, a taxi driver who was passing by stopped his car and helped the old man up. The first sentence that the old man said was: "Thank you. Don't worry. I won't blackmail you!"

Wang Tiejun has been driving a taxi for over ten years. He is one of the first "Taxi Stars" in Handan City. He also pioneered the "Respect the Elderly" taxi service in the province - free rides for people over 60 years old.

On the afternoon of November 15th, Wang Tiejun was taking a passenger from Lianfang Road to Guangtai Middle School. When he passed the front gate of Chunhui Residential Area, he noticed an old man had fallen on the road.

"The car had already passed him. I felt something was wrong. Through the rearview mirror, I saw the old man trying to reach out to grab the edge of the barrier but failed." Wang Tiejun said at that time he felt very uneasy. It was so cold and there were so many cars passing by, yet no one stopped to ask what happened.

Wang Tiejun stopped his car and told the passenger, "I can't take you anymore. I need to go back and check on this old man. And I will not charge you for the ride." The passenger reminded Wang Tiejun when he got off, "It's okay to do good deeds, but be careful not to get blackmailed!"

Wang Tiejun turned his car around and rushed to the old man without even turning off the engine. "Grandpa, what's wrong?" Knowing that the old man had only fallen and couldn't stand up by himself, Wang Tiejun hugged the old man's waist and helped him stand up. Then, Wang Tiejun drove his car over and helped the old man into the back seat. After getting into the car, the old man sighed and said, "Thank you. Don't worry. I won't blackmail you!"

However, the old man didn't know which residential area he lived in. He only knew it was Building 23. Wang Tiejun drove around all the nearby Renda, Guangtai, and Guang'an residential areas, and finally found the old man's home at Building 23 in Guangxia Residential Area.

After the old man safely arrived home, his daughter-in-law took out 100 yuan to thank Wang Tiejun, but he politely refused.

It turned out that this old man's surname was Kang, and he was 78 years old. A month ago, he moved to his son's house in Guangxia Residential Area. At 10 o'clock in the morning of November 15th, he went out for a walk alone and walked until 3 o'clock in the afternoon. While crossing the road, he accidentally fell.

Yesterday, Grandpa Kang recalled the situation at that time: "I couldn't get up. I tried to grab the barrier in front of me to stand up, but I couldn't reach it. Before this kind-hearted driver helped me up, I had been lying on the ground for more than half an hour. I saw cars pass by one after another, and I was so anxious. I wanted to shout, 'Help me up! Don't be afraid, I won't blackmail you!'"

"I know about Peng Yu in Nanjing who was sued after helping an old lady who fell down. I am also afraid of being blackmailed. But if we put ourselves in others' shoes, everyone has parents. If your own parents fall and no one cares, how would you feel as their children?" Wang Tiejun frankly admitted that if he encounters such a situation again, he will still help!
