Fruit shell filter media [http://www.watercl.com/BGK-LL.html] adopts apricot kernels, peach shells, and walnuts [http://www.watercl.com/BGK-LL.html]. Due to their inherent high hardness, ideal specific gravity, porosity, and multi-faceted nature, they undergo special physical and chemical treatment (removing pigments, fats, oils, and electrically charged ions), which makes them have strong oil removal performance in water treatment. They are suitable for water treatment in oil fields and petroleum refining enterprises. They have the advantages of high hardness, wear resistance, good compressive strength, and strong adsorption and pollution-carrying capacity. Direct pre-filter backwashing can be used, and the effect on oily wastewater treatment is significant. The main specifications are: 0.4-0.6, 0.5-1, 0.8-1.2; 1-2, 2-4, 4-6.
