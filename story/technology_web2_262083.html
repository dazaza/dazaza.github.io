"The old gentleman" wants to smoke, "the young miss" lights the fire for him. Beneath the passionate rock lies the home of "the old gentleman" and "the young miss." All happiness coalesces into a sweet pear at this moment.

A tale of a knight saving a beauty leads to a romance between a sister and her younger brother. At that time, he was in his prime, unmarried, and roamed the four seas. She, six years his senior, lived as a widow deep in the mountains with her two children...

Beautiful times pass swiftly; lovers with silvered temples still stubbornly guard their cave home, inseparable, rising with the sun and resting with its setting, fully enjoying their private world.

Nowadays, she at 76 is still "the young miss" in his eyes, and he at 70 remains "the old gentleman" in her heart. Their harmonious relationship inspires envy.

"Passionate Rock," located in the small moonlight community No.3 of Shuangfeng Village, Berlin Town, Jiangjin District, hides deep within dense forests at an elevation of over 1500 meters. The male and female owners of "Passionate Rock" — Hu Renfa, aged 70, and Wang Yongfang, aged 76, have lived there for nearly half a century and are affectionately referred to by locals as "celestial companions."

On October 22, after the rain cleared up, I set out from the main city by car and arrived at Shuangfeng Village at noon. The village cadre said that Mr. Hu had been waiting on the field earlier, but due to lack of contact information, worried about his wife staying alone at home, he had returned.

The winding dirt road stretches deep into the jungle, muddy paths making passengers sway left and right. "Now, cars can drive all the way to the foot of their mountain, but few vehicles go in. They still walk, taking at least an hour to enter or exit," said Wang Zhenyou, director of the town's cultural station. When mentioning "Passionate Rock," locals will tell you about Hu Renfa's treasured love for his elder wife.

After turning twice on the stone path at the mountain waist, a cliff wall appeared faintly ahead, with a wooden door embedded in the cave entrance. Before seeing any figures, dog barks were heard first. "Don't bark, it must be guests arriving." As the words faded, an elderly woman appeared holding an iron shovel in the courtyard.

Wearing a blue shirt and black pants, her gray hair tied into two shoulder-length braids, with a white headscarf wrapped around her head, her nimble movements and clear voice made it hard to believe that this seemingly fifty-year-old aunt was actually Wang Yongfang, 76 years old.

"Is this the journalist Mr. Hu mentioned?" Wang Yongfang took out her mobile phone and dialed a series of numbers skillfully: "Mr. Hu, your awaited guests have arrived, please come back quickly."

Fifteen minutes later, high-pitched singing echoed through the valley: "When the sun rises, the whole mountain turns red, the brother's feelings are understood by the sister, I go west while you go east, our love fills the entire mountain..." Hu Renfa's entrance exuded a somewhat "immortal-like" aura: silver-haired and bearded, sharp-eyed, with a straight back despite carrying a pesticide box, wearing rubber boots, and walking with strong strides. "I just feared that you would arrive and no one would be here, so I asked Miss Younger Sister to wait at home."

As soon as "the old gentleman" sat down, "Miss Younger Sister" Wang Yongfang bent down and placed a pair of cloth shoes before him: "Hurry up and change your shoes, so your feet don't get wet." In front of strangers, the elderly couple openly displayed their affection without hesitation: Grandma occasionally helped Grandpa adjust his clothes and comb his beard, while Grandpa picked wildflowers from the mountainside to adorn Grandma's hair and fed her freshly picked pears. All happiness crystallized into a sweet pear at this moment.

"The old gentleman" planted grains and various seasonal vegetables within a kilometer radius of his home. Without special reasons, the elderly couple would go uphill after breakfast and return home only after finishing their work. "Today, because she waited for you at home without help, I felt uneasy doing my tasks." "You always hold hands when entering or leaving, reluctant to separate even for half a day, isn't it?" A joke from the younger generation made "Miss Younger Sister" blush and lower her head, while "the old gentleman" burst into hearty laughter.

Going to the market, visiting relatives, working on the farm, washing clothes, cooking meals, feeding pigs, collecting firewood... "The old gentleman" and "Miss Younger Sister" are always side by side. The warmth between the couple makes others both envious and jealous. Someone asked them for advice on keeping love fresh; the answer from "the old gentleman" and "Miss Younger Sister" was simple: "We don't want to experience longing and worry in separation."

How did Hu Renfa and Wang Yongfang end up together?

Wang Yongfang is a local, with seven siblings, ranked fifth in the family. At the age of 18, the beautiful and skilled needleworker married someone about two kilometers away from her hometown, who was 11 years older than her and worked as a tailor. "He was very caring, all my clothes were hand-sewn by him."

Soon, a son and daughter were born. Although life was hard, the couple shared hardships and loved each other deeply. Unexpectedly, when her eldest son You Shengchao was ten, her husband passed away due to illness. The following year, her mother-in-law also passed away.

A young widow raising young children faced unimaginable difficulties. Wang Yongfang tended pigs and cattle for the production team, worked in the canteen, and during the era of earning points for labor, providing for a family of three became a major issue. Despite enduring poverty and hardship, the gossip surrounding a widow's household made her feel helpless and powerless...

Hu Renfa and Wang Yongfang's husband were from the same production team. In his youth, Hu Renfa traveled extensively with his herbal medicine skills and martial arts. When Wang Yongfang got married, he happened to be at home, witnessing the bride's beauty and memorizing her radiant smile.

"At that time, besides her beauty, what made people admire her were her sewing, cooking, and rice wine-making skills." Talking about his beloved's charm, Hu Renfa's pride and affection were evident in his expression. Like facing a beautiful painting, every encounter left Hu Renfa merely admiring Wang Yongfang from afar, too nervous to breathe. "Afraid of disturbing her."

After the death of her husband and mother-in-law, Wang Yongfang moved to live in a cave halfway up the mountain. During this time, Hu Renfa worked at the village health clinic. The health clinic was located at the foot of the mountain directly across from the cave, and Wang Yongfang's reserved land was right in front of the health clinic. One day, on her way out, Wang Yongfang was stopped by a rogue from the same village intending to harass her. Fortunately, Hu Renfa intervened, beating the rogue until he fled in panic. "She cannot be bullied anymore." Thereafter, every day at work, Hu Renfa would frequently observe Wang Yongfang's home and reserved land. "Whenever I saw her carrying heavy loads, I would rush over to help her."

In the lean months of May and June, Wang Yongfang's family of three often lacked food and clothing. "Without me asking, he always timely delivered money and grain." No one dared to cause trouble anymore, but rumors about the two spread throughout the countryside. Afraid of affecting Hu Renfa, who had yet to marry, Wang Yongfang subtly suggested they maintain a distance. However, Hu Renfa firmly declared: "An upright person has nothing to fear from shadows." Moreover, he visited the cave even more frequently.

From then on, rice wine and cloth shoes became Wang Yongfang's specific ways of entertaining and thanking Hu Renfa. Indescribable longing and attachment grew between this couple who found true feelings in adversity, and "the old gentleman" and "the young miss" became their pet names for each other.

The Fire Could Not Stop the Lovers

Time flew, and Wang Yongfang's children successively left the mountains to start families. Once, using the opportunity of his eldest daughter-in-law's visit, Hu Renfa told her the story of how a "roving doctor saved a beauty" and revealed his extraordinary bond with "Miss Younger Sister," probing: "After both children established their own families, what kind of ending do you think these two elders should have?" At that time, Hu Renfa was 40 years old, having frequented Wang's house as "the old gentleman" for nearly a decade, while Wang Yongfang was nearing fifty.

As a teacher at school, the 24-year-old eldest daughter-in-law Yuan Lianfen immediately expressed her opinion: "They should live together and support each other through the rest of their lives." Then, she added: "Mr. Hu, I know the protagonists of the story are you and my mom." Yuan Lianfen decided that she would be the one to break the unspoken barrier between her husband and younger sister.

After returning home, Yuan Lianfen repeated Hu Renfa's story to her husband, who was two years older than her. "The clumsy man actually said it wasn't his business, unable to guess the outcome." Yuan Lianfen angrily shouted: "How is it not your business? The heroine in the story is your mom!" Her husband was speechless for a long time and finally responded: "What should we call him?" "Isn't it easy? You can call him Dad. Since he ranks ninth in his family, let's call him Uncle Nine."

The difficulty faced by the children made "the old gentleman" and "the young miss" hesitantly waver between love and family... An unexpected fire truly connected the hearts of the old gentleman and his children.

"That year during the Spring Festival, I had just finished my winter vacation, thinking the two elders worked hard daily, I persuaded them to visit my aunt in the city for a few days while I stayed behind to make cured meat for them." Recalling, Yuan Lianfen said, coincidentally, a friend on the opposite side of the cave invited her to a birthday celebration. "While eating, I saw thick smoke billowing from the cave, the fire used for smoking meat blazing red, and in an instant, everything in the cave turned to ashes." The 2000+ yuan in cash hidden by "the old gentleman" in the cave also vanished in the flames.

To prevent the two elders from being saddened, Yuan Lianfen proposed they stay at her place, but Hu Renfa was worried—afraid of being treated as an outsider by the younger generation. "Uncle Nine, without you, there wouldn't be our today. You are our real father." Both You Shengchao and his sister You Shengxian agreed, thus "Uncle Nine" became the title replacing "teacher" among the children.

"Miss Younger Sister" Is the Tree, Brother Is the Bird

"Uncle Nine's goodness towards my mom is indescribable." You Shengchao stated that no matter what way, his family could never repay Uncle Nine's kindness in this lifetime.

After spending a year at his eldest son's home, "Miss Younger Sister" proposed moving back to the cave residence. "The old gentleman" promptly arranged for helpers to renovate the cave, expanding it with four guest rooms and equipping all eleven beds with necessary items. Televisions, washing machines, soy milk makers... once everything was ready, the couple returned to their private world.

"Miss Younger Sister" was illiterate, unwilling to leave the cave, prone to motion sickness on cars and boats, and fond of snacks. "The old gentleman" had a solution: he would describe the outside world to her, watch TV together and act as her commentator. During leisure time, he performed tricks like holding a 1.5 kg tobacco pipe in his mouth. For her casual comment "instant noodles taste good," the old gentleman traveled dozens of kilometers to the county town and bought a whole box of various flavors. Golden earrings and rings were treasures "Miss Younger Sister" never parted with. "I've worn them for over a decade," "Miss Younger Sister" said: "Seeing others wear them, I commented 'how pretty.' The next day, he claimed he was visiting our daughter, but it turned out he specifically took me to buy these two items."

One day in September 2003, "the old gentleman" went to buy cakes for "Miss Younger Sister." Alone on the mountain gathering vegetables, "Miss Younger Sister" slipped and fell against a tree. When she woke up, she was already lying in bed, with "the old gentleman" tearfully calling her name beside her. It turned out that upon returning with the cakes that afternoon and finding her missing, the old gentleman frantically searched everywhere, eventually discovering "Miss Younger Sister" unconscious halfway up the mountain. He cried loudly. After bringing her home, "the old gentleman" repeatedly boiled herbs to wash her wounds, chewing the herbs and applying them to her injuries. Three months later, "Miss Younger Sister" recovered completely without a single scar.

As the interview concluded, the two elders embraced and escorted the reporter out of the valley. Unable to suppress their deep emotions, "the old gentleman" sang an ancient love song: "Younger Sister dressed in green, like a willow spirit in the mountain; you are the evergreen willow, and I am the pigeon seeking shade..."

Reporter Dong Yi, Correspondent Cao Cheng, Photography Report
