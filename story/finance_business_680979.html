[Refined in the Crucible - Golden Armor Divine Ox]

The Golden Armor Divine Ox is the mount of Laozi, appearing in the "Ao Jian" world as a blue ox clad in battle armor. [Arctic Titan - Heavenly Executioner Savage Wolf]

Its snow domain radiance cannot be obstructed by its gold-forged super battle armor; it is the Heavenly Executioner Savage Wolf, a legendary mount in "Ao Jian."
