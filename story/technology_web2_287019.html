Can't help but do website content optimization

by tuoqing05 on 2009-12-01 01:53:14

The content of a website is the core of search engine optimization. Many SEO practitioners believe in the concept of "content is king." SEO merely enhances the role that website content plays. Despite years of development, China's SEO industry has gradually become more regulated and less impetuous. Most SEOers emphasize the importance of website content.

However, there are currently no systematic articles in China about website content optimization analysis. Most articles only stress the originality of website content. While original content indeed greatly helps with search engine optimization, it is just a small part of SEO content optimization. There are other aspects of SEO based on website content that we need to pay attention to.

Reliability of Website Content

The reliability of website content should be the first principle of website content. Imagine if a website's content was all false and had no reliability at all. Such a website would definitely not be valued by search engines, let alone users. Many people think that search engines cannot identify the accuracy of website content, but many things are complementary. Moreover, given the sophistication of search engine algorithms, we cannot conclude that search engines cannot understand this content.

Therefore, when you cannot create original or authoritative content, you should try your best to select high-credibility content to enrich your website.

Authority of Website Content

Authoritative content is more reliable. The best example is the Chinese Government website. From the perspective of content optimization, search engines will never question the authority of www.gov.cn because it represents the Chinese government. Its content authority is undoubtedly the highest.

In terms of content authority, edu and gov domain names have certain advantages. However, content authority needs time to cultivate. Unless your website's reputation is very high, which can shorten the time needed to establish your website's authority, ordinary websites must rely on time accumulation to establish their authoritative status in search engines, in addition to strictly requiring high-quality website content.

Uniqueness of Website Content

Content uniqueness refers to original content. Regarding this point, readers of Siyiou should already be very familiar. Search engines have strict content filters aimed at reducing the inclusion of duplicate content. If a website's content uniqueness is too low, it will create obstacles for the website's development. It will be harder to gain weight and traffic.

Completeness of Website Content

The completeness of website content is also a key focus of website content optimization. In China's SEO industry, some pseudo-original content theories have emerged. What is pseudo-original? It means either disrupting the structure and order of an article or copying segments from other articles, piecing them together into one article. Some domestic SEOers call this pseudo-original. The incompleteness of website content greatly harms the development of the website. Moreover, using pseudo-original methods to solve the originality of website content may not work. Because some search engines store articles by paragraph, not by article. Therefore, even through methods such as disrupting the structure and order of an article or copying content from other articles, it is still impossible to escape the algorithm of search engines.

Optimization based on website content is the core of SEO. Non-original content, through time accumulation, can also gain the attention of search engines, as long as your content is good enough, rich, and relevant.
