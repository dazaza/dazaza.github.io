Economically applicable men

by jimwang on 2010-09-21 17:32:10

The definition of this type of man is: average height, traditional hairstyle, forgettable appearance; gentle personality, salary willingly handed over to wife; non-smoker, non-drinker, mobile phone never turned off, no gambling, no close female friends; monthly salary between 3000-10000 yuan, with the ability to pay the down payment on a house; generally working in education, IT, mechanical manufacturing, technical industries, etc.

"Men who are more family-oriented than my husband aren't as wealthy as him, and men who are wealthier than my husband aren't as family-oriented." This is why many female white-collar workers have changed their ideal partner from a "golden turtle son-in-law" to an "economically affordable man." On the internet, "Sha Wujing" has been chosen as the image spokesperson for "economically affordable men," and the reason "Sha Wujing" became popular is because "he is a good son, a good employee, and has the potential to be a good husband and father, he can be called a growth stock."

Other types of men:

Toy Boy

This is a new term from South Korea. It originates from a popular music group in South Korea in the 1990s called "Toy," which attracted many female fans due to its melancholic and poetic lyrics.

"Toy Boy" refers to the new generation of men emerging in South Korea. They differ greatly from the traditional Korean male image, being sensitive, poetic, gentle and considerate towards women, but they advocate celibacy. These men are mainly aged between 20 and 30-plus, and their typical characteristics are: finding solace in music, enjoying shopping for clothes in small shops, knowing how to play the clarinet, riding small motorcycles, privately collecting red wine. Moreover, these men are quite "homebodies," they enjoy cooking pasta at home and strictly control their diet.

Bento Man

Since the outbreak of the global financial crisis last year, Japan's single nobility is no longer obsessed with money games, but instead has developed a strong interest in simple living. Under the slogan of "saving money first," "Bento Men" have emerged. Originally, Japanese department stores only did business in student bento boxes, but recently they have designed bento boxes that look "cool" and are easy to carry for Japanese male office workers. Men entering the kitchen is no longer a shameful thing in Japan. Moreover, "Bento Men," after "Three Low Men," have become the recent popular romantic partners for Japanese women.

Herbivore Men

In popular descriptions, "Herbivore Men" are like herbivorous animals. They are friendly and gentle, but in matters of love and marriage, they often lack the initiative that men should have. Their biggest characteristic is that they do not actively pursue romance or sexual relationships, but prefer to keep a lukewarm pace with girls.

"Herbivore Men" are often also "Popular Men." They don't just use their affability on the opposite sex, but more widely on friends and family. They prefer to be with friends or relatives, enjoying happiness.

Buffalo Striving Men

Contrary to "Diamond Men" who have houses and cars, the main feature of "Buffalo Striving Men" is that they currently lack a good economic foundation, yet they possess reliable charisma and trustworthy qualities. They work diligently for life, have ambition, and are very committed to their families and emotions. They belong to the traditional and honest type of men, usually without a dating history, and high loyalty. They are a "good stock" that hasn't been overhyped, with a low price-to-earnings ratio, low risk, and a high safety margin, worth owning for life.

Phoenix Men

As the name implies, "a golden phoenix flying out of the mountain ravine," it refers to those who come from humble origins (especially from rural areas), endured much hardship to get into university, and after graduation, stayed in the city to work and live. The cruelty and hardship of life have left deep marks on their hearts, making them generally possess the spirit of hard work and the courage to fight that people with good family backgrounds do not have. However, after entering the city, they married "Peacock Women" and lived urban lives.
