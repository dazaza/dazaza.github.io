Episode 1

In the mid-80s, Colonel Luo Yangba, who already had three daughters - Deqi, Xueqi, and Mei Qi, was eagerly expecting his wife to give birth to a son. His mother-in-law was also certain that this time it would be a boy. However, the result was another daughter. In frustration, Yangba decided not to have any more children and named this daughter "Zhongqi."

Xueqi and Mei Qi are twin sisters but are completely different in appearance and personality. Although Xueqi is not as pretty as Mei Qi, she studies hard and is the most favored daughter by Yangba. She is currently preparing to take the entrance exam for the Military Academy. On the other hand, Mei Qi has no interest in studying and only cares about her appearance. Whenever the report cards are announced, one receives praise while the other gets punished. The eldest daughter, Deqi, does not want to follow her father's wish to marry a soldier and thus marries Goo Soohan after meeting him just three times through her mother's introduction.

In 2006, Zhongqi is retaking classes to enter the Military Academy but secretly dates her tutor, Crown Prince. Mei Qi incurs credit card debt due to her vanity and asks her sister, Lieutenant Xueqi, for money. However, Xueqi, who has already helped Mei Qi pay off her debts many times, refuses her request, leading Mei Qi to complain about Xueqi.

Episode 2

Ri Han originally planned to attend a couples' gathering with Xueqi but couldn't make it due to an emergency at Xueqi's base. Uncle Kong Xiubiao, concerned about the fine, introduces Ri Han to Mei Qi from a role-playing event center. Together, they attend the couples' gathering. Mei Qi's natural social skills and charm, along with her outstanding dance moves, bring much glory to Ri Han in front of his friends. Ri Han falls in love with Mei Qi at first sight and invites her to meet again after the gathering, but Mei Qi declines immediately.

Excited about the couples' gathering with Ri Han, Xueqi is called back to her base when a soldier goes missing. She searches for the missing soldier alongside younger soldiers and eventually finds him drunk in a small eatery. The next day, Xueqi apologizes to Ri Han for not attending the gathering. Ri Han tells Xueqi about how he met Mei Qi. Watching Ri Han immersed in happiness, Xueqi feels disappointed inside.

When loan collectors come to their home because Mei Qi borrowed money from a high-interest lender, Mei Qi pleads with her mother for help. Just as her mother is swayed by Mei Qi's words, Xueqi overhears their conversation and stops her mother from helping Mei Qi. An angry Mei Qi argues with Xueqi. Suddenly, their father Yangba comes home, causing panic among the three.
