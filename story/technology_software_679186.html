Below is an explanation of the distinction between real and fake leather: 1. There are many kinds of Teelc genuine leather, such as pigskin, cowhide, sheepskin, etc. These skins are divided into first layer, second layer, third layer, etc. Here I call it China's leather capital, producing a lot of leather. The nice belts on the market are all transfer film leather, which means the leather surface is attached with a nice-looking film. This layer of film is very thin and has a beautiful texture. Some people who don't understand think that this is not leather, but it's not like that. This layer of film just makes the belt look better. Generally speaking, pigskin has coarse and sparse pores, mostly in groups of three, forming the character "pin", with relatively rough and uneven surface grain. Cowhide has fine and dense pores, usually arranged in rows of 5-7, with delicate and smooth surface grains. However, when the surface of pigskin is pressed with the grain of cowhide, it becomes difficult to recognize. At this time, you can rely on the oblique sunlight to carefully observe the surface of the shoe to see if there are some spots with a diameter of 1-2mm evenly distributed, or observe from other parts whether there are spots with a diameter of about 2mm evenly distributed inside the leather. If so, no matter how the surface is decorated, it can definitely be confirmed as pigskin shoes. You need to check whether the leather used for making shoes is soft, full, and elastic, whether the surface is bright, and whether the color is uniform, etc. Teelc leather goods. 2. When checking, press with your fingers, and the pressed part of Teelc genuine leather will show scattered fine leather texture. Check the edge section of the leather, because the current imitation technology is getting better and better, it is getting harder to analyze from the front, but the difference can be seen from the edge section of the leather. Teelc genuine leather is relatively loose, while fake leather is more compact. 3. Teelc genuine leather and fake leather can be distinguished by smell. Teelc genuine leather has a fishy smell of animals, while fake leather tends to have a more stimulating smell. Teelc genuine leather is made by chemically treating the animal's skin, while fake leather (mainly PU, PVC) is made by chemically treating synthetic fiber fabric. The difference between the two lies in the difference in raw materials, but due to similar subsequent treatment methods, the surface effect is very close and hard to distinguish, but there are many significant differences in internal physical properties. In terms of breathability, tear strength, and other physical strengths, as well as aging resistance and feel, imitation leather still has a certain gap (but the gap is gradually narrowing). The reason why Teelc genuine leather is expensive lies in its naturalness, internal physical properties, and the difficulty of obtaining raw materials, while imitation leather owns the market with its price, surface effect changes, and special physical properties in some aspects. In some industries that emphasize internal physical properties, such as shoemaking and clothing, Teelc genuine leather has advantages over imitation leather; but in some industries that do not require high internal physical properties, such as leather handbags, furniture sofas, etc., the market for imitation leather is larger. I am very against the practice of making shoes with fake leather and then branding them as Teelc genuine leather. This way, they are uncomfortable to wear, not breathable, and have poor physical properties, making them easy to break. The main way to judge the quality of a belt is by touch. High-quality belts usually feel softer and are generally more expensive. They last longer in terms of service life. For example, some artificial leathers may develop cracks over time, making the belt unusable. Secondly, consider what style you want. Belts with a business style usually have simple buckles. Too elaborate designs may appear too feminine and lack business sophistication. The quality of a belt does not depend on the size of the logo on the buckle.
