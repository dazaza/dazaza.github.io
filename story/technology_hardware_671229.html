Flower Network Flower Network Pursue You to the End (Sketch Script)

by kissxh770l on 2012-02-21 17:07:14

Male lead: Wenxing (played by Guo Zhaoyang) Female lead: Huiwen (played by Wang Xiaoqing) Supporting male role: Xiaohua (played by Zhang Mingming) Background of the play: Without chocolates on Valentine's Day, without eye-catching bouquets of roses, and without the ubiquitous embraces on campus. This is just a simple and unadorned story that happens around us. Our male lead didn't ride a white horse to sweep away Cinderella; with his glasses and his clumsiness, he clumsily chased after his girl. And Huiwen, who was cheerful and lively, was confused, and rejection seemed to be the fastest way. How will they face the first rain of love after saying goodbye to high school? Props: Glasses, mobile phone, photo Stage background: Somewhere on campus (the male lead enters from the left side of the stage) Wenxing (carrying a schoolbag, holding a group photo from military training): He said this man is not young anymore, how can he not have a wife? I met the most beautiful scenery since entering university during military training, I fell in love with a beautiful lady from our chemistry department, she is so pretty (Wenxing raises the photo of the military training group to show the audience), I not only know she is Huiwen from Class 2 of Applied Chemistry 09, but I also got her phone number and QQ number, I've been pursuing her for a long time, hehe (laughing smugly) she even agreed to meet me! (The female lead enters from the right side of the stage) Huiwen (slightly irritated): University boys these days are really something, instead of studying during their precious time, they either indulge in online games or do meaningless things. I don't know who I offended, but recently, one guy has been leaving random messages on my QQ space or sending emotional texts like "You are my only pursuit in university, the only truth in life" and "Your kindness and gentleness make my heart ache" (changing tone) Tell me, can you believe someone who claims to love you when you haven't even met them? Audience shouting: Can't believe it! Can't believe it! (After the audience's voices subside) Huiwen: That's right, whoever believes it is an idiot. Fortunately, I managed to get him out today, we need to clear this up. (Huiwen looks around) Wenxing: (seeing Huiwen at the center of the stage, suddenly comparing the photo, almost sticking his nose to the photo) Really, really, it's really her! Everyone says there are many beauties at Zhangzhou Teachers College, but there are even more beauties in our Applied Chemistry major. (calms down, walks forward) Fellow student, are you Huiwen from the Applied Chemistry Department of the Chemistry Department? Huiwen (hesitates slightly) Yes, who are you? Wenxing (excited) I am also from the Chemistry Department, I am Wenxing from Science Education. Huiwen (takes a few looks) You are Wenxing, did you write those text messages and the comments on my QQ space? Wenxing (a bit proud) Yes, ever since I saw you for the first time, my world has been filled with light and vitality. The boring university life has become sweet ever since. Whenever the wind blows, I recall your faint smile. Huiwen (apologetically) I don't know if I misunderstood, but I think you shouldn't waste your time on me. Wenxing (shyly) Actually, you didn't misunderstand. I have secretly liked you. Your smile, your mischief, your innocence and beauty always make me marvel at what dreams and scenes are. Huiwen (feeling speechless) Have we met before? Wenxing: We have, definitely. Once during the tug-of-war competition at the end of military training, another time at the school sports meeting. And now is another time. Huiwen (seeing Wenxing's attitude, somewhat puzzled if she was familiar with this person before) Do you really like me? But we have only met a few times? Wenxing: Your question seems too naive. A few years ago, there was such a thing as love at first sight, I just have love at second sight. If you think we haven't met enough, flowers and gifts, we can meet more times. Huiwen (still puzzled) But if you don't understand a person, how can you like them? Is it because of appearance? Wenxing (his face suddenly changes, unsure how to answer, awkwardly takes out his phone, pretending to take a call, jogs away) Hello! Xiaohua, she asked if I like her appearance, this question doesn't have an answer in books, we didn't prepare for this question. (A male enters the stage) Xiaohua: Absolutely cannot say it's because of appearance, otherwise girls won't trust you. Wenxing: But if I say it's not about appearance, isn't that saying she is not pretty enough? Xiaohua: For example, if you want to express that you like the other party, you must use "I love you, beauty", and not "Beauty, I love you!" Using "beauty" at the front will make it seem like you lust for beauty. Wenxing: Got it, but I've only met twice, besides appearance, what else do I know? Xiaohua: Just make up something, I'll come over to help you soon. (Xiaohua leaves the stage) Wenxing (walks forward again) I like your gentle character, quiet temperament, not just your appearance. Whether on the tug-of-war field or on the sports field, when I see you cheering, my heart deeply indulges, moved by you. Huiwen: That's collective honor, everyone has it. If you really like someone, what you should like most is her soul. Wenxing (quietly scolds himself) How did I not think of using the word 'soul'? Huiwen: Don't waste time on hopeless things, I won't accept you, no matter how long you persist, I can only say sorry. Wenxing: I won't give up, because you are the only truth in life, though I may die nine times, I will not regret. Huiwen: I already have someone I like, becoming boyfriend and girlfriend is just a matter of time, so you'd better find another girl! (Wenxing looks painful, insert music "Farewell My Concubine") In this world, there are hundreds of twists and turns... Huiwen (seeing Wenxing sad, feels a bit sorry) When you pursue a girl in the future, best let her see you, text messages are too weak, they can't replace the reality of a person. Wenxing (pauses for a long time, bitterly says) Is that guy real? We studied you for so long, we've already determined that you don't have a boyfriend. Huiwen: We grew up together, our parents are all friends, so becoming boyfriend and girlfriend is just a matter of time. Wenxing: All I can do is silently bless you. Wenxing turns around, facing away from Huiwen, wanting to leave the stage. Huiwen: By the way, how did you know my name and phone number? Wenxing (weakly says) Don't mention that, I even know your QQ number, your dormitory number, even your bed number. Huiwen (covers mouth and says) That's terrifying! Wenxing (calmly looks) Yes, since I decided to pursue you, I read "The Art of War", fully investigated methods to pursue you, formulated a battle plan. Warfare primarily values intelligence, so I must know the names of your class president and dorm leader, and comprehensively analyze your personality and preferences, thus achieving a decisive victory. Huiwen (can't help but laugh) Then wasn't I being watched every day, feeling uneasy inside. Wenxing: It's nothing much, I just slightly asked some people around you, gathered information, then analyzed possible defense weaknesses you might have back in the dorm, and planned accordingly. The main focus last semester was on defense, first ensuring invincibility, then waiting for the enemy's opportunity... Huiwen: Aren't you very tired? Wenxing: When you like someone, you go crazy for her, change for her. Huiwen: (softly) I'm really sorry. Wenxing: (laughs bitterly, pretends to be relaxed) No problem, reading so many books, isn't it also gaining knowledge? Unfortunately, I couldn't win your affection. Huiwen: You can use them to pursue other girls, there are many beauties in our class, or I can introduce someone to you. Wenxing: Look at you, how could I be such a philandering man? As ancient sayings go: "Among the flowers, I lazily look back, half due to cultivation, half due to you." Huiwen (laughs and cries) Truly can't tell, you cultured man. Wenxing: Of course, remember when I scored full marks on the college entrance exam essay? Huiwen: You're just boasting, look how high you've blown yourself up, aren't you scheming something in your mind? (Seeing him getting excited, she continues to fish for more details) So what were your original plans, what are your plans for next semester? Wenxing: According to military books, "Attack requires seizing opportunities, swift as lightning," so the strategy for next semester will be a full-scale offensive. First, there will be love letters, then flowers sent to your dormitory from my dormitory building through the air, plus several "accidental" encounters, like when you're eating in the canteen, I "accidentally" bump into your tray, or when you're carrying a pile of books and I "unexpectedly" knock them over, ordering flowers for Christmas, or even going to your classroom to sit in on classes..." Ring...phone rings Xiaohua: Brother, how's the situation, I've arrived. Wenxing: It's useless, she has a childhood sweetheart boyfriend. Xiaohua: Impossible, Fresh Flowers Network, I've been classmates with her for so many years, not only do I know if she has a boyfriend, I even know how many people are in her family, I'll guide you to her mother-in-law's house in the future. Wenxing (shocked) What! Huiwen (hearing the voice on the phone, thinks for a moment) Xiaohua, get out here! Xiaohua holds the phone, not knowing whether to put it back in his pocket or not, smiles sheepishly: Beauty, you're here, what a coincidence! Wenxing you're here too, what a coincidence. Let's chat... Huiwen rushes forward, twists Xiaohua's ear: Old classmate, you care for me by sending flowers for your shop opening, calculating me with military tactics. Xiaohua (tilts his neck): Ow, ow... coldly laughs: What new tricks do you have today, bringing basketballs, aren't you planning to throw them later so Wenxing can heroically save me, followed by familiar faces meeting, then eating fruit together? Xiaohua: Ow, ow, look what you're saying, if you don't let go, I'll shout "harassment"! Huiwen: Go ahead and shout! (Despite saying this, she still lets go.) Wenxing: Could this be the Huiwen classmate we describe as tranquil as a mountain stream, gentle like a spring breeze in February? Isn't this damaging your image? Huiwen: So what? I can be very spoiled, careful not to scare you. Wenxing: Individuality, I like it. Huiwen: Be careful, the number of boys I've beaten would probably scare you. Xiaohua: I believe that! (Huiwen glares at him, Xiaohua quickly shrinks back, clearly afraid of being twisted again.) Wenxing: No problem! Actually, when I initially planned everything, I had already considered the factor of your bad temper, I'm strengthening myself! Xiaohua: I can vouch for that! Wenxing absolutely treats you like a fool. Huiwen: Are boys pursuing girls all actions coordinated by the dormitory? Too insincere. Wenxing: My sincerity is deeper than the sea, harder than steel, can I ask you out again? Huiwen: Apart from love, don't you have any other pursuits? In our school, there are senior students like Liang Hongyan actively joining the female soldiers, and many senior students and alumni dedicating themselves to grassroots work. Huiwen: The Central Committee has proposed the Haixi Construction Plan in its "Several Opinions", Fujian is about to undergo significant changes, good timing, we should learn skills well and seize this opportunity. Wenxing: Isn't this premature? Huiwen: Since you're from the Chemistry Department, mastering your skills can contribute to the petrochemical industry. Wenxing (looking at Huiwen): Yes, I understand. (Then emotionally says) Remember the first time I saw you, you wore a delicate watch, carried a white small bag, that lively figure, I knew I was struck by you. Huiwen (startled), delicate watch, white bag? I never had a watch, nor a white bag. Wenxing (takes out his glasses): Ah! I mistook someone, the one I saw wasn't you. Xiaohua laughs until his stomach hurts, runs off stage. Huiwen (chases Xiaohua off stage), don't laugh! Wenxing (turns around): After chasing for so long, mistakes happen, can I still chase you? (No one in front) Curtain falls This is a comedy of campus boys pursuing girls, full of youthful energy and carefree joy, it's the most authentic reflection of their naivety upon entering the small society of university. With this, we wish the freshmen of 2009 a pure first love before entering the big dye vat of society. Scriptwriter: Zhang Gongchang, Longjiang River Literature Society, March 28, 2010
