The advent of technology has brought the world of betting right into the palm of one's hand through the mobile sports betting capabilities of smartphones. Betting on a sport has become a sport in itself, with more and more people placing their money to win big on various sports. There is no sport these days that doesn't get betted on. Now, technology is being used to make betting an even more accessible sport! Gone are the days of standing in long queues at horse races or missing out on betting due to household chores. Bookmakers also lost potential customers who couldn't make it down to place bets on their favorite football teams in league matches or other sports that were being wagered on.

Betting companies are now coming up with innovative ideas to ensure that wagering continues using mobile sports betting. Mobile sports betting eliminates all the previous problems associated with physical presence. So if a person has a mobile phone, they just need to register their mobile number and download the mobile betting application on their web-enabled device to start mobile betting. On-the-go betting applications now make it easy for football fans to wager using web-based mobile football betting software. Some betting portals also focus on iPhone-based betting applications to enable iPhone betting.

The rise of web-based mobile technology has introduced the concept of Mobile sports betting, with portal applications designed specifically for mobile users. It was the need to be able to place a bet without losing out on possible successful wagers that gave birth to this concept. The bettor simply needs to download the application on their mobile device and enjoy betting anytime, anywhere!
