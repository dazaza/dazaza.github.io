In the previous Career Lecture, Professor Wang brought us the first lecture of the Public Relations Etiquette Series - PR Etiquette. Building on this, the 19th Career Lecture will bring you the second lecture "Public Relations Management", enhancing everyone's awareness of public relations and improving social communication skills.

Harmonious interpersonal relationships are a lifeline for career success, and public relations plays an important role in our work. Nowadays, enterprises increasingly recognize the importance of public relations. Its contribution to corporate image and marketing will gradually surpass that of advertising. Especially when dealing with emergencies, the importance of PR becomes even more evident.

Professor Wang Shengduo, Senior PR Trainer, Ph.D. candidate in Philosophy at Fudan University, Senior Trainer for enterprises and institutions, hailed as the "Pioneer and Authority of PR in Wenzhou," author of "New Perspectives on Public Relations" which has been widely disseminated.
