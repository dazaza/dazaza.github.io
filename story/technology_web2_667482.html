Yes Donna Putter An exclusive hybrid model came into the scene when Yes Golf Company came up with a new design of a putter. High quality aluminum is used in the making of the putter face and the body of the putter is made from high quality stainless steel. With this combination a great moment of inertia can be generated which makes sure that if a golfer hits the ball off the line of putt,NIKE Victory Red Forged Wedge, then the putter would be more forgiving. Besides the exclusive putter face,Odyssey White Hot Tour # Putter, the head of the putter cannot be termed as a complete mallet as it also consist a low cavity back blade design. The face of the entire putter is balanced ideally and can stand upright on its own. The weight of the putter head is 355gms and the loft is 2.5 degrees. The weight and the loft thus ensure that the ball is ideally pushed outside its depression and doesn’t even bounce. The company also provides anodized grooves and a bending shaft feature. This putter also has custom fitted head covers. Yes Donna Putter has much similarity with Anser blade putter and the only difference being that it’s slightly larger than that. It’s quite similar to the traditional blade putter design and would be loved by those who love smooth, simple yet effective design of a blade putter. This putter is an insert putter and offers extremely good feedback and feel to the player trying to offer a stroke. Yes Donna putter would certainly rank among the top insert putters due to its ultimate feel. It’s also ideal for short putts as it gives a solid feel once held in the hand. Incorporating the C grooves in the putter head is the greatest advantage in playing with Yes Donna putter. This putter offers a more guaranteed distance control and better movement along the line of putt.
