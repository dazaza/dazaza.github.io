Real Estate Mortgage Loan Contract for the Special Economic Zone

The contracting parties, hereinafter referred to as the mortgagee and —— (hereinafter referred to as the mortgagor) (for detailed information of the mortgagor, please refer to Appendix I), have signed this real estate mortgage loan contract (hereinafter referred to as the contract) on —— at —— on the —— day of —— month, —— year. The mortgagee has lent HKD —— (hereinafter referred to as the loan) to the mortgagor on the same day, and a promissory note has been signed. The mortgagor is willing to pledge the real estate listed in Appendix II (hereinafter referred to as the mortgaged property) to the mortgagee, granting the mortgagee the first priority mortgage right, and is willing to fulfill all terms of the contract.

Both parties have agreed to establish the following terms, which should be followed:

(1) Payment:

(1) The mortgagor must pay the amount due to the mortgagee or according to other methods designated by the mortgagee, in accordance with the method specified in the promissory note and Appendix II, within the prescribed number of installments and payment dates. If the mortgagor pays off the debt on time according to the aforementioned method and fulfills all terms of the contract, they may redeem the property pledged under this contract to the mortgagee according to Article (7).

(2) The mortgagor shall pay a handling fee of five per thousand of the loan amount and must provide all relevant information regarding the loan absolutely truthfully. If it is found after signing the contract that the information provided by the mortgagor does not match the facts, the mortgagee has the right to immediately recover the loan and interest payable, and will not refund the handling fee collected according to the contract.

(3) According to Appendix II, paying in installments is the primary condition of this contract. If the mortgagor fails to pay all or part of the due amount on time, they must pay overdue interest. The overdue interest rate is calculated monthly and determined by the mortgagee. The minimum monthly interest rate is 2%. This interest accumulates daily from the due date until the date when the payment is received, whether before or after the determination of the debt. If the mortgagor does not pay the principal and interest or any part thereof on time, all outstanding amounts and interest, regardless of whether they are due, must be repaid immediately to the mortgagee.

(4) Before the loan is fully repaid, the mortgagor must pay interest at the rate stipulated in this contract. However, the mortgagee may adjust the interest rate at any time based on market conditions. Once adjusted, the new interest rate takes effect immediately. Starting from one month after the disbursement of the loan, the principal and interest will be repaid in installments according to the prescribed number of payments, each installment's amount, and payment dates. If there is a change in the interest rate, the mortgagee will notify the mortgagor in writing of the higher or lower rate. The mortgagee has the absolute right to adjust and modify the amount of each installment or the number of repayments if necessary.

(5) All monthly installment amounts must be paid according to the prescribed repayment schedule. In case of overdue payments, the mortgagor must immediately make up the missed payments and overdue interest. The overdue interest rate is unilaterally determined by the mortgagee, with a minimum monthly interest rate of 2%.

(6) If the mortgagor fails to pay all amounts stipulated in this contract as agreed, leading to the mortgagee collecting or deciding through any means or ways to pursue collection, all costs incurred thereby (if collateral exists, including various handling fees, management fees, insurance premiums, etc.) shall be borne by the mortgagor. These costs will accumulate daily from the actual payment date at the above-mentioned overdue interest rate until the date of receipt.

(7) The mortgagor must open a deposit account at —— Bank —— Branch, and hereby authorizes the mortgagee to transfer the principal and interest related to this mortgage loan and all fees to that account. If this leads to an overdraft or increased overdraft in the account, the mortgagor assumes full responsibility for repayment.

(8) All payments made to the mortgagee should be delivered to —— Bank —— Branch or transferred via the bank’s head office or branch. These payments must be made in Hong Kong dollars.
