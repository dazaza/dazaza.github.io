Website: http://translator.live.com

Website: http://translate.live.com

Website: http://windowslivetranslator.com

Provide free online translation service, can translate text documents and web pages, support English and Spanish, French, German, Italian, Dutch, Portuguese, Russian, Norwegian, Chinese simplified, Chinese traditional, etc. Many people can't find the address of SDL's free simultaneous translation service, check out the Shout network, we help you find everything.

Provide mutual translation between 34 languages, is a relatively complete one, automatic detection, input text can automatically detect language, multi-language synchronous translation, you can choose a variety of languages at the same time, a translation into multiple languages, real-time translation, with your input real-time online translation, simple translation, no need to register the installation of green services.

Frengly

Website: http://www.frengly.com/

Small, fast, and very "smart". Frengly provides translation between 24 languages, enter your text message, Frengly will automatically parse the language, click to select the desired language, soon Frengly will provide the corresponding translation in the dialog box below. Tried several times, the speed is good, the translation is more accurate, at least the Chinese-English translation effect is good.

Jollo

Website: http://www.jollo.com/

If you want to use online translation, Jollo has it all! First of all, Jollo is an online translation aggregation service that aggregates the most popular online translation engines such as Google Translate, Yahoo Babelfish, Microsoft Live Translate, Worldlingo, SDL and Youdao Translate, and users can vote yes or no for each translator according to the effect of the translation. Thus, the star rating for each translator is used as a reference when you choose the translation result. Secondly, Jollo itself is also an online translator, although it provides not many language translation functions, but it provides a powerful interactive translation community, in special occasions, you can submit your own text as an "increase translation request", request friends in the community to help translate.
