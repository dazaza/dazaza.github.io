Quick, easy and stylish decoration inspiration. Stylish decoration gives the home a brand-new look.

by kjp1982 on 2007-08-17 15:33:54

Actually, it doesn't require a big budget to decorate your house with charming and fascinating colors. Here we will introduce some fast, simple and fashionable decoration inspirations, using a personalized approach to give the living space a brand-new look.

Furniture Revival

Dress up your furniture with trendy and personalized looks. For example, change the textile cover of a chair or paste modern wallpapers on cabinets, and the furniture will immediately look brand new.

Various Glass Mirrors Bring Crystal Clearness and Sparkle

Place decorations of different shapes and styles on the mantelpiece or decorative shelves, which will immediately become the focus of attention. Old-fashioned butterfly mirrors are interesting when paired with two flower-patterned mirrors.

Visual Experience: http://diy.e-jjj.com/refine.asp?id=162

Decorated Door Handles

The simplest things often have the most vitality. Change the door handle to a fashionable and compact style, for instance, use transparent materials or hang a beautiful necklace or tie - this can brighten up a narrow corridor.

Scattered Items Become Focus

Behind the door, use thumbtacks or adhesive tape to display your favorite pictures, photos, postcards, etc., which will be a surprising decorative highlight.

Visual Experience: http://diy.e-jjj.com/refine.asp?id=234

Curtains Emanate New Art

Fabric with novel patterns, made into half-length curtains, can be simply hung with taut metal wires and copper rings, without even needing hemming.

Visual Experience: http://diy.e-jjj.com/refine.asp?id=234

Graffiti on Windows

Interesting writings can add a sense of fun and uniqueness. First, choose a poem or prose that you like (the text here is excerpted from "Alice's Adventures in Wonderland"), then write it on the window with a pen. These words are very easy to clean.

Visual Experience: http://diy.e-jjj.com/refine.asp?id=3662

Fresh and Fashionable Flowers and Plants

Select some bulb plants, soil, and gravel from a florist or nursery, and do some simple planting in acrylic containers or square glass vases, which will invigorate the room while you quietly wait for the flowers to bloom.

Visual Experience: http://diy.e-jjj.com/refine.asp?id=180

Exquisite Embroidery Erases Plainness

Decorate cushions with beads or small metal pieces, and the original simplicity will instantly exude an elegant charm. Handmade and processed accessories have been very popular since the beginning of this year, so visit those handicraft stores where you might find something new.

Visual Experience: http://diy.e-jjj.com/refine.asp?id=175

Highlighting Personalized Walls

Add some glowing colors or print some floral patterns on the walls to bring a touch of lightness and joy to the living space. The chairs are also painted in corresponding colors and printed with the same patterns.

Wallpaper Ingeniously Combines for the Finishing Touch

Mix different wallpapers to create a wall with a personalized style. The key to choosing wallpaper lies in color coordination, with intervals of two or three colors that can be repeated in arrangement.
