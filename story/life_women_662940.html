Eye cream recommendation: The earlier you start, the younger you stay.

by liu514113345 on 2012-02-10 20:28:33

Ladies, skincare is an eternal topic. When it comes to skincare, the earlier you start, the better. Someone once said, "Your skin stays as young as the age you start caring for it!" ... We should start taking care of our skin now, especially the delicate skin around our eyes. Are you ready at the age of 20? Below, I will recommend some eye creams (eye gels and refreshing types) that are suitable for people in their twenties currently available on the market.

Nowadays, girls mature early. If you're under 20, I would still suggest starting to cultivate a skincare habit! Enough chit-chat, here are some treasures suitable for young ladies.

**The Body Shop Elderflower Eye Gel**

This is Empress Dowager's favorite eye cream, consistently popular on Taobao. Its main function is to reduce dark circles. It has a gel-like texture, feels very refreshing, and doesn't cause milia.

**DHC Brightening Eye Gel**

This eye cream is very moisturizing but won't cause milia. It's also very economical and worth the price. There are no added fragrances, with a jelly-like consistency that is light and non-greasy. While hydrating our delicate eye area, it prevents dark circles and puffiness, making it the top choice for young skin.

**Avon Plant-Origin Brightening Eye Cream**

Natural Vitamin C helps remove dark circles and brightens the eye area. Vitamin A combined with rich minerals soothes eye fatigue. The texture is light and easily absorbed, gentle and non-irritating.

**Xiangyi Botanical Moisturizing Anti-Wrinkle Eye Cream**

After use, it feels very moisturizing without causing milia. The price is also very reasonable, making it one of the high-cost-performance eye creams.

**Herborist Eye Repair Gel**

Light and fine, never greasy, it moisturizes and helps repair the delicate and fragile skin around the eyes. It significantly reduces fine lines and improves uneven eye skin tone. It promotes blood circulation and can prevent dark circles.

**Nivea Eye Repair Essence**

An oil-free essence formula, it has a refreshing texture that is easily absorbed without irritating the delicate skin around the eyes. It effectively improves fine lines, reduces puffiness and dark circles, restoring firmness around the eyes.

The above ranking of eye creams for 20-year-olds basically includes creams that are effective and not too expensive. I hope this can be helpful to everyone. Also, there's one more recommendation: Dabao eye cream, which is also pretty good.
