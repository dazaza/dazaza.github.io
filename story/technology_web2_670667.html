Man abducts elderly woman after hitting her with his car and sexually assaults her

by anonymous on 2012-02-21 09:53:13

At around 4 a.m. on September 1st, a traffic accident occurred near the intersection of Tongling Road and Wangjiang Road in Hefei City. A flatbed cart loaded with vegetables pulled by an old woman collided with a black Honda sedan driven by a young man. What happened next was probably unimaginable to anyone: when the patrol police searched for the parties involved in this car accident after receiving the alarm, they actually found that the young man had taken the old woman to the back seat of the Honda and sexually assaulted her.

After the Traffic Accident

At around 4 a.m. on September 1st, another traffic accident happened near the intersection of Tongling Road and Wangjiang Road in Hefei City. A flatbed cart loaded with vegetables pulled by an elderly woman collided with a black Honda sedan driven by a young man. Some passers-by called the emergency number: the male driver got out of his car and beat the woman pulling the flatbed cart.

After receiving instructions from the command center, patrol cars No. 601 and 603 of Baohe Patrol Police arrived at the scene. However, apart from finding a flatbed cart, the patrol police did not find any other abnormalities.

The responding officers contacted the person who reported the incident. The informant said, "After beating the woman pulling the flatbed cart, the male driver took the woman along Wangjiang Road towards the direction of the Second Ring Road."

The informant described that the male driver was in his twenties, about 170 cm tall, of medium build; the woman pulling the cart was over 50 years old, slightly overweight, and looked like a vegetable seller based on her clothing.

Full Search

After the traffic accident, it's not uncommon for one party to physically assault the other. But why did the male driver take the woman away after beating her? The police speculated that the most likely reason was that the man took the victim to another place to continue the beating.

"The black Honda car, tail number 1787." The informant provided vehicle information.

There were many intersections near the scene of the incident, more than five within a kilometer radius. The smooth road conditions at dawn provided convenience for the man to quickly leave the scene, making the entire search process full of suspense.

Two patrol cars began searching along the route the Honda had taken, checking each suspicious intersection one by one, looking for the suspicious vehicle with the tail number 1787.

Patrol Police Shocked

On the north side of the green belt on the road east of the intersection of South Second Ring Road and Masan Road, a parked black car caught the attention of the police.

Upon closer inspection, the license plate ended in 1787, exactly the Honda car the informant claimed had forcibly taken the victim away.

Subsequently, what appeared before the patrol police left everyone shocked: in the rear seat of the car, a man was having sexual intercourse with a woman, their actions abnormal.

"Police! Get out of the car!" The officer forcibly pulled the man out of the car. The officer saw that the man was in his twenties, the woman in her fifties, matching the description given by the previous informant. It was later confirmed that these two were respectively the male driver and the female flatbed cart puller involved in the traffic accident.

The old lady looked dazed, clearly traumatized. After being asked multiple times, she finally spoke up and identified the man as having sexually assaulted her.

"What's your name? How old are you? What are you doing?" The officer repeatedly questioned, but the man remained silent.

When the officer put handcuffs on the man, they smelled a strong smell of alcohol.

Conflicting Confessions

Currently, this case has been transferred to the jurisdictional criminal investigation team.

After the incident, the elderly woman was very agitated and unwilling to talk about what happened.

Later, it was learned from the police that the man was 22 years old, and the woman was 59 years old.

It was understood that after the incident, the woman's emotions were always very agitated, and she was reluctant to mention what happened at the time, "Of course there were injuries, both physical and mental suffered great trauma, and she is so old..." said an informed person.

After learning this news, the woman's family members also rushed over from home to comfort her. While demanding that the public security organs severely punish the suspect, the emotions of her family members were also very agitated.

"The woman's children are older than the suspect," it was understood that after going to the hospital for a check-up, the woman then left. It was reported that the confessions given by the two were inconsistent, and further investigation and evidence collection of the case were needed.
