Charlene Cheung Felt Grieved after Nicholas Tse's Confession of Love, Returned to Hong Kong with 30 Security Guards Escorting

by doudouqaj on 2011-10-15 15:54:09

[Lead] Zhang Baiji, who was shooting a special program in Milan, returned to Hong Kong at 6:45 today. She refused to answer the questions raised by the reporters and hired more than thirty airport security personnel as an escort. It is reported that after Zhang Baiji learned of Nicholas Tse's confession, she felt extremely sad, using work to numb herself.

Tencent Entertainment News - On June 28, 2011, Hong Kong time, at 6:45 am, Baiji returned to Hong Kong from Milan where she had been shooting a special program. Her younger brother Hao Long accompanied her. Even before entering the arrival hall, more than thirty airport security personnel were already on standby. Dressed in a black leather jacket, sunglasses, and earphones, she seemed prepared to avoid reporters' questions about her marital issues. The reporters' inquiries received no response, and the scene was chaotic until she left in a limousine.

Zhang Baiji deeply saddened by Tse's confession

After publicly expressing his feelings about the marriage crisis two days ago in a five-minute heartfelt confession in Beijing, Nicholas Tse flew away early yesterday morning, exhausted and sleeping in the VIP lounge at the airport. It is rumored that Nicholas Tse and Zhang Baiji will divorce and go their separate ways. To ensure the healthy and stable growth of their two beloved children, Tse will strive for custody rights and personally guide them to maturity. As for Zhang Baiji, who is currently working in Milan, it is said that after learning of Tse's confession, she was extremely heartbroken, resorting to work to numb herself and forcing smiles.

Nicholas Tse attended the promotion of "The Money God" in Beijing the day before yesterday and gave a five-minute speech about his marriage with Zhang Baiji, frankly stating that although they still love each other, they are unsure how to proceed, seemingly hinting at a divorce. Although Zhang Baiji did not make any public statements, it is said that after hearing Tse's sincere confession, she was deeply saddened!

Praised as a Goddess by Fans

Yesterday, Zhang Baiji continued to shoot a special program for Now TV at an antique market in Milan. Dressed in a floral off-shoulder long dress, she bore her own pain silently, tears flowing inwardly, forcing smiles during filming. During this time, many enthusiastic citizens recognized her and approached her for photos. Zhang Baiji politely smiled and agreed to each request. Due to the large number of people wanting to take photos with her, those who couldn't get a chance lamented online, while those who managed to take photos treasured them greatly, uploading them to social media to boast. Among them was a netizen who claimed to have designed clothes for Lady Gaga and other celebrities, while another netizen described Zhang Baiji as a goddess: "The goddess touched me! She touched me! It was intoxicating!"

It was rumored that Zhang Baiji would return to Hong Kong in early July, but in fact, she had already completed her work yesterday and would fly back tonight, arriving in the morning. Afterward, she would prepare for the upcoming movie "Fear My Wife 2." Zhang Baiji was eager to complete the filming as soon as possible, not only to prepare for the new movie but also to quickly return to Hong Kong to take care of her two sons. As for when Zhang Baiji's special program filmed in Milan will be broadcast, a spokesperson for Now TV stated that due to Zhang Baiji's current family issues, adjustments will be made to the content shot in Milan, and the broadcast date will change accordingly, with no definite date set yet.

Delay in the Start of the New Film

Additionally, the film "River East Lion Roar 2," which Zhang Baiji starred in, was originally scheduled to start filming on July 1st but was postponed yesterday. When reporters called Yu Yuxing of New Asia Entertainment, one of the investors in the film, he stated: "We indeed decided to delay the start of filming, but it has nothing to do with Zhang Baiji's situation. July 1st was considered an unlucky day, and since the costumes designed by Cheung Shu-ping are still being rushed, we decided to delay filming by a few days. However, Zhang Baiji will arrive earlier to report to the production team."

Forced smiles amid inner turmoil.
