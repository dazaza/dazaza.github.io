Lessa "43210" service, worry-free all the way.

by ftleisa on 2011-12-09 09:27:12

Following the introduction of China's first National IV standard self-own-brand pump truck, the after-sales service sector within FTou Lei Sa, a subsidiary of Foton Motor - the world’s largest commercial vehicle company - has once again broken new ground. A few days ago, FTou Lei Sa officially announced a new service initiative under the "Full Journey Worry-Free" after-sales service brand of Foton Motors, tailored to the unique characteristics of the pumping machinery industry: the 43210 Service Commitment. Simultaneously, they launched the "Heart Service ? Ten Thousand Li Journey" user event.

FTou Lei Sa's "Full Journey Worry-Free" 43210 Service Commitment aims to meet the "Operation First" usage requirements of pumping machinery users. It is characterized by rapid response, one-stop chassis and bodywork services, and zero-time-difference service support. This commitment establishes a new benchmark for the domestic after-sales service sector in the pumping machinery industry. Vice President of FTou Lei Sa's Pumping Division, Shi Qiang, stated: "In the pumping machinery industry, what users fear most is machine downtime. FTou Lei Sa focuses on creating mid-to-high-end pumping machinery that delivers maximum value to our users. On one hand, we provide reliable and high-speed products so that users can operate with peace of mind. At the same time, our comprehensive, meticulous, and agile after-sales service ensures user satisfaction. This is also the original intention behind launching the 'Heart Service ? Ten Thousand Li Journey' user activity. The numbers 4, 3, 2, 1, 0 are like a countdown timer, reminding us that our customers’ operational time is money. When our users encounter issues during their operations, they will receive the fastest service response from FTou Lei Sa."

The "43210" New Standard Model represents a new standard. The 43210 Service Commitment is based on the internal essence of Foton Motor's "Full Journey Worry-Free" service and tailored to the unique characteristics of the pumping machinery industry. It introduces a new standard model for one-stop service and zero-time-difference service support, which is a first in China.

Specifically, it includes five major service commitments across various aspects such as response, training, maintenance, repair, and warranty:

- 24/7 hotline available throughout all four seasons, responding to customer needs at any time.

- Three value-added service projects: free training, monthly inspections, and free maintenance.

- Two-year ultra-long warranty period in the industry, providing users with more confidence and assurance.

- One-stop service: chassis and bodywork services resolved at one location, ensuring quality and speed control.

- Zero-time-difference service support: equipped with spare pump trucks for repairs, ensuring uninterrupted production.

Efficient and reliable Lei Sa pumping machinery products give users peace of mind, while the 43210 service commitment addresses all potential concerns comprehensively. Supported by Foton Motor's robust service network, FTou Lei Sa's service network is extensive with short service distances. Upon receiving a customer call, the nearest service station will respond quickly, implementing fast rescue and repairs. A complete service parts system ensures ample availability of parts, timely supply, low prices, and minimal repair costs, offering users convenience and cost-effectiveness. Compared to competitors in the industry, FTou Lei Sa offers the shortest service cycle, the longest warranty period, and free service maintenance, providing customers with an exceptional experience. Both the chassis and bodywork of FTou Lei Sa products are proprietary, allowing nationwide one-stop solutions through its 1,100 integrated chassis and bodywork service network, giving users a sense of ease when using Lei Sa products. Notably, FTou Lei Sa is equipped with dedicated spare pump trucks, providing free use of pump trucks during repairs to avoid disrupting customer production, fully safeguarding customer interests from the customer's perspective.

The 43210 service commitment not only represents FTou Lei Sa's service advantages but also adds highlights to Foton Motor's "Full Journey Worry-Free" service brand. Behind this lies FTou Lei Sa's innovative after-sales service system, fully implementing the after-sales service value pursuit of "Customer Needs First" and "Service Professional Standards." FTou Lei Sa users enjoy the internal essence of Foton Motor's "Three Full Services, Four Modernizations, Five Worry-Free Aspects, Six Unifications," along with exclusive one-stop services, zero-time-difference service support, and a series of value-added services from FTou Lei Sa.
