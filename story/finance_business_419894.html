I. Interpretation of individual stock information

1. Longjing Environmental Protection (600388) wins a 1 billion yuan contract in Indonesia. Positive attention recommended.

Interpretation: The company received news on Thursday evening that it partially won the bid for the Indonesian power plant and environmental protection equipment project. The total contract amount is about 1 billion yuan, which is approximately 30% of the total revenue in 2009. This project is expected to bring around 70 million yuan in net profit to the company, having a positive impact. Meanwhile, this contract also recognizes the competitiveness of the company's environmental protection equipment. It should be noted that both parties still need to sign a formal agreement. Short-term positive attention recommended.

2. Feiyada A (000026) plans to issue 50 million shares at no less than 12.45 yuan per share, and the plan has been approved by SASAC.

1) Investment project: Invest 410 million yuan in the expansion and reform of Heng Jixiang famous watch chain network, generating an annual net profit of 85.54 million yuan after completion.

2) Investment project: Invest 50 million yuan in the new product launch project of Feiyada watches, generating an annual net profit of 14.15 million yuan.

3) Investment project: Invest 40 million yuan in the marketing promotion project of Feiyada brand, with no profit. Positive attention recommended.

Interpretation: Feiyada is a well-known domestic watch manufacturer. In this targeted issuance, the parent company AVIC Group subscribed 80 million yuan, indicating their confidence in the projects. Moreover, the gross profit margin of the company's watch manufacturing business reaches over 60%, attracting many funds. Currently, funds hold 24% of the floating chips of this stock, and all have increased holdings without any reduction. The stock price has been around 14 yuan, not far from the issuance price region. Once the market stabilizes, this stock is likely to rebound first. Stay away from the issuance price region. Medium-term attention recommended.

3. Hanwang Technology (002362) announces the launch of 7 new facial recognition attendance machine products. Positive attention recommended.

Interpretation: Hanwang Technology is the leading domestic manufacturer of handwriting tablets. Since its listing, it has always been the focus of the market. From Hanwang e-books and facial recognition access control attendance machines, to tablet computers and the current facial recognition attendance machines, it fully demonstrates the company's enthusiasm and creativity in new products and projects. However, it often takes several years for new products to go from market cultivation to maturity to generate profits. Therefore, these projects cannot bring significant short-term benefits. But they lay the foundation for Hanwang's future development. Recently, the market seems to have developed a fear of high-priced stocks above 100 yuan, and negative news has emerged one after another. There is a trend of suppressing high-priced stocks. Long-term attention recommended.
