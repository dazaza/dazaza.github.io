BenQ Bling Limited Edition Digital Camera: Show off your fashionable personality to the fullest.

A second of pleasure, a lifetime of memories. The moment you press the shutter of the BenQ Bling limited edition digital camera, decay instantly evolves into magic. The Bling limited edition digital camera mainly includes two models: T700 and T800. With the "Crystal Love Ode" and "Romantic Stars Everywhere" theme styles carefully designed by BenQ designers on its shell, the Bling Bling color crystal water diamonds will surely be your personal accessory to show off yourself and highlight your personality. Style, fashion, and elegance are the characteristics of BenQ's limited edition cameras. Their exquisite thin beauty amazes you at first sight. Both cameras adopt a quad-directional extension design, giving an infinite visual experience. The body is extremely light and thin, only about two-thirds of the diameter of a one-yuan coin, with wrist strap design for easy carrying; available in multiple bright colors to meet your different personalities.

In terms of hardware configuration, the two digital cameras are quite similar. The T700 camera, with 7 million pixels, uses advanced smc PENTAX super multi-layer coated lenses, making the captured images exceptionally clear, sharp, realistic, and vivid. The unique SSR super anti-shake mode effectively avoids image blurring caused by hand shaking or object displacement. Similarly using smc PENTAX professional lenses, the T800 has increased its sensor component to 8 million pixels and added an advanced face priority focus mode, making the captured human facial images more distinct and prominent. It is worth mentioning that the intelligent zoom mode of the digital camera can provide different levels of zoom shooting according to the size of the captured image, ensuring that the enlarged image still maintains the original detailed pixel quality. The Z-lighting function can effectively guarantee post-adjustment of local brightness of photos. In addition, features such as face priority focus, high ISO value portrait shooting, and support for 3S super anti-shake ensure that clear images can be taken even in dim evenings.

Both Bling edition cameras have achieved a 3-inch ultra-large LTPS touch screen on their slim bodies. Compared to traditional LCD screens, the LTPS screen used in the Bling limited edition camera offers better display effects with lower power consumption and energy savings. The luxurious large screen provides a wide shooting view, allowing you to set frames or create personalized doodles. As long as you can imagine it, you can unleash your creativity directly on the camera screen. After completing your "artwork," you can use the local magnification function to check the effect immediately. Simply draw a circle with your finger on the screen to enlarge and view the local image, fully experiencing the creative joy of achieving what you desire instantly.
