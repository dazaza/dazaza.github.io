Laser acne scar removal: the National Sales Director of US Allergan Group visited Mei Zhiyuan Hospital

by dsaf12tr on 2012-02-15 13:13:18

On the 15th of this month, Kevin O'Brien, National Director of Sales for Allergan Group in the United States visited Shanghai Meizhiyuan Plastic Surgery Hospital. When discussing minimally invasive cosmetic procedures, he expressed that these are relatively easily accepted by beauty enthusiasts as a form of minimally invasive aesthetics.

After thoroughly understanding the treatment process for beauty seekers at Meizhiyuan Hospital, Kevin O’Brien said that this should be the most scientific treatment process in Shanghai so far, truly reflecting the service philosophy of cosmetic institutions which is all about serving beauty enthusiasts. Subsequently, Kevin O’Brien visited the hospital's wards, operating rooms, and laser beauty rooms one by one, repeatedly expressing that the environment of Meizhiyuan Hospital is elegant and clean. Doctor Cui Shuang from Meizhiyuan introduced him to COOL TOUCH and AFT composite polychromatic light instruments, and Kevin O’Brien stated that these instruments are among the most advanced in today’s cosmetic industry, with astonishing results. Upon learning that Doctor Cui had just won the "Minimally Invasive Cosmetic Expert Award" certified by the Chinese Medical Doctor Association in 2011, Kevin O’Brien extended his warmest congratulations.

Later, Doctor Teng gave Kevin O’Brien a brief introduction about the hospital's actual operation status and the projects popular among current patients. The proportion of minimally invasive cosmetic procedures in the hospital's total treatments has been increasing day by day. Especially projects like Botox injections and hyaluronic acid injections, which are minimally or non-invasive, have attracted many beauty enthusiasts. He mentioned that minimally invasive cosmetics are also more readily accepted by customers. Meizhiyuan Hospital has already made achievements in this direction and established a comprehensive minimally invasive cosmetic service process, achieving high customer satisfaction, which was affirmed by Kevin O’Brien. He indicated that the future trend of cosmetic development will inevitably focus on minimally invasive cosmetic technology, which is also the global trend in medical beauty consumption.

After understanding the overall situation of the hospital, Kevin O’Brien expressed his satisfaction before leaving, nodding continuously and praising: "That’s very good! (Very good!)" He was very happy that Allergan Group could cooperate with Shanghai Meizhiyuan Plastic Surgery Hospital.

On November 15, 2011, Kevin O’Brien, National Director of Sales for Allergan Group in the United States, visited Meizhiyuan Hospital. The hospital staff warmly received him and provided detailed introductions to the hospital's medical equipment and services.
