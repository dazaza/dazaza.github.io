Manchester United's number 1 defensive leader has seen his value skyrocket by 4 times, while a Barcelona mainstay was merely his substitute last year.

by qinglai888 on 2009-12-18 08:17:01

The first leader of Manchester United's defense has quadrupled in value, while Barcelona's mainstay was only his substitute last year. In the 110th league start, Vidic finally scored his 9th goal for Manchester United in the league. As early as January this year, Vidic had continuously scored in the league. After such a long period of no goals in the league, he finally ended with a strong finish at the end of the year. This goal may be a symbol, representing that the Serbian's position in Manchester United's backfield is undoubtedly the first, unmatched by anyone.

Including cup matches, Vidic's last goal was scored against Inter Milan in the round of 16 of the Champions League, when he powerfully headed the ball past Vieira's defense. Today, against Wolves, it was a similar action, a dominant header from the front post, despite an average angle but with immense power, leaving Hannemann powerless even though he touched the ball. Vidic finally succeeded in his fifth shot attempt this season. This goal once again confirmed to people: the absolute advantage Serbia has in aerial duels. In fact, before Rooney scored the penalty, it was because of his powerful impact that forced the opposing defender into a handball foul. So in today's match, Vidic created two goals. Ferguson praised Vidic after the game, stating that he was the most dangerous player on set pieces.

Due to small injuries throughout this season, Vidic has only played 16 games for Manchester United so far, missing 10 games. With other defenders also having "episodes" of injuries, Ferguson has been making large adjustments to the center-back line-up. After Rio missed a lot of games due to back injuries, if Vidic were to have problems, the blow to Manchester United's defensive confidence would be unimaginable. Therefore, as long as Vidic can play, the defensive confidence index will soar. In the game, we can see that because of Vidic's presence, the young Darron Gibson dared to make bold breakthroughs on the right wing, knowing that someone was covering the space behind him, and the other center-back Carrick also played effortlessly. Of course, this was not a very difficult game, and Vidic's command ability was displayed more fully. Remember, he was the captain of the starting lineup for this game, and Ferguson's trust in him was expressed through that small armband.

Recently, Vidic has reportedly had some awkwardness with the club, with his agent stating that the club should raise Vidic's salary to £100,000 per week. However, with the contract still having two and a half years left, the club would not act so early, and even with two years left by summer, the club might still not take action. Since the management insists on starting renewal talks only when the contract enters its final year, there is a risk of being poached by other big clubs. Although Vidic did not outwardly express any dissatisfaction, it was hard for him to control his freely acting agent. If a lucrative contract offer came from Barcelona, it would be hard for Vidic to resist. Pique, who became an absolute starter for Barcelona, was Vidic's first substitute until last summer. Barcelona would certainly be willing to continue pursuing the deal for their top defender.

But now, despite outside noise, Vidic indeed focuses more on the present. Recently, he stated that he only cared about Manchester United and not about the World Cup which was still over half a year away. He said, "I don't want to think too far ahead. Sometimes you only care about your injury, so the best way is to work hard for the next game and then keep going, that's all. There is still plenty of time before the World Cup, so currently I am fully focused on playing for Manchester United." Such statements are what any Manchester United supporter would like to hear.

Before the game against Wolves, Vidic urged his teammates to definitely secure three points. In an interview with the official website, he said, "This is our best opportunity to play beautifully, score a few goals, and bring the confidence back. When you lose a game, it is important to win the next one immediately. So you need to play better and make up for the mistakes made in the previous game." Clearly, he and his teammates achieved the goal. For someone who maintains strong aerial dominance at both ends of the penalty area, as long as he is healthy and fully committed without distractions, it was just a piece of cake for him, with the power of last season's Premier League MVP, to crush Wolves.

When Vidic arrived at Manchester United, his purchase price was £7 million, already quite high for a central defender. But after these few years of refinement, according to the price marked by Transfermarkt, Vidic's current value is around €35 million, or £31.15 million, more than four times higher in just four years. This data speaks volumes, and it can be imagined that Manchester United will eventually meet his salary increase demands, allowing him to enter the top income bracket in the Premier League. This is determined by Vidic's superior field strength and dominance.

(Selwyn Meng)
