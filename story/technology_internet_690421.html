The Micro-light Quantum Environmental Protection Teaching System is omnipresent, and the electronic whiteboard is gradually being replaced, step by step.

by anonymous on 2012-03-05 10:12:59

In the field of educational equipment, electronic whiteboards have always held a certain advantage. However, as the technology in the eco-friendly teaching aids industry matures, the dominant position of electronic whiteboards in the educational equipment field is gradually being challenged. Nevertheless, while teaching equipment is impacting the traditional teaching mode industry belt, it is also advancing towards the entire educational equipment domain. With its high-tech content and eco-friendly advantages of being dust-free, non-toxic, and odorless, the Micro-light Quantum Environmental Protection Teaching System has already taken root in various fields. The emergence of the Micro-light Quantum Environmental Protection Teaching System has freed people from the traditional concept of teaching modes being merely chalk and blackboards. This system already has the full ability to independently meet most of the needs of teaching. At the same time, with its more advanced technological content, the Micro-light Quantum Environmental Protection Teaching System creates an ultra-clean teaching environment that even electronic whiteboards can't match.

The Micro-light Quantum Environmental Protection Teaching System allows teachers to conduct multimedia courseware teaching directly on the board using a teaching pen, replacing the electronic whiteboard. It integrates the functions of green boards, projection screens, and electronic whiteboards into one, ushering classrooms into a new era of healthy, environmentally friendly, low-carbon, scientific, and clean teaching environments, truly entering a new age of dust-free teaching, bringing more benefits to both teachers and students. Yet, the Micro-light Quantum Environmental Protection Teaching System does not rest on its laurels. As a remarkable phenomenon in the education market in 2011, Wuzhishan garnered widespread attention. In 2012, it introduced the concept of green environmental protection into the office market; carrying the "Micro-light Quantum" and "Photo-sensitive Hydrolysis" technological concepts, it entered the office market. This trend of development seems endless. The Wuzhishan Enterprise Group supports the "Classroom Connectivity" system project proposed by the Ministry of Education, providing solutions for educational informatization. The Micro-light Quantum Environmental Protection Teaching System and other peripheral products are increasing production efforts in line with the policies of the Ministry of Education. This truly realizes "Classroom Connectivity," improving the teaching environment, updating teaching methods, impacting the traditional concepts of teachers, changing teaching methods, and injecting new vitality into school educational reforms.

Wuzhishan showcased a hand-touch type environmental protection teaching system, which is a national first, surpassing the pen-touch type environmental protection teaching system. This is another new type of teaching environmental protection system, an upgraded version of the Micro-light Quantum Environmental Protection Teaching System. It is compatible with all teaching functions of electronic whiteboards, requires no external receiver, uses a manual induction operating system, and allows all teaching operations to be performed directly through finger touches on the teaching board. It can control the computer via the teaching board, supports writing with normal pens, brushes, and flat brushes in the Windows interface, can erase annotations on the screen at any time through hand-touch physical buttons or circle and erase specific areas, and can clear the entire screen's content in one go. It also has the function of closing the current window, reducing operational steps, lowering operational difficulty, and facilitating classroom teaching. The hardware equipment provides a platform support for teaching, integrating multiple functions such as traditional blackboards, computers, and projectors into one, making it convenient and fast to use. It ushers in a more humanized, greener, dust-free teaching new era. Its powerful functions leave electronic whiteboards far behind, offering people the enjoyment of high-tech teaching techniques while effortlessly creating a green teaching environment.
