Financial Prosperity - How To Achieve It! Financial Prosperity - How To Achieve It! Copyright 2006 David Brown In this article, you will learn exactly what financial prosperity is and how to achieve it. First of all, what is financial prosperity? According to the Collins Student Dictionary, prosperity means success and wealth. It is vitally important to remember that success is a step-by-step process and needs to be connected to a long-term goal of 10 years or more. It also needs to be attached to core values. How many people have won the lottery only to return to their original state after five years? This is because a lottery win is an event, not a process. The dictionary defines the word "wealth" as the state of being rich. It's about being rich in terms of pursuing your life's passions, not just being in it for the money. For example, I know a person who has been working in a factory for 15 years. She works for the coins and notes. Due to her job, she has health problems and is very unhappy. On the other hand, I know people who pursue their passions daily, are happy, and attract an abundance of finance. These individuals are wealthy because they are rich in their lives. Therefore, financial prosperity means living your passions and attracting an abundance of finance to achieve your financial goals. It is a process, not an event. There are several other ways to achieve financial prosperity:

- Having good money habits and building accurate knowledge.

- Believing in yourself and having the correct mindset.

- Developing a millionaire mindset.

- Using the laws of attraction.

To be financially successful, it involves 80% behavior (positive habits) and 20% knowledge (from experts). This means that the right money habits are needed along with the right knowledge. If negative habits exist, these need to change, and a finance coach/mentor can assist. Accurate knowledge from the best can help you learn and build this. A person must truly believe in themselves to build prosperity. Any lack of confidence will lead to doubt, which needs to change. By using positive affirmations like "I am attracting financial prosperity," it will help to change the mindset. Learn from millionaires, buy books written by millionaires, attend courses. The mindset of a millionaire is different from someone who is broke. It has to be learned and changed. Use the law of attraction. This means visualizing attracting prosperity, focusing on the thought, and feeling good about it. For example, if you want to attract £1000.00 into your life, visualize someone giving you a check for that amount. Even write a check yourself and keep looking at it; your thoughts create energy which leads to attraction. Thank you for reading this article.
