Post-80s entrepreneurship, became a CEO in 5 years with assets exceeding tens of millions yuan

by gskfk5x6 on 2012-02-27 14:13:45

With more and more post-80s becoming successful, new entrepreneurs are beginning to jump into the market continuously. So what is the most profitable business to start? He is a post-80s CEO who has been stuck on a difficult problem for 5 years. He is an entrepreneur without a diploma. As a boss, he rides a bike around the ring road to find inspiration. How many people can actually do that? That's how a fortune worth millions is made. What is the most profitable business to start? Let's take a look at the business philosophy of this 28-year-old general manager.

The 28-year-old general manager realizes the free changes of the stage.

Yang Zhen was born in 1983, not tall, and when thinking, he would adjust his glasses. On the table in the office, there are "Capital" and "Wolf Management in Huawei", next to the notebook, there are his soft-pen written "Rain Bells" and "Farewell Again to Cambridge", and the bookshelf is full of professional books.

The company is located in the college student entrepreneurship park on the second section of the third ring road in Chengdu, and his home is nearby. Yang Zhen's life goes back and forth between these two points, rarely entering the inner ring, spending most of his time pondering technical issues and company operations.

[There are many devices in the theater, such as the stage needs to rise and fall, which requires a motor to drive it, and the motor needs to be operated by a computer. It seems simple, but large theaters have hundreds of devices, and motors also have hundreds. To connect them all for operation is not so simple.] Yang Zhen said that Yanxing Technology's main product is the stage mechanical control system. This system is specifically developed for theaters, clubs, and other places. Users only need to input operational information on the control panel to control the corresponding motor and complete the debugging of the stage equipment such as lifting, speed adjustment, and positioning.

[Using this system, whatever effect you want, the stage can present it.] Yang Zhen said. Nowadays, Jincheng Art Palace, Southwest Theater, Gansu Grand Theater, and others are using this system.

Based on the already mature stage mechanical control system, Yang Zhen's company began to extend into the industry, currently mainly focusing on lubrication systems and engineering machinery. He said, although the company has only been established for a few years, its annual output value has already entered the million-level.

Campus romance turns girl into wife

Once, while walking with a few classmates, Yang Zhen walked ahead and said: [Let's hurry up.] This sentence was heard by a female student from another school who came to find him, and she developed a liking for him just like a TV drama plot. This girl later became his wife.

In 2006, at the age of 23, Yang Zhen, a student at the University of Electronic Science and Technology, and his partners developed a system that not only solved the trouble of door-to-door meter reading for meter readers but also brought him the first pot of gold in his life.

After that, Yang Zhen and his classmates founded a company and formed a habit - if there were no projects, they would ride bicycles around the first ring road to look for inspiration.

In 2011, Yang Zhen, who was 28 years old, had not wandered around the first ring road for over a year. At this point, he was the boss of a company with 60 employees, and the stage control system he and his partners developed was successfully applied in many famous theaters such as Jincheng Art Palace, Southwest Theater, Gansu Grand Theater, etc.

Nowadays, he spends all day thinking about how to apply this core technology to more fields. Meanwhile, he still occasionally thinks about the math problem that has troubled him for many years and even caused him to suffer from neurasthenia.

Changing the long-standing meter reading habit in university

In 2006, near the University of Electronic Science and Technology, there was an office of more than 60 square meters, a few desks, a few computers, and a few people who liked to conduct research. This was the predecessor of Yanxing Technology.

In his sophomore year, Yang Zhen participated in the National College Student Electronic Design Competition with two classmates and won an award. Afterward, they formed a team named ADT, which stands for [Advanced Design Team], and these three letters later became the company's logo.

They rented a house near the school, called a few juniors and seniors, and started taking jobs. To buy equipment, they even contributed their tuition fees, which infuriated their teachers. However, the teachers admired their courage and paid their tuition fees for them. Talking about this, Yang Zhen was deeply moved. A teacher named Ding Qingsheng retired from the University of Electronic Science and Technology in the first half of this year and came to Yanxing Technology as the director of the R&D department, often communicating and providing guidance to them.

In the second half of 2006, a power meter reading company found them through the school's teaching and research office, hoping they could develop a wireless meter reading system. They earned 300,000 yuan after researching this project for half a year. Yang Zhen said that before this, meter readers had to go door-to-door to read the electricity meters. Their system changed this situation by installing a data collector on the electricity meter and a wireless signal relay device downstairs, transmitting the data collected by each electricity meter to the computer through the relay device.

In April 2007, holding the first large sum of money in their lives, the group didn't know how to distribute it. After deliberating, they decided to establish a company named [Yanxing Technology]. In the team, Yang Zhen was always the core figure and naturally became the general manager of the company.

When the company was just established, everyone didn't know what to do well. Sometimes they would ride bikes around the first ring road to look for inspiration. Not long after, through a friend they met while doing a previous project, the company finally received its first project - to create a set of stage mechanical operating systems for JiaYu Pass Grand Theater in Gansu.

This contract, worth more than 2 million yuan, excited them, but the progress was fraught with difficulties. The project required the use of controllers. When Yang Zhen went to purchase Siemens PLC controllers, the other party required full payment, amounting to more than 700,000 yuan. The company couldn't afford it, and they were frustrated for a long time. [Out of frustration, we decided to develop it ourselves.]

No money to buy equipment forced them to develop themselves

Two months later, the new controller was developed, but the results were not optimistic.

[Trying it out with the stage equipment in Gansu, hundreds of devices were moving chaotically, completely out of control. We had to dismantle it and redesign it.] Yang Zhen recalled with a smile. After failing two or three times, they finally succeeded in creating a usable controller.

It took a total of one year to complete the project successfully, and the customer was very satisfied. [But we lost money; we didn't make any profit.] Yang Zhen said that on the train returning from Gansu, he asked the partner in charge of finance how much money was left in the accounts. The partner replied: [Boss, we still have enough money for bus fare when we return, but not enough for taxis.]

However, precisely because of this [money-losing] project, the company welcomed a new situation. What is the most profitable business to start? Since this industry is relatively [narrow], the reputation of Yanxing Technology's stage operating system for JiaYu Pass Grand Theater being [very useful] spread quickly. Two months later, they cooperated with a stage mechanical factory in Gansu. Subsequently, through word-of-mouth, several engineering companies needing this system gradually approached them. The company slowly overcame its difficulties and got on track.

Yang Zhen and his team carefully considered that this project was relatively specialized, with few people working on it, fewer competitors, and thus relatively larger market potential. Therefore, they decided to make it the company's main project. Over the past three years, their business has covered Sichuan, Shaanxi, Guangdong, Chongqing, and other places. The scale of their projects has grown larger, and the number of employees has increased from a few to around 60 now.

What conclusion did the math problem he thought about for many years come to?

Yang Zhen has a regret - not obtaining a university degree certificate.

Actually, Yang Zhen's grades were not bad. Teacher Ding Qingsheng always praised him for being [smart] and [capable]. Starting from his sophomore year, he focused his energy on projects, neglecting exams, and by graduation, he hadn't accumulated enough credits, so he didn't receive a degree certificate.

However, he did harvest love in university. Once, while walking with a few classmates, Yang Zhen walked ahead and said: [Hurry up.] This sentence was heard by a female student from another school who came to find him, and she developed a liking for him just like a TV drama plot. This girl later became his wife.

Although the company's profits are already quite substantial and his family is very happy, Yang Zhen is still troubled by how to find better talent. And then there is that math problem that has troubled him for many years - what is the conclusion?

Talking about this math problem, Yang Zhen's voice suddenly rose significantly. He said that during his studies, he kept pondering it but couldn't solve it, which caused him to suffer from neurasthenia and take medication every day. Even now, he still continues to explore this problem from time to time. He even hopes to return to campus one day, obtain a degree certificate, and study mathematics to research this problem.

The question is: since the positive and negative infinite points of electric field lines are the same point, can the XY axes of the Cartesian coordinate system be bent into a circle in both the X and Y directions like electric field lines? Then, under the condition where the plane becomes infinitely large, it bends into a curved surface. Establishing many analytical geometry methods tending towards infinity in such a bent plane coordinate system, can Riemann geometric space algebraic methods also be established? Can it also analyze whether the sum of the interior angles of a triangle is greater than or less than 180 degrees? Can it also explain that two parallel lines intersect at the same point at infinity?
