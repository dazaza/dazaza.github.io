Learn home decoration and layout skills

by longersing on 2009-08-30 15:03:48

Many people think that home decoration is a very easy thing, but often in the process of actual implementation, they realize that it is also a science. Today, let's learn together how to arrange home decoration, which is indeed a science.

1. Home decoration: symmetrical balance and reasonable placement

To combine some home decorations together so that they become part of a visual focus, the sense of symmetrical balance is very important. When there are large pieces of furniture nearby, the order of arrangement should be from tall to short to avoid visual disharmony. Alternatively, you can maintain the same center of gravity for two home decorations. For instance, placing two identical style lamps side by side or arranging two cushions with the same color and pattern next to each other. This not only creates a harmonious rhythm but also gives a peaceful and warm feeling.

Additionally, when placing home decorations, arranging them in layers with smaller items in front and larger ones at the back highlights the characteristics of each decoration, making it visually comfortable.

2. Home decoration should match the overall home style

First, find the general style and color tone, and follow this unified theme for decoration to avoid mistakes easily. For example, if your home design is minimalistic, then home decorations with a design sense will fit well with the space’s personality; if it's a natural country style, then focus on natural-style home decorations.

3. Not all home decorations need to be displayed

Generally, people tend to want to display everything when decorating. However, displaying too many items loses the uniqueness. At this point, you can first categorize your decorative materials, grouping similar attributes together without rushing to show everything. After categorizing, you can change the setup according to seasons or festivals, altering the mood of your home accordingly.

4. Start with small home decorations

Decorative items, cushions, tablecloths, small ornaments, and other medium and small-sized home decorations are the easiest to handle when starting with home decoration. Beginners can start with these before gradually moving on to larger furniture arrangements. Small home decorations often become visual focal points, better reflecting the owner's interests and hobbies.

5. Home fabric decoration is key

Each season has different colors and patterns for home fabrics. Whether it's colorful printed cloth, luxurious silk, or romantic lace, simply changing the style of home fabrics can transform the home atmosphere, more economically and easily than replacing furniture.

The color scheme of home fabric decorations should be unified to make the combination more harmonious, enhancing the overall feel of the room. Hard lines and cold tones in the home can be softened with fabrics. In spring, choose fresh floral patterns to bring a vibrant spring atmosphere; in summer, opt for refreshing fruit or flower patterns; in autumn and winter, switch to fluffy cushions for warmth during the cold months.

6. Flowers and green plants bring vitality

To bring the essence of nature into your home, flowers and plants are the best choice for home decoration. Office renovations cannot do without plants, especially when changing seasonal arrangements, where flowers play a crucial role. Different seasons have different flowers, creating distinct seasonal atmospheres.
