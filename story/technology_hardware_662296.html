China Railway Director Xue Shiwu - The first year in Xiamen, committed to excellence in quality _1278 China Railway Yuanwan

by xmnqhqzi on 2012-02-10 09:17:46

2011 was the first year that China Railway Property Group entered Xiamen. In this year, China Railway Property's first project in Haixi, China Railway Yuanwan [Details Consultation], officially launched with a "high starting point" standard, frequent actions, and became a highlight in the real estate market. China Railway Yuanwan is adjacent to the Wuyuan Bay area, which is the only piece of land in Xiamen Island that integrates various natural resources such as wetlands, bays, etc., and has been favored by many homebuyers.

Fangmi Network joined forces with Fujian Economic Channel to jointly interview Mr. Xue Shiwu, Director/General Manager of Xiamen China Railway Yuanchang Property Co., Ltd., to see how China Railway Property reviews 2011, feels about the past, and looks forward to the future? Xiamen China Railway Yuanchang Property Co., Ltd. Director/General Manager Xue Shiwu Residing in Xiamen for the First Year Shaping the Five Core Values of China Railway Yuanwan

As the first construction investment project of China Railway Property in Xiamen, China Railway Property adheres to high standards and requirements for creating China Railway Yuanwan. Mr. Xue said that in 2011, Xiamen China Railway Property focused on shaping the quality of China Railway Yuanwan and created five core values of the project.

First, the construction quality of China Railway Yuanwan is guaranteed, and the construction speed is also fast.

Second, China Railway Property has a central enterprise background, ensuring quality. Mr. Xue said: "China Railway Property has a central enterprise background and is also a flagship enterprise in China's real estate enterprises. Our company believes in the brand concept of 'Trust stems from responsibility,' with responsibility being paramount."

Third, the location of the China Railway Yuanwan project itself is superior and incomparable. In the one year since entering Xiamen, Mr. Xue deeply felt that Wuyuan Bay is becoming fertile ground. "For example, when we just came, it was difficult to take a taxi in Wuyuan Bay, but now there are no problems even at midnight. Initially, Wuyuan Bay was sparsely populated, but now it is very lively."

Fourth, the garden hall architectural style of China Railway Yuanwan. Mr. Xue frankly stated that China Railway Property determined the design unit after confirming the architectural style. "The design unit proposed this creative idea, and after our approval, we chose him."

Fifth, the layout of China Railway Yuanwan. "The layout feature of China Railway Yuanwan is the cross-shaped garden hall. This is the most core highlight of the project. The layout is generous and well-organized, with a larger entrance garden." Mr. Xue confidently stated.

Trust stems from responsibility "National Artisan Spirit" pursuing perfection

China Railway Property's status in Xiamen's construction industry is undeniable. From social infrastructure construction to the real estate industry, China Railway Property Group always remembers China Railway's commitment to the country and people, inherits China Railway's traditional construction technology advantages, adheres to the brand construction philosophy of creating excellence and winning by quality, and has contributed a large number of landmark projects to more than 20 major cities across the country, continuing the spirit of national artisan craftsmanship. With strong support from the headquarters, Xiamen China Railway Property uses the 91 Luban Awards as its backbone and creates the China Railway Yuanwan project with the high requirements of national artisan craftsmanship.

"During the process of evaluating civilized cities in Xiamen, the China Railway Yuanwan project was rated as a model base for on-site civilized construction. However, within the group, we can still firmly point out our shortcomings. Because our group has higher and stricter requirements and will not stop pointing out existing shortcomings just because we have received some honors. We need to provide the best products to the owners, matching our promoted 'National Artisan Spirit.'"

Pursuing perfection means innovation. Mr. Xue said that a special feature of the China Railway Yuanwan project is the use of seawater heat pump technology. It is unique in communities in Xiamen and even nationwide. After using seawater heat pump technology, the community provides hot water around the clock and can save more than 37% of electricity consumption. Currently, this technology has been approved by the government, engineering investigations, and site selections. The government has already approved it, and it will be officially launched after the New Year.

Comprehensive planning Pursuing organic integration of architecture and nature

"In the core concept of architectural Feng Shui, the key idea is that buildings should organically integrate with the natural environment. We have always believed in this and have made very detailed and thoughtful considerations in planning," Mr. Xue said. Since the China Railway Yuanwan project is not short of water, it is full of water scenery everywhere. Therefore, the central courtyard landscape of China Railway Yuanwan designed a golf course instead of water scenery.

Considering that the traffic volume on the ring road near the project is relatively large, China Railway Property designed an isolation belt around the China Railway Yuanwan project to reduce noise and dust. Additionally, the isolation belt can increase privacy. In fact, the China Railway Yuanwan project itself is a community with good privacy. On the side adjacent to other projects, the project designed some enclosures for isolation to maintain the project's purity, nobility, and privacy. "As a central enterprise, China Railway Property adheres to the concepts of environmental protection and greenness and will continue to pursue a low-carbon direction in the future." Mr. Xue said that China Railway Yuanwan will adopt seawater heat pump technology to ensure hot water availability throughout the neighborhood, which is rare in southern region neighborhoods.

"The trend of 'luxury housing palace' is about both quality and taste"

"Chinese luxury housing, especially those passed down through generations, all have square layouts. We want to align with this trend." In terms of architectural style, the garden hall design of China Railway Yuanwan pursues symmetry, reasonable layout, and typical architecture like the White House in the United States.

"Now, the 'luxury housing palace' is also a trend. Five years ago, there was almost no steel-hung stone in residential buildings. Recently, steel-hung stones have become exclusive to palace architecture. Now they are used extensively in the residential field. Some elements of palaces are well reflected in our project." Mr. Xue said, "We don't just pursue quality; we also pursue taste."

To enhance the project's sense of nobility, China Railway Yuanwan has put a lot of effort into landscaping, exterior facades, architectural styles, and layouts. In terms of layout, there are larger building spacings. We have created a garden landscape similar to a golf course in the central area. These are all quite distinctive features.

Luxury housing aggregation effect highlighted Optimistic about the prospects of Wuyuan Bay

Relying on Wuyuan Bay, the only piece of land in Xiamen Island that integrates water scenery, hot springs, vegetation, wetlands, bays, and other natural resources, China Railway Yuanwan has naturally developed into luxury housing.

"Now, the concept of Wuyuan Bay as a luxury housing area in Xiamen has been widely recognized by the public. The area is filled with luxury housing,配套设施 are built to international standards, and they are increasingly improving. The aggregation effect of the luxury housing area is becoming evident." Mr. Xue said that through more than a year of residing in Xiamen, China Railway Property has witnessed the transformation of Wuyuan Bay from barren land to fertile ground, much like witnessing certain areas of Shenzhen transform from wasteland to fertile ground. Mr. Xue is optimistic about the prospects of Wuyuan Bay and believes that "Wuyuan Bay should have a large market."
