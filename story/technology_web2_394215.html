Online Promotion

Viral Marketing with Images

This is a rather creative way of advertising that is sure to bring a lot of traffic to an online store in a short period. The initial impression of such images was the signature graphics often seen on forums: a small cartoon character holding a sign showing your IP address, operating system, and browser name. The content displayed in this section can be changed. At that time, it just seemed interesting, and no one thought it could be used for other purposes. Later, I saw New Year's gold medals widely circulated online, where you could write down the name of the person you wanted to send it to, and it would appear on the medal in the picture. I thought it was done by a master using Photoshop, but later found out that you just needed to input text.

Then there were banners on the Eiffel Tower, all of the same type. By the way, the most commonly used method in this category is adding a logo to uploaded pictures. Some of the earliest websites to use these methods gained a large number of clicks. Some websites I learned about from these pictures, like HAHA168 and PCPOP. But now many forums and websites are using this method, and its effectiveness seems less obvious. Just a side note.

Forum Promotion

The forum promotion mentioned here absolutely does not mean posting ads one by one on different sections of the forum, nor does it mean adding the online store address to your signature and then spamming wildly. That takes energy and doesn't work well either. Forum administrators only need to click a few times to delete all your posts, block your ID, and such behavior brings negative effects, like sending junk emails which people find annoying. I once saw a clever way of promoting a website on a forum.

It was a small website providing job hunting, employment, and learning materials. Their promotional staff turned various articles on the website into link forms, categorized them, and posted them on different forums. This way, it could provide convenience to those who need it, while at the same time avoiding being deleted due to too straightforward advertisements. The promotional effect was undoubtedly much better. If these materials were posted on forums corresponding to their positioning, they would surely attract a lot of clicks.

Search Engine Promotion Methods

Search engine promotion refers to the method of promoting online stores using search engines, classified directories, and other network tools with online information retrieval functions. Since the basic form of search engines can be divided into web spider-type search engines (simply called search engines) and human-based classified directory search engines (simply called classified directories), the forms of search engine promotion also correspondingly include methods based on search engines and methods based on classified directories. The former includes search engine optimization, keyword advertising, bidding ranking, fixed ranking, content-based positioning advertisements, and more forms, while the latter mainly involves website registration in appropriate categories within classified directories. With further development changes in the form of search engines, some other forms of search engines have also appeared, though most are based on these two forms.

Search engine promotion methods can also be divided into various different forms, common ones including free classified directory registration, paid classified directory registration, search engine optimization, keyword advertising, keyword bidding ranking, webpage content positioning advertisements, etc.

From the current development trend, the status of search engines in online marketing remains important, and more and more enterprises recognize it. The ways of search engine marketing continue to evolve, so suitable methods should be chosen according to environmental changes.

Email Promotion Method

Using email as the main promotional tool, common methods include electronic newsletters, member communications, professional service provider email advertisements, etc.

Permission-based Email marketing differs from spam. Permission marketing has obvious advantages over traditional promotional methods or unsolicited Email marketing, such as reducing ad annoyance to users, increasing the accuracy of potential customer targeting, enhancing customer relationships, improving brand loyalty, etc. You can let your customers leave their email addresses, and after obtaining their consent, send them product information, discount information, etc.

Resource Cooperation Promotion Method

Through methods such as exchanging links, exchanging advertisements, content cooperation, user resource cooperation, etc., achieve mutual promotion between online stores with similar goals. The most commonly used resource cooperation method is the online store link strategy, which uses the access volume resources of partners to promote each other.

Each online store can have its own resources, which can be expressed as a certain amount of traffic, valuable content and functions, online advertising space, etc. Using website resources to cooperate with partners, achieve resource sharing, and jointly expand profits. Among these resource cooperation forms, exchanging links is the simplest form of cooperation. Surveys show that it is also an effective way to promote new websites.

Link exchange or reciprocal linking is a simple cooperation form between online stores with complementary advantages, i.e., placing the LOGO or online store name of the other party on their own online store and setting a hyperlink to the other party's online store, allowing users to discover their own online store from the cooperative online store, achieving the purpose of mutual promotion.

The role of link exchanges mainly manifests in several aspects: gaining traffic, increasing user impressions when browsing, gaining advantages in search engine rankings, increasing the credibility of visitors through recommendations from cooperative online stores, etc. Link exchanges also have a deeper meaning beyond whether direct results can be achieved. Generally speaking, each online store tends to link to other online stores with high link value, so gaining links from other online stores means gaining recognition from partners and similar online stores in the field.

Information Release Promotion Method

Releasing relevant online store promotion information on other websites that potential users may visit, utilizing the opportunity for users to obtain information on these websites to achieve the purpose of website promotion. Websites suitable for information release include online yellow pages, classified ads, forums, blog sites, supply and demand information platforms, industry websites, etc.

Information release is one of the commonly used methods of free promotion, especially in the early days of Internet development when the amount of online information was relatively small. Often, satisfactory results could be achieved through information release. However, with the explosive growth of online information, the role played by this method of relying on free information release is decreasing day by day. At the same time, due to the emergence of more effective online store promotion methods, the importance of information release in commonly used online store promotion methods has also significantly decreased. Therefore, the method of sending a large amount of free information has little value. However, some targeted and specialized information can still attract great attention, especially when these pieces of information are released in places with higher relevance.

Online Advertising Promotion Method

Online advertising is one of the commonly used online marketing strategies, playing a significant role in online brand promotion, product promotion, and website promotion. Common forms of online advertising include: Banner ads, keyword ads, classified ads, sponsored ads, Email ads, etc. Banner ads rely on web pages, keyword ads belong to a form of search engine marketing, and Email ads are a form of permission-based Email marketing. It is visible that online advertising itself cannot exist independently and needs to combine with various online tools to realize the function of information transmission. Therefore, it can also be considered that online advertising exists in various online marketing tools, just in different specific forms.

Using online advertising for online store promotion has advantages such as a wide range of selectable online media, diverse forms, strong applicability, and timely placement. It is suitable for any stage of the online store's launch and operation period.

Friendship Links

Strive to establish links with popular online stores, thus increasing visit rates and search engine weight.

Regularly offer special price items, similar to daily specials in restaurants.

For example, from Monday to Wednesday, sell an item originally priced at 150 yuan for 88 yuan, limited to the first 20 buyers (whether this quota is reached or not is up to you, it's just a gimmick to stimulate purchasing desire).

Gunmen, soft articles, stir up posts on major forums and websites.

Soft articles are soft advertisements, attracting attention.

Flash Promotion

For example, when a new MP3 or sketch comes out, make it into a flash animation, add a small advertisement at the bottom because well-made flashes spread widely now. Of course, you can also contact existing flash authors to place advertisements and pay a small fee.

Link Promotion

Regardless of the method (QQ, MSN, forum, MAIL, etc.), publicize your online store's link, or hire someone to help promote it. As long as someone clicks on this link and visits your online store, give them a certain amount of commission or store coupons, and offer corresponding discounts to those referred to your store.

Keyword Optimization

When people buy things, they usually start by searching for related products. When setting product names, design commonly used keywords to allow buyers to search for your store and products multiple times, or use terms like lowest price and special offers, describing goods as detailed as possible.

Data Promotion

If your online store sells certain products, you can write product data and small knowledge-related content. In the article, you can attach your online store's address, or set it in the signature for promotion.

In-store Activity Promotion

Analyze the buyers of your online store

Based on past buyers, analyze the characteristics of these customers, their age, occupation, interests, etc. You can conduct corresponding promotions in forums and places they often visit, promoting according to the different features of buyers. At the same time, actively seek business opportunities and target purchases accordingly. Success or failure is secondary; the key is to have an active awareness.

Additionally, always pay attention to "buyer purchase requests" information, perhaps there are buyers who need something from your store. Even if our store doesn't have what the buyer is looking for, you can discover many potential customers. Add them as friends and casually promote items similar to what they're seeking; it's easy to close a deal.

Promotion and Publicity

Make good use of every holiday for promotion and publicity. During holidays, there are a large number of searches related to holidays. Therefore, everyone can focus on keywords and take advantage of holidays to launch promotional activities, which will bring more customers. Also, during each shipment, include some personal business cards or promotional materials with the order.

Making Promotional Gifts

Print your online store's logo and URL on T-shirts and give them away as gifts to your online customers, which is a good way to promote your store. Remember, the T-shirt must be of medium or better quality; otherwise, it might become a rag after one wear, which wouldn't be worth it! Now that the weather is getting cooler, consider making hats, cups, or other promotional gifts.

Joint Promotion

Plain friendship links have limited effects. However, if several sellers collaborate and do joint promotions, it can yield different results. Try to find complementary stores. For instance, a photography book specialty store collaborating with a digital camera specialty store.

Earning Coins for Ad Placement

Not only are there too many people competing, but it also requires a lot of coins. They can only earn coins by participating in activities. On the other hand, working hard to get community ads is worth it! Reasonable Use of Auctions

Auctions are divided into single-item auctions and multi-item auctions (Dutch auctions). Taobao has a dedicated section on its homepage for auctions, which buyers can see as soon as they open the homepage. Choosing the auction method means buyers can easily find us. Moreover, auction prices are generally low, making them attractive to buyers, which helps increase the popularity of the store.

Have you noticed that Taobao actually takes care of sellers in many aspects and launches many promotional activities? Some shop owners occasionally put out one or two items for one-yuan auctions. This should be done within your capacity, and once you do a one-yuan auction, be prepared to lose money. After the buyer successfully bids and completes the transaction, ensure the quality of the item sent to them, even if it costs money. A one-yuan auction is meant to gain good reviews and popularity, so avoid receiving bad reviews, which would be counterproductive.

Using Reviews to Promote the Store

Sometimes we see very simple reviews made by sellers, either saying "Good buyer" or leaving it blank altogether. Actually, we can fully utilize this opportunity to speak. Many buyers check the seller's credit before purchasing, so this is a chance to advertise. My current reviews say: "Great buyer, hope to see you again at Youyou Toy Shop, old customers will receive discounts." Or: "New stock will be added regularly, come take a look when you have time."

Participating in Activities

Many C2C websites periodically have promotional events. Discover these related activities frequently, stay updated with the latest dynamics, and strive to utilize these activities well, such as essay contests, charity sales. You can sponsor prizes or donate to let more people know about you.

Offline Promotion

The offline promotion methods introduced in this article all require some cost. Online store owners should act within their capabilities.

Gift Advertising

The following gifts are distributed for free, in bustling areas with high foot traffic. Eco-bags, because they are used frequently now, with low production costs, print online store information on the bags. Calendars, print online store information on them. Mouse pads, print online store information on them and distribute them for free, in internet cafes, office buildings, etc.

Campus Agents

Nowadays, many college students have spare time, no need for a base salary, just give them commissions, let them promote, unrestricted by online or offline means, give them free business cards (with titles like campus general agent of the online store), a set of materials, let them promote and sell, earning money while effectively training their marketing skills, spreading word-of-mouth among student councils.

Street Questionnaire Surveys

Hire part-timers, surveys are secondary, mainly for promotion.

Before the promotional phase, conduct an online survey

Targeted surveys, such as piloting on Xici, surveying concerns about popular commodities and usage issues, safety issues. Answering questions can subtly promote your online store.

Roadside Lightbox Ads

Mainly bus stop lightboxes, bus ads, bus body ads, internal banner ads, etc.

Hire a few students to dress up as popular cartoon characters like Superman, Ultraman, Pleasant Goat, Big Big Wolf, etc. in busy downtown areas.

Of course, they should have online store ads on them, tourists may want to take photos with them, the promotional effect should be good. The key is that the media is always looking for news, they love this kind of thing, once it's on the newspaper, the traffic will come.

Internet Cafe Collaboration

Collaborate with internet cafes, pay a small fee, set the default homepage of the browser to the homepage of the online store. Find thirty to fifty internet cafes, with more people going to internet cafes, the traffic will also increase.

Place a small stand-up sign at the front desk or cashier of restaurants, hotels, and leisure venues

This small stand-up sign should have a brief introduction of the online store and the URL, and guests may glance at it while consulting or paying the bill.

Purified Water Ads

The customer base for purified water is extensive, including office buildings, hospitals, schools, government offices, families, etc. It's not hard to glance at an ad while pouring water, right? We can collaborate with producers, stick ads on the barrels, and pay a small fee.

Printing Business Cards for Wide Distribution

You might think that since most of the promotion is done online and people never meet, isn't making business cards a waste of cost? Wrong! Although the store is online, you are still you in real life, and sending items to buyers happens in reality. Design a unique business card, print your contact information and the store's address, and include a few in the mail. Because of its beautiful design, buyers are unlikely to throw it away, and if there's an opportunity, they will definitely show it to others, which is equivalent to helping you promote.

Distributing Business Cards or Flyers

After printing business cards, they should be distributed. Printing flyers is also cheap now, costing only a few cents per sheet. Print them and distribute them in places where potential customers gather, such as near schools, etc. Different products face different customers, so when using this strategy, the right place must be chosen.

Tell Your Family and Friends

Do your family know that you have opened a store online? Do your classmates and friends know? Be sure to tell them immediately. Leave messages on alumni records and share the joy of opening a store with your classmates.

Organize Relatives and Friends to Help Promote

Since relatives and friends already know you have an online store, ask them to help promote it.

You've known a lot of friends since growing up, haven't you? From elementary school to junior high school, Guangzhou VIP Information Technology Co., Ltd., and then to high school and university, you've had many classmates, haven't you? So mobilize them, ask them to help us promote...
