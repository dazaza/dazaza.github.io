When he found an opportunity to assassinate Hong Zhong, he was mistakenly arrested by the police chief Holmes as a reformist rebel and thrown into the Tian prison.

Outside a casino, Lu Er was mistaken for a troublemaker by Hong Zhong and dragged inside. Police Chief Holmes arrived to capture the escaped prisoners but got into a misunderstanding with Hong Zhong and was beaten up by Hong Zhong and Dongxi Nanbei Feng. Forced to flee, Hong Zhong and his gang ended up at a black inn run by their old nemesis Xu Sandao. Unexpectedly, Hong Zhong's subordinate Dongfeng discovered that Lu Er and the Grand Secretary were also staying at the inn, and that Lu Er's tattoo concealed a monumental secret about a treasure. Meanwhile, Xu Sandao and the innkeeper were secretly plotting a crazy plan...

At a Tianjin brothel, the Singaporean pirate Yangtze River No.7 was waiting for Lu Er to escort the Grand Secretary overseas. Unbeknownst to them, Holmes and the assassins had already received the news and laid an ambush there. Tracing them, Hong Zhong and his gang also arrived. Upon arriving hastily, Lu Er and the Grand Secretary unexpectedly spotted Yuan Shikai enjoying wine and women. Enraged, the Grand Secretary insisted on personally killing Yuan Shikai to avenge the reformists.
