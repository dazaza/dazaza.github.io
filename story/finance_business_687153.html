New Year's Message:

Fruitful years are more stacked. Gently turn the page, we bid farewell to a memorable 2005 and move forward with hope toward 2006. I would like to take this opportunity, on behalf of the school party and the President’s Office, to extend my sincere respect and New Year congratulations to the dedicated, hardworking faculty and staff; to our vibrant students, I express good wishes and New Year’s greetings. As we recall the golden years of 2005, we take pride in our sons and daughters: in the first half of the year, on the SARS front, the National People united as one, achieved the victory against SARS; in the second half, the land of China celebrated wedding after wedding: the victory of the Fifth Plenary Session of the Sixteenth Party provided institutional guarantees for building a moderately prosperous society; at the ninth World Cup Volleyball Tournament, the Chinese Women’s Volleyball Team won 11 matches in a row, triumphing after a 17-year wait; on January 15, Moncler Jacken Zürich, Shenzhou V was successfully launched, people cheered, attracting worldwide attention. This symbolizes overall national strength and is the pride of the descendants.

Looking back at the time of 2003, the school staff and students are full of pride: we have won the title of Jiangsu Province Modernization of Education Demonstration Junior High School and Nantong City Model School, fully affirmed, which is a powerful spur to our future work.

As the era's wheels are about to enter 2006, this new year is just as fascinating as 2004. As educators, we must fully understand the special role of educational innovation in cultivating a number of highly qualified personnel with an innovative spirit and practical ability. Educational innovation, along with theoretical innovation, institutional innovation, and technological innovation, is equally important. However, it also provides higher requirements for all aspects of innovation intelligence data ownership, secretarial resources network strictly prohibits plagiarism support, which applies to all of us teachers. In the fall of 2004, the opening of the curriculum of new teaching materials began. The new round of the curriculum presents both challenges and opportunities for our teachers. I hope the teachers will seriously study the new curriculum, quickly establish a new concept, and grow alongside the new curriculum and curriculum reform.

Students, the New Year not only updates the years but also updates our concepts. Innovation changes the learning requirements for our students: independent learning, active inquiry, and learning to cooperate. We ask our students to develop the habit of observing problems, being thoughtful, and diligent in their thinking. We ask our students to dare to question whimsically... In 2006, a year full of challenges, opportunities, and hope, our students will surely make new achievements, and our school will make new developments. June 2006 is our harvest season, when the third-year students will accept the test in exams. I wish you to write a brilliant chapter in life with wisdom and sweat!

A kind of happiness is called devotion, and this happiness is real happiness! Our teachers already possess this happiness, which is the glory of Yuelong Secondary School, which is the pride of Hao River Middle School! A pleasure called enduring hardship, this happiness is true happiness, willing students to experience this happiness every day! Let the agony and the sweat teach each page of the book, let our sleepless nights learn each subject for Yuelong Secondary School to take off, for the prosperity of Hao River Middle School, teachers and students make greater efforts to a new glory!
