Wang Quan'an and Zhang Yuqi passionately kissing on the street were secretly photographed by paparazzi, and the young wife set the old house on fire.

by mlwang on 2011-11-24 13:59:19

More than a year has passed since Wang Quan'an, who poured his heart and soul into directing the film "The White Deer Plain", completed the filming. However, Wang Quan'an is still unable to finish the post-production work, and the premiere seems far away. Nevertheless, Wang Quan'an and Zhang Yuqi, whose relationship was developed through this film, are not in a rush at all. They are fully enjoying their sweet married life, as if they have fallen into a pot of honey and cannot get out.

On a recent evening, a reporter spotted Wang Quan'an's Land Rover outside an upscale Western restaurant on Jiangtai Road. Inside the restaurant, Wang Quan'an and Zhang Yuqi were seen enjoying a warm candlelit dinner.

After more than an hour, Wang Quan'an and Zhang Yuqi walked out of the restaurant together. The reporter noticed that Zhang Yuqi was makeup-free that day, wearing a luxurious fur coat. There were rumors recently about Zhang Yuqi being pregnant, and her figure indeed appeared quite full and relaxed that day. Wang Quan'an and Zhang Yuqi strolled hand in hand along the street, whispering to each other. Zhang Yuqi occasionally let out sweet laughter. Behind a car, Zhang Yuqi took the initiative to embrace Wang Quan'an's neck, and the two passionately kissed. After more than half a year of marriage, this couple still maintains such passion, which is rare. Especially Wang Quan'an, who originally gave people the impression of being steady, now acts like a house on fire under the provocation of his beloved wife.

Wang Quan'an and Zhang Yuqi drove to the entrance of the apartment building where they lived. Still not satisfied, they went shopping at the supermarket, returning to their usual demeanor as "cultural workers."

According to informed sources, due to the excessive length of the initial cut of "The White Deer Plain," Wang Quan'an is currently editing it down. In order to highlight his beloved wife Zhang Yuqi's performance as Tian Xiaoe, Wang Quan'an deleted a large section of the storyline. Some industry insiders jokingly remarked that "The White Deer Plain" is almost becoming "The Story of Tian Xiaoe."
