Hertz reminds you of the precautions to take before renting a car.

by juyaqufa003 on 2012-02-06 16:07:12

As a global leading car rental company, Hertz Rent A Car provides car rental services in 146 countries around the world, with more than 8,500 rental outlets, and receives over 40 million bookings annually. North America, Europe, Australasia, and major countries in Asia are the largest and most mature car rental markets, while China is increasingly becoming one of the fastest-growing emerging car rental markets. Hertz has long-term cooperation projects with more than 60 foreign airlines, hotels, and banks, continuously offering consumers benefits and surprises through activities such as mileage redemption points for renting cars and seasonal discount promotions.

Hertz reminds you that before renting a car, you can log into the car rental website to understand the price information of different vehicle categories, check if there are any special promotional activities, find out about the distribution of rental stores at your destination, and whether there are convenient pick-up locations for you, be it at the airport or hotel.

**Regarding Booking and Payment:**

Booking and paying online is a very convenient method. You can visit various car rental websites to learn about the pricing information of different vehicle groups, check if there are any special promotional activities, look up the distribution of rental stores at your destination, and see if there are sufficiently convenient pick-up locations for you, regardless of whether it’s at the airport or hotel. Afterward, you can select a car rental company and proceed with booking according to the system's prompts. Regarding payment methods, you can choose based on your own situation. For instance, prepayment services offered by companies like Avis and Dollar allow you to prepay and then take the prepaid voucher to pick up the car at an overseas location. This not only avoids the hassle of currency exchange locally but also reduces the risk of carrying large amounts of cash. Additionally, when making a prepayment domestically, you can freely choose the payment currency and convert it based on the daily exchange rate. Prices for advance reservations often come with attractive discounts. After completing your reservation on Hertz's booking system, a confirmation number will be generated. You can pay upon arrival at the rental location at your destination. This payment method is more flexible, avoiding risks such as having to pay a certain amount of penalty due to unforeseen circumstances preventing you from fulfilling the reservation, or needing to pay a service fee for modifying the order halfway through. Of course, you can also opt for their soon-to-be-launched domestic prepayment service for overseas rentals. You can make a prepayment using a credit card domestically, and when picking up the car abroad, simply show the confirmation voucher provided by Hertz China. This saves the limited credit card limit for other expenses and avoids potential language barriers encountered abroad.

**Vehicle Selection:**

Choosing the right vehicle is crucial, as it directly affects the enjoyment of your self-driving trip. Wei Dongmei, regional market manager of Hertz, suggests: "You can choose the most suitable model based on your budget, itinerary, and number of people." If it's just two people traveling, you can rent a compact sedan or mid-sized SUV, which is economical and practical; if it's four people, it's recommended to rent a standard-sized SUV for better comfort and visibility during long-distance travel; if you want to experience luxury models, you can try Lincoln Navigator or Cadillac Escalade; on sunny days and warm seasons, you can give convertible sports cars a try; for business trips with multiple people, seven-seater minivans or five-seater wagons with plenty of luggage space are excellent choices. In terms of vehicle selection, Dollar collaborates with America's best RV rental company, Dream Adventures RV, to offer RV rental plans, allowing you to drive this dream house and travel everywhere. Of course, besides your dearest family members, you can also bring your beloved pets along, who will certainly not object—just remember to pay a small additional fee!

**How to Pick Up the Car:**

Typically, you can pick up the car directly at major airports worldwide. These international car rental companies have stores at airports providing the most convenient and quick services. Their prominent logos ensure you won't miss them. At the counter, you just need to provide your passport, Hertz rental agreement, original Chinese driver's license and notarized English translation, name or order number. Both parties sign the rental documents, and then use your credit card for authorization to get the car keys. Alternatively, you can go to a store near your hotel to pick up the car, or some stores may offer delivery services within a certain range during working hours—you just need to inform them of your specific time and location. Whether there is a charge depends on the company and location, so further inquiries are recommended. However, I strongly suggest going directly to the store to pick up the car. Within the same vehicle group, you can choose a car that suits your preferences based on details such as color and newness, which cannot be confirmed during the booking process.
