Notice of the People's Government of Hebei Province on Issuing the "Twelfth Five-Year" Development Plan for the New Energy Industry in Hebei Province (2011-2015)

Jizheng Letter [2010] No. 68 June 2, 2010

To the people's governments of all prefecture-level cities, the people's governments of all counties (cities, districts), and all departments of the provincial government:

The "Twelfth Five-Year" Development Plan for the New Energy Industry in Hebei Province (2011-2015) has been reviewed and approved at the 59th executive meeting of the provincial government on June 1, 2010. It is now issued to you. Please carefully organize and implement it in combination with the actual conditions of your localities and departments.
