Love came suddenly

Xiaojie (a pseudonym) is my roommate and also my best brother. He is a thorough handsome guy. Regarding his stories, I don't know how to comment. For us college students, when we first enter the university, more or less, we can't stand loneliness. I am a tragedy. My abstract appearance denies everything about my love. But he is different. He is a handsome young man and very popular with girls.

When he first entered the university, he had a crush on a fairly pretty girl in the class. To be honest, at the beginning, I thought that girl was quite beautiful. Of course, she is still the same now. It's just that I'm a bit tired of looking at her. At that time, he somewhat shyly started his "love battle". At that time, it seemed that things were going well, since the two were always sticking together. But not long after, the two broke up because the girl didn't look up to him. It was the first time he had experienced a breakup.

We didn't know how to comfort him without experience, so we could only say some bad things about that girl, like: "Leaving a big tree, you will own the whole forest", "Don't hang on one tree, try a few more." Then things gradually faded away. Just at that time, a very pretty girl from Class 5 confessed her love to him when he was heartbroken.

He was very conflicted at the time. He didn't want to harm the girl because of his heartbreak, so he refused her several times. He often asked me whether he should accept her. At that time, I could only say that if he liked her, he should accept her. In the end, he was still stuck in the prison of the girl in the class and rejected the girl from Class 5. That was the end of the matter.

In June last year, he finally bravely walked out of the prison of heartbreak. He fell for a girl from his hometown. At that time, he was crazy. After having an incomplete love, he cherished love even more. Every day, he chatted with the girl in the Internet cafe, and handed over a lot of money to the network administrator. Before the holiday, he revealed to me that he would confirm their relationship after returning home.

In September, it turned out that he was really good. Their relationship was confirmed as he said. Since then, there was one less single person in the dormitory. Among the three good brothers in the dormitory, only I was single. But I wasn't in a hurry, since I looked a bit strange and couldn't change anything anyway.

The long-distance love began its long journey from then on. From then on, he bought a computer and every day he was glued in the dormitory. Occasionally, they would have small quarrels, but they got through it. The relationship was stable. However, his phone bill was a bit hard to control, but for people in love, this was not a problem.

This spring came late. Girls are all strange. My girl who hasn't yet confirmed the relationship also had a small quarrel with me for no reason, but fortunately, it was resolved in time by 'apologizing'. On the same night, another person in the dormitory also quarreled with his beloved, and the severity scared me so much that I didn't dare to play games. I quietly watched, fearing that something happened between them. I really didn't want to see such a perfect couple break up. But what I didn't expect was that Xiaojie had problems that night. However, Xiaojie is a rather profound man. They didn't argue fiercely, so I didn't pay attention. It wasn't until yesterday that I found out that they broke up.

I was stunned. I didn't see them argue much. How did they break up? It was only then that I noticed that he hadn't been out of the dormitory building for three days. He skipped today's class. He quit smoking for the girl before, but now he picked up cigarettes again, lighting them alone in the dead of night...

Although Xiaojie cannot be said to be a very passionate person, he is a boy who doesn't want to make mistakes very much. If even he can't get love, I began to waver. What exactly is love? What kind of boys do girls need? Anyway, let's write a poem. This is the only thing a brother with a light writing style can do!

"Inquiry of Love"

One sentence, two sentences, three or four sentences, only deep feelings create absolute verses.

One paragraph, two paragraphs, three or four paragraphs, true love in the world is the hardest to sever.

In the past, seeking love but without affection, what is happening now that breaks the two apart?

A graceful lady, a gentleman's pursuit, the end of fate and severed affection leaves only white hair!
