Food therapy + technology, the best combination for whitening

by wswen356 on 2012-02-27 09:59:07

In the aesthetics of Europe and America, chocolate-like skin symbolizes a kind of sexiness and temptation. After all, for those naturally born with white skin, this color is a highlight of individuality. For Asian women, however, having fair skin is the ultimate dream of beauty. China has long had the saying "one whiteness covers three uglinesses." This desire for whitening has almost become an obsession for Asian women, who will not stop until their goal is reached. So between dietary therapy and technology, which is the best choice for whitening?

**Dietary Therapy:**

1. **Whitening Mulberry Soup**

- Ingredients: 1 cup (300ml) of water, an appropriate amount of canned mulberries, 3 slices of lemon.

- Directions: Boil water, add canned mulberries, bring to a boil again, then pour into a cup. When warm, add lemon slices or juice, stir gently. If a stronger flavor is desired, add a few spoonfuls of mulberry juice, mix well, and drink. Chew the mulberry seeds thoroughly as they are the essence. The "whitening mulberry soup" has detoxifying effects and also helps replenish blood. Drinking one cup daily for 3 months will result in finer skin texture, gradually fading dark spots, and disappearance of acne.

2. **Moisturizing Whitening Soup**

- Ingredients: 15g Polygonum multiflorum, 15g Radix Glehniae, 20g Solomoniae Rhizoma, 15g Coicis Semen, 3 jujubes, 1 pig lung, and an appropriate amount of salt.

- Directions: Cleanse the herbs and set aside. Boil the pig lung (or pork) in water, add all ingredients, cook on high heat for 10 minutes, then simmer for 2 hours. Alternatively, after boiling for 10 minutes, transfer to a slow cooker and cook for 8 hours. Season with salt before serving. Solomoniae Rhizoma, known as Nüwēi in the *Shennong Herbal Classic*, is classified as a superior medicinal herb that can remove facial dark spots over time, improve complexion, and moisturize the skin. This soup nourishes yin, moistens the lungs, generates fluids, beautifies the face, and benefits the liver and kidneys. Those with rough and dark skin should drink it regularly.

3. **Fat-Reducing Whitening Soup**

- Ingredients: 60g fresh radish greens, 30g coix seed, 40g fresh purslane.

- Directions: First, boil 30g of coix seed in water for 20 minutes, then wash and add fresh radish greens and purslane, cook for another 10 minutes. Treats grayish complexion or excessive oil production.

**Foods That Help Whiten Skin:**

- **Soy Milk:** Made from soybeans, it contains extremely rich proteins that brighten skin while detoxifying.

- **Sesame Seeds:** Rich in protein and Vitamin E, they moisturize the skin and aid metabolism.

- **Asparagus:** High in protein and low in calories, it whitens skin without causing weight gain.

- **Corn:** Contains large amounts of Vitamin E and phosphorus, promoting cell growth and delaying aging.

- **Fish Soup:** Rich in protein, it makes the skin smooth and tender.

- **Walnuts and Almonds:** Contain protein that promotes cell growth and metabolism within the body.

- **Milk and Eggs:** Rich in protein and vitamins, they moisturize and whiten the skin. Women concerned about their figure may choose skim milk, and eggs should be limited to one per day.

- **Watermelon Juice:** Helps the skin absorb moisture, providing nutritional whitening. Watermelon rind can be used to massage the face, promoting skin metabolism, reducing wrinkles, and making the skin fine, soft, and radiant.

**Technology:**

1. **Photodynamic Laser Whitening:** Ideal for when your skin has been sunburned by UV rays and you want to quickly whiten it.

- Procedure: Apply a black mineral oil containing tiny charcoal particles evenly to the treatment area, then shine laser light of specific wavelengths onto the skin. Charcoal enhances the skin's absorption of laser energy, amplifying its effect several times to reach deeper layers of the skin, effectively stimulating collagen production, reorganizing collagen fibers and elastic fibers, and repairing UV-damaged skin.

2. **IPL Photorejuvenation:** If after a summer of sun exposure, you notice your skin has darkened and fine lines have appeared.

- Treatment Principle: Selectively acts on pigments or blood vessels under the skin, breaking down hemoglobin or pigment particles. It thickens collagen tissue. IPL photorejuvenation is a non-abrasive treatment suitable for fine lines caused by skin aging, various spots, facial redness, and enlarged pores.

3. **Ion Infusion Whitening with IPL Photorejuvenation:** If your skin lacks luster and appears dull due to reasons beyond just sun exposure.

- Method: Using the principle of electrode repulsion, water-soluble whitening components such as "L-ascorbic acid" are infused into the dermis. L-ascorbic acid is a form of vitamin C that the skin easily absorbs. Its particles are the smallest among all forms of vitamin C, allowing it to penetrate deeply into the skin. Through biochemical whitening reduction, it fades melanin, activates skin cells, and accelerates the reverse reduction of already formed melanin back to its original colorless state. While maintaining the tenderness and elasticity of surface skin, it also prevents post-sun exposure aging and enhances the skin's self-defense ability, thoroughly whitening the skin.

4. **Chemical Peeling with Fruit Acids:** If your overall complexion is good but due to sun exposure, you have developed faint spots.

- Function: Removes excessively keratinized layers, helping repair damaged outer layers of the skin, stimulating skin cell growth. It noticeably reduces skin pigmentation, treats acne, minimizes pores, controls excess oil, making the skin whiter, brighter, softer, and more elastic.

5. **Microdermabrasion:** If after a humid summer, you find your skin has darkened and your pores have enlarged.

- Process: Uses crystal particles similar to sand to gently remove blackheads, skin spots, and superficial wrinkles. Multiple treatments can remove thicker layers of keratin, promoting skin metabolism. Each microdermabrasion session gently removes thin layers of skin, adjusting superficial conditions like dull skin and enlarged pores.
