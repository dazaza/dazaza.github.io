Changzhou Tianning Temple Tea Culture Festival focuses on building tourism brand

by a12ewf213j on 2010-04-01 13:01:10

Ms. Sun from Changzhou Tianning Temple said regarding the Tea Culture Festival: During this year's Biluochun Tea Cultural Tourism Festival, we will focus on promoting one brand to form a spring tourism publicity offensive around Taihu Lake in Wuzhong. The main theme will be “Biluochun Questions Green, Spring Tour of Taihu”, fully utilizing the influence formed by the successful hosting of the "Biluochun Tea Cultural Tourism Festival" over the years. We will increase brand promotion and ignite the spring tourism around Taihu Lake in Wuzhong. We will strongly recommend two routes to reach the climax of spring tourism around Taihu Lake in Wuzhong. To better connect with the 2010 Shanghai World Expo, combining with the actual situation of tourism in Wuzhong, we specially package and recommend two premium routes: "Taihu Water, Ancient Village Style" Yangtze River Delta Expo Experience Tour Demonstration Site "Taihu Mountain-Water Ancient Town-Ancient Village Cultural Experience Tour" and "Taste Dongting Biluochun Tea, Tour Wuzhong Taihu Scenic Area". We also cooperate with the Shanghai Tourism Distribution Center to open a special tourist bus line "Langya Mountain; Shanghai Expo Taihu Tour - Taste Dongting Biluochun Tea, Tour Wuzhong Taihu Scenic Area". We sincerely welcome friends from Shenyang, Liaoning, to visit our Suzhou Wuzhong while visiting the Shanghai World Expo, experiencing the beautiful ecological landscape around Taihu Lake and feeling the unique charm of Wu culture.

We will make three key promotions to create an atmosphere for the Biluochun Tea Cultural Tourism Festival. The focus will be on the promotional events in Shenyang, Beijing, and the mass promotional event in Shanghai organized by the major tourism companies in Wuzhong District, further expanding the medium and long-term customer market around Taihu Lake in Wuzhong. We will recommend a batch of characteristic products. Combining with the spring characteristic tourism products of various scenic spots in Wuzhong, especially in the East Hill and West Hill scenic areas, we will recommend a series of farmhouses, teahouses, and tea buildings, as well as a series of Wuzhong characteristic tourism products. In the 2010范XiaoXuan Chengdu Concert, we will arrange performances of tea art, Pingtan (storytelling), Kunqu Opera and interactive experience tourism projects such as tea picking, tea selection, and tea frying. Through rich and colorful tourism promotion, we sincerely invite citizens and tourists to participate in the agrarian fun of tea picking, tea selection, and tea frying, appreciate the elegant realm of Biluochun tea, enjoy the beautiful scenery of Taihu Lake in Suzhou, taste mountain delicacies and lake fresh food, and feel the charm of Wu culture and natural scenery, further expanding the spring tourism industry chain around Taihu Lake in Wuzhong.
