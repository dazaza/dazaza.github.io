Love You, Never Leave You

The boy and girl had been together for three years. The girl loved the boy, and the boy loved the girl.

The girl was beautiful and cute, but she came from an unfortunate family. She never knew what her mother looked like. Her father was busy with work and didn't give her much attention when she was young. The girl felt abandoned by the world. Until one day, she met the boy, and then she realized how happy it was to be cared for. Gradually, there were more smiles on her face.

The boy was handsome and sunny. In every school sports meet, his figure could always be seen. Last provincial games, he won again. He was very popular among girls. When a fashion show was held in another school, the boy met the girl. Standing on the stage, the girl was so charming that the boy fell in love with her. Then they started dating. The boy was luckier than the girl. He had a happy family. Sometimes, the girl envied him. The boy liked to pat the girl's head gently and said: "Silly, you will also be happy. As long as you're willing, I'll stay by your side, never leave you." The girl thought having the boy was the greatest happiness. Every word of the boy was so warm.

The boy would ride an electric bike to send the girl to school and pick her up, even though they weren't in the same school; the boy would hold the girl's hand and take her shopping, buy her favorite sweet ice cream; the boy would take the girl to Sugar Nest, order two cups of original flavor milk tea, two hot dogs, and a bag of popcorn; the boy would also stew soup for the girl by hand and then deliver it to her school. Every time, the boy would bring the girl different surprises. The girl really felt very happy. Maybe God found out that he was a little harsh on the girl, so he sent the boy to her side. The girl always thought this naively.

For three years, the girl recorded every moment she spent with the boy in her diary. The girl hoped that one day, when she could spend her whole life with the boy, she would show those memories to the boy and share the past beauty with the boy, and continue their original beauty with the boy.

Two

In the end, the girl's dream still failed, and the girl hadn't waited for that day.

The girl's father found her a stepmother, who was American. So they decided to send the girl to America to study. The girl refused. The girl didn't want to leave the boy. Being with the boy was her only happiness. The girl really didn't want to leave the boy. The boy once promised the girl: "As long as you're willing, I'll always be by your side, never leave you." The girl also wanted to be with the boy forever.

On the day before Valentine's Day, the boy didn't come to pick up the girl, and his phone was off. The girl waited at the school gate for a long time, but the boy still didn't come. "Maybe the boy has something and can't come," the girl thought. Then the girl walked out of the school gate alone.

Tomorrow is Valentine's Day. The girl also wanted to give the boy a surprise. She went to "Mi Yue Xiao Wu", where there was a pair of lovers' watches. The girl had wanted to buy them for the boy for a long time. When the girl arrived at the door of "Mi Yue Xiao Wu", she saw the boy. However, the boy was holding another girl's hand, laughing and talking while walking out of "Mi Yue Xiao Wu". The girl stood there dumbfounded, staring at the boy. At this moment, the boy also saw the girl. They silently stared at each other for a long time. There were tears in the girl's eyes. The girl told herself not to cry, not to be so fragile. The girl turned and ran into the crowd. At the moment she turned around, the girl's tears burst out of her eyes. She wished she hadn't seen anything. The girl really wanted to pretend that nothing had happened and still be happily together with the boy like in the past. But the girl couldn't do it. That was the boy she deeply loved for three years, three years of deep love! Her heart was about to break. She felt that her own happiness was going away. When the boy realized it, the girl had already disappeared in the crowd and couldn't be found anymore.

The girl returned home dejectedly. Finally, the girl agreed to go to America to study. She was leaving tomorrow. That night, the boy called the girl all night, but the girl's phone was always off.

The next day, the girl got to school very early. She didn't take anything with her. The girl placed the tear-stained letter on the corner of her desk. She knew the boy would definitely look for her and see it.

The boy came to pick up the girl again, but he didn't wait for the girl. The boy dialed the girl's number again, which was still off. The boy went to the girl's school and saw the letter the girl left for him. The boy wanted to rush to the airport to stop the girl. The boy couldn't lose the girl. The boy really loved the girl and wanted to explain everything. After being together for so long, the boy had never been so afraid.

On the way to the airport, an accident happened. To save time, the boy ran a red light and got into a car accident. The boy lay in a pool of blood, still tightly holding the Valentine's Day gift he wanted to give to the girl - a pair of watches. The watches were exactly what the girl wanted to give to the boy. The boy's mouth was still half-open, as if he wanted to explain something to the girl.

Actually, the girl didn't know that the other girl holding the boy's hand was his cousin. They went to "Mi Yue" to pick out the Valentine's Day gift for the girl. The boy bought the only pair of lovers' watches engraved with "Never Leave You". The reason the phone was off was because it was out of power. These things the girl would never know.

The girl believed that the boy would definitely go to the airport to see her off or try to stop her. The girl thought, as long as the boy said three words "Don't go", she would immediately jump into the boy's arms, never go to that strange place, and never leave the boy. Even if what she saw yesterday was true, the girl wouldn't care anymore.

Until the plane took off, the girl didn't see the boy's figure. When the rumbling sound rang in her ears, the girl cried. The girl regretted it. The girl didn't want to leave the boy. The girl didn't want to lose that precious happiness. The girl left the address in the letter for the boy. The girl thought, as long as the boy wrote to her and asked her to come back, she would return to the boy's side no matter what. But the girl didn't know that one hour before she left this familiar city, the boy had left her forever.

The girl waited somewhere in the world for the boy's letter, while the boy watched over the girl from somewhere in another world. The girl didn't know anything yet. The girl believed that the boy would call her back, because the boy had once promised: "As long as you're willing, I'll always be by your side, never leave you."

Three

The girl waited and waited, but still didn't receive any letters from the boy. The girl couldn't contact the boy anymore. Maybe the boy encountered something and couldn't contact the girl temporarily, the girl always consoled herself like this.

It had been more than 3 months. Why was there no news from the boy? The girl was worried. Although she believed that the boy would find her someday, the girl still decided to return home. The girl's stepmother always used the excuse that the girl must study abroad and couldn't go back now. Eventually, the girl went back anyway. Life without the boy was torment for the girl every day. The girl hadn't truly smiled happily for a long time. The girl knew that her stepmother wasn't really concerned about her, otherwise why didn't the stepmother come to see her off when she arrived in America?

Still the familiar street, countless times the boy had held the girl's hand and passed through here. It had been 3 years, almost every corner here bore the footprints of the boy and the girl. At this moment, tears began to well up in the girl's eyes again. The girl missed the boy so much, but the boy didn't even send a single letter. Her heart hurt faintly. The girl really wanted to know what the boy was doing at this moment, whether he was missing her just as much.

Before the girl even got home, she rushed to the boy's house first. Unexpectedly, the boy had moved. Very disappointed, to find the boy, the girl dragged her tired body to the boy's school. The boy's seatmate told her that the boy had died in a car accident on Valentine's Day. The girl's heart felt like it was being twisted hard, it hurt so much, she forgot how to cry, standing there dumbfounded for a long time. It's impossible, it's impossible. Just 3 months, merely 3 months, how could he just leave like that? After waiting so painfully, the result turned out to be this.

The girl turned and rushed out of the classroom. Tears streamed down her face like beads falling from a broken string. She sobbed her heart out under the cypress tree on the playground. The tears flowed into her heart, bitter. This must be fake, it must be fake, he was playing a trick on her. The 3 years of happiness couldn't just disappear like that. The boy must still be somewhere waiting for her. The boy had promised: "As long as you're willing, I'll always be with you, never leave you." The girl seemed to see the boy's gentle eyes again. The boy was smiling at her and scolding her in a teasing tone: "You silly, after being gone for 3 months, don't you know how much I've missed you...?" The girl wanted to run over and hug the boy, tell the boy that she missed him too, when suddenly, everything disappeared. It was just a dream. The girl cried even harder.

Four

The girl cried until she was exhausted, truly exhausted. Her small body curled up under the tree, her arms hugging her legs trembling slightly. Through her tears, the girl saw her broken heart dripping blood bit by bit. A shadow approached the girl closer and closer, looking extremely like the boy. With only the last thread of hope left, the girl lifted her head. Her bloodshot eyes were swollen from crying. The girl was disappointed, it was originally the boy's seatmate. The girl ignored him and continued to lower her head, trying hard to stop herself from shedding more tears. "Stop crying, I'll take you somewhere." His tone was very calm. The girl followed closely behind him. She had already guessed where they were going.

The stairs were steep and long. The girl had been here a long time ago, her memory was vague, but she knew her mother's grave was here. Only, she had already forgotten which one it was. In her memory, her father only brought her here once.

"We're here." The boy stopped, his tone still as calm. Before the girl's eyes was a large area of graves. The girl was already standing in front of the boy's grave. "No, this can't be possible, it's not true, tell me, this isn't true." The girl was almost breaking down. The boy's posthumous photo was indeed attached to the tombstone. It was still the familiar face, still the gentle eyes, but everything was far away from the girl.

The girl knelt down in front of the boy's grave on both knees. Her eyes had no more tears, her heart had become numb from crying repeatedly. Those so-called happiness had become all the girl's memories. The girl only wanted to quietly, quietly accompany the boy. The girl only learned today that the boy had already been gone for more than 3 months. What a foolish joke.

If that day the girl hadn't gone to "Mi Yue Xiao Wu", if that day the girl hadn't seen anything, if that day the girl hadn't chosen to leave, then the following events wouldn't have happened. The boy would still be sending the girl to school and picking her up like in the past; the boy would still be taking the girl shopping and buying her favorite sweet ice cream like in the past; the boy would still be personally cooking soup for the girl and delivering it to her school like in the past. How ironic the "if" was, how ironic the past was. It was she who had destroyed her own happiness, the girl kept blaming herself.

Nightfall began, the girl's legs were already numb from kneeling. She forgot about the boy's seatmate standing beside her. Actually, he knew the girl was very sad, but everything was irreversible. "It's late, let's go back." He slowly helped the girl up.

The girl returned to her old school to attend classes. Only the boy couldn't come to pick her up anymore. Every afternoon, the girl would go to accompany the boy. She didn't want the boy to feel lonely. The girl knew the boy must need her. Every time the girl visited the boy, she would remember a lot, a lot from the past. Those unforgettable memories were like movies replayed yesterday, still vivid. During those days, the girl would always see the boy in her dreams, and every time she woke up, she was in tears, soaking her pillow. In front of the boy's grave, the girl never cried. She dared not cry. She knew the boy certainly didn't want to see her sad, otherwise the boy in heaven would also be sad.

The girl always believed that the boy was an angel sent by God to her side. The boy would always protect her. Now God had a cold, and the boy needed to go back to take care of God. The boy would come back soon. Because the boy had promised: "As long as you're willing, I'll always be with you, never leave you." The girl knew such an excuse was a bit absurd, but she was willing to believe it.

The girl began to be silent. In her world, there was no need for words, returning to the melancholy she once had.

That day it was raining heavily. The girl didn't carry an umbrella and went to see the boy again. Her white dress was soaked wet by the rain, tightly clinging to her body. Her long hair was messy. From afar, she looked like a white butterfly flying through the rain.

"Screech—" The sudden brake broke the most beautiful scenery in the rain. The girl fell down. She was walking too fast and didn't have time to dodge. The blood dyed her white dress bright red. In the rain curtain, the girl dazedly opened her eyes. She seemed to see the boy. She wanted to get up, embrace the boy, tell the boy that she was wrong, she missed him, she wouldn't leave rashly anymore, she would stay with him, never leave him.
