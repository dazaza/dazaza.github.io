Spanish well-known mattress brand Pikolin expands into the Chinese market

by seo17519 on 2012-03-08 22:55:55

Specialized youth and children's mattresses have long been a void in the Chinese market. In September 2010, the world's first mattress designed to assist in the healthy development of youth and children’s spines — Spain's ECUS Youth and Children's Mattress (hereinafter referred to as "ECUS") — officially entered the Chinese market. In just over a year, the ECUS brand has developed rapidly in the Chinese market, with nearly a hundred specialty stores making their debut. The brand's positive image, professional quality, and excellent service have earned it favorable reviews and consumer favor.

The ECUS brand is a well-known mattress brand in Spain that has consistently dedicated itself to the cause of human sleep health. It specializes in creating scientifically-designed mattresses for children aged 0-18 years old that support spine development and effectively prevent kyphosis (hunchback). The brand boasts decades of experience in researching, producing medical equipment and hospital beds, along with collaborations with numerous authoritative medical institutions, providing them with significant technical advantages.

From childhood through adolescence, a child's height, weight, and body shape undergo rapid changes, with spinal development being at the core. Spine growth is scientifically divided into four stages: ages 1-7, 8-10, 11-13, and 14-18. Each of these different age stages presents unique demands on mattresses for proper spine development. Therefore, it is necessary to select a mattress that can effectively support the S-shaped spine development during different stages of a child's growth. However, parents cannot replace a mattress every two or three years for their children. To address this need, ECUS offers a solution, which is also one of its key patents (the ECUS mattress brand holds a total of 38 patents): by adjusting the density distribution of internal materials and usage methods, a single mattress can be used for individuals aged 1-18 years old.

Supported by decades of research and production of youth and children's mattresses, along with long-term user evaluations, ECUS possesses an absolute professional advantage. It is the sole designated supplier of youth and children's mattresses for the Spanish Royal Family and the Madrid Royal Hospital in Spain, showcasing an exceptionally high level of brand reputation.

Relevant background of ECUS:

With 50 years of European medical technology heritage, clinical certification from 350,000 youths and children in Europe and America, and eight years of in-depth research on the growth and development of Asian youths and children, a mattress that can support the future of Chinese children has finally been born.

"ECUS" was born in Valencia, Spain, in 1945. It is a world-renowned brand specializing in the production and sales of high-end medical beds and high-quality medical mattresses. With decades of research and development in the field of medical beds, it has been highly recommended by many world-renowned medical institutions. In 1978, "ECUS" began producing and selling high-tech civilian health mattresses. Through continuous market promotion and increasing public recognition, "ECUS" mattresses have now become a famous high-end civilian furniture brand in Europe and around the world.

In 1995, "ECUS" built Spain's first solar-powered mattress production factory in Yecla, equipped with the world's most advanced automated production equipment. This maximizes the application of artificial intelligence, truly achieving fully automated intelligent production lines without human intervention.

Today, "ECUS" is popular in 47 countries across Europe, North America, Asia, and South America.

Currently, international furniture brands are trending towards Asia, with particular attention focused on China. After nearly eight years of research in China, in 2010, ECUS brought this "world's first mattress assisting in the healthy development of youth and children's spines" to China. Its aim is to revolutionarily change the poor sleep habits of Chinese youth and children and bring blessings to the healthy development of the spines of 290 million Chinese youth and children!
