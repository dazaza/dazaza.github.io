For e-commerce websites, whether now or in the future, product display is one of its cores. With the development of e-commerce, e-commerce has more attempts in product display: video experience, flash animation, virtual space and other methods keep emerging. In summary, it's about strengthening the product image packaging and user experience, ultimately promoting the transaction rate and creating more benefits. And as a user, the most hope is to clearly see the shape of the product from all angles on the website, the most ideal state is to be able to see the physical object. The 360-degree panoramic display of products is a kind of display method that is closest to the physical object, and it is becoming the latest application trend in e-commerce. It can be seen on e-commerce platform websites such as JD.com, Meixi Fashion, Youzhong Network, Mai Bags, and Newegg! (With the emergence of manufacturers specializing in this technology like Yingshang Technology, general application has become possible.)

What is the 360-degree panoramic display of products? It is also called 360-degree product display. Generally, it refers to shooting pictures around the product, then synthesizing them using Flash technology, so that the product can be displayed in full rotation at 360 degrees on the website. Another way is video production: recording the 360-degree rotation effect of the product. Its advantage is that the shooting requirements are lower, but its disadvantages are that there is a difference in accuracy and interactivity is not strong, and the video processing process is relatively troublesome. The 360-degree product display using Flash technology allows the product on the website to interact fully with the mouse. Users can click and drag to play these 360-degree product animations, view the product images they need from any angle, and check the details of the product from all angles.

It can be seen that through the application of 360-degree product display, e-commerce websites can significantly enhance the product's image packaging, information display volume, and user experience, giving users a sense similar to viewing the actual product. This increases users' trust in the website and eliminates doubts about product uncertainty, thereby promoting purchases. In fact, due to the inherent limitations of flat picture displays or excessive pursuit of decorative effects, customers often find significant differences between the received product and the picture, leading to psychological disappointment, resulting in returns or directly listing the merchant as untrustworthy. For merchants, reduced customer experience ultimately leads to consumer loss. And high return rates are also very painful problems for e-commerce companies, directly increasing transaction circulation costs and affecting corporate efficiency and revenue.

As the best supplement to product picture display, 360-degree panoramic display can convey product information closest to the physical item while merchants pursue luxurious packaging. By applying 360-degree product display, e-commerce enterprises can increase transaction rates, effectively reduce product returns, and improve conversion rates and operational efficiency, thus bringing tangible benefits to e-commerce companies.

Of course, the 360-degree panoramic display of products is not flawless. At this stage, it has not fully utilized its own display capabilities. E-commerce companies still have certain doubts about applying 360-degree panoramic display. I will summarize some of the issues here:

1. Data volume problem: If there is enough browsing volume, can a regular website handle the data traffic? Answer: A 360-degree product panoramic animation plays a buffer file of about 400k. To avoid slowing down page speed, a common solution for e-commerce applications is: only when the user wants to learn more about the product and clicks on the 360-degree display playback box will the effect be shown, which does not affect the overall speed of the website.

2. Production and application difficulty issues: Is it difficult to shoot? Do you need professional shooting personnel skills?

Answer: Shooting a 360-degree product display animation of about 36 pictures takes about 3 minutes, and this process is fully automatic. Software operation is quite simplified and does not require professionals. That is to say, as long as the current conditions allow good pictures to be taken, the animation effect can be well shot, and the output file is completed in one step because of its inherent shooting characteristics, no post-production single modification is required.

3. Format size and page effect issues: What size is the file, will it open slowly?

Answer: Under the current network speed, it cannot fully utilize the effectiveness of 360-degree product display. Of course, under the premise of ensuring opening speed, the application of 360-degree product display in e-commerce is no problem. If it is synthesized from about 36 shots, with dimensions like 360X500, 400X600, these sizes can fully meet the needs of displaying the entire product. The page opening time is about 3 seconds. Users just need to double-click the screen to realize the original high-definition detail enlargement of the picture, making the 360-degree product display go deeper.
