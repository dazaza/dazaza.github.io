Korean drama Triple plot introduction

This is a story about the dreams and love between female figure skater Lee Haro and three young men working in an advertising company. Shin Hwa (played by Lee Jung Jae), Haeyoon (played by Lee Sun Kyun), and Hyuntae (played by Yoon Je Moon) are longtime friends who work at the same advertising company with completely different approaches and attitudes towards their work. The reason they can work together is because of the traces of friendship in their hearts... The arrival of Shin Hwa's younger sister Haro (played by Min Hyorin) brings changes to the lives of these three men. Haro is a figure skater who only likes this sport because her brother Shin Hwa also likes it.
