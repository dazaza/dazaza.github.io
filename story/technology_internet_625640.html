Lessa Auto 43210 Service - Carefree Throughout

by ftleisa on 2011-12-15 16:17:36

FOTON LeiSa's "Carefree Journey" 43210 service commitment aims to satisfy the users of concrete pumping machinery with the goal of achieving the "No.1 in Operation" application requirements. It is characterized by rapid response, integrated chassis and superstructure services, and zero-time difference service support, thereby establishing a new benchmark for the after-sales service industry of domestic concrete pumping machinery. Vice President of FOTON LeiSa’s Pumping Division, Shi Qiang, expressed: "In the concrete pumping machinery industry, what users worry about most is machine downtime. FOTON LeiSa focuses on creating the highest value for mid-to-high-end concrete pumping machinery users. On one hand, we provide reliable and high-speed products so that users can use them with peace of mind; at the same time, our comprehensive, meticulous, and agile after-sales service ensures that users feel secure. This is also our initial intention behind launching the 'Heartfelt Service? Miles Away' user activity. The numbers 4, 3, 2, 1, 0 are like a countdown timer, reminding us that our customers' operational time is money. When our users encounter problems during operations, they will receive the fastest service response from FOTON LeiSa."

The "43210" New Standard Model represents a new standard where the 43210 service commitment is based on the essence of FOTON Commercial Vehicle's "Carefree Journey" service, specifically tailored to the unique characteristics of the concrete pumping machinery industry. It has pioneered a new standard model for one-stop service and zero-time difference service support in China.

Specifically, it includes five major service commitments covering response, training, maintenance, repair, and warranty:

- A 24-hour hotline available all year round across four seasons, responding to customer needs at any time.

- Three value-added service projects including free training, monthly inspections, and free maintenance.

- An industry-leading two-year extended warranty period providing users with more confidence and assurance.

- One-stop service for both superstructure and chassis, ensuring quality and speed control.

- Zero-time difference service support with standby repair pump trucks to ensure uninterrupted production.

Efficient and reliable LeiSa concrete pumping machinery products give users peace of mind, while the 43210 service commitment addresses all user concerns comprehensively. Backed by FOTON's robust service network, FOTON LeiSa's service network is extensive with short service distances. Upon receiving a customer call, the nearest service station will respond quickly and implement the fastest rescue and repair. A complete service parts system ensures that parts are readily available and supplied promptly, keeping costs low and giving users a truly convenient and cost-effective experience. Compared to competitors in the industry, FOTON LeiSa offers the shortest service cycle, the longest warranty period, and free service maintenance, providing customers with an exceptional experience. Both the chassis and superstructure of FOTON LeiSa products are self-owned, and with a nationwide network of 1,100 integrated chassis and superstructure service stations, vehicle issues can be resolved in one stop, making users feel comfortable using LeiSa products. Notably, FOTON LeiSa is equipped with dedicated repair standby pump trucks which provide free pump truck usage during repairs, ensuring no interruption to customer production and fully safeguarding customer interests from their perspective.
