Tenant disappears after borrowing 40,000; landlord spends 7 years chasing the debt

by dehua900 on 2011-05-08 09:41:42

, http://www.vdisk.cn/down/index/7694974A1336

Could Wang Jiangfan have just vanished from the face of the earth like that? Wang Shifeng was not ready to give up: the money borrowed from her relatives must be repaid, and with her own poor health urgently needing this money for treatment. She would find Wang Jiangfan no matter what. Wang Shifeng analyzed that Wang Jiangfan was working in Dalian. If she could find Sichuanese workers in Dalian and ask them, she would surely be able to locate him. Thus, starting from 2003, the elderly woman traveled to construction sites and factories in Dalian, asking one by one the Sichuanese workers there.

On January 17th at around 7 p.m. this year, Wang Shifeng took a bus to Dingshui Town in Nanbu County. Less than 100 meters after getting off, the elderly woman found the Dingshui police station. Instructor Yang Bo received her, and the elderly woman took out the four IOUs written by Wang Jiangfan years ago, excitedly explaining the purpose of her journey. Yang Bo arranged for her to stay at an inn in town and requested the innkeeper to take good care of her. Subsequently, Yang Bo drove to the village where Wang Jiangfan lived, learning through acquaintances that Wang Jiangfan was currently building a house at home.

The next morning, Yang Bo led the police officers together with Wang Shifeng and the village head to Wang Jiangfan's home. Wang Jiangfan, who was building the house, suddenly looked up and saw Wang Shifeng, his expression immediately changing: "Mrs. Wang, I'm truly sorry, I will repay the money right away." Wang Jiangfan put down his tools, went to Dingshui Town to withdraw his savings and borrow some money, gathering 40,000 yuan which he handed over to Wang Shifeng. Upon receiving the 40,000 yuan in cash, the elderly woman was overwhelmed with mixed feelings: "Child, you've really caused me a lot of suffering. You should remember, no matter how hard life has been for me, I never thought of reporting it to the police! I know your life hasn't been easy either, I hope you can learn from this."

Working tirelessly on the construction site every day

In 2003, Wang Jiangfan's 74-year-old father came from their hometown in Sichuan to Dalian. Soon after, Wang Jiangfan's father was diagnosed with esophageal cancer and needed surgery, but where could they quickly raise so much money for the treatment? Helpless, Wang Jiangfan approached his landlord, Wang Shifeng, explaining his situation and hoping the elderly woman could lend him some money. Wang Shifeng was a kind-hearted person, and Wang Jiangfan's filial piety in borrowing money to treat his father deeply moved her. Without hesitation, the elderly woman took out her only 20,000 yuan in savings, then gathered her relatives and friends, guaranteeing with her own credibility, borrowing another 20,000 yuan plus from them.

Seven years ago, a migrant worker from Nanbu borrowed 40,000 yuan to treat his father and then disappeared. For seven years, the elderly landlady chased the debt, traveling tirelessly across each construction site in Dalian. Seven years later, she finally traced the borrower's address, retrieving the debt with the help of the Nanbu police.

This year, Wang Shifeng is over 70 years old and lives in Dalian City. In 2002, a migrant worker named Wang Jiangfan rented her house. The elderly woman only knew that Wang Jiangfan was from Sichuan, never asking where exactly in Sichuan he was from, and rarely interacting with Wang Jiangfan.

Police helping retrieve the debt

Kindly lending out life-saving money

Yang Bo was worried about the elderly woman carrying so much money, so he used a police car to take her to the county, depositing the cash into her bank card, then sending her back to the bus station. Zhang Li, Li Yongzhong

Chasing from Dalian to Sichuan

Unexpectedly, the borrower disappeared

The 42,000 yuan lent by the elderly woman resolved Wang Jiangfan's urgent need. On the night before Wang Jiangfan's father was discharged and returned to their hometown in Sichuan, Wang Jiangfan brought a basket of newly bought apples to visit the elderly woman Wang Shifeng and express his gratitude. The next day, the elderly woman did not see Wang Jiangfan all day, so she went to check his room, finding all of Wang Jiangfan's daily necessities still there.

There, the elderly woman dismissed her doubts. Following that, for several consecutive days, the elderly woman did not see Wang Jiangfan himself, nor did she receive any calls from him. When she called him, she was informed that his number had been deactivated.

A month later, there was still no news of Wang Jiangfan. Feeling the situation becoming serious, the elderly woman went to the construction site where Wang Jiangfan had once worked to inquire. Unfortunately, the construction site where Wang Jiangfan had worked had already been completed, and she gained nothing from her efforts. While Wang Shifeng was searching everywhere for Wang Jiangfan, her relatives and friends came knocking, demanding the 20,000 yuan she had guaranteed.

Last October, Wang Shifeng learned from a migrant worker in Nanbu County that Wang Jiangfan was from Dingshui Town in Nanbu County.
