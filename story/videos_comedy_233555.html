"Family Outing" is a segment of the large weekly Sunday variety show "Happy Sunday" on SBS Korean Television, hosted by MC Yoo Jae-suk and featuring several regular guests and special guests.

Each episode sees the artists form a family and select a rural household in the countryside where they spend two nights and one day. During this time, the family must complete various farming tasks left by the owners, participate in team-based competitive games, collaborate on cooking meals, and compete within the family for better sleeping positions, delivering a humorously genuine portrayal of the artists' lives.
