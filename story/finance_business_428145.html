Product Description of Dry Magnetic Separator

The Fengde brand dry permanent magnetic drum magnetic separator is a new and highly efficient magnetic separation device successfully developed by our company. The magnetic system is entirely made of high-performance rare earth neodymium iron boron materials and high-quality ferrite materials, with an ingenious open magnetic circuit design. The maximum magnetic induction intensity in the sorting area on the drum surface reaches above 0.8T, which is 3-5 times stronger than that of regular medium magnetic machines. The magnetic force in the sorting area can reach the magnetic force level of electromagnetic strong magnetic separators. The sorting drum is made of wear-resistant stainless steel; the minerals to be sorted are evenly fed onto the upper part of the sorting drum through a vibrating feeder. The rotating drum throws away the non-magnetic materials from the drum, while the magnetic materials are attracted to the drum due to the action of the strong magnetic field force. A mineral partition plate is used to conveniently and accurately separate the magnetic and non-magnetic materials.

The strong magnetic field force makes it possible to use a drum magnetic separator for sorting medium and weak magnetic minerals. The equipment has a large processing capacity, a wide range of particle sizes for sorting minerals, high separation accuracy, and does not clog. Its structure is simple, easy to maintain, and its power consumption is only 20% of that of an electromagnetic strong magnetic separator.

Main applications of the Fengde brand dry magnetic separator: iron selection operations in waterless areas; iron removal operations for refractory materials and other materials. This series of dry magnetic separators are suitable for iron removal (selection) of various materials such as refractory materials, chemical materials, grain and oil machinery, abrasives, ceramics, metallurgy, cement, powder metallurgy, rubber, and ores. It can also be used in conjunction with Raymond mills, crushers, and ball mills.

Related Equipment for Dry Magnetic Separators

A commonly used magnetic separator in mines, suitable for separating strongly magnetic minerals.

Wet permanent magnetic drum separators are divided into three types according to the trough structure: co-current, counter-current, and semi-counter-current.

In addition to being used as a separation device in magnetic separation plants, wet permanent magnetic drum separators have been recently used as pre-filtration concentration equipment to replace magnetic dewatering tanks.

There are many types of strong magnetic field separators. According to working conditions, they are divided into wet and dry types; according to the magnetic source, they are divided into electromagnetic and permanent magnet types; according to equipment structure, they are divided into disc type, roller type, flat ring type, vertical ring type, induction roller type, etc.
