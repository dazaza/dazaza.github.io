This particular motorcycle brand name has expanded very well beyond its tangible dimensions into a mark of a cult. The sound of a Harley-Davidson engine is unique and contributes in significant measure to the culture which encompasses enthusiasts and proud owners. The main component of a Harley-Davidson’s performance is similar to any other motorcycle. The fundamentals of a motorcycle are an engine which generates power, a transmission which transfers the engine power to wheels for motion, and a structure to hold the engine and provide a seat for the driver and rider. A motorcycle motor typically has a couple of cylinders arranged in a V shape. Each cylinder has an exhaust. The transmission uses regular gears and a chain for drive. Motorcycle engines usually do not carry mufflers to reduce noise or catalytic converters to lessen emissions. Power is transferred from the engine to the rear wheel. Disc brakes are typically positioned on the wheels. Suspension is provided for the front of the motorcycle. The frame includes Harley handlebars and a light. A motorcycle features the essential structure of a bicycle with an engine that replaces the pedals. The frame is significantly stronger since it must carry the weight of the engine, transmission, and fuel tank, and the wheels are wider to allow the motorcycle to run at speed with safety. These design features give a Harley-Davidson high power at low speeds and its distinctive sound. Harley-Davidson has recently designed a new engine where the two cylinders are set at a wider angle with each other. The new engine is water-cooled rather than air-cooled, traditionally used in motorcycles, and has overhead camshafts. These characteristics give the engine more power and a higher RPM capability. Harley-Davidsons are generally bigger and heavier than most other motorcycles. This new engine will create concrete evidence of the impression that a Harley-Davidson conjures. However, Harley-Davidson continues to set itself apart from its competitors by working hard to develop its intangible strengths rather than introducing new models, as has been the norm in the automotive industry. Yet, it also offers distinct and concrete benefits of speed, power, and comfortable rides across long distances. It is one of the most enduring and well-loved brands in every product category in the world. Many owners invest in installing custom features to their Harley-Davidsons. The exhaust system and body are common areas for incorporating a personalized touch to a Harley-Davidson. The large size lends itself to creative color combinations, making a Harley-Davidson stand out in a crowd, even among others of its kind.
