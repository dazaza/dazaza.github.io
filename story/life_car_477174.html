Overview of the Advantages and Disadvantages of Various Engine Guard Plates in Car Beauty and Maintenance

In car maintenance and beauty care, engine guard plates mainly fall into four categories: hard plastic, resin, steel, and aluminum alloy. The characteristics of guard plates made from different materials have essential differences. After reading the following introduction, you will understand how to choose the right engine guard plate.

4S Online Experts remind you of the advantages and disadvantages of various engine guard plates.

1. Hard Plastic and Resin Guard Plates

They are relatively inexpensive with simple production processes. However, it's important to note that these material guard plates can easily break, especially during winter. Once damaged, they cannot be repaired and fail to provide long-term protection.

Advantages: Lightweight, low price

Disadvantages: Easily damaged

2. Steel or Manganese Alloy Guard Plates

When choosing this type of guard plate, it is crucial to consider its design compatibility with your vehicle and the quality of accompanying accessories. Always select products from reputable manufacturers. This material of guard plate can provide maximum protection for the engine and critical undercarriage components, but its downside is its heavy weight.

Advantages: Strong impact resistance

Disadvantages: Heavy weight

3. Aluminum Alloy Guard Plates

It’s worth noting that many beauty shops heavily promote this product due to the high profits behind its high price, but its hardness is far inferior to that of steel guard plates. Repairing damages is quite challenging as the alloy material is extremely complex, making it difficult to determine its properties. However, its main feature is being lightweight.

Advantages: Lightweight

Disadvantages: High price, insufficient material strength

Installing an engine guard plate is very simple. You just need to purchase a guard plate that matches your car model, and it can be installed within 10 minutes, and you might even be able to do it yourself.

How to Choose Among Different Prices of Underbody Armor on the Market?

Currently, the prices of underbody armor in the market vary significantly, ranging from a few hundred to over a thousand yuan. However, almost all places offering underbody armor installation use similar materials, differing only in the manufacturers. Due to the lack of relevant management systems, the profit margin for underbody armor is very high. Therefore, when choosing an installation location, please pay attention. Generally, a relatively good-quality underbody armor costs around 500-800 yuan. Currently, the main brands used in the market include Henkel from Germany and 3M from the USA.

Additionally, if you decide to install underbody armor, make sure to check the equipment at the installation site. The installation process for "underbody armor" is quite labor-intensive. First, the oil contamination on the underbody must be carefully removed using a specialized cleaner to thoroughly eliminate asphalt and oil stains, followed by drying. Any oversight in these steps will affect the firmness of the "armor." Then, seal rotating parts like the driveshaft and heat-dissipating components like the exhaust pipe with tape or old newspapers before spraying. This prevents accidental damage and ensures their normal operation. After construction, remove the tape or newspapers to avoid any potential hazards.
