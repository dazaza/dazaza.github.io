Bus routes are adjusted, and vehicle turnarounds become a problem.

by bjjdwxgs on 2009-12-06 07:35:42

This morning our 88400000 hotline received a response from Mr. Gao who reported that at the entrance of Taoyuanju Residential Quarter located on the west side of West Ring Road, many No. 207 buses had pulled over and as a result, citizens wanting to take the bus couldn't leave anymore. When the reporter arrived at the entrance of Taoyuanju Residential Quarter, he saw a lot of people standing there with several No. 207 buses parked on the west side of the road. After making some inquiries, the reporter finally learned the ins and outs of the matter. Recorded sound: resident of the residential quarter "Approaching 10 vehicles were blocked this morning, here (buses) got into an argument with security guards of Taoyuanju, and they didn't allow them to make U-turns, thus more than 100 people were 'trapped' here and more than 10 vehicles were not allowed to leave". Residents said that it was a good thing for No. 207 bus to come to the entrance of the residential quarter, because it would facilitate residents' travel. However, now that the security guards of the residential quarter do not allow the vehicle to make U-turns here, residents have quite a few objections. Recorded sound: resident "There are more than 1000 households here. We have a very large population, and we all welcome No. 207 bus. It is convenient for us to go downtown and for children to go to school. Today the property management company stopped the bus and did not allow it to make U-turns". Then why does the property management company of the residential quarter not allow the bus to make U-turns? The manager of Zhongkangju Property Management Co., Ltd., Huang Jianguo, told the reporter that according to the original plan, the place where No. 207 bus makes U-turn should not be at the entrance of the residential quarter but near a station on the east side. However, since that place has become a market for surrounding residents to do business, the bus cannot make normal U-turns there. Making U-turns at the entrance of the residential quarter, on the other hand, causes trouble for the management of the residential quarter. Recorded sound: project manager of Yancheng Kangju Property Management Co., Ltd., Huang Qing "Making U-turns at the entrance of the residential quarter will loosen marble tiles. They will become loose after a long time. Secondly, it's easy for owners of the residential quarter to hit people when entering or leaving". Security guard of the residential quarter "Our residential quarter has 5000 people and more than 100 cars. With cars constantly turning around here, we (residents) can't go out". Some residents in the residential quarter also expressed that it's not safe for buses to make U-turns at the entrance of the residential quarter. In addition, Manager Huang of Kangju Property Company said that the bus company has never coordinated with them about this matter. Recorded sound: project manager of Yancheng Kangju Property Management Co., Ltd., Huang Qing "Including his No. 207 and including the dispatcher of the bus stop, none of them has ever coordinated with us, never" Resident "If there are conflicts, everyone should sit down and negotiate mutually to solve problems instead of doing things like this" Editor's note: What ordinary people say is the most realistic. Both sides can't just waste time here without negotiating properly to solve conflicts. Such practice eventually brings troubles to passengers. Fortunately, the property management company agreed to let No. 207 bus make U-turns here today, otherwise passengers wouldn't know how long they need to wait. But the property management company said that the bus company must coordinate this matter and ensure the convenience and safety of residents in the residential quarter.
