This is the pond where the two teenage girls committed suicide. "My good friend Xiaohua (a pseudonym) lost the remote control for her family's roller shutter and decided to commit suicide. I don't seek to be born in the same year and month as her, but I wish to die in the same year and month. After death, I want to travel through time and space, go back to the Qing Dynasty to make a movie with the emperor..." (according to villagers' accounts). The afternoon before yesterday, after writing a will in class, fifth-grade student from Guogang Village in Qianting Town of Zhangpu County, Xiaomei (a pseudonym), along with her desk mate Xiaohua, jumped into a pond over 2 meters deep. By the time their families found them, it was too late; their lives, like flowers, vanished in the pond.

Xiaohua and Xiaomei were students in the fifth grade at Guogang Primary School in Qianting Town of Zhangpu County. They sat in the last row of the second group in the classroom. Both were 12 years old and were inseparable sisters who shared everything.

On the afternoon of March 1st, after the first class ended, Xiaohua looked flustered, saying that she had searched all her pockets but could not find the remote control for her family's roller shutter. She told this to Xiaomei. The two then discussed what to do. During the second Chinese class, no one knew what Xiaohua was thinking or what she whispered to Xiaomei. It was probably while they were writing their wills.

According to the police officers at the local police station, the handwriting on the will found afterward was very neat. Xiaohua even deliberately stuck two rings on her notebook; these were gifts for her older sister.

At around 4 p.m. that day, after school let out, Xiaohua and Xiaomei secretly hid two "wills" in the cabinet at Xiaohua's home where the discs were kept, then went to jump into the pond. As for the reason for jumping into the pond, most villagers thought it might be because Xiaohua took losing things too seriously, which became a psychological burden. However, many villagers did not agree with this view. One villager said it might be due to psychological problems in the two children, and the loss of the remote control was just a trigger. "And Xiaomei may have been too close friends with her, overly committed, or perhaps watched too many穿越 dramas."

Xiaomei's Will

Xiaomei wrote in her notebook: Dad, Mom, I can't take care of you anymore, I'm leaving. My brother must take good care of Dad and Mom. My good sister (referring to Xiaohua) lost the key. She is afraid of being scolded when she goes home, so she wants to die. I've decided to die with her. You must stay calm. We cannot be born in the same year and month, but we wish to die in the same year and month. In this life, I have two secrets. One is to travel through time and space, go to the Qing Dynasty, and make a movie about the emperor. The other is to go to outer space.

Xiaohua's Will

According to the police officers and several villagers, Xiaohua's will read: Dad, Mom, Sister, I'm sorry, I'm leaving. You must stay healthy. Although my sister and I often argue, I love my sister very much. Having you as my sister is enough for this life. I also often make Mom unhappy, I'm really sorry. Sister, you must take good care of Dad and Mom.

Character Reconstruction

Xiaomei: Usually gentle and sensible

The reporter came to Xiaomei's house last night. Xiaomei's mother lay in bed, unable to see guests. The photos showed that Xiaomei was a girl with short hair and delicate features, revealing some liveliness. Her father Wang Zhixiong said that his daughter was usually very sensible and independent. He often taught her to take care of her younger brother, and she was very obedient. Wang Zhixiong said that he often went out to work, while his wife worked in Bai Shui, Longhai. The couple were often away from home, leaving Xiaomei and her younger brother to live with their grandparents.

Wang Zhixiong repeatedly checked the schoolbag he retrieved from Xiaohua's house, trying to find any words or phrases that would reveal his daughter's emotional process, but found nothing. "There is no diary, only textbooks, extracurricular books... and no report cards either. Her grades were average."

The last impression Wang Zhixiong has of his daughter remains that of a gentle and sensible child. "My daughter is the type who does what she says. She wouldn't say things lightly, maybe that's why she got herself into trouble..." Indeed, Xiaomei kept her promise to her desk mate, leaving behind immense sorrow for her loved ones.

Xiaohua: An optimistic child

Xiaohua's home is a newly built single-story small house. There is no decoration inside the house yet. This small house is located by the roadside. A local villager told the reporter that her family was not well-off. Her father made a living fishing at sea all year round, and there were three people in the family: Xiaohua, her sister, and her mother.

In the eyes of Xiaohua's cousin's mother, Wang Yanfeng, Xiaohua was very sensible and often helped her mother with household chores.

Yesterday afternoon, the reporter rushed to Xiaohua's home. Her relatives had all arrived and were sitting in the living room. Xiaohua's elder sister sat by her mother, who had fainted, crying bitterly. She was in the third year of junior high school at Qianting Middle School and only returned home once a week on Saturdays. This time, she was planning to buy something tasty for her younger sister, but never expected that her sister would be separated from her forever.

"Mom cried all night yesterday (March 1st) and fainted again this morning (March 2nd). She even received an IV drip this morning." Thinking about all this, Xiaohua's sister couldn't help but cry bitterly, "It's completely unexpected. My sister was a very optimistic person. Mom cared about her a lot and never blamed her for anything, so how could this happen..."

Department Statement

Actively organizing aftermath affairs

The reporter learned from the Qianting Police Station in Zhangpu County that this incident has been classified as a suicide event.

Relevant officials from Qianting Town introduced that after the incident, the director of the Education Bureau of Zhangpu County and others rushed to the scene to organize personnel to assist in rescue efforts, retrieve bodies, and console the families. Currently, the bodies of the two students have been transported to the funeral home.

Yesterday afternoon, the reporter went to Guogang Primary School for an interview, and the school was holding a meeting. The principal of the school introduced that they paid attention to the mental health education and counseling of students under normal circumstances. Such incidents were indeed unexpected. "Both of their academic performances were average, and I only learned about the situation after the incident happened."

Expert Opinion

Cultivating a healthy personality is very important

Whether it is schools or parents, they need to cultivate an optimistic attitude towards life in children. Teenagers in their teens face pressure from learning setbacks and interpersonal relationships among other aspects.

Teachers should carry out a caring education for children. Without love, there is no education. Therefore, when they make mistakes, they should be guided; when they deceive others, they should be accepted. Understanding, tolerance, and encouragement in both learning and psychology can naturally increase their self-confidence, improve their shortcomings, and enable them to positively face more pressures and difficulties in society and family in the future, laying a foundation for the future.

Children at this stage have poor self-control. Parents should play a guiding role in time management, sleep schedules, etc., helping them form good habits and personalities, which will naturally promote improvements in thinking, judgment, harmonious interpersonal relationships, and other aspects, which in turn cultivates the child's learning ability and helps them find life goals. Therefore, cultivating a good personality is very important.

Parents should also verbally encourage their children, believe in them, and believe that they can do better next time. Negative language can make children lack confidence and become overly cautious, which cannot promote their healthy growth but rather hinders the formation of a healthy personality. Therefore, parents should encourage and praise their children more, and correct educational methods can promote the healthy psychological development of children. (Reporters Zheng Ruiqing and Zhang Hanfeng from Southeast Network - Strait Herald, text/photos)
