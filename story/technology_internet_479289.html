During the exhibition, we will invite government agencies at home and abroad, senior experts and scholars to attend the forum and deliver special speeches. Enterprises can also apply to hold high-level seminars and new product/new technology launch conferences during the exhibition. Each seminar lasts for one hour, and domestic enterprises are charged 10,000 yuan per session (including venue for exchange, audio equipment, drinking water, etc.); High-standard technical exchanges will attract many professionals, providing an irreplaceable opportunity for the exchange of advanced technologies, equipment, and experiences between China and foreign countries. (Forum supporting units, please consult the organizing committee for details.)

Distributing admission tickets and invitation letters to professional users and merchants across the country and in neighboring countries and regions, the exhibition will be promoted and reported on in hundreds of professional newspapers, magazines, websites, radio stations, and TV stations at home and abroad, including Ariton Cranes.

Focusing on overseas promotion, comprehensively establishing contact with international authoritative media and dealers associations, timely releasing exhibition information and product information compilations, and providing humanized visiting and business negotiation services for overseas buyers. Showcasing mainstream premium products that reflect industry development trends, forming a brand cluster and strong synergy effect, achieving deeper industry influence and better trade results.

The 5th China International Exhibition of Lifting Machinery and Accessories will be held in Beijing in 2012. This exhibition focuses on safe production and improving work efficiency through innovation, development, exchange, and cooperation. It not only showcases the company image, new products, and new technologies for exhibitors but also directly facilitates commercial negotiations for technical development and equipment procurement personnel in urban construction, ports, steel industry, power, coal mines, transportation, metallurgy, logistics, industrial and agricultural production, lifting installation projects, and other fields.

Hosting high-level thematic report sessions and technical exchange meetings, inviting well-known figures from home and abroad to discuss modern hot technologies. Government agencies, design units, construction units, project owners, urban construction, port, steel industry, power, coal mine, transportation, metallurgy, logistics, industrial and agricultural production, lifting installation engineering field enterprise top decision-makers and procurement professionals are invited to attend. Cooperating with multiple industry websites, creating online exhibition halls and live broadcasts so that buyers who cannot be present can still query exhibitor information via the network, and providing free promotional activities on industry websites for three months before and after the exhibition.
