Shijiazhuang Orient Science and Technology Secondary Professional School

by xie11 on 2009-02-09 16:47:34

Combining work and study, the school runs on a model of work-study with first-class training facilities in the province, a simple, fast and accurate teaching model, fully closed quasi-military student management, complete employment placement, and the lowest tuition fees in the province.

Shijiazhuang Oriental Science and Technology Secondary Vocational School is a full-time comprehensive secondary vocational school approved by the Shijiazhuang Municipal Education Bureau and registered with the Hebei Provincial Department of Education. The school is located at No. 29 Shihuo South Road, Shijiazhuang City, Hebei Province, at the intersection of Xinhua Road and West Second Ring Road, with superior geographical conditions, convenient transportation, and a beautiful environment.

Our school has always adhered to the development philosophy of "emancipating mind, being pragmatic and innovative, and thinking diligently for change", constantly carrying out teaching and management reforms, firmly establishing the awareness of "market competition", taking the market as the guide, and using enterprise needs as the standard. We explore new ideas and methods for theoretical and practical teaching, accumulating rich teaching and management experience. After employment, our students have received widespread praise from employers.

First, with enterprise standards as the guide, we have equipped ourselves with first-class teaching facilities in the province.

Adequate and advanced teaching experimental equipment is the foundation and guarantee for doing a good job in vocational education. Our school has cumulatively invested more than 10 million yuan, building a numerical control (NC) training workshop equipped with over 50 NC lathes, NC milling machines, ordinary lathes, ordinary milling machines, etc., as well as an ordinary lathe training workshop. The school has also built supporting facilities such as communication electronics laboratories, piano rooms, electronic reading rooms, grinding rooms, body shape rooms, and new teaching buildings.

Second, with employment positions as the guide, we have created an enterprise-style teaching model.

Establishing a teaching model mainly based on practice, in the teaching process, all students are managed as enterprise employees. Through competitive job placements, students take on roles such as team leaders, line managers, and employees in their "work" and are evaluated through regular assessments and exams. Outstanding team leaders and technical experts are selected among the students. This effectively promotes students' enthusiasm and initiative in learning.

Each day's "work" situation is monitored by quality indicators, which are statistically analyzed monthly. Wages are calculated based on the scores obtained each month, consisting of workload + performance evaluation + work discipline + appearance + safe operation + bonuses, etc. This effectively motivates students' initiative to learn and enhances their competitive awareness, allowing them to master the knowledge and skills they have learned faster and better.

Through enterprise-style teaching reform, students can gradually experience and adapt to enterprise management models during their time at school, meeting the needs of employment positions and initially reaching enterprise employment standards. The goal of enterprise-style teaching is to gradually surpass enterprise employment standards.

Third, with enterprise demand as the guide, we have created a fully enclosed quasi-military student management system and formulated a daily norm for students and a manual for practical operations in vocational quality training.

Fully learning from enterprise employee management models, we cultivate qualified talents that enterprises welcome. Enterprises need not only skilled workers with strong technical abilities but also focus on employees' overall qualities, such as team spirit, obedience, execution ability, competitive awareness, etc. The cultivation and improvement of overall qualities require efforts and collaboration from all aspects of the school, and overall quality education runs throughout the entire learning process of the students. To enhance the overall quality of our students, our management mainly highlights two characteristics:

Characteristic Management One: Strict Quasi-Military Management

Our school adheres to the educational concept of "becoming a person first, then becoming talented," hiring retired military officers to specialize in quasi-military management of students. Starting from daily life systems, clothing, behavior, etiquette, and housekeeping, we help students develop good living habits, cultivate excellent organizational discipline, and quickly become disciplined talents with a good military demeanor.

Characteristic Management Two: Vocational Quality Education Management

During the students' learning process, we offer vocational quality courses divided into theoretical instruction and expansion practice, complementing and enhancing each other. Vocational quality theoretical instruction focuses on practical approaches, working on students' ideological transformation, while expansion training adopts "personal experiences" to consolidate teaching results, turning these vocational quality requirements into students' conscious behaviors.

For vocational quality expansion training, our school signs agreements with enterprises, sending our students in batches to enterprise positions under the accompaniment of school leaders and teachers. After a period of quasi-employee life, students can understand society and enterprises before truly starting their internships, entering a formal enterprise employee state in advance, laying a good foundation for adapting to employment positions.

Fourth, with employment as the guide, we have successfully completed employment placement work.

With the continuous improvement of our students' qualities and employment channels, we have widely established student internship and employment bases in the Pearl River Delta region, Beijing-Tianjin-Jinji area, and Jiaozhou Peninsula.

The employment direction of our school's CNC major is mainly in the automotive parts manufacturing industry. All student internship units are world-renowned mechanical manufacturing enterprises ranked among the top 500 globally. The professional alignment rate of student internships and employment reaches 90%, and the student employment satisfaction rate exceeds 95%.

Fifth, considerate后勤services, uniquely creating a "free meal system" for current students in the province.

All current students in our school implement a "free meal system" for three meals a day. On one hand, this reduces the burden on students' parents, and on the other hand, the school formulates scientific menus according to the physical development needs of students, fully ensuring the nutritional needs for students' physical development and adaptation to intensive studies, effectively avoiding adverse consequences caused by students eating outside the school.

The school has complete service facilities such as student dormitory buildings, canteens, supermarkets, phone booths, and bathrooms. The logistics management department always adheres to the principle of "service," striving to be proactive, thoughtful, intimate, and convenient.

Sixth, only miscellaneous fees are charged. The source of tuition and accommodation fees for three years.

Our school is a three-year secondary vocational school. With a strong sense of social responsibility, originating from society and giving back to society, relying on powerful educational capabilities and forward-thinking educational concepts, we save costs for students' parents to the maximum extent. Students coming to our school only need to pay three years of miscellaneous fees. The three years of tuition and accommodation fees are paid through the combination of work and study, i.e., vocational quality training. The school has also specifically established a "Hongzhi Class," fully or partially exempting all miscellaneous fees for academically excellent and highly comprehensive quality students from humble backgrounds. Our students enjoy a national scholarship of 1500 yuan per year provided by the government.

The extraction of "school culture" outlines the grand blueprint and fighting direction of our school. The practice of school culture is also the process of shaping our brand and further enhancing our influence.
