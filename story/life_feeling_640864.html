If you want to be a permanent couple, you need to study carefully.

by mimi456 on 2011-12-22 16:15:46

A friend felt that his wife was becoming increasingly lazy and selfish, and her temper was getting more and more irritable. Therefore, they quarreled every day, and in the end, the friend had an affair. The result was simple: divorce and remarriage. His ex-wife also quickly remarried. Because they had no children, they both entered new marriages relatively smoothly and adapted well. - In this new marriage, the previous third party became his new wife, but the warmth began to disappear completely. Once again, his home was filled with arguments just like before. The new wife didn't even do housework, leaving the friend to clean up everything inside and out. The friend thought he had bad luck, finding each subsequent wife worse than the last, and thus sighed every day.

One day, by chance at a dinner gathering, the friend met his ex-wife's current husband. Initially, the two men had nothing to say to each other, but after some polite conversation and a few drinks, the friend finally couldn't help but inquire about the man's marital status. This man, of ordinary appearance, spoke with great conviction: "She is a very good woman, extremely gentle and considerate, takes care of all the housework, loves me so much, and is generous and caring towards my relatives and friends. Such women are truly rare."

The friend said that at that moment, he felt as if his brain went "buzz," thinking, "Is she really that good? How did I not notice? Is he just flattering me for the sake of face?" - Not long after, coincidentally, the friend saw his ex-wife and her current husband shopping at the supermarket. The friend observed them from a distance for a long time and finally confirmed that they were indeed very happy. That happiness was etched on the ex-wife's radiant smile and hidden in the warm embrace of the man beside her.

It seems that whether a wife is an angel or a witch depends entirely on how the man shapes her. When a woman marries a good man, she makes a decision to live a good life with him. Her choice may bring her gains and losses; she might forever miss someone who loves her more. But if, after making such sacrifices, the man still doesn't cherish her and treats her like a housemaid, then she will truly become one.

Therefore, if you want your wife to always be an angel, you must first treat her like one. Even if her appearance and temperament aren't those of an angel, if you genuinely treat her as one, she will cultivate the disposition of an angel and gradually transform into one.

There are no born witches, only yellow-faced wives made later. A man who does not respect women, who doesn't understand women, cannot see the angel within her; he can only encounter a witch.

Although patience is a virtue in marriage, if there is love, there is no need for patience, only tolerance. When you find everything about your partner unappealing, she stops caring about being anything else. So, if you want an angelic woman, you should first place her on a pedestal in your heart. Because every woman called "wife" in this world has the potential to become an angel. You'll discover that a shift in your attitude can create a true angel.

This is both creation and maintenance, easier to achieve than patience.

What is a wife? She was once a stranger... a former friend... now a partner... a life companion... an emotional confidant... a lifetime summary. A woman is like a piano; when played by a master, it produces a famous tune. If played by an ordinary person, it might produce a popular song. And if played by someone who knows nothing, it might not produce any music at all. I believe this is a fitting metaphor for marriage. Being a good woman is really hard; a good woman isn't just a good mother! She should also be a good wife! A good daughter-in-law! As the saying goes, it's hard to please everyone, and doing everything perfectly is... really hard! Everything happens as it should, let it go, cherish what you have, and make the most of today, and that's enough! An individual's power is actually quite limited. There are many things in life we cannot change, so why not try changing yourself? Life is like a mirror; when you smile, it smiles back. Some people have endured many calamities together, shared tough times, and can easily recognize each other in the vast sea of humanity in this life. Others, due to mutual blame and resentment from a past life, must endure the hardships of searching and weathering storms to reunite in this life.

It's not easy for two lovers. Some must face gossip, others betrayal, some societal pressures, and others life-and-death decisions... But no matter what, their world of two is paradise, and their life together is their wish.

Too many beautiful moments are reasons for two people to come together. Can facing the trivialities of life and unpleasant arguments sever past affection and discard solemn vows?

Two people truly wish to grow old together, watching the sunset of life. Please remember: love is responsibility, and affection is inclusiveness!
