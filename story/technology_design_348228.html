For doing foreign trade, I only have a superficial understanding - this situation belongs to wings not yet strong, it is not recommended to go solo, as you will suffer losses.

Conditions: stable supply sources, a stable and solid customer base. Since you are going solo, there won't be a company's financial chain to support you, so the relationship with the factory at the source must be stable. Try to avoid situations where no deposit or delayed payment results in discontinued goods, otherwise, you will be dragged down. The relationship with your customers must also be solid; the credibility of both you and your customers is extremely important. Once the customer changes, you may fall into a situation where you ship the goods but cannot get paid.

2. Are there any good websites similar to China's "Taobao" that can sell things in English? - It seems you are not very professional, Taobao is a domestic retail product online platform. If you want to involve international business, it should be Alibaba, the parent company of Taobao.

3. I am not familiar with the entire work process of SOHO, could someone answer in detail! Thank you - It's very simple, find your own supply sources and customers, find an export agency company. Sign a contract with the export company, negotiate the settlement time, settlement rate, and agency fee, whether the required documents are handled on your behalf. Then negotiate the contract with your customer and place the order with the factory. You are responsible for managing the production progress and quality of the factory. When the goods are ready, under the customer's permission, the agency company will handle customs clearance and arrange shipment. The customer's payment is transferred to the agency company, which deducts the agency fee and pays you according to the agreed time. The factory issues the VAT invoice to the agency company, which pays the factory after receiving the payment from your overseas customer. If there are prepayment situations, you need to discuss with the agency company beforehand. Generally, the agency company does not advance payment.

Know English, have perseverance, be low-key (SOHO is illegal, fines will be imposed if found).
