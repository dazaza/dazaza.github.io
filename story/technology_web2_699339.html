Man rides self-made tricycle with 97-year-old mother to hang out for fun

by xue94fwsh on 2012-03-09 14:33:13

Yesterday, in the May Day Community on Tiebei West Road in He District of Luoyang City, a special tricycle caught many people's eyes. Attached to the bicycle was a wheelchair, in which sat an elated old lady. The elderly woman's name is Wang Xiulan and she is 97 years old. This vehicle was made for her by her 64-year-old son Peng Changshui as a "Happiness" exclusive car.

According to Peng Changshui, when his mother was young, she was a very active person. However, with age, she became physically unable to move around easily and could only walk around the courtyard. After some time, the old lady began to feel bored and expressed her desire to go outside. In the winter of 2006, Peng Changshui, who had a background in mechanical manufacturing, decided to make a vehicle for his mother that could be pushed or ridden and would also allow the elderly woman to get on and off easily.

Peng Changshui then thought of combining a bicycle with a wheelchair. To ensure stability, the wheelchair could only have one wheel, so it had to be custom-made. Peng Changshui rode his bicycle to the Luoyang building materials market to buy various tools and scoured scrap yards for steel. After three months, this "Happiness" vehicle finally came into being.

The first time they went out, the old lady was extremely delighted. Having not ventured far from home for over ten years, the old lady was highly spirited throughout the journey, constantly chatting with her son about how much things had changed: there were more buildings, taller buildings, more cars, and how lively the streets were. There was just too much to see.

The filial 64-year-old son said, "Wherever my mother wants to go, I will take her."

After experiencing the "benefits," Wang Xiulan became even busier. Every evening, she would discuss with her son, "Let's go out again tomorrow." Over the years, Peng Changshui has already taken his mother on countless tours around Luoyang city.

Each time they went out, when Peng Changshui got tired from riding, he would sit on the seat to rest. Only when he felt that the old lady was satisfied would he suggest heading back home. Peng Changshui laughed and said, "Once the old lady goes out, she doesn't want to come back, spending half a day on the road, so I always bring a pump whenever we go out."

Peng Changshui said that the older his mother gets, the more like a child she becomes. When the weather wasn't good and they couldn't go out, the old lady would show signs of unhappiness. Last winter, when the old lady kept insisting on going out, Peng Changshui patiently told her, "Wait until winter is over, and when it gets warmer, I'll take you out."

This year during the Spring Festival, the old lady discussed with Peng Changshui, "It's warm now, let's go out." Although it was still quite cold, Peng Changshui decided to take his mother to a temple fair to make her happy. The old lady was finally satisfied. Recently, the old lady mentioned, "The Luoyang Bridge was being expanded recently, I wonder what it looks like now." Peng Changshui laughed and said that what the old lady really meant was that she wanted to go out again.

Because the early spring weather was still rather cold, Peng Changshui could only take his mother around nearby areas recently. Yesterday, after helping his mother onto the vehicle, Peng Changshui adjusted her collar and said, "Today's weather is a bit cold." Worried that her son wouldn't take her out, the old lady quickly responded, "It's fine, not cold at all, I'm in great health!" making Peng Changshui laugh as well.

Peng Changshui said, "In the past, father was busy with work, and all the responsibilities fell on mother. Father passed away in his sixties, and mother endured a lot of hardship. I hope that mother can spend her twilight years peacefully and try to fulfill her requests. Wherever my mother wants to go, I will take her."

Peng Changshui added, "My mother is particularly attached to me. As long as I am not sleeping, she wants me to be with her. As long as I am by her side, the old lady feels secure."

The daily eating, drinking, and personal care of the old lady are all handled by Peng Changshui. Speaking of her son, the old lady smiled and said, "In the morning, my son helps me dress, and when it's mealtime, he brings the food to me. My son is filial, and there aren't many who can match him." (Editor: SN005)
