Safety and Environmental Protection; Straw Gasifier Sparks a Buying Frenzy

With rising prices, spending 300 yuan on gas every month is not a small expense. In rural areas, people directly burn straw for heating and cooking. Although burning straw does not cause poisoning, it poses hygiene issues and incomplete combustion wastes a large part of the energy. The current use of energy is very unreasonable. Therefore, solving this problem with the Jia Mei Straw Gasification Furnace is an urgent necessity.

This has led to rapid development in rural areas and towns. Since straw is abundant in rural areas, discarding it is wasteful, but keeping it can be cumbersome.
