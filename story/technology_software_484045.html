Maku Notes New Version PC Client Review Contest with Prizes (Pictures of Prizes Included)

by xdash on 2011-08-31 20:23:13

Previously, Sync Control had exposed the floating window tool of the new version of the MaiKu Notes PC client under development, which aroused everyone's interest and heated discussion. Today, the MaiKu team collaborates with Sync Control to launch a rewarded review event. You can now click here to download the new version of the client (initially limited to 100 downloads; if the download volume is large, it may be increased). After experiencing it, you can provide any opinions, suggestions, or bug feedback through blogs, spaces, microblogs, etc., and have the chance to win prizes provided by the official side.

Rewarded Review Event Rules:

Participants: All readers

Event Time: From now until 24:00 on August 29th (Monday)

Participation Requirements: Regarding 1-2 feature points of the new client, publish your opinions, suggestions, or bug feedback on personal blogs, spaces, microblogs, etc. The word count should be no less than 100 words. After publishing, please reply with the URL below this article, or email it to fanbingx[at]gmail.com, or send a private message via microblog to Sync Control/XDash (Please note "MaiKu Rewarded Review").

Winning Participants: The MaiKu official will select the top 10 review articles for quality and give away prizes. The list of winners will be announced on this site.

Prize Settings: A set of MaiKu official commemorative gifts (multi-functional notebook + note-taking mouse pad), a total of 10 sets will be given away.

Test Version Download Address: http://www.everbox.com/f/uWT6cFUARsjoBO59VdQUmG8NF2
