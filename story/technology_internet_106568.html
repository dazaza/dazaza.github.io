Mobile Internet will enter the spring.

by langmzz on 2008-11-26 22:13:25

Beijing, November 26, 2008 /PRNewswire-Asia/ -- The 2008 Olympics have just ended and the NBA has become another focus of attention in China. Compared to TOM obtaining the hosting rights for the NBA Internet official website this season, the live broadcasting of the NBA in the wireless Internet domain had already begun. Since the beginning of the 07 season, Kongzhong Corporation spent several million US dollars to obtain the hosting rights for the NBA mobile official website, becoming an industry focus. Nowadays, well-known websites such as 3G Gate, 3G Tencent, and Sina are not willing to be left behind. They have successively opened NBA channels on their wireless Internet portals, providing information, reviews, text and image live broadcasts and other services related to bypassing copyright restrictions, determined to get a share of the NBA mobile live broadcast market.

Despite the impact of the economic crisis causing some network operators to take action, according to gateway data from China Mobile, the live broadcast data during the arrival of the NBA season already accounts for one quarter or even more of the traffic of several mainstream wireless Internet portals.

Accessing the Internet via mobile devices will soon become the main way of going online. Telecommunications restructuring, 3G, and wireless cities all make people look forward to the future prospects of mobile Internet. Mobile Internet and traditional Internet are moving towards integration. In the future, there will be more and more Internet content transferred to mobile phones, and WAP 2.0 as a technology that facilitates the transfer of content between the two will certainly play an important role. With the development of 3G and wireless networks, mobile Internet is about to enter the WAP 2.0 era. Therefore, the development prospects of China's mobile Internet are also optimistic. On one hand, traditional Internet companies like Sina and Tencent have launched upgraded versions of WAP 1.0 based on Internet content, partially realizing the functions of WAP 2.0.

Some website construction companies have also timely launched mobile website construction. For example, E Road Fengxing Network has specifically launched a mobile network column with its professional technology and forward-looking strategic thinking. Therefore, we also have reason to believe that mobile Internet will surely become a small grass growing vigorously in the winter of the Internet.
