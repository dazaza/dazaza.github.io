"Chain switchboard" becomes a new hotspot for chain franchising

by bwmei5750 on 2009-12-07 18:35:14

Recently, an innovative interactive marketing platform tailored for chain enterprises, integrating the telecommunications network, the Internet, and the mobile network, has emerged. This brand-new communication service for chain enterprises - "Chain Switchboard" - jointly launched by Beijing Kobee Interactive Information Technology Co., Ltd. and China Unicom, allows you to simply dial 4006006565, state the name of a particular enterprise, and you will immediately be connected to your target customer.

According to information obtained from the headquarters of "Kobee Interactive" in Beijing, the "Chain Switchboard," as an innovative national distributed call center platform, provides chain enterprise customers with brand communication, call distribution, customer management, and targeted marketing services. It helps chain enterprise customers build an online marketing platform covering the telecommunications network, the Internet, and the mobile network with the least investment. For example, by dialing 4006006565 and calling out "Little Fatty Sheep," no matter which city you are in, you only need to pay local call charges, and you will instantly be connected to "Little Fatty Sheep's" franchise customer service center.

For a long time, chain enterprise brand marketing has been plagued by various problems: such as potential franchisees remembering the brand but forgetting the contact number when they want to find the chain enterprise, thereby causing the chain enterprise to lose potential business opportunities. Now, the "Chain Switchboard" is a customized solution for chain enterprise brand marketing. Franchisees can directly call the "enterprise brand/business name" through the 4006006565 platform to conveniently find the registered enterprise and connect the call.

Currently, the "Chain Switchboard" has been officially put into use and is now soliciting chain enterprise brands for the chain industry. In the future, as the business expands, the Chain Switchboard will focus on developing chain integrity brand enterprise customers, building a trustworthy multifunctional telephone interconnection platform for chain enterprises and potential franchisees.

According to statistics, 28.6% of a company's advertising budget is spent on promoting the enterprise sales/service telephone number, yet such high expenses do not bring good results. For instance, through advertisements, the brands of Little Fatty Sheep and Quanjude have become deeply rooted in people's hearts, but few ordinary franchisees can remember the service phone numbers of these two chain enterprises. If chain enterprises use the Chain Switchboard service, franchisees can use the very easy-to-remember number 4006006565 to directly call the enterprise brand and connect the call, without needing the franchisees to memorize complicated numbers, greatly shortening the communication distance between chain enterprises and potential franchise customers.
