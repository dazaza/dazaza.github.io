[Forward] If someone around you has a bad stomach, copy it down, it will be useful.

by john947578 on 2011-05-17 10:41:44

I admit.

I don't mind accommodating you.

I can keep looking up to you.

But, do you really never look at me?

I'm here! I'm here! Can you hear me?

I just want to ask, are you deaf or blind? Why are you so indifferent?

Sometimes I wonder,

What exactly do I like about you?

What am I waiting for from you?

Maybe what can't be obtained is truly the best.

Or perhaps, I just haven't met someone better than you.

Do I really have to wait until I forget you?

Do I really have to wait until I give up on you?

Do I really have to wait until I leave you?

Only then will you truly realize how important I am to you.

You don't know how sad I get sometimes.

You don't know how tiring it is to wait without a response.

You don't know how much courage it took for me to remain unforgettable.

Or maybe, you're not unaware, you're just pretending to be.

You're so selfish. I'm so foolish.

Over and over again.

Cried myself out, fell silent.

Wanted to give up, grew distant.

But once time passes, I still end up missing you.

Can't let go, can't forget, can't quit, can't walk away.

The people around me feel heartache.

Everyone nearby tries to console me.

Perhaps only you remains so indifferent.

And perhaps only I continue to be so devoted.

What should I say?

Is it one willing to hit and the other willing to take it?

I've been waiting for someone,

Waiting for a look back,

Waiting for a smile,

Until finally, I've lost my own smile.

Isn't it enough yet? I'm really tired.

But why is it that no matter which way I turn, I can't escape the circle of loving you.

I don't expect to find happiness with you,

Nor do I dare to imagine any sacrifices from you.

I just thought, since I've made the decision, I would follow through without hesitation.

Ironically, you didn't even give me the chance to follow through without hesitation.

I tell myself,

To make myself leave you.

I tell myself,

This is the last time I cry.

Many things have boundaries,

Often even the strongest people get tired.

I'm not a real fool, I was just willingly so for you.

And now, I've finally found a way to exit this unfair game.

Occasionally,

You'll reach out to me, contact me.

Your sudden appearance still stirs something inside me.

But now, I've learned to put up a front with you, neither warm nor cold, indifferent.

I laugh carelessly, and won't shed those cheap tears anymore.

Then I hear you softly say, "You've changed."

I don't know whether I should laugh or cry.

It doesn't matter anymore, does it?

Just suddenly,

Seeing a similar figure,

Hearing a familiar voice,

I involuntarily fall into memories.

But slowly, I've also learned to let go.

It's not that I've changed, it's that I'm truly powerless, I've conceded.

I can't keep going like this anymore.

Suddenly,

When I hear those songs,

I suddenly think of you.

Where will you be?

Are you happy or feeling wronged?

Whenever I hear such lyrics, I always unconsciously think of us.

But, I know, we are not who we were, and what we wanted before isn't what we want now.

So, after a long while, if we meet again,

Faced with such an embrace, I probably won't have any significant emotional stirrings.

Even if there's chaos inside, I'll pretend to be calm.

All my former passion has eventually been drained by you.

Blame you? No, I don't blame you, I don't blame anyone, there's no one to blame.
