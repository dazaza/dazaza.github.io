Huangjing, as a famous Chinese town for polyester texturing, has in recent years not only seen rapid development in its traditional industries but also in its private enterprises. Its GDP, industrial production and sales, and the total volume of private economy all account for 1% of Suzhou's total. The development of its economy and society shows vigorous vitality. Currently, Huangjing is at a stage where its industrial cluster is transforming into an industrial base. The hosting of the first Texturing Festival will play a positive role in further expanding Huangjing's influence and promoting economic development.

"Huang" means jade, "Jing" refers to water systems; Huangjing implies a place with excellent Feng Shui. Located in the northeastern part of Taicang City, Jiangsu Province, Huangjing Town is a brilliant pearl on the south bank of the Yangtze River estuary. Polyester texturing is the dominant industry in Huangjing. The town has over 1000 various texturing production units, more than 1800 texturing machines, and produces over 1 million tons of textured polyester filament annually, accounting for about 20% of China's output and 10% of the world's output, making it the world's largest texturing industry cluster.

"The world of texturing, texturing for the world." More than four hundred domestic and international equipment suppliers, raw material suppliers, and customers from countries such as Sweden, Germany, France, and Japan gathered today in Taicang Huangjing Town, known as the famous Chinese town for polyester texturing, to witness the grand opening of the first China Huangjing Polyester Texturing Festival themed "China Huangjing, Silk Connecting the World."

The polyester texturing industry chain in the Huangjing area has extended upstream and downstream, forming a complete industrial chain from spinning, texturing, weaving, dyeing and finishing to garments. It is gradually establishing support systems for quality inspection, specialized markets, product research and development, brand shaping, and information services. Huangjing Town's industrial economy, characterized by private and specialty economies, is booming, and its economic pattern characterized by the polyester texturing industry is becoming increasingly evident.

It is understood that the texturing equipment in Taicang Huangjing Town comes from developed countries such as Germany, Japan, France, and Italy, reaching international advanced levels, making it akin to a world exposition of texturing machines. Shenjiu Chemical Fiber, with a total investment of 2.5 billion yuan, and Tongkun Chemical Fiber, a leading domestic textile enterprise, have both joined Huangjing. The well-known down clothing company, Yalu Group, has also invested in the texturing industry.

At the opening ceremony, Academician Ji Guobiao of the Chinese Academy of Engineering presented his personally inscribed title "China's Famous Town for Polyester Texturing - Huangjing" to Huangjing Town. Additionally, there was a unveiling ceremony for the Huangjing Texturing Market. Representatives of the first batch of units and enterprises entering the Huangjing Texturing Market held signing ceremonies.

Mr. Chen Shujin, Vice President of the China National Textile and Apparel Council, stated that the town has made significant contributions to the development of China's textile industry. The achievements made from one strand of textured filament are impressive, and the spirit of the people of Huangjing is admirable. Due to the development of the polyester texturing industry, the town's overall economy has been promoted, enabling the residents to realize their dreams of wealth and laying a solid foundation for building a new socialist countryside.
