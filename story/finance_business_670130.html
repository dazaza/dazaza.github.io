IBM Successfully Builds Enterprise Collaboration Platform for Shanghai Mitsubishi Elevator, Creating Another Business Miracle

by qiuman8013 on 2012-02-20 15:48:09

IBM announced that Shanghai Mitsubishi Elevator Co., Ltd. (hereinafter referred to as "Shanghai Mitsubishi Elevator") has successfully built an enterprise collaboration platform by choosing IBM Lotus Domino. This helps achieve office automation within the company and realizes seamless connection with core business applications, penetrating into various core links such as production, sales, and services. It effectively boosts the enterprise's productivity. The continuous innovation technology of IBM Lotus Domino software, along with its flexible deployment and powerful secondary development capabilities, provides solid technical support for the construction, upgrade, and expansion of the enterprise collaboration platform, providing core competitiveness for Shanghai Mitsubishi Elevator to maintain its leading position in the industry.

The concept of "large-scale collaboration" supports high-speed development. In 1987, Shanghai Electric Machinery Co., Ltd. and Japan's Mitsubishi Electric formed a joint venture with four parties to establish Shanghai Mitsubishi Elevator Co., Ltd., mainly focusing on the manufacture, sale, and installation maintenance services of elevators. Initially, the company had about 1,800 employees and produced more than 800 elevators annually. After more than twenty years of development, the current number of employees is approximately 2,000 people, producing over 30,000 elevators annually, with annual revenue exceeding 8 billion yuan. It is one of the largest elevator manufacturing and sales enterprises in China and ranks first worldwide in single-factory elevator production. Its product market share has maintained a leading position in China's elevator market for more than ten consecutive years, and it has ranked among the top in key economic indicators in China's elevator industry for many consecutive years.

As a major characteristic of the elevator manufacturing industry, each elevator is composed of hundreds of complex parameters such as load capacity, operating speed, floors, and operation methods. Whether it be production, planning, or later maintenance, all these factors are complex, determining that each elevator is an independent and complex product. Elevator manufacturers must produce elevators according to orders based on different user needs. How does Shanghai Mitsubishi Elevator achieve a significant increase in production while maintaining almost the same personnel scale? To a large extent, this can be attributed to the highly developed information construction of Shanghai Mitsubishi Elevator and the concept of large-scale collaboration throughout the entire enterprise core business system.

Shanghai Mitsubishi Elevator started planning and comprehensively promoting information construction in 1993. In 1998, Shanghai Mitsubishi Elevator found that although the ERP system could effectively realize the application and integration of finance, sales, production, and logistics, the cost of implementing the ERP system was relatively high, with expensive user licensing fees. If all processes were to use the ERP system, it would require a huge capital investment. Additionally, the company needed to carry out some custom development of business processes in the informatization aspect of office management. Therefore, Shanghai Mitsubishi Elevator considered building a more suitable system platform for office collaboration, process design and development, providing a more friendly user experience and better cost-performance ratio to effectively integrate with the ERP system, thereby optimizing the office process.

After a series of research and evaluations, Shanghai Mitsubishi Elevator ultimately chose IBM and Shanghai Tongxun Technology Co., Ltd.'s collaboration solution based on Lotus Domino. Regarding this, Xu Liang, IT Manager of Shanghai Mitsubishi Elevator Co., Ltd., said: "As the earliest software to introduce intelligent office features, IBM Lotus has mature and complete product lines that can provide email systems and comprehensive office platforms for enterprises; it has a powerful development platform, making it very convenient for us or our partners to carry out secondary development; additionally, it has excellent openness, enabling integration and consolidation with different business systems; from the perspective of product sustainable development, Lotus software has a reliable, continuously innovative, and developing technical background, and IBM's global technical team has provided strong technical support, which is a very important guarantee factor for us."

Shanghai Mitsubishi Elevator has cumulatively invested 100 million yuan in information construction, and the system application has comprehensively penetrated into every link of the company's operation and management, including marketing, production and manufacturing, installation maintenance services, finance, and management. Especially in process management, efficiency enhancement, and process control, information technology plays a crucial role. After more than ten years of information construction, Shanghai Mitsubishi Elevator has formed an efficient and unified integrated system, covering OA office automation, MES manufacturing execution system, PDM product data management, CRM customer relationship management, SRM supplier relationship management, BI business intelligence, and ERP enterprise resource planning, encompassing all core application systems of the enterprise. Information success fully covers more than forty branches nationwide. The collaboration platform based on Lotus Domino efficiently integrates various core business systems, and the data integrated in various application systems flows through collaboration, ensuring that any production information and status of each elevator from sales, order generation, to manufacturing execution, data management, and customer service can receive timely and accurate feedback and tracking. Throughout the product lifecycle, information at both the front-end and back-end of the business can be shared, and business processes become more standardized. Optimizing the management process improves the daily office work and decision-making efficiency of Shanghai Mitsubishi Elevator, significantly enhancing the enterprise's response speed and decision-making ability, effectively strengthening the standardization of management, and the efficient operation of business processes greatly enhances the production efficiency of Shanghai Mitsubishi Elevator, allowing it to smoothly deliver a single elevator within thirty days, giving Shanghai Mitsubishi Elevator a leading advantage in the industry.

At the beginning of OA construction, Shanghai Mitsubishi Elevator clearly established the concept of "large-scale collaboration" for collaborative manufacturing construction. Over the past twelve years, the scale of the enterprise's business has continuously expanded, and higher requirements have been placed on informatization and collaboration. Following the planning of "unified planning, phased implementation," the collaboration platform of Shanghai Mitsubishi Elevator has steadily developed. Through long-term accumulation and phased implementation, it continuously improves and enhances according to business needs, developing more business modules on the Lotus Domino platform, expanding collaboration into more business processes. As an industry-leading collaboration software, Lotus Domino has played a huge role in the construction and upgrading of the collaboration platform due to its strong stability, high developability, and high scalability. Now, the collaboration platform of Shanghai Mitsubishi Elevator runs through the entire business process of the enterprise, integrating marketing, production and manufacturing, engineering services, quality management, and comprehensive office work into a whole. With the help of the enterprise collaboration platform, Shanghai Mitsubishi Elevator continues to optimize management, improve standardization, and enhance the enterprise's production efficiency, response speed, and decision-making ability. Li Yudong, manager of IBM partner Shanghai Tongxun Technology Co., Ltd., said: "The collaborative project of Shanghai Mitsubishi Elevator has surpassed the traditional concepts of workflow and office automation. Its collaborative office work has gradually penetrated into various aspects of production and organically integrated with marketing management, production automation, and service management. It can be said that this will be the direction of OA development."

Shanghai Mitsubishi Elevator considers the issue of informatization from the height of enterprise development and the perspective of the manufacturing industry, deploying informatization construction with foresight and practicality. Relying on the collaboration platform built by IBM Lotus Domino, combined with the characteristics of the elevator industry to develop application modules, it incorporates collaboration into the entire business process, solving the problem of seamless linking and smooth operation of internal business processes within the enterprise, making internal communication more timely and efficient. Xu Liang, IT Project Manager of Shanghai Mitsubishi Elevator, said: "For an enterprise, if various resources can be reasonably applied, that is a perfect system. I believe that the separate application of ERP or OA cannot solve everything; they must be organically combined, which can better adapt to the advancement of informatization." Under this concept of large-scale collaboration, Shanghai Mitsubishi Elevator uses collaboration as a means to form an organic whole of enterprise business systems such as ERP, MES, and PDM throughout the entire enterprise, playing a truly efficient and significant role, ultimately converting technical advantages into the core competitiveness of the enterprise, ensuring that Shanghai Mitsubishi Elevator becomes the world's largest single-factory elevator manufacturer.
