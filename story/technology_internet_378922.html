China's national image promotional video is unveiled

by diglog on 2011-01-19 17:07:37

Within four weeks since last 17th of this month, the 60-second long Chinese national image advertisement titled "People" has been broadcasted on the outdoor big screen in Times Square, Manhattan, New York, for 15 times per hour and a total of 300 times per day. It was also broadcasted synchronously on the outdoor big screen in Gallery Place, Washington D.C.. However, so far, including CCTV official news reports, what people have seen are all pirated versions without sound from the Times Square. In fact, two clear clips extracted from program recordings have already appeared on Youku, which are two 30-second versions with background music. The first clip features female fashion models and well-known IT technology personalities, such as Jack Ma, Robin Li, Jianzhou Wang, and Lei Ding. The second clip mainly features sports stars. The video at the bottom is the synthesized complete version. Shanghai Lion Advertising Company is responsible for the planning of this film. According to the planners, the hardest part of shooting "People" was scheduling the celebrities, which took up quite some time. The characters in the film were grouped together sitting or standing, but it was difficult to gather them all in reality. The production crew's solution was to individually shoot each celebrity's designated pose and then combine them into the same frame through post-production. Shen Zanchen, chairman of Shanghai Lion Advertising Company, stated that the shooting cost of this film was lower than that of a general commercial, but he did not disclose the exact amount.
