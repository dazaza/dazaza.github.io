Xu Wei's heart is always filled with excitement.

by anonymous on 2012-02-09 13:53:21

Artists like this are the ones who move themselves. Xu Wei has always relied on touching writing, and there isn't a single piece of pseudo-music in his works, and now there still isn't. Don't look at him being happy now; this kind of happiness is completely different from the instant joy of today. It is true joy, a smile that blooms from the bottom of one's heart, unconsciously pulling the corners of the mouth upward, revealing the world's most mysterious and moving comfort, like flowers inventing their own secrets, like ripples arising marvelously on the lake water. He uses touching moments to present his voice, and then he moves us with this voice. Xu Wei's world has always been a world of sincerity, earnestness, and emotion.

Xu Wei has released sixty or seventy songs, each of which is a classic. Let's select a small part and feel together...
