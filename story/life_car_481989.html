Car beauty requires environmental protection

So-called car beauty refers to the aesthetic, practical, and comfortable visual experience of a car's exterior and interior.

Who proposed the concept of car beauty? It was Turtle Wax, known as the "ace wax in the automobile kingdom."

As early as 1992, Turtle Wax first introduced the concept of car beauty and popularized car beauty knowledge and lectures nationwide, while also opening car maintenance training classes.

Over more than a decade, Turtle Wax, especially its Turtle Super Hard Shell Wax, has dominated the American market and led the car wax market.

Its sales in the car wax market have ranked first year after year, with a market share exceeding 60%. With the increasing exchange and deepening of the global economy, Turtle Wax's sales in various countries around the world have remained consistently high.

Cars are like a girl's face; they also need care. In the late 1990s, when people began paying attention to car maintenance, Turtle Wax once again proposed the new concept of "environmentally friendly beauty products."

Cars not only need beauty but also require environmentally friendly beauty products. Take the car wax available on the market now for example. Many car beauty shops use very low-quality car wax for car owners, which costs about 5 yuan per box, nearly 30 yuan to maintain one car, and a box of wax can be used to maintain 7-10 cars. Therefore, non-standard car beauty shops make huge profits but ignore the lifespan of the car.

Using such car wax for maintenance will not only fail to provide proper care but also significantly reduce the lifespan of the car body and damage the car paint.

Therefore, Turtle Wax reminds all car owners and car beauty shops to choose good quality car wax and branded car wax for car maintenance; otherwise, it will result in losses outweighing gains.
