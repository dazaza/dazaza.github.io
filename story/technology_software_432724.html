Overview:

In 2004, Dongfeng Peugeot's first "Blue Box" was born at the Shuanglong Yihetong Auto Exhibition Center in Beijing. Established the same year, Huijing Kemane is currently China's largest Dongfeng Peugeot 4S store and one of the world's largest Dongfeng Peugeot 4S stores. With the establishment of Tianzhi Long, Beijing now has seven "Blue Boxes."

"Blue Box" is a synonym for Dongfeng Peugeot 4S stores, named for its distinctive "blue box" architectural design. The deep Mediterranean blue perfectly embodies the new European style. In France, the "Blue Box" has gone through several generations of evolution to form its current appearance, becoming the globally unified iconic architectural form of Dongfeng Peugeot brand 4S stores.

Beijing's seven "Blue Boxes" strictly adhere to Dongfeng Peugeot's unified standard specifications while each having its own creative characteristics. Some have complete and luxurious entertainment facilities, some have advanced hardware facilities, and some host many events. Appropriate differentiation attracts more customers and makes the "Blue Box" even more famous.

Manufacturer Appearance:

"Blue Box" implies global service unification

He Wei, Sales Director of Dongfeng Peugeot Beijing Region: Dongfeng Peugeot currently has seven authorized dealers in Beijing. Beijing is our key market nationwide, and our annual sales are also the largest among all cities nationwide. We currently have no plans to expand the dealer network in Beijing. For each dealer, the manufacturer has both a unified strict standard specification and encourages them to actively create their own characteristics.

The 120 standards introduced by Dongfeng Peugeot from Peugeot France are very comprehensive and detailed, with strict regulations on various aspects of the dealer's hardware and software. The manufacturer conducts follow-up visits with new customers regarding whether the dealer has reached these standards, detailed to whether the customer is satisfied with a certain piece of hardware, whether the service personnel proactively offered coffee, and whether the children enjoyed themselves. Each item has four options: completely satisfied, fairly satisfied, average, and dissatisfied. However, only if the customer chooses completely satisfied can the dealer score points. Those ranking in the top 20 nationwide will receive rewards, while those in the bottom 40 will face penalties such as deduction of rebates.

Currently, in Changchun, there are three Dongfeng Peugeot 4S stores dealing in imported Peugeots in Beijing, but this is not authorized by the manufacturer due to some historical reasons. It is still unclear whether domestically produced and imported Peugeots will be sold under the same network in the future. However, according to national policies, only those authorized by the manufacturer will be able to sell imported vehicles in the future.

Vehicle Condition Update:

Peugeot 206 1.4L arriving mid-September

Currently, the supply of various models of the Peugeot 206 1.6L and 307 at each Dongfeng Peugeot 4S store in Beijing is relatively sufficient. The newly launched Peugeot 206 1.4L is expected to arrive around mid-September.

"The 206 1.4L in other regions has already started supplying goods, but the Beijing market needs to install OBD, so it will take approximately until mid-September," said Ge Chunyuan, Deputy General Manager of Dongfeng Peugeot Huijing Kemane Exclusive Store. As of August 10, the store had already received over 30 orders.

The manufacturer has increased the exclusive supply of goods to Beijing. Currently, all colors of the four styles of the 206 1.6L are available.

Additionally, according to Ge Chunyuan, currently, all stores in Beijing only have four new 307 models: 1.6 manual basic type, 1.6 automatic basic type, 2.0 manual, and 2.0 top configuration. Due to low demand for other models, the manufacturer has reduced supplies, and none of the stores have inventory. Booking requires 1 to 1.5 months. Silver and black inventories are more abundant, while red and Aegean blue inventories are less.

At Dongfeng Peugeot Shuanglong Refined Exclusive Store, the inventory of the 206 and 307 is also relatively sufficient. For imported vehicles, colors such as green, silver, and red for the 206CC, crystal silver and champagne for the 407, and crystal silver for the 407SW are available. Other colors, as well as the 307CC and 307SW, require booking, and the required time depends on the manufacturer's supply situation.
