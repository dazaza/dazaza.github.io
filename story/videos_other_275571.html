How to wear a suit? Kobe will teach you!

by 6mks616n5h on 2009-11-24 15:51:35

Kobe, this name has undeniable influence on the NBA courts. Similarly, Kobe, this name can also start a charm wind in the fashion circle. When Kobe wears a suit, his tall figure not only represents awe-inspiring momentum, but also infinite gentlemanly atmosphere. Let's take a look at what benefits Kobe's suit styling can bring us. This article is exclusively integrated and published by NetEase Women's Channel. Please indicate the source for reprinting!

1. Tie + Suit + Shirt: All three matching methods are applicable. Comment: Kobe's suit matching method is a typical two-pattern one-solid matching method, straight pattern suit + square diagonal pattern tie, the overall directionality is very coordinated. Tips: 1. Three-solid matching method. The tie, suit, and shirt are three solids, which is the safest and most practical matching method. 2. Two-solid one-pattern matching method. Whether it is a shirt, tie or suit with patterns or patterns, the color of the pattern or pattern must be one of the other two colors. 3. Two-pattern one-solid matching method. If you wear a straight stripe suit or shirt, avoid using a straight or horizontal stripe tie, and use a diagonal stripe, dot, or ameba color tie without directionality.

2. Suit buttoning method: Very particular. Comment: Dark blue suit + pink shirt, steady but not stiff, one of the principles of matching is to fasten one of the two buttons. Tips: Double-breasted suit buttons should be fastened in any formal occasion, otherwise it will give people a sense of frivolity and instability. For single-breasted suit with two buttons, fasten the top one; for three buttons, fasten the top two. Of course, single-breasted suits can also be unbuttoned, with the lapel open, giving people a sense of ease and elegance.

3. Suit pants fit should be moderate, the length extends to 1/2 of the heel. Comment: A unified dark color system suit matching makes the body appear taller. If you are like Kobe, with a very long leg proportion, then the suitability of the suit pants becomes particularly important. Tips: In natural breathing, if it is not loose or tight and just fits one hand, this indicates that the waistband is suitable. The adjustable range of the waistband, when modified smaller, can only be within 5 centimeters, and when larger, cannot exceed 3.8 centimeters. The length of the pants from the back should be exactly at the junction of the heel and the shoe upper. If you want your legs to look longer, the length of the trouser leg can also extend to 1/2 of the heel.

4. Wide shoulders and thick arms can enhance the width of the waist. Comment: Kobe is a typical basketball player with wide shoulders and long arms, and the hard-edged design of the shoulders adds more masculinity. Tips: If a man's shoulders are too wide relative to his arms, you need to increase the width of the waist, such as choosing covered pockets to increase the width, avoiding wider collars or boat-shaped collars. If your shoulders are still slanted, you can use some shoulder pads. If your arms are thick and short, you can make your cuff length longer than before and reduce the width of the cuff fold. Try not to have decorations on the arm, which will make it look longer visually.

5. Tie selection: Color and texture are the key. Comment: A dark blue coat gives people a sense of stability, and a slightly darker tie becomes the best transition between the light blue shirt and the suit. Tips: Wearing dark blue or dark green suits, orange-yellow, cream, light blue, rose-colored ties are suitable, and such wearing will give people a sense of deep and restrained beauty. In autumn and winter, the colors should be mainly warm, such as deep red, coffee-like warm tones that will produce a sense of warmth in vision. In spring and summer, cold tones can be the main focus, with warm tones as a supplement.

6. "Handkerchief pocket" and shirt cuffs should slightly show a small edge. Comment: Pay attention to Kobe's suit, both the handkerchief pocket and the shirt cuffs are slightly showing a small edge. Don't underestimate these small details, they are also the key to dressing well. Tips: When wearing a suit, the shirt cuffs should be exposed about 1 centimeter, and the shirt collar should be higher than the suit collar by 0.5 centimeters to protect the suit collar and add beauty. The small pocket on the upper garment is called the "handkerchief pocket", which only places folded flat handkerchiefs and shows a shallow small edge, except for this, it is not suitable to place other things, to maintain your gentlemanly demeanor.
