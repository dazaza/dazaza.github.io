【Introduction to Jiang Taigong's Island Game】

"Jiang Taigong's Island", as the producer Liuliu's new attempt at the future of independent games after 7 years of creation. It will present unique stories and battle methods to a wide range of players for an unprecedented gaming experience. Compared to traditional RPG games, "Jiang Taigong's Island" has many revolutionary creative elements:

• Characteristic Battle System

The game features a distinctive battle system different from ordinary RPGs. Its new system combines the fun of strategy games with the unique concept of "territory," making battles full of strategic thinking from strategy games and the tension and excitement of real-time games.

• Rich Game Levels

The game provides extremely rich levels. Even the same characters can play different roles under different level conditions. How to reasonably utilize the characteristics of the level to maximize the potential of the characters will be one of the highlights of the gameplay.

• Realism-filled Storyline

"Jiang Taigong Island"? Zhang Likun? Li Gang? Dr. Aoi? These "familiar" characters will bring their special meanings and time-travel to the Yuan Dynasty, creating a different kind of exciting legend. This is also a game that will make players constantly reflect on the era they live in, just like other earlier domestic games such as "Beijing Floating Life" etc., filled with the creators' unrestrained mockery, satire, reflection, and vision of the era we live in during the game process.

In addition, "Jiang Taigong's Island" also has a theme song sung by professional singers, beautifully performed by singer Qingwan with a strong classical style.

Game duration: about 4 hours

Plot summary:

At the end of the Yuan Dynasty, Japanese pirates attacked Jiang Taigong's island. Patriotic youths like Zhang Likun and Chang Yuchun spontaneously sailed to Jiang Taigong's island to defend their country. During the fighting on the island, they encountered various perplexing events and met people with different backgrounds: righteous fox demons, uncles willing to be collaborators and little girls, cold-blooded sword saints... What awaits them ahead? This is an era of revival and prosperity, but also an era when the four cardinal principles of Huaxia have been forgotten by its descendants... About the author:

The producer of "Jiang Taigong's Island", Liu Liu (real name Liu Xiaoyu), is a Tsinghua University alumnus along with another well-known domestic independent game "Soulframe" author Liang Qiwei. The 66RPG forum he founded has always been a backbone force in the domestic RPGMaker community. Since its launch in June 2005, the 66RPG forum has faced numerous difficulties in its development process. Despite this, 66RPG has now become the largest domestic exchange, cooperation, and sharing website based on RPGMaker amateur game production.

From 2005 to now, a large number of domestic independent game fan-made works familiar to players have been born on 66RPG, such as "Soulframe", "Fighting Emblem", "Dark Holy Sword Legend", etc. These games are all filled with the passion and enthusiasm of the creators. And "Jiang Taigong's Island" is the latest gift from the 66RPG forum to players in 2011.

Author's words:

Even now, the vast majority of people still think that computer games are just entertaining little toys, or at most toys that can be used to communicate with others (online games). Like other art forms when they first appeared in a certain region (such as Indian films today, Japanese home video games in the late 80s, etc.), they initially only had entertainment functions. However, as the industry progresses, some of these art forms with great potential will evolve into carriers for expressing the artistic concepts of their creators. "Jiang Taigong's Island" is such a game.

Nowadays, we live in a good era of economic prosperity, but also in a bad era where parents dare not let their children go to and from school on their own. We shed tears and donate money for natural disasters inland, but we also coldly or even fearfully avoid elderly people who fall by the roadside. "Jiang Taigong's Island" was born in such an environment, and the restlessness and confusion of our generation of young people have also been integrated into the game's storyline. Behind each character in the storyline and every metaphorical plot segment symbolizes a perspective of our society. Players of different ages will have completely different understandings of these plots: younger players who haven't yet left school may only see the blood and battles, while those who have already worked in society for a few years may see a profound sigh that runs throughout the entire game.

In the past, no one in our country ever made games this way, and it might be that no one will make games this way again in the short term. This is not a perfect work, but it is a sincere effort. I hope my efforts will gain your approval.
