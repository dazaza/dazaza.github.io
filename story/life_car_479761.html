Latest Investment Entrepreneurship Plan for Car Beauty Shop

I have been engaged in the investment of car beauty shops for three years, and I am still investing. This industry has enormous potential for development. Through several years of entrepreneurship experience, I have also written many investment entrepreneurship plans.

Now I will share it with everyone for reference.

Firstly, the investment entrepreneurship plan of a car beauty shop is a written summary of the entrepreneur's planned business. It describes the internal and external environmental prerequisites and element characteristics related to the proposed business and provides a roadmap for business development and a measure of business progress.

Generally, the entrepreneurial plan is a combination of marketing, finance, production, human resources and other functional plans. To write a good business plan, you need to consider the following issues: 1) Focus on the product; 2) A brilliant plan abstract; 3) Dare to compete; 4) Indicate the direction of action; 5) Showcase your management team; 6) Understand the market.

Secondly, the content of the investment entrepreneurship plan for a car beauty shop usually includes the following aspects:

1. Market prediction for car beauty shop investments

It should include the following contents:

1) Demand forecasting;

2) Target customers and target markets;

3) The market position of this company’s products;

4) Market prediction overview of the current market situation;

5) Overview of competing manufacturers;

2. Abstract of the car beauty shop investment plan

It is the essence of the condensed business plan.

The plan abstract covers the key points of the plan, aiming for clarity so that readers can review the plan and make judgments in the shortest time possible.

The plan abstract generally includes the following contents:

Company introduction;

Managers and their organizations;

Main products and scope of business;

Market overview;

Marketing strategy;

Sales plan;

Production management plan;

Financial plan;

Status of funding needs, etc.

The abstract should be as concise and vivid as possible. In particular, it should explain the unique aspects of one's own enterprise and the market factors that lead to its success.

3. Cover Design for Car Beauty Shop Investments

The cover design should be simple and aesthetically pleasing. It varies from person to person; some require visual impact, while others need elegance and attention to color coordination and overall harmony. The focus should highlight what is important, creating a thought process when viewed. Regardless of whether we are at work or in our daily lives, we must have a plan before doing anything! Planning the start of an event, planning the process, and setting expected goals for the results we aim to achieve! Of course, this applies equally to the design of book covers. There is also a process from planning, implementation, to reaching the final result. For my years of work in book cover design, I can summarize it into mainly three aspects:

a) Conceptualization b) Composition c) Production

The design of the cover should have aesthetic appeal and artistic value. A good cover will create initial goodwill among readers and form a positive first impression.

4. Introduction to the Enterprise for Car Beauty Shop Investments

The purpose of this section is not to describe the entire plan nor provide another summary, but rather to introduce your company. Therefore, the focus should be on your company philosophy and how you formulate your strategic goals.

5. Product (Service) Introduction for Car Beauty Shop Investments

Product introduction should include the following contents: the concept, function, and characteristics of the product; main product introduction; the market competitiveness of the product; the research and development process of the product; plans and cost analysis for developing new products; market prospect predictions for the product; the brand and patents of the product, etc. In the product (service) introduction section, entrepreneurs need to provide detailed explanations about the product (service), ensuring accuracy and simplicity so that non-professionals can also understand.

Generally, product introductions should be accompanied by product prototypes, photos, or other descriptions.

Thirdly, Store Renovation

When renovating the beauty workshop, convenience for future use should be prioritized, and various situations should be considered as much as possible. Proper arrangements should be made for water storage (water tank), drainage (wastewater), filtration (wastewater), etc., and electrical outlets should also consider moisture-proofing and short-circuit prevention. Workshop design should be as simple as possible, with reasonable wiring and piping. It is best to have an independent switch for the workshop's electricity to prevent circuit breakers from affecting the normal power supply of the entire store due to short circuits in the future. Additionally, space should be reserved for future expansion needs, such as reserving space for film application rooms and lift positions. Here are some suggestions for friends preparing to renovate:

(1) It is preferable to design at least three car washing positions. If the area is insufficient, there should be at least two positions; otherwise, it will be impossible to keep up with demand after rainy days.

(2) It is best to install a lift machine, whether for oil changes or undercarriage armor spraying, as it is the best way to show your professional image to customers.

(3) Retain the film application room, and it is better to set it closer to the outside instead of deep inside the store. During film application, close the glass door so passersby can see.
