Leping Procuratorate Zhang Wanye: The fourth session of the Fourth People's Congress was successfully concluded

by zrq827888916lxgx on 2012-02-08 12:59:35

The fourth meeting of the fourth People's Congress of Leping City successfully completed all agenda items and victoriously closed at the Leping Culture Center in the morning of April 18. The conference legally elected Liao Yundong as the mayor of Leping City People's Government. Zhang Zhenhua, Secretary of Leping Municipal Party Committee presided over the meeting and delivered a speech. Wu Changshou, Director of the Standing Committee of Leping Municipal People's Congress gave the closing address. There were supposed to be 260 representatives present for the meeting, but 10 were absent due to illness or other reasons, leaving 250 representatives actually present, which met the statutory number. Yu Zhen Tai, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the People's Congress of Jingdezhen City and Secretary of the Jingdezhen Municipal Party Committee, Executive Chairmen of the Conference Zhang Zhenhua, Wu Changshou, Shi Jianhua, Sun Gengjiu, Luo Jianguo, Huang Chuanshou, Xu Haizhi, Xu Lizhen, Wang Mingsheng, Wang Xiaohua, Wu Chunfang, Zou Shouqing and others sat on the front row of the stage. Sitting on the stage were also Liao Yundong, Zhan Dongsheng, Sun Yanfeng, Fu Jinlin, Wang Zhiming, Wan Yuhua, Wang Ximin, Wang Ximei, Liu Shengqing, Zhu Jinghua, Zhang Wenbang, Cai Xinping and others. Other members of the Presidium of the Conference, veteran cadres' representatives and deputy county-level leaders of relevant units sat on the stage. The Conference unanimously passed through raising hands voting method "Resolution on the Report of the Leping Municipal People's Government", "Resolution on the Implementation of the National Economic and Social Development Plan of Leping City in 2009 and the National Economic and Social Development Plan of Leping City in 2010", "Resolution on the Implementation of the Financial Budget of Leping City in 2009 and the Financial Budget of Leping City in 2010", "Resolution on the Work Report of the Standing Committee of the Leping Municipal People's Congress", "Resolution on the Work Report of the Leping Municipal People's Court", and "Resolution on the Work Report of the Leping Municipal People's Procuratorate". Zhang Zhenhua pointed out that 2009 was an extremely extraordinary year in the history of Leping development. In face of the enormous impact of the international financial crisis, the whole city focused on "three guarantees and one promotion" and took every opportunity, worked hard to seize projects, planned and worked hard to promote development. Major breakthroughs have been made in expanding economic volume, advancing key projects, increasing fixed asset investment, accelerating urban development, completing industrial enterprise reform, implementing people's livelihood projects, maintaining social stability, and strengthening Party building, striding forward on the journey of leapfrog development. Zhang Zhenhua required that first, it is necessary to further improve and perfect the work of the People's Congress and continuously create a new situation in the work of the People's Congress. The Leping Municipal People's Congress and its Standing Committee should deeply implement the "Opinions on Further Strengthening the Work of the People's Congress" issued by the Leping Municipal Party Committee last year, pay more attention to the new situation, study new problems, solve new situations, and further improve and perfect the work of the People's Congress. Promoting economic and social development must be regarded as the primary task of the work of the People's Congress; realizing, maintaining, and developing the fundamental interests of the people must be regarded as the first pursuit of the work of the People's Congress; conducting legal supervision and work supervision must be regarded as an important responsibility of the work of the People's Congress; strengthening self-construction must be regarded as the fundamental guarantee of doing a good job in the work of the People's Congress. Second, it is necessary to give full play to the exemplary role of deputies to the People's Congress and make achievements for the accelerated development of Leping. Deputies to the People's Congress at all levels must fully play the role of bridge and model leadership. They should be good propagandists of the Party's guidelines and policies, promoters of reform and development, and maintainers of social stability. At present, various unstable factors still exist during the period of social development. Deputies to the People's Congress should do a good job in cooperation and supervision with the spirit of masters, tell the masses to believe that the Municipal Party Committee and the Municipal Government are determined and confident to govern the environmental pollution in the industrial park well, and that the government will certainly compensate according to national standards for requisitioning and demolition, and solve the living security problems of farmers who lose their land. Third, it is necessary to earnestly strengthen and improve the Party's leadership over the work of the People's Congress and provide strong guarantees for supporting the People's Congress to perform its duties in accordance with the law. It is necessary to really include the work of the People's Congress into the overall layout of the Party committee, place it in an important position, further improve and perfect the system of the Party committee leading the work of the People's Congress, and ensure the effective implementation of the Party's leadership. It is necessary to further publicize the work of the People's Congress, strive to form a strong atmosphere of upholding the authority of the People's Congress and supporting the work of the People's Congress throughout the city, and create a good social environment for the People's Congress to perform its functions in accordance with the law. We must attach great importance to the proposals, opinions, and suggestions put forward by deputies to the People's Congress, adhere to the responsibility system of accepting and handling the proposals and suggestions, criticisms, and opinions of deputies to the People's Congress, seriously supervise, and earnestly implement them. We must further strengthen the team building of the People's Congress organs and strive to build a high-quality team of the People's Congress with strong politics, excellent business, strict discipline, and correct style, providing strong guarantees for supporting the People's Congress to perform its functions in accordance with the law. In his closing remarks, Wu Changshou pointed out that in the new year, the Standing Committee of the Leping Municipal People's Congress would, under the strong leadership of the Leping Municipal Party Committee, focus on the center, serve the overall situation, support in supervision, supervise in support, focus the work on promoting the overall situation of economic and social development, focus on the issues that the masses are most concerned about, direct, and realistic, further pay attention to people's livelihood, further pay attention to the environment, constantly promote the comprehensive, coordinated, and sustainable development of Leping City's economic construction, political construction, cultural construction, and social construction, unite as one, take advantage of the momentum, tackle difficulties, work hard, and strive for the new leap of Leping City. At around 10:00 am, the conference victoriously closed amid the majestic national anthem.
