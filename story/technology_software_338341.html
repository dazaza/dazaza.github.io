Get used to being alone ------ [For all those who appreciate loneliness]

by jsjsineh3 on 2010-04-01 12:09:36

Be used to living alone --- There are many times to collect tears. Countless dejection and bewilderment, until it can directly fall on the shoulders of another person...... Be used to a solitary sunset --- Although a solitary sunset is inevitably lonely and sad, the fiery rosy clouds often make people surge with inexplicable excitement. Such a feeling of tearing up will not be shared by another person until...... Be used to imagining alone --- Although one's imagination is inevitably unrealistic and full of sadness, I still keep such imagination until someone walks into my fantasy. Be used to looking up at the sky alone --- Such steel and concrete buildings make people feel suffocated. This kind of civilization is far less intimate and comfortable than the primitive fields. Maybe it's because I am too stubborn, without the sense of home belonging, an incomplete sky...... Be used to a solo stage --- A solo stage is indeed so empty, but it has enough space for me to perform. Gradually, I changed from being extremely happy to feeling bored. Even the best actor needs enthusiastic audience...... Be used to reading the moon alone --- In fact, the moon also has a lot of worries. We tell each other our feelings. It always silently asks me: Why are you always alone? I said: I have my shadow with me! It smiled and said: Why does the other person always say the same thing? Be used to traveling alone --- Without concern, without direction. Wandering around to release oneself according to youthful wild thoughts. A solo journey is not without poetry and romance, of course...... The world of two people will be more wonderful.
