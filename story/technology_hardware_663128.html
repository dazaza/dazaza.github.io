by xywyw937 on 2012-02-11 10:44:37

MP4 is everywhere, be careful of deafness. Many students or young people like to listen to music, whether on the subway or walking in the square, wherever they go, they carry MP4 with them. Ear experts from Shanghai Xupu Traditional Chinese Medicine Hospital proposed that long-term use of MP4 can overload the ears and easily damage hearing, so be cautious of deafness. Why does listening to MP4 cause deafness? Due to the high level of external environmental noise, earphones generally leak sound easily, leading users to unconsciously increase the volume. The ears are subjected to noise for a long time, which can easily cause temporary or permanent hearing damage. Experts point out that if the hair cells responsible for transmitting signals to the brain are permanently damaged, it could lead to deafness.

What are the harms of deafness? Deafness brings a lot of embarrassment and troubles to patients' lives and work; just like eyes, ears are one of the most important ways for the human body to stay connected with the outside world. People obtain more than 70% of external information through their ears and eyes.

1. Harm physical health: A person's physical health is closely related to their hearing ability. In multiple studies, researchers measured the health status of elderly people with hearing loss, then fitted them with hearing aids. After three months, they made the same measurements again and found significant improvements in both their physical and mental health.

2. Seriously affect interpersonal relationships: Hearing impairment often makes it difficult for patients to communicate with classmates or relatives in school or social settings. They also struggle with phone conversations, leading to feelings of isolation. Misunderstandings may arise due to unclear hearing, affecting interpersonal relationships.

3. Hinder career development: Important business decisions are made during business meetings. If people with hearing loss cannot effectively communicate with others or hear speakers clearly, they might miss or misinterpret crucial information, thereby impacting business decisions, losing opportunities, and affecting business development. It also causes significant trouble at work.

4. Harm mental health: Requiring others to repeat themselves to understand speech content, listeners may avoid communication to avoid embarrassment, possibly isolating themselves gradually. This may result in irritability, introversion, or depression. Children with hearing loss often have lower academic performance, leading to poor self-image and behavioral or psychological problems such as irritability and lack of concentration.
