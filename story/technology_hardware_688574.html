On the 24th, the quotations for the three major components: memory prices rise again, CPU price increase

by anonymous on 2012-03-03 11:36:31

Today, in Beijing's Zhongguancun spot market for Intel processors, the prices of Intel's SNB (Sandy Bridge) processors and car decoders have risen across the board, with increases ranging from 5 to 50 yuan, most commonly between 10 and 30 yuan. The lower-end processors from Intel have seen smaller price hikes compared to AMD, offering better cost performance. Notably, the individual units of G530 and G620 dual-core processors are worth attention.

In today's Beijing Zhongguancun AMD processor spot market, AMD processor prices have also risen again. The older 45nm Athlon processors, which had experienced continuous price drops, have become the main drivers of this price increase, gradually losing their appeal. Today's price increase ranges from 5 to 15 yuan, most commonly between 10 and 15 yuan.

In today's Beijing Zhongguancun desktop memory spot market, memory prices have risen again, with the 2GB/1333 type seeing particularly rapid price increases. Kingston regular memory leads the price hike, but the 4GB/1333 by Adata has not yet followed suit, remaining at 105 yuan. Merchants mentioned that Samsung DDR3-1600 and Adata 8GB/1333 memories have been launched, making it harder to sell the 2GB/1333 memory due to its price increase.

In today's Beijing Zhongguancun notebook memory spot market, there is no change in the prices of notebook memory or car decoders. Due to the long sales cycle of notebook memory, the speed of price decline is slower than that of desktop memory. Kingston 4GB/1333 notebook memory is quoted at 115 yuan. Adata and Apacer 4GB/1333 notebook memory is quoted between 112-113 yuan.

In today's Beijing Zhongguancun desktop hard drive spot market, overall hard drive prices remain stable. As the early stockpiling and speculative selling of hard drives approaches its end, the sustained week-long slight price declines have been curbed, and hard drive prices are gradually aligning with those set by the Hong Kong general distributor.

In today's Beijing Zhongguancun notebook hard drive spot market, Hitachi notebook hard drive prices are stable. The Hitachi single-disc 500GB/5400 RPM notebook hard drive is quoted at 510 yuan; the dual-disc 500GB version is quoted at 500 yuan.

Intel's SNB processors see a full-line increase with the highest rise being 50 yuan.
