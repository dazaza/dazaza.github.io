Elderly woman's offer of gifting a house to the investigating police officer was declined

by zzfdhdb on 2011-07-29 14:22:28

Our reporter Yu Jie and correspondent Liu Chunjiang - "I want to give my house to community police officer Liu Qing!" Retired elderly person JinHua Zhang from Caiao Community in Fengtai was a victim of fraud several days ago. Police officer Liu Qing helped her recover a 200 yuan loss, and in gratitude, the elderly woman expressed her wish to gift her house. Yesterday, the Fengtai police department revealed that the police officer had politely declined the elderly woman's offer.

On September 25th, during a people-loving practice seminar at the Fengtai Public Security Bureau, 76-year-old JinHua Zhang from Caiao Community in Fengtai made a surprising statement to Director Sun Fang of the bureau: "I want to write a will, giving my one-bedroom apartment worth 1.5 million yuan to our community police officer Liu Qing." Elderly Ms. Zhang is a retired nurse from Beijing Hospital, childless, and lives alone. One day last April, two people who were promoting long-distance telephone services came to her home, claiming that her phone had added long-distance service and required a 200 yuan fee. After handing over the money, the elderly woman felt something was amiss but didn't dare to question them face-to-face for fear of causing trouble. After they left, the elderly woman called the mobile number left by the other party to request a refund but was refused. She searched for these two individuals based on the address on the business card but couldn't find them after three days of searching, and out of desperation, she reported it to the police. That afternoon, she received a call from community police officer Liu Qing, who consoled her: "Don't worry, let me handle this matter for you." Several hours later, Liu Qing called back, "Aunt Zhang, the issue has been resolved." That evening, Liu Qing returned the recovered 200 yuan to the elderly woman.

At the beginning of this year, the elderly woman proposed to gift her house to Liu Qing as a reward for him, but it was declined. The elderly woman then approached the Yuchuanying police station, but the response remained the same: "We appreciate your kindness, but we cannot accept the house." On the 25th, the elderly woman brought up the matter again at the seminar, only to be politely declined once more by Director Sun Fang.
