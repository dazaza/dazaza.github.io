Enrollment procedures for the 2012 Master's degree application with equal qualifications

by shuoshi on 2011-09-30 11:00:23

The registration for the same academic qualification examination adopts a method that combines online registration with on-site confirmation. First, you should go to the corresponding registration website within the designated registration period to register online and fill in and submit the registration information. Then, within the specified date, bring the "National Unified Examination Qualification Review Form for Personnel with Equivalent Academic Qualifications Applying for Master's Degree" reviewed and approved by the degree granting institution to the selected on-site registration point to pay the registration fee, take photos, confirm the registration information, and collect the "Rules for Candidates of National Examination for Non-Full-Time Master's Degree and Handling of Violations". If you only register online and do not go to the designated on-site registration point to handle relevant procedures such as taking photos, the registration will be invalid.

The specific registration time for the 2012 equivalent academic qualifications master's degree examination has not been announced yet, but you can refer to the online registration time for the 2011 equivalent academic qualifications master's degree examination.

According to news from the Office of the State Council Degree Committee, the national unified examination for foreign language proficiency and subject comprehensive level for personnel with equivalent academic qualifications applying for a master's degree will be registered online in mid-to-late March, confirmed on-site at the end of March, and the exam will be held on May 29th.

It is understood that the examination registration adopts a method that combines online registration with on-site confirmation. Within the specified online registration period, candidates log in to the website designated by the relevant provincial authority via the Internet to fill in and submit the registration information; then, during the specified on-site confirmation period, they bring the "National Unified Examination Qualification Review Form for Personnel with Equivalent Academic Qualifications Applying for Master's Degree" reviewed and approved by the degree granting institution to the designated location for on-site photography, payment, and confirmation of registration information. Once the registration information is signed and confirmed, it cannot be changed.

Candidates generally need to register and take the exam in the province (autonomous region, municipality) where the degree granting institution that accepts their master's degree application is located. In case of special circumstances, if you need to apply for registration and examination at your workplace, you must obtain the consent of both the degree granting institution that accepts your master's degree application and the provincial authority where your workplace is located before you can register and take the exam. The main languages for the foreign language proficiency test are English, Russian, French, German, and Japanese; the scope of the subject comprehensive level test covers 27 first-level disciplines including philosophy and economics. The specific examination time is from 9:00 AM to 11:30 AM for the foreign language proficiency test, and from 2:30 PM to 5:30 PM for the subject comprehensive level test.

In addition, it is learned that in 2010, a total of more than 123,000 people nationwide took the national unified examination for personnel with equivalent academic qualifications applying for a master's degree, with a slight increase in the number of registrants compared to 2009. Among them, more than 71,000 people took the foreign language proficiency test, and nearly 52,000 people took the subject comprehensive level test. English remains the most popular language, with more than 66,000 people taking the test, while the popular subjects for the subject comprehensive level test are clinical medicine, business administration, law, public administration, education, economics, journalism and communication, psychology, etc. From the registration situation in each examination area, Beijing has the largest number of registrants, reaching 25,275.
