Developing Good Living Habits -- Bad Living Habits Make the Brain Dull

In modern society, due to fierce competition and increased pressure, people's brains are generally tired and lack vitality. In order to maintain a youthful and creative mind, modern people must avoid some bad living habits and pay attention to environmental factors around them.

Long-term Overeating Modern nutritional research has found that after eating too much, the substance known as "fibroblast growth factor" in the brain will significantly increase. These fibroblast growth factors can increase the number of capillary endothelial cells and fat, leading to arteriosclerosis, resulting in early brain aging and intellectual decline.

Neglecting Breakfast

Not eating breakfast will cause the blood sugar to be lower than normal supply, insufficient nutrition supply for the brain, and long-term harm to the brain. In this regard, the quality of breakfast is closely related to intellectual development. According to research, children who usually eat high-protein breakfasts generally have relatively prolonged optimal thinking in class, while children who eat vegetarian food experience relatively faster emotional and energetic decline.

Excessive Consumption of Sweet Foods

Children who consume excessive sweet foods often have lower IQs. This is because the development of children's brains cannot do without sufficient protein and vitamins from food, while sweet foods damage appetite, reduce the intake of high-protein and various vitamins, leading to functional malnutrition, which affects brain development.

Insufficient Sleep

The main way for the brain to eliminate fatigue is through sleep. Long-term insufficient or poor-quality sleep will only accelerate the degeneration of brain cells, making smart people become confused.

Long-term Smoking German medical researchers have shown that long-term smoking causes varying degrees of brain tissue shrinkage, making one prone to Alzheimer's disease. Because long-term smoking can cause cerebral arteriosclerosis, over time it leads to insufficient blood supply to the brain, neuronal degeneration, and subsequently brain shrinkage.

Being Silent

There is a specific area in the brain responsible for language. Regular speaking not only promotes brain development but also exercises brain function. Normally, one should say more content-rich, philosophically or logically strong words. People who remain silent all day and rarely smile are not necessarily smart.

Sleeping with the Head Covered

When sleeping with the head covered, as the concentration of carbon dioxide in the quilt increases and the oxygen concentration continuously decreases, long-term inhalation of damp and polluted air is extremely harmful to the brain.

Unwillingness to Think

Thinking is the best way to exercise the brain. Only by using your brain frequently and thinking diligently can people become smarter. On the contrary, unwillingness to use the brain can only accelerate the degeneration of the brain, making smart people become foolish.

Using the Brain While Sick

When feeling unwell or sick,勉强坚持学习或工作不仅效率低下，而且还容易造成大脑损害。

此外，大脑是全身耗氧量最大的器官，平均每分钟消耗氧气500至600升，只有充足的氧气供应才能提高大脑的工作效率。因此，在工作学习的时候，一定要讲究周围环境的空气卫生。
