The children's furniture market is broad, and the design needs to gradually cater to diversified demands.

by seo175110 on 2012-03-08 17:38:17

With the increasingly progressive home living concepts of many current consumers, a lot of consumers are no longer confined to traditional home design concepts. Breaking conventions and pursuing individuality has become a consensus for many people. And when it comes to children's room home design, this is even more so. Nowadays, parents, in order to promote the all-round development of their children, will not restrict their children's personality preferences. Therefore, this directly leads to the need for many children's furniture products to focus on meeting the diversified needs of the children's market during design and development. There is a high demand but also plenty of blanks. Ms. Zhang from Baohe District in Hefei has twin sons, and having both children in the same room troubles the parents. "Even though they look the same, their preferences are completely different. The older one likes active toys like guns, while the younger one loves writing and drawing. Neither allows the other to place toys in their own space. Do I have to buy two storage cabinets?" Similar confusion to Ms. Zhang's not only exists in twin families but also in single-child households with children's rooms. Some children's rooms are irregularly shaped, making it hard to find suitable furniture. The lack of "considerate edition" children's furniture leaves many parents at a loss. The market demand is large, but it is difficult to find satisfactory furniture in the market. The person in charge of Tianjin Juranjiagou introduced that children's room furniture mostly comes in bulk from production lines, all made according to preset styles, unable to be customized. A guide at Red Star Macalline store also mentioned that there are quite a few consumers who bring their own design drawings asking for customization, but most brands can't do it yet. Even some Danish imported children's furniture brands explicitly state that they don't take custom orders. Design needs to gradually cater to diversified demands. Modular freedom is just a "minor adjustment." This is a set of wardrobes with acrylic panels where you can place your baby's photos; this set of desks can be freely combined, you decide whether to place the bookshelf on the left or right," a guide from Juranjiagou's furniture brand introduced. However, if you want to design and customize children's room furniture according to your own ideas, the guide firmly rejected it. Size and color changes, various cabinet bodies and boxes freely combined, pattern changes on the wardrobe, these "minor adjustments" are often labeled as "customization." However, for children and parents with genuine personalized needs, this kind of "minor adjustment" has long been unable to meet market demands. A certain home square manager frankly stated that children's preferences are relatively hard to fathom, sometimes coming fast and going fast. These practical problems are also one of the main reasons why some parents abandon customizing furniture for their children and instead purchase mass-produced products. But he still believes that custom children's furniture will become a future trend. Children's furniture lacks standards. Children's furniture emphasizes personalized design, but insiders in Hefei's furniture industry revealed that most manufacturers of children's furniture on the market do not have the original creative capability, with numerous copycats. "Chinese children differ greatly from foreign children in height, weight, preferences, and living habits. Therefore, using products modeled after foreign children's furniture often causes inconvenience and unpleasantness for them. In fact, children need professional and considerate exclusive furniture design more urgently than adults. On this point, it is hoped that children's furniture manufacturers improve promptly," a lady selecting children's furniture appealed. Emphasizing ergonomics, which benefits children's physical health, is a promotional phrase used by most children's furniture brands. However, since the country currently does not have special standards for testing the functions, quality, and environmental friendliness of children's furniture, everything follows the national standard for adult furniture as the entry threshold. This means that there are no standards to determine whether many children's furniture products on the Hefei furniture market conform to ergonomics.
