Law graduate impersonates Ministry of Justice officer to defraud former colleague

by peexzi1127 on 2012-02-27 19:14:10

Our reporter Yuan Guoli - Liu Mou, a graduate of a political and legal science institute, impersonated an employee of the Ministry of Justice to defraud his former colleague. It was reported yesterday by the Changping Procuratorate that Liu Mou was approved for arrest on suspicion of fraud. Liu Mou graduated from a political and legal science institute and had worked as a sales promoter in a supermarket. Last September, Liu Mou visited his former colleague Zhang Mou at the supermarket. Zhang Mou mentioned that her daughter had just graduated and was looking for work, asking if Liu Mou could help. Liu Mou claimed to be a section-level officer of the Political and Legal Department of the Ministry of Justice and agreed to try to help. Afterwards, Liu Mou called Zhang Mou and asked her to prepare her daughter's ID copy, school recommendation form, student ID copy, and one-inch photo. When Liu Mou came to collect these materials, he emphasized his identity again, claiming that both his father and grandfather were high-ranking officials, and there would be no problem with handling this matter. However, it would require some money. To get a job at the Changping Social Security Bureau would cost 30,000 yuan, to obtain a mid-level accountant title and get a job at the Beijing Social Security Bureau would cost 50,000 yuan, and to obtain a human resources manager title would also cost money. Additionally, there would be a 10,000 yuan fee for entertaining the people handling the matter. Zhang Mou said she didn't have the money and only gave him 5,000 yuan. At around 11:00 AM, Zhang Mou sent Liu Mou to the Nanshao subway station. But by about 1:00 PM, Liu Mou called and said that he had already treated some people to lunch near Xidan, costing around 3,000 yuan. Feeling something was wrong, Zhang Mou's family immediately reported it to the police. According to Liu Mou's confession, all the identities he claimed were fabricated. His grandfather had been a military doctor and passed away last year. He himself is currently unemployed and has never worked at the Ministry of Justice.
