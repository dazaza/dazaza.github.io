Snowtown Season 4

by g92193019 on 2009-09-21 10:42:32

A world-renowned surgeon moves with his son and daughter from the bustling city of New York to a snowy mountain town. They've never been here before, but they come because his late wife once told him: "In my heart, it's as beautiful as paradise." In the past, when he was a globally acclaimed surgeon, he buried himself in work to avoid his own marriage and family issues that he wasn't good at handling. Now, he has to start over and learn how to be a competent father. Thus, one story after another unfolds in this town: children who have lost their mothers, girls who have lost their boyfriends, parents who have lost their sons, sisters who have lost their brothers, wives who almost lose their husbands when discovering they are homosexual, and pregnant girls abandoned by their piano tutors deciding to have an abortion. It's hard to say if the people in this town are cruel, even though they occasionally do stupid things that hurt others. Still, it remains a town where everyone in the story lives through their ups and downs. The adults grow older, the children grow up, life twists and turns, leaving more and more scars in their hearts.
