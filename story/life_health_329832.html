Small tricks to reduce radiation from home appliances

by 60888888 on 2010-02-23 11:08:21

If you want to talk about the "blessing" of modern life that is convenient and fast, modern home appliances have brought us a fast and convenient life. But while we enjoy a high quality life, we also worry that the electromagnetic radiation produced by these electronic products will affect our health.

So, how can we reduce the radiation from home appliances in daily life? Especially for white-collar workers who face computers all day, radiation protection seems more important.

Recommendation: To deal with computer radiation, you can place a bottle of clear water next to your computer because water is the best medium for absorbing electromagnetic waves;

Caution: Metal eyeglass frames can significantly lead to an increase in electromagnetic fields, increasing the user's absorption rate of radiation. If you wear metal framed glasses for a long time while using a mobile phone, the electromagnetic radiation could be conducted to the brain and around the eyes through the metal eyeglass frame, causing damage.

Tips for reducing radiation:

1. Home appliances should not be placed together, and should not be used at the same time;

2. For appliances that are not in use, the power must be turned off because powered-on appliances can still produce a large amount of electromagnetic radiation;

3. After looking at the computer or TV for a long time, you should wash your face and hands in time.

4. Eat more foods rich in vitamin A, C, and protein, such as carrots, cabbage, tofu, milk, eggs, etc., especially seaweed, which can enhance the ability to resist electromagnetic radiation.

5. Drink more tea, as the substances like tea polyphenols in tea are beneficial for radiation protection.
