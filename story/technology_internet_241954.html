IDC industry grows in taking the responsibility of purifying network environment

by now on 2009-09-23 17:05:05

At the end of last year, our country initiated a wave of internet rectification. Various industries carried out these rectification actions in an orderly manner. This year, the internet rectification remains strict. In order to rectify and standardize the business order of the internet industry and effectively purify the internet network culture environment, the IDC industry has also made important contributions.

In the first half of 2009, the financial crisis was still severe, and the internet rectification action intensified. This could be called the breakthrough year for the IDC industry, but it inevitably led to the elimination of many IDCs that couldn't withstand the severe tests. Since the IDC is a high-investment and high-cost industry, companies with strength and competitiveness would have more advantages in this severe test. A relevant person in charge of Era Interconnect believed that if an IDC service provider wants to have a promising future, not only must its own resources be sufficiently guaranteed, but its service level must also be significantly improved.

In general, only with strong network resources as support can an IDC provide users with stable guarantees. If network resources cannot be guaranteed, there's no point in talking about improving service quality. According to reports, Era Interconnect (www.todayidc.com) has five-star telecom computer rooms and standard China Unicom computer rooms, as well as Hong Kong data centers and dual-line data centers. However, having just network resources is often insufficient. In today's rapidly developing internet, the management level of servers is also required to be higher. Especially in cooperation with actions to purify the network environment, many website managers are troubled by related issues such as website registration and illegal information investigation. Faced with such situations, IDC service providers can only purify the network environment from the source by enhancing server management performance, strengthening the identification of illegal information, and managing registration. Therefore, Era Interconnect gives each server user a free gift of Huadun Server Management Home. After installing this green software, it has functions such as detecting illegal information, registration management, and website security management.

I believe that if we want to actually purify the network environment, comprehensively resist vulgar trends, and improve the service level of the IDC industry, IDC providers should start from basic equipment like servers and hosts. Only by continuously improving server levels and strengthening server management can websites obtain better development environments, thus highlighting the advantages of IDC service providers.

The internet is the carrier of information dissemination. The number of internet users in our country is increasing daily, and the network has become people's primary channel for obtaining information. Therefore, a good network cultural environment is very important. In the past six months, our country has launched a massive wave of network rectification. Strengthening internet management has become an important task for website-related managers, and internet management will be a long-term project. Therefore, strengthening server management is the long-term plan for the healthy development of websites.
