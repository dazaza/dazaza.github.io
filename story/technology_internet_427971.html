Liu Yishou + Zhao Benshan

by anonymous on 2011-05-09 17:59:00

Liu Yishou reports that every year at the Spring Festival Gala, Zhao Benshan's language-based performances are always kept under wraps until the rehearsal stage before being revealed. Previously, media reports suggested that this year Zhao Benshan would partner with a film and television star, with rumors swirling about possible pairings with celebrities like Big S, Lin Chi-ling, and Yan Ni for a skit on the Year of the Rabbit Spring Festival Gala. Yesterday, Zhao Benshan appeared alongside his apprentices Xiao Xiangyang, Shen Chunyang, Tang Jianjun, Wang Xiaohu, and others at the Beijing TV "Comedy World" press conference. Zhao Benshan confirmed that he would continue to bring his apprentices to the Spring Festival Gala, not partnering with any new faces.

Zhao Benshan stated in an interview: “This year, I will definitely bring my apprentices to the Gala again; I won't partner with other new artists because I understand my apprentices well, and they understand me.” Regarding whether Xiao Xiangyang would return to the stage, Zhao Benshan said, “I have plans for this. Bringing apprentices to the Gala will be done on a rotational basis, not just Xiao Xiangyang alone. I'll go back to Shenyang today and select suitable actors based on the script.” With a touch of humor, Zhao added, “Tomorrow in Shenyang, we’ll draw lots, and whoever gets picked will come with me.”

Previously, there were reports suggesting Zhao Benshan might partner with actresses such as Lin Chi-ling, Big S, or Yan Ni for the Year of the Rabbit Gala. Zhao Benshan denied these claims, saying, “How would I know? These are all pairings decided by others.” He joked, “If you (pointing to the reporter) want to act, I could choose you too.”

Netizens have begun voting on which of Zhao Benshan’s apprentices will dominate the Spring Festival Gala stage. Among Zhao Benshan’s 35 apprentices, names like Zhang Xiaofei, Tang Jianjun, Wang Xiaobao, Wang Xiaoli, Cai Weili, Yan Guangliang, Wang Xiaohu, Wang Jinxin, Sun Lirong, Liu Xiaoguang, Xiao Xiangyang, Tian Wa, and Hong Hai are particularly active. The question of who among Zhao Benshan's apprentices will make it to the Gala has become a hot topic among netizens. Recently, netizens have started voting for their preferred apprentice to appear on the Gala stage and predicting who is best suited for it.

Xiao Xiangyang remains the most anticipated. Following his overnight success with "Not Short of Money," he performed across the country in "I Want to Be a Star." In this show directed by Zhao Benshan, Xiao Xiangyang imitated singers like Dao Lang, A Du, Andy Lau, Zhang Yusheng, and A Bao, drawing applause and cheers from audiences wherever he went. Netizens eagerly anticipate seeing Xiao Xiangyang on the Year of the Rabbit Spring Festival Gala stage, bringing laughter to the New Year's Eve night.

Netizens also haven't forgotten Liu Xiaoguang. Zhao Benshan once revealed that in 2009, he had considered bringing Liu Xiaoguang to the Gala but abandoned the idea due to some mistakes made during that period, instead opting to bring Xiao Xiangyang. However, Zhao still hopes to give Liu Xiaoguang a chance, believing that if he can focus on art, he could excel in both rural dramas and skits. Liu Xiaoguang's performance with his master in "Just Short of Money" at the Liaoning Spring Festival Gala in the Year of the Tiger was highly praised. In an online vote, Liu Xiaoguang garnered more votes than Xiao Xiangyang.

Tang Jianjun is another highly anticipated candidate. Known for his distinctiveness on the Er Ren Zhuan stage, Tang Jianjun is one of the most popular actors when portraying elderly women, drawing enthusiastic applause upon his entrance. On screen, Tang Jianjun has been memorable in roles such as Secretary Xu Mai in "Old Root Liu," Little Tang in "Big Brother Ma," Xie Guangkun in "Rural Love," and Liu Yishou in "Country Gentleman." Additionally, among the apprentices, Tang Jianjun holds a leadership position. It's surprising that Zhao Benshan hasn't previously taken Tang Jianjun to the Spring Festival Gala. Given Tang Jianjun's significant contributions to Ben Shan Media and his leadership role among the apprentices, will the master extend special favor this time? Will Zhao Benshan give Tang Jianjun a chance?

Wang Xiaoli is seen as the most talented choice. As Zhao Benshan's fifth apprentice, Wang Xiaoli has given Zhao much to worry about. However, with age, Wang Xiaoli has matured significantly. Particularly in "Rural Love," Wang Xiaoli's portrayal of Liu Neng, a character vastly different in age from himself, was exceptionally successful, leaving a deep impression on viewers. Originally, the role of Liu Neng was tailored for Fan Wei. Due to Fan Wei's departure, Wang Xiaoli took over the role and excelled beyond expectations. Some even believe Wang Xiaoli is the only one capable of replacing Fan Wei. However, for Wang Xiaoli, the success of Liu Neng could also be a hindrance, as the character may overshadow his chances of successfully making it to the Spring Festival Gala.
