Taobao shop opening process

Doing well in a Taobao shop sometimes doesn't require perfection in every aspect; perhaps excelling in just one or two aspects is enough!

So, for those engaged in Taobao entrepreneurship, what are the various steps involved? What issues should be noted at each step?

Taobao entrepreneurship can roughly be divided into the following steps: 1. Website positioning 2. Sourcing 3. Shop setup 4. Promotion 5. Customer service and after-sales

1. Shop positioning: That is, what you plan to sell, the price range of the products, the main consumer group's age and gender, and even occupation! The reason why shop positioning is placed first is because it directly affects the subsequent four steps! Shop positioning should be based on your actual situation, generally choosing an industry you are interested in or products for which you have sources!

2. Sourcing: Finding good sources accounts for at least one-third of success! And for most people, it's hard to find sources with significant advantages. Regarding how to find sources, please look forward to the upcoming "Taobao Shop Opening - Sourcing Edition"!

3. Shop setup: Including shop decoration, product descriptions, Guangzhou VIP Information Technology Co., Ltd., and even product pricing! For a new shop, it's best to try to perfect each step as much as possible!

4. Shop promotion: This should be considered the top priority. Common shop promotion methods include utilizing Taobao's ranking rules, Taobao community promotion, Taobao affiliate promotion, independent website promotion, forum group buying, forum ad space, forum serial promotion, Taobao Express, and Google bidding promotion! Each promotion method will be specifically discussed in subsequent articles!

What should I sell in my Taobao shop?

A good shop positioning equals half the success!

Shop positioning, simply put, is deciding what you intend to sell, what price range, whether the customer base is male or female, and which age group!

Good shop positioning directly affects future profitability, so it's essential to carefully position your shop during the initial stage!

What to sell to make more profit is something most people focus on and worry about! Should you sell popular products or new and unique products?

I've summarized a few principles of shop positioning, hoping they'll help everyone!

1. Products with repeat consumption characteristics

In business, the most important thing is repeat customers, so the best products are those with repeat consumption characteristics!

Repeat consumption actually has two scenarios. For example, clothes and cosmetics both have repeat consumption characteristics, but customers won't buy the same style of clothing again, but they might buy other styles from you. Cosmetics, on the other hand, might involve buying the same product or trying other brands or different products from the same brand! Any product with repeat consumption characteristics meets the first principle!

2. Fields you are familiar with or interested in

There are thousands of products, and each product surely has sellers who do well and others who don't. I believe the products are the same; the key lies in people's abilities! As for what kind of product to choose to start your Taobao journey, I think it's best to select something you're familiar with or interested in! Only when you're familiar with or interested in something will you have the motivation to continue!

3. Products with distinct features

What does it mean to have distinct features? Through some descriptive words, people can imagine what the product is!

For example, brand recognition, specialized functions, etc.! Specialized functions essentially mean specific functionality.

Take a simple example: if a cosmetic has whitening, spot-removal, and acne-clearing effects, how should this cosmetic be packaged? Write down all the functions? No, it's best to highlight one function, giving customers a subconscious awareness that if they want XXX, they must choose this product! A practical example is Wang Lao Ji highlighting its heat-clearing and detoxifying function!

4. Products with sufficiently high profit margins

Unless you're focusing on volume, it's still better to choose products with sufficiently high profit margins!

For most newly opened Taobao shops, sourcing is undoubtedly a headache! Because, after all, only a small portion of people have their own physical stores. So, for others, how to solve the sourcing problem?

For those without physical stores, it's generally one of the following three situations:

1. Relying on large wholesale markets, taking orders from others and then picking up goods from the market!

This sourcing model has the advantage of not tying up funds and offering price advantages, but it's more troublesome! Of course, many people aren't near large wholesale markets, and even if they are, part-time Taobao sellers might not have time to run errands daily!

2. Buying in bulk from platforms like Alibaba and selling on Taobao, with the advantage of price but the disadvantage of tying up funds! This sourcing model is generally suitable for items priced around 10 yuan, such as small accessories!

3. Drop shipping! This is also the main form, with the advantages of not tying up funds and simple shipping, but problems can be tricky to coordinate! Cosmetics, clothing, shoes, etc., can adopt this sourcing model! Apart from general products, you can also join brands for consignment sales, such as cosmetics brands, using drop shipping and not tying up funds.

Ideally, secure exclusive agency rights for a brand, with fewer similar products being ideal, such as smoking cessation products. If you can get exclusive agency rights for a new smoking cessation product, it will be relatively easier to promote!
