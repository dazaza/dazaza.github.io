Shanghai Motel 168 Yan'an West Road - Hotel Introduction

Shanghai Motel 168 Yan'an West Road, Beijing Jinjiang Star, has a total of 516 guest rooms, Jinjiang Star Beijing Olympic Park Store, which is currently the largest economy hotel. The hotel is located in the bustling high-end business district of Yan'an West Road, surrounded by high-star hotels and high-star office buildings. The transportation around the hotel is very convenient. In addition to more than ten bus routes, Jinjiang Star Zhengzhou Culture Road Store, the nearby light rail, subway, and inner ring elevated road will make it convenient for travelers to arrive at and travel to Hongqiao Airport, Pudong Airport, and Shanghai Railway Station.
