Measurement of Penis Length

For Chinese people, if the length of the erect penis (non-erect state not counted) is shorter than 12 cm and the diameter is less than 3.1 cm, it is considered as a small penis. Anything exceeding these figures is considered normal.

There are two methods for measuring penis length. One method involves measuring while the penis is in a flaccid state. In a warm environment with a calm mind, gently hold the penis flat with your hand. Place one end of a hard ruler against the pubic symphysis and read the number at the other end, which is the urethral opening of the glans penis. Then use a flexible tape measure to measure the diameter one centimeter below the glans penis. The second method involves measuring while the penis is in an erect state, following the same procedure as above. In hospitals, doctors often use the measurement of the stretched length of the penis instead of the erection length. This involves pinching the glans penis with the thumb and index finger and pulling outward to stretch the penis fully before measuring the distance from the pubic symphysis to the urethral opening. The length of the foreskin is not included in the measurement. The stretched length is basically consistent with the erection length.
