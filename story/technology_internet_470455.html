Strategic emerging industries 12th Five-Year Plan to be announced in September: covering 45 industries

by tuoqing1188 on 2011-08-04 09:25:29

Seven major industrial chains launch a new industrial cycle

Reported by Fang Jiaxi from Beijing

The Economic Reference News reporter recently learned that the main report of the "12th Five-Year Plan" for the development of strategic emerging industries led by the National Development and Reform Commission (NDRC) with the participation of relevant ministries, as well as seven Plans facing the seven strategic emerging industries, are being submitted to the State Council one after another and will be announced externally starting in September. According to the plan, among the seven strategic emerging industries, four are positioned as pillar industries, and three are positioned as leading industries. The seven industrial chains will lead China into a new industrial cycle over the next ten years.

"The industrial chains of the seven strategic emerging industries will gradually form and improve over the next few years, producing positive effects on China's economy and the formation of a new industrial cycle," said Zhou Zixue, Chief Economist of the Ministry of Industry and Information Technology (MIIT). Calculating based on the development speed and scale of China's GDP in recent years, it is expected that by 2015, the added value of strategic emerging industries will reach approximately 4.3 trillion yuan, and by 2020, it will reach 11.4 trillion yuan. To achieve the above-mentioned development goals proposed by the State Council, it is estimated that strategic emerging industries need to achieve an average annual growth rate of 24.1% from 2011 to 2015, and an average annual growth rate of 21.3% from 2016 to 2020.

The reporter learned from the NDRC that in the planning of the seven strategic emerging industries, the new energy plan is led by the National Energy Administration; the energy conservation and environmental protection plan is led by the Environmental Resources Department of the NDRC; the new materials and biopharmaceuticals plan is led by the High-Tech Department of the NDRC; the new energy vehicles and high-end equipment manufacturing plan is led by the MIIT; the next generation information technology plan is jointly formulated by the MIIT and the NDRC. Relevant ministries such as the Ministry of Science and Technology also participated in the formulation of the plans.

"A relevant ministry has already summarized the content of the '12th Five-Year Plan' for the development of strategic emerging industries involving each department to the NDRC. Since the preparation work for the main report has not yet been completed, the publication time for the main report and the seven plans will be in September this year," a relevant official of the NDRC exclusively revealed to the Economic Reference News reporter.

Relevant personnel from the MIIT recently expressed that, considering the development stage and promotion level of emerging industries, it is expected that by 2020, energy conservation and environmental protection, next-generation information technology, biotechnology, and high-end equipment manufacturing will become pillar industries of the national economy, while new energy, new materials, and new energy vehicles will become leading industries of the national economy.

The reporter saw from the draft opinions of the "12th Five-Year Plan" that the industrial chain planning for the seven strategic emerging industries is complete and clear, with a total of 45细分 industries.

The "12th Five-Year Plan for Next-Generation Information Technology" pointed out a long industrial chain, including information network infrastructure, next-generation mobile communication, core equipment of the next-generation Internet, intelligent terminals, triple network convergence, Internet of Things, cloud computing, integrated circuits, new displays, and high-end software and servers.

Relevant personnel from the MIIT who participated in the drafting of the plan expressed that in the key sub-industries of next-generation information technology, the new flat panel display industry has a huge market space from upstream materials and equipment to downstream televisions, laptops, mobile phones, and displays. The penetration rate of mobile phone touch screens has increased sharply, iPads are popular worldwide, iPad-like tablet computers are about to be launched, and at any time and place, one can feel the booming prospects of this industry.

The "Development Plan for Energy Conservation and Environmental Protection Industry" proposed that industry and construction are currently the two most energy-intensive areas in China, making industrial energy conservation and building energy conservation very important. The environmental protection industry belongs to public utilities, where government funding and policy support are the core driving forces. During the "12th Five-Year Plan" period, China's environmental protection investment is expected to reach 3.1 trillion yuan, with an environmental protection output value exceeding 2 trillion yuan.

It was revealed that the energy conservation and environmental protection industry chain in the plan covers six key areas. During the "12th Five-Year Plan" period, the country will add 20 million tons of sewage treatment supporting pipeline construction capacity, increase sewage treatment scale by 90 million tons, upgrade and transform sewage treatment scale by 50 million tons; add 47,000 tons/day of sludge treatment capacity; add 250,000 tons/day garbage landfill treatment capacity, and add 150,000 tons/day garbage incineration treatment capacity; add 180 million kilowatts of coal-fired power plant denitrification facilities; carry out heavy metal pollution control demonstration pilot projects; support the construction of a number of clean production demonstration projects.

"The '12th Five-Year Plan' proposal discusses high-end equipment manufacturing from the perspectives of manufacturing upgrading and emerging industries, which means a great transformation from Made in China to Created in China," Zhou Zixue expressed. The reporter noticed that the Plan clearly identified five key areas for future high-end equipment manufacturing, including aviation equipment, satellite manufacturing and application, high-speed rail and urban rail transit equipment, marine engineering equipment, and intelligent manufacturing equipment.

It was revealed that the "12th Five-Year Plan for the Biological Industry" pointed out that the focus of future biological industry development will be in five aspects: biomedicine, bio-agriculture, bio-energy, bio-environmental protection, and bio-service outsourcing. According to industry experts, regarding the development of biomedicine, the emphasis over the next five years will mainly be on innovative drug varieties such as biotechnology drugs, novel vaccines, diagnostic reagents, and chemical drugs for the prevention and treatment of major diseases. "By 2015, the sales revenue of the top 100 new drug enterprises will account for 50% of the total sales revenue of the entire industry; by 2020, five companies will enter the world's top 100 pharmaceutical companies," Wang Changlin, Director of the Institute of Industry and Technology Economics of the Macroeconomic Research Institute of the National Development and Reform Commission, expressed.

According to the "12th Five-Year Plan for Renewable Energy Development", by 2015, China's wind power, nuclear power, and solar power installed capacity will reach 150 million kilowatts, 80 million kilowatts, and 20 million kilowatts respectively. This will bring enormous business opportunities for related industries' equipment and contractors.

Tang Jianmao, Director of the Consulting Department of the Chinese Materials Research Society, told the Economic Reference News reporter, "According to relevant plans, the application of new materials can almost penetrate all fields of the national economy, and they are also the cornerstone of strategic emerging industries. The demand space for new materials in medical, environmental protection, electronic information, construction, and chemical fields is unimaginable." Data shows that the "12th Five-Year Plan" determined five directions for the development of the new materials industry, with more than 12细分 application fields, involving nearly a hundred listed companies.

According to relevant materials from the MIIT, in the industrial chain of new energy vehicles, there are batteries, charging stations, whole vehicle production, etc. Guosen Securities' research report believes that after the concept of emerging industries was proposed, new energy vehicles were the first to gain attention and became the hottest topic.
