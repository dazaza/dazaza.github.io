IXWebHosting Virtual Hosting Cooperative Renting: A Crazy Idea

by xwkj0983 on 2012-02-21 16:21:41

Since IXWebHosting entered the Chinese market, it has successively launched a Chinese website cn.ixwebhosting.com and Chinese customer service to meet the needs of Chinese users. On the other hand, IXWebHosting keeps launching various types of hosting services: shared hosting, VPS hosting, cloud hosting. As long as you need hosting, IXWebHosting will have one that suits you. However, many webmasters now try to take shortcuts, and some even share an IXWebHosting hosting plan among multiple people. They share the 15 unique IPs given by IXWebHosting. Although sharing IXWebHosting hosting does not violate its usage policy, what you may not know is that sharing IXWebHosting hosting comes with significant risks!

So, what are the risks of sharing IXWebHosting virtual hosting? Here's a brief explanation:

**CPU Resource Overload - Website Can't Open**

IXWebHosting virtual hosting imposes limits on CPU usage for each hosting account. Specifically, each account is restricted to using no more than 10% of the CPU. If someone runs a highly CPU-intensive program (such as a content scraper), your site's resources can easily exceed this limit. Even without such programs, if 10 people share one IXWebHosting virtual hosting plan, each person would only be able to use around 1% of the CPU. Once this limit is exceeded, everyone's websites will experience issues, such as intermittent unavailability. Imagine who would put up with that.

**Excessive Resource Usage - Space Cannot Be Used**

Many American virtual hosting providers claim to offer unlimited space and traffic, but in reality, this isn't entirely true. The same applies to IXWebHosting. While many of its plans support "unlimited" space and traffic, there are still restrictions on system resource usage. For example, IX's Windows virtual hosting has a file count limit of 100,000 files, while its Linux virtual hosting has a limit of 300,000 files. If 10 people share a Windows hosting plan, each person would only be able to upload 10,000 files on average! Since there are file count restrictions, you would need to limit the number of files each person uploads, which could lead to technical problems. How do you restrict the number of files uploaded by others? Moreover, if one of the 10 people exceeds the limit, all websites will be affected.

**Violation of Space Usage Policy - Account Banned**

Since this is shared hosting, many people might not value their accounts. Everyone knows that IXWebHosting virtual hosting doesn't support adult content websites. If someone uses IXWebHosting hosting for such purposes, they might think: "Since the price is so low, it's not a big deal if the account gets suspended." However, IXWebHosting doesn't care about such excuses. Once any content violating its policies is discovered, the site will be immediately shut down. Additionally, some people use IXWebHosting hosting to send spam emails, which IXWebHosting also does not support.

Besides the three points mentioned above, the issue of permissions for each account is also difficult to resolve. If everyone has too much permission, it could pose a security threat to others' websites. IXWebHosting provides one hosting plan for individual use. If you share the hosting, several people will share a single unified account. Sharing an account is a scary thing; if one person's account information is leaked, all websites could be compromised, leaving no security at all. Furthermore, since it's a shared account, one problem that arises is that co-tenants can view and even modify each other's website content. From a technical perspective, the risks of sharing IXWebHosting are considerable.

In summary, whether you're purchasing IXWebHosting virtual hosting or any other virtual hosting, don't be tempted by cheap prices. A virtual hosting plan costing just a few dozen yuan is likely not a good product and could very well be a shared hosting plan. Therefore, it's better to spend a little more money to buy a quality virtual hosting service. Sharing hosting space is strongly discouraged, as it is a reckless idea.
