Xinhua News Agency, Taiyuan, October 26 (reported by Min-da Fan) -- On October 25, Meng Peijie, known as "the most beautiful girl in Linfen" who has just won the title of National Model of Morality, became an honorary employee of China Post, which will sponsor all her tuition fees during her college study. Shanxi Branch of China Post presented Meng Peijie with the employment intention letter under the entrustment of the group company. General Manager Guo-hua Li of China Post Group Company was deeply moved after he learned about Meng Peijie's touching deeds of practicing filial piety and love for relatives day after day for 12 years and promoting traditional Chinese moral culture through media. He said that Meng Peijie's deeds are worth studying carefully by all employees of China Post nationwide. In order to further build harmonious postal culture and promote the extensive development of activities of "striving for excellence, serving the people", China Post decided to hire Meng Peijie as "honorary postal employee" and will sponsor all her tuition fees during her college study and sincerely invite her to work for postal services after graduation. Meanwhile, Guo-hua Li also wished Meng Peijie's mother Liu Fangying to recover soon. The leaders of Shanxi Branch of China Post made arrangements for studying and publicizing Meng Peijie's touching deeds throughout the province at the first moment, and rushed to Meng Peijie's home in Xiji County, Linfen City overnight to visit Meng Peijie and her mother Liu Fangying, convey the condolences from China Post Group Corporation, and present consolation money. Holding the employment intention letter in her arms, Meng Peijie excitedly said: "I thank post for its care and help to my mother and me. I have thought that mailmen are the most diligent and professional since I was young, so I feel very honored to become one of them."
