Harmony is precious among neighbors.

by bjjdwxgs on 2009-12-03 09:40:59

Mr. Zhou, a resident of Dongzhai Xincun in the urban area, reported that over a month ago his wife was beaten by a neighbor, costing more than one thousand yuan in medical expenses. However, not only did the neighbor refuse to compensate them, but they also smashed the Zhou family's door, creating deep resentment between the two families.

Yesterday morning, the reporter visited Mr. Zhou’s home. At this point, Mr. Zhou's wife, Ms. Ge, had largely recovered from her injuries. She recounted to the reporter the painful events of over a month ago. The incident originated from a spot of phlegm on the screen door of the Zhou household. Mr. Zhou's wife was just about to wash it off when, unexpectedly, an accident occurred.

同期声: Mrs. Zhou (Ms. Ge) "She (the neighbor) came from behind me, and I didn't know. I accidentally got some water on her. She walked over here, grabbed me and started beating me. Her husband came from behind and pulled my hair, hitting and kicking me. If it weren't for a kind-hearted person helping me, I would have been unable to catch my breath. I said, 'You can't keep hitting me.'"

After the incident, Mrs. Zhou went to the Fourth People's Hospital for a check-up. The results showed that Ms. Ge had multiple minor injuries and a mild concussion. After treatment, the medical expenses amounted to over a thousand yuan. When Mr. Zhou sought compensation from the neighbor, they firmly refused. In the following period, conflicts between the two parties continued.

When the reporter located Mr. Zhou's neighbor, the situation seemed more complicated than what Mrs. Zhou described.

同期声: Mr. Zhou's neighbor, Mr. Wang, "You say I injured you, but where exactly are these injuries? Why didn't you see a doctor on the same day? It took four or five days before you saw a doctor. Should I pay for your medication? Why didn't you go to another hospital..."

According to Mr. Zhou's neighbor, Mr. Wang, the incident was caused by the other party. On the day of the conflict, his wife was also injured. Mrs. Zhou not only refused to apologize but also brought people to intimidate him and damaged his property. Therefore, Mr. Wang believed their losses should also be compensated.

The local police station informed the reporter that after the incident occurred, they conducted multiple mediations. However, due to the lack of cooperation from surrounding residents, investigations and evidence collection were difficult, and the current outcome of the handling is still pending.

同期声: Reporter, "This matter can only be resolved through impartial judgment. Otherwise, you will only escalate the conflict further. You are still neighbors, seeing each other every day. You need to continue coexisting in the future. The truth must be investigated through legal means. Whoever is responsible must take responsibility, right?"

Mr. Zhou, "Yes, yes, we are in a society governed by law. We must trust the government."

After the reporter's persuasion and mediation, both parties agreed not to engage in direct conflicts anymore and awaited a fair legal verdict.
