Yangshuo countryside tour, Yulong River rafting one day tour

by zengxr840824 on 2008-05-30 09:12:29

Related Attractions

Yulong River, Bicycle

Itinerary Arrangement

Take a bicycle tour of Yangshuo countryside. After arriving at the pier, start the rafting on the Yulong River. After the rafting ends, the guide will recommend having authentic Yangshuo farmhouse meal (at your own expense) in Lincun. Then continue to ride bicycles to visit the Ten-Mile Gallery on land in Yangshuo, Moon Mountain, and Big Banyan Tree. Return to Yangshuo county town and end the pleasant journey!

Itinerary Price: 150 yuan per person

Price Details:

1. This itinerary includes two people on one bamboo raft. If there is only one person in the group, an additional fee of 100 yuan for the bamboo raft and guide service is required.

2. Children under 1.2 meters tall are free of service charges. Children over 1.2 meters tall are charged as adults.

What's Included in the Above Price:

1. Bamboo raft drifting on the Yulong River, Ten-Mile Gallery on land in Yangshuo, Moon Mountain, Big Banyan Tree.

2. One rented bicycle in Yangshuo, high-quality guide service, travel agency liability insurance.

What's Not Included in the Above Price

1. Meals are at the tourists' own expense. We recommend you enjoy farmhouse meals in Lincun.

2. Personal accident insurance is not included. For those who voluntarily purchase travel accident insurance, please inform us in advance. The insurance premium for domestic tourists is 10 yuan per person with a coverage of 100,000 yuan. For foreign tourists (including compatriots from Hong Kong, Macao, and Taiwan), the premium is 30 yuan per person with a coverage of 300,000 yuan.

Participation Notes

1. Yangshuo countryside tours and Yulong River rafting attract countless Chinese and foreign tourists. Therefore, Yangshuo was rated as one of the world's top ten self-guided travel destinations in 2006. Especially the bamboo raft dam-crossing drift for two people is highly sought after.

Remarks

1. This itinerary is a completely self-guided tour with no shopping stops.

2. We provide airport pick-up and drop-off services for tourists. A 7~11 seat business car costs 150 yuan per trip. The one-way distance from the airport to downtown hotels is about 40 kilometers, and the market price for a 7~11 seat minivan is 200 yuan per trip.
