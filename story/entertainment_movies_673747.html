Feng Xiaogang holds a party in Chongqing, spends 200 million yuan to invite a pianist for the event transition

by zls122 on 2012-02-23 20:15:43

200 million

"In 1942" has a high investment of 200 million yuan. The president of Huayi Brothers, Wang Zhonglei, also once expressed that "the investment can be increased". This means that the production side expects to ease the pressure on domestic box office revenue through income from international markets.

2000 people

The film started with 2000 extra actors. Feng Xiaogang once sighed: "The concept of two thousand extras is: 40 buses for transportation, meals for two thousand people, and the cost of one shoot is about 350,000 yuan (not including costume and prop production costs), and the cost of just the extras reaches 11.5 million yuan."

5 months

A long outdoor shooting time of 5 months made Feng Xiaogang not only hardworking but also inevitably complain on Weibo. The release date is set for this summer.

"The movie 'In 1942' has helped me understand history, blowing away a lot of dust, revealing the truth of history. This is also why I want to make it." - Feng Xiaogang

After finishing the arduous filming in Shanxi, Feng Xiaogang's heavyweight film of the year, 'In 1942', which has an investment of 200 million yuan, moved to Chongqing. Recently, the advance team of the crew has arrived in Chongqing to build the exterior scenes. Subsequently, Feng Xiaogang will start shooting with Oscar-winning actor Adrien Brody, along with Chen Daoming, Li Xuejian, Su Youpeng, Ke Lan, and others.

Yesterday, a reporter from Huaxi Metropolis Daily secretly visited the set in Chongqing ahead of time. Whether at the Stilwell Museum or at the French Navy Barracks, the presence of crew members could be seen, and the scene building work is almost complete, waiting for Feng Xiaogang's arrival. According to the latest information obtained by the reporter from Huaxi Metropolis Daily, Feng Xiaogang and other big stars will arrive in Chongqing today, and there will be two important night scenes shot this weekend.
