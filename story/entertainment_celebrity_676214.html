Yin Xiaotian beats people

by edivista on 2012-02-25 21:20:22

http://www.7weibo.com/blog/post/711.html

On the evening of February 24, a Weibo user named Zhi Xin Yu revealed that actor Yin Xiaotian had recently been involved in a physical altercation with actress Bian Xiaoxiao on the set of "Bullet Rain" in Hengdian due to creative differences. Subsequently, actor Jia Nailiang forwarded the post and stated that the actress was his classmate, and he had confirmed the incident over the phone.

At 9:18 PM on the evening of February 24, Zhi Xin Yu posted on Weibo: "Today, I witnessed something shocking at the filming site of 'Bullet Rain' in Hengdian. The male lead actually resorted to violence against a female actress after a disagreement about the creative direction. The female actress was immediately protected by the staff, and the entire crew was indignant. This male actor is extremely arrogant; when his mood is bad or he lacks motivation, he refuses to film. He demands that co-actors follow his every wish—standing while reciting lines, conveying full emotions, and even shedding tears—his behavior is utterly unreasonable!" The Weibo post explicitly identified the male actor as Yin Xiaotian. By midnight on the 25th, the post had been retweeted approximately 2,800 times and received around 800 comments. Actress Bian Xiaoxiao subsequently forwarded the post, confirming she was the victim: "Our work relies on team effort—it's not just one person's solo performance. There will be disagreements and conflicts during work, but they require mutual cooperation. Where is professional ethics? Where is integrity? And how can a man hit a woman? No matter what, hitting someone is wrong, and girls working outside should always protect themselves!"

Subsequently, actor Jia Nailiang retweeted the post, stating that the actress was his classmate, and he had confirmed the incident via phone: "I just called to verify this, and it turns out to be true. My classmate was crying uncontrollably, and I am very angry. Being approachable and having both moral integrity and artistic excellence leads to greatness! It’s not easy for a girl to venture out into the world, Xiao Xiao, stay true to yourself—we all support you! Senior, you need to reflect on your actions!!!" He also declared his intention to stand up for his friend: "I thought about how some people might exploit or misinterpret this before posting, but does that mean I should endure it silently?! If one day I don’t have the courage to stand up for my classmates, would you want me to become like that? I’m willing to be the cannon fodder for my friend!"
