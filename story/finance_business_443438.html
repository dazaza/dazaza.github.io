Gourmet flower

by qaswr673 on 2011-06-09 12:53:14

This word is really good, I don't know who discovered it, but it is indeed very suitable to describe some people around me.

That day, when I was eating "Haidilao" with my classmate Bubble, she took advantage of my inattention and ordered N=N+1 portions of content. We had clearly agreed that we would all learn to be ladies and not too savage, but the result was that all the half-portion things were changed to whole portions by her. I strongly recommended Haidilao's private specially made soybean chili sauce to her, but she hardly ate a few bites and claimed that it would fill her stomach and affect her performance later hh

My eating habit belongs to the "no meat, no joy" type, so I need to be with friends who like vegetables to be "inspired" or "cultivated", in order to change my current extremely unhealthy diet structure. Unfortunately, I made a bad choice of friends hh

Mr. Ren held the menu for a long time without letting go. Suddenly, he realized that we didn't have a single dish, and then asked with a sense of conscience: "Should we order some vegetables to mix?" I was just about to marvel at how hard-won this sentence was, when she quickly answered herself in a flash: "Still all meat is better" hh #%￥# Friends, take a look, this is power!

Also, I met someone like me who is not very self-disciplined. Meeting together with her, this plan to control diet will probably never be achieved in this life. In that case, I can only let it go hh

The benefit of perseverance is that I found that the duck tongues that Bubble strongly recommended are indeed "very very" delicious. Another insight is that from now on, I will only order "red pot" at "Haidilao". When boiling fish slices, remember to use the ladle holder. Astrological knowledge. Also, eating sesame paste as seasoning takes up more space in the stomach. It must be paired with garlic oil + oyster sauce + vinegar. This suddenly reminded me of the feeling of eating "Ba Zi Hot Pot" together in the past. Even if I gain a few pounds because of this, it should be nothing serious. The three gentlemen of Fans family have said: "What's the big deal in life? Eat to your heart's content today, and start to lose weight tomorrow!"
