(1) Marrying a man without an economic foundation is a kind of adventure; even if he succeeds in his career in the future, he will look for a younger woman. By then, you would have lost your competitive ability, and who could you turn to?

(2) "You must not marry those men who love to spend money recklessly, gamble, drink, and do not like saving. These kinds of people may be rich now, but they will become poor in the future, and you will be dragged down by him."

(3) "Getting rich by marrying a wealthy person is much faster than getting rich through one's own struggle."

(4) Modern marriage can be done in two steps. The first step is to try to find a rich person to marry; the second step is that when you have money, you can then look for your ideal 'Prince Charming,' perhaps a Canadian overseas student."

(5) Having money doesn't necessarily guarantee winning a woman, but not having money definitely won't work.

(6) Having money doesn't necessarily guarantee winning a woman, it depends on how much you're willing to offer.

(7) 80% of women can be swayed by money, 20% cannot because they are richer than you.

(8) Women need love, and love is measured by money.

(9) Classic question: "Can you afford me?"

(10) Classic accusation: "Are you asking me to drink the cold west wind with you?"

(11) Classic promise: "If you have a car and a house, I will marry you right now."

(12) Foreign classic: No money? No Honey! (Without money, there's no honey!)

(13) How much money you spend on me indicates how much you love me; if you're unwilling to spend money on me, it shows that money means more to you than I do.

(14) Even if you succeed in winning her over, it might not last long as her desires grow faster than your wealth.

(15) Since men can be bought off with money, why can't women use money to achieve the same?

(16) It’s due to financial constraints, after all, even the cleverest housewife can't cook a meal without rice.

(17) Women come in two types: smart ones who can be swayed by money now, and dull ones who will only be swayed by money later.

(18) Without money, you can win over women you don’t like; with money, you can win over women you do like.

(19) Women who can be bought off with money, I don't want; but the women I want all require me to have money.

(20) Economy is the base, this is the truth.

(21) Women can be swayed by money, and money can be swayed by women.

(22) Women can be swayed by money, men can make money through women.

(23) Women need security, and money brings security!

(24) Women who don't care about money certainly exist, they are celestial maidens untouched by worldly affairs.

(25) Wealthy men talk both about feelings and money with women; poor men only talk about feelings and not money.

(26) Women lacking money can be swayed by money, women not lacking money cannot be swayed by money.

(27) Men before women: with money, they're dragons; without money, they're worms.

(28) Men only stand tall when they have money.

(29) Rather than waiting for you to have money and go after young women, let me have money and go after young men in the future.

(30) Short-sighted women talk about feelings first, then money; far-sighted women talk about money first, then feelings.

(31) Love only lasts for a while, bread can last a lifetime.

(32) Women like wealthy men, men prefer wealthy women even more.

(33) If Huang Shiran lived in modern times with superior family conditions, he might appear suave and elegant. With money, why wouldn't you marry him? Even if he's older, it doesn't matter.

(34) If I married a rich man like 'Huang Shiran,' I could donate his money to charity to help those in need.

(35) Falling in love with a rich person is just as easy as falling in love with a poor person, so why choose a poor person?

(36) A man who can sway a woman without money must be very extraordinary.
