How to Quickly Improve Website Ranking

For many webmasters, there are numerous methods to enhance keyword rankings. Those experienced in the field often summarize and share these techniques! Therefore, many of these methods are already well-known. However, today Hefei Network Company will avoid those conventional methods and briefly discuss other ways to improve website rankings, hoping to be helpful to both new and experienced webmasters. Of course, these unconventional approaches do not include cheating!

1. Take the Essence and Discard the Dross

Regarding the homepage issue, we need to be targeted. We can spend time analyzing our competitors' websites, checking their inclusion status, which articles are included, examining their external link situation, where they place their external links, and observing the website's movements at all times. Generally, websites ranked on the first page tend to be relatively stable without significant fluctuations. This is why many webmasters say that after a website has been established, later optimization becomes much simpler. At this point, we need to pay attention to new websites growing rapidly, as their sudden entry onto the first page could impact your site since the positions on the first page are limited.

2. The 11th Position Problem for Websites

The eleventh position for websites might be a headache for many webmasters. Looking at the top ten, aside from some promotions, there may be a few websites that have or are continuously engaging in Hefei website optimization, and the rest are likely Baidu products. Regarding Baidu products, some friends have mentioned Baidu Zhidao (Baidu Knows). Baidu Zhidao often features self-asked and self-answered questions. When we find that the content ranking at the front is self-asked and self-answered, we can report it to Baidu. Once verified, such content will naturally be removed. The main reason Baidu products, especially older information, dominate the first page should be that the website itself has not reached the standard required for that ranking position. At this moment, we can create relevant information for Baidu products. Since it is similar information but newer and better done, our content will occupy this position at this time. This also adds a high-quality external link to ourselves, and entering the first page is just a matter of time.

3. Website Reporting or Sabotage

Website reporting is still feasible. If you discover that a website ranking on the first page is involved in cheating, you can report it. After the website is reported and verified, it will inevitably suffer from a penalty, losing its ranking once penalized, and your website will naturally rise. As for website sabotage, it is not recommended because it's not easy for every webmaster to build a site. However, sometimes this can also be considered a method. Taking appropriate measures to cause punishment for the opponent's website allows you to enter the first page.

4. Page Dwell Time

When users search for related information and enter a website, the longer they stay reflects a higher user experience to a certain extent. If users enter a junk website, they will leave within a very short period. This is what is meant by "the good gets better, and the bad gets worse." There are two ways through which this method can improve your ranking:

(1) Start from your own website. After entering the website, open the page and let it stay open for a while.

(2) Start from the competitor's website. A short page dwell time indicates poor user experience. Therefore, at this time, we only need to quickly leave after entering the competitor’s website. Over time, this repeated action will affect the competitor’s website.

5. Website Traffic

Many webmasters know that Baidu uses such a standard when judging the quality of a website: the click-through rate after users search for a website using keywords, which is commonly known as the click-through rate. In general, after users input keywords, websites with higher traffic are judged to have a higher user experience. For websites with higher user experience, they will inevitably receive a good ranking. When a website's traffic gradually increases, the website will gain attention. If the website is well-built and provides good user experience, the ranking will also improve.
