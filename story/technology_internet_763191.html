Taobao store transfer online application officially opens

by anonymous on 2013-07-29 17:02:33

Taobao’s online entry point for “online store transfer” has officially opened. This means that in the future, online store operators will be able to submit a "transfer" application to the platform as long as they meet some necessary conditions; after the transfer, the store's credit rating will remain unchanged, and all business-related activities will be uniformly retained.

After the online application entry for store transfers opens, shop operators can directly enter the "store transfer" entry through the "Customer Service" operation section on the seller center page. They can then choose the type of transfer application based on their needs. Currently, the types of transfers supported include divorce by agreement, divorce by court order, and legal inheritance. After clicking the selection, the corresponding approval process begins.

The application process can be completed autonomously by the store operator through online submission. From selecting the transfer type, signing the application form to finally changing the binding of Alipay, there are six steps to complete the transfer. The system can provide real-time feedback on the progress of the application process and the required materials for each step.

As early as the beginning of the year, a post on the Taobao forum titled "What Happens to Our Online Store After Divorce?" caught the attention of many Taobao sellers. Within a week, the post garnered over 100,000 clicks and replies, sparking heated discussions among industry insiders and experts.

Moreover, if the registered owner of an online store passes away and normal operations cease, whether the income source that supports the livelihood of a family will be cut off is a question that has drawn significant attention from Taobao's 7 million store owners.

In fact, from the implementation of the store transfer rules in March this year to the current opening of the online application entry, Taobao has conducted user demand surveys, analyzed the background and risks of transferring stores, and ultimately confirmed that transfers must be based on facts and carried out under legally recognized circumstances. At the same time, explanations were provided regarding the materials applicants need to submit.

For divorce and inheritance transfers, applicants are required to provide: divorce certificate, property division notarization certificate, death certificate, notarized will certificate or inheritance division agreement notarization certificate. The regulations also require that applicants must be between 18-70 years old, and all ongoing transactions in the store must be completed before the transfer.

At the same time, Taobao imposes certain constraints on both parties involved in the store transfer. For example, any service agreements signed by the original store will be taken over after the transfer. Additionally, if the transferee already has an open store or is under penalty, the transfer will temporarily not be supported. A store transfer fee must be paid during the application process, and any outstanding loans from the original store must be repaid.

It is understood that more types of store transfers will be added in the future, and related processes and surveys are currently underway.
