Heavenly Devilish Resolution Spontaneous assistance of more than 20 passers-by to an injured senior during morning exercise in Shenzhen

by sznstejx3q3 on 2012-03-02 08:26:49

Citizen Morality, Backbone of Society

Reporters reported: On the morning of the 20th, at Shiling Ridge Park in Bujia, Longgang, Shenzhen, a 65-year-old man accidentally fell while descending the mountain and broke his leg. More than 20 kind-hearted people spontaneously came to his rescue and helped the emergency medical personnel from 120 carry the elderly man down the mountain to send him to the hospital for treatment.

When reporters climbed to the top of the park's mountain on the tip-off from readers, the old man had already been sent to the hospital for treatment. Aunt Li, who participated in the rescue, said that when the white-haired old man finished his morning exercise and was coming down the mountain along the stone steps, he stepped on a garbage bag in the middle of the way. The bag was wet with water, making it very slippery, and as a result, he slipped and rolled down.

Aunt Li introduced that upon seeing the old man fall, many passersby began shouting for help. Upon hearing the cries for help, those dancing halfway up the slope, practicing swordsmanship at the waist of the mountain, and climbing the mountain all rushed over, about 20 people in total. Among them were white-haired elders, young men in their thirties, and even children over ten years old. They carried the old man to the rest pavilion on the mountaintop. Some called 110 to report the incident, while others noticed the blood still flowing from the old man’s foot and used towels to bandage the wound. Worried that the old man's head shouldn't lie directly on the ground, one young man surnamed Lei supported the old man's head on his own leg.

Someone also tried calling the old man's son, but as both the son and daughter-in-law were working night shifts and had their phones turned off, they then dialed the telephone number of the residential complex management office where the old man lived, asking them to help contact other family members.

About 20 minutes later, an ambulance from a nearby Zhonghai Hospital arrived. The kind-hearted passersby assisted the 120 medical staff in placing the old man on a stretcher and carrying him down the mountain. Only after seeing the old man safely loaded onto the ambulance did they disperse.

The reporter subsequently went to the hospital and met the injured old man, Mr. Fan. By this time, his family had also received the news and rushed to the hospital to take care of him. It was learned that the old man was from Hubei province and was 65 years old. According to the doctor, the old man had suffered a comminuted fracture of the right ankle and needed immediate surgery.

Mr. Fan recounted that he fell and got injured after slipping on the garbage bag. At the time, many passersby gathered around to help. These kind-hearted people helped the doctors carry him down the mountain without leaving their names. He sincerely expressed his gratitude from the bottom of his heart: "I wish them good fortune!" When saying this, the reporter noticed the old man wiping away a tear with the corner of his sleeve.
