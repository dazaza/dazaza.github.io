JAVA mobile game developer Zhangyu Infinite transforms to intelligent platform, first batch of products online

by anonymous on 2013-07-29 18:03:56

On July 27th, Haiyuang Huang, COO of domestic mobile game developer iDreamSky, stated in an interview with TechWeb during Chinajoy that as a JAVA game developer since 2008, they have fully transitioned to the smartphone platform under the trend of mobile internet. Huang revealed that iDreamSky has launched its first batch of products after the transformation - "Dragon General" and "Great Talk of Immortals", and is expected to launch more than twenty mobile game products in the second half of the year.

iDreamSky was founded in 2008, mainly focusing on cross-platform single-player and online mobile games suppliers and publishers. Currently, there are about 50 products in operation, mainly consisting of single-player and online games, covering platforms such as JAVA, middleware, iOS, Android, Brew, Bada, etc.

Based on the JAVA platform, iDreamSky has always been dedicated to the development of single-player games, with annual revenue reaching up to tens of millions. Huang stated that starting from the first half of 2012, the company began considering transitioning to the smartphone platform, with products moving from single-player games to strong online games.

"We went through a one-year transformation period, which was extremely painful," Huang said. In fact, game companies from the JAVA era were not successful in transitioning to smart platforms, involving reintegration of development teams and accumulation in technology, channel operations, etc. During this process, iDreamSky remained very low-key and had few actions in the market.

Huang stated that having a "tolerance for error" attitude during the transformation period was key, and finding the root cause and making timely corrections in failure. She revealed that during the transformation process, the company prepared a specific budget as a trial-and-error cost. "We didn't directly jump from JAVA single-player games to strong online games in the smart domain, but instead went through an adaptation phase of Android weak online games, which also required sufficient patience and persistence."

The two key products promoted by iDreamSky this year, "Dragon General" and "Great Talk of Immortals", represent their current focus. "Dragon General" is a game that combines "King of Fighters" and "Sanguosha", co-developed by iDreamSky and a third party. The Android version has already been released, and the iOS version is currently under review, expected to be launched at the beginning of August. "Great Talk of Immortals" is a card game self-developed by iDreamSky, with distinct Chinese characteristics, fitting the current "nostalgia wave". It is also expected to be launched in August. Besides these two products, iDreamSky will also launch more than twenty products in the second half of the year. (Chak)
