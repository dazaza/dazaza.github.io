Local specialties: Sunflower Chicken 95% of the feed for sunflower chickens is fresh sunflower components, with a fragrant taste and crispy bones, containing 10 times more vitamin E than market chickens. Xinken Lotus Root Panyu is a famous land of fish and rice, with a wide variety of agricultural by-products. The local delicacies made from fresh local agricultural products have a strong regional flavor. Panyu Xinken lotus roots are large and starchy, a major specialty of the area. Many restaurants use them to create signature dishes, attracting many diners from Guangzhou and Hong Kong/Macau. Among these, braised pork belly with lotus root, braised duck meat with lotus root, fermented tofu stir-fried lotus slices are all local specialties. The most unique dish is green bean stuffed lotus root. Chefs first cook the green beans until they are peeled, then stuff them into the lotus root holes, add special ingredients, and stew until ready to serve. This dish is both powdery and fragrant, delicious and tasty. Shawan Ginger Milk Pudding: Sweet and Beneficial When seeking delicious food in Panyu, be sure to try Shawan's ginger milk pudding, a traditional snack passed down since the 1930s, suitable for modern tastes. At Shawan's Qinfangyuan Dessert Shop, according to Zeng Hui Zhuang, the successor of the famous ginger milk pudding master "Milk King," making ginger milk pudding requires selecting yellow, high-starch ginger, and using concentrated fresh water buffalo milk. During production, ginger juice is placed at the bottom of the bowl, and milk around 80°C is poured in and mixed evenly. If a spoon can be placed on the surface of the milk without sinking, the ginger milk pudding is considered successful.
