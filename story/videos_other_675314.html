I. The promotion of new generation information technology and informatization opens up broad growth space for the software industry

According to the "12th Five-Year" plan outline, as one of the strategic emerging industries, during the "12th Five-Year", new generation information technology will be given policy support in national economic transformation and thus open up a broad development space for information technology. The imbalance in information technology development and differences in the technical development cycle determine the existence of structural investment opportunities in the future information technology. Focus on the software and service industry, high prosperity intelligent terminal industry chain, domestic new market start-up (EMV migration, mobile payment), China Unicom benefiting from the 3G era, broadband access investment wave.

In the next five years, there is still broad development space for the domestic software industry, for the following reasons: wider coverage and greater strength of new software policies; In the "12th Five-Year", as one of the seven strategic emerging industries, the new generation information technology will be key cultivated under policy support and background of economic transformation. It mainly includes new-generation mobile communications, the next generation of the Internet, three-network convergence, the Internet of Things, cloud computing, integrated circuits, new displays, high-end software, high-end servers, and information services. During the "12th Five-Year", China will comprehensively improve the level of informatization, accelerate the construction of the next-generation national information infrastructure that is broadband, integrated, secure, and ubiquitous, promote the deep integration of informatization and industrialization, and promote informatization in all fields of economic and social development. Mobile Internet, three-network convergence, the Internet of Things, and cloud computing will achieve world interconnection, greatly changing life and work models, and greatly expanding the application space for software information. The advancement of informatization will form a powerful pull on the growth of software and services. It is expected that the annual compound growth rate of the software industry during the "12th Five-Year" period will not be less than 25%. The growth rate of software services will exceed 30%.

II. Continuation of Prosperity in the Intelligent Terminal Industry Chain

1. General Decline in the Semiconductor Industry

Affected by the European debt crisis, the global economy is not performing well, and both consumer PCs and commercial PC sales have been affected. Adding to this, the floods in Thailand have led to low demand for semiconductors. In October, global semiconductor revenue was $25.7 billion, a slight decrease of 0.1% month-on-month and a year-on-year drop of 2.28%. The order-to-shipment ratio indicates industry decline. The preliminary estimate of the September North America semiconductor equipment order-to-shipment ratio (BB value) was 0.75. The BB value has been below 1 for 12 consecutive months, indicating weak equipment expenditure by semiconductor manufacturers and a cautious outlook for the semiconductor industry.

The capacity utilization rate decreased slightly quarter-on-quarter in Q2. Inventory days decreased as manufacturers cut production to cope with the decline. According to IHS iSuppli's inventory research report, due to manufacturers cutting production in response to weak demand prospects, the inventory days of analog semiconductor suppliers are expected to fall by 4.4 days in Q3, marking the largest decline since 2009. For manufacturers, the decrease in inventory days in Q3 shows they are becoming more cautious for the rest of the year.

It is estimated that the current sluggish situation will continue until Q1-Q2 of 2012. With the arrival of the peak selling season in the second half of 2012, the semiconductor industry's prosperity will rebound somewhat, but the semiconductor industry will only show a slight growth trend in the future. WSTS expects global semiconductor industry revenue to reach $302 billion in 2011, increasing slightly by 1.7%. As the European debt crisis is resolved and the global economy recovers slightly, both consumer and commercial PCs will see improvement, with expected growth rates of 3.7% and 5.8% respectively in 2012-2013.

2. Explosive Growth of Smart Terminals (Smartphones, Tablets)

The world is entering the 3G era, with mobile bandwidth significantly increased. The excellent experience brought by smartphones led by IPHONE and followed by the Android camp, along with increasingly rich application services, mutually catalyze and promote the flourishing development of mobile Internet. Smartphones have entered a phase of explosive growth. According to IDC data, global smartphone sales were approximately 165 million units in Q2, an increase of 64.4% quarter-on-quarter. IDC expects that this year's total smartphone sales volume will grow by 55% compared to last year. IHS iSuppli predicts that by 2015, smartphones will account for 54.4% of total mobile phone shipments, with shipments growing from 294.3 million units in 2010 to 1.03 billion units in 2015, with a compound annual growth rate of 28.5%. The competitive landscape of the smartphone market has undergone significant changes, entering a tripartite pattern dominated by iPhone, Android, and Windows Phone, with the rise of domestic smartphone manufacturers such as Huawei and ZTE. Meanwhile, the launch of iPad ignited the tablet computer market, which will also continue to grow rapidly in the next 3-5 years.

The launch of the iPad series ignited the tablet computer market, and the introduction of Android and Windows tablet computers in the future will further accelerate the popularization of tablet computers. According to Gartner's forecast, global tablet computer sales in 2011 will reach 70 million units (IDC forecasts 53.5 million units, ARM company forecasts 60 million units), about four times the global tablet computer sales of 17.6 million units in 2010, with sales in 2012 expected to reach 108 million units. According to Sanford C. Bernstein & Co. analyst Toni Sacconaghi's forecast, global tablet computer shipments will grow from 17 million units last year to 215 million units in 2015, with an annual compound growth rate of 66%.

The rapid development of smartphones is accompanied by a reshuffling of the competitive landscape in the mobile phone market, forming a tripartite pattern of iPhone (iOS), Android, and Windows Phone. The free and open Android operating system platform promotes the rise of emerging mobile phone manufacturers such as HTC, ZTE, and Huawei, while traditional mobile phone leader Nokia lacks a platform and is declining in the smartphone era.

The rapid growth of downstream smartphones and changes in the competitive landscape promote the rapid growth and structural changes in the upstream smartphone industry chain, with the capacitive touch screen industry rising against the trend. Meanwhile, as the main global mobile phone production base and the rise and fall of mobile phone manufacturer competition patterns, some domestic smartphone accessory enterprises (connector manufacturer Changying Precision, antenna manufacturer Sunway Communication) have experienced rapid growth. Domestic smartphone brands have limited influence, offering limited investment opportunities. Advantageous manufacturers in the upstream smartphone industry chain will benefit, including smartphone antenna manufacturer Sunway Communication and connector manufacturer Changying Precision.

The semiconductor slump results in unclear short-term investment opportunities in components, requiring the arrival of an industry recovery cycle. However, Derun Electronics' product structure upgrade, with the release of high-end connector capacity and improved gross profit margin, is entering a period of rapid growth, worthy of attention.

III. Profile of Key Listed Companies

1. Hikvision: Leading the Development of the Video Surveillance Market

The company is a leading video surveillance manufacturer with relatively advanced R&D and strategy. The product + solution + EPC model will elevate the company's strategy to a higher level, fostering closer relationships with high-end customers. The Chongqing project serves as a good demonstration for high-end market expansion and can be replicated and expanded elsewhere. In the future, with the increase in video surveillance penetration, digital upgrades at the front end, and the product solution and project approach, the company will continue to grow rapidly.

2. Soft Control: Multiple Points of New Products, New Fields, and New Markets Support Sustainable Growth

Soft Control still has strong growth momentum in the future. After three years of cultivation, the semi-steel business is starting to grow faster. Domestic tire manufacturers are accelerating their layout of semi-steel tire capacity. In March 2011, the company received a 5.7 billion yuan semi-steel order, with most of the revenue realized in 2012. The company has hired German experts to accumulate internal strength and aims to develop into a supplier for multinational joint ventures in the future. The chemical field has enormous potential for development. Besides continuing to receive orders from Yisco, the company has newly opened up Luoyang Junma Chemical orders this year, with further room for development in the chemical market. Overseas markets are expected to make major breakthroughs, including cooperation with Vietnam, Thailand, and Iran, and Indian orders with larger market space in the future will also increase. In the future, with the rise in labor costs, the speed of industrial informatization and automation will accelerate, and the company's MES software and robot business have significant growth potential.

The company's management is outstanding, with obvious competitive advantages. Future sustainable growth momentum is extremely strong. It is estimated that the company can achieve a 30%-50% annual compound growth rate in the next three years, making it an excellent strategic investment.

3. UFIDA Software: Entering a New Phase of Growth

The company has ranked first in the domestic management software market share for nine consecutive years, with the share increasing year by year. Due to the company's advantages in channels, localized services, cost-performance ratio, and understanding of China's national conditions, its market share relative to foreign brand manufacturers will further increase in the future. Large-scale personnel expansion in the past two years has led to fast-rising expenses affecting profit growth. The market opening effects will become apparent in the coming years, with income accelerating and economies of scale gradually manifesting, resulting in profit growth outpacing revenue growth. Under the background of deepening management informatization, UFIDA will increase R&D efforts, improve operational efficiency, and maintain a relatively fast growth rate in the future.

4. Rongji Software: High Growth Trend Unchanged

The company leads in software细分 industries, including quality inspection, collaborative management, security, and e-government. Collaborative software is vigorously expanding into prefecture-level cities and counties, with the quality inspection network being rolled out nationwide and steady user growth. Network security and e-government are growing steadily. Breakthroughs have been made in the environmental protection sector. The company is still in a period of high-speed growth.

5. Hengbao Shares: EMV Migration and Mobile Payment Will Open Up Broad Blue Ocean for the Company

The company is the largest domestic bank card manufacturer with deep cooperation foundations in the banking system. It has qualified as an IC bank card supplier for most large banks except ICBC and ranks among the top in the IC bidding of Bank of China and Construction Bank of China. The company has qualified as a major supplier for mobile payments of the three operators and occupies a favorable position in future mobile payments. Its social security co-branded cards lead in the social security sector. The launch of EMV, mobile payment, and social security card markets brings enormous elasticity growth space for the company.

6. Sunway Communication: Benefiting from Rapid Growth of Smartphones and Optimization of Customer Structure and Product Technology Upgrade

The company is a domestically competitive mobile phone antenna manufacturer and will further narrow the gap with leading foreign manufacturers through talent introduction and enhanced R&D. The company actively optimizes its customer structure, developing large manufacturers like Samsung, Huawei, JABIL, and RIM while reducing non-strategic customers. Meanwhile, the company is actively developing clients such as MOTO smart phones and AMAZON e-books. There is substantial room for market share growth. Starting in the second half of the year, the company began mass supply of antenna connectors to RIM and plans to launch high-end, thinner, and globally leading laser 3D precision antennas within the first quarter of 2012.

7. Derun Electronics: Release of High-End Capacity, Clear Growth Trend

The company's high-end CPU and DDR connectors have entered the market and started scaling up, with high thresholds and fewer competitors, improving profit margins. The growth trend is clear for the next 2-3 years.

8. Changying Precision: Benefiting from the Explosion of Smartphones and Connector Industry Transfer

The company is a major supplier of shielding connectors for Huawei and ZTE and an important supplier for Samsung, benefiting from the rise of Huawei and ZTE in the smartphone era. Meanwhile, the company actively develops new customers and promotes the mass production of new products such as LED brackets, with a clear growth trend.
