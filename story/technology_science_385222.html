Robots to be Involved in Search and Rescue Operations for Japan Earthquake - Robot Intelligence Network

by yiyix on 2011-03-16 21:18:12

Japan, as the country with the most advanced and widely applied robot technology, will use robots in the rescue work of this earthquake. Rescue robots can reach areas that humans cannot enter, such as rubble and areas with nuclear contamination. According to foreign media reports, at least two rescue teams have been confirmed to use robots to assist in their work. One of these robots is a snake-like robot that is 26 feet (about 8 meters) long and equipped with an endoscopic camera, which can penetrate deep into the rubble to detect if there are any injured people present.
