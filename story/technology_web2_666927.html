www.vangjia.com Xiao Liu has just turned 21 years old and is a freshman at a university in Qingdao. He should be spending some of the most wonderful time of his life on campus. However, by chance Xiao Liu became addicted to drugs. In order to take drugs, he sold drugs as an agent for drug dealers. After being caught, he will face a life behind bars... Caught selling drugs to support his addiction Selling drugs, Xiao Liu didn't know that he had already been under police surveillance. When selling crystal meth to Li Mou, he was caught on the spot by public security personnel. Later, the public security personnel found five packets of white crystals at Xiao Liu's residence. After testing, these six packs of white crystals weighed a total of 4.1 grams and all contained methamphetamine (commonly known as crystal meth) components. Although only 17 years old, Xiao Zhang appeared quite formidable in Xiao Liu's eyes. After further interaction between the two, Xiao Liu actually discovered that Xiao Zhang was a drug dealer. The enormous cost of taking drugs gradually made Xiao Liu unable to cope. Being timid, he didn't dare ask his parents for money. Moreover, with his drug cravings often occurring and causing him great pain, Xiao Liu began working as a subordinate for the drug dealer Xiao Zhang, selling drugs. Reporter Xu Yongguang Share to: Welcome to post comments I want to comment In late August 2011, Xiao Zhang sold 7.3 grams of crystal meth to Xiao Liu in two separate transactions near Nanjing Road and Wenxing Road in Qingdao City for a total price of 4300 yuan. After obtaining the crystal meth, Xiao Liu used some himself. On August 23, 2011, he went to a residential building on Nanning Road in Sifang District and sold one pack of white crystals to Li Mou for 700 yuan. Xiao Liu lives in Shinan District, Qingdao City. His family is well-off, and after entering university this year, he has been diligently studying day and night. By chance, he accidentally consumed terrifying drugs and gradually became addicted. Xiao Liu fully understands the harm caused by drugs, but under the powerful onset of drug cravings, he who had already decided to quit drugs, had to repeatedly "surrender." In August 2011, while playing pool at a billiard hall, Xiao Liu met Xiao Zhang. After chatting, he learned that Xiao Zhang was younger than himself, only 17 years old this year, and originally from Heilongjiang Province. After his parents divorced, Xiao Zhang dropped out of junior high school and came to Qingdao with his mother. Because he graduated from junior high school and lacked specialized skills, Xiao Zhang never found a stable job. Freshman becomes addicted to drugs After Xiao Liu and Xiao Zhang were arrested, they quickly confessed voluntarily. Their relatives paid the fines on their behalf. Since both were first-time offenders, the court considered their situations when sentencing them. According to the Criminal Law of the People's Republic of China, both were convicted of drug trafficking. Xiao Liu was sentenced to one year in prison and fined 7,000 RMB; Xiao Zhang was sentenced to one and a half years in prison and fined 8,000 RMB. Under Xiao Liu's confession, Xiao Zhang was also soon arrested. Xiao Zhang admitted that his subordinates included not only Xiao Liu. On August 23, 2011, he sold 0.15 grams of crystal meth to Ruan Mou near Taishan Road No. 8 for 300 yuan. Xiao Zhang also admitted that his drugs all came from someone nicknamed "Huang Mao," who was his superior. Following the clues, with Xiao Zhang's cooperation, the police arrested "Huang Mao" (Gao Mou). Recently, the Sifang District People's Court legally held a hearing on this case. College student Xiao Liu expressed deep regret in court: "I deeply regret my actions. I hope the court will give me another chance to reform, allowing me to complete my studies and become a useful member of society."
