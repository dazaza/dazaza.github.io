The Wilde family's twin siblings, Noah (Chris O'Neil) and Emma (Rhiannon Leigh-Wryn), discover a mysterious box on the beach. This box, filled with strange objects, quickly becomes the children’s newest toy. As they play with these "toys," they begin to display increasingly astonishing intelligence and special abilities. In class, the teacher is amazed by the twins' insights, which surpass those of geniuses; at home, their parents notice some surprising changes.

At first, they thought their children were simply gifted, but brain scans reveal that the children's brains have developed beyond normal limits and are out of control. Increasingly worried about her children’s abnormalities, the mother learns from her daughter that the ragged toy rabbit named Mimzy is actually teaching them everything! One night, an accidental power outage disrupts the entire city, and the government traces the source of the outage to the Wilde family!

Events soon spiral out of control. The strange toy box seems to carry an important mission, and the seemingly simple toy rabbit may contain serious messages from the future. Through scientific testing, the National Security Agency discovers that the little rabbit is actually an ultra-advanced artificial intelligence organism! Everyone realizes that they have been caught up in an incredible and significant event, but what exactly is this event?
