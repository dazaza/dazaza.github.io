"Black Hand" - The Bloody Sins of the Real Estate Market's Unspoken Rules in the Bureaucracy

by jqrxass on 2009-10-09 15:46:23

The paper audit order pointed like a sharp sword to the Binjiang real estate market. Just at this time, the director of the audit bureau and a young female real estate developer were found naked and dead in a car. Rumors spread rapidly, and once again, the focus was on the thriving real estate market.

In this city, the real estate tycoon ruled the territory, with the mayor, the director of the Land and Resources Bureau, the director of the Public Security Bureau, and various other officials gathered under his wing. He turned the entire city into a huge money-making machine, using land and houses to build an economic empire devoid of morality and social responsibility, along with a hypocritical image of success.

Power, money, lust - in the smoke-filled real estate market, how much sin and bloodshed is hidden? Father, son, siblings - amidst the stormy winds and bitter rains of life's journey, they perform the final act of love, hate, and revenge.

"The Black Hand" Part One

Counting this one, it was already the third case of naked death in Binjiang City. These days, why do people favor this? Clothes piled up in the front row, Hua Yingying's designer suit was taken off in the driver's seat, Xiang Shusen's shirt and casual pants were taken off in the passenger seat. Before the car entered the garage, Xiang Shusen was sitting in the front row, but later, they ended up stark naked on the backseat. The rear seat of the BMW 7 Series, with its novel design, was very spacious, and lying down felt comfortable. However, it was unexpected that it would become the grave for two people.

"The Black Hand" Part Two

Based on his years of experience working in the public security bureau, he had his own judgment about the serial killings. First, the motive of the criminal was unclear. Whether it was "Zhu Jiahui" or "Guangtou Gang," both lacked the motive to kill. For wealth? Or for revenge? After Cheng Haoqing and the other three victims were killed, their properties were not damaged in any way, proving that the criminals were definitely not after money. Revenge? Cheng Haoqing was a low-profile person, Zhou Xiaoyun and Liu Jiawei were the same, and there hadn't been any talk of enemies. Moreover, in recent years, while developing their own businesses, they also engaged in social welfare activities. The three companies spent tens of millions of yuan annually on social assistance and relief. They had good reputations in Binjiang, especially Zhou Xiaoyun, whose Wantong Garden triggered a wave of price reductions in the housing market, causing the previously high prices per square meter in Binjiang to drop by more than 300 yuan, benefiting ordinary people. People like them, who could have enmity with whom? Without enmity, how could they be killed?

"The Black Hand" Part Three

Upon arriving at the municipal committee, the secretary informed him that Secretary Bingyang was waiting in the meeting room on the third floor. Fan Hongda ascended to the third floor with great displeasure, unexpectedly encountering Zeng Li in the elevator. Zeng Li was dressed exceptionally brightly this day, her rose-red long-sleeved blouse lighting up the entire elevator area in red. She wore a dark green long pant, accentuating her slender figure, making her appear younger. Fan Hongda did not have a deep impression of Zeng Li, but he had heard from Pang Bin and Liang Ping'an several times that this woman was very calculating, and it seemed that the two men harbored jealousy over her. Encountering her so closely today, Fan Hongda couldn't help but glance a few extra times. Zeng Li, on the other hand, greeted him warmly and confidently called out Mayor Fan, greeting him with both enthusiasm and propriety.

But recently, especially after meeting that woman, the plains seemed suddenly closer to Fan Hongda. Several times in his dreams, he dreamed of the plains. The plains were so vivid, blossoming widely before his eyes. He ran, played, fell, and got up again on the plains, only to fall again.
