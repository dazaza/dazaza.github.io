Northern Huangshan - Misty Spirit Mountain National Forest Park

by xiangwo on 2009-04-21 21:10:51

Tourist attractions around Beijing - Wuling Mountain is located in the northwest of Xinglong County, Chengde City, Hebei Province. It is surrounded by five cities: Beijing, Tianjin, Chengde, Tangshan, and Qinhuangdao, with very convenient transportation. Wuling Mountain is the main peak of the Yan Mountains, with an altitude of 2,118 meters. The total area of the park is 14,400 hectares, and the forest coverage rate is 76.2%. Within the forest park, the mountains are majestic, shrouded in mist and clouds, with overlapping green peaks. Streams flow gently, the forest sea is lush, trees reach to the sky, and there are rare flowers, exotic birds, wild animals, and flying creatures. The area is quite extensive, with most peaks having an altitude of over 1,500 meters.

Wuling Mountain, a good place for tourism around Beijing, is a national nature reserve and a provincial forest park, and is very famous among tourist attractions around Beijing. The plants within the park belong to the warm temperate deciduous broad-leaved forest. The forest is dense, with a wide variety of plant species, and the vertical distribution of plant communities is evident. Experts believe that "Wuling Mountain is a rare green treasure house not only among Beijing's tourist attractions but also nationwide, representing the typical vegetation type of North China." There are 1,870 species of higher plants, including 10 species listed as nationally protected plants, among which there are 178 species of rare wild flowers.

The unique geographical and historical environment, rich natural resources, and excellent ecological environment of Wuling Mountain have formed numerous natural and cultural landscapes. The only forest and wildlife type nature reserve around Beijing - the Wuling Mountain National Nature Reserve, the observatory with Asia's largest telescope - the Xinglong Observation Station of the Beijing Astronomical Observatory, the fifth relay station approved by Premier Zhou Enlai - the O Five Relay Station, nearly a hundred kilometers of Ming Dynasty Great Wall in its original state, the Six Li Ping Forest Park with towering pines, the mysterious Jin Niu Cave, and the dynamic Jiu Long Tan, etc., are all great places for tourism around Beijing. The Wuling Mountain area is well-known far and wide as the "Flower and Fruit Mountain," producing various kinds of dried and fresh fruits and their processed foods, which are sold both domestically and internationally. The abundant agricultural resources have laid a solid foundation for the development of Wuling Mountain agriculture. These landscapes are unseen among Beijing's tourist attractions.

Intense tectonic movements have made Wuling Mountain rise prominently from the Yan Mountains after several transformations between sea and land. Around the main peak, the mountains are high and the valleys deep, with peculiar terrain and geology. Peaks such as General Peak, Cool Boundary Peak, and Po Mo Ridge stand tall, while cliffs like Golden Eagle Cliff, Green Dragon Cliff, and Ten Thousand Feet Cliff are perilous. Structures like the Immortal Tower, Three Elephant Stones, and Mountain God Seat exhibit countless postures, earning it the reputation of "Northern Huangshan." Therefore, many Beijing tourists give up visiting other Beijing attractions to come here to appreciate the beauty of Northern Huangshan.
