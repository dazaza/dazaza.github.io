Posterous: Show up and get everything done via E-mail!

by xjyzhenai on 2008-12-24 09:43:38

You need to show yourself, and you can do it by email! Maybe you didn't think Posterous could do that for you. But what JarryJarry wants to share with you is a fact.

If you have access to email, you can have your own website, share ideas and media with friends, family and the world at large, and present yourself. Well, there's a new use for your email! Well, see what files you can send to Posterous? I don't think you need to worry about that anymore. Because you can send everything. But here are some common file formats to see if they meet your general needs:

image

The nice thing is: you don't have to bother registering. Just email your email to post@posterous.com and you can sign up at Posterous with your email address!

Then you will get a share address, similar to a BLOG, such as Jarry: http://jarry.posterous.com/ you can preview. Then you can start your POST! For example, if you want to share a DOC document, send it as an email to post@posterous.com. The document will appear on your page, and you don't need to download and open it, but directly view it online through the WEB! Similarly, send an MP3 can listen online, send a video, you can watch online, send a MM, you can YY online! :)

image

OK. It works very well. Chinese support is also very good! Posterous is arguably more powerful and simpler than some blogs. Because you don't have to do anything, just send an email and it's done!
