"Legend of the Tiger Talisman" Episode 1-30 Detailed Introduction of Final Episodes

Nuannu saw Xinning Jun and told him that she did not want to follow Rujie anymore, but instead wanted to return to Xinning Jun's side. However, Wujie refused and was determined to send her back. Nuannu started crying, and seeing her pitiful state, Wujie agreed to let her stay temporarily. At this moment, Mengchang Jun saw them and advised Wujie not to keep her. Wujie listened to his advice.

Nuannu returned to the residence of Changting Hou. Rujie questioned her about who she really was. Nuannu told Rujie that she was Xinning Jun's woman and was very disrespectful to Rujie. As a result, Rujie angrily left.

Changting Hou, seeing his daughter's sadness, became very angry and scolded Nuannu. Rujie advised her father to send Nuannu back, and thus, Nuannu left.

At night, Nuannu secretly sneaked into Xinning Jun's residence and stole his books. The next day, Xinning Jun and Mengchang Jun talked about it, and Mengchang Jun reminded him that it might be because he possessed rare books that everyone wanted.

King Zhao praised his wife as a heroine among women. She told King Zhao that she would go ask Nuannu if she encountered any trouble.

Xinning Jun was very angry when he knew that Nuannu had returned to the residence. He persuaded Nuannu to go back, but suddenly Nuannu hugged him and cried. Mengchang Jun called out to Xinning Jun, telling him that Nuannu absolutely could not stay in the residence. Xinning Jun didn't take it seriously.

Nuannu threatened to die unless she could stay, insisting on remaining. She also said that she was expelled by Changting Hou. Therefore, Xinning Jun had no choice but to let her stay. While sweeping the courtyard, Nuannu secretly slipped into Xinning Jun's study to search for the secret manual of Duke Zhou.
