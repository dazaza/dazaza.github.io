On September 4 and 5, a two-day investigation by the reporter showed: The Tianyi mobile phone campus package cannot connect or is unstable when calling China Mobile phones, but calling Tianyi mobile phones from China Mobile works fine; Tianyi mobile phones purchased elsewhere function normally within the higher education park. So far, more than a thousand mobile phones have been sold.

People's Network Wenzhou Channel reports on September 7, Wang Jiaqian arrived at school early on August 28. He was going to attend a meeting, as the team leader, he was very excited now.

This meeting mainly focused on how to promote the telecom mobile phones to new and old students coming to school soon.

This year is the first time Wenzhou Telecom has entered the university mobile phone market. Wenzhou Telecom has established its own student marketing teams in all schools in the Wenzhou Higher Education Park, and Wang Jiaqian is the team leader of Wenzhou Medical College.

Facing Wenzhou Mobile, which has occupied the top spot in the university mobile phone market for many years, Wang Jiaqian still had full confidence. "The main reason is that this telecom mobile phone package is extremely favorable," said Wang Jiaqian.

According to the reporter's understanding, the campus package launched by telecom includes prepaying 300 yuan for phone credit, allowing you to get a free mobile phone worth 300 yuan. Within the higher education park, calls to all telecom numbers nationwide are free, including landlines.

When talking about the sales scene on the first day (September 2), there was a hint of excitement in Wang Jiaqian's voice. "It was a hit," Wang Jiaqian said.

However, the "hit" only lasted one day, and Wang Jiaqian's joy also only lasted one day.

Starting from September 3, Wang Jiaqian was busy running between various dormitories and business halls. He had to go "put out fires," as students complained that the purchased telecom mobile phones could not connect to China Mobile phones. He kept explaining to students who had already bought and intended to buy telecom mobile phones: "I have already reported this issue to Wenzhou Telecom, it will be resolved soon." Sometimes, he would add, "The problem does not lie with telecom."

Wang Jiaqian was beginning to doubt. He told the reporter that whether making calls to fixed lines or Unicom with the mobile phones sold this time, there were no problems, "but when calling China Mobile phones, it’s always a busy signal." "Is it possible that China Mobile is really up to something like what the posts say?" he muttered to himself.

The post Wang Jiaqian referred to was published by netizen "dfxy" on the afternoon of September 3 on the Wenzhou Forum titled "China Mobile messing with China Telecom? Don't mess with us students!!!"

In the post, "dfxy" said that he/she just started school a few days ago and subscribed to the telecom campus package, and the calls were normal until yesterday when trying to call his/her mother's mobile phone, it wouldn't connect. Nearby classmates were the same, using telecom phones to call China Mobile phones couldn't connect either, but calling others worked fine. Telecom's response was, "it might be a China Mobile network issue, they are negotiating with China Mobile."

"dfxy" asked: "Could it be that China Mobile set up a block against telecom phones? Is that even possible?"

Subsequent replies showed some accusing China Mobile of "messing around", while others accused China Telecom.

Xu Weijie, head of Chashan Telecom Office, believed that accusing China Telecom of "messing around" was unreasonable. "If such a situation arises, we are the ones being complained about.
