Yangshuo West Street

by zengxr840824 on 2008-05-15 14:17:47

Xijie is an ancient block within Yangshuo county, with a history of over 1400 years. It's located in the center of the county, with a total length of 1,180 meters and a street width of 8 meters. The houses on Xijie are elegant and simple, reflecting the Ming and Qing style of Northern Guangxi - small green tiles, sloping roofs, whitewashed walls, and hanging balconies.

"Xijie" is a street filled with Western charm, a foreigner's street. It represents the unique face of Yangshuo. Due to its existence, the entire Yangshuo is filled with infinite warmth, and everyone who has been to Yangshuo's Xijie feels like they're coming home. As China's first foreigner street, the largest foreign language corner, and a true global village, "Xijie" has inspired numerous tourist cities and attractions across China to replicate it. Stone-paved roads, Northern Guangxi residences, bars, restaurants, hotels, folk artists, tourist souvenirs, climbing bars, tourists of different skin colors... "Xijie" truly has everything one could need.

"Xijie" is a Chinese town that condenses the essence of Chinese culture. The Western-style stalls on Xijie display traditional Chinese ornaments: cloth-dyed in cloisonné blue patterns, hand-embroidered sachets, hanging straps, various styles of Chinese knots, and blessings for peace. Shop after shop, situated in this natural town surrounded by mountains and water, attracts countless foreigners eager to learn about China. This sentiment ties every corner of the street together like a Chinese knot. Foreigners open restaurants and coffee shops here, enthusiastically infusing the small bourgeois ambiance into the Yangshuo town.

"Xijie" is an ancient street permeated with a strong historical and cultural atmosphere. This was once the place where Mr. Sun Yat-sen gave speeches, where the artist master Xu Beihong once resided, and where presidents, prime ministers, premiers, and ministers from over 150 countries have lingered. Clinton, Nixon, Kissinger, Thatcher, Ho Chi Minh, Sihanouk, and many others have visited. The staggered podiums and the stone slabs of Xijie bear witness to the hardships and glories of these masters, marking the imprints of China.

"Xijie" is an ideal paradise for taking a break from schooling and going on vacation. As the intersection of Chinese and Western cultures, Eastern traditional culture and Western modern civilization converge, exchange, and complement each other here. Touring Xijie allows one to experience foreign风情without leaving the country, and studying at Xijie is akin to studying abroad without actually leaving. This is the ideal classroom for Chinese people to learn foreign languages and foreigners to learn Chinese. Many people take holidays to bring their families to "see Xijie"; many schools organize English "summer camps" and "winter camps" to "study Xijie". The people of Xijie warmly welcome these "countrymen" from near and far, urban and rural areas, as if they were foreign guests. Isn't it a joy when friends come from afar?

When you walk on "Xijie", pacing leisurely in a modern way, you can hear the profound and rich sounds of culture. Its melody is so soft and distant, it is China's eternal sentiment, eternal knot, and eternal street.
