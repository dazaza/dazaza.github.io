A simple introduction to the types and usage of carpet cleaners.

by kaile6429 on 2010-05-31 19:01:52

There are roughly the following types of carpet cleaners. Now let me briefly introduce their characteristics and methods of use:

1. Dry foam carpet cleaner

Principle and application: Rich in various surfactants and carpet protectants, it can be used as a stain retardant, anti-corrosion agent, and whitening agent for cleaning all kinds of carpets.

Characteristics: (1) This product is neutral and suitable for use with foaming box-type carpet washing machines;

(2) Mix with water at a ratio of 1:30 for use;

(3) Using this product makes the carpet more vivid, soft, and reduces static electricity and dust adhesion.

Note: If accidentally splashed into the eyes, rinse with water or seek medical attention.

2. Low-foam carpet cleaner

Principle and application: Rich in various surfactants, brighteners, and softeners, it has a rapid cleaning ability for oil stains and can be safely used to clean carpets of various textures and colors.

Characteristics: (1) This product is neutral and suitable for use with extraction-type carpet washing machines;

(2) Mix with water at a ratio of 1:20 to 1:30 depending on the degree of dirt on the carpet;

(3) Using this product prevents the carpet from becoming sticky or moldy, keeping it fluffy and soft.

Note: If accidentally splashed into the eyes, rinse with water or seek medical attention.

3. High-foam carpet cleaner

Principle and application: Rich in various surfactants, it has the functions of stain retardation, anti-corrosion, whitening, and brightening, and can extend the life of the carpet.

Characteristics: (1) This product is neutral, suitable for high-foam carpet washing machines;

(2) Mix with water at a ratio of 1:30 for use;

(3) After using this product, the carpet becomes more vivid, fresh, and pleasant, reducing static electricity and dust adhesion.

Note: If accidentally splashed into the eyes, rinse with water or seek medical attention.

4. Carpet stain remover

Principle and application: This product is a powerful cleaner that can fully dissolve heavy dirt on the surface of the carpet, used for carpets that are not easy to wash.

Characteristics: (1) This product is alkaline, has an extremely strong cleaning effect on oil stains, coffee, and fruit juice on the carpet, pH value 12;

(2) Use diluted solution or concentrate based on the local dirt level of the carpet, then wipe clean with a soft cloth;

(3) This product should not be used on pure wool carpets.

5. Defoamer

Principle and application: This product is a highly effective concentrated product specifically designed to eliminate residual foam after cleaning and maintaining carpets and using foam-type products. It contains multiple defoaming components that can quickly eliminate large amounts of foam produced by foam-type products.

Characteristics: (1) This product is a concentrate;

(2) Suitable for various extraction-type machines and suction tubes, this product is neutral, pH value 6-7.

Note: If accidentally splashed into the eyes, rinse with water or seek medical attention.
