Another Time - Memories

On a rainy day, what's wrong with you? I can no longer see your smile. Why are you crying? The water on your face, is it tears or rain, I can't tell them apart anymore. I want to ask if you're okay, but I'm afraid of disrupting your pace. Please tell me, what should I do with you? It's raining now, are you crying? And so my heart aches again!

I once thought that time would make me forget those unpleasant memories, like flowing water easily fading away. I tried hard to learn how to forget, as memories suffocated me. I thought I would die from the pain, and when the memories were stirred up again by someone, I forgot how to breathe. Your arrival was never doubted as malicious by me, yet you made me lose myself repeatedly. I pretend to be composed as I walk past you, trying not to look at you, and forcing a smile to face it all. Are you too cruel or too kind? Your repeated looking back makes me sad over and over again. Am I not strong enough, or are you too unpredictable, causing the fortress I just built in my heart to crumble. If you can't stop for me, I can only forget, only learn to forget. Time won't stop for anyone, and I can only avoid reminiscing. I pray in my heart, maybe tomorrow when I wake up, I'll have forgotten. But I've never regretted meeting you. If regret could lead to growth, perhaps after letting go, I will smile and face the past, and we will grow. Maybe only by forgetting can we live happier lives. Perhaps the past written here will be forgotten by time. Behind every "perhaps" there should be some hope and waiting!

But I wish time could really make me forget about you. At least when I recall our memories again, I can still laugh and say, "It's a little regrettable!" And treat everything in life calmly, without pain.

In fact, I really want to ask you, who are you sad for? But I don't dare, nor do I want to. I'm afraid of being trapped again, unable to extricate myself. I think my place in your life has come to an end.

I want to be with you, but I am absent from your future. The love I gave you, those beauties, have grown into vines entwining my life.

Is love always the same? No matter how long it lasts, it eventually ends with separation. But I don't want to hesitate out of fear and end up disappointed halfway through.

I hope you are happy when I'm not around; I hope you are joyful when I no longer smile at you; I hope you forget me after I turn you into a memory. I sincerely bless you because I know the one who will accompany you far into the future won't be me. I also know I'm not your ultimate happiness, and you no longer need my smile. Because we both should forget the memories that belong to each other and live well beside another person at another time!
