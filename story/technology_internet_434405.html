Doctor Wang Dongxu introduced that, apart from having the nutritional components of ordinary sweet potatoes, purple sweet potatoes are also rich in selenium and anthocyanins. Doctor Wang Dongxu introduced that anthocyanins in purple sweet potatoes have preventive and therapeutic effects on more than 100 diseases. Anthocyanins are known as the seventh essential nutrient after water, protein, fat, carbohydrates, vitamins, and minerals. Anthocyanins are currently the most direct, effective, and safe free radical scavengers discovered by the scientific community for disease prevention and maintaining human health. Its ability to clear free radicals is 20 times that of vitamin C and 50 times that of vitamin E. Anthocyanins in purple sweet potatoes have a small molecular structure and are the only substances that can pass through the blood-brain barrier to clear free radicals and protect brain cells.

Purple sweet potato can also be used as a raw material for extracting natural purple sweet potato red pigment. The purple sweet potato red pigment extracted from purple sweet potato belongs to the class of anthocyanidins pigments, which are processed by water extraction from purple sweet potato tubers and roots, followed by resin adsorption and other refined processes. This product is a purple-red liquid or powder, and its main component is anthocyanin. Its main characteristics are: easily soluble in water and ethanol solution, relatively easy to undergo acid hydrolysis under thermal acidic conditions, and shows significant color change reactions under different pH values. When pH is less than 5, its aqueous solution appears red to reddish-purple; when pH is greater than 5, the color increases, gradually changing from purplish-red to unstable blue-green.

Since purple sweet potato has so many benefits, how can we eat it both healthily and scientifically? Doctor Wang Dongxu introduced that when processing purple sweet potato, it must be thoroughly steamed or boiled. Because the cell membranes of starch in purple sweet potato cannot be destroyed without high temperature, making it difficult to digest. Moreover, the gasifying enzyme in purple sweet potato cannot be destroyed without high temperature, eating it afterwards would cause discomfort. Due to the low protein content in purple sweet potato, eating it alone would lead to unbalanced nutrition intake, so cooking it with rice into porridge is a relatively scientific method.
