Synopsis:

Susan (voiced by Reese Witherspoon) has always lived in California—she was born here, went to school here, and fell in love here. Now it’s her wedding day, where she is about to marry her dream man. Without any unexpected events, her life would have been set from this point on. However, when the priest asked if she would take the man standing before her as her husband, an incredible event occurred: her body began to grow several times its original size, even breaking through the roof of the church! The rapidly growing Susan was at a loss as she faced everything around her—her husband-to-be, her family, her house—all becoming smaller and smaller...

When Susan woke up again, she found herself in a research facility. The researchers informed her that the strange incident wasn't accidental; it happened because she came into contact with an alien object. Any contact with extraterrestrial materials could cause unpredictable changes in a person. In comparison, Susan's transformation was relatively mild. In the facility, Susan met various creatures who had similar issues: one turned into a cockroach-like insect (voiced by Hugh Laurie), another became a gelatinous blob resembling snot (voiced by Seth Rogen), another transformed into a half-ape, half-fish creature named Link (voiced by Will Arnett), and there was also a gigantic caterpillar. The government gathered all these individuals who had been contaminated by alien life forms in one place, studying them tirelessly to find a solution. However, Susan and the others didn’t enjoy being treated like bizarre specimens under constant scrutiny. They caused chaos in the facility, attempting to escape, but ultimately failed.

Time flew by quickly, and before Susan and her companions' problems were resolved, the aliens had already prepared for an invasion. The alien technology was incredibly advanced, and their army consisted of massive metal robots. Faced with these steel monsters, the Earth's military forces were no match. As Earth's civilization was on the brink of destruction, the government had no choice but to turn to the monsters in the research facility for help. Due to their unique abilities, they were immune to traditional physical weapons, which meant they might have a chance to defeat the aliens. Although Susan and the others weren't too thrilled about being used as weapons once again by the government, they understood the importance of their mission to protect the Earth. Thus, these diverse creatures utilized their unique abilities to engage in a fierce battle against the alien invaders...
