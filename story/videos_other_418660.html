Free General Training

In the iPhone version of "Emperor of Dreams," there are over four hundred famous generals from various dynasties, as well as countless talented individuals from taverns whose attributes can rival those of the famous generals. Additionally, all talent and generals will level up during battles, and players can freely allocate attribute points, adjust general skills, and cultivate their ideal famous generals.

Beautiful Concubines Accompany the Emperor

In the iPhone version of "Emperor of Dreams," you can also rescue and recruit the most beautiful women from various historical periods to assist in the emperor's quest for supremacy. These include the Twelve Beauties of Jinling, the great beauties of Jiangdong, both large and small Qiao, talented women like Zhong Wuyan, and enchantresses like Pan Jinlian. Be careful though, prolonged interaction with these enchantresses may lead to natural disasters occurring with a certain probability.

Story-based Dungeons

The iPhone version of "Emperor of Dreams" also features an adapted storyline dungeon called "Yellow Turban Rebellion," which transports you back to that tumultuous period of history to participate in the campaign to exterminate the three Yellow Turban generals. The storyline dungeon offers multiple difficulty levels to choose from, rewarding players with various items and equipment. Players can also collect dungeon medals to exchange for top-tier Yellow Turban set gear used by wise generals.

About "Emperor of Dreams"

"Emperor of Dreams" is a client-based strategy web game independently developed by Duoyi Network, recognized as the best web game in the 2010 online game rankings. It breaks through time and space barriers, allowing players to carry heroes from various dynasties such as Qin, Han, Ming, Tang, and more into chaotic times, possessing beauties like Xi Shi and Diaochan within their mansions, owning both power and beauty, and experiencing the life of an emperor. The "Emperor of Dreams" client is only 18MB, taking just 15 seconds to download, allowing you to experience the most visually stunning and smooth gameplay.
