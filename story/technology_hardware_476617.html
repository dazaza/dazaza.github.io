　　Personality recommendation

　　First recommendation: Shy is the most loving

　　Second recommendation: Accelerated innocence and impatience.

　　Of course, not excluding careless, steady, conservative, reclusive, and mischievous personalities

　　Elite talent

　　Stamina: 15+ (thick blood is durable) Attack: 15+ (ordinary standard) Defense: 10+ (don't expect too much)

　　Speed: 18+ (fast speed gives the first hand) Magic Attack: 18+ (rely on this for food) Magic Resistance: 10+ (don't expect too much)

　　Leveling route guide:

Proxy leveling

　　① Level 25-45: Dog suicide practice brushing Ice Knight

　　② Level 45-100, at this time proxy has three choices:

　　1) Dual dog proxy practice brushing Lucia

　　2) Yuanbao suicide proxy practice brushing Leo

　　3) Blaze Dragon / Grievance Magic Fish proxy practice brushing General Ximing

　　Solo leveling (provided by Cheese)

Levels 1-5:

Leveling location: Pet Garden

Pets to brush: Stupid Firebird

Skills to use: [Bubble] + [Hydro Pump]

Skills learned during this period: None.

Experience gained: 41-102

Summary: The newly hatched Duo is quite tragic, only level one, and there is no guarantee of winning against the stupid firebird. So, during this time it is relatively tough, but once you start leveling, the speed will be very fast.

Levels 5-10:

Leveling location: Vesuvius Crater

Pets to brush: Guard Dog

Skills learned during this period: None.

Experience gained: 141-236

Summary: This stage progresses quickly, and our magic attack is high, so attacking feels visually satisfying. It's a wonderful time.

Levels 10-24:

Leveling location: Vesuvius Crust

Pets to brush: Guard Dog

Skills to use: [Shadow Punch] + [Foam Power] + [Water Cannon]

Skills learned during this period: Confusion Light (Opponent has a certain chance of confusion), Shadow Punch (Power: 60)

Experience gained: 258-353

Summary: Leveling speed is rapid, plus double experience during May Day, this period passes in a blink. Gather energy for the next stage of leveling.

Levels 24-36:

Leveling location: Vesuvius Mantle

NPCs to brush: Richard

Skills to use: [Bubble] + [Foam Power]

Skills learned during this period: Steel Shell (Our defense +1), Ghost Mirror (Next turn opponent invalid, recover 1/3 HP next turn)

Evolution during this period: Brimmed Duo (Level 32)

Experience gained: 803

Summary: Beautiful 2409 experience... upgrade slowly, work hard until the next stage.

Levels 36-48:

Leveling location: Vesuvius Core

NPCs to brush: Cornel

Skills to use: [Foam Power] + [Water Cannon]

Skills learned during this period: Waterfall (Power: 80), Magic Devour (Power: 80),

Counter Technique (Opponent physical attack and defense -1)

Evolution during this period: Sprite Duo (Level 46)

Experience gained: 1500

Summary: Do not use Waterfall due to species differences. Ignore it and focus on leveling up.

Levels 48-55:

Leveling location: Second floor of Dark Space

Pets to brush: Sleepwalker

Skill to use: [Magic Devour]

Skills learned during this period: Water Flow (Power: 95)

Experience gained: 1408-1526

Summary: For Magic Devour with power 80, sleepwalkers are relatively fragile and provide good experience. (Note: There may be instances where they cannot be defeated instantly.)

Levels 55-75:

Leveling location: Second floor of Dark Space

NPCs to brush: Slo

Skill to use: [Magic Devour]

Skills learned during this period: Tail's Full Strength (-Opponent current HP 1/5), Sacrificial Rain (Power: 100), Slash (Power: 70)

Experience gained: 7307

Summary: Victory is in sight, seize the time to level up. It's a relatively beautiful period.

Levels 75-100:

Leveling location: Recycle Black Hole

Pets to brush: Sleepwalker

Or you can go to the Earth Temple

Skills to use: [Magic Devour] or [Water Flow]

Skills learned during this period: Water Wave (Power: 120)

Experience gained: 11763 (Sleepwalker)

Earth Spirit Temple Experience:

First floor Total 5575

Second floor Total 6429

Third floor Total 6584

Fourth floor Total 4680

Fifth floor (+boss) Total 12410

Total: 35741

Summary: These two leveling methods are highly efficient. Don't choose Lucia, as the night troll in Lucia's area is extremely annoying.
