"The Wonderful Era of Daughter-in-law" breaks the pattern of emotional drama about mother-in-law and daughter-in-law relationships. In previous TV series of this kind, the conflict between mother-in-law and daughter-in-law was pushed to the extreme, either the daughter-in-law is sad or the mother-in-law is crying. However, the screenwriter Wang Liping of "The Wonderful Era of Daughter-in-law" got rid of this old routine that has caused aesthetic fatigue, showing the new changes in mother-in-law and daughter-in-law relationship in the era of only child. The heroine Maodoudou is a new daughter-in-law of the "post-80s", because her husband's parents divorced and remarried respectively, she has two mothers-in-law, and both mothers-in-law want to win her over and compete for helping her take care of the children. Therefore, the status of mother-in-law and daughter-in-law is reversed. They don't argue every day, but think for each other. The tragedy turns into a light comedy. People see the harmonious, sunny, and beautiful side of family relations.

The city girl Maodoudou is outgoing, hardworking, and filial. Her greatest advantage: optimism. In short, she is a once-in-a-millennium good daughter-in-law. But the husband's home she met is also rare "chaotic": two distinct mothers-in-law who divorced and remarried, they are always at odds over big and small matters, and they are always trying for their own petty interests; the newlywed sister-in-law who lost her husband, handling problems a bit "abnormal", no matter how the sister-in-law tries to befriend her, she just doesn't like it, always thinking that everyone is bullying her; this newly married daughter-in-law Maodoudou goes back to her parents' house and dare not complain, swallowing her tears...
