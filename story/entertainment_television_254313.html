Summary of the plot of "Canghai":

In September 1949, after the great victories of the Liaoshen, Huaihai, and Pingjin campaigns, the remnants of the Nationalist forces retreated to the Jinma Islands. A part of the Third Field Army advanced southward into Jiangnan. Hongliu Regiment's Third Battalion Commander Wang Benli and Political Commissar Li ShanKui received orders to prepare for cross-sea landing operations. Unexpectedly, they suffered a crushing defeat. Li Shangkui was killed, and Wang Benli renamed himself "Wang Shangkui" in memory of him.

The failure of the "Jinmen Naval Battle" prompted the high-level leaders of the new China to strengthen the Chinese Navy. After the victory in the Korean War, Wang Shangkui returned to the Red Sea and joined the Navy, marrying doctor Xu Jinyun.

Wang Shangkui devoted himself to studying naval tactics and later accidentally fought a brilliant naval battle that made him famous. However, double Ph.D. Ma Jiancheng looked down on him with disdain. During the construction of the long-wave station, Soviet aid was withdrawn halfway through, and Wang Shangkui strongly recommended Ma Jiancheng as the chief engineer. He unexpectedly learned that Ma was his wife's first love.

The long-wave station tests failed one after another, and Ma Jiancheng ran away. Later, Wang Shangkui and Political Commissar Lu Xinquan persuaded Ma Jiancheng to return despite opposition. Ma Jiancheng risked his life to try, and the long-wave station was finally successfully developed.

Lu Xinquan accidentally learned that Li Shangkui had survived in the past. Li asked Lu to keep the secret and hoped that his son Li Qiang would serve in the Navy in the future.

More than ten years later, Wang Shangkui's son Wang Haijun wanted to take the college entrance exam, but Wang Shangkui insisted that his son join the army. Unable to reach an agreement, Wang Shangkui angrily sent his son to work as a cook on a supply ship. Ma Jiancheng finally agreed to become the director of the No. 509 Institute, but the condition was to send Wang Haijun to take the college entrance exam, which Wang Shangkui refused.

Ma Jiancheng's report on "information platform construction" conflicted with the issue of "solving employee housing problems." The arrogant Ma Jiancheng stormed off to the supply ship and coincidentally met Wang Haijun, encouraging him to have lofty ideals.

Wang Haijun wanted to leave the army, leading to fierce conflicts between father and son. Wang Shangkui felt lonely, thinking of Li Shangkui and shedding tears. Seeing his father cry for the first time, Wang Haijun decided to stay. But he played a "dirty trick" during a military exercise and was punished with forty days of confinement.

Unexpectedly, the period of confinement changed Wang Haijun's life, and his academic performance improved rapidly. The usually proud Ma Jiancheng finally put aside his pride and sought out Commissar Lu to secure 30 million yuan in funding.

Li Qiang was admitted to the Naval Academy but was replaced by Lu Xiangdong, the son of Lu Xinquan. Lu personally came to apologize, and Li Shangkui passed away with a smile. Wang Haijun, Li Qiang, and Lu Xiangdong became the famous Three Musketeers at the Naval Academy.

Despite his father's opposition, Lu Xiangdong went into business, while Wang Haijun and Li Qiang were assigned to the No. 159 ship after graduation. Later, Wang Haijun furthered his studies at the Guangzhou Naval Academy and, after graduation, worked on the toughest South China Sea minesweeper as deputy commander, marrying Ma Yumei, Ma Jiancheng's daughter.

At this point, Ma Jiancheng was already in the late stages of lung cancer. The simulation of the information platform entered a critical stage, and the strong sense of mission extended Ma Jiancheng's life miracle.

In 1990, Wang Haijun and Li Qiang served as captain and political commissar respectively, testing the domestically produced No. 159 ship after modifications and practically verifying its various performances. The trial run was a great success, and Ma Jiancheng passed away peacefully on duty upon hearing the good news.

Lu Xiangdong returned to invest in the Red Sea and helped Li Meihua, attracting police attention. After understanding the whole story, the estrangement between Lu Xinquan and his son was finally eliminated.

In 1995, after a lifetime in the military, Wang Shangkui retired.

In the new era, maritime defense became more important. Wang Haijun and Li Qiang participated in the largest-scale deterrent military exercise in the history of the Navy, "Changjian No. 1." A fire broke out in the boiler room, and Li Qiang was seriously injured while saving his comrades. At this time, Wang Shangkui learned about Li Qiang's background.

As an expert in China's maritime rights and interests, Ma Yumei was invited to give a lecture at the Boao Asia Forum, receiving enthusiastic praise. Wang Haijun achieved excellent results in both theory and practice and was commended by the Military Commission and the Navy Headquarters. In 2007, he was ordered to lead the fleet in a multinational joint military exercise, winning respect from all participating countries with perfect performance and successfully completing the mission of showcasing the strength of our Navy and national image.
