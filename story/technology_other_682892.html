Relevant matters for the second grouting of ball mills

by hx515317 on 2012-02-29 10:26:47

Grouting material is made of high-strength materials as aggregate, cement as binder, supplemented with high fluidity, micro-expansion, anti-segregation and other substances. At the construction site, a certain amount of water is added, then it can be used after being stirred evenly. It has good self-flowing property, rapid hardening, early strength, high strength, non-shrinkage, micro-expansion; non-toxic, harmless, non-aging, no pollution to water quality and surrounding environment, good self-compacting property, rust prevention and other characteristics. In terms of construction, it has the advantages of reliable quality, cost reduction, shortening the construction period and convenient use.

So how is the second grouting of the ball mill equipment carried out? Jinbang Heavy Industry will explain it for you:

After the cylinder is installed in place, a comprehensive inspection shall be carried out. After all the data of the cylinder part meet the requirements, the secondary grouting of the main bearing can be carried out. Before grouting, tighten the anchor bolts, clean the oil stains, paint and rust on the bearing seat and base plate, sweep away the oil stains and dust on the foundation, and wash it with water before pouring. Before pouring, epoxy resin (compressive strength meets 650~862kg/cm2) is first poured between the lower convex platform of the main bearing base plate and the foundation groove, and then cement mortar is poured.

In order to ensure the quality of the secondary grouting layer of the main bearing base plate, holes are drilled on the surface of the foundation, and Φ14 steel bars bent into right angles are anchored with epoxy resin. Drill Φ30 deep 100~125mm holes on the foundation to anchor the steel bars. During the secondary grouting, they are buried in the grouting layer.

During the secondary grouting, special attention should be paid to filling the bottom of the bearing base with cement mortar. When pouring, it should be compacted and there should be no gaps. After the curing of the secondary grouting layer, two coats of water glass are brushed on the surface of the foundation to prevent oil from eroding the foundation and the grouting layer.

The surface of the secondary grouting foundation is chiseled into a rough surface. The method of chiseling into a rough surface is to chisel out a series of concave pits on the surface of the foundation with a steel flat chisel. The diameter is about 30~50mm, and the distance between the concave pits varies according to the size of the foundation, generally around 150mm. The edges and corners of the foundation should be chiseled into notches.

Clean the oil stains, gravel, soil, water accumulation, etc. in the reserved holes of the equipment foundation and anchor bolts.

After the foundation is inspected and meets the requirements, the center marking plate is embedded as shown in the attached drawing, which serves as the basis for hanging the center line during the installation of the equipment to correct the position of the equipment. At the same time, reference points also need to be embedded, which serve as the standard reference for measuring the elevation during the installation of the equipment. As shown in the figure, the longitudinal center line of the ball mill cylinder is controlled by the No. 4 and No. 8 center marking plates, and the center line of the main motor and small gear shaft is controlled by the No. 1 and No. 3 center marking plates. The center marking plate can be made of angle steel, its inner angle is welded with round steel anchorage claw, and it is embedded on the surface of the foundation, preferably welded together with the foundation reinforcement. Its surface is slightly lower than the surface of the foundation to prevent collision. The benchmark point is generally located at a position convenient for measuring elevation, and its elevation is measured based on the plant zero point. As shown in the figure, it can be embedded on the main motor foundation (point A). The elevation benchmark point can be made of Φ20mm round head rivet and welded on the foundation reinforcement. The embedding height of the benchmark point should be 30~50mm lower than the upper surface of the concrete, and a circular pit is made around the benchmark point, covered with a steel plate to prevent heavy objects from bumping.
