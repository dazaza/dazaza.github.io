Treasure those who have loved each other.

by meimei234 on 2011-12-29 20:06:19

Cherish the ones you love and those who love you.

Because of love, you will not become enemies; because of hurt, you will not be friends.

If it takes five hundred glances in a past life to exchange for a mere passing by in this one, then that must already be very blissful - in fact, passing by is also a deep kind of fate. The Buddha says: Five hundred glances are needed to exchange for a mere passing by in this life. You can meet a person in a second, know a person in a minute, fall for a person in an hour, and love a person in a day, but it takes a lifetime to forget a person.

When she does not love you, no matter if she loved before or forgot later, or even never loved at all, when you cannot be the one in her heart anymore, her heart will not remember you. Please do not disturb her when you are unhappy, she is absolutely not where you should be going right now. Please do not tell her your trivial matters, she has no time and even less interest in knowing about you and your life. Even if you tell her, she will soon forget. Without love, you are destined not to squeeze into her life. Please do not cry in front of her, she cannot give you care and concern, at most only a little sympathy. Only the ones who love you will truly cherish you, not the bystander's sympathy and pity.

When she does not love you, your love becomes her burden. Please do not calculate your own efforts, do not expect any returns. You are sincere, she is not, loving someone who does not love you back is itself without reward. Do not count the rights and wrongs, this way you will be happier. Please do not lose confidence, because loving someone is not because of their excellence, but a feeling. She makes you have this feeling, so you love her. Similarly, she does not love you, it is not because you are not excellent. Excellence is not a reason for love. There are still so many people who love you, a faint smile is also unusually sweet.

When she does not love you, you must also bless her. With love, there should be no hate, because there was once love, the days with love were happy, the happiness of being together was also happy, having had happiness and love, there will be no more hate. She loses a person who loves her, and you lose a person who does not love you, but get a chance to live again, to love again. Please take a deep breath, on the road of life, it is paved with flowers of love, there will always be a flower for you, although there are many flowers, there is no repeated one, this is predestined through lifetimes.

When she does not love you, it is also the time for you to disappear from her life, leave her immediately, proudly live your own life. At the same time, you also hope she can be happy and find her future. Gently embrace the warmth in your memories, softly gaze at the gentle fading away. No matter what the result is, it will destroy the beauty of the past. Leave cleanly, perhaps after several years, on a certain afternoon, she squints in the sunlight and remembers a beautiful moment, smiling sincerely. All these reminiscences are worth it.

Love does not necessarily last forever. What you once owned might be the best memory of your life. Because of love, you will not become enemies; because of hurt, you will not be friends; only the most familiar strangers. Love knows the weight, drunkenness knows the strength of wine. The memories of love should be well kept, but the happiness in the future needs to be sought individually.

Love is a feeling, not loving is also a feeling, and often difficult to decide is the feeling in the heart, whether it is love or not. Originally held in hand, it is not necessarily what you truly own; what you own is not necessarily what you truly engrave in your heart. Life often requires conscious abandonment, because when you own, you may be losing, and when you abandon, you may be regaining.

The wise understand abandonment, the sincere understand sacrifice, the happy understand transcendence. For those who do not love you, the most necessary is understanding, abandonment, and blessing. Too much self-pity is begging for the other's charity. Love and being loved are things that make people happy. Do not let these things turn into pain. Since you have experienced them, many years later, occasionally recalling, hoping they are all beautiful memories. Live confidently, happily, leaving the most beautiful smile to the one who hurt you the most. Smart people know they need to be happy.

Cherish the ones you love and those who love you.
