New Legend of Mir server opened. Novices promoted to experts.

by 93zt112222 on 2009-11-29 15:57:25

In the game "Haotianlong," you can claim a hero at level one. 93zt, as soon as your character is born, you should go to the Hero Envoy as soon as possible to claim your hero. This is the most important step.

1. Press F9 to open your inventory, double-click on the weapon given by the system in your inventory to equip it.

2. Press F10 to check your character status information. Click on the yellow arrow on the left side of the status window to view basic attributes, skills, and equipment worn by the character. Skill information can also be viewed by pressing F11.

3. Don't forget F12; it's very important for you. You can set up game sounds, name display, durability warnings, shift-free (forced attack), map markers, item tips, display filters, automatic item pickup, pickup filters, shortcut key settings, medicine protection, and built-in AFK settings through F12.

4. After equipping everything, run to the main city gate and click on the teleporter to be teleported inside the city for free. (Left-click to walk, right-click to run.)

5. Go to the center of the main city to find the Hero Envoy. Talk to the Hero Envoy, choose to lead a hero, select the hero’s profession and gender in the dialogue interface, and name your hero.

6. The Hero Envoy will prompt you to find the Quest Envoy. Gain experience to reach level 2, and then automatically navigate to the Quest Envoy. (Note: Do not click the mouse during this process, or it will stop navigation. When there are many people, auto-navigation may fail, requiring you to run there yourself. If you do not talk to the Hero Envoy, the Quest Envoy will ignore you.)

7. At the bottom right of the game interface, you can find the button to summon your hero. Click to summon your hero.

8. Talk to the Hero Envoy again, complete the task, and gain experience. You will reach level 4, and your hero will reach level 2.

9. Talk to the Quest Envoy, who requires 100 gold coins from you before giving you a quest.

10. How to quickly get gold? Go to the side of the main city and kill chickens, deer, and sheep (left-click on the monster to attack). Left-click on the animal corpse + ALT to dig out meat. (Of course, you can also earn money by killing monsters that drop items and gold. When you see potions or equipment on the ground, stand on them for a while, and they will be automatically picked up. Step on trash, and soon you'll earn your first pot of gold in the game.)

11. After killing some monsters, you should reach level 5. Go back and talk to the butcher, choose to sell the meat. Press F9 to open your inventory, drag the meat into the round black frame, and click OK. (Note: The butcher only recycles meat.)

12. Be careful when selling meat, as in Legend Outlaws, PK is allowed outside safe zones, even in quest areas. You might accidentally get killed. After dying, your screen will turn black and white, and items in your inventory or on your body may drop. At this point, press ALT+X or click "OUT" at the bottom of the game interface to return to the character selection screen. Re-select your character and start the game again. In the world of Jianghu, how can one avoid getting cut? Die and come back again. (Note: The first thing to do after re-entering the game is to summon your hero, who is your most loyal comrade-in-arms.)

13. Now, you should have enough money. Go back to the Quest Envoy. Pay him 100 gold coins, complete the task, gain experience, and you should reach level 6, with your hero reaching level 4. (Note: If you don’t summon your hero, your hero won’t gain experience.)

14. The Quest Envoy gives you a task to find the Hero Envoy. Find the Hero Envoy, talk to him, and based on your and your hero’s professions, receive experience and skill books for both you and your hero. You will reach level 8, and your hero will reach level 6.

15. Open your inventory, double-click on the character skill book to learn level 7 skills. Open the skill window (F11), single-click on the skill icon to set the skill shortcut key. In the future, pressing the skill shortcut key will allow you to cast spells. Then open the hero's inventory, drag the hero's skill book from the quest inventory to the game inventory, double-click the hero's skill book, and the hero will also learn the skill. The hero's skills are automatically released. Afterwards, the Hero Envoy will tell you to find the Quest Envoy. Find him, talk to him, and gain experience. You will reach level 9, and your hero will reach level 7.

16. The Quest Envoy asks you to find the jewelry store owner. Find him, talk to him, and gain experience and a complete set of novice jewelry (1 necklace, 2 rings, 2 bracelets). At this point, you should reach level 11, and your hero should reach level 8. Double-click on the equipment to wear it.

17. The jewelry store owner asks you to find the clothing store owner. Find him, talk to him, and gain experience and novice helmets and armor. Double-click on the equipment to wear it.

18. Proceed sequentially: Jewelry Store Owner -> Weapon Store Owner -> Pharmacy Owner -> Quest Envoy -> Hero Envoy -> Bookstore Owner -> Quest Envoy. Reach the level 15 novice weapon for your class, medium-sized bandages (double-click to get 7 bandages), and a full set of equipment for your hero. Move the hero's equipment from the character inventory to the hero's inventory and double-click to equip it on the hero. (Note: Items must be placed in the hero's inventory before they can be used by the hero.) At this point, you should be level 15, and your hero should be level 12.

19. If you forget what tasks to do midway, you can go back to the Quest Envoy to inquire. Do not exit the game before completing the monster-killing task, otherwise the count will restart.

20. The Quest Envoy asks you to kill 10 scarecrows outside the city. Note: Do not log off during the task, or you will have to start over after logging back in.

Additionally, let me briefly introduce how to form a party. Click the party button at the bottom of the game interface, check the small box before "Allow Party", click "New", enter the name of the person you want to group with, and click "Confirm". You can only group with someone if they also have "Allow Party" enabled. Uncheck "Allow Party" to leave the group. (You can also enable voice chat in the party window.)

21. Kill 10 scarecrows -> Quest Envoy -> Warehouse Keeper -> Kill 10 frogs -> Return to Warehouse Keeper to submit the task. Gain a large amount of experience.

22. After reaching level 16, go to the Quest Envoy to accept a task. Quest Envoy -> Homeless Person (Coordinates 328.45) -> Kill 10 Orcs -> Homeless Person gives equipment -> Anjia Shop Owner (Coordinates: 557.538) -> Kill 10 Orc Warriors -> Gain a large amount of experience and a return scroll -> Double-click the return scroll to return to the main city, find the Quest Envoy, and gain medium-sized magic potions, medium-sized bandages, and a large amount of experience.

23. After reaching level 22, visit our old friend the Quest Envoy again. Talk to the Quest Envoy and accept the task: find veterans in Bai Ri Men, Bi Qi Province, and Feng Mo Valley. After completing the task, gain a large amount of experience and a double experience scroll. Then, you can wander around the land of Ma Fa, enjoy the scenery, familiarize yourself with the environment, and go to places like the Wuma Forest, Wuma Natural Cave, Natural Cave, Beastman Ancient Tomb to fight monsters and level up.

24. Before you know it, you've reached level 25 and received the task: go to the second floor of the cave to find Mr. Zhang, the pharmacy owner (coordinates: 190, 146) -> Complete the task -> Quest Envoy -> Find the bookstore owner -> Quest Envoy -> Cave -> Task completed, go to the Quest Envoy to submit the task. Gain equipment, a large amount of experience, a double experience scroll, a small vitality stone, and a Devil Ridge voucher. Now, you can go to these places to fight monsters and level up: Beastman Ancient Tomb, Natural Cave, Bi Qi Mine Area, Feng Mo Mine Area, Valley Mine Area, Poisonous Snake Valley, Meng Zhong Monster Area.

25. The path of cultivation needs to be accumulated slowly. Brother, don't rush, keep mixing in the land of Ma Fa, and one day, you will create your own legendary story.
