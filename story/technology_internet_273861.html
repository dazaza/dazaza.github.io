Shan Road No. 80 RMB 169 Reservation Home Inn Suzhou (Xiangcheng Jiayuan Branch) Suzhou Xiangcheng District Jia Yuan Road No. 698 RMB 170 Reservation At 9:00 a.m. on October 26, the ribbon-cutting ceremony of the "Sentimental Yueyang Tower" exhibition hall was held in the Yueyang Tower scenic area and officially opened to visitors. The construction and exhibition of the exhibition hall took half a year, with a building area of more than 500 square meters, located 400 meters southeast of the Yueyang Tower. Inside the exhibition hall, there are precious photos of successive Party and state leaders visiting the Yueyang Tower and historical old photos collected from various sources related to the Yueyang Tower, truly reproducing the appearance of the Yueyang Tower in various historical periods. On the same day, the Yueyang Tower Calligraphy and Painting Research Institute and the Yueyang Tower Culture Research Institute held establishment and hanging ceremonies.
