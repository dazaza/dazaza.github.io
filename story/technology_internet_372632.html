However, looking at the increasingly mature e-commerce industry, there is still no third-party C2C/B2C review site that can rival Dianping (focused on the catering industry), Douban (focused on books), or Time (for movies). Reflecting on some of the reasons, I personally believe that the special nature of e-commerce itself makes it difficult to successfully or smoothly follow a third-party route (market). Since the entire e-commerce process is carried out entirely on the Internet, whether or not to buy, the influence of user reputation (reviews) on the purchasing intentions and behaviors of buyers is extremely significant, and there are bound to be many issues related to profit distribution. Therefore, uncontrollable false information can easily spread, and ensuring the authenticity and reliability of information cannot rely solely on the administrator's ability to identify the truth or falsehood of information, which would likely pose great difficulties for operators.
