Patent technology for garlic fresh-keeping storage

by qiwengsyuuei on 2012-02-24 21:58:39

1. A Method for Preserving Garlic Cloves

A method for preserving garlic cloves involves the steps of peeling garlic, grading garlic cloves, rinsing, ozone disinfection treatment, preservation treatment using a preservative made from sodium ascorbate and citric acid, drying the surface moisture of garlic cloves, pre-cooling at a constant temperature of -1.5°C, vacuum nitrogen packaging, and storage at a constant temperature between -2°C to -1.5°C. The preserved garlic cloves using this method can be stored for over 60 days at -1.5°C without change in color or flavor, making them suitable for long-distance transportation and export. This method not only benefits domestic consumers but also creates favorable conditions for foreign exchange earnings.

2. Preservation Method for Fresh Tender Garlic

A preservation method for fresh tender garlic involves harvesting high-quality white-skinned fresh garlic 7 days earlier than usual without damage, air-drying for 3-4 hours, removing 1-2 layers of outer skin, trimming roots, cutting the stem while retaining a length of 30-40mm, and pre-cooling in a -1°C to 0°C warehouse for 2-3 days. The garlic is then packed into specialized fresh garlic preservation bags with a capacity of 15-20kg per bag, sealing tightly, and storing at 0°C ± 0.5°C. Using this new technology, white-skinned tender fresh garlic can be stored for over 10 months, remaining fresh and crisp with a milder taste, more refreshing and palatable, and sweet-spicy flavors. It provides fresh raw materials for year-round market supply and processed sugar-vinegar garlic, increasing added value by more than five times.

3. Preservation Method for Fresh Tender Garlic

This invention relates to vegetable preservation technology, especially the preservation method for fresh garlic. The technical solution of this invention is: the preservation method for fresh tender garlic, characterized by controlling the storage temperature of fresh tender garlic within the range of -0.5°C to +0.5°C, humidity controlled within the range of 90-95%, and storing in an environment where the surrounding gas contains 4.5-5.5% oxygen and 15-16% carbon dioxide. This method places fresh tender garlic in a state similar to hibernation, allowing it to be preserved for six months to one year.

4. Specialized Membrane (Bag) for Preserving Fresh Tender Garlic and Its Manufacturing Method

A specialized membrane (bag) for preserving fresh tender garlic consists of polyvinyl chloride resin, di-octyl phthalate, sebacic acid di-octyl ester, epoxidized soybean oil octyl ester, phosphite ester, calcium stearate, zinc stearate, stearic acid, paraffin wax, nano-level active calcium carbonate. The resin undergoes screening to remove impurities; other powdered materials are mixed with plasticizers in proportion through slurry grinding to form a paste. Ingredients are weighed and mixed, high-mixed for 15-25 minutes, extruded by a single-screw plastic extruder, and blown into a film. Bags of different sizes and specifications are produced. The production process is simple and easy to operate. The membrane has a certain degree of breathability, capable of bidirectional regulation of O2 and CO2. It has strong moisture permeability and good anti-condensation properties. It remains flexible and highly transparent even at low temperatures, with good toughness and strength, making it less prone to damage during use. It can replace harmful gases in the storage environment, improving storage effects and extending the freshness period of fresh garlic up to more than 10 months.

5. A Preservation Method for Garlic Scapes

A preservation method for garlic scapes includes steps such as warehouse disinfection, scape sorting, shelf pre-cooling, bagging, and storage. The storage conditions in the warehouse are: ozone concentration of 25-30ppm, operating the ozone machine for 3-5 hours every 15-20 days, regular ventilation, early-stage warehouse temperature control at -0.7°C to -0.1°C, opening the bag every 5 days to release heat, late-stage warehouse temperature control at -0.7°C to -0.3°C; cold storage humidity control at 90-95%. Garlic scapes preserved with ozone according to this invention can be stored for over a year at -0.7°C to -0.3°C, maintaining their original color and flavor, suitable for long-distance transportation and export, providing convenience for domestic consumers and creating favorable conditions for foreign exchange earnings.

6. A Preservation Method for Vacuum-packed Small Packages of Garlic Cloves

This invention involves a method of preserving peeled garlic cloves using vacuum and small packaging techniques. The process includes selecting garlic heads, cutting the root ends, peeling, manual selection, rinsing, disinfecting, drying, bagging, vacuuming, and placing in larger bags. This method prevents bacterial infections, decay, extends the preservation period, and offers convenient consumption and storage advantages.

7. A Garlic Scape Preservative

This invention relates to a vegetable preservative mainly used for the storage preservation of garlic scapes. The preservative is a solid smoke agent composed of the following substances: thiabendazole: 3%~4%; ammonium nitrate: 3%~7%; plant growth regulator: 0.001%; sawdust: 89%~94%. Thiabendazole, approved as a food additive under GB2760-1996 Food Additive Usage Hygiene Standard, is the main effective component of the garlic scape preservative. It is convenient to use, has low toxicity, high stability, broad antibacterial spectrum, and long-lasting bactericidal effect. Using this preservative combined with small-package silicon window modified atmosphere storage, the garlic scapes remain crisp and the tips maintain fresh green color for up to 8 months.

8. A Plastic Film for Garlic Scape Preservation

A plastic film for garlic scape preservation is a product of several high molecular materials co-mixed modification. It contains materials and their weight percentage ranges as follows: Low Density Polyethylene (LDPE) 50~70%; Linear Low Density Polyethylene (LLDPE) 20~40%; High Density Polyethylene (HDPE) 10~20%; Ethylene-Vinyl Acetate Copolymer (EVA) 0~15%. This plastic film can be made into corresponding specification garlic scape preservation bags. The oxygen and carbon dioxide ratio in the bag meets the preservation requirements of garlic scapes. Using this film eliminates the need for regular bag-opening and air replacement, extends the storage period, ensures good preservation effects, and realizes integration of bagging, preservation, and sales.

9. Preserved Garlic Cloves and Their Preservation Method

A preservation method for garlic cloves belongs to food preservation technology. It is mainly used to handle garlic to keep it fresh all year round. The preserved garlic cloves are fresh peeled cloves that have been manually peeled, placed in sealed containers, air removed, filled with a mixture of nitrogen and carbon dioxide gases, etc., to place them in a "suffocated" state. The preservation method includes selecting materials, manual peeling, grading, weighing, container sealing, air extraction and static placement, filling with mixed gases, and low-temperature warehouse storage. Advantages include clean peeling, complete cloves, long-term storage without sprouting, no root growth, no mold or rot, no loss of water or color changes, and simplicity of the method.

10. Fresh Garlic Clove Preservation Method

A fresh garlic clove preservation method, including manual peeling, washing, air-drying, selection and removal of impurities, bottling and sealing, vacuuming and nitrogen filling, re-sealing and checking for leaks, and low-temperature storage and transportation. This method is simple, operable, uses simple equipment, has low processing costs, ensures sealed containers do not break during low-temperature transportation, provides reliable preservation, and ensures garlic cloves do not mold or rot, sprout, grow roots, change color, or lose water and nutrients over a long period, ensuring a steady supply of fresh garlic cloves in the market throughout the year.

11. Garlic Head Preservation Method

A garlic head preservation method utilizes the natural dormancy period of garlic, controlling factors like temperature to preserve garlic. The method includes controlling the time of garlic entering the warehouse, screening garlic for warehousing, controlling the temperature and humidity of the cold storage, and CO2 and O2 content. Using this method provided by the invention, the cost is low, the preservation time is long, the effective preservation period can last up to 12 months, and the preservation effect is good.

12. Vacuum Oxygen Suppression Storage Preservation Method for Dry Garlic Heads

As dry garlic heads approach the Mid-Autumn Festival, they may naturally sprout due to seasonal changes. Therefore, the principle of vacuum oxygen suppression storage preservation is adopted, encapsulating dry garlic heads in a vacuum oxygen suppression state, preventing sprouting and maintaining the aroma of seasonal garlic all year round.

25. Active Silicon Window Preservation Method for Garlic Scapes

An active silicon window preservation method for garlic scapes involves selecting, processing, and pre-cooling fresh garlic scapes, then packing them into polyethylene plastic bags. Garlic scapes breathe through silicone rubber films, adjusting the effective area of the silicone rubber film based on different respiration rates during different storage periods, i.e., according to the physiological changes of garlic scapes. This method can store garlic scapes for 210-270 days while maintaining freshness without deterioration, with a stable commodity rate of over 90%. Silicone rubber films can be reused, reducing packaging costs.
