The creative competition of Changhong smart TV sounds the war drums together.

by changhds on 2012-01-05 13:39:46

Television can also play through, a heated competition for the collection of subtle stories about smart TVs crossing time and space began several days ago. As novels become more subtle and watching TV becomes smarter, both are continuously satisfying the growing new needs of many people. We often read 140-character subtle stories with admiration and sighs, but what kind of stories and dramatic plots will emerge when the element of smart TV becomes the material for these subtle stories? Up to now, by logging onto the activity page of Changhong Smart TV Creative Competition, uploading your creative micro-novel from December 21st to January 18th, 2012, you have the chance to win a Changhong Smart TV grand prize.

Most Popular Time Travel Carnival

The Changhong Smart TV Creative Competition can be said to be full of "common folk style," without technical barriers, large quantities of sufficient prizes, as long as you participate, let your imagination run wild, chat in a few words, and write a micro-story with the theme of "TV can also play through," showcasing various dreams and ideas about time, space, or intelligence. To enhance the participation in the competition, Changhong's login method is simple and flexible. You can log in through Changhong's official website, register as a member of the event site, or log in via Sina Weibo, QQ, or Renren entry on that page, freely choosing among the three methods.

Most Powerful Abundant Rewards

Up to now, the activity website has already uploaded numerous brilliant creative suggestions. The thoughts of netizens are indeed incredibly powerful. For example, Zhuge Liang borrowing the east wind was due to watching weather forecasts, going back in time to make up for the guilt towards deceased relatives... Whether it's TV drama plots, science fiction fantasy, comedy romance, or suspense thrillers, you can freely doodle or rewrite according to your own ideas. After 29 days of work collection and voting, Changhong will conduct the evaluation selection on January 20th, 2012, and announce the winners on February 1st. Awards include Best Micro-drama Work Award, Best Idea Award, Jury Nomination Award, Original Popularity Award, Daily Lucky Award, with prizes ranging from Changhong Smart TVs, smart tablet computers, smartphones to U disks, phone credit. Besides "practical" prizes, the best micro-drama work will be made into a micro-film, truly achieving "once famous worldwide."

Most Intelligent Wonderful Future

Although the curtain of the creative collection has just been raised not long ago, it seems like the suggestions from netizens are very absurd, and smart TVs wouldn't be so incredible. However, smart TVs have already led us to start experiencing and enjoying the future world. We can abandon the remote control, use voice control to change channels, we can play tennis or race cars against each other in front of the TV; we can also weave microblogs and browse web pages on the TV...

At the same time, TV is quite different from mobile phones and computers. It has the characteristic feature of a large screen and unique human-computer interaction forms, which determines that the "intelligence" of Changhong TVs will be an innovative R&D direction. Carefully listening to user voices and meeting more living needs, Changhong will infinitely expand its own applications.
