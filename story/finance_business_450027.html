The "10,000-li Medical Quality March" campaign in our district has entered the organizational implementation phase/www.zp-nmg.com

by chovbiur on 2011-06-22 11:51:03

In accordance with the requests of the Ministry of Health and the Chongqing Municipal Health Bureau for the development of the "10,000-Mile Medical Quality Campaign," our department attaches great importance to it and has actively made arrangements. The campaign in our district is being carried out in an orderly manner according to schedule. Below is a summary of the progress:

### 1. High Importance and Thorough Arrangements

The "10,000-Mile Medical Quality Campaign" is a significant initiative aimed at improving medical insurance management and enhancing medical quality by addressing existing issues related to quality and safety in healthcare institutions, building upon previous hospital management years and 100-day special inspections for medical safety. Our department places high importance on this campaign, incorporating it into the main agenda, and making arrangements from the perspective of implementing scientific development and constructing a harmonious society, which has effectively promoted the progress of the campaign.

- Strengthened organizational leadership by establishing a leading group headed by the principal responsible person.

- Developed the "2010 Implementation Plan for the Medical Quality 10,000-Mile Campaign in Hechuan District, Chongqing Municipality" based on local realities and distributed it to all levels and types of medical institutions in the district.

- Leveraged the opportunity of the core group study of the Party Committee to convene a mobilization meeting for the campaign, thoroughly arranging its implementation.

Currently, the "10,000-Mile Medical Quality Campaign" in our district has entered the stage of organizational implementation, with various specific tasks steadily progressing.

### 2. Full Efforts, Solid Work, and Comprehensive Promotion of All Aspects of the Campaign

#### (a) Focusing on the theme of "Continuous Quality Improvement and Assurance of Medical Insurance," widely conducting publicity and educational activities to enhance public awareness of safe medical care.

This campaign emphasizes institutional construction and public education. Internally, it aims to strengthen internal hospital construction and highlight medical quality management. Externally, it focuses on social publicity and public opinion guidance. According to these requirements, the following works will be conducted:

- Vigorously promote medical quality and safety education among medical personnel to enhance their risk and safety responsibility awareness. Intensify management and education for key responsible persons in medical institutions, quality and safety managers, and all staff members, updating safety concepts and improving theoretical levels and practical operational abilities in quality management.

- Conduct public education on medical knowledge, fully utilizing the "10,000-Mile Medical Quality Campaign" through various forms such as on-site lectures, exhibitions, and special reports to guide the public to correctly understand medical science and risks, choose appropriate medical services, improve public awareness of medical safety, and enhance the ability to identify fake doctors, counterfeit drugs, and false medical advertisements, ensuring patient rights.

- Enhance public opinion guidance, focusing on the theme of the "10,000-Mile Medical Quality Campaign," organizing and coordinating local media to report on the campaign's progress, promoting new ideas, actions, and achievements in medical quality and insurance management, explaining the significance, requirements, and good practices of the campaign, and publicizing advanced examples of well-managed, reliable, and satisfactory medical institutions to create a favorable atmosphere for promoting medical insurance, enhancing medical quality, and improving doctor-patient relationships.

- Increase efforts to crack down on illegal medical practice and false medical advertising, exposing and severely punishing such behaviors.

#### (b) Focus on identifying and managing medical insurance risks through specialized inspection campaigns.

From May to present, combining with the previous 100-day special inspection campaign for medical insurance, we have conducted comprehensive medical quality supervision in mainly public medical institutions across the district. Each institution has conducted a self-inspection and rectification of medical safety hazards. However, continuous improvement is needed in medical quality and safety. In line with the requirements of the "10,000-Mile Medical Quality Campaign," focusing on medical technology, rational drug use, blood safety, hospital infections, biosafety management of pathogenic microorganism laboratories, hospital logistics security, and fire safety, we will continue to carry out specialized inspection and management campaigns. By checking and promoting improvements simultaneously, we will comprehensively sort out and address exposed prominent issues and potential safety hazards, take targeted measures to eliminate risks, improve mechanisms, systems, and regulations where necessary, and thoroughly investigate and manage areas where relevant regulations and work measures are poorly implemented.

#### (c) Focus on key links, implement specific measures, and strive to solve outstanding problems.

Surgical cases in surgery and obstetrics/gynecology departments are professional fields with more prominent medical insurance risks and higher rates of medical disputes, representing key aspects of medical safety. Given the recent occurrence of multiple surgical disputes in our district, which were related to some grassroots medical institutions or personnel exceeding their conditions or technical capabilities, we have formulated the "Provisional Regulations on Surgical Grading Management in Hechuan District, Chongqing Municipality" after careful study and preparation. This establishes a surgical grading management system, formulates detailed implementation rules and management measures, reviews physicians' technical capabilities, grants corresponding surgical permissions, and implements dynamic management. This effectively prevents grassroots medical institutions or personnel from exceeding their conditions or technical capabilities when performing surgeries.

#### (d) Strengthen blood quality management work.

We conscientiously implement the "Blood Station Management Measures," "Blood Station Quality Management Standards," "Blood Station Laboratory Quality Management Standards," "Clinical Blood Use Management Measures," and "Clinical Blood Transfusion Technical Standards." We further standardize blood collection and supply activities and clinical blood use management to ensure clinical blood safety. In September, the Chongqing Hechuan Central Blood Station received positive evaluations during a special inspection of blood quality by the Ministry of Health.

### 3. Innovation, Continuous Improvement, and Establishment of Long-Term Mechanisms for Medical Quality Management

The goal of the "10,000-Mile Medical Quality Campaign" is to improve medical quality, ensure medical safety, and harmonize doctor-patient relationships, with the core being "continuous improvement of medical quality" and the entry point being "the 10,000-mile journey." Our department will follow the legal requirements of medical and health laws and regulations, combine with public hospital reforms, change thinking, boldly practice, especially in areas such as hospital personnel staffing, basic drug use, promotion of clinical pathways, changes in payment methods, and improvement of hospital evaluation and review mechanisms, actively explore and innovate.

- With a full spirit, embrace public hospital reform, promote the development of the health sector, and ensure medical safety.

- Actively engage all forces to promote hospital development and mobilize all medical personnel to participate in healthcare reforms, fully stimulating everyone's enthusiasm, initiative, and creativity.

- Actively explore mechanisms for resolving medical disputes from aspects such as perfecting the legal system and optimizing the practice environment.

- Further explore effective practices for facilitating patient medical care, such as appointment registration. Medical institutions should focus on cultural construction, strengthen internal management and intrinsic construction, ensuring that management enhancement and business growth proceed simultaneously, promoting stable development.

Through the implementation of the "10,000-Mile Medical Quality Campaign," gradually explore and establish long-term mechanisms for hospital management, balancing short-term and long-term goals, fundamental solutions and symptomatic treatments, prevention and handling, truly strengthening the legal, systematic, and mechanistic construction of medical quality and insurance management, improving medical services, standardizing medical behavior, enhancing personnel capabilities, ensuring medical quality and safety, and continuously promoting the comprehensive, coordinated, sustainable, and healthy development of medical institutions.
