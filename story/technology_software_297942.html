The issue of energy shortage is no longer an empty phrase; it is rapidly approaching us. The alarm bells are ringing incessantly. It is imperative to be prepared and substitute renewable energy for non-renewable resources. Central government directives have been issued, offering strong support to enterprises engaged in the research and development of new energy sources. Beijing Zhongke Jiamei Technology Development Co., Ltd. has actively responded to the Party's call, collaborating with prestigious universities and organizing a large number of specialized scientific personnel dedicated to the research and development of new types of energy. Zhongke Jiamei Straw Gasifier (http://www.zkjm168.com) uses biomass as raw material, including rice straw, cotton stalks, wheat straw, grain husks, corn stalks, sorghum stalks, rice shells, fruit shells, tree branches, sawdust, wood shavings, weeds, and various plant vines, stems, and leaves. Additionally, waste residues from pharmaceutical factories, sugar factories, and tobacco factories can also be used. Straw-based biomass gas production uses renewable resources that are readily available, with almost zero cost for raw materials. It can be used for boiling water, cooking, heating, and bathing, making it suitable for various gas needs such as restaurants and canteens. The installation of the stove is done through ordinary pipelines without the need for steel cylinders, ensuring safe and reliable combustion (no pressure). A unique secondary filtration device ensures that the gas burns cleanly, producing no smoke or dust emissions. CO emission rates by Zhongke Jiamei Technology Co., Ltd. are below 0.1%. When in use, simply add the crop straw directly into the furnace chamber, and within 1 to 3 minutes, clean gas will be produced. Using only 3-5 kilograms of straw per day, daily needs can be met, saving more than 70% compared to ordinary firewood stoves.

When in operation, electronic ignition is used, allowing for instant lighting with adjustable flame intensity, blue flames reaching temperatures over 900°C, comparable to liquefied gas. A cigarette butt or piece of newspaper can ignite the stove, enabling long-term use after one initial ignition, with fire retention lasting up to 72 hours.

The straw gasifier adopts highly purified gas technology, pressure-free high-temperature pyrolysis gasification technology, and scientifically advanced gas production techniques, leading domestically in quality. The products are reliable, technologically advanced, clean, hygienic, energy-saving, and environmentally friendly. Each new gas stove retails for 150-360 yuan, ready to use upon purchase, requiring no additional equipment, convenient and economical, suitable for most families.
