The aerial platform fire truck, which integrates high-altitude fire fighting, rescue and risk elimination functions, is available at 18671079399.

by xfxybk on 2012-02-18 16:37:30

Our news report: Yesterday, the 68-meter high-reach aerial platform fire truck purchased by the Jiaonan municipal government for 8.6 million yuan officially joined the Haichang Road Special Fire Brigade, setting a new record for the highest firefighting vehicle in any county-level city in the entire province.

According to reports, this fire truck combines high-altitude firefighting, rescue, and emergency response functions into one unit. It is an electro-hydraulic driven, fully rotatable, articulated arm-style giant multi-functional fire truck, using an original German Mercedes-Benz chassis. The total length is 15.06 meters, height 4 meters, width 2.5 meters, with a rated working height of 68 meters and a rated working depth of 13.73 meters. Its work platform has a rated load capacity of 400kg and a maximum working span of 23 meters. It uses imported hydraulic water cannons and features 25 safety protection devices, forming a complete multi-layer protective system and intelligent fault diagnosis system, providing strong stability. With a wide range of operations, powerful fire rescue capabilities, and excellent mobility, it can be widely used for firefighting tasks in tall and super-tall buildings in large cities.

As of now, Jiaonan City has 166 buildings over 24 meters tall, making firefighting and rescue tasks increasingly demanding. The commissioning of the newly purchased 68-meter high-reach aerial platform fire truck marks a new step forward in the vehicle equipment construction of the Jiaonan Public Security Fire Brigade.

Hubei Jiangnan Dongfeng Special Automobile Co., Ltd. is a designated production factory by the National Development and Reform Commission, the only fire truck manufacturer certified by the Ministry of Public Security's Fire Bureau in the "Special Vehicle Capital", and an authorized modification plant of Dongfeng Motor Corporation. Their main products include fire trucks, tow trucks, aerial work platforms, expandable box transport vehicles, water sprinklers, fuel tankers, chemical liquid transporters, flatbed transporters, mobile power supply vehicles, garbage trucks, and many other varieties. All models have been listed in the national announcement directory, obtained ISO9001-2000 quality management system certification, and their products have passed CCC certification.
