The decoration of the marital home has always been very particular about Feng Shui. As an especially important part of the house that requires the most attention, for couples busy decorating their marital home, understanding the Feng Shui principles of the kitchen is very necessary! Today, I will bring everyone to learn about the taboos of kitchen Feng Shui.

The kitchen should not face south because the south belongs to fire, and the kitchen also belongs to fire. Fire on top of fire can be detrimental to the household, and food facing south tends to spoil easily. Therefore, the kitchen should not be set in the south if possible. The kitchen should also not be located in the center of the house. The center of a residence is most taboo against pollution, and the most suitable location is in the east or southeast of the residence. Both east and southeast belong to wood in the five elements, which can coexist peacefully with water and fire, making them particularly suitable as a good place for the kitchen and breakfast.

The kitchen door should not directly face the main door. As the saying goes, "Opening the door to see the stove results in wasting money." This layout can harm women's health and make it difficult for the family to accumulate wealth, leading to fluctuating luck. If such a layout is encountered, the only way to avoid it is to change the door.

The kitchen and toilet should not share the same door: Some families save space by having the kitchen and toilet share the same entrance and exit, causing an imbalance of water and fire in the home, which is very unlucky; even worse, entering the toilet first and then the kitchen completely ruins the appetite!

The kitchen floor should not be higher than the living room or bedroom floors. On one hand, this prevents backflow of dirty water; on the other hand, since there is a distinction between primary and secondary spaces, the kitchen should not dominate over the living room or bedrooms; finally, when moving from the kitchen to the dining area to serve food, it should be step-by-step ascending, otherwise, there may be a risk of losing wealth.

The stove should not have nothing behind it: The stove represents the health of the family, marriage, and fame, and should have some support. There should not be emptiness behind it, and it must rely on a solid wall. Even a glass wall is inappropriate.
