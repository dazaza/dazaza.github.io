Details on the Precautions for Car Beauty Decoration

The period before the Spring Festival every year is the peak season for consumers to buy cars. Besides paying purchase tax, registering the car, and buying insurance, many car owners also like to decorate their new cars well. Tinting, laying floor mats, seat covers... These have almost become the "essential" actions for many new car owners nowadays. However, this has its pros and cons. Do you know how much benefit it can bring to your beloved car? What are the precautions for car washing, beauty care, and decoration?

According to industry insiders, if not done carefully, new car decoration can easily damage the car, especially in cold winter climates, which makes car decoration even less suitable. This is related to the car parts; low winter temperatures can easily cause them to crack.

Laying floor mats can easily damage plastic components.

Many new car owners worry that the original carpet of the car will get dirty and hard to clean, so they always want to quickly lay a set of floor mats in their new car after purchasing it. In fact, this practice is completely unnecessary. To lay floor mats, all seats need to be disassembled and reinstalled. However, compared to professional workers on the assembly line, the assembly skills of ordinary workers at car accessory stores definitely fall short.

In winter, with lower temperatures, the elasticity of plastic parts decreases, making them easy to break or crack during installation. If some small parts get damaged, they may not be easily noticed. Although it might not be immediately apparent, from the perspective of long-term vehicle use, it is definitely not good. Additionally, since floor mats are waterproof but not breathable, once water enters between the car's floor and the mat, it is difficult for the owner to notice in time, leading to corrosion of the car's floor. Especially with some cheap floor mats, if the quality is substandard and toxic substances exceed standards, it can easily cause air pollution inside the car.

The Worst DIY Car Beauty Decoration Methods for Car Owners

Everyone wants their car to look nice and unique. Most car owners like to focus on car beauty decorations. After a makeover, do you know whether your car has changed for the better or looks like a mess? Currently, there are four popular yet terrible DIY methods we can review one by one.

First DIY Car Beauty Decoration Method: Cheap Underbody Lights

In China, most underbody lights seen are actually the worst examples of modified cars. The more modifications, the worse they look.

Second DIY Car Beauty Decoration Method: Car Body Stickers, Ugly

If you want to make your car more personalized, beautiful, and fashionable, car body stickers are one of the simplest and most effective ways. You can buy them online or at professional car decoration stores and directly apply them to your car for an extraordinary look—cute, fashionable, and tasteful. However, some people like to put outdated, tacky tattoo-style stickers on their cars. Not only do these lack aesthetic appeal in color coordination, but the gaudy patterns resembling gangster images are truly speechless. While it’s understandable that these car owners want to look cool, they lack any sense of aesthetics.

Third DIY Car Beauty Decoration Method: Using Perfume in Old or Low-End Cars

When choosing perfume, opt for genuine high-quality ones. A few yuan in cost, but you end up spending over a hundred yuan. You might think it's very stylish and good, but in reality, the smell of such perfumes is quite unpleasant.

Fourth DIY Car Beauty Decoration Method: Placing a Emblem on the Car Hood

Whether it's a car worth tens of millions or just a few ten thousand, car owners treat their vehicles like precious gems. This sentiment is understandable. Nowadays, many car owners like to imitate luxury cars by adding emblems to their own cars... I've personally seen rusty emblems standing upright on car hoods. You might fool someone who doesn't know much about cars, but if you encounter a professional, the owner's image takes a big hit. If your car is like this, you should change it right away.
