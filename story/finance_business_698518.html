by juyaqufa003 on 2012-03-09 09:56:23

Currently, the automobile industry has reached a consensus: with the development of economy and society, women's social status and income level are continuously improving. In addition, the popularization rate of the family "second car" is gradually increasing, and young women have gradually become the mainstream of car buyers. Automakers that accurately judge the trend of the car market have also confirmed this through sales data: in the "Let Go to Love" promotion activity for the L3 automatic model conducted by Lotus Automobile, the proportion of female new owners has even exceeded 50%.

Compared to men who pursue high configuration and high performance, women pay more attention to the appearance and comfort of the car when buying a car. Facing increasingly congested urban traffic, they want to feel at ease while driving in the city. The convenience of handling the car model is also quite important.

The results of a market survey in a certain province reveal some clues: when choosing the type of transmission for the pre-purchased car, 36.8% of female consumers chose to buy an automatic model, 31.6% of female consumers clearly chose a manual model, and more than 30% of women were not quite sure about the type of transmission for their intended purchase. Considering only the urban market, the proportion of women who prefer AT models is much higher than this number.

After all, the peak hour road conditions are very complex nowadays, and driving a manual transmission car is a nightmare for female friends. Automatic cars undoubtedly free up the left foot and alleviate the pain. For women who pursue driving convenience, Lotus L3 is obviously more attractive.

For women, apart from being a means of transportation, cars also have the spiritual meaning of embodying personal style and individuality, which has the same effect as fashion, jewelry, and bags. Compared with the invisible operation performance, the visible exterior design and brand are more intuitive for women, thus receiving more attention. This plays a decisive influence on women's car purchases.

Lotus L3 inherits the design concept and manufacturing process of Lotus sports cars. It integrates strong sports car elements into A-class sedans. The interior also extensively uses the design of circular aesthetic elements, such as three-spoke European sports steering wheel, cat ear-shaped round instrument panel, vertical round air conditioning buttons, round audio control keys, etc., creating a strong sports car driving atmosphere. Why should beautiful women with fine cars only appear at car shows?

It has become the most cost-effective automatic transmission model in the domestic mainstream A0 sedan configuration, truly achieving "manual transmission price, automatic transmission enjoyment."

Instant speed control makes it one of the most driving-sense automatic transmission sedans in the same class domestically, allowing women to free up their left foot and relax their mind during the driving process.
