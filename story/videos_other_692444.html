A warm cotton-padded jacket for a family member

by mooectorer on 2012-03-06 09:44:57

When you feel the biting cold, a warm cotton-padded jacket from a loved one always comes just in time; when you get lost, the gaze of your loved ones always makes you feel there is always a glimmer of light ahead; when you encounter failure and difficulties, the warm and strong hands of your loved ones are always so powerful; when you feel surrounded on all sides, your loved ones always appear first in front of your eyes; when you are sad and in tears, your loved ones are always by your side silently wiping away the tears at the corner of your eyes; when you are far away, among the countless stars in the sky, some are always the tears shed by your loved ones for you; when you are full of excitement, your loved ones are always the first to rush to your side to share your joy and happiness; when you inadvertently find wrinkles creeping onto their foreheads and silver threads mixed in their hair, it is only then that you realize they are no longer young, they are slowly aging, yet they continue to work tirelessly without any thought of reward, in their eyes, you will always be a child who never grows up. In my memory, mother has always been in motion, rarely seen her stop. After closing father's eyes, looking at father's peaceful sleeping face, his features were very haggard, but not scary, maintaining the kindness he had shown all his life, quietly leaving. During this period, I had to work while studying. When I was listless and returned home after tendon reinforcement surgery, I did not see father. Mother told me that father had improved greatly after being treated by a miracle doctor and was now at someone else’s home, so I didn’t have to drop out of school. I knew they were superstitious, but after that, father miraculously got better, and I escaped the misfortune of dropping out of school again. Why didn't mother listen to her daughter? Even if she had rested one more day or an afternoon, perhaps everything wouldn't have happened. To prevent accidents, mother stayed awake for two days and two nights to take care of her, while preparing three glucose bottles to continuously keep her daughter's body temperature stable.

Unexpectedly, when I returned home dispirited, I didn't see father. Mother told me that father had improved much after being treated by a miraculous doctor and was now at someone else's house, so I didn't need to drop out of school. I knew they were superstitious, but after that, father miraculously got better, and I therefore escaped the misfortune of dropping out of school again.

I tapped my daughter's frail shoulder and said, "Don't talk nonsense, how could that happen?" The daughter said, "Just in case the grading goes wrong, or there is an error in totaling the scores! Just in case, you know, you need to be prepared for the worst."

Regardless of how hard we try, regardless of what we do, this family can never be restored... Looking at the open space she swept, the moss on the ground had grown thick.

"Mother remained silent for a moment, then said: "Why is your second sister so heartless? She reincarnated but didn't even send me a dream."

Even though I searched everywhere nearby, I couldn't find the child.

My dearest father, but I haven't given anything back to you, and you've left us. Every day, grandma wants to see her grandson. Sometimes, when the son and daughter-in-law take the child out to play, the old lady feels restless if she doesn't see him for a day. She calls several times to remind them that the child should eat, drink water, and sleep. Her care is meticulous, and when she talks about her obedient grandson, she smiles broadly. But grandmother doesn't know all this.
