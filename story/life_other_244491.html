by longersing on 2009-09-30 18:23:14

Talk on Feng Shui of indoor decoration, the theory of "man left and woman right", suggests that men sleep on the left while women sleep on the right. Wardrobes and air conditioners should be on the left or left upper side of the bed. Many friends have heard this theory since they were young. But what are the other sayings about "man left and woman right" in house decoration? The following are some theories we've compiled.

For a big turnaround in luck, unlucky ones can soon become lucky. Men sleep on the left while women sleep on the right, wardrobes and air conditioners should be on the left or left upper side of the bed.

There is an ancient saying: "Man left and woman right," which isn't just for fun! Jiang Bai Le points out that loving couples shouldn't forget which side to sleep on. If the wife sleeps on the left and the husband sleeps on the right, it's either the woman who tends to be dominant and hardworking, or the man who is weak and useless. Especially, the position of wardrobes and air conditioners can also affect relationships. Sleeping behind a shrine not only makes it hard for singles to find a partner but also easily causes problems in marital relations!

Jing Wen, host of a metaphysics program, is particularly worried about women not finding their Mr. Right and not being loved by their husbands.

She suggests that too many windows in the bedroom can attract bad romantic luck. A single bed could be changed into a double bed, or two pillows could be placed on the bed. She also provides clever methods such as using love beans, marriage-controlling shoes, pink crystals, or even bathing to attract good lovers. Even a woman's powder compact has its use!

Face reading is still no problem!

If one eye is bigger than the other, it's called "yin-yang eyes." If a man has a larger left eye, he tends to harm his wife or beat her; if the right eye is larger, he fears his wife. For women, a larger right eye indicates a harsh personality and lack of femininity, often leading to a hardworking life. A larger left eye brings help to the husband but early marriage is not advisable.

Palm reading is also fine!

The "peach blossom lines" are short curved lines extending from the wrist towards the palm, representing charm with the opposite sex. If it crosses the life line, it becomes the "excessive desire line," indicating excessive sexual desires.
