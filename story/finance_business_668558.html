Nike Footwear - Making Thuds on the Globe of Fashion. In 1987, a new line of Nike shoes was launched. In 1985, the Dunk shoe was launched for basketball players. It was named the Nike Dunk SB and was initially targeted only at basketball players. The embedded technology was designed to support the sporty features of the shoe. In 1998, the Dunk range of shoes was re-launched as a fashion icon among the youth. Compared to Nike's first shoes, the Dunk range was launched as a lower-profile shoe. However, the sole was lighter than the original shoe. To popularize the Dunk range, Nike even launched a program called "College Colors" Program. The Dunk range was further expanded into Nike Dunks Premium, Nike Dunks Low, and Nike Dunks Mid. The Nike Dunk Highs are impressive to say and undoubtedly eye-catching to look at. Compared to the Dunk High Shoe line, the Nike Dunks Low and Nike Dunks Mid range shoes have a street style but certainly with higher performance quality.
