Appreciation of Namie Amuro's 11 versatile hair styles

by sonlence on 2009-11-30 22:20:55

Namie Amuro has long passed the age of 30, but her sex appeal remains undiminished. This is largely due to her versatile hairstyle designs. Apart from her signature long curly hair, she has also tried out sweet hairstyles. Today, let's take a look at some of the classic looks Namie Amuro has presented for us! The bohemian style that started this year has already been imitated by Japanese magazines. Tying a headband on the forehead and pairing it with long straight hair highlights femininity.

Loose curls add a touch of cuteness, while hair clips and small hats are the finishing touches to the hairstyle. Of course, pink is synonymous with Namie Amuro.

Her seductive curls, deep gaze, and bright red lips create a stunning portrayal of the Moulin Rouge dancer's charm.

A retro young lady look from the 60s, with the length of the curls being just right.

Straight hair makes her look younger, without heavy makeup, she appears youthful and energetic.

A retro and luxurious European court style. A deep gray smoky eye makeup and the updo have a distinctive flavor.

With a large bow on top of her head, she looks like a cute Barbie doll. This is a very sweet retro look. Thick bangs and a big bun express the adorable qualities of a young girl.

The straight hair + baseball cap look instantly transforms her into a sporty teenager, full of youth and vitality. It can be seen that Namie Amuro really loves the color pink.

This is a very sweet retro look. Thick bangs and a big bun express the adorable qualities of a young girl.

This is a sexy and mature smoky makeup look. To match her hair color, brown eyeshadow is used to create a light smoky effect. The curls on top of her head are loosely tied up, making her face appear smaller.

A rare middle-parted black hair look, with the eye makeup still being the focus. The eyeliner is bold, and the makeup has a metallic sheen, exuding a mature and sexy charm.
