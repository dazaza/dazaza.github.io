Introduction of dust removal accessories - selection principle of electromagnetic pulse valve

by hblxgggs on 2009-12-07 14:05:20

The electromagnetic pulse valve belongs to one category of dust removal accessories, and its selection has four principles - applicability, economy, safety, and reliability.

1. Applicability: Not applicable equals to waste money on useless things and cause trouble!

1.1 Medium characteristics

- Gaseous, liquid or mixed states should select different types of electromagnetic valves respectively, such as ZQDF for air, ZQDF-Y for liquid, ZQDF-2 (or -3) for steam, otherwise it is easy to cause misoperation. The ZDF series multifunctional electromagnetic valve can be used for both gas and liquid. It's best to specify the medium state when ordering so that the installation user does not need to adjust it.

1.2 Medium temperature

- Different specifications of products should be selected according to the medium temperature, otherwise the coil will burn out and the seal will age, seriously affecting the service life.

1.3 Medium viscosity

- Usually below 50cSt. If this value is exceeded, and the diameter is greater than 15mm, use the ZDF series multifunctional electromagnetic valve for special orders. If the diameter is less than 15mm, order a high-viscosity electromagnetic valve.

1.4 Medium cleanliness

- When the medium cleanliness is not high, a backflush filter valve should be installed in front of the electromagnetic valve. If the pressure is low, a direct-acting diaphragm electromagnetic valve can be selected.

1.5 Medium direction

- If the medium is unidirectional circulation and reverse flow is not allowed, ZDF-N and ZQDF-N single-directional circulation must be used. Please make special requirements if bidirectional circulation is needed.

1.6 Medium temperature

- The medium temperature should be selected within the allowable range of the electromagnetic valve.

2. Pipeline parameters

2.1 Select valve bore and model according to the direction of the medium flow and pipeline connection method.

2.2 Select nominal bore according to flow and valve Kv value, or select the same as pipeline inner diameter.

2.3 Working pressure difference

- For minimum working pressure difference above 0.04Mpa, indirect pilot type can be selected; for minimum working pressure difference close to or less than zero, direct-acting or step-by-step direct-acting type must be selected.

3. Environmental conditions

3.1 The highest and lowest environmental temperatures should be selected within the allowable range. If there are differences, please make special orders.

3.2 In environments with high relative humidity and water droplets or rain, waterproof electromagnetic valves should be selected.

3.3 In environments with frequent vibration, shaking, and impact, special varieties should be selected, such as ship-use electromagnetic valves.

3.4 In corrosive or explosive environments, corrosion-resistant types should be selected based on safety requirements.

3.5 If environmental space is limited, please select multifunctional electromagnetic valves, which eliminate bypasses and three manual valves and facilitate online maintenance.

4. Power supply conditions

4.1 According to the type of power supply, select AC or DC electromagnetic valves respectively. Generally, AC power is convenient to obtain.

4.2 Voltage specifications should prioritize AC220V.DC24V.

4.3 Power voltage fluctuation

- Typically, AC selects +%10%.-15%, DC allows ±%10 around. If there are differences, stable voltage measures must be taken or special orders made.

4.4 Select rated current and power consumption according to power capacity. Note that VA value is higher during AC start-up, and indirect pilot type electromagnetic valves should be prioritized when capacity is insufficient.

5. Control accuracy

5.1 Ordinary electromagnetic valves only have open and close two positions. When high control accuracy and parameter stability are required, multi-position electromagnetic valves should be selected; Z3CF three-position normally open electromagnetic valve has three flow rates: micro-start, fully open, and closed; ZDF-Z1/1 combination multifunctional electromagnetic valve has four flow rates: fully open, largely open, slightly open, and fully open.

5.2 Action time

- Refers to the time from connecting or cutting off the electric signal to completing the main valve action. Only our company's patented product multifunctional electromagnetic valve can separately adjust opening and closing times, not only meeting control accuracy requirements but also preventing water hammer damage.

5.3 Leakage amount

- The leakage amount values given in the sample are commonly used economic grades. If considered too high, special orders can be made.

Economy: Uneconomical is a waste of funds, energy, and even life.

- One of the criteria for selection, but must be based on safety, applicability, and reliability.

- Economy is not just the product price, but more importantly, consider its function and quality as well as installation, maintenance, and other accessory costs.

- More importantly, the cost of an electromagnetic valve in the entire automatic control system or production line is negligible. If cheap choices lead to errors, the resulting damages could be significant. In short, economy does not solely refer to product price but rather the performance-price ratio and comprehensive cost-price ratio.

Safety: Ignoring safety can lead to disasters!

1. Corrosive medium: Plastic King electromagnetic valves and all stainless steel ones are recommended; for strongly corrosive media, isolation membrane type must be selected. Neutral media should preferably use copper alloy as valve shell material electromagnetic valves, otherwise, rust chips often fall off, especially in infrequently moving situations. Ammonia valves cannot use copper materials.

2. Explosive environment: Must select corresponding explosion-proof grade products; outdoor installation or dusty occasions should select waterproof, dustproof varieties.

3. The nominal pressure of the electromagnetic pulse valve should exceed the maximum working pressure inside the pipe.

Reliability: Unreliable will harm the entire system!

1. Service life

- This item is not included in the factory test items, belonging to type test items. To ensure quality, choose branded products from reputable manufacturers.

2. Work mode: Divided into long-term work mode, repeated short-time work mode, and short-time work mode. For long-term valve opening with only short-time closure, normally open electromagnetic valves should be selected. For short-time work mode with large batches, special orders can be made to reduce power consumption.

3. Work frequency

- If high action frequency is required, the structure should preferentially select direct-acting electromagnetic valves, and power should preferentially select AC.

4. Action reliability

- Strictly speaking, this test has not yet been officially included in China's electromagnetic valve professional standards. To ensure quality, choose branded products from reputable manufacturers. In some cases, the number of actions is not many, but the requirement for reliability is very high, such as fire protection, emergency protection, etc., do not take it lightly. Especially important, dual insurance with two connected should be adopted.
