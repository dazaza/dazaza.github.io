The Strongest Break-up Letter in History

by wlhch on 2007-06-08 20:32:24

Actually, the feelings between us cannot be described in a day or two. There's no need to worry about whether we have any disagreements that lead to arguments. Because you are my favorite, it doesn't matter even if you're wrong. I heard from your mom that you've been restless recently. Is there something on your mind? Do you still remember Zhou Hui's "Promise"? If there's anything you want to tell me, even if it's just a small thing, we have no secrets between us, right? Do you know what a fried egg looks like? You are the yolk and I am the white. The yolk and the white are inseparable, you are me, and I am you. Yesterday, when you talked to me on the phone impatiently, my heart hurt so much. What's troubling you? Is it because you haven't been sleeping well? Otherwise, from the bloodshot eyes, I can tell you stayed up late. I want to ask you, shall we go for a walk this Sunday? Because I've been so busy lately, and I haven't had time to meet you for a long time. My friends invited me to hang out recently, but I declined them one by one. My friends are angry, saying that I've been like a recluse recently. I think that if it goes on like this, those words might come true. I really want to go out for a walk, but how could I be happy without you? Not seeing you recently, I feel very uncomfortable, not thinking about tea or food. Today, you called me and said you have to work overtime and can't go out with me. I want to visit you at your company, but I'm afraid of disturbing you. I want to tell you, I miss you so much, I just want to hear your voice. I asked your mom if you've been busy recently? She said she hasn't heard you mention it. Are you really avoiding me? Or is it because you have someone else now? I'm so petty! Why am I so petty to think like this? I'm so low! I almost ruined you with my selfish thoughts. No one can replace your innocence, your purity, and everything about you. I think, maybe our fate has really come to an end. Even you, who loves snacks, didn't touch the cookies I bought for you. Having good luck has never been my destiny, and of course, you don't belong to me. If you want to pursue your dreams, go ahead! I won't stop you. That's all! My dearest person, I will always remember you. Alright! I should let the wind carry my love for you to the sea!
