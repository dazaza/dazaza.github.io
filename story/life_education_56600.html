Using 1 yuan in this way is simply genius!

by wxfily0607 on 2008-03-24 16:11:58

Starting with a one-yuan coin, you buy a fifty-yuan counterfeit note from a brother on the streets;

You then go to a bookstand near an elementary school and make a phone call, getting 49.6 yuan in real money from the grandfather with presbyopia;

Go to the underground market to buy pirated discs, purchasing nine for 45 yuan (bargained down by 0.4 yuan), leaving five yuan to take the cheapest long-distance bus to a nearby county, where you sell each disc for 15 yuan, earning 135 yuan;

Spend five yuan on the return trip, then buy 260 pens for students at a small commodity wholesale market. Sell them outside the school gate for 1.5 yuan each, earning 390 yuan;

Deliberately lose 100 yuan playing mahjong with your street brother, learning from him the supply channel for Ecstasy pills. Buy 58 pills for 290 yuan and sell them to nightclubs for ten yuan each, earning 580 yuan;

Pay 80 yuan as protection fee, leaving 500 yuan to recruit ten high school students as your juniors in the nightclub (50 yuan per person is enough). They collect protection fees for you, earning 2000 yuan per day. After dividing the profits, you earn 1500 yuan;

Go to the entertainment center to find escorts, offer 300 yuan but pay 1000 yuan. The escort will be loyal to you and recognize you as the boss, introducing her sisters to you. With 500 yuan left, print advertisements at a nearly bankrupt printing factory and paste them all over the city. Each of the ten escorts receives five customers per day, giving you 100 yuan per customer. Daily income is 10*5*100=5000 yuan. In a month, it's 150,000 yuan;

Rent a shop for 100,000 yuan to open an entertainment center, hire 30 escorts (high-end) for 50,000 yuan. Each escort receives three customers per day, giving you 300 yuan per customer. Monthly income is 30*3*300*30=810,000 yuan;

Pay 100,000 yuan to industrial and commercial tax and public security experts as protection fees, leaving 710,000 yuan to bring goods from Yunnan. Once the goods reach the mainland, they are worth ten times as much, taking about half a month, earning 7 million yuan (100,000 yuan spent on the road and necessary gun purchases);

Quit this business, register a real estate company, and hire migrant workers from the countryside (you might even hire those who bought your pirated discs, haha);

Hiring 100 workers at a monthly salary of 600 yuan costs 60,000 yuan. Spend 1,000,000 yuan on entertaining municipal officials (never spend less), securing all municipal projects. Build substandard projects, demolish and rebuild roads, earning 20 million yuan in two months;

Compensate 30,000 yuan for the life of a worker (one died from overwork in two months, no problem since you have connections above). Hire a Ph.D. graduate fluent in five foreign languages at a monthly salary of 50,000 yuan to find brands overseas to act as agents. Agent Italian shoes for sale in China, then take samples to Wenzhou to produce counterfeits, earning 150 million yuan in three months;

Discovered by the Italian manufacturer, compensate 10 million yuan, change industries. Use the more than 100 million yuan earned to seek financing from American financial groups to create a website (keep the Ph.D. busy). Raise 200 million US dollars, use a year to crush Sohu and Sina, becoming the largest portal site in China. Go public, reaching a market value of 3 billion US dollars;

Sell some stocks to bribe securities companies, become a dark horse, rising tenfold on the first day. On the second day, sell 49% of the stocks, earning 5 billion US dollars.

In less than two years, you become a Chinese billionaire with assets exceeding 10 billion US dollars, ensuring comfort for the rest of your life. What? Still not satisfied? Then you can return to your old trade, buy weapons, become an assassin. I have contacts with Bin Laden. Would you like me to connect you?
