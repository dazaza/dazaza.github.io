A hero of the age can only be the latter surpassing the former!

by vorx2473 on 2012-02-25 08:11:07

Every day, I write down what I see and hear. Maybe, this is my habit. Last night, I slept the most soundly since I came to Beijing. I don't know why. Perhaps, it's because I have really settled down and started to pursue the first step towards my future. Or, it's because of the feeling in my heart. People can never see through, can never see clearly. What my heart has always hoped for is a peaceful home, a nest where I can rest when I am tired. I don't ask for it to be as warm and full of love as home. I only hope that I can rest well here, and continue to strive for tomorrow.

At seven in the morning, I got up. I couldn't sleep, so I picked up the camera out of habit, put on my backpack, and went to the nearby overpass to take pictures. I don't know why I take pictures, or what I am taking. I only know that I want to leave these footprints behind. No matter whether I succeed or fail in the future, I will not regret it because I have tried hard. These mementos will be my mourning for the present in the future. In the early morning, the ordinary bustling crowd has not yet appeared. Only the rushing cars and the cold air accompany me. After walking around casually, it was almost time to go to work. The first day, I should give myself some encouragement. When I arrived at the subway station, I realized why people in Beijing get up so early every day to go to work. It's not to arrive early, but to avoid being late, to fight for every second.

The day at the company was indeed very happy. I met people who had the same goal as me. They are also striving for tomorrow, for survival. To put it bluntly, everyone is striving for tomorrow's breakfast, even for tomorrow's rent, everything about tomorrow. I like to listen to Liu Kunshan's song. The lyrics are really realistic. Making a living in Beijing is not that simple. Yes, gradually, I found that my innocence will change with my experiences and encounters. I believe that I will persevere. Not for anything, it's very simple. For my parents and sister waiting for me far away, and all the people who care about me and love me. There is a saying: A promise made is a debt owed. I have made a commitment to myself, and I will fulfill it. Now, I find myself really mature. Since we want to enjoy everything this society brings us, what is our condition? We should strive, we should make ourselves have the conditions to let ourselves and our families enjoy. Those things are really far away, but as long as I am full of hope, no matter what happens every day, as long as I still have the spirit to fight, because we are still young, youth is our greatest power, our biggest gas station. Others cannot take it away from us. As long as we are still young, we will possess everything. Those things are just the foundation I lay for the future.

Today, many people hope to become the CEO of the company group in the future. But have they thought about it? When they have these ideas, have they thought about the reality they will face? The ruthless elimination system of this society. Yes, this is a game. We have already entered this game. There is no way not to follow the rules of the game, unless we leave. This is the road we choose ourselves. No one can change it, unless we do. I have thought about it. What I want is not the person who receives salary every day, every month, every year, but the person who gives salary to others. Someone asked me, why do you have this idea? I believe, it's not just me. Maybe there are other people. Many people will be like me, for certain reasons. They only hope to dedicate their youth to the work they love. Behind them, it's not only others' expectations, but also someone. Perhaps, it's a promise made to someone, it's the love and hate for someone, which makes them strive so hard for everything tomorrow, sparing no effort to fight.

After looking at the company's website, yes, the boss wasn't born a boss. They were once young, they fought hard. They also have those little-known bitter stories. And now, they can proudly stand in front of everyone and share everything. Because they have the capital to boast. They succeeded, became a generation of heroes, became role models in the eyes of thousands of people. Maybe, some people will succeed, some will fail. But effort is the thing a person cannot lack. I like those few words: Success breeds kings, failure breeds bandits. Whether success or failure lies in the struggle. Yes, we should struggle! For tomorrow, for ourselves, say to ourselves: Come on, we can definitely do it!
