Many current smart home systems are set up by logging into the system host through a router, which is essentially an open invitation to hackers. Anyone with basic computer knowledge can use common card kings to crack wireless passwords and then crack smart home host passwords, allowing them to turn on/off your lights and curtains at any time, turn on your speakers in the middle of the night, even remotely record footage from your cameras, disable your security system, and open your doors.
