Men's underwear fabric February 9 domestic various light纺material prices daily fast report

by hjkls4qlop on 2012-02-29 13:51:32

On February 9, the PTA spot price was adjusted, while the MEG price remained temporarily stable. The prices of semi-dull polyester chips and bright polyester chips continued to consolidate. The CDP chip price was stable, and the polyester bottle chip price was temporarily stable. The prices of acrylic short fibers (1.5D x 38mm) and acrylic tow (3D x 102mm) were also adjusted. The CPL price showed a slight increase, and the nylon-6 chip price trend was flat.

The polyester filament market remained relatively quiet with average trading volumes. Various products were traded in small quantities, but the polyester filament market quotes remained stable. For example, mainstream factories in Shengze reported POY 75D/72F at around 13,400 RMB/T. Additionally, it was noted that warp knitting purchasing power was slightly higher than water-jet weaving. Several local and surrounding fiber manufacturers indicated that downstream weaving factories and draw-texturing enterprises currently hold certain polyester filament inventories, resulting in insufficient enthusiasm for replenishment purchases. As such, trading volumes in the polyester filament market are low, with almost no bulk transactions. However, due to high polymerization costs, the opposing situation in the polyester filament market is expected to continue in the short term. Some individual popular differentiated products might see slight price increases.

The full cotton yarn trading volume was not large, but some varieties continued to show rising trends. From the perspective of product sales, the demand for 32s full cotton yarn was better, mainly used for air-jet nylon-cotton series. Other specifications had relatively average trading volumes. In the blended yarn products, the prices of some individual varieties of polyester-cotton yarns continued to rise; the prices of polyester-viscose yarn series products remained steady with slight increases, though trading volumes were not significant. The pure polyester yarn market showed stability with limited trading volumes, and the polyester short fiber price remained suppressed. The viscose rayon yarn market continued to rise, with prices showing an upward trend. The market price of viscose short fibers continued to increase. It is expected that the viscose rayon yarn market will have an upward trend in the future.
