Hebei spends a huge amount of 3.3 million to purchase the first batch of compressed air foam fire trucks 18671079399

by anonymous on 2012-02-29 11:36:49

On the 26th, reporters learned from the Shijiazhuang Public Security Fire Brigade that two domestically produced CAFS fire trucks with special chassis, purchased at a cost of 3.3 million yuan and representing the first batch in Hebei Province, had successfully arrived. This significantly strengthened the firefighting and rescue capabilities of the Shijiazhuang fire department.

It was understood that the domestically produced special chassis CAFS fire truck is manufactured by Hubei Jiangnan Dongfeng Special Purpose Vehicles Co., Ltd. Jiangnan Dongfeng Special Purpose Vehicles Firefighting Equipment Technology Co., Ltd. is a national high-tech enterprise that undertakes scientific research projects for fire trucks under the Ministry of Public Security's Fire Bureau. The company provided 70% of the fire trucks needed for the 2008 Beijing Olympics, and has so far obtained 11 national patents.

The domestically produced special chassis CAFS fire truck purchased this time has superior overall vehicle handling performance. The cab structure is made of fully steel-stamped welded parts and can accommodate eight people. It has the ability to spray dry and wet Class A foam simultaneously and possesses the capability to extinguish fires using both Class A foam and pure water. It has undergone a 330-meter high-rise test and successfully achieved a foam spray over 10 meters at 330 meters. The integral compartment is made of full aluminum alloy structure, with an internal independent liquid tank (centered) on both sides being equipment boxes. Both the pump room and the equipment box are equipped with sufficiently bright lighting lamps, and LED light strips are installed on both sides of the roll-up doors. The compressed air foam system is centrally located, with a liquid tank capacity of 4000L water and 200L Class A foam, utilizing CAN bus communication. This vehicle integrates firefighting, personal protection, and demolition capabilities, so these two fire trucks will play a significant role in future firefighting and rescue operations.

According to reports, the Shijiazhuang Public Security Fire Brigade will next contact the technical personnel of the manufacturer to provide training in batches for all officers and soldiers across the city on the specific operation of the domestically produced special chassis CAFS fire truck, ensuring its swift deployment into current firefighting duty and standby work.

Hubei Jiangnan Dongfeng Special Purpose Vehicles Co., Ltd. is a designated production factory by the National Development and Reform Commission, the only manufacturer in the "Special Purpose Vehicle Capital" with qualifications from the Fire Bureau of the Ministry of Public Security, and a supporting modification plant for Dongfeng Motor Corporation. The main products produced and sold include fire trucks, recovery vehicles, aerial work platforms, wing-opening box-type transport vehicles, sprinkler trucks, fuel trucks, chemical liquid transport vehicles, flatbed transport vehicles, mobile power supply vehicles, garbage trucks, and many other varieties. All models have been listed in the national announcement directory, obtained ISO9001-2000 quality management system certification, and their products have passed CCC certification.
