Benxi Large Iron Mine Hopefully to Start Operation in Two Months

by gfdsskkgfek1 on 2012-02-08 10:46:27

The Benxi large iron mine is expected to start mining operations in two months - Today's Focus - Industry News - Building Materials Network. The General Manager of Benxi Yizhongxin Company, Zhao Wenxing, stated that the experts from the design institute require a larger initial mining scale to ensure the most reasonable cost control for development. Currently, technical experts are conducting the final argumentation on this issue. If everything goes smoothly, mining operations could begin in two months.

It has been over a month since the discovery of the Benxi large iron mine was announced to the public. Has the mine already entered the mining process?

Yesterday, "The 21st Century Business Herald" exclusively interviewed Zhao Wenxing, General Manager of Benxi Yizhongxin Company.

Regarding the mining rights of the Benxi iron mine, Zhao Wenxing said: "There is no final conclusion yet. According to relevant regulations and the cooperation agreement between Yizhongxin and Liaoning Province, Yizhongxin obtaining the mining rights of the Benxi iron mine is not a problem. The State-owned Assets Supervision and Administration Commission of Liaoning Province and the Department of Land and Resources have repeatedly urged and encouraged early development."

A related company of Yizhongxin Group, as well as the chairman of its backing group, Wanlijia Group, Li Weibo, also introduced yesterday: "The Benxi iron mine project has not yet officially started mining because it is currently undergoing preliminary work such as design and engineering equipment preparation. Once there is news, it will be released immediately."

Facing the doubt of "limited financial strength," Li Weibo frankly said, "Due to various factors such as deep burial, the Benxi iron mine indeed has significant mining difficulty and investment requirements, but there is no problem with the company's funds."

"The registered capital of 10 million yuan for Yizhongxin does not represent the company's investment capability being only 10 million yuan. Currently, the entire large group has dozens of companies under its umbrella, and if necessary, tens of billions of yuan in funds can be mobilized," Li Weibo said.

Below is the full transcript of Zhao Wenxing's interview with our newspaper.

"The 21st Century Business Herald" (hereinafter referred to as NBD): The industry is highly concerned about the mining rights of the Benxi large iron mine. Has Yizhongxin obtained the mining certificate?

Zhao Wenxing: According to relevant industry regulations, the right to prospect and the right to mine naturally transition. That is, whoever finances the exploration of underground iron mines has priority for the mining rights of that iron mine. Currently, we are still waiting for the issuance of the mining certificate, which requires going through relevant approval procedures.

NBD: Has the exploration of the Benxi large iron mine ended? What is the latest progress?

Zhao Wenxing: According to our estimates, the Benxi large iron mine will officially be approved in two months, followed by the commencement of mining operations, including the initiation of pre-construction in the mining area. Currently, all preparatory work is progressing, while communication with relevant local government departments continues to ensure starting construction according to plan.

NBD: If the mining certificate is not obtained, can construction proceed on schedule?

Zhao Wenxing: We are not worried about the mining rights at present because of previous agreements. Additionally, we are not an exception; the "Mineral Resources Law" includes relevant provisions. This scheme has particularly high technical requirements, so it takes a lot of time.

NBD: Are there any preliminary intended partners for mining technology?

Zhao Wenxing: We have conducted multiple inspections and technical discussions. The final mining plan has not been determined yet and still requires final argumentation.

NBD: Is the Benxi large iron mine more inclined towards adopting overseas technology for mining?

Zhao Wenxing: Not necessarily. Based on our investigations, domestic mining technology schemes are also very advanced. However, the Benxi large iron mine will be built into one of the world's most renowned mining areas, so it is not excluded that overseas-related technologies may be introduced. One important point is that overseas mining machinery and equipment will likely be purchased more extensively.

NBD: Why hasn't the exploration report of the Benxi large iron mine been released yet?

Zhao Wenxing: Strictly speaking, exploration work will continue alongside the mining project and will never truly end. The exploration report currently released actually just accurately selects the first mining area. Once the first mining area is determined, construction of the mining area can commence.

NBD: How should the first mining area be understood? Is it phase-based mining?

Zhao Wenxing: We will first create an overall mining plan, while simultaneously focusing on the technical plan for the first mining area. The first mining area has basically been determined, and now supplementary exploration is needed, along with hiring relevant technical design departments and mining design research institutes for final argumentation.

NBD: If development begins this year, will there be any issues with funding? Can it be guaranteed?

Zhao Wenxing: We have continuously tracked this for 13 years, and there is no problem with funding. Most of the funds will be used to purchase advanced equipment, and we are currently negotiating with domestic and international equipment manufacturers.

NBD: Has Benxi Steel Group completed its shareholding participation up to this point?

Zhao Wenxing: Not yet, but negotiations are ongoing. Benxi Steel’s participation is almost certain, with 20% equity already allocated to them. We are awaiting final approval from relevant state-owned asset departments, which just takes longer due to state-owned enterprise approval processes. We have specifically negotiated this, where they contribute a small portion of the funds, and specific details are not convenient to disclose. Although we are reluctant to give away shares, we still welcome Benxi Steel's participation.

NBD: How large is the first mining area of the mining district?

Zhao Wenxing: Experts from the design institute require a larger mining scale to ensure the most reasonable cost control for development. Currently, technical experts are conducting the final argumentation on this issue.

Shenzhen Connection

"Yizhongxin operating out of a restroom is laughable"

Yesterday, rumors surfaced about Shenzhen Yizhongxin Company allegedly having a false registration, making the Benxi large iron mine once again a focal point.

"Saying we operate out of a restroom is simply laughable!" Zhao Wenxing, General Manager of Benxi Yizhongxin Company, said during an interview with "The 21st Century Business Herald."

As for why the office cannot be found at the registered address, Zhao Wenxing explained that since Benxi Yizhongxin Company is a subsidiary of Shenzhen Yizhongxin Mining Investment Company, he cannot discuss too much about the headquarters' information in Shenzhen.

The official website of Wanlijia Group points out that Shenzhen Yizhongxin Mining Investment Company, as an affiliate of Wanlijia Group, was established on December 26, 2007. At the same time, relevant staff from Wanlijia Group confirmed that the Shengyi Hotel on the first floor of Metro Tower and Shenzhen Yizhongxin Company belong to the same major shareholder—Shenzhen Wanlijia Group—and Metro Tower is also Wanlijia Group's own property.

Upon checking the website of the Shenzhen Administration for Industry and Commerce, the address of Shenzhen Yizhongxin Mining Investment Company had undergone one change, with the change date being August 6, 2009. The specific changed address could not be queried.

Yesterday afternoon, reporters from "The 21st Century Business Herald" met Zhao Wenxing and Li Weibo, chairman of Wanlijia Group behind Yizhongxin, who had just returned to Shenzhen at the Diwang Tower.

Zhao Wenxing said that the registration of Shenzhen Yizhongxin Company is completely legal. It's just that not much attention was paid to the arrangement of the office location at the time because actual operations were not conducted there.

Reporters noticed that the "Yizhongxin Mining" sign has now been hung at the front desk of Wanlijia Group's office in Diwang Tower. Wanlijia explained that considering branding, it has decided to relocate Yizhongxin's office to Shenzhen's landmark building—the Diwang Tower, and the relevant change procedures are currently being processed.

An acquaintance of Li Weibo remarked, "This person has a strong background and his capabilities should not be underestimated." After investigation, reporters learned that Li Weibo has battled in the capital market for many years and was the founder and chairman of the board of Hong Kong-listed Weicheng Group, whose market value once reached over 7 billion Hong Kong dollars. "Li Weibo has very strong financing abilities," the above-mentioned individual commented.
