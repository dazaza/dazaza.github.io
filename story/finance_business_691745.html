Since its establishment in 2003, the "China International Wallpaper & Fabric Exhibition" has been cultivated for nearly a decade by Beijing Zhongzhuang Huagang Building Technology Exhibition Co., Ltd. and peers in the wallpaper industry. It has now developed into the largest professional exhibition of wallpapers and fabrics not only in China but also globally. It is the preferred platform for distributors, agents, and principal units to conduct trade negotiations. The exhibition area has expanded year by year. The "China (Beijing) Wallpaper & Fabric Exhibition" held on March 2, 2011, set a record as the largest ever, with an exhibition area reaching 55,000 square meters and over 600 exhibitors. According to electronic gate statistics, the four-day exhibition received 180,000 visitors, laying a solid foundation for manufacturers to expand their national market.

The year 2011 was a milestone in the maturation of the home decoration industry. As the fashionability of China's home decoration industry gradually increased, product styles increasingly followed the trends of home decoration design. Soft furnishings will become the leading force in the fashion trend of home decoration, leading the diversified fashion style of home decoration. Under the concept of "innovation, breakthrough, development," the exhibition organizing committee continuously innovates and creatively proposes that products such as wallpapers, fabrics, carpets, and window decorations should be better applied to home decoration, making business opportunities and benefits more evident. From March 5-8, 2012, the thirteenth China International Wallpaper & Fabric Exhibition will continue to be held at the new venue of the China International Exhibition Center in Beijing.
