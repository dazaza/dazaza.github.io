The past is unbearable to look back on.

by gege on 2012-02-03 19:03:23

The past is too painful to look back on.

It's been over a year! Finally, it has come to an end here. I should be happy, but I just can't cheer up anymore. This war hurt everyone. And I am the main culprit.

I begged Jiang to let me go to another province. I said: "I will take care of myself there. I promise I won't contact my ex. If you still don't trust me, I'll sign a contract and work in a big factory. Anyway, you know, I don't want to stay at home for a moment. Under my earnest request, Jiang finally reluctantly allowed me to go to another province. On the 6th day of the New Year, I secretly bought a ticket to Zhejiang without telling my mom. After two days and one night of travel, I finally reached my destination. In fact, I really didn't plan to get involved with my ex again. But I don't know how he found out that I had returned to the city where we used to date. That day was very cold, and he was already waiting for me at the station. I originally had no intention of dealing with him. According to my plan, I was going to rest at the hotel for a day and then find a factory and move directly into the dormitory. Most of my friends there knew him, and those who didn't know him were married, so I didn't want to bother them. He seemed to see through everything. He said: "You don't have anyone to help you here. Look, come to my place first, and after I help you find a factory, you can move out. I won't force you to do anything. We can be friends." I'm really a fool who can't say no. But since he said we could be friends, I reluctantly agreed. After all, it's not easy to be alone outside. I just wanted to find a factory and leave as soon as possible, so I wouldn't feel guilty towards Jiang. However, I didn't realize that this had already hurt his feelings. In the last few days, I was looking for a factory with my ex and one of his friends. They are all picky guys, they don't enter big factories (usually big factories are very strict, follow fixed working hours, and need multiple approvals to take leave), they don't enter if the salary is not high enough, and they don't enter if they don't have freedom (they want to work when they want and leave when they want, as long as they can ship the goods). They also don't enter if the boss isn't fun. But I just wanted to enter a big factory and pass the time, staying far away from my ex, which was my promise to Jiang. Seeing my ex again already made me feel guilty towards him. But every time I looked at big factory recruitment, they would take it over. My ex's friend always said: "What's good about big factories? Let's all find a factory together. Anyway, you know how to operate machines, we can operate machines together, and Xiao Qian can handle the assembly, isn't that great?"

During this period, Jiang knew that I was together with my ex again. We had constant conflicts. Gradually, I got annoyed with Jiang. He didn't understand the situation here, and every time he called, he had endless things to say. Over time, I didn't even want to answer his calls. I thought that after some time, things would calm down.

Later, I didn't enter a big factory either. I followed my ex and his friend into a small factory to work together, and the three of us ate together. As a result, my relationship with my ex became more and more complicated, tangled and unclear. In front of his friends who knew me, I was still his girlfriend. No matter how I explained, it was useless. After all, I worked and ate with him. Even if I told a ghost, the ghost probably wouldn't believe it. Over time, I didn't feel the need to explain so much. My relationship with Jiang continued to be strained because I increasingly disliked his constant chatter in my ear, and I became colder towards him. He said I no longer loved him, so I went along with his thoughts. But he would still call or text me every now and then. I paid little attention.

By the time it was almost July, my mom kept calling me to go home. Mom said, "The Ghost Festival is coming. If you still have any conscience, come back and kowtow twice to your father. If you think you can face your father, then never come back. Disobedient thing, I've worried enough about you. I've done what I needed to do. You decide." Actually, without mom saying this, I had already planned to go back! First, to see dad. Second, during this time here, I really broke his heart. Although I was always cold to him on the phone, he stayed up late every night without sleeping, and I felt sorry for him. Thirdly, it's about my relationship with my ex. Before, it was unclear with his friends, now it's unclear with him too. It's better to just avoid it. I don't want mom to cry because of my ignorance.

Using the reason of going home to worship dad's grave, I came home a month early (on June 28th, the 27th day of the fifth lunar month). Although mom scolded me on the phone for being disobedient, she still loves me. When she knew I bought tickets to come back, she was extremely happy. And then there's Jiang, maybe his mood was the same as mom's! They've shed no small amount of tears for me during this time! Mom said, "Since you're back, don't go out again. I really don't feel safe when you're outside, especially when you're with your ex, I feel even less safe." Mom has never liked my ex, and I don't know why.

I thought that once I came back, everything would be peaceful. No more entanglement with my ex, no more worrying mom, no more making Jiang cry for me. But reality wasn't as simple as I imagined. Not long after I came back, my ex followed me home. He said he wanted to find me, tell my mom about our relationship, and make her agree to let us be together. I was completely against seeing him again. I didn't want to bring him home, fearing gossip could crush a person, and I didn't want others to point fingers behind my back. But I forgot that I had once told him the way to my house. Unexpectedly, he actually came. I thought since he didn't know which house was mine, I could just let him wait. Mom knew he was looking for me, and after being scolded, she made me bring him home. Then mom told him her thoughts. Anyway, the result was one sentence: "As long as I'm alive, I absolutely won't agree to you and Yan being together." Seeing that he couldn't persuade mom, he could only reluctantly nod. But he wouldn't let go so easily. He thought mom's disagreement was all because of Jiang's interference. He thought my unwillingness to be with him was also Jiang's fault. But the truth is that after I came back, I didn't reconcile with Jiang either. Although he kept asking me to wait for him at his place, I knew that because of my ex, he had left too many indelible shadows.

Once, my younger sister went out to play and somehow offended some small-time hooligans and got beaten up. Knowing that I couldn't ask anyone for help, having bad interpersonal relationships and unable to find a big brother to protect me, he actually said to me, as long as I agreed to be with him, he would help solve the problem. Haha, at that time, I just laughed and didn't say anything. Is this what love looks like? Apparently, breaking up with him was the right decision. Now I may not be able to do anything about those who bully my family, but it doesn't mean I will never be able to. Knowing I wouldn't agree, he stopped mentioning it and started a new plan. He planned to start with me and Jiang. As long as Jiang let me go, he thought there would be no obstacles for him. He knew my QQ account was logged in by Jiang, so he sent extremely ridiculous messages to Jiang, saying that I had slept with him, that I was his worn-out shoe, and if Jiang didn't mind, he could pick me up. He also said we had recently done it, and I might even be pregnant with his child. If Jiang still didn't mind, he could keep the name of father. I didn't expect him to be so shameless. How could I face Jiang after this? How could I fabricate such nonexistent stories to tarnish my reputation? Could this really get what he wanted? Or was it revenge? Jiang didn't tell me these things. I saw the saved chat records online. Every response from Jiang was: "It doesn't matter, I still love her; these things don't matter." Looking at these few short words, I couldn't hold back my tears streaming down. I really regretted believing his nonsense. The story didn't end here. Seeing that his actions only destroyed my reputation and damaged others' views of me without achieving the results he wanted, he decided to extort a large sum of money from Jiang. He told Jiang that I owed him a lot of money and asked Jiang to pay him back. If I hadn't seen the chat records in time and stopped Jiang, he might have succeeded.

After this, I no longer contacted Jiang. I felt humiliated in front of him. Regarding what my ex said, I couldn't explain, so just forget me. Keep the best memories of me in your heart, and I will be satisfied.

I wrote this story just to tell everyone to be cautious in making friends. I am a lesson in this story!
