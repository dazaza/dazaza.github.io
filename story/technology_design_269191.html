Han Geng and Li Xiaolu were revealed to have a romance between a young man and an older woman

by lujing9001 on 2009-11-21 15:48:40

The singer Han Geng, who became famous in the past two years after "exporting to domestic sales", not only skyrocketed in value and popularity, but also became entangled in gossip and scandals. Among his ever-changing romantic scandals, the longest-lasting and most attention-grabbing one is undoubtedly the rumor about him and Li Xiaolu. From 2007 to now, it has been spreading for more than two years, with many twists and turns. However, until now, neither of the two parties involved has made any "explanation" about this rumor. A recent midnight stakeout by a reporter finally "revealed" the veil of emotions surrounding Han Geng and Li Xiaolu, quietly unveiling another "age-gap romance" in the entertainment circle.

Li Xiaolu's affectionate actions exposed their relationship. On a certain evening, a reporter unexpectedly spotted Han Geng's car in front of a mall in Wangjing. Han Geng himself was driving, wearing a pair of black-rimmed glasses, looking scholarly. Sitting next to him was a young girl wearing a small hat, but her face was unclear due to the dark tint of the car windows. Around 7:30 PM, Han Geng, along with this "hat-wearing girl" and his male assistant, arrived at a hot pot restaurant on Xiaoyun Road. After parking, the three walked into the restaurant while chatting and laughing. At this point, the reporter recognized that the "hat-wearing girl" was none other than Li Xiaolu, Han Geng's "chief" rumored girlfriend. Li Xiaolu and Han Geng appeared very close, laughing and joking as they went upstairs into a private room to dine with a few friends waiting there.

Around 9:30 PM, Han Geng and his assistant happily left the restaurant. They drove the car to the front of the restaurant in the parking lot. Soon after, Li Xiaolu and two friends came out of the restaurant. Li Xiaolu looked cheerful, waving goodbye to her friends with a smile. Han Geng's assistant quickly got out of the car to open the door for Li Xiaolu, who sweetly got in and sat next to Han Geng.

Han Geng's car, along with two other cars driven by friends, formed a convoy heading towards Houhai. Along the way, the friends' cars couldn't keep up, so Han Geng got out of the car to smoke out of boredom. He then leaned into the passenger side window, seemingly having an intimate "whisper" with Li Xiaolu. Not long after, Han Geng straightened up, laughing heartily with a satisfied look on his face.

Upon arriving at Houhai, the convoy parked in a small alley. The group of friends strolled along the lakeside, but Han Geng and Li Xiaolu weren't interested in the moonlit scenery. Instead, they entered a bar with their "third-wheel" assistant to drink and chat. The reporter noticed that they chose a sofa by the window, with Han Geng and Li Xiaolu sitting back-to-back against the window together, and the assistant sitting across from them. The three chatted animatedly. Soon, the reporter saw Li Xiaolu leaning on Han Geng's chest, who gently stroked her. Witnessing this scene, the romantic relationship between Han Geng and Li Xiaolu was beyond doubt. Although they had admitted to being good friends, even the best opposite-sex friends wouldn't display such intimacy.

Ironclad evidence of the "age-gap romance": Returning to the apartment late at night around 11:30 PM, Han Geng, Li Xiaolu, and the assistant left the bar and met up with friends who had finished shopping. This time, Li Xiaolu took the wheel, with Han Geng sitting in the passenger seat, possibly because he had been drinking at the bar. Han Geng's car first dropped people off near World Trade Tianjie and Shuangjing, then headed towards Wangjing. Around 1 AM, the car entered the underground garage of a newly opened apartment complex. It seems that Li Xiaolu and Han Geng have already become inseparable lovers.

Rumors about Han Geng and Li Xiaolu have been circulating for years. Back in 2007, rumors about them started to surface. Many fans and netizens felt that they were quite "compatible," sparking rich imaginations. In mid-2008, Han Geng and Li Xiaolu both appeared at the Golden Eagle Award gala performance, arriving on the same flight but being rather evasive when facing the media. By the end of the same year, some netizens confidently revealed that Han Geng was indeed dating Li Xiaolu, and they had been together for half a year before breaking up for unknown reasons, with Han Geng still pursuing her relentlessly. Recently, reporters uncovered Han Geng and Li Xiaolu's "underground age-gap romance." These two stars, who have never publicly acknowledged their relationship and are seen as single, are now together again. It can be imagined that either their friendship deepened over time into love, or they rekindled an old flame. All of this awaits their official confirmation.
