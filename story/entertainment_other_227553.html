QQ Music Green Diamond VIP: "Pleasing" Your Life with Sound

In QQ2007 Beta3, the monthly subscription service of QQ Music "Music VIP" was officially upgraded to "QQ Music Green Diamond VIP," becoming a new member of the QQ Diamond VIP services. While retaining original privileges such as free music use on Qzone and free song requests via QQ, the Green Diamond VIP also added the privilege of downloading high-quality legitimate music through the QQ Music Player, providing users with a more considerate musical experience. The QQ2007 Beta3 QQ Music Green Diamond VIP badge display is as follows:

You can choose from various convenient methods to upgrade or renew your subscription to become a QQ Music Green Diamond VIP, including Q Coins, Q Points personal account, landline phone, bank card, VNET account, PHS mobile, and China Netcom broadband. Click here to activate the Green Diamond VIP service immediately.

Exclusive Privileges for QQ Music Green Diamond VIP:

1. Display of Prestigious Green Diamond Symbol

Upon activation of the Green Diamond VIP, you will be able to light up the Green Diamond VIP status symbol on QQ, showcasing prestige everywhere:

* Personal information display page when hovering over a QQ avatar;

* Personal profile page;

* Diamond VIP status display area in the chat window's QQ Show.

2. Free Download of High-Quality MP3s

Using the QQ Music Player, Green Diamond VIPs enjoy the exclusive privilege of free downloads of high-quality MP3s:

* Extensive library: all genres covered, including the latest hits, classic oldies, and international music;

* Fast download speeds: average download time for a song is just 30 seconds, 5-10 times faster than regular web downloads;

* Convenient and smooth downloads: no broken links, support for simultaneous downloads of multiple songs, and resumable downloads.

3. Free Use of Background Music on QQ Space

Green Diamond VIP users can freely use background music on their QQ Spaces:

* Diverse styles: cool, romantic, nostalgic - there's always suitable background music to create your unique space atmosphere;

* Up-to-date trends: the space music library stays synchronized with the QQ Music Player, ensuring access to the most popular music;

* Increased limit: regular users can set only 20 background tracks, but Green Diamond VIPs can increase this limit to 50 tracks.

4. HD Music Video Watching and Uploading

HD music video watching and uploading (regular users may experience using Q Beans):

* Unlimited viewing of HD music videos for Green Diamond VIPs; Lv3 and above levels can upload videos;

* Exclusive server: smooth video playback all the way!

* Green Diamond exclusive MV upload privilege (currently limited to Lv3 and above users)!

5. Upload Local Music to QQ Space

Upload local music to your QQ Space:

* Or maybe you're an amazing singer who wants to showcase your singing talents by placing your recordings on your space.

* By activating the Green Diamond VIP, you can upload your local computer music directly to your QQ Space. Try it out now!

Green Diamond VIPs can claim a free second-generation QQ Space music player:

7. Gifting Background Music to Friends (Lv2 and Above)

Lv2 and above level Green Diamond VIPs can gift a certain number of songs from the QQ Music Library each month to friends as background music for their spaces:

The recipient can receive the gifting message via Space Notes and use the song as background music.

The validity period is 15 days (starting from when the friend activates the song).

The higher the level, the more songs can be gifted each month, with Lv7 Green Diamond VIPs being able to gift up to 40 times per month.

What is the Green Diamond VIP?

The Green Diamond VIP is a tailor-made online music value-added service for QQ users who love music, including numerous music privileges such as free use of space background music, free MP3 downloads, and unlimited music sharing!

What are Green Diamond levels and experience points?

Tencent launched the Green Diamond VIP level system in April 2008. Continuous active behavior of Green Diamond VIPs can earn Green Diamond experience points, and your Green Diamond level is determined by the accumulated experience points.

How are Green Diamond levels calculated?

The Green Diamond level is determined by experience points, which are calculated as follows:

Green Diamond Experience Points E = Usage Experience Points E0 + Activation Experience Points E1 + Old User Base Experience Points E2
