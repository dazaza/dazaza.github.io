I. Goal Management: In September 2007, the school formulated the "Hongqi Primary School Plan for Creating a Learning-Oriented Campus Activity." The plan established overall goals, activity content, specific measures, and implementation steps based on the actual situation of our school. This plan was read at a school-wide meeting and received high recognition from all teachers. Each content has relevant responsible persons and completion time. The activity leadership group leader is Su Shuangquan, deputy leader Zhou Youfu, members Zhang Xin, Zhang Ju, Liao Darong, Li Hongli, and Wang Kejian; the activity working group leader is Zhou Youfu, member Li Hongli (responsible for "lifelong learning ability" training), Ding Xuequan (responsible for "professional expression ability" training), Wang Kejian (responsible for "classroom teaching ability" training), Liu Yuting (responsible for "curriculum development ability" training), Zhao Guoqiang (responsible for "information technology application ability" training), Chen Xiauju (responsible for "student education ability" training), Zou Yu (responsible for "educational research ability" training), Fan Hua (responsible for "characteristic creation ability" training). In order to make educational research a strong support for teaching and serve educational teaching, the school specially conducted a "Teacher and Small Topic Research" training session at a faculty meeting in late September to increase teachers' enthusiasm for participating in educational research. The training content included: the definition of small topics, sources of small topics, determination of small topic content, writing of small topic plans, research methods for small topics, research process for small topics, forms of small topic results, and writing of small topic conclusion reports. After the research class ended, Principal Su wrote an article titled "Lifting the Mysterious Veil of Elementary Comprehensive Practical Activities," guiding the school's teachers to find the connection points between disciplines and life resources, social resources in daily teaching, then develop related course examples, and use the concept of comprehensive practical activities to teach every subject well.
