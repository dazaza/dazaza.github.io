Journalist Investigation:

Tan Jing Did Sign with Asia Television

Yesterday afternoon, according to the online-provided "Tan Jing" information, the reporter located the Guangdong Asia Television Performing Arts Vocational College where she once studied. In the lobby of the administration building on the first floor, the reporter immediately spotted a photo of a girl named Tan Jing in the bulletin board, identical to the photos provided by netizens. The bulletin board data showed that Tan Jing was a graduate of the class of 2000, and after graduation, she became one of more than ten students who signed as artists with Hong Kong Asia Television.

Yesterday afternoon, the reporter interviewed Dean Xiong from the Student Affairs Office, but he said he was unclear about the situation of graduated students. Dean Liu of the college, after hearing the reporter explain the general course of events, initially stated that he needed to contact the Student Affairs Office before responding, but afterward refused to answer the reporter's calls.

Compared to the evasive attitude of the school authorities, several students interviewed by the reporter all said, "We know of a senior named Tan Jing; her photo was in the promotional booklet we received upon entering the school." A student surnamed Zhang said that when he entered the school, the administration used examples of several seniors signing with Asia Television after graduation for extensive promotion.

Sister's Narrative:

"At 7:00 AM on April 5th, on the fifth crossbeam between buildings (between the 12th and 13th floors) of Building T7, Lihao Tower, Phase Four of Dongfeng Plaza on Dongfeng East Road in Guangzhou, a female corpse wearing only pink underwear was found hanging on the crossbeam with limbs suspended and waist bent..." On April 6th, major media outlets in Guangzhou reported this news, claiming that "the woman was a prostitute, suspected to have been thrown from the 30th floor by three foreign men due to a dispute over payment." Subsequently, some media reported that the woman might have fallen to her death on her own. Upon the news spreading, there was an uproar online. Yesterday, several people who claimed to know the deceased appeared online, strongly refuting the claim that she was a prostitute, stating that the naked deceased woman was a model from Guangzhou.
