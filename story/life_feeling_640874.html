Beautiful girls and ordinary girls

by zhouzhou56 on 2011-12-22 16:22:45

Beautiful girls have pretty faces and good figures. They are pursued by many boys, their skies are always clear, and everyone thinks they are happy.

Ordinary girls have ordinary faces and ordinary figures. Few boys pay attention to them. Looking at everything around them, sometimes they also complain about the injustice of life.

Around beautiful girls, there are always many extraordinary boys, or at least they feel very excellent. With such superior conditions, beautiful girls naturally raise their standards. The image of a boyfriend or lover they envision is undoubtedly flawless. They keep choosing; they need romantic happiness and wait for the arrival of their prince on a white horse!

Ordinary girls live like ordinary people. Often, the boys beside them treat her as a friend but frequently ignore the fact that she is a girl. They also imagine their prince on a white horse, but the difference is that she is an ordinary person. Ordinary girls can tolerate some flaws in their boyfriends; they only need unchanging love for this lifetime.

Beautiful girls often don't know which one to choose, and they often pray to the moon for guidance from the gods.

Ordinary girls believe in their own choices and in the choices of their lifetime.

Beautiful girls have too many opportunities; they like to play with opportunities, enjoy fleeting romance to show how different they are. They often tell their suitors: "You're just an ordinary one among them."

Ordinary girls cherish every opportunity with all their heart, and they use their hearts to maintain this relationship for a long time. They will tell him with their eyes and gaze: "This is our choice for life."

The admirers of beautiful girls consider two questions every day: "Are there more rivals?" and "How can I win?" Usually, the answer to the first question is yes, and the second is no.

The admirers of ordinary girls will occasionally think: if she agrees with me, we'll go cycling around the island.

There is no essential difference between the boyfriend of a beautiful girl and her admirers. Although it is full of pride to embrace her, he has to plan the next day's battle when lying in bed because the competition is too fierce. This kind of life usually continues until the moment she puts on her wedding dress.

The boyfriend of an ordinary girl may not have that pride, but he feels happy in his heart. He quietly tells himself: life needs calmness, and I am looking for a partner to share hardships with, not something to show off.

The love of beautiful girls is full of romance, and they have never considered what lasting love is.

The love of ordinary girls may be unremarkable, but in their hearts, they yearn for romance and understand the value of lasting love.

The husbands of beautiful girls are all outstanding, and beside these outstanding husbands, there are many girls who are even prettier and younger than the beautiful girls. Thus, beautiful girls start the campaign to defend their love because they know that what attracted their husband initially can also attract other girls. Only then do beautiful girls begin to ask themselves: "What is love?"

The husbands of ordinary girls may be very outstanding, and although there are also outstanding girls around him, ordinary girls know: among so many choices, the husband who ultimately chose her will not betray her because he understands that love is not a beautiful appearance. Ordinary girls tell themselves: "I now understand the meaning of love."

The family of beautiful girls is not stable. The beautiful appearance of beautiful girls gradually ages, and they have many questions in their hearts: what did I pursue initially, and what did I gain.

The family of ordinary girls is very happy, and they often hear their children who have just become sensible whispering in their ears: "Mom, you are the prettiest," and thus the ordinary girl smiles.

Time flies, and there is not much difference between beautiful girls and ordinary girls. Time mercilessly covers up the once-beautiful appearance of beautiful girls. Perhaps beautiful girls finally find the answer: "Is this what love is?"

Ordinary girls remain ordinary, and in recalling the past with an ordinary heart, they tell others when surrounded by grandchildren: "I didn't miss out on precious love."

If you are a beautiful girl, then please forgive me for saying that beauty does not equal everything. Heaven's arrangement is just a random choice, and ultimate happiness still requires your own careful choice and effort. Don't let your looks mislead you. Life needs beauty, but beauty is not life.

If you are an ordinary girl, then please hold your head high because the world is not just for beautiful girls. People who understand love do not compromise their love because of a beautiful appearance. Believe in yourself and in love, cherish it carefully, and love sincerely. Life is originally wonderful.

If you fall in love with a beautiful girl, then usually you have to undergo arduous struggles. Whether successful or not, you must say confidently to yourself: "The important thing is not the result but the experience." If you succeed, you should have long-term plans to maintain stability. If you fail, you should understand that getting into Tsinghua University is difficult. I just want to tell you: you may like the beautiful girl's appearance and decide to move forward, but they also need real love. Deceiving someone's feelings to achieve bragging rights will eventually cost you the right to love.

If you fall in love with an ordinary girl, then please pursue her sincerely and love sincerely. You will find that their hearts are so lovely, and their feelings are so delicate. You must gather the courage to tell others: "Between love and beauty, I chose love." You must believe that sincere love is eternal!
