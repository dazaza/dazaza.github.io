Leveling up by fighting monsters is an eternal part of RPG games worldwide, an irreplaceable joy. PVP Arena Let’s Have More Blood

My friend, The Legend will soon open various different PVP battles. This is a man's game, a game for the masses. The Legend, tailored to players' various combat desires, divides into individual matches and team matches.

Image 2

1. Individual Competition

Registration Method: At any time, click on the icon on the mini-map to register.

Battle Rules: The system automatically allocates players based on segments, matching opponents after which an arena entry instruction is sent.

You have 30 seconds to enter the arena, otherwise it's considered a forfeit. After entering the battlefield, there is a 30-second preparation time (overlapping with the previous 30 seconds), during which movement is not allowed. No props or auto-recovery items can be used in the battle scene, but various pill effects can take effect.

Judging Victory: After the battle starts, if the opponent is knocked down within 5 minutes, victory is declared. If the time runs out and neither side knocks down the other, it's a draw. If you leave the battlefield after the battle starts, it's a loss. If the opponent doesn't enter the battlefield, it's a draw. Note: Upon entering the arena, there will be a 30-second immobility period!

Event Rewards: Monthly rewards are given based on rankings. If the server hasn't been open for a full month, it will be merged into the next month's calculation.

Image 3

2. Battlefield Flag Capture

Event Time: Daily 10:00-22:00

Opens once every hour. Players reaching level 30 can participate, entering through the arena guardian.

In the battlefield, both red and blue sides each have a single military flag. The side whose flags are all knocked down loses, the other side wins.

Judging Victory: If one side's all flags are knocked down, that side loses. If the time ends and the remaining flag quantities are the same, the side with more remaining total flag health wins.

Event Rewards

Flag capture in the battlefield can earn rich rewards, specifically:

Battlefield Medals: Can exchange for blue quality equipment, and even purple quality weapons!

Military Merit Badges: Can exchange for honorable battlefield treasure boxes.

Money Treasure Box: Obtain a large amount of money.

Experience Treasure Box: Obtain a large amount of experience.

Prop Treasure Box: Obtain props.

Equipment Treasure Box: Obtain 50-level blue quality equipment.
