I. Introduction to the components

1. This product mainly contains various chemical components such as amino (NH2) and hydroxyl free radicals (OH), which can quickly decompose formaldehyde in boards.

2. This product is non-toxic, and after reacting with toxic gases such as formaldehyde, it will not cause secondary pollution to the environment.

II. Reaction principle

The special formaldehyde remover is composed of three components with different functions: the first component plays a penetrating role, allowing the formaldehyde remover to quickly penetrate into various materials; the second component surrounds the formaldehyde molecules, playing an adsorption capture role; the third component can play a polymerization elimination role. This component is the main component of the special formaldehyde remover, which actively adsorbs and captures formaldehyde molecules and produces a polymerization reaction, thereby achieving the purpose of removing formaldehyde. The special formaldehyde remover has a long-lasting effect and can remain effective for a long time in artificial boards.

III. Technical parameters

This product appears as a colorless liquid / pH value is 7+/-0.5 (at 25℃) / can be diluted with water arbitrarily, with good dilution stability / penetration time 0.5h, drying time 2-8h / stable physicochemical properties, non-toxic, harmless, and non-corrosive.

IV. Application instructions

This product can strongly penetrate into the interior of artificial boards such as plywood and particleboard, efficiently adsorbing and polymerizing free formaldehyde in the boards, forming stable and environmentally friendly high molecular polymers, with an effectiveness rate reaching 98%. Spray or brush the liquid on both sides and all cross-sections of wooden materials, with a usage amount of about 20-30㎡/L.

V. Precautions

1. Strictly prohibited from use on fiber products, leather products, and paper products such as carpets, curtains, genuine leather sofas, calligraphy and paintings, to avoid affecting the surface fiber structure and softness of these items due to its strong adhesion.

2. Strictly prohibit contact with eyes. If accidentally splashed into the eyes, rinse immediately with clean water.

3. When spraying with a spray gun, there will be significant mist, so ventilation should be ensured.

4. Strictly prohibited from mixing with other chemical agents.

VI. Storage methods

1. Packaged in plastic containers, store in a shaded place at room temperature, strictly avoid exposure to direct sunlight;

2. Below 35℃, the storage period can reach 24 months;

3. This product is non-hazardous and can be stored and transported as a general agent.
