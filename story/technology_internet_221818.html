CN domain names are gaining popularity, registrar sparks 1 yuan exchange offer

by now on 2009-08-12 15:12:49

Since the Internet has been developing dramatically in the global economy, domain names have been playing an important role in this economic development. There is a continuous flow of information about domain name disputes, buying and selling of domain names, domain name squatting, and domain name investment. Nowadays, domain names are no longer limited to the Internet industry; they have expanded into all industries, and more and more people are becoming aware of the potential value of domain names.

In recent years, there have been countless examples of how a good domain name can affect the survival and development of a company. In order to take control of their own development, both companies and individuals have launched campaigns to protect their domain name brands. Many companies not only hold one domain name but would also like to register all domain names related to their business so that they do not fall into others' hands and affect future development. Under this trend, China's leading domain registrar, NowCN (www.now.cn), recently launched a "1 Yuan Upgrade to Premium Domain" brand protection campaign. The company's representative stated that many years ago, when people talked about domain names, most of them did not know what they were, but now it is different. People's understanding of domain names has improved because they have seen many disputes over domain names, big and small, which made them realize the importance of domain names, thus naturally enhancing their awareness of protection.

According to reports, the "1 Yuan Upgrade to Premium Domain" event held by NowCN aims to provide users with an opportunity to protect their online brands. During the event, as long as users purchase products from NowCN, they can get a CN domain name by just adding 1 Yuan. The event will bring great opportunities to many people who need domain names for developing the Internet economy and will also stimulate enterprises' enthusiasm for protecting domain names.

With the development of the Internet, domain names are no longer just network addresses; they also play important roles such as "global trademarks," "corporate brands," and "network foundations." This has attracted more and more people to enter the domain name investment field. With the booming development of the Internet economy, the wave of domain name protection, investment, and application will penetrate all areas of society. From this perspective, the protection of domain names by enterprises or individuals will be a long journey.
