Wanbao Garden is an estate with a classical garden architectural style. It is located in the main park of the national AAAA-level scenic area - Anhui Tian Tangzhai National Forest Park. It is 100 meters away from the geological park stone tablet to the southeast, across the bridge.

Wanbao Garden is the garden within Tian Tangzhai National Forest Park, the leading enterprise of agricultural industrialization in Lu'an City, and the ecological tourism project aided by Beijing's Agriculture Commission. It is also the main scene of the movie "Weekend Village Official" filmed by Bayi Film Studio.

Inside Wanbao Garden: three sides along the river, pavilions and terraces, doors and buildings are magnificent, small bridges and winding paths, ancient roads and corridors, carved beams and painted rafters, all exuding an ancient charm.

There are rare flowers and strange grasses, butterflies dancing and spreading fragrance, green shade covering, red jujubes and yellow persimmons, potted landscapes and sculptures, ancient trees intertwining branches, rippling clear water, fish leaping, birds singing, tea aroma wafting, and harmonious scenery.

All scenes in Wanbao Garden are beautiful, with dining, accommodation, entertainment, and transportation all integrated into a green environment. Our garden not only has picturesque scenery but also a cluster of home-style villa buildings, arranged neatly and elegantly. Inside each building, there are three bedrooms and one living room, high-end guest rooms, business suites, meeting rooms of various sizes, card rooms, exhibition halls, indoor and outdoor dance halls, restaurants, and fishing platforms. The cuisine here has its unique characteristics.

Wanbao Garden owns nearly ten thousand acres of wild tea primitive base, 600 acres of natural organic tea and white tea base, more than 300 acres of large-leaf tea and small-leaf fragrant tea base, over 5,000 acres of wild mountain tea oil base, and 1,000 acres of natural pickles raw material base. In addition, through the company plus farmer joint operation model, they traditionally raise over 1,000 black country pigs, with an annual output of over 800 heads.

The farmstead under the garden consists of six departments and one factory and one garden, namely the "Natural Green Food Factory" and the "Potted Landscape Garden". You can eat a variety of precious wild flavors in Wanbao Garden. We have a wide range of self-collected and self-made mountain vegetables, wild mountain tea oil, wild fruits, and natural pickles, which are valuable gifts for respecting elders and giving to friends and relatives.

We raise our own black country pigs, black goats, black earth dogs, local geese, local ducks, local eggs, as well as wild river fish, wild eels, and wild crabs. These ingredients are used to prepare excellent dishes and medicinal diets with unmatched deliciousness.

Please come to Wanbao Garden to enjoy the long chopstick hanging pot feast, drink homemade small hanging wine, and taste the farmhouse banquet.

In the sound of firecrackers, we frequently toast our guests, who then enjoy folk songs and dances happily.

Main business: Tian Tangzhai Hotel, Tian Tangzhai Inn, Tian Tangzhai specialties
