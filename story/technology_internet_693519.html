For women who love beauty, care is the most important.

by king5c5t on 2012-03-06 16:24:32

Are you still troubled by the pimples on your face? Are you still worried about your dull and dark skin? Don't worry, we will give you some little secrets to throw troubles and worries away.

Skin care is very important for women. So female friends are always trying to take care of their skin. In fact, skin care is not just simply using skin care products. Skin care should also start from the details in life. Below, we will introduce some daily skin care knowledge for everyone. Let's learn together!

1. Do not wipe your face with a towel.

After washing your face, you should use a towel or tissue to dry the water. This method causes much less pulling and damage to the skin than wiping it with a towel.

2. Do not pat toner with your hands.

Toner must be used with cotton pads, wiped from bottom to top so that the skin can really absorb the moisture. Especially the pore direction of the nose is opposite to the face, so the wiping direction is also opposite, from top to bottom.

3. Do not apply a mask while doing housework.

The best posture for applying a mask is lying down. Because when lying down, the body is in a relaxed state, the facial muscles are not tense, which helps the pores on the facial skin to fully open and absorb the nutrients of the mask.

4. Use sunscreen all year round.

All year round, no matter spring, summer, autumn, winter, any weather, including cloudy, sunny, rainy, snowy days, sunscreen should be used.

5. Do not do deep exfoliation frequently.

The frequency of exfoliation is different. Oily skin can do it once a week; mixed skin, it is recommended to remove the keratin in different areas separately. The T-zone with more oil can be done once a week, and the dry V-zone can be done every three weeks; dry skin due to dryness and slower keratin metabolism can be done at most once a month; sensitive skin is suggested not to exfoliate.

6. If you make up, please definitely remove makeup.

No matter how tired you are from work or how late it is. However, if you don't wear makeup, there is absolutely no need to remove makeup. For sunscreen, SPF 20 times or more requires makeup removal, and SPF 20 times or less can be completely handled with facial cleanser.

7. Whitening products can only lighten spots.

Any beauty care, even fruit acid peeling, can only lighten spots, but cannot completely remove them. Therefore, girls should still spend money on concealer or sunscreen.

8. Use a towel to remove blackheads, whiteheads, and keratin.

After taking a bath, under the condition that the keratin layer is wet and soft, take advantage of the steam in the bathroom that has not yet dissipated and is still warm. You can use medical gauze, but the denser the fabric, the better. Gently massage in circles on areas with blackheads and where keratin needs to be removed. This will have the effect of removing blackheads and keratin. Using a towel instead of hands for daily face washing will also have a keratin-removal effect, but people with dry or sensitive skin should be cautious.

9. Use sugar for massage, keratin removal, and care.

Wet your face in advance, lather the facial cleanser in your palm, put in sugar (the particles should not be too large, otherwise it may scratch the skin), or finely ground mung bean powder, apply it on areas with blackheads and keratin, and slowly massage in circles. The benefit of this is that it can achieve the effect of keratin removal, and as the sugar gradually melts, it will also have a nourishing effect on the skin.

10. Use frozen towels to shrink pores.

Put a completely dry towel into a sealed zipper bag and then put it in the refrigerator. Before putting on makeup in the morning, take it out and place it on your face, which can help shrink pores. The towel must not have any moisture, otherwise, it will freeze and injure the skin.

11. Massage for firming the skin and reducing swelling.

Drainage massage method: use the pads of your index and middle fingers, gently press from the sides of the nostrils, cheeks to the ears, stay for about 5-10 seconds each; then place both hands on both cheeks extending to the sides of the ears and stay for 5-10 seconds. Here are also the Tinghui acupoint, Jiaochexue acupoint, and Daying acupoint mentioned in Traditional Chinese Medicine, which can help eliminate excess fat. Below the ear is the position of lymph nodes responsible for metabolism. With the help of hand warmth, it helps lymph metabolize water and waste. Make a fist with your hand and slide it over the cheek, through massage making the protruding masseter muscle become smoother, and also lift the skin that has become loose due to aging.

12. Use watermelon for post-sunburn repair.

Use watermelon rind to cool down the skin. The function of watermelon rind is similar to aloe vera, which can help soothe the skin after sun exposure.

The above twelve points are common methods for skin care. Do you now understand, beautiful ladies who love beauty? If you want to take care of your skin, you must start from the details of life. Only in this way can you have good skin. So, what are you waiting for, beautiful ladies?
