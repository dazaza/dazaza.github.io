Lingshan Yundi Lixing Hot Spring

by lji8jo34i74 on 2011-12-29 15:14:13

The outdoor landscape area of the first phase of Ling Mountain Yuanyi Lixing Hot Spring covers an area of 58,000 square meters, with a total of 72 characteristic soaking pools and hot spring rooms, divided into six major features: dynamic hot springs area, special hot springs area, dense forest hot springs area, business lake view hot springs area, outdoor swimming pool and children's water play area. Its hot spring originates from deep within the earth's crustal fault, transferred to the surface after decades of evolution and infiltration through underground thermal magma. The bottom temperature of the hot spring well is 70℃, and the outlet temperature is 50℃. The pH of the spring water is 7.49. The minerals, trace elements, and special chemical components contained in the hot spring not only have therapeutic effects on various diseases but also provide health care, beauty, skin care, and recuperation benefits. Surrounded by lush green forests while soaking in the hot spring, visitors face the vast Taihu Lake, naturally fostering a sense of harmony between humans and nature.

Ling Mountain Yuanyi Lixing Hot Spring Resort Hotel is an Asia-class leisure health resort created by Yuanyi Group with an investment of 3 billion yuan. Located on the west side of Ling Mountain Scenic Area in Wuxi Taihu National Holiday Resort, it leans against Maji Mountain and overlooks the expansive scenery of Taihu Lake covering 36,000 hectares. It occupies an area of 100 mu (about 16.6 acres), with a total construction area of 25,000 square meters. The first phase covers 15,000 square meters, and the second phase hotel covers 10,000 square meters, featuring diverse theme hot spring rooms and 72 theme hot spring pools. It is managed by the well-known domestic professional management team, Licheng Group.

The first phase of the club includes functional areas such as the largest-scale Noble Ladies Club and Gentlemen Club in Asia, Li SPA, Health Center, Imperial Foot Palace, Lifu Ligia, Sinology Hall, Yoga Hall, and premium luxury guest rooms; the second phase offers 135 premium specialty luxury guest rooms and hotel ancillary facilities. By integrating hot spring culture, Zen culture, health culture, and leisure resort culture, Ling Mountain Yuanyi Lixing Hot Spring creates a harmonious atmosphere between humans and nature, reflecting its high-end positioning of "Ultimate Life, Made in China."

With winter approaching, soaking in hot springs has become a popular choice for many people to relax during their "New Year's Day" holidays. For residents in the Yangtze River Delta region, there are quite a few nearby options for hot springs: Drunken Hot Spring in Huangshan, Tangfeng in Wuyi, and Tangshan in Nanjing, among others. However, due to high customer concentration, the experience reputation of some hot spring clubs has declined. Now, another star-level member has joined the "hot spring circle" in the Yangtze River Delta region - Wuxi Ling Mountain Yuanyi Lixing Hot Spring. In August, this place just hosted the contestants of the 61st Miss World China Final, where the beauties took swimsuit photo shoots.
