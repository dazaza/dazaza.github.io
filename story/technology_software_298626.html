[Ding Lei's Brief Professional Experience]

Born in Ningbo in October 1971, he also introduced himself as a native of Fenghua, Zhejiang in some public forums.

Graduated in 1993 from Chengdu University of Electronic Science and Technology, majoring in wireless microwave communication, and according to his own statement, he also concurrently studied computer science;

Worked at Ningbo Telecom Bureau from 1993 to May 1995. He stated that it was precisely this "state-owned enterprise work model" tending towards idling that gave him the time and opportunity to delve into programming;

Worked at Sebyse Guangzhou Branch from May 1995 to May 1996. This was a domestic institution of a foreign company, and the good working environment gave him the chance to get deeply involved in the network, thus enabling him to make various kinds of netizens;

Served as CEO of NetEase from May 1997 to March 2000. He stated that his role as CEO was due to his role as one of the main founders and the developmental mission, although he realized that he was not suitable for such high-level management work and had little interest in it. During this period, he also attempted various forms of learning to improve his managerial qualities;

On July 1, 2000, facilitated the successful listing of the company on the US NASDAQ;

Currently serves as Co-CTO of NetEase. Regarding his active choice of this position, he believes it is based on considerations for the long-term development of the enterprise.
