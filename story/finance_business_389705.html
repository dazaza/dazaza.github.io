Let's Explore Traditional Chinese Medicine (TCM) Beauty Written by the Taste of Whitening in Intensive Care

Today, we change our taste and return to our traditional treasure - TCM adjustment. TCM not only focuses on treating diseases and saving lives but also excels in beauty adjustments. Throughout history, countless TCM practitioners have spent their entire lives devising methods to enhance the beauty of court ladies, leaving behind rich experiences that are definitely worth our attention!

So, let's follow the editor together into the world of TCM beauty.

"TCM Beauty" has deep roots in Chinese culture. Through centuries of people's practice in beauty, these time-tested folk beauty and health methods have gathered infinite Eastern wisdom.

Whether in the ancient imperial courts or modern cities, women have always been thoughtful about how to make themselves more beautiful.

And "TCM Beauty" with its comprehensive internal and external adjustments, and the concept of achieving health and beauty from within, has become increasingly admired by those who value beauty and health.

However, now TCM beauty products come in various types. What kind of method will be effective for oneself? What kind of product will benefit oneself? How can one find the right product through the correct method? I think this is what everyone cares about the most, and it's also a headache-inducing problem.

For this reason, I visited supermarkets, malls, and beauty salons. Later, I found an issue: Supermarkets and malls mostly don't sell TCM products, while some beauty salons do use them. When asking merchants, without exception, they all introduced the advantages of their products, telling me how effective they are after use and how satisfied customers are. But these are ultimately just the merchants' statements; it's hard to find actual users to understand unless your friends around you are using them. I'm not saying their products are bad; every product that survives in the market must have a certain customer base. Personally, I still value our traditional TCM beauty products more.

Due to work reasons, I often surf online, specifically at Guangzhou VIP Information Technology Co., Ltd., so I decided to use the internet to search. Taobao is China's largest shopping and trading platform, and I usually buy clothes and various international brand cosmetics there. However, I haven't yet looked online for our own TCM beauty-related items. After searching, I found quite a few interesting options. I decided to ask a shop with relatively high credibility.

I clicked into a TCM beauty store named "Green Forest." Honestly, what initially attracted me was their product images featuring ancient beauties—beauty knows no gender boundaries! After reading their introduction, it turned out they were from a family of TCM practitioners, and the products in their small store were uniquely formulated by their elderly TCM doctor. This piqued my curiosity—I wondered if they could also formulate a secret recipe for me to instantly become a youthful and invincible beauty girl.

After looking at several product introductions, I discovered that the old TCM doctor in this store, around 50 years old, looked like he was only 35 in his photos. That's impressive, making me admire and envy him. I thought if I look like this at 50, it would be great.

Thinking about this, I decided to consult the shopkeeper as a customer to see how she introduces her products.

Editor: Shopkeeper, hello, are all your products pure TCM?

Shopkeeper: Hello, yes, they are all pure TCM. Do you need anything?

Editor: I want to learn first.

Shopkeeper: Sure, no problem. Which aspect do you want to know about?

Editor: Your products, according to the description, are formulated by an old TCM doctor. How long does it take to see results?

Shopkeeper: Yes, they are formulated by an old TCM doctor. TCM emphasizes internal and external adjustment. It's different from general beauty products containing chemicals or hormones, which may show immediate effects, but TCM treats both symptoms and root causes, fundamentally solving the problems. Our product course lasts a month, and the speed of effectiveness varies from person to person. Some people see results after one course, while others need two courses or more to notice improvements. Generally, the longer you use it, the better the results.

Editor: Are there any customers who say they didn't see any effect?

Shopkeeper: Yes, some customers are impatient and stop using it after a few tries, claiming it doesn't work. Our products are not miracle drugs, so it's impossible to achieve satisfactory results after just a few uses. For example, some customers have very yellowish skin but are still young without other ailments. These customers generally suffer from qi and blood deficiency, so we use a dual-supplement method of qi and blood, combined with internal and external adjustments. Such adjustments usually take at least two months. Some customers give up halfway, then claim the product doesn't work. As long as they persist, there will be results.

Editor: It seems that using TCM methods for beauty cannot be rushed.

Shopkeeper: Yes, because each person's situation is different, the time required for adjustments will vary. As long as customers can persist, their skin will significantly improve compared to before.

Editor: Alright, thank you, Shopkeeper. I've learned a bit. If I need something next time, I'll come and buy. Wishing you booming business!

Shopkeeper: Okay, no need to be polite, feel free to visit anytime.

The Editor believes that the quality of a product depends on its reputation. The store's credibility is pretty good, with many repeat customers. The Editor decided to investigate the opinions of their customers to verify the truth. Among numerous online buyers, I chose one with a Yellow Diamond credit rating. After explaining my intentions, she was happy to chat with me. Below is the main part of our conversation (to protect her privacy, I'll refer to her as Xiao A).

Editor: How long have you been using the TCM beauty products from this store?

Xiao A: About eight months ago, due to prolonged sleepless nights, my skin was in very bad condition. My complexion was dull, dry, with fine lines, and even spots appeared. Since I have sensitive skin, I dared not use random spot-removal products. I was looking for a mild TCM spot-removal product and accidentally discovered this store. So, I started trying it out then. Seeing good results, I continued using it.

Editor: What products did you use?

Xiao A: The shopkeeper recommended a whitening and spot-removal jade complexion pill and a full-effect whitening and spot-removal mask, along with a moisturizing and anti-wrinkle mask.

Editor: Did it really work?

Xiao A: Yes, after one month of use, my skin tone became much more even, my face wasn't as dry, the spots faded slightly, and my skin lightened a bit. Although it wasn't extremely white, it was no longer as sallow as before.

Editor: How is your skin now?

Xiao A: Now, my skin has truly become whiter and softer, the spots have faded significantly, almost unnoticeable. With foundation applied, they're completely invisible. The fine lines have also become much shallower. My skin looks well-hydrated and radiant, and when I wake up in the morning, my skin still appears rosy. Honestly, I'm very satisfied with the current results. Moreover, I've recommended some friends to use it, and they also say the effects are great.

Editor: Have you experienced any side effects?

Xiao A: No, since the shopkeeper asked about my physical condition before recommending the products, I didn't notice any abnormalities. However, due to the slow nature of TCM, it takes a longer time.

Editor: Are you still using it now?

Xiao A: Yes, I've been taking the whitening and beauty jade complexion pills for six months. I've consistently used the full-effect mask and the moisturizing and anti-wrinkle mask twice a week, for eight months now. The shopkeeper suggests I take the jade complexion pills again every two months to prevent aging since women fear aging the most, haha.

Editor: It seems our long-standing beauty treasure is really excellent.

Xiao A: Yes, you can try it out and talk to the shopkeeper.
