These two days, Yi Shui goes online to submit her resume every day.

by cq3118wpeq on 2009-12-07 13:23:22

Yishui was reminded of the lyrics of "Hua Du Kai Xin": If I hadn't encountered so many turns, how could I have come to your side? Looking back now, every step of chaos originally concealed a direction. Once, I thought I couldn't bear another tear's weight. Today, I finally know that tears can also brew fragrance. Yishui really liked these words: turns, side, chaos, direction, bear, tears, fragrance. She felt that these were what she wanted to express, to Zhao Feng, to the past. No matter how many difficulties and hardships the road with Zhao Feng would bring, she wouldn't give up or be afraid. With Zhao Feng's love and her love for Zhao Feng, when they reached the last day of the world, she wanted to say to Zhao Feng: thank you... Yishui tightened her clothes and said in her heart: winter was going to pass...

These two days, Yishui went online to submit resumes every day, sometimes two or three, sometimes four or five, and sometimes none at all. Yishui didn't know what she wanted to do. She had always been doing work as a manager assistant. Now, looking at so many positions, Yishui felt lost. Was she going to take a job just for the sake of working? Thinking about her previous stubbornness, Yishui suddenly had a sense of fear towards work. She needed a lot of polishing...

After lunch, Yishui prepared to go to the supermarket to buy some things. The elevator was going up from below, and Yishui was waiting, watching the red numbers rise one by one. Finally, the elevator stopped, and the door opened. Just as Yishui was about to step inside, she pulled her foot back.

Zhao Feng in the elevator also widened his eyes. As the elevator doors slowly closed, Zhao Feng quickly blocked them, took a step out, and hugged Yishui tightly. The elevator doors closed behind him.

Zhao Feng and Yishui hugged tightly for a long time. "Girl, when did you come back?" Zhao Feng asked gently while looking at Yishui.

"Last week..." Yishui answered very softly. When she saw Zhao Feng appear behind the elevator door, she was so surprised that she momentarily forgot who this person was.

"Stupid girl..." Zhao Feng lightly kissed Yishui's forehead, where his stubble lightly scratched her.

Yishui raised her hand and gently touched the stubble on Zhao Feng's chin. It seemed that Zhao Feng hadn't shaved for a few days, making him look more haggard and weathered. Yishui thought he looked like what her friends usually described - a man! Zhao Feng lowered his head and kissed Yishui's fingers, and Yishui saw a particularly clear image of herself in Zhao Feng's eyes.

When they got home, Zhao Feng first changed his clothes, washed his face, and shaved. When he came out of the bathroom again, he was once again the deep, steady, powerful yet elegant Zhao Feng.

The two sat on the sofa, with Yishui gently leaning on Zhao Feng's shoulder, and one of her hands tightly held by Zhao Feng. This feeling was truly wonderful, and Yishui felt very happy. "Girl, I missed you..." Zhao Feng said while pinching Yishui's hand.
