Will "Qifan Qunxiong Zhu Lu" produce just as many "Xiahou experts"? Let's wait and see!

In the first round of the open selection, Sima Yi's playstyle was roughly divided into two categories. One was the "Big Pancake Flow" home-destroying Sima Yi, fully equipped with "amulets" that added mana and health, focusing on skill-based clearing of minions and burning towers. The other category was the "magic damage," "Little Mengde" instant-kill Sima Yi, emphasizing personal operations. Relying on the early advantage of farming minions, they purchased expensive "magic damage" items or "Mengde's Scroll," then used small skills like C and E to instantly kill opponents once they had magic damage in hand.
