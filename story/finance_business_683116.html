Summer Travel Skin Care: 5 Reminders for Whitening and Sun Protection

by cshelive9520 on 2012-02-29 11:51:54

1. Cleansing Products

Cleansing products are one of the essential items we need when traveling. Keeping the skin clean is the first step in skincare, and toner plays a significant role. Nowadays, there are more and more cleansing products available on the market. Besides the basic makeup removal and face washing products, those with skincare and cleansing functions can achieve deep cleaning effects.

During the Dragon Boat Festival travels, you may encounter various dusts and makeup. After a day, your face accumulates a thick layer of dirt, which requires thorough cleaning to allow your skin to breathe smoothly.

Choosing a two-in-one cleansing and makeup removal product that can quickly and thoroughly remove makeup and dirt from the skin not only cares for and moisturizes the skin but also keeps it fresh and comfortable. It saves space in your bag and is the best choice for travel.

2. Whitening Sunscreen Products

Whitening sunscreen is the most important thing that cannot be overlooked. When playing outside, if you don't do a good job of sun protection and whitening, being exposed to the sun for several hours continuously can easily make you tan, and in severe cases, it can even burn your skin.

3. Hydrating Moisturizing Products

During the trip, we need to hydrate and moisturize our skin at any time. The hot weather accelerates the evaporation of skin moisture. Therefore, when choosing skincare products, choose those with higher water content. You should carry a moisturizing cream that can quickly restore hydration to dry skin.
