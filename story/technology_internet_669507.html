A Novice's Website Building Insights: Virtual Hosting

First, let me introduce myself. I've only been doing websites for a few days, so I'm a standard super novice. Here, I'll briefly discuss my views on the future development of personal webmasters over the past few days and throw out the topic of cooperation with internet cafes for discussion with everyone.

Firstly, the development of a personal webmaster must have a clear self-positioning. This positioning must be based on a comprehensive analysis of one's own identity and the larger environmental factors. Taking the creation of Chongqing Life Online as a topic, we will analyze it step by step. Before, I always regarded the computer as a network entertainment tool. Although I often saw various website owners making money, which made me itchy, I knew I wasn't cut out for it and had no knowledge of technology. Later, because a friend who was an agent for Wanwang gave me space and domain names, and after practical efforts ended in failure, I temporarily shifted my focus to the Internet, treating it as entertainment. As I gradually learned more, I became somewhat obsessed and started thinking about what kind of website I should build, or what we usually call positioning. Since I don't have much education or skills, building a specialized site is definitely not feasible. Because I've always been in advertising, from a salesperson to a sales director, and since it was a media agency, my understanding of the industry was only superficial, making it difficult to build an industry-specific site. The Internet has also been rejected by many predecessors. I kept thinking, what kind of website should I build? At this point, some experiences from my advertising business told me that if you don't have enough capital (not just financial), it's still best to start with what's around you. So, I told myself, everything should start from what's around me.

Generally speaking, we live in an economic society. When we haven't reached a certain level, most of us, like me, whether building a website or starting a business, are mostly for making money. The positioning here is to clarify one issue: the goal of positioning and the direction it brings. Simply put, whose money do you plan to make, and how do you get them to voluntarily open their wallets? That's the carrier for making money. I told myself, I need to start from what's around me. Since I know nothing about making money online, I also know little about so-called advertising alliances, SP alliances, etc., even though I've met many people and seen mostly unsatisfactory results.

Sorry for being verbose, I'm too tired. Let me briefly write this down first. If anyone reads it, I'll go into detail; otherwise, never mind.

Website Positioning: Determine what kind of site to build based on your own and surrounding factors, and whose money to earn. Look at the newspapers at hand; they will tell you. As for what kind of site to build, I won't elaborate here, as it depends on individual factors. We'll discuss it later.

Website Promotion: Although there are many methods, the use of SEO is indispensable. However, the love story with internet cafes will help you mature sooner. Just revealing a bit, set the homepage of internet cafe computers to your website.

The Love Story with Internet Cafes goes through three stages: First Love, Passionate Love, and finally Breakup or Marriage, possibly even marrying a second wife.

First Love: After setting it as the homepage, develop related sites targeting the internet cafe audience. Whether selling products or advertisements, create simple web pages and manage the relationship with the in-laws well.

Passionate Love: Comprehensive and deep cooperation, strategic alliances, organizing local gaming alliances. When online games become popular, gaming alliances naturally follow, and relevant products will naturally gain popularity. Don't tell me that every site already has its own forum or large alliances, etc. I'm talking about local gaming alliances. If you haven't played online games, then don't bother reading further. Upward, we can directly approach advertisers, and downward, we can organize clubs, etc.
