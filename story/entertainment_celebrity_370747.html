High-definition images: "Detective Dee" breaks through the 300 million yuan mark, Li Bingbing graces the cover of "Fashion" magazine

Sina Entertainment News: In "Detective Dee and the Mystery of the Phantom Flame", Li Bingbing's masculine image and stylish martial arts moves have captivated many audiences. Now, the mainland box office revenue of the film is approaching 300 million yuan. In the film, Li Bingbing, who has shown off her handsome male costume, returns to the "big sister" route and graces the cover of "L'OFFICIEL Fashion" November issue.

Recently, Li Bingbing once again graced the cover of "L'OFFICIEL Fashion" November issue. A black rose embroidered golden low-cut dress, rose chain gold necklace, paired with an elegant makeup look, Li Bingbing appears sexy and unrestrained, like a perfect combination of Baroque and Gothic styles. After the magazine was released, many netizens praised her as the true "big sister spokesperson".

Since its release during the National Day holiday, "Detective Dee and the Mystery of the Phantom Flame" has been continuously popular. The mainland box office revenue has approached 300 million yuan, ranking second only to "Aftershock" among this year's mainland Chinese films. The film also set a new record for the highest box office revenue of a movie starring Li Bingbing. In the film, she first appeared in 15 different outfits, then stunningly as Shangguan Jing'er and Guoshi Luli. Her acting in the drama was praised as "the most outstanding", while her action scenes were rated as "the coolest". Even director Tsui Hark praised her for driving the plot development in the movie and boosting the box office outside of it.
