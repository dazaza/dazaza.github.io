Ultra Electronics (000823) (Enter the stock forum, new version of market conditions): The company mainly focuses on the research, development, production, and sales of printed circuit boards, liquid crystal displays, ultra-sonic electronic instruments, and ultra-thin or special copper-clad laminates.

Anticipation for added value in 3G mobile phone components and high-end communication products: Considering that the traditional products of the company are already at full production capacity, performance growth will primarily come from new technologies, new products, and product structure optimization. The gross margin of traditional PCBs is expected to maintain an upward trend. With the release of production capacity for 3G product components and the increasing proportion of high-end communication products, the company currently has a production capacity of 1.2 million square meters for second-stage HDI boards used in 3G mobile phones and internet cards. It is anticipated that second-stage HDI boards will be extensively applied this year in 3G mobile phones, smartphones, and internet card products. Therefore, as the shipment volume of the high-end communication market continues to increase, the utilization rate of the second-stage HDI board production capacity will also rise, ensuring a stable profit margin and providing stable performance contributions.

New patent technology supports future performance growth: The company's capacitive touch screen technology is currently applying for a patent. The produced touch screens offer higher cost-performance compared to similar products, with promising market competitiveness prospects. Currently, the profitability of small-size LCD displays is expected to significantly improve, and the downward price trend is basically over, waiting for a faster price recovery. This year, the significant price increase of large-size LCD panels will gradually be passed on to small-size LCD displays. It is estimated that the LCD display could generate profits of at least 20 million yuan in the second half of the year. Meanwhile, the company is gradually expanding its touch screen production capacity and has started small-scale shipments. With increased investment in touch screens, there is a strong possibility for rapid development in the next few years.

Earnings forecast: The EPS for 2009-11 are 0.27 yuan, 0.36 yuan, and 0.45 yuan respectively, corresponding to dynamic P/E ratios of 28 times, 21 times, and 17 times. The dynamic P/B ratios are 2.12, 1.98, and 1.83 times.

In the secondary market, the stock has been building a large momentum platform in the previous period. In recent days, it rebounded with the market index, with ideal price-volume coordination and intact moving average systems. Under the expectation of good future performance, it is expected to initiate an upward trend. Investors are advised to closely monitor it.

The procedures for buying and selling stocks are as follows: When buying or selling stocks, you must specify whether you are buying or selling, the name (or code), quantity, and price of the stock. Moreover, this order is only valid on the day it is placed. The content of the commission includes the abbreviation (code) of the stock you want to buy or sell, the quantity, and the price of the stock. The abbreviation of the stock usually consists of three to four Chinese characters, while the code is six digits long. When placing a trade order, the code and abbreviation of the stock must match. At the same time, there are certain regulations regarding the quantity of stocks bought or sold: The quantity for buying stocks must be a whole multiple of 100, but the quantity for selling stocks does not have to be a whole multiple of 100.

There are four methods of commission: counter submission, telephone automatic commission, computer automatic commission, and remote terminal commission.
