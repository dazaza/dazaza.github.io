Man disappears after throwing sulfuric acid at his wife and son; boy hopes father turns himself in soon

by sznstejx3q3 on 2012-03-03 10:57:46

One morning three months ago, the husband splashed prepared sulfuric acid on his wife and son, Binbin (a pseudonym), who was just about to go to school for enrollment. When talking about his father, 14-year-old Binbin’s eyes were filled with deep hatred.

Sixteen years of marriage like a nightmare

On the afternoon of December 15th, under the guidance of Zhang Haiqin, a staff member of the Shaanxi Women's Research Association, I met Zhang Ping in a rented house shared by three families. What a face it was: the skin on the entire left side of her face seemed as if it had been torn off, revealing flesh that wasn't pink like normal human skin. From her forehead, eyes, and neck, it was impossible to tell what she originally looked like. Seen from the side, her ear had shrunk into a small lump due to the corrosion of sulfuric acid.

Zhang Ping told the reporter, "You must find me terrifying, right? My arms are even worse than my face. All this was caused by him (her husband)." Now, she has to rely on applying medicine every day, never daring to look in the mirror, fearing that she would be scared by herself. In 1995, she met her husband, who worked as an inspector at a hospital, through an introduction while living in the countryside. Due to the lack of emotional foundation, her husband often worked outside, so they rarely saw each other. After two years of marriage, Zhang Ping had her son, Binbin. At the time when she needed care and comfort the most, her husband had an affair.

"From childhood to adulthood, I took care of my son, as if he wasn't his (husband's) son. I remember clearly that whenever I wasn't home, he would give my son 10 yuan as meal money every day and then go gamble. He spent little time at home. Every time he came back, without caring about my son doing homework, he turned up the TV volume very loud. If I said a word to him, he would immediately hit me. If it weren’t for our son, if we didn’t live in a rural area where traditions are strong, and if my parents hadn't firmly opposed divorce, I couldn't have gone on long ago. But now looking back, not divorcing him was the biggest mistake of my life, harming myself and also my son."

After the husband splashed sulfuric acid, he disappeared

At around 7 o'clock on the morning of August 31st this year, she and her son were washing up in the washroom when her husband rushed towards them with a bottle from the bedside. More shockingly, he splashed sulfuric acid onto her head, shoulders, and arms, and even splashed it onto her son. In a panic, she used her right hand to protect her son. The searing pain spread across her body wherever the liquid touched her. Zhang Ping thought it was boiling water, so she quickly pulled her son out of the rented house and shouted for help, but the more she ran, the more she felt something was wrong. The burning sensation on her face almost made her faint.

Binbin still vividly remembers the situation that day. Although his mother protected him, his arm still bears permanent scars. Since then, Zhang Ping's husband has seemingly vanished, and despite the police's active search, there has been no information about him yet.

When talking about his father, Binbin's expression turned cold, and he remained silent without any expression, but the reporter could see deep hatred in his eyes.

Son hopes for father's swift apprehension

Before the reporter left, Teacher Zhang Haiqin handed over 3,000 yuan in medical expenses donated by well-meaning people to Binbin, encouraging Zhang Ping to stay strong and continue treatment. She also discussed with Zhang Ping to provide psychological counseling for Binbin every week going forward.

When talking about his wishes, Binbin didn't want to say much, but Teacher Zhang Haiqin said that the child's greatest wish is for the person who hurt his mother and him to be caught soon and receive legal punishment.

Currently, Zhang Ping's burn area covers 15% of her body, and her face is completely disfigured. She urgently needs corneal transplant surgery, and further treatment is required for the burn areas on her arms. The total cost of all treatments is as high as 800,000 yuan.
