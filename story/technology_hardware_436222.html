Ku6.com makes frequent post-Olympic moves, and the homepage remodeling indicates its entry into the video media.

by kfjro619 on 2011-05-24 11:47:55

At 19:00 on September 19, the latest revamped homepage of Ku6.com went online. We found that the revamped Ku6.com has strengthened its news section, with the news attribute significantly increasing. This is the first action taken by Ku6.com at the product level after Li Shanyou publicly expressed not long ago that China's video industry should transform towards a portal model. After the revamp, the information is richer, the structure is clearer, and it gives netizens a refreshing experience. Surveys show that 65% of netizens are very satisfied with the current homepage, and nearly half of the respondents said their favorite column is video news and information.

The main features of this revamp by Ku6.com can be summarized into four major points: First, increased attention to news, highlighting newsworthiness - Ku6.com was the first in the industry to propose the concept of "video news", enhancing the focus on news for video sharing websites, providing comprehensive video interpretations of hot news events related to national economy and people's livelihood. At the same time, various types of video information such as entertainment, sports, education, automobiles, fashion, life, and original content from netizens will also be enhanced. Second, through the revamp, Ku6 strengthened its ability to integrate content, highlighting watchability - Ku6.com boosted the strength of its editorial team and enhanced the integration of information by editors. Through special reports and other formats, the watchability of various videos was enhanced. Third, netizens can freely comment on the news, highlighting participation - The Ku6 Paker channel will provide a platform for netizens to comment on the news, allowing them to interpret the news from their own perspective, reflecting the participatory nature of Web 2.0 media. Finally, strengthening cooperation with copyright holders, highlighting legitimacy - Ku6.com directly addresses the copyright issues that have long existed in the video industry, actively engaging in strategic cooperation with content providers to enrich the content of movie, TV series, and anime channels.

Ku6.com CEO Li Shanyou stated: "Through the coverage of Olympic events, Ku6.com has accumulated extensive experience in covering major events, successfully entering the ranks of domestic new media and becoming an outstanding representative. Subsequently, Ku6.com will continue to strive to create the first video portal in China's internet industry that is the newest, fastest, and most comprehensive. This revamp is just our first step, and we will continue to launch more innovative products to meet the needs of Ku6.com's continuously improving media portal attributes."

After more than two years of exploration, the business model of China's video industry has gradually become clear: combining original sharing with media attributes. Through this homepage revamp, Ku6.com has initially shown the prototype of a video media portal website, combined with its unique UGA original force, demonstrating its determination to be the industry leader.
