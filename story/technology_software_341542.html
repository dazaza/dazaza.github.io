Journal of Hubei Adult Education Institute Call for Submissions

The Journal of Hubei Adult Education Institute serves the theoretical research of adult education, vocational education, and private education. It provides policy guidance in promoting the national guidelines and policies related to adult education, vocational education, and private education. Additionally, it offers an academic exchange platform for a wide range of educational scientific researchers, administrative department officials, and educational practitioners.

Submission Requirements:

1. Manuscripts should be based on reliable data, accurate figures, and possess creativity, scientific rigor, and practicality. The argumentation should be novel, well-supported with sufficient evidence and reliable data. Authors are responsible for their own work (plagiarism is strictly prohibited), and the writing should be concise.

2. Names of authors should be listed in order below the title, and this order should be determined at the time of submission. Authors' names, institutions, detailed addresses, and postal codes must be clearly written. For manuscripts with multiple authors, all authors' consent must be obtained for the order of authorship, which cannot be altered after receiving the acceptance notice.

3. Paper format generally includes: title, author(s) and institution(s), postal code, content abstract, keywords, main text, and references.

4. To shorten publication cycles and reduce errors, authors are encouraged to submit electronic soft disks along with their manuscripts. Below the paper, the author's detailed mailing address [e.g., XX Province, XX City (District/County), XX Road, XX Number, XX School], contact telephone number (landline or mobile), contact email, and QQ number should be noted for us to communicate with you promptly and efficiently.

5. The editorial board reserves the right to edit submissions. If you do not agree with any editing, please declare this in your submission.
