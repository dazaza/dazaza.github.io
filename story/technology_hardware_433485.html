Mobile World (bbs.ccmobi.com) Introduction:

Today, the latest price quote for Sony Ericsson W995 is 950 yuan, with a configuration that includes one battery, one charger, headphones, data cable, and a 2GB card. The Sony Ericsson W995, as a Walkman music phone, is equipped with an 8.1-megapixel camera, addressing the previous issue of Walkman phones not supporting auto-focus. However, in an era dominated by smartphones, even a feature-rich music phone like the Sony Ericsson W995 was priced at only about three thousand yuan when it launched, which is less than half the price of the Nokia N97.

In terms of appearance, the Sony Ericsson W995 continues the common slider design used by Sony Ericsson, complemented by a body crafted with metal brushing techniques, making the overall design very modern and stylish. In terms of display, the Sony Ericsson W995 features a 2.6-inch TFT screen with a resolution of 240x320, providing excellent visual performance. The dimensions of the device are 97.0 x 49.0 x 15.0 mm, and it weighs 113g, offering a good feel in hand.

The Sony Ericsson W995 is equipped with a universal 3.5mm headphone jack and adopts the Clear Audio Experience concept, supporting Clear Stereo and Clear Bass. Combined with dual speakers and dedicated music keys, as well as the new version of the Walkman player, the W995 performs well in terms of music playback. Notably, the Sony Ericsson W995 is also fitted with an 8-megapixel camera that supports auto-focus and face detection functions, along with digital zoom and electronic image stabilization, providing a commendable shooting experience.
