December 5th, Lanzhou, China National Radio - Under the backdrop of the international financial crisis, China continues to place energy conservation, emissions reduction and environmental protection in a prominent strategic position. After the state announced its ten measures for maintaining economic growth, it clearly stated that a total investment of 4 trillion yuan will strictly prevent funds from being used in high-energy consumption, high-pollution industries and low-level repetitive construction. As the capital city of Gansu Province, Lanzhou alone uses nearly 1.5 million tons of fresh water for car washing every year, which is equivalent to three times the water volume of West Lake. In the face of various problems brought by illegal car washing, the car washing industry in Lanzhou urgently needs to be reorganized. Washing away three West Lakes in a year. Lanzhou is a water-deficient city, with an average annual available water resource per capita of only 742 cubic meters, lower than the seriously water-deficient city standard of 1000 cubic meters per capita set internationally. According to incomplete statistics, Lanzhou has more than 200,000 motor vehicles (excluding farm vehicles) and over 1000 car wash shops of all sizes. Calculating at half a ton of water per vehicle washed once a month, Lanzhou uses nearly 1.5 million tons of water just for car washing annually, equivalent to three times the water volume of West Lake, making it a viable entrepreneurial project. Although the Yellow River runs through the city, 10 years ago, groundwater extraction in Lanzhou could reach 200,000 tons daily, whereas today, Lanzhou can only extract 50,000 tons of water daily from underground sources. With the rise of some large and medium-sized enterprises and the increase in migrant population, water resource shortage has become one of the important bottlenecks restricting Lanzhou's economic development. Urban management rectification is effective. Recently, with the continuous deepening of urban environment rectification work and the gradual implementation of the 'three-package responsibility' for sanitation in front of doors, stores failing the 'three-package responsibility' have been fined 3000 yuan in this urban management storm. This is like a warning to those who conduct car washing operations outside their stores on sidewalks. The most severe situation occurs on certain sections of South Binhe Road, where many illegally operated car wash shops take advantage of the road conditions, connect their own water taps, and start washing cars on the sidewalk, generating substantial income after rainy days. Detergent wears out old cars. This is the black curtain behind the super-profit operation in the car washing industry, advocated by the government. The car washing industry needs to be reshuffled.
