9k: 50M bindable rice, no ads, free static space

by vinas on 2008-09-08 09:22:12

9k offers 50M of free website space for webmasters with a monthly traffic limit of 1G. The 9k free website space is static and does not support PHP, ASP or other program scripts, nor does it support FTP; files can only be uploaded and managed via the Web. 9k will provide you with a free secondary domain name: yourname.9k.com, or you can choose to bind your own domain name. Although 9k states that there will be advertisements, free8 did not find any ads and the speed is also quite good. If you do not need this free static space, forwarding 9k as a free secondary domain to your site is also a good choice.
