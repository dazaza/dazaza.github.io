More focus on driving, Roewe Voice Cloud Driving iVoka解放both hands

by juyaqufa004 on 2011-12-14 16:26:47

Let's say, if the Siri voice assistant of iPhone 4S makes "iFone" even "crazier" and more convenient, then for cars, the meaning of artificial intelligence voice interaction lies in the driving safety assurance it brings to consumers. Roewe 350's Voice Cloud Driving iVoka enables cars to truly communicate with owners through the simplest and most effective language interaction, completely freeing the owner's hands and eyes, thereby reducing accidents. The Voice Cloud Driving iVoka, developed based on cutting-edge cloud computing technology, not only can provide extraordinary safe driving life for Roewe 350 owners, but also bring an entertaining driving experience through intelligent human-car dialogue.

Nowadays, it is an era of informatization and networking. People's life pace is fast, pressure is relatively high, and communication is also frequent. People often answer phone calls or send/receive text messages while driving, which easily leads to car accidents and other unexpected incidents. The iVoka Human-Machine Intelligent Interaction System operates entirely based on voice commands, significantly reducing the occurrence of accidents caused by improper operations due to hands leaving the steering wheel.

The system can read out text messages received on the phone via voice, and if a reply is needed, the driver can dictate the message content verbally, and iVoka will convert the spoken words into text and send them out. During this process, the driver's eyes remain focused on the road ahead, and their hands do not leave the steering wheel, yet the communication need has been met. Additionally, stock quotes and flight information can be checked, all under control. Using the iVoka Human-Machine Intelligent Interaction System can free your hands and reduce risky behaviors. The convenience and intelligence of Voice Cloud Driving iVoka are like an invisible safety umbrella, automatically preventing accidents from happening, making Roewe 350 a safer mode of transportation.

The navigation of the inkaNet intelligent driving service system is based on the voice search function of Voice Cloud Driving iVoka, which is very different from previous car navigation systems. In the popular online videos, many car enthusiasts challenged iVoka at the Guangzhou Auto Show. For example, when an experimenter said "I want to eat beef noodles" to iVoka, iVoka would converse with the experimenter, locate their position, and inform them of the addresses of nearby beef noodle places. This kind of navigation completes the search based on location and destination characteristics, and then determines the location through navigation. While driving, it allows drivers to focus on the road conditions, ensuring driving safety, and at the same time enriches the driving life of the driver.

The inkaNet intelligent driving service system never neglects the entertainment needs during driving, and it is changing the in-car entertainment methods of car consumers through powerful intelligent voice interaction technology, Voice Cloud Driving iVoka. Through "conversations" with Voice iVoka, "human and car" can achieve entertainment interaction. Whether listening to music, listening to the radio, or checking real-time information, just by speaking, the system can provide automatic queries and selections for various entertainment functions, allowing drivers to enjoy thorough entertainment. Moreover, inkaNet has connected with Douban Music Library to realize network music playback and search, satisfying the massive needs of music lovers. With entertainment applications as excellent as iPhone 4S, iVoka has become a rich mobile information platform through its simple and quick voice interaction method, extending the basic attributes of cars while also changing the traditional usage habits of car consumers, catering to the trend of new-generation consumers' pursuit of automotive informatization lifestyle.

Although designed for car users, the smart and humorous Voice Cloud Driving iVoka, like the Siri voice assistant of iPhone 4S, is mainly designed with humanized colloquial language. When users feel bored while driving, they can call iVoka anytime, and the intelligent and humanized Voice Cloud Driving iVoka will interact with the owner, making the journey more enjoyable with it as a driving companion.

Roewe adheres to the brand essence of "Taste, Technology, Realization". As the leader of domestic automakers in independent innovation, the announcement of the Voice Cloud Driving iVoka function at this Guangzhou Auto Show has brought tailor-made human-machine interactive driving experiences for Chinese consumers, allowing us to see the new way of future car life and leading us into the era of human-car interaction. Shanghai Automotive leads the future trend of automobile information innovation technology under the information age, once again standing at the forefront of technological innovation and change.
