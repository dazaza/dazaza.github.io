BIZPROUD Product Features Introduction: Operated on the company's own server, BIZPROUD provides a settlement marketing system that integrates e-commerce online mall, sales process management, call center management, customer relationship management, instant sales statistics and other functions with media order recognition technology at its core. By using this system, enterprises can achieve:

1. Identify Media Sources/Monitor Advertising Effectiveness

For companies, ensuring advertising effectiveness is the most important condition for expanding the market. However, under circumstances where companies do not trust the advertising effectiveness of the media, identifying media sources can achieve the purpose of monitoring advertising effectiveness. Identifying media sources means that when consumers make purchases through advertisements on the media, the system can automatically identify the source of the media.

For example, if a consumer sees an advertisement placed by Tong Ren Tang in the World News Report and makes a purchase by calling 4008106650 or logging into the online mall, the system will automatically identify the source of the media as the World News Report.

2. Achieve Settlement Marketing with Media and Reduce Advertising Investment Risk

Settlement marketing with the media means that the media first provides media resources for the company, the company conducts advertising planning and placement, and the resulting sales are divided according to the pre-negotiated settlement ratio. This requires a fair, impartial, and transparent third-party settlement platform for support. Through this platform, the media and the company can monitor the real-time order information and protect the interests of all parties involved in the settlement. At the same time, the members brought to the company by the media also become members of the media. The company is responsible for maintaining these members. The repeated consumption generated by the company serving members through DM information delivery or telephone follow-ups still needs to be settled with the media. For example, if the World News Report brings 500 customers to Tong Ren Tang, and Tong Ren Tang makes repeat purchases from 230 of these members through telephone follow-ups or sending DM information, then the resulting repeat sales still need to be settled with the World News Report.

3. Convenient Call Center, Customer Interaction, and Sales Process Management

By adopting this system, companies no longer need to build separate platforms for sales process management, call center management, and customer interaction. The BIZPROUD system integrates e-commerce online mall, sales process management, call center management, customer relationship management, and instant sales statistics functions into one system for comprehensive management. This allows companies to freely switch between different platforms for convenient operation. Especially in the customer interaction section, it can uniformly distribute the company's group buying information, preferential information, and DM information through various networks, traditional media, and mobile text messages, maintaining consistency in preferential information.

4. Independent Database, Multiple Backups

The BIZPROUD system is placed on the company's personal server, and the data obtained belongs solely to the company. The authenticity and systematicness of the database can provide the most effective information for the company, including membership data, advertising effect data, and sales data, etc.

To operate this system, companies need to prepare in the following two aspects:

1. Realize Storeless Marketing

To realize storeless marketing, companies need to establish an e-commerce website. Guanglian Times can assist companies in building their e-commerce websites.

2. Have a Complete Operation Team
