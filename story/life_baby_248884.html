According to information found online regarding infant diarrhea symptoms:

Judgment 1: Based on bowel movement frequency

A normal baby usually has 1-2 bowel movements a day, with stool being yellow and solid. During diarrhea, bowel movements increase compared to normal conditions; mild cases may have 4-6 times, severe cases can reach over 10 times, even dozens of times.

Judgment 2: Based on stool consistency

It is watery diarrhea, resembling egg drop soup, sometimes it could be mucus or bloody stool. The baby may also experience vomiting, abdominal distension, fever, restlessness, and poor spirits.
