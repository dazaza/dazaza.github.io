The official website of Transformers is online

by fifacom on 2007-04-29 17:08:39

The official website of the Transformers Game version "Transformers: The Game" has been opened, and players can feel the style of this all-platform masterpiece in advance.

To http://www.transformersgame.com/ after you can see a big loading progress indicator, the use of digital is unique, the appearance of a little transformers - is animated version, is not a movie. Of course, the website design style goes without saying, everywhere is full of metal, but also to check the latest news, preview the game features, learn about the game characters, enjoy the game screenshots, watch the movie trailer and game production tidiums, but the download link is just a decoration.

In addition, the website also provides a small game. Sign up, log in, choose Autobot or Decepticon, and you can start a battle. The Righteous Autobots are now in absolute dominance, with a maximum score of 820, while the Decepticons are only 280.

Oh, and remember to wear headphones or turn on the speaker, the background music of the website is not to be missed.

The game version of Transformers, developed by Traveller's Tales and published by Activision, will launch alongside the movie version this summer for PC, PSP, PS2, PS3, Xbox, Xbox 360, Wii, DS, GBA, GC and almost all other platforms
