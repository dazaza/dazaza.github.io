【Everything is fine now, and I can still take care of you】 They met, fell in love, and got married during the best time of their youth. When they planned to have a baby, she had persistent high fever and was diagnosed with leukemia. After chemotherapy, radiotherapy, and bone marrow transplantation, her condition was finally under control. Her pelvis has already necrotized. Even if there's no recurrence in five years, she will only be able to live with the help of a wheelchair. During this period, her parents forced him to divorce, but he directly moved to the hospital. He only said to her: "Everything is fine now. I am still working and can take care of you."

【It's been six years, and I've been waiting for this one sentence from you】 A strong-willed woman didn't say a kind word to her husband in the six years after marriage. One day, she couldn't help asking her husband: "Why don't you fight back? It makes me feel uncomfortable inside." Her husband smiled and said: "It's been six years, and I've been waiting for this one sentence from you." The wife cried as all the scenes of the past six years flooded her mind, and tears streamed down her face. Since then, this beautiful world has gained a good wife.

【The street lamps on the side of the road】 She accompanied him through the hardest times of his entrepreneurship. After he succeeded, he fell in love with someone else. She didn't say anything. On the night he broke up with her, she pointed at the street lamps. Later, when he invested and failed, his company went bankrupt. On the night he was drunk, he looked up unintentionally and suddenly burst into tears. He saw the street lamps on the side of the road and finally understood her feelings: Street lamps use all their strength to accompany you through the darkness but are forced to leave your world when light comes.

【I spent my youth pining for you, but I cannot be with you】 She liked him for four years, collecting everything about him, liking the way he bowed his head, getting angry when she saw him with other girls, and writing countless love letters. However, none of this, he knew. Several years later, they were no longer in the same city. Before her wedding, she received his gift, which was an earring she accidentally lost once. On the letter: "I spent my youth pining for you, but I cannot be with you."

【"I'm back," just like 10 years ago】 She walked alone on this bustling and noisy street. She looked at the candy shop where he once fed her cotton candy; she looked at the banyan tree where he once held her and let her cry and vent; she stared at a bridal shop where he once said he would make her wear that deep red wedding dress... Unknowingly, she reached the end of the street. Suddenly, he gently hugged her from behind. "I'm back," just like 10 years ago when they were 17, and he said: "I will come back."

【I traverse the seasons just to melt in your embrace】 He was a sick child, and no one was willing to play with him. Until the first snowfall, he built a small snowman. He asked: "Can I hug you?" The snowman countered: "Why?" He said: "Because I like you." The snowman silently embraced him. The next second, he heard the snowman softly whisper in his ear: "I traverse the seasons just to melt in your embrace, thank you for daring to love me."

【He learned to resolve being single, and she finally learned to leave him...】 He grew up with her. He learned guitar, and she learned to quietly listen to him. He learned to fight, and she learned to bandage him. He learned to stay out all night at bars, and she learned to catch him at fixed points. He learned to flirt openly with class beauties, and she learned to secretly send flowers for him. He learned to work overtime and not return home, and she learned to make midnight snacks but lied about it being delivered food. He learned waltz steps, and she learned to watch him from afar at dances. He learned to resolve being single, and she finally learned to leave him...

【I mean it: "I like you"] At the graduation ceremony, he suddenly leaned close to her ear and whispered: "I like you." She laughed: "Hey, teasing me for three years is enough, isn't it?" For three years, she concealed her feelings and faced the repeated teasing of her secret admirer, and she was really exhausted. "I mean it!" He looked serious again when he saw her disbelief. "You always mean it." He lowered his head: "In fact, every time was real, Christmas flower reservation."

【The deepest three words: "School's out"] On the evening Grandma passed away, Grandpa accompanied her through the last journey of her life in the ward. Before passing, Grandma said to Grandpa: "School's out." Grandpa, who had been pretending to be calm, burst into tears like a child after hearing these words. After the funeral, I asked Grandpa about the meaning of these three words. Grandpa told me that this was a phrase Grandma often used when they were still in elementary school: "School's out, let's go home together."

【Only a dime, but it's "a lot" already] When they first met, the first gift he gave her was a very expensive LV bag; during their passionate romance, the gift he gave her was a luxurious villa; at their engagement, his business failed, and he was penniless, unable to give any gifts. Holding the few dimes in her hand, he said: "Sorry! I can't give you happiness anymore." She shook her head and gently pried open his big hand: "Although it's only a dime, it's 'a lot' already."

【She always said he wasn't romantic】 She always said he wasn't romantic. After breaking up, she sat quietly in the house, hoping he would call her again. After waiting for a long time, she picked up the phone and found it had turned off automatically; wanting a drink of water, she discovered the kettle was empty; wanting to go out for a walk, she didn't know where the keys were. She sat down and realized she had lost love.

【Who will give you happiness?] He encountered an air crash, and among more than a hundred people on board, he was the only survivor. When the rescue team found him, the doctors kept saying it was a miracle that he survived such serious injuries. Later, she asked him how he managed to survive. He smiled: "I was thinking all the time, if I'm gone, who will give you happiness?"

【Everything is fleeting clouds, loving each other is enough] A family discussed their ideas after becoming wealthy at the dinner table. Dad laughed and said: "When we have money in the future, I'll replace your mom." Mom didn't think much of it. Dad became serious and said: "No, I won't." "Because of the two yuan we spent several days on when we were poor, your mom stayed with me. I will never betray her in my life." Mom looked at Dad and smiled happily. Happiness is the simple mutual support through life.

【Happy Valentine's Day] For three consecutive Valentine's Days, he would receive blessing messages from the same unknown number, only five short words: "Happy Valentine's Day." He never replied. In the fourth year, on Valentine's Day, that message didn't appear. He hesitated for a long time and finally sent a "Happy Valentine's Day" to that number. Quickly came the reply: "Thank you, who are you?" Love won't wait for anyone in its place, a moment of carelessness, and it's taken away by time.
