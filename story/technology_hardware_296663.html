La Liga - New Ballon d'Or Winner Scores Twice, Ibrahimovic Also Nets, Barcelona 3-1 Deportivo La Coruna

by djbgufgire on 2009-12-06 14:07:58

La Liga - New Ballon d'Or Winner Scores Twice, Ibrahimović Adds Another as Barcelona Triumphs 3-1 Over Deportivo La Coruña

At 00:00 on December 6th, Beijing time, in the English Premier League - Chelsea lost 1-2 to Manchester City due to a missed penalty by their key player, leading by only 2 points. In the 13th round of the 2009-10 La Liga season, Juventus defeated Inter Milan 2-1 in Serie A with two red cards nearly causing a brawl, and the defending champions FC Barcelona visited Riazor Stadium to face Deportivo La Coruña. Messi opened the scoring in the first half with a powerful shot from the center, and the home team equalized through Adrián's header. In the second half, Pedro assisted Messi who scored again with a header, and just before the final whistle, Ibrahimović added another goal with a volley. Ultimately, Barcelona won 3-1 away from home, continuing to lead the league table.

After narrowly defeating Real Madrid 1-0 at home last weekend in the Clásico, Barcelona reclaimed the top spot in the standings. Then in the advanced match of the 15th round held midweek, they extended their lead to five points with consecutive victories. For this game, Barcelona fielded their full-strength starting lineup that had won the treble the previous season, with the formidable attacking trio of Messi, Ibrahimović, and Henry leading the charge, aiming for three consecutive wins in La Liga as they sought to overpower Riazor. Deportivo La Coruña countered with a 4-2-3-1 formation.

Barcelona's Three-Pass, Three-Shot Display with Messi Opening the Scoring

In the 2nd minute, Henry easily dribbled past Pablo but his pass lacked any threat. In the 5th minute, Rodriguez delivered a brilliant pass for the home side, forcing Piqué to clear it for a corner. In the 6th minute, Deportivo launched a quick attack down the left flank, but Guardado's powerful shot went high. In the 9th minute, Barcelona's free kick saw Messi's attempt go wide. In the 10th minute, Guardado's through ball found Mista, Abidal made up for the defense but the ball eventually fell to Adrián's feet for the home side, almost costing Barcelona a goal.

In the 11th minute, Barcelona's through ball sent Henry in on goal alone from an acute angle, but the Frenchman was denied by the goalkeeper diving ahead to clear the danger, still met with boos from the crowd. In the 14th minute, Alves delivered a cross from the right flank, and among a cluster of players, Ibrahimović leaped to head the ball, which Aranzubia firmly caught.

In the 20th minute, Messi carried the ball forward in the midfield, dodging five defenders before shooting from the left rib, only for Lopo to block it out for a corner, the ball narrowly missing the post, almost resulting in an own goal. In the 22nd minute, Iniesta took two long-range shots that were headed clear by Deportivo. In the 23rd minute, Barcelona's diagonal pass to the right wing saw Messi deliver a chip into the box, and in front of the goal, Ibrahimović's scissor-kick attempt came close but not quite.

In the 25th minute, Barcelona maintained continuous attacks in the frontcourt; initially passing behind the defense to Henry, but Aranzubia cleared it early. The ball was then intercepted by Iniesta outside the box who fed Xavi whose shot was blocked. The ball was recovered by Barcelona once more, quickly passed to the right flank where Alves dribbled into the area and subtly rolled the ball to Messi. Messi then brought the ball into the central arc and fired a fierce shot, sending the ball straight into the bottom-right corner of the net. Barcelona led 1-0 away.

Deportivo Counterattacks with Adrián's Header Equalizing

In the 29th minute, Messi was fouled in midfield by a Deportivo player. In the 32nd minute, Deportivo's offensive effort saw Mista's strong shot deflected away. Subsequently, Deportivo's Thomas sent a beautiful through ball, allowing Mista on the left wing to break through, but his header to the center failed to find a teammate. In the 36th minute, Deportivo's over-the-top pass was flagged offside for Mista.

In the 37th minute, Busquets intercepted the ball in midfield and delivered a diagonal pass. Inside the box, both Xavi and Ibrahimović ran to the same position, Xavi controlled the ball and passed to Ibrahimović, but the Swede's raised right arm touched the ball first, preventing a shot.

In the 38th minute, Deportivo's goal kick directly reached the frontcourt. Busquets attempted to head it clear but inadvertently directed it towards his own goal. Adrián, sandwiched between Puyol and Abidal, cleverly flicked the ball with his forehead past everyone, leaving Valdés no chance to react. Deportivo equalized 1-1.

In the 43rd minute, Deportivo's quick breakthrough down the right flank resulted in a slightly too far cross, leaving an unmarked Mista on the left wing unable to get to the ball. Immediately after, Barcelona counterattacked with Ibrahimović delivering a fine pass, but Messi's shot inside the box was blocked by Aranzubia diving with his leg. The first half ended with both sides tied 1-1.

Pedro Assists Messi's Header for a Second Goal

Neither side made substitutions at the start of the second half. In the 47th minute, Messi combined with Ibrahimović inside the box but Henry failed to connect with the ball. Alves received a yellow card for a dangerous tackle on Guardado from the side. In the 53rd minute, Messi's corner from the right directly aimed at the goal was caught by Aranzubia. Immediately after, Deportivo launched a long pass counterattack, Mista breaking through Piqué for a one-on-one chance but his shot grazed wide.

In the 58th minute, Mista intercepted Iniesta's pass in midfield to counterattack, but Deportivo's subsequent shot posed little threat, the ball was repossessed by Iniesta, prompting Mista to foul from behind and receive a yellow card. In the 59th minute, Xavi fell in pain during midfield contention, drawing boos from the crowd. Messi's free kick into the box was headed clear, followed by Iniesta's long-range shot from the left wing being comfortably gathered by the goalkeeper.

In the 62nd minute, Deportivo's through ball in midfield was cut out by Piqué ahead of Adrián. In the 63rd minute, Messi's penetration pass inside the box was blocked, Xavi continued with a through ball from outside the box, and Ibrahimović backheeled the ball while falling down, enabling Henry on the left rib to shoot but it was blocked. In the 67th minute, Mista fought valiantly amidst Barcelona's three defenders to win a free kick in the frontcourt. After Alves headed the clearance, Barcelona counterattacked swiftly, with Iniesta's shot from the left rib being blocked. In the 69th minute, Iniesta delivered a diagonal pass from the left flank to the far post where Ibrahimović's angled header went wide. Pedro replaced Henry.

Ibrahimović Adds a Final Touch to Secure Victory

In the 75th minute, Alves set up Messi who broke past Sergio inside the box and unleashed a powerful shot that slid narrowly past the post. In the 76th minute, Abidal was booked for fouling Rodriguez. In the 79th minute, Pedro broke through on the right flank inside the box and delivered a precise assist to an unmarked Messi who scored with a header from less than ten meters out. Barcelona regained the lead 2-1.

In the 80th minute, Messi delivered a through ball inside the box, Iniesta's low-angle shot from the left was blocked, earning a corner. In the 86th minute, Deportivo's long pass counterattack saw Mista flagged offside again. In the 87th minute, Iniesta played a pass in the frontcourt to the left flank, Abidal crossed, and an unmarked Ibrahimović inside the box tapped the ball effortlessly into the net. Barcelona expanded their advantage to 3-1. With two minutes of stoppage time, the game concluded with Barcelona triumphing 3-1 at Riazor.

Starting Lineups:

Deportivo La Coruña (4-2-3-1): 1-Aranzubia; 2-Pablo, 19-Colotto, 7-Lopo, 3-Felipe; 16-Thomas, 8-Sergio; 22-Rodriguez, 24-Mista, 18-Guardado (14-Alvarez, 86'); 10-Adrián (6-Jurado, 62')

Barcelona (4-3-3): 1-Valdés; 2-Alves, 5-Puyol, 3-Piqué, 22-Abidal; 6-Xavi, 16-Busquets, 8-Iniesta (15-Keita, 88'); 10-Messi, 9-Ibrahimović, 14-Henry (27-Pedro, 69')
