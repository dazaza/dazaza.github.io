Ten Heartfelt Sentences Written for Men

Ten Heartfelt Words Written for Men (2010-03-31 06:36:18) Repost Tags: Long'er Emotion

One: Men must remember never to compare your own woman with other women.

Don't always talk about how good other people's wives are. Don't criticize her for not being beautiful; it's your good fortune that she married you. To say such things is really inappropriate. For most women, hearing a word of praise from the person they love is more cherished than all the compliments from others combined. Moreover, if you love her, how could you bear to hurt her?

Two: You shouldn’t neglect her every now and then.

Women are sensitive and suspicious. She will think negatively about many things. In fact, a quick kiss before going out or a hug when you come home can make your woman feel excited later on. These are not difficult for men to do, are they? Remember to tell her when you're sad so that the two of you can share the burden. When she's upset, hold her hand and pass the warmth of your palm to her.

Three: Don't give her the cold shoulder, as a man who throws tantrums at his woman is very detestable.

You are neat and tidy in business settings, while she is busy inside the house. The busy household chores have already made her quite irritated. You should know that it’s because of everything she does behind you that allows you to be worry-free. Imagine a man coming home after work and saying: "Honey, you've been busy all day, you've worked hard!" The woman smiles and says, "It's nothing, you've worked harder." Isn't that a harmonious picture?

Four: Men gather together to compare jobs and positions, and women gather together to compare their men and children.

Therefore, in front of her girlfriends, you must definitely show your indulgence and care for her, making her feel like a princess who has the complete love that makes all women jealous. If she is a reasonable woman, she will certainly also give you enough face in front of your buddies, allowing you to be a towering man in front of your friends.

Five: Just as men love looking at beautiful women, women also love looking at handsome men.

You can be jealous and angry, but a woman who truly loves you actually feels simpler when she sees a handsome man than a man seeing a beautiful woman. She just feels some admiration for seeing something beautiful, like looking at a beautiful painting, without the additional fantasies that men might have.

Six: A wife is taken to be cherished, and a cultivated man absolutely will not beat his wife.

If you truly love her, you must respect her and not resort to violence casually. If you no longer love her, then touch your conscience: Can I still make her happy? If the answer is no, then let her go, allowing her to find someone who can truly treat her well. Let yourself stop making mistakes and let her be free!

Seven: Women like men's machismo, which makes them feel secure.

However, women can't resist a man's gentleness even more. If women's gentleness is a weapon against men, then a man's unintentional gentleness can be considered a nuclear weapon for women. But women also have their innate nature, which is the maternal instinct. Occasionally, she may indulge you as if you were her child, but remember not to push your luck.

Eight: Family always comes first.

Men need to be responsible for their work but also need professional ethics, deriving joy from work. However, don't become a slave to work. We work to be happier with our families and enjoy life. It is important to remember to spend quality time regularly with your wife and children.

Nine: Your lover's parents are your own parents. Treat them as you would want to be treated, extending love to those associated with the one you love.

Respect the elderly as you would respect your own elders. If deep down you truly feel that these are my own parents, emotionally connecting closely with the elderly, they will sense this sincerity. Besides, old people are much like children; just like soothing children, making the elderly happy works well. We will all grow old someday, so set an example for the younger generation and teach them how to respect the elderly.

Ten: When two people are together, remember to be honest, trustful, tolerant, and understanding.

There's no need to hide everything, but you can tell harmless lies when necessary, keeping the standard within your own grasp. Try to see things from the other person's perspective, consider her situation, and understand her difficulties. Remember: You are her beloved person. You need to understand her, support her, and be forgiving when she makes mistakes.
