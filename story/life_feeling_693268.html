Waiting is also a happiness.

by meimei234 on 2012-03-06 15:21:37

Waiting is also a kind of happiness.

Time flies. I've known him for a long time. In the past, I never believed in the so-called online love, but now I believe it because I have experienced it. There is a song called "There will be forever be you in my heart".

When listening to this song, I will think of him far away. I don't know if he's doing well. Every time I call him, he says he's so tired. It makes me heartache, except encouraging him and showing my care. I can't help him because I'm not by his side.

Time flies, he's coming back. His brother is getting married. I look forward to his arrival, but I'm afraid of facing him. In fact, I am very contradictory inside.

We don't need to send messages or make calls everyday. I believe we both care each other. I know he is a good boy, so I cherish it. But everything depends on fate. If we are destined to be together, it will be our happiness. We are all sincere to each other. In fact, it's really hard to find someone who understands and trusts each other. Not only should two people understand each other...

But also trust each other. The most important thing is to be filial. Be kind, diligent, sensible, smart. Sometimes you need to think from another person's point of view. You need to encourage, care and take care of each other more.

Maybe he left a good impression on me. With the passage of time, mutual understanding, I trust him although he is not by my side.

But I always think of him. His every move. Especially his brilliant smile on his face. Even if he gives me a little care, I feel sweet when I think of it. I also feel happy and happy. Although he didn't give me too much, I learned to be content. At the same time, I have to think about him under great pressure.

In fact, I want to share everything with him. No matter what happens, in the future or now, I really want to. I don't want him to be too tired. Many things are for two people. It's not easy for one person to do it alone.

So many people are chasing me. I refuse them all. Because a person's heart can only be given to one person. Once given, it won't be easily taken back.

It's painful and boring without him by my side. When I'm sad and unhappy, no one is with me. I just bear it silently. I just think of him and it's not so painful. Because there is expectation, I go on strongly. Every time I look at the text message he sent, I can't help smiling. That's happiness. A happy smile.
