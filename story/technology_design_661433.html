The price of seed cotton in Dali, Shaanxi rises while the cottonseed price falls.

by cp3287bm on 2012-02-09 08:32:00

On September 20th, the local cotton enterprises in Dali, Shaanxi purchased grade 3 seed cotton at a price of 4.20 yuan per jin (lint percentage 37-37.5%, moisture content 17-18%), grade 4 at 4.10 yuan per jin, and mixed flowers at 3.95 yuan per jin. All prices increased by 0.10 yuan per jin compared to the beginning of September. The cottonseed price was 1.22 yuan per jin (due to water content exceeding 14%, it dropped by 0.125-0.15 yuan per jin later). If calculated based on these prices, the profit for grade 4 lint cotton is 19,076 yuan per ton (including processing fee of 500 yuan per ton), and the selling price is 19,300 yuan per ton (net weight, self-pickup). Cotton enterprises are barely profitable, spinning enterprises have low purchasing intentions, and overall business operations have suspended buying.
