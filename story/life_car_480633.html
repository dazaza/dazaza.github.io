The Development Path of Car Washing Machines in Automotive Beauty Supplies

The entry of fully automatic computerized car washing machines into the automotive beauty supplies industry is due to the continuous development of science and technology and the gradual refinement of industries. Although fully automatic computerized car washing machines are becoming more widespread, many car owners still have misconceptions about their use and performance.

Currently, the method car owners use to wash their cars is typically a bucket of water from start to finish. They believe that manual car washing conserves water, while using computerized car washing involves high-pressure water and undercarriage flushing, which they think consumes more water. In reality, the practice of using just one bucket of water is not advisable. While it may seem like it saves water, the sand and gravel in the wash water can be carried by the cleaning cloth to the car's paint surface, causing repeated abrasion that damages the paint. Over time, this can lead to fine scratches on the car body, loss of luster, or even discoloration. According to the head of Jinan Turtle Brand Auto Products Wholesale Network, fully automatic computerized car washing machines are excellent automotive beauty supplies, especially water-saving models that perform better. Moreover, these machines come with a water recycling system, allowing 80% of the water used for washing to be recycled, fundamentally resolving the conflict between undercarriage rinsing and energy conservation, significantly reducing water consumption.

Therefore, car enthusiasts should stop washing their cars with buckets and rags. This method does not save water and can harm your vehicle. It is recommended to visit professional automotive beauty supply stores for cleaning and maintenance. Additionally, you can choose other automotive beauty supplies such as hard shell wax and glass fluid for regular maintenance, keeping your car looking brand new.

Future Development Path of Automotive Beauty Supplies

Economic development and the increase in the number of cars have opened up the market for automotive beauty supplies. Decorating a newly purchased car has become a choice for many car owners. However, since the automotive products market is relatively young, it lacks maturity in various aspects, which to some extent restrains the development of automotive beauty and care supplies. According to the head of Turtle Brand Auto Products Wholesale Network, automotive beauty supplies will develop in the following directions:

1. Improve the quality of automotive beauty supplies. The profit from decorating automotive beauty supplies mainly comes from the difference in material costs and labor fees. Some merchants, in pursuit of profit, often substitute inferior products, affecting the quality and overall image of automotive beauty supplies. Poor-quality anti-explosion films and rearview mirrors pose significant risks to drivers, leading consumers to lose trust in automotive beauty and care supplies.

2. Enhance the skills of service personnel in the automotive beauty supply industry. It is difficult to distinguish the quality and effects immediately after decorating with automotive beauty supplies, so there are quite a few "four-no" beauty supplies in the market—those without proper training, professional brand products, professional machinery, and service quality guarantees. According to statistics, there are currently 9,000 companies specializing or partially engaged in automotive beauty supplies decoration, employing approximately 150,000 people, many of whom operate without licenses.

3. Establish industry standards for automotive beauty supplies. Due to the lack of unified service standards, there is a significant variation in the prices quoted by different merchants for automotive beauty supplies decoration services. Industry insiders believe that only by establishing uniform service standards can the service pricing be standardized. If the automotive beauty supplies market becomes a market of excessive profits with unmeasurable service quality, consumers will eventually lose confidence in the beauty supplies decoration market.
