Lumia 1520 domestic model selling for 4999? Don't rejoice too soon.

by anonymous on 2013-11-16 15:28:02

Today, Nokia's large-screen flagship, the Lumia 1520, officially began taking pre-orders on its Tmall flagship store. The pre-order price is set at 4999 yuan, leading many to believe that the official price of the domestic version of this Nokia flagship has been confirmed at 4999 yuan. However, this assumption is incorrect.

In the latest pre-sale interface for the Lumia 1520, Nokia clearly marked in bold red font on the image that the actual selling price will be subject to the official announcement, with any excess refunded and any shortfall supplemented.

In other words, 4999 yuan is not the official final selling price of the Lumia 1520; it is merely a reference price. In the future, the price of the Lumia 1520 could be higher or lower than this amount.

Additionally, customers who place a pre-order are required to pay a deposit of 99 yuan, which will not be refunded if the order is canceled for personal reasons.

Nokia officially announced the price of the Lumia 1520 at 749 USD, which converts to approximately 4560 yuan. If we factor in domestic tax rates based on this price, the domestic price of the Lumia 1520 would exceed 5300 yuan. However, perhaps Nokia will surprise us with some unexpected pricing strategies.
