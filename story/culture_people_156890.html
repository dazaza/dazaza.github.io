Qianjiang Evening News: These days, famous scholar Yu Qiuyu has run into trouble again, being attacked by a blog post on the Internet. The author of the post is Xiao Xiaolin, an "old enemy" who once sued Yu Qiuyu for defamation. The article titled "The Truth Investigation of Yu Qiuyu's Fake Donation of 200,000 Yuan" was reprinted by many domestic blog websites and forums. The author claims that Yu Qiuyu's donation in the 2008 Wenchuan earthquake was a "fictitious donation." The author points out that Yu Qiuyu's donation did not exceed 60,000 yuan, and the promised construction of "Qiuyu Jiujiu Hope Primary School" and "99 Book Friends Hope Primary School" have had no news so far...

Last year, Shanghai even built a "Master Memorial Hall" for Yu Qiuyu. Ugly Man (the author) believes that a master must have a childlike heart, always keeping promises and achieving results. Otherwise, his image in people's hearts will be greatly discounted.

To be fair, even if Master Yu donated fifty or sixty thousand, it would be beyond reproach. The amount of donation is a manifestation of personal love and also the freedom of the master. No one can say anything about it. But why does Master Yu need to take advantage of this opportunity to gain fame and release some false information? Ugly Man thinks there may be several reasons.

Firstly, the master forgets easily. Nowadays, Master Yu is no longer the writer who silently strives for literature and art like he used to be, but a celebrity who is busy traveling around the world every day. With too many social engagements, he has long forgotten about his promise to donate.

Secondly, the wife controls too strictly. As everyone knows, Yu Qiuyu and Ma Lan are a loving couple who have rarely quarreled since their marriage. In such couples, it is generally the woman who makes the decisions. Therefore, it is very likely that Master Yu made a promise without getting the approval from his wife beforehand, which led to his idea being "vetoed." If this is the case, Master Yu is in an awkward situation this time, losing face.

Thirdly, Master Yu originally did not want to donate so much, but due to media pressure, he had to make a gesture. He thought that as time went by, everyone would gradually forget. Unexpectedly, his old rival Xiao Xiaolin has been paying attention to this matter. Anyway, if what Xiao Xiaolin said is true, Master Yu has really miscalculated this time.
