9 Secrets to Rapidly Improve Your Design Skills [Recommended]

by jason123 on 2009-10-22 12:21:40

To improve design skills, the premise is of course to master basic software technology and understand professional theoretical knowledge, etc. Below are nine secrets to quickly improve your design skills.

1. Think

Designers must first be good at thinking, have their own ideas and understanding of design, which is the most important. Don't worry if your ideas are "bad", no one can do it well at once, but you can definitely do well in your habits and attitudes. Learn to boldly explore and try your ideas, know how to draw on others, and pursue your own creativity. However, if you use templates or materials without thinking while designing, or even blindly imitate and plagiarize, that is simply destroying your creativity and nurturing your laziness, and you will never make progress. Moreover, it will be very difficult to make progress in the future.

2. Look

1) Appreciate a large number of other people's works, especially excellent works, cultivate artistic appreciation ability, which is necessary for improving design skills. When looking, don't skim through, need to combine professional knowledge with thinking while looking, carefully study the places worth learning in the works. But some people complain that they don't have many design books to look at, and it takes a lot of time and effort to find a large number of design works and resources online that they need. Here, it is recommended to find websites that gather all the excellent design resources on the Internet, such as Design World sheji360.net, which can save a lot of time and effort to improve learning efficiency.

2) Repeatedly appreciate excellent works. Reviewing old knowledge leads to new insights, and design is the same. Collect works that you think are better, and look at these works after a period of time, you will often gain more than the first time.

3) Often look at your own works. Often look at your past designed works, you will understand whether your level has improved, and at the same time, you will have more design experience.

3. Practice

Repeatedly practice a lot. If there is not enough practice, even if you have enough design talent, you cannot improve your design skills. Only by practicing continuously and repeatedly can you quickly improve your design ability. The more you do, the faster you improve. There is no shortcut here, you must work hard. Of course, if you don't think while practicing, it will only be half the result for twice the effort, just like learning to paint, you need to draw with a pen, but also need to think.

4. Review

Is your work that moves yourself a good work? In fact, being satisfied with your own work is not a good thing. Beginners often lack sufficient understanding of design and their own abilities, and often commit the "self-obsessed" problem, overly satisfied with their own designs, always thinking that their designs are perfect, there is nothing better, but they don't know how bad their designs are. On the contrary, when your level truly reaches a certain height, you will have a relatively objective and comprehensive understanding of your own design, and you won't easily be satisfied with your own design. Therefore, learn to question yourself, humbly ask others for advice, and you will avoid many detours. When you are very satisfied with your own design, remember to question yourself, is it really that good?

5. Ask

Learn to communicate, ask if you don't understand. Although most designers are not very outgoing, knowing how to communicate with others is still a course that every designer must master. Design is not done behind closed doors. Designers not only need to communicate with customers, but also need to communicate with colleagues and classmates, and of course need to use the Internet to communicate with other designers. Only designers who are good at communicating can improve themselves better and faster.

6. Know

1) Designers need to master relevant professional theoretical knowledge, read more professional theoretical knowledge to fill gaps. At the same time, they need to learn all kinds of knowledge. With rich knowledge, the "materials" in your brain will be more, they may all participate in your design, making your design vivid and full, rich in ideological connotation. Therefore, well-known design masters are often all-rounders, they are not only proficient in design, but also proficient in many other fields.

2) Designers need to understand life and understand the world. This requires actual investigation and personal experience. Otherwise, your design will easily be detached from life and lose its appeal, because design originates from life and serves life. At the same time, designers need to understand the world to gain more knowledge and keep up with the latest ideas. A designer who is backward in thought and ignorant, no matter how rich his design experience is, his works will always seem clumsy and vulgar.

Of course, to have rich knowledge and insight, you need to actively pay attention and accumulate in your daily life.

7. Diligence

Designers are not an easy profession, and they need to study hard on their own. Most excellent designers are "self-taught". If you don't take time to improve yourself actively in your spare time, it will be difficult to improve your design skills, and you may even fall behind. Therefore, it is suggested that you spend more time on design in your spare time for study or work, so that you can improve your design skills more quickly.

8. Morality

The moral quality of designers will not only be reflected in every detail of life, but also will seep into the design, which may be a deeper interpretation of "design for people". If a designer is narrow-minded in ideology and morality, then sooner or later it will kill his artistic cells and block his artistic path, because art is refined from life and is what people pursue in pure goodness and beauty. Designers should have a broad mind to have a broad design space and inspiration space, and your design path will go further and smoother.

9. Persistence

Design cannot succeed overnight, only by doing it for a long time can you have outstanding design skills and valuable design experience. Since you have chosen the profession of designer, you need to be patient and strive to do well. Although you will encounter a lot of hardships and difficulties, don't retreat or give up. Without these, how will your design skills grow, and how will you gain experience? So what you need to do is forget them, look forward, and confidently persevere. One day, your steps will step onto a smooth road, and at that time, success will be waving and smiling at you.
