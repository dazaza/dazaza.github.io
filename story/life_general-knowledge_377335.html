Scammers have many tricks up their sleeves, Tanggu sees a fashionable new scam_ Tianjin Culture_ Holiday Network

by huangjun187 on 2011-01-07 12:56:57

According to the deceived taxi driver Xiao Liu, on New Year's Day, when he was driving past the Tanggu Riverside area, a fashionable woman carrying many bags stopped him. After getting in the car, the woman asked Xiao Liu to take her to the Yaguan community in the Development Zone. When the taxi was driving to the Xinhua Road Flyover, the woman suddenly received a phone call. "Judging from the content of the call, it seemed like a friend needed to borrow money, but she said that she had just bought a lot of things and only had enough money left for the taxi fare," Xiao Liu said. After receiving the call, the woman started to ask him in a coquettish way if she could borrow 200 yuan first, and then pay him back when she got home. Xiao Liu saw that the woman had bought so many things and thought that she would not be the type to deceive people. "Moreover, she was helping her friend, which made me think she was very righteous." Subsequently, Xiao Liu did not hesitate at all and agreed to the woman's borrowing request.

After hearing Xiao Liu's consent, the woman expressed her gratitude several times and asked Xiao Liu to turn around and go to No.1 Road of the new port, saying that her friend was waiting there. Then Xiao Liu drove the woman to the destination and personally watched her hand over the 200 yuan to a man. Next, Xiao Liu chatted with the woman all the way to the entrance of Yaguan community. The woman asked Xiao Liu to wait for a moment at the entrance, saying that she would go home to get the money and then come back. In order to make Xiao Liu feel at ease, the woman placed two handbags she was holding inside the car, saying that there was a newly bought piece of clothing and a pair of shoes inside, worth more than 1000 yuan. However, after waiting for more than 20 minutes, Xiao Liu still did not see the woman's figure. Feeling something was amiss, Xiao Liu opened the handbag and found that there was actually an old pair of shoes that could no longer be worn and an old scarf inside. It was only at this point that Xiao Liu realized that he had been deceived by the fashionable woman, and lost the 200 yuan.
