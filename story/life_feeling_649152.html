Love, is not temptation, but warmth

The weather is cool, and the dust does not rise.

--Epigraph

I have always known that true love only flows warmly in the heart.

At this moment, basking in the warm sunshine, I quietly sink into that delicate heart, as if flying like a feather, gentle and lively.

I haven't figured out how to define love, but I vaguely know that the gradually approaching and intimate soft words may turn into a distant eternity in a blink of an eye.

After many years, especially at the end of the year, festivals are connected one after another, magnificent and prosperous. But I don't care or value the meaning of festivals to me. It's just the diversity or coldness that should be present in my own outdated life.

In my sight, I see passers-by who walk past me shoulder by shoulder, laughing and talking, stretching or extending their entire figures, making my nose sour and tears welling up in the corners of my eyes. But I stubbornly look up, watching the clouds lightly drifting in the sky, listening to the whispering wind, caring about the fleeting appearance of streams, trees, and grass...

I walk lightly in the season of falling leaves. Sometimes spinning, high then low; sometimes crawling on the ground, fallen then flying again... rising and falling repeatedly, as if dancing to the rhythm of music, like the graceful figure in the cooking smoke. In a daze, I seem to feel my body floating with my gaze, just like the elegant dance of the leaves. Carefully counting the time and the cycles given to me for thought and calmness.

In the age when I didn't understand love, I recklessly broke away from the tenderness of love, indulging in self-centeredness. In that era much earlier than today, having food served and clothes handed over was a luxury, yet in my youth, I insisted on not eating unless it suited my taste and not wearing unless it met my satisfaction. This pursuit was in line with personal standards. The summer vacation for farming was a period of labor training, yet in my youth, I found ways to idle around and chat aimlessly, pursuing absolute personal freedom. My parents' endless nagging often fell on deaf ears, as if their complaints were the most tragic excess in the world.

And that so-called self-centeredness was deeply imprinted in my parents' hearts, like a scar, without a single complaint. They worked tirelessly day after day, year after year, persisting without hesitation... Honestly, they didn't owe their children anything, yet they took responsibility without complaint. If they failed to meet their children's needs, they would blame themselves and sigh. They immersed and nurtured their children with the world's richest warmth and deep love every day through their actions, setting examples and investing genuine feelings. Even facing countless misunderstandings... they still held firm to their selves, unwavering!

In the prime of youthful ignorance, I casually resisted the warmth of love, cherishing fairy tales. Life was simple, with too much love coming too one-sidedly and plainly. At first, I really thought it was like an angel descending or a celestial being entering the world, as if we could understand each other and become perfectly attuned just by talking and playing... But time eventually scattered this acquaintance lacking brilliance. It wasn't about bad timing or lack of fate, but rather clinging to beauty and waiting for another splendid and dazzling meeting.

In the beautiful years, everyone has been respected and enjoyed a real past. There was once someone who paid without hesitation, holding an umbrella in the rain, shielding from the sun in the heat, anxiously searching when lost, joyfully circling when found... The deep affection and happiness of walking side by side. But even with the hope of melting hearts with warmth to fly together, it remained powerless. Unable to guess, in the magnified dreams, there was actually only moved, a luxurious disguise and bubble of happiness. Who would really treasure and collect this unexpected encounter that made one's heart ache and evoke compassion, yet was not blessed or cherished by the original intention!

In the truly loving years, knowing the warmth of mutual understanding in life, and rejecting extravagance from afar, just like a pure flower blooming in the secular world. Resting in prosperity, on the left sprouting greed, on the right stirring material desire, like the two ends of a balance. The fulcrum of balance - placed in composure. The weight of熏陶, added and subtracted, subtracted and added... In the repeated twists and turns, in the ups and downs of desires, besides gaining and losing, what else is there? And forgot the mirror-like lake of life, calm and shimmering with ripples. Like the flow of water, not hindered by mountains or blocked by stones, tranquil and adaptable, it still has a destination.

In the slow days, encounters don't mention the past or expect the future. It is the most fragrant and charming flower that blooms in anticipation, bright and profound. The familiar feeling in the faint smile, the purity flowing out of the light words, the tacit understanding conveyed silently by the eyebrows, the deep affection expressed in the singing voice, the harmony perceived in the sincere interests... Really, in the mutual gaze, the determination, confidence, goals, and future seen in each other's eyes... interpret all the oaths that make temptation pale. In the time that will be frozen in memory, even the sound of a leaf falling can hear the echo of the wind, so why bother to say more to describe the colorful years of accidental meetings!

Walking along and letting flashes of inspiration take root in the mind.

It seems that I am also starting to wait for something long-distant, not sure who it should be, maybe just a greeting.

Turn off the phone, isolate the irrelevant from life. The world remains the same.

In the life of singing, there is a shadow that only appears for brightness, listening intently to my clear melody, accompanying all the way!
