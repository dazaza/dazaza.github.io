He earns $40,000 a year from his online store without needing an inventory or managing sales.

by anonymous on 2011-03-28 10:17:39

So far, online shops have realized the dreams of most entrepreneurs. But there are still some online shop entrepreneurs who keep hesitating and dare not open a shop because they haven't fully understood this industry yet. Without trying, the chance of success is zero. Behind every successful person, there are unusual experiences. These people didn't know much or were good at it when they started their business either. They also knew that starting a business has risks, but they believed in themselves more. They believed that as long as they could make an effort, they would get something in return.

Zhang Kaixiang studied accounting in college. After graduation, he entered a company to work. Since he had just graduated, the company did not let him start working immediately but instead let him learn from the old employees for a period of time. Zhang Kaixiang thought it made sense. Although he studied this major, he hadn't practiced it yet and had no social experience. He should study well. Therefore, Zhang Kaixiang decided to humbly ask the old employees about work-related matters.

He thought his humble requests would get the old employees' detailed answers, but the reality wasn't as good as he imagined. When Zhang Kaixiang asked the old employees questions, they often casually fobbed him off with a few words, sometimes directly saying they were too busy to answer. Gradually, Zhang Kaixiang finally realized what was cruel, what was deception. What was intrigue. Gradually, Zhang Kaixiang lost confidence in his work. After three months of work, he learned nothing. He finally understood the difference between the strong and the weak in this society, and even more so, the pressure of life and the fierce competition among colleagues. Zhang Kaixiang made up his mind to resign and start a business, creating his own success.

Due to his own professional issues, it was extremely difficult for him to choose a project to start a business. He didn't know what to do. Later, he remembered that when he was in school, he often shopped online with his classmates. At that time, some classmates said they would open an online store after graduation. In the Internet era, many people start online stores, and many have achieved success and created miracles. Zhang Kaixiang then got the idea of opening an online store, which is also one of the entrepreneurial projects. After making a choice, Zhang Kaixiang began to understand the knowledge of online stores from various aspects. Guangzhou VIPshop Information Technology Co., Ltd. Zhang Kaixiang learned that there are many types of online stores, each with its own advantages and disadvantages. Finally, Zhang Kaixiang comprehensively considered his own factors: insufficient funds, lack of shop-opening experience, etc. He bought a set of cash-back online stores without inventory.

After opening the online store, Zhang Kaixiang began to operate it. Because the online store doesn't need inventory or sales management, Zhang Kaixiang saved a lot of trouble. From purchasing goods to providing after-sales service to customers, he didn't need to be busy anymore. Zhang Kaixiang's job was to promote and advertise. With the advice of promotion expert Mr. Bao, Zhang Kaixiang had a rough understanding of promotion. It was the soft article promotion commonly used by promotion experts on the Internet. Since Zhang Kaixiang's online store could help customers save money when shopping on major well-known websites, it was more convincing when promoting. He received his first order within just ten days of promotion. This made Zhang Kaixiang more determined to believe in his entrepreneurial confidence.

Writing articles for promotion is indeed very boring and tasteless. Zhang Kaixiang once wanted to give up. But every time he saw the income brought by writing articles, he decided to stick to it. Successful people are those who have the perseverance to persist until the end. Zhang Kaixiang persisted in promoting with one article per day, and he has been doing it until now. Two years of time have brought him very considerable harvests. In two years, Zhang Kaixiang earned a net profit of 80,000 yuan through online store entrepreneurship. Although this income is not as high as physical business, it is the income brought by Zhang Kaixiang writing only one article per day. Zhang Kaixiang is already very satisfied.

As a common saying goes, trying may not necessarily bring rewards, but not trying will definitely bring no rewards. Through practice, trying plus hard persistence are the most common experiences of successful people. In the e-commerce era, online stores are an excellent entrepreneurial platform.
