08 Korean Drama "Who Are You" Plot Introduction:

One body, inhabited by two souls—one is a cold-hearted entrepreneur, the other a passionate young man. Can a young man truly understand the deep and persistent love of a father? Borrowing another person's body just to spend the final 49 days with his daughter, the plot of the Korean drama "Who Are You" will once again touch the screen with its profound father-daughter bond.

Son Il-geon is a cold-hearted entrepreneur whose life was taken in an unexpected traffic accident. However, his soul still cannot let go of his daughter who is left alone in this world, so he borrows another person's body to accompany her. For three hours every day, during these final 49 days, he eats things he never used to eat and does things he once disdained—all to fulfill his daughter's wishes and express boundless paternal love within the limited time.

A young man, for three hours each day, has his body possessed by another soul, turning him into a completely different person who helps care for the other's daughter. But when he regains consciousness, what feelings will he have about the events that occurred?
