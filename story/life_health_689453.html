What is a unicornuate uterus? A unicornuate uterus forms when one paramesonephric duct develops fully, resulting in a well-developed single-horned uterus with a normally developed fallopian tube. The development of the opposite paramesonephric duct completely stops. The function of a unicornuate uterus may be normal.
