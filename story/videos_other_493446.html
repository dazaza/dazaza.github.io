How are you?

by sacxiaoyanl on 2011-09-22 17:13:51

In my childhood days, no one cared for me. Everyone thought that since I had no father or mother, I was good for nothing and would not amount to anything in the future. Only you never gave up on me, and you cared for me! Whenever other children had something, you would try your best to make sure I had it too. When I craved snacks and my grandmother refused to give them to me, you would secretly buy them for me and put them in my schoolbag halfway. You also often took me around everywhere. I followed you happily wearing shorts, and you never tired of me as long as I wasn't a burden to you!

The following days were really quiet and peaceful! I was very lazy, and in order to satisfy my laziness, I could even skip meals. But every day, you went to the market to buy good food and deliver it to me. You often cooked and prepared meals for me, and whatever you thought was delicious, you would save a portion for me. Even when you made soup, you would pack it in an insulated bucket and deliver it to me! Whatever I wanted to eat, you would buy it for me! Whenever you saw something beneficial to my health, no matter how far away it was, you would go and ask about it! As long as it was something I wanted, whether it was a table or cabinet at home or anything else, you let me take it before! Back then, we quietly hid in the room to eat egg tarts. Every time I sneaked some food into your pocket while grandma wasn't paying attention, I regretted not being able to give you anything back then!

That year, when I was less than a year old, I lived with you. Your heavy preference for boys over girls never showed on me. I believe this is fate! Since I was young, I have been in poor health, cried a lot, caused trouble, and didn't listen, giving you a lot of trouble!

Whenever I did something wrong, you never scolded me. Instead, you would teach me patiently. Even when you knew I was wrong, you still stood by me. You said you were my shelter, and whenever I felt wronged or tired outside, I could come back and rest by your side. With you there, I always had a place in the family! Whether I disappeared or got lost, you always cared for me and protected me differently!

After my business failure, my mood couldn't be restored for a long time. Upon knowing the truth of my failure, my heart turned cold like ashes. Under your guidance, I moved back to live with you. Seeing your white hair and your difficulty moving due to a stroke, I felt so guilty. In a blink of an eye, I had been away for so many years, leaving you lonely for such a long time! Watching you drag out a pile of food left for me from under the bed, my heart warmed up. In order to be capable of taking care of you in the future and repay your love, at that moment, I swore to start anew.

I deeply remember that when I was little, every time someone bullied me or hurt me, you always ran to the school to settle accounts with them and argued with others! Watching your figure and listening to your teachings made me learn to be strong, learn not to care about others' cold stares, learn to prove myself with certificates of merit, and learn to walk proudly on my own path! Learn to live well and not bring shame to you!

When the tree wants to be still but the wind won't stop, and when the child wants to support but the parents are gone! Death is so cruel, there's no "what if." There's not even a chance to correct mistakes! But if there is another life, I will still be your granddaughter, living happily with you. Sitting by your bedside listening to your stories, supporting you as we walk down the street, playing chess together in our free time, and laughing happily together. Cherishing every minute and second with you, I won't be reckless and mischievous like before, making you sad, upset, or angry! Beloved grandpa, I will always remember you, miss you, and may you rest in peace!

When I returned to that familiar yet strange home and looked at your portrait, I could no longer hear your voice or see your figure. I could only sit in the chair you used to sit in and quietly recall your kindness in the past. My heart hurts so much, but I have to endure it. I cannot show it in front of grandma because I know that after you're gone, she is the one you worry about the most. So, it is my responsibility to take care of grandma. Although I can't replace you in taking care of her, as long as I am capable, I will certainly respect grandma! Please forgive me, I really don't dare to stay there longer because I can't bear the pain of missing you! Therefore, I avoided visiting your grave during the Qingming Festival and only burned some paper money for you from afar. Even on this 14th day of the seventh lunar month, I avoided facing it! Until now, I can't accept that you are forever gone, and until now, I feel like you are still here! Every time I prepare to call you, when I open the phone, I realize there is no line that can connect to you!

Later, I went to Hubei and Beijing, and you supported me so much while worrying about me. No matter how bad your health was, you always said everything was fine on the phone! By the time I returned from Beijing, you had added more white hair! I was too selfish, too unaware of cherishing time, and too unaware of spending good time with you properly—it was all my fault!

Every time Mom did something harmful to me, although you couldn't be physically present to solve the problem, you called her countless times and scolded her harshly. You strongly urged me to leave and abandon them! I know that besides you, no one cares about my feelings and future. When I was forced to attempt suicide, you received the call thinking it was Mom who attempted suicide, and the phone was filled with curses wishing her dead early. When you realized it was my self-harm, your tone immediately softened, filled with tenderness and pain, and you even fainted from high blood pressure immediately. It's my unfilialness; I apologize again!

Until now, I believe that the last time I saw you, you knew your life was nearing its end. You urged me to return quickly, work hard, cherish myself, and live well. But I never imagined that this farewell would be eternal—it's all because I was too stupid! Hearing the news of your passing, I collapsed! I was so sorrowful, feeling like my heart was dying. Rolling and crawling, crying and shouting, but I couldn't see you one last time because the doctor said that even a bit of running around could endanger my life. During those days, I ate as much as I could and took care of myself as much as possible, hoping to recover quickly and visit you sooner, but it was already too late! Then I realized how painful it is to lose a loved one!

It has been half a year since you left, but your kindness and love remain vivid in my mind. Especially in recent days, missing you has become an indispensable part of my life. The longing is no longer limited to the festivals related to honoring you; it could be a scene from a TV drama or even some words that make me think of you, causing waves of emotions, regret, and tears.

Grandpa, are you doing okay? Missing you is like a flood of water, overwhelming me. Each time I think of you, my throat tightens, and my heart begins to ache. Regret, self-blame, and guilt overwhelm me and linger for a long time. Though I know my clumsy writing cannot express everything, and words seem powerless in front of the most sincere emotions, I can no longer speak to you personally. Please allow me to use my inferior words to express my longing and regret...
