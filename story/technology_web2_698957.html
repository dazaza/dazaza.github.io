As winter approaches, experts have designed a set of exercises for male partners of all ages to strengthen their strength, enhance lung function, and maintain limb agility. Everyone can exercise according to this plan, three times a week, each time 45-60 minutes. If you persist, you won't have to worry about poor physical condition and obesity.

1. Around 30 years old. People at this age group have surpassed their physical peak. At this time, if you neglect physical exercise, the oxygen intake that is very important for endurance will gradually decrease. But don't be afraid, because you are still young. At this point, the joints often make some noises, which is a sign of joint disease. In order to keep the joints flexible, you should do more stretching exercises and pay attention to cardiovascular system exercises.

Three times a week, every other day, each time for cardiovascular system exercise (jogging or swimming) for 5-30 minutes, the intensity is not as strong as at the age of 20. 20 minutes of strength training, compared with the age of 20, the weight to try is lighter, but the number of repetitions can be more. 5-10 minutes of stretching exercises, focusing on back and leg muscles. People who sit in the office for a long time should pay more attention to stretching exercises. The method is: lie on your back, try to lift both knees to the chest, and hold for 30 seconds; lie on your back, raise both legs separately, try to lift them as high as possible, and hold for 30 seconds.

2. What to do after 40? Compared with the age of 20, people over the age of 40 have reduced muscle trainability by 25%, physical strength gradually decreases, muscles shrink year by year, and the body begins to gain weight. Therefore, people over the age of 40 choose sports not only to maintain a good figure, but also to prevent common geriatric diseases such as hypertension and cardiovascular disease.

Twice a week on Monday and Friday, including: 25-30 minutes of cardiovascular exercise, medium intensity, such as jogging, swimming, cycling, etc. For people over 50, the pulse should not exceed 130-140 beats per minute. 10-15 minutes of equipment exercise, the equipment weight should be lighter than at the age of 30, too heavy weight will harm health, but the number of repetitions can be more. To avoid accidents, it is best not to use dumbbells, but fitness machines. 5-10 minutes of stretching exercises, especially paying attention to exercising all joints and those muscles that are prone to shrinkage. Add one 45-minute strength training session on Wednesday without using equipment, push-ups, half squats, etc., repeat multiple sets, about 20 times per set, the quantity depends on individual reception capacity.

Recommended sports: tennis, long-distance skiing, swimming, jogging, golf, dancing, walking.

New fitness method: skin friction

In the past, some people used dry cloth to rub the skin, or cold water to rub the body to prevent colds and fitness. With the continuous development of medical technology, such fitness methods seem to have been forgotten by people. However, after someone asked some people who had been rubbing for a long time, they were surprised: almost all of the 200 elderly people who insisted on rubbing looked energetic and full of energy. They were on average 6-7 years younger than those who did not rub at the same age, almost no colds, and even showed a kind of enviable youthful vitality.

According to preliminary investigations, from a psychological point of view, rubbing exercises are scientific.

First, vigorous rubbing of the skin can dilate the capillaries of the skin and accelerate blood circulation.

Secondly, rubbing can promote metabolism like exercise, so that waste in the body can be excreted as soon as possible.

There are many ways to exercise the skin by rubbing. Rubbing the whole body with a dry towel is called "dry cloth rubbing"; rubbing with a soft brush is called "brush rubbing"; rubbing with a wrung-out cold towel is called "cold water rubbing"; In addition, the "hand rubbing method" can also be adopted. Generally speaking, the effect of "cold water rubbing" is better than "dry cloth rubbing". But no matter what method is adopted, it should be gradual, and in winter, attention should be paid to avoid catching a cold. And when doing friction, the speed should be fast, first light then heavy, gradually increasing the force. Friction exercise is the same as other exercise methods, and perseverance is needed to adhere to it for a long time to achieve the purpose of fitness.

New fitness idea: "bionic" exercise

In winter, many people love to stay in bed. In fact, there are several animal-simulating fitness exercises that can be done in bed:

Imitate cat arching: After waking up every morning, lie on your stomach, spread your hands, stretch and close your legs, and stick up your buttocks, just like a cat arching its back. Repeat more than ten times, which can promote the smooth flow of qi and blood throughout the body and prevent and treat lower back pain and other diseases.

Imitate dog walking: Walk like a dog, put your limbs on the ground, stretch your right hand and left foot, left hand and right foot out to move the body forward. Persist in walking 20 steps every day, which can prevent waist pain, stomach prolapse, hemorrhoids and swelling of the lower limbs caused by standing or walking for a long time, especially for preventing waist pain.

Imitate locust legs: Lie face down, bend your elbows, and place your hands on the bed under your chest. Then lean back, keep your feet together and try to raise them as high as possible, slowly perform 3 abdominal breathing, several times a day. This action, imitating the locust's legs, is especially suitable for women.

Imitate camel yoga: This is a yoga posture imitating the steps of a camel. First, put your hands on your waist, kneel on the ground, and then gradually lean your upper body backward. When it is almost impossible to support, hold your ankles with both hands and maintain this backward posture, repeat abdominal breathing 3 times. This method allows the thigh and abdominal muscles to get sufficient exercise, prevents fat deposition, and is beneficial for weight loss. In addition, because the abdomen is tightened, it affects the intestines and has obvious effects on preventing constipation.

Good winter fitness method: cold air bath

The currently popular cold air bath in Japan is a good winter fitness method. There are two ways to take a cold air bath in the world:

1. Specialized air baths: Fitness enthusiasts wear shorts and short pants, take air baths outdoors or in well-ventilated indoor areas, and generally combine appropriate sports activities.

2. Air bathing during daily life and labor: Fitness enthusiasts can use the way to work or school, try to wear relatively thin clothes, and take cold air baths.

Many research results show that the impact of cold air on the human body itself is a good fitness exercise method. Using the temperature difference between the air and the skin temperature, strengthen the regulation activity of the body temperature center, make the skin blood vessels contract, reduce the skin blood flow and sweat gland secretion, improve the excitement and contraction ability of the muscles; the temperature of the internal organs also rises accordingly, increase blood circulation, effectively improve its functional condition. Therefore, after taking a cold air bath, you feel good, work efficiency improves, and resistance to colds and infectious diseases is enhanced.

Of course, if the method is improper, low temperatures can cause local frostbite and overall hypothermia. Long-term exercise (such as long-distance running, etc.) in cold conditions can lose too much body heat, and symptoms such as dizziness, decreased coordination ability, unsteady gait, or even wanting to lie down may appear. Based on the above reasons, several issues should be noted when taking a cold air bath:

1. When starting cold air bath exercises, start with cool air baths (15°C to 20°C), get used to them, and gradually transition to cold air baths.

2. Control the duration of the cold air bath according to individual differences, generally not causing chills and shivering.

3. The best time for a cold air bath is when the sun shines, when the air is slightly warm and contains ultraviolet rays, which can be combined with sunlight baths.

4. In windy and extremely cold weather, sports warm-up activities should be carried out to prevent excessive drop in body temperature.

5. Intense exercise afterwards, windy or foggy weather, unhealed skin wounds or open injuries, women during menstruation or late pregnancy should not take cold air baths.

6. When exercising in a cold air bath, pay attention to self-perception and weight changes frequently. If self-perception is bad or weight loss occurs, stop exercising immediately.

In addition to choosing a cold air bath for exercise in winter, jogging, jumping aerobics, practicing Tai Chi, indoor equipment exercises, winter swimming, etc. are all suitable exercise methods.
