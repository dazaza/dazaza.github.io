Six Precautions for Autumn Facial Plastic Surgery

As the autumn breeze blows, you who love beauty may have long wanted to undergo facial plastic surgery or nose job. With the cool wind blowing and the National Day holiday approaching, it's a good time. But don't rush! First, take a look at the six precautions for autumn facial plastic surgery compiled especially for you by our editor.

1. Choose a正规 hospital

Facial contour modification is a highly challenging plastic surgery procedure. Currently, there are relatively few plastic surgery hospitals in China that can carry out this operation, mainly concentrated in larger specialized hospitals or tertiary hospitals...

2. Choose an experienced doctor

Selecting a plastic surgeon with a solid foundation in plastic surgery can greatly reduce the error rate. Tell your doctor your requirements, occupation, hobbies, and your standard of beauty. This is very important because everyone has different views on beauty. Good communication can reduce postoperative disputes between doctors and patients.

3. Take an accurate 3D picture

This is crucial for the extent of bone cutting during the operation. Before the operation, a good doctor will carefully study the distribution of your facial contour bones and muscles. Some medical institutions only remove or grind part of the mandibular angle while lacking comprehensive design for the relationship between the chin, jawline, and mandibular angle, resulting in not obvious changes in face shape and lower satisfaction rates after the operation. Currently, some hospitals conduct three-dimensional digital overall design for the chin, jawline, and mandibular angle before the operation, significantly improving the success rate, satisfaction rate, and postoperative effects of the operation.

4. Comprehensive preoperative examination, especially blood typing

Hospitals prepare blood for every patient before the operation to prevent possible major bleeding during the operation and ensure the life safety of each patient.

5. Avoid menstrual period

Because surgery during the menstrual period may cause excessive swelling or major bleeding.

6. Have a good plastic surgery mindset

1. Postoperative swelling is unavoidable

Swelling is an inevitable reaction to surgery, and the speed of swelling subsiding varies depending on individual constitution.

2. Asymmetrical face shape

The modification of face shape mainly depends on the rich clinical experience of the physician to grasp the degree of removal of bones, muscles, and fat. Sometimes, due to avoiding the patient's specific location vessels or other conditions, there may be slight differences in the bilateral bone cutting. This can be adjusted with minor surgery.

3. Let friends and family adapt to the new face

Some patients originally feel good after the operation, but when friends say they like their past appearance, they gradually begin to doubt the effect of the surgery. Therefore, it is necessary to repeatedly communicate with the patient before the operation to adjust their mindset.
