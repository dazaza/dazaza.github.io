by meimei234 on 2012-03-05 12:58:15

Love with happiness, joy, pain and sorrow

Why am I so worried?

Why am I afraid?

I don't know what's wrong,

But I always feel that you have changed.

Is it my suspicion or have you really changed?

Your words make me feel there is a barrier between us.

I feel a bit helpless because I don't understand you.

I don't know what you're thinking.

I also don't know if you still care about me.

But I place you in the spot closest to my heart.

Will you forget me after some time?

Nowadays, we seem to have nothing to talk about.

Every time I call you, we can't talk for more than 3 minutes.

You tell me you don't know what to say.

It doesn't feel good when I hear that.

Maybe you don't know how it feels,

But it really makes me uncomfortable.

You say since we see each other every day, what is there to talk about?

Is that really what you think?

Every day when you come home, you go online and chat with your friends and colleagues.

I'm right behind you, but it seems like you can't even see me.

It's as if you ignore my existence and treat me like I'm transparent.

Perhaps your friends are more important than me.

Perhaps you find it annoying to be with me every day,

So you don't want to look at me or talk to me.

Do you really think that just because we see each other every day, everything is fine?

Do you really think that we talk a lot every day?

Do you think that just seeing me every day is enough?

Do you assume that because I don't express my pain, I don't feel anything?

Do you think my presence or absence makes no difference?

I don't know why things are like this.

But every time I call you,

I feel like you're making excuses and finding reasons.

I feel so foolish and stupid.

Sometimes your words are like a knife.

I don't know where I stand in your heart,

But I know that now,

I don't even occupy a small corner in your heart.

Every day when you finish work, I rush down first,

Like a little kid.

But the expression on your face when you see me

Seems like you don't even want to see me.

Maybe I am nothing now.

Maybe I no longer have the qualification to be in your heart.

Maybe I don't even have that qualification.

Perhaps you've found your own love outside,

While I wait for you at home foolishly,

Thinking that you'll be happy to see me first when you come home every day.

Maybe I'm wrong.

Maybe you've found a girl more suitable for you outside.

Every day I ask you these questions, but you don't answer,

And you don't say anything. I don't know what it means.

But at least I understand that you have the thought of wanting to find another girl {maybe I'm wrong}.

I don't want to ask too much, I don't want to argue.

My sister asked me,

"Are you living well?"

I said, "Yes, very well."

"You treat me very well."

My sister asked me again,

"Do you feel happy now?"

I said, "Yes, very happy."

In fact, everything is not as good as I said.

I don't want my sister to worry, I don't want my dad to know,

Because I'm afraid you'll overthink it.

I also want to ask you,

"Do you treat me well? Do you think I'm happy?"

"Have you kept your promise to me?"

"Am I important to you?"

I really want to ask you all these questions, but I'm afraid you'll be unhappy.

I'm afraid you'll get angry, so I haven't said many things out loud.

There are so many things bottled up inside me,

And I can hardly bear them anymore, but there's no one to share the burden with me.

I'm all alone, waiting foolishly.

Often when you chat happily with others {regardless of whether they are boys or girls},

You ignore my existence.

When you take photos, you don't want me in them.

You casually ask me to move aside.

But do you notice me?

Probably not, because I'm so insignificant.

Every day is like this.

As for now, all I can say is helplessness.

We are still too young to understand the feelings of happiness, joy, pain, and sorrow.
