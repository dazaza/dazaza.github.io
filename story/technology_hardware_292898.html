(7) Before you kiss me —— The rich son falls in love with me, a young widow. Watching the police car drive away, I shook my head and sighed. Actually, when people can't bear the pressure, they often have suicidal thoughts, but those thoughts only last for a moment, like a small cluster of fireworks growing in the dark night. If someone nearby can timely extinguish them, many tragedies will not happen.

I accidentally looked at the time; my God, it's already 10 o'clock, I'm late. Buddha bless me, considering I've saved a life, please don't let the people from the investment company blame me.

However, I still got rejected. Operations Director Lin Mo Feng had already gone out to handle some affairs. His assistant, with a professional smile on her face, coldly said: "Sorry, Director Lin has instructed that we won't accept the superstar's proposal."

No matter how I explained, the assistant refused to compromise and accept the proposal. I became anxious and sat on the sofa. I told the assistant: "Then I'll wait here until Director Lin comes back, and I'll explain to him in person."

The assistant panicked, not expecting me to be so stubborn. She had no choice but to surrender: "Then leave the proposal here! I will forward it."

Walking out of the investment company building, I took a deep breath. At this moment, the sun was shining brightly, and there were crowds of people on the street. Walking under the sun, I felt as insignificant as a speck of dust. I am a small person whose fate is controlled by others. A single look or command from others can make me run around.

Realizing the cruel social reality clearly, what could I do? I still couldn't escape my humble destiny. I am just a tiny screw in this huge social gear. The gear may not need me, but I cannot detach myself from the gear, otherwise, I would even lose the qualification to be a screw.

When I returned to the company, I was immediately met with a harsh scolding from supervisor Liang Lan. Her sharp voice pierced through the doors and echoed in the hall.

"Director Lin called, saying to cancel the cooperation. Kang Nan, deal with it yourself. You were supposed to be there at 10 o'clock, why were you late? Was it intentional? Do you not want to work anymore?... ..."

I lowered my head and silently endured her sharp rebuke, trying hard to control my tears of委屈. I knew that even if I explained that I was late because I saved someone and delayed delivering the proposal, even if they believed me, they would still blame me for being nosy. This city of steel and concrete has hardened people's hearts. Everyone only cares about their own little sky above their heads, who would care about the thousands of snow outside other people's doors?
