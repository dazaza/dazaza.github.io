"Holding hands and looking at each other, to the ends of the earth"

Written by Zi Mo Wu Die

I am a woman who is very attached to home. I like the peaceful life when all family members are together. The impression of the Spring Festival has always been very strong, and I have never spent the Spring Festival away from my parents. However, when I fell in love with this man who loves to travel around the world, when he promised to take me to all corners of the earth, even though I had not fully recovered from a cold, and even though the call of the New Year was getting closer and closer, I still followed him with our son to Sanya to find the warmest Spring Festival.

When I stepped on the land of Sanya, my eyes could hardly keep up. The coconut trees on both sides of the street touched my heart. Their lush leaves danced: spring has come, we carry hope and run towards spring together! I, from the north, was attracted by the scenery of the south, and my steps couldn't walk fast either. When I came from a frozen north where all flowers were withered to this sunny and beautiful south, the difference in weather was hard to adapt to immediately. It's not spring here, it's obviously a hot summer! So I changed into a long skirt, beach shoes from the clothes I brought, and wore a sun hat and sunglasses. Summer is truly here!

When we arrived at Yalong Bay, the azure sea and the soft sandy beaches made me change into my swimsuit and run barefoot on the sand. Diving into the sea, tasting a sip of seawater, it was so salty. The sunshine, the beach, the sea, the Dai Mountain, and the coconut trees are all so real in front of me. The warm seawater of the South Sea embraced me, a woman from afar. Swimming in the sea, I felt like in a dream, unreal. There were many tourists on the beach. Beautiful women passed by with fragrance. In this stunning realm, I listened to the sound of the waves hitting the coast, lost in deep thought. Sitting on the beach, I buried my legs in the fine sand. Under the sun umbrella, I saw my husband and son playing in the sea, feeling incredibly warm. Only water is so devoted, only the sea has such a broad heart. As long as we come, the sea will welcome us there!

Then we visited the Daxiaodongtian scenic area, the Nanshan scenic area, and the Wuzhizhou Island Resort Center. I saw the picturesque scenery of Sanya, with green mountains and blue waters, lush vegetation, telling the unique poetic charm of the south. Of course, because the Spring Festival is the peak tourist season in Sanya, all attractions were crowded with people. Waiting for sightseeing vehicles under the sun took about an hour. Even using the restroom required queuing up in long lines, the flow of people was amazing. The things inside the scenic spots were expensive too. Coconuts that cost five yuan outside the scenic spot were fifteen yuan inside, and a simple meal could cost hundreds of yuan. But coming out for a vacation is all about the mood, so we indulged once. Therefore, in every scenic spot, we tasted the local seasonal fruits, satisfying our cravings.

The place "Ends of the Earth" that I had envisioned countless times in my dreams, we finally got to be there. The Love Square, a romantic place, attracted tourists from all over to stop and take commemorative photos. The Love Stone, two heart-shaped stones symbolizing the moon and the sun, carries a tragic love story. These interdependent stones infected us, making us ordinary men and women involuntarily hold our lovers' hands tightly, look at each other holding hands, no matter how far apart, never leaving or abandoning each other. The greening in Sanya is impeccable, with coconut trees planted every few steps along the coastline, boundless seas, variously posed tourists taking photos, and huge gray stones carved with the words "Ends" and "of the Earth", meaning the edge of the sky and the end of the sea. At this edge of the sky and corner of the sea, our family of three wove our beautiful dreams. From now on, no matter what happens, because we have already accompanied each other to the ends of the earth, our hearts will forever be together!

On the last day, we went to the Deer Looking Back Mountain Park and the Tropical Paradise Forest Park. The Tropical Paradise Forest Park was the filming location for "If You Are the One 2", where Ge You's villa was located. However, due to overcrowding, the scenic spot limited ticket sales, and with the sea of people, the visit inside was shortened. The villa area was closed to visitors due to too many tourists, and we could only overlook it from the viewing platform, which left some regret. The tropical rainforest lives up to its name. Without taking the cable car and hiking up the mountain on foot, appreciating the lush trees and vegetation of the south, hearing the chirping of birds, holding hands with my husband, and our child happily jumping ahead, this was truly a fairyland on earth, with bright spring scenery, no matter how far the road, love goes just as far!

No matter how far the road is, there is a kind of love that follows closely and accompanies one to the ends of the earth. Beautiful Sanya, romantic corner of the sea, although we only traveled for five days, the memories are set, and I have no regrets in this life, because I have been here.
