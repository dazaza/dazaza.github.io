Taokae, taoking out the wonderful, taoking out the fun! Mytaoke.com

by yinyin on 2007-06-01 16:21:18

Simply put, Taokewang is an information discovery and sharing interaction platform. On this website platform, everyone can together discover, share, and "search" for some interesting and valuable information on the Internet. This mass participation and experience-sharing method has become popular on the American Internet.

Of course, this is not all of Taokewang; there's even cooler stuff...

Favorites and Discovery:

If you are still using the Favorites function on your local computer to save articles, it seems a bit "outdated." Try Taokewang, and you'll be pleasantly surprised. Here, you can easily save web pages and share them with friends. Once you try it, you'll understand its "advanced" and "user-friendly" features.

Blog Interoperability:

When your friend's blog updates, whether it's Sohu Blog, Sina Blog, or other blogs... Taokewang can promptly detect the update and notify you, presenting all updates on one page. You no longer need to visit each blog individually - isn't that convenient? You can also import your own blog into Taokewang, allowing friends to conveniently read your blog. Even cooler, different websites' blogs can interconnect here!

Building a Personal Portal:

Perhaps you have a Sohu blog, a Flickr photo album, Douban book reviews, Google Reader shares, and online bookmarks... All of these can be aggregated onto one page on Taokewang and published externally. At the same time, your publicly shared blog posts, saved articles, and photo albums will automatically be added to Taokewang's ranking recommendation system, allowing more people to read and get to know you, which can also improve your search rankings.

Sharing My Joys:

Sharing your discoveries with friends is a joyful thing. When you see a good article, a great picture, or write a blog post, don't you want to share it with your friends right away? Then quickly join Taokewang with your friends! This place is prepared for those who know how to enjoy life!

In short, Taokewang is designed for those who want to save time, stay cool, love to share happiness, and are a little "lazy"!
