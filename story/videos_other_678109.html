Man takes one-third of his salary to form a volunteer association for voluntary services

by wjwf8179 on 2012-02-27 11:44:59

Every weekend and holiday, he is active in the nursing home, elderly care center, and cerebral palsy children rehabilitation center. He can also be seen on the mobile blood donation bus. Sometimes he is a volunteer for large events or a friend to impoverished students... In the eyes of the people of Suining in Xuzhou, Wang Yong, a young man from the 80s generation, is the "Lei Feng" by their side.

□ Fast Reporter Zhong Qian, Text/Photography

The loving young man established a volunteer association

Spending one-third of his salary on volunteering, earnestly persuading others to join

"Hello, this is Wang Yong from the Volunteer Association. Today we are going to the nursing home to take care of personal hygiene for the elderly. Please don't forget to bring nail clippers, combs, and towels. We will gather at Tianyuan Square at eight o'clock." "Hello, this is Wang Yong from the Volunteer Association. Today we plan to visit Grandma Gu, the widow of a martyr"... Every morning on weekends, Wang Yong, the founder and captain of the volunteer association, gets up early, tidies up cleaning tools and love materials, then calls volunteers to gather and serve the elderly in need.

At first, not many people were willing to join the activities of the volunteer association, only a few good friends of Wang Yong participated. However, there are many people around who need help, and individual power is limited. To recruit more people, Wang Yong began to persuade people around him. Whenever he had time, he would introduce the situation of those in difficulty to his relatives and friends. But when many young people heard that they had to give up their rest days to participate in volunteer activities, they hesitated. Hearing that they even had to wash clothes and change diapers for the elderly, they were even less willing to go. After learning that all the expenses for volunteer services were borne by individuals, some even called Wang Yong a fool and suspected him of having ulterior motives.

At that time, Wang Yong worked at the Suining Mobile Company. Out of a monthly income of less than 2000 yuan, he spent one-third on volunteer services. Even his mother expressed incomprehension towards her son's actions. But Wang Yong persisted in his actions, warming those in need with his love. "Even if I am the only one, I will organize the Suining Volunteer Service Team to serve those in need." Recalling those days, Wang Yong still firmly repeated his words at that time. Now, through his practical actions, he has inspired many colleagues and friends and gained support from many netizens online. The volunteer association team has gradually grown stronger. "Last year, a 60-year-old retired teacher from the town learned about our activities online and cycled 20 kilometers to participate in volunteer services, which strengthened my determination to continue."

Caring for the lonely elderly for four consecutive years

Cutting nails, cleaning ears, and changing clothes for the elderly, he never complains about the dirt or fatigue

Last Saturday, I followed Wang Yong and the volunteers of the Volunteer Association to the geriatric care center in Suining County. Most of the elderly in the care center are bedridden, either completely paralyzed or semi-paralyzed. Seeing the volunteers, the elderly became energetic and laughed heartily. The volunteers divided into groups: the companionship group pushed the elderly in wheelchairs to bask in the sun and chat; the cleaning group helped the elderly wash clothes, bedding, and wipe tables; the supplies group orderly distributed fruits, pastries, and adult diapers to the elderly; the food group helped the cafeteria wash vegetables and cook to change the taste for the elderly.

Every time the 76-year-old Huang Yulan saw Wang Yong, she was very excited and said that Wang Yong was a filial child. The elderly woman suffered from rheumatoid arthritis, and Wang Yong always massaged her legs. "It's still cold these two days, you must not take off your clothes, you still need to keep warm." The 80-year-old Chen Lanhua was another person Wang Yong visited every time. Although she had undergone cataract surgery, her vision was still poor. Each time, Wang Yong specially trimmed her nails and cleaned her ears. Wang Yong also brought an MP3 player to play old songs for her.

Ms. Liu had been in the care center for more than half a month and was always melancholy. Wang Yong asked her and learned that the elderly woman suffered from heart disease and myocardial obstruction. Her son and daughter were working outside and worried about her being alone at home, so they sent her to the care center for recuperation. However, after more than half a month, the elderly woman had not adapted to the new life and missed her family and loved ones. Wang Yong consoled the elderly woman, "Auntie, look, there are so many of your relatives here. Everyone comes to visit you and helps you recover. If you have any worries, tell us. Consider us as your son." Under Wang Yong's guidance, the elderly woman gradually integrated into the big family.

When Wang Yong learned that an elderly woman who had been paralyzed for many years returned home due to inability to pay medical fees and could not receive rehabilitation treatment, Wang Yong and his team quickly raised some money, contacted the elderly woman's family, and coordinated with the care center to bring the elderly woman back. Wang Yong did not mind the dirt or fatigue, picking up the elderly woman to change her clothes and bedding. The elderly woman placed her hands on Wang Yong's shoulders, tears streaming down her face, and mumbled the word "son".

Helping brain-damaged children with rehabilitation exercises

Not only that, but they also established a scholarship fund to help poor students realize their university dreams

Next to the geriatric care center was a rehabilitation center for brain-damaged children, where the volunteers of the Volunteer Association often went to help the brain-damaged children with rehabilitation exercises. Due to congenital cerebral palsy, these children cannot walk, talk, eat normally, and even experience incontinence. The volunteers of the Volunteer Association first learned the techniques of rehabilitation exercises from doctors and then took the children to the activity room for corrective exercises. These children rarely had friends, and over time, Brother Wang Yong became their best friend. Playing games with them, teaching them to speak and read books. This New Year, the Volunteer Association specifically planned a party for the children, buying balloons and ribbons to decorate the activity room, bringing the children into a joyful and warm atmosphere to sing and dance, welcoming the New Year together.

At the end of the party, Wang Yong and the volunteers donated heating materials to the rehabilitation center. The director, moved, held Wang Yong's hand and said, "If there were more young people like you with love in society, it would be great!" A six-year-old brain-damaged child hugged Wang Yong's leg and said, "Uncle Wang, the party was like this, so beautiful. Will you come next year?" "Yes, we will definitely come next year." This was Wang Yong's promise to the six-year-old brain-damaged child, and also the encouragement he gave himself and his teammates.

Since October 2007, Wang Yong and his Volunteer Association have been volunteering for more than four years. Each time, more than 30 fixed volunteers participate in the activities, and over four years, more than 500 people have participated in volunteer services. Last year, Wang Yong and the Volunteer Association also established a love scholarship fund to help poor students realize their university dreams. Now, many people in Suining know the contact information of the Volunteer Association volunteers. They seek help from the "Volunteer Association" when they encounter difficulties and want to help others through the "Volunteer Association".

Reporter's Notes

The "Lei Feng" Around Us

Can Also Be a Fashionable Young Man

Wang Yong, the initiator of the "Suining Volunteer Association," is a young man from the 80s generation. When I met him for the first time, I was somewhat surprised, unable to believe that such a tall, slim, handsome, and lively young man was the volunteer who went weekly to cut nails, wash bedding, and feed medicine to paralyzed elderly people. It wasn't until I followed him to the geriatric care center in Suining County and heard the name of the elderly woman lying in bed calling his name that I truly believed Wang Yong was a regular visitor here, a common relative of the elderly here.

Helping the elderly, disabled, students, poverty alleviation, charity performances, voluntary blood donations... This public welfare enthusiast, who was an excellent volunteer in college, fully utilized his strength after entering society, using online forums, microblogging interactions to record each volunteer activity, widely recruiting volunteers, gathering more love to help those in need around him. Under Wang Yong's organization and influence, all his colleagues from the 80s generation joined his volunteer service organization, and their unit became the strong backing of the Volunteer Association.

In fact, Wang Yong also likes surfing the internet, exercising, and often takes his parents out hiking. He is an ordinary young man from the 80s generation, but he just has more perseverance and love, always remembering to save a part of his money, starting from small things, helping those in need around him. As long as you are willing to start from small things and persistently help others, you can also become the Lei Feng in people's hearts.
