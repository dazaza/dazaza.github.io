Now, what kind of talent does society really need? This is worth our deep reflection. From the data provided by Shanghai Desou Advertising Company, we learned that 95% of the employees in the company have not received university education, but the company has become one of the top agents of Alibaba in Shanghai. What does this indicate? The HR manager of the company, Han Lu, said that academic qualifications are not the first priority. Many high school graduates from computer training schools have solid and rich computer knowledge, strong practical skills, and are more capable than college students. We are more willing to cooperate with such people.

In order to further understand this issue, the author also visited Hangzhou Hengsheng Graphic Co., Ltd. Ms. Lin Shaofang, the human resources director of the company, also expressed her views on this matter: [Our company has more than twenty direct stores, and the demand for personnel is very large, but universities do not have corresponding majors to cultivate talents. On the contrary, some high school students who have undergone specialized training, especially computer training, have a clearer understanding of the industry and very proficient skills. We need talents who can start work immediately, so we warmly welcome high school students who understand emerging industries.]

From here, we can see that modern society values ability more than academic qualifications, which is reflected in how well you master the industry skills required by the development trend of modern society. It is understood that IT technology has been widely used in all walks of life. The practitioners are divided into those engaged in IT main careers, IT application careers, IT-related careers, and IT peripheral industries, covering a wide range, which can be described as all-encompassing. Computer training has become an excellent choice for more high school and college graduates to recreate themselves. Moreover, many emerging industries have emerged due to the development of IT technology, such as e-commerce, online games, and computer design, driving a huge demand for talents. The IT industry is ever-changing, and technology updates rapidly. However, the four-year training mechanism of universities makes the skills learned by graduates outdated when they graduate. From what Director Lin of Hangzhou Hengsheng Company told us, more than 90% of mid-to-senior level talents in the graphic industry are cultivated internally within the company.

The rapid development of emerging industries corresponds to a lack of corresponding talents. The computer training industry is making great efforts to cultivate the talents needed for the new century. Some powerful computer training professional educational institutions across the country have fully utilized their flexible characteristics to specifically cultivate urgently needed talents for emerging industries. According to Mr. Wang, the director of the Career Guidance Center at Henan Xinhua Computer College, Xinhua has always maintained close contact with emerging industries, constantly keeping abreast of industry developments, understanding emerging market trends, grasping cutting-edge technologies, and updating course systems in a timely manner. Based on a full understanding of corporate needs, Xinhua sets courses according to the job responsibilities required by enterprises, ensuring that students learn the latest computer training professional skills. It can be said that Xinhua Computer Training has become the best choice for most people to achieve success and talent.

Currently, many students who have missed out on university education realize "overtaking on a curve" through computer training, quickly acquiring professional skills in emerging industries. Through the cooperation between schools and enterprises, they gain the right to enter emerging industries first. When a large number of graduates rush to the job-hunting road, those who master professional computer training skills have no problem achieving success and talent.
