Internet service market is huge, intensifying competition promotes technological innovation

by now on 2007-10-12 09:33:07

According to the latest release from DCCI Internet Data Center, China has 162 million netizens, and the annual growth rate is as high as 30%. Moreover, in the first half of 2007, the total amount of personal internet consumption in China reached 16.1811 billion yuan RMB, and it is expected that this figure will reach 20.2303 billion in the second half of the year, exceeding the entire year's internet consumption scale in 2005. The rapid development of the internet has made internet service products such as domain names and virtual hosts etc. in high demand. Where there is a market, there is competition; facing the huge internet services market, providers are racing to innovate technologically to improve their competitiveness. Characteristic services such as the launch of VDNS Global Edition and the addition of recommendation functions to the domain name query system have continued to emerge.

Domain name resolution is the process of converting domain names into IP addresses. Domain names correspond one-to-one with IP addresses. Generally, people are used to remembering domain names, but machines recognize each other through IP addresses. The conversion work between them is called domain name resolution. Domain name resolution is completed by DNS (Domain Name System) servers.

It is understood that, with the increasing demands of customers, VDNS (Visual Domain Name Server) - Visual Domain Name Resolution System is now mainly used for domain name resolution. It has all the functions of the DNS system, and it adopts a visual interface which makes operations simple. There are many versions of VDNS. In the industry, Now.CN (www.now.cn) has the most diverse VDNS versions, including Standard Edition (VDNS 20) and Advanced Edition (VDNS 35). Recently, they have formally launched the VDNS Global Edition Domain Name Management System after accumulating 7 years of domain name resolution service experience. This technology leads globally and allows free unlimited addition of subdomains, multi-level subdomains, Chinese subdomains, and Chinese multi-level subdomains. Its functions are more powerful, and its operations are even simpler.

In addition, for customers, querying a domain name is the first thing they need to do because domain names are unique, and it is very likely that the domain name they need has already been registered. Often, customers need to repeatedly think and check, wasting a lot of time and energy. In response to such situations, Now.CN has introduced advanced domain name fuzzy queries. This query function is an industry-first and was developed based on the concept of finding more good domain names faster. When users query a domain name, the system will recommend a batch of domain names to the customer at the same time. These recommended domain names are highly relevant to the queried domain name. For example: when you input book.com for a query, many unregistered domain names like Thebook.cn, Thebook.cc etc. appear for the user to refer to.

Industry insiders believe that the market brings competition, which in turn triggers technological change, and this is the rule of the market. Just taking domain names as an example, there have been a series of innovations such as the launch of the global edition of domain name resolution and the humanized design of domain name query technology. It is believed that with the increase in industry competition pressure, more new technical products will appear, and only those companies that continuously meet customer needs and keep innovating can go further.
