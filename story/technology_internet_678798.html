Characteristics of thyroid diseases: Hyperthyroidism presents symptoms such as heat intolerance, excessive sweating, palpitations, irritability, increased appetite, and weight loss; Hypothyroidism presents symptoms such as cold intolerance, swelling, weight gain, dry skin, and decreased appetite; Acute or subacute thyroiditis may cause neck pain accompanied by fever, especially with palpable lumps in the thyroid area that are tender to touch. In case of any of these situations, it is important to visit the endocrinology department of a hospital promptly for further examination, which can lead to timely diagnosis and appropriate treatment.
