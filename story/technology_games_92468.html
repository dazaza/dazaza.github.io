"Taboo Angel" Introduction:

In the 334th year of creation, the Creator separated his divine essence and demonic nature in order to create a world of truth and goodness, and had the angel Qianling take them to the underworld. Unexpectedly, Qianling broke the Creator's seal out of curiosity about the demonic nature. The fellow angel, Kiku Maru, bravely took on the responsibility of re-sealing the evil demonic nature to save Qianling. This is a side-scrolling action game where players can accept various tasks in the village and then go on adventures outside the village. Players can set their physical skills in the training ground within the village. Thus, when battling monsters, they can use their own combination of attack moves against the monsters, greatly enhancing the playability of the game. Items dropped by monsters can be used in the village to upgrade one's weapons and magic levels. The player's weapons can be forged from ordinary genuine swords into legendary divine weapons. The game's plot is full of twists and turns, with a simple and brisk style.
