**Main Characters of Mischievous Kiss 2**

**Shiang-Chun Chiang (played by Vincent Chong)**

A super genius with an IQ of 200, he is smart, handsome, and excels in everything. Practically nothing can stump him. However, because he is too intelligent, he finds everything easily achievable, leading to a lack of purpose in life. After meeting Shiang-Chin, his defenses gradually lower as he discovers a direction for his life—medicine—and accepts her love. Before they even graduate, the two marry. What kind of challenges will his quirky new wife bring to his life?

**Ah-Li's Wife (played by Winnie Chao)**

Chiang's mother, a housewife with a quirky personality who loves excitement and photography. She gets along well with Shiang-Chin and tries her best to bring them together. She often disguises herself to spy on Chiang and Shiang-Chin at school or spreads rumors about their relationship, but her disguises are often seen through. Despite her good intentions, her overbearing nature causes friction between her and her son, planting the seeds for future conflicts. With Shiang-Chin becoming her daughter-in-law, she plans to create the perfect "house of love" for the newly merged families.

**Yuh-Chu Chiang (played by Peter Chang)**

Chiang's younger brother, who idolizes his older brother immensely. His greatest wish is to work as his assistant after Chiang takes over their father's company. He doesn’t respect people he deems unintelligent, especially Shiang-Chin. Initially, he treats her as a research subject for his summer project, observing all her silly actions. Over time, however, he becomes moved by her sincerity and earnestness. As Yuh-Chu grows up, he encounters a girl named Hao-Mei, who resembles a mini version of Shiang-Chin. Should Yuh-Chu accept Hao-Mei’s feelings?

**Ah-Li (played by Yong-Zheng Zhang)**

Chiang's father and the owner of a panda toy company. Kind and benevolent, he hopes Chiang will attend Taiwan University and eventually take over his business.

**Ah-Cai (played by Cong Cong)**

Shiang-Chin's father and the owner of the "Happy Little Restaurant." A skilled cook, he has been friends with Chiang's parents since middle school. After losing his wife early in life, he raised Shiang-Chin alone. He feels regretful that Shiang-Chin cannot cook or do housework well, believing it's his fault.

**Ah-Jin (played by Dong-Cheng Wang from F4)**

Shiang-Chin's classmate, not very bright but athletically gifted. He has liked Shiang-Chin since freshman year, with his ultimate dream being to marry her. After high school, he chose not to pursue further education, working part-time at the school cafeteria during the day and apprenticing at "Fukichi" restaurant at night. In the sequel, Ah-Jin meets Christine and slowly falls for her innocence, finally letting go of his long-standing affection for Shiang-Chin.

**Pei Zi-Yu (played by Wei-Ning Hsu)**

A wealthy heiress who admires Chiang's intelligence and charisma so much that she chooses to attend Dou Nan University's College of Engineering. Though brilliant and capable, Zi-Yu and Shiang-Chin often clash. However, when faced with competition from another rich heiress, they unite against the common threat. Despite her deep feelings for Chiang, Zi-Yu remains a strong-willed woman who knows when to let go.

**Ah-Bu (played by Aaron Yan from F4)**

Pure-Mei's boyfriend, who loves her deeply but is restrained by his mother's authority. Encouraged by Shiang-Chin, he finally decides to fight for his love and earns his mother's approval.

**Pure-Mei (played by Pei-Ting Yang)**

Shiang-Chin's high school friend and close sister. Gentle and shy, she stands up fiercely for Shiang-Chin whenever needed. In college, she falls in love with the wealthy Ah-Bu, leading to a romantic story. When she unexpectedly discovers she is pregnant before graduation, with Shiang-Chin's help, they navigate a hilarious yet touching prelude to married life.

**Liu-Nong (played by Rong-Jia Liu)**

Another of Shiang-Chin's high school friends, known for her bold and protective nature. Though her ideas sometimes backfire, her kindness always stems from good intentions toward Shiang-Chin.

**Ouyang Gan (played by Yu-Zhe Tang)**

Shiang-Chin's nursing school classmate, with a delicate appearance often mistaken for a girl. Sarcastic but caring, he dreams of wearing the nurse's cap in honor of Florence Nightingale.

**Tseng Ni-Na (played by Pei-Chen Chiang)**

Shiang-Chin's nursing school classmate, known for her curvaceous figure and cheerful personality. During a social event, she charms a medical student named Chuan-Jin, creating an interesting dynamic between the bookish nerd and the vivacious butterfly.

**Yang Qi-Tai (played by Shao-Zong Zeng)**

Another nursing school classmate of Shiang-Chin, extremely principled and dedicated to patient care. Seeing Shiang-Chin's sadness when neglected by Chiang, he develops protective feelings but quietly steps aside upon realizing their unbreakable bond. He remains her loyal friend.
