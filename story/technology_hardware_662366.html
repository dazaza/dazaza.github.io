Lotus Car L5, as a token of gratitude, is now offering a direct cash discount of 11,800 yuan!

by juyaqufa003 on 2012-02-10 09:53:59

[Tianjin Binhai Car Market] Recently, our station editor learned from the Tianjin Qinglian 4S store of Lotus Cars that the Lotus L5 is now available with a direct cash discount of 11,800 yuan. Friends who like the L5 can visit the store to view and test drive the car. Quantities are limited, so it's first come, first served!

The front design of the Lotus L5 is full of British elements, resembling the L3 in some ways. However, unlike the purely sporty elements of the L3, the L5 has a more fashionable and elegant style. The Lotus L3 has a noticeable downward trend at the head, tail, and sides, giving it a distinctly British feel. There is more chrome decoration on the front, adding layers to the appearance. The grille is relatively large, featuring a chrome-woven mesh inside, looking quite refined. The willow-leaf-shaped headlights are more fashionable, and the low-beam lights are equipped with lenses, enhancing the car's class. Above the fog lights, there are LED daytime running lights, further ensuring driving safety.

The Lotus L5 achieves a perfect fusion of handling and power, equipped with a 1.6L CPS high-efficiency intelligent engine, with a maximum power output of 90.5kw, comparable to larger displacement engines of 1.8L. Currently, only high-performance models like Porsche and Subaru use this CPS technology. This engine was developed by British Lotus and features two core technologies: "CPS Variable Valve Lift System + VIM Variable Intake Manifold System," leading the trend in balancing power performance and fuel economy. Combined with the Lotus technology chassis, the L5 offers consumers an unprecedented driving experience with powerful and controllable performance.

Following the latest consumption trends, the Lotus L5 strictly adheres to international high standards in terms of design, craftsmanship, and quality control systems, truly achieving five outstanding aspects: "exterior," "interior," "power," "handling," and "space." These distinct characteristics of the L5 best define the "driving trend."

For those interested, online reservations for test drives of the Lotus L5 are also available. The car delivers a dynamic and comfortable ride experience with its robust engine and advanced technology.
