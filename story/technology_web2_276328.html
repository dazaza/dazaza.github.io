Moving is a tiring task. In view of this, CITIC has prepared a practical manual. The manual includes preparation work, details to be noticed during the move, when to contact Guangzhou moving companies for on-site valuation and the most appropriate time for moving. It reminds you to carry out the relocation in the fastest and simplest way.

During the move, it's easy to cause inconvenience to life due to not being able to find things. Especially if you have to go to work or school the day after moving, it can be even more troublesome. So by paying attention to some small secrets while packing, you can ensure that the move does not affect your daily life.

(1) Categorize, pack and label items

Firstly, a few days before moving with Guangzhou Dazhong Moving, disassemble various items, classify and securely tie them up. Don't forget to note down the contents of the box with a marker pen outside the box, mark the box number so as not to forget what's inside the box, making it easier to find later.

* Loose items should be packed in boxes as much as possible to avoid scattering during the move and difficulty in finding them.

* Boxes should be numbered outside for quick understanding whether any box is missing after the move.

* Items used daily should be packed together, such as public items, children's textbooks, stationery, or daily necessities.

* Other items like clothes, books, etc., can be further categorized based on usage frequency and importance.

* Cleaning cloths, cleaning agents, etc., should be packed separately for immediate use upon arrival at the new home.

(2) Pay attention to parts and key management

* When disassembling objects, pay attention to the placement of parts and keys to avoid wasting time looking for them during reassembly later.

(3) Precautions for fragile and valuable items from Guangzhou moving companies:

* Fragile items, valuable electronics (computers, audio equipment, etc.), should be clearly marked on the outside of the box to inform the service personnel. Use scraps of cloth, newspapers, shock-absorbing cotton, etc., to fill the gaps inside the box to prevent damage caused by shaking or collision during the move.

* Fish tanks and aquariums should have all water emptied, and the fish placed in buckets for easy transport.

* To ensure the safety of relocated items and reduce risks, our company offers clients insurance services for valuable property at a rate of 0.4%.

* Valuable items should be stored separately and watched carefully. Important documents, seals, passbooks, jewelry, cash, antiques, etc., are best stored in advance with relatives or in safe and reliable places, or carried personally.

(4) Furniture interiors should be cleared and fixed, locked, and sealed

* Fridges, wardrobes, drawers, washing machines, etc., must be emptied inside. If not emptied, the service personnel may find it too heavy to move, and the furniture itself may get damaged due to internal movement, falling off, or collisions.

* After dismantling furniture and wardrobes, place them in an easily accessible location for moving. Additionally, secure the drawers of desks, doors of wardrobes, and other sliding parts with locks or nylon ropes.

(5) Contact neighbors to facilitate Guangzhou Dazhong Moving and subsequent contacts

* Inform the neighbors beforehand and reserve the Guangzhou Dazhong Moving time to avoid misunderstandings during the service personnel's work. Leave the new address and telephone number for convenient forwarding of subsequent documents or important information. Reserve parking spaces for moving vehicles at the old and new residences. Large residential complexes require prior reservation of parking spaces.

* If there is a building manager, please inform them and complete entry and exit procedures to ensure smooth relocation.

(6) Phone and mail transfer

* Apply for phone transfer and address changes for related bills and important mails (such as credit cards, mobile phones, subscribed newspapers, magazines, etc.) in advance.

(7) Others

* Packing boxes should not be too large or too heavy, as large boxes are not only difficult to move but also prone to bottom rupture due to excessive weight.

* Separate the packed boxes into categories, keeping fragile and valuable furniture together to remind the Guangzhou Dazhong Moving service personnel (easy to find when moving out of the old residence to the new one).

* If it rains, and you're worried about items getting wet, you should contact the Guangzhou moving company in advance to decide whether to reschedule or move after the rain. Make sure your items are well-prepared for rain protection.

* During each family move, our company is equipped with tools such as felt, bamboo baskets, piano covers, refrigerator covers, and large cabinet covers.

* We are not responsible for damages to living plants and animals (such as pet cats, dogs, valuable flowers, etc.) during transportation.

Furniture Parking

The ease or difficulty of furniture entering and exiting rooms is the most critical part of the moving process. Prior reconnaissance work should be done to fully understand all aspects of the residential environment, distances, vehicle routes, and parking positions to avoid delays in moving time or furniture damage due to negligence.

Garbage Disposal

Before Guangzhou Dazhong Moving, according to the space and furniture arrangement of the new home, determine whether old furniture and miscellaneous items are needed. Plan accordingly if they are to be given away or discarded, and label them appropriately.
