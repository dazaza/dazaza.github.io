Which woman does not want him to cherish her for a lifetime? However, if you want him to spoil you, you need to learn some art of love. I list seven standards for my sisters, which can help you shape the perfect wife.

Standard One: Be a wife like a lover

It's not hard to be a good wife, and it's not difficult to be a good lover either. But a wife who is as charming as his lover, making him miss and adore her, really needs some effort.

Standard Two: Always keep tender

No man can escape from the tenderness of women. But don't just be gentle. When he is well-behaved, you must take this opportunity to tell him your recent thoughts or requests. In this way, men will never refuse. Coax him and tell him that you want to eat ice cream... But you also have to remember to go to his study and give him a cup of hot tea, send him a bowl of noodles with a fried egg when he works overtime. This is more effective than doing any amount of housework.

Standard Three: Learn some good dishes for him

Here we are talking about not general delicacies. You may not know how to make tomato egg soup, but you must know how to make the braised pork that your husband likes. You don't need to know how to make luxurious dishes, but you must know how to make a few home-style dishes that your husband likes. In this way, when he goes out, he will say that these dishes are not as good as those made by his wife. He will want to come back to you as soon as possible. Even the way you study recipes will leave an impression on him.

Standard Four: Always have a heart for beauty

It doesn't matter whether you are ugly or beautiful in appearance, but you should accompany him with the posture of a beautiful woman. Remember, no matter how old you get, you should dress up in front of the mirror and understand the rules of dressing. Know his clothing style. Your pajamas and underwear should be more particular because clothes are for others to see, while pajamas and underwear are specially for your husband to see!

Standard Five: Be the only private assistant of your husband

This does not mean that you should wash all the clothes at home. Other clothes can be washed by the nanny or someone else, but you must wash your husband's underwear and smelly socks, no matter how dirty or smelly they are. Ensure that he changes into clean socks every day and never wears broken socks. Polish each pair of his leather shoes clean. Ensure that the underwear he wears has the scent you dealt with.

Standard Six: Satisfy his desire to conquer

There are many principles in the affairs between two people. Men hope that their wives are ladies outside and passionate in bed, which is almost the truth. Understand his nature of pursuing novelty, let him study you for a lifetime, and always give him the feeling of conquering you, so that he will feel that you are a charming woman and he is a real man.

Standard Seven: Give him a reason to love you

No woman does not yearn for a man's lifelong love, and no woman does not long for her family to be harmonious and happy forever. But youth will fade, and there will always be younger women than you, especially if your man is mature and elegant, wealthy and gentle, there will inevitably be many women who will flutter around like butterflies. To make a man love you for a lifetime, you must give him a reason to love you, right? Even if you achieve what the teasing text message says: either you are beautiful, if you are not beautiful, then you must have charm, if you don't have charm, then you must have talent, if you don't have talent, then your character must be good, if your character is not good, then you must be kind... In short, you must have one outstanding "brand" to make your husband always infatuated with you.
