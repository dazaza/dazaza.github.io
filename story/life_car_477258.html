New Knowledge of Car Beauty Decoration: A Must-read for Beginners

Most 4S stores offer services such as spoilers, xenon headlights, stickers, waxing, spraying, chrome plating, interior decoration, personalized interior lights, and night driving lights. In fact, car beauty is not only about decoration but also requires attention to some common knowledge. The Car Beauty Recruitment Network offers you tips:

This can be roughly divided into three points:

First, Common Car Exterior Decorations

Car exterior decorations or protective measures can be selected based on individual conditions as follows:

1. Wax removal and polishing: When new cars leave the factory, their outer shells have a layer of protective film. Therefore, your new car should go to a professional car beauty center for wax removal and polishing to restore its bright and charming appearance.

3. Paint sealing: The appearance of a car is like a person's appearance. After using a new car for a period of time, the surface may become dull and dark. Therefore, it is recommended to use "glaze" to seal the car paint and isolate it from the outside world, eliminating the need for waxing.

4. "Underbody plastic sealing": This involves spraying a highly adhesive, highly elastic rubber resin twice on the car's underbody, reducing the impact of gravel. It provides anti-corrosion, rust prevention, and sound insulation effects. It is also effective in resisting the strong corrosiveness of winter snow water and de-icing agents.

5. Changes in body color and body frame: An application must be made to one of the six branches of the Traffic Management Bureau's Vehicle Administration Office. After vehicle confirmation and approval by the Vehicle Administration Branch, changes can be made. To change the engine or the nature of vehicle use, registration for changes can be done at the Vehicle Administration Branch after the changes are completed.

Second, Matters to Note for Car Interior Decoration

When considering internal decoration for a new car, start with the window treatment. Applying window film to a new car not only provides heat insulation and waterproofing but also explosion protection, offering significantly different results compared to not applying film or hanging curtains. Car interior accessories mainly include car perfumes, car miscellaneous bags, cup holders, mobile phone stands, and various other clever small accessories.

When placing car interior accessories, four principles should be followed:

a. Comfort: The color and texture of car interior accessories should match the owner's aesthetic preferences. Perfumes should be fresh, avoiding overly strong scents.

b. Take transparent protector, shake well, and apply it evenly to cleaned leather and rubber surfaces, then wipe clean with a clean cloth.

c. Safety: Car interior accessories should not hinder driving safety. For example, hanging objects inside the car should not be too long, large, or heavy. Decorations on the rear windshield should not affect reversing visibility.

d. Cleanliness: Accessories should be clean and hygienic, neatly arranged, giving a sense of relaxation and comfort.

e. Practicality: When selecting accessories that fully express individuality, choose practical items according to the size of the car's interior space, such as cup holders, perfume bottles, and storage boxes.

f. Coordination: The color of accessories should coordinate with the color of the car. Do not blindly pursue high-quality, high-priced items to avoid ruining the effect.

Third, Legal Knowledge Related to Car Decoration and Modification

Before decorating or modifying your beloved car to make it beautiful, it is important to understand related regulations to avoid falling into pitfalls. Otherwise, it may not only fail inspection but also cause safety hazards or even legal penalties.

a. The legality of vehicle modifications depends on whether the vehicle matches the photo on the driving license and the technical parameters of the vehicle at the time of manufacture. If they do not match, the annual inspection cannot be passed.

b. The new Road Traffic Safety Law clearly stipulates that no unit or individual shall assemble motor vehicles or arbitrarily alter the existing structure, construction, or characteristic markings of motor vehicles. The structure of a vehicle includes body color, length, width, height, and relevant technical parameters of the engine.

c. Before modifying a car that has already received a license plate, registration and reporting should be made to the Vehicle Administration Office. After the modification technical report is reviewed and approved by the Vehicle Administration Office, modifications can be made. After the modification is complete, modification change procedures must still be handled at the Vehicle Administration Office.
