Quenching processes are widely used in modern mechanical manufacturing industries. Important parts in machinery, especially steel parts used in automobiles, airplanes, and rockets, almost all undergo quenching treatment. To meet various technical requirements for different parts, various quenching processes have been developed. For example, according to the treated area, there are whole, partial, and surface quenching; according to whether the phase transformation is complete during heating, there are complete quenching and incomplete quenching (for hypo-eutectoid steels, this method is also called subcritical quenching); according to the content of phase transformation during cooling, there are graded quenching, isothermal quenching, and insufficient speed quenching, etc., such as FAW Ha Chi, Hebei Xinde Power Accessories Co., Ltd., Qinghai No.1 Machine, Taiyuan Machine Tool Plant, Lunan Machine Tool Plant, Yuyao High-Strength Standard Parts Plant, and hundreds of other enterprises and institutions are using our company's equipment and have achieved good results.
