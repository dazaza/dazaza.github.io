Unveiling the Production Process of "If You Are The One": Post-80s Generation Supports the Backstage Team

Li Zheng, the chief editor of the program "If You Are The One", is mainly responsible for screening guests.

Producer Wang Gang attributed the success of "If You Are The One" to "timely opportunity, advantageous location, and harmonious people," where the "harmonious people" referred to the young backstage team in the production crew. From guest screening, on-site recording, to post-production, all these processes are inseparable from the hard work of these young backstage staff. So, how exactly is this program produced? How are the guests selected? Recently, reporters visited the filming site of "If You Are The One" to uncover the secrets of this group of post-80s backstage team and listen to their stories about the program.

Revelation One: Guest Selection and Pairing

Interviewee: Chief Editor Li Zheng

Function: Guest Selection

Selection Criteria: Preference for those who are good at expressing themselves and dare to express

Ma Nuo, Ma Yimi, Zhu Zhenfang, rich second-generation heirs, penguin men... Numerous internet celebrities have emerged from "If You Are The One." Their popularity is inseparable from the keen eyesight of the backstage directors responsible for selecting guests. The "If You Are The One" production team consists of eight directors, one chief editor, and one producer. Li Zheng serves as the chief editor in "If You Are The One," mainly responsible for guest selection work. He joined from the tenth episode onwards and has now "seen countless people." Li Zheng has a five-month-old baby, but due to his busy schedule, he hasn't had time to accompany his wife and child for two consecutive months.

Currently, more than 160,000 people have registered to participate in "If You Are The One," and it will take a long time to digest all these guests. Recently, the production team set up registration points in Beijing, Shanghai, Guangzhou, and Nanjing, conducting on-site reviews of the guests. Each guest needs to fill out a registration form on-site, followed by a brief interview conducted by the program's director. The content mainly involves occupation income, interests, family background, emotional experiences, and mate selection criteria, etc. Generally speaking, during the interview, the directors mark guests with special professions, identities, or experiences accordingly. After verifying the materials, the directors contact the guests to shoot short clips, mainly showcasing the guests' living conditions and friends' evaluations of them.

Li Zheng stated that when selecting guests, they hope to present "diversity" in terms of age, profession, marital status, and income. Moreover, whether the on-stage guests can "express" themselves is an important selection criterion. "The matchmaking in 'If You Are The One' is not ordinary online dating where posting photos and information suffices; the guests need to face the camera, so being brave and willing to express oneself is the most important."

Li Zheng also mentioned that Zhu Zhenfang, the former No. 2 "mansion girl," was a typical example of someone who dared to express herself. She was heavily criticized by netizens for saying things like "a monthly salary of 200,000 yuan is needed just to touch her hand" and "my boyfriend must have a mansion." In reality, Zhu Zhenfang grew up in extreme poverty, and after working, she mostly interacted with people who spent lavishly, leading her to mistakenly believe that a monthly salary of 200,000 yuan was a very ordinary level. In the eyes of the production team, this was a classic case of someone who was brave but not adept at expressing themselves. Li Zheng indicated that after Zhu Zhenfang, the production team would prioritize selecting guests who were "good at expressing themselves." Even if other conditions were not particularly outstanding, guests who were good at expressing themselves would still be given priority consideration.

Guest Pairing: Five Male Guests Each Have Their Own Characteristics

The directors of "If You Are The One" are divided into Group A and Group B. One group is responsible for selecting and shooting guest materials, while the other group begins recording the program. To ensure the richness reflected in each episode, before recording the program, the guests are paired based on the shot materials. Li Zheng stated that among the five male guests in each episode, there must be one with a good appearance, one with a good profession, one with a rich emotional story, and one who dares to express a certain value, making the program have more watchable content within limited time.

Program Format: Female Guests Can Stay Up to 20 Episodes

Due to the excellent ratings of "If You Are The One," many female guests with impure motives hope to stay on stage longer and do not want to be chosen too early by male guests. Regarding this, Li Zheng stated that since "If You Are The One" is not a variety show, it cannot use a competition format to determine results. However, if a female guest stays on stage for too long and raises doubts among netizens, the production team will conduct private communication and ask the guest to specify a final deadline. Currently, Ma Yimi has stayed on stage for 22 episodes, while the production team's psychological limit is around 20 episodes.

Female Guest Profession Mode: Position 11 Reserved for Professional Models

In the already aired episodes of "If You Are The One," number 1 Xie Jia, number 11 Ma Nuo, Na Di, and number 13 Ma Yimi have gained significant popularity online. With their successful pairings, the production team hopes to reserve their positions for female guests with similar characteristics. For instance, position 1 will always be reserved for girls dressed in a unisex style; position 11 will be reserved for professional models; position 5 must have high academic qualifications; position 24 must be very tall; similarly, positions 20 and 21 will be reserved for a mother-daughter duo.

Revelation Two: On-Site Coordination

Interviewees: On-site Director Chen Chen, Female Guest Director Zhang Mingming, Male Guest Director Guo Juntao

On-Stage Function: Control the overall situation of the program on-site

As mentioned earlier, the "If You Are The One" production team consists of eight directors. Generally, there are three directors on-site during program recording: On-site Director Chen Chen, Female Guest Director Zhang Mingming, and Male Guest Director Guo Juntao. On-site Director Chen Chen simultaneously serves as the chief editor, a highly experienced young woman responsible for controlling the overall situation on-stage, handling emergencies, and communicating with hosts and backstage staff. Occasionally, when hosts or guests need touch-ups or equipment malfunctions occur, resulting in short pauses, Chen Chen steps in to resolve various small issues on-site. Additionally, she oversees the VCR materials during male guest interviews and participates in post-production editing after the program concludes.

Female Guest Director Zhang Mingming is a lively young man responsible for leading the female guests. Before going on stage, he reminds the female guests of some precautions. If the female guests' posture isn't elegant enough or they whisper to each other, he will appropriately remind them. During the program recording process, the Female Guest Director closely monitors the female guests' situations on stage and can promptly handle any physical discomfort. According to Zhang Mingming, one female guest once fainted due to low blood sugar, but was quickly treated thanks to timely discovery. When asked if he had a "crush" among so many beautiful female guests, Zhang Mingming shyly said, "I'm not handsome, so the female guests wouldn't like me. Perhaps this is why the production team let me lead the female guests."

Male Guest Director Guo Juntao, besides being responsible for the male guests' attire, also calms their nerves before going on stage. Many male guests get extremely nervous before going on stage, sweating profusely, and Guo Juntao consoles them by saying things like, "Don't worry, just express yourself truthfully." Some male guests feel very disheartened after being "rejected and leaving the stage," and the Male Guest Director needs to provide psychological counseling. Male and female guests are strictly prohibited from meeting before the program recording to preserve the freshness between them.

Revelation Three: Program Music and On-Site Sound Effects

Interviewee: Sound Engineer Cai Kunyan

Role: An indispensable bonus item in the program

The great success of "If You Are The One" owes much to the music on the stage. The upbeat background music has become the preferred choice for audience members' mobile phone ringtones. Simply typing "If You Are The One music" into a search engine yields tens of millions of search results, and enthusiastic netizens have even compiled all the music used in the program.

At the "If You Are The One" site, the sound engineer is a young man named Cai Kunyan, who previously served as the live sound engineer for programs like "Superman Nationwide" and "Who Dares to Sing." All the background music is carefully selected by him. In an interview, Cai Kunyan admitted that before choosing music, he analyzes the meaning expressed in the lyrics and then repeatedly selects the most suitable one to be used as fixed background music for the stage. For some unexpected situations on the stage, it relies on his improvisation skills. Cai Kunyan studied composition in university and has accumulated a large amount of music material. The materials on his computer are categorized by emotion as "sad," "lively," "funny," etc., allowing him to quickly retrieve them to deal with unexpected situations on the stage.

When talking about his proudest improvisation, Cai Kunyan mentioned the background music he chose when male guest Li Meng proposed successfully to female guest Li Yang, which was "I Love You More Than I Imagined" by JS. As Li Yang and Li Meng hugged tightly on the stage, the melody "I've realized I love you more than I imagined, only because I accidentally missed you" echoed perfectly with the situation where they parted due to misunderstandings and later realized each other's importance. The background music moved all the audiences, even the emotionally restrained Meng Fei was moved to tears. Le Jia couldn't help but tease the sound engineer, "Your background music is too sentimental."

For Cai Kunyan, "If You Are The One" has become a stage where he can fully display his talents. More improvisations include some short and quick small sound effects, such as screams and laughter, which often effectively enliven the atmosphere among the audience. Host Meng Fei once told a story about his difficult days when he was young, and Cai Kunyan humorously matched it with a tragic piece of "Moon Reflected on Second Spring," causing the entire audience to burst into laughter. Meng Fei himself couldn't help laughing, "This music is indeed tragically sad." Would the extensive use of background music on the stage lead to copyright disputes? Relevant responsible persons stated that the length of the music used on the stage is within the allowable range of copyright.

Revelation Four: Costumes and Makeup

Interviewee: Makeup Artist Xin Yuan

Revelation: Female guests aren't divas, costumes and props are free of charge

The female guests on "If You Are The One" appear fashionable and graceful on the program. Many viewers wonder if they prepare their own costumes and makeup, and whether they need to pay for it. Makeup artist Ms. Xin Yuan explained that most of the female guests' costumes are prepared by themselves, but occasionally, if a female guest needs the program team to provide costumes, this rental service is completely free. The entire makeup team consists of about ten people, with each makeup artist responsible for one female guest. It takes approximately one hour to apply makeup to each female guest, and many female guests are already very skilled at applying makeup themselves, so they come on the program already made up. Besides makeup, there is also a styling team equipped with various styling tools that can quickly create various hairstyles. Sometimes in the program, all the female guests have their hair up or curled, and the stylist explained that this is not intentional arrangement, but rather a "herd mentality" among the female guests. When they see a hairstyle that looks good on another girl, they naturally request the stylist to try it on them too.

When the female guests walk onto the stage accompanied by lively music, they appear as if they have been trained like models. In fact, no special training has taken place. Director Zhang Mingming, responsible for the female guests, said that they usually tell the female guests to maintain proper stage demeanor by saying, "Being a woman is pretty good," meaning they should stand straight. If they don't want to look bad on TV, they must pay attention to their posture and walking style. Despite the tiring experience of standing on stage for a long time, the beauty-conscious female guests still maintain a straight posture to leave the best impression on the audience.

I really like this program, I watch every episode. It truly reflects the myriad aspects of life... But I believe that everyone should still face life with a positive attitude!
