The Next Wave of the Internet - Online Accounting Websites

Previously, I saw news that Tencent was going to get involved in the development of online software. At that time, I did not see any specific products. Later, I started using Taotao.com developed by Tencent and have been using it since then. I found it pretty good. Recently, an accounting function suddenly appeared. Today, I finally got the official information. Taotao.com under Tencent has launched an accounting function, which is commonly known as an online ledger. This shows how fierce the competition in the online accounting website space is.

Taotao.com under Tencent has launched online accounting. According to official sources, this is to increase the added value of Taotao. Anyway, currently there is a quiet trend of online accounting on the internet. There was once CaiZhi software for family accounting, but it was a standalone version. On February 10, 2006, China's first online accounting website (www.coko365.com) officially went online. Although CaikOnline started early, it did not develop well in 2006 because as a pioneer, it was always exploring. It wasn't until 2007 that it began to gain traction. Before May 2007, CaikOnline lagged behind its competitors. After May 2007, based on its own advantages and resources, CaikOnline made strategic adjustments and quickly seized the market. To date, it has become the leader among all peers in terms of overall strength. Currently, the most pressing task for CaikOnline is to maintain its leading position and widen the gap with competitors.

If we calculate the development of China's online accounting websites from the official launch of CaikOnline on February 10, 2006, then today, China's online accounting websites have been developing for more than a year. Why did CaikOnline name itself "Caik" at that time? It was to call those who like to keep accounts and manage finances "Caik". Just like people who like to write diaries are called "bloggers". After more than a year of development, CaikOnline has evolved from its initial version to today's relatively powerful functionality, a more complete structure, and gradually formed member loyalty, recognition, brand image, and reputation. During the operation of CaikOnline, I increasingly feel that many members start learning how to keep accounts, use the services provided by the online accounting website, and derive pleasure and help from them.

If blogs, videos, communities, and Web 2.0 are hot trends in the internet, then the next wave of the internet will definitely be online accounting websites. Currently, the market for online accounting websites is still in its infancy. There are dozens of such websites in China, but very few truly perform well. I predict that within a year, online accounting websites will be as popular as blogs were back in the day, sweeping across the entire network and the whole world. Not long ago, Fortune magazine selected an overseas online accounting website as one of the network models that will change the world in the future.

From a macroeconomic perspective, the global economy is starting to slow down, with signs of an economic crisis gradually emerging. In China's current situation, prices are skyrocketing while people's wages remain stagnant, making money seem increasingly scarce. Accounting and financial management tools are becoming increasingly important. We should learn how to plan our spending, how to spend, and how to save.

From a micro perspective, with the rapid development of material economy, more and more new products are being introduced to the market, but people's pockets are getting tighter. Only through accounting and financial management can people clearly track where their money goes. Control the waste of money at every stage.

CaikOnline (www.coko365.com) has its unique strategy and advantages in the field of online accounting. After more than a year of development, it has a deep understanding of members' actual needs for online accounting websites. Targeting members' actual needs and real difficulties, CaikOnline has launched a unique function that no other competitor possesses — multiple accounts. This is an original function among all competitors, so it immediately distinguishes itself from others.

The multi-account function launched by CaikOnline can effectively allow parents to grasp and timely understand their children's consumption situation and allow wives to grasp and timely understand their husbands' expenditure situations. For example, if children are studying at university away from home, parents can open a sub-account for their children, allowing them to log into CaikOnline daily to keep accounts. In this way, parents can effectively manage, control, and understand their children's consumption situation to some extent, achieving the goal of saving for the whole family. As for wives, they can also open a sub-account for their husbands on CaikOnline, allowing them to keep accounts daily to achieve the purpose of not wasting money. Once the habit of keeping accounts and managing finances is cultivated, unnecessary expenses can be greatly reduced, improving family income and capital utilization rate. Therefore, if CaikOnline can correctly and timely guide members to use CaikOnline and cultivate the habit of keeping accounts and managing finances daily, it can significantly enhance the wealth of the whole country and achieve the goal of thrift.

After more than a year of operating China's earliest online accounting website — CaikOnline, I am very optimistic about the prospects of the online accounting market in China and even the world! Because online accounting and financial management services are closely related to each member's daily life, although there are many difficulties at present, many members leave messages for CaikOnline every day, providing feedback on their needs and suggestions, giving me and my team great encouragement and confidence.
