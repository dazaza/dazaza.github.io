More than 40 schools are recruiting 1,730 teachers, and registration has started.

by hophi on 2009-04-10 08:02:35

Bole Zhike website reported: Graduates who want to be teachers, attention please. Yesterday, the Provincial Department of Human Resources and Social Security and the Provincial Department of Education jointly issued an announcement that more than 40 provincial colleges and universities will publicly recruit 1730 teachers from the society.

Specifically: 30 provincial universities plan to publicly recruit 1607 teachers, and 13 directly affiliated colleges and universities under the Provincial Department of Education plan to publicly recruit 123 teachers. Applicants must log in to http://zhaopin.haedu.cn from 8:00 am on April 11 to 6:00 pm on April 15, and enter the "Teacher Recruitment Management System for Provincial Colleges and Universities of the Henan Provincial Department of Education" to register. The two departments also announced the supervision telephone numbers: 0371-65900466 (Policy and Regulations Division of the Henan Provincial Department of Human Resources and Social Security), 0371-69691975, 69691977 (Personnel Division of the Henan Provincial Department of Education).

The recruitment situation of undergraduate institutions is as follows: Zhengzhou University 130 people, Henan University 100 people, Henan University of Science and Technology 80 people, Henan Polytechnic University 90 people, Henan Institute of Technology 80 people, Henan Normal University 73 people, Zhengzhou University of Light Industry 50 people, North China Water Conservancy and Electric Power University 75 people, Zhengzhou Aviation Industrial Management College 65 people, Zhongyuan University of Technology 32 people, Henan University of Chinese Medicine 55 people, Henan Finance University 47 people, Henan Institute of Education 15 people, Henan Radio & TV University 12 people, Xinxiang Medical University 50 people, Henan Institute of Science and Technology 35 people, Xinyang Normal University 50 people, Nanyang Normal University 50 people, Anyang Normal University 60 people, Luoyang Normal University 30 people, Zhoukou Normal University 30 people, Shangqiu Normal University 40 people, Xuchang University 40 people, Henan Urban Construction College 35 people, Luoyang Institute of Science and Technology 52 people, Henan Engineering University 60 people.

The recruitment situation of junior colleges is as follows: Zhengzhou Animal Husbandry College 31 people, Yellow River Water Conservancy College 20 people, Zhengzhou Railway Vocational and Technical College 15 people, Henan Mechanical and Electrical Higher Vocational College 25 people, Henan Business College 18 people, Henan Economics and Trade Vocational College 15 people.

The recruitment situation of secondary vocational schools is as follows: Zhengzhou Surveying and Mapping School 4 people, Zhengzhou Industry and Trade School 7 people, Henan Mechanical and Electrical School 15 people, Henan Industrial School 4 people, Henan Arts and Crafts School 7 people, Henan Light Industry School 7 people, Henan Industrial Design School 5 people, Henan Medical School 4 people, Henan Chemical School 10 people, Henan Electronic Science and Technology School 2 people. In addition, Henan University Affiliated High School plans to recruit 16 people.

From the recruitment requirements this time, undergraduate institutions mainly recruit doctoral students, junior colleges recruit both doctoral and master's students, and secondary vocational schools and high schools mainly recruit master's and undergraduate students. Graduates can check the specific recruitment requirements of each school on the website of the Department of Education.
