Reprint: The classic quotes of Zhejiang University Professor Zheng Qiang, interrupted by 127 rounds of applause. (Everyone is watching)

by mixuan4304 on 2010-04-17 12:28:03

1. The Japanese would rather like black people than us, because Chinese people nowadays lack energy. 2. Everyone is mocking Russia, but I know that Russia will definitely develop in the future, because the Russians can still queue up even when they have not eaten for two days, while we will fight even if there are only two of us. 3. The Japanese invaded us because we had many traitors. Will there be traitors when the Japanese invade us again in the future? Who will be the traitors of future China? A large part of you will be. Because you mock patriots, revere power and money, and despise ideals and ambition. 4. Who are the traitors now? Students from Peking University and Tsinghua University, because they use their knowledge to help foreigners open up markets and defeat our Chinese enterprises. 5. We neglect the value of history and always think that the newer the building, the better. But when you go to the center of Paris, you will find that there are almost no new buildings. They take pride in their historical accumulation, while we keep tearing down buildings and building new ones to torment ourselves. 6. The main purpose of education is not to make a living, but to arouse interest and encourage energy. Making a living and getting rich through education is also possible, but we have overemphasized it. 7. Even if China develops in the future, you will see that those rich people driving luxury cars spit and throw garbage out of the car window. You will know that without education, China will never be strong no matter how rich it is. 8. Reading is for taking responsibility. But today's education makes women bear too much responsibility and lets men avoid too much responsibility. 9. Human history is actually a series of emotions. So don't despise emotions, because emotions are all lovable. 10. Chinese basketball does not lack skills or money, but they lack a sense of responsibility, even the responsibility to consumers. 11. People who can sing well use air to produce sound, so they don't get tired easily. When I see you speak with your throat moving, I know that you can't sing well. 12. In the next 20 years, Chinese people will respect knowledge rather than officials. We should learn from Japan on this point, as this nation respects knowledge to an extreme degree. But now in China, people with a little money or a little power - even a section chief - can make university professors lose their dignity. This kind of seemingly smart wisdom and smug vulgarity is so shallow. 13. A man can only kneel to his parents and wife, can only bow to his teacher, and absolutely should not bow to wealth and power. But now, most people do just the opposite. 14. Why don't the Japanese apologize? Why doesn't the Japanese president repent? Because they know that the dignity and history of the Japanese are the most important, while the dissatisfaction of other Asians is too insignificant, so they can ignore it. 15. Education should make China understand self-respect. But now when we see foreigners, we lower our heads. Female students even want to flatter foreign men who are trash. Colleagues, we have no dignity in front of foreigners. Among the people studying in Tokyo University, I am the only one who came back, but the Japanese admire me because I live with spirit and backbone.

Excerpt: "In this developing country of China, if you can build 10 world-class universities, how many does the United States have? How many does Japan have? The current situation is: among the top 200 universities in the world, China cannot even rank one! How many can rank in Asia? After I went abroad and saw, I feel that building Zhejiang University into a world-class university is like a communist ideal."

"Previously, we said 'ignorance is fearless', now it is 'only ignorance can be fearless'. Many enterprises invite people from the Zhejiang Province Technical Supervision Bureau and the Science Commission to eat a meal, drink some wine, then sign, and force us professors to go there, stamp it, and then it becomes 'filling domestic and international gaps' and 'international advanced level'. Writing papers is 'international leading level research results' and 'first scientific discovery' etc. These are very serious problems now! As a university professor, I deeply worry about this! This is not our duty, it is the ignorance of our leaders, they advocate this mainstream. I know that the lives of department directors or general managers here are hard, because if you don't write such reports, you won't get money, projects won't be approved, and professors are the same, writing reports every day instead of quietly doing research in the laboratory, this is very serious!"

"Our country's reality and development is like this: whatever we rely on others for, we can do it ourselves quite well, such as the two bombs and one satellite; whatever can be introduced, we can't succeed... Many joint ventures are like this now, selling things without considering these deeper issues. Unknowingly, this is a vicious cycle of society!"

"I think: language and computer are tools. Chinese foreign language professors speak English worse than American farmers! How to deal with this problem? There are many Japanese PhDs and German professors who cannot speak English! How can we say that a person who cannot speak English is illiterate? Language is a tool! Without that environment, how can he speak that language?... If I were the Minister of Education, I would reform two things: first, abolish the sixth-level exam. If a graduate student cannot pass the first level of Chinese, why bother taking the sixth level of English? Look at the papers written by graduate students, they haven't learned their own national culture well, yet they spend every day studying English – check: TOEFL checked, GRE checked, high scores in English exams. But which paper written in English could pass my inspection? It wouldn't! What can such people trained do? They haven't learned their own specialty at all!... Saying that someone who cannot use a computer is illiterate is another misconception! Now I am a professor, I don't have time to mess with computers!"

"Look at me, I started learning English in high school, then in university, master's, and doctoral studies, how much effort did it take! Do you think Chinese people can achieve high-tech research results? Recently, I taught a few female students under me, asking them what they were doing, and couldn't see their figures. All day long, they were preparing for various exams, planning to go to the United States. What will they do there? There are plenty of things to do domestically! You study English all day long, Americans charge you money even to register, Japanese do the same. Chinese students going to Japan have to pay handling fees, studying in Japan is working for the Japanese. Hardly earning any money, paying tuition fees, after graduating and working in Japanese companies, they earn some money and return to China to buy appliances, giving all the money to the Japanese. You haven't noticed these economic issues. This is what quality education really is."

"Education in primary and secondary schools is about obedience, teachers manage cadres, cadres manage classmates, children all learn adult ways of dealing with the world. This is harmful! After forcing to do some good deeds, they don't link doing good deeds with being a person, but only learn to flatter, please teachers, and talk like adults. Once, on TV, a child won an award, and the host asked him what he wanted to say most, he said: 'I want to report to Grandpa Jiang!' Who is 'Grandpa Jiang'? Isn't it what the teacher taught! Children are very tired in primary and secondary schools, and when they reach university, no one manages them anymore, so they play and fall in love."

"We have many excellent students who can do nothing. In our university, at my level, we never look at grades when recruiting doctoral students. Grades mean nothing! In the field I am currently engaged in, there are three outstanding talents in China, all of whom failed courses during their postgraduate studies, while those who scored high in exams all went to America to sell Chinese medicine. What does this indicate? Bosses can't do this!... The echelon of talents must be reasonable, and we shouldn't think that professors or doctors are omnipotent. The Chinese education system is to give every commoner hope and dreams, educate children to establish lofty ideals. Actually, people's abilities are different. Sweeping the floor well should also be respected; cleaning the toilet well should also be respected. Don't blindly pursue high education. I want to remind you: it's not like that abroad. It's hard for Ph.D.s in the U.S. and Japan to find jobs, why? Because bosses are stingy, hiring Ph.D.s means paying them high salaries, but what can they do specifically? This is a specific issue."

"What should science and technology do? What should high technology do? If I were the Minister of Science and Technology, let them play, like Chen Jingrun, he was playing! If Chen Jingrun were in today's China, he would definitely beg, because he wouldn't industrialize, his English wasn't good, he didn't speak fluently, and his Chinese wasn't good either. According to today's 'standards,' he is illiterate, let alone a professor! The Japanese like Americans, I told the Japanese: whoever your nation loves, they will drop atomic bombs on you. The Japanese like black people more than Chinese people... I especially tell our female professors and students: don't speak a word of Japanese in front of the Japanese, even if you can, don't; when the Japanese hear you speak English, especially seeing Chinese girls speak English, their legs go weak, it's true!"

"Why do Chinese people run away these years? Most importantly, we need to make citizens love their own country... If I were the mayor of Hangzhou, I would not have narrow nationalism – if there were any disasters in Hangzhou, I would first arrange Hangzhou residents in the Shangri-La Hotel, letting foreigners wait outside! (applause!) Only then will you make your citizens love their own country! A Japanese farmer goes to Mount Emei to play, breaks his bones, and you send a Chinese Air Force helicopter to rescue him, while a Chinese student dies in the dormitory of a Japanese university after seven days before being discovered; a Chinese doctor couple and their child in Nagoya University mistakenly ate poisonous mushrooms, the child and mother died, the father had severe hepatitis, waiting in the outpatient room of Nagoya University Medical College for 12 hours without a single Japanese professor coming to see! And why do you still show such friendliness, thinking you are generous, actually being laughed at, laughing at your ignorance! Your nation *! We can't be like this! Our leaders visit foreign countries, see a few people welcoming them, and feel quite honored; while when a foreign figure comes, police cars lead the way, what is this all about? Does this make us Chinese proud or sad? All these are deep-seated issues for educational work. So I often say, as a natural science worker, I teach my students to first learn how to be a person. Without these, learning polymer science and foreign languages are all superficial."

This is just a point of view. Just read it, don't delve too deeply.
