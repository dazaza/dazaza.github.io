Yahoo Leans on Facebook for Promotional Tips Sharing

by yahoobing on 2012-03-08 15:55:27

Facebook is the only site today that can rival Google in terms of traffic. As SEM professionals, we need to not only capture Google's traffic but also take advantage of the large flow brought by Facebook promotions. So, how do we drive traffic to our website from Facebook? Below are a few commonly used methods.

First, make full use of personal information profiles.

Facebook is a communication-based community where people like to find those who or what they are interested in. At this point, how to write a personalized profile that catches people's attention is something everyone must research.

Second, place various images and information related to your website on the wall and photo albums.

Facebook's wall is very similar to China's microblogging service, allowing you to express your current mood. Additionally, common photo albums can be used for website promotion images and logos that you usually create.

Third, build your own network.

Facebook is an interactive platform for making friends. You need to learn how to establish your circle of friends and identify people who are interested in your website.

Fourth, keep regular updates.

You must regularly update various information about your Facebook promotions, including blog posts, in order to continuously attract traffic.

Fifth, be active.

How do you get more people to pay attention to your Facebook page or leave the deepest impression? Just sitting and waiting won't work; you need to be active. Participate more in others' blog sharing and join various circles.

Sixth, organize your personal homepage well.

Facebook applications are very flexible. Arrange the applications you need, and fully utilize the RSS submission function.

Seventh, determine which applications you need.

Among Facebook's many applications, choose the ones you are most proficient in and need the most, such as link publishing and blogs, and place them on the homepage.

Eighth, use Facebook's ad network.

Facebook provides its internal ad network, which can also be considered PPC. This feature is paid, so it depends on each SEO's usage situation.

Ninth, create your own circle.
