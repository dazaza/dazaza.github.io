2000 ET9910 Data-type Multi-parameter Water Quality Rapid Analyzer

by yq91566g8 on 2009-12-03 12:05:40

Based on the characteristics of world water quality and monitoring standards, combined with our own technical advantages, using a modular configuration concept, the electrode method and microcomputer spectrophotometric measurement principle are cleverly combined. The newly launched ET9910 series of data multi-parameter analyzers not only meet the requirements for water quality testing in various outdoor environments but are also suitable for rapid water quality detection during emergencies and routine water parameter measurements in laboratories, allowing users to satisfy their water testing needs with minimal investment. The spectrophotometer is equipped with over 120 built-in application programs and electrode methods, enabling it to measure more than 135 important water quality parameters, widely used in industrial, municipal, environmental protection, educational and other fields for water quality monitoring.

The instrument quickly measures various water parameters with humanized convenient operation, featuring large capacity storage, RS232, USB data interfaces, allowing the storage and management of data to be transmitted to a computer for further analysis, printing, etc. It is suitable for comprehensive water analysis in the field, production sites, and laboratories, as well as rapid water quality measurements.

A. Photometric determination uses a wide range of continuous wavelengths, automatically selecting wavelength based on the measurement project;

B. Microprocessor patent technology, humanized design, large screen display, process graphic display, simple operation, DZF-6210 vacuum drying box;

C. Conventional or rapid measurement of pH, conductivity, dissolved oxygen, turbidity, COD, TOC, total chromium, total phosphorus, total nitrogen, ammonia nitrogen, fluoride, iron, copper, phosphate, sulfate, nitrate, and more than 130 other important parameters;

D. Large capacity data storage function, routinely storing 200 to 1000 data sets;

E. Use of pre-prepared reagents, simple and convenient, greatly saving your time, and the reagents have stable, easy-to-store features, with some having a shelf life of up to 5-10 years;

F. Convenient connection to a computer for data transmission, real-time printing or data management;

G. Equipped with portable packaging, waterproof and shockproof design, PB1502-L, economical consumables, suitable for field and laboratory analysis and testing.
