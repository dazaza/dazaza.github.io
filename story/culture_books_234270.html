She, Shangguan Fuliǔ, the only princess of a different surname in the Xihua Dynasty, is actually a modern person who has traveled through a thousand years because of a gorgeous golden hairpin.

He, Luo Qian, the power minister who usurped the throne of the Xihua Dynasty, was however the emperor with the shortest reign.

A political marriage brought a南海素莲 (a kind of pure white lotus from the South Sea) to his side, which he himself had never expected. She exuded a faint fragrance, confusing his mind. Thus, they both became pawns on the chessboard of emotions, could only advance, and could not retreat!

The battle for the successor broke out again. After embracing, at the moment of turning around, where would they go?
