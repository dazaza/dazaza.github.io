China Pinke Website

[Brief Review]: A well-known pinke website domestically.

[Operation and Features]: Users can publish various pinke event information (in 9 major categories) through the management backend, conveniently join, save, or recommend pinke events posted by others. After applying to become a merchant, users can also post product and discount information. Additionally, it supports group and friend functions.

[Shortcomings]: Pre-set event locations are limited to large domestic cities.

Yipin Website

[Operation and Features]: Users can post various pinke information (in 9 major categories) via the homepage and channel links. When posting, they can easily select the event location (supporting all major cities across the country). The posted information can be managed through the management backend. For events initiated by others, users can leave comments, etc. Additionally, it supports club and friend functions.

[Shortcomings]: The backend displays garbled characters when accessed.

Beijing Pinke

[Brief Review]: A pinke website built on a forum platform, slogan: Make life more valuable.

[Operation and Features]: Users can post new threads or reply to posts in various sub-forums (in 6 major categories) to release and respond to messages.

[Shortcomings]: Due to the limitations of the forum format, information cannot be published or activities participated in according to a unified format.

Metropolitan Pinke Website

[Brief Review]: A pinke website built on a forum platform, focusing on serving the Shanghai area.

[Operation and Features]: Users can post new threads or reply to posts in various sub-forums (in 7 major categories) to release and respond to messages. They can also browse the main information of the entire site through the homepage.

[Shortcomings]: Due to the limitations of the forum format, information cannot be published or activities participated in according to a unified format. The homepage lacks quick links for posting information.
