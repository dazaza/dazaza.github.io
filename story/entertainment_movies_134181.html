Movie time traveler

by hebseo on 2009-03-25 15:02:36

Time travel has always been an endless fantasy of mankind, and it has also brought inexhaustible creative motivation to films. In the real world of Paris, Remy is a oil painting restorer. He is shy, cautious, and unremarkable. He and his wife Lucille have two lovely children and live a seemingly happy life. In another space-time, the people of the primitive tribe are also praying for God to give them a savior to get rid of the oppression of the giant Zoton, the ruler of the cannibal tribe.

Due to forgetting to turn off the tap, Remy's studio became flooded. While he was saving the oil paintings, he was suddenly sucked into the water. When he struggled out of the water, he found himself in a strange world where primeval forests were everywhere and people covered their bodies with animal skins. Remy lost his job, but worse still, his wife Lucille proposed divorce because she fell in love with someone else. The unfortunate Remy went to his parents' house for comfort. Just as he was pouring coffee, strange things happened again. Time seemed to be frozen at this moment, and he felt like being stuck on the floor. This time, Remy wasn't so lucky. When he arrived at another world, he just met the cannibal warriors having lunch together. To avoid becoming their meal, he had to run away quickly.

Because the insurance company refused to compensate, creditors came knocking on Remy's door, troubling him endlessly. At this time, he once again came to the primitive tribe. This time, all the men, women, and children of the tribe welcomed him. They believed that Remy was the savior - the Butterfly King. To verify what they thought, Remy had no choice but to jump from a tall tree, but the result was greatly disappointing. However, not being the savior meant being executed, which was not fun. Under such threats, Remy had no choice but to agree to help them get rid of the life of slavery. Led by Remy, everyone set off towards Zoton's tribe. On the way, they encountered two powerful warriors. Remy, who had to fight alone, displayed extraordinary power and defeated the strong enemies, making people more convinced that he was the savior sent by God.

Remy traveled back and forth between these two spaces and times. He even bought many strategic books including "The Art of War" by Sun Tzu to defend against external enemies. Finally, the day of confrontation between the two armies arrived. In order to return to the battlefield in time, Remy tried various methods in Paris, but unexpectedly appeared on the fiercely fought battlefield wearing only a little clothing while sleeping. According to the guidance in the book, Remy set up traps before dawn, then lured the enemy deep into the battlefield alone. By the time the enemy saw the archers on the high ground, it was too late to run. In an instant, arrows rained down on the battlefield, and the enemy scattered. Giant Zoton could only flee. Remy indeed achieved victory through unconventional means, and the voices of "Long live the king!" echoed incessantly on the battlefield. Remy finally tasted the feeling of commanding the country, full of pride, building city-states, repairing bridges, and experiencing the glory of being above ten thousand people.

Although in the real world, Remy is still a small character, he gradually changed. He was no longer timid and cowardly, but dared to live his true self and took responsibility willingly. All these changes were seen by Lucille. In another space-time, Remy wasn't so lucky. His people rose up in rebellion one after another, and Remy wanted to leave here forever, feeling disheartened. But at this moment, Giant Zoton attacked. After an exciting battle, Zoton slowly walked out of the battlefield, but then collapsed. The final victory still belonged to the intelligent and brave Remy.

When Remy bid farewell to the new female customer he had met, he unexpectedly fell into a coma. His entire family came to the emergency room, and at the end of the corridor, Remy's wife and the female customer who had a good impression of Remy were sitting separately. When Remy finally woke up, he walked towards his wife. Lucille thought they could reconcile, but Remy said they couldn't reunite, and Lucille left heartbroken. A month later, on the beach in Barcelona, Lucille was vacationing with her children when Remy suddenly appeared. It turned out that the people in love had never really separated.
