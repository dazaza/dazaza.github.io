60-Year-Old Mother Gains Popularity for Her Artistic Photoshoot, Phone Goes Black Due to Too Many Incoming Calls

by jyq2011668 on 2011-07-01 10:44:58

Spicy Mom's life photos with her husband.

The "Four Sisters" photo that made Aunt Ma an overnight sensation, fuel saver.

To respond to the overwhelming interviews, the inarticulate Spicy Mom had no choice but to call for help and asked her daughter, who had just started working at a company, to urgently take leave and rush home to "escort" her. What Spicy Mom didn't expect was that yesterday afternoon, Miss Wang from the director team of Qinghai Satellite TV’s *Flower in Full Bloom* saw our newspaper's report and immediately called, inviting Spicy Mom and her daughter to participate in their upcoming large-scale female singing talent show *Flower in Full Bloom*.

An overnight sensation, reporters from all major media in Suzhou swarmed in.

Spicy Mom: Urgently ask your daughter to take leave and come home to "escort."

Yesterday, as soon as our newspaper's article about Spicy Mom was online, it was instantly reprinted by major websites across the country such as People's Network, Xinhua News Agency, Sina, etc., becoming the hottest news of the day. Some websites even set up specific entries for this. I noticed that netizens were actively participating in discussions on this news event, creating quite a lively atmosphere.

What Aunt Ma didn’t expect was that because she and her daughter had so many relatives and friends spread all over the country, she began receiving congratulatory calls from places like Beijing and Shandong early yesterday morning. By noon, due to the high volume of calls, her phone remained continuously busy, eventually causing the screen to go black and rendering it unusable. “I’ll have to wait until tomorrow when I have time to get it repaired,” she said.

Aunt Ma told the reporter that after our newspaper's report was published yesterday, journalists from all major media in Suzhou rushed in. Having never experienced anything like this, Aunt Ma was momentarily flustered. Being inarticulate, she immediately called her already working daughter for help. Subsequently, Ms. Yin, who had just started working at the company, had to temporarily take leave and return home to "escort" her mother, accompanying her throughout the interviews with various media.

“Fortunately, everything went smoothly, and nothing unpleasant happened.” Last night, reflecting on the scene, Ms. Yin let out a long sigh. According to Ms. Yin, when she arrived at the company yesterday morning, a colleague approached her to congratulate her, saying that she and her mother had become celebrities, leaving her completely baffled. Upon further inquiry, she learned that our newspaper's report about them had gone viral online, becoming the hot news of the day.

Subsequently, her mother called for help, urging her to quickly return home to "escort" her. Since the company is a Sino-Japanese joint venture, Ms. Yin immediately took our newspaper to both the Chinese and Japanese general managers to request leave, and both approved her request on the spot. “Actually, many people in our company, including our boss, know my mother well. They have a good relationship, and they admire and approve of her positive and optimistic lifestyle. This special approval is actually recognition of my mother’s actions.”

Heated Discussions: Some praise her positive attitude, while others criticize her unconventional behavior.

Spicy Mom: Not bothered, as both her husband and daughter support her.

Regarding Aunt Ma's act of sharing "glamorous photos" online, netizens also had much to say. Many netizens expressed that Aunt Ma has a great mindset, something to envy! In fact, if people don't obsess over numbers, many can live younger lives. They wish her to continue living happily forever and hope that parents of her age will learn from Aunt Ma's youthful mindset! Change the way of living, be more carefree, and happier!

Of course, some netizens do not agree with this view, thinking Aunt Ma's behavior is a bit too unconventional, making it hard for them to accept. Some netizens' words were even quite harsh. A netizen commented, "Frankly speaking, I really don't want a mom like her. Bragging about moonlighting, is that something to be proud of?"

In response to these comments, some netizens immediately countered. They stated that parents maintaining good health is the best support for their children. Elderly people having a positive lifestyle deserves encouragement! A Sina netizen named "Hope Better" appealed: Children, life is short, and elderly people have the right to pursue their own interests. You will grow old too, so please be more understanding.

Regarding the mixed reviews online, Aunt Ma told the reporter that she was mentally prepared for this and remains very open-minded. She doesn't pay attention to unusual glances or words. "Anyway, my mindset has always been like this. Unhappy things pass overnight. Besides, my husband and daughter have always supported me."

According to Ms. Yin, Aunt Ma's daughter, all the calls they received yesterday were filled with blessings and congratulations. At present, most relatives and friends know about this incident, including neighbors, and the reactions have been positive. Everyone feels proud to have relatives or friends like them. "My father's community where he worked even called today, inviting my mother to perform during this year's March 8th Women's Day celebration! Of course, we cannot rule out that there might be a few people who don't understand us and gossip behind our backs."

Regarding her sudden rise to fame, Aunt Ma expressed surprise. This unexpected turn came entirely from her daughter's unintentional action. According to Ms. Yin, a while ago, while browsing online, she accidentally discovered that a certain forum in Suzhou was holding a "Charming Mommy Show" event. Participants could win prizes such as portrait vouchers and SAP coupons. Subsequently, she posted the recent family portraits taken with her mother under the activity thread. Unexpectedly, after seeing the photos, the organizer found them very interesting and asked her to post separately, allowing netizens to share the secrets of staying young and beautiful.

"Because my mom is just as fashionable as us and loves taking portraits, we often go together to take them." Ms. Yin explained that uploading the photos was purely for the purpose of winning a portrait voucher from the event organizers and then going together to take more portraits. She didn't expect it to cause such a big stir.

Opportunities Arise: Qinghai Satellite TV invites her and her daughter to participate in the selection event.

Spicy Mom: We will hold a family meeting to discuss and then decide.

Spicy Mom Aunt Ma's unconventional behavior has also attracted relevant attention. Yesterday afternoon, after seeing our newspaper's report, Miss Wang from the director team of Qinghai Satellite TV's *Flower in Full Bloom* immediately called, inviting Spicy Mom and her daughter to participate in their upcoming large-scale female singing talent show *Flower in Full Bloom*.

According to Miss Wang, *Flower in Full Bloom* is the first large-scale female singing talent show to receive approval from the State Administration of Radio, Film, and Television in 2011. The event will officially start in March this year. Yesterday, they learned online that Aunt Ma is very unique, so they wanted to invite Aunt Ma and her daughter to participate in their event.

Regarding this invitation, Aunt Ma's family expressed that she is just an ordinary retired female worker with a normal life, not articulate, and has never appeared so publicly before, let alone participated in any talent shows. If she now needs to step out and participate in such events, there will certainly be many difficulties. Recently, they will hold a family meeting, gather opinions from various relatives and friends, and then provide the final response. "Our family does enjoy singing and often goes to KTV rooms to sing. My mother sings averagely, but some Peking Opera pieces are sung exceptionally well."

Xue Ma Yi
