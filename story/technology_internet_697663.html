Advantages of Registering a Hong Kong Company

Hong Kong is an extremely information-developed international metropolis and one of the world's freest trading ports. With its well-established infrastructure and sound legal system, it provides entrepreneurs and businessmen with a unique business environment. Currently, more and more mainland businessmen are establishing their own companies in Hong Kong for various purposes, aiming to develop their business, enhance their company image, and increase overseas customers' confidence.

(1) Freedom in choosing a company name: Company names can include regional and industry-related terms, such as China, Beijing, Zhejiang, Hong Kong, USA, France, International, Asia, Academy, Publishing House, Association, Group, Holding, Industry, Development, Investment, Finance, Fellowship, Technology, Healthcare, Fund, Promotion Association, etc.

(2) Minimal restrictions on business scope: Clothing, furniture, jewelry, electronics, finance, medicine, shipping, import/export trade, real estate, construction, decoration, information networks, tourism, academies, cultural publishing, associations, research institutes, and other high-tech industries can all be part of our business. A Hong Kong limited company can conduct any business in Hong Kong except for illegal activities.

(3) Favorable low-tax environment: Hong Kong has low tax rates and fewer tax categories, which many international institutions use to achieve reasonable tax avoidance. When we establish a company in Hong Kong, we generally need to pay two types of taxes: One is a one-time capital registration stamp duty at a rate of 1/1000, which is determined based on your actual registered capital; the other is profits tax at a rate of 16.5%, calculated based on our actual profit (net profit). If the company does not make a profit, no tax is required. Except for tobacco and alcohol or special cases, there is no import/export tax in Hong Kong.

(4) Low registered capital with no requirement for capital verification: The Hong Kong government requires a minimum registered capital of HKD 10,000. You can increase the registered capital according to your actual situation without needing to transfer funds to Hong Kong banks, laying the foundation for establishing international groups.

(5) Hong Kong companies allow zero business operations and shell companies to exist. Establishing a Hong Kong shell company can become an effective advertising tool.

(6) A gateway to international markets and a stepping stone to enter the Chinese mainland market: Due to Hong Kong's unique historical conditions and geographical location, it creates favorable conditions for mainland enterprises to expand outward. Therefore, establishing a company in Hong Kong can serve as a window for external expansion, easily gaining the trust and cooperation of foreign companies. On the other hand, because China has just opened up, overseas investors prefer to set up regional headquarters in Hong Kong as a stepping stone for investing in China.

(7) Easy access to international credit and loans: As widely known, Hong Kong is the economic and financial center of Asia, with almost every street having a bank. We can leverage the credit from Hong Kong banks, which serves as the foundation for developing international business. After obtaining credit, we can use Hong Kong as a financial center for financing, directly issue letters of credit to overseas entities for large transactions with minimal capital. If needed, we can also apply for SME assistance loans from the Hong Kong government and invest this capital into the market for profit.

(8) Free flow of people, goods, and capital: Hong Kong is one of the freest and most prosperous trading ports in the world, with excellent infrastructure. Not only is the movement of people free, but so is the movement of goods and capital. The freedom of people movement is reflected in visa-free agreements with over 100 countries, and an upcoming visa-free travel plan with the Pearl River Delta. Goods move freely without tariffs, and logistics handling speed through sea, land, and air is very fast. Capital flows freely due to the absence of foreign exchange controls, allowing various currencies to be exchanged and transferred anytime without restriction. Businessmen often utilize the convenience of Hong Kong banks for issuing and receiving letters of credit.

(9) Hong Kong is one of the best places in the world due to its comprehensive intellectual property protection laws and global recognition mechanisms. Utilizing Hong Kong's reputation, you can create a Hong Kong brand. Given Hong Kong's significant international status, emerging companies often use Hong Kong's fame to package their own enterprises. For instance, Hong Kong's clothing, toys, and cultural publishing industries have certain competitive advantages internationally. Establishing such a company in Hong Kong allows you to immediately benefit from the overall international image and status, enhancing your company’s competitiveness to some extent.

(10) Opportunity to obtain Hong Kong residency: If your company has contributed to Hong Kong, you can apply for multiple business visas from the Immigration Department. After residing for seven years, you can acquire permanent residency in Hong Kong. Within this year, the Hong Kong government will launch an investment immigration program. For more information, please stay updated with announcements from the Hong Kong government.

(11) Superb financial system: Hong Kong is a super financial center where, apart from freely transferring remittances to various countries, over 200 financial services are available via internet and electronic transaction services, making banking operations very convenient.

(12) Lending, Mergers, and Financing: Hong Kong Limited Companies can apply for any bank services such as credit cards, bank loans, letter of credit services, and apply for any trust funds from the government to assist in business development.

(13) Limited Liability: A Hong Kong Limited Company is a legal and independent entity. Legally, you are completely separate from your limited company, and you do not bear any responsibility for the limited company.

(14) Reliable Confidence: Hong Kong is a global super financial center with a sound legal system, giving your partners and collaborators a trustworthy impression, undoubtedly beneficial for promoting and developing your business.
