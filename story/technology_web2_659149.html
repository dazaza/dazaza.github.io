The total length of the planned subway network in Gusu will reach 318 kilometers.

by jncheacd on 2012-02-05 15:06:30

The reporter learned from yesterday's expert review meeting on the final report of the revision of Suzhou Rail Transit Network Planning that, the total length of the rail transit network planning area in Suzhou for the long term (before 2050) is 318 kilometers, including 265 kilometers in the central urban area. Counting in the extended dotted segments (see figure), the network range of the planning area is 380 kilometers, including 286 kilometers in the central urban area. There are 181 stations in the central urban area and 37 transfer stations. Vice Mayor Zhu Jian Sheng was absent from the review meeting. The review meeting was jointly organized by the Municipal Planning Bureau and the Municipal Rail Transit Company, inviting seven experts from Beijing, Shanghai, Shenzhen and other places to form an expert group for the review. The expert group and the representatives listened to the report given by SYSTRA Company on the "Final Report of the Revision of Suzhou Rail Transit Network Planning" (hereinafter referred to as the "Report"), and the presentation given by Suzhou University on the "Passenger Flow Prediction Study of the Revision of Suzhou Rail Transit Network Planning".

The reviewers believed that, the "Report" made a reasonable analysis of the development trend and recent changes of Suzhou's overall city plan, as well as the population growth, job distribution, and travel characteristics of Suzhou, predicting the development trend. It analyzed and sorted out the original network and reasonably explained the necessity of revising the network. The proposed revision principles and objectives were reasonable, the work ideas and concepts were appropriate, and the understanding of urban development and current urban traffic conditions was relatively clear. Meanwhile, the recommended plan for the long-term network framework basically matched with the "T-shaped" overall spatial structure and functional layout formed by the "two centers, two areas, and two pieces" in the planned area of Suzhou. The core network consisting of lines 1, 2, 3, 4, 5, and 6 in the central urban area was basically reasonable.

The reviewers believed that, the "Report" basically achieved the research content requirements. According to the expert opinions, after supplementation and perfection, it can serve as the basis for the next stage of work. At the same time, the experts suggested that, based on the "Final Report of the Revision of Suzhou Rail Transit Network Planning", combined with the "Suzhou Urban Master Plan" and the "Comprehensive Urban Traffic Plan", corresponding to the "Construction Plan", carefully sort out and adjust, combining with the recent development trends of the city and various districts, compile the "Suzhou Urban Rail Transit Network Plan" to provide a working basis for the "Construction Plan".
