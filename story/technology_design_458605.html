REDFLY Smartphone Companion

Highly Praised by "The New York Times" and "Yahoo! TECH"

Operate your smartphone on a larger screen and keyboard, expanding your daily tasks beyond the small world.

Smartphones absolutely meet the needs of professionals for a mobile office, but when working on a smartphone for long periods, you'll find that typing emails, browsing the web, and handling documents on a smartphone's keyboard and screen is a daunting task. The REDFLY Smartphone Companion (REDFLY Mobile Companion, hereinafter referred to as REDFLY), developed by the U.S.-based company Celio Corp, specializing in smartphone accessories, will surely free you from the troubles of a smartphone's small screen and keyboard.

Winner of the 2008 CES, the largest consumer electronics show in the United States - 2008 CES Innovation Award for Enabling Technology, Revolutionizes the Industry with New Mobile Solution for Smartphones, and highly praised by "The New York Times" and "Yahoo! TECH", these accolades are solid proof of REDFLY's strength!

REDFLY is very simple to use, compact in design, measuring only 25mm (thick) x 150mm (wide) x 225mm (long), making it convenient to carry. Owning a comfortable mobile office has never been so easy and convenient.

Larger and clearer screen, paired with a QWERTY keyboard, for more comfortable operation

Connect your smartphone to REDFLY, and the content on your phone will instantly appear on REDFLY's 8-inch VGA screen. Through REDFLY's 8.3-inch QWERTY keyboard and touchpad mouse (Touchpad Mouse), you can control the data within your phone. For users who frequently send and receive emails, browse the web, or handle files on their smartphones, REDFLY not only improves comfort but also increases efficiency.

REDFLY can also be connected to a projector, allowing you to display presentations, charts, photos, or other information from your phone during meetings.

Battery life up to 8 hours

REDFLY can operate continuously for up to 8 hours. After each charge, it can handle daily work without the need to constantly carry a charger, making file handling worry-free.

Simple connection method, easy operation

REDFLY is compatible with smartphones using Windows Mobile? 5.0 and 6.0 operating systems (Frances: Waiting Alex to test for WM6.1). Simply install the REDFLY software on your smartphone and connect it to REDFLY via USB or Bluetooth, and the contents of your smartphone will be fully displayed on REDFLY's screen without any synchronization or function adjustments required. Additionally, since REDFLY uses the same operating system as your smartphone (no additional operating system needed), users do not need to relearn anything.

Make full use of smartphone functions Wi-Fi / HSDPA/ GPS all-in-one

Handling emails and browsing the web on your smartphone through REDFLY is no longer a chore. Whether your phone is online via HSDPA/Wi-Fi or using GPS navigation, REDFLY can navigate the internet without requiring any additional software or hardware installation.

Smartphones equipped with LogMeIn software can log into another computer anytime, anywhere. With REDFLY, users can remotely handle their office or home computers via their phones with greater satisfaction. (Frances: Alex, please advise if you have any concerns about the LogMeIn description)

No need to worry about data loss or virus risks

Any files processed on REDFLY will be stored within the smartphone, eliminating the risk of data loss. Moreover, since REDFLY does not require an installed operating system, it not only avoids virus risks but also eliminates the time-consuming process of updating and managing the operating system.
