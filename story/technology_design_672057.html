How to distinguish the quality of breast implant materials

by huamei9727 on 2012-02-22 15:22:08

Introduction: In order to have a perfect figure, many women choose breast augmentation surgery. Currently, the more common breast augmentation surgery is prosthetic breast enlargement. One of the key factors affecting the effect of breast augmentation surgery lies in the quality of the breast implant. How to distinguish between good and bad breast implants? The following will introduce methods on how to identify the quality of breast implants.

The surgical outcome is related not only to the doctor's skills and the hospital's equipment but also closely linked to the materials used in the surgery. Breast augmentation surgery commonly uses certain types of implants as shaping materials. The quality of breast implants directly affects the surgical outcome. So, how can one differentiate between good and bad breast implants? Here, I will introduce you to a few methods to help you determine the quality of breast implants.

How to distinguish between good and bad breast implants?

1. Complete certification: Business license or operating permit, production license, product qualification certificate.

2. Product number, patients will receive one, and the plastic surgeon will keep one in the medical record.

3. Insurance, all such products are insured.

4. Do not overly trust so-called "imported products" or expensive products.

How to choose the size of breast implants?

If the physical examination is qualified, experts will clearly understand the desired breast size of the person undergoing breast augmentation. Based on their overall and local conditions, they will estimate how much silicone gel is needed for each side. Choosing breast size mainly refers to factors such as age, chest shape (narrow or wide), body shape (fat or thin), height, skin tightness, etc. Generally speaking, taller and heavier women tend to have relatively larger breasts.

How long can breast implants last?

Previously, it was said that implants should be replaced after a certain number of years. However, currently, the understanding among plastic surgeons is that they can be implanted for life and do not need to be replaced. The longest duration of silicone gel implants in foreign countries is 35 years, with no toxic side effects found so far. Moreover, under special circumstances, breast implants can be completely removed without affecting breast function and restoring the original shape.

The above are some methods on how to distinguish between good and bad breast implants. If you want to learn more, you can consult online with relevant plastic surgery hospitals and doctors.
