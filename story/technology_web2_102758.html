webnode: Lets you build websites and blogs for free

by xjyzhenai on 2008-11-12 17:00:49

In the Internet age, it is no longer uncommon for individuals to own websites and blogs. But there are a lot of people who, for many reasons, have a hard time owning their own website or blog. Webnode brings good news for these people.

The Webnode system is a free tool that can completely manage your entire web project. Anyone, without any technical know-how, can create and manage advanced web presentation systems very easily. There is no need to install any software on your computer or server. Everything is through your web browser.

image

Registered users of Webnode have a web space and an administrative backend. All of the tools mentioned above are controllable in the admin background.

Webnode includes a revolutionary website generation tool (Webnode generation) :

Webnode generation enables users to build a website easily and quickly. This is a pioneering network management tool for both individuals and companies. You can use it to make a standard website, blog, photo gallery, e-store, product catalog and other powerful web pages. The creation process is a simple drag and drop operation, rather than time-consuming programming, or using complex administrative tools.

Fully interactive web editing lets you add website content (images, surveys, forums, etc.) to your web page easily by dragging and dropping them from the webnode toolbar. You can create flexible menus to manage departments on your articles or customize dynamic listings. Use bulk image uploads to create beautiful photo effects. Zoom, rotate, crop, and restore images in your browser. You can also create your own templates to produce high-end unique websites, as well as gorgeous content.

Advanced website statistics (Webnode statistics) :

Webnode Statistics is an outstanding marketing tool that monitors and analyzes traffic to your website. Detailed analysis includes page views, visitors, data transfer, browser type, search engine, keywords and more. Webnode statistics can help you conduct behavioral research on visitors. Find out where your site is working on your goals. Visits, page views, visits, First visits, unique users, repeat customers, new visitors, showcases, singles and bounce rates.

Mail (Webnode email) :

Webnode Email is a powerful email solution for reading and managing your email. You have a simple interface to your webnode live email account. You can manage your mail with folders, such as folders with file archiving and protection. Use keywords to search for emails or attachments immediately. All email writing and formatting is done online. Webnode email contains contact manager, an intelligent spam detection system and antivirus. You can also use the IMAP protocol to access your mailbox.
