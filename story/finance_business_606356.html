One false move could result in death In March 2007, during a video conference, Eskeil performed a dangerous balance game above a glacier waterfall in the Norwegian national park: Two ropes were strung 300 meters above the waterfall, and a block of ice 60 centimeters long and 35 centimeters wide was placed on the ropes.
