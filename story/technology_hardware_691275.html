Traffic police guard a mother and baby under the blazing sun (Photo)

by xue94fwsh on 2012-03-05 15:12:31

Near Hong'en Temple in Jiangbei District, under the scorching sun, a traffic police officer held an umbrella for a citizen carrying a child. Netizen photo

Focus on Lei Feng-style police team - People-friendly chapter

Under the blazing sun, holding umbrellas for citizens, opening days to report work to citizens, distributing "love relay cards" to citizens... Since the beginning of this year, Chongqing's public security authorities have carried out activities titled "Everyone does good deeds, strive to be a living Lei Feng." With a people-friendly and love-for-people attitude, our city's police officers have composed vivid and moving chapters of "close ties between police and people." One by one "living Lei Feng," one by one real-life versions of "Ma Tianmin" are appearing around us... We joyfully see that "people-friendly and love-for-people" is becoming a prevailing trend in this city.

Report by our newspaper (reporter Zhang Zhang, intern Xiang Qing) Last May, during a handling of a public dispute, traffic police officer Zhang Yang from the Jiangbei City traffic police platform helped an elderly person with injuries carry a burden uphill, and was photographed by a netizen. After being published in our newspaper, Zhang Yang was hailed as the "most beautiful traffic police officer." Recently, Chongqing has again seen the "most beautiful traffic police officer." A mother stripped down to protect her child from a swarm of bees and died after being stung. A netizen posted such a photo on websites like Tianya Community and Liangjiang Forum: Under the scorching sun, a woman holding a baby crossed the road, and a traffic police officer voluntarily held an umbrella for her to provide coolness...

Frozen Moment

Holding an umbrella under the scorching sun to escort a mother and child

On July 14, netizen "ZJY1" posted a thread on the Liangjiang Forum titled "I saw such a scene near Hong'en Temple Park in Jiangbei..." The post mentioned seeing a traffic police officer holding an umbrella for a woman holding a baby to shield her from the sun. That day, the outdoor temperature was 40°C, and the traffic police officer preferred to expose himself to the sun rather than let the crowd suffer, leaving a very impactful and visually striking impression. "He endured the scorching sun to provide coolness for others, bravo!" said "ZJY1." This is undoubtedly another "most beautiful back view" of a traffic police officer. From the photo, we can see two figures' backs, with the traffic police officer holding an umbrella for the woman holding a baby, escorting her across the zebra crossing. The shadow cast by the umbrella under the scorching sun perfectly covered the woman holding the baby, while the umbrella-holding traffic police officer was exposed to the blazing sun.

Moved

Netizens rush to repost and tweet

Perhaps it was just an unintentional act by the traffic police, but it attracted the attention of many netizens. After the eyewitness posted the thread, netizens from forums like Tianya Community and Dayu Community quickly forwarded it. Some netizens even reposted the picture on their own microblogs. By yesterday, the thread had been clicked over ten thousand times, with netizens who replied expressing their approval. Netizen "Guoguo ABCABC" said that the traffic police officer was very thoughtful in holding the umbrella, completely shielding the mother and the baby. Although it was just a small matter, this behavior deserves affirmation! Netizen "Desert Navy" strongly supported, stating that this traffic police officer demonstrated what serving the people means through his actions... Netizen "Wei Liang" said that after posting the photo on his microblog, more than a thousand netizens had already forwarded it, and the number of microblog "fans" had increased by several hundred.

Humility

Helping to hold an umbrella in the heat is part of the job

Yesterday afternoon, I went to the Hong'en Temple traffic police platform in Jiangbei, and after asking around, finally found the traffic police officer holding the umbrella in the post, Deputy Captain Zhou Kai of the Hong'en Temple traffic police platform. "This is just a trivial effort, something we should do anyway!" Zhou Kai recalled that at around 12:30 PM on July 10th, the weather was hot, and a drenched woman holding a baby came to the platform wanting to borrow a convenience umbrella to take home. During registration, Zhou Kai, who was on duty, discovered that the woman's registered address was in the nearby Hong'en Yajuan community. He noticed that the baby was already asleep, and its mother, having borrowed the umbrella, couldn't find a hand free to hold it, so he voluntarily escorted the mother and child home.

"This kind-hearted man has done a lot of good deeds; he's naturally warm-hearted." Zhou Kai's colleague Ma Menyin said that on a day in May, after work, Zhou Kai spent more than two hours patiently helping a blind man look up information and personally sent him to the shelter. "He has always been our role model." Ma Menyin said that Zhou Kai won the title of one of the top ten traffic police officers in Jiangbei last year, which was earned through hard work. Despite suffering from severe gout in his right foot, he still persisted in working while ill.

Revealing

The original "most beautiful traffic police officer" sponsors twins to attend university

Report by our newspaper (reporter Zhang Zhang) The photo of Zhou Kai holding an umbrella for a woman carrying a baby was called by netizens as a reenactment of the "most beautiful back view." Coincidentally, the original character of the "most beautiful back view" is none other than Zhang Yang, who belongs to the same Jiangbei Traffic Police Brigade as Zhou Kai. Over the past year or so, Zhang Yang has always regarded being close to the people as part of his life.

A pair of twin sisters, Xu Wenqi and Xu Wenxi, who live near Wuli Store and come from a financially struggling family but excel academically, are students at Southwest University. After their parents divorced, sister Xu Wenqi was raised by her father, who works as a construction worker, while sister Xu Wenxi lives with her unemployed mother and paralyzed grandmother. The grandmother's monthly retirement pension of more than 1,000 yuan is the entire family's income, making life difficult. In September last year, upon learning about the difficulties faced by the Xu family sisters, Zhang Yang took out 3,000 yuan and handed it to sister Xu Wenxi, indicating that he would sponsor the financially struggling sisters until they finish university. Xu Wenxi said that Brother Zhang Yang is not only handsome but also very approachable, and we all like him.

If you have passed on the "love relay card," then the good deeds you have done can be checked online. This is a new method adopted by the Jiangbei Traffic Police Brigade in the "everyone does good deeds, strive to be a living Lei Feng" activity, methods for treating hemorrhoids. This brigade selected 27 highly educated, high-quality, and highly responsible police officers in May this year to form the city's first traffic police "love service vanguard team," and Zhang Yang volunteered to serve as the captain of the vanguard team. Zhang Yang and his colleagues use holidays to do good deeds for the public and handle practical matters, simultaneously influencing and bringing along colleagues, families, and friends around them to participate in the "everyone does good deeds, strive to be a living Lei Feng" activity, and distributed 10,000 love relay cards to citizens.

Link

Jiangbei "Police Camp Open Day" where police officers report to residents

Report by our newspaper (reporter Zhang Zhang) In the monthly "Police Camp Open Day" activities organized by the Jiangbei District Public Security Bureau, community police officers reporting to residents is an indispensable people-friendly activity. Yesterday, Director Zheng Ce of the Political Department of the Jiangbei District Public Security Bureau stated that this represents the police's people-friendly and love-for-people attitude.

"From July 2010 to now, we have conducted collection and registration for 4,031 households and 16,453 people in our jurisdiction..." At 10 AM on July 23rd, Community Police Officer Zhang Fuhai from the Hongtudi community in Wuli Store began his report with a string of data. After the report, community residents scored and placed their evaluation opinions into the ballot box.

Zheng Ce indicated that the Jiangbei District Public Security Bureau's Public Order Brigade will organize and sort the residents' evaluation opinions, ultimately deriving the comprehensive scores of community police officers and police clerks, which will be included in the individual annual assessment, and those who fail will be suspended from duty.
