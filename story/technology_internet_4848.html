Baidu Legal Search

by cxq on 2006-08-20 19:43:00

Baidu Legal Search is a professional legal search tool jointly launched by Baidu and Peking University Yinghua Company. It provides various legal articles from the founding of the People's Republic of China to the present, including central and local laws, a large number of rich legal cases, judicial documents, and a legal dictionary, offering convenience for you to find relevant legal materials. If you have any questions, please visit the Baidu Legal Search forum to communicate and share with more people.

Due to work requirements, I often look for various laws and regulations and have been using the New Regulations Express service. When comparing Baidu Legal Search, I found that New Regulations Express has the following advantages: 1. It can accurately locate a specific law rather than just related regulations or normative documents, which is very useful when looking for legal basis. Baidu cannot quickly locate in this aspect, and I hope Baidu adds the "issuing authority" function in the search options for accurate location search, so as to accurately find specific laws; 2. It provides the latest information on laws and regulations, making it convenient for users to know what new laws have been issued recently, better meeting user needs.

Of course, since Baidu launched its legal search not long ago, it is currently further improving various functions to meet market demands. We also look forward to Baidu Legal Search becoming more practical for work use.
