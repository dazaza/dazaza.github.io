I Learned to Take Care of My Teeth - Hu Mingchang, Class 1, Grade 2, Mashang Central Elementary School

When I first went to kindergarten, I thought it was fun to see my parents brushing their teeth, so I nagged my mom to go to the supermarket to buy a toothbrush and toothpaste for me. I started brushing my teeth like adults, initially doing it seriously, but after a few days, I just brushed casually.

In elementary school, one day my tooth suddenly started hurting, and my mom quickly took me to see a dentist. The doctor looked at my teeth and said: "Your teeth are decayed." I said: "I brush my teeth." The doctor said: "You definitely didn't brush seriously. Let me tell you how to brush." You should brush twice a day, in the morning and evening. When brushing, start with the inner wall of the teeth, then brush the outer side. The toothbrush should form a 45-degree angle with the teeth and gums. I nodded and said to the doctor: "I know how to take care of my teeth." Since then, I've followed the doctor's advice and brushed my teeth seriously every day without being lazy.

Take Care of Your Teeth - Zhou Ziheng, Class 1, Grade 2, Mashang Central Elementary School

Good teeth mean a good appetite. Brushing your teeth morning and evening is something we should all do, and brushing at night is especially important. Food residue stuck between the teeth ferments over time, turning white teeth black, and eventually causing toothache. Additionally, protecting teeth requires eating less sugar, rinsing with saltwater more often, brushing along the gaps between teeth, and repairing cavities promptly if they appear. If you want a set of white teeth, take care of your teeth.

Tooth Extraction Diary - Song Xincheng, Class 1, Grade 2, Mashang Central Elementary School

When I was in kindergarten, I started losing my baby teeth. Two of my bottom teeth were loose for a long time without falling out, while new teeth grew in. My mom was worried that my teeth would become misaligned, so she took me to the dental hospital.

The doctor looked at my teeth and said: "The loose teeth need to be extracted." When I heard about the extraction, I hid behind my mom in fear. My mom said: "Son, for your teeth to be aligned, be brave!" The doctor said: "It won't hurt. Come here, let me disinfect your mouth first." I timidly walked to the doctor and only saw him use tweezers to wipe some cotton on my teeth. I thought the extraction would happen later after disinfection, so I relaxed. But at that moment, the doctor quickly pulled out my tooth. I felt a sharp pain in my mouth. The doctor had already pressed a piece of cotton on the bleeding area. This tooth extraction made me realize how difficult it is to lose baby teeth. From now on, I will take good care of my teeth and hope my friends join me in taking care of our teeth.

The Benefits of Brushing Teeth - Yu Jiahui, Class 1, Grade 2, Mashang Central Elementary School

I hope to have a set of snow-white, beautiful, and healthy teeth forever with me.

First, you must develop the habit of brushing your teeth every day, brushing twice a day, each time for no less than 3 minutes. Second, rinse your mouth after eating sweets to keep your teeth clean.

At first, it didn't hurt much, but not long after, it hurt so much that I couldn't eat. As the saying goes, "Toothache isn't a disease, but it hurts like hell." My parents took me to the dental hospital, and the dentist examined me and said: "Cavities, need to fill them." So the dentist used a drill and a turbine to grind away the infected part of the bad tooth, and it hurt so much that I screamed. At that moment, I secretly decided that I must take good care of my teeth and protect them.

Finally, go to the dental hospital regularly for check-ups to ensure "Good teeth, good appetite, body feels great, everything tastes good."
