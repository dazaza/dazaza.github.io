Today's market has indeed attacked the 2300-point level again, and then started to fall under the guidance of a big drop in non-ferrous metals, coal, and cement stocks. The pharmaceutical sector I emphasized yesterday, especially traditional Chinese medicine (TCM) stocks, as well as liquor brewing stocks, have risen overall. The rally of coal and metal stocks has temporarily taken a break, while the "drinking and eating" sector is beginning to deepen.

Today, the sharp decline in non-ferrous metals and coal has triggered some capital outflows. Cement also fell sharply due to environmental protection standards. In fact, good companies are not afraid of strict environmental protection standards. Those that do not meet the standards will be eliminated, and those that do will gain more market share. For some cement stocks, this is not bad news but good news. The pharmaceutical stocks and liquor brewing stocks I have been talking about for these two days rose overall today. More and more funds are entering. Their market performance has just begun. Especially TCM stocks, which are clearly benefiting from favorable conditions, the more unique formulas they have, the more market potential they possess. And low-priced liquor stocks as well as brewing stocks, since they are necessities of life, their growth and development are inevitable. As I mentioned yesterday, historically at the start of a bull market, coal and non-ferrous metals move first, and when they take a rest, it’s time to "drink and eat." So please pay more attention.

The CSRC and the Social Security Fund Council said pension funds are about to enter the market, while the statement by the spokesperson of the Ministry of Human Resources and Social Security about no immediate plan for pension funds to enter the market has already made its position clear. Today, relevant officials from the Guangdong Provincial Department of Human Resources and Social Security stated that the pension funds under their jurisdiction are still deposited in banks. Who is lying? Moreover, the international board that terrifies retail investors has caused another stir. Shanghai Financial Office Director Fang Xinghai said at a press conference yesterday that the international board will definitely be launched in the future, but needs to wait for mature conditions. The market had just improved for a few days, and official news was flying everywhere.
