Set against the backdrop of the Tang Dynasty court, it tells of the power struggles within the four rooms responsible for preparing the empress's attire. These four constantly vie for power and position, forcing the innocent Sanhao and Jinling to become scapegoats. Despite enduring much hardship, Sanhao remains steadfast in her three goodnesses philosophy, while Jinling, after being mistreated, seeks revenge.
