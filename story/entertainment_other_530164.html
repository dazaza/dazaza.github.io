Beautiful woman in Chengdu puts out Weibo notice for her own marriage: Banquet has been booked, only the bridegroom is missing

by mlwang on 2011-11-01 16:08:07

"Before November 11, I must get myself married! Tomorrow I will book a hotel, and after the hotel is arranged, I will look for a groom..." On October 28, a matrimonial Weibo post caught the attention of many netizens. As everyone was debating the authenticity of this event, @Chengdu Qin WaWa then released another Weibo post: "I have already booked ten tables for the banquet, paid the deposit, and selected the dishes." Between the lines, it implied that everything was ready except for the groom.

Yesterday afternoon, the reporter met with the blogger @Chengdu Qin WaWa. The girl said that she had just graduated in July from the music department of a university in Sichuan Province. She currently works as an administrative staff member at a cultural company and also does some part-time performances, playing the yangqin, singing, and dancing.

Regarding the confusion among netizens, she stated that she truly wanted to get married, not as a joke. "I started school early, so my classmates are generally a few years older than me, and quite a few of my junior high and high school classmates have already gotten married." She is from Jiangxi but feels lonely sometimes being alone in Chengdu, wanting to find someone who truly loves her. She seeks a stable life, including financial stability and a calm mindset.

The reason she posted a matrimonial advertisement on Weibo was due to her recent breakup with her boyfriend. Her ex-boyfriend was her high school classmate, studying in Chengdu, and graduating this year. She had proposed marriage, but her ex-boyfriend repeatedly declined, citing "the career is still in its infancy, and the economic foundation is insufficient." In early October, they broke up. "Posting the matrimonial advertisement on Weibo actually involved very complex emotions, partly out of spite towards him, but more importantly, it was the urgent desire to get married."

Regarding the condition mentioned in the matrimonial advertisement of "having good chemistry," she said it meant finding someone reliable, not too far away, and not too old. Someone with ambition, not necessarily rich, but also not too poor.

About booking the wedding banquet before finding her other half, "Qin WaWa" said, "If I want to do it, I'll just do it." Some friends asked if she had gone crazy, but she thought it was a romantic and correct thing to do. She indicated that once she finds the right person, the wedding banquet would definitely be held on the same day.

If she doesn't find someone "with good chemistry" by November 11, what would she do? When the reporter raised this question, she hesitated for a moment, "I think I will definitely find someone." She then confidently said.

Yesterday afternoon, the reporter called "Qin WaWa's" ex-boyfriend, Xiao Xu. After explaining the situation to Xiao Xu, he was surprised: "I didn't expect her to do this." Xiao Xu said he wasn't following "Qin WaWa" on Weibo, so he didn't know about these events happening on Weibo. "She mentioned marriage before, but I felt that both of our economic foundations were very weak, and it wasn't the time to get married," said Xiao Xu, who now teaches art at an art tutoring class and paints watercolors in his spare time. He said their living places were far apart, he was busy with work, and running back and forth every day was exhausting, leading to their eventual breakup.

Xiao Xu expressed that although there was no possibility of reconciliation between them, he still wished "Qin WaWa" all the best. "As long as she wants to do something and believes it's right, I support her."

Yesterday afternoon, the reporter called the hotel where "Qin WaWa" booked the banquet, provided her real name, and the staff confirmed that indeed a lady had booked ten tables for lunch on the 11th, each table costing 1588 yuan, with a 3000 yuan deposit already paid.

"Qin WaWa" said that she did not tell her parents about breaking up with Xiao Xu, "I will first hold the wedding banquet and then inform my parents afterward."
