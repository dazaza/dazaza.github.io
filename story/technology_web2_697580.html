5 electric fire trucks serve old communities with 24-hour patrols through the back streets and alleys. The new use of electric fire trucks is introduced by the street office director. There are more than 870 alleys in the jurisdiction where ordinary fire trucks cannot enter. Now, with this "mini" electric fire truck, it can not only patrol for fire hazards and extinguish fires but also check and control illegal buildings, making it very practical. Yesterday, the smallest electric fire truck in Wuhan was showcased at Changfeng Street in Qiaokou District. Yesterday, three old communities in Wujia Mountain Street, Dongxihu District, welcomed the electric fire trucks that can patrol 24 hours a day. Equipped with fire sirens, fire axes, large wrenches and other fire-fighting tools, as well as water guns that can spray water up to 15 meters away and nearly 10 meters high. According to Yang Shengtao, the team leader of the special duty class of Wujia Mountain Fire Station, the community electric fire truck is specially equipped by the municipal government for residents. It is light and convenient, can shuttle through the back streets and alleys, and promptly put out fires, reducing residents' losses. It is understood that these 3 community electric fire trucks are mainly used in densely populated, old residential areas concentrated in Qiajia Mountain, Wenjiawan, and Hengchunli.
