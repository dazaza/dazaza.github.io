IV. Why didn't I see the Yellow Diamond mark after activating it?

It may be due to data not being synchronized. It is recommended that you re-login to QQ or check again after 24 hours after activating the Yellow Diamond Aristocrat. You can also try to "Refresh" under the statistics in your personal information. If you choose to activate the Yellow Diamond via mobile phone, please confirm whether your mobile phone has been bound to the QQ number for activation. For detailed activation methods, please refer to "Mobile Phone Activation Yellow Diamond Method" or "Little Smart Phone Activation Yellow Diamond Method". Thank you!

V. Why didn't the nutrition index increase after activating Yellow Diamond?

For users who activated Yellow Diamond this month, the nutrition index will not increase this month. The nutrition index will start increasing from the 1st to the 10th day of the next month after you activate the Yellow Diamond Aristocrat service, with an index amount of 100 points.

Note: There is no full score for flower vines, and 2000 points is a stage. When the nutrition index reaches 2000 points and any other index has not reached 200 points, the nutrition index will not increase.

VI. Why did my saved space scheme disappear?

It might be because your Yellow Diamond service has expired. On the second day after expiration, the saved schemes in your space will be cleared. You just need to activate the Yellow Diamond service again to start decorating again. It could also be due to a busy server at the time, which failed to successfully retrieve relevant data. It is suggested that you can check after "Refreshing" under the statistics in your personal information. It might be due to your password being leaked; it is suggested that you modify your password first and then redecorate or choose to restore historical decorations. Thank you!

VII. Can Yellow Diamond give gifts to friends for free?

Currently, activating Yellow Diamond only supports saving common items for free, and does not support giving related items to friends. You can use Q-coin or Qzone shopping vouchers for payment to give gifts to friends.
