Bilingual education without teaching: the best early education teacher for children

Nowadays, more and more parents are using methods like Xiao Wu's "animation early education" to give their children English early education. Along with this trend, an early education animation called "No Need to Teach Bilingual" is beginning to enter more and more families.

Watch animations to learn bilingual? Experts reveal the secrets of "No Need to Teach Bilingual"

Experts remind parents: the choice of measures for young children learning English is the most critical. Before the age of 6, children mainly need to feel the language context, stimulate interest, and develop habits. During this period, a good language environment should be provided for the child, guiding them to see, listen, and speak more. The child should choose a suitable and fun learning method that combines life with English; let the child learn happily while playing. For example, watching English background animations with the child, listening to English songs, and having simple conversations with the child are all effective ways for young children to learn English early.

Regarding the heated discussion among parents about the "early childhood phenomenon" of English early education, some early education experts say: young children learning foreign languages is not necessarily "pulling up seedlings to help them grow". In fact, many people who started learning two languages from an early age can speak fluent English with standard pronunciation. Abroad, it is not uncommon to see children proficient in three or four languages from a young age. Ages 0-6 are the best period for children to learn languages. Under the premise of good teaching methods, learning foreign languages should start early rather than late.

Early education instance: Fresh mom uses "animation" to teach bilingual

"The most effective way to teach babies foreign languages is through play. I do not agree with traditional rote memorization learning methods. So when I came across 'animation early education', I was deeply attracted. Watching a few episodes of 'No Need to Teach Bilingual' every day, as the animation plot develops, the baby happily watches the animation and learns many simple English dialogues and words in a relaxed and joyful atmosphere, just like learning the mother tongue, basically without my teaching, saving me a lot of time and energy, and also saving a considerable amount of early education fees."

The practices of these parents in the news have caused quite a bit of controversy. Most parents express deep agreement, saying that their babies started learning English very early to help them build a solid foreign language foundation; while some other parents express their concerns, will early English learning put too much pressure on the child, resulting in the opposite effect of "pulling up seedlings to help them grow"?

Compared to adults, young children have certain advantages in learning English. Children are in the sensitive and critical period of language learning, with strong language imitation ability, pure control of pronunciation, and amazing learning ability. The animation tutorial "No Need to Teach Bilingual" uses the development of animation plots, clever design of images, and changes in character thinking to present multi-screen images in the baby's mind, allowing the baby to fully understand the content of the story, gradually activating the right brain of the baby, possessing super memory, enabling the baby to understand and learn English, breaking away from the boring mode of traditional education "rote memorization". Let the child learn English happily in an interesting story environment, and easily develop a second language.
