Common Q&A in home decoration

by longersing on 2009-09-30 18:25:29

The common questions and answers about home decoration include: What should be paid attention to when laying the floor specifically? How to choose fine woodworking boards? What are fine woodworking boards? How to choose a stainless steel sink? What aspects does the design cover? All these questions will be answered here one by one.

Question One: What should be paid attention to when laying the floor specifically?

Answer: It's best to buy the floor from a certain seller and have them install it, so they can't shirk responsibility. The subfloor of the floor and moisture content is a quantified index. Generally, the moisture content of the ground and wooden beams should be less than 16%, so that the floor won't get damp. Also, plastic film, moisture-proof pads, and the beams must be laid relatively flat.

Question Two: How to choose fine woodworking boards? What are fine woodworking boards?

Answer: Fine woodworking boards are processed with solid wood core and veneer outside, commonly known as large core boards. Its characteristics are high strength, firm quality, and good screw fastening performance. Currently, it is the main promoted material in the home decoration industry. About how to choose: There are many brands of plywood on the market. When purchasing, pay attention to three major items: first is its environmental protection index, which generally needs to reach E1 level or above, second is its board thickness, which is generally 1.8cm. Third is the flatness. It is recommended to consider brands like Jinfuxiang, Tubaobao, and Lvye when buying.

Question Three: How to choose a stainless steel sink?

Answer: One, Classification of stainless steel sink materials: 1. 304# stainless steel, 2. 202# stainless steel, 3. 201# stainless steel. The biggest difference among the above types of stainless steel materials lies in the difference in corrosion resistance performance, with 304 being the best, followed by 202, and then 201. Of course, the price difference is also significant. So please make sure to ask the merchant clearly when purchasing to avoid being deceived. It’s best to have a quality guarantee certificate from the manufacturer. Two, Surface treatment classification: 1. Pearl silver surface, 2. Sandblasted brushed surface, 3. Satin surface, 4. Polished mirror surface.

Question Four: What aspects does the design cover?

Answer: 1. Reorganization of space - Using design techniques such as connection, transition, contrast, unity, and reality vs. illusion to create a reasonable indoor space environment. This is commonly referred to as the overall design concept. 2. Space interface processing - Structural and surface decoration processing of walls, floors, and ceilings, color matching and response between each other, determining construction technology and requirements. This is commonly referred to as basic renovation project design. 3. Built-in object design - Designing indoor furniture, decorative structures, decorative embellishments, special components, etc., including material usage, detailed structure, process handling, special materials, construction requirements, etc.
