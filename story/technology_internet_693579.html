1) Three aspects: finger trimming, massage, and finger painting. Nail correction should not use scissors or nail clippers but instead use specialized nail files for smoother, longer nails. The choice of nail shape generally depends on the hand shape. For slender fingers, round nails can be chosen. For short and thick fingers, pointed arcs are preferred. There is a half-circle of dry skin at the base of the nail; this dry skin should be removed before applying nail polish. Soak your fingers in a container with cuticle softener, then use a cuticle remover stick wrapped with cotton paper to lift off the dry skin.

2) Nail massage involves applying a layer of nail nutrition cream at the base of the nail and using the thumb of the other hand to gently circle and massage on the nail surface. You can also soak your fingers in olive oil at a temperature of 20-30℃ for 2-3 minutes, then wipe clean, which effectively makes the nails soft and smooth.

3) When applying nail polish, keep the finger being painted still. The order is left hand first, then right hand; start with the little finger, followed by the ring finger, middle finger, index finger, and finally the thumb. There are techniques in applying nail polish: apply the base color first, thinly, ensuring every part of the nail is covered for a flat surface. After the base color dries, officially apply the colored nail polish, starting from the middle of the nail, then the sides, quickly and continuously. If the nail shape is relatively wide, when officially applying the colored nail polish, don't fully cover the edges of the nail to give a visual effect of long nails.

Additionally, here are some tips for those who want to learn carefully!

After applying the protective nail oil and base color, wait for it to completely dry. Short nails suit dark colors such as deep blue or black as the base color, which looks good and cool. Apply it in two coats for saturated color. Long nails can choose white or transparent as the base color.

Recommendations: Short nails - deep blue-purple base color; Long nails - transparent or white as the base color.

Dark base color + glitter nail polish; White base color + silver blue; Transparent base color + metallic green.

3) Use white or glitter nail polish to draw an arc at the front end of the nail. For beginners, it might be difficult to master the curvature of the smile line. You can first adjust the arc with a double eyelid tape and stick it on the nail (be careful not to stick too tightly, as it needs to be peeled off later), exposing the part of the smile line. Then paint the exposed part of the smile line with nail polish as usual.

Fill up the part from the front end of the nail to the fingertip, wait for it to dry, and then gently peel off the double eyelid tape. A beautiful smile line appears.
