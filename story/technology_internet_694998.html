New Green Tea Weight Loss Formula One: Coix Green Tea

Ingredients: Appropriate amounts of green tea powder and coix seed.

New Green Tea Weight Loss Formula Two: Hawthorn Green Tea

Tea Formula: 6 grams of green tea powder, 5 qian (Chinese measurement) of hawthorn.

Preparation: Boil with three bowls of water for six minutes, drink after each meal, and you can add more hot water to continue drinking. One dose per day.

Benefits: Reduces excess body fat and oils effectively, and also helps disperse blood stasis.

New Green Tea Weight Loss Formula Three: Lotus Leaf Refreshing Tea

Benefits: Effective for dry mouth, prone to acne, poor blood circulation, loose facial skin, and obesity.

New Green Tea Weight Loss Formula Four: Rhubarb Green Tea
