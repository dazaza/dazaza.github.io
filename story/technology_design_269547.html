The PlayStation Portable, PSP for short, is a handheld game console created by SONY and released on December 12, 2004 (Japan). The PSP’s goal is to be a complete entertainment platform since it is able to run video games, play video content, digital audio, and display digital photos stored in memory sticks or UMD disks.
