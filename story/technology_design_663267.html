According to legend, beside the Earth there is another world called the Underworld. The environment of the Underworld is very similar to that of Earth, and humans also live in the Underworld. However, the intelligence of humans living in the Underworld far surpasses that of humans living on Earth, and they can also practice legendary magic.

-Scene shifts to the Human World-

"The humans in legends can use legendary magic? Preposterous!" Luo Qinsu said with a look of disdain on her face as she stared at the person who was introducing her to the Underworld. "Hey! It's useless for you to look at me~ Anyway, that place exists, and since you can't see it, how do you know it doesn't exist!" Su Ziyou, the person Qinsu was staring at, returned her gaze with equal disdain.

"Hmm~ I can't see it, right? Then neither can you! Unless you find some evidence for me~ Everything requires proof, doesn't it?" After finishing her sentence, Qinsu turned around and left. "Ah, you know it's a legend and still argue with me, no class!" Ziyou watched her troublemaker friend leave without trying to persuade her. In fact, these two troublemakers would always make up by the afternoon after a fight, so everyone was used to it.

-Scene shifts to the Underworld-

"Get lost." A refined-looking woman irritably shouted at a serious-looking woman. "Get lost? On what grounds? My duty is to protect her, and you will not awaken her!" The serious woman looked at the refined woman seriously, "On the grounds that she is the Blood Princess, she should not have been sealed away, scram! Yin-Ying · Blood Abyss!" The refined woman shouted, and the serious woman began resisting. Two minutes later, the woman claiming to be the protector of the Blood Princess finally collapsed.

"Such a nuisance, if you hadn't blocked my way, I might have gone easier on you, but you forced my hand." The refined woman lightly glanced at the fallen woman, red blood flowing in the darkness, terrifyingly bright.

The refined woman pushed open the door and saw the girl known as the Blood Princess, "Wow, truly beautiful, worthy of being the heir of the Underworld, it's such a pity she was sealed here, let me take you out." After saying this, the woman cast an unsealing spell. "Mm..." The eyelashes of the Blood Princess fluttered.

"Used too much magic power." A trickle of blood flowed from the corner of the woman's mouth. "Was it you who unsealed me?" The Blood Princess looked at the woman.

"Chapter 1046: No Fun At All - Genius Doctor Latest Update," "Mm... Yes.. Save me.." The woman looked into the Blood Princess's eyes, feeling her own insignificance. "Blood Princess · Revival Spell, thank you." After saying this, the Blood Princess left this place, the woman looking at the Blood Princess with an expectant gaze, hoping she could manage the Underworld well.

After escaping that cursed place, the first place the Blood Princess wanted to go was the Sky City.

"What is rightfully mine has not been obtained, and what I had has all been lost, Saint Lores, you must return everything to me." The sound of footsteps echoed through the bright hall as a girl with purple long hair dressed in a witch costume walked through the hall, her black pupils reflected on the shiny floor. Several guards rushed out.

"Hey! Who are you! Dare to intrude on the Sky City!" The leading guard shouted at the Blood Princess, clearly indicating she was the general here.

"I want to see Saint Lores." The bangs of the Blood Princess covered her black pupils, "Is the name of the King of Hades something you can call?! People! Kill!" The guard general loudly issued orders, and a group of guards surrounded the Blood Princess.

Suddenly, a figure appeared from afar, silver-white cloak billowing, disheveled silver hair.

"Everyone step back." The magnetic male voice made all the guards retreat, and they obediently stepped back, showing that this young man wasn't simple.

"Saint Lores, you've arrived." The Blood Princess spoke to the young man with a contemptuous tone. "Yes, you've returned too." A bead of sweat rolled down the young man's forehead, clearly preparing for a life-or-death battle. "Oh, so nervous? There's no need to be nervous, I've only come back to reclaim what belongs to me." The Blood Princess's lips curled into a wicked smile. "Is there anything left here that belongs to you?" The young man was already gathering his strength.

"Saint Yan · Silver Light Storm!" After the young man finished speaking, a huge tornado surged behind him, silver-white in color. "Blood Princess · Blood Mist Shield." This move was easily deflected by the Blood Princess. Later, seeing the tornado weakening, the Blood Princess took advantage of the situation and attacked, "Blood Princess · Crescent Needle!" Dozens of glowing needles simultaneously shot towards the young man. "Saint Yan · White Light Wings" [Shui Zi: The white light wings are a shield, not for flying - -]

What qualifications do you have now to talk to me about our childhood? I loved you so much! But you sealed me away for the throne!! How do you explain yourself?! Damn Saint Lores!"
