How to do promotion on Baidu Entertainment Tieba

by fantexi2009 on 2009-11-28 15:38:53

Baidu Tieba is very popular and is a must for many webmasters to promote. In general, posting on Tieba is more convenient than updating blogs, with higher traffic. The range of topics discussed on Tieba is narrower than that of forums, making it easier to find the topics you care about. Therefore, Baidu Tieba is a form of promotion between blogs and forums.

At the same time, Tieba is also one of the largest exchange platforms in the world. While understanding the changes in the world, you can make a lot of friends and discuss your favorite topics with other users... Its simple layout and easy operation are deeply loved by young people. Therefore, using Tieba for some simple online promotions has become popular.

If someone wants to promote a website, they can update some entertainment information on the website in time. Because entertainment information has a wide range of attention, high recognition from Internet users, and strong enthusiasm for participation in discussions. Based on this information, relevant themes can be extracted and the following operations can be performed:

1 If the information involves the celebrity themselves, you can directly post in the celebrity's Tieba and link to your own website.

2 If the theme of the article involves movies, you can post in the "Tieba" of the director and actors in the movie, or post in the Tieba named after the film or directly in the "movie" Tieba.

3 If it is entertainment news, you can not only post in the Tieba of the related celebrities, but also in the Tieba of their close friends.

The same content can be posted flexibly from different angles to achieve the desired result.

Here are some things to note:

1 Do not repeat posts in one Tieba. The title and content of the post should be as unique as possible, but not overly exaggerated.

2 Register multiple accounts for flexible posting. If the moderator sees that a certain account has repeatedly posted links in different Tiebas, they will delete them without hesitation. At the same time, the content of the post lacks credibility, which is not good for promotion.

3 Try to keep your post at the top (preferably with different accounts or anonymously) to achieve secondary use.

4 Generally speaking, posting in popular Tiebas is better, but posting in some "slow-burning" Tiebas is also a method. Perhaps the users there are more interested in the promotional content on your website?

5 If posts with links are always deleted, you can post the link anonymously on the second floor.

How to use entertainment information to promote on Tieba needs personal experience. There is no fixed pattern. What we need to do is to break the rules, as long as the result is the same. If you want to promote your website in a short period of time, promoting through entertainment information on Tieba is a good choice!
