15-Year-Old Daughter Found Her Father Selling Drugs and Reported to the Police Twice for the Failure of Persuasion

by lovei0c1 on 2012-03-06 13:45:02

Chutian Metropolis Daily News, by reporter Shan Liu and correspondent Xin Gao

After discovering her father's drug trafficking and failing to dissuade him, a 15-year-old girl reported him twice to the police, who ultimately arrested her father. Yesterday, Donghu New Technology Development Zone Court announced that the man was sentenced to 1 year and 9 months in prison for drug trafficking and fined 2000 yuan.

The daughter reported her father twice for drug trafficking.

In mid-March last year, the Liufang Police Station of the Donghu New Technology Development Zone received an emergency call from a young girl. She claimed that her father was dealing drugs at home and provided their address, asking the police to intervene. However, when the police arrived on the scene, they found that her father had already fled.

The girl introduced herself as Fang Chen (a pseudonym), aged 15. Recently, she discovered that her father, Mr. Chen (40 years old), was involved in drug trafficking. He obtained the drugs from a woman and resold them to others. She mentioned that earlier this month, her father gave her a plastic bag to keep safe, which contained eight red pills. "I asked him if it was medicine, he said no, but told me not to eat it because it was toxic." Soon after, her father took the pills back from her.

About a week later, she saw her father hand over a plastic bag containing some red pills to two strange men at home. She heard her father talking to them about these red pills being ecstasy. She tried to persuade her father not to sell ecstasy, but he wouldn't listen. Worried that her father would get deeper into trouble, she had no choice but to seek help from the police.

A few days later, the police received another emergency call from Fang Chen. When they arrived, Mr. Chen had run away again. On April 25th at 4 PM, while conducting a drug transaction on the streets of Liufang, Mr. Chen was caught red-handed with 10 ecstasy pills on his person.

Mr. Chen confessed that he had been consuming ecstasy himself but always paid for it himself. In January, he met a Ms. Yao who could purchase ecstasy at a low price of 36 yuan per pill and resell them at 50 yuan each, making a profit. Following her instructions, he became one of Ms. Yao's underlings, and in return, she provided him with free ecstasy.

"I will wait for my dad to come out."

What motivated Fang Chen to report her father for drug trafficking? Yesterday, the reporter contacted the prosecutor handling the case. Speaking about this case, the prosecutor expressed regret.

The prosecutor introduced that Mr. Chen once had a happy family. In 2008, due to drug addiction and gambling, he divorced his wife, but they did not separate, and the three of them continued to live together in the same house.

After her parents' divorce, young Fang Chen felt that her life changed significantly. She told the police that her father seemed like a different person after using drugs, no longer loving her or caring about the family. Her mother also told her that her father used money borrowed from others to buy ecstasy. She and her mother deeply hated her father's drug use, "but deep down, they still considered her father and mother as a family."

After the Spring Festival last year, Fang Chen was shocked to find that her father brought a female drug dealer home to cohabit. She and her mother were even more heartbroken and determined to make her father change. In March, after obtaining evidence of her father's drug trafficking, she and her mother tried hard to persuade him, eventually leading to the police report. Fang Chen said, "I just hoped the police would manage my father more, I didn't expect him to end up in jail."

On September 7th, the prosecution charged Mr. Chen with drug trafficking. The prosecutor handling the case stated that during the trial, Mr. Chen admitted his guilt well, showing no objection to the charges against him, saying, "I made a mistake, I don't hate my daughter for reporting me, I only feel sorry for her."

Fang Chen cried sadly outside the courtroom, saying, "I will wait for my dad to come out, hoping he can start anew."
