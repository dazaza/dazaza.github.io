Send a message SEO primary school student (official member) I have been in contact with search engine optimization for nearly ten months. During this period, I have done a lot of websites, Yiwu web design, and have had some successful cases, of course, there are also some failed cases. Whether it is success or failure, especially the failed cases, they are good opportunities for me to learn valuable lessons and experiences, so I will share my recent research on Baidu rankings with everyone!

Firstly, personally, I feel that the most popular profit model for individual station masters last year was: Taobao single-page site! And by the end of the year, what became even more popular were site clusters. However, such sites have one characteristic: they are purely made for hanging advertisements, and their article content is almost all unreadable pseudo-originals! Such websites are called: garbage sites. It is not without reason, the articles are almost unreadable, users bounce out at a high rate, let alone revisiting!

Therefore, these types of websites have become the key targets of Baidu's punishment in large quantities!

Fuzhou SEO student Keqi also started doing quite a few such Taobao stations, and until now gradually realized that such websites are already very difficult to survive. Because as Baidu's algorithm continues to improve, Baidu always puts the user experience of the website first, and its biggest manifestation of user experience is the content of the website!!

Thus, in the websites Keqi has done, there is such a phenomenon: those websites with pure original articles written by themselves or carefully designed readable pseudo-original content articles all have good rankings, while those purely unreadable pseudo-original Taobao stations do not have good rankings, and it is very hard and tiring to do them!

Here, Keqi wants to say: the era of pseudo-original may be coming to an end!

Because, with the recent punishment of websites with pseudo-original content, even if they haven't been punished but find it difficult to rank well, this begins to slowly prove the point that Baidu is adjusting, Baidu is gradually moving towards a more user-experience oriented SEO method, and Baidu must begin to understand how to distinguish the quality of an article from certain special aspects. Gradually, many websites with a large amount of pseudo-original content will eventually be slowly eliminated by Baidu!
