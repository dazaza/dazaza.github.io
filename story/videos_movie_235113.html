This program will be broadcast at 10 PM every Sunday on Taiwan Minsheng Television, co-hosted by renowned entertainers Zhang Yu and Aya. The program is mainly divided into two segments:

[Segment One] For NT$100,000 - Amateurs can sign up to challenge themselves by singing a song for the prize! Can you believe it!? Earn NT$100,000 in just three minutes! Taiwan Minsheng TV's "My First Time" will launch an incredible plan called "For NT$100,000." All you need to do is sing an entire song without mistakes, and you can take home NT$100,000! For registration methods, please visit the official website of Taiwan Minsheng TV's "My First Time" program.

Rules: The production team will pre-announce a list of 100 songs. On the show, contestants will randomly select one song from this list to perform. If the contestant can successfully sing the entire song (without forgetting lyrics, skipping lines, or adding incorrect lyrics), they pass the challenge and win NT$100,000!

[Segment Two] My Signature Song - This segment invites well-known artists to perform the three most meaningful songs in their lives and share the emotional stories and touching memories behind them. In this segment, each episode will feature one famous artist who brings the most important songs from their life. They will share the significance of these songs and the moving stories behind them. The musical stories wrapped in the artists' life journeys are sure to touch everyone's hearts.
