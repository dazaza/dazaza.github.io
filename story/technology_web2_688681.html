Smart appliances - Smart home brings new industrial opportunities

by gdshstyle on 2012-03-03 12:50:45

The Internet of Things (IoT) is changing the world, smart homes are transforming lives, and smart shoe cabinets are just one example of how wireless IoT technology is emerging to bring new industrial opportunities to traditional smart appliances and homes. Through this technology, various home appliances can be conveniently organized wirelessly into a complete system, enabling seamless perception and comprehensive management.

As one of the few Chinese members of the global IoT standards organization, the ZigBee Alliance, Nanjing IOT SensTechnology Co., Ltd. has been dedicated to using wireless IoT technology to change our lives. To date, they have launched a full range of wireless smart home products that can be freely combined and self-assembled (DIY), catering to different consumer groups and needs, and have started providing free cloud services.

According to the company's responsible person, traditionally, smart homes are seen as enhancing the quality of life. However, IoT-enabled smart homes are changing this perception with significant changes being practicality, convenience, universality, and ease of integration. Various appliances in our homes, whether they are smart refrigerators or air conditioners, or traditional lights and televisions, have always worked independently due to inconsistent standards. From a systematic perspective, they are fragmented, chaotic, and lack order. As the homeowner, dealing with these disorganized appliances often incurs high time, management, and control costs, which are unnecessary. The emergence of wireless IoT technology brings new industrial opportunities to traditional smart appliances and homes, allowing them to be conveniently and organically organized into a complete system through wireless means, thus achieving seamless perception and comprehensive management. These previously unimaginable and challenging applications become easy, convenient, and fun once connected via wireless IoT technology. These applications do not merely enhance the quality of life but can also be considered a basic need for 21st-century families.

This responsible person explained that the reason why IoT-enabled smart homes excite the industry more than traditional ones mainly lies in their massive scale and rich applications.

From an industrial scale perspective, the current status of household appliances resembles the state of computers before networking. At that time, computers primarily served as typewriters and storage devices, generating only hardware industries from CPU to cases. Today, we know that networked computers have created much larger internet industries and even spawned many derivative industries! IoT-enabled smart homes will undergo such an upgrade. We know that the number of home appliances far exceeds the total number of computers and mobile phones. If we add various wireless sensors and controllers like IoT drawer locks and smart switches, roughly calculated, the quantity is already over a hundred times that of traditional computers and fifty times that of mobile phones. It could be said that the market for just wireless IoT smart homes might be at least twenty times the sum of traditional internet and mobile internet markets. From this angle, the home market will be the most important market for IoT, far exceeding the scale of smart transportation, smart power, and smart environmental protection industries. In a sense, without smart homes, smart cities or a smart Earth would lose their foundation. Smart homes will become the mainstream application market for IoT, which explains why global giants like Google are betting on the future of homes.

In terms of provided services, IoT wireless smart homes offer very rich and inexpensive, fast applications. They allow you to monitor your home's every move no matter where you are. For instance, if your water pipe freezes and bursts, you can immediately sense it from thousands of miles away and cut off the water valve without returning home to find chaos. Or, if you forget to close the window during a hurried business trip and hear a storm forecast, you don't have to panic. All you need to do is take out your phone, switch to the business travel mode, and the window will automatically close. After the rain clears up, the window will open again to ventilate and prevent mold growth in your home. You can also entrust your home's safety alerts to trusted netizens. If you want to travel lightly, you just need to turn off your phone and share security information such as wireless door magnets, window magnets, drawer locks, and human intrusion to social media platforms like Weibo. Your home's safety will gain attention from more people, and those guarding your home won't just be a few property security guards but millions of netizens. Of course, any intruder's criminal behavior will be exposed on the entire internet, increasing the crime risk so much that criminals may think twice. This responsible person joked that if guarding homes could be charged, in the future, it might spawn a vast service industry. In short, IoT smart homes make natural risks and human-made risks predictable and controllable, reducing them to the minimum. Its value will be further enhanced as cloud services mature.

This responsible person further introduced that as a leading brand in IoT wireless smart homes, we focus more on realizing the experience of wireless IoT functions rather than improving traditional manufacturing processes. We aim to let more families achieve comprehensive perception, control, and system management of the objects they care about. As a member of the global ZigBee Alliance, we will strengthen interactions with all members, jointly explore, and promote the application of IoT wireless technology in homes.

With the sweeping trend of IoT across the globe, we can foresee that great companies will emerge in the IoT field, and great applications will transform humanity's traditional home life!
