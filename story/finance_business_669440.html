The sales center of Shoukai Lingxiang International is officially open to the public _1096 Shoukai Lingxiang International

by xmnqhqzi on 2012-02-19 18:09:47

At ten o'clock on the morning of March 30, the sales center of the first project of HaiXi by Shoukai Co., Ltd. - Shoukai Lingxiang International Project located in the new Xing digital city of Lianba in the island was officially opened to the public. Shi Shoujing, general manager of Xiamen Shoukai Xiangtai Real Estate Co., Ltd., and Na Jun, vice general manager, jointly unveiled the sales office. The Xiamen company, customers and media attended the opening event together. Since the groundbreaking ceremony of Shoukai Lingxiang International Project was held on March 1, the project has been progressing smoothly. Currently, the foundation pile construction is underway, and it is expected that the sales office outside the island will be open in July. The Shoukai Lingxiang International Community is equipped with more than 30,000 square meters of commercial space, including a nearly 1700 square meter fresh produce supermarket and an English-style open-air promenade street. At the same time, the project is close to the town center of Xindian - a traditional commercial street with thriving pedestrian traffic and a strong commercial atmosphere; connected quickly to the mature commercial areas within the island such as SM through the Xiang'an Tunnel, enjoying a colorful life. A 400,000 square meter living community, with an English-style garden, features a layout different from the previous rectangular pattern, replacing traditional gardens with a broader view. The English-style garden is mainly grassy, growing old trees in their natural form, with winding rivers and ponds, giving people a sense of tranquility, while also appearing open, generous, and comfortable. Low density and high greenery create a comfortable lifestyle for Shoukai Lingxiang International. Shoukai Lingxiang International will hold a groundbreaking ceremony on March 1, upholding the corporate mission of "practicing responsible real estate, building a better life", combining the concept of constructing Olympic projects with unique Minnan culture according to the "four high" requirements of the Xiamen Municipal Party Committee and Municipal Government. It will better leverage Xiamen's advantages of having mountains and sea and being lush all year round, while organically integrating new concepts and elements from Beijing, creating a 400,000 square meter international elite community. Address of the sales center within the island: Xiamen Siming District Lianba New Xing Digital Harbor. Appreciation hotline: 0592-5206788 (Source: China Real Estate Network).
