Classic 4-day self-driving tour from Guilin to Yangshuo

by zengxr840824 on 2008-05-13 18:10:26

Day 1: (Free Guide)

Drive to Guilin, known for its unparalleled natural beauty. After a brief rest upon arrival, take a boat tour of the Two Rivers and Four Lakes. The bridges are newly built, blending harmoniously with the scenery; pavilions and gazebos shimmer under the lights. Enjoy views such as the Reflections of the Twin Pagodas, the bridge spanning like the Milky Way, and Song Street connecting the city. You can also take a walk to explore. Stay overnight in Guilin.

Day 2:

After breakfast, visit classic Guilin attractions: Elephant Trunk Hill, the symbol of Guilin City; Fubo Mountain, a magnificent peak by the Lijiang River; Reed Flute Cave, a masterpiece of nature; Seven Star Park (Camel Hill, Flower Bridge, Qixia Temple), Guilin's largest comprehensive garden landscape area. Visit a high-tech jewelry exhibition and purchase local specialties. Stay overnight in Guilin.

Day 3:

After breakfast, take a vehicle to Mopan Mountain Wharf and board a ship to cruise down the Hundred Li Gallery - the Lijiang River. The green mountains on both banks reflect beautifully in the flowing water. Admire the artistic brushwork of the Nine Horses Painting Mountain and experience the magical ambiance of Huangbu Beach where "the ship travels above the clouds." Upon reaching Yangshuo, you may choose to visit the stunning Moon Hill at your own expense, the large banyan tree where Liu Sanjie and Niu Ge sealed their love with an embroidered ball, and Silver Cave, a world-class karst art treasure house. In the evening, you can enjoy the large-scale live performance "Impression Liu Sanjie" directed by renowned director Zhang Yimou. Stay overnight in Yangshuo.

Day 4: (Free Guide)

After breakfast, engage in free activities. With a happy heart, conclude your unforgettable joyful journey in Guilin and return home.

Precautions:

1. Obtain advance information about various scenic spots in Guilin.

2. Refer to the information published on websites and travel forums to prepare for the trip.
