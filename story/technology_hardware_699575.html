Prostitutes quarrel with customers for tips, are strangled to death with bed sheets and then burned

by yan88exue on 2012-03-09 15:56:44

A man from Ningxia took a prostitute to his dormitory in a KTV in Jingbian County and had sex with her. The next day, they argued over the payment of "tips". The embarrassed and angry man actually strangled the woman with a bed sheet, then bought gasoline and used a motorcycle to transport her body to the outskirts where he burned and buried her. On December 7, after 5 days and 4 nights of continuous efforts, the Jingbian police successfully solved the "10・16" murder and incineration case, and the suspect Wang was successfully captured.

On December 2nd at 13:00, the Jingbian County Public Security Bureau received a report from the public that a burned body was found in a ditch in Huochangwa Village, Zhenjing Township. After receiving the report, the bureau highly valued it, and the director Zhou Ning, deputy director in charge of criminal investigation Wang Shigang, chairman of the union Ma Gang and other leaders led the technical personnel to rush to the scene at the first time. After on-site investigation and preliminary examination of the body, the deceased was identified as a young female who was killed and then thrown here and burned.

In the afternoon of December 4th, the special case group police learned an important situation: Zhang (female, 20 years old, from Zhoutzhi County), an employee of a KTV in Jingbian County, disappeared on October 15, 2011, and has been out of contact ever since. After comparison, it was found that the clothing worn by Zhang before she disappeared matched the characteristics of the remaining clothes of the victim in this case. Combining with other evidence, it was determined that the victim was Zhang. There was a turning point in the investigation of the case. The special case police quickly focused on Zhang's social relationships and her whereabouts before she disappeared. After careful investigation, the key suspect Wang was finally locked down.

At noon on December 6th, the arrest team captured the suspect Wang in Changqing Road, Jingbian County.

Upon investigation: On the evening of October 15, 2011, the suspect Wang (male, 26 years old, from Yanchi County, Ningxia) took Zhang (female, 20 years old, from Zhoutzhi County) from a KTV in the county town to his dormitory, and they had sexual intercourse. At around 8 o'clock the next morning, they had an argument over the payment of "tips", and Zhang threatened Wang in various ways.

At around 12 o'clock on the same day, in anger, the suspect Wang strangled Zhang's head and neck with a bed sheet until she died. He then removed Zhang's gold necklace and hid the body behind the bed. In a gold acquisition point in the county town, Wang sold the dead woman's gold necklace and purchased gasoline, rope and other tools for the crime.

At around 7 p.m. on October 16, the suspect put the body into a woven bag and carried a shovel and gasoline, driving a motorcycle to transport the body to a ditch in Huochangwa Village, Zhenjing Township. He poured gasoline on the body and began to burn it. Later, due to the vigorous fire, the suspect feared being discovered and exposed, so he extinguished the flames with soil and hastily buried the body on the spot before fleeing the scene.

Currently, the suspect Wang has been criminally detained by the Jingbian County Public Security Bureau, and the case is under further review.

Source: Yangguang Daily
