Life needs some sunshine.

by sqrfeifeic on 2011-09-20 22:25:55

This morning, my husband got up early and bought me breakfast. Last night I was hungry at midnight and didn't eat anything. He said, "You go to sleep, you won't feel hungry when you sleep. I'll buy you breakfast when it's light." I said, "I don't believe you. On our wedding anniversary day, you said you would buy me breakfast, but when you woke up, it was already 11:30 in the morning." (We worked very late, read Ai Qing's modern poetry, and went to bed very late) still breakfast? Hmm... When he came back this morning, I was still sleeping, feeling very tired. But I still got up full of gratitude and ate my loving breakfast.

Recently, we often go to the beach. Sometimes we just go to take a look, occasionally we also go down to the sea to learn swimming. I haven't learned it yet. I am a little afraid of the vast sea. When my feet leave the ground, I feel uneasy. My husband is my swimming coach. He can swim himself, but when he teaches me, he always despises me for being stupid. It's the same when I roller skate. I always can't learn, my balance is a bit poor. Maybe I really don't have much athletic cells. Fortunately, I am pretty good at playing badminton, hehe...

Life needs some sunshine, this is my only thought today...

When I am in a bad mood, think about these trivial things in life, but they make me feel warm instantly. This is my sunshine. I need sunshine to support myself in this realistic society, and not to care about those people and things that I can't stand...
