Man owes more than 4000 yuan and does not return, villagers suspected of retaliating by killing his children

by zzf000zxyn7 on 2011-07-01 15:34:42

■ Reporter Wang Haitao / Report

According to reports, at 16:55 on February 19th, a neighbor of villager Kang from the ninth group of Gaojiatuoz子弹村 in Baliyingzi Village, Huangyuquan Township Police Station, Nongan County reported an alarming situation. Just returning from the grocery store's mahjong parlor, Kang and his spouse were shocked by what they saw: their two children lying in a pool of blood. Their son was dead on the scene, and their daughter passed away on the way to the hospital. The police quickly determined it was a homicide case, and the suspect was soon identified.

Changchun Public Security Bureau Chief Gives Instructions, Special Task Force Breaks the Case Overnight

The police arrived at Huangyuquan Township around 23:00 that night, and the crime scene at Kang's house was temporarily sealed off. After the incident, Vice Mayor and Public Security Bureau Chief of Changchun City, Gao Xuezhang, gave instructions requiring the Nongan County Public Security Bureau to solve the case quickly and eliminate social impact. The Nongan County Public Security Bureau promptly formed a special task force led by You Mingyan as the team leader and Deputy Director Zhang Xingliang as the deputy team leader to carry out the investigation work.

Villagers Speculate That the Bloodbath is Related to Borrowing and Debt Collection

At around 2:00 AM yesterday, the Huangyuquan Township Police Station and the Huangyuquan Township Government Office of Nongan County were brightly lit. The police and government staff were still discussing the case-solving strategy.

From the villagers, it was learned that Kang and his wife had one son (4 years old) and one daughter (16 years old). Kang used to be a foreman at a construction site and knew the father and son of villager Wang (26 years old). In the eyes of the villagers, the relationship between Kang and Wang's father and son was quite good. About a year ago, Kang borrowed 4700 yuan from Wang (the exact amount provided by the villagers has not been verified), and Wang had repeatedly asked Kang for repayment without success. Before the Spring Festival, Wang's father had a conflict with Kang and beat him up, injuring his face (information provided by villagers, not verified). Afterwards, Kang asked Wang's father for medical expenses but was unsuccessful.

On the afternoon of February 19th, Wang went to Kang's house again to collect the debt. Kang believed that since Wang's father had injured him, Wang's father owed him medical expenses, so he could not repay Wang's money. Villagers said that Kang's intention was to offset the money he owed Wang with the medical expenses owed by Wang's father. After the bloodbath occurred, some villagers secretly speculated that this incident was related to borrowing and debt collection.

Villagers Analyze That the Suspect Was Prepared When He Came Twice

After failing to collect the debt, Wang left Kang's house and found Kang and his wife in a nearby mahjong parlor. Villagers said that at the time, Wang asked Kang to lend him a car (a new purchase by Kang), but Kang refused, saying the car was broken, which made Wang somewhat angry. Not long after he left, a neighbor informed Kang that something had happened at home...

Upon returning home, Kang discovered his daughter covered in blood in the yard of the neighbor in the west (the daughter crossed the courtyard wall to seek help from the neighbor after being injured), while his son inside the house had blue-purple lips and was already dead. While sending his daughter to the hospital, she mentioned Wang's name on the way... Some villagers said that when Wang found Kang again and asked to borrow the car, he might have already had a premeditated plan, and upon failing to borrow the car, he returned to Kang's house and harmed the children.

Grasping Clues, Suspect May Soon Be Arrested

Wang, 26 years old, unmarried, was identified by the police as a major suspect.

Although Kang's daughter passed away on the way to the hospital, mentioning Wang's name played a critical role in the police's further investigation. The police investigated and found that after the incident, Wang took a taxi and left, gradually forming a chain of clues. The police stated that they have already grasped significant case-breaking clues, and the suspect will be arrested soon.

The loss of their child dealt a severe blow to Kang and his wife. Villagers said that the couple was almost collapsed on the bed. As of 19:00 last night, the investigation was still ongoing.
