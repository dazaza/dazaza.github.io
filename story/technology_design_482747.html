Focus on the story behind the painting.

by nyyutjykyj on 2011-08-29 10:39:11

This is a delicate emotional story that naturally arises from the work of "Ancient Female". The painting was not created by an artist who is mature and strong in personality, but rather by the naive hands of a female protagonist who is full of ideals and ambitions for life, the future, and animation, and is innocent and romantic. While I was attracted by the painting, I was also captivated by the lengthy text below. I really wanted to know what it was all about... The story is like a novel, intriguing to read, and it moves people in some ways, makes them envious in others, earns their admiration in yet other ways, and fills them with melancholy in still others... The girl's delicate thoughts are touching, her youth and literary talent are enviable, her unreserved expression of emotions is admirable, and the age difference, geographic distance, and development of feelings between the two make people worried and melancholic... In the female protagonist, one can see a shadow of one's own past - innocent, simple, and straightforward in thought, without having experienced setbacks or become cynical about the world. In the male protagonist, one sees oneself now - realistic, practical, assimilated by secularism, consumed by society, gradually becoming corrupt, moving further and further away from fantasies, dreams, and ideals, though still persisting in selfhood, changing constantly with the environment and time. One truly hopes that the female protagonist can remain true to herself.

The story is a bit special; its content consists of the comments below "Ancient Female," which must be read from bottom to top.
