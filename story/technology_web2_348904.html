[Place Introduction]

From Taicheng entering Guangdong western coastal expressway westward for 95 kilometers, crossing Zhenhai Bay Bridge, ahead built by the sea is Beidou Town at the southwestern corner of Taishan. Continuing forward 20 kilometers along the green mountains and clear water road, passing over an S-shaped mountain pass, suddenly the view opens up, revealing the picturesque stone forest by the sea. Looking around, the blue sea and green mountains form their own scenery; walking between them, you can see marvelously crafted, strangely shaped rocks everywhere. By the sea stands a towering stone mountain, on top of which three strange huge stones overlap, looking proudly down on the bay. On the huge stones, three words are written: "Longjing Bay."

Longjing Bay is purely a seascape of stone, with crystal-clear seawater visible to the bottom, soft and fine sandy beaches, under the glow of dawn and twilight, sparkling brilliantly like gold sand, hence its alias "Gold Sand Beach." Above the blue sky and sea, the rugged mountains are very enchanting, with one peculiarly shaped stone after another scattered across gullies, slopes, ridges, and peaks, or standing tall amidst the blue sea waves. Some resemble galloping horses, others shy maidens, some crane their necks towards the ocean just like "waiting husband" stones, others resemble tigers descending from the mountain, seals crawling, monkey kings emerging from caves, swans dancing, fish playing with waves. There are also stone peaches, stone longans, stone lychees, as well as stone benches, chairs, and beds, making it a fully equipped and vivid marine flower garden and animal kingdom. The primitive charm of Longjing Bay has long been famous far and wide, its unique scenery attracting tourists.

Especially precious is that Longjing Bay now remains an undeveloped "natural treasure land". In spring, touring Longjing Bay, feeling the coolness of winter, accompanied by the gentle sea breeze, driving along the coast, you will surely experience how desirable this spring beach is.

[Route] Transportation from Guangzhou to the Wuyi area is extremely convenient, the entire highway is about 200 kilometers, taking less than two hours. Including: from Guangzhou take Fokai Expressway and exit at Sitian, then via Xintai Expressway to Taishan;

Starting from entering Longjing Bay, the journey changes, surrounded by mountains along the way, strangely shaped stones piled up forming stone mountains, creating clusters of stone mountains. There are many stone caves on the mountains.

Longjing Bay, the name filled with romantic colors, seeing the scenery there, makes one's mood soar, troubles vanish. Along the shore lie variously shaped huge reefs, under the impact of waves, splashing water flowers. A small boat lies quietly on the beach, accompanying the waves, welcoming and sending off sunrises and sunsets. On the reefs, there are several groups of people taking wedding photos, the snow-white flowing wedding dresses blown by the wind blend with the blue sky and white clouds, filling the air with happiness, truly romantically boundless. Walking barefoot on the beach, the fine sand caresses the soles of the feet, on the tidal flats washed by the waves, you can vaguely see many small holes, countless hermit crabs rush in and out of the sand, leaving many natural works of art.

Coming to the beach, girls show off their bikinis, boys also flaunt their pot bellies or ribs. If the weather is good, the beach won't be too sunny, refreshing breezes greet you, almost intoxicating, but applying some sunscreen to avoid getting roasted is better. The morning tide rises quite fiercely, so camping isn't suitable, easily burying you. After the tide retreats, starting from 2:30 PM, the waves become larger, making it more fun, bringing water balloons to float in the waves among the sea is also enjoyable.

If you're hesitant to go in the water, you can climb onto the large stones on the beach to overlook the endless coastline, or buy fresh seafood from newly docked fishing boats, and rent local fishing boats to go out to sea to fish or sightsee. Half of the sand on the beach is black, possibly due to some special minerals, somewhat like Macau's Black Sand Bay, the sand is finer and smoother, comfortable to walk on barefoot.

Since development is recent, there isn't what you'd call refined cuisine near Longjing Bay, however, the stalls along the beach roadside are quite distinctive, providing fresh and original-tasting seafood. These stalls don't have menus, simply select fresh seafood directly, cooking methods include boiling, stewing, stir-frying, only these few methods can ensure the true freshness and deliciousness of the seafood. Large shrimps, meat crabs, shellfish of various kinds are already on the table, though the presentation may not be attractive, the taste is indeed good.

According to the boss, the seafood here is caught and cooked on the same day, which is an important reason for its especially pure flavor. Thoughtful tourists can also buy seafood from nearby fishermen and have it processed by the restaurant, processing fees do not exceed 10 yuan per dish.

[Souvenirs] Local villagers self-produce shrimp paste, salted fish, dried shrimp for tourists to purchase, remember to bargain, although they're not too expensive, it's still good to exercise your eloquence a bit.
