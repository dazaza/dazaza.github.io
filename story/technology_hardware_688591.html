The refurbishing of iPhone 4 is getting more and more serious, with buy-back prices as high as 4400 yuan.

by anonymous on 2012-03-03 11:42:36

The iPhone 4 remains one of the hottest phones on the market.

"Ifans Club" is a small stall at E-Go Flagship Plaza specializing in Apple products. The owner, Yang Bo, has been selling digital products for decades and became an "Apple fan" (or "fruit fan," as they are known in Chinese) about four or five years ago, switching to selling Apple products exclusively. His little shop is well-known in Hangzhou's "fruit fan" community (Weibo). "If you're in a rush to buy an Apple phone, I suggest you get the 8GB iPhone 4. Don't buy the 16GB or 32GB versions," Yang told reporters after chatting with them, revealing some "insider information" about the market: "Recently, the market for the iPhone 4 has been chaotic, not only with sky-high prices but also a high likelihood that what you buy is a used phone."

The refurbishing of iPhone 4s is becoming more prevalent.

After circling the digital market, I found many counters displaying signs for "high-price buy-back of iPhone 4" and "high-price buy-back of old phones." Not long after taking out my own iPhone 4, merchants began asking if I wanted to sell it, offering a "high price buy-back."

"A while back, a customer bought an iPhone 4 from me, but there was an issue with the power connector. After using it for two months, they returned it to me. I got it back from the Shanghai Apple repair center and kept it here until yesterday when I sold it to someone who buys second-hand iPhones. Guess how much I sold it for?" Mr. Yang mysteriously told reporters, "4400 yuan! That's a few hundred yuan more than my purchase price!"

It is understood that these recycled phones are all used for "refurbished units." So-called refurbished units mean replacing the front glass and rear shell of old Apple phones, then repackaging them and selling them as new. "They can offer high prices because the profit is definitely very high," Mr. Yang said. Generally speaking, buyers will resell the phones to professional refurbishers at a price 200-300 yuan higher than the buy-back price. These professional refurbishers then purchase large quantities of original or high-quality imitation Apple parts, such as front glass for around 100 yuan per piece and rear shells for about 80 yuan. After simple packaging, the refurbished units can re-enter the market for sale. "Refurbishers can make at least 500 yuan per phone, sometimes even over a thousand," he said.

There are generally two channels for selling refurbished units: online sales and market sales. Some online sellers will specify "lightly handled" beforehand, with prices lower than new units; but those sold through social channels are directly marketed as new units, leaving consumers unaware.

"The reason refurbished units are so rampant is due to the immense demand for Apple phones," Mr. Yang said. Since the release of the new iPhone 4S, Apple officially adjusted the price of the 16GB iPhone 4 to 4548 yuan. However, with the discontinuation of the 16GB and 32GB versions of the iPhone 4, the official channel began experiencing stock shortages, causing grey-market prices to soar, leading to a situation where "one unit is hard to find." "In September, the price of the iPhone 4 dropped to its lowest point at 3900 yuan, later rising back to 4200 yuan in October, and continued to rise, peaking at over 5000 yuan. Now, even refurbished units cost 4800 yuan!"

Currently, buying the 8GB version of the iPhone 4 is still a better deal.

With the severe issues surrounding the refurbished units of the iPhone 4, how about the newly released iPhone 4S? After checking, it turns out that the official version of the iPhone 4S has not yet hit the market, only grey-market versions are available, with prices generally around 5600 yuan, which is quite high.

Mr. Yang said that based on his experience, the price of the iPhone 4S won't drop anytime soon, at least not until March next year when it might stabilize. "If you really need to buy an Apple phone urgently, the relatively affordable option would be the 8GB version of the iPhone 4. Currently, the official price on the market is around 4200 yuan." It is understood that the memory of the 8GB version of the iPhone 4 remains at 512MB, and compared to the 16GB and 32GB versions of the iPhone 4, there isn't much difference in appearance and configuration. It runs the latest iOS 5 operating system.
