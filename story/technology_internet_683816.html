1. That famous "[My Hand Through Your Black Hair]" --- & nbsp; Kelp Stewed Pig's Trotters

2. I ordered a dish at the restaurant downstairs from my company called "Reunion of Mother and Son". When the dish came, I was stunned! It turned out to be soybeans + bean sprouts!

3. I ordered a dish called "Flying Fox in Snow", which was just fried shrimp chips (white) with a few small fried shrimp skins on top! However, the "Flying Fox in Snow" at Xi'an Restaurant is much better - they put some fried little scorpions on the shrimp chips.

4. "Walking on the Country Road" - Oh my goodness, it turned out to be braised pig's trotters, with some coriander on the side!

5. Once, I went to Ba Mountain with classmates and found a cold dish called "One Country, Two Systems". I casually asked the server what it was, and the server said: [It's boiled peanuts and fried peanuts]...

6. In Daqing, I saw an advertisement on a blackboard outside a small restaurant for a dish called "Bosnia War". Very strange, so I asked, and it turned out to be spinach stir-fried with black fungus!

7. I also ate a dish called "Green Dragon Lying in Snow", which was just a plate of white sugar with a cucumber on top.

8. "Volcano Covered by Snow" is just cold mixed tomatoes sprinkled with white sugar.

9. One day, while dining at Yanbin Building, we ordered a dish called "Whispering Words". When it came, it turned out to be pig's tongue and pig's ears.

10. Remember last time in Tai'an, we ordered a dish ----- "Three Beauties of Mount Tai". When it came, it was just cabbage and tofu, so I questioned the waitress, who replied: The three beauties are cabbage, tofu, and water, and the most beautiful water is from Mount Tai......

11. I ordered a dish called "The Peerless Twin Beauties", which was ---- green peppers + red peppers:

12. Also, "Red Light District" ---- diced chicken in hot sauce

13. "Guan Yu Fights Qin Qiong", ft: scrambled eggs with tomato ---- red face and yellow face

14. There's one called [Heartache Feeling], which is actually a glass of plain water (50 yuan), truly painful

15. A friend went to Mount Tai and ordered a bowl of [Niu's Noodle]. Later, they found there wasn't a single piece of beef. So they called over the owner to reason, and the answer was: the noodle maker's surname is Niu! The friend almost fainted on the spot, asking: Don't you consider long-term business? The owner replied: Most customers only come to Mount Tai once in a lifetime, so I'm already very lucky to have them eat a bowl of noodles here, haha.

16. Do you know what "Beating Pig Bajie with Clubs" is? It's bean sprouts stir-fried with pig head meat!

17. I remember once ordering a dish at dinner, named "Unique Peak". When it came, wow, it was actually a plate of duck butts.

18. Have you eaten "Spicy Kiss"? ---- Haha, it's just chili stir-fried with pig's snout!

19. The dish name "Little Black Gets Married" originally was a plate with two peeled century eggs!

20. When the US bombed our embassy, one restaurant introduced a dish called "Braised American Embassy". When it came, ah! It turned out to be braised large intestine... I didn't even touch it and shoved it under the table...

21. On a business trip to the south, while eating, I saw a dish called "Jue Chao Tian" ... Upon bringing it up, hey, there were 8 neatly arranged goose butts in the plate!

22. While on a business trip in Xi'an, seeing others eat fried chicken legs and red tomato sauce... I ordered one too... When paying... Miss called out to me... Sir, your "Virgin's First Night" is 12 yuan...

23. Who likes eating [Naked Beauty]? ---- It's just shelled peanuts, hahaha....

24. [Black Bear Playing with Stick], guess what dish it is? Hehe, it's actually wood ear fungus stir-fried with bean sprouts!

25. At a restaurant in Mount Huang, we ate [Mount Huang's Unique Dish], damn! It turned out to be a plate of ferns, I fell over!

26. A classmate of mine went to a restaurant and saw a dish called [Monkey Fetches the Moon]. Thinking it might be good, he bought it, only to find it was just a plate of vinegar with a drop of sesame oil on top, absolutely infuriated!

27. While dining at a large restaurant, after ordering several main courses, I also ordered a few vegetable dishes, one of which was called [Silver Sprouts Covered with Quilt]. The name sounded fresh, but when the dish came, do you know what it was? It was the white part of mung bean sprouts with both ends pinched off, covered with a layer of omelette.

28. During a gathering with friends at a small restaurant, I saw a soup called "Qinglong Crossing the River" on the menu and asked the waitress what it meant. She replied that it was just clear soup with green onions, sweat! Good thing I asked first, otherwise I would've lost money!

29. The most classic one is this ----- One time, I saw a dish called "White Horse Prince". At the time, none of the people with me knew what it was, so we ordered a plate. When it came, it was just a big block of tofu... with a picture of Andy Lau stuck on top... Everyone fell over, foaming at the mouth.
