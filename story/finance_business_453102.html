Abstract: With the continuous development of society, the material and cultural life of the citizens has undergone tremendous changes. Many urban residents have entered a well-off life, and their requirements for living conditions have also correspondingly improved, hoping to live in an environment closely connected with beautiful nature. The landscape and environmental design of residential areas need to develop certain artistic expressions, thereby conveying high standards for residential living environments.

Keywords: Community; Landscape Environment, Master's Thesis Writing, Design

The landscape design and environmental design of residential areas are extensions of material life, not merely implementing spatial environmental settings from aesthetics and functionality, but also needing to integrate the elements of landscape design through intent and thrust. For example, by highlighting unique social and cultural trends or emphasizing the real essence of the residential area and its surrounding environment, advanced design intentions can add picturesque scenery, enhancing the living environment. Through each residential neighborhood, the landscape becomes an attractive, distinctive, and iconic carrier.

Landscape design methods no longer wait until after the main design and construction like in the past, then adding and repairing environmental features. Instead, the planning and design of the environment are integrated into the entire process of residential design to ensure that important planning and construction are coordinated uniformly, ensuring the residential building maximally combines with natural terrain and natural resources, fully reflecting the overall planning and design concept. Residential area landscape design should not be limited to one type of style but should adapt according to different design ideas and specific decisions, ultimately achieving good visual effects. Landscape design expands lines and shapes and smoothly integrates into the environment in lively and flexible ways, improving and expanding original garden design techniques.

In establishing the concept of people-oriented design for community landscape design, it means shifting from simply gathering greenery and facilities in residential environment design to fully meeting residents' hierarchical needs for living environments. The idea of being people-oriented focuses on the delicate emotional needs of individuals (such as various garden landscape ornaments), making the design centered around people, and further introducing the profound connotations of successful living space design for residents. Integrating ecological design into environmental design makes the residential environment part of the city's ecosystem as a systematic whole; creating a residential environment where environmental design functions transition from single national spatial planning to systematic design; creating an artificial natural green environment in residential areas.

For instance, not only is a high greening rate needed, but also scientifically reasonable characteristics of plants, trees, shrubs, flowers, and vegetation must be considered; water environments in residential areas need to consider water circulation and utilization. As lifestyle and income levels improve, the pursuit of residential practices undergoes fundamental changes, especially during people’s leisure time after work, resulting in more residential areas offering activities such as fitness, conversation, meetings, and other events. Therefore, living needs are more reflected in various landscape elements like garden landscape ornaments and fitness equipment, which not only meet habits and usage needs but also require a certain level of culture to enrich citizens' lifestyles during their leisure time.

In static landscape design, pursuing dynamic landscape composition and pleasing visual effects is emphasized, but more importance is placed on the streamline design of dynamic landscape elements. In most cases, linear tracking connects a series of landscapes forming the main axis line within the community, creating a continuous, dynamic landscape sequence, such as green axes and blue axes of different types. Due to various reasons causing landscape spaces, this dynamic effect allows people sufficient space to feel and experience the atmosphere, creating a cultural residential ambiance and enhancing its signature appeal effectively. Interactive residential environment planning not only focuses on satisfying people's visual aesthetic effects but ultimately aims to be used by residents. Interaction between residents and natural and residential environments incorporates the interaction between people and natural spaces into the residential environment.

Two appreciative and functional aspects of home environments should form a landscape that is both aesthetically pleasing and functional, decorated with green grass in an open layout, small gatherings, and arrangements that are more conducive to promoting interpersonal relationships, making it easy for residents to integrate into nature. This allows every member to enjoy beautiful natural scenery and fresh air, harmonizing neighborly relations, realizing the ideal garden for humanity.
