Announcement Regarding the Registration List of Personnel for the Ninth Batch of Level 1 Constructors in 2011

Announcement No. 1203 of the Ministry of Housing and Urban-Rural Development of the People's Republic of China

In accordance with the provisions of the "Management Regulations for Registered Constructors" (Ministry of Construction Order No. 153), the "Implementation Measures for Level 1 Constructor Registration," and the "Announcement on Relevant Provisions and Content of Administrative Licensing Matters Directly Implemented by the Ministry of Construction" (Ministry of Construction Announcement No. 278), after review, Zhang Guoxin and 13,932 other applicants meet the registration requirements for Level 1 Constructors and are hereby approved for registration.

This is to announce accordingly, Level 1 Constructor Training.
