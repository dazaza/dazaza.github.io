The Surge of Intelligent Trends: How to Make Smart Homes More Human-Centered

Currently, with the development of technology, the application of intelligent furniture is becoming increasingly common in our daily lives. Air conditioners that automatically adjust temperature or working status, refrigerators that can display the condition of stored items, and so on. Intelligence has made our lives more vibrant. Generally speaking, the working mode of smart homes mainly falls into two aspects: one category adopts advanced intelligent technologies for autonomous operation, while the other relies on recognizing and judging based on the operator's previous work habits. However, regardless of the type of operation, its core function is to adjust according to user habits and surrounding environment, providing more convenience, creating a better living environment, and meeting people's needs. Therefore, we can infer that the future development direction of intelligent furniture will likely seek more breakthroughs from the following aspects.

Intelligentization. As smart furniture, intelligentization is inevitably its main direction of development. Currently, whether it’s smart homes or security monitoring, intelligentization is the key focus of product development. For smart furniture, the highest goal of intelligentization is to identify and judge the surrounding environment through self-sensing systems and make corresponding reactions, thereby achieving the role of the human brain. Although, at the current technical level, identification capabilities completely matching the human brain are temporarily unachievable. However, through the sensing system of the equipment, making judgments on more complex environments around and correctly and stably issuing more humanized instructions is the main direction for improving intelligent furniture in the future.

Enrichment of Network Functions. With the continuous improvement of network technology and the increasingly superior network environment, the ability of remote control is gradually being valued by everyone. Especially with the increasing richness of available network carriers, the number of furniture that people can control is also increasing, and the distance of control is getting farther. How to use the existing networks well, dig out greater utilization value from them, and achieve richer control capabilities largely determines whether smart homes can break through more bottlenecks.

Energy Saving. Currently, energy expenditure has unknowingly occupied an increasingly large proportion of daily expenses, and the issue of energy saving is being paid more attention to by people. Especially with the increasing functions and types of smart homes and their growing popularity, the energy consumption of related facilities will undoubtedly increase accordingly. Therefore, as smart homes that bring great convenience to people's lives, how to be more energy-saving during use is probably an important reference indicator affecting whether such furniture can bring more benefits to users.

As an emerging technology, the development prospects of smart homes are still very broad. And how to expand functions using existing resources is also the key to whether smart homes can be more humanized. Facing such extensive market demands and a good scientific and technological environment, we believe that smart homes won't keep us waiting for too long.
