To help everyone cure urticaria earlier, we have collected some information about the causes of urticaria for your reference.

1. The cause of urticaria is related to infection factors. Mainly infections such as bacteria, viruses, fungi, and parasites that affect urticaria patients.

2. Implants, mainly surgical metal nails, metal dentures, etc., can also lead to the occurrence of urticaria.

3. Animals, plants, and inhalants can easily trigger urticaria. Bites from certain insects, inhaling pollen, animal dander, feathers, dust, and certain volatile chemical substances are all causes of urticaria.

4. The cause of urticaria is also linked to physical factors, such as seasons, cold and heat, sunlight, friction, pressure, mechanical stimulation, etc.

5. Food and additives are one of the causes of urticaria, mainly animal proteins like fish, shrimp, crab, meat, eggs, and food coloring, flavorings, preservatives, etc. Therefore, to prevent the occurrence of urticaria, more attention should be paid to diet.

6. Internal diseases are also causes of urticaria, such as cancer, infectious mononucleosis, rheumatism, nephritis, liver disease, peptic ulcer disease, metabolic disorders, endocrine diseases, etc., which can easily lead to urticaria.
