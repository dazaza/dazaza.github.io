Since the advent of online shopping, my attitude towards cosmetics is: absolutely not buying cosmetics domestically! With years of online shopping experience, I have my own way of distinguishing genuine from fake products regarding the quality issues everyone worries about: 1. Check if the store specializes in selling products from one region (such as all Japanese or Korean products), if they sell everything, there is likely to be fakes, as no one can manage everything; 2. Check if there are production date serial numbers, understand the basic characteristics and prices of the products, generally the price of genuine products cannot be too low, don't be greedy for ultra-low prices; 3. Check the reviews of previous buyers, this is actually a good method, time-saving and reliable. When I first started shopping, I hadn't yet summarized these experiences, so I also bought counterfeit goods before, which I immediately threw away. But it made me more cautious, and now I've developed a keen eye!
