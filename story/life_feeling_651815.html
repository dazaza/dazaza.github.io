Nostalgic, we are tender like water.

by lili1 on 2012-01-03 00:38:31

When reminiscing, we are still as tender as ever.

The passage of time has dimmed the cycles of many years, making it hard to discern the footprints of the past. Time flies like a shuttle; flowers bloom and wither. Where is the true haven of love? Life extravagantly consumes itself year after year. Suddenly looking back, what we've lost turns out to be the most beautiful memories.

In such times, living blindly and helplessly, I often find myself on sleepless nights disturbed by a storm, trying to recall how many familiar sleepless nights of rain there have been from a memorable summer in the 1990s to tonight.

Yes, more than a decade has passed. For some reason, with every seasonal change, every sunset, or a momentary flutter of the heart, when I gently close my eyes, I often remember standing dazed beside the lilac tree under your dormitory building. The moon rises quietly while I gaze longingly alone.

All these scenes have replayed in my mind more than once.

In our graduation year, at the moment of parting, watching you board the school bus, I once again felt that familiar loneliness and helplessness. I had originally wanted to say "Wait for me" in the last instant our eyes met, but those words buried deep in my heart for so long were hard to utter. I always felt that these three words were like a soft farewell, equally hurtful.

What exactly does life expect? Regarding the choice between mutual support and forgetting each other, I truly don't know which one to choose, even though it's hard to say whether loving until old age or missing someone to tears is better...

Sometimes, people encounter mountains and rivers, or distant horizons, but I always think that the person who loves you is always in the farthest yet nearest place, waiting for you.

Perhaps, some feelings may not last forever, but I am always willing to keep them hanging in my heart, harboring unspeakable affection, hesitating to forget.

Farewells are fleeting like the wind, and before I knew it, years had passed. Some things no longer matter whether you know or not, or whether at a certain moment, you shared the same flutter with me. I believe that all my experiences and insights are the most real existence between us.

Those silent yet steadfast times can leave me at any moment, but most importantly, I still remember thinking of you, remembering the beautiful and romantic days when you accompanied me.

However, the you and me of now feel like we're separated by an entire world.

Tonight, the moon shines brightly in the sky, without wind, fog, or rain. When reminiscing, we are still as tender as ever...
