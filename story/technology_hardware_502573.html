iPhone5 will be unveiled in a few hours, the suspense is about to be revealed - SweetIT.Net

by anonymous on 2011-10-04 20:49:35

At 10:00 am, October 4 in the United States (1:00 am, October 5 in China), Apple is going to release its latest iPhone products at its Cupertino headquarters. The iPhone5 will be released in five countries including the United States, the United Kingdom, France, Germany and Japan. Employees of the stores in these countries have been told not to take a vacation on October 14-15 in order to prepare for the release.

The theme of the invitation received by many media is "Let’s talk iPhone". There are four icons on the invitation: one is a calendar, one is a clock, one is the address of the headquarters, and one is a telephone icon~ there is a "1" in the upper right corner of this telephone icon. Users of iPhone all know that this means there is one missed call, but it means more than that here!

Even hours before the release, fans still do not know how many definite answers the new generation of Apple mobile phones will give to our suspense.

One model or two?

Will the iPhone 5 and iPhone 4S be released together, or only one of them? According to the current situation we understand, the information about the iPhone 5 case has appeared in AT&T's internal inventory system, so the listing of the iPhone 5 should not take too long, and the release should be at this moment. 9To5Mac once said that it had seen the iPhone4S in the warehouse and that the new iPhone would offer three different capacities of choice: 16GB, 32GB and 64GB, and the colors would still be black and white. If this news is also true, then the two new iPhones might appear on stage at the same time.

How big is the screen?

In previous rumors, the screen size of the new iPhone has expanded rapidly from 3.5 to 3.75, then to 4.0, and even to 4.3. In fact, under the drive of the large-scale development of large screens on the Android platform, the iPhone screen faces a dilemma. If the screen size expands to over 4.0, then the applications developed for the iPhone previously will not perform well on the new iPhone because their zoomed display will reduce the quality of the picture. If the screen size is not increased, what is the advantage of the iPhone when the Android devices have grown to 4.7 inches? This is really a tough decision.

Is there a laser keyboard?

A video online has aroused people's fancy about the laser keyboard on the new iPhone. We had searched the Internet for information previously and found that the laser keyboard is nothing new, and commercialized products have been launched as well. As netizens say, this thing has existed for several years, and it seems somewhat "outdated" if it is launched now. But for mobile phone users, this function will attract many eyeballs. As to whether there is a laser keyboard, we will wait for Apple's revelation tomorrow morning.

Leaving the suspense to the end is also Apple's consistent practice. Let us together quietly look forward to the moment when Apple reveals the answer to us in a few hours.
