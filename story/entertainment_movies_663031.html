Puss in Boots (2011) Movie Summary: In fact, like many characters in "Shrek", Puss in Boots is also a famous character from classic fairy tales, only slightly parodied in a whimsical way in "Shrek". The original fairy tale of "Puss in Boots" tells the story of the youngest son of a miller who inherited a clever cat from his father. This cat, wearing boots, helped the miller's son gain the king's favor and facilitated his marriage to the princess.

However, in DreamWorks' version of "Puss in Boots", the original fairy tale may be unrecognizable. Antonio Banderas said he has read the script, and he really likes it. He revealed that the entire story is original, starting from Puss in Boots' childhood. Audiences will see why he became an assassin, how he became one, and why he gave up this trade. Unlike "Shrek", which is full of parodies of popular culture, this film will take a different path, being somewhat epic and even very touching at times.

Antonio Banderas also revealed that the script for "Puss in Boots" took three years to create, and he believes audiences will be greatly surprised because it is completely different from what they expect.
