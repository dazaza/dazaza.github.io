2009 National Interior Wall Art Design Competition

by longersing on 2009-10-24 16:24:37

Theme: Sina Leju • Ridale Cup 2009 National Interior Wall Art Design Competition

Date: October 17, 2009

Venue: Second Floor Multipurpose Hall, City Hotel

In recent years, design competitions in the home furnishings industry have been emerging one after another, including the national lighting application design competition, bathroom space design competition, China furniture design competition, etc., almost covering every field of the home building materials industry. However, there has never been a design competition specifically for wall art. The 2009 Sina Leju National Interior Wall Art Design Competition appropriately filled this gap.

The development of wallpaper in China has experienced many twists and turns. In the early days, it was accompanied by pain and failure, but in recent years, under the influence of some major brands, the market share of the wallpaper industry has gradually increased from 3% to 8%. According to data released by the IGI International Wallpaper Association, in 2008, China and North America saw the largest growth in global wallpaper consumption, with a year-on-year increase of 20%. On one hand, the industry is developing rapidly, and on the other hand, the brand awareness of wallpaper companies is constantly increasing. Therefore, insiders predict that China's wallpaper market share will reach 70%-80% in the future. With such a huge industry development prospect, the product characteristics of wallpaper determine the importance of design in it. To be both critically acclaimed and commercially successful, undoubtedly, humanized and aesthetically pleasing designs are crucial. The 2009 National Interior Wall Art Design Competition jointly organized by Sina Leju, the Interior Design Branch of the Architectural Society of China, and CIID American Interior Design Magazine aims to discover design elites and promote emerging designers. This is like a thunderclap in the rarely marketed wallpaper industry, causing quite a stir in the industry.

The first event invited many famous experts and scholars from the international, Taiwan, Hong Kong, and mainland interior design circles, as well as seasoned professionals in the industry, to serve as judges for the competition. Mr. Bai Qingcong, founder of Langpin Group, as one of the chief judges of this competition, was invited to attend the launch ceremony of the first event. In his speech, he mentioned that Sina Leju and the Interior Designers Branch of the Architectural Society of China provided designers with an excellent platform. Walls play a very important role in home design. Walls not only have functional aspects but are also a very important element in presenting style. Therefore, such a competition is a very good starting point. At the end of his speech, Mr. Bai Qingcong expressed his hope that more sparks of design exchange and more creative collisions would emerge through this competition. Professor Lai Zengxiang, a professor at Tongji University's Department of Architecture and doctoral supervisor, who served as an expert consultant for this competition, also mentioned at the meeting that such a competition could elevate people's home lives to a new level of taste. Meanwhile, those attending the launch ceremony included chief judges Xu Mincong, Leung Chi Tin, Shen Lidong, and senior indoor architects Wang Shiwei and architect Liu Jiachung, among others, who were part of the expert advisory team.

The first event was officially named the Ridale Cup with the strong support of Shanghai Xinwang Wallpaper Co., Ltd. It will be held annually in the future, aiming to become the most influential fixed competition in the interior wall art space design industry.
