17-Year-Old Boy Stabs Someone to Death Over Delivery Dispute (www.dnfaa.com)

by dehua900 on 2011-05-08 17:11:54

Evening Post Report: Just because of an argument with a food delivery person, minor Lin Chen stabbed one person to death. Recently, the Jiading District Procuratorate prosecuted Lin Chen for涉嫌故意伤害罪 (suspected intentional injury).

Not long after Lin Chen returned to the inn, a friend called and invited them to eat spicy crayfish. They immediately forgot about ordering takeout and enthusiastically went out together. At around eight o'clock in the evening, Lin Chen and his group returned to the inn. At that time, the food delivery owner arrived and seeing Lin Chen and others, couldn't help but complain: "I've already made three or four trips, why is no one here?"

In front of a bathhouse, Lin Chen saw Mr. Lü preparing to make a phone call and directly rushed over, hitting Mr. Lü's head with his fist. Mr. Lü swung his hands and wrestled with Lin Chen. At this moment, Lin Chen took the folding knife he had in his hand and stabbed Mr. Lü's waist. Injured, Mr. Lü held his wound and ran towards a nearby restaurant, while Lin Chen also fled the scene. Mr. Lü eventually died due to ineffective rescue efforts the next day. According to the forensic examination, Mr. Lü died from hemorrhagic shock caused by a rupture of the abdominal aorta due to being stabbed in the waist by a sharp object. A few days later, pressured by circumstances, Lin Chen turned himself in to the police. After reviewing the case, the Jiading District Procuratorate believed that the suspect Lin Chen intentionally harmed another person's body causing death, which constitutes suspected intentional injury. On August 31st this year, the Jiading District Procuratorate approved the arrest of Lin Chen. Recently, the procuratorate has filed a public prosecution with the court.

A reporter from Evening Post, Cheng Yi, communicated with Xu Leilei and Zhu Tianxiao.

Lin Chen’s friend explained they were out on business, and the daughter of the food delivery owner became somewhat angry, saying: "You should have called to explain. Are there people who order food like you? If you don't want it, just say so!" Lin Chen and his few friends got upset after hearing this and said: "Since it's delivered so late, we really don't want it anymore!" As a result, both sides began arguing.

Lin Chen was born in November 1991. He dropped out of school in the second year of junior high and stayed home farming. In 2008, after practicing martial arts in Henan for two years, he returned home and became a security guard. In April this year, under the persuasion of classmates, he came to Shanghai to work. However, as a minor, he faced numerous obstacles in finding jobs. Due to financial difficulties, he and a few newly acquainted friends spent their days in motels and gambled at night, leading a life of reversed days and nights, frequenting casinos. On the evening of July 24th this year, Lin Chen, as usual, ordered five takeout meals from a nearby individual restaurant near the motel and instructed the restaurant owner to deliver the food to the motel room.

Upon hearing about the argument, Mr. Lü, a friend of the boss’s daughter, rushed over. Lin Chen and his friends exchanged a few words with Mr. Lü. The boss, trying to settle the dispute, pulled Mr. Lü and her daughter back, but Mr. Lü couldn’t help turning back and cursing a few times. This enraged Lin Chen and his friends, and they chased after Mr. Lü. Lin Chen was in front, closely pursuing Mr. Lü. Filled with anger, he took out a folding knife from his pocket and held it in his hand.
