What is the nature of love in this world that it commands life and death promises? The two flyers from the farthest ends of the earth, how many times have their old wings braved the cold and heat. In their joys and parting sorrows, there are also infatuated sons and daughters among them. You should have something to say; through ten thousand li of layered clouds, a thousand mountains blanketed in evening snow, whose shadow would I follow? ——Yuan Haowen

Love is an eternal topic, and everyone has their own ideal lover and dream romance in their hearts. However, real-world love is both sweet and cunning, like floating clouds in the sky that you can't grasp. When you fall in love, have you ever thought about what love is? Will he or she be my true love? Do you yearn for grand, unwavering love, or perhaps a love where you hold hands with someone and grow old together?

Definition of Love

Love is an emotion, usually referring to the special feelings people display during the dating phase. The essence of love is a chemical reaction, where hormones and pheromones emit scents that the brain identifies, determining preferences and generating a feeling.

In the dating stage, love is the desire to give some or all of one's emotions and thoughts to the person they love.

The Essence of Love

The essence of love lies in giving without expecting anything in return.

Love vs. Like

Liking is shallow love, while love is deep liking.

Love and Marriage

Love is the greatest emotion of humanity, whereas marriage is merely the legal confirmation of the relationship between spouses. Having a marriage does not guarantee having love, and having love does not necessarily lead to marriage.

The Highest Realm of Love

Is the highest realm of love life and death? In fact, those sweeping romances never really happen. Most worldly loves only consist of ordinary love and hatred, sorrow and happiness.

What is True Love?

True love resides in your heart; you don't need to search for it. You already possess true love, but you keep it hidden, unwilling to share it with others. However, only by sharing true love with others can you truly feel and enjoy it, because true love is selfless. True love is meant for giving, not taking.

What Exactly is Love?

Love is a beautiful mood; it makes you happy and gives you indescribable feelings. It’s almost like a belief—"if you believe, it exists; if you don’t believe, it doesn’t."
