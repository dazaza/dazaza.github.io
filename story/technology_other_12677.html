The top 10 romantic cities of the world exude an exotic charm

by hanhai1314 on 2007-02-04 17:26:09

Top 10 romantic cities of the world Exude a charming exotic atmosphere (Photo)

Number one: Paris

There seems to be no explanation, and probably everyone in the world knows that Paris is the most romantic place. It will make you forget where you came from and where you are going, and you will be overwhelmed by its romantic feelings. The Seine will tell you what love is, Notre Dame will tell you what beauty is... The whole of Paris will show you exactly what romance is...

Number two: Los Angeles

It's impossible to think of Los Angeles without thinking of Hollywood movies. It has the most familiar movie actors and the most beautiful love stories. We can't say no to the romance of Hollywood, we can only fall into it and wish we were the protagonist of the movie. Los Angeles has witnessed the growth of world cinema, and we can only grow with it...

Number three: London

People say the British are stiff, and London is a serious city. But on Valentine's Day, the British send more cards than any other people in the world. Speaking of London, we have to think of Hugh Grant's films. Beautiful images from 2002's Love Actually and his movie Mount Lording with the famous American actress Julia Roberts still come back to us... London, the romantic city in the fog

Number four: Rome

"Roman Holiday" left many fond memories of love for the people of that era. The "Eternal Capital" is still writing Italian romance in its Italian way.

Number five: Seattle

Remember Tom Hanks, Meg Ryan in Sleepless in Seattle? Perhaps we will never forget the most moving love story of the last century, just as we cannot forget the city.

Sixth place: Monaco

Located in the northeast of France, the small pocket country, the world's second largest gambling city, has the world's best Monte Carlo circuit, Monte Carlo Tennis Masters. In May each year, the Laureus Sports Awards are presented here. A small country of less than two square kilometres has countless honours...

Seventh place: Montreal

The second largest French-speaking city in the world and the second largest city in Canada, Montreal has inherited the most romantic aspects of the French. Go to Canada, you can not go to Toronto, you can not go to Vancouver, but you can not miss Montreal.

8. Melbourne

Australia's cultural and economic center, one of the most prosperous cities in the southern hemisphere. Inherited the cultural heritage of London, but does not lack the romantic feelings of Paris. It has been said that the only city that can represent Australia is not Sydney, but Melbourne.

Ninth place: Dubai

The only city in Asia to make the list is also the only city in the world with a seven-star hotel. Known as the "City of Sunshine", Dubai has its own way of interpreting the romantic feelings of the West Asian capital.

10. Casablanca

Famous for the movies, because of this beautiful city, there are classic movies. We have no reason to forget Casablanca. Beautiful North African star...
