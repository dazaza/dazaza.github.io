Several HTC smartphones exposed on Ccmobi

by lkeokldl74 on 2011-05-10 12:31:17

Cellphone World (bbs.ccmobi.com) Guide:

Focused on smartphones, HTC has launched many Android phones that are regarded as classics by enthusiasts. Information about new HTC models continues to emerge, indicating that HTC is continuously preparing for its next moves. Today, we have gathered several pieces of information about new HTC devices from foreign media sources to share with you.

HTC Doubleshot

The phone codenamed HTC Doubleshot is not the first time it has been seen. Previously, foreign media learned some details about this phone through official configuration documents and found a photo taken by this phone. According to previous reports, the HTC Doubleshot will use the Android 2.3 operating system, a side-sliding QWERTY full keyboard design, a WVGA resolution capacitive touch screen, a dual-core processor, and an 8-megapixel camera.

A new HTC model appears on the FCC website, possibly the Doubleshot

Recently, a new model numbered PG58100 appeared in the FCC review directory, and foreign media speculated that this phone might be the HTC Doubleshot.

This product's code is PG58100

Limited information was revealed in the FCC document; we only know that this HTC PG58100 will support T-Mobile network bands. Some foreign media found the connection between this model number and HTC Doubleshot through search engines and believed these two codes refer to the same phone, but there is no other corroborating evidence.

Exposure of HTC Kingdom

The previously leaked HTC Kingdom now has the latest news. Foreign media recently exposed an official render image and detailed specifications of the Kingdom, revealing this phone with a 4.3-inch large screen and powerful configuration completely before us.

Comparison of HTC Kingdom (left) with EVO 3D

Previously, foreign media thought HTC Kingdom was the codename for EVO 3D, but from the renderings and specifications, HTC Kingdom is a completely new device. This phone uses a Qualcomm Snapdragon MSM7630 single-core processor, with a clock speed reaching 1.2GHz, and 768MB RAM ensures smooth operation of the Android 2.3 system.

Parameter comparison of HTC Kingdom and EVO 3D

In addition, the 4.3-inch touchscreen of HTC Kingdom has a high resolution of qHD (960x540 pixels), and it also features a 5-megapixel main camera and a 1.3-megapixel front-facing camera.

HTC Bliss

Smartphones are often considered exclusive to men, but in fact, many women are potential smartphone users, especially those with fashionable designs that are quite popular among female users. HTC also plans to launch an Android phone specifically targeting female users, according to foreign media reports, named HTC Bliss.

Conceptual image of HTC's female-oriented phone Bliss

According to reports, this HTC Bliss will maintain the typical HTC style design, similar to HTC Desire S, but as a product specifically aimed at female users, HTC Bliss will have distinctive features in terms of color, material, and accessories. It will feature a green appearance, rubber back cover, and a pendant with a similar call flash effect. In addition, HTC will also provide trendy accessories for Bliss such as wireless charging dock, speaker dock, stylish Bluetooth headset, etc.

In terms of functionality, HTC Bliss may not overly pursue top-tier hardware, but it will have mainstream configurations. Moreover, HTC will pre-install applications favored by women, such as calorie calculators and shopping price comparisons. However, currently, there are no pictures of this phone leaked, and the above are just conceptual images provided by foreign websites. It is reported that HTC will launch this phone together with Verizon, and we will continue to follow up.

HTC Rider

Additionally, a week ago, foreign media exposed a phone model called HTC Rider, and now more information about this phone has been disclosed. Foreign media claims that this phone will be the international version of the EVO 3D, a phone with a dual-core processor and a glasses-free 3D screen.

Spy shots of HTC Rider

Foreign media reported that they discovered the detailed specifications of HTC Rider from a leaked phone firmware. This phone will use the Qualcomm Snapdragon MSM8660 platform, featuring a dual-core CPU with a clock speed of 1.2GHz and Adreno 220 GPU, paired with 1GB of RAM memory and a 4.3-inch qHD resolution glasses-free 3D screen, making HTC Rider one of the highest-spec smartphones available today.

Moreover, the HTC Rider has dual cameras on the back supporting 2D/3D shooting, with 8-megapixel static resolution and capable of recording 1080p HD video. Unlike the EVO 3D, HTC Rider supports WCDMA networks and will be released in Europe and Asia.

HTC Lead

In addition to the aforementioned products, foreign media also published render images of a new phone called HTC Lead. From the picture, this HTC Lead is also an Android phone with a large touchscreen.

Spy shots of HTC Lead

From the design drawings, this HTC Lead is not entirely the same as any HTC phones already released, so it should be a new model. However, there are many doubts about this HTC Lead. On one hand, there is no HTC brand logo on the front of the phone, and on the other hand, the menu key still spells out the word MENU in the old style, whereas current new products all use the three-bar style.

Therefore, foreign media believes that this HTC Lead is likely just a Photoshop creation or could be a research and development design drawing of a previous product that was eventually canceled or modified. Of course, anything is possible, and we cannot completely rule out the possibility that it might be a completely new product.
