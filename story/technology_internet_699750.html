Wulingyuan Scenic Area Spends 700,000 Yuan to "Settle" Macaques

The macaques of the Suoxiyu scenic area in Wulingyuan now have a home of their own. On March 7th, at the end of the Ten-Mile Gallery, the essence tourist route of the Suoxiyu scenic area, I saw nearly 30 macaques from the first group settling down playing and jumping around freely in the monkey house within the monkey garden, enjoying the warmth of having a home.

According to reports, the Suoxiyu scenic area is one of the main activity areas for macaques within the core scenic area of Wulingyuan, with the "Monkey Slope" at the end of the Ten-Mile Gallery being the most concentrated spot. To protect the monkeys as much as possible, the Suoxiyu scenic area office and relevant nature conservation departments have set up publicity and warning signs in the concentrated activity areas of the macaques, arranged for dedicated personnel to conduct regular inspections, and gathered information on the whereabouts and survival conditions of the macaques. They also regularly and fixed-point distribute food. Currently, every day, two or three hundred macaques linger and play at Monkey Slope, becoming another beautiful sight along the Ten-Mile Gallery.

To provide maximum protection for the monkeys, at the end of last year, the Ten-Mile Gallery Sightseeing Tram Company invested an initial 700,000 yuan to specially construct a monkey garden (including monkey houses, management rooms, and a scenic belt) at the end of the Ten-Mile Gallery, and settled nearly 30 monkeys in the monkey house. The local nature conservation department has dispatched two staff members to specifically manage and feed them, while also distributing food to the monkey groups that "visit" the monkey garden daily.
