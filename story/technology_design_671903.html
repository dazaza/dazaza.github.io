China Resources橡树湾: The project has completed dismantling the scaffolding, and the ready-to-move-in houses are now presented. According to XMHOUSE news, since its launch, the large-scale central government-owned China Resources橡树Bay has been favored by homebuyers for its solid quality and mature supporting facilities. Currently, the project's construction has finished dismantling the scaffolding, and the architectural style of HaiXi Academy can be seen in reality. In addition, phase one buildings 3#, 5#, 6#, and 10# are currently on sale, with main unit types including two-bedroom units of 89㎡, three-bedroom units ranging from 112-140㎡, and garden townhouses ranging from 140-150㎡. These units come in both bare and fully renovated options.

It is reported that China Resources橡树Bay mainly features exquisite two-bedroom units, high-rise apartments sized between 80-90㎡. The starting price for fully renovated units is 11,000 yuan/sq m, while the starting price for bare units is 9,000 yuan/sq m. Each floor sees an increase of about 60-80 yuan/sq m.

Community Real View of China Resources橡树Bay

The project’s engineering work has completed dismantling the scaffolding, presenting the exterior view.

[Project Link] The project is adjacent to the planned "Jimei New City". It consists of high-rise, mid-rise, and multi-story residential buildings. Kangqiao Red Oak Tree Kindergarten has been introduced, and there are plans for a large supermarket, independent commercial areas, and other amenities to provide more living conveniences within the community.

The entire residential building layout is arranged in an encircling pattern and linear design, making the residential space more interesting. This creates open landscape zones between homes, significantly enhancing the living quality of the neighborhood. The residential buildings adopt an ART-DECO design, with staggered heights, dignified and elegant shapes. Unit designs center around the living room, ensuring ample sunlight and good viewing orientations for both the living room and master bedroom. The internal functional zoning is reasonable, achieving clear separation between inside and outside, quiet and active spaces, as well as day and night usage.

The high and low-rise buildings are arranged in an orderly manner, creating a typical academic atmosphere. With comprehensive living and recreational facilities as well as educational facilities, Xiamen橡树Bay will serve as a model of HaiXi academy architecture, showcasing China Resources Land's commitment to improving urban living standards.
