Caike Financial Management: Clicking Mice and Typing Keys

With the booming of funds and stock markets, financial management has increasingly become an issue people need to face in their daily lives. Various online financial tools have been favored by many citizens. Through online searches, journalists found that among the top ten most downloaded software on some well-known websites, seventy percent are various types of financial management software. The popularity of online finance has given rise to a group known as "Caike" among young people.

Citizens Favor Online Financial Management

"Caike" refers to those enthusiastic about online financial management. "Half-year return of 60.7%," a Caike recently posted their stock investment report on a financial website, attracting numerous replies. It is understood that sharing accounts online has become a lifestyle for the Caike group. Compared to openly sharing accounts, online bookkeeping seems to be more popular. "I started registering my expenses online recently and supplemented the consumption of these half months. I was shocked when I saw it - I had spent over two thousand yuan. I need to save money from now on." Miss Cao, who lives in Jing Tian North, recently became obsessed with online bookkeeping. She excitedly told reporters that compared to the tediousness of ordinary account books, this free electronic account book is much easier for statistics and calculations, no longer making her dizzy from daily trivial accounts.

There are currently quite a number of people like Miss Cao who are enthusiastic about online financial management. Xiao Liang, 23 years old and just graduated from university, works in a public institution with a monthly income exceeding 5000 yuan. Although he feels he doesn't spend much, he always finds himself short of money before his salary is paid. It wasn't until recently when he downloaded an accounting software that he figured out through analysis charts and exported consumption records that he spent too much on taxis and dining out.

It is understood that online bookkeeping is mainly done through two methods. One is through bookkeeping websites (www.coko365.com), where you can record your expenses online without worrying about data loss; the other is through bookkeeping software installed on a fixed computer, which offers relatively more comprehensive functions. Xiao Liang, a Caike, pointed out that compared to self-made EXCEL account books and manual bookkeeping, using an intelligent platform via the network not only records but also analyzes one's financial status, making him somewhat addicted to financial management.

Comprehensive Functions for Bookkeeping and Investment

Miss Shu, who works in a bank, has downloaded all sorts of new financial management software on her computer, including bookkeeping software for recording consumption, various stock trading software, and even paid financial management software. Miss Shu told reporters that she likes the classification and analysis function of financial management software. Through automatically generated statistical tables each month, she quickly understands the proportion of various expenses. "Recently, I am renovating my house and used the renovation budget function in the financial management software, choosing an economical plan, which indeed saved effort." Citizens like Miss Shu who are enthusiastic about using financial management software are increasing. Reporters found that the download rates of financial management software on major websites remain high.

It is understood that household-type financial management software generally consists of daily financial management, investment financial management, account reminders, and home management parts, and updates bank data online. Through data entry, financial management software can automatically statistically analyze and generate various charts such as budget vs. actual expenditure comparison table, annual income and expenditure statistical table, investment portfolio chart, etc. In addition, financial management software often comes with various financial calculators such as optimal deposit combination calculator, personal income tax calculator, etc.

Computers Still Can't Replace Human Brains

Some people believe that the popularity of financial management software undoubtedly replaces part of the function of financial planners. However, most financial planners do not agree with this view. Liu Wenyin, a financial planner at Jia Hua Financial Management Shenzhen Service Center, pointed out that financial management software processes user information regarding income and expenditure and leaves it to the users for analysis and decision-making, which may excel in quantitative calculation. However, financial management is very personalized, and some indicators require financial planners to decide based on experience and changing situations. Financial management involves many aspects, and it cannot be replaced by a single software. A staff member from the sales department of a certain financial software company also stated that financial software mainly manages funds after planning, and the two are not mutually replaceable. By Yishao Ling, reporter for Crystal Daily.

Fund Investment Return Rate Analyzer: Input the purchase time, unit fund purchase price, fund shares, and expected investment return rate, and it will calculate the future selling price of the fund.
