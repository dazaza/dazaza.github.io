Wuxi has "underfloor heating"? The news that Rongke Jiuyu Peninsula (introduction, price, floor plan, group buying, community) is launching with underfloor heating has attracted widespread attention from the industry. This is the first real estate project in the Wuxi area to use underfloor heating. What exactly is underfloor heating? How costly is it? And what advantages does it have over traditional heating methods? Recently, the sales office of Rongke Jiuyu Peninsula has been overwhelmed with calls, receiving hundreds of inquiries every day.

The application life of underfloor heating can last up to 50 years. According to Yu Wenjie, the interior decoration design director of Rongke Zhidi, underfloor heating is generally used in high-end properties. Currently, there are no real estate projects in the Wuxi area using underfloor heating technology. "Many people do not understand the principle of underfloor heating. In fact, underfloor heating uses the entire floor as a radiator, evenly heating the entire floor through the heat medium in the floor, utilizing the floor's own heat storage and upward heat dissipation law to conduct heat from bottom to top to achieve the purpose of heating."

Compared to traditional heating methods, what are the advantages of underfloor heating? It is understood that in terms of lifespan, radiators can normally be used for 8-15 years, air conditioners have a slightly longer usage period of 10-15 years, while underfloor heating has a lifespan of up to 50 years. Moreover, underfloor heating provides better temperature sensation. Yu Wenjie stated that using underfloor heating allows people to feel both the radiation temperature and the air temperature simultaneously, greatly reducing the heat loss at the upper part of the room. "Reducing thermal pressure also reduces cold air infiltration. The actual temperature of floor heating is 2-4°C higher than the indoor temperature, and a design temperature of 18°C can achieve the same heating effect as traditional heating at 22°C. For air conditioners, cooling is their strong point, while heating is weak. Winter use of air conditioners results in low comfort. Moreover, dual-use of air conditioners leads to energy waste."

Underfloor heating ensures indoor temperature for 24 hours and saves 30% more energy compared to traditional heating methods. Yu Wenjie introduced that in rooms with underfloor heating, heat is relatively concentrated below human height, resulting in less ineffective heat near the ceiling. Radiators and air conditioning heating work oppositely. "Moreover, the floor layer and concrete layer store a large amount of heat, providing stable heat for floor heating. When intermittent heating occurs, the indoor temperature change is minimal. Even if power and water are cut off at 20°C, the indoor temperature can remain at 18°C for 24 hours without frequent switching of heat sources."

Although underfloor heating technology has obvious advantages, is the cost substantial? Studies show that under equal conditions, floor heating is 30% more energy-efficient than radiator heating. For users using single-household gas boilers as the heat source, the savings on heating costs are significant. The gas fee for one heating season is usually around 30 yuan per square meter, so for a 100-square-meter residence, it would be 3000 yuan, but with floor heating, you could save 900 yuan annually. Additionally, the service life of underfloor heating exceeds 50 years, and the saved money could buy a Xiaoli car. If using an air conditioner for heating, the monthly electricity consumption is about 300 degrees, clearly showing that underfloor heating is much more economical.

Additionally, underfloor heating easily achieves independent temperature control in each room (separate room temperature control), further enhancing quietness and energy efficiency. Yu Wenjie stated that Rongke Jiuyu Peninsula fully adopts separate room temperature control, "Adding a separate room temperature control system, users can adjust according to their needs, making the heating system more energy-efficient." Stuttgart University in Germany conducted relevant experiments, and the results showed that the energy-saving effect of underfloor heating with separate room temperature control was 20%-40%.

Underfloor heating says "NO" to maintenance. Is underfloor heating convenient to repair? Yu Wenjie indicated that the underfloor heating system uses anti-corrosion materials, rust-proof pipes will not clog or change flow due to rust after being filled with concrete into the building structure layer. If there is no human damage, no maintenance is required within 50 years, which can be ignored. "Ordinary radiators and galvanized pipes, etc., because of corrosion, will experience running, dripping, leakage, blockage, and non-heating faults. Regular maintenance is required during the usage period. Similarly, using radiators for 50 years, the radiators and pipe materials need to be replaced at least four times. Even without considering the repair and regular maintenance costs, the cost of replacing four times exceeds 150 yuan per square meter."

Health benefits of underfloor heating: warm feet and cool head

According to the human body's heating needs, the ideal indoor temperature should be the traditional Chinese medicine advocated "warm feet and cool head." Modern medicine also proves that the feet have fewer capillaries, and blood circulation is not as good as other parts of the body, especially when the foot temperature is low, the whole body feels cold, long-term exposure can increase blood pressure accordingly and cause other diseases. The underfloor heating method is evenly heated from bottom to top, keeping the feet warm and the head cool, very consistent with traditional Chinese medicine health preservation.

In addition, since the surface temperature of underfloor heating radiation is lower than conventional heating panels, water evaporation is reduced, overcoming the dryness, odors, skin dehydration, and dry mouth caused by traditional radiator heating methods. The indoor environment feels like spring, thus forming a truly heat environment that meets human heat dissipation requirements, improving blood circulation, promoting metabolism, and having inhibitory effects on cardiovascular diseases; it is particularly suitable for the elderly and children, and has preventive and curative effects on arthritis and cold legs.
