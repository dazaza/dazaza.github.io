FOTON LEISAR 52-meter pump truck is launched with "three most" advantages

by ftleisa on 2011-11-04 14:06:59

On September 29th, the launch ceremony of Foton LeiSa's National IV Standard 52-meter pump truck was held at the福田EMC Digital Plant in Huairou, Beijing. As China's first self-owned brand pump truck to meet the National IV emission standard, this newly launched Foton LeiSa National IV 52-meter pump truck has created "three mosts" in China's pump truck industry: firstly, it is currently the most environmentally friendly pump truck in China; secondly, its pumping height reaches 52 meters, making it the highest pumping height among domestically produced specialized chassis pump trucks in China so far; thirdly, from a performance perspective, among all 52-meter pump trucks, LeiSa pump truck has the widest and highest range of concrete placing, as well as the largest pumping capacity, making it the most advanced 52-meter pump truck in China so far.

According to Fan Xianjun, President of the Pumping Division of Foton LeiSa, a bigger advantage of LeiSa pump truck lies in its "integration" advantage derived from the Foton Motor Group. "What we mean by integration is: integrated development, integrated manufacturing, and integrated service. These are our three treasures to win the market," Fan Xianjun explained, "So far, for pump truck manufacturers in China, the chassis and upper structure are mostly produced separately, making it difficult to achieve seamless coordination in development, manufacturing, and service. Foton LeiSa is different. Both our chassis and upper structure come from the Foton Motor Group, allowing us to achieve full integration from development to manufacturing and then to service, reaching new heights in pump truck technology, quality, and service."

It was learned that LeiSa introduced advanced R&D technology from its development headquarters in Beijing and two major R&D cores in Germany and Japan, applying CAE engineering simulation analysis methods to reduce the cycle of "design trial test improvement", effectively accelerating the development speed of new products, shortening the R&D cycle, and better controlling and improving the quality and performance of products before material input and manufacturing. In the process of "integrated development", LeiSa followed the concept of integrated development of chassis and upper structure, relying on Foton's development platform to develop from the perspective of the whole vehicle, successfully achieving seamless connection and optimal matching of the entire vehicle, ensuring the stability and reliability of the whole vehicle.

LeiSa pump truck also stands internationally, utilizing Foton's global component collection and purchasing system, comprehensively adopting imported components in key parts and assemblies, and starting to use world-class supply chains such as Cummins, Eaton, ZF, Rexroth, HBC for central component selection, not only significantly increasing the lifespan and reliability of the entire vehicle but also enhancing the market competitiveness of core components, successfully realizing the efficient integration of global professional resources.

Notably, based on the company's owned national network of 1100 integrated service stations for chassis and upper structure, from vehicle financing to vehicle warranty and parts financing, LeiSa provides users with one-stop financial solutions. Financial services offer low down payments, long loan terms, low interest rates, simple procedures, flexible repayment options, and light burdens.

Wu Yuejun, Vice General Manager of Foton Motor Group, stated that the 52-meter pump truck is a key product in LeiSa's "12th Five-Year Plan" strategy. To support the faster and better growth of LeiSa business, Foton Group continues to increase strategic resource investment in LeiSa. In terms of development resources, Foton invests its latest product platforms into LeiSa, continuously upgrading LeiSa's chassis resources; in terms of manufacturing resources, Foton incorporates the Zaoyang production base in Hubei into LeiSa business and invests heavily in building a new manufacturing plant in Huairou. Within the next 10 years, Foton Motor will follow the concept of "world standards, mid-range products" and focus on expanding five pillar industries: new energy vehicles, automotive Internet of Things, financial services, new energy, and high-end equipment manufacturing, based on continuing the existing business, growing into a comprehensive automobile company.
