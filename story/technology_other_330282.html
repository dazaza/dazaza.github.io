To earnestly study and implement the important speech spirit of Secretary Hu

by guogaiyanxiu on 2010-02-25 15:34:01

Recently, the Lao District Office of Sanming City held consecutive cadres meetings to study and convey the important speech spirit of President Hu Jintao during his inspection in Fujian, as well as the spirit of the meetings held by the province and city. In conjunction with the actual situation of their own unit, they are determined to seriously study and implement the important speech spirit of President Hu, take practical actions to do a good job in the Lao district work and accelerate the development of the Lao district, and not辜负the care and expectations of the Party Central Committee and President Hu for the Lao district.

One, Strengthening learning and striving to improve the quality of the cadre team. All cadres and Party members in the office should regard the serious study and implementation of the important speech spirit of President Hu as an important political task for the current and future period, and make sure to grasp it firmly. Focus on improving six abilities of cadres in the Lao District Office: First, improve policy understanding ability. Currently, the National Development and Reform Commission has clearly defined the scope of policies enjoyed by the original central Soviet counties and revolutionary old districts in Fujian. The provincial party committee and provincial government will introduce support policies for revolutionary old districts not included in the policy enjoyment range. We must strengthen communication with the province, seize opportunities, strengthen policy对接, and use well, fully, and flexibly the policies of the seven original central Soviet counties in our city and three old district counties (Datian, Yong'an, Youxi) that enjoy provincial support; Second, improve execution ability, especially the ability to independently handle work; Third, improve the ability to serve the masses, take the development of the Lao district as a responsibility, be concerned about the Lao district, take root in the Lao district, and serve the Lao district; Fourth, improve project operation ability, use limited Lao district funds well and flexibly, and improve the economic, social, and ecological benefits of assisted construction projects; Fifth, improve the ability to overcome difficulties, strive to overcome difficulties in terms of funding, manpower, institutions, and project-driven development, enhance the determination and confidence to do a good job in the Lao district work; Sixth, improve the ability to unite and cooperate, look at the Lao district from outside the Lao district, firmly establish the concept of a "big Lao district", strengthen unity and cooperation, strengthen coordination and cooperation with departments, and strengthen communication and contact with higher-ups.

Two, Emancipating mind and constantly updating concepts. First, be good at seizing opportunities. Seize the opportunity of the seven original central Soviet counties enjoying national western policies and the provincial party committee and provincial government introducing policy support for old district counties, increase support, increase assistance, increase inclination, do a good job in project research, actively think, actively act, actively study, actively对接, and actively implement. Second, dare to pilot first. From the perspective of benefiting the development of the Lao district, improving the living and production conditions of the people in the Lao district, and facilitating work, carefully consider the fields and channels for piloting the Lao district work. With innovative concepts, promote the Lao district work to a new level, produce new results, and achieve new effectiveness. Third, bravely take responsibility. Adhere to the system of collective research on major matters. Enhance execution ability and implementation ability, ensure "zero" obstacles in work advancement, truly achieve "four less four more": less requests for instructions, more thinking about ways to solve problems; less explanations, more solving actual difficulties; less bickering, more doing practical work; less formalism, more doing practical and effective things.

Three, Contacting reality and focusing on implementing work. According to the deployment of the province and city, in conjunction with the actual work of the Lao district, focus on doing "five works" currently: First, strengthen publicity and lay a solid ideological foundation for the Lao district. Through various channels, publicize the important speech spirit of President Hu during his inspection in Fujian, especially the important speech spirit regarding Lao district construction. Combine the study of President Hu's important speeches with the study of a series of important discourses on Lao district development in recent years, further understand the requirements of the Party Central Committee and President Hu for Lao district construction, and lay a solid ideological foundation for Lao district construction; Second, strengthen research and play the role of advisor and assistant to the Lao District Office. Enhance the foresight and forward-looking nature of work, focus on the central work of the municipal Party committee and municipal government, focus on the hot and difficult issues and deep-seated contradictions in Lao district construction and development, strengthen research, and provide references for decision-making by the municipal Party committee and municipal government; Third, strengthen guidance and effectively implement assistance work. With deep feelings for the people of the Lao district, do more work in terms of policy level, atmosphere creation, and seeking support, and implement various support policies from the central, provincial, and municipal levels; Fourth, strengthen service and do a good job in all aspects related to the "five old" personnel of the revolution. Seriously implement the "five old" living and medical subsidy policies and deliver the care of the Party and government to the "five old"; Fifth, strengthen learning and build a strong team of Lao district cadres. Build a learning-oriented agency, improve ideological and policy levels and the ability to solve practical problems; strengthen self-construction, promote work development, and open up a new situation in Lao district work.

(Sanming Lao District Office)
