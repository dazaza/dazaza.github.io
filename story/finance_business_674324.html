Hotpot formula (top secret) Among all the snacks, the unique flavor "hotpot" ranks first. Hotpot, as the name suggests, is food that is both numbing and spicy and quite hot. Since it's numbing, there can't be no Sichuan peppercorn; and since it's spicy, there can't be no chili pepper. Speaking of which, it seems a bit similar to our Sichuan cuisine. Of course, we can't just eat Sichuan peppercorn and chili pepper. The main food for hotpot is vermicelli. If you want to upgrade its quality, making it more delicious, you can appropriately add meat and green food, etc., that way it will be more nutritious, but may change the original taste of hotpot, losing the pure satisfaction. Hotpot also emphasizes the word "hot", which means eating it while it's hot, while it's scalding, so that you can experience the numbness thoroughly and the spiciness powerfully.

Hotpot Formula - 1

Half a catty of rapeseed oil, half a catty of lard, half a catty of beef tallow, two taels of Sichuan peppercorn, four taels of dried chili peppers, one bag of Pixian broad bean paste, two taels of rock sugar, half a catty of ginger, half a catty of garlic, five star anises, a little fennel seed, twenty-five cloves, five small pieces of cinnamon, one tael of chicken essence. Mix three oils - add rock sugar and stir-fry over low heat until melted - add Pixian broad bean paste and stir-fry over low heat until fragrant (at least half an hour), without carbonizing the sugar, otherwise it will turn bitter - stir-fry the rest (except for Chinese medicine) together, after the ginger and garlic release their flavors - divide into five pots - add water (preferably bone soup) - evenly add Chinese medicine and boil for half an hour.

Hotpot Formula - 2

250 grams of beef tallow, 100 grams of rapeseed oil, 150 grams of Pixian broad bean paste, 50 grams of Yongchuan fermented black beans, 10 grams of rock sugar, 5 grams of Sichuan peppercorn, 2 grams of black pepper, 30 grams of dried chili peppers, 20 grams of fermented rice wine, 20 grams of Shaoxing wine, 10 grams of ginger, 100 grams of salt, 10 grams of cardamom, 10 grams of cinnamon, 10 grams of grass root, 10 grams of white fungus, 250 grams of chili powder, 1500 grams of fresh broth. Place the wok over high heat, add rapeseed oil until six-tenths heated, add finely chopped Pixian broad bean paste until fragrant, quickly add minced ginger and Sichuan peppercorn, stir-fry until fragrant immediately add fresh broth. Then add crushed fermented black beans, finely ground rock sugar, beef tallow, fermented rice wine, cooking wine, salt, pepper powder, dried chili peppers, cardamom, etc. After boiling, skim off the foam to make the stock.

Hotpot Formula - 3

Condiments: Sichuan peppercorn, Sichuan pepper, star anise, cinnamon, cool ginger, bay leaf, nutmeg, white sugar, dark soy sauce, broad bean paste, ginger, scallion.

Main ingredients: tender chicken legs, squabs (two, depending on the amount of soup), pork bones, chicken bones, buffalo bones, water buffalo stomach.

Oil: Lard, beef tallow, rapeseed oil.

Production: Add appropriate amount of rapeseed oil, when oil temperature reaches six-tenths, add white sugar and stir-fry until it turns into a flowing lake-like consistency. When oil temperature rises to nine-tenths, add tender chicken legs. When color turns bright red and slightly darker, add high broth, simmer lightly, add beef tallow, lard, chicken bones, buffalo bones, water buffalo stomach, add Sichuan peppercorn, star anise, cinnamon, cool ginger, nutmeg, bay leaf, grass root, a little dark soy sauce, slow fire light stew. Start another pot, add vegetable oil, stir-fry broad bean paste until fragrant, add minced ginger; when meat is tender, broth has color, remove odor three times, broth is ready.

Side dishes: Enoki mushrooms, cilantro, green bamboo shoots, etc.

Flavoring: Stir-fry garlic paste or sesame oil.

Method and steps of making hotpot

Hotpot belongs to the lower category of hotpot varieties, it is the "snack type" of Sichuan hotpot, mainly operated at night markets in the evening or night time. It has thick spicy and numbing flavor, economical and practical, mainly suitable for young people to operate.

Condiments: 20 kilograms of beef tallow; 5 kilograms of rapeseed oil; 4 kilograms of Pixian sauce; 2.5 kilograms of chili pepper (Tianjin chili); 2 kilograms of Sichuan peppercorn; 2 liang of rock sugar; 1 kilogram of ginger; 0.5 kilogram of scallion; 0.1 kilogram of star anise; 0.5 liang of licorice; 0.5 liang of mountain nai; 1 liang of cinnamon; 1 liang of bay leaf; 20 grams of piper longum; 0.8 liang of white cardamom; 0.5 liang of fragrant fruit; 0.5 liang of sand ginger; 0.7 liang of purple grass; 0.3 liang of clove; 0.5 liang of gardenia; 0.6 liang of star anise; 2 bottles of rice wine; 1 bag of fermented black beans 100 grams; 0.5 liang of pepper.

Method:

① Finely chop the Pixian sauce, remove the stems from the dry chili peppers, cut into sections, wash clean with water and soak thoroughly. Soak the ginger with the skin on along with the dry chili peppers, then grind into paste in a meat grinder. When grinding, include the ginger and grind into paste. Crush the rock sugar into pieces for backup. Slice the scallions and ginger for backup; soak the purple grass thoroughly and cut into small pieces for backup; soak all the spices thoroughly for backup; mix the two oils evenly and heat to eight-tenths hot, then remove from heat until cooled to four-tenths hot for backup; chop the fermented black beans into paste, mix the Pixian sauce, chili paste, and fermented black bean paste evenly to form the spicy paste for backup.

② Heat the oil in a clean pot to four-tenths hot, add the soaked purple grass and fry until fragrant, then remove and discard. Add the scallions and ginger blocks and fry until fragrant and dry, then add the rock sugar and dissolve to achieve caramel color. Add the spicy paste and heat over low heat for about 1.5 to 2 hours, add the spices and continue heating for 0.5 hours, add the powdered Sichuan peppercorn and heat for 15 minutes until the spicy and numbing flavors come out. Add the rice wine and stir evenly, when the water is almost gone, remove from heat and let cool to room temperature to obtain the base material.

Spicy fried chicken wings, I've tried a few times, and I think it's pretty good, so I'd like to share it with everyone, and hope for guidance from experts. www.6park.com

When buying chicken wings, buy the smaller ones from whichever store sells them, as larger wings aren't as tasty.

Cut the entire chicken wing into two pieces, season with: salt, chili powder, ginger powder (a little), black pepper powder (a little), if you want to add a little sugar, I can't stop you. Let sit for 20 to 30 minutes.

Add an egg, then sprinkle some flour (not too much), stir evenly (it's best to use your hands), so that the surface becomes pasty.

Before frying, use chopsticks to dip the chicken wings in dry flour, shake off excess flour, then they're ready to go into the pot.

If you have a fryer that can control the temperature, follow the method the cooks at the KFC store in Munich told me: 180 degree oil temperature, 10 minutes. If not, rely on your senses, fry until golden brown before removing. Note that you should flip each chicken wing with chopsticks in between.

Drain the oil from the chicken wings before removing from the pot, first place them on a plate lined with a few layers of paper towels to absorb the oil, then transfer to a clean plate to serve.

I've heard that supermarkets sell pre-made fried chicken powder or seasoning, but I haven't used them, and I don't think it's necessary. The chicken wings fried using the above method are already tastier than KFC's.
