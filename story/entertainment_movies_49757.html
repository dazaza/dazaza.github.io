**Flower Heart Detective Plot Summary**

Detective Mike is a wealthy playboy who loves to take center stage and spends his own money lavishly on investigations. His colleagues, annoyed by his antics, nickname him "Flower Heart Detective." One day, Mike is suspended from duty after a conflict between his ex-girlfriend Lisa and a mainland police officer named Lin Heng.

Lin Heng is the younger brother of billionaire Lin Yong, who was recently murdered. Lin Heng asks Lisa, who used to be his brother's assistant, to continue managing the business. He also approves of club manager Lai Zihong. Mike hopes Lin Heng will solve the case quickly so he can return to the mainland. To assist him, Mike spends large sums of money helping Lin Heng with the investigation.

However, Mike's father goes bankrupt, forcing Mike to become a true detective without relying on his family's wealth. Together with their informant, Rat, they search for the suspected gang leader Scorpion Yong, who killed Lin Yong. Meanwhile, Lisa and Lai Zihong are accompanying a group of billionaires on a yacht tour when pirates hijack the yacht at sea. Lai Zihong is shot and falls into the ocean.

When the water police arrive, Lisa and the other billionaires have disappeared, having been held hostage in a wooden cabin on a floating raft. Cleverly, Lisa manages to call Mike for help. Mike and Lin Heng rush to the scene just as the hostages seem to be rescued, but a masked man arrives on a speedboat and abducts them.
