Beijing Guoan (Weibo) has returned to Beijing on the 26th after a one-month training camp in Portugal. Tomorrow at noon, the team will reconvene at the Workers' Stadium and then head to the Xianghe Base for training, entering the final sprint phase. Beijing Guoan assistant coach Xue Shen (Weibo) stated in a BTV telephone interview tonight regarding the level of foreign players that this season's imports, Renaldo and Karu, are stronger than last year's Joel and Little Martin, but still need to be tested in the league and the AFC Champions League.

During the Portuguese training camp, Guoan played 10 friendly matches with a record of 8 losses, 1 draw, and 1 win. The host expressed concern about Guoan's friendly match results, stating frankly that in the last two games last week, Guoan's performance was not ideal, especially losing three goals against Navarro. Guest Liang Yan then said, "Solving problems is the key." Xu Liang (Weibo) believed that "The overall strength of the team is stronger than last year, and the player structure is more reasonable now."

Although the overseas training results were average, Guoan completed several tasks before the season during this trip to Portugal: the two forward foreign players arrived as expected, and the new season's main lineup and tactical combinations were completed. In addition to the earlier confirmed Matic, Francois, and Manu, the signings of Renaldo and Karudelovic finalized the foreign player selections for Guoan. Regarding the high-intensity Portuguese training, Renaldo said, "Everyone feels very tired. You know, if you don't feel tired, then you won't play well this year. We are all happy to train with the coach, which is beneficial to each of us."

Tianjin Teda (Weibo) introduced Sumi, known as the "Balkan Gerrard," as the Macedonian vice-captain. As a defensive midfielder, his style can be said to be similar to Guoan's Matic. Regarding this, Teda goalkeeper coach Fu Bin (Weibo) said when discussing their styles, "Their positions are very close, but their styles are different, and the differences are quite significant. One is more focused on defense, Matic. The other focuses on organizing and initiating attacks, which is Sumi's type."

Regarding the actual level of Guoan's new foreign players, Liang Yan questioned where the highlights of the foreign players are. Previously, Xu Liang had mentioned that this year's new foreign players are stronger than last year's Joel and Little Martin. When connecting with Guoan assistant coach Xue Shen, Shang Yi humorously asked if the foreign players' speed was faster than his. Xue Shen replied, "Manu is definitely faster than me. Manu's absolute speed is the fastest I've seen among Chinese Super League players, and his agility is also good." Xue Shen indicated that Manu needs some time to adapt to playing with the ball and hopes everyone can be patient.

After signing with Guoan, Renaldo and Karudelovic flew directly to Portugal to join the team. When talking about these two new foreign players, Xue Shen frankly stated that the levels of the two new arrivals are absolutely higher than those of the departing Joel and Little Martin. "Renaldo played a game with the team three days after joining. At the time, I thought he hadn't trained systematically for a while, so his physical condition might not be up to par. But after coming to the team, his adaptability exceeded my expectations. Renaldo has strong ball control and scoring abilities in the front field. Moreover, his personality is very outgoing, and he quickly blended with the players."

Compared to Renaldo, Karudelovic, nicknamed AK99, is less communicative and has an introverted personality. Xue Shen said, "AK is closer to Matic now because he only speaks Serbian, and his English and Portuguese aren't proficient. AK's quiet demeanor is somewhat like the previous Guoan foreign player Yelich, but character doesn't matter as long as he can score like Yelich." After arriving in Portugal, Karudelovic missed nearly a week of joint training due to a thigh muscle strain. After recovering, Pacheco only arranged for him to play half a teaching match. Xue Shen continued, "Karudelovic is a player with excellent goal-scoring sense and awareness. Every time there is an internal group confrontation within the team, he can score. It gives me the feeling that he always finds shooting positions and opportunities to score. And previous data already shows that he is an excellent forward."

Xue Shen also emphasized that the overall level of Guoan's new foreign players has improved this season, but they still need to be tested in the league and the AFC Champions League, "As mentioned earlier, many things still need to be tested in real games. This year, Anelka joined Shanghai Shenhua (Weibo), just like when Muriqui (Weibo) joined Guangzhou Evergrande (Weibo). The transfer fee was $3.5 million, and there were doubts about whether it was worth it, but now looking at him, it's indeed worth it." Further discussing Anelka, Xue Shen said, "Anelka is indeed a great forward from his performances in European leagues, but whether he can integrate into Shenhua is actually still unknown. There have been cases of famous foreign players coming to China who didn't adapt and left. However, based on the abilities shown by the new Guoan foreign players in just a few weeks, they are both very good."
