The English version of China Culture Industry Network was launched

by yinyin on 2008-11-07 15:51:30

On November 7, 2008, the English version of the fully upgraded China Culture Industry Network (en.cnci.gov.cn) was officially launched. This is the second major upgrade of the website after the Chinese version of the China Culture Industry Network in August 2008. The upgrade of the English version not only comprehensively improves the exclusivity, professionalism and authority of China's cultural industry network, but also opens a broad window for China's excellent cultural industry projects and products to enter the international market, providing a professional Internet service platform for China's excellent cultural industry projects and products to participate in international competition.

China Cultural Industry Network is a state-level authoritative cultural industry portal jointly created and operated by Shenzhen Press Group and Shenzhen International Cultural Industry Fair Co., LTD., which is in charge of the Ministry of Culture of the People's Republic of China and sponsored by the Department of Cultural Industry of the Ministry of Culture. There are currently three versions: simplified Chinese, Traditional Chinese and English.

With the rapid integration of the global market, the demand for international display platforms for Chinese cultural industry projects and products is becoming more and more urgent. Under such circumstances, the English version of China Culture Industry Network moves with The Times, and strives to make due efforts for Chinese cultural products to face the world and go to the world with a new look and system. After the comprehensive upgrade of the English version, there are four sections: information center, product trading center, national cultural industry project service engineering, and online cultural expo.

In order to facilitate overseas audiences to instantly understand the domestic industry trends, the information center has three major contents: news information channel, legal and regulatory channel and cultural garden. The news and information will be updated simultaneously with the Chinese version, so that overseas audiences can learn the latest cutting-edge developments of China's cultural industry from the China Cultural Industry network at the first time; The Legal and Regulatory Channel is a new column designed for overseas professionals, covering Chinese cultural laws and regulations, Chinese economic and trade laws and regulations and other policy content. The opening of the legal channel fully reflects the characteristics of the national authoritative portal website. Culture Grand View Garden Channel, including literature, music, folk art, customs and other 9 columns, brings together a large number of interesting and informative cultural information, to meet the overseas audience to understand Chinese history and culture at various levels and angles.

The newly upgraded product trading center optimizes the member supporting service function system, provides a series of convenient services for online trading, and provides a foundation for the absorption of overseas members. The Project Trading Center is the only publishing platform for China's cultural industry project service projects. At present, it collects authoritative data of key cultural industry projects in seven regions of China, including North China, Central and South China, Hong Kong, Macao and Taiwan, providing authoritative information sources at the national level for overseas people to search and understand the domestic cultural market. In the future, the English version of the online pavilion will be combined with the overseas investment promotion of the Expo to exclusively show the latest developments of the overseas branches of the Expo, display the products in a three-dimensional manner, and create a unique online exhibition hall.

The upgrade of the English version of China Cultural Industry Network has greatly improved in content and quality, including the core layer of China's cultural industry, the outer layer and the related layer of major industries, the international service function of the website has been further strengthened, and has taken a more solid step on the road of joining hands with Chinese cultural products to the world.
