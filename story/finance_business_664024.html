Traffic accident occurred yesterday on Provincial Road 83 in Linhai City, Zhejiang - Hangzhou Law Firm

by zjpjls312 on 2012-02-13 08:54:06

At around 3:45 PM yesterday, Feng drove a large passenger bus ZheJA3565 (with a capacity of 45 people, actually carrying 31 passengers and 3 crew members) from the new bus station to Yongquan Town. On the way, at the 8KM+70M section of Provincial Road 83 in Zhishan Village, Shijia Crossing Street, it collided with a truck ZheJG0924 driven by Wang, resulting in 3 deaths on the spot and 31 people injured to varying degrees. Among them, 3 were seriously injured (but not life-threatening), and the injured were immediately sent to Taizhou Hospital and the First People's Hospital for treatment.

Upon receiving the report, Yin Xuequn, member of the Standing Committee of the Taizhou Municipal Party Committee and Secretary of the Linhai Municipal Party Committee, and Li Zhijian, Deputy Secretary of the Municipal Party Committee and Mayor, immediately gave instructions, requiring high attention and sparing no effort to rescue the injured and handle aftermath matters. They immediately dispatched Huang Wenqing, member of the Standing Committee of the Municipal Party Committee and Director of the Public Security Bureau, and Lu Shanfu, Vice Mayor, to rush to the scene to organize relevant units such as Shijia Crossing Street, traffic police, transportation, fire, health, and work safety supervision to go to the scene to do a good job in rescuing the injured and dealing with the accident-related work. Yang Jingxuan, deputy director of the Traffic Police Detachment of Taizhou Municipal Public Security Bureau, went to the accident site to guide relevant work. Vice Mayors Xu Lind and Lu Shanfu rushed to the hospital to guide the treatment of the injured. At 7 PM that evening, our city organized a coordination meeting for the handling of the "June 17" traffic accident. Huang Wenqing and Lu Shanfu briefed on the accident situation and made arrangements for related work: First, establish an injury treatment group composed of health, transportation, Taiyun Group, etc., organizing capable medical forces to fully do a good job in treating the injured, requiring all injured to be observed in the hospital, especially doing a good job in rescuing the seriously injured; Second, establish an accident cause investigation group composed of traffic police, work safety supervision, transportation, road transport management, Taiyun Group, etc., to quickly ascertain the cause of the accident; Third, establish a post-accident work disposal group composed of the General Office of the Municipal Government, civil affairs, transportation, Taiyun Group, public security, and the towns (streets) involved in the injured, ensuring the funding for treating the injured, and doing a good job in consoling the families of the dead and injured, ensuring social stability; Fourth, establish a traffic safety rectification group composed of work safety supervision, traffic police, transportation, road transport management, etc., to inform all transportation companies and drivers in the city of the accident situation when they start work in the morning on June 18, learn lessons, carefully check and implement all safety measures, and eliminate accident hazards; Fifth, do a good job in guiding public opinion and actively maintaining social stability.

Currently, relevant work is being carried out actively.
