I'll be there.

by fkflr230 on 2011-06-14 09:54:39

Listening to TVXQ's "I'll Be There" made me feel excited again and again, because the song is filled with the love we've shared along the way... Although it's just the third album, although we've only been aware of each other for two years, in these two years, we have experienced so much. THANKS EVERYTHING THANK YOU HERE, indeed I must thank you all, thank you for coming back, thank you for staying briefly in Korea, thank you for your music, thank you for everything.

When I heard Jay Chou say at a music awards ceremony that we should stop listening to Korean music, I became even more calm, because I proved that Jay Chou is truly an immoral person.... I want to say to Zhang Hongliang, Zhang Guoli, and everyone who shouts patriotic slogans and criticizes us for listening to Korean music: 'You have no right! Because you haven't even created music that makes us stop listening to Korean music! Watching Hua'er plagiarize time and time again, watching Hong Kong and Taiwan remake songs repeatedly, I've long grown tired of this kind of music, and find it disgusting. You wave the flag of patriotism, but it's just for your own personal gain. I tell you, we do things for the love in our hearts, we listen to what we like, we live for what we love. Music is not a tool for making money, especially for someone like Jay Chou. Stop contaminating sacred music, don't see it as something as dirty as you! We will continue to listen to Korean music, and will keep listening forever.'

Xiu Xiu Bao Bao, why did you sing Tim's "I Love You," which hurt my heart again? You know this kind of song shouldn't be sung casually. But Cai Yan is leaving X-Man soon, so your song can be considered as a blessing for her, and I always wish her well. Thinking about the girl by your side, thinking about the sweet songs you sang for her during high school, I feel tears welling up in my eyes. But I believe that the songs you sing on stage are for me, and for everyone who loves you. I believe you can sing for a long time and become a respected musician!

My heart will always wait here for you, listen to you sing for us. My most precious memories, my most valuable treasures are your music, and I am proud of loving your music.

Today, Light Music announced that it will no longer cover news about Lee Seung-gi. Suddenly, I don't think Light Music is as professional as I imagined. Moreover, Lee Seung-gi has already apologized. Why do they have to be like this... I'm very disappointed with Light Music. I didn't expect my favorite magazine to be so unprofessional. I thought it was focused solely on music. From now on, I have eliminated any intention of working for Light Music (even though I probably couldn't get in anyway), because I believe a music magazine should absolutely not be like this! Patriotism isn't demonstrated here either. Only tolerance can show our sophistication, and make them regret from the bottom of their hearts. This kind of behavior will only cause disputes! I don't like Light Music's high-handed attitude, I dislike it very much, very much!
