In the recent textile market, one of the mainstream T/C (65/35) polyester-cotton blended fabrics, specifically the thinner type made from carded yarn (45S45S, 13372 specification), has been popular among suitable customers, with its price increasing accordingly. The previous transaction price for batches of T/C 65/35 carded yarn (45S45S, 13372 specification) plain weave fabric was between 5.40-5.50 yuan/meter. For smaller batches of about 1000 meters, the price was around 5.50-5.60 yuan/meter. However, last week, the price for such small batches rose to approximately 6.20-6.30 yuan/meter. Currently, for batches less than 1000 meters, the price is about 6.80-6.90 yuan/meter, while for larger batches between 2000 to 5000 meters, the price is around 6.50-6.60 yuan/meter. At present, the price of this type of T/C polyester-cotton fabric has approached the previous price of finished fabric, showing a relatively large increase.

Due to the crazy rise in cotton prices, the price of polyester-cotton grey cloth continues to rise, driving up the market transaction price of finished dyed polyester-cotton fabric. Currently, the spot transaction price for thousands of meters of T/C 65/35 carded yarn (45S45S, 13372 specification) machine-dyed plain fabric has risen to about 8.10-8.20 yuan/meter. Compared to earlier stages, the increase ranges from about 0.30-0.60 yuan/meter to as high as 1.00-1.10 yuan/meter.

Because there is a 4 million ton shortfall in cotton this year, cotton prices have continued to soar crazily. By November 9th, the China Cotton Price Index (CC Index 328) stood at 29,886 yuan/ton, an increase of 995 yuan/ton from the previous day. By November 11th, the index rose further to 31,302 yuan/ton, an increase of 441 yuan/ton from the previous day. Recently, pure cotton grey cloth inventory has been limited overall, and prices continue to rise. Consequently, the supply of grey cloth in the market has become relatively limited, pushing prices higher continuously. Currently, for larger batches, the transaction price is around 11.50-11.70 yuan/meter, and for smaller batches, it is around 11.80-12.00 yuan/meter, reaching or even exceeding the previous price of finished dyed goods.
