Nanjing Normal University's on-duty master's degree examination and admission matters

by qindingdangx on 2012-03-07 15:44:40

**ZhenSheng Education [http://www.zsjykj.com/] News Network**: The following is a notice regarding the provisional admission list and related matters for the 2012 Master's Degree Examination for Part-time Students:

---

### **I. Provisional Admission List**

The provisional admission list for part-time master’s degree candidates in 2011 has been determined. Please check: [http://202.119.104.167/chaxun/zzlq/default11.asp](http://202.119.104.167/chaxun/zzlq/default11.asp). The final admission list will be submitted to the higher authorities after qualification review, subject to their approval.

---

### **II. Qualification Review**

According to the notification from the State Council Academic Degrees Office, the qualification review for part-time master’s degree candidates will be conducted post-exam. Candidates on the provisional admission list who do not require a复试 (interview) should follow the procedures below:

1. Log onto the Degree Network website ([http://www.chinadegrees.cn](http://www.chinadegrees.cn)) to download your "National Joint Examination Qualification Review Form for Part-time Master’s Degree Candidates in 2011" and sign it personally.

2. Submit the Qualification Review Form to your employer’s personnel department (or archive management department; for education master’s candidates, submit to the city or county education bureau or commission). Ensure the content of the form is verified, fill in the recommendation comments, and affix the official seal (also stamp the printed electronic photo).

Note: Legal Master’s, Education Master’s, Accounting Master’s, Engineering Master’s, and Public Management Master’s exams are all part of the part-time master’s degree examination series. These were held at the end of October 2012 as double-degree recruitment exams.

3. Bring the following documents to our university for qualification review:

- "National Joint Examination Qualification Review Form for Part-time Master’s Degree Candidates in 2011"

- Graduation certificate

- Degree certificate

- Second-generation Resident Identity Card or passport (originals and copies of all documents)

After the qualification review, submit the original Qualification Review Form, copies of your academic credentials, and ID documents to the school for record-keeping.

If you hold an overseas diploma or degree, you must also submit the certification report issued by the Ministry of Education's Center for International Exchange and Cooperation during the qualification review (both original and copies).

Candidates on the provisional admission list who do not require an interview must undergo the qualification review on February 18, 2012 (9:00 AM - 11:30 AM, 1:30 PM - 4:00 PM) at the first-floor lobby of Duboxue Building in Xianlin Campus. Only candidates who pass the qualification review will be included in the final provisional admission list. Failure to attend on time or failure in the qualification review will result in the revocation of admission eligibility. Candidates requiring interviews have already undergone the qualification review during the interview period and do not need to repeat it.

---

### **III. Admission Agreement Signing Instructions**

Before formal admission, an agreement must be signed between the candidate’s employer, the candidate themselves, and Nanjing Normal University (NNU). After signing, the school will then submit the agreement to the higher authorities for approval before issuing the admission notice. To ensure smooth signing of the agreement, please read the following instructions carefully:

1. All candidates confirmed to be on the provisional admission list can download the agreement form (various types of agreements are available in the attachment).

2. The agreement must be signed by three parties: the candidate’s employer, the candidate themselves, and NNU. Three copies of the agreement are required.

3. All three copies of the agreement (all with red seals) must be signed and stamped by the candidate’s employer and the candidate themselves.

For candidates in non-interview majors who are on the provisional admission list, please bring three copies of the agreement form, stamped by your employer’s personnel department, during the qualification review. After passing the review, submit the forms to the reviewers. If the review fails, the agreement will automatically become void. Responsibility for any impact on admission due to improper handling of the agreement lies with the candidate.

For candidates in interview-required majors who are on the provisional admission list, please send the three copies of the agreement form, stamped by your employer’s personnel department, via express mail (EMS) to the designated address before February 18 (postmark date is the reference). Do not use private courier services to ensure the safety of the documents. Responsibility for any impact on admission due to delayed or missing agreements lies with the candidate.

**Agreement mailing addresses:**

1. For Subject Teaching (English), please send to:

Graduate Office, College of Teacher Education, Suíyuán Campus, Nanjing Normal University, No. 122 Ninghai Road, Nanjing, Postal Code: 210097.

2. For Art Master’s (Fine Arts and Art Design fields), please send to:

Graduate Office, School of Fine Arts, Suíyuán Campus, Nanjing Normal University, No. 122 Ninghai Road, Nanjing, Postal Code: 210097.

3. For Art Master’s (Music field), please send to:

Graduate Office, School of Music, Suíyuán Campus, Nanjing Normal University, No. 122 Ninghai Road, Nanjing, Postal Code: 210097.

4. After the school stamps the agreement, two copies will be returned with the admission notice. One copy should be handed over to the candidate’s employer’s personnel department. The admission notices will be mailed out in April.

5. The unit stamping the agreement must match the unit listed on the Qualification Review Form. Otherwise, the agreement will be invalid, and no admission notice will be issued. If there are changes due to work relations, please attach a written explanation with the original unit’s official seal.

---

**Graduate Department, Nanjing Normal University**
