"North Wind Blows" - A Story of Devoted Love from the Sent-down Youth Era

by jqrxass on 2009-08-23 12:12:23

In the severe winter of 1974, Niuxianhua, the famous "Iron Girl" of Moon Bay, met Shuai Zi, a "notorious" young man of culture and art. During the process of supervising and educating Shuai Zi, Niuxianhua was moved by his versatility and mischievous straightforwardness, and her long-frozen heart began to melt quietly...

Seeing the comrades in the educated youth point returning to the city like migratory birds one after another, Shuai Zi racked his brains and did a stupid thing that would affect his whole life, almost becoming a disabled person. At this time, Niuxianhua revealed her feelings and married him resolutely. Later, Shuai Zi took Niuxianhua and their two daughters to the city. Shuai Zi's first love girlfriend Liu Qing had an unhappy marriage, and attributed all hatred and pain to Niuxianhua. She clung to Shuai Zi and coerced him to go south to Guangzhou for business with her, and set up a scheme to throw her and Shuai Zi's mentally challenged son to Niuxianhua.

Part One: The North Wind Blows

Pang Dianzhang wore a big fur hat similar to Yang Zirong's. He looked gloomy, like a great military strategist commanding thousands of troops and bearing the responsibility of saving the country and nation. He walked back and forth in front of the stove with his hands behind his back and said indignantly: "Comrades, the class struggle is very complicated at present, and we must remain highly vigilant at all times. What happened today in the point is a conspiracy, absolutely a conspiracy! The black hand behind the curtain has been thinking about this pig day and night, even in dreams!"

Part Two: The North Wind Blows

Zhao Chunli chimed in from the side: "That's right, why be so suspicious!" Liu Qing also thought that what Shuai Zi did was inappropriate, and hurriedly persuaded him, "Shuai Zi, don't do this!" Shuai Zi called out: "Then what should I do? I must find the book. There is a traitor in our Youth Point. I fell into his hands last time, and I will expose him so that you can recognize him!"

Part Three: The North Wind Blows

It seemed that Shuai Zi had practiced for some time, panting heavily from exhaustion: "Oh, it's like this. When I practice, the villagers gather around to watch, which makes me really embarrassed. Only practicing alone in the snowy field gives me a sense." "Don't freeze," Niuxianhua said concernedly, "In this performance, you have to participate in several programs alone. If you catch a cold, it would be terrible. You need to take care of your body and bring back the red flag of the entire commune's performance for me!" Shuai Zi confidently replied: "Captain Niu, just rest assured, there will definitely be no problem."

Part Four: The North Wind Blows

As Shuai Zi was wrestling with Da Pang and Li Zhanhe on Rabbit, the music of "The North Wind Blows" suddenly came out of the loudspeaker. Shuai Zi immediately slipped off Rabbit. Rabbit felt surprised and asked Shuai Zi: "Hey, why stop playing?" "There's something," Shuai Zi ambiguously said. Jing Meili poked Rabbit and said, "What do you know? Be quiet." Rabbit suddenly realized and stopped talking. Liu Qing's face immediately turned gloomy, and she glared resentfully at Shuai Zi's retreating figure.

Part Five: The North Wind Blows

In the middle of the night, a shadow darted to the grain warehouse, making a lot of noise while opening it to steal grains, waking up the sleeping mute Pan. As he got up to grab the flashlight beside his pillow, the shadow rushed into the house and subdued Pan, tying his hands and feet with ropes. After doing all this, the shadow rushed out. The courtyard then echoed with Shuai Zi's shouts: "Catch the bad guy! Someone is stealing the collective's grains!" Then followed sounds of fighting and cries of pain.

Part Six: The North Wind Blows

"Shuai Zi, what's wrong? What happened? Tell me, you're driving me crazy!" Niuxianhua stood there and wouldn't move. "No more exams, let's go home!" Shuai Zi left Niuxianhua and walked forward. Niuxianhua stared at Shuai Zi's back in a daze, then suddenly stomped her feet and started cursing: "You coward, you're useless, come back to the exam room, you can't stay in the countryside for your whole life!" Niuxianhua cursed and cried as she chased after Shuai Zi...
