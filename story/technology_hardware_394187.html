Ten Essential Mindsets for Running an Online Store

1. Persistent Mindset

No matter what you do, persistence is one of the keys to success. Once we set our goals, we need to strive step by step with unwavering determination. Opening a store on Taobao is no different from reality; it's not just about getting goods and putting them on shelves. We also need to do a good job in service and promotion. Sometimes, even after initial investment, business may be slow for a while. If we lack perseverance, we will never see the day of victory.

There have been reports that many people searching for water in the desert died very close to the water source. The reason was they didn’t persist. If they could give themselves more hope and stick to their beliefs, they might have found the water.

The same principle applies to opening a store.

2. Learning Mindset

Continuous learning doesn't just mean learning from the experiences of seniors. It also means learning various skills. There are many experiences from seniors on the forum, so I won’t elaborate here. There are other things to learn as well, such as visiting successful stores to see what you can learn from them. This is the simplest way to "steal" knowledge.

If you sell clothes, learn more about clothing and color coordination, fabric differentiation, and characteristics. Be knowledgeable about everything, making customers feel your professionalism.

If you don’t know how to edit pictures, take time to learn. Practice photography, don’t be afraid of trouble or tiredness. Only through continuous learning can you improve. Remember, life is a constant process of learning.

3. Practical and Patient Mindset

Work should be done earnestly. Ask questions if you don’t understand. Don’t pretend to know when you don’t.

Don’t expect overnight success. Successful people start with small tasks. Only by doing small things well can we prepare for bigger ones.

Running an online store involves repetitive tasks every day: store management, product promotion, posting and replying on forums... Dealing with customers, some may be troublesome. Sometimes after explaining at length, the customer finally decides to buy something, but when preparing to pay, they change their mind and want something else. Or worse, after spending a lot of time, the customer buys nothing.

Misunderstandings also happen, requiring your patience to explain. Without enough patience, you will lose many customers and fail to run a successful business.

4. Optimistic and Cheerful Mindset

I’m sure many of you have heard this fable: “Two people each have a glass of water, both drank half. One says: I only have half a glass left, another says: I still have half a glass.” This story tells us that facing the same situation, some show helplessness and bitterness, which diminishes their motivation. Others remain optimistic, always encouraging themselves to keep trying.

Which mindset is more likely to succeed?

5. Willing to Give Mindset

There’s a saying: Giving doesn’t guarantee a return, but not giving guarantees no return. Sometimes we work hard but get nothing in return.

But in the process of effort, we gain intangible benefits, like learning how to work hard and cherish things.

Don’t overly focus on short-term gains and losses. As long as you truly put in the effort, one day you will gain much more than expected. A life dedicated to serving others is the most valuable!

6. Innovative Mindset

This society changes too quickly. We must stay at the forefront of the times to avoid being eliminated.

Watch more news, learn more. Observe the changes in the world and the market around you. Keep your ideas up-to-date and adaptable to society. Change bad habits and methods, and innovate.

With this mindset, we will continuously improve and not fall behind.

7. Helping Mindset

In this world, everyone needs help from others. No one can survive alone without others' help.

When you see someone seeking help, try your best to assist, don’t find it troublesome to type a few words. Helping others makes them grateful, which feels better than selling something.

Sometimes, when customers look at products in another store and ask you for advice, help them to the best of your ability, even if they don’t buy from you. They already consider you a friend, so they open up to you. Isn’t it a happy thing when someone trusts you? Helping others makes them aware of your kindness, which indirectly promotes your store. Why not?

8. Treating Customers as Friends Mindset

Even if you cannot treat customers as gods, at least treat them as friends.

Only by sincerely treating someone can the business last and develop. If you always think about taking advantage of others, your store cannot grow.

9. Courage to Take Responsibility Mindset

Once there’s a problem with your goods or any other issue, don’t make excuses when the customer contacts you.

Take responsibility bravely, apologize when necessary, even if you feel wronged.

If the customer says something harsh when they're upset, don’t argue with them.

Having this kind of courage will earn others’ trust and resolve misunderstandings. With mutual trust, business won’t be a problem.

10. Boss Mindset

A boss mindset means having a high vision.

Doing every task requires great energy and passion, making decisions decisively. He treats his work as a lifelong career to fight for.

Without a boss mindset, always thinking about quick profits and only focusing on short-term interests, fearing hard work and being indecisive, your progress won’t be far. A simple bread can become a globally famous hamburger, so what is impossible?

So everyone, let's cheer up and work hardest!
