[Omega Constellation Men's Watch] Grandma's superstitious sayings! Every word is worth pondering.

by ffgytj96151 on 2012-03-08 20:21:26

1. If there is a newborn in the family, family members, especially men, should not come home late. If they do come home late, it's best to stay at the door for a while, maybe smoke a cigarette. It is said that there might be some evil energy that could be brought back and scare the child.

2. Money you find cannot be put into your own pocket; it must be spent immediately.

3. Using a red wallet will definitely lead to financial loss or an inability to save money.

4. Hotels are heavily filled with resentment, and strange events often occur. When staying in a hotel, you must not stay in the last room at either end of the corridor. The bathroom light must always be kept on when sleeping at night. While sleeping, you should lie in a "figure eight" position on the bed, or sleep horizontally, or in various awkward positions—just don't lie on your side leaving a large empty space on the bed.

5. Relatives should not compare the sizes of their hands.

6. Good things generated by YY (YY likely refers to a form of online chat or imagination) or events leading to one's success, even if they start well, will end up having opposite results nine times out of ten—it's extremely accurate.

7. Passing by the graves of one's ancestors without making offerings may cause illness.

8. Do not talk about people during the day, and do not talk about ghosts at night.

9. When walking, do not step on manhole covers as it brings bad luck and affects one’s fortune. If you do step on one, quickly pat your buttocks.

10. Wherever you are, if you grab the toilet seat, it will definitely rain.

11. Women during menstruation should not touch incense or related items.

12. Whistling outside at night easily attracts wandering spirits.

13. Many things should not be mentioned aloud; once mentioned, they tend to happen. For example, saying “I haven’t caught a cold for a long time” will likely result in catching a cold soon after.

14. Do not mock others' shortcomings, or you will suffer the same fate yourself—it’s very effective.

15. If you use being sick as an excuse to decline someone's invitation or skip class, you will definitely get sick after some time, and the type of illness will match the lie you told.

16. Buying a comb (one with very fine teeth), if you dream of something unpleasant, upon waking up, do not speak. First, break off one tooth from the comb, and the bad thing will be avoided.

17. If you have a bad dream, flip your pillow over and spit twice, and it will be fine.

18. Left eye twitching signifies wealth, right eye twitching signifies misfortune.

19. People born in the year of the sheep during winter, when there is no grass on the ground, have tough lives.

20. If a mason places a paper figure inside a wall while constructing it, those who later live in the house will experience misfortune.

21. If an elderly person passes gas before passing away, it signifies prosperity for their descendants.

22. If someone is very liked by the elderly or children, this indicates that the person has a good destiny and is a noble individual.
