PLA revises barracks area standards, adds clubhouses and bathrooms

by 8clt601y1m on 2009-12-07 13:20:16

Core tip: On December 6th, the Ministry of National Defense announced that "The Floor Area Standards for Chinese People's Liberation Army Barracks" will be implemented from January 1st, 2010. The standards have increased the per capita area of dormitories and canteens, added clubs, shower rooms, health rooms, barber shops, drying rooms, etc., and strictly adjusted and limited the floor area indicators of office buildings and reception houses at all levels of the military.

Published on the website of the Ministry of National Defense in Beijing on December 6th, after being approved by Chairman Hu Jintao of the Central Military Commission (CMC), the CMC recently issued the newly revised "The Floor Area Standards for Chinese People's Liberation Army Barracks", which will be implemented from January 1st, 2010.

The new "Standards" carefully implement the important instructions of President Hu about the development and construction of the army, adhere to the combat effectiveness standard, rely on relevant national and military laws, regulations, and policy systems, focus on researching and solving new situations and problems, and based on the logistics support capabilities of our army, follow the principles of ensuring preparedness, looking at development, putting people first, facing the grassroots, inheriting and developing, paying attention to coordination, doing what we can, and appropriately improving, to standardize the floor area standards for various types of new barracks in our army.

Compared with the current "Standards" issued by the CMC in 1979, the new "Standards" mainly made revisions in three aspects:

- Reclassified the categories of barracks. According to their usage functions, barracks are consolidated into nine categories, making the content focused, the structure clear, the classification explicit, and simple and applicable.

- Moderately adjusted the area indicators. Increased the floor area of living quarters for soldiers at grassroots companies, raised the per capita area of dormitories and canteens, added clubs, shower rooms, health rooms, barber shops, drying rooms, etc.; supplemented officer training centers, battle command rooms, and other military function rooms; referenced national standards to moderately increase the floor area indicators of public rooms such as officer residences, medical and health care, meeting rooms, teaching rooms, etc.; based on "The Floor Area Standards for Office Buildings of Party and Government Agencies", strictly adjusted and limited the floor area indicators of office buildings and reception houses at all levels of the military.

- Standardized the calculation method of special professional room areas. According to the actual situation of diverse and changing professional rooms, for professional rooms such as supporting facilities for national defense engineering, weapon equipment testing and inspection guarantee rooms, indoor business operation guarantee rooms, and professional warehouse rooms, specific area indicators are not limited, only the general calculation methods and approval procedures are standardized.

The issuance of the new "Standards" is an important measure to deeply study and practice the scientific development concept, faithfully fulfill the historical mission of our army in the new century and new stage, implement the requirements of comprehensively building a modern logistics system, and promote the scientific development of logistics. It will have a profound impact on enhancing the cohesion of troops and improving the combat effectiveness of troops.

(Source: Ministry of National Defense, Author: Fan Juwei)
