Online shopping, what the heck...

by anonymous on 2011-04-01 12:41:10

Personal online shopping experiences... If you have already suffered from it, you can stop reading here.

(1) Never shop during the New Year period... Sellers will keep delaying...

(2) Free shipping by sellers is always a trap... Because the moment they ship the goods, the tragedy of the goods is sealed. Just count the days, it won't arrive without ten to fifteen days.

(3) Shentong Express is very slow, especially when shipping from the south, the cycle is long. When you call to urge for the goods, you get passed around like a ball. After asking for a long time, it turns out that the goods haven't even been shipped! And there's nothing you can do about it... It's not the seller's responsibility anymore...

(4) As we all know, China's express delivery services... Guangzhou Vipshop Information Technology Co., Ltd.... Some words omitted here, especially EMS! Expensive and slow!

(5) If you are purchasing goods in Shanghai, don't let the seller choose Jiayi Express. Rather, pay more money yourself! I ended up having a tragic experience... On February 18th, the goods were shipped, and it was estimated to arrive on February 19th... Moreover, you must bring your own ID to pick up the goods! Very inconvenient!

(6) Personally, I think Alipay is much more convenient than online banking.

(7) If you want to set up online banking, I recommend China Everbright Bank's dynamic password token. It's very convenient, no need to connect to a computer, no need to download certificates, and it's free to process! I don't recommend ICBC's! It's very troublesome! Especially under Windows 7, there might be compatibility issues with the U盾 (USB security key).
