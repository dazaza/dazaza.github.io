First Experience with the Album Function of Tianya Lai Bar

by spmobile on 2008-05-07 08:30:47

"Uploading a photo is so troublesome! Will my star-like face never have a chance to shine?" — I'm sure we've all heard complaints like this before. Not all websites support local file uploads; many can only handle image links, meaning users first need to store their photos elsewhere on the internet and then link them. This process is already complicated for one photo, let alone multiple ones.

If you're experiencing these frustrations, why not give Tianya Laiba a try? Its album feature will surely surprise you. All images are sorted by upload time, allowing you to view newly uploaded pictures from netizens in real-time.

### Build Your Personal Album on Laiba

On Laiba, we provide each user with 1GB of free storage space, ensuring sufficient capacity for forum posts, blogs, and community picture sharing. This solves the common problem of insufficient photo album storage and makes it easy to upload your local photos. By accessing "My Album," you can create themed photo albums according to different topics, giving your collection a personal touch! Laiba's album has powerful upload capabilities, offering both single and batch uploads (no limit on the number of photos, individual upload size up to 5MB, total album capacity 1GB).

![Image](http://googlechinablog.com/uploaded_images/image003-722649.jpg)

### Browse Netizen Albums Effortlessly

On Laiba's homepage, you'll find the "Latest Albums" section. Clicking it takes you directly to the latest uploaded images area. All images are sorted by upload time, enabling you to instantly enjoy photos shared by other users. You can also click directly on an image to enter the owner's album and browse through all their photos.

![Image](http://googlechinablog.com/uploaded_images/image005-726621.jpg)

### Easily Post Pictures on Laiba

When you want to reply with an image in a thread on Tianya Laiba, we offer three options: local upload, selection from your album, or linking an external image. For local uploads, you can directly upload up to 5 photos at once and choose which album to store them in.

![Image](http://googlechinablog.com/uploaded_images/image008-782938.jpg)

Similarly, by selecting "From Album," you can share cherished photos stored in your collection with friends.

![Image](http://googlechinablog.com/uploaded_images/image010-737949.jpg)

If you come across cool photos elsewhere and don't mind the extra effort, you can always use the more "primitive" method—just go to "Link Image" and paste the URL.

![Image](http://googlechinablog.com/uploaded_images/image012-777436.jpg)

Laiba's album also includes features for saving favorite images and albums, allowing you to collect photos and albums from other Laiba users.

![Image](http://googlechinablog.com/uploaded_images/album3-728755.jpg)

Tianya Community will gradually integrate Laiba's album features into its forums, blogs, and tribes, making it even easier for users to post images. Additionally, Laiba supports external image linking, allowing you to easily share your uploaded photos on other websites.

In addition to these practical album features, Laiba users now enjoy an online chat function. If you have QQ, MSN, or Gtalk installed and wish to chat with someone, simply click on their respective links, and the system will automatically launch the corresponding software for a chat session.

As the Olympic torch lights up cities around the world, the Google engineers supporting Laiba have ignited their own creative passion. There are still many new features waiting for you to discover. Visit Tianya Laiba now: [http://laiba.tianya.cn](http://laiba.tianya.cn)
