Zhejiang High Court President: Official lending case trials should rely on party and government harmony

by abcfofas on 2012-02-29 10:32:26

The president of the Higher People's Court of Zhejiang Province, Qi Qi, said in an exclusive interview with the Legal Daily today that since this year, people's courts at all levels in Zhejiang province have been paying attention to changes and tendencies in economic and social development in various places, timely communicating major situations involved in cases, ensuring smooth flow of information between upper and lower levels, forming a judicial synergy, adhering to the general tone of work determined by the central government of "seeking progress while maintaining stability", deepening active judiciary and people-oriented judiciary, and continuously improving the level of trial services for the overall situation.

Currently, courts at all levels are closely monitoring four issues related to capital chains: first, the capital chain issue of small and micro enterprises; second, the capital chain issue of private lending and guarantees; third, the capital chain issue of real estate developers and mortgage buyers; fourth, the capital chain issue of some local government debts. The focus is on handling five types of disputes involving cases well:

1. The differential treatment problem of small and micro enterprises involved in cases. Indiscriminate assistance may result in counterproductive effects. Enterprises that can survive should be legally assisted, and those that should die should exit in an orderly manner. On the one hand, actively assist small and micro enterprises involved in cases that have market potential and prospects in mediating disputes, maintaining production, and overcoming difficulties, legally ensuring the effective productivity of enterprises with orders and more employment, avoiding adverse impacts on enterprise production and operation activities due to improper judiciary; on the other hand, increase publicity and guide small and micro enterprises that can be eliminated to take the path of market-based bankruptcy, especially some high-pollution, high-energy-consuming backward enterprises that should exit the market, actively explore difficult bankruptcy methods, force economic transformation and upgrading, eliminate bubbles, and achieve the reorganization and redistribution of effective assets as much as possible according to law.

2. Wage arrears disputes involving migrant workers and employees. In recent years, the provincial higher court has issued notices every year-end and beginning-of-year to legally prevent and handle disputes over wage arrears by enterprises. Courts in various places actively respond and effectively deal with them, legally protecting the livelihood and legitimate rights of employee groups involved in cases, achieving early warnings, early suggestions, and early coordination, preventing the accumulation and intensification of conflicts, accumulating rich experience in trial and execution, and achieving good results. This year, we will also legally punish the crime of refusing to pay labor remuneration newly included in the criminal code, and strengthen judicial publicity for typical cases.

3. Disputes involving private lending. Private lending involves both enterprises and people's livelihoods, and also relates to regional stability, with strong judicial policy implications. Courts throughout the province should increase judicial synergy. Courts in Wenzhou and other places with many cases involved should rely tightly on local party and government coordination, legally and appropriately handling issues involving wide coverage and large volume, civil and criminal intersections, etc., carefully grasping the boundary between illegal private lending and illegal fundraising crimes, and legally recognizing the validity of private lending contracts. Regarding the legality and illegality of private lending, the scope of legality is larger than illegality because according to the interpretation of the Contract Law, as long as it does not violate the prohibitive provisions of laws passed by the National People's Congress and its Standing Committee, the contract can be recognized as valid. There are at least two large spaces between this and illegal contracts: one is where the law does not provide for it, and the other is where the law has encouraging provisions rather than prohibitive ones. These two situations can be recognized as legal but cannot be recognized as illegal.

Courts should dare to lead and be good at pioneering, expanding new strategies for handling private lending disputes for courts throughout the province, summarizing new experiences, jointly safeguarding the stability of the market economy and social order in various places. They should keep pace with the new round of financial reforms in our province, legally supporting the central government's financial innovation actions, promoting the transparent and standardized operations of private lending and private capital, alleviating the financing difficulties of small and micro enterprises. This year, on the one hand, we need to seriously explore and summarize the practical experience of trials of private lending disputes by courts in Zhejiang Province, and on the other hand, actively propose revision suggestions for formulating relevant judicial interpretations for reference by the Supreme Court.

4. Disputes involving forced demolition of key projects in various places. This is actually a difficult issue regarding how to legally balance and moderately coordinate the intervention of the judiciary in public interests and collective interests, economic development and social stability. Last year, the State Council's newly published "Regulations on the Expropriation of Houses on State-owned Land and Compensation" abolished administrative forced demolition, requiring judicial review by the courts. For this, the mainstream opinion of the court is to implement the "separation of adjudication and execution" model of forced demolition, which means that in principle, the court only reviews the request for forced execution, and the specific forced execution is mainly implemented by the administrative agency. However, because the relevant judicial interpretation is still difficult to be issued in the near future, the issue of forced demolition in various places cannot wait or be avoided. Especially after the completion of the municipal and county-level party and government leadership changeovers, the momentum of starting and promoting new key projects will significantly increase, leading to more frequent forced demolition conflicts and disputes. For this, courts throughout the province should adhere to the principles of "strict judicial review standards, more application of hearing procedures, and efforts to promote self-execution," relying on local party and government authorities, cautiously conducting judicial reviews and execution tasks for forced demolition, balancing the protection of the legitimate rights of expropriated persons and the objective needs of promoting key projects and urbanization development in various places, upholding the bottom line that human life value is higher than property value, and guarding against extreme incidents and serious events.

5. Disputes under real estate regulation. Closely monitor disputes and social unrest that could be triggered by capital chain issues for real estate developers and mortgage buyers. Higher courts should carefully study the impact of real estate regulation policies on the validity of housing transaction contracts and promptly unify judgment standards. With the continued firmness of real estate regulation policies, housing prices may further decline, and housing transaction breach of contract disputes may significantly increase. In Hangzhou and other places, there are already signs of group incidents similar to "house riots." For this, courts at all levels should, based on the actual circumstances of individual cases, respect the judicial principle of the validity of housing transaction contracts, and rely on local party and government authorities to appropriately coordinate and resolve these issues.
