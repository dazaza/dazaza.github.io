With the continuous development of domestic 3G networks, the support that 3G networks provide for mobile terminals has also continued to increase. Samsung Electronics is one of the manufacturers that has launched more 3G mobile phones. It also maintains close links with the three major network operators. Today, Samsung and China Telecom have launched a customized 3G phone, the Tianyi mm Samsung W799, which is equipped with dual internal and external 3.0-inch screens, making it quite impressive.

The Samsung W799 is a flip phone, but due to its powerful dual 3.0-inch touch screen design on both the inside and outside, it can function as two separate devices in one. When the flip is closed, the W799 is equivalent to a candy bar touch phone, allowing all operations to be performed using only the external screen, which is quite convenient. And when the flip is opened, the W799 transforms into an elegant business phone.

As a result of having dual 3.0-inch internal and external screens, along with a large 1500 mAh battery, the Samsung W799 did not adopt the popular ultra-thin body design. Its thick body gives a very stable feel and exudes an air of sophistication when held.

The back of the Samsung W799 features a 5-megapixel camera with an LED flash, improving shooting results. Additionally, the back of the phone is designed with metal, enhancing both its appearance and feel while effectively avoiding troubles caused by fingerprints and oil stains. The rose gold metal frame surrounding the phone adds a luxurious touch.

The keys on the Samsung W799 are designed to be larger, effectively preventing misuse while ensuring an excellent tactile experience. There is also a shortcut key for accessing the internet via Tianyi megaupload, enabling one-touch online access.

Official Standard: Two lithium batteries (EB504465VU, 1500mAh), charging cable, cradle charger (EBH941SBBC), 4GB MicroSD memory card, data cable, stereo headphones, external stylus, video cable, manual, warranty card.
