Arming the Latest National Defense Education Tourism Base with US Military Training Methods

The nation's first national-level defense culture education base project - the China Air Defense Expo Garden in Zhaobao Mountain, Zhenhai, Ningbo, Zhejiang, is about to open. It is the first comprehensive venue in China to systematically exhibit and display knowledge of civil air defense and national defense education. The Ningbo Air Defense Expo Garden has for the first time adopted the most advanced interactive simulation large-scale weapon equipment developed by Super-View Simulation Center (www.super-view.com.cn, 88553466, 13801105972), a renowned high-fidelity cutting-edge national defense education equipment manufacturing expert.

After its opening, visitors will be able to directly participate in interactive simulated combat projects such as driving fighter planes, submarines, tanks, and sniping drug lords. This interactive high-fidelity combat system evolved from the US military's practical interactive training system.

1) Artillery experience themed around bombarding terrorists;

2) Naval submarine experience themed on underwater attacks;

3) J-20 fighter experience themed on "Eagle Strikes Long Sky";

4) Destroyer naval battle experience themed on Latin Bay peacekeeping missions;

5) Lone hero sniper experience themed on eliminating jungle terrorists;

6) Rocket experience using handheld rockets against armed helicopters;

7) Tank battle experience themed on offensive operations.

The China Air Defense Expo Garden is located in the Zhaobao Mountain Scenic Area, with a total investment of 180 million yuan and covering an area of 40,000 square meters. It includes three parts: the prelude hall, the civil air defense tunnel exhibition hall, the outdoor weapon display area, and the military experience area. The China Air Defense Expo Garden will be inscribed by Defense Minister Liang Guanglie.

Inside the panoramic movie theater, visitors can watch films about a hundred years of air defense history; the 1700-meter-long civil air defense tunnel will form a light and shadow time-space tunnel. Visitors will not only appreciate sculptures and murals but also experience war air raid environments created through multimedia technology such as sound, light, and electricity, including London air defense during WWII, Pearl Harbor air defense, Korean War air defense, Yugoslav air defense during the Kosovo War, and Vietnam Northern air defense scenarios.

Super-View’s most advanced interactive simulation large-scale weapon equipment is located in the military experience area, which includes four parts: aviation zone, navigation zone, ground air defense zone, and land warfare zone. In the aviation zone, one can experience flying over hometowns, taking off and landing, air-to-ground attacks, air-to-sea attacks, and air-to-air attacks. In the navigation zone, one can experience the feeling of commanding ships through stormy seas. In the ground air defense zone, there are experiences such as anti-aircraft machine gun shooting, 37mm artillery shooting, and shoulder-fired anti-aircraft missile shooting. In the land warfare zone, there are armored vehicle driving experiences and sharpshooter shooting experiences.

Of course, all these feel just like real guns and ammunition, giving people an immersive sensation.

The Expo Garden will be a comprehensive tourism base integrating exhibition, academic research, cultural exchange, and tourism, and more importantly, it is a cultural project for patriotic and revolutionary heroism education that can systematically spread air defense and civil air defense knowledge and skills to the public, combining education with entertainment, filling the gap of China not having a systematic air defense education venue.
