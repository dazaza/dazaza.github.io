The agricultural machinery safety production operation trend in Binyang County was stable in the first half of 2010 -- Industry Information -- China.

by qiyouchax02 on 2011-05-17 08:58:15

Since the beginning of this year, the agricultural machinery department of Binyang County has further strengthened the supervision and management of agricultural machinery production safety to ensure that agricultural machinery is safely, efficiently and effectively invested in agricultural production. The work of rectifying agricultural machinery production safety with the focus on preventing mass death and serious accidents caused by agricultural machinery has been highlighted, thus consolidating three major lines of defense for agricultural machinery production safety. In the first half of the year, the zero target of agricultural machinery safety responsibility accidents has been achieved throughout the county. Rural road traffic accident risks have been effectively eliminated, ensuring the life and property safety of the masses, and providing security guarantees for increasing agricultural efficiency and farmers' income.

Firstly, a work leading group for agricultural machinery production safety has been established with the director as the group leader, the discipline inspection group leader and two deputy directors as the deputy group leaders, and the heads of agricultural machinery groups of each town and township and the main person in charge of the second-level units as members. Responsibilities have been clearly divided among individuals, providing strong organizational guarantees for carrying out the 2010 agricultural machinery production safety work.

Secondly, we have ensured early publicity, early mobilization, early organization, and early action, earnestly grasping the annual inspection work of tractors this year. At the same time of carrying out publicity and mobilization, the Agricultural Machinery Bureau of the county promptly convened a meeting of the heads of agricultural machinery groups of each town and township for deployment and arrangement. Twelve banner slogans were printed and hung at twelve major intersections throughout the county, and more than 6,000 annual inspection notices were delivered by designated personnel to tractor drivers. During the annual inspection, specialized maintenance personnel were assigned at each inspection and review point to provide quality services for the machine operators. In the first half of this year, more than 3,120 tractors have completed the annual inspection, and 332 new vehicles have been registered. Compared with last year, the annual inspection work has reached a new level.

Thirdly, adhering to the working principle of "safety first, prevention-oriented, comprehensive management", focusing on accident prevention, we have earnestly grasped the agricultural machinery production safety work during various holidays. According to the characteristics of different holidays, we have further increased the publicity and education efforts on agricultural machinery safety, especially in remote mountainous areas, traffic blind spots, etc., where large numbers of machine operators and farmers gather. Face-to-face publicity and education are provided to remind them that the braking system of tractors is different from cars, and they should not violate regulations by carrying passengers, let alone illegally carrying guests. Through organizing safety learning days for tractor drivers, rural residents are educated to enhance their self-prevention awareness, not to ride tractors, and strive to improve the safety production awareness of tractor drivers and farmers, ensuring the safe production of agricultural machinery; Three field inspection teams are organized to inspect and rectify roads, focusing on monitoring the two mountainous towns of Chenping and Silong, as well as key sections such as Wuling's Scai Road, Gantang's Baduo Road, Luhu's Wanyao Road, and Liangtang's Ouyang Road. Serious violations and illegal activities such as tractors illegally carrying passengers, unlicensed driving, and black car non-driving in these areas are investigated. Responsibilities are assigned to individuals, villages, and machine operators, adopting the 'three surveillance' measures of person-to-person, person-to-vehicle, and person-to-road. When necessary, joint actions are carried out with the public security traffic police, transportation, and safety supervision departments of the county for comprehensive rectification to prevent mass death and serious accidents caused by agricultural machinery; the duty system during holidays is adhered to, ensuring the smooth flow of information channels for agricultural machinery production safety. The Agricultural Machinery Safety Supervision Station strengthens inspections, once potential hazards or accidents occur, they are reported according to regulations in a timely manner, and the emergency rescue plan for agricultural machinery is quickly activated, organizing forces for handling to control the losses caused by accidents to the lowest degree.

Fourthly, a safety inspection and special rectification of all tractors traveling on roads outside the standard, rural (village) roads, and farm roads throughout the county is carried out, focusing on correcting and investigating serious violations and illegal activities such as tractors violating regulations by carrying passengers, unlicensed driving, and using tractors to illegally carry passengers. Through special rectification, the number of tractors violating regulations by carrying passengers and illegally carrying passengers in the whole county has significantly decreased, ensuring the safe operation of all agricultural machinery in the county.

Fifthly, taking the theme of "promoting hazard governance, preventing accidents" and seizing the opportunity of creating a peaceful agricultural machinery activity, we conscientiously grasp all aspects of the agricultural machinery safety month activities. During this period, 12 promotional banners were hung, and more than 5,000 materials were distributed. On the morning of June 18, a safety day consultation event was held in front of the County Federation of Trade Unions building. Through distributing materials, answering questions on-site about agricultural machinery production safety knowledge and agricultural machinery issues, the laws and regulations and agricultural machinery knowledge related to agricultural machinery production safety were promoted, explaining the "Regulations on Agricultural Machinery Management of Guangxi Zhuang Autonomous Region," attracting many machine operators and passers-by to participate in the activities. Meanwhile, two working groups were organized to conduct publicity in the concentrated points of agricultural machinery in Luxu, Xinwu, Xinqiao, and Liangtang towns, delivering small booklets on agricultural machinery safety publicity to machine operators, promoting typical cases of agricultural machinery, so that a wide range of machine operators receive warning education, thereby enhancing the awareness of agricultural machinery production safety among machine operators and farmers, improving the voluntaryness and operational skills of agricultural machinery operators in terms of production safety, reducing the occurrence of agricultural machinery accidents from the source, and promoting the steady improvement of the overall situation of agricultural machinery production safety in the whole county.

Sixthly, making full use of modern media such as radio, television, newspapers, networks, and safety film groups for tractor drivers, agricultural machinery associations, and agricultural machinery social service organizations as propaganda platforms, we carry out diverse and rich agricultural machinery safety education and publicity activities, vigorously promoting the important significance of creating a peaceful agricultural machinery. Highlighting the "ten ones" publicity theme (organizing one peaceful agricultural machinery publicity and education activity in each town and street, sending an open letter to create a peaceful agricultural machinery initiative to each agricultural machinery operator; screening a safety education warning film for agricultural machinery to agricultural machinery operators and farmers; sending a set of agricultural machinery safety publicity posters to each model village created; giving a manual on peaceful agricultural machinery knowledge to each agricultural machinery household; teaching a class on peaceful agricultural machinery knowledge in model villages and primary and secondary schools; holding a competition with prizes for creating a peaceful agricultural machinery; carefully arranging a consultation activity for creating a peaceful agricultural machinery; signing a "Safety Commitment Letter" for peaceful agricultural machinery with each agricultural machinery operator; conducting a consoling activity for peaceful agricultural machinery safety), advancing the "five entries" project, and educating tractor drivers on "ten no-violations". Through extensive and deep publicity and mobilization, the safety awareness of the broad masses of farmers, agricultural machinery drivers, and rural middle school students has been truly enhanced, creating a good atmosphere for the creation activities.

(Huang Bingjian)
