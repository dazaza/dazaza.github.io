Benefits

For Computer Users:

- Customize your sit or stand workstation as needed - alternating between standing and sitting during work hours can make you feel more energetic and productive. You can change your working posture at will.

- Balanced adjustment points allow users to adjust positions effortlessly without tools while working!

- Applying an ergonomic workstation effectively alleviates back and neck muscle pain by increasing body movement during daily work, promoting user health and comfort when using computers.

- Easily and synchronously adjust the height of the keyboard and LCD screen for optimal comfort.

- Patented Constant Force technology allows you to position the keyboard and monitor exactly where you want them with just a touch.

For IT and Equipment Staff:

- Friendly IT deployment – convert most workspaces into efficient and complete computer workstations within minutes.

- No expensive professional installation costs; simply clamp the WorkFit-S onto existing work surfaces to transform office or workspaces.

- Wiring is safe and tidy.

- Flexible open design provides ample room for future computer equipment upgrades.

For Human Resources/Risk Management and Cost Savings:

- Enhance physical and mental well-being during daily work to reduce medical expense expenditures.

- Provide employees with standing work platforms without having to purchase costly height-adjustable chairs.

- Investing in ergonomic solutions brings a high return on investment – healthcare costs are closely related to unhealthy computer use habits. Healthy computer usage improves productivity and reduces employee absenteeism.

- The WorkFit workstation helps alleviate back and cervical spine pain, which is considered one of the most expensive expenses affecting employees according to the OE Medical Daily in 2007.

- Studies in office ergonomics show that providing employees with well-designed furniture can increase productivity by 12%-18%.

Technical Specifications

LCD Size: ≤ 30"

Weight Capacity: 16–28 lbs* / 7.3–12.7 kg

Lift: 23"† / 58 cm

Tilt: 30°

Swing: —

Rotation: 90° P/L

VESA: MIS-D†

Description: WorkFit-S Sit-Stand Workstation for Mid-Size Monitor, HD, with Surface Mount Base (black and polished aluminum)

Includes height-adjustment column, desk clamp, LCD pivot, keyboard tray with left/right mouse tray.

* Weight Capacity: Maximum load on height-adjustment assembly = 35 lbs (16 kg). LCD pivot capacity = 16–28 lbs (7.3–12.7 kg)

† Lift Range: LCD and keyboard adjust 18" (46 cm) in tandem; LCD adjusts 5" (13 cm) independently; maximum LCD height adjustment = 23" (58 cm)

‡ Mounting Standards: VESA FDMI, MIS-D, 75/100, C (hole pattern = 100 x 100 mm & 75 x 75 mm). To attach displays with a larger hole pattern, order the VESA Adaptor Bracket Kit (pt # 97-474), which will accommodate the following patterns: VESA FDMI, MIS-E, C (hole pattern = 200 x 100 mm); VESA FDMI, MIS-F, C (hole pattern = 200 x 200 mm & 400 x 200 mm)

Desk Clamp attaches to surface edge .47" to 2.4" (1.2 to 6 cm) thick

NOTE: Because mounting surface materials can vary widely, it is imperative that you make sure mounting surface is strong enough to handle mounted product and equipment

Ergonomic back-tilt keyboard tray ensures wrists remain in a neutral position during data entry—avoid repetitive-stress injuries while increasing comfort and productivity

Keyboard tray includes mouse tray that installs on left or right

Durable, easy-to-clean exterior composed of aluminium, high-grade plastic and powder coated painted steel

Patented Constant Force (CF) lift-and-pivot motion technology

NOTE: Computer systems mounted on a WorkFit-S may require extra long power and video cables in order to span between standing and sitting heights

Shipping Dimensions: 8"H x 23"W x 44"D (20 x 58 x 115 cm)

Shipping Weight: 38.5 lbs (17.5 kg)

Warranty: Five (5) years
