Chapter Sixty-One: True Soldiers (Part 1)

Sun Jianguo turned his head with a smile, running backward as he instructed those behind him: "For the first thirty kilometers, we will be running forward! There will be two stops for rest along the way, each lasting one minute. If you can't keep up, you may slow down. After thirty kilometers, if you still can't keep up, press your distress signal and then withdraw!"

All of us more than three thousand people have a distress signal each.

There are two main uses for this signal: one is for help, the other is to signify elimination.

If you can't endure hunger or fatigue, you can request help. Not only will fresh meat and milk await you, but you can also sleep comfortably. The price? Withdrawal!

In this 300-kilometer cross-country exercise, there might be enemies or traps along the way. If you're hit by the paintball guns used in the drill, fall into a trap, or get captured, you must press the distress signal to confirm your withdrawal.

Our group, the Seven Wolves, threw away our distress signals the moment we received them because we don’t need them! Although we all feel uncertain inside, doing this helps us unleash our potential even more!

After thirty kilometers, we finally encountered an ambush. By this time, out of the nineteen brothers following us, two had already dropped out. And during the ambush, another brother was eliminated.

"Captain, retreat or charge?" Sun Jianguo shot down an enemy who was about to rush at us and asked me.

I lay on the ground, observing the current battle situation. The enemy has at least thirty men, while we have twenty-three. The gap isn't too big, but if we really fight, it would be like killing a thousand enemies but losing eight hundred ourselves. I decisively ordered, "Getong, Gefei, and Kende provide cover! Everyone else, retreat! Take the small path and encircle from behind."

"Yes!"

Just as we began retreating, we heard a loud rumbling sound from behind. I suddenly turned around and cursed under my breath, "The enemy definitely has heavy artillery! Isn't this just slaughter?"

Sun Jianguo grabbed my arm and whispered, "Captain! This is just bad luck, but we must retreat! Trust that these two can provide cover!"

I clenched my teeth and said, "Jianguo, take the lead and continue retreating in this direction! I'll go back to rescue!" I pointed towards the retreat route and told everyone.

Everyone shouted together, "Captain!"

I waved my hand and looked at Sun Jianguo, saying, "Make sure you bring everyone to safety! Did you hear that?!"

Sun Jianguo saluted sharply, "Yes!"

After watching Sun Jianguo lead everyone away, I picked up the training machine gun and rushed back.

Gejia's two brothers and Kende were lying on the ground like dead dogs, helplessly watching the enemy's bombardment without any countermeasures.

I ran to their side and nudged Kende, "Counterattack! Retreat immediately when there’s an opportunity!"

Kende didn't expect me to come back. When they saw me, they suddenly shouted, "Captain! You...—"

My response to them was a series of gunfire sounds, "Dada dada…, dada dada…"

"Damn it, do you have to be so intense?!" I crazily sprayed bullets while cursing until the whole magazine was empty, then crawled back.

Not bad, the enemy was indeed suppressed by me.

But just as we relaxed a little, the enemy launched an even more fierce attack!

"Retreat!" Getong suddenly pushed Kende and me aside and said to me, "Captain, retreat quickly. I'll handle this round. You guys retreat fast!"

"Retreat?" I laughed, "If I leave you behind, I won't come back! Let's retreat together, let's go!"

"Boom…" "Dada dada…" "Dada dada…" "Boom…" "Dada dada…"

The intense attack forced us to retreat in a sorry state, with dirt constantly falling on our heads.

"Captain," Kende shouted while retreating, "Who is that group over there? It looks like John?!"

I followed Kende's direction and indeed saw John! John did not act together with our battalion but moved with the reconnaissance team's scouts. Their scouts have the most up-to-date information, but once they encounter the enemy, it's truly life-and-death struggle.

Just as I was about to go and help, I noticed them moving collectively, playing guerrilla warfare with the enemy. The enemy found them, but couldn't hit them. As soon as the enemy prepared to attack, they had already retreated far from the enemy. When the enemy chased after them, they rushed to the enemy's rear...

I shook my head bitterly and sighed, "Scouts are tough! Let's retreat, they're fine!"

"Boom!..."

We had just escaped the enemy's encirclement circle and were preparing to meet up with Sun Jianguo when suddenly a bomb fell right in front of us, throwing all of us into the air!

Thankfully, there were no casualties. However, most of our clothes were torn apart, and our food bags were blown away!

"Kende!" I suddenly pulled Kende, who was about to retrieve the food bag, and dragged him further back. "Kende, don't go! We can endure hunger. Once we meet up with Sun Jianguo and the others, we'll have food! But we cannot be eliminated! Did you hear me?"

Kende sighed guiltily, "Sorry Captain! I..."

We all knew deep down that we wouldn't meet up with Sun Jianguo and the others anymore because we were lost...

When we all came out, the sounds of gunfire and cannons gradually faded away. We circled around and continued advancing.

We hid in a small valley. I went out to find something edible, Gefei kept watch. The other two rested.

When I returned, Getong suddenly stood up, "Password!"

"Seven Wolves!"

After approaching, I coldly stared at Gefei and sternly said, "Gefei! How did you keep watch?!"

Gefei blushed, stood up and saluted, "Sorry Captain, I... "

Getong hurriedly explained, "Captain, he was too tired, so I helped him keep watch..."

I sighed lightly and threw the "night grass" (a kind of edible grass) I had collected onto the ground. I said to Getong, "I know you care about your younger brother and want him to rest longer. I also know that you might even be more tired than your younger brother! In this regard, I can't criticize you. But Gefei..." I coldly stared at Gefei, "Tired? Who isn't tired? Do you dare say you're tired?"

"Not tired!"

"You're too loud!"

I suddenly kicked Gefei, knocking him to the ground, "Do you want to expose us to the enemy?"
