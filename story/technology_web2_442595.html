Ready to launch the 2011 central air conditioning offensive

by fjk11mj8 on 2011-06-07 15:53:18

June 1, 2011 13:16:24 Source: Southern Daily Author: He Jianwen

Starting from June 1 this year, the energy-saving subsidy policy for fixed-frequency air conditioners that has been implemented for two years will be abolished. The world's first national standard for soybean milk makers will also officially come into effect on June 1 this year... In addition, according to an announcement issued by the Ministry of Industry and Information Technology: 35 home appliance standards will be formulated or revised in 2011, including 21 new standards and 14 revised standards. Industry insiders pointed out that the implementation of this series of policies will surely bring about many changes in the home appliance market.

Policy One

Cancellation of Energy-Saving Subsidy for Fixed-Frequency Air Conditioners

Policy Review

In order to promote energy-saving air conditioner products and increase the proportion of high-efficiency products in the air conditioner market, on June 1, 2009, the National Development and Reform Commission and the Ministry of Finance officially announced the first batch of the "Energy Saving and People's Livelihood Project" high-efficiency energy-saving room air conditioner promotion catalog. The subsidy standard was between 300-850 yuan, and the policy lasted until May 31, 2010.

With the withdrawal of products with energy efficiency ratios of 3, 4, and 5 from the home appliance market, in order to continue promoting the development of energy-saving air conditioners, the state subsidy policy stipulated that starting from June 1, 2010, the energy-saving subsidy for air conditioners would be extended for another year. However, the scope of subsidies is for fixed-frequency level 1 and level 2 air conditioners under the new energy efficiency standard, which will receive financial subsidies, and the subsidy standards have also decreased. The highest subsidy amount was directly reduced from 850 yuan to 250 yuan, and the lowest subsidy amount was 150 yuan. That is: the subsidy for new fixed-frequency level 1 air conditioners is between 200-250 yuan, and the subsidy for new fixed-frequency level 2 air conditioners is between 150-200 yuan. Old fixed-frequency level 2 air conditioners will no longer enjoy national subsidies.

Starting from June 1 this year, all energy-saving subsidies for air conditioners will be completely cancelled, and thereafter citizens who purchase air conditioners will no longer enjoy any subsidies.

Industry Rumors Predict a Price Increase of About 10%

According to this regulation, starting today, consumers who buy fixed-frequency air conditioners of energy efficiency grades 1 and 2 will no longer enjoy the maximum 250 yuan "energy saving and people's livelihood" subsidy from the state.

"Hurry up and buy it, otherwise it will definitely rise next month." Starting from mid-May, reporters found that almost all air conditioner salespeople were talking about the cancellation of energy-saving subsidies, and claimed that if they didn't buy now, prices would definitely rise by hundreds of yuan starting June 1.

The reporter noticed that in order to boost sales, major retailers took the opportunity to launch promotions. Many "energy-saving people's livelihood air conditioners" were marked with prominent price reduction signs. Compared to the promotional activities for frequency conversion air conditioners, most air conditioner brands had similar promotional activities for "energy-saving people's livelihood air conditioners". According to the rough calculation of the reporter, the discount was basically between 100-200 yuan per unit.

It is worth noting that while air conditioner manufacturers are launching promotional offensives to boost sales, rumors of price increases have also been rampant. Salespeople from almost all air conditioner brands claimed that prices would definitely rise in June. Some merchants even said that they had received price adjustment notices from manufacturers, requiring a price increase of around 10%.

Market Investigation: Shortage of Energy-Saving Air Conditioners, Prices Unchanged Temporarily

Is the fourth price increase of the air conditioning industry this year really coming?

"This one-horsepower single-cooling machine, originally priced at more than 2500 yuan, is now only 2200 yuan during the event," a salesperson of domestic air conditioners at an electrical appliance store on Hongfu Road said yesterday. During the investigation, the reporter found that as of yesterday, the relevant promotional offensives of various air conditioner manufacturers were still ongoing, and the prices of fixed-frequency air conditioners subsidized by the state remained unchanged compared to before. In addition, the reporter recently found that some brands of energy-saving subsidy models were out of stock, and some salespeople said they did not know when the goods would arrive. Other salespeople from several brands claimed that the factory was replenishing stocks, and delivery and installation could be arranged within one to two days. Regarding the price increase in June, all salespeople were confident that it would definitely happen.

However, some industry insiders analyzed that the cancellation of energy-saving subsidies would actually create certain sales pressure for air conditioner manufacturers themselves. Because the cancellation of state subsidies would make designated model air conditioners lose their price advantage, and sales would inevitably suffer setbacks in the short term. With only one day left, manufacturers are trying to sell the already produced products faster, so they are subsidizing the prices themselves to boost sales.

In addition, the insider analyzed that although the rising costs of raw materials and other factors have greatly increased the pressure on air conditioner companies, since the beginning of this year, the air conditioning industry in Dongguan has experienced three price increases, and these increases have already alleviated the pressure on air conditioner companies. Therefore, the person analyzed that based on this, the possibility of final price increases by air conditioner manufacturers is not great.

"The price of air conditioners may fluctuate in the short term, but looking at the future trend of air conditioner prices, the fluctuations will not be significant." Lin Lu, the marketing director of Dongguan Suning, said that the main range of energy-saving subsidies is the new level 1 energy efficiency and new level 2 energy efficiency models of fixed-frequency machines. These two energy efficiency grade models account for only about 30% of the inventory in Suning, and the overall energy efficiency subsidy accounts for only 5%-8%. It is possible that air conditioner prices may fluctuate to some extent in the short term, but in the long run, the prices will remain stable.

Changing Landscape: Further Expansion of Frequency Conversion Air Conditioner Market

With the cancellation of this policy, the air conditioning market landscape quietly changed. The reporter also noticed that before the policy was implemented, major home appliance retail enterprises had made corresponding adjustments in their marketing strategies.

In recent promotional activities, in order to achieve better sales results during the peak sales season, a relevant person in charge of the Dongguan Suning Air Conditioning Procurement and Sales Center said that Suning had reached agreements with ten mainstream air conditioner manufacturers such as Midea, Whirlpool, Chigo, York, and Daikin. Starting this week, Dongguan Suning launched the "Buy Air Conditioner, Get Electricity Bill" campaign. During the activity period, customers who purchase air conditioners of different energy efficiency levels and different capacities at Dongguan Suning can receive electricity bill subsidies of different levels.

In addition, according to the overall situation of several malls, all major home appliance malls are also preparing to start the mid-year air conditioning offensive of 2011, actively coordinating resources with the factories. In this procurement wave, the proportion of frequency conversion air conditioners has even increased to 70% in some cases. Industry insiders pointed out that with the further narrowing of the price gap between frequency conversion air conditioners and fixed-frequency air conditioners, most air conditioner manufacturers will reduce the promotional activities of fixed-frequency air conditioners and increase the promotion of frequency conversion air conditioners to improve the market share of frequency conversion machines, thereby promoting the popularization of high-efficiency air conditioners.

Policy Two

Soybean Milk Maker Standard Comes Into Effect on June 1

Policy Review

Since 2009, many media reports have indicated that the soybean milk maker industry has developed rapidly in recent years, but the overall quality of the industry varies greatly. This is because its safety standard follows the "General Requirements" and "Special Requirements for Liquid Heaters" in the "Safety of Household and Similar Electrical Appliances." Therefore, relevant departments have formulated the first national standard for soybean milk makers to raise the technical threshold of the entire industry, but there is no accurate timetable for implementation yet.

Last year, many messages emerged indicating that according to the announcement released by the National Standardization Committee, the national standard for soybean milk makers would be implemented on June 1, 2011. It is reported that this standard makes clear provisions for the degree of cooking of soybean milk, nutritional concentration (i.e., solid content), and grades the nutritional concentration of soybean milk made by various soybean milk makers. Level one aromatic soybean milk has high concentration, good taste, and easy absorption, meeting the demand for high-quality enjoyment; level two aromatic soybean milk has good taste and absorption, meeting the health needs of the general public; the soybean milk made by ordinary mesh-free technology soybean milk makers has average nutrition and taste, but satisfies the quick need to drink soybean milk anytime.

Industry Rumors Expect Further Standardization of Industry Standards

"For the small home appliance industry with low thresholds, this is undoubtedly good news," said Mr. Yang, an industry insider. From the previous soybean milk maker market, due to the low entry threshold and good market prospects, large and small enterprises have flooded in. And precisely because there is no industry execution standard, the entire soybean milk maker industry is mixed with good and bad elements. The implementation of this standard will significantly enhance the entire industry.

According to relevant data, there are already more than 800 companies producing soybean milk makers domestically, but only more than 100 have their own brands. Industry insiders pointed out that this standard has relevant regulations on the nutritional content and quality indicators of soybean milk, providing very practical guidance for consumers choosing soybean milk makers.

Market Investigation No Relevant Changes in the Market Yet

According to past practice, a few days before the implementation of the new standard, new standard products would be sold, or new and old products would be sold in combination during the transition period. However, during the market visit yesterday, the reporter found that this much-anticipated industry standard did not arrive as expected.

"Industry standard? I've never heard of it, nor have I received any relevant notice," said a salesperson of a well-known domestic soybean milk maker brand in the small home appliances sales area of an electrical appliance store on Hongfu Road when asked whether the industry standard for soybean milk makers would soon be implemented. Subsequently, the answers from salespeople of several home appliance malls were similar.

"As for the implementation of the performance standard for soybean milk makers, neither our mall nor the manufacturer side has received any message," said Guangmei Dongguan.

In response, Dongguan Suning said that with the implementation of this standard, manufacturers will produce soybean milk makers according to this standard, and Suning Electric will strictly enforce it according to national regulations. However, there will still be a buffer period in sales. At that time, Dongguan Suning will strictly monitor the sourcing channels for soybean milk makers according to national regulations.
