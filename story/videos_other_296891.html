High enthusiasm for political participation, first proposal out early

by dtjhsb119 on 2009-12-06 16:15:37

As in previous years, this year's CPPCC meeting also received many proposals from members. The first proposal received by the conference proposal committee gave the staff a pleasant surprise. Zhou Xinmin is the Vice Chairman of the Yancheng Municipal Federation of Trade Unions. This year, he has the honor of being the member who submitted the first proposal.

同期声: Director of the Proposal Committee of the Yancheng Municipal Committee of the CPPCC, Wu Yucai: "Chairman Zhou, this is our first proposal of the year, and I would like to congratulate you."

As a CPPCC member from the Federation of Trade Unions, Zhou Xinmin's proposal naturally cannot be separated from his work in the trade union, representing the interests of workers and reflecting their voices, including positive pressure air breathing apparatuses.

同期声: CPPCC Member and Vice Chairman of the Municipal Federation of Trade Unions, Zhou Xinmin: "We found that all migrant workers participating in engineering construction have very poor conditions. Their incomes are not very high, and wage arrears occur from time to time. If we can establish a trade union organization in a timely manner, they can report to the trade union, and we can also protect their rights through higher-level trade unions."

Through Zhou Xinmin's proposal, we can truly feel his strong sense of responsibility as a CPPCC member.

同期声: CPPCC Member and Vice Chairman of the Municipal Federation of Trade Unions, Zhou Xinmin: "As a CPPCC member, first of all, it is an honor, secondly, it is also a trust from everyone. We have the responsibility to reflect the situation of our workers in various aspects. The government departments attach great importance to the CPPCC proposals."

Zhou Xinmin is just one representative of all CPPCC members. During the CPPCC meeting, the members actively participate in political affairs, offer advice, and make many good suggestions and opinions through proposals, which clearly demonstrate their significance and role.

同期声: Director of the Proposal Committee of the Yancheng Municipal Committee of the CPPCC, Wu Yucai: "To promote the democratization and scientification of decision-making by the Party committee and government, as well as the improvement of work by relevant departments, playing a positive and effective role. At the same time, through the form of proposals, it reflects the enthusiasm of the members in participating in politics and the passion for discussing politics."
