18-Year-Old Guy Who Robbed and Killed Female Supermarket Owner Gets Arrested

by mjzd6713 on 2010-04-17 23:12:31

After 8 days and nights of intense investigation, at around 4 PM on the afternoon of April 15th, the suspect Pei from the supermarket robbery homicide case in the urban area of Suqian was caught on the spot by the police at his doorstep. Yesterday morning, under police escort, Pei was taken to the crime scene for identification.

At 7:38 AM on April 8th, the Happiness Road Police Station of the Suburban Public Security Bureau of Suqian received a 110 command indicating that a murder case had occurred in the Xinja Supermarket located at the northwest corner of the intersection of Bayi West Road and Yellow River Road. After receiving the alert, the police officers from the Happiness Road Police Station and the Criminal Investigation Team of the Suburban Public Security Bureau quickly arrived at the scene and found that the supermarket door had already been opened. On the ground inside the store lay a woman who had already died. After on-site investigation and interviews, it was determined that the deceased, Zhao, aged 42, was the owner of the Xinja Supermarket and had died from injuries inflicted by a sharp weapon to her head.

The person who reported the incident was an employee of the store. The employee stated that when she came to work at 7:30 AM, she found the store door already open. She assumed that Zhao had opened it herself, but after calling out twice without hearing Zhao's voice, she walked into the store and saw Zhao lying on the ground with blood all over her head. The floor of the store was also covered in blood. After screaming, the employee rushed out of the store and immediately called the police.

At more than 4 PM on April 15th, with the strong assistance of the Siyang police, the special case group officers captured Pei at his home in Siyang County and discovered a pack of Su cigarettes in his room, which were from the same batch as those stolen from the scene. After being interrogated, Pei confessed to the crime of robbing the Xinja convenience store and killing the owner Zhao in the early hours of April 8th, faced with overwhelming evidence.

It is understood that the suspect Pei is from Shiji, Zhongxing Town, Siyang County, and is only 18 years old this year. When Pei was 1 year old, his mother passed away due to illness, and he was raised by his father, older brother, and sister. His family loved him very much, which led to his impetuous, capricious, and stubborn character. During an argument with his brother, Pei actually picked up a kitchen knife and cut off one of his own fingers.

Around 2007, Pei had studied photo-making techniques at a photography shop in the city for about a year, so he was relatively familiar with the urban area. He also frequently visited internet cafes near the robbed store, so he knew there was a store there.

Before the incident, Pei had planned to rob this store in the city for some money. That afternoon, Pei came to the city from Siyang. Taking advantage of the quiet night, Pei managed to trick the store owner into opening the door and proceeded to rob the store, killing the owner in the process.

In order to confuse the police, Pei did not return directly to Siyang after committing the crime. Instead, he first took a taxi to the Sijiqing wholesale market area, then changed to another taxi to Shuyang. After spending the night in Shuyang, he returned to his hometown in Siyang the next day.

But even the smartest fox can't escape the hunter's hand. After extensive investigations and screenings, the special case group still found traces of Pei and accordingly identified Pei as the suspect in the store homicide case.

At around 10 AM on April 16th, when Pei was brought down from the police car, the boss of a nearby restaurant immediately lit the firecrackers that had been prepared long ago. After identifying the crime scene, Pei then led the police to a small square opposite the store to identify the place where he had discarded the weapon. Along the way, Pei's head remained lowered, and he could barely walk. If it weren't for the police supporting him on both sides, he would definitely have collapsed on the ground.

"Thanks to the police protecting him, or else he would definitely have been beaten to death by the angry crowd," an elderly woman said angrily while looking at Pei.
