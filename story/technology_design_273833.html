Nanning • Southeast Asia International Tourism Food Festival Attracts 600,000 Foodies in 9 Days

by syd17u123 on 2009-11-23 17:53:37

This is the ninth successful hosting of Nanning - Southeast Asia International Tourism Food Festival in the capital city of Nanning since 2000. It was also another successful attempt to face the public and allow citizens free access. In order to reflect the purpose of the food festival for the people, the eighth Nanning - Southeast Asia International Tourism Food Festival in 2007 changed from a chargeable operation in previous editions and opened its doors to the citizens. According to statistics, last year more than 300,000 citizens went to the main venue of Southeast Asia Food City and the branch venue of Yongzhou Old Street. Relevant officials of the organizing committee said that this edition of the food festival moved to Yongzhou Old Street, and the number of tourists received exceeded last year.

As an important activity during the "two meetings and one festival", this food festival not only set a high popularity index, but also received good reviews on the scale, content, and quality of the festival. The three major food areas of local cuisine, Chinese cuisine, and ASEAN country cuisine had a total of 131 booths with more than 250 kinds of food, plus entertainment and tourism goods booths numbering over 150, forming a nearly kilometer-long tourism food dragon on Yongzhou Old Street. Tourists can fully taste delicacies from all over the world, purchase tourism goods, experience ethnic customs, and enjoy two wonderful artistic performances every day. The fragrant food and rich activities not only were loved by the citizens of Nanning, but also attracted domestic and overseas merchants attending the "two meetings and one festival" to come in droves.

Wine, food, and beautiful scenery. On October 26th, the 2008 Nanning - Southeast Asia International Tourism Food Festival, which integrates internationality, massiveness, and cultural significance, came to a successful conclusion at Yongzhou Old Street. According to preliminary estimates by the organizing committee, during the nine-day period, a total of 600,000 citizens and domestic and foreign guests attended the food festival, setting a new historical record for tourist reception.
