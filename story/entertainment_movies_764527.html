Tie Dan (Gérard Depardieu) is a big talker who chats with others regardless of time, place, or audience. Because of this, he has no friends and keeps changing roommates in prison until he meets Ruby, the killer who never speaks. Tie Dan can keep talking to Ruby endlessly while Ruby (Jean Reno), silent and unbothered, is always planning his escape.

Despite Ruby's flawless escape plan, he finds himself stuck with Tie Dan. However, thanks to Tie Dan’s help, the two manage to escape amidst chaos. Ruby still has a plan for revenge for his girlfriend, but Tie Dan just wants to be friends with Ruby forever and open a restaurant.
