Zhang Shangwu responded to Han Qiaosheng on Weibo: You are up in the sky, you can't imagine the people at the grassroots level.

by edivista on 2012-01-04 08:58:41

Recently, well-known host Han Qiaosheng once again talked about acrobat champion Zhang Shangwu in an interview with the media. He warned Zhang Shangwu that one cannot do as one pleases in society and should be self-disciplined, learning to respect others. Subsequently, Zhang Shangwu responded to Han Qiaosheng on Weibo, stating that the high-and-mighty Han Qiaosheng could not empathize and did not think of grassroots people like himself. (Click the image to enter Zhang Shangwu's Weibo.)

Han Qiaosheng's advice:

One cannot do as one pleases in society.

Former world university games gymnastics champion Zhang Shangwu ended up destitute, performing on the streets for a living. Recently, Han Qiaosheng, when discussing this incident, stated that although he had no direct contact with Zhang Shangwu, he had interacted with many people who had worked with Zhang Shangwu. If one or two people said there were shortcomings in Zhang Shangwu, it would be understandable, but too many people told him too much, including Chen Guangbiao.

In Han Qiaosheng's eyes, Zhang Shangwu constantly lamented how society had abandoned him and how he suffered. He believed Zhang Shangwu should first reflect on himself and admit his mistakes. Honors already belong to yesterday; Zhang Shangwu should be self-disciplined and learn how to respect others.

Han Qiaosheng advised that if you want others to treat you equally, you must also treat others equally. You cannot place yourself above others. If everyone in this society only thinks of themselves and not others, chaos will ensue. One cannot do as one pleases in society.

Zhang Shangwu's response:

You are high up and cannot think of grassroots people.

After learning about Han Qiaosheng's words, Zhang Shangwu quickly responded, believing that the famous host Han Qiaosheng could not put himself in others' shoes and understand his situation. First, I really like Teacher Han. I grew up watching your programs since I was very young. I would like to make a few points. I have never said that society has been unfair to me. It is I who have wronged society. I should not have committed crimes back then. That was my mistake, and I sincerely admit it.

Zhang Shangwu thanked those who cared about him while refuting Han Qiaosheng: I would like to ask, do you know why society has shown me infinite understanding? Because the people who care about me come from the grassroots. They know that life is tough and bitter for grassroots people. Work is hard, but the rewards are too little. This is a problem of putting oneself in another's position. I saw your photo on China News Service, and you are much fatter than 20 years ago. I would like to ask you, when you are in the studio, do you ever think of grassroots people? You are high up, so of course, you do not think of them.

Support for Zhang Shangwu from All-around Games Gymnastics Champion Sun Xuguang:

Struggled after retirement, truly hard

After seeing Han Qiaosheng's advice, Sun Xuguang, the gymnast who won the first gold medal for Qingdao at the National Games, also expressed support for Zhang Shangwu via Weibo. Similar to Zhang Shangwu, Sun Xuguang had no job after retiring and had to sell goods on the street to support himself.

In his Weibo post, Sun Xuguang wrote: Hello Teacher Han Qiaosheng, I saw your comments about Zhang Shangwu. I don't know how to say this, but after retiring, I received 130,000 yuan. The rule was that I couldn't work for public institutions for five years. Can 130,000 yuan buy my youth? After 22 years in gymnastics, I am full of injuries. With four years of teaching experience and a bachelor's degree, all I wanted was to be a coach. But I can't. Since retiring in 2006, I have been working odd jobs until now. Truly, you are high up and do not know the hardships below.
