Chongqing Bi Zhuyuan Textile Fiber Co., Ltd. is located in Shanghai. It is a comprehensive enterprise integrating product research and development, production, marketing, and product recruitment. The company combines independent innovation with the introduction of advanced foreign technology, using advanced technology to transform traditional industries. It mainly produces and markets bamboo fiber and ultra-fine fiber series products in the domestic market, with a very large market demand.

Since its establishment, the company has been committed to deeply exploring the market, closely combining unique concepts with the popular elements of international brand market fashion. It mainly produces bamboo fiber and ultra-fine fiber series products. Using high technology and selecting high-quality materials for production, it undergoes high-temperature sugar removal and degreasing. The whole process utilizes biodegradable, natural treatment methods, which are green and environmentally friendly. The bamboo fiber series products produced have antibacterial and bacteriostatic functions, as well as oil-repellent, stain-resistant, easy-to-wash, and quick-drying characteristics. They can be cleaned without detergents, just by rinsing with clear water. Washing your face doesn't require facial cleanser, and washing dishes saves detergent. Long-term use will not cause slipping, greasiness, hardening, odor, or static electricity, changing the traditional towel's disadvantages of being easily dirty, difficult to wash, and having an odor. Moreover, bamboo fiber series products have a delicate appearance, softness, and comfortable hand feel. Products are generally single-colored but can feature embroidered patterns. Due to bamboo's natural beauty-enhancing effects, it has high moisture absorption and quick-drying properties, excellent breathability, drapability, elasticity recovery, wear resistance, and dyeability; it has a bright luster, cool and smooth hand feel like silk; it resists ultraviolet rays; it prevents mold and pests, providing good natural antibacterial and bacteriostatic effects, catering to the modern trend of pursuing health and comfort. For example, bamboo fiber beauty towels are suitable for women's beauty use and represent a new concept of beauty products among towels. It is very suitable for individuals and companies joining to increase new product sales.
