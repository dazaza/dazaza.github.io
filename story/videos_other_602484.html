For a Samsung fully automatic washing machine, what are the components of the transmission system? Beijing Samsung washing machine after-sales service telephone @ provides information. The transmission system of a fully automatic washing machine consists of a motor, clutch, V-belt, and capacitor.

1) **Motor**: The motor is one of the critical components of the washing machine. During the wash cycle, the motor operates under the control of the program controller, producing a short-term forward rotation - stop - reverse rotation motion. During the spin cycle, the motor's pulley on the motor side and the pulley on the clutch side reduce the speed, driving the spin axis in the clutch to rotate at high speed in a single direction.

2) **Clutch**: The clutch is a key component of drum-type fully automatic washing machines. Its main function is to achieve low-speed rotation during the wash and rinse cycles and high-speed rotation during the spin cycle, as well as perform braking actions when the spin cycle ends. Currently, large-drum new water flow fully automatic washing machines typically use a减速clutch (reduction clutch). The reduction clutch mainly consists of the drum shaft, spin shaft, torsion spring, planetary reducer, brake band, fork, clutch rod, ratchet wheel, pawl, holding spring, clutch sleeve, outer sleeve shaft, and gear shaft. The actions of the reduction clutch are controlled by the drainage electromagnet and have two states: wash and spin. During the wash cycle, the clutch reduces the speed, causing the drum to intermittently rotate forward and backward while the wash/spin tub does not rotate. During the spin cycle, the clutch drives the wash/spin tub to rotate clockwise (viewed from above the washing machine, the same applies below), performing the spin action, with the drum also rotating together with the wash/spin tub.

3) **Capacitor**: Washing machines use single-phase asynchronous capacitor-run motors, and the capacitor is an important part of it. The capacitors used in single-phase asynchronous capacitor-run motors are usually metallized paper dielectric or polypropylene film dielectric capacitors, with a capacity of 12-15μF and a voltage rating of over 400V (AC). The shape can be cylindrical or rectangular. Beijing Samsung washing machine repair telephone.
