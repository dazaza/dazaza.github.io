Cooperation Agreement for Hotel Expansion and Construction

by chandadada on 2012-02-12 17:03:16

Our company, based on its strategic thinking, has successfully completed in-depth communication and investigation with the leadership of your hotel regarding the construction of an expansion base. We hope to continue our in-depth cooperation with your organization. Meanwhile, the leadership of our company attaches great importance to our bilateral cooperation and, concerning the development direction of the contracted project, Beijing Expansion Training Company hereby puts forward the following suggestions and hopes that your company will provide valuable feedback.

We summarize the advantages of our bilateral cooperation as follows:

1. The hotel is a four-star garden-style hotel located within the Ying Mountain Forest Park, covering more than 100 mu (approx. 16.5 acres). Its advantages in terms of land reserve and financial operations are self-evident. Since entering 2009, under the impetus of diversified projects, the hotel brand hopes to introduce expansion training facilities to adapt to the ever-changing market competition.

2. Based on the judgment of market development trends and the planning of the base construction, it will inevitably enhance the market competitiveness of Haotewan Hotel and achieve the goal of diversifying service projects.

3. The foundation of our cooperation lies in complementary strengths. There is no direct conflict in corporate interests but rather a relationship of interdependence. As a service-oriented company, we have always adhered to improving our business level and strengthening our service awareness during our enterprise development process, contributing with a more solid professional standard and practical accumulation to the expansion training cause.

General Analysis:

From its inception to standardization and then maturation, the expansion training market took nearly 15 years. With the reduction of urban land reserves and the maturity of consumer ideologies, making accurate market positioning and analysis in the early stages of expansion project development plays a crucial role in the accuracy of investors' investments and the speed of capital recovery.

The similarity between expansion training and other commodities is: they need to cater to the market and be marketable to meet effective demand and achieve maximum profit. As a special commodity, its characteristics are expressed as follows:

1. Capital-intensive: From initiation to completion, the expansion training facility project requires hundreds of thousands of funds. Capital intensity becomes a characteristic of the expansion base, which also increases investment risks.

2. Short investment cycle: The investment cycle for an expansion base is generally two months, i.e., the base construction time and testing/trial period. Extending the investment time is for the maintenance and update of the facilities, which requires investors to have a longer-term perspective.

3. Market information change prediction: Due to the maturity of investment projects, the expansion training market stabilizes, so short-term investment can produce long-term benefits without causing the training facilities to become obsolete.

Because expansion training differs from other industries, many factors restrict the realization of the final product and return on investment. Therefore, the conditions of the project itself and the developers can provide certain feasibility references for timely investment decisions by investors.

Project Site Conditions:

Consumer Analysis:

Consumers of expansion training are generally enterprises, institutions, government agencies, schools, etc. Each participation ranges from dozens to hundreds of people. Private companies usually take advantage of weekends for 1-3 days of training, while public institutions and government units usually conduct activity training on weekdays. Thus, the weekly usage rate of expansion training facilities is very high.

Placement Position Analysis:

Based on consumer use and training practice experience, it is suggested that expansion training facilities be installed independently, with at least a 25-meter distance between each facility without affecting mutual use. It is recommended to install and construct within 200 meters of the entrance to Ying Mountain Forest Park (hotel entrance). Reasons: 1) This area has a large open space, reducing tree felling rates; 2) The expansion area is close to the hotel, facilitating meals and accommodation needs for trainees; 3) There are relatively fewer low trees here, making it convenient for the installation and use of high-altitude facilities.

Facility Safety:

All expansion training facilities are high-risk outdoor sports facilities. Project participants must be led and guided by professional trainers to use them. To avoid injuries caused by unauthorized climbers, all training facilities installed by our company are equipped with anti-climbing devices. The functions of anti-climbing devices are: 1) Prevent unauthorized climbing that could cause danger; 2) Prevent unauthorized use by expansion institutions without your hotel's permission.
