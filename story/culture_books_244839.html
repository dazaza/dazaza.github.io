This is a love story that makes even the words cry. The queen of tragic romance, Fei Wo Si Cun, passionately narrates this tale of life and death, interpreting in the most heart-wrenching way: love means being faithful until death...

She was already engaged when she accidentally saved a handsome and powerful nobleman. Originally thinking it was just a fleeting encounter, she never expected that he would dare to take great risks and snatch her away at the wedding. However, their story did not end up like the fairy tale where the prince and Cinderella live happily ever after in the castle... How many times in one's life does one fall in love with someone? The greatest tragedy in love is when the words "I love you" haven't even been said, yet the person you love has already disappeared into the crowd. This tumultuous tale of life and death, told by the queen of tragic romance Fei Wo Si Cun, uses beautiful but sorrowful language to express the hidden pain of love, leaving an unforgettable mark on people's hearts.
