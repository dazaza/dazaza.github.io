Chen Kun spent 300,000 yuan, and Zhao Wei went for a C-section to give birth to a baby girl (pic)

by edhardyuk on 2010-04-15 21:45:10

In the early hours of yesterday morning, a netizen named "Xuan Xuan Niu Niu" revealed on a Singaporean lifestyle and consumption forum that Zhao Wei had become a mother. However, Zhao Wei's agent adopted an "unwilling to answer" attitude. The netizen claimed to be a long-term resident of Singapore who often went to a famous hospital near the Botanical Gardens on Orchard Road for check-ups after becoming pregnant. On April 13th, while chatting with a nurse, she learned that Zhao Wei had given birth to a daughter via cesarean section two days earlier (April 11th) at this hospital.

The netizen "Xuan Xuan Niu Niu" also said: "I don't know if revealing this will affect her life, but I'm really happy for her. It's said that her husband accompanied her throughout the process, showing how good their relationship is. I'm so happy. I wish her and her baby all the best. There have been too many things affecting her mood recently."

Costing at least hundreds of thousands

The netizen also revealed that this hospital is a private one, which can be considered quite expensive. She spent over 3000 Singapore dollars (approximately 15,000 RMB) on an abortion here, whereas ordinary hospitals in Singapore only cost around 600-700 Singapore dollars. Cesarean sections at this hospital usually require a hospital stay of 2-3 days, so if everything is true, Zhao Wei should be discharged these past few days.

It was learned by the reporter that Orchard Road is located in the center of Singapore, and this hospital is surrounded by dining and shopping areas, making it one of Singapore's famous attractions. Besides its superior environment, the hospital's interior is decorated like a hotel, aiming to make patients feel as comfortable as they would at home. There are single rooms, double rooms, and four-bed rooms available. Each room is equipped with basic facilities such as a TV, air conditioning, table, sofa, bathroom, refrigerator, vanity, and cabinets. Additionally, there are CEO-exclusive rooms and VIP rooms. Compared to other celebrities like Hu Jing giving birth in luxury, Zhao Wei's childbirth expenses were also quite high. According to estimates by nurses at Singaporean private hospitals, giving birth at this hospital costs at least several hundred thousand yuan.
