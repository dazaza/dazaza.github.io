Plot Introduction of the Movie "Changjiang 7"

Stephen Chow plays a down-on-his-luck father in the movie, living with his son in an old house by the side of a major road in Ningbo. In order to provide his son with a better life, Chow is forced to work at a construction site, but unfortunately encounters the tragic treatment of wage arrears for migrant workers - not only does he not receive his salary, but he also gets chased and beaten by the foreman for "causing trouble". Despite his pitiful state, Father Zhou can't afford toys worth hundreds of yuan for his son, yet he can't bear to give up. By chance, he finds something strange in a garbage pile.
