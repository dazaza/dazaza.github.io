Steel-wood doors need to enter a virtuous track of sustainable development.

by dknfkkgfed1 on 2012-02-07 08:55:51

Steel-wood doors stepping into a virtuous track of sustainable development - Building Materials Knowledge - Industry News - Building Materials Network. Only sunset categories, no sunset industries.

Where there are houses, there will be doors; the door industry will always be a sunrise industry. With the deepening of national reform and opening up, especially in the past decade of rapid economic development, consumers have significantly improved in terms of awareness and aesthetic perspective, with environmental protection and health concepts deeply rooted in people's hearts. The requirements for the door industry will become increasingly higher, and the choices for door categories will become more extensive. Since its birth, steel-wood doors have secured their place in the door industry through stable quality and moderate pricing, developing positively. However, some inherent problems also constrain the development of the steel-wood door industry. After the establishment of the Steel-Wood Door Special Committee as an industry organization, it ended the previous scattered state of the steel-wood door industry where each company fought alone. The author believes that the establishment of an industry organization merely announces that steel-wood doors now have their own organization. How to solve the adverse issues within the steel-wood door industry? How to guide the steel-wood door industry towards healthy development? How to unite the entire national steel-wood door industry closely, make joint efforts at critical moments, and enhance the overall image of steel-wood door brands among consumers are problems that the steel-wood door industry needs to face, which is also a challenging task. It is believed that the steel-wood door industry will surely overcome various difficulties and achieve further progress.

1. Exerting the guidance of industry organizations, fully promoting steel-wood door standards

Since Chongqing Meixin first developed steel-wood doors, they have released enormous market potential, quickly occupying vast rural markets, putting significant pressure on previous paint-free wooden doors. Currently, after being constantly tested by the market, the steel-wood door industry has formed three main steel-wood door production bases in Guangdong, Zhejiang, and Sichuan provinces. It has also created leading steel-wood door brands represented by Longshu, Jinkai De, Jinyajv, and Ai'er Mei. While the overall situation keeps improving, thousands of steel-wood door enterprises across the country have emerged like mushrooms after rain. Many small enterprises and newly established companies lack basic professional ethics, substituting inferior products, cutting corners, and disrupting the market with low prices, although these are individual phenomena, causing adverse effects on the overall image of steel-wood doors. The main reason for this situation is the lack of industry supervision mechanisms and too low entry barriers. Among the more than one thousand steel-wood door companies nationwide, they are relatively scattered and are basically small and medium-sized enterprises, with no unified standards in the past, causing such conditions. Now, the Steel-Wood Door Special Committee as an authoritative industry organization has been successfully established and is running smoothly, with steel-wood door standards smoothly introduced. Adhering to the promotion of steel-wood door industry standards can help resolve these situations.

2. Establishing correct brand awareness, avoiding price wars, and pursuing a brand-oriented path.

Some people think that building a brand is an investment that increases costs without immediately visible benefits. They prefer to save money, believing that savings are all theirs. However, Mr. Deng Shusheng, Chairman of Guangzhou Longshu Door Industry Co., Ltd., believes that building a brand is an investment, an intangible asset that brings substantial returns. Some enterprises believe that placing advertisements on TV transforms them into brand enterprises overnight, but this is a misconception. As the President unit of the National Steel-Wood Door Special Committee, Longshu has always adhered to quality and service as its foundation, continuously following the brand-oriented route. Each year, millions are spent on brand promotion, event planning, and advertising campaigns, almost equal to the annual turnover of a small to medium-sized steel-wood door enterprise. In comparison, Longshu products have much higher added value. With the continuous rise in raw material prices, the steel-wood door industry has entered an era of slim profits. Since 2007, the steel-wood door market has gradually been accepted, with more enterprises participating, resulting in intense competition. Since the second half of 2008, the growth rate and number of enterprises have far exceeded the increments of previous years, and since the New Year of 2009, market competition has become fierce. To cut costs and engage in price wars, previously uncommon practices of cutting corners have started to appear. Pursuing a brand strategy means avoiding price wars, which ultimately lead to the regression of steel-wood doors. Therefore, practitioners in the steel-wood door industry should not treat steel-wood doors as just a business but as a cause. Following a brand-oriented path ensures the longevity of steel-wood doors.

3. Driving product innovation upgrades with quality and brands.

Most steel-wood door products rely on quality for survival, with the biggest challenge being product research and development. According to reports, among the thousand steel-wood door enterprises nationwide, apart from a few pillar enterprises, almost none have their own R&D teams. Due to the high cost of about five ten-thousand yuan for forming a patterned steel plate mold, many enterprises are either unwilling or unable to make such investments, relying instead on copying others' designs. This leads to the homogenization of steel-wood door products, making timely innovation and new product releases nearly impossible.

The reason why wooden doors remain popular is due to their originality, which endows products with unique artistic and creative qualities. The steel-wood door industry lacks precisely this aspect. The steel-wood door industry should carefully consider innovation and explore an innovative path suitable for its own development. At the same time, through technical innovation and research, develop mid-to-high-end products based on the positioning of mid-range products to meet the multi-level needs of consumers and occupy the largest possible market share.

4. Transitioning services from focusing solely on distributors to balancing both distributors and consumers.

At this stage, the services provided by steel-wood door enterprises are only at the level of serving distributors, narrowly interpreting market orientation as revolving around distributors. The success of product promotion locally and the hope for sales volume depend entirely on the personal ability and initiative of distributors. Developing and maintaining a distributor results in satisfaction when sales are good, and dissatisfaction when sales are poor, leaving little choice but to develop another one. This places the enterprise in a passive situation, relying on orders akin to depending on the weather. Of course, the author does not deny the importance of maintaining distributors; manufacturers and dealers are always a community of shared interests. As consumer awareness and living standards improve, what they require goes beyond just a product; more importantly, it’s humanized service. For example, when buying an air conditioner at Suning Appliances (check map), there will be dedicated personnel calling back after purchase, installation, and later stages, allowing you to feel Suning's meticulous service through simple conversations. Because of the good service, we will definitely choose Suning next time we buy appliances, and if friends want to buy appliances, we would recommend them to buy from places with good service. This is a simple chain reaction, yet an infinite business opportunity. Not only do we want consumers to buy our doors, but we also want consumers to introduce their friends to buy our doors for sustainable development.

A large consumer base is the soil for local brand operations, and consumer recognition determines whether the product has good sales locally. Therefore, establishing a platform for communication with end consumers and enhancing the brand's good reputation among consumers is particularly important. Longshu Doors has always been known for its excellent service. During its continuous development, it realized this point. On the basis of having market department specialists develop customers, market followers follow up new customers, the business sales department maintain customers, and the training group train customers, a consumer revisit service center was added to track the service of products in the consumer process. Every consumer who purchases Longshu products will receive a follow-up visit, with prompt solutions provided for any issues, enhancing consumer satisfaction and expecting them to become repeat consumers. Maintaining distributors and maintaining terminal customers are not contradictory but mutually reinforcing. Ultimately, it achieves a win-win situation for distributors, manufacturers, and brands.

5. Continue advancing into rural markets, then surround cities from rural areas.

The steel-wood door industry has always positioned itself as a mid-range product, catering to the needs of salaried classes, taking a scaled path, thus finding it difficult to capture the high-end market, focusing instead on secondary cities and county-level markets. With the gradual advancement of the national urban-rural integration strategy and the increasing purchasing power of farmers, rural areas will still be the largest market in the coming years. Since 2008, when Longshu proposed the slogan of advancing into rural markets, many steel-wood door enterprises followed suit. Nowadays, many Longshu township specialty stores have opened and are operating well. The author's hometown is in a plain rural area, where many family-style small door factories have appeared in recent years, generally producing baked enamel doors. These small factory products are not cheap, with the price of a standard indoor door, excluding the sales环节, reaching around 750 yuan, yet the business is exceptionally good, especially during the house decoration season in the second half of the year, where supply cannot meet demand. Therefore, there is no need to doubt the purchasing power of rural areas, and the steel-wood door industry must adhere to the rural market in its current phase.

After occupying and consolidating second and third-tier markets and townships, the brand effect can be widely promoted and enhanced, gaining recognition from grassroots people. Simultaneously developing mid-to-high-end products, surrounding cities from rural areas, advancing to high-end cities, and capturing the high-end market.

6. Enhancing the influence and vitality of industry organizations, elevating the overall image of steel-wood doors.

In 2009, the All-China Federation of Industry and Commerce established the Steel-Wood Door Special Committee, a grand event for the steel-wood door industry. Strengthening inter-enterprise connections, integrating steel-wood door advantageous resources, establishing industry standards, and enhancing self-discipline within the industry will play an increasingly important role in the future development of the steel-wood door industry. We look forward to more exchanges, references, activities, and timely joint actions under the macro leadership of the Federation of Industry and Commerce, using collective strength to build the overall brand image of the steel-wood door industry.

The author believes that the previous steel-wood door industry was like a "guerrilla force," powerful but relatively scattered, while now it is in the transition phase towards a group army. The increasing maturity of the steel-wood door industry, the establishment and operation of industry organizations, the formulation and implementation of industry standards, and favorable national macro policies provide a good soft environment for industry development. The demand for steel-wood doors in the market will significantly increase in the next three to five years, utilizing the characteristics of the product being environmentally friendly and reasonably priced, which will inevitably lead to the transformation of steel-wood doors towards scientific and scaled development, achieving sustainable development. We believe that the future of the steel-wood door industry is bright.
