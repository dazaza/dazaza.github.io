Guangdong has approximately 9.7 million WAP users, accounting for one-fourth of the nation's WAP users. Beijing and Shanghai have 1.7 million and 1.3 million WAP users respectively. The geographic distribution of WAP users is concentrated in relatively economically developed areas in the eastern part of China.

Gender Distribution

Among WAP users, males account for 80%, while females only account for 20%. In contrast, among traditional internet users, the male proportion is 58.3% and female is 41.7%, indicating that the proportion of male WAP users is higher.

Gender distribution of WAP users

Age Distribution

Among WAP users, those aged 18-24 years old account for more than half, at 51.6%, and those aged 25-30 years old exceed one-fourth, at 27.8%. The proportion of those aged 31-40 is 13.1%, and those over 40 years old is only 2.2%. Notably, "post-80s" users account for 73.2% of all users. Compared to traditional internet users, WAP users are younger and more concentrated in the 18-30 age group.

Age distribution of WAP users

Educational Attainment Distribution

Among WAP users, the highest proportion (35.8%) has a high school education, followed by university undergraduates and junior college graduates at 27.1% and 21.7%, respectively.

Figure 7: Educational attainment distribution of WAP users

Among WAP student users, more than half have a university undergraduate degree, whereas among non-student users, the highest proportion (39.9%) has a high school education. Overall, the educational level of WAP users is concentrated between high school and university undergraduate degrees.

Figure 8: Educational attainment distribution of student and non-student WAP users

Occupation Distribution

Among WAP users, students account for more than 20%, employees of enterprises and public institutions account for about one-fourth, managers of enterprises and public institutions account for 10.8%, and professional technicians account for 16.2%. Surprisingly, migrant workers, who were originally believed to be a significant user group, account for less than 2%. Based on comprehensive judgment, the two groups where WAP users are relatively concentrated are white-collar workers and students.

Occupation distribution of WAP users

Personal Monthly Income Distribution

Among WAP users, the proportion with a personal monthly income between 1,000-2,000 yuan is the highest, reaching 29.5%, followed by those with incomes between 2,000-3,000 yuan at 16.2%. The no-income group accounts for 21.1%, due to the relatively high proportion of student users. Among WAP users, the proportion with a monthly income below 3,000 yuan reaches 78.1%, while the proportion with an income above 3,000 yuan is only 21.9%.
