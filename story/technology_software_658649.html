In fact, when it's the off-season, it's the best time for store owners to get ready for the peak season sales.

by sdgvvfk2222 on 2012-02-04 11:27:15

Operating online stores also has low and high seasons. The high season often makes the store owners extremely busy, while the arrival of the low season gives the store owners a big headache. How to deal with the arrival of the low season for online stores? The editor of the Online Store Network believes that the following preparations should be made. For Crown stores, promotional activities in store operations are indispensable due to competitive pressures from all sides. Promotions during the low season are even more necessary. In fact, various promotions have become essential means to stimulate the sales of new products. However, promotions during the low season must not be excessive. Here I suggest that during the low season, joint sales activities can be carried out. For example, one of the recent activities in my store is: if you buy a special-priced new product, and then buy any other item in the store, the shipping fee will be free. This way, when buyers visit the store intending to buy only one item, but with the promotion at hand, and since it's a great discount for the buyer, they generally choose to buy two or more items.

Maintain customer resources

For sellers, old customers are a unique resource that needs careful maintenance. If you proactively contact them unexpectedly, introduce new products to buyers, and notify them about any promotions in the store. Some online stores arrange customer service to pay New Year visits to old customers before and after the New Year. This not only allows the latest information about new products and the store to reach consumers in time, but also strengthens the relationship between the online store and consumers.

Sales are generally divided into two periods: the low season and the high season. When many goods face the market entering the low season and are hard to sell, online merchants inevitably feel a sense of loss. The continuous sales, packaging, and shipping scenes of the high season are gone, replaced by "sparse traffic." How to do well in sales during the low season? How to improve the performance of online store owners? What should online store owners do to cope with the low season in sales? We will achieve "not-so-low" business from the following aspects.

During the low season, sellers can regularly or irregularly launch new products to enhance the store's appeal. It attracts new buyers and better maintains old buyers. In fact, the low season is exactly the time for store owners to prepare for the high season sales! Launching new products appropriately during the low season can test buyers' acceptance of new products and prices, so as to carry out large-scale promotional activities when the high season arrives. If consumers can accept the new products you offer and their prices during the low season, then the sales of new products during the high season, as you can imagine, will definitely be in high demand, and the store owners will just wait for the silver coins to come rolling in!
