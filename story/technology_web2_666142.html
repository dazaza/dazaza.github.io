China Telecom version of iPhone 4S to be released next week, timeline dominated by Apple

by 54704780 on 2012-02-15 14:34:21

Insiders in the telecom industry frankly told the Nandu Daily that, although China Telecom hoped to use star phones such as the iPhone 4S to attract high-end users, the timing of its introduction determined that China Telecom would not and could not rely solely on the iPhone —— after all, Unicom had already taken the first step.

Other sources revealed that China Telecom offered three package options of 389 yuan, 489 yuan, and 589 yuan for platinum card and gold/silver card users, and provided a tenfold monthly rental terminal subsidy policy. That is, if a user subscribes to the 389 yuan package, China Telecom will subsidize 3890 yuan for the phone purchase cost.

The website of the Telecommunication Equipment Certification Center under the Ministry of Industry and Information Technology showed that the model number of the China Telecom version of the iPhone 4S was A1387 —— the same as the US version. Guangdong Telecom insiders told the Nandu Daily that the China Telecom version of the iPhone 4S was the "authentic" US version of the iPhone 4S.

Currently, provincial companies of China Telecom have received group notifications to conduct final training and deployment in the south by the end of this month. Participants in the training were mainly responsible persons for the Tianyi terminal sales in various provinces and cities. The retail price and contract plans that users are most concerned about will also be determined at the training meeting at the end of the month.

The subsidy ratio will exceed 60%, and China Telecom may invest 2 billion yuan to compete with Unicom.

"The 2012 Terminal Development Dragon Plan focuses on the iPhone as the main phone but also includes other cost-effective Android smartphones." Telecom insiders said that according to the strategic deployment of China Telecom Group, the terminal strategy would primarily focus on mid-range smart phones priced between 700 yuan and 2000 yuan, promoting multiple operating systems, "the iPhone is just an added bonus."

"China Telecom envisions 2012 as having an 80 million CDMA terminal market, with the iPhone 4S coming to assist." Guotai Junan's latest research report pointed out that China Telecom has finally obtained the Apple magic weapon, but shared minimal experience. However, who are the target users of the iPhone 4S? How to sell differentiation? How to achieve added value, "this is a major test for China Telecom."

The China Telecom version of the iPhone 4S may be launched in March.

Nandu exclusively learned from internal sources within China Telecom that Apple will officially announce cooperation with China Telecom next week and release the China Telecom CDMA version of the iPhone 4S. Currently, local provincial companies of China Telecom are making the final preparations before the launch, including system integration for sales and channel distribution.

There are already several versions of the China Telecom version of the iPhone 4S fee packages circulating. The rumored promotional material of the China Telecom version of the iPhone 4S shows that its contract total price is 5399 yuan. This package has two-year and three-year plans, with the lowest package for the two-year plan being 49 yuan, and the lowest package for the three-year plan being 89 yuan, which is 481 yuan lower than the China Unicom iPhone 4S package plan. Meanwhile, signing up for a three-year contract with the 389 yuan package can get you the 16GB version of the iPhone 4S for free.

Apple CEO Tim Cook admitted during last month's telephone conference that the company underestimated the demand for the iPhone 4S in the Chinese market, "The demand for the iPhone 4S in the Chinese market exceeded our expectations," and stated that apart from China Unicom, Apple will seek more partners, "continue finding ways to expand this market."

Guangdong Telecom insiders told Nandu that when using the China Telecom version of the iPhone 4S locally, it will not support the WCDMA and GSM networks of Unicom and Mobile, meaning that Unicom and Mobile SIM cards cannot be used. However, when roaming abroad, it will prioritize CDMA networks; if there is no CDMA network, it can automatically roam to WCDMA or GSM networks without changing numbers.

Related Reports

Guangdong Telecom has clearly defined the composition of the iPhone 4S sales outlets: these include their own service halls, authorized social service halls, as well as channels like Suning and Gome —— given that Suning and Gome have already started selling the Unicom version of the iPhone, China Telecom will directly compete with Unicom in the social channels.

China Telecom's latest data show that in 2011, there were over 500 models of Tianyi 3G phones on sale, totaling 34 million units throughout the year, among which the annual sales of 3G smartphones exceeded 16.6 million units, increasing more than tenfold compared to 2010. Telecom expects that by 2014, the scale of China Telecom's Tianyi users will exceed 200 million households, with over 70% of new market users being 3G users.

The top priority is still adding flowers to brocade?

The China Telecom version of the iPhone 4S supports WCDMA and GSM outside the country.

China Telecom became the second domestic operator to partner with Apple. Telecom insiders told Nandu that Apple will officially announce its cooperation with China Telecom next week, "This time point is dominated by Apple, and the official launch time of the China Telecom version of the iPhone 4S may have to wait until the first week of March, even though we can't wait."

It was also learned that the subsidy ratio of the China Telecom version of the iPhone 4S will exceed 60%. It was widely rumored in the industry that China Telecom invested approximately 2 billion yuan as marketing expenses for the iPhone 4S.

After the official release of the China Telecom version of the iPhone 4S, there will be two domestic versions available in the market: the China Telecom version and the China Unicom version. Unlike the China Unicom version which blocks the CDMA network, the China Telecom version of the iPhone 4S supports CDMA, WCDMA, and GSM three network formats.

"Due to the confidentiality agreement between China Telecom and Apple, the package prices for the CDMA version of the iPhone 4S will only be announced later this month," an insider from China Telecom told Nandu reporters. The CDMA version of the iPhone 4S is the core model of the second phase of the 'Dragon Plan'. The 'Dragon Plan' adopts a strategy of 'unified package', 'unified subsidy', 'unified presentation', and 'unified promotion' nationwide, which means that the release and subsidy policies of the China Telecom version of the iPhone 4S are also unified nationwide, and provincial companies do not have the authority to formulate packages.

By the end of 2011, China Telecom's mobile business users exceeded 129 million. China Telecom set the development tone for 2012 as breaking through scale, especially in terms of mobile user scale. Chairman of China Telecom, Wang Xiaochu, expected that the total demand for CDMA terminals in 2012 would be 80 million units, among which 45 million would be smartphones.

A Guangdong Telecom manager revealed that China Telecom's 'Dragon Plan' began implementation at the end of last year. The first phase of the Dragon phones included six models such as Samsung I509 and Motorola XT882, with a subsidy ratio reaching 42% in the first phase, mainly in the form of package and call fee subsidies; while the subsidy ratio for the China Telecom version of the iPhone 4S will exceed 60%.
