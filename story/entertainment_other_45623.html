by gogole111 on 2008-01-28 22:26:15

This week, the most anticipated update on the PlayStation Network is the playable demo of the action game masterpiece Devil May Cry 4. The game offers two modes for players to choose from, allowing them to experience the fun of timed challenges and boss battles. Although the level itself is basically the same as the one provided at last year's Tokyo Game Show, since it is directly extracted from a section of the official version, the content is more substantial. The demo has a size of 640 MB and should not be missed by players eager to experience this game.

Additionally, the much-anticipated FPS game from Sega, The Club, also begins offering its demo for download. An alternative mini-game developed by the creators of PS3's XMB background interface and Earth simulation music effects, PixelJunk Monsters, finally meets the players.
