Tomb Sweeping Day Injects New Energy into the Market_8366-www.zp-nmg.com

by vwxyebmz on 2011-09-29 09:39:27

World Marketing Review [mkt.icxo.com]: On the eve of Qingming Festival, a large number of beverage brands launched new products to seize market share. In order to attract consumer attention, most brands carried out price-cutting promotional activities, which made the beverage counters in supermarkets more popular.

■ Orders for plain-colored flowers significantly increased

On March 31st, I saw on Minzu East Road, Sun Wen West Road, Baiyuan Road, and other streets in the urban area that many flower shops had put chrysanthemums and other plain-colored sacrificial flowers as their main products. Normally placed in less conspicuous positions, red roses, tulips, calla lilies, etc., have been replaced by chrysanthemums, lilies, and other plain-colored flowers. Some flower shop owners told reporters that recently, there have been many people ordering plain-colored flowers because citizens are worried that the prices of sacrificial flowers before Qingming might rise. However, the prices currently remain basically the same as before.

A female owner of a flower shop on Baiyuan Road said that for this Qingming Festival, she purchased more varieties and quantities of flowers. The number of citizens booking sacrificial chrysanthemums has also clearly increased these days. Just on March 30th alone, she received over ten orders, with most people picking up the flowers on Saturday, the day of Qingming Festival.

■ Fruits and pastries see strong sales

Fruits are indispensable items for Qingming sacrifices. As Qingming approaches, fruit consumption is experiencing a small peak. I observed in supermarkets and farmers' markets that the variety of fruits before Qingming is abundant, and sales have significantly increased in recent days. The most popular types among consumers are apples, bananas, oranges, and strawberries. Due to sufficient market preparation earlier, the current fruit prices are basically stable compared to before. For example, supermarket apples range from 3-5 yuan/500 grams depending on quality, bananas are 2 yuan/500 grams, and oranges are 3-3.5 yuan/500 grams.

I also noticed in the supermarket that cakes costing around dozens of yuan per box, which can be used as sacrificial "offerings," were prominently displayed on counters, with quite a few consumers making purchases. Supermarket promoters said that due to the financial crisis, some ordinary families cannot afford expensive sacrificial items like "golden pigs." Replacing them with various pastries is more economical and practical, convenient to carry, and can still express sentiment, so they are very popular among citizens.

■ Supermarkets heavily promote discount sales

I found during my interviews that all major merchants attach great importance to this "mini-golden week" and have launched various promotional activities to attract consumers. In the Yi Jia Yi supermarket, if consumers spend 68 yuan or more in one go between April 3rd and 5th, they can add 3 yuan to exchange for (originally priced at 9.8 yuan) a bottle of Hongxi Harmonious Oil, with a limit of three bottles per person. On April 4th, Qingming Day, if you spend 78 yuan or more in one go at Yi Jia Yi, you will receive a "Wife Cake Gift Voucher" from Caidie Xuan. You can redeem "Six Fresh Wife Cakes" at designated Caidie Xuan stores, with a limit of two vouchers per person until supplies run out.

The Zhongshan branch of CR Vanguard also has very affordable promotional activities. From April 4th to 6th, if you buy new clothing on the first floor of CR Vanguard, you get a 40 yuan discount for every 100 yuan spent, 80 yuan discount for every 200 yuan spent, with more discounts for larger purchases; if you spend 168 yuan or more, adding 1 yuan will give you a 1.8-liter bottle of Gaofuli vinegar dishwashing liquid, with two bottles given for spending 336 yuan, and more given for larger purchases.

Mr. Zheng, the manager of the Zhu Yuan Mall of Yi Jia Yi, told reporters that the revision of legal holidays has turned the traditional Qingming Festival into a rare "mini-golden week" in the off-season. This year, the concentrated spending during the "May Day Golden Week" has been diverted to Qingming, "May Day," and Dragon Boat festivals. The Qingming holiday will become a new consumption burst period. Around Qingming is precisely the time when summer beverage new products fully hit the market and outdoor tourism goods reach their sales peak. Merchants will make big moves during this holiday. Currently, dozens of beverage brands in supermarkets have launched many preferential promotional activities, with many cool beverages and mineral water originally priced at 1.2 yuan per box (bottle) now only costing 0.8 yuan per box (bottle).
