Low-price group buying stimulates movie consumption demand: "Low-price group buying of movie tickets is a win-win approach for film producers, cinemas, and users, as it can stimulate consumption. Therefore, we call on relevant authorities to rationally view group buying," said a related person in charge of WoWo Group.

Regarding whether this move by the Film Bureau will affect group-buying websites, the above-mentioned group-buying industry insider believes that it currently won't have an impact, but if this opinion is implemented, it will definitely affect the revenue of group-buying websites.

The related person in charge of WoWo Group expressed support and understanding for this draft opinion from the Film Bureau. However, he pointed out that group buying is actually a tool for movie marketing, which can attract a large number of users, especially those who don't usually watch movies, into cinemas. Although the price of group-buying movie tickets is relatively low, it's better than having empty seats during the off-season.

As one of the most popular projects on group-buying websites, movie tickets often have a group-buying price of around 30 yuan, with discounts reaching 30-40% off the original price. But according to the recent "Guidelines on Further Regulating the Ticket Management of the Movie Market" issued by the Film Bureau of the State Administration of Radio, Film, and Television, member tickets and group tickets should not be less than 70% of the cinema's listed price. This may mean that the good days of low-price group-buying movie tickets are gone for good.

In fact, many cinemas have gathered popularity due to group buying. According to Wang Huiwen, Vice President of Meituan, some cinemas that need to do market marketing and initially adopted a low-price strategy highly require this kind of group-buying method. "For example, last year, Beijing Yaolei International Cinema used group buying to gather popularity and impacted other cinemas," Wang Huiwen said.

Recently, the Film Bureau of the State Administration of Radio, Film, and Television proposed that the price of group-buying movie tickets should not be less than 70% off, which has drawn widespread attention from netizens and the industry. Although group-buying websites currently have mixed reactions to this consultation draft, they all call on relevant departments to rationally view the price of group-buying movie tickets.

Li Yunlong, PR Director of F Group Buying Network, pointed out that group buying is actually a "defective discount". It should bring added value to merchants rather than replace their existing value.

"For example, take Wanda Cinemas, which has long-term cooperation with us. We rarely promote group-buying products during peak seasons or prime time because the cinema itself doesn't lack customers at these times. Another example is Xia Pu Xia Pu; we rarely promote group-buying products in winter because they don't lack customers then, but in summer, Xia Pu would have a need for group buying," Li Yunlong said.

He also believed that after setting the price standard, this price would be fair to all group-buying websites, and could indirectly balance the price war between group-buying websites, which might not be a bad thing for the development of the industry.

Some personnel in the group-buying industry commented on this matter, stating that group-buying websites currently hold an observing attitude towards this issue, as it is still a consultation draft and hasn't been implemented yet. Sina Tech checked some group-buying websites today and found that movie tickets are still a hot group-buying item, capable of completing thousands of orders. According to data cited by the aforementioned group-buying personnel, last year, the number of people watching movies brought about by group buying accounted for 9% of the total.
