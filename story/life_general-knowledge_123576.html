Shijiazhuang Oriental Science and Technology Secondary Professional School

by xie11 on 2009-02-09 16:40:32

Integration of industry and education, work-study program, first-class training facilities in the province, simple, fast and accurate teaching model, fully closed quasi-military student management, perfect employment placement, and the lowest tuition fee in the province.

Shijiazhuang Oriental Science and Technology Secondary Vocational School is a full-time comprehensive secondary vocational school approved by the Shijiazhuang Education Bureau and registered with the Hebei Provincial Department of Education. The school is located at No. 29 Shihuo South Road, Shijiazhuang City, Hebei Province, at the intersection of Xinhua Road and West Second Ring Road, with superior geographical conditions, convenient transportation, and beautiful environment.

Our school has always adhered to the development philosophy of "emancipating mind, being pragmatic and innovative, and thinking hard for change", continuously carried out teaching and management reforms, firmly established the awareness of "market competition", oriented by the market, and based on the needs of enterprises, explored new ideas and methods for theoretical and practical teaching, accumulated rich teaching and management experience, and after employment, students have received general praise from employers.

First, with enterprise standards as guidance, it is equipped with first-class teaching facilities in the province.

Sufficient and advanced teaching experimental equipment is the foundation and guarantee for doing a good job in vocational education. Our school has cumulatively invested more than 10 million yuan, and built a numerical control training workshop and a general vehicle training workshop equipped with more than 50 numerical control vehicles, numerical control milling machines, ordinary vehicles, and ordinary milling machines. The school has also built supporting facilities such as communication electronics laboratories, music rooms, electronic reading rooms, grinding rooms, body shape rooms, and new teaching buildings.

Second, with employment positions as guidance, an enterprise-style teaching model has been created.

Establishing a teaching model mainly based on practice, during the teaching process, all students are managed as enterprise employees, through competitive job postings, students take on roles such as team leaders, line managers, and employees in their "work", and through regular evaluations and examination results, excellent team leaders and skilled technicians are selected among students. This effectively promotes students' enthusiasm and initiative to learn.

Students' "work" situation every day is monitored by quality indicators, which are statistically analyzed monthly, and wages are calculated based on the scores obtained each month. The wage composition includes: workload + performance evaluation + work discipline + appearance + safe operation + bonus, etc. This effectively motivates students' initiative to learn and competitive awareness, allowing them to better master the knowledge and skills they have learned.

Through enterprise-style teaching reform, students can gradually experience and adapt to enterprise management models while they are in school, meet the needs of employment positions, initially reach the enterprise's employment standards, and the goal of enterprise-style teaching is to gradually surpass enterprise employment standards.

Third, with enterprise demand as guidance, a fully enclosed quasi-military student management system has been created, and a daily standard for students and a practical manual for vocational quality training have been formulated.

Fully learning from enterprise employee management models, cultivating qualified talents that are popular with enterprises. Enterprises not only need skilled workers with strong technical skills but also pay more attention to employees' overall qualities, such as team spirit, obedience, execution ability, competitive awareness, etc. The cultivation and improvement of overall qualities require efforts and cooperation from all aspects of the school, and overall quality education runs through the entire learning process of students. To enhance the overall quality of our students, our school highlights two characteristics in management:

Characteristic Management One: Strict Quasi-Military Management

Our school adheres to the educational concept of "becoming a person first, then becoming talented", and hires retired military officers to specialize in quasi-military management of students. Starting from the daily life system, from dressing, behavior, manners, and tidying up the living quarters, we let students develop good living habits, cultivate students' excellent organizational discipline style, and make them quickly become disciplined talents with good military temperament.

Characteristic Management Two: Vocational Quality Education Management

In the learning process of students, vocational quality courses are offered, divided into theoretical teaching and extension practice, complementing each other and improving together. Vocational quality theoretical teaching mainly focuses on practical combat, transforming students' thoughts, and extension training consolidates teaching results through "personal experiences" of students, thus making these vocational quality requirements become students' conscious behaviors.

For vocational quality extension training, our school signs agreements with enterprises and sends batches of our current students to enterprise positions under the accompaniment of school leaders and teachers. After a period of quasi-employee life, students understand society and enterprises before truly interning and taking up positions, entering the state of formal enterprise employees in advance, laying a good foundation for adapting to employment positions.

Fourth, with employment as guidance, employment placement work has been properly completed.

With the continuous improvement of our students' qualities and employment channels, internship and employment bases for students have been widely established in the Pearl River Delta region, Beijing-Tianjin-Hebei-Shanxi area, and Jiaozhou Peninsula.

The employment direction of our school's CNC specialty mainly focuses on the automotive parts manufacturing industry. All student internship units are mechanical manufacturing enterprises listed in the world's top 500 companies. The professional alignment rate of student internships and employment reaches 90%, and the employment satisfaction rate of students exceeds 95%.

Fifth, thoughtful后勤service, pioneering the "free meal system" for current students in the province.

All current students in our school implement the "free meal system" for three meals a day. On one hand, it reduces the burden on students' parents, and on the other hand, the school formulates scientific menus according to the physical development needs of students, fully ensuring the nutritional needs for students' physical development and adaptation to intensive study, effectively avoiding the adverse consequences caused by students dining outside the school.

The school is equipped with complete service facilities such as student dormitory buildings, canteens, supermarkets, phone booths, and bathrooms. The logistics management department always adheres to the principle of "service", striving to provide proactive, thoughtful, considerate, and convenient services.

Sixth, only miscellaneous fees are charged. The source of tuition fees and accommodation fees for three years of students.

Our school is a three-year secondary vocational school. With a strong sense of social responsibility originating from society and giving back to society, relying on powerful educational capabilities and forward-looking educational concepts, we aim to save costs for students' parents to the maximum extent. That is, students coming to our school only need to pay miscellaneous fees for three years, and the tuition fees and accommodation fees for three years are paid through the integration of work and study, i.e., vocational quality training. The school has also specially established the "Hongzhi Class", where all or part of the miscellaneous fees are exempted for academically excellent and highly competent students from poor families. Our students enjoy a national scholarship of 1500 yuan per year provided by the government.

During the educational process, our school continuously summarizes experiences and lessons, creatively refining our school culture.

The refinement of "school culture" outlines our grand blueprint and direction for struggle. The practice of school culture is also the process of shaping our brand and further enhancing our influence.

Small victories depend on intelligence, great victories depend on virtue; small success depends on friends, great success depends on opponents. We wish to co-develop with all vocational education schools in Hebei, providing more and better learning environments and employment opportunities for young people with aspirations.
