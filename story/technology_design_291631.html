Shu Qi's Anti-aging Beauty and Skin Care Tips

by onebelles on 2009-12-04 10:12:50

Tip one: Love cleanliness, avoid nightlife

No one would deny that Shu Qi is sexy. Even when she cries, she can't erase the title of the sexiest actress in the history of the Golden Horse Awards. Facing the TV cameras, she doesn't hide her tears. When asked about her secret to maintaining good skin, she smiled and said, "I don't eat greasy or fried food, nor do I drink sweet or carbonated drinks. This is part of my daily skincare routine."

In today's world, age isn't necessarily a woman's secret, and Shu Qi, who is over 30, is an example. Proper care isn't something everyone can achieve, but Shu Qi does it well because off-screen, she is a charming woman who enjoys life and takes care of herself. Her secret is loving cleanliness and avoiding nightlife. However, this is easier said than done, which is why Shu Qi sighs, "Actually, I work really hard to maintain it!"

Tip two: Love natural beauty, apply masks frequently

In real life, Shu Qi prefers no makeup, with the basic requirement being natural beauty. She says, "When I go out, I dress casually (relaxed). I'll at most apply a little lipstick, blush, and eyeshadow, never heavy makeup. I try not to put too much on my face, letting my skin rest. So every day, I use one moisturizing mask, and if I'm working, I'll use two—one for cleaning and one for hydration. Because acting requires heavy makeup, it can make the skin very dry."

Tip three: Un-makeup is crucial

To keep the skin glowing long-term, thorough cleansing is essential, according to Shu Qi's skincare insights. She says, "When I was younger, sometimes after finishing work, I'd just sleep without removing makeup, but now I won't. If you don't remove your makeup, your skin can easily get worse. Therefore, proper care is more important. Adequate sleep is crucial, ideally eight hours, but at least five to six hours. You should also drink plenty of water and stick to exercising regularly. I like running, badminton, or spa treatments. On average, I visit the beauty salon for a facial treatment at least once every ten days. Sometimes before an early shift, I'll take time to run and sweat, which makes me feel more energetic and keeps my skin healthier."

Tip four: Spot removal is key

Regarding spot removal, Shu Qi has her own method. She says, "I will use professional spot-removal products. While consistently using these products, I ensure I drink enough water daily. Additionally, I drink more soup. Of course, if conditions allow, eating bird's nest can be very helpful. As for fruits, they are indispensable."

Tip five: Give yourself a vacation

Having experienced both the ordinary, tranquil life and the hectic, day-and-night inverted life of the entertainment industry, Shu Qi believes that besides focusing on personal care and dietary habits, knowing how to enjoy life is also important. Therefore, she often gives herself vacations and even makes a whimsical demand to her agent: "For every period of filming, I want an equal amount of vacation time." Her most recent break lasted nearly half a year.
