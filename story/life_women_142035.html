The requirement for DIY spot-removal masks is increasing.

by xiangwo on 2009-04-21 20:59:55

With the concept of "environmental protection, happiness" taking root in people's hearts, more and more people like to DIY their own spot-removing products. Even for skin care, many of them choose to research their own beauty and skin care methods. They make their own spot-removing products - spot-removing masks, using some natural raw materials to make these masks. This is indeed environmentally friendly. No matter how high-end beauty products are, they may still contain some chemical components. However, making your own spot-removing mask isn't that random. There are still many conventions about "spot-removing masks" that need your attention. If you neglect the spot-removing products, your face may not be "environmentally friendly". Let's take a look together at what you need to pay special attention to when making your own spot-removing masks.

a: Applying a spot-removing mask generally should not exceed 30 minutes.

Some ladies think the longer the mask is applied, the better. Some even apply it before bed and remove it the next morning. In fact, applying a mask generally should not exceed 30 minutes or until it is eight-tenths dry. Otherwise, an overly dry mask can harm the skin when removed, and an overly dry mask will also absorb moisture from the face!

b: The usage frequency of spot-removing masks cannot be too frequent.

Do you think if you do a mask every day, your skin will definitely be good? In fact, applying a mask too frequently will only overburden the skin. Doing it twice a week is enough.

c: Each application of the mask should not be too thick.

The thicker the mask, the more nutrients? Not really. Too many nutrients cannot be absorbed by the skin all at once. An overly thick spot-removing mask will instead make the skin unable to breathe. Oxygen-deprived skin won't be beautiful.

d: Be cautious in choosing mask types.

Facial skin, like body skin, can have allergic reactions to certain ingredients. It is even more sensitive than body skin. And the face is the window of one's image. Once there are scars left from allergies, it will have a significant impact on one's appearance. Therefore, if you have ever been allergic to a certain cosmetic or belong to an allergy-prone constitution, then you need to be more careful in choosing masks. What suits others may not suit you.

Testing method: You can first apply a small amount of mask on the inner side of your elbow. If there is no allergic reaction after 20 minutes, you can try applying it on your face.

e: After applying the mask, you need to apply skincare cream.

Some people think that since the mask has already provided nutrients to the skin, there is no need to apply skincare products after applying the mask. This is not true. Spot-removing masks mainly remove dead skin and supplement deep skin nutrition. Without locking in moisture on the surface of the skin, the nutrients are easily lost. Therefore, you still need to apply skincare products after using a mask.

f: Do not immediately apply makeup after using the mask.

Expert tip: Although homemade spot-removing masks are good, they don't always have good effects. It is suggested that women use homemade spot-removing masks along with spot-removing products for better results.
