It is reported that Nanling Campus of Jilin University is located in the urban area of Changchun. It is one of the six campuses of Jilin University, mainly consisting of engineering departments and a large number of male students.

Network screenshot

●The ragged senior brother who welcomes you on enrollment day may only look decent once per semester.

Journalists found that compared to online opinions like "the university goes too far," evaluations such as "simple, fast, straightforward, modern young people's wisdom" were more widespread. Meanwhile, some netizens questioned the authenticity of the banners.

As universities across the country reopened, students displayed various eye-catching banners to welcome new students. Following the popularity of Taobao-style welcoming banners, last Saturday and Sunday, the banner hung by Jilin University’s Student Broadcast Station at the Nanling campus, reading “Little devils, you have finally come,” went viral online, attracting countless forwards and immediately drawing attention from netizens. A relevant teacher at the school told reporters yesterday that the banner was hung by the student association without approval, and the joke went "too far." The school has issued a public reprimand but will not further punish the involved students.

Interestingly, corresponding to the curiosity of male university students towards new junior brothers and sisters reflected by the banner, a summary posted by a netizen titled "University Freshman Sister Must-Knows" has been widely forwarded on Weibo. It humorously guides freshmen girls on which senior brothers are "unreliable" and reminds them to "keep their eyes open."

According to the standards set in the "Must-Knows," the content of Jilin University's banner clearly exceeded the "specifications," making the students who made the banner obviously "not good senior brothers."

●Senior brothers from your hometown are the most dangerous;

●Senior brothers who actively approach you are not good senior brothers;

●The more senior brothers you know, the less respect you gain;

Currently, the school has publicly reprimanded the involved students, who have also written self-examinations, but the school will not impose further punishment. "The head of the broadcast station is a sophomore with insufficient work experience, and we don't want strict punishment to affect him," Teacher Wu said. The school would handle the matter leniently, and he himself bears responsibility for supervision.

●A senior brother's smile is a sign of mischief, and his ambiguity is the prelude to a plan;

In the picture, this red banner was hung between two small trees in the campus, addressing the younger sisters as "little devils, you have finally come," signed by "Jilin University Student Broadcast Station." The content of another banner was even more "outstanding."

The humorous version of the "University Freshman Sister Must-Knows" spread online

"The little devil" banner became popular on the Internet

Subsequently, the reporter contacted a relevant person in charge of the Youth League Committee, Teacher Wu. He confirmed that the banner was indeed present at the Nanling campus last Saturday, hung by broadcast station students without approval and removed the same day. The broadcast station belongs to one of the school's associations.

Reporters called Minister Shen of Jilin University's External Publicity Office yesterday, but she had no prior knowledge of the banner and said, "It's temporarily hard to make a judgment; I need to understand the situation." She introduced that the Nanling campus is the location of Jilin University's Engineering Department, mainly consisting of automobile engineering, machinery, materials, and other engineering departments, with a higher proportion of male students.

Morning Post reporter Guan Jian

After searching, the reporter found that the photo was initially uploaded to Renren Network by the school's students on August 27 during the enrollment day, but the photo was deleted yesterday. Several comments below expressed surprise: "Times have indeed changed." "I've seen university enrollments, but never like this. I'm afraid the younger sisters can't hold it together when they see it." The picture quickly spread widely on Weibo and forums, sparking heated discussions among netizens and going viral online. Some netizens enviously said, "Why didn't I see such a playful banner when I enrolled?"

"Indeed, it went too far." Teacher Wu said that if the banner contained trendy internet slang, it could be acceptable, but this joke was too excessive, "Moreover, it was released under the name of the broadcast station without the approval of the Youth League Committee." He said this violated school regulations.

Public reprimand has been given to the involved students

At this point, it is already the enrollment season. Hanging distinctive welcome banners in the campus is a "standard action" for students in various institutions. However, these two banners from Jilin University's Nanling campus stood out uniquely among the many boring banners, which was inevitable.
