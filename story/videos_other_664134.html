**Bidding for Procurement and Installation of Automation Control System Equipment at the Wastewater Treatment Plant**

1. **Bidding Conditions**

1.1 The bidding project for the Mianyuan River Urban Domestic Wastewater Treatment Plant in Deyang City has been approved for construction by the Deyang Development and Reform Commission under Document No. [2009]10. The project owner is Deyang Wastewater Treatment Plant. The funding sources include national post-disaster recovery reconstruction subsidy funds, local matching funds, corporate self-raising funds, and bank loans. The bidder is the Deyang Wastewater Treatment Plant. The project now meets the bidding conditions, and an open tender is being conducted for the procurement and installation of all automation control systems and instrumentation equipment within the plant.

1.2 This bidding project is a state-invested engineering construction project within the administrative region of Sichuan Province. It has been approved by the Deyang Development and Reform Commission (Approval Document No. [2009]290). The form of bidding organization is entrusted bidding. The selected bidding agency, with the project code on the designated selection website of the Provincial Development and Reform Commission (Code: 2009102206), is China National Machinery & Equipment Import & Export Corporation.

2. **Project Overview and Bidding Scope**

2.1 Project Location: Group Three, Baizhu Village, Babaijing Town, Jinyang District, Deyang City.

2.2 Construction Scale and Main Content: Procurement and installation of all automation control systems and instrumentation equipment within the plant.

2.3 Bidding Scope and Lot Division:

- Bidding Scope: Procurement and installation of automation control systems and instrumentation equipment for the Deyang Mianyuan River Urban Domestic Wastewater Treatment Plant (details as per drawings and "Bill of Quantities").

- Lot Division: Procurement and installation of automation control systems and instrumentation equipment.

3. **Bidder Qualification Requirements**

3.1 This bidding requires bidders to possess valid independent enterprise legal person status and must have a valid qualification of Grade II or above in mechanical and electrical installation engineering general contracting issued by the construction administrative department, as well as a valid qualification of Grade II or above in architectural intelligence engineering professional contracting. The bidder must have completed at least one similar project involving automation engineering for waterworks with a capacity of 100,000 tons/day or more or wastewater treatment plants with a capacity of 50,000 tons/day or more since January 2008. The bidder must also have the corresponding capabilities in terms of personnel, equipment, and financial resources.

3.2 This bidding accepts consortium bids.

4. **Obtaining Bidding Documents**

4.1 All interested bidders may obtain the bidding documents (including drawings, technical specifications, and bill of quantities) online from August 19, 2011, 9:00 AM to September 7, 2011, 11:59 PM by logging into the Deyang Public Resources Network (website: http://ggzy.deyang.gov.cn, contact number: 0838-2516762, fax number: 0838-2516762) using the enterprise key obtained during registration. Enterprises that have not completed online registration on the Deyang Public Resources Network should complete the registration process and obtain the enterprise key before obtaining the bidding documents according to the above requirements. This is the only way to obtain the bidding documents.

4.2 Bidding documents can be obtained free of charge on the Deyang Public Resources Network.

5. **Submission of Bid Documents**

5.1 The deadline for submission of bid documents (bid closing time) is 10:00 AM on September 8, 2011. The location is the Deyang Public Resources Trading Center (No. 19 Foshan Street, Deyang City, Sichuan Province).

5.2 Late submissions or those not delivered to the designated location will not be accepted by the bidder.
