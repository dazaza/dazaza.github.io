   Why do beauties only love rich men?

   Some of the ladies who participated in the matchmaking event were photographed by the photographer on-site. Since it was in front of a crowd, many girls still felt shy. Photo taken by Guo Ketang, a reporter from Information Times.

At the beginning of this year, Information Times reported on "Several millionaires each spending 20,888 yuan to attend a matchmaking party," and the millionaire's "beauty pageant"-style matchmaking method sparked widespread controversy in society. Recently, a new round of "matchmaking parties" has once again opened the preliminary selection process, attracting over 1,500 young women from Guangzhou and surrounding cities to sign up, among whom more than ten percent are female college students.

Men's "admission ticket" costs 36,800 yuan

According to the person in charge of a certain matrimonial dating website organizing the event, the basic requirement for male participants is to "prove that they are single millionaires with assets exceeding ten million." A single "admission ticket" has been raised to 36,800 yuan, while women need not pay a cent. However, there are strict restrictions on appearance, figure, and education level, and ultimately, only 40 outstanding beauties will have the chance to meet the men at a celebrity yacht party.

Yesterday noon, the "preliminary selection" event was held in a high-end office building on Dongfeng Middle Road. Starting at 1:00 PM, the girls who had already signed up began arriving one after another to fill out forms, take photos, and show their ID cards and original academic certificates to the staff for verification. The entire "preliminary selection" site appeared rather calm. Browsing through the preliminary selection data sheets, most of the applicants had bachelor's degrees or higher, with salaries around 5,000 yuan. Many graduated from prestigious universities such as Zhongshan University, Jinan University, and South China Normal University, and quite a few had good looks and figures. According to the organizer's statistics, more than ten percent of the applicants were female college students.

In the form submission and photo-taking phase, several staff members began "visually assessing" the beauty of the ladies. Only a very small number of exceptionally attractive girls were brought into a nearby small conference room by the staff to have face-to-face conversations with professional matrimonial advisors, while most of the girls were informed to "wait for notification" after submitting their forms and taking photos, then left the venue.

Female: Partner must have 5 million yuan

A 21-year-old female university student named Xiao Yu was one of the girls "fortunate" enough to enter the interview phase yesterday. During the conversation, she informed the matrimonial advisor that she came from a well-known university in Guangzhou, and her dating goal was very clear: successful men over 35 years old, with a net worth of over 5 million yuan, owning a house and car, and being divorced was fine as long as they had no children.

Zhou Xia, a matrimonial advisor participating in the interviews, told the reporter that although Xiao Yu's various conditions were quite excellent, she did not plan to arrange Xiao Yu to participate in the next round of matchmaking parties, because she felt that Xiao Yu's "life experience was insufficient, her understanding of marriage was shallow, and she did not quite meet the requirements of the men."

Questions & Responses

1 How do you prove they are millionaires?

"Do they really have 10 million yuan if their information says so?" According to Cheng Yongsheng, the director of the South China Activity Department of the website, the organizers will review the objective conditions of the men who have registered. Men must provide original copies of property certificates, stock options certificates, company capital registration certificates, driver's licenses, and other documents. The organizers claimed that the ten millionaires who had successfully "passed" had fixed assets ranging from 50 million yuan to over 100 million yuan. From the copies of the documents provided by these millionaires, some had nearly 15 million yuan in stock options, some owned villas worth nearly 30 million yuan, some drove Porsche Cayennes worth over 1 million yuan, and others had personal companies with registered capital close to 80 million yuan.

2 How do you prove the millionaires aren't just looking for fun?

"Is the millionaire matchmaking about seeking marriage or just having fun?" In response to this question, Cheng Yongsheng told the reporter that the millionaires who passed the asset review would also undergo a psychological review regarding honesty and matrimonial motivation. Professional psychological counselors would judge through telephone communication and face-to-face interviews whether the registered millionaires had strong, sincere, and positive desires for marriage and love, whether their motives and attitudes for marriage headhunting services were correct, and whether they had any major psychological disorders or mental illnesses. Additionally, at the time of registration, the organizers had already signed relevant agreements with the millionaires. If any complaints were received from female members, the organizers had the right to terminate the service.

3 Are there any successful examples of millionaire matchmaking?

At the beginning of this year, similar millionaire matchmaking preliminary selection activities were also held in Guangzhou. Did anyone successfully pair up? Or was it just hype by the organizers? Cheng Yongsheng stated that a total of 37 millionaires participated in the event earlier this year, along with nearly a hundred young women. According to the latest feedback information, currently, only one couple has registered their marriage, ten couples are still dating, and the remaining 26 millionaires have not found suitable partners. Among them, a university professor and a 31-year-old wealthy man will continue to participate in this matchmaking event. (Reported by Wu Xia, intern Wang Shuaishuai)
