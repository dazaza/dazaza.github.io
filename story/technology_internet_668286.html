"Elite soldiers and strong generals" - the new battleground for men's apparel to exert its force

by shenli470z on 2012-02-17 16:21:58

On February 7, Nine Wolves Men's Wear released an announcement about a stock option and restricted stock incentive plan totaling 13 million shares for 150 mid-to-senior level employees. With this, all three major listed Quanzhou business-casual men's wear brands have launched their own equity incentive plans. Last May, Septwolves completed the second revision of its equity incentive plan covering 18 recipients with a total of 2.65 million shares; on November 29 of the same year, Lillang also announced the granting of options over 1.13 million shares to eligible employees.

Previously, similar listed companies in the industry, such as Metersbonwe and Bosideng, also introduced equity incentive plans. This indicates that equity incentives have become a collective action within the men's wear sector of Taobao, aiming to maintain long-term stability internally while attracting more capable talents for even fiercer market battles.

The purpose of equity incentives is market competition

Compared to the scale of Septwolves and Lillang, this equity incentive plan by Nine Wolves not only has a larger volume but also covers a broader scope, including some mid-level cadres in the equity incentive program. Many industry insiders believe that Nine Wolves' high regard for talent also reflects a preemptive strategy to face increasingly fierce market competition.

The current Chinese men's wear, especially business men's wear, is characterized by low industry concentration and small gaps between leading brands. Compared to sports sectors, securities institutions generally position men's wear as a high-growth industry. Based on this judgment, various brands have rolled out ambitious market expansion plans. In this context, there is not only an urgent need for various talents, but stabilizing the existing management team is also a top priority.

"From this perspective, it is not difficult to understand why men's wear brands have recently successively introduced equity incentive plans," said Huang Yongsheng, Director of Wealth Management at Huatai Securities Quanzhou Branch. The collective introduction of equity incentive plans by business men's wear can be seen as a prelude and signal of increased market efforts, and is also one of the most effective ways. Additionally, most clothing enterprises are family businesses where other than the major shareholders, the shareholding of other management levels has always been low. Currently, these enterprises mainly rely on store expansion to maintain profit growth, but the turnover rate among managers in the clothing industry is relatively high. Therefore, incentivizing management and key business personnel becomes strategically important.

"The Golden Handcuffs" sometimes don't work

Equity incentives are often interpreted by the market as a "golden handcuff" given by the company to core backbone employees. The reporter found that equity incentives are not always attractive to some executives. During the period when many Quanzhou enterprises announced their equity incentive plans, some executives resigned for personal reasons and voluntarily gave up their equity. Some companies were even forced to adjust their incentive plans due to the departure of incentive targets.

In 2009, Septwolves launched an equity incentive plan, but because 15 executives collectively abandoned their rights, the plan was abruptly halted. In the following year and a half, Septwolves was forced to lower the exercise conditions twice for the same plan. Securities professionals pointed out that the previous Septwolves plan set too high a threshold, which had already affected the effectiveness of its equity incentives. Ni Zhongsen, Chairman of Hengrun International Capital Limited, believed that "setting too high a threshold makes the incentive plan ineffective, and for employees, it will lose interest." Relatively speaking, this time Nine Wolves adopted a combination of stock options and restricted stocks, with the restricted stock grant price at 10.34 yuan per share, close to half the current price, making the expected benefits obvious for executives; the stock option exercise price at 21.73 yuan is very close to the recent stock price. Perhaps learning from Septwolves' experience, the exercise conditions of Nine Wolves' incentive plan this time are not high, overall still easy to achieve, and highly attractive to employees.
