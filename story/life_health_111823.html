Arthritis treatment

by gaoxia on 2008-12-16 10:27:47

Arthritis is a common chronic disease, and the most common types are osteoarthritis and rheumatoid arthritis. It is estimated that there are currently over 100 million arthritis patients in China, and the number is still increasing.

Depending on the degree of disease progression, osteoarthritis treatment includes pharmacological treatment for arthritis, supportive treatment for arthritis, and surgical treatment for arthritis. Surgical treatment for arthritis can be a good treatment option for patients whose disease has progressed to the late stage. For patients in the early or middle stages, pharmacological treatment for arthritis is generally adopted, combined with other methods for supportive treatment. It is important to actively adopt correct and effective means to start arthritis treatment as early as possible.

Surgical Treatment for Arthritis: Currently, surgical treatments for arthritis include arthroscopic surgery, osteotomy, and artificial joint replacement.

Supportive Treatment for Arthritis: Supportive treatment for arthritis is a method used when the patient's joint disease is in the early or middle stage, and it is usually combined with pharmacological treatment. Supportive treatment for arthritis refers to what we commonly call physical therapy and exercise therapy, such as electrotherapy, massage, acupuncture, and a series of other methods. Although supportive treatment does not provide fundamental curative effects for osteoarthritis, it can alleviate local discomfort for patients and has sedative, relaxing, and analgesic effects.

Pharmacological Treatment for Arthritis: In general, when treating diseases, before adopting surgical treatment, pharmacological treatment is a relatively effective method chosen by both doctors and patients for arthritis treatment.

Patients with arthritis should pay attention to their diet because diet can play a positive role in the treatment of arthritis.
