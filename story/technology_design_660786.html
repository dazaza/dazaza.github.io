The draft of two local standards for ceramic production energy consumption in Fujian passes the preliminary review.

by fdjrkkgfed3 on 2012-02-08 10:57:00

Two draft local standards for ceramic production energy consumption in Fujian pass the preliminary review – Policy and Regulations - Industry News - Building Materials Network. The statement "Existing decorative art ceramics product manufacturers and new manufacturers (including new production lines) should achieve advanced energy consumption limits through energy-saving technology upgrades and strengthened energy management" was changed to "The advanced energy consumption limits of existing decorative art ceramics product manufacturers and new manufacturers (including new production lines) should comply with the relevant requirements."

On December 3, 2010, the Fujian Energy Standardization Technical Committee convened a standard review meeting in Fuzhou City. The meeting passed the preliminary review of the drafts of the Fujian local standards "Energy Consumption Limit per Unit Product for Daily-use Ceramics" and "Energy Consumption Limit per Unit Product for Decorative Art Ceramics."

Participants in the meeting included representatives from the Light Industry and Textiles Office of Fujian Provincial Economic and Trade Commission, the National Daily-use Ceramics Standardization Technical Committee, Fujian Institute of Standardization, Fujian Energy Conservation Supervision (Monitoring) Center, Fujian Ceramic Industry Association, Minqing County Ceramic Science Research Institute, Material Science College of Fuzhou University, Dehua County Standard Metrology Institute, Jiamai Group Corporation of Fujian Province, Fujian Guanfu Modern Homeware Co., Ltd., and Fujian Dehua Bide Ceramics Co., Ltd. The standard review expert group was led by Professor Yu Jianchang from the Material Science College of Fuzhou University. The expert group listened to the main drafting units' explanation of the standard compilation situation and carefully reviewed each clause of the standards, conducting a thorough discussion.

The experts fully affirmed each clause of the standards, proposed modifications, and unanimously passed the preliminary review. They agreed that the materials provided by the standard drafting units were complete, the compilation methods were scientific, and the data collection was detailed and representative. The meeting strictly followed the requirements of GBT1.1-2009 "Guidelines for Standardization Work" to improve the texts of the daily-use and art ceramics standards drafts. The meeting decided to modify related clauses in the daily-use and art ceramics standards drafts, changing "Existing daily-use ceramics product manufacturers and new manufacturers (including new production lines) should achieve advanced energy consumption limits through energy-saving technology upgrades and strengthened energy management" to "The advanced energy consumption limits of existing daily-use ceramics product manufacturers and new manufacturers (including new production lines) should comply with the relevant requirements"; similarly, "Existing decorative art ceramics product manufacturers and new manufacturers (including new production lines) should achieve advanced energy consumption limits through energy-saving technology upgrades and strengthened energy management" was changed to "The advanced energy consumption limits of existing decorative art ceramics product manufacturers and new manufacturers (including new production lines) should comply with the relevant requirements." (Relevant requirements refer to specific values specified in the standard clauses, which should be referenced according to the relevant standard texts.)

"The preparation of this standard conforms to the relevant provisions of the 'Fujian Local Standards Management Measures,' adapts to the development characteristics of the daily-use and art ceramics industries in our province, and its energy consumption limit indicators have advancement and applicability. The implementation of this standard will help promote the development of energy conservation and emission reduction work in the ceramics industry in our province," said Ye Shaofen, Secretary-General of the Fujian Ceramic Industry Association.

It is understood that in order to earnestly implement the 'Opinion of the Fujian Provincial People's Government on Further Strengthening Energy Conservation Work,' establish and improve the energy conservation standard system, and promote energy conservation work across all industries in Fujian Province, according to the 'Fujian Local Standards Management Measures,' the Fujian Quality and Technical Supervision Bureau approved 29 energy-related Fujian local standard formulation plans submitted by the Fujian Energy Standardization Technical Committee and the Fujian Metrology and Science Technology Research Institute this year. Among these, the Fujian Ceramic Industry Association is the main drafting unit, and the Dehua County Energy Metrology Institute is the participating drafting unit for the two standards "Energy Consumption Limit per Unit Product Standard for Daily-use Ceramics" and "Energy Consumption Limit per Unit Product Standard for Decorative Art Ceramics."
