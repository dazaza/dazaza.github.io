Main technical parameters of the aging room: 1.1 Temperature range: Room temperature +5℃--60℃ (Room temperature +5℃---80℃) 1.2 Temperature fluctuation: ±0.5℃; 1.3 Temperature deviation: Maximum accuracy ±3℃ (Can be designed according to customer requirements, under certain conditions, the highest can reach ±1℃) 1.4 Room size: As required; 1.5 Operation mode: Adjustable temperature, constant operation or program operation (Touchscreen program operation optional) 1.6 Installation power supply: AC~380V; 50 Hz; 1.7 Noise level: ≦75 decibels Aging room, constant temperature aging room, high-temperature aging room Chamber structure and material description: 2.1 Wall materials: The chamber body is made of double-sided color steel insulated panels (EPS panels, polyurethane panels or rock wool panels) assembled together. The panel thickness is selected based on both the needs of the aging room itself and customer requirements. Panel thickness options include 50mm, 60mm, 70mm, 100mm specifications, and the color steel plate thickness includes 0.326mm, 0.5mm specifications. The entire chamber uses a steel frame structure, fixed and supported by aluminum profiles, with aluminum profiles or stainless steel plates used for edging inside and outside the chamber. (Ground treatment is based on customer requirements), joints are sealed with glass glue, effectively ensuring the sealing and aesthetics of the room. (The combustion performance of the room material meets the B2 grade specified in the "Grading of Combustion Performance of Building Materials". 2.2 Doorway and window design: According to actual requirements; Aging room, constant temperature aging room, high-temperature aging room Air force constant temperature system; The air force pipeline system complies with the GB50243-2002 standard "Code for acceptance of construction quality of ventilation and air conditioning engineering", usually consisting of a circulation system, heating system, and overheating exhaust system. These systems, including the ductwork, are installed at the top of the aging room without occupying other spaces. The circulation system uses circulation fans and matching ducts for circulation, ensuring uniform temperature within the test area. The heating system uses electric heaters for heating, with the heater placed in the circulation duct on the top of the aging room, surrounded by fireproof insulation materials. PID temperature module control is adopted, when the ORT aging room reaches the required product temperature, it automatically adjusts the heater power according to the indoor temperature fluctuations, cooperating with the thermal insulation of the insulation panels, making the indoor temperature precisely stable at the set temperature value. Within the designated time, the room temperature can be increased to the set temperature. When the temperature rises to the set value, the heater stops heating. The heater has an overheating protection device (EGO). If the customer's own product generates heat, then the overheating exhaust system is used. For small heat generation, automatic negative pressure exhaust using electric louvers is employed, while for large heat generation, low-noise fan exhaust is used, with the speed controlled by a frequency converter. Circulation temperature control process: When the machine starts, the heater begins heating. When the temperature reaches the set value, the heater stops heating. As time goes by, the product area temperature gradually rises. When the temperature exceeds the upper limit, the exhaust system begins to operate, discharging overheated gas from the product area to the outdoors. The frequency converter controls the outdoor exhaust fan operation. When the temperature drops to the lower limit, the fan stops exhaust, and the exhaust system closes simultaneously. During the aging process of the products, the circulation system remains continuously circulating to ensure even temperature distribution. The whole system has features such as stable performance, precise control, small temperature fluctuations, high balance, and low noise. Noise treatment: Low-decibel high-quality circulation fans are used, ducts are wrapped with 3mm thick asbestos, which not only insulates but also reduces noise. Based on acoustic principles, all dynamic parts of the MAX-ORT aging room are treated with soft connections using canvas and springs, aiming to reduce noise to the lowest standard.
