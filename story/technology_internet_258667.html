People from the south rarely see snow, therefore they yearn for the scene of "thousands of miles of ice and snow". Especially in early winter, when people in the south just put on their coats, the mountains and rivers in the north are already covered with snow. Among these, the most charming is no doubt the snowy Changbai Mountain.

"Millenniums of accumulated snow and pine trees, rising to the first peak on earth", the snow scenery of Changbai Mountain has long been famous. It is listed as one of the "Three Great Snow Mountains in Asia" along with Mount Emei and Mount Fuji. It is also the birthplace of the Manchu ethnic group and the "Sacred Mountain" of the Korean ethnic group. Its towering height is the crown of domestic mountains, at 2749 meters, making it the highest mountain peak in the eastern part of the Eurasian continent. It stands like a pure and elegant fairy lady amidst the forest sea and snowy plains.

Looking at the snow is especially suitable for single women because the crystal clarity of the snow can highlight the fantasies that girls have about the future. Hokkaido in Japan has become the most romantic couples' vacation destination, 80% of which is due to the snow. In the heavy snowfall, the quiet mountain paths, how much more necessary it is to have a warm shoulder to walk alongside. Making a wish in this world full of white may actually come true! However, single women going to Changbai Mountain should preferably not go alone but travel with fellow hikers. Perhaps they will also encounter a pure white romance in this fairy-tale world!

The Hu sky flies snow in August. Changbai Mountain enters the snow season in October, and this year the snowfall came earlier. Going to see the snow in November allows you to experience the beautiful scenery of snowy Changbai Mountain while benefiting from lower costs due to it not being the peak tourist season. Fortunately, our country is vast, and with convenient transportation, you can fly from the warm south to the foot of snowy Changbai Mountain within a day, to personally experience that fairy-tale world of ice and snow.

Scenic Spot 1: Heaven Lake

Heaven Lake is located in the middle and northern part of the scenic area. Legend has it that this was once the bathing pool of the Queen Mother of the West. The altitude of Heaven Lake is around 1980 meters, its length is approximately 3.4 kilometers, the widest point is 1.5 kilometers, and the deepest point reaches 105 meters. The lake water is clear and greenish-blue, surrounded by dense spruce forests, with the snow-covered Bogda Peak behind. The lake scenery and mountain colors form the enchanting high-altitude scenery of Heaven Lake. In winter, this place becomes an excellent high-altitude skating rink.

Scenic Spot 2: Chengcha River

Chengcha River, also known as the Milky Way, flows out from the gap between Longmen Peak and Tianque Peak on the north side of Heaven Lake - the valve, which serves as the outlet of Heaven Lake. The water from Heaven Lake flows through the valve and passes through the Cowherd Crossing, forming a river valley running nearly north-south. The total length is 1250 meters, connecting Heaven Lake and the Changbai Waterfall, acting as the "white ribbon". Chengcha River is currently the shortest river in the world.

Activity Point 1: Skiing

The Changbai Mountain skiing site is located in the primeval forest of the Changbai Mountain Nature Reserve. The ski field ranges from 1640 meters to 1820 meters above sea level. The quality of the snow here is very similar to that in the northern regions of France and Italy. The natural conditions of the ski field are excellent, with beautiful scenery. The skiing period runs from late October each year to May of the following year. The average winter temperature is 13.7 degrees Celsius, making it currently the longest skiing period site in China.

Activity Point 2: Hot Spring Bathing

The Changbai Mountain hot spring cluster is a trace of volcanic activity in Changbai Mountain. Calculating from 1597 to 1702, there were three eruptions, and the residual heat remains till now. Rainwater seeps into the deep layers of the earth through cracks and infiltrates into the deeper crust where it gets heated gradually to form underground hot water. The groundwater under heavy pressure breaks free from the hard crust's constraints with a gentle body, undergoes a long and arduous journey through rock fissures, singing a gurgling tune, trickling out under the blue sky and white sun, beside lush grass and dense forests. It brings bubble-like gas bubbles, spreading boiling hot steam. The water temperature of the Changbai Mountain hot springs is all above 60 degrees Celsius, with the highest reaching 80 degrees Celsius, earning it the title of high-temperature hot spring for its fiery vitality.

Jilin's most famous hot springs are the Changbai Mountain hot spring clusters, mainly including Changbai Hot Spring, Tiyun Hot Spring, Fushong Hot Spring, Tun Hot Spring, Changbai Eighteen Gou Hot Spring, Antu Medicinal Hot Spring, Jin Xian Hot Spring, Yu Jiang Hot Spring on the west side of Heaven Lake, etc., which have good therapeutic effects on treating rheumatoid arthritis, neurological diseases, and post-trauma sequelae.
