★ Day 1

Meals: On your own

Accommodation: Train

In the morning, gather at the Shenzhen train station at the designated time to board the train to Shangrao, Jiangxi. Enjoy the beautiful scenery along the way on the train. Entertainment activities will be organized in each carriage. Spend the night on the train.

★ Day 2

Meals: Breakfast, lunch, dinner

Accommodation: Tian Du or Ming Zhu or Jin Niu

After breakfast, take a bus to Wuyuan (3.5 hours). Visit the ancient village of Li Pit (about 1.5 hours). A winding stream runs through the village where most of the 260 households live along the clear water. The reflection of the ancient houses and washing women form a beautiful picture. The "Shen Ming Pavilion" is a testament to the clan system used to manage the villagers. View from afar the Moon Bay: an island between the two banks, shaped like a crescent moon. This is Moon Bay, a green oasis in the middle of the river, named for its crescent shape. It has become a unique scenic spot in Wuyuan with its charming moon-like figure reflecting against the backdrop of Huizhou-style houses. Moon Bay is known as the first bay of Wuyuan. This is the best place to capture the poetic sunset. During the blooming season, golden rape flowers cover the landscape (about 20 minutes). Visit Wangkou Ancient Village, which was originally built by Yu Gao, a Doctor of the Song Dynasty (third rank), during the Daguan period, over 1100 years ago. Because there is a blue water pool in front of the village, it is named "Wangkou". The ancient village is located in a hilly area, surrounded by green mountains and flowing water (visit for about 1 hour). After dinner, check into the hotel.

[Today's Highlight] The most beautiful season in Wuyuan is when the rapeseed flowers bloom, covering the slopes in golden yellow, contrasting beautifully with the white walls and gray tiles of Huizhou-style architecture, offering a sense of belonging to those escaping the hustle and bustle of the city.

★ Day 3

Meals: Breakfast, lunch, dinner

Accommodation: Cha Yuan Hotel or Home Inn

Wuyuan - 80KM - Huangshan City Center

Visit "Heaven on Earth" - Jiangling, one of China's four great seas of rapeseed flowers. Every early spring, from south to north, the rapeseed flowers bloom, creating breathtaking scenes of harmony between man and nature: mountains embrace the landscape, blue skies and clear waters, amidst lush greenery, fields of golden rapeseed flowers exude a rustic charm, akin to waves of flowers rushing in, forming a picturesque ink painting with yellow flowers and green willows. Huizhou-style houses with whitewashed walls and black-tiled roofs are scattered throughout the vast sea of rapeseed flowers, complementing each other (the blooming season of Wuyuan rapeseed flowers may be affected by local climate conditions, resulting in less than ideal viewing due to specific weather reasons; please understand). Later, visit Xiaqi Village, established in 787 AD, divided into upper and lower Xiaqi villages. There are nearly 100 houses over 600 years old in the village, with distinct styles and grandeur. The Jinshi House, Dafu House, Ronglu House, and other merchant and official houses all reflect the brilliance of this land of outstanding natural beauty (visit for about 1 hour); Visit Jiangwan. The architectural layout of Jiangwan is unique, with one street and six alleys crisscrossing orderly over a thousand years, maintaining its formation without disorder. Each alley has its own personality, not repeating others, ancient and elegant, forming its own scenery. Seen from Long Mountain, the central alley forms a large "An" character. Jiangwan's old streets are still preserved. After the visit, take a bus to the Huangshan city center (Tunxi), have dinner, and check into the hotel.

[Today's Highlight] The most beautiful season in Wuyuan is when the rapeseed flowers bloom, covering the slopes in golden yellow, contrasting beautifully with the white walls and gray tiles of Huizhou-style architecture, offering a sense of belonging to those escaping the hustle and bustle of the city.

★ Day 4

Meals: Breakfast, lunch, dinner

Accommodation: Cha Yuan Hotel or Home Inn

Huangshan One-Day Tour Option One: After breakfast, take a bus to Huangshan Scenic Area (1.5 hours), then take the cable car up the mountain (about 10 minutes, cable car fees included for the ascent), visit Yupinglou Scenic Area, admire the world-renowned Huangshan Welcoming Guest Pine [Huangshan Pine, an independent species in botany, grows almost everywhere on the peaks, showcasing its myriad postures], view the Sleeping Buddha of Yuping, gaze at Tiandu Peak, climb the Hundred-Step Cloud Ladder, walk through (those with good stamina can go through the Sky Passage, while those with less stamina can choose the Ao Fish Cave route) to reach Ao Fish Peak, have lunch. Return via the original route, after reaching Yupinglou, take the cable car down the mountain at your own expense (about 10 minutes, walking takes about 2 hours), take a bus (about 0.5 hours) to the Xin Qifeng Tea Museum (stay for 45 minutes), taste the famous Huangshan Mao Feng tea series. Then take a bus to Tunxi [about 1 hour], passing through the ancient countryside of Huizhou, thoughts wandering with the green mountains and clear waters, shifting scenery before your eyes; have dinner and rest in the hotel.

Option Two: After breakfast, head to the World Natural and Cultural Heritage Site, the majestic and picturesque Huangshan Scenic Area (about 1-hour drive), take the Yun Gu New Cable Car up the mountain (operation time 10 minutes, walking takes about 1.5 hours) to Bright Summit, visit Huangshan's peculiar rocks - Flying Stone, overlook the beautiful scenery of the Western Sea Peaks, enjoy Huangshan's strange rock wonders, Paiyun Pavilion Scenic Area, after lunch, head to Beihai Scenic Area, the core of the Huangshan Scenic Area, concentrating the essence and gathering wonderful sights. Lion Peak, Qingliang Terrace, listen to the ancient love stories, look at the affectionate stone monkey, Dream Pen Blossoms, Eighteen Arhats Facing the South Sea, etc.; later visit Xinxin Peak, it is said that an ancient person climbed the mountain with doubts, and upon reaching here, began to believe in the wonders of Huangshan, hence the name. Main attractions include Black Tiger Pine, Twin Pine, Child Worshipping Guanyin, and other beautiful landscapes, evoking endless imagination. Later, take the cable car down the mountain at your own expense (about 10 minutes, walking takes about 1.5 hours), then head to Huangshan city center, have dinner, and check into the hotel.

[Today's Highlight] Spring is the best season to visit Huangshan. The charming spring scenery of Huangshan captivates visitors. Along the road leading to Huangshan, fields of golden rapeseed flowers and patches of azaleas on the hillsides welcome you even before you arrive at Huangshan, immersing you in the natural beauty.

★ Day 5

Meals: Breakfast, lunch, dinner

Accommodation: Yingshen or Xinghe Hotel

Tunxi - 280km - Hengdian (Interactive Movie Shooting)

After breakfast, take a bus to "China's Hollywood" Hengdine Film Studio (about 3.5 hours), head to the main filming site of "All Glitters", either the Ming-Qing Palace or the "Qingming Riverside Scene" scenic area for the much-anticipated "I Act in Hengdine" film experience activity. Each bus serves as a unit, with 40-45 people forming a film crew to shoot a movie. Here, there are professional sets, scripts, costumes, directors, cameramen, and tailor-made scripts for each visitor. After the director assigns roles, makeup artists and costume designers prepare the actors, then formal shooting begins. Emperors, eunuchs, sedan bearers, matchmakers, rich young men, vividly portrayed... Laughter fills the shooting site, creating a lively atmosphere. (Carefully edited, everyone will receive a DVD of the movie they participated in about ten days later as a precious keepsake.) Visit Zhejiang's largest bamboo fiber R&D base, the Green Ease Bamboo Healthy Living Hall, explore bamboo charcoal-made non-toxic, odorless, side-effect-free pure natural living products, and experience a bamboo-healthy lifestyle! After dinner, you can optionally visit the large interactive dream night tour scenic area "Dream Valley" to experience the country's first ecological disaster experience - flash floods, feel the shock of 400 tons of floodwater rushing in instantly, and dance with Wa ethnic youth around a big bonfire in the dream water town for the Wild Dance Torch Festival.

[Today's Highlight] Shoot movies at Hengdine Film Studio, parents themselves become stars shooting their own blockbuster, the joyful atmosphere and funny plot will surely make them laugh heartily, and there will be professionally made movie DVDs as precious souvenirs.

★ Day 6

Meals: Breakfast, lunch, dinner

Accommodation: Qidouxing or Zhentian Hotel

Hengdine - 90km - Zhuji - 70km - Shaoxing

After breakfast, visit the main filming site of the movies "Hero" and "The Mummy 3" - Qinwang Palace Scenic Area, experiencing the grandeur of Emperor Qin Shi Huang unifying the world, witnessing the special effects of the chess hall sword fight in "Hero", and appreciating the high-tech multimedia drama "Dreaming Back to the Qin-Han Dynasties" featuring 3D movie special effects. Head to the ancient capital of the Yue Kingdom and birthplace of Xi Shi - Zhuji (about 1 hour drive), after lunch, visit the Xi Shi Birthplace Scenic Area, learn about the lesser-known life story of Xi Shi, visit the exhibition hall dedicated to famous women of Chinese history, the Famous Women Pavilion, and Fan Li Shrine, participate in fun sports events organized by Zhuji Tourism Bureau at the Xi Shi Birthplace Square (kick shuttlecock, bounce table tennis balls, double-person hundred-meter race... one fun event after another, surprise prizes distributed on-site). Then take a bus to the ancient water city and hometown of celebrities [Shaoxing] (about 1 hour drive), visit the Dong Lake, known as the "best landscape in the world", one of the three famous lakes in Zhejiang alongside Hangzhou's West Lake and Jiaxing's South Lake. Visitors can take a ride on a traditional Shaoxing-style Wu Peng boat, rowing on the lake and admiring the strange rocks and caves of Dong Lake (costs not included: 90 yuan per boat for three people). After dinner, watch a large-scale evening performance arranged specifically for the special train tourists at Shen Garden by the local tourism bureau, experiencing the opening ceremony of a wealthy household's garden in the Song Dynasty, touring the garden accompanied by a steward, enjoying enchanting Yue Opera and Lotus Falling songs, interacting with the stewards of Shen Garden from the Song Dynasty, singing songs from different eras... A joyful journey and a dreamlike night leave every tourist deeply moved.

[Today's Highlight] A magnificent evening performance at Shen Garden in the ancient city of Shaoxing, enchanting Yue Opera, and the tragic love story of Lu You and Tang Wan evoke countless beautiful memories, indulging in such a beautiful moonlit night.

★ Day 7

Meals: Breakfast, lunch

Accommodation: Train

Shaoxing - 130km - Hangzhou - Railway Station

After breakfast, visit the birthplace of a literary giant and the "treasure of the city" of Shaoxing - Lu Xun's Hometown: the authentic Three Flavor Study, Hundred Herb Garden, Lu Xun's former residence, Lu Xun's ancestral home, Zhou Family New Courtyard, etc., stroll through the historical street in front of Lu Xun's Hometown with unique Jiangnan style, ancient city, and culture [Lu Xun Road], taste spiced beans and local snacks, then take a bus to Hangzhou (about 1 hour drive), stroll leisurely through the West Lake under sunny, rainy, snowy, or windy weather, viewing numerous beautiful sceneries such as Su Causeway, Broken Bridge, Solitary Hill, Three Pools Mirroring the Moon, Quyuan Wind Lotus, etc., looking afar at the legendary Leifeng Pagoda, visit the Flower Harbor Watching Fish located between the Western Su Causeway, Yingbo Bridge, and Suolan Bridge, a large park skillfully combining Jiangnan and Western garden art, watching silk performances, then head to the railway station to take the train back to Shenzhen. Spend the night on the train.

[Today's Highlight] The beauty of the West Lake, especially in spring, the tranquil lake surface, graceful weeping willows... No matter how many times you visit the West Lake, its beauty never gets old, and new discoveries always await.
