The origin of Singles' Day has many versions, one of the most widely recognized is that it originated from the campus humor culture of universities in Nanjing during the 1990s.

Singles' Day is an entertaining holiday popular among young people in mainland China, celebrating with pride those who are still single ("guanggun" means "single"). It originated on campuses and spread through media such as the Internet, gradually forming a cultural phenomenon around Singles' Day. January 1st is Mini-Singles' Day, January 11th and November 1st are Mid-Singles' Day, and November 11th, with four "1"s, is known as Big Singles' Day. Generally speaking, Singles' Day refers to the Big Singles' Day on November 11th.
