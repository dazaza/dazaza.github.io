II Game Operation Method:

Button 5: Confirm/Dialogue/Open treasure chest

Button 0: View map

Button 2: Move up

Button 8: Move down

Button 4: Move left

III Complete Guide to Main Storyline and Side Quests:

The Ghost Invasion

After an interesting plot, the player can control the protagonist. First, go to the store to sell items, then head to the mayor's house. At this point, the mayor will give you three pets to choose from. It is recommended to choose Little Sheep at the beginning because it has high blood and defense (and many evolution times), very useful! You will also get some items and a little turtle that can avoid enemies and speed up (if you don't want to level up, just ride this one! Very powerful). After leaving the mayor's house, you see Jingjing. After dialogue, you get a side quest. Catch a Laughing Grass Mushroom for Jingjing (note: this quest cannot be done after Chapter 8). Still on the same map as the mayor's house, you can pick up another side quest in the lower left corner. Running back and forth between Frank's house and the mayor's house completes the quest (it is recommended to seriously complete the side quests in the early stage to get some items).

After leaving the village, you encounter Kate, the heroine of this story, and then start the first battle (relatively simple, just pay attention to healing). After the battle, talk to Kate. At this point, an option appears, so choose to help from the beginning, otherwise you'll have to listen to the heroine's nagging. Accompany Kate to Silent Hill. Along the way, you'll encounter pet hunters (the hunters throughout the game...) If you want easier battles later, fight them, not difficult. Arrive at Metal Ruins and find the door closed. Go fight the monkeys on the map! (After fighting two, you'll encounter the Monkey Boss). The Monkey King deserves his name, so prepare well for this battle. If your pet has learned skills, use them frequently. Also, make sure to capture metal-based pets earlier, which will make the battle much easier. When you return to the previous door, it opens automatically. Hurry up and go! After leaving the door, something happens. After defeating the Laughing Grass Mushroom, you get a side quest. Completing this quest later in the greenhouse will give follow-up quests.

Turbulent Iron City!

Encounter Kate in Iron City. After a few words, start the fight! Later, meet Luna. After a plot, Kate runs away (seems like there's something between Kate and Luna), exchange a few words with Luna and then soldiers come to cause trouble, continue the fight! After the fight, you can wander around the city. It is recommended to find the grandfather with the emblem on his head to upgrade, then buy some things at the store (Note: the store in the city has everything, make sure to replenish supplies before leaving the city) Walk to the upper left of the store and you'll encounter a mysterious ghost, exchanging can get mutation items (for strengthening pets). Also worth noting are the arenas in each city! If you want to collect the five hidden pets, you must pass all of them! (There are many fun mini-games inside, it is recommended to play more arena battles, there are many rewards, mainly because the hidden pets are unique and worth collecting!) After wandering around, go to the largest house and talk to Luna.

After finishing the task, go to the biggest house, find Luna to talk. After talking, go to Tieling towards the northwest, where an uncle asks you to deliver a letter. Find a soldier in the tavern of Tieliang to talk. After delivering the letter, prepare to fight the cunning uncle. After the fight, the way to the Cold Iron Ruins is opened. Arriving at the Cold Iron Ruins, there's a grandfather in front of the door, you need to either pay or fight to pass. After leaving the Cold Iron Ruins, this chapter ends.

Capture Humans?!

You've encountered Kate again, but this time Kate was taken away by a green monster using a capture device before the fight started. Hurry to inform the lord of Yincheng! Walking to the bottom of the logging site map, you can receive a side quest. The client wants a Pippi Fairy, which can be captured in the Silver Light Land above Yincheng. Arriving at Yincheng, but you are misunderstood by the lord of Yincheng, prepare for a fight! All the pets of the Yincheng lord are level 21, prepare well. On the right side of the exit of Yincheng, you can accept the Gigantic Flower side quest (recommended to definitely accept, you can get a Kissgrass that can evolve three times). Arriving at the logging site, defeat the Gigantic Flower and return to the client who doesn't give money. Defeat the henchman again, and come back later to get the monetary reward. There's an uncle in the city, talking to him can get items (this will also appear later in the Pet Village and Sky Castle). Remember to go to the Yincheng Arena! Then arrive at the Silver Light Land, cross the Silver Light Land to the Metal Ruins. Just entering, there's a door leading to an Emperor Lion (level 60), at this stage, forget about it for now, wait until you reach around level 60 before thinking about it. After defeating the Emperor Lion, you get a Cracked Lion Cub (recommended to complete, this pet is very useful~). Continue forward, see a pet hunter, defeat him, he'll tell you that the wall next to him can be passed through.

Meeting the Metal Emperor

Finally arrived at the Metal Town, met Luke, the male lead of Gold! Go to the tavern on the left to accept the errand task. Arrive at the Metal Palace, after finishing the conversation, return to the Silent Village with Luke. At this point, Luke will tell you a shortcut, the guard will challenge you or you can pay to pass. However, because of Luke's divine might, even if you choose to challenge, you don't have to fight. (Choosing to challenge can save you 2000 gold)

Flower Language Plain

Enter the palace secret passage from the old Metal Palace. Here, you encounter Big Mouth and Pippi Fairy, both are level 35, after winning, return to the Silent Village! Returning to the Silent Village, you find it has been occupied by ghosts. Several ghosts approach, level 58, this battle is certain to fail, don't waste potions! After the defeat, Luke lures the ghosts away, and the protagonist temporarily can't return to the village. Arrive at the previously mentioned palace secret passage, walk north to reach the Flower Language Plain. Arrive at the Flower Language Plain, see General Tie capturing humans, ignore it for now, arrive at the Lost Ruins, find the door closed. At this point, return to the previous Flower Language Plain, the entrance in the upper right leads to the Pet Village. In the lower right of the Pet Village, there's a house where you can accept a side quest, find the Moon Bear and give it to the blacksmith to get the Bear Armor. Arrive at the Pet Village, first find Kate, then defeat the guards (level 42) to rescue the village chief and get the key to open the Lost Ruins. Return to the Flower Language Plain again, at this point, you find a Furious Tiger King (level 50) on the road, defeating it will get you another very useful pet! At this point, arrive at the Lost Ruins, the door opens automatically, arrive at the deepest part of the ruins where there's a teleportation array, enter it and you can reach the Sky Castle, save your progress!

Sky Castle

In the house next to the shop in the Sky Castle, you can accept a side quest, catch the King Vulture in the Sky Secret Realm and give it to Fred. If you have passed all the dojos before, go to the Sky Dojo before entering the Sky Secret Realm, defeat Xiao Jing to get the last hidden pet, the Sky Dragon Man. Find the Sky Dragon God (level 60) in the Sky Secret Realm 3, Miro offends the Sky Dragon God again, no words, just fight. However, since it's a god, the Sky Dragon God will only use 60% of its strength. After the fight, after a bunch of words, the Sky Dragon God still refuses to help. Don't worry, continue upward and you can encounter a young dragon, talking to it will make it agree to go with you. At this point, it is recommended to finish the side quests in the Sky Castle and Sky Secret Realm first, because you won't be able to return to the Sky Castle in the short term. With the young dragon, parachute to Garden Plain 2, here encounter General Tie, Miro is captured and sent to the Pet Village.

General Tie's Counterattack

At this point, the female lead finally appears, beauty saves hero? But still can't avoid fighting with the guards, after the fight, you can return to the Silent Village.

Exploration of the Dark Forest!

Return to the Silent Village, learn that the villagers have been tied up and taken to the Dark Forest, go to the Dark Forest to rescue the villagers! Keep walking to the right to trigger the rescue of Harry Potter's brothers, after rescuing the brothers, send them out of the Dark Forest first, re-enter the Dark Forest, go up on the second chapter map, go left at the intersection with the exclamation mark ghost to trigger the rescue of the mayor plot (3 monsters around level 57), similarly, go right at the intersection with the exclamation mark ghost to rescue Aunt Fu (3 monsters around level 55). After rescuing the two people, send them out of the Dark Forest, when re-entering the Dark Forest, there's a level 55 Fairy Queen causing trouble, defeating her can get a level 40 Clever Fairy Queen. Directly go up to find the Dark Ghost King! (5 level 55 monsters, the Dark Ghost King is level 60, prepare well). There are 5 side quests in the Dark Forest. At the entrance map, Jingjing has a task to find Lele, Lele is on the second map of the Dark Forest going up, defeating a level 55 monster can get Lele, giving Lele to Jingjing can get the pet ghost; After rescuing Harry Potter's brothers, a Night Elf appears on the left, talking to it can get a Night Elf and some money. Going up on the second map of the Dark Forest, you'll encounter a greedy Lamp Monster, giving it what it wants can get the pet Lamp Monster. Similarly, going up on the second map of the Dark Forest, you'll encounter a ghost, defeating all the ghosts in the forest can get the Void Spirit (more evolution times, recommended to do, choosing unable to act will get a pet 10 levels higher than choosing to relieve it).

Forward, Metal Palace!

After defeating the Dark Ghost King, trigger the plot outside the Silent East Suburbs, then come to the Metal Palace with Luke. At this point, discover that the palace has been sealed by General Tie, defeat the soldiers (monsters around level 40) and enter the palace with Luke from another secret passage (go to the place with stones on the right of the Old Metal Palace). Entering the palace, find that General Tie has already occupied it, Luke lures the soldiers away, letting Miro go save the king and princess. Prepare well beforehand, the final boss fight. Two consecutive battles, the first battle with 5 monsters around level 58, the second battle with 5 monsters around level 60. After victory, the female lead appears and takes away General Tie, the game ends. Although the plot is all finished, there's still one main quest left: collect all the pets, since it's a collection element game, this is necessary to do!

At the beginning of the game, our protagonist "Lin Xi" is dreaming of "saving the world", only to be grabbed out of bed by Aunt Li, getting a good beating (how familiar this scene is). Sure enough, this is the inn from the first "Xianjian", and Aunt Li is still as fierce as ever, after a lesson, Lin Xi has no choice but to unwillingly get up and work (your brother Xianyou had it worse than you, haha). Remember to take the hidden items on the cabinet, just like the computer games back then, there are no prompts, you have to find them yourself.

Walking downstairs, Aunt Li tells Lin Xi that "Li Xiaoyao" will return today, and asks him to attend to guests. From the guests, learn that the home of "Jing Tian" in Yuzhou is haunted. After ordering the dishes, find out that there's no osmanthus wine left, Aunt Li sends Lin Xi to the pharmacy to buy some osmanthus honey to make fake osmanthus wine (sweat...).

Go out from "Yuhang East" to the pharmacy, the owner says the osmanthus honey hasn't arrived yet, hoping Lin Xi can go to the wild "Hundred Flowers Path" to get some. Leaving the city to "Yuhang West Suburbs", find a beautiful woman knocked down by a monster, naturally, Lin Xi "heroically rescues the beauty". Easily win, get the osmanthus honey. Lin Xi finds out that the other party is a stunning beauty, so enthusiastically invites her to Yuhang, even bringing up the name of Li Xiaoyao. The other party turns out to be a fan of Li Xiaoyao, so Lin Xi's small plan succeeds, and the two return to Yuhang together.

After returning to Yuhang and completing the task at the pharmacy, Master Tong will reward you with the recipe for "Feng Ling Blade" (best to carefully listen to how to synthesize items), finally learn that Li Xiaoyao is in trouble. Lin Xi rushes back to the inn, finding Li Xiaoyao unconscious, and the doctor is at a loss. Just when everyone is at a loss, Jinghua, who returned with Lin Xi, uses magic to wake up Li Xiaoyao. After Li Xiaoyao wakes up, he foresees that the six realms are in crisis, at this point, Jinghua's image appears before everyone, informing that there's a problem with the demon realm. So Li Xiaoyao, Lin Xi, and Jinghua decide to go to Kunlun Mountain together.

After leaving the city, still set out from "Yuhang West Suburbs", originally thought having Brother Li would make leveling up easy, but he never fights, it's still the juniors doing the work, strongly despised. Walk to a crossroads, while Li Xiaoyao goes to scout, Lin Xi and Jinghua have a love drama, poor Li Dading comes back and everyone continues to the "right". Remember to save your game.

After passing through a scary cave, arrive at the foot of Kunlun Mountain. There's a merchant at the foot of the mountain, change equipment quickly, there will be a tough battle ahead. Like the computer version, flowers on the ground can be picked too, if you don't want to spend money on medicine, look down more often while walking. Soon, you'll encounter the protagonists of "Xianjian 4", Yun Tianhe and Liu Mengli. Time starts to become chaotic, Lin Xi remembers Yun Tianhe saving the world by shooting down the Jionghua Sect with a divine bow. But Yun Tianhe doesn't remember, it turns out the two have traveled 600 years into the present. After a conversation, learn that Xuan Xiao is actually on top of Kunlun Mountain, so everyone decides to climb the mountain together. There's a medicine merchant on the left, a save point ahead, it will be difficult below.

On the top of the mountain, corpses are everywhere, see Xuan Xiao gone mad. Yun Tianhe, Li Xiaoyao, and others try hard to persuade, but unfortunately Xuan Xiao's heart is deeply troubled, ultimately resorting to sword against them. Although Xuan Xiao says, "Come on all of you," the ones fighting are still just the two juniors (tears~ that's Xuan Xiao ah...). After a tough battle, Xuan Xiao finally falls (Xuan Xiao has always been a tragic character, after the victory, seeing Xuan Xiao's poem, a wave of sadness arises).

Finally, Jinghua has to return to the demon realm, Lin Xi, although reluctant, feels he doesn't match up to Jinghua, and dares not confess. Li Xiaoyao thinks that although Xuan Xiao failed, things won't be so simple, decides to continue searching for clues, and lets Lin Xi go to Hangzhou to seek help from "Wang Xiaohu" and "Li Yiru", and reminds Lin Xi to say a good word for him to Aunt Li when going to Yuhang, to avoid being beaten again when he returns (childhood trauma ah). After Li Xiaoyao leaves, Yun Tianhe asks about what happened after he shot down the Jionghua Sect, Lin Xi can't bear to let him know the tragic ending, so he lies. After Liu Mengli returns, everyone decides to return to Yuhang first. Not far from walking, Jinghua suddenly appears and wants to travel with them, Lin Xi naturally feels happy inside. Turns out Liu Mengli doesn't want Jinghua to repeat her emotional path, and frankly advises (Yun Tianhe is a pighead~~).

After returning to Yuhang Xianjian Inn, everyone goes to rest. Aunt Li naturally sees the ambiguity between Lin Xi and Jinghua, takes out the "ancestral jade bracelet" and gives it to Lin Xi, helping create opportunities for the two. But tragically, even with Lin Xi's cover-up, Aunt Li still decides to discipline Brother Li (mourning~). Lin Xi voluntarily proposes to take Jinghua for a tour of Yuhang, after a love drama, the two's relationship advances further. After returning to the inn, Yun Tianhe is eating heartily, making Aunt Li feel pain, and after the meal, everyone is driven out. (Embarrassed~) Everyone then heads towards Yuzhou City, at this point, prepare equipment and medicine, there's a save point at the inn.

Arriving at the pawnshop in Yuzhou City, find Jing Tian and Xue Jian from "Xianjian 3" battling the Evil Sword Immortal, naturally everyone helps. After a tough battle, Jinghua blocks the Evil Sword Immortal's final attack for Lin Xi, and disappears. Lin Xi is heartbroken, finally expressing his feelings for Jinghua, and puts the "ancestral jade bracelet" on Jinghua's wrist. At this point, Xue Jian proposes a method to revive Jinghua, go to the ghost realm to retrieve Jinghua's soul, but need to find the "Water Spirit Pearl" first to preserve Jinghua's body. Lin Xi, based on Jing Tian's suggestion, decides to go to the "Beihai" in the northern suburbs of Yuzhou to find the "Water Spirit Pearl".

Now you're fighting alone, so save often and prepare plenty of medicine. The final guardian of the Water Spirit Pearl is a water dragon, not difficult, just use thunder magic. After getting the Water Spirit Pearl, everyone hurries back to the inn and freezes Jinghua's body. Continue rushing to "Fengdu" to find the entrance to the ghost realm.

Arriving at Fengdu, encounter Jinghua's sister "Shuiyue" at the inn, after a quarrel, to revive her sister, Shuiyue joins Lin Xi's team (Shuiyue's level is based on Lin Xi's level, so it's best to level up Lin Xi as much as possible). From the innkeeper's mouth, learn that there's a problem with the guest in the middle room. Sure enough, this is the guide to the ghost realm, after arriving in the ghost realm, there will be a battle with Mother Meng at the Bridge of Helplessness. After crossing the Bridge of Helplessness, encounter the Ghost King at the Ghost Gate, naturally another tough battle. After defeating the Ghost King and retrieving Jinghua's soul, everyone hurries back to the inn in Yuzhou and finally revives Jinghua. But Shuiyue hates the Evil Sword Immortal for injuring her sister, and goes to Shoushan alone for revenge. After discussion, Liu Mengli et al stay behind to take care of Jinghua, Lin Xi et al go to Shoushan to retrieve Shuiyue. Lin Xi catches up with Shuiyue in the southern suburbs of Yuzhou, and everyone goes to Shoushan to find the Evil Sword Immortal together.

At the foot of Shoushan, encounter Nangong Huang and Wen Hui from "Xianjian 3 - Asking About Emotions", learn that there's a change in the earth veins of Shoushan, everyone suspects it's related to the Evil Sword Immortal, so they decide to investigate together. After entering the earth veins, find three entrances, choose the middle one. Save your game, then fight the fire dragon. After solving the anomaly in the earth veins, everyone continues toward Shoushan.

Arriving at Shoushan, find demons running rampant, along the way there's a Flame Beast King blocking the way. Arriving at the summit, meet Xu Changqing, the master of Shoushan, everyone suspects that the Evil Sword Immortal entering the Demon Tower caused all this. So Lin Xi et al decide to investigate the Demon Tower.

In the Demon Tower, encounter Wang Xiaohu and Li Yiru from "Xianjian 2", learn that the Evil Sword Immortal is indeed in the Demon Tower, and used illusions to deceive Li Xiaoyao and Zhao Ling'er. The most classic maze mode of the four Xianjian games finally appears, actually not too difficult, just remember the route and it can be easily passed, prepare plenty of "Demon-Exorcising Incense" or it will cost a lot of time and effort. Finally, meet the "Evil Sword Immortal" at the top of the tower, turn out that the chaos in time and space is all caused by a "mysterious person" from Wuhua Mountain. Regardless, the revenge for Jinghua is definitely necessary, so start the fight. This battle is extremely difficult, it's recommended to come back after level 40, of course, if your equipment and medicine are sufficient, there's no problem.

After leaving the Demon Tower, everyone bids farewell to Xu Changqing, Nangong Huang and Wen Hui join, the team heads toward Wuhua Mountain. Encounter Yun Tianhe et al at the shadow lake in the western suburbs of Yuzhou, turns out someone found the ancient artifact - Kunlun Mirror, which has the ability to travel through time.
