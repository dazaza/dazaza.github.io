If we can be together, I will not leave you.

by lili1 on 2012-02-19 22:49:33

If we can be together, then I will not leave or abandon you.

I always think of you unintentionally, and then imagine the scenes when we are together in my mind. I think about what kind of dialogue we will have, what kind of plot will happen. As I think, I suddenly feel sad inside. I know that after the dream wakes up, only a large amount of loneliness and longing will accompany me. Really, sometimes I really can't bear that emotion. I feel painful. I said that I hate "distance", hate "later". But reality is like this, there is distance, and there is later.

I should say thank you now. It's not because we are strangers. I just want you to know my true feelings. Thank you for the days you've accompanied me. When I am the most upset and helpless, you are with me, even if you are not by my side. You said as long as we have each other in our hearts, it is enough. Yes, as long as we have each other in our hearts, it is enough. You don't know, when you said this sentence, I had already been in tears.

I always imagine your life alone. I know that is something I cannot participate in. I imagine what kind of people you will meet, what kind of past you will have with them, what kind of girl you will encounter. When emotions cannot be relieved, I always go out for a walk, foolishly watching my steps moving forward one by one. Every time I see a tall and thin figure on the road, I would be silent for a long time, thinking of you. I feel my heart being torn apart.

I open our chat records, which is the only memory that records our time together. I dare not delete it. If those were accidentally cleared one day, what would I do? What would I use to review your tender gentleness?

I didn't expect university life to be so busy. Many times, I no longer hang QQ or Feixin. I remember the summer vacation, and I feel that the whole world only has me and you left. Otherwise, how could my memory have no one else? I am delighted every time the avatar blinks. I think I really live very simply. At least, I know my happiness is simple, and it is in your world. I want to keep living simply like this.

Many times, I feel lonely, at least when I think of you, I am always lonely.

I remember how we met for the first time. You are a person who will always be happy, at least in my heart, it is like this. I remember you said that the failures given by heaven to you can be counted on one hand. I laugh at my own continuous failures. I remember you posted two pieces of sorrowful texts, so I was very afraid. How could you, who are always unbreakable, be so low? Seeing you in pain is more painful than myself being sad. I am still very afraid of you posting such texts because your sorrow is always due to your past, those people I am unfamiliar with. I know they gave you unforgettable memories. No matter how I comfort you, I cannot clear those fragmented memories. I hope you are always happy. If possible, if all the pasts including mine are sorrowful, let's throw them away, okay? Let's just be the simple you and me now.

I tried hard to enter the same university as you, but finally, it did not come true. My June was black. Even though you were with me, I still felt cold.

I suddenly crazily replayed all the bits and pieces of our journey in my mind. I am like the cold and quiet blackness of the night, and you are like a ray of light. It came suddenly but illuminated everything for me. I thought I disappeared, but I was actually held in your arms, always warm... always warm...

I think you are the gift that heaven gave me in my life. But will I also be the gift that heaven gave you? For me, what kind of feeling will you have? I just hope that at some unintentional moments, you will also think of me, and share your happiness with me when you are happy.

The story continues without a beginning or an end. I just hope it will never end. If you are my interlude, I just hope you will quietly play it. If we meet, I will tightly hold you, not leaving or abandoning.

"Distance", "later", sometimes they are really unbearable. If these can become reality one day, I will make myself accept it. I will quietly wait for their end.
