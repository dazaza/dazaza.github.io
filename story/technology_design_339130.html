The educational model of Churchman University: Churchman Business School

by k3p3vbq7u2 on 2010-04-02 13:37:15

Churchman Business School, part of Churchman University, stands out among the prestigious business schools in the United States due to its rigorous academic approach. Its MBA course is also highly distinctive compared to other MBA programs across the U.S. Many mid-to-senior level managers from multinational corporations such as IBM and Boeing have participated in the curriculum development, which enhances the practicality of the courses.

The board of trustees at Churchman University sees great potential in distance education. Similar to other universities in the U.S., the remote degree certificates issued by Churchman University are identical to those awarded on campus. Professors at the university believe that their advanced online learning system ensures that students' learning outcomes can even surpass those achieved through traditional on-campus study. For instance, remote students can repeatedly access various resources, re-watch video tutorials, and engage more deeply in online discussions with classmates.

Churchman University's online business degree programs allow mid-to-senior level managers around the world to study, take exams, and submit papers online at any time and place without affecting their current jobs. The degrees earned through this program are exactly the same as those obtained on campus, making it the preferred educational model for mid-to-senior level managers who are working professionals in Europe, America, and China, including Churchman Business School.

Churchman University offers bilingual courses in English and Western languages, as well as English and Chinese, which are very popular among multinational companies. The university awards MBAs, bachelor’s, master’s, and doctoral degrees. Once a low-income scholarship is successfully applied for, students need only a few thousand dollars to complete their degrees.

Churchman University has campuses in Pennsylvania, California, Texas, and other locations in the U.S. The oldest branch, Churchman Business School, was established in 1911 and has nearly a century of history, making it one of the oldest business schools in the U.S. It specializes in cultivating high-end talent for the American financial industry and ranks 17th in the "National MBA Rankings for Business Schools" among all U.S. business schools.

Besides Churchman University, other century-old prestigious universities in the U.S., such as Boston University and Brown University, are also widely recognized for their teaching quality by white-collar workers in China.
