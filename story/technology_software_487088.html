The Secret Method to Cut Off Extramarital Affairs:

In society, news of extramarital affairs frequently spreads. Whether it's a husband or wife having an affair, the spouse is the most deserving of sympathy. If you still deeply love your husband or wife and want to bring back their temporarily lust-blinded heart, it is suggested that you try this method: "The Secret Method to Cut Off Extramarital Affairs." If you have climbing plants growing at home or potted flowers on the balcony, you can use them. If not, go buy one, preferably with many flowers like chrysanthemums or periwinkles.

Next, prepare a tea tray with seven small teacups. In the living room, find the桃花位 (Peach Blossom Position), which is usually also the wealth position. Buy a small bag of "red rice" (a red dye) and mix it with water to make it red. Pour it into the seven small cups. Master Huitong, take a newly bought pair of scissors, sprinkle the red water from the cups onto the scissors, then use the scissors to cut the flowers off the potted plant. You can cut one to six flowers each time, and continue this for seven days.

Then, take a piece of old clothing worn by your husband or wife (underwear is better), lay it on the table, put all the flowers you've cut over the seven days inside, wrap it up well, and tie it with red thread. Throw it into a river, and this will sever your spouse’s ill-fated relationship, bringing him or her back to you.
