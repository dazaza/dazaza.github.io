What is the most suitable way to correct buck teeth in adults?

by 454kq on 2011-06-07 14:04:12

Adult buck teeth correction? Orthodontic treatment is not the exclusive right of teenagers. However, adults should choose the right method for dental correction. Patients with buck teeth often show open lips and exposed teeth, and their lips cannot close in a natural state. When smiling, there is excessive exposure of the gums, often accompanied by a receding chin. When forcibly closing the mouth, there is an obvious soft tissue bulge between the lower lip and the chin.

What is the most suitable way to correct adult buck teeth?

Firstly, adults can adopt the following methods for correction:

1. Traditional orthodontic correction, commonly known as braces

The best age for this method is 11-14 years old. Adults who have missed this age can still undergo correction, but it requires good condition of the teeth themselves, no cavities, no periodontal disease, and requires good cooperation and perseverance. The time required for adult orthodontic treatment varies depending on the situation, usually lasting 1-2 years or even longer, which needs to be determined according to individual circumstances. It should also be noted that adults have better tooth calcification and comprehensive tooth development. Teeth themselves have memory functions, so they may be more prone to relapse after correction. This state cannot be well overcome at present.

2. Correction method to overcome relapse: "Cosmetic Crown"

This is a new type of tooth correction and beauty method popular in Europe, America, Japan, and South Korea. It can not only correct teeth but also beautify them. Since its introduction to China in 2000, it has become very popular among celebrities and white-collar workers, and then spread to the working class in major cities.

The "Cosmetic Crown" technology draws on the strengths of various tooth beautification technologies, using German VITA color matching technology, American infallible narcosis (Beylan anesthesia) painless technology, and 3D digital positioning technology. Customers who choose "Cosmetic Crown" are guaranteed lifelong tooth beauty assurance, so doctors from official Cosmetic Crown institutions have strict medical skill requirements and must possess strong artistic design capabilities. Because Cosmetic Crowns focus not only on the functionality of teeth but also on their aesthetics, it is a combination of medical science and art, and is a fast-acting method for correcting adult buck teeth.
