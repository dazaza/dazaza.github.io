Regulations on Subscription Quantity and Frequency:

(1) For this online issue, the minimum subscription quantity for a single securities account is 500 shares, and any quantity exceeding 500 shares must be an integer multiple of 500 shares. The upper limit of subscription quantity shall not exceed 20,000 shares. For new share subscription mandates exceeding the subscription upper limit, the Shenzhen Stock Exchange trading system will automatically cancel the mandate as an invalid mandate without confirmation.

(2) Investors participating in the online public offering of stocks can only use one securities account. Each securities account can only subscribe once for each new stock issue. Once a new share subscription mandate is confirmed by the Shenzhen Stock Exchange trading system, it cannot be canceled. If multiple securities accounts (as of April 13, 2010, account registration data) with the same "account holder name" and "valid identification document number" participate in this online issue subscription, the first subscription confirmed by the Shenzhen Stock Exchange trading system will be regarded as the valid subscription, and other subscriptions will be invalid.

(3) Investors must comply with relevant laws and regulations and bear corresponding legal responsibilities.
