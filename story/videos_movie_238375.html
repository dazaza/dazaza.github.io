Starring: Liu Xiaofeng, Wang Yan, Wang Yanhui, Zhang Lin

The story takes place in 1951, shortly after the founding of New China, when a series of terrifying incidents occurred in a small city in Southwest China. A key figure dies and becomes a corpse with red spots, prompting public security officer Lu Fei to begin a difficult investigation... Clearly, this kind of anti-espionage drama is packaged with a sinister and terrifying atmosphere. The lead actors, Liu Xiaofeng and Wang Wu, mentioned that they were scared several times during filming, such as running through a graveyard on a dark and windy night or hearing intermittent dripping water sounds in an old house late at night... These scenes often made people's hair stand on end. However, Liu Xiaofeng stated that being spooky and mysterious is only a small aspect of the entire show. It's more like an intellectual game. "For an anti-espionage drama to be interesting, it's not about us performing while the audience watches, but rather us performing while the audience guesses along with us. The character I play is more like a detective like Wei Si Li; he is constantly analyzing problems and reasoning through cases, unraveling them bit by bit and leading the audience to solve the case. This show is actually a multiple-choice question; everyone around seems to be the enemy, but the presence of a corpse doesn't necessarily mean that person is dead, and someone who appears to have done bad things isn't necessarily a bad person."

Because of his handsome appearance, investors rarely cast Liu Xiaofeng as a villain, which has become a knot in his heart. Liu Xiaofeng inadvertently mentioned a currently popular TV series, "Please Turn On Your Mobile Phone After Landing," believing it to be an excellent script. But at the same time, he boldly declared, "Sun Honglei looks like a bad guy, and he's trying hard to play a good guy. I look like a good guy, so I am more suitable for the role of Shen Kang than Sun Honglei."
