**Top 10 Benefits of Establishing a Company in Hong Kong**

1. **Freedom in Choosing a Company Name**: Company names can include words such as "Academy," "Publishing," "Association," "International," "Group," "Holding," "Industry," etc.

2. **Few Restrictions on Business Scope**: A wide range of business activities are allowed, including finance, accounting, import/export, pharmaceuticals, cultural publishing, information networks, trade, shipping, etc.

3. **Convenient Licensing**: It is extremely convenient to apply for licenses under the name of a Hong Kong company, such as trademarks, publishing house registration, international book numbers (ISBN), journal numbers, foundation licenses, society/association licenses, etc.

4. **No Capital Verification Required**: The registered capital can be increased arbitrarily without requiring full funding, laying the foundation for establishing an international group company.

5. **Permitting Shell Companies**: Hong Kong allows companies to have no active business operations. Establishing a shell company can even serve as a powerful advertising tool.

6. **Joint Ventures with Mainland China**: Partnering with mainland companies or setting up processing plants in mainland China can provide access to various policy incentives.

7. **Low Tax Rates and Few Tax Types**: Hong Kong has low tax rates and few types of taxes. If a company does not make a profit, it does not need to pay taxes. This allows for effective tax planning and reasonable tax avoidance.

8. **Remote Banking Services**: Through electronic banking services, you can manage current accounts, savings accounts, foreign currency accounts, and letters of credit remotely from Hong Kong banks.

9. **Enhanced Brand Image**: Leveraging Hong Kong's reputation, you can create a Hong Kong brand, enhancing your company's international image.

10. **Global Business Window**: A Hong Kong company can act as a window company for domestic enterprises, making it easier to gain trust and cooperation from foreign partners. Hong Kong is one of the freest and most prosperous trading ports in the world, with excellent infrastructure that provides a favorable environment for doing business.

You don't need to personally visit Hong Kong to enjoy prices and services synchronized with Hong Kong.
