by xjyzhenai on 2009-07-31 10:39:21

When you don't have PhostoShop installed on your machine, when you need to deal with several photos in an emergency, you will definitely think of online image beautification processing tools. One for you today: PICNIK

image

PICNIK is a very simple and convenient online image beautification tool. Multiple languages are supported, at least traditional Chinese. PICNIK provides many commonly used photo processing tools such as autocorrect, rotate, crop, resize, exposure adjustment, color adjustment, sharpening tools, red eye removal tools, and more.

image

Of course, there are many features to choose from. But some of them have to be paid for.

image

When the image processing is complete, PICNIK also provides users with storage space that can be used for storage and sharing.

In fact, there are many online applications like PICNIK on our Internet. These online applications have their own merits, they provide us with very simple and convenient tools, so that we can do all kinds of work in a low cost and high efficiency, in fact, this is the core value of Web 2.0. WEB2.0 needs such valuable applications, not slogans and gimmicks every day. Otherwise, WEB2.0 is another Internet bubble.
