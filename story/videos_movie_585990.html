【One Day 1 Horror Movie】 "Friday the 13th" - The Bone-Chilling Crystal Lake Murders!

This film is the first installment of New Line Cinema's most famous horror series - "Friday the 13th", released in 1980. For audiences who prefer newer films, it may seem like an old production. However, its scare tactics are by no means outdated or obsolete. It's clear that the "Friday the 13th" series has maintained its influence up to today for good reason. Back in the day, Paramount Pictures' release of "Friday the 13th" was very well-received by audiences, so they took advantage of its popularity and began releasing a sequel every year. In 1984, the fourth installment was named "The Final Chapter," seemingly bidding farewell to the audience, but due to market demand, it couldn't come to an end. In each episode, Jason either gets buried or sinks into the water; even if he goes to space, he eventually returns to the bottom of Crystal Lake. It's evident that each director leaves a clue to ensure the next story unfolds logically. Faced with the allure of box office revenue, who would dare let him die completely?! ...

Until 2003, audiences could still see masked killer Jason wreak havoc on the big screen, causing screams throughout the theater. And at the end of the movie, Jason comes back to life again, showing that the "Friday the 13th" series still had unlimited potential even in the 21st century. This situation is similar to how writers can't easily kill off successful characters in their works, otherwise, the backlash from readers would be overwhelming. Therefore, the "Friday the 13th" series is hailed as the longest-running horror film series in movie history.
