New share subscription, offline new share subscription

by gollfutures on 2007-11-24 15:14:25

The method of combining offline book-building placement to the pricing objects (hereinafter referred to as "offline placement") with online capital subscription and fixed-price issuance (hereinafter referred to as "online fixed-price issuance"), where offline placement and online fixed-price issuance are carried out simultaneously. The specific schedule is as follows: Offline placement begins earlier than the day before the online issuance (9:30~11:30, 13:00~15:00). Offline placement is organized and implemented by the sponsor (lead underwriter), while online fixed-price issuance is organized by the lead underwriter through the Shenzhen Stock Exchange trading system for online fixed-price issuance.

During the preliminary inquiry period (T-5 to T-3), only those "allocation objects" that submit effective quotes and meet the conditions specified in the "Definitions" section can participate in this offline placement. For investor convenience, eligible inquiry objects are listed in the following table. Only the inquiry objects listed in the table may participate in this offline subscription. However, inquiry objects that have actual control relationships with the issuer or its lead underwriter are not allowed to participate in the stock's inquiry or offline placement but can participate in the online issuance.

Allocation objects participating in the offline subscription must fully pay the subscription amount. Each allocation object participating in the offline subscription must transfer the subscription funds in advance and fax the payment voucher and a copy of the subscription form to the sponsor (lead underwriter).

Allocation objects must ensure that the subscription funds arrive at the designated bank account of the sponsor (lead underwriter) by the specified time. Subscriptions that fail to timely remit the subscription funds according to the above regulations will be considered invalid subscriptions.

The bank account from which the allocation object transfers the subscription funds must match the refund bank information filled in the subscription form and must also match the corresponding capital account registered with the Securities Association of China.
