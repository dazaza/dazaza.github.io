China Textile Engineering Society [2011] No. 82 Notice of the "Jingwei Stock Cup" Third National Cotton Textile Industry Technical Reform Seminar

To textile engineering societies of all provinces, autonomous regions, and municipalities:

To relevant textile enterprises, colleges, and research institutes:

The year 2011 is the opening year of China's "Twelfth Five-Year Plan". In order to better promote technological progress in the textile industry, improve the quality of cotton textile products, promote technical transformation in textile enterprises, increase enterprise benefits, and help cotton spinning enterprises better deal with recruitment difficulties, high cotton prices, and market fluctuations, the China Textile Engineering Society has decided to hold the "Jingwei Stock Cup" Third National Cotton Textile Industry Technical Reform Seminar on December 17-18, 2011. Experts from domestic large-scale well-known cotton spinning enterprises, higher education institutions, and research institutes will be invited to give special lectures, discuss difficulties encountered by enterprises in product development and production practice, share advanced experience, and solve practical problems.

I. Conference Theme

Process Innovation, Advanced Equipment, Reduce Labor, Energy Saving and Environmental Protection

II. Conference Organizing Units

Guidance Unit: China National Textile and Apparel Council

(C) Special Discussions

The conference will organize domestic large-scale well-known cotton textile experts, production technicians, university professors to exchange discussions with representatives on the theme of this meeting. Important discussion topics include:

² Experiences and results of technical reforms in reducing labor force per ten thousand spindles in cotton spinning enterprises;

² Discussions on coarse-fine linkage and fine-drawing linkage technology;

² Relationship between semi-finished product quantification, processes, yarn quality, and economic benefits in each process of cotton spinning;

² Exchanging experiences on using intelligent, informational modern control technologies in spinning equipment to improve process control accuracy and reduce energy consumption;

² Upgrading and key component selection in cotton spinning equipment technical reform;

² How to reasonably adjust production processes to improve yarn quality.

(D) Visits

Conference representatives will visit Fujian Chang Le Zheng Xin Textile Co., Ltd.
