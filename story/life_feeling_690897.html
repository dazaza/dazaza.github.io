If love is missed, will it come again?

My name is Xiaohan. I am 15 years old and in the eighth grade. My studies are pretty good, and I am an ordinary, unremarkable girl. In elementary school, I liked a boy from our class. His name was Yi. He had the air of a prince, was a top student, excelled at playing the violin, and was also good at basketball. More importantly, he was very easygoing, so there were many girls who pursued him. Although I liked him, I never told him because I knew he would not choose me, and I did not want to humiliate myself.

When I graduated from elementary school, I chose a junior high school not far from my home. After being assigned to a new class with new classmates, I found that I didn't know anyone, and neither Yi nor I were in the same class. I felt very disheartened. On the registration day, I was pleasantly surprised to see Feng. Feng was in the same class as me in elementary school and was a top student, but we didn’t interact much. However, at least I knew someone. Feng sat in front of me, which made me feel very close. Strangely, Feng had been admitted to an excellent junior high school, but he told me he had a reason for choosing this school, though he wouldn’t tell me what it was, and I didn’t ask further.

Feng and I gradually became familiar with each other. I regarded him as my best friend and told him everything, whether good or bad. He always helped me, both academically and in daily life. Being with him made me happy and secure. After school or during breaks, I was always accustomed to being with him.

Later, I don't know why the leader of our class, Yu, suddenly started bothering me. He always bullied me and played tricks on me. Feng always wanted to stand up for me, but I stopped him every time because I didn't want him to lose his image as a good student in the eyes of the teacher due to me. Unexpectedly, Yu even threatened me to go out with him. Out of helplessness, I had to agree.

After being with Yu, I slowly realized that he wasn't so detestable. He treated me well, so I was gradually moved by him and fell in love with him. Being with Yu made me happy and content, and I thought we could be together forever. But our relationship only lasted a month. A month later, Yu suddenly broke up with me, saying he had fallen in love with Ni. Ni was the school belle. I begged him not to leave me, but he left anyway.

The day he broke up with me was at school. I was heartbroken, sad, and in pain. I finally couldn't hold back my tears. The next class was history, and I kept crying while resting my head on the desk. Feng kept passing notes to comfort me, but I continued to cry. It felt like my heart was being crushed underfoot, and it hurt so much.

After class, Feng turned around and said to me, "Xiaohan, stop crying. Crying for someone who doesn't love you isn't worth it."

"But I really feel so sad. I don't understand why. I don't know where I went wrong. Why did he break up with me? Why did he choose Ni? Is it because Ni is prettier than me?"

"It's not like that. You're prettier than Ni. He doesn't know how to cherish you. He doesn't understand your goodness. Losing you is his misfortune."

"You're lying. If I'm prettier than Ni, then why is she the school belle? And why do so many people chase after her?"

"I'm not lying. What I said is true. Xiaohan, please don't be sad anymore, alright? Breaking up isn't a big deal. In the future, you'll find someone who truly loves you."

"Feng, thank you." Listening to Feng's words, I felt warmth.

Later, I started dating other boys, maybe to forget about Yu. Fortunately, I finally forgot about Yu, but I didn't fall in love with those boys either. Gradually, my actions upset some people. They began to say I was fickle, shameless, and playing with others' feelings. I pretended not to care, but inside, I felt very dissatisfied and wronged.

"Xiaohan, no matter what others say, I will believe in you. I understand your grievances, so whatever you do, I will support you. Don't worry about what others say; they don't understand." When I was troubled by those words, Feng was always by my side comforting me.

"Thank you, Feng. You are the best friend I've ever had."

I always thought that Feng and I would remain friends forever, but I realized I was wrong. At the end of the eighth grade, Yi gave me a letter. Feng asked him to give it to me and said that Feng had transferred schools (Feng and Yi were good friends). When I heard this, I felt like my whole world had collapsed.

The moment I opened the letter, I was stunned. I never expected that Feng had liked me since elementary school. He came to this school because of me. He said that when he saw me being bullied by Yu, he really wanted to step forward and protect me, but he knew he didn't have the right. He said that the last time he told me I was prettier than Ni was sincere; in his heart, I was the most beautiful person in the world. He said that every time I dated other boys, although his heart ached, he still blessed me because as long as I was happy, that was enough for him. He said that rather than breaking this window pane, it was better to be friends with me for life.

After reading the letter, I didn't know why I cried, feeling suffocated and unable to breathe.

"Xiaohan, do you know how sad Feng was every time he learned you were dating someone else? He even cried several times in front of me. Now he plans to forget about you, so he has transferred schools. He asked me to tell you that you two are lifelong friends," Yi said and then left.

At this moment, I felt so helpless that I squatted down and burst into tears. Why did my heart feel so painful, like I was dying?

Feng, I was wrong. Will you come back? I understand now—I like you, but I didn't realize it myself. Feng, I can't live without you. What should I do without you by my side? Who will protect me? Who will care for me? Who will comfort me? Feng, it's only now that I realize how important you are to me.

Feng, I want to tell you that I love you, but it's too late...

Feng, when I realized I liked you, you had already left me behind and decided to forget about me.

Our feelings were missed, so our love was also missed...

Since that's the case, Feng, let's agree to be lifelong friends! Forgive me, Feng. Before, I told you all my secrets, but this time, I can't tell you the secret that I like you.
