"Reward Event" AIRPLAY User Experience Open Evaluation

by ybcz0519 on 2011-11-20 19:10:27

I. Introduction to AirPlay

AirPlay (shortened as AP, Chinese name: Fei Le) is a compact free audio player with an excellent user experience. It's the software I use most often to listen to music on my Windows system. If you're not familiar with this software, you can check out more details here.

Link: http://www.airplay3.com/

II. Event Methodology

To better improve AIRPLAY, AIRPLAY invites everyone to participate in an open evaluation. AIRPLAY has simultaneously released new versions for both PC and mobile platforms. Simply download and use the latest version of AIRPLAY on either platform to participate in the evaluation. Write down your various experiences and suggestions while using AIRPLAY, or compare AIRPLAY with one or multiple familiar similar software products and write an evaluation article for a chance to win great prizes.

III. Event Time

October 20, 2011 ~ November 20, 2011

IV. Participation Methods

1. Talk about your feelings: Write an original article about your insights and suggestions on using AIRPLAY (can include but is not limited to practical tips, overall software impressions, functional improvement suggestions, etc.).

2. Write reviews: Create an original review article comparing AIRPLAY with one or multiple familiar similar software products (can include but is not limited to interface design, operational logic, functional usage, sound quality, etc.).

3. Publish your feeling-based articles or review articles on any public website (personal blog, review site, etc.), and record the valid URL that directly accesses the article content.

4. Post on Sina Weibo with the content #AIRPLAY Activity# + your article title and link address, and mention @AIRPLAY User Experience Center. Then forward the original Weibo post via private message to AIRPLAY User Experience Center.

5. Post on Tuita with the content #AIRPLAY Activity# + your article title and link address, and mention @AIRPLAY. Then send the original Weibo post link via private message to AIRPLAY.

Note: To participate via Sina Weibo or Tuita, both conditions must be met: mentioning the official designated account and sending a private message to the official designated account.

6. Users without Sina Weibo or Tuita can send the article title and link address to the official event email exp@airplayme.com (this email is only used to receive participation emails; please set up a read receipt when sending emails so we can notify you as soon as we receive your email). The email subject should follow this format: AIRPLAY Experience Event - Your Article Title.

7. There is no limit to the number of articles each participant can submit within the effective time of the event.

V. Award Settings

1. Most Valuable Award: We will invite industry experts or partners to evaluate the collected articles. According to the effective weeks within the event time, we will alternate weekly between selecting the most valuable feeling-based article or review article, with a prize worth around 500 RMB.

2. Excellent Award: Two articles will be selected each week as excellent awards, with a prize worth around 100 RMB.

3. Lucky Draw: At the end of the event, one author from all participants in the AIRPLAY user experience essay will be drawn to receive a prize worth around 500 RMB.

VI. Prize Details

1. Winners of the Most Valuable Award can freely choose one of the following prizes:

- Apple (Apple) iPod new shuffle MP3 player (random color)

- Creative (Creative) Sound Card Sound Blaster X-Fi Surround 5.1 Pro

- Bambook 6-inch electronic ink screen e-book (built-in Wi-Fi, random color)

- AKG K430P over-ear headphones (random color)

- Musiland Monitor 01 US external USB sound card

2. Winners of the Excellent Award can freely choose one of the following prizes:

- Philips (Philips) SHE6000 in-ear headphones (random color)

- Sony (SONY) MDR-Q21LP headphones (random color)

3. The lucky draw winner will randomly receive one of the Most Valuable Award prizes.

VII. Prize Collection Method

Follow Sina Weibo AIRPLAY User Experience Center or Tuita AIRPLAY. During the event period, every Saturday, the list of winners will be announced on Weibo and @the users. The users who are mentioned should inform their name, phone number, postal code, and address via private message. The list of winners will also be published on the official website accordingly.
