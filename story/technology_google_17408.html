Preface

Dear Reader,

Thank you for purchasing the expanded second edition of "What Google Doesn't Tell You: The Trilogy to Making Big Money with Adsense"!

The content of this book focuses on advanced applications of Google Adsense. Therefore, I assume that the readers of this book already have a basic understanding of Adsense while writing it. Don't let that statement intimidate you because this book is indeed very simple and easy to understand.

If you're new to Adsense, you may need to refer to the Adsense Support Pages or an online glossary index for help during your reading.

I've tried to keep the structure of this book concise and focus the main content on how to increase your Adsense income. In this book, you will find some very practical solutions, such as attracting targeted traffic, enhancing content relevance, improving the visibility of Adsense ads, etc., which are issues website owners care about and often find challenging. Many people (including myself) have benefited greatly by using some simple and legitimate methods presented in this book.

No matter what type of website you run or what your website's content is, you'll find many helpful ways here to significantly increase your Adsense income.

In this book, I will teach you all the techniques I use without reservation. These techniques once increased my Adsense income tenfold almost overnight. You can try applying these techniques yourself and see how effective they are.

If you were expecting a large book in the style of Dummies, with entire chapters dedicated to explaining "What exactly is Adsense?" or "A Brief History of Content-Related Ads," then this little booklet might come as a surprise. The reason I wrote this book this way is that I don't want readers to lose interest before they reach the truly useful material. After all, this is the first time I'm openly sharing these proven techniques to improve Adsense click-through rates.

Isn't that why you bought this book?

If so, then you won't be disappointed.

Of course, you can find a lot of related knowledge and skills for free online. I don't deny their existence and value. In fact, if you want to start from scratch, there's no better way than Google's own Adsense quick guide.

You might want to print it out and carry it around, referring to it when you have questions. Why pay for something you can get for free? Just go directly to Google!

Actually, this book isn't entirely useless for beginners. In this revised edition, I've added a small section at the beginning as an introduction for newcomers. If you don't have a website yet, I'll teach you how to create one, how to put it online, and how to start earning money with Adsense as soon as possible. If you already have all that and just want to know how to earn more, you can skip those sections and go straight to the core part. Using Google Adsense is easy; the hard part is actually making money with it. That is ultimately the purpose of this book.

Readers of the first edition sent me many emails asking questions like "Where can I find free information?", "How do I optimize my web pages for search engines?", "How can I bring traffic to my site?" etc. In this edition, I've added chapters on search engine optimization, gaining website traffic, content writing, and ad formats, along with a host of new suggestions and tips. All of these can be applied immediately to yield results.

I guarantee that what this book offers is something you won't find elsewhere. My Adsense success story is — from a pitiful $3/day to an explosive $600/day — Adsense paid off my housing mortgage, car loans, internet fees, and much more. All of this has taught me increasingly valuable experiences and methods for making money with my own websites.

Every page of this book is filled with little-known Adsense secrets, tricks, tips, and effective strategies, all generously shared by successful website publishers who are willing to share their experiences.

Read and apply these methods. Remember to let me know the results!

You can email me anytime at joel.comm@adsense-secrets.com. I love seeing them "in action"!

In this rapidly evolving content-related advertising industry, your unique questions and real-world results will help keep the content of future editions of this book timely and relevant. Thank you sincerely for your contributions!

Wishing you more Adsense income,

Joel Comm
