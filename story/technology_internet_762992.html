Gross Monthly Revenue Breaks Through 12 Million USD

by anonymous on 2013-07-29 12:05:46

This morning, Chen Haozhi, CEO of Chukong, sent out a message on Weibo and WeChat to his circle of friends. According to Chen Haozhi, "Chukong is expected to have a monthly income of 12 million US dollars in July," and he generously disclosed the revenue figures for July 14th, where the daily revenue reached an astonishing 2.87 million RMB. Calculating by this single-day standard, Chukong's July income is indeed expected to break through 70 million RMB, and the anticipated scale of 12 million US dollars in monthly income also sets a new industry high.

Chen Haozhi stated: "The authenticity of the income can be verified using audited data if necessary, but the following data can be cross-checked from multiple sources. There are many doubts about the bubble in the mobile game industry, the authenticity of the income, and even recently, this massive acquisition has been questioned. However, there are few people who recognize the explosive development of this industry, its healthy ecosystem, and the diversity value of products."

How did Chukong's income grow? Gamelook reviewed the historical records of Chukong's disclosed income, which were sequentially 30 million RMB in February, 50 million RMB in April, and 72 million RMB in July. The "Time Hunter" game distributed by Chukong was released publicly on iOS on March 6th this year, "I'm MT" launched its Android version on April 26th, and the iOS version of "Rando Fight" started gaining momentum in May.

In July, from the income table, it can be seen that the two highest-income single-player games (the Fishing Joy series) from Chukong had a target income of 38.21 million RMB. Comparing this July's fishing game income with the 30 million RMB in February, although there is still some growth, it is not much. If user volume plays a decisive role in product income, then increasing user volume can pull up income whether it's a single-player or online game. But the Fishing Joy game hasn't been able to significantly increase income over the past six months. The main driver of Chukong's growth in the first half of the year was its distribution business. In simple terms, the domestic single-game product, whether single-player or online, has entered a state of capping monthly income. A relatively effective measure to continue to rapidly expand income may be to distribute products.

In this chart, below are the daily revenue figures for each product, and each income figure has a completion rate. We can simply calculate the parts that Chukong didn't clearly specify.

Chukong's target income for July is: 72.44 million RMB

Revenue completed in the first 14 days of July: 34.93 million RMB

Completion rate of revenue for the first 14 days of July: 48.23%

Actual average daily revenue for the first 14 days of July: 2.495 million RMB

Comparing the July 14th income of 2.87 million RMB with the average daily income of 2.495 million RMB over the first 14 days, it shows that Chen Haozhi isn't just showing off a peak day's income created by activities, but rather maintaining around 2.5 million RMB daily.

The target income for three mobile online games in July is: 17.41 million RMB

The target income for seven single-player games in July is: 46.48 million RMB

Subtracting the target income difference for July is approximately 8 million RMB. In this table, there are three online games and two single-player games without written target incomes. The unwritten single-player products have a monthly income target below 1 million RMB, meaning the remaining three unwritten online games have an average monthly income of around 2 million RMB.

Chukong's mobile online games include one at 8 million RMB, one at 6.15 million RMB, one at 3.3 million RMB, and three others with nearly 2 million RMB monthly income.

Chukong's single-player games include one at 31.5 million RMB, one at 6.76 million RMB, one at 3.3 million RMB, one at 2.62 million RMB, two at the million level, and two with less than a million RMB monthly income.

From the income completion rate, after 14 days of a 31-day month, the normal completion rate should be 45%, compared to the income progress in the table.

One mobile online game exceeds the target, while two fall short of the income target.

Six single-player mobile games exceed the target.

From this perspective, Chukong has very rich experience in operating single-player games, continuously setting new highs in single-player mobile game income. In terms of online games, the top performer is undoubtedly "Time Hunter" exceeding income expectations, followed by "Rando Fight," indicating that Chukong has exceptional performance, as well as cases where fierce competition leads to falling short of targets, which is normal.
