Female master, don't let chastity take away your "sexual happiness"?

Recently, there has been a very popular news story about Tu Shiyou, a 38-year-old woman from Ezhou, Hubei, who spent a thousand yuan to create the "Chastity Website" in an attempt to persuade people to preserve premarital chastity. To prove herself, she even posted her virginity certification report from Wuhan Tongji Hospital on Weibo. This has caused many comments. Naturally, for today's society, talking about sexual issues is no longer done shyly, and the concept of "chastity" is becoming more indifferent. For many men, they hope that the women who will be with them in the future will have them as their first and only sexual partner, like LED street lights. However, there is a huge contradiction here. The sages said, "Correct oneself before correcting others." When a man demands something from a woman, he should also ask himself if he can achieve it. Therefore, we always find that many people set high requirements for themselves but ignore their own behavior. We residents of our humble abode, roaming through the streets and alleys, often see such scenes: when a man goes out partying, it's called being romantic; even if caught by his wife, everyone will defend him, saying "Men inevitably indulge in casual encounters." But when a woman goes out on a date, it's called being vulgar, criticized by all, and people would love to drown her in spittle! In this society advocating gender equality, thinking about it, it seems quite laughable!

Chastity, for many people today, has already been thrown to the nine heavens. As for Master Tu's actions, they are clearly incompatible with this society. If for her personally, it's indeed not easy to maintain chastity at the age of 38, but these are merely personal behaviors. One must know that people in the world come in all shapes and sizes, each different. And the pursuit of sexual desire, most mortals cannot escape its entanglement. Therefore, Master Tu guarding her chastity can be proud of it, but she cannot use it to demand the same from others. Some say that her behavior is a resistance against the current societal sexual promiscuity, a silent rebellion. From the perspective of today's societal "sexual" behavior, it is indeed a terrible thing. Many people not only lack a sense of shame but also dignity. But these are just bad habits in society, accumulated over a long time. And Master Tu's behavior cannot change this society because of this.

In today's era, people not only pursue happiness but also "sexual happiness." Master Tu's behavior can be said to constrain herself with chastity, taking away the "sexual happiness" she herself possesses, perhaps because she hasn't met her ideal sexual partner. But her behavior of using chastity to verify her identity and establishing the Chastity Website is not worth promoting. Whether it's turning back the clock on history, we won't discuss for now. Master Tu's behavior is merely requiring others to be like her based on her personal experiences. On the other hand, whether she verifies her virginity or establishes the Chastity Website, they are personal matters. But now, loudly publicizing them changes their meaning.

Guarding chastity, in this materialistic society, is indeed a rare feat, but one cannot therefore exclude those who lose their chastity but gain "sexual happiness." Everyone pursues different things in life. You can guard your purity, and others can equally pursue their own vulgarity. Don't think that because you guard your chastity, you appear noble while others seem vulgar. These are completely two different things! Therefore, I want to say to Master Tu, if you want to marry, don't let chastity take away your "sexual happiness." Whether you adhere to celibacy or remain chaste until old age, that's a personal matter. Don't mislead society with your personal preferences and behaviors.
