Wuxi Setex: Spintech Instruments Sold Overseas

Introduction: 2011 was a year of harvest for Wuxi Setex Huaguang Spintech Company. The company's director informed reporters that Zhejiang Jianke Group Textile Co., Ltd. had already ordered more than 60 spintech instruments from Setex this year, while the Wantai Group in Zaozhuang, Shandong also ordered nearly 20 spintech instruments.

To date, the textile instrument products of the company have been officially used in over 300 textile enterprises nationwide. Renowned companies such as Weiqiao Textile in Shandong, Huamao Textile in Anhui, Nanyang Textile in Henan, and Changsha Second Textile Printing & Dyeing Co., Ltd. in Hunan are all regular customers of Setex. It is understood that the market share of the company's products in the Shandong market alone reaches 80% to 90%. In peak periods, the sales of the combing intelligent self-leveling instrument can reach over 20 units per month. Additionally, Setex products are also exported to Southeast Asian markets, with export products accounting for about 30% of the total production volume.

Setex has actively optimized and integrated existing market resources according to current market conditions. In March this year, they acquired Wuxi Fifth Rubber Factory, specializing in conveyor belts, rubber, and waterproof materials.

According to introductions, Setex’s spintech instruments mainly feature three patented products: electronic yarn clearer, intelligent slub control device, and combing intelligent self-leveling instrument. Among them, the S3000-F Electronic Yarn Clearer is suitable for ordinary winding machines and has functions such as absolute fault detection, online grading, length statistics, printing, online detection, fault alarm, and automatic temperature and humidity compensation.

The STH-2008 Intelligent Slub Control Device features up to 100 to 200 groups of slub yarns, suitable for various ordinary or medium-long fibers or blends. The yarn count generally ranges from 5 to 80, and any type of yarn can be spun, whether it follows a pattern, is short, medium, or long, or has arbitrary spacing. The shortest slub length is 2.0 cm, with no upper limit on the longest length, and the maximum thickness is six times that of the original yarn.

The Setex Combing Intelligent Self-Leveling Instrument uses newly designed film button operations, making control simple and easy. The operation panel allows convenient forward and reverse operations. The control circuit board is compactly and reasonably designed with a high degree of integration. This leveling instrument achieves a weight positive roll rate of 99%, a cotton roll unevenness rate of 1%, a spinning cotton weight unevenness rate of around 0.8, and a chemical fiber weight unevenness rate between 0.8 to 1.2. More than 95% of the components of this self-leveling instrument are imported parts, ensuring reliable and convenient external connections.
