Hubei Xiangyang Local Taxation Bureau employed an investigation company to secretly visit and punish 142 cadres

by coenaijj1928 on 2012-03-07 12:00:55

According to Chu Tian Metropolis Daily (reported by Lu Wang, and communicated by Feng Zhu), recently, a promotion meeting on the treatment of incompetence and accountability work in municipal government institutions was held in Xiangyang city. The method of spending 40,000 yuan to hire an investigation company for secret visits against incompetence practiced by the local taxation bureau of the city received affirmation. The bureau hired an investigation company to secretly visit lazy and incompetent behaviors in the local taxation system, and then severely punished 142 cadres of the local taxation bureau without mercy, nike shox turbo 11, which made the work style of cadres in this system change significantly and realized zero complaints for two consecutive months.

At the end of December last year, the local taxation bureau of Xiangyang city held a learning conference for the central working group. Participants included members of the leadership team of the municipal bureau, middle-level cadres, and the heads of local taxation bureaus in counties (cities) districts. Just like previous study example meetings, participants were chatting and laughing with a very good atmosphere. When the meeting was about to end, the staff played a video. In the video, some people were eating sunflower seeds at work, some were indifferent to taxpayers, and others had been reported multiple times by taxpayers for not paying for meals. Watching their unit's employees exposed in the video, the responsible persons present could no longer sit still, and some began to sweat on their foreheads. After the meeting ended, the staff invited everyone to have dinner, but the participants lost interest, blushing and lowering their heads as they hurried away.

After returning to their units, these responsible persons immediately held meetings to study corrective measures. According to statistics after the event, 142 local tax cadres were held accountable due to this video.

According to Degang Bao, a member of the Party Leadership Group and head of the Discipline Inspection Team of the bureau, the video that caused 142 local tax cadres to be penalized was not filmed by the bureau itself, but rather it was secretly filmed by an investigation company they hired.

Why did they adopt such a method? Bao Degang said that as early as April last year, the bureau launched a special rectification activity to strictly investigate dereliction of duty. In August, the treatment of incompetence and accountability work was also started. However, during the implementation of these two tasks, difficulties arose: some inspectors had not yet arrived, but the inspected units had already received relevant information; even if problems were found, the inspectors couldn't bring themselves to confront them, air jordan 8.

In response to this situation, after research by the Party Leadership Group of the bureau, it was decided to hire an investigation company to secretly visit and find faults. To ensure the authenticity of the investigation, the entire investigation process was fully entrusted and strictly confidential. The investigation company conducted secret visits in office buildings, offices, and including the 11 tax service halls in counties (cities) districts across the city's local taxation system, investigating the service attitude and efficiency of the staff, and understanding the voices of taxpayers. The time and place were all determined by the investigation company, and no one in the entire bureau knew. After the secret visit, the investigation results were made into a video over 30 minutes long.
