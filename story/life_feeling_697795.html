To the person who will be with me years from now

by yesebaby5s5j on 2012-03-08 17:47:05

I am very ordinary, and once I am with you, I will absolutely not like anyone else.

I won't think about anyone else. I will love you with all that I have.

I don't need you to remember many dates, but at least you must remember your parents' birthdays.

I don't need a lot of money, but at least our life must be very satisfying.

I don't need you to be any certain way, but at least you should know what you are doing.

No matter how busy you are, at least you must visit your parents twice a month.

You can be unromantic, but you cannot be ignorant. I can do without anything, but at least a ring, which doesn't have to be diamond.

I am stubborn. When we argue, I hope you can come and apologize to me. If I am wrong, I will obediently beg for your forgiveness without you saying.

I am capricious and I need your tolerance.

I want nothing,

I can learn to do more housework, but you can't do nothing either.

I can do without anything, but at least when you come home, you can give me a hug.

I may not be the best one, but I will definitely be the one you like the most and suit you the most.

I may make mistakes, but you cannot not tell me.

I may talk a bit too much, but you should know that this is because I care about you, I am concerned about you.

I hope there is at least trust between the two of us.

I hope we can make cakes on our birthdays, then messily smear cream on each other's faces.

I hope when I am tired, you can say let me carry you.

I hope when I am sick, you can be by my side. If there is really something important, I will understand if you are not by my side.

I hope when cooking, I buy the ingredients, you cook. If we go together, then we buy the ingredients together, but you still cook.

I wash the dishes, you clean the plates. I clean the chopsticks, you clean the pot.

I hope you let me accompany you to buy your clothes, because in my eyes, you look the best.

I hope we can go to the amusement park, because I want to ride the carousel and Ferris wheel with you.

I think we can watch a movie once a month. We must eat popcorn and drink lemonade.

I think when I am sad and want to cry, you can hold me in your arms and say that I like your smiling face.

I think you are my only support. When I want to tell you something from my heart, you must listen carefully.

I think you will also get tired sometimes. You should remember that someone is silently cheering for you in her heart, even though she doesn't say it.

I said, if we are together, then the road of being together is the end, it's a lifetime. You are my man.

I can do without so much care, but at least let me feel that you have me in your heart.

I like eating egg tarts, I like lemonade. These are hobbies I cannot change, so you need to understand me.

I like listening to music, I like words. Occasionally, I also write.

I may not be able to help you in many ways, but you should know that I have always been supporting you, believing in you, and never changing.

I can do without a lot of clothes, but my clothes must be ones you like.

When we take a walk, you must hold my hand and let me feel that you are always with me.

I am not very good at caring for others. If there is anything in some matters that makes you feel upset, I will change, I will definitely change.

I want to hear you say "I love you", and you must unconditionally comply.

I hope you remember that love is something that two people cultivate with their hearts.

I will work hard, so that you won't be so tired and pressured.

We must have at least one house of our own. It doesn't have to be big, but it must be sweet and full of furniture that both you and I like.

There must be a big balcony. I like sitting by your side enjoying the sun, watching the sunrise and sunset.

We must also raise a puppy. It doesn't have to be expensive, but it must be one that both of us like.

We must also grow a lot of plants, and watch them grow together. I like green.

We must also have a camera, to record our simple little lives.

I want a message board, to write down our daily wishes.

I can do without a lot of money, but you shouldn't be so tired.

These are things I hope you understand.

That's a lifetime.
