Whitening masks are the top choice for whitening. First, if you want to give your skin the most thorough whitening care, then whitening masks should be your first choice. Here, I will teach you an effective yet economical homemade whitening mask. The ingredients include: 5 grams each of silver ear fungus, Huangshi, Angelica dahurica, Poria cocos, and Polygonatum odoratum. Grind them into fine powder, mix with 5 grams of flour, and adjust with water. Carefully apply it on your skin, leave it on for 30 minutes, then wash it off. After using, you'll find your skin hydrated and whitened, becoming healthier with a faint aroma of Chinese herbs. Whitening experts remind us that after using the mask, you must quickly follow up with whitening skincare products. Professional whitening products like "Xinsiyuan" are highly recommended, allowing your skin to retain its whiteness and become even more beautiful.

Apple Whitening Hydrating Mask

Effect: Moisturizing and replenishing hydration

Ingredients: 1 apple, 2 tablespoons of honey

Method: 01. Cut the apple evenly into 4 pieces, put them all into a blender and blend them into juice, add honey and mix evenly.

02. Place in the refrigerator to chill, take out after about 10 minutes.

Usage: 01. Lightly pat the mixture onto your entire face until it feels slightly sticky.

02. Keep it on for about 30 minutes, then rinse clean with water.

TIPS: This mask is suitable for normal skin types. It is recommended to use twice a week for the first 3 weeks, then once a week thereafter for better results.

Watermelon Peel Honey Whitening Hydrating Mask

Effect: Post-sunburn repair, calming the skin

Ingredients: 1 piece of watermelon peel, an appropriate amount of honey

Method: Mix watermelon peel juice with honey to form a mask.

Usage: Use a mask brush to dip into this mask, directly apply it on the face for about 25 minutes. Rinse after full absorption.

TIPS: After sun exposure, the skin usually feels uncomfortable, which indicates that the skin needs soothing before further treatment. The watermelon peel honey mask can hydrate and cool down the face, helping to soothe the skin.
