The "Anti-Time-Travel Alliance" is a coalition established by the United Nations in response to the increasing number of time travelers going back to ancient times. These time travelers, either maliciously or unintentionally, alter the established history, causing more or less deviation. When these deviations stray too far from true history, the real world will develop in another direction, and the modern society developed based on past history will vanish.

To prevent history from going astray and to preserve the modern society that humans rely on, members of the Anti-Time-Travel Alliance can only travel through time and fight in various dynasties, rooting out these time travelers one by one.

Recently, the Anti-Time-Travel Alliance has detected serious deviations in the history of the early Han Dynasty, so three members, including Gu Yu, have been dispatched to the early Han period to investigate the situation and then correct history, catching those time travelers who maliciously tamper with history and bringing them back to the present day.
