2009 has begun, what should I write? I don't know, let me just think randomly and write as thoughts come to me! It's been a year since I started working, and I've gained a lot. Let me start from the annual summary meeting this time. Although I didn't win the individual excellence award, it's because my performance wasn't as good as others. However, when I went on stage to speak, the feelings were mixed - I was so frustrated when my name was drawn. But then I thought, it was a rare opportunity to go on stage. And then I realized there were more than three hundred people below, including the company presidents (there were three of them), N managers (N>20), our trainers, lecturers... Anyway, there were a lot of people, and I was extremely nervous. When I got on stage, I found myself even more excited and nervous. I took deep breaths many times, but my heart was still pounding at over a hundred beats per minute. Since I was going up, I might as well read from the script. I thought I could take more than five minutes, but unexpectedly, including getting on and off the stage, I used less than five minutes. Haha, but the audience reacted positively, generally commenting that this trainer didn't talk nonsense on stage, haha! To be honest, it was indeed an exercise, a rare opportunity, and I felt great, hahaha!!

After returning, I kept thinking about the differences between who I am now and who I was a year ago. After much thought, it boils down to one sentence: I've grown up (hehe, saying nonsense again), become more mature, learned some methods for dealing with people and situations, and understood some principles of being a person. Although it's tiring, it's worth it!

Fidelity Red Whirlwind; these days I've spent a lot of money, and I've summarized one sentence: life is about being happy!! As the saying goes, money can't buy happiness, don't think about anything, just be happy!!

Let me also talk about something I've wanted to say all year in 2008. 2008 was a touching year, from the heavy snow at the beginning of the year, the *** incident in March, the heart-shaking earthquake in May, to the long-awaited Olympics in August... Every event was touching and unforgettable. The heavy snow blocked the way home for travelers, but it couldn't block their hearts longing to return home; although the *** separatists tried to split the world, the united Chinese people's hearts would not be split by these scum; in May, that earthquake shook the Chinese land, but we could see the actions of our country and people. Remember, May 12, 2008, at 14:28, an unforgettable moment, in the Taibao workplace of Tongguan, all the tables and chairs were shaking violently, I was nervous. When I ran down from the fourth floor, honestly, I thought about everything, how to self-rescue if I managed to get out, what to do if I couldn't get out, and more... The 3 o'clock news reported a 7.6 magnitude earthquake in Wenchuan, Sichuan, two minutes later it became 7.8, and after a day, it became 8.0. Everyone's heart was tied to Wenchuan, Sichuan at that moment. Premier Wen flew to Sichuan, the People's Liberation Army moved out, walking into Wenchuan, one was saved, another was saved... One after another moving moments, I also wanted to become a volunteer, but reality forced me to only donate money to the disaster area, contributing my small strength. I remember once driving from home to Tongguan, seeing hundreds of ambulances and supply vehicles heading towards the disaster area on the highway, which was another kind of moving experience; Wenchuan, a place that will forever be remembered by Chinese descendants! On August 8th, the Olympics opened, the whole world was watching, our pride, moved again thoroughly! I just want to say, thank you, my motherland; thank you, PLA!!

I don't know what else to say, so let's talk about 2009. In 2009, I need to learn to be strong, to arm myself, armed like a big wolf or a hedgehog, no more words, I don't like playing around!

That's it, I don't want to talk anymore...
