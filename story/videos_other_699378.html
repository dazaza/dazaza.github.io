Sincerely hope that there is a good man to hold your hand.

by yayun71q47 on 2012-03-09 14:44:16

I sincerely hope that a good man will hold your hand and accompany you with a Zen heart. "Who will hold my hand and alleviate my lifelong loneliness; who will kiss my eyes and shield me from the vicissitudes of life; who will caress my face and console my half-life of sorrow; who will support my shoulder and drive away my lifelong silence; who can understand my intentions and make me live without regret." I sincerely hope that a good man will promise you happiness for a lifetime, correct drooping eyelids, accompany you until eternity, hold your hand, grow old with you, and in the setting sun, he can watch the world's changes with you and count the years together. He will endure the hardships of life with you. I sincerely hope for a good man to embrace you and give you deep affection. I sincerely hope for a good man to be by your side and make you live without regrets; I sincerely hope for a good man to protect you and shield you from life's storms with a Zen heart. Perhaps you might lament that it's hard to find a good man. Men with talent are ugly; those who look handsome earn little; those who earn much don't care about their family; those who do care about their family are unremarkable; those who are remarkable aren't romantic; those who are romantic can't be relied upon; those who can be relied upon are wimps. I am silently praying, sincerely hoping you find a good man sooner. This good man, most importantly, should love you and cherish you, have you in his heart. He should have some talent, allowing for intellectual exchanges with you; he should have a sense of humor, able to say things that make you laugh; he should be somewhat handsome, making you proud to go out with him among friends, satisfying your desire to see a handsome man often; he should earn enough money, allowing you to live a prosperous life; he should have a sense of responsibility, taking care of the small family you build together; he should have some romance, often giving you romantic gestures, making you feel that life is truly wonderful.
