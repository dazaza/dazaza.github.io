XPS board; During the interviews, reporters found that designers generally believed: "At present, the title of 'City of Design' seems to have limited impact on the advancement of the design industry. Meanwhile, as design becomes more closely intertwined with commerce, it also appears to be increasingly restless. Manufacturers require design companies to produce prototype models within two weeks. Just imagine how precise such designs can be, but in order to survive, these design companies have no choice but to accept such orders. This is the current situation."

Kong Hongqiang said that the prosperity of Shenzhen's industries has provided a broad platform for its prototype model industrial design. "It can be said that Shenzhen has the most enterprises involved in industrial design nationwide, but they lack originality." According to the introduction, eighty percent of Shenzhen's industrial design enterprises mainly focus on mobile phone design, and among them, many are engaged in the design of knock-off phones. Moreover, the development of Shenzhen's knock-off enterprises represented by mobile phones is closely related to industrial design, which is not beneficial for the growth of designers. "The threshold for industrial design is relatively low, and a large number of new design enterprises have grown like 'mushrooms after rain'. After continuous integration, the design industry will become stronger for the strong and weaker for the weak," Kong Hongqiang said. How can the prototype model design industry grow bigger and stronger? Kong Hongqiang proposed the concept of creating a "century-long design."
