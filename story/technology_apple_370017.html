iPhone4 delivery to your door! Apple's official Chinese online store is launching today.

by shootero on 2010-11-01 13:25:21

iPhone 4 Delivery to Your Doorstep! Apple's China Online Store Officially Opens - Today, Apple's online store (http://store.apple.com/cn/) officially launched! On this platform, we can basically find any Apple product, including laser engraving and Mac customization services. The page indicates that the delivery time for the iPhone 4 is one to two weeks, covering most areas of the country. If supplies are sufficient ("if" reflects the author's preparedness for disappointment), the iPhone 4 will enter a popularization phase (which might make gray market dealers shed tears...).

The advertisement for this online store on Apple's official website is very impressive!

Cities supported by Apple for initial door-to-door delivery include: Beijing, Changchun, Changzhou, Kunshan, Nanjing, Nantong, Suzhou, Wuxi, Xuzhou, Yangzhou, Chengdu, Dalian, Fuzhou, Xiamen, Dongguan, Guangzhou, Huizhou, Shantou, Shenzhen, Zhongshan, Zhuhai, Guiyang, Harbin, Haikou, Hangzhou, Jiaxing, Ningbo, Shaoxing, Wenzhou, Hefei, Wuhu, Wuhan, Xiangfan, Jinan, Qingdao, Weifang, Yantai, Kunming, Shanghai, Tianjin, and Chongqing. (The number of supported cities will continue to increase in the future!)

Each customer is limited to purchasing one unit, and the shipping time of one to two weeks is acceptable.

Although Apple's Chinese official website had previously provided pre-order services for the iPhone 4, it was not yet in the form of a fully functional online store with as complete a range of products as now. Currently, from this page, we can see that Apple also offers a large number of third-party accessories for consumers in China.

The white version of the iPhone 4 remains elusive.

In comparison, this webpage has already aligned its functionality settings with Apple's English and Hong Kong official websites, all adopting the same free shipping promotional policy. As for the iPhones everyone is concerned about, both the 8GB version of the 3GS and the black version of the iPhone 4 will start shipping within 1-2 weeks after placing an order. However, the white version of the iPhone 4 is still not available for pre-order at the moment.

The delivery time for the fourth-generation Touch is only 1-3 days!

Moreover, regarding other highly anticipated Apple products: the new Air model currently does not accept orders, while the fourth-generation Touch requires only 1-3 working days for delivery.
