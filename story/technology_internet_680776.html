Two men were caught in three days for using carpooling as a bait to murder three women

by uelu6c6b on 2012-02-28 09:34:12

We pushed open the heavy iron doors of the detention center and walked into the fully enclosed interrogation room. There, we saw the afternoon sun filtering through iron bars, casting shadows on the faces of two suspects who appeared slightly pale. Their eyes were eerily similar — vacant, hollow, yet filled with ignorance and confusion...

At 30 years old, an age when one should be in the midst of starting a business or enjoying the warmth of family life, they had instead found themselves behind bars, falling into the depths of sin. And yet, who would have known that these two individuals were responsible for the brutal murders of three innocent women.

Three women went missing, causing panic among the townsfolk.

On December 19, 2011 at 3:30 PM, Naa某某, a resident of Bayan Cagang Su Mu in Ewenki Banner, Hulunbuir City, Inner Mongolia, came to Xianghua Police Station to report her sister missing since December 17 in the afternoon. Attempts to contact her were unsuccessful, and her whereabouts were unknown.

On December 21, 2011 at 11 AM, the Daz Yan North River Police Station received a report from Sun Mou, a local resident, stating that his mother Ma Mou Mou left at 5:45 AM on December 20 from Beihou in Daz Yan Town to go to Bayantohai Town and had not returned, with no way to reach her.

At 15:15 PM on December 21, 2011, the North River Police Station received another report from Han Mou, a local resident, stating that his mother Shi Mou Mou left at 6 AM on December 19 from Beihou to go to Bayantohai Town and had not returned.

Upon receiving the reports, the police officers from Xianghua and North River Police Stations immediately took action and by 6:10 PM on December 21, entered the information of the three missing people into the Missing Persons Information System, initiating search efforts.

With three cases of disappearance within three days, the news spread like thunder on a clear day, unsettling the otherwise peaceful and quiet town. The ages of the missing women ranged from 26 to 49 years old. Were they victims of revenge killings, or had they been lured away? Such bizarre disappearances seemed incomprehensible. As time passed, the residents around the incident location became restless, rumors spread, and a sense of panic enveloped the hearts of the people.

The police acted swiftly upon hearing the news, conducting thorough investigations.

After investigation, it was found that the three missing individuals did not share any related social connections. Moreover, during her last phone call with her friend Su Mou, Naa某某's voice was abnormal. Given that the residence of the missing individuals were all in Daz Yan Town, which is almost connected to Yakeshi City and mainly consists of mining enterprises with high human and logistics traffic, this case might affect the surrounding areas. It would be difficult for the individual police officers from Ewenki Banner Public Security Bureau to handle this alone. After consultation, relevant personnel from Hulunbuir City Public Security Bureau went to Daz Yan Town to provide support and assistance.

At 11:15 PM on December 21, the "12·17" Series Missing Persons Command Center was established, along with seven action groups including a visiting group, a search group, a video communication data retrieval group, a missing persons various financial card withdrawal information retrieval group, a roadblock inspection group, a temporary population and criminal release education personnel (with driving skills) and other key personnel screening group, and a taxi and other non-standard passenger vehicle screening group. These groups conducted investigations and visits, set up roadblocks, and retrieved video communication and various financial card information data in the Daz Yan Town area.

Based on the evidence in hand, the police conducted a large-scale investigation of individuals aged between 20 and 40. In the first week of the investigation, the police screened over 80,000 permanent residents, over 10,000 migrant residents, and over 300 enterprises and individual businesses, but there was no progress in the case.

According to the command center's arrangements, a group of police mobilized the jurisdiction's security committees and public security patrol teams to set up strict controls at major intersections and channels, carefully checking and verifying passing vehicles and pedestrians, blocking the escape routes of the suspects. Another group of police conducted comprehensive visits and screenings of the residents around the incident location and key places such as hotels, bathhouses, rental houses, and internet cafes within the jurisdiction.

After the screening, relevant situations were continuously reported back to the command center but were subsequently negated.

Two suspects captured, truth revealed.

At this point, the special task force promptly adjusted its strategy, narrowing the screening age range to between 20 and 35 years old. At 3 AM on December 22, based on various clues, after comprehensive analysis, the command center decided to classify the three missing events as the "12·17" Series Robbery Murder Case and relocated the special task force command center to the Ewenki Banner Public Security Bureau Daz Yan Branch, forming the "12·17" Special Task Force. After careful analysis and research, the task force identified gaps and decided to focus the screening on individuals aged between 25 and 35.

On December 22, while investigating at a toll station, the police learned that someone had seen the suspect vehicle. They immediately began investigating the whereabouts of the two individuals. After several hours of work, Wang Mou Mou was unclear about his recent whereabouts, so the police took him back to the special task force for review.

At 5 PM on December 22, a significant breakthrough occurred as the suspects Wang Mou Mou and Zhao Mou Mou were identified and successively arrested.

After being brought to the review team, confronted with irrefutable evidence, Wang Mou Mou confessed to collaborating with Zhao Mou Mou to commit robbery, murder, and rape.

It turned out that the suspects Wang Mou Mou and Zhao Mou Mou lured passengers under the pretense of offering rideshare services on roads near Da Yan No. 1 Well. They robbed and murdered three female passengers, discarding two bodies in the cellar of abandoned houses on Dongsheng Street south of the railway tracks and one body in an underground sewage well of a residential complex. They stole over two yuan in cash and four pieces of jewelry, selling them at a gold and silver jewelry processing and recycling shop in Yakeshi City, obtaining a total of 9,700 yuan in ill-gotten gains.

According to the police, both suspects were previously released criminals. Wang Mou Mou had been sentenced to seven years in prison for theft; Zhao Mou Mou had been sentenced to four years in prison for theft and then nine years for theft and robbery. Years of imprisonment fueled their desire to seek revenge on society, leading them to personally create a series of tragic cases.
