Jiangsu TV's "If You Are the One" saw the departure of Ma Na, only to welcome Zhu Zhenfang, the "Second Sister Feng"! Zhu Zhenfang, with excavator-like teeth, delivered her famous lines: "I don't see money on your face," "I don't sense a mansion from you," and "I puke, I puke, I puke!"

Zhu Zhenfang's self-assessment at work:

I have been working in this company for more than a year now, starting as a salesperson and then promoted to production supervisor. I am very familiar with some materials and their incoming control.

Now let me give a detailed introduction:

Sales: Pre-sales service is very important. Besides enthusiasm, there is also a responsibility, including ensuring that delivery schedules can be met accurately.

During sales: This includes quoting prices and introducing product selection (of course, one must be very familiar with the products produced by our company to make a detailed introduction).

After-sales: Actively contacting customers about post-use conditions and promoting other products from our company.

Production Management

Production Scheduling: After receiving formal orders from the sales department, they are directly passed to production management. We need to clear existing finished goods inventory first (if specifications differ, additional production will be required). Then we proceed with a production schedule (which is essentially making a plan in advance).

Daily Scheduling Status: Adjust the daily production schedule to check if today’s scheduled items are completed, reasons for any delays, and estimated completion times, so that sales can respond to customers (this requires communication with the production floor).

Production Reports: Weekly and monthly production reports should be timely submitted to superiors, which also helps for future material preparation and annual production statistics.
