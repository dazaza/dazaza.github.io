Record those loves

by ncgpvrph06 on 2012-03-09 11:11:05

February 14th, Valentine's Day.

1. To My Dearest

Dear, we've been together for over two years. Unconsciously, you have become irreplaceable and someone I can rely on in my heart. There was a time when I probably hated you with all my strength. That heart-wrenching disappointment, that helpless despair. But even that unforgettable pain couldn't make me leave you decisively, and at that moment I knew I was "deeply stuck." My girlfriends said I loved you too much, loved you to the point of losing myself, and I don't deny it. Yes, having someone in my heart is better than feeling so empty. At least my happiness and sadness have somewhere to go. I admit, I love you.

Today on Valentine's Day, you are not by my side. In fact, you haven't accompanied me through many festivals, and my heart still feels sour, slightly bitter. But when I think about the words you whispered in my ear, that every Valentine's Day in your life will belong to me from now on, my eyes gradually moisten. Perhaps the rare reunions and long separations have made me overly sentimental. I simply want to cling to you. That day when I saw you off at the station, I knew it wasn't our last goodbye, but I still couldn't bring myself to say it. Standing in a corner, watching the bus drive away before my eyes, tears came suddenly. Choking back sobs, I dialed your number. At that moment, I just wanted to say, dear, I'm waiting for you.

I don't know if I can be lucky enough to become your bride, to wear your wedding dress, with a ring on my finger symbolizing your lifelong devotion. I also don't know if I can see the Tianchi Lake of Changbai Mountain with you, holding hands while watching the sunset when we're old. But I know that now I will love you well, live our little lives, believe in you, and believe in myself.

Dear, I never thought about forever, but because of you, I am willing to have forever. Because of you, I believe in the power of love.

2. To That You

The only surprise on Valentine's Day was receiving your gift.

I don't quite understand the meaning of that bottle of perfume, but I still thank you.

If, those years ago, we weren't so capricious, perhaps the ending would be different now. I wonder. But once missed, it can never return to the original state, never return to the age when I was so infatuated with you, with an innocent ponytail, and you were everywhere in my world. One turn, and time has already separated us into two corners. The only thing I'm glad about is that we are still good friends who talk about everything, good friends who aren't awkward around each other.

Hehe, our worlds are no longer just about ourselves. We have our beloved ones, so we try every possible way to protect them, not wanting to hurt them. I know you are just like me, strong and good kids.

Now I want to say, dear, please be happy.

3. About Movies

A child asked me to watch "Pre-Marital Love Test". I don't know how to express my feelings accurately in words, maybe that sentence, guilt is a good way to maintain love, is right. I just know that some things in my heart should be completely forgotten, I should believe in miracles, believe in love.

Thank you, I have learned to let go, not so tired, not so wronged, not so insecure.

I think my love can now grow simply.

Because of love.
