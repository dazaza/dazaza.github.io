Analysis: The Competition Behind ".China" Domain Names

by now on 2008-08-15 15:07:39

Recently, according to relevant reporters, the online Chinese domain names "中國僑都.cn", "中国侨都.中国", "中國僑都.中国", and "中国侨都.cn" have all been preemptively registered. Since the introduction of the Chinese domain name ".中国", it has received great attention from the Chinese community. Phenomena such as the preemptive registration of "东汽投资.中国" and "少林寺.中国" have occurred repeatedly and have yet to subside. Chinese domain names have become a hot spot for registration domestically.

Recently, according to a survey by NetEase, in a vote participated by a large number of netizens, as high as 79.24% of netizens indicated that they would register the ".中国" domain name.

Industry insiders expressed that the occurrence of preemptive registration of the Chinese domain name ".中国" was expected. The reasons for purchasing Chinese domain names are quite obvious. For Chinese domain names themselves, they are simple and easy to remember and spread, conforming to the language habits of the Chinese people. Moreover, the domain name resources are abundant, and the registration procedures are simple and fast. For domestic enterprises and individuals, they are convenient to use and easy to remember. Enterprises can enhance their reputation domestically and protect company resources by registering Chinese domain names. Individuals can create personalized websites, satisfy spiritual civilization, and reflect their own value by registering Chinese domain names. Many people specialize in domain name investment, purchasing Chinese domain names related to celebrities' names and company names in advance to make profits from the price difference, further causing the phenomenon of preemptive registration.

Chinese domain names being listed as international top-level domain names is a reflection of the development of the global internet industry and also demonstrates the borderless nature of the network. According to a relevant person in charge of Times Network (www.now.cn): "Chinese domain names occupy the domestic market with their advantages of convenient use, clear at a glance, and rich in meaning, allowing Chinese-speaking people who do not understand English to surf the Internet directly in Chinese. Currently, the registration of Chinese domain names domestically is still in full swing, and similar registered domain names include: 中国电信.cn, 时代网.com, 长虹.中国, and 一汽.中国, etc. Although Chinese domain name resources are abundant, personalized domain names are limited, and preemptive registration is inevitable."

Experts believe that the Chinese domain name ".中国" not only promotes the development of our country's Internet but also accelerates the process of social informatization and promotes the development of traditional Chinese culture.
