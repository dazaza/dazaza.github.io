Goodbye, my past

Whether I have to disappear for you to realize my existence; whether I have to shed tears for you to see my scars; whether I have to give up for you to see my efforts; whether I have to be silent for you to hear my voice? Why is it harder to forget someone than to like someone; why are beautiful things always short-lived, and the end of the story is still goodbye. Let the bitter tears flow down my face quietly, hiding in the night leaning against the wall alone, who can know the pain in my heart, all I can do is to show a smile on the surface that others can see. When the story ends and I no longer talk about you, leave the setbacks of loving you to time to deal with, accompany you for a while, let myself think clearly, love lies between dreams and reality, impossible to intersect. You pursue and become沧桑 (weathered), I turn around silently, we eventually return to our own worlds. I love you so much but can only stand still in silence, like a lost child left behind in the crowd, I love you so much, knowing full well that I cannot get your memories, my mood is like a lone goose flying at dusk. In fact, I don't want to make things easy for you, I don't want to let you fly to a place where there's more happiness. If one day you could come into my heart, you would surely cry, because it's full of the sadness you've given me... If one day I could come into your heart, I would also cry, because it's full of your indifference!! Finally, I see clearly the role I play, and I finally see your heart, originally I was just a dispensable puppet in your heart. There are some people whose appearance is destined to be unforgettable forever! He moved into your heart, my love, homeless!

The painful thing is not the past; it's the memory...

Looking back on the bits and pieces of the past, this relationship is like an hourglass; even if I have invested so much, paid so much, the sand will still flow away bit by bit; in the end, all I have in my hand is just a wisp of wind, and my tear-streaked face already dried by the wind!

Only the person you deeply love can make you laugh sweetest; yet also hurt you most truly! Memories may be sad but also beautiful; it's better to keep this only beauty in your heart forever... At least, I still have a reason to smile!!!

Life encounters trials every day;

Dreams are wasted in reality every day;

I feel extremely lonely; only the me in the mirror understands my heart!!! Missing you is a kind of pain; a hidden pain! It doesn't come often, but it lingers;

Missing you is a kind of bone-deep pain! It doesn't come often, but it remains deep!

Once thought; as time goes by, love would fade like a night-blooming cereus; but, I will still occasionally at an unguarded moment, have it breeze through my mind, clear as a knife cut; missing you, never tiring, yet excruciatingly painful! Unknowingly seeping into the blood, tearing the lungs apart...

After walking such a long way, I finally woke up from the dream, understanding what it means to let go! To let go means to have both giving up and gaining; I understand that true happiness is letting go!

I wasn't happy in the past; because I hadn't let go yet; immersed in pain;

Now I've learned to forget, realizing that only by letting go can I be happy! The painful thing is not the past; it's the memory!

Beautiful memories are just occasional fleeting smiles at the corners of the mouth; laughing to forget...

Wiping away the last tears I shed for you, waving goodbye to yesterday; love has become a thing of the past

I understand more clearly what I want; real care; drop-by-drop affection; a plain life; warm days...

Goodbye; forgive me for making such a self-centered decision; I really can't bear it; only because I love too deeply; when the city becomes unnatural, all I can do is let go; I start to believe that the greatest happiness in life is to let go; blessings to you, my love; as long as you live better than me...

Because of certain reasons, I have to give up, but giving you up doesn't mean giving up my love for you... It's not that I love someone else. It's because I'm too heartbroken. Giving you up doesn't mean giving up my love for you. As long as I live, I won't give up my love for you...

If one day I no longer put aside all my pride to disturb you; if one day you can no longer see my icon, which is always visible only to you, jump again, will you feel a moment of sorrow? Women are different from men. If a man takes the initiative, others will say he is bravely pursuing his own love, but women are different. She will be afraid. Afraid that disturbing you without reservation will make you unhappy; afraid that in the chat box with you, only her words will sit quietly and sadly; afraid that her taking the initiative will make you not cherish her. So boys, you shouldn't think that she doesn't take the initiative to talk to you means she doesn't care about you. On the contrary, maybe she cares about you too much, so she has so many fears. Remember, even if she doesn't take the initiative to talk to you, you shouldn't be petty and get angry with her, ignoring her as well. When two people are together, as long as one person takes the initiative, they can lead the other person.
