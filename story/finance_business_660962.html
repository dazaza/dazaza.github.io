The orientation of the floor plan is: North at the top, South at the bottom, West on the left, and East on the right. Picture 1. Regarding the external environment: From the satellite image, this building is located in the middle of the community, with a view to the front and mountains to the back, encircled by dragons and water, indicating a relatively good large environment. The floor plan is basically square, making it an ideal overall layout.

However, the elevator on the due west moves up and down, which could adversely affect the menstrual cycle and gynecological health of the women living here. It may also have some negative impact on the fortune and health of men, requiring resolution through the use of a small Taishan stone. The northwest corner (Qian Palace) is slightly missing, affecting the fortune of the male head of the household, as well as his head, waist health, and sleep. This also requires resolution through the Taishan stone.

Decoration Suggestions:

Firstly, this floor plan is oriented from east to west. Entering through the due west metal area and residing on the 9th floor, which is associated with the metal element, we recommend placing "Five Emperor Coins" at the entrance to step onto auspicious land, enhancing the prosperity of the home for all things to flourish harmoniously. The door's opposite wall should feature celebratory calligraphy or paintings, avoiding bright red colors. Fish should not be kept directly opposite the door to prevent water from stealing the metal energy.

Secondly, entering from the due west leads to the kitchen in the northwest where wealth accumulates. This often creates a "fire enhances wealth" effect but also violates the feng shui taboo of "fire burns the heavenly gate." Fortunately, the northwest corner is slightly missing, reducing this influence. We suggest that the stove location should be as far away from the northwest corner as possible to avoid negatively impacting the heads and nerves of men. The stove and sink should be separated, not aligned on the same line. White or green marble countertops are recommended, while black or blue ones should be avoided. Electrical appliances like refrigerators should be placed away from the northwest corner, which can be used for cabinets instead.

Thirdly, a round dining table is preferred as it can enhance wealth stars. Placing one or two pots of noble plants in the dining room can also increase wealth and food luck.

Fourthly, the bathroom to the north does not cause any harm being in an isolated position. A screen wall can be set up at the west exterior wall opposite the door to create auspicious and prosperous effects. Note that the length, width, and height of the screen wall must exceed those of the entrance door.

Fifthly, the guest bedroom to the northeast, located in the Yannian star position, promotes marital harmony, career success, and longevity. It is most suitable for couples and elders. Based on your life information, we suggest designating this area as the master bedroom, moving the master bathroom door here, and orienting the bed towards the east. The color scheme should be based on your preferred colors, creating a harmonious and stable home atmosphere where the wife is virtuous and the husband is respected.

Sixthly, the bathroom to the east affects the Qi field of purple Qi coming from the east, with unclean air severely impacting the home’s fortune and possibly affecting boys' vision and skin. Therefore, peach wood beast heads can be used to resolve unclean air, and the toilet should be positioned towards the northeast corner of the bathroom.

Seventhly, the southeast master bedroom is located in the literary star and peach blossom areas. While beneficial for academic pursuits and social interactions, it tends to promote licentious desires, making it half auspicious and half ominous. Couples living here might lead to men easily committing adultery and inviting trouble. Although there is no indication of peach blossom afflictions in the life charts of both owners, considering the absence of literary stars in the boy’s eight characters, it is suggested to make this place a children's room and study room, which can complement the child's life chart deficiencies and positively guide his studies and interpersonal relationships, helping his future social relations. For single individuals, living here can turn misfortune into fortune. The color scheme should be mainly blue or white, the child's preferred colors. Due to defects in the floor plan, the bed can only face east, which can be resolved with a cold tone layout, such as light blue bedding and curtains. However, water, fish, and plants should not be placed in this room.

Eighthly, the living room to the south is located in the Mars area. Sitting facing east is optimal and aligns with the direction flow, enhancing the popularity of the living room and resolving the adverse effects of evil stars. The overall color tone should be based on the preferences of the homeowners. This area is best suited for keeping fish to enhance wealth and blessings, and using feng shui fish to resolve the disadvantages of Mars (water overcomes fire). However, plants should be avoided because wood generates Mars energy, causing the evil star to become powerful, leading to loss or bloodshed disasters.

Ninthly, the study to the southwest is located in the official star area. This area enhances official promotion and career advancement. Using it as a study would be wasteful; we suggest converting it into an elder's room. Elderly people living in a prosperous star area protect their children, promoting their careers, and benefiting their physical and mental health. If the elderly do not live with you regularly, this room should be kept clean and tidy, and they should occasionally sit here. This room can also be used as a child's bedroom, with the original master bedroom converted into a study.

Tenthly, on the right side upon entering, a shoe cabinet is recommended, preferably white or metallic, with a height not exceeding 1.2 meters. The nearby changing area should be open shelving, where a dressing mirror can be placed, but mirrors should be avoided on both sides of the door to prevent reflection issues.

Through the above adjustments and allocations, the layout of this house can achieve a harmonious and stable living arrangement for the main bedroom, elderly bedroom, and children's bedroom, promoting prosperity and smooth business, and ensuring the homeowner's health.
