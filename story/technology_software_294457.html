by fj0953210 on 2009-12-05 22:16:32

1. After the private placement, the company's main business will increase the production and sales of heavy trucks and chassis. Before the private placement, Xingma Automobile’s main business was bulk cement trucks, concrete mixer trucks, and concrete pump trucks, making it the largest tanker special-purpose automobile manufacturer in China. In the first half of 2009, the company's revenue structure was as follows: concrete mixer truck revenue accounted for 63% of total revenue, bulk cement truck revenue accounted for 29%, and concrete pump truck and dump truck revenues each accounted for 4%. The main business of the acquired asset, Huali Auto, is the production and sales of heavy trucks, vehicle chassis, and automotive parts. From the revenue in the first three quarters of 2009, Huali Auto had an income of 2.5 billion yuan, which was 1.3 times that of the listed company Xingma Automobile at 1.9 billion yuan. Huali Auto achieved a net profit of 200 million yuan, which was 3.9 times that of the listed company Xingma Automobile at 53.06 million yuan.

2. Acquiring assets can improve the company's gross margin. Before the private placement, Xingma Automobile mainly purchased its primary raw material, vehicle chassis, from Huali Auto, forming related party transactions. After the completion of the private placement, it is expected to enhance the gross margin of existing products. Additionally, after the decline in raw material prices, the gross margin of Huali Auto products improved. In the first three quarters of 2009, the gross margin was 13.36%, higher than the original product special-purpose vehicles' gross margin of 11.16%. Therefore, it is predicted that after the asset integration is completed, it will enhance the company's overall gross margin.

3. We are optimistic about the heavy truck industry where the acquired asset company operates. Currently, Xingma Automobile's special-purpose automobile manufacturing ranks first domestically in the same industry, with a market share of approximately 30%. Huali Auto ranks eighth in the domestic heavy truck industry, with a market share of about 3%. With the 4 trillion yuan investment stimulus, increased infrastructure construction, and the warming up of the logistics industry, since 2009, the sales of heavy trucks have rebounded from their low point, with the industry growth rate changing from a 60% decrease in January to a 71% increase. It is predicted that the heavy truck industry will gradually become more prosperous as the economy warms up, which will also be beneficial to the company's profit increase.

4. The purchase of assets at a relatively cheap price can enhance the company's overall profitability. Calculated by the disclosed net assets of the target asset for the first three quarters of 2009, the acquisition asset's PB ratio is 2 times, and PE is 8 times, while the listed company's PB ratio for the first three quarters is 3 times, and PE is 32 times. The acquisition price is relatively low, which is conducive to optimizing the listed company's assets.

Profit forecast assumptions: 1) Completion of the non-public offering in 2009; 2) Expected future improvement in the prosperity of the company's dedicated automobile and heavy truck industries with economic recovery; 3) Gross margin improvement following company integration; 4) Deduction of related-party transaction revenue based on the 2008 related-party transaction ratio. Assuming the company completes the non-public offering and asset purchase in 2009, it is estimated to increase the 2009 EPS by 0.2 yuan and the 2010 EPS by 0.23 yuan. Therefore, it is estimated that the 2009-2011 EPS will be 0.61 yuan, 0.71 yuan, and 0.86 yuan respectively. Based on the closing price of 9.72 yuan on November 30th, the corresponding dynamic P/E ratios are 14 times, 12 times, and 10 times respectively. After the transaction, the company's profitability will be enhanced, and the valuation will have relative advantages. According to the reasonable P/E ratio of 20 times for 2010, the corresponding price is 14.3 yuan. Due to the company's current lower valuation and optimism regarding the heavy truck industry, the company rating has been upgraded to "Buy."

Risk warnings: 1) Approval risk for this transaction; 2) Risks associated with the progress and price changes of the restructuring; 3) Risk of the impact of supplementary tax payments on current profits and losses; 4) Raw material price fluctuation risk.
