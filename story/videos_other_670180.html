Slimming essential oil graduates shoot micro-films to encourage college entrance examination students

by nim264zdbm on 2012-02-20 16:22:37

Weibo Recommendation | Today's Weibo Hot Topic

According to Zhan Zilin's introduction, this short film named "Gaokao, What's the Big Deal! 50 College Students Give Infinite Strength to Gaokao Candidates" was produced with the collaboration of students from the class of 2010 who graduated from Dongguan No.1 Middle School and are now studying at Sun Yat-sen University, Jinan University, South China Normal University, and other provincial institutions within Guangdong. The messages of encouragement in the film were deeply moving. "I remember when this movie premiered at No.1 Middle School, some female students even cried out of excitement."

Hope the Film Records Real Life

Written by reporters Zhou Ruimei, Chen Chen, and Zhong Dawen

Based on real stories, it allows juniors and seniors to resonate.

Expectation: To premiere just before the 100-day countdown to the Gaokao.

It is reported that soon this film will enter post-production. Zhan Zilin told reporters that, as last year, they hope this micro-film can premiere just before the 100-day countdown to the Gaokao. He had already thought about the premiere location: "Hopefully at No.1 Middle School."

They have just entered the ivory tower. Looking back at life a year ago, they decided to give the warriors about to set off a different kind of strength.

As someone who has been through it, you would feel that a few warm words, a few spirited actions, and a few sincere smiles are the best encouragement for your juniors. But for "@Dongguan 2012 Gaokao Inspirational Micro-Film Crew," this is far from enough.

Predicament: No sponsorship received, only self-funded

Talk is cheap. A few people, a few copied scripts, one DSLR camera - starting from February 5th, they kept busy for three days to produce a film that lasts over ten minutes. According to plan, today they will shoot scenes at Dongguan No.1 Middle School, recording some blessings and encouraging words together with several graduates recruited via the official Weibo account from the class of 2011. In addition, they will also travel to Beijing and shoot scenes at the school where the main actor studies.

Moreover, Mr. Hu, the teacher responsible for publicity work at Guangming Middle School, believes that the strong sense of responsibility and mission shown by the crew members in promoting the positive image of middle school and university students in Dongguan is commendable and convincing.

Zhan Zilin, the initiator of the micro-film, was still a senior student at Dongguan No.1 Middle School a year ago. Yesterday, he told reporters that when there were less than 100 days left until the Gaokao, some graduates from the class of 2010 initiated the production of a video full of blessings and inspiration, which was played within the school.

Understanding that many scenes of this film will be shot at Dongguan No.1 Middle School, the crew has received support from the school administration.

The crew also does not forget to propose "micro-needs." Today, they will shoot scenes at Dongguan No.1 Middle School, needing 12 graduates from the class of 2011 to participate in the shooting. Additionally, they need two actors to play the roles of dormitory manager and teacher. Yesterday, Zhan Zilin mentioned to reporters that the crew urgently needs Apple all-in-one computers and other post-production equipment. They hope netizens with conditions will @ "Dongguan 2012 Gaokao Inspirational Micro-Film Crew" to support them.

"After this film premiered at No.1 Middle School, I thought, after I graduate, can I also make such a film to give to the next generation of juniors?"

However, when expressing his expectations for this micro-film, Lu Xuchang said he hopes the micro-film first maintains the correct orientation, then truthfully reflects the protagonist's struggle from average grades to entering their ideal university, thereby setting an example for juniors preparing for the Gaokao and achieving an inspirational effect.

Inspired by the creation of an inspirational short film by seniors

In Zhan Zilin's words, this yet-to-be-born micro-film was extraordinary from its conception. At 8:38 PM the previous evening, their official Weibo account "@Dongguan 2012 Gaokao Inspirational Micro-Film Crew" officially launched. Netizens can learn about the story outline, main target audience, and shooting progress related to the micro-film here. They do not forget to publish stills here either, such as methods for slimming legs with gua sha.

Since going online on the evening of February 6th, "@Dongguan 2012 Gaokao Inspirational Micro-Film Crew" has seen increasing popularity. As the Weibo account continues to release new content, netizens' attention and support have also continuously increased.

Zhan Zilin said this is not a plot created based on dramatic conflict needs but a true story. "It is based on one of my close friends, showcasing his bittersweet experiences and emotional ups and downs during his senior year. Authenticity makes it easier for juniors to resonate."

Remember the days of hard-fought struggle in your senior year? I believe that at that time, what you needed most was inspiration and blessings.

Lu Xuchang said that alumni graduating with feelings for their alma mater returning to encourage and bless their juniors, facing an incoming train, the courage to lift a child who fell onto the platform, this starting point is worth affirming. The crew members took photos during their spare time. Still from the film.

Regarding students filming inspirational micro-films, Hu Bin, the teacher in charge of publicity at Guangming Middle School, stated that he had previously seen a similar student self-produced film, finding it generally good but with some flaws in content, such as complaints and parody content. "So, if students want to screen this film at school, it should first be reviewed by some teachers on a small scale, which is necessary. Since it will be spread among students, schools and teachers naturally need to consider the impact of the film on a wide range of students."

However, Zhan Zilin said that opening an official Weibo account does not count as the biggest innovation. As the screenwriter, he only wanted to depict one character: someone who had mediocre results until just before the Gaokao but managed to enter their dream university in the final battle.

Hoping to set an example for juniors preparing for the Gaokao

This is a micro-film. A student who once had average grades made a comeback in the Gaokao and entered their desired university, and this is entirely true. Yesterday, the creators said they hope to move the audience with a story rather than mere words.

Yesterday afternoon, the reporter interviewed Principal Lu Xuchang of Dongguan No.1 Middle School regarding this. He told reporters that although he had not yet seen this micro-film and did not know how the students would turn out, "But a few days ago, some seniors applied to the school, saying that alumni wanted to shoot some scenes at the school, and I immediately approved it."
