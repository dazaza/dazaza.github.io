Synopsis:

In 1582, Oda Nobunaga, who sought to dominate the land, was assassinated by his disloyal retainer Akechi Mitsuhide at Honnō-ji Temple. Toyotomi Hideyoshi, Nobunaga's trusted ally, rose up in arms against Mitsuhide and established the Toyotomi regime.

Though the chaos of war had not been entirely extinguished, a temporary peace was achieved. However, the disparity between the rich and poor remained vast, and life for the common people continued to be arduous. At this time, Ishikawa Goemon emerged as a legendary thief, using his extraordinary skills to rob from the wealthy and give to the poor, becoming a hero in the hearts of the people.

One night, Goemon discovered a chest made by the Nanban (Westerners) among the hidden treasures in Kii-no-kuniya Bunzaemon's house. After stealing the chest, he found it empty and discarded it. Soon after, Goemon received word from Sarutobi Sasuke that Ishida Mitsunari had failed to obtain the chest from Kii-no-kuniya and had dispatched Kirigakure Saizō to search for it everywhere. Determined to retrieve the chest he had thrown away, Goemon went to the slums where a young boy named Heihachiro had accidentally obtained it. Witnessing the cruel scene of a government official, Matahachi, killing Heihachiro's mother reminded Goemon of the tragic moment when his own parents were killed. Saizō confronted Goemon, leading to an intense battle, while Hattori Hanzō, who had once rigorously trained them as part of Nobunaga’s ninja army, secretly observed their conflict.

Ishida Mitsunari sent Kirigakure Saizō, and Tokugawa Ieyasu dispatched Hattori Hanzō to recover the mysterious chest. This chest contained significant secrets about the assassination of Oda Nobunaga. Upon learning the truth, Goemon traveled alone to Osaka Castle and unexpectedly reunited with Chacha. In the past, at Nobunaga's Azuchi Castle, Goemon had served as Chacha's bodyguard, and the two shared feelings of mutual admiration.

The story unfolds with Goemon confronting his destiny head-on, Kirigakure Saizō aspiring to become a samurai, Toyotomi Hideyoshi consolidating power while continuously taking away those Goemon loves, Ishida Mitsunari plotting to kill Hideyoshi and seize control over the realm, Chacha making difficult decisions to end the strife, and Tokugawa Ieyasu along with Hattori Hanzō scheming a new era. Interwoven with love and ambition, a grand battle is set to commence...
