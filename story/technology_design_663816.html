Game Section: 1. The Story of A Shui

Next, we will start the game section for the whole company's participation in the expansion training. The name of the game is "The Story of A Shui". We need all our friends here to participate together. First, please stretch out your right arm horizontally with your palm facing down. Place your palm in the middle between you and your friend on the right. Then, extend your left index finger and gently place it on the right hand of your friend on the left.

Alright, let's do it together again. Stretch out your right arm horizontally with your palm facing down. Place it in the middle between you and your friend on the right. Then, extend your left index finger and gently place it on the right hand of your friend on the left.

We have now mastered the basic moves. So why is this section called "The Story of A Shui"? Because after everyone has prepared, I will read a short story for you. Whenever the word "water" is mentioned in the story, we need to quickly grab the index finger of the friend on the right with your right hand. At the same time, our left index finger should escape from the other person's right hand.

Let's do a rehearsal. Everyone, get ready. When I count to three, we will quickly grab and escape. Alright!

It seems like we got caught again, doesn't it? It appears that some people were not focused. We also need to add a rule: Punishment Rule - each table selects the team member who was caught the most times. They are required to come to the stage and perform a set of cartoon jumps, gorilla steps, or exaggerated funny faces (Note: only one set).

Alright, please prepare yourself. "The Story of A Shui" begins now.

The Story of A Shui:

Once upon a time, there was a mountain, inside the mountain there was a temple, inside the temple there was an old monk and a young monk. One day, the young monk said to the old monk, "Master, I want to go down the mountain and take a look." The master told the young monk, "The women below the mountain are tigers, be careful. Before the sun sets, you must return."

The young monk happily skipped down the mountain. The young monk ran and ran, sweating all over (water). The young monk was parched and very thirsty, wanting to drink some tea (water). The young monk looked and looked, finding a river, on the river there was a bridge, and on the bridge there was a woman. The young monk watched and watched, becoming entranced. Suddenly, he remembered what his master had said about the women below the mountain being tigers, his heart skipped a beat. Carelessly, he fell into the river (water), getting completely soaked. In a hurry, he climbed ashore, took off his shirt, wrung and wrung, squeezing out a lot of sand. The young monk hung his clothes on a tree, under the sunlight, the water on the clothes evaporated. The young monk looked at the sky, the sun was about to set. The young monk put on his clothes and rushed back to the temple. This is the story of the young monk A (Shui).

2. Support the Frontline

Now we proceed to the next game project named "Support the Frontline".

Requirement: Each table as a unit needs to select one frontline transport officer. Our central stage is like the frontline battlefield. Some necessary supplies need to be transported to support the battlefield. All of us are like the villagers in our base. We must obtain the supplies from the villagers and pass them to the transport officer to deliver them to the frontline (the host's location).

When the game starts, the host will loudly say "Support the Frontline," and everyone will ask "What to support?" The host will then announce the urgently needed items...

Alright, everyone understands the content of the game. I still have two requirements: 1. Villagers cannot leave their seats, only the transport officer can transport the supplies. 2. We must try every possible way to complete the transportation task (even if it means grabbing the supplies to send them to the frontline).

For the first few tables, the position of the transport officers must be directly opposite the podium.

Please select one transport officer per group.

After selecting the transport officers, the host announces the punishment rules: The two groups with the slowest supply transportation speed, along with the fastest transport officer, will slowly walk around the venue while forming pairs of one male and one female. As they walk, the female partner should occasionally insert her hand under the other person's arm, and both shout together, "Are we a good pair? Jealous much?"

Alright, sound engineer, get ready to start. Background story and music begin... Story and music.
