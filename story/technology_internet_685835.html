Walk past "pipes", feel the unusual "fashion" —— Shulang participates in the 2011 CHIC exhibition_women's cardigan sweaters

by abctskoe on 2012-03-01 17:18:27

From March 28th to 31st, 2011, Shulan Enterprises will showcase its comprehensive brand portfolio at the 19th China International Women's Apparel Fair (CHIC2011). A multi-brand show named [Guanzi] will present the essence of contemporary fashion trends and explore the origins of the brands. Shulan will engage with domestic and international peers as an industry pioneer to discuss the innovation and intrinsic power of brands.

Shulan’s women’s wear Spring/Summer 2011 new collection

The CHIC exhibition is acclaimed as the largest international fair in China's women's apparel industry. At that time, many well-known domestic and international brands will gather in Beijing to learn from each other and grow together. The CHIC exhibition has played an indispensable role in the development of Chinese women's apparel brands. It has truly promoted the restructuring and integration of the apparel industry, pushing some companies and consumers to develop a brand consciousness. Meanwhile, through this platform, it also allows overseas entities to understand the thriving situation of China's women's apparel companies. In recent years, market segmentation and homogenization trends have become more apparent. The impact of foreign brands on the domestic market has strongly stimulated the transformation of local companies. Participating in exhibitions is not only about showcasing self-worth but also about understanding the transformation of the global textile and women's apparel industry. Through exchanges, one can deeply comprehend the meaning of "[innovation is the soul of industry and brand development]. This participation in the exhibition will allow Shulan to engage in more exchanges and interactions with peers to promote continuous growth and expansion of the company.

After ten years of experience, Shulan has cast its brand, creating brilliance through quality. Shulan plans for gradual diversification, owning unique characteristics such as "elegant," "refined," "mature," and "colorful" to keep up with the consumption upgrade and diversified needs of the textile and women's apparel industry. With innovation upgrades, Shulan aims to seize historical opportunities.

At the 2010 Fashion Expo, Shulan first introduced the "experiential" franchise model and the "low-carbon environmental protection" booth, showcasing its three women's apparel brands: [Shulan], [Meizhiteng], and [GOGIRL]. In 2011, Shulan will bring seven new brands—[Mingzuo], [Langni], [Keruidamu], [Zuiku], [GOGIRL Children's Wear], [Fengrusong], and [ANGEL IN US]—to perform a brilliant showcase of contemporary fashion brands.

In this exhibition, Shulan actively innovates and adjusts product display models, thoroughly implementing the concept of "low-carbon environmental protection." The Shulan design team focuses on the exhibition theme of "discovering essence, innovating fashion," uniquely using recyclable environmental protection decorative materials where appropriate. Using the primitive image of "tubes" as carriers, different styles of products are displayed in separate zones, clearly distinguishing spatial divisions. As a new way to display various new products under its umbrella, tubes provide insight into the flexible space of Shulan's design thoughts, highlighting the quality and value of the dresses themselves.

Shulan, as one of the representatives of quality women's wear for the "Made in China" humanistic autonomous brands, has consistently focused on excavating the essence of "dresses" over the years. To create a successful brand, a company must focus on expanding domestic demand, adjusting industrial structures, conserving resources, strengthening independent innovation, enhancing corporate culture construction, and more. Brands are accumulations of culture; a successful brand must withstand the test of time. The Shulan brand is progressively upgrading rather than suddenly rising, providing consumers with a fresh feeling every year and every season. Shulan's loyal consumer base experiences the brand's progress and upgrade, generating respect alongside surprise. Not only does a single brand gain recognition, but a multi-brand strategy gives consumers a whole new perspective, allowing them to re-examine and improve their own lifestyles. Whenever the brand's upgrades or new creations are acknowledged by the market and liked by consumers, it is a matter of great honor and joy for the creators themselves.

"Low-carbon environmental protection" has become the main melody of China's future economic development and a global consensus. Shulan fully implements the low-carbon environmental protection concept into its actual exhibition actions, actively fulfilling its social responsibility for sustainable development, guiding the company and product transformations, setting an example as an industry leader, promoting the integration of fashion innovation and technological innovation in the women's apparel industry chain, and incorporating the company culture of "[Shulan, Sunshine, Health]" to influence societal fashion lifestyles.

The exhibition design embodies the concepts of life and brand origin, symbolizing Shulan's forging of a cluster of fashion brands. "[Respect tradition, create the future]" is the development philosophy that Shulan people have firmly upheld for ten years. The derivation of the company's own brands cannot be stagnant; there must be enhanced exchange within and outside the industry, learning from advanced practices, strengthening corporate cultural construction, continuously integrating the advocated innovative concepts into corporate culture, achieving practical integration, enhancing the company's own value, and successfully realizing more social values. Shulan's Chairman Wu Jianmin leads all of Shulan's people to open up the company's development path through self-reflection.

Shulan's decade-long journey, rooted in "dresses" and sourced from "innovation," respects the laws of brand development while researching and developing women's clothing. By recruiting excellent talents to strengthen the company's innovation capabilities and appropriately expanding the company scale, Shulan forges and strengthens its design, time-honored website marketing, logistics, and other models, achieving diversity through "quantity" and surpassing through "quality."

In 2011, Shulan will continue its multi-brand strategy, nurturing people with culture, telling legends through brands, creating brand style diversification, expanding the overall consumer base, striving to unleash the intrinsic power of brands, and providing consumers with broader dressing spaces. At the 2011 CHIC exhibition, Shulan will lead visitors to deeply interpret the connotations of "time·fashion," experiencing the essence and ideals of contemporary fashion. With transcendence as motivation, Shulan will boost the company's progress and brand inheritance, injecting new elements into China's women's apparel industry.
