Great Mountain Echo - A Grand Event Featuring Chinese Famous Painter and Photographer Hua Shan's Artistic Inspiration Trip

by xyxcandy8 on 2012-03-08 09:43:41

Grand Mountain Echo - A grand event for the Huashan artistic inspiration gathering of China's famous painters and photographers was held. The launch ceremony of the Grand Mountain Echo - China's famous painters and photographers Huashan artistic inspiration gathering was hosted by Ma Jianping, the Deputy Secretary of the Working Committee of Huaguan. Wen Hojun, the Director of the Huashan Scenic Spot Management Committee and the Chairman of Huashan Tourism Group Co., Ltd., delivered a speech. Notable artists took group photos with the leaders of the Huashan scenic spot.

"Several early orioles are competing for warm trees, whose new swallows are pecking spring mud." In this vibrant spring, the Huashan Scenic Spot Management Committee held a grand launch ceremony for the "Grand Mountain Echo" Huashan artistic inspiration gathering of China's famous painters and photographers on April 8th at the Huashan Lotus Mountain Villa. Attending the launch ceremony were Wen Hujun, the Deputy Secretary of the People's Government of Weinan City, the Director of the Huashan Scenic Spot Management Committee, and the Chairman of Huashan Tourism Group Co., Ltd., as well as Lu Genwen, Shu Feng, Lu Jianming, the Deputy Directors of Huaguan, Zhang Xiaojian, the General Manager of the Group Company, and famous painters Li Baolin, Xiao Yingchuan, Zhou Shunkai, etc. The launch ceremony was hosted by Ma Jianping, the Deputy Secretary of the Working Committee of Huaguan. Notable enterprise photographers Zeng Shiquan, Wang Chen, etc., participated in the artistic inspiration gathering.

Director Wen Hujun first represented the Huashan scenic spot to deliver a speech. He said that the purpose of hosting the "Grand Mountain Echo" China's famous painters and photographers Huashan artistic inspiration gathering is to use the magic brushes and light shadows in the hands of all the great masters to make the fascinating natural scenery and profound historical culture of Huashan shine before the world, realizing the splendid transformation of Huashan culture and comprehensively elevating it. He expressed his belief that through the hosting of this event, the cultural connotation of Huashan will be further deepened, its external image will be further enhanced, and Huashan's tomorrow will be even better!

Li Baolin, representing the various artistic masters participating in the event, praised the magnificent natural landscape and brilliant and long-standing human history of the Huashan scenic spot. He stated that he would use the brush in his hand to discover the beauty of Huashan, continuously expanding Huashan's influence, allowing more people to understand and pay attention to Huashan.

In subsequent activities, painters and photographers will ascend the main peak of Huashan, tour the Xianyu scenic area, pay homage to the West Peak Temple, and experience the old腔of Huashan, conducting painting and photography creation activities, presenting the beauty of Huashan to the public.

Culture is the soul of tourism, and tourism is the channel for spreading culture. Closely integrating culture and tourism and further enhancing the cultural connotation of the scenic spot has always been the direction that the scenic spot management committee strives for. In recent years, the Huashan Scenic Spot Management Committee has innovated development concepts, planned high-standard development blueprints for the scenic spot, actively attracted investment, increased project construction efforts, continuously improved tourism infrastructure, maintained steady growth in tourist numbers, and achieved remarkable results in all aspects of the scenic spot's work. In 2010, it successfully created a national 5A-level tourist attraction, comprehensively enhancing the scenic spot's image and realizing the healthy and rapid development of Huashan's tourism industry. Meanwhile, relying on its own strong cultural heritage, the Huashan Scenic Spot has focused on strengthening cultural construction, deeply exploring Huashan Taoist culture and folk culture, hosting multiple large-scale cultural activities to drive tourism industry development. This event is another large-scale series of cultural activities organized by the Huashan Scenic Spot Management Committee after last year's "Li Xiaochao Art Exhibition", "Huashan Sword Discussion Series Forum", "Experiencing Peril - Chinese Masters Enter Huashan", "Mirror Beautiful China - Traveling with Masters Experiencing Huashan's Beauty" and other activities.

More than 100 employees from relevant units within the scenic spot attended the launch ceremony. Renowned media outlets such as China Tourism News, Hong Kong Evening Post, Shaanxi Daily, Shenzhen Evening News, Huashang Daily, Sanqin Metropolis Daily, Shaanxi Television Station, and People's Network provided full coverage of the event.
