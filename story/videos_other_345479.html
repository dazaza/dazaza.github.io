In fact, entering the park, the access pass is the most headache for us. The World Expo registered journalist's certificate has not yet been issued, and if you want to enter the park, you can only apply for a temporary pass. To apply for a temporary pass, Hangzhou Xingcheng Hotel (Wulin Square Store), you need to make an appointment three days in advance. For our tight interview schedule, it is obviously impossible. Fortunately, the World Expo side is still "accommodating" to the media, allowing us to borrow the temporary certificate of Shanghai peers. This interview entered the park three times and borrowed different media passes three times. When the reporter of Liberation Daily handed over her certificate to us, she repeatedly instructed: I must return it to me tonight! My ID card and journalist certificate are pledged there!

& nbsp; There was a little episode: On March 31, we went to the park for the first time for an interview. Due to the cold weather, none of us brought water. After running for three or four hours, our mouths were dry and parched, but there was nowhere to buy water, as all shops were still under renovation!

For example, in addition to bringing the access pass to enter the park, security checks are naturally inevitable. After passing through the security gate, the detector will probe around the body, and both inside and outside of the outerwear will also be detected. Compared with airport security checks, the armed police soldiers responsible for the security checks of the World Expo Park are not only serious in attitude and delicate and gentle in action, but more importantly, they will smile: "Please come over for security check!"

In view of the successful security experience of the Olympic Games and the well-known meticulous work of Shanghai people, almost no one worries about the security of the World Expo. Is the security of the World Expo very strict? The detector probes all over the body.

When we left the park, a worker carrying an oxygen tank also wanted to go out. An armed police soldier stopped him: please show your exit certificate. The worker fumbled all over his body: sorry, I forgot to bring it. He wanted to explain something, but before he could say it, he already looked desperate and carried the oxygen tank back again.

How to pass the security check smoothly, not carrying dangerous goods, everyone knows - carrying these things, you can't even get close to the World Expo Park. At each subway entrance, train station, and bus station entrance, there is a security check - the key is, can some suspected dangerous goods be carried? For example, fruit knives? It should be fine to peel fruits and eat when you're tired of touring the park, right?

However, the units responsible for construction will not be able to be "accommodated" like us. They have to rely on showing special access passes to enter and exit. If you forget to bring it, it won't work, even if you are familiar with the face, you can't get in. At the east entrance, the person in charge of a construction company negotiated with the security guard many times, but due to the lack of a special certificate, he couldn't get in. This person in charge had no choice but to call while scolding his subordinates: after such a long time of construction, even this cannot be solved?!

Our suggestion is: don't bring it. Starting from April 1, Shanghai requires real-name purchase of fruit knives. Although we haven't tried it, it is absolutely impossible to bring fruit knives into the park - they can't even pass the plane security check, let alone the stricter World Expo security check?

And this, does not contradict strictness.

If you want to eat fruit, bring one that doesn't need to be peeled. There will certainly be some sold in the World Expo Park too.

Of course, after the opening of the World Expo, you holding a World Expo ticket will not have the problem of access pass, but the security check is still inevitable.
