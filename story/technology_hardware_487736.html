The flowers bloom on this shore.

by haitaohnf on 2011-09-10 17:37:47

The flowers of the other shore bloom, without loss or sorrow, weeping alone in the netherworld. The flowers are bright but the people do not return. Who in this world of dust will bear the pain of parting? On the road to the Yellow Springs, eyes are blurred with tears.

Note:

The flowers of the other shore bloom for a thousand years and fall for a thousand years, the flowers and leaves never meet.

It is red, it is the blood-curdling color, it is the boundless prosperity after the withering and blooming again. It is poison, and it is medicine. It is the last feast on the verge of eternal slumber.

Legend has it that these flowers only bloom on the Yellow Springs, and they are the only scenery on the road to the Yellow Springs.

Du, the guardian of the Naihe Bridge, is neither alive nor dead, neither good nor evil.

On the Naihe Bridge, how many souls wander like loneliness escaping in the cold wind.

Among the sea of flowers on the other shore, there is a soul hesitating. Du showed a strange smile, another person couldn't cross. Du looked at the sea of flowers on the other shore, then turned his eyes to the soul who couldn't cross. How many couldn't cross? The souls that couldn't cross would become the flowers of the other shore beside the Naihe Bridge, blooming for a thousand years, falling for a thousand years, forever enduring the冲洗of the river of hell, watching others pass by calmly.

You, are you the legendary God of Death?

I am just the guardian of this bridge. Only when you can't cross will I become the God of Death.

I can't cross. Why? Life is like ashes, it's your own fault!

Du, I have one last wish unfulfilled. That's why I can't cross the Naihe Bridge.

I can help you fulfill your wish, but you must also promise me that when your wish is fulfilled, you will become a flower of the other shore, forever brilliant beside the Naihe Bridge. I am not only the ferryman on the Naihe Bridge, but also the flower god of the underworld.

I want to love my mother again, simply love her. In the last life, I was sorry to her. I didn't fulfill my duty as a son, I didn't listen to my mother's advice, I made mistake after mistake, and went down the wrong path. I thought my mother no longer cared about me, but I was wrong. On the way to the Yellow Springs, I always heard my mother sobbing. If I had time, I hope she would grow old, just as she watched me grow up.

Before the cycle begins, the final promise. Do you feel it? Have you heard?

As if a gentle breeze swept across the vast sea of flowers, the flowers of the other shore trembled slightly. There is no wind in the underworld, could it be that the flowers of the other shore are about to wither? Legend has it that when the flowers of the other shore wither, it means a soul has touched the great taboo of the underworld: having expectations. No, Du is the flower god, he will not allow the flowers of the other shore to wither. Du's heart had slight ripples.

I can satisfy your other wish, for example, you can ask for gold, agate, pearls, I can also promise you wealth and honor in the next life, warmth and comfort all your life...

No need. The soul interrupted Du's words, how would you understand? The guardian of the Naihe Bridge, the flower god of the underworld, sees through life and death, how could you understand family affection? Well, I don't blame you, asking the God of Death for anything is absurd.

Du fell into deep thought. Suddenly looking back, the previous ferryman on the Naihe Bridge became trapped in the cycle of life and death because of having expectations. She once said to Du: I have never regretted, as the ferryman of the underworld, though I have eternal life, what use is that? What the underworld lacks most is the warmth of human life. At that time, Du did not understand, but now Du has followed in the footsteps of the previous ferryman.

When Du was not yet the ferryman of the underworld, she also had a warm family in the human world, kind parents, but happiness was as fragile as walking on thin ice. When Du was 10 years old, a natural disaster took the lives of her whole family. In the underworld, on the Naihe Bridge a thousand years ago, Du thought she had experienced life and death in the human world, and did not want to be trapped in the cycle of life and death anymore, so she became the youngest ferryman in the underworld.

This bridge, this sea of flowers, has been guarded for a thousand years.

Du ferried countless souls, but also developed expectations, Du, could not cross either.

Du wanted to experience the cycle, to search for the warmth left behind in the human world. This expectation was so strong, accumulated in her heart for a thousand years, like a tide swallowing Du's heart, driving her to madness.

I allow you, go ahead, when you finish your wish, stay in the human world.

Really?

Yes, the ferryman of the underworld never lies. But, would you like to go with me?

In the underworld, if a soul returns to the human world and lives happily ever after, the ferryman on the Naihe Bridge must pay with their eternal life, entering the mortal world together with the soul and experiencing the cycle.

Du, aren't you guarding this bridge anymore?

I have expectations, even I cannot cross.

Du left, petals of the flowers of the other shore fell everywhere behind her, like a sea of red tears.

The vague thoughts of the other shore dissipate far away, the flowers bloom endlessly, pure like celestial music, distant and yet entangled, peaceful and yet delicate. The clouds on the horizon reflect the color of the flowers of the other shore, pure. When the crabapple flowers wither, when the scattered years of yore, the flowers of the other shore quietly bloom far away.

Under the Naihe Bridge, what can be done?
