Company Introduction

I. Company Overview

Yuyang IP, established in 1998, consists of three major institutions: Zhejiang Yuyang Intellectual Property Agency Co., Ltd., Hangzhou Yuyang Patent Office, and Zhejiang Yuyang Law Firm. It is a professional agency authorized by the State Administration for Industry and Commerce, the National Intellectual Property Administration, and the Ministry of Justice of the People's Republic of China, specializing in legal affairs related to intellectual property. Yuyang IP is a one-stop professional intellectual property company that integrates domestic and international patent, trademark, copyright applications, rights protection, and relevant legal services. Over the past decade, to meet the continuously growing business needs, the company has purchased and moved into a new office building with a floor area of 1250 square meters. We will provide you with the most comprehensive services in the field of intellectual property.

II. Industry Ranking and Business Status

The core services of Yuyang IP mainly include domestic and foreign trademark and patent applications, trademark and patent transactions, famous trademark agency, intellectual property rights protection, etc. The services cover various regions of China and more than 40 countries and regions. In the domestic market, the volume of trademark registration agency has ranked first in Zhejiang Province for five consecutive years. In the international market, the company has good business relations with intellectual property agencies in more than 30 countries and regions. While constantly expanding its agency business, the company has also opened up trademark and patent trading platforms on its website and "Yuyang Information". In addition, it jointly founded the "Zhejiang Trademark Resource Trading Center" with the Zhejiang Administration for Industry and Commerce and the Zhejiang Trademark Association, and set up "Yuyang Online" in the "Zhejiang Trademark" magazine. After ten years of efforts and accumulation, Yuyang IP today is constantly moving towards the direction of becoming the "first brand in the industry region".

III. Operation Situation and Business Structure

Adhering to the service principle of customer supremacy, Yuyang IP provides all-round intellectual property legal consulting services. After ten years of efforts, Zhejiang Yuyang Intellectual Property Agency Co., Ltd. has gathered a group of experienced trademark agents, patent agents, several intellectual property lawyers, and excellent trademark design planners; it also owns modern office equipment and patent and trademark inquiry and management systems. Each department has clear responsibilities, a perfect economic assessment responsibility system, and a sound financial management system. It has now developed into one of the largest intellectual property agencies in China with complete personnel and facilities, and a comprehensive range of business fields.

While continuously expanding its own business, Zhejiang Yuyang Intellectual Property Agency Co., Ltd. actively responds to government calls, voluntarily cooperates with government requirements, and participates in some representative intellectual property projects. The intellectual property protection street on Zhongshan North Road in Xiacheng District has already shown initial results, and the pilot project for upgrading the industrial structure from "Made in Zhejiang" to "Created in Zhejiang" is also underway. With the continuous expansion of business fields and capabilities, Yuyang IP is striving to develop from "agency" to "agency + planning", a higher-end direction.

IV. Market Reputation and Public Awareness

The company enjoys a good business reputation, having received honors such as "AAA Credit Rating Enterprise", "Famous Trademark of Hangzhou City", "Well-known Trade Name of Zhejiang Province", "Zhejiang Business Most Investment Potential Enterprise in 2009", and Outstanding Patent Agency in the 2008 "Creative Hangzhou" Industrial Design Competition. Yuyang IP is also a member of the China Trademark Association and the International Trademark Association, a director of the Zhejiang Trademark Association, and the deputy director unit of the Professional Committee of Trademark Agencies under the Zhejiang Trademark Association.

The company attaches great importance to publicity work: in the domestic market, it conducts long-term publicity through various forms such as television, mobile media, newspapers, magazines, and outdoor advertisements. Under the constant care and support of all sectors of society, the brand image of Yuyang IP has taken root in people's hearts; in the international market, the company leaders have visited many countries such as the United States, the United Kingdom, and Dubai for exchanges and promotion, and organized and conducted the "Spanish and Portuguese-speaking Countries Intellectual Property Seminar", inviting international experts to give lectures in Hangzhou, greatly enhancing the international reputation of Yuyang IP.

V. Implementation of Integrity Service and Labor Protection

The company has an internal Customer Service Department specifically responsible for providing consultation, follow-up visits, and professional handling of customer complaints. Long-term enthusiastic and considerate intellectual property services reflect the "high-quality, efficient, integrity" service standards advocated and adhered to by Yuyang IP, and the "customer-first" service principle.

Yuyang IP's management model is "making full use of talents, people-oriented", implementing humanized management. For employees in trademark and patent business departments, they must have a bachelor's degree or above in law or science and engineering, undergo one year of training and internship, and pass strict assessments before they can independently conduct client agency services. In work, the company pays attention to the cultivation of employee qualities and arranges annual quality development training for employees.

In terms of labor protection, Yuyang IP signs labor employment contracts with each employee, actively cultivates talents that meet the requirements of Yuyang IP, and allows employees to continuously integrate into the Yuyang family with generous welfare benefits and a good corporate atmosphere. At the same time, it continuously enhances the corporate culture of Yuyang IP.

Yuyang positions itself with "harmony, integrity, professionalism, and grandeur", takes high-quality, efficient, and honest service as its service principle, and has handled trademark affairs for many domestic and international enterprises.
