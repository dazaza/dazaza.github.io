Naked into the "South Pole" - The penguin exhibition area is set up to simulate the penguin's living environment - Antarctica, with a temperature of about -10℃ here. The crystal box where the Ice King is located will be further cooled down by ice blocks, and the temperature inside is about -30℃." Staff members of the Polar Ocean World introduced.

The staff helped him remove the ice around him. After slightly moving, Jin Songhao walked to the backstage, and his body showed no discomfort, only a little redness. He wiped off the ice chips on his body with a towel while drinking a cup of warm water. A few minutes later, he had already adapted to the room temperature of the "Northern Hemisphere." Walking out of the rest room, Jin Songhao smiled and said: "The environment of Tianjin Haichang Polar Ocean World is very suitable for challenging the limit of cold resistance. The enthusiasm of tourists also gave me great support. So the success of this challenge can be said to be an expected event."

In the -30℃ environment, eating ice lollipops, drinking ice beer, drawing comics, feeding penguins... This year, Jin Songhao, who has just won the title of "World's First Ice King," surpassed himself again at Tianjin Haichang Polar Ocean World. He broke his own world record of 120 minutes by being buried in ice naked for 128 minutes, successfully defending the title of "World's First Ice King."

Facing this stranger, the little penguins were very curious and kept coming in groups to watch. They either looked up or walked around, and their cute movements made the surrounding tourists burst out laughing. Around 15:00, it was time to feed the penguins. The curious penguins gave up watching and ran happily towards the feeder. Seeing this scene, the "Ice King" proposed to feed the penguins personally. A king penguin cautiously approached the "Ice King," hesitating several times to get closer. Then the "Ice King" handed the fish in his hand to the king penguin, and the big guy stretched out its long mouth to pick up the fish and proudly turned away, as if boasting to the surrounding little penguins: "Did you see? The 'Ice King' fed me personally!"

Drawing attracted penguin spectators

Perhaps in order to further show his cold-resistant qualities, Jin Songhao asked the staff to bring 3 ice lollipops, which he ate one by one. Perhaps still not satisfied, he then opened 3 bottles of chilled beer and drank them cup by cup. During the eating and drinking process, he did not forget to showcase his talents, holding a pen in one hand and a board in the other, writing words such as "Tribute to the people of Tianjin" and "I love you, Tianjin Haichang Polar Ocean World," and even drew a cartoon of the cute penguins.

When the timer exceeded 120 minutes, everyone present began cheering for the "Ice King," but the "Ice King" continued to challenge his own limits, pushing towards 128 minutes. Accompanied by a 10-second countdown, Jin Songhao overcame himself amidst the cheers of the tourists, creating a new record.

At 13:30 on August 6th, the 56-year-old "World's First Ice King" Jin Songhao arrived at the Tianjin Haichang Polar Ocean World. He walked into the penguin living area wearing only a pair of shorts. This scene intrigued the "little masters" of the aquarium, who boldly followed the feeder to look at the "Ice King." After 25 minutes of warm-up exercises, Jin Songhao calmly sat in the prepared crystal box, and the staff poured about 0.8 cubic meters of ice into it until it covered his shoulders.
