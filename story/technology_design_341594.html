Discover The Real Beauty Of Opt-In Email Marketing Campaign

Persuasive copywriting will drive qualified targeted traffic to your website and an opt-in email marketing campaign is a great way to market your site to prospects that are ready and willing to purchase your products or services online. The best thing that you need to do is to use targeted opt-in lists and these are lists where people have given their permission to receive information on particular products or services that interest them. In fact, 90 percent of opt-in email marketing recipients read that piece of information. That is why it is very important to use targeted, emotionally charged sales words in the subject line of your direct email marketing. You should craft the subject line to guarantee that recipients, who are ready to purchase product, will open that email and this is absolutely critical to your marketing success. They come to your website ready to buy your products or services and that is the real beauty of direct email marketing campaigns.

Many prospects will save the direct email marketing piece in their "to do" file or it will linger in their inbox. Therefore, traffic still continues to trickle in for months. That traffic will turn into even more leads and sales. There are companies who write three different emails for each opt-in email marketing campaign. By writing 3 pieces of emails, you could measure which ones bring the most traffic to your site. Then, the most effective will then become the Control for further opt-in email marketing campaigns. (A control is simply the standard by which further direct email marketing is measured against.)

Therefore, a persuasive opt-in email marketing campaign copywriting will:

- Get your direct emails read by crafting a powerful subject line.

- Get prospects to your site by selling them great benefits in the body of the email.

- And explode your web profits by getting ready, willing and able buyers to your website.

Having an ongoing opt-in email marketing campaign directed at your current customer or prospect list is a must for all successful website owners. There are literally thousands of direct email marketing lists available and the website design company can tailor your direct email marketing campaign to market to the most suitable lists for your particular product or service. They could sort through all of those direct email lists and make the most profitable choice for your opt-in email marketing campaign.

PS: 70% of your opt-in email marketing campaign readers will read the word "PS"! Use the PS to either restate the benefits of your product or service online, to offer bonus, or to offer a secondary product or service online.

PSS: Another proven secret to web marketing success is placing inexpensive ads in targeted direct email newsletters and ezines. You will find this to be one of the great advertising tools for your budget.
