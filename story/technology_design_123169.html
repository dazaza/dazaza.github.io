Introduction:

Jackie Chan's works over the years have always been impressive. Each time, his performances become more thrilling and he strives for perfection in every scene, which brings excellent box office results. In this movie, in order to capture the leader of a car theft gang, he risks his life racing against skilled drivers. Besides showing off his almost all-round racing skills, making the audience feel satisfied, from the first second to the last, the tense and exciting scenes are truly breathtaking... Since he was young, Ah Huo has loved racing cars, but due to poor family economic conditions, he could not realize his dream of becoming a race car driver. After growing up, he worked as a technician at a large Japanese car factory, responsible for testing new cars for test drivers. However, good times didn't last long, and he was falsely accused and fired. After returning to Hong Kong, he worked as a modification worker in his father's old car factory and joined the police investigation team for illegal racing organizations. With his familiarity with cars, the investigation work went smoothly. During the mission, Ah Huo met female journalist Amy, and they became close friends. One day, they discovered that the car theft gang used illegal racers to transport stolen luxury cars. The head of the gang, Cook, is a reckless driver. Ah Huo assisted the police in capturing Cook, but due to insufficient evidence, the trial was delayed, allowing Cook to escape from prison and declare that he would challenge Ah Huo on the race track. Soon, Cook joined a Japanese reckless racing team and participated in the Sendai International Car Race. Ah Huo used half of his life savings and financial assistance from his friends to buy a racing car. He fiercely competed with Cook on the Japanese race track. Ultimately, Ah Huo defeated his opponent, won the racing championship, and successfully captured the professional killer Cook...
