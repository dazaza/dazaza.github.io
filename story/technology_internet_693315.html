Admission Prospectus for Part-time Master of Education Program at Central China Normal University in 2012

I. Admission Requirements

Applicants must have obtained a bachelor's degree or above from a recognized educational institution before July 31, 2009 and hold a graduation certificate (generally with a degree certificate). They should be currently employed as full-time teachers or administrators of cultural foundation courses in ordinary middle schools, primary schools, kindergartens, or other secondary schools. Also, those working in provincial, municipal, district, or county-level education research departments or government education systems who have teaching positions equivalent to middle school, primary school, or kindergarten teachers are eligible. Applicants meeting the requirements will need their qualification review form filled out by their employer’s personnel department with a recommendation.

II. Disciplines Offered

The program offers admissions in 19 fields including Educational Management, Subject Teaching (Political Education, Chinese Language, Mathematics, Physics, Chemistry, Biology, English, History, Geography, Physical Education, Art), Modern Educational Technology, Mental Health Education, Science and Technology Education, etc. The field of Modern Educational Technology is specifically offered to teachers engaged in Information Technology courses in middle and primary schools.

III. Examination Subjects

Political Theory, Foreign Language (English), Pedagogy, Psychology, Professional Foundation Course, making a total of five subjects. Political Theory is a re-examination subject; Foreign Language (English), Pedagogy, and Psychology are part of the national joint examination. The professional foundation course is self-proposed by our university and has the same exam time as the national joint examination.

IV. Joint Examination Syllabus

1. "National Joint Examination Syllabus for Part-time Master Degree English" (Science and Technology Literature Press); for the direction of Subject Teaching (English), "National Uniform (Joint) Examination Syllabus for Part-time Master of Education Degree (English II)" (Beijing Normal University Press) is used.

2. "National Uniform (Joint) Examination Syllabus and Guidelines for Part-time Master of Education Degree" (Pedagogy, Psychology) (Beijing Normal University Press).

V. Enrollment Plan and Admission

In 2012, the planned enrollment is 200 students. The admission score line is independently determined by our university based on candidates' entrance examination results (including re-examinations), with merit-based selection.

VI. Teaching and Degree Awarding

Graduates admitted to our Master of Education degree program will follow the curriculum plan for master's level professional degrees in education. Depending on personal circumstances, they can choose between full-time study (concentrating on campus learning for one year) and part-time study (working while studying). The study duration is 2 to 4 years. After completing all courses with satisfactory exam results, finishing the thesis, and successfully defending it, the Master of Education professional degree will be conferred.

VII. Tuition Fee

Tuition fee is 18,000 RMB (excluding textbook fees and accommodation fees).
