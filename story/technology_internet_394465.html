Let's Explore Traditional Chinese Medicine (TCM) Beauty Written by Taste of Whitening in Intensive Care

Today, let's switch gears and return to our traditional treasure ―― TCM adjustment. TCM not only focuses on treating diseases and saving lives but also excels in beauty adjustments. Throughout history, countless TCM practitioners have spent their entire lives devising methods to enhance the beauty of palace women, leaving behind a wealth of experience that is absolutely worth our attention!

So, everyone, let's explore TCM beauty together~

"TCM Beauty" has a profound cultural heritage rooted in Chinese culture. Through centuries of beauty practices, generation after generation of folk beauty and health preservation methods have accumulated infinite Oriental wisdom.

Whether it was the ancient imperial court or modern cities, women have always been thoughtful about how to make themselves more beautiful.

And "TCM Beauty", with its comprehensive internal and external adjustments, and the concept of achieving health and beauty from within, is increasingly favored by those who value beauty and health.

However, with the myriad of TCM beauty products available now, what methods would be effective for oneself? Which products could benefit us? And through what methods should we seek out products suitable for ourselves? I believe these are the issues that concern us all the most, and also the most headache-inducing problems.

To address this, I've visited supermarkets, malls, and beauty salons. I found an issue: supermarkets and malls generally do not sell genuine TCM products, while some beauty salons do use them. When asking the merchants, without exception, they all introduced the merits of their products, claiming what would happen after usage and testimonials from customers. However, these are ultimately one-sided statements from merchants, making it difficult for us to find actual users to consult unless our friends around us are using them. I'm not saying their products are bad; every product that survives in the market must have a certain customer base, like Guangzhou VIP Information Technology Co., Ltd. Personally, I still place greater emphasis on our traditional TCM beauty products.

Due to work reasons, I often surf online, so I decided to utilize the internet to search. Taobao.com is China's largest shopping and trading platform. Usually, I often buy clothes and various international brand cosmetics there. But I've never explored our own TCM beauty-related items online. After searching, I found quite a few treasures. I decided to find a store with relatively high credibility to inquire.

I clicked into a TCM beauty store named "Green Forest." Honestly, what initially attracted me to click in was the image of an ancient beauty on their products. Hehe, the love for beauty transcends gender. After reading their introductions, it turned out they were from a family of TCM practitioners, and all the products in their small shop were uniquely formulated by their elderly TCM doctor. This piqued my curiosity. Wow, I wondered if they could also formulate a secret prescription for me, transforming me overnight into an invincible youthful beauty. After checking a few more product introductions, I discovered that the old TCM doctor in this shop was around 50 years old but looked as if he were only 35 in the photos. Truly impressive, making me admire and envy him. Thinking that if I looked like him at 50, it would be acceptable.

Thinking about this, I decided to consult the shopkeeper as a customer to see how she would introduce her products to me.

Editor: Shopkeeper, hello, may I ask if your products are all pure TCM?

Shopkeeper: Hello, yes, they are all pure TCM. May I ask what you need?

Editor: I want to understand first.

Shopkeeper: Sure, no problem. What aspect would you like to learn about?

Editor: Your products are said to be formulated by an old TCM doctor. How long will it take to see results?

Shopkeeper: Yes, they are formulated by an old TCM doctor. TCM emphasizes internal and external adjustments. Unlike general beauty products containing chemical substances or hormones, the effects won't be immediate, but they can address both symptoms and root causes, fundamentally solving the problem. Our product course lasts one month, and the speed of effectiveness varies from person to person. Some people can see results after one course, while others need two courses or more. Generally, the longer you use it, the better the results.

Editor: Are there any customers who say it didn't work?

Shopkeeper: Yes, some customers are impatient and stop using it after a few times, so they say it didn't work. Our products are not miracle drugs, so it's impossible to achieve satisfactory results after just a few uses. For example, some customers have very yellowish faces, are young, and don't have other illnesses. These customers generally suffer from blood and qi deficiency, so we adopt dual supplementation of blood and qi, along with internal and external adjustments. For such customers, the adjustment usually takes at least two months. Some customers give up halfway, then claim the product doesn't work. As long as they persist, there will be results.

Editor: It seems that beautifying through TCM methods cannot be rushed.

Shopkeeper: Yes, because each person's situation is different, the time required for adjustment will vary. As long as customers can persist, their skin will improve significantly compared to before.

Editor: Okay, thank you, Shopkeeper. I have a basic understanding now. I'll come back next time when I need something. Wishing your business prosperity!

Shopkeeper: Sure, you're welcome. Feel free to visit anytime.

The Editor believes that the quality of a product depends on its reputation. The store's credibility is quite good, with many repeat customers. The Editor decided to investigate the opinions of their customers to verify the truth. Among numerous online buyers, I chose one with a Yellow Diamond credit rating. After explaining my intentions, she willingly chatted with me. Below is a segment of our main conversation (to protect her privacy, she is referred to as Xiao A here).

Editor: How long have you been using this store's TCM beauty products?

Xiao A: About eight months ago. At the time, due to prolonged sleep deprivation, my skin was in terrible condition. My complexion was dull, my skin dry, with fine lines and even spots. Since I have sensitive skin, I didn't dare to use random spot-removal products. I was looking for a mild TCM spot-removal product and accidentally found this store. So, I started trying it from then on. Seeing good results, I continued using it.

Editor: What products did you use?

Xiao A: The shopkeeper helped me choose a whitening and spot-removal jade face pill, a full-effect whitening and spot-removal mask, and a moisturizing anti-wrinkle mask.

Editor: Did it really work?

Xiao A: Yes, after one month of use, my complexion became much more even, my face wasn't as dry, the spots faded slightly, and my skin lightened a bit. Although I wasn't extremely fair yet, it wasn't as sallow as before.

Editor: How has your skin improved now?

Xiao A: Now, my skin is genuinely whiter and smoother. The spots have become much lighter and less noticeable, almost invisible after applying foundation. The fine lines have also become very shallow. My skin looks hydrated and radiant, and in the morning, my skin appears rosy. To be honest, I am very satisfied with the current results. Moreover, I recommended some friends to try it, and they also said the effects were good.

Editor: Have there been any side effects?

Xiao A: No, the shopkeeper asked about my physical condition before recommending the products, so there haven't been any abnormalities. However, since TCM works slowly, the process took longer.

Editor: Are you still using them consistently?

Xiao A: Yes. I've been taking the whitening and beauty jade face pills for six months, and I've been using the full-effect mask and the moisturizing anti-wrinkle mask twice a week for eight months now. The shopkeeper suggested that I take the jade face pills again every two months since they can also prevent aging. Women fear aging the most, haha.

Editor: It seems our long-standing beauty treasure is indeed excellent.

Xiao A: Yes. You can try it too, talk to the shopkeeper.

Eternal youth is humanity's beautiful wish, but natural things always go from birth to death, from prosperity to decline. As a part of nature, humans cannot escape this objective rule. Therefore, as age increases, the human body will also age.

In TCM theory, although it is impossible to stay young forever, replenishing the loss of organs and qi-blood balance can maintain the yin-yang balance of the human body, which indeed delays the arrival of aging. We believe that in today's world where people value nature and green concepts, TCM, with its five-thousand-year accumulation of strength, still has its unique charm in preventing aging and beauty care.
