After heartbreak, the disguises of the 12 zodiacs:

Aries disguise: Progress

Aries are always pure like children, so during heartbreak, they are most likely to follow the textbook advice..."turn sorrow into strength," working harder and improving themselves. Due to Aries' competitive nature, they usually hold the view of "after breaking up, I must live better than you." So when you encounter an Aries behaving as described, you don't need to point it out. Just knowing what happened internally, there's no need to worry excessively.

Taurus disguise: Busy

Heartbroken Taureans typically fill their schedule completely. Overtime work, travel, and spending time with parents at home are the best "disguises" for Taureans during the heartbreak period. For Taureans, forgetting someone is difficult, so it's better to shift the energy of missing someone to work and entertainment, not allowing oneself free time to think about past feelings.

Gemini disguise: Nonsense Chat

Gemini talks a lot, famously so, so at first when they start blabbering nonsense, you usually don't notice they've gone through a breakup. Gradually, when you realize that the logic in their speech isn't as clear and sharp as before, and they start pulling unrelated things together, you should suspect that Gemini has probably gone through a breakup. This person won't speak well if heartbroken. They might play the piano alone.

Cancer disguise: Acceleration

"This coffee shop, my ex and I used to come here frequently. Last April, he gave me my first bouquet of beautiful white roses right here. We always liked sitting near this window because it overlooks the herbs in the backyard..." If Cancer invites you out at night and tells you these kinds of stories, don't assume she's recounting the sweetness of her romance. The truth could be quite the opposite.

Leo disguise: Rename

In the proud Leo's logic, even breakups are the fault of the other party. So even if they never mention it, when you hear from Leo about the person who was once called "one of a kind in heaven and earth," suddenly turning into someone worthless, you should understand what has happened.

Virgo disguise: Out of Control

To uncover Virgo's post-breakup "disguise" often requires some time, as Virgos are also skilled at maintaining a "calm surface." So as a Virgo girlfriend, don't be surprised by such occurrences... why does a seemingly fine Virgo stop in her tracks upon hearing a song from a roadside shop window? Why do her tears uncontrollably flow while she claims it's just dust in her eyes? There's a significant possibility that the pain of the breakup has accumulated for too long and too heavily, finally bursting forth.

Libra disguise: Temper

For Libras, who pursue harmony and balance, a breakup is their moment of imbalance. During this time, Libras' tempers are the worst, small matters can make them angry, not only puffing up their faces but also making their words somewhat harsh. If you understand the real reason behind their anger, it wouldn't hurt to tolerate them as they vent their frustrations.

Scorpio disguise: Deep Thought

Post-breakup Scorpios are more likely to fall into deep thought than usual. Many people believe that Scorpios have the strongest desire for revenge after a breakup, but in reality, while they may consider ways to severely punish the unfaithful person during their deep thought, those who truly loved won't really act on it. What they think more about is: letting go of me was their loss, let them regret it for a lifetime.

Sagittarius disguise: Happiness

Sagittarians are optimistic and forgetful, so when faced with a heart-wrenching event like a breakup, they instead appear happier. Sometimes inexplicable bursts of laughter confuse others, because only in this way can the forgetful Sagittarius gradually become numb to this feigned happiness, eventually forgetting the pain of the breakup.

Capricorn disguise: Calmness

Just as few people notice their romances, Capricorns' post-breakup "calmness" makes it hard to detect their inner pain. They continue to work and live as usual, even when alone, they remain composed without revealing a hint of emotion. No, after enduring life's hardships, time grants you the gift of wisdom. Actually, if you're observant enough, you'll discover: the pain exists, hidden in the slight hesitation when Capricorn interacts with others.

Aquarius disguise: Solitude

During a breakup, Aquarians prefer more time alone, whether hiding in their own room, running to a quiet suburb, or staying in a rarely frequented café. They appear calm on the surface, but inside, they repeatedly analyze the lost relationship. However, as a friend, you don't need to worry; once rational, they'll move on once they've thought it through.

Pisces disguise: Tears

Some say that since fish live in water, people can't see their tears. This saying is indeed the true reflection of a heartbroken Pisces. Sensitive and emotional Pisceans, if planning to "hide" their heartbreak, often choose to read a touching novel or watch a moving movie, not for any other reason, but to give themselves a suitable excuse to cry.

The Zodiac Signs That Shouldn't Be Loved!!!

Aries shouldn't date: Capricorn, Cancer, Leo

Aries are always the child leaders in love, both capricious and prone to mood swings, always thinking of themselves first and rarely considering their partner's feelings. Capricorn personalities are rational and mature, finding it particularly hard to accept Aries' unpredictable behavior. Initially, the complementary personalities may seem compatible, but gradually, they may grow distant and end up breaking up hastily; Cancer is often emotionally unstable in love, which irritates Aries; additionally, the prideful Leo usually can't tolerate Aries' capriciousness, leading to fiery conflicts between the two fire signs that are hard to resolve.

Solution:

Aries often exhibit various issues after the honeymoon phase. Once the relationship stabilizes, their usual carefree behavior reveals itself, easily surprising their partner. Remember, even after the honeymoon phase, one should still pay attention to one's image!

Taurus shouldn't date: Pisces, Scorpio, Aquarius

Most Tauruses don't know how to romantically express love and often misunderstand emotions. Pisces, who drink romance like water, may initially be attracted to Taurus' maturity and stability but will eventually leave due to boredom; Scorpio and Taurus start with intense passion, but after some time, they are prone to major arguments, ultimately falling out; Aquarius usually falls in love with Taurus impulsively, but these lightning-fast romances often end just as quickly, resulting in no further contact.

Solution:

Taurus should really work on improving their communication skills and remember to explain things clearly when necessary. Simply staying silent won't help anyone understand what you're thinking. When dealing with the above three signs, consider writing cards or love letters to compensate for verbal inadequacies.

Gemini shouldn't date: Virgo, Scorpio, Leo

Gemini's love life is always exciting, filled with former lovers, senior/junior classmates, and countless ambiguous relationships, making their current partners extremely anxious. Virgos, who are particular about love, will not only lose control emotionally but also become mentally exhausted; Scorpios react fiercely to unfaithful partners, employing various retaliatory tactics; Leos cannot tolerate their partners spreading affection around, resulting in frequent tantrums—crying, sulking, and threatening suicide—which make Geminis try to avoid such situations at all costs!

Solution:

Cutting off Gemini's flirtatious tendencies might be difficult, but at least remember where the boundaries lie. This is not only a responsible attitude towards love but also a sign of respect for your partner. It's best to be a well-behaved lover!

Cancer shouldn't date: Gemini, Aries, Sagittarius

Cancers are very afraid of getting hurt in love, especially by a single word from their partner, causing their moods to fluctuate wildly—like weather changes from sunny to cloudy with occasional rain showers. Joke-loving Geminis often unknowingly offend Cancers, leading to constant quarrels; straightforward Aries, though mostly unintentional, already cause Cancers to lose control emotionally; Sagittarius' tendency to bring up uncomfortable topics can make Cancers explode!

Solution:

Anything said or done by a partner can significantly affect a Cancer's mood. Cancers should learn to differentiate between jokes and temporary grievances to avoid difficulties in living with the aforementioned three signs.

Leo shouldn't date: Sagittarius, Capricorn, Leo

Leos tend to control their partners in relationships, similar to how kings govern their subjects. They are often authoritarian and expect compliance, but when paired with freedom-loving Sagittarius, not only can they not control them, but it leads to major arguments; when encountering opinionated Capricorns, Leo's capriciousness only causes the relationship to fracture; Leo with another Leo is a disaster, like star-crossed lovers. Arguments and reconciliations are manageable, but ending with a falling-out is hard to mend.

Solution:

Rein in your controlling temper! Although Leos are kings or queens in love, their partners are not slaves. An overly authoritative attitude will only drive partners away, especially when dealing with the uncontrollable three signs. Leos should look the other way occasionally to avoid self-inflicted misery.

Virgo shouldn't date: Taurus, Gemini, Sagittarius

Virgos often silently contribute in relationships, becoming subordinate partners, which is a manifestation of effort in love. However, if paired with greedy Tauruses, their contributions are often taken for granted, making Virgos feel perpetually indebted; encountering flirtatious Geminis, Virgos' efforts fail to capture Gemini's heart; and facing unrestrained Sagittarius, Virgos' sacrifices and contributions are deemed unnecessary.

Solution:

Focus on yourself, and the relationship will progress smoothly. Remember to take care of yourself first, and the partner will love you even more. When Virgos are happy and doing well, the relationship will be harmonious. Excessive sacrifice will make the partner perceive it as cheap and unworthy of cherishing!

Libra shouldn't date: Aries, Cancer, Taurus

Librans value fairness in love, but this standard is often set by themselves. When a partner's actions make Libra feel that the balance between giving and receiving is uneven, extra caution is needed! Especially with Aries, who always prioritize their own stance, Libra feels treated unequally; when paired with emotionally volatile Cancers, Libra finds it challenging to manage the situation; and when paired with calculating Taureans, Libra discovers significant differences in standards, leading to endless arguments over fairness.

Solution:

Actually, Librans with good temperaments rarely encounter too many relationship problems, but being overly strict about fairness can make them feel their partners don't treat them well enough, leading to endless complaints. It's recommended that Librans relax their standards a bit and stay more positive.

Scorpio shouldn't date: Aquarius, Pisces, Libra

Scorpions are possessive in love, with sharp intuition detecting any movement from their partner immediately. However, dating Aquarians, who habitually lie, Scorpios often experience intense anxiety until the breakup; facing Pisces, who flirts indiscriminately, Scorpios struggle to accept and control it, leading to frustration; and with Libras, who represent kindness and goodwill, Scorpios easily become anxious seeing Libras surrounded by third parties.

Solution:

Relax a bit! Constantly bombarding your partner with calls isn't a good idea. Learning to trust your partner is better than pushing them away with high-pressure tactics. Moreover, if your partner intends to deceive you, catching them won't change much, but it's advisable to avoid relationships with overly flirtatious individuals.

Sagittarius shouldn't date: Cancer, Taurus, Libra

Sagittarians approach love somewhat loosely, pondering the future prospects of being with someone. If it's just a short-term fling, they won't invest much emotionally, appearing unfocused and unfaithful. When paired with sensitive and pure-hearted Cancers, Cancers easily sense Sagittarius' lack of devotion, making arguments unavoidable; when paired with persistent Taureans, Sagittarians may feel annoyed by their inability to shake them off, potentially turning them into enemies; and with Librans, Sagittarians find it hard to endure Libra's dependency, leading to constant arguments and eventual separation.
