Listen to the sound of the night

At night, a lonely person stands in the empty corridor, quietly searching for the entrance to the night.

Close your weary eyes and listen to the sound of the night with your heart. A series of footstep sounds drift past your ears with the cool breeze. Those heartfelt melodies are beautiful and serene. The summer in Chengdu is still maddening; it always rushes in without the gentle spring breeze.

The night deepens gradually, and the temperature becomes cooler. Looking at the crossroads I often pass by, I can't help but recall the worries I had each time I walked there. Indeed, memories mark what is most beautiful or most painful for oneself. In daily life, those extremely ordinary things have long been buried in the bamboo forest behind the mountain, where no one can find them, not even myself.

In such a quiet night, thoughts are like puddles on the cobblestones after the rain, reflecting dazzling light under the care of the moonlight. Clear pictures reappear in my mind again. A wonderful childhood is what I yearn for now - free from emotional constraints and financial pressures. I remember that when I was a child, my grandfather could easily pacify my mischievousness with just a crab bread and a pack of angel potato chips. I used to love going for walks with my grandfather after dinner, listening to his stories about the past while walking. Although I didn't fully understand many of the stories, I always asked him to tell more, holding his hand and walking unsteadily under the warm sunset. Looking ahead, the elongated shadows cast by the remaining sunlight were always so warm and happy.

Time slips away silently between the fingers like moonlight, and many things are no longer as they were in childhood - innocent and pure. Every event cannot always bring you happiness; perhaps, it will bring you immense pain, which will be deeply imprinted in your mind.

Looking up at the night sky, bright stars decorate the dark Milky Way. How I wish the heavens would grant me a few bright stars to illuminate my inner self, allowing me to stay away from darkness and sorrow.

Confused, hesitating... my heart feels empty. The beauty I once fantasized about has been diluted by reality in this moment, hazy and indistinct. It's as if wrapped in layers of gauze, visible but unattainable.

Bathed in moonlight, I cleanse my turbid thoughts with its pure glow. Not everything is something I should do. Perhaps, unless I let go of everything, I won't know what I truly need. Unless I forget everything, I won't realize what I can never forget.

I stand up, brush off the dust on my body, and set out on the journey home again. No matter when, we must strive to be lovable people. Without blaming anyone, mocking anyone, or envying anyone, we shine under the moonlight, run through wind and rain, dream our own dreams, and walk our own paths.
