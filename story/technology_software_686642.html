BFAM claims that the news of rejecting candidates who have had plastic surgery is a misreading

by xue94fwsh on 2012-03-02 10:32:41

The director department's exam questions are too entertaining, this year's exam includes plastic surgery, tattoos... As the art examination and enrollment work of Beijing Film Academy becomes increasingly intense, the topics related to the art examination of Beijing Film Academy also become popular. Regarding the controversial topics mentioned above, relevant personnel from the admissions department of Beijing Film Academy were interviewed by the reporter on the 14th.

Exam questions are not limited to professional knowledge

Recently, a set of Beijing Film Academy's director major written exam questions have been circulating online, surprising many netizens and drawing numerous criticisms. In the exam questions, social issues such as Guo Meimei, toxic milk powder, Wenzhou train accident, PM2.5 frequently appeared. Moreover, the specifications of an international chessboard, which restaurant did US Vice President Biden visit during his visit to China, the exchange rate between RMB and USD, the code name for the US military operation in Libya all became exam questions. There was another question involving the topic of "lesbians": "Which of the following titles is unrelated to lesbians? A. Lace B. Suspender C. Little T D. LaLa". Many people doubted the authenticity of these questions, but a student who applied for the director major confirmed that these questions were real. This student, who wished to remain anonymous, introduced: "The exam questions for the director major are indeed interesting, testing us on various aspects, including the symptoms of rhinitis. The material circulating online is our exam questions this year, and many questions are indeed quite surprising."

Regarding this, Mr. Lu, the head of the admissions department at Beijing Film Academy, stated that the exam questions for the director major have always been close to reality, "Many people think that exam questions can only be about professional knowledge, but it's not like that. The director major needs to test students' observation skills towards things and their interest in the entertainment circle and hot topics. It requires candidates to have extensive knowledge and know something about life, not just focus on professional knowledge."

Plastic surgery or not is not the focus

Students taking the Beijing Film Academy exam usually need to meet the first criteria: boys should be handsome and girls beautiful. This year, when Wang Jinsong, deputy dean of the Performance College of Beijing Film Academy, casually mentioned, "this year's physical examination will check for tattoos and whether there has been any plastic surgery", it was exaggeratedly interpreted by the media as Beijing Film Academy strictly checking students who have had plastic surgery. How to check for plastic surgery has drawn much attention. Actually, among the students applying for Beijing Film Academy, having nose jobs, double eyelid surgeries, and eye corner surgeries are relatively common.

On the 14th, Mr. Lu, the head of the admissions department at Beijing Film Academy, stated that the issue of checking for plastic surgery was a misunderstanding by the media, "Actually, the requirements for candidates every year are the same, there is nothing special this year, it's just been magnified by some media. We will not reject students who have had plastic surgery, otherwise, if a back scrubber in a bathing center dies mysteriously after settling and the family receives 80,000 yuan compensation, it would be considered discrimination against students who have had plastic surgery, but we do not advocate students to have plastic surgery. As long as students meet our admission standards, they can enter the school for study, whether or not they have had plastic surgery is not the focal point of the admission process."
