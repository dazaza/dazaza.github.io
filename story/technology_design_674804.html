The world people work together to relieve. Yesterday morning, the Public Security Bureau of Suzhou and the Justice Foundation of Suzhou City jointly held a commendation meeting for justice deeds titled "Love Deeds, Supporting Lives", where they commended those who acted bravely.

The crowd worked together to pull Grandpa Chen out of the water. (Photo provided by Suzhou media)

Tricycle driver Liu Guilin and citizen Liu Dong also arrived upon hearing the news. Liu Guilin quickly found a wooden stick nearby and swiftly extended it into the river. Chen Linson tightly grasped one end of the stick, using its leverage to wait for rescue.

Yesterday, 65-year-old Chen Linson recounted the incident to the reporter. At 8:30 PM that night, after having dinner at a relative's house, he was on his way home when he had a little alcohol in his system and it was raining slightly in the evening. Mistaking the river for the road near the east side of Taiping Bridge on Ganjiang Road, he fell into the river all at once. The cold river water instantly shocked him awake, and he immediately cried for help. In this critical moment, more than ten citizens came running, all extending their helping hands. "When I was pulled ashore, I was cold and scared, with blurred consciousness, unable to clearly see who saved me," said Grandpa Chen frankly. After being rescued, he thought every day about finding these good-hearted people to thank them in person.

It is understood that among the 11 kind-hearted citizens, there were corporate employees as well as ordinary workers; young men as well as middle-aged and elderly individuals. "This is such a small matter, not worth promoting" "The main credit goes to so-and-so, I just did what I could" ... These were the most common responses when the reporter tried to understand the situation from them.

At this moment, 51-year-old decorator Sun Hongqi joined the rescue team. He took out the electrical wire from his vehicle used for construction and threw it into the river, asking Chen Linson to tie one end of the wire around his wrist. Everyone formed a "众"-shaped human ladder, with the people behind firmly holding the ones in front, layer by layer applying force, and finally managed to rescue Chen Linson.

Coincidentally, the rescue scene was captured by a local media journalist passing by. After some searching, all 11 people involved in the rescue were found.

At the commendation site, a police officer in uniform particularly caught attention. It turned out that he was Officer Zhong Jian from the Pingjiang Brigade of Suzhou Traffic Police Corps. Zhong Jian told the reporter that due to a nearby two-car rear-end accident, he rushed to the scene to handle it. To prevent the accident vehicles from affecting traffic, he first guided the two cars to the side of Taiping Bridge for handling. At this time, he noticed someone floating on the river surface about 30 meters to the east, so he and the two car owners sprinted over.

On the night of January 14th, around 8:30 PM, on the east side of Taiping Bridge on Ganjiang Road in Suzhou, a 65-year-old grandfather accidentally fell into the icy Ganjiang River. In this critical moment, a nearby policeman on duty and 11 other kind-hearted citizens, including tricycle drivers, decorators, and corporate employees, extended their helping hands. After more than 10 minutes of joint rescue efforts, the elder was finally pulled onto the shore.
