Skillfully use color accessories in every corner of the house.

by longersing on 2009-11-04 15:25:18

The bridge uses color to decorate every corner of the house: color has always been the biggest highlight of the home, and guests can feel the visual impact brought by the colors as soon as they enter. The designer can cleverly reflect the owner's understanding of home and their taste for life through unique color combinations. Below are successful cases made by foreign designers, through which you can feel the different cultural understandings of home. If you want to renovate your beloved home, why not take a look at their design works first?

Colors can create emotions. Imagine a tranquil aqua green bathroom, a cheerful yellow dining area, or a children's room full of vitality in white... Positive colors can certainly make your mood rise quickly. As the golden autumn arrives, why not give your home a makeover with colors? The editor of Soufun Home has carefully prepared the following 20 cases, which will surely help you.

Fresh and tender

The distinct and ingenious combination of blue and white makes people feel comfortable. The lively lemon yellow and vibrant green bring a fresh and modern visual sensation. The mix of colors and patterns is very bold, but because of the soft-toned walls, wooden floors, and white furniture as the base, it is just right and very harmonious.

Just one highlight

Imagine if all the furniture and decorations in this room were kept, including the elaborately designed bed and fashionable striped bench, but the green was replaced with dark brown or white, what would it look like? The hardware is still the same, but this space is no longer sexy or individualistic. Sometimes, using just one bright color can make a dull and boring room lively and vivid.

The key lies in accessories

Doesn't this colorful little leisure area make you feel great? Take a closer look, actually the tone of the space itself is quite neutral, with light brown furniture and elegant pale yellow walls. The focus of decoration lies in those small accessories covered with patterns on the surface, such as sky blue, crimson, pink, light green... making the entire space refreshingly stylish and full of femininity.

Just enough, appropriately

Painting a wall with bright eye-catching colors is the fastest and most effective way to create a home focal point. The sweet and warm purplish red, if painted on all four walls, would appear too crazy and morbid. In this case, an appropriate use of violet on one wall contrasts with the yellow headboard, highlighting the "main character" - the bed.

Bold mix-and-match

Aqua green, orange yellow, lime, white - usually only the boldest painters dare to make such arrangements. In fact, as long as the combination is skillful enough, some color combinations look better than we imagine. In this case, these colors are organically integrated by an exaggerated mixed-color stripe carpet, and the white furniture and simple curtains also give the eyes a rest.
