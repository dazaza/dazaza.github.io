Uncle Big Belly

There was an uncle, his belly was very big, as if there was a pot hidden in his clothes. Once, Uncle Big Belly was taking a walk on the playground, where a group of children were playing football. The football hit Uncle Big Belly and then rolled into the grass and disappeared. After the children couldn't find the ball, they saw Uncle Big Belly's big belly and asked if he had hidden the ball under his clothes. He said no, but they didn't believe him until Uncle Big Belly lifted his clothes and showed his white belly, then the children laughed and walked away.

Because of his big belly, when Uncle Big Belly went into a room, it often happened that his belly arrived before he did. One summer, he went to someone's office to handle some affairs. When he got to the door, he remembered that he had forgotten something, so he hesitated for a moment standing there. As a result, the people inside only saw his belly come in and didn't see his face come in, so they were curious and said: "How come this kid's face is so round and big, and why is his face covered with a T-shirt."

Because of his big belly, Uncle Big Belly often made jokes. Once he went to catch a bus. Because there were many people and the door was narrow, after everyone squeezed, Uncle Big Belly got stuck there. People around him spent a great deal of effort to pull him onto the bus. There were also a lot of people on the bus, everyone was squeezed straightly in the compartment like sugarcane. While squeezing, suddenly, an old lady called out, saying: "Don't squeeze, don't squeeze, there's a pregnant woman here, be careful of danger." Everyone heard and hurriedly moved away, leaving Uncle Big Belly standing there alone with his belly, and everyone laughed. Just when Uncle Big Belly was embarrassed, a child stood tired and cried, demanding to be held by his mother. Uncle Big Belly saw this and patted his big belly, saying: "Child, don't cry, come here, there's a leather sofa available," then he picked up the child and put him on his belly, everyone laughed again, but this time they laughed more happily and cheerfully.
