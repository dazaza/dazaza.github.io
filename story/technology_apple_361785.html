Wrapped in "Apple skin", iTouch becomes iPhone society _ News _ Liberation cow network

by cacard on 2010-08-07 17:33:26

With a special case, the iTouch can take the job of a sibling's iPhone. Recently, an iTouch peripheral called the Apple Skin 520 has become available for pre-order. It is claimed that in a matter of seconds, the iTouch can be wrapped in the "apple skin", and it can be used to talk and text. Only need to spend about half the money, you can get an alternative "iPhone", for some cash-strapped "fruit fans", this is undoubtedly good news.

The reporter saw on Taobao yesterday that the iTouch peripheral named "Apple Skin 520" has begun to accept reservations. The customer service staff of the shop told reporters that there are already hundreds of customers have submitted booking applications, as long as the arrival of goods will be in accordance with the order of booking and buyer contact delivery. "At present, manufacturers are stepping up production, the fastest will have to wait about half a month."

According to the manufacturer, "Apple Skin 520" is specially tailored for the iTouch function jacket, its communication function is achieved by a back-button communication module, which also has a SIM card slot, battery and related components. Earlier, some communication shops near the Shanghai railway station also sold similar devices, which can be connected to the bottom of the iTouch to make phone calls, which can be said to be the predecessor of the "apple skin", priced at about 100 yuan each, but it has not sold well.
