Maternity evening dresses, wedding dress details, and pregnancy do not have to mean the end of date nights, work celebrations, or flattering, hot, and gorgeous night dresses. For those special nights out, every expectant mother should have her go-to gown. Whether you prefer long to short, an LBD (little black dress) to a fun jewel tone, there is sure to be a great dress available for you. Find a maternity evening gown that flatters your form and is special to your personal style so you are sure to look and feel great all night long.

One lovely style for a maternity evening dress option is the maxi dress. A maxi dress is quite flattering, as it creates a long and lean appearance. While there are many casual maxi dresses, the evening options exemplify such details as richer fabrics, elaborate necklines, and a unique gown quality. Fresh for the season is the Gold Band Maxi Dress from Maternal America. This Grecian-inspired style combines rich gold and elegant black for a true night look. The gold band emphasizes a beautiful v-neckline and empire waist, creating a flattering shape that allows the fabric to drape wonderfully over your baby bump. Paired with gold strappy heels, you’re sure to be a showstopper.

The Maxi Maternity Dress from Olian Maternity is another beautiful choice. This sleek fabric stretches exactly where you need it and creates a slimming appearance. The most beautiful aspect of this gown is its neckline, with criss-crossing straps creating a stunning design. Paired with heels or flats, this maxi gown is sure to impress!

Another maternity evening gown style that becomes more popular in the fall and winter months is a dress accented with a jeweled neckline. Jeweled accents bring a rich, warm feel and create a lovely frame for one’s face. They add special detailing to whatever dress they adorn and elevate your look to the next level. One beautiful option for an evening out with your significant other or even a dinner extravaganza with your office could be the Embellished Dress from Maternal America. Classic and stylish, the empire waist creates a wonderful silhouette, and the stretchy fabric ensures your utmost comfort. The neckline is the focal point of the gown, consisting of beautiful beading in a gentle scoop-neck style. The jewels, layered on top of the deep navy blue fabric, truly exemplify elegance.

Another lovely beaded option is the Beaded Neck Cocktail Dress from Olian Maternity. The wonderful scoop-neck beading layered against the black creates a high-fashion feel, and the cut accentuates both the neck and shoulders beautifully.

A little black dress, more affectionately known as the LBD, is another great option for maternity evening wear. LBDs are classic, pretty, and flattering. Great for cocktail parties, intimate dinners out, and even business events, they are versatile, and you are sure to get a lot of use from them. While all black, each LBD has its own unique qualities that allow you to choose a perfect dress for your character and style.

The Black Diamond Beaded Gown is a beautiful option from Maternal America. With long sleeves that are perfect for the fall months, it features a detailed v-neckline that culminates with a beaded diamond design on the bust. Another trendy LBD is the Satin Border Dress, also from Maternal America. Easily dressed up with a great set of heels, it has a scoop neck and a flattering empire waist. The delicate pleating in the bodice and satin band at the hem provide a beautiful feminine look. Wherever you choose to wear your dress, an LBD is the perfect go-to staple for many occasions.

An evening gown is a crucial staple to have in one's maternity wardrobe, and there are a variety of options available for your unique style. Find the style that works best for your body and taste, and you are sure to look and feel great all night long.
