Datong Coal Industry (601001): As a domestic high-quality power coal producer, the company's currently owned mines develop Jurassic coal seams, which have significant advantages such as low ash content and high calorific value. The company, as a high-quality power coal producer, has formed four world-famous brands, with a higher market share in the North China region, possessing a strong competitive advantage. The company receives strong support from superior departments in all aspects of coal production, transportation, and sales. With the continuous advancement of coal mine closures, production restrictions, resource integration, and the gradual commissioning of new construction projects, the company's industry status will become increasingly prominent.

Policy-driven long-term benefits

The state has approved Taiyuan, Datong, and other cities to enjoy the Northeast Old Industrial Base Revitalization Policy. Recently, it also approved Shanxi Province as a pilot province for ecological construction and circular economy development. The effects of these policies, funds, and platforms will continue to be released, presenting long-term benefits for the company’s development.

Leveraging its own advantages to drive price increases

The company will fully utilize its advantages in resources, brand, location, management, etc., relying on or leveraging the powerful resource platform of Tongmei Group and its special status in China's energy development strategy, continuously expanding and enhancing the company's core competitive advantages. The direct price of Tongmei is 520 yuan/ton, up 4% year-on-year; the downwater coal price is 595 yuan/ton, up 8% year-on-year.

Group asset injection enhances production capacity space

The group's plan is to implement asset injections in phases, ultimately achieving overall listing. Currently, the listed company has a production capacity of 33.4 million tons; the group has 13 operating coal mines with a combined production capacity of 31 million tons. Additionally, there are 47 integrated coal mines with a production capacity of 33 million tons. The company's asset injection is actively advancing, and in the long term, there is considerable room for production capacity enhancement.

In the secondary market, the stock price has recently shown a rebound after hitting the bottom and has re-crossed the annual line. Currently, the overall market trend is upward, with relatively strong support from the moving averages. It is expected that the rebound trend will continue with the cooperation of trading volume.
