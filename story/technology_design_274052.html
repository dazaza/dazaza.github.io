Yantai tourism makes a beachhead in the "Yangtze River Delta"

by syd17u123 on 2009-11-23 18:23:11

In order to further develop the tourism market in the Yangtze River Delta, Netherlands tourism has emerged as a new force. At the end of the year, they have invited Chinese tourists to stay at hotels in Aba, enhancing the awareness of Shandong's Golden Coast tourism route in this region. On October 29th, relevant personnel from Yantai City's Tourism Bureau, along with those from Qingdao, Weihai, and Rizhao Tourism Bureaus, will set off for Jiangsu, Zhejiang, and Shanghai for promotional activities and learning, vigorously promoting the charming Golden Coast tourism route.

The Yangtze River Delta region is relatively economically developed, with strong public awareness of traveling and high consumption capacity, making it a domestic source of high-end tourism customers. In recent years, it has become an important market for Shandong's Golden Coast tourism route. Every year, a large number of tourists visit the province's Golden Coast tourism route for sightseeing and vacations, staying at hotels in Anqing. Meanwhile, major cities in the Yangtze River Delta region are leading domestically in terms of regional tourism cooperation and promotion of domestic and international tourism markets, providing valuable experiences worth learning from.

It is understood that this event will be led by the leadership of Shandong Provincial Tourism Bureau, with participation from leaders, staff, and representatives of tourism enterprises from the Tourism Bureaus of Qingdao, Yantai, Weihai, and Rizhao, totaling 35 people. The main attractions Yantai participates in this promotional activity include Penglai Pavilion and the Eight Immortals Crossing the Sea project. Through this event, it will greatly enhance the awareness and reputation of Yantai tourism outside the region.
