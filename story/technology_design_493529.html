That was a happy time.

by ydjfeifeib on 2011-09-22 18:37:43

First, put the oil in, then put the rice in, stir a little, and add a bit of salt.

He said, that's the smell of a good wife.

I said, we will be together forever.

I took him to a small bustling area I passed by every time I bought groceries. There were many small stalls. I pointed to a ring in a small box and said, I want it.

He said, this is how much I love you.

So, when he came back and put a thousand yuan in my hand, I cried.

I lay on the bed and started dividing the money.

Sometimes, there would only be fried rice with eggs. That's my specialty, he often praised me for it. I learned this from an experienced chef.

Then, serve it in a large bowl.

He also likes to smell the scent of the sun. However, he prefers to hold me and smell my scent.

At noon, he asked me, have you had lunch yet?

In fifteen days, we spent eighty-five yuan.

I accompanied him, taking the bus to his friend's house to return the money.

Thirty yuan was used for our dinner.

I'm afraid he doesn't eat breakfast, so I often buy him breakfast. One night, at 11:30 PM, we were both asleep, and we dreamed of many bakeries. After waking up, I realized I forgot to buy him breakfast. I ran downstairs in my pajamas. He stopped me and said, where are you going? Be careful not to fall.

Then we could wait for him to come back and eat.

I saw him cry. At the door, he kissed me without any hesitation. He kissed me so hard I couldn't breathe.

In the evening, it was very interesting, eggs cost two point four yuan per catty. I know how to pick fresh eggs. The market was dirty, I squeezed among a group of middle-aged women to pick eggs. I would bargain.

After he received his salary, he took me shopping and said, what do you want? Let me buy it for you.

Thus, we said, the mountains would crumble and the earth would join before I dare to part from you.

Ten yuan remained as contingency funds, just in case.

I said, I'm sorry, I forgot to buy you breakfast.

He spent twenty-six yuan on the bus fare. Breakfast, thirty yuan. Lunch, I didn't eat. Dinner, twenty-nine yuan.

This thirty yuan was for him to take the bus in the morning. Sometimes, when the weather was bad, he would take a taxi home.

That night, the sky was beautiful, with many stars. On the grass, I lay on his lap, counting the stars. I said, how beautiful the sky is, how beautiful our love is.

On the first morning, I got up with him, watched him wash his face and brush his teeth, then held hands and sent him to work. I watched him get on the bus. There were many people on the bus, I saw him squeezed among them, his eyes full of happiness. We waved, I watched the bus drive away, then went home, washed clothes. Cleaned the room, used a very clean cloth to wipe the floor, kitchenware, doors.

We rented a house in a very quiet neighborhood, bought necessities, opened our wallet, and counted, only having one hundred yuan left.

Then, mix the rice and eggs all together in the pot. Steam for a few minutes in the pot.

He likes to eat spinach, which is very cheap. A bunch costs one yuan. I can cook many styles of spinach. For example, boiled or steamed. He eats it very well. I look at him and smile.

Dinner is made by me, buying a little bit of mushrooms. Place them in boiling water, then beat the eggs into it, watching it boil. Add a little scallion and chicken essence. It smells very good. A bowl of soup probably cost us eighty cents.

Fifteen days, only need to endure fifteen days, and he will receive his salary.

I am so strong, keeping his love in my heart, refusing to let him borrow money.

During that time, I did not go out, I did not want to spend money. There was no television at home, nothing, but I had a lot of things to do. I needed to air our quilts. I like to smell the scent of the sun when sleeping. Like our love, warm and pleasant.

We ate from a large bowl. Two spoons, heads facing each other. Laughing happily. A greasy mushroom soup, a large bowl of fried rice with eggs. He told me some happy stories.

I said, don't borrow, if you borrow once, you'll borrow again. We must be able to endure any hardship. Only then can we stay together forever.

I said, I am not someone who fears hardship. But, I want us to be able to persist in doing anything, just like when I came to your city, I persisted in giving up what my parents gave me, giving up my job. Leaving my friends far away. Coming to this strange city, just to be with you in love.

Actually, I didn't eat. I wanted us to get through this month without borrowing money.

That night, I bought a "wife cake." What an intimate name.

I said, yes, I ate.

He lay on top of me, calling me wife, burying his face in my chest, silent. I knew he was in pain inside.

He said, it's fine, we can borrow a little from a friend. It will be better after receiving the salary.

Take a small bowl, beat two or three eggs, stir. Add monosodium glutamate, salt, and other seasonings. Stir evenly. Then put oil in the pan. Put the eggs in, stir with chopsticks. Let the eggs scatter. Don't overcook them.

That was a ten-yuan ring.

And thus we embraced each other, I hummed a little tune, and he kept his face buried in my chest, listening to me hum.

A friend of his borrowed his savings due to a lawsuit. So, his money was almost gone.

The other thirty yuan was for buying breakfast.

I recited words for you to listen. Whose do you like? Hmm, Qin Guan's "Magpie Bridge" (Que Qiao Xian). Su Shi's "Divination Son" (Bu Suan Zi). Unconsciously recited "River Town Son" (Jiang Cheng Zi). He looked up and kissed me. He said, we will not separate.

That was a happy time, when the two of us only had one hundred yuan in our pockets.
