The strength of Xi'an lies in the dominance of one party, as no city in the northwestern region can shake Xi'an's absolute NO.1 position. The opening of Meimei Chang'an Department Store means that Xi'an is no longer solely reliant on the prestigious Zhongda International. The dual-power dynamic will continue to play out in Xi'an, and each round of adjustments at Zhongda International will ensure its top position among high-end department stores in central and western China at least until the opening of the second phase of Chengdu's Meimei Licheng. However, what puzzles people about Xi'an is: why has it made no progress in the jewelry sector? While CARTIER has entered many other cities, there is no sign of it in Xi'an, let alone brands like BALLY (Bally official website), BVLGARI, TIFFANY, etc. The rumored CHOPARD boutique later turned out to be just a counter. Of course, it cannot be denied that if these brands enter the northwest, Xi'an would undoubtedly be their first choice. Some records of big brands in Xi'an include BOTTEGA VENETA (Bottega Veneta official website) at Chang'an Meimei, GUCCI, FENDI, CHLOE stores, VERSACE, EA stores at Zhongda International, and LLADRO store at Sofitel, etc.

Shenzhen's situation is similar to Guangzhou's. Although this year GUCCI's 2400 square meter flagship store at MixC caught everyone's attention, it still couldn't change the trend of its ranking gradually declining. Other reasons for Shenzhen are the same as Guangzhou's.

Shenyang has climbed from outside the top 10 previously to today's TOP5 in just over a year, with the frequency of big brand entries and the performance they have created being impressive. On the opening day of Shenyang's LV store, the exact sales data cannot be disclosed, but it exceeded an astonishing 4 million RMB, breaking the sales record set by Chengdu's Seibu store. Seibu, Zhuozhan, the veteran Zhongxing and New World, the soon-to-open Isetan, and long-term projects such as Hang Lung and Jusco all give this city something to look forward to. It could be said that if not for Chengdu having DIOR, TIFFANY, HERMES (all of which have intentions to enter Shenyang), and the sales performance of some key brands in Chengdu, Chengdu might have been overtaken by Shenyang this year.

Some records of big brands in Shenyang include Zhuozhan's GUCCI, LV, BVLGARI, BALenciaga (Balenciaga official website), CHOLE, CELINE, FENDI (Fendi official website), CARTIER, LOEWE stores, Seibu's LV, PRADA, BURBERRY (Burberry official website), VERSACE, TOD'S, FERRAGAMO, DUNHILL, ZEGNA, ETRO, AC stores, etc., details omitted.

From the rankings, Guangzhou's position has been continuously declining. This does not necessarily mean that Guangzhou's luxury market is deteriorating or that its consumption power is weaker than other cities. On one hand, other cities are catching up too fast, and on the other hand, the presence of Hong Kong nearby also affects Guangzhou. With Guangzhou people's consumption power ranking third nationwide, can you say that its luxury development is poor? Li Bai Plaza remains the most luxurious mall domestically, and Taikoo Hui is highly anticipated. If we were to talk about Guangzhou's misfortune, it can only be attributed to so-called radiation and being radiated.

Some records of big brands in Guangzhou include Li Bai's BVLGARI store, CHLOE store, MISSONI Garden Hotel.
