Seventy-nine classic quotes from "Mengya" over the past ten years (too abstruse)

by qingchuyulan on 2010-04-07 22:08:54

1. One is a splendid but fleeting dream, the other is a harsh and prolonged reality.

2. How can we retrieve the bygone years? Your smile in my memory still lingers.

3. The former me didn't know how to cry, now I often shed tears.

4. In the pocket watch, there's an old photo of a woman who said she would meet me in heaven. I placed her smile in the nearest place to time. I just want her smile to be present in my life, always.

5. I am a child who shines with the stars and feels lonely with the night.

6. Those who yearn for the sky are all lonely.

7. I like fairy tales because I regard them as childhood.

8. When a kite gets tired of the sky, will it fall into the sea without hesitation?

9. When I most want to say something, it's often when I am most silent.

10. Under the sunset, it's a world like a fairy tale.

11. When you truly wait for someone, that person will never appear.

12. If heaven is too crowded, then let's go rampant in hell together.

13. When the weather clears up, maybe I'll love you again properly.

14. Talking to loneliness is just because I don't want to believe you have already left me.

15. I decided not to cry anymore, just as you decided to leave me firmly.

16. Just one day away from you, you look disheveled like a child who only needs my comfort.

17. Time flows through my fingers, I feel my constellation slowly falling from its orbit.

18. Without happiness, smiles remain. Without fairy tales, childhood remains.

19. When you want to hold onto something more, it's easier to lose it.

20. At that moment, it seemed as if the whole world collapsed before my eyes. The bricks in the ruins were all engraved with vivid memories, now quietly sticking to the ground. Even if I try to walk silently, I eventually find myself to be a person exiled by memories.

21. Crying is to remember a person, striving to think of a person, is to remind myself, at least there is one person worth crying for.

22. Who can resist the ebb and flow of life?

23. The summer carved in memory is the tears we shed together under the sun.

24. Who can take me back to the days full of fairy tales?

25. When you really start doing something, it's often when many things can't be turned back.

26. When you truly want to forget someone, that person has already been etched in your heart.

27. Those who haven't experienced it, haven't understood its meaning to him.

28. People who call themselves realists are often because their ideals have deepened into a form resembling reality.

29. Sometimes saying "dislike" is to make saying "like" more precious.

30. Is it because I care about her too much? Or perhaps, it's just that she doesn't care about me.

31. We exist in each other's memories.

32. What can shallow and deep represent? It's just forgetting earlier or later.

33. Some people exist because of fate, so he shouldn't complain about anything.

34. Even if drunk in dreams, it's also a kind of memory, because you can never forget.

35. When I can no longer leave you, you have already left me.

36. We forget each other in each other.

37. Because we've never met, so we've never been sad, nor have we ever been happy.

38. You use your cold fingertips to lightly write dependence on my palm.

39. Even crying is a kind of remembrance. Even if crying, you can't return to childhood.

40. Cold vows sleep in fairy tales.

41. Breaking isn't the cruelest thing. The cruelest is stepping on these fragments pretending not to hurt, stubbornly searching...

42. A person's existence is often revealed long after they've disappeared.

43. Sometimes, giving up some things can get you something else, but after getting it, you might want what you gave up.

44. She taught me to smile, he taught me to cherish, who taught me to forget?

45. Loneliness is just a pretentious show.

46. Without you bothering me, I start to bother myself.

47. Many things are slowly taken apart and pieced together in our hearts, then everything changes.

48. I forgot you in the place where we first met.

49. Some words, you will naturally say them when you want to, and if you don't want to, those who hear them will only hear lies.

50. Your sighs can be beautiful like smiles. How can I paint your sadness?

51. Actually, being drunk in dreams is just a joke with forgetting. When you think you've forgotten someone, you'll realize it's more painful than remembering her.

52. What we missed are the things we wanted. What we didn't miss, we can accept calmly.

53. Should I count your worries with the stars, or should I count the stars in the sky with you?

54. When I truly want to protect someone, I discover that I've been silently protected by that person all along.

55. I never understand your expressions, even when you're smiling, I still feel melancholy.

56. Whose smile disappears from whose sight, and then they understand happiness?

57. Xiao Xi said she saw bright sunshine shining on the mountain. I just smiled at her, without saying I saw the outline of sorrow pressing on the green mountains.

58. When I cry, you cry too. I think you're less lonely than me.

59. When I smile, you're still crying. Turns out you're lonelier than me.

60. Happiness and sorrow are stories told to ourselves.

61. My mood has gotten used to migrating with the birds, it's just a bit hectic.

62. When everyone says they're lonely, I think they're happy.

63. Saying they've all left, is to bring them back one by one when I'm most alone.

64. Saying I lost courage, is to wait for someone to give me strength.

65. Getting lost once is to better recognize the road ahead.

66. A girl who's been hurt, the only thing she learns is to be strong.

67. Because we need to survive, I start learning to give up.

68. The loneliest person should be the one forgotten in memories.

69. Not only tears represent sorrow, not only you represent love.

70. If you think you've forgotten someone, then you won't be stupid enough to mention forgetting to bring her up.

71. People who say they've forgotten, often still remember.

72. Joy stops on paper, paleness stops on face.

73. Smiling, not because of being happy for too long, but forgetting to be sad over a long period.

74. A lonely person, you can never feel how lonely he is. He simply appears in your life, then disappears simply.

75. Protecting someone isn't because liking someone.

76. I expectantly and curiously learn to look at the sky with a pure 45-degree angle as taught by Xiao Si. When I really do it, I don't shed tears.

77. I use smiles to make the ending sadder.

78. How many secrets do painters hide in oil paintings that allow countless sorrowful and quiet gazes over time.

79. I run like a child, a child who looks at the sky and cries.
