Representatives of "happy women" in the entertainment circle -- Tao Jingying and Xu Xidi collaborated for the first time to shoot a cover for the public welfare activity "Love Life" which cares for autistic children, conveying their love and care, and sharing their happy moments.

Tao Zi: Happiness is the sacrifice of laughing with tears. There's no doubt about Tao Jingying's intelligence and sharpness. She dares to express her views, and is not afraid to speak out against injustice. She can have a spicy dialogue with listeners through the radio, shouting "Girls are like this", fully representing modern new women. However, in the high-difficulty subject of managing marriage and family, she is not the supreme queen of hosting, nor the strong queen of variety shows. She has a family that everyone envies because she knows how to pay for love. She said: "Happiness is not as simple as what is shown in idol dramas. It's not just about being in love; happiness requires a price, it's the sacrifice of laughing with tears." However, in Tao Zi's eyes, her husband Li Ren has sacrificed more for the family. "I am really more selfish, I haven't given up completing myself." She holds the baton of hosting "Super Star Avenue", "University Students Have Not Yet", and the Feidie Radio Station. She also published a new book "I Love Therefore I Am" and opened a sisterhood website. During the busy season of year-end parties, she couldn't finish all her hosting jobs. In addition to rushing to record for the New Year, her eyes were extremely painful. She barely finished the Foxconn year-end party on New Year's Eve before seeking medical attention, discovering three holes in her conjunctiva. At that time, she was so busy that she had no time to take care of her health, let alone spend time with her family. Li Ren took on all the housework without complaint, only telling her: "I forgive your absence." Tao Zi was deeply moved, "It's too important for couples to understand each other. He made me feel guilty instead." Li Ren gave up acting opportunities to spend more time with his children, focusing on being a stay-at-home dad. Tao Zi happily revealed the inside story of her husband and son, "He and our one-year-old son are like lovers. They will look at each other and kiss when they say 'I love you'."

When Tao Zi first met her considerate husband Li Ren, she didn't think he was her true love, "At the time we were both heartbroken and stuck in sad emotions until our first solo date. On the way back, we got stuck in traffic on the highway. We talked for three or four hours straight, realizing how comfortable and happy it was to talk to him, completely forgetting the frustration of being stuck in traffic," making her realize that the right person had appeared. She continued, "When you meet the right person, you'll find that family background and character aren't that important. Chinese people are really hypocritical. We've been educated from a young age about propriety, righteousness, integrity, and shame, loyalty, filial piety, and chastity, not to judge people by their appearance, but when choosing a spouse, we tend to look at whether they are rich or have a car, judging people solely by their appearance." Her unique perspective erupted instantly.

Because the right person appeared, Tao Zi made the craziest thing for love, which was getting pregnant. Before giving birth to her first child, she entered the delivery room without learning the Lamaze breathing method. After two hours of struggle, her beloved daughter Dou Dou was born. At that moment, she instinctively felt she could have another child. The second childbirth seemed smooth within half an hour, but the entire process made her shout, "I won't have any more!" Because the pain didn't stop within half an hour, "It's like someone keeps hitting you hard for half an hour non-stop. All I could do was scream, there was nothing else I could do." Now, her married life is blissful, but Tao Zi also admitted that she fears every day that one day this love will disappear. Every now and then, she sings "I Only Care About You" to her husband, "So I beg you, don't let me leave you." Is she worried that her husband might have a mental affair? She laughed, "Based on my years of observation, men are more likely to have physical affairs. After all, spending energy dealing with one woman is enough. Men prefer to put their remaining energy into friends and hobbies." However, if one day Tao Zi herself had a mental affair, she said, "I would be honest. I demand fidelity in relationships. If I fall in love with someone else, I would honestly tell them. I hope the other party does the same."

Xiaoxi S: Love is willingly accepted bondage. Xiaoxi S is the same as Tao Zi, balancing career and family. After marriage, she lives in a mansion, her husband loves her, her in-laws take care of her, she smoothly gives birth to a daughter, and household chores are taken care of, making her a representative of a dream-like happy life. She never hides how happy she is. But behind her funny image on stage and her glamorous exterior, she understands the value of blessings very well, "There is happiness when you have someone you truly love, but because you love someone so much, life is no longer truly free. It will be bound, but it is a bondage that I am willing to accept." For her now, the greatest happiness is going home after work, shouting "Hello", and seeing her precious daughter rush towards her. Looking at her husband's face makes her feel glad that this person will accompany her until death. Her life is filled with deep love, enjoying ordinary happiness. But this husband who never gets old, she confessed, was far from her ideal lover's standard when they first met. She envisioned her true love as tall, with bulging veins in his arms when exerting force, dressed casually fashionable, a super manly type of man. She joked, "My husband is too flashy, he's the kind of person who wears silk clothes." Although their first impression didn't match, her husband's devotion touched her, "Being with him, no matter what I'm doing, I always feel his gaze following me. He always looks at me." Now, she highly praises her husband as a flawless good man, "Having such a person, I must seize the opportunity." She also mentioned that many people neglect to cherish the people around them, unaware of their blessings until they lose them. This is her profound understanding after watching the animated movie "Up." Has she ever been disappointed in love? She laughed, "Looking back at my past relationships, I am a person who cherishes them." Her nonsensical style captivates hearts, so even though she boldly touches popular handsome men like Zhao You Ting on "Kangsi Coming," the effect and laughter far exceed the speculation of "infidelity." She doesn't think it's using the program as an excuse for "mental infidelity." She said that after marriage, she has completely eliminated the possibility of infidelity because she loves her husband so much and values her family, "Infidelity causes too much harm to the family, it's too unwise, and simply not worth it." Agreeing with Tao Zi, the craziest thing Xiaoxi S did for love was also getting pregnant. However, since both pregnancies were cesarean sections, unlike Tao Zi who endured labor pains, she considered it crazy because nurturing a life is risky, akin to "sailing towards an unknown universe," unsure of the child's future, worrying about the child's health, bearing great responsibility. Nevertheless, this fear of the unknown does not deter her determination to try for a third child www.gzhl78.com. And Xiaoxi S's harmonious relationship with her father-in-law and mother-in-law is enviable. She said, "My in-laws are really business information, my father-in-law focuses on stocks every day, and my mother-in-law asks me if she should cook porridge for me when I come home from work. I am really lucky." Understanding the elderly, she has her own set of ways to get along with her in-laws, "I won't conflict with elders over 50 years old. They have lived according to their own worldview for so many years, there's no need to argue with them about life principles. Mostly, I just listen, and appropriately compliment my mother-in-law for being young and beautiful, and my father-in-law for being smart and handsome, making it easy and pleasant!" Some people say Xiaoxi S's happiness comes from better luck than others, but many things cannot rely solely on luck to reach the shore of success. Xiaoxi S creates her own happiness with love and wisdom in the unpredictable marriage, focusing all her attention on her family. She said, "If my husband's birthday coincides with hosting the Golden Melody Awards, I will definitely choose my husband." In her heart, the brilliance in front of the camera is far less valuable than the happiness behind the scenes.
