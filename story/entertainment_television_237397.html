Plot: On June 30, the tribute film for the 60th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China, "Founding of a Republic," released its latest poster and trailer, and the release date was set for September 17. Director Huang Jianxin frankly admitted that the shooting pressure was enormous. He stated that there were 83 actors who played male and female lead roles in this film, with a total of more than 170 actors participating. Additionally, within the 120-day shooting period, the film was shot across numerous locations in over 90 different scenes, changing scenes almost every day. Since the film had to be completed before National Day, the crew worked overtime on post-production.
