Introduction to the plot of "Fei Chang Dao":

Detective Lei Ming returns to his hometown, Donghai City, due to a job transfer. One of his two best childhood friends, Lv Tianzhuo, is now a successful real estate developer in the city.

Lv Tianzhuo holds a welcome party for Lei Ming, coinciding with the occurrence of a shooting case involving real estate developer Wang Qinghe. Lei Ming is mistakenly regarded as a suspect, and the team leader happens to be his other childhood friend - Shen Ting, who is now a backbone member of the city's criminal investigation team.

The reunion of Lei Ming and Shen Ting reignites the waves of emotion between them. It turns out that they were deeply in love years ago. Due to conflicts between their parents' generation, they were forced to break up, and Lei Ming left Donghai City. By this time, Shen Ting has become Lv Tianzhuo's girlfriend.

Lei Ming joins the investigation of the shooting case and learns that it shares similar methods with a murder case three years ago, leading him to suspect that the killer might be female.

A territorial dispute and fighting event occurs at the cargo port, prompting Shen Ting to suspect that Liang Long, the main offender, and his boss Chen Xiong are involved in organized crime. Meanwhile, a project auction by the municipal government is taking place, where Chen Xiong and Lv Tianzhuo are pitted against each other. Pharmaceutical research expert Su Nuocheng and his assistant Guo Qiang are murdered. Lei Ming and Shen Ting start an investigation, discovering that the drug the two were studying is related to narcotics, and find similarities between this case and the previous shooting case. Chen Xiong's subordinate Ah Kun is killed, leading Shen Ting to conclude that Chen Xiong is a major suspect. During the investigation, Shen Ting is forced by Chen Xiong's subordinate Dali to jump into the sea. She is luckily saved by Chen Xiong's sister, Chen Xiaoyu, who drives a boat. However, they are both washed up on a deserted island due to ocean currents.

Upon hearing the news, Lei Ming goes to another island to search for Chen Xiong's drug manufacturing factory. Chen Xiong escapes by boat into international waters. The investigation reveals that the equipment and products in Chen Xiong's factory match those used by Su Nuocheng and Guo Qiang in their drug production research, so Liang Long is immediately taken back to the bureau for questioning. However, scientific analysis shows no relation to drugs, and Liang Long is released. Shen Ting's disappearance makes Lv Tianzhuo suspect Chen Xiong's involvement. Consequently, Lv Tianzhuo reveals his ties to the underworld and orders the kidnapping of Liang Long. However, they learn nothing from Liang Long. With the help of professional maritime knowledge provided by a maritime expert, Lei Ming and Lv Tianzhuo finally locate Shen Ting and Chen Xiaoyu. Overjoyed, Shen Ting and Lei Ming embrace, hinting at the rekindling of their romance. Lv Tianzhuo, feeling disheartened and melancholic, is left heartbroken.
