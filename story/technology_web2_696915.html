The Early Education Chapter of the Scholarly Home: The "One Breath Recitation Method" and the "One Breath Rolling Broadcast Classic Guidance Method," abbreviated as the "One Breath Method." This method is especially suitable for young children, aligns with the natural disposition of children, is easy to implement, and allows children to easily and happily enter a learning state, yielding excellent results.

Applicable Audience: This method is particularly suitable for children aged 2-13 years old; for children over 14 years old who are entering school, the Feng Family Early Education recommends Wang Caigui's teaching method known as the "Three Hundred Classics Reading Method" (100 words per day, early education institutions, prenatal education stories, read 100 times, score 100 points).

Learning Content and Order: (Suggested Chinese-English study time ratio: Chinese 3 : English 1)

Chinese: "Xue Yong Lun Yu", "Laozi Zhuangzi Selection", "Mencius", "Yijing", "Shijing", "Shujing", "Traditional Chinese Medicine Health启蒙", "Filial Piety Three Hundred Thousand", "Tang Poetry Three Hundred"

English: "Shakespeare Sonnets", "A Midsummer Night's Dream", "Selected English Literature", "English 1000 Sentences", "Children's English Classic First Reading", "Edison English"

Educational Progress: (Adjust according to the child's age and actual educational situation)

- For children aged 2-3 years, an average of about 50 words per day is appropriate. (Approximately 10,000 words per year)

- For children aged 3-4 years, an average of about 100 words per day is appropriate. (Approximately 20,000 words per year)

- For children aged 4-5 years, an average of about 150 words per day is appropriate. (Approximately 30,000 words per year)

- For children aged 5-13 years, an average of about 200-300 words per day is appropriate. (Approximately 40,000 words per year)

★Within four years, it is possible to memorize approximately 100,000 Chinese classic characters and 30,000 English classic characters.

Recitation Method: Treat the article (such as Analects 1-5 or chapters) as a whole, from beginning to end in one breath. It is recommended to use the right index finger to guide reading, point-read (Chinese), and underline-read (English).

Implementation Steps:

(Primarily listening, environmental influence, supplemented by recitation, parent guidance)

- "1" means reading once a day, each session about 15 minutes;

- "3" means reading three different selections daily, morning, noon, and night, such as "Analects", "Great Learning", "Doctrine of the Mean";

- "7" follows the principle of reviewing every seven days after 7 days.

★Parent-child co-reading fosters warm family affection and shares the joy of growth through reading. Play CDs repeatedly at home for auditory influence.

★Friendly Reminder: Using "Love and Joy All-Round Whole Brain Educational Music" as background music can activate the right brain and improve learning efficiency.

★After reciting, if combined with "Character Palace" mythological character recognition art series, Dr. Doman's character recognition card series, the speed of character recognition will be faster.

The One Breath Method involves letting the child learn holistically, imitatively, repetitively, and unconsciously.
