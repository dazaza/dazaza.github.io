Fun garlic crusher

by 26z on 2008-06-19 23:08:10

Chopping garlic is not an easy job for some people because of the smell of garlic itself. But with such a product, you no longer need to worry about its smell, and such work has also become an easier accepted fun thing.

This product uses external force to drive the internal blade to rotate, thereby chopping garlic. Its shape is designed like a small car. Put the garlic in the "cockpit", then close the "cockpit", push the little car around for a few circles by hand, which completes the task of chopping garlic. In that case, I wonder how many young dads and young sons will be fighting in the kitchen to complete the task of chopping garlic. Available on Amazon, priced at $9.99.
