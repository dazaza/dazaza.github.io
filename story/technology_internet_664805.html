Yao Kee Poker Corporation Announcement Regarding the Completion

by eawdqrxbh on 2012-02-14 01:16:01

Yao Kee Poker Corporation hereby announces the completion of the change in business registration. The Company and all members of the Board ensure that the disclosed information is true, accurate, and complete, with no false or misleading statements or material omissions.

Yao Kee Poker Corporation was granted a license [2011] 1090 by the China Securities Regulatory Commission (CSRC), approving the public offering of 2,350 million ordinary shares for the first time, at an issue price of 21.00 yuan per share.

[2011] No. 234, as agreed by the Shenzhen Stock Exchange, allows the RMB ordinary shares issued by the Company to be listed on the SME board of the Shenzhen Stock Exchange on August 5, 2011.

Tianjian CPA Limited conducted an audit of the funds raised from the initial public offering of shares on July 29, 2011, and issued Tianjian Experience [2011] 313. The Board of Directors, authorized by the Company's second extraordinary general meeting of shareholders in 2011, has completed the business registration and filing procedures for the change in registered capital.

On August 17, 2011, the Shanghai Administration for Industry and Commerce renewed the registration number 310,114,000,010,600 for the Company. The registered capital was changed from RMB 70,000,000.00 to RMB 93,500,000.00; the paid-up capital was changed from RMB 70,000,000.00 to RMB 93,500,000.00. The business scope and other registration matters remain unchanged.

This announcement is hereby made.

Yao Kee Poker Corporation Board of Directors

August 18, 2011
