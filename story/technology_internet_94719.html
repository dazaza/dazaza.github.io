Google Search Tools Collect - Google's Search Empire

by zqjilove on 2008-09-24 22:43:57

As the world's largest search engine, Google also has a wide range of businesses. Recently, the one that has caught the most attention is undoubtedly Google Chrome. Regarding this browser, Google developed it to establish a safer, faster, and more stable platform for all Internet users and to create a stronger platform for the development of next-generation web applications.

Currently, aside from Chrome, Google’s other businesses aim towards the same goal: building a platform rather than just being a search engine. If we were to talk about search engines alone, some emerging ones might be hotter than Google right now. (For example, 20 unusual search engines worth remembering.) These new engines are not mainstream because they cannot build a large-scale platform like Google already has, and continues to expand into various industries.

Today, I'd like to take a look back at Google's search tools with everyone. There isn't anything particularly groundbreaking here; I'm just providing a list to help you understand Google better and learn about some of their extended search-related businesses. Their empire is gradually taking shape, and I believe Google's scope will continue to grow. If you know these search tools, then you should also be aware of some Google search techniques.
