Analysis of the Value of SEO Optimization Forum Signatures

by seo175110 on 2012-02-19 19:11:27

The methods of website external link optimization are countless, http://amuletworld.net/viewthread.php?tid=10918&extra=. However, the simplest optimization method is undoubtedly to increase external links through "forum signatures". One post, one external link is its basic feature. If a large number of links are stably indexed, the weight brought by them is even more considerable. But in recent years, many webmasters have found that forum signature optimization is far less effective than before. So, how much value does forum external link optimization still have? Binary analysis believes: the value of forum signature optimization has not decreased, it's just that search engines have stricter requirements. So how to fully utilize these values and make search engines further recognize them?

1. Forum Weight Determines

Many webmasters are accustomed to registering forums, then adding connections in their signatures, and forming a habit of replying to posts and posting with signatures. But not all links can be indexed by search engines, nor can all indexed links bring weight to the website. Why is this so? If the forum's weight is not enough, the more links you post, the greater the possibility of encountering demotion. As we all know, search engines always have a rule to eliminate external link phenomena. And the fundamental reason is that the website publishing links doesn't have enough weight, especially such external links as forum signatures are more likely to be eliminated. Therefore, webmasters should not blindly publish links. More often than not, they cannot ignore the inherent weight of the forum itself, otherwise, it will be counterproductive, and the more you post, the more severe the demotion may be. In the webmaster circle, Binary recommends A5 Forum, Seowhy, Luowuzhe, and Chinaz. These forums still have good weight.

2. Post Quality Determines

Although signature is the simplest way of external link optimization, you cannot blindly reply to posts for external links. The common phenomenon of increasing Baidu weight shows that blindly optimizing often results in twice the effort for half the result. To fully leverage the maximum value of forum signatures, first, you need to ensure the quality of the posts. How to judge the quality of posts? First: Content freshness. An article that has been reprinted hundreds of times, even if posted on a high-weight forum, may not necessarily be indexed by search engines, let alone bring much weight to the "signature". Second: Content quality level. What quality can be said about a post with just dozens of words hastily written? Such posts are not worth considering. Even if they are indexed, they are often removed. If there is no problem with both points, then the post quality is okay.

3. Post Floor Determines

There are quite a few people who see posts and immediately start posting links, but Binary similarly believes this is really not worth it because some posts are simply not worth replying to. For example: a post that has been replied to dozens or even hundreds of times, what's the point of replying again? Expecting the signature to bring weight to the website? Generally speaking, in forum posts, the closer the signature link is to the front floor, the better the effect. If you flip to the second page, it basically has no effect anymore. Therefore, the floor of the post determines the quality of the link, and you should focus on replying to posts with fewer replies.

4. Whether Content is Indexed or Not

If the post has already been indexed by search engines, the weight obtained from replying to the post will definitely not be as good as an unindexed post, and may even get no weight at all. But we can't possibly check each content item to see if it's been indexed, right? The simplest method is to look at the posting time and find fresh posts to reply to, which undoubtedly makes it easier for the signature link to gain value.

5. Section Quality

Even within a forum, the weight given by search engines to each section will absolutely differ. For example, the "AD Watering" area and the "Soft Article Release" area in a webmaster forum will definitely receive different weights for their signature links. First of all, the quality of posts in the AD area may generally be low, after all, they are all advertisements, external links will inevitably be everywhere, and content quality is generally low. On the other hand, the soft article release area will be different. All posts have high content quality, and the weight given by search engines to the section will also differ. At this time, even if you reply to a post in the forum section, the role of the signature will differ. Therefore, when using forum signature external links, to maximize their effectiveness, you must consider the quality of the forum section.

6. Forum Signature Techniques

Besides the above five points, the last point we emphasize is about the design techniques of forum signatures. First: Text hyperlinks vs. text links. I believe that if possible, try to use described text hyperlinks instead of plain text links, which not only provides a backlink for the site but also enhances the function of specific keywords. Second: If the forum supports it, try to give your links "special effects" treatment, such as bolding, changing colors, etc. Third: Number of links. One link in the signature is sufficient; continuously changing keywords for the same link serves no purpose. If you want to link to different pages of a website, you can do that, and if search engines visit, they will equally crawl and assign weight to all of them, http://www.cnnorip.org/bbs/viewthread.php?tid=88312&extra=.
