In recent years, with the change in people's aesthetic concepts, more and more people have started paying attention to facial feature plastic surgery, among which rhinoplasty is one of the most common procedures. The nose is located in the center of the face, and its beauty directly affects a person's appearance. From foreign celebrities to domestic stars, there are countless examples of celebrity rhinoplasty. So, what are the criteria for judging whether a nose is perfect?

Since ancient times, people have been picky about the shape of the nose. A well-proportioned face has its focus on the nose. In ancient China, women were considered beautiful if they had a delicate, upright, and well-defined nose with moderate nostrils. The noses of Asians tend to be flatter and smaller. Chinese faces are generally more delicate, with low and flat nasal bones, and a small and narrow nose bridge is considered beautiful. The curve from the nasal bone to the tip of the nose should slightly concave in women, and a slightly upturned nose end appears softer and more attractive. Specific aesthetic parameters have already been statistically analyzed by scholars, with the following values: the length of the nose is 1/3 of the forehead-to-chin length; the normal length of a human nose is usually between 6 to 7.5 centimeters. A saddle nose is shorter, often less than 5.8 centimeters. A nose longer than 1/3 of the forehead-to-chin length is called a long nose, while one shorter than 1/3 is called a short nose.

Nasal plastic surgery experts introduced that during actual rhinoplasty, hyaluronic acid is not used strictly according to a single standard for every patient because everyone's face shape is different, and the proportions of the nose must be designed according to the face shape. For example, a round face should not have too high a nose; a longer face should have a higher and longer nose for a more balanced and harmonious look; a square face should have a wider and thicker nose to match. Since Chinese faces are generally more delicate with low and flat foreheads, in this case, a small and narrow nose bridge is considered beautiful. For men, the nose bridge should ideally resemble a straight line, whereas for women, a slight concavity with an upturned tip and soft curves is considered aesthetically pleasing.

Therefore, those who wish to undergo rhinoplasty must find professional cosmetic surgeons. Only through scientific design and precise surgery can one achieve their expected or even better-than-expected results.

Rhinoplasty isn't about making the nose as high as possible. Beauty seekers should make rational choices — what suits them is the best-looking option. Do not blindly follow trends in cosmetic surgery.
