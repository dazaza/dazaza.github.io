Some basic rules for promoting a personal website!

by fkjb22j on 2009-11-26 10:15:37

Basically, it covers the main promotion methods on current websites. As for the actual effects of the aforementioned free and paid website promotion methods, and whether more promotion methods can be devised, it depends on each webmaster to summarize based on their website's characteristics and accumulated experience. There are a thousand ways to promote a website. If all these methods were combined, would it become a promotional encyclopedia? Based on the experiences summarized by various webmasters, there are N kinds of promotional methods for personal websites, which may assist individual webmasters. Promotion methods can generally be divided into two categories: free promotion methods and paid promotion methods. Free promotion methods are always the most concerning way for webmasters. It is important to see how webmasters apply them in practice. Basically, free promotion methods include the following aspects: search engine registration, SEO, friendly links, forum marketing, blog marketing, instant messaging tool marketing, etc.

Firstly, I must understand that free does not necessarily mean ineffective. For the promotion of a website, the traffic brought by search engines will increasingly grow. Search engines are indispensable; Baidu, Google, Yahoo, etc., should all be registered without omission so that your site may have an endless flow of traffic. After all, search engines are still the largest source of traffic on the internet.

If you want to consider SEO issues, which is the ranking of your website, can SEO be done well without spending money? Of course, it can! The key lies in whether individual webmasters have technical foundations. After registering with search engines and being indexed by major engines, if you don't even know how to change the source code, it's best to hire someone to do it for you. Besides technology, research is also required, researching the preferences of various search engines. For instance, if you're pursuing a girl, the most important thing is to understand what kind of food she likes, what kind of movies she enjoys watching, and which places she dislikes going to... etc. Applying this principle to SEO means optimizing your website according to relevant rules and making detailed strategies such as title design, tag design, content layout design, etc., which will help improve your website's ranking in search engines.

When discussing friendly links, it's best to connect with websites that have higher traffic and reputation than your own. Next, connect with websites whose content complements yours. Then, connect with similar websites. Friendly links can bring stable traffic to a website. When connecting with similar websites, ensure that your website's content quality has unique features and can attract people; otherwise, it's better not to connect with similar websites. However, few webmasters can persist with forum marketing because it is relatively cumbersome. There is no one-time solution; only continuous efforts can lead to success. Although forum marketing is labor-intensive, its results are very good. Of course, there are certain methods for operating forum marketing: choose forums where your potential customers frequent or forums with high popularity; don't directly post advertisements, as such posts are easily deleted as spam; use avatars and signatures effectively, including introducing and linking your website in the signature; focus on the quality of posts rather than the quantity; appropriately support your posts by finding supporters or registering multiple accounts yourself.

The principles of forum marketing also apply to blog marketing and instant messaging tool marketing. Don't start with advertisements; even if you need to post ads, do it skillfully. After all, promotion is a technical task, not just physical work. How can visitors who see your website help promote it for you? First and foremost, you must do well yourself. The best way to promote a website is through word-of-mouth. Eight words: Content is king, interaction is divine. Content is king refers to ensuring that your website's content has unique aspects, either original or comprehensive, etc. If your content lacks a certain advantage, it loses appeal to users. Additionally, interaction is divine, meaning a modern website must be open and interactive. Your website doesn't have to be an SNS but should definitely encourage user interaction. For example, community gaming is a good interactive entertainment project. This was first proposed by Dongwang Pioneer after launching the Bubble Play webpage game platform game.popwan.com, combining website communities. Due to its significant role in enhancing interactivity, entertainment, profitability, etc., it has now been widely accepted in the industry.

Paid promotion methods mainly focus on return on investment. For webmasters, insufficient funds are a common feature. Therefore, the so-called paid promotion method here does not refer to large-scale spending promotion methods but rather cost-effective methods that achieve great results with small expenses. Such methods include email marketing, viral marketing, online advertising, event promotion, etc. When used appropriately, they can create large-scale publicity within a short time. Email marketing requires special attention to avoid becoming spam. Email marketing is a double-edged sword. If effective, it can yield very good results, but once regarded as spam, it risks being blocked by netizens, which is detrimental to reputation. Therefore, when designing emails, extreme caution is necessary. Below are some suggestions you might consider. Title suggestion: Attractive, simple, and clear without deception. Content suggestion: HTML format is better, and the layout must be clear. In terms of costs, such mass-sending software usually isn't expensive; a few hundred yuan can buy it.

Viral marketing mainly involves a small investment from yourself, letting netizens help promote your content, creating a virus-like spread effect. Below are some commonly used methods. Free services: If possible, provide netizens with free message boards, free domains, free mailing lists, free news, free counters, etc., and include your advertisements or links in these services. Since they are free, they can be promoted quickly. Interesting pages: Beautifully designed or entertaining pages are often rapidly promoted by netizens online. Thus, you can create some beautiful or entertaining pages to recommend to friends.

Although network advertising requires spending money, it is highly effective. How to spend the least amount of money to achieve the best results requires many skills. For example, websites with less fame but high traffic generally have low advertisement prices, yet they can bring hundreds of daily visitors, making it a cost-effective deal. Sometimes, placing advertisements isn't solely about the cost but more about the benefits they bring. For instance, if it's a business website, the quality of visitors is as important as the quantity. Choosing media for such advertisements is very particular. First, understand your potential customer demographics and habits, then select websites they frequently visit for advertisement placement. Although the price might be higher, the quality of customers brought to you will be higher, thus increasing your returns.

Some friends might say that directory website registration is a free promotion method. While being included in a directory site indeed requires no cost, to gain a certain level of exposure and traffic, using a paid method is a reasonable investment with a good return on investment. A website was once included in a non-prominent section of UrlsHome and brought around 200 daily visitors to my friend's website. Moreover, general directory sites' fees aren't too high.

Event promotion is also an excellent way to promote. To achieve good results, meticulous planning is essential. If planned successfully, it can form a positive reputation in users' minds.
