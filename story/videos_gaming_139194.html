"Random Play" Newbie Mistakes and Suggestions - Warcraft Secrets

by qhmywllf on 2009-04-11 18:35:38

1: Qi Jie Ming You is synthesized by two Wu Xing rings + a divine stone at the combination altar (many people think it drops from Bai Hu!!)

2: The pharmacy only drops when you defeat the canteen! It's located to the right of the monster spawn point! (Some people think it's a bug!!)

3: When killing Tian Xian and get entangled, you can use Purification to remove it! (A lot of people just wait to die blindly)

4: The attributes added by mini-games have little effect on yourself, so you can take a rest and prepare for the next wave of monsters' attack!

5: Each person can only do the Wu Xing mission once! Therefore, during double-clearance, only one Qi Jie will appear!

6: Only Shang Shen can drop divine artifacts, Tian Xian cannot!

7: When WM is farming water, they can unleash an ultimate skill which can kill the Stone Golem that drops the Combination Stone, saving time!

8: Many people don't understand the use of dwarves. In fact, dwarves' function is counter-damage! Not just physical attacks!

9: During She Shen's fight against the Phoenix wave, there's no need to control the egg hitting manually; it can hit automatically! Also, She Shen in the later stages clears monsters faster with higher clarity!

10: When doing single-clearance, never save and continue playing! Otherwise, the recording will be lost!

11: In the wave before Skeleton Mage, you can leave some monsters alive to farm money, meaning not killing them until Skeleton Mage arrives! For more details, see the Double-Clearance BT video by the Bandit...

12: There are no useless heroes! Only players who don't know how to use them! Don't casually call any hero weak! Beast King can also clear solo!

13: When the monster freeze cooldown ends, don't immediately stop spawning! Coordinate well with your monster clearing speed. If this wave of monsters isn't cleared, then freeze and keep clearing!

14: In Wave 27 with the Phoenix monsters, if you don't have She Shen or similar heroes, press A to focus fire on the eggs! Otherwise, if you let your heroes stand there attacking, you'll find they'll never finish!

15: When encountering new players, don't start cursing at them! You were once a newbie too! Play with civility!

16: From the back of the school mountain, go left-down to find the mysterious person. The role of Xuanyuan Sword Soul is to kill the final BOSS! Method: Right Left Left Middle

17: When encountering poisonous or self-explosive monsters, directly bring life potions, as this will greatly increase endurance! Especially during Wave 21 with counter-damage! If your equipment isn't very good, make sure to bring them!

18: The dragon below the back mountain drops Immortal Stones and Divine Stones. Some newbies have hit it twice and got Immortal Stones both times, so they think it only drops Immortal Stones, and then ask everywhere: Where do I get Divine Stones??

19: In high difficulty bases, don't buy Invincibility! Don't think that because you're killing Shang Shen outside and don't have time to come back and clear monsters, you should buy Invincibility.. By the time you realize you can't buy it, it's already AG again.

20: During the Heavenly Tribulation, bringing blood-sucking potions will greatly increase endurance. Blood-sucking potions are useful for both melee and ranged combat! (Some friends think blood-sucking potions are useless for ranged - -)

21: When killing Office and Tian Xian early on, you can bring blood-sucking potions. This way, you basically won't lose much health. Actually, bringing blood-sucking potions while killing Office is more useful than bringing Magic Immunity, because the Director's physical attack can still hurt you!

22: Some monsters have extremely powerful magical attacks, requiring Magic Immunity potions. Buy the 10k Ice Milk from the pharmacy granny. The following waves of monsters need it: 5, 10, 12, 18, 23. (To avoid making newbies feel overwhelmed after reading, so it's written relatively simply and clearly.)

23: Friends playing Beast God, when releasing pigs in the early waves, it's best to wait until the monsters reach your doorstep before releasing them, within the range where they can heal at home. The reason is that the pig's lifespan is exactly the duration of one wave of monsters. Release the pig when the first monster arrives, and by the time the last monster dies, the pig will also be about to disappear. In BT difficulty, the early waves can kill the pig! So place it in a position where it can heal at home...

24: Let's all look forward to the new version together! Don't nag Ghost to update every day! The author has his own plan, his own life, and shouldn't have his daily routine affected just because we want him to update the game for us to play~~

25: Before Wave 20's mini-game, you can bring counter-damage to the No.1 Training Room to level up and gain experience and exclusive items. After the mini-game, you can also freeze monsters and continue leveling up. But remember to bring wings and life gems, otherwise, even with 100% counter-damage, you'll still die...
