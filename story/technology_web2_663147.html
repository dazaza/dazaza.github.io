18-Year-Old Girl Kaelin. Kaelin's favorite thing to eat is a TV remote control. Kaelin is eating a plastic water bottle. Plastic water bottles, plastic forks, remote controls, and CD cases are all part of her delicious meals.

According to reports, a girl from California has developed an addiction to plastic, often consuming plastic items as if they were snacks. It is reported that over the past 11 years, she has eaten 60,000 plastic items, including plastic water bottles, forks, CD cases, and even TV remote controls.

She has consumed 1,000 cocktail forks. This 18-year-old girl named Kaelin is from Sacramento, California. In the upcoming documentary "My Strange Habits," Kaelin admits that among the 60,000 plastic items she has consumed in the past 11 years, there were "12 remote controls, more than 5,000 beads, over 1,000 cocktail forks, over 100 forks, 10 water bottles, 2 rubber nipples, 3 CD cases, 50 headphones, 25 takeout cup plastic lids, etc."

Her favorite thing to eat is the TV remote control. When asked why she likes plastic so much, Kaelin said, "I feel like plastic is a necessity for me. It’s not about the taste of the plastic but the texture. I also enjoy the sound when I chew on it."

Kaelin's favorite thing to eat is the TV remote control. She says, "I put the buttons on both sides of my mouth and then swallow them." The item she eats most frequently is the cocktail fork, which she finds at the restaurant where she works. "When there's nothing else to do, I can eat up to 15 in a day," Kaelin said. However, Kaelin admits that this habit is harming her health because she consumes too much every week.
