A newly opened self-service barbecue store on Sichuan North Road has launched a low-price group buying coupon, 23 yuan per person.

by beetgk2958 on 2012-02-21 14:13:41

Babylon BBQ stated that they had initially agreed to issue 4000 group-buying vouchers, but now Juqiwang has issued 10,000 group-buying vouchers, far exceeding their reception capacity. Therefore, after the Spring Festival, they will no longer accept group-buying reservations and have also negotiated refund matters with the website. Cai Yue said that he contacted Juqiwang's customer service, who said that the money could be refunded, but only back to his Juqiwang account, not for cash withdrawal. Cai Yue thought that since he didn't consume anything, why couldn't the money be returned to his bank card? The group-buying voucher hasn't expired yet, but the restaurant says all reservations are full; in Beijing group-buying; if you want to find a group-buying website for refunds, they say it can only be returned to the group-buying website account, not for cash withdrawal. Consumer Cai Yue feels that neither the restaurant nor the website makes sense, so he complained to our newspaper hotline. After communication, the group-buying website agreed to return the payment to Cai Yue. Cai Yue lives near Sichuan North Road. Last October, he saw on Juqiwang that a newly opened Korean BBQ restaurant on Sichuan North Road was offering low-price group-buying vouchers for 23 yuan per person. He thought it was quite a good deal, so he bought three vouchers. Cai Yue also noticed that the validity period of these three vouchers was from October 31, 2011, to March 15, 2012. The after-sales responsible person from Juqiwang's Beijing headquarters stated that if consumers cannot enjoy services due to the merchant's inaction, the website would ensure consumer interests. Finally, the customer service personnel from the group-buying website contacted Cai Yue to handle the refund for him. This year, after the Spring Festival, Cai Yue wanted to go to this Babylon Korean BBQ to eat. He called the restaurant ahead of time, but as soon as they heard it was a group-buying deal, they immediately said "all reservations are full". Cai Yue said that then he wouldn't book for the weekend, just book for a weekday. But the other side indicated that weekdays were also fully booked, even lunchtime. They told him that because the quantity of group-buying vouchers sold by the group-buying website exceeded their reception capacity, so they had long ago negotiated with Juqiwang to stop accepting group-buying customers, and the website could refund those who couldn't dine.
