The Struggle for Power: The Empress and the Concubine

by jqrxass on 2009-08-10 11:23:55

The heavy palace gates closed heavily behind Ling Xuewei... The reason why this daughter of the prime minister became the empress of the current dynasty was a temporary measure due to the secret struggle between her father and the emperor.

On their wedding night, the emperor left without meeting the empress.

How will Ling Xuewei face this seemingly concluded situation?

Inside the palace, the low-profile Ling Xuewei encounters Prince Yu, the emperor's brother. His powerful and passionate nature makes her inexplicably trust and rely on him.

Outside the palace, Ling Xuewei in disguise meets the emperor also in disguise. His elegant and sentimental demeanor makes her feel inexplicable hesitation and attachment.

How will Ling Xuewei deal with this seemingly endless situation?

In the palace intrigue for favor, Consort Liu, Consort An, and others, driven by jealousy, trigger a series of complicated open struggles. Between laughter and anger, life and death are separated.

In the struggle for power in the court, the emperor finally secretly eliminated Ling Xuewei's father, but still doted on her. However, she has already learned the truth...

How will Ling Xuewei choose her own ending?
