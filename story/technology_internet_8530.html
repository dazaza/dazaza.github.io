We will ultimately form a unique and noteworthy characteristic group. Perhaps, everything here is for living better, knowing more, and spreading information more conveniently, and that's all.

Your blog is written with great focus and brilliance. You anticipate the day when someone will discover it, pay attention to it, and engage in communication with more people. However, you suddenly realize that you are just one individual, quietly tucked away in a corner of the Internet. Even if you were to stand up and shout at the top of your lungs, you might still remain unnoticed. Actually, you are not alone; in every corner of this vast world, there are many people just like you. How many corners does the world have? Who could possibly know? Thus, in one particular corner, some lonely souls, unable to endure their solitude any longer, begin to draw closer to one another. They transition from strangers to friends, gradually forming a network, an organization. Here, they admire each other, share common interests, and continue to grow until they are eventually discovered and acknowledged by an increasing number of people. It is at this point that they suddenly uncover the true value of blogging—this is 6YIFE, a non-aligned, non-governmental blog network organization committed to discovering and promoting grassroots celebrities.

We have just begun, we are just setting out. If you are willing, start your application to join us now. We affirm that 6YIFE is a virtual collective formed by various bloggers, where we pursue freedom, brilliance, development, and compassion. The only concrete online presence of 6YIFE consists of an RSS-aggregated link network and the emblem symbolizing the 6YIFE community. Ultimately, we will evolve into a distinctive group worthy of global attention. Perhaps, everything here revolves around living better, knowing more, and disseminating knowledge more efficiently—nothing more, nothing less.
