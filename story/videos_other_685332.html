Perhaps life needs self-relaxation, and people have begun to understand the convenience and speed brought by modern life. Or perhaps fashion-conscious young people like new things; an intelligent revolution about wardrobes has quietly started without notice. When you walk into a home market, you will be surprised to find that the original wardrobe has taken on a new look, developing towards intelligence, humanization, and artistry with a high profile...

Some say that intelligence will be a trend in industry development. With the development of the market, competition in the wardrobe industry has reached a white-hot level. There are also quite a few intelligent wardrobes being sold on the market, but for the entire industry, intelligent wardrobes have not yet been widely developed. Although intelligent wardrobes have already appeared, there are always some unsatisfactory issues for both enterprises and products that need our attention and improvement.

**Problem One: Lack of Industry Standards**

Intelligent wardrobes are a cross-industry system project covering multiple sectors. Equipment manufacturers produce devices according to different interface standards and protocols. However, there is still no basic communication protocol or interface standard for intelligent wardrobe products. This makes interconnection and interoperability between different devices very difficult. It also gives unscrupulous merchants a chance to exploit the situation. Therefore, establishing common standards and protocols is a problem that must be solved first in the development of smart homes.

**Problem Two: Incomplete After-sales Service**

The intelligent wardrobe system is currently not very stable and will more or less encounter some small problems. At this time, the after-sales service of the enterprise becomes very important. Because at this point, the enterprise's product has been successfully sold to consumers, but the quality of after-sales service will directly affect the consumer's secondary investment or product upgrade, thus leaving a bad reputation for the enterprise. For consumers, if the problem cannot be effectively resolved, the good intelligent wardrobe will become a mere display, causing a huge waste of resources.

**Problem Three: Exaggerated Publicity**

Some enterprises, in order to promote their intelligent wardrobes, use many exaggerated statements during product promotion to impress consumers. For example, when promoting the functions of smart home products, they often deliberately exaggerate the effect of intelligence, resulting in consumers feeling that the "promotion" does not match the "actual" experience when using the smart home system.

Despite some unsatisfactory aspects in the development of intelligent wardrobes, how to break through the crisis, stand firm in fierce competition, and enhance the competitiveness of the enterprise is a problem that many intelligent wardrobe enterprises should think about. Therefore, disinfection shoe cabinets, experts in the industry have given some suggestions regarding the main problems currently highlighted in the industry.

**One, Emphasize Product Quality and R&D**

Wardrobe enterprises should further improve intelligent products and increase technical research and development investment. Let intelligent products better meet user needs. During this period, enterprises should understand a principle: only when product quality is guaranteed will customers be satisfied. Moreover, intelligent wardrobes need continuous innovation in technology to continuously meet the market.

**Two, After-sales Service Cannot Be Overlooked**

Looking at the feedback from problems currently appearing in the market, some intelligent wardrobe systems are currently not very stable and may encounter some small problems. At this time, the after-sales service of the enterprise becomes very important. The quality of after-sales service will directly impact the enterprise's reputation.

**Three, Strengthen Cooperation within the Industry**

Enterprises need to invest great effort in all aspects, including technology research and development, humanized design of products, and production processes. Only through cooperation can enterprises form a more complete product series and provide customers with more perfect services.

**Four, Actively Cultivate the Market**

Consumers actually do not understand the actual concept of intelligent wardrobes. Most consumers equate electronic with intelligence, resulting in just "good reviews but no sales," while manufacturers face the awkward situation of "one-sided enthusiasm." Therefore, enterprises need to first create a deeply rooted correct consumption concept, guiding the market to establish a consumption view!
