A self-claimed reader's lament that I saw today, she got hurt

by a20110325 on 2011-04-01 11:15:28

This morning, I accidentally saw several text messages on his (my husband's) mobile phone. The company is Guangzhou VIPshop Information Technology Co., Ltd. The content is as follows: 1. ("Girl, I haven't seen you online for a few days. I guess you are very busy again"). 2. ("I said it, how come you didn't go online? I thought you were on a business trip"). 3. ("Hehe, if you disappear, then I will post advertisements all over the street"). 4. ("Absolutely womanly and magnetic voice, very good, girl"), when I saw these, my whole body was shaking, I was icy cold, but I still didn't wake him up, so I went out to work. Before leaving, I wore a coat. I felt really cold. On the way, I encountered a mobile service outlet, and I printed out his call records. When I got to the company, I carefully studied his call records. I found that many times, he sent messages or made calls to that number right after he left home.

At noon, I returned home and saw him still sleeping. I was really angry. So I pulled open the curtain. He probably felt that sunlight came in. He got up and closed the curtain again. I couldn't help myself anymore. I said, a woman called you, he said, who. I said, that woman you said has womanly charm, then he asked me what I said to her. I said I scolded her. He asked me why I scolded others. I said I was deceiving you, then we started arguing.

He has not found a job for a long time, I go to work, I think I am very good to him. Whatever he wants to buy, as long as I have money, I will definitely buy it for him. I always thought he was a man of principle. Even if I die, I don't believe he would do such things. He actually said I was not good to him, saying it was difficult to get money from me, always asking him what to do with the money, he didn't want to live like this anymore. He said I couldn't help him with anything, and my family also helped him. He said that woman is from Shanghai, an important person in the company, they got familiar with each other, and later she could introduce a job in Shanghai for him. He also said I cut off his way out, and he would find another one; he said there was nothing between him and that woman. I can't stand it. I know divorce is impossible. I am very sad; I don't know why he treats me like this, I trusted him so much. I said, whatever he does, I would forgive him, except for this aspect, which I cannot tolerate at all.

Please forgive my incoherent speech. My mind is really messed up.
