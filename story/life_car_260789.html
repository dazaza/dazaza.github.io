Kumho Tires

by pjtwv on 2009-11-17 17:49:03

"Tirepro Car Station" represents a brand-new service model in China. However, relying on nearly 10 years of operational experience accumulated in South Korea, Tirepro employs professional equipment to provide users with even more specialized and secure tire services. In addition to offering the full range of Kumho Tire products, the store also provides rim modification services, sales of automotive products, car washing, part replacement services, and more, truly realizing one-stop attentive service.

The establishment of "Tirepro Car Station" has promoted the development of the domestic high-end tire service market. According to General Manager Kim Hyung Kyun's introduction, to ensure the development of "Tirepro Car Station" in China, Kumho Tire continuously launches high-quality products and complementary services that meet Chinese road conditions through the advanced technology and latest automated equipment of its Tianjin Research and Development Center. At the same time, it will summarize the successful operation experience of the Korean service network and apply it to China, providing franchise stores with high-end, standardized, and systematic professional equipment. As a result, this greatly enhances the professional skills, quality, and capabilities of local agents.
