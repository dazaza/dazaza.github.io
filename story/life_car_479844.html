Introduction to Car Beauty Entrepreneurial Investment: Car beauty is a simple-to-operate and broad-market entrepreneurial project. People's Car Wash and Beauty requires small investments with quick returns, making it an excellent choice for first-time entrepreneurs!

The car beauty entrepreneurial investment plan systematically and detailedly explains the implementation steps, operation processes, management, and marketing strategies for opening a car beauty shop. It emphasizes details and practical operability, clearly and simply informing entrepreneurs on how to seize opportunities, win customers, improve services, strengthen management, avoid risks, and make money.

Details of the Car Beauty Entrepreneurial Investment Plan (Preparations before Opening a Car Beauty Shop):

First, Market Positioning

The car beauty industry has increasingly attracted people's attention and participation, with car beauty services becoming widely accepted by people. However, the market positioning for car beauty must be clear. If initial funds are sufficient, one can target mid-to-high-end cars for higher profits and quick returns. If initial funds are insufficient, one can target lower-end cars with lower risks. Issues to consider for initial investment include counters, storefront decoration, computers and simple furniture, worker wages, and adequate operating expenses. When opening a new store, several months' worth of car decoration materials and exhibits should be prepared.

Second, Operating Projects

Main operating projects for a car beauty shop: New car wax removal; car glazing beauty treatment; paint surface waxing; paint surface polishing; chassis armor; car interior sauna; car paint surface scratch repair; care for leather products inside the car; photocatalytic interior sterilization and disinfection; tire renewal; body color stickers, car accessories, car interior decoration; car sun film; installation of reverse parking radar; central door lock; anti-theft device; Global Satellite Positioning System (GPS navigation); car soundproofing; leather seats, etc.

Third, Storefront Renovation

When renovating the beauty workshop, arrange properly for water reservoirs, drainage, filtration (wastewater), and electrical outlets while considering moisture-proofing and short-circuit prevention. Additionally, reserve space for future expansion needs to accommodate new business requirements.

Prepare some necessary items: At least two water pumps or more, one carpet wringer, preferably two vacuum cleaners, two waxing machines, three polishing machines, one ozone disinfection machine or high-temperature disinfection machine.

Fourth, Recruitment of Car Beauty Talent

Experienced personnel are preferred as they can quickly adapt to professional situations, helping the store operate more formally and train apprentices.

Careful employees are essential since car beauty is a service industry, and given the high value of cars, any negligence causing vehicle damage not only results in compensation but also reduces reputation. Thus, carefulness is a primary requirement for employees.

It’s best to hire trustworthy personnel because the car beauty industry is indeed a big market that everyone wants to capture. If you hire random people who learn professional car beauty techniques and then abandon your store, you will suffer losses, wasting time and resources to recruit again.

Choosing a franchise must involve selecting a good brand. Franchising aims to rely on the fame, reputation, and technology of professional brands. Given the current lack of standardization in the car beauty market, many small brands pass themselves off as better ones. Therefore, franchisees must be vigilant and choose professional car beauty brands.

Fifth, Product Purchase. If you have chosen a franchise chain brand, then most likely you will use the products provided by the brand. But if you haven’t chosen a franchise, what products should you use? Actually, this isn't difficult. It depends on whether your market research work is thorough enough. By carefully observing competitors, it's not hard to find out which brands they typically use. Since everyone chooses these brands, they naturally won't be bad.
