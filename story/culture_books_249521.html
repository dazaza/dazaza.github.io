Love and freedom

by jqrxass on 2009-10-19 21:15:04

"Love and Freedom" is the fully updated fourth edition of the classic book "Love and Freedom" by Sun Ruixue Educational Institution. From the first edition to today's fourth edition, "Love and Freedom" has entered hundreds of thousands of families. Millions of parents have been completely changed because of this book; they have felt "a shock to their hearts, moved and guilty," and have learned true love and real education from it. Millions of children have grown up healthily and happily because of this book, becoming a new generation with psychological strength, a powerful self, and creative qualities!

At the request of many parents, this revised edition retains all the original classic content while adding the latest classic lectures by Teacher Sun Ruixue, systematically and comprehensively explaining the educational philosophy and practice of "love and freedom."

In the newly added chapter "Love and Freedom: Rules and Equality," the educational philosophy and methods of "love and freedom" by Sun Ruixue Educational Institution are fully explained. It details the education of love, freedom, rules, and equality, answering the questions of many parents on how to give love to children, explains the three major rules for family use, and the seven major rules practiced in kindergartens. These were not present in the previous edition.

In the newly added chapter "Freedom in Children's Growth," it focuses on the five freedoms that children should have, answering the confusing question for many parents: How much freedom should we give to our children?

Teacher Sun Ruixue warns parents: You may think you love your child, you imagine you can love your child, you believe this is loving your child, but there is a difference between this and truly loving your child. Perhaps, you need to pause and see: Are you loving based on your thoughts, your fears, your anxieties, or your habits? This might mean you love your thoughts more, you love your fears more, you love your anxieties more, you love your habits more. Love is love, fear is fear, anxiety is anxiety, thought is thought, everything is as it is. We need to learn to separate love from everything as it is, so your child can truly receive the gift of love. Because the only thing we can give our children in our lifetime is love!
