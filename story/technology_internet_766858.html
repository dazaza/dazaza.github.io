Taobao classmates are coming! Accidentally launched a live streaming course system

by anonymous on 2013-10-01 22:18:21

At yesterday's Taobao Classmates operations exchange meeting held in Beijing, news leaked that Taobao will launch a course live-streaming module called Wangwang Live Room, supporting up to 100,000 people online simultaneously.

The interface of the Wangwang Live Room is simple, with blue and white as the main color tones, supporting multi-video interaction (three-screen division, allowing teachers to see two screens at the same time). It is understood that in the Wangwang Live Room, each institutional seller can open 5 main venues, and each main venue can have 50 sub-venues. The sub-venues support charging functions and can accommodate up to 100,000 people online simultaneously.

Previously, Pei BinFeng, head of Taobao Education, had shared four stages of Taobao Classmates: First, the classroom, where classmates engage in SNS; Second, an education video transaction system, enabling third-party teaching videos iframe embedding; Third, integration of Wangwang video teaching tools, supporting third-party online teaching tools (such as YY, ZhanShi Interactive); Fourth, Taobao Video Live Room, connecting live and recorded broadcast functions, forming an O2O + online live streaming + video course transactions + rating evaluation system + SNS model.

The promotion of Wangwang Live Room indicates that Taobao Classmates has already moved beyond its original plan directly into the final stage.

Most insiders believe that YY Education will be significantly affected, but actually, the biggest impact should be on internet companies like Chuankè and Duobei, which primarily focus on educational live streaming.

"YY in education is basically just a side gig, purely an extension of tools, but YY has never spent any cost on attracting students. Moreover, YY's largest income still comes from the 'diao si' economic model, where accompanying chat and singing are the main sources of revenue," said a market manager of an internet company to DuoZhi.com.

On the other hand, Chuankè and Duobei's main businesses are educational training. For a long time, they have similarly presented themselves as tools to training institutions and users, and the establishment of a marketing platform remains far off. This gives Taobao and all large internet companies an opportunity. Some teachers commented that if Taobao Classmates can directly bring in students, even if the tools are harder to use, both teachers and training institutions would certainly choose it without hesitation.

A year ago, a senior executive of an internet company told DuoZhi.com that pure tool-based applications like these aren't good entrepreneurial directions because big companies can easily copy them, having traffic, recruitment, and marketing capabilities, making it difficult for small companies to survive. However, prior to this, Chuankè received investment from Baidu, and Duobei received investment from Xueersi.

Taobao Classmates also announced its recruitment plan yesterday. Institutions joining Taobao Classmates only need a deposit of 10,000 yuan, a commission rate of 1%, and an annual fee of 30,000 yuan, which will be returned upon reaching 600,000 yuan in turnover. Compared to other service or physical goods e-commerce platforms, this threshold is already quite low. Subsequently, the "Juhuasuan Education Edition" or "JuXiangXue" module will be launched, adopting reverse pricing, where buyers bid, expected to be introduced when the new homepage of Taobao Classmates goes live in October.

"The debut of Taobao Classmates does not make the industry feel that the wolves have come. After all, Taobao Classmates' position on the Taobao homepage is currently not important. When Alibaba executives' 'internal emails' or 'structural adjustments' mention Taobao Classmates, then something significant will truly happen." An ex-Alibaba employee wrote on Weibo.
