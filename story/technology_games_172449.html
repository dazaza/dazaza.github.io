The Ultimate Guide to Rapid Leveling in the Swords and Swordsman Emei Faction: Reach Level 80 in 10 Days

by lk0gameb on 2009-07-08 02:08:39

Before reading this article, make sure you meet the following 3 conditions:

1. Very fast internet speed

2. A lot of time

3. Extremely patient

The first step to entering the jianghu (martial arts world) is to choose a good name. If you like to be called "Ah Miao" or "Ah Gou," then it's best not to continue reading. A master can be nameless, but they absolutely cannot have a bad name.

Nonsense isolation zone -

Choose the Merchant Family background. Some people say that Sang Xin adds the least insight, so this article does not recommend it. The initial training is too slow.

After completing the beginner tasks, you'll be level 7. For the peddler task, just rotate conversations with A to Wu twice, then buy some food, go to the upper right corner of the inn and kill animals until level 10. No need to save money.

At level 10, accept the Liu Yi task, then talk to the Emei guide to join Emei. From the upper right corner of Emei to Chang'an, complete Diao Sanye's shoe task (Note: when picking up the shoes, click one by one, otherwise there will be a bug where you lose 5000 experience) and the Liu Yi task to reach level 12. Then immediately find a large faction to join and start doing faction tasks for money (Yangzhou Central Square). Generally, 100 faction contributions reward 100,000.

Faction task tips: Buy a bunch of all 0-10 level foods and medicines and carry them with you. Absolutely do not abandon any task. The Seven Immortals of Quanzhen are in Chang'an, the Seven Freaks of Jiangnan are in Suzhou, Liu Zhengfeng is in Hankou, and others are generally in the sect (specifically see the NPC directory and shopping guide in the lower left corner of the game interface). Give equipment if needed. Below level 20, only 0-10 level equipment is needed, costing 2000-5000 per piece, which is very cheap. If you pass by Chang'an or Emei, take the opportunity to do the sect tasks. Familiarize yourself with the map, especially the location of the carriage drivers (before level 20, you can return to Emei by finding a place with water to fish. If a snake appears and dies, you can return. Actually, being familiar with the map doesn't save much time, so it doesn't matter whether you do it or not).

After achieving more than 1000 faction contributions, you can do more sect tasks and fully upgrade your faction skills. Upgrade your level when you have enough experience, until level 29. Then allocate points to martial arts 4, 5, and 6 up to level 39, and store experience until the 10x experience bar is full. If you're unlucky and keep getting level 20 equipment tasks, you can request more rewards. As long as you have enough contributions, money won't be a problem.
