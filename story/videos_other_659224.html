On the evening of February 6, the first full moon night of the Dragon Year, China's Top Ten Brands of Guangzhou Guangchen Commerce and Trade Co., Ltd., CCTV Comprehensive Channel and Variety Channel invite you to enjoy together with your family - the 2012 CCTV Lantern Festival Gala!

Sina Entertainment reported that the theme of the 2012 CCTV Lantern Festival Gala was determined to be "auspicious and如意celebrate the Lantern Festival", which is expected to return to the basic cultural connotation of the Lantern Festival. "Celebrating the Lantern Festival" is a unique positioning of the Lantern Festival formed by Chinese culture over thousands of years, and only this festival should be celebrated in the form of "celebration". Therefore, the 2012 Lantern Festival Gala will fully render and highlight the festive atmosphere of "celebration" through multiple links such as host language status, connection methods, and program design.

Firstly, in the design of the hosts, it will conform to the theme of "auspicious and如意celebrate the Lantern Festival", dividing the 6 hosts into "auspicious" and "如意" teams, connecting the whole scene in the form of "three-on-three" competition to send greetings. In terms of host language, a relaxed and natural tone will continue to be maintained, while at the same time, the popular phrases left over from the 2012 Spring Festival Gala programs will be integrated throughout.

Secondly, the gala will retain the family-style reunion format of the 2012 Spring Festival Gala. The entire program still starts with the joint singing format of star families from all over the country, including the Dai Yuqiang family, Hu Yue and Lu Jianmin couples, Liu Hegang and his wife, etc., thus creating a warm and festive atmosphere of reunion.

Thirdly, in program design, the creative ideas of the Lantern Festival Gala use more "cross-border" and "mixed" forms, aiming to give the audience a refreshing experience and increase the joy of the entire gala. For example, the song "Because of Love" will sound again in the Lantern Festival Gala after the Spring Festival Gala; the difference is that the stage packaging mode of Wang Fei and Chen Yixun from the Spring Festival Gala is retained, but the singers are replaced by Cai Ming and Liao Changyong, bringing new interest to the same song work. In addition, Liu Qian, who has a high audience expectation, will also appear again in the Lantern Festival Gala. However, this time, he and his golden partner Dong Qing did not perform magic, but sang songs, which will surely bring new expectations to the audience. In addition, Li Yundi, the piano prince from the Spring Festival Gala, this time will play the piano solo with Sa Yi's saxophone, accompanied by the double dance of Li Qian and Zhang Aoyue, the lead dancers of "Dragon and Phoenix Presenting Auspiciousness" from the Spring Festival Gala, forming a mixed program "Colorful Clouds Chasing the Moon".

In addition to innovative performances of classic works, the Lantern Festival Gala will also heavily feature new people and new works. It is hoped that during the lively and festive Lantern Festival, it can send a blessing of reunion and perfection to all TV viewers across the country and Chinese people around the world.

Broadcast time: CCTV-1 Comprehensive Channel and CCTV-3 Variety Channel will be broadcast simultaneously at 20:00 on the evening of February 6. (Editor: wwt)
