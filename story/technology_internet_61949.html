Maxthon server was maliciously attacked, causing some users unable to use account services normally

by 127702 on 2008-04-11 09:24:50

Because of recent continuous malicious attacks on several servers of Maxthon, a great number of users were not able to use the account service normally. We sincerely apologize for that. We strongly condemn the destroyers who initiated this attack. Regarding the seriousness and danger of this vicious network attack event, we have reported it to the public security organ and submitted relevant evidence so as to severely punish the destructive molecules causing this accident.

In addition, the server fault is being urgently repaired. Users who cannot log in their Maxthon accounts please do not be unduly worried. This accident is a test for everyone, but please believe that Maxthon will try its best to recover the service within the shortest time.

Hereby, Maxthon solemnly calls on domestic Internet enterprises to all observe the industry norms. We always respect lawful competitors, but despise unscrupulous malicious competition.
