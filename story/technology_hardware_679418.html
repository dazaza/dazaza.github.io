On February 8, during the team's training camp in Turkey, Sun Tian celebrated young player Qu Cheng's birthday. For Qu Cheng, celebrating his birthday overseas was just one of the happy events; after the training camp ended, he and teammate Yang Xiaotian went to Parma for a trial, which was part of a comprehensive cooperation plan between the two clubs. "These two players are just pioneers; if this model succeeds, more players will go for trials or even join the club," said Gong Jianren, chairman of Sun Tian Football Club.

Dragan supports his disciples studying abroad

Last year in the Chinese Super League (Weibo), Sun Tian eventually ranked fourth, while the young players participating in the reserve league made greater progress, finishing third. Among them, forward Qu Cheng scored 12 goals alone, becoming the top scorer of the team. Notably, this player was loaned to Indonesia Super League champion Persipura in the first half of 2010, scoring 6 goals in 10 appearances.

"I don't have too many thoughts; since I'm here, I'll train well and definitely won't embarrass Sun Tian," Qu Cheng said. "This time, I will play some friendly matches with the team, and I can also watch the Serie A at Parma's home ground, it will be very interesting, I can't wait." Qu Cheng is also very admiring of Parma's head coach Donadoni, who once coached the Italian national team. "This time, taking a photo with him is a must."

Compared to Qu Cheng, who has played in the Chinese Super League, Yang Xiaotian hasn't yet appeared in the league. This young man was born in Xi'an and moved to Lianyungang with his parents when he was in the fourth grade of elementary school. Not long after, he was selected into the Jiangsu Football Association's youth team through a selection process, until he was picked by the Sun Tian coaching staff after the 2009 National Games.

For the study abroad of these two disciples, Sun Tian's head coach Dragan is fully supportive. "The selection of players is jointly decided by the club and the coaching staff; Dragan is very pleased with this model. Only by going out will Sun Tian's young players keep emerging. Dragan said that Sun Tian must have its own good prospects, so as not to always have to spend big money on buying players and be constrained by others, so he highly appreciates our cooperation with Parma," said Gong Jianren.

Parma high-standard reception

When the main body of Sun Tian returned from Turkey to China on the 23rd to prepare for the Chinese Super League, Qu Cheng and Yang Xiaotian arrived at Parma, where the club specially held a press conference for the two players and invited many local media outlets. On the evening of February 23 local time, the press conference was held at Parma's home ground, Tardini Stadium. Sun Tian Club expressed through its official Weibo account that Parma specifically equipped two Chinese translators for the two Sun Tian players, responsible for their daily life and training.

At the press conference, almost all of Parma's senior management, including Chairman Gilardi, attended, and Qu Cheng and Yang Xiaotian wore Parma uniforms and took photos holding Parma jerseys as souvenirs. The Parma official website also provided a relatively detailed introduction of the two Chinese young players. "Qu Cheng has already experienced important matches such as the AFC Champions League, but this time at the Tardini Stadium is different. Yang Xiaotian is a left-sided winger who once participated in the reserve league with Sun Tian and won the Winners' Cup Championship with the team in 2009."

Qu Cheng said in an interview with the Parma official website that he was very happy to come to Italy. "I know this team has very good players like Giovinco and Biabiany." Yang Xiaotian said that being able to try out for Parma made him feel "unbelievable", "My favorite player is Ronaldo, and Biabiany in the Parma team is also a good player. I hope through my efforts, I can learn knowledge and experience on the field here."
