Family Affection Rating: Five Stars

Eldest sister Song Tianhao is the head of the household.

As the absolute leading character in both series, Song Tianhao and Zhu Kaishan fully demonstrate the differences between men and women when it comes to family affection. After her father's death, Song Tianhao took on the responsibility of supporting the entire family. To raise her younger sister, Tianhao worked various jobs and eventually joined a funeral cooperative carrying dead bodies, keeping the truth hidden from her sister. When her second sister Tianting got into legal trouble and there was no money to bail her out, Tianhao resolutely went to jail in her place. When she was told by a doctor that her bones had been incorrectly set and needed to be reset, she decisively broke her leg again without hesitation, all because she still had to farm to support her younger siblings. When she and her sister Tianting both fell in love with Wei Demin, she quietly wiped away her tears in a small room and then acted as the head of the family to bring Tianting and Wei Demin together. When faced with choices, as the head of the household, she firmly placed family affection first, with peace and reunion being her greatest responsibility in life.

In Zhu Kaishan's world, the country and morality are more important than family affection.

During the Boxer Rebellion, Zhu Kaishan left his family behind. Clearly, Zhu Kaishan values family affection less than Song Tianhao does. For Song Tianhao, home is her entire sky, whereas in Zhu Kaishan's world, the country and morality weigh more heavily than family.
