by xfxybk on 2012-02-16 16:05:06

Reporters learned from Tiantan Town Government of Shunyi District yesterday that, in order to guarantee the fire safety in the villages under its jurisdiction, the town government equipped all four villages under its jurisdiction with a motorcycle fire truck each.

Because the streets and lanes in the four natural villages under its jurisdiction are narrow, it is very difficult for large fire trucks to drive in the villages. In order to solve the hidden dangers of fire and improve the ability to cope with emergencies, recently, Tianzhu Town invested in purchasing five motorcycle fire trucks, allocated one to each of the four natural villages, and kept another one within the town to execute mobile tasks. It is understood that the four-wheeled motorcycle fire truck can drive freely on narrow streets and has strong mobility.

Hubei Jiangnan Dongfeng Special Purpose Automobile Co., Ltd. is a designated production plant by the National Development and Reform Commission, the only manufacturer in the capital of special purpose vehicles with qualifications approved by the Fire Bureau of the Ministry of Public Security, and an accompanying modification plant of Dongfeng Motor Corporation. The main products produced and sold include fire trucks, obstacle-clearing vehicles, aerial work platforms, wing-opening box-type transport vehicles, water sprinklers, refueling vehicles, chemical liquid transport vehicles, flatbed transport vehicles, mobile power vehicles, garbage trucks, etc., hundreds of varieties. All models have been listed in the national announcement directory, obtained ISO9001-2000 quality management system certification, and the products have passed CCC certification.
