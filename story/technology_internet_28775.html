Tianlai Network: the first banner in the field of online planning

by mulinsen on 2007-09-01 18:15:15

Accidentally came to the TianTi website - TNTBBS, and since then I've become a sincere friend of TianTi.

I believe that every person who wants to achieve something in the Internet era, every person who enjoys helping others, giving advice, planning for network success, finding joy in this and realizing their own value, will fall deeply in love with TianTi.

TianTi is the first and only website planning forum in China. More interestingly, this site embodies a model that I think is very distinctive and promising. This model is an original creation of TianTi, called the "WeiQi" model. It builds an online advisory, planning, and consulting platform for numerous experts (although there are few experts now, but looking at the whole of China, how many network planning experts can there be?) and webmasters, including aspiring ones. I found that the most active on this site are actually the aspiring webmasters, maybe they have a more urgent need to learn, communicate, and seek guidance.

I only know a little about the WeiQi model; the webmaster hasn't provided a detailed introduction about it.

TianTi has been around for seven or eight months now. It already has more than 4000 members, which isn't bad. However, the webmaster seemed to never promote the site before. Many people, including me, found out about it by accident. Once here, we all feel regret for not discovering it sooner. I think that a site like this would develop slowly if it relies solely on word-of-mouth because many people are selfish and reluctant to recommend it to others, fearing that others might surpass them. Haha, I don't know if I'm right, but if the site were promoted more aggressively, how many times faster could it grow! For example, Admin5 today, its development entirely depends on a group of people promoting it everywhere. Today, there was even a report in the Computer Newspaper advertising Admin5. TianTi should learn something from Admin5, I personally think so.

However, it seems that the webmaster has his own reasons. But I did see some enthusiastic members helping to promote TianTi, including myself, as I also want to give TianTi a boost in the future. I worry that one day the webmaster might get upset seeing the site not developing well and close it down, haha.

TianTi claims to be the website with the highest PV in the TNT industry. Recently, I noticed some changes in Alexa rankings, but I believe TianTi's PV must be very high. When I first discovered TianTi, I was like a thirsty man, reading posts all day without daring to speak. Too high PV can lead to penalties from Alexa.

TianTi hides dragons and crouching tigers! Here, I hope to seek advice, learn, and exchange ideas with many top domestic experts. Let's not be shy and practice our skills!

This is a pure land, mutual help has become a trend here, what is lacking is publicity!!!!!!!

Each member is willing to help others. Just look at the website diagnosis and commentary section, everyone is so enthusiastic! They offer each other valuable suggestions and encouragement! In such a long time on TianTi, I haven't seen anyone scold another! What I feel more of is human warmth!! The warmth, civility, mutual assistance, and high-quality side of Chinese webmasters!!

Thank you, Administrator Wang Yihui, for creating such a great place for us!

I want to shout to all network entrepreneurs: If you're a network entrepreneur, if you have problems related to network planning, visit www.tntbbs.com, where many enthusiastic experts will provide you with help!
