Faded Flowers and the Flow of Time, Loneliness Smells Sweet

Fully aware that the rain of fireworks will eventually fade from my life, I have been searching for an unknown ending. The broken string of tears cannot reconnect the lingering fragrance of bygone years. When the splendor fades, who remains in the flow of time to softly chant those weathered vows?

- Preface

The petals on the other shore flutter gracefully, accompanied by sorrowful songs at midnight. They gather the lingering attachment of a shallow summer, while under the moonlight, white feathers dance silently. Indifferent to the full moon blooming with fireworks, who accompanies you in your infatuation by the lakeside of the mortal world? Fingers flickering again, they cannot play the beautiful pretense; memories wander through the years among the dimly lit lanterns, forgetting how far forever is. Tears slide over the eyelids, hastily embracing the prophecy.

Who knows, for whom does one stitch cause pain? Waiting for a firework display until the wind and moon wither away the splendor, yet never encountering beauty.

Who knows, for whom does a flower bloom in intoxication? This cycle of life follows you through the mortal world, quietly dancing across the horizon where I get lost.

Who knows, for whom does a simple brush sketch a lifetime? The time we've walked through has not clearly revealed any face, but when I turn around, tears flood my city.

At dusk in the waning years, what devours youth? Unable to touch the former clear skies, only memories remain scattered on paper. Time flows silently and quickly, accompanying you through solitude, darkening a grand encounter. My heart aches slightly as faded vows ripple through memory. Grasping the fleeting life proves impossible because you forgot to wait for me. The fragrance lingers through the years, while the disconnected rain sings loneliness in a string. Unable to hold the brilliance of youth, looking back, I see your smiling face.

Those pains flowing through the years are remembered. When time reveals the ending of fairy tales, I can only try to discern your gradually retreating figure.

Those dreams sinking into midnight are shattered. Actors entering paintings foretell a life of sorrow, and ultimately, I lack the strength to pick up the scattered fragrance amidst the floating dust.

Those compromises frozen in the years are entangled. Residual flowers swirl indifferently, their eternal vows turning cold. The wounds between eyebrows scatter the season's reverie.

Warm-colored dreams lie shattered everywhere. I crouch down, picking up piece by piece the faint fragrance scattered in the years, quietly leaping onto my brows. Yearning escapes its bounds, accompanying me in burying the end of time and earth. The colors blooming from fireworks pale my lifelong wait. I walk quietly until I lose the path of memory.

Picking up a page of floral rafts, I carve the tender affection of yesteryears. Among the vast sea of people, I sink into the dust.

Drunk on a page of poetic lines, I recall the extravagant flower affairs of past years. A倾city beauty, I think of your smiling glance.

Dancing in a curtain of dreams, I grow indifferent to the promises made yesterday. As night falls and the moon mourns, time rushes far away.

Gently intoxicated in the years with you, amidst the swirling red dust, I solely yearn for the joy you once promised me.

Faded flowers and the flow of time, each petal carries sorrow, dyeing the loneliness sweet like fragrance. When the splendor fades, who will build for me an eternal haven?
