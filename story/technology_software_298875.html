(a) An uncut cake: large market blank, good market prospect

Mr. Chen Yuanbin, a franchisee from Jiangsu, entered the crystal jewelry industry 20 years ago and made his first pot of gold through hard work. With the continuous improvement of people's living standards and aesthetic tastes, he thought this business would be even better, but the reality was completely opposite. The reason is that there were only about three crystal jewelry stores in the city ten years ago, while now there are nearly a hundred of all sizes. A mid-range crystal jewelry item cost between 200-300 yuan ten years ago, but now some stores sell it for as low as 30 yuan. The jewelry industry may seem prosperous today, but in fact, it is struggling to survive. Mr. Chen Yuanbin deeply realized: the jewelry industry has already reached saturation, and this cake has been divided up almost completely. He needs to find a new industry. But what kind of industry still has market blanks? Whether it is clothing, catering, or car beauty, which one is not overcrowded with many participants?

In Dangyang's Balin Office, Wang Lin, a returned migrant worker, founded the "Dongsheng Buddhist Incense Factory". In the newly built factory building, more than ten workers are busy at work, and the freshly produced Buddhist incense is neatly placed aside waiting to be packed.

Driven by necessity, in 1995, Wang Lin set out on a difficult journey to work away from home, and this journey lasted for 13 years. In 2007, he came to a Buddhist incense factory in Yongchun County, Fujian Province, to learn the technology of making incense, becoming an excellent technical worker. "The operation process of producing Buddhist incense is not difficult. The 'Fo Yun Lai Science and Technology Development Co., Ltd.' shows that the market demand for Buddhist incense from coastal areas to inland areas is very large, plus the great potential of tourism resources in Dangyang, famous temples are excellent sales markets." At the beginning of this year, after carefully investigating the market, Wang Lin, at the age of 41, finally mustered up the courage to return home and start a factory.

Seeing some policies promoted by Fo Yun Lai Science and Technology Development Co., Ltd., he became even more interested and decided to join Wuhan Fo Yun Lai. "Wuhan Fo Yun Lai Science and Technology Development Co., Ltd." has a long history in the incense industry. Franchisees relying on the 'Wuhan Fo Yun Lai' platform will shorten the market launch period, directly entering the growth and rising periods. The brand effect of the product is your first key to success. Meanwhile, the headquarters will invest a large amount of funds in advertising campaigns across various media to make the 'Fo Yun Lai' brand deeply resonate with a wide range of consumers, enhancing product awareness. To enable franchisees to quickly occupy the local incense market, we will exchange and share our corporate culture and successful marketing experiences, helping them grow rapidly. Through our joint efforts, 'Fo Yun Lai' will become even stronger and help people get rich.

When Wang Lin's idea was known, the Balin Office and Qunli Village held multiple coordination meetings, helping him choose the factory site, raising 50,000 yuan in funds to renovate the old village premises, leveling the factory land, and setting up dedicated power lines. Wang Lin also invested 300,000 yuan to purchase two sets of incense-making equipment from Fujian, training 12 displaced farmers from the surrounding area. On June 25th, his 'Dongsheng Buddhist Incense Factory' officially opened for production. In the past month, five merchants within the province have come to contact him about purchasing incense, and local Buddha statue sellers have also come in succession.

"After production stabilizes in the second half of the year, I plan to invest another 500,000 yuan to add three more sets of incense-making equipment, further expanding the scale, and hire 30 more workers to work with me!"
