"Love's Vines: A Lifetime of Tangled, Warm Encounters"

A drifting snow, a tragic and beautiful love.

The moment they met, the pure white world was blanketed in heavy snowfall, and on that first night, love blossomed into an absolutely stunning flower.

Wei Xi discovered He Qianxun under the dim streetlights, troubled by nightmares. She warmed his dreams and expelled the fears that had haunted him for eight years!

From this moment on, it was destined that he would not let go! It was destined to be a warm wait.

Memories settle in the tides of time like surging waves. When light and shadow change instantly, the girl standing alone in the snowy night quietly waits for the blooming of her love.

Suddenly looking back, you are nowhere to be seen, only a thousand searches...

"The Vine of Love: A Thousand Searches" First Search: Snow Fog

Even if I have to go through countless pain and sorrow, even if I have to face hundreds of hopeless searches, I will still come back, because in this world, you are the only one. Because no one can replace you! Because I once loved you so simply and deeply. And that lingering love ties us together, crossing the heavens and the earth, over millions of searches...

"The Vine of Love: A Thousand Searches" Second Search: Warm Memories

He Qianxun sat at the table opposite Wei Xi with two cans of beer. He didn't notice Wei Xi, just gazed coldly out of the window, and with another hand, pulled open the tab of the beer can with a "snap".

His face remained pale as he continued to drink large gulps of cold beer, without any regard for whether his fragile stomach could bear it. The fingers holding the spoon unconsciously tightened, and worry appeared in Wei Xi's eyes. It seemed like he drank too quickly and got choked by the cold beer. Wei Xi watched as He Qianxun suddenly bent his head down, his shoulders shaking continuously, coughing uncontrollably, while his fingers gripping the beer can were unknowingly applying more force.

"The Vine of Love: A Thousand Searches" Third Search: Encounter

Ling Wei Xi walked in carrying a bowl of fragrant hot porridge. The porridge was very hot and full, so she held the bowl carefully with both hands, always looking down at the hot porridge in her hands, slowly stepping forward. After much effort, she finally entered the room, turned slightly to place the bowl of porridge on a small table by the door, and hurriedly lifted her hands, which were burned from the heat, pinching her ears, muttering, "So hot!" When she was young, her brother taught her. When your fingers get burned, as long as you pinch your ears with your fingers, it will feel much cooler and comfortable.

"The Vine of Love: A Thousand Searches" Fourth Search: Passing By

Luo Shanni's return was always as rash and unexpected as her personality! During the third class in the morning, just before the class was about to end, there was suddenly a sound of "ding ding dang dang" coming from the corridor outside the classroom, getting closer and closer. The face of the ancient literature teacher turned from red to white and then green, because at the moment her face turned green, the door of the classroom was slammed open, followed by a series of even louder "ding ding dang dang", and a figure stumbled ungracefully and fell at the entrance of the classroom.
