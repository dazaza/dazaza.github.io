Man Admires Girl, Takes Her Phone, Convicted of Snatching罪

by zsh0827901 on 2012-02-13 10:10:12

China News Service, Xingyang, May 25 (Wu Yang, Zhang Tuling, Xing Faxuan) - A man in Xingyang, Henan Province who was infatuated with a girl took the unusual step of taking her phone to try and get her to spend more time with him. However, the girl didn’t appreciate his efforts and reported him to the police. On May 24th, the People’s Court of Xingyang City, Henan Province sentenced the young man to four months of detention for robbery and fined him 2000 yuan.

At around 8:00 pm on September 13th, 2010, Liu from Xingyang was invited by his schoolmate Cheng to visit his home. During the meal, Liu was struck by the beautiful appearance of a girl surnamed Ma who was also at the table. He wanted to start up a conversation with her but since they had never met before, he wondered how he could establish a connection with her. Liu appeared calm on the surface but secretly looked for an opportunity. Suddenly, Liu noticed that Ma had placed her phone on the dining table. Seeing his chance, while complimenting Ma's phone, he quickly picked it up.

After getting hold of the phone, Liu immediately removed Ma’s SIM card and replaced it with his own, pretending to make a call to someone else. He then asked everyone else at the table, including Cheng, to leave the room for a moment. Subsequently, Liu and Ma began arguing over the phone. Ma became flustered and started shouting in the room. Hearing this, Cheng entered the room and tried to mediate between them. However, until the end, Liu still refused to return the phone, jokingly saying that he liked Ma so much that he did this.

The next day at noon, Ma, in order to retrieve her phone worth more than two thousand yuan, proposed to treat Liu and Cheng to a meal. Unexpectedly, Liu agreed to eat but refused to return the phone. Feeling aggrieved that her phone was taken without reason, Ma went to the public security bureau to report the incident.

At this point, Liu still thought it was just a way to interact more and didn't take it seriously. It wasn't until the police detained him that he realized the severity of the situation. In court, his defense attorney argued that Liu only intended to use the phone as a means to initiate contact with Ma and did not resort to violence. Although the method of possession was illegal, the circumstances were relatively minor, thus requesting exemption from criminal punishment.

The court ruled that Liu, taking advantage of the situation when others were unaware, snatched the phone and refused to return it, with a significant value involved. His actions meet the elements constituting the crime of robbery. As such, he was found guilty of robbery, sentenced to four months of detention, and fined 2000 yuan.

After the verdict, Liu lamented, “Pursuing a beauty must be within the bounds of the law; I truly regret my actions.”
