Pirate King - Captain Kidd's abandoned ship is discovered

by joyie8joy6 on 2007-12-17 15:45:55

A scientific research group claims that their divers have discovered the wreckage of a 17th-century pirate ship abandoned by Captain Kidd near the shallow coastline off the Dominican Republic. The underwater archaeology team from Indiana University claims they have found the remains of the Quedagh Merchant, which treasure hunters have been searching for in recent years. Charles Beeker of Indiana University states that his team has obtained permission to study the wreckage and plans to designate the site as an underwater preserve. Although the wreckage is located just 70 feet off the coast of St. Catalina Island in the Dominican Republic and less than 10 feet underwater, it has remained undisturbed for years.
