FAQs:

1. Event Time and Location:

Starts at 2 PM on May 20th, approximately 2-3 hours long. Third floor, Jinmao Shenzhen JW Marriott Hotel (five-star hotel).

2. Participation Requirements:

Each family is limited to three participants. Babies must be born in May and under three years old (those who have just turned three are also eligible).

3. Items Customers Need to Bring:

Regular baby items. Remind customers to dress nicely as there will be photography and videography. Matching parent-child outfits are preferred.

4. How to Obtain Photos:

After the event, we will provide download links for on-site activity photos on the You You official website and group.

5. What Activities Are On-site:

Baby crawling competition, hand and footprints, Zhuazhou ceremony, activities for 2-3 year olds, and various selection and award ceremonies.

6. Is There a Fee for the Event?

This event is free to attend, with no charges.
