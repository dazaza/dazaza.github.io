Roewe MG group buying extravaganza begins, half-price car purchasing becomes a reality

by rabihdq993 on 2011-09-29 17:50:37

As the National Day holiday approaches, the domestic automobile market has also ushered in the traditional "golden September and silver October" sales peak season. In anticipation of this year's most anticipated market hot period, the Roewe MG brand models from Shanghai Automotive have launched a frenzied group-buying campaign, offering the most cost-effective discounts to all consumers. With 84 group-buying events taking place across 46 cities nationwide, the maximum discount reaches up to 30,000 yuan, making it possible to achieve the dream of buying a car at half price! Additionally, there are more prizes to be given away.

Car group-buying is no longer a novelty. Internet users who wish to purchase the same brand of car gather online to collectively negotiate prices with dealers. This time, Shanghai Automotive has teamed up with famous websites to launch a crazy group-buying event, aiming to allow participants to obtain even greater discounts than traditional in-store purchases. As the Roewe and MG brands from Shanghai Automotive gain increasing recognition and choice from more car enthusiasts, more consumers are preparing to buy these brand models while hoping for even bigger price discounts. Shanghai Automotive does not disappoint the expectations of consumers, simultaneously announcing on Pacific Auto Network and Tencent Paipai.com ten special supply models of the Roewe 350, Roewe 550, MG3, and MG6, available for online group-buying at discounted prices. Simply fill out your personal information to claim free discount coupons; once every 1,000 people participate, one Roewe/MG car model will be given away as a thank-you gift to netizens. There are also opportunities to win attractive prizes upon visiting the store, oil card raffles for placing orders, and grand prizes like a 4999 yuan car purchase fund, an IPAD2, and a SONY camera set for those who make a purchase, truly embodying the concept of delivering value to the end.

To capture the favor of consumers towards a particular car, it must possess the characteristic of high cost-performance. In this aspect, Shanghai Automotive deeply understands the consumer's mindset, consistently adhering to meeting the practical needs of consumers as its guiding principle. It continuously adjusts and perfects its products, enhancing product quality so that each model under its brand holds significant advantages whether in appearance, power, handling, safety, comfort, or functionality.

The Roewe 350 is a family sedan positioned in the A-class market and also a home-use sedan integrated with 3G technology, targeting young people who love life, making it suitable for a three-person family for urban commuting. The Roewe 550 has consistently shown excellent market performance since its launch, not only equipped with high-end configurations but also offering super-value pricing. The MG3 can be considered a star model with both style and substance. Under the current trend of domestic oil prices approaching 8 yuan per liter, low fuel consumption has become an unavoidable factor for car buyers.

The MG3, whether powered by a 1.3L or 1.5L engine, adopts an innovative independent dual-layer cooling system, enabling quick engine warming after startup, reducing fuel consumption and carbon dioxide emissions, allowing the MG3 to operate in a highly efficient energy-saving mode with low fuel consumption and low emissions without compromising performance, appealing to the eco-conscious crowd. The MG6, through extending the roof curve backward to the trunk, creates a more spacious headroom compared to regular three-box sedans and enhances its aerodynamic effect. Even in the premium luxury car market, crossover models like BMW X6, 5 Series GT, and Honda CROSSTOUR adopt similar fastback coupe styles. Many of Shanghai Automotive's high cost-performance models have captured the hearts of consumers.

"Golden September and Silver October" marks the peak season for car purchases throughout the year. Compared to numerous promotional activities, this group-buying event by Roewe and MG stands out as exceptionally valuable. From obtaining discount coupons online, visiting stores to view cars, signing purchase agreements individually or as part of a group at 4S dealerships, there are prizes to be won everywhere. After group-buying, participants can draw lots for oil cards, with a 100% chance of winning, ranging from thousand-yuan denomination oil cards to the grand prize of purchasing a car at half price. Consumers flock to the 4S dealerships, facing discount levels that are absolutely impossible to encounter on ordinary days, many customers immediately seek out sales consultants, gaining some understanding before promptly placing orders, resulting in exceptionally vibrant sales.
