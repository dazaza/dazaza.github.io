College girl hesitates between two boyfriends; one stabs the rival to death

by fhh451dfg on 2012-03-09 16:42:44

On the day of Zhang Baojuan's birthday, Wei Junyong specially bought a set of brand-name clothes to give to Zhang Baojuan. Wei Junyong's generosity made Zhang Baojuan very happy. Wei Junyong also carefully prepared a candlelight dinner, which made Zhang Baojuan feel even more trapped in her suffering. In the star-like candlelight, she excitedly said to Wei Junyong: "I have never been this happy before, you are really good to me!"

The campus romance evolved into a tragedy.

At around 10 p.m. on January 2, 2010, the sky was covered with a gloomy cloud, and students of a certain university were walking around the campus in groups of three or five. Suddenly, a scream tore through the quiet night, and students immediately ran towards the source of the scream and quickly called the police.

In others' eyes, Zhang Baojuan had a boyfriend who protected her in every possible way, which was indeed enviable. But in Zhang Baojuan's view, there should also be fun in life for the two of them. She liked romance, but Zhu Guoliang thought that was wasteful; she loved shopping, but Zhu Guoliang always bought cheap street goods. In Zhang Baojuan's eyes, he did not satisfy her wishes many times, plus the differences in their values, they often quarrelled over small things.

Wei Junyong pulled out a fruit knife and stabbed at Zhu Guoliang's head. After being stabbed, Zhu Guoliang fought with Wei Junyong. During the struggle, Wei Junyong continuously stabbed Zhu Guoliang in the neck, chest, and shoulder several times, and blood spurted out. Zhu Guoliang fell on the ground gasping for breath, then Wei Junyong kicked his head violently and fled.

At 6 p.m. on January 2 this year, Zhang Baojuan received a text message from Zhu Guoliang: "I'm coming to see you, can we meet?" She replied: "After evening self-study, wait for me at the library." She was afraid of being discovered by Wei Junyong, so she deleted the text message.

Zhu Guoliang came from a poor family, his parents were both farmers. Although Zhang Baojuan did not ask for too much from him, he did not want Zhang Baojuan to suffer with him in the future. Besides studying, Zhu Guoliang became a promoter in a mall near the school, using the money he earned partly for his own living expenses, and sending the rest to Zhang Baojuan. Zhu Guoliang was diligent and studious, especially excelling in mathematics, mechanics, and other subjects, deeply respected by the department leaders, and selected as the class leader. According to a responsible person of his college, the college had already determined Zhu Guoliang as the cultivation object for the school's teaching staff.

After meeting and chatting for more than ten minutes in the second-floor reading room, Zhang Baojuan and Zhu Guoliang walked out together. When they reached the stairs downstairs, a familiar figure passed by, Zhang Baojuan turned back and saw it was Wei Junyong! At this moment, Wei Junyong's body was filled with a surge of anger rushing to his brain, he thought if he didn't teach Zhu Guoliang a lesson, that guy would not stay away from Zhang Baojuan.

The young woman spoke incoherently: "Zhu Guoliang just happened to come see me today... I didn't expect... We were fine just now..." As she spoke, she was already sobbing uncontrollably, "It was done by Wei Junyong, he ran... This is all my fault! It's me who ruined all three of us!"

They were all excellent students once.

The entangled relationship led to a triangular dispute.

Not long after, the police car and ambulance roared to the scene, and the situation on site was horrifying. A disheveled young man lay by the side of the road. His hair was messy, his face pale, and his entire body soaked in blood from various wounds. About three to four meters away from the young man, a fruit knife about twenty centimeters long with a milky white handle stood out conspicuously, with spots of blood on it. After examination by the emergency personnel, the young man had been stabbed more than twenty times on his head, chest, neck, back, and upper limbs, with his heart and lungs pierced.

On January 2, 2010, the first day after New Year, the festive atmosphere had yet to fade, but a young and vibrant life disappeared from the world. Wei Junyong, a 21-year-old college student, killed Zhu Guoliang, who was the same age and had a bright future. Behind the motive for murder was a campus romance caught up in a triangular dispute.

Around 11 p.m. that night, Zhu Guoliang died after unsuccessful rescue attempts. Not long after Wei Junyong fled, he was captured by the police and was recently approved for arrest on suspicion of intentional harm by the procuratorate.

At the crime scene, the police found a young woman standing there blankly. Among the scattered books were several bloody photos, which were exactly the合影of her and the injured young man. It was not hard to guess that she and the young man had an unusual relationship.

Although Zhu Guoliang was very good to Zhang Baojuan, he was too boring. The distance between their schools made Zhang Baojuan often feel lonely and isolated. Wei Junyong took the opportunity to use every trick in the book, often finding various excuses to invite Zhang Baojuan to eat, drink tea, and chat. As the number of interactions increased, their feelings deepened, and Zhang Baojuan's heart gradually leaned towards Wei Junyong. On December 20 last year, Zhang Baojuan formally proposed breaking up with Zhu Guoliang. Despite Zhu Guoliang's repeated requests for her to change her mind, Zhang Baojuan left without looking back.

On a day in December 2009, the five-year-long relationship developed cracks. Zhu Guoliang was unwilling to let go of this feeling, his heart was extremely painful, and his emotions were very unstable.

Different from Zhu Guoliang, Wei Junyong, a university classmate of Zhang Baojuan, was eloquent and capable, and quite popular among girls. When Zhang Baojuan was lost in sorrow over her past relationship, Wei Junyong appeared and launched a fierce romantic pursuit.

Zhang Baojuan's words made Wei Junyong overjoyed. He held Zhang Baojuan's hand and solemnly swore: "If you are willing, I can guarantee that I will give you surprises every day!" "But me and Zhu Guoliang..." Before she could finish speaking, Wei Junyong interrupted her: "Are you happy with him? Why torment yourself?"

During the more than two hours of self-study class, Zhang Baojuan was restless. Around 9 p.m., after finishing the evening self-study, she left the classroom and headed directly toward the library, coincidentally seen by Wei Junyong who was giving her postgraduate entrance exam materials. Just as Zhang Baojuan arrived at the library building, Wei Junyong caught up and gave her the postgraduate entrance exam materials. Zhang Baojuan said: "Go back first, I still have something to do." Then she went upstairs along the library staircase. Her uneasy expression aroused Wei Junyong's suspicion.

Zhang Baojuan's shoulders touched Wei Junyong's, which dealt a fatal blow to Zhu Guoliang. Zhu Guoliang heard that Wei Junyong had a complex circle of friends. To win back his girlfriend's heart, he found Zhang Baojuan and warned her not to stay with such a person. These words reached Wei Junyong's ears, and he confronted Zhu Guoliang several times asking for explanations.

The young woman is named Zhang Baojuan, who has grown up gracefully and beautifully since childhood. In 2005, she entered high school and became classmates with Zhu Guoliang. They admired and encouraged each other, and three years later successively entered universities, moving from Hunan to Shanghai.

Zhang Baojuan's parents were both cultural workers, influenced by the family environment, she showed innate artistic talent from a young age. In a university-organized song and dance evening party, she won unanimous praise from teachers with an exquisite ethnic dance. With outstanding performance ability and a warm personality, she was recommended to serve as the publicity commissioner of the student union.
