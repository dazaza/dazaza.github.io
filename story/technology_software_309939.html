The treatment of National Security being received is on par with Olympic champions: Fans shout Lee Chang-soo's name on site   According to reports, last night at the National Stadium, as the team anthem "National Security Always Strives for First" sounded again, a series of celebratory activities following Beijing Guoan Football Club's first league championship came to an end. This morning, the team will fly back to Kunming to prepare for the YOYO test on December 26th. Although the "National Security Forever Thankful to You" appreciation evening lasted two hours, all the true highlights were created by the National Security players, making other big-name stars pale in comparison.

The performance began at 7:30 PM. After a lively dance, the appearance of the National Security players sparked the first climax. After all the players took the stage, captain Xu Yulong held the "Fire God Cup" and walked onto the stage shaped like the "National Security emblem," standing on the golden star when Xu Yulong's face broke into a smile. "That moment felt just like we had just won the championship, to be honest, it was really exciting," Xu Yulong said afterward as he passed the trophy to his teammates one by one, who then raised the golden cup high to salute the audience. The fans of the National Security Supporters' Association sat in the upper stands opposite the chairman's platform, and their chants of "National Security are the champions" turned the National Stadium into the Workers' Stadium.

Compared to the appearance of the National Security players, the subsequent performance by Weiwei failed to elicit cheers from the audience and even led to several awkward silences. Even Weiwei's extensive stage experience could not completely resolve the awkwardness. In the following performances, the actors pulled out all the stops, but the on-site fans remained unimpressed. It wasn't until Xu Yulong, Du Wenhui, Wang Changqing, Yan Xiangchuang, and other players performed "True Hero" live that the second climax was reached. Before Yuquan sang "The Most Beautiful," some fans went up on stage and presented the team with a trophy made of premium chocolate, eliciting strong applause from the audience.

At 9:40 PM, Song Zuying's "Good Days" brought the evening to a close. In the final stages, it was once again the National Security players who walked onto the stage to wave goodbye to the fans, bringing the performance to its final climax. "We've seen many celebrity performances, but we prefer the National Security players," one fan said while walking away: "It would be great just to chat with the players, such opportunities are truly rare."

The three national team players Yang Hao, Yang Zhi, and Huang Bowen, who did not attend the event on-site, sent faxes expressing their gratitude to the fans. After the event, the celebration activities for the National Security championship officially concluded. Meanwhile, Tao Wei, Yang Pu, and seven other players formally bid farewell to their identities as National Security players and will face the National Security team in their new roles next season.

■ Captain's Performance

National Security Captain Becomes Top Star

Xu Yulong Performs Live Improv

National Security captain Xu Yulong was undoubtedly the most dazzling figure at last night's evening gala. Not only did he appear last to lift the "Fire God Cup" and lead the singing of "True Hero," but he also performed a live "stand-up comedy routine."

An interactive segment between a football-loving family and the National Security team was arranged at the gala site, with Xu Yulong representing the team on stage. As soon as he stepped on stage, he was embraced by the third-generation female fan of the family. "No problem, I'm married," Xu Yulong's comment made the on-site fans burst into laughter.

When the father of the fan family spoke on behalf of his family and mentioned hoping "Xu Yulong plays for another 20 years," Xu Yulong, holding the microphone, said: "I'll do my best, let's see how long I can hold out." The players and Hong Yuanshu sitting below the stand burst into laughter after hearing this.

In the closing stages of the interactive segment, Xu Yulong shared his feelings on stage, saying: "When I chatted with my roommate Yang Pu, I asked him if there was a time he wanted to cry. Yang Pu said he cried when he saw TV footage of the fans, not just him, but all of us players have similar feelings. We really thank all the fans." After these words, the venue once again echoed with the chant of "National Security are the champions."

After stepping off the stage, Xu Yulong immediately handed over the chocolate trophy from the football-loving family to head coach Hong Yuanshu. "All those words were my real feelings at the time, the fans really don't have it easy," Xu Yulong said.

■ Leadership Concerns

Beijing Municipal Government Receives the Team

National Security Treatment Comparable to Olympic Athletes

Before attending the "Appreciation Evening," the Beijing Guoan players first visited the Beijing Municipal Government and were received by Party Secretary Liu Qi and other municipal government leaders. The location was on the second floor of the Beijing Municipal Government office building, the same place where municipal government leaders received Beijing athletes who participated in the Olympics.

The reception activity started around 5:15 PM and lasted nearly 30 minutes. Led by Beijing Party Secretary Liu Qi, Mayor Guo Jinlong, Vice Mayors Cai Fuchao, Jilin, Liu Jingmin, Huang Wei, and other municipal leaders greeted all the members of the Beijing Guoan team. Also attending the event were Sun Kanglin, Director of the Beijing Sports Bureau, Li Shilin, Chairman of CITIC Guoan Group, General Manager Sun Yalei, Deputy Chairman Luo Ning, General Manager Li Xiaoming of Beijing Guoan Football Club, and Deputy Chairman Zhang Lu.

After speeches by Sun Kanglin and Li Shilin, Liu Qi delivered the final speech on behalf of the Beijing Municipal Government. Besides encouraging Guoan to continue upholding the spirit of "always striving for first," he also expressed hopes for the vigorous development of youth football in the future and the cultivation of reserve talents.

■ Celebrity Support

Jiang Kun Teases Three Coaches, Praising Fans

Lee Chang-soo Returns to Korea Today

In last night's performance, the actors not only loved football but were also mostly Guoan enthusiasts. Among them, Jiang Kun and Dai Zhicheng performed the crosstalk piece "Joy Outside," with Jiang Kun's question "Who has the greatest ability in the Guoan team?" being the most grounded.

Dai Zhicheng successively threw out three coaches: Jin Zhiyang, Lee Chang-soo, and Hong Yuanshu. However, each was rejected by Jiang Kun, with reasons being: Jin Zhiyang didn't win the championship; Lee Chang-soo resigned midway through the season; Hong Yuanshu became a coach too late. The final answer given was: Fans have the greatest ability, supporting Guoan for 16 years regardless of weather conditions.

Not only was Lee Chang-soo mentioned in the crosstalk, but his image also appeared multiple times on the large screen on site, with some fans shouting "Lee Chang-soo" on site. Lee Chang-soo will leave Beijing today and return to Korea. "Thank you everyone," Lee Chang-soo frequently said in recent interviews: "The most important thing is that the Guoan team has won the championship."
