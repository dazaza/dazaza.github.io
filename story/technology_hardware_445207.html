Man donates 20,800 milliliters of blood throughout the year, becoming the national champion (Picture)

by zzf000zxyn7 on 2011-06-13 13:01:58

December 28, in the apheresis room at the Jiangxi Blood Center, Yang Chunrong came here again to donate platelets by machine. Reporter Deng Xiaoyong took the photo.

The director of the Jiangxi Blood Center sent flowers to Mr. Yang. Reporter Deng Xiaoyong took the photo.

December 28, Nanchang, Jiangxi Province - According to the report by reporter Deng Xiaoyong: Yang Chunrong is an ordinary employee of a subsidiary company under the Jiangxi Construction Engineering Group. At 9:30 on December 28, he arrived early at the blood collection hall of the Jiangxi Blood Center to prepare for his 13th apheresis (machine collection of platelets) of the year. It was reported that since the beginning of this year, Yang Chunrong has donated blood voluntarily 13 times and donated 26 therapeutic units of platelets, which is equivalent to 20,800 milliliters of whole blood, ranking first nationwide in terms of the amount of blood collected by machine in 2009. In order to be able to donate blood in his hometown, he spent more than 2,000 yuan on round-trip travel expenses between Zhongshan, where he worked, and Nanchang!

Mr. Yang told reporters that his first blood donation was in 2004. Not long after donating blood, he received a greeting card from the Jiangxi Blood Center, with a very touching thank-you letter. These small details from the staff of the Jiangxi Blood Center surprised and deeply moved Mr. Yang, and further strengthened his determination to continue donating blood.

Yang Chunrong said that at the beginning when he started donating blood, his mother always opposed it, but later after explaining the reasons clearly, the elderly agreed. Donating blood can enhance the body's immunity and help others. "My blood flowing through the bodies of those in need is a very happy thing," Yang Chunrong said that he would keep donating blood voluntarily.

Doctor Xie of the Jiangxi Blood Center told reporters that compared with last year, the amount of blood collected by machine in Jiangxi Province this year increased by 28%. Mr. Yang donated 20,800 milliliters of whole blood this year, which is the highest nationwide. Since blood can be collected once every month, this frequency and capacity of blood collection will not have any negative impact on the donor's health.
