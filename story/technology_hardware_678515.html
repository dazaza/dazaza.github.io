Thanks to the words

by chao7wudi7 on 2012-02-27 15:03:38

Every flower in bloom, Zhuo Zhuo Qi Hua by Su Yi Huang Youth, is the most beautiful years of our lives. Youth, is the uninhibited laughter and crying without a care in the world. Our fleeting youth leaves behind everlasting memories accompanied by little joys and pains, which we all experience. Everyone has different stories, different entrances, and different performances. My youth has gone forever, the days dripping away in the flow of time, without a sound or shadow. Thank goodness for words that allow us to record our memories and commemorate the splendid times we spent on campus. What about you? Do you still remember your youth and your love affairs? First encounter, university At the end of summer, the sky was a deep blue. The sunlight filtered through the gaps between the leaves, casting a picturesque mosaic of light and shade. The moisture in the air was drying up rapidly, taking away the bustling summer along with the restless emotions of this season. When Zhen DuoDuo stepped out of the station, she saw a prominent signboard displaying the name of her university as written on her admission letter. Dragging her suitcase under the signboard, a group of enthusiastic seniors swarmed around her asking if she was from their school and which department she belonged to. One of them offered to carry her luggage... With furrowed brows, Zhen Dodo remained silent until the surroundings became quieter. She then said, "Excuse me, brother, could you move your foot?" Everyone looked down to see a large footprint on her white canvas shoes. The culprit stared at Zhen Dodo in disbelief, as if it were her who had stepped on him. Zhen Dodo glared fiercely at the unmoving student, hoping to memorize his face for future revenge. Uncomfortable under her intense gaze, the student broke out in goosebumps even in the scorching summer heat. Several school buses arrived, and the seniors arranged for the new students to return to campus. The bus drove smoothly all the way, lifting the spirits of everyone onboard. As the speed slowed down, Zhen Dodo looked out of the window. It seemed they had reached the school. The road was wide, flanked by tall and broad trees with lush green leaves dancing in the wind, creating a very pleasant scene. The bus stopped, and Zhen Dodo got off to look for her Economics and Trade Department. Amidst the crowd, the sweat smell was strong, and the thirsty Dodo felt momentarily lost, unsure of where to go or how to get there. Just as she regained her composure, she saw a tall teenager wearing roller skates weaving through the crowd before disappearing, leaving no chance for an awkward reunion. After finding the reception desk for the Economics and Trade Department, submitting the relevant documents, collecting daily necessities and dormitory keys, a senior named Song Nan, who looked like an uncle due to his scruffy beard and unfriendly appearance, kindly offered to guide Zhen Dodo to her dormitory. Why call him an 'uncle-level' senior? Because his scruffy appearance reminded Dodo of various creepy uncles. Song Nan generously took charge of all Dodo's luggage and enthusiastically introduced the history of the school and the department. Despite Song Nan's enthusiasm, Zhen Dodo felt quite embarrassed because his image made her feel uneasy and scared. When they reached the 6th apartment building, Song Nan stopped and handed Zhen Dodo a card: "This is my phone number and QQ number, along with my dormitory number. You can find me if you have any questions, or even if you don't have any..." Zhen Dodo nodded, smiled, took the card and her luggage, took a deep breath, and headed towards her dormitory - Room 217, Building A, a four-person room with bunk beds, an independent bathroom, and a balcony facing south with excellent lighting. Satisfied with her accommodation, Dodo chose the bed closest to the balcony, thus beginning her university life. All Zhen Dodo's roommates were from the Economics and Trade Department, including Yuan Yuan, who was also in her class. Overweight kids seem to have a special kind of telepathy. When Zhen Dodo saw Yuan Yuan, Yuan Yuan just happened to turn and look at Zhen Dodo. There was a flash of sharpness in her eyebrows, and Zhen Dodo was ready to rush forward aggressively but suddenly flashed a smile instead: "Ah, hello, I'm Zhen Dodo." Opposite, Yuan Yuan also squinted and smiled: "I'm Yuan Yuan." Zhen Dodo's mouth twitched slightly as she shivered for two reasons: first, what did she just say? Her name was Yuan Yuan? That was quite... round, haha. Second, why did she look so much like a cat when she smiled? And Zhen Dodo was most afraid of cats. However, overweight kids are always so kind; even if someone looks like an animal, they are still human! How could she discriminate against others? Right! So, Yuan Yuan became the first feline-human friend that Zhen Dodo met at university. The summer breeze was warm and dry, and even continuous wiping of sweat was ineffective. While chatting idly in the dormitory, Zhen Dodo and Yuan Yuan became parched when their roommate Tan Ning returned with a box of mineral water. This was a pretty girl with a good figure who had just seen off her parents who brought her to school. "Dodo, Yuan Yuan, come drink some water," said Tan Ning. Upon hearing this, Yuan Yuan immediately sprang forward, uncaringly grabbed a bottle, drank more than half in one breath, and then said, "Oh my, I was so thirsty. Sister Ning, you saved my life, I'll never be able to repay you..." Tan Ning smiled and replied, "You just don't want to repay me!" Zhen Dodo quickly "rescued" Yuan Yuan by saying, "Sister Ning, look at her round shape; it's indeed hard to hug her!" Thus, an invisible battle smoke arose, 2-1, Yuan Yuan lost. Tang Jinghan appeared in the eyes of Zhen Dodo and others as a "reader". In the evening, unable to wait for the last member of their dormitory to arrive, the three hungry girls decided to protect themselves by going out to find food. After dinner, they casually visited the night scenery of the school and returned late. The moment they entered the dormitory, Zhen Dodo thought she had traveled back in time. Were these really college students? It was only the first day of university, and they were already studying into the night (well, not exactly "night" yet)! Seeing them, Tang Jinghan just gave a friendly smile. Yuan Yuan, being her usual loud self, jumped in front of Tang Jinghan and flipped open her book: "Wow! 'Wuthering Heights,' oh my gosh, and it's in English, talent ah!" Tang Jinghan silently pulled the book back from Yuan Yuan, smiled, and explained, "I had nothing else to do, so I read a book." Then she continued reading. Feeling awkward, Yuan Yuan asked further, "What's your name? Have you eaten? When did you come? Wow, your things are so neatly packed..." Zhen Dodo, seeing Yuan Yuan's nagging nature, wanted to grab her and give her a couple of slaps. That night, only the whirring of the electric fan and the chirping of cicadas outside the window remained. Zhen Dodo lay on her side, having trouble getting used to the new environment due to her habit of sleeping in a familiar bed since childhood. "Are you guys asleep?" Yuan Yuan whispered. "What's wrong?" Tan Ning, who was lightly asleep, responded. "Sister Ning, I can't sleep, I miss Mom..." Saying "Mom," Yuan Yuan started to sob slightly. "Me too..." Zhen Dodo, who had been silent, sat up and said, "I can't sleep either, I miss my mom too, sigh..." "Dodo isn't asleep either? Then, let's chat a bit." Tan Ning said gently. "What should we talk about? Um... have you guys watched 'City Hunter'? I absolutely adore Lee Min-ho, his height, his build, his skills... his eyes kill everything, and also Kim Rae-won in 'Playful Kiss,' and Zhao You Ting in 'Mozart of the Jungle,' they're stunning, literally dream boyfriends for countless teenage girls..." This was Yuan Yuan's obsessive monologue... "Go to sleep!" Zhen Dodo decisively chose to ignore Yuan Yuan. Closing her eyes, an unfamiliar silhouette passed through her mind. The next morning, Zhen Dodo was rudely awakened. She got out of bed, leaned on the balcony watching the bustling street outside, and turned back to ask, "Kids, is this the legendary recruitment for clubs?" Yuan Yuan immediately sat up in bed, "What? Clubs? Are they those clubs with handsome presidents?" "You idiot, apart from handsome men, what do you know?" Zhen Dodo sarcastically replied. "Haha, lustful younger sister." After staying quiet all night, Tang Jinghan finally spoke. Answering the call of many roommates, they unanimously decided to go check it out. There was a long line on both sides of the road, with some booths bustling with activity while others were deserted. When they reached the English Club, Tang Jinghan walked ahead and chatted with the seniors. Yuan Yuan shrugged, showing no interest. Ahead, a club was surrounded by people, and the commotion sparked Yuan Yuan's powerful curiosity. Relying on years of bus-riding experience, she combined martial arts, yoga, judo, and balance beam skills to push her way into the crowd, not emerging for a long time. Zhen Dodo looked at the crowd devoid of Yuan Yuan's silhouette with disdain and walked forward with Tan Ning. Seeing the etiquette club, Zhen Dodo expressed immense pressure. There were only tall and beautiful women gathered there, ready to try out. Turning around, she carefully examined Tan Ning from head to toe and said, "Sister Ning, speaking of your looks and figure, you should at least score eight or nine points... Shall we take a look?" Tan Ning frowned and asked, "Etiquette? What does it do?" "If I'm not mistaken, it teaches etiquette!" Zhen Dodo confidently stated. Tan Ning already had countless black lines running across her forehead. During dinner, Zhen Dodo asked Yuan Yuan, "Hey, Little Yuan, what club did you squeeze into today? There were so many people!" Judging by the question, Yuan Yuan immediately perked up, "Hey, do you know? The drama club in our school has three handsome presidents! It's truly a pity not to have lived this life... tsk tsk~" "Here we go again, sigh, why am I such a fool to ask her this kind of question?" Zhen Dodo showed no interest in handsome men or celebrities. "Aren't children all so lustful?" Tang Jinghan threw out this comment while quietly eating her meal and continued without looking up. "Why are you so excited? Haha, how is liking handsome men considered lustful? Also, where am I still a child? Huh?" Yuan Yuan's face turned red with frustration over the word 'lustful'. "What's with all the excitement? Haha, you're naturally lustful." Zhen Dodo actually liked Yuan Yuan's little obsession. Later, they found out that Yuan Yuan joined the drama club, Tang Jinghan talked about English with the seniors of the English Club for the whole afternoon, and Tan Ning was inexplicably pushed by Zhen Dodo into the Etiquette Club, where she filled out questionnaires all afternoon. Then, what about Zhen Dodo? When everyone turned their eyes to her, she lowered her head shyly and then raised it to declare with great righteousness: "I joined... the Roller Skating Club!" "Ah..." The three looked at Zhen Dodo in disbelief. Zhen Dodo didn't know why she joined the Roller Skating Club. It was just that when she turned the corner, she saw a lonely table with only a stack of paper, and above it, a banner read "Roller Skating Club". After filling out the form placed on the table with a few other scattered students, she left. What kind of club was this, without even a host? Zhen Dodo had many questions she wanted to ask.
