However beautiful the past was, it will be forgotten in the end.

by mimi456 on 2012-02-03 21:38:38

No matter how beautiful the past was, we will eventually forget each other in the turmoil of life.

It's been a few months since I joined this circle! I've met so many people, but it seems like there are only a few that I truly remember. Maybe it has something to do with my bad memory. But there is always one person who stays in your heart, no matter how hard you try to forget them. Even though they're no longer part of your life, they still hold such importance. As someone once said, some people come into your life, teach you something, and then disappear... Haha!

I'm not a shrewd person; my thoughts are always very simple, or maybe even naive! My classmates call me an idiot because I think everything is so simple and believe everyone is good, warning me that I'll be deceived sooner or later! But I've always thought what they say is wrong! I won't cause trouble or deceive others! Perhaps this is my uniqueness. Sometimes I get melancholic because I also have deep thoughts. The more people I meet, the more I realize that there is no such thing as purely good or bad people! Because I believe that unless there is a deep grudge, why would anyone want to harm you?

Even though I am already 22 years old and not young anymore, I always hope I can stay younger, at least to make up for the lost childhood! Occasionally, when pimples appear on my face, I get worried. I remember the first time YJ scared me half to death, and I used to blush when seeing pretty girls! They say that despite my age, I act like a kid. In fact, my naivety and idiocy are just part of my mindset! Life is short, and happiness is the most important thing! However, I haven't forgotten that if something exists, it has its value and responsibility, and we must support our own sky!

In everyone's eyes, I have always been the strong one, competitive, and unwilling to lose, but who understands my thoughts? Who knows my heart? I strive for everything because I feel that life is too short, and I don't want to waste a single moment or leave any regrets! To be honest, I am a very simple person. Sometimes I behave like a child, sometimes like an elder, sometimes crazy, and sometimes very formal! Because I don't want to be monotonous.

As for university, I cherish it greatly. No matter who criticizes my current school, I still think it's the best! Recalling how I entered through self-study exams, it was entirely unexpected. Therefore, during my university years, I work hard, study diligently, and get along well with everyone, smiling at everyone! But you don't know how exhausting it is to pretend. Today, when someone said I wasn't competent, I didn't argue back because I know I've done my best...

The legend of love, of course, I've experienced it. Honestly, I am quite devoted. Back in the day, I once gave my all for a jerk, crying my eyes out, disregarding my image, sobbing uncontrollably. But after that, I never felt that way again. When it comes to emotions or feelings, I now approach them with more rationality and indifference! Faced with those who are kind to me, I remain unmoved! It's like this... Not that I don't love anymore, but I'm afraid to love anymore! Looking up, I see others' happiness. Some wounds, given enough time, will heal slowly; some grievances, once endured and understood, will dissipate; some pains, once tolerated long enough, become habitual... Yet in many lonely moments, these feelings resurface. —— Actually, some words hidden in my heart are not intentionally concealed, but not all pain can be expressed aloud.

In life, there are many fleeting moments, like farewells at the station. Just moments ago, we were hugging, but now we are worlds apart. Often, you don't understand, and neither do I. Thus, talking turns into change, listening leads to fatigue, watching brings boredom, following slows down, walking together drifts apart, loving grows distant, thinking ends in giving up.

Life is too short. Whoever we meet is worth cherishing; being together is fate, staying together is happiness! I know I'm not a great recorder, but I enjoy looking back on my journey more than anyone else. I constantly look back, pause, and then, time rushes forward without me, moving on powerfully. Better by far you should forget and smile than that you should remember and be sad. (It's better to forget and smile than to remember and be sad.) Isn't life just like that? Why burden yourself and make things difficult for others?

No matter how beautiful the past was, in the end, we will still forget each other in the chaos of life. That's about it! I'll stop writing here today...
