As we all know, round nails, cement steel nails, and collated nails are hardware products indispensable to every household, every factory, and every industry. They are ubiquitous, and their market is everywhere. Especially with the rise of the construction decoration industry, the sales of round nails and collated nails will continue to grow, making the markets for round nails and collated nails never saturated. However, in the past, small round nails were produced by large state-owned enterprises or big factories using large equipment or whole coils of raw materials, which required significant investment, had high costs, complex processes, and kept prices high. Meanwhile, the waste wire materials, scrap rebar ends, scrap iron wires, scrap cold-drawn wires, and scrap welding rods from wire drawing plants, cement product factories, rolling mills, and the construction industry pile up like mountains and could only be used as raw materials for steelmaking. In response to this situation, our company decided to make full use of waste materials, turning them into treasures, and developed a fully automatic scrap rebar nail-making unit. This unit is small in size, consumes little electricity, is inexpensive, operates simply, and can use scrap wire materials to produce round nails. The quality of these round nails rivals those made from new steel, but the price of scrap rebar is only half that of new steel coil raw materials, thus offering a significant profit margin, making it an excellent opportunity for individual entrepreneurship and factory establishment.
