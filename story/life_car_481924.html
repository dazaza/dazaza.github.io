Car beauty knowledge: answers to car maintenance, just like people, cars need care and maintenance. If you want to beautify and maintain your car, you must understand the relevant maintenance knowledge, as well as the car beauty products and car maintenance products often seen on the market. Only by understanding can you compare and distinguish the quality and authenticity. Some car accessories are very cheap, such as car wax, a box costs about 5 yuan. When we talk about snow cream, everyone knows it. Twenty years ago, little girls all used this, 2 yuan per bag, now they use 200 yuan per bottle. These seemingly cheap products have no effect on car maintenance and beauty, and may even be harmful to the car body. Below are some common questions that car owners encounter during car maintenance or washing, and we will answer them one by one.

A. What to do if the car freezes while washing?

Answer: In cold weather, water often turns to ice immediately. The biggest taboo when washing a car is having water droplets freeze on the vehicle, as this can directly lead to paint layer cracking. Therefore, it's best to wash the car with warm water instead of cold water. If possible, wash the car indoors. After the car enters the workstation, let it stay for 5 to 10 minutes until the engine cools down before washing. Sudden temperature changes are not good for the paint surface.

B. What's the difference between polymers and silicon resins?

Answer: Car wax contains different types of polymers. Polymers usually enhance the durability of the product and/or seal the car paint to prevent corrosion caused by environmental pollution. Silicon resin components are used to enhance the brightness of the paint surface.

C. How to wash your car in snowy winter days?

Answer: It snowed for several consecutive days, and Mr. Li kept postponing washing his car. By the time he wiped it clean, he found that the car paint was not as glossy as before? Wiping the car should be timely. Even if the weather forecast says it will snow in the next few days, don't delay wiping the car. As long as the car is covered with snow, you should immediately rinse off the snow with clear water. Snow contains corrosive components. Whether it is the paint surface, chassis, tires, or rims, being covered with snow for a long time will corrode the car paint, which is why it is not as glossy as before.

D. Are the dashboard and car doors made of genuine leather?

Answer: No (very expensive cars might have genuine leather). They are leather products, so they only need surface polishing and do not require penetrating nourishing liquid.

E. What's the difference between leather and synthetic leather?

Answer: Synthetic leather is fake leather, without pores, it's just shiny on the surface. Genuine leather is similar to our skin, the internal structure of genuine leather is actually very similar to a dense bundle of silk, they are connected together by protein chains. Once the protein chain shrinks and dries out, the genuine leather will start to crack.
