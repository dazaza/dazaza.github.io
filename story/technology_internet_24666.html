Chinese.com 800,000! Chinese.com.cn 200,000!

by 127702 on 2007-07-13 14:37:56

WininDomain Exclusive News, July 13th - The recent news that the domain name chinese.com was sold for a high price of 800,000 euros on the internationally renowned domain trading website SEDO has attracted industry attention. Today, we found on the SEDO website that the domain chinese.com.cn is being auctioned with a starting bid of 200,000 euros.

http://tool.winindomain.com/whois/?d=chinese.com.cn

Through a WHOIS query using the above URL, we contacted Ms. Cai, the registrant of chinese.com.cn. She told us that last week, many friends saw the news that chinese.com had been sold for 800,000 euros, and afterward, everyone suggested that chinese.com.cn should also be put up for auction. After analyzing the trading conditions of .cn domains over the past two years, it was decided to auction this domain with a starting bid of 200,000 euros. "Chinese" itself is an excellent theme, and the application of Chinese language and the Chinese internet market are areas worth paying attention to and capable of supporting performance. This domain's auction on the SEDO website aims, first, to confirm the status of .cn domains in the international domain investment market, and second, to validate our confidence in the value of .cn domains through this price.

Professional analysts believe that the auction of the chinese.com.cn domain starting at 200,000 euros is exactly 25% of the previous selling price of chinese.com. Considering the application fields and market recognition of both .cn and .com domains, the offer of 200,000 euros is not high. From the analysis of successful transactions like google.cn in the past two years, the value of "chinese.com.cn" goes far beyond this.
