A lively baby can bring a great atmosphere to the family after birth, with everyone smiling. Mothers all hope that their babies have good intelligence and are more active, which is endearing. At this time, they will set some relevant rules for their own babies and also look up many books. They also need to understand a lot about diet, exercise, and prenatal education. In today's society, many mothers focus on health and prenatal education knowledge. Experts affirmatively say that prenatal education is absolutely beneficial and has no harm to children.

American brain scientists once conducted a slice observation of Einstein's brain cells. They found that Einstein's brain only weighed 1230 grams, which is below the average weight of modern human brains. However, his parietal lobe was about 15% wider than an average person’s. Further research discovered that the more dendritic spines there are, the smarter a person's brain becomes. However, after birth, the development speed of dendritic spines in humans slows down. Only during the fetal period, with specific stimulation, can the number of dendritic spines increase. Do you envy those giants with remarkable achievements? Da Vinci, Mozart, and Einstein possessed intelligence far surpassing that of ordinary people, leaving behind rich intellectual legacies for human society.

Indeed, during the fetal stage, the brain develops rapidly. A fetus at 90 days already has pressure and touch sensation; at 120 days, it has cold sensation; at 150 days, it has warmth sensation; and at 210 days, it is highly sensitive to pain. At 120 days, the fetus already has hearing, and by 180 days, its hearing is almost equal to that of an adult. Expectant mothers who don't want their babies to fall behind from the start should scientifically and reasonably conduct prenatal education without delay.

Smart mothers know the benefits of music for their babies, and prenatal education is also important. However, they still feel somewhat anxious and lack direction. Without a systematic guidance tutorial as a personal nurse, they always feel something is missing, and are uncertain among conflicting concepts and information.

Through learning, a fetus is born understanding approximately 15 words and their meanings, and can respond to these words. Babies who have undergone prenatal education are generally smarter, more active, and show excellent results in various aspects compared to those who haven’t.

Prenatal education may not have been very popular a few years ago, but in this society, no one would say that prenatal education is useless. Everyone conducts relevant prenatal education for their babies. Therefore, let us together witness the wonders of the prenatal education method mentioned above.
