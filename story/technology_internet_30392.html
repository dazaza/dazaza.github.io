China's Youtube - Battle of the Video Sharing Sites

by huahuaniu on 2007-09-24 20:18:03

Videos are shot in order to be put on the Internet and shared with netizens. Where to upload, so that you are neither overwhelmed nor neglected?

Why Youtube succeeds can be summed up in three points: first, convenient use, no need to consider format when uploading; second, technological leadership, no need to install any video playback software to enjoy a good picture; third, good recommendation for grassroots podcasters, completely user-led, user behavior decides which one comes first.

After the success of Youtube, many domestic video websites have emerged like mushrooms after rain, and these websites have also witnessed the growth of a number of famous podcasters. Do you want to become a network podcaster? Find a segment of video and upload it online. With so many video websites, which one should we choose?

The reporter compared this issue with the three major successes of Youtube and conducted an experiential test on five relatively large online video websites. These five websites are 6 Rooms, China Podcast Network, Youku.com, Tudou.com, and Ku6.com.

·Test Section 1:

Upload For the testing of this section, the reporter chose the same file (17M, wmv format), and uploaded it to these websites respectively at the same network time and in the same network environment. Through the testing of time, speed, and stability, the reporter gained a certain understanding of the upload function of these websites. Due to the poor network environment where the reporter is located, the upload speed was not very fast.

China Podcast Network Score 7.0

Supported Formats: wmv, rm, mpg, mpeg, avi, 3gp, mov, mp3, wma

File Size: Normal Upload 100M, Super Upload 200M

Upload Time: 20 minutes, average speed 15k/s.

The upload function of China Podcast Network is still acceptable. It was successful at the first upload, and you can see the video a few minutes after uploading.

Tudou.com Score 6

Supported Formats: wmv, .asf, .asx, rm, .rmvb, mpg, .mpeg, .mpe, 3gp, movmp4, .m4v, avi, .dat, .mkv, .flv, .vob, mp3, .wma

File Size: 100M 15 minutes

Upload Time: 25 minutes Average Speed 12k/s

The upload function of Tudou.com is not very satisfactory. In this test, the speed of Tudou.com was the slowest. Compared with other websites, it was about 1/3 slower, and the performance was not satisfactory.

Youku.com Score 6.5

Supported Formats: avi .dat .mpg .mpeg .vob.mkv .mov .wmv .asf .rm .rmvb .ram .flv.mp4 .3gp .dv .qt .divx .cpk .fli.flc .m4v

File Size: 200mb Automatically split if over 20 minutes

Upload Time: 19 minutes Average Speed 16k/s

The upload speed of Youku.com has a slight advantage compared to other websites, although the advantage is not very obvious. However, the stability of Youku's upload is not good. The reporter's first upload failed, and the speed display during upload reached an astonishing speed of 2673k/s, but after showing upload complete, it kept showing "acquiring file" and then stopped moving. The reporter successfully uploaded the video on the second attempt.

6 Rooms Score 6.5

Supported Formats: Wmv, wm, asf, asx, rm, ra, rmvb, ram, mpg, mpeg, mpe, vob, mov, 3gp, mp4, m4v, avi

Super Upload supports flv files Size: Normal Upload 100M Super Upload unlimited

Upload Time: 21 minutes, average speed 14k/s

The upload speed of 6 Rooms is average, but relatively stable. The reporter successfully uploaded once, and after uploading, it automatically selects representative frames from the video as thumbnails, with good effects.

Ku6.com Score 0 (Unable to upload)

Supported Formats: Unknown (not clearly given on the website)

File Size: Unknown (not clearly given on the website)

Upload Time: Upload failed

The upload of Ku6.com is the worst among all the tested websites. The reporter uploaded 8 times consecutively without success, all of them got stuck at a certain point and could not continue. The reporter also tried various methods, such as changing browsers and restarting the computer, but none of them worked. After struggling for several hours, the reporter chose to give up.

·Test Section 2:

Playback After uploading this batch of files, the reporter conducted playback experiments on the participating websites. When the network conditions were relatively good, all websites could play videos smoothly. However, after uploading the same video, it was found that there was still a gap in video quality between different websites.

China Podcast Network Score 7.5

From the screenshot, we can see that the video on China Podcast Network is still relatively clear, but some details are not good, especially the edges of the characters are not clear, which is more obvious in panoramic series scenes.

6 Rooms Score 6.5

The video quality of 6 Rooms is somewhat unsatisfactory. The overall feeling of the picture is soft, the scanning texture is relatively clear, but the picture shakes a bit, and the blurriness around the characters is also relatively high. It does not look ideal, and the overall feeling is uncomfortable. There are also some noise points on the picture, and the picture performance is not ideal.

Youku.com Score 7.8

The video playback quality of Youku.com is still pretty good. Although the edges of the characters are still a bit blurry and the sharpness is not very high, there are fewer noise points, and the shaking phenomenon is not obvious. It is still tolerable when watching, and it is one of the better ones among the websites participating in the evaluation.

Tudou Score 7.0

The video playback quality of Tudou is relatively average, without any outstanding features. Compared to the quality of 6 Rooms, it is still better, but problems such as blurred edges of characters and overall fuzziness still exist. The overall performance is barely satisfactory.

Ku6.com Score 7.0

Since the upload to Ku6.com failed, the same frame cannot be used for comparison. However, the reporter still chose a frame to participate in this test. From this frame, it can be seen that the picture performance of Ku6 is average, and the edges of the characters are also relatively blurry. However, the control of noise points is relatively good, and the noise points are not very obvious, and the picture shaking is not strong. The overall picture can be accepted.

·Test Section 3:

Spread The main purpose of uploading videos is to let others appreciate them, and it is also the wish of many podcasters to let more people appreciate their videos. What is the level of video promotion on each website? The reporter conducted such a test. The reporter uploaded an original funny short film to five websites (Ku6 failed to upload and exited the test). The reporter named this video "Beauty Murder Planning Meeting" and labeled it with four keywords: beauty, murder, gunfight, and humor. The video introduction is: A beauty was forced to call her brothers to plan a murder. One day after uploading, the reporter checked the click rate of this video.

China Podcast Network Score 9 Clicks 3790

On China Podcast Network, the click rate of this video reached 3790 times, and it was placed in the recommended video of the website, displayed on the homepage for nearly a day. The click count obviously exceeded other websites.

Youku.com Score 6 Clicks 241

On Youku.com, the click count is not much. The key is that this video can only be found on your own page, and the website did not recommend it. Maybe because there are too many excellent videos on this website.

6 Rooms Score 5 Clicks 50

One day after uploading, the click count of this video on 6 Rooms was only 50 times, and it did not enter any recommended channels. This performance is not satisfactory, with fewer viewers and poor spread effect.

Tudou.com Score 7 Clicks 359

The click count of Tudou.com might be considered normal, with hundreds of clicks. Although it is not as high as China Podcast Network, the performance is still relatively satisfactory.

·Special Function Bonus

China Podcast Network Bonus: 0

China Podcast Network almost has no special additional functions, everything is performed in a regular manner.

Ku6.com Bonus: 3

Unique income system, every time a netizen clicks and finishes watching the file, a certain amount of income will be credited to the broadcaster's account, and when the account reaches a certain amount, you can apply to withdraw the income.

6 Rooms Bonus: 0

The special functions of 6 Rooms are not well done either, similar to China Podcast Network, there are no particularly obvious additional functions.

Youku.com Bonus 1.5

One of its important special functions is the PK Arena System. This system is very similar to the popular talent shows in recent years, allowing various podcasters to indulge in their own "Super Girl" or "Fast Man" experience online.

Tudou.com Bonus 2.0

Through Flying Potato, netizens can accelerate while browsing Tudou.com videos, easily configured and supporting resuming from breakpoints.
