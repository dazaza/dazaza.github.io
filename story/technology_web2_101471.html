RSS Mixer: Pool your FEEDs

by xjyzhenai on 2008-11-07 08:26:51

Feed-based services have become commonplace in the web2.0 era. In China, although the application of feed or RSS is relatively late, some of the more famous feed service providers in the industry, such as feedsky, catch shrimp, fresh fruit, etc., they have accelerated the popularity of RSS services in the country, so that more and more people use RSS and subscribe to RSS. But it would be a mistake to say that feeds are only for subscriptions. Because there are already more services based on feeds

Like RSS Mixer. What is RSS Mixer? RSS Mixer allows visitors to efficiently aggregate multiple files into a single file. The aggregated files are used as Web pages, mobile format pages, or Apple or Web applets.

What's the use? For example, if you have multiple blogs or multiple RSS feeds, you can combine them into one FEED. When aggregating, you only need to enter the URL of the page containing the FEED, and the RSS Mixer will automatically find the address of the FEED contained in the page. Now, here we go!
