I whispered to Xiao Yu: Isn't such a big private room unnecessary for just your family?

The hall manager quickly picked up the walkie-talkie and instructed other service personnel to prepare for us.

Xiao Yu's father sat on the sofa and gestured for me to sit down.

Xiao Yu's father said to me: Xiao Yu said you usually take care of her. There's no other meaning today, just want to meet you together with Xiao Yu's mother and have a meal.

I, on the other hand, felt rather nervous, which Xiao Yu didn't notice.

Xiao Yu ran to the incoming matron with open arms and coaxed: Mom, where have you been? Why are you just coming now?

Fortunately, Xiao Yu's father helped me out: Stop talking so much, hurry and sit down, call the waiter to bring the dishes.

Xiao Yu actively sat next to me, very close to me. She kept helping me with the food.

Xiao Yu's father was also taking care of Xiao Yu's mother beside him.

During the meal, at Xiao Yu's father's request, I accompanied him to drink two cups of wine. I also appropriately toasted Xiao Yu's mother.

After accompanying Xiao Yu for a casual stroll on the street, I checked my watch and it was about time, so I sent Xiao Yu home.

Walking back home, I remembered Xiao Yu's fierce glare and those words, feeling a great amount of pressure.

Because of my impulsive actions, a passive situation was created. I really don't know how I, Xiao Yu, and Xiao Nan will get along in the future.
