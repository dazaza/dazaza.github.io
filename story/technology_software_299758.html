"The Deathly Hallows" Releases First Trailer

Report by Su Lei: The highly anticipated "Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows (Part 1)" has recently released its first trailer. In the clip, Harry Potter, Ron, and Hermione come to the Muggle (human) world to escape Voldemort's pursuit. The trailer showcases an extensive range of scenes and new characters with limited footage. Producers David Heyman and director David Yates both expressed their anticipation for this work. Yates also revealed that Harry would appear noticeably older in the new film, with a beard and even a full-nude scene.

The 56-second trailer reveals some plot details. "Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows" (shortened as "HP7") is the final chapter of the series, divided into two parts. In the trailer, Harry and his friends leave Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry and mix into the Muggle world, weaving through crowds. The film's colors are noticeably brighter, with Hermione in a striking red dress. However, as the trio enters the forest and begins running, the atmosphere quickly becomes heavy.

Director David Yates mentioned in an interview that "HP7 (Part 1)" is more realistic than any previous film in the series and features the least magic. In the movie, Harry, Ron, and Hermione cannot return to Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry. Faced with the search and pursuit of the Death Eaters and snatchers, they struggle to survive in the Muggle world without the protection of adult wizards, making it more akin to a "road movie."

Harry has kissing and nude scenes in the film. Additionally, Yates revealed that one of the film's selling points is that all three characters have entered adulthood. Harry and Ron have grown beards, and audiences will see a naked Harry. Furthermore, popular lead actors Daniel Radcliffe and Emma Watson will share a kissing scene. "A Horcrux (containing a fragment of Voldemort's soul) protects itself by creating a hallucination for Ron, making him see Hermione and Harry kissing. During this scene, Daniel Radcliffe, who plays Harry Potter, will be fully naked."

Since "Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows" is the seventh part of the "Harry Potter" series, to be more faithful to the original work and extend its money-making period, Warner split it into two parts: "Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows Part 1" will be released on November 19, 2010, and "Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows Part 2" on July 15, 2011.
