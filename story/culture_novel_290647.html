Cigarette (Eric) is a high-ranking official's son from Beijing, studying abroad in the UK. Due to his handsome appearance and rebellious personality, he has quite a bit of appeal to the opposite sex. Cigarette has a long-term girlfriend named Ding Xue back in Beijing. However, during his time studying abroad, Cigarette has had no shortage of girlfriends. By chance, Cigarette meets another Beijing native and fellow international student, Green Tea (Suki). Cigarette and Green Tea get along very well, but because there is still a woman who deeply loves him back in Beijing—Ding Xue, whom he calls "wife," Cigarette cannot openly express his feelings to Green Tea.

Green Tea asks Cigarette for a gift, and Cigarette prepares to give her a cherished Zippo lighter that has been with him for many years. He engraves a line of letters on the lighter: "TMYRTWWF." However, Green Tea can't figure out what it means. During the Christmas holiday, both Cigarette and Green Tea return to Beijing. Ding Xue proposes a breakup to Cigarette because she has found new love. But Cigarette refuses to agree, and with his deep affection and actions, he manages to bring Ding Xue back into his arms. Green Tea suffers greatly without Cigarette, and in her numbness, she accepts the affections of a persistent suitor named Alex.

After returning to London, Cigarette meets an outgoing and generous Hong Kong exchange student named Jans. Jans looks extremely like Cigarette's first love, Qing. Back in the day, Qing and Cigarette were deeply in love, but unfortunately, Qing couldn't bear Cigarette's flirtatious and reckless nature. When Cigarette abandoned Qing and chose Ding Xue instead, Qing attempted suicide by cutting her wrists...
