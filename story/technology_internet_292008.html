Man publicy hurts himself with a knife to see his dream lover (picture)

by mingdaolin on 2009-12-04 12:27:23

The man who mutilated himself was taken away by the police for investigation after the incident, 2009 CET-4 and CET-6 exam answers. A picture showed him holding a knife to his neck as a threat.

Reported by Xin Kuai Daily journalist Fangjian Xu with both text and photos: "If you don't come to see me, I'll die right in front of you." At around 1 PM yesterday afternoon, near Jinhe Square in Zhangmutou Town, Dongguan City, a man stabbed himself twice in the abdomen, forcing his dream lover to meet him. After a standoff with the police that lasted over three hours, the police finally found his dream lover and successfully seized the knife while they were talking, saving him.

At around 1:30 PM yesterday, at the scene, a man about 1.7 meters tall wearing a yellow short-sleeve shirt and black pants, around 20 years old with long hair, held a fruit knife about 50 centimeters long, facing a residential building on Civilized Third Street in the town. The man's shirt had been stained red with blood. Staff from 120 (emergency medical services) and 119 (fire department) were on standby at the scene.

Neighbor Mr. Lin said that at around 1 PM, the man came to Civilized Third Street, shouting a girl's name towards Building 17, but received no response. Subsequently, the man shouted loudly, "If you don't come to see me, I'll die right in front of you." As soon as he finished speaking, Mr. Lin saw the man stab himself twice in the abdomen. After self-mutilation, the man became increasingly agitated, pressing the fruit knife against his throat, "continuously staring at the building opposite Building 17, without saying a word." Seeing this, the neighbors immediately called 110 (police).

The police tried multiple times to persuade him, all in vain

An eyewitness said that after the police arrived at the scene, they attempted to approach the man in the yellow shirt for rescue, but he threatened, "If you come over, I'll die right in front of you." The police had to retreat and continuously persuaded him not to be agitated, while arranging staff to search for the girl the man in the yellow shirt was shouting for. However, since the girl did not live there, she still hadn't appeared by 2 PM.

By 3 PM, the man in the yellow shirt had been in a standoff with the police. A plainclothes policeman approached and tried to persuade him to drop the knife, but was refused. The plainclothes policeman also offered him water for hydration, which was also rejected. On-site doctors analyzed that judging from the mental state of the man in the yellow shirt, his abdominal wounds might not be deep, and he was temporarily out of life danger.

"He is one-sided in love"

The involved woman helped the police save him, but revealed that they had never dated

At around 3:30 PM, a police car brought a woman in red to the scene. Accompanied by two policemen, the woman in red walked to about 30 meters away from the man in yellow. The man in yellow stared at her, eyes wide open. The woman in red urged him to drop the knife, but the man didn't respond.

After 1 hour and 20 minutes, while the man in yellow was talking with the woman in red and his attention was distracted, several rescuers quietly approached the left side of the man in yellow. When they were less than 2 meters away from him, a policeman suddenly leaped up, grabbing the hand holding the knife, while other rescuers quickly followed up, pinning him down. Subsequently, the man in yellow was taken away from the scene.

According to the woman in red, she and the man in yellow used to be coworkers, but they had never dated, "He is one-sided in love, and I never expected him to do this."
