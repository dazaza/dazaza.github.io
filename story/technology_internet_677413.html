【Working Principle】

Firstly, understand the working principle of the scale/truck scale: A truck scale/scale mainly consists of components such as load-bearing devices, weight display instruments, load cells (resistive strain types), connecting parts, limit devices, and junction boxes, as well as optional external equipment like printers, large screen displays, computers, and voltage stabilizers. When the object or loaded vehicle is placed on the load-bearing platform, under the force of gravity, the load-bearing device transfers the force to the load cell, causing deformation of the elastic body of the load cell. The strain gauge bridge circuit attached to the elastic body loses balance and outputs an electrical signal proportional to the weight value, which is amplified by a linear amplifier. Then, the signal is converted into a digital signal through A/D conversion, and the microprocessor (CPU) of the instrument processes the weight signal and directly displays the weight data. If configured with a printer, the weight data can be printed and recorded; if configured with a computer, the measurement data can be entered into the computer management system for comprehensive management. Whether it's a scale or a weighbridge, the basic principle is to convert the object's gravitational force into an electrical signal using sensors (force sensors), and send it to the conditioning, A/D, and computing circuits (MCU), then convert it into a digital display.
