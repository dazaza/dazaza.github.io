[Synopsis] A story about a family with four sons that no one would willingly join.

Character Introductions:

Song Jin-pung (Sun Hyun-jo): The eldest son of the pharmacy family, pharmacist.

Large face shape, a simple smile, dressed quite plainly, with bright eyes like a deer. He is a rather silent type who won't speak unless it's necessary. Although his mother considers him the manliest of men, many women find him uninteresting.

However, even such a person had a pure first love when he was young.

He secretly loved a woman since junior high school and continued to do so through high school and university. After entering university, he originally planned to confess his feelings to her, but upon seeing her chatting happily with another man in front of her house, he immediately decided to enlist in the army.

Lee Soo-jin (Park Seon-young): Sister of Bruiser, lawyer.

Her brother Bruiser wandered due to family instability and got angry easily, but Soo-jin focused on studying and entered a prestigious university. Later, she worked at a famous law firm in the U.S. Realizing the limitations of being an Easterner living in the West, she returned to Korea after being invited by Korea's top law firm.

She wanted to return to Korea and convinced her brother to come back as well.

In Su-jin's calm and rational view, Koreans tend to be overly passionate, but she finds this place relatively normal. However, she couldn’t adapt to the alleyway of Huwha-dong. These people were too impolite and immoral, infringing on others' freedoms as if it were a daily occurrence. Among these people, the worst offenders were the people from "Song Pharmacy."

Song Dae-pung – played by Lee Bim-mo

Second son of the Song family, pediatrician, 180 cm tall, thick eyebrows, humorous, especially courteous towards women, and graduated from the best medical department in Korea. Considering all these conditions, there can only be one answer: a playboy. Because he wants to fully utilize and enjoy these advantages, he is still busy dating various women. Nurse Bok-shil, who has been helping him in the hospital for three years, has been secretly in love with him. Unexpectedly, this dating expert has recently suffered consecutive failures. He started pursuing lawyer Lee Soo-jin who moved next door, but she ended up liking his older brother Jin-pung. Even the artist Woo Eun-jee living next door ended up fancying his ugly younger brother Seon-pung. What is happening in this world?

Kim Bok-shil – played by Ryu Seon

Nurse, as plain as her name suggests. In Dr. Song Dae-pung's clinic, there is only one doctor and one nurse, so she takes care of all the work, from registration to injections, cleaning, comforting crying children, and running errands when Dae-pung is hungry. This is the all-purpose nurse Kim Bok-shil.

Song Seon-pung – played by Han Sang-jin

Third son, social department reporter at a television station, knowledgeable about politics, economy, society, and cultural arts, hence earning the nickname 'walking internet'. Just not very good-looking, with a chubby face, garlic nose, thick lips, and glasses from the 70s. But the bureau chief who sees his potential wants to introduce his daughter to him. Unexpectedly, he refused the beautiful actress Woo Eun-jee because his heart already belonged to someone else - Kim Bok-shil. He liked her ever since she came to their house. In the end, will this stubborn ugly man marry anyone? Will it be Bok-shil, his brother's lover, or the beautiful star Woo Eun-jee?

Woo Eun-jee – played by Yoo Na

Artist, lost her parents in a car accident when she was young and was raised by her childless uncle. The pretty and cute her might be due to losing her parents at a young age, knowing how to make others like her, so everyone liked her when she was little. After growing up and becoming an entertainer, now all of Korea likes her except one man. This man isn't good-looking either. She was reluctantly pushed by her father to go on a blind date, but unexpectedly, this guy named Seon-pung didn't show up. One day, Seon-pung gave her a big stuffed bear and apologized. En-jee found herself crying on the street while hugging the bear. Why did you buy me a bear? And thus, she fell in love with this man who resembles a clumsy bear.

Song Mi-pung – played by Ji Chang-wook

Youngest son, a repeater student. Objectively speaking, among the four sons, he is the most handsome. Perhaps because he is the youngest in the family, his character is feminine, gentle, kind, and lacks strength. His hobbies are also typically feminine, such as cooking, knitting, and cross-stitching. Suddenly one day, his best friend Yong-chul left his child with him before going off to serve in the military. Raising the child in place of his friend made Mi-pung feel happy, and just like his name implies, he matured under the beautiful breeze.

Choi Soo-hee – played by Kang Eun-bi

Yong-chul's girlfriend, became a mother at less than 20 years old and ran away out of fear. After reconsidering, she decided to return. Unfortunately, Yong-chul went to serve in the military, and the child was entrusted to a friend. She came looking for the child but was afraid to raise it alone, so she stayed with Mi-pung to raise the child. Seeing Mi-pung sincerely treating her child filled her heart with gratitude. Under Mi-pung’s introduction, she works at an embroidery shop while raising the child.
