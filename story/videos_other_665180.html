1. Men and Women in the Ultimate World

Novel: Ultimate World | Author: Cha Ge De Ming Zi | Category: Urban Romance

The saying "men should marry when they grow up, and women should marry when they get older" was based on the age of men and women in the past. The latest update of the genius doctor. And now in this ultimate world, the main reason for marriage between men and women may be due to the constellation issue. Today, we will take a look at the easily obtainable ultimate constellation men from the perspective of women.

Leo opens his mouth, and says there is something. Leo is the king of the twelve constellations, and they seem to always carry a sense of hero pride and royal cohesion. They are righteous and would go through fire and water for their friends. In Leo's values and moral views, there seems to never be such things as good and evil. Their standard is, "friends are always right." Moreover, they are tolerant towards their friends, and it is not surprising that they can become the first choice of women. They can treat their friends like this, and they are even better to their lovers. Besides, Leo men can be said to be one of the most generous constellations in terms of money. As long as there is a Leo present, they absolutely do not allow their lovers and friends to spend money. They want to take care of everyone around them, especially their lovers. In addition, Leo's fifth house (love palace) falls into Sagittarius (the infinitely passionate constellation), which means that even if Leo men don't like a woman very much, they won't reject her too much. They like to be surrounded by various opposite sexes to show their infinite charm, so it can be said that it is easy to get such a premium man. But at the same time, they may also not refuse other opposite sexes.

Complicated Pisces, born with romantic and sentimental personalities. If you are a woman who pursues sentimentality in love and likes romance, then Pisces men will definitely not disappoint you. Their dramatic expressions and the surprises they often bring to your love make you feel as if you were in a Chiung Yao novel. He will tell you what true love is, and they will give the best standard of dating, which is the yearning of countless women. Actually, it is not difficult for women to win the heart of Pisces men. Pisces is an extremely sentimental constellation. They have no discernment for emotions. As long as a woman is good to them, they may easily fall in love with her. Once they fall in love, their tolerance and dedication to this person are as vast and profound as the sea. However, Pisces' sentimentality is their greatest advantage and also their greatest flaw. They can easily like you and dedicate everything to you, but at the same time, they may easily like countless others. To lock the heart of a Pisces man, you need to put some thought into it.

Very unstable Capricorn, because of their innate inferiority complex, the desire to gain recognition from others and to stand out is exceptionally strong. If it's a young Capricorn man, they may not want to have a long-term and serious relationship, because Capricorn men plan their careers very high. When they feel they haven't established themselves in society, they have no qualifications to fall in love. At this time, even if Capricorn men are in love, they will think clearly, it's just a game. But after middle age, Capricorns often already have a high social status and career height. At this time, they finally want to slow down and enjoy their lives, indulging in the love they have set aside for a long time. In fact, it's also very easy to touch the heart of Capricorn men. As long as you have a decent appearance and persistence, you can definitely move them. Because Capricorn men are not a constellation that pays too much attention to emotions. Even if they have someone they like, they often won't be too active, so at this time, if they want to fall in love and you are more active, success isn't that difficult.

Beautiful Libra, with delicate features that make women envious, and a graceful demeanor that exudes elegance. Libra values their external image, values their beauty and harmony in the eyes of others, even if they are men. Libra men, in the eyes of women, are so visually appealing, and the key point is, if you show them kindness, they often don't know how to refuse. Even if they don't want to, they will only stutter and half-heartedly resist, so it's easy to get a Libra man. As long as you're direct, take the initiative, and get straight to the point, they may be very troubled, but if you take the attitude of "it has to be done," they have no way out. Moreover, once you get a Libra man, they won't initiate a breakup with you in love. In love, it's always you who breaks up with them; they absolutely cannot say rude and heartless words. But the problem is, although it's easy to get a Libra person, it's not that easy to get a Libra's heart. Pale skin subtly reveals a rosy glow.

Aries is the big man among the twelve constellations. Their brave personality often brings them success in their careers, and they usually have good gains in the pursuit of wealth, so Aries men can also be considered successful men. In fact, it's very easy to deal with Aries men, just tease and reject them. As long as you can tease Aries men to be interested in you, or even like you, but you don't cater to him, and often create some freshness, then Aries, who have a spirit of pursuit, will keep trying to pursue you, because the biggest feature of Aries people is that the more they can't get, the more they want to get.

In fact, I think Scorpio should be the first among the top-tier ones. Although their character is solitary, they are very loyal to girls. Although their self-male chauvinism is quite large, they still listen to their wife's words. So, little girls who have Scorpio men, don't miss the opportunity, capture the top-tier Scorpio man, and be happy in the top-tier world of love.
