The 5th China (Guangzhou) International Machinery and Equipment Manufacturing Expo

by seosh on 2008-01-21 09:30:32

Original source: Marking Machine Network http://www.technifor.cn/

Approval Authority: Ministry of Science and Technology of the People's Republic of China

Organizer: Guangzhou Municipal People’s Government, China Machinery Industry Federation

Organization Unit: Guangzhou Economic and Trade Commission

Undertaking Unit: Guangdong Convention and Exhibition Promotion Co., Ltd. (a member of the International Association of Exhibition Managers IAEM)

GIMEE’2008 is an important event in the field of machine tools, mechanical equipment, and related supporting sectors in China and Asia. It has successfully been held four times, with a total exhibition area of 150,000 square meters and over 2,500 domestic and foreign exhibitors. This year's exhibition covers an area of 40,000 square meters, with 2,000 pre-set booths. More than 600 domestic and foreign machine tool, mechanical equipment, and related supporting enterprises will jointly present new highlights in the industry. With the strong support and participation of numerous domestic and foreign authoritative institutions and top professional buyers, this will stimulate business contacts between exhibitors and international markets. The exhibition aims to achieve a comprehensive breakthrough in group exhibitions both domestically and internationally, as well as in the organization of domestic and foreign professional buyers, enhancing the influence of the exhibition and creating opportunities for cooperation among exhibitors.

- Highlighting the strong competitiveness and brand value of enterprises, uncovering unlimited potential business opportunities. China is a major driving force behind global economic development. In 2007, China's GDP is expected to rank third in the world. China has been the largest consumer of equipment in the world for five consecutive years. This exhibition will create a platform for domestic and foreign equipment-related enterprises to expand their markets, showcase their brand value, and maintain strong competitiveness.

- Advancing into China's most dynamic market, opening the golden key to South China business opportunities. South China is China's largest manufacturing base, and Guangdong is China's first manufacturing province. The industrial upgrading of the Pearl River Delta, a world-class manufacturing base, is accelerating, and heavy chemical industries are rising strongly. There is a pressing demand for large quantities of machine tools, metal processing, molds, and other mechanical equipment and supporting products. Guangzhou is the strongest industrial base in Southeast Asia and the primary front for domestic and foreign manufacturers to develop the South China market. Guangzhou's automobile, shipbuilding, petrochemicals, mechanical equipment, steel, and other heavy chemical industries are developing rapidly. It is estimated that by 2010, the output value of the mechanical equipment industry will reach 550 billion yuan, showing enormous market potential!

- Mobilizing superior resources, three-dimensional publicity expands your return on investment in participating in the exhibition. The exhibition will use integrated marketing methods to actively coordinate social resources (manufacturers, traders, dealers, media, experts, social celebrities, industry organizations, government agencies, overseas partners) and the organizer's resources (human resources, financial resources, and many years of professional organization and planning experience). Using a three-dimensional publicity strategy, it will focus on attracting merchants, buyers, and media attention, enhancing the influence of the exhibition and participation effects.

- High-quality audiences gathered, close contact with quality buyers. The organizing committee will promote the exhibition through various channels such as magazines, newspapers, the Internet, television, SMS broadcasting, advertising direct mail, email, exhibition news, and telephone communication. They will invite professionals and buyers from various industries and fields such as machinery, automobiles, steel, shipbuilding, military industry, metal processing, home appliances, electronics, IT, metallurgy, petrochemicals, toy manufacturing, etc., to visit and purchase at the exhibition. It is estimated that more than 40,000 visitors from all over the world will attend.

- Major brands exhibited in previous editions: General Electric, Siemens, ABB, Hitachi, Mitsubishi, Emerson, Rockwell, Toshiba, Swiss Mikron, Itochu, Emag, Hardinge, Haas, Charmilles, Sodick, RSF, Dazhan Laser, Zhen De, Zhen Jian, Yifu, NOK, GMG, SEW, OTC, THK, Sumitomo, Esaar, Datong, Qiqihar No.2 Machine, Kia, Hongli, Guangshu, Guangji, Nuo Xin, Yellow River Precision Machine, Phoenix Contact, Ingersoll Rand, Bao Yuan, Bao Li, Lisheng, Dow, Yi Li, Wafang Heavy Machinery, Ming Pan, Min Jia... Over 2,000 exhibitors in total.

- Exhibits Scope:

◆ Machine Tools and Metalworking Equipment:

Machining centers, milling machines, boring machines, broaching machines, drawing machines, planers, electric discharge machining machines, wire cutting machines, flame cutting equipment, coordinate milling grinders, various types of grinders, CNC lathes, engraving machines, mold closing machines, drilling machines, sawing machines, shearing machines, spinning machines, bending machines, CNC machines, stamping machines, forging machines, hydraulic presses, presses, vacuum forming machines, rapid prototyping systems, etc.

◆ Processing Manufacturing and Auxiliary Equipment:

Mold CAD/CAM/CAE technology, software, hardware, automatic programming devices and flexible processing systems, CNC digital display systems, cold/hot working processing equipment, polishing machines, industrial marking machines, positioning and locking devices, various measuring instruments (gauges), three-coordinate measuring machines, ultrasonic cleaning machines, motors, bearings, cylinders, die sets, templates, hot runner components, punches, springs, grinding wheels, etc.

◆ Molds/Tools:

Various molds for stamping, plastics, die-casting, forging, ceramics, drawing, hard alloys, rubber, etc., mold standard parts, mold materials, mold forming equipment and devices, mold repair equipment and materials, mold parts and components, manual, pneumatic, and electric tools, fixtures, cutting tools, grinding and polishing equipment, etc.

◆ Mechanical Components:

Bearings: Bearings and their components, bearing production and processing equipment, related equipment and accessories, linear motion systems, etc.;

Transmission: Gears, chains, reducers, bearings, transmission shafts, couplings, clutches brakes, belt chain transmission servo motors, etc.;

Fluid Power & Sealing: Hydraulic components, accessories, systems, technologies, devices, pneumatic three-piece sets, pneumatic components, accessories, pipes, valves, cylinders, etc., various sealing technologies, materials, products, etc.;

Air Compressors: Air compressors, dryers, filters, and air compressor accessories;

Fasteners: Fastener production equipment, fasteners, accessories, and connection products, spring equipment, springs, and raw materials for fastener production.

Exhibition Fees:

◆ Domestic Exhibitors:

Area A: Standard Booth: RMB 9,000 per booth; Naked Space: RMB 930 per square meter.

Area B: Standard Booth: RMB 8,500 per booth; Naked Space: RMB 880 per square meter.

◆ International Exhibitors: USD 2,000 per booth.

Note 1: Standard booths are 3m x 3m and include white panels, one table, two chairs, two lights, bilingual header boards, carpet covering the booth floor. Sockets need to be rented separately.

Note 2: Naked space within the hall starts at 36 square meters. Non-standard booths do not come with any facilities and must be designed or decorated by the exhibitor themselves or by commission.

Note 3: Free services provided by the exhibition include: a 300-word company profile in the conference publication, promotional reporting of the exhibition and targeted audience organization, exhibitor badges, standard booth setup (excluding naked space), exhibition site cleaning and security, providing two copies of the conference publication, and providing exhibition reports and selected audience lists.

Note 4: Related paid conference publications and outdoor advertisements can be consulted with the organizing committee or refer to the "Exhibitor Manual".
