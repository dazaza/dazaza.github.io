Sweet Chocolate Final Episode Plot Introduction (Episodes 1-22)

The Thai drama "Sweet Chocolate" stars the popular actors Aom and Mos. Aom became famous in China for her role as Nara in "The Escaping Princess," while Mos is a popular singer in Thailand who has released 8 solo albums and 10 special albums since his debut. Together, they portray a romantic love story between a beautiful waitress who meets a wealthy young man from another country. With its unique high-quality production team and concise storyline that caters to Chinese audiences' tastes (unlike some Thai dramas with overly simplistic themes), this series appeals to viewers of all age groups.

Sweet Chocolate Episode-by-Episode Plot Introduction - Episode 1

Achuan and Sasha were both born into wealthy families and grew up together as childhood sweethearts. Achuan's father is renowned in the hotel management industry and hopes his son will follow in his footsteps, but Achuan has no interest in it. The virtuous Wan and the straightforward Dan are close friends who are passionate about hotel management work and aspire to achieve success in their careers. Wan gives up the opportunity to study abroad to meet her boyfriend Man's requirements, preparing to be a devoted wife after marriage. Dan feels very sorry for her. Man repeatedly cheats on Wan with other women behind her back. Although she is extremely heartbroken, she endures it, hoping that Man will change for the better.

Sweet Chocolate Episode-by-Episode Plot Introduction - Episode 2

Misunderstandings often occur between Achuan and Sasha, leading to constant arguments. The relationship between Achuan and his father is rather strained. Under his father's pressure, he is forced to go abroad for further studies. Before leaving, he invites Sasha for dinner and hides an engagement ring in a chocolate cake. Unexpectedly, Sasha unknowingly swallows the ring, leading to her being rushed to the hospital. Man causes trouble again, and Wan, unable to bear it any longer, decides to leave Man and go to Switzerland for further studies with Dan.
