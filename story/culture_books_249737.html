"Exposure" reveals the myriad aspects behind the bureaucracy.

by jqrxass on 2009-10-20 14:02:13

The "Lamb Kebab" traffic accident has shocked the entire city. The sudden death of a female radio host, the abrupt jump from a building by the secretary, and the suicide of a discipline inspection commission officer... Whose hands are pulling the strings behind these series of events?

A beautiful and elegant city, yet it has become the source of many scandals, thus begins our story. A series of bizarre cases hide many human desires. Under the layers of conspiracy, humanity is deteriorating. In the life of power, greed, and lust in the officialdom, relationships between people have turned into transactions.

"Exposure" Chapter 1

When Qin Tai entered the bedroom, he didn't find Yang Huan's figure. Unable to help himself, Qin Tai called out: "Little Yang, Little Yang, where are you? Are you in the kitchen? In the bathroom? In the toilet?" As Qin Tai shouted, he searched room by room. For some unknown reason, he suddenly had an ominous premonition, as if something unexpected was about to happen again. Finally, Qin Tai found Yang Huan in the restroom. To his surprise, the Yang Huan that Qin Tai saw wasn't alive but dead. Seeing this, Qin Tai immediately became dizzy, lost his balance, and fainted.

"Exposure" Chapter 2

The mastermind behind this tragedy is Jiangshan City's Hexi Elementary School, a key elementary school. Most students at key schools come from wealthy and powerful families, and since China implemented the family planning policy, many children have become their parents' only child. Therefore, such tragedies evoke even more sympathy.

"Exposure" Chapter 3

Yang Huan listened happily to Secretary Qin's promises. This seemed more enchanting than sweet nothings. Yang Huan understood that getting close to Secretary Qin would bring about a complete transformation in her life. Her younger brother would also soon rise to prominence in Jiangshan.

"Exposure" Chapter 4

Due to the裸体盛 affair, Liu Wenwu and Qin Tai's relationship became much closer. The relationship between people, whether opposite sexes or same-sex, is merely a layer of paper. Once this layer is broken, both parties will talk freely about everything.

"Exposure" Chapter 5

In his vast office, Qin Tai faced the pain of Yang Huan's murder, reminiscing about every rendezvous they had over the years, each one breaking his heart. From meeting Yang Huan during his inspection of the television station to experiencing the pleasure of their secret affair in his home. From helping her younger brother Yang Xi collaborate with Singaporean businessman Liu Wenwu to create the first entertainment center in Jiangshan, to weathering storms in the entertainment industry competition. Now, Yang Huan has left him. Who killed her? Who did she offend? Is it related to the Tianle Entertainment Center?

"Exposure" Chapter 6

Qin Tai thought and thought, believing that to uncover the mystery of Shi Dake's suicide, he must break through using Ding Xiang. Even without any warning signs, Ding Xiang should know what Shi Dake thought and did before his death. Qin Tai firmly believed in one saying: no matter how smart a man is, once he's in a woman's arms, his IQ drops to around 20%. Although Shi Dake was Qin Tai's confidant, Qin Tai believed there were things Shi Dake wouldn't tell him but would tell Ding Xiang.

"Exposure" Chapter 7

Mayor Yu Fan of Jiangshan City never expected that Discipline Inspection Commission staff member Man Xiaoyuan would fall in love with him. How awkward! As the mayor, Yu Fan always admired Man Xiaoyuan. She was upright, cute, and bold. Yu Fan always regarded her as a junior and cultivated her as a young cadre. However, this clever girl, how did she know he was divorced? Where did she get this information? Yu Fan remained puzzled.

"Exposure" Chapter 8

The nourishment of "sister-brother love" made Ding Xiang youthful and radiant. And Xia Wen also experienced an incomparable kind of "happiness." They spent at least one night a week as husband and wife, had an outdoor activity weekly, and sang karaoke at the Tianle Entertainment Center every week, which could adjust their mental and physical states and release stress.

"Exposure" Chapter 9

As Qin Tai watched Man Xiaoyuan's graceful figure leave, melancholy crept back into his heart. Originally, he wanted to keep Man Xiaoyuan today, do her ideological work well, then take her back to his house. If she was willing to follow him, like Yang Huan, dedicating her youth to him, Qin Tai decided to treat her like Yang Huan, allowing her to enjoy wealth and luxury, letting her experience and understand the opulence and grandeur of the presidential suite at Longhu Mountain Villa. However, Qin Tai couldn’t achieve his wish. As the Party Secretary, he couldn’t act like a thug on the street, grinning and touching her frivolously, nor could he tear off his mask and reveal his greedy nature. He could only probe her thoughts with dignified language. Unfortunately, Man Xiaoyuan had no such intentions.

"Exposure" Chapter 10

Yan Feifei had long heard of Secretary Qin's philandering ways and knew that the murdered female hostess Yang Huan was Secretary Qin's lover. She understood the implications of Secretary Qin’s words. What he meant by wanting to meet them was actually asking for a service lady to accompany him. Among the 27 service ladies, all wanted to accompany Secretary Qin, considering it an opportunity. For a woman to succeed, the quickest way is to attach herself to a rich man or an official. Most women understand this principle, including Yan Feifei.

"Exposure" Chapter 11

Song Junyi's son Song Tianhe studied architecture and was very familiar with real estate development. He was currently in Beijing, touring the Great Wall with a young woman. This nearly fifty-year-old man was a playboy, unmarried but having several lovers. It was rumored that he was Song Junyi's illegitimate son, living on the mainland. He owned properties in Beijing, Shanghai, and Wuhan, generally residing in Shanghai. Now he was waiting in Beijing for news from his father Song Junyi. Once the deal for the seven streets and eight alleys of Jiangshan was finalized, he would immediately fly to Jiangshan.

"Exposure" Chapter 12

Women naturally possess maternal instincts, and Yan Feifei was no exception. Last night, when the Party Secretary and the Public Security Bureau Director visited Longhu Mountain Villa, she guessed that there must be important matters. Before their conversation, the moment she saw Secretary Qin, she collapsed drunk into his arms. She never imagined that Secretary Qin was a debonair politician. In his romantic and rough manner, he guided Yan Feifei into a realm of desire expansion, making her unable to resist the demand rising from her marrow. Thus, a scene of soul tremors and physical totems was permanently etched in her mind.
