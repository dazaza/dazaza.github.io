Bamboo parsnip and silver ear soup can remove summer heat and help sleep.

by sunkang147 on 2010-07-15 14:29:01

Efficacy: In summer, eating corms can have the effect of clearing heat and generating body fluid, transforming dampness and removing phlegm, as well as aiding digestion and eliminating bloating. Silver ear fungus can stimulate appetite, clear the intestines, promote sleep, strengthen the stomach, nourish yin and clear heat, moisten the lungs and generate saliva. Its rich dietary fiber can help gastrointestinal peristalsis, promote digestion, and reduce fat absorption. Long-term consumption of silver ear fungus rich in natural gelatin also has the effect of moisturizing the skin, removing chloasma and freckles on the face. Corms are warm in nature, while silver ear fungus is cool in nature. Adding lotus seeds and wolfberries with heart-nourishing and tranquilizing effects to stew into a soup, it can be consumed chilled in summer for the purpose of relieving summer heat and aiding sleep.

Ingredients: 5 dried silver ear fungus, 10 corms, 20 dried lotus seeds, 30 wolfberries, 20 grams of rock sugar, 1500 milliliters of water.

Method:

1. Soak the silver ear fungus, wolfberries, and peeled corms separately in bowls with cold water for about an hour. After the silver ear fungus becomes soft, cut off the yellow hard parts. Then cut it into small pieces, and dice the corms into 5mm grains. Wash the wolfberries and set aside.

2. Add water to the pot, put in dried lotus seeds, silver ear pieces, and corm grains, bring to a boil over high heat, then simmer for 40 minutes.

3. Add the wolfberries, continue to stew for 5 more minutes, add rock sugar, stir until dissolved. It tastes better when chilled in summer.
