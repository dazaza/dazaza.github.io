Bathroom video records the process of two people killing a brick factory owner in 100 seconds

by xue94fwsh on 2012-03-09 13:05:45

①The glass at the scene of the bathhouse was smashed. ②Two assailants with knives (within the red circle) chased into the bathhouse and hacked people. ③The younger son gently wiped away his mother's tears. ④The loyal big yellow dog stood guard by the door, but its master would never return.

On December 7, it rained continuously in the provincial capital. In front of a brick factory in the suburbs of Shuihu Town, Changfeng County, Gang (a pseudonym), aged 17, waded through muddy land and opened the door of the on-duty room. The big yellow dog that guarded the factory suddenly darted out and kept circling around his knees. Gang said that in the past, the big yellow dog was more "devoted" to his father Zheng Libo. Just two days ago, this "preference" continued.

At around 20:00 on December 5, Zheng Libo, returning from the brick factory, encountered an unexpected event not far from home: two men with machetes slashed him and then pursued him all the way to a bathhouse on Changhuai Road in the town, hacking him over a dozen times until they killed him. After committing the crime, the two assailants fled. This incident was recorded by the surveillance camera at the entrance of the bathhouse. During the reporter's investigation, it was found that the local residents seemed to have many taboos regarding this event.

Surveillance shows

In less than a hundred seconds, the man was hacked to death by two people.

A bathhouse named Xingwang (pseudonym) on Changhuai Road in Shuihu Town has been doing poor business since the heinous murder case a few days ago. A ticket seller at the bathhouse said that there are not many bathhouses on Changhuai Road, but after the news of the murder spread throughout the town, old customers who wanted to take a shower preferred to "make do" at home rather than come to the scene of the crime for "enjoyment."

On the evening of the 5th, the bathhouse emitted a shrill scream. The surveillance video of the bathhouse showed that at 20:23:9 on the 5th, a man suddenly appeared in the black-and-white footage. The man quickly ran towards the entrance of the bathhouse, followed closely by two men with knives. After entering, the man was hacked repeatedly by the two men. After being hacked more than ten times, the man fell to the ground. The two men with knives got up after committing the crime, pushed open the curtain door, and walked away quickly. Both men had short hair and were about 30 years old.

The reporter noticed that the stabbing lasted from 20:23:9 to around 20:24:40, and in less than a hundred seconds, a life ended under their knives. An informed person introduced that after the incident, the police arrived, and the injured was sent to the hospital, but before treatment, the person had already stopped breathing and had no heartbeat, making all rescue efforts futile. A doctor at the hospital said that the deceased man was middle-aged, with nearly 20 cuts and stabs all over his body, including several fatal wounds.

Reporter Investigation

Broken glass windows at the crime scene

An informed person confirmed that the deceased was named Zheng Libo, 42 years old, originally from Huainan. When investigating the scene of the crime, the reporter found that the glass window behind where Zheng Libo died was already in shambles. "That night when the murderer was hacking people, the machete accidentally broke the glass," said an informed person.

Zhao Feng (pseudonym), a cousin of Zheng Libo, claimed to have heard some things directly from Zheng about what happened between him and the stabbers before. Zhao Feng introduced that the two stabbers were locally known as "Eben" (pseudonym) and "Xiaowu" (pseudonym), a pair of brothers surnamed Yan.

"Xiaowu" had just been released from prison in recent years. "In January 2011, a friend of Zheng Libo had a conflict with 'Eben' and a few others at a music hall in the area, and Zheng intervened in the fight and clashed with this pair of brothers."

Many local residents also said they had heard of "Eben" and "Xiaowu." Residents mentioned that the two brothers had an older brother who was "quite influential," having earned a lot of money doing engineering projects in the area in recent years. "Eben and Xiaowu often fought with people, and they could be described as having connections both in the underworld and above."

The reporter noticed that when identifying the scene, some residents refused to let the reporter take photos, citing the reason: "The murderers dared to cause a fatal incident in public places, showing great audacity. We are afraid of being implicated."

Eyewitness Account

After the stabbing, the murderers left very arrogantly

At the time of the incident, Hua was on duty at the front desk of the bathhouse. "It was like a nightmare... I buried my head under the counter out of fear," Hua told the reporter at 11:00 AM on the 7th. At the scene of the crime, when Hua got up again, the victim was already lying on the ground, "Someone's dead, someone's dead..." The screams quickly spread from the bathhouse throughout the entire town.

Zhang Qiang (pseudonym) was one of the few eyewitnesses to this murder case. He could determine that it was done by "Eben" and "Xiaowu" based on the perpetrators' physique. "The victim got out of a minivan that evening, preparing to open the car door to get something, when a black sedan stopped. Two men quickly got out of the car and chased the man with machetes."

"After being stabbed once in the back, the victim turned around and saw the two men, and immediately started running, while the two attackers chased after him," Zhang Qiang said. The hacked man shouted, "Help! Someone's killing me!" and panicked into the Xingwang Bathhouse dozens of meters away, with the two men also rushing in with knives. "Then, cries of pain came from inside the room..." Zhang Qiang said, "About a minute later, the two killers each holding machetes about fifty to sixty centimeters long walked out of the bathhouse, and passersby were scared and scattered everywhere." What Zhang Qiang didn't expect was, "When the two left, they looked unhurried, and one of them arrogantly said, 'Taking a life, spending some money, still can get out!'"

Orphans and Widowed Mother

The wife is heartbroken and crying, and the two children have dropped out of school

At around 1 PM that day, the reporter went to the rented house where Zheng Libo lived with his wife and children. The residence was only dozens of meters away from the place where he was killed. The simple house had only one bedroom and one living room, with nothing but a few pieces of simple furniture, a worn-out floor, and bare walls. Kong Yifang, Zheng's wife, lay in bed, with her two sons Gang and Xiao Gang (pseudonyms), four years younger, hiding behind her. On the first day after her husband's death, Kong Yifang cried and asked someone to make a portrait of her husband. She said that early on the 5th, her husband and she finished working at the brick factory and then went out, and did not return all afternoon. Around 8 PM that evening, she suddenly received a call from her eldest son Gang: "Dad was hacked to death, and he was sent to the hospital." Kong Yifang fell down the stairs in shock upon hearing the bad news, and finally saw her bloodied husband at the hospital. On the 6th, this 44-year-old woman lay in bed "mute" all day.

After being silent for a whole day, the first words spoken by Kong Yifang were: "Bring the two sons back home, don't let them go to school anymore." She was worried that the murderers might "throw caution to the wind" and harm her children. Starting from the 7th, Gang, who had only half a year left until the college entrance exam, and Xiao Gang, who was about to enter junior high school, became children without books to read.

At 3 PM that day, Kong Yifang let Gang go out for some fresh air. Gang walked slowly, and unknowingly arrived at the gate of a brick factory in the suburban town. In front of a row of dilapidated houses and processing rooms, piles of hollow bricks were scattered in disorder. Two years ago, Gang's parents moved from Huainan to Shuihu Town, Changfeng County, borrowed seventy or eighty thousand yuan, and set up a hollow brick processing plant in the suburbs of the town. Gang waded through the muddy land and opened the door of the on-duty room. The big yellow dog guarding the factory darted out "chislu" and kept circling around his knees. Gang said that in the past, the big yellow dog was more "devoted" to his father Zheng Libo. Just two days ago, this "preference" continued. But now, things are different, and the big yellow dog will never be able to call back its master.

Currently, the local police have preliminarily identified the suspects involved in the crime and are fully investigating the case further to apprehend the suspects as soon as possible.
