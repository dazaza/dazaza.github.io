QQ Personal Diary: I just don't want you to be better than me!

Tags: QQ personal diary, QQ classic diary, personal diary, unconventional diary There is a very noble saying that roughly goes like this: if you love someone, you just want them to be happy and hope they are happy. As long as they are happy, you can endure any委屈, accept all conditions, give up pursuing them, stay far away from them, and watch them flirt with others in a corner where they cannot see you.

Nonsense!

I believe that if one person loves another, they can cry for them, laugh for them, defy their parents for them, betray their master's door for them, live for them, die for them. However, they definitely do not want them to leave themselves, and even more so, they do not want them to leave and be doing well, at least not better than themselves.

Du Shiniang and Li Jia also loved each other passionately. She rejected the pursuit of noblemen for him, and he wasted his studies and money for her. However, in the end, he refused to marry her, not only refusing to marry her but also selling her. Not only did he sell her, but he also planned to use the money from selling her to explain to his father, then study hard, become an official, and marry a dignified and quiet woman from a good family, living happily ever after. Anyway, all the love has turned into clouds and smoke. Li Jia left Du Shiniang, and he could only be better, not worse. But Du Shiniang was different. The reason she followed Li Jia was because she loved him and wanted to be a legitimate wife rather than a low-status concubine, completely changing her status and fate. Who knew that Li Jia eventually sold her as a concubine? With her identity as the top courtesan, many officials much higher than Sun Fu were available for her to choose from, where would it be Sun Fu's turn to buy her, or Li Jia's turn to sell her? Moreover, she secretly harbored rare treasures and had dreams of making the man she loved rich and famous. Falling to such a state, her heart must have been like dead ashes. According to the above noble saying, Du Shiniang should have tearfully blessed Mr. Li's bright future and happiness, giving all her jewels to Mr. Li to express her deep and unrepentant love, and then jumped into the river. But wasn't that like losing her mind?

Did Daiyu love Bao Yu? Yet she would not let go of him to someone with better family background and temperament than her, someone with better popularity and reputation than her, someone who would be more helpful to Bao Yu's career than her, even knowing she was short-lived, she still wouldn't. When burning her manuscripts, how much resentment and hatred she held! How smart was Daiyu? Knowing everything, understanding everything, she ended up like this anyway. Women in ancient times, raised under the teachings of the three obediences and four virtues, would not give up either, let alone today!

A friend who is quite successful in her career and very happy in her marriage traveled a long distance to attend a university reunion. Before leaving, she bought a lot of outfits, looking very satisfied, and said resentfully, "I just want to show him that I am doing better than him! He gave up on me back then for just a little benefit of staying in school and chose the principal's daughter. It was a mistake!" What does my friend lack? Nothing. And she is kind-hearted, absolutely a good person. Even after ten years, she still has a heart that desires to see his embarrassment. But she didn't succeed. That person was close by but did not appear. His condition was definitely not bad, yet he did not come, because she was doing better than him. In this regard, men and women are the same.

There is one situation where you can hope the other person is doing well: You are doing well, and the other person keeps disturbing you because they are at the end of their rope. So, you hope they do a little better, just a bit worse than yourself.
