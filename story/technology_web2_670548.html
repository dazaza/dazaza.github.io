A fool's story of playing Xunxian 2012-02-20

by fanli5uv7b639 on 2012-02-21 02:14:24

09:58:36 Internet user comment 11 | Source: Duowan Xunxian Forum | Author: Lifting, My Kiss For You | Enter the forum. The fool rarely went to the internet cafe and had never played online games. In the internet cafe, he saw many people playing Xunxian, so he joined them. The fool chose a sword rider because he thought it was cool. Thus began his journey in Xunxian. After entering the game, the fool didn't know how to use skills to fight monsters. He played for a long time in Xiaosang Village and finally rescued Fox Xiaomei. Fox Xiaomei said thank you as usual, but the fool still typed and said no need to thank me. The fool was very stupid and it took him several hours to enter Ninghai City. Along the way, many NPCs greeted him, and he was very happy. He was glad to meet so many new friends. A few days later, the fool slowly reached level 50, learned to go to the skill master to learn skills, and seriously helped everyone complete tasks. Every time he completed a task and the NPC gave him some equipment, he would be very happy. One day, he was about to enter the Tongtian Conspiracy instance dungeon when he suddenly saw a short girl shouting: Is there any big brother who can take me along? I'm only level 40... She shouted for a long time and no one paid attention to her. The fool thought she was pitiful and went over and said: I'll take you. So the fool took her to brush through the Tongtian Conspiracy instance dungeon. Since the fool was level 50, it was easy for him to fight these monsters. The fool told the girl to stand far away or she would get hurt. The girl said the fool was really considerate. The fool chuckled. The fool said, "Be careful, don't bump into walls, you're just a child." The girl said, "Then call me child. I like hearing you call me child." The child said she liked the glossy inner dan that dropped from the boss, and the fool said he would give them all to her. The child said the fool was really good, and the fool felt very happy inside. The next day, the fool didn't even eat breakfast and rushed to the internet cafe to log into the game because they had agreed to team up and level up the next day... The child hadn't arrived yet, and the fool was lost in thought in the game. At around noon, the child logged in and said sorry for being late and asked if the fool had eaten yet. They planned to level up in the afternoon... The fool said he had already eaten and was just waiting for her. Then the fool took the child to train levels. The child said Princess Changning was very beautiful. The fool smiled and said she wasn't as cute as the child. The child made a cute expression ^-^. On the street, the child saw another player wearing a set of fashion clothes and exclaimed that if she wore it, she would definitely look better than them. The fool blushed and didn't say anything in front of the screen. He really wanted to buy a set of fashion clothes for the child, but the fool was really poor and couldn't even afford the internet fees. Not to mention the expensive fashion clothes in the game, although he didn't know how much they cost. The fool decided he must buy a set for the child. That's what the fool thought. The fool felt he liked someone for the first time, and it was a happy thing to have her by his side... Thus, while fighting monsters, they chatted and the child told the fool a lot of knowledge about the game: Qi Tianlie is unforgettable, Wei Laoshi is a headache, Du Grandma doesn't need to be dealt with... The fool listened carefully, realizing how big the world of Xunxian was. The fool said, let's walk this road together in the future. Hmm, the child softly said. The child also told the fool that she had an account over level 80, and when your level is high enough, I'll take you to fight for equipment. The fool's eyes were wet, and it was the first time someone was so nice to him... The fool, who had never done anything, found a job. Someone asked the fool what he could do, and the fool shook his head. They looked at the fool and thought he was foolish and refused him, telling him to try somewhere else. The fool ran to many places. At a place delivering bottled mineral water, the fool earnestly begged the boss to hire him, saying he didn't need much money, just enough to pay for the internet fees. The uncle looked at the fool and thought he was sincere and pitiful, so he said, 300 yuan a month, 50 cents per delivery. Come to work tomorrow. The fool kept thanking the boss and was very happy to find his first job... The child was the fool's seventh QQ friend. The child sent a video request and said she wanted to see the handsome guy. The fool didn't dare to accept it because he was afraid the child would see his ugly face and ignore him, so he told the child there was no video in the internet cafe. The child said then forget it, come to my space when you have time. The fool clicked into the child's space and found it very beautiful. He told the child it was very pretty. The child said she spent a lot of time and effort making it herself, and the fool praised the child for being very thoughtful. By accident, the fool clicked into the child's album and saw her picture. The child with a ponytail was smiling brightly and was very beautiful. The fool thought she was an angel and secretly downloaded the picture to his phone. When sleeping at night, the fool thought about the child all the time, looking at the child's picture, and the fool experienced insomnia for the first time... By this time, the fool was level 65, and the child was level 60. The child said let's go to the capital city tower, there's no more experience here. The fool chuckled and said no problem, so they entered the tower. The fool's operation was very clumsy and he didn't know how to use skills, so he got kicked out. The child said you're so stupid, and the fool embarrassingly said sorry to the child. The child pouted and said she could solo clear it when she was level 65. The fool blushed and didn't say anything. He thought since he couldn't protect the child now, he decided to wait until he became stronger before finding her again. So the fool told the child to wait for him, and traded the only 2Z he had left to the child. She didn't say anything and turned around and left... The fool watched the child's delicate back disappear... The next day, he entered the capital city tower again... Because he didn't have money to learn skills, the fool always failed every time. He repeatedly charged into the capital city tower, starting from floor 15 to floor 17. The fool thought as long as he worked hard, there was nothing he couldn't do. Gradually... The fool still found floor 18 difficult. A few days later, when the fool reached level 70, he could easily pass through the capital city tower. Related articles reprinted: 1.
