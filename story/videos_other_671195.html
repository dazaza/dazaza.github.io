Many female friends are afraid of accidental pregnancy and want to know what are the symptoms of accidental pregnancy in one month? In this way, they can find out if they are pregnant in the first time, so as to make preparations in advance. What are the symptoms of accidental pregnancy in one month?

Generally speaking, the main symptoms of accidental pregnancy in one month include:

(1) Menstruation stops

Amenorrhea is the earliest and most important "signal" of early accidental pregnancy. Any married woman of childbearing age with regular menstrual cycle, if menstruation overdue exceeds 10 days, should consider the possibility of pregnancy. If amenorrhea exceeds 2 weeks, it is necessary to go to the hospital for cause detection.

(2) Increased frequency of urination

After 8 weeks of pregnancy, there may be an increase in the number of urinations due to the enlargement of the uterus pressing and stimulating the bladder. After 12 weeks of pregnancy, the uterus extends beyond the pelvis, the bladder is no longer compressed or stimulated, and the frequent urination symptoms subside on their own.

(3) Physical fatigue

Most women after 6 weeks of pregnancy may experience dizziness, weakness, drowsiness, increased saliva secretion, loss of appetite, nausea and vomiting. Vomiting mostly occurs in the morning or on an empty stomach.

(4) Changes in appetite

Some women begin to experience changes in appetite a few weeks (1-2 weeks) after their period. They no longer like the foods they usually enjoy. After eating something once, they don't want to eat it again. Some people almost don't want to eat or even feel nauseous, while others crave sour-tasting foods. Some pregnant women have a special preference for sour and cold foods. These phenomena are called early pregnancy reactions, which often disappear around 12 weeks of pregnancy. A few people have more severe early pregnancy reactions that last longer.

(5) Abnormal basal body temperature

Under normal circumstances, the basal body temperature of women of childbearing age is low from the onset of menstruation to the middle (2 weeks before the next menstruation), then high (about 0.4℃ higher than the previous temperature). If the high temperature persists for more than 21 days and menstruation does not come, it indicates early pregnancy. This is an important indicator of whether one is pregnant. If the temperature fluctuates significantly, the fetus may be at risk, often due to luteal function disorders, and timely treatment is required.

(6) Skin changes

During pregnancy, a pregnant woman's skin undergoes significant changes. In the early stages of pregnancy, some people experience noticeable pigment deposition due to hormonal reasons. Some people develop acne during the early stages of pregnancy, while others who previously had acne now find it gone, making their face clearer.

Female friends should be aware of the symptoms of accidental pregnancy in one month so they can prepare mentally and better protect their health.
