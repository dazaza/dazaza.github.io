Sleeping at the beginning of Hai hour and ensuring sleep quality

Sleep is the first great supplement for life. If cancer patients want to maintain health and longevity, they must follow natural laws by achieving "I wake when nature wakes, I sleep when nature sleeps." You should fall asleep at the beginning of Hai hour (Hai time 21-23 PM), so that you can enter a deep sleep state at 23 PM, allowing your blood vessels to rest and recuperate. This helps improve the body's immunity and aids in recovery. During Hai time (21-23 PM), the triple burner meridian is most active, and the triple burner connects all the blood vessels. From 21 PM to 03 AM is the best time to nurture the liver and gallbladder. The liver stores blood, and when lying down, blood returns to the liver. When blood returns to the liver, it can eliminate waste and generate new substances, with the flow of qi and blood nourishing all the blood vessels; and after all the blood vessels are nourished, there will be enough energy to generate sufficient new cells to replace aging cells, which leads to health and longevity. Centenarians mostly sleep at the beginning of Hai hour.

If you go beyond Zi hour (23 PM), even up to midnight (24 PM), the new cells in your body will find it difficult to regenerate, making it impossible to replace already deceased cells. Over time, the body will appear deficient. If you don't go to bed early at night, relying on sleeping late the next day as a supplement, it often proves ineffective. Because from 05 AM to 07 AM is Mao hour, during which the large intestine meridian is active, and the human body has already entered the excretion period, the division of new cells has weakened. Traditional Chinese medicine finds little effect on those seeking medical help who do not sleep during Hai hour and sleep too late at night. Therefore, if you want to recover, you need to go to bed early. Maintaining an early bedtime ensures that you wake up refreshed and energized in the morning, feeling lively throughout the day. As long as we replenish our qi and blood and ensure sleep quality, we will recover.

There is another important point: I first drink a glass of water, waiting until the water reaches the dantian before going to the bathroom. Doing this lifts the qi and prevents the loss of primordial qi. Many elderly people experience sudden cardiovascular diseases, often when they go to the bathroom in the morning. As people age, their yang qi becomes deficient, and upon excretion, the primordial qi becomes even weaker. We, as cancer patients, also suffer from qi deficiency, which makes it difficult to keep the meridians open, leading to illness, further exacerbated by high-dose radiotherapy and chemotherapy, resulting in greater deficiency. Due to work reasons, I rarely went to bed before midnight in the past. Long-term violation of natural laws has led to severe insufficiency of my liver qi, causing frequent cramps. This is one of the reasons for my illness, even liver metastasis. Now I have achieved falling asleep at the beginning of Hai hour, and my sleep quality is very high. Appropriate exercise, balancing work and rest

We cancer patients should exercise appropriately and moderately. But note, the timing for exercise varies. Achieve changes with the sky and earth. In spring and summer, nurture yang qi by getting up early and doing outdoor activities; in autumn and winter, nurture yin qi by going to bed early in autumn and waking up early, while in winter, going to bed early and waking up late. In winter, you must wait until the sun comes out before going out to exercise. As the saying goes, "hibernating," meaning preserving yang qi and preventing its external leakage. If you don't feel like going out to exercise, don't force yourself.
