Unveiling the Secrets of Chen Hui Guang Bao's Success

by yyadorg2012 on 2012-02-19 18:48:23

Departing from Hangzhou Xiaoshan Airport and passing through the highway, it takes less than an hour to reach Shangyu City, Lamps Network. Here, the scenery is beautiful, and the economy is developed. In the development zone shaded by green trees stands a modern professional lighting company. Seventeen years ago, this village-run small factory with only about 20 people has weathered many storms and developed into today's optoelectronic high-tech enterprise with three R&D and manufacturing bases, covering an area of 105,000 square meters, and a factory area of 125,000 square meters. It adheres to the excellent tradition of scientific and technological innovation and green healthy lighting, relying on five major categories: light source, special light source, engineering lighting, home lighting, and LED lighting, showing its prowess in market competition, becoming one of the largest production bases in China's lighting industry with the greatest production capacity and the most complete range of varieties. It is Zhejiang Chenhui Group Co., Ltd. This article will take you into Chenhui Guangbao under the Chenhui Group, attempting to use the six core words summarized after the journalist's in-depth interview to uncover the secrets behind its success and future changes.

Core Word: Product - Leading the Market with Differentiation

After leaving the president's office of Chenhui Guangbao, Manager He from the planning department chuckled and asked: "Did you feel that the air was particularly good after spending an hour or two in Mr. Gan's office?" After being reminded, the reporter recalled that the air in the office was pure and fresh, making people feel comfortable and at ease.

"All the products used in Mr. Gan's office are Chenhui Air Purification Bao. The Air Purification Bao can adsorb, decompose, or convert various air pollutants such as formaldehyde, benzene, dust, peculiar smells, and other decoration pollution, bacteria, and allergens, effectively improving air cleanliness... Three Chenhui Air Purification Baos are equivalent to one young white poplar tree." Manager He continued to explain.

In the lighting field, Chenhui Guangbao has always been leading the market with a market demand-oriented approach and a differentiated route. For example, in the design of special light source products, the company designs and develops products with different细分 functions according to the different needs of people and application scenarios, such as energy-saving treasure, air purification treasure, plant growth series fluorescent lamps, vision treasure, supermarket treasure, performance treasure, sterilization and disinfection professional lamps series, and mosquito-repellent treasure lamps, attracting many customers who come to customize products with their special requirements.

Currently, whether globally or in the Chinese market, from technology to application, the current lighting industry is in a revolutionary update and replacement period in terms of green energy saving. Incandescent lamps are about to be eliminated, energy-saving lamps are at their peak, and LEDs are just beginning. Chenhui Guangbao's LED series products have taken the lead in obtaining national energy-saving product certification and are now leading the development process of China's LED lighting in a planned manner.

Core Word: Technology - Maintaining a Strong Position through Innovation

To maintain its strong position in the market, Chenhui Guangbao has paid great attention to technological innovation and research investment since its establishment. Over the past decade, Chenhui Guangbao has been leading in the fields of optics and light science research, and has closely cooperated with and exchanged technologies with more than ten universities and research institutions at home and abroad, including Fudan University. The market research capability is one of the core competitive advantages of Chenhui Guangbao. Since its establishment, the company has cumulatively invested over 30 million yuan in R&D funds. Currently, the company has built a "National Laboratory" for light sources, lamps, and electronic testing, and a "Special Light Source Research Center," equipped with China's top visible light optoelectronic colorimetry measurement and analysis system and internationally advanced high-precision UV light analysis and measurement systems. Nowadays, Chenhui Company owns nearly 200 patents of various kinds, has won the "National Energy Saving Contribution Award" twice consecutively, is the only brand enterprise in China's lighting industry to win this honor consecutively, and its products are listed in the National Torch Program Project and the "National Key New Product" Project. It is currently one of the enterprises in the lighting industry that has received the most national honors. Its energy-saving products have successfully been selected for the "National Energy-Saving Product Government Procurement List". Under the concept of scientific and technological innovation, Chenhui Guangbao has pioneered in winning multiple honors such as "Patent Demonstration Enterprise" and "High-Tech Enterprise" within the industry, becoming a participant, formulator, and promoter of relevant new standards in China's lighting industry standard.

Core Word: Culture - Excellent Corporate Culture is Fundamental

A country without cultural heritage loses its soul; a company without cultural heritage cannot have cohesion and will also lose long-lasting competitiveness. In modern economics, when consumers choose a company's product, to some extent, they are also choosing the culture of that company, indicating their recognition of a certain culture. Only good corporate culture can win customers and thus win competitions.

Chenhui Guangbao centers its core values on "righteousness", which leads to the corporate mission of "changing life with light, letting light change the world", and further evolves into the corporate vision of "innovators and leaders in the lighting field", aligning all employees clearly and precisely understanding the positioning, goals, and tasks of the company. Corporate culture is the quality habit and service habit shared by all personnel of Chenhui for one day, five days, one year, five years, twenty years, fifty years, even one hundred years, ten thousand years, consistently and perpetually. It is also the sum of Chenhui's unwavering commitment to superior quality and services. It permeates every aspect of the company’s production and life, serving as the soul of the company. Research and development can be imitated, manufacturing can be imitated, marketing can be imitated, but corporate culture as Chenhui's core competitiveness is irreplaceable. Therefore, corporate culture is the foundation of Chenhui's establishment and the strongest competitiveness in market competition.

Core Word: Quality - Strictly Grasping Quality Standards

Currently, Chenhui Guangbao has a complete product chain in the lighting field, including special light sources, ordinary light sources, LED lighting, home lighting, commercial lighting, electronics, electrical appliances, etc., with over ten major series and over a thousand varieties and specifications. In terms of innovation, variety, and sales of lighting products, it holds a leading position in the domestic market. In the production and manufacturing process, Chenhui Guangbao always places product quality in the most prominent position.

Currently, Chenhui Guangbao has established almost stringent standard requirements in each link of R&D, procurement, production, quality control, and service. During production, the best raw and auxiliary materials are used, and a large number of advanced production equipment and testing facilities are applied, such as the domestically advanced fully automatic energy-saving lamp production line and LED automatic production line, round arrangement machine, computer-controlled wave soldering machine, automated insertion, placement machines, PMS-3, PMS-50, SPR-920D, SPR-920E optoelectronic color comprehensive testing system, etc. Intelligent and computer program control technology is used for the entire production flow control.

Meanwhile, to strengthen employees' quality awareness, Chenhui Guangbao continues to carry out quality month activities. During the quality month activity in September this year, on one hand, through slogan promotion, departmental lectures, knowledge competitions, essay contests with prizes, speech competitions, production line quality evaluations, and other forms, the concept of "quality is the life of the enterprise" was deeply rooted in the hearts of every employee; on the other hand, training intensity was increased, and employees were trained comprehensively and multi-dimensionally in various ways to improve their skill levels and overall qualities.

In addition to striving tirelessly for product quality, Chenhui Guangbao also pays great attention to safety during the production process. It is understood that the company arranges emergency drills four to five times a year and assigns dedicated personnel for management. In April this year, to prevent and control potential accidents or emergencies and provide security for production, Chenhui Guangbao established a "backup emergency team" to establish a unified command, fully functional, sensitive reaction, efficient operation emergency mechanism within the company, thereby making production and grassroots management more standardized and procedural.

Core Word: Layout - Strengthening LED Development

With the increasingly deep-rooted concept of low carbon, LED has shown a vigorous development trend in recent one or two years. Chenhui Guangbao, which has always practiced "healthy lighting", while maintaining its original advantage products such as T5, T8 light source products and special light source products, has also increased its efforts in LED research and development. After collecting extensive information on the development trends and information of the domestic and international lighting industries, centered around the theme of energy-saving lighting, Chenhui Guangbao smoothly completed the strategic deployment of transitioning from traditional lighting to LED lighting. Currently, the company has successfully researched and produced a series of high-quality products such as dimmable LED bulbs, LED panel lights, LED tube lights, LED recessed lights, and LED self-ballasted PAR lamps. The newly completed Chenhui Guangbao Shangyu LED production base has started operations with high starting points and high standards. To seize the opportunities brought by energy conservation and emission reduction for high-efficiency lighting, Chenhui Guangbao will have some new strategic deployments this year: first, the company will further expand its production capacity, introduce outstanding talents, and ensure synchronized development of enterprise management and production capacity expansion; second, the company will increase the promotion of green lighting products and achieve a significant leap in promoting state-bid products; third, the company will continue to strengthen brand promotion and strive to make Chenhui Guangbao a top-tier well-known brand in the country.

Marketing: Service + Network Winning

With the intensifying competition, nowadays, many lighting companies adjust their strategic deployments and marketing strategies in a timely manner according to changes in the market environment, and Chenhui Guangbao is no exception. In recent years, Chenhui Guangbao has continuously strengthened its domestic market promotion, complementing domestic logistics network marketing with overseas sales network construction. Through various forms of sales methods such as participating in domestic and international top-level exhibitions, academic seminars, strengthening media publicity, and market terminal publicity, it has driven the comprehensive development of the marketing market. Just in July and August this year, Chenhui Guangbao spent heavily to build large high-column advertising boards near the Shaoxing service area on the Hangzhou-Ningbo Expressway and the East Guan toll station entrance in Shangyu, increasing brand promotion.

With the increase in domestic promotional efforts, so far, Chenhui Guangbao has established more than 30 marketing service offices nationwide, set up more than 50 logistics merchants, and the number of Chenhui brands' second and third-level distributors across the country has reached more than 2,000. Now, in all provinces across the country and relatively developed cities, there are basically Chenhui sales service outlets. In terms of engineering projects, Chenhui Guangbao has also had frequent good news in the past two years, such as the "Beijing Subway Line 6 Project", the "China Mobile Business Hall Energy Saving Renovation Project", the "Wuhan University Energy Saving Renovation Project", and the "Shenzhen Universiade LED Lighting Engineering Project". In the national high-efficiency energy-saving lighting promotion project from 2008 to 2011, Chenhui Guangbao won the bid for four consecutive years.

In addition, with the vigorous development of e-commerce, Chenhui Guangbao has also actively tried the new channel of online sales. In April this year, Chenhui Guangbao officially joined the Juyouwu Lamp Mall, and in August, its flagship store in the mall officially began operations, providing customers with online procurement services for national B2B orders. This has become a reflection of Chenhui Guangbao's bold innovation and change.
