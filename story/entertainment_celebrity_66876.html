Rare to see: 15 of Zhang Bai Zhi's stunningly bloody costumes

Since her debut, Zhang Baiqi has filmed more than 20 movies with various styles. Cai Kangyong once said that "each of Zhang Baiqi's styles are different." What are the classic styles of this future queen of changes? Let me help you recall, you will be shocked.

To become an excellent actress, one must fully commit oneself. Zhang Baiqi has already merged with her movies; her achievements, I believe everyone can see!

An award-winning actress who never looks at the script, a superstar who consistently refuses media interviews, and a stunning beauty who claims "to yearn for love but cannot obtain it" - she is Zhang Baiqi. Although producer Chen Hong had previously told me that Baiqi is a "barely naked" girl, her true nature displayed during the interview still surprised me.

She is unrestricted and candidly innocent, except for one topic that made her feel awkward - the final question in the interview where she sighed repeatedly, hesitated to speak, until her eyes turned red. If we were to freeze that moment, you would definitely think that this is a beautiful woman who accidentally wandered into the end of 2005.

After filming "The King of Comedy," everyone said it was very good, so I accepted this way of acting. Therefore, everyone sees me always running around on set, not looking at the script. But let me tell you, that's just the exterior; I put immense pressure on myself internally because I am a perfectionist.

Zhang Baiqi once said, if you ask me to choose between being a mother and my career, I would definitely choose to be a mother, but I won't give up on movies either. Many senior female actors, such as Maggie Cheung, have told me: "You are the only successor in Hong Kong. You're very smart and talented, you need to strive and make more movies." Gong Li and sister Brigitte Lin also told me: "Don't waste your talent, you must work hard." So I told sister Lin: "But I really want to get married, like you, to have a great husband and children."

In fact, when she debuted at 18, she didn't deliberately say she wanted to support her family; she just hoped to earn money using her own abilities. Her parents weren't poor, they could support her even if she didn't work. After having the opportunity to earn money, she wanted to provide for her family, and gradually got used to taking care of everyone. At 18, she wasn't very fond of acting. When she reached her twenties, she found herself increasingly demanding in her performances, and now she has unconsciously fallen in love with movies, not shooting for the money. However, the money is definitely important because she feels she has sacrificed too much.

Zhang Baiqi can be said to be an excellent artist we've watched grow up. From her debut to now, she has experienced many things, whether in relationships, career, or even life-and-death situations. But she is a strong girl, and her efforts have not been wasted!
