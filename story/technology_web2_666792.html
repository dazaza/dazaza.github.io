Eleven embroidery needles found in man's body (picture group)

Report from Leping by reporter Wang Jingping: Recently, it was discovered by doctors that Cheng Guohua, a man from Leping, had 11 sewing needles inside his body. This has left medical staff baffled as to how these needles entered Cheng Guohua's body.

Cheng Guohua, a villager from Zhongdian Village in Ji Yang Street of Leping City, noticed that whenever he squatted down, his abdomen would hurt. Initially, Cheng Guohua didn't pay much attention to it. However, at the end of December last year, Cheng Guohua's abdominal pain suddenly worsened and began to swell. Cheng Guohua's wife quickly accompanied him to the Dalian General Hospital in Leping City for a check-up. The result was that after looking at Cheng Guohua's X-ray, the doctor was greatly shocked. There were 11 needle-like metal objects inside Cheng Guohua's body, nine of which were in the abdomen and two in the thighs. A sewing needle close to the internal iliac artery posed a threat to Cheng Guohua's life safety. Subsequently, the hospital removed all nine needles from Cheng Guohua's abdomen through surgery. The longest one measured 3.6 centimeters. Moreover, most of the sewing needles had already rusted.

Cheng Guohua said that he worked on nearby construction sites and often walked at night. Due to local customs believing that needles can ward off evil, he once bought a pack of needles to keep in his pocket, but he never felt any needle pricks.

Cheng Guohua's attending physician, Wang Fayou, said that they ruled out the possibility of accidental ingestion, as ingested needles would only be in the intestines and not in the abdominal cavity. Some people analyzed that it was likely that while Cheng Guohua was working on the construction site with the sewing needles, they accidentally pierced into his body. Because Cheng Guohua's body was constantly in motion and his spirit was fully focused, he did not feel any obvious pain at the time.
