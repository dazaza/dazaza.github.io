The US virtual hosting sees a fierce battle among the top three, with service and cost-performance ratio becoming important competitive chips.

by xwkj0983 on 2012-02-21 14:57:44

The continuous holidays have made the consumer market this spring exceptionally bustling. This lively market atmosphere has not only stimulated consumers' enthusiasm for shopping, but also ignited the战火of American virtual hosting promotions. Whether it's medium and small-sized hosting providers or large brand hosting providers, they are all aggressively entering the domestic market. Among them, the three major American hosting giants with substantial strength - Godaddy, IXWebHosting, and HostEase - have successfully occupied the top three positions in the market due to their superior services and high cost-performance advantages. Therefore, this battle is essentially a showdown among the three giants.

### The Three Giants Compete on Service

As the level of consumer needs increases, merchants are increasingly focusing on competition in service. From the service measures of the three giants, each has its own advantages and none of them are clearly superior. HostEase has a higher degree of Chinese localization and is currently the only American virtual host provider that simultaneously offers four key elements: a Chinese website, Chinese customer service, Chinese submission forms, and a Chinese backend operating system. In comparison, although Godaddy lacks the advantage of Chinese localization, it excels in the variety of domain and hosting options and the flexibility of control panel management, far surpassing HostEase in these areas. As for IXWebHosting, another one of the three giants, while its Chinese localization and range of domain hosting products do not match HostEase and Godaddy respectively, it has unique advantages in providing free independent IPs and free domains, as well as customer service. Offering 15 free independent IPs, 3 free domains, live chat, and ticket-based Chinese customer service are distinctive features exclusive to IXWebHosting.

### The Three Giants Compete on Cost-Performance Ratio

Godaddy holds a significant advantage in the competition among the giants, offering a wide range of product discounts covering domains, virtual hosting, SSL certificates, and virtual data centers, with substantial discount magnitudes. Users can purchase .COM domains for just $5.99 through promotional links and enjoy a 30% discount on annual payment virtual hosting via promotional links. Additionally, Godaddy has launched a promotion for its Virtual Data Center where the first month is discounted to $1. Compared to Godaddy’s diverse discounts, HostEase and IXWebHosting’s discounts are relatively conservative but still highly competitive. Virtual hosting users can obtain a 20% discount on blog-type, basic, and business virtual hosting by using the "hostease" discount code. Furthermore, IXWebHosting not only offers 15 free independent IPs and free domains when purchasing hosting, but also provides an 80% discount link. Purchasing hosting through the secret discount link at the bottom of IXWebHosting's Chinese official website allows users to enjoy an 80% discount on newly registered annual payment hosting plans.

The competition in the virtual hosting market is extremely fierce. The promotional activities launched by the three American hosting providers have caused a storm in the domestic virtual hosting market. The price war initiated by the three American virtual hosting providers will become increasingly intense. Hosting providers are not only fiercely competing on cost-performance ratio but also intensifying their focus on service, an area often overlooked by merchants. However, regardless of how the hosting providers compete, the ultimate beneficiaries are the domain and hosting users.
