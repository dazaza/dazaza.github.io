& nbsp, Stock Knowledge; & nbsp; & nbsp; & nbsp; The company mainly engages in the production and sales of spandex. Affected by the financial crisis, the export of the downstream textile and garment industry was not vigorous, which dragged down the development of the spandex industry. In the first three quarters of 2009, the company achieved a net profit of about 8 million yuan, a year-on-year decrease of about 91%, but in the third quarter alone, it achieved a net profit of more than 4 million yuan. The company's performance gradually emerged from the trough, showing signs of recovery. & nbsp; Positive factors increase, recovery is guaranteed & nbsp; & nbsp; & nbsp; & nbsp; & nbsp; 1. Capacity expansion. The company's current annual output of 10,000 tons of spandex project is proceeding smoothly, and will be put into operation at the end of 2009 or early next year, as well as Youli Special Fiber's 0.43 million tons of spandex technical renovation project. Once fully operational, the company will add 16,300 tons of differentiated spandex capacity, and the total capacity will rank among the top three in the industry. & nbsp; & nbsp; & nbsp; & nbsp; & nbsp; 2. Against the backdrop of the gradual recovery of the global economy, the export of textiles and garments has improved. According to predictions by the Ministry of Agriculture and the Cotton Association, China's cotton planting area decreased by about 13% in 2009. Stimulated by these favorable factors, the price and demand for spandex have quickly rebounded. & nbsp; On the secondary market, the stock recently gradually broke out of the bottom area of 7 yuan - 9.50 yuan, the moving average system showed a bullish alignment, accompanied by an increase in volume, and the stock is expected to continue to rebound in the future. It might be worthwhile to pay attention to Youli Holdings (000584) on dips.

The procedures for buying and selling stocks are as follows: When buying or selling stocks, you must specify whether you are buying or selling, the name (or code) of the stock, quantity, and price. Moreover, this order is only valid on the day it is placed. The content of the commission includes the abbreviation (code) of the stock you want to buy or sell, the quantity, and the price at which you want to buy or sell the stock. The abbreviation of the stock usually consists of three to four Chinese characters, and the code of the stock is six digits long. When entrusting to buy or sell, the code and abbreviation of the stock must be consistent. At the same time, there are certain regulations regarding the quantity of stocks being bought or sold: that is, the quantity of stocks entrusted to buy must be a whole multiple of 100, but the quantity of stocks entrusted to sell does not have to be a whole multiple of 100.

There are four types of commissions: counter submission commission, telephone automatic commission, computer automatic commission, and remote terminal commission.
