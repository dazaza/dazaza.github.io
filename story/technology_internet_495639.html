by gstookha7 on 2011-09-27 10:47:19

In the second half of this year, the General Party Branch of the Jiegao Community will continue to make serving the public as the basic starting point of community work. It will implement the Party's core leadership role and policies, service efficiency into all areas of community construction, strengthen the attractiveness of the party organization, continue to promote community services through multiple channels, making the community organizational cells more orderly in democratization management and civilian service provision, fostering a more harmonious community. The work will focus on the following aspects:

1. Actively play the leading core role of the party organization, strengthen the responsibilities of party members, and establish various long-term governance mechanisms for the community party organization. Reinforce leadership, implement responsibilities, improve services, and do well in the exemplary role of party members. Expand the "1+3" party member service commitment to all areas of the community, achieving all work tasks arranged by the subdistrict working committee and office.

2. Seriously and solidly carry out the campaign to strive for excellence.

3. Carefully complete the national population census work.

4. Do well in the rotation election work of the community party organization and the community residents' committee.

5. Do well in the community's "core work" mainly consisting of routine content such as family planning and community health.

6. Further implement the "Dual Five-Package in Front of Door" and "Wednesday Sanitation Day Every Week" system. Adopt a combination of intensive management and long-term governance methods to increase the intensity of regional environmental remediation. Mobilize group leaders, party members, cleaners, volunteers, etc., to participate in environmental remediation work at all levels. Strengthen patrols of daily sanitation in neighborhoods, ensure garbage is bagged and cleared daily with no dead corners, eradicate the "four pests", identify problems promptly, and address them immediately.

7. Do well in the "safety work" aimed at community stability, enhancing comprehensive social security management.

8. Strengthen the service awareness of the community integrated service management station and improve handling efficiency.

9. Deepen the distinctive volunteer work and expand the scope of services. Uphold the spirit of volunteer service: "I participate, I contribute, I am happy". Let volunteer services penetrate into resident units and property management neighborhoods, popularize volunteer service awareness throughout the community, seek support from all parties, build platforms for social organizations, enterprises, and other sectors to participate in the development of volunteer services and support volunteer service projects, forming a mechanism linking the Communist Youth League, social organizations, and enterprises, advocating new trends in volunteer services.
