[House Viewing Bus] Mayflower: High cost-effectiveness, Netizens Want to Buy

The traditional Spring Festival has just passed, and the whole city of Xiamen is still immersed in a festive atmosphere. With the arrival of the Lantern Festival on February 6th, the festive atmosphere becomes even more intense. On this occasion of the festival, XMHOUSE's first house viewing bus of 2012 departs today, heading towards Jimei. Nearly a hundred netizens explore two major projects by central state-owned enterprises (SOEs), namely, CMH Hai De Park and Poly & CMH Mayflower.

Netizens arrive at the assembly point.

Netizen registration.

Today, XMHOUSE’s house viewing bus departs for the first time after the Spring Festival, taking nearly a hundred netizens to explore two major projects by central SOEs. The second stop is the Poly & CMH Mayflower project. Mayflower is a monumental work jointly launched by CMH Property and Poly Property, two major central SOEs, with a total land area of 140,000 square meters and a total construction area of 350,000 square meters. Phase one includes livable high-rise sea-view residences and a comfortable low-density villa area for improvement.

Entering the sales office of Poly & CMH Mayflower.

At the house viewing site, netizens actively approach property consultants to learn about the project details. Staff members say they have never seen so many proactive netizens before, and there are quite a few of them. After the sale-out of building 3 in plot 6, building 2 quickly followed suit, leaving only a few units available. Currently, building 1 is being launched, with unit sizes ranging from 80-110 square meters, offering two or three-bedroom options. The average price is 9,600-9,800 yuan per square meter. Five special-priced units are currently being offered, located on floors 6-10, with discounts of around 92%.

Five special-priced units are being launched in building 1.

House viewing site.

Unit model.

It is learned that the project offers 80-square-meter two-bedroom units and 110-square-meter three-bedroom units for sale. The units are square-shaped, with north-south ventilation, all-bright design, and a comfortable large balcony for viewing, which netizens believe offer relatively high cost-effectiveness. They expressed strong intentions to purchase. Even when the scheduled house viewing time was over, half of the netizens remained at the sales office for further detailed discussions. Even after the house viewing bus returned, a few groups of customers stayed on-site for detailed consultations.

Poly Property and CMH Property, two powerful central SOEs, present their 2011 masterpiece - "Mayflower." Located at the intersection of the beautiful Xiamen Bay West Avenue and Yinghuan Road, it stands at the first station of the Jimei New City, benefiting from the billion-yuan new city plan of Jimei. It is within a 10-minute BRT radius of the urban core. Enjoy the scholarly influence of Jimei's century-old academic village, with an educational system spanning from kindergarten to university.

Backed by the lush oxygen bar of Meiren Mountain, the project faces a stunning coastline. A 7.9-kilometer West Coast scenic belt, a 4.7-kilometer artificial beach, sports park, seaside square, mangroves, and a cluster of star-rated hotels bring superior natural and artificial living environments.

The project covers a total land area of 140,000 square meters and a total construction area of 350,000 square meters. Phase one includes livable high-rise sea-view residences and a comfortable low-density villa area for improvement.
