Online memorial services exceed cyber begging

by ericksonx on 2010-04-05 19:28:27

Online memorial services are a new type of commemoration method that has emerged in recent years. It leverages the spatiotemporal characteristics of the internet to "move" real-world memorial halls and cemeteries onto computers, allowing people to pay respects to their deceased loved ones at any time and place. This method does not contradict traditional forms of worship but is rather an inheritance and extension of them.

"The feelings for family deepen especially during festivals," every year during the Qingming Festival, people always go to their ancestral graves to burn incense and clean the tombs. In today's world where material enjoyment and spiritual civilization, humanistic science and technology are rapidly developing, many people who work away from home or run businesses overseas find it difficult to fulfill the wish to commemorate their deceased relatives due to various reasons. By "inviting" ancestors online, such as on websites like panzhihua.liao59.com, even if one cannot physically visit the grave, they can still enter the ancestor's virtual tomb at any time, feel their presence, review their ordinary yet great lives, and revisit their earnest teachings. One can also light a candle, offer flowers, leave a message at the online tomb they built personally, ... providing some solace spiritually.

Currently, domestic websites offering such services include Global Memorial and Blessing Network, Online Memorial Network, Endless Love Memorial Network, Root Path Memorial Network, Paradise Network Online Memorial Hall, Huaxia Memorial Network, China Mausoleum Network, Tonghuai Network, Taiyuan Xianju, Sihuai, Yanhuang Memorial Network, etc. These websites all provide functions to establish online memorial halls or online cemeteries. Some websites eliminate the illusion of online memorials by adopting realistic simulation methods, further promoting online memorials. Among them, the interactive simulated online memorial platform created by the Global Memorial and Blessing Network simulates the entire process from establishing a cemetery to conducting a memorial service, narrowing the distance between the internet and reality, making online memorials no longer seem illusory. The website also features unique functions such as sending letters to heaven (with replies) and praying for the deceased, which are rooted in life but transcend it, gradually gaining popularity and acceptance.

"During the Qingming Festival, rain falls thick and fast, passersby with spirits almost broken," describes the scene of people busy sweeping tombs during the Qingming Festival each year. With the advent of fashionable online memorials through the internet, this scene will no longer exist. People admire green and fashionable lifestyles, pursuing a relaxed and perfect way of commemorating. You just need to easily click the mouse to complete the entire memorial process, giving you a sense of being there in person. It also allows you to feel the arrival of a whole new era of memorials.

The Ministry of Civil Affairs issued the "Notice on Doing a Good Job in Relevant Work During the Qingming Festival for the Public's Memorial Activities." The notice requires the promotion of online memorials, guiding the public to break old funeral customs and advocate civilized memorial methods during the concentrated public memorial activities during the Qingming Festival; promoting civilized memorial methods such as "family remembrance meetings" and "online memorials."
