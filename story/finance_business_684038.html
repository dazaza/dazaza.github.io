Beijing Bailing Expansion has hundreds of training programs, starting first from the spirit and determination of the team members. This method has been proven effective in practice. In fact, in our future lives and work, I suggest that everyone adopt the practice of loudly expressing their inner voices. This will be a powerful weapon for us to achieve our life goals and a spiritual pillar to inspire us to strive for strength.

The trainers from Beijing Bailing Expansion Company often quote classic motivational articles to share this truth with the team members. Below is an article by Sato Tomoyuki to share with everyone.

The content of the article is: "The life you speak of is your life," I say to many people.

"Good," "There will definitely be a way," "No problem," those who can say these positive words every day, their days will go very smoothly. Even if they encounter difficulties, they can overcome them. On the contrary, those who complain every day about "It's terrible," "It's so infuriating," "There's nothing I can do," tend to face more setbacks and seem to have particularly bad luck. Haven't you or the people around you experienced such things? If you cannot see yourself clearly, then try looking at the people and events around you. You will certainly find that people live the life they speak of. Especially when it comes to money-related matters, this point becomes even more evident. Surprisingly, those who cry out every day about "no money" are truly those who seem to be destined to be disconnected from money. The key information here is not "because of poverty there is no money," but "saying no money every day leads to poverty." You must realize that the words that come out of your mouth every day have great power, thus changing your speech. Every word you say each day points the direction for your day. What you say will surely become reality after it is spoken. This is decided by the human brain and autonomic nervous system. The autonomic nervous system controls the body through the cerebral cortex. Our brains transmit thoughts to various parts of the body through learning the autonomic nervous system, manipulating them to turn our thoughts into reality. From buying drinks from vending machines to flying into space on spacecraft, all things in the world are realized because people initially had certain ideas, considering "let's do it this way," "it will become like this," and eventually achieving them. If we were to find an appropriate carrier for people's thoughts, it would certainly be the words they speak. When we consider problems, language actually appears in our minds already. Therefore, the most important thing is: when considering problems, how to make language appear in our minds, and how to better express what we are thinking about using language. That is to say, the final words you decide to say will affect your life, big or small. Positive language can lead you to a beautiful life. Language is like an automatic engine that takes the plane to its destination. As long as you press the button, it will definitely take us to our destination.

So, everyone needs to remember first: we must speak positively. By continuously using very positive words, we can accumulate relevant important information. Before we know it, we have already taken action and gradually turned what we said into reality.

Everyone knows that in our lifetime, we have goals at different levels and stages. Why does the success rate vary greatly when it comes to achieving these goals? Who doesn't want to have a greater chance of success? The reason is that we need to create positive factors for ourselves in the process of achieving our goals. First, when various goals are already clearly displayed in our brains, we should loudly express our inner voices, calling out the results we want to achieve and the methods of effort. This gives us a clear motivation psychologically and a firm belief in achieving our goals. We must avoid negative language such as "I can't," "I don't know how," "I can't do it." If you don't even dare to try, how can you talk about success?

I want to shout loudly: from today, from now, after setting goals for different periods, we should frequently give ourselves loud encouragement, express our desire to achieve our goals, constantly cheer ourselves on and affirm ourselves. When necessary, loudly express your heart's voice for achieving your goals in front of others besides yourself, adding a heavy weight to the realization of your goals. Speaking out this language can better motivate your determination and efforts to achieve your goals.

Indeed, speaking is crucial; it is the most solid foundation for us to achieve our goals. Let us loudly declare together for the realization of our goals at various stages! May we always use positive, healthy, and inspiring motivational language to encourage ourselves daily in the process of achieving our goals!
