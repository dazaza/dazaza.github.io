It seems that childhood was an era of toys and snacks. Snow's childhood was filled with laughter, carefree and worry-free. Her straightforward and cheerful personality left a strong mark on Snow's world. When the elf carried the memory box, the childhood memories were covered at the deepest point, the memory box full of dust opened once again to recall long-sealed memories. Upon first contact with junior high school, Snow's mood was filled with joy and excitement. In her young heart, she had already been looking forward to a dream-like junior high school life. Saying goodbye to that innocent childhood, Snow grew up quietly in that summer where the wind blew...

On that asphalt road, Snow began the journey home with her friends. They agreed to go home together after school, singing outdated songs on the way, enjoying the happiness belonging to their youthful season. Whenever birds flapped their wings and flew away, her friend always liked to laugh at her: "Your song scared the birds away..." At such times, Snow always liked to scratch her head, pretending to be angry, "You dare to laugh at me?" Laughter had faintly dispersed on the road, and the faint floral fragrance floating in the air seemed to bear witness to that happy and sincere friendship.

But on that day, Snow cried. Her friends were so shocked they didn't dare to breathe, and the originally joyful atmosphere seemed frozen all at once. It turned out that Snow's beloved grandfather had passed away. Snow cried until her voice became hoarse, but her grandfather still did not respond to her. Snow's heart felt as if it had been struck hard, leaving her speechless. By the little river, along that small path, everything related to her grandfather was locked away by Snow. After that day, despite still smiling, her smiles carried evident sadness and estrangement. From that day onward, Snow was quietly changing...
