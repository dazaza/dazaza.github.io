    Germany Bosch Rexroth(BOSCH REXROTH STAR) ball bearing rail guidance system is specially designed for machine tools and industrial robots. This compact ball-supported linear guidance system is divided into different accuracy levels and has extremely high load-carrying capacity and very high rigidity. The compact assembly unit comes in eight common market specifications, with the highest rated load in all four main load-bearing directions. Both the rails and sliders are precision machined in the raceway areas and can be combined within the same accuracy level. They can even be fully interchangeable with roller rail guidance systems.    Germany Rexroth
