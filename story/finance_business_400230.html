Napkin folding method 1:

1. Fold the napkin in half and then fold up a small edge. 2. Fold the two sides back separately, and finally insert a flower.

Tips:

As shown in the above figure, if the folded napkins are for multiple people using a self-service light meal, the knives and forks arranged on the napkins must be fixed with a rope knot. The knife and fork must be placed separately and uniformly. This way it is neat, beautiful, and safe.

Tablecloth folding method: pocket

1. Fold the tablecloth twice continuously to get a small square; align the four corners at the top left position.

2. Flip the left corner of the first layer on the surface to the center point and continue to flip it down to the bottom right corner along the center line; flip the left corner of the second layer inward.

3. Turn the well-folded square to the back, fold the left and right sides to the center; return to the front, forming two pockets.

Effect demonstration: place a single-person set of knives and forks and a flower.

Tablecloth folding method: hat

1. Fold the tablecloth once, then fold the two diagonally opposite corners to the centerline to form a large diamond shape.

2. Flip the diamond to the back, align the two edges and fold them once to get a long strip, with a triangle on each side of the strip.

3. Turn up the two ends, insert one end into the pocket of the other end, stand it up after inserting, unfold and tidy it into a hat.

Tablecloth folding method: small stool

1. First, fold the tablecloth into a thin long strip.

2. Fold an appropriate length upwards from one end of the long strip to make the core.

3. Starting from the folded segment, roll the long strip into a cylindrical shape.

Tablecloth folding method: windmill

1. Fold the tablecloth twice continuously to get a small square.

2. Fold the two sides of the square to the centerline to form a rectangle.

3. Fold the shorter side of the rectangle to the center, then press the center point with one hand and pull out one corner with the other hand, pressing and fixing it. Handle the other three corners in the same way.
