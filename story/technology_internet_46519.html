SEO is a technology, but more importantly, SEO is a strategy, an auxiliary tool. So many people go to those websites to read so-called SEO technical articles, and after reading, they take immediate action. Perhaps there will be some short-term effects, but do you know that you have been misled by those articles? It's not that the articles are poorly written, but rather your understanding is wrong.
