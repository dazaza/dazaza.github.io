The transition from traditional energy to new energy

by ditan360 on 2010-06-30 14:57:06

Low-carbon Network exclusive report: Last year, Premier Wen participated in the Copenhagen Climate Change Conference and announced to the world: "China is a country with the fastest growth rate of new energy and renewable energy. The installed capacity of hydropower, the scale of nuclear power under construction, the collector area of solar water heaters, and the installed capacity of photovoltaic power generation all rank first in the world." Data shows that the total output value of the solar thermal utilization industry reached 57.85 billion yuan last year, an increase of 34.7% over 2008. It can be said that 2009 was a year full of change and upgrading for China's solar thermal utilization industry.

In 2010, in the process of developing and applying traditional energy to new energy, Beijing Kuntailong International Energy Investment Co., Ltd. took the lead in the industry.

As early as before the "low-carbon" wind blew strongly in 2010, Beijing Kuntailong International Energy Investment Co., Ltd. had already paid close attention to the development of the solar industry, and collaborated with relevant research institutions and high-tech enterprises such as Tsinghua University's Electric Engineering Technology Research Team, Tianjin Vocational and Technical Normal University, and its New Light Energy Joint Laboratory, investing in the construction of the Kuntailong New Light Energy Product Manufacturing Base Project in Erdos City, Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region.

The Kuntailong New Light Energy Product Manufacturing Base Project in Erdos City is a comprehensive series of product R&D and manufacturing systems that apply new energy, new light sources, and new technologies. It has industrial characteristics such as production and manufacturing, high technology, related driving force, and resource conservation and environmental protection.

The Kuntailong New Light Energy Product Manufacturing Base Project in Erdos City will adhere to the principles of advancedness, applicability, and economy, facing both domestic and international markets. The first phase will focus on developing and manufacturing: permanent magnet direct drive movable small and medium-sized wind power stations, movable small and medium-sized wind-solar complementary power stations, solar charging and discharging and inverter control function integrated machines (inverters), as well as new light energy building materials and architectural product series.

The Kuntailong New Light Energy Product Manufacturing Base Project in Erdos City will be invested and constructed in the Modern Equipment Manufacturing Base in A Town, Yijinholo Banner, Erdos City. The first phase investment from July 2009 to July 2012 will exceed 1 billion yuan. The project will be organized and implemented by the project company set up by Kuntailong International in Erdos City. The total planned land use for the project is about 200 mu. Among them, the first phase will construct 15,000 square meters of factory buildings, 8,800 square meters of R&D, office, and supporting facilities. The factory itself will become a demonstration project for new light energy products. Production will gradually start in 2010, and after 2011, the annual output value will reach more than 1.5 billion yuan.

Beijing Kuntailong International Energy Investment Co., Ltd. was founded in 1998, mainly engaged in coal mining and sales. It integrates science, industry, and trade. It has successively established seven wholly-owned subsidiaries with independent legal person status: Changchun Longda Material Trade Co., Ltd.; Jilin Longda Heat Power Co., Ltd.; Jingyu Coal Mine of Baishan Zhongyuan Energy Development Co., Ltd. (referred to as Jingyu Coal Mine); Songshu Town Coal Mine Comprehensive Operation Company of Baishan City (Songshu Town Coal Mine); Dakuochin Coal Mine of Jilin; Baishan Ju Sheng Coal Co., Ltd.; and Laos Zhanba Tobacco Co., Ltd.

In recent years, the company has carried out a series of corporate restructuring and consolidation. Through corporate acquisitions and capital operations, the company has continuously grown and developed, accumulating rich management experience and strong financial strength, providing high-quality raw coal for large listed companies such as Jilin Chemical Fiber and Dacheng Corn. It has been awarded the title of "Major Taxpayer in Jilin Province" multiple times. It has become one of the largest coal suppliers in Jilin Province. The company's scale continues to grow, with up to 1,578 employees at its peak, including six senior managers, 32 mid-to-senior level technical engineers, and 238 technical personnel, laying a strong technical foundation for the further development and expansion of the company.

At the beginning of 2008, the company reorganized again and moved its headquarters to Beijing, making strategic adjustments to its development. On the basis of energy project development and operation, the company's main business has shifted to investment management. The company will enter the green energy industries of new energy and new light sources through corporate mergers and acquisitions, asset restructuring, and other capital operation methods, while continuing to expand the coal mining, washing and selection, coal chemical industry, and other traditional energy industries, further improving the integrated production, transportation, and sales system, achieving the strategic goal of diversified development related to coal mining.

Kuntailong moving forward will welcome another development peak in the era of low-carbon economy.
