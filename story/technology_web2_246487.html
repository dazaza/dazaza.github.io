CCTV's overseas theater is currently airing a popular Indian love TV series called "Eternal Love". The story is very strong, with twists and turns that captivate the audience. Through the bizarre and unpredictable fates of the characters in the drama, people see the power of love and the true goodness and beauty of human nature.

"Eternal Love" plot introduction: Although the two have never met, they learned many things about each other through Arun and secretly developed an interest in each other. On the other hand, Shruti, an intern doctor under Dr. Sanjup and an unemployed youth named Akash fell in love. However, Shruti's mother wanted to fulfill her father's wish before he unexpectedly fell into a coma until now, which was for her to marry Rahul, her childhood playmate. At the last moment of the wedding, Rahul found out that Shruti loved Akash, so he canceled the wedding. Arun wanted to settle his sister's life matters because their parents had already passed away, and it was his responsibility. Thus, he chose a man named Niregi for his sister and set the wedding date. However, on the day of the wedding, Niregi fell ill and was diagnosed with late-stage leukemia, so he chose to leave. Dr. Sanjup attended the wedding; he finally confessed his love to Asta and decided to marry her. On the day of Shruti's wedding, Akash had to go home to take care of his sick father. After his father got better, his parents forced him to marry a girl named Nopur that they had chosen for him. Both Dr. Sanjup and Akash's weddings were on the same day, and coincidentally, they boarded the same train back to Mumbai. When the train passed over a bridge, an accident occurred. Both Dr. Sanjup and Akash survived. They struggled to find their respective brides. Dr. Sanjup found Nopur's corpse and mistakenly thought it was Asta because the body was severely disfigured and unrecognizable. He claimed the body and solemnly swore to her. Akash found Asta who had lost consciousness but was still breathing, wearing the same red bridal gown. He mistakenly thought she was his wife and took her to the nearest hospital. After several hours of emergency treatment, Asta gradually regained consciousness but at the cost of losing her memory. Shruti met her superior Dr. Sanjup while participating in a medical rescue team and told him that she would do everything in her power to help find Asta. Can Dr. Sanjup find his beloved Asta? Will the amnesiac Asta spark feelings with Akash? How will their stories continue? Will everyone ultimately find their own love and happiness?
