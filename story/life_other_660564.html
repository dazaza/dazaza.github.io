The Broken-Wing Angel Never Leaves

The day she met him, he was crying his heart out. She thought to herself that perhaps he was a broken-winged angel.

The day he met her, she was very silly, almost childishly naive. He thought to himself that maybe this naive girl was a kind-hearted one.

He and she were like two lines, what kind of lines exactly, they couldn't quite tell yet, but there would definitely be a story unfolding...

She fell for this broken-winged angel and wanted to mend his damaged wing. She had confidence in herself that she could do it. From then on, the cheerful her turned into a melancholic girl. She became increasingly afraid, fearing that she would get too deep, fearing that in the end she wouldn't be able to help him and instead would hurt herself. But her unwavering belief kept her going, even if it meant losing her life, she would mend his broken wing. Although she knew that angels cannot fall in love with humans, nor would they ever fall for someone as ordinary as her, especially a girl as foolish as her.

He thought she was very kind, but due to various reasons, he couldn't reciprocate her feelings. He knew she was good to him, she was a good girl, and he also knew she liked him a lot. However, after an angel's wounds heal, they always leave this place. Their relationship wouldn't last long...

She understood why he rejected her, because angels always have to leave. Though she believed that angels have emotions too. She wanted to help him, mend his wounds, and fill his surroundings with happiness and joy. She had no ill intentions, she just hoped his wounds would heal quickly, even though he would leave once healed. Perhaps this was the most meaningful thing she thought she could do!

She grew more and more melancholic. She realized she had fallen in love with him. She was scared, afraid of the day he would leave...

His wounds and pain were repeatedly uncovered by her. He was also afraid, afraid of being understood so thoroughly by her, because he didn't want anyone, any angel, to know his inner thoughts. Every day, he pretended to be happy, but inside he wasn't at all. He was exhausted, so let the wounds continue, at least here, he could still watch her happiness while by her side...

She didn't care about anything else anymore. She wanted to be with him, she wanted to protect him, she wanted him to recover quickly, and forever be happier than herself... But, she knew being together was impossible...

He started avoiding her, keeping her from mending his wounds. Once mended, he would have to leave. He began making her angry, hating him, misunderstanding him, disliking him. He refused everything she did for him. He knew she was hurt, he knew he deeply wounded that foolhardy her, but he had to do it this way, otherwise, in the end, she would be hurt even more. He could only keep her in his heart, not letting anyone know. In his heart, she would always be the kindest angel, his personal angel!

She cried, tears streaming down her face. Maybe her feelings for him weren't love after all...

He left, walking towards another girl he didn't like. Although this girl he didn't like adored him so much, she didn't know how to mend his wounds. This way, he wouldn't leave. Because he could keep watching the kind girl who belonged to him, watching her happiness...

He gritted his teeth, enduring the pain, using both hands to break the other wing. He became human, but as he looked at that kind girl, he shed an angel's tear, something he would never let her know... This way, he could watch her forever... Silently protecting her by her side...

She was hurt, shedding many tears. She knew he didn't belong to her. She wanted him to become the perfect angel, without considering his feelings, without asking about his inner thoughts, nor knowing that he had never intended to hurt her. Yes, she was wrong, everything was her fault. As she blamed herself, tears streamed down her face...

He saw all of this from afar, and he too shed tears. These tears would transform into happiness and joy, surrounding the girl for the rest of her life... He thought, it might be better that she doesn't understand these things! After all, why was she so dumb and naive? Ha—! This silly girl! You will definitely be very happy!

She shed her last tear, thinking perhaps she and he were like two parallel lines, watching each other's happiness, but never intersecting... Alas... The broken-winged angel! He must be very happy too!
