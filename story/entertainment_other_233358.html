There is a boy who is particularly good to each person.

He is not the one in your heart, nor the one with whom you are together.

At least after a long time, you will depend on him, and you cannot live without him.

____________________________________________________

Listening to a song repeatedly .

I think the lyrics are really classic. If we can't get along, why can't we break up?

____________________________________________________

What I'm afraid of is that even if I try my best,

Running as fast as I can, running and running, I can never reach you.

____________________________________________________

I can't tell if this is real or just a dream. Why do I feel inexplicable loneliness?

Memories are still intermittent, lingering sadness turns into sorrowful cycles.

____________________________________________________

The ups and downs of sorrow appear faintly in memories, highlighting layers of ripples. Perhaps after

a year. When I look at these words again, I won't be able to recall how sad I was describing.

____________________________________________________

For the world, one person's death is just another grave,

but for those who depend on each other, it's like the whole world has been buried by a grave.

____________________________________________________

It may take me a lifetime to figure out these two things.

The eternal love we once talked about,

and now your resolute departure.

____________________________________________________

The blind worship in our youth, is our worthless rashness.

Overflowing emotions are a kind of bewitchment, not harm that can be corrected.

____________________________________________________

Embrace yourself, feel the existence of loneliness, but still have to survive alone, stubbornly.

See through others, ignore those wounds, yet still walk blindly, carefree.

Remember the past, discard unworthy illusions, yet still can't step out of your own world, blindly.

Look forward to the future, adorn your own allure, yet still look back at the helplessness of the past, aimlessly.

____________________________________________________

Don't always talk about love. Love is very important. It can make people crazy.

But it's not all of life. It's not the focus of living.

When you truly grow up one day, you'll understand.

Love, it is actually a purely imagined world.

____________________________________________________

If time could flow backwards and we returned to the past, I would stand as a watcher, offering you devout feelings.

And not caring or doubting the nature of your feelings for me, like a blindly deaf person who hears nothing and asks nothing.

Only giving you fervent emotion to warm your lengthening nightly loneliness, would this be what you desire?

____________________________________________________

In youth, one really shouldn't lightly speak of love, putting all their strength into it,

only to find that loving and forgetting are both such simple matters.

____________________________________________________

In the end, I am still alone.

In the end, I am still used to being alone.

In the end, I am still an outsider.

____________________________________________________

It turns out that harm can be covered by happiness.

It turns out that letting go with words does not equal giving up in the heart.

It turns out there is already someone who makes you happy just by seeing them.

____________________________________________________

Things I once pursued with everything I had later became worthless. Matters my parents strongly opposed, I only understood when I got hurt, that they were right all along.

I asked myself countless times why I lived? Later, I became too lazy to think about the meaning of life.

I neglected my studies for love, but later I could abandon love for work.
