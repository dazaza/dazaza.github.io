Xiao He teaches you how to start an online store and make money in your spare time.

by anonymous on 2011-03-25 13:59:19

Friends who have just entered Taobao and want to open an online store on Taobao, if you don't know where to start, let me tell you. To open a store on Taobao, what do you need and what should you pay attention to? Xiao He is doing the Speed Recharge software agency QQ: 584824892

　　First, before opening the store:

　　A. Registration

　　Username:

The name is very important. A distinctive name can make others notice you and remember you more easily. How to be distinctive depends on different opinions. What is not recommended are overly long English names or completely meaningless strings of numbers. We are all Chinese, there are indeed some people who are good at English, but I believe that most buyers on Taobao are not overseas returnees with high education (if this point is wrong, please forgive me). For those who are not familiar with high-level English, a string of letters is like a string of meaningless numbers to me. Even after seeing it N times, I may not remember it.

Using my own name as an example:

wq_xiang_qian_chong (so straightforward, just for making money, what else do you expect?)

tao_yi_ju (selling clothes on Taobao, so simple)

Password: Not much to say here, you need to pay attention to security. The password is preferably a random combination of English letters, numbers, and symbols, and it's best to be 16 characters long. Become Alipay member: Alipay = Wealth Treasure, it's a great thing that will bring you various conveniences in your online transactions, and it's free. Free forever! When registering, remember to check the box (Become Alipay Member).

B. Apply for certification:

Just click a few times on the mouse. You need to wait patiently for a long time. It's best to use a digital camera or scanner to scan your ID card and upload it, then wait three working days. The certification result should be out by then. If for some reason you cannot pass the certification, Taobao customer service will contact you.

During this waiting period, what should you do? This is the time for market research and learning. Before opening a store, there are quite a few things you need to learn. In order to prevent encountering scammers and avoid troubles in the future, you should definitely finish this lesson well. Taobao University helps you solve the issues you need to consider in the future, including sourcing, pricing, delivery, after-sales service, etc. All these matters can be found in relevant knowledge on Taobao University. It's good to absorb some experience from predecessors.

Second, After passing the certification:

A. Sourcing:

Although you have passed the certification, you still cannot open a store yet. You need to maintain at least 10 items for sale. If you have considered what to sell and where to source, you can start. The essence posts you discovered in Taobao University come in handy here. If you encounter any problems, you can refer to them. You can also join a gang and find like-minded friends to gain experience.

If you don't have a physical store or a very good source of goods, Tao Yi still suggests you stock up with small and cheap items or virtual agents to support your shop. Most buyers on Taobao are students and young workers aged between 15 and 35. As long as you find the right product positioning, you should visit similar product stores more often, study high-level stores, see their sales situation, characteristics, decoration, and evaluation situation. It's best to achieve a deep understanding and ensure you have something unique.

B. Taking photos (for virtual items, no need, generally they have compressed packages, upload them directly, very simple)

After getting the items, how do you promote them? Taking a nice photo becomes necessary. I don't recommend using the photos provided by suppliers because sellers nowadays are smart, and the photos provided by suppliers are really fake. They are made to look very beautiful. It's better to use real photos and consider the buyer's perspective, add more detail pictures to show the seller's meticulousness.

There are many posts about taking and editing photos on Taobao University, you can go and learn. After taking good photos, you can adjust them to the best and most beautiful state with software, but the condition is not to lose authenticity. Over-processed photos can easily lose their true appearance, which might cause trouble in future transactions. Buyers may shout "the item does not match the picture" when they receive the goods. After taking good photos, you can put a light watermark on the photos and mark your store name (there are many watermark-making software available, I won't explain here, just search on Baidu). After opening the store, you should also mark your store address to prevent others from stealing your precious pictures. If you have marked it, and someone still steals your pictures, then this person is either crazy or stupid...(they are giving free publicity to your store)

C. Naming the items:

With beautiful photos, it's time to list them. The item name is crucial. Think from the buyer's perspective, what words would you use to search if you were a buyer? Besides coming up with a beautiful name, you need to pay attention to using common words. This is an important factor to ensure your items can be searched. Although it's said that strange names are easy to survive, no one will use strange names to search.

Also, the most important thing, never forget the direct name of the item. Don't describe a lot without stating what the item actually is. For example, necklaces, earrings, bracelets, sometimes made of crystal, the seller describes how good the crystal is, even puts the name of natural pink quartz, but forgets to mark necklace, earrings, bracelet. Then who can search it?

D. Description:

To sell beautiful items, their descriptions are indispensable. I used to win awards in art, and my composition was pretty good (hey, another little self-indulgence), I am very against seeing some item descriptions that use too many fonts, colors, and set many different font sizes. This not only lacks logic, makes people unable to find the key points, but also makes too large or too small fonts easily annoying. Adding colorful colors throughout the text is like Picasso's abstract paintings, full of oil paints, although it may not be ugly, few people understand it.

True descriptions should be logical, highlight the key points, maybe not too colorful, but convenient to read and make people feel comfortable. I suggest that when you write descriptions, use one to three colors at most, preferably no more than three. Songti font is the most suitable for reading, and it's best to use one size of font. Title fonts can be highlighted by color. When the description is messy, you can use tables to standardize it so that buyers can read it better.

Besides beauty, you also need to think about the content of the item description. If you have a graceful writing style, making the description tempting is certainly correct. Excellent salespeople attract buyers with eloquence, and we can also attract buyers with our writing. Remember, being unrealistic will bring you trouble. But if you don't have such good writing skills, remember not to write overly exaggerated nonsense.

That's all for now. The work of opening an online store on Taobao has just begun. Next, you need to decorate your store overall. This is a big project, so you need to do your homework well.
