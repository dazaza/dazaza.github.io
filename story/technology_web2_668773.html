GoDaddy Promotion Continues: COM Domain Registration Discounts Unaffected by Cost Increases

by yyy852456 on 2012-02-18 14:50:10

At the beginning of the year, VeriSign once again increased the registration fees for .COM and .NET domain names. The market expects that the domain name registration prices of most domain service providers will be affected, leading to significant price increases. However, the largest domain service provider, GoDaddy, continues to promote its .COM domain discount sales, and has not yet been affected by the increase in domain registration fees.

In January this year, VeriSign made some adjustments to the wholesale prices of .COM and .NET domains. The registration fee for .COM domains increased from $7.34 to $7.85, a rise of 6.9%. VeriSign's registration fees do not include ICANN's annual tax of $0.18. After adding the tax, the wholesale price of a .COM domain will rise to $8.03. After the increase in .COM domain prices, the industry speculated that large domain service providers would also raise the registration fees for .COM domains.

As the world's largest domain hosting service provider, GoDaddy did not follow the industry speculation and raise prices in response to the increase in .COM domain prices. Instead, it continued to promote its .COM domain discount sales activities. As of now, the registration price of .COM domains provided by GoDaddy has not been affected at all by the increase in wholesale prices. Users can still enjoy a special promotional price of $5.99 for registering a .COM domain through the discount website. This is very encouraging news for many domestic users who intend to register .COM domains.

Compared with the COM wholesale price given by VeriSign, GoDaddy's .COM promotional price is very attractive in the market. Despite the increase in wholesale prices, GoDaddy continues to steadily promote its discount policies. Such a rare discount opportunity is bound to attract a wide range of users, and GoDaddy will trigger a wave of .COM domain promotion.

With the promotion of .COM domain discounts, other related products of GoDaddy have also gained attention. The 30% discount on annual payment shared virtual hosting plans continues. Purchasing its 4GH annual payment hosting plan through the discount website allows for an additional 30% price discount on top of the original price. Additionally, the special preferential policy launched by GoDaddy Virtual Hosting Service Provider, where the VirtualDataCenter data center only requires $1 for the first month, has attracted considerable attention from users. GoDaddy has captured the constantly evolving market demands and places great importance on the expansion and deepening of cloud hosting. The industry speculates that cloud products may become the focus of GoDaddy's next phase of expansion and research.

From the perspective of the fluctuation in .COM domain prices, various factors may cause fluctuations in domain registration prices. Even as domain service providers continue to offer domain discounts despite rising registration costs, these promotions are likely time-limited. It is recommended that users in the industry who have a need for .COM domains take action early to register their domains and seize the best opportunity for domain discount promotions.
