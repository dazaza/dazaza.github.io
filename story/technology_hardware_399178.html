Tokyo Film King Qianyuan Wang supports the premiere of Fan Bingbing's new film "Guanyin Mountain"

by tadeyisheng on 2011-03-31 15:19:39

Tokyo Film Festival Emperor Wang Qianyuan Cheers for the Premiere of "Guanyin Mountain"

Wang Qianyuan with "Guanyin Mountain" director Li Yu

Wang Qianyuan being interviewed by the media

Baidu Entertainment News - Yesterday, Wang Qianyuan, Emperor of the 23rd Tokyo International Film Festival, was invited to attend the national premiere of "Guanyin Mountain" starring Fan Bingbing. Wang Qianyuan appeared in a handsome outfit, exuding the charm of a big star.

Fan Bingbing won the Empress title at the 23rd Tokyo International Film Festival with "Guanyin Mountain," and Wang Qianyuan, who became Emperor with the film "Steel Piano," also made an appearance at the red carpet event for "Guanyin Mountain" to support the film. During the interview segment, the host asked Wang Qianyuan if he had considered collaborating with Empress Fan Bingbing. Wang Qianyuan expressed that he looks forward to working with director Li Yu on his next project, but everything is still under discussion and has not been finalized yet.

In 2011, Wang Qianyuan has been busy with numerous projects and non-stop work engagements. The spy-themed TV series "I Am Real," which stars Wang Qianyuan, is currently popular in Hebei, Heilongjiang, and other regions. The classic red drama "Tunnel War," where he plays the heroic anti-Japanese fighter Gao Chuanbao, is also airing nationwide. Not long ago, Wang Qianyuan was invited to attend the 3rd Sydney Chinese Film Festival held in Sydney and collected the Jury Recommendation Award on behalf of the film "Steel Piano." This movie, starring Wang Qianyuan, is also expected to be released domestically later this year.

It is reported that Wang Qianyuan has confirmed his role as the male lead in the new series "Heroic Advance Detachment," and he will soon head to Xining for filming. (Text/ Lily Flower)
