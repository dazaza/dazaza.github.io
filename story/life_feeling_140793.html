Online audio recording of a conversation between post-90s men and women circulates on the Internet: A woman pursuing a man encounters sexual extortion

by sisi1212 on 2009-04-17 09:59:37

The post of a recording of a conversation between a 90s generation boy and girl on the well-known Foshan forum "Everyday New Forum" sparked heated discussion among netizens. In the dialogue, a girl professes her love to a boy, but unexpectedly the boy makes an unreasonable request: if you want to date, let's sleep together first. To the netizens' astonishment, knowing that the boy doesn't like her, knowing that the boy only wants a sexual relationship without love, after nearly four minutes of dialogue, she silently accepts his invitation.

After investigation by reporters, it turns out that this recording has already been circulating among middle school students' mobile phones in Foshan for many days, and has become a popular and shocking topic among middle school students in Foshan recently. Later, after being uploaded to the forum by netizens, it quickly became a hit online.

However, behind the popularity of the recording, it has caused widespread deep reflection in all sectors of society. How to correctly guide teenagers' sexual concepts has become the focus of everyone's discussion.
