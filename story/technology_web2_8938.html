HiPiHi - China's online virtual living platform

by hello on 2006-11-12 22:27:22

HiPiHi World is a 3D virtual world that is entirely owned by users and created in real-time.

The moment you step into this virtual world, you will be pleasantly surprised to find a group of residents with diverse appearances and full of creativity, entertaining, creating, and communicating in this magical new continent;

There are some basic rules in this virtual world. You can choose or create your own 3D avatar and social role; build homes, buildings, and commercial and entertainment venues on the land you own... You can drive or ride a helicopter, parachute through the mountains and rivers; communicate and entertain with residents from different corners of the real world, with different skin colors and races in bars, cafes, and beaches; you can also try and expand business models and forms that cannot be realized in the real world here, and create new business opportunities.

Residents in HiPiHi World have full ownership of their digital creations, whether they are character images, clothing, buildings, games... all kinds of digitized works and modes that people can imagine. They can buy and sell among themselves and make profits. Here, creation and exploration continue endlessly, jointly creating and enjoying a virtual world and life full of fun and infinite opportunities.

The economic activities in HiPiHi World will be supported by the trading system provided by the system. Residents can not only share the joy of creation but also realize the commercial benefits of creation.
