"Ultimate Three Kingdoms" Plot Summary:

Guan Yu and Zhang Fei are high school students, but due to their excessive love for chivalry, they have been expelled from twenty-four schools and are known as the "twenty-four schools." Just when the two feel desperate after being expelled again, a man with large earlobes suddenly appears. He introduces himself as Liu Bei, styled Xuande, a fallen noble who often changes schools because he can't afford the tuition fees. Liu Bei proposes a plan: since he has noble bloodlines, he can attend the best national noble school, "Donghan Academy," but it's too expensive, and only royal relatives can attend. Zhang Fei, wealthy and generous, says he can help pay everyone's tuition. Liu Bei replies that as long as the money issue is resolved, everything can be solved. He suggests the three of them swear brotherhood, making them all royal relatives! Thus, they can all enroll in the academy. The three then go to a beautiful and meaningful place to swear brotherhood—just after saying the last sentence, “We do not seek to be born on the same day, month, and year, but we wish to die on the same day, month, and year,” four people from the Iron Space-Time—Xiu, Wang Daxiong, Ding Xiaoyu, and Wang Yaser—unexpectedly appear through a space-time portal into the Silver Space-Time. Due to Wang Daxiong accidentally dropping a coin, which triggers a chain reaction causing a falling rock, the rock strikes Liu Bei, killing him! Guan Yu, committed to his promise, wants to follow his elder brother and die on the same day. Zhang Fei tries to stop him but fails, so he knocks Guan Yu unconscious. They soon discover Liu Bei isn't dead, just severely injured and unconscious. After consulting with Daxiong, Xiaoyu, and Yaser, they decide to leave Xiu, who looks similar to Liu Bei, in the Silver Space-Time as a substitute while the other three take the seriously wounded Liu Bei back to the Iron Space-Time for treatment. When Guan Yu wakes up, he finds that Xiu, pretending to be Liu Bei, is still alive, and becomes very happy. Zhang Fei is also glad that they don't have to die together. Thus, the three finally enter "Donghan Academy," where Guan Yu and Zhang Fei believe they can achieve great success. Their hope is to graduate from Donghan Academy, but due to its prominence, it attracts attacks from Gold University and Hedong University (where Dong Zhuo is the principal), putting Donghan Academy in constant danger. Even Master Zuo Ci's "Eight Gates Golden Lock Formation" was set up at the school gate to prevent students from attending classes. Naturally, the three take it upon themselves to break the formation. However, breaking the Eight Gates Golden Lock Formation is not easy. Moreover, their leader, Liu Bei Xiu, along with Xiao Qiao, are trapped in the formation, facing life-threatening situations. Therefore, Guan Yu and Zhang Fei, following the student council president Cao Cao's suggestion, find an extremely resilient and cheerful young man, Ma Chao, and Ma Chao's good friend, a hundred percent accurate marksman Huang Zhong, to help break the formation. Additionally, the always rivaling super handsome youth Zhao Yun joins in. Together, the five break the camp to rescue Liu Bei Xiu and Xiao Qiao. During the process of breaking the formation, Guan Yu saves Zhao Yun's life by shielding him with his arm in a critical moment, resolving the previous animosity between Guan and Zhang with Zhao Yun. From then on, Zhao Yun calls the other four brothers, becoming sworn brothers, and together they successfully break the Eight Gates Golden Lock Formation.

After breaking the formation and gaining new brothers, they thought they could smoothly finish high school, but on one side, Gold University covets Donghan Academy's resources, and on the other side, Dong Zhuo, the principal of Hedong University, has ambitions for the position of the principal of Donghan Academy, which houses the Young Lord of the "Same School Alliance." This means the Five Tigers and Liu Bei Xiu are destined not to live peaceful campus lives. A battle for world domination is about to begin...
