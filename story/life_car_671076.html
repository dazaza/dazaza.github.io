In recent years, with the increase in the number of vehicles, the incidence of car fires has also increased year on year. Car spontaneous combustion can be extremely hazardous, causing not only severe damage to vehicles but also posing a threat to human life. Since the onset of summer, there have been countless reports of car spontaneous combustion. In addition to several major bus self-combustion accidents, there are more news reports about the spontaneous combustion of mini-vans, sedans and other private cars.

The automatic vehicle-mounted fire extinguishing device is one of the effective means to combat car spontaneous combustion, installed in the engine compartment of the car. The root cause of car spontaneous combustion is often the ignition of the engine. The automatic vehicle-mounted fire extinguishing device can sense the fire information at the first moment of ignition, and spray ultra-fine dry powder fire extinguishing agent using the start-up device to suffocate and extinguish the flame. This effectively prevents the fire from getting bigger and minimizes losses. It is understood that in our country, buses and long-distance coaches in many provinces and cities are equipped with automatic vehicle-mounted fire extinguishing devices. However, fewer private cars are equipped with such fire extinguishing devices. The frequent news of private car spontaneous combustion makes us realize that the phenomenon of private car spontaneous combustion is also very serious. Why hasn't this kind of device become popular in private cars? For this reason, the editor contacted Director Yang, the relevant person in charge of Hubei Jiangnan Dongfeng Special Purpose Vehicles Co., Ltd., to talk about the market situation of vehicle-mounted fire extinguishing devices.

Director Yang introduced that currently, those who purchase and install automatic vehicle-mounted fire extinguishing devices are mainly bus and long-distance coach units. Very few private cars are equipped with such devices. Talking about the reasons, Director Yang mentioned a few points. First, buses and long-distance coaches have long usage times, generally running for more than ten hours without turning off, putting a heavy load on the vehicle's engine. Especially in hot summers, the probability of the engine catching fire due to overload is higher. Private cars, on the other hand, do not have such high usage rates, so their engines bear less load and the probability of catching fire is relatively lower. Second, buses and long-distance coaches carry more passengers, and once a spontaneous combustion accident occurs, it is more likely to cause major casualties with serious negative impacts. However, private cars carry fewer people, so even if spontaneous combustion occurs, there is usually sufficient time to escape. Third, price issues. For buses and long-distance coaches, the cost of equipping with automatic vehicle-mounted fire extinguishing devices is borne by the company, while for private cars, the cost must be borne by the car owner. Fourth, the influence of national policies. The state stipulates that buses and coaches must be equipped with corresponding fire extinguishing equipment. For private cars, however, it is merely required during vehicle inspections to have a handheld fire extinguisher. Many car owners believe that having a handheld fire extinguisher is enough, and more people take a侥幸attitude, thinking that spontaneous combustion will never happen to them. Finally, Director Yang believes that there hasn't been enough publicity. Many car owners don't even know what an automatic vehicle-mounted fire extinguishing device is, let alone spend money to buy one.

Although the probability of private cars spontaneously combusting is smaller, from media reports, the absolute number of private car spontaneous combustion incidents is still quite large. Many private car owners choose to purchase spontaneous combustion insurance to reduce losses, but if they could prevent problems before they occur and nip spontaneous combustion in the bud, they can achieve the goal of minimizing losses while avoiding a lot of trouble and worries.
