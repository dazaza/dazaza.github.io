When I fell from the mountain, Tian You tried to hold onto the rope around my waist, but the inertia was too great, and he was pulled down as well. When we touched the accumulated snow on the ground, I lost consciousness. Vaguely, I felt Tian You carrying me into this small cave. The radio was broken, I didn't know how much time had passed, nor did I know if we would be rescued. My legs might have been broken, for I had no sensation in them. Tian You only suffered minor abrasions. He kept taking care of me. Occasionally, when I woke up, seeing his face and his gentle eyes made me feel a little better, even though he also looked worried.

"Tian You, do you think others will find us?"

He smiled tiredly, "Hui Hui, don't think about it so much. Are you still in pain?"

"My feet still have no sensation, just feeling very cold."

Tian You moved closer, holding me in his arms. "Do you feel better now?"

"Mm." Maybe it was just a psychological effect, but I did feel a bit warmer.

"Hui Hui, I have some good news and some bad news. Which one do you want to hear first?"

"The bad news."

"Our food is almost gone, there's only one bar of chocolate left."

"What's the good news then?"

"The avalanche has sealed most of the cave entrance, but air can still come in. We can probably crawl out with some effort."

"This is what you call good news! We're going to die here!" Tears streamed down my face.

"Stupid, it's heaven's way of letting us enjoy our private world," Tian You laughed.

"You still have the mood to joke around."

He gently patted my back, hugging me in his arms.

"Hui Hui, when we get back, will you marry me?"

"Will we even get back?"

"Of course! We've been together for so long, and I've never formally proposed to you. Maybe it's not the right time, but I think you won't refuse me at this moment. So, will you marry me?"

I cried, but these were tears of happiness.

"Yes, Tian You, I will marry you."

"Good girl, for that day, you must not give up easily. Live well, will you?"

"Yes," I nodded hard, but suddenly felt dizzy.

"Alright, go to sleep, I'll wake you up."

"Tian You, my feet still have no sensation."

Tian You crawled to my feet. "Can you feel me pinching you?"

"No."

"How about now?"

"Still no."

"Like this?"

"No."

Tian You smiled, "Hui Hui, your feet are just tired from climbing, they need rest..."

"If... no matter what you become, I still want you!"

"Tian You..."

"Alright, Hui Hui, go to sleep, close your eyes."

I fell asleep.

When I was shaken awake again, my dizziness was worse. Tian You was waving the chocolate in front of me.

"Hui Hui, it's time to eat."

"You eat it, I'm not hungry."

"No, good girl, listen."

"But my throat is too dry to swallow anything."

Tian You thought for a moment, broke off a small piece of chocolate, put it in his mouth for a while, then kissed me.

The melted hot chocolate flowed from his lips into my dry mouth. Although my taste buds were numb, I could vaguely sense a hint of sweetness. Tian You then melted a small piece of snow with his mouth and fed it to me. One bite of chocolate, one bite of snow, Tian You managed to feed two small pieces of the chocolate bar into my body. My stomach faintly warmed up, but my head spun more.

"Tian You, aren't you eating?"

He put the remaining chocolate away.

"I've already eaten when I fed you."

"But you gave it all to me!"

"I still got a little bit in, aren't you going to fight me for that too? That's greedy, huh. Hehe."

I gripped Tian You's hand tightly, "You're really good to me."

"So you must live well, good girl, go back to sleep."

"Mm."

My head was spinning badly.

In this way, Tian You fed me the entire chocolate bar, but he didn't eat any himself. When I asked him, he said there were mountain rats in the cave, and he had caught two which he ate entirely, skin and all, which kept him energetic.

I knew he was lying to me, but his energy was indeed quite good, maybe I was just too weak. I was so dizzy I barely had the strength to speak.

Then, we heard the roar of a helicopter. Tian You tried his best to lean out and say something, but he was too weak, I couldn't even understand what he shouted. The roaring sound faded away.

"Hui Hui, wake up, Hui Hui."

I tried to open my eyes.

"Did you hear that? The helicopter went back to fetch rescue equipment."

"Are we finally saved?"

Tian You smiled.

"Sorry, Hui Hui, they can only take one person. So, I am the one being saved."

I didn't immediately understand what he was saying.

Tian You seemed to see my suspicious expression.

"I told them that there's only me here. Thank you, Hui Hui, without you, I wouldn't have made it this far."

I still didn't fully grasp what he was saying, but I saw him repeatedly stabbing my abdomen with a Swiss Army knife.

"Hui Hui, you know, the supplies are insufficient. I didn't know when we would be rescued, so you don't need to thank me for giving you all the food. If you hadn't survived, I wouldn't have had any food or hope of survival. But now since I've been saved, your mission is over. Thank you." Tian You inserted the Swiss Army knife one last time deeply into my body, slowly rotating it. He kissed my forehead. "Hui Hui, I'll marry you again in the next life. Farewell, I love you." After saying that, he pulled out the knife and crawled out.

I gathered my last strength to lift the sleeping bag covering the lower half of my body.

Below my knees, there was nothing but a pile of bloody white bones.
