Li Guoqing exposed the author of Morgan Stanley's apology letter, demanding resignation and then taking it back

by edivista on 2011-01-26 08:15:27

Go to Weibo website. Note: Li Guoqing's Weibo address: http://t.sina.com.cn/1878923963 Retweet:

Dangdang.com CEO Li Guoqing again attacked Morgan Stanley on his Weibo yesterday, claiming that although the general manager of the other party had already apologized to him in writing, he required the other party to resign for cause. By the time the reporter went to press, Li Guoqing had retracted his demand for resignation on Weibo.

Dangdang.com Chairman Yu Yu revealed in an interview with our newspaper this Monday that employees of Morgan Stanley have apologized orally and in writing to Dangdang.com for the low IPO pricing of Dangdang.com. However, she did not disclose the position of the apologizing employee or the specific content of the apology letter.

Yesterday, Li Guoqing expressed on his personal Weibo: The managing director (MD) of Morgan Stanley's investment banking department/product department sent me a written apology letter. But I require not only an apology from the managing director (MD) of the pricing department, but also resignation for cause. It is understood that MD is the abbreviation for Managing Director, the position of general manager in investment banking. Li Guoqing then explained that the big MD is the manager of MDs, and MD is the manager of vice presidents (VP).

As the identity of the female from Morgan Stanley who insulted Li Guoqing was denied by both Dangdang.com and Morgan Stanley, and Dangdang.com claimed that Morgan Stanley had apologized to them, the heat of the event between Li Guoqing and the investment bank seemed to be decreasing. However, Yu Yu said in an interview with our newspaper this Monday that it was unknown whether the issue of stock price underestimation could end here. The next day, Li Guoqing immediately issued a Weibo demanding the resignation of Morgan Stanley's big MD. But by the time the reporter went to press, Li Guoqing had issued another Weibo to the CEO of Morgan Stanley, saying that due to many comments opposing his action, he withdrew the demand for resignation, and he also said: "Respected CEO of Morgan Stanley: You must retain the big MD of the pricing group; CORF ah, you must not resign, otherwise I will be scolded by kind compatriots, how painful my life would be. If you leave Morgan Stanley, it will punish my arrogance, and I will sue you for it."
