In ancient times, there were stories like "reading at night by candlelight" or even "stealing light by making a hole in the wall," all because of the underdeveloped technology back then. After the invention of electric lights, nighttime reading became an effortless task, but it still wasn't perfect. Unless you're single, any type of lighting you use will affect others around you in the same room, such as spouses or roommates. The focused light from this flatbed reading lamp is contained and does not spread outward; it only illuminates the open book pages without disturbing the environment. This is truly a product of modern high technology with human-centered design. Equipped with LED white light, it provides uniform brightness that is easy on the eyes and gently protects your vision. Lightweight, thin, portable, and with adjustable brightness, it is the ideal tool for reading in the evening, camping, traveling on overnight transportation, or for middle and high school students reviewing before exams.

Features & Advantages:

1. Does Not Disturb Others: A completely new lighting structure design, featuring flatbed illumination with precisely designed angles so that the light only shines on the reading area. It is especially suitable for reading in bed without affecting others' rest. An innovative light-blocking panel design ensures no stray light leaks out, and the darker the reading environment, the better the effect.

2. Protects Vision: A more scientific optical design with a light wavelength close to natural light. The slightly yellowish light creates a warm ambiance, making it more suitable for prolonged reading without causing eye fatigue. The brightness can be infinitely adjusted, with the initial brightness already suitable for reading (correcting the old model's issue of having a large range of useless brightness). A specially designed ultra-brightness mode can be used for close-range lighting when students walk at night.
