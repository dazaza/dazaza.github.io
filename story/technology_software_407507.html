Cuisine style: Western cuisine buffet

Address: 8th floor, Zhongguo Hualian Building, No. 219, Zhongshan Fifth Road, Yuexiu District Tel: 83278527

Nine, Store Name: Yijing Western Restaurant (Tianhe Store)

Per Capita: ￥70

Cuisine Style: Western Cuisine Buffet

Address: Room 704, 7th Floor, Tianhecheng Plaza, No. 208, Tianhe Road, Tianhe District Tel: 85592818-37086 85592818-37088

Introduction: At Yijing, there are two chefs with skin tones different from ours, they are from India, making Indian flatbread, which is a food highlight of Yijing Western Restaurant. Making this Indian flatbread uses a beautiful motion. First, the master will knead some dough, those doughs all have a bright luster. The master first flattens the dough, then continuously turns the dough in his hand. After a few turns, the dough becomes a flatbread. Then, just like when we make buns, fill the flattened dough with filling, then fry it, divide it into several pieces, and that's the Indian flatbread. According to the type of filling, the flatbread has different tastes, among which, there are salty scallion flatbreads, sweet banana flatbreads. The most Indian-flavored ones are curry vegetable flatbreads. The curry taste is very authentic, but it's still quite spicy. The banana flatbread, on the other hand, is sweet and fragrant upon entry, accompanied by soft and fluffy bread, the taste is indeed good.
