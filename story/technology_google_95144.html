Google Toolbar 5 For FireFox Beta is released

by rikulu on 2008-09-26 16:29:38

Google Toolbar 5 for IE has been released for quite a while, and now the beta version of Toolbar 5 that supports Firefox has also been officially launched. Similar to the functions in the IE version, Google Toolbar 5 for Firefox also supports Google Gadgets, integrates with Google Notebook, and can synchronize Toolbar settings data to a Google account, etc.
