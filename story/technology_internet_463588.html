Man was bitten when he tried to take advantage of a female internet friend at home

by zzfhhxy11 on 2011-07-15 16:55:06

Chongqing - A man lured a female Internet acquaintance to his home on the night of October 24 and attempted to force himself on her. The determined woman, after failing to get help over the phone, bit the man and fled. At around 1:00 a.m. the following day, policemen from the Hongsheng Hotel patrol police platform in Nan'an District, who had received an emergency call, arrested the man at his home. Yesterday, a reporter learned from the Nan'an District People's Procuratorate that the man, suspected of attempted rape, had been formally approved for arrest.

The suspect, surnamed Xiang, is 27 years old. Not long ago, he met a female Internet friend named Xiao Yan (a pseudonym). Around 6:00 p.m. on October 24, Xiang invited Xiao Yan out for dinner. Although she had never met Xiang before, Xiao Yan went alone to the appointment. Around 11:00 p.m., Xiang invited Xiao Yan back to his place for a visit. Xiao Yan refused, but Xiang cajoled and tricked Xiao Yan into going back to his home located at the Three Kilometers Halfway area in Nan'an District. During this time, Xiao Yan pretended to call a friend, claiming that she had something come up and would leave early, but Xiang ignored her completely. As soon as they entered the house, Xiang pushed Xiao Yan down onto the bed. Realizing that Xiang was about to assault her, the quick-witted Xiao Yan took out her mobile phone and dialed 110. However, before the call connected, Xiang snatched the phone away.

Xiang continued his assault, and the resolute Xiao Yan resisted with all her might and shouted for help. In the struggle, Xiao Yan was beaten all over her body, but she managed to bite Xiang and escape, thwarting his attempt. Xiao Yan ran to the Hongsheng Hotel patrol police platform in Nan'an District to report the incident, and the policemen arrested Xiang, who was still at home, around 1:00 a.m. the next morning.
