However, due to the inability to secure tens of millions of yuan in registered capital, and the desired company name being already taken by others, later through a friend's introduction, he established the group company in the Cayman Islands [http://www.rf.hk/company/cayman/introduction.html], while also establishing holding subsidiaries in Hong Kong, Samoa [http://www.rf.hk/company/samoa/13235.html], the Bahamas, and mainland China, becoming a true multinational group. 2. [Import/Export] Ms. Du from Hefei: Long-term engagement in international import trade. Once, a French chemical raw material supplier refused to sign a contract with her and insisted that she entrust an overseas intermediary company to sign the contract and issue a letter of credit [http://www.rf.hk/exp/xinyongzheng/], citing the high credibility and reliable payment of overseas intermediary companies. Due to the long-term nature of this business, Ms. Du had no choice but to establish a company in Delaware, USA [http://www.rf.hk/company/usa/], opening an offshore account for the Delaware company at a foreign bank in Shanghai (this account possesses all settlement functions required in international trade). This enabled her to establish a long-term good cooperative relationship with the client.

3. [Trade Settlement] Mr. Jin from Tianjin: Engaged in international textile product export trade, he had always relied on middle agents for exports but was concerned about business secrets and settlement security. To ensure long-term stability and avoid exchange rate differential losses, he wanted to register an overseas offshore company [http://www.rf.hk/company/offshore/], independently export, and directly settle payments, provided that the offshore company did not require cumbersome financial reporting procedures. With the help of Ruifeng professionals, Mr. Jin registered a BVI offshore company in the British Virgin Islands [http://www.rf.hk/company/bvi/bvijr.html] (this company does not need to provide annual financial reports to the government), while simultaneously opening an offshore account at a foreign bank in Beijing. This fully met Mr. Jin's offshore trade planning requirements and allowed him greater international development.

5.
