Baijiu ad植入rejects drunk driving slogan, traffic police say it is hung without permission

by nim203pqms on 2012-02-24 14:23:34

The Greatest Alcohol Advertisement Ever in History (Picture from the Internet) "The greatest embedded advertisement ever" has been removed, and the Yibin traffic police will sue the ad company. Due to the refusal of drunk driving promotional slogans by traffic police being embedded with a baijiu advertisement, it caused a heated online debate. Surrounding this post, netizens have made jokes, and the banner was mockingly referred to as "the greatest embedded advertisement" by netizens. In the afternoon of March 3, the Yibin Public Security Traffic Police Detachment publicly responded, stating that the banner was hung without permission from the detachment by an ad company, and expressed that they would sue the ad company, using legal means to protect the rights and interests of the traffic police detachment. The online post: alcohol advertisements embedded in traffic police promotions. On the last day of the previous month, Yibin netizen "Xiao Yu" posted on a local online forum, claiming that two traffic police promotional banners appeared on the outer wall of the road at the former satellite observation station in Nanan, Yibin, where a certain brand of baijiu advertisement was embedded. From the pictures posted, there were two red banners with white letters hanging on the outer wall of the road, respectively printed with the promotional slogans "Traffic Police Warm Reminder: Refuse Driving Under the Influence" and "Set Off with Peace, Return with Happiness." On the left side of both banners were advertisements for a certain brand of baijiu, and in the lower right corner of the banners were printed: Publicized by the Yibin Municipal Public Security Bureau Traffic Police Detachment. Once the post was released, it sparked much attention from many netizens, and was quickly forwarded to various major online forums and communities such as Dacheng Net. Some netizens, with a teasing attitude, thought that "on one side is refusing drunk driving, on the other side is alcohol advertising, too perfect!" Other netizens expressed confusion about the embedded advertisements in the promotional slogans, questioning whether the traffic police had received sponsorship fees, and mockingly referring to it as "the greatest embedded advertisement." In the afternoon of March 3, when the reporter went to the former satellite observation station in Nanan mentioned in the online post, the banners on the exterior walls had already been removed. "It's been removed for several days." A nearby citizen said. The traffic police have already filed a lawsuit against the ad company. "We have no idea what the situation is." Xiao Yong from the Publicity Department of the Yibin Traffic Police Detachment introduced that he saw the post and pictures online by chance. Subsequently, the traffic police detachment conducted an investigation into the pictures in the post. After the investigation, it was found that the banners mentioned in the online post were made without permission by a certain ad company in Yibin, and no prior verbal or written commission or permission was obtained from the traffic police detachment and its direct subordinate No. 2 Detachment. "We don't even know which ad company it is," Xiao Yong said. Subsequently, the reporter contacted the person in charge of the ad company, who stated that the banner was indeed hung by the company without the permission of the traffic police detachment. Xiao Yong expressed that in this incident, the traffic police took the "black pot" for the ad company. In the afternoon of March 3, the Yibin Traffic Police Detachment publicly responded to the matter, stating that they have filed a lawsuit with the Cuiping District People's Court of Yibin City regarding the behavior of the ad company, and will protect the rights and interests of the detachment through legal means.
