How Apparel Distributors Promote to Achieve Performance

by shenwu130s on 2012-02-14 11:01:04

Currently, many clothing distributors in ordinary prefecture-level cities and county-level markets often exhibit two states: First, they frequently lament the difficulty of doing business and poor performance; Second, they sit in their stores every day waiting for customers without thinking about how to attract customers into the store to consume. Apparently, they want the horse to run fast but don't want to feed it grass. This kind of "good thing" does not exist. Therefore, if clothing distributors want to achieve booming sales performance, they must change the traditional practice of "sitting in the store waiting for customers" and should instead consider how to attract more target consumers into the store. Of course, this also requires clothing distributors to make a certain investment, such as through large-scale theme marketing activities and high-quality brand promotion systems, which can quickly make the store crowded with people, rapidly achieving booming sales performance.

So, how can clothing distributors quickly establish large-scale theme marketing activities and their brand promotion systems while controlling input costs, ultimately achieving booming sales performance throughout the year? Due to the limitation of space, this article focuses on explaining how a large number of clothing distributors can operate large-scale theme marketing activities to improve their brand promotion systems, thereby achieving the following two goals: First, quickly enhancing the brand's awareness and laying a solid foundation for future good sales performance; Second, effectively promoting the rapid realization of booming sales performance in large-scale theme marketing activities. Shen Haizhong, the main speaker of the "Low-Cost Booming Sales Special Training Camp for Distributors," suggests the following:

1. Low-cost operations, multi-dimensional promotions. Considering that the majority of clothing distributors have limited financial resources, plus Shen Haizhong's uniquely created large-scale theme marketing activities, which are carried out and succeed under cost control, clothing distributors should scientifically select communication carriers when conducting promotional work to ensure both low price and high quality, i.e., low-cost operation. Secondly, various communication carriers should simultaneously conduct promotional work rather than choosing only one carrier for promotion or ending one carrier's promotion before selecting another. Scientifically selecting communication carriers and carrying out multi-dimensional promotions is necessary to achieve the greatest promotional effect.

2. Brand promotional flyers are the main carrier. Brand promotional flyers are one of the best promotional carriers but are often overlooked by many clothing distributors, who may even think that flyers have no effect. Clearly, this is because most clothing distributors have not handled the content, creativity, design, printing, and distribution of promotional flyers properly, leading to poor results. Conversely, if these aspects are handled well, brand promotional flyers will unleash an exceptionally strong promotional force, boosting clothing distributors to quickly achieve excellent or even booming sales performance. For this reason, Shen Haizhong, the founder of Distributor Network, will focus on the creation and distribution of brand promotional flyers in the next two articles, so that clothing distributors will fall in love with brand promotional flyers because they are both inexpensive and highly effective.

3. Brand flower car promotion is the promotional superstar. It is true that brand flower car promotion is the superstar of market promotion. Relatively speaking, brand promotional flyers are weak and static, while brand flower cars are huge, dynamic, flowing, and noisy, making them quickly familiar to a wide range of consumers. This is because brand flower cars are constantly moving and equipped with loudspeakers, allowing people to hear them from dozens of meters away, which also enhances the effectiveness of brand flyers' distribution. Therefore, when clothing distributors conduct promotional work, they must do a good job in brand flower car promotion work, especially in flower car body advertising design and audio advertising creation, which are key points requiring proper handling.
