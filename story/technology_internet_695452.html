Number of Examinations and Question-setting Units

Higher education self-study exams are held four times a year (the number of exams in each province or city is decided by the provincial examination office), with test dates in January, April, July, and October. The national examination committee coordinates the question-setting process, which can be done through three methods: nationwide unified question-setting, regional question-setting, and provincial question-setting.

Where to Find Exam Questions and Answers

Exam questions, reference answers, and grading standards are classified as top-secret materials before they are used, and remain state secrets even after their use. Since 2002, the National Examination Office no longer publishes answers. Huaxia Earth Education Network launched over a thousand exam questions and related internal answers for candidates' reference in December 2005. Candidates can also obtain more analysis of past exam questions by purchasing relevant tutoring books.

Which Majors Cannot Be Applied For

Applicants can voluntarily choose their examination major within the range of majors offered in their region. However, majors with occupational restrictions only accept departmental unified recruitment (such as public security systems, medical institutions, etc.).

The Effectiveness of Self-study Examination Graduation Certificates

The "People's Republic of China Higher Education Law" clearly stipulates: The state implements a higher education self-study examination system, and those who pass the examination will be awarded corresponding academic certificates. The Provisional Regulations of Higher Education Self-examination Examination issued by the State Council clearly states that [the treatment of graduates with higher education self-examination examination graduation certificates is the same as that of graduates from regular colleges and universities], allowing them to pursue postgraduate studies, study abroad, etc. Universities in Australia and the UK recognize these qualifications, and more than 20 countries worldwide acknowledge self-study examination diplomas.
