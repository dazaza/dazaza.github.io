Dry my sadness

I thought I was cold-blooded enough, thought I would not give anymore, thought my will was strong enough, thought I would have no regrets in this life, thought I could resist all the invasion of love. But God still would not let me go, and let me meet you, when I had no illusion and yearning for love, it brought you to my side, making me feel the stirring deep in my heart again.

I remember avoiding your confession again and again, pretending, even speaking ill words, but your persistence, your innocence, your selflessness made me crumble again and again. Every time you ran away crying with sorrow, every time you shouted at me "I hate you", every time you appeared before me, I really wanted to keep you, let the time only stay at this moment. But I did not dare to tell you, because I lied to myself, telling myself that my love has been buried and would not wake up again. Until that night, at the moment you turned away, I wanted to reach out and pull you back into my arms, and tell you, I love you, it has already started a long time ago. But the cowardly me still let go, watching you disappear from my sight with others. Someone told me that you were just angry with me, in fact, you deliberately let me see his existence because you loved me. But I still thought that you found happiness, found the peace and tranquility you wanted. At least he is not like me, so incomplete, can't even love completely, full of sadness everywhere, he can make you happy, believe that your love for him will be deeper, stronger and more complete than for me.

I do not know if you are happy now, but I am still selfishly hoping for your appearance, hoping you to care as before, even if it is just between friends. Is your curse coming true? At the moment of leaving, with tears on your face, sobbing and saying, I will make you regret in this life, you will regret letting me go now. At that time, I still looked at you indifferently, thinking that you would be like other girls who appeared in my life, soon become a thing of the past, but imagination is always just imagination, reality is always too different from it.

At the moment of turning around, my love was destroyed forever, and scattered with the wind along with you...
