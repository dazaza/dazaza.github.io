Si Haijian_How to turn a product into a high-end brand

by stonepx918 on 2012-03-02 08:59:20

The consumption concept of people is also beginning to change. In recent years, food safety incidents have been frequently occurring. The quality and safety of products have become the first reason for consumers to make a purchase. Especially for high-end brand consumption, brand consumption has already become the inevitable trend of food industry development, and management consulting companies will become the mainstream of the times' development. According to relevant surveys by Shanghai Management Consulting Company, high-end brands give people the feeling of luxury goods. Generally speaking, but relative to the food industry, it can be considered as quasi-luxury goods at most. It's like Tetra Suzhou and Jin Dian compared to ordinary milk; De Qingyuan compared to ordinary eggs. But it hasn't exceeded the consumer acceptance range. The price is several times more expensive than ordinary products. But how to make consumers willing to buy products that are several times more expensive? What kind of product do consumers consider as a high-end product? How exactly is a high-end product made? A simple price increase cannot create a high-end product; it needs systematic shaping. High-end products are not just simply changing packaging. The cornerstone of high-end products is outstanding product quality. This is the external manifestation of high-end products. High-end products are priced high and have high profit margins. In essence, the product quality must be relatively high; otherwise, it would be like Walmart selling counterfeit organic pork. Without solid product quality, there can be no talk of brand, let alone high-end. Building a high-concept image isn't empty talk; it must be based on facts, choosing the strongest value support.

High-end product support: excavating value. De Qingyuan sells for 1.2 yuan. Why do many consumers rush to buy such expensive eggs? Because consumers buy safe ecological eggs, loose eggs sell for 40 cents each. This is the premium space brought by high-end value to De Qingyuan eggs. Shaping value is about finding differences, forming unique characteristics, and creating a strong support point for high-end products. Through the operational ideas of some high-end products in the market and my previous service experience, the supporting points of high-end brand value can be deeply explored from eight key points.

Many local products are affected by unique soil, climate, water quality, etc., which causes their unique taste, nutritional value, or other features. For example, Shanxi aged vinegar, Xinjiang Hetian jujubes, Yangcheng Lake hairy crabs, etc. Origin support: one land produces one famous product. If this unique origin advantage can be transformed into a brand advantage, it will greatly enhance the brand height and premium capacity of the product. Mengniu "Te楞Su" has achieved this by using origin as its most advantageous support point, creating the core area of the dairy capital in Helinger. "Where is Helinger? No one paid attention before. But after Mengniu's education, it became the concept of an excellent production area, not only successfully countering competitors but also making Helinger instantly become the paradise of dairy products in consumers' minds.

Every local specialty product has a story, like Qinzhou Yellow Millet. Cultural support: China's five thousand years of brilliant culture. Due to Emperor Kangxi's extreme love, it was regarded as the imperial court's millet, royal tribute rice, and bestowed as the "Top Four Famous Rice." One could say that Emperor Kangxi is the best image spokesperson for Qinzhou Yellow Millet. By cleverly integrating these cultural and historical stories into the product, on the one hand, it meets the consumer's pursuit of high-class culture, and on the other hand, fully interprets and packages the product's cultural heritage, integrating it into the product to highlight the brand's cultural atmosphere. Seizing the high ground of cultural characteristics, thickening the brand's value and premium capacity, is another important method to build a high-end brand.

From raw materials to processing to waste, standard support: for general products, the difference points are not obvious, and consumers cannot perceive them. The highest realm of branding is setting standards, and companies that set standards often have the most say in the industry. What is standardization? Green? Pollution-free? Organic? Standardization is the high ground of competition. Lack of standardization means leading enterprises need to establish their own standards, take the lead in the industry, actively guide and popularize standards, and promote the establishment of industry standards by administrative authorities, occupying the high ground, making the whole industry follow, and establishing discourse power in the consumer's mind. Like De Qingyuan established the standard for good eggs: "Eat green corn, drink mountain spring water"; Nongfu Spring established the standard for natural water: "Natural source Thousand Island Lake, naturally weakly alkaline, produced at the source."

Especially for many traditional techniques, ancestral secret methods, process support: distinctive processing techniques can effectively increase product added value. If you can possess "special techniques" and incorporate the concept of technology with simple language, it will become the most competitive support point of the brand. Luhua has always focused on "5S physical pressing," effectively distinguishing itself from the immersion process, and the price is much higher. Wujiang pickles refined the advanced Fuling pickle-making method, preempting the "three cleanings and three washes" and "three marinating and three squeezing" processes, becoming the representative of authentic Fuling pickles.

Time and again, they provoke consumers' sensitivity to food safety. Safety support: Sanlu milk powder, Sudan red, plasticizers, lean meat powder, dyed steamed buns, and other food safety incidents have made safety and health one of the most concerned topics in society. It is one of the most important means for products to build brands, values, and high-end status, and it is also an indispensable important means. Why does KFC's "Anxin" oil sticks dare to price at three yuan? Cultivate the internal skills of safety and health, make consumers eat with peace of mind, and this is the ace behind the high-priced oil stick! In China's vermicelli market, there is a lack of brand awareness and industry standards, scattered packaging, low prices, and frequent quality problems. Xiangdao heavily promotes safety, uses food safety as a niche point, selects only the first batch of sweet potato original pulp, goes through the entire process of ecological ingredients, fine screening, standardized production, and strict testing, providing four levels of safety assurance, successfully establishing an industry benchmark.
