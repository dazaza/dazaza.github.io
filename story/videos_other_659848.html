Husband invents wife's adultery and is forced to drink urine, leaving two people seriously injured during the resistance

by hj1718004 on 2012-02-06 22:16:28

China News Service, Guiport, May 11 (Tan Jinheng and Wang Gang) - A 57-year-old man in Guigang City, Guangxi Province, was not only severely beaten after he loudly scolded his wife and her "lover" for their affair, but he was also forcibly held down by the two of them and forced to drink pig urine. After breaking free, the man immediately pulled out a cleaver, heavily injuring the two before fleeing. On May 11, the Guigang police reported that under telephone persuasion from the police, the surnamed Mo man had turned himself in, and is currently under criminal detention.

The Gangbei Branch of the Guigang Public Security Bureau disclosed that the 57-year-old Mo is from Qiaowu Town, Gangnan District, Guigang City. His wife Wei is the same age as him. Mo has a skill in livestock breeding, and more than ten years ago, he and his wife rented land to raise pigs. In late 2009, Mo returned to his rural hometown to take care of his seriously ill father, leaving the pig farm to be managed by his wife and daughter. Not long after, Mo heard rumors about an ambiguous relationship between his wife and a sixty-year-old man named Xiao. The two of them argued multiple times over this issue, causing cracks in their relationship. Afterwards, his wife no longer returned to their home in Qiaowu Town and refused to give Mo money to treat his father, rendering their marital relationship in name only.

At around 10 o'clock on the morning of May 9th this year, Mo came to the pig farm, only to unexpectedly see his wife Wei and her rumored lover Xiao driving pigs inside the pig farm, which made him extremely angry. Since his father's treatment urgently needed money, Mo asked his wife for money again. His wife's attitude was very cold, ignoring him while continuing to drive the pigs with Xiao. This caused Mo to fiercely argue with his wife, eventually leading to him loudly cursing his wife Wei for secretly having an affair with Xiao behind his back.

After hearing this, Xiao became very angry and began arguing with Mo alongside Wei, escalating into a physical fight. Due to Xiao's large and robust build, they quickly overpowered Mo, throwing him into a pig urine pool next to the pigpen and riding on top of him, hitting him indiscriminately, and forcefully pressing Mo's head into the pig urine pool. Mo was "drowned" in the urine pool several times, ultimately managing to break free with great effort. Thinking about the humiliation and beating he endured, Mo pulled out a pig-killing knife from his bag, wildly slashing at his wife Wei and Xiao without any hesitation, causing Wei's right hand to be severely injured and receiving four stab wounds on her back. Xiao suffered a stab wound to the abdomen, with his intestines exposed. Mo then immediately fled the scene.

Upon seeing this, the villagers immediately called the police, and officers from the Gangcheng Police Station of the Guigang Public Security Bureau swiftly arrived at the crime scene. The officers analyzed that since the murderer's clothes were dirty and wet, and reeked, he should not have gone too far. Thus, the police called Mo's mobile phone, persuading him to turn himself in. After more than ten minutes of sincere persuasion from the officers, Mo finally put down his mental burden and agreed to surrender. At around 11 o'clock on the same day, Mo surrendered himself accompanied by a friend.
