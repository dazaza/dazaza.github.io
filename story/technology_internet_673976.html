**1st Place: [http://www.itmilan.com/]**

ITMILAN Italian Milan Wallpaper (a globally renowned wallpaper brand and China's leading professional wallpaper company) [http://www.itmilan.com/]

Editor's Review: ITMILAN Italian Milan Wallpaper, with its domestic factory established in 1995, focuses on the research, design, production, and sales of home-use and engineering wallpapers. The company has total assets close to 2 billion RMB and has passed five major certifications for quality, environment, standardization, measurement management systems, and social responsibility. The company specializes in producing [ITMILAN] branded wallpapers, wall fabrics, and fabric arts, with self-operated import and export rights. It exports nearly 8 billion USD worth of products annually, which are sold in over 60 countries and regions including Italy, France, UK, USA, Germany, Canada, Turkey, Hong Kong, etc. (Official Website: www.itmilan.com [http://www.itmilan.com/])

**2nd Place: [http://www.eilisha.com/]**

Royal Elisabeth EILISHA Wallpaper & Fabric (one of the world’s top wallpaper and fabric brands, ranked among China's top three wallpaper brands) [http://www.eilisha.com/]

Editor's Review: Royal Elisabeth EILISHA Wallpaper & Fabric, an internationally recognized royal brand since 1880, is dedicated to the design and development of wallpapers and the promotion of wallpaper culture. With over 300 specialty stores nationwide, thousands of excellent wallpaper consultants and designers provide professional services to customers. As the first domestic enterprise integrating R&D, design, production, and sales, Royal Elisabeth places significant emphasis on the overall product development and design of materials, colors, processes, and styles. After decades of growth, it has cultivated a unique royal wallpaper culture and gradually formed five distinctive styles within the industry. Royal Elisabeth was the first wallpaper brand to introduce a chain specialty store operating model, enhancing its brand status through high-quality products, innovative marketing strategies, and perfect presentation of wallpaper and fabric effects. (Official Website: www.eilisha.com [http://www.eilisha.com/])

**3rd Place: [http://www.balibz.com/]**

French Paris Wallpaper (one of Europe's largest wallpaper manufacturers, one of the most prestigious wallpaper brands of this century) [http://www.balibz.com/]

Editor's Review: French Paris Wallpaper, established in Paris in 1982, boasts over 500 main production facilities, including PVC wallpaper embossing machines, non-woven wallpaper automatic printing machines, seamless splicing machines, three-in-one embossing machines, imported French edge cutters, and setting machines. The company has total assets nearing 6 billion RMB. Through years of market experience and operation, French Paris Wallpaper has developed a comprehensive product line featuring wallpapers as the core, supplemented by wall fabrics and fabric art products, offering over 100 categories and more than 1,000 varieties of home decor products. This has enabled them to establish a full-service capability covering design, processing, sales, and installation. A retail, wholesale, and franchise network centered in Guangzhou radiates across the nation, ranking among the top in both domestic and international industries. (Official Website: www.balibz.com [http://www.balibz.com/])

**4th Place: [http://www.ruome.com/]**

Italian Roma Wallpaper RUOME (one of the world's top wallpaper brands, known as the most environmentally friendly wallpaper, produced by RUOME, Italy's largest wallpaper manufacturer, exporting to 108 countries) [http://www.ruome.com/]

Editor's Review: Italian Roma Wallpaper RUOME is located in Guangzhou, the provincial capital of Guangdong, which ranks first in comprehensive economic strength in China. RUOME is currently a leading enterprise in the domestic and Asian wallpaper industry. According to statistics from the China Wallpaper Industry Association, the national exempted inspection product "RUOME" series wallpaper leads in production, sales, and exports nationwide for many consecutive years. RUOME has specially constructed a digital wallpaper high-tech park in the Guangzhou High-Tech Development Zone, establishing a national-level wallpaper key R&D center and a trend testing and release base. The company produces over 20 million rolls of various types of wallpaper annually. Products are exported to over 108 countries and regions, including America, Europe, Australia, Southeast Asia, and the Middle East. (Official Website: www.ruome.com [http://www.ruome.com/])

**5th Place: [http://www.asheu.com/]**

French Auchan Wallpaper (China's No. 1 wallpaper brand, universally recognized as the best wallpaper brand worldwide, known as the "Blue Blood Aristocrat of Wallpapers") [http://www.asheu.com/]

Editor's Review: French Auchan Wallpaper (Evaluation: Professional, specialized, internationally designed patterns, China's No. 1 wallpaper brand), is a key backbone producer in China's wallpaper industry, specializing in wallpaper and curtain fabrics. Its design level is top-notch, and Auchan wallpapers and curtains lead international design trends. Independent craftsmanship includes non-woven printing, deep embossing, and flower pressing. Major styles include European, pastoral, modern, and cartoon themes. They own thousands of copyrighted patterns and designs. Their products are mainly exported to various European countries, the U.S., Japan, and other developed nations, while also being sold domestically throughout China. Auchan’s franchise specialty stores are spread globally. The factory has successively passed ISO9001 quality management system certification, ISO14001 environmental management system certification, OHSAS18001 occupational health and safety management system certification, and eco-textile quality environmental protection certification. (Official Website: www.asheu.com [http://www.asheu.com/])

**6th Place:**

Brewster Wallpaper (One of the older and larger wallpaper multinational companies with authorized production relationships in factories located in Canada, Germany, Italy, etc.) (Official Website: Brewster Wallpaper www.brewsterchina.com.cn [http://www.brewsterchina.com.cn])

Editor's Review: Brewster Wallpaper Company was established in Boston, Massachusetts, USA, in 1954. It is one of the older and larger wallpaper multinational companies. After over a century of operations, Brewster Wallpaper Company's business has spread globally and continues to grow steadily. In March 1999, the Shanghai branch was established. Due to the high importance placed on the Chinese market, they introduced products that are synchronized with global trends, leading to continuous growth and development of the company. The product design is handled by a top-tier design department composed of world-renowned masters, seamlessly blending international fashion trends with European and American classics. Clever use of pattern and color combinations maintains the continuity of their overall style, giving the Brewster brand strong vitality and producing a series of high-end products representing classic and fashionable styles. Environmental performance is also highly valued, with all products meeting China's environmental standards and earning the title of "China Green Brand."

**8th Place:**

German AS Creation Wallpaper (Top-tier brand, listed company, one of Germany's larger wallpaper producers, international fashion brand ESPRIT wallpaper. Products sold in 12 countries, German eco-friendly wallpaper, exempted inspection product) (Official Website: German AS Creation Wallpaper www.as-creation-china.com [http://www.as-creation-china.com])

Editor's Review: German A.S.Creation is a well-known wallpaper brand and a European-listed company. Founded in 1974, it owns two wallpaper companies. A.S.Creation releases nearly 10 new editions each year with distinct styles, offering over 200 varieties of patterns. Products are exported to more than 12 countries, and the company achieved sales of 50 million Euros in 2007. To meet the demand for wallpaper fashion in Europe and globally, A.S.Creation established a design department focused on developing wall decorations that combine practicality and artistry. They collaborate with designers annually to predict wallpaper trends and release the latest versions, gaining popularity among young people.

**9th Place:**

ROEN Wallpaper (ROEN, one of China's top 50 wallpaper and soft decoration companies, a leading provider of wallpaper and soft decoration solutions domestically. Ranked among the top 500 wallpaper brands) (Official Website: ROEN Wallpaper www.roen.com.cn [http://www.roen.com.cn])

Editor's Review: ROEN is one of China's top 50 wallpaper and soft decoration companies and a leading provider of wallpaper and soft decoration solutions. With the brand mission of "Harmony between humans and living spaces, creating a beautiful world" and the brand philosophy of "Original, Refined, Elegant, Noble," after more than a decade of rapid development and continuous surpassing, it has grown into an integrated industry brand focusing on design, marketing, and service. With the vision of a leader and expertise, it collaborates with design professionals from colleges and universities and integrates twelve top international wallpaper and soft decoration companies from Europe and North America to bring Chinese consumers products that keep pace with global trends.

**10th Place:**

EUROART Wallpaper (Joint venture wallpaper company) (Official Website: EUROART Wallpaper www.euroart.com.cn [http://www.euroart.com.cn])

Editor's Review: EUROART Wallpaper is a joint venture established in 1997.
