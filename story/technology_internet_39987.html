10 Tips to Help Your Article Rank TOP

by geekzhang on 2007-12-21 11:21:12

【dailyblogtips article, via】It is well known that a popular blog post can bring you more clicks, links, etc. Here are my 10 tips:

1. Time beats talent. This means spending more time defining your article, formatting it, and filling it with more value.

2. Use the best ideas. Find those things you have always wanted but haven't obtained, and consolidate a large resource library from your perspective. The more helpful it is to everyone, the better!

3. Use formats to highlight your strengths. Do your best to find some funny lines, etc., and expand your strengths to make your useful materials stand out.

4. Brainstorm big headlines. There are only a few people who truly master writing headlines for social media, so dig them up through brainstorming and find the right one among many choices.

5. Add a lot of value to your article. Share more small tips and consolidate more resources.

7. Beauty is in the eye of the beholder. If your article looks beautiful, it will naturally attract more readers. Spend some time inserting images, sketching, and arranging to give others a visual feast.

8. Tell them what you're going to tell them. In other words, state your purpose. What will you gain from this article, and how can I help you?

9. Send messages to keep in touch.

10. Utilize your network. If you want people to like your post and digg your article, let them know. Your loyal readers like you, and you help them, teach them. If voting is just a click on a link, they will be willing to do it. You need to get the snowball rolling, and others will do more for you.

Special Tip:

11. Investigate what has been done before. Study your most popular articles so far. What are their common points? Why are they effective? Find those key points through those examples.
