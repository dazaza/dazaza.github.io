Appendix:

Dreamland Game Introduction

Dreamland is the third part of the trilogy of online role-playing games "Century Record" independently developed by Shanda Network. It represents a "dream" world.

Dreamland is a 2D online role-playing game. The game graphics style is fresh and beautiful, characters are Q-version designed, cute and cartoonish.

The game uses real-time combat mode, tense and exciting, with strong expressiveness.

The game has an extremely rich costume change system. The hairstyles, clothes, weapons, and pet accessories of the characters can all be changed in terms of style and color.

The pet system in Dreamland is one of the biggest features of the game and deserves praise. Compared with other similar games, pets in Dreamland occupy an important position in the game: they chat with their owners, constantly evolve, and master dozens of pet skills. Pets in Dreamland can not only craft weapons and armor, synthesize accessories and props, but also dig treasures, send letters, trade, set up stalls, etc. With outstanding artificial intelligence and pet decoration systems, the pets in Dreamland can be called unique.
