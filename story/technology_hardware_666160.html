The happy times of life! My grandpa had a special small cart, divided into two sides by a wooden frame in the middle~ When grandma was cooking, my brother and I would be placed in the cart, one on each side, and grandpa would push us around in the fields behind the house. The scenery was green and lush, with reds and yellows all around, and the crickets chirping merrily. My brother and I would chatter and laugh noisily, and grandpa would smile contentedly! When it was about time to go back, grandpa would push us home. Back then, he was so strong that he could easily push us over the doorsteps! Grandma would already have prepared delicious meals waiting for us to come back~ How blissful it was! Even though we couldn't often eat meat, grandma was very clever and always found ways to make delicious food for us~ She made pancakes, fried fruit, steamed green bean cakes, vegetable buns, etc.~~~~ So, my brother and I were both as round as little balls, hehe~ Each of us had a small pillow, and every day we fought over them. My brother could never win against me~ because grandpa always protected me~ hahaha! I was so proud and full of myself! The mischievous younger brother even once grabbed a bag of big green worms and threw them on me to get revenge~ Ever since then, I've been really afraid of bugs! That memory is still vivid in my mind! Every night, grandpa had to scratch our backs before we could fall asleep, and in the morning, we would sit on little stools while grandma combed our hair into two pigtails~ Then we'd head off to school happily! As the sun set, the two little ones would return to their warm embrace! Now? Grandpa walks with a cane~ Grandma's frail body~ Her white hair~ Their expectant eyes~ It breaks my heart~ I must let them see my happiness!
