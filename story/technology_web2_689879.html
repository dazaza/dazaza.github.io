The phrase "steal a half day of leisure from life" captures the essence of finding tranquility amidst the busyness of life.

As for the mineral spring at Longyou Chibi, its water is bright and clear, free of bacteria, toxins, and sediment. When bathing in it, the skin feels smooth and cool, offering an excellent bathing experience. According to tests conducted by the Environmental Health Testing Institute of the Chinese Academy of Preventive Medicine, the mineralization level of Longyou Chibi Hot Spring is 1312 milligrams per liter. Praised by experts as a "divine spring," this precious medical hot spring is suitable for both bathing and drinking, and has therapeutic effects on diseases. The water's chemical composition includes silicic acid, radioactive radon, and calcium sulfate, which can also be referred to as weakly radioactive radon calcium sulfate spring.

Compared with some famous springs at home and abroad, Longyou Chibi Hot Spring not only contains abundant minerals but also has a well-proportioned composition. Located within the city of Chibi in Hubei Province, the Longyou Chibi Hot Spring Resort is 5 kilometers from the city center, 110 kilometers north of Wuhan, 70 kilometers south of Yueyang, 210 kilometers from Changsha to the east, and 40 kilometers west of the ancient battlefield of Chibi. Adjacent to the famous Lu Shui Lake scenic area, the resort features beautiful natural landscapes such as ten thousand mu tea gardens and kiwi orchards, making it a paradise for sightseeing, leisure, and vacationing.

The hot spring water of the Longyou Chibi Hot Spring Resort comes from five self-flowing wells under Wuhong Mountain, with a daily flow rate of more than 5,000 tons. The water gushes out from over 200 meters underground through man-made drilled wells, with an outlet temperature of 62 degrees Celsius. It has been proven that Longyou Chibi Hot Spring can treat common ailments like arthritis and skin diseases, and also offers good results for cardiovascular, nervous, digestive system, metabolic, and endocrine-related diseases such as insufficient blood supply to the brain, bone hyperplasia, osteoporosis, etc., achieving effects that drugs cannot match.

With its unique characteristics, Longyou Chibi Hot Spring is an extremely rare and valuable medical hot spring. Surrounded by picturesque scenery, enjoying the waterfall, green mountains, and blue sky gives one the feeling of being in a "paradise on earth." It allows you to temporarily forget the hustle and bustle of the world, enjoy a moment of peace away from urban noise, and get close to nature. While soaking in the hot spring, you can admire the scenery and relax your mind, experiencing the charm of "stealing a half day of leisure from life."

The entire design of the landscape hot spring area maintains a natural appearance, with hot spring water flowing out of stone caves, winding along the lakeside and mountains. Immersed in the hot spring pool, you can feel bubbles gurgling up beneath you, look down at the continuous green valleys and mountains, and be surrounded by the mist from the waterfall and the steam from the hot spring, creating a serene and enchanting atmosphere.
