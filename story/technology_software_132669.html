9. Stock Star Rating

Tonghuashun's star rating method mainly evaluates listed companies based on several aspects such as business performance, operational risk, and industry environment. Key considerations include the company's profitability, management capability, debt repayment ability, cash flow, growth potential, operating environment, market position, corporate culture, and human resources. Each listed company will be evaluated on various indicators, then after considering the weight coefficients of each indicator, a total score is calculated, and finally, the listed companies are classified into five star ratings according to different scores.

The quality of star ratings mainly depends on the accuracy of financial and other data provided by third parties (such as listed companies). When Tonghuashun finds questionable financial data through research, it will make appropriate adjustments to the stock star ratings.

Note: Star ratings are mainly for value investment reference and do not constitute specific buy or sell recommendations.
