Shanghai's 2007 college entrance exam car rental service starts on the 30th day, and renting a car must be done early.

by okzhuce on 2007-06-01 11:33:38

As the college entrance examination candidates are making their "final sprint", after careful consideration, the major taxi dispatching enterprises in Shanghai have successively launched plans for taxis to serve the college entrance examination. I learned yesterday that starting from June 30th, taxi dispatching centers will begin to accept telephone reservations for college entrance examination taxis. According to past experience, industry experts still suggest this year that "reservations should be made as early as possible."

Bus Taxi Dispatch Center Director Lu Xiangdong told reporters that according to an analysis of the situation of candidates taking taxis for the college entrance examination in recent years, although the total number of candidates this year is slightly lower than in previous years, the demand for taxis during the three days of the college entrance examination will remain stable. Therefore, Bus Taxi has prepared 1,200 special taxis for the college entrance examination through "capacity potential tapping" and will start accepting candidate reservations at midnight on June 30th. It is introduced that the taxis reserved for the college entrance examination account for 20% of the company's total vehicles. "While ensuring the priority of candidates' use of taxis, we must also take into account the needs of other passengers," said Lu Xiangdong. During the college entrance examination period, car rental in Shanghai is very popular.

This time, Jinjiang Taxi has also allocated 500 taxis, which will start accepting candidate reservations from June 30th. Jinjiang Dispatch Director Wang Yonggang told reporters that according to past experience, all 500 taxis reserved for the college entrance examination can be fully booked on the first day of reservation opening. To ensure the connection rate of the dispatch hotline on that day, Jinjiang will add five more duty operators based on the original ten fixed seats. This year, Haibo Taxi Dispatch, which is executing the college entrance examination taxi reservation for the first time, will try to arrange high-star and experienced veteran drivers to handle the task of picking up and dropping off candidates during the college entrance examination.

Industry insiders remind that in recent years, parents reserving taxis to send their children to exams has become a common phenomenon. However, for some candidates, this care from their parents may become a psychological burden for the candidates. In addition, since the college entrance examination coincides with workdays, there are many uncertain factors in ground transportation. Candidates traveling medium to long distances should prepare multiple travel options.
