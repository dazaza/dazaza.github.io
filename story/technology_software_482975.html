Does the court have a subpoena? Don't believe it if you get such a message!

Police warn that criminals often use number changers to set network numbers as local numbers.

Shenzhen News Network (reported by Duan Linjun) - Recently, a citizen informed the reporter that he received a mobile phone message of a court subpoena. The message reminded him that the consultation telephone number had the area code 0755 for a local landline. He could not tell whether it was true or false at the moment. For this reason, the reporter called the telephone number in the short message. After a round of sparring with the other party, a vicious telecom fraud scheme was also uncovered.

The scammer claimed to be a court staff member.

The informant said that at 11:27 AM, his mobile phone suddenly received a text message from the number 15509487054: an urgent notice from the court requiring him to collect a subpoena, and legal responsibility would be pursued if overdue. It also reminded him to consult the appeal department's telephone number: 0755-21965168.

The reporter then dialed the so-called appeal department telephone number.

Reporter: Is this the court?

Scammer: Yes, the Intermediate People's Court of the city.

Reporter: Did I receive information saying there is a subpoena for me? What time?

Scammer: What is your name? What is your telephone number? We need to verify your identity.

Reporter: Zhang Li, 1363003. (The reporter randomly reported a fake name and a fake number.)

Scammer: It has been verified that there is a subpoena for you. In March this year, you applied for a Construction Bank credit card, and currently owe 36,800 yuan. The Construction Bank has already filed a lawsuit with our court, and your bank account will be frozen subsequently.

Reporter: What should I do? There are still 100,000 yuan in my account?

Scammer: As long as you transfer the deposit in the bank to our court's safe account now, everything will be fine, and the bank will not freeze your account.

Reporter: How to transfer? When can the account be unfrozen and returned to me after the transfer?

Scammer: 6222... ...

Finally, the scammer gave the reporter the so-called safe account and asked the reporter to transfer money quickly. And kindly reminded the reporter not to tell others, lest they be held legally responsible.

Using number changers to confuse

Regarding such summons scams, the reporter interviewed relevant staff members of the Futian District Court. According to the introduction, there are three ways for the delivery of court summonses: one is based on the defendant's contact number provided in the plaintiff's complaint, the court notifies the defendant by phone to come to the court with valid identification documents such as their ID card to collect the summons; the second is through mail to deliver it to the defendant's residence or workplace for signature acceptance; if these two methods cannot reach the defendant, the third method is to publish summons information through media announcements. By this point, the fraudulent lies were debunked.

Subsequently, the reporter also interviewed relevant staff members of the Municipal Public Security Bureau. According to the introduction, due to low crime costs and high success rates, such non-contact frauds have been increasing in recent years. Criminals mainly commit crimes outside the province, or even abroad, using number changers to set network numbers as local numbers to gain citizens' trust and carry out fraud. The police remind citizens that if they receive such fraudulent messages or calls, especially those involving transfers or remittances, they must not believe them easily and should immediately report to the police.

It is understood that currently, in response to the situation of many merchants and frequent bank transfers in its jurisdiction, the Huaqiangbei Police Station of the Futian Branch has set up cartoon police reminders in major bank outlets in the jurisdiction, effectively reducing the incidence of non-contact fraud, with remarkable results; the Meilin Police Station, in response to the non-contact fraud alerts among the elderly population in its jurisdiction, specially cooperates with banks in the jurisdiction to implement a linkage mechanism, setting up the last line of defense, timely and effectively preventing citizens from being defrauded and losing property.
