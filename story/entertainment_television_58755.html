The story begins with a certain type of girl found all over Asia. This kind of girl is called "sticky note girl." She is simple, plain, and content, with small wishes. She has never been anyone special nor does she expect to become one. They have small functions but are indispensable, just like a sticky note that can be torn off and used at any time. It's not eye-catching or special, but there will definitely be someone like her around you! A 'luxury cruise' journey allows such an ordinary girl to encounter the most extraordinary circumstances.

Xinyi has always been afraid of others not liking her since she was young. So, she has always tried hard to be nice to others. As long as someone gives her a little response, she easily falls for them. Everyone calls her the sticky note girl because when someone needs her, they tear her off and use her, and when they don't need her, they throw her away carelessly. But she is a fool who values feelings deeply. Her lack of strength often makes her hesitate when she should make a decision. One day, in order to win back her playboy boyfriend Gucci, she spent a lot of money on a trip on the Love Boat. Unexpectedly, she went into the wrong room by mistake, and when she woke up, she was shocked to find that she had spent a night of passion with a stranger. She also discovered that her boyfriend Gucci had cheated on her with a rich widow and left her behind, which dealt her a devastating blow.

A month later, when Xinyi saw the result of the pregnancy test, she regretted not jumping off the ship. A 25-year-old law firm clerk, for a bad boyfriend, is now in debt for 200,000 yuan and is carrying the child of an unknown man. It is simply the most ironic joke in the world. She took a week off from work to return to her hometown of Jiangmu Island to think about what she should do.

The stranger in bed that day was named Ji Cunxi. He is the ideal prince charming in every girl's mind - wealthy, handsome, and talented. When he met the ordinary office girl Chen Xinyi, it was unclear whether this was the start of trouble or the beginning of happiness.

Due to the hidden illness inherited by the Ji family, each generation has had fewer male descendants, continuing through nine generations with only single heirs. This made Grandma Pearl restless every night, fearing that this tiny spark of life would accidentally be extinguished! Grandma hoped that her grandson Ji Cunxi could be as promiscuous as other young entrepreneurs. However, this grandson remained pure. One day, Grandma accidentally discovered that Xinyi was carrying Cunxi's child. Perhaps it was the ancestors of the Ji family protecting them; unexpectedly, heaven really bestowed upon her a 'little golden grandson,' making Grandma insist that Cunxi marry Xinyi. Only then would she have the face to meet the ancestors of the Ji family when she passed away.

Thus, Grandma threatened to expose Cunxi's three-year girlfriend if he didn't marry Xinyi. Being "tricked into marriage," Cunxi had no choice but to marry Xinyi to fulfill his grandma's last wish while alive. However, Cunxi realized he was unwilling to face the reality of marriage and couldn't decide whether to run away or bravely take responsibility for the consequences. At this moment, they suddenly found out that under Grandma's requirements, they had to start dating and falling in love.

In fact, Cunxi also had a three-year relationship with a ballet dancer named ANNA. Despite multiple romantic proposals, ANNA always gave him a soft rejection. Yet, Cunxi silently waited, waiting for ANNA to let go of everything and agree to his proposal. ANNA chose not to marry for her career, and at this point, Cunxi unintentionally got Xinyi pregnant due to an accident. Fortunately, ANNA was on an international tour, allowing Cunxi to hide this blunder. However, without Grandma's knowledge, Cunxi signed a prenuptial agreement with Xinyi, stating that after the baby was born, they would divorce. Cunxi promised to give Xinyi a sum of money, and they would owe nothing to each other afterward. For the future of the child in her belly, Xinyi agreed after serious consideration. Cunxi calculated that once Xinyi gave birth, ANNA would finish her tour and return home. Their contractual relationship would expire right on time, allowing him to rekindle his romance with ANNA.

Unaware of the situation, Xinyi moved into Cunxi's house and began learning how to coexist with him. The evolution between these two strangers, from discomfort to becoming accustomed to each other's presence, was very subtle. Originally, the two thought they would live under the same roof without much interaction, but gradually, their lives intertwined due to many daily trivial matters. Under Grandma's request, Xinyi moved into Cunxi's bedroom. Cunxi then used an excuse to sleep in the guest room but soon found that he disliked the loneliness of sleeping alone. In the middle of the night, Cunxi always returned to the original room, and over time, having someone beside him during sleep became a habit. Cunxi, who disliked going home for dinner, started returning home for meals. Cunxi, who swore never to help with anything, also began buying wallpapers for the nursery and toys for the child.

When they thought this was what happiness looked like, the unexploded bombshell, ANNA, appeared. Cunxi never expected that his unresolved relationship with ANNA would hurt Xinyi. Leaving behind only a divorce agreement, he disappeared without a trace. Originally thinking that this ending was the best conclusion, Cunxi realized that Xinyi had unknowingly taken a place in his heart. He vowed that if he ever found Xinyi again, he would pursue her anew, no longer as the father of the child to the mother, but as a man pursuing a woman...
