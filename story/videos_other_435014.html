On July 18, the film "Tough Guy 2" crew was peacefully shooting in Qingdao. Liu Ye plays the tough guy Lao San, who angrily beats up scalpers specializing in reselling expert clinic numbers. Faced with these scalpers, Lao San shows no mercy; with three palms, he knocks one of them to the ground, reminiscent of the scene in "Water Margin" where Lu Zhishen kills Zheng Guanxi with three punches. During his rest time on set, Liu Ye can be seen practicing his moves while playfully handling the clapperboard, showing his playful side.
