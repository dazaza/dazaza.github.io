How to light up the QQ Free Fantasy icon?

by lwf225 on 2009-09-01 14:45:53

Tags: Light up QQ Free Fantasy icon, QQ Free Fantasy icon, Light up QQ icon. Originally, just by creating a character, the icon on QQ would light up. Now, you have to reach level 10 and change your class. It's recommended to train an assassin because of their fast attack speed, which is best before level 10. Moreover, the class-changing mission is quite simple; you just need to fight squirrels in East Fragrant Grass Forest.

The QQ Free Fantasy Icon Upgrade System has arrived, bringing the era of personalized icons! Individuality, fashion, nobility, uniqueness - it's all in your hands.

Rules for lighting up:

Notes:

After the new icon upgrade system is launched, users who have already lit up their icons will have their original icons replaced by the new system icons upon completing the class change task when they first log into the game. If the player's level meets the requirements for lighting up subsequent icons, then during the second login (a short logout will suffice), the icon will automatically update to the one corresponding to the current level. After reaching a new icon level, please log out and back in to have the current role level's corresponding icon displayed on QQ automatically. The icon display rule is: take the information of the highest-level character within the current QQ number to display the icon. Please note. When a QQ number with an already lit icon does not log into Free Fantasy for a week, the icon will automatically dim. When the player logs into Free Fantasy and remains online continuously for 2 hours after the icon dims, the dimmed icon will be lit again. The process of lighting up the icon is irreversible, meaning that once a higher-level icon is lit, lower-level icons cannot be lit again.

Common Questions and Answers:

Why do I need to complete the class change task to light up the icon? Isn't that increasing the difficulty?

This is to make each player's golden Free Fantasy icon more valuable and to pursue higher goals! At the same time, it's also a test!

How do I light up the Free Fantasy icon?

You just need to register and own a character in QQ Free Fantasy and complete the class change task to light up the icon.

How do I turn off the Free Fantasy icon?

You can go to http://ffo.qq.com/act/a20071009dl/ to turn off the icon yourself. You just need to correctly input the reason for turning it off. If self-service operation fails, we suggest you go to the http://gcs.qq.com/gcsv6/ffo/ffo_Quesindex_v6.shtml Free Fantasy online customer service section, select Other - Game Complaints to report your issue. We apologize for any inconvenience this may cause.

How long do I have to wait after turning off the icon to light it up again?

7 days. The interval between turning off and lighting up the icon cannot be less than 7 days.

Why isn't my number's icon lighting up on QQ even though I've completed the class change task?

Please use the official website's recommended QQ version. Upgrade your version to QQ2007II beta1 or a newer version. Versions below QQ2007II beta1 will not display the Free Fantasy icon.

Why am I still unable to light up the icon even though I've completed the class change task, my version meets the requirements (not lower than QQ2007II beta1), and I've performed the self-service operation via http://ffo.qq.com/act/a20071009dl/?

Firstly, we apologize for the trouble caused. We suggest you visit the http://gcs.qq.com/gcsv6/ffo/ffo_Quesindex_v6.shtml Free Fantasy online customer service section, select Other - Game Complaints to report your issue. We apologize for any inconvenience this may cause.
