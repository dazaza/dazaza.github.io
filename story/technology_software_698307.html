At the same time, Nedved also criticized in another place that the current Juventus lacks personality. As a former tough guy, Nedved said: "Juventus has completely lost its way. Regarding the new coach candidate of Juventus, Agnelli said Antonio Conte is just one of the candidates reported, and there is no conclusion yet. Nedved also expressed: "I haven't participated in any discussions so far. Delneri is still the coach of Juventus. I don't know what the final decision of the club will be." So will Conte become the coach of Juventus in the future? "It is destined for Conte to become the coach of Juventus. He is a great player and the leader we relied on when we played." However, Nedved did not reveal when Conte would take over Juventus. There is only one round left in the league, but if Juventus wants to qualify for the Europa League next season, they can only wait for a miracle to happen - winning themselves while Roma loses. Delneri's coaching record this season is even worse than last year's Juventus team, and the disappointment of the Juventus management towards the team has been fully exposed at the last moment. "At the beginning of the season, I said that Juventus would end up in 7th or 8th place in the league. It turns out my prediction was correct because the level of this Juventus squad is too low." When Juventus internalized the blame on Delneri, Moggi indicated that another person should bear more responsibility than Delneri, and that person is Marotta. "This is more Marotta's fault. This squad needs to be refreshed now. The defense and midfield should be updated first. Most of the players signed last summer need to be cleared out." Talking about potential candidates to replace Delneri, Moggi said both Conte and Gasperini are quite good choices. "If you can't choose between Mazzari, Boas, or Conte, then I think Gasperini will be another choice." At the celebration of Napoli's entry into the Champions League, the club chairman De Laurentiis shouted to the wild fans in the stadium, hoping that the coach Mazzari would stay. Being able to participate in the Champions League is obviously the biggest advantage for Napoli to keep Mazzari, but this is actually not safe because Mazzari might leave Napoli like Mourinho left Inter Milan last year. As the agent of Balotelli, Ibrahimovic, and others, Raiola, who dominates the Italian football scene, said: "Mazzari will leave Napoli because he himself believes that he cannot do better than this year, and De Laurentiis will definitely not embarrass him over this matter." In an interview in Milan, Juventus President Agnelli criticized the players' lack of honor sense towards Juventus, saying: "A large number of players can hardly understand what Juventus really is. This is the most disappointing thing for me at the last moment of the season." Regarding Delneri, who hasn't thought he made any mistakes from the start of the season until now, Agnelli was utterly unkind. He said: "We will make evaluations at the last moment, but I must support. I am very dissatisfied and disappointed, but I must wait until the season ends to make the final decision."
