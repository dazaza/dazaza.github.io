Which situations require nasal cosmetic surgery

by 865512 on 2008-12-19 10:05:52

The nose is the ridge of the face, and its height and shape are extremely important in shaping a person's image. Different ethnicities, genders, and aesthetic standards all contribute to perceptions of beauty. The beautiful noses of Caucasian women are envied by women worldwide, while the nose of David, the Greek handsome man sculpted by Michelangelo, is admired by many men. For the Chinese population, due to differences in facial shape and height, one type of nose cannot be universally adopted as the standard for a beautiful nose.

(1) How to Choose the Right Nose Shape

① For delicate women, Lin Daiyu's nose can serve as a standard to aspire to. In fact, most women are willing and more suitable to choose this type. The characteristics of this nose shape are: moderate height of the nasal bridge, with an upward curve, a small and slightly upturned tip, exuding a hint of "pride."

② Women who are tall, have a prominent forehead, and sunken eye sockets should not feel inferior. Such women are precisely the "Europeanized" type that modern aesthetics pursue. Appropriately raising the nasal bridge, refining the nose tip, and pairing it with large, deep-set eyes and full lips can recreate the appearance of a European woman.

(Picture 1)

(2) Which Noses Can Be Surgically Enhanced

The nose is divided into three parts: the nasal root, the nasal bridge, and the nasal tip. Any part that is congenitally insufficient can be beautified through surgery. A wide or flat nasal root, or even one that is concave, can be raised. Vertical skin grooves can also be corrected surgically or through autologous tissue transplantation.

A saddle nose with insufficient height on the nasal bridge can be raised to enhance beauty. For those with a hump nose and angular deformity in the middle of the nasal bridge, the nasal bone needs to be reshaped to eliminate the protruding angle, thereby beautifying the nose.

The lower part of the nose consists of the nasal tip and nostrils. An unattractive nasal tip may appear collapsed or thick and blunt, which can also be surgically enhanced. Wide nostrils, upturned noses, or short nasal tips can all be beautified through surgery.

In summary, nose beautification surgery is not limited to rhinoplasty alone. If you find your nose less than ideal, modern cosmetic surgery can help improve it.

(3) What Are the Methods for Beautifying the Nose?

① Makeup techniques include general makeup and contouring makeup. However, these methods take too much time daily and only rely on optical perception to conceal congenital deficiencies, with limited effects. Therefore, they will not be discussed in detail here.

② Surgical beauty enhancement, such as rhinoplasty. Materials for raising the nose include autologous skull bone, rib bone, and cartilage, animal bones and cartilage, and tissue substitutes—silicone nose supports. The latter has been widely applied worldwide with very few side effects, so it can be used with confidence.
