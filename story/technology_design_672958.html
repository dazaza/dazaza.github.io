Buying and selling society January 24th news - Louis Vuitton Spring/Summer 2011 women's fashion大片 was recently exposed. Supermodels Freja Beha Erichsen, Raquel Zimmermann, and Kristen McMenamy jointly interpreted the collection with photographer Steven Meisel personally directing it. Designer Marc Jacobs designed these ghostly, dazzling new outfits for Louis Vuitton's Spring/Summer 2011 series, featuring jade green, light purple colors or animal patterns, as if specifically designed to flatter wealthy Chinese customers. Marc Jacobs presents a combination of Art Deco, Art Nouveau, and Oriental style. "Basically, I didn't want anything to be done in a very natural way," he said. The dresses included cheongsams with fringes; off-the-shoulder, backless tops made of lustrous gold and silver threads combined with lace skirts embroidered with LV letters; metallic dresses decorated with small sequins from the waist to the hips; sweaters printed with animal heads, etc. There are also evening bags, this style being smaller than the Speedy bag from the previous season but not low-key - especially those distinctive handles adorned with rhinestones spelling "Vuitton." This series straightforwardly rejects minimalism, which dominated the runways of various brands in the previous season. The giraffe, tiger, zebra, and China's national treasure giant panda prints in the Spring/Summer series appear alongside animal patterns on suits and shift dresses, indicating that animal print will become one of the upcoming trends for spring and summer.
