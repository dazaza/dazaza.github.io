or did you wake up because of the heat?

by wxgo804vw6 on 2010-04-19 15:18:41

I looked up, and it was already 2 a.m. on July 17th. Time flies when you're busy. I've never been one for keeping diaries, but teaching in the rural areas is special. It may be a once-in-a-lifetime opportunity. So, I decided to document every single day of my experience here, recording events and feelings. When I flip through this diary again, I'll be proud of it. It's my memoir of teaching here. Seeing it will remind me of the process, recalling this indescribable time.

Today is July 16th, 2009. This day, we carried our dreams, riding the gentle breeze, heading towards the place where dreams fly - Zhaoqing Huaiji, more specifically, Tanmiao Primary School in Lengkeng Town, Huaiji. We gathered at 7 a.m. at the canteen square, took a group photo with the other two teams (Heyuan, Raoping), then took the subway to Tianhe Passenger Station. I met my sister at the subway station, what a coincidence. Thanks to her help. For the first time in my life, I was squished like a pancake in the subway station, surrounded by a sea of people, with a long journey ahead. This day was destined to be extraordinary.

After four hours of travel, we arrived at another part of the country - Huaiji, transferring to Xiangjiang Hotel for lunch with the Deputy Secretary of the Huaiji County Education Bureau Party Committee. Afterwards, we headed to Tanmiao Primary School with expectations. The environment where we will live for two weeks is as follows: teachers' dormitory, simple, wooden beds propped up by chairs, a desk, that's it. As for the school: a two-story teaching building, a flag-raising platform, three ping-pong tables, a small playground, and that's all.

Later, under the guidance of the principal, we walked around the village. It's a relatively backward area, isolated from the outside world, quite remote. Many children were curious when they saw us. Ha~ Back at the school, after dinner, we showered, had a meeting, prepared materials, wrote in our diaries, it was 3 a.m., couldn't hold out anymore, went to sleep!

Teaching began - Day One

July 17th, Friday, Weather: Sun shining like sunflowers

The first day, I overslept, the alarm went off at seven, I slept like a pig, luckily, my senior woke me up. After a hurried breakfast, I went to the flag-raising platform to gather students, then led them to the classroom. Class: Little A Class, Headteacher: Yumeng, Yuxin, Jiasheng. Wow! The students are so lively, can be described with one word - chaotic! The kids are hard to manage, very mischievous. Choosing the class name, class song, and class slogan, the students were a bit shy, not expressing many opinions. After some deliberation, we finally decided:

Class Name: Happy Class

Class Song: Reading Boy

Class Slogan: (Boys) We are courteous, (Girls) We are beautiful and graceful, (Together) We are the perfect happy class.

Then came the class committee election, which was pretty active. To avoid discouraging the students, we gave many of them a chance. Originally, we only planned to select one, but ended up selecting two. The sports committee was particularly special, with 10 people vying for the position, showing their passion for sports (indeed, evident during PE class). Through voting, we finally confirmed the sports committee members. Let's introduce our class committee:

Class President: Huang Tianji

Vice Presidents: Huang Jialiang, Huang Jiayi

Study Committee: Huang Lianghua, Huang Huiyi

Life Committee: Huang Yanhong, Huang Zhiwei

Publicity Committee: Huang Yimei, Huang Xiaodie

Sports Committee: Huang Peipei, Huang Ruifang, Huang Jianming, Huang Jianwen

Group Leaders: Huang Yuezhu, Huang Yingying, Huang Haiqing, Huang Wenying

That's it! Our class needs their maintenance and support, we need them to cooperate with our work.

In the afternoon, classes started, it was the first lesson, and I went to assist. The kids were difficult to teach, knowing little. What impressed me most was when Yumeng asked them to take out paper and pens to write something, many actually hid their stationery away. It seems they don't want to write, or maybe stationery is precious to them. I was speechless... a bit helpless.

Then came the class meeting, PE class.

This school has a unique rule: after dismissal, students must gather first, then teachers escort them to the intersections on either side of the school before they go home. After sending the children away, I sighed with relief, finally able to rest a bit. However, many children stayed at school after school was over, playing basketball, chatting with us... still no rest.

In the evening, eating, having a meeting, taking a shower, that's how the day goes. If there's class tomorrow, then lesson preparation is also needed.

The first day felt a bit chaotic, the children are quite mischievous. Hmm, tired, going to sleep!

Good night!

Light of Hope

July 18th, Saturday, Weather: Even hotter than yesterday

I got up on time today, in the morning teaching the eighth set of radio calisthenics, shouting myself hoarse. In the morning, there were no classes, I assisted again. This time it was Big A Class, good, Big A Class is strong, especially the chairperson Huang Tiansong, and there's also a junior high school student who knows quite a lot. Big A Class gives me hope. The class went smoothly.

The students gradually integrated with us, surrounding us during breaks, chatting with us. Many students wrote my name on the blackboard, moved and touched by their warmth, I gradually became part of this collective. I believe in our Little A Class, hoping that we will get along well in the next few days.

Today, I discovered a batch of promising talents. First is Jianming, mischievous but understanding. As the sports committee member, he does what he should for the class, and he's quite smart. Then there's the class president Tianji, my first impression of him is steady, not talkative, rather quiet. There are also two little girls, Yi Mei and Xuejun, who are very cooperative, listening attentively in class. They have good grades, according to what I know, two very obedient girls, I like them very much.

I found that being with the students is a happy thing, so I basically spent the whole day with the students. Speaking of the afternoon, I assisted Senior Yu Fei in her art class as a model, I'm dizzy! The students of Big A Class described me like this: thin eyebrows, big nose, big ears, small eyes, wow, completely unlike me. Senior Yu Fei's class was great, I also learned some things from her class. Very good, very powerful!

In the evening, the children seemed more willing to stay at school rather than go home. This is because most of them are left-behind children, their parents aren't at home, only grandparents are, who also have to do farm work, leaving no one to play with them, and they eat very late. At school, they can chat, play games, play basketball, how nice! The childhood of these children should be spent freely engaging in various activities. This reminded me of my own childhood, back then, besides studying, playing basketball, skipping rope, playing war games, digging holes, exploring, throwing sandbags... truly interesting. Childhood is so good, free to play, do whatever you want.

Actually, when I was their age, I joined an organization, thinking it was fun. I remember, we did some secret things, the deepest impression I have is stealing fish on a rainy day! Ha ha~

We chatted and played games with many students, in the courtyard, some were learning to sing, some were playing games, some were chatting, others were helping pick vegetables, everywhere was filled with warmth. It seems like we are one big family, everyone laughing together, what a warm scene! Perhaps what they need most is care from others. At home, without parental care, they lack love, lacking the fatherly and motherly love they should have as children.

At this moment, I realized that we have been accepted by the children, we are no longer as awkward as the first day. Now, we have reached a point where we can trust each other. Two little girls chased Icefeng, he dodged, and the victim became me. We played for a long time until dinner. Today was very happy, not because of myself, nor because of our fourteen people, but because of the children. They made me see hope, feel warmth, deeply touch my heart.

Alright, finished early today, going to rest early, don't worry Mom and Dad too much.

Good night!

Pretty popular

July 19th, Sunday, Weather: Rainy, windy

Today is the third day of teaching, fully integrated into the collective, though tired but happy, really happy being with the kids. It rained in the morning, so we didn't do morning exercises.

In the morning, I assisted in a few classes, seems like my real classes are next week! I found myself quite popular, two small classes are fighting over me, very moved. Don't know if it's because I'm funny or kind.

I vaguely remember, in high school, classmates seemed to prefer asking me questions rather than the teacher, so I once asked Huan, she said, you're easier to get along with, kinder, explaining things clearer than the teacher. And the teacher, perhaps noticing this, often used the excuse of exercising the students, making me give lectures, correct test papers. But I indeed gained a lot, learned a lot in this process. Very grateful to my former chemistry teacher Xiao, who was also my head teacher for three years.

By the way, I washed dishes today, since starting university, I haven't washed dishes at home even once, today I washed twice. When I go home, I want my parents to see that coming here to teach was the right choice. Teaching can completely change a person. In the future, I will try to help my parents more. Those children all bear the responsibility of helping with farm work, sometimes even more important than studying, they are so young, while their peers enjoy their childhood freely, they spend their childhood in the fields.

Today's weather was cooler, I took a nap at noon, slept soundly. In the afternoon, I went to the classroom, dazedly seeing a few words on the podium, rubbed my eyes and looked closely: a heart drawn, inside written my name, so moved~~ wu wu~~ Do the students really like me? Although I can't judge the truth, I am still very happy.

In the afternoon, a student from our class cried, comforting her. Watching Small B Class students always fighting and walking around, they are really too mischievous, making our class seem more obedient and understanding. Your own class is like your own flesh and blood, when it performs well, you feel happy and proud for yourself.

Still teaching gymnastics in PE class, still shouting myself hoarse. After school, I visited homes, today was the first time, only went to Jianming's house. His parents work outside, his grandfather grows rice at home. Jianming is very smart, lots of awards at home, all first prizes, I like him very much, not only academically excellent, but also very understanding.

Coming back, I was still chased by the same children from yesterday, so tired! Listen to our dialogue: I don't want to play anymore / No / Teacher is tired, all of you chase the teacher, the teacher just chases one of you, you're bullying me, how could the teacher not be tired / Oh, then let's play 100 more times / Heaven! Last time, just last time / No, last three times / Okay, last three times. (Then we played three more times) Alright, already three times / No, wasn't it supposed to be the last three times? There are still two times! ... Might as well escape! So I sneaked away unnoticed.

In the evening, I read what they wrote, the class president has great ambitions, wanting to become an economist when grown up, very promising! Tomorrow, I have a heavy task, related to everyone's lives. Actually, it's simple, just cooking! Ha ha~~ So let's go to bed early. (～ o ～)~zZ Snore snore~~

I woke up earlier this morning, went to buy groceries, came back, did morning exercises, sweeping students actually didn't sweep, annoyed me again. Back in the classroom, no morning reading, explaining the rules of the fun sports meet (tomorrow), occupied the entire morning reading time.

In the morning, I assisted in two of Senior Yu Fei's classes, content: instruments, I was flipping pictures, desperately looking for the instrument Senior mentioned. We also played Abing's "Moon Reflected on Second Spring", so melancholy!
