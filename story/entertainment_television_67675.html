The sequel version of the scenario and plot after the return of Little Fool, an unofficial version of *The Return of Little Fool*.

The conspiracy of Bull Demon King has been completely shattered, and Little Fool has returned to the year 2060. In the laboratory, Little Fool looks gloomy. At this moment, Cui Xuejia says to Little Fool: "Little Fool, select the happy expression state option." But Little Fool shows no reaction. Dr. Cui finds it strange and asks, "What's wrong with Little Fool?" Little Fool replies, "Report, Dr. Cui, Little Fool's on/off switch and expression state options have been destroyed." It is at this moment that Dr. Cui remembers that Little Fool was destroyed by Bull Demon King. He patiently inquires, "Why are you so gloomy, Little Fool?" Little Fool says, "Little Fool misses Brother Xiaoqian." This time, Dr. Cui truly believes that Little Fool has developed human emotions. So he comforts her with a smile, saying, "I know you miss Xiaoqian, but you must understand that if you had stayed in 2007, the world might have changed because of your existence..." Before he could finish his sentence, Little Fool interrupts him, saying, "Big Brother Doctor, I understand what you're saying, but..." Her brows furrow as she gazes at the large screen in the laboratory, as if expecting Xiaoqian to emerge from it at any moment. But all she can do is stare wistfully.

"Dr. Zhao, Dr. Zhao!" At this moment, a hurried voice approaches the laboratory and addresses Dr. Zhao, "Someone is here to see you." Dr. Zhao asks, "Who is it?" A business card is handed over, and upon seeing it, Dr. Zhao becomes very pleased. He immediately takes the card and goes downstairs. A short while later, Dr. Zhao returns, accompanied by a young man who appears to be in his thirties, handsome and with a healthy, tanned complexion. Dr. Zhao quickly introduces him to everyone, "This is Dr. Chen, my university classmate." Everyone shakes hands and exchanges pleasantries, saying, "Hello, Dr. Chen."

Then they begin discussing serious matters. Dr. Chen says, "I heard you've developed a smartphone for an intelligent robot?" Dr. Zhao knows this is a big secret and cannot reveal it temporarily, so he says, "No, we haven't." At this point, Little Fool's automatic lie-detection function activates, and she picks up a small hammer, standing behind Dr. Zhao. She starts tapping the hammer on Dr. Zhao's head repeatedly. Dr. Zhao quickly exclaims, "I'm lying! I'm lying!"...
