"下战书"的英文翻译是 "to issue a challenge" 或 "to send a challenge". 在某些特定语境下，也可以用 "to declare war"（宣战）来表达类似的意思，但通常 "issue a challenge" 更为贴切和通用。

In the afternoon, a cold wind. In the dormitory, silence.

I sat on the bed like a statue. There was little sunlight; the quilt on the balcony was blown down by the wind. My mind was calm as I listened to quiet music. I turned around and looked back. We parted ways. Maybe it was just self-indulgent lamenting, or perhaps there was no real understanding. The happiness I wanted was so simple, but as time went on, some things just couldn’t be maintained.

Once, we loved each other, we laughed and played, with only each other in our eyes. Once, we were so foolish, you accompanied me in enjoying our happiness. We wrote letters to each other, many every day.

Once, I loved deeply, jealously and foolishly preparing your birthday gifts. I bought a black diary and wrote down my little secret that was only related to you. I pasted our photos on that notebook. Once, you forgot, forgot to prepare a birthday gift for me. Forgot to visit me, and forgot to write me letters.

Once, I quietly walked ahead of you, feeling your presence behind me. You knew, but didn’t step forward. Once, I was so foolish, walking beside you, waiting for you to talk to me, but you didn’t. Once, after class, I hurried home, hoping to see you on the way, creating many coincidences for us. But many times, we just passed each other without much acknowledgment. Once, helplessly, I said goodbye, helplessly agreeing to part. Once, for a month, two months, you never called. Once, you accompanied me, saying you missed the afternoon game.

No sound, no breath, so no return. That day, I left. You started to feel sad. The phone rang, my voice became distant, and I began telling others that I didn’t love you anymore. Later, we never spoke again. Later, when we met again, my heart still skipped a beat, but I lacked the courage to continue...

Every time I let go of you, every moment I avoided your gaze, every time I acted carefree, every laugh, disappearing in your world, letting silence replace everything. I liked to walk quietly, listen to music quietly, recall our past quietly, think about you quietly, then cry silently, and then announce to the world that I fell in love again, especially with you. You felt sad, you also fell in love again. You broke up again. Your call came, yet you still didn't know where you went wrong, blaming me for being capricious, childish. You asked me to come find you, but in the end, I didn't. You said if we broke up again, tell you, but what difference would it make?

Thus, one year passed, and we finally could miss each other, even though our smiles weren't natural, we carefully pretended.

And we finally really had no more intersections.
