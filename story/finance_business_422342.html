Centipedes have been used as traditional animal-based Chinese herbs for thousands of years.

In terms of nourishing health care, centipede-based health supplements with health benefits are increasingly attracting people's attention and affection. Shennong Liquor is a healthy therapy integrating traditional dietary therapy, treatment through food, and dietary tonics. Produced using modern techniques, it is a fitness beverage wine. Toxicological studies, nutritional analysis, and efficacy studies on this product show that Shennong Liquor is highly nutritious, containing over 15 amino acids and trace elements required by the human body, with significant analgesic and detoxifying effects. In addition, centipede health supplements have multiple functions such as tonifying the kidneys, enhancing male virility, and boosting the immune system. Therefore, vigorously developing centipede farming and extensively developing centipede-related series products to gradually develop towards industrial utilization is a contemporary production need. For a long time, the state has called for vigorous capturing of centipedes each year. In some main producing areas of centipedes, the original seasonal capture and seasonal acquisition have gradually evolved into cross-seasonal capture and year-round acquisition. The purchase price of each centipede in major Chinese herbal medicine markets has soared from a few cents to over 1.5 yuan, with even higher export prices and constant demand, leading to annual price increases. However, due to deforestation, pesticide and fertilizer pollution, and other ecological damages, wild resources are becoming increasingly scarce, resulting in a severe shortage of centipede resources and rising prices. Therefore, an opportunity that comes once in a hundred years is now at hand. In the coming years, the centipede farming industry will surely become a shining highlight among special breeding industries, with significant economic and social benefits. As a long-term development project, when farmers sell commercial centipedes, they can select superior female centipedes for reproduction, naturally increasing their profits year by year. Breeding advantages:

1. Easy to breed: Century Xinxin golden-headed centipedes are gentle in nature and can be bred at home or in factory-style three-dimensional farms. Factory-style three-dimensional farming is our company’s original method, being the most efficient and fastest way to farm centipedes.

2. Strong reproductive capacity: One clutch contains 60-80 eggs, and under constant temperature farming, they lay eggs twice a year.

3. Low food consumption, wide range of feed: Large-scale farming allows for artificial breeding of feed. Natural farming only requires wheat bran, vegetable leaves, fruits, insects, animal viscera, bone powder, etc., almost cost-free. Prospect of centipede farming - Centipede Farming Base.

4. Strong disease resistance: Adaptability is strong, vitality is vigorous, not prone to diseases, absolutely no risk of avian influenza (chickens), foot-and-mouth disease (pigs), mad cow disease (cattle).

5. Labor-saving and time-saving: Generally fed between 6-8 pm, can be farmed during leisure time.

6. Low investment: Apart from introducing breeds, almost no additional investment is needed. Ordinary families can use empty rooms, balconies, courtyards, front and back yards, and can raise them in jars, boxes, or pools; large-scale farming can involve pen farming or three-dimensional farming, growing green feed and raising insects themselves.
