The main treatment method for kidney tumors is usually surgical resection, which can be divided into radical and palliative surgeries. To improve the quality of life after kidney tumor surgery, patients need to take scientific and effective maintenance measures. If performed reasonably and appropriately, these measures can effectively enhance the patient's physical condition and immunity. Renal cancer patients also need to protect their kidney function post-surgery. Experts from the Biological Treatment Center of the Armed Police General Hospital of Guangxi introduce specific postoperative care methods as follows:

1. Diversify food intake: Consume a variety of vegetables, fruits, and whole grains, focusing primarily on plant-based foods. Ensure daily intake of 3-5 types of vegetables and 2-4 types of fruits, paying special attention to dark-colored vegetables rich in vitamin A precursors and fruits rich in vitamin C.

2. Maintain an appropriate weight, avoiding being underweight or overweight.

3. Keep suitable physical activity levels and actively participate in appropriate sports activities.

4. Strictly quit smoking and it is recommended not to drink alcohol.

5. Limit daily red meat (beef, lamb, pork, and their products) intake to less than 80 grams. Prefer poultry and fish over red meat and reduce soy product consumption.

6. Restrict animal fat intake and choose plant oils in limited quantities. Total fat and oil energy should account for 15%-30% of total energy.

7. Follow a light diet with less salt, limiting daily salt intake to no more than 6 grams, and restrict sugar intake.

8. Avoid moldy or spoiled food. When cooking fish and meat, keep temperatures low, avoid eating burnt food, grilled meat, or preserved meat.

9. Monitor safe limits for food additives, pesticides, and other chemical pollutants.

10. Avoid using nephrotoxic drugs such as non-steroidal anti-inflammatory drugs and certain antibiotics.

11. Actively monitor and control blood pressure within the normal range; maintain normal blood glucose levels.
