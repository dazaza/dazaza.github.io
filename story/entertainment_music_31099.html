This legendary musician who emerged in the UK music scene in 2005 is none other than Captain Poet James Blunt. Despite such brilliant musical achievements, this low-key singer has a legendary quality akin to characters from novels. Born into a military family and graduating from the University of Bristol, before releasing this classic album, James Blunt joined the army and rose to the rank of captain, serving as a member of the Queen's personal guard, and was stationed in Germany and Kosovo where he witnessed bloody civil wars. He became the first British officer dispatched to Pristina, leading a peacekeeping force of 30,000 people. By day, James Blunt was an officer leading a peacekeeping force of 30,000, but every night, he would climb onto his tank and under the tranquil night sky, strum his guitar and write the songs that would be included in this album.
