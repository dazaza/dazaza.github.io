Lin Xuan's children's song CD said that the Nine Days Bright Moon Ring returned to his side, swirling and dancing. The three-eyed corpse crows also had their feathers standing on end, and the clamorous sounds entered the ears. However, this was not yet over. Lin Xuan extended his left hand, and with a flash of spiritual light, a fire-red Zen staff appeared, its design ancient and simple. As soon as a little spiritual power was infused, a vast amount of Buddhist force immediately surged forth.

In the cultivation world, the principle is that the strong are respected. Apart from those at the same level, no one dared to challenge Lin Xuan, especially when another patrol team flew over. Lin Xuan's figure flickered, and he took the initiative to stand in front. The cultivators couldn't help but be greatly alarmed, their faces showing expressions of fear and trepidation. However, the elder among them remained relatively calm, and after deeply bowing to Lin Xuan, he said: "Greetings, Elder. I wonder what matters you have that require the assistance of us juniors?" This was an elder at the peak of the Foundation Establishment stage, his cultivation not high, yet he seemed quite intelligent.

Long before this, he had planned to strongly support Bai Xuan Pavilion so that the cultivators under him would have a place to settle down. However, although Bai Xuan Pavilion dominated Youzhou, it did not have a single Yuan Ying stage expert. This was clearly unacceptable. To develop further, this was a significant obstacle. He himself could not possibly always stay here; Ying'er's cultivation was too low. If they recruited a Yuan Ying stage cultivator, once he left, it would be extremely likely that they would lose control.

The other party's eyes were clear, not like someone telling a lie. However, Lin Xuan didn't have the patience to waste time with a mortal on such things. Shaking his head, he said, "I don't care who your master is; I'm not interested. Don't bother." After saying this, a great surge of blue light enveloped his body, and he was about to leave. "Venerable Sir, please wait. My master is also a mortal."

What triggered the conflict is unclear because both sides have their own accounts, accusing each other. In fact, these are all meaningless; brute force is the only hard truth. From the very beginning of this calamity, nearly two-thirds of the sects in Youzhou’s cultivation world were drawn into it. The rest were on edge, their hearts filled with fear and anxiety.

As Lin Xuan spoke, he released his divine consciousness. The magical treasure in front of him had not lost its spirit nature. Through it, he should be able to understand some information. Of course, there was a prerequisite: having sufficiently powerful divine consciousness. Otherwise, those tiny details would be impossible to detect.

Of course, the peacock knew what Lin Xuan was doing and quietly stood to one side. A short while passed, and Lin Xuan raised his head, his face revealing a look of deep thought. "Leisurely entertainment, your inference should be correct. This woman is a cultivator at the later stage of the Yuan Ying period."
