Let's explore traditional Chinese medicine beauty together Written by Whitening Taste in Intensive Care

Today, we change our taste and return to our traditional treasure ―― Traditional Chinese Medicine (TCM) adjustment. TCM not only focuses on curing diseases but also excels in beauty adjustments. Throughout history, countless TCM practitioners have devoted their lives to finding ways to enhance the beauty of court ladies, leaving behind a wealth of experience that is absolutely worth our attention!

So, everyone, let's explore TCM beauty together with the editor~

"TCM Beauty" has a deep cultural heritage of China. After thousands of years of people's beauty practices, generation after generation of folk beauty and health preservation methods have gathered infinite oriental wisdom.

Whether it is the ancient imperial court with a long history or modern cities, women always rack their brains about how to make themselves more beautiful.

And "TCM Beauty", with its comprehensive internal and external adjustment, and the concept of achieving health and beauty from inside out, is increasingly admired by those who value beauty and health.

However, now there are various TCM beauty products on the market. What kind of method will be effective for oneself? What kind of product will be beneficial? How to find the right product through what kind of method? I think these are the issues that everyone is most concerned about and also the headache-inducing problems.

For this reason, I went to supermarkets, malls, and beauty salons. Later, I found a problem: supermarkets and malls basically don't sell TCM products, while some beauty salons do use TCM products. When asking merchants, they all without exception introduced me to the advantages of their products, telling me what would happen after using them, and that customers said they were great. But these are after all one-sided statements from the merchants. It's hard for us to find actual users to understand unless our friends around us are using them. I'm not saying their products are bad; each product that can survive in the market must have a certain customer base. Personally, I still value our traditional TCM beauty products more.

Because of work, I often surf the internet, so I decided to use the network to search. Taobao.com is the largest shopping platform in China. Usually, I often buy clothes and various international brand cosmetics on it. However, I haven't yet understood our own TCM beauty products online. After searching, I found there are quite a few items here. I decided to ask a store with higher credibility.

I clicked into a TCM beauty store named "Green Forest." To be honest, what initially attracted me to click in was the image of an ancient beauty on their products. Hehe, the love for beauty is shared by both men and women. After reading their introduction, it turned out they were from a family of TCM practitioners, and the products in the small shop were exclusively formulated by their old TCM practitioner. This piqued my curiosity, wow, could they also give me a secret formula, transforming me into a youthful and invincible girl overnight? After reading several product introductions, I found out that the old TCM practitioner in this shop was around 50 years old but looked like he was only 35 in the photo; truly amazing, making me admire and envy him. Thinking about myself looking like this at 50 would be fine too.

Thinking about this, I decided to consult the shopkeeper as a customer to see how she introduces her products to me.

Editor: Shopkeeper, hello, are your products purely made from TCM?

Shopkeeper: Hello, yes, they are all pure TCM. Is there anything you need?

Editor: I want to learn more first.

Shopkeeper: Sure, no problem. Which aspect would you like to learn about?

Editor: Your products, according to the introduction, are formulated by an old TCM practitioner. How long does it take to see results?

Shopkeeper: Yes, they are formulated by the old TCM practitioner. TCM emphasizes internal and external adjustments. It differs from general cosmetic products containing chemical substances or hormones. The effects won't be immediate, but it can address both symptoms and root causes, fundamentally solving the problems. Our product course lasts for a month. The speed of effect varies from person to person. Some people can see results after one course, while others need more than two courses to see results. Generally, the longer you use it, the better the effect.

Editor: Are there any customers who say they didn't see any effect?

Shopkeeper: Yes, some customers are impatient and stop using it after a few times, so they say it's not effective. Our products are not magical elixirs, so it's impossible to achieve satisfactory results after just a few uses. For example, some customers have very yellowish faces but are still young without other illnesses. These customers generally suffer from qi and blood deficiency. We adopt dual supplementation of qi and blood and internal and external adjustments. For such customers, at least two months of adjustment is needed. Some customers give up halfway and then say the product is not effective. As long as they persist, there will be effects.

Editor: It seems that beautifying through TCM methods cannot be rushed.

Shopkeeper: Yes, because everyone's situation is different, the time required for adjustment will vary. As long as the customers can persist, their skin will improve significantly compared to before.

Editor: Okay, thank you, shopkeeper. I've learned a bit. I'll come back and buy when I need it next time. Wishing you booming business!

Shopkeeper: Okay, no need to be polite. Welcome to visit often.

The editor believes that the quality of a product depends on its reputation. The store's credibility is pretty good, with many returning customers. The editor decided to check the opinions of their customers to see if it's true. Among many online buyers, I picked one with a high credit rating. After explaining my intentions, she was happy to chat with me. Below is the main part of our conversation (to protect her privacy, I'll call her Little A).

Editor: How long have you been using the TCM beauty products from this store?

Little A: About eight months ago, due to staying up late for a long time, my skin was in very poor condition. My complexion was dull, my skin was dry, I had some fine lines, and even spots appeared. Since I have sensitive skin, I dared not use random spot-removing products. I wanted to find a gentle TCM spot-removing product. I accidentally discovered this store, so I started trying it then. After seeing good results, I continued using it.

Editor: What products did you use?

Little A: The shopkeeper helped me choose a Whitening Spot-Removing Jade Complexion Pill, an All-in-One Whitening Spot-Removing Mask, and a Skin-Nourishing Hydrating Anti-Wrinkle Mask.

Editor: Did it really work?

Little A: Yes, after using it for a month, my complexion became much more even, my face wasn't as dry, the spots faded a little, and my skin got whiter. Although it wasn't extremely white, it wasn't as sallow as before.

Editor: How has your skin improved now?

Little A: Now my skin is really whiter and softer. The spots have become much lighter and are no longer noticeable. After applying foundation, they are completely invisible. The fine lines have become very faint, and my skin looks moisturized and radiant. My skin even looks rosy in the morning. Honestly, I am very satisfied with the current results. And I've also recommended some friends to use it, and they all said the effect is good.

Editor: Have there been any side effects?

Little A: No, because the shopkeeper asked about my physical condition before recommending the products, so I didn't notice any abnormalities. However, since the medicinal properties of TCM are slow-acting, it took a relatively long time.

Editor: Are you still using it now?

Little A: Yes. I've been taking the Whitening Spot-Removing Jade Complexion Pill for six months, and I've been using the All-in-One Mask and the Skin-Nourishing Hydrating Anti-Wrinkle Mask twice a week consistently for eight months. The shopkeeper suggested that I take the Jade Complexion Pill every two months for two months now because it can also prevent aging. Women are most afraid of getting old, haha.

Editor: It seems our long-standing beauty treasure is really good.

Little A: Yes. You can try it out and talk to the shopkeeper.

TCM believes that although it is impossible for humans to remain young forever, by replenishing the deficiencies in the organs and qi-blood, maintaining the balance of yin and yang in the body, it is indeed possible to delay the arrival of aging.
