The process of opening a Taobao store is as follows.

Step one: The goal of opening an online store isn't just on the internet, but in your mind. Many people want to open a store, some have indeed opened their own stores, with various items. It's difficult to gather ten items, but they are unclear about who their target audience is and the future direction of their store. Therefore, the first step is to conduct market research, understand our own business goals and the market, and have a clear understanding and reasonable plan for the future direction of our store.

Step two: Apply for an account. A prerequisite for this step is to activate online banking (recommended China Construction Bank Online Banking, Industrial and Commercial Bank of China Online Banking, Agricultural Bank of China Online Banking, China Merchants Bank Online Banking, etc.). After ensuring that you have activated online banking from one of the banks, complete the following steps: First, apply for a Taobao account. Applying for a Taobao account is as simple and fast as applying for a QQ number. Just follow the prompts and fill in the relevant information (Note: try to fill in real information). Second, apply to activate Alipay. Alipay is to Taobao what cash is to daily transactions. Alipay is an insurance tool provided by Taobao for online transaction payments. Almost all C2C transactions on Taobao are realized through Alipay. The application process for Alipay is similar to that for a Taobao account. After activating Alipay, we need to obtain Taobao's real-name certification. Once real-name certification is completed, everything is ready.

Step three: Stocking up. Like opening a physical store, stocking up is a necessary part of opening a store. There are many channels and methods for stocking up. You can get supplies from surrounding markets, familiar areas, relatives, friends, or find online store agents. Online store agents can save many intermediate steps. Major sources of supplies include:

1. Wholesale markets. This is a common channel for stocking up. For example, clothing, we can choose large clothing wholesale markets, where large clothing wholesale markets have significant price advantages. If you have good negotiation skills or luck, and build long-term relationships with wholesale market bosses, you may receive many benefits, such as exchanges and returns.

2. Manufacturer supplies. Especially during this special period, manufacturers have large inventories. For companies mainly focused on exports, affected by the international financial tsunami, there are large amounts of inventory products in warehouses, with sufficient supply, and product quality naturally not worrying.

3. Online store agents. As a network agent, we don't need inventory, just reach agreements with merchants in certain aspects, and use their supplies to directly open our own stores. Merchants providing network agents generally have sufficient supplies, diverse types, complete information, and good service attitudes, but may require certain franchise or agency fees.

4. Large wholesalers. For larger wholesalers, we can go to “Ali Buying” to find them. However, here the information volume is large, we need to carefully analyze and compare, find merchants that meet our requirements and are reliable, but note that the wholesale volume here is generally large. If you have a few acquaintances who buy together, this can be a great choice.

Step four: Upload goods. Taobao specifically provides sellers with a free product upload tool - Taobao Assistant. This tool makes it more humanized, easier, and faster for sellers to upload products. We can first edit the related product information (name, origin, location, nature, appearance, quantity, transaction method, transaction deadline, etc.) offline, then perform batch uploads, and can also batch-edit product information.

Step five: Marketing and promotion. Once the store is open, how to boost your popularity becomes a key issue. There are many ways to promote marketing, such as browsing forums, replying to posts, attaching your store address, promoting among classmates and friends, attaching your store address in QQ groups and Wangwang group chats. These are all good methods. If you're still a student, you can also promote your store through posters.

Step six: Mid-sale service. The quality of mid-sale service may directly affect the success or failure of this transaction. When customers decide whether to buy this item, they have many uncertainties. They hope to further communicate with the seller to eliminate these uncertainties. When the seller communicates with you, we should be as sincere as possible, answer the questions they care about, and introduce the product's advantages appropriately, thereby gaining their recognition.

Step seven: Transaction. When both parties have agreed on the trading issues, they start the transaction.

Step eight: After-sales service. Don't think that once the transaction is completed, your trading activity is over. That's not true. After the transaction is completed, we need to appropriately remind the buyer when shipping, or inquire about matters related to this transaction. If there are problems with the goods, resolve them timely and decisively, and never keep finding excuses for yourself.

Step nine: Evaluation. After both parties successfully complete the transaction, they can evaluate each other for this transaction. Creditworthiness is an important factor in online transactions. To jointly build a credit environment, if the transaction is satisfactory, it's best to give the other party a positive evaluation and gain their positive evaluation through good service. If the transaction fails, give a negative evaluation or complain to the website to reduce losses and warn others. If the other party complains, handle it quickly to avoid leaving stains on your credit.

Finally, wish you a thriving business. Please adopt if satisfied.

Once you have these accounts and have passed real-name certification, newcomers face a choice - what should my store sell? As a newbie seller, there are generally the following situations:

How to choose recharge software for virtual recharge stores

There are too many virtual recharge software options online. As a newbie seller, it's often confusing to choose when entering the virtual recharge market. With so many options, each agent can boast about the advantages of the software they represent, but in reality, choosing an agent is like choosing a person. I think newcomers should be cautious about agents who maliciously criticize other software to elevate their own. This reflects a character issue, and such agents can be ignored. Competition in this market is normal, but denigrating others to elevate oneself indicates psychological issues. How much can someone who hasn't used the software know about its pros and cons? Sometimes, agents might falsely accuse competitors' software of flaws while ignoring their own software's shortcomings. Is it worth dealing with someone of questionable character? If they can speak ill of others today, they can speak ill of you tomorrow!

Professional virtual software rechargers generally know the commonly used software. I'll analyze each one individually. Since I represent five, I might not cover all the details completely, so apologies in advance.

1. Yi Sai

Yi Sai can be considered a veteran in the recharge software market. Let's directly discuss its pros and cons.

Advantages:

1. Supports automatic recharges for Taobao, Paipai, and Baidu stores, capable of recharging QQ services, national mobile phone bills, and game point cards. It supports many regions for recharges ranging from 1 to 10 yuan.

2. Offers four agency options: monthly card (30 yuan), quarterly card (70 yuan), annual card (200 yuan), and lifetime card (300 yuan), all of which can develop sub-agents. Monthly cards can only develop monthly cards, quarterly cards can develop monthly and quarterly cards, annual cards can develop all versions.

3. High rebate percentages. Once you join Yi Sai, you can develop sub-agents and enjoy 65%, 9%, and 6% three-level rebates. For example, if your subordinate A opens a lifetime authorization, your rebate would be 65% of 300 yuan, i.e., 195 yuan. If A develops another subordinate B, your rebate would be 9%, i.e., 27 yuan, and if B develops C, your rebate would be 18 yuan. Of course, you can develop unlimited A's, and you will earn commissions when subordinates renew or open additional stores.

Disadvantages:

1. Higher failure rates during peak recharge periods at the end or beginning of the month.

2. Although game point cards are more than Jie Yi Tong, they are still not comprehensive enough.

3. Smaller denomination recharges cost 0.2 yuan more than face value, e.g., a 3-yuan recharge costs 3.2 yuan, up to 20 yuan.
