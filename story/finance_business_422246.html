by fuukee0019 on 2011-04-28 10:58:53

At around 15:00 on April 25th, Zhan visited Kang Clinic at Luoxi New City Food Street with her friend Qin to undergo an abortion. At about 20:00, during the operation, Zhan suddenly exhibited adverse symptoms and died after unsuccessful emergency treatment by the clinic doctor. After Zhan's relatives arrived at the scene, they believed that Zhan's death was a medical accident caused by improper surgical measures taken by the doctor, and demanded that the clinic take responsibility. The clinic responded that they would provide a response after relevant departments issued an investigation conclusion.

Dissatisfied with this, over 30 relatives and acquaintances of the deceased subsequently gathered at the clinic demanding an immediate explanation. Relevant staff from the local government and health department of Panyu District quickly arrived on the scene to handle the situation and immediately organized negotiations between family representatives, the clinic director, and relevant departments.

In the early hours of the 26th, when the funeral home staff were normally handling the remains, the deceased's relatives obstructed the removal of the body. After providing sufficient explanations to the crowd present, the police and relevant government departments took measures to control the scene order and legally removed those involved in causing trouble for investigation. Meanwhile, the male director of the clinic and the female doctor who performed the treatment were also taken back for review, swiftly quelling the situation. Currently, the health department has sealed the clinic, and the involved doctor and clinic director have been criminally detained by the police on suspicion of medical malpractice.
