Driver runs red light, hits 3 cars, abandons car and flees - the accident car was driven away forcibly

by zzf000zxys6 on 2011-08-05 13:00:18

The luxury car involved in the accident drew a crowd of onlookers.

Firefighters rescued the passenger trapped inside the car.

The license plate of the offending vehicle had been knocked off.

On the night of the day before yesterday, at the intersection of Zhongshan Road East and Hongwu Road in Xinjiekou, Baixia District, Nanjing City, a Jaguar worth millions of yuan forcibly ran a red light, successively colliding with three cars. The driver of the Jaguar then fled the scene. Subsequently, the offending Jaguar was also driven away by someone. At 8 PM last night, reporters learned from the Second Traffic Police Brigade of Nanjing that the owner of the offending vehicle had come to the Accident Handling Detachment at 6 PM yesterday to accept investigation. He admitted that he was driving the car at the time of the accident. The reason for leaving the scene was fear, and also because his head felt dizzy and he wanted to vomit due to the impact of the airbag. Later, he left the accident scene in confusion. As for who drove his car away later, he is currently asking his friends to find out.

Luxury Car Collides with Three Cars in the Dead of Night

At 11:45 PM the night before yesterday, reporters received a tip-off that a chain collision occurred in Xinjiekou, the bustling downtown area of Nanjing City. A car collided with three others, after which the driver abandoned the car and fled. Moreover, the offending vehicle was also driven away.

Reporters immediately rushed to the scene and discovered that the accident happened at the intersection of Zhongshan Road East and Hongwu Road. There were many cars parked haphazardly at the scene. Among them, a red taxi was severely damaged, almost detached from its body. A male passenger was trapped inside the car, covering his head and shouting for help. At the scene, there was also a black Santana sedan, whose front part was severely damaged due to the collision. Meanwhile, in another lane, there was another taxi that had also been hit and stopped at the scene.

The offending driver fled the scene after abandoning the car.

During the course of the reporter's interview, the driver of the offending vehicle was never seen. Onlookers all expressed that the driver of the offending vehicle had already been arranged to leave the scene by taxi.

A master Chang, who was among the onlookers at the scene, told the police that at the time of the incident, he just happened to be passing by the intersection. Due to waiting for the red light, he stood still at the intersection. At this moment, he noticed a red taxi moving normally from west to east on Zhongshan Road East. Meanwhile, a black luxury car was speeding south to north on Hongwu Road. However, facing the red light, the luxury car showed no intention of stopping, directly rushing towards the taxi and ramming into the right front side of the taxi. The sound was quite alarming, and Master Chang was stunned, thinking that it would surely result in injuries. But the luxury car did not stop, continuing to move forward and colliding with a black Santana traveling from east to west on Zhongshan Road East. After colliding with two cars, the luxury car could no longer continue forward, so it reversed. During the reversing process, it collided with another taxi traveling from west to east on Zhongshan Road East. At this point, the luxury car finally came to a stop.

Subsequently, a young man in his thirties got out of the driver's seat, hailed a taxi, and drove away. The incident happened suddenly, and everyone was too late to stop him. At that time, Master Chang saw three other passengers in the luxury car, two men and one woman.

The Luxury Car Was Driven Away

Master Chang introduced that when the drivers of the crashed vehicles saw the luxury car driver abandon the car and flee, they surrounded the luxury car. Some criticized, while others cursed loudly. A man sitting in the back seat of the luxury car got out, pointing at everyone and cursing back, even threatening to beat people up. Everyone feared being beaten, so they dispersed. The man took advantage of the situation, got back into the car, and drove the offending car away. No one dared to stop him at the time.

Originally, both the offending driver and the offending vehicle had left the scene, but a license plate reading "Su A77T55" from the offending luxury car was still embedded in the taxi. The traffic police of the Second Brigade immediately informed the duty leader of the brigade about the situation at the scene. Facing multiple-car collisions, the offending vehicle and its driver had privately left the scene, immediately qualifying as fleeing after causing an accident. Firefighters who arrived subsequently used professional demolition tools to forcibly open the taxi door and rescue the trapped passenger, who was then sent to the hospital for emergency treatment.

Later, Mr. Bai, the driver of the first taxi that was hit, recalled that at the moment of the incident, not only was the passenger trapped, but he himself was also dazed by the collision. When he came to his senses, he was only concerned with saving people and did not have time to deal with the offending vehicle. He thought that the offending vehicle driver would take the initiative to come forward and handle the matter, but didn't expect him to run away.

At the accident site, a middle-aged man in his thirties, carrying a bag, told the police, "What's the big deal! Isn't it just about compensating money!" When the traffic police indicated that they would take him back to the traffic police station for investigation, he quickly changed his tune, saying he didn't know the offending vehicle driver and was just watching the commotion. However, eyewitnesses identified him as the person who drove away the offending vehicle, but the man denied it outright.

At this time, Master Song, riding a motor scooter, quietly revealed to the police that the offending vehicle that had been driven away was parked in a parking lot 500 meters away from the accident scene. It turned out that Master Song also witnessed the accident process. He was indignant at the offending driver's escape and even more disgusted with the arrogance of the people in the offending vehicle. So, he secretly followed the offending vehicle on his motor scooter and found that the offending vehicle, after traveling 500 meters along Zhongshan Road East, turned into a parking lot within an alley. Based on Master Song's identification, the traffic police handling the accident indeed found the severely damaged offending vehicle. Reporters saw that it was a British luxury car worth a million yuan - a Jaguar.

The Offending Vehicle Owner Is a "Good Kid"

The on-site accident wasn't fully handled until 1 AM yesterday morning, and at that time, it was still impossible to obtain more information about the offending vehicle owner.

At noon yesterday, reporters learned from the Nanjing Traffic Management Bureau that according to the vehicle information comparison, there were no issues such as cloned plates, and the offending vehicle completely belonged to the case of fleeing after causing an accident. More details about the offender are still under investigation.

In the afternoon yesterday, reporters again rushed to the Second Traffic Police Brigade Accident Handling Detachment. Deputy Chief Xu of the detachment said that on the night of the accident, based on the vehicle registration information, the police had already gone to the registered address of the owner to look for the owner, but failed to find the owner themselves. Calls to their mobile phone remained unanswered via text message. Currently, in response to the owner's delay in coming forward, the police were searching everywhere while also explaining the consequences to the relatives, hoping the family would cooperate in persuading the owner to come forward early to explain the situation to the traffic management department.

Deputy Chief Xu also stated that from the owner's violation information, this Jaguar worth a million yuan was purchased by the owner in January last year. In over a year of driving, there was only one instance of running a red light and a few minor parking violations. In theory, during regular driving, the owner was relatively law-abiding.

However, every bridge has its own path, every road its own direction. If the owner himself was the offending driver, regardless of whether he was driving a luxury car or not, the police would not tolerate it, firmly treating it as a case of fleeing after causing an accident. Upon finding the offending vehicle driver, they would impose a fine of 1000 yuan, deduct 12 points, and detain for 15 days.

The Offending Vehicle Owner Has Actively Accepted Investigation

Yesterday afternoon, reporters found the residence registered by the offending vehicle owner. This housing belongs to the municipal government housing of Nanjing City and is one of the few high-rise residential buildings on Chengxian Street in Nanjing. A man claiming to be the brother of the offending vehicle owner told reporters that his younger brother had always been doing business and was relatively low-key. In the morning, he had contacted his younger brother once, and he said that due to dizziness, he planned to go to the Second Traffic Police Brigade in the afternoon to accept the investigation. "My younger brother should not have been driving under the influence, as he usually doesn't drink alcohol at home. My younger brother may have delayed dealing with the accident because he was scared, having never encountered something like this!"

In the evening yesterday, reporters again contacted Deputy Chief Xu. Deputy Chief Xu said that the offending vehicle owner later realized the seriousness of the problem and also felt uneasy inside. At 6 PM, he voluntarily went to the Second Traffic Police Brigade to accept the investigation. Regarding why he fled the scene after the accident, Mr. Han explained that at the time, seeing such a serious incident made him afraid, and also because his head was dizzy and he felt nauseous due to the impact of the airbag. Later, he left the accident scene in a daze. As for who drove his car away later, he is currently asking his friends to find out. Deputy Chief Xu of the Second Traffic Police Brigade Accident Handling Detachment stated that after the investigation of the facts, they would punish the offending vehicle owner, Mr. Han. They would also appropriately penalize the person who helped Mr. Han drive the offending vehicle away from the scene depending on the severity of the circumstances. For more details, the traffic police department is currently investigating.

By Li Haiyong, our newspaper correspondent
