More than 40 urine bags flew out of a residential building and hit passers-by (Picture)

by aehslih57 on 2012-02-26 12:03:03

More than 40 urine bags were thrown down from upstairs

It was learned that, before Xiao Huang was hit by the plastic bag last night, at least seven or eight pedestrians had been hit by "urine bags", but because they could not find the person who threw the plastic bags, they had no choice but to leave the scene.

No one in the building admitted throwing urine bags downstairs

Finally, Xiao Huang gathered all the plastic bags on the ground to the roadside and waited for the sanitation department to clean them up. The property management personnel said that they would continue to look into this matter, and once the owner who threw the plastic bags was found, they would immediately notify the police station.

The evening before yesterday, on the sidewalk and slow lane outside the Jiangnan Mingfu residential area on Jiqingmen Street, small plastic bags filled with urine fell from the sky from time to time, and passers-by were frequently affected. Seven or eight passers-by were hit by plastic bags, and their clothes were wet with urine. Eventually, the police intervened, and no one threw plastic bags downstairs anymore. However, none of the residents upstairs admitted that they were the ones who threw the bags.

"This time I must get an explanation. I can't let this person without public morals harm others!" Xiao Huang made up his mind and called the police. To prevent other passers-by from being hit by plastic bags, Xiao Huang stood by the roadside and kindly reminded the pedestrians to walk around to avoid being hit by filth. While Xiao Huang was waiting for the police to arrive, another plastic bag filled with turbid liquid fell down. After landing, the plastic bag broke, and the liquid inside flowed out. An extremely angry Xiao Huang looked up at the building but could not see anyone throwing plastic bags.

At around 9:30 PM the previous evening, Xiao Huang, who worked in a restaurant, was on his way home after work. He was holding an umbrella when he suddenly heard a sound on his umbrella. Then he felt something hit his umbrella. Xiao Huang's umbrella tilted, and he only saw a white small plastic bag fall down, hitting his leg directly, wetting his pants. Upon closer inspection, the white plastic bag on the ground had already broken, containing a slightly yellow liquid. Immediately afterward, he smelled a urine odor on his umbrella. He lifted his leg and smelled it, finding that the wet pants also emitted a urine odor.

Xiao Huang noticed that there were already quite a few such urine bags on the roadside. Counting carefully, there were more than 40. These urine bags resembled the thin plastic bags used to hold soy milk at breakfast stalls. Some were tied at the mouth, but most had already broken. "You are not the first person to be hit. Some people are even unluckier; the plastic bags directly hit their bodies, and both their shirts and pants were soaked with urine!" Zhang Shifu, who was waiting for the bus nearby, told Xiao Huang that about ten minutes before he was hit by the plastic bag, a young man passed by here without an umbrella. As a result, a plastic bag fell directly onto the young man's shoulder. After breaking, it rolled onto the ground. The young man discovered that the plastic bag contained urine and angrily cursed at the upstairs residents. However, since no one admitted it, the young man eventually left cursing.

Property management personnel of the Jiangnan Mingfu residential area stated that these plastic bags falling outside the community were definitely thrown from the nearby small high-rise residential building. Because apart from the building close to the roadside, people in other positions could not possibly throw them so far. Property management personnel said that on the evening of the 25th, plastic bags were thrown down at this place. Several pedestrians were hit by plastic bags. Someone reported the situation to the property management, and after searching for a long time, they still couldn't find the person who threw the plastic bags.

After the police arrived, they went to the residential building with the property management personnel to begin investigating house by house, but no one admitted that it was their family who threw the bags. The property management personnel said, "Someone threw them bag by bag. It is estimated that there is a bedridden patient at home who cannot use the toilet themselves. Even so, dirty things should not be thrown directly downstairs! This kind of person is too immoral!" According to the investigation by the property management personnel and feedback from passers-by, the plastic bags might have been thrown from the households on the 6th or 7th floor. However, when the police knocked on doors on these two floors to inquire about the situation, no one admitted doing it.

A young man was hit by a "urine bag" thrown from upstairs

Xiao Huang looked up and saw a small high-rise residential building with more than 10 floors near the roadside in the community. It was clear that the plastic bags were thrown from upstairs. "What kind of person is so immoral? Throwing such things randomly downstairs!" Although Xiao Huang was very annoyed, he observed for several minutes but could not clearly see which floor the plastic bags were thrown from.
