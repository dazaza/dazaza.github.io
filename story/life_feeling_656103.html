Flowers Bloom in the Rainy Season

Flowers bloom in the rainy season. This year, I am sixteen, full of youthful vitality. The flowers are in full bloom, the grass is lush and green. In my colorful and flawless dreams, there is a piece of blue sky, where many indescribable years are stationed, and interspersed with unspeakable adolescent thoughts...

We rush through the vibrant campus and rest in a corner bathed in sunlight. In our youth, we sincerely exchange unique heart voices, splash with distinctive passion, and listen to each other's released serenity.

Time is too stubborn, always moving forward without ever looking back with longing. We, who possess both innocence and maturity, met that year, which predetermined today's separation. Foolishly spending large amounts of time meticulously counting the thorns along the way, we were left with countless scars, vividly recalling how beautiful the places we once inhabited were... After a period of confusion and helplessness, we strive hard to train ourselves to have strong wings, madly searching for our own dreams...

The fleeting years dock at the harbor of dreams, chasing all the way to the next crossroad. Now, at the intersection of seventeen, we pause, turn around, and look far ahead. The pain and harm, happiness and warmth on this road, and the splendid and colorful dreams blooming on bicycles, we count the past buried with bits of joy and sorrow.

Strength understood through tears is the happiest kind of bravery.

Our years flow like this, reaching a climax, then a low note, finally drawing to a close...

When flowers bloom, it's lively; when they fall, it's quiet, as if all related to our lost years.

Time has strong corrosiveness, drying up test papers filled with words, yet it cannot dry up our memories. Memories cannot be diluted.

No matter what, in the fleeting years, we have walked a part of the journey.

In the next leg of the journey, I believe that we young ones, fearless of heaven and earth, will charge ahead like warriors on the road to pursue our dreams, pondering over it, sacrificing everything for it, marveling at the quiet blossoming of a miracle.

Youth is brief but intense, unique and magnificent, like a meteor gracefully streaking across the sky, self-absorbedly blooming. A kind of beauty is called waiting alone in solitude for memories, and time will gradually settle into the color of memory... The sunset is so fiery red, like deep longing, forming a golden wheel of blessings and prayers, gently recording these parting words before departure. I hope this dialogue will be the perfect ending in our imagination...

I hope we meet again at the next crossroad...
