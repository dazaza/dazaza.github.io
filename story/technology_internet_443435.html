Tarot Divination for Each Zodiac's Mid-Year Fortune in 2007 [Repost]

by kfjro082 on 2011-06-09 12:52:25

Aries - Devil (Upright) + Seven of Cups (Upright)

Monthly Focus: This is an idealistic February, please don't overly rely on feelings to make judgments!

Perhaps due to wanting too much internally, you have inadvertently fallen into a state of greed. Whether it's career work, love relationships, money, or power, you want to hold everything tightly in your hands without letting go. However, the actual situation does not meet your expectations, so you turn to indulge in fantasies, hoping that one day all those things will belong to you. This month, you seem to be living according to intuition, but that kind of self-righteous happiness can blind you. Therefore, when encountering problems, it is recommended to be rational and step out of the situation to deal with blind spots, so as not to be led astray by negative energy!

Taurus - The Hanged Man (Reversed) + Empress of Cups (Upright)

Monthly Focus: This is a liberating February where you should let go of constraints and pursue inner happiness!

Previously, you seemed to be living with a sense of injustice and endurance, finding pleasure in an uncomfortable life regardless of how others around you perceive you. But this month, you can finally break free from those real-world constraints or unnecessary persistence. The release of mental and physical shackles will make you feel enlightened about everything. This is a moment of self-liberation. What you need now is emotional comfort; temporarily stop thinking about the pressures of reality, love yourself well, find ways to make yourself happy, and you will be full of energy to face your path!

Gemini - The Star (Reversed) + King of Pentacles (Reversed)

Monthly Focus: This is an easily overthinking February, please stop creating unrealistic dreams!

Whether it's due to others' critical views or your own inner weakness and inability to rally, this month you seem to lack the ability to resist reality. All you want to do is cover your eyes, plug your ears, and retreat into your own world, pretending that the problems don't exist. But this is not a time to dream peacefully. Your innocence and unrealistic ideas only bring short-term security and are of no help to the actual situation. It is suggested that you look back and examine the crux of the problem, recognize your current situation, and then you will find the true opportunity to solve the problem!

Cancer - The Sun (Upright) + Seven of Swords (Upright)

Monthly Focus: This is a joyful and pleasant February, please overlook the details and focus on greater goals!

Everything seems to be going according to your wishes, giving you a sense of satisfaction. This month is the phase where your fortune is rising. Not only is your life enjoyable with a good mood, but your existing work, career, and love relationships are progressing smoothly. It is suggested that you seize the opportunity to double your efforts and take it to the next level! However, perhaps habitual insecurity is at play, making you still wary of certain things. Please remember not to overthink and cleverly avoid the unnecessary small details; focusing on larger goals is your priority!

Leo - The High Priestess (Reversed) + Nine of Swords (Reversed)

Monthly Focus: This is a February filled with excessive worry, please relax your mind and unlock the psychological key!

Even if you want to live simply and carefree at this stage, it seems impossible. Too many worries and concerns are troubling you, making you endlessly think and rethink without resolution. This month, your thoughts are in chaos. Sometimes you feel the nightmare has passed, sometimes you think you're smart enough to solve problems, but reality proves that these are just drops in the bucket, and you're still stuck in the problems. It is suggested that you stop straining your brain, relax your mind, open your eyes, and find the psychological key to resolve those worries and concerns!

Virgo - The Moon (Upright) + Two of Wands (Reversed)

Monthly Focus: This is an uncertain February, please wait patiently to clarify the direction of the next step!

Your originally planned plans seem to have failed. Now you can only lament the lost goal. For you, this month represents a dark low point. Whether it's expanding in your work or love relationships, you've lost the initiative opportunities and can only stay in place. It is suggested that you don't rush, don't retreat because of temporary setbacks. Although you face bottlenecks and difficulties, it is undoubtedly a good opportunity to adjust direction. The previous methods obviously didn't work, so give yourself some time to reflect and wait for your good opportunity!

Libra - The Lovers (Reversed) + Two of Cups (Upright)

Monthly Focus: This is an emotionally intense February, please carefully choose each new and old relationship!

For you, maintaining balance in relationships is most important. But this month, it seems difficult to achieve. Whether you focus on your career or love relationships, there is a tendency for cracks to appear. Existing cooperative relationships may change, or emotional issues between two people may become more prominent. It is suggested that you pay special attention to selection issues. Whether you want to maintain old relationships or shift to new ones, you must think clearly before acting. Your emotions are very intense this month, so don't make impulsive decisions due to fleeting novelty!

Scorpio - The Tower (Upright) + Nine of Wands (Upright)

Monthly Focus: This is a challenging February, please strengthen your position to cope with environmental changes!

The current environment seems unfavorable to you. If you are concerned about your career, carefully observe internal organizational issues. Upcoming personnel changes may affect your current position. Regardless, you need to work harder on job performance. If you are concerned about love relationships, face the issues between the two of you. Long-suppressed emotions are about to erupt. Pretending everything is fine and continuing to avoid could lead to emotional crises! It is suggested that you remain vigilant and face everything this month. Holding your ground will prevent fear of environmental changes!

Sagittarius - Justice (Reversed) + Four of Wands (Reversed)

Monthly Focus: This is an unstable February, please firmly establish your intentions to stabilize future plans!

What was once your source of security has now become something you cannot control. This month, you will carry a deep sense of uncertainty, aimlessly exploring the road ahead. In fact, this is the result of your wavering stance. Your heart has lost direction, you have forgotten your original trust, thus shaking the stable foundation. Therefore, it is suggested that you listen to your inner voice, don't doubt what you have built in the past. Once you regain your initial firm willpower, you can continue to create a beautiful future plan!

Capricorn - Wheel of Fortune (Upright) + Nine of Cups (Upright)

Monthly Focus: This is a satisfying and wish-fulfilling February, please seize luck to create your future life!

Whatever your previous efforts and prayers were aimed at, this month is the stage where you receive the favor of fortune. As long as you sincerely paid and nurtured, the long-awaited desire has the chance to come true! You can choose to passively welcome this good luck, or actively create opportunities following the trend, opening up a bigger pattern. Therefore, it is suggested that you boldly plan your future life, whether expanding in work or touching base in love relationships. As long as you are willing to act, unexpected good results await!

Aquarius - Strength (Reversed) + Six of Cups (Reversed)

Monthly Focus: This is an emotionally unstable February, please summon courage to overcome inner fragility!

It might be the interference of past memories, or the impact of the current environment. Currently, you are retreating step by step, almost powerless to resist the pressures you perceive. You might choose to give up, gradually being subdued by dissatisfaction. But this is merely a temporary loss of belief. You haven't lost your original energy; it's just that your emotions are unstable now, and your inner fragility has been triggered. Once you understand the core of the problem, you should have the courage to face it. Don't think you can't do it; summoning courage will overcome your inner weakness!

Pisces - The Emperor (Reversed) + Three of Swords (Upright)

Monthly Focus: This is a vulnerable and easily melancholic February, please muster your spirit to confront the inner key!

Some things seem to target your weaknesses, hitting your heart one after another and sprinkling salt on your wounds. Thus, this month, your mood is prone to depression, and you lose the ability to manage yourself. Perhaps others have seen through your vulnerability and attacked you effortlessly. Therefore, please gather yourself, and no matter what, stay resolute so that others can't hurt you! It is suggested that you try to shift your attention and do some easy, simple things that make you happy. Things you don't care about won't become your Achilles' heel!
