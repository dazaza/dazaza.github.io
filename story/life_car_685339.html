As the automotive beauty market expands, competition among car beauty shops is also becoming increasingly fierce. So, how can one stand out in such intense competition? Good management plays a crucial role, and as a result, management software tailored for the automotive beauty industry has emerged. Nowadays, there are various types of software available on the market, so how do you determine whether the software you choose can better manage the operation of your shop?

Firstly, let's analyze the issues present in managing a car beauty shop:

1. Disorder and loss of control over customer profiles, vehicle records, beauty treatment records, etc.

2. Loss of old customers due to the inability to systematically monitor customer activities and provide effective warnings; lack of initiative in customer service.

3. Accessory prices change daily, so how can you effectively track these changes, maintain reasonable accessory inventory levels, and improve procurement?

4. There are many accessory suppliers, producing different parts, but how well do we understand their consumption trends within our own factory?

5. Low efficiency in beauty services, with no effective way to control the progress and quality of treatments;

6. The calculation of commissions for technical staff and sales personnel involves a large amount of work, making it difficult to conduct truly effective performance evaluations;

7. Manual statistics on product sales, inventory backlog, customer distribution, and company operating results involve heavy workloads and are prone to errors, preventing real-time, accurate monitoring.
