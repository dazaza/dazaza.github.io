Drowning victim and rescuer become friends (pic)

by xue94fwsh on 2012-03-02 18:40:48

Ma Shoumin was on his knees on the bed giving Ning Jianshe a massage treatment. □ Reporter Fang Lin, Correspondent Lu JiaoJiao, text and pictures. Rescuer Ning Jianshe, who suffers from sinusitis symptoms but expects no reward, and rescued person Ma Shoumin, who is determined to repay kindness! Two people originally from different provinces, different ethnic groups, each living on opposite sides of the Yellow River, became close brothers due to one act of bravery in saving a life in danger and the other's understanding of gratitude.

At noon on December 6th, in a less than 20 square meter rented room in a city village in Sanmenxia, Ning Jianshe's family lived there. When the reporter arrived, Ma Shoumin was on his knees on the bed giving Ning Jianshe a massage treatment (as shown in the picture). Subsequently, the two spoke about their experiences of getting to know each other.

"I just want to prove that good people will definitely get good rewards."

Every July is the time when the Sanmenxia Dam opens its gates for flood release. Villagers on both sides of the Yellow River go to shallow waters to catch fish, and Ma Shoumin is no exception.

On the evening of July 3rd last year, while fishing near Guanyao Village in Sanmen Town, Pinglu County, he accidentally fell into the turbulent Yellow River water. Ning Jianshe, who was at the scene at the time, risked his life, jumped into the Yellow River, and saved him. In the aftermath, Ma Shoumin only remembered that the person who saved him was from Henan, but he didn't know his name or what he looked like.

On July 9th, 2010, based on a media report about this incident, Ma Shoumin found Ning Jianshe's home. Since then, the two have kept in regular contact. During an accidental conversation, Ma Shoumin heard Ning Jianshe say that his lumbar disc pain was severe, so he kept this matter in mind.

Using the traditional Chinese medical skills he learned from his grandfather since childhood, Ma Shoumin prepared a prescription and external ointment, went up the mountain to collect herbs, and then specially went to Sanmenxia to treat Ning Jianshe, giving him massages and treatments... After one course of treatment, Ning Jianshe's condition had improved significantly.

When talking about his actions of gratitude, Ma Shoumin said slowly: "In society, they say that good people don't get good rewards, and good people don't receive the respect they deserve. We need to use our actions to refute this negative trend and prove that good people will definitely get good rewards!"

They let love continue infinitely.

In Ning Jianshe's eyes, Ma Shoumin is a "solid and straightforward person who doesn't beat around the bush and thinks it's normal to take a little loss."

Ma Shoumin also laughed and said: "Old Ning is honest and eager to help others, whether it's friends or strangers, he treats them all the same."

Their shared personalities made Ning Jianshe and Ma Shoumin friends, and their common compassion for helping those in danger forged a brotherly bond between them.

"After experiencing a great calamity, one sees through life and death and might as well do some things they want to do, live life uprightly," Ma Shoumin said. After being saved by Ning Jianshe, he felt an even stronger desire to do good deeds and charitable acts.

In October of last year, during a chance encounter where he helped someone else see a doctor, Ma Shoumin joined the National Health Work Committee for Pregnant Women, Infants, and Children Industry Development, becoming a caring individual dedicated to paying attention to the safety of food and products for pregnant women, infants, and children, helping them protect their rights.

And "China Good Person" Ning Jianshe continues to extend his kindness. Now already a member of the Sanmenxia 110 Water Rescue Team, he has participated in three water rescues to date.
