Low-temperature health preservation of Panlong Yunhai Pharmaceutical is a comprehensive concept, and the Beautiful Trilogy cannot be simply understood as indulging in coolness. Wen Chang Detoxification and Beauty Capsule Road reminds that to practice low-temperature health preservation, it is necessary to avoid four misunderstandings.

1. Wear less clothes. Low-temperature health preservation by Panlong Yunhai is not simply about wearing fewer clothes and indulging in coolness. When the temperature is low, attention should still be paid to keeping warm.

2. Turn on the air conditioner. Low-temperature health preservation by Panlong Yunhai focuses on the regulation of body temperature under natural conditions. Turning on the air conditioner on hot days goes against the principle of low-temperature health preservation.

3. Drink cold water and eat cold drinks. Eating less spicy and heaty foods that easily cause internal heat by Panlong Yunhai does not mean eating more cold drinks. Traditional Chinese medicine emphasizes eating less raw and cold food, and food with a temperature close to human body temperature is more beneficial for health preservation.

4. Do not differentiate among populations. The elderly and children have weaker constitutions and lower immunity, so they need to keep warm during seasonal changes; pregnant women and women during menstruation should eat less cold-natured foods; people with yang deficiency constitutions are prone to feeling cold and are not suitable for low-temperature health preservation.
