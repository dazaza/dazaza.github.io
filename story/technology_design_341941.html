Thailand tourism photography base, a paradise for wedding photography

by xiezhen543 on 2010-04-08 09:07:58

Dressed in a beautiful wedding dress, holding hands with your beloved one, taking pre-wedding photos in Tangshan, touring around Phuket Island with picturesque scenery, and also hosting a Thai-style wedding with exotic flavor. With the official launch of Jetta Holidays International Travel Service's "Thailand Tourism Photography Base", going to Thailand for weddings, pre-wedding photo shoots, and vacations has become the choice of many newlyweds.

Jetta Holidays International Travel Service General Manager Zhang Xiangming introduced that the "tourism + wedding photography" product launched this time is the core product of "Extraordinary 1+1". Regarding wedding photography, there is a professional Chinese wedding photography team providing on-site services in Tangshan; guests can proceed with just one couple, the travel time can be determined by the guests at any time, with Chinese communication, making the guests feel relaxed and worry-free. This is the key difference of this route from similar products in the market.

Currently, the itineraries of the wedding plus tourism routes include two categories: the first is the "6-day Phuket Wedding Photography Tour - Super Value Edition", with a market price of 18,999 yuan per couple. The itinerary provides round-trip flights and airport transfer services to and from Phuket Island, accommodation at a four-star resort hotel on Phuket Island, one day of wedding makeup and photography services, full-day exclusive car service, and a gift of 100 digital wedding photography pictures. The concept of one-price inclusive service means no extra charges for additional selections.

The second category is the "6-day Phuket Wedding Photography Tour - Prestige Edition", with a market price of 36,999 yuan per couple, which is a high-end product of five-star resort-level wedding photography. The highlights of the itinerary include providing the entire journey in a five-star seaside resort hotel, allowing guests to experience the prestigious service of Phuket hotels favored by Thai royalty and world-class celebrities; providing round-trip flights from Beijing to Phuket, luxury exclusive car transfers to and from the Thai airport; enjoying eight sets of clothing designs and make-up for both men and women, along with two whole days of shooting services; organizing and planning Thai wedding blessing ceremonies with photography services; gifting Thai-flavored meals and seafood meals during the two days of shooting; and providing dedicated nanny car services during the two days of shooting. Among them, due to the organization of Thai wedding ceremonies, tourists need to make reservations 30 days in advance for the high-end route.

Jetta Holidays Market Department Manager Zheng Qijun stated that in the "Extraordinary 1+1" route, they also utilized various resources to plan out the "Thai Royal Wedding" itinerary, fully adhering to the Thai wedding procedures, including blessing ceremonies, bridegroom procession, respect-for-elders ceremony, holy water baptism ceremony, and wedding banquet celebrations, while also gifting professional wedding photography throughout the entire process.

It was learned that while launching the pre-wedding routes initially, Jetta Holidays will also extend to "family tours", "happy family trips", "elderly blessings", and "photography skills training" routes. The "family tour" is the concept of tourism plus family photography services, while the "happy family trip" is designed for middle-aged and young couples traveling with their parents and children, and the "elderly blessings" targets the senior tourism market. The itineraries are the same as those for wedding photography services, except that each route has different makeup and styling styles, but every product includes Thai-style costumes. Due to the climate characteristics of the destination, the products are not constrained by seasonal changes.
