The Cao family, although wealthy and considered a prestigious household, harbored many internal conflicts and struggles unknown to outsiders. The second son of the Cao family, Cao Tianji, was kind-hearted and gentlemanly, but he had been living with deep guilt since childhood. As a child, due to his mischief, he urged his elder brother to take him on a hike in the wilderness, which resulted in his elder brother Cao Tianyu contracting a serious illness that never healed, leaving behind years of regret. Because of this, their mother, Xue Jianlan, had not forgiven Cao Tianji for many years and cried every day.

Throughout his growth, Cao Tianyu suffered from the torment of illness and eventually lost his ability to have children, unable to marry, which became the biggest regret of the Cao family.

In order to compensate for the eldest son Cao Tianyu who was despised by others, after multiple failed matchmaking attempts, Xue Jianlan, out of her love for her son, made an exchange condition with the Miss Xiang from Xiang's dyeing workshop. She arranged for the smart, beautiful, and cunning Xiang Xuexin to marry her eldest son Cao Tianyu unconditionally, while arranging for her second son Cao Tianji to marry Xiang Xuexin's younger sister Xiang Xueling.

To make up for his inner guilt towards his brother and to eliminate his mother's prejudice against him, Cao Tianji painfully left his girlfriend Chen Yongyue, and reluctantly accepted the marriage arranged solely by his mother.

Cao Jineng, Cao Tianji's father, strongly disapproved of such a marriage-of-convenience and tried hard to stop Cao Tianji from making such a decision. However, Cao Tianji did not listen, insisting on treating his brother's lifelong happiness as the most important thing in his life, believing that as long as he sacrificed himself, it would bring his brother a happy marriage.

But what Cao Tianji did not know was that the bride he was supposed to marry, Xiang Xueling, was actually a severe mental patient! During the wedding, Xiang Xueling had a breakdown, scaring everyone present. Cao Jineng and other relatives repeatedly advised Cao Tianji to immediately dissolve the engagement with the Xiang family.

However, contrary to everyone's expectations, Cao Tianji not only refused to break off the engagement but also stayed by Xiang Xueling's side, adhering to the agreement between the Cao and Xiang families, respecting their marriage, and hoping that his wife could slowly recover under his careful care.

Cao Tianji's actions earned the praise of his mother-in-law Wei Shuxian, and Xue Jianlan gradually changed her attitude towards him, believing that her son had already made the greatest sacrifice for his brother, which was very commendable.

On the other hand, after marriage, Xiang Xuexin looked down on Cao Tianyu because he could not consummate their marriage. She played two-faced games, secretly consorted with her old lover Cha Hetin, preparing to elope, and constantly deceived Cao Tianyu, creating lies that disturbed the household.

Xue Jianlan accidentally learned that her daughter-in-law had an affair, and she was furious, but she could not find out who Xiang Xuexin's lover was. She could only worry secretly and regret trusting Xiang Xuexin so easily.

Both newlyweds of the Cao family were trapped in the sea of love. Cao Tianji deeply loved Xiang Xueling but could never win her heart, as Xiang Xueling's thoughts were always on her ex-husband Song Xiajun.

Song Xiajun was originally the son of a military family. On the day of his marriage to Xiang Xueling, he was cruelly attacked by his enemies, who mercilessly slaughtered his entire family. Although Xiang Xueling managed to escape, she suffered a great shock and became mentally ill...

Song Xiajun was severely injured, and many thought he had died in the massacre. In fact, three years later, both Song Xiajun and Xiang Xueling lost contact with each other, each thinking the other had perished. Unexpectedly, Song Xiajun became Cao Tianji's close friend, making their relationship even more complex and subtle.

From then on, within this wealthy family, there were numerous conflicts among parents and daughters-in-law, siblings, and friends. Kind hearts, misplaced love, sincere emotions, and false lies intertwined throughout the story...

The true faces of some people were revealed one by one. For example, Xiang Xuexin claimed to marry into the Cao family for the lifelong happiness of her sister, but in reality, she coveted the vast fortune of the Cao family, showing her hypocrisy and greed. After being rejected by Xiang Xuexin, Cha Hetin, in order to seize the property, deliberately married the youngest daughter of the Cao family, Cao Yuezi, hoping to gain prominence through his wife and secure a place in the Cao family.

After Cao Tianyu learned of his wife's infidelity, he was devastated. To prevent his wife from abandoning him and fleeing at any time, he selfishly held Xiang Xueling hostage, hoping to use her madness to control Xiang Xuexin, forcing her to stay for her sister's sake.

Chen Yongyue, Cao Tianji's first love, sympathized with his plight. Every time he faced difficulties, she stepped forward, trying to help him overcome them, and constantly tried to foster the romance between him and Xiang Xueling.

Caught between Chen Yongyue and Xiang Xueling, Cao Tianji's emotional pendulum always swung toward his mentally ill wife Xiang Xueling, whose sincerity moved many. Ultimately, heaven rewarded those with good intentions, and Xiang Xueling found warmth and comfort in Cao Tianji, leading to her recovery from her madness.

The scheming Xiang Xuexin and Cha Hetin both received their comeuppance, designed by Cao Tianyu to be killed one by one. Afterwards, Cao Tianyu also committed suicide out of guilt.

After the storm, the Cao family restored its original peace and harmony. Under the cultivation of love, the romance between Xiang Xueling and Cao Tianji finally blossomed. The mad bride Xiang Xueling completely emerged from the shadow and spent the rest of her life with her devoted groom Cao Tianji.

Truly, there is genuine affection in the world, and in times of crisis, the golden heart shines.
