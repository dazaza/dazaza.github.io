Monday, February 21, 2011

I think it's not too late to enter the e-commerce field right now. I've been thinking about e-commerce for a whole year, but it has just stayed in the stage of idle fancy. Recently, I finally decided to take action. On the 19th, I went to Shenzhen with Sister Mei to visit a certain company and consult on relevant cooperation details. I also listened to their team meeting. This industry has a very promising future, and this enterprise is very strong. That night when I came back, I created my Taobao store, named it "Bei Ma Jia," and the domain name is http://bbmmhome.taobao.com. At the same time, I studied the software "Taobao Assistant." Now, some products are already listed. But many things haven't been resolved yet, so I'm not accepting orders at the moment.

The signboard shop name is my own calligraphy. The next step is to sign an agency contract with a certain company and implement the detailed processes. Product pictures also need to be photographed more extensively, which is a huge task. The shop decoration is also being carried out simultaneously. To save funds, all these things have to be done by myself.

Tuesday, February 22, 2011

In the past few days, many people have contacted me on Wangwang. They all said they could help me improve my credit rating, and without exception, they asked for QQ contact. Guangzhou VIP Information Technology Co., Ltd. I know this is what's known as credit manipulation, and I didn't pay attention to it.

I think that if I want to open my own shop on Taobao, I should cherish this thing called credit. Moreover, it should be real to be valuable. All fictitious credits are fleeting illusions. Can you deceive your conscience even if you can deceive others temporarily?

From the day the shop opened, I solemnly promise: I will never participate in credit manipulation. I will operate sincerely, no matter how difficult the start may be, and I will persist. Please support me, dear friends.

Wednesday, February 23, 2011

Yesterday, besides strangers contacting me on Wangwang offering to increase credibility, there were also those who provided a URL and then claimed they couldn't make payments. I can't help but marvel at how deep the water is in the online market!

At the same time, the messages from strangers in the past two days really made me start thinking about how to promote my online store after its official opening. For this reason, I specially made a promotion plan yesterday. From now on, I will follow the plan, which can be continuously improved. Having a plan is like having a direction to move forward, knowing which path to choose.

This advertisement is a work of pride, though it's inspired by others.

Also, I would like to clarify that the purpose of writing this diary is actually one of the ways to promote my online store. I hope more friends will pay attention to my online store. In fact, I don't want to bother my friends for help later. During the early operation stage of the online store, discount promotions will definitely be the main goal to build up credibility. Now, learning from others' experiences is also a key part of the work.
