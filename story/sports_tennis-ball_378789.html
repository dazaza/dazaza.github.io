Gods, Demigods, and Heroes – bring the wonderful world of Greek/Roman mythology to your iPhone, iPad, or iPod Touch!

Inside, you'll find an Etymological Dictionary of Greek Mythology. Over a thousand terms, from Ἄβας (Abas), a Trojan warrior and son of Euridamantes, to Ζεύξιππος (Zeuxippos), the son of Apollo and the Naiad nymph!

For every god, demigod, or hero, the original Greek name, the etymology of the word, and its story are provided.

But you'll also discover animals, peoples, mythical creatures, and monsters.

Other sections couldn't be missing, such as the pairings of Greeks / Romans (Aphrodite / Venus, Asclepius / Aesculapius, Boreas / Aquilo, Dike / Justice, etc.), anecdotes, stories, and traps, a profile of Greek mythology and a profile of Roman mythology, a high-definition image for each myth, the role played, and countless other pieces of information.

Did you know, for example, that the well-known sports brand NIKE actually takes its name from the personification of victory, meaning we shouldn't pronounce it in the Anglo-Saxon way as 'NAIK,' but rather as 'NIKE'?

Hundreds of additional profiles of heroes and characters complete the work, all fully illustrated with images (Heracles, Epimetheus, the Hesperides, Philoctetes, etc.).
