"Today, I failed to notice carefully. I didn't expect that in such a small Longling Village, there would actually be a strong warrior at the level of a Sword Marshal. I accept my defeat willingly. However, I advise you to give up. If that man comes, even you will find it hard to resist." Lei Hu forcibly suppressed the discomfort caused by blood surging and said in a low voice.

"Hmm? So you came here with a purpose? What exactly is your goal?" After hearing Lei Hu's words, Delong was secretly surprised. His first thought was that someone had targeted Longling Village. Could it really be because of...

"I advise you not to ask. The more you know, the more dangerous it becomes."

With a cold snort, "Do you think I'm the kind of person who fears trouble? Now, I'm quite interested in the force behind you. Since you're unwilling to speak, then stay behind." Suddenly moving his body, he swiftly rushed towards Lei Hu with his sword. Just as Lei Hu was about to die under the sword, there was a 'ding' sound, and the Zhanling Sword that should have pierced down slid to the side. At this moment, a young, thin man stood in front of Lei Hu. He had white robes fluttering, his face entirely pale, appearing more like a lifeless person. His sharp, intense gaze directly stared at the Zhanling Sword in Delong's hand.

Seeing Lei Hu respectfully standing behind this man, it was obvious without thinking that this should be the main instigator behind the scenes.

"May I ask how you are called, sir, and what is your purpose for coming to our Longling Village?" Delong's eyes directly looked at the man in front of him. From the moment he appeared, Delong's expression became very serious. He knew this man was very strong and had become a threat to him. Even if perhaps he could not yet defeat himself, there were still many villagers behind him, so he had to treat the situation cautiously.

"Dark Main Bishop Puming Siya, I've come here to help you get rid of poverty and suffering, and return to the embrace of the Lord."

The Church, the most sacred and powerful existence on the continent. The Church has the Pope as the highest leader, and its hierarchy goes: Pope, Chief Justice, Four Main Bishops, Cardinal Priests, Clerics. Within the Church, apart from religious personnel, there are also knight guards. Besides the Pope's exclusive Holy Knights and the Chief Justice's exclusive Judgement Squad, there are two top-notch knight groups, along with many powerful Temple Knights.

These Temple Knights are all composed of the finest knights from various countries. Their strength is extremely astonishing.

It is said that the entry threshold for Temple Knights is warriors at the level of War Witches. Of course, the entry thresholds for Holy Knights and the Judgement Squad are higher, requiring reaching the levels of War Generals, Senior Mages, or Archery Spirit Respected Ones. The Four Main Bishops are only second to the Pope and the Chief Justice. It's rumored that both the Pope and the Chief Justice have long surpassed the Saint level and are stepping towards the God level.

"Hmph hmph, I see, you're here to seize our home instead, when did the Church start doing such things?" His face was full of disdain.

Puming Siya didn't bother explaining, only staring at the Zhanling Sword in Delong's hand. To say here, only Delong could give him a hint of threat, but merely a hint. As long as he could defeat Delong, everything would naturally fall into place. Slowly taking out his staff, a green light slowly condensed into the shape of a shield, enveloping him within. "Since you're so obstinate, then we'll have to offend."

"Great dark elements, destroy your enemies before you—Shadow Impact!" A layer of black material condensed into a sharp blade, quickly flying towards Delong. Just as it was about to strike him, Delong moved, his entire body shooting sideways like an arrow leaving the string.

In mid-air, the blade flowing with black light seemed alive, suddenly aiming straight for Delong's heart.

"Die!" Puming Siya was confident; Delong in mid-air couldn't possibly dodge, he could only let the blade pierce through.

"Hmph" Facing the opponent's attack, Delong remained unmoved. Just as the blade was about to reach him, suddenly, the Zhanling Sword turned white, and an overwhelming aura emanated from Delong. Roaring, the surging energy dissolved the blade into nothingness. Leaping downward, he surged forward with unparalleled momentum directly towards Puming Siya. His left foot stepped on the ground again, this time creating a large pit where he stepped. With a thunderous crash, the Zhanling Sword had already swung down.

How could this be? Why did he suddenly become so powerful?

Finally, facing that extreme pressure, Puming Siya still dared not to confront it head-on. The magic shield quietly rose, and the immense pressure collided with the magic shield. Puming Siya's body was sent flying backward in the collision.

Deep blue light drifted, and the ground was cut open by the Zhanling Sword during the impact, forming a huge crack.

Puming Siya suddenly realized—he had lost. Although his body hadn't suffered much damage, the severe shock had already made his blood surge. Looking at the crack in front of him and sensing the opponent's formidable aura, he knew it was impossible to defeat him.

"I lost, you're very strong, but next time, it won't be this easy." While saying this, he roared loudly at Lei Hu and the others who were stunned behind: "Let's go!"

Soaring into the air, he unleashed his flight spell and headed westward. On the way, he couldn't help but look back. This battle, he had lost completely. He no longer had the strength to fight, while the other person seemed unaffected. Looks like it's necessary to ask the Chief Justice to intervene now.
