[China's first financial encyclopedia - Hexun百科 launches grandly on November 30th]

by jay on 2006-11-30 20:45:59

After months of hard preparation work, Hexun.com publicly launched a new online knowledge base system to all users on November 30th, 2006 - Hexun Baike. Hexun Baike is a Chinese encyclopedia mainly about financial knowledge that everyone can edit. At the time of its launch, it already included approximately 3,000 entries, and the content is still continuously expanding.

Hexun always places user experience first and is committed to reducing the difficulties users encounter when obtaining information. When you read financial information on Hexun every day, you inevitably come across unfamiliar terms or want to delve deeper into the background of a financial figure or institution. In the past, you could only sift through countless unrelated search results from mechanized search engines to find what you wanted. Now, you can use Hexun Baike!

Hexun Baike helps you conveniently search for the information you want to understand. More importantly, this information has been organized with human intelligence and is interconnected, significantly reducing the difficulty of obtaining information.

If you are not satisfied with the explanation of an entry in Hexun Baike, you can add your own interpretation to it. This is because Hexun Baike is an encyclopedia that anyone can edit. Every entry is created and continuously improved by visitors like you. Hexun Baike is a product that closely combines the Web 2.0 concept with the application of building an online knowledge base.

We welcome you to visit Hexun Baike and sincerely hope that you will join in the creation of Hexun Baike, together compiling an online knowledge base that provides help to global Chinese readers, truly embodying the sharing spirit of "everyone for me, I for everyone" in the Internet era.

If you want to create an entry, please read the copyright information and management guidelines of Hexun Baike first. This will help you fully understand your rights and obligations when using Hexun Baike. You can also read how to write an entry and how to write a high-quality one.

The usage help of Hexun Baike will be a great assistant for you to quickly master how to use Hexun Baike.
