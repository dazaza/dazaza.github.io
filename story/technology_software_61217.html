QQ2008 Beta1 is officially released.

by xloong on 2008-04-08 23:17:20

Nightfire: I believe friends who often follow the Nightfire & Trajectory’s Blog already know what I am going to say next... Tencent QQ's money-making plan is ongoing at all times. A few days ago, QQ 2008 beta1 was released for VIP members, and now it's finally our turn - the "general public". Making money isn't wrong either, so I won't say much more about it, let's take a look at the content.

This version focuses on "security" and "wonderfulness", safeguarding the security of QQ accounts while also providing QQ users with a cool personalized experience, full of surprises.

# QQ2008 Beta1 (Compatible with Windows Vista)

Software language: Simplified Chinese

# Software size: 24.7MB

# Update date: 2008.04.08

Introduction to new features:

Optimized status reply prompts, automatic replies no longer repeat

Auto-reply is a very thoughtful feature. In previous versions of QQ, when a friend went offline or was busy, every time you sent them a message, you would receive a repeated auto-reply. Now, QQ2008 Beta1 has optimized the custom reply function, preventing custom replies from repeating.

All-new QQ voice and video, efficient, smooth, and clear

The quality of QQ video and audio has been optimized, making super video even clearer. Even in poor network conditions, it can run smoothly. Super voice has also been further optimized in terms of call quality. Want to give it a try?

Learn more

Beyond network limitations, customizable signatures at your fingertips

A new way to change your personal signature has been added. Regardless of whether your QQ is online or not, as long as you are a Taotao user, you can update your personal signature by posting "qm+content" to Taotao (www.taotao.com) anytime, anywhere.

Learn more

Group shopping area, exclusive ultra-convenient and secure point card purchase channel

You can go shopping with your group friends! Currently, QQ groups targeting online game types have opened up exclusive shopping areas, allowing you to conveniently learn about the latest dynamics of online games during communication with your group friends, and also easily purchase relevant point cards!

Learn more

Optimize QQ VIP doodle expressions, DIY and expression templates easily selected

QQ VIP users can more easily use the doodle expression function. Whether it's highly customized DIY or convenient and quick expression templates, simply click a button to switch effortlessly. QQ VIP's wonderful expression privileges, my QQ expressions definitely speak for me!

Learn more

Quick outfit changes, chat and change QQ Show outfits while chatting

No need to repeatedly log into the QQ Show Mall. Simply open the chat panel and click the quick outfit change button to change your QQ Show outfits while chatting on QQ! Here we provide you with the latest popular items from the QQ Show Mall, along with free items such as moods, expressions, and current events, giving you a different look and mood every day!~

Learn more

Added Dungeon Fighter Online game avatars, freely choose personalized avatars

Added four sets of game avatars for Dungeon Fighter Online, CrossFire, QQ Speed, and QQ Three Kingdoms, while also optimizing the QQ Huaxia avatar. You can choose an avatar that suits your preference based on your own liking, showcasing your individual side on the QQ interface.
