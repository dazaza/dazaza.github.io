Young fisherman dies while rescuing a drowning companion, ten thousand people attend the funeral procession

by xue96pwsh on 2012-03-03 10:11:21

Sun Tong's once brilliant smile has become a forever memory in the hearts of his family. The 22-year-old fisherman from Linhuai Town, Sihong County, without hesitation jumped into the wind-swept and wave-tossed lake to save his drowning companion. He used all his strength to lift him up, winning precious time for the rescue of the drowning victim! Unfortunately, this good young fisherman never surfaced again...

In recent days, while local villagers have been unanimously praising Sun Tong for risking his life to save others, they all deeply mourn the untimely death of this good young man who was only 22 years old! According to relevant regulations, the Sihong County Righteousness Foundation is actively applying for recognition of Sun Tong's and others' acts of bravery and helping in danger!

Communicator: Sun Dongcheng, Reporter: Gao Feng

Accident:

The companion who did not know how to swim held the bamboo pole to paddle the boat and accidentally fell into the lake.

The 19-year-old rescued young man, Qin Liang, lives in Group 11, Wu Xi Village, Wu Ji Town, Shuyang County. He and Sun Tong met when they were working outside and later became good friends who often visited each other.

On October 25th, Sun Tong and his younger brother Sun Peng took the 19-year-old young man Qin Liang and drove a small boat to visit "Wangsha Island" inside Hongze Lake. On their way back, the diesel engine of the small boat emitted black smoke and broke down. Regardless of how long it had been, I still remember...

At this point, it was time to return with favorable winds, so Sun Tong let the boat drift with the wind. Meanwhile, the brothers Sun Tong and Sun Peng used bamboo poles to paddle the boat to increase speed. After paddling for a while, the two brothers felt tired and placed the bamboo poles casually on the bow of the boat.

Qin Liang, who did not know how to paddle the boat, curiously picked up the bamboo pole, wanting to try paddling the boat to reduce the fatigue of the brothers Sun Tong. Unexpectedly, when Qin Liang forcefully pushed the water with the bamboo pole, the small boat swayed, and Qin Liang immediately fell into the lake water. Qin Liang shouted repeatedly, "Help! Help!" Due to the cold water stimulation, Qin Liang's legs cramped, causing his body to sink. Seeing this situation, Sun Tong took off his clothes, jumped into the lake water, swam to Qin Liang's side, grabbed his hand to prevent him from continuing to sink, and at the same time pulled Qin Liang towards the side of the boat. "Don't panic, maintain your body balance, take several deep breaths." Sun Tong, who grew up by the lake, urgently reminded Qin Liang.

Rescue:

The drowning companion persuaded him to give up, but he said, "If I leave you, can you still survive?"

At this moment, the lake surface was windy and the waves were urgent. Qin Liang didn't want to drag down his good friend, so he intermittently said to Sun Tong, "Let me go, otherwise we will both be in danger!"

"I must do my best to save you... If I leave you, can you still survive?" Although Sun Tong also felt exhausted, how could he watch his good friend sink into the water and leave alone?

On the small boat, Sun Peng, who couldn't swim, was calling home for help while shouting loudly, "Help! Help..." However, due to the strong wind and waves, the small boat couldn't stay fixed and drifted further away from Sun Tong and Qin Liang.

Just as Sun Peng was about to lose hope, he saw someone paddling a boat in the distance, which gave him new hope. He shouted loudly, "Come quickly to save us! Come quickly to save us!" The person heard the call and paddled the boat over. Unfortunately, this boat was not a motorized one but a bamboo-poled boat, which moved slowly. When the small boat finally reached Sun Tong and Qin Liang, the boat owner Sun Jiajun only saw one person in the water, floating up and down, seemingly almost "gone."

When Sun Jiajun pulled Qin Liang onto the boat, he could no longer speak. About three to five minutes later, Qin Liang was able to talk. Sun Jiajun hurriedly asked if there was anyone else underwater. Qin Liang struggled to say that there was another person who saved him and had been desperately holding him up in the water; otherwise, he would have sunk long ago. Seeing no movement in the water, Sun Jiajun tried fishing while paddling the boat but could not see Sun Tong.

Finally, Sun Tong's family and many fellow villagers arrived by boat one after another. Seeing Qin Liang, who was rescued and shivering from the cold, Sun Tong's parents, uncles, aunts, and younger brother Sun Peng quickly moved him to a larger boat, dried his body, covered him with blankets, and then prepared hot water in a large beverage bottle wrapped in towels, placing it in Qin Liang's arms to keep him warm. Qin Liang gradually regained consciousness.

But Sun Tong's figure was never seen again. In the end, Sun Tong's family and fellow villagers gathered more than ten boats and searched the accident waters for over two hours until around 5 PM, when Sun Tong's body, who sacrificed himself to save others, was finally recovered.

Moved:

Tens of thousands of people shed tears

to bid farewell to the good young man

Upon hearing the news, Qin Liang's parents quickly came from over a hundred miles away to thank and console the Sun family. "Brother Tong is gone, and my sister is getting married soon. From now on, you are my real parents..." Qin Liang hugged Sun Tong's parents, tears streaming down his face, filled with guilt. After the incident, Linhuai Town Party Secretary Wang Ketao, Town Mayor Gu Mingchun, and others personally visited the house to express gratitude to the Sun family for raising such an excellent son for society.

On the day of Sun Tong's funeral, when the mourning car draped in black crepe slowly drove through the streets, more than ten thousand people from Linhuai Town stood on either side, wiping away their tears as they sent off the good young man, refusing to leave for a long time! Currently, relevant departments in Sihong County are following procedures to actively apply for recognition of Sun Tong's act of righteousness.
