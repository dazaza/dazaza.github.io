Advantages and Issues of Hydrated Lime Powder in Coating Emulsion Paints

1. The advantages of hydrated lime powder (calcium hydroxide) emulsion paint in improving the performance of putty coatings

Hydrated lime powder itself has the property of converting into calcium carbonate, as well as its role in modifying polymer resins, which significantly improves the water resistance and scrub resistance of the coating film. The coating film of hydrated lime powder-based paints feels smooth to the touch, and with appropriate technology, semi-gloss effect latex paints can be formulated at a very low cost, resulting in better decorative effects.

2. The advantage of using hydrated lime powder (calcium hydroxide) in reducing the cost of putty coatings

Hydrated lime powder belongs to air-hardening cementitious materials. In the coating film, it not only serves as an inorganic binder but also enhances the properties of polymer resins, thus saving on emulsion usage. Hydrated lime powder can improve the hiding power of the paint, reducing the amount of titanium dioxide or lithopone pigments used in the paint. When the raw material cost of hydrated lime powder emulsion paint is around 1750 yuan/ton, it can meet the standard of superior products as per GB/T9756-2001. Without the use of hydrated lime powder, it would be difficult to achieve this result.

3. Performance deficiencies brought by the application of hydrated lime powder (calcium hydroxide) emulsion paint in putty coatings

When hydrated lime powder-based paints are used on old walls, there may sometimes be issues with yellowing of the coating film, a problem that has yet to be resolved. This issue of yellowing in such paints still exists and requires further research to solve. Additionally, due to the high alkalinity that hydrated lime powder imparts to the paint, there are certain requirements for pigments when mixing colors. Clearly, pigments that are not alkali-resistant cannot be used in hydrated lime powder paints; when color pastes are used to mix colors, besides the alkali resistance of the color itself, the compatibility of other components in the color paste with the alkaline medium is also an important influencing factor for whether they can be used in hydrated lime powder paints.

4. Enhancing the environmental performance of hydrated lime powder (calcium hydroxide) emulsion paint in putty coatings

The enhancement of the environmental performance of hydrated lime powder in paints can be reflected in the following aspects:

(1) Reducing VOC content in the paint: Hydrated lime powder emulsion paint uses VAE emulsion as the base material, does not require the use of film-forming aids (solvents), and through the selection of an appropriate thickening system, the amount of freeze-thaw stabilizer used is also very low, thereby significantly reducing the VOC content of the paint.

(2) No need for mold inhibitors: The highly alkaline environment provided by hydrated lime powder in emulsion paints makes it impossible for mold and microorganisms to survive, so no additional mold inhibitors are needed during the production of emulsion paints.

(3) Absorbing CO2 from the air: The main component of hydrated lime powder is Ca(OH)2, which reacts with CO2 in the air to form CaCO3, consuming (absorbing) CO2 from the air. Therefore, these types of paints can be called carbon dioxide-absorbing paints, and after application, they can improve indoor air quality.
