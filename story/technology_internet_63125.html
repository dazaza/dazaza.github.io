Xinhua Du Group formally hired Tang Jun as the president and CEO with a compensation of 1 billion yuan.

by eeyye on 2008-04-15 18:22:31

Xinhua News Agency, April 15th, at 1:45 PM -- Xinhua Du Group announced that it has formally hired Tang Jun as the group president and CEO to succeed the group founder Chen Fashu in order to take full responsibility of the daily management, long-term strategy, group operation, external investment, capital operations and other work of Xinhua Du Group. Chen Fashu will thereafter exclusively serve as the company chairman.

In the announcement issued on the afternoon of the same day, Xinhua Du Group Chairman Chen Fashu expressed that Tang Jun possesses rich ten-year management experience in multinational enterprises and more than four years of first-class company management experience in overseas listed companies in China. He stated that Tang Jun’s joining would further perfect the management of Xinhua Du Group, strengthen the group's daily operation and capital operation ability, enrich and improve the group in all fields, and seize more market opportunities.

Tang Jun is known as the "emperor of employees" in China's IT industry. In this joining of Xinhua Du Group, he will receive stock options worth 1 billion yuan. Previously, when serving as Microsoft China President, his income exceeded 100 million yuan; when serving as Shanda President, his income exceeded 400 million yuan. It was also learned that two companies under Xinhua Du Group will be listed on domestic A shares within the next six months.

Fujian Xinhua Du Group owns or holds shares in Fujian Xinhua Du Shopping Plaza Co., Ltd., Fujian Xinhua Du Engineering Co., Ltd., Huafu Grand Hotel (Real Estate) Co., Ltd., Hong Kong-Macao Information Industry Co., Ltd., Fujian Xinhua Du Information Technology Co., Ltd., Zijin Mining Group Co., Ltd. and Wuyi Mountain Tourism Development Co., Ltd.

According to its official website introduction, Xinhua Du Group has a registered capital of 139.8 million yuan, employs more than 5,000 people, and the annual turnover of its controlled enterprises exceeds 5 billion yuan.
