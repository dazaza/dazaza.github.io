Man was beaten to disability for dating the wife of a local black society leader

by yanq412exue on 2012-03-07 19:41:23

All seven suspects of the criminal gang have been arrested. According to the Xian Evening Post, a husband suspected his wife of having an affair with another man and gathered his brothers to teach him a lesson. The victim's left leg was disabled as a result. Yesterday, the Yan Tower police announced that this evil force gang has been successfully dismantled and all seven suspects have been arrested.

The male netizen was injured when meeting a female net friend. At around 1:00 am on October 9th, a citizen reported to the Changyanbao police station of the Yan Tower Public Security Bureau that a man was lying motionless on the ground with a large pool of blood. When the policemen arrived at the scene, they saw a man lying in a pool of blood at the entrance of a parking lot on Dongyi Road, motionless. The policemen immediately called 120 to urgently send him to the hospital. After a preliminary diagnosis by the emergency personnel, the man's left leg artery was cut off. Due to serious injuries, he had no consciousness, no breathing, and no heartbeat. The situation was critical, so the medical staff immediately began emergency treatment.

The Yan Tower police formed a special investigation team to visit the crime scene and retrieved surveillance footage from nearby roads but found no information helpful for solving the case. The policemen on guard at the hospital found an ID card, mobile phone, and 2000 yuan in cash on the victim. The ID card showed that the injured person surnamed Xu, aged 35, was from Zichang County. After a preliminary investigation, the police ruled out robbery as the cause of injury.

At around 4:00 am on the same day, after emergency treatment by the medical staff, Xu regained consciousness. The policemen learned that Xu met a female net friend online and their relationship was ambiguous. That day, Xu specially came from northern Shaanxi to Xi'an to meet the female net friend and temporarily stayed in a hotel in Xi'an. Two hours before the incident, the female net friend Wang invited him to meet in the southern suburb. Unexpectedly, Xu was attacked by a group of strangers and had his left leg slashed without seeing the female net friend.

The Black Gang Leader Set Up A Trap To Teach His Rival

According to the police, this female net friend Wang had significant suspicion of committing the crime. After more than two months of investigation by the police, the police finally discovered Wang's whereabouts. On December 13th, the police captured Wang in the northern suburb. Investigations revealed that Wang is from Yan'an and is 29 years old. Wang often surfed the internet at home to pass the time. During this period, she met the victim Xu online, and their relationship became ambiguous.

In early October, Wang's husband Li discovered his wife's ambiguous relationship with Xu and immediately flew into a rage, deciding to teach Xu a lesson. Li immediately gathered Zhao, Chen, and others to prepare for retaliation, then asked his wife Wang to invite Xu out. At around midnight on October 9th, unaware Xu followed his appointment with the female net friend Wang, rushing to the entrance of a parking lot on Dongyi Road in the southern suburb. Suddenly, five men with knives rushed out from around him. Without saying a word, they started punching and kicking him. One of the men stabbed his left thigh, then the five men scattered and fled.

The Evil Force Gang Was Dismantled

After Wang's arrest, as of yesterday, the police arrested her husband Li, Zhao, Chen, Yao, Shi, and six others. Further investigations revealed that Wang's husband Li had a gang of brothers under his command who frequently collected debts for others, forcibly took over projects, acted as an underground police force, and occasionally undertook small projects. Li was their "boss." This gang was a rampant evil force gang. On the day of the incident, all five suspects who stabbed and disabled Xu were Li's subordinates. That night, to thank the five suspects who carried out the stabbing, Li gave each participant 500 yuan in cash and a pack of Furongwang cigarettes as a reward.

The police introduced that the evil force gang led by Li had, from September to November this year, multiple times acted as an underground police force and illegally profited by collecting debts for others and participating in forcibly taking over projects. Among them, Yao and Shi were previously dealt with by the high-tech police for acting as an underground police force and being suspected of provoking trouble. This evil force gang is also the second such gang dismantled by the Xi'an police since the anti-black action began. Currently, the seven suspects are criminally detained on suspicion of intentional injury, and the case is still under further review.
