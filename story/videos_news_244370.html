National Day Evening Party in 2009: Playing four major movements

The executive director of the National Day evening party, Jia Ding, introduced in an exclusive interview with reporters that the layout of the evening party would be divided into six levels: "Light Cube" performance, central celebration area, thousand-person choir, fireworks curtain, and two mass celebration areas, lasting for 100 minutes.

According to the current design plan, the National Day evening party will be divided into four major movements: "This is a Great Motherland", "It is the Place Where I Grew Up", "On This Vast Land", and "Everywhere is Filled with Bright Sunshine". After each movement, there will be a star singing a theme song. The theme songs of the four movements are sequentially "Love My China" sung by Wang Ying, Li Danyang, and Lü Wei; "Today is Your Birthday" sung by Tan Jing and Liang Changyong with Lang Lang accompanying; "Proud Constructors" sung by Zhang Mai, Guo Rong, and Wang Zhengzheng; and "Sunny Road" exclusively sung by Peng Liyuan. Jia Ding said that in each movement, there will also be fireworks displays, Light Cube performances, solo and choral singing, as well as mass celebrations.

The evening party will start with festive preludes around 7:30 PM. At about 8 PM, when the melodious sound of "The East is Red" comes from the Telegraph Building, the audience on the square begins to cheer, officially opening the curtain of the evening party. The party will continue until around 10 PM.

National Day Evening Party in 2009: Stars Make a Dazzling Appearance

At 3:40 PM that day, Tan Jing and Liang Changyong arrived at the rehearsal training ground. Tan Jing was wearing a sun hat and simple leisure sports attire, while Liang Changyong, who had a bit of a poet's temperament, had long hair, wore a pair of gold-rimmed glasses, and was dressed in a beige short-sleeved shirt, also in casual attire. After a brief conversation with Jia Ding, they both ascended to the stage below the highest command platform.

The piece they performed was "Today is Your Birthday". Although there was no live piano accompaniment by Lang Lang that day, the two still cooperated seamlessly and passed the rehearsal in one go.

Tan Jing said in an interview with reporters that singing on such a high stage felt very good. She also felt very happy to participate in such a significant performance event. "To personally participate and sing a song on the 60th anniversary of National Day is my greatest honor. I will express my deepest love for Motherland through my songs!" Tan Jing said. Liang Changyong then expressed that as long as the intentions of the director group could be implemented, "the performance will definitely succeed." During the intense rehearsals, a large number of singers and stars, including Li Guyi, Zeng Gegge, and Feng Xiaoquan, successively took the stage to perform.

At 4:30 PM that day, another singer took the stage - Song Zuying, who would perform "Good Days" during the evening party. When the cheerful prelude of the song started, the stage under Song Zuying's feet began to rise slowly. "Today is another good day, all your wishes can come true, tomorrow is a good day, open the door and welcome the spring breeze..." Her cheerful and melodious voice echoed throughout the entire training ground.

National Day Evening Party in 2009: Gentleman Jackie Chan Cares for His Partner

At 17:05, Jackie Chan, escorted by many armed police officers, arrived at the training ground. Reporters saw that Jackie Chan was wearing a T-shirt, with a jacket tied around his waist and holding a folding fan. His dark face already had many wrinkles, and his temples were filled with white hair...

Soon, Jackie Chan began to duet with singer Liu Yuanyuan on the closing song of the evening party, "Country". Even though it was a rehearsal, Jackie Chan sang with great effort, expressing his emotions vividly, and his gestures were perfectly synchronized with his partner. The rehearsal went smoothly, and when he stepped off the stage, Jackie Chan noticed that the steps were too high for Liu Yuanyuan to descend. At this moment, Jackie Chan showed his gentlemanly side: he pushed aside the staff guarding him, stepped down one step first, then tightly held Liu Yuanyuan's hand, allowing her to successfully descend the stage.

National Day Evening Party in 2009: "Flowers" Need "Green Leaves" to Complement Them

Although the shining ones are the main characters of the evening party, the one thousand members of the choir below the stage are the backbone supporting the entire evening party. It is understood that this thousand-person choir is composed of citizens, elementary school students, and college students from Beijing. Some come from amateur choirs, some from capital universities, and others from various primary and secondary schools. Among them, the youngest member, He Xinyi, is only 6 years old, while the oldest performer is over 80 years old. In one performance, they have to perform for more than two hours, singing more than 20 songs. Nine-year-old Zhang Yuchen from Beijing Huangchenggen Primary School told reporters that although the rehearsals were very hard, she could endure them, and she sincerely wished Motherland a happy birthday!

"The joint rehearsals today were very successful, and the dress rehearsal reached the expected effect," Jia Ding said, hoping that this celebration evening party could "convey the beautiful mindset of the Chinese people to the world, as well as their life state of great unity and great harmony."
