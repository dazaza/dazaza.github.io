Rolls-Royce's 11th Authorized Dealer in China Grandly Opens in Zhengzhou

On February 9, 2012 at 11:36 AM Tencent Dayu News - I want to comment (0) Font size: T|T Rolls-Royce Motor Cars' 11th authorized dealer showroom in Greater China grandly opened in Zhengzhou.

On February 7, 2012, Rolls-Royce announced the official opening of its 11th authorized dealer showroom in Greater China. The new showroom is located on the CBD Business Inner Ring in Zhengzhou City, Henan Province. Following the announcement of Rolls-Royce's record-breaking performance in 2011 over its 107-year history, the Zhengzhou showroom marks the first showroom opening in Greater China for Rolls-Royce in the beginning of 2012, symbolizing the company's continued expansion in the Chinese market.

The newly opened showroom in the Central Business District of Zhengdong New Area spans an area of 750 square meters and can accommodate up to five Rolls-Royce vehicles simultaneously. It will provide customers with a top-tier, all-round Rolls-Royce experience. The Zhengzhou showroom features the latest Rolls-Royce brand identity image, highlighting a perfect blend of the carmaker’s noble classic elegance and modernity, featuring Imak composite wooden floors.

The showroom was jointly developed by Rolls-Royce Motor Cars and its new partner, Henan Harmony Industrial Group Co., Ltd., marking an important step towards further refining Rolls-Royce's business layout in Central Plains region. With the grand opening of the Zhengzhou showroom, Rolls-Royce aims to meet the growing demand for Phantom and Ghost models from clients in the Central Plains region.

Paul Harris, President of Rolls-Royce Motor Cars Asia-Pacific, attended the opening ceremony and stated: "Rolls-Royce is a top luxury automobile brand with over a century of brilliant history. Our cooperation with Henan Harmony Industrial Group Co., Ltd. is based on our shared appreciation and passion for this brand. Rolls-Royce has cultivated a customer base that values handcrafted craftsmanship and strives for excellence, echoing the brand philosophy and production standards deeply rooted in Rolls-Royce."

Ms. Lillian Cheng, General Manager of Rolls-Royce Motor Cars Greater China, also remarked: "Rolls-Royce is delighted to be in Zhengzhou, a significant economic, cultural, and historical center in the Central Plains region. We consistently adhere to the principle of exceeding customer expectations and fulfilling their inner desires. Through our full product range and unique customer customization plans, we are confident in achieving this goal."

Mr. Feng Changge, Chairman of Henan Harmony Industrial Group Co., Ltd., expressed: "We are honored to become an authorized dealer for Rolls-Royce in Zhengzhou. We have already garnered significant attention from customers in Zhengzhou and surrounding areas. The opening of the showroom allows us to better serve clients who seek impeccable quality; thus, we have invested heavily in facilities and personnel, establishing a comprehensive series of supporting facilities."

Relevant leaders from the Zhengzhou municipal government of Henan Province and numerous distinguished enterprise representatives also attended the opening ceremony. On display during the event was the Ghost Extended Wheelbase model, which received widespread acclaim upon its release in April 2011, making a remarkable contribution to the growth of sales in 2011. This model embodies the outstanding design philosophy of the Rolls-Royce brand, offering ample driving power while providing a comfortable and quiet driving environment.

Just before the opening of the Zhengzhou showroom, Rolls-Royce announced that due to strong demand in the Asia-Pacific region, it achieved record-breaking sales in 2011, with Greater China becoming the largest single market globally for Rolls-Royce for the first time.

In parallel, the personalized customer customization program also set a historical best performance. In 2011, the proportion of customer customization elements in Phantom series models in the Asia-Pacific region increased from 50% in 2005 to 79%; globally, 56% of Ghost and Ghost Extended Wheelbase models sold contained customer customization personalization elements. To date in 2011, Rolls-Royce offers six models: Phantom Standard, Phantom Extended Wheelbase, Phantom Drophead Coupé, Phantom Coupé, Ghost, and Ghost Extended Wheelbase.
