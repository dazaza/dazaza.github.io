The police of Baotou made a phone call. In response to media reports about the emergence of 'mind-control drugs' criminal activities in Guangzhou, the Guangzhou police stated that from the current situation controlled by the police and relevant scientific identification, there is no evidence proving the existence of "smoke spraying", "shoulder patting" or "spray mist" causing others to be "mind-controlled" and lose consciousness, being manipulated at will. The police reminded the public to maintain a clear mind and treat so-called 'mind-control drugs' rumors with a scientific attitude, distinguishing right from wrong.

At around 8 PM on the evening of the 14th, Xiaoyu's class leader called Xiaoyu's father asking if Xiaoyu had returned home; at around 10 PM that night, Teacher Wan from the college also called Xiaoyu's father asking if there was any news about Xiaoyu. After receiving two calls looking for Xiaoyu in one night, Xiaoyu's father Xie Mou Shan foresaw that "something must have gone wrong." On the morning of the 15th, he rushed from Guanzhu Town, Dianbai County, Maoming City to the college.

The college security department quickly contacted the Minzu Police Station of Baotou Public Security Bureau. On the same afternoon, the college dispatched a vice president along with four members of the security department to fly to Baotou City, Inner Mongolia, over a thousand miles away. On the 20th, Xiaoyu and the college leadership returned to Zhanjiang.

When contacted by the reporter from Yangcheng Evening News, Yang Kechang, head of the security department of a certain college in Zhanjiang, said that Xiaoyu had been safely brought back from Baotou. As for other details, Yang Kechang refused to say more.

Xiaoyu sent a message from her phone to her father's phone during those five or six days.

"How did you get through those days?"

At around 11 AM on the morning of the 18th, the fixed telephone at Xie Mou Shan's home rang. The person on the other end spoke Mandarin: "We are from the Minzu Police Station of Baotou Public Security Bureau, your daughter is now at our police station..." Then, Xiaoyu's voice came through the phone, leaving Xie Mou Shan overwhelmed...

According to Xie Mou Shan, he learned through other channels that this message was sent from Beijing! How did Xiaoyu "fly" to Beijing?

After reading this message, Xie Mou Shan became extremely worried about Xiaoyu's safety. He returned the call, but no one answered. A little later, the other party's mobile phone was turned off. The college leadership reported the matter to the local police.

A female college student was mysteriously found on the streets of Baotou City in Inner Mongolia after being "patted" on the shoulder and becoming confused at the Haitian bus station in Zhanjiang... It sounds like an unimaginable event. For this reason, reporters from Yangcheng Evening News conducted multiple interviews regarding the incident. Yesterday, the victim Xiaoyu (a pseudonym) nervously told reporters from Yangcheng Evening News, "Thinking about it still makes me quite scared."

After Xiaoyu left school and didn't return, the college used various methods to contact and search for her, but still had no news. The college security department reported the case to the local police on the same day.

An alumnus who worked part-time with Xiaoyu said that at around 7:30 PM on the 13th, when calling Xiaoyu to chat, she seemed very relaxed and natural, her personal freedom was estimated not to have been restricted. Later, her mobile phone was always turned off.

Yesterday, when interviewed by Yangcheng Evening News, when talking about the incident on the 13th, Xiaoyu said: "At the time, I was at the station, suddenly someone seemed to pat my shoulder, I didn't even see clearly who patted my shoulder, then I knew nothing, and later I completely forgot what happened."

When talking about the situation in Baotou, Xiaoyu said: "That morning, when I suddenly woke up, I found myself near a traffic intersection. At the time, I was very nervous, and seeing a traffic policeman at the intersection, I reported the situation to him. A little while later, a police car came and took me back to the police station. There was nothing left in my possession - my mobile phone, money, ID card, etc., were all gone. I gave them the phone number of my family..."

Father received a heart-wrenching text message.

On the morning of June 13, Xiaoyu, a female student from the Food Class of Grade 2009 at a certain college in Zhanjiang, told her roommate, "I'm going to find someone and then go home," before leaving the college.

At 2:54 AM on the 16th, Xiaoyu's father received a distressing text message sent from Xiaoyu's phone: "Hello, your daughter is currently in our hands, please don't panic, read the whole message! We are researchers from a well-known international organization conducting research on human genes, which is an important and influential study! We especially need the support of people like you! We are preparing to send your daughter abroad for research, we hope you won't waste effort searching for her. After the research is complete, we will erase all her memories and give her a brand new and good life, so please don't worry about her future life! If this great research succeeds in the future, we will also apply to the institute for a certain amount of compensation for you!"

The disappearance of Xiaoyu sparked various speculations. If she had been kidnapped by kidnappers, why didn't they ask for ransom? People were puzzled.

"For those few days, I don't know anything, it was all blurry."
