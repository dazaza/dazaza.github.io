Jackie Chan looks aged when rushing for a play, Will Smith loves to shoot films with his son who has enough style

by zhlatwet80 on 2012-02-08 16:05:52

Brother Jackie Chan looks a little haggard. In the past, every time I met Jackie Chan, he always had an energetic expression. However, since he took on the new movie "Kung Fu Kid", with his short hair and beard, Jackie Chan always looked dejected, and appeared very worn out. The other day, when reporters went to the set of "Kung Fu Kid", we once again saw Jackie Chan with a vacant look in his eyes - indeed, time spares no one, not even Brother Jackie.

Jackie Chan was busy filming day and night; it was raining when he returned to the hotel to rest. Around 9:00 am on the 4th, reporters went to the filming location at Beijing Film Studio. Jackie Chan and Jaden Smith were shooting "Kung Fu Kid" here. Although the reporters thought they had arrived quite early, we still found Jackie's makeup van on the set, and upon inquiry, learned that Jackie had already started filming.

Because there were many security guards on the set, the reporters could only wait outside for Jackie to appear. Who would have thought that this wait would last for six hours, until a heavy downpour began at 3:00 pm, allowing us to finally see Jackie Chan emerging from the set surrounded by staff members. Due to continuous work, he looked even more haggard than during the opening ceremony not long ago, with a contemplative expression on his face. We later learned that the rain had disrupted normal filming, giving Jackie some time to rest.

Although there were staff holding umbrellas for him, because of the heavy rain, Jackie’s clothes were soaked. However, it seemed he didn't care about it, focusing solely on getting into the car as quickly as possible. Around 4:00 pm, the driver brought Jackie to his hotel. After getting out of the car, he quickly entered his room. It wasn’t until 7:30 pm, after dark, that Jackie reappeared at the hotel entrance. After a few hours of rest, Jackie clearly looked more spirited. The driver soon arrived, and after Jackie got into the car, it headed back towards the set.

Will Smith's son makes waves on the set

Sometimes you can't help but admit, having a powerful father can save years of struggle. Take Hollywood superstar Will Smith's son Jaden for example. With his father's support, this young boy who is crazy about Chinese Kung Fu is finally realizing his dream of martial arts - collaborating with Jackie Chan on the movie "Kung Fu Kid". Of course, the most important thing is that the lead role is not Jackie Chan, who is also famous in Hollywood, but Will's son Jaden. In other words, as one of the producers of the film, Will brought in the kung fu superstar just to fulfill his son's kung fu dream - this father truly has no complaints.

The other day, reporters at the filming site of "Kung Fu Kid" at Beijing Film Studio captured the grand entrance of Jaden. First, the little guy walked out from the lounge, and just as the reporters wondered why there were no staff around, several towering black bodyguards suddenly rushed out and surrounded Jaden. Subsequently, several staff members with walkie-talkies followed up. Jaden strutted confidently while looking around, seemingly enjoying the attention. Most of the local residents didn’t recognize the son of this Hollywood star, but seeing such a grand scene, they all stopped to watch and comment. Jaden seemed to be enjoying it immensely. Later, reporters learned that those staff members with walkie-talkies were actually clearing the way for Jaden to go to the set. This scene was no less impressive than his father Will Smith's. Jaden soon entered the set, and while a few bodyguards accompanied him closely inside, the rest remained vigilant around the perimeter, diligently scanning the surroundings - truly professional.
