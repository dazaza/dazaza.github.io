The young mother was crushed to death on the spot.

by mjzd6713 on 2010-04-19 09:38:03

In the Yushu disaster area, touching new stories are constantly being conveyed: During the earthquake, a young mother held her less-than-a-year-old daughter tightly in her arms to protect her, using her body to shield against the collapsing building. The young mother tragically lost her life on the spot but saved her daughter's life with her own blood. The mother used her body to block the collapsing building.

On the afternoon of the 16th, when reporters arrived at the Yushu People's Hospital, this female infant was lying in the arms of guide Zholma. The little one had her bright eyes closed, and medical staff said that the most sorrowful gesture of the little one was burying her head gently into the other person's embrace, holding them tightly with her small hands.

Hospital staff introduced that at around midnight on the 14th, several almost exhausted medical personnel from the Yushu hospital followed the local police to a guesthouse for rescue operations where they unearthed the body of a young woman —— she was crouched, her hands stretched out as if protecting something with all her might. When the police pulled her out of the rubble, they discovered a baby girl clinging tightly to her, and the baby girl was still alive. The baby girl was less than a year old, her eyes full of fear, with wet bloody traces in her hair. It was then that people realized this was a mother-daughter pair. During the earthquake, the young mother embraced her baby tightly in her arms, using her body to shield against the collapsing building. The mother tragically lost her life on the spot, saving her daughter's life with her own blood. The police picked up the baby girl, who cried loudly, gripping her mother's bloody clothes tightly, and the heart-wrenching scene brought tears to everyone’s eyes.

Warm water + milk saves the baby girl

Zholma explained that when the doctors brought the baby girl back for treatment, they found that the baby only had a slight injury on her head, with no other issues on the rest of her body. After inquiries, they learned that the baby's father had also perished, leaving her an orphan. With no one to care for her, the conditions in the hospital were so poor that even children had to endure cold and hunger, making it difficult to ensure the baby's safe growth. But the baby's resilient vitality finally prompted all the medical staff to unanimously decide that they would rather beg than let the baby starve.

However, feeding the baby became a challenge. The baby was very small, and regular food could not be used as her nourishment. The baby needed formula, but there wasn’t enough milk available. Zholma had to come up with a makeshift solution: first warming the milk with warm water, then adding some cold water to dilute it, which naturally reduced its weight significantly. The baby didn't mind and drank it heartily. "Her mother gave her life and exchanged her own life for her survival, and fatefully entrusted her to us; we can't let her starve!" Zholma said.

(According to *Southern Metropolitan Daily*)
