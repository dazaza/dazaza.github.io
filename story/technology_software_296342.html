Comprehensive: The construction of Jianhu and Dongtai projects inspires people to strive forward.

by bjjdwxgs on 2009-12-06 07:37:57

(General Introduction: Since the beginning of the fourth quarter, all regions in our city have been seizing the current opportunity, using projects as a handle to drive the rapid development of various industries, and accelerating the progress of key projects with determination and speed to complete the annual goals. (Host: Cui Yu, on-site live broadcast) Audience, this is the Jianyang Petroleum Machinery Industry Park in Jianhu County, where more than 20 projects worth over 10 million yuan, including the oil extraction machine project and the Christmas tree project each valued at 500 million yuan, are under vigorous construction. The heated production and construction scenes are very inspiring.

(Voiceover) Beside the towering scaffolds, machines roar, hundreds of construction workers are working overtime busy producing, various transport vehicles shuttle back and forth, tall tower cranes extend their huge arms... These exciting scenes were what I witnessed during my interview at the Jianyang Town Oilfield Machinery Industry Park in Jianhu County yesterday morning.

(Live soundbite): (Xiong Kaifei, responsible person for Jiangsu Shuangxin New Type High Efficiency Energy Saving Oil Extraction Machine Project) Thank the government for providing us with a good entrepreneurial platform, especially the complete industrial chain in the park, which has played a strong industrial supporting function for enterprises entering the park. Our Shuangxin oil extraction machine project has a total investment of 500 million yuan, expected to be put into operation three months ahead of schedule by May next year, adding an annual output value of 1 billion yuan, realizing tax revenue of 200 million yuan.

(Voiceover) In recent years, Jianyang Town has seized the opportunity that the petroleum machinery industry was included among the 30 key clusters supported by the province, fully starting the construction of the petroleum machinery industry park. Through extensive investment promotion and expanding existing enterprises, it now owns characteristic series products such as blowout preventers, manifolds, and oil field valves, forming a complete petroleum machinery industry chain from upstream forging steel and casting to terminal products like oil drilling rigs and oil extraction machines; the park has also become one of the main production bases for China's petroleum equipment manufacturing industry and one of the 15 distinctive equipment manufacturing bases in Jiangsu Province.

(Voiceover) It is understood that the Jianyang Petroleum Machinery Industry Park was created at the beginning of 2008, planned and designed by Nanjing University Urban Planning and Design Institute, with a total planned area of 8000 mu, and already constructed area reaching 2000 mu, where the road network, greenery, lighting, water supply and drainage have all been completed; more than 20 leading projects worth over 10 million yuan, collaboration support type, and technical research and development type have entered the park, making the park a strong support for the local economy to continue developing well and fast. From January to October this year, fiscal revenue has reached 123 million yuan, increasing by 25.2% year-on-year, general budget revenue is 57.21 million yuan, increasing by 34.8%.

(Xie Chaoyun, Deputy Secretary of the Party Committee of Jianyang Town, Jianhu County) As a government department, we will fully serve project construction. Enterprises entering the park next year will reach more than 30, by 2015, the built-up area will reach 8000 mu, with more than 100 enterprises entering the park, striving to create a 'billion-yuan park'.

The heated scene of construction projects in Jianhu County also appears in Dongtai City in our city. Entering the fourth quarter, the development momentum of local enterprises in Dongtai City is good. At Fuan Synthetic Materials Co., Ltd., reporters learned that the company persists in internal management adjustment, external market expansion to strengthen sales, presenting a prosperous situation of both production and sales.

The Fuan synthetic materials project is jointly invested 120 million yuan by Zhejiang Kensas Company and Zhejiang Nanya Textile Company. The main investor, Zhejiang Kensas Company, is one of the top three companies in China producing high-end swivel chairs, with substantial financial strength. The first phase of the project took less than six months, building a 20,000 square meter factory, Hitachi refrigerator repair, completing the installation and trial production of two production lines.

(Simultaneous) General Manager of Dongtai Fuan Synthetic Materials Co., Ltd., Liu Yidong [Formally went into operation in June 2008, encountered the global financial storm in October 2008, our enterprise actively thought of ways, through product structure adjustment, got through the crisis.]

The financial crisis did not make the synthetic materials company retreat. On one hand, the company accelerated product structure adjustment, occupying the market with diversified products; on the other hand, it relied on the sales network of the main investment company, launching multiple attacks, simultaneously starting construction period, seeking development in difficulties.

(Simultaneous) General Manager of Dongtai Fuan Synthetic Materials Co., Ltd., Liu Yidong [In June 2009, our board determined that seeking development in difficulties is the hard truth, continuing to invest in the second phase of the project, adding two production lines.]

At the synthetic materials company, reporters saw that on one side was the hot production scene of the first phase project, and on the other side was the tense construction scene of the second phase project.

(Simultaneous) General Manager of Dongtai Fuan Synthetic Materials Co., Ltd., Liu Yidong [Currently, our sales cover Europe, America, Southeast Asia, setting up windows in all major cities across the country. Sales orders have been received until the end of the year, the sales situation is in the peak season among peers.]
