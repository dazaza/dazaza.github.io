Tencent used WeChat to promote four mobile games which took the top four rankings in Apple's free list

by anonymous on 2013-10-01 21:01:53

Recently, Tencent WeChat gaming platform launched its fourth game "天天跑酷". After ranking first on the free chart of Apple's AppStore, on September 17th, it ranked first on the best-seller chart for the first time. The long-term occupant of the top spot on the best-seller chart, "我叫MT Online", had to settle for second place.

In the AppStore free rankings, the top four positions were held by Tencent WeChat games "天天跑酷", "节奏大师", "天天连萌" and "天天爱消除". In fifth place was the rumored soon-to-be Tencent partner, the best-selling mobile game "Plants vs Zombies 2". As of yesterday, all of the top five games in the AppStore free rankings were related to Tencent.

An analyst noted that Tencent's mobile gaming platform, through the launch of "天天酷跑", has achieved its first "grand slam" in the mobile gaming field, further enhancing the value of Tencent's mobile gaming platform.

However, it is worth noting that currently most of the mobile internet games launched by Tencent are free. How profitable is WeChat at present? Can these two businesses become the next growth point for Tencent?

Tencent's second quarter report shows steady increase in gaming revenue

On August 14th, Tencent released its second quarter 2013 performance. In the report, Tencent stated that the company's second quarter revenue reached 14.4 billion yuan, an increase of 36.6% year-on-year; non-GAAP net profit was 4.152 billion yuan, an increase of 22.6% year-on-year. Of which, online gaming revenue was 7.595 billion yuan, an increase of 1.6% quarter-on-quarter. This increase mainly reflected the income growth of major games such as "地下城与勇士" and "英雄联盟" in China and increased contributions from international markets.

At the same time, in the performance report, Tencent pointed out that the company's online advertising business revenue was 1.2973 billion yuan, an increase of 52.7% quarter-on-quarter and 47.5% year-on-year; the combined monthly active account number of "WeChat/WeChat" reached 235.8 million, an increase of 21.3% quarter-on-quarter and 176.8% year-on-year; value-added services revenue increased 0.8% quarter-on-quarter, reaching 10.7521 billion yuan, accounting for 74.7% of total revenue in the second quarter of 2013.

Regarding the second quarter performance, Tencent Chairman and CEO Pony Ma said: "Benefiting from our market leadership position in online gaming and online advertising business, we achieved solid financial performance in the second quarter, including healthy year-on-year growth in profits and free cash flow. Investments in the mobile sector and platforms have improved user activity on our mobile end products. Smartphone usage of applications such as QQ Mobile Edition, QQ Space Mobile Edition, WeChat, and WeChat have all seen rapid growth. Recently, our first mobile game launched on QQ Mobile Edition and WeChat has been warmly welcomed by users, showcasing our platform strength, high level of activity in our network community, and superior game execution capabilities. We will continue to invest in mobile applications to strengthen our position in the Chinese market and expand our business internationally through WeChat."

Regarding Tencent's second quarter performance, an analyst told a reporter yesterday: "With the strong growth of WeChat users, Tencent stands to gain an advantage as Chinese gamers switch to playing games on smartphones, boosting Tencent's development of online games and other online services."

"Tencent plans to use WeChat to sell games, mobile applications, and advertisements to users. For example, a new feature of WeChat gives it the functionality of a mobile wallet," the aforementioned analyst said.

Institutional valuation of WeChat and WeChat reaches $17.5 billion

Now, gaming and WeChat are gradually becoming one entity. Just a year ago, any brand enthusiastically discussing their mobile marketing plan would talk about Sina Weibo and Apps, but now not mentioning WeChat is considered a dereliction of duty by the marketing department.

This might be an exaggeration, but there is no denying that WeChat's rapidly growing user base is increasingly attracting the attention of brands. According to official data published by Tencent, in January 2013, WeChat's total number of users exceeded 400 million, with more than 100 million daily active users, and 70 million overseas users. Even some industry insiders have commented on WeChat, calling it "one of the most active mobile internet platforms in the world today."

For Tencent's future development, some brokerage institutions believe that given Tencent's introduction of new games and sticker shops on the WeChat platform, WeChat's monetization plan is gradually becoming apparent. Due to the company still having significant monetization opportunities for its mobile user base in the future, especially in the mobile gaming field, they maintain a positive outlook on the stock's long-term growth potential.

Regarding the recent milestone of Tencent's market capitalization exceeding $10 billion, CLSA expressed its view that it believes Tencent's gaming business performance will remain strong. At the same time, CLSA predicts that Tencent's mobile gaming business will become a new source of growth momentum next year, expecting mobile gaming revenue to increase 2 to 3 times next year. Additionally, the institution also raised its valuation of WeChat and WeChat, estimating the valuation to reach $17.5 billion, or $74 per share.
