This lesson talks about the quantity and quality of sending text messages:

1. Quality over quantity. Girls probably don't like overly clingy guys, always buzzing around them all day (unless you're already a couple).

2. Don't always send copied text messages from the internet. Use your own brainpower. Since you like her, put some thought into your messages.

3. Occasionally adopt the strategy of three days fishing and two days drying the nets, catching and releasing. After a series of intense text message attacks, suddenly stop sending any for a day or two. When your text messages become part of her daily life, she will wonder why you haven't sent any today. Whatever you say when you start again, you've already succeeded half the time.

4. After giving enough attention through text messages, the first text message she sends you is often a complaint, like "I'm so annoyed" or "I'm about to collapse." Hehe, what are you waiting for? The best time to pursue a girl is when she needs help the most. Encourage her quickly: Actually, the sky is really blue, dark clouds will eventually disperse; actually, the sea isn't wide, the other shore connects to this one; actually, tears are sweet, when your heart desires; actually, I want you, happy every day. Hold onto the direction of progress. No matter what happens, I will stand by you. I hope to see a strong me, and even more, a strong you!!!

After the text message, give her a call, that's essential.
