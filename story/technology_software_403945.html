The scene unfolds, new decisions are made constantly, we choose and give up, and make promises that are hard to keep, eventually regretting them. Even old flames can reignite. Aries - Translated by Luha

Now is the time to enjoy the feast of love. Your ruling planet Mars, symbolizing passion and self-centeredness, gives you willpower, constantly motivating you, and also gives you strength and temper. This week, astrology suggests letting go of those obsessions, don't try to control every minute of their time, and you'll start to understand that the confusion of love is an eternal mystery. The missing piece of the puzzle, like a story that never ends, is itself an interesting game. Discover it with your heart.

Go hiking and mountain climbing, breathe fresh air, bring just a backpack, go alone or with friends, and take a holiday to release yourself. Be carefree, sometimes innocence can indeed help us understand ourselves better.

Gemini - Translated by Luha

Logic can't solve problems at this moment, but you can put yourself in others' shoes. This way, love can leap over mountains and valleys. I have a feeling that in the coming months, love will be a great adventure. Don't believe in others' assumptions, maintain steady breathing and expectations in your heart.
