In August this year, Jiuquan City, known as the birthplace of flying celestial beings and the cradle of spaceflight, welcomed a group of special guests. They came from all over the country to Jiuquan, not to admire the graceful postures of ancient flying celestial beings in Dunhuang or to specifically experience the grandeur of the modern space city, but for one purpose only: to attend the eighth national micro-irrigation conference, to study and visit at Gansu Da Yu Water-Saving Co., Ltd. The arrival of more than 500 people in Jiuquan was unexpected by the leaders of the Ministry of Water Resources and also left Gansu Da Yu Water-Saving Co., Ltd. overwhelmed with visitors. Director Wang Xiaodong of the Rural Water Conservancy Department of the Ministry of Water Resources said that the national micro-irrigation conference has been held eight times, and the number of participants this time is twice that of previous editions, making it an unprecedentedly grand event.

Walking into the modern production workshop of Da Yu Water-Saving Co., Ltd., the representatives praised the eight production lines for drip irrigation pipes (belts) with world-leading technology. Director Wang Xiaodong mentioned in his opening speech at the eighth national micro-irrigation conference: "I arrived here two days ago, and on the afternoon of the same day, I visited Da Yu Water-Saving Co., Ltd., where we received a lesson. After hearing this, I felt very pleased, truly feeling that the trip was worthwhile and rewarding."

Not only the leaders and experts, but also the peers in the industry were greatly impressed by Da Yu Water-Saving's water-saving drip irrigation demonstration projects and praised the company's overall strength.

One of the projects undertaken by Gansu Da Yu Water-Saving Co., Ltd. is the micro-sprinkler irrigation project at the Qianmu edible fungus base in Xidong Town, Suzhou District, Jiuquan City. After the implementation of the project, the temperature and humidity in the greenhouses were effectively controlled, the survival rate of fungal strains improved, the quality of mushrooms improved, farmers' income increased, achieving the purposes of water saving, land saving, and yield increase, with significant economic benefits. The town mayor of Xidong introduced that each square meter of the Qianmu edible fungus base in Xidong Town can produce 10 kilograms of high-quality double-spore mushrooms. Selling at 5 yuan per kilogram, a 400-square-meter greenhouse can earn more than 20,000 yuan, and most mushroom greenhouses can recover their investment in the same year they are built, showing very promising economic benefits. After completion, the park can produce 2.78 million kilograms of fresh mushrooms annually, generating sales revenue of 12.4 million yuan, with the average net income from edible fungus planting reaching 830 yuan per farmer.

The drip irrigation project of Gansu Xiagheqing Industrial Co., Ltd. is another project undertaken by Gansu Da Yu Water-Saving Co., Ltd., with a total investment of 71 million yuan. To date, 48 drip irrigation wells have been built, controlling an average area of 479.2 mu per well. The main economic crops planted include hops, potatoes, onions, cotton, tomatoes, and peppers. Technicians from Xiagheqing Industrial Co., Ltd. introduced to the representatives that after the implementation of the drip irrigation project, the uniformity of irrigation and fertilizer utilization has significantly improved, water-saving effects are noticeable, machinery and labor power have been saved, labor intensity has decreased, arable land area has effectively expanded, soil in the tillage layer has been improved, yields have increased, and product quality has improved; pest and disease control has achieved zero breakthroughs, water and fertilizer savings range from 40% to 52%, and cost-effectiveness per acre has increased by 419.36 yuan. For example, after implementing drip irrigation for hops, the average yield per acre (dry flowers) reached 320 kg, with a maximum of 338 kg, and α-acid content increased by 1%. Cotton grew uniformly, had higher boll setting rates, heavier individual bolls, generally increasing production by 20%, and low-yield fields by 25%, with better maturity, fiber length, fiber uniformity, and appearance gloss.

Da Yu Water-Saving: Can the advantage of replacing similar products be sustained?

According to public data, Da Yu Water-Saving's (300021) drip irrigation technology is at an advanced level domestically. The company is one of the few companies in China that first introduced and early achieved the domestication of embedded patch-type drip irrigation tapes. The produced embedded patch-type turbulent pressure-compensating dripper and drip irrigation tape, embedded patch-type underground drip irrigation pipe have received national patents. The self-developed DY-D type hydraulic piston stacked disc self-cleaning filter device fills the gap of no domestic equipment in drip irrigation filtration systems. In 2007, the company was listed as a key national high-tech enterprise under the Torch Program and is the only company in the drip irrigation field undertaking the National 863 Program.

According to Wang Guangmin, the board secretary of Da Yu Water-Saving, the international water-saving industry mainly started early and has leading technology in Israel (large-scale agricultural irrigation) and the United States (urban landscaping irrigation). Da Yu Water-Saving absorbed and transformed Israeli technology since 1999, starting at a high level, and after nearly 10 years of development, it is currently the most advanced domestic water-saving technology company.

Wang Guangmin stated that there are more than 500 domestic PVC pipe manufacturing companies and about 20 companies producing complete sets of water-saving engineering dedicated equipment, but only 2-3 companies are on a large scale. Da Yu Water-Saving adopts a "product + project" model, gradually becoming a specialized water-saving irrigation engineering system supplier integrating the R&D, manufacturing, sales of water-saving irrigation materials with the design, construction, and service of water-saving irrigation projects since 2003. In the segmented market of domestic drip irrigation systems, the company's market share exceeds 20%, over 80% in Gansu Province, and over 30% in Xinjiang Province, second only to Tianye Water-Saving (0840.HK), surpassing Beijing Green Source Plastic Union Company, Yangling Qin Chuan Water-Saving Irrigation Equipment Engineering Co., Ltd., and Gansu Risheng·Amite High-Tech Agriculture Co., Ltd.

Among mature water-saving irrigation technologies such as channel irrigation, pipeline irrigation, micro-irrigation, and sprinkler irrigation, micro-irrigation represented by drip irrigation systems has the highest technical content and the best water-saving effect, being the mainstream and direction of global irrigation water-saving technology development. Currently, the domestic drip irrigation market is transitioning from single-wing labyrinth edge-seam drip irrigation tapes to embedded flat dripper drip irrigation tapes (embedded patch-type drip irrigation tapes) and pressure-compensating drip irrigation tapes. Drip irrigation products represented by Xinjiang Tianye (600075) Water-Saving, characterized by single-wing labyrinth edge-seam drip irrigation tapes, have low technical content, poor drip uniformity, short laying lengths (not exceeding 60 meters), and difficulty in improving product quality, facing challenges from embedded flat dripper drip irrigation tapes and other types of drip irrigation tapes, showing a trend of being replaced by embedded flat dripper drip irrigation tapes (embedded patch-type drip irrigation tapes) and pressure-compensating drip irrigation tapes.

Wang Guangmin indicated that some of Da Yu Water-Saving's products can replace similar products from Tianye Water-Saving, showing partial replacement signs in recent years. However, Da Yu Water-Saving's drip irrigation tape selling price is higher than Xinjiang Tianye Water-Saving's similar products, with the latter priced at 1.4-1.5 cents per meter, while Da Yu Water-Saving is around 2 cents. By June 30, 2009, the company had cumulatively completed the construction of water-saving irrigation projects covering over 2 million mu and completed or ongoing large-scale water-saving irrigation projects numbering over 400. Recently, at the end of September and beginning of October, Da Yu Water-Saving secured a 105,000-mu drip irrigation project in a county in Inner Mongolia.
