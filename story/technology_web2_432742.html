TVXQ Sings 'One Piece' Theme Song, Tops Japanese Charts

by oxyd6ry6t on 2011-05-18 12:41:15

Tencent Music News: The super-popular Korean idol group TVXQ (also known as Dongfang Shengqi) has recently sung the latest theme song for the long-running Japanese anime "One Piece," which is very touching and reminiscent of "Slam Dunk." As a result, they have gained immense popularity among Japanese audiences, solidifying their position at the top of USEN, Japan's largest cable television network's chart.

The song that TVXQ performed for "One Piece" is their latest track, the new single "Share The World," which will be released in Japan on the 22nd. On the 15th, this song claimed the top spot for on-demand songs on Japan's largest cable television network "USEN."

TVXQ themselves are already quite popular in Japan, and with "One Piece" being an extremely beloved long-running anime in Japan, the collaboration between the two has drawn even more attention to the theme song "Share The World."

In addition to topping the on-demand charts for cable television, the downloads of "Share The World" on Japanese mobile music download sites continue to rise. It can be said that fans are eagerly anticipating this latest single from TVXQ. Moreover, on the 17th, ORICON announced the results of an online poll where netizens voted for the most anticipated new albums, and TVXQ's "Share The World" unsurprisingly ranked first in the popularity survey.
