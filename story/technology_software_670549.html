Forgot when the last log was written.

by fanli2q829 on 2012-02-21 02:16:53

What is written here? The wheels of time crush memories over and over again. The broken memories are like snapped strings, unable to play the tune of recollections... I am stubborn, care about appearances, and too straightforward in speech. I am a selfish person; I know I have flaws. Perhaps my friends have been too tolerant. All those people who went crazy together in those years, brother will always remember you. Don't overthink it, no homo. Haha... I love playing games. Although games are illusions, not real, they at least let me feel the camaraderie of a team. As the saying goes, the bond between soldiers comes first. Even though we aren't really on the battlefield, the mutual help in the game, the daily greetings among friends, that's what I want most. In the past, with CS, our group of friends would sit in a row at the internet cafe, each with a can of Pepsi. We would cheer or bang the table due to mistakes. Back then, I wasn't very good at playing, just starting middle school. Those days will forever be unforgettable for me. Back then, I even thought it was paradise. But reality is always so harsh. Everyone has to live their life. Some are now making money out of province, some are driving cars, and several have been married and started families for years... Later, I played alone and met my current amateur team King#_NO.1 on HaoFang. Although we've also been beaten badly, when I first joined, everyone took care of me. Thus, I got the name Xiao T, mysteriously adding a "Xiao" (small) character. More team competitions temporarily made me forget the sadness of those friends who left from playing CS... Later, I gradually got into "The Stellar Transformation" Zhejiang 2nd district Yin Di Star's number one celestial-magic guild 【Return of the Emperor】, winning the cave residence battle at first and becoming feared by both celestial and magic factions. However, under the leader absconding with thousands of dollars, the morale of this guild he had managed for two months plummeted. We all questioned: could thousands of dollars betray all the brothers? Later, reality is reality, he never showed up again... Games are reflections of reality. Without strength, you'll be bullied. Interests exist similarly, just not as obvious. Later, we formed a new guild called "Dominance," still the top celestial-magic guild, but some previous problems remained. With Shanda's continuous glitches, many friends were permanently banned. One must say Shanda isn't that competent. There are too many stories to recount individually. Brother doesn't just play games; I'm learning too. I'm not an RMB player, and online games are never green games. Aren't electricity and internet fees money? Life itself is a game. It doesn't matter how you compete with others, but how you enjoy it. After all, different ways of playing lead to different ways of living. Uh... What I said makes me sound old. To sum it up: some people and events just tell us something. Games count too. When they finish teaching us, they leave. They are just passers-by, yet they leave behind contemplative crowds.
