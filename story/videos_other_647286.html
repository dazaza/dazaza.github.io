Lingshan Yuan Yi Lixing Hot Spring

by lingxi54 on 2011-12-29 15:12:05

With the arrival of winter, soaking in hot springs has become a choice for many people to relax during their "New Year's Day" holiday travel. For people in the Yangtze River Delta region, there are indeed many places nearby where they can soak in hot springs: Drunken Hot Springs in Huangshan, Tang Dynasty style hot springs in Wuyi, and Tangshan in Nanjing, etc. However, due to excessively concentrated customer flow, the experience reputation of many hot spring clubs has declined somewhat. Now, within this "hot spring circle" in the Yangtze River Delta, there is another star-level member - Lingshan Yuan Yi Lisheng Hot Spring in Wuxi. In August, it just hosted the contestants of the 61st Miss World China Final here, where the beauties took swimsuit photo shoots.

The first phase of Lingshan Yuan Yi Lisheng Hot Spring has an outdoor landscape area of 58,000 square meters, with a total of 72 characteristic hot spring pools and houses. It is divided into six major characteristics: Dynamic Hot Spring Area, Characteristic Hot Spring Area, Dense Forest Hot Spring Area, Business Lake View Hot Spring Area, Outdoor Swimming Pool, and Children's Water Play Area. Its hot spring originates from deep within the earth's crustal fault, formed by the evolution and infiltration of underground thermal magma over several decades before finally surfacing. The bottom temperature of the hot spring well is 70°C, the mouth temperature is 50°C, and the pH of the spring water is 7.49. The minerals, trace elements, and special chemical components contained in the hot spring make Lingshan Yuan Yi Lisheng Hot Spring not only effective for various diseases but also beneficial for health preservation, beauty, skin care, and recuperation. Tourists in the hot spring pool are surrounded by lush forests, facing the vast Taihu Lake, naturally fostering a sense of harmony between man and nature.

The first phase of the club features functional areas including Asia's largest Noblewomen's Club, Gentlemen's Club, Lisha SPA, Health Care Hall, Imperial Foot Palace, Lisha Lijia, Sinology Hall, Yoga Hall, and premium luxury guest rooms; The second phase includes 135 premium characteristic luxury guest rooms, along with hotel ancillary facilities. Lingshan Yuan Yi Lisheng Hot Spring integrates hot spring culture, Zen culture, health care culture, and leisure resort culture in creating a harmonious atmosphere between man and nature, reflecting its high-end positioning of "Ultimate Life, Made in China."

Lingshan Yuan Yi Lisheng Hot Spring Resort Hotel is an Asian-level leisure and wellness resort destination invested in by Yuan Yi Group at a cost of 300 million yuan. Located on the west side of Ling Mountain Scenic Area in Wuxi Taihu National Holiday Resort, it leans against Maji Mountain and overlooks 36,000 hectares of Taihu Lake scenery. The resort covers an area of 100 mu (about 67 acres), with a total construction area of 25,000 square meters, including 15,000 square meters in the first phase and 10,000 square meters in the second phase. The hotel features multiple themed hot spring houses and 72 themed hot spring pools, managed by the renowned domestic professional management team Lisha Group.
