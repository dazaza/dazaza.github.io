【Omega Ladies' Watches】 A story of a man and a woman

by gopium34321 on 2012-02-24 22:57:59

"Honey, you have a phone call!"

Male: Hello?

Female: Hello, are you...?

Male: Yes, who is this?

Female: I'm...

Male: Oh, it's you! Is there something you need?

Female: Can you come out for a moment? I'm outside your house, by the Omega Seamaster series.

"Should I go? Honey, I trust you."

Female: Let's have some tea!

Male: What is it, just say it quickly! It's late. I don't feel comfortable leaving my wife alone at home; she's afraid of the dark.

Female: I'm divorced!

Male: Why? Didn't your husband love you very much?

Female: That bastard, he cheated on me with another woman, wailing.

Male: Alright, don't be sad. You're still young, and better things await you...

Female: Do you hate me?

Male: It's all in the past now. I consider you a friend, so there's no question of hatred or not.

Female: Really?

Male: Of course, have I ever lied to you?

Female: Do you still love me?

Male: ...Yes.

Female: Let's get married then, I will definitely be a virtuous and capable wife, an Omega women's watch.

Male: I already have a wife. We just got our marriage certificate last week, and we'll be getting married in two months.

Female: But aren't you still not officially married?

Male: No, it's not right. She loves me deeply, and I can't hurt her.

Female: Would you feel bad if I went to tell her...?

Male: Even that wouldn't work.

Female: Why? Wailing.

Male: Because I am a man, and I mustn't do such a thing.

Female: I want to know exactly why. You still love me, and I still love you, why can't we be together again?

Male: Do you really want to know why?

Female: Hmm.

Male: 1: Knowing how hard it is to fall in love with someone and understanding the pain after being hurt, I can't leave her.

2: The reason I decided to get married is because I've chosen her for this life. Choosing her means she is the only woman in my life. No matter what we encounter in the future—whether wind or rain—I will stand in front of her to protect her from the elements.

3: She loves me deeply, she's silly, kind-hearted, and always thinks of me in everything she does. I came here today only after her advice. She trusts me, believes I won't betray her. Her decision to marry me is her decision to give her entire life to me. A woman’s life cannot be trampled upon.

"Understand?"

Female: I understand...

"There are two kinds of beauty in the world: inner beauty and outer beauty. Outer beauty may satisfy your vanity but may not bring lifelong happiness. Inner beauty, though it might not give you enough face, it certainly brings lifelong happiness. Women, is vanity more important or happiness more important?"

Female: Can we be friends?

Male: No, I don’t need female friends outside of my girlfriend! Girls like you don't share common interests with me; the things I enjoy playing with, you girls don't like either. Second, you can't meet the needs that my girlfriend provides. Third, I don't have time to hang out or eat with you, those are things I do with my girlfriend. Fourth, my girlfriend would be jealous. So why bother?

The greatest pride for a man isn't sleeping with many women, but having one woman willing to let him sleep with her for a lifetime.

The greatest pride for a woman isn't having many men, but having a man willing to refuse countless other women for her.

Men must withstand temptation, and women must endure loneliness.

**Translation Note:** This text involves dialogue between characters and explores themes of loyalty, trust, and values in relationships. Certain references (e.g., watches) are used metaphorically or as part of the narrative context. Some phrases were slightly rephrased for natural flow in English while preserving the original meaning.
