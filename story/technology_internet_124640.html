by applelew on 2009-02-14 12:29:30

--- --- Your life information expert. Originating from life, surpassing life, and striving to improve the quality of people's lives.

To liven up the atmosphere of Ailu Road and enrich its content, Ailu Road urgently needs resources or articles of quality and substance that can be helpful to everyone. Therefore, we are hosting this event where the main task is to post articles for a chance to win a cash prize in a <Top Red Packet> giveaway. The details of the event are as follows:

1. This event focuses on posting articles. Each registered user earns 1 point for every article posted.

2. For already published articles, each "like" (or "top") received from other users adds 1 point.

3. We encourage financial management and online earning-related articles. Articles in this category have a weight of 1, while other categories have a weight of 0.5 (i.e., each "like" adds 0.5 points).

4. Every 1 point represents a vote for your username in the prize pool. The higher your score, the greater your chances of winning.

5. The final interpretation rights of the event belong to the Ailu Road website.

(Note: If malicious "liking" behavior is detected during the event, the scoring strategy may be adjusted.)

Event duration: February 15th ~ March 15th

The lottery algorithm is implemented through static scripts, increasing the transparency of the event. The site administrator will conduct the lottery, announce the results on this page with screenshots, and contact the lucky members via the email provided at registration. Payments will be made through Alipay, and payment screenshots will also be published on this page.
