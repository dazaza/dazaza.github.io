The story of "Gleam of Midsummer" starts in summer and ends in summer. Every girl has a little secret in her heart, related to a young man, and related to the assumption of happiness.

Jiang Xiaojian, who became associated with Ke Ruixi due to her father's death, secretly liked this young man in her heart, but was overshadowed by the huge shadow of revenge. He cared for her, tolerated her, and put up with all her little tantrums. Almost everyone envied and was jealous of Jiang Xiaojian and Ke Ruixi. However, the arrival of another person broke this complete pattern...
