Cooperation with Japanese customers can enhance the quality of the company's products - Interview with Yiwu Oujie Clothing Co., Ltd.

by baiyasin2s on 2012-02-27 20:41:35

Interviewed company: Yiwu OuJie Apparel Co., Ltd. Interviewee: General Manager Fu Rongzheng Main products: Belts, scarves, hats, accessories, headwear, bags, promotional gifts, etc.

Four years ago, with a simple and pure thought, Fu Rongzheng proactively reached out to Alibaba. "It wasn't because of the lack of orders; I just wanted to find a network where a few employees could stay busy with work and take the company to the next level. At that time, I saw that the most famous name in the field of e-commerce was Alibaba. So, I made a call and said, 'Send someone over to talk to me.'" This call marked the beginning of the relationship between Yiwu OuJie and Alibaba. "To date, I am developing a series on Alibaba's English site, Taobao, Japan site, and more. I deeply appreciate my choice."

Since its establishment, Yiwu OuJie has been focusing on developing products for mid-to-high-end customers. "Our quality system management and product development are well done, which can be considered a good prelude to doing business in Japan. Japan relatively demands high-quality products, and since Alibaba launched its Japan site, I feel it is an excellent opportunity to develop Japanese clients."

There is basically stable growth every month, and the Japan site increasingly boosts my confidence. Since joining the China-Japan Express service for half a year, Yiwu OuJie has seen steady growth each month from inquiry volume to transaction volume. "We receive many Japanese clients, averaging over three per month."

Fu Rongzheng smiled and said, "The Japan site gives me increasing confidence." Fu Rongzheng still remembers the first order he received through the Japan site vividly. It was a Japanese client ordering belts. After two weeks of negotiation, the client placed an order with OuJie. "The client ordered over 600 belts. Each belt had tens of thousands of beads, with 95% completed by hand." Belts are OuJie's strong product, and Fu Rongzheng is confident in their quality. However, not all ten thousand beads can be perfectly round and smooth. "This client has a relatively good understanding of the Yiwu market and the Chinese market, so they raised some concerns."

According to general views, having one or two uneven beads is inevitable. Nevertheless, Fu Rongzheng sincerely accepted the client's feedback. "He was quite satisfied with my service, and this client has been in continuous contact and cooperation with us up to now."

"Speaking with strength gave me room to maneuver." Fu Rongzheng has always been grateful to this Japanese client who provided feedback. He frankly stated, "For example, if I sell this belt to European and American clients for 20 yuan, Japanese clients can pay up to 25 or even 30 yuan. Price isn't the issue, but you must ensure the quality. This realization made me understand that succeeding with Japanese clients is your greatest opportunity for success." The style of Japanese clients, who speak with strength, undoubtedly provides a broad stage for OuJie's development. "The first goal of Japanese clients is that the product they need must help establish a brand or set a standard within the industry. They consider this as their primary goal rather than profit. This is why I value the Japanese market. With the support of Japanese clients in terms of unit price and funds, whatever the client needs, I can provide."

"Cooperating with Japanese clients can enhance the quality of our company's products." Currently, China's trade has shifted from price competition to quality competition. There is no longer purely price-based competition; instead, more emphasis is placed on quality competition. The astute Fu Rongzheng has adhered to the quality route since the company's inception. "I want to cooperate with Japanese clients to enhance the quality of our company's products. I believe that cooperating with them will significantly improve the quality of our company's personnel and workers in cooperative factories. Workers are trained and taught by managers and practitioners. If their thinking does not reach a certain level of improvement, even the best factory cannot excel."

Finding inspiration for product development through observation and learning Fu Rongzheng's insights into the Japanese market are profound and unique. The reporter couldn't help but wonder if there was a think tank providing strong support? "In foreign trade, you must understand the market trends and national culture. This is another hardware requirement for foreign trade. Therefore, I have put in a lot of effort myself regarding the Japanese market." Despite his busy schedule, Fu Rongzheng always reminds himself to keep "recharging."

"For instance, American clients generally bring fashion magazines whenever they come, as fashion trends and styles are inseparable. These pieces of information genuinely give me some direction for product development." Fu Rongzheng has a keen sense of fashion, "Observing what large Japanese department stores and direct sales companies are selling now and what they are purchasing allows me to recommend similar items to potential clients. Conversely, Japanese clients, through my perspective, might think this company has a good understanding of the Japanese market, otherwise, how would they recommend products that are currently popular or about to become popular in Japan? I think this is a great breakthrough."

Post-interview notes Fu Rongzheng humorously remarked that his decision to join Alibaba was somewhat "serendipitous." Watching the company grow from a handful of people to over forty employees, Fu Rongzheng feels most deeply about the advice and help Alibaba has provided in management and systems.

This transformation from small to big moved the reporter immensely.

Company Profile Yiwu OuJie Apparel Co., Ltd. is an enterprise integrating production processing and wholesale distribution, specializing in belts, scarves, hats, accessories, headwear, bags, promotional gifts, etc. The company has established long-term cooperative relationships with multiple enterprises due to its strong capabilities, reasonable prices, and excellent services. Its products are exported to various international markets including the United States, Europe, Japan, and more, receiving widespread praise from both new and old clients.
