Yang Yuying, who has been out of the public eye for 11 years, made a comeback last night on Shenzhen TV's "The Show of Decades," marking the beginning of Yang Yuying's return. At the recording site in Shenzhen from 5 PM to 11 PM, news about Yang Yuying's impending appearance was buzzing non-stop. By 11:00 PM when this article was finalized, Yang Yuying finally revealed her true face and sang a duet with Mao Ning titled "Every Day In My Life". (Click on the image to enter Mao Ning's Weibo)

Golden Boy Meets Jade Girl: Mao Ning’s Eagerness to Ride the Coattails

Gently tell you, I'll wait for you at the old place. Yesterday afternoon, Mao Ning posted a photo of himself and Yang Yuying from their heyday on Weibo, trying to build momentum. As the famous Golden Boy and Jade Girl duo from the past, Mao Ning has frequently appeared on television, not only performing and commenting on talent shows but also releasing a new album this year with a rather disruptive style. Mao Ning mentioned that many people hoped he would persuade Yang Yuying to make a comeback, and he did try hard, but eventually gave up completely. When asked about the reason, he declined to elaborate, only stating that he and Yang Yuying were still good friends who kept in touch regularly.

According to reports, Ms. Su, the publicity planner of Yang Yuying's record company Guangdong New Era, confirmed that Mao Ning had extended several invitations to Yang Yuying, hoping to collaborate once again on stage. Does this appearance on Shenzhen TV's "The Show of Decades" mean the official comeback of the two? Mao Ning's agent Chen Bo stated that negotiations with Sichuan TV are still ongoing. Regarding Mao Ning's plans for a full comeback, Mr. Chen frankly said: It's under consideration, and we need to see more details. Some people believe that Mao Ning's evasive responses are an attempt to stir up market interest for his future career.

Behind the Scenes of the Comeback: Private Friendship With the Female Director Plays a Key Role

In recent years, photos of Yang Yuying shopping in supermarkets, watching plays, or taking children on vacation have occasionally surfaced online, but she never appeared at public events. Mao Ning had once remarked that Yang Yuying had seen through it all and would never return to the limelight. So what prompted Yang Yuying to decide on a comeback this time?

It was learned on-site that Yang Yuying chose "The Show of Decades" for her comeback mainly due to Yi Hua, Vice Director of Shenzhen TV. It is understood that Yi Hua was once a top producer at Hunan TV, responsible for producing numerous hit TV programs such as "Happy Camp", "Super Girl", and "Gold Magic Group". She officially joined Shenzhen TV in May this year and successfully turned "The Show of Decades" into one of the top three rated shows in its timeslot.

Around 2000, Yang Yuying participated in a gala at Hunan TV, where she formed a great friendship with Yi Hua. This appearance was the result of Yi Hua's repeated heartfelt invitations. Moreover, "The Show of Decades" aims to revisit classic memories of different eras, which perfectly aligns with Yang Yuying's presence. Staff members from the production team revealed that Yang Yuying had no special requirements regarding hospitality, maintaining a very low profile. She said that as long as the program was good and suitable, it would be the best thing.

It is reported that Yang Yuying's episode of "The Show of Decades" will air on January 13th next year.

Years of Silence: Karaoke and Learning English, Never Living in a Mansion

What kind of life has Yang Yuying led over these years? Recently, Ms. Su, the publicity planner of Yang Yuying's record company, disclosed in an interview with the media that in recent years, Yang Yuying spent much time traveling and enjoyed being with her family. She participated in some friend gatherings and charity activities and spent time learning foreign languages. Her life was very peaceful and simple. Ms. Su said that over the years, Yang Yuying had always loved singing, especially during private friend gatherings where she would sing many songs. She liked karaoke and often sang songs by Jolin Tsai, as well as popular songs by Hong Kong and Taiwan stars. She also sang folk songs and nostalgic tunes; her vocal range was quite broad, and she sounded good singing many songs.

There were previous reports claiming that Yang Yuying ran a successful beauty salon. Ms. Su stated that Yang Yuying herself had little interest in investment and finance, leaving those matters to her family. She maintained a calm attitude towards life, simple and serene. Perhaps due to this mindset, her appearance hasn't changed much over the years, and everyone envies her. People on the street can easily recognize her.

Ms. Su introduced that Yang Yuying currently resides in Shenzhen. Regarding rumors that she lives in a luxury mansion, Ms. Su denied them: It's just an ordinary house in Shenzhen, which may have become a 'mansion' due to property value appreciation. Talking about Yang Yuying's personal life, Ms. Su also expressed that they usually communicated about work-related matters and hoped that people would give her some private space.
