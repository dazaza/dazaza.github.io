I dreamed of you again last night.

by lili1 on 2012-03-04 22:45:20

Last night, I dreamt of you again.

When the night can no longer bear the weight of longing, dreams appear. When I cannot resist my thoughts of you, you appear in my dreams. Last night, I dreamt of you again. You danced with your long hair flowing and your clothes fluttering in the breeze, softly singing under my gaze. We lived by the water, in a humble bamboo-walled cottage, free from the noise and splendor of modern cities, without barriers made of concrete. We lived simply and happily. You washed silk by the stream, while I fished in the water, enjoying the warmth of each other's company and the tranquility bestowed upon us by nature. Under the moonlight, I sang ancient love songs for you, and you recited timeless poems for me. "At the clear white stone beach, green rushes grow thick. Homes are scattered east and west of the water, where women wash their silk under the bright moon." What serenity and purity! I silently clasped my hands in prayer to Buddha, if only I could remain forever drunk in this dream!

But the dream ended with your laughter as I woke up. Yet, I didn't want to leave the dream. Closing my eyes, I let the fantasies of reality continue the beauty of the dream. The faint warmth of your embrace still lingered in my arms, and before my eyes was your charming expression. In a daze, I couldn't tell whether I was in a dream or reality. This feeling made me drunk and reluctant to leave. Some say that yearning is sweet, but mutual yearning is happiness. My dear, are you dreaming of me at this moment? Do you also smile gently and laugh when I'm in your dreams? I wish I could sail a small boat to your heart's sea, gently open the door of dreams, and see if we are wandering in the same dream...

My dear, do you know? Thinking of you has become the heaviest emotion in my heart, not a burden, but an enjoyment! During the day, I can only suppress my thoughts of you with frantic work. Thus, I yearn for the arrival of night like an addict craving drugs, because only at night can I hold you in my arms and protect you from wind and rain. Only at night can you occasionally enter my dreams and speak moving words of love to me. This night carries the weight of our longing and holds my thoughts of you. I wish to hold your hand and embrace your shoulders, walking into the next cycle together, not asking how far eternity is, only hoping to go as far as it takes.

My dear, let me tell you, being able to embrace you in my dreams already makes me very content. I use a colorful brush to add color to my dreams of you, and use musical notes to decorate the space of my longing. I believe that one day Buddha will be moved by us and build a rainbow on the tunnel of time and space, compressing the distance between us into a beautiful fairy tale.

My dear, "Let me dream of you every night, though you have gone far away. Thank you for occasionally returning to my dreams, speaking moving words of love to me..."
