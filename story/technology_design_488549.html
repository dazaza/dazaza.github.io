Five Kinds of Women You Should Not Marry

After knowing which women you should marry, of course, you should also know which kinds of women absolutely cannot be married. Marrying them could lead to family discord, and the relationship may always be on the verge of breaking at any time. This is a small matter; if it causes your interpersonal relationships outside to deteriorate as well, then it would truly be counterproductive. Men, take a good look! The next time you encounter a woman like this, don't lose your mind and start pursuing her!

Women's Faces That Should Not Be Married - Type One: Eyebrows Too Wide Apart

The space between the two eyebrows is called the "Yintang." A wide Yintang represents a broad-minded person, someone who doesn’t care much about being with another man. Originally, separated eyebrows indicate a person with great tolerance, but if they are wider than two fingers apart, it turns into promiscuity. If the eyebrows droop at the ends forming an eight-like shape, it signifies even more a lack of assertiveness. Such individuals are easily tempted by external money or benefits and can easily be led astray by friends. If friends invite them for drinks, they go for drinks; if invited for illicit activities, such women might follow along too. Because these women accept all kinds of friends from various social strata without discrimination, marrying such a woman requires extra caution to ensure she doesn't stray.

Women's Faces That Should Not Be Married - Type Two: Hooked Eye Corners

If the eyelashes form an arc and appear round and lively, constantly flickering, this is known as hooked eye corners. Women with such eyes are very quick-witted, but because their minds move so fast, calling them cunning and crafty wouldn't be far off. Many outwardly elegant and seemingly sincere and polite women are not as simple as they appear. While they may not say much to your face, they like to meddle behind the scenes and aren't honest at all. They may juggle multiple relationships or associate with married men for personal gain, saying one thing but doing another. Despite appearing vulnerable to being caught, they are actually quite adept at what they do and won't be discovered so easily. If you plan to marry such a woman, it’s recommended that you first develop the ability to see through people’s hearts.

Women's Faces That Should Not Be Married - Type Three: Fox Eyes

Fox eyes are commonly referred to as alluring eyes. The inner corner of the eye is slightly lower and curved, while the outer corner tilts upward, making them particularly seductive. Women with such eyes may not be exceptionally beautiful, but they have a certain charm that attracts some types of men. These women are very protective of their men, keeping a close watch to prevent them from being taken away by other women. However, this behavior puts immense pressure on the man, making him feel like he's under constant surveillance. Excessive jealousy and suspicion make the relationship tense. If you don't want to be bombarded with calls every day, it's advisable not to marry such a woman. Otherwise, living in constant tension will not sustain a long-lasting relationship.

Women's Faces That Should Not Be Married - Type Four: Pointed Nose

People with pointed and thin noses generally aren't very kind-hearted. They are manipulative and skilled at scheming. Women with such noses can easily see through others' thoughts. In terms of personality, they are considered precocious. When most girls are still dreaming about romance, they already understand human relations well. After entering society, they no longer adhere to moral principles. These women have very strong egos and are prone to anger. They cannot tolerate criticism, regardless of whether they are right or wrong. Criticizing them is seen as incorrect. They are difficult to teach and often have poor interpersonal relationships. As a partner, you must always be vigilant against their tricks and careful in your interactions to avoid hurting their pride. Men who deal with such women need to be smarter and more intelligent.

Women's Faces That Should Not Be Married - Type Five: Jagged Teeth

Jagged teeth are messy, sharp, and slanted. Their front teeth don't resemble proper front teeth, and their canine teeth don't look like proper canines. At first glance, it looks like encountering the ghost from "The Ring." Who would dare to bring a woman like this home to sleep beside? Seeing her late at night might scare the life out of you! Women with such teeth are extremely deceitful and love spreading rumors. They seem insincere, speaking differently depending on the audience. Usually, women with jagged teeth have bad luck throughout their lives. Their future isn't bright, and they often experience poverty and unemployment. So, if men want to marry a wife to reduce ten years of struggle, such women would not be the best choice!
