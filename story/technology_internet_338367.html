Sichuan Travel: The opening of the Wuhan-Guangzhou high-speed railway boosts tourism, making travel more enjoyable for Hong Kong residents and people from Guangdong.

by f2ds1dw7 on 2010-04-01 13:00:53

On the 31st, a large and significant delegation organized by the Wuhan Municipal Tourism Bureau arrived at Shaoguan, a key town in northern Guangdong, to promote Wuhan's tourism products. Enthusiastic "Nine-Headed Birds" flew to Guangdong to invite people from Guangdong to visit Hubei and Sichuan for tourism. Their slogan was: "Experience world speed, feel Chu culture and Han charm."

This official also said that this time, the Wuhan tourism industry brought specially designed high-speed rail tourism products to Guangdong for promotion, having already visited Shenzhen, Guangzhou, Foshan, etc., with very enthusiastic responses. In Guangzhou alone, contracts were signed on the spot for more than 40 high-speed rail tourism groups to visit Wuhan soon. For this reason, the Wuhan Municipal Tourism Bureau also initiated the establishment of a "High-Speed Rail Tourism Alliance," receiving strong support from all parties.

An official from the Market Promotion Department of the Wuhan Municipal Tourism Bureau told reporters that the "Romantic Cherry Blossom Festival in Wuhan" product launched by the Wuhan tourism industry in March has been continuously popular, with an average of more than 300 high-speed rail tourism groups from Guangdong and Hong Kong-Macau regions visiting every weekend. During this year's Spring Festival, on average, more than 10,000 tourists from Guangdong took the high-speed rail to Wuhan daily to climb the first tower under heaven - Yellow Crane Tower, and tour China's largest urban lake - East Lake, fully experiencing the Chu cultural charm.

Deputy Director Chen of the Shaoguan Municipal Tourism Bureau stated that Shaoguan, entrusted by the Guangdong Provincial Tourism Bureau, will host a High-Speed Rail Tourism Forum in May this year, aiming to further promote the development of the high-speed rail tourism era.

In the run-up to the opening of the Wuhan-Guangzhou High-Speed Railway, the China Travel Association of Hong Kong organized a 30-plus member tourism inspection team for a three-day visit to Shaoguan's main tourist attractions. Renowned figures in the tourism industry, Secretary of the China Travel Association of Hong Kong, Mr. Chen Lizhi, is very optimistic about Shaoguan's tourism market. He expressed that the China Travel Association of Hong Kong is willing to make efforts to promote cooperation between Hong Kong and Shaoguan and jointly promote the development of the tourism industry in both places.

The opening of the Wuhan-Guangzhou High-Speed Railway has greatly boosted tourism, making travel more convenient for residents of Hong Kong and Guangdong. With the opening of the Wuhan-Guangzhou High-Speed Railway, the ground transportation time between Wuhan and Guangzhou, two major cities separated by thousands of miles but with deep historical ties, has been shortened from over 10 hours to less than 4 hours, greatly facilitating exchanges between the two places, along the line urban and rural areas, as well as Hong Kong and Macao tourists. The opening of the Wuhan-Guangzhou High-Speed Railway has also greatly promoted the tourism industry in Central South China and the Hong Kong-Macao region, making travel much more enjoyable for residents of Hong Kong and Guangdong.

The excellent Chinese tourism city of Shaoguan, which owns the World Natural Heritage nomination site (to be decided in July this year) Danxia Mountain in Guangdong, has benefited significantly from the opening of the Wuhan-Guangzhou High-Speed Railway. Since the beginning of this year, travel agencies from the Pearl River Delta, Hong Kong, Macao, Taiwan, Hunan, Hubei, and other places have launched projects titled "Take the Wuhan-Guangzhou High-Speed Railway to Explore the Magical Danxia," which have been extremely popular in the market, resulting in a significant increase in group tourists. Statistical data shows that during this year's Spring Festival alone, Danxia Mountain in Guangdong received 585 tourist groups, an increase of 31% compared to last year, with high-speed rail groups accounting for half of them. Especially, the number of overseas tourists from Hong Kong, Macao, and Taiwan increased by as much as 380%. A tourist surnamed Wang from Hong Kong once told reporters: "We not only experienced the fastest train in the world but also saw the poetic and picturesque scenery of Danxia Mountain. This Spring Festival was truly unforgettable!"
