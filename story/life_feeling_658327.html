Silent Keeping Without Mutual Cherishing

The autumn dawn is always so desolate, so unsatisfactory. There's no floral fragrance, no bird chirping, only the cold wind filled with thick mist. I remember once, also at the end of autumn, we met. No one would have thought that we would fall in love because our love was so hard, so unreal.

My home and yours are not in the same place; neither do we study in the same place. Thus, by a chance encounter, we got to know each other. Perhaps we were destined to be different, even our meeting was extraordinary. I often joked and said: our experiences could be written into a romantic drama. However, we couldn't escape from the plot of a romantic drama, nor did it have an ending like one, and I seemed more like the third party in a romantic drama.

Since we were both studying, we were both busy, so we had an unwritten rule to make a phone call every Saturday night. Back then, we both looked forward to Saturdays because of you, because of me. Time gradually passed, and my busy high school life also came to an end. But when I stopped to look at you, I found that you had changed. Yes! I broke our initial vows, I failed the college entrance exam again, and you made the decision to leave me.

Picking up a yellowing leaf, its veins were still vaguely visible. It was already autumn, and the autumn on the university campus was bound to be sorrowful. As a freshman, I wandered around this unfamiliar environment over and over... Maybe you were right, I'm not an undergraduate student, following me might really not lead to happiness. The truth is also such, you found a boyfriend from a technical university, you kept it hidden from your friends, and also from me, but eventually, it couldn't be hidden anymore. I sent a congratulatory text message, and thus tried as much as possible to disappear from your world.

Saying I don't miss you is self-deception. Saying I'm not sad is even more self-deception. So I chose to indulge in the internet, to fall, to fall without any bottom line. Unconsciously, I hurt another girl. I knew she wanted to save me, and she gave her all. But the window of the soul had already closed, and it was difficult to open again.

Thus, I spent the first year of my university life. Just before the summer vacation, you called me, I hesitated for a moment, but still answered. You said you broke up with him, I tried my best to comfort you. You asked if we could still be together, I didn't answer. You said you know I love you, I can't deny it. You asked if you could wait for me for another year? By then, I will be out, and we can be together. I said okay.

The heat of early summer makes our moods restless, you began to take the initiative to contact me. You learned how to act cute, liked crying loudly to me when you were sick or sad. After I consoled you, you smiled and said: "I like it when you're good to me." Thus, we were like normal couples, but a sensitive instinct made me feel a trace of unease.

Summer vacation arrived, I went to work in another city, and you were preparing for your undergraduate entrance examination. Suddenly one day, you didn't answer my call, I thought you had something going on. Thus, one day, two days... more than two weeks passed, you still had no news. I started to worry, I worried that something might happen to you, worried that you got sick and didn't tell me.

Finally one day, you replied to my text message, you said you went home and forgot to bring your phone. But it was as if it was a matter of course. I asked if you still want to be together? You silently replied, "I think we don't need to be together anymore." I asked why? No reason. I said, "In two days, I'll go see you there." You wanted to say something else, but finally just said, "Whatever."

Thus, I quit my job, returned to that familiar city, to meet the now very strange you. I stayed at a classmate's house, just to see you, waiting for you to have time, and the day also came as expected. After meeting, we didn't talk too much, I almost forgot what I came here to do, I just liked looking at you, looking at you. After I went home, I asked: "Were you happy today?" You said it was okay. I seemed to see hope, "Then can we continue?" "Sorry, we really don't suit each other." Thus, no matter if I called or texted you, you didn't reply.

Originally, you really left. No, you've been gone for a long time, I just didn't realize it. Until now, your QQ password has been changed long ago, but mine has never changed.
