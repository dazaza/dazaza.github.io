How could such enterprises enter the park?

by cgccg001 on 2009-12-20 02:40:49

Voiceover: If you worked hard for several months but didn't get paid, what would you think? This is exactly what happened to more than a hundred workers at the Haida Electronics Factory in TeYong Town, Sheyang County. It's strange that in today’s society where everyone denounces wage arrears, how can such a thing still happen? After hearing the news, reporters immediately went to the scene for an interview. Mr. Wu is the delivery man of Haida Electronics Factory in the city area. Under his guidance, the reporter drove all the way to Haida Electronics Factory. On the car, Mr. Wu told us about his unfortunate experience.

Mr. Wu同期声 "I was recruited by him in the talent market. Later I asked him how much salary per month, he said 1000 yuan per month, then I started working for him."

Thus, from December last year to last month, Mr. Wu worked for nearly three months, but he never expected that when it came to getting paid, something unexpected happened.

Mr. Wu同期声 "I asked Ms Meng Lihua, the boss, Ms Meng, now it's almost time for the New Year, what about our wages? He said: no money, no money. I said how come there is no money, I work for you, how come you have no money for me! Now all the workers have no money to pay, I said when will we be paid? He said tomorrow, or wait a few more days."

This waiting seemed endless. In two or three months, out of the thousands of yuan Mr. Wu should have received, he only got 600 yuan. When the reporter arrived at the factory, many workers recounted similar experiences to Mr. Wu's.

Worker同期声 Reporter: What job do you do here?

Worker: I iron the appearance here.

Reporter: How long have you been working here?

Worker: More than two months.

Reporter: How much salary did they give you?

Worker: They gave me more than one hundred yuan.

Reporter: How long have you been in this factory?

Worker: I came on December 5th last year, I am the first batch.

Reporter: Then how much did they give you after working for so long?

Worker: Last year they gave me more than two hundred yuan, this year they just gave me a little over one hundred yuan.

Reporter: How much do they still owe you?

Worker: I don't know, I don't know how their salary table is calculated. We often work overtime until midnight, sometimes we are called at two o'clock in the morning, and now I only get more than one hundred yuan. We braved heavy snow to work overtime for them, but we only got that little amount of money.

In the factory, facing the workers' inquiries, the attitude of a person in charge of the factory made the reporter also feel indignant.

On-site同期声 Reporter: Where are the management personnel?

Worker: Over there.

Factory supervisor: You make a fuss, bring the reporter over, please pay your salaries well.

Reporter: You are the factory side, people work for you.

Factory supervisor: They invited you over, we can't issue the payment, how can we pay them!

What kind of attitude is this! However, Ms Meng Lihua, the boss of Haida Electronics Factory, did offer an explanation to the reporter, but his explanation made the workers very dissatisfied.

On-site同期声 (not normally filmed) Reporter: The factory has started issuing wages, right?

Worker: Issued half.

Haida Electronics Factory boss Ms Meng Lihua: Yes, it's still being issued today, started yesterday, probably five or six people left, cards haven't been credited yet, others have been credited.

Worker: Why issue only half the salary? Is it because you want to keep us here?

Ms Meng Lihua: As for how much to issue, that's our business, it's none of your concern, this is the operation of the enterprise, not something you say how much to issue.

Look, this is how the person in charge of a factory talks to workers! During the interview, the reporter learned that this company is actually an illegal business with no procedures at all.

Worker同期声 "The labor bureau people came to check, to see his credentials, there were none."

Such a company was actually brought in as an investment by TeYong Town. At the scene, the reporter saw that this kind of company even rented the factory space temporarily from the town industrial park, and up to now, the factory hasn't even hung up its own name. After this labor dispute occurred, although the town government intervened to mediate, in response to the reporter's interviews, there was mostly evasion and maneuvering. By the time the report was filed, the matter had not been completely resolved.

Tonight's commentary: Government image, public interest, which weighs more?

In this incident, according to reason, after learning about the situation, the Sheyang Labor Bureau should have promptly investigated the employer's wage arrears behavior. So, why wasn't it investigated? Why is the employer so audacious? The reporter contacted the Sheyang Labor Bureau several times, and the staff there clearly stated that this is an illegal business with no procedures at all. But when the reporter proposed the request for a cooperative interview, they dodged with the excuse that it would damage the local government's image. We must ask, since it is an illegal business, who brought it in originally, and who supervised it? Now, after the incident has happened, they still talk about taking care of the local government's image. Then, who will ensure the interests of the masses? We are puzzled, Sheyang Labor Bureau, as the responsible department and law enforcement unit, what position are you standing on, whose words are you speaking, fulfilling the responsibilities you should bear?
