First, Analysis of Computer Network Teaching in the 21st century, the development of high technology has become an important driving force for economic development, the development of China's IT industry has become an important indicator to measure a country's economic development potential, as an important part of the IT industry---computer network technology and its application, has become a development focus. The official launch of China's port and information highway projects requires a large number of talents that can adapt to network design, network construction, website management, and network technology applications. However, computer network teaching cannot meet the needs of national development and there are also many problems, mainly manifested in the following aspects.

(I) The relevant computer professional knowledge content behind the network teaching course is very extensive, it is the rapid development of computer technology and communication technology, further integrating them. With the continuous emergence of new technologies, the application field is constantly expanding and deepening, you need to continuously update the teaching content and practical plan of university computer network majors. However, the current situation is: Many new technologies and new applications cannot be reflected in time, which requires updating and supplementing the original teaching materials. Due to teachers and students putting forward their own reasons, the emphasis on practical teaching is not enough, practical teaching is too formal, students do not value practical teaching, although there are training courses at the opening ceremony, but less than needed teaching.

(II) Unreasonable teaching methods First, correcting, thereby making the teaching process unscientific and unreasonable, affecting the improvement of teaching quality. Second, the textbook knowledge is generally theoretical, light on theory, heavy on practice. Especially when involving network equipment, technology keeps changing, and we usually keep up with the changes in material usage. In addition, computer networks have more tightly linked theory and practice courses. If the textbook still uses the core, then the gap between advanced student training knowledge and relatively backward theory and practice will widen.

(III) Single examination form Currently, the computer network course continues to use the test mode of public category exams, focusing on basic theoretical research, ignoring practical and network examination abilities. As a result, students do not often practice during normal times, cramming before exams can still yield good results. This exam has seriously affected the normal state of network teaching, does not conform to the characteristics of the computer network course, and graduates cannot meet business needs.

Second, Suggestions for Teaching Reform

(I) Scientific Development of Training Programs for Professional Training Courses in Computer Networks should adhere to at least one large-scale industry research per year, mastering the direction of industry development, understanding industry dynamics, analyzing industry needs, and determining professional and industry starting points. Through a large amount of research data, organizing industry experts and backbone teachers to analyze the first-hand changes in industry professionals' demands, conducting in-depth analysis of larger job requirements, decomposing work requirements into knowledge, abilities, and qualities. Related knowledge, abilities, and qualities will be integrated into a treatment, several interrelated courses combined into a professional module.

(II) Integration of Basic Courses, Increased Reform Efforts in Basic Directions, Adhering to Necessary Sufficient Levels, such as: Integrating C language and data structures into program design circuits, foundations of computer organization, computer hardware, computer networks, and computer network technology, Windows servers and Linux servers integrated into network operating system application technology, network security integration consolidated. This serves to reduce unnecessary burdens on students, reduces time spent on practical teaching processing, designs different network environments, masters papers, improves their practical and innovative abilities. Strengthening basic English education. English is an important computer language course to learn, with the ever-changing knowledge of computer networks, daily internet reading, using advanced software, all inseparable from English. Now, especially in vocational schools, students generally do not pay much attention to English, and the inability in English severely affects normal network teaching and software information.

(c) Continuing university computer courses, reforming the teaching practice content behind general teaching practices, especially the content development of computer network courses on machine operation instructions. To improve current practices that have been unable to meet current trends, highlight practical teaching. There is a need to organize teaching and research subjects in computer networks, revising the practical teaching experiences of computer network courses by teachers. The design of experimental plans must closely track the actual development of networks, delete outdated content, and add some advanced technical practices. The practical teaching of computer networks mainly falls into three types: individual extracurricular experiments, training courses, and graduation designs. Individual experiments are mainly organized based on classroom teaching, aiming at personal technical training, examining knowledge and students' learning skills, deepening the understanding of classroom content. Through course learning, course training allows students to comprehensively apply knowledge and skills, achieving training in a certain aspect of work. As a simple local network architecture, design a small network based on domains. In practical internships, graduation design occurs after students have completed all professional courses.
