Beauty and skin care in all seasons

by nrmt123i on 2009-11-30 11:55:42

In winter, the temperature is low and the humidity is small. The skin will become tight and dry due to the reduction of secretion from sweat glands and sebaceous glands and the loss of more water. Therefore, it is more important to carry out beauty and skin care in winter. (1) Before going out, apply some oily moisturizing cream on the exposed skin, especially use lip balm on the lips. (2) Reduce the number of times you wash your face with hot water, once or twice a day is enough, and use less de-greasing cleaning products to wash your face. (3) Massage your facial skin frequently to promote blood circulation, and use a mask once or twice a week. (4) After washing your face, apply greasy skin care cosmetics. If cracks appear on the skin of your hands and feet, you can apply some anti-crack ointment.

In summer, the skin is prone to imbalance. Often, all neutral skins will turn into oily or dry skin. At this time, people should adjust their beauty and skin care products according to the changes of the season to give their skin the best protection. Generally, the ultraviolet rays in summer pose the greatest threat to the skin. It can cause keratinization and loss of elasticity, leading to premature aging. It can also cause chloasma and sunburn dermatitis. When going out, it's best to wear a hat and carry an umbrella, while applying some sunscreen on your face or exposed areas, which can effectively resist the damage of ultraviolet rays to the skin. In summer, flower water can often be used as a disinfectant and bactericide. It consists of 3% fragrance and 70% alcohol and appropriate amount of water. Sprinkling it on washing water, bathing water, bedroom, living room or body can not only remove odor and sweat, kill bacteria and relieve itching, but also refresh the brain. In addition, in summer, an appropriate amount of talcum powder or prickly heat powder can be applied to clean the skin, both of which have a refreshing, sweat-stopping, and itch-relieving effect.

Spring skincare: Spring is warm and windy. All human skin patterns start to stretch after being contracted, and the blood supply of peripheral capillaries increases. The secretion of sebaceous glands and sweat glands increases, making it a good season for skin care. Spring 1975 is also a season when bacteria and viruses easily multiply and spread, easily inducing infectious diseases and skin problems. Meanwhile, it's also the time when flowers bloom and pollen from the flowers can easily cause allergic reactions in some people with sensitive skin, resulting in dermatitis and eczema. These can bring adverse effects to the skin, easily causing allergic dermatitis, measles, and acne. Although young people's facial acne can occur in all seasons, there is a tendency to worsen in spring. Therefore, young people who are prone to acne should try to maintain a cheerful spirit and keep bowel movements smooth, quit smoking and avoid alcohol, pay attention to facial cleanliness, and use facial cleanser to wash their face to remove dirt and clear the clogged pores. Never squeeze pimples with unclean fingers to avoid infection.

In spring, it's important to keep the skin clean. Wash your face at least three times a day, choosing soap with less irritation and fewer fragrances, and thoroughly clean with warm water. After washing your face, you can use skincare products with antibacterial effects. Besides, regular bathing is very beneficial for skin maintenance. Thoroughly clean the joints such as knees and elbows when bathing, and massage your face and limbs after bathing to make the skin smooth. Women should completely wash off makeup before sleeping, then apply high-quality nourishing cream or massage with oily cream, and it's best to wipe off the remaining oil.

For autumn skin care, after the arrival of autumn, beauty-loving ladies should also "change seasons" for skin care. Due to the large temperature difference in autumn, the sudden cold and hot weather reduces the skin's resistance, making it susceptible to bacterial infections. Therefore, autumn skin care should focus on cleansing the skin first. Preferably choose a strong germ-killing, well-cleaning, weak acidic sunscreen facial cleanser. Secondly, consider the temperature difference between morning and evening. During the day, use summer light sunscreen skin care products, such as various sunscreens and moisturizers; at night, choose moisturizing and hydrating skin care products like night cream and nourishing cream. Because the autumn weather is dry, the oil secretion from the sebaceous gland decreases, and the evaporation of water accelerates, making the face feel tight. Therefore, in autumn, it's important to emphasize the hydration care of the skin's stratum corneum, except for not using alcohol-containing toners and moisturizing milk. After September, the autumn atmosphere becomes stronger, and people often ignore the existence of ultraviolet rays. In fact, the ultraviolet rays in early autumn are quite strong, and during this period, the skin is more susceptible to sun damage. Tanned skin usually does not fade easily because the metabolism of the skin slows down as the weather cools, so it's still necessary to apply sunscreen when going out.
