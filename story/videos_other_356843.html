XBOX360 "Medal of Honor" Single-player Campaign Trial Real Battle Experience

by jiayuan7v on 2010-05-29 11:26:41

According to the latest trial play information of Bangli's "Medal of Dishonor", the combat part of the entire game has not yet been finalized by EA Los Angeles Studio. With five months remaining until the release on October 12th, the development team has ample time to refine and optimize details and points. This edition of "Medal of Honor" will be a work of assured quality.

Unlike previous traditional FPS games, in this one, players will portray elite soldiers of the US military special forces deployed secretly in the Afghan mountains. Thus, there will be numerous encounters. For instance, during the trial play, the scenario took place in a sandy, barren mountainous region. As the protagonist and his team approached a dilapidated cottage, enemies suddenly appeared from windows and under walls, launching an attack. The entire gaming process requires adequate mental preparation to deal with unexpected situations at any time.

The attention to detail is already very satisfying. For example, the reloading animation is extremely delicate and realistic. The emphasis on voice acting leaves a deep impression. In the vast mountainous area, gunshots echo between the valleys, effectively enhancing the atmosphere. The movements of various participating characters also produce corresponding faint sounds, which are incredibly lifelike.

In practical combat, players can request air support in emergencies but must first mark the target. To ensure the success of the entire operation, the special force elites must coordinate with the air force for precise strikes against the enemy. During this process, it is essential to fully utilize the terrain of the Afghan mountains to find cover while continuously moving to avoid being targeted by the enemy.

The multiplayer combat section of "Medal of Honor" is developed by DICE, the team behind "Battlefield: Bad Company." This team has proven its excellent capabilities and unique creativity in "Bad Company 2."
