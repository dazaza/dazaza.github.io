Indian dumplings have fillings that are very different from those of China, with cheese, onions, and egg yolks as the main ingredients. Sometimes spinach and beef are also added. There is another type that uses chicken and cheese as the main ingredients, with butter, onions, lemon peel, and nutmeg as the main seasonings. To make the dumplings, the dough is pressed into a long strip, a spoonful of filling is placed on it, water is applied to the edges of the dough, then another strip of dough is placed on top and pressed down, and finally cut into pieces.
