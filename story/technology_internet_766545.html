Taobao continues to decentralize: sellers can independently promise the deposit

by anonymous on 2013-09-18 21:53:05

September 16 news: Taobao will return the commitment rights of the Consumer Protection Service Deposit for Taobao stores to the sellers. Sellers can commit to a deposit amount exceeding Taobao's limit based on their own business strength and consumer demand in their store.

For example, for infant formula/baby food/nutritional products/snacks which require a deposit of 1,000 yuan, sellers can voluntarily commit to 1,500 yuan, 1,888 yuan, 2,000 yuan or more.

It is understood that starting from September 13, Taobao will display the real-time deposit amount of the store on the seller's business card at the product detail page of the seller's store, so that consumers can have an accurate and clear understanding of the store's deposit when making shopping choices.

Taobao stated that voluntarily committing to a deposit is the seller's business choice right, it will not affect search rankings, but merely inform consumers of the store's voluntarily committed fund guarantee amount when choosing products.

In addition, if sellers want to adjust and reduce the deposit amount, they can also apply independently on this page. After application, the real-time deposit amount on the store's product detail page will be reduced. All transactions generated under the original deposit amount conditions will be automatically released to the seller's Alipay available balance after exceeding the protection scope of consumer protection (i.e., meeting the unfreezing conditions).

If the seller has not generated any transactions during the period of high deposit commitment, the voluntarily committed deposit applied for unfreezing will be immediately released to the seller's Alipay available balance account.
