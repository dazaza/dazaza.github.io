A proper diet can effectively assist in the treatment of epilepsy.

(l) Tianma Tangerine Peel Porridge. 10 grams each of Tianma (Gastrodia Rhizome) and tangerine peel, 100 grams of rice, and an appropriate amount of sugar. Cook Tianma (sliced), tangerine peel, and rice together to make porridge. After it's cooked, add sugar evenly, divide into two portions, and finish within one day.

(2) Alum Olive. 1.5 grams of alum, 12 olives. Can epilepsy be treated well? Wash the olives, use a knife to cut several longitudinal grooves on the olives, then sprinkle alum powder into the grooves. After the alum seeps into the olives, eat 1-2 per hour, chew slowly, swallow the juice, and spit out the residue.

(3) Bamboo Sap Porridge. 30 grams of bamboo sap, 2 grams of earthworm, 100 grams of rice. Dry the earthworms, bake them dry, and grind them into fine powder. Cook the rice to make porridge. After the porridge is cooked, add bamboo sap and earthworm powder, and eat in 1-2 servings.

(4) Ginseng Tangerine Peel Soup. 10 grams each of sun-dried ginseng and tangerine peel, and an appropriate amount of sugar. Boil the ginseng and tangerine peel first, strain out the dregs and take the juice, add sugar, and drink as tea.

(5) Wolfberry Stewed Sheep Brain. 30 grams of wolfberry, one sheep brain, an appropriate amount of oil and salt. Place the wolfberry and sheep brain in a double boiler, add an appropriate amount of water, stew away from direct fire, season with oil and salt.

(6) Huai Yam and Wolfberry Stewed Lean Pork. 30 grams of Huai yam, 15 grams of wolfberry, 100 grams of lean pork. Place all ingredients in a pot, add an appropriate amount of clean water, cook until done, season with oil and salt, and eat in portions.
