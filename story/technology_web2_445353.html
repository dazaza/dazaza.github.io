As a working dad, I cannot just be an onlooker in the journey of raising children. After getting married, I included having a child in my plan. That's how Xiang came into our lives according to our plan. From the moment my wife became pregnant, I made up my mind to be a qualified working dad and accompany our child together as parents on this journey.

During my wife's pregnancy, I accompanied her to lectures. Back then, I was definitely a rare breed — all mothers-to-be, with only me, the father-to-be, there. Things like prenatal education were done together with my wife. I loved touching her belly and talking to the baby inside. When the baby would move in response, the pride and sense of honor of becoming a father would naturally arise. In my free time, I loved reading parenting forums and websites. The games I used to love playing have long lost their appeal to me. When exchanging information with expectant mothers at work, my knowledge of parenting is no less than theirs. My friends gave me a nickname: Crazy Dad.

The other day, I saw online the "Dumex 1000 Days Immunity Campaign," whose theme is: the first 1000 days of growth are crucial for a baby's development and also the most important time for building immunity. A good foundation of resistance can affect a baby's health and development for life. After reading it, I deeply agreed. Reflecting on the path of raising Xiang, I believe we have been relatively scientific. Xiang is now over one year and eight months old, and his body is quite strong. I roughly adopted the following methods:

One, cultivating good sleep habits for the baby.

Sleep plays a very important role in a baby's development, especially for newborns who spend most of their time sleeping. Initially, Xiang had inverted sleep patterns. There wasn't much we could do at first, but when he was 14 days old, we started teaching him to differentiate between day and night. During the day, when he was awake, we played with him as much as possible, kept his room well-lit, and didn't intentionally reduce normal living noises, such as telephone rings, TV volume, or washing machine hums. If the baby was still sleeping during feeding times, we woke him up. At night, when the little one woke up to feed, we dimmed the lights, kept the surroundings quiet, and didn't talk to him much. Soon, the baby began to realize that it was nighttime for sleeping. As he grew older, we let him practice sleeping on his own. Now, Xiang goes to sleep obediently every night.

Two, promoting father-son affection through touch.

Touch — I should say, I am professionally skilled even as an amateur. Not only did I learn from childcare experts in real life, but I also studied online and on TV. Touch can enhance the skin barrier function of newborns. Systematic and scientific touch is beneficial for a baby's healthy development, increases resistance, promotes the absorption and utilization of nutrients, reduces crying, improves sleep, and enhances emotional exchange between parents and the baby. When parents personally give their baby a touch massage, they themselves need to relax completely and maintain a happy mood so that the baby doesn't feel tense. This way, the baby will feel your happiness and will be pleased as well.

Three, observing the baby's bowel movements to timely understand health status.

A baby's stool is the barometer of intestinal resistance. Stool care holds an important position in the daily care of newborns. By carefully observing the baby's stool conditions, you can understand the health of the baby's intestines. The health of a baby's intestines is a major factor affecting its nutrient absorption and an indispensable condition for strengthening resistance. One time, Xiang had gastrointestinal discomfort which I promptly discovered by observing his stool, allowing us to minimize the severity of the illness.

Four, proper nutrition pairing for good resistance.

Breast milk is the natural and ideal food that a mother gives to her baby. It not only provides balanced nutrition for the baby but also contains a large amount of immune substances that increase the baby's resistance, something that artificial feeding cannot compare to. Therefore, mothers must not miss the opportunity to breastfeed their babies. Both my wife and I support breastfeeding. She has always impressed me in this aspect; she, who used to care so much about her figure, has long put her figure aside and has been fully committed to being a dairy cow for Xiang. She continued breastfeeding until Xiang was one year old before choosing formula milk. Regarding my wife's diet and the baby's complementary foods, I take full charge. Every day, I change up delicious and nutritious meals for them. Nowadays, the trend of cooking husbands is popular, and our dad stays ahead of fashion trends.

Five, frequent sunbathing for a healthy body.

The human body's surface skin contains a substance called 7-dehydrocholesterol. This substance, upon receiving ultraviolet light from the sun, converts into vitamin D, hence earning the nickname "sunlight growth hormone." Warm sunlight makes the baby's body stronger, thus enhancing resistance! Of course, this professional knowledge is something I learned through continuous study.
