In July this year, 52-year-old Jian Guo Deng married Zi Qi Huang, a Guangxi girl who is 33 years younger than him. Due to the significant age difference between them, they have faced doubts from the outside world. Recently, some netizens have speculated based on the content of their Weibo posts that there may be changes in their relationship.

On October 10th, Zi Qi Huang uploaded a picture of her arm and posted a one-word Weibo: Pain. Before this post, she had also posted: "If someone wrongs me for the first time, I'll give them three steps back; if they wrong me again, I'll retaliate with a needle; if they keep wronging me, I'll cut all ties." From the photo, it can be seen that there is obvious redness and swelling on her wrist, leading netizens to suspect domestic violence. On October 14th, Jian Guo Deng posted several Weibos in a row, saying that his wife left and his hair turned white, asking who could understand his pain and the pressure he was under. Event Recap: Deng's Young Wife: The Dream Collapsed Instantly

Based on the screenshot information provided by netizens, reporters found a blogger named Zi Qi Huang. Although not verified with a V badge, from her profile picture and the information revealed on her Weibo, she has been identified by many netizens as Jian Guo Deng's newlywed wife, Zi Qi Huang.

On October 6th, Zi Qi Huang posted on Weibo: "It was my choice to be with you, no one can change this separation." On October 7th, she posted again: "Start over. The dream collapsed instantly. Why was I so foolish at the time? I still wanted to marry him out of deep love, but now I resent him. Whether we can let go or not, it’s over."

On October 13th, she posted a photo of the living room at home on Weibo and said: "I've returned to the most familiar place. Now, I gradually see light. Mom has always been by your side protecting you. Your facial expression tells me, your dreams are too painful, forget it! It's dawn."

Assistant's Response: No Possibility of Domestic Violence, But There Was an Argument

Yesterday afternoon, reporters repeatedly called Jian Guo Deng's phone, but it was always off. According to information from media colleagues in Xi'an, a journalist reached a secretary surnamed Zhang of Jian Guo Deng. The secretary denied the speculation about domestic violence against his wife, stating that it was impossible. However, the secretary admitted that the two had indeed argued recently. It might be because Jian Guo Deng has been busy lately, and somewhat neglected his wife. When the reporter asked if they could help contact Jian Guo Deng for a personal response, the assistant said: "There's no way to reach Jian Guo Deng today. In a few days, they can help arrange an interview."

In early August, online pictures showed that Jian Guo Deng originally planned to visit his in-laws in Laibin, Guangxi during the Qixi Festival, but due to public opinion pressure, his in-laws were unwilling to meet him. Zi Qi Huang had to return alone to Laibin to see her parents, staying for about three to four hours before returning to Nanning the same day. Moreover, Jian Guo Deng and his young wife Zi Qi Huang had conflicts, but fortunately, Jian Guo Deng used every trick in the book, such as treating her to fine cuisine, to win her heart back.

In early September, Jian Guo Deng appeared with his 19-year-old wife in Chengdu to promote a certain alcohol brand and announced the start of auditions for Sichuan actors for his new drama "Princess Out of the Mountain." While answering media questions, Jian Guo Deng made a slip of the tongue, which made Zi Qi Huang unhappy. She put down her hand, which was originally holding Jian Guo Deng's arm, and said, "When did I say that!" With so many people around, I am really angry. Five minutes later, when hearing Jian Guo Deng's response to the suspected performance of the wedding banquet, Zi Qi Huang coughed while throwing down the words "I'm angry" and then got up and left.

Celebrity Domestic Violence: Pain Becomes Habitual

Recently, the Li Yang domestic violence incident has caused a stir in society. In fact, domestic violence incidents in the entertainment circle are not a new thing.

● Wei Wei In 2010, Wei Wei revealed in a television interview that she had once experienced domestic violence. The scariest part was that her ex-husband once followed Wei Wei and tampered with her car, causing it to malfunction. Wei Wei realized that her life safety was threatened. Wei Wei also described her marriage life with her ex-husband using the movie "Sleeping with the Enemy."

● Kwong Man Wai In December 2009, Hong Kong model Kwong Man Wai held a press conference, claiming she was beaten by her boyfriend Choi Kin Bon. At that time, Choi Kin Bon was a TVB host at the peak of his career. Although the court only imposed a small fine without leaving a criminal record, Choi Kin Bon's career still fell into a long-term low.

● Choi Jin Sil In October 2008, South Korean famous actress Choi Jin Sil committed suicide in her bathroom at home; the direct cause of the suicide was false rumors on the Internet, but the root cause was long-term depression triggered by domestic violence. In 2000, Choi Jin Sil married baseball star Cho Sung-min, who used violence multiple times, even hospitalizing his wife. A month later, they officially divorced, and depression plagued Choi Jin Sil for a long time.
