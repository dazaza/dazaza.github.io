Mr. Ma is a local person from Wujian City, has been doing the urn business for 10 years, has an operating license issued by the local industrial and commercial department. He often sends urns to Wujian City Funeral Home at the request of the deceased's family, but always encounters unreasonable obstruction from the staff.

According to reports from China National Radio on May 18th (Jiangsu station reporters Miao Pengcheng, Liu Haobang), when relatives pass away, it is originally a sad thing. However, when family members requested to bring urns into the funeral home, they were refused; salespeople who delivered urns for the families were beaten, and the price of the urns was decided by the funeral home. Such incidents sound absurd, but they truly happen around us.

Several family members told reporters that the funeral home would negotiate with them, stating that if they didn't buy urns from the funeral home, they would bear the consequences themselves.
