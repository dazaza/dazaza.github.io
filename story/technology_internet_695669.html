Zhejiang Cixi sleeper coach crashes into a five-story building causing 2 deaths and 13 injuries (picture)

by l8cdds09338 on 2012-03-07 16:43:53

Accident scene: On the 329 National Highway in Cixi, Zhejiang, a sleeper bus broke through the railing while driving and the entire front of the vehicle crashed into a five-story building.

According to China News Service in Ningbo on June 16 (He Jiangyong, Wang Jianguo), at around 9:30 PM on June 15, a Guizhou-registered sleeper bus on the 329 National Highway in Cixi, Zhejiang, accidentally broke through the guardrail while driving. The entire front of the vehicle crashed into a five-story building. It is understood that this accident resulted in at least two deaths and thirteen injuries.

It is learned that at around 9:30 PM on June 15, the sleeper bus near Lianming Company on the 329 National Highway in Cixi, Zhejiang Province, accidentally broke through the guardrail while driving, www.qqzajinhua.com, and the entire front of the vehicle crashed into a five-story building. Many people were trapped. According to the person who reported the incident, oil was leaking from the car at the time of the accident, with the possibility of explosion at any moment.

After receiving the alarm, the Cixi Fire Brigade quickly dispatched the nearby Zhouxiang professional team to the scene to prevent the car from exploding due to oil leakage. At the same time, they dispatched two vehicles and twelve personnel from the Cixi Detachment for rescue operations.

During the journey of the fire brigade, the soldiers in the duty room quickly contacted the Zhouxiang professional team which had arrived first, learning that more than ten people were trapped inside the vehicle. The commander immediately reported to the brigade and detachment duty leaders and requested the dispatch of the Cixi West and Bridge detachments to the scene, as well as large tow trucks and cranes in case of emergency.

When the firefighters arrived at the scene, they found that the sleeper bus had broken through the guardrail about 20-30 meters along the national highway and collided with the five-story shift office of Lianming Company. Since the middle of the car's hood directly hit the corner of the shift office wall and penetrated the wall, it caused serious deformation of the entire car hood, firmly stuck inside the house. The crowd surrounded the scene tightly.

According to Ms. Xu, who was in the company at the time, she just happened to be back home eating watermelon indoors when suddenly the whole house shook. She immediately went outside to see what happened and saw the sleeper bus crash into the shift office. Her first thought was about the shift personnel. When she ran over, she found he was already bleeding heavily from his head, so she immediately sent him to the hospital for emergency treatment.

At this point, friends around also came out. They quickly broke the car windows and rescued people one by one from inside the car. They had already saved more than ten people until the professional fire team arrived, after which they stopped the rescue. Subsequently, the Zhouxiang professional team successively rescued three more people from inside the car. At this point, there were still five people trapped in the driver's seat area.

At around 10 PM, the Cixi, Cixi West, Bridge, and Yuyao Yaobei four fire stations successively arrived at the scene and formed a rescue command group led by Instructor Wang Hongxing and Captain Ding Mengyun of the Cixi Fire Brigade, www.qqzajinhua.com, organizing the rescue. The battle group was divided into three rescue teams from Cixi, Bridge, and Yuyao Yaobei, respectively implementing forced demolition rescues from both sides of the car hood. Meanwhile, Cixi West and Zhouxiang were responsible for on-site alerts and maintaining order at the scene.

The battle quickly unfolded. Due to the entire car hood being stuck in the corner of the wall, the demolition work of the demolition team could not be effectively carried out. At this point, the previously notified tow truck arrived at the scene. The on-site commander immediately ordered all demolition work to stop and let the tow truck forcibly pull the sleeper bus away from the corner of the wall. This significantly reduced the resistance to the rescue operation.

By around 11 PM, the five trapped individuals inside the car were successfully rescued. It is understood that this accident resulted in at least two deaths and thirteen hospitalized patients.

Currently, the cause of the accident is under investigation.
