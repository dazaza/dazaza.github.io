Reaching the age of marriage, many post-80s chose to get married in 2012. Marriage is one of the happiest things. From getting acquainted and knowing each other to stepping into the palace of marriage, the biggest problem faced by the young people who are about to enter the palace of happiness is how to decorate a set of their own wedding house together. Below is a brief introduction to the precautions for wedding house design and decoration. Save trouble and money. Quite a few couples-to-be have set their wedding date at the end of this year, so from the time point of view, it's now urgent to decorate. Because preparing for the wedding is a very complicated and trivial matter, it is difficult for the newlyweds to run the market, buy materials, and supervise the project themselves. Even if they take leave to decorate, they will inevitably lose their beauty and become exhausted after all that hustle and bustle. If the decoration work is handed over to the parents at home, it may tire the elderly, so designer Tian Xiaoming suggests that everyone should choose a reputable home decoration company to decorate the new house. As long as the responsibilities of both parties are clearly written in the contract, you can wait for the acceptance. Light decoration and heavy decoration The overall design of the bridal suite should reflect a festive atmosphere, which is best achieved through soft decorations such as furniture arrangements. Tian Xiaoming told reporters that compared with previous years, consumers preparing for the decoration of the bridal suite account for half of the home decoration market this year. They pursue modern simplicity in decoration, among which European country style is deeply favored. In the process of decorating the bridal suite, it is best to focus on light decoration and heavy decoration. Materials containing harmful substances such as wood and glue should be used as little as possible to reduce the toxic gas pollution of the new house. Generally speaking, soft decoration has great potential in the decoration of the bridal suite. For example, red gauze curtains, crystal bead curtains, lace, flowers, checkered fabrics, etc., are all excellent tools for dressing up the bridal suite and creating an atmosphere, and are more convenient for future flexible changes. Environmental protection and health wedding room The pollution of the bridal suite mainly manifests in air pollution, among which formaldehyde超标and benzene超标are the most harmful. Although the environmental performance of various building materials has improved compared to the past, due to the fixed wedding date, the newlyweds usually move in quickly, making it difficult for indoor polluted air to completely volatilize. Adding to that, the bride and groom are busy preparing for the wedding before the wedding, resting poorly, and having poor physical fitness, making them more susceptible to harmful substances. According to reports, the construction period for general family decoration is two months, during which the materials release pollutants. However, after the decoration is completed, ventilation must be carried out to eliminate harmful gases. It is best to ventilate frequently after the decoration. After comprehensive management and inspection of indoor air by testing institutions, only then can you move in. Show your individuality The bridal suite, as the house where the newlyweds get married, whether it is a new house or a second-hand house, decorating it into various styles of bridal suites has become an indispensable highlight of marriage. In the traditional sense, the colors of the bridal suite are mostly warm tones such as bright red. Bright red and purple are traditional celebratory colors in China. Even if the entire style does not have warm tones during the decoration process, corresponding bright red carpets or red lampshades will still be selected in the final accessories to create a strong newlywed atmosphere. Using cold tones such as blue and black is a big taboo in bridal suite decoration. This is what is traditionally known as the "bridal suite". But in this era of showing individuality, how to break the routine and highlight individuality has gradually been integrated into the decoration of the bridal suite. Nowadays, more and more young people like unique styles, and being unconventional has become a fashion in bridal suite decoration. Many newlyweds have changed their previous thinking patterns, and their requirements for function and style are often unexpected. There are previously encountered owners of bridal suites who believe that large chandeliers in traditional living rooms are quite rustic. Every home without exception decorates with large chandeliers, unable to create a romantic atmosphere throughout the house. Therefore, they bought several large floor lamps and placed them in different corners of the living room. Moreover, the originally banned black color became extremely popular, while the so-called festive warm colors were not used at all. Unconventional wedding room decoration, mostly for post-80s According to statistics, most of the owners of unconventional wedding room decoration are post-80s. These mostly single children born in the 1980s, who grew up in honey pots, are more emotional than rational in decoration, and pursuing individuality has become the main melody. Their requirements for kitchens are not very important, and their distinction of room functions is relatively vague. They don't even consider nursery rooms... But they will definitely choose the latest decorative materials and definitely spend time on lighting. Even when buying a small ornament, they will go to great lengths to find the one that best suits their personality. Regarding the decoration of the wedding room, perhaps everyone's understanding is different. But do you have enough courage to try the "unconventional" wedding room decoration? Industry insiders remind consumers that wedding room decoration varies from person to person. You cannot blindly follow trends or purely pursue individuality. Practical functions cannot be lost. What suits you is the best. The overall feeling of the room layout is the most important First, you should fully consider the size of the space, lighting, and the surrounding environment, and make an overall design of the room. There should be an overall feeling, the style of decoration and furniture should be unified, and the colors should be coordinated. To reflect the festive atmosphere of the new marriage, there can be some bright and vivid colors locally. Big red double happiness characters and wedding photos are indispensable and can be hung in prominent positions. But the newlywed room should also consider long-term use in the future. In general, the tone should be fresh and elegant, making people feel comfortable and relaxed. If you already have furniture, and only need to purchase a small amount of furniture, you should also follow the principle of harmony. Either modify the existing furniture, re-color it, and unify it with the furniture you want to buy; or when purchasing new furniture, try to match it with the existing furniture. Doing so well follows the principle of "diversity in unity" in the form of beauty. Buy suitable furniture that's the best The newlywed's room must be decorated harmoniously and warmly, with a unified style, to provide a good environment for newlywed life. Furniture, in addition to having a unified style, must also be adapted to the conditions of the house itself to achieve the best effect. Therefore, when purchasing or making new house furniture, it is necessary to fully measure and analyze the housing conditions to make choices. There are many factors to consider, including the size of the room, lighting, and the living habits of the owner. Cleverly matching colors makes the bridal chamber lively and cheerful The bedroom, also known as the bridal chamber, is the center of the newlywed family and a place highly valued by newlywed couples. Its decoration should reflect a festive, warm, and romantic atmosphere. Red is the main color of traditional Chinese weddings, so Chinese people also like to use celebratory red to decorate the new house. Red bedding, red curtains, etc., on the day of the wedding, rely on these decorations to create a festive atmosphere. Curtains can be made of two layers, thin outside and thick inside, which block the line of sight, maintain the room temperature, and do not affect daytime rest. The selection of patterns and colors of curtains, bed covers, sofa covers, and other textiles should be coordinated. Add appropriate decorative items with individual characteristics to make the room full of festive and romantic atmosphere, reflecting the pursuit of a better life by the owner. The decoration of the newlywed's home should be considered in the long term. It should be easy to replace, easy to clean, and consider the living environment of future children, leaving enough activity space for the children. In addition, avoid clutter inside. Many newlyweds buy a lot of clothes and daily necessities when they get married, as well as gifts from relatives and friends. Various decorations, tea sets, wine glasses, etc., should be placed in coordination with their own home environment. Do not display everything at once, making the already crowded room seem even narrower. Not only will it fail to play a decorative role, but it will also destroy the indoor atmosphere.
