Currently, with a permanent population of 290,000 people in the five districts and 29 cities of Fuzhou, there are a total of 5,245 taxis, approximately one taxi for every 180,000 people, which is much lower than other provincial capital cities with ratios of 27-35. In the recently released "2011 Fuzhou Public Transport Satisfaction Evaluation Report", the satisfaction rate for Fuzhou taxis was only 62 points, with 65.6% of respondents reflecting that it was hard to get a taxi, especially during rainy days and rush hours. The areas with the lowest satisfaction were: “proactively providing invoices,” “ease of hailing a taxi,” “whether taxis follow traffic rules,” and “condition of vehicles,” with satisfaction rates at 28.6%, 34.4%, 41%, and 50.5%, respectively.
