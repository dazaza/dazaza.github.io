How to make Google pay ad fees starting at $1

by jssz on 2007-01-03 12:43:08

This idea suddenly came to me last night when I couldn't fall asleep. Since it just came out, it might be immature and naive. At the very beginning, putting ads on my blog is more about trying and experiencing, of course, I like having dollars coming in, hehe.

After nearly a year, the income in the account is pitifully little, only a few dollars, far from the payment amount of 50 dollars, which seems unreachable. Last night, I idly wondered why Google's payment threshold is so high? Is it afraid of trouble? Or does it take this considerable income (which should be our promotion of its ads) into its own pocket because it doesn't reach the payment amount? I secretly think that Google, such a big company, should not do things that can't see the light of day. It might be afraid of trouble! But we are not afraid of trouble, hehe.

Is there any way to get paid starting from one dollar? Later, I thought of a method, which is to be a "wholesaler" for the ads provided by Google. The ad code obtained from me can be paid starting from one dollar. After thinking for a long time, I think it is very feasible and operable. For both Google and the majority of blogs and small personal websites, it is an extremely beneficial thing. For Google, it can attract those blogs and personal websites that think its payment threshold is too high and do not participate in its ad promotion. For the majority of friends who use Google ads, they can get real dollars. Why not!!
