A country was struck by famine.

by vghfeifei on 2011-09-12 12:44:00

Once upon a time, there was a famine in a country. The king did not want to see his people starve, so he opened the granaries and treasuries, distributing the food and wealth of the palace to the masses. But the famine continued for two years, and the king's food and wealth were gradually exhausted. Even the king himself became poor, and there was no grain left even for the king’s own rations. At this point, the king called his only son to ask what they should do now.

The prince thought for a moment and said, "Father, if the ruler of a country cannot even guarantee the livelihood of his own people, then he has no right to rule them. Therefore, we should leave here and go to another country to live as ordinary people."

The king and queen thought their son's words made sense, so the three of them left their country.

They walked for several months, sometimes going days without a full meal. Hunger and exhaustion left them in a sorry state. Finally, they arrived in another country. The prince said to his father, "I will go to meet the king of this country. You two dress up as my servants and come with me. Once at the palace, I will sell you two to this king in exchange for a good horse and some decent clothes. Then I will go to another country to test my luck. I will soon find a good job."

The parents agreed with their son's plan. The three of them went to the palace. The prince bowed to the king of that country and said, "Your Majesty, these two are my servants. My current situation is difficult, and I would like to sell them to you. Would you be interested?"

The king carefully examined the two servants and said, "Alright, I will certainly buy them. What would you like in exchange?"

"I don't want much, just a good horse and some decent clothes. These things I am temporarily borrowing from you, and when I return them to you, I hope you can also return these two servants to me." The king also agreed to return the servants later. He then ordered his soldiers to bring a good horse and found some new clothes, all of which were given to the unfortunate prince. The prince took off his tattered clothes, put on the new ones, mounted the horse, and quickly rode away.

One day, the prince arrived in another country. As he entered the capital of this country, something strange happened: everyone burst into tears at the sight of him. He felt utterly confused and couldn’t help but feel embarrassed. Had some great tragedy occurred here?

He approached an old man and asked, "Sir, do you have any difficulties? Why is everyone crying?"

The old man looked at the prince, and tears immediately filled his eyes. He then said, "Child, listen to me, you must turn back right away!"

"Why?" the prince asked in confusion.
