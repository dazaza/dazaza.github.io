Man slashes girlfriend several times before committing suicide by cutting his own throat; the woman had proposed breaking up beforehand

by zxyasdn20 on 2011-07-04 14:00:41

The doctor is treating the injured woman, Huang Li (a pseudonym).

Time: 12 PM yesterday

Location: The Dongren crossing in Xinglin

Around 12 PM yesterday, a bloody incident occurred at the Dongren crossing in Xinglin: A man repeatedly slashed a woman with a fruit knife and then immediately cut his own neck. According to the woman's family, the two were lovers. Before the incident happened, the woman proposed breaking up with the man.

A "blood-covered person" rushed out of the grass

The bloody incident happened in the green lawn at the intersection of Dongren Road and Xingqian Road.

The reporter from the Straits Herald arrived at the scene and saw that the blood stains on the grass were shocking, and a white scarf on the ground was covered in blood. The injured people had already been sent to the hospital.

Xiao Zhang, who was selling car seat cushions in a minivan by the road, witnessed the occurrence. He recalled:

"At around 11:30 AM, I noticed a motorcycle parked outside the grass, but I didn't pay much attention. After about 20 minutes, I suddenly heard someone shouting 'Help'. I quickly looked out of my van, but I didn't see anything unusual. I was wondering if it was an auditory hallucination when suddenly, I saw a woman covered in blood rushing out of the bushes."

"I was shocked at the time. She ran to the side of the road and knelt down to ask for help when she saw a passing car. However, the car stopped for a few seconds and then drove away. Subsequently, the woman ran towards me. I hurriedly searched for my phone to call the police, but I was too nervous and couldn't remember where I had placed my phone. At this moment, a motorcycle taxi driver on the roadside dialed 120."

At this point, Xiao Zhang noticed there were two men in the grass. "One man was holding another man's neck, both covered in blood." Not long after, the ambulance arrived and took the three people to the hospital.

Xiao Zhang told the reporter from the Straits Herald that he heard from others that one of the men and the woman had planned to get married, but they didn't end up doing so. As a result, the man attacked the woman with a knife and then attempted suicide.

The woman proposed breaking up before the incident

The reporter from the Straits Herald arrived at the emergency room of the Xinglin Hospital, where doctors were treating the injured woman, Huang Li (a pseudonym).

Outside the emergency room, a man named Huang Zhong (a pseudonym) said that he was the sister's brother, and the perpetrator Hu Wei (a pseudonym) was his sister's boyfriend.

Why did Hu Wei attack his girlfriend? Huang Zhong explained the reason to the reporter from the Straits Herald.

All three are from Jingdezhen, Jiangxi Province, and have known each other for many years. Huang Li is 26 years old, and Hu Wei is 29 years old. Since Huang Zhong works in Xinglin, three years ago, Huang Li came to Xinglin to work and established a boyfriend-girlfriend relationship with Hu Wei. Later, Hu Wei also worked at the machinery factory where Huang Zhong was employed.

Huang Zhong revealed that due to disharmony in their relationship, Huang Li proposed breaking up with Hu Wei after the Spring Festival this year. On the 6th day of the month, Huang Li returned to Xinglin early. One reason was that she found a new job on the island and planned to resign from her previous factory; the other reason was that she wanted to return home, pack her things, and break up with Hu Wei.

"The next day, Hu Wei also returned to Xinglin. He knew my sister was leaving and went to her factory. My sister refused to see him, so he waited outside the factory gate. A few days ago, he caught up with my sister and tried to strangle her. Luckily, someone noticed and he left. After knowing this, I called Hu Wei and advised them to part ways peacefully. However, he threatened me, saying that if he wouldn't be happy, neither would I or my sister. We had a heated argument over the phone."

Yesterday morning, Huang Zhong just returned to Xinglin and called both Huang Li and Hu Wei out, wanting to counsel them. Around noon, after they finished eating together, they were walking back when Hu Wei suggested having a private talk with Huang Li while Huang Zhong waited outside the grass.

"About 20 minutes later, I suddenly heard my sister scream. Looking in that direction, I was stunned by what I saw: My sister was covering her head, which was all bloody, and Hu Wei held a blood-stained fruit knife! I quickly ran over, and suddenly Hu Wei slashed his own neck. I rushed to hold his neck. At that moment, Hu Wei was still swinging the knife around. In the chaos, my pants were torn, and I was stabbed in the chest. Fortunately, the wallet in my clothes blocked the blade," Huang Zhong recalled.

The reporter from the Straits Herald saw at the hospital that Huang Li had a noticeable cut on her face, from her forehead to her nose, requiring more than ten stitches. According to the doctor, she also had wounds on the back of her head. Meanwhile, Hu Wei had knife wounds on his neck and was undergoing surgery in the operating room. Both were temporarily out of danger.

Currently, the police have intervened to investigate the matter.
