The 1+1 repair technique involves combining Baihemei capsules, made from pure Chinese herbal ingredients, with a specialized dietary and lifestyle guidance plan. The entire process lasts about 72 days and is divided into three stages: the repair stage (24-28 days), the maintenance stage (7-11 days), and the transition stage (3-7 days).

**Repair Stage:** Take Baihemei Yihong capsules twice daily, once in the morning and once in the evening. Starting from the first day of the repair stage, discontinue taking antidiabetic medications and stop insulin injections.

**Maintenance Stage:** This stage focuses on maintaining the health of the pancreas after repair. Simply follow the prescribed dietary and lifestyle plan without continuing to take Yihong capsules.

**Transition Stage:** This is the phase where one transitions from the prescribed diet back to a normal diet. After this stage, patients can drink alcohol and eat fruits like normal individuals.

Extensive experiments have shown that some foods are beneficial for pancreatic repair while others are harmful. Based on the effects of different foods on pancreatic repair, the 1+1 repair technique categorizes food into three types: edible foods, foods that can be consumed in small amounts, and foods that should not be consumed at all.

Edible foods are beneficial for pancreatic repair and can be eaten in larger quantities. It's important not to feel hungry, and there are dozens of meal plans available for breakfast, lunch, and dinner. The 1+1 repair technique is not a starvation therapy. Patients using this technique should maintain sufficient food intake and nutrition, eating as much as possible.

Foods that can be consumed in small amounts may have some impact on pancreatic repair and should be minimized.

The 1+1 repair technique is an integrated system. The main function of Yihong capsules is to repair diseased pancreatic cells, while the specialized dietary plan creates conditions for the capsule to repair the pancreas. These two components cannot be separated; using only Yihong capsules or only following the dietary plan will not achieve the expected results.

The ultimate effectiveness of the 1+1 repair technique depends on two aspects: First, the condition of the patient's pancreas before adjustment, specifically how many beta cells are still alive. The more surviving beta cells, the lower the blood sugar levels after adjustment, leading to better outcomes. Second, whether the diseased beta cells are truly repaired during the adjustment process, which is very critical. If the diseased cells cannot be repaired or are damaged again shortly after repair, it will not truly achieve a high-quality drug-free life.
