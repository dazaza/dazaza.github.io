Left Beauty Right Silver Jewelry Gains Popularity at the 2010 Expo

(Before Joining)

I am from Jixi City, Heilongjiang Province. Since I am about to resign from my current job, I have been looking for a suitable project and opportunity to start a business. Everyone says that investment carries risks, and one should always be prepared to accept losses. My friends and family support me and remind me to be cautious. Personally, I like women's intimate jewelry (such as silver jewelry, small accessories, etc.), and I have always wanted to open a small specialty store to manage and operate. By chance, I learned that there was an expo for product projects in Guangzhou, so I decided to conduct an on-site investigation. This trip to Guangzhou was only hoping that I could find a project product that suits my financial capacity, has low risk, and is still in a blank market locally. Thus, I boarded the train to Guangzhou and began my investigation.

At the 2010 Guangzhou Expo Exhibition, I saw more designs that differ from the traditional jewelry's regular shapes. The styles and shapes are very rich, and every season’s fashionable and avant-garde elements are continuously integrated. The product categories include necklaces, bracelets, rings, brooches, earrings, and the variety of pendants alone can be described as dazzling. Besides ordinary silver jewelry, Thai silver is also beginning to be liked by avant-garde people due to its mysterious regional color. Whether it's non-mainstream, retro, kawaii, or elegant style clothing, modern fashion silver jewelry can always find matching varieties. As a Chinese characteristic product, silver jewelry always causes a wave of enthusiasm whenever it appears. Participating in the 2010 Expo, Guangzhou's silver jewelry once again sparked a craze.

The distinctive brands are widely loved by female friends, such as Left Beauty Right Silver Jewelry, which has become a fashion silver jewelry brand pursued by consumer groups across the country. They pursue unique styles and a strong artistic atmosphere, with their inspiration coming from the combination of Chinese traditional culture and the latest fashion trends as well as the personal feelings of designers. Every product of Left Beauty Right Silver Jewelry can be considered a work of art, all finely crafted, implementing self-research and production, which highlights the transcendent quality of this brand even more. Silver jewelry embedded with colored gemstones is more suitable for young girls to wear, while mature women can choose silver jewelry embedded with pure-colored gemstones. Currently, Guangzhou Left Beauty Right is the only brand positioned for colorful silver jewelry. Guangzhou Left Beauty Right Colorful Silver Jewelry includes 990, 925 silver plated with platinum, inlaid with jade, gemstones, crystals, pearls, etc., with thousands of styles and colorful colors, deeply loved by female friends. Policies such as zero-cost joining, silver jewelry agency joining, zero-inventory operation, flexible exchange of goods, etc., are managed uniformly. All aspects are relatively in line with the local market and my own investment situation, and the discussions with the company were pleasant and satisfactory.

Finally, I decided to join the Left Beauty Right brand, completing the first step of my initial entrepreneurship!
