Program Overview

"If You Are The One" is a large-scale dating show broadcast by Jiangsu Satellite TV, catering to the modern lifestyle. It provides a public platform for single men and women to meet and interact. The high-quality production and innovative dating model have attracted significant attention from audiences and netizens alike. The interactive format of the new program completely breaks away from traditional dating methods, fully reflecting the matrimonial values of contemporary men and women. In the show, 24 single female participants decide the fate of the male contestant by lighting or extinguishing their lights. Through stages such as "First Impressions of Love," "Further Judgment of Love," "Final Selection of Love," and "Male's Right," the program determines whether the male and female contestants successfully match.
