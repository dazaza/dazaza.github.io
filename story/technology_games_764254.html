"World of Warcraft" Ink Animation Premiere

by geekzhang on 2013-08-05 12:12:20

As we all know, in recent years, Blizzard's game works, from "Warcraft III", "World of Warcraft" to "StarCraft II" and then "Diablo III", have attracted a lot of players' favor with their cinematic high-quality CG animations. However, the animation short film released this time is not like that. Instead, it adopts an ink-wash style to match the Chinese elements of "Mists of Pandaria", and its presentation method is similar to the domestic animated films seen by many post-85s players in their childhood.

It is understood that the ink-wash short film played this time is named "Sha of Doubt", which is the prologue of the subsequent six short films. The series of animations take Emperor Shaohao in the plot of "Mists of Pandaria" as the main character. Through the introduction of Zhou Zhuo, the so-called "famous storyteller of Pandaria", he leads everyone to understand the story of Shaohao, the last emperor of Pandaria.

At the same time, Alex Chiu, President of Blizzard Greater China, revealed that the official will launch chapters 1-6 in the next one or two weeks. Each chapter will focus on describing a "Sha". After all the short films are broadcast, players should be prepared to challenge various new bosses in the game, including the seventh "Sha" - the Pride Sha.
