"World of Warcraft: The Stolen Winter Veil Gifts"

by gensen on 2008-12-17 15:00:38

Blizzard has always been renowned for its outstanding parodying skills. Even the main character from the movie "How the Grinch Stole Christmas" has been transported into the world of Azeroth, becoming a key figure in today's Winter Veil quest "Frostbite" (though he is a legendary snow beast, he can also be called a character). Although players generally don't care about the origins of these NPC names and quests, understanding Blizzard's little parodies while completing the quests can indeed uncover a lot of fun. This article begins with the key character "Grinch" in the Winter Veil quest "Frostbite" and offers everyone a detailed guide to the Winter Veil series of quests —— "Frostbite," helping everyone complete it quickly and prepare for Blizzard's next Winter Veil mailing recipe.

The Grinch (Grinch) is a character from Dr. Seuss's work "How the Grinch Stole Christmas." He lives in a cave on Mount Crumpit near the Arctic Circle town of Whoville. He is a snowman (or perhaps a Yeti?) covered in long fur. His personality is solitary, living only with his dog Max (interested players may see when Blizzard will add this name to the game), rarely leaving the cave. As Christmas approaches, the festive atmosphere in the town makes Grinch feel even lonelier and more resentful. So he pretends to be Santa Claus and steals all the presents from the townspeople (in the game, Grinch steals the gifts from Smokywood Pastures), thinking that this will ruin the festive mood in the town. However, he discovers that even without presents, the town remains joyful at Christmas. At this point, Grinch finally understands that happiness isn't just based on material things...

Blizzard not only "borrows" the character Grinch into the game, but the quest plot is also very similar. First, the time is set during Winter Veil, a special day very close to Christmas. Then, Grinch is "arranged" to steal the gifts prepared by Smokywood Pastures for the players. Finally, Grinch's appearance in the game is strikingly similar to the one in the movie, except one has white fur and the other green fur (please see the picture below)... Let's stop chatting and start the quest!

First, receive the initial quest "Stolen Winter Veil Gifts" from Gnome Ur'mot near Grandfather Winter in Ironforge (Khadgar's Code). The quest requires you to find the missing Smokywood Pastures spy in the Alterac Mountains.

Actually, this spy in the Alterac Mountains (location shown in the picture below) has already been frozen into a snowman, looking particularly funny, shivering while speaking (please see the task description text in the picture). After suppressing your laughter and reading the task description, you learn that the lost gifts from Smokywood Pastures were stolen by Grinch. You need to defeat Grinch to retrieve the lost gifts. Accept the quest "Frostbite."

Quest "Frostbite"

Quest Objective: Retrieve the lost gifts from Smokywood Pastures

Key Points: Defeat the 36-level elite snow beast Grinch

Task Guidance: Grinch's respawn location is marked in the map. When he respawns, he will shout (in red letters): "Ah!~~~". If you don't see Grinch at your current respawn point after seeing this sentence, immediately go to the other respawn points to search. Grinch is a 36-level elite, which is easy for max-level players. However, if there are other players nearby who also need to complete this task, it is recommended to form a full team before killing him. This way, each team member can obtain the quest item, and it saves time waiting for Grinch to respawn.
