Guide to Buying Discounted Air Tickets

According to national statistics, the number of passengers during the 2006 Spring Festival travel rush exceeded 2 billion, with over 17 million people traveling by civil aviation; it is estimated that the number of passengers during the 2007 Spring Festival travel rush will reach 2.17 billion, setting a new historical record, with 19.3 million people traveling by civil aviation, an increase of 9.4% compared to the same period last year.

In fact, there's no need to be intimidated by these numbers. Although the ticket prices listed by major airlines may seem high and even suggest a situation where tickets are hard to come by, there are indeed ways to purchase discounted air tickets. Fortunately, some people are well-versed in this art. As the saying goes, "There is skill in numbers, and numbers in skill. The balance of yin and yang lies within, and opportunity resides therein. Opportunity cannot be contrived, for contrivance leads to failure."

Recently, our reporters have interviewed multiple experts from eLong Travel Network, "Qunar" travel search engine, and Tianyiyou Business Travel Network on the topic of how to buy discounted air tickets. These experts have provided valuable tips that we believe will give you confidence in selecting affordable tickets before the holiday season.

**First Move: Be Proactive Early**

Anyone who frequently travels by plane knows that buying tickets in advance is much cheaper than purchasing them at the airport on the day of departure; the earlier you book, the more discounts you can enjoy. Experts suggest that booking tickets at least 15 days in advance usually offers better deals. During peak seasons like the Spring Festival Golden Week, extra attention is required.

According to several travel websites, tickets for the Spring Festival period are already available for reservation. Depending on the booking time, the discount varies, and some flights offer as low as 30% off.

Regrettably, many people are unaware of this. According to statistics from China Air Transport Information Network, more than 50% of civil aviation passengers book their tickets within three days of travel, while less than 20% book tickets more than 30 days in advance. The main reason passengers don't book far in advance is due to accumulated consumer habits over the years, and some are unable to confirm their itinerary a month or more in advance. Many still do not fully understand the discount benefits of booking tickets early.

**Second Move: Seize Opportunities Midweek**

Weekends generally see a higher proportion of travelers. If your schedule allows, try to book midweek flights to avoid the peak passenger flow times such as weekends. Since this year’s Spring Festival falls on February 18th, the week leading up to it will be the most紧张 period for ticket availability. If your travel dates are flexible, consider booking midweek tickets either before or after this period.

Professional ticket booking sites like eLong Travel Network and "Qunar" allow you to check and compare price distributions anytime. Tianyiyou Business Travel Network started publishing an "Air Ticket Pre-purchase Index" in 2006, which forecasts ticket price trends for the coming week or half-month, encouraging more people to book tickets early and reduce travel costs.

**Third Move: Grab Deals When They Appear**

Currently, China's civil aviation industry is relatively competitive, especially with the emergence of private airlines like Spring Airlines and Okay Airways, which has loosened the monopoly previously held by a few established airlines and broken down price barriers. With China now fully integrated into the WTO, people have good reason to expect even lower ticket prices. Many airlines implement various discount measures. While some discounts may only apply to members or specific groups, regular consumers can also take advantage of these opportunities if they choose wisely.

For example, Spring Airlines recently offered ultra-low promotional fares, and China Southern Airlines released tickets at 1.5 times the base fare, both offering significant discounts. Consumers can select targeted promotional flights from various airlines and enjoy considerable discounts. Generally, keeping track of different airline promotions can be cumbersome. Dai Zheng, Vice President of "Qunar," explained that their search engine links, integrates, and publishes product information from over 100 airlines and large ticket providers in real-time, saving consumers time and effort and providing clear and orderly options.

**Fourth Move: Leverage Group Power**

Unity is strength. In the process of buying discounted tickets, group advantages should be leveraged. Normally, getting a 40% off individual ticket price is already quite cheap, but during the peak demand periods like Spring Festival, it's nearly impossible to secure such low discounts on your own. However, if you're buying as a group, the possibility increases significantly.

During the Spring Festival, travel needs mainly consist of returning home and tourism. People with the same destination can contact each other in advance, gather a group of around 10 people to qualify for group tickets. After receiving quotes from ticket providers, use the group purchase as a bargaining chip when making phone reservations, often revealing additional room for negotiation. The amount of negotiation space depends highly on how early the tickets are booked, so it's recommended to combine this strategy with early booking.

**Expert Tips**

**Price Reflects Value**

Before purchasing discounted tickets, first clarify the airline's restrictive conditions; airlines are not responsible for changing your tickets.

For differently priced discount tickets, international airlines provide varying levels of service to passengers. For instance, within the same class, full-price or slightly discounted tickets offer better seat positions, while heavily discounted tickets offer poorer seat positions. Service procedures may also differ under basic requirements.

Currently in China, apart from differences in purchasing restrictions between discounted and full-price tickets, there is no evident difference in the services provided by airlines.

**Online Booking Search Engines**

Travel search engines provide a clear comparison of prices, services, or times offered by major airlines and ticket agents, helping you find the best option suited to your needs. The biggest benefit is being able to get a clear ranking of prices from lowest to highest across over 100 airlines and ticket agents, saving time and effort by completing everything on one website interface without switching between multiple sites. In fact, considering factors like time, effort, and price comprehensively, using a search engine provides the best value for money.

**Early Booking Offers More Discounts**

Starting two weeks before the Spring Festival, tickets on all routes begin to tighten. The closer it gets to the Spring Festival, the fewer discounts are available, and the fewer cabin choices remain.

For those unsure about their vacation dates, estimate the most likely travel date and choose flights that allow same-day refunds or rebooking, allowing you to decide your travel date flexibly. If you can't travel as planned due to company holidays, there will be no loss from canceling the ticket. Currently, eLong offers a rich selection of tickets for the Spring Festival period, with plenty of price options and relatively sufficient cabin availability.
