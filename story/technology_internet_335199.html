Characteristics and advantages of online soft articles (news)

by juncheng on 2010-03-26 11:27:48

Characteristics of Online Soft Articles

1. News (soft article) publication has a strong advertising effect, combining the features of soft article advertising and news. It possesses the effects of an advertisement while maintaining the authority, truthfulness, and objectivity of news, making it more credible and easier for consumers to accept. In an era where advertisements are starting to annoy people, news undoubtedly plays a powerful role in helping businesses quickly increase consumption.

2. News (soft article) publication can guide trends. According to recent survey data, there are 253 million internet users in China, with 206 million browsing news regularly. Eighty percent of people have developed the habit of reading online news. News can subtly influence the consumption habits of internet users.

3. News (soft article) publication can help establish a corporate image, making the company trusted by consumers. In the 21st century, competition among similar products is the biggest survival challenge for companies. Many companies offer identical products and services, but consumers can only remember one. The impression this company leaves on consumers is very important. This impression needs to be established through news soft articles because the uniqueness of news can create a unique public image for the company, establishing a trustworthy brand and a distinct service image that consumers will remember, which is more effective than any amount of advertising.

4. News (soft article) publication can transmit word-of-mouth effects. Through news dissemination, internet users may unconsciously remember certain information and casually mention it to friends, creating an effect much stronger than traditional advertisements.

5. News (soft article) publication can enhance the effectiveness of advertising. In the past, people often said that they had spent a lot on advertising but saw little effect, while some companies achieved good results without spending much on advertising. This is because when there are too many similar advertisements, consumers tend to recognize a brand they trust. The more advertisements there are, the more purchases reinforce the recognition of a particular brand.

6. News (soft article) publication can create group effects, making it easier to open up markets. If internet users see related reports about a company in one place and then see them in other places, including information about services, products, corporate culture, market trends, and fashion, the cumulative effect makes it hard for consumers not to believe. Unconsciously, a deep impression is left in the minds of consumers, and next time they shop, they are likely to choose these two products.

Modification/Collection Not convenient for publishers to modify and collect in time. Simple publication, can be modified, queried, and stored long-term.

Five Advantages of News Marketing

First, news has the characteristic of timely transmission. When a company experiences a major event with external promotional value, the information must be disseminated in the first instance; otherwise, it loses its news value. At this moment, only initiating news dissemination can achieve this goal. Indeed, TCL's acquisition of Thomson's TV business and Lenovo's acquisition of IBM's PC business were both released through news.

Second, news has the function of complete interpretation. The inherent attributes of advertisements determine that they cannot adopt reasoning or narrative methods of expression. However, news can interpret corporate culture, brand essence, product mechanisms, benefit commitments from multiple angles and layers, disseminate industry information, lead consumer trends, and guide purchasing decisions.

Third, news dissemination has the function of crisis public relations. Why do many companies think of initiating news dissemination immediately after a crisis event? Because news dissemination has the function of crisis public relations, which advertisements lack. In late November 2004, Skyworth experienced the "Huang Hongsheng Crisis," and the next day, they convened media for crisis public relations. It’s hard to imagine what Skyworth would be like now if they hadn’t conducted effective media public relations at the time.

Fourth, news dissemination has the advantage of high cost-effectiveness. Why does Galanz rarely invest in advertisements but prefers to hype news? Because compared to advertisements, news dissemination has the advantage of high cost-effectiveness. Generally speaking, the cost of enterprise news dissemination on the same page is only one-fifth of an advertisement, or even lower, which is very cost-effective for companies with tight advertising budgets.

Fifth, good news has the characteristic of secondary dissemination. Secondary dissemination refers to a medium publishing something first, and other media re-posting it. Such cases are common. However, we absolutely never see a situation where an advertisement is reprinted by other media just because it was well-designed—no media would do such a foolish thing.

It can be said that the five advantages of news dissemination determine its irreplaceable position in market promotion. Today, more and more Chinese enterprises are paying attention to the role of news dissemination in market promotion and brand building. Many companies have established functional departments responsible for market promotion, media publicity, and corporate image construction. For example, TCL established a brand management center at the group level, and each of the seven business units also set up their own public relations and media departments. The Haier Group's Corporate Culture Management Center also performs similar functions. In the era of integrated communication, we have moved from single-advertising campaigns to a combination of advertising and public relations strategies. How to make good use of news marketing is an urgent issue that enterprises need to consider today.
