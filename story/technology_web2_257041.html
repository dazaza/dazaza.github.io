The 45th Baeksang Arts Awards

by b117112744 on 2009-11-07 12:24:36

The 5th Baeksang Arts Awards, held on the evening of May 2nd, proceeded smoothly with all major awards finding their rightful owners. In the television category, the SBS family comedy "Mom Has Horns" (also known as "Mom Is Overheated") won the Best Drama award. Kim Myung-min secured the Best Actor award for his outstanding performance in "Beethoven Virus," while Moon Geun-young won the Best Actress award for her role in "Garden of the Winds." Lee Min-ho, gaining immense popularity for his role in "Boys Over Flowers," received the Best New Actor award in the television category. Meanwhile, YoonA was awarded the Best New Actress award in the same category.

Here's the complete list of winners:

**Film Category:**

- Best Film: "Celebrating Our Love"

- Best Director: Lee Yun-ki ("A Fabulous Day")

- Best New Director: Lee Joong-ryul ("Bellflower")

- Best Actor: Ju Jin-mo ("Frost and Fire")

- Best Actress: Son Ye-jin ("My Wife Got Married")

- Best New Actor: So Ji-sub ("Movie is Movie")

- Best New Actress: Park Bo-young ("Speed Scandal")

- Best Screenplay: Kang Hyung-chul ("Speed Scandal")

- Best Popularity: Joo Ji-hoon, Park Bo-young

**Television Category:**

- Best Work - Drama: "Mom Has Horns"

- Best Work - Educational: "The Choice of Dokdo"

- Best Work - Variety: "Concert"

- Best Director: Shin Woo-cheol

- Best New Director: Boo Sung-chul ("Star’s Lover")

- Best Actor: Kim Myung-min ("Beethoven Virus")

- Best Actress: Moon Geun-young ("Garden of the Winds")

- Best New Actor: Lee Min-ho ("Boys Over Flowers")

- Best New Actress: YoonA ("You're My Destiny")

- Best TV Show Host (Male): Kim Byung-man ("1 Night 2 Days")

- Best TV Show Host (Female): Park Mi-sun ("Concert")

- Best Screenplay: Ryu Hyun-mi ("God's Scale")

- Best Popularity: Kim Hyun-jung, YoonA
