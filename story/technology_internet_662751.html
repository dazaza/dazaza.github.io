14-Year-Old Boy Afraid of Being Reported for Smoking Commits Matricide and Siblings Murder

by aia7892c on 2012-02-10 15:59:05

Incident occurred in Zhaigang, Lian Nan - Just because of fear of being reported for smoking. According to the Qingyuan Daily (reported by Cao Jing, communicated by Liu Xiangxia), it was learned yesterday that Meng Mou, a 14-year-old boy from Lian Nan, due to the fear of his mother informing his father about his secret smoking habit, cruelly killed his mother and younger sister and staged a scene to fake a burglary. He was sentenced to 12 years in prison by the Qingyuan Intermediate People's Court for intentional homicide. It is understood that Meng Mou, who lived in Zhaigang Town, Lian Nan, was originally an excellent student with high expectations from his parents, who disciplined him very strictly. Whenever Meng Mou made a mistake, he would be punished by kneeling on a washboard or getting slapped. After entering junior high school, Zhao Mou became addicted to online games, which caused his grades to drop. His father had beaten and scolded him multiple times regarding this matter. In early March 2011, defendant Meng Mou was caught smoking secretly by his mother Chen Mou. Afraid that his mother would tell his father about his smoking habit, leading to severe beating and scolding, Meng Mou decided to kill his mother.

Fearful of his mother "tattling", he conceived murderous intentions. On the evening of March 7th, Meng Mou learned that his father would return from their hometown in Hunan the next day, so he decided to kill his mother that night. At around 5 a.m. on March 8th, Meng Mou quietly got up from his bed in the third-floor bedroom, went to the second-floor kitchen to grab a cleaver, and then proceeded to his mother’s room on the fourth floor. Taking advantage of her deep sleep, he covered her mouth with his left hand and used the cleaver in his right hand to slash her neck. Upon waking up and resisting, Chen Mou was repeatedly slashed on her head, neck, and upper limbs. At this point, his younger sister woke up due to the struggle and screamed while grabbing Meng Mou. Consequently, Meng Mou also inflicted multiple slashes on his sister's head, neck, and limbs. Ultimately, both Chen Mou and her daughter died from hemorrhagic shock.

Subsequently, Meng Mou pried open drawers and wardrobes, taking cash to stage a burglary scene. Around 7 a.m., Meng Mou went to his uncle claiming that he found his mother and sister dead in bed. Together with his uncle, they reported the incident to the police station. Later, investigators suspected Meng Mou of being involved in the crime and arrested him on the same day.

The court believed that Meng Mou illegally deprived others of life, constituting intentional homicide. Being only just 14 years old at the time of the crime, due to the long-term fear of his father's violent education and fearing that his mother would inform his father about his smoking habit, he developed a motive to kill. This indicates that the defendant Meng Mou's psychology was suppressed, leading to his immature mindset. The harm he caused was limited to family members, with relatively less social danger compared to other cases. Before the incident, Meng Mou had a good behavior record, and after committing the crime, he confessed truthfully and showed evident remorse for his actions. Other family members unanimously petitioned the court to deal leniently with the defendant. Therefore, the court decided to impose a lighter punishment accordingly and handed down the aforementioned verdict.
