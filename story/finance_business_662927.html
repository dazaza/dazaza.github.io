Valentine's Day Hilarious Poem Mashup, Only Chinese Can Do It

by wangxin324 on 2012-02-10 19:48:38

Super hilarious poetry mashups, ancient poems have ushered in a new spring, sweat... 1. "In heaven we wish to be birds flying wing to wing, but when disaster strikes, we fly away separately." (You two are really a match...) 2. "The beauty rolls up the pearl curtain, all paths are devoid of human traces. The gibbons on both banks cry without pause, startling a chorus of frogs." (Is this still a beauty?!) 3. "I urge you to finish one more cup of wine, from now on you will be a stranger to me." (So heartless?) 4. "Startled and sit up straight while gravely ill, laughingly ask where the guest comes from." (Pretending to be sick? Deathbed recovery?) 5. "Chariots rumble, horses neigh, the spring breeze of February is like scissors." (Is the Jia family organizing a spring outing?) 6. "The whole hall of flowers intoxicates three thousand guests, yet not a single one is a kindred spirit." (Geniuses are always lonely) 7. "Departing young and returning old, how can you tell if I am male or female?" (Poor thing, what have you been through these years?) 8. "Red delicate hands, yellow vine wine, two golden orioles sing among the emerald willows. Outside the long pavilion, by the ancient road, a line of egrets ascends into the blue sky." (Such an artistic conception, only Chinese can achieve it) 9. "At midnight, suddenly dreaming of youthful affairs, only dream of idle people, not you." (Do you have a grudge against this person?) 10. "May I ask where there is a wine shop? Outside the city of Suzhou lies the Cold Mountain Temple." (Don't talk nonsense!) 11. "If friends and relatives in Luoyang ask about me, the light boat has already passed ten thousand mountains." (Owe money? Run fast enough!) 12. "Upon hearing the daughter's arrival, father leaps into the clear pool; upon hearing her younger sister's arrival, she hangs herself on the southeastern branch. Upon hearing his sister's arrival, the younger brother pauses his pipa playing, hesitating to speak." (Miss, your moral quality is really poor) 13. "Startled and sit up straight while gravely ill, at nightfall, the concubine comes again." (Unrepentant to the end) 14. "Searched for in vain with worn-out iron shoes, that person is at the dimly lit corner." (Should you feel lucky or depressed?) 15. "Ask you how much sorrow you can have, a different kind of taste lingers in my heart." (Is this the legal spouse?) 16. "A waterfall plunges straight down three thousand feet, falling short of Wang Lun's farewell sentiment." (Did Wang Lun jump off a cliff?) 17. "When awake, we revel together, suddenly the children form a row." (Cause and effect relationship) 18. "The road is long and arduous, the brave warrior departs never to return!" (Neat and orderly) 19. "Three thousand beauties in the imperial harem, an iron rod ground into a embroidery needle." (Realism school) 20. "The sky is vast and blue, the wilderness boundless, a tree full of pear blossoms presses against the crabapple." (Spring light leaks out...) 21. "Ask the world, what is love? The gibbons on both banks cry without pause." (The sorrowful ape)
