Party school correspondence Currently, there are mainly three types of academic qualifications in China's national education system: ordinary high and secondary institutions included in the national unified enrollment plan, adult high and secondary institutions, and national higher education self-examination graduation diplomas. The key to these three forms of academic qualifications lies in the unity and seriousness of the "import" and "export". To enter ordinary high and secondary institutions or adult high and secondary institutions, one must pass the national unified examination to obtain admission qualifications, such as the college entrance examination, middle school entrance examination, or adult college entrance examination, which controls the "import." Only by passing the examination and obtaining admission qualifications, and completing all course assessments within the specified study period, can one be awarded the corresponding graduation diploma. National higher education self-examination is a state-authoritative academic certification examination. Its "import" is open, anyone can register, the state does not care how the examinee "studies," but one must pass the unified course examination, and only those who pass can obtain the graduation certificate, which controls the "export." These "import" and "export" examinations are all organized and completed by the educational departments specified by the state. In addition, national education academic qualifications have an obvious mark: all diplomas are stamped with the steel seal of the corresponding educational department. From the above two points, it can be seen that party school correspondence is a completely independent form of education outside the national education system. Neither the entrance examination nor the graduation examination is organized by the educational department, but only within the system. Therefore, its graduation diploma should belong to an internal training certificate and does not represent a national education academic qualification. Party school correspondence is actually an internal training conducted by the party to improve the theoretical literacy and business level of party members and cadres and build a team of high-quality party members and cadres. Compared to national education, the "import" is wider, and the "export" is looser. Up to now, I have not heard of anyone who participated in party school correspondence and did not graduate. Moreover, the undergraduate section does not require English, which is far from the strict requirements of English in national education. According to the provisions of the national judicial examination registration qualifications, one must have a bachelor's degree or above in national education to apply, so the undergraduate diploma of the Central Party School correspondence cannot be used to apply. 2. Judicial Examination: Personnel who meet the following conditions can register for the national judicial examination: 1. Have the nationality of the People's Republic of China; 2. Support the Constitution of the People's Republic of China and enjoy the right to vote and be elected; 3. Have full civil capacity; 4. Meet the academic and professional conditions stipulated in the Judges Law, Prosecutors Law, and Lawyers Law; 5. Have good conduct. The aforementioned condition 4 regarding the academic and professional conditions for registering for the national judicial examination, based on Article 9 of the Judges Law, Article 10 of the Prosecutors Law, Article 6 of the Lawyers Law concerning the appointment of judges, prosecutors, and obtaining lawyer qualifications, and the Ministry of Justice's Opinion on Determining Places for Relaxing Registration Academic Conditions for the National Judicial Examination, are: First, having a bachelor's degree or above in law from a higher institution, or having a bachelor's degree or above in other majors from a higher institution with legal knowledge; Second, counties (banners) under the jurisdiction of provinces, autonomous regions, and municipalities directly under the central government, counties (banners) under the jurisdiction of each autonomous region, counties under the jurisdiction of each autonomous prefecture; counties identified by the State Council as key areas for national poverty alleviation and development work; cities, regions, counties, county-level cities, and urban districts under the jurisdiction of the Tibet Autonomous Region, can relax the academic conditions for registration to a junior college degree in law from a higher institution. The aforementioned higher institutions, according to Article 68 of the Higher Education Law, refer to universities, independently established colleges, and higher vocational schools, including higher vocational schools and adult higher schools. Correspondence is a form of learning in adult education. Since the registration conditions also include adult higher schools, this means that correspondence can also be used to apply. Whether you can apply in the year of graduation depends on whether you need to show your graduation certificate when registering. If you do not have the graduation certificate at the time of registration, it will depend on whether the school's certificate is accepted.
