Around this little tavern, all kinds of farces were played out, so the stories of wine culture and people related to wine frequently took place here. Zhuo Yajun also gradually matured from the initial inability to cope, and skillfully dealt with various relationships and problems. As Si Ma Xiangguan offered strategies for Zhuo Yajun countless times, the two began to appreciate each other, and their love slowly developed.
