How to Host a Fashion Party Wedding -- In-depth Explanation

by fmaxi63t on 2010-04-11 19:58:46

To host a perfect party wedding, you first need to set a theme or determine a main color scheme, and then select appropriate attire, venues, and items based on the chosen theme. Regardless of the type of wedding theme you choose, it is important to specify your general style and dress code in the invitations sent to guests so they can prepare accordingly.

1. Bridal makeup: Communicate thoroughly with the makeup artist before the wedding and inform them about the general format of the ceremony. If the ceremony is held outdoors in a garden, the makeup should be elegant and natural without excessive embellishment. If there is a party in the evening, the makeup can be slightly more intense when changing. Zhuhai flowers, lip gloss, and blush should lean towards warm tones, and the eyes can be emphasized, but not with multiple colors to avoid looking gaudy.

2. Attire for the couple: Since the couple will be moving around frequently during the party wedding, cumbersome palace-style or long train dresses that may cause inconvenience are not suitable. Simple straight-cut or A-line skirts, or fishtail styles that highlight line beauty, are preferred. The groom's attire can be light-colored for the daytime ceremony and dark suits for the evening party.

3. Venue decoration: The party wedding venue can be selected in a bar, café, or private club. The venue should be spacious enough to accommodate free exchanges and movements of the guests. As this is a Western-style wedding, the decorations should preferably use props such as fresh flowers and chiffon to create an atmosphere, but the tone of the flowers should ideally be uniform to avoid looking cluttered.

4. Attention to detail: From confirming the decoration of tables and chairs, exquisite full sets of Western dining utensils, charming music playlists, candlelit environments at night, to small details like ribbons on wine glasses, placement of the guestbook, and flavor characteristics of pastries, numerous details require careful preparation. Skillful use of details can enhance the perfection of the wedding.

5. Gift selection: Since this is a fashion-oriented wedding, the selection of gifts or wedding candies for guests should also avoid monotony. If a whole set of small gifts can be custom-made for this wedding, or if the gifts can be embedded with wedding photos, it would make people feel refreshed. However, make sure to confirm the customization time well in advance to avoid missing the wedding schedule!

Who is suitable for a party wedding?

1. Young, fashionable white-collar and golden-collar workers. If both parties' parents and friends cannot attend from out of town, then most of the wedding guests will be the newlyweds' friends and colleagues. They share a similar cultural background, which facilitates emotional or intellectual exchanges. Additionally, since they are familiar with Western etiquette, the atmosphere required by a party wedding will be more harmonious.

2. From a cost perspective, although party weddings are simple, considering the price of Western cuisine and venue fees, the cost per guest is approximately 200 yuan. In terms of purchasing power, they can afford it, and they also identify with the Western catering style of party weddings.
