Wuhan University 2012 Recruitment Prospectus for the Part-time Study of Master of Law Professional Degree

Part-time Postgraduate Studies

Part-time postgraduate studies are a form of graduate education within the national plan, where individuals maintain their employment status, work part-time, and study at school part-time. This falls under the national education system. There are differences in enrollment, examination requirements, and admission methods depending on the category. Part-time postgraduates are officially enrolled by the university and receive the same degree as full-time postgraduates upon graduation. To meet the needs of high-level professional talent for China's economic development, according to the regulations of the State Council Academic Degrees Committee, combined with the learning and working situation of part-time personnel, Wuhan University will enroll relevant field personnel to pursue a Master of Law professional degree.

Introduction to Wuhan University

Wuhan University is a key comprehensive university directly under the Ministry of Education, and is a key construction university under the national "985 Project" and "211 Project". The campus of Wuhan University covers an area of 5600 mu, with a building area of 2.42 million square meters. The university has a beautiful environment and picturesque scenery, making it one of the most beautiful universities in the world. Wuhan University has a complete range of disciplines, strong comprehensiveness, and distinct characteristics, covering 11 major categories including philosophy, economics, education, literature, history, science, engineering, agriculture, medicine, and management. It has doctoral degree granting authority in 43 primary disciplines, master's degree granting authority in 14 primary disciplines, doctoral degree granting authority in 244 secondary disciplines, master's degree granting authority in 333 disciplines, 32 post-doctoral research stations, more than 1200 doctoral supervisors, and more than 2400 master's supervisors. The excellent teaching resources, strong humanistic atmosphere, long-standing educational tradition, and vibrant innovation mechanism of the university have won widespread international renown.

I. Enrollment Conditions and Admission Principles

1. Graduated from a national education series university with a bachelor's degree or above and obtained a graduation certificate before July 31, 2009. Generally, a degree certificate is required (the number of students without a degree generally does not exceed 10% of the current year's admissions).

2. The admission process is organized by our university, and the admission score line is set by our university.

3. The proportion of non-legal system candidates admitted generally does not exceed 20% of the current year's admission quota of our university.

4. Candidates who fail the interview will not be admitted.

5. Candidates with degrees will be given priority under equal conditions. The number of candidates admitted without degrees shall not exceed 10% of the total admissions.

6. The adjustment of candidates' admissions is limited to institutions within Hubei Province, and inter-provincial (autonomous region, municipality) institutional adjustments are not allowed.

II. Examination and Training Methods

1. A learning method that involves entering school without leaving the job (mainly concentrated courses each semester on campus).

2. Entrusted training, no transfer of household registration, personnel relations, etc., during the study period. Wages, benefits, medical expenses, etc., are borne by the candidate's employer.

3. Pass the national joint examination for part-time postgraduates held in October, with examination subjects being English and professional comprehensive (including criminal law, civil law, legal theory, Chinese constitutional law, and history of Chinese legal system).

4. Duration: The study period is generally 3 years.

5. Tuition fee is 48,000 yuan, specific fees may vary depending on the location of the program, subject to the signed agreement.

III. Degree Awarding

After completing the study period, fulfilling the credit requirements specified in the training program, achieving satisfactory results, independently completing the thesis, meeting other relevant requirements, and passing the review of the Wuhan University Academic Degrees Committee, the Master of Law professional degree can be awarded.
