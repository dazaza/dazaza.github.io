Latest QQ feature preview: QQ voice message留言

by lzfly5i5 on 2008-02-28 19:21:11

What is QQ Yin Xin voice mail? Simply put, it allows you to leave a voice message for your QQ friends through QQ Yin Xin. You can type out what you want to say, and QQ Yin Xin will convert the text into speech and send it to the recipient. Alternatively, you can record your own voice using QQ Yin Xin to convey messages such as confessions, blessings, or thanks. Not only can the voice message be sent to the recipient's mobile phone or telephone, but it can also be sent directly to their QQ account. Regardless of whether the recipient is online or not, the message will not be lost. According to insider information, the public beta test of the QQ Yin Xin voice mail feature will begin in March. Users participating in the beta test will be able to send voice messages to their QQ friends for free.

QQ Yin Xin is a service launched by Tencent in 2007. It allows users to send voice greetings to loved ones, friends, and colleagues, which they can listen to on their phones. Expressions of love, gratitude, apologies, and other "hard-to-say" words can all be easily conveyed through QQ Yin Xin. Since its launch, it has been favored and praised by many QQ users. Some users have used QQ Yin Xin to confess their hidden feelings of "I love you" to their girlfriends, while others have expressed gratitude and respect to their parents. Especially during the Spring Festival, many users used QQ Yin Xin to send New Year greetings and blessings to their leaders, bosses, friends, and colleagues.

The voice mail feature of QQ Yin Xin is a great addition to the existing service. With this feature, you can not only send a voice message to the recipient's phone, but also directly to their QQ account. This way, there's no worry about the call failing due to an unreachable phone number, as the voice message is stored on the server like a QQ message. Even if the recipient is offline at the time, they will still receive your voice message when they come online.

Another distinctive feature in the new functions is the ability to record your own voice. This opens up more creative ways to express yourself through voice messages, including regional dialects, humor, and playful tones.

Looking forward to the public beta test of QQ Yin Xin voice mail...
