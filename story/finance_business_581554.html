The mandatory standard for HD video: The shooting of HD video corporate promotional videos must adopt the H.264 mandatory standard, which can provide high-quality video transmission and low-latency encoding and decoding, making the video stream smoother and more natural. However, compared to previous H.26x algorithms, the H.264 mandatory standard requires higher processing performance.

Resolution requirements for "HD": The optimal resolution for HD video shooting is 1080p and 720p. To obtain high-resolution images, a camera that supports true HD (minimum 720p) in a 16:9 standard must be configured. Ideally, the camera and the video conferencing terminal equipment should come from the same manufacturer to ensure that the functions of the camera and terminal equipment reach their optimal state, thus obtaining the best end-to-end HD video conferencing experience.
