**Know Your Skin Type**

The first key to makeup is understanding your skin type. People with different skin types need different kinds of makeup. For example, a woman with oily skin might not suit glossy makeup. Similarly, a girl with dry skin might not suit matte finish makeup, whereas someone with normal skin can try any type of makeup. If you know your skin type, it will be easier for you to buy suitable cosmetic products.

**Check Out Makeup Products**

As mentioned earlier, the market is filled with beauty products. Some makeup products target the lips, such as lipsticks, lip glosses, lip liners, lip plumpers, lip balms, and lip boosters. For the face, there are foundations, blushes, bronzers, and concealers. For the eyes, there are mascaras, eyelash enhancers, eye balms, eyeliner, eyeshadows, eye shimmers, shimmering eye pencils, eyebrow pencils, creams, waxes, gels, and powders. There are also nail polishes and other products selected based on your needs and skin type.

**Types of Makeup - Know What Suits You Best**

Some people prefer a natural look, while others want to have those stunning appearances seen in fashion shows. It is certain that this is a matter of choice. For a sun-kissed look, women tend to use more bronzers and shimmers. However, gel-based makeup is usually applied to create a wet look. Women who want to look beautiful at all times opt for permanent makeup, which is actually a type of cosmetic procedure. On the other hand, ladies who prefer a natural look choose organic mineral makeup containing mineral pigments and organic plant extracts, oils, and organic makeup, which is certainly good because it won't harm the skin or other organs.

**Makeup for Occasions**

You cannot always look like a princess. Depending on the occasion and time, you must change your makeup accordingly. For instance, on a hot summer day, glossy makeup or dark tones might not appeal to you. At that time, it would be best to use soft tones and fresh makeup. For a social gathering, you must do makeup according to your clothing and the party mood. Perhaps it's always best to use soft tones during summer.
