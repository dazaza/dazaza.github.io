③ Because the work is not self-sufficient

by clockd63 on 2011-06-28 15:04:05

Heaven is about to descend a great responsibility on this person, must first bitter his mind, labor his muscles and bones, hungry his body, empty his body.

Although the difficulties we Xuetong社 face are not as arduous as those in the poems, we do not take them lightly, and we face every challenge with the most positive attitude. No matter how hard or tired we are, we will persevere through gritting our teeth. Even when tears well up in our eyes, we will not let them fall. We can only face difficulties with a smile, and only then will difficulties smile upon us. Not only do we act this way, but all the branch of the Youth League Committee and student union cadres are the same.

In the blink of an eye, this semester is nearing its end, and we have walked through it with smiles. After a semester's work and hardships, while increasing our work experience, the members of our Xuetong社 have also become more mature and prudent. This semester, our Xuetong社 has successfully completed a series of tasks.

1. Photo collection for the school sports meeting.

The sports meeting is a large group event that both teachers and students in our school highly value. It not only can exercise personal perseverance but also demonstrates team spirit and our service consciousness. Not only does it enhance the cohesion among students, but it also exercises one's fighting spirit. In 2009, our school held the 31st school sports meeting. Our college athletes bravely fought on the field, and our Xuetong社 members followed closely with cameras in hand, recording their moments of running, jumping, and sprinting, capturing their smiling faces and the excitement of tight embraces. Despite insufficient manpower and cameras, in order to timely capture brilliant moments, all members of the Xuetong社 remained steadfast at their posts throughout the sports meeting, running between multiple venues, and did everything possible to complete the tasks assigned by the leadership. Not only did we complete them, but we also excelled in doing so. Only in this way can our Xuetong社 become more perfect and filled with an upward-striving atmosphere.

2. Publicity reporting for the Party Training Class.

In October, the college held a two-day Party Training Class for the new batch of aspiring Party members. Our Xuetong社 members also took notes and took pictures in the classroom alongside them. Although the new members of our society were receiving such a task for the first time, they still worked diligently in publicizing our intra-Party activities. Publicizing all the events of the institute is an inescapable responsibility of our Xuetong社. We can only face it bravely and cannot retreat. Promoting the Party is even more important, and being able to better promote the Party to our fellow students is our goal.

3. Student Cadre Training.

To improve the quality of student cadres and strengthen the development of student work, in November, our college conducted the third phase of student cadre training. The training invited Secretary Zhou Huazhong from the university Youth League Committee, Secretary Yin Zhushou from our college, Deputy Secretary Xiang Guangdong, and two counselor teachers to give lectures. While attending the training classes well, our society members actively carried out photo collection and publicity work for the training. A news article about the music and dance college holding student cadre training was published on the school news website. As part of the student cadres, we must constantly enrich ourselves and improve ourselves to set an example for the classmates and to help share responsibilities for the leadership and serve our classmates.

4. The Third Faculty and Staff Representative Conference.

Our college held the third faculty and staff representative conference in December 2009. Our society actively paid attention to the construction of the teaching staff of the college, made reports on the conference, and published the news on the news website and the school newspaper. Although there were some errors in this news report, it gave all members of our society a big lesson, sounding a warning bell for our future work. Only in this way can we better accomplish our future tasks. Failure is the mother of success. We continuously improve and explore to create a brighter future.

5. Journalist Day.

November 8, 2009, was China's tenth Journalist Day. To celebrate Journalist Day, our school's Propaganda Department held a "Journalist Day Series of Events." Our Xuetong社 actively cooperated with the school's Propaganda Department, organizing two excellent performances during the Journalist Day evening party and designing and exhibiting a "College Style Exhibition Board" representing the characteristics of our college. The entire event went smoothly, giving our society a good start for future smooth operations and providing a valuable experience.

6. A series of works for academic style and dormitory cultural construction month.

In November, our school designated December as the "Campus Dormitory Cultural Construction Month." Our college seized this opportunity to actively carry out academic style and dormitory culture rectification and construction activities, including projects such as "strictly cracking down on truancy and tardiness," "rectification of items inside dormitories," and the investigation of prohibited electrical appliances. Our society cooperated with other departments of the Youth League Branch and Student Union to successfully complete the investigation of prohibited electrical appliances, neatness evaluation, and dormitory hygiene inspections. We also took photos and announced the results for both excellent and substandard dormitory hygiene conditions.

7. Calligraphy and Handicraft Art Exhibition.

On November 16th and 17th, the "Boiling Youth・Calligraphy Handicraft Exhibition" organized by the Quality Expansion Center of our college's Youth League Branch was a great success. To ensure proper publicity for the event, our society cooperated well with the responsible department head and effectively publicized the event, publishing the news on the school news website.

8. Cooperation with other departments.

During this semester, our society actively cooperated with the Labor and Health Department and the Propaganda Department to conduct hygiene inspections and produce various bulletin boards.

In summary, this semester's work was busy and tiring, yet fulfilling and happy. At the same time, we discovered many shortcomings in our work: ① delays or errors in reporting due to carelessness; ② insufficient close exchanges and assistance among members; ③ lack of initiative in work, resulting in many bulletin boards not being displayed in a timely manner... In future work, the Xuetong社 will actively make improvements, strengthen team awareness among members, summarize experiences and lessons learned, so as to be able to welcome the challenges of the new semester with greater enthusiasm under the guidance of the college leadership, teachers, and other departments of the Youth League Branch and Student Union.
