Polarization in the hardware industry is becoming more and more serious, with competition focusing on sales channels.

by anonymous on 2011-08-02 13:50:09

[China Hardware Tools Network] The dichotomous situation of hardware enterprises will become more and more serious. Some enterprises that are not adaptable to market competition will exit the market competition through different channels, while hardware giants will gradually emerge. China's hardware industry will still continue the momentum of structural adjustment, but at the same time, there will be some highlights constantly appearing. In the global competitive environment, in order to gain a favorable competitive position and improve competitiveness, industrial capital is another theme of industry operation.

It is expected that in the coming years, there will absolutely be fewer hardware enterprises that can survive in the market than now. However, this high-speed oscillation in the hardware industry will bring great opportunities, and the result of the oscillation will make market operations more rational. The next few years will be a period of high-speed oscillation for the hardware industry, and the direct consequence of this high-speed oscillation will expand the trend of polarization among the current hardware kitchen cabinet brand camps.

The competition between sales channels is becoming increasingly fierce. Due to the oversupply of domestic hardware kitchen cabinets, the pressure on quality increases, making sales channels one of the key competitive factors, and the competition for channels is becoming increasingly intense.

(I) Kitchen appliance manufacturers have strengthened their control over retail terminals, striving to reduce sales links, save sales costs, and develop sales channels towards professionalization. The enterprise sales model is developing in a direction that can adapt to diverse markets simultaneously.

(II) The development trend of the sales industry has continuously increased the status of large home appliance chain stores. Large retailers rely on their broad market coverage, procurement scale, and cost advantages to enhance their control over production enterprises in terms of product pricing, payment settlement, etc., thereby strengthening their control over the industry and participating in and triggering price competition previously dominated by manufacturers.

Many hardware enterprises have increased their technological investment, developed new products with high technical content, and used product differentiation as a long-term strategy for enterprise development, seeking new market demands and establishing new economic growth points (entering industries such as small appliances and other related industries) to achieve sustainable development of enterprises. Market competition will shift from price-based to high-quality, high-tech content products. With the deepening of competition, the profit margins at each stage of the hardware industry chain are being compressed, and the space for price reductions is decreasing day by day. More and more enterprises realize that relying solely on price competition cannot build core competitiveness and is not a long-term development direction, thus they strive to explore new development paths.

While continuing to expand traditional markets such as the United States and Japan, there will also be comprehensive development in Southeast Asia, the Middle East, Russia, Europe, Africa, and other regions. The integration of domestic and foreign enterprises will further accelerate. Domestic hardware enterprises will enhance their own strength and faster expand into international markets through various means, accelerating the integration with foreign enterprises to improve product quality and competitiveness.

International markets' requirements for China's hardware products will also gradually evolve, with higher demands for the quality, packaging, delivery deadlines of Chinese products, and even extending to the production process and product research and development, combining products with environmental protection, energy resources, and humanistic environments. The polarization of enterprises will further intensify, and the next few years will be a period of high-speed oscillation for the hardware industry. The direct consequence of this high-speed oscillation will expand the trend of polarization among the current hardware kitchen cabinet brand camps. It is expected that in the coming years, there will absolutely be fewer hardware enterprises that can truly survive in the market than now. However, this high-speed oscillation in the hardware industry will bring great opportunities, and the result of the oscillation will make market operations more rational.
