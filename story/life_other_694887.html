Due to the continuous drought, the living water of a large number of people has been seriously threatened. Water is here!

On the morning of March 2nd, Dongchuan Fire Brigade received a distress call from Shuijing Village, Wulong Town, Dongchuan District. The caller said that due to the recent continuous drought, the whole village of Shuijing in Wulong Town was in urgent need of drinking water. After receiving the distress call, the brigade immediately dispatched a water tanker fire truck to Shuijing Village of Wulong Town under the condition of ensuring the strength of fire duty, delivering drinking water for the villagers and solving the problem of villagers' water use.

When the fire truck appeared at the head of Shuijing Village, the villagers who had waited for a long time rushed to the fire truck, scrambling to put their buckets, basins, and pots neatly beside the fire truck. In order not to waste the villagers' time, the soldiers quickly laid out the water hose and poured clear water into each villager's bucket and water pool.

Seeing the clear water flowing from the fire truck into the bucket, the villagers' faces were filled with radiant smiles.

"Thank the firefighters too much, this is really life-saving water!" An old man held the firefighter's hand and said excitedly. The soldiers smiled and said that it is our duty, serving the people is our responsibility and mission.
