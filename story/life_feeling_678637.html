Missed for a lifetime, don't miss again in the next life...

by xiaoxiao67 on 2012-02-27 15:52:22

Miss a lifetime, don't miss it again in the next life……

Meeting him was the most beautiful beginning.

One person, one face, even one name, became a blade of grass planted in her heart. It sways when the wind blows, but can never be pulled out……

She still remembers that she first noticed him during a Chinese class. The teacher asked each student to introduce themselves. At first, she found it very boring - all the introductions were unoriginal. Until he introduced himself and mentioned his nickname, she laughed. Her smile was as radiant as the blooming gardenias outside the window. It was still summer then……

Before this, although they were in the same class, they had no verbal communication until an art class where they truly got to know each other…

He sat in the row in front of her. The art teacher asked everyone to make clay figures. When she was chatting with her friend, she noticed him placing a clay figure on her stationery box. She looked at him angrily, and he gave her a mischievous smile, made a funny face, and she reached out to hit him…

That’s how they met. Perhaps from that moment on, his playful and mischievous face had already been etched into her heart, though she hadn’t realized it.

She forgot when exactly she started liking him, maybe it was on her first Christmas?

It was her first Christmas. Watching her friends busy preparing gifts, she felt a bit flustered. She didn’t know who to give a gift to.

Just at this time, a friend approached her, asking if she had someone to give a gift to. She shook her head. Her friend happily said, "That's great! Then come with me to give him a gift." She looked at her friend in confusion, and her friend's face turned slightly red. She seemed to understand something. Her friend said, "I'm just too shy to do it alone, so please help me." And like that, she unknowingly gave him a gift. But what she didn't expect was that he also gave her a gift. For some reason, she felt a little happy. On Christmas Eve, she had such a wonderful time. Every time she recalls it now, a smile still appears at the corner of her mouth. From then on, she remembered this holiday, and because of him, she deeply fell in love with this festival……

The blue color of the sky falls into the wind.

Until now, she still doesn't understand why she likes him. Maybe loving someone really doesn't need a reason……

Every day they bickered like enemies. To this day, she clearly remembers that during one of their scuffles, she scratched several bloodstains on his hand, causing a lot of bleeding. When he placed his hand in front of her, while her mouth was still arguing with him, fear and a touch of pain flashed through her heart. Without her noticing, he suddenly wiped the blood onto her hand and walked away, leaving her standing there dumbfounded. When she came to her senses, she realized that the blood was still on her hand. Perhaps these traces of blood had seeped into her heart, flowing together with her blood. She only felt her face slightly warm……

She liked quietness, while he loved excitement. He often liked to wander around in front of her. Maybe due to a girl's modesty, she always ignored him, remaining silent. Finally, he lost patience every day dealing with this kind of girl. He gradually began to distance himself from her, starting to fool around with other girls. As for her, it was the first time she experienced the feeling of heartbreak, a tearing pain……

He and she became increasingly estranged. She started quietly reading books, quietly eating meals, and quietly going home. She could no longer hear his laughter, could no longer see his smile. Every night before sleeping, she would think about him. She hoped she could bring him into her dreams, perhaps seeing his smile in her dreams, hearing his voice. But every time she woke up, looking at the empty room, loneliness was like a devil tightly clinging to her. She couldn't find anyone to talk to about her inner thoughts.

Gradually, she started liking to daydream. She didn't know what she was thinking about, only feeling as if she had flown to a distant place, but her heart remained tied to him.

In the blink of an eye, the fragrance of the gardenias in the campus began to fade, and she realized that summer had passed long ago……

For so long, she didn't know if he liked her too, until rumors started spreading in the class that he liked her. She was happy for a long time. Although she knew it was just a rumor, every time she saw his cold expression, her heart cooled down. But she still fantasized that one day, he would tell her "I love you" in person. She gradually learned to wait……

Until graduation day, she waited hopefully for him to call her and say those three words: "I love you." But in the end, he left. She stared at his retreating back for a long time, unable to believe it, until he completely disappeared from her sight. She stood there, years of waiting finally ended in vain. She forgot to cry, only sighing heavily. It was just a beautiful dream……

After loving, let go gently……

She thought that from then on, he had completely left her life. She thought she would never see him again. But she didn't expect that the high school they attended was actually the same school, just not in the same class……

She smiled, a calm smile. She didn't know if it was fate playing a joke on her. She originally thought that by moving to a place without him, she could gradually forget him. But now, how could she possibly forget? After all, she had loved him so deeply once!

Although they were in the same school, they never exchanged a single word. It seemed like he intentionally avoided her. Whenever he saw her, he always lowered his head and ran past her. Her heart hurt again and again. She finally decided to give up. Just at this time, she met someone who said he liked her. She quickly agreed to date him, naively thinking that this way she could forget him without a trace. But she was wrong. For two years, she had never forgotten him. She cried, knowing that what she did had already hurt another person. In the end, she was still alone……

Christmas was coming again. She wanted to hold on to something, so she asked her friend to ask him: If she gave him a gift, would he still give her one? The message her friend brought back was that he told her to focus on her studies and not think about things she shouldn't be thinking about now. It was better for one person to have bad grades than two. She didn't know whether to be moved or sad after hearing this. It turns out he always thought she would affect him. On that Christmas Eve, when her classmates were celebrating wildly, she hid in a corner and cried for a long time, a very long time. She told herself that this was the last time she would cry for him, and she would never cry for him again, never again……

From the start of Christmas to its end, everything seemed so matter-of-course, with not even a hint of reluctance…… She finally understood: He would never belong to her. Although she always thought he was the only one who could make her happy, she let go, completely let go, stopped praying, and stopped chasing that unreachable dream……

Summer came again, and the scent of gardenias filled the air, making her dizzy. She gazed at the azure sky above her head and sighed, "How lonely!" Those vague loves, those untold stories, let them all go……

Epilogue: This story seems cruel when placed here. We've all forgotten that the future is still so long, long enough for me to fall in love with someone else again, just like I did with you at first. Whose heart holds these memories? Who apologizes to whom at night? We can't hear any of it. Many things, missed for a lifetime, must be missed even in the next life……
