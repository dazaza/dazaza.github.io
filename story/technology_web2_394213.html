III. Market Survey Overview Analysis

1. The 21st century is the digital information age, the age of the Internet. According to Taobao's disclosed data, in 2007, Taobao's transaction volume was 43.3 billion yuan, second only to the Bailian Group in China's retail industry. Currently, the average daily transaction volume provided by Alipay exceeds close to 3 billion yuan. In contemporary society, with the deepening of China's reform and opening up, cities are getting bigger, work hours are getting longer, and traffic is getting busier. E-commerce (online shopping) will become increasingly important and adopted by more people.

Advantages of online stores: Buying a seasonal piece of clothing through traditional methods requires setting aside specific time to go to commercial areas to browse, select, try on, and purchase. If unsatisfied later, there’s also the hassle of returning it. Thus, apart from the price tag, buying this piece of clothing involves additional costs such as time and transportation, which are labor-intensive. In contrast, online shopping allows you to click a mouse and complete the selection comparison within half an hour. Apart from not being able to try it on, everything else is done, allowing you to wait for the clothes to be delivered. Moreover, the price is definitely cheaper than in malls (because no property, personnel, advertising, etc., costs need to be shared). It saves both time and money.

At the same time, we can frequently distribute questionnaires for end-user surveys to understand what services they truly need and how to promote our brand, always keeping track of market trends.

2. Target Market Setting: Men's wear, women's wear, and children's wear focus on young consumer groups aged between 20 and 40. These consumers will be our store's largest customer base.

3. Analysis of product consumer groups, consumption methods, consumption habits, and main factors affecting the market: With the development of the Internet and e-commerce, most young consumers increasingly prefer online shopping because it saves shopping time and provides a large number of products for customers to choose from, making it very convenient. Modern young people adapt well to online shopping, which is a great advantage for our online store, providing us with numerous business opportunities. However, the biggest concern with online shopping remains credibility issues. Therefore, our marketing model will adopt simultaneous operation of physical and online stores, greatly enhancing our online store's credibility.

4. Market Trend Forecast and Market Opportunities: After our market research, we found that the proportion of daily necessities purchases has significantly increased. This upward trend in daily necessities purchases will continue in the future and eventually become the mainstream of online shopping. Therefore, we have chosen this category of products as our main product.

IV. Competitive Analysis

1. Is there industry monopoly? No industry monopoly.

2. Main Competitors' Situations: Company strength, product conditions (types, prices, features, packaging, marketing, market share, etc.): After investigating multiple similar product online stores, we discovered that our competitors are already relatively mature. However, their operating models contain some obvious mistakes. We conducted in-depth research on some consumer groups, and their biggest worry about online shopping remains being deceived. For consumers, the popularity of clothing products significantly influences their purchasing decisions. Through these in-depth understandings, I believe it will be highly beneficial for our online store's development. Currently, these online stores offer a reasonable variety of products, but for consumers, the credibility is still not high enough, and the products' popularity is insufficient, which is advantageous for us.

3. Product Competitive Advantages of the Company: We use simultaneous operation of physical and online stores to enhance our online store's credibility; furthermore, during the initial phase of online store operations, we will heavily advertise to increase the popularity of our online store products. On this point, we will collaborate with various clothing manufacturers, sewing our online store labels or logos on the back collars of each purchased item or on the waistbands of pants. This way, our online store's advertising costs will decrease significantly, while simultaneously boosting our online store's popularity. We also provide online consultation to help customers understand our products.

V. Main Development Strategic Goals and Stage Goals of the Online Store

1. Strategic Goal: To make online shopping simpler, more efficient, more credible, and more acceptable to different groups of people. Let our customers enjoy a happy and simple life.

2. Stage Goals: Find suitable sources of goods for the online store to ensure quality and quantity, gradually penetrate the market, and develop customers. To enhance the popularity of our store in the early stages, appropriate marketing promotion should be carried out, but relying solely on online channels is insufficient; promotion through multiple online and offline channels is necessary.

3. Progress Schedule: First stage: Market research, analysis of questionnaires, understanding customer needs;

Second stage: Determining the source of goods, developing customers;

Third stage: Opening of the physical store; opening and maintenance of the Taobao store;

Fourth stage: Promotion;

Fifth stage: Entering regular operations for both online and physical stores;

Sixth stage: Expanding and strengthening the online store;

Ultimate goal: Using the online store to create a globally distributed chain "store," meaning we will have warehouses in various locations across the country, ensuring timely and accurate delivery of orders to designated locations.

VI. Marketing

1. Formulation of Sales Policies: Physical store marketing + online marketing. A physical store that can be found in real life gives customers a sense of security and stability when shopping online. At the same time, during the initial phase of online store marketing, due to the high cost of advertising, part of the funds can come from the profit of our physical store sales. When the online store grows larger, it can, in turn, positively impact our physical store, creating mutual promotion and advertisement, combining both to naturally bring faster economic benefits.

Using multiple online stores to sell the same products from one online store, i.e., using multiple Taobao accounts in the early stages to uniformly sell our online store's products. By incorporating our brand into multiple Taobao stores, we can naturally increase the popularity and brand awareness of our online store.

2. Sales Channels, Methods, Marketing Processes, and After-sales Services: Online sales + physical store sales, focused service.

From storefront selection to store design, considering differences in location, season, consumption habits, and economic levels comprehensively for distributors. Helping with reasonable inventory allocation, carefully planning promotional gifts, and other aspects to provide comprehensive services along with strong market strategies, advertising campaigns, image packaging, and problem-solving plans.

① The physical store will be located in the city center or at the junction of urban and suburban areas, ensuring a steady flow of customers while keeping costs relatively low.

② In the early stages of online store operations, freight forwarding via express delivery will save logistics costs. As the online store grows, the volume of goods to be shipped will increase significantly, at which point we will choose to handle logistics ourselves to reduce expensive courier fees.

③ Warehouses across the country will stock different products based on local consumption levels and habits.

3. Status of Major Business Relationships: Our online store belongs to retailers.

Qualification Standards and Policies at All Levels: Payment methods: Online payment, cash on delivery, etc., available for consumers to choose; Freight methods: Express delivery and postal services initially, logistics later; Discount policies: Products are already sold at discounted prices to ensure affordability.

4. Sales Team Situation and Sales Welfare Distribution Policy: Besides salaries, commissions based on sales volume, we will also allow all employees to own shares of our online store to fully motivate employee participation.

5. Promotion and Market Penetration (Methods and Arrangements, Budget):

① Main Promotion Methods: Offering small gifts, offering free shipping depending on the price, striving to satisfy customers as much as possible.

② Payment Methods: Coexistence of multiple methods like Alipay, online banking, and cash on delivery, improving user experience to promote sales.

③ Advertising/Public Relations Strategies, Media Evaluation: Adding advertisements on large video websites (such as Youku, Tudou, Pipi Movies), adding advertisements on the Taobao homepage, advertising on blogs, posting promotional plans on Baidu Tieba:

② User Interaction: Establishing a new section on the online store for users to exchange ideas, discuss, and provide feedback on clothing styles, colors, fabrics, etc.

② User-Oriented Product Display: Breaking down all products according to target user groups, i.e., user-oriented, so users can quickly find products suitable for themselves.

Purpose and Advantages of the Online Store:

Establishing a New Corporate Image: For a lifestyle creative living supplies company, corporate branding is crucial. Especially in today's era of highly developed internet technology, most customers learn about a company's products, image, and strength through the internet. Therefore, the image of a corporate online store often determines customer confidence in the company's products. Building an internationally recognized online store can greatly enhance the overall image of the company.

Optimizing Internal Corporate Management: Building an online store will introduce a new management model to the company, with the online store serving as the platform for this model. It plays a significant role in reducing internal resource waste, cutting costs, and strengthening communication and connections between employees and between the company and its employees, ultimately achieving the greatest optimization of the company's operations and processes.

Enhancing Sales Power: Sales power refers to the manifestation of the comprehensive advantages of products in sales. Modern marketing theory suggests that the success of sales depends not only on whether the various advantages of the product can be fully communicated but also on how much effective information the target audience receives.

Due to the "one-to-one" nature of the internet, the target audience can autonomously choose useful information for themselves, which already decides that consumers are interested in the information. This makes the dissemination of information no longer subjectively imposed on consumers but rather actively absorbed by consumers with choices. Meanwhile, through advanced design of the online store, product information combines the advantage of newspapers having large amounts of information with the integrated audio-visual-electronic stimulation of television, firmly attracting the target audience. Therefore, the effectiveness of product information dissemination will be significantly improved, thereby also enhancing the sales power of the product.

Increasing Added Value: Many people know that buying a product involves not only tangible items but also intangible after-sales services. This is the added value of the product. The higher the added value of the product, the more competitive it is in the market and the more popular it is among consumers. Therefore, companies need to find ways to increase the added value of their products. In the current stage, traditional after-sales service methods are far from meeting customer needs. Providing convenient, effective, instant 24-hour online services to consumers is a new way to enhance project added value. Customers worldwide can download the materials they need at any time through the online store, obtain answers to questions online, and submit their problems online.

Online Store Promotion Plan

1. Discount Promotions: Since online sales cannot give people a comprehensive and intuitive impression of the product, nor can they be tried on or touched, combined with the complexity of delivery costs and payment methods, this reduces the enthusiasm for online shopping and ordering. However, a relatively large discount can prompt consumers to try online shopping and make purchasing decisions.

2. Gift Promotions: The advantages of gift promotions include increasing your visibility; encouraging people to visit the online store frequently to get more discount information; and analyzing marketing effects and product reactions based on the enthusiasm of consumers seeking gifts. However, be sure not to choose inferior or substandard products as gifts, as this would have the opposite effect.

3. Limited-time Supply: "Limited-time supply" is always tempting to consumers. Phrases like "Only 2 days left at a discount!" must be genuine; otherwise, the trust of consumers will eventually be lost.

4. Establishing More Links with Other Online Stores: As the saying goes, "More friends mean more paths." On the internet, "more links mean more windows." By exchanging store links, a small network can be formed, enhancing mutual influence. Especially when someone gets recommended on the homepage, you benefit too, and when you're doing well, others can benefit from your success. We will collaborate with sister online stores, for example, if customers buy from our online store, we will give gifts provided by sister online stores, and vice versa, if customers buy from sister online stores, they will receive gifts given by our online store. The purpose is to promote the products of our small store.

5. Utilizing Other Promotion Methods: Our salespeople can promote in various forums, QQ groups, MSN, etc.

VII. Risk Analysis

1. Resource (Raw Materials/Suppliers) Risks

Our online store primarily selects goods that are carefully picked by the owner, integrating fashion, personality, and quality; secondly, goods that can bear our online store labels (clothes with our online store label or LOGO language on the back collar, pants with our online store label or LOGO on the back waistband). However, our store will dedicate itself to offering the best, finest, and most beautiful products, aiming to provide the most economical and convenient gift services, specifically offering fashionable, personalized, and high-quality clothing to users. Primarily, selecting good raw material suppliers and establishing a good supply-demand relationship ensures access to first-hand trendy goods, guaranteeing low online sales prices while ensuring that all products carry our online store labels or LOGOs.

2. Procurement Risks

① The single source of goods is not liked by customers.

② Quality issues arise with goods purchased from wholesale markets, and returns or exchanges are impossible.

③ Mistakes made by procurement staff.

④ Procurement predictions are inaccurate, exceeding the budget.

⑤ Accidents occur during transportation (traffic accidents causing casualties).

3. Market Uncertainty Risks

Market share is an important measure of a company's success. Most of the funds for e-commerce companies are used for product procurement and inventory, and the quantity of goods must be determined based on market share and sales volume. Analyze the choice of target markets through market size, status, trends, network consumer demand, situations, pricing, marketing strategies, promotional activities, product procurement, inventory management, safety stock levels, etc.

4. R&D Risks

What customers like in terms of style and variety may not necessarily appeal to customers, leading to product accumulation, putting the company's funds at risk of economic crisis due to incomplete varieties and specifications.

5. Production Uncertainty Risks

Product quality is key to the survival of a company, and the quality of products is an important factor in enhancing the company's reputation. The chain reaction where one consumer affects at least 20 surrounding consumers shows the importance of quality to customers. If there are quality issues with the product, the merchant must bear the responsibility.

6. Inventory Risk

① Accumulated inventory prevents capital circulation, lacking funds for restocking.

② Accumulated inventory occupies a certain amount of warehouse space, preventing the purchase of new goods.

7. Cost Control Risk

Analyze the financial status of the company mainly from capital investment, product cost analysis, period expenses, product sales profit, predicted cash flow statement, predicted income statement, predicted balance sheet, main business profit margin, total asset income statement, cost-expense profit margin, etc.

8. Cash Flow Risk

① Unsold inventory leads to inability to recover the proceeds.

② Unable to purchase new goods, resulting in product obsolescence and loss of customer purchasing desire.

③ Wages for workers cannot be resolved on schedule.

9. Competition Risk

In an era of fierce competition, it is necessary to clearly understand the various conditions of competitors, mainly comparing with competitors in terms of technology, product price, service, etc., to formulate competitive strategies, identifying strengths and weaknesses, enabling better adaptation to the development of the times.

10. Transaction Security Risk

① Data theft or destruction during network information transmission;

② Damage during transportation or incomplete carton packaging.

11. Policy Risk

In the 21st century, the development of e-commerce has become a technical means and method of economic activity in the information age and has entered the broadest application field of the Internet. Economic crises can also bring risks, as markets are unpredictable, and the government will make decisions to respond to market fluctuations, predicting government macro and micro policies.

12. Financial Risk

Since enterprises with loans have liabilities, there will inevitably be risks. At the beginning, it is necessary to reduce blindness and improve scientificity. When the enterprise's pre-tax and interest rate on capital is higher than the interest rate on borrowed funds, the profit obtained from using borrowed funds compensates for the interest and leaves a surplus, thereby increasing the profit on self-owned funds; otherwise, the enterprise needs to borrow funds to repay principal and interest, falling into financial risks, thus strictly controlling the proportion of fixed costs to sales revenue. Due to the uncertainty of the difference between the enterprise's pre-tax and interest rate on capital and the interest rate on borrowed funds, it is necessary to have a reasonable capital structure, maintain an appropriate level of debt, fully utilize borrowing operations to obtain financial benefits, and enhance profitability.

13. Management Risk (Including Personnel/Employee Turnover/Key Employee Dependence)

Investment is a business of managing talent. Initially, we build a management team with direct technology and experience to formulate feasible decisions for the company, execute the most efficient tasks, and invite personnel with various professional technical and management experiences to join and hold important positions. Due to employee turnover, it is necessary to have a reasonable employee hiring criterion, creating a sustainable and harmonious environment, and controlling emergencies.

14. Bankruptcy Risk

Due to increasingly fierce market competition, lack of management experience, low management quality, and single business operation methods, the market may not fully accept our products, leading to product stagnation in the online store, significantly increasing the risk of bankruptcy. Only by stimulating customer purchasing desires and making products unique can we secure a place in the market.

VIII. How to Deal with These Risks

1. Purchase goods from surrounding large wholesale markets. Purchasing goods from wholesale markets requires strong bargaining skills, striving to lower the wholesale price as much as possible. Also, establish good relationships with wholesalers, clarify the rules regarding returns and exchanges to avoid disputes later. Find uncommon or non-circulating products offline to discover the cheapest, highest quality, and stable sources of goods. Not only rely on one supplier; seek related unique products for sale and design new products for manufacturers to produce.

2. Purchase goods at extremely low prices and resell them online. Utilize the advantages of online sales to gain sufficient profits through geographical or temporal price differences. Therefore, regularly visit the market to closely monitor changes.

3. After shopping, notify both parties' contact information and conduct transactions according to agreed methods. You can choose face-to-face transactions or transactions through remittance and mail, monitoring the items en route.

4. Give sufficient attention to customer relationships, establish customer contact cards, and widely publicize positive opinions from customers.

5. Each marketing method has clear goals, informing customers or potential customers what to do next.

6. In the information age, use the internet to send regular updates about related products to customers, keeping them informed about the latest discounts, promotions, and new products.

7. Innovation always comes with failure, but in continuous innovative exploration, minimize risks and cultivate good psychological resilience.

8. Find the unique "selling points" of your online store.

9. Regularly send newsletters to customers. Use the email and contact information left by customers to send regular updates about discounted products, member-exclusive gifts, and thoughtful birthday presents for members.

1. Select products, determine market positioning, and lock onto target consumer groups.

2. Experiment, experiment, and experiment again (because you will learn many things in this process, accumulating bit by bit).

3. Learn value innovation. Selling products just by selling products won't make money; the money earned is only enough for daily expenses, also known as pocket money.

4. Be familiar with the rules of online trading platforms.

5. Grasp sales techniques and maintain customer relationships. Open for business, integrity as the foundation. Answer comments promptly and honestly, resolve buyers' doubts, and increase their trust. Online stores have low costs, require no complicated procedures, and generally offer more affordable prices. Some store owners also use methods like "discounts", "gifts", and "lotteries" to provide benefits. When doing business online, credit must be valued. Only with good credibility can more stable customers be gained. Some store owners also use methods like introducing star-rated customers, exchanging life experiences, and publishing random thoughts to turn their small stores into small communities, turning customers into friends.
