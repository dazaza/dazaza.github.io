Samuel Tam backs 'Sa基' for concealing marriage: it's not like he committed murder or arson (picture included)

by 5tn6dh on 2010-04-01 16:58:59

The Sun reported on March 31st; Alan Tam, known as the "Principal of the Music Scene," attended a football player recruitment event in Hong Kong yesterday (March 30th). Regarding the divorce announcement by Charmaine Sheh and Justin Cheng, Tam remarked that their decision to keep their marriage private was reasonable: “It’s not like they committed murder or arson.”

Justin Cheng was interviewed over the phone by a radio station yesterday, where he commented on his divorce with Charmaine Sheh: “Whether or not to make it public is the right of the parties involved. Personally, I don’t feel we did anything wrong.” When asked, “If you got married, would you make it public?” Cheng responded, “It depends on many factors. If it's appropriate, I would hope to make it public.”

Samuel Chan humorously mentioned wanting to take over Daniel Deng's job as the host of the Hit Songs Golden Melody Awards. Additionally, when Samuel Chan was asked about the marital changes between his friend Justin Cheng and Charmaine Sheh at an event yesterday (March 30th), he joked that he wasn't part of the "Crown Prince Party" and had never understood Justin Cheng's marital status. However, seeing his friend cry uncontrollably made him feel heartbroken.

Alan Tam previously inquired about Donghan Zheng, who highly praised his son Justin Cheng for being sensible. Tam stated, “The press conference has been made to look like a crime was committed, but there really isn't any need! If they didn't speak, surely they have their reasons. Privacy is a fundamental human right, so don't stifle it. It's not some unforgivable sin.” “They can choose not to talk, but isn't lying still wrong?” “There was no need to say anything at all. Everyone forced them to speak, which left them no choice but to lie! It's not like they did something morally reprehensible or committed murder or arson. Give them some space!” Alan Tam expressed that he wasn't very familiar with Charmaine Sheh and Justin Cheng. When mentioning his old friend and Justin Cheng's father, Donghan Zheng, he said, “I met him earlier during the BIG FOUR concert and asked if his son was sensible, and he said yes.”
