Among the ten severe environmental issues, the following are highlighted: Environmental pollution problems in Lishui Economic Development Zone's base fabric and synthetic leather industries; environmental pollution problems in Lishui Industrial Park; environmental pollution problems in Suichang Industrial Park; LED display screens; environmental pollution problems in Yunhe wooden toy industrial park; environmental pollution problems in Longquan industrial park; surface treatment industry environmental pollution problems in Nuoxi Economic Development Zone; stainless steel industry environmental pollution problems in Jingning County; old Xiu Xi River basin environmental pollution problems; Zhu Xin Xi River basin environmental pollution issues; and environmental pollution problems in the plastic reduction agriculture industry in Jingning.

Of these ten severe environmental issues, eight pertain to environmental pollution problems in development zones (industrial parks) below the provincial level. These eight development zones (agricultural parks) primarily face issues such as inadequate construction of environmental protection infrastructure, some enterprises discharging pollutants beyond standard levels, and wastewater and exhaust gas pollution. Among them, the environmental pollution problems in Lishui Economic Development Zone's base fabric and synthetic leather industries have been listed by the provincial government as one of the first batch of 11 key environmental issues under provincial supervision within the "811" three-year environmental protection action plan, with a requirement to "remove the hat" (resolve the issue) by the end of 2009. Meanwhile, as a province-wide environmental issue, the environmental problems in development zones (agricultural parks) have also been included in the list of 11 key environmental issues under provincial supervision. According to the requirements of the provincial government, cities should achieve the goal of resolving environmental disorder issues for development zones (industrial parks) by completing tasks involving one enterprise or less by the end of 2008, 50% completion by the end of 2009, and full resolution by the end of 2010.

The city has issued an "811" three-year environmental protection action plan, which will strengthen the construction of environmental infrastructure, strictly implement various environmental protection systems, increase supervision and evaluation efforts, and aim to resolve the ten key environmental issues by the end of 2010.
