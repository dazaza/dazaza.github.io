Introduction to the RG-13 Magnetic Drill (Magnetic Base Drill):

The RG-13 magnetic drill (magnetic base drill) is a popular compact and practical magnetic drill internationally. The magnetic base drill is specifically designed for twist drill bits, featuring a small size that makes it easy to carry, allowing workers to operate it more effortlessly.

Features of the RG-13 Magnetic Drill (Magnetic Base Drill):

• The main structure of the machine is made from die-cast aluminum alloy;

• Compact design with a small size and light weight (only 11kg), reducing labor intensity and saving working time;

• Optimized mechanical spindle design ensures smoother transmission;

• Equipped with a high-power motor for easier operation;

• High-density dual copper coils using German technology provide longer-lasting and more durable suction force;

• Power supply options available at purchase: 220V 50Hz/110V 60Hz;

• Complete set of accessories included, ready to use upon purchase;

• All components are domestically produced in China, reducing future maintenance costs.

The R series of Reajan magnetic drills meet international standards for high-quality magnetic drills, offering the best cost-performance ratio among domestic magnetic drills. The overseas brand name for Reajan magnetic drills is REAJAN, which enjoys a significant export share.
