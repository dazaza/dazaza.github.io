by asodlrp520 on 2008-03-13 12:51:10

Dear QQ Yinxin Users:

In order to thank you for your long-term support of QQ Yinxin, we have specially collaborated with China Mobile Shenzhen Branch to launch a series of activities called "Gold Service, Satisfaction 100":

Activity One: [Gold Service Free Greetings]

Event Time:

March 13, 2008 - April 11, 2008

Event Content:

During the event period, all Shenzhen QQ users or national QQ users who send Yinxin messages to Shenzhen mobile users only need to log in to the QQ Yinxin official website to have the chance to win free Yinxin sending opportunities provided by Shenzhen Mobile! No matter where your family and friends are, they can hear your love and blessings through the phone!

Every day during the event period, 8000 free opportunities will be given out until they run out.

[http://yinxin.qq.com]

Activity Two: Shenzhen Mobile [Gold Service Satisfaction 100] Reward Survey

Event Time:

March 13, 2008 - April 11, 2008

Event Content:

During the event period, all mobile users can participate in Shenzhen Mobile's reward survey and have the chance to win great prizes.

Prize Settings:

First Prize (1 winner): 50 Q Coins, 30 free Yinxin opportunities

Second Prize (5 winners): 10 Q Coins, 10 free Yinxin opportunities

Third Prize (10 winners): 5 Q Coins, 5 free Yinxin opportunities

Participation Award (50 winners): 2 free Yinxin opportunities

The list of winners will be announced on the official QQ Yinxin Q Bar after the event. Please fill in your personal information accurately for prize distribution. Users whose personal information is incorrect and leads to inability to distribute prizes will be deemed to have automatically abandoned the prize.

[http://yinxin.qq.com/survey/szm.shtml]

Activity Three: I Enter China Mobile

March 13, 2008 - April 11, 2008

Shenzhen Mobile users, other regional users are also welcome to participate.
