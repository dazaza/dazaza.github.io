What issues should be noted when installing the diesel engine of a garden sprinkler truck?

by yz61ok40 on 2012-03-05 12:50:17

The repaired diesel engine of Mu can be reinstalled on the car after debugging and inspection. If necessary, a brand new diesel engine can be installed on the car. The installation of the diesel engine should be carried out in the reverse order of disassembly, and the following points should be noted: 1. Before installing the diesel engine, check the integrity of the chassis and repair if there are cracks, deformation, loose rivets, rust, and paint peeling as needed. 2. Replace all the mounts of the diesel engine and tighten the fixing bolts under idle conditions for long-term use. 3. Assemble the fixed bolts on the clutch, transmission, and drive shaft according to the specified torque to ensure reliable power transmission of the diesel engine. 4. Replace the sealing gaskets and connect all exhaust, air, fuel, compressed air, coolant, and steering hydraulic oil lines to ensure smooth flow and no leakage. 5. Connect all operating mechanisms to ensure reliable connections and check in the normal driving position whether each control mechanism operates freely and whether the pedal free travel and working stroke meet the specifications; otherwise, readjust. 6. Ensure the electrical system works reliably, the starting device operates freely, and the instruments function properly. 7. After the car trial run, check the assembly situation again, make a full adjustment, and ensure reliable operation during the running-in period. 8. At the end of the running-in period, conduct a comprehensive inspection and adjustment of the entire vehicle, tighten the fixing bolts, and ensure everything is correct before normal operation.

When your car "whims" halfway: When driving, you may encounter situations where your car "whims." If a fault or obstacle occurs while driving and there are no spare parts available but you need to use it urgently, we must take some emergency repair measures. Here are some simple and feasible emergency repair methods:

1. Fuel tank damage: During use, if you find the fuel tank leaking, clean the leaking area and apply soap or bubble gum to temporarily seal it; using epoxy resin adhesive for repair will yield better results.

2. Fuel pipe rupture: If the fuel pipe ruptures, clean the ruptured area, apply soap, wrap with cloth strips or adhesive tape around the ruptured area, and tie tightly with wire, then apply another layer of soap.

3. Fuel pipe breakage: If the fuel pipe breaks, find a rubber or plastic tube that matches the diameter of the fuel pipe to connect. If the connection is not tight enough, tighten both ends with wire to prevent leaks.

4. Cylinder head sand hole causing oil or water leakage: Choose an appropriate size of electrician's fuse wire, gently hammer it into the sand hole to eliminate oil or water leakage.

5. Oil pipe joint leakage: If the engine oil pipe joint leaks due to poor sealing between the oil pipe bell mouth and the oil pipe nut, wrap cotton thread around the bottom edge of the bell mouth and tighten the oil pipe nut and the oil pipe joint; alternatively, chew bubble gum or maltose into a paste and apply it to the oil pipe nut seat opening, allowing it to dry and form a seal.

6. Inlet and outlet water hose rupture: For small ruptures, wrap the leaking area with a soap-coated cloth; for larger ruptures, cut off the damaged section, fit a bamboo or iron tube in the middle, and tie tightly with wire.

7. Fan belt breakage: You can secure the broken belt with wire or drive the car intermittently.

8. Screw hole stripping: Stripped screw holes may cause oil leakage or loose connecting rods, preventing work. Flatten the original screw with a hammer to increase its width on both sides and secure it again, but avoid multiple disassemblies. Repair during the next maintenance.

9. Diaphragm or fuel pump diaphragm rupture or breakage: Remove the pump and diaphragm, cut a piece of bakelite board, electric insulating bakelite, or plastic sheet to the original shape and size, saw and file it into shape, polish it, and install it.

10. Valve spring breakage: After the valve spring breaks, remove the broken spring and reverse the two broken pieces for use. Alternatively, find a 1mm thick iron sheet, cut it into a circle 1mm larger than the spring diameter, cut a central hole smaller than the spring diameter by 4mm, cut 4mm long slits every 6mm along the outer edge, fold every other slit to form double-sided spring seats, then reverse the spring and install it into the iron sheet groove for use.
