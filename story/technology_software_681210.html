Currently, in the entire e-commerce shopping websites, women's clothing occupies a huge share of the shopping market. Compared to the conservative consumption attitudes of male consumers, women's emotional recognition of online brands and their tolerant attitude towards products have continuously driven the popularity of women's websites. According to sales data, in 2008, the expected scale of online clothing purchases nationwide was 17.11 billion yuan, accounting for 6.3% of the total national retail volume, with a sales ratio of women's to men's wear at 4:1. The trend of market segmentation accompanied by the speed of market expansion has become the key to initiating the online clothing market in 2009. Now it's 2012, so there is still a considerable amount of time for the rapid development of the women's wear market and enormous potential for future development.

In fact, an online store is ultimately just a channel - one that enables smooth shopping, accurately collects customer needs, and can present on a single platform the equivalent product quantity of ten large malls. If apparel enterprises can sell large quantities of women's wear offline, then according to the current trend of online shopping development, perhaps soon we will see one or even multiple e-commerce platforms for women's shopping websites emerge. All concepts considered, if the selling price of online women's wear is lower than traditional stores, sales are greater than regular malls, and updates are faster than branded clothing, then this would signify success in the first stage.

So how can one create a successful women's shopping website?

Looking at foreign website operation models, the mainstream approach is a wide variety of styles, precise inventory, and superior service. The internet is also providing new characteristic services to female shoppers that traditional shopping cannot offer. This service reflects changes in how online retailers sell products to women. From traditional sales channels, it can be seen that for fashionable clothing, especially women's wear, many small clothing stores are the main channel for moving goods. This explains why so many small shops on Taobao can meet so much demand for clothing purchases, while simultaneously gathering a large number of unique, personalized clothing products, cultivating a large group of fans for online women's wear purchases.

The essence of online women's wear sales is the same as traditional channel women's wear sales - that is, new, fast, and diverse. New fashion styles, quick product line updates, and richly varied styles. These are the three principles that independent online direct sales platforms for women's wear must follow. Finding a unique market positioning, analyzing different user characteristics to satisfy shoppers from central-western regions and non-urban areas. Analyzing different consumer philosophies, different purchasing capabilities and behaviors, and providing products and services that can surprise and delight consumers.
