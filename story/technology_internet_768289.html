For the Double Eleven Battle, Taobao Launches an Insurance Product with 7% High Returns

by anonymous on 2013-11-16 20:08:23

GF Bank and GuoShou Everbright Life Insurance have both recently unveiled top-notch financial products with an annualized return of 7%, warming up for Taobao’s "Double 11" event.

“The detailed product descriptions are still being fine-tuned and will be released closer to November 11th.” On November 4th, a person from Taobao Finance told our reporter.

After a year of operation, Taobao Finance has now connected with 1 bank, 9 life insurance companies (wealth management type), 17 fund companies, and Yu'e Bao as the four major camps.

“There are too few banks, the fund companies just went online on the 1st of this month and were not adequately prepared, Yu'e Bao has the largest capital stock but also the highest output on activity days, so this time the main role is wealth management insurance products.” The above-mentioned Taobao person revealed that one of the most popular products has already garnered over 200,000 views, “It is expected to reach 400,000 people on the 11th.”

Interestingly, regardless of whether the insurance companies made substantial profits last "Double 11" or were on the sidelines watching, they all expressed to our reporter that they would not make any special publicity or preparations this year because "GuoShou Everbright's product offering a 7% return is too eye-catching."

On the 4th, GuoShou Everbright held a press conference in Shanghai. The management explained the source of the high returns, stating that the funding allocation for this universal insurance product includes bonds with yields between 5.8%-6%, as well as stock funds and specific high-yield bank wealth management plans within the regulatory requirements ratio; at the same time, the profits saved from channel costs are also "returned" to the policyholders.

“(Such high expected yield) we have been paying attention to, regarding the risk aspect, what kind of assets should be used to match short-term liabilities. Universal insurance products may have already been filed, but the settlement interest rate fluctuates according to the investment situation of the insurance company, no further filing with the regulator is needed. But we will conduct a more in-depth understanding of this product.” On the 4th, a relevant person from the CIRC told our reporter.

7% High Yield Sparks Controversy

“With such high returns, we can't achieve it, so there's no need to put much effort into Double 11.” An insider from a new type of insurance company whose 30% premium income comes from online sales channels said.

At the beginning of the month, GuoShou Everbright launched two universal-type wealth management products, "Hua Rui No. 2" and "Hua Rui No. 3" on its official Tmall store. Among them, "Hua Rui No. 2" is planned to be available for three days (November 11th-13th), capped at 1 billion yuan, with an expected annualized yield of 7%, and a guaranteed annualized settlement rate of 2.5% for the first three years. Starting price is 1000 yuan, no fees for withdrawal after one year (no initial fee, no policy management fee, no risk insurance fee).

Meanwhile, "Hua Rui No. 3" has an expected annualized yield of 6%, capped at 300 million yuan, available only on the 11th, with no fees for withdrawal after 180 days.

According to data provided by GuoShou Everbright, the total cap for these two products is 1.3 billion yuan. If realized as planned, it will exceed the total sales of 1.06 billion yuan through e-commerce channels from December last year to October this year.

As one of the first insurance companies to enter the Taobao finance platform, GuoShou Everbright occupies about 59% of the share. At the same time, 80% of its e-commerce channel performance comes from Taobao.

GuoShou Everbright's management stated that apart from allocating high-yield stock funds and specific bank wealth management products, "the cost savings from the e-commerce channel will also be used to reward policyholders."

“This kind of product is indeed a new change worth paying attention to this year. On one hand, channel costs are decreasing, on the other hand, the risks of short-term liabilities and such marketing methods, although there are no existing regulations explicitly restricting them, still have a significant impact and shock on the market. We need to further understand and pay attention to them.” The above-mentioned CIRC person said.

There are speculations in the industry that for this product with an expected yield of 7%, GuoShou Everbright has already prepared to cover the difference between actual investment returns and promised expected returns. However, this statement has not been officially confirmed.

In addition to GuoShou Everbright significantly increasing the expected returns, HongKang Life Insurance is also trying to develop investment-linked insurance products beyond universal insurance on e-commerce channels.

“Compared to universal insurance, investment-linked insurance can invest in a wider range of fields, and since there is no guarantee, the risk is relatively higher. But the investment-linked insurance product we reported to the CIRC clearly does not invest in stocks. Mostly in infrastructure, real estate, and bonds.” A person from HongKang Life Insurance revealed.

Large life insurance companies have also adjusted their strategies on internet channels. “We are relatively cautious about promoting investment returns, and indeed cannot even achieve 5% or 6%. So while others are busy with Double 11, our focus is preparing for next year's opening red.” Said a person from PICC Life Insurance.

Is Yu'e Bao About to Lose Its Popularity?

Through continuous business integration, Alibaba has unified the four major financial camps - Yu'e Bao, insurance wealth management, banks, and funds - under an independent financial division for overall coordination, avoiding competition among financial departments.

“We don't worry about Double 11, Taobao's traffic system has been tested enough times. The current focus of the division is to develop internet financial innovation products with influence even exceeding Yu'e Bao. Relying solely on Yu'e Bao is hard to last long, we need to innovate more products that are both risk-controlled and meet the financial management needs of Alibaba customers.” The above-mentioned Taobao person revealed that negotiations are ongoing with various types of financial institutions including banks, insurance, and funds.

By the end of September, the scale of funds in Yu'e Bao had reached 55.6 billion yuan, with cumulative account openings exceeding 16 million, and cumulative subscription scale surpassing 130 billion yuan.

“A large portion of Yu'e Bao's funds will definitely be used for shopping and purchasing other financial products during the Double 11 event, and the settlement department has already made adequate preparations for this. For Tianhong Fund, the risk is still within predictable and controllable limits.” The person continued.

And for the fund companies that just went online four days ago, sales are relatively quiet. “The CSRC suddenly approved it, and both us and the fund companies still need some time to prepare, so the fund camp will not become the main player in Double 11.”

Currently, only GF Bank is connected to the Taobao finance platform, planning to launch bank wealth management products with yields ranging from 5.5%-7% on the 11th, all starting at 50,000 yuan. Therefore, wealth management insurance products are very likely to become the "main player" in this round of Double 11 financial management.
