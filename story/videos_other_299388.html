Free Bluetooth with WiFi Nokia N97 Nini new low price

by murxkh179 on 2009-12-08 20:26:10

By Yang Xuetao [Beijing Market] The launch of the Nokia N97 Mini (specifications, pricing, images, forum) has taken the miniaturization of handheld computers to a whole new level. Today, the official version of the N97 mini reappeared at the dealer "Tianxi Universal Mobile Phone Network" with its latest price set at 4550 yuan (official white deluxe version). In addition to dual batteries, single charger, headphones, and data cable, an additional Bluetooth headset is also included as a gift. Friends who like this phone might want to consider it.

The Nokia N97 mini maintains a high degree of consistency in body style with the N97, but its more compact size makes it much lighter than the N97. With dimensions of 113×52×14.2mm, it is easy for even female users to handle well. Moreover, the device features a built-in 3.2-inch TFT display screen. Although the screen is smaller compared to its predecessor's 3.5-inch screen, the color quality remains at the 16 million color level.

In terms of network support, the Nokia N97 mini is a China Unicom WCDMA 3G mobile phone. Equipped with a front-facing camera, it can easily achieve video call functionality. The addition of WiFi internet connectivity also enhances the networking advantages of the device. Like other Nokia touch-screen smartphones, the N97 mini runs the powerful SymbianOS S60 V5 operating system, offering rich third-party software resources for users to choose from and install. It also includes a 5-megapixel Carl Zeiss certified camera that supports auto-focus, while the addition of dual LED flash allows users to take clear photos even in poor lighting conditions.
