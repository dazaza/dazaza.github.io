Son puts up sign on roadside to look for his father who is working as a laborer (Picture)

by iface001 on 2011-04-06 15:04:19

Features

According to news yesterday, although the weather was cold, it did not hinder Li Limin's determination to find his father. From Antu to Changchun, he stopped and rested along the way, all in an effort to find his father who had been missing for two months. He stood from morning to night with a sign hanging on his chest advertising for information about his missing father, but there was still no news of him.

It was not until around September 16th last year that people who worked with his father returned to town and said that his father had taken a train home with over 200 yuan on September 5th. Hearing this news, Li Limin's family became worried as his father, Li Qingjiang, had not returned.

At around 2 pm yesterday, near the Culture Square in Changchun city, a man with a sign around his neck searching for his father attracted quite a number of passers-by. "Wanted: male, 57 years old, height about 1.72 meters, a long face, a scar on the forehead..." At first glance, passers-by thought he was a street beggar, but upon closer inspection, they realized that the man was searching for his father who had been lost for nearly two months with his brother-in-law.

According to Li Limin's introduction, in August last year, his father went to Antu to work with people from the town, so he took his ID card and some money and left from his hometown, Heilinzishen Town in Gongzhuling City, to Antu.

Male, 57 years old, height about 1.72 meters, a long face, a scar on the forehead, wearing a black top, black pants, and gray cloth shoes when he left...

After learning about this matter, Li Limin and his brother-in-law took a train to Antu to search for his father, but they did not find him. Thus, in order to find his father, they first reported the case to the police, then called many media outlets seeking help, yet there is still no news to this day.

To be able to find his father faster, Li Limin hung a large sign on his chest and followed the route his father took by train, stopping at each station to look for his father. Antu, Dunhua, Huangnihe, Jiaohe, Jiangbei, Jiutai, all the way to Changchun. Although he knew this method would not be very effective, Li Limin's urgent desire to find his father moved every passer-by who watched him, "All possible methods have been tried, I can only do this and hope to find my father".

For the sake of finding his father, Li Limin has forgotten the cold.
