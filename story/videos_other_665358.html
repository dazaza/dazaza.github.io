Adding luster to business operations: 3 essential tips for website construction selected for corporate websites. Corporate website building has gradually become a fashionable trend under the wave of informatization. Establishing its own website benefits a company by enhancing its image, enabling network communication capabilities, promoting the company and its products through the vast outlet of the internet, and can also include e-commerce functions. So, what common knowledge is needed for website construction? Today, IDC commentary website has compiled some small pieces of knowledge for everyone's reference.

First, types of corporate website building.

1. Basic Information Type: Mainly targeting customers, industry professionals, or general browsers, focusing on introducing the basic information of the enterprise to help establish the corporate image; it can also appropriately provide news or knowledge information within the industry.

2. E-commerce Type: Mainly targeting suppliers, customers, or consumer groups of the company's products (services), providing services or transactions directly related to the company's business scope, or services or transactions serving the business; such websites can be said to be in a middle stage of e-commerce. Due to differences in industry characteristics and the depth and breadth of corporate investment, their level of e-commerce may range from relatively basic service support and product lists to more advanced online payments.

3. Multimedia Advertising Type: Mainly targeting customers or consumer groups of the company's products (services), focusing on promoting the core brand image or main products (services) of the enterprise. This type is more like a print advertisement or television advertisement in terms of purpose and actual presentation techniques compared to ordinary websites, so calling this type of website "multimedia advertising" is more appropriate.

4. Product Display Type: Mainly targeting demanders, showcasing detailed information about its products as well as the company's strength. Providing the most comprehensive introduction to product prices, production, and detailed descriptions. The primary purpose of this type of corporate website is to showcase its products in the most direct and effective way. While paying attention to brands and images, it is also important to emphasize the introduction of your products.

Second, choosing a suitable website service provider.

1. Strong graphic design capabilities, interconnected artistry. Website content should be rich and professional, and page design must reflect the company's business philosophy and strength, thereby enhancing the company's competitive advantage in the same industry.

2. Fast access speed, stable service, and supporting interconnectivity. According to surveys, people usually only have a tolerance of seven seconds when waiting for web pages to open. Many visitors often turn to other websites due to slow webpage opening speeds, so the browsing speed of the website is crucial.

3. Try to choose professional website builders with strong capabilities, good reputation, and guaranteed follow-up services. Professional network companies can provide professional websites that meet demands. Choose quality over low price, and make careful decisions for the benefit of the enterprise.

Third, matters to pay attention to after building the corporate website.

1. Domain ownership issues. Domain ownership issues need to be discussed with the website construction service provider. Some unethical service providers register domains for enterprises but keep the ownership in their own hands. If the enterprise wants to transfer virtual space, the website will be directly shut down. Therefore, the enterprise needs to confirm with the service provider whether the domain username and password can belong to the enterprise.

2. If renting a virtual host, the space belongs to the enterprise itself. The enterprise needs to retrieve: 1. FTP username; 2. FTP password; 3. IP address; 4. Control panel username; 5. Control panel password; 6. Control panel address; if the service provider does not plan to give the source code, Hong Kong hosting, then the above information will not be disclosed.

3. If the website program has an administration backend, then the management address, username, and password need to be requested from the website construction service provider.

Conclusion: If enterprises neglect the issue of their own website construction and only conduct exploratory operations with less website information and technical content, then such a website cannot bring added value during the operation of the enterprise. Therefore, enterprises need to continuously update and enrich their own websites to play a greater role.
