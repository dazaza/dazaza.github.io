Spicy female teacher posts self-exposed humorous quotes, sparking heated discussions (picture)

by wenkuon19 on 2011-12-06 14:47:10

Spicy Teacher Deng Rui, Video Conference System

"Wow, the two of you are perfectly in sync with your little tricks during class. Let's call you the TWINS, but the chubby version." "How do you dare to wear NIKE? The NIKE logo is a tick, but you keep making crosses. If you make another mistake, I'll let you wear TeBu, because TeBu is all about crosses..."

These spicy and humorous sentences, did they give you a refreshing feeling? They came from the mouth of the fashionable version of "spicy teacher," Deng Rui. During the National Day holiday, she posted her compiled "spicy quotes" on the Fourth City forum, sparking heated discussions among netizens... Compared to the "spicy teachers" in Japanese dramas like "GTO" and "Great Teacher Onizuka," Deng Rui's spiciness does not lie in "martial arts," but in her quotes!

"Spicy Quotes" Replay

The opening of the post was full of confidence: "Being a teacher, especially an elementary school teacher, is tiring, but I always find joy in life... Nowadays, students have strong personalities. They evaluate teachers based on their clothing and language. They like teachers who are fashionable, can talk about non-mainstream topics, music, and vague feelings. So, here I am."

Next were 14 quotes that made people chuckle, such as: "Please don't look at me with those dreamy eyes pitifully. (Sleepy, actually, the kids work hard)." "Please don't strike such exaggerated poses during class. (Sleeping pose)" "No matter how you hide, I can still see your big teeth moving around. Are your big teeth diamond-encrusted? (During class, children secretly eat while covering their mouths with books or hands)" "Don't try to escape my gaze because my eyes are only on you. YOU ARE STILL IN MY EYES. (When a child is caught doing something small, they won't dare to look me in the eye)"

These were all spicy words used by Deng Rui when students were distracted during class.

Among them, the most popular quote among netizens was the 10th one: "If a student makes the same mistake again, I will say to him with a slightly crying tone: Look, how do you dare to wear NIKE? The NIKE logo is a tick, but you keep making crosses. If you make another mistake, I'll let you wear TeBu. Look, TeBu is all about crosses..."

Many netizens replied with flowers, while some expressed concern for Deng Rui: "A teacher with such a bold personality, has the principal talked to you?"

This "spicy quotes" also sparked exchanges among teacher netizens. "babyq87320" said: "I just graduated this year, and I'm also a primary school teacher now. Every time I encounter students disrupting the class discipline, I only use one trick against them, which is standing punishment... In the future, I will try your methods." "yuqian" then said: "If I were like that... the kids would have gone wild long ago."

"Spicy Teacher" Real-life Show

Small in stature, fashionably dressed, with a cherubic face, sparkling big eyes, fast speech, and quick thinking—this is 24-year-old Deng Rui. Yesterday morning, facing the reporter's interview, she smiled and said: "This thunder struck big, and it came with lightning."

"Spicy Teacher" Deng Rui comes from a family of educators. "The blood of a teacher flows in my veins," Deng Rui said. She is from Anyue, where her grandfather, mother, younger aunt, uncle, and even her father once were all teachers. Since childhood, she has liked children and dreamed of becoming a teacher. After graduating from the English Education major, she worked at an educational training institution in Chengdu and started teaching at Chengdu Wuhou Foreign Language School last year. Now, she teaches lower-grade English and higher-grade bilingual classes. Her life and teaching are both filled with humor. Someone suggested she collect her classic phrases and compile them into a record, leading to this post.

Deng Rui said that there are actually many "spicy teachers" around her. They are friends with the children, dress fashionably, and are lively and creative. Usually, some students hide behind pillars and suddenly jump out to scare her. The students also tease her about having pimples on her face. To become a teacher liked by the students, she dresses in bright and fashionable clothes according to the children's aesthetic preferences, discusses Jay Chou and Super Girls with them, sometimes self-deprecates to close the teacher-student relationship, and admires the students when they are excellent. These quotes are not meant to be funny; she integrates education into every aspect of daily life. For example, discussing why Yu Ke Wei was eliminated, the children said it was because she lied about her age. At that moment, she took the opportunity to tell the children that lying is wrong and one should be honest.

Various Opinions

"I like Teacher Deng, I like her classes," said six-year-old Xiao Xin. When asked why she likes Teacher Deng, she naively answered: "Because she likes me." Xiao Xin is a first-grade student. Her father, Mr. Yang, frankly admitted that his interactions with Teacher Deng are not yet extensive, but he has seen Teacher Deng's efforts through his child's progress. He said that before starting primary school, Xiao Xin had never been exposed to English. Although they had tried to teach her some English before entering primary school, she strongly resisted it. After more than a month of school, he happily noticed her changes—she loves English and her spoken English is very fluent.

Colleague Teacher Yang said, "Ruirui works efficiently, is kind-hearted, and takes her work seriously. She likes to joke around and speaks humorously."

"Our school encourages this kind of teacher," said Principal Fan, the executive principal. The school's educational philosophy is diversified teaching, encouraging students to come into contact with teachers with unique personalities. Teachers should not only be gentle and elegant but should display various personalities. Students will meet people with different personalities in society in the future—gentle and elegant ones, outgoing ones, introverted ones... Allowing them to interact with different teachers now is precisely to prepare them for adapting to society in the future.
