Love is really terrifying. Descendants of Confucius critique the epic film 'Confucius': the soul is too weak - Chinese film.

by letaoeoe on 2011-06-05 23:09:20

In the scene of suppressing the rebellion at Wu Zitai, Confucius applied fire attack tactics to defeat the enemy. At this time, Confucius stood high with a sword and broke through the enemies. Confucius stood on a high platform, vigorously beating the drum to command the soldiers to pour boiling oil from a giant cauldron... Regarding this scene, another expert Tang Shaorong expressed that he had some difficulty understanding it. The fierce fire burning and killing so many people was very cruel, which was inconsistent with Confucius' spirit of benevolence. Moreover, Confucius personally beat the drum to boost the morale, which was hard to comprehend.

Su Shixiong stated that Confucius once said that a gentleman should avoid four things: no guessing, no certainty, no prejudice, and no self-centeredness. This was also the realm of Confucius' entire life. Judging from this perspective, Confucius would not have died in such a tragic way as depicted in the film but should have passed away peacefully. Confucius' final moments should not be portrayed in a pessimistic state. He also mentioned that Confucius once said, "If I could live a few more years, I would study the Book of Changes at fifty and make no great mistakes." In his later years, Confucius developed a strong interest in the Zhouyi (Book of Changes) and hoped to live longer to complete his study and research on the Zhouyi. However, these aspects were not reflected at all in the film, indicating that the director was too hasty and did not release enough energy. Confucius lacked soul and was too thin in the portrayal.

Problem One: Zi Gong's appearance has a logical issue.

Problem Two: Confucius' death was too pessimistic.

However, Kong Xianyu also pointed out problems existing in the film. After the campaigns of destroying three cities, the meeting at Jiagu, the battle between Qi and Lu, and suppressing the rebellion at Wu Zitai, Zi Gong appeared. But according to my impression, the book records that Zi Gong appeared during Confucius' travels around the various states.

Yesterday, the epic movie "Confucius" premiered nationwide. Before its release, the film had continuously sparked doubts among experts. After its premiere, people's evaluations of this film were mixed. Yesterday, our newspaper invited Kong Xianyu, the 72nd generation descendant of Confucius living in Chengdu, and five experts from Chengdu Confucius Institute to watch the film at Wanda Cinema Property Store. Regarding the problems existing in the film, the experts expressed their respective opinions. The experts believed that "Confucius" succeeded by 30% and failed by 70%.

Tang Shaorong indicated that the ending of "Confucius" was too rushed without reflecting the process of Confucius traveling around the various states and establishing schools, which was an important part of Confucius' life. The film depicted four battles, each with grand scenes. Regarding these battle scenes, Tang Shaorong claimed that Confucius was a great person, so there was no need to arrange so many battle scenes. With such a large investment, the depiction was overly exaggerated.

Regarding the problems existing in the film, another expert Su Shixiong expressed that Confucius' life was divided into two major stages: the first stage was the political career, and the second stage was the process of education, which was the stage of Confucius' humanity elevation. This part was precisely not reflected in the film, indicating that the director did not understand Confucius deeply and failed to grasp the essence, still lacking maturity.

Kong Xianyu expressed that as a descendant of Confucius, she was deeply moved after watching this film. Overall, this film was quite good. In the film, there was such a scene: in the cold winter, Confucius and his disciples passed by a frozen river, but the ice broke, and Yan Hui along with a carriage of books fell into the water. Yan Hui in the icy water continued to retrieve the books from the bottom of the water multiple times until he froze to death. It was said that Chow Yun-fat was moved to tears by the scene of Yan Hui's death. Kong Xianyu said that she cried when she saw this moment. Some people said this part was too sentimental, but in her view, it was precisely an extraordinary humanized expression.

Problem Three: Fire attack was cruel, Confucius should not have beaten the drum.

Deputy Dean Cao Wulin of Chengdu Confucius Institute expressed that from the experts' perspective, "Confucius" succeeded by 30% and failed by 70%. Hu Mei was an exceptionally bold woman, but her foundation was too shallow. Additionally, the dialogue between Nanzi and Confucius was extremely unsuccessful. Confucius did not dare to face Nanzi's temptation and provocation. Confucius could turn devils into saints, and virtuous ladies are the objects of gentlemen's pursuit... This made the experts very angry. The film did not highlight the theme and failed to show that Confucius was a great educator. According to feedback from Chengdu's major cinema chains, the heat of "Confucius" on its opening day in Chengdu was clearly less than that of "Avatar". Some female audiences born in the 1980s explicitly stated that they did not like the film, while some male audiences fell asleep during the latter part. Female audiences mostly found the latter half uninteresting, while male audiences mostly found the earlier half engaging.
