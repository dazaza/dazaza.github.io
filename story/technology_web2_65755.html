Want to publish a book? It's very simple, just print it online - Chao Yin Su.

by bugbear on 2008-04-28 11:37:27

Can you really print books? Are they the same as the ones outside?

Without a doubt, these are all true. Tens of thousands of users have already experienced the effects of POD (print on demand). In fact, abroad, personal book printing has long formed a mature market, while domestically, due to cost issues, it has been difficult to promote. Now, ChaoyinSu collaborates with printing manufacturers in Europe and America, enabling everyone to own their own book through very few steps and a short amount of time.

Is it possible to print just one copy?

Completely possible. Through scientific management, we provide the best service for every user, and can certainly meet your need for printing from just one copy.

Can I really print for free?

Yes. To quickly promote the website and allow more people to understand and enjoy ChaoyinSu's services, we have held a free book printing event. Our free book printing event will end at the end of September, and there will be不定期free book printing events in the future. Free book printing is limited to original works, and each user can only print one book. For specific event details, please refer to the event announcement.

Can I print someone else's work?

Of course you can. We are professional printers, just like you can freely print someone else's work at home, these are all your personal rights.

Is the act of printing books legal?

Completely legal. Just like you can freely print works at home, we provide printed materials, not publications.

Will the copyright of the work I submit still belong to me? Will you show it to others?

Of course, the copyright still belongs to you, we are merely printers. We will not disseminate your work in any form. With your permission, we will only display the cover and title of your work on the website, and will not provide downloads or browsing.
