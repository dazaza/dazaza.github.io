Carefully analyze the problems that are likely to occur in the group buying process.

by beetgk2958 on 2012-02-11 10:23:18

On January 21st, as the last employee happily received his/her goods from Luo Song, the person in charge of Taoyangxiang website, the group-buying event held jointly by Henan Branch of Construction Bank and Taoyangxiang website for a seven-day "Golden Dragon Dancing for New Spring Festival" group-buying event for bank employees came to a successful conclusion. It is reported that this event started on January 9th, lasted for seven days, completed six phases of group buying and one flash sale with more than ten thousand participants. Meanwhile, in order to ensure that customers were not affected by the suspension of logistics courier companies during the Spring Festival travel rush, Luo Song, the person in charge of Taoyangxiang website, personally drove his private car, accompanied by a rented truck, traveling over three thousand miles along the Jingguang Line and Longhai Line to more than ten cities, delivering the group-buying goods of online employees of the entire Henan branch of the Construction Bank to each customer.

Deputy General Manager of Xinyang Branch of Construction Bank, Chai Jie, said that in order to boost the morale of all employees and assist in achieving a good start for the marketing season, the provincial branch decided to carry out the "Golden Dragon Dancing for New Spring Festival, Seven Days of Group Buying" employee group-buying event on Taoyangxiang website, presenting a substantial gift for the spring festival to all employees. He said: "In order to cooperate with the provincial branch's group-buying event, the Party Committee of Xinyang Branch paid high attention, not only providing strong assistance in terms of personnel and technology but also providing strong financial support. General Manager Zhang Weihong specially approved two hundred thousand yuan to support this province-wide employee online group-buying event, injecting a strong stimulant into the successful hosting of this event!" A responsible person from the Electronic Banking Department of Xinyang Branch said that the department fully participated in the testing of the group-buying process on Taoyangxiang website, carefully analyzing potential issues in the group-buying process, leaving no blind spots, no loopholes, and no regrets, laying solid groundwork for the smooth operation of the group-buying event. Relevant personnel introduced that from January 9th until the end of the event, according to statistics from the provincial branch and Xinyang branch, a total of 689 employees registered on the Construction Bank's exclusive group-buying website on Taoyangxiang, with over ten thousand page views.

A staff member of Taoyangxiang, Xiao Liu, told reporters that the employee group-buying event on Taoyangxiang had been long planned. Since May 2011, when Xinyang Branch of Construction Bank and Taoyangxiang began their strategic cooperation, the Electronic Banking Department of Xinyang Branch has held multiple discussions with Taoyangxiang website to prepare for the group-buying event, enhancing the website's reputation, optimizing the group-buying process, and building an electronic payment platform, upgrading existing servers and other basic equipment for future province-wide group-buying activities, and preparing talent, customer base, and product inventory.

It was learned that in September 2011, Xinyang Branch and Taoyangxiang jointly held the launch ceremony of the online payment cooperation between Construction Bank and Taoyangxiang at Sunshine Hotel. During the National Day holiday in October 2011, Xinyang Branch used Taoyangxiang Construction Bank's exclusive group-buying website as a platform to conduct the "Welcoming National Day, Celebrating Birthday" employee group-buying event within the scope of Xinyang Branch, accumulating experience for this group-buying event. After the group-buying and flash sale events, the organizers of Xinyang Branch and Taoyangxiang website encountered a problem: all logistics companies stopped accepting business, and the goods could not be sent out. What if they couldn't deliver the discounted goods to all employees before the New Year? Could it still be called a New Year goods group-buying?

In response, the Party Committee of Xinyang Branch required that the event organizers must ensure timely delivery of all goods without error! The organizers of Xinyang Branch and Taoyangxiang website, after consultation, considering the delivery cost and time limit, could only rent a box truck to deliver the goods to each second-level branch!

On January 16th, Luo Song, the person in charge of Taoyangxiang website, took the initiative to shoulder the responsibility, driving his private car accompanying the rented box truck, traveling over three thousand miles along the Jingguang Line and Longhai Line to more than ten cities, completing the delivery of all goods within two days and two nights! During this period, the delivery fleet split into three routes, braving the wind and snow, tirelessly aiming to deliver the goods to each employee in time, with the fire in their hearts burning fiercely! On January 18th, the delivery fleet returned home, everyone too exhausted to stand, sleeping for a whole day and night!

On January 21st, as the last employee happily received the discounted goods, the "Golden Dragon Dancing for New Spring Festival, Seven Days of Group Buying" employee group-buying event on Taoyangxiang website came to a successful conclusion.

A responsible person from the provincial branch said that this group-buying event was the first true online payment group-buying event in our province, truly enriching the connotation of Construction Bank's electronic banking application, presenting a substantial gift for the Spring Festival! "For future Construction Bank events with larger scale and longer duration, this event accumulated valuable experience and achievements!"
