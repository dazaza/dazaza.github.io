If cosmetics are essential for women, then perfume can be said to be the taste of women. Of course, it is not to say that perfume is only suitable for women, it is also suitable for men because perfume also shows the charm and fashion sense of men, and more and more men are using perfume. Because perfume contains mysterious power.

Where should perfume be applied on the human body?

Since fragrance will evaporate with the increase of temperature, in order to better highlight the characteristics of perfume, try to apply it on parts of the body with higher body temperature and pulse.

Hair: A place where even sensitive skin can be sprayed without worry. Especially when applying a small amount of perfume on longer hair, the fragrance will drift with the wind. Behind the ear: A commonly mentioned place, but it evaporates easily, so it is suitable for high concentration Parfum or Eau de Parfum, lightly applied. Neck: Due to the blockage of hair and head from direct sunlight, applying a small amount of high concentration perfume is enough. Chest (left side): Applying above the heart has a better effect, but since it is the easiest place for your nose to smell, the amount should be properly controlled. Inner elbow: Suitable for spraying spray perfume lightly on both elbows, but be careful during seasons with strong sunlight. Wrist: Another best place for fragrance retention, with the pulse, the fragrance will gradually diffuse to the surroundings, applying on the ankle has the same effect. Waist: Lightly apply refreshing and gentle perfumes like Toilette or Cologne. But if you are eating, it is best to use a lower position. For people with sensitive skin, you can spray perfume on underwear, handkerchiefs, trouser hems, and the previously mentioned hair, allowing the fragrance to spread with the movement of the body. Underarms and sweat glands are forbidden areas for perfume, otherwise, the mixed smell of your chosen perfume and sweat will not only fail to attract people but also "scare" them away from a distance. Do not spray perfume on white clothes, jewelry, and leather goods, as the alcohol component in perfume can easily pollute the quality of these items, or even leave stains.

Several aspects where men should not use perfume:

1. Men who smoke should not use perfume, as the strange smell mixed with cigarette smoke will drive everyone around away.

2. Men using perfume should avoid being too concentrated or excessive, as those who pass by leaving a gust of fragrance can make people feel nauseous.

3. Women using men's perfume are considered to have personality, but if men use women's perfume, they will be questioned if their brains are malfunctioning, especially sweet fragrances which are least suitable for men.

4. Men who wear perfume must not wear sandals barefoot, otherwise, it would be like wearing sneakers with a suit, making it hard for people to get used to.

5. Men using perfume should avoid using too much hair oil or fragrant hair gel, mousse, as the fragrance on the body and head mixed together can be "vulgar".

6. Men with dusty faces should not "reapply" perfume, it is better to keep the "original flavor". If a man covered in dust with a strong fragrance appears in front of you, you might not help but doubt your eyes and nose.
