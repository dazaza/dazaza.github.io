Yang Lelé's plastic surgery exposed? Hunan female anchors embroiled in “plastic surgery scandal”?

by gogole111 on 2007-05-31 07:36:10

Yang Leluo (file picture) (reported by Ding Hong of Stars Online) -- when pictures of Zhang Zilu were mocked online and compared to "Sister Lotus," some netizens pointed out a few years ago in the Sichuan dialect drama "School's Out, Be Strong" that: "Is that Yang Leluo? So high cheekbones, so wide face, eyes don't look the same. It seems like Yang Leluo has had plastic surgery..."

Stars Online reporters had interacted with a plastic surgery doctor friend not long ago. Without asking, it was fine, but upon inquiry, it was discovered that at least three female hosts from Hunan were suspected of having plastic surgery.

Bar Discussions Questioning Yang Leluo's Appearance

In one of Yang Leluo's Tieba forums, a netizen commented: "The Chongqing station replayed 'School's Out, Be Strong' recently. I noticed someone who looked very similar to Yang Leluo, then checked the cast list and really saw Yang Leluo's name. Compared to now, it was simply heaven and hell! The old her looked both dull and rustic, while the current her is as beautiful as a fairy."

Subsequently, someone questioned: "Is Hu Rong Yang Leluo? The cheekbones are so high, and she doesn't look anything alike." "The eyes also don't look the same. It seems like Yang Leluo has had plastic surgery."

The reporter found a photo of Yang Leluo from this TV series, and it indeed matched the description given by netizens—this photo was especially prominent.

Since Yang Leluo transferred to Hunan TV, she has been presented as impeccable, elegant, and beautiful, which contrasts greatly with her image in Ouyang Fenqiang's 2000 production "School's Out, Be Strong."

Coincidentally, before discovering this post, the reporter learned during interactions with a plastic surgery doctor friend that many female hosts from Hunan have undergone plastic surgery.

Bone grinding, double eyelids, porcelain teeth

The plastic surgery doctor told Stars Online reporters: looking at two photos taken seven years apart, Yang Leluo's face became narrower, and there is no longer a sense of jowls—normally, viewed from the angle of the nose, there should be a curve, but Yang Leluo's current facial features appear too perfect, losing their natural original shape, almost flawless.

According to this doctor, Yang Leluo underwent plastic surgery in areas such as grinding down her cheekbones, enhancing her double eyelids, exposing smiles showing seven porcelain teeth. Generally, people don't have such white teeth; ordinary teeth aren't that bright unless they undergo porcelain veneers or porcelain crowns.

Plastic Surgery Completed Before 2004

The reporter also found a passport photo of Yang Leluo from 2004. At that time, it had been four years since she filmed "School's Out, Be Strong." By then, Yang Leluo's appearance was already quite similar to what it is today.
