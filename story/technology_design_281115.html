Hengtong Hydraulic Oil Press          The 6YY-230 type Korean hydraulic oil press is a product introduced to the market after improvements based on Korean technology, and has received consistent praise from users. It is suitable for oilseeds such as sesame, walnuts, and pine nuts. Each loading is 7-8 kilograms, and it only takes 6-10 minutes to fully press the oil. This hydraulic oil press equipment has low power consumption and low noise. Customers can come at any time to have their oil pressed, ensuring they consume safe and reliable oil.

The 6YY-230 type Korean hydraulic oil press is a new product developed by integrating China's rural traditional oil pressing techniques. Among the existing similar products, it features four major characteristics:

A. Multi-functional with wide application scope.

B. Hydraulic system completed jointly by gear pump and three-plunger pump, reaching high pressure up to 70 kg/cm², increasing pressure by 27%, and improving oil yield by 5%.

C. Transmission mechanism uses bevel gears, crankshaft, and connecting rod mechanism, consuming less energy, having high transmission efficiency, and operating smoothly.

D. Sealing elements are manufactured using advanced technology, effectively avoiding leakage caused by increased oil temperature and thinned oil quality.

The oil produced by this machine has pure quality, fragrant taste, and an oil yield of up to 98% of the oil content in the pressed material. The machine is easy to operate, simple to learn, safe and reliable to use, with a high return on investment, making it an ideal wealth-building business project for farmers.

Applicable oilseeds: Sesame, pine nuts, sunflower seeds, etc.
