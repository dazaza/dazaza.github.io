Ultimate Three Kingdoms Full Series Plot Introduction:

Guan Yu and Zhang Fei are high school students, but due to their excessive love for chivalry, they have been expelled from twenty-four schools, known as the "twenty-four schools." Just when the two are feeling desperate after being expelled again, a man with large earlobes suddenly appears. He introduces himself as Liu Bei, styled Xuande, a fallen noble who often changes schools because he can't afford the tuition. Liu Bei proposes a plan: since he has noble blood, he can attend the best noble school in the country, the "Donghan Academy," but the tuition is too expensive, and only royal relatives can enroll. Zhang Fei, being wealthy, says he can help pay the tuition for everyone. Liu Bei replies that as long as the money issue is resolved, everything can be solved. He suggests that the three of them become sworn brothers, making all of them royal relatives so they can enroll. The three then go to a beautiful and meaningful place to swear brotherhood. But just as they finish saying, "We don't seek to be born on the same day, but we seek to die on the same day," four people from the Jin timeline—Xiu, Wang DONG, Ding Xiaoyu, and Wang Arthur—accidentally travel through a time portal to the Yin timeline. Due to Wang DONG accidentally dropping a coin, which sets off a chain reaction causing a rock to fall, the rock ends up killing Liu Bei! Guan Yu, committed to his promise, wants to follow his elder brother and die on the same day. Zhang Fei tries to stop him and knocks him unconscious. They soon discover that Liu Bei is not dead, just seriously injured and unconscious. After consulting with DONG, Xiaoyu, and Arthur, they decide to leave Xiu in the Yin timeline as a substitute (since Xiu's counterpart in the Yin timeline is Liu Bei). Reluctantly, Xiu agrees to maintain the order of the timeline, while the other three take the severely injured Liu Bei back to the Jin timeline for treatment. When Guan Yu wakes up, he finds that the fake Liu Bei (Xiu) is alive and is very happy. Zhang Fei is also glad that they don’t have to die together. Thus, the three finally arrive at Donghan Academy, where Guan Yu and Zhang Fei believe they can achieve great things.

The three hope to graduate peacefully from Donghan Academy, but due to its fame, it attracts attacks from Huangjin High School and Hedong High School (where Dong Zhuo is the principal), putting Donghan Academy in constant danger. Master Zuo Ci even designs an 'Eight Gates Golden Lock Array' at the school gate to prevent students from attending classes. Naturally, the three take the responsibility to break the array. However, breaking the Eight Gates Golden Lock Array is not easy, and even Liu Bei (Xiu) and Xiaoqiao get trapped in the array, facing life-threatening dangers. Therefore, Guan Yu and Zhang Fei, following the advice of student council president Cao Cao, find Ma Chao, an extremely resilient and cheerful young man, and his good friend Huang Zhong, an infallible archer, to help break the array. Additionally, they enlist Zhao Yun, a super handsome youth who has always been at odds with them, to rescue Liu Bei (Xiu) and Xiaoqiao. During the process of breaking the array, Guan Yu saves Zhao Yun's life by using his arm to block an arrow during a critical moment, resolving the previous animosity between Zhao Yun and the other two. From then on, Zhao Yun calls the other four brothers and works together with them to break the Eight Gates Golden Lock Array.

After breaking the array and making new brothers, they thought they could finish high school smoothly. However, on one side, Huangjin High School is eyeing the resources of Donghan Academy, and on the other side, Principal Dong Zhuo of Hedong High School, Student Council President Cao Cao of Donghan, and Yuan Shao, the leader of the Guandong School Alliance, all have ambitions for the throne of the 'Alliance Leader.' This dooms Liu Bei and the Five Tigers to live anything but a peaceful campus life. A battle for world domination is about to begin...
