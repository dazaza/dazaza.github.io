On March 4, 2010, Meituan, the first group-buying website in China went online and launched a group-buying event in Beijing. Soon after, online group buying attracted many consumers due to its unique features such as high discounts and simple, quick shopping. Many young people even became "crazy for group buying." According to a survey by a well-known group-buying website, most online group buyers are young executives, with 77% being women.

Online group buying is when a group-buying website partners with merchants to negotiate a lower discount price for consumers. Consumers can simply click their mouse to purchase discounted goods or services.

The large discounts are the "trump card" of group-buying websites. After browsing through many group-buying websites, it was found that dining and entertainment are the most popular projects for online group buying. Movie tickets, skirts, cosmetics, wedding photo shoots, and haircuts are some of the more commonly purchased goods or services among internet users. Group-buying discounts are often below 30%, and sometimes as low as 5%.

By chance, Ms. Zhao joined a group-buying event recommended by her colleague for a hair salon. The original price for a wash-cut-blowdry service was 58 yuan, but group-buyers could enjoy it for only 8 yuan. Seeing how cost-effective this form of consumption was, Ms. Zhao fell in love with online group buying from then on, unable to stop. She said that now, whether she's buying clothes, getting a haircut, or even buying napkins, she always participates in online group buying.

Through online group buying, netizens have benefited, and many merchants are also happy to expand their business through this method. A restaurant on Dongming Road launched a group-buying event where the original price of 128 yuan for hot pot was reduced to 30 yuan. The merchant originally expected to attract at most three to five hundred people, but unexpectedly over a thousand people came. This made the restaurant manager realize the strong appeal of the internet: "Even though I'm losing money at such a low price, the restaurant has gained popularity and increased its reputation through group buying. Many people have even become repeat customers, achieving a win-win situation for both sides!"
