After-sales service for heat pump development must be strengthened

by jdhgsa078 on 2011-08-24 16:27:10

This year, low-carbon and heat pumps have become popular buzzwords in the industry. With the fierce market competition, consumers are paying more attention to after-sales services of products. Therefore, as the darling under the low-carbon economic environment, air-source heat pump water heaters need to gain greater development space with the strategy of after-sales service in the absence of market norms.

According to research data from Jitongbao: 96% of consumers who experience inadequate services do not file complaints, but 90% of dissatisfied consumers will not purchase any products or services from the company again, and will tell at least nine other people about their experiences. Thirteen percent of dissatisfied consumers have told over 20 people about their experiences. Consumers who listen to others' experiences form a negative impression of the company beforehand, or pass these experiences on to other consumers through word-of-mouth.

It is evident that good or bad after-sales service has an impact on the development and growth of enterprises. As we all know, the air-source heat pump water heater market is still in its initial stage, with certain issues existing in product quality, technology, and after-sales service. Air-source heat pump water heaters are innovative products in the industry, unlike other home appliances which have relatively mature after-sales networks and some level of warranty for after-sales issues.

According to our reporter's investigation, most air-source heat pump water heater companies are currently in the early stages of channel construction, making after-sales service the biggest challenge in the industry. Therefore, when choosing an air-source heat pump water heater, after-sales service becomes a major concern for many consumers. Companies are caught in price competition and have not given due importance to key factors such as after-sales service, leading to high repair rates and affecting consumer trust in the products, said Deputy Secretary-General of the China Refrigeration and Air Conditioning Industry Association, Yu Xueshun.

Therefore, doing a good job in after-sales service is crucially important for the development of the air-source heat pump water heater industry. If after-sales service meets customer requirements, customer satisfaction naturally increases, which benefits the brand image of air-energy companies immensely; conversely, if after-sales service fails to meet the conditions promised during sales, customer satisfaction decreases, negatively impacting the development of air-energy companies.

After-sales service is a required course in the process of enterprise development. It is well known that air-source heat pump water heaters differ from similar products like solar water heaters or electric water heaters. During use, the compressor of an air-source heat pump water heater continuously operates. Due to differences in installation by vendors and personal usage habits, issues such as noise, temperature control failure, and leaks occur from time to time. If air-energy companies can establish service stations, manage after-sales service projects well, and promptly resolve problems encountered during product use, even if small problems arise, considerate service will make customers accept them willingly. Furthermore, thoughtful and heartfelt service can touch customers deeply. Often, no matter how well the product advertising campaigns are executed, nothing beats a simple acknowledgment from a satisfied customer!

Good after-sales service serves as the best promotion before the next sale, is the primary way to enhance consumer satisfaction and loyalty, is an important path to establish product reputation and spread corporate image, and is also an effective tool for air-energy companies to explore the market. In today’s era where product homogenization is becoming increasingly severe, after-sales service, as part of marketing, has become a powerful means for manufacturers and merchants to capture consumer minds. Good after-sales service helps companies soar in development.

Resolving after-sales service issues is a practical problem that companies need to face. Agents often get tired of dealing with customers because of after-sales issues. Heat pump manufacturers should communicate effectively with agents and assist them in handling after-sales problems. Currently, the installation, maintenance, and service of heat pump products are constraining the development of the industry. Therefore, training marketing personnel is very important. Systematic knowledge training should be provided to marketing personnel regarding the composition, working principles, and performance comparisons of products. As product technology continues to break through, marketing personnel should also be given continuous learning opportunities so they can fully explain the products and promote the brand when facing consumers.
