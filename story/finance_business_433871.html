Urban Romance Symphony - The theme song of "Dreaming Back to Jinsha City" achieves excellent results. The MV is impressively launched while it's hot - Part Middle

by letaoehv on 2011-05-20 10:54:57

Not long ago, the online version of the MV of the theme song "The Flower in Heart" of the film Symphony of Country Love has been released. The theme song "The Flower in Heart" was sung by a new generation female star, Han Xue. After a period of online promotion, Symphony of Country Love has achieved fairly good results and ranked at the top of various music lists. On the eve of the upcoming release of the movie, the production team took advantage of the momentum to grandly release the MV of "The Flower in Heart" which combined animation with real people.

This version of the MV includes the entire process of Han Xue recording the song. Han Xue expressed that "Dreaming Back to Jiacheng" is definitely a domestic animated blockbuster worth watching. Excellent production techniques demonstrated the rapid progress of Chinese animation. She hoped that everyone would support domestic Chinese animation.

Ms. Su Xiaohong, the producer, stated: Han Xue's vocal line showcased this heartfelt song cleanly and refreshingly, complementing the exquisite hand-drawn images of the film without conflict. It is believed that Han Xue's clear voice would bring everyone a touch of coolness in the hot summer.

Statement: Exclusive manuscript of China Film Network, please indicate the source for reprinting!

"Dreaming Back to Jiacheng" was produced by Hangzhou Shengshi Longtu Animation Co., Ltd. and distributed by Huaxia Film Distribution Co., Ltd. It is scheduled for grand nationwide release on July 10th.
