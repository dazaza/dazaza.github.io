How to Get Rid of Elephant Legs After Childbirth

by ciaifuke on 2012-03-05 16:47:17

Many women after childbirth either develop "elephant legs" or become very slim. So, how can we get rid of those unwanted "elephant legs" after childbirth? Thick calves look unattractive in skirts or boots. How can we achieve more balanced calf lines?

Let's take a look at different types of thick calves and their respective leg-slimming methods:

I. Slimming Solution for Fatty Legs:

Judgment Criteria: If you pinch your leg and can easily grab a lump of fat, it means your legs appear chubby and disproportionate to your actual weight.

Slimming Key Points: Apart from exercise, avoiding high-sugar and high-oil foods is the most effective method. Consuming more seafood and vegetables with low calories, reducing snacks, and engaging in aerobic exercises like running, swimming, and cycling can eliminate excess fat on the legs.

Slimming Method One: Postpartum Leg Aerobics

Starting from the fifth day postpartum until the end of the month, appropriate leg exercises can be done to strengthen leg muscles and improve the return flow of venous blood in the lower limbs. Sit on the ground with both legs straight and together, back straight, arms stretched behind, fingers spread supporting the ground. When inhaling, try to curl your toes upward; when exhaling, stretch them out as much as possible. Then lie on your back, both legs straight and slightly apart, arms placed by your sides. When inhaling, bend your left leg towards your chest with toes stretched; when exhaling, straighten your left leg making it perpendicular to your upper body with toes curled up. Alternate between both legs.

This aerobics routine is suitable for mothers who have had normal deliveries. Due to generally weaker constitutions postpartum, one should exercise according to their specific conditions without rushing. Each set of exercises should last two to three minutes, twice a day (morning and evening), paying special attention to coordinating breathing with movements. After the first month, various muscle group exercises can be performed to restore thigh muscle strength and elasticity. Suitable exercises include slow jogging, leg stretching and bending, and swimming.

Slimming Method Two: Gua Sha (Scraping) for Slim Legs

Every night after bathing, apply baby oil or olive oil with lubricating effects onto the calves. Use a gua sha board (or the back of a wooden comb, a smooth spoon, etc.) to vigorously scrape the calf muscles from the ankle all the way up to the knee bend, 100 strokes per leg.

It is best to perform this after bathing and drink some warm water afterward. If you can also do some air cycling or scissor leg exercises (lying down, lifting and straightening both legs, opening them as wide as possible, pausing for a few seconds, then closing them again like scissors), there are no particular dietary restrictions unless you're aiming to lose overall body weight. In that case, just reduce oily and high-sugar foods. Combining this with eating less during dinner can help slim faster.

Effect: Generally, people will notice a noticeable reduction in leg circumference after persisting for two weeks to a month.

Solution Three: Liposuction on Legs

To address thick calves caused by fat accumulation, leg liposuction is an option, though it may be somewhat painful.

Typically, a small incision of about 0.5 cm is made on the inner ankle or behind the knee, swelling anesthesia is administered, and ultrasound waves are used externally to break down and dissolve fat cells, which are then suctioned out using negative pressure. The surgery time is controlled based on the amount of fat in the calves, usually requiring about 1.5 hours for both calves combined.

This procedure removes fat cells from the body, significantly reducing the number of fat cells compared to methods like exercise, dieting, or medication. Therefore, the results won't rebound post-surgery.

Costs range approximately from 3000 to 4000 RMB.

II. Slimming Solution for Varicose Veins or "Edema" Legs

Judgment Criteria: Pressing the calf muscles reveals poor and slow skin rebound, indicating this type of thick leg.

Slimming Key Points: Avoid fast food, vacuum-packed food, pastries, and salty snacks. Do not consume carbonated drinks like cola or Sprite. Avoid maintaining a single posture for over an hour. Performing simple muscle-strengthening exercises can help alleviate swelling.

Solution One: 10-Minute Quick Slimming Method

Thick calves are often due to edema. To achieve a more perfect leg shape, quickly massage your legs 10 minutes before a date. After massaging, allow the body to expel accumulated waste products, then use slimming cream. Elevate your legs for a short rest, which can reduce the size by 2-3 cm.

1. Alternately caress with both hands. Place the palms of both hands around the calf area and alternately move upwards. Perform this action 10 times.

2. Twist and knead with both hands. Cover the calf muscles tightly with both palms, twist and knead continuously until the entire calf becomes warm.

Solution Two: High-Leg Elevation Method

After sitting for long periods, place your legs higher than your heart. Before sleeping, you can place your legs against the wall or elevate them by 20-30 cm while sleeping. Lie on your side, supporting your upper body with your hand. Bend one leg and extend the other. Then repeatedly lift the bent leg, 10 times. Turn to the other side and repeat the same actions with the other leg.

Solution Three: Slapping and Massaging Method

Following the lymph nodes of the legs, perform rhythmic slapping and massaging to make the lymphatic system and blood circulation smoother. Moderate massage can eliminate the heavy sensation and edema in the legs.

Start from the ankle and move rhythmically upward toward the back of the knee with both hands. After finishing the right leg, perform the same process on the left leg.

If you decide to choose this leg-slimming method, you should receive massages at least twice a week, each lasting an hour or more to achieve effective results. You can choose to do this while watching TV in the evening.
