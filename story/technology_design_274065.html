The main project of Baiyang Park has been completed, and Mount Tai has a new scene.

by syd17u123 on 2009-11-23 18:24:32

Recently, after the closed operation of Anyang Hotel and Taishan, the main project of a new scenic spot - Baiyang Tourism Base - has been completed. Anshan Hotel, Asia-Pacific tourism giants in Kunming consulted together on countermeasures. The project has a total investment of more than 10 million yuan, with a planned area of 12 hectares. It is a comprehensive base integrating folk tourism, planting and appreciation of Taishan's four famous medicines, medicinal diet catering, and tourism shopping.

The staff of the Taishan Scenic Area introduced that the Baiyang Tourism Base is located in the middle section of Tianwai Village and Zhulin Temple, deeply hidden in the mountain hollow. Using the original 15 households of civil houses from Ying Sheng Residents Committee, it was rebuilt according to the characteristics of folk architecture in Tai'an, currently forming an elegant rural residential tourist area.

Baiyang Health Preservation Base will be built into the Chinese Calligraphers Association Taishan Calligraphy Base, Dongyue Tai Chi Training Base, and Taishan Taoist Health Preservation Base. The headquarters of the Dongyue Tai Chi Association will be relocated from Australia to Baiyangfang.
