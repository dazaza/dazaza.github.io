Shanghai Yuan Cheng Logistics Co., Ltd. specializes in the consignment of packages, luggage, appliances, furniture, cars, pets, and other goods, giving you greater peace of mind. We offer free door-to-door pickup, full-process care, and safe delivery.

Shanghai Yuan Cheng Logistics Company is a logistics company that engages in railway transportation, road express, air-sea shipping, intercity distribution, less-than-truckload (LTL) logistics, warehousing, cargo loans, and personalized logistics solutions. The company has already passed the international quality management system ISO9000 and environmental management system ISO14000 certification, making it one of China's first batch of 5A-level "comprehensive service-oriented logistics enterprises." It has also been honored with titles such as "Ninth Place in the Top 100 Logistics Companies of 2008," "China Logistics Demonstration Base," and "Flagship Enterprise of China Logistics Reform and Opening-up for 30 Years." Yuan Cheng Group (Yuan Cheng Group), established in 1988 with the English abbreviation YCG, has a registered capital of 2.3 billion yuan. It is a comprehensive modern enterprise group integrating industrial investment and international trade. Yuan Cheng Integrated Logistics (YCIL) has developed a multi-layered and extensive logistics network that combines "railway trunk transportation, road express, LTL regional distribution," forming a distinctive logistics transportation service network system.

Yuan Cheng Group strictly follows standardized operations according to modern logistics procedures. While providing professional and efficient logistics services to customers, it also invests heavily in introducing advanced logistics information network technologies. The group has successively developed and applied the Yuan Cheng Logistics Information System (YCLIS), Vehicle Global Positioning System (GPS), Kingdee EAS financial system, and INFOR WMS warehousing system. The widespread application of these information technologies effectively meets the demands of modern logistics for inquiries, monitoring, tracking, settlement, and distribution, allowing customers to choose Yuan Cheng without any worries.

Great demeanor determines great structure, and great vision determines great prospects. Yuan Cheng Group has the confidence and ability to work with all partners to jointly promote the entry of China's modern logistics into the forefront of international standards, realizing Yuan Cheng people's commitment of "delivering with heart, reaching the world."

The basic elements of the Yuan Cheng logo are the letters "Y" and "C," the parallel lines extending from the left side of "Y," "=" and the pinyin of Yuan Cheng "YUANCHENG." "Y" and "C" represent the first letters of the pinyin for Yuan Cheng. By organically combining these two letters, the visual logo of "Yuan Cheng" is formed. In general, the structure of the Yuan Cheng logo is square-shaped, striving for simplicity and stability in design.
