"Ms. Yang" txt e-book free download, following "Ms. Liu", is the second formally published series of emotional crime novels by Zhang Yihe.

Xu Dishang said: "Love is punishment." These words basically encapsulate the theme of the story in "Ms. Yang."

He Wujie's originally unremarkable life was changed by a bloody love affair, making his short life like fireworks at night - "whoosh," shooting up into the sky, instantly dazzling with golden light, then quickly falling and returning to silence. Yang Fenfang's romance with him was as beautiful and brilliant as cherry blossoms. But because of one night of bloodshed and violence, all the fragrance and sweetness were washed away. While Yang Fenfang was passionately in love with her neighbor He Wujie and had given herself to him, she was also "married" to an unfamiliar and domineering soldier, Liu Qingsheng. The story unfolds amidst the intense conflicts between tragic love and military marriage, between consensual sex and rape, between fervor and coldness, ultimately culminating in a deadly case of adultery and murder...

Most tragically and pitifully, almost every choice Yang Fenfang made was wrong, including taking the risk to engage in illicit relations with counselor Sun Zhixin, and even her final refusal of Zhao Yonghai. Alas! Based on real events, "Ms. Yang" reveals the worldly nature of human beings.
