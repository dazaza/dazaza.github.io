The Spring Festival Gala director team has sent invitations to Faye Wong and Andy Lau and are waiting for their replies.

by 3yll869c5t on 2009-11-23 13:11:39

On December 1, the director team of the Spring Festival Gala in the year of Tiger will conduct an initial review of the programs. Yesterday (November 22), reporters learned that after carefully investigating the audience's preferences, the director team has extended invitations to superstars like Jacky Cheung and Faye Wong. In response, Jacky Cheung's side stated that they are considering it.

Jacky Cheung and Faye Wong: "Whether or not to appear on the Spring Festival Gala, I need to think about it" (designed dialogue).

Chongqing Business Daily reported on November 23: On December 1, the director team of the Spring Festival Gala in the year of Tiger will conduct an initial review of the programs. Reporters learned yesterday that after carefully investigating the audience's preferences, the director team has extended invitations to superstars like Jacky Cheung and Faye Wong. One is a king of pop who has temporarily left the music scene, and the other is a queen of pop who has been away for many years. If agreements can be successfully reached, the Spring Festival Gala in the year of Tiger will undoubtedly become a booster for their comeback.

The Spring Festival Gala invites Faye Wong to make a comeback. Rumors about Faye Wong making a comeback and singing have been circulating since the beginning of this year, but apart from much thunder and little rain, there haven't been any concrete actions. Last week, rumors spread again that Faye Wong would hold ten concerts next year. Actually, if Faye Wong could make another comeback and sing, it would already be the long-held wish of numerous fans. After listening to opinions from multiple audiences, the director team of the Spring Festival Gala in the year of Tiger also sent out an invitation letter to Faye Wong.

In the 1998 Spring Festival Gala, the song "Appointment in 98" specially created for Faye Wong and Eileen Chang is still being sung today and has become one of the most successful songs in the history of the Spring Festival Gala. However, this year's invitation for Faye Wong to perform on stage hopes that this queen of pop can sing a solo. Looking back, Faye Wong's last time showcasing her voice on television was during last year's CCTV May 12th earthquake relief gala. The long-absent voice of the queen fills people with anticipation. Yesterday, reporters verified this matter with Faye Wong's side. Unlike previous times, Chen Jiaying did not deny it, and her tone didn't sound as firm. Over the phone, she just said: "Not decided yet."

The Spring Festival Gala invites Jacky Cheung to make his first appearance. Among the former Four Great Kings, the only one who hasn't appeared on CCTV's Spring Festival Gala so far is Jacky Cheung. After the tour two years ago, Jacky Cheung temporarily left the music scene, but due to the impact of the financial storm, King Cheung also had to sing again, with the concert set to start next year. It was precisely because of this intention of Jacky Cheung that the director team of the Spring Festival Gala extended him an invitation. An informed person told reporters that the reason Jacky Cheung is interested in accepting the challenge is that he saw the success of the Ox Year Spring Festival Gala's "Super Band." At that time, Super Band was also preparing for a national tour, and the promotional effect of the Spring Festival Gala on the concert was undeniable, so the negotiations went smoothly. Moreover, Jacky Cheung has never appeared on the Spring Festival Gala, and his fans span all age groups. If it can be realized, it will surely attract more viewers. Yesterday, reporters also contacted Jacky Cheung's side, and they indicated that due to scheduling issues, they are currently carefully considering it.

Related News: Confirmation of abandoning the Little Tiger Team plan

Previously, it was rumored that the Spring Festival Gala in the year of Tiger would invite the Little Tiger Team to reunite. However, reporters learned yesterday that this plan has been abandoned. The main reason lies in the fact that now the members of the Little Tiger Team are busy pursuing their own careers, and whether in terms of appearance or development direction, it is difficult to find a common point.

(Original Source: Chongqing Business Daily)
