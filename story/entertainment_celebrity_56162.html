To all netizens who aspire to become moderators:

A moderator is the foundation of a forum, and we have always believed this.

Please seriously consider your dedication and capability. "Dedication" refers to the willingness to do work, and "capability" refers to the ability to do work.

The willingness to work is the most important condition for becoming a moderator!

If you want to be a moderator just for the sake of it, without truly wanting to serve the netizens or fulfill the responsibilities of a moderator, then we hope that you do not apply, but instead give the opportunity to those who are truly dedicated to doing the work!

As for "capability," it refers to the "ability to work." In fact, being a moderator does not require any special skills. Of course, having a good understanding of the board is necessary. Beyond that, what's needed is time! Being a moderator requires a significant amount of time spent on the site. If you don't have enough time, even if you're willing, how can you properly perform the duties of a moderator?

Therefore, before you submit an application, please carefully think about this issue. If you have both the willingness and the capability, we sincerely welcome you to join the ranks of 'sock' moderators!

[Application Qualifications]

Please first go to the community - modify personal information. If your personal information is incomplete or incorrect, we will not process your application.

As long as you meet the qualifications for applying as a moderator, please fill out the [Moderator Application Form] in this section. Once you've filled in the relevant information, you can apply. The review period by the station is approximately 1-7 days.

-Moderator Application Qualifications-

1. Passed the general certification of this site.

2. Registered on the 'sock' forum for at least one month.

3. Published more than three good articles on the board (please provide article links).

4. Each person is limited to applying for two board moderators.

5. Community points exceed 100 points;

For children under 12 years old, considering their physical and mental development and maturity, we do not recommend applying for the position of moderator.

For teenagers aged 12 to 18, when applying for the moderator position, you should inform your parents (guardians) to read and agree to the application content. After submitting the application, it will be assumed that the applicant has obtained the understanding and agreement of the parents (guardians) to serve as a moderator.

※Note:

Those who have been banned from posting for more than a week (excluding one week), within three months from the date of release from the ban, are not allowed to apply for any moderator positions;

Those found to deliberately flood the board, after being announced, will permanently lose the eligibility to apply for moderator positions;

The initiators of new boards will serve as the first moderators;

Each board may have up to four moderators.

★ Each person can only serve as a moderator for two boards.

Review Time:

1-7 days, depending on your application conditions, please wait patiently.

[Moderator Rights and Responsibilities]

Moderators need to pay attention to the rights, responsibilities, and other related matters concerning moderators, while also keeping an eye on the administrative zone and cooperating accordingly.

If users post articles that violate the regulations of the 'sock' community, moderators have the right and obligation to delete them.

In case of verbal conflicts among users, moderators are obligated to mediate disputes fairly. If users still refuse to heed advice, they can notify the administrator for assistance.

Moderators should promote discussions on the board, frequently post articles related to the board's content or answer user questions to establish the style and theme of the discussion area.

Moderators should mark excellent articles on the board and collect these articles and answers to common questions in the highlights section for easy reference by board members.

If there is an argument within the board, moderators should take responsibility for mediation. When encountering obvious irrational disputes between users in the discussion area, moderators must actively intervene to resolve disputes and maintain proper discourse in the discussion area.

If there is intentional flooding on the board, moderators should proactively discourage and delete such posts; otherwise, it will be considered as failing to fulfill their duties. If moderators participate in flooding or use their moderator privileges for personal purposes, it will be regarded as serious dereliction of duty, subject to criticism and possible removal of privileges.

[Moderator Duties]

Moderator tasks and authorities: Delete useless posts, set sticky posts, create a discussion atmosphere on the board, submit accounts that need to be banned to the administrator.

The deletion criteria should be strictly followed by the moderators. Generally, board management should be handled strictly by the moderators.

[Things to Note After Taking Office as Moderator]

After publishing a post to apply for moderator, announce your governance plan and board development plans in the application post; the description should be specific and detailed.

The governance plan must be published within the corresponding board and pinned until the moderator steps down.

Moderators must manage the board with a certain amount of time devoted to it.

[Process of Applying for Moderator]

Confirm application qualifications - Fill out and publish the application form - Reviewed by current moderators and administrators - Internship as a moderator - Board affairs review whether qualified to turn regular - Turn into a formal moderator.

Difference in authority between intern moderator and regular moderator: Cannot ban IDs or modify today's topic.

One month after the internship, submit an application along with work reflections. Upon approval, the moderator will be turned into a formal moderator.

[Moderator Application Form Template]

Instructions:

The application form should be posted with the title "[BBBB] xxx applies for YYY board moderator", where BBBB is the name of the main section, xxx is the applicant's ID, and YYY is the name of the applied board.

Example: [Socks Forum] ‘Bug’ applies for [Nylon Souls] moderator, [Socks Forum] Bug applies for [Admin Home] moderator.

The moderator application form should be filled out in detail. Applications that are not filled out carefully will not be approved.

Any applications that do not conform to the above format will be considered invalid, and the administrator will not process them.
