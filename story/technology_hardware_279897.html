Black, white, and gray as the main tone

Therefore, manufacturers put a lot of effort into promoting men's skincare products. First, they must eliminate feminine colors, and all flashy colors are avoided. Men's skincare products from various brands are basically similar to men's clothing — black, white, and gray are the main tones, emphasizing texture and stability. The packaging design also avoids fancy and luxurious designs, opting instead for simple rectangular shapes to attract men's attention.

The scent of men's skincare products

Considering that men feel that using cosmetics is not manly enough, the fragrance must cater to men's preferences. Scents commonly used in women's skincare products, such as floral and rose fragrances, are absolutely forbidden. Currently, men's skincare products have two directions in terms of fragrance: either make the scent acceptable to men by using wood, tobacco, or aquatic scents, or completely remove any fragrance, making the skincare products odorless. This way, there's no need to worry about being teased as a "sissy" because of strong fragrances, so relax!
