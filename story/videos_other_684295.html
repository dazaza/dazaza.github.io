The Historical Origins of Jitai Tablets

The Origin of Jitai Tablets: In the mid-19th century, around the time of the Opium Wars, drugs began flooding into China in large quantities. For more than a century, Chinese citizens suffered greatly from the harm caused by drugs. Over the course of 150 years of fighting against drugs, many enlightened individuals in China discovered and summarized numerous detoxification drugs and effective prescriptions passed down through generations. Among these, Jitai Tablets were developed based on a series of secret recipes created by Mr. Yang Jitai, one of the four famous doctors of Eastern Hubei during the Ming and Qing Dynasties (Li Shizhen, Pang Anshi, Wan Mizhai, and Yang Jitai), specifically for breaking opium addiction during the Daoguang and Tongzhi periods of the Qing Dynasty. Drawing upon the essence of traditional Chinese medicine and targeting the characteristics of ancient drugs, the leadership core of Beijing Jitai Recovery utilized ancient scientific techniques and adopted advanced manufacturing processes to develop Jitai Tablets, a pure Chinese herbal detoxification-specific drug that contains no anesthetics and has no side effects. This new drug was officially approved for market release in 1999 under the name Jitai Tablets and received a patent certificate from the National Intellectual Property Administration.

Mr. Cai Zhiji, a renowned scholar internationally and then Director of the Institute of Drug Dependence Studies in China as well as an anti-narcotics official at the United Nations, specially inscribed a message for this occasion: "Developing Chinese herbal detoxification medicines is a cause that the country highly encourages and supports, and it is also a focus of international attention. I hope that through further scientific clinical trials, the detoxification efficacy of Jitai can be brought into play, contributing to the national and global anti-drug campaigns." After Jitai Tablets settled with Zhangjiang Shanghai Traditional Chinese Medicine Pharmaceutical Technology Co., Ltd. in the National Pharmaceutical Valley, both production technology and product brand have reached a new level. The tablets are selected for the Ministry of State Security's National Detoxification Drugs Directory due to their unique detoxification efficacy.

The emergence of Jitai Tablets marks a significant step forward in detoxification drug technology. The drug not only addresses the shortcomings of traditional Chinese medicine detoxification drugs which generally have less satisfactory results and cannot achieve desired therapeutic outcomes, leaving patients still feeling pain after taking the medication, but also stands out with its advantages of high efficiency, comprehensiveness, safety, stability, non-addictiveness, and non-anesthetic properties, making it incomparable to current Western detoxification drugs. Jitai Tablets not only provide new, non-substitutional detoxification drugs for those working in the detoxification field but more importantly, will help millions of addicts break free from drug addiction, return to society and family, and move towards a new life.
