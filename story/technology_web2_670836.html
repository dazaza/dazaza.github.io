At around 7 p.m. on February 11th

by lhltckfrtg on 2012-02-21 11:52:58

Under the accompaniment of policemen, Zhong found his girlfriend in a residential building beside the school. His girlfriend had just escaped from the "kidnappers" and her body was covered with wounds. Did she file a false report? While increasing investigation measures, the policemen asked the parties concerned again with these doubts. Under the patient guidance and teaching of the policemen, Qiu finally denied the whole process of making up the kidnapping case. Being drugged, kidnapped and robbed were all fictitious. "I threw away more than 30 thousand yuan in the shop to persuade my fiancé Zhong not to gamble anymore; another purpose is to let Zhong go to the Civil Affairs Bureau with me to register for marriage." Qiu eventually revealed the cause of the false police situation. After checking all the videos in the urban area, the policemen did not find any valuable clues. "The girl came out from the shop and everything was normal in the park. When she went back to buy things, she passed by the shop door without going inside, and there was nothing unusual." Currently, Qiu has been administratively detained by the public security organs. On February 14, which should have been a warm and romantic Valentine's Day, Qiu could only spend it alone in the detention center. After continuous two days and nights of fighting, the police of Jialing County successfully solved this case of kidnapping and robbery. But what Zhong couldn't foresee was that this kidnapping case was actually a farce directed and acted by his girlfriend Qiu to test their love. And Qiu was also administratively detained by the public security organs, spending a "unforgettable" Valentine's Day in the detention center. Originally, Qiu, 21 years old, started working in the car beauty shop run by her boyfriend Zhong since October 2011 and cohabited with Zhong who had just divorced, brushing credit. During this period, Qiu repeatedly asked Zhong to go through the marriage registration procedures, but Zhong always made excuses such as heavy pressure. After receiving the alarm, the public security organs immediately carried out external investigations. At about 9 p.m., when the policemen were looking everywhere for the girl, Zhong received a call from Qiu, asking Zhong to pick her up at a school 40 kilometers away from Jiacheng in Meicheng East. After more than ten hours of work, the three groups of policemen returned empty-handed. Was the "drug" really so powerful? Were the suspects really so meticulous, leaving no trace at all? With these questions, the investigating policemen immediately adjusted their thinking. The reporter learned that at about 7 p.m. on February 11, Zhong, who operated a car beauty maintenance shop in Jiacheng, reported to the Jialing County Public Security Bureau that his girlfriend Qiu, who worked as an accountant in his shop, was kidnapped on a mountain at 4 p.m. that afternoon, and the other party used her mobile phone to send messages demanding ransom. "We did not find the exact location where the girl woke up." The policemen who investigated the scene on the mountain climbed all morning and crossed several mountains but still did not find any traces. "We did not find any useful things either." The policemen who visited the public also came back empty-handed. After finding Qiu, the policemen learned that at around 4 p.m. that afternoon, Qiu suddenly smelled a whiff of perfume on her way back to the shop from a nearby park to buy meatballs and then lost consciousness. When she woke up, she found herself lying on a mountain, and her handbag, mobile phone, and more than 30 thousand yuan in cash inside the bag were gone. After regaining consciousness, she ran down the mountain under the lamplight at the foot of the mountain and ran to a resident's house along the way, and then called her boyfriend Zhong. "In broad daylight, using drugs to knock out the victim and then kidnap and rob property." This news immediately caused a great stir in the quiet mountain city of Jialing.
