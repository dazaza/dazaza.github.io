Primary school student posts online to thank traffic police who saved his/her life

by xue94fwsh on 2012-03-09 14:35:43

Chutian Jinbao News - Faced with an oncoming car, she was terrified; but a large hand pulled her from behind. This unexpected encounter moved the heart of a fourth-grade elementary school student. Recently, the elementary school student posted online to thank the kind police officer. Yesterday, I saw this thank-you post on the Tianya website, titled "Uncle, I'm sorry, thank you." The child with the online name "Hu Lulan" wrote: "Last night after school, while buying spicy rice noodles at a stall with my classmates, a strong wind blew my hat away and it rolled into the middle of the road. I quickly went to pick it up. At that moment, a car was driving towards me, and I was scared stiff. I didn't know who had pulled me hard. When I turned around, it was a young traffic police officer." Hu Lulan said: "I thought he would severely scold me and ask which school I am from, but he just comforted me briefly in a soft voice and then helped me pick up my hat. Uncle, I'm really sorry, you must be very tired after working all day, and I've caused you trouble!" At the end of the post, Hu Lulan playfully said: "I forgot to ask the uncle's name, otherwise, I would have written a thank-you letter." Yesterday, I contacted the poster "Hu Lulan" - Luo Jiayu, a fourth-grade girl from Chongren Road Elementary School. She said she will always remember the warmth of the large hand that pulled her back, making her feel especially warm; after returning home that evening, she immediately told her parents about the incident and posted it online. The next day, her parents specially took her to look for the police officer near Youyi Road, and they unexpectedly found him quickly - Officer Wu Dongbing from the First Team of the Kou District Traffic Police Brigade. Upon meeting, Wu Dongbing smiled shyly and repeatedly said it was his duty, and also reminded Luo Jiayu: "Be careful in the future, don't run around on the road." After Luo Jiayu's post was released, more than 30 netizens replied enthusiastically. Netizen "Dazhumao Guoguo" said: "Haha, what a cute child, thumbs up"; Netizen "Fan" called it a small matter that touched the heart, paying tribute to this good traffic police officer... (Reporter Dai Hui, Correspondent Deng Gang) (Editor: SN005)
