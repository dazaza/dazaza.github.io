The减速 of CPI still remains a悬念. We are waiting for the arrival of stock index futures.w8

by xw0972406 on 2010-04-15 22:36:18

According to the macro data of January released by the Statistical Bureau this morning, the GDP in the first season increased by 11.9%, slightly lower than the previously expected 12%, but it is still at a relatively high level, reflecting that the Chinese economy is still in a strong recovery. In addition, the CPI in March has drawn much attention. The increase rate of 2.2% fell compared with last month's 2.7%. The complicated news also brought strong fluctuations to the market. After the two cities had a low opening, they rose quickly and formed the highest point of the morning at 10:20. Then, there was a quick fall, once breaking through yesterday's low point. The Gem board which had a collective plunge at the beginning of this week experienced another large-scale decline today. Although the petrochemical weight pulled the index up at the end of the market, it was obvious that the investors' confidence had been frustrated. In the future, the stock index futures will debut tomorrow. China's capital market will turn over a new leaf from then on. It is expected that the short-term general situation will have some performances stimulated by this. The low-speed operation of the CPI has reduced the worry about interest rate hikes to a certain extent. However, combined with the recent situation, the pressure of policy tightening is still severe. The current structural contradiction of price is prominent, coexisting with the low CPI and the rapid rise in the prices of agricultural products, international bulk raw materials, and real estate. Especially, few people mentioned the role of low interest rates in the recent skyrocketing of real estate prices. However, the role of interest rates in this industry has always been great. We can take an example to illustrate the importance of interest rate issues to the real estate industry. At the beginning of 2008, the applicable interest rate for second-home loans was 8.613% (7.83% floating up by 10%). For a loan of 1 million yuan to buy a house, the monthly payment for 20 years was 8750 yuan. However, in 2009, the applicable interest rate for second-home loans was 4.158% (5.94% floating down by 30%). For a loan of 1 million yuan to buy a house, the monthly payment for 20 years dropped sharply to 6146 yuan. Under the new applicable interest rate, the monthly payment of 8750 yuan could only be reached when the loan reached 1.424 million yuan. This is equivalent to a 42.4% increase in housing prices. Therefore, the catalytic effect of ultra-low interest rates on real estate bubbles cannot be underestimated. Yesterday, Premier Wen Jiabao held a standing meeting to summarize the operation of the macro-economy in the first quarter and make arrangements for the next stage of work. Among them, the remarks on real estate market regulation accounted for a considerable proportion, showing that the central government is highly vigilant about the real estate breeding asset bubbles and accumulating potential risks. Policies for later real estate regulation are likely to focus on two aspects: one is the introduction of property taxes, and the other is potential interest rate hike policies. The probability of the latter is lower than the former, but it cannot be ruled out.

In addition, there is another point. The industrial profit of enterprises above designated size from January to February was 486.7 billion yuan, the highest in history during the same period, and exceeded the historical second-highest by 13.85 billion yuan or 40%. Interest rate is the "profit divider" between the industrial department and the banking department. The new situation of industrial profits provides an objective basis and requirement for interest rate hikes, and enhances its expectation. There are already 0 comments. I want to say something.
