State-owned Henan Mine Equipment Factory is a shareholding enterprise integrating R & D, production and sales, mainly producing large and medium-sized series of crushing and grinding machines. The company headquarters is located in Zhengzhou, the capital of Henan Province, covering an area of about 30,000 square meters, with several wholly-owned subsidiaries, and its annual output value is more than 100 million yuan. Since its establishment in 1982, our factory has adhered to the scientific management methods of modern enterprises, carefully manufactured, continuously innovated, and rapidly developed into a brilliant pearl in China's machinery manufacturing industry.

Company Scale

Our factory has gathered a large number of enterprising management and scientific research elites representing the leading level of the industry. There are currently 12 senior engineers, 25 engineers, 40 undergraduates from key universities, 98 senior technicians, and more than 500 online employees. The chief engineer is a well-known domestic crushing and grinding expert in China's machinery industry, who has designed and developed several generations of leading domestic grinding and crushing equipment. Our factory attaches great importance to international technology exchange and cooperation, and has introduced advanced technologies and processes from the United States, Germany, Australia and other countries, owning more than 100 sets of production equipment, establishing internationally advanced production lines and first-class modern testing bases. The company pays great attention to the development of new products and new technologies, and has won 6 national patents successively. The company actively absorbs advanced management ideas at home and abroad, and strives to build a first-class employee team.

Company Products

Our main products include: crushers, impact crushers, self-impact crushers, impact crushers, roller crushers, jaw crushers, hammer crushers, sand making machines, sand making equipment, mechanical sand equipment, stone crushing equipment, mechanical sand production line, stone crushing production line, bar mill sand making machine, Raymond mill, Raymond mill machine, Raymond machine, Raymond grinder, high-pressure mill, high -pressure suspension roller mill, ball mill, cone ball mill, ceramic ball mill, wet ball mill, dry ball mill, flotation machine, friction brick press, asbestos tile machine.

Sales Service

Our factory owns a group of experienced and technically excellent sales service team and a perfect sales service network. Customer service methods are flexible and diverse. In addition to adopting traditional sales service methods, we also implement Internet sales and tracking operation services. Before customers buy the machine, the company will send engineering and technical personnel to the site free of charge to plan the site for users and design the best process and solution; after purchasing the machine, the company will assign professional after-sales service personnel to the site free of charge to guide customers in installation, commissioning, planning and management of equipment.

Corporate Culture

What users need is what we are fully committed to; user satisfaction is what we always pursue. Our factory has been constantly innovating, surpassing ourselves, challenging limits, and striving to provide customers with 100% quality products over the past decade. Our factory believes that product quality is the life of the enterprise, and also follows the business principle of honesty, pragmatism, and efficiency, always insisting on building brands with quality and improving brands with service.
