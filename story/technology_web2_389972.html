Let's delve into Traditional Chinese Medicine (TCM) beauty together Written by Taste of Whitening in Intensive Care

Today, we switch flavors and return to our traditional treasure ―― TCM regulation. TCM not only focuses on treating and saving lives but also excels in beauty regulation. Throughout history, countless TCM practitioners have spent their entire lives devising methods to enhance the beauty of palace women, leaving behind a wealth of experience that absolutely deserves our attention!

So, let's follow the editor and explore TCM beauty together~

"TCM Beauty" has deep roots in Chinese culture. Through centuries of people's practical experiences in beauty, generation after generation of folk beauty and health preservation methods have accumulated boundless Eastern wisdom.

Whether it's the ancient imperial court or modern cities, women have always been thoughtful about how to make themselves more beautiful.

And "TCM Beauty" with its comprehensive internal and external regulation, and the concept of achieving health and beauty from within, is increasingly favored by those who value beauty and health.

But now, TCM beauty products are varied. What kind of methods would be effective for you? Which products would benefit you? And through what methods can you find suitable products? I believe these are the most concerning and headache-inducing questions for everyone.

For this reason, I visited supermarkets, malls, and beauty salons. Later, I found an issue: Supermarkets and malls generally don't sell TCM products, while some beauty salons do. When asking merchants, without exception, they all introduced the advantages of their products, telling me what would happen after using them, similar to what Guangzhou VIP Information Technology Co., Ltd. customers say. However, these are ultimately one-sided opinions from merchants, making it hard to find actual users unless there are friends around you using them. I'm not saying their products are bad; every product that survives in the market definitely has its own customer base. Personally, I still place more emphasis on our traditional TCM beauty products.

Due to work, I often surf online, so I decided to use the internet to look for information. Taobao is China's largest shopping and trading platform. Usually, I often buy clothes and various international brand cosmetics there. But I haven't yet explored our own TCM beauty-related items online. After searching, there were quite a few treasures available. I decided to ask a store with relatively high credibility.

I clicked into a TCM beauty store named "Green Forest." Honestly, what initially attracted me was the image of an ancient beauty on their products — men and women alike have a love for beauty. After reading their introduction, it turned out they were from a family of TCM practitioners, and the products in their small shop were uniquely formulated by their elderly TCM doctor at home. This piqued my curiosity, wow, could he also prescribe a secret formula for me, turning me into a youthful and invincible young girl overnight? After reading more introductions about several products, I discovered that the old TCM doctor in this store was around 50 years old but looked like he was only 35 in the photos; truly impressive, making me admire and envy him. If I looked the same at 50 as I do now, that would be fine too.

Thinking about this, I decided to inquire as a customer to see how she would introduce the store's products to me.

Generally, the longer you use them, the better the results.

Shopkeeper: Yes, because each person's situation is different, the time needed for regulation will vary. As long as customers can persist, their skin will improve significantly compared to before.

Shopkeeper: Alright, you're welcome. Feel free to visit anytime.
