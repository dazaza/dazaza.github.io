Give the children a good character: slow parenting

by jqrxass on 2009-10-20 14:04:50

Mr. Hei Youlong has long enjoyed the reputation of "the father of Carnegie in Chinese". With remarkable achievements, he believed that his greatest success in life was not the fame, status and money he had gained, but the fact that he had educated four excellent children with the method of "slow parenting".

Children grow up slowly. "Slow parenting" does not mean leaving children to their own devices, but rather giving them the opportunity to learn and grow through learning. It can be seen from the two books that Mr. Hei Youlong and his wife also went through the rebellious, disobedient, and worst-behaved years of their children, and the children were not the "good" children in the eyes of ordinary people. After more than 20 years of patient "slow parenting", all the children have settled down and established their careers. Each of them has a good life in both work and family. Mr. Hei Youlong's "slow parenting" education method finally reaped abundant rewards.

In the narratives of each member of the Hei Youlong family, readers can feel a strong sense of family affection. From the narratives of every member of the Hei Youlong family, readers can also feel the cohesion of a harmonious family, as well as the positive and optimistic influence possessed by each person.

Slow Parenting - The First Chapter: Opening the Time Capsule of Home

Since the concept of slow life was proposed, slowness has become the quality of life pursued by urban dwellers. I just saw a book on family education, titled "Slow Parenting". The first thought that came to mind was whether the eager-to-succeed Chinese parents would accept such a gentle and calm educational philosophy? There happens to be another similar type of parent-child education book, with a large-font title on the cover reading: "Ivy League at One Year Old". As you can imagine, such a dazzling quick-fix brand will attract the attention of parents even more.

Slow Parenting - The Second Chapter: Home is a Concentric Circle

Nowadays, families often have both spouses working, returning home late, children busy doing homework, and parents dealing with household chores. Every day is like this, going out early and coming back late, so there is little time for parent-child interaction. Occasionally, everyone goes out to the suburbs on weekends. But I suggest to parents that no matter if you go mountain climbing or fishing in the suburbs with your children, it's best to arrange more time and find opportunities to sit down and chat with your children. This is the most precious and important thing.

Slow Parenting - The Third Chapter: Gifts Children Give to Parents

My mother is very good at praising children. She often tells me: "Among all the children, I like you the most, I love you the most!" But she says the same thing to other siblings; this is my mother's kindness, but it causes us children to compare with each other and be jealous of each other. Everyone hopes to be better than other children, so as to win my mother's praise and attention. Therefore, when parents praise their children, they should be extra careful. Otherwise, children may develop the habit of jealousy in society and among people in the future.

Slow Parenting - The Fourth Chapter: When We "Card" Together: Carnegie in the Hei Family

Later, after thinking about leaving Guangqi Society and failing in the legislative election, although Hughes Aircraft Company in the United States wanted to hire him back, I felt that it was meaningless to take the old road again. Fortunately, I still had Carnegie's phone number at the time, so I asked Hei Youlong to call again. Anyway, treat a dead horse as if it were alive. Since this is an opportunity for a job that he likes, why not try again? Thus, after half a year, we made our second call to Carnegie. This time, the response from the other side was not so cold. They asked Hei Youlong to leave his experience information and promised to consider it.
