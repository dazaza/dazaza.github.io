When the object to be weighed or a loaded vehicle is placed on the load-bearing platform, under the force of gravity, the platform transmits the weight to the weighing sensor, causing the elastic body of the sensor to deform. The strain gauge bridge attached to the elastic body loses balance and outputs an electrical signal proportional to the weight value, which is amplified by a linear amplifier. Then, the signal is converted into a digital signal through an A/D converter, processed by the microprocessor (CPU) of the instrument, and directly displayed as weight data. With a configured printer, the weight data can be printed and recorded. If a computer is configured, the measurement data can be entered into a computer management system for comprehensive management.
