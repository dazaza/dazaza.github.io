1. BRT connects the First Wharf with Xiamen North Station; BRT property value is promising.

Starting from November 12, 2011, the terminal station of the BRT Express Line 1 has been extended from the current Institute of Science station to Xiamen North Station. As the transportation hub of Jimei New Town, the new transportation hub of Greater Xiamen, and the core artery linking the Yangtze River Delta and the Pearl River Delta, the economic agglomeration effect of Xiamen North Station cannot be underestimated. Meanwhile, the impact on properties along the BRT line is even more profound, comparable to metro-top properties in first-tier cities. After the BRT was extended to Xiamen North Station, it connected a series of urban facilities in southern and northern Xiamen into an organic system, complementing each other. This has woven a comprehensive convenient living network inside and outside the island for future residents of Lotus Shangyuan, allowing homeowners to enjoy a modern urban life where everything is interconnected.

2. Hua Da Student Street bus stop opens; Route 900 arrives nearby.

In the second half of 2011, Bus Routes 902 and 900 opened, filling the public transportation gap between the central area of Jimei New Town, Xingxi, and old Jimei. This organically linked the central area of Jimei, Jimei New Town, and Xingxi. The opening of the Hua Da Student Street bus stop near Lotus Shangyuan provided increasingly complete basic supporting facilities for the formation of a mature living area around Lotus Shangyuan.

3. Hua Da Student Street opens, initially forming the Lotus Shangyuan lifestyle circle.

The total construction area of Hua Da Student Street is 28,000 square meters, with a street length of 450 meters and a total width of 57 meters. Phase one has already opened, forming a complete commercial street together with the commercial part of Lotus Shangyuan. The southern section has also opened, with shops currently under renovation and will be fully operational after the Spring Festival. It has already gathered popularity and will provide convenience for the owners of Lotus Shangyuan and the surrounding teachers and students for work, study, and daily life. It is an ideal place for culture, shopping, leisure, sightseeing, and entertainment. Lotus Shangyuan passes through Hua Da Student Street, directly connecting from the west gate of Hua Da to the Hua Da campus.

4. Completion of pedestrian paths, bicycle paths, and accompanying landscapes for the Xinglin Bay scenic project.

The total investment in the entire Xinglin Bay scenic project exceeds 500 million yuan, including pedestrian paths, bicycle paths, docks, sports facilities, etc., which will make full use of the original ecological wetland landscape of the bay area to build an "ecological garden" mainly based on natural succession with auxiliary human management. Accompanying ecological trails, bird-watching corridors, science popularization squares, and other service and educational facilities are outdoor classrooms for citizens and tourists to observe wetland biodiversity. The first phase of the project, the western waterfront section, has already built a 3-kilometer-long pedestrian path, bicycle path, and accompanying landscape. 5. Jimei Middle School's attached school opens, truly qualifying as a school district property.

The already completed Jimei Middle School's attached school is one of the two ten-year schools in Jimei New Town, located within the daily life circle of Lotus Shangyuan in the waterside community. A total of 8 preschool classes, 48 elementary school classes, and 24 junior high school classes have been constructed, and enrollment began in September 2011. Children in the Lotus Shangyuan community have been confirmed to attend this school.

6. Xinglin Bay Business Operation Center tops out, nearly 150 large and medium-sized enterprises signed.

The total land area of the Xinglin Bay Business Operation Center is approximately 121,982 square meters, with a total construction area reaching 550,000 square meters of urban complexes. The rich resource advantages will make the operation center become a new type of international business center with good ecology and environmental landscape, further boosting the upgrade of Jimei's modern service industry and accelerating the formation of Xiamen's new core. It is reported that the operation center has signed contracts with nearly 150 large and medium-sized enterprises, and here will be the newly rising Jimei CBD commercial center.

On December 30, 2011, the construction site at the northeast intersection of Xinglin Bay Road and Heyue Road in the Jimei New Town area was won by Xiamen Xinglin Construction Development Co., Ltd.
