2010 Wedding Planning - Introduction

Since the reform and opening up, traditional Chinese weddings have gradually evolved from folk ethnic customs to standardized mass weddings. For example, the initial practice of beating gongs and drums during weddings, decorating with lanterns, and having dragon dances are now gradually fading away. The modern trend among young people is to take wedding photos and host banquets...

2010 Wedding Planning - Overview of Planning

Planning - Location Selection (can be planned separately based on city characteristics): Guilin. With its unique natural and cultural landscapes, Guilin is a world-famous tourist city with a romantic atmosphere, making it suitable for honeymoon trips. It is also an ideal place to hold wedding celebrations and banquets.
