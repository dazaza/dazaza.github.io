Unexpectedly, the day after the wedding, the groom announced his fear of marriage and disappeared on his own, demanding a divorce. Resigned to fate, Mao Doudou and Yu Wei couldn't help but seriously consider each other. Yu Wei revealed the purpose of his consecutive blind dates, explaining that he had been pretending to be his drowned childhood friend and sending his own photos to the friend's grandmother for many years.
