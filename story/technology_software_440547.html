Here the important thing is to share some experience and skills, not preaching. Today I will first write about the most popular map Desert Gray.

First, the birth point: It refers to the position where players are born at the beginning of each round. This is very important for friends who love to charge and attack. T and CT have eight birth positions respectively. The T birth position closest to point A can switch to a knife and directly rush to the A door after being born. After rushing out, throw a O3 directly behind the A Avenue, then pull it flat. The fastest CT also appears at the moment when O3 is thrown out. The T birth position closest to point B can rush to the B cave. In classic games, switch guns and move forward one step away from the entrance of the B cave F home. You can rush out of the cave before the opponent's sniper goes on stage (Listen carefully to the sound of B1, the closest position of the CT side can directly pull from B1 to B2). As for the two positions of CT closest to the middle door, you can go straight up the middle road or pull B1, both of which have the effect of unexpectedly seizing advantageous terrain.

Second, sneaking up on people: Don't squat in dead corners when sneaking up on people. If the other party has an aiming point, the reaction may not keep up. It is better to take a step forward and block the corner point, which will be much better than squatting in the dead corner.

Third, gunfight: If you are not confident in your shooting skills, it is recommended that you do not stand still when fighting with people far away, nor run in one direction all the time. Taking my own experience as an example, it is much easier to hit the head of a person who does not move than the head of a person who swings left and right. And I am easily eliminated by people who swing back and forth and shoot at the body.

Fourth, Sniper rifle: The sniper rifle in CS is much more useful than in CF, because of its fast mirror opening and small jump instant mirror. But anyway, I feel that playing CF and playing sniper in CS, mastering the skills of hitting points will make a qualitative leap. The hitting point is that when you flash out of the cover, open the mirror at the same time, and aim the sniper point at the place where there are often people after flashing out. Then shoot directly without looking at people. This depends on the understanding of the map.
