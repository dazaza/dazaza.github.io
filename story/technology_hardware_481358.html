June 24th's big three components quotation: memory falls again, Seagate 2TB hard disk drive price cut

by shilan1770 on 2011-08-25 10:10:24

Today in Beijing's Zhongguancun spot market for Intel processors, the computer configuration prices of Intel processors have fallen. Among them, the i5 2500K has slightly dropped by 20 yuan, with a quote of 1440 yuan, featuring Turbo Boost 2.0 technology. The loose-piece i5 2310 has further reduced in price, slightly dropping by 10 yuan, with a quote of 1050 yuan, making it more cost-effective than the loose-piece i5 2300. Additionally, four other processor models have also seen price reductions, ranging from 5 to 10 yuan.

Today in Beijing's Zhongguancun spot market for AMD processors, today's AMD processor prices remain unchanged. Yesterday, four mainstream old 45nm loose-piece Athlon processors saw price drops. The author received two versions of information from the market: one indicates that loose-piece processors will fall in price, while another message suggests that boxed processors may see price increases.

Today in Beijing's Zhongguancun desktop memory spot market, Transcend 4GB/1333 memory has again dropped by 2 yuan, with a quote of 125 yuan. The overall price of 4GB/1333 memory is trending towards the 120-yuan mark. The price of 2GB/1333 memory continues to decline slightly.

Today in Beijing's Zhongguancun notebook memory spot market, there is no overall change in price. Due to the drop in desktop memory prices, the impact on notebook memory prices is slower, and the pressure to reduce prices has significantly increased. Merchants are purchasing smaller quantities each time due to concerns about unsold stock, as holding onto inventory overnight results in losses.

Today in Beijing's Zhongguancun desktop hard drive spot market, hard drive prices have once again fallen. Seagate 2TB hard drives have dropped by 15 yuan, with a quote of only 485 yuan. Moreover, Seagate 1TB/7200 RPM hard drives have slightly dropped by 5 yuan, with a quote of 345 yuan. Western Digital/Hitachi hard drive prices remain unchanged.

Today in Beijing's Zhongguancun notebook hard drive spot market, there are minor fluctuations in price without significant changes. Seagate/Hitachi prices remain stable. Yesterday, Seagate/Hitachi notebook hard drives saw price drops, with decreases ranging from 10 to 15 yuan, primarily involving 500GB/750GB 5400 RPM notebook hard drives.

The advent of 32nm Athlon processors is pressuring Intel processors to lower their prices.

Today in Beijing's Zhongguancun Intel processor spot market, Intel processor prices have fallen. Among them, the i5 2500K has slightly dropped by 20 yuan, with a quote of 1440 yuan. The loose-piece i5 2310 has further reduced in price, slightly dropping by 10 yuan, with a quote of 1050 yuan, making it more cost-effective than the loose-piece i5 2300. Additionally, four other processor models have seen price reductions, ranging from 5 to 10 yuan.

Due to competition from the boxed AMD 32nm new Athlon quad-core processor priced at 580 yuan, both Intel's 32nm i5 and i3 processors face intense competitive pressure. Although Intel holds a performance advantage, AMD offers better cost-effectiveness, initially curbing the market's one-sided favoritism towards Intel (Note: all prices below were obtained by editors at counters in Zhongguancun, provided by certified agents, and subject to change; they are for reference only).

Intel loose-piece i5 2310 quad-core arrives in bulk, with a quote of 1060 yuan.

The i5 2310 frequency represents an upgrade model of the i5 2300, with a frequency of 2.9G, capable of Turbo Boost acceleration up to 3.2G.
