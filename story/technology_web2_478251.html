College student drowned while swimming due to suspected lack of strength (Photo)

by hj1718004 on 2011-08-16 19:31:34

The drowning victim was recovered. Photo by reporter Gong Hui.

At around 12:00 noon yesterday, Xiao Ma (a pseudonym), a first-year university student in Jinan, went swimming with two classmates at the Yan Pool near their school. Xiao Ma swam from the north bank to the south bank with his friends. After resting on the south bank for a while, Xiao Ma called his companions to swim back to the starting point. At 12:50 pm, when Xiao Ma was about 10 meters away from the north bank, he sank into the water. The 22-year-old boy left this world forever... Reported by Wang Xiaodi.

12:50

He had an accident.

No struggle or cries for help before sinking.

Yesterday at noon, Xiao Ma invited his classmates Xiao Lin (a pseudonym) and Xiao Jie (a pseudonym) to go swimming at the Yan Pool near their school. The three of them raced from the north bank of the pool to the south bank. After reaching the destination, they rested on the rocks for a while and then jumped back into the water to swim back to the starting point.

Xiao Lin and Xiao Jie swam ahead, followed closely by Xiao Ma. When they were about 10 meters away from the north bank, Xiao Lin looked back and immediately swam back because he saw Xiao Ma slowly sinking. However, before sinking, Xiao Ma did not struggle or even shout "help" once.

Xiao Lin and Xiao Jie quickly swam towards the direction where Xiao Ma sank. When they reached Xiao Ma, he had lost consciousness. They tried to pull him ashore but ultimately failed. They immediately went ashore and took out their mobile phones to call the police. After making the call, they searched around the Yan Pool for people to save Xiao Ma. Xiao Lin looked at his watch; it was already 12:55 pm. There were almost no people swimming at the Yan Pool at that time.

12:58 - 13:30

Rescue difficulties

Seeking help from professional divers

At 12:58 pm, the patrol officers from the third team of Lixia Patrol arrived at the scene. A few minutes later, the 120 emergency vehicle also arrived. However, because they did not have any swimming equipment, the rescue operation faced certain difficulties. Just then, they encountered a middle-aged man who came to swim. The patrol officer asked if he could help look for Xiao Ma. After learning about the incident, the man immediately prepared his swimming gear. After Xiao Lin told him the exact location where Xiao Ma had the accident, he swam towards the place where Xiao Ma sank.

"He swam there, first kicking the water with his feet, probably testing the depth of the water. Then he dived under the water. About 30 seconds later, he surfaced and dove again. He repeated this several times. Finally, he swam back and told us that he dived to a depth of 5 meters, but it was pitch black underwater, and he couldn't see anything," said Patrol Officer Wang Qingguo. Subsequently, the man informed the patrol officer that the place where Xiao Ma sank was very deep and professional divers were needed to find him. Around 1:30 pm yesterday, I arrived at the scene. By then, the school leaders had already arrived, and Mr. Miao, who was responsible for student affairs, said, "We will immediately contact professional divers to recover the child."

16:12

Diver finds Xiao Ma

He was buried in the mud 6 meters underwater

Around 3:30 pm yesterday, professional divers from a diving club in Jinan arrived at the scene. After a brief exploration of the surrounding environment, two divers equipped with diving gear and let Xiao Lin show them the location where Xiao Ma had the accident. First, they fixed an inflatable buoy at the spot where Xiao Ma sank, then both divers submerged to begin searching.

Forty seconds later, the two divers surfaced, removed the inflatable buoy, and swam back to the shore. One of the divers told the patrol officer, "We found the person, but his body is buried in the mud at a depth of 6 meters. We've already tied our own rope around him, and everyone can pull him ashore by holding the rope at the shore." At 4:12 pm, the patrol officer and the 120 emergency personnel pulled Xiao Ma ashore together. Unfortunately, Xiao Ma had permanently stopped breathing.

"This child is really pitiful. He just started his first year at university and now he's gone. His family is in Heze, and the school leadership has already informed them. They are on their way to Jinan," Patrol Officer Jia Jianren told reporters. After Xiao Ma's body was recovered, many onlookers speculated that the reason for his drowning might be due to exhaustion, as the distance from south to north of the pool is approximately 200 meters. Currently, the cause of Xiao Ma's death is still under further investigation.
