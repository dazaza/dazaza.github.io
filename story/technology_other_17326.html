Jiuzhaigou Scenic Area Tour Guide Map

The tour guide map of Jiuzhaigou scenic area provides you with references for understanding the attractions in the沟and reasonably arranging the Jiuzhaigou travel route.

The Jiuzhaigou Scenic Area has currently opened four tourist scenic lines: Shuzheng, Rize, Zechawa, and Zharu, which are more than 60 kilometers long. The landscapes are distributed in six major scenic spots including Shuzheng, Nuorilang, Jianyan, Changhai, Zharu, and Tianhai. Represented by the three gorges and one hundred eighteen seas, the main attractions include water scenery such as five shoals, twelve waterfalls, ten streams, and dozens of springs, which jointly compose the natural landscape of the high mountain river valley with the Twelve Peaks of Jiuzhaigou.
