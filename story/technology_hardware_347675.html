Urgent disclosure of multiple financial reports, institutional adjustment of warehouse thinking changes m5

by xl0922958 on 2010-04-18 16:11:09

Editor's Note: The market is currently in a period of dense earnings report releases. As the disclosure of 2009 annual reports nears its end, the disclosure of 2010 first-quarter reports is entering its climax, and at the same time, some companies are starting to disclose their 2010 mid-year performance forecasts.

In such a mixed period, two-way trading (both long and short) is gradually becoming the norm. Investors should calm down and carefully examine the listed companies' financial reports, observe how institutional investors make their investments, and gain new insights from them.

New opportunities emerge during the dense earnings report disclosure period. As of April 16, 1283 A-share companies in Shanghai and Shenzhen have disclosed their 2009 annual reports, 66 companies have disclosed their 2010 first-quarter reports, and 30 companies have forecasted their 2010 mid-year performance. From the current data statistics of these companies' financial reports, the majority of listed companies are experiencing an upward trend in profitability both year-on-year and quarter-on-quarter. With the gradual cooling down of speculative stocks, fundamentals may become the most reliable perspective for investors to select stocks at present.

Post-“2009 Annual Report Period”: Opportunities arise for stocks bought by institutional seats for the first time.

Currently, the total number of A-share companies that have announced their 2009 annual performance accounts for 73.27% of all A-share companies, with less than one-third of companies yet to disclose their annual reports. As the 2010 first-quarter reports are being released enthusiastically, the market is entering the post-“2009 Annual Report Period.”

During the previous speculation on annual report trends, “high dividend payout” was a major hotspot. Despite everyone understanding that “high dividend payout” was merely a numerical game played by listed companies, there was still enthusiasm for the expected price recovery after dividends. Market performance showed that before the release of annual reports, people speculated on dividend expectations, as seen in the sharp rise of individual stocks like禾盛新材and东湖高新; after the official distribution plan was implemented, investors began to shift towards speculating on price recovery trends, as seen in the trading of individual stocks like东方园林,三安光电,丹化科技, and歌尔声学. However, with the formal release of the 2010 first-quarter reports, the star effect of speculating on “high dividend payouts” in the 2009 annual reports is gradually losing its luster. Data shows that among the 19 companies that announced high dividend payouts since April 10, only大厦股份achieved a daily limit-up, while名流置业and乐普医疗experienced significant declines.

At this point in time, from an annual report investment perspective, investors can focus on those stocks where recent annual reports show concentrated shareholdings and where institutional seats have made their first appearance in buying stocks, indicating potential opportunities.

Latest cases show that once some stocks showing concentrated shareholdings appear on the dragon-tiger lists in trading seats after being bought by institutional exclusive seats for the first time, they often experience a noticeable upward trend, such as机构exclusive seats buying创元科技for 123.42 million yuan on April 8 and again for 187.84 million yuan on April 12. By April 15, the stock had risen by 34.09% in six trading days. Similarly, after重庆啤酒announced its annual report data, it was the first time on April 14 that institutional exclusive seats were net buyers of 117.37 million yuan, resulting in two consecutive daily limit-ups. Other examples include铁龙物流and华东科技where institutional seats made their first large-scale purchases recently, leading to significant stock price increases.

2010 First Quarter Reports: Enter industries with high prosperity and unexpected earnings

On April 8,巨轮股份unveiled the curtain for the 2010 first-quarter reports, thus officially replacing the 2009 annual report speculation with the 2010 first-quarter report speculation.

As of April 16, the financial reports of 66 companies that have already disclosed their 2010 first-quarter performance show that apart from*ST华源which did not have net profits in the first quarter of 2009 and神剑股份and丹甫股份which did not have net profits in the fourth quarter of 2009, the remaining 63 listed companies collectively achieved a net profit of 3.011 billion yuan in the first quarter, representing a year-on-year increase of 103.34% compared to the first quarter of 2009 and a quarter-on-quarter increase of 17.09% compared to the fourth quarter of 2009. In addition, a large number of pre-announcements of positive first-quarter results also indicate that positive announcements will be the mainstream of the first-quarter performance pre-announcements this year. Excluding companies that have already published their first-quarter reports, out of the 242 companies that have pre-announced their first-quarter results, 197 expect positive trends, accounting for 81.4%. Individual stocks like獐子岛,宁波华翔,法拉电子,金种子酒showed a sharp rise after announcing their likely substantial pre-announcements of increased performance.

While investors focus on listed companies with significantly increased first-quarter results, they should also pay attention to the overall prosperity of the industries in which these companies operate. Only by fully understanding the development status of the industries in the first quarter can one buy stocks in industries with good prosperity and first-quarter results exceeding expectations. According to the latest research report from CITIC Securities, it is believed that sectors with first-quarter results exceeding expectations mainly concentrate in aviation, electronics, machinery, agriculture, forestry, animal husbandry, fisheries, medicine, and automobiles. The latest macroeconomic data also shows that heavy industry grew by 22.1% in the first quarter, light industry by 14.1%, automobile category by 39.8%, furniture category by 37.6%, and household appliances and audiovisual equipment category by 29.6%... These data confirm the possibility of exceeding expectations in the first-quarter results of the industries mentioned by CITIC Securities.

2010 Mid-Year Reports: Lotus Tips Emerge

With the sequential disclosure of first-quarter results, the mid-year performance pre-announcements for 2010 are also beginning to emerge. Currently, a total of 30 A-share companies have taken the lead in issuing mid-year performance pre-announcements, with 27 companies reporting pre-announcements of growth, slight growth, continued profitability, or turning losses into gains. The number of companies with growth rates exceeding 30% has reached as high as 25, fully demonstrating the ongoing trend of "spring blossoms."

From the statistical analysis of the announced growth rates, among the 27 companies with positive mid-year performance pre-announcements, five are expected to achieve growth in net profits in the second quarter compared to the first quarter, with growth rates exceeding 100%. For instance,威尔泰realized a net profit of 1.4203 million yuan in the middle of 2009. If the forecasted 50% growth materializes, the net profit for the middle of 2010 could reach 2.1304 million yuan, successfully reversing the loss of 0.2365 million yuan in the first quarter of this year. 歌尔声学expects a 280%~310% increase in net profit for the middle of 2010. Based on a net profit of 2.06004 million yuan in the middle of 2009, if it grows by 310%, the net profit for the middle of 2010 would be approximately 8.446 million yuan. After deducting the 2.70404 million yuan realized in the first quarter, the single quarter in the second quarter could potentially realize 5.742 million yuan, representing a quarter-on-quarter increase of 112.35%. The reasons for improved performance mainly stem from improvements in the company's own operating conditions rather than non-core investment returns from financial or real estate sectors.

Fund Investment Cautiousness: Growth Stocks Favored

Facing a complex market environment in the first quarter and uncertain policies in the second quarter, how will institutional investors adjust their holding strategies? Although the quarterly reports of funds and other institutions that clearly reveal fund holding directions have not yet been released, the latest disclosed partial quarterly reports of listed companies provide us with an initial glimpse.

Institutional Holdings Outperform the Market Overall

As of April 16, a total of 66 companies have disclosed their 2010 quarterly reports. From the changes in their top ten floating shareholders, 55 stocks feature institutional presence (including funds, QFII, insurance, social security, etc., excluding general legal entities), among which the top ten floating shareholders of歌尔声学,武汉凡谷,博瑞传播are entirely composed of institutions.

From the currently disclosed quarterly report data, stocks newly acquired by institutions include武汉塑料,景谷林业,大东南,三峡水利, etc., and newly listed stocks held by institutions in the first quarter include北京科锐,丹甫股份,神剑股份, etc. Among the 25 stocks increased by institutions in the first quarter, seven stocks including国脉科技,康恩贝have been increased by more than 10 million shares, with华芳纺织and丹化科技increasing over 20% in April. Compared to the 2009 annual report, institutions have continuously increased holdings in国脉科技,康恩贝,日照港,超声电子, etc., and due to continuous institutional increases, stocks like国脉科技that rose against the market trend by 31.32% in the first quarter despite a 5.13% drop in the overall market appeared. Although the stock fell by 3.14% in April, the Shanghai Composite Index only rose by 0.68% during the same period, further proving the strength and resilience of continuously increased institutional holdings.

From the overall market value changes of institutionally held stocks in April, as of April 16, the overall market value of 55 stocks increased by 3.3% in April, while the Shanghai Composite Index only rose by 1.80% during the same period. From the individual stock changes, 28 out of 55 stocks outperformed the Shanghai Composite Index. Therefore, from the current situation, the institutional holdings shown in the quarterly report demonstrate stronger comprehensive capabilities than the market average.

Increased Holdings Favor Weak-Cycle Defense

Due to the overall volatile adjustment state of the market in the first quarter, blue-chip stocks performed poorly, while small-cap stocks were active. From the currently increased stocks by institutions, growth potential is the main factor considered by institutional investors when selecting stocks. Among the 21 stocks rated by institutions, except for 6 neutral ratings, the remaining 15 are rated as 'increase' or 'buy'.

The valuation levels of these 21 stocks are relatively high, but even though short-term valuations are too high, it does not hinder institutional willingness to hold these stocks. Institutions value the growth potential of these stocks. Institutional prediction data shows that although 16 of these 21 stocks had valuations exceeding 50 times at the end of the first quarter of 2010, based on institutional predictions for 2011 valuations, only 3 stocks—武汉塑料,中通客车,益民商业—will exceed 50 times. Precisely because institutions can predict the exceeding expectations of individual stock quarterly reports in advance, this becomes an important reason for some institutions to increase holdings in these stocks, such as significant increases in 益民商业,怡亚通,大洋电机with first-quarter earnings growing substantially, single-quarter EPS increasing by 170% year-over-year. 大东南,*ST华源,顺发恒业showed single-quarter EPS growth exceeding 60%, confirming this view.

From the current industry attributes of increased holdings by institutions, the increased stocks are not the cyclical industry stocks imagined by everyone, but are concentrated in non-cyclical industries or technology stocks, represented by electronic information stocks 超声电子,歌尔声学,国脉科技, pharmaceutical stocks 康恩贝,*ST天方,紫鑫药业, etc. Looking back at the first quarter of 2009, among comparable stocks, institutional increased holdings mainly favored chemical and real estate industry stocks.

Social Security, QFII Increase Holdings While Funds Remain Cautious

From the current data, the largest institutional investor in the market, funds, have begun to turn cautious. According to existing quarterly report data, although 114 funds have intervened in 43 stocks, they have reduced holdings in 21 stocks to a certain extent, especially reducing holdings in stocks with larger previous gains, such as 上投摩根内需动力,华安中小盘成长reducing 福耀玻璃by 26.81 million shares and 15.26 million shares respectively, 广发聚丰reducing 特变电工by 18.59 million shares, 南方成份精选reducing 长航油运by 17.13 million shares, etc.

In fact, the cautious sentiment of funds is also reflected in the process of new fund establishment. Taking the newly listed Changxin CSI Central Enterprises 100 this week as an example, the fund announcement shows that as of April 9, the fund's stock investment amount was only 249,600 yuan, accounting for only 0.03% of the total assets of the fund. In just half a month since its establishment, the fund has invested only more than 200,000 yuan in stock configuration, reflecting its cautious attitude towards establishment.

Additionally, from the quarterly fund investment review published by华夏fund, one can also see the cautious attitude of华夏fund. It has overall increased its defensiveness, made structural adjustments, started avoiding cyclical industries, and increased holdings in growth industries. Its investment portfolio for华夏大盘精选in the first quarter also shows that it maintained good defensiveness and flexibly grasped structural investment opportunities.

Compared to the caution of funds, social security and QFII have made certain increases. Among the 8 stocks where social security positions appear, only 黑猫股份has been reduced, while the remaining 7 have been increased. Notably, 福耀玻璃and 特变电工作have become stocks with significant increases by social security funds, which were previously significantly reduced by funds. Similarly, among the 11 stocks held by QFII funds, QFII generally holds, with increases in a few stocks. The largest purchase was Columbia University’s new purchase of 13 million shares in 日照港.

Stocks Held by Fund Companies Deserve Attention

Although only 66 listed companies have disclosed their quarterly reports so far, 11 stocks clearly show collective holdings by multiple funds under the same fund company. This characteristic is most evident in华夏fund. Four funds under华夏fund heavily hold 武汉塑料and丹化科技, three funds heavily hold 国脉科技and 北京科锐, four funds under银华fund heavily hold南玻A. 中邮fund company has mobilized all three of its funds to heavily hold 益民商业, showcasing the institutional confidence in the stock through collective holdings. Judging from the recent market performance of these stocks, the funds seem to value their future market performance.

Additionally, among the top ten floating shareholders of 神剑股份only two funds from 宝盈are present, and among the top ten floating shareholders of 华芳纺织only two funds from 摩根士丹利华鑫are present. Such stocks dominated by a single fund management company are also worth investors' close attention.

Consumer Industry Becomes the "Treasure" in a Volatile Market

Since the beginning of the year, amidst the widespread decline of "strong cycle" industries, the defensive characteristics of consumer industries with clear earnings growth and favorable expectations have emerged.

In the first quarter, the CSI 300 index fell by 9.69%, while the pharmaceutical and catering tourism sectors rose by 4.3% and 9.9% respectively, demonstrating a certain degree of resistance.

Pharmaceuticals: "Export Recovery + Domestic Demand Pull" Prospects Look Promising

With the increase in national investment and the promotion of the basic drug system policy this year, the revenue and profit of the pharmaceutical industry have regained high-speed growth momentum. From January to February, the pharmaceutical manufacturing industry realized cumulative revenue of 143 billion yuan, up 28% year-on-year; achieving a profit total of 14.75 billion yuan, up 45.7% year-on-year. As of this Friday, 11 pharmaceutical companies have announced their first-quarter reports or performance forecasts, with Zhejiang Pharmaceutical, New & Cheng, and others seeing net profit growth of over 100%.

CICC believes that the positive impact of the expansion of the medical insurance catalog and the basic drug system will continue to manifest in 2009-2010, with industry sales revenue expected to grow by 25%-30%. "Export Recovery + Domestic Demand Pull" is the investment theme of the industry, with chemical raw material pharmaceuticals affected by export recovery and proprietary varieties of traditional Chinese medicine companies entering the medical insurance catalog being worth investor attention.

The basic drug catalog was launched in August 2009, and provinces initiated basic drug bidding at the beginning of 2010. Over 100 varieties of traditional Chinese medicine were selected into the catalog, with proprietary varieties accounting for more than 20%. Listed drugs in the catalog will be mandatorily used, benefiting the increase in the share of traditional Chinese medicine. Proprietary products, lacking competition from similar varieties, face much less potential price reduction threats, and some products might even see price increases. Therefore, traditional Chinese medicine companies with proprietary products benefit more significantly. For instance, Yunnan Baiyao has varieties that account for 53.8% of sales revenue, and also Huarui Sanjiu, Ma Yinglong, Jinling Pharmaceuticals, and Qianjin Pharmaceuticals will benefit.

On the other hand, with the recovery of the macroeconomy and the gradual restoration of exports, the worst times for the chemical raw material pharmaceutical industry have passed, and raw material prices are gradually rising. The performance growth rate of sub-industries turned positive at the end of last year, and from January to February this year, they achieved income growth of 31.69% and profit growth of 67.32%, becoming the biggest highlight of the pharmaceutical industry. Vitamin A, Vitamin E, and Vitamin B5 are generally used as additives in livestock feed to supplement nutrients needed by animals. Bohai Securities analyst Wu Yang believes that "it is expected that with the arrival of the traditional peak season for export demand in May-June, export volumes will see significant growth, providing short-term investment opportunities for corresponding raw material pharmaceutical companies."

Shenwan Hongyuan analyst Luo Yi expects that the prices of Vitamin A and Vitamin E will moderately increase in 2010, with industry leaders Zhejiang Pharmaceutical and New & Cheng having 30%-40% growth space in performance, and their valuations are currently low, making them suitable for value investors to enter at lower levels. Xinfu Pharmaceutical, which has absolute pricing power in the Vitamin B5 industry, raised prices by 10% after the Spring Festival. Wu Yang stated that "this price hike is just the beginning, and the price of Vitamin B5 is expected to continue to rise, contributing about 20% to its performance this year."

Tourism: Regional Concepts and Event Factors Complement Each Other

In 2009, affected by the financial crisis and avian flu and other adverse factors, the global tourism market generally declined, but domestic tourists in China still increased by 11%, reaching 1.9 billion visits, making it the brightest spot in international tourism. During the Spring Festival Golden Week in 2010, the country received 125 million visitors, generating 64.62 billion yuan in tourism revenue, increasing by 14.8% and 26.9% respectively, indicating that the tourism industry in China is recovering. Additionally, every year from April to October is the peak tourist season for most regions in China, and with the upcoming "May Day" Golden Week, securities analysts generally expect a positive outlook for the tourism sector in the second quarter.

Recruitment Securities analyst Su Ping revealed to our publication that policy-wise, the National Tourism Work Conference is expected to be held in the second quarter, possibly bringing further industry-friendly policies, and some event-related factors might also boost the tourism sector.

“There are rumors that the 'Yunnan Construction Facing Southwest Region Bridgehead Plan' is about to be approved. It is expected that more regional development plans will be released in the second quarter, with the likelihood of encouraging regional tourism policies in Yunnan and Guangxi provinces being relatively high. I am optimistic about Lijiang Tourism, Expo Shares, and Guilin Tourism located in Yunnan and Guangxi,” he said.

Furthermore, the Shanghai World Expo will open on May 1, expecting around 30% of the 70 million visitors to the Shanghai World Expo to continue touring in the East China region. According to the latest market expectations, supported by a vast domestic tourism market, the 70 million visitor projection for the Shanghai World Expo is conservative, estimating that the Shanghai World Expo will receive around 80 million to 100 million visitors. The 16th Asian Games will be held in Guangzhou, China, from November 12 to 27, 2010, and Su Ping believes that Huangshan Tourism, Jinjiang Shares, and Orient Hotel, benefiting from the World Expo and Asian Games, are worth tracking. ■
