Virgin Atlantic Relocates to Terminal 2 of Pudong International Airport on March 26, 2008 Document Number: 08JL0318-05

On March 26, Virgin Atlantic will officially relocate from Terminal 1 to Terminal 2 at Shanghai Pudong International Airport. At that time, the VS251 flight (code-share: CA7021) and VS250 (code-share: CA7022) operating between Shanghai and London will take off and land at the new Terminal 2.

Check-in counters for Virgin Atlantic are located at counters 1-10 on Island F in Terminal 2, www.cqkeqiao.com, near entrances 24 and 25 of the terminal, www.cqpiaowu.com. Check-in procedures start three hours before departure and close one hour before departure.
