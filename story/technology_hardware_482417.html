The mineral resources in Xinjiang are very abundant, among which coal, stone materials, sand and gravel are the most prominent. Yi Sheng Mining Machinery series of sand and gravel equipment (crusher, sand making machine, iron ore equipment, industrial grinding mill, sand making production line, stone material production line, sand and gravel production line equipment) are widely applied in various large-scale engineering project bases in Xinjiang, including railways and highways and other infrastructure constructions. The state is vigorously promoting the western development policy to promote the industrialization and urbanization process in the west. With the heating up of various large-scale construction projects, there is a strong demand for sand and gravel aggregates, which is a crucial period for sand and gravel equipment to stand at a new starting point, achieve new breakthroughs, and reach new steps. The great strides in western infrastructure construction have injected new momentum into sand and gravel equipment. Large-scale sand and gravel production lines, stone material production lines, and industrial powder production line equipment from Yi Sheng Mining Machinery have entered Xinjiang to support the western construction.

It is understood that the sudden change in supply and demand in the cement market in Xinjiang has accelerated the pace of entering the sand and gravel production line. In the first half of this year, due to the massive commencement of assistance work from various provinces and cities and major livelihood projects, some areas experienced temporary cement shortages, resulting in rapid price increases. Leveraging the rich mineral resources in the Xinjiang region to develop the sand and gravel aggregate industry is not only a trend but also offers enormous market space and competitive advantages with a very broad prospect.

In the national 12th Five-Year Plan, places like Xinjiang and Gansu are the focus of the western development shift. A large number of infrastructure projects are waiting to be launched. At the same time, the state's deployment to build Xinjiang into a strategic channel for national energy resources connecting domestically and internationally makes Xinjiang’s transportation position more prominent in both domestic and international transportation networks. As a production base for crushers, grinding mills, and sand making machines, the company has increased its attention to industrial projects in Xinjiang and other places.

Xinjiang is formulating more preferential policies and measures to strongly attract enterprises and groups within and outside the region, as well as private capital, to invest in hydropower development, participate in the construction of water conservancy projects, roads, bridges, and other infrastructure, forming a diversified border development mechanism.

It is expected that future investments in water conservancy projects will double, and the domestic hydropower and water conservancy market is developing rapidly. Industry competition will inevitably become fiercer, imposing higher quality requirements on corresponding auxiliary enterprises, especially sand and gravel aggregate suppliers. This requires enterprises to innovate and break through issues such as process design and equipment selection. Yi Sheng Mining Machinery, as a specialized manufacturer of sand making equipment, has made comprehensive preparations and is determined to spare no effort in assisting national water conservancy projects.

The VSI high-efficiency sand making equipment produced by the company is the crystallization of Yi Sheng's many years of wisdom. The VSI high-efficiency sand making equipment from Yi Sheng Mining Machinery has become the preferred equipment for stone shaping, artificial sand making, refractory materials, national large-scale engineering projects, railway, highway construction, and large-scale water conservancy engineering projects. The VSI impact fine crushing sand making machine is suitable for medium and fine crushing of various ores and rocks with different hardnesses, such as iron ore, non-ferrous metals, silicon carbide, quartz sand, basalt, etc., for medium and fine crushing operations of high-hardness materials. The VSI impact fine crushing sand making machine can directly serve as secondary crushing equipment for jaw crushers and cone crushers, or it can be used independently for shaping.

As is well known, the construction of water conservancy projects requires a large amount of sand and gravel materials, and the sand making machine is currently the most widely used artificial sand and gravel production equipment. With the vigorous development of China's water conservancy projects, sand making machine manufacturers will also welcome their golden development opportunity.

At the same time, the Ministry of Finance issued the "Opinion of the Ministry of Finance on Fully Implementing the Spirit of the Central Water Conservancy Work Conference," emphasizing significantly increasing the total amount and growth rate of fiscal funds invested in water conservancy and clearly proposing to prioritize water conservancy as a national infrastructure construction field, further increasing the proportion of water conservancy construction funds in national fixed asset investments. Additionally, the Ministry of Finance encourages financial institutions to further increase credit funds for water conservancy construction.

It is understood that various regions in China are increasing investment in water conservancy projects. Especially with the tilt of national policies towards the water conservancy industry, water conservancy engineering construction will enter a golden development period. It is reported that in the first half of the year, 2.1 billion yuan in funds were implemented, ensuring the steady progress of key water conservancy projects in Qinghai. Meanwhile, Xinjiang continues to increase water conservancy engineering construction to benefit all ethnic groups. The 18 settlement-promoting water conservancy projects constructed in Xinjiang include 17 reservoirs and one water diversion project, which is the first time in Xinjiang's water conservancy construction history. Guangxi collected 130 million yuan in the first half of the year for major water conservancy projects.
