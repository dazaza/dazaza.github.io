Plot Summary of "Good Fortune and Health":

Sixty years ago, a mountain girl Huang Chunxiang (played by Tan Aizhen) accidentally saved Yan Wangcai (played by Ding Qiang), who had collapsed while gathering herbs and mistakenly triggered a trap. They fell in love at first sight, but unfortunately, they had to part ways sorrowfully under the beautiful Paulownia rain. Chunxiang kept the watch that Wangcai left her, guarding it for six decades like a dream. Chunxiang has always hoped that one day Wangcai would come back to find her. This deep affection moved her granddaughter Xie Fuan (played by Ruby Lin) immensely...

"If only I could have such a beautiful first love..."

Yan Yungao, the chairman of the world's largest Chinese medicine group 'Babao Tang Pharmaceutical Group,' was unconscious due to a car accident and lay weakly in the ambulance racing through country roads to the hospital for emergency treatment. Soon after, his grandchildren, who were like wolves waiting for him to die so they could inherit his estate, arrived one by one. Upon seeing Yungao critically ill, everyone except his carefree grandson Yan Yang (played by Show Chin-kai), who genuinely felt sad, was actually happy. Even when the doctors tried to save Yungao, they all strongly opposed it. "Let Dad (Grandpa) go peacefully!" These words reached the ears of Fuan, who happened to be secretly treating patients with athlete's foot at the hospital to earn extra money. To her, it sounded like something from outer space. With a strong sense of justice, Fuan decided not to let Yungao's grandchildren succeed in their plans. She sneaked into the emergency room and used Eucommia leaves to save Yungao. Smelling the familiar scent, Yungao vaguely woke up, dimly recognizing Fuan's face, recalling a familiar face, and faintly called out:

"Chunxiang?"

Yungao's eldest grandson Dafeng (played by Blue Luan) saw Yungao unexpectedly saved and immediately scolded Fuan for meddling. Both left bad impressions on each other without knowing that this was just the beginning of their destiny.

Fuan is not only a very righteous person but also someone who deeply cares for her family. She takes good care of her sick grandmother Chunxiang and her young brother Pidan. Because Fuan gave Yungao more time from the hands of death, upon waking up, Yungao was very disappointed with his inhumane grandchildren. However, Yungao knew that this was his own retribution for raising them to be like vultures. But this wasn't what Yungao regretted most; what he regretted most was lying to and deceiving Chunxiang sixty years ago!

It turns out that the Wangcai mentioned by Chunxiang was none other than him. Back then, he approached Chunxiang to swindle away the secret formula for treating gastrointestinal discomfort and then changed his name to Yan Yungao, using the formula to open Babao Tang and become wealthy. Ironically, Chunxiang steadfastly guarded this first love, while Yungao only remembered it after meeting Fuan...

Yungao decided to make amends to Chunxiang and revealed that he would announce the successor on his birthday. This news caused great tension among the Yan family members, unsure of themselves. Following the advice of his "underground girlfriend" reporter Zhenzhen (played by Wang Yiren), Dafeng personally came to Manfu Mountain to look for the super tonic 'Niu Zhang Zhi' for prolonging life.

When Dafeng came to Fu Man Mountain, he unexpectedly learned that Fu An was the granddaughter of an herbal expert. Fu An sternly lectured him when she heard that Dafeng was looking for Niu Zhang Zhi. Due to an accident, Fu An's first kiss was stolen by Dafeng, and at that moment, her home caught fire and had an 'accident'...

Fu An sent off Chunxiang in deep sorrow and, following Chunxiang's last wish, left Fu Man Village to go down the mountain to find the first love of her grandmother, 'Grandpa Wangcai'. Just as Fu An went down the mountain, Yungao came to Fu An's house, looking at the ruins and listening to the old village chief recount the many years of hardship in Chunxiang's life. Yungao decided to fully find the 'Fu Lan' and 'Pi Dan' mentioned by the village chief.

On his eightieth birthday, Yungao announced: "I will give the inheritance rights of Babao Tang worth 8 billion... to whoever can marry the descendant of Xie Chunxiang named Xie Fu Lan!"

Yan Yungao's prediction was spot on, and everyone hurriedly searched. However, Dafeng wanted to find 'Fu Lan' first and kill her to end everything.

The real Fu An accidentally saved Dong Jie (played by Joe Chu) who was ambushed during her search for Grandpa Wangcai. In return, Dong Jie ordered his subordinates to secretly protect Fu An and her brother.

After arriving in the unfamiliar city, Fu An and Pi Dan had nowhere to go and could only spend the night in the park. It rained at night, and Pi Dan developed a high fever the next day. With no money, Fu An remembered Dafeng and took Pi Dan to find him. Moved by compassion, Dafeng helped take Pi Dan to see a doctor and arranged accommodation for Fu An and her brother. After Dafeng left, Dong Jie visited. Because he owed Fu An a favor, he left his business card and told Fu An to contact him if she needed help. Unexpectedly, a subtle relationship developed among these few people...
