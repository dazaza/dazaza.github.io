Smart Home Enters Growth Phase: Uneven Development Across Regions

by gdshstyle on 2012-02-29 11:04:10

Smart homes enter the growth phase, uneven development in different regions

In our country, the concept of smart home has been promoted for nearly ten years, but it is still in a state of a newborn market. Currently, its manufacturers are mainly concentrated in economically developed areas such as Shenzhen, Guangzhou, Shanghai, and Beijing. Cities in the central and western regions are still in the early stages of smart shoe cabinets. In 2011, the market demand for smart homes gradually expanded, and active market demand led to the emergence of a large number of smart home manufacturers. Therefore, places where manufacturers are relatively concentrated are also regions where the market is well-developed. After ten years, smart homes are currently in the prime time of the market. At this stage, smart homes need development. Whether in terms of consumption concepts or user acceptance, they cannot be separated from the recognition of more consumers. Just like many smart home manufacturers and even more smart home companies and agents increasingly mention, smart homes need to face the terminal market concept.

More than a decade ago, media reports claimed that 2000-2002 was the conceptual year for smart homes, 2003-2005 was the trial and popularization year, and after 2006 was the development and prosperity period. But until today, smart homes have not only failed to enter the prosperous period, but the market has remained stagnant with basically no major breakthroughs. We face the backwardness of overall home hardware, market chaos, lack of industry standards, and limitations in the audience to a certain extent. Regardless of product prices or technical operations, there is room for optimization. In recent years, with the improvement of domestic digital hardware and technology, smart homes have begun to take off, especially with the imminent arrival of mature triple network integration, bringing confidence to this industry. The entry of smart homes into thousands of households is just around the corner.

Domestic smart home products are mostly concentrated in high-end families such as villas and duplex residences. High-end intelligent products account for a large proportion of smart home sales, while the penetration rate of mid-to-low-end products in the market is relatively low. Moreover, development is imbalanced between regions, mainly concentrated in the eastern coastal developed cities, while the smart home market in central and western cities is relatively blank. In the South China region, companies like Shenzhen's Videon Legrand, Guangzhou's Anjubao, and Xiamen's Qiushi Intelligence are currently leaders in the smart home industry. Additionally, in the East China region, the demand for smart home markets in Shanghai is greater than other cities, mainly because Shanghai's economic development is better. Many international smart home brands are stationed in Shanghai, making it the main smart home market in East China, including well-known domestic and international brands such as Honeywell, Siyat, and Ingersoll Rand. Nanjing's Putian Tianji, Zhejiang's Zhongzheng, Tiangong Intelligence, Hongyan are all leading the development of regional smart home markets. The development of the smart home market in the North China region is relatively not as rapid as in the South China and East China regions. Most of the North China region consists of agents and distributors of smart home systems without large factories for production and manufacturing! According to surveys, the high housing prices in Beijing have somewhat compressed the consumption capacity of mid-range apartment customers, but the competition in the smart home market is becoming increasingly fierce. The smart home market in Beijing is currently showing a trend of rapid development, whether in real estate projects or individual users, the market demand is increasing more and more. According to incomplete statistics, there are already dozens of system integrators or agents specializing in smart home systems in Beijing. Relevant personnel estimate that the smart home market capacity in Beijing must exceed hundreds of millions of yuan.

With the continuous advancement of urban community construction and intelligent neighborhood construction in our country, how to create a comfortable, safe, and scientifically managed home living environment is a severe challenge facing real estate development units, property management departments, and relevant national management departments. For this reason, industry management departments, manufacturers, and engineering contractors need to cooperate with each other to promote the healthy development of the smart home market. People's living standards are constantly improving, and the demand for intelligence is becoming increasingly strong, providing favorable conditions for the development of smart homes. It is expected that by 2012, the global market size will reach $10.52 trillion. By then, our country's intelligent control industry will have good development opportunities. It is estimated that next year, the overall scale of intelligent control products in our country will reach 625 billion yuan, with a growth rate reaching 22.5%. It is predicted that by 2020, China's smart home output value will reach 1 trillion to 2 trillion yuan. Meanwhile, with the development and maturity of network technology, it provides an environmental foundation for the application of smart home devices, and the demand for smart homes is on the rise.

After more than ten years of development, the smart home industry has gradually become a very active industry in the field of building intelligence. Professional manufacturers, distributors, integrators, and decoration companies have all reached a certain scale, and users' understanding of smart home products is also improving. Comfortable, safe, convenient, and efficient home life will always be people's pursuit. Home intelligence will be an inevitable trend. In the next few years, our country's home market will surely rise rapidly!
