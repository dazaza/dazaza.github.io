Introduction to the plot of Chinese Family: Original name: "The New Yearning for Chinese Families". On New Year's Eve, snowflakes are flying. Mi Jia runs back home, crying and complaining, making her mother furious. She curses the Yan family for looking down on them and insists on dragging her daughter to confront them. She even wants to drag her son, Mi Qiang, along. But Mi Qiang, Mi Jia's younger brother, is not at home. Unbeknownst to them, he has been having a cyber-romance with Xiao Yun Shang and is hiding in her rented apartment, enjoying their "sweet" time together. However, this night of online romance sows the seeds for future calamities and affects his sister's fate, leading to a massive family tragedy!

Mi Jia is forcibly dragged by her angry mother to the Yan family, where she flips over the dining table. With both families in turmoil, divorce becomes the formal topic under such rage. Ultimately, Yan Chuan irrationally decides: Divorce!

Mao Mao, who has been Mi Qiang's girlfriend for many years, suddenly rushes to the Mi family in a panic, claiming she is pregnant and must get married as soon as possible or else she will die at their hands. The mother is enraged and slaps her son, ordering him to immediately arrange the marriage procedures. Under immense pressure, Mi Qiang reluctantly agrees to marry Mao Mao as soon as possible and dares not see Xiao Yun Shang anymore. Thus, on the 28th day of the first lunar month, Mi Qiang marries Mao Mao, while Mi Jia divorces Yan Chuan.
