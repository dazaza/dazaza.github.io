Preoperative preparation for breast reconstruction: A comprehensive examination should be conducted before breast reconstruction surgery. Overweight individuals, chest wall radiation damage, diabetes, and smoking are all considered risk factors for this surgery. When using the transverse rectus abdominis myocutaneous flap, it is important to avoid upper respiratory infections, constipation, and any factors that may increase intra-abdominal pressure before the surgery. Preoperative sit-up training can help improve blood circulation in the flaps. Patients with a smoking history should quit smoking at least three months before the surgery. A low-residue diet should be given one week before the surgery, and 1-2 units of blood should be prepared. On the day before the surgery, the patient should be given a liquid diet, fluids, and antibiotics. The morning of the surgery, the patient should receive a cleansing enema.

Methods for nipple reconstruction surgery:

1. If one nipple and areola are missing while the other healthy side's nipple and areola are intact and large, half of the healthy side can be cut and pulled together to reconstruct the healthy nipple and areola as well as recreate the missing side's nipple and areola.

2. If both nipples and areolas are missing, the labia minora or toe pad can be transplanted to reconstruct the nipple; alternatively, the earlobe or skin from the ear with cartilage can be transplanted to reconstruct the nipple.

Postoperative care for nipple reconstruction: After the surgery, daily life will not be affected, but it is important to minimize activities and prevent other objects from touching the wound to ensure proper healing. Attention should also be paid to maintaining hygiene and keeping the wound clean to avoid contamination of the surgical incision with unsterilized water. In terms of diet, patients should avoid spicy and stimulating foods. Postoperatively, the doctor will prescribe antibiotics for 3-5 days to prevent infection of the wound. Proper care helps the wound heal faster and improves the results of the surgery.
