How is a "Chinese Master of Engineering Supervision" made?

by yibadou13 on 2012-02-29 11:38:34

He, has been vigorously striving in the construction supervision industry of Hunan Province for nearly 20 years. He successively obtained the professional qualifications as a national registered supervising engineer, a national registered cost engineer, and a traffic department registered supervising engineer. He was also rated as a professor-level senior engineer.

In 2008, to honor his outstanding contributions to the supervising cause, the China Construction Supervision Association awarded him the glorious title of the first [China Engineering Supervision Master] at the summary and commendation conference marking the 20th anniversary of innovation and development in Chinese engineering supervision.

He is Pan Xiangming, the chairman and general manager of Hunan Changshun Engineering Construction Supervision Co., Ltd.

Bearing Heavy Responsibilities

Pan Xiangming is one of the founders of Changshun Supervision Company. At the time of its establishment, he was already the leader in the engineering cost industry at China National Light Industry Changsha Engineering Co., Ltd. (formerly China National Light Industry Changsha Design Institute), serving as the director of the Engineering Cost Department. In its early days, Changshun Supervision Company undertook the supervision work for two projects: the Guangzhou Weida High Paper Industry Co., Ltd. project (producing 100,000 tons of coated white cardboard annually, introducing production lines, Sino-foreign joint venture project, with a total investment of 14 billion yuan) and Suzhou Industrial Park Asia-Pacific Paper Products Co., Ltd. (producing 350,000 tons of cultural paper annually, Indonesian sole proprietorship, with a total investment of 5.5 billion US dollars). The key equipment for these two industrial projects were all imported from abroad, and personnel involved in the construction and management of the projects came from more than twenty countries and regions. Changshun Supervision Company was the only domestic supervising unit participating in the construction supervision of these projects. For a newly established supervising company, this was an extremely challenging task, very arduous. At a critical moment, Pan Xiangming was entrusted with the important mission and successively served as the representative of the chief supervising engineer for these two projects. He led the research on business with dedication, endured hardships ahead of others, and led the project supervising department to carry out supervising work carefully and orderly. The meticulous and perfectionist work of the project supervising department won high praise from the sole proprietorship enterprises. Subsequently, Pan Xiangming served as the chief supervising engineer for the expansion project of the terminal building of Huanghua Airport in Changsha, Hunan. With his outstanding business ability and organizational work, he led the project supervising department to successfully complete the supervising task. After the completion of the project, it received the [Luban Award] in 2001.

Striving for Governance Improvement

Due to his strong business capability and innovative pioneering spirit, Pan Xiangming was soon promoted to the position of vice president of Changshun Supervision Company, responsible for the company's production and operation work. He acutely realized that for the company to open up a situation in fierce market competition, stand firm, and grow stronger, it could not do without the company's own strong strength and scientific management. One of the characteristics of the supervising company's work is that the supervised engineering projects are scattered, and the company employees are stationed in various construction sites. To enable the company to operate and be supervised effectively, Pan Xiangming strongly advocated the establishment of a modern enterprise management system. In 1999, Changshun Supervision Company was among the first in the national supervising industry to establish the ISO9001 quality management system, making the division of labor clear in each department of the company, responsibilities explicit, and company management having rules to follow, achieving proceduralization and standardization. A complete scientific quality assurance system was formed in practice. Pan Xiangming required Changshun Supervision Company to strictly implement the established management system, avoiding formalism and ostentation. Due to the company's down-to-earth and meticulous adherence to the company philosophy of [scientific fairness, integrity in law-abiding, diligent dedication, and first-class service], one project after another gained good reputations from the owners. Changshun Supervision Company became well-known, and its supervising business steadily developed. It successively obtained Class A qualifications in engineering supervision, highway engineering supervision, and engineering bidding agency. Its supervising business covered most provinces across the country, including supervision of mechanical and electrical installation projects, municipal public works, housing construction projects, forestry and ecological projects, as well as power projects; first, second, and third-class highway projects, bridge projects, and second and third-class transportation construction supervision; engineering bidding agency and construction technology consulting. In October 2008, Changshun Supervision Company obtained the highest qualification in national engineering supervision —— comprehensive qualification for engineering supervision.

Specialized Expertise

Pan Xiangming usually pays great attention to theoretical learning and has a tenacious spirit in studying business. In his spare time, he persists in learning management knowledge, participating in the first batch of cost engineer training [http://www.otje.com.cn/html/jianshegongcheng_sheji/zhucezaojiashi/] and cost engineer exams [http://www.otje.com.cn/html/jianshegongcheng_sheji/zhucezaojiashi/], obtaining the national registered cost engineer certificate. Pan Xiangming also published articles such as "Reflections and Thoughts on Supervision Practice", "Strict Supervision, Fully Achieving Engineering Construction Goals", and "Enhancing Company Management, Creating Well-Known Brands in the Industry" in the Hunan Construction Supervision Journal, elaborating on the ideas of strict, standardized, and refined management. In 2007, after Comrade Pan Xiangming was promoted to the general manager of Changshun Supervision Company, he set an example and worked diligently. His busy figure could be seen every day, with almost no holidays. Under the guidance of his strict and refined management ideology, Changshun Supervision Company continuously strengthened its own construction, especially increasing employee training in human resource construction, formulating a series of incentive policies and corresponding assessment systems, and organizing backbone staff to go abroad for exchanges and study. In terms of enterprise organization construction, each management department of Changshun Supervision Company constantly added strength. To strengthen the company's guidance and supervision of the on-site project department, experienced excellent chief supervising engineers were transferred from the front line of supervision to the technical quality department. In terms of corporate culture construction, various cultural and sports activities were carried out, enhancing employees' sense of identity and cohesion, and stimulating their work enthusiasm. Under his leadership, Changshun Supervision Company has repeatedly received the titles of advanced supervision units in Hunan Province and advanced engineering construction supervision units nationwide. In 2008, it received the title of advanced unit in the 20th anniversary of national engineering supervision innovation and development, which is the highest commendation for supervising enterprises.
