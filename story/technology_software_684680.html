Plot of "Only Love You" Episode 1: Victory and Xiaoqing are a very loving couple. Victory was supposed to join the army with Xiaoqing, but just before boarding the train, Zhigao told Xiaoqing she couldn't join because the local dance troupe leaders disagreed and wouldn't let her join. Victory tearfully bid farewell to Xiaoqing on the train. After half a year, Victory became an artistic soldier in the army, and they could only communicate through letters.

Xiaoqing, as one of the main actors in the dance troupe, was asked by Hu Deputy Director to have dinner with Director Yang, who specifically invited her. Xiaoqing didn't want to go alone, so she wanted to bring Shuxiang along. During the dinner, some directors and Zhigao kept Xiaoqing there. Shuxiang wanted to warn Xiaoqing to be careful, but Sun Director said not to make things seem so serious.

During the dinner, Hu Deputy Director got Xiaoqing drunk and instructed Zhigao to open the room reserved by Director Yang for Xiaoqing to sleep in. Later, Hu Deputy Director sent Sun Director home. After everyone left the inn, Hu Deputy Director entered Xiaoqing's room and took advantage of her unconscious state. The next day, Xiaoqing woke up naked and realized what had happened. She sat on the bed panic-stricken and helpless. When Zhigao came, Xiaoqing said she was awake and fine.

After going back, Xiaoqing told Shuxiang that she was drunk and someone undressed her, and now her stomach feels strange. Shuxiang listened silently and suggested taking Xiaoqing to the hospital for a check-up, but Xiaoqing refused, feeling too embarrassed. The waitress Sanxiao found a pen while tidying up the room and discovered that a red pillowcase was missing.

Shuxiang went to the inn and asked Sanxiao if she noticed or heard anything unusual during her night shift, then asked her to show her around. Shuxiang didn't find anything suspicious after visiting the inn. Back home, Shuxiang still felt uneasy. Xiaoqing insisted she was fine and urged Shuxiang to leave since she was okay.

In the second episode, Victory performed well in the army. After his performance, Deputy Director Zheng informed him that his program clashed with another one, which was reported earlier, so his might be cut. Victory felt uncomfortable after hearing this and read Xiaoqing's letter expressing her good condition and homesickness.

The troupe gave two permanent positions, and Sun Director urged Hu Deputy Director to quickly arrange Xiaoqing's promotion, but Hu Deputy Director said it needed organizational approval and dismissed Sun Director, indicating he knew better. Before leaving, Sun Director's mood was terrible, and he told Hu Deputy Director's secretary Zhigao to keep track of Xiaoqing's situation.

While working, Xiaoqing experienced nausea, and everyone advised her to get checked. Xiaoqing declined, but Sun Director arranged for her dorm mates to help her rest. Yingzi liked Sun Director but felt upset seeing his care for Xiaoqing. That night, Yingzi went to give Xiaoqing medicine.

Yingzi questioned Xiaoqing about her menstrual cycle, and upon silence, guessed that Xiaoqing might have had relations with Sun Director and conceived his child. People in the troupe privately discussed Xiaoqing's pregnancy. Shuxiang brought Xiaoqing to the hospital for a check-up, confirming her pregnancy.

At a meeting, Sun Director noticed everyone avoiding eye contact with him and inquired about Xiaoqing's absence. No one spoke until someone mentioned Xiaoqing was at Shuxiang's place. Sun Director learned about Xiaoqing's pregnancy at Shuxiang's and accused Hu Deputy Director. Shuxiang suggested keeping it quiet to protect Xiaoqing and recommended aborting the child before it was too late.

Yingzi eavesdropped on Shuxiang and Sun Director's conversation, then confronted Sun Director admitting her mistake. Sun Director slapped her and left. On the way, Yingzi met Zhigao, Hu Deputy Director's secretary, and informed him of the situation.

Zhigao relayed the news to Hu Deputy Director, revealing his immoral nature. He pretended to be righteous, claiming he wasn't such a person, accusing Xiaoqing of being involved with Sun Dahu, and ordered Zhigao to investigate. If true, Sun Director must be severely punished. Zhigao left as Hu Deputy Director announced his regional assignment and requested Zhigao to take care of his sister Xiaofeng.

The next day, Zhigao, on behalf of Hu Deputy Director, investigated Sun Dahu and Xiaoqing with Shuxiang, who refused to talk. Sun Dahu took Xiaoqing to the hospital for an abortion. In the third episode, Shuxiang, after hearing Zhigao's words, criticized him for unjustly suspecting Sun Director and demanded a proper investigation. At the hospital, the doctor revealed Xiaoqing's heart issues, making an abortion impossible, and she fainted.

After Victory's performance, he planned to visit Xiaoqing, concerned as she hadn't written for over a month. An Guibao, knowing the truth, apologized to Sun Director, who expressed concern for Xiaoqing despite his own troubles. They worried about informing Victory. Xiaoqing prepared to leave the troupe per Shuxiang's advice to give birth away from prying eyes. Meeting Gui Bao late at night, she explained her urgency due to Victory's imminent return and urged confidentiality. Gui Bao informed Sun Director, who rushed to the station to see Xiaoqing off.

Sun Director inquired about Xiaoqing's destination and learned she planned to stay with a distant cousin in Wanzhou. Xiaoqing begged Sun Director not to inform Victory. Coincidentally, Victory returned just as Xiaoqing departed. Deceived initially by Sanyou, Victory eventually learned Xiaoqing intended to leave and rushed to bring her back. Meanwhile, Sun Director was isolated for investigation, though everyone in the troupe believed him innocent.

Victory searched anxiously for Xiaoqing, frustrated by others' silence. Eventually, Victory visited Gui Lan's home, asking her husband about Xiaoqing's whereabouts. The husband denied knowledge, explaining Xiaoqing stayed briefly. Victory left, promising a return visit. Gui Lan's husband informed her of Victory's visit, prompting regret. Urging Xiaoqing to find Victory quickly, Gui Lan encouraged her to pursue him at the station. However, Xiaoqing silently watched Victory depart from afar.

In the fifth episode, Xiaoqing attempted to drown her baby in a river but was stopped by Gui Lan. Realizing Xiaoqing's disappearance, Gui Lan located her with the child. Comforting Xiaoqing, Gui Lan offered to raise the child herself, urging Xiaoqing to reveal the truth to Victory soon. However, Xiaoqing refused, ashamed to confront him again.

Gui Lan's husband later demanded Xiaoqing's eviction due to his drunken tantrums. Packing quietly, Xiaoqing left with her child. Just then, Victory arrived at Gui Lan's home, learning of Xiaoqing's recent departure. Missing their encounter yet again, Victory boarded a train, unknowingly passing Xiaoqing placing her child on a bench at the station. A couple adopted the child, unseen by Xiaoqing watching nearby.

Years passed into the 1980s. Xiaoqing worked as a waitress at a cooperative store, occasionally visited by Gui Lan's husband bringing gifts. Xiaoqing regularly supported Gui Lan's family financially. Learning of Gui Lan's hospitalization, Xiaoqing arranged her admission and cared for her children temporarily.

In the sixth episode, five years apart, Victory continued searching for Xiaoqing, regretful and determined to reunite her safely. Despite his feelings for Xiaoqing, Victory remained unaware of Meijuan's affection. While assisting Meijuan with a sprained ankle, Victory learned of her difficult family circumstances.

Upon returning home, Victory's mother mentioned a visitor named Shuxiang, leaving a note before departing. Visiting Gui Lan's again, Victory questioned her husband about Xiaoqing's whereabouts. Disappointed by his ignorance, Victory left, promising a future visit. Learning of Victory's arrival, Gui Lan's husband informed Xiaoqing, who cried bitterly. Urged by Gui Lan to catch Victory at the station, Xiaoqing silently observed him aboard the train.

Coincidentally encountering Xiaoqing, An Gui Bao updated her on troupe affairs and Sun Director's well-being. Expressing mutual joy, Xiaoqing disclosed her stable life without returning home. Repairing her furniture and windows, An Gui Bao departed, asking if she'd seen Victory. Xiaoqing denied any encounter and inquired about Shuxiang, learning she moved to Shijiazhuang. Shuxiang sought evidence to expose Hu Deputy Director's misconduct.

In the seventh episode, Shuxiang revisited the inn, questioning the former attendant Sanxiao and confronting Zhigao about Xiaoqing's incident. Emotionally charged, Zhigao dismissed her claims, asserting his limited role as a secretary following orders. Rejected, Shuxiang vowed persistence.

An Gui Bao frequently helped Xiaoqing, crafting a large wooden chest for her clothes. Sharing a meal, Xiaoqing appreciated his kindness. Accepting a hairpin gift, Xiaoqing blushed as An Gui Bao stutteringly proposed marriage. Tearfully declining, Xiaoqing confessed her inability to bear more children. Shocked, An Gui Bao persisted, valuing Xiaoqing beyond her past.

As Victory prepared to demobilize after eight years, his team captain and comrades bid farewell, urging post-service dedication. Upon departure, Meijuan presented a knitted sweater, wishing Victory success awaiting her discharge in Beijing.

Reconsidering, An Gui Bao reiterated his proposal, willing to forego adopting a child for Xiaoqing's happiness. Declining, Xiaoqing explained her inability to reciprocate fully. Demobilized Victory returned to Beijing, assigned to a factory's cultural department. Union Chairman allocated an office shared with Shen Hong. Observing an accordion, Victory reminisced about his time with Xiaoqing in the troupe.

Working as a waitress at a station restaurant, Xiaoqing nearly encountered her lost child when the adopting couple dined there. Unaware, Xiaoqing attended to a chef's injury instead. At the factory, Victory practiced programs amidst Shen Hong's jealousy and musical rivalry. Forced to play the accordion publicly, Victory captivated Shen Hong's attention.

In the eighth episode, Shen Hong developed feelings for Victory, dressing elegantly and secretly placing chocolate on his desk. Encouraging participation in city-wide competitions, Shen Hong subtly flirted. An Gui Bao persuaded Ma Master and his wife to support Xiaoqing's marriage, acknowledging Victory's influence on her heart. Despite understanding, Ma Master deemed it inappropriate.

Confessing his long-standing affection, An Gui Bao admitted his inadequacy and respect for Victory. Seeking clarity, Xiaoqing tearfully affirmed her feelings for Victory. Offering unconditional support, An Gui Bao pledged devotion. Reluctantly accepting for her child's sake, Xiaoqing resolved to create a complete family.

Learning of Victory's visit to Shen Hong's home for her birthday celebration, tension arose among the trio. Interested in pursuing Shen Hong, He Bi awkwardly joined them. Declining Shen Hong's advances, Victory reaffirmed his commitment to Xiaoqing, causing Shen Hong's emotional outburst.

Sharing meals, Victory confided in He Bi about his enduring love for Xiaoqing, vowing to locate her. Preparing to marry Xiaoqing, An Gui Bao received Ma Master's blessing to treat her well.

In the ninth episode, Ma Master lamented Xiaoqing's departure, hoping An Gui Bao would cherish her. Returning home, Xiaoqing diligently organized and improved An Gui Bao's workshop finances. Addressing quality complaints, Xiaoqing's strategies enhanced business significantly.

Harboring resentment towards Victory, Shen Hong reluctantly collaborated on a factory performance under union pressure. Acknowledging his mistakes, Victory apologized, prompting Shen Hong's confession of admiration triggered during rehearsals. Sympathizing with Xiaoqing, Shen Hong tearfully embraced Victory.

Devotedly managing An Gui Bao's household, Xiaoqing frequently reminisced about Victory. Notified by police of a matching child profile, Xiaoqing identified her lost daughter at the station, only realizing it was a hallucination. Heartbroken, Xiaoqing confided in An Gui Bao, contemplating adoption.

Requesting leave to visit Jing County, Victory's relentless pursuit continued. Discussing Victory's dedication, He Bi and Shen Hong's dynamics evolved.

In the tenth episode, seeking Sun Dahu unsuccessfully, Victory located Sanyou through inquiries. Reviewing Xiaoqing's correspondence, Victory resolved to find her based on provided addresses. Meanwhile, Xiaoqing considered expanding instrument sales to Beijing, reassuring An Gui Bao of no intentions to reconnect with Victory.

Escorting Xiaoqing to the station with health reminders, An Gui Bao explored orphanages for adoption opportunities, finding none available. Encountering an acquaintance while drinking alone, An Gui Bao returned home to learn of Victory's visit seeking Xiaoqing.

Identifying Victory, An Gui Bao panicked but welcomed him warmly. Learning of Victory's leadership in the instrument factory, An Gui Bao clarified his marriage to Xiaoqing. Stunned, Victory emotionally declared his intention to reclaim Xiaoqing, disregarding consequences.

Persuading Victory to calm down, An Gui Bao shared a meal recounting events. Reflecting on photographs, Victory accepted the reality, leaving a heartfelt letter and gifts for Xiaoqing. An Gui Bao respected Victory's wishes to remain undisclosed.

Exploring Beijing markets, Xiaoqing successfully negotiated with a major retailer, promising substantial returns.

Awaiting Xiaoqing's return at Anping station for days, Victory ultimately returned to Beijing, reminiscing about their shared memories aboard the train, embracing the necessity of facing reality.
