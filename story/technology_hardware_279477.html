According to Yuri's recollections, when he went to the United States, he noticed that no one bothered to pick up small denomination coins off the street, which puzzled him. After returning home, coinciding with the financial crisis affecting Russia and a sharp devaluation of the ruble, fearing that small denomination coins might disappear, Yuri began collecting coins from all over.

Yuri himself is extremely fond of these coins. He piles them up in his house, covering the living room floor with a glittering carpet of coins. Moreover, Yuri uses the coins to create various decorative items such as wine bottles and vases to adorn his home. Not only that, Yuri made a coat out of coins, often wearing it over his suit jacket. The remaining coins are placed into thousands of cloth bags by Yuri, and at night, he sleeps directly on top of these bags without needing to diet to lose weight.
