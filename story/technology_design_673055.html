Milan's ultimate goal is指向 Fabregas: 35 million Euros plus a defensive midfielder to buy Arsenal captain

by wuyvzrno on 2012-02-23 13:17:07

According to the analysis of "Milan News", there were four key targets for Milan's midfield reinforcement, namely Ganso of Sao Paulo, Lamela of River Plate, Hamsik of Napoli, and Pastore of Palermo. All four are under 24 years old and possess excellent football talent and technical skills. It can be seen that the Rossoneri need a creative organizing midfielder who can fully replace Pirlo, who is about to leave, become the true core of the team, and lead Milan to reclaim the peak of the Champions League. And this person is Cesc Fabregas, the superstar who currently best meets the requirements of the Red and Black Club's management in the transfer market. After Milan won the Serie A title this season, club president Berlusconi promised to bring mysterious gifts to fans in the transfer market. Who this gift is has sparked many speculations from Italian media. The mouthpiece media "Milan News" then uncovered Milan management's ultimate target - Fabregas - by analyzing the previous rumors of the four key targets. Meanwhile, another Italian media outlet, "Football Market," has even made public the price Milan might offer: 35 million euros plus player incentives. The highest probability is sending Arsenal's former player Flamini back to Emirates Stadium to convince the veteran coach Wenger to sell his captain. The importance of Fabregas to Arsenal goes without saying. Arsenal continues its embarrassing season without any titles, but the Spanish international's performance remains commendable. He appeared 36 times, contributing 9 goals and 16 assists in 2551 minutes. Whether such a price can ultimately break through the psychological bottom line of Arsenal's management still needs time to consider. As "Milan News" said, perhaps bringing back Fabregas can only remain at the level of fantasy, but when two great transfer experts, Galliani and Braida, secretly work together, unimaginable surprises may eventually appear before the eyes of Milan supporters.
