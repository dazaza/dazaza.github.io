Website Planning Document for "Phantom at Midnight" (www.phantom.com)

The Broadway of the United States has many musicals that go beyond reactions, and "The Phantom of the Opera" is one of them. It is a Broadway musical composed by Andrew Lloyd Webber. "The Phantom of the Opera" can be said to be a work that reflects the charm of post-modern times. First, it successfully adapted Gaston Leroux's original novel, preserving the style of the original while making it more suitable for stage performance, enhancing the watchability of the work. Secondly, the ingenious play-within-a-play lets the audience wander between reality and fantasy. It premiered in 1986 and won seven Tony Awards in 1988, becoming one of the most successful musicals in history. Moreover, there are over 16 versions of the musical and related films. Meanwhile, China's first horror movie, "Midnight Song," also has a close connection with this, which can be considered the Chinese version of "The Phantom of the Opera." This website combines these two epic movies, allowing readers to understand them from different angles.

I. Website Positioning

1. Target Audience: "The Phantom of the Opera" is more suitable for young people who are enthusiastic about Broadway musicals, while "Midnight Song" is more suitable for middle-aged and elderly people.

2. Theme: Introducing and promoting two excellent movies, allowing people to understand them more deeply and like them.

3. Confirm Website Name: Phantom at Midnight (www.phantom.com)

4. Overall Framework Trend: Through music, pictures, videos, and related historical materials, along with some personal opinions and feelings, let more people understand them.

5. Website Feasibility Analysis: Through various materials and investigation analysis, with the development of society, people have more ways to enjoy life, and quite a part of people use music for entertainment. Moreover, Broadway musicals have a place both nationally and worldwide, so many young people are passionate about Broadway's classic songs and their tragic love stories. "The Phantom of the Opera" provides those young people with a platform for appreciation and exchange. Like "The Phantom of the Opera," "Midnight Song" is also well-known in China. From the birth of the first black-and-white film in 1937 to the completion of the 30-episode TV series in 2005, each one gives numerous movie fans different feelings. People over 40 almost vividly remember the black-and-white films, filled with countless memories. Therefore, this website integrates "The Phantom of the Opera" and "Midnight Song," blending Eastern and Western cultures while allowing all age groups to find what they like on this website, attracting more people to visit. Thus, the "Phantom at Midnight" website has its feasibility and necessity.

II. Network Art Style Positioning

This website will appear as an art website, aiming to introduce and promote movies, displaying beautiful elements within the movies. Through personal elements, the entire website becomes more individualistic and avant-garde, warm and romantic, letting music fill every corner of the website, cultivating people's hearts.

III. Color and Framework

When opening the website, the color leaves an overall impression on the reader, deciding whether to continue browsing. "Phantom at Midnight" incorporates elements from "The Phantom of the Opera" and "Midnight Song," naturally occupying the whole page style with its melancholic songs and eternal classic stories. Therefore, the main color should have moderate brightness and saturation, primarily using cool colors. Colors of the same hue system should be used together, avoiding contrasting or complementary colors, flexibly applying transition colors to create a sorrowful visual effect.

Based on the above reasons, the following colors will be used for the website design:

Black: Displaying the overall melancholic feeling, highlighting the website's theme.

Red: Highlighting the most important part of the melancholic story—love—and running through the entire website.

Purple: Mysterious yet beautiful, showcasing the characteristics of the phantom.

Auxiliary colors include:

Yellow: The brightest color, bringing happiness and hope.

Orange: Representing brightness, lively and full of passion.

Blue: Symbolizing depth and eternity, showing the inheritance of this classic story, as vast as the sky and as profound as the sea.

White: Sacred, pure, innocent, faithful, giving people a sense of relaxation and cleanliness.

IV. Website Content Organization and Structure Design

The homepage of the website adopts a simple image display form without too much information text. The preliminary idea is to use a full-page beautiful picture as the background, accompanied by exquisite music, emphasizing the website name, highlighting the website's characteristics. The homepage layout design serves aesthetic functions.

The main content of the website includes the world of "Phantom at Midnight": deep night silence, half-moon brilliance, opera phantom, long-lasting vocal music.

Exchange Center: Forum, author's original works, latest dynamics of "Phantom at Midnight."

Customization: Pictures, music.

1. The first-level pages mainly consist of the world of "Phantom at Midnight," Exchange Center, and Customization. Clicking "Phantom at Midnight" enters the main content section, including deep night silence, half-moon brilliance, opera phantom, and long-lasting vocal music. Connecting the first characters of each sentence forms "Midnight Song." Clicking each character corresponds to related backgrounds, music, pictures, and videos of "Midnight Song." Clicking "Opera Phantom" enters the relevant background, music, pictures, and videos of "Opera Phantom."

2. Clicking Customization allows users to enter customizable pages where they can set the background music and pictures of the website using the optional music and pictures provided by the website.

3. Clicking the Exchange Center allows users to enter the forum to post some opinions. If interested, they can enjoy the author's original works, including self-introductions and some personal creations. Additionally, they can enter the latest dynamics and resource downloads, containing some latest dynamics about the movies and corresponding resource downloads to meet customer requirements.
