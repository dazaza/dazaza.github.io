"Come quickly, someone jumped into the river." At 9:30 yesterday morning, in the Pigeon Bridge area of Bai Xia District in Nanjing City, a woman wearing a black top suddenly jumped off the bridge and fell into the river. After seeing this situation, the eyewitness immediately called the police for help.

Eyewitness Ms. Zhang said that the woman was about 1.6 meters tall, wearing a black down jacket. "I just happened to pass by Pigeon Bridge," Ms. Zhang said, and then saw this black-clad woman standing on the bridge, suddenly stepping across and standing on the railing of the bridge, then jumping, falling into the water. Ms. Zhang was very anxious and together with other eyewitnesses shouted for help.

Subsequently, several young people came to the riverside and tried to pull the fallen woman out of the river, but the woman was too far from the bank and could not be reached. Time passed slowly, the woman could still splash around in the river initially, but gradually began to sink due to exhaustion. At a critical moment, Master Qi, a property security guard at Rong Zhuang Xincun beside Pigeon Bridge, threw a bundle of ropes into the river. Subsequently, the woman caught the rope in the river and tied it around her waist. On the shore, Master Qi and two kind-hearted people dragged the woman onto the shore.
