Junior high school boy insists on carrying his disabled female classmate to class, saying that he will continue to do so until graduation.

by erbai558 on 2012-03-01 15:06:46

Zeng Cuiru, who often helps her classmates in study, is an excellent student. The story of Zeng Cuiru's indomitable spirit and her classmates' careful care moved people, and at the same time made people feel another kind of sigh. As a disabled teenager, although Zeng Cuiru does not want others to treat her as a special person, her congenital disease and physical disability have deprived this girl in the bloom of youth of much happiness and joy that should belong to her peers. Fortunately, she has her family, enthusiastic classmates and teachers who help her, especially classmate Luo Xiong, who promised to carry her to class until graduation.

Zeng Cuiru's family is not wealthy. Her father works as a security guard in a company, and her mother stays at home to make plastic flowers because she needs to pick her up every day. She also has a brother who just started working in a company. In order to treat her illness, the family has spent all their savings. But despite this, her parents still look for doctors everywhere and take her to Guangzhou for treatment every year, which costs tens of thousands of yuan each time. Now, the biggest wish of her parents this year is to buy her a wheelchair, but they have not been able to get the approval from the relevant department. Her mother said that it is too dangerous to send her to school by electric scooter, and sometimes they are penalized by the traffic police. She hopes that this wish can come true this year.

"I did what I could for my classmate," said Luo Xiong, who is 1.65 meters tall and weighs 80 kilograms, and is indeed the most physically imposing classmate. This shy boy claims that he feels no shame in carrying a female classmate. Since then, they have become the best friends in the class. When asked if he was tired after sticking with it for so long, Luo Xiong, the study committee member of the class, patted his strong body and said: "It doesn't matter, I am used to it. My parents also support me very much."

Luo Xiong: I will carry her to class until graduation

After entering junior high school for a period of time, the classmates found that she almost never drinks water. Later, they learned that because she was worried about troubling her classmates when going to the toilet, she restrained herself like this. The classmates all advised her that actually helping her with these small things is nothing, don't let thirst hurt her body.

For fear of giving trouble to her classmates, Zeng Cuiru often takes care of her classmates. With excellent academic performance, she always helps her classmates in study. Everyone would think of her when they encounter problems they cannot understand.

But during such a long time, letting one child's difficulties be borne solely by another child's shoulders makes people worry. Yes, in a society full of love and warmth, children like Zeng Cuiru who suffer misfortunes should feel the care from the society. One person's misfortune should not be borne only by one person or only by the people around her. Social security and institutional care are more important. With these, the weak will be strong, the strong will be benevolent, and the warmth of love will spread to every corner. (Shenzhen Special Zone Daily reporter Wang Jianfeng)

Calling for More Love

Characters: Luo Xiong, Zeng Cuiru

Zeng Cuiru's classroom is on the fourth floor, and the computer class classroom is on the fourth floor of another building. She still remembers that when she first went to class in the first year of junior high school, she wondered how to "walk" to that classroom. At this time, a male classmate sitting next to her walked over, picked her up without any hesitation, and carried her on his back. Since then, whether it is music class, computer class, art class or lab class, this male classmate named Luo Xiong has been carrying her until now.

Location: Pingshan Middle School

Reporter's Notes

After entering junior high school, 15-year-old Zeng Cuiru from Heping Community in Pingshan packed her schoolbag every morning, sat on her mother's electric scooter, and came to Pingshan Middle School. This road has been traveled countless times. Zeng's mother said that since entering junior high school, riding a bike to send her child to school every day, four trips back and forth, regardless of wind and rain, after arriving at school, the classmates would voluntarily come to meet Cuiru, some carrying her schoolbag, some moving her seat, making her feel like she is at another home. The help from her classmates and her own strength make her feel so lucky.

A teacher said: "They always learn from each other, promote each other, and their grades are always among the top in the class, and they are both model students."

"She is an outgoing and positive girl, she is the treasure of our class," said head teacher Ms. Chen. Zeng Cuiru is a model student in the school. Her strength and optimism have inspired all the classmates, and their class is the best-performing class in the entire grade.

What makes Zeng's mother and Zeng Cuiru feel even luckier is that since entering junior high school, there has been a male classmate who has taken on the task of carrying her to class.

Love-filled student Luo Xiong said: "I will carry her to class until graduation, and if we enter the same high school, I will continue to carry her."

Disabled female student Zeng Cuiru said: "My greatest wish is to be a translator and a person who contributes to society."

Zeng Cuiru's strength and optimism have influenced all the classmates. Luo Xiong carries disabled female student Zeng Cuiru to class.

Congenital vertebral chest, born with grade one physical disability, for a teenage girl like Zeng Cuiru, fate is so cruel.

"I am not special," Zeng Cuiru always hopes that people treat her like a healthy ordinary middle school student. Because of this, she tries not to bother others.

In the second half of the year, she will enter the third year of junior high school, and she will graduate from junior high school in more than a year. Luo Xiong said, "I will carry her to class until graduation, and if we enter the same high school, I will continue to carry her."
