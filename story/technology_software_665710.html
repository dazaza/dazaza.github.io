There are many sayings about having sons or daughters, among which the diet for having a boy or girl is considered by many people as a reliable secret for having a son. Below, the parenting network editor has collected four diets that experts say can help pregnant women have a son. Let's take a look! Geckos, especially the tail of geckos, contain higher levels of male hormones, which have a certain stimulating and adjusting effect on the female sex gland axis. At the same time, it may also increase female ovulation. If a woman becomes pregnant while taking geckos, the fetus is prone to twins or multiple births. Whether a woman gives birth to a boy or a girl may be related to her own physical constitution being yin or yang, but it does not entirely depend on this. There are many other factors such as body acidity and alkalinity, sperm vitality, age, stress, living environment, etc. Therefore, the practice of eating geckos to assist in having a boy lacks sufficient scientific basis and may lead to some negative results. Salty for boys, sweet for girls. In South Africa, there is a saying "salty for boys, sweet for girls" which has recently been confirmed by scientists. South African scientists believe that eating red meat and salty fast food leads to having boys, while eating chocolate helps to have girls. A recent experiment conducted by scientists at the University of Pretoria in South Africa found that when consuming high-sugar foods, the offspring had more females than males; whereas when blood sugar levels were maintained at normal levels, the offspring had an approximately equal ratio of males and females. Research leader Professor Elissa Cameron stated that the results of this experiment also apply to humans. Alkaline foods: According to scientists' research on food, they found that some foods are acidic while others are alkaline. If a couple really wants to have a boy during their family planning, then in order to increase the chances of having a boy, women should eat more alkaline foods. This type of food can balance the body's acidity and alkalinity, making the body present in an alkaline state, increasing the opportunity for Y sperm to successfully fertilize with the egg. What kind of food makes it easier to have a son? High-calorie foods: British scientists have found that there may be a certain connection between the gender of a baby and its mother's eating habits before pregnancy. However, some experts say that this finding still needs further research. This study was jointly completed by scientists from Oxford University and Exeter University. They surveyed more than 700 women in the UK who were pregnant for the first time, asking them to provide their dietary records before conception. It was found that among pregnant women who consumed calorie-rich foods daily before becoming pregnant, 56% had boys, while only 45% of those who consumed low-calorie foods had boys. Among pregnant women who ate cereal for breakfast every day before becoming pregnant, 59% had boys.
