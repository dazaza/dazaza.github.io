Google launches new maps for mobile phones

by yinyin on 2008-11-07 15:15:38

I don't know if you have ever sat in a taxi, watching the pedestrians around you, but you have been blocked for half an hour on the path of hundreds of meters. The car has been turned off, but can relax for a nap, only to wake up to find that the building next to the same building. The number of vehicles in Beijing has exceeded 3 million, and many roads are saturated. Choosing a clear route before a car trip is the key to ensuring your plan for the day. Not long ago, Google Maps (the web version) added traffic flow. Today, Google engineers are bringing traffic flow to your phone, so you can keep track of current traffic conditions anytime, anywhere! As one of the most popular mobile apps in the world, Google mobile Maps is constantly improving its features. In addition to the previous version has been launched in My location, bus/drive, lifestyle . The new version of Google Maps for mobile phones adds the ability to live traffic . The real-time traffic function currently supports two cities, Beijing and Shanghai, and will be expanded to more cities soon. Green indicates smooth traffic, yellow indicates slow traffic, and red indicates congestion.                 with My Location function, even if not high-end GPS phone, you can quickly locate your current location, instantly understand the current location of the road information. Choose the green route, you can save more time. It is worth mentioning that this version of Google mobile Maps supports a variety of operating systems and platforms, and can be applied to most mainstream brands such as Nokia, Samsung, SONY Ericsson, MOTOROLA, Dopod, blackberry and other mid - to high-end mobile phones. Google Maps for mobile phones are all free to use, but you may have to pay your network operator for data. You can Google mobile products page to find suitable for you on the phone.
