Churchman University: American Century Business School Enterprise Internship

In order to meet the requirements of practical working ability for graduates from employing enterprises, more than 20 century-old prestigious universities in the United States including Churchman University and Stanford University have reformed the current education system. The Churchman Business School provides and encourages students to participate in cooperative internship programs with globally renowned enterprises during their studies. While earning an income, students will also earn credits that can be counted towards their total credits.

This educational reform measure enables students to complete multiple enterprise training programs before graduation, increasing their experience in large enterprises, allowing them to adapt faster to future actual job positions. It also improves the employment rate of university graduates.

Founded in 1911, Churchman University is one of the top business schools in the United States, offering undergraduate, master's, and doctoral degrees. The university has trained many high-end financial talents in the United States who mainly work in high-end financial industries such as accounting, auditing, securities investment, insurance, and futures.

Students from Churchman University can integrate into work faster than general university graduates because since its establishment, Churchman University has always advocated cultivating students' practical abilities. Close alumni connections and job recommendations are important factors in the success of the school's students. Therefore, the university highly values cooperation with many famous large enterprises in the United States, providing valuable internship opportunities for students.

Churchman University offers various tuition assistance programs for students with outstanding potential in business, such as scholarships and enterprise internships.

The distance learning business degree program at Churchman University allows mid-to-senior level managers around the world to study courses online, take exams online, and submit papers online without affecting their original work. The degree obtained is exactly the same as studying on campus, making it the preferred educational model for mid-to-senior level managers who are employed globally, including in Europe, America, and China.

Churchman University's bilingual courses in English-Western and English-Chinese are very popular among multinational corporations. The university offers MBA, undergraduate, master's, and doctoral degrees. The low-income scholarships offered by the school require only a few thousand dollars to complete the degree once successfully applied.

Churchman University's campuses are located in Pennsylvania, California, Texas, and other places in the United States. Among them, the earliest established branch, Churchman Business School, was founded in 1911 and has nearly a century of history. It is one of the oldest business schools in the United States, specifically cultivating high-end talent for the U.S. financial industry. In the "National Business School MBA Overall Ranking," this century-old prestigious university ranks 17th.

In addition to Churchman University, the teaching quality of several other century-old prestigious universities in the United States, such as Boston University and Brown University, is also consistently recognized by white-collar professionals in China.
