The broken-winged love is destined by heaven.

by ssre3021 on 2012-02-25 02:21:09

How far I can fly, that's how big the world is. The camping machete, the wings of angels are so sacred. Once upon a time, angels were full of ecstasy, filled with strength, easily took off, and flew endlessly. But one day when she woke up, she was terrified to find that her wings were broken. Oh God, is an angel without wings still an angel? She couldn't accept such a cruel reality. She wanted to cry but had forgotten how to cry. Her heart was bleeding, she could no longer breathe from the pain, she had lost all feeling, she felt disappointed, she thought of death, using death to end the unbearable pain...

When she was in despair, she heard a voice that was both沧桑(cang1桑 sang2) (weathered) and gentle. She knew it was Grandma Angel, the angel she respected and loved the most. Grandma Angel was the most prestigious angel among the angels. She had a pair of extraordinary wings, wings that would never break.

She cried bitterly in Grandma Angel's arms. She was in great pain, not knowing what else she could do. At this moment, Grandma stroked her head and smiled as she told her a story: A long, long time ago, there was an angel who was destined to be an angel without wings from the moment she was born. She was in pain and helplessness, proud and lonely, because all the other angels ridiculed her, saying she was a freak, saying that she didn't have angel wings and wasn't even an angel. However, the only comfort she felt was that there was one angel who never looked down on her. On the contrary, he ignored the ridicule and mockery of the other angels and stayed by her side, doing everything he could to protect her so she wouldn't get hurt. Slowly, she realized she had fallen in love with him. Later, they fell in love and were very happy... But one day, she was cruelly told: An angel without wings cannot have love. She would bring disaster to the one she loves. That is, once an angel without wings starts a love affair, the angel she loves will die from losing his heart. How can one live without a heart? How can one love without a heart?

She fell into pain again, but now she had no way out. She loved him, she couldn't harm him. Loving someone doesn't necessarily mean owning them; as long as he is happy, that's enough. So she ruthlessly left him, leaving no trace behind. She just told him: "We are angels from two different worlds. We are destined not to have love. If you love me, please let me leave quietly and don't come looking for me." He was helpless. He knew she must be in great pain and helplessness. He wanted to look for her, wanted to tell her all the truth, but... He struggled for a long time, he was also in great pain. He loved her but couldn't be in love with her. Finally, one day, he made up his mind to find her and tell her everything... After enduring countless hardships, he finally found her. She was foolishly staring at the knot of hearts he gave her. He saw that she had become much more haggard. She also saw him. She was surprised yet at a loss. At that moment, all things became desolate, and the movement of heaven and earth stopped instantly. They embraced each other regardless of everything, and that instant became eternal... Perhaps everything was predestined, predestined that they couldn't be in love. Their love was so unacceptable to heaven and earth. Happiness for them was actually too short-lived because this was their last meeting and embrace. Maybe their love was predestined to be unacceptable to heaven and earth. In fact, everything was coincidental. They met at the wrong time. It was precisely the millennium encounter of the universe explosion. In an instant, everything would be annihilated. All the angels spread their wings and fled. In that instant, he hesitated not a bit and tore off his own wings and gave them to her, letting her escape quickly. But without him, what would surviving mean? She preferred to stay with him so they could be together forever. He agreed. He tightly hugged her... She was thinking: we can finally be together forever, we don't have to endure the tormenting pain anymore, why fear death. At this moment, he pushed her hard. She was pushed several meters away and found herself able to fly. But where was he? When she turned back, he had already started to dissipate. He used all his strength to say to her: "If love has destiny..." Everything was too late to explain... Only she remained..."No, don't leave me alone..." This was a heart-wrenching scream, but everything was too late...

"No, nothing can stop us from being together," she carried her tears and flew forcefully, carrying his wings, carrying his love, flying forever...

This is a very tragic love story. Many people think this is the end of the story, very beautiful and tragic. However, the story of these two angels did not end here. Although she escaped the disaster, she could not escape her sorrowful heart. Without him, every day was filled with disappointment. She attempted suicide many times, but every time she was mysteriously awakened. However, every time she didn't know who saved her. She hated the person who woke her up. Why was it so difficult for her to die? She just wanted to be with him forever. Could it be fate that they couldn't even be together in death? But... she didn't understand, she understood nothing... Until one day, she accidentally learned a shocking truth: he never had a heart, from the day he was born, he was destined to be an angel without a heart. But, but, she had too many questions. He didn't have a heart but was still alive. She thought, if he didn't have a heart, why could they still fall in love? Why could they fall in love? She couldn't figure it out... One without a heart, one without wings, was destined not to have love, but they fell in love, and their love was so deep. Perhaps this was what he said: "Love has destiny"... She understood everything. It was the destiny of love. Even though he was dead, they had already become one. As long as there was love in the heart, that was enough. Carrying the destiny of love, carrying his wings, she gained true happiness...

This is a very perfect ending. The little angel liked this story very much. She smiled through her tears and gazed at Grandma Angel. She understood everything. She believed in the story of the destiny of love that Grandma Angel told. From then on, the angelic realm had a lively and lovely little angel. She was very happy and content. She no longer felt sad or cried. Perhaps one day we will discover that the once-broken wings of the angel will heal to become even more beautiful...
