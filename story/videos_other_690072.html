★ Day 2

Meal: Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner

Accommodation: Tian Du or Ming Zhu or Jin Niu. Arrive at Shangrao in the morning. After breakfast, take a bus to [Mount Sanqing] (about 2 hours by bus). After arrival, you can choose to take the southern cable car (8 minutes) up the mountain (optional, one-way cable car costs 70 yuan per person, if walking it takes about 3 hours). Then visit [Nan Qing Garden Scenic Area]: Divine Dragon Playing with Pine Trees, One-Line Sky, Spring Goddess, Giant Python Emerging from the Mountain, Ten Thousand Jade Sticks Pointing to the Sky, Immortal Courtyard Beautiful Peaks, Three Dragons Emerging from the Sea, etc. [East Coast Scenic Area], [West Coast Scenic Area]: Neglecting Pine for Reflection, Guanyin Delivering a Child, Jade Maiden Offering Flowers, Flower Fruit Mountain, Monkey King Offering Treasures. Here you can personally experience the process of applying for the Guinness World Records, the entire West Sea Plank Walkway is several kilometers long. Afterward, guests can choose to take the cable car down the mountain (optional, one-way cable car costs 55 yuan per person, if walking it takes 2.5 hours). Have dinner and check into a hotel at the foot of Mount Sanqing or Wuyuan (about 1.5 hours by bus).

Highlights: Mount Sanqing has thousands of peaks competing for beauty, countless valleys rushing with streams, ancient trees thriving, rare birds nesting, and has been a famous Taoist mountain for over 1600 years. It is an example of the integration of billions of years of natural evolution and human culture.

★ Day 3

Meal: Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner

Accommodation: Cha Yuan Hotel or Home Inn. After breakfast, visit "Heaven on Earth" [Jiangling]. Jiangling is known as one of the "Four Great Rapeseed Flower Seas of China." Every early spring, rapeseed flowers bloom from south to north, creating breathtaking visual landscapes that harmonize nature and humanity: mountains embrace, blue skies and clear waters, amidst lush greenery, full fields of golden rapeseed flowers mixed with the scent of farmland, like waves of flowers rolling in. Ancient villages scattered throughout vast fields of rapeseed flowers shine brilliantly together (the blooming period of Wuyuan rapeseed flowers is affected by local climate conditions, please understand if weather reasons cause less than ideal viewing conditions). Visit [Moon Bud Bay also known as Little Moon Bay]: A small island between two shores, shaped like a crescent moon, hence named Moon Bud Bay. Moon Bud Bay is also called Moon Bay, a green oasis in the center of the river, shaped like a crescent moon, forming a unique scenic spot in Wuyuan when paired with the Huizhou-style architecture across the river. Moon Bay is known as the first bay of Wuyuan. Then visit [Hong Village, the Hometown of Longevity]. The village is located in a narrow and winding valley, with a small stream running alongside. There are three stone bridges built over the stream. Villagers have good health practices, with many living to a ripe old age, thus earning the name "Longevity Ancient Village". Afterward, head to the Huangshan city area. Have dinner and check into the hotel.

Highlights: The most beautiful season in Wuyuan is when the rapeseed flowers bloom, covering the slopes in golden yellow, contrasting beautifully with the white walls and gray tiles of Huizhou-style architecture, providing a sense of belonging for those escaping the hustle and bustle of city life.

★ Day 4

Meal: Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner

Accommodation: Cha Yuan Hotel or Home Inn

Option 1: After breakfast, take a bus to [Huangshan Scenic Area] (1.5 hours). Then take the optional cable car up the mountain (about 10 minutes, if walking it takes about 2 hours). Visit Yubinglou Scenic Area, admire the world-famous Huangshan Welcoming Guest Pine [Huangshan Pine], which is an independent tree species in botany, breaking through soil to grow almost "every peak with stone bones and every peak with pines", everywhere visible are the ever-changing postures of Huangshan Pines on dangerous cliffs, steep rocks, and precipitous peaks. View the Sleeping Buddha of Yubinglou, look afar at Tiandu Peak, climb the Hundred Steps Cloud Ladder, pass through (those with good stamina can walk through the One-Line Sky, those with less stamina can choose the Ao Fish Cave path) to reach Ao Fish Peak. After lunch, return via the original route. Upon reaching Yubinglou, take the optional cable car down the mountain (about 10 minutes, if walking it takes about 2 hours). Take a bus (about 0.5 hours) to the Xin Qifeng Tea Museum (stay time 45 minutes), taste the series of famous Chinese teas including Huangshan Maofeng. Then take a bus to Tunxi [about 1 hour]. Along the way, enjoy the scenery of ancient Huizhou countryside; thoughts wander with the green mountains and clear waters, scene after scene appears before your eyes. Have dinner and check into the hotel to rest.

Option 2: After breakfast, head to the World Natural and Cultural Heritage, majestic and picturesque Huangshan Scenic Area (about 1 hour by car). Optionally take the Yun Gu new cable car up the mountain (operation time 10 minutes, if walking it takes about 1.5 hours). Climb Guangming Peak, visit the Huangshan strange stones - Flying Stone, look afar at the beautiful scenery of the Western Sea peaks, fully appreciate the strange stone wonders of Huangshan, Paiyun Pavilion Scenic Area. After lunch, head to Beihai Scenic Area, which is the heartland of the Huangshan Scenic Area, concentrating the essence and marvels. Lion Peak, Cool Terrace, listen to ancient love stories, look afar at the affectionate stone monkey, Dream Brush Producing Flowers, Eighteen Arhats Paying Homage to the South Sea, etc. Then visit Start Belief Peak, it is said that an ancient man doubted while climbing the mountain, but upon arriving here started to believe in the wonders of Huangshan, hence the name. Main attractions include Black Tiger Pine, Love Pine, Child Worshiping Guanyin, etc., beautiful scenery that stirs the imagination. Later, take the optional cable car down the mountain (about 10 minutes, if walking it takes about 1.5 hours). Head to Huangshan city area, have dinner, and check into the hotel.

Highlights: Spring is the best season to visit Huangshan. The charming spring light of Huangshan captivates, with golden rapeseed flowers in the fields and scattered rhododendrons on the hillsides along the road leading to Huangshan. Even before reaching Huangshan, you will be enchanted by this natural beauty.

★ Day 5

Meal: Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner

Accommodation: Movie Star or Galaxy, Tuni. After breakfast, take a bus to "China's Hollywood" [Hengdian Film City] (about 3.5 hours by bus). Go to the main filming location of "The Banquet" - [Ming-Qing Palace] or [Qingming Riverside Scene] to participate in the much-anticipated "I Come to Hengdian as an Actor" film experience activity. Each bus forms a crew of 40-45 people to shoot a movie. Here there are professional sets, scripts, costumes, directors, and cameramen, as well as customized scripts tailored to each tourist. After the director assigns roles to each visitor, they are dressed up by makeup artists and costume designers, then begin formal shooting. Emperors, eunuchs, sedan bearers, matchmakers, wealthy young men, vividly portrayed... Laughter fills the shooting site, creating a lively atmosphere. (After careful editing, everyone will receive a DVD movie disc of their participation as a precious souvenir about ten days later.) Visit Zhejiang's largest bamboo fiber research base [Qingyi Bamboo Healthy Living Museum], view bamboo charcoal-made non-toxic, odorless, side-effect-free pure natural daily necessities, and experience bamboo healthy living! After dinner, optionally visit the large interactive dream night tour scenic area [Dream Valley] to experience the country's first ecological disaster experience - torrential rain and mountain floods, feel the shock of 400 tons of floodwater rushing in instantly, and dance wildly with Wa ethnic youth around a large bonfire during the Torch Festival in Dream Water Village.

Highlights: Shooting movies at Hengdian Film City, parents becoming stars themselves, shooting their own blockbuster movie, filled with joyous atmosphere and funny plots, sure to make parents ecstatic. Also, professionally made movie discs serve as precious souvenirs.

★ Day 6

Meal: Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner

Accommodation: Seven Stars or Zhen Tian. After breakfast, visit the main filming location of the movies "Hero" and "The Mummy 3" - [Emperor Qin's Palace Scenic Area], feeling the grandeur of Emperor Qin unifying the world, experiencing the special effects of sword duels in the leaking roof chess hall in "Hero", and appreciating the high-tech multimedia drama "Dreaming Back to Qin-Han" with 3D movie special effects. Head to Zhuji, the ancient capital of Yue State and hometown of Xi Shi (about 1 hour by bus). After lunch, visit [Xi Shi's Hometown Scenic Area], learn about Xi Shi's lesser-known life story, visit the exhibition hall of famous women throughout Chinese history and Fan Li Shrine, participate in interesting sports events organized by Zhuji Tourism Bureau on the square of Xi Shi's Hometown (kick shuttlecock, bounce table tennis ball, double-person 100-meter race... interesting projects one after another, surprise prizes distributed on-site). Then take a bus to the ancient water town and hometown of celebrities [Shaoxing] (about 1 hour by bus), visit [Dong Lake], praised as the "Best Natural Landscape Big Basin in the World", it is one of the three famous lakes in Zhejiang along with Hangzhou's West Lake and Jiaxing's Nan Lake. Visitors can take a traditional Wenzhou-style black-roofed boat described by Lu Xun, cruise on the lake, and admire the strange rocks and caves of Dong Lake (self-paid: 90 yuan per boat for 3 people). After dinner, watch a large-scale evening performance specifically arranged by the local tourism bureau at Shen Garden to welcome the tourists of this special train, experience the opening garden ceremony of a wealthy family in the Song Dynasty, stroll through the garden accompanied by the steward, enjoy touching Yue Opera and Lotus Fall songs, interact with the stewards of Song Dynasty's Shen Garden, sing songs from different eras... Joyful travel and dreamlike nights move every tourist.

Highlights: A wonderful evening performance at Shen Garden in the ancient city of Shaoxing, touching Yue Opera, and the tragic love story of Lu You and Tang Wan, evoke countless beautiful memories, intoxicated in such a beautiful moonlit night.

Then take a bus to Hangzhou (about 1 hour by bus), stroll leisurely through the [West Lake] under all kinds of weather conditions, view Su Causeway, Broken Bridge, Solitary Hill, Three Pools Mirroring the Moon, Quyuan Wind Lotus, etc., numerous beautiful scenes, look afar at the legendary [Leifeng Pagoda], visit the green oasis located between Su Causeway, Yingbo Bridge, and Suolan Bridge [Flower Harbor Watching Fish], a large park ingeniously combining Jiangnan and Western garden art, watch [Silk Performance], then go to the train station to take the train back to Shenzhen. Spend the night on the train.

Highlights: West Lake is beautiful, especially in spring. The tranquil lake surface, graceful weeping willows... no matter how many times you visit West Lake, its beauty never bores you, and you always discover something new and different.
