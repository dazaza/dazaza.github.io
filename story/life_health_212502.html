Understanding one's constitution and selecting appropriate Chinese herbs is important. Common constitutions include: qi deficiency (weakness), yang deficiency (cold intolerance), blood deficiency (hypoxia), yin deficiency (heat intolerance), damp-heat obstruction, cold-damp phlegm excess, liver qi stagnation, and blood stasis, among nine types.
