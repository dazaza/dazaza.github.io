"All Low Carbon" chief director: We are the special force of Hunan TV

by ditan360 on 2010-07-06 14:32:40

Exclusive to Low Carbon Network: "As the 'special forces' of Hunan TV, we not only need to hold the 'low carbon' position but also continue to produce forward-looking programs for viewers." —— Wang Liang, Chief Director of China's first low-carbon information trend program, "Everyday Low Carbon"

On May 31, 2010, a program called "Everyday Low Carbon" premiered on Qinghai Satellite TV. As China's first "low carbon" program, the words "low carbon" gave the program a trendy and avant-garde label. Meanwhile, the female anchors selected from the "Flowers in Full Bloom" singing competition added more highlights to the program.

On the occasion of the one-month anniversary of the premiere of "Everyday Low Carbon," a reporter from Low Carbon Network conducted an exclusive interview with Chief Director Wang Liang.

The Bond with Low Carbon Due to the Marriage Between Hunan Broadcasting and Qinghai Satellite TV

Wang Liang said: "Our team comes from Hunan Broadcasting, known as Hunan Broadcasting's special forces. This time, the marriage between Hunan Broadcasting and Qinghai Satellite TV has created a platform and opportunity for our program. Qinghai Satellite TV currently adopts a customized program system, which helps us quickly enter. Golden Eagle Media officially expands its thinking and work direction through such a form. At the same time, it fully combines television programs with our strengths."

Wang Liang's team at Golden Eagle Media specializes in planning and operating large-scale events. This time, being "specially dispatched" by Hunan Satellite TV to "Qinghai Satellite TV" to produce a TV show was considered a long-awaited new challenge. It was rumored that the main team originally planned to create a spicy news commentary program, hoping for a more entertainment-oriented approach. With Qinghai Satellite TV's new positioning of "Great Beauty of Qinghai • Green China," Wang Liang and the channel's leaders and colleagues engaged in repeated communication and exchanges. The question of "what kind of program to produce" gradually became clear. This program evolved from an entertainment show into one that represents the channel's characteristics, which is "Everyday Low Carbon."

"G2C" Concept Introducing Low Carbon into People's Lives

Regarding the "G2C" concept introduced for the first time in "Everyday Low Carbon," Wang Liang said that this is an idea. Strictly speaking, G2C means e-government. The production team aims to create an outlet for communication between the government and the people, gradually integrating the government's interpretation of "low carbon" into people's lives, influencing their daily living concepts and habits.

To do well in this "G2C," efforts are needed in many areas. It's not just about broadcasting government documents in a simplified manner; more importantly, content with expressiveness and representativeness must be produced. In terms of program format, traditional boundaries must be broken, especially utilizing the internet platform effectively. A strong online interactive platform needs to be established, mobilizing netizens' participation, while also requiring government officials and experts to appear in the program and on the online platform.

"We aim to create a 'Hundred Schools Forum' for low-carbon environmental protection, representing the mainstream values of the channel." Wang Liang said, "First, introduce a 'Low Carbon Expert Forum' on the website, promoting expert interaction with netizens, which can then be reflected in the program once mature."

Innovative Program Format with Assistance from "Flowers in Full Bloom"

"Our main format uses the anchor's voice broadcast, with rich content encompassing advanced low-carbon technology, low-carbon information, and fun low-carbon news. We use a combination of one studio host and outdoor hosts. Hosts with fresh appearances and pure hearts reflect the natural purity of Qinghai Lake. The studio host mainly broadcasts low-carbon and environmental information, complemented by outdoor low-carbon correspondents' discoveries, making the program content more vivid, closer to life, and more entertaining. Each episode revolves around a theme, with the host using witty and relaxed language to broadcast the latest global low-carbon information, cutting-edge technology, and most creative expressions as the main segments. The entire program process is logically structured, layer by layer, cleverly combining the host's narration with low-carbon experiences, ensuring both richness and entertainment value."

Wang Liang mentioned that "Everyday Low Carbon" chose "Zhou Denglin" from "Flowers in Full Bloom." A distinctive image and voice will aid in the promotion of the program. Zhou Denglin was one of the top 50 contestants in "Flowers in Full Bloom," with a relatively large number of fans and considered a rather talkative "little flower." "Flowers in Full Bloom" is a talent show fully crafted by Qinghai Satellite TV, enjoying significant attention and competitiveness in the current talent show market. Any contestant favored by Qinghai Satellite TV is usually a rising star. Both the program's and the host's attention will increase with the popularity of "Flowers in Full Bloom."

Focusing on Low Carbon, Aim to Produce Forward-Looking Programs

Speaking about the effects after one month of airing, Wang Liang emphasized that ratings are still not the primary concern, but the audience's response has been very good, receiving numerous cooperation requests from organizations and individuals. "We are China's first low-carbon program, filling a gap for governments, enterprises, and the general public. Currently, feedback from all sides is quite positive."

"After airing for a month, we have become clearer about what we need to do. Some new things have been explored, leading to better material selection, more trendy positioning, and a more lifestyle-oriented approach. We need to guide everyone with a low-carbon trend lifestyle, leading rather than following." Simultaneously, Wang Liang mentioned that they have strengthened communication with various carbon emission exchanges, experts, and the government, gradually planning some ground activities. "We need to utilize multimedia dissemination to jointly promote the low-carbon trend."

"I personally believe that low carbon is not something purely high up there, not the work of the government and scientists. It is becoming a concept, seeping into our consciousness. It's not a forced habit; it's trendy, increasingly becoming a fashionable way of life. It can become a trend, a life-related trend." Wang Liang's explanation of low carbon is very clear. "Hunan Satellite TV is an old brand, Golden Eagle Media is a new brand, needing continuous effort to support each other. We are gradually shaping Golden Eagle Media into a 'pioneer' brand through programs and shows. Golden Eagle Media needs to establish a new brand and face the market test."

"Without focusing on ratings, looking at audience comments, doing well in network interactive communities, enriching and solidifying the content, holding the 'low carbon' position, doing well with the Golden Eagle Media brand, and persistently producing forward-looking trend programs —— these are the things we are currently doing."
