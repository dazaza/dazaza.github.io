59-Year-Old Man Suspected of Raping a Mentally Challenged Woman, Causing Her Pregnancy

by xue94fwsh on 2012-03-02 19:13:40

Yangcheng Evening News report by reporter Wang Manqi with photographs: The diagnosis result of "early pregnancy" from the hospital's ultrasound examination came as a bolt from the blue to Xiao Xue (a pseudonym) and her family, leaving them both shocked and indignant. After repeated questioning, the girl identified that she was raped by Fu, a 59-year-old neighbor in the same village who had lured her into his house. The incident happened in a certain village of Donggang Town, Huilai County, Jieyang City. One of the parties involved, Fu, admitted to "touching the girl", but denied raping her and then disappeared. Currently, the local police have intervened in the investigation.

After receiving the tip-off, Yangcheng Evening News reporters went to the scene for an investigation yesterday.

The victim was only 13 years old at the time of the assault.

Yesterday at noon, the reporter entered Xiao Xue's home. Upon seeing strangers, a slim girl quickly retreated into the inner room, hiding behind the bed and refusing to come out, accompanied by low sobs. Her 53-year-old father, Chen Yincai, silently smoked, while her mother looked worried. Her sister Shujun continuously consoled her younger sister Xiao Xue, this little girl born in July 1998, who is not yet 14 years old, but whose abdomen has already begun to swell, indicating a pregnancy of nearly two months. According to the family, Xiao Xue developed a fever when she was six or seven months old, which affected her intellectual development after recovery, leaving her with the intelligence level equivalent to a child aged seven or eight.

During the Spring Festival this year, Shujun brought her younger sister to the hospital for an ultrasound examination upon returning home for the New Year, confirming that Xiao Xue was pregnant. After repeated questioning by the family, Xiao Xue recounted how she was lured and raped: on the afternoon of December 9th last year around 1 pm, her father Chen Yincai finished eating lunch and was about to go to the fields to work. He encountered their neighbor Fu at the door. Their houses were several dozen meters apart, and Fu occasionally came over to drink tea. After greeting Chen Yincai, Fu walked away. A short while later, Xiao Xue heard Fu calling her from outside the door: "Your mom entrusted me with a piece of pork, come and get it." So, Xiao Xue followed him. As soon as she entered Fu's house, which was empty at the time, Fu turned around and locked the door, revealing his true nature and raping her.

According to Xiao Xue's recollection, afterwards Fu gave her 30 yuan and threatened her not to tell her parents. When Xiao Xue returned home, she did not mention anything about her experience. It wasn't until her mother noticed that her daughter's menstruation was delayed that the family took Xiao Xue to the hospital for an ultrasound examination on the second day of the lunar New Year, discovering that Xiao Xue was nearly 50 days pregnant. Based on this calculation, it was determined that Xiao Xue was raped around December 9th last year, when she was only 13 years old.

The suspect suddenly went missing.

Shujun told the reporter that when her sister mentioned it, she felt very angry but also did not want to wrong any good person. After repeated inquiries, Xiao Xue insisted that it was Fu himself. The straightforward Chen Yincai family first informed Fu's wife, who had a big argument with Fu. It was said that Fu eventually only admitted to giving Xiao Xue 30 yuan, but just touched her body, denying any rape.

According to Xiao Xue's family, after the incident, the village cadres also intervened in the mediation. Fu hoped to "settle privately", finally proposing a compensation of 20,000 yuan, but was rejected by Xiao Xue's family. On January 27th this year, Xiao Xue, accompanied by her family, reported the case to the Donggang Town Police Station of Huilai County.

At around 7 pm that evening, when the police officers arrived at Fu's house to take him back for assistance in the investigation, Fu was nowhere to be found. The next day, Fu's wife also left home. Since then, the investigating police officers have conducted four investigations in the village, but Fu was not at home each time.

Yesterday at noon, the reporter knocked on Fu's door, where Fu's daughter and daughter-in-law were eating inside. Fu's daughter claimed that she could not contact her father either. Regarding Fu's alleged rape of Xiao Xue, she stated: "Everyone in the village is talking about it, I almost can't stand it anymore. That's a rumor, I don't believe my father would do such a thing!" Why did Fu leave home? Fu's daughter told the reporter that she thought her father couldn't bear what people were saying around him, even his own wife "wronged" him, so he left. She hopes that her father will return soon to clarify the situation to the public security authorities and prove his innocence.

Shujun said that her younger sister is so young, the fetus cannot be allowed to be born. On January 30th, the police officers took Xiao Xue to a relevant institution in Huilai County for a gynecological examination. The forensic doctor said that the fetus must wait until it is three months old before an abortion can be performed. Xiao Xue's family expressed that they now only hope that the police can catch the suspect as soon as possible. As long as a DNA comparison is made between the suspect and the fetus in Xiao Xue's belly, the truth will come to light.
