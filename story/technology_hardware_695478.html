Female Entrepreneurs “Fly Back” to Participate in Donation

On the eve of Mother's Day, the condition of 11-year-old "aplastic anemia" boy Sun Kai from Cangzhou touched the hearts of 22 women in the provincial capital. Representatives of the Qiaodong Female Entrepreneurs Chamber of Commerce handed over 9000 yuan in donations collected by 22 sisters to the newspaper office. They said that little Sun Kai is a pitiful yet adorable child. Everyone was deeply moved after reading the report. Due to their busy schedules, they couldn't visit him personally, so they entrusted our newspaper to pass on the money to Sun Kai's family, hoping that their small gesture could help Sun Kai with his treatment.

"Sisters: The Hejian section of the Yanzhao Daily has continuously reported on the case of 11-year-old Sun Kai from Guoziwawa Village who suffers from aplastic anemia and whose family is heavily in debt, making it difficult to treat Sun Kai," "The Qiaodong Women’s Chamber of Commerce is hosting a 'Spring Charity' event. Let's light up the lamp in Sun Kai's life with your love!" This was the charity SMS sent by Ms. Xu Shequn of the Qiaodong Female Entrepreneurs Chamber on May 3rd.

Chamber Executive President Jin Shujuan told reporters that Vice President Wu Lanzhi was deeply moved after reading the reports about little Sun Kai, thus initiating a donation for Sun Kai. The call received an overwhelming response, and on the same day, Vice Presidents Zhang Jihong, Jin Shujuan, Wu Lanzhi, Xu Shequn, and others donated 1800 yuan. Subsequently, everyone contributed generously—some donating 300 yuan, others 500 yuan.

Ms. Wu Yanfang, who was on a business trip in Guangzhou, upon receiving the chamber presidents' charity SMS, flew back specially to join the fundraising efforts.

Ms. Zhang Jihong stated that 9000 yuan may not be much, but it carries the heartfelt love of the sisters. Hearing that Sun Kai's preliminary matching was successful, we sincerely hope for Sun Kai's good luck and quick recovery.

Yandu Group Netizens Have Raised 38639 Yuan for Sun Kai

Since our newspaper launched the "Save Little Sun Kai" initiative and initiated the "1 Yuan Group Buying" charity public welfare donation activity on Yanzhao Metropolis Network, it has been warmly responded to by a large number of netizens and readers.

On April 26th, Yandu Group sent the first batch of 5000 yuan in donations to Sun Kai, followed by another 24000 yuan into Sun Kai's charity account on April 29th. By 18:41 PM on May 5th, Yandu Group netizens had raised a total of 38639 yuan for Sun Kai. Each donation is filled with the blessings and hopes of loving netizens. Everyone hopes to help Sun Kai change his fate and overcome the illness through their own efforts.

Donation Amount for Little Sun Kai Exceeds 300,000 Yuan

At around 18:00 on May 5th, when the reporter contacted Liu Wenfang, she was processing the relevant procedures for bone marrow matching at the Beijing Military Region Hospital. The attending doctor informed her that after further screening, two of the volunteers who initially matched successfully were selected as the most suitable donors, and happily, both volunteers were very willing to donate bone marrow to Sun Kai. Next, the two volunteers will need to submit their blood samples. After further comparison, the final donor can be determined. "The doctor said that once the blood sample is delivered, the result will be out in seven days. We are really excited," Liu Wenfang said.

Meanwhile, the amount of social donations for Sun Kai continues to increase. Liu Wenfang mentioned that during the May Day holiday, a father-daughter pair from Hejian specifically went to Cangzhou to visit Sun Kai. The girl, aged 12, encouraged Sun Kai to recover well and get healthy soon, leaving behind 600 yuan. Liu Wenfang asked the father to leave his name and contact information, but he refused, only revealing his surname Miao.

"Thank you all so much. Currently, I have a total of 252,000 yuan in my bank account. Adding the cash donations from kind-hearted people and departments, the total amount of love donations has exceeded 300,000 yuan," Liu Wenfang said excitedly.

Vocational Education Students Send Letters to Encourage Sun Kai

During the May Day holiday, a letter from Zhangjiakou arrived at our Cangzhou workstation, expressing concern and blessings for Sun Kai between its lines.

This letter was written by all students of Class 15 Accounting at Zhangjiakou Vocational Education Center. In the letter, the students wrote: "Sun Kai, are you feeling better now? Although the disease has caused physical harm, we believe that inside, you should be an optimistic and strong little boy, right? Don't feel lonely because we are always paying attention to you from afar. We are willing to accompany you through difficulties, and we are willing to listen to your growth stories."

Inside the envelope was also a letter written by the head teacher of Class 15. She wrote: "Sun Kai, when you get well, let your parents take you to Zhangjiakou for a visit. When we have holidays, we'll come see you and bring you Zhangjiakou specialties and many gifts. This is our agreement. I want to tell you, you must be strong. Believe that all of us will help you. What we all hope to see most is your radiant smile. Behind you, there are countless uncles, aunts, brothers, and sisters supporting and encouraging you. Be ambitious!"
