Liu Yan makes an appearance in "Xiantu", temporarily retires to the US and strikes a deal with Shi Yuzhu

On May 18, the online game "Xiantu" held its internal testing launch event by West Lake in Hangzhou. Liu Yan, the latest image spokesperson for "Xiantu" and the most sensual female broadcaster, made her appearance dressed in chic clothing, showcasing a classical elegance unlike anything seen before through song and dance, and even struck a "deal" on-site with Shi Yuzhu, the mastermind behind "Xiantu".

Exhibiting classical elegance, Liu Yan's stunning appearance in "Cai Zi Qu"

For this event promoting "Xiantu", Liu Yan changed her usual fashionable and versatile style, appearing in an elegant blue and white ancient dress, gracefully performing a segment of "Jia Ren Qu" from Zhang Ziyi's movie "Ten Faces of Danger". Liu Yan's light and agile dance moves, both heroic and delicate, transported the audience into a fantastical realm, capturing the attention of many media members present, and eliciting continuous praise from Shi Yuzhu, the head of "Xiantu". This allowed everyone to witness the elegant and classical side of this "most sensual female broadcaster" that they had never seen before.

Liu Yan expressed that representing a game like "Xiantu", filled with ingenious fantasies and hero battles, was not just a successful collaboration but also the realization of her childhood dream. She stated that sometimes games are more than just games because everyone harbors a hero dream deep down inside. Real life may be uneventful, but games bring a completely different kind of excitement. Liu Yan said: "When I was young, I once dreamed of vanquishing demons and becoming a heroine who roams the江湖 (jianghu). Now, this desire has been fulfilled within the game."

Liu Yan is about to go to the US, striking a deal with Shi Yuzhu on-site

Regarding Liu Yan, this devoted fan of "Xiantu" who has a special fondness for games, Shi Yuzhu, the mastermind behind "Xiantu", also meticulously prepared a special gift. On-site, he presented Liu Yan with a crystal seven-color Bao Lian lamp. Shi Yuzhu stated that this was a limited-edition divine object in "Xiantu", hoping that through this gift, he could express his support and encouragement for Liu Yan, wishing her progress and success akin to having divine assistance in her upcoming work, life, and "cultivation" journey in "Xiantu".

In response to earlier rumors that Liu Yan would temporarily retire from hosting to further her studies in the US, Liu Yan gave a positive response at the press conference. She indicated that this was merely a way for her to pursue new changes, hoping to achieve new "cultivation" in a new environment. Liu Yan joked that she would recommend foreign friends understand Chinese culture through "Xiantu". Upon hearing this, Shi Yuzhu immediately expressed his full support for Liu Yan's decision and even promised on-site to conduct a "deal" with Liu Yan. If the number of participants in the internal test of "Xiantu" exceeds 300,000, he will provide support for Liu Yan's studies and life in the US, and will engage in deeper cooperation in the future.

The "cultivation" path of "Miss Shi" Liu Yan

At the launch event, Shi Yuzhu revealed on-site why he chose to collaborate with Liu Yan among a host of female stars. He stated that choosing "Miss Shi" was simple, adhering to the principle of "beauty and intelligence equally important". Inviting Liu Yan this time was not only due to her youthful and versatile appearance suitable for both ancient and modern settings but also because in this fiery female broadcaster, he saw the chivalrous spirit of a righteous woman. Liu Yan's persistent journey forward and constant self-transcendence perfectly aligns with the "cultivation" spirit advocated by the game.

Liu Yan, the immensely popular mainland "most sensual female broadcaster" and representative figure of the new generation of multi-talented actresses. Born in Hunan and raised in Guangdong, Liu Yan entered the entertainment industry in 2006 after participating in a hosting competition and signing with Glitter Media. With her sweet looks, fiery figure, and fresh natural hosting style, Liu Yan flourished in the variety and entertainment hosting world. Having worked alongside seasoned male hosts such as Wu Zongxian, Li Bin, and Dai Jun, Liu Yan quickly expanded her career territory thanks to her quick-witted and fiery hosting and "daring to compete and perform" spirit. Currently, she hosts nearly ten programs including Zhejiang TV's "Love to Win", Anhui TV's "Sunday's Greatest", Glitter Media's "Music Storm Ranking", Ningxia TV's "Fashion Star Talent", and showcases her charm at various concerts and award ceremonies on CCTV and local satellite channels. In 2010, Liu Yan won the "Most Potential Host" award evaluated by the "Variety" magazine under the State Administration of Radio, Film, and Television, with her unique hosting style recognized within the industry and by audiences.

Beyond hosting, Liu Yan, who can sing and dance, develops in multiple fields such as film, television, music, and modeling. In acting, Liu Yan made guest appearances in films like "Painted Skin" and "Mars No Problem", acted in TV series like "Eagle and Falcon" and "Secret History of Yang Guifei", and starred in the annual New Year stage play "Happy Mahua 2008 Nobody Is Allowed to Laugh". Her outstanding acting talent continues to be discovered. In 2009, Liu Yan also ventured into the music scene, releasing her debut EP "Liu Yan Yan Color" with a super luxurious production team, presenting three singles with distinct styles: "Sun Shangxiang", "Unintentional Mistake", and "I'm Not Afraid", winning several awards for new artists in the music industry. Known as the "Dance Queen of the Hosting World", Liu Yan also excels in dancing, successfully advancing to the finals of Dragon TV's "Dancing World" in 2009 and winning the "Dancing Champion" through online voting with her outstanding dance steps and amiable charm. With both beauty and talent, Liu Yan has become a favorite of fashion media, frequently appearing on prestigious fashion magazines like "Men's Health", "Esquire", and "GQ", showcasing her graceful body paired with a sunny smile, and interpreting her own "Sunny Sensuality" in front of the camera.
