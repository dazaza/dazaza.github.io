Guangzhou Asian Games Ticket Purchasing Guide: 3 Channels Available for Buying Tickets

by hipposhen on 2010-04-06 21:55:04

The much-anticipated tickets for the Guangzhou Asian Games have finally gone on public sale, with ticket sales divided into three phases and sold through three channels. According to Yuan Yue, Deputy Director of Market Development Department of the Organizing Committee, the opening and closing ceremony tickets are priced at a maximum of 6800 yuan and a minimum of 1600 yuan; while the event tickets are priced at a maximum of 1600 yuan and a minimum of 10 yuan. The ticket design fully takes into account the factors of Lingnan auspicious culture, with "6" and "8" as lucky numbers. The tickets will use multiple anti-counterfeiting technologies that can be identified by machines, experts, and ordinary audiences to ensure their authenticity.

1. Ticket Prices

More than 2 million tickets for this Asian Games will be publicly sold globally. Tickets below 100 yuan (10 yuan, 30 yuan, and 80 yuan) account for 45.3% of the total available tickets; tickets ranging from 10 yuan to 400 yuan account for 87% of the total available tickets; tickets ranging from 160 yuan to 800 yuan account for 51.9% of the total available tickets; tickets over 1000 yuan account for 2.6% of the total available tickets.

According to Yuan Yue, to avoid the phenomenon of "ticket scalping," the opening and closing ceremony tickets for the Beijing Olympics adopted a real-name system, but the opening and closing ceremony tickets for the Guangzhou Asian Games will not adopt a real-name system. However, citizens must register their names and ID numbers when collecting tickets, which can also be considered a form of real-name system.

2. Sales Phases

Phase One: Time - January to March 2010,

Core Sales Policy: Advance payment, public subscription, lottery confirmation;

Phase Two: Time - July to August 2010,

Core Sales Policy: Real-time sales, first come first served;

Phase Three: Time - October 2010 to the end of the Asian Games,

Core Sales Policy: Real-time sales, first come first served.

3. Purchase Channels

The public can purchase tickets through three channels: the official ticketing website, ticket agency outlets, and venue ticket booths during the event period.

China's banking partner, Industrial and Commercial Bank of China, will set up 400 official ticket agency points nationwide, including in Hong Kong and Macau regions.

4. Anti-counterfeiting Measures

To prevent counterfeit tickets, the Guangzhou Asian Games tickets will use at least three advanced anti-counterfeiting technologies. Yuan Yue revealed to the reporters that the adoption of ticket anti-counterfeiting technology is mainly to ensure the order and safety of the Asian Games and prevent the appearance of counterfeit tickets, stop "scalpers" from reselling tickets, and protect the legitimate rights and interests of the audience. "A single ticket can adopt up to seven or eight anti-counterfeiting technologies," said Yuan Yue.

These anti-counterfeiting technologies are roughly divided into three categories: the first category is machine detection; the second category is expert identification; and the third category is what ordinary people can identify with the naked eye.

5. Ticket Quotas

Ticket sales for the Asian Games are divided into international and domestic parts. Internationally, the Organizing Committee will cooperate with the market development agent of the Olympic Council of Asia to promote Asian Games tickets outside of China, especially in East Asia, together with local Olympic Committees, encouraging people from all over Asia to come and watch the games.

Yuan Yue introduced that the international part of the ticket sales is mainly applied for by the Olympic Committees of various countries and regions, and the Guangzhou Organizing Committee can prioritize meeting their needs.

Currently, the official ticketing website for the Asian Games (www.tickets.gz2010.cn) is technically supported by Urban Online E-commerce Co., Ltd., using the same electronic payment technology as the sales platform under Urban Online (www.311piao.com, a professional ticketing website), which is already quite mature. Therefore, everyone can place orders with confidence.
