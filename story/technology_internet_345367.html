At Kyotanda Airport, in the bustling airport hall filled with people coming and going, a delicate figure was making her way through the crowd with effort. Her beige leisure suit accentuated her youthful and charming beauty. Perhaps due to the long flight, her beautiful eyes were filled with fatigue, and there was a hidden weariness in her brows.

"Hmm, have I finally arrived?" Su Yixuan opened her eyes wide, looking at the endless stream of people around her, muttering to herself. The originally somewhat dim little face now radiated with a certain brilliance at this moment.

Su Yixuan, congratulations, you've finally left that noisy home. From now on, you will have to make your own way here. Believe in yourself; everything will be as wonderful as you imagined.

She silently cheered herself up in her heart, then pulled the black suitcase behind her and walked briskly and resolutely toward the outside of the airport. What she needed most right now was a clean and comfortable bed where she could have a good sleep.

After walking a few steps, Su Yixuan suddenly remembered that before boarding, she had put her phone and handbag inside the suitcase, along with some Hai Tai outdoor gear. Thinking that she would need to take a taxi soon, she stopped hurriedly. Then she squatted down, unzipped the suitcase, and prepared to take out the wallet and phone inside.

On the other side of the airport,

A man dressed in a linen-colored casual outfit was running quickly toward Su Yixuan. Due to his running, his slightly long bangs fluttered lightly, adding a touch of elegance to his handsome appearance. He was pulling a black suitcase whose wheels spun rapidly on the smooth floor, emitting a "la la" sound.

All the passersby couldn't help but look at this handsome man, and several young girls even wanted to run over and talk to him. However, they were intimidated by the group of 'black-clad men' following behind him.

They can't be from the mafia, can they?

Almost everyone had this thought in their minds. Some excited young ladies even shouted for the man to run faster, not wanting such a handsome prince to be captured and humiliated by the mafia. Their imaginations had already conjured up countless different versions of stories.

Lan Jialuo, hearing the shouts, raised a bright smile on his handsome lips. He turned around with a laugh to look at the black-clad men who were chasing him closely. A hint of mockery and a faint sadness passed through his clear and enchanting eyes.

I will absolutely never go back with you!

He vowed silently in his heart, and his pace quickened even more as he ran forward. However, he didn't notice the small figure squatting on the ground ahead when he turned to look at the black-clad men. Therefore, naturally, when Lan Jialuo turned back and saw the silhouette, his body was already unable to stop itself.

Just as Su Yixuan was about to stand up holding her wallet, she suddenly felt a heavy force pulling her down. Then, she felt a pain at the back of her head, followed by a weight on her body.
