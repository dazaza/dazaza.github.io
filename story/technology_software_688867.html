After summer rhinitis surgery, what else do we need to pay attention to? Category: Health Hotspots Date: June 8, 2011. After rhinitis surgery in the summer, what should we do? Experts from Chengdu Good News Ear, Nose and Throat Hospital introduced that for patients who have undergone rhinitis surgery, maintenance is crucial, and one must not think that there's nothing to worry about after the surgery in summer. Patients who have had rhinitis surgery in the summer should eat more fruits and vegetables rich in vitamins to enhance the self-repair metabolic function of the mucosa. Smoking, drinking alcohol, and eating spicy food should be avoided. One should also avoid crowded public places to reduce air stimulation to the nasal cavity. In addition to strengthening nutrition, physical exercise is also important. It is recommended to go out for a walk or run in the morning if possible, breathing fresh air.

Summer rhinitis is a common and easily occurring condition, so it is essential to pay attention to the diet after summer surgery. Experts pointed out that good dietary habits are the key to completely treating summer rhinitis. Therefore, everyone must pay attention to their diet after surgery:

After summer rhinitis surgery, the following foods should be avoided:

1. Beef, caffeinated beverages, chocolate, citrus juice, corn, dairy products, eggs, oats, oysters, peanuts, salmon, strawberries, cantaloupe, tomatoes, wheat;

2. Cold drinks: overly cold foods can lower immunity, cause chronic rhinitis, and lead to respiratory allergies;

3. Stimulating foods: such as chili peppers, mustard, etc., which can easily irritate the respiratory mucosa;

4. Special processed or refined foods;

5. Artificial colors: especially yellow No. 5 color.

After summer rhinitis surgery, it is recommended to eat more of the following foods:

1. Foods rich in vitamin C and vitamin A: spinach, Chinese cabbage, baby bok choy, white radish, etc.;

2. Warm-natured foods: ginger, garlic, leek, coriander, etc.;

3. Glutinous rice, yam, jujube, lotus seed, barley, brown sugar, and longan.

Finally, experts from Chengdu Good News Ear, Nose and Throat Hospital kindly remind all patients that the above content is only for reference and cannot be used as a prescription or treatment method. It is suggested that everyone follow the doctor's advice regarding their diet after rhinitis surgery! Early detection, early prevention, and early treatment of any disease are the keys to maintaining health!
