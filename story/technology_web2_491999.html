Because of love

by mgvfeifeix on 2011-09-20 12:45:03

When she does not love you, you should also give her blessings. With love, there should be no hate, because there was once love, and the days of love were happy. The time spent together was also joyful. Having had happiness and love, there will be no more hatred. She loses someone who loves her, and you lose someone who does not love you, but you gain a new opportunity to live and love again. Take a deep breath; on the path of life, there are blossoms of love, and there is always one that belongs to you. Although there are many flowers, none of them are repeated; this has been destined for countless lifetimes.

Love is a feeling, and not loving is also a feeling. Often, what is difficult to determine is the feeling in your heart: is it love or not? Originally held in your hands may not necessarily be what you truly possess, and what you possess may not necessarily be what you truly cherish in your heart. Many times in life, you need to consciously let go, because when you have, you might be losing, and when you let go, you might be regaining.

Because you've loved, you won't become enemies; because you've been hurt, you won't become friends.

A wise person knows how to let go, a sincere person knows how to sacrifice, and a happy person understands transcendence. For those who do not love you, what is most needed is understanding, letting go, and blessing. Too much self-pity is like begging for the other person's charity. Loving and being loved are both things that make people happy. Don't let these turn into pain. Since you've already experienced them, years later, occasionally recalling them, I hope they are all beautiful memories. Live confidently and happily, leaving the most beautiful smile for the person who hurt you the most. A smart person knows how to make themselves happy.

Cherish those you love and those who love you.

When she does not love you, your love becomes her burden. Please do not calculate your contributions or expect any returns. You care, but she does not. Loving someone who does not love you back is inherently without reward. Do not dwell on right or wrong; this will make you happier. Do not lose confidence, because loving someone is not about their excellence, but a feeling. She makes you feel this way, so you love her. Similarly, she does not love you, it is not because you are not excellent. Excellence is not a reason for love. There are still so many people who love you. Smiling faintly can also be very sweet.

If five hundred glances in the past life bring a fleeting encounter in this life, then that must already be very happy. In fact, a fleeting encounter is also a deep kind of destiny. Buddha said: five hundred glances bring a fleeting encounter in this life. It may take only a second to meet someone, a minute to know someone, an hour to fall for someone, a day to love someone, but it takes a lifetime to forget someone.

Love does not have to last forever. What you once had may be the most beautiful memory of your life. Because you've loved, you won't become enemies; because you've been hurt, you won't become friends; you can only be the most familiar strangers. Love reveals the depth of feelings, and drunkenness reveals the strength of wine. The memory of love should be cherished, but the future happiness needs to be sought independently.

When she does not love you, whether she once loved you or later forgot, or perhaps never loved you at all, when you can no longer be the person in her heart, her heart will not remember you. Please do not disturb her when you're unhappy; her place is definitely not where you should be going now. Please do not tell her your trivial matters; she has neither the time nor the interest to understand you or your life. Even if you do tell her, she will soon forget. Without love, you are destined not to enter her life. Please do not cry in front of her; she cannot give you care or concern, at most just a little sympathy. Only those who love you will truly cherish you, rather than just offering passive sympathy and pity.

When she does not love you, it is the time for you to disappear from her life. Leave her immediately and live your own life with pride. At the same time, you hope she finds happiness and joy, and discovers her future. Gently embrace the warmth of your memories and softly gaze at the fading tenderness. No matter the outcome, it would ruin the beauty of what once was. Leave cleanly and perhaps, after several years, on some sunny afternoon, she will squint and recall a beautiful moment, smiling knowingly. All the nostalgia, worth it.
