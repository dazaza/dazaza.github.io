by jifengkay on 2009-05-15 16:50:43

Time: Winter

Place: A highway in Northeast China

Characters: Me and a female online friend whom I had chatted with for a long time. She had just come to my city for our first meeting, and we both felt good about each other and were ready to develop further.

The incident happened on the way back when I was driving her home. The two-hour journey became even slower due to heavy snowfall.

Not long after getting into the car, I noticed her expression looked unnatural. Her face grew increasingly stiff. After more than an hour, she told me she really had to go to the bathroom but couldn't hold it any longer. With the vast snowy landscape around us on the highway, there was no sign of a restroom in sight. We found ourselves in an extremely embarrassing situation. After about 20 minutes of enduring, she reached her limit. I had to stop the car, open the rear door, and stand facing the direction of oncoming vehicles as a gentlemanly shield while she relieved herself in the small enclosed space formed by me and the car door.

The sound of "shushing" made both of us very uncomfortable. At that moment, I thought this must be the most awkward date of my life. But what happened next completely overturned my thoughts.

After the sound stopped, the girl remained silent for a long time. Just as I was wondering why, I suddenly heard her say, "I'm stuck," and she asked me to come over. I was momentarily thrilled, but when I turned around, I was shocked — everyone knows that when the temperature is too low, if there's water on your skin, it can freeze upon contact with metal, causing you to be "stuck." In this case, her skin and the car contributed... her buttocks...

I immediately panicked. Her face turned red as if blood would seep out, and I was also flushed. Those with experience know that the only solution at this point is to pour warm water to melt the ice, which will free the person. But where could we find warm water?

It was at this critical moment that the girl quickly came up with a plan. Her timid voice almost made me faint, "How about you try peeing...?"

After the rescue operation, the rest of the trip was silent, and we never contacted each other again...
