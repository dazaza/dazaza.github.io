Xiaomi's new executive, Hugo Barra, appears at Xiaomi Corporation and will attend the new product launch event.

by anonymous on 2013-09-04 15:03:21

Figure 1: Hugo Barra at Xiaomi Corporation

Figure 2: Hugo Barra taking a group photo with the employees of Xiaomi Corporation

On September 3, it was reported that Hugo Barra, former senior executive of Google who had just announced his joining of Xiaomi Corporation, appeared in the headquarters of Xiaomi Corporation in the afternoon. Lei Jun, CEO of Xiaomi Corporation, confirmed that Barra would attend the annual launch event on September 5.

Many employees of Xiaomi Corporation revealed that Barra came to Xiaomi not long after arriving in Beijing and was warmly welcomed by the employees. Employees from different business departments of Xiaomi all requested to take photos with Barra, and Barra was very cooperative.

It is also disclosed by informed sources that after joining Xiaomi Corporation, Barra has already received the latest Xiaomi 3 mobile phone and is currently experiencing this latest product.

Hugo Barra, Global Vice President of Android at Google, joined Xiaomi Corporation as its Vice President this time. According to available information, Hugo Barra joined Google in 2008 and served as the manager of Google's UK mobile product department. In 2010, Barra became the product director of the Android team and later was promoted to Global Vice President. (End)
