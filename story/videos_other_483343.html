Love, is the melon seeds in the cup, every bite has a taste of happiness, making him feel warm even in the cold winter day.

Preface

The stairs were covered with snow water, and each step was taken with extra caution. Slowly walking upstairs to the second floor and turning left, I saw my deskmate gazing tenderly at Yingying, reminiscent of the Broken Bridge by the West Lake. The only difference is that instead of a silk umbrella in Xuxian's hand, there was a cup full of melon seeds...

Back at my seat, I took a closer look and saw an orange glass cup with Hello Kitty printed on it, filled with melon seeds. What excited me most was that there was a stainless steel spoon in the cup. Many beauties have faded like flowers, but only a considerate woman can do this.

Thinking carefully, they are quite a good match. One is the center of the school basketball team, 1.8 meters tall, tall and mighty. The other one is a demure beauty, very lovely. Because her name contains "Yingying", I couldn't help but associate her with Ren Yingying, the Holy Maiden of the Devil Sect in Jin Yong's masterpiece "Smiling Proud Wanderer". Therefore, the title "Chongge" is undoubtedly suitable for my deskmate.

When did I start feeling so tired of everything?

In the afternoon class, the teacher asked me why I looked so gloomy. I kept silent. This sweetness can only be understood, not expressed in words. Watching his happy face, I felt as if I was in love too. Maybe happiness is contagious.

Writing up to here, Chongge invited me to share, but I refused by shaking my head. Thinking about that cup of melon seeds, she must have spent a lot of time on it. I, who hasn't contributed anything, don't deserve such a generous gift.

Looking at the white snow outside the window and glancing at the melon seeds in the orange glass cup on the desk, I felt much warmer. Even as an outsider, I still feel some warmth. I think he should be even warmer!

This short article of a hundred words is my witness to my deskmate's love story. Of course, I have always been against dating during schooling. However, given the situation, I can only wish them happiness.
