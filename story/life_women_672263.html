On the occasion of International Women's Day on March 8th, Nanchong Oriental Hospital specially launches a women's health care program to pay tribute to the women of Guocheng, Nanchong, allowing them to enjoy better quality, more affordable, and more efficient medical services.

Health Care Program One: All female friends who make online appointments can receive one free gynecological examination.

Relevant medical examinations are the first step in disease diagnosis, but many women give up seeking medical treatment due to concerns about high examination fees or false hospital advertisements. On this Women's Day, Nanchong Oriental Hospital fully promotes gynecological disease examination projects, bringing real medical discounts to the women of Guocheng, Nanchong.

- Routine Gynecological Examination: Diagnosing uterine, adnexal, and vaginal lesions

- Routine Vaginal Discharge Test: Diagnosing vaginal inflammation such as trichomoniasis and fungal infections

- Routine Urine Test: Diagnosing urethral inflammation, infection, etc.

- Early Pregnancy Test: Diagnosing pregnancy

- Color Doppler Ultrasound: Diagnosing uterine, adnexal, and uterine cavity myomas, cysts, etc.

Total: 198 RMB

Note: After registering at the third-floor consultation desk with the online appointment discount number, you will be eligible for a free examination. Single use only.

Health Care Program Two: Medical cost assistance available for the following diseases

For diseases such as cervical erosion, unintended pregnancies, uterine fibroids, and ovarian cysts, minimally invasive diagnostic assistance not only allows women to experience advanced minimally invasive technology but also significantly reduces costs, truly achieving less spending and effective treatment.

- Cervical Diseases: Shadun 120 Cervical Repair Workstation - Surgery fee reduction of 120 RMB

- Unintended Pregnancy: Safe and Painless Abortion Type I - Fixed price of 660 RMB

- Uterine Fibroids: German WOLF Laparoscopic Minimally Invasive Technology - Surgery capped at 4000-6000 RMB

- Ovarian Cysts: German WOLF Laparoscopic Minimally Invasive Technology - Surgery capped at 4000-6000 RMB

Health Care Program Three: Expectant mothers can participate in the following promotional activities

1. During the event period, the first 3 expectant mothers each day who undergo a four-dimensional ultrasound can enjoy a 50% discount (i.e., 130 RMB for a four-dimensional ultrasound, plus a free commemorative CD of the baby's four-dimensional images).

2. During the event period, expectant mothers who register at the obstetrics department will receive a free fetal heart monitoring voucher and a prenatal education CD.

Health Care Program Four: Free consultations with renowned Nanchong experts for 38 cases of gynecological and obstetric complex diseases

Jiang Yu

Leader of the Obstetrics and Gynecology Department, Director of the Obstetrics and Gynecology Department, Renowned Obstetrics and Gynecology Expert in Nanchong

Leader of the Obstetrics and Gynecology Department at Oriental Hospital, member of the Obstetrics and Gynecology Committee of the Chinese Medical Association, and member of the expert group of the Nanchong Medical Malpractice Technical Appraisal Committee. Graduated from North Sichuan Medical College, served as the director of the Obstetrics and Gynecology Department at Gaoping District People's Hospital (formerly Nanchong County People's Hospital) for 21 years, and completed training in obstetrics and gynecology at West China University of Medical Sciences, Chongqing Ninth People's Hospital, and the First and Second Affiliated Hospitals of Chongqing Medical University. Specializes in gynecological tumors, female reproductive organ inflammation, infertility, endometriosis, perinatal care, high-risk pregnancy monitoring and treatment, and has rich experience in cervical cancer radical surgery, vulvar cancer radical surgery, late-stage ovarian tumor debulking surgery, sigmoid colon vaginoplasty, transvaginal hysterectomy series surgeries, and laparoscopic surgery.

Sheng Hongying

Deputy Chief Physician, Director of the Obstetrics Outpatient Department

Graduated from the Clinical Medicine Department of Luzhou Medical College. Engaged in obstetrics and gynecology for over 30 years, with a systematic medical theoretical foundation and rich clinical experience. From being a young doctor to becoming an expert, she has performed countless surgeries without any errors or accidents. She practices medicine rigorously, striving for perfection in her technical skills. She loves learning and research, accepting new concepts and technologies. In the late 70s, she pioneered the "new cesarean section cosmetic incision" and "amniocentesis induction" techniques in the Nanchong region, receiving commendations from the Nanchong Regional Health Bureau. In the 80s, she successfully performed a rare removal of an 11-kilogram uterine fibroid. Cervical cancer, endometrial cancer surgery, and chemotherapy treatments are among Dr. Sheng's specialties.

This seasoned obstetrics and gynecology expert has excellent medical ethics and has earned the respect and admiration of many patients.
