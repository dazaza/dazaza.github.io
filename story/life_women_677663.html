Sanqin experts tell you how women can self-check for infertility.

How can women self-check for infertility? Infertility causes great physical and psychological trauma to women, not only intensifying family conflicts but also increasing social problems. Experts from Xi'an Infertility Hospital believe that there are many details in daily life that if you pay attention to, you can conduct a self-check and find issues. Below is how women can self-check for infertility:

Self-check 1

Menstrual disorders: Menstruation comes earlier or later; excessive or insufficient menstrual flow; obvious prolongation of menstruation. Cause: Menstrual disorders are often related to endocrine disorders, inadequate luteal function, and uterine lining inflammation.

Self-check 2

Amenorrhea: Age over 18 years old without menstruation; after the onset of menstruation, continuous amenorrhea exceeds six months. Cause: When suffering from uterine, ovarian, pituitary, or hypothalamic infertility, amenorrhea is very likely to occur.

Self-check 3

Dysmenorrhea: Those who originally had no history of dysmenorrhea suddenly experience severe abdominal pain during menstruation. Cause: When diseases such as endometriosis, pelvic inflammatory disease, uterine fibroids, poor uterine development, or abnormal uterine position exist, menstrual abdominal pain may occur.

Self-check 4

Premenstrual syndrome: Symptoms such as "breast distension", "headache", "swelling", "fever", "acne" appear before and after each menstruation. Cause: These symptoms are caused by endocrine disorders leading to inadequate luteal function, which can also lead to infertility.

Self-check 5

Abnormal vaginal discharge: Increased vaginal discharge, yellow color, with odor, like tofu residue or watery, accompanied by external genital itching, pain, etc. Cause: Often accompanies various gynecological inflammations, such as vaginitis, cervical inflammation (cervical erosion), endometritis, adnexal inflammation, pelvic inflammatory disease, and various sexually transmitted diseases.

Self-check 6

Abdominal pain: Chronic lower abdomen, bilateral abdomen dull pain or lumbar sacral pain. Cause: Pelvic inflammatory disease, cervical inflammation, oophoritis, endometriosis, uterine fibroids, ovarian tumors will present with abdominal pain symptoms.

Self-check 7

Breast galactorrhea: During non-lactating periods, breast milk leaks spontaneously or when squeezed. Cause: Related to hypothalamic dysfunction, pituitary tumors, prolactinomas, primary hypothyroidism, chronic renal failure, etc., and can also be caused by contraceptives and antihypertensive drugs such as reserpine. Galactorrhea often combines with amenorrhea leading to infertility.

Many women often neglect these issues due to being too busy, thinking they are minor problems, or because of carelessness, do not take them seriously, and just take some medicine to finish it off. Experts on infertility from Xi'an Sanqin Hospital remind all women of childbearing age to pay attention to their own "minor issues", pay attention to daily self-inspection, and prevent regretting infertility.
