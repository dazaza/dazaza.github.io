The emergence of DINK (Double Income No Kids) families in cities and the increasing number of elderly empty-nesters has made having pets a consumer trend. It is estimated that urban residents in our country spend as much as tens of billions of yuan annually on pets. Since entering the 1990s, China has become one of the countries with the fastest growth in pet product production in the world, with the demand for all aspects of pets growing at an annual rate of 15%. This has brought a huge market to pet investment operators.

A certain product advertised extensively on television claims to use an American formula, but its sale price of less than 200 yuan far exceeds its production cost of just over ten yuan per box, clearly showing that profit still accounts for the majority. What sells best in online stores? The editor of Online Store Network has summarized the most profitable industries of 2012, hoping it will provide inspiration and help to those who run online stores.

We can get a side glimpse of the high profits of liquor. In previous years, CCTV's advertising bid king was almost monopolized by liquor companies every year. One year, Qinchuan Liquor shocked the nation when it bid for the prime-time advertising bid king by making a bold claim. Without sufficient profits, which company would dare to make such a big investment?

Our country is an agricultural powerhouse, but lags behind in the deep processing of agricultural products. The proportion of deeply processed agricultural products accounts for less than 12% of the total output of agricultural products. Taking corn as an example, in the U.S., more than 20% of the corn processed is deeply processed, with over 2000 varieties, whereas in our country, the amount of deeply processed corn is less than 9% of the total corn production, with only over 100 varieties.

In 2012, the most profitable industry number seven is the high profit of agricultural product processing.

Among all beverages, non-carbonated fruit juice drinks have the highest profit margin. Market retail prices of fruit juice drinks costing just a few yuan have production costs only one-tenth of their retail price, and canned drinks are no different. To put it simply, the cost of some beverages is almost the same as the cost of a perfume bottle. Of course, if we strictly calculate the cost of beverages, logistics costs must also be considered. However, no matter what, the profit margin of beverages is extremely high.

Everyone knows about the excessive profits in pharmaceuticals. In the eyes of ordinary people, the profit margin of pharmaceuticals seems to be the highest. But the facts are otherwise. Compared with the aforementioned industries, the equipment, factory, and R&D costs of pharmaceuticals are much higher. The R&D costs of imported drugs are even higher due to high wages and other reasons.
