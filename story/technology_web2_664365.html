Miu Miu Launches 2012 New Wrinkle Lambskin Bag Series

To celebrate the Chinese Lunar New Year, Miu Miu will release a special collection featuring two styles of lambskin handbags with fold effects and detachable jewelry straps as part of the Capsule line. These handbags will be exclusively available in selected boutiques across the Asia-Pacific region, with each style limited to 80 pieces.

Simultaneously, three pieces of silver metal jewelry adorned with ruby crystals will also be released, including necklaces, earrings, and bracelets. Each piece is a limited edition of 38 and will only be available in selected boutiques within the Asia-Pacific region.

The 2012 Miu Miu wrinkle lambskin bag series embodies a rebellious and uninhibited spirit, staying true to oneself. Founded in 1992, Miu Miu represents avant-garde, innovative, and independent values, beloved by fashion pioneers. The brand name "Miu Miu" is a nickname for Miuccia Prada, reflecting her rebellious personality while maintaining a sweet demeanor, akin to another side of Miuccia Prada: independent, self-assured, and always creative.
