Nostalgia sent to heaven

by lili1 on 2012-03-04 22:46:30

Missing You in Paradise

In the vast sea of humanity, I search desperately for a past that has slipped away. My tears have soaked the path before me, and yet you remain unyielding. How can I blame you? All I ask is for your soul to find peace.

This is written for you, but there is no longer an address in this world where I can send it.

Not long ago, you and I strolled hand in hand through the white poplar forest by the apricot grove. You said your wish was to be a teacher, taking children every afternoon to watch the beautiful sunset. You spoke with such sincerity, your face filled with joy. Looking at your adorable expression, I smiled. You also said that one day you hoped to travel across all of China, exploring its magnificent landscapes.

You said so much more...

But now, your words echo throughout the universe.

"Green mountains never grow old, their heads turn white only with snow; green waters are naturally carefree, their faces ripple only because of the wind." Every time I think about our past together, regret and heartache overwhelm me, and tears quietly stream down my face. Time flies, emotions linger, and though it pains me, I choose to remember.

You left so suddenly, without even a simple goodbye. The numbness prevented me from crying, only leaving behind endless longing. Perhaps you haven't really left, but instead chosen a special way to stay by my side. Your presence comforts my lonely heart.

It’s been two years, and I still feel your careful protection, so warm. Even though fate has placed us on opposite shores, I have never forgotten any of your wonderful qualities.

For the rest of my life...
