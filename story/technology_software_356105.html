The Incident of a 12-Year-Old Girl in Hangzhou Giving Birth to a Boy Baby Has Triggered Deep Reflection!

by wobusuiba on 2010-05-26 21:07:54

Henan Culture Industry Network: On the morning of the 25th, a 12-year-old girl from a school in Hangzhou gave birth to a baby boy in the toilet. Both teachers and family members had noticed that the girl was slightly fatter, but they were unaware that she was pregnant. This "Baby Boy Incident" has exposed defects and loopholes in sex education in our country. Let's hear what the experts have to say.

Middle school students' "intimate contact" with the opposite sex is mainly to get rid of loneliness and oppression. In subway stations, buses, and on the streets, you can occasionally see teenagers wearing school uniforms holding hands, hugging, or even kissing. A sampling survey report published at the International Conference on Cross-Cultural Mental Health and Mental Health showed that most middle school students believe that intimate relationships with the opposite sex are normal. Middle school students stated that important reasons for developing intimate relationships with the opposite sex include getting rid of loneliness and oppression, reducing study pressure, and satisfying curiosity.

In class, students calmly discuss the "Sex Scandal." Last week, during a psychology class at a key high school in Huangpu District, the recent topic was puberty education. "What does the word 'sex' make you think of?" The high school boys and girls first chuckled, then calmly talked about the "Sex Scandal" and the "Tiger Woods Scandal." "The students' knowledge of sex is surprisingly extensive. They do not share this information with their parents; most of this knowledge comes from the internet. However, their sexual morality and sense of responsibility are not yet fully developed, which is worrying," said the psychology teacher who taught the students to the reporter. This survey conducted in Pudong also showed that 68% of high school boys and 46% of high school girls obtain sexual knowledge through the internet.

Some schools and parents avoid discussing social topics related to sex, but students talk about them enthusiastically. Some students are even influenced by some negative factors on the internet and support premarital casual sexual behavior as personal freedom, using these idols as role models to imitate. Not long ago, there was an incident where high school students from a suburban school went out to rent a room. After the teachers found out, they immediately invited experts to give the students a "make-up" lesson on sex education and sexual morality. Statistical data from the "Teenage Girls Unexpected Pregnancy Hotline" last year showed that 15 teenage girls became unexpectedly pregnant due to the failure of emergency contraception.

Three reasons lead to close interactions between male and female students. This survey shows that most students believe that close interactions with the opposite sex are normal, with a significantly higher proportion of high school students compared to junior high school students.

Notably, many students engage in close interactions with the opposite sex due to three main reasons: getting rid of feelings of loneliness and oppression, reducing academic pressure, and satisfying curiosity, accounting for nearly one-third of the total. Xu Lin, a psychology teacher at Yangjing Senior High School and a graduate student in the Psychology Department of East China Normal University, who was responsible for the survey, said that lacking communication partners and seeking stimulating emotional experiences have become the psychological portrait of many middle school students.

During interviews, most students held tolerant attitudes towards their peers' close interactions with the opposite sex, and some even envied others' close relationships. Some students view close interactions with the opposite sex as a way to demonstrate their own abilities, which further encourages some students to display their intimate behaviors openly and without restraint in public.

One-third of male students' parents adopt the "ostrich policy."

Survey leader Shen Xiufang said that a considerable proportion of parents take a laissez-faire attitude towards their children's close interactions with the opposite sex, especially parents of high school boys, nearly one-third of whom adopt the "ostrich policy," always avoiding the topic for fear of corrupting their children. However, with the continuous development of the internet, the sexual information that children come into contact with far exceeds adult imagination, warning "ostrich-style" parents to take on the responsibility of being the first person responsible for sex education.

Teachers find themselves in a dilemma regarding close interactions with the opposite sex. A high school teacher told the reporter that when facing students' close interactions with the opposite sex, they want to intervene but don't know how. Teachers avoid conflicts by adopting a non-interference attitude as long as there are no irregular behaviors within the campus. Even if they do interfere, it is mainly aimed at not affecting studies, leading to narrow and ineffective sex education.

Love education and etiquette education go hand in hand.

The survey results showed that 35% of high school girls and 25% of high school boys hope to receive consultation through a hotline, while 50% of high school girls and 39% of high school boys hope to receive puberty education through youth websites. Psychological experts stated that relying solely on civilian efforts cannot meet their needs. More educational administrative departments, social organizations, and foundations need to actively invest in this public welfare cause to help teenagers smoothly pass through the "stormy period" of life.

In response to the phenomenon of excessive intimacy between middle school students of the opposite sex, schools such as Yangjing Senior High School, Gezhi High School, and Guangming High School began exploring new methods of puberty education. Through lively and interesting open educational forms such as free association, role-playing, and peer assistance, timely guidance and correction are provided to help young people establish positive and healthy sexual concepts and behaviors. Xu Lin suggested that first, the ability to love should be cultivated in middle school students to promote the common growth of both sexes. One of the important tasks of psychological development in middle school students is to learn how to interact with the opposite sex, which is beneficial for future interpersonal interactions. Secondly, strengthen etiquette education and learn the correct ways to express love, making young people understand that physical contact is not the only aspect of interaction between the sexes; more importantly, it involves sincere respect, reverence, trustworthiness, and loyalty in moral interactions.
