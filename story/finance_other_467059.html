I. Franchise Requirements

The operator must possess good personal qualities

Must have certain business management and market operation capabilities

Strong brand awareness and service philosophy

Dedicated to operating the Luqi specialty store wholeheartedly

Possessing good commercial credibility and personal integrity

Adequate start-up capital preparation

Shop area meeting company requirements (minimum 30 square meters, storefront width over 3 meters)
