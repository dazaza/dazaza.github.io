Introduction to the full series of Korean drama "Who Are You"

We yearn for predestined, eternal love... But such love is not easy for me or anyone else. The more you don't want to love, the deeper you fall; the more you want to hate, the deeper and sweeter your feelings become.

All people in the world, as long as they are alive, constantly yearn for and seek a formidable yet beautiful love. Because love is a sweet dream...

A brief encounter with a wealthy young man who knows nothing leads her into a fatal and beautiful love. However, she also suffers immense sorrow and, after six years of waiting, meets that man again. Love is a sweet dream! The only love in fate!

Yoon-jae, who prefers cooking to doing homework, secretly participated in a cooking competition and won the championship. As part of the prize, Yoon-jae went to an Italian culinary school for further study. To get the secret of making pasta, Yoon-jae went to find the owner of the house she once rented, but this owner had already moved. With no money left after failing to find the house owner, Yoon-jae encounters a haughty guy - Han Yi-jun - who was looking for the same person.

Facing such a man, Yoon-jae unconsciously opened her heart to him and fell in love with him. One morning, upon seeing the sudden appearance of Jung Hyun-sung, Yi-jun mistakenly thought he was Yoon-jae's boyfriend and flew back to Korea... A meeting brought about in the name of destiny... But, a love has already begun to sprout.

Introduction to the main characters of the Korean drama "Who Are You"

Cute and lively chef - Cha Yoon-jae / played by Han Chae-young

Background: Eldest daughter of a crane operator mother and a father working in a doll-making factory.

Occupation: Started as a kitchen hand in various third-rate restaurants, now the owner of a small Western restaurant.

Education: Studied cooking in Italy after high school, but dropped out of ICIF Culinary School before finishing.

Personality: 70% like the wind.

Excessively optimistic and blindly affirmative, never discouraged. Sometimes a bit scatterbrained, but because of her purity and cuteness, anyone who sees her can't forget her.

The other 30% is innate madness.

Even though Yoon-jae is a talented chef, due to her lack of vigilance and self-restraint, she always goes too far or is ahead of time.

Cool and elegant heir to the fortune - Han Yi-jun / played by Cho Hyun-jae

Background: Heir to a large group with over 30 branches.

Occupation: Head of the planning department of one branch

Education: MBA from the United States

Personality: Grade A prince with obsessive-compulsive disorder.

Flawless, almost perfect. If you really have to find a flaw, it would be his parents' divorce when he was five.

Since he has always eaten the best and worn the best, he is quite far from the concept of "good enough."

In fact, he is also a very pitiful person. Just before graduating from university, he learned about his biological mother and, despite everything, ran to Italy to find her.

He is also a "pure school" person who can give up his life for love, encountering a woman he doesn't even dare to think about, and his cautious nature surrounds that woman.

Yoon-jae's younger sister - Cha Soo-jae / played by Jeon Hye-bin

As her name suggests, her IQ is 140, completely opposite to Yoon-jae.

She is smart, dislikes strangers, and a bit selfish. Although polite, she is not overly sentimental, and she is very thorough in what she does and how she treats people. She is a new generation of women who invests in her future and enjoys the present. Since childhood, she has liked Hyun-sung, who followed her sister around, and to get Hyun-sung, she tries to bring Yoon-jae and Yi-jun together. She helps Yoon-jae get selected by the Western restaurant and promotes her skills through television, making Yoon-jae a "star."

Yoon-jae's mother - Park Mi-jeong / played by Song Ok-sook

On a Christmas night in her senior year of high school, she had an "accident" with "Church Brother" Cha Sung-taek, thus becoming Yoon-jae's mother at the age of 20.

Yoon-jae's father - Cha Sung-taek / played by Jung Won-jong

He left pregnant Mi-jeong to join the army and held a simple wedding during his leave. He has always felt guilty about this and, to compensate for his wife, he continuously dreamed up so-called "businesses" after retiring from the military, but repeatedly failed.

He also keeps causing trouble between Yoon-jae and Yi-jun.

Yoon-jae's son - Cha Jin-song / played by Lee Byung-joon

An angel wearing a "little devil" mask. Says he's dressing up a little black dog, dyes the puppy blue; says he wants the little chicken to fly, throws it off the roof; says he wants to see what a thief looks like, deliberately leaves the gate unlocked, letting thieves come and go freely. Truly a real "little devil."

Like mother, like son, Jin-song looks exactly like Yoon-jae did when she was little. He is Yoon-jae's toy, friend, and confidant. He just likes his mom, anything with mom is fine. Jin-song always circles around Yoon-jae, so he is jealous of Han Yi-jun and Jung Hyun-sung, kicking and hitting them, and talking badly about them behind their backs.

Han Yi-jun's father - Han Seung-ryeol / played by Jung Woo

A prominent successful entrepreneur domestically. Although married to his beloved junior in college, his wife could not withstand the pressure of being a wealthy housewife and asked for a divorce. The perfectionist President Han naturally did not accept this request. Consequently, his wife attempted suicide, and the reluctant President Han ultimately agreed to the divorce. He is extremely strict with his son because he has very high expectations for Yi-jun.

Han Yi-jun's mother - Hee-jin / played by Jung Ae-ri

A fragrant and warm-hearted woman. Despite marrying her lifelong first love, President Han, she couldn't endure the monotonous marriage life and eventually left the children to go to Italy alone. Although she doesn't outwardly show it, her heart is filled with love for her children. Therefore, when Yi-jun came to find her, she resolutely sent her son back to Korea. In Italy, she married a free-spirited and considerate tour guide and started a new life. For Yi-jun, such a mother is difficult to understand. Hee-jin is also the decisive factor for Yoon-jae and Yi-jun to meet.

Han Yi-jun's sister - Han Yi-jeong / played by Lee Ah-hyun

Dignified, elegant, yet cold and cautious. Not married, she has already taken on the responsibilities of the female head of Daehon Group. Since she always believed her mother abandoned her and her brother for her sake, she completely denies her biological mother. Han Yi-jeong understands her father's character well, so she always helps and reminds Yi-jun to meet her father's requirements. However, when Yi-jun tells his sister that he has fallen in love with an unwed mother, Yi-jeong cannot tolerate it. Yi-jun leaving his family and Daehon Group for such a woman makes his sister Han Yi-jeong feel immense betrayal and guilt. She will resort to any means necessary to separate Yi-jun and Yoon-jae.
