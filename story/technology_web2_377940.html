"Event" NetEase Tech "Wudaokou Salon" Third Session: Mobile Phone Advertising (Beijing, Jan 15)

by ybcz0519 on 2011-01-13 14:25:27

The mobile Internet has become one of the hottest entrepreneurial fields today, and in its business model, it's easy to think of mobile phone advertising. Whether it's Google acquiring AdMob, Apple launching iAd, or start-ups constantly emerging, companies of all sizes are vying for a piece of this yet-to-be-formed "pie," trying to grow it and lead a reliable wave of gold rush in the mobile Internet. Mobile phone advertising holds even more special significance for China. In the case where paid app downloads have not yet matured domestically, the advertising model may be a good way for app developers to make profits. Although it now appears to be just "quenching thirst by looking at plums," the "plums" definitely exist. The theme of the third session of NetEase Tech's "Wudaokou Salon" is "mobile phone advertising," which will be held at the Rainbow Bar in Beijing on January 15th (Saturday). We will invite entrepreneurs from the domestic mobile phone advertising field and mobile application developers to exchange and discuss topics including the current state of mobile phone advertising, how to attract advertisers, the technology and presentation formats of mobile phone advertising, and how to combine applications to expand mobile phone advertising.

About NetEase Tech's "Wudaokou Salon"

As the Internet continues to deeply integrate into people's work and life, more people choose the Internet as the starting point for entrepreneurship and career establishment; when China's Internet users surpass 420 million and when Internet companies create capital myths, the whole world believes that China's Internet industry has the largest and most promising market and business opportunities.

In this gold rush process, replication and innovation have always accompanied each other. Looking at the various enterprises in China's Internet, too many can find prototypes abroad, but they are different, so-called "innovation in replication, replicate while innovating." If we all recognize this way, then from small ideas and small website models, new ideas and new websites that integrate various Chinese elements will be derived, promoting the development of the Internet industry wave after wave.

When these ideas and concepts become the starting point for entrepreneurship, NetEase Tech, as a media and observer, through the "Daily Site" column, collects and organizes new models and creative ideas from both domestic and international Internet industries, aiming to provide new clues and thoughts for Internet entrepreneurs, to generate intellectual collisions, and thereby inspire more creativity, conduct Chinese-style entrepreneurship, and finally give birth to and drive the rise of a batch of emerging websites and Internet companies.

NetEase Tech's "Daily Site" column aims to discover innovative genes on the Internet and share those lively and creative websites (applications). In one and a half years, we have introduced hundreds of fun and creative websites, and now we hope to take this column as the basis, officially launching the "Wudaokou - Daily Site Demo" activity since November 2010.

The "Wudaokou Salon" is jointly hosted by NetEase Tech and the globally renowned venture capital firm KPCB China (Kleiner Perkins), together with well-known domestic independent IT blogs TechNode, Tianya Haige, and 36Kr (formerly TC Chinese Website), aiming to uncover new creative ideas, new models, and new opportunities in the Internet and mobile Internet fields, stimulate innovation awareness in China's Internet and mobile Internet fields, advocate an entrepreneurial spirit, and lead entrepreneurial actions.
