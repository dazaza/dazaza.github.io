A. What if I fail the first exam? How many chances are there for a retake? Is there a fee?

Answer: You will have one free chance to retake the exam. For the second and subsequent retakes, you must pay the regular exam fee.

B. Can the tuition fee be discounted?

Answer: In fact, many students and parents have asked this question, and I can understand everyone's concerns. After all, this is a significant investment, not only in money but also in everyone's time. Making a cautious choice is right. First of all, education is an investment, not consumption! We have many students, many of whom are from middle-level educated groups with low salaries and lack competitive ability in society. Through my training, they have entered the IT industry and become highly paid white-collar workers admired by others, thus completely changing their lifelong development trajectory.

All our Beida Qingniao training centers are managed under standardized management under the unified leadership of the Beida Qingniao headquarters. The headquarters requires that tuition fees be paid in full before receiving books so that student records can be established, which are uniformly built by the headquarters. Therefore, there is no issue of cheaper or more expensive tuition fees. Moreover, Beida Qingniao's training charges are uniform and transparent nationwide. In summary, the tuition fees are uniformly set by Beida Qingniao, and Beida Qingniao strictly manages the tuition fees, prohibiting discounts.

Beida Qingniao courses include network engineer courses, software engineer courses, and software testing courses. Among them, the network engineer course has the lowest tuition fees because it has the shortest academic term and fastest employment opportunities! The return on investment is high (analysis of the characteristics and employment prospects of network engineers).

C. How much is the Beida Qingniao tuition fee?

Answer: To allow more people who are willing to commit to this industry to join the learning ranks, we have divided the entire course into two teaching modules. After successfully completing each module, students will receive the corresponding international network engineer certification. This way, students can use the certificate to find a good job first, then work while studying. This allows for the combination of study and practical work experience, reducing economic burdens. If charged per class hour, the first stage consists of 188 class hours, and the second stage consists of 350 class hours (each class hour includes 55 minutes of instruction and 5 minutes of rest).

D. Why are tuition fees higher in some regions and lower in others?

Answer: Beida Qingniao’s training charges are uniform and transparent nationwide. However, Beida Qingniao divides areas into Class One and Class Two regions. Due to differences in local standards and employment recommendations, the cost per class hour in Class Two regions is 5 yuan cheaper than in Class One regions. If students want better development opportunities, it is still recommended to study in Class One cities. Although the tuition may be slightly higher, the employment opportunities and development environment will absolutely be much better in Class One cities. Because each Beida Qingniao center collaborates with local enterprises, the employment recommendation capabilities of Beida Qingniao centers in different cities vary. In more developed cities, students will have more learning and practice opportunities as well as better employment opportunities! For those studying network engineering, the tuition in Class One cities is uniformly set. Students should pay special attention when choosing, as they must personally inspect the facilities. Network engineering learning requires high-quality equipment, and Xiangji Lide (which owns the most advanced domestic equipment unmatched by other schools) provides its equipment through Guangdong Likang Information Co., Ltd., one of the few professional computer technical service companies with exquisite technical strength and years of professional service experience in South China and even nationwide. It is also one of the best accessory suppliers, mainly representing IBM, HP, SUN, and other minicomputers and accessories. Therefore, the learning machines and experimental equipment at the center are relatively advanced, allowing students to come into contact with mainstream network devices and technologies during their studies. Additionally, Beida Qingniao Xiangji Lide has its own library resources (extracurricular reading materials), which other schools generally do not possess!

6. Are there any discounts or reductions for the tuition at Beida Qingniao Xiangji Lide in Guangzhou? Can the tuition be discounted?

Answer: There are no discounts on the tuition at Beida Qingniao Guangzhou. Education does not offer discounts, and neither does employment. However, students enrolling at our school will receive additional courses as gifts (such as: A. A 5000-yuan value IBM minicomputer course, the only one in the entire southern region; B. CCNA pre-exam tutoring class; C. Soft exam tutoring class; D. Cantonese language learning class). Additionally, some study supplies and daily necessities will be provided (such as hacker manuals and other book materials, USB drives, etc.).

5. Will there be any random charging situations?

Answer: Absolutely not. We strictly adhere to the price initially promised to the students. Each center has been given strict regulations to only collect predefined fees. Any additional charges beyond these regulations can be reported directly to Beida Qingniao headquarters. We welcome supervision from students at all centers. Choosing a career path is more important than finding employment for university students.
