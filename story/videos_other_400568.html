Online shopping

by anonymous on 2011-04-01 13:07:09

Due to the need for listening, I had to buy an MP3/4 player. After weighing the options, I decided to buy a P4. After all, it has more functions and is not limited to just sound; it can also play videos. I originally wanted to buy one when I returned to school during the Mid-Autumn Festival, but I couldn't find one. Later, I heard that there were more available at the electronics market. Perhaps I could buy one there during the National Day holiday, and there might even be promotions offering discounts. But I couldn't wait, nor did I want to drag things out too long. It would be better to act decisively. So, I thought the fastest and most convenient way would be to shop online.

On the 25th, I went to an internet cafe to access Taobao. It was my first time doing this, and I didn't know anything, so I just followed the prompts step by step. After selecting the product and filling in the relevant information, I realized that I hadn't registered as a Taobao member or activated online banking. I tried these two methods, but one was too complicated, and the other had some system issues. In the end, I could only choose to pay in cash. The next day, due to conflicting schedules, I couldn't make the remittance, so I had to wait until the third day. After making the remittance, I logged into Taobao again to complete the payment process and waited for the shipment. Everything went relatively smoothly. Yesterday, the courier finally arrived, but after class, I couldn't find anyone to receive it. I made dozens of phone calls, but no one answered. Frustrating. Today at 6 PM, Shentong Express came again, and I finally got my P4. That marks the end of the entire online shopping experience, and the transaction was successfully completed. I was a little excited!

The one I chose is the Lanmo RM970, with 4GB of memory and a black color. It doesn't have all the functions, but it's sufficient. The device cost me 209 yuan, and the shipping fee was 8 yuan. Regardless of whether it's worth it or not, this was my first online purchase, my first MP4 player, and the first electronic product I've ever bought, and now it's come to an end!
