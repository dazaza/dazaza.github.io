Recently, Sichuan News Network reported that Chen Daibing, a villager from Jin Tan Village in Daxian County, Sichuan Province, made a real-name online report that a coal mine boss broke his father's leg bone. Afterwards, the boss bragged, "We just wanted to show off with some money." What is the truth behind this incident? The reporter conducted an investigation.

On February 19th, a post appeared on the Phoenix Mountain Forum in Dazhou: "Black coal mine severely beats workers until disabled." The poster identified himself as Chen Daibing, a villager from Juren Village in Jin Tan Township, Daxian County, and provided his identity information and contact details. The post stated that on the morning of February 16th, while reporting for work at a coal mine in Tanmugou without any mining permits, Chen’s father, Chen Kaidao, had an argument with the mine manager over wages while the manager was eating porridge. During the argument, the manager threw a bowl on the ground and called several shareholders who dragged Chen Kaidao out of the room and used a stick as thick as a bowl to break his leg before stopping.

The post continued that on the way to the hospital, the coal mine side threatened Chen Daibing who had rushed to the scene: "We just want to show off with a little money, we'll treat your father, it's already a big face for you. You don't know, every time the Coal Administration checks, they notify us in advance. Even if the police station comes, they won't care about you."

After the post was released, it immediately sparked "spectator" interest, with many netizens condemning the coal bosses for "acting recklessly because of their wealth."

Is the content of the post true? The reporter interviewed relevant parties for this matter.

Victim: Severe pain unable to move

According to the phone number left in the post, the reporter contacted the poster, Chen Daibing. He confirmed that the post was indeed his and said that due to threats from the coal bosses, he had now "hidden" away.

At a certain hospital in Dazhou, the reporter found Chen Kaidao who was being treated there. When the reporter arrived at the hospital, two police officers from the Guancun Police Station of Daxian County Public Security Bureau were taking his statement. One of the police officers told the reporter that the police had already filed a case for investigation and would take further action once the results of the forensic examination were available.

Chen Kaidao recounted the incident to the reporter: He had previously worked at the mine but left midway. At the time, the mine had promised him that when the piecework wages increased, he could return. Now that the wages had increased, he decided to come back to work. However, the mine manager said he could not be accommodated. During the argument, he accidentally knocked the manager's bowl, "possibly because he didn't hold it steady, so it fell and broke." The manager then called the shareholder, Gou Maogui, and others who dragged him out and beat him.

"I got hit on my left foot, I fell down and couldn't move," Chen Kaidao said. "Now my whole body hurts, I can't even turn over. The coal boss boasted that he would flatten my entire family, both of my sons are afraid of being beaten, so they have hidden, no one is taking care of me."

Regarding detailed medical records, Chen Kaidao said they had been taken by the coal bosses for appraisal, so the reporter was unable to see them.

Coal Administration: No black coal mines in Daxian County

On the afternoon of the 19th, the reporter called a responsible person at the Daxian County Coal Administration.

"Daxian County does not have any black coal mines. The Tanmugou coal mine belongs to Pingchang County, we only manage safety on behalf of them," the responsible person said.

Regarding the claim in the post that coal bosses claimed "every inspection, the Coal Administration notifies us in advance," the responsible person said, "It's none of my business. We notify those who need to be notified and don't notify those who don't need to be notified."

He further explained: "Some inspections are special inspections, we need to convene meetings beforehand and notify in the form of documents. For surprise inspections, we do not notify."

On the 20th, the Daxian County Coal Administration replied on the Phoenix Mountain Forum: After verification, the mine is located in Jin Tan Township, Daxian County, but its industry management belongs to the Economic and Information Bureau of Pingchang County. According to the document issued by the Economic and Information Commission of Bazhong City (Ba Shi Jing Xin Fa [2011] No. 99), the mine has complete procedures and is a legally constructed mine.

Mine Side: There is a reason, deeply regretful

On the morning of the 20th, the reporter drove to Jin Tan Township, Daxian County. The township party secretary was receiving intravenous treatment in the office due to illness. He called the deputy township head Li Liangfeng, who accompanied the reporter to the coal mine where the incident occurred.

Halfway there, we learned that Gou Maogui and others were visiting Chen Kaidao in the hospital, so the reporter returned to the city for interviews.

Gou Maogui admitted during the interview that there was a reason for the incident: That day was the mine's first working day of the new year. Chen Kaidao wanted to return to work at the mine, but since the mine was undergoing technical renovations and did not need so many people, the mine manager expressed that he could not arrange for Chen Kaidao to work. Unexpectedly, Chen Kaidao grabbed the manager's bowl and threw it on the ground.

"This mine manager is from another place, how dare he argue with local people like Chen Kaidao?" Gou Maogui said, feeling very angry after hearing this. First, he felt sorry for the manager, second, he thought it was unlucky. "Coal mines all respect these old customs, breaking a bowl on the first working day is very unlucky," Gou Maogui said.

Gou Maogui admitted that "in anger, I hit Chen Kaidao." He said that the stick he used was only 5 cm in diameter, absolutely not as thick as a bowl. "I intended to hit his buttocks, but he sidestepped and it hit his foot."

"After the incident happened, I deeply regretted it. Today, I specially went to the hospital to visit Chen Kaidao and apologize to him," Gou Maogui told the reporter: "I have already prepaid more than 10,000 yuan for medical expenses and arranged for nursing personnel. I will take full responsibility."

Gou Maogui firmly denied threatening Chen Kaidao and his son, and also denied saying "the police station and the Coal Administration are all paid by us."

"I am also a teacher by origin, I wouldn't talk nonsense like that. It's hard to do things nowadays. Although I am also a local person, I cannot provoke villagers either," Gou Maogui said.

Police Station: Gou Maogui has been criminally detained

On the evening of the 20th, the Guancun Police Station of Daxian County Public Security Bureau accepted the reporter's interview.

"On the 16th, after our station received the alarm, we immediately responded, sent the injured to the hospital for treatment, and urged the coal mine side to pay the medical expenses. Since the severity of the injuries was unclear at the time, it could only be registered as an administrative case. Currently, Chen Kaidao has been preliminarily identified as having minor injuries by the county public security bureau forensic doctor, so the case has been transferred from an administrative case to a criminal case," said Li Keyou, director of the Guancun Police Station.

It was learned that on the evening of the 20th, Gou Maogui was legally criminally detained, and his son was administratively detained.
