Groupon companies that could possibly make a profit will appear in the second half of the year.

by beetgk2958 on 2012-02-14 14:16:35

"The differentiation of group-buying websites has already begun, and the reshuffling will continue." Shen Boyang believed that it is an indisputable fact that the group-buying industry is facing a "cold winter". As the capital market is gradually closing its doors to group-buying websites, at most only 3 to 5 large domestic comprehensive group-buying websites will survive. However, the impact on small specialized group-buying websites will not be very obvious, and they will still not disappear.

After burning money, the group-buying industry will start reshuffling. "No matter how people criticize group-buying, helping consumers save money will never go out of style," said Shen Boyang. Group-buying is actually a variant of e-commerce. It simply moves local merchants related to food, drink, and entertainment online to sell, creating value for merchants and saving money for consumers, so it meets market demand.

Group-buying websites may become profitable in the second half of the year. It was reported that Nuomi.com's advertising budget last year was 2 billion yuan, and Lashou.com also had a budget of 2 billion yuan. Other group-buying websites with advertising budgets exceeding 1 billion yuan were everywhere. But now, a large number of group-buying website advertisements have disappeared. Some comments believe that after the Spring Festival, the wave of group-buying website closures will further intensify, and at least 70% of group-buying websites will close or transform.

In Shen Boyang's view, 2010 was the budding stage of China's group-buying websites, while in 2011, the group-buying industry experienced rampant growth. Shen Boyang stated that in 2011, many websites used the venture capital funds recklessly, whether it was newspapers, television, networks, office building advertisement screens, or bus stop signs, all were flooded with group-buying website advertisements.

Although group-buying websites are still in a state of full-industry loss without making money, if the market does not become as crazy as last year but instead returns to rationality, then it will be a different scenario. "Running a business must return to the essence of business operations, and the essence of business operations is to make a profit," said Shen Boyang. He believed that 2012 would be the year when the group-buying industry returns to rationality, and by the second half of the year, there might be some profitable group-buying companies, and Nuomi.com could be one of them.

Winter has arrived, can spring be far behind? Shen Boyang believed that the group-buying industry is still developing upward in a healthy form. In the long run, there is no problem with its development and expansion.

Shen Boyang also expressed that "only those group-buying websites with the best physical condition and the strongest basic skills can survive, and the others can only become sacrifices... Those group-buying websites accustomed to spending extravagantly may not be able to tighten their belts and get through this period."
