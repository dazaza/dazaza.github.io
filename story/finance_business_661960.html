Detailed comments during the move

by dx2e5sx5x on 2012-02-09 15:31:47

Detailed comments when moving: A comfortable home environment is not only a guarantee for daily life, but also brings people security, happiness, and good fortune. In life, there are always times when we need to move, such as when upgrading to a new house. From furniture to miscellaneous items, big or small, everything needs to be properly arranged and taken care of. Moving is indeed a labor-intensive task, but if handled carelessly, it may bring some negative effects.

So, what should we pay attention to when moving?

The customs of moving vary slightly depending on the region. When moving, you need to prepare all necessary items. Generally speaking, the following items are indispensable:

1. Rice: Use a rice bucket, filled about eight-tenths full.

2. Red envelope: Place it on top of the rice bucket.

3. A pair of new brooms and dustpans: Bind red ribbons on them.

4. Water: Fill a water bucket about one-third full.

5. Bowls and chopsticks: Preferably in even numbers, placed inside the water bucket.

6. Stove

When moving, these items must first be placed in the kitchen before other things can be moved into the house. If necessary, you can also bring some soil to avoid being uncomfortable with the local environment. Additionally, red spring couplets should be posted on the door of the new house for auspiciousness. Turn on all the lights at home to ensure safety. After moving, you can worship the land god in the evening. According to legend, the land god is short, so the worship should take place at the kitchen entrance, and the table for offerings should not be too high.

1. When moving, items that need to be removed from the new house should preferably be handled by others. Items being moved into the new house should preferably be handled personally. When moving into the new house, no one in the family should enter empty-handed.

2. The time to move into the house should be in the morning, noon, or before sunset, avoiding nighttime moves.

3. After moving, regardless of how long it takes, for the safety and prosperity of the family, a family deity worship ceremony should be held. A small string of firecrackers can be set off at home.

4. If there is no auspicious time for setting up the bed on the moving day, the bed can be preliminarily placed in position and then moved to its final location during the auspicious time.

5. If there is a pregnant woman in the family, it's best not to move. If moving is unavoidable, a brand-new broom should be purchased, and the pregnant woman should sweep all the furniture before moving, to avoid offending the spirit of pregnancy.
