4, Crystal Nail Powder (Nail Powder):

Function: Very fine powder made from acrylic glass fiber. When combined with crystal nail liquid, it can create various nails, which are strong and wear-resistant.

Classification: Crystal nail powder is divided into pink nail powder, white nail powder, transparent powder, and natural color. Additionally, there is colored nail powder. White and pink nail powders can be used to create French crystal nails, while transparent powder can create transparent crystal nails.

Advantages: The nails made with crystal powder are of the highest grade and require the highest technical skill. Moreover, the nails last the longest.

Disadvantages: Due to the high technical requirements, beginner manicurists find it difficult to master.

5, Silk Net (Silk):

Function: A fine silk net attached to the nail plate, making artificial nails more secure, hence called silk nails. Disinfectant and drying adhesive (Nail Primer)

Function: A type of adhesive that must be used when making crystal nails.

Classification: There are acidic and non-acidic types available on the market.

Pros and Cons: It makes the nails adhere more securely, but acidic substances should not come into contact with the skin, and there are certain requirements during use.

6, Crystal Pen (Brush):

Function: Made from arctic marten hair, it has good resilience. Professional crystal pens must be used when making crystal nails, and they are used to dip nail liquid and pick up nail powder.

Classification: Crystal pens range from size 4 to 10, and professional manicurists generally use an 8-sized crystal pen.

7, UV Lamp (UV Lamp):

Function: A type of ultraviolet lamp mainly used for gel nails. After applying a layer of gel-like substance on the nails, the nails will have an excellent glossy appearance after being exposed to the UV lamp.

Pros and Cons: Making gel nails is easy to operate, with a good gloss, but ultraviolet rays may have some impact on the human body.

8, Gold Decorations, Pendants (Charm):

Function: A type of metal ornament specially processed for hanging on the nails.

Classification: Divided into gold and silver colors. Various patterns are available for selection.

Pros and Cons: The ornaments are corrosion-resistant, do not change color, and can be reused multiple times. They look very pretty and cute when decorated on the nails, but they may snag stockings if not careful.

9, Gold Powder Set (Nail Paint Kit):

Function: A set of gold powder used for painting. Each set contains 4-8 bottles, and inside each bottle is a small brush similar to a drawing pen.

Classification: Divided into gold powder sets and oil paint sets.

Pros and Cons: The brushes are very fine and can directly draw patterns on the nails.

10, Nail Tips: Used for making overlay crystal nails. Nail tips, also known as flexible nail bodies, are made from ABS material.

Classification: Divided into shallow overlay, half overlay, and full overlay. Colors include white and natural colors. Natural color nail tips are used for American overlays.

Crystal nails are used for white overlays for French nails.

Pros and Cons: Using overlays to make nails is relatively convenient and time-saving, but the retention time is not as long as crystal nails.

11, Decals:

Function: Decals are paper stickers applied to finished crystal nails. They have thousands of patterns to choose from and combine according to customer needs, allowing quick creation of various patterns on the nails.

Classification: Divided into water decals and non-water decals.

Pros and Cons: They save time for customers and assist manicurists who are not strong in art skills. However, these decorations can also be done at home and lack the artistic feel.

12, Top Coat:

Function: The final coat applied after finishing the crystal nails, preventing the nails from turning yellow. It has an excellent glossy finish.

Classification: Divided into UV top coat and regular top coat.

Pros and Cons: UV top coat has a light purple glow and prevents yellowing; regular top coat, if applied over crystal nails, may cause the nails to turn yellow.
