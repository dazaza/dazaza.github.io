The sensual play in the presidential suite of the uninhibited female president

by mylovehisa on 2008-07-10 15:38:27

The entire sexy process of playing with the Taipei free-spirited female president's suite (Samsung Soul U908). It seems that Samsung really values this phone, as they even held the event in a presidential suite. However, judging by the price, it is clear how important this phone is. Its features are very distinct; once you hold it, you'll know there's a variable "tactile button" below the screen (Samsung calls it Magic Touch). We've tested this phone before, so those interested can take a look.

This is the environment of the presidential suite. Thank her for her hard work; she was the one explaining and introducing the phone during this press conference. I also recorded a short segment where she explained a bit about Samsung’s Magic Touch.

The most important function of Magic Touch is not just that the patterns can change, but when you press it, there's a vibration feedback. This phone isn't exactly small, but personally, I feel its features are quite appealing to women. So after she finished her explanation, I asked her a question: what does she think about this phone?

She said: "At first glance, it seemed a bit big, more suitable for business people. But after playing with it for a while, I actually found it quite attractive, especially with its 5-megapixel digital camera that has face detection functionality."

For phones, having a 5-megapixel camera that can autofocus is indeed a major selling point. However, speaking of which, this touch interface is quite appealing to women. I'm not sure if businesspeople would like such a touch interface, but if Samsung were to release a similar touch interface with a more compact design, I believe it might be even more popular.

The manufacturer specifically asked me to capture a scene by the piano. Were they hinting at the strong music capabilities of this phone? XD
