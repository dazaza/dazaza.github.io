■ Prosecution Reminder

"Based on traffic accident responsibility determination, turning vehicles should yield to pedestrians, and when ruled by traffic police, the driver would be determined as the responsible party," said the prosecutor. Drivers often choose to settle quietly, noticing that Sun does not demand excessive compensation, thus agreeing to negotiate a settlement. The prosecutor handling this case reminded that if drivers encounter such infringements in the future, or suspect being intentionally collided with, they must retain evidence of compensation and the other party’s phone number, and promptly report the case to the police station. Sun is 65 years old. According to his confession, since 2001, he has been using the method of deliberately causing traffic accidents to defraud drivers of money, and has been living off this means. Last year, due to his own physical limitations, he hired his neighbor Li as a driver to drive him out to carry out these intentional collisions.

Mostly colliding during vehicle turns

Report (by reporter Liu Yang, correspondent Feng Ying): Yesterday, I learned from Dongcheng prosecutors that the old man Sun, who has been living off intentional collisions for many years, and his "driver" Li have been approved for arrest on suspicion of fraud. The prosecution introduced that currently, it is preliminarily determined that there are 39 victimized drivers, with a case amount close to 40,000 yuan.

According to the introduction, the process of victims encountering intentional collisions was almost identical. Drivers usually hear a sound similar to a collision from behind the car when turning, get out of the car, and then discover Sun sitting on the ground with his bicycle lying next to him. Then Sun insists that the driver hit him and demands medical expenses.

Arrested together with the "driver"

If suspected of an intentional collision, the driver needs to retain evidence.

In February this year, the Dongcheng Procuratorate Supervision Department accepted the case of Sun and Li suspected of fraud. The prosecutor handling the case introduced that according to existing investigation evidence, it can be confirmed that from 2004 to 2010, Sun successively carried out intentional collisions at crossroads in Dongcheng District locations such as Wangfujing, Beiluoguxiang, Yonghe Palace Bridge, Xiaojie Bridge, Anwai Avenue, Dongdan, Dongsi North Street, Beixinqiao, as well as in Chongwen, Chaoyang, and Haidian districts, defrauding 39 people including driver Zhang of nearly 40,000 yuan. The highest amount defrauded was over 5000 yuan, and the lowest was two or three hundred yuan.
