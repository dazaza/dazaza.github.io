　　Incision method for double eyelids: The advantage is cutting the upper eyelid skin to make the double eyelids anatomically similar to natural ones. The formed double eyelids will not disappear, and excess muscle layer and fat can be removed, as well as some skin for those with loose skin and more wrinkles to reduce the looseness of the upper eyelid and wrinkles. The disadvantage is obvious postoperative swelling and early fine scar left from the incision. This method is suitable for any recipient.

　　Suture ligation method for double eyelids: The advantages are short operation time, no need for incision, no worry about suture reaction after removing the sutures, longer maintenance time after surgery, and good long-term effects. The disadvantage is relatively obvious postoperative swelling. The indications for this surgery are the same as the suture method.
