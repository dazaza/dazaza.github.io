CHINA NEWS, Changsha, January 17 — Title: A 90-Year-Old Grandmother in Hunan's Anren Raises an Abandoned Girl by Collecting Recyclables

Author: He Bingwen

Xu Fengying, aged 92, lives in Chengguan Town, Chengdong Community of Anren County, Hunan Province. He Sisi, a 13-year-old junior high school student, is the "daughter" that Grandma Xu picked up and raised.

Thirteen years of ups and downs, thirteen years of bitterness and sweetness, thirteen years of nurturing grace—facing the grandmother with her weathered face full of wrinkles, more maternal than any mother, He Sisi said with deep emotion: "It was Grandma who gave me a second life, infinite warmth, and happiness."

On the eve of the Spring Festival, I visited Grandma Xu. Though her hearing has deteriorated significantly, she recounted to us the touching story of how she found an abandoned infant and raised her into adulthood.

On April 25th, thirteen years ago, just as dawn broke, then 79-year-old Xu Fengying went to collect garbage and recyclables outside the county's central hospital. Beneath a tree, she spotted a cardboard box, assuming it to be trash, she bent down to pick it up. To her surprise, inside was an abandoned baby, its umbilical cord still uncut, its face already turning bluish-purple.

Grandma Xu quickly undid her buttons, wrapped the baby in her clothes, and brought it home. She asked a neighbor for some white sugar and fed the baby with warm water. The baby began to cry loudly, hearing the cries, Grandma Xu said with relief, "There's hope! There's hope!"

From that day forward, Grandma Xu's life became inseparably tied to the fate of this abandoned child. Living in hardship, Grandma Xu relied on collecting garbage and recyclables from the streets to make ends meet. Unable to afford milk, she would boil rice porridge and feed it spoonful by spoonful to the baby, getting up again at midnight to feed her once more. After the baby turned one month old, sometimes Grandma Xu would carry He Sisi while collecting recyclables, other times she would lock He Sisi safely at home, rushing back after a quick trip around the neighborhood.

With the help of family members and kind-hearted people, little Sisi grew up day by day. During her primary school years at Chengguan Central Elementary School, both the school and caring individuals from society offered her maximum support.

At the age of seven, the understanding He Sisi began picking up garbage alongside her grandma. While studying at school, she would pay attention to leftover mineral water bottles, plastic bags, and other recyclable items, bringing them home for her grandma to sell.

Every day, the few cents or yuan earned from collecting recyclables were carefully saved by the elderly Xu Fengying. She skipped breakfast every morning, saving the money to buy buns for little Sisi, leaving herself with a severe stomach ailment.

Sisi mentioned that all the clothes Grandma wore, whether on her feet or body, were washed clean from collected recyclables. Once, the clever He Sisi told her grandma that she wanted to buy a new piece of clothing. However, she deliberately bought a larger size. When they returned home, she told her grandma that it didn't fit her, asking her grandma to wear it instead. This way, Grandma Xu unexpectedly got to wear a new set of clothes.

Not until the winter of 2008 did Grandma Xu entrust her personally raised "daughter" to her forty-something grandson for foster care. (End)
