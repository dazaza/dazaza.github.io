Washing or Reaching the Peak: How will the Major Players Compete in the Future Market＿Stock Forum

by xl0958870 on 2009-12-05 02:37:57

I. Information Affecting the Behavior of Market Players and Interpretation

1. The National Development and Reform Commission (NDRC) once again strictly controls the approval of overcapacity industries such as steel, cement, and flat glass;

2. The State Council approves the development plan for the efficient ecological economy zone of the Yellow River Delta;

3. Fund "one-to-many" dedicated account business enters another intensive issuance period.

Interpretation: The upcoming Central Economic Work Conference at the weekend is currently receiving significant market attention, with one of the key topics being structural adjustment. Recently, the NDRC's rectification of overcapacity industries, preparation to support emerging industry policies, and the State Council's consecutive approval of regional revitalization plans are actually practical actions in the aspect of structural adjustment. This will lay a foundation for China's long-term sustainable and healthy economic development, which is a major long-term positive factor. Moreover, after issuing a large number of new funds recently, the "one-to-many" fund business has again entered an intensive issuance period. This will further replenish market liquidity, providing conditions for future market upswings.

II. Analysis of Market Player Behavior

1. Funds: Resonance sentiment index 55, main target sectors: consumption, machinery, finance, power

Short-term behavior: Balance between bulls and bears, playing against retail investors;

Medium to long-term behavior: Defensive strategy.

According to the position estimation data from DeSheng Fund Research Institution on November 26th, stock fund positions began to diverge last week. Although the overall position was roughly the same as the previous week, the position ratio of heavily positioned funds decreased, while lightly positioned funds increased their positions.

This week’s dragon-tiger list data showed that institutional seats bought more. Institutional seats are generally dominated by funds, suggesting that with the fading negative effects of the Dubai incident and the emergence of domestic favorable news, funds have started to increase their holdings again. These increasing funds should mainly be newly established funds. Since the fourth quarter, a large number of new funds have been issued, and the "one-to-many" fund business has also entered an intensive issuance period. In a situation where the economy and trends are improving, these newly entering funds have a very high possibility of accelerating their position building.

On the other hand, as we approach the end of the year, the ranking competition among funds seems about to intensify. Under the fund battle scenario, the market is likely to experience significant fluctuations due to mutual suppression and individual lifting among them. Particularly, those stocks heavily held by funds deserve investor attention. These heavy-weighted stocks contribute significantly to their performance. If there are any favorable news stimuli, these stocks are very likely to be lifted by funds to improve overall performance.

2. Insurance, Social Security: Resonance sentiment index 50, main target sectors: real estate, chemicals, low-carbon

Short-term behavior: Swing trading;

Medium to long-term behavior: Long-term layout, stock forum.

By October, the total assets of China Social Security Fund reached RMB 700 billion, with an investment return rate of 12.98%, where 32% of assets were used for stock investment. Previously, the upper limit for social security fund stock investment was 20%, but due to the transfer of state-owned shares, the proportion of social security fund stock investment has greatly increased, enhancing its influence in the stock market. Therefore, every move of the social security fund deserves investor attention.

According to the latest disclosed data from the Shenzhen Stock Exchange, the social security fund intensified its increase in real estate in October. In October, the social security fund's buying ratio was 17.82%, while the selling ratio was 4.63%, resulting in a buy-sell difference of over 10 percentage points. The social security fund is a typical value investor; they purchased a large amount of real estate when most believed it was overvalued. There must be something many ordinary investors haven't seen, perhaps inflation expectations, or RMB appreciation, or other factors beneficial to the real estate sector.

Contrary to the behavior of the social security fund, insurance funds reduced their positions significantly in October. Since the beginning of this year, insurance funds have been relatively cautious, adopting a "small steps, cash out" strategy throughout the year. This is one reason for the frequent buying and selling activities of insurance funds this year. Therefore, the behavior of insurance funds mainly affects the short-term market, while their fluctuation operation strategy only increases market volatility in the long term without significantly impacting trend direction.

III. Investment Opportunities and Risks

Summary of behavioral direction: Today's news is neutral. The stock market opened with oscillatory downward movement, accelerated retracement in the afternoon, but rebounded during the last hour, basically recovering the decline gap. In the end, the Shanghai Index fell slightly by 5.12 points for the day. From the K-line chart, today seemed to form the so-called "hanging man line." Technically, the appearance of a "hanging man line" usually suggests the peak of the market, but from the market surface, it appears more like a washout scenario. These two situations are completely different and worth paying close attention to. We at Wanon Growth Investment Advisory Group believe the probability of a washout is higher, which was also our judgment in yesterday's column. The reasons are: first, the current level does not allow the main players to exit fully; they either go long-term bullish or quickly push the market up. Second, bearish news has just been digested, and the long-term benefits of structural adjustment, economic recovery, and inflation won't be digested so quickly. These benefits still have much potential to be utilized by the main funds.

Short-term (within 1 month) opportunities and risks: Short-term market resonance sentiment index is 60. Wanon Stock Hotspot Tracking System indicates short-term hotspot sectors: seed industry, energy conservation and environmental protection, low-carbon economy.

Medium-term (1~3 months) opportunities and risks: Medium-term market resonance sentiment index shows 60. Wanon Stock Hotspot Tracking System indicates medium-term hotspot sectors: finance, machinery, agriculture, low-carbon economy, and stocks expected to undergo asset injection and reorganization.

Master stock introduction knowledge, stock knowledge, and stock forums before studying. The process of buying and selling stocks is as follows: When buying or selling stocks, you must specify whether to buy or sell, the name (or code), quantity, and price of the stock. And this order is only valid on the day it is placed. The content of the order includes the abbreviation (code) of the stock you want to trade, the quantity, and the price of buying or selling the stock. The abbreviation of a stock usually consists of three to four Chinese characters, and the stock code is six digits. The code and abbreviation of the stock must be consistent when placing orders. At the same time, there are certain rules regarding the quantity of stocks traded: namely, the quantity of stocks ordered to buy must be a whole multiple of 100, but the quantity ordered to sell does not have to be a whole multiple of 100.

There are four ways to place orders: counter submission orders, automatic telephone orders, automatic computer orders, and remote terminal orders.
