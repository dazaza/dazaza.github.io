Wu Dong Qian Kun Chapter 126: Battle against Wei Tong

by fanyete on 2011-09-15 18:38:01

"What a sharp-tongued little bastard, I will knock your teeth out one by one and cut off your tongue while I'm at it!"

Wei Tong's forehead veins were jumping with anger at Lin Dong's calm words. No matter what, he was still a man of some importance in this Flame City, yet today he was publicly humiliated by Lin Dong in front of so many people. This hatred was enough to make the narrow-hearted Wei Tong want to tear Lin Dong into pieces immediately.

"Enough talk, let's settle this with our skills." Lin Dong sneered coldly.

"Bang!"

Wei Tong glared at Lin Dong with sinister eyes, then suddenly took a step forward. The powerful spiritual energy immediately erupted from his body, causing his robes to flutter violently. A terrifying oppressive aura, filled with killing intent, spread out.

To be able to carve out a position of power in Flame City was enough to show that Wei Tong was no fool. Over the years, he had been through countless life-and-death battles, and the abilities honed in blood and fire had protected him until now. This opponent was indeed not comparable to those Lin Dong had encountered before.

Wei Tong's combat experience was several times richer than his own, and his methods were ruthless. A single misstep could lead to failure, which was not an impossible outcome.

Therefore, Lin Dong's expression quickly became serious.

Although he was quite angry at the start due to Lin Dong's provocation, once entering the combat state, Wei Tong's rage quickly subsided, replaced by a cold and cruel demeanor, like a ferocious wolf about to pounce on its prey.

As for Wei Tong's transformation, Lin Dong also slightly narrowed his eyes, realizing that the former's ability to become the leader of Blood Robe Sect was indeed based on some formidable qualities.

This guy was a strong rival.

"Boom!"

Just as this thought flashed through Lin Dong's mind, Wei Tong directly attacked at this moment. His figure moved like lightning, appearing in front of Lin Dong in an instant, right fist tightly clenched, then without any frills, he threw a punch straight towards Lin Dong's chest.

Wei Tong's punch looked simple and unremarkable, but under the wrapping of fierce pure spirit energy, it possessed even more destructive power than a sharp blade!
