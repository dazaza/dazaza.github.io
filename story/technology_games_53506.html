First Login and Empire Creation

After completing registration and logging into the game for the first time, the system will prompt you to create a character. First, you need to input your empire’s name, then select your civilization, enter the verification code, and click "create character." After seeing the system's confirmation of successful creation, click "login" and choose a server to begin your empire's journey.

Land Exploration

Task Content: Upon entering the game, how do you start? First, under [Military Management], find [Explore Land] and dispatch your explorer.

Completion Conditions: Total flat land exceeds 2600 units.

Completion Reward: 2 diamonds.

Note: Rewards for exceeding 2600 flat lands will be issued next month.

Hunting Ground Construction

Task Content: With land secured, you can build various structures to meet your empire's needs. Start by building hunting grounds to provide food for your empire. Under production and construction, select to build structures.

Completion Conditions: Total hunting grounds exceed 150.

Completion Reward: 100,000 gold.

Note: Rewards after completion of building numbers will be issued next month.

Lumberyard and Iron Mine Construction

Task Content: Continue exploring your land and construct lumberyards and iron mines.

Completion Conditions: Lumberyards and iron mines both exceed 50 each.

Completion Reward: 3000 wood, 3000 iron.

Note: Rewards after completion of building numbers will be issued next month.

Accelerate Building Construction

Task Content: The time spent constructing buildings is related to the number of your construction workers. You can build tool factories to increase the number of workers and tools, thereby speeding up construction times.

Completion Conditions: Tool factories exceed 100.

Completion Reward: 1 diamond, 3000 wood, 3000 iron.

Note: Rewards after completion of building numbers will be issued next month.

Adjust Living Standards

Task Content: The speed of your population growth is related to the living standards within your empire. You can adjust living standards to control population growth or decline.

Completion Conditions: Set living standards to high.

Completion Reward: 300,000 gold.

Note: Rewards after adjustment completion will be issued next month.

Increase Your Explorers

Task Content: The number of explorers you can send out at one time is related to the size of your city center. Increasing the city center can allow more explorers, enabling exploration of more land.

Completion Conditions: City centers exceed 20.

Completion Reward: 1 diamond, 1,000,000 gold.

Note: Rewards after building completion will be issued next month.

Mind Your Warehouse

Task Content: All resource materials are stored in warehouses. If materials exceed warehouse capacity, they may be stolen. Always ensure your warehouse has sufficient space.

Completion Conditions: Warehouses exceed 50.

Completion Reward: 500,000 gold.

Note: Rewards after building completion will be issued the following month.

Increase Technology Research Points

Task Content: For an empire to grow strong, technology is the primary productive force. You can build magic towers to increase research points and enhance your research level.

Completion Conditions: Combined research levels exceed 10 points.

Completion Reward: 1,000,000 gold.

Wall Construction

Task Content: Walls can help defend against external enemies. Adjust the percentage of wall-building workers under wall construction in production and construction. They will automatically build walls for you monthly.

Completion Conditions: Owned wall units exceed 20 units.

Completion Reward: 2,000,000 gold.

Weapon Production

Task Content: Now that you understand production, you can produce weapons. First, build some weapon factories, then arrange the quantity of swords to be produced under weapon production.

Completion Conditions: Monthly sword production exceeds 10.

Completion Reward: 2,000,000 gold.

Build Your Army

Task Content: You can train your surplus farmers into an army. Go to military management's army training section to establish your army.

Completion Conditions: Total army exceeds 500.

Completion Reward: 1 diamond, 2,000,000 gold.

Local Trade

Task Content: In the game, you can buy and sell resources locally. Set your automatic trade to automatically purchase 100 grain.

Completion Conditions: Automatic grain purchase setting exceeds 100.

Completion Reward: 3,000,000 gold.

Treasure Hunt Exploration

Task Content: With an army, recruit a hero from military management. Let the hero lead your troops to fight monsters on the map.

Completion Conditions: Have one exploration message.

Completion Reward: 1 diamond, 2,000,000 gold.

Note: During exploration, you can check the exploration status in the exploration information.

First Use of Alliance (Joining and Creating)

Under the alliance diplomacy page in empire management, you can join or create an alliance. Joining requires selecting the alliance name and knowing the password. Creating requires inputting the alliance name and password, and it will cost 60 diamonds.
