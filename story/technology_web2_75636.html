Douban [Brief Review]: The most well-known community website in China, focusing on discovering and sharing favorite films, music, books, and blog posts. [Features]: Mainly includes four sections: Books, Movies, Music, and Nine O'Clock (i.e., blog articles). Users can independently submit, comment, and support their favorite content. For the first three categories, they are divided into "currently enjoying," "previously enjoyed," and "want to enjoy" based on timeliness. Users can submit descriptive information autonomously, from which Douban selects the best description as the standard one (which will also be updated continuously). Users can comment, add to favorites, include in Doulists (equivalent to personal recommendation lists), and even buy or sell through price and version information (so when submitting information, complete copyright information is required). For the Nine O'Clock section, it consists of articles pushed from user-submitted blog RSS feeds. Douban divides these articles into two to six channels based on their content. Users can comment, recommend (Digg), save, include in groups, Doulists, etc. Through "My Douban," users can easily manage personal information (including Doulists, comments, discussions, blog display plugins, etc.). Independent personal homepages facilitate interaction and exchange among users. Additionally, Douban's group and neighbor sections are also very distinctive. [Shortcomings]: Numerous functions and sections make it challenging for beginners to get started. [Affiliate]: RSS subscription type.

Redit [Brief Review]: A well-known free platform for information release and interactive communication, slogan - Share the joy of life. [Features]: Users can post new discoveries, classified information, and store product information. Through the personal center, users can manage posted and saved information. Additionally, there is a community section for interactive exchanges. [Shortcomings]: The cities available for selection in the classified and store information sections are too limited, not accommodating users from small and medium-sized cities; the method of submitting information is relatively single.

MySpace.cn (You Ni. You Wo) [Brief Review]: An internationally famous social networking service website (Chinese version), slogan - You Ni, You Wo; a trusted social platform by 200 million people globally. [Features]: Provides interpersonal interaction, customizable friend networks, personal profile pages, schedule arrangements, schools attended, blogs, circles, photo and music and film sharing and storage, along with an internal search engine and email system. Personal display pages and blog pages have proprietary short domains (skin changes allowed); provides a dedicated instant messaging tool MySpaceIM (downloadable on this page). [Shortcomings]: Further localization work for China is necessary.

Di Pan [Brief Review]: A professional, feature-rich, distinct personal online living space community, slogan - My Di Pan, my personality, my friends, my life. Supports publishing diaries, articles (collections), photos, music, videos, online large headshots taking, video recording, etc. Offers professional friendship and contact list services, URL saving, saving, commenting, leaving messages, etc. The multimedia upload and editing functions are quite powerful, supporting album MTV function (i.e., animated albums). Provides custom domain personal homepage (with rich templates allowing customization modifications, supports music playback) and album homepage, video homepage (both support custom settings), additionally allows setting personalized QQ number addresses. Contact lists and URL collections do not support external imports, personal homepage does not support module customization.

Personal World [Brief Review]: A domestic personal online living space community, claiming to be - China's first personal service portal. [Features]: Composed of three major sections: reading and communication, interpersonal interaction, and life services. The reading and communication area consists of information (news) submitted by users (well-organized classification), allowing posting of articles (reprints, originals), pictures, videos, etc. Also supports blogging, RSS subscriptions, personal albums, event initiation, music boxes, etc. Provides a proprietary domain personal homepage, the interpersonal interaction area offers strong friendship interaction functions. [Shortcomings]: Many functions in the life service area are currently under testing and temporarily unavailable; personal homepage does not provide skin change and other customization functions.

Ximanyi Personal Space [Brief Review]: A personal online living space community under Ximanyi, slogan - With benefits, more fun. [Features]: Supports article publication, personal albums, Google AdSense setup, etc. Provides a short domain personal homepage (allows custom appearance, ad display, etc., supports friendly links, data statistics, etc.), offers friendship functions, allows applying and binding top-level .cn domains, provides independent personal email (format: username@xicn.net, size: 5M). [Shortcomings]: The personal homepage customization function is not powerful enough, failing to display personal characteristics.
