Firef.ly: Build a virtual instant messaging platform on your own blog page.

by spmobile on 2008-05-15 08:36:23

Firef.ly is a highly creative blog widget, as well as a single-user social interaction platform.

Firef.ly is a new type of blogging communication platform (Widget?) that is currently still in the Alpha testing phase. In fact, I can't really define Firef.ly (Firefly?) very well, but the creative inspiration brought by Firefly has really caught my attention: just a piece of JS code can turn your personal blog into a single-user social interaction platform! Users don't need to install any plugins to activate Firef.ly on your blog page. Then, Firefly can transform your blog page into a Flash-based virtual interface, allowing convenient real-time text communication between the blog author and readers within this virtual interface!
