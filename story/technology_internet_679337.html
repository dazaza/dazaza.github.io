Use Baidu Wenku for SEO optimization

by lele081105 on 2012-02-27 23:21:47

Baidu treats its products like parents treat their children, with meticulous care. "Baidu it once, and you'll know," — the top link is always to Baidu's own products. Due to Baidu's emphasis on its own products, webmasters have no choice but to carefully study Baidu’s products. To do Baidu SEO optimization well, one must first focus on optimizing Baidu’s products. Baidu has many products: Baidu Zhidao (Baidu Knows), Baidu Baike (Baidu Encyclopedia), Baidu Tieba (Baidu Forums), Baidu Wenku (Baidu Library), and more. These are all very important products of Baidu with high weight.

Baidu Zhidao, Tieba, and Blogs are important SEO tools commonly used by webmasters. They are among Baidu’s most important products with very high weight. For example, Baidu Zhidao (http://zhidao.baidu.com) has a PR8 and a Baidu weight of 9. Baidu Space (Blog) (http://hi.baidu.com) has a PR8 and a Baidu weight of 8. Baidu Tieba (http://tieba.baidu.com) has a PR9 and a Baidu weight of 8. All these are important products of Baidu. They have large user bases, high usage rates, and high traffic, which is why they also have very high PR values. Naturally, a large number of webmasters perform SEO optimization on these products.

What about Baidu Wenku's (Baidu Library) position among Baidu's products? Take a look at Baidu’s homepage (http://www.baidu.com). If you are a regular browsing user, you won't see a link to Baidu Wenku on the homepage. News, Web pages, Tieba, Zhidao, MP3, Pictures, Videos, Maps — these are the links that browsing users see on Baidu’s homepage. If you are a logged-in user, there is still no link to Wenku on the homepage. Instead, there are only additional personal information navigation page links and space navigation page links in the upper right corner, along with reminders about new activities from friends below the search input box. There is no link to Wenku anywhere on the entire page. This shows that Wenku is not one of Baidu's core products. So, what is Wenku’s weight? According to seo.chinaz.com, Wenku has a PR9 and a Baidu weight of 7, with 237,000 pages indexed in the last week. Baidu’s traffic is 8.3 million. Due to copyright restrictions, content submitted to Wenku requires a long system and manual review process, making updates much slower compared to Tieba, Zhidao, and Spaces. The fact that so many documents can be indexed daily indicates that a lot of netizens are submitting documents to Wenku. Are any of these documents using Wenku for SEO? I believe the answer is yes.

To understand the effectiveness of SEO on Wenku, SEO software provider 1751SEO verified the SEO effect of Baidu Wenku through their own experience (http://blog.tianya.cn/blogger/post_show.asp?BlogID=4115292&PostID=38859970).

Upload a document. 1751SEO uploaded a document titled "SEO Optimization Common Commands for Search Engines." It takes a long time for Baidu Wenku to review documents.
