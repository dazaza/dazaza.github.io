Customers in the Shenzhen area can pick up goods at our door, but we follow the principle of delivery after payment. Payment can be made in cash or by transfer, or we can deliver the goods and then collect the payment. For customers from other regions, we usually handle consignment or express delivery (payment must be made in advance before consignment is arranged. If insurance is required, please inform us beforehand so that we can arrange it when processing the consignment. If there are no problems with the goods during customs clearance, the order is basically complete... We look forward to our next collaboration!
