Mobile QQ 2007 official version was released on January 11, 2008.

by yinyin on 2008-01-11 16:19:20

The official version of Mobile QQ 2007 fully integrates the hot news from Mobile Tencent Network, the wonderful content of Mobile QQ Space, and the most popular mobile comprehensive community. The improved interaction mode and the newly added information extraction function allow mobile and Internet information to be interconnected at all times, enabling smooth sharing of online life.

The official version of Mobile QQ 2007 now supports the following models:

Nokia: 5300/5310XM/5610XM/6126/6131/6133/6233/6234/6263/6265/6265i/6267/6270/6280/6282/6288/6300/6301/6500c/6500s/7370/7373/7390/7500 Prism/7900 Prism/8600 Luna/8800 Arte

Sony Ericsson: K770/K790c/K800i/K810i/K818c/W580/W830c/W850i/W888c/Z750/K858c/S500c/T658c/W908c/W910i/K850i/S500i/W880i/T650i

Motorola: ROKR E2/RAZR2 V8

The list of supported models will continue to grow, with certain differences based on phone performance.
