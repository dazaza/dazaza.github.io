Baidu Online Sues Baidu Barbecue for Trademark Infringement, Claiming Millions in Compensation

by anonymous on 2013-10-02 10:38:16

Baidu Company

Baidu BBQ

The trademark infringement case of Baidu BBQ was heard in the Shenzhen Intermediate People's Court, where Baidu BBQ insisted that its registered trademark was legal.

South China Morning Post reporter Liu Xiaozhan reported that Baidu BBQ has been sued by Baidu Online with a significant compensation claim amounting to tens of millions of yuan. Yesterday, the Shenzhen Intermediate People's Court heard a trademark infringement case where Baidu BBQ claimed that its Baidu trademark was also legally registered, just under a different category.

Baidu Online: Baidu BBQ misleads the public

What is the relationship between the well-known "Baidu BBQ" familiar to Shenzhen residents and the famous IT company Baidu Online? This question, which once aroused curiosity among many people, eventually prompted Baidu Online to take action. Baidu Online Network Technology (Beijing) Co., Ltd. (hereinafter referred to as "Baidu Online") claimed: In recent years, Baidu Online has discovered that there are varying numbers of barbecue restaurants named "Baidu" in several cities across the country. Investigations revealed that these barbecue restaurants belong to Shenzhen YiBaidu Catering Management Co., Ltd. (hereinafter referred to as "YiBaidu"), and they are its franchise stores. Both YiBaidu and its franchisees use business names containing the words "Baidu," and on their signs, menus, and interior decorations of their establishments, they also use the text logo "Baidu BBQ," with some prominently using the text logo "Baidu."

The spark that ignited this lawsuit was the association people made between the two entities. Baidu Online stated that through other search engines, it can be seen that a large number of internet users have misunderstandings and controversies regarding whether Baidu BBQ is related to Baidu. Many internet users who expressed opinions mistakenly believed that Baidu BBQ was opened or authorized by Baidu, and have already consumed at Baidu BBQ restaurants.

Baidu Online believes that Shenzhen YiBaidu's actions have misled the public and infringed upon its registered trademark rights. Therefore, it has filed a lawsuit against Shenzhen YiBaidu Co., Ltd., its legal representative Sun Luming, Shenzhen Mingjia Baidu BBQ Store, and the store investor Song Yuxia, demanding that the four defendants immediately cease trademark infringement, issue a statement in the media to eliminate the influence, and jointly compensate Baidu Online for economic losses amounting to 11.0424 million yuan, expenses incurred to stop the infringement amounting to 40,000 yuan, and order the four defendants to bear litigation costs.

Baidu BBQ: Not in the same category, thus no infringement

In yesterday's court session, the lawyers representing YiBaidu and its legal representative Sun Luming cried foul. The Baidu BBQ side claimed that Sun Luming invented paper-based barbecue and applied for a patent right. Based on the owned patent, he established Shenzhen YiBaidu Company, which has been approved by the National Trademark Office for registration and is therefore legal. Even if Baidu Online suspects an infringement exists, it should apply to the National Trademark Office rather than file a lawsuit in court.

The lawyer for Baidu BBQ argued that the Baidu trademark registered by Shenzhen YiBaidu Company falls under Class 43, which belongs to the catering industry trademark, differing from the Class 42 Baidu trademark registered by Baidu Online Company. Therefore, it does not constitute trademark infringement. Moreover, Baidu BBQ had spent 5,000 yuan on promotion with Baidu Online five or six years ago. At that time, Baidu Online was already aware of the existence of Baidu BBQ but did not promptly raise objections to the trademark rights, and now the statute of limitations has passed.

Song Yuxia stated that she is merely a franchisee, and even if there is an infringement, it is a problem between Shenzhen YiBaidu Company and Baidu Online Company, and she should not bear the relevant infringement compensation responsibilities.

[Focus] Claiming tens of millions, Baidu BBQ thinks it's too high

Baidu Online explained its understanding of claiming tens of millions: According to the publicity on the website of Shenzhen YiBaidu Company and media reports, from 2005 to present, Sun Luming has established Shenzhen YiBaidu Company as headquarters, setting up four branches in Shenyang, Sichuan, Guizhou, and Yunnan, developing hundreds of direct-operated or franchised stores such as Baidu BBQ stores in Futian District, Shenzhen. Its illegal profits come not only from the direct sales of food but also mainly from franchise fees. If calculated according to the publicly disclosed data on the YiBaidu website, with 568 franchised stores charged the lowest "one-star store" franchise fee of 158,000 yuan and royalty fee of 10,000 yuan, the total income reaches as high as 95.424 million yuan, in addition to profits from direct-operated stores and franchised stores.

In response, the lawyer for Baidu BBQ believed that YiBaidu charges only 38,000 yuan per store for franchise fees, and the turnover is not as high, making Baidu Online's claim of tens of millions too excessive.
