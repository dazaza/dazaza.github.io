2009 Bordeaux Chateau Parker Scores

 

In the wine industry, there is a strange phenomenon. Bordeaux is globally recognized as the most famous, best quality, and most expensive wine region, but the quality of its wines is evaluated by an outsider who significantly influences the prices in this area. This is none other than the famous American wine critic Robert Parker (Robert Parker), who has recently released his scores for the 2009 Bordeaux wines!
