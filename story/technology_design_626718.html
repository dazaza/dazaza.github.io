by lqqwfy on 2011-12-16 09:23:49

More or less, everyone knows that sitting in front of a computer for long periods is actually like being exposed to a hidden poison. Yet, countless heroes and beauties continue to succumb to its allure—spending hours at their desks, completely ignoring the consequences of backaches, leg cramps, kidney deficiency, and yin deficiency (Hmm!). Calcium supplement ads will tell you: just take more calcium, and everything will be fine; ten bottles a day will make you stronger. But designer Shih-Chan Chiu has something else to say: "Forget calcium! Clearly, the problem is lack of exercise. What we need is my YoYo Mouse."

This is a playful concept because it uses more power than usual, so it frequently forces you to give your YoYo Mouse a spin to recharge it. In this simple hand motion, you unknowingly rejuvenate your hands and shoulders.

---

The YoYo Mouse is designed as a fun way to encourage people to stay active while working on computers. By forcing users to periodically engage in small physical activities (like spinning the mouse), it promotes both the device's functionality and better health habits.
