Lovers' Knot: Su Youpeng and Ju Wenpei make a joint appearance at the College Student Imaging Festival - China Film Network

by letaoehv on 2011-05-27 11:11:21

"Love Knot"

Su Youpeng and Ju Wenpei, a perfect match admired by all

The 2009-2010 International College Student Film Festival concluded successfully on the 11th. This year's festival invited numerous prestigious universities from home and abroad. Su Youpeng and Ju Wenpei were invited to attend together and jointly presented the award for the "Most Anticipated Film by International College Students" to Feng Xiaogang's yet-to-be-released new film "Aftershock."

Second Collaboration with Unspoken Understanding

Su Youpeng and Ju Wenpei had just finished working together in the movie "New Kangding Love Song." In the film, Su Youpeng plays Li Sujie who meets Ju Wenpei's character, Dawa, during wartime. They vow to stay together for life but are tragically separated for 60 years, depicting a grand and pure love story against the backdrop of war. Through their collaboration in this film, they brought their characters' chemistry into real life, showing even more默契 (chemistry/synchrony) when they worked together again. They also encouraged new forces in the film industry, boosting the morale of Chinese filmmakers on their path to internationalization.

Ju Wenpei, dressed in an elegant white long dress, appeared like a sacred goddess, exuding noble grace. Beside her, Su Youpeng looked exceptionally handsome and dashing, leaving the audience present in awe. Their joint appearance drew waves of cheers from the audience on-site.
