The Cheapest Place for LV Globally ---- The UK

With the sharp fall of the pound exchange rate, the UK has become the cheapest place globally to buy LV products. LV has counters in five cities in the UK (London, Edinburgh, Birmingham, Manchester, and Leeds). London offers the most choices for buying LV and often gives access to the latest LV products. As we all know, LV never discounts, so seize the opportunity of the plummeting pound to buy the cheapest LV bags globally!

1. LV Counter at Selfridges Department Store, London

This is one of the favorite malls for Asians. Goods are relatively concentrated here, and it has a more trendy feel. After purchasing your desired LV item here, you can conveniently visit the Selfridges food hall to enjoy some delicious cuisine.

Address: Selfridges London, 400 Oxford Street, London W1A 1AB

2. LV Boutique on New Bond Street, London

This LV boutique looks impressive from the outside. There are relatively fewer customers here compared to Selfridges, allowing for a more relaxed shopping experience. One thing to note is that this store is closed on Sundays.

Address: 160 New Bond Street, W1S 2UE, London

3. LV Boutique in London's Financial City

The service quality of this LV boutique is said to be the best. The staff will provide you with sincere advice based on your needs. Note that this store only sells leather bags.

Address: 6 Royal Exchange Buildings, EC3V 3NL, London

4. LV Counter at Harrods Department Store, London

Harrods is universally recognized as the highest-class mall in the UK. LV has two counters here. The counter on the ground floor sells leather bags, watches, and jewelry, while the counter on the first floor is the lifestyle section, mainly selling women's clothing, shoes, and sunglasses. You can even find LV bathrobes here!

Address: 87 Brompton Road, Knightsbridge, SW1X 7XL, London

5. LV Boutique on Sloane Street, London

LV is not just about handbags. If you're also interested in LV sunglasses, coats, watches, or even jewelry, the selection at the Sloane Street LV boutique is the broadest.

Address: 190/192 Sloane Street, SW1X 9QX, London

Louis Vuitton (路易·威登) is one of the most outstanding leather goods designers in French history. He opened his first luggage shop under his own name in Paris in 1854. A century later, Louis Vuitton became one of the top brands in the field of luggage and leather goods, symbolizing high society. Nowadays, the Louis Vuitton brand is not limited to designing and selling high-end leather goods and luggage but has become a giant trendsetter spanning fashion, accessories, leather shoes, luggage, jewelry, watches, media, fine wines, and more.
