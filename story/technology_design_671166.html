Whitening should be done early, and the earliest spring is the most natural whitening trick.

by wswen156 on 2012-02-21 16:42:37

The cold winter has gradually passed, and the spring of all things finally came. As the saying goes, a good beginning is half done, if you want to have whitened skin, of course the whitening homework should start from early spring. The editor collected the annual whitening plan presented by whitening experts, and took this beautiful early spring to present to all the beauty-loving women so that everyone can easily whiten from head to toe. First of all, if you want to give your skin the most sufficient whitening care, of course, the first choice is the whitening mask. Here we will teach you an effective and money-saving homemade whitening mask. The components are: 5 grams of each of Tremella, Huangshi, Angelica dahurica, Poria, and Polygonatum odoratum, then grind them into fine powder, mix with 5 grams of flour, and adjust with water. Carefully apply it on the skin, wash it off after 30 minutes. After use, you will find that the skin becomes moist and white, and becomes healthier in the light Chinese herbal fragrance. Whitening experts remind that after using the mask, you must quickly supplement the whitening products. Professional whitening products such as Xinsiyuan are highly recommended to let the skin retain its whiteness and beauty. Secondly, if you want your skin to be bright and translucent from the inside out, food whitening is essential. Super whitening drinks such as honey lemon tea and yogurt papaya juice are both delicious and whitening. Experts also recommend eating more whitening foods such as peas, potatoes, and radishes, which not only whiten the skin but also remove wrinkles and detoxify. Daily skincare habits are also important for whitening. When using whitening products, such as Xinsiyuan whitening, it is advocated to use the palm of the hand to apply and massage. After carefully pressing, rub your hands hot and cover the whole face, which allows the skin to absorb the whitening essence more effectively and comprehensively, doubling the skincare effect. Whitening tips, making it hard for you not to be white. There are some whitening tips, such as adding about 10 drops of white vinegar to the washing water when washing your face, which can whiten and moisturize the skin and inhibit sebum secretion. You can soak two vitamin C tablets (the cheapest kind) in water as a spray, and the skin will become tender and white. Drink more water, eat fresh vegetables and fruits, and get enough sleep. A healthy lifestyle can make your skin look fairer and healthier.
