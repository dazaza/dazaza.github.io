But three hours later

by kfjro877 on 2011-05-30 11:46:47

On a business trip in Guangzhou, I still feel that it is the height of summer. However, three hours later, when the plane descends at Beijing Capital Airport, consumers are given a kind of association. Without knowing it, one can feel the quiet approach of autumn. Beijing's autumn is the shortest season of the year but also the most lingering and unforgettable time.

Recently, I have been on consecutive business trips. The French plane trees in Shanghai's lanes, the hibiscus flowers in Nanning, and the palm garden at Guangzhou's Oriental Hotel have left memories of this summer in my heart. Although I am always in a hurry, I can still feel the different rhythms of life and living in each city I visit. Every time, I hope I can stay longer, not to the extent of exaggerating every signal of nature, but at least to experience more of life itself, love oneself, and cherish life.

As September arrives and autumn begins, if you observe carefully, you will find that our bodies also start to undergo some changes. This is the time when we must avoid being negligent with our bodies; otherwise, ignoring some details may cause great discomfort for our health. Throughout the summer, I have been constantly traveling like a "flying person," from Beijing to Tokyo, from Tokyo to New York, and recently flying back and forth across the country. Despite all this travel, my skin has remained in good condition, which even surprises me. Recently, while attending events, I was asked by many media outlets about my experiences with beauty, fitness, and makeup. It was only when sharing these experiences that I realized there were many little tricks involved.

The most important thing is the cleanliness of the body and skin. Many friends use skincare products but neglect the cleanliness of their skin, allowing waste to accumulate and preventing nutrients from being absorbed. Skin that has been made up must be cleaned first with卸妆油 (makeup remover oil). If necessary, it should be washed several times. Never be lazy and wash your face carelessly. No matter how late you work, never forget to clean your skin. Only then can your skin get a good night's rest, making you look refreshed the next day. Additionally, cleaning the body also includes clearing out internal waste. Unhealthy food, unhealthy emotions, and other negative factors are all forms of waste inside the body. Personally, I feel that the best way to eliminate internal waste (including negative emotions) is to maintain regular exercise. A six-session makeup course will be taught every weekend in the future. Sweating and stretching the body can help expel bad energy and bring fresh energy.

When autumn arrives, another important task is to keep the skin hydrated. Especially in cities like Beijing where the air humidity is very low, replenishing water becomes particularly important. Even though I am constantly on business trips, I always carry a hydrating mask with me and supplement moisture for my face whenever needed. Sometimes, when I am too tired, I do two or three masks a day. This benefits the makeup artist in applying makeup easily and makes the makeup appear fresh and natural.

When we possess healthy bodies and skin, we already own beauty. Decoration serves as the finishing touch. On this point, I agree with many makeup artists' views. In the past, we held ourselves to the same standard for clothing and makeup, using external embellishments to cover up our flaws. However, as we deepen our understanding of nature and self-awareness, and as we embrace the diversity of social culture, more and more friends have begun to realize that maintaining natural characteristics and highlighting individual features is more important, whether in makeup or dressing. Highlighting one feature makes it easier for people to remember. Whether in makeup or clothing coordination, my suggestion is to find one or a few of your greatest strengths and fully showcase them. Find a small highlight every day, giving yourself and others some small surprises!
