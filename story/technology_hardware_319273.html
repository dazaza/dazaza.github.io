Dongtai's watery countryside sees ten thousand villagers busily attending the fair (please relay to the provincial station)

by cgccg001 on 2009-12-22 22:45:04

At present, it is the annual big market of Dongtai Lixia River water town. In recent days, people from all over have rushed into Hougang town. And the integration of modern elements has made this traditional grand gathering more vivid, lively and full of new meaning. With tens of thousands of people and more than a thousand stalls, the ten-mile long street has become crowded. Seeing this spectacular scene of a sea of people rubbing shoulders and heels, the little animals also come to add some excitement. However, they obviously can't be the main characters. The little white rabbit in the cage looks bewildered, as if scared by the scene, while the parrot beside it remains unmoved, whispering contentedly with great general demeanor.

Clothing, food, shelter, transportation, pots, bowls, pans, basins, sandblasting machines, are of course indispensable. The villagers didn't forget their real business because of the temptation of dazzling goods. They paid more attention to agricultural machinery and tools. Picking a good knife or choosing a good scale would undoubtedly bring more convenience to future farm work. In the tug-of-war of bargaining, the steel wire baskets displayed by an old man didn't need much trouble and were very popular.

同期 Customer: [The baskets sold by this old man are of high quality, pretty good, CNC machine tools, can be used for more than ten years, large sales volume, not expensive, durable.]

Shopping obviously consumes a lot of energy. Drinking a cup of beverage, eating some fruits, and having various snacks naturally becomes the best choice. After being full and drinking enough, buying a few beautiful elastic balloons, riding a carousel horse, enjoying a piece of popular music, and participating in on-site interaction is really enjoyable. In this bustling and noisy atmosphere, a pleasant Hulusi melody floats by, allowing people to experience the wonderful realm of "building a cottage in the human world, but without the noise of carriages and horses."
