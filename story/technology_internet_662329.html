"I'm really not afraid."

by baichazu3264 on 2012-02-10 09:42:19

Zhang introduced that every time when he sneaked into the victim's house for stealing, he often stayed there, just like being in his own home. He smoked, went to the toilet and even flipped through photos. He said he was not afraid of being caught. "I am not really afraid. If I meet the house owner, either we settle it privately or end up in a lose-lose situation." However, sometimes he failed. Once when he had just climbed to the fourth floor via the balcony, a woman shouted loudly. Scared, he fell directly from the fourth floor. Luckily, he only got slightly injured. According to Deputy Team Leader Zhang of Yuxi Criminal Investigation Team, Zhang is from Ningjin county. He is just 18 years old this year. He was once arrested for theft but was mainly criticized and educated because he was still a minor. In July last year, he returned to Shijiazhuang from another place and continued to steal. He usually sneaked into houses by climbing through windows at midnight. He had been to houses from the first floor to the fourth floor. After stealing money, he left. When he could not find cash, he looked for valuable items and left a note for blackmail. Through investigation, the police confirmed six times of Zhang's break-in thefts, but Zhang confessed more than 30 cases. At about 19:00 on April 21st, Ms Wang returned from a business trip and found the curtains she drew before leaving were open and the closed windows were also open. After checking, she found that her mobile phone, camera, jade jewelry at home were all gone, and even the property certificate, household registration book, pension insurance, etc. were missing. But one thing appeared - a piece of "Night Note". There was even a dagger under the note. Ms Wang reported the case to the Yuxi Criminal Investigation Team. The policemen contacted the suspect Zhang and arranged the meeting time and place. At about 21:00 pm, Zhang showed up and was captured immediately. The stolen goods worth more than 100,000 yuan were confiscated on the spot. Subsequently, multiple bank cards and ID cards were found in Zhang's residence. The first paragraph of the note wrote "Alas, full of indignation", followed by the list of stolen items. And the beginning of the second paragraph wrote: "When I came to your home, I walked away with tears in my eyes. It was a good home, but there was no money." Then he proposed that if the owner wanted to get the things back, they could meet and redeem the things. Finally, he even left his telephone number, claiming that he was surnamed Zhang and was 18 years old. According to the report (by Liu Tao, a reporter of the Yan Zhao Metropolis Daily and intern Guo Xin), "It was a good home, but there was no money." At about 19:00 on April 21st, Ms Wang found that she was stolen after returning home, and there was a paper "Night Note" left by the thief. The list of stolen items was listed on it, along with the signature and telephone number. At about 21:00 on April 21st, the Yuxi Criminal Investigation Team of the Qiaoxi Branch of the Shijiazhuang Public Security Bureau arrested the intruder near the Minxin River on Zhongnan Street. Currently, the case is still under further processing. The thief mocked the owner in the note. During the entire interview, Zhang was always clear-minded and spoke slowly. Only when he mentioned his growth experience and family situation would he frown. "When I was two years old, my mother left because our family was poor. Afterwards, I lived with my grandparents. My father was a construction worker, busy with work, had no time to take care of me and had no money. I did not have enough money and did not want to be inferior to others, so I began to steal things when I was six or seven years old. At first, my father beat me, but later he ignored me. My grandparents died when I was eight years old, then I went back to live with my father. I stopped schooling after graduating from primary school and then wandered around. I have been to many places, all of which were stealing, and there was no way to go straight. I returned to Shijiazhuang in July last year." On the afternoon of April 22nd, the reporter met the suspect Zhang. Zhang was always calm, as if sitting on the sofa at home chatting with people. When talking about his theft methods, he pointed to his forehead from time to time and said, "Here, I thought of it myself, no one taught me." "At first, I just wanted to get some money, but as I stole more times, sometimes I found there was not much cash in people's homes, so I stole some things. Sometimes it was not easy to sell the stolen goods and it was also troublesome, so I thought of leaving notes and asking for some money. I did not ask for much each time, just a few hundred yuan. In fact, this was pretty good. The loss of the other party was also relatively small, and it was very suitable for both sides. Leaving notes was also my own idea. I did it like this before, and I left notes three or four times in total. The first time I left a note was in Guilin. The victim settled it privately with me. She even said that she wanted to help me and persuaded me not to steal anymore. Later, I called her sister."
