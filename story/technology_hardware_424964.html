660 Points = 55 Times of 12 Points

According to statistics, the total accumulated fines have reached 72,800 yuan, with a total of 660 points deducted. According to regulations, if one accumulates 12 points in a year, their driver's license will be revoked and they must retake the exam. This single vehicle has accumulated 55 times the deduction of 12 points.

Vehicle湘D 82821 has 381 unresolved illegal records, accumulating fines of 57,100 yuan;湘D 82824, 211 violations, 31,400 yuan;湘D 82822, 201 violations, 26,700 yuan;湘D 82823, 189 violations, 24,100 yuan.
