iWedding Planner – the perfect wedding

by anonymous on 2011-06-01 22:41:24

The culmination of a journey of two people who love each other is such an intense and significant event that it should not be left to chance. There are many preparations and equally as many details to consider for a wedding that is, according to your taste, unique and perfect. Whether you desire a formal ceremony or something less demanding doesn't matter; what counts is that it reflects your dreams.

iWedding Planner gathers a series of information and rules for the flawless wedding but also provides practical help with sections dedicated to themes like the spending checklist and the guest list.

You'll have everything at your fingertips directly on your device, no matter where you are!

In detail:

**Checklist** – This is a real budget planning tool: you enter the initial budget, any potential financial contributions, and fill out, item by item (from the ceremony and all it entails, to favors, to the honeymoon, etc.), every field related to deposits, balances, delivery dates, etc. The fields are editable.

**Guest List** – This is the list, divided between His and Hers, of the invited guests. For each person, you insert their name, the total number of family members, and the address. The fields are editable.

Each section is carefully curated and delves into the subject with historical references and meanings, traditions and folklore, or precise guidelines on good taste and what etiquette demands. Additionally, there’s information on florigraphy, original ideas, formal and informal management of the wedding registry, what to gift for each anniversary, how to handle invitations and RSVPs, and much, much more.

iWedding Planner is available for iPhone, iPod Touch, and iPad.

Congratulations and prosperity to you, newlyweds!
