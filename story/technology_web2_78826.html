Ranking of the Most Practical Online Service Websites

The software we use in our daily lives is increasingly integrating internet functions. In the past, we liked to process data with various local software first and then transmit it through the Internet. However, this model has long been outdated on today's Internet. A new generation of faster and more convenient web services is changing our habits.

Many times, we can directly handle various data on the Web. The most classic and common example is Gmail-like online post offices, which seem faster and more stable than some traditional email client software. If you haven't yet boarded the express train of internet applications, take a quick look at these selected next-generation web service sites we have chosen for you. They provide all kinds of functions from instant messaging to office spreadsheets, and some of these sites' small plug-ins also have very thoughtful uses, ensuring that you become an expert in new-era internet online applications within minutes.

NO.1 Caakee Online: An Online Accounting Platform

http://www.caakee.com

Caakee Online is a completely free web-based online financial management software. It has powerful online accounting and financial management functions, a rich reporting and statistics system, a unique multi-account function, and a simple and convenient custom reminder function.

What help can Caakee Online bring to you?

1. Help you manage personal finances: By consistently recording your expenses on the Caakee Online platform, you can clearly understand how and where your money is spent.

2. Help you manage family finances: By recording family members' expenses, income, assets, stocks, funds, etc., on the Caakee Online platform, you can manage family finances well, truly save costs, reasonably plan family life, and achieve financial goals.

3. Help you manage the finances of online stores, shops, or small businesses: How much money you earn each day, how many goods you purchase, how many products you sell, how much you spend on shipping, etc., can all be recorded one by one on the Caakee Online platform, allowing you to understand the store's business status anytime and anywhere. There is also a sub-account function that allows you to set up sub-accounts for your branch stores or employees, enabling remote bookkeeping and timely understanding of the store's business situation.

What are the characteristics and advantages of Caakee Online?

1. Online bookkeeping anytime, anywhere without installation: As long as you can access the internet, you can do online bookkeeping anytime, anywhere, without downloading or installing anything, making it simple and convenient to use.

2. Data security: Data is stored on the server and will not be lost even if the system crashes. MD5 encryption technology is used, and data is backed up twice daily, once in the morning and once in the evening.

3. Support for data export: Data can be exported in Excel table format to any computer.

4. Automatic bookkeeping: Monthly fixed expenditures, such as rent, will be automatically recorded by the computer for you.

5. Custom reminders: You can freely set shopping plans and over-budget plans.

6. Statistical analysis: Powerful reporting and statistical functions can summarize annual income and expenditure and daily income and expenditure. You can clearly see where your money was spent.

NO.2 AskForm: A Professional Interactive Questionnaire Survey Platform!

http://www.askform.cn/

Facing the market data needed by the company, you always feel at a loss; occasionally wanting to conduct a small survey, you don’t know where to start; the survey homework assigned by the teacher drives you crazy. No one is perfect when it comes to grasping the market, "A gentleman is not different by nature, but he is good at using things," so if you're not good at it, why not rely on something else?

The original intention of AskForm is a tool, existing for users to rely on. Although the interface of AskForm may not be fancy, there is practical service beneath its simplicity; although the functions of AskForm may not be powerful, the innovative widget mode makes surveys less boring; AskForm does not have a community, allowing you to avoid wasting time on chatting, exchanging, or other irrelevant matters.

AskForm is simple. It only wants to provide functions to users, whether it is creating questionnaires, publishing questionnaires, or the more novel external widget surveys, and after the survey, the data statistics, every step of AskForm aims to make users use it in the simplest and most intuitive way, serving easily and working easily.

NO.3 KoolIM: In the testing phase, supports IM message pop-up windows.

http://www.koolim.com/

KoolIM opens a new browser window for each instant messaging software and each conversation you log into. When someone sends you a message, it will pop up a dialogue window like regular IM software. However, KoolIM is still in the testing phase, lacking basic features such as chat history, cannot display the time when receiving messages, and cannot assign avatars to friends. Its security is about the same as Meebo.

NO.4 Hexun Bookmark: Rich features, support for grading, personalized attribute settings, etc.

http://bookmark.hexun.com/

Similarly, Hexun Bookmark requires you to register and install its plugin. This way, a button named "Add to My Hexun Bookmark" will automatically appear in the right-click menu of your IE or IE-based Maxthon browser. To use it, just click the right mouse button on the webpage you want to bookmark and select "Add to My Hexun Bookmark" in the pop-up menu. All collected pages are saved on the website server, so you don't have to worry about losing bookmarks due to reinstalling the system or other hardware/software failures. You can access the pages and sites bookmarked in the Hexun Bookmark from any computer connected to the Internet, and you can also share your bookmarks with friends.

You can also right-click on hyperlinks on the page to perform the bookmarking operation. At this point, what is being bookmarked is the linked webpage. After clicking the bookmark, you can set options such as tags, summaries, and comments in the pop-up window, which can make future searches more convenient. Tags are keywords added during the collection process for the saved webpages, and they carry social attributes, making it easier to share with others.

No.5 Google Docs: Clean and simple interface, good at translation, excellent collaboration!

http://docs.google.com/

Google’s document and spreadsheet interface is very simple, with a blue and white design, and only one row of buttons in the text window. Its strength lies in automatic translation (you can compare translations in Wi-Ki style) and it can easily save HTML source code. You can conveniently upload documents via email, and even if you and your colleagues use different operating systems, you can both open and edit the same document, and any changes made by one party will be immediately visible to the other.

Although it has common document processing functions, it lacks built-in text search tools and find/replace functions. When you open multiple documents, they can only be displayed separately in multiple windows and cannot be shown as multiple tabs on a single page. Interestingly, the spell-checking functions of Google Docs, Spreadsheets, and Zoho cannot correctly identify the name of their parent company.

NO.6 Thinkfree: Word-like functionality, similar to traditional desktop programs, making it more familiar!

http://www.thinkfree.com/

Thinkfree is a Java-based document editor that looks very much like a traditional multi-functional desktop application, making you feel very familiar. This editor has almost all the common functions of Microsoft Word, such as headers, footers, automatic correction, etc. But because it must run on a large Java program, it takes about 30 seconds to start. Unlike Google Docs and Zoho Writer, Thinkfree does not provide the ability to open multiple documents or real-time collaborative editing.

No.7 Zoho Writer: Rich features, good compatibility, tight integration!

http://writer.zoho.com/jsp/home.jsp

It provides a hideable document list that can be categorized as private, shared, and public, and also provides templates. Like Google Docs and Thinkfree, Zoho Writer can directly publish files to podcasts and can save and export text documents in various formats, such as Microsoft Word documents and HTML format. Moreover, it can be used as a plugin to integrate well with Microsoft Word, allowing you to directly save content from Word to your Zoho account.

It provides most commonly used document processing functions under various text formats, from multiple undo to find/replace, almost everything you need. You can also edit HTML documents, though this feature is hidden, but you can search for it in its search bar. Zoho can handle documents in various formats through Tab key switching in a single window. You can also insert tables generated by Zoho into your documents, and when you modify the content in the text, the tables will also be updated automatically. Zoho also offers a free version that can run on a personal small server, but the number of users cannot exceed 10 people. It can greatly reduce server load and provide stricter protection for sensitive business files.

NO.8 365Key: Simple to use, embedded in the browser.

http://www.365key.com/

To use 365Key - Every Day Bookmarks Online Collection, you need to register for free first, then download a plugin. This is a registry file written in JavaScript code. After importing it into the registry, a "Submit 365Key" bookmark button will appear on the browser link bar. Clicking this button will save the page you are browsing to your own web archive. Before clicking save, you can choose whether to add your commentary and classification for the webpage.

NO.9 Meebo: Beautiful interface, rich features, supports multiple IM software simultaneously.

http://wwwl.meebo.com/index-zh-CN.html

The best web-based instant messaging service, you can use it to log in to AIM, Google GTalk, ICQ, Jabber, MSN, and Yahoo Messenger simultaneously. Anyone who registers can store login information and passwords, and the page will automatically log you into all your instant messaging software next time.

Meebo's interface is very beautiful, allowing you to run multiple instant messaging software in a single browser window, and you can chat with contacts from multiple instant messaging software in a single popup window. In single-window mode, the contact list is attached to the right side of the browser. You can adjust the window size or minimize it in the browser. Meebo supports changing default font formats, saving chat records, sending emoticons, and more.

Next comes security, unfortunately, Meebo hasn't put in much effort in this area. You certainly wouldn't want to share your password with a third party, would you? Since Meebo does not encrypt communication content, it's best not to discuss sensitive private topics when using wireless internet. As long as you don't discuss the company's top secrets, it's a good choice for running multiple instant messaging software.
