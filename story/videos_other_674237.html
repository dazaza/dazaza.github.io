Large Character Board Repair:

1. Unable to score: This board cannot add points when turned on, though the image is normal with functions like account checking 1 and 5. Pressing 3 has no effect. Try replacing, check the ground wire for scoring, and discover a diode 1N4148 has broken down. Replace with a good one, turn on and score 100 points, then shut down and replace the original two pieces. Start again, and it doesn't work. Try each piece individually and find a nearby 6821 is faulty. Replace with a good one, and everything is OK.

2. Deduction without recording: This board deducts points without recording. First, check if the fifth foot of the switch is not properly connected, and if the deduction is 10 points per time. Generally, welding the fifth foot switch should be OK.

3. Fixed machine board: Turns on with an image, but flashes a few times and then locks up. Measuring at the 74SL139 location, the signal is found to be incorrect. Replace with a new 74SL139, and everything is normal after being turned on for a long time.

4. Garbled board: This board turns on with overlapping images. Checking the image section, at the 6116 location, it is found that the 1st, 2nd, 3rd, and 4th feet have no signal. These go to the 4th, 7th, 9th, and 12th feet of the 74SL157. Replace with a new 74SL157, and everything is normal upon turning on.

Dragon Tiger Struggle Fault: Turns on with an image but no background.

Repair: Insert the lower right corner 62256 which heats up slightly. Replace it, and it’s OK. Note: Best to use 62256-15 or above. Otherwise, there may be garbled spots. Earth brand is the best.

Big Character Poker No Image:

(1) This board is new and used for a few hours before losing the image, only having sounds for adding points, betting points, deducting points, and revealing cards. Replace with a 6845, and everything is OK.

(2) This board has sounds for adding points and revealing cards but no image. Measure and check the image section, finding a 74LS174 logic error. Replace with a new one, and everything is OK upon turning on.

(3) This board has sounds for adding points and deducting points but no image. Try replacing 74LS174 and 74LS245, but the fault persists. Then replace with 74LS273, and everything is normal.
