Risking everyone's opinion: What's Twitter for? !

by cacard on 2007-07-03 20:29:01

Today I saw two of my must-read bloggers, Mr.6, Keso, both talking about Pownce, the Twitter "killer" created by Digg.com founder Kevin Rose. Keso is obviously a consistent skeptic :), and Mr.6 has reorganized and figured out 3 reasons why every company should hire a hacker.

Just when everyone is in full swing to COPY Twitter (Jaiku, Tumblr, Frazr, "fan Fu", "Ji Wei de", "Lai Gu la"... Even Kevin can't get away with it), let me be a historical sinner and say a few honest words: What is the use of Twitter? ! Anyway, I'm no longer a web 2.0 man and I'm not afraid to offend anyone.

What is Twitter?

The first time I saw Twitter in the middle of last year, it was very clean interface, very clear functions, a word was Cool, but it was useless. Played for a while and forgot.

Then a few months later, Twitter took off! Took it out again and thought about it, and felt that it was indeed a very delicate toy... But it's a toy. It's not much fun. It's still useless.

In February, I visited Silicon Valley and talked with several people about what good things have recently, I talked about Twitter recently, but the few people living in Silicon Valley, and eating Google free dinner guys actually do not know what Twitter is, took out a look at the demonstration, some people do not know how to use it? "Toys, useless!" I will answer honestly.

Later, more people around Twitter, I also occasionally check, but has not changed my opinion, twitter is a useless toy.

Twitter for Toys?

Perhaps it is a toy that shows that it at least has the value of toys, there are many things to make even toys are not, and not fun, and no only more useless! Well, I used to make a bunch of these useless things that weren't even toys, just rotting in places everyone knows. Useless things are garbage, not toys. Toys must be beautiful, healthy, novel, fun, not everyone can do it.

Perhaps the Twittwer lies in the value of this toy, and this toy has a social effect, and it is interesting to play with more people. Now my Twitter is also very hot, batch after batch of updates... But I really did not understand the value and need of his long-term existence. If anyone knows, I'd like to know.

Silly imitation

With all due respect to Twitter, it's a waste of time and energy. Certainly not for those who are unhappy with Twitter's Ruby on rails architecture and spend a weekend writing clones to amuse themselves. It's boring to see Twitter going viral and jump in.

Earlier this year, someone in Idea Factory China asked, "Why no Chinese people copy twitter?" Now the Chinese version is a hundred flowers blooming, ten thousand horses mute.

The future of Twitter

Predict that Twitter may not be popular in the future, and most of the imitations will disappear and die out. Toy popularity is regular and cyclical, of course, does not rule out such as Babie girl's enduring popularity.

The above is purely personal prejudice, but also psychological words, maybe there are others who see it, but see him so fire is embarrassed to say it, or I will take the world's opinion.
