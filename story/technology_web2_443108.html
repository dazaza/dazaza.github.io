The Real Reason Huang Jianxiang Was Fired by CCTV

Photo/Text by Xiao Xiaopang

This kind of person, CCTV will never use again.

mm A certain leader of the sports department of CCTV

Starting today, I am a "middle-aged unemployed" person.

Don't guess blindly.

To those friends who call, I thank you. Please understand my call forwarding.

mm Translated from Huang Jianxiang's blog

Amidst the media and friends' praise for his choice of "freedom", Huang Jianxiang left with a shabby bag on his back. Two former colleagues from the sports department who used to comment on pig football together leaned against the door, chewing gum, squinting their eyes as they watched him walk away. A small yellow flower quietly bloomed at the corner of their mouths in the sunset.

One of the colleagues said: "It was clearly a firing, yet they insisted on saying it was an approved voluntary resignation, what a good way to embellish oneself!"

The other colleague replied: "Forget it, forget it, our station has never done such a ruthless thing. It's just giving him a bit of face. For those who do sports commentary, leaving our station is basically a dead end."

Huang Jianxiang left like this, on a late autumn evening with scattered leaves. The afterglow of the sunset fell, stretching his shadow longer and longer. The sunset completely disappeared, and there were no more rosy clouds at the horizon. Along the Kunyu River, a tall and thin figure walked alone.

It was already a moonless night, but Huang Jianxiang still didn't want to go home. Only he knew that he only had 33 yuan in his pocket, which wasn't even enough for his wife Liangying to buy vegetables tomorrow. Going back so early, with Liangying not asleep, he really didn't want to kneel on the washboard again.

Huang Jianxiang's head hurt terribly. He wasn't without thoughts of staying in the station, but what could he do here? Repeated unauthorized bathroom breaks without asking for leave had already made the leaders endure him. Could he really continue to stay in the station, take a monthly salary of 2500 yuan, replace the janitor's job, and clean the toilet opposite the sports department every day? Or should he wait like other famous hosts with good looks for rich people to help, be remarried to a good family, and then honestly serve as a wife and mother later? Or secretly sell his hair under the banner of CCTV, each strand worth 20,000 yuan?

Huang Jianxiang thought about it, this body of meat couldn't grow for nothing, so he came up with the idea of performing arts. First, he went to Tsinghua University, but the audience only cheered but didn't pay. Then he thought of his good friend Guo Degang. Guo Degang looked at Huang Jianxiang, laughed, and said: "Look at your pig kidney face, and you still want to perform? If we draw a picture based on your appearance and stick it on the door to ward off evil spirits, or hang it on the bed to prevent pregnancy!" Huang Jianxiang threw a punch, and the man had no choice but to agree, but with the condition of a trial period for one session.

After the trial period, Huang Jianxiang was still fired.

Huang Jianxiang cursed his mother, feeling increasingly frustrated. Looking at the Kunyu River water, a stench rose up.

At this moment, someone patted his shoulder from behind. He turned around and saw this person with a crew cut, lips hanging like a spear and a dagger. Oh, it was Lu Xun! He wore a coarse cloth robe, speaking in a heavy dialect, and said: "Are you still a man? You can't die in this broken place."

Huang Jianxiang immediately felt a lump in his throat and said: "The tragic scene has made me unable to bear looking; the rumors have made me unable to bear hearing."

Mr. Lu smiled and asked: "What happened?"

Huang Jianxiang took out a tissue, blew his nose, and said: "I've always been willing to assume the worst intentions of CCTV, but I didn't expect, nor believe, that they would be cruel to this extent. Moreover, how could the always smiling and amiable Huang Jianxiang submit a resignation letter for no reason? I'm stupid to resign voluntarily?! Look at each pig football match, isn't the microphone at the forefront always from CCTV? Better to live poorly than die well, how can the treatment of Oriental TV compare with CCTV? Saying I resigned to join Oriental TV? Have you ever seen water flow uphill?"

Mr. Lu patted Huang Jianxiang's shoulder and said: "Then why did you resign?"

Huang Jianxiang was speechless.

Mr. Lu lit a cigarette and slowly said: "In fact, they have long wanted you to leave. They originally wanted to give you a chance to resign voluntarily, but after such a long time, you didn't move. You lingered unwillingly to leave, but they didn't keep you. Haven't you had any matches to comment on recently? Are you doing anything serious? Don't you have to go to Tsinghua University to perform? Don't you have to go and perform comedy? Seeing you not leaving, they had no choice but to have someone write a denunciation letter. Do you know now? CCTV saying you resigned voluntarily is giving you face. How would they slap their own faces? You were fired by CCTV!"

Huang Jianxiang got angry! "What evidence do you have???"

Lu Xun said: "Haven't you heard? A certain leader of your sports center personally said in a private setting: This kind of person, we will never use again! Isn't that enough?"

Huang Jianxiang wiped his tears, suddenly enlightened. Then he straightened his collar and said: "If they don't want me here, there are places where they need me."

Mr. Lu said: "You still haven't changed your bad habits. Stop using character issues as excuses. Your big mouth always gets you into trouble. Another piece of advice: People fear fame as pigs fear getting fat, bullets hit the leading bird. You are famous, you make money, but your colleagues' bonuses are all gone because of your shout. As long as you are in CCTV, the two people who talk about pig football will be unemployed, and the boss of your sports center will have a sword hanging over his head. Who knows when you might cause another incident? Sigh, look at your poor relationships, wherever you go, you're destined to be fired!"

Finally, Huang Jianxiang fully understood that he was forced to resign. He stared at Lu Xun and said: "The truly brave ones dare to face the bleakness of life, dare to confront the gushing blood. I will fight, fight like a man!"

Mr. Lu smiled and said: "You are not fighting alone, you are not alone! Even if the whole world abandons you, there are still countless people like Liangying who support you, worthy of your cherishing!"

Thus, from this moment, Lu Xun's spirit possessed Huang Jianxiang. Although Huang Jianxiang no longer comments on football for CCTV, his fans will always follow him.

Huang Jianxiang lost his voice, his right to speak was taken away. But the era of television broadcasting of Chinese pig football is over, and the era of reading begins.
