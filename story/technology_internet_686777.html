Over 80% of Guangzhou government departments adhere to old Weibo accounts

by lovet9v9 on 2012-03-02 11:09:39

Yesterday, the third batch of members of [Guangzhou Weibo Announcement Hall] officially took their places, with a total of 28 units. So far, more than 80% of government functional departments in the city have set up civilian microblogs.

70 units have woven the [big scarf]

On November 17, 2011 and December 22, the first and second batches of members of [Guangzhou Weibo Announcement Hall] came online, with 26 commissions, offices, and bureaus, 16 districts and counties, and 7 government spokespersons' microblogs going live. In over a hundred days, they have announced more than 20,000 pieces of information, replied to over ten thousand netizen comments, and accumulated nearly 6 million followers. Yesterday, with the official microblogs of 28 units such as the Municipal Cultural Relics Bureau, the Municipal Fire Bureau, the Municipal Federation of Trade Unions, and the Municipal Disabled Persons' Federation going live on mobile scaffolding [http://www.itsen.net], 70 units in the city have now woven the [scarf]. Among them, the coverage rate of official microblogs of government functional departments has exceeded 80%.

[Today Guangzhou is warm and cozy, many 'scarves' are here.] [Hope for more official microblogs to listen to our common people's talk and do things for our common people...]... On the first day of the launch of the third batch of official microblogs, many netizens sent congratulations and also raised questions. www.baode.cc

Netizen [Snail Superman] said: [The Municipal Social Affairs Office is really a unit that confuses yet gives hope, can it manage everything outside the door?]

[Actually, I'm not quite sure what the Social Affairs Office ultimately is either,] the relevant person in charge of the Municipal Social Affairs Office's answer drew laughter from everyone. [However, communities, social organizations, and social services are all within our scope of work. We hope to hear more voices from netizens and gather more forces to build Guangzhou.] At this moment, laughter turned into applause.

The list of the third batch of online official microblogs includes: the Municipal Economic and Information Commission (Weibo), the Municipal Ethnic Affairs Commission, the Municipal Finance Bureau (Weibo), the Municipal Land Bureau, the Municipal Planning Commission (Weibo), the Municipal Population and Family Planning Commission (Weibo), the Municipal Social Affairs Office (Weibo), the Municipal Local Taxation Bureau, the Municipal Radio, Film and Television Bureau (Weibo), the Municipal News Publishing Bureau (Weibo), the Municipal Cultural Relics Bureau (Weibo), the Municipal Gardening and Greening Bureau (Weibo), the Municipal Financial Bureau (Weibo), the Municipal Intellectual Property Bureau (Weibo), the Municipal Civil Air Defense Bureau (Weibo), the Municipal Agriculture Bureau (Weibo), the Municipal Food and Drug Administration (Weibo), the Municipal Fire Bureau, the Municipal Grain Bureau (Weibo), the Municipal Traditional Chinese Medicine Administration (Weibo), the Xizhan Area Management Committee, the Guangzhou Economic and Technological Development Zone Management Committee, the Municipal Foreign Trade Promotion Association (Weibo), the Municipal Park Management Center (Weibo), the Municipal Federation of Trade Unions (Weibo), the Municipal Federation of Industry and Commerce (Weibo), the Municipal Disabled Persons' Federation (Weibo), the Municipal Meteorological Bureau (Weibo) and other units gathered together. www.tingfeier.com

Fans love livelihood-related Weibos

Yesterday, a hundred official microblog fans were also invited to participate in the launch ceremony, and the departments related to people's livelihood in the third batch of official microblogs became the focus. [Tree Planting Day is coming soon, where can we participate in voluntary tree planting?] [Will the weather forecast be updated in real time on Weibo?] [Will the scope of the park annual ticket expand, and what special garden tours will there be this year?] The relevant persons in charge of the Municipal Gardening and Greening Bureau, the Municipal Meteorological Bureau, and the Municipal Park Management Center were surrounded by netizens.

According to the previously released "Guangzhou Microblog Announcement Hall Evaluation Report," from the three comprehensive indicators of activity, dissemination power, and coverage, [@Safe Guangzhou], [@Guangzhou Announcement], and [@Transportation Guangzhou (Weibo)] ranked in the top three among the first two batches of online official microblogs. For any official microblog related to people's livelihood, housing, transportation, culture and education, health care, etc., their fans are very active.

The activity of fans also stimulates the activity of official microblogs. According to statistics, currently [@Transportation Guangzhou] is the most active, averaging more than 15 posts per day, without interruption even on weekends, forming fixed topics such as traffic updates, bus services, subway services, traffic weather, and illustrated traffic, attracting countless fans to watch, forward, and comment.

Online official microblogs also need to know how to go offline

[Not to be a zombie, not to put on a show.] This was the promise made by Wang Hui (Weibo), Director of the Government News Office and Spokesperson, when [Guangzhou Weibo Announcement Hall] went online.

In just two short days, [@Guangzhou Announcement] found 11 blood donation volunteers for a sick student at Guangzhou University of Technology; [@Guangzhou Tongzhou] solved the drinking water supply problem in the Tongzhou Xinhualian residential area; [@Capital Industry (Weibo)] helped netizens get a refund; [@Guangzhou Xicheng (Weibo)] cleared away the garbage blocking the entrance of the residential area that troubled netizens on the same day; [@Guangzhou Wang Hui] coordinated with [@Transportation Guangzhou] and [@Guangzhou Haidian] to solve the parking problem on Lin Cui Road. To date, various official microblogs of [Guangzhou Weibo Announcement Hall] have solved more than 200 annoying problems for netizens. www.syzc020.com

On February 11, more than 40 [Wang Hui Fans] participated in an emergency training session at the "Red Cube" in Dongcheng District, organized by [@Guangzhou Wang Hui]. Meanwhile, [@Transportation Guangzhou] started a metro transfer station guessing game, and [@Guangzhou Fire Department] organized netizens to visit firemen's training. Wang Hui stated that while managing online Weibos well, offline activities should also be managed well. [As official Weibos gradually mature, we should move more from online to offline, face-to-face communication with netizens, eliminate barriers, and this will also be one of the main tasks of official Weibos in the future.]

Relevant scene

Officials use cute language to promote Weibos

[Dear friends!] When this sweet internet phrase was read out by a solemn and serious deep male voice, the strong contrast drew curious glances from the netizens in the venue. Yesterday, government officials used various creative methods to recommend their own Weibos, not hesitating to appear cute.

After calling out [friends], the person in charge of the Municipal Social Affairs Office cleared his throat and continued reading carefully, [Bathed in the gentle breeze of March, we come gently, bringing the sprouting hearts of all workers in Guangzhou's social construction field to wish everyone happiness and health! In harmonious March, the social construction field of Guangzhou will organize the large-scale Learn from Lei Feng public welfare event 'Love in Guangzhou', let us all enjoy the happy and wonderful times of Public Welfare Guangzhou! Don't forget to come, review, and give support, thanks!]

Other official Weibos did not want to be left behind. The person in charge of the Municipal Ethnic Affairs Commission read out an advertisement slogan, [Watch Weibo, watch Guangzhou Ethnic Affairs, please applaud!]

[Hoeing under the noon sun, sweat drips onto the soil beneath the crops. Who knows the hardship in each grain on the plate? People take food as heaven, and food takes grain as priority, everyone come join the 'grain fans'!] The person in charge of the Municipal Grain Bureau borrowed ancient poetry to grab attention.

The relevant person in charge of the Municipal Park Management Center sincerely said, [The Awakening of Insects season is coming, please don't play on the ice anymore!]
