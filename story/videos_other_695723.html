Love knows no boundaries, no age, and no racial lines! Love can be low-key and ordinary, or it can be grand and showy, but ultimate love is growing old together! Two people supporting each other! I remember once seeing an elderly couple outside a shop; the weather was very cold! The grandmother's clothes didn't include a hat, and she was holding vegetables in her hands! The grandfather walked up and put the grandmother's hat on her head, then took off his own scarf and wrapped it around her neck. He said, "Why do you always forget to wear a hat and scarf? It's so cold, how will you deal with a cold!" Then the two of them supported each other as they walked away. At that moment, I felt this was love, this was growing old together! Recently, I've had a lot of thoughts. A classmate from my old school and two of my girlfriends were both dating him at the same time! He was sentenced to two years in prison for fighting and was just released recently! These two girlfriends of mine have been waiting for him, one often goes to his house to see his parents, the other frequently goes to see him! After he got out, it was hard for him to choose because they were all waiting for him, hoping for him! I asked one of the girlfriends why she waited for him? What could he give her after getting out? Why not break up? She said, "Should I break up with him just because he went to jail? That would make me too inhumane! No matter if he chooses me or not after getting out, I won't regret waiting!" Isn't it silly for women? In the end, he didn't end up with this girlfriend of mine! Actually, time is the greatest healer when it comes to matters of the heart! We have a mistaken understanding of the essence of love, and our way of recognizing it is also wrong. Love is a belief, and beliefs have transcendental characteristics that science cannot explain.
