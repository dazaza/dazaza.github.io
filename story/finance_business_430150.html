Rebecca Ferguson

The X Factor may be over, but Rebecca Ferguson hasn’t stopped taking style cues from her former mentor Cheryl Cole. The 24-year-old star wore a pair of bright purple trousers, similar to the ones Cheryl wore during last week's X Factor USA auditions, while filming a new Walkers crisps advert yesterday.

Nevertheless, Rebecca added her own vintage flair to her outfit by pairing the trousers with a printed pussybow blouse, sky-high wedges, and voluminous, wavy hair. Rebecca was spotted shooting the advert for Walkers' Sunbites crisps, which now include the Cheddar and Caramelised Onion flavor, in her hometown of Liverpool.

In the advert, Rebecca returns home for a family visit, but things take a turn for the worse when she steals a pack of Sunbites from her grandmother’s neighbor. Rebecca is then seen running off to her waiting helicopter, chased by an angry pensioner on a mobility scooter.

Speaking about the filming experience, Rebecca said: "This has been a fantastic homecoming. I was really nervous when I arrived because it’s my first TV advert, but it’s so exciting to be filming in my own hometown!"

And, during filming today, Rebecca wrote on her Twitter page: "Having a lovely day, met some lovely people."

X Factor runner-up Rebecca made headlines last week when she confirmed she is dating fellow X Factor contestant Zayn Malik.
