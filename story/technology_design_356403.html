Story Mode + Single Mode | -- Quest Mode (appears after clearing once) |- - Battle against the computer | -- Survival Mode Communication Battle Mode + Options | - Difficulty Setting (normal / difficult) |- Data Delete D-Pad Move/ (In battle) → Move forward, ← Move backward, ↑ Rotate clockwise, ↓ Rotate counterclockwise A Confirm/ (In battle) Ultimate Super Combo (Can be activated when the blue gauge is full) B Cancel/ (In battle) Light attack X Jump Y Heavy attack L Rotate view counterclockwise/ (In battle) Body flicker technique (Go behind the enemy) R Rotate view clockwise/ (In battle) Defense SELECT START Pause/ Select Story Mode —— Suitori Mode Prologue: Departure, Kakashi Team begins. Character (NARUTO) has 3 items (random). Follow the map in the upper right corner of the screen to go to Tsunade, accept the mission, then leave the village. Arrive at the Sand Village, after the dialogue, control NARUTO to the second yellow point (displayed on the map). Along the way, you can pick up items and encounter enemies (a bit like Pokémon), more manga like Dragon Ball. Then use Might Guy vs Kisame, defeat him, then SAVE. Enter the next chapter, Chapter 1: Sakura VS Sasori. After the dialogue, it's Sakura vs Sasori battle, defeat him, then SAVE, enter the next chapter, Chapter 2: Kakashi Opens His Eye!! After the dialogue, it's Kakashi vs Deidara. Note that Deidara's clay bombs are annoying, also watch Kakashi's combo... and Kakashi's ultimate move (Kamui) isn't very powerful. After defeating him, it's Naruto vs Deidara, it's easy to win with Naruto, after defeating SAVE, enter the next chapter, Chapter 3: The Inherited One. No battle, after the dialogue SAVE, enter the next chapter, Chapter 4: Comrades. After the dialogue, return to Konoha, you will see 2 red dots on the map. If you choose the top one, you can directly SAVE and enter the next chapter. If you choose the bottom one, follow the map instructions, you will meet Temari, after the dialogue, leave the village, go to the next yellow dot (you can pick up items along the way), meet Temari, return to the village, have two dialogues with Temari, leave the village, collect 8 glowing items (some are on rock mountains, use X to jump, some require using R.L to rotate the view to discover), after collecting all 8, return to the village, then talk to Temari again, you can use Itachi and Kisame. Then follow the map back to the top, SAVE, enter the next chapter, Chapter 5: Surprise Attack. After the dialogue, Shikamaru vs Sai. After defeating, Naruto vs Sai, after defeating SAVE, "Naruto Shippuden" episode 256, enter the next chapter, Chapter 6: Simulation Combat Training. After the dialogue, Naruto vs Yamato, after defeating, Sai vs Yamato, after defeating SAVE, enter the next chapter, Chapter 7: The Reborn Kakashi Team Sets Out!! After the dialogue, walk to the next yellow dot, SAVE, enter the next chapter, Chapter 8: Heaven's Bridge. After the dialogue, Yamato vs Kabuto, after defeating, immediately Yamato vs Orochimaru (HP won't be fully restored, so be careful not to lose too much HP while fighting Kabuto), then Naruto (Nine-Tails Chakra Mode) vs Orochimaru, after defeating SAVE, Anime Space, enter the next chapter, Chapter 9: Fourth Match!! After the dialogue, Naruto (Four-Tails Mode) vs Orochimaru, after defeating SAVE, enter the next chapter, Chapter 10: Sai's Betrayal. After the dialogue, use X to jump over Heaven's Bridge, then exit the forest, SAVE, enter the next chapter, Chapter 11: That Which Moves the Heart Walk to the next yellow dot, SAVE, enter the next chapter, Chapter 12: Infiltration Begins Walk to the next yellow dot. Naruto vs Orochimaru, after defeating SAVE, enter the next chapter, Chapter 13: Sasuke. After the dialogue, Yamato vs Sasuke (Sasuke's attack power is strong... Ultimate move's power is even stronger...), after defeating it's Sakura vs Kabuto, after defeating SAVE, enter the next chapter, Final Chapter: Unfulfilled Wishes. After the dialogue, Naruto vs Sasuke, after defeating clear the game. After clearing in normal difficulty, you can use Sasuke. After clearing in hard difficulty, you can use Nine-Tails Naruto.
