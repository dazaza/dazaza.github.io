A video clip of 6 minutes and 33 seconds in length has recently caused a great stir in Shunde. The male and female protagonists in the video look like high school students, and close to 90% of the video's scenes are explicit sexual content. On the largest local forum in Shunde, there were over 100 pages of replies.

Reporters learned yesterday that this incident has caused extremely adverse effects among middle school students in Shunde, especially in Xingtan Town. Currently, the Shunde police have intervened in the investigation. "It can be confirmed that the teenagers in the video are not currently enrolled students of Xingtan Town," the Shunde police said to reporters yesterday.

Incident: Student sex video appears online

At 11:24 AM on the 12th of this month, a netizen with the ID "AiDeJiShi" posted a thread titled "Dongcun Video" on the Xingtan section of the Shunde BBS, along with a segment of a sex tape. This video was available online for more than a day until 11:49 PM on the 13th when "AiDeJiShi" edited the post content, making the video no longer accessible.

From the video sent by an informant to our reporter, both the male and female protagonists appear to be around 16-17 years old. The female protagonist is even wearing a school uniform. During their encounter, the male protagonist adjusts the position and angle of the recording device multiple times. From their accents, it can be heard that both young people are from Shunde.

Such an explosive video being available for over 24 hours triggered a frenzy of downloads among netizens. Some even conducted human flesh searches, claiming that both the "male and female protagonists" were from Dongcun Village in Xingtan. They attended Xinglian Middle School together during junior high school. The male protagonist surnamed Huang is the son of a teacher at Dongcun Primary School, living opposite the school, and he is no longer in school. The female protagonist is a student at Xinglian Middle School who just entered her first year of high school this year.

The netizens' investigations caused even bigger waves as the name of the school was made public, leading to several days of large-scale screening efforts by the implicated middle schools. Eventually, the Shunde police also got involved in the investigation.

Yesterday, the police informed us that the identities uncovered by netizens were not authentic, confirming that the two young people are not students from Xingtan Town.

School: Use the opportunity to conduct adolescent education

The principal of Xinglian Middle School in Xingtan admitted to our reporter yesterday that the pressure endured these past few days was "indescribable." Before clarifying the truth, Xinglian Middle School decided to conduct a thorough investigation and have teachers educate the students accordingly.

Yesterday, officers from the Xingtan Police Station of the Shunde police notified Xinglian Middle School that they are pursuing the identities of the young people in the video and confirmed that the individuals in the video are neither current students nor graduates of Xinglian Middle School. Upon receiving the notification, the principal of the school said, "Although our school has cleared its name, this event has already caused very negative impacts among the students. We will use this opportunity to carry out ideological education work for students in adolescence."

Director Chen of the Xingtan Education Group revealed that today, a moral education meeting for all schools in Xingtan Town will be held to educate the entire town's students regarding this incident.

Additionally, a female netizen who claimed to be well-informed about the internal details of the video event stated yesterday that the male protagonist had split the video into an upper and lower episode, "The lower episode was originally planned to be uploaded online, but now that it has caused such a big stir, he probably dares not to do so."

Regarding the claim of a sequel to the sex video, the Shunde police indicated that they are investigating. If there is indeed a follow-up to the video, they will make every effort to intercept it to avoid further adverse effects. (Source: Guangzhou Daily, Reporter Zeng Yi)
