Synopsis:

"Jewel Mixed Rice" is a family drama about the story of four siblings named Emerald, Ruby, Coral, and Amber.

Just like bibimbap made with a variety of ingredients, it offers the audience a sumptuous weekend feast for the autumn season.

MBC production staff involved in the making said, "Jewel Mixed Rice is the story of four children named Emerald, Ruby, Coral, and Amber. When different gems are mixed together, will they really be beautiful? They might break when they collide. From the parents' perspective, all their children are equally dear to them, hence the metaphor of bibimbap."

They also stated, "If the family members coordinate well, they could become fine jewelry. However, if there's not enough harmony, they may lose their luster. Jewel Mixed Rice tells the diverse stories of each member of the family," explaining the significance of the title.

The father, Gong Sang-shik, who has worked as a doorman at a hotel for 30 years, is portrayed by veteran actor Han Jin-hee. The mother, Pi Hye-in (translated phonetically), full of vanity and troublemaker, is played by Han Hye-suk.

The eldest daughter, Emerald, who appears outwardly upright but inwardly hopes to meet a wealthy man to live a good life, is portrayed by Ko Na-eun, co-starring with Lee Tae-kun as the male lead.

Similar to her sister, the second daughter Ruby, who believes that meeting a successful man is the way to escape poverty, is played by So Yi-hyun. The son, Coral, who dreams of becoming a prosecutor, is portrayed by Lee Hyun-jin, while the youngest son, Amber, a high school student skilled in exposing fakes, is played by newcomer Lee Il-min.
