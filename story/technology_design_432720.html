Let's go into more detail below.

The Car Sound Network Mobile Navigation Edition is first and foremost a complete wireless satellite positioning receiver. It uses highly sensitive SiRFStarIII chips with unlimited transmission capabilities, 20 GPS channels, and can quickly receive GPS signals, enabling rapid positioning even in poor GPS satellite signal areas. Its design architecture is based on new indoor GPS communication technology with ultra-high sensitivity of -157dBm (-187dBw) and low power consumption technology. This revolutionary system provides incredible sensitivity and makes it easier for you to quickly locate under any adverse conditions in cities and canyons. You can use it strictly for car navigation, mapping, security, agriculture, etc. The Car Sound Network Mobile Navigation Edition transmits data efficiently using Bluetooth wireless technology.
