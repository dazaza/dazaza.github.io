[The Truth About Enlarged Pores] 1. Dehydration: When the dermis layer of the skin lacks water, the epidermal cells begin to shrink, making issues like pores and wrinkles more apparent. Therefore, keeping the skin hydrated is very important! 2. Stratum corneum: If the skin is not thoroughly cleaned for a long time, it will affect the metabolism and shedding of old keratin, causing the pores to expand! 3. Excess oil! 4. Pressing the skin: When taking care of the skin, if the method is inappropriate, it can also cause pores to become enlarged. For example, when removing blackheads, if we press the skin too hard randomly, it can easily break the surface cells of the skin. Once the dermis is damaged, the skin will have difficulty producing new cells! In response to these reasons for enlarged pores, Guangzhou experts suggest paying attention to the following matters in daily life to better help us create "zero-pore" skin. 1. Supplement enough water: While hydrating the skin, don't forget to hydrate our bodies as well. 2. Regular exfoliation: Removing the keratin on the surface of the skin allows the skin to breathe smoothly and helps to reduce those pesky pores. 3. Pay attention to getting an appropriate amount of sleep. Too much sleep can slow down blood circulation, retain water, make the skin loose, and make the pores look large and scary. Adults should ideally sleep for 8 to 9 hours per day. 4. Pay attention to resting and avoid staying up late: Similar to point 3, insufficient rest and excessive staying up late can worsen the skin's condition, making it loose and droopy, thus affecting pore contraction. 5. Control oil secretion. However, note that controlling oil should be done appropriately, and it’s not about washing away all the oil. Sebum is a necessary substance for the human body, secreted to moisturize our own skin. If there is too little oil, the skin will dry out and crack, causing discomfort! Therefore, normal skin needs to maintain a balance between oil and water, which is exactly this principle. The correct way to control oil is to wash away the excess oil while supplementing an appropriate amount of water and oil. Gradually, the skin's oil secretion function will be inhibited, reducing oil secretion and naturally shrinking pores!
