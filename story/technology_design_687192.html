24-Year-Old Man Hammers 82-Year-Old Woman to Death and Robs Her of Over 1500 Yuan

by erdf6skkk on 2012-03-02 14:15:04

On April 12 this year, Wang, an 82-year-old woman from Xiping Village, Nanbai Township in Yuanping City, was killed in her own courtyard with an iron hammer and stones. After the neighbors discovered the incident, they reported it to the public security authorities. The police of Yuanping received the report and preliminarily determined that this was a case of murder for financial gain. The criminal investigation personnel investigated and visited the family and surrounding officials of the deceased Wang, learning that the elderly Wang had three sons who were doing business and working locally all year round. The elder lived comfortably on her own at home, so the suspect should be someone familiar with the elder's situation in the village. Based on the traces and evidence collected at the scene, after the special case police officers conducted a secret investigation of key personnel in the village, the scope was narrowed down to a young man named Liu Jie in the village. After investigation, it was found that this person had left the village after the incident and his whereabouts were unknown. Based on this situation, the special case group police officers secretly pursued and gathered evidence in places such as Fujian, Beijing, Taiyuan, and Linfen. In the past three months, the special case police officers have visited more than 1,000 people, investigated 68 suspicious points, and gradually tightened the net of pursuit. On the afternoon of July 26, the special case group police officers obtained reliable information that the suspect Liu Jie had secretly returned to the village, and the police decisively took action, capturing the suspect Liu Jie at his home.

According to reports by our special correspondent Ma Mao Hu and correspondents Zhen Hai and Hu Wei: Although only 24 years old and in the prime of life, Liu Jie from Xiping Village, Nanbai Township in Yuanping City did not engage in proper work. Instead, he cast greedy eyes on Wang, an 82-year-old wealthy woman in the same village, cruelly smashing her to death with an iron hammer and stealing over 1500 yuan in cash. Unexpectedly, just over a hundred days after fleeing, he was captured at home by the Yuanping police. After trial, Liu Jie confessed to the crime without reservation.

It turns out that the suspect Liu Jie, upon discovering that the elderly woman Wang lived alone at home while her son was doing business outside and she led a relatively affluent life, developed the intention to steal money from the elder. On the morning of April 12, the suspect Liu Jie carried tools for the crime, climbed over the wall into the elder's yard, but due to the tightly locked door, he could not break in to steal. He then waited until dawn, when the elder got up to fetch water in the yard. Seizing the opportunity, the suspect Liu Jie entered the house, only to be discovered by the elder. After being scolded by the elder, the suspect Liu Jie struck the elder's head violently with an iron hammer, killing her. Afterward, he stole over 1500 yuan in cash and fled the village.

Currently, the suspect Liu Jie has been criminally detained, and the case is under further review.
