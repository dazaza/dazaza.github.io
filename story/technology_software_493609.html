Every woman

by wenfjslove on 2011-09-22 20:27:01

He, may pat your back because of your sadness, may hold your hand because of your fear of darkness, and may also embrace you to comfort your confusion and sobbing. But it stops here. Maybe he is a romantic man in daily life, but he will not do anything improper in front of you. You two just get close in jokes and fabricate your feelings in jokes. He is the one who doesn't care too much about your words and deeds or your appearance. He is the one who can go through your surface and walk into your heart.

Such an open-minded and selfless male friend should be a beautiful landscape in life, a kind of property that is difficult to measure with money. Only by maintaining the distance and pure interaction can this friendship last longer.

For many years, he has always been your emotional hotline. When you are so happy that you want to cheer up, you will tell him at the first time because you want him to share your happiness and worries even if he is not by your side. When you are depressed and sentimental, you will also think of him. You just want to confide in him alone. You even hope that he can be by your side and give you a firm arm to lean on. Although you don't need any of his words or comfort, as long as he is willing to listen, your worries will gradually release and your smile will slowly bloom.

In this lifetime, everyone will encounter a few special people. This kind of person may just be your pure spiritual寄托, but he cannot be simply classified as a friend because the care you pour into him surpasses the boundaries and concepts of ordinary friends. However, there has never been any idea or specific action to elevate it to love between you two, and you have even never held hands.

He will not let himself emit dazzling love light, nor will he let himself burn with hot flames of love. You quietly think of him, silently miss him. You hide him in your heart, in your spiritual home. He always lives in your dream. With him, your loneliness and fragility all have a place to be stored.

Sometimes, after telling him, you often worry that your own sadness will disturb his peaceful life. You don't want him to bear the pain with you. You just earnestly hope that there is only sunshine in his world. You might think of him because of a nostalgic old song, a scene of lovers holding hands, his tolerance, his mischief. You might also think of him because of a familiar landscape, a face that touches your heart, his wisdom, his sincerity. You will more likely think of him because of the midnight starry sky, the distant moon, the joy he once brought you, the sleepless beauty he brought you...

He does not have the arrogance and neglect of a husband, nor the greed and pain of a lover. He has the broad mind of a man and the soft-heartedness of a man. You and he discuss life and society; you and he talk about ideals and moods. You and him don't need to support each other back-to-back; you and him often laugh happily on the phone. You keep talking endlessly, and he always listens silently to your voice no matter when.

Every woman deep down has such a sentiment: to have a male friend. He is not a husband, not a lover, but the person residing in your spiritual realm. He does not have to be handsome or older than you, but he must be mature, wise, and considerate...

Of course, he will not accompany you forever; although, you have never asked him to do anything for you, you still hope that he will live well, have a happy family, and a successful career. At the same time, you know that he hopes you live well too. He hopes you can be happy like a bouncing rabbit every day. You both regard each other's happiness as your own.

Perhaps, throughout your life, you will not experience the classic "holding your hand, growing old with you" love story, but you will love your life and cherish your life more because of having such a friend. Because of his existence, your life has added a rainbow after the rain, and your life has become lush and green.

You thank heaven every day for having such a friend, such a feeling, pure and warm, sincere and lasting. You miss him and keep a small space in the depths of your heart for this person, quietly nurturing that indescribable emotion. Perhaps your acquaintance only lasts for a few years, but it will take you a lifetime to completely forget him, and even throughout your life, he will occupy a deep place in your heart. But, you are very grateful to fate, grateful to heaven for giving you such a person, a person who makes you no longer lonely or lonely in this world, even if it is painful, it is better than numbness and pallor...

Maybe over time, your confessions to him have become dependent. You are used to thinking of him every day and contacting him every day. Sometimes you can't even guarantee that you and him are on the same level of friendship. You both fear that the rising emotions will spoil, and that the love factor will exceed friendship. Every time, the smart male friend will help you maintain a calm mind. He will add some ice when your emotions are about to ignite. He won't let himself accidentally fall into the abyss of love with you, because he knows that being friends lasts a lifetime, while being lovers only lasts for a moment.

He is the most loyal listener and the most real friend when you are troubled. He won't leave you because of your incessant chatter, nor will he abandon you because of your nonsense. He will tell you the best way to solve the problem and then accompany you out of your gloomy sky. And when you are happy, he will fade out of your sight and quietly share your happiness! He is your true friend in life!!

He is the person who understands you most besides your other half. Sometimes, there are some things you won't say to your other half but you will share your mood stories with him. There are some things you can't say to others but you can say to him. Having such a male friend is like having an extra psychologist, an extra diary of the soul. He is like a trash can that can hold all your bad moods; he is like an air conditioner that sends both hot and cold air.

The kind of emotion between you and him, which is beyond ordinary friendship and cannot simply be categorized as romance, is the fourth kind of emotion that lies between friendship and romance. Perhaps you place it above friendship and romance, perhaps in your heart it is a deeper and richer sentiment than friendship and romance.

In fact, you and he are destined to be two line segments that never intersect, two stars shining in the night sky that will never collide. No love fruit will be brewed, and you feel as if talking about love would desecrate this feeling between you. This can only be a super natural, pure alternative emotion that transcends love and friendship. Because of this transcendent emotion, you understand life more and live stronger, smiling through the mediocre life. Even if you don't grow old together, you are still intoxicated by the infinite memories and dreams you possess. Looking back on the past and imagining the future...
