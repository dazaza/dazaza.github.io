Happiness and that group of proud teenagers

by ken119 on 2008-08-03 14:10:32

A black car drove into the gate of Fengyun High School. A guy in all black quickly got out of the car, walked around to the other side, slightly bent over, respectfully opened the door, and made a "please" gesture.

"Miss Yanxin, we've arrived at the school. Please get out."

A pair of fair, slender legs stretched out from the car, followed by the girl in the car smiling as she gracefully stepped out.

Fengyun High School was absolutely an aristocratic school, so theoretically, such a spectacle wasn't particularly noteworthy. However, after the girl stepped out of the car, every student and teacher passing by the car couldn't help but stop in their tracks. The girl's breathtaking beauty was truly astonishing. At that moment, her smile was so radiant and beautiful that it made everything around her lose its color. She was the only vividly moving color, an existence so striking.

"Well, you can go back now. And please help me rent a house near the school."

The girl paid no attention to the surrounding gazes that were sizing her up. After all, she had been accustomed to such "peculiar" looks since she was little. She absentmindedly waved her hand at the man and gracefully walked towards the teaching building ahead.

"Miss, Master Long has already bought you a villa. Shouldn't there be no need to rent a house?" The originally expressionless face of the man showed slight changes, his thick eyebrows knitted together, making his rugged face look quite fierce, causing those who saw him to involuntarily step back.

Even before they arrived in Fengyun City, Master Long had already purchased a large villa near the school, with the aim of ensuring Miss Yanxin lived comfortably. Miss Yanxin also knew about this matter. Why then was she going through the trouble of renting a house?

"Who says I have to accept because he bought it? Just do as I say. See ya."

The slim figure soon disappeared from everyone's view. Yanxin turned into the staircase entrance of the teaching building, her joyful smile not yet fading when, unfortunately, her nose collided with someone’s chest first—hard like stone. Instinctively, she took a step back, covering her nose with her small hand, her eyebrows slightly furrowed, presenting an adorable and pitiful appearance that made Ou Yang Tian's heart flutter.

"Sorry, did I hurt you?"

Slightly squinting his eyes, Ou Yang Tian spoke indifferently, with a faint smile on his lips that seemed to float in the wind.

"What do you think?"

Responding with annoyance, Yanxin immediately lifted her head, wanting to see what this person, who was much taller than her, looked like.

Hair as lustrous as black jade, deep and captivating eyes, a high-bridged nose, and a faint smile hanging at the corner of his mouth. Oh my God, she must be dreaming! How could there possibly be a teenager in this world who could rival Long Yang? But, the person in front of her indeed possessed extraordinary features, and the smile on his face made her feel comfortable no matter how she looked at it, far more pleasant than that obnoxious Long Yang.

Yanxin was lost in examining him, while Ou Yang Tian was also looking at her, observing her cute expressions as she alternated between frowning and wide-eyed amazement. Unknowingly, the laughter lines at the corners of his mouth grew deeper and deeper.

"Are you a new transfer student?"
