In recent years, with the rapid development of the town's economy, it has received honors such as "National Advanced Unit for Creating Civilized Villages", "National Forestation Park and Shop Town", "National Model Town for Small-Town Construction", "National Investment Environment and Small Town", "National Spark Model Town", "Provincial Environmental and Economic Development", "Provincial Model Hygiene Town", "Provincial Civilized Town", "Provincial New-Type Demonstration Town", etc. In 2007, China successfully held an "Exposition" where Yancheng became known as the first township for shoe machinery, hosting a large exhibition that highlighted industrialization, urbanization, new rural construction, and unique characteristics of the town’s work.

As one of the key towns in Yancheng City and one of the seven characteristic townships in Yandu, the intention is to follow up with distinctive strategies to consolidate the industrial backbone town. The shoe machinery industry has seen considerable development, being listed among the top 10 industrial clusters in 2004. This strong support from the shoe machinery industry has promoted the sound and rapid ecological-social development, making the market bustling with prosperity and enhancing the class of small-town construction. The Wolong Holiday Hotel, the first three-star hotel in the township, has become a place for guest reception and leisure. Supermarkets and large shopping malls are located on famous commercial streets, embodying integrity and becoming a radiation center for the surrounding economy, culture, commerce, and medical services in Dahan Town.

Shoe-making machinery was listed as a pillar industry in the national Xinghuo Plan, driving the town to become a demonstration of town enterprises nationwide and a concentration of Spark Technology. The shoe machinery industry continuously produces high-tech content and high added-value new products. New shoe machinery products are more widely applied in various industries and fields such as automobile interior decoration and medical machinery. There have been developments in 35 national new products, provincial high-tech products, and sole proprietorship national patent products, amounting to 84 national and provincial "Spark" and "Torch Plan" products. Three enterprises have achieved 150 ISO9000 international quality system standards, ranking at the top of Chinese shoe machinery. Machine Trillion Company becomes the first enterprise to obtain European CE certification.

If enterprises are involved in shoes, they will find Dahan's shoe machinery products. Keegan has become a major Chinese shoe machinery manufacturing enterprise, having the most regional clusters of high-tech products and being China's largest production and export base. Products are sold in more than 40 countries and regions including the UK, Germany, the USA, Japan, Italy, Russia, and Southeast Asia.

Recently, Yandu Large Town received an invitation from the National Development and Reform Commission to exhibit its small and characteristic shoe machinery industry at the 2010 Shanghai World Expo China Town Museum.
