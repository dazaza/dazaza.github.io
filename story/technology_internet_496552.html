Hong Kong drama "Unscheduled Appointment" Stimulates Old Love, Demonstrates Passion

Tai Feng encourages Dong Jian to pursue Xiao Fang, but Dong Jian still tells Tai Feng that he will only see Xiao Fang as a patient testing the new anti-allergy medicine, for which Tai Feng scolds him for saying one thing and meaning another. Xiao Fang invites Dong Jian to the shoe store to discuss the drug trial, while Xiao Fang is distracted trying on shoes and talking with Dong Jian, venting her emotions over her breakup. Dong Jian follows Xiao Fang to the nail salon where Tai Feng suddenly appears, indicating that he had already informed Xiao Fang about the drug test; Dong Jian is fuming with anger at Tai Feng's self-righteous actions but feels powerless against him. Xiao Fang unintentionally sees Yong Shen following her in his car, so she immediately enthusiastically drags Dong Jian and Tai Feng to go shopping with her, making Yong Shen both jealous and angry. When Tai Feng and Dong Jian meet with Jing Tian and others, Yong Shen takes the opportunity to criticize them for using work as an excuse to pursue Xiao Fang.

Jing Tian angrily scolds Yong Shen for not separating public from private matters, making Yong Shen feel extremely wronged. To facilitate the drug trial, Xiao Fang transfers to work at Dong Jian's clinic, much to Dong Jian's delight to be able to spend time with Xiao Fang. Hui Mei mistakenly assumes Xiao Fang is Tai Feng's girlfriend, Xiao Fang quickly clarifies that they are just good friends, making Dong Jian secretly pleased. Yong Shen finds Xiao Fang and proposes reconciliation, but when he sees Dong Jian and Tai Feng accompanying Xiao Fang again, he gets angry and drives past the two men, splashing them with dirty water and soaking them completely.

Tai Feng notices Xiao Fang being unhappy due to Yong Shen's affairs, Xiao Fang finally reveals her worries to her two friends. Tai Feng wants to take advantage of the situation to pursue Xiao Fang but is stopped by Dong Jian; unexpectedly, Tai Feng just gives Dong Jian an ambiguous smile, leaving Dong Jian puzzled about Tai Feng's thoughts. Xiao Fang sees someone groaning in pain outside Chu Qiao's Chinese medicine clinic, so she brings the person back to Dong Jian's clinic for treatment. Hui Mei fears Xiao Fang's actions will anger Chu Qiao and quickly tries to stop her, but Xiao Fang disregards Hui Mei's advice, prioritizing saving lives. After Chu Qiao learns about this from the patient, he immediately assumes that Dong Jian is deliberately opposing him and goes to Dong Jian's clinic to confront him fiercely.

In Hong Kong drama "Unscheduled Appointment," Xiao Fang Challenges Overbearing Chu Qiao

Dong Jian sees Chu Qiao's unreasonable behavior scaring away other patients and can't help but argue with Chu Qiao. Xiao Fang, unable to stand it, helps Dong Jian to jointly reprimand Chu Qiao and drive her away. Chu Qiao vents her anger on Hua Xi, demanding that she return to her hometown and not stay in Hong Kong. Hua Xi refuses to comply, so Chu Qiao threatens to reclaim Dong Jian's shop space; Hua Xi has no choice but to agree for her son's sake. Dong Jian officially begins the clinical trials of the new anti-allergy drug, Xiao Fang becomes worried after being injected with the drug, so Dong Jian has to console her. Dong Jian knows that Tai Feng has made another appointment with Xiao Fang, so he closely follows her, not letting Tai Feng have any chance to act. Tai Feng keeps creating romantic surprises for Xiao Fang, making her heart flutter. Just as Dong Jian thinks the new drug test is going smoothly, Xiao Fang and the other patients' allergies flare up again.
