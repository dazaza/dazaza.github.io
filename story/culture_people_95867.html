Garden Wedding Beauty

by fendan on 2008-10-12 14:42:02

The sky in May is very blue, and it gives people the feeling that something happy is bound to happen. I don't remember who once said: the bride in May is the happiest, because even the most beautiful flowers cannot compare with the bride's smile. In the bright afternoon, a pure white bride stands gracefully on the green grassy lawn, leaning on him: a beautiful male and female protagonist in a poetic painting.

A white tent was erected on the green grass, tulips and lilies decorated the entire garden. Transparent champagne glasses and all kinds of fruits and pastries were arranged. People casually held their favorite drinks or juice, or sparkling wine, stopping to chat and raising their glasses to toast each other. If someone could climb onto the tree branch at this moment for an aerial view, they would surely marvel at this flowing beautiful scene: elegantly dressed guests gradually arrived on the lawn, smiling, rejoicing, sharing in their sweetness. Today is the most wonderful day for him and her, and the grand wedding of the prince and princess in a fairy tale is about to begin...

Accompanied by the sound of sacred music, two flower children around 7-8 years old walked down the carpet, especially small white veils made them look like little angels of love. Petals flew in the air, spreading endless blessings for the newlyweds. Behind them, the bride walked slowly towards the eagerly awaiting groom, leaning on her father's arm. Under the flower gate adorned with moonlight roses and white lilies, the bride's father handed over his beloved daughter to the groom waiting under the flower gate. At the moment their hands clasped together and their eyes met, people could feel the happiness and love between them, explosive yet lasting. A life-long journey of holding hands has begun. Although there may be hardships and obstacles in the days ahead, at this moment, both hearts are filled only with overflowing sweetness.

Standing before the flower-covered stage, under the guidance of the officiant, they read out the "Vows of Love" to each other: "I, wish to become your spouse, from now on whether poor or rich, sick or healthy, I will do my best to love you, be loyal to you, please parents and all relatives and friends as witnesses..." The former vows of eternal love have become today's perfect marriage. From now on, their worlds will merge, their hearts and hands connected, facing storms together, ready to face future challenges together.

At this time, the adorable little flower child dragged the lace-edged pillow with a pair of shiny diamond rings wrapped in satin bows. The groom gently took one and slipped it onto the ring finger of the bride's left hand. The bride also put this precious and eternal symbol of love on the groom's finger. Meanwhile, they engraved each other's names forever in their hearts. "Holding your hand, growing old with you..."

At this moment, the feelings of the parents are not only joy and relief, but also an overwhelming sense of happiness. The bride's father could not suppress his inner excitement, the flower he held slightly trembled. His simple blessing words sent to the newlyweds are not only expectations for the future, but also deep affection for his children.

Elders, oysters, old classmates, new colleagues went up to give blessings. As the wedding cake and champagne tower were shared, the ceremony proceeded slowly. What people shared was not just the sweet cake and golden flowing champagne, but also their immense happiness and anticipation. "Cheers! Cheers!"

It's time. All unmarried girls present are ready. Unless she doesn't want to become tomorrow's beautiful heroine soon, then the tempting bouquet in the bride's hand becomes their "pursuit goal". According to legend, the unmarried woman who catches the bouquet thrown by the bride will become the next wedding's heroine. Our bride turned around and drew a beautiful arc in the air with the bouquet. The girls were all somewhat excited, and during the scramble, it finally landed in the arms of a happy girl. Once again, enthusiastic applause erupted on the lawn.

As the sky gradually darkened, the banquet began. People freely selected their favorite foods, taking photos with the bride and groom without restraint amidst the pleasant scenery. When the flashlights flickered frequently in the twilight, the newlyweds lit the crystal candle cups already placed on the lawn. A heart symbolizing completeness gradually lit up surrounded by fresh flowers and green grass. At the same time, fireworks shot out dazzling patterns from above the flower pillars. The guests could not contain their inner joy, and colorful butterflies leapt from the firework tubes in their hands. The entire lawn was filled with unprecedented festivity and joy, cheering for the new couple's tomorrow...
