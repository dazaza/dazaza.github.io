by hongmengdadi on 2012-02-10 11:29:20

Perhaps many people complain that there is always no order at home. Perhaps many people think that there is no place for small items at home, and they are always "revealed" outside. Maybe some people think that after buying storage boxes, a lot of things still occupy space at home. Now let me tell you how to choose suitable storage items for your own home. We can choose the appropriate storage items according to the specific situation of our home. For the living room, you can choose wall storage or sofa storage, that is to say, you can choose storage hanging bags and other storage items. The advantage of choosing this kind of storage is saving space, and you can put some small items. Sofa hanging bag storage can hold magazines, remote controls, etc., which can be used at hand and do not take up space.

For the bedroom space, you can choose according to the home decoration style. For the country-style decoration, you can choose wicker storage. For the popularized decoration style, you can choose pattern-beautiful storage boxes and such storage items are also quite suitable. The benefits of choosing these products: one can store clothes and other items, and secondly, they can provide a decorative function, achieving two goals with one action!

The kitchen is an important area in the house. If the kitchen is dirty and messy, it will always affect your mood when cooking and even your appetite at home. Therefore, the cleanliness of the kitchen is also very important, and you should cleverly use storage to decorate the kitchen. At this time, in terms of space application, you need to adopt a concise and clever way, pay attention to the compact functional layout of the cabinets, and try to make use of walls, shelves, etc. You can also consider making the corner of the odd space into a partition for placing objects to improve the utilization rate of the kitchen space by using the method of zero-whole splicing.
