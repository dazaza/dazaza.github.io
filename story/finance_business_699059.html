Summer don't sleep on stone, autumn don't sleep on board. Spring don't expose your navel, winter don't cover your head. Be more active in the day, and you'll dream less at night. Washing your feet before bed is better than taking supplements. Sleeping with the window open makes for a fragrant night's rest. Greedily seeking coolness without covering up properly is bound to lead to illness. Early to bed and early to rise keeps one refreshed and spirited; indulging in laziness adds sickness and subtracts years. Grinding teeth at night indicates worms in the stomach. Eating an entire pig in a day is not as beneficial as snoring in bed. Eating a whole sheep in three days is not as good as washing your feet and then going to bed. If your pillow isn't right, the more you sleep, the more tired you feel. Sleep comes first from the heart, then the person - sleeping can make a great beauty. Facing the wind, the head stays warm; having your feet face the wind, and you'll need a doctor. Don't sleep in alleys, for the coldest wind passes through them. Don't keep lights on while sleeping, and you'll wake up without dizziness. To sleep easily, never let your feet point west and your head east.
