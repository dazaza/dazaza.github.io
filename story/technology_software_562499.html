Grasping the opportunity well enables you to purchase high-end and high-quality building materials at a promotional price much lower than the regular selling price.

by beetgk2958 on 2011-11-16 15:13:16

Experts' advice: Be vigilant when participating in group buying

Han Rui, General Manager of Dadi Home Decoration Dream Workshop, said that the workshop has a vast building materials exhibition hall. It often initiates special discount events for building materials using its channel advantages to express gratitude to a wide range of customers. Attentive consumers can purchase flooring, tiles, sanitary ware, and other building material products at much lower prices than market rates through these discount events, with both brands and quality well guaranteed.

Gao Jianzhang, General Manager of Langjing Sanitary Ware in Henan Province, said that similarly, many building materials merchants take advantage of opportunities like "anniversary store celebrations" to offer substantial promotions. Consumers just need to regularly pay attention to such information and seize the opportunity to buy high-end quality building materials at promotional prices far below their usual selling prices.

Sun Dongming reminded that there are fewer and fewer merchants in the market who solely represent a single building materials brand; many merchants simultaneously represent several brands. Taking tiles as an example, consumers must ensure they clearly identify the selected product as their desired brand before paying a deposit, and whether the order specifies the exact model and type of tile. When merchants deliver the goods, consumers should verify the brand and authenticity of the tiles by checking the bottom markings and anti-counterfeiting marks on the tiles, and calling the anti-counterfeiting hotline.

"There is no such thing as free lunch falling from the sky; online shopping may seem simple and fast, but it is just as complicated as real-world shopping," said Sun Dongming, Executive Deputy Secretary-General of the Henan Provincial Decoration Association. Nowadays, many building materials merchants initiate group purchases through online channels at ultra-low prices much lower than current market rates. This is undoubtedly a "positive development" for consumers. However, there is no such thing as a free lunch, so one must be wary of unscrupulous merchants taking this opportunity to "replace beams with pillars."

In fact, there are many ways to purchase building materials. As long as you are careful, you can save money everywhere, including in Beijing group-buying scenarios. For instance, on July 18th, Langjing Sanitary Ware held a grand "President's Personal Signature Sale" event in Zhengzhou. Toilets usually priced over 3000 yuan could be purchased for less than 500 yuan, with the promise of "being able to pick up goods at any time."

Seizing Opportunities: Many Ways to Save Money on Purchasing Building Materials

From this, we can see that under the prevailing trend of "one-stop" consumption across various commercial fields today, choosing building materials from home decoration companies is undoubtedly a "multi-benefit" good choice. It reduces the hardship of running around selecting varieties, greatly reduces the intermediate coordination phase of home decoration, ensures the quality of the purchased building materials, and maximizes the guarantee of the pre-designed home decoration effect.

In the era when online shopping is increasingly popular, more and more consumers are beginning to participate in building materials group-buying through online channels. This way, they can not only purchase high-quality branded goods at low prices but also significantly save time spent wandering around building materials markets. By typing "building materials group-buying" into Baidu, I found over 4.9 million related web pages. Clearly, group-buying building materials online has indeed become a mainstream channel for building materials consumption.

Additionally, to realize the dream of "one-stop" consumption, all major well-known home decoration companies in Zhengzhou have set up building materials exhibition halls covering more than 1000 square meters. The building materials inside include various types and brands of floor tiles, wall tiles, floors, wallpapers, sanitary ware, hardware components, etc., used in home decoration projects. It is no exaggeration to say that each of these building materials exhibition halls is a small-scale home building materials mall.
