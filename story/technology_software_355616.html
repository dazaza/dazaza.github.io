Aokang has its way of making money, "Internet marketing model" grows up

by dunk9371 on 2010-05-22 20:17:39

With the rise of the Internet, which is characterized by "no borders, large amount of information, fast dissemination speed", the thinking of Wang Zhentao, known as the "Master of Chinese Business", began to become active again - on November 29, 2006, Aokang Group, the largest private shoe manufacturing enterprise, signed a strategic cooperation declaration with Sina.com, the world's largest Chinese portal site in Wenzhou, officially establishing a strategic partnership.

In 2004, Aokang Group officially started using the Shoebiz software system.

Doing "specialty stores that don't display shoes"

In 2003, the Information Technology Center of Aokang Group was officially established.

In 2005, Aokang successively opened its own "specialty stores" on professional business websites such as Taobao, officially entering network marketing.

Aokang will first become the overall sponsor of Sina's 2006 Network Grand Ceremony, promoting national fitness alongside the Olympics as Tebo reappears in Yangzhou. Meanwhile, Aokang will jointly promote the 2006 Network Grand Ceremony through its more than 3000 specialty stores; Sina will use its strong resources to provide online marketing promotion services for Aokang Group. "The dual combination of online brand communication and offline marketing is a major innovation in Aokang's marketing in this cooperation," Zhang Yongheng from Aokang Group's Brand Planning Center introduced, "For example, in this way, the 2006 Network Grand Ceremony instantly has more than 3000 offline outdoor flat advertisements nationwide, and Aokang also gets closer to hundreds of millions of netizens."

In November 2006, Aokang Group and Sina.com reached a strategic partnership.

The birth of the "foot-measuring shoe customizer" can be seen to some extent as the initial form of expression of Aokang's "online marketing model." Because it not only changed the past situation where customers could only passively choose shoe styles, giving them the fun of personal participation in design, but also got closer to the real needs of customers, making the connection between customers and Aokang tighter.

In August 2006, China's first "foot-measuring shoe customizer" was born at Aokang, marking the formation in the mind of Wang Zhentao, President of Aokang Group, of a completely new "online marketing model" based on the Internet and facing the international market.

In October 2006, Kanglong, a sub-brand under Aokang, partnered with Tencent to open the Kanglong online flagship store.

In fact, starting from 2004, Wang Zhentao has been closely monitoring the trends of the Internet. "Opening shops online" has become a pretty good "way to make money" for many Chinese netizens. Moreover, with the gradual improvement of China's network security and network payment platforms, Chinese enterprises and individuals are increasingly believing that the "gold rush era" of the Internet has just begun. Naturally, Wang Zhentao would not miss this opportunity.

Behind the brand expansion lies a series of technological innovations and continuous changes in business models by Aokang.

This cooperation is full of imagination.

As an excellent enterprise in the industrial sector and a powerful force in online media, Aokang Group and Sina.com will integrate their respective resources based on the goal of "win-win cooperation," exploring cooperation opportunities in various aspects and fields, and achieving comprehensive deep cooperation between both parties.

Such a communication effect is obviously "multi-speed." "In the near future, this cooperation will certainly make the Aokang brand have a stronger sense of time, a more international flavor, greater influence, laying the foundation for Aokang to become a world-renowned brand," believes Wang Zhentao, President of Aokang Group.

The strategic cooperation with Sina.com is precisely based on the market characteristics of a new marketing era. More importantly, it accelerates the deep integration of resources between industry and the network.

"In the context of the entire market economy entering a highly informatized era, Aokang Group choosing Sina.com, the world's largest Chinese portal site, as its strategic partner for online integrated marketing and brand promotion, is undoubtedly a marketing innovation after deeply analyzing its own needs and market environment," pointed out a senior marketing expert.

"The 'online marketing model' has initially taken shape."
