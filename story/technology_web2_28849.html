Game Introduction

This place was once called Loulan, and it has a history of at least three thousand years. During the Western Han Dynasty of China, Loulan was one of the "Thirty-six Kingdoms of the Western Regions". At that time, Loulan was right in the middle of the traffic between Han and the various countries of the Western Regions. Han could not bypass this area to fight against the Xiongnu, and the Xiongnu could not threaten the Han dynasty without the help of Loulan. Therefore, both Han and the Xiongnu tried their best to implement a policy of conciliation towards us.

Do you know about the "Silk Road"? The "Silk Road" is vividly referred to as the "golden belt tied across the Eurasian continent." The prosperity of ancient Loulan was directly related to the "Silk Road"! Loulan was located at the junction of the northern and southern routes of the Silk Road back then, which means that anyone traveling on the Silk Road had to pass through Loulan. The "Records of the Grand Historian: The Account of Dayuan" described Loulan as being "on the main road," showing how important this geographical location was. The importance and prosperity of Loulan back then were comparable to today's Hong Kong, Shanghai, and Singapore – a place that everyone aspired to visit. Ha ha!! How could you not know about the "Silk Road"?

Nowadays, there are many different theories about this issue, with dozens of speculations, ha ha!!

In fact, the real reasons are roughly as follows:

Firstly, changes in the natural environment. The retreat of glaciers led to reduced river flow and land desertification, causing Loulan to be abandoned.

Secondly, the shift of political and economic centers. After the Jin Dynasty, the Silk Road route changed to the northern route, and the garrisoning and farming activities of the Central Plains in Loulan moved northward, gradually causing Loulan to be forgotten by people.

But these are not the true reasons. The real reason is that human activities disrupted the harmony of nature. While creating highly advanced civilizations, they also destroyed forests, grasslands, and rivers at an astonishing speed, turning the land into deserts, leading to the disappearance of Loulan.

Yi Du is a mysterious tribal organization from prehistoric times. No one knows where it is, how it came into being, or what it does. The only thing known for sure is that it possesses a terrifying power that can be used to save humanity in disasters, or easily destroy humanity. There was once a theory that Yi Du was an alien civilization from outer space or another star system, but who knows! Anyway, many scientists and explorers have already lost their lives in the vast sandy sea trying to uncover the secrets of Yi Du, all in vain. It's really a pity! I sincerely hope these tragedies won't happen again.

The connection between Loulan and Yi Du began over a thousand years ago during an unprecedented sandstorm.

At that time, the people of Loulan were facing the dire situation of land desertification, water shortages, and epidemics, preparing for a national exodus when they encountered that terrifying sandstorm. It was said to be a massive scene: dark skies, flying sand and rolling stones. In the hazy confusion, Loulan disappeared suddenly...

Just as death was approaching, King Yuan Meng of Loulan prayed to heaven, willing to sacrifice his life in exchange for the survival of the people of Loulan. Suddenly, there was a loud noise, and a beam of white light flashed in the west, illuminating the entire sky like daylight. People felt as if they were under a spell, unable to move, with constant thunderous sounds in their ears. Then, their bodies became weightless, as if the space had collapsed, and everyone lost consciousness.

When people woke up again, they found themselves in a strange fertile land. A faint voice seemed to echo in their ears: "You have suffered the devastating revenge of nature. Yi Du has saved you. From now on, for the next 1500 years, you will not be able to contact your original world." From then on, the people of Loulan started a new life here. Since they didn't know where they were, they called this place the "Mysterious City."

Occupation Introduction

There are no predetermined occupations in Keltang. Various types of people emerge based on the players' experiences.

For example: 1. Close-range attack type (all or most of the potential books' bonuses upon leveling up go to strength) 2. Long-range attack type (all or most of the potential books' bonuses upon leveling up go to spirit).

Game Features

1. Modern communication devices exist in the game. For instance, there are mobile phones capable of sending videos, music, and text messages. Moreover, the function of communicating between game phones and real phones will be realized.

2. Modern aerial combat equipment exists in the game. There are various types of fighter jets that can bomb groups of enemies.

3. High-tech positioning and teleportation terminals exist in the game. These are high-tech items from the future world.
