Sisyphus' response: The list is purely fabricated

The exposure of "Sisyphus' gift-giving list" has drawn countless netizen reviews. Regarding the poster's action, netizens either questioned or affirmed it, but some people thought: It is very normal for operators to give gifts, there is no need to make a big fuss.

Upon learning that personal information was leaked, Sister Wu was very indignant and said she would file a complaint with Sisyphus. Sister Wu also said that since she is a customer of Sisyphus, she receives small gifts from them every year, but she hasn't received any this year, nor has she received notification about gifts. As for what kind of small gifts she received in previous years, Sister Wu said: "There's no need to disclose."

Netizens enthusiastically discussed online, seemingly without touching the emotions of Sisyphus bookstore employees. Yesterday afternoon, reporters came to Guizhou Sisyphus Culture Communication Co., Ltd near Guizhou Normal University, and the employees' attitude seemed very calm. A Sisyphus employee stated that they have no idea about the "gift-giving list" matter.

However, Director Wang insisted that he did not receive any gifts from Sisyphus bookstore. "I would like to receive gifts from others, but there really weren't any."

At the same time, the gift-giving list was still clear at 8:00 AM yesterday morning, with the names, workplace, telephone numbers, and gift content of the recipients clearly visible. But by 4:00 PM, all the personal information contained in the list had been obscured.

The heatedly circulating online is the Sisyphus bookstore's Mid-Autumn Festival public relations maintenance list, which publicly disclosed the names, workplaces, and contact phone numbers of the recipients. So, are the people listed on the list real? How reliable are these contact phone numbers? Did they actually receive gifts? Yesterday, reporters called some of the "recipients" to verify this.

Giving gifts to public officials sparked public opinion

Compared to the increasing caution of the poster "1563994731", the level of attention from onlooker netizens to the event is increasingly rising. In addition to posting replies, netizens also forwarded the list through various forums and microblogs, directly calling for "truth".

Regarding the gift-giving list, Ms. Gao, a responsible person from Sisyphus, said that she only heard about it from a friend when she went to work yesterday morning. However, she stated that the bookstore absolutely did not engage in "bribery" towards relevant departments. Ms. Gao also said that because the PR gift-giving list affects the image of the company and also impacts the individuals on the list, involving defamation and slander, the bookstore reserves the right to report the incident and pursue legal responsibility of the poster. Upon learning that the poster could be traced through the forum, Ms. Gao said that this suggestion would not be ruled out.

Guiwang News September 1 (Qianzhong Morning Post reporter Zhang Liping Lin Xinrong) At 11:23 PM on August 30, netizen "1563994731" claimed to have picked up the Mid-Autumn Festival PR gift-giving list of Guiyang Sisyphus Bookstore and posted it online. Due to the list involving various relevant departments of Guiyang City, this post immediately ignited public opinion. Sisyphus Bookstore responded yesterday: The list is purely fabricated, and the bookstore reserves the right to pursue legal responsibility.

Interestingly, the netizen who posted the thread is a newly registered forum user, registered on August 30, 2011, and it cannot be ruled out that it was registered specifically to post the list.

Did they actually receive gifts?

Regarding the investigation progress at Sisyphus, Ms. Gao stated that an investigation has already been initiated, but it is inconvenient to say more. "Once we have investigation results, we will immediately inform you (media)."

Ms. Wang said that she is a regular customer of Sisyphus Bookstore, often buying books at the store when she worked in Guiyang and leaving her personal information, but she didn't receive any gifts from the store.

Regarding this, a Sisyphus employee pointed out that regardless of whether the matter is true or false, Sisyphus has come a long way in eighteen years, especially as a physical bookstore. "In recent years, Sisyphus is in a critical period of transformation and development, and has seen some improvement. At this crucial moment, it might be intentional defamation and damage to Sisyphus' image by certain competitors."
