Weibo marriage certificate goes viral online, netizens parody KFC and McDonald's (pic)

by anonymous on 2012-02-16 10:13:12

Online Screenshot By Zhu Yangjian These days, "Weibo addicts" are busy getting certificates. What kind of certificate? A marriage certificate! To be precise, it's called a "Weibo Marriage Certificate." How many people have gotten one? By 9:00 AM yesterday, in just three days, more than 30,000 netizens had already obtained it. Why get a marriage certificate? The netizens quote a line from the movie "If You Are the One 2": "Life is short, I'm willing to make mistakes!" How do you get the certificate? On the special Weibo page, netizens just need to upload a photo of "you and him/her", fill in your nickname and a love declaration, and after confirming and publishing, a "Weibo Marriage Certificate" will be generated, complete with a big red stamp. After successfully obtaining the certificate, a Weibo post will automatically be generated: "The China Wedding Expo has approved our marriage!" Simple, fun, fresh, fashionable... So everyone's becoming more daring: "Those who haven't even started yet," quickly draw that first stroke; those who secretly admire someone, take this opportunity to confess; couples in love can get the certificate again and recall their sweetness! Love declarations are touching. "In the morning, at night, in my dreams, I think of your beautiful and charming face. This certificate proves that we will last forever. If you don't agree, how dare I sign." "Love, there's nothing worth or not worth it about it, as long as you love me, and I love you, that's enough." There are also some funny ones. "If you dare to cheat, you'll bear the consequences!" "Remember, what's yours is mine, and what's mine is still mine!" Of course, any online activity is bound to be parodied. So on Weibo, some netizens have "married" Jay Chou and Li Yuchun, others have married Xiaoyueyue and Zhu Bajie, and some have even played matchmaker, helping Sharp Brother and Feng Sister "get married instantly." Recently, Grandpa KFC and Uncle McDonald got married and obtained a certificate, with the love declaration being "Since you came into my life, everything tastes better, I really like it." Even "Xiaolongxia" (a current topic), has gotten married and obtained a certificate, unfortunately, his partner isn't "He Jie", but "Hao Shaowen". Netizens even wrote a love declaration for them: "When we were young, we were inseparable, now we're together, He Jie is just a fleeting cloud." Poor "Ji Xiaolan" and "He Shen" couldn't escape either, their love declaration was "You're my He Fatty, I'm your Lan Baby." ... Regardless of whether it's sincere or playful parody, the "Weibo Marriage Certificate" is very popular. It's the weekend, and the number of people getting certificates is skyrocketing. I just checked the number of certificates issued, and when I went back to check again, wow, it's already exceeded 40,000. Counting with my fingers, nearly 10,000 people got certificates in just over an hour, "sweat"! In order not to be "out" (outdated), I quickly went and got one too! By the way, have you gotten yours?
