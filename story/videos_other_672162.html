Sister, let me tell you some harsh truths that luxury cosmetic brands will never tell you.

by jgojid7 on 2012-02-22 16:00:40

Towards the end of summer, due to my business trip to Xishuangbanna in the summer, I was exposed to a lot of sunlight there. I found that my complexion looked terrible - dark yellow with obvious spots. These scars would bother me no matter if my skin tone is light or dark. Every day, even after applying foundation, it still looks like a layer floating on my skin. I can't bear to look in the mirror; I can't accept that my face has become one that cannot conceal the traces of time. I feel depressed, even depressed, but in reality, I am powerless. All I can do is stare at new cosmetics and hope that applying these things will create a miracle. Sunscreen,隔离霜,妆前乳,粉底液, powder... Just think about how high the temperature is in Beijing during the summer! This suffering is unbearable for me, so I decided to go without makeup every day. But when I actually face others, I feel insecure. Or when I see a young, full face, I really lament in my heart why I don't have naturally good skin. Even if I'm not naturally blessed, I could at least cover up with makeup and appear as a beauty! ... Save me! I don't want to become so ugly! Oh no, I haven't enjoyed enough beauty yet! How could she lose her beauty?

In recent years, I've been using Estée Lauder skincare products. Gradually, I noticed that the originally clean cheeks, except for the forehead, also showed obvious spots. This was quite annoying. One day, I suddenly decided not to trust those advertisements anymore and not to fantasize about becoming the stunning beauty in the ads by simply applying their products... I put all my Estée Lauder skincare products, Chanel妆前乳, etc., into the drawer (all bought with real money, throwing them away would be such a waste). I went to a small store downstairs and bought Anan cucumber cleanser, 片仔癀 pearl cream, Chunjuan 黄芪 cream, Qianqian grass rose water, Yongfang whitening cream, Dabao eye wrinkle cream... Ha ha, a total of 174 yuan, so cool!

As of now, I've been using them for more than four months. No new scars have appeared, my skin tone has become much rosier, my face looks good without foundation, and I feel great without makeup. The color of my eyebrows and eyelashes is natural and clean. Ha ha, you really shouldn't blindly believe in those imported so-called high-end products. You can try it for a few months too. Before this, I've used Shiseido, Oblea, L'Oreal, Aupres, Avon, Biotherm, Estée Lauder, Helena Rubinstein, Dior, Chanel, Laneige... Amway, Nu Skin... and many other well-known brands whose names I've forgotten... Over nearly 15 years of using cosmetics, I've changed countless products and spent a lot of money! It's like a patient seeking treatment, treating the salesgirls with red lips and exquisite eye shadows as doctors, treating my face. Thus, the worse my complexion became, the more I thought it was because I wasn't using high-end enough cosmetics; the more acne I had, the more I covered it up; the more wrinkles appeared, the more I believed the advertising hype about leaving no trace of time!

One day, I suddenly heard an old Chinese doctor on TV say: "This face of ours must use cheap things, but we must eat nutritious things. That way, our intestines will be comfortable, and our complexion will be beautiful and natural."

I felt this was absolutely true. My skin, especially around the eyes, is very thin. As long as I use foreign-made imported products, fat granules appear (to be honest, I think some new domestic products are also problematic, so I dare not use them), probably because of the mineral oil components in these cosmetics. Fat granules are just small white dots. Besides picking them out with a needle, nothing can make them disappear. Needling on your face is painful, especially around the eyes; if you don't pick them, over time, the fat granules will oxidize due to sun exposure, turning into black spots on your face or blackheads settling in your pores. If you choose the latter, no matter how painful it is, you would still pick them, but needling might leave a small scar. Using keratin products will make the skin thinner, and slowly, faint red blood vessels will appear on the high areas like cheekbones. To be honest, even if you give me a free high-end beauty salon card, I wouldn't dare to go because that would only make my facial skin thinner.

Therefore, I packed away all those expensive, fancy, shiny little brown bottles, blue bottles, and whatever 紫竹 water,翠竹 water, and repair creams. I started using the domestic goods I bought seriously, absorbing pearls with Ou Shiman. At first, I thought this water was too thin, and using it gave no feeling on the face. But unlike using those big brands which irritate the eyes. Previously, I thought I was indeed sensitive because if a mall of tens of thousands of square meters had a small corner being renovated, I would immediately feel unable to open my eyes upon entering, so I didn't pay attention to this point; applying 片仔癀 pearl cream feels thick and greasy, but it's fine after a while. Later, I found that applying Chunjuan 黄芪 cream leaves no greasiness but keeps the skin moist. After two months, I indeed noticed improvement - almost no fat granules, and the spots have faded somewhat. Friends I hadn't seen for a while also commented that my complexion had improved significantly. Ah, I'm still using them. A few days ago, an old friend who was once a beauty and now is the mother of a beauty came from out of town. Her face hurt after wandering around in Beijing for a few days. After washing her face, she applied toner and immediately felt pain, so she washed it off again. I thought it might be because the toner contained alcohol. She then started using my 25-yuan-per-100ml rose water with her. It felt comfortable after application. When she left, she took two bottles with her, saying she would save the money for big brands and try domestic products for a while.

In daily life, I also make homemade masks, such as honey green tea powder plus mineral water, honey rose powder plus mineral water, brown sugar honey mix, royal jelly egg white, leftover milk after drinking washed on the face for a while before applying anything else, fruit juice cucumber slices, etc.

Well, in fact, I've always been hesitant to tell those sisters that I'm using 25-yuan-a-bottle skincare products. They would definitely think it's unreliable and won't believe that these can resist the ravages of time on youth. But I'm really scared of those previous treasures I used. So, I compromised - using domestic skincare products and selectively using makeup. For example, Face Shop mascara is good, easy to wash off, and cheap. Chanel妆前乳, Dior powder and blush, CLINIQUE light lip gloss (using only this prevents chapped lips), and perfume - I like several kinds, anyway, foreign perfumes are better, like Dior, Chanel, Issey Miyake, etc.

Uh~ dear ones, that's it. When you're at a loss, try my method. The face is an important reflection of the body's internal condition. Women's vital energy and blood are most important, and getting enough sleep is crucial.

Of course, if your complexion is bad, you should still find a reliable traditional Chinese medicine doctor for a thorough examination. After all, that is the root cause of the problem.
