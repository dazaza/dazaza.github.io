Decorating winter with different colors

by longersing on 2009-11-17 15:33:28

How to make your home warmer? Decorating the winter with different colors, reasonable color matching can also adjust the psychological temperature and keep warm, making you "feel" much warmer. Together with other decorations, it helps you spend a comfortable winter.

Researchers say that colors have a unique impact on people's psychology. Even if the environmental temperature does not change, reasonable color matching can also adjust the psychological warmth and keep you warm, making you "feel" much warmer.

She said that if the winter room decoration is mainly in warm tones such as red, yellow, and orange, it will make people feel warm psychologically. However, for the use of colors, it is necessary to pay attention to harmonious matching. Take red as an example, among all the colors, red is a typical warm tone, but the room should not use too much red. Previous psychological studies have shown that red accelerates the heartbeat more than any other color. If exposed to too much red, one may feel pressured physically and mentally, leading to irritability. Long-term exposure to red can also make people feel tired, even exhausted. She suggested that when choosing colors for room decoration, you can match a small amount of cool tones based on warm tones. The coordinated matching of the three major blocks of indoor colors (wall color, furniture color, fabric color) creates a warm atmosphere while making people feel relaxed.

Give the wall a warm coat

Architects tell you that scientific decoration can also achieve the goal of keeping warm.

Use thermal insulation materials on walls

The architect said that from a building perspective, room insulation can be achieved by adding insulation layers to the walls and windows of the room. He said that building insulation mainly relies on the insulation layer of the outer walls. If pure cotton fabrics are used to wrap particleboards and other boards and attach them to the walls during renovation, it can increase the thickness of the insulation layer and improve the insulation function. In addition, there is a simpler method. Since commonly used concrete walls have the phenomenon of "leaking wind," you can use wallcloths, which also have good insulation effects.

Ventilate when lighting is good

The room needs to be ventilated from time to time to ensure indoor air quality. However, if the windows and doors are open for a long time, it is difficult to maintain the indoor temperature. Therefore, to keep warm, you can choose to open the windows and doors for ventilation when the lighting is good, and close them at other times. After closing the windows and doors, you can choose thick curtains in warm tones to prevent cold air from seeping in. Additionally, for rooms where the bedroom and balcony are connected, placing some clutter or books under the balcony exterior wall can help enhance the wall insulation effect.

Choose electric heaters based on heat dissipation

Whether the house is warm or not, in the absence of central heating, the heat dissipation function of the electric heater plays a decisive role. Recently, reporters visited the electrical counters of several malls in Beijing and found that this year's electric heater products are promoting high-tech heat dissipation.

Quartz tube heater: This is the most common product on the market, using electric heating wire to heat up quartz tubes, emitting far-infrared rays.

Advantages: Lightweight design, rapid temperature rise, uniform heat dissipation, waterproof and explosion-proof, easy maintenance.

Disadvantages: Due to radiant heating, the heat transfer distance is relatively short. Electric heating wires are prone to deformation, and quartz tubes can be easily broken if not handled carefully.

Electric heating wire heater: Generally, the electric heating wire is installed in a fan-like "big head," and through the left-right rotation of the "big head," the entire room can be warmed.

Warm air heater: The principle is similar to the electric heating wire, except a small fan is installed on the heater to enhance heat diffusion, allowing warm air to circulate throughout the room.

Advantages: Good insulation, no noise during operation, suitable for living rooms, dining rooms, and bedrooms.

Oil-filled electric heater: This heater looks like a radiator. Inside the heater is filled with heating oil. When the heater works, the oil around the electric heating tube is heated and then circulates along the radiator, dissipating heat through the tube walls to warm the indoor space.

Advantages: Good sealing performance and insulation.

Disadvantages: Slow heat-up, power-consuming, relatively poor economy, suitable for bedrooms and bathrooms.

Carbon fiber heater: This is the "new equipment" just launched this year. It uses carbon fiber with good thermal conductivity as the heat-conducting body of the two end heaters, achieving rapid heating effects. And due to the insulating characteristics of carbon, this heater can also be used in bathrooms.

Choosing a heater depends on the family area

In the market, I saw that apart from the power-hungry "giant" oil-filled electric heater whose power is nearly 2000 watts, the power of other heaters ranges from a few hundred watts to over a thousand watts. So how to choose an electric heater that can keep the indoor warm while reducing electricity consumption?

Salespeople of electric heaters at Shuang'an Mall gave some suggestions: The purpose of using electric heaters in winter is to make the room warm, not overly hot. Therefore, to maintain indoor temperatures suitable for human comfort, generally, the living room should be maintained at 17℃-19℃, children's rooms at 18℃-20℃, and bedrooms at 14℃-16℃ are more appropriate. If you want to be warmer before sleeping to fall asleep, you can set the electric heater to turn off automatically. Salespeople introduced that for a room of about 10 square meters, an electric heater of 400 to 800 watts would be suitable. For a room of about 16 square meters, an electric heater of 800 to 1500 watts should be sufficient.
