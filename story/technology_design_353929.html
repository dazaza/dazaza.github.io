Three, Dungeon Strategy Guide: Composition of Dungeons: Each dungeon consists of several scenes. Eliminate all monsters in the current scene or destroy the seal at the entrance to the next scene to proceed to the next scene. Follow the map's instructions to enter the final Boss scene, defeat the Boss to complete the entire dungeon adventure. Obtain various tips about the current scene conveniently through the entrance markers on the mini-map. Dungeon Process: Precautions: 1. Each time you enter a dungeon or the next scene of a dungeon, stamina will be consumed. Once stamina is depleted, you cannot enter the dungeon. If stamina is depleted after entering a certain scene of the dungeon, no further stamina will be consumed in the subsequent scenes of that dungeon. 2. Exiting the dungeon using methods such as "Abandon Stage", "Switch Channel", or "Exit Game" will result in equipment durability penalties. 3. For some dungeons related to the main quest, after defeating the Boss, you must also complete the relevant main quest to generate the completion interface. Dungeon Difficulty All dungeons in Dragon's Nest include multiple difficulty levels: The first time a player enters a dungeon, they can choose between easy and normal difficulties by default. After completing the normal difficulty with a certain evaluation, players can then enter the difficult difficulty dungeon. This way, players can gradually challenge different difficulty dungeons until they can enter the highest difficulty dungeon. During the testing phase, after players complete dungeons under Master difficulty with a certain evaluation, they can then enter Abyss-level dungeons. As the dungeon difficulty increases, the health, attack power, and aggressiveness of the monsters will greatly increase. At the same time, the number of monsters will also increase accordingly. Conversely, the rewards for killing monsters and the item rewards for clearing the dungeon will also increase significantly. To fully enjoy the fun of dungeons, it is best to team up with friends to challenge them. High-difficulty dungeons in Dragon's Nest are not easily conquered by "lone heroes." If you don't want to be overwhelmed by monsters, it's better to try cooperating with other classes. Note: 1. The selectable dungeon difficulty is determined by the team leader. Even if some members of the team cannot enter specific difficulty dungeons, as long as the leader has been approved, the whole team can enter. However, upon completing the dungeon, the members do not automatically gain permission to enter the corresponding difficulty. Even if the conditions for entering the next difficulty are met, only the leader gains the corresponding qualification. 2. All dungeons have appropriate levels. If the dungeon level differs too much from the player's level, the player cannot gain basic experience. Therefore, please choose dungeons more suitable for your level. 3. In high-difficulty dungeons, the number of monsters increases significantly. It is recommended that players form teams to challenge them and enjoy the pleasure of combat more smoothly. Four, Key Points Review: Enter dungeons through the red entrances on the wild maps. Often, the same entrance leads to multiple dungeons, so pay attention to selecting the dungeon you wish to enter when entering. A clearance screen will appear after defeating the Boss. Exiting the dungeon in any other way before this will result in punishment. Many hints about the current scene can be obtained through the mini-map in the center of the interface. The team leader determines the difficulty of the dungeon the team can enter. Be sure to form a team to challenge the high-difficulty mode of the dungeon.
