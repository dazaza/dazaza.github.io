Vishay 2010 New Product Launch Held Grandly

by ditan360 on 2010-07-07 11:39:32

Exclusive to Low Carbon Network, Vishay (Weishi) held a media event titled "Integrate Vishay into Your Designs - Super12 Series Products" at the Beijing Art Garden Crown Plaza Hotel on July 6, 2010. More than 30 industry media outlets attended.

Mr. Gu Zhiming, Senior Vice President of Sales for Asia at Vishay (Weishi)

Mr. Gu Zhiming, Senior Vice President of Sales for Asia at Vishay (Weishi), gave an overview of the company's development. He introduced that in the future, Vishay (Weishi) will complement its product lines with a complete product portfolio, contributing over 40% to the gross margin. Additionally, acquisitions will be made in the passive components and discrete semiconductor fields, strengthening and expanding Vishay (Weishi)'s position as a supplier of specialized products, increasing market share.

Mr. Craig Hunter, Global Online Marketing Director of the Market Communication Department at Vishay (Weishi)

Mr. Craig Hunter, Global Online Marketing Director of the Market Communication Department at Vishay (Weishi), provided a detailed introduction to the technical advantages of the Super12 product series. The "Super12" series mainly includes six semiconductor and six passive components, which are innovative products launched based on customer demand for recent equipment trends and potential markets. Mr. Craig Hunter stated that in 2011, Vishay (Weishi) will launch a different "Super12" from this year, which will include six semiconductor chips and six passive components, aiming to maintain leading technology in the industry, assist customers in achieving success, and achieve mutual benefits.

Mr. Yang Yizhang, Director of Market Development and Technical Applications in Asia at Vishay Weishi

Mr. Yang Yizhang, Director of Market Development and Technical Applications in Asia at Vishay Weishi, expressed that the "Super12" product series is a selection of highly competitive products from among tens of thousands of products offered by Vishay Weishi. It has positive layouts and performances in four emerging application areas: automotive electronics, clean energy, LED lighting, and portable electronic products.

Mr. Yang Yizhang emphasized that the markets for new energy vehicles and clean energy in China are vast. As a global company, Weishi's entry into the Chinese market has profound strategic significance. Vishay Weishi hopes to establish cooperation with more energy-saving and environmentally friendly enterprises in China to promote the development of the global energy-saving and environmental protection field.

Introduction to Vishay (Weishi)

Vishay (Weishi), as a global technology leader in the fields of discrete semiconductors and passive components, has achieved excellent performance growth through acquisitions, strong cash flow, and flexible financial management, providing sufficient funds for R&D investment and robust logistical support. This enables the continuous launch of customer-driven solutions required for growth and the realization of one-stop services.

Vishay (Weishi) offers electronic devices needed for various fields such as industry, computing, automotive, consumer goods, telecommunications, military, aviation, and medical care. With a global footprint, all Vishay companies worldwide consistently adhere to the principle of serving customers, continuously innovating and consolidating, which is the inexhaustible driving force for Vishay's continuous development.
