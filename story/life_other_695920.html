The "Flower and Bird Striving for Beauty Office Desk" by Huaming Huaju has been awarded the Fujian Famous Brand Product.

by mumu321 on 2012-03-07 17:45:33

Recently, the People's Government of Fujian Province conferred the title of "Fujian Famous Brand Product 2011" on the product "Hua Ming Hua Ju + Birds and Flowers Striving for Beauty Office Desk" produced by Fujian Huamin Classical Art Furniture Co., Ltd.

Fujian Famous Brand Products refer to products whose physical quality reaches the advanced level of domestic similar products, are in a leading position among similar products in the province, have high market share and fame ranking at the forefront of the industry, enjoy high user satisfaction, and possess strong market competitiveness. The validity period is three years. A total of 555 products were selected this time. Enterprises strictly followed the evaluation requirements in the "Management Measures for Fujian Famous Brand Products". After being reviewed and recommended by various designated cities, the Fujian Famous Brand Products Evaluation Work Committee finally determined the products after publicizing major economic indicators, social satisfaction measurement, product quality supervision spot checks, expert reviews, and the review by the Fujian Famous Brand Products Evaluation Work Committee. This is a comprehensive assessment of enterprise market recognition, customer satisfaction, and enterprise economic benefits.

Hua Ming Hua Ju strives for the brand name to hold itself to a higher standard, to compete in the market with high-quality, high-standard products, and to give back to society with greater benefits. Hua Ming Hua Ju winning the title of "Fujian Famous Brand Product" is the result of all employees creating famous brand products as their main consciousness, continuously strengthening management foundations, enhancing technical levels, and focusing on research and development innovation. This is another new milestone, a higher standard, and a new image. The company will continue to take "quality first" as its guiding ideology and fully leverage the advantages of the brand.

Hua Ming Hua Ju will continue to adhere to serving customers with high-quality products, innovating in development, and striving to create brands in the redwood furniture industry and enterprises. This selection is an affirmation of Hua Ming Hua Ju's long-term development philosophy, and it will also promote Hua Ming Hua Ju to continue strengthening enterprise quality management and product development, improving the after-sales service system, shaping a good social image for the enterprise, standing firm in Fujian, facing the whole country, and expanding broader market space. (Editor: Tiantian)
