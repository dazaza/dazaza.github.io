Car Beauty and Maintenance Knowledge

Everyone who drives will find: the fine ash that falls on the foot mat, the patches of stains on the surface of the seat, the worn-out shoe prints on the lower side of the inner door panel, and the dusty dashboard and roof. How to solve the cleaning and maintenance of car interior? Beauty experts point out that the cleaning and maintenance of the car interior is not an easy task, so it is necessary to pay attention to the daily cleaning inside the car and go to a professional shop regularly for interior maintenance.

Professional Cleaning and Maintenance

If you are a busy car owner, then all the daily maintenance introduced above can be taken care of by a professional car beauty shop. Professional interior maintenance includes three parts: full car interior cleaning, leather protection, and steam sterilization.

Full Car Interior Cleaning: This strictly follows the three steps of dust removal, cleaning, and maintenance to thoroughly clean and comprehensively maintain the dashboard control panel, roof, rear cylinder platform, seats, floor carpet, and inner door panels. The entire process is operated by four staff members and takes about two and a half hours. In the cleaning process, neutral or weakly acidic cleaning agents are used, whose residues do not produce harmful gases and automatically evaporate within a few seconds, ensuring safety and environmental protection without causing any harm to human health.

Leather Protection: Professional car beauty shops are equipped with complete sets of leather care tools, selecting different tools for different parts.

In addition, professional leather protection has certain requirements for the dosage of cleaning agents and maintenance agents because excessive liquid agents will remain on the leather surface and absorb dust from the air, polluting the car's interior environment.

Steam Sterilization: Professional steam sterilization not only performs high-temperature sterilization of the car's air but also focuses on sterilizing areas such as air conditioning vents, seats, and carpets where dust and bacteria tend to accumulate. This ensures the complete elimination of invisible mites, mold, and microorganisms, protecting the health of your family and yourself. At the same time, it does not damage the car's interior decoration nor cause secondary pollution to the environment.

Dashboard Control Panel: The dashboard control panel is the easiest place to accumulate dust and has many blind spots. When the car owner cleans it themselves, they can use a brush to brush the dashboard, air conditioning inlets, switches, and buttons daily to prevent dust accumulation that is difficult to remove. If the dashboard control panel is relatively dirty, a special dashboard cleaner should be sprayed, and wiped clean with a soft cloth. After cleaning, a layer of dashboard wax can be sprayed.

Ceiling: The ceiling is a place that is often overlooked, and once cleaned, there will inevitably be liquid residue dripping onto the seats or carpets.

Experts suggest: Normally, car owners can use a car vacuum cleaner to remove dust. When doing a comprehensive interior maintenance at the car beauty shop, a thorough cleaning of the ceiling can be performed.

Rear Cylinder Platform: We usually pile up tissue boxes, dolls, cushions, and other miscellaneous items on the rear cylinder platform. Tissues and cushions are frequently used by people sitting in the car, so the cleaning and dusting of the rear cylinder platform must not be neglected. Its cleaning method can refer to the dashboard control panel.

Seats: Seats are the most touched and easily dirtied interior parts, so maintaining the seats,

First, add fabric seat covers outside the seats and have another set ready for replacement and washing.

Second, try to avoid food or liquids spilling on the seats, especially alcohol-based or perfume-based items. Once they get on, if they are not cleaned within 12 hours, these stains will be hard to remove.

Third, try to avoid scratching the leather surface with metal objects.

Fourth, regularly use leather cleaning agents and maintenance agents for cleaning and maintenance to prevent leather aging, fading, and cracking.

Experts suggest: Every six months, the leather decorative parts inside the car need to be cared for.

Floor Carpet: When the floor carpet gets dirty, it is difficult to clean due to its inability to be removed from the car, so most car owners choose to go to a professional car beauty shop for cleaning, and normally lay a foot mat on the floor carpet for easy daily cleaning. After washing the foot mat, squeeze out the water. Professional car beauty shops have dedicated pad washing machines, making the cleaning of foot mats quick and clean.

Inner Door Panels: The main stains on the car's inner door panels are shoe prints, mainly concentrated on the lower edge of the door panel. Attention should be paid to this area regularly, and frequent cleaning is recommended. Car owners can use professional foam cleaners for cleaning, and then gently wipe with a clean soft cloth.
