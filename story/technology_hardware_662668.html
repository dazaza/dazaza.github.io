No one discovered this little episode.

by chao7wudi7 on 2012-02-10 15:13:39

Meeting Su Jijie, the latest update of the genius doctor, was on the day before my brother went abroad for further study. A party was held to see him off. But I still can't figure out why I was involved. Maybe it's destiny that made me meet him. Su Jiejie wore a dazzling plaid shirt, which made me notice him at a glance among the crowd. He seemed very good at telling jokes and quickly livened up the atmosphere. However, this didn't suit me. The surrounding atmosphere pressed me so much that I couldn't breathe. I wanted to go out for a walk. I took a deep breath and saw him. His expression was very rogue-like. He said to me: "I like you." I was stunned and replied: "We don't even know each other, how could you say 'like'?" He showed a carefree smile: "People always start from not knowing each other to knowing each other. Honey." "Who is your honey?" I sighed. Anyway, we won't meet again, so what's wrong with letting him call me that. But I didn't know that he would appear in my sight again, and again. I looked up at him. The light here wasn't good, so I didn't notice that he was an extremely handsome guy. His chestnut hair was lazily draped on his head, his bangs covering his eyebrows, revealing a pair of bewitching eyes, a high nose, and those bewitching eyes paired with his bewitching smile looked surprisingly harmonious. Plus, his perfect face shape... "Hey, honey. Drooling." I unconsciously touched my chin. His crooked smile turned into a big laugh, then into a wild laugh. I glared at him, feeling the heat on my face, probably red all over. Turning around, I ran to the private room where my brother was. The dimness of the private room indeed concealed my blushing face. My brother asked me with a bit of humor: "What's wrong? Your face is so red." I stammered: "Uh... nothing, it's just the light." This inexplicable party ended in an inexplicable way, all mixed up. No one noticed this little episode. The next day, the heavy fog reminded me of a funeral. "Goodbye, brother." I met him again. He laughed and said: "Honey! Honey!" I gave him a glare and said: "Who is your honey." After he came, the atmosphere seemed to ease. How dare I think about him! I slightly despised myself in my heart. It was time to board the plane. The handsome guy who called himself Ah Hui kept muttering. We waved goodbye with a smile, but there were hidden tears in our eyes. My brother always sneaked into my room on stormy nights because he knew I would be afraid. Then he would chat with me about something or other, just so I wouldn't feel lonely. Finally, he would give me a goodnight kiss on my forehead and hold me tightly, just so I wouldn't feel cold until I fell asleep, then he would leave.
