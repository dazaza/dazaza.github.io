TOP 10 Smartphones of 2010 (US version)

by cacard on 2010-06-01 14:43:50

Although a new version of Apple's iPhone will be released in the middle of the year, this article will make a top 10 selection based on the phones that are already on the market.

1. Sprint HTC EVO 4G

None of the smartphones are as big as the Sprint HTC EVO 4G, and reviews so far have been pretty good. The specs of this superphone include a 4.3-inch 480x800 display, 1 GHz Snapdragon processor, 4G, and Wi-Fi sharing network for up to eight devices, an 8 megapixel camera, 720p HD camera, HDMI video output, video phone function, and more. Google Android version 2.1, and HTC Sense.

2. Apple iPhone 3GS

Although it has been on the market for almost a year, it is still a smart phone that sells well. Apple has already announced that it will update the iPhone 4 OS this summer, which will bring some new features to the iPhone 3GS.

The specs aren't all there is, but there are some benchmarks. The iPhone uses an ARM Cortex A8 600 MHz processor with PowerVR graphics, 16 or 32 GB of internal storage, a 3 megapixel camera, and a 3.5-inch 320x480 display. The iPhone 3GS isn't the highest spec, but it's pretty fast, and the photo/video quality is pretty good.

Third place: Nokia N8

It's not easy to find a carrier in the United States to support Nokia phones, but the upcoming Nokia N8 is the world's first 5-band 3G phone, which is a real world phone with pretty good specifications.

The N8 is the first phone to use the Symbian^3 operating system, and although the interface looks similar to the S60 and Maemo, there are considerable improvements inside.

Phone specifications include 5-band 3G support, 3.5-inch 640x360 resolution OLED display, 720p camera function, 12 megapixel Carl Zeiss lens, built-in 16GB memory, support for microSD card, HDMI interface, USB, FM radio, 680 MHz processor, USB On-The-Go, can be directly inserted into the USB device, no computer required.

Fourth place: HTC Droid Incredible

It is one of the hottest mobile phones in the United States, the author used it for a while, and found that the speed is really super fast, and it is the fastest Google Android mobile phone at present, but the battery life is poor, but this may be the relationship that everyone loves to use.

The HTC Droid Incredible specs include a 3.7-inch 480x800 WVGA AMOLED display, an 8-megapixel camera, Android 2.1, a 1 GHz Snapdragon processor, 8GB of RAM, and support for microSD cards.
