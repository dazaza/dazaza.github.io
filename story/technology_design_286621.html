Why is Li Yapeng sensitive to Wang Fei's comeback?

by 125eggdgf on 2009-11-30 16:32:55

The 2009 Yanran Angel Fund Charity Dinner press conference was held yesterday (the 25th). The couple, Chow Yun Fat and his wife were invited to attend with the fund initiator, Li Yapeng. From start to finish, Brother and Sister-in-law Chow faced the media with a relaxed smile, answering all questions confidently and freely, from their promise to do their best for charity to expressing their desire to cooperate with Faye Wong. However, after being asked about whether Faye Wong would appear on the Spring Festival Gala or not, Li Yapeng's emotions escalated from slight embarrassment to anger, and he publicly scolded the reporter: "Shut up! Shut up!"

Why is Li Yapeng so sensitive when it comes to Faye Wong's comeback issue?

First, let's review the situation at the dinner. The reason Li Yapeng became so angry might have been caused by Brother Chow (Chow Yun Fat). Brother and Sister-in-law Chow took over from Maggie Cheung this year as the new fund coordinators. Not only will they donate their own photography works for charity auction, but yesterday they also visited cleft lip and palate children undergoing treatment together with Li Yapeng. Brother Chow's quick agreement couldn't help but make people think that it was due to Faye Wong's face. Brother Chow responded semi-seriously and semi-jokingly, saying, "That's why she didn't show up today, I'm a bit upset, she tricked me here, Kiehls, and she still didn't come." Sitting beside him, Li Yapeng quickly explained to Brother Chow, saying, "I am also a staff member on site today, but we will privately invite you and Sister-in-law Chow for dinner."

Brother Chow always reveals his true feelings in public, laughing and joking, mixing truth with fiction. When asked by the media about his impression of Li Yapeng before, Brother Chow laughed and said, "We just met each other 30 minutes ago, and I only knew him after he married Faye Wong. Oh, but he seems to have beaten reporters in Hong Kong before, it appears that his relationship with the media isn't good, but it might be because the weather in Hong Kong is too dry." This made the reporters below burst into laughter. It's unknown whether it was because Brother Chow said he knew him through Faye Wong, but at this moment, Li Yapeng sitting beside seemed somewhat embarrassed. When mentioning that Faye Wong once said that Chow Yun Fat was her idol, Brother Chow didn't avoid the topic, laughing and saying, "Faye Wong is also my idol, I hope one day she can act in a movie with me, preferably without Li Yapeng participating." It's unknown whether it was because Faye Wong had mentioned too much, Li Yapeng signaled the host to stop the reporter's question, and at this moment, a reporter asked if the news of "Faye Wong appearing on the Spring Festival Gala" was true, Li Yapeng directly picked up the microphone and scolded, "Shut up! Shut up!" causing a stir in the entire venue. As soon as the words left his mouth, Li Yapeng turned around and left the scene, leaving Brother and Sister-in-law Chow to hurriedly follow.

Ha ha, it turns out Li Yapeng was jealous. Love is selfish, real love and concern for someone leads to jealousy, so naturally, when others touch what he loves most, he becomes jealous! Li Yapeng has always been a macho man, wishing to keep Faye Wong hidden away every day, not letting other men see her, so it's natural that Brother Chow wants to act with Faye Wong and doesn't want Li Yapeng to participate, making him angry.

Secondly, Li Yapeng sincerely does charity work, the "Yanran Charity Foundation" was initiated by Li Yapeng, and he was the host of this banquet. During the press conference, Li Yapeng was absolutely cooperative with all charity-related questions, answering everything freely. If reporters hadn't always asked questions unrelated to charity, it's believed that Li Yapeng wouldn't have gotten so angry. Of course, Li Yapeng's anger was greatly related to Brother Chow bringing up his past of beating reporters in Hong Kong. As the saying goes, "Don't hit the face when hitting a person, don't reveal the shortcoming when revealing a person," men's face issues are still very important.

Thirdly, Li Yapeng doesn't want to be seen as someone who eats soft rice. Brother Chow's meaning was that he knew Li Yapeng because of Faye Wong, which shows how big Faye Wong's fame is, giving Li Yapeng quite a bit of pressure. When Faye Wong shot advertisements and made a comeback, some people said Li Yapeng was eating soft rice. This is understandable, Li Yapeng has been acting for many years but has never stood out. Although personal effort is important in art, talent is more crucial. Li Yapeng may realize that he isn't cut out for acting, so he started a business, founded a film and television company, and established the "Yanran Charity Foundation". If Li Yapeng wants to be taken seriously by others, he needs to continue working hard and achieve a remarkable career, then naturally his backbone will straighten, and he won't care what others say. Finally, it's hoped that Li Yapeng remains reserved and cultivated, improving his relationship with the media and reporters, after all, the media and reporters are also his bread and butter.
