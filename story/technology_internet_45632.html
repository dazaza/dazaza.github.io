The world-leading security vendor Kaspersky announced today the launch of "Ruthless Blizzard, Compassionate Kaspersky" - a large-scale disaster relief campaign giving away free antivirus software for one year. Starting from January 29, 2008, Kaspersky will give away activation codes for the one-year version of Kaspersky Anti-Virus (KAV) 7.0 via the internet. Now, consumers just need to register as members of the official Kaspersky technical support forum - the Kaspersky Family - to obtain the activation code for the one-year version of Kaspersky Anti-Virus (KAV) 7.0 for free.

Mr. Zhang Lishen, Managing Director of Kaspersky Asia-Pacific, stated that this activity aims to respond to the disaster relief actions across society, meeting the urgent needs of users affected by the recent catastrophic snowy weather, ensuring that all consumers can spend a peaceful and virus-free Spring Festival.

Since mid-January, the central and eastern regions of our country have experienced continuous large-scale catastrophic snowy weather, causing extremely severe impacts on transportation, energy communication, and people's daily lives and production. This large-scale catastrophic weather has also severely impacted the transportation of Kaspersky products, leaving a large number of products stranded in transit, making it impossible for many users in disaster-affected areas to obtain Kaspersky products in time. The Spring Festival period is often a high incidence period for viruses, and computers without antivirus software protection pose significant safety hazards. To meet the urgent needs of a wide range of users, Kaspersky decided to quickly deliver Kaspersky products to consumers via the internet, allowing a large number of consumers to spend a safe and virus-free peaceful Spring Festival. According to relevant personnel at Kaspersky, Kaspersky will decide the duration of this large-scale disaster relief activity based on the specific development of the disaster situation, ensuring that all consumers can quickly and conveniently obtain Kaspersky products.

This disaster relief activity is another large-scale mobilization by Kaspersky after the 2007 earthquake caused the submarine cable breakage and emergency services were launched, providing free KEYS to netizens. Both its timely response efficiency and large-scale investment determination reflect Kaspersky's core concept of "everything for user security." As Mr. Zhang Lishen, Managing Director of Kaspersky Asia-Pacific, said: "Thinking about what consumers think, providing security for all consumers is Kaspersky's unwavering goal. Disaster situations are orders. When consumers need us, we are willing to share our technology and resources, creating a safe and harmonious year for users."

Note: The Kaspersky activation code given away during this event must be activated within 14 days!
