Another step forward for environmental protection: investment implements environmental access system

by guard17u on 2009-11-19 15:36:06

Another step forward for environmental protection: investment implements environmental access system. Yesterday, Chongming signed 6 projects with a total investment exceeding 8 billion yuan at the same time with CSR Jiangnan Heavy Industry, Greenland Group, Bailian Group, Baosteel Group and others, while launching 6 key recruitment sectors. After the opening of the Yangtze River Tunnel Bridge, Chongming's tourism first saw an explosive growth, and the past difficulties in attracting investment also showed significant changes. "Some investment companies that were hesitant before the opening of the Yangtze River Tunnel Bridge have recently determined their investment intentions," said a Chongming leader responsible for investment promotion. With the tourism boom and the possible future investment boom, Chongming's Hanting Express Hotel has also drawn attention.

Chongming promotes six key sectors for investment attraction

Yesterday, Chongming held a large-scale investment promotion conference. While signing contracts with CSR Jiangnan Heavy Industry, Greenland Group, Bailian Group, Baosteel Group, Xian Da College of Shanghai International Studies University, and Canada Harmony Organic Agriculture Park with a total investment of more than 8 billion yuan, Chongming launched six key sectors for investment attraction, mainly involving industry, production services, modern services, agricultural product deep processing, etc.

The six sectors include: Shanghai Changxing Marine Equipment Industry Base Investment Project; Shanghai Chenjia Town Modern Service Industry Agglomeration Area Project (i.e., International Forum Business District); Shanghai Chongming Industrial Park Production Service Industry Area Project; Chongming Modern Agricultural Park Green Food Processing Demonstration Base Six Agricultural Product Deep Processing Projects; Four plots of Chongming Hanting Hotel and Linzhongyuan Hotel Project; Chongming New City Commercial Service Area Project, introducing real estate projects in appropriate amounts.

Strict implementation of environmental access during investment attraction

Yesterday, Vice County Magistrate Li Zhihong, who is in charge of investment attraction in Chongming County, told reporters: "Before the opening of the Yangtze River Tunnel Bridge, some enterprises were still hesitant about investing in Chongming, but now they have all determined their investment intentions." However, Li Zhihong indicated that during the process of attracting investment, Chongming would strictly screen incoming projects, implement strict environmental access, and eliminate or transfer polluting enterprises on the island through technical transformation and other methods.

Vice Mayor Ai Baojun also stated that during the process of attracting investment, Chongming should place ecological protection in a priority position and strive to blaze a new trail in developing a low-carbon economy.

Jinmao Group will build a five-star hotel in the Chenjia Town area

Yesterday, Cheng Erjun, vice president of Jinmao Group, revealed that Jinmao Group will create a comprehensive community in the Chenjia Town area of Chongming Island, integrating a five-star hotel, outdoor ball sports leisure venue, low-density ecological villa area, etc. Currently, the project has signed contracts with internationally renowned planning and architectural enterprises, and the brand of the five-star hotel has been determined, striving to complete this comprehensive community by 2011.

It is understood that the Chenjia Town area is a key development area for Chongming in the future, which will build nine ecological functional areas: Riverside Sports Park, International Forum Business District, Experimental Ecological Community, Yulan New Rural Community, Hanting Express, Wetland Tourist Area, Ecological Agriculture Demonstration Area, Green Industry Area, and Education R&D Area.

Reporters learned that last year, the municipal government organized more than ten experts from different fields to formulate construction indicators for Chongming's economy, society, management, environment, and ecology, including five categories and 24 indicators. During the topic research process, experts referred to the construction of Prince Edward Island in Canada, Long Island in New York, USA, Jeju Island in Korea, and Hainan Island in China.
