The Development Prospects of Automotive Beauty Products

In recent years, the automobile sales have maintained a high-speed growth. The popularity of car sales has caused the market for automotive beauty products and car maintenance products to continuously heat up. However, due to the relatively low entry threshold and lack of industry standards in the automotive beauty products sector, the industry suffers from severe disorderly competition, which hinders its healthy development. Therefore, how to effectively resolve the contradictions encountered in the development of automotive beauty products has become an issue that must be considered.

According to staff members from the business registration department of the Administration for Industry and Commerce, with the rapid development of the automotive beauty industry, numerous car beauty shops have emerged. Up to now, the number of registered car beauty shops at the administration department has increased nearly 100 times compared to five years ago. Due to the relatively low entry threshold, some small shops engage in fierce "price wars" just to survive, leading to a rampant spread of counterfeit car decoration products. Since it is difficult to distinguish the authenticity of some car beauty products, these small shops can pass off fake products as genuine ones to lower prices, but this also compromises product quality. Why does such a situation occur in the car beauty products market? According to the person in charge of the Jinan Guipai Automotive Products Wholesale Network in Shandong Province, the lack of industry standards is one of the main reasons causing disorderly competition in the car beauty market. Take silk seat pads for example; some are made entirely of silk, others contain 50%, and some only contain 20%. So, what percentage of silk content qualifies a product to be called a silk seat pad? There has never been any relevant standard. The absence of industry standards not only leads to the proliferation of counterfeit and substandard car beauty products but also plunges this industry into a vicious cycle of low-price competition.

Therefore, in order to promote the healthy development of the automotive beauty products industry, the automotive beauty and car maintenance products markets should establish relevant laws and regulations to improve industry order. Relevant departments should focus on supporting larger-scale merchants and severely crack down on counterfeit and substandard products.
