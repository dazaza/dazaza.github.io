The Housing Authority's Report on the Implementation of Party Conduct and Clean Government Accountability

The City Commission for Discipline Inspection:

Since 2008, under the correct leadership of the municipal Party committee and the City Commission for Discipline Inspection, our bureau’s leading Party group has always placed the construction of a clean and honest administration as an important part of party work. In accordance with Haiyang City's 2008 target assessment opinions and the accountability assessment rules for section-level leading groups regarding Party conduct and clean government, we have comprehensively deployed and fulfilled all requirements. Each task has achieved significant results. I will now report on the fulfillment of the 2008 clean government construction within our bureau as follows:

**One, Strengthening Organization and Leadership, Enhancing Responsibility Implementation**

Throughout the year, our bureau's Party conduct and clean government construction has consistently adhered to the unified leadership of the leading Party group, with joint efforts from the Party and administrative departments, organizational coordination by the Commission for Discipline Inspection, reliance on the masses, support and participation from all levels, and the principle of 'whoever is in charge is responsible'. We have conscientiously improved organization and strengthened responsibility implementation.

1. Formed a leading group for Party conduct and clean government construction and style building; clarified responsibilities.

- Established the secretary of the leading Party group as the first responsible person for Party conduct and clean government construction.

- The Party secretary is responsible for leading the entire cadre team in Party conduct and clean government construction, bearing leadership responsibility in education, propaganda, and ideological and political aspects.

- Responsible for the duties of the leading Party group secretary in clean government construction.

- Section-level cadres and Party secretaries, along with middle-level cadres and leaders, signed responsibility books for Party conduct and clean government, forming a top-down network of clean government construction where each layer is accountable.

2. Established a leading group for style transformation activities headed by the Secretary of each group leader and deputy director, with all members responsible for organizing these activities. An office was also established to ensure specific personnel were responsible for daily affairs. Combined with the actual conditions of the housing bureau and industry characteristics, we formulated the Housing Authority's opinion on the activity to transform styles. Clarified the guiding ideology and overall working goals of the activities, identified key areas for rectification and problems to solve, and outlined methods, steps, and concrete measures for the work.

**Two, Paying Close Attention to the Construction of Leading Cadres' Eight Fulfillments, Strengthening Conduct and Discipline Education, Stimulating Leaders' Self-discipline**

Implemented the requirements of studiousness and learning for practice, strengthening the education of leading cadres. The Party and government leadership conducted serious central group studies, improving the study systems of each center group; formulated center group learning plans; studied the Party Constitution, the Communist Party of China Disciplinary Regulations, the scientific outlook on development, the CPC Central Committee's documents on strengthening the ruling ability construction of the party, the Eight Honors and Eight Disgraces, and the 17th National Congress of the CPC document compilation. Combining self-study with centralized study, reading activities were initiated within the leading group. This enhanced the theoretical level of leading cadres, changed their ideas, grasped opportunities, and strengthened competitive awareness, market awareness, service awareness, responsibility awareness, and innovation awareness.

At the same time, leading cadres fully understood the importance, necessity, and urgency of Party conduct and clean government construction, seriously implemented the principle of who is responsible for executives, and built specific divisions of tasks for Party conduct and clean government construction to fulfill. Insisted on intra-party democracy, giving full play to collective wisdom. Our bureau's leading group established pragmatic solidarity, clean-fingered diligent politics, development innovation, mass trust, and established and perfected various supervisory assessment mechanisms. The main Party member cadre team took the lead, maintaining high agreement with the Party Central Committee in thought and action. In daily work, they strictly followed the requirements of Party conduct and clean government construction to regulate their own behavior, strictly observing Party discipline and state laws.

Adhered to the principle that important matters, amounts of expenses, major equipment purchases, and major personnel matters must be collectively discussed and decided upon by the leadership, enhancing transparency. Economic activities were strictly audited beforehand, supervised during, and audited afterward. Our bureau's Party conduct and clean government construction work became standardized and institutionalized. Advocated not doing what one says arbitrarily. Pioneering, pragmatic, strict with oneself; divisional deputy chiefs cooperated in division of labor, worked together with one heart, forming a collective leadership that enjoys high prestige among the general cadre and workers. Globally, there were no cases of violation, such as illegal entertainment expenses or equipping with various communication tools. Strengthened discipline inspection work and improved supervision measures.

In accordance with relevant regulations and requirements, we improved and perfected the cadre supervision system, strictly regulating leading cadres' honest administration behavior. Improved work systems, financial reimbursement systems, authority and other internal audit systems, strictly implemented leading cadres' reporting systems for important matters, income declaration systems, and gradually improved the leaders' conversation systems, further promoting the cadres' economic responsibility auditing system, strengthening leadership and cadre supervision and organizational measures. Since this year, team members did not produce any disciplinary violations, overall no significant events and major incidents occurred, no pressure led to unchecked situations, checked but not reported or evaded issues. At the same time, strengthened the work of complaint letters and visits, ensuring no superfluous visit events occurred.

**Three, Extensive Conduct Propaganda Education, Improving System Measures, Actively Building Clean Government Culture Construction**

According to the special arrangements by the municipal Party committee, city hall, and the Commission for Discipline Inspection regarding Party conduct and clean government and anti-corruption work, and through many group meetings on Party conduct and clean government construction, we undertook specialized subject conferences for deployment, making the construction of a clean government and administrative work the same arrangement, examination, unity.

Our bureau actively participated in the construction of a clean government culture, held television education activities, where all Party member cadres regularly watched anti-corruption audio-visual pieces; organized all cadre workers to watch educational films, extensively conducted propaganda, justice, wholeheartedly serving the people's good judge, Comrade Song Yushui, president of the Intellectual Property Tribunal of Beijing Haidian District People's Court's advanced achievements. Studying Comrade Song Yushui's lofty spirit, organizing watching minimalist as studying and carrying out the spirit of the 17th Party Congress and the construction of a socialist harmonious society, further consolidating and expanding to maintain a communist advanced sex teaching activity achievements, promoting the socialist concept of honor and disgrace education activities and socialist legal concept and education activities as important content by holding movie forums, further studying Comrade Song Yushui's activities, solidly doing work for housing construction, providing a strong guarantee for socialist society.

Organized workers to participate in 'Do the Asian Sand, Civilization Do I Do' law knowledge contests, among fifty-one entrants, finished fifteenth, and won the organizational award; the team attended the Royal Garden Cup workers track and field sports, ranking sixth among thirty-three units in the city, showcasing the vast cadre workers of the housing system.
