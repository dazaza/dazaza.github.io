Teach you two tricks to avoid having a small belly

by slim5com23 on 2010-04-20 15:56:57

After the craziness, my belly expanded several times, and the bloated feeling was indeed uncomfortable. To regain my previous good figure, I still need to do more abdominal exercises, so that the lines will reappear. Doing the following exercises 1-2 times a day will make the small belly quickly disappear.

Snake Stretching Pose

Action ——

Please lie face down and relax your whole body;

Turn your head and place your chin on the mat;

Extend both arms towards the back of your body, palms facing each other, interlock your fingers behind your back with your arms straight;

Take a deep breath, lift your head and shoulders, try to raise your upper body as much as possible, stretch your arms back to lift your body to the limit, expand your shoulders and chest as much as possible, lift your head, look up, and maintain natural even breathing;

Exhale, slowly return your upper body to the ground, lightly touch the mat with your chin, and extend your arms straight behind you;

Take another deep breath, lift your head and shoulders again, and try to raise your upper body to the limit, hold your breath;

Exhale, slowly lower your body back to the ground, return your arms to your sides, and place your chin on the mat.

Turn your head and place either side of your chest on the mat, relax your whole body.

Maintain for 10-15 seconds, repeat 3 times.

Benefits ——

1、Beautifies the neck, prevents cervical spondylosis

2、Strengthens wrist strength, makes arms firmer

3、Softens the spine, boosts energy, can prevent various back pain and spinal injuries

4、Tightens waist and abdominal muscles, beautifies buttocks and leg lines

5、Massages internal organs, nourishes kidneys and detoxifies, aids digestion, relieves constipation, regulates endocrine

Latch Pose

Action ——

1、Kneel on the ground with knees together, let your arms hang naturally, fingertips pointing down, eyes looking straight ahead.

2、Stretch your right leg out to the right, foot sole on the ground, foot stretched, raise both arms, parallel to the ground, palms facing down.

3、Exhale, bend your upper trunk and right arm to the right, place your right palm on your right foot, palm facing down, left arm vertical to the ground, eyes looking at the tip of the left finger.

4、Continue exhaling, lower your left arm to the right, overlap your left palm with the right hand, left upper arm touching the ear, head between the two arms. Maintain this posture, breathe 3—6 times, inhale, return to the original position. Do the same exercise on the other side, repeat 3—6 times on the left and right.

Benefits ——

1、Eliminates fatigue and back pain caused by nervous tension.

2、Activates spinal nerves, eliminates stiffness in the back.

3、Strengthens abdominal muscles and organs.

4、Eliminates excess fat around the waist.
