Quanzhou Bodafa Proctology Hospital has invested heavily in introducing the Johnson & Johnson PPH (Procedure for Prolapse and Hemorrhoids) surgery from the USA, which is currently the most popular international method for treating hemorrhoids.

The mechanism of the PPH surgical method involves a circumferential resection of the mucosa and submucosal tissue of the distal rectal wall just above the prolapsed internal hemorrhoids. During the resection, the mucosa at both ends is sutured together, lifting and pulling up the prolapsed internal hemorrhoids and mucosa so that they no longer prolapse. At the same time, the arteries supplying blood to the hemorrhoids from the superior rectal artery in the submucosal layer are cut off, reducing blood supply to the hemorrhoids after the operation. The PPH hemorrhoid surgery is short in duration, reduces postoperative complications, completely removes hemorrhoids in one go, and prevents recurrence.
