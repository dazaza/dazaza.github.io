I look forward to growing up, just as I desire to be kept.

I look forward to growing up, and when I grow up, I will become an urban manager, getting rid of the halo under the shadow of this damned dynasty. I know that street vendors will run away when they see me, but at least I will chase them very slowly.

I look forward to growing up, and when I grow up, I will be a real estate boss. That way, my father won't have to work hard to earn a meager salary, and pay the damn house loan every month.

I look forward to growing up, and when I grow up, I will be a chef. I will not add carcinogenic chromatin to my dough, nor use gutter oil even if it means losing money, so as to prevent my heart from being corroded.

I look forward to growing up, and when I grow up, I will be a driver. I will refuse drunk driving, not touch alcohol, obey rules and regulations, and if there is a car accident, I will definitely not chop down the aunt who was injured.

I look forward to growing up, and when I grow up, I will do engineering design. There will be no more famous projects like the Three Gorges Dam, but only for the common people not to be forced to leave their homes and still suffer from droughts and floods.

I look forward to growing up, and when I grow up, I will be the director of public security in Baoding North District. Damn Li Gang, let you torture under pressure, let your son drive recklessly at Hebei University.

I look forward to growing up, and when I grow up, I will be a doctor. Whoever gives me a bribe, I will leave the scalpel in his body.

I look forward to growing up, and when I grow up, I will drive the subway. Special seats are set aside for migrant workers to rest, and whoever dares to sit on it, I will make him kneel down and call me grandpa.

I look forward to growing up, and when I grow up, I will be a director. If there are any unwritten rules, let her go develop in Japan, and a magnitude 9 earthquake will be your damn karma.

I look forward to growing up, and when I grow up, I will study abroad in Brazil. Get rid of this damned Heavenly Kingdom, buy a hundred square meters of land for three thousand yuan, and watch the property price control in China with a dazed smile.

In the exam room, we are merely "expected to grow up". Only Tianchao brand milk powder and Zhongguo brand milk expect us to grow up and mature early. But people always look forward to something, always grow up. We look forward to salary, status, and the healthy growth of the next generation, rather than the crazy rise in prices and housing prices.

Three years ago, I did indeed look forward to growing up, now that I've grown up, I start looking forward to death!
