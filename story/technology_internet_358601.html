"Low-carbon" measures are art in themselves

by ditan360 on 2010-06-21 10:49:29

Mr. Chen Yansheng, a famous low-carbon artist

Exclusive article of Low Carbon Network: Interview with Mr. Chen Yansheng, a famous low-carbon artist.

The low-carbon and environmental protection of the soul is the intrinsic basis for human beings to enter a harmonious and beautiful life. In today's society, the values of the human group are all distorted, and the crazy material desire leads to a certain collective moral loss, which is a manifestation of lack of faith. Low-carbon art is a spiritual pursuit at a higher level than life and art. The development of low-carbon art is particularly urgent in today's society. To eliminate the杂 thoughts and evil thoughts in the human heart, and establish a sacred and noble faith, so as to achieve the low-carbon and environmental protection of the human heart, so that everything can develop normally under the guidance of environmental protection and health. This is the heavy responsibility of contemporary artists.

A good artist is not only a master of using the pen, but also a master of purifying the soul.

Reporter: Art is another kind of environmental protection, and the responsibility of the artist lies in making this kind of environmental protection more vividly displayed. How do you understand this sentence?

Chen Yansheng: Good works of art can purify people's spirit and elevate their thoughts. The responsibility of the artist lies in touching the inner changes of people through external visual images. The expression of art must be through various carriers. Simply put, these carriers are carbon-containing substances: such as paper, pigments, wood, etc. When using paper, we should save paper, choose low-carbon and environmentally friendly pigments, and try to avoid painting on wooden materials. Reducing the consumption of these carbon-containing substances is the environmental protection of art.

The ancients said: "Waste three thousand sheets of paper" means that a successful calligraphy or painting work wastes three thousand sheets of paper (artists know it is not an exaggeration). If today's calligraphers and painters all follow the ancient teachings, then it goes against the "Kyoto Protocol" advocated "low-carbon" measures; waste paper and inferior works are garbage, which needs to be burned. How many sheets of waste paper will an artist burn in his lifetime? How many sheets of waste paper will a group of artists burn in their lifetime? Calligraphers and painters have the same feeling: a person cannot produce a few good works in his lifetime, and the "waste three thousand sheets of paper" thing is inevitable. But to achieve "low carbon", we must avoid "wasting three thousand sheets of paper".

Artists should create top-notch works (including top-notch works with low-carbon content) with positive brushstrokes, creating a good artistic environment for the healthy development of mankind. Not only responsible for oneself, but also for all mankind, this is the low-carbon performance of environmental protection, and the responsibility of the artist.

A good artist is not only a master of using the pen, but also a master of purifying the soul.

Vulgar behavior is never behavioral art, we need to achieve the low-carbon and environmental protection of the soul

Reporter: Artists interpret their own art in various ways, such as interpretation on the painting, interpretation under the painting, which makes some people's behavioral art seem inevitable. However, art is called art because it has been accepted by tradition and morality. So, how do you understand the relationship between behavior, art, and behavioral art?

Chen Yansheng: Writing, painting and other forms of art themselves are manifestations of artistic behavior, which are not general behaviors of people in the usual sense, such as working, eating, signing contracts, etc. The social function of art has a standard: that is, art releases beauty while adjusting people's thoughts and emotions. If this point cannot be reached and instead makes people feel repulsed, then it is the complete opposition of ordinary human behavior and art. The relationship between art and behavior must be an internal and rule-abiding unity of beauty. Simply put, if human behavior has artistic value, then it is behavioral art. Vulgar behavior is never behavioral art.

Reporter: From some of your words, it can be seen that you have explored the deep levels of the human spirit quite a lot. Please talk about what influences have led to the collective loss of humanity in a certain upward field, becoming arrogant and conceited, etc. How can we use the low-carbon and environmental protection of the soul to save this loss?

Chen Yansheng: It is the crazy material desire that leads to collective loss, the distortion of human group values, and the manifestation of lack of faith. To eliminate the杂 thoughts and evil thoughts in the human heart, and establish a sacred and noble faith, so as to achieve the low-carbon and environmental protection of the human heart. Such as comparing the exhaust volume of cars, big officials have big houses, pursuing the luxurious style of the Jin Dynasty aristocracy, one person eats two tables of food, etc., are all the yeast sources leading to arrogance and conceit.

As artists, we need to use our artistic works and our own behaviors to form a good upward trend within the industry, while using the感染power of artworks to expand our positive and healthy influence. Use our works more often to reflect those unenvironmental and non-low-carbon phenomena, while praising environmental and low-carbon behaviors, causing resonance among people. When a work is placed there, everyone who sees it feels a shock in their heart, and through this shock, they realize their own shortcomings, and at the same time find the way to save these shortcomings. I think, if we can do this, we are worthy masters without question.

Reporter: Some people say that wearing leaves is low-carbon and environmentally friendly. Please analyze this statement from both the artistic and life levels?

Chen Yansheng: In this highly civilized society, it is impossible to return to the era when humans ate raw meat and drank blood. Wearing leaves cannot keep warm, and they smell sweaty, are inconvenient, unclean, and bring many inconveniences to people. The cost of cleaning them ultimately cannot achieve low carbon. If everyone wears leaves, then the whole world will be full of garbage. I once said in an article about low-carbon art: reducing paper waste is low-carbon art. Someone said: then let people wear leaves. Please don't forget that leaves can be made into paper. After the leaves are made into paper, their uses are expanded, and this itself is low-carbon art. From this perspective, wearing leaves in art and life are two fundamentally different concepts.

Reporter: As far as we know, you are an artist who pays great attention to low-carbon environmental protection and has a strong sense of social responsibility. What do you think the contemporary art circle should do to play a leading role in the development of low-carbon China from the spiritual level?

Chen Yansheng: Artists should promote low-carbon art through their works; artists educate people humorously and interestingly. Artists must first produce more top-notch works to achieve low-carbon examples. If people see beautiful works of art (top-notch), creations and discoveries of geniuses in society, then they play a leading role at the spiritual level. The flow of inferior art works and ordinary art works to society is not a low-carbon act, it is a waste of resources.

"Low-carbon" measures themselves are art

Reporter: How do you understand the phrase "‘Low-carbon’ measures themselves are art"?

Chen Yansheng: The concept of "low-carbon" art requires us to create art with thought and effort, improving success rates. Reduce energy consumption, shorten time consumption, and thereby reduce human resource consumption. Speaking, writing articles, education, architecture, and all industries are like this. "Low-carbon" measures themselves are art, which is not only behavioral art, but also administrative art. And as for art itself, for example, creating a top-notch work with a single sheet of rice paper avoids countless repetitions of failure, and such high artistic accomplishment is low-carbon. Artists must first have extraordinary basic skills, and before creation, think more. Create effortlessly when you are fully prepared, and this is a low-carbon measure that achieves the effect of artistic beauty.

Reporter: Art is a product of the times. If one day, we can only see some natural beauties in photos or paintings, it will absolutely be a tragedy of the times. In the low-carbon era, how do you think art should develop?

Chen Yansheng: We need forward-thinking ideas, creating a large number of works with low-carbon content, and taking the lead in creating with low-carbon materials. I firmly believe that in the near future, all of humanity will set up prominent low-carbon advertising boards. Novelists and painters should create some works of art due to neglecting low-carbon behavior leading to the end of the world. In the future, legislation and law enforcement should be strengthened to establish people's low-carbon awareness.

Reporter: Please tell us about your low-carbon lifestyle.

Chen Yansheng: Never compare eating or dressing, take simplicity as beauty. In terms of calligraphy creation, I establish this concept: wasting paper and ink is shameful, better to have less than too much. I deeply believe that low-carbon awareness can bring wisdom to an artist. Never blindly follow behind masters aimlessly, which will shorten the journey for an artist to become talented.
