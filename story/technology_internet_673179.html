The function of Tongbao All-in-One Card on mobile phones

by comdwzno on 2012-02-23 14:26:50

[Mobile Treasure One Card] is a type of mobile customer digital identity marker issued by Guangdong Mobile (hereinafter referred to as Guangdong Mobile) that supports various electronic commerce applications. It uses the digital certificate built into the card and binds it with the phone number to carry out various e-government and e-commerce applications. Currently, the [Mobile Treasure Yangcheng Pass] in Guangzhou, the [Mobile Shenzhen Pass] in Shenzhen, the [Duke Pass] in Meizhou, and the [Mo Yang Pass] in Yangjiang, all representative of their regions, have been put into use and are warmly embraced by citizens.

One card in hand, worry-free travel

According to relevant staff members of Guangdong Mobile, the [Mobile Treasure One Card] integrates payment and authentication functions and has now been applied in areas such as people's livelihood, consumption, finance, security, and public transportation. Card-holding users can enjoy services such as bus and subway card swiping, discounts at merchants affiliated with the mobile merchant alliance, community access control management, and office attendance management. The [Mobile Treasure One Card] can make access control authentication, ticket authentication, and network authentication all accessible with one "dip," and can also make mobile payments, shopping, and financial transactions easy with just a swipe of a card, making life more convenient with just one card.

Ms. Huang, who works in Guangzhou, successfully brushed her phone at the gate machine of Metro Yuexiu Park Station using her newly processed [Mobile Treasure Yangcheng Pass], smoothly taking the subway to the cinema. An hour earlier, she had ordered a discounted movie ticket through China Mobile's wireless city mobile client. After arriving at the cinema, Ms. Huang used the movie ticket authentication function of the [Mobile Treasure Yangcheng Pass] and brushed it on the terminal machine at the cinema to easily obtain her movie ticket. "It's so convenient!" Ms. Huang expressed that now with the [Mobile Treasure Yangcheng Pass], not only can you take buses and subways, but you can also watch movies and shop with just one phone.

It is understood that currently, Guangzhou Global Pass, M-Zone, and神州行users only need to bring their ID cards to Run Yue Service Hall, Animation Star City Service Hall, Xingang West Road Service Hall, and other designated China Mobile Communication 100 Service Halls, recharge a certain amount of phone fees, and they can receive the Mobile Treasure Yangcheng Pass card for free. In addition to being able to swipe the card at locations supporting the Yangcheng Pass for bus and subway rides and small-amount consumption, [Mobile Treasure Yangcheng Pass] users can also enjoy super-value services such as discount offers, VIP treatment, and point redemption in the merchant alliances expanded by the mobile company. They can also check balances and transaction details via their phones. Users of the [Mobile Treasure Yangcheng Pass] can recharge the Yangcheng Pass electronic wallet within the card by simply "dipping" it on the POS recharge machines of designated Yangcheng Pass merchants such as 7-11 convenience stores, and also support recharging the Yangcheng Pass account with phone fees.

Mr. Zhang, who lives in Nanshan District, Shenzhen, frequently uses the payment function of the [Mobile Shenzhen Pass] and praises its authentication function even more. According to Mr. Zhang's introduction, thanks to the personal identity certificate built into the [Mobile Shenzhen Pass], forms of authentication such as brushing the phone for flying, watching movies, and accessing the neighborhood gates have quietly become popular. He also activated a Mobile Treasure card for his son and bound it with his own phone number, so even if he goes out of town on business, he can know his son's departure and return times through reminder SMS sent from the neighborhood access control system via the Mobile Treasure card.

Different from the [Mobile Treasure One Card] in Guangzhou and Shenzhen, Chaozhou focuses on local realities and uses the [Mobile Treasure One Card] to create a wireless digital safe campus. With the [Mobile Treasure One Card], schools can accurately understand the attendance, tardiness, early departure, leave, and absence of every faculty member and student. This effectively ensures campus safety and creates a convenient and safe environment for teachers and students.

Reinterpreting a vibrant fast life

Regarding the launch of the [Mobile Treasure One Card], Lin Jingxin, a famous domestic marketing expert from Sun Yat-sen University, stated that in the mobile internet era, integrated application mobile terminals will completely change people's lives. According to him, as early as 2008, Japanese citizens could easily realize almost all daily consumption by simply brushing their phones when commuting by taxi or subway, shopping at supermarkets or malls, eating, singing, watching movies, flying, etc.

For this highly integrated consumer trend that incorporates identity authentication and small-amount payments, Lin Jingxin believes that the most pressing issue to solve is the security problem. Guangdong Mobile staff introduced that the [Mobile Treasure One Card] adopts multiple security encryption technologies during the transaction process to ensure the security of all functions. In addition to supporting static passwords, the [Mobile Treasure One Card] also supports multiple security protection measures such as SMS passwords and dynamic passwords. When large-amount consumption occurs, the system automatically notifies customers of account changes via SMS, verifies each transaction, and realizes safer and more convenient consumption.

Currently, the Mobile Treasure One Card is gradually penetrating into various fields such as people's livelihood, consumption, and finance, and will continue to expand in the future. For Guangdong Mobile, the goal of developing the Mobile Treasure One Card is not only to develop customers but more importantly to provide customers with a more complete service experience, while offering customers more diversified, convenient, and secure e-commerce applications, thereby fully meeting customers' needs for card-swiping consumption and work management in different application scenarios.
