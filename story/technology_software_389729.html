Let's Dive into Traditional Chinese Medicine (TCM) for Beauty Written by Taste of Whitening in Intensive Care

Today, let’s switch gears and return to our traditional treasure - TCM adjustment. TCM not only focuses on treating diseases but also excels in beauty adjustments. Throughout history, countless TCM practitioners have devoted their entire lives to finding ways to enhance the beauty of palace women, leaving behind a wealth of experience that absolutely deserves our attention!

So, let's follow the editor and delve into TCM beauty together~

"TCM Beauty" carries profound Chinese cultural heritage, refined through people's beauty practices over thousands of years. These time-honored folk beauty and health methods embody infinite Eastern wisdom.

Whether it's the ancient imperial court or modern urban settings, women have always put great thought into how to make themselves more beautiful.

And "TCM Beauty," with its holistic internal and external adjustment, and the concept of achieving health and beauty from within, is increasingly admired by those who value beauty and health.

However, now TCM beauty products come in all shapes and sizes. What kind of methods would be effective for you? Which products would benefit you? And how can you find products suitable for yourself? I believe these are the most concerning and headache-inducing questions for everyone.

For this reason, I visited supermarkets, malls, and beauty salons. Later, I discovered an issue: supermarkets and malls generally do not sell TCM products, while some beauty salons do use them. When asking merchants, without exception, they all introduced the advantages of their products, telling me what would happen after using them and how customers said they were good. However, these are ultimately one-sided opinions from merchants, making it difficult for us to find actual users unless our friends around us are using them. I'm not saying their products are bad; each product that survives in the market must have a certain customer base. Personally, I still place more emphasis on traditional TCM beauty products.

Due to work reasons, I often surf online, so I decided to use the internet to search. Taobao is China's largest shopping platform. Normally, I often buy clothes and various international brand cosmetics there. But I've never looked into our own TCM beauty-related things online. After searching, I found there were quite a few items here. I decided to ask a store with higher credibility.

I clicked into a TCM beauty store named "Green Forest." To be honest, what initially attracted me was the images of ancient beauties on their products—beauty knows no gender boundaries. After reading their introduction, it turned out they were from a family of TCM practitioners, and the products in the small shop were uniquely formulated by their elderly TCM doctor. This piqued my curiosity—wow, could they also concoct a secret formula for me, transforming me into a youthful, invincible young girl overnight? After reading more introductions about several products, I discovered that the old TCM doctor was around 50 years old but looked like he was only 35 in the photos! He was truly impressive, filling me with admiration and envy. If I looked like I do now at 50, that would be fine too.

Thinking about this, I consulted the shopkeeper as a customer to see how she introduced her products to me.

Editor: Shopkeeper, hello. Are your products all pure TCM?

Shopkeeper: Hello, yes, they are all pure TCM. Do you need anything specific?

Editor: I'd like to learn more first.

Shopkeeper: Sure, no problem. What aspect would you like to know more about?

Editor: Your products are said to be formulated by an old TCM doctor. How long will it take to see results?

Shopkeeper: Yes, they are formulated by an old TCM doctor. TCM emphasizes internal and external adjustment. Unlike regular chemical or hormone-based beauty products that show immediate effects, TCM treats both symptoms and root causes, fundamentally solving problems. Our products follow a one-month course. The speed of effectiveness varies from person to person. Some people may see results after one course, while others may need two courses or more. Generally, the longer you use them, the better the results.

Editor: Have any customers said they didn't see any effect?

Shopkeeper: Yes, some customers get impatient and stop using the products after a few tries, so they say it's ineffective. Our products aren't magic pills, so it's impossible to achieve satisfactory results after just a few uses. For example, some customers have very yellowish skin despite being young and without other illnesses. These customers usually suffer from qi and blood deficiency, so we adopt a dual supplementation of qi and blood, adjusting internally and externally. For such customers, adjustment usually takes at least two months. Some customers give up halfway, claiming the product is ineffective. As long as they persist, they will see results.

Editor: It seems that using TCM methods for beauty cannot be rushed.

Shopkeeper: That's right. Since everyone's situation is different, the required adjustment time will vary. As long as customers persist, their skin improves significantly compared to before.

Editor: Alright, thank you, Shopkeeper. I've learned quite a bit. I'll come back to buy when I need it next time. Wishing you booming business!

Shopkeeper: You're welcome, feel free to visit anytime.

The Editor believes that the quality of a product depends on its reputation. The store has a decent credit rating and many repeat customers. The Editor decided to investigate the opinions of their customers to verify the claims. Among numerous online buyers, I chose one with a high credit rating (Yellow Diamond). After explaining my intentions, she willingly chatted with me. Below is the main part of our conversation (to protect her privacy, I'll refer to her as A).

Editor: How long have you been using the TCM beauty products from this store?

A: Around eight months ago. At the time, due to prolonged sleepless nights, my skin was in terrible condition, my complexion was dull, my skin was dry, there were fine wrinkles, and I even developed spots. Since I have sensitive skin, I didn't dare to use random spot-removal products. I wanted a gentle TCM spot-removal product, which I found accidentally at this store. So, I started trying it then, and seeing good results, I continued using it.

Editor: What products did you use?

A: The shopkeeper recommended a Brightening Spot Removal Jade Complexion Pill and an All-in-One Brightening Spot Removal Mask, along with a Softening Moisturizing Wrinkle-Removing Mask based on my skin condition.

Editor: Did they really work?

A: Yes, after one month, my skin tone became much more even, my face wasn't as dry, the spots faded slightly, and my skin became a bit whiter. Although it wasn't extremely white, it wasn't as sallow as before.

Editor: How has your skin improved now?

A: My skin is now genuinely whiter and smoother, the spots have become very faint and are barely noticeable, disappearing completely under foundation. The fine lines have also become much shallower, and my skin looks hydrated and radiant. Even when I wake up in the morning, my skin has a rosy glow. Honestly, I am very satisfied with the current results. Moreover, I have recommended some friends to try it, and they also said the effects were excellent.

Editor: Did you experience any side effects?

A: No, because the shopkeeper asked about my physical condition before recommending the products, I didn’t notice any abnormalities. However, since TCM has a slower effect, it took some time.

Editor: Are you still using them?

A: Yes, I’ve been taking the Brightening Spot Removal Jade Complexion Pill for six months, and I consistently use the All-in-One Mask and the Softening Moisturizing Wrinkle-Removing Mask twice a week. This has been going on for eight months now. Regarding the Jade Complexion Pill, the shopkeeper suggested I take it every two months for another two months since it can also prevent aging. Women fear aging the most, haha.

Editor: It seems our time-honored beauty treasures are indeed excellent.

A: Yes, you can try it. Talk to the shopkeeper.

Eternal youth is a beautiful aspiration of humanity, but all natural things inevitably go through birth and death, prosperity and decline. As a part of nature, humans cannot escape this objective law. Therefore, with age, the human body also ages.

In TCM, although it is impossible to stay perpetually young, by replenishing the deficiencies of vital organs and qi-blood, maintaining balance between yin and yang in the body, it is indeed possible to delay the arrival of aging. We believe that today, with the growing appreciation for nature and green concepts, TCM, with its five-thousand-year accumulation of strength, still holds unique charm in anti-aging, beauty care, and health preservation.
