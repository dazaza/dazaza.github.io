Samsung WCG: Nine Years of Partnership, Nine Years of Harvest

by lk0gamem on 2009-07-21 23:28:43

On May 22, 2009, the Beijing competition of Samsung WCG in China officially kicked off. Beijing, which had just hosted the Olympics, once again welcomed a feast of e-sports. With the conclusion of local preliminary contests of Samsung WCG, the call to arms for regional competitions has sounded, and station competitions in fifteen districts are being carried out vigorously in major cities across the country.

Founded in 2000, WCG is a world e-sports competition supported by the Korean government. It has gone through nine years. Over the past nine years, WCG, with the slogan "beyond the game," has become a global competitive gaming event and the first largest-scale game cultural festival globally, becoming a new demand in people's cultural sports life in the information age and a new driving force leading the development of the information industry.

Looking back on the nine-year development journey of WCG, Samsung, as the official partner of WCG for nine consecutive years, has witnessed and promoted the gradual maturity and success of WCG. Meanwhile, Samsung has also utilized its capabilities and advantages in the IT field to display its advocacy of E-Olympic digital culture in an excellent manner on the e-sports digital experience platform.

WCG aims to promote the global development of e-sports, aiming to facilitate communication, interaction, and exchange among people in the network era. Since the inaugural event in 2001, the organizers have positioned it as a global e-sports Olympic event, an electronic sports meet organized in the form of the Olympic Games, shouldering the responsibility of communicating between top e-sports athletes worldwide and facilitating international exchanges, becoming a pioneer of new sports forms.

Nine years ago, when WCG was founded, it was merely a small-scale industry competition newly emerged in Korea. Over the past nine years, with the sponsorship and promotion of Samsung Electronics, WCG has gradually grown into the world's premier e-sports event, admired by hundreds of millions of e-sports enthusiasts worldwide.

As a sponsor of WCG, Samsung not only provides financial support and promotional assistance to WCG but also continuously provides product and technical support to WCG. The nine-year journey of WCG events records the development footprints of e-sports hardware equipment. The continuous innovation of these "electronic Olympics" equipment has effectively supported the smooth progress of WCG competitions, bringing new digital life experiences with outstanding product quality.

Samsung's enormous investment has driven the tremendous development of WCG events. At the same time, Samsung has used WCG to convey the brand connotations of health, fashion, and high technology to the widest possible audience. It can be seen that fashionable digital products, beautiful display devices, and live streaming at the scene of WCG competitions all convey the scientific and technological, fashionable information represented by Samsung. Through WCG, Samsung Electronics is becoming an advocate of new lifestyles in the network era. Through WCG, we find a digitally embraced lifestyle - science, health, and fashion.

Since Samsung first introduced the concept of "e-sports" to China in 2001 until today when the WCG World Finals landed in Chengdu, China, Samsung has supported WCG competitions in China from multiple angles such as funds, equipment, and public welfare causes, thus greatly promoting the development of China's e-sports industry. At the press conference launching WCG on April 22, Mr. Park Jeong-jun, Vice President of Marketing for Samsung Electronics in Greater China, said that as the introducer and promoter of WCG in China, Samsung has confidence that during the first entry of WCG World Finals into China, it will utilize its capabilities and advantages in the IT field, through cutting-edge technology and products close to consumer needs, through the spirit of WCG "challenge, transcendence, confidence," and through the influence of Samsung WCG, further promote the popularization of China's e-sports movement and the development of e-sports culture, driving a comprehensive upgrade of China's e-sports industry chain.

As a leader in the digital age, under the slogan "beyond the game," Samsung leverages the World Cyber Games WCG to build a digital culture promotion platform, promoting the development of the IT industry; while utilizing sports marketing strategies to enhance brand influence, leaving its own brilliant mark in the digital era.

As a sport, e-sports has many unique characteristics. It brings a tight relationship between humans and machines and technology, containing the unique culture of the electronic digital age, closely following the young generation's pursuit of fashion and cool experiences, showcasing youthful vitality, tension, and the future. And these cores of the electronic digital age can be conveyed through the competition.
