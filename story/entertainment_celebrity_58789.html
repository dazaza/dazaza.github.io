Sisters Dajiao and Xiaojiao reveal their "youth-preserving" secrets at Peking University, attracting hundreds of student fans

by antian168 on 2008-03-31 10:57:42

Reported by Southern Network on March 31, how much charm do the two sisters, Big S and Little S, have? The two sisters not only have great appeal among white-collar workers but also are crazily pursued by Peking University students.

Yesterday afternoon, Big S and Little S walked into the Centennial Auditorium of Peking University as the spokeswomen for a certain teenage girl's clothing brand. The two sisters not only made advertisements for the brand at the scene but also showed off their fashion sense by boasting about their beauty. What surprised the reporters was that hundreds of Peking University students crazily pursued the two sisters. The scene was extremely chaotic, and all the Peking University students on-site turned into super fans of the two, shouting their names. Moreover, Little S's popularity among university students surpassed her sister's.

The two stayed for about half an hour before rushing away. Prior to this, the news of Big S and Little S entering Peking University had been questioned, with accusations that they were not qualified enough. At that time, the agent defended by saying "it's not a lecture, just a meet-and-greet." Judging from the on-site situation, it was indeed just a brand promotion event.

Knowing how to look young, Little S posed sensually: My figure is just good!

Both Big S and Little S have quite a few endorsements under their belts, and the two sisters are also well-known cash kings. They both once endorsed a mobile phone brand together, and yesterday, they again co-endorsed a teenage girl's clothing brand. It was Little S's first appearance in mainland China after giving birth to her second child. The two came to Peking University as endorsers to exchange fashion tips with the students. Big S and Little S also perfectly matched the theme on-site, both appearing in pink spring outfits. The two sisters continued their respective styles: Big S paired pink tops with long pants, showcasing a knowledgeable and elegant style, while Little S wore a pink mini-skirt, maintaining her lively and sexy mom style. The two sisters caused a sensation as soon as they appeared, with students praising them as worthy sisters, truly having their own styles.

Although the two are no longer in their youthful prime, they have both been chosen by the teenage girl's clothing brand to be their endorsers, which naturally made the two sisters exceptionally happy and proud. Little S straightforwardly claimed she was "pretending to be younger," and as the Beauty King, her sister Big S naturally took good care of herself. Both of them "looked fresh and adorable" and boasted "cuter than teenagers." Although she had just given birth, Little S's figure had recovered very well and was praised repeatedly by the host. Little S then proudly boasted, "My figure is just good!" She even deliberately posed in exaggerated ways, showing off her signature S-shaped action. With effective weight loss methods, she indeed knew how to "look young," completely not resembling a mother of two children, making many female students envious.

High Popularity: Peking University Students Chase Sisters: Little S More Charismatic

Although Big S and Little S's appearance this time was for brand endorsement promotion, they chose the dignified venue of the Centennial Auditorium at the prestigious Peking University. On-site, hundreds of Peking University students swarmed to support the two sisters. What shocked the reporters was that the students at Peking University were abnormally enthusiastic about the two sisters. The tickets for the event were extremely scarce, and a large number of students waited outside the door hoping the organizers would let them in to see the two sisters.

When Big S and Little S entered the scene, the atmosphere on-site became quite热烈. The Peking University students crazily called out their names, and the majority of the students admitted that they liked Little S more: "Little S is more genuine; she has a real and cute charm." Moreover, among those chasing the S twins on-site, there were not only female students but also many male students. Many male students also frankly stated that they liked Little S's shows more.

Some students were selected to interact with Big S and Little S on stage. Although they only discussed so-called corporate culture issues, these students still felt honored to closely contact the two, feeling very excited and thrilled. Big S and Little S were also quite proud to be able to interact with college students at Peking University. Little S was especially delighted, saying, "I'm very happy, didn't expect to be so popular, it's much better than hosting TV shows!"

Bold Exposure: Little S Gossips: Big S Loves Pretending to Be Elegant

As the currently popular hosts across Taiwan, Hong Kong, and Macao, the two sisters naturally have excellent eloquence. Although on-site they cooperated with the organizer's theme of "pink" and chatted about their pink dressing tips, the always outspoken Little S couldn't help but expose some embarrassing stories about her and her sister after having her baby. She revealed that her sister loves pretending to be elegant, verbally saying she likes children, but actually finds kids dirty. "Recently, I often changed my baby's diapers, and one time, my sister was nearby and saw the baby's dirty poop, she almost threw up."

Her uncensored remarks left Big S speechless beside her, who could only retaliate by exposing her younger sister's shortcomings: "Little S loves wearing revealing clothes. In the past, she wouldn't wear anything unless it exposed something. But after getting married, she seemed like she had her chips replaced overnight by aliens and started knowing how to be more restrained, though her skirts are still shorter than mine!"

Small Mishap:

Little S Accidentally Exposed Herself

Their poses on stage were also quite interesting. Big S sat exceptionally upright, claiming "this pose is learned from Princess Diana," while Little S intentionally twisted herself into an S-shape, claiming "I've already become a mom, I'm afraid of accidentally exposing myself, so I need to be more dignified," but she was still caught by the on-site photographers exposing herself.
