Reasons for solving workpiece quenching cracking

Quenching process is widely used in modern mechanical manufacturing industry. Important parts in machinery, especially steel parts used in automobiles, airplanes, rockets, are almost all treated with quenching. In order to meet various technical requirements of different parts, various quenching processes have been developed. For example, according to the treated part, there are overall quenching, local quenching and surface quenching; according to whether the phase transformation is complete during heating, there are complete quenching and incomplete quenching (for hypo-eutectoid steel, this method is also called subcritical quenching); according to the content of phase transformation during cooling, there are graded quenching, isothermal quenching and under-speed quenching, etc.
