The clock that can weave

by gadgetoflife on 2011-04-27 00:49:57

Time passes by, a clock can tell the time, but also if you want to knit a scarf for your other half in winter, this is a very nice design.

This design was first exhibited in January this year at the "International Design Exhibition in Berlin, Germany", but it is believed that it is still not available in Hong Kong.

The clock will knit one stitch every half hour, and it will take one day to knit a circle. After 365 days, a two-meter-long scarf will be completed. After a year has passed, what remains is a woolen scarf of commemorative value (we can record each year by color).

It's really an interesting design!
