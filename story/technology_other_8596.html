Laozi graduated from Sichuan University that year! (A Flash describing four years of college life)

by heroty on 2006-11-06 21:49:35

It was that summer when I graduated from Sichuan University.

Damn! Among thousands of enterprises at the job fair, not a single one hired me. It's so sad!

Back then, when I was taking the exam to get into Sichuan University, I thought I would find a good job after graduation. Now I realize that Sichuan University is not all that great either.

Indeed, there are those who study well and are fierce. Some in English majors pass the level 8 exam, have senior titles in computer science, are good-looking, and have multiple girlfriends.

Now I really shouldn't blame anyone. I know, it's all my fault.

I shouldn't have fooled around back then. When I think about it now, I really regret it deeply.

Alas, four years have been wasted. For a long time, I wanted to reflect on myself. To be honest, I wanted very much to study hard, but I ended up like this.

The future is bleak, the road is winding. Every turning point in life is right in front of me, but I don't know how to cherish it.

Four years flew by. After calculating, I found out that I have retaken 32 courses, not including those I passed after retaking.

Until now, I haven't passed the Deng Xiaoping Theory course.

Oh well, never mind, there's no hope for a good job.

A few days ago, one of my buddies came over and told me that I had been accepted.

I went to check it out, and it turned out to be the Chengdu Municipal Sanitation Bureau. They asked if I could drive, and I couldn't.

I thought to myself, it would be fine if they assigned me to drive the garbage truck, but since I can't drive and don't have a driver's license, they made me empty trash bins instead.

How frustrating! Looking back on the four years, I really don't know what to do.

Four years! All the guys in the same dormitory have girlfriends, except for me, who is still single.

I admit I'm ugly, but how is it my fault that I'm ugly?!!

Oh well, never mind, let's start all over again.
