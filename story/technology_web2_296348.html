The public pays attention to the grand conference of the CPPCC, and talks about the future with confidence.

by bjjdwxgs on 2009-12-06 07:41:03

The members of the CPPCC made suggestions for the scientific development of Yancheng and the prosperity and well-being of people's lives at the meeting venue. Our citizens paid special attention to the convening of the meeting, and were also full of confidence in this grand event. As for the repair of Philips induction cooker... Reporter Zheng Yongshan said to the audience: "The first meeting of the sixth session of the CPPCC of Yancheng City opened today. There are many important contents related to the people's livelihood in this meeting, so not only do the attending members and representatives pay great attention to it, but also our vast number of citizens are very concerned about it." Talking about the past five years, the development of our city of Yancheng is really gratifying. The overall strength of the city has significantly increased, the quality of development has continuously improved, the people's lives have obviously improved, the appearance of urban and rural areas has profoundly changed, and the social undertakings have made comprehensive progress. The city has successfully completed all tasks of the 'Tenth Five-Year Plan', and smoothly achieved a good start for the 'Eleventh Five-Year' development. All these make the people of our city deeply feel that the city they live in is developing rapidly with a strong momentum. Citizens 1 & 2: "In 07, things were still okay. For instance, the big bronze horse looks quite beautiful, and the road surface of urban construction has become wider. We are relatively satisfied with the current government, especially in terms of roads and markets." The convening of the two sessions has focused the eyes of all the people on this grand meeting. And as ordinary people, what everyone pays more attention to is whether the spirit conveyed by this meeting is closely related to the life of the common people. During the process of street interviews conducted by our reporter, many citizens expressed their true feelings in front of the camera. Citizens 1, 2 & 3: "When holding job fairs, expand the scope of employers or consider more for middle-level students. The traffic jam is too serious, the government should deal with it. We just hope to increase the salary so that we can live better and happier." Reporter Zheng Yongshan: "Actually, what the majority of the citizens expect are the issues that the municipal Party committee and government are about to solve. Our citizens are also full of confidence in the government." Citizens 1 & 2: "Wish our Yancheng a better future. In a harmonious atmosphere, think more for us ordinary people, make our lives better and more harmonious, let the two sessions make contributions for us." Conclusion: The convening of the CPPCC meeting inspires people, making us more confident about Yancheng's future. We wish the conference to be successfully concluded, and Yancheng's tomorrow will be better!
