The first leader of Manchester United's defense has seen his value skyrocket by four times, while a Barcelona mainstay was merely his substitute last year.

by qinglai888 on 2009-12-16 19:52:25

The first leader of Manchester United's defense has quadrupled in value. A Barcelona pillar was only his substitute last year.

According to Sina Sports, on his 110th league start, Vidic finally scored his 9th league goal for Manchester United. As early as January this year, Vidic had successfully scored in consecutive league matches. After such a long period without scoring in the league, he finally ended with a strong finish at the end of the year. This goal might be a symbol; the Serbian's status in Manchester United's backfield is undoubtedly the first, unmatched by anyone.

Including cup games, Vidic's last goal was against Inter Milan in the Champions League round of 16. At that time, after shaking off Vieira's defense, he scored with a powerful header. Today against Wolves, it was a similar move, a commanding and forceful header from the front post, despite an average angle. Due to its immense power, even though Hannemann touched the ball, it was futile. Vidic scored for the fifth time this season. This goal once again confirmed the absolute advantage the Serbian possesses in aerial duels. In fact, before Rooney scored from the penalty spot, it was precisely due to his strong impact that forced the opposing defender to commit a handball foul. Therefore, in today's match, Vidic created two goals. Ferguson praised Vidic as the most formidable player in set-piece attacks.

Due to injuries this season, Vidic has only played 16 games for Manchester United so far, missing 10 games. With other defenders also suffering from "intermittent" injuries, Ferguson has been making large-scale adjustments in the center-back position. After Rio had to be absent for a long time due to back injuries, if Vidic were to have problems, the blow to Manchester United's defensive line would be unimaginable. So as long as Vidic can play, the confidence in defense will soar. We can see in the game that because of Vidic's presence, young De Laet dares to break through boldly on the right wing, knowing that someone is covering the space behind him. The other central defender, Carrick, also plays effortlessly. Of course, this is not a very difficult match, and Vidic's command ability is shown more vividly. Don't forget that he was the captain of the starting lineup in this game. Ferguson's trust in him is fully expressed by that small armband.

Recently, Vidic has reportedly had some awkwardness with the club. His agent stated that the club should increase Vidic's salary to 100,000 pounds per week. However, with the contract having two and a half years left, the club will not act so early, and even with two years left at the end of summer, the club may not take action. Since the management insists on starting renewal when the contract enters the final year, there is a risk of being poached by other major clubs. Although Vidic shows no apparent dissatisfaction on the surface, it is hard for him to control his freely acting agent. If a sky-high contract comes from Barcelona, it is difficult to ensure that Vidic won't be tempted. Pique, who became an absolute starter at Barcelona, was Vidic's first substitute until last summer. The blockbuster transfer deal is something Barcelona would be willing to operate.

However, from what we see now, external factors are one thing, but Vidic indeed focuses more on the present. Recently, he stated that he only cares about Manchester United and does not think about the World Cup which is still more than half a year away. He said: "I don't want to think that far ahead. Sometimes the only thing you care about is your injury. So the best way is to focus on the next game and then keep playing. There is plenty of time until the World Cup, so currently, I am fully focused on playing for Manchester United." Such statements are what any Manchester United fan would like to hear.

Before the match against Wolves, Vidic urged his teammates to secure three points. In an interview with the official website, he said: "This is our best chance to play well, score a few goals, and bring back confidence. It's important to win immediately after losing a game. So you need to play better and make up for the mistakes made in the previous game."

Clearly, he and his teammates have achieved their goal. A player who maintains a strong aerial presence at both ends of the box, as long as he stays healthy and fully commits himself without distractions, could easily defeat Wolves with the power of last season's Premier League MVP.

When Vidic joined Manchester United, his purchase price was 7 million pounds, already quite high for a center-back. But after these few years of development, according to Transfermarkt's valuation, Vidic's current worth is around 35 million euros, or 31.15 million pounds, more than quadrupling in just four years. This data says it all. It can be foreseen that Manchester United will eventually meet his salary increase demands, allowing him to enter the top income bracket in the Premier League, determined by Vidic's superior field strength and dominance.
