The Development Path of Car Washing Machines in Automotive Beauty Supplies

The introduction of fully automatic computerized car washing machines into the automotive beauty supplies industry is due to the continuous development of science and technology and the gradual specialization of industries. Although fully automatic computerized car washing machines are becoming increasingly common, many car owners still have misconceptions about their use and performance.

Currently, the method car owners use for washing their vehicles is typically a single bucket of water from start to finish. They believe that manual washing conserves water, whereas computerized car washing uses both high-pressure water jets and undercarriage rinsing, which they think consumes more water. In reality, this "one bucket of water" approach is not advisable. While it may seem to save water on the surface, the gravel in the wash water can be carried by the cleaning cloth onto the car's paint surface. This repeated abrasion from the gravel is highly detrimental to the car's paint. Over time, the car body will develop fine scratches, lose its luster, and may even fade. According to the head of Jinan Turtle Wax Automotive Products Wholesale Network, fully automatic computerized car washing machines are excellent tools for automotive beauty care, with water-saving models performing even better. Moreover, these machines come equipped with a recycled water treatment system that allows for up to 80% of the water used during car washing to be recycled, effectively resolving the conflict between undercarriage rinsing and energy conservation while significantly reducing water consumption.

Therefore, car enthusiasts should stop using buckets and rags to wash their cars, as this method does not save water and can harm your vehicle. It is recommended to go to professional automotive beauty supply stores for cleaning and maintenance. Additionally, you can choose other automotive beauty products such as hard shell wax and glass fluid for regular maintenance, which can make your car look brand new.

Future Development Path of Automotive Beauty Supplies

Economic development and an increase in the number of cars have opened up the market for automotive beauty supplies. Decorating a newly purchased car has become a choice for many car owners. However, since the automotive products market has been developing for a relatively short period, it remains immature in various aspects, which to some extent restrains the development of automotive beauty and care products. According to the head of Turtle Wax Automotive Products Wholesale Network, automotive beauty supplies will develop in the following directions:

1. Improve the quality of automotive beauty supplies. The profit from decorating automotive beauty supplies mainly comes from material price differences and labor costs. Some merchants cut corners to gain profits, affecting product quality and tarnishing the overall image of automotive beauty supplies. Inferior sun protection films and rearview mirrors pose significant risks to drivers, leading consumers to distrust automotive beauty and care products.

2. Enhance the skills of service personnel in the automotive beauty supplies sector. It’s difficult to immediately identify the quality and effects after installing automotive beauty supplies. Therefore, there are currently many "four-nothing" beauty supplies in the market: no formal training, no branded professional products, no professional machinery, and no service quality guarantee. It is reported that there are around 9,000 companies specializing or partially engaged in automotive beauty supplies decoration in China, employing approximately 150,000 people, many of whom operate without proper licenses.

3. Establish industry standards for automotive beauty supplies. Due to the lack of unified service standards, there is a large variation in pricing for automotive beauty supplies decoration services among different merchants. Industry insiders believe that only by establishing uniform service standards can the industry unify service pricing. If the automotive beauty supplies market becomes a high-profit market where service quality is hard to measure, consumer confidence in the beauty supplies decoration market will eventually be lost over time.
