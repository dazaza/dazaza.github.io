When online shopping is led to a new era of video shopping

Hongyi

If we talk about shopping, Taobao is definitely the leader. If we talk about videos, Ku6 is also a leading player in the video industry. Now, these two are going to collaborate, with the goal of creating a "video shopping platform." According to news reports, Ku6 has obtained the API interface and open data provided by Taobao through deep cooperation, allowing them to freely select information from Taobao's database based on their own platform construction needs. This is the information I found online, and after checking it out (website: shop.ku6.com), I found it quite impressive!

This platform is now being promoted as the "only video shopping platform," and indeed, it is currently the only one. This represents another realization of Taobao's "Powered by Taobao" platform openness and model output. The benefits of this collaboration are obvious. As I mentioned at the beginning, when it comes to online shopping, Taobao is the leader, and Ku6 has always been strong in the video industry. Now, with the support of the Taobao Open Platform, Ku6's newly launched Shop channel can instantly retrieve all the latest uploaded product information from Taobao’s merchant database and display it on this shopping platform, achieving synchronization in terms of product launch time and content. The intuitiveness of videos makes it easier to showcase products, making shopping more convenient for consumers, sales more direct and reliable for products, and helping merchants sell goods more effectively. At the same time, it provides users with a richer shopping experience. Additionally, the biggest benefit is that video websites seem to have found an effective way to profit, as Ku6 can earn revenue through the "Taobao Affiliate Commission" set by merchants in Taobao’s merchant database.

This is a very meaningful initiative. From the perspective of online shopping, the evolution from online shopping to video shopping represents significant progress. It transforms the traditional single-image and text-based displays into something outdated, while ushering in a new era of video shopping that allows users to experience the three-dimensional beauty of products. From the perspective of video websites, the collaboration with Taobao enhances the value of video websites, specifically their commercial value. Personally, even if you don't shop, just browsing those videos on the site could be enjoyable!

Taobao sellers also gain an opportunity for improvement. According to news reports, Taobao sellers can add video functionality as needed for their products. Through network video presentations, they can provide buyers with a more three-dimensional, intuitive, and convenient shopping experience, ensuring the authenticity of pre-purchased items and reducing the risks associated with online shopping. When words like 'all-around,' 'three-dimensional,' and 'multi-angle' are introduced more deeply, it means that Taobao sellers have more new opportunities to capture users!

Imagine, when you visit Ku6 not just to watch videos but possibly also to "Taobao" (shop on Taobao), you'll realize that the video shopping platform is not just a perfect combination of video technology and e-commerce. By leveraging their respective advantages in their fields, both parties are not only combining resources but also defining a new concept of the internet and opening up a "video shopping" new era! The emergence of "video shopping" makes people feel that video websites in 2009 are not only becoming more mature but are also actively exploring diversified developments for the future. And this "video shopping," which directly brings in profits, is undoubtedly more charming among these diversified development directions! (Hongyi http://www.yuzhongcheng.com/)
