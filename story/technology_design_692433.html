How to open an online store: Detailed process for opening a store on Taobao, Free steps to open a store on Taobao.

by wdls8com on 2012-03-06 09:38:30

Everyone knows that opening a store on the Taobao homepage is very popular now, and it will also be an inevitable trend in the future. Shopping on Taobao is not just a trend but a developmental direction that will gradually become part of people's lives. Below, I will introduce to you all the detailed process of opening a store on Taobao.

Three steps to open a store —— Taobao is a product trading website certified by the state. As long as you register for free as a Taobao member, you can freely purchase the items you need on Taobao, and you can also open a store for free.

Step 1: Registering as a Taobao member is very simple. You need to fill in your username on the registration page, which can be a Chinese name. Set up a password and provide an email address for confirming the membership account. The email address can be obtained for free from large websites such as Yahoo, Sina, Sohu, and NetEase.

After you have filled out your registration information, Taobao will send you a reply email. Log into your mailbox and automatically reply with a confirmation message according to Taobao's prompt. Once you do this, your Taobao membership registration is considered successful.

Step 2: After successfully registering as a Taobao member, you can apply for Alipay for free. Alipay is the core guarantee for safe online transactions on Taobao. As a trustworthy and neutral third party, Alipay fully ensures the safety of goods and money, as well as the interests of both buyers and sellers during transactions without any worries.

Due to the implementation of real-name registration on the network, you need to undergo "Alipay personal real-name authentication," which includes three parts: "filling out personal information," "ID verification," and "bank account verification." Each part must be submitted and reviewed before you can proceed with opening your store.

Store-opening conditions: First, register as a Taobao member and activate your account. Then, apply for Alipay and pass the certification.

Step 3: Post 10 items for sale on Taobao and keep them in the "for sale" status. This way, you can open a store for free on Taobao.

Posting items is very simple. Click on "I want to sell," choose either a fixed price or auction, and fill in the detailed content of the item for sale so that buyers can understand.

Each time a member successfully completes a transaction on Taobao, they can make one credit evaluation of the transaction partner. Credit evaluations are divided into praise, encouragement, and criticism. Praise adds one point, encouragement does not add points, and criticism deducts one point.

In summary, the steps are:

1. Apply for a Taobao account;

2. Pass the certification (requires a digital photo of your ID);

3. Post 10 or more items you want to sell on the website.

Once these three conditions are met, your store is ready.

(Detailed Steps for Step Two) Operation process for applying for Alipay real-name authentication:

1. Log in to your Alipay account (account type: personal account). On the "My Alipay" homepage, click "Apply for Certification";

2. Enter the introduction page for Alipay real-name authentication, then click "Apply Now" to continue;

3. Carefully read the real-name authentication service agreement, then click the "I have read and agree to accept the above agreement" button to enter Alipay real-name authentication;

4. You have two options for real-name authentication. Choose one and click "Apply Now." If you choose "Alipay Cartoon" for real-name authentication, follow the prompts to apply and activate it. If you choose "Other methods for real-name authentication," click "Apply Now."

5. Correctly select the region where your ID is located. Only after making the correct selection can you smoothly complete your Alipay real-name authentication.

6. Correctly fill in your ID number and real name, then click "Submit" to continue;

7. Correctly fill in "Your Personal Information" and "Your Account Information." When filling in bank account information, if you find that the personal information you filled in does not match the bank information, click "Change Identity Information" to modify it. If your real name contains rare characters, please fill in your bank account name in the input box below the bank account holder's name.

8. Verify the "Personal Information" and "Bank Account" you filled in. Confirm that there are no errors and click "Confirm Submit" to save the information you entered;

9. Successfully submit the certification application, wait for Alipay to deposit an amount less than one yuan into the bank card you submitted, and check the exact amount received in your bank account after two days. Then log in to your Alipay account, click "Apply for Certification," and enter the received amount.

-----------------------------------------------------------------------------

After finding out how much the amount under one yuan transferred by Alipay to your account is, perform the following operations:

1. Log in to your Alipay account - My Alipay - click "Apply for Certification" to enter the remittance amount confirmation page;

2. Check the specific amount received on the bank card you filled in, click "Input Remittance Amount" to enter the amount input page;

3. Input the accurate amount you received, click "Confirm" to continue completing the confirmation. You have two chances to input the amount correctly. If you fail twice, you will need to resubmit your bank account for review.

4. After entering the correct amount, Alipay will instantly review the identity information you filled in. Please wait patiently for 2 seconds;

5. After passing the review, you have successfully passed Alipay's real-name authentication.
