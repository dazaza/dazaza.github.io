Nanshan Ski Resort Nanshan Ski Resort's first day of skiing in 2009 is Saturday, November 21st at 9:00 AM.

by huaxue9657 on 2009-12-01 11:45:51

Beijing, the first week of trial operation announcement for the 09~10 snow season at SaiBei Snowfield and NanShan Ski Resort;  . Confirmed by NanShan Ski Resort, YunFeng Mountain Ski Resort will hold its first slide of the trial operation period at 9:00 AM on Saturday, November 21st, 2009. The trial operation will continue until 5:30 PM on Friday, November 27th, 2009. During this trial period, NanShan will open two intermediate slopes (new and old intermediate slopes), a U-shaped trough, three beginner slopes (south slope of the reception hall, front mountain beginner slope, back mountain small "S" slope), an intermediate mogul slope with a medium-sized jump below it, a wave slope, a second single-board teaching area, and a snow play zone featuring flying saucers on snow. At the same time, they will also operate a double chairlift, an old four-person chairlift, and two large T-bars, along with three small T-bars to support operations.

    In order to thank the wide range of skiing enthusiasts for their long-term support of NanShan, during the trial operation period, the resort will offer a 90% discount (including one set of ski equipment rental fees, excluding entrance tickets, ski suits, lockers, instructors, and catering costs) to guests who have made reservations in advance. Starting from Saturday, November 28th, 2009, NanShan Ski Resort will enter the regular business period, and skiing projects will return to normal pricing. Only 50 people per day are allowed;

    After the discount, the price including the entrance ticket is as follows: weekdays full-day 42 yuan, weekdays half-day 34 yuan, weekends full-day 56 yuan, weekends half-day 40 yuan. Ticket reservation hotline: 010-51289796  , LianHua Mountain Ski Resort Beijing enters the skiing season earlier than usual by half a month;    010-51289795
