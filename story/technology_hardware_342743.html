The first choice brand ranking in lighting fixtures: NVC, Philips, and Opple stage a "Romance of the Three Kingdoms"

by kdchen5188 on 2010-04-09 23:28:55

On April 9th, the China Real Estate Evaluation Center officially released the "Top Brand Selection List for Building Materials Procurement by the Top 500 Chinese Real Estate Developers of 2009" at the Beijing Raffles Hotel. In the lighting fixtures category, NVC received the most favor from developers. The top three brands in the lighting fixtures category were NVC, Philips, and Opple, which presented a tripartite confrontation with NVC taking the crown, Philips coming in second place as LED flashlight, and Opple closely following behind. These three brands had relatively high market shares, forming a tripod structure together with Panasonic, SanXiong Jiguang, Yamming, TCL, Dibao, Osram, and Huatай, constituting the top ten tier of the lighting fixtures list.

Currently, China's lighting fixtures industry has nearly 3,000 enterprises, leading the world in production volume. However, there are only more than a dozen large-scale enterprises in the industry, making it large but fragmented with low industrial concentration. Previously, the high-end market was dominated by foreign brands like Philips and Panasonic. In recent years, domestic self-own brands such as Opple and NVC have begun to strengthen their efforts, increasing investment in independent research and development to compete with foreign brands in the high-end market.

The China Real Estate Evaluation Center found that currently, most real estate developers prefer lighting brands with significant brand influence. Among them, the selection rates for NVC, Philips, and Opple were relatively high, at 27.34%, 20.31%, and 17.97% respectively. The total sum of these three brands' usage rate among developers reached 65.62%. The first choice brands of the top ten national developers accounted for nearly 90% of the market share.

This shows that developers have a relatively high concentration when choosing lighting fixtures. The three giants - NVC, Philips, and Opple Lighting - occupy more than 60% of the market share, dominating the domestic lighting market consumption and leading the lighting fixtures industry towards diversified development.

From the regional distribution of each brand's developer selection rate, domestic big brands firmly occupied the top five preferred choices in various regions. NVC, Philips, and Opple stably ranked in the top three in all regions. While consolidating their regional markets, they also advanced into other regions by establishing branches or adopting regional agency models across major regions nationwide, striving to expand their brand market share.

The research report pointed out that when selecting lighting products, developers mostly choose well-known brands with significant influence. Well-known brand lighting products offer many varieties, large quantities, good quality, complete product lines, and ample selection options. Excellent brand images can effectively enhance the quality and aesthetics of projects, while deeply rooted brand impressions in consumers' minds also become an important factor for developers choosing lighting brands.
