Qihoo CFO Zhouli Xu Admits in Talks to Buy Sogou, Denies Having Expectations for Negotiations

by anonymous on 2013-07-29 12:38:17

July 19th news, Qihoo 360's CFO, Zuo Li Xu, recently stated in an interview with Reuters that Qihoo 360 is negotiating with Sohu to acquire Sogou, a company under Sohu. However, Xu Zuo Li also mentioned that Qihoo 360 has no overall expectations regarding these negotiations.

"There have been many rumors in the market about Sogou's capital operations. There are multiple potential acquirers for Sogou, and indeed, Qihoo 360 is in talks with Sogou," Xu Zuo Li said. He did not comment on whether Qihoo 360 and Sogou had negotiated prices or evaluated whether they could secure this asset and fully integrate it.

Xu Zuo Li mentioned that during an hour-long exchange with Reuters journalists, the discussion mainly revolved around Qihoo 360’s business itself, with less than a minute dedicated to Sogou-related discussions. Some of these were hypothetical discussions by analysts. For instance, some analysts pointed out that Sogou's search business is valued between $1.2 billion to $1.4 billion, and Qihoo 360 would not be making a complete cash purchase. However, the interaction did not involve any discussion about Sogou's selling price.

In fact, the senior management of Qihoo 360 has been ambiguous about the sale of Sogou. Not long ago, when Qihoo 360's Chairman, Hongyi Zhou, gave a semi-formal speech at Stanford University, he did not provide a direct answer to this question. He only mentioned that Qihoo 360 has invested in and acquired a series of companies but generally does not announce them publicly. Compared to already mature and large-scale user base companies, he prefers acquiring early-stage teams.

It is reported that since entering the search market in August last year, Qihoo 360's search engine now holds a market share of 15%, ranking second after Baidu. Sogou Search Engine holds an 8% market share. Credit Suisse analyst Elinor Leung indicated that this deal would benefit Qihoo, as Qihoo 360 can quickly enhance its search market share through acquisition and rapidly generate revenue.
