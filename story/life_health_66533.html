21-day Weight Loss Method: Stage One, First 3 Days:

Complete fasting, only drinking water. (Prerequisite: Normal physical condition, drink no less than 2500 milliliters of water per day.)

21-day Weight Loss Method: Stage Two, Middle 8 Days:

Each meal (breakfast, lunch, and dinner), eat a small dish of lightly salted boiled vegetables (excluding tofu and starchy foods like potatoes). Eat until you're 80% full and drink plenty of water. Have one medium-sized fruit in the morning and afternoon.

21-day Weight Loss Method: Stage Three, Last 10 Days:

Resume normal eating habits but only eat until half-full (just enough to satisfy hunger). Avoid snacks entirely. Try to have dinner between 5-6 PM and do not consume any food after that.
