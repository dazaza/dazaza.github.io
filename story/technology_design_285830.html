New Internet marketing, have you applied it?

by net6612822 on 2009-11-29 19:36:45

As a branch of marketing, network advertising, online news, and search engine marketing constitute the main body of traditional online marketing. In the era of rapid network development, online marketing is increasingly valued. With the development of the network, online marketing has also extended to various new marketing methods, such as blog marketing, forum marketing, crowdsourcing marketing, video marketing, embedded marketing, SEO, and survey marketing.

1. Blog Marketing

Blog marketing can be considered a marketing method related to blogs. You can promote your brand and products through celebrity blogs, hire a large number of grassroots bloggers to help you promote, or establish your own corporate or personal blog. Writing high-quality articles and publishing them on blogs is important. Pay attention to the quality of the articles, strive to be recommended by the site, and then many websites will reprint them, generating a powerful promotional effect.

2. Forum Marketing

When it comes to forums, people think it's just about posting threads. However, there are countless skills involved in posting threads. Besides the main content of the thread, your avatar, name, and signature are areas where you can make an impact. This involves some technical knowledge about code and image animation processing. Therefore, forum marketing is not just about posting threads; maximizing the role of threads requires a lot of knowledge. There are specialized network consulting companies that provide forum marketing services for enterprises. They have a large number of forum users and moderator resources scattered across various forums nationwide, which they use to stir up hype together. However, their fees are not cheap. If you do forum marketing yourself, make your name and avatar attractive, display promotional content in your signature, and write engaging thread content with advertising content that feels natural and reasonable. Such threads will have high visit and reply volumes, high re-print rates, thus forming a strong and continuous promotional effect.

3. Crowdsourcing Marketing

Crowdsourcing marketing is a relatively newer marketing method. To master crowdsourcing marketing, you first need to understand the operational model of crowdsourcing. The crowdsourcing model generally refers to the operational model of crowdsourcing websites, which is the commonly used reward model. Enterprises publish reward-based creative collection according to their needs. While collecting creativity, the promotional effect brought to the enterprise is immeasurable. Crowdsourcing marketing values this ancillary value. On crowdsourcing websites, you often see names being collected for tens of thousands of yuan, naming products, design competitions, etc., all of which are ways of crowdsourcing marketing. The creative ideas they collect may not be worth that much money, but the promotional value far exceeds the reward. If other methods were used for promotion, it might cost several times more, but the effect might not be as good as crowdsourcing marketing. Speaking of this, we should also ask: Is crowdsourcing marketing omnipotent? The answer is no. We need to consider whether the crowdsource group is the target audience of the enterprise. If not, this marketing method is not suitable. Currently, mainstream crowdsourcing websites like Zhubajie, Task China, First Crowdsourcing Network, Crowdsourcing China, K68, etc., all support crowdsourcing marketing.

4. Video Marketing

Video marketing is a marketing method that has emerged with the popularity of video websites. Of course, placing various advertisements on video websites is also called video marketing. Here, I am talking about a "four liang moves a thousand jin" style of promotion, which is basically a form of promotion without financial investment. Video websites are open and allow free uploading and downloading, providing opportunities for ordinary users to use clever tactics for promotion. Simply uploading video materials usually has limited promotional effects. The video production should be humorous, relevant to current events, and promotional, capturing the social hotspots at the time, aligning with user thoughts, speaking for the people, and subtly including some content promoting the enterprise brand or product. Such videos will have high attention and high re-print rates. As long as they are reprinted frequently, the promotional effect will be achieved.

5. Embedded Marketing

The development of the gaming industry, especially some life-like games like the SNS websites that have been popular on the Internet in recent years, has pushed embedded marketing to its peak. This involves embedding the brand or product to be promoted into games, life-like scenes, or even directly displayed advertisements. For example, the embedded marketing done by MOTO mobile phones on Kaixin.com before the New Year is a very typical case of embedded marketing. Actually, embedded marketing is not a new marketing idea; it has been used before, such as content advertisements in TV dramas giving a big shot to a certain brand product, which is consistent with the idea of embedded marketing. It’s just that at that time, it was not called embedded marketing.

6. SEO Marketing

SEO is the English abbreviation for search engine optimization, which has become an industry. It was generated alongside search engine marketing. Search engine marketing generally refers to doing advertisements on search engines, which is usually bidding for rankings, costing a lot and requiring continuous investment. SEO mainly focuses on optimization, aiming to rank optimally without advertisement fees. This depends on the search engine's crawling rules, with specialized optimizers. The knowledge in this area is not quantifiable but experiential. Important indicators include the standardization degree of the website's code, the quantity of backlinks, the daily IP volume and PV of the website, the setting of keywords and descriptions, the quantity of internal and external links, etc.

7. Survey Marketing

There is a group of websites on the Internet whose core business is surveys, and there are also people who earn money by participating in surveys. Enterprises can cooperate with survey websites to conduct comprehensive surveys of their brands, obtaining brand survey data while achieving a promotional effect, which is a new marketing method.

Older forms of online marketing still occupy the main position in online marketing, but new forms of online marketing should not be underestimated, as they sometimes produce unexpected results.
