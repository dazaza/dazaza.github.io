—When the other party is disappointed, it's a good time to pursue them—

If you compare the self to a balloon, then the psychological state of the balloon rapidly ascending in a cloudless sky is what we call the sense of expansion. On the contrary, the mental state when it’s drenched by autumn rain and lying on the ground is what we call the sense of shrinkage. A person who is experiencing this "sense of expansion" does not need the opposite sex as urgently. If they experience a sense of shrinkage, the situation changes completely. At this point, they will earnestly seek out their true love. What is referred to as experiencing a sense of shrinkage is when a person encounters some kind of failure, setback, or crisis; deeply feels loneliness; is disheartened and their self-esteem is hurt; falls into severe feelings of inferiority... If you have someone special at your workplace, you can approach her when she experiences this sense of shrinkage, for example, when she has been scolded by her boss and looks gloomy, sitting there alone—this is your good opportunity. If she catches a cold that doesn't heal for a long time, it's a great chance. You can straightforwardly tell her: “You must take care of yourself, otherwise, I'll be worried sick!” When a lover is hospitalized, visiting with flowers to increase intimacy is a common method used by many people, so you might as well seize the opportunity and use it effectively.

—Love reaches its peak when it encounters some degree of obstacles—

Assume two lovers say, "We're going to get married," and both sets of parents, relatives, friends, seniors, and acquaintances all approve and give blessings. In such a case, the feelings between the two may not ignite. Because everything goes smoothly without stimulation, instead leaving a vague feeling. Conversely, if love faces stubborn opposition from the mother saying, "What! As long as I'm alive, no matter who pleads for you, I absolutely won’t allow it—I say no and that's final!" This firm opposition makes both parties anxious, trying hard to persuade. If this path doesn't work, they might even think since they can't be persuaded, there's no point in hesitating—just do it first and worry about consequences later!

—It's a good time to pursue a working woman when she feels tired of her job—

The following are good opportunities to meet professional women:

• During group activities. Since the atmosphere is entirely different from that in the company, even pure-hearted girls can be attracted by male colleagues.

Below are periods when women are more relaxed and thus easier to meet:

• After drinking chilled white wine. After drinking alcohol, guard relaxes and excitement increases, which men should understand.

• On rainy days. On rainy days, most stay indoors, feeling a bit irritable, and guard is also lower.

• On payday. On payday, naturally cheerful, wanting to enjoy oneself.

You should appear gentle and considerate towards her, treating her like a fragile doll. Unless it's a matter of principle, try to accommodate her and make her happy. If the timing is right, you can convince her. The characteristic of girls at this age is to enjoy playing and having fun; taking her to entertainment venues will make her very happy. Activities with excitement should definitely include her. Sometimes, "coaxing" is important.
