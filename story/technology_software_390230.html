The store is up and running, Guangzhou VIPshop Information Technology Co., Ltd., now it's time to consider what to sell. There are two types of online stores: one sells physical goods, the other does agency business. Another consideration is how to find the source of goods. It's very difficult to deal with physical goods initially. First, it requires a large investment; second, buying goods is tiring; third, it's hard to attract customers to a new store.

Generally, physical goods sources are in wholesale market cities, such as Guangzhou, Hangzhou, Yiwu, etc. Alternatively, there’s also overseas purchasing, which usually involves foreign goods. This requires either having foreign friends or being able to frequently travel abroad. Moreover, customs clearance for overseas purchases can be troublesome.

There are two types of agencies: one is physical goods agency, the other is virtual goods agency. Agency is a good choice for those just starting their store because: 1. Low investment; 2. Low risk; 3. Easy to start; 4. No need to find sources of goods yourself, saving effort.

Now let's talk about the pros and cons of physical goods agency. The advantages are: no need to ship goods yourself, no need to worry about sourcing and inventory issues.

The disadvantages are: first, since you can't touch the quality of the goods yourself, buyers may be dissatisfied with the product they receive, leading to disputes.

Second, since your store is new, very few buyers will come, especially when many sellers offer the same products you're representing, like clothes or novelty daily-use items for home. These things aren’t bought or used every day.

Third, you don’t have a price advantage because the goods might already be third-hand from the agent, with prices marked up. For example, if they give you clothes for 40 RMB, you'll have to sell them for 45 or 50 RMB to make a profit. However, others might sell them for 40 RMB, leaving you without profit.

Fourth, generally, these agents who recruit sub-agents offer mediocre-quality goods. Consider shops with high popularity and customer traffic; they often have unique items. So, mass-market item sellers recruit agents, making it harder to attract customers based on appearance alone.

Fifth, these merchants usually just provide data packages and don’t teach you anything else. Since your new store can't attract customers due to price and appearance, this will lead to low sales, merely displaying someone else’s pictures without earning money or building credibility.
