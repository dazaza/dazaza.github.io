What is Online Group Buying B2T?

by hophi on 2008-11-19 06:46:21

Internationally, it is commonly referred to as B2T (Business To Team), which is another e-commerce model following B2B, B2C, and C2C. It refers to a team purchasing from a business.

Group buying (B2T) was originally defined as "group procurement," but now with the widespread use of the internet, group buying has become a consumer revolution participated in by many Chinese people. The internet has become a new way of consumption. So-called online group buying refers to consumers who do not know each other using the power of the internet to gather funds, increasing their bargaining power with merchants to obtain the best price. Although online group buying has only been around for a little over two years, it has already become a popular new way of consumption among internet users. It is understood that the main force behind current online group buying is the young group aged 25 to 35, and it is very common in large cities such as Beijing, Shanghai, and Shenzhen.

The prices of group-buying goods are more favorable. Although group buying is not yet the mainstream mode of consumption, its explosive power has gradually revealed itself. Industry insiders say that online group buying has changed the rules of traditional consumption. The core advantage of group buying lies in more favorable product prices. Depending on the number of people participating in the group buy and the quantity of products ordered, consumers can generally receive discounts ranging from 5% to 40%. It is introduced that there are roughly three types of current online group buying: the first type is self-organized group buying; the second type is professional group buying behavior, where quite a few different types of group-buying companies, websites, and individuals have already appeared; the third type is group buying organized by the salespeople themselves. The common point among these three forms is that participants can obtain products at a lower price than market price while ensuring they are genuine. How can one make the most cost-effective purchases through online group buying? Ms. Zhao, who has rich experience in online group buying, believes that before participating in group buying, one should conduct market research and understand the price, brand, and performance of the product they want to buy. Only when one has a clear understanding will they not be confused by so-called "group-buying discounts" on the internet.

The earliest appearance of group buying in China was when companies gathered all their subsidiaries to purchase in order to reduce costs. The development to the current "personal level" owes much to the internet, where influential individuals in the industry or professional group-buying service companies (group-buying websites) organize potential buyers of the same product into large-scale purchases directly from manufacturers or distributors, thus obtaining ultra-low prices and service guarantees while ensuring quality; or spontaneous group buying, where consumers organize themselves and post group-buying product information on websites. Online group buying has already become popular in large cities such as Shanghai, Beijing, and Guangzhou, and is gradually developing into a new type of consumption model. China Wangzhong has built a network group-buying platform in response to the demands of the times, combining the actual situation of China's e-commerce to provide the most professional group-buying services for both businesses and customers.

Now, under the slogan of "saving money is the truth" on group-buying websites and posts, small items like books, software, toys, home appliances, digital products, mobile phones, computers, and larger items like furniture, building materials, real estate, whose prices are not very transparent, all have consumers forming groups online to purchase. Moreover, online group buying has also expanded to health check-ups, insurance, travel, education and training, as well as various beauty, fitness, and leisure service sectors.

The Development Process of B2T

For online group buying (B2T) and real-life group buying, there still exists a bottleneck, which is logistics, transaction security, and payment. Therefore, B2T cannot immediately reach its ideal stage without going through some steps. According to the development process of China's e-commerce, B2T should still not be detached from reality and immediately enter the ideal stage. Its development is divided into two stages: First, mainly as an information-sharing stage, where buyers interested in the same product would first learn through the B2T platform how many people are interested in jointly purchasing the same product, then contact each other offline through the network to conduct transactions, serving as an information-sharing platform. Second, a trading platform that not only concentrates purchasing power through the network but also allows direct transactions on the network. Buying and selling transactions are conducted on the B2T platform. From a regional perspective, B2T should first start from cities and then spread nationwide, meaning B2T must first localize, and after local development, expand across provinces.

The Main Differences Between B2T and B2C E-commerce Models

The B2T e-commerce transaction model is primarily an e-commerce model centered on online group buying. Of course, this alone is insufficient to explain the differences between B2T and B2C because slightly modifying the B2C process can form B2T. So what are the main differences between the B2T and B2C e-commerce models? The B2T e-commerce model has its own era characteristics, which distinguish it from all other e-commerce models. One of the initial era characteristics of the B2T e-commerce model is taking a localized approach, requiring the operated B2T e-commerce platform to support both online and offline payment methods. At this stage, the association between the e-commerce platform and physical stores will play the maximum advantage of B2T. Under the rich variety of sales models, network group buying plays a dominant role. Through the communication system of this e-commerce platform, the functions of network group buying are fully demonstrated (no nation in the world loves online chatting more than ours). Logistics should not be a problem for localized e-commerce.

The differences between B2T and any other e-commerce transaction model can be simply described in a few sentences. With the continuous improvement of our country's e-commerce system in all aspects, the B2T e-commerce model has specific detail adjustments. In the current stage of our country's internet technology development, too many consumers who wish to participate in e-commerce lack the ability to operate Alipay, too many middle-aged and elderly people who wish to participate in e-commerce lack trust in merchants, and too many people who wish to participate in e-commerce find the operation process of existing e-commerce platforms too complicated. Our country's e-commerce has indeed achieved commendable results, but these defects remain undeniable. However, due to these defects, out of the 160 million internet users in our country, only more than 40 million users participate in e-commerce, which is worth pondering by relevant personnel in our country's e-commerce field.
