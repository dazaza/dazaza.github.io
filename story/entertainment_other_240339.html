So-called three talents

They are天才 (talent of heaven), 人才 (human talent), and 地才 (talent of earth). They are respectively the unit digits of the numbers in 天格 (heaven grid), 人格 (human grid), and 地格 (earth grid).

There are a total of 10 numbers in the numerological principles of heaven, earth, and human talents. If the unit digit is 0, it is calculated as 10.

The five elements are divided according to numerology.

The relationship between the five elements is: wood, fire, earth, metal, and water generate each other when adjacent, and overcome each other when separated.

Thus, based on the intrinsic connection between numerology and the five elements, the configuration derived from the calculations is referred to as the three talents configuration.

By observing the auspiciousness or inauspiciousness of the three talents configuration, one can judge and grasp your overall fortune, predict your chances of career success, and assess your physical condition.
