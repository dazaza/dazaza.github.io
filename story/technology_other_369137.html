The Ministry of Agriculture deploys disaster relief work

Jindi Agriculture摘自Beijing Evening News, October 23 (reporter Feng Hua) - According to information from the Ministry of Agriculture, in response to the strongest cold air mass since the beginning of autumn this year, the Ministry recently issued an urgent notice to the 17 provinces (regions, municipalities) affected by the strong winds and temperature drops. It requires all levels of agricultural departments to quickly implement various measures, earnestly do a good job in disaster prevention and relief work, and strive to minimize disaster losses.

October 23 report (reporter Liu Yi) - Affected by the strong cold air mass, temperatures have dropped in most parts of northern China. Compared to the same time yesterday, at 14:00 today, temperatures in the northwestern part of Qinghai, central and western Gansu, most parts of Inner Mongolia, northern Ningxia, central and western Heilongjiang, western Jilin, and western Liaoning have dropped by 4-8℃. Among these areas, the central part of Gansu and Inner Mongolia experienced the sharpest temperature drop, with the temperature in Xilinhot dropping by 18℃.

The Central Meteorological Observatory issued a yellow warning for strong winds at sea at 18:00 today. The Central Meteorological Observatory continued to issue a blue warning for cold waves at 18:00 today. It is expected that from 20:00 on the 23rd to 20:00 on the 25th, most parts of Gansu, northern Qinghai, Ningxia, Shaanxi, southern and eastern Inner Mongolia, Northeast, North China, Huaihai region, Jianghuai region, Jianghan region, northeastern Jiangnan, eastern Sichuan Basin, and central and northern Chongqing will experience a temperature drop of 6-12℃. In some areas of Ningxia, northeastern Shanxi, northern Hebei, southeastern Inner Mongolia, and most parts of Northeast, the temperature drop could reach 14-16℃; These regions will also be accompanied by 4-6 level northerly winds. Some areas along the Tianshan Mountains in northern Xinjiang will see moderate snowfall, with heavy snow in some areas; central and eastern Gansu, Inner Mongolia, Ningxia, northern North China, and Northeast China will experience light to moderate rain turning into sleet or light to moderate snow, with heavy snow in some areas.

The notice pointed out that it is currently a critical period for autumn agricultural production. The Northeast, North China, Jianghuai, and Huaihai regions are the main agricultural production areas of our country. Strong winds and temperature drops can easily cause damage to vegetable greenhouses, greenhouses, livestock pens, and other facilities, leading to the death of young livestock due to freezing, crops in the field being frozen, and having an adverse impact on autumn agricultural production. All levels of agricultural departments must attach great importance to the defense against strong winds and temperature drops, effectively enhance a sense of responsibility and urgency, focus on key areas, act swiftly, deploy immediately, implement responsibilities, and ensure the safety of autumn agricultural production.
