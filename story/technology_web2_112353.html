Tunerec: Your music, Everywhere - Sweet IT.Net

by xjyzhenai on 2008-12-18 09:23:05

Tunerec is a special music website. Our common music websites generally provide online search for music, online listening to music and other functions. Joining the SNS music sharing community allows us to better discover and share music, and make more friends with music as the theme, such as Xiami network, Tadpole network, etc. So what is Tunerec up to?

image

Tunerec is a platform that can help you build your own music library and make several thousand songs available to you. When you sign up for an account, the platform automatically starts recording music for you from thousands of radio stations. The service is completely web-based. No need to download any software and plug-ins. Therefore, Tunerec can be well adapted to mobile environments and different operating system platforms.

Tunerec has a clean and simple interface that allows you to enjoy music in the most intuitive way. Of course, in the era of SNS popularity, Tunerec did not forget to add SNS elements to its website architecture. Tunerec has built a small social network that aims to enhance your music experience. Perhaps the establishment of many sharing communities is SNS for the sake of SNS. However, Tunerec's goal of establishing SNS is to serve Tunerec itself. Then you can organize your songs into playlists and share them with friends. You can also watch other people's music. Here is an official video to help you get to know Tunerec better.
