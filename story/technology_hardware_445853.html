He touched my heart DBA

by tiantian2d87 on 2011-06-14 17:15:01

1. A girl can't fall asleep, so she takes her mobile phone to go on QQ while lying in bed. She sees that he is online and starts chatting with him. Then she falls asleep by accident without replying. When she wakes up the next morning, she sees his last sentence: "Fool, are you asleep again? Good night!" Wouldn't those days be very happy? Wouldn't you feel very warm?

Is there someone who stays on QQ for you during the sleepless nights, waits until you sleep soundly, then softly says: "Good night, fool!"?

2. More than 700 years ago one day, the Prince of North Wales went out hunting and left the dog at home to look after the baby.

When the prince returned, he saw blood-stained blankets but could not find the baby.

And the dog, licking the blood from its mouth, looked at him happily.

The prince was furious and stabbed the dog's stomach with a knife.

The dog let out a painful cry and woke up the baby sleeping under the blood-stained blanket.

At this moment, the prince found a dead wolf lying in the corner of the room.

3. When he proposed to her, he only said three words: believe me;

When she gave birth to their first daughter, he said to her: you've worked hard;

On the day their daughter married far away, he hugged her shoulder and said: I'm still here;

On the day he received the notice of her critical illness, he repeatedly told her: I am here;

At the moment she was about to leave, he kissed her forehead and whispered: I'll wait for you.

In this life, he never once told her "I love you," but his love has never left her side.

4. In the year of famine, the son decided to abandon his blind old mother.

One day, he made up a lie and carried his mother into the distant mountains.

After putting down his mother, he was already exhausted and felt no strength at all.

He said: Mother, wait here for a moment, I will go and get food for you.

Just as he was about to leave, his mother grabbed him: Son, I have a piece of bread in my pocket, eat it before you go.

Upon hearing this, the son burst into tears and fell to his knees in front of his mother...

5. When studying, Mom said: when you graduate, Mom will be happy, DBA.

When graduating, Mom said: when you find a job, Mom will be happy.

When working, Mom said: when you get married, Mom will be happy.

When getting married, Mom said: when you have children, Mom will be happy.

Now the child has grown up, I say we can be happy now.

But Mom, can you hear me?

6. After marriage, the boy treats his wife better than before marriage.

At a gathering, friends laughed at him: why are you still so sweet after getting married...?

He smiled awkwardly and said: before marriage, many boys wanted to pursue her, many boys would treat her well, I had to treat her even better to win her; after marriage, fewer and fewer boys treat her well, I have to treat her even better so that she doesn't feel disappointed.

All I do is to make her happy.

After he finished speaking, all the friends present were silent.
