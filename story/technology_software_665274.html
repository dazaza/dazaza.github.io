Blessings are a bunch of flowers, blessings are deep affection, and blessings are a responsibility. On the occasion of bidding farewell to the old and welcoming the new, I send these blessings to you. A hasty parting, who would have known that it would be hard to meet again, with eager anticipation and long thoughts, all integrated into heartfelt wishes. May my friend be healthy and content in the new year. Auspicious snow foretells a bountiful year; beneath the deep snow lies a friend's silent attention and soundless blessing for you. Wishing you a fulfilling and romantic New Year, may happiness always reside in your poetic heart. Meeting is fate, knowing each other is understanding, meeting is so difficult, and gathering is so brief. Alas, I can only send a blessing from afar, wishing you a pleasant holiday and smooth sailing in all matters. Your enthusiasm has warmed my frozen heart; your boldness has stirred my passion for love; your care has aroused my gratitude. At the start of the new year, let us look forward to a beautiful future through the shimmering waves and misty waters of the lake of love. Often thinking of you in my heart; silently blessing you in my soul, with romantic messages. Wishing you a happy holiday, sweet and blissful. Gently pushing open the winter window, watching the snowflakes drift. Thus reminded, I send you a message: are you alright? Truly missed. Praying that you find sweetness and joy in the new year. Year after year, through wind and rain; day after day, sunrise and sunset. The deep affection of friends seeps into the heart. On this occasion of the Spring Festival, I offer a cup of pure wine, wishing you health and happiness! With more joy comes more beauty, with more happiness comes more good fortune. May the festival's joy forever accompany you. Two tracks of footprints recite long poems; the power of love supports the heavy winter nights, leading us into a white dream. Happy New Year to you! Hoping the best blessings and joys fill your coming year. From your heart to my heart. Let us tread a path of flowers, in the new year, all things go well, auspicious and healthy happiness. Picking a cluster of concentrated poetry, let me gently tell you, I am silently missing you. Let the beautiful memories remain in remembrance, sending warm blessings to you, wishing you a happy holiday. This holiday is like a key, opening the days of longing. The flowers of blessings speak in detail about our once-held expectations. Happy holidays! Good fortune, prosperity, wealth, and fullness of gold and jade, blessings of happiness, longevity, and good luck! On the canvas of my memory, many beautiful reminiscences have been added, as splendid as brocade! Please accept my deep blessings. May all joy accompany you forever.
