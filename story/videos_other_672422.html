The newly launched Tianrui series by Nuolo - the round arc seamless large waterway radiator, has a fashionable appearance, achieved through electrostatic spraying equipment. Upon close inspection, you will find that the cap of the radiator is completely seamless. The cap uses thickened dedicated high-quality deep drawing steel plate, integrally formed without seams, thus offering higher pressure resistance and ensuring higher safety. How to choose a steel radiator:

Material: The quality of the steel raw material directly affects the service life of the radiator. Currently, using seamless steel for the main pipe and low-carbon steel for the branch pipes is an ideal combination. Among the many steel manufacturers, Shanghai Baosteel, which produces high-quality low-carbon steel from imported foreign ores, has the best reputation.

Its interior is also coated with enhanced "nano-water-based radiator internal anti-corrosion coating," which can effectively resist oxygen corrosion and significantly enhance the durability of the radiator.

To strengthen the internal convection effect of the radiator, there are now new products on the market that use two waterways per piece. These are divided into flat dual waterways and 3D dual waterways according to their arrangement. Many manufacturers currently produce the flat type, while due to patent protection, only Nuolo exclusively owns the 3D type.

Steel radiators are currently the mainstream in the market. The following points will guide you in selecting one:

Copper-aluminum composite radiators, as the name suggests, are made up of copper and aluminum materials. Thanks to copper's corrosion resistance and aluminum's fast heat conduction, they form radiators with high heat dissipation and long lifespan. However, due to the high cost of TP2 pure copper materials required for radiators, they are much more expensive than steel radiators.

Internal anti-corrosion treatment: Internal anti-corrosion is very important because the current heating water quality varies due to system and equipment issues. More advanced internal anti-corrosion technologies include nano-water-based anti-corrosion coatings.

Surface treatment: To ensure the radiator's outer surface is smooth, glossy, and has long-lasting anti-corrosion properties, processes such as phosphating, electrophoresis, resin electrostatic spraying, and high-temperature baking must not be skipped.

In the radiator market, brands like Nuolo, Send, and Kajie are all good choices. These brands each have their strengths in material selection, craftsmanship, and adoption of new technologies, appealing to consumers at different levels.

Although domestic new-type radiators have not been around for very long, their development speed has been extremely rapid. Currently, China has become the world's largest radiator production and consumption country.
