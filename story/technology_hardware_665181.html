First, I wish everyone a happy Valentine's Day, remember to throw flowers. Last night I dreamed of an ultimate event, money, future, women, love, life, and I dreamed of many things. Guo Lao once said, "What you think about during the day will appear in your dreams at night." It seems that I was thinking about my silly wife during the day, otherwise, I wouldn't keep dreaming about her at night. And in my dream, I was very unfortunate. Below are some interpretations of the dream:

[1. Celestial Phenomena Section] (Sun)

If a man dreams of the rising sun, he is about to have good luck, and his life will be happy. If a woman dreams of the rising sun, she will give birth to a boy who will grow up to be an outstanding person. If a man dreams of the setting sun, he will encounter misfortune. If a married woman dreams of the setting sun, she will give birth to a girl. If an unmarried woman dreams of the setting sun, she will marry into a poor family. Dreaming of the noonday sun means promotion. If a sick person dreams of the noonday sun, their health will soon recover. Dreaming of the sun suddenly being covered by clouds indicates that bad days are coming. Dreaming of sunlight falling on one's bed is not a good omen, it means illness. Dreaming of sunlight filling one's house indicates happiness and joy in life. Dreaming of the scorching sun means one may suffer from eye disease. If a merchant dreams of the blazing sun, his business will suffer losses. If a traveler dreams of the blazing sun, they will encounter many difficulties and accidents on the journey. Dreaming of sleeping under the scorching sun means one will fall into trouble but eventually succeed.

[2. Celestial Phenomena Section] (Clouds)

Dreaming of clouds is an ominous sign. Dreaming of clouds being blown away by the wind, leaving a clear sky, indicates that disasters will soon be eliminated and will not return. Dreaming of clouds near the sun is a good omen, meaning one can escape troubles with the help of influential people. Dreaming of white clouds indicates that one's crops and those of the neighbors will have a bountiful harvest. Dreaming of dark clouds indicates that there will be an epidemic in the residential area. Dreaming of colorful clouds indicates that there will be bloodshed among the neighbors or enemies invading the country. Dreaming of flying in the clouds indicates that one will become a leader in the residential area.

[3. Mountain and River Section] (Earth)

Dreaming of the earth and other planets orbiting the sun is a good omen, indicating good health and longevity. Dreaming of the earth cracking or having fissures indicates that most areas of the country will suffer disasters and cholera will spread. Dreaming of the surrounding earth rising indicates sudden promotion.

[4. Animal Section] (Toad)

Dreaming of a toad eating the sun or moon indicates that a working person will soon get a raise or promotion; students will see rapid academic improvement.

- Dreaming of a moon hanging on the treetops indicates restlessness. Conversations with friends or lovers might easily upset you. It's crucial to stay calm.

- Dreaming of the moon shining on your body indicates potential health issues. Pay special attention to respiratory diseases like colds or tonsillitis.

- Dreaming of the moon visible in the daytime indicates impending misfortune. You might be extorted or harassed by drunkards. It's best to stay home and lead a quiet life.

- Dreaming of the moon reflected on water indicates an increase in leisure activities. Instead of wandering around cafes or amusement parks, going outdoors will bring good fortune.

- Dreaming of walking on the moon indicates increased intelligence. Your mind will become exceptionally sharp, leading to good results in exams or work, gaining recognition from others.
