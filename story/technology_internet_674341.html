Mother strangled to death, 4-year-old daughter leads the way to capture the suspect

by xue94fwsh on 2012-02-24 12:24:09

"I woke up and found my mom dead, I couldn't wake her up no matter how I pushed." The little girl cried while holding onto Aunt Wang's clothes. Aunt Wang hugged the little girl tightly and dared not look into the inner room. She trembled as she used her mobile phone to call 120 and 110 respectively.

"Once I saw them arguing, the man wanted the woman to repay the money, there might be financial dealings between them," an informed person said. Last week, they had a quarrel due to property disputes and parted on bad terms.

(Clue provided by Mr. Xiao: 200 yuan)

"Spicy character, very loving towards her daughter"

At 3:20 PM, the emergency doctor arrived at the scene and found that the little girl's mother was ice cold all over, her body stiff, lying by the bed in the inner room. There were no vital signs, indicating death for quite some time.

According to those who knew the deceased well, the deceased surnamed Liu, originally from Tongcheng, Hubei, had just moved to the rental house less than two months ago with her daughter.

"Although of humble origin and with a spicy character, she loved her daughter very much." It was introduced that after the birth of the little girl in 2007, Ms. Liu always kept her by her side, and this year, she started kindergarten, which was also handled by her.

"I know where uncle is." Under the lead of the little girl, several policemen captured the suspect on the spot in a rental house 500 meters away.

The informed person said that the deceased was married, but her husband was rarely around, never seen before.

Yesterday evening at 6 o'clock, the crime scene was sealed off by police tape, with multiple criminal investigation personnel on site for inspection. According to one of the case handlers, the suspect was indeed caught, but more details about the case are not convenient to disclose.

It was understood that Ms. Liu and the suspect had maintained an intimate relationship for at least three years, "they already knew each other, sometimes he came over to stay." The informant said that after his wife became a vegetable due to difficult childbirth and passed away, he always regarded Ms. Liu as his lover.

Under the leadership of the four-year-old girl covered in bruises, the police caught the suspect who strangled her mother to death at their residence. At 3 PM yesterday afternoon, a woman was found dead in a rental house in Wenyuan Village, Wenchong, Huangpu District, with obvious strangulation marks on her neck. It was learned that the suspect had an intimate relationship with the deceased before her death. Currently, the police are still conducting further investigations.

The emergency doctor preliminarily determined that the little girl's mother should have been strangled to death.

"My mom was strangled to death by an uncle, he bought us breakfast in the morning, then they fought, and I don't remember anything after that." After the police arrived, the little girl hurriedly told the police.

Yesterday evening, the little girl who had lost her mother was sent to the hospital for treatment, fortunately without major issues.

■ Deceased

At 3 PM yesterday afternoon, the cries of a little girl echoed through the alleyway of Wenyuan. Aunt Wang from the neighboring convenience store followed the crying sound and found the little girl, "Her face was swollen, with red strangulation marks on her neck, tears and snot had soaked her clothes." Aunt Wang recalled that the four-year-old girl was only wearing a single piece sleepwear, barefoot and shivering at the door.
