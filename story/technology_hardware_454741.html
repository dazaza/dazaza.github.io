Praying for you (The Great Compassion Mantra)

Na Mo He Na Da Na Duo Na Ye Ye Na Mo

Namo, drink the nectar, give it to others, bestow upon them night and day. Namo,

AO Li Ye Po Lu Jie Di Shuo Bo Na Ye

Oh lotus flower, ferry across the sea, tell the Bodhisattva's story.

Pu Ti Sa Duo Po Ye Mo He Sa Duo Po Ye

Praise the Bodhisattva who is beyond measure, praise him in every way.

Mo He Jia Lu Ni Jia Ye An Sa Bo Na Fa Yi

Beyond measure, bring peace to all living beings, let there be no end to compassion.

Su Da Na Da Xia Na Mo Xi Ji Lie Duo Yi Mong AO Li Ye

Praise the Buddha who has great compassion, namo to the one who saves all sentient beings, immeasurable ones, oh lotus flower.

Po Lu Jie Di Shi Fu Na Leng Tuo Po

Ferry across the sea, open the door to enlightenment, let the light shine brightly.

Na Mo No Na Jin Chi Shi Li Mo He Ba Duo Suo Mi

Namo, honor the one who brings blessings, with great kindness and compassion, protect all sentient beings.

Sa Po AO Ta Dou Shu Peng AO Shi Yun Sa Po Sa Duo

All beings, come together, gather like clouds in the sky, let the light of wisdom shine forever.

Na Mo Po Qie Mo Fa Te Dou

Namo, the one who is beyond words, the Dharma wheel turns eternally.

Da Zhi Ta An AO Po Lu Xi Lu Jia Di Jia Lu Di Yi Xi Li

Great compassion, oh mysterious power, ferry across the sea, subdue the demons, bring peace to all.

Mo He Pu Ti Sa Duo Sa Po Sa Po

Beyond measure, the Bodhisattva's compassion flows endlessly, spreading everywhere.

Mo Na Mo Na Mo Xi Mo Xi Lie Tuo Yun Ju Lu Ju Lu Jie Mong

Mourn not, mourn not, may all beings be free from suffering, gather together the clouds of compassion, eliminate all obstacles.

Du Lu Du Lu Fa She Ye Di Mo He Fa She Ye Di

Dwell in peace, dwell in peace, let the Dharma rain fall, purify all defilements.

Tuo Na Tuo Na Di Li Ni Shi Fu Na Ye

Lift up your heart, lift up your heart, tread the path of virtue, enter the realm of purity.

Zhe Na Zhe Na Mo Mo Fa Mo Na Mo Die Lie

Shelter us, shelter us, protect the Dharma, guard the teachings.

Yi Xi Yi Xi Shi Na Shi Na Ou Na Sen Fu Na She Li

Let the light of wisdom shine, let the Dharma wheel turn, may all beings attain liberation.

Fa Sa Fa Seng Fu Na She Ye

Spread the Dharma far and wide, let the Sangha flourish, may all beings attain Nirvana.

Hu Lu Hu Lu Mo Na Hu Lu Hu Lu Xi Li Suo Na Suo Na

Hail, hail, may all beings be protected, spread the Dharma everywhere.

Xi Li Xi Li Su Lu Su Lu Pu Ti Ye Pu Ti Ye

Let the light of wisdom shine, let the Dharma wheel turn, awaken to the truth.

Pu Tuo Ye Pu Tuo Ye Mi Di Li Ye

Prajñāparamitā, Prajñāparamitā, enter the gate of wisdom.

Nuo Na Jin Chi Di Li Se Ni Na Po Ye Mo Na

Protect all beings, bring them to safety, let there be no end to compassion.

Suo Po He Xi Tuo Ye Suo Po He Mo He Xi Tuo Ye

Sabbāññu, sabbāññu, may all beings be enlightened.

Suo Po He Xi Tuo Yu Yi Shi Bo Na Ya

Sabbāññu, sabbāññu, may all beings attain liberation.

Suo Po He Nuo Na Jin Chi Suo Po He

Sabbāññu, sabbāññu, protect all beings.

Mo Na Nuo Na Suo Po He Xi Na Sen AO Mu Qie Ye

Mourn not, mourn not, may all beings be free from suffering, gather together the clouds of compassion.

Suo Po He Suo Po Mo He AO Xi Tuo Ye

Sabbāññu, sabbāññu, may all beings be enlightened.

Suo Po He Zhe Ji Na AO Xi Tuo Ye

Sabbāññu, sabbāññu, may all beings attain liberation.

Suo Po he Bo Tuo Mo Ji Xi Tuo Ye

Sabbāññu, sabbāññu, may all beings be enlightened.

Suo Po He Nuo Na Jin Chi Bu Qie Na Ye

Sabbāññu, sabbāññu, protect all beings.

Suo Po He Mo Po Li Sen Ji Na Ye

Sabbāññu, sabbāññu, may all beings be free from suffering.

Suo Po He Na Mo He Na Da Na Duo Na Ye Ye

Sabbāññu, sabbāññu, namo to the one who saves all sentient beings.

Na Mo AO Li Ye Po Lu Jie Di Suo Bo Na Ye

Namo, oh lotus flower, ferry across the sea.

Suo Po He An Xi Dian Du

Sabbāññu, sabbāññu, may all beings attain liberation.

Man Duo Na Ba Tuo Ye Suo Po He

Mandala, vajra seat, sabbāññu.
