Fake coatings pass themselves off as genuine ones to give "kickbacks", and Hualei coatings deal heavy blows to fake ones on 3.15 Consumer Rights Day

by jinqingyang on 2009-07-14 11:31:14

Fake coatings impersonate and give "kickbacks" - Huaren Paints strikes hard on 3.15

Not only is "3.15" the Consumer Rights Protection Day, but it is also the rights protection day for well-known and quality enterprises. In response to the persistent issue of counterfeit coatings in the paint industry, Huaren Coatings, which has long been troubled by "piggybacking on famous brands," launched a large-scale anti-counterfeiting and rights protection campaign across the country as the 2009 "3.15" approached.

Starting from March 2, the legal department of Huaren Coatings Group went to various provinces and cities such as Beijing, Jiangsu, Fujian, Sichuan, Yunnan, Hubei, and Jiangxi, cooperating with local industrial and commercial departments to carry out anti-counterfeiting and rights protection actions. According to incomplete statistics, by March 16, the industrial and commercial departments across the country had seized more than 2,500 pieces of counterfeit Huaren coatings infringing products, over ten kinds of suspected infringing counterfeit products, involving an amount of more than 300,000 yuan.

It was learned that the names of the products involved in "piggybacking on famous brands" and infringing on the trademark rights of Guangdong Huaren Coatings Co., Ltd. that were uncovered during this anti-counterfeiting and rights protection activity include: "Hong Kong Huaren Paint", "New Century Huaren", "Guangde Huaren Paint Industry", "Century Huaren", "Nine Dragons Huaren", "Runmei Paint", "Runjing Paint", "Runpai Paint", "Huaren Aijiamai", "Huaren Le Wan Jia", "Guanrun Paint", "Huaren Ai Jia", "Jiangmen Silver Sea Huaren Paint Premium No.1". These products have respectively taken advantage of Huaren Coatings' brand recognition and reputation to mislead consumers for improper gains. The industrial and commercial law enforcement officers carefully compared these types of products, collected evidence, and confiscated them all.

According to Fang Xin, vice general manager of Huaren Coatings, although Huaren Coatings actively cooperates with the industrial and commercial department every year in anti-counterfeiting efforts, counterfeit and substandard products in the current coating industry are still rampant. Only in places like Nantong, Yangzhou, Zhenjiang, and Wuxi in Jiangsu province, the number of investigated counterfeit Huaren Coatings products such as "Huaren Paint Industry", "Century Huaren", "Nine Dragons Huaren", "Huaren Aijiamai", and "Huaren Le Wan Jia" reached more than a thousand pieces. The latest "Hong Kong Huaren Plant Oil Series Environmental Protection Paint" product was investigated for suspected misuse of the reputation of Huaren Coatings' well-known product "Natural Plant Oil Environmental Protection Wood Paint". In Chengdu and other places, the "New Century Huaren" product counterfeiting "Natural Plant Oil Environmental Protection Wood Paint" has almost identical factory name, address, and packaging compared to Huaren Coatings' authentic products.

In Wuhan, Hubei, the local office worked with the industrial and commercial department to destroy an infringing manufacturing den located at Jinghe Farm, investigating nearly 300 pieces of suspected infringing "Huaren Family Happiness" and "Huaren Star" products, along with over 100 packaging cans. At the same time, the Wuhan industrial and commercial law enforcement officers also assisted Huaren's legal department in dismantling fake Huaren store signs that misled consumers. In Yunnan, Beijing, Jiangxi, Fujian, and other places, the law enforcement departments also conducted inspections of the local building materials markets, and the anti-counterfeiting and rights protection actions in various places are being carried out in an orderly manner.

For a long time, Huaren Coatings, as a leading brand in China's coating industry, has always adhered to the principle of putting product quality and credibility first. However, being a big tree invites the wind; Huaren Coatings has been plagued by imitators for a long time. Counterfeit products imitating Huaren Coatings keep emerging. Meanwhile, regarding the "kickback" phenomenon currently troubling the entire coating industry, Fang Xin, vice general manager of Huaren Coatings, told reporters: As a quality enterprise, Huaren Coatings has always firmly advocated purifying the market environment and opposing unfair competition practices. "Some companies that imitate famous brands are very audacious; they not only openly manufacture and sell counterfeit products, but also to increase sales, extract high kickbacks for painters under the name of the legitimate manufacturer. This illegal behavior not only seriously damages the interests of consumers but also poses a huge threat to the personal safety of consumers, severely tarnishes the brand image of the legitimate coating manufacturers, devours the market of the legitimate enterprises, and disrupts the normal order of the market economy."

Fang Xin also expressed that although Huaren Coatings' anti-counterfeiting and rights protection actions this year on 3.15 have achieved great results, this also makes Huaren realize that the anti-counterfeiting actions are arduous and long-term. In the future, while Huaren Coatings continues to provide high-quality products to consumers, it will also work together with the industrial and commercial department to severely crack down on illegal imitators who harm the rights of consumers and Huaren Coatings under the guise of Huaren, vowing to continue the anti-counterfeiting and rights protection actions until the end.
