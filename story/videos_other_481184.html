How [men and women] can have a pleasant date

If you want to ask a girl out for dinner, what are some suitable places?

Since I'm not really a good conversationalist, it will be difficult to make her happy just through talking. So how can I make her feel happy? Are there any other ways? Preparing before the date is very important. This way, it also shows the girl that you are polite and considerate for the date.

I did some research on dating issues between men and women and grabbed two key points for your reference:

Important qualities that the other person should possess to become a dating partner:

1. Pleasant 2. Trustworthy 3. Considerate 4. Humorous 5. Clean appearance 6. Honest and upright 7. At ease 8. Emotional 9. Smart 10. Thoughtful 11. Appropriately dressed 12. Good at sports

Self-acceptance is quite an important quality: Try imagining this - if you became someone else, would you like the original you? Would you want to be friends with the original you? A person who loves themselves accepts themselves as they are, and is then willing to love others; someone who can rely on and trust themselves will enjoy cooperating with others and constantly improve themselves. When we love ourselves, accept ourselves, find real-life romantic stories, and express ourselves rather than judge our worth based on others' opinions of us, we become truly mature individuals. It is only then that we are truly ready to cultivate more mature love and interpersonal relationships.

From the two key points mentioned above:

1. Pleasant 2. Trustworthy 3. Considerate 4. Humorous 5. Clean appearance 6. Honest and upright 7. At ease 8. Emotional 9. Smart 10. Thoughtful 11. Appropriately dressed 12. Good at sports

Actually, having a pleasant date is quite easy.

Based on these points, I believe you can easily achieve them:

Clean, upright, considerate, appropriately dressed, thoughtful, and pleasant (having a pleasant attitude).

These qualities alone will make the other person feel that you are great during the date.

No one likes a guy who is:

Not clean, inconsiderate (such as not helping carry things, not being courteous, not paying attention to the other person's safety, etc.), sloppy in dress, thoughtless (speaking without thinking during conversations), or unpleasant (going on a date with a bad mood).
