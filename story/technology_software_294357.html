Bohai Securities: Bank of Ningbo adheres to the characteristic of serving small and medium-sized enterprises, benefiting from the warming of exports＿Stock Theory

by fj0953210 on 2009-12-05 21:27:45

Investment Highlights: Expectations for export improvement have strengthened, and the economy has returned to the boom period. According to data from Ningbo Survey Team of National Bureau of Statistics, in the second quarter of 2009, Ningbo entrepreneurs' confidence index and enterprise prosperity index reached over one hundred points, returning to the boom period. This shows that there are obvious signs of stabilization and recovery in Ningbo's economy. The product order prosperity index of Ningbo City in the second quarter was 97.7, which rose by 30 points compared to the previous quarter and still runs below the prosperity period. It also decreased by 19.4 points compared to the same period last year.

The growth rate of credit has accelerated and is expected to maintain a high growth rate in the second half of the year. By September 30th, the total loan amount reached 79.606 billion yuan, with an increase of 15.357 billion yuan in the third quarter, while in the first half of the year, it increased by 15.093 billion yuan. The proportion of loans from branches in other places has increased rapidly, with Shanghai branch increasing by 2.09%, Nanjing and Suzhou branches increasing by 8.17%, and Shenzhen branch increasing by 2.70%. Deposits grew by 37.5%, reaching 104.8 billion yuan.

Ningbo Bank adheres to its unique feature of serving small and medium-sized enterprises. Ningbo Bank has a different business feature from other banks - focusing on developing SME loan business. The company has a strict access system for SME customers, segmenting the customer base, and only considers clients who have been in operation for more than two years as potential customers. For loan approval of potential customers, not only financial indicators but also non-financial indicators such as utility bills and customs declaration forms are considered, along with the hobbies of the enterprise owner.

Investment Recommendations: We predict that the company's net profit will grow by 9% in 2009, with a net profit of 1.452 billion yuan and EPS of 0.58 yuan per share. In 2010, the net profit is expected to grow by 41.2%, reaching 2.05 billion yuan, with earnings per share of 0.71 yuan (after dilution). The corresponding PE is 19.03X, PB is 2.52X, maintaining a buy rating.

The procedures for buying and selling stocks are as follows: When buying or selling stocks, you must specify whether you want to buy or sell, the name (or code) of the stock, quantity, and price. Moreover, this order is only valid on the day it is placed. The content of the order includes the abbreviation (code) of the stock you want to trade, the quantity, and the price at which you want to buy or sell the stock. The abbreviation of the stock usually consists of three to four Chinese characters, and the stock code is six digits. When placing an order, the stock code and abbreviation must match. At the same time, there are certain regulations regarding the quantity of stocks traded: the quantity of stocks ordered to buy must be a multiple of 100, but the quantity of stocks ordered to sell does not need to be a multiple of 100.

There are four ways to place orders: counter submission, telephone automatic order, computer automatic order, and remote terminal order.
