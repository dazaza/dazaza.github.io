DELL-8135

Purchased in August 2007.

At that time, there was a lot of hype around water goods (grey market products) and counterfeit items.

In the end, I still got an official Chinese version of the 8135 for mainland China, at $195 including shipping.

This is the price I paid; it might have been a bit expensive, but I won't discuss that here...

_____________________________ A Glittering Divider __________________________

Feel Section.

The 8135 is, relatively speaking, one of the better keyboards I've used so far. It has good elasticity, with keys that are moderately soft.

When paired with a wrist rest, the comfort level improves noticeably...

But! Please don't spread rumors about how many keys on this keyboard have no conflict! Alright?

A+W+E conflicts, other conflicts I don't remember. But it doesn't affect playing CS, WAR3, or typing.

Just to brag a little, my English typing speed is 260 words per minute...^_^ Hehe...

I've previously used Logitech keyboards, Cool Shadow, Storm Hand, Avantgarde Crystal Sets, as well as others === ===.......

Maybe they're all low-end products in general.

However, the feel is not in the same league as these...

I haven't used mechanical keyboards yet, so I can't comment on them...

In my humble opinion,

Feel: 8/10

_____________________________ A Sad Divider ____________________________

Appearance Section.

When I first got it, the yellow shell was in a very simple packaging (in my opinion), which was plain and low-key...

Included inside: the keyboard, wrist rest, and a piece of paper....

The first impression when I saw it online was that it looked ugly (my opinion), not the type I liked. Maybe it's a bit unconventional, but it's not something most people, especially girls like me, would like...

However, generally speaking, guys really love its design... After attaching the wrist rest, it looks completely refreshed.

Due to personal reasons, I prefer keyboards with wrist rests.

Reason: Unknown....

Everyone has their own preferences... Hehe ^_^

But black keyboards always give a good feeling. The silver-white edges are simple and elegant, without losing any classic charm... You might gradually discover its beauty after using it for a while. At least, that's how I feel....

The design of the multimedia row is also pretty nice... In general, it's still good.

Drawbacks: The edges may start peeling paint. Mine peeled a little, but not severely...

Different people have different opinions...

Appearance: 8/10
