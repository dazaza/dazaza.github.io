Maekelin Changes Business Philosophy to Prepare for 2012 Chinese E-commerce

by maikaolin0111 on 2012-03-05 16:49:34

China's largest online apparel company MECO Logic is committed to providing customers with fashionable, value-for-money products and a pleasant shopping experience. MECO Logic not only offers its own brands Euromoda and Rampage, but also provides carefully selected third-party brands, including well-known international brands and some distinctive local brands. China's e-commerce industry is still booming. Investors from all over the world are rushing in, and the purchasing enthusiasm of Chinese consumers is also rising. MECO Logic is one of the witnesses of this wave. Among MECO Logic's three retail channels, the development momentum of online business has the potential to replace the other two. This can be seen from the company's third-quarter financial report: MECO Logic's offline direct stores (excluding franchise stores) have decreased from 143 last year to 115 this year. Although the total net revenue was $53.13 million, down 4% year-on-year, the average monthly visitors to Maivn increased by 23% year-on-year. "This trend also verifies our strategy," said Gu Beichun, CEO of MECO Logic in a public statement. As a veteran e-commerce company, how to reposition itself in fierce competition and shape core competitiveness? MECO Logic CEO Gu Beichun said that some companies adopt a multi-product sales platform strategy. On the issue of doing brands or platforms, after considering and balancing market competition, economic development trends, Maivn's own strengths, styles, and scale, etc., we have adjusted our strategy: return to the positioning of focusing on brands. We will go deep and thorough in the vertical field of clothing, providing unique style fashion products for a specific demand population, and differentiate ourselves from platform-type e-commerce with our own style. Platforms and brands each have their advantages and disadvantages. Platform companies can reach a considerable scale. However, the current competitive landscape of domestic platform e-commerce is already relatively clear, with Taobao.com holding more than 80% of the market share. To win in this market, a large amount of capital investment is required, and one must be prepared to make no profit for 5-10 years. Secondly, there is a phenomenon of winner takes all in the platform e-commerce market. In a certain category, there may only be 3-4 successful platforms, so if you want to position yourself as a platform e-commerce, you must face greater risks. At the same time, in such a competitive landscape, the requirements for operational capabilities and capital investment in the platform aspect are very high, including technical platforms and logistics platforms, etc. As a brand e-commerce, it can have more advantages. The Internet can become one of your channels. You can use online channels, such as Taobao's Singles' Day, or other platforms to expand brand influence. Meanwhile, we can focus more on enhancing our own style and products to attract consumers. Moreover, the specialties and capabilities required for being a brand merchant and a platform operator are quite different, as are the talent reserves. The team doing the platform focuses on operations, while doing the brand requires a strong creative and design team. Therefore, the current strategic positioning is a decision made after considering the company's style and strengths, as well as combining with the market competition situation. After having the brand positioning and style, a fixed customer base will be obtained. We hope to provide better fashion products for Maivn's customers, allowing more customers to choose their favorite items.
