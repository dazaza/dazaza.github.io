Time flies like an arrow.

by vghfeifei on 2011-09-21 14:39:23

We throw ourselves into the bosom of nature, breathing in the refreshing air that invigorates our hearts, listening to the sounds of all things awakening and grass sprouting, greedily enjoying the taste of spring. Our bodies feel much lighter, and our minds are extraordinarily joyful. I love the warmth that ripples in the spring breeze; I love how the vibrant colors of spring冲击my vision. In the green mountains and clear waters, we savor the various flavors of the countryside's charm... We search for the taste of spring, for happiness, for... sharing the joy of outings!

The gentle east wind blows, softly caressing the hair on my forehead, making my heart incredibly happy! Gazing sideways at the graceful willow trees beside me, it seems they have taken on a new hint of green. The tender green at the tips of the willows appears to be telling me: the vibrant spring has arrived! The air is filled with the warm scent of spring. Walking along the path, I see the resilient grass breaking through the soil again with its strong perseverance, settling down under the walls, using its fresh green color to decorate the beautiful spring without reservation. I catch a whiff of a strong spring fragrance! Spring is truly beautiful! Spring stays forever in my heart, and even now when I think about it, I still feel immense joy!

Time flies like an arrow, days pass like weaving. In the blink of an eye, spring has passed and summer has arrived. At this moment, I am still savoring the taste of spring. On this year's Qingming Festival, the sunshine was especially bright, the sky exceptionally clear, the wind particularly gentle, and the flowers unusually splendid. We and our niece Yan Yan came to Poyang Lake for an outing and sightseeing! Far away from the bustling city, we enjoy nature, feel the tranquility and ease of the fields, and experience spring.

It is said that Poyang Lake is experiencing a severe water shortage this year, a drought situation never seen before in history. Although there was also a water shortage at Poyang Lake during the Qingming Festival, it was still very charming. Standing on the embankment of Poyang Lake, the vast lake scenery unfolds before us. We can not only stand by the side of Poyang Lake and feel the warmth of the spring sun, admire the beautiful Poyang Lake bathed in sunlight, but also personally walk into the fields to see rape flowers and smell the earthy aroma. We pick wild flowers, eat farmhouse meals, truly enjoying boundless joy!

Spring warms up, flowers bloom, all things come back to life. The sun shines on the earth, patches of soft grass grow, and gorgeous red flowers blossom. The branches on the trees have already sprouted. It's spring, and everywhere you can smell the fragrance of flowers. The yellow rape flowers, the golden forsythia; the red azaleas, and the delicate peach blossoms. Those tender yellows, fresh greens, light pinks, elegant whites... those verdant branches, warm sunlight, moist soil... Everywhere is filled with the scent of spring, making people feel extremely comfortable; making people drunk; making people infinitely excited; in spring, we feel the power of life!

Dreaming back to Poyang Lake, the shimmering lake water nourishes the April sun, the beautiful lake scenery makes us drunk in it, stepping into the fields, looking forward to the harvest fills our hearts with joy.
