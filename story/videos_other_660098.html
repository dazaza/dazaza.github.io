Milan's new target for left-back emerges: Gazza's "couple" recommends an unexpected candidate

by lz3771hk on 2012-02-07 12:14:51

Position: Defender, Dídac Vilà, Weight: 81 kg. Perhaps all of this is not important; what is important is Dídac's agent. Yes, it's Mino Raiola, the Dutchman born in Italy, who after making Ibrahimović and Balotelli leave Inter Milan, quickly established a close relationship with another club in Milan. Count the players he brought to Milan this season: Ibrahimović, Robinho, Ely (the Brazilian young defender called the new Thiago Silva, currently in Milan's youth team), Emmanuelson, Van Bommel, now it’s Dídac's turn, and it’s rumored that Balotelli will come in the summer. The "Milan Sport" newspaper calling Raiola Galliani's lover is not entirely a joke. Contract expiration: June 30, 2013, Height: 186 cm. Dídac would be happy to join Milan, as having the opportunity to play in the Champions League is particularly attractive to this young player. Due to Raiola's connections, the Spaniard has replaced Criscito as Milan's top target for introducing a left-back. Previously, AC Milan was also rumored to be interested in Cesena's defender Yuto Nagatomo, and Japan's coach Zaccheroni even recommended this player to his former club. But Cesena's asking price for the Japanese international reached 7 million euros, which is 5 million euros more than Dídac's, making Milan hesitant.

Nationality: Spain

After successively bringing in Cassano, Emmanuelson, and Van Bommel, has Milan's winter transfer window closed? The answer is no, because Galliani is still busy with new targets. "Is it very busy these days? Obviously, I hope it stays this way tomorrow." Milan's vice president stated after defeating Sampdoria. With less than five days remaining until the January transfer window closes, AC Milan still hopes to bring in a defender, which is why Galliani has been so busy recently.

Nationality: Spanish

Birthplace: Mataró (Spain)

Player Information

Dídac is the starting left-back for Espanyol, born in 1989, with great potential for improvement. Dídac made his debut for the team two years ago but only played once in the Copa del Rey (in the derby against Barcelona) that season. He completed his La Liga debut on January 31st last year in a game against Athletic Bilbao, and this season he has made 13 appearances with one assist. The "Rome Sport" newspaper reports that the player's value is only 2 million euros.

English Name: Dídac Vilà

In addition to Dídac, Milan is also keeping an eye on another Spanish player as a backup option, Newcastle's left-back José Enrique. The latter is an absolute starter for the Magpies, and the Premier League club won't let him go easily, and his price won't be less than Nagatomo's. Milan also needs to consider that over the past dozen or so years, Spanish players have rarely succeeded at Milan.

Date of Birth: June 9, 1989

Preferred Foot: Left
