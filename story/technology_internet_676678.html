When to drink milk for weight loss: drink milk when you get up and before sleeping

Drink milk when getting up at midnight and before sleep to lose weight. When to drink milk for weight loss? Drinking three glasses of milk every day is helpful for weight loss, so when is the best time to drink milk for weight loss? Before sleeping at night: a glass of milk. It should be noted that if you drink milk before going to bed at night, you should appropriately reduce the amount of food for dinner to avoid extra calorie intake which could lead to obesity. Let's take a look at how much energy is contained in milk. Milk contains about 87% water, and a bag of 250 grams of whole milk only contains 150 kcal of energy, equivalent to half a bowl of rice. It can be seen that milk cannot be considered as an easy-to-get-fat food! Moreover, the nutrients contained in milk are much richer than a can of cola. Compared with many foods, the energy content of milk is actually very low. A 100-gram pack of cookies contains more than 450 kcal; half a catty of pork butt meat contains 828 kcal; a can of cola contains 140 kcal of energy, almost equivalent to a bag of whole milk. A Fuji apple contains 110 kcal, equivalent to three-quarters of milk, yet it is called a weight-loss food. (Intern Editor: He Zhiyan, Editor-in-Charge: Song Xue)

Principle of drinking milk for weight loss: Utilize the rise in blood sugar and the feeling of fullness to limit calorie intake. Drinking a glass of sugared milk 15-30 minutes before meals can help slim down. The most important aspect is the use of brain physiology mechanism. When the body's blood sugar drops, the brain's feeding center sends out a signal, and people will feel hungry and want to eat. As hunger increases, obese people tend to eat faster, and the amount they eat can be 1.5 times that of normal individuals. If the amount and speed of eating can be controlled, losing weight is not difficult. The combination of sugar and milk is precisely the weight-loss method under this theory.

Warm Reminder: Milk has an excellent sense of fullness. Drinking a glass of milk before meals can effectively alleviate hunger and reduce appetite, and the total calories consumed in the end are not higher than when not drinking milk. Replacing dinner with two glasses of milk may even have a weight-loss effect. Or drink a cup of yogurt during lunch. Drinking a glass of yogurt half an hour after lunch helps digestion and prevents fat deposition.

Sugar: According to nutritionists, using white granulated sugar has the best effect, with the fastest rise in blood sugar levels. However, some other nutritionists believe that using black sugar is a better choice because the rise in blood sugar levels is similar but also provides additional mineral nutrients, making it healthier. But black sugar is relatively rare in the market and hard to find.

Every morning after getting up: First, drink a glass of warm water, then drink a glass of milk. Both help eliminate toxins in the body and prevent the formation of edema. Milk is the most nutritious among natural foods, but this does not mean that drinking milk will definitely cause weight gain. Whether or not you gain weight depends on whether the total daily energy intake increases, not on whether or not you drank milk.

Usage Method: Every evening before dinner, add about one small spoon (5 grams) of sugar to a cup of about 240cc of milk, stir evenly, and drink slowly.

Usage Time: Drink a cup of 240cc sugared milk 15-30 minutes before dinner every day.

You might ask, does this kind of weight-loss method have any side effects?

Nutritionists say that unless you end up gaining more weight due to improper control of your food intake, otherwise, there are basically no side effects.

Key Tip: When to drink milk for weight loss? Experts have found that drinking milk upon waking up and before sleeping can effectively reduce weight.
