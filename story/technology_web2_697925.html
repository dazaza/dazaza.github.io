[Get rid of boring leveling up, easy entertainment]

I believe many players have had similar experiences: killing monsters for upgrading every day or running errands across the map for those rewards that are too little. Starting from this, the R&D team of "LIEGUO" has designed reasonable daily tasks, activity schedules, and offline experience collection for players. This not only allows players to experience the game in a healthy way, but also guides them to participate in high-yield tasks and activities.

[Reasonably designed according to human rest regulations]

According to human living and resting habits, different task activities are designed based on time nodes, such as "tasting wine life" at noon and evening, "hanging out for leveling" during the day, and "national tasks" in the evening. These allow players to participate in the game reasonably, hang out during the day without spending energy, gain experience while sitting down during meal times, and enjoy passionate entertainment and relaxation in the evening.

[Target system, different passion every day]

Even if the schedule is set reasonably, if players are doing the same game content every day, even the most exciting content will become boring. Considering this issue, in addition to designing different activity schedules every day, the R&D team of "LIEGUO" has also designed a unique target system. If players follow the target guidance provided by the target system throughout the game experience, they can enjoy different passions every day in the game. Moreover, the target system has rare game rewards, allowing players to execute easily and harvest happily.
