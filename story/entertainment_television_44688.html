Introduction to the Subplots of Delicious Fairy Tale

Episode 12

Hearing that Oda went to Nantes Forest to help Ke Xin, Bai Hui felt extremely disheartened. To cheer her up, He Ma took Bai Hui to eat street food. The delicious roadside snacks made Bai Hui very happy. In Episode 20 of Delicious Fairy Tale, in order to open up sales channels, Ke Xin invited a radio station reporter for an on-site interview. Oda was cold and indifferent, ignoring the reporter who became very angry with an icy face.

He Ma became the head chef at Little Bear. Due to the taste not being quite right, the business got progressively worse. With no other options, the boss sent his daughter Xiao Qiang out onto the streets to distribute flyers and attract more customers. Only three people remained in the small shop: the boss, Xiao Qiang, and He Ma. There were not enough hands, and everyone was exhausted. The busier they got, the more chaos ensued, eventually causing a group food poisoning incident. Reporters came to report on it, and He Ma looked at the scene feeling utterly helpless.

Inspectors from the Food Inspection Bureau also arrived at Little Bear's, carefully checking every ingredient in the shop, including thoroughly examining the boss's and Chef Oda's hands. Before the investigation results came out, the shop had to close for a week.

Oda and Bai Hui, each in different places, learned through the TV about the food poisoning incident at Little Bear's and both rushed over to console He Ma. Bai Hui arrived earlier, while Oda came later. Seeing Bai Hui consoling He Ma attentively, Oda turned around intending to leave. However, he accidentally heard He Ma say, "I want to resign; I can't drag Little Bear down." Oda turned back, angrily scolding He Ma through the kitchen door, accusing him of being irresponsible. Oda made He Ma recall step by step how he cooked, finally discovering the problem was with the boiled egg. It turns out the egg was made too early, and without air conditioning, it had been left out for over three hours, naturally breeding bacteria. After guiding He Ma out of his confusion, Oda went out to eat with Bai Hui. Watching Oda enjoying the food happily, Bai Hui went into a daze. Oda invited Bai Hui to have dinner in Nantes Forest the next day. In Nantes Forest, Ke Xin told Bai Hui, "Every man harbors ambition, and Little Bear has made Oda lose his drive." Hearing this, Bai Hui felt very depressed, but Oda's cooking plunged Bai Hui back into daydreams where her master fed her delicacies. When Oda asked Bai Hui what she thought, she recalled the taste of the food and cried happily. Ke Xin, watching from the side, thought to herself: "Oda will be taken away."

The next day, a heavy rain fell. Holding an umbrella, Ke Xin went to find Bai Hui and said, "As Oda's girlfriend, I must tell you, don't go see him often." Bai Hui innocently countered, "Why?" Ke Xin had no choice but to use Oda's future as an excuse. Bai Hui felt indignant inside and showed a resentful expression on her face.

Back home, Bai Hui met He Ma's father. Unable to find He Ma, He's father was very worried. Bai Hui led He's father to Little Bear's, where father and son fought. Bai Hui tried to stop them, but ended up getting hit on the nose by He's father and started bleeding. Seeing He Ma unwilling to leave with him, He's father returned home alone. Watching his father's retreating back, He Ma felt very bitter.

Episode 13

Bai Hui found Grandma Jin Dao and asked her to give He Ma some guidance. Poor He Ma began his career chasing pigs with Grandma Jin Dao. Grandma Jin Dao personally trained He Ma in cooking, from catching shrimp in the river himself to dish pairing, making He Ma understand everything meticulously. Under Grandma Jin Dao's guidance, He Ma’s culinary skills greatly improved. Meanwhile, Bai Hui started her own life of developing new dishes. Chef Takahashi smiled and gave advice, stunning everyone because they didn’t expect Takahashi's sense of taste to have recovered. Takahashi himself was stunned for a moment, then his face lit up with happiness.

Grandma Jin Dao told He Ma and Bai Hui that someone would come to assess their culinary skills. The two were puzzled, unable to guess who the person was or what they should do. Just as they were confused, they saw Oda standing before them. Bai Hui was both surprised and delighted, finally seeing her dream lover. Bai Hui also told Oda that she already knew Ke Xin was his girlfriend. Upon hearing this, Oda was furious.

Back in Nantes Forest, Oda saw Ke Xin's face full of resentment. The contrast between Ke Xin's grievances and Bai Hui's smile made Oda start missing Bai Hui and begin disliking Ke Xin.

Little Bear reopened, and Xiao Qiang encountered a poor man bringing his child to eat. Watching the two maintain their family despite poverty moved Xiao Qiang deeply. She not only provided them with food but also cared for them all the time. The boss of Little Bear noticed He Ma's improved culinary skills and helped him and Bai Hui contact an international competition. If they succeeded in the competition, the two would be surrounded by glory.

Ke Xin again appeared on TV with Oda. Oda treated the matter with his usual cold indifference, remaining silent in front of the camera, driving the director crazy and leaving Ke Xin embarrassed. After the program ended, Oda coldly told Ke Xin, "I'm going to leave Nantes Forest." This sentence struck Ke Xin like a bolt of lightning, leaving her frozen in place.

Episode 14

Oda walked out of the filming site with a cold face, telling Ke Xin, "You don't understand me at all. I'm going to quit." Ke Xin replied, "How can you be so irresponsible?" Oda answered, "That's just who I am—an irresponsible person," and then turned around and left, leaving Ke Xin to clean up the mess alone.

At Little Bear's, Bai Hui sweetly ate the food He Ma made for her. On TV, a food program featuring Oda was playing. Everyone in the restaurant saw Oda's cold and distant face on the big screen. People teased Bai Hui, saying she came to Little Bear to watch Oda on TV. Bai Hui became embarrassed by the jokes, and He Ma, seeing this, became troubled and picked a fight with the fish stall owner. While everyone was laughing and talking, Oda suddenly appeared in the restaurant. Oda told everyone that he had resigned. Bai Hui said, "What you did didn't consider your girlfriend's feelings at all." Oda immediately replied, "Who says she's my girlfriend?" Compared to the liveliness at Little Bear, Ke Xin sat alone watching TV, feeling incredibly lonely.

Bai Hui found Grandma Jin Dao and tentatively asked her about the relationship between Oda and Ke Xin. Grandma Jin Dao said, "Oda is a very sentimental person. He feels that he owes a lifetime of gratitude to those who have helped him. Ke Xin and Nantes Forest are like that. Since he wants to leave Nantes Forest, it must be painful for him." Hearing that Oda's feelings for Ke Xin were merely out of gratitude, Bai Hui's heart finally settled. At the same time, Grandma Jin Dao also told Bai Hui, "I'm going to travel south to visit old friends. You need to work hard in the cooking competition."

Back in La Morl, Bai Hui told everyone about Grandma Jin Dao's trip south. Everyone felt that since Grandma Jin Dao was old, visiting her old friends might not happen again. Chef Takahashi sighed and told Bai Hui, "People's time is limited, and this fact cannot be avoided. So if there's something you want to do, seize every opportunity, including love." Hearing Takahashi's words, Bai Hui decided to confess her feelings to Oda.

Receiving Bai Hui's call, Oda was very happy and spoke gently. The next day, at the agreed dining location, Bai Hui waited for Oda and carefully wiped her shoes. The late-arriving Oda saw this and coldly said, "What kind of posture is that? Afraid of exposing yourself?" Then he stood beside Bai Hui and said, "Quickly wipe your shoes, I'll block for you." Seeing Oda's meticulous care, Bai Hui felt a surge of sweetness in her heart. During this meeting, Oda opened up to Bai Hui, telling her that the reason he loved cooking so much was because he wanted to see the happy smiles of friends and people. Moved, Bai Hui softly sang a Huangmei tune for Oda.

Not long after, Bai Hui wanted to date Oda again, but He Ma answered the phone. He Ma volunteered to meet Bai Hui and take her to a big meal. Helplessly, Bai Hui agreed. In the high-end restaurant, Bai Hui waited and waited but He Ma never showed up. A gentleman sat across from Bai Hui and said, "My name is Gu Jianxian." Bai Hui looked at him in surprise, as they didn't know each other.

At this moment, the woman sitting nearby kept calling, upset that her date hadn't shown up. It turned out Gu Jianxian was there for a blind date and had mistakenly sat in the wrong seat in a hurry. Gu Jianxian turned off his phone and smiled at Bai Hui, saying, "Do you know what I'm thinking? Sitting here, I feel we have a connection, and the person you were waiting for probably ditched you. Letting a girl dine alone in a high-end restaurant is impolite." Bai Hui saw his elegant demeanor and excellent bearing and gladly accepted, happily thinking, "An unexpected date with an unexpected person, but at least it's not with someone unpleasant."

Meanwhile, He Ma was preoccupied with matters at Little Bear's. Xiao Qiang, having unexpectedly met a poor father and his many hungry children, generously gave them food and money from the store to supplement their nutrition, which was eventually discovered. The boss saw Xiao Qiang stealing money from the store and got very angry, leading to an argument. He Ma rushed over to mediate, missing his appointment time.
