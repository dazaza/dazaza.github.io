Choose a wedding dress package

A wedding dress package is a variety of product specifications that a bridal shop offers based on price, photography style, dresses, and gifts. If you want to have a suitable wedding dress package, there's only one secret: before paying the deposit, make sure to understand all the details so that you truly know what you're getting into!

Mental preparation:

After choosing a bridal studio, the next step is to book your wedding dress package! Here are some important points to note: no matter how beautiful the dresses or how dreamy the photos look, do not pay a deposit at the first store. Also, don't carry cash, debit cards, or credit cards with you. When negotiating with the staff about the wedding dress package, even if you feel satisfied, do not show it on your face; if you're not satisfied, leave immediately - dragging out the time will only be disadvantageous for you.

It's recommended to compare several bridal shops. Create a table of your requirements for price, photos, and styling, record the details of the packages from the bridal shops you've visited, organize them, and then choose the one that best fits your ideal. Remember, when signing the photography agreement, include all your requirements in the contract!

Points to note when choosing a package:

- Number of sets / size / wedding makeup / includes selected prints, negatives / price for unselected prints, negatives / whether anniversary photos are gifted.

- Minimum number of sets for shooting / additional selection price (must negotiate to the lowest) / traditional or digital / how many sets are needed to make two photo albums / unlimited photo selection times.

- Photo album material and design, whether includes artistic processing / unlimited full-page photos / both traditional and digital can be proofed / all photos will undergo retouching.

- Enlargement photo size, frame material, image processing / other free standing frames gifted / signature silk size.

- Mother's album as full-page or print version / mother's album size / number of hand-sized print pages and whether includes artistic processing / whether a photo CD is gifted.

- Thank-you card quantity / friend cards / royal invitations / whether invitations are gifted (no plate fee), candy boxes / boutonnieres / wedding day fresh bouquets / wedding gift boxes, red envelope books / car decorations, hood and handle flowers.

- The initial deposit should not exceed 20% / can be paid in installments, can use card / pay the final balance only upon receiving the wedding attire / seven-day return period promised if unsatisfied.

- Request during photo selection that the photos not be scattered, keep the same series together / if dissatisfied with the results, free reshoot / reshoot does not charge extra for styling, dresses, and location can be reselected.

- All requirements must be written in the agreement, signed and stamped by the bridal shop's responsible person. All items that require additional charges must be explained beforehand by the bridal shop and recorded in the agreement.

Regarding dress selection:

- Number of dresses in the store / whether dresses for photoshoots and weddings are differentiated / whether new, VIP dresses cost extra / special dresses like Han costumes, cheongsams / bridesmaid dresses and flower girl dresses / whether outdoor shoot dresses can get wet.

- Whether groom's white suit, white shoes, dark-colored suits cost extra / whether trial fitting is allowed.

- How far in advance can dresses be chosen, unlimited selection times / photo and wedding dresses can be tried on and adjusted.

- Whether dresses can be photographed for family reference / whether deposits are required for wedding dresses and matching jewelry accessories.

Photography and styling:

- No extra charge for designated photographer / free borrowing of jewelry and accessories for photoshoots and wedding day / whether fresh bouquet is provided on shooting day / whether positive film shooting provides black and white, blue film, or infrared negative.

- Number of photo shoot styles / whether styling for outdoor shoots costs extra / same stylist for makeup and hair / whether trial makeup and pre-shoot styling is allowed / clear calculation method for hourly fees.

- No regional or time limits for indoor and outdoor shoots on the same day / whether providing an outdoor shoot vehicle costs extra / no extra charge for rescheduling due to rain.

- Must communicate and discuss with the photographer and stylist before the photo shoot.
