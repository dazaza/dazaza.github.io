10 Small Details of a Good Man Taking Care of a Woman

by yang5230 on 2009-12-04 17:22:53

A lot of women will answer this question correctly at first glance: why do only women care about men, and not the other way around? This seems unfair. However, in reality, because men usually perform better than women emotionally and tend to be more discreet, they easily overlook details. Therefore, men should learn a bit more, which is not only out of care for their own women but also for the commitment and protection of their own marriage. So, let's take a look at which details can show that men care about women. Careless men must pay attention carefully:

1. When introducing her to your friends, gently put your arm around her waist instead of standing with your hands on your hips, smoking, or sitting aside pointing with your foot. This makes her feel very safe and shows that you have already integrated her into your life and world. Women usually appreciate this gesture, and it’s likely she will enthusiastically introduce you to her friends later.

2. Behind her friends, show even more concern and relaxation towards her. At this moment, no amount of affection is excessive. People often wear masks, so if you give her face in front of her friends and make her feel very happy and satisfied, she will be more inclined to be good to you and do a lot for you.

3. When she misses you, squeeze out time to comfort her immediately. At this time, she doesn't need any reason, nor does she need you to find any excuse to show that you're busy. Even if you are really busy, you should still manage to call her and offer some comfort; otherwise, she may feel lost all day, and even develop psychological shadows. Gradually, she may stop missing you as much.

4. When she makes a mistake, you can point out her flaws, but don’t lose your temper. Everyone makes mistakes. Importantly, if you get angry when she makes a mistake, after getting angry, you must use words to comfort her properly. This way, she will feel guilty for making a mistake and be more grateful for your tolerance.

5. When she is being unreasonable, don't confront her directly. Sometimes women being unreasonable is due to coquetry rather than being unruly. If you understand this point, you won't always raise your voice and argue with her. Instead, patiently listen to her venting. After she has finished complaining and venting, offer her a warm cup of tea and a big hug. Even without saying anything, she will understand your understanding and care.

6. Remember a few special dates, especially the date that is unique to the two of you. For women, this day is more important than their own birthday. Therefore, you should generously invite her to a high-end restaurant for a meal or go somewhere fun to enjoy yourselves. Most importantly, don't forget to give her a big bouquet of roses. If necessary, don't be stingy about giving her some special commemorative gifts.

7. Whenever she calls, don't hang up on her, even if both of you are upset and arguing. Please patiently wait for her to hang up first; otherwise, she will feel very disappointed and lost. In fact, as long as you don't hang up, she will feel very secure. Even if you appear angry, she will think you just have a tough exterior but a soft heart.

8. Don't praise another woman behind her back, and definitely don't brag about how popular you are. Regarding these gossip-like news, whether fabricated or true, she doesn't want to know and doesn't want you to use them to enhance her insecurity. On the contrary, what she truly cares about are the new things between you and her.

9. When you're angry, don't ignore her. Don't give her time to cool off; in fact, she doesn't need time to calm down. A timely hug, a gentle kiss, or an intimate gesture from you will make her more convinced than just proximity.

10. You may be someone who sees a pretty face and forgets your friends, but for the sake of your beloved, sometimes you should appropriately suppress your interest in others.
