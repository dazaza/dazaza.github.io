Hundreds of students protest against the lavish teachers' meals by dumping their food(Photos)

by syd17u123 on 2009-11-24 15:58:58

"The incidents of student complaints happen from time to time, and we will also make improvements according to the students' opinions," the person in charge said. Generally speaking, each boxed lunch contains two meat dishes and one vegetable dish. "Plus the free soup given with the meal, the restaurants outside the school definitely cannot provide that. And according to our survey, for some schools in Guicheng which have their own cafeterias, the cheapest meal is at least 6 yuan. On one hand, we can only charge according to the regulations of relevant departments; on the other hand, raising the price will surely cause dissatisfaction among some students and parents, so we have maintained the status quo."

Yesterday afternoon, the reporter went to Guijiang No.2 Middle School, where several third-year junior high school students told the reporter that the "dumping food incident" did indeed happen, and it was done by them. The incident was due to the long-term dissatisfaction of students with the quality of the meals provided.

Netizen "Ya Ya Ah": "The information provided by the pictures tells me that the food provided by the school is very terrible. However, teachers enjoy their meals on the other side. Since we are all people, why is there such a big difference? Moreover, students are growing up and need nutrition. If parents entrust their children to the school, but from the pictures, we see yellow vegetables etc., where is the nutrition?"

After the post was sent out, it immediately caused a strong reaction from netizens. Some supported the students' rights protection behavior, others thought the students went too far, and even more netizens recalled their past experiences, saying that what they encountered in school back then was worse than the students of Guijiang No.2 Middle School.

Given the enthusiastic response, yesterday afternoon, Foshan's local forum "Tiantianxin" specially opened a special page for debate and voting. By around 6 p.m. last night, the side supporting the students won slightly with 38 votes against 34 votes from the opposing side.

Netizen "Incinerator": "If the taste is not good, parents who feel sorry for their children can choose to let the third-year students dine out or have food delivered from home. They can also make suggestions to the school, without taking such extreme actions. Can you arbitrarily destroy things if you are dissatisfied with the consumption? Is this the style that a civilized society should have?"

Hu Shenzi said that students are in puberty, and they have a very strong desire to control society and life. "They hope the world becomes fairer, but often, this is their unilateral wish, and the psychological gap arises accordingly." Hu Shenzi analyzed that what made the students react strongly were mainly the unpalatable food and the unfair treatment. "The first point can explain that it is the explosion of resentment accumulated by students over a long period of time, while the second point exacerbates the students' sense of imbalance. Although it seems like an accidental event, its occurrence has a certain inevitability."

Hu Shenzi said that solving the problem is actually very simple, "which is to negotiate and communicate with students on an equal footing. But in most cases, schools place students in too low a position, which is the fundamental reason for the lack of smooth communication." He told the reporter that the large-scale protest also had something to do with the herd mentality of students, "whether the next step of the incident can be smoothly resolved depends on whether the school treats students as equals."

Yesterday noon, a post titled "Dumping Food Protest Against Guijiang × Middle School" appeared on a Foshan forum. The poster claimed that on the evening of the 20th of this month, the third-year students of Guijiang No.2 Middle School dumped multiple meal boxes onto the tables in the canteen because they were dissatisfied with the quality of the meals provided.

A male third-year student said that November 20th was the annual sports meeting of the school. After the sports meeting ended that evening, the third-year students came to the canteen as usual to eat dinner. At this time, the students found that there were hotpot and accompanying dishes placed on one side of the table, while on the other side, each student meal contained only five meatballs and a small amount of green vegetables. Subsequently, many teachers came to eat hotpot, and some children of teachers studying in the school also ate at the hotpot table. As more and more students came to the canteen to eat, some students saw how simple their meals were and angrily overturned the boxed lunches on the table. This action was echoed by the rest of the classmates, who also overturned their boxed lunches. The boy said that about 80% of the students overturned their meals on the table.

Yesterday afternoon, the reporter contacted the Guicheng Meal Distribution Center in Nanhai District, Foshan City. A person in charge of the center said that relevant departments stipulated that each boxed lunch could only charge 5 yuan, "we all distribute dishes according to this standard, it's already quite good if we can break even. Last month, due to the sharp rise in vegetable prices, we lost quite a bit." The person in charge said that since September last year, according to the regulations of relevant departments, the price of each boxed lunch increased from the original 4.5 yuan to 5 yuan, "although the daily menu is self-proposed, the cost of each boxed lunch has already reached about 3.9 yuan, plus the 7 jiao for the lunch box and packaging costs, the profit margin is very low."

Netizen "Saxia": "I support the students! From the perspective of a consumer: your consumer rights, you must speak up when justified! From the perspective of a student: the school is a place for education, teachers should teach by words and example, but teachers are creating inequality. There are hidden rules in introducing meal distribution, monopoly! Students... Only the most basic and direct way to express dissatisfaction is left, dumping the food!"

Many students expressed that third-year students have evening study sessions at school, so many of them eat at the school cafeteria. Each meal costs 5 yuan, sometimes containing only one meat dish and one vegetable dish, with little oil, "tasteless". This time, seeing that they ate poorly while teachers and their children ate well, the students felt unbalanced and thus did this.

Netizen "One Egg": "There are a lot of things in this world that are not satisfactory. If you are dissatisfied, you should express it through normal channels, not protest by dumping food in such a foolish way! Food is innocent!"

Posterszdsd posted multiple photos online. In the photos, each student's meal box contained four or five meatballs and already yellow cucumbers. Several meters away, there were nearly ten dining tables where many teachers were eating hotpot. The poster also added a text explanation on a photo showing several students sitting around eating hotpot, "Teachers' children, what kind of world is this?" Multiple photos showed that many students overturned their fast food boxes, and the white rice scattered on the table was very noticeable.
