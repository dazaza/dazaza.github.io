Everything returns to the starting point, 5258! Everything is because of 5258. This was the confession that the son left for his father on Weibo. However, it was precisely because of these Weibo posts that the whereabouts of Wang Mou, a fugitive from Zitong, Mianyang, who had fled after forcibly breaking through police checkpoints and running over a public security officer at a police station 14 years ago, were exposed. It turned out that Wang Mou's son had recently broken up with his girlfriend because she knew that his father was a fugitive. With no one to confide in, he expressed his feelings on Weibo.

Unexpectedly, these Weibo posts provided clues for the police in their pursuit. The officers analyzed that 5258 was a homophone for "I love my dad" and believed that Wang Mou and his son must have been in contact. Subsequently, they followed the trail and arrested Wang Mou. Yesterday, Chengdu Business Daily reported that during the recent web-clearing operation, Wang Mou was arrested by the police in Xinjiang and brought back to Mianyang. Currently, Wang Mou has been criminally detained by the public security authorities.

Fled for many years

The man who caused death by collision fled 14 years ago

Investigating officers of the Zitong County Public Security Bureau introduced that Wang Mou is 50 years old and is from Dong'an Township, Jiangyou City. On the evening of August 30, 1997, Wang Mou drove a passenger minibus carrying Liu Mou and others to an entertainment venue in Shuangban Township, Zitong County for leisure. At around 3 o'clock in the morning, when Wang Mou and his group left the entertainment center, they were stopped by the owner of the entertainment center due to assaulting the waitresses and using counterfeit money.

Subsequently, Wang Mou abandoned Liu Mou and drove away with the other people. On the way, they encountered a check by the police from the Shuangban Township Police Station. Out of fear, Wang Mou refused to cooperate, forcibly broke through the checkpoint, and ran over Jiang Mou, a public security officer at the police station, killing him. After the incident, Wang Mou fled out of fear of criminal responsibility, fleeing overnight to the Xiaoxibei train station in Jiangyou, climbing onto a train to escape. Thereafter, Wang Mou successively fled to Guangzhou, Beijing, and Xinjiang, hiding under a false name, embarking on a long road of escape.

The investigating officer introduced that after the incident, Wang Mou fled upon hearing the news. For 14 years, public security authorities at all levels have paid close attention, continuously trying to capture him but failing, listing Wang Mou as a fugitive under the supervision of the Provincial Public Security Department.

Exposure of his whereabouts

Son sends Weibo message 5258, police follow the lead

In the online inspection special chase and clear net operation, Zitong police mobilized capable forces to pursue Wang Mou. Through various investigations and visits, the fugitive officers discovered that in recent years, Wang Mou's wife Liu Mou went missing, and his son was studying at a university within Sichuan Province, but Wang Mou still remained completely untraceable, showing not even the slightest trace.

"Regardless of where Wang Mou fled, his son needs economic support for schooling. Wang Mou should be providing him with funds!" Just as the pursuit once again reached a deadlock, Detective An Dengji proposed his thoughts. Soon, An Dengji and others, through the internet, found that Wang Mou's son had appeared multiple times in Mi'lei County, Honghe Prefecture, Yunnan Province in the past two years, including going online and staying at accommodations. Meanwhile, the police also found a comment on his Weibo: "Everything is only because of 5258, everything returns to the starting point, 5258."

"5258 means 'I love my dad'!" An Dengji said that after discovering this Weibo information, the police confirmed that Wang Mou had always been in contact with his son. Through further investigation, it was learned that Wang Mou's son grew up with his grandfather since childhood. Three years ago, his grandfather passed away, and in September this year, he graduated from university. The possibility of a reunion among the three family members was very high. Subsequently, we followed the lead and discovered that on October 7th, someone transferred 25,000 yuan in cash to Wang Mou's son's bank account through an ATM machine in Jinghe County, Xinjiang. A week later, Wang Mou's son traveled from Sichuan to Xinjiang. We preliminarily judged that Wang Mou was likely hiding in Xinjiang.

An Dengji introduced that two months ago, because her boyfriend's father was a fugitive, Wang Mou's son's girlfriend broke up with him. Unable to confide in anyone, Wang Mou's son could only express his feelings on Weibo. Unexpectedly, these Weibo comments provided effective clues for the police in their pursuit.

Caught on his birthday

Said he would post the surrender announcement on the wall and read it every night

Zitong police contacted the Bozhou police in Xinjiang. Soon, the Bozhou police verified that the person who transferred money to Wang Mou's son's bank account was indeed Wang Mou's wife Liu Mou. Simultaneously, the Bozhou police also found Liu Mou's temporary residence record in Xinjiang eight years ago when she sought refuge with Wang Mou.

Immediately afterward, a Sichuanese man named Wang Deyu, who was in close contact with Liu Mou, came into the investigative sights of both localities' police. On the evening of October 21st, based on the photos and related information provided by the Zitong police, the Bozhou police in Xinjiang rushed to Jinghe County and apprehended Wang Mou. After an intense interrogation, Wang Mou confessed without reservation to the crime of driving a vehicle 14 years ago and fatally running over Jiang Mou, a public security officer at the Shuangban Police Station in Zitong.

The day Wang Mou was captured happened to be his 50th birthday. At that time, Wang Mou was hosting a banquet. When the police directly called out his real name, Wang Mou responded, "I know what's going on, I'll go with you," without any resistance. An Dengji introduced that all these years, Wang Mou never returned home. His wife has been hiding with him in Xinjiang. The couple has built a house in Xinjiang, leased land to grow cotton, and only met their son during winter and summer vacations.

After being captured, Wang Mou told the Zitong County Public Security Bureau's fugitive pursuit officers that the police's self-surrender announcement was posted on his wallhead. He carefully read it every night, originally planning to return to Sichuan to surrender after finishing picking the cotton. Unexpectedly, he missed the opportunity to reduce his criminal responsibility.
