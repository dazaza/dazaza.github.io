In 2009, enterprises in the Yangtze River Delta admire skilled talents

by 304885068 on 2009-02-12 09:49:17

The day before yesterday was the Lantern Festival, also a good day for the workplace in various places of the Pearl River Delta to begin to recover. With the return of most migrant workers and the resumption of work by most enterprises after the Spring Festival, the workplaces in Shenzhen, Dongguan, Foshan and other places have basically recovered, presenting a prosperous situation of supply and demand, and skilled talents are still the "great love" of the workplace.

What positions are available for job seekers in the major cities of the Pearl River Delta? Reporters found that the demand for the tertiary industry in Shenzhen is still strong, and it is difficult to solve the problem of labor shortage with an average of 4 job fairs per day; In Dongguan, the daily necessities and medical supplies industries hire the most people; Foshan lacks clothing production workers, service industry talents and technicians the most; Jiangmen lacks technicians, operators, machine repairers and packers the most.

Reporter investigation:

The workplace in the Pearl River Delta gradually warms up after the Lantern Festival

The day before yesterday, the recruitment area of the Foshan Central Labor Market saw the first recruitment boom after the festival. More than 60 enterprises came with more than 700 positions, attracting more than 2,000 job seekers to find jobs. According to statistics from the Foshan Labor Department, as of yesterday, the enterprise resumption rate in the city reached 82%, and the demand for talents naturally increased.

Yesterday, the Dongguan Zhitong Talent Market held the second live job fair after this year's Spring Festival. The three local markets of the headquarters in Guancheng, Tangxia, and Chang'an had about 1,200 enterprises recruiting more than 10,000 positions, and the number of people entering the venue exceeded 20,000, doubling the first job fair held on the 4th. According to rough statistics, nearly one-fifth of the enterprises recruiting at the scene recruited more than 50 people, and Nianhui Home Decor even recruited more than 124 positions in the General Office, finance, R&D, marketing, etc. In addition, many well-known companies such as Coca-Cola, Vtech, JDB, and Xu Fuxi also came to recruit. Miss Li, the recruitment representative of JDB, said that the overall recruitment volume this year will be the same as last year, and a batch of marketing, quality control, and other positions will be increased due to business expansion, and employee benefits will be the same as last year.

Regarding this recruitment boom, a person in charge of the Dongguan Zhitong Talent Market said that because the Spring Festival came earlier this year, some enterprises took longer holidays, and many migrant workers temporarily held an观望attitude towards the employment situation in Dongguan this year, causing the estimated peak recruitment period in Dongguan to be delayed compared to previous years. "From today's situation, the workplace in Dongguan has warmed up after the Lantern Festival, and the peak will appear in early March."

Jiangmen's workplace has seen a fiery scene very early this year, and after the Lantern Festival, this trend continues to increase. According to Mr. Ouyang Zhenpeng, the general manager of Jiangmen Yingtai Talent Market, the number of enterprises participating in the market recruitment is expected to increase by 15% after the Lantern Festival, and the number of positions and types of jobs will also increase. After some companies took early holidays last year, with the increase in orders, these companies are now tallying up new labor demands, and after the Lantern Festival, these companies will recruit workers from society, and there will be more positions and job types for job seekers to choose from.

Mr. Ouyang Zhenpeng said that another reason for the Jiangmen market to welcome a labor peak after the Lantern Festival is that enterprises in subordinate counties and cities such as Kaiping and Xinhui are gradually starting operations. Due to the later start-up time of these enterprises, some are expected to start at the end of February to early March, when there will be more management-oriented positions.
