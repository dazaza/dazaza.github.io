The practice team of Southwest Petroleum University conducts environmental protection research in an ecological farm.

by ditan360 on 2010-07-27 17:37:51

Yitan.com Exclusive - Do you know what organic living is? Can ordinary people afford organic products? Is there a place for organic products in the market? Recently, the summer practice team of Southwest Petroleum University's Green Boat Association, with the theme of "paying attention to food health and advocating organic living," went to the Guoyuan Xiangzhu Organic Ecological Farm in Luojiadian Village, Xinglong Town, Shuangliu County, Chengdu City on the 11th for a 10-day summer practical activity.

It is reported that Sichuan Province launched the provincial ecological county construction project in 2008. On December 15, 2009, Chen Wenhua, vice governor of the Sichuan Provincial People's Government, wrote an article pointing out that the 17th National Congress of the Communist Party proposed to build ecological civilization, and ecological province construction is an important handle and carrier of ecological civilization construction. On January 24, 2010, the General Office of the Sichuan Provincial People's Government issued a notice recognizing 9 provincial ecological counties, and Shuangliu was on the list.

This summer practical activity will conduct research on the production status of ecological farms, the operation model of CSA (Community Supported Agriculture), and the sustainable development of organic agriculture in the market by participating in the production and sales links of the Guoyuan Xiangzhu farm.

What is organic living? What is the concept of organic living? Luo Yu, the seventh generation inheritor of Yang-style traditional Tai Chi and the farm owner, said: "Organic living is actually eating agricultural products free from pesticides and fertilizers, and persisting in physical exercise every day. Like me, I started practicing Tai Chi at the age of 13, and I am still insisting now. After exercising, one becomes robust. Harmony and sustainable development, this is organic living." No matter how busy he is, Luo Yu gets up at 6:30 every day to start practicing Tai Chi, and this habit still exists today. He once joked about himself: "In the 8 years after graduating in 1993, when I worked as a securities trader at Chengdu Securities Company, I often controlled billions of yuan with one hand and kept several bottles of antibiotics in my pocket, ready to take them at any time. It was then that I realized the importance of organic living."

During the 10 days, the practice team learned about the operation of the organic farm through daily labor with the farm staff, and understood the sales and operation model by delivering vegetables to the community: the three-dimensional circular ecological economic system of fragrant pigs - fruit and vegetable garden - catering - delivery - training. Due to the small number and scale of organic farms, the price of agricultural products is 20-30% higher than the market price. However, in the long run, compared to eating toxic "pesticide vegetables" which can induce Parkinson's disease, leukemia, lymphoma and other diseases through hidden accumulation, organic food still has great development prospects in the market. Now people not only want to live good lives, but also want to live organic and pollution-free lives.

It is reported that through this survey, the practice group will sort out the results of the survey on August 1st and systematically answer questions about the development prospects of organic agriculture and whether CSA can solve the lack of credibility in the general certification of organic or green food in China.
