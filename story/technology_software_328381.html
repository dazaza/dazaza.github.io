Haijian HNA Initiates Strategic Cooperation

Both parties will integrate their advantages in resources, enterprise management mechanisms, and brands to achieve a strong alliance and mutual benefits. One is the third largest farm area in the country with rich tourism and tropical agriculture resources, the other is China's fourth largest airline with advanced enterprise management concepts and operational mechanisms. On the afternoon of the 4th, Hainan Farm Corporation held a strategic cooperation seminar with HNA Group, marking the official start of their strategic cooperation. It was learned that both parties will combine their strengths in resources, enterprise management mechanisms, and brands, working together to contribute to the construction of Hainan as an international tourist island, especially accelerating the development of western Hainan.

"Perfect Combination of Sky and Ground"

As early as last year, Haijian and HNA reached a comprehensive strategic cooperation agreement.

"One flies in the sky, one walks on the ground, complementing each other’s advantages for a full-scale strategic cooperation," said Wang Yixin, General Manager of Hainan Farm Corporation, vividly describing the collaboration between the two large enterprises as a "perfect combination of sky and ground." This not only refers to the differences in their respective main business areas but also highlights the current differences in their enterprise management systems and operational mechanisms.

After reforms over the past two years, Hainan Farm Corporation has made gratifying progress in solving people's livelihood issues and historical problems. Currently, Haijian aims to take advantage of the opportunity presented by the construction of Hainan as an international tourist island, utilizing its high-quality resources for industrial upgrading, developing tourism and real estate industries, and achieving leapfrog development. On the other hand, with the continuous advancement of reform, Haijian is gradually transforming into a modern enterprise. Especially with the independent operation of the Hainan Farm Bureau and the corporation, the company transformation of farms has also been put on the agenda.

Wang Yixin stated that in terms of industrial upgrading and development, Haijian will cooperate with HNA to complement each other's strengths and jointly contribute to the construction of Hainan as an international tourist island. In improving internal mechanisms, they will also rely on HNA's advanced enterprise management concepts and operational mechanisms to accelerate the transformation of Haijian.

"Cooperating with Haijian is very exciting and inspiring," said Li Xianhua, Director of the Board and Executive President of HNA Group. HNA's industry covers aviation transportation, tourism services, airport management, logistics, hotel management, financial services, real estate, retail trade, aviation food, and other related industries. Meanwhile, Haijian's reform has made gratifying progress. Haijian not only has abundant resources but also plans its future industrial development direction in natural rubber, modern tropical efficient agriculture, tourism and real estate, financial services, education, and medical care sectors. Both parties have many points of convergence in development, especially in the green food industry and tourism industry, where there is enormous potential for cooperation.

Deep Cooperation Boosts Western Tourism

As an important part of the construction of an international tourist island, our province has initiated the acceleration of the development of western Hainan. As two large enterprises in Hainan, during the seminar, both parties also focused on the development of western Hainan. They exchanged opinions on jointly planning and creating high-end leisure tourism projects relying on the hot spring resources of Lan Yang Farm, contributing to the development of western tourism.

It is understood that Lan Yang Farm is only 12 kilometers from Nada Town in Danzhou City, 70 kilometers from Yangpu, and 148 kilometers from Haikou. The farm has a 5.46 square kilometer hot spring resort area, adjacent across the road is a 5660-hectare national forest park, which has great value for developing leisure health and ecological tourism projects. Both parties expressed that they would further discuss specific plans for jointly planning and creating high-end leisure tourism projects.

In addition, both parties proposed conceptual ideas for cooperative development in education and training, health and wellness, green food, and other industries. They also discussed introducing HNA's advanced management concepts and operational mechanisms to conduct overall corporate transformations of Haijian's farms, activating farm productivity, and quickly changing the farm's appearance.

During the seminar, both parties reached a consensus, assigning dedicated personnel to be responsible for the strategic cooperation matters between the two parties and establishing a long-term regular exchange mechanism.
