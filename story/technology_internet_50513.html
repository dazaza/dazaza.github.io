Wenhu Comprehensive Search, Categorized Search

by f4wen on 2008-03-03 13:51:15

Wenho.cn Comprehensive Search, one site integrating all search engines.

This is the first comprehensive search engine in China that can query multiple databases.

"What exactly is comprehensive search?"

"Comprehensive search means that when you input a search keyword, all search results that meet the query conditions, whether they are news, websites, or web pages, will be displayed to the user."

"Oh, that's quite convenient. Can it even search for news?"

"Yes. The new Sina comprehensive search is convenient and practical, saving users the trouble of categorizing searches. These all-round pieces of information are arranged according to how well they match the search criteria, providing users with more choices while maintaining speed and accuracy."

"That sounds a bit abstract. Let me try it out. When I input 'Andy Lau' in Wenhu Search, wow, I can not only find websites about Andy Lau but also search for his latest updates, photos, and many other pieces of information. That's great."

"Similarly, when searching for 'mp3', you can easily find various directories and websites related to mp3; images of mp3 players; in the Wenhu content section, you can find timely and detailed relevant information such as the latest mp3 player software, mp3 players, and digital cameras with MP3 functionality; and in the product information, you can learn about the brand, price, and technical specifications of the latest mp3 products."

"Wenhu Search has some unique features. For example, it provides the most relevant parts of the content related to the searched keywords for selection. When searching for 'oicq', in addition to providing comprehensive results including directories, websites, Sina content, and product information that meet the search requirements, it also offers related keywords based on previous users' search habits, such as icq, Tencent, oicq tools, Tencent oicq, and oicq download. If users want to search for oicq tools next, they just need to click on the word 'oicq tools' provided on the webpage."

"By the way, this technology of related keywords is not exclusive to Sina. In fact, it is Baidu's technology, so Baidu's clients, like Sohu and 263, also have this function."

"In addition, Wenhu Search specifically has a search forum. If users have any questions during their search or cannot find something temporarily, they can ask questions on the search forum and immediately get answers from enthusiastic netizens, forming a culture of search teaching and helping."

"Yes, the search forum is quite popular. Actually, a lot of my and Xiao Zhang's search knowledge was learned from the forum. The VIP netizens and moderators in the search forum are all knowledgeable search experts, such as search engines 9238, shuxun, wyle, and others."
