In the name of the "National Day" Golden Week, I have been lazy for several days without updating my blog. On the one hand, I plan to take advantage of this rare opportunity to relax and enjoy myself, appropriately distancing myself from the computer and internet that I can't live without on a daily basis. On the other hand, I have indeed spent more time exploring nearby attractions, acting as a tourist and joining in the festivities. Although that is true, I had initially set a minimum goal of two blog posts per week in my heart. So, with the "Golden Week" about to end and this "assignment" not yet started, I couldn't help but feel somewhat uneasy. Fortunately, today my blogging friend linjuming consulted me and requested an invitation code for yo2 (Youbo Network). Logging into yo2, which I haven't visited for a long time, I discovered that it has now adopted an invitation-based registration system along with relevant domain binding reward mechanisms. Therefore, I couldn't resist writing this short article to make up the numbers, considering it as my "Golden Week" assignment, hoping to barely pass:-)

Yo2 (Youbo Network) is a relatively mature WordPress self-service website building platform, also the "WordPress mentor" of [Net Traveler's Notes] and the "base" before independently starting a blog in 08. It provides completely free WordPress blogging services (domain binding requires payment or corresponding reward support).
