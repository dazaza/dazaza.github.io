Wife Performs Arts for 9 Years to Cure Her Husband, Agree to Donate Corpses (Pictures)

by vmniah929 on 2012-03-01 12:01:05

Chen Huifang and Yuan Chunsheng had planned to celebrate a joyous Valentine's Day. Xinhua News Agency reporter Cheng Min took the photos. On February 13, Chen Huifang played the violin in an underground passage in Wuhan. A kind-hearted person donated money to Chen Huifang while she was playing the violin and encouraged her to keep going. Also on February 13, Chen Huifang (the first one on the left) and the nurses helped Yuan Chunsheng, who was recovering after surgery, wash his hair.

According to Xinhua News Agency, "Living together with shared dreams, dying together with the same pursuits." From "classmates" to "lovers," and then to "a couple in adversity," from the age of 12 to 53, the couple Yuan Chunsheng and Chen Huifang have known each other and stayed together for 41 years. Yuan Chunsheng and Chen Huifang were once classmates in elementary school; they knew each other at the age of 12. Yuan Chunsheng loved singing since childhood, and Chen Huifang started learning the violin in elementary school. In 1984, the lovers finally got married. Less than half a year after their marriage, Yuan Chunsheng suddenly became paralyzed and bedridden. They sought medical help everywhere and it wasn't until 1999 that Yuan Chunsheng was diagnosed with "bilateral femoral head necrosis." However, both were laid-off workers, and their daughter needed to go to school. Chen Huifang had no choice but to earn money by carrying passengers on a tricycle. After Wuhan City completely banned tricycle operations in 2003, Chen Huifang decided to pick up her violin, which she hadn't touched for decades, and perform in the underground pedestrian passage to support her family and raise money for her husband's treatment. She has been playing the violin for 9 years, spending 8 hours a day in the underground passage.

On the seventh day of the first lunar month this year, Yuan Chunsheng finally underwent artificial femoral head replacement surgery at the Puai Hospital in Wuhan. The surgery went smoothly, and he is currently in the postoperative recovery phase. Before the surgery, Yuan Chunsheng and Chen Huifang made a decision: to donate their bodies to the Red Cross after death and give all their organs to those in need.
