Introduction:

Among the green mountains and waters, people work from sunrise to sunset. Xu Sanduo (played by Wang Baoqiang) likes reading, but his father wants to send him to the army, thinking that only in this way will Xu Sanduo, who has been timid since childhood and is called a "turtle son," have some success.

Xu Sanduo blindly stepped into the military camp, seeing Sergeant Shi Jin (played by Zhang Yi) as his support, while Deputy Sergeant Wu Liuyi (played by Xing Jiadong) looked at him with hatred - worried that Xu Sanduo would drag down the sergeant and shame the group. After the new soldier training was over, Sanduo was assigned to a remote and arduous logistics pipeline maintenance class, while his fellow villager Cheng Cai (played by Chen Sicheng) went to the famous Steel Seventh Company.

The life in the maintenance class was lonely and boring, and the veterans spent their time playing cards and finding entertainment. The simple Sanduo still went out for physical exercise and training every day, and the veterans thought he didn't fit in, but Xu Sanduo didn't understand why.

Sergeant Ma (played by Fan Lei) casually mentioned wanting to build a road here, and Xu Sanduo took the sergeant's words as an order, building the road all by himself. The veterans were moved, and Class Five underwent tremendous changes. The colonel heard about it and transferred Xu Sanduo to the Steel Seventh Company, where he reluctantly left the much-loved Fifth Class.

After arriving at the Steel Seventh Company, Xu Sanduo became increasingly unconfident - everyone around him was stronger than him. The more he feared making mistakes, the more mistakes he made, and as an armored reconnaissance soldier, he even got carsick...

Xu Sanduo dragged down the entire class's performance, and Sergeant Shi Jin gradually inspired and taught Xu Sanduo again. Commander Gao Cheng (played by Zhang Guoqiang) reminded Shi Jin not to affect his own future because of a slow-witted Xu Sanduo. To overcome his motion sickness, Xu Sanduo repeatedly rotated on the horizontal bar and fell off again and again. Until during a full regiment assessment, encouraged by his sergeant, Xu Sanduo greatly surprised the entire company...

Gradually, Xu Sanduo became a top performer in training and competitions. But due to his age, Sergeant Shi Jin, who had also been promoted to class leader because of Sanduo, was put on the list for demobilization and retirement. Xu Sanduo, who had desperately trained to achieve results in order to keep his sergeant, was confused...

In the pain of parting and tough training, Xu Sanduo grew up. In the divisional confrontation exercise, he captured Captain Yuan Lang (played by Duan Yihong), who was renowned throughout the army for his reconnaissance skills. However, the Steel Seventh Company he belonged to could not escape failure in this exercise - due to the need for military reform, our traditional mechanized forces rapidly advanced towards modern combat forces, and the Steel Seventh Company was ordered to disband.

One by one, his comrades-in-arms left, and the commander was transferred to another unit. Xu Sanduo, who had always relied on his sergeant and comrades, became the last soldier of the Steel Seventh Company, guarding the barracks once filled with youthful passion. Sanduo endured loneliness and loss, and over time even began talking to himself. By persistence and daily consistent actions, Xu Sanduo silently persevered...

Father Xu Baishun came to the military camp to let Sanduo be discharged and return home, and Wu Liuyi led a group of comrades to make it seem like Sanduo had accomplished great things in the army. Xu Sanduo refused his father but felt deeply guilty towards him. But he indeed did not know what he would be like if he left the army.

The whole army established a multi-environment combat unit, code-named "A Team." Yuan Lang was ordered to form it, and the first person he thought of was Xu Sanduo, who had once captured him.

Xu Sanduo, Wu Liuyi, and Cheng Cai participated in a brutal long-distance combat competition. Wu Liuyi paid the price of losing a leg, and Xu Sanduo and Cheng Cai eventually gained the qualification to be selected.

Xu Sanduo entered a world he had never anticipated - "A Team". Different from all the previous units he had been in, there was no understanding or care here, only cold-bloodedness and training. Yuan Lang hammered them, letting all the members completely abandon themselves without preparation, and then re-forge them into a military group that met the requirements of modern warfare.

The new combat form required Xu Sanduo, who was simple-minded, to not be too simple, and needed Xu Sanduo, who liked depending on others, to independently judge and decide his own actions. In one training session after another that pushed the limits of physical and psychological endurance, Xu Sanduo persisted through his own strength. And the talented Cheng Cai was eliminated.

In a real operation against drug traffickers composed of foreign mercenaries, Xu Sanduo killed the enemy. The look in the drug trafficker's eyes before death and the impact of killing someone for the first time left Xu Sanduo unable to recover mentally. The kind-hearted Xu Sanduo even began to doubt whether he could continue.

As a veteran who had experienced life and death many times, Yuan Lang made a decision that others could hardly understand at the time - allowing Xu Sanduo to temporarily leave the military camp and go anywhere he wanted.

Xu Sanduo left the army he had lived with day and night, and behind him were the worried eyes of all his comrades.

Xu Sanduo returned to his old unit and met the comrades who had accompanied his growth. As a military secret, he could not tell them about his situation, but the understanding of soldiers allowed his comrades to see Xu Sanduo's pain and struggle. Xu Sanduo was searching for an answer, a release.

At this time, Xu Sanduo's family also encountered a major change. His father started a lime factory, and the stored explosives collapsed the house, leading to imprisonment. His irresponsible older brother ran away, and his second brother guarded the ruins at home by bullying those who came to collect debts. Xu Sanduo returned to his hometown, picked up his father from prison, and through the fundraising of Yuan Lang and others, gave his family new prospects.

Xu Sanduo returned, and finally, Yuan Lang and the others set their minds at ease.
