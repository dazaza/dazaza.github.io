Crisp and refreshing: three cool side dishes

by heyu50 on 2011-09-30 10:43:06

The dishes stir-fried in the pot are still not cool enough. The dishes that truly want a refreshing texture are cool skin, cold mixed and other refreshing cold dishes.

1. Cool Skin

Obviously, crispy cool skin is one of the most popular foods in summer. Moreover, more and more people are willing to make their own cool skin at home now. Mix a piece of flour, put it in water to wash the dough, wash out a pot of starch, then steam the powder slurry, and the cool skin is done.

People who like to eat cool skin can also use mung bean starch. The cool skin made from mung bean starch is snow white in color, high in transparency, and very beautiful. But the texture is not as good as the real cool skin, illegal drug advertisements, a bit tough.

2. Cold Mixed Side Dishes

When appetite decreases in summer, many people are willing to eat cold mixed dishes. Nutrition research has also confirmed that eating raw vegetables can maximize the preservation of nutrients in the vegetables, because some bioactive substances in vegetables will change their internal properties when exposed to temperatures above 55℃, losing their health functions. Fresh cold mixed dishes are simple to prepare, refreshing and appetizing to eat. Vegetables suitable for eating raw include carrots, radishes, tomatoes, cucumbers, bell peppers, cabbage hearts, and red cabbage, etc.

○Cold mixed fern root powder:

Fern roots themselves are wild primitive plants that can be used as both food and medicine. Compendium of Materia Medica once recorded that fern roots have effects such as smoothing the intestines, promoting bowel movements, clearing heat and detoxifying, reducing fat and lowering blood pressure, promoting blood circulation and unblocking meridians, lowering qi and transforming phlegm, and helping sleep. The main ingredients needed for this dish are fern root powder, cucumber, red cabbage, salt, soy sauce, vinegar, garlic paste, sesame, etc. However, the preparation method is quite simple. Boil the fern root powder in hot water for 5-8 minutes until there is no hard core, then rinse with cold water, and mix evenly as usual.

○Cold mixed sprouts and kelp:

Kelp is a highly nutritious vegetable. Compared to spinach and rape, besides containing vitamin C, its crude protein, sugar, calcium, and iron content are several times or even dozens of times higher. Moreover, kelp is a seaweed with a very high iodine content. People lacking iodine can prevent goiter by eating more kelp, prevent arteriosclerosis, and reduce the accumulation of cholesterol and fat. Kelp also has anti-radiation effects. Office workers spend a lot of time in contact with computers every day, so eating kelp regularly is very healthy.

The main ingredients of this dish are only yellow sprouts and kelp. On the seasoning side, there can be more options, such as fish sauce, aromatic vinegar, sugar, chopped chili, salt, garlic paste, chili powder, etc. The taste of the finished dish is cool and refreshing, with a sour and spicy flavor, which is very appetizing.
