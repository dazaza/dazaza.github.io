"6677bank.com" is operated by Digital Financial Services (Hangzhou) Co., Ltd. It is a financing and loan service information platform that pioneered the "Internet + Finance" model domestically. It provides distinctive "1+3+3" one-stop services for customers, including lifelong customer managers, loan review managers, and follow-up managers. It also offers access to more than three financial institutions, transforming the financing concept of small and medium-sized enterprises (SMEs) from "seeking financial institutions" to "financial institutions competing for customers", providing SMEs with a "financing banquet".

Differences between applying for loans through 6677bank.com and directly at a bank:

Firstly, based on your qualifications, your application can be sent to multiple suitable banks, where multiple banks/institutions will handle your loan.

Secondly, the company has close cooperation with multiple banks, which will reserve quotas for 6677bank.com customers, and give priority to handling 6677bank.com customers.

Thirdly, bulk customer groups can be pushed to banks, improving the efficiency and success rate of loan processing. If bulk customers apply simultaneously, they can also enjoy cost discounts on loans.

6677bank.com gathers loan products from multiple banks. You only need to apply for a loan through 6677bank.com, and it will help push your application to several suitable banks, after which the banks will proactively contact you. This saves you the time of visiting multiple banks and queuing at the bank. In the current situation of tight loan quotas, multiple banks will reserve loan quotas for 6677bank.com customers, saving customers' waiting time for disbursement, while also giving priority to handling customers referred by 6677bank.com.
