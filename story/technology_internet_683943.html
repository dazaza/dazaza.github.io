Benefits of Registering a Hong Kong Company

Hong Kong is an extremely information-developed international metropolis and one of the world’s most free trading ports. Coupled with its excellent infrastructure and sound legal system, it provides entrepreneurs and businessmen with unparalleled business environments. Currently, more and more mainland businessmen are establishing their own companies in Hong Kong for various purposes, developing their business, enhancing their company image, and strengthening overseas customers' confidence.

(1) Free choice of company names: Company names are allowed to contain regional and industry-related terms, such as China, Beijing, Zhejiang, Hong Kong, USA, France, International, Asia, Academy, Publishing House, Association, Group, Holding, Industry, Development, Investment, Finance, Friendship Association, Technology, Medical, Fund, Promotion Association, etc.

(2) Minimal restrictions on business scope: Clothing, furniture, jewelry, electronics technology, finance, medicine, shipping transportation, import-export trade, real estate, construction, decoration, information network, tourism, academy, cultural publishing, association, research institute, and other high-tech industries can all become our businesses. A Hong Kong limited company can operate any business in Hong Kong, except for illegal businesses.

(3) Favorable tax environment for development: Hong Kong has low tax rates and fewer types of taxes. Many institutions around the world use Hong Kong's tax advantages to achieve reasonable tax avoidance. When we establish a company in Hong Kong, we generally only need to pay two types of taxes: one is a one-time capital registration stamp duty at a rate of 1/1000, which is determined based on your actual registered capital; the other is profits tax at a rate of 16.5%, which is calculated based on our actual profit (net profit). If a company does not make a profit, it does not need to pay taxes. Apart from tobacco and alcohol or special cases, Hong Kong does not have import-export taxes.

(4) Low registered capital and no need for capital verification: The Hong Kong government requires a minimum registered capital of HKD 10,000. You can increase your registered capital based on your actual situation, with no limit on the amount of capital required to be in place. There is no need to transfer funds to a Hong Kong bank, laying the foundation for us to establish an international group company.

(5) Hong Kong companies allow no business operations and permit shell companies to exist. Establishing a Hong Kong shell company can turn into powerful advertising.

(6) A window to expand international markets and a stepping stone to enter the mainland Chinese market: Due to Hong Kong's unique historical conditions and geographical location, it creates favorable conditions for mainland enterprises to develop outwardly. Therefore, establishing a company in Hong Kong can serve as a window to the outside world, easily gaining the trust and cooperation of foreign companies. On the other hand, because China has just opened up, overseas investors prefer to set up regional headquarters in Hong Kong as a stepping stone to invest in China.

(7) Easy to obtain international credit and loans: It is well known that Hong Kong is Asia's economic and financial center, with almost every street having a bank. We can use the credit of Hong Kong banks, which is the foundation for developing international business. After obtaining credit, we can use Hong Kong as this financial center for financing or directly issue letters of credit to overseas parties to conduct large transactions with minimal capital. If necessary, we can also apply for SME assistance loans from the Hong Kong government and then invest these funds into the market for profit.

(8) Free flow of people, goods, and capital: Hong Kong is one of the freest and most prosperous commercial ports in the world with excellent infrastructure. Not only is the movement of people and goods free, but the flow of capital is also unrestricted. The freedom of people movement is reflected in Hong Kong's visa-free agreements with over 100 countries, and the upcoming visa-free travel plan with the Pearl River Delta region. The freedom of goods movement mainly manifests in the absence of tariffs on imports and exports, with extremely fast handling of sea, land, and air logistics. The freedom of capital movement is reflected in Hong Kong's lack of foreign exchange controls, allowing various currencies to be freely exchanged and transferred without restrictions. Businessmen like to use the convenience of Hong Kong banks to handle letters of credit.

(9) Hong Kong is one of the best places in the world due to its comprehensive intellectual property protection laws and mechanisms: Utilizing Hong Kong's reputation to create a Hong Kong brand. Due to Hong Kong's significant international status, emerging companies often use Hong Kong's reputation to package their own enterprises. For example, Hong Kong's clothing, toys, cultural publishing, and other industries have certain competitive advantages internationally. Establishing such companies in Hong Kong allows them to immediately share the overall international image and status, enhancing their competitiveness to some extent.

(10) Opportunity to gain Hong Kong residency: If your company has already contributed to Hong Kong, you can apply for multiple-entry business visas from the Hong Kong Immigration Department. After residing there for seven years, you can obtain permanent residency in Hong Kong. Within this year, the Hong Kong government will launch an investment immigration plan. For more information, please keep an eye on the announcements released by the Hong Kong government.

(11) Superb financial system: Hong Kong is a super financial center where, apart from being able to freely conduct remittance transactions with various countries, more than 200 types of financial services are available. Bank operations can be conducted via the internet and electronic transaction services, making it very convenient.

(12) Loans, mergers, and financing: A Hong Kong limited company can apply for any bank service, such as credit cards, bank loans, letter of credit services, and can also apply for any trust fund from the government to assist in business development.

(13) Limited liability: A Hong Kong limited company is a legitimate and independent entity. Legally, you and your limited company are completely unrelated, and you do not need to bear any responsibility for the limited company.

(14) Reliable impression: Hong Kong is a global super financial center with a sound legal system, giving your partners and collaborators a trustworthy impression, undoubtedly helping promote and develop your business.
