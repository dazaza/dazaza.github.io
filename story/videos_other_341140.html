The massage girl made the general manager of the mobile company so angry that he was spitting blood! One day, after working late, a general manager of a mobile company wanted to find a place for a massage to relax. He drove to a sauna and massage center.

Upon entering the center, the manager chose the prettiest massage girl and followed her inside. "Boss, would you like a massage in the hall or in the VIP room? Would you prefer regular service or the mobile VIP service?" the massage girl asked sweetly.

"In the VIP room; I want the mobile VIP service," replied the manager.

The massage girl led him into a VIP room. "Boss, there is an additional 200 yuan tip for the VIP room. According to our rules, please pay the tip first before we can provide the service."

"Isn't this tip too high compared to other places?" the manager asked.

"Sorry, but since you're transferring your consumption from elsewhere to here, this 200 yuan tip is equivalent to the 'roaming fee' of the mobile company, which isn't at all high."

The manager, though finding the rule peculiar, thought that the girls here were not only very polite but also more beautiful than those in other places. Thus, he handed over two "Red Bulls" (a slang term for red-colored bills) to the massage girl without hesitation.

After receiving the money, the massage girl asked again: "Boss, do you want a regular massage or a full package?"

"The full package," the manager replied.

"The full package requires a 500 yuan basic consumption fee upfront. Please pay first." The massage girl said.

"This is outrageous!" The manager was somewhat upset but still paid the money since he had already come.

After collecting the money, the massage girl asked again: "Boss, would you like to take a shower first?"

The manager thought, "Since I've already paid, why not?" So he said, "Yes."

"Would you like hot water or cold water?" the massage girl asked.

"Hot water."

"Please add 100 yuan for the hot water fee." the massage girl added.

"Damn it, is there no law and order?" Though complaining, with his clothes already off, the manager had no choice but to pay the money.

"Miss, why is it so dark in the bathroom that I can't see anything? Could you turn on the light?"

"Sure, I'll turn it on for you right away. However, please pay the 100 yuan fee for call display first." the massage girl politely responded.

"Are you trying to rip me off?" the manager was furious.

"You are our respected customer, how could you be considered a pig? Boss, you're really funny!" the massage girl replied politely.

While washing, the manager said to the massage girl, "Do you have any shower gel?"

"I'm sorry, shower gel is charged separately. However, we currently have a promotion where soap is free."

"Then bring me a piece of soap."

Two minutes later, the massage girl brought a piece of soap that had been used by many others. The manager, seeing this, angrily asked, "How can you give me a piece of soap that's been used by others?"

"I'm sorry, the soap here is freely available. You have to use whichever one you pick up."

"In that case, I don't want to use it."

"Then please settle the soap usage fee for this month, which is 300 yuan." the massage girl said kindly.

"Wasn't it supposed to be free? Why is there a charge now?" the manager stared wide-eyed.

"It's free for tonight, but next time will be charged."

"But I won't come here next time. I don't need your damn soap. Why should I pay?"

"Our rule is that if you use the soap, you must use it for at least a month, not calculated by days."

"Shit! It's darker than Sun Ergou's inn!" Having no choice, the manager paid another 300 yuan, saying, "This time, can you at least give me a new piece of soap?"

"Sorry, just use this one. Have you ever seen a mobile user choosing their own ringtone when using the color ringback tone service?"

Helplessly, despite feeling nauseous about using something others had used, the manager finished bathing quickly and unhappily.

"Let's start the service now." The manager wanted to finish quickly and leave.

"Alright, please pay 500 yuan for the service fee first." the massage girl answered.

"Haven't I already paid 500 yuan earlier?" the manager angrily questioned.

"That 500 yuan was the basic consumption fee - equivalent to the monthly rental fee of the mobile company. You need to pay another 500 yuan to get the service." the massage girl explained.

The manager thought there was no way he could continue playing here and decided to leave quickly. So he said, "Forget it, forget it. I don't want the service anymore. Give me back the 500 yuan I paid earlier. I'm leaving."

"Are you sure you don't want the service and are leaving?" the massage girl asked.

"Am I staying here to let you treat me like a pig? Give me my money back quickly." The manager was already fuming with anger.

"Well, since the front desk has already started timing, the 500 yuan cannot be refunded. Just like once a mobile card is activated, the monthly rental fee cannot be refunded." the massage girl patiently explained.

"To hell with it..." Taking a bath with someone else's used soap cost over 1000 yuan. This was too ruthless and humiliating... The manager completely lost it, angrily dressed up, and stormed out without looking back.

Before he even reached the door, the massage girl spoke again: "Boss, please pay a 3000 yuan deposit for canceling the service before leaving. We will refund it to you three months later with the receipt."

"I'm leaving and will never come back here. Why should I pay a service deposit?" The manager was boiling with anger.

"It's not a 'service deposit,' it's a 'cancellation deposit.' Even though you say you're leaving, how do we know you won't go to another room for a massage or come back another day? Therefore, we need to collect a 3000 yuan 'cancellation deposit.' If we confirm after three months that you haven't received services from us, we will return the deposit - just like when you cancel your account with the mobile company."

"If I refuse to pay, what are you going to do?" The manager was extremely angry.

"We certainly won't rob you, but if you don't go through the cancellation process, we will charge you 500 yuan every month as the basic massage consumption fee." the massage girl politely replied.

"If you have the guts to find me, then come and ask for it." The prestigious manager of the mobile company, who had seen everything, couldn't be scared by a mere massage girl. So he walked out without looking back.

Just as he stepped out of the massage center's gate, the massage girl followed and said: "Boss, when you opened the VIP room earlier, you registered with your ID. Your information is now saved in our computer system. If you don't cancel the service now, we will send a massage bill to your company via court every month. Meanwhile, our customer service personnel will also regularly call your home to urge payment!"

After hearing the massage girl's words, the manager suddenly felt a gush of blood rushing to his throat. With a "spit," a mouthful of fresh blood sprayed out, followed by his eyes blackening, and he collapsed outside the massage center's door!
