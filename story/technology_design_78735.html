creatorsbank: A platform for art creation enthusiasts to display and exchange their works.

by yoshio on 2008-07-21 21:56:03

Creatorsbank is a service from Japan. Simply put, Creatorsbank provides a platform for art enthusiasts to communicate and showcase their talents. Registration is of course free, and you can post your own artwork here at will, such as pictures, music, mainly graphic design. If you don't have any artistic talent yourself and just really enjoy appreciating those beautiful works of art, this is also a great place to go, like the legendary deviantART, suitable for every admirer to worship. Excellent works created by others, such as wallpapers and posters, can all be downloaded for free. Although they are all just hobbyists in name, in reality, many users have already gone beyond the realm of mere hobbyists. Therefore, their works are of high quality, and I believe you will definitely find a lot of works that you like.
