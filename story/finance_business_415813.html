The New Channel "Sing in English" College Student Singing Contest is the first competition launched by Chengdu New Channel in the Chengdu area, Panasonic Washing Machine Repair Center in Beijing. The purpose of this competition is to promote interest and enthusiasm for English learning among college students in Chengdu.

This competition encourages all students on campus to boldly showcase themselves. Learning English is the same; it requires courage to show it off.

The prizes set up for this competition are also very attractive. As long as you have enough courage, the grand prize can be yours!

Come and show if you've got what it takes; believe in yourself and you're already a winner... Hurry up and join!

Five competition zones will start simultaneously: Sichuan University Zone, Southwest Finance and Economics University Zone, Southwest Jiaotong University Zone, Sichuan Normal University Zone (East Campus), and Western Petroleum University Zone.

Registration Information:

1. All currently enrolled university or graduate students with a valid student ID can register on-site.

2. Online registrants must fill out the form strictly according to requirements. Any falsification will result in personal responsibility. (Contestants selected for the semi-finals must submit 5 casual photos and a personal VCR for online voting by other students.)

3. Registrants must abide by the competition rules; violators will be disqualified from the competition.

Competition Schedule:

1. Preliminary and initial rounds in each zone from March 30th to April 2nd. During the preliminary stage, 40-50 contestants will advance to the initial round, with 15 contestants from each zone advancing to the semi-finals.

2. Semi-finals from April 8th to April 10th. Five zones will select a total of 15 contestants to advance to the finals. The top 2 contestants from online voting will directly enter the finals.

3. Finals on April 23rd.

Registration Methods:

Online registration at the domain name provided.

On-site registration addresses can be found on posters in each zone;

Campus registration: Room 415, KEN Business Office Building, No. 22, Section 1, South Yihuan Road, Chengdu (100 meters east of the North Gate of Sichuan University).

Prize Settings:

The competition consists of preliminaries, initial rounds, semi-finals, and finals.

Finals: Champion - 10,000 yuan; Runner-up - 5,000 yuan; Third place - 3,000 yuan.

Internet Popularity Award - IPOD.

Semi-finals: Champion - 500 yuan; Runner-up - 300 yuan; Third place - 200 yuan. Bosch Washing Machine Repair in Beijing.
