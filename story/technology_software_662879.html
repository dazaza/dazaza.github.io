February 14, Taining, Fujian invites all the lovers in the world to come for a honey "benefit"

by ailvyou80 on 2012-02-10 17:13:51

In the spring light of February, love is heating up. The Valentine's Day of 2012 is about to come. For young couples in hot romance, honeymooning couples or intimate lovers who have been together for many years, are you excited yet nervous at this moment? On February 14th, Valentine's Day, what should you prepare for your beloved one? How to spend a unique romantic Valentine's Day with him/her? During the festival, Lingxiu Taining has prepared a special "love honey" gift for all lovers. On February 14th, friends who reserve a couple set meal at the Western restaurant of Jinhu Hotel can enjoy a buy-two-get-one-free discount. The set meals are divided into different levels and accompanied by surprise gifts such as red wine, red roses, chocolates, fruit platters, etc. In addition, the Canvas Coffee Shop also offers a couple set meal discount on that day, with five kinds of set meals and various combinations, allowing all friends who come to Taining to enjoy the romantic dating style of the city while feeling the simple and passionate atmosphere of love in the ancient city. Wander freely in Taining, no need to envy mandarin ducks or immortals.
