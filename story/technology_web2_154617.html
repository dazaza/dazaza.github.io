Several more mainstream online photo album evaluation

by immei on 2009-05-31 14:25:07

With the advent of digital photography, almost everyone now has hundreds of gigabytes of digital images that they want to post to the Internet to share with friends and family, record their lives, or show off. For bloggers, where should they put a lot of images in their content? Or, what kind of web album/online album do you choose? This has always been a controversial issue, and the choices can be broadly divided into three categories:

1, upload the photos to a professional web album/online photo album service website.

2, upload the photos to their own purchased virtual host, server space, and use the corresponding software to build albums to organize and present.

3, put the photos in the storage space of various BSP/ portal websites, such as Sinang, Sohu, NetEase photo album, Baidu Space, msn live space, QQ photo album, etc.

As for the third option, I personally dislike it. The reason is that the photo storage services of these BSP/ portals lack sufficient openness and autonomy, and there are many restrictions or deficiencies in their presentation and use, and the storage space is also limited. The photos stored above are difficult to relocate as a whole, there are restrictions on external chain references, and you can't see them in RSS... In the long run, it may cause you even more trouble.

For the first two choices, it should be said that each has advantages and disadvantages, you can choose according to your actual needs:

The first, the use of professional network photo album/online photo album website services, there is a strong hardware guarantee, technical support, a good browsing experience and user interface, its application and maintenance for users almost no technical threshold, its free account can basically meet the needs of most users, while the fee account will bring better service. At the same time, these sites emphasize the concept of sharing and community, and have the function of "meeting friends" or communicating with friends and expanding social networks.

Second, the advantage is more flexible and free, such as different access permissions for different photos can be set, there are no artificial restrictions on the use of the overall relocation in the future by their own control, if the use of fixed domain name to build albums, after the relocation will not affect the previous BLOG, forum reference links. However, relatively speaking, the cost and threshold are higher, network technology, the cost of purchasing domain names and space, software installation and configuration, etc., may become the obstacles in front of ordinary users; The access speed and stability of web hosting, the browsing experience of self-built albums, and the interaction and sociality of other users cannot be compared with the first option. Therefore, most people are better off with the first option.

For the first solution, I recommend three web photo/online photo album sites that I have used myself: Flickr, ikaca, and Youpai.
