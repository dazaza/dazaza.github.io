QQ Zone emotional diary: The savior has already passed away.

by lwf225 on 2009-08-31 18:21:58

(Actually, I am very sick.)

Don't go.

Don't forget me.

Okay? Okay?

If you had never said you liked me, then would we now be good friends or strangers?

If you had never said you loved me, then would we now have this kind of ending? Separated by life and death.

Why can you be so brave, knowing my obsession, still bravely confessing to me. Why? Actually, I have a lot of things to ask you, a lot of doubts for you to answer. But you left gracefully, and I was in a mess.

Actually, it never even started before it ended. Because you smiled brightly at me and said thank you. I was deeply saddened. My memories kept going wrong, constantly flickering, stopping and starting. But your face is still vividly clear. I can't avoid the tenderness you gave me, nor can I avoid your sad expression and sorrowful eyes. Such naked honesty. I laugh at myself for being pathetic.

I admitted to you that I am a coward. And I also admitted that the love I feel now is profound, I have fallen in love with the man I am with now.

I don't know what kind of feelings I have for you, because I am also very confused. Until you left, I never gave you an answer. You smiled at me and said it's okay. Apologizing for troubling me, as if it was your fault, but actually, I know it was my fault. At that time, I was extremely sad. Four years ago when you met me, there were many ups and downs. Leaving home, arguing, resisting, getting hurt, I don't know why heaven treated you so strictly, until now, I realize it was my responsibility.

Pure white person, pure white smile. In your world, everything is pure white, never leaving such a world, but now it has turned pale. You are in great pain but pretend to be happy to get through it. Your parents spoil you immensely, almost excessively. You get whatever you want. Your mother hates me, hating why I suddenly intruded into your life, giving you regrets before you left. She hates that I couldn't give you pure love. But your father is very tolerant of me. Every time he sees me, he gives me a gentle smile like you do. That smile makes me want to cry, crying out all those years of sadness and pain.

I'm not tall, not big, just normal. Yet I have to bear so much parting and sorrow. Those who will eventually leave can't bear it for me. Because you know, so you never forced me. You never threatened me with your failing body. You were too good to me, just like your parents who selflessly gave to you.

I am in great pain, very tired. But I have nowhere to rest. I just keep walking, walking. Very lost, very empty. Seeing you wave at me, in that instant, I froze. Because I seemed to see that boy again who would gently call my name.

That period of leaving. I met you. A pure person.

I have always been very cold towards you, very indifferent. But you didn't care at all. If loving someone is like losing all pieces in a chess game, then you lost everything the moment you saw me. You knew, you understood, why didn't you try to resist. Is it because you have already submitted to fate?

I am sorry, but thinking about how you quietly lie in that cold wooden coffin now, I remain cold and expressionless. Because I have faced such deaths before. I have become numb to death. It's like suddenly knowing I will die today and not being surprised at all. Your mother still screams over the phone, because she thinks I am too unfeeling, too cruel, too cold-blooded. At that moment, I wanted to laugh, but it was a miserable laugh, uglier than crying.

You would definitely frown and say to me, such a good face, you've ruined it completely.

Your mother once again says, she will never forgive me her whole life.

You know, I suddenly realized, I, this person, am either loved or hated by others. I can never find balance. No one reaches out to save me. Am I too much of a failure? Imagining your peaceful and clean appearance, I suddenly remember hearing about that person's death. Even standing under the sun, I still felt icy cold all over. Unable to stop trembling. At that moment, I formally felt the power of death. It seems to be everywhere.

XX once asked me, if I were to die today, what would be the thing I most want to do.

I tilted my head and said, smoke a cigarette, say goodbye to everyone I know.

He was surprised by my answer.

Now while writing this article, I remembered, the girl named Mi asked me, if tomorrow is the end of the world, what would you want to do.

I answered her, say goodbye to people I know. This should be the answer. It seems like I am always facing death and departure. Sometimes, some memories that aren't so good, people with poor memory often involuntarily recall some past events, and these past events are related to what you're doing now.

Usually, things we think we've forgotten, actually haven't been forgotten. They're just buried too deep, making us think we've forgotten.

Many times, I don't think I am just one person. I feel more like a lunatic. A complete lunatic, often making people feel at a loss.

Suddenly, I don't know how to continue writing. To you, it seems like I really haven't forgotten, the memory has always existed. Whether it's your transparent rims, or your smiling eyes, or your pretending to be happy despite being in pain. Suddenly, they come rushing at me like a flood, and I find myself powerless. My fingers are typing quickly, but my body is out of my control.

Let me tell you something, I have truly experienced too much. Too many sorrowful memories. In the end, I can't distinguish which are real and which are illusions. I am trying hard to live, to be a person.

Living like a person, with full dedication. Would you praise me? I have written a lot of words for him, but I have never written anything for you. Now, I turn my memories of you into words, calling it a delusional hallucination would also be fine.

Once like a walking corpse, now living like a person. Sigh.

After typing these words, my mind went blank, I couldn't remember anything. I have been too sad recently, it seems like I am returning to my previous life. Even if someone sends me somewhere, I don't care. I don't know what I should be doing, I have set goals, to live like a person. Why can't I achieve it? I am so tired. I want to tell you, I am so tired. I want to tell you, I don't want to live anymore. I want to tell you, let me sleep. I want to say, I am scared of being cold. I want to say, I severely lack security. I want to say, please don't go, okay?

Mm. Just consider it as me talking nonsense alone.

Mm. Just consider these as my hallucinations, my pseudo-conceptual texts used to deceive the masses.

If possible, I wish these were just hallucinations, and you were still alive, wandering in that gentle city. You have never mentioned leaving first, yet you always say goodbye before I do.

I always say I hope one day you'll tell me first, take care, you won't protect me anymore. But unexpectedly, the day it really came, you did it in such a decisive way. You must be reluctant, right? You once said, because I need care, you never wanted to die.

But I, in the neighborhood next to heaven, can no longer find you.

This article is dedicated to a certain Mr. Wang who said he loved me four years ago.

These words, I will fold them into flowers, burn them into ashes.

Thank you for taking care, please do not reproduce this article.
