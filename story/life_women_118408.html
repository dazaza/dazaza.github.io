Principles and Details of E-light Spot Removal

Different colored E-light is absorbed by different colored skin, and uses E-light energy to cause dye particles to disintegrate and vaporize, sealing the blood vessels, then the body absorbs the dye particles and expels them, causing pigmentation to fade.

Details: Since E-light of a certain wavelength is only absorbed by the corresponding color of pigment, only diseased cells absorb specific E-light, leaving normal skin tissues undamaged, thus no scars are left behind.
