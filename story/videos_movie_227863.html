Back at DIE, Jing entrusts her baby to her extended family for care, but this causes disagreements between her mother and grandmother over how to look after the baby, leading to constant bickering day and night. Consequently, Jing brings her baby to work, where the DIE team takes turns caring for the baby, resulting in endless laughter.
