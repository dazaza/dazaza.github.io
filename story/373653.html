Danxia Hot Spring Scenic Area

by zhou519621 on 2010-12-07 16:58:48

Guilin Danxia Hot Spring Resort is carefully built according to the national AAAA scenic area standard. The whole area covers 360 mu (Chinese acre), and it is a new type of comprehensive hot spring scenic area integrating hot spring bathing, leisure health care, tourism sightseeing, vacation, business meeting, ecological tourism, science popularization education as well as perfect accommodation, meal, entertainment and shopping facilities. The scenic area is located in Zhongfeng Township, Ziyuan County, Guilin City, Guangxi Zhuang Autonomous Region. It is 6 kilometers away from Ziyuan County, 96 kilometers away from Guilin City, and it takes only 70 minutes by car to reach Guilin Airport. The transportation is very convenient. To the north of the scenic area is Hunan Province, with National Highway 322 passing by and Provincial Highway 202 leading directly to the scenic area. The geographical location and transportation are very superior.

The Danxia Hot Spring product has broken through the previous development concept and strives to shape a new image of hot spring products, that is, while integrating the natural, leisure, dynamic, healthy, and scientific characteristics of hot spring products, it emphasizes the cultural and differentiated features of hot spring products. There are more than 40 different styles and sizes of functional hot spring pools scattered throughout the scenic area, including dynamic pool areas, children's pool areas, health-care pool areas, exotic风情pool areas, ecological pool areas, and lovers' pool areas. In addition, the hot spring pool area also has large indoor SPA hydrotherapy, fitness and beauty combinations, landscape waterfalls, cold and hot dual waterfalls, Eight Precious Soups, wave pools, exotic beaches, open-air furo, fish therapy pools, Hakone wooden house baths, Bali SPA, hot spring food workshops, open-air stages, etc. The entire hot spring area incorporates the bathing styles of various countries and infuses unique and profound cultural connotations, such as the tranquil Zen flavor of Japanese hot springs, the romantic charm of South Asian hot spring houses, and the local characteristics and folk bathing culture of hot spring pools. While enjoying the hot springs, visitors can also receive professional rubbing, massage, traditional Chinese medical treatment, and authentic South Asian SPA packages in the rest hall or massage center. The warm and romantic wooden hot spring houses and various types of medicinal hot springs for health promotion form an entirely new hot spring world.

The scenic area boasts a beautiful and serene environment, with broad fields, green valleys, winding paths leading to secluded spots, steep cliffs and cascading waterfalls, and winding streams. Especially the dense primeval ecological forest forms a natural "oxygen bar" making the air even fresher. The lakes and pavilions within the park complement each other beautifully, and being here will make you feel refreshed. There is no noise or pollution from the city here. For urban dwellers accustomed to fast-paced living, fully enjoying the tranquility and comfort of the countryside at night is indeed a pleasure that washes away the hustle and bustle and makes one drunk with delight.

The scenic area has more than 160 rooms of various types, which can accommodate more than 300 people at the same time. Among them, the 8 foreign-style hot spring bathhouses designed by famous masters can be used for both accommodation and separate rental. They are equipped with complete facilities, and with warm and thoughtful service, they will surely fulfill your dream of a wonderful hot spring experience.

To accommodate relevant business conference activities, the scenic area has a conference center consisting of five conference halls of different sizes. Conference rooms of different scales can accommodate various conferences ranging from 10 to 350 people. The conference hall is equipped with high-quality projectors, imported sound systems, and Internet terminals, providing first-class efficient services for various meetings and business presentations.

The elegant "Hot Spring Chinese Restaurant" allows you to taste dishes from various cuisines and mountainous rural flavor snacks and seasonal fresh vegetables with distinct local characteristics, adding much fun to your trip. The restaurant has nearly 15 luxury private rooms, and together with the dining hall, it can accommodate about 600 people dining at the same time. Delicious food intoxicates travelers, and a variety of authentic local traditional famous dishes and snacks will definitely leave you with endless aftertaste. To truly experience the fun of leisure and relaxation, the scenic area also has various recreational projects such as a gym, table tennis room, internet cafe, sandpot ball room, and billiards.

After the opening of the scenic area, it will undoubtedly attract people from all walks of life with its unique charm of hot springs and rich cultural connotations. At the same time, the good service atmosphere will certainly win the praise of tourists from all sectors.

As a brand-new hot spring leisure tourist attraction, Guilin Danxia Hot Spring Resort sincerely welcomes your visit. Danxia Hot Spring will welcome guests from all over with a brand-new business philosophy, perfect service facilities, and thoughtful distinctive services.
