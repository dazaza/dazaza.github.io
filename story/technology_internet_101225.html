The new Unicom officially announced the internal institutional setup

by yinyin on 2008-11-05 10:07:38

Only half a month from the official listing, China United Network Communications Co., LTD. (hereinafter referred to as "New Unicom") announced the company's internal organization and senior management list. The clarity of the internal organization and its responsible person indicates that the reorganization of the new Unicom has entered the final stage.

            In addition to Chang Xiaobing, head of the preparatory group of New Unicom, the senior executives of New Unicom Group are Lu Yimin, Zuo Xunsheng, Pei Aihua, Tong Jilu, Li Jianguo, Zhao Jidong, Li Fusheng, Li Gang, Zhang Jun 'an and Jiang Zhengxin. Among them, Tong Jilu, Li Gang, Zhang Jun 'an are the former vice presidents of China Unicom, the remaining 7 vice presidents are from China Netcom, plus Chang Xiaobing, in the new management team from the former China Unicom is 4. Due to the large number of leaders after the merger of China Unicom and China Netcom, the new Unicom basically adopts the practice of two sets of groups and listed companies, in addition to some important executives, other executives are no longer part-time. At the same time, the new Unicom also announced the general manager of the provincial company, the relevant personnel allocation uphold the principle of balance, the ratio of the original Netcom and the original Unicom personnel is basically 1:1.

            In order to highlight the principle of customer service positioning, the new Unicom has set up a total of 28 departments such as Marketing Department, individual customer department, family customer department, group customer department and international business Department, in addition to the establishment of 18 direct production support units. Chang Xiaobing said in the video conference that the work of the internal organization setting and responsible person arrangement mainly follows the four principles of adapting to the development needs, ensuring a smooth transition, taking into account the current and future, and reasonable and balanced equipment. As a newly established full-service operator, the new Unicom also announced the establishment of a separate mobile network company to develop 3G services, which will be mainly responsible for the construction and maintenance of 3G mobile networks.

            On October 15, the new Unicom was officially established. Chang Xiaobing said at the time that the new Unicom's positioning is "broadband communications and information service providers", in the next period of time, will fully integrate wireless and fixed network business to promote full business operations, and increase the construction of fixed network broadband to promote fixed and mobile network broadband.

            According to the latest financial report, China Unicom's net profit in the first three quarters of this year was about 7.41 billion yuan. During the period, the company achieved operating revenue of 53.67 billion yuan, up 3.9% from a year earlier. Among them, the revenue of G network is 49.48 billion yuan, and the average monthly income per household is 42.8 yuan. In addition, the C network business, which has been sold to China Telecom, also posted a net profit of 1.6 billion yuan in the first three quarters.

            In the face of the rumors of opening a new official website, the new Unicom also made a clarification. New China unicom, said foreign provide information service website, there are two, one is www.chinaunicom.com , The other is www.cnc.cn, which has been revised. Given that the follow-up work related to the merger will take some time to complete, the new Unicom will use the above two websites to provide information services to the public and customers and promote related businesses during the transition period. When the time is ripe, the new China Unicom will merge the above two websites, among which the www.cnc.cn domain name will cease to be used, and its current service functions will be taken over by another website.

Business Daily Reporter MAO Taotao
