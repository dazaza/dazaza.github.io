Everywhere is lush with greenery, grass grows and orioles fly, willows sway gently by the lakeside, peach blossoms by the road are in full bloom. When the spring breeze passes through, petals of peach flowers dance chaotically in the wind. In no time, the stone paved ground becomes a sea of bright red, dotted with faded red hues, yet still retaining their graceful demeanor, making one hesitate to step on them.

Following the green stone paved path straight ahead, passing through a green bamboo forest and turning left, you can see a two-story Chinese-style building. It has black tiles, red walls, and intricately carved railings. In the center of the plaque, there are a few elegant large characters written in cursive script: "Past Life Present Life".

"Past Life Present Life", this is the name of a teahouse, and also my workplace - I am Ye Yin, a 19-year-old girl. In this city where drinking tea is a popular custom, such teahouses are countless. Of course, the teahouse is a place for business, but besides tea, we also do other businesses, a special kind of business related to past and present lives.

Have you ever heard of such a saying? To know the events of your previous life, look at what you experience in this life. The cause from your previous life leads to the effect in your next life. If you have sown this cause, then no matter how many reincarnations you go through, you cannot escape this result. So how can it be resolved? Perhaps this is an inevitable and difficult-to-resolve fate, but our business precisely aims to return to the life when you planted the seed, find the root of destiny, and change it. This business does not take money, it only takes---a single tear from you.

Some may say that isn't that requiring time travel? How could it possibly be done? Indeed, time travel is impossible for ordinary people, but for him, it's not a difficult task. He---is my master, the owner of this teahouse.

Master has a beautiful name---Si Yin, just as beautiful as his name.

I was adopted by Si Yin since I was young, naturally considering Si Yin as the closest person to me. Since I understood things, I rarely saw Si Yin smile, nor did I see him angry or upset. He was always calm, as if nothing in this world could move him. In my memory, he only showed a hint of gentleness when teaching me the art of communicating with spirits and exorcising evil spirits. Apart from the name Si Yin and the spirit communication technique, I know nothing about him, including his age and nationality.

There are usually three people in the teahouse: Si Yin, me, and my senior apprentice Fei Niao. Although Fei Niao's name sounds very Eastern, he has a head of dazzling golden hair and eyes as blue as the sea. I don't know where he came from. When Si Yin adopted me, he was already there, four years older than me, a warm and outgoing boy. Maybe because of his character, his girlfriends keep changing like a carousel. However, we who grew up together have a deep bond, a feeling better than siblings. He is highly talented and became a master much earlier than me. All along, he has been constantly traveling through different times and spaces, searching for the source of the client's past life.

The past lives of different clients come in all shapes and sizes, not limited to ancient China. Occasionally, there are also ancient Europe and ancient Africa. The language problem is not big. Before each mission, Si Yin would let him swallow a pill called Jie Yu Wan, so that there would be no language barrier in any time and space, any country. However, the Jie Yu Wan only works in different times and spaces, it is completely ineffective in the current time and space. After knowing this, I had no choice but to pick up my English textbook again with frustration. I originally thought that as long as I had a Jie Yu Wan, I could dominate the real world too.

However, today is a special day for me because I have officially become a master. Starting today, I can also soar through five thousand years of history like my senior apprentice. Every time I listened to my senior apprentice talk about the bits and pieces of different times and spaces, I always felt itchy inside. Now, I can finally stand on my own. Thinking about this, I couldn't help but laugh out loud. I hope the past life of my first client is in the era I am interested in. No, I shouldn't think like that, this is work, not playing-------- Hmm, but after completing the task, I can play for a while, right?
