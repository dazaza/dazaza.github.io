by yingkeduo1 on 2012-02-24 09:31:21

First, it is essential to understand the logistics system of Taobao:

1. If it's a physical goods transaction, you must fill in the courier waybill number, and this waybill number must comply with the numerical rules of the logistics company.

2. If the filled-in waybill number does not match the logistics company's rule, then it will be 100% detected by Taobao.

3. Only when the order conforms to the logistics company's rules, the chance of being detected will be greatly reduced. Taobao has hundreds of thousands of orders every day. It's impossible for all of them to be reviewed manually, and Taobao's database doesn't have the capacity to store and check all the orders from every courier company.

Understanding Taobao's logistics waybill review process, now we need to understand the waybill number rules of major logistics companies:

YTO Express (YTO waybill numbers consist of 10 digits, usually starting with 1**, 2**, 8**, etc.)

STO Express waybill numbers consist of 12 digits, usually starting with 268**, 58**, etc.

ZTO Express waybill numbers consist of 12 digits, usually starting with 2008**, 6**, etc.

Yunda Express waybill numbers consist of 13 digits, usually starting with 10*, 12*, etc.

SF Express waybill numbers consist of 12 digits, usually starting with the last three digits of a telephone area code.

EMS waybill numbers consist of 4 English letters and 9 digits, generally with two uppercase English letters at the front and back like: ES in the front and CN at the back, with 9 digits in the middle, example: ES120769088CN. The first two English letters can be EA, ES, EI, EH, EX. The middle numbers can be any 9-digit number.

Now, I'll provide a few methods for everyone, which will make the process safer:

1. If you have real courier waybills, use real ones for brushing, one waybill can be used for five transactions. If you don't have real courier waybills, it's not hard to find some. You can contact the courier staff to cooperate with you and provide you with waybill numbers. If you have many stores and different couriers every day, you can also exchange them between stores. Use Paipai's waybills on Taobao, and Taobao's waybills on Paipai, achieving mutual benefits.

2. Use real courier waybill numbers found online. Input the regular waybill numbers into the official website to search, then modify a few numbers at the end and input again to search. Usually, if the difference isn't too far, and if the waybill number is real and the time matches, it can be used. As for the delivery address, you can modify it in the buyer account or leave a message on Wangwang saying that your delivery address has been changed.

3. Require the buyers who take on your tasks to be in the same city as you. The filled-in logistics company can be some very small local logistics companies, and the freight number can be randomly filled, which Taobao cannot detect.
