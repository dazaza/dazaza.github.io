Trendy and meaningful dating gameplay

The concept of a marriage and dating program is not exactly new. During the years when "Very Men and Women" became popular, many marriage and dating programs sprang up like mushrooms after rain across the country, and Jiangsu Satellite TV itself launched "Happy Eden." So does this program called "If You Are the One" repeat the old path? Wang Gang said it was not "putting new wine in old bottles": "Although marriage and dating programs were very popular before, our current program is definitely not a copy of the old programs. In the past, the number of men and women in dating programs was equal, with round-by-round pairings, which would be slow. However, our program takes place under an extremely imbalanced ratio of men to women, possibly one man facing twenty-four women, creating a dramatic conflict effect, with a fast pace, where each round of pairing takes only 5 minutes, fitting the appreciation habits of young people."

In addition to the pace and dramatic effects, another highlight of "If You Are the One" is its real reflection of modern views on marriage and love. Wang Gang said: "We won't deliberately promote anything, but the design of the program will allow everyone to see the views on marriage and love of today's young people. For example, in situations where one man faces multiple women, some men we think are very excellent are immediately eliminated, while others who don't seem to have obvious advantages are very popular... Thus, the value choices of young people naturally come out." While allowing young people to make their own choices, "If You Are the One" also adopts the form of expert panels offering opinions, discussing topics that are currently very popular on the internet and in society: houses, children, mother-in-law and daughter-in-law relationships, etc. To sum up, this dating gameplay is both trendy and meaningful.
