"Former Governor" gives advice to Governor Wei: Focus port construction on the west

Date: 2006-01-21 12:25:19 Source: Hainan Daily

Haikou Evening News reported on January 21st that on the morning of January 20th, Governor Wei Liucheng visited the Linqiong delegation to discuss Linqiong's development with representatives. When talking about the development of fisheries, former Vice Governor of Hainan Province and current farmer Chen Suhou, who is also a representative of the Linqiong delegation, seriously said to Wei Liucheng, "Governor, our delegation has some opinions about you when reviewing the 'Eleventh Five-Year' plan." After hearing this, Wei Liucheng humbly asked, "What opinions?"

Chen Suhou said, "Governor, Linqiong and Danzhou are major fishing areas in Hainan, producing two-thirds of the province's fish output, with Linqiong accounting for one-third of the province's fishing catch. However, what we cannot understand is that in the 'Eleventh Five-Year' plan, the provincial government focused the central port construction on the east instead of the west."

After listening, Wei Liucheng said, "During my last research trip to Linqiong, I suggested establishing a large-scale fishery trade market there." Upon hearing this, Chen Suhou immediately nodded in agreement, saying, "That's right, very good!"

Wei Liucheng continued, "When making the 'Eleventh Five-Year' plan, I suggested including Linqiong's fishery trade market, but you should first prepare a plan, at least conceptual, such as the size, location, and business content, so you have a clear idea beforehand."

Wei Liucheng further added, "Linqiong is currently a major county for fishery catching in our province, but how to become a strong county in fishery economy requires careful planning from all of you." Chen Suhou agreed, saying, "The fastest way for the people of Linqiong to reach a well-off standard is through fisheries." Wei Liucheng responded, "Yes, we will also provide direct subsidies to fishermen in Linqiong through the Ocean and Fishery Bureau, encouraging everyone to build larger boats and venture into deeper seas."

In conclusion, Wei Liucheng smiled at Chen Suhou and said, "Old Governor, I accept your opinion, and in the future, we will work together to genuinely focus on improving Linqiong's fishery production."
