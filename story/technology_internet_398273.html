After turning back, the romance is just a hollow reputation.

by piniinq58 on 2011-03-30 15:59:48

It was her who turned back and separated first. All that I had with her stopped because of this. I don't hate her. At that time, I could only call her that person, because I couldn't find any better words to describe her! I even hope that I would never remember her name for the rest of my life. The wind blew away love, and everything seemed to be getting better. What is love? My heart also went with the wind. I knew she loved me, but she wanted me to hate her. Unfortunately, I didn't hate her. What I want to sacrifice is that past, as well as memories of the past. Even if that person has caused me great harm, what is love? I completely don't hate her. Love is a very luxurious thing. And for me and that person, there has been no love already, or even never had a moment of affection. We have been living an ordinary and simple life, which is also a kind of habit. It takes a lot of courage to choose to leave this road, to face loneliness, and to face the life of being alone. We unknowingly formed a dependence on each other, which suffocates and tires us. It's not easy to turn around and leave, because I left reluctantly. This made my heart heavier and more painful. This makes me feel breathless... Maybe I shouldn't talk about love anymore in my whole life...! After this failed relationship, I feel that sometimes, feelings and love are just in name only...!
