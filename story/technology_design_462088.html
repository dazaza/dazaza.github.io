Truck driver dissatisfied with punishment for beating people and smashing cars detained for 7 days

by zxyasdg00 on 2011-07-13 15:41:15

According to our news report, yesterday the truck driver Han who drove overloaded vehicle was detained for 7 days by the police. He also had to pay thousands of yuan for the repair cost of law enforcement car - the night before last night, after he received the notice of illegal acts from highway law enforcement officers, he suddenly pulled an officer out of the law enforcement car and chased him with beating. After being dissuaded by bystanders, he smashed the law enforcement car with an iron rod.

Han is 35 years old this year and is from Jiangjin District. The truck he drove has a license plate number of Yu B48519. At around 9:40 PM on the evening of August 13th, at the Yurong toll station law enforcement checkpoint on the Chengyu Expressway, team members Hu Zhao and Zhang Yong of the First Brigade of the Municipal Highway Law Enforcement Corps were conducting routine inspections of passing vehicles. It was found that the truck driven by Han was engaged in oversized transportation and did not have reflective stickers as required, so they immediately issued a notice of illegal acts.

According to regulations, these two illegal acts must be handled at the law enforcement station, which are not suitable for simple procedure handling on site. Han did not understand this and said that a truckload of coal must be delivered to its destination on time. He requested that the law enforcement officers handle it according to the simple procedure, but was refused.

An unexpected scene occurred: when Hu returned to the law enforcement car, Han suddenly pulled open the door and dragged Hu out of the driving room. Zhang filmed what happened later: Han grabbed Hu's shirt and kept pushing him, then chased and beat him. During this period, Hu did not fight back. There were quite a few drivers at the checkpoint who saw this and came forward to dissuade him.

After Han was pulled away by the crowd, he returned to his truck and took out an iron rod, smashing the windshield of the law enforcement car. Then, the police of Ronglong Police Station of Rongchang County Public Security Bureau arrived and controlled Han.

Yesterday afternoon, the First Brigade introduced that Han did not deny the illegal transportation afterwards and had paid a fine of 1200 yuan. According to the "Public Security Punishment Law", he was also administratively detained for 7 days and ordered to compensate for the repair cost of the damaged law enforcement car.

Chief reporter Huang Yanchun
