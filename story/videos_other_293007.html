Eventually, we will miss some people..

by yang5230 on 2009-12-04 17:26:49

During our lifetime, we will experience some twists and turns in emotions, sooner or later. As we follow the emotional lines further and further away from life, looking back suddenly, the past is filled with impressions of joy, sorrow, happiness, and sadness, all intertwined. Those people who once surged quietly in our lives, in this meadow trampled by hatred, can they still preserve a precious feeling to savor the time that has passed? But we have missed it, some people who could have accompanied us for a lifetime.

There are some sighs in the tranquility of our souls, like the fleeting appearance of a night-blooming cereus, dreamlike memories swaying in the light of new events. At the moment when tears silently choke us up, we realize that in the process of our lives, happiness and beauty were always within reach, but we did not cherish them well enough. No matter how long the reminiscence lasts, it can only be consigned to the flow of time, becoming a lifelong regret.

Life is long, and for survival, we move from one environment to another, from one state to another. Following the continuous pursuit of our aspirations, we inevitably say goodbye to some people and some times to chase after the halo of our dreams. With the changes of the times, our thoughts and actions also continuously evolve, following the steps of the era. In the space of emotions, we are influenced by new ideas, deviating from our original beliefs, vows, and promises. These are just necessary elements in the grand narrative of love, but in real life, none of us have the courage to face the future fate. Therefore, we often pass by some people, until we reach the age limit for marriage and discussions, through constant memories and comparisons, we suddenly realize that indeed, we have missed some people who could have been together for a lifetime. This realization leaves us disheartened and sorrowful, wondering if it's fate that plays tricks on people, or people playing tricks on fate. Among these, there is no definite answer, as relationships come and go. When love arrives, we should cherish it, not miss the happiness that can be easily obtained. Once lost, we can only fill the void with memories, regretting the lost time for the rest of our lives.

When we encounter the right person, someone who can integrate into our lives, whether simple or complex, we should not hesitate. During hesitation, he or she may become someone else's. We should not crave material enjoyment nor spiritual superiority. There are no perfect people in the world, nor ideal lives. Wealth and poverty, as long as we are content, are enough. Often, because we are confused by some truths of life, it makes our path of love full of mud and obstacles. Love is what, no one can give an exact answer, just like our own lives, which are immeasurable. The only thing we can do is cherish what we have. The future is an unknown number, no one can predict its specific form, nor can anyone foresee their own or others' futures. Therefore, don't be deceived by appearances, leading to "This emotion can be waited for in memory, but at the time it was not worth it."

However, as our worldview, outlook on life, and values continue to change, so does our view on love. Looking at ourselves and those around us, we find that many people lose love while losing themselves, thus becoming gloomy and lost in life. Each of us has beautiful beginnings hidden beneath our calm exteriors, but due to environmental changes, we deviate from the central track. Once we lose firm belief, we also lose strength and ability. Thus, we lose even more opportunities to possess happiness, and love can become a fleeting good time in life. When we discover the problem and try to make amends for the losses caused by our own mistakes, it turns out that things remain the same, but time does not.

In life, we will inevitably encounter some people, and we should thank these people who have crossed our paths, as they teach us the value of happiness. It's time to stop missing the right people for ourselves and not let the regrets of the past occupy the painful heart. Time waits for no one, only people wait for time. Don't believe that what is destined to be yours will naturally come to you; without striving and grasping, there will never be an opportunity. What is fate? Fate is giving you a chance to meet. Happiness depends on your own efforts. There is no such thing as a free lunch falling from the sky, nor is there such a thing as unexpected good fortune from nowhere. Heaven's gifts rely more on human effort. We should all strive and cherish, and no longer miss the chance to "hold your hand, grow old with you," a loyal companion in life. Life is unpredictable, and we should remain steadfast in our beliefs no matter when or where, not letting regrets consume our souls, and not wasting our lives in wasted time...
