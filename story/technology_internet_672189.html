American virtual host providers launch a full-scale promotion for the Spring Festival, ready to carve up the year-end market pie.

by xwkj0983 on 2012-02-22 16:13:16

With the arrival of the New Year, American virtual hosting providers have also begun to actively prepare for their battle to capture a large share of the cross-year market. Just as people were still immersed in dreams about the New Year's Eve gala, GoDaddy, the American hosting giant, launched its strong entry into China with a variety of well-known products, firing the first shot in the New Year's market promotion. Subsequently, many other well-known American hosting providers, including HostEase and IXWebHosting, also joined this year-end promotional campaign. Thus, American hosting providers formally ignited full-scale competition in the Chinese market.

GoDaddy’s Domain and Hosting Products See Comprehensive Price Reductions

As a leader in the hosting industry, GoDaddy did not disappoint during the New Year period, offering various discounts. It is understood that the currently popular .COM, .ORG, and .ME domain names are now available for only $5.99, $6.99, and $7.99 respectively. Users can purchase these domains at discounted prices by using the promotional links www.gd5.org, www.gdoff.org, and www.gdoff.me respectively. GoDaddy offers numerous domain name discounts, and its hosting discounts are equally impressive. In response to user needs, GoDaddy partnered with the American Hosting Detective to launch a 30% discount on annual payment plans for hosting services. This is a great opportunity for users purchasing the 4GH annual payment plan. By purchasing through the promotional link www.gd30off.com, users can enjoy double discounts. After the discount, the deluxe hosting plan with 150GB of super-large space costs only 318 yuan per year. Additionally, users can purchase SSL certificates through the promotional link www.gdssl.org for just $12.99, and they will also receive a free dedicated IP. Clearly, with the arrival of the New Year, GoDaddy's promotional efforts have become increasingly intense.

IXWebHosting Hosting: 20% Discount

IXWebhosting has launched a 20% discount promotional campaign. By purchasing virtual hosting through the secret link below the Chinese official website http://cn.ixwebhosting.com, users can enjoy an 80% discount. Through this promotional link, users can purchase one-year, two-year, or three-year plans and still enjoy an 80% discount. After the discount, the annual unlimited hosting originally priced at $107.4 is now only $85.92, and the two-year unlimited hosting originally priced at $190.8 is now only $133.36. It can be seen that purchasing IXWebHosting hosting through the promotional link offers extremely high cost-effectiveness. To meet user demands, the well-known American hosting provider HostEase also released the discount code "hostease." Users can enjoy a 20% price discount on any shared hosting plan by using this discount code. Moreover, the payment method is very flexible, supporting both annual payments and monthly payments.

The beginning of the New Year signifies the start of new competition. To gain a competitive advantage, it is necessary to seize the timing of competition. The year 2012 holds significant importance for American virtual hosting providers, so naturally, merchants will not easily miss this favorable promotional opportunity. Among many American hosting providers, GoDaddy and HostEase have played leading roles, pushing the New Year's virtual hosting promotions to their peak.
