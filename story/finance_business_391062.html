Showing off silver with YUKI: Modern Silver Accessories and Girls

When YUKI, dressed in a black and white Armani velvet suit, sporting an ultra-short fluffy black hairstyle, sipping white wine at a trendy bar in the Bund Center in Shanghai, she was wearing that Tiffany silver necklace around her neck. This exaggerated-style necklace, with its string of large and small silver beads catching the eye, didn't fall like pearls onto a jade plate but was strung together into a chain. This necklace is a seductive little thing that can instantly capture your attention. A few days ago, while listening to a jazz concert at the Sheraton Cigar Bar, music-loving YUKI wore this silver necklace again, which under the dim yellow lights and the accompaniment of jazz music, took on a different kind of elegant charm.

Silver, mysterious and warm, is filled with magical meaning. It's polished bright without being ostentatious or flashy. Although silver no longer possesses the exorbitant value it once had in medieval times, surpassing even gold, its mature, elegant, and unrestrained style has firmly secured its place in fashion pairings. In the warm autumn, silver accessories evoke a sense of good feeling.

Simple and lively YUKI is a fashionable Aquarius girl who has an intuitive fondness for silver. In her view, silver accessories have a mysterious tension, and when carefully paired, they exude a unique aura: transcendent, unbridled, indifferent, disdainful... Amidst such fondness, YUKI gradually amassed nearly a hundred various styles of silver accessories. Not long ago, at Charles Bar, YUKI even held a private exhibition of her cherished silver accessories, and she still keeps half of them there for display. Loving silver to the fullest, YUKI went on to become the agent for Iku Silver Shop! This finally resolved her silver accessory complex. YUKI says that the strong malleability of silver determines the versatility of silver accessories, from very cool unique designs to elegant and quiet suits, all can be formed, and silver can be set with gemstones or imitation gemstones and other materials, making the design even more varied. YUKI prefers more exaggerated silver accessories. When asked why, she said it expresses individuality. What kind of individuality? Ha ha, it’s a simple, unrestrained, youthful vibe.

That ring set with a large amber reveals a mysterious charm; the three-piece set of silver accessories in golden, rose gold, and silver colors with a rice grain pattern is YUKI's beloved treasure found in a small shop in Seoul, costing over a thousand RMB; the silver ring engraved with "I love you" is a small Valentine's Day gift; the leaf-shaped necklace is most suitable for wearing in autumn...

YUKI also told me a little secret for maintaining silver accessories: before wearing silver jewelry, apply a layer of transparent nail polish to prevent oxidation.

Wearing Tips:

Professional attire suits pairing with simple, delicate pure silver necklaces; casual wear pairs well with uniquely designed, exaggerated pure silver rings; Kawaii costumes are perfectly complemented by a silver bracelet with small bells.

Silver accessories embedded with colored artificial gems (excluding black) are suitable for young girls. A medium-length silver chain set with red coral or blue turquoise looks great over a dark sweater and can be paired with matching bracelets and earrings, adding a leisurely feel. Silver-blue porcelain surface bracelets are elegant and versatile, belonging to the classic easy-to-match category. Silver necklaces, earrings, and rings set with garnets, amethysts, black zircons, or green turquoises exude a mature womanly charm. Tibetan silver has a strong personal color and isn't recommended for professional wear as it may give an impression of being less professional or polished.

It's not advisable to mix silver accessories with gold jewelry due to differing aesthetics, but different styles of silver accessories can be worn together to present a rich texture.

When silver accessories turn old and blackened, it's not necessary to clean them thoroughly before continuing to wear them; the vintage look is also excellent. Silver accessories are fashionable items, so it's important to frequently change the styles worn. Don't wear silver accessories with obvious trends past their season. The price of silver accessories isn't determined by weight; they can be very affordable or extremely expensive, depending entirely on the design and craftsmanship. Therefore, the only criterion for choosing silver accessories is whether you love them or not.
