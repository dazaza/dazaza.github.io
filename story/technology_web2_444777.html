News Background

According to relevant media reports, the number of people in Germany who died after being infected with hemorrhagic E. coli (EHEC) due to eating toxic cucumbers increased from six to ten. This news immediately drew attention from various countries. After bacterial culture by Hamburg medical laboratories confirmed that cucumbers imported from Spain were virus carriers, the Spanish Ministry of Health announced an investigation but did not rule out the possibility of cucumber contamination in Germany. Besides Germany, other European countries, including Sweden, Denmark, the Netherlands, and the UK, also reported suspected cases, with about 300 suspected cases in the past week.

Many varieties of fruit cucumbers introduced into our country do not come from Germany or Spain.

Many people in Nanjing wonder how such small cucumbers could cause so many deaths. Some citizens ask whether the cucumbers they buy regularly might also have problems. Mao Jiugen said during a press interview yesterday that most of the cucumbers on the market are domestic varieties, like Qingfeng brand selected Tangshan autumn cucumbers, Hebei's Spring-Autumn Green Jade, Shandong's New Four, and Tianyan No.4, all of which are large cucumbers sold at farmers' markets. However, the problem in Germany involves small cucumbers, or the fruit cucumbers sold in fruit stores and supermarkets. Director Mao Jiugen mentioned that these fruit cucumbers are indeed mainly imported. In recent years, fruit cucumbers have become popular among citizens, and our country has indeed introduced some foreign varieties of fruit cucumbers. However, currently, there are only varieties from approximately three countries: Japan, the Netherlands, and Israel. There are absolutely no varieties from Germany or Spain.
