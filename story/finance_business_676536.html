Whitening: Nine Key Points

by wswen156 on 2012-02-26 10:15:41

Whitening is a permanent topic for the ladies. They whiten every year, but get dark every year. Where is the problem? Is there something we haven't done or haven't done well? Read carefully today's article brought by the editor, and you may find out why your whitening has failed. Every year, the methods of whitening are various, but they all boil down to preventing ultraviolet rays from outside and resisting melanin from inside. This year's whitening theme also advocates returning like fashion trends, reiterating the whitening effect of vitamin C. After last year's wave of analyzing ultraviolet rays and developing new products, businessmen found that it was better to use them solidly than to develop them continuously, so various kinds of vitamin C emerged accordingly.

The main function of vitamin C is to inhibit the production of melanin and prevent skin damage caused by pollution and ultraviolet rays, thereby making the skin healthy and white. Most of the products launched by major skincare brands adopt plant-derived vitamin C, which is obviously beneficial and safer when directly applied to the skin. Moreover, vitamin C extracted from plants is more stable, which helps to maintain its efficacy on the skin for a long time. Some products claim that pure vitamin C is the most effective, and use capsule technology to apply concentrated vitamin C liquid for whitening.

Another feature of this year's whitening products is that many famous brands have launched a complete set of skin whitening care products. From makeup removal, cleaning, hydration, moisturizing, sun protection, deep repair to night-time nourishment, they are all provided in series, aiming to achieve an interlocking effect and carry out a lasting and comprehensive whitening project.

Speaking of "all-round", people now increasingly realize that only when the body and mind are in harmony can the skin reach true health and whiteness from the inside out. In this regard, COSMO has the following suggestions:

1. Sunscreen is the first priority. Prevent ultraviolet rays from damaging the skin and inhibit melanin.

2. Whitening work should be started early. Don't wait until summer to take measures.

3. The intake of vitamins is crucial for the skin. Eat more fruits and vegetables to replenish the skin's vitality.

4. Try the results of new technology research and development, but choose reputable brands and pay attention to testing before use.

5. Pay attention to maintaining air quality in the environment.

6. Whitening should be taken care of both day and night. What kind of eye cream is good for removing dark circles?

7. Drink more water, ensure sleep, and exercise moderately - we don't need to tell you that, right?

8. Keep a happy mood.

9. Firmly believe in the belief of getting fair skin. We cannot underestimate the power of intention.
