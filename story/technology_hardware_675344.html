This system has already been applied on other B2C platforms like Taobao. It might create a stunning effect when applied to the group-buying industry. Simply put, a group-buying site launches a service, participants enjoy the service and evaluate it, which is then published on the site, allowing more consumers to correctly choose group buying. The key to implementing this method is to let major group-buying sites, like "Taobao sellers," have a credit score and conduct sales based on this credit score.
