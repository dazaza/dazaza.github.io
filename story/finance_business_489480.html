◆ Current Status of Underground Parking Lot Lighting Control

Parking lots are places in cities where vehicles are centrally parked. Currently, with the rapid increase in urban populations worldwide and the swift rise in vehicle ownership, more and more city parking lots are developing into underground garages. Lighting energy consumption for parking lots constitutes a major part of the operating costs of underground parking lots, and lighting control is an effective solution method.

Parking lots that use traditional lighting control mainly adopt energy-saving lamps to reduce energy consumption. Energy-saving light sources only lower energy consumption during use and do not achieve the maximum energy-saving effect —— using them only when necessary.

A few parking lots use intelligent lighting control systems, which can achieve regional control, timed control, and other effects, but there are many issues regarding operational complexity and compatibility with security systems. How to achieve both energy savings and effective management is one of the problems faced by many companies managing parking lots.

◆ Parking Lot LED Energy-saving Lighting Control Solution

Using infrared sensors to control each LED lighting fixture in the parking lot, when there are no vehicles entering or exiting, all fixtures are in standby mode, with each fixture consuming 2W of power, satisfying the requirements for security surveillance lighting while greatly saving electricity.

When vehicles enter or exit, the corresponding area's infrared sensor sends a signal, waking up the standby fixtures to turn them on, with a power output of 16W, achieving the brightness of a 40W regular fluorescent lamp, making it convenient for drivers to park. When vehicles or people are active within the sensing area, the LED lights remain constantly lit. Approximately 30 seconds (adjustable) after the vehicle or person leaves the sensing area, the LED lights automatically re-enter standby mode, with power consumption of 2W, providing lighting for the underground garage.
