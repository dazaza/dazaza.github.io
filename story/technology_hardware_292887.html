(4) Before you kiss me —— The rich boy fell in love with me, a young widow. I didn't expect to have made a wrong judgment; the following week remained peaceful. Supervisor Liang still assigned tasks to me like before, and the general manager, upon seeing me, still treated me as if I were invisible. I calmed down slightly and therefore worked even harder.

At Giant Star Advertising Co., Ltd., I was just an ordinary planning staff member. Supervisor Liang had only joined the company two months earlier than me, but her promotion was rapid. In four years, she went from being a junior clerk to supervisor, while my position did not change at all except for gaining some seniority.

However, I was the "popular one" in the entire company.

Because my name was called most frequently. Supervisor Liang could assign tasks to me at will, including not only work-related matters but also some of her personal errands, such as buying fashion magazines, purchasing coffee, and picking up or dropping off her relatives and friends at the airport or train station. Because I came at her beck and call, I became a laughingstock in the eyes of my other office colleagues.

But I had no choice; I couldn't refuse these extra tasks because I cherished this job that paid over 3000 yuan per month. In Haifeng City, this income was neither high nor low. Now, with the economic crisis, many younger college graduates are eagerly waiting for vacancies to fill. As part of the "older" working class, I can see that my youth is fading away. With no background or connections, I can only struggle to keep this position.

I often consoled myself by thinking that leaders would only ask their confidants to do private things, and I refused to believe that she was simply bullying honest people. My "Ah Q spirit" in the workplace was thoroughly implemented.
