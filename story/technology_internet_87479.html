Hefei Baoduo website makes a strong landing in Hefei, and is committed to building a Taobao website exclusively for the people of Hefei!

by joepue on 2008-08-27 10:53:12

With the rapid development of computer and electronic communication technology and the increasingly widespread application of networks, many people across the country have spontaneously realized that using the network for entrepreneurship and doing business by opening online stores has become their first choice. They have successively established their own marketing platforms online, while various activities such as online exhibitions, online auctions, online information releases, and online recruitment have also moved onto the network, showcasing the powerful media advantages of the internet. Promoting one's products through a website, establishing the image of one’s store, and increasing the store's market visibility is also currently the main promotional method for most online shops. Compared with traditional promotional methods, it has obvious effects of less investment and greater returns.

Under this major development trend, Hefei Baoduo Network has strongly landed in Hefei with a brand new image, dedicated to creating Hefei's own Taobao.com.

Different from Taobao.com, Hefei Baoduo Network has its own characteristics. It has launched the Baoduo Union, perfectly combining physical stores with online shops. A series of local transaction security measures have been introduced to give you peace of mind when shopping.

Online window shopping fully embodies your shopping pleasure. The online map allows you to click wherever you want to go, helping you quickly and accurately find the physical stores you trust and like on the internet, so you can shop for your favorite items more confidently.

The launch of Tongcheng Information enables you to understand the market changes in Hefei faster and more comprehensively, grasp more useful messages for yourself, and make corresponding policies. This allows you to always stay ahead of others and win the favor of more customers.

The introduction of the Baoduo Community provides you with more space to communicate with customers and merchants. Discussing experiences with each other and sharing happiness and sorrow with others. Heart-to-heart conversations with customers, understanding customer needs, interaction between merchants and customers, exchanging information, and understanding each other's dynamics will give you a valuable life treasure.

Hefei Baoduo Network is committed to the idea of Hefei shopping, local transactions, and enjoying Baoduo, wholeheartedly providing better services for the broad masses of people in Hefei!
