Fight Dryness

Members: Those with dry skin, those who never pay attention to neck care

The number of sebaceous glands and sweat glands on the neck is only one-third of that on the face. With less oil secretion, it's easier for the neck to become dry. Moreover, its self-repairing ability is relatively weaker than other parts of the skin. A moment of carelessness can immediately reveal dryness.

Use moisturizing neck products every morning and evening

Skincare products containing shea butter and glycerin can increase the moisture and elasticity of the skin, making them the most effective water-locking ingredients. You can also try using natural olive oil or avocado oil, applying it directly on your neck, which is especially effective for people with particularly dry skin. Before applying the product, rub it in your palms until warm, then gently apply with the middle part of your fingers using an upward motion from the bottom.

Sun exposure causes a large loss of moisture in the neck

While you're in a hurry to take off your high-necked sweater, don't forget that neck sun protection is the first step in preventing dryness. When applying sunscreen, besides the front of the neck, the sides, back, and clavicle areas should also be taken care of, as these are places easily burned by the sun. If you find these positions difficult to apply, it's recommended to use spray-on sunscreen.

GOOD Tip:

The cleaning process for neck skin is the same as for the face. First, wash with a mild facial cleanser, then pat on alcohol-free toner. You can also use facial cream on your neck, but remember that neck skin is thinner than the face, so it's unsuitable to use overly greasy products.

Combat Dullness and Spots

Members: Those who love wearing high-necked shirts and spraying perfume on their necks

Who made your face and neck two different colors? Besides sun exposure, high-necked fashion items, scarves often cause friction with the neck, making it look dull and rough. Wearing necklaces, spraying perfume on the neck, or skin sensitivity caused by wool clothing might also lead to skin inflammation, even forming dark spots on the neck.

Regularly remove old dead skin from the neck

Choose a gentle exfoliating product and massage in a spiral motion from bottom to top. The area near the ears on the neck should also be thoroughly cleaned, then rinse with clear water. Once or twice a week, you'll find that the dull neck skin gradually gains luster!

Gentle whitening ingredients

Besides choosing neck creams with whitening effects, facial whitening essences and creams can also be used on the neck. However, since neck skin is particularly delicate, the concentration and gentleness of whitening ingredients in the product are very important. For example, hydroquinone is currently the only ingredient recognized by the U.S. Food and Drug Administration (FDA) for fading dark spots, but when used on the neck, its dosage should be between 2%-4%. It's advisable to carefully read the product ingredients before use and consult beauty advisors more often.

GOOD Tip:

A slow metabolism in the body can also make neck skin look dull! During a shower when your body temperature rises, gently rubbing the neck skin with a towel can accelerate blood circulation in the neck, promoting metabolism.

Battle Slackness

Members: Aging, lack of exercise

With age, collagen in the body begins to gradually decrease. Combined with fewer fats in the neck, the elasticity of the skin will slowly decline, eventually leading to signs of sagging and looseness. Additionally, if you've long lacked exercise or experienced weight fluctuations due to dieting, neck slackness is inevitable.

Choose skincare products specifically designed to increase collagen in neck skin to maintain elasticity

Many brands have specially formulated creams aimed at tightening neck lines. Their ingredients can accelerate the renewal of fibroblast cells in the deeper layers of the skin, achieving the effect of lifting neck skin. It's important to remind you to use it daily, as it takes some time before results are visible.

GOOD Tip:

For such a fragile area as the neck, massage must be gentle and moderate; otherwise, the skin may become even looser.

Anti-Wrinkle Campaign

Members: Those who frequently work with their heads down, those who hold phones between their necks

Neck wrinkles can be divided into two major categories: aging wrinkles and expression wrinkles. The formation of aging wrinkles is mainly due to sun exposure and aging, where collagen in the skin gradually decreases. Expression wrinkles, on the other hand, are caused by small actions in our daily lives, such as frequently working with heads down, holding the phone between the neck, or sleeping with too high a pillow.

Apply neck mask before sleep

Before going to bed, apply an appropriate amount of neck cream or massage balm to your neck and gently massage for about 5 minutes, which helps reduce neck wrinkles. Applying a neck mask before sleep is also highly beneficial. The essence in the neck mask can penetrate deep into the skin, achieving moisturizing, soothing, and anti-wrinkle effects. You can also use a face mask or mask cloth instead, but remember that neck skin is softer, so avoid using peel-off neck masks, as they may loosen the skin further.

Stay away from these bad habits

Stop indulging in soft high pillows, as they can worsen your neck wrinkles. It's best to choose slightly harder pillows with a height of around 8 centimeters. No matter how busy you are at work, remember to stretch your neck upwards frequently and avoid the bad habit of holding the phone between your neck.

If your neck wrinkles are already severe, consider non-surgical facelifts like ACR. This non-surgical treatment does not cause damage, and substances derived from autologous blood will not produce rejection reactions within the body, but only regular medical institutions can ensure effectiveness.

In this season where we expose more of our bodies, quickly join the "Swan" operation, do a good job of neck care, and defend the last line of youth!
