My heart aches so much.

by lili1 on 2012-03-09 16:02:01

My heart aches so much

All along

I thought I had found my happiness

I was the happiest woman

I would steal a smile just thinking about him

I couldn't sleep when he wasn't by my side

But today I've come to know

He's still thinking of her, still loving her

He's still calling out her name

He's still writing her love poems

He's still getting drunk for her

Do you know

When you were drunk, you told me that she treated you best

You miss her so much

Watching you call out her name in tears

Watching you cry out her name so desperately

As your wife, I comforted you while my heart bled

Do you know how much my bleeding heart hurts? So much, so much

God

Why do I only know now

Why must I know

Why must I know now

From now on, we both have her in our hearts

From now on, happiness no longer belongs to me

From now on, bliss has left me

From now on, my sky has changed color

From now on, my smile has vanished

From now on...

My heart understands and aches so much

I've heard that when a heart bleeds dry, it turns to stone

And then it won't hurt anymore. Is that true?

Then let it flow quickly, faster, faster

I really don't want to hurt anymore, I've hurt enough

I really have nothing left

There's really nothing left

Gone...
