(1) How to provide first aid in case of liquefied gas fire?

1. Use the gas-off fire extinguishing method. That is: if the stove catches fire, turn off the angle valve first; if there is fire on the angle valve, you can quickly cover the steel cylinder guardrail with wet towels, cloths, medical skirts, etc., and then close the angle valve.

2. Fire extinguishing method before cutting off gas. You can cover the flame with a wet cloth or apron, or use a dry powder to forcefully hit the root of the flame to make it go out, and then quickly close the angle valve.

3. Comprehensive fire extinguishing method. If the fire has ignited some furniture and clothes, while using water or fire extinguishers to put out the fire, quickly cover the angle valve with a wet towel and transfer the steel cylinder to prevent it from exploding due to heat.

4. Cooling and transfer method. When the room is full of smoke, the fire is large, and visibility is poor, you need to fight the fire while looking for the steel cylinder, put out the fire on the steel cylinder, close the valve, cool down the steel cylinder, and transfer it to a safe place. Under no circumstances should you transfer it while it's leaking gas.

(2) What to do when an oil pan catches fire?

When an oil pan catches fire, do not pour water to extinguish the fire. Instead, close the gas valve of the stove, or directly cover the pot with a lid or a wet cloth to suffocate the fire; you can also put cut vegetables into the pot to cool down and extinguish the fire.
