Talk of all kinds of women

by xiaomyan2004 on 2012-03-03 23:55:33

A smart and charming woman does not necessarily mean that you are very pretty. But a beautiful woman does not necessarily mean that you are smart and charming. Most men know how to appreciate women with wisdom and charm, because there is always a special quality in them. A few men only know how to appreciate women with beautiful faces. Admit it or not, beautiful women are like flowers, but that is just a fleeting beauty, which will wither over time. Of course, decent women are not necessarily pretty, and pretty women are not necessarily decent. Therefore, women also have the distinction of being proper or improper.

The famous "noble rogue" Hong Huang once said: "There are two kinds of women, fake proper and fake improper, fake proper women are annoying, while fake improper women are lovable." This insightfully points out the different attitudes of men and women in love. Women think that being reserved can create a unique charm, but they don't realize that men are straightforward creatures, too many detours will only make him confused, and even want to escape.

Who makes women pretend to be proper

Clearly wanting to go on a date, yet covering one's face half-heartedly; clearly wanting to be intimate, yet half-resisting and half-consenting. I don't know when it started, but women have learned to pretend to be proper, forcibly suppressing their inner desires, having to show another side in front of others, not daring to be clear and open, instead pretending to be aloof and reserved, presenting an overly proper attitude.

Women who pretend to be proper have many classic performances, for example, when someone tells dirty jokes, a woman who pretends to be proper, in order to maintain her proper demeanor, will pretend not to understand and act innocent; when a man invites her out for a trip and is late, she will make excuses and pretend to be very busy, as if handling myriad affairs and could barely find time to go on a date; at the end of the date, she constantly pays attention to the time, showing that she is a good girl who goes home on time, which only makes the man feel very disappointed.

In fact, women pretending to be proper are afraid. If they are not proper, they would be called cheap by men, behaving like the unfaithful Yang Huayuan. Women who are not proper give people a wrong impression of being frivolous, as if their behavior is not self-disciplined enough, and their actions lack consideration. Thus, women dare not love even though they want to, and dare not be intimate even though they want to, only maintaining a proper appearance cautiously. In order to give men a good impression, women do not hesitate to suppress their nature and pretend to be proper.
