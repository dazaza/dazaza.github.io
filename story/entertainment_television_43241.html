Watch the 13th episode of "Wild Grandma vs Gossip Queen"

**Watch "Barbaric Grandma vs. Sister Ge" Episode 13**

**Episode 10 Summary**

Fan caused a big commotion at the restaurant, making Ma lose interest in Fan. However, Bi not only wasn't disheartened but also arranged for Fan to give an exclusive interview. Bi told Fan that this was the last chance to find out the whereabouts of "Fiona," and after hearing this, Fan decided to abide by his agreement with Bi. When everyone returned from Shanghai, Min was nowhere to be found. It turned out Min had forcibly dragged Xiang along to teach her how to dress up, and she indeed looked stunningly transformed. Min learned that Bao had a secret crush on Xiang but hesitated due to rumors that Xiang was gay. Min investigated and discovered that the rumors actually originated from Bao herself and also found out that Xiang had experienced a failed marriage and had twin daughters. Bi suddenly realized that changing Fan wasn't necessary and managed to make him famous through the exclusive interview. In "Barbaric Grandma vs. Sister Ge" Episode 13, Fan begged Bi to reveal the whereabouts of "Fiona." Bi lied, saying "Fiona" had already passed away, leaving Fan feeling like the sky had fallen.

**Episode 11 Summary**

After being enlightened by Bi, Fan decided to start anew and asked Te for help in finding a new place to live. Min doubted the death of "Fiona" and questioned Bi directly. Bi ordered Min not to meddle and secretly bought back the old photo taken with Tim from a photo studio. Min promised Xiang to take the twin daughters out to play, aiming to let Bao get closer to them. But Bao was stung by a bee and ended up in the hospital. Coincidentally, Xiang's shooting project had an issue with the male model, and upon receiving news of Bao and the twin daughters being hospitalized, he abandoned his work and rushed to the hospital. Min randomly found a handsome man named Mark on the street to substitute. Bi criticized everyone for neglecting their duties for personal reasons and privately urged Bao to choose her career over love. In "Barbaric Grandma vs. Sister Ge" Episode 13, Mark suddenly appeared at the magazine office and flirted with Min. Just then, the big boss Ma showed up and revealed that Mark was actually his biological son...

**Episode 12 Summary**

At the meeting, Ma announced that Mark would take over "Elva," causing great distress for Bi and her son. In "Barbaric Grandma vs. Sister Ge" Episode 13a/b, Bao finally learned that Bi was "Fiona" and demanded that Bi promote her to the editor-in-chief of "Elva." However, Bi angrily criticized Bao for being unqualified, which made Bao harbor resentment and decide to take revenge. Bao secretly let Min know that Bi was "Fiona," and despite Xin trying hard to stop Min from confronting Bi, Min felt increasingly uneasy when she saw Bi become a vice-chairman candidate for the Women's Association. On the day of the Women's Association gala, Min kept feeling that Bi was hypocritical and drank to suppress her anger. Suddenly, Fan appeared, but Bi harshly criticized and tried to drive Fan away. Min couldn't hold back any longer and revealed Bi's identity to Fan, causing Fan to furiously rush into the gala and expose Bi's past. Enraged, Bi slapped Min in front of everyone and stormed off...

**Episode 13 Summary**

After Bi's past was exposed, Te became upset that his mother had deceived him. Bi explained that it was actually Bao's sister who was the third party in the marriage. In the end, Te, overwhelmed with anger, left home. Ma used this opportunity to promote Mark as the publisher of "Elva" to restrain Bi. Mark proposed reforms, which Bi strongly resisted, but Min supported Mark at every turn. Eventually, Bi reluctantly agreed to allow Mark to launch another magazine called "ElvaGreen" and assigned Min and half of the colleagues to work for the new magazine. Qiao comforted Te and successfully persuaded him to return home. "Barbaric Grandma vs. Sister Ge" Episode 13 - Min discovered that Bi had stolen her idea of giving away free books and immediately confronted Bi. Te once again became the focus of conflict between the two, so he sought comfort from Qiao. Qiao took the chance to persuade Te to work for his investment company, which intrigued Te. "ElvaGreen" was successfully launched, but the mother-in-law and daughter-in-law quarreled again. Unable to bear it, Te publicly resigned from his job to Bi...
