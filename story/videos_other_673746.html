One morning, Mr. Zhu, the general manager of the mobile company, suddenly felt the urge to go to the bathroom while outside, so he had to look for a public restroom.

"What are you doing?" The old man guarding the toilet shouted.

"I'm the general manager of the mobile company, I urgently need to use the restroom," Mr. Zhu replied.

"You don't know everything costs money these days?" The old man said calmly.

"Alright, how much?" Mr. Zhu asked anxiously.

"Five mao to enter, three mao to exit," the old man slowly said as he looked at Mr. Zhu.

"What! You also charge when exiting?" Mr. Zhu stared wide-eyed.

"Why are you staring? We're learning from the mobile company and implementing two-way charging. If you buy a toilet package, you can pay one way," the old man stood up and said loudly.

"Alright, I'll pay." Mr. Zhu took out ten yuan.

"Are you pooping or peeing?" The old man pinched the money and asked further.

"Pooing, hurry up," Mr. Zhu said impatiently.

"Hmm, don't rush. Do you want to purchase a package? If you poop fifty times at once, we can give you a discount for another thirty poops," the old man said seriously.

"Don't talk anymore, I'm going in first and will pay later," Mr. Zhu, the boss, went inside and chose the last stall, enjoying himself for quite a while before coming out.

"Sir, you chose stall number five, which costs five mao for the number selection fee. You released a fart, so you need to pay one yuan for roaming fees. Exceeding three minutes requires an additional one yuan for overtime fees. The restroom has background music. Since you didn't say no to playing music while you were inside, we played soothing music for you to forget the distress and annoyance caused by your urgency. Therefore, each song costs six mao. Additionally, you squatted for fifteen minutes and one second. The first minute is charged at fifty mao per minute, and the rest is charged at forty mao per minute. Any fraction of a minute is counted as a full minute. Also, due to your excretion occupying our sewer bandwidth, please pay an additional monthly fee of fifty yuan. Lastly, you can see other people entering the restroom through the small hole, so please pay one yuan for the display of incoming people."

The boss was furious: "Which law allows this?"

The old man waved his hand: "I make the rules on my territory!"

Mr. Zhu, the boss, was already standing there dumbfounded like a wooden chicken. The old man continued relentlessly: "Therefore, sir, we don't accept cards here. In total, you need to pay fifty-nine yuan and four mao. If overdue payment occurs, a penalty of three per thousand per day will be applied. We won't notify separately. Once accumulated to a thousand yuan, we will collect through legal means. For any questions, the final interpretation right belongs to the Residents' Committee of No. 250 Doorplate, 250th Street, Keng Ni City! Or log in: beat you six points pit boss one point is one point, if not beat you boss one point I am not your grandpa ah hahaha!"

Just as the old man finished speaking, his voice hadn't fully faded when Mr. Zhu, the boss, fell unconscious into the urinal...
