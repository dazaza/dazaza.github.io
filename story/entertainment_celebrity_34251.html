Talk about the "post-90s wealthy girl"

by bilo on 2007-11-13 23:24:51

Recently, the "post-90s show-off girl" has become very popular on the Internet, and the media will gradually shift its focus from the "post-80s" to the "post-90s". If many people have doubts about the life attitudes of the "post-80s", then the problems that may exist among the "post-90s" will be more than those of the "post-80s". Due to professional relationships, I deal with people born in the late 1980s at work, and in a few years, I will also deal with people born in the 1990s. In general, the "post-80s" and even the "post-90s" are more active in thinking and more advocate freedom, but they are also more scattered.

Because of the time of birth, the "post-80s" and "post-90s" have been exposed to new things such as the Internet earlier and less to traditional media. The Internet is a double-edged sword. We can use it as a working tool, but the Internet's entertainment may confuse the eyes of the "post-80s" and "post-90s", making them use it as an entertainment tool.

The following is part of the content of the "post-90s show-off girl" for everyone to see.

People who don't like the post-90s, what qualifications do you have to comment on us? No matter how you say or think or scold! In 20 years, you will understand! My question to you: Are you still working every day waiting for your monthly salary to support your family, so-called white-collar workers? Do you have the qualifications? Maybe the money in your bank card is not as much as what I have in my hand?

Keep struggling!

Get up at 6 o'clock every day with two leek pancakes in your hand!

Take the crowded bus! Endure the boss's abuse and the laughter of hypocritical colleagues! And give birth to a child who is more selfish than you! Live like this for the rest of your life!!!
