Baoman Paisley has received a flood of good reviews, and franchisees are signing up in an unbroken stream.

by tensteps0 on 2012-02-08 13:03:57

As we all know, Paisley patterns have always been exclusively owned by Bama Home Textiles, a well-known brand in the home textile industry. Now, Luohai has also launched its own Paisley-related products, which undoubtedly shows its intention to get a share of the Paisley product market from Bama Home Textiles. However, under such great competitive pressure, Bama Home Textiles still insists on following its own path and continues to launch new spring and summer Paisley products under such circumstances.

The new product Perajie in Bama Home Textiles' Spring and Summer Como series promotion has received a strong response in the market since its release, with demand far exceeding supply. The creative concept of the Perajie product comes from the Perajie Islands in Italy. In terms of pattern, this product uses high-density satin printed fabric, which has good luster and rarely seen in the industry with more than 13 color applications. It features the classic Paisley pattern combined with rose flowers, expressing the joy of being in a passionate romance through intricate Paisley patterns surrounding the gorgeous roses. In terms of color, it uses the collision of coral pink and gemstone blue, creating bright and bold colors. Precisely because of its perfect balance in color, pattern, and creativity, this product is not picky about home decoration styles. Whether it's modern minimalist, European style, or neo-Chinese style, it can present a unique feeling.

It is precisely because of Bama Home Textiles' continuous persistence and efforts over the years that it has gained the status of the progenitor of Paisley, and even when competing with giants like Luohai, it does not fall short. This can be seen from the recruitment meeting held by Bama Home Textiles at the end of March. Despite the short time, the recruitment meeting achieved a success rate far beyond the plan. Success and failure are both due to circumstances. This Paisley competition not only did not have an adverse effect on Bama Home Textiles but also led to a sharp increase in its franchisees. From this, we can see that as long as one focuses on oneself and keeps striving, there is no opponent that cannot be defeated and no obstacle that cannot be overcome.
