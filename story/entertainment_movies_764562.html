Synopsis:

In 1928 Los Angeles, Christine Collins (Angelina Jolie), who works at the telephone exchange, raises her son Walter (Gattlin Griffith) alone, living a simple and happy life. However, their lives are soon disrupted when one day after work, Christine discovers that her son is missing.

Under public pressure, the corrupt LAPD claims to have found Walter five months later. But Christine realizes this boy is not her real son. She repeatedly demands the police clarify the situation and help her find the real Walter, but she faces obstruction and accusations, even being committed to a mental institution for obstructing police business. Reverend Gustav Briegleb (John Malkovich), who exposes police misconduct, fights for this unfortunate woman.

On the other hand, Detective Lester (Michael Kelly) stumbles upon a major child serial murder case while investigating a smuggling case. It seems the mystery of Walter's disappearance is beginning to unravel...
