This 39-year-old man is seen as a "successful man" by his friends. He owns a shop in the urban area of Yantai, and his family is very happy and harmonious. However, he was sentenced to 10 years and 5 months in prison and fined 20,000 yuan by the court for helping his friend sell drugs, even though he didn't take any money. This is the information obtained recently by the reporter from the Zhifu District Procuratorate.

Recently, a drug user was arrested at the entrance of a hotel by the Yantai police. During routine interrogation, Liu Mou confessed a lead on a major drug trafficking case - 50 grams of methamphetamine were going to be traded at the Civil Aviation Building at noon on that day.

Liu Mou said that a fellow countryman from Northeast China had contacted him half a month ago, asking him to help find a buyer who intended to sell methamphetamine in Yantai. The other party had already arrived in Yantai yesterday with 50 grams of drugs. Through his coordination, Zhang Mou, the seller, and Gao Mou, the buyer, agreed to conduct the transaction at around noon on July 16th at the Civil Aviation Building.

The police immediately set up surveillance at the Civil Aviation Building, booked a room, and let Liu Mou call both parties to come for the drug transaction. At around noon, Zhang Mou and Gao Mou arrived one after another at the trading location, where the officers moved forward to arrest both parties and found a cigarette box containing 50 grams of methamphetamine prepared for the transaction on Zhang Mou's body. It was understood that these 50 grams of methamphetamine were planned to be traded at a price of 28,000 yuan.

Unexpectedly, Zhang Mou, who was caught, was not the seller but rather the intermediary responsible for connecting both parties. The real seller was waiting outside the Civil Aviation Building for Zhang Mou to return after the transaction. The officers immediately searched around the Civil Aviation Building and eventually arrested the seller who came from Northeast China.

Zhang Mou said that he and the seller were good friends. In early July, the other party suddenly called him, asking if it would be possible to sell methamphetamine in Yantai. Zhang Mou helped contact Liu Mou, who then found the buyer Gao Mou.

Because of their friendship, Zhang Mou did not ask for any benefits and even told the seller not to give him any money, just giving Liu Mou a little benefit fee would suffice. Both Zhang Mou's and the seller's confessions confirmed this fact.

The prosecutor handling the case at the Zhifu District Procuratorate said that Zhang Mou, aged 39, was seen as a successful man by his friends. He was in the ascending phase of his career, owning a decent shop in the city, and his family was also very happy and harmonious.

Even though he took no money, he was still sentenced to 10 years.

After investigation, the police confirmed that Liu Mou had once gathered a crowd to fight in his hometown in Northeast China, seriously injuring someone. Gao Mou had previously sold drugs multiple times in the Zhifu District.

The prosecutor handling the case said that although Zhang Mou did not gain any benefits from it, he personally participated in drug trafficking and provided communication between the buyer and seller. They still prosecuted Zhang Mou for the crime of drug trafficking.

The court finally sentenced Zhang Mou, known for his "brotherhood," to 10 years and 5 months in prison and fined him 20,000 yuan for drug trafficking; sentenced Liu Mou to 14 years in prison and fined him 20,000 yuan for drug trafficking and intentional injury; since Gao Mou was caught before the 50-gram methamphetamine transaction, he was sentenced to 3 years and 9 months in prison for drug trafficking and fined 10,000 yuan.
