The eight characters determine when your financial luck will come.

by dd26huiuygui on 2011-08-18 10:15:44

Seeing this title, many people will be interested, because money is the source of life for people, and everyone will care about when they will have good financial luck and make a fortune. However, to tell the truth, not everyone can have good financial luck and make a big fortune. First of all, one must look at whether there is prosperous financial information in their先天八字 (predestined eight characters). If so, once they reach a place where wealth prospers, they naturally gain wealth; if not, even if they work themselves to death in this lifetime, they won't become rich. This is what is known as being manipulated by fate, as Master Huitong says. Whether one can have good financial luck is roughly divided into several situations:

One situation is when oneself is strong, and during periods of financial luck, one can obtain wealth. As the saying goes, "When the sun is noble and wealth is prosperous, one does not need to seek others." Such people rely on starting from scratch and enriching themselves through hard work. If further subdivided, that would mean that in the original destiny chart, if the body is strong and wealth is prosperous, one becomes rich quickly; in the original destiny chart, if the body is strong but wealth is weak, there is still wealth to be gained. When encountering a major period of financial luck, if one also encounters an official injury, they can gain great wealth, because food and injuries release energy and generate wealth.

The second situation is when the body is weak but wealth is prosperous, making it difficult to obtain wealth. One must walk the path of companionship to obtain wealth. If the body is weak and then walks the path of official injuries, the wealth in one's destiny will be obtained by others, and encountering prosperous wealth will surely bring misfortune, with more wealth leading to greater calamity.

The third situation is when there is wealth in one's destiny but no treasury, making it difficult to preserve wealth; if there is too much wealth but the treasury is small, the wealth cannot be contained; if there is little wealth but a large treasury, one can become wealthy during periods of financial luck, but will surely fall into ruin during periods of companionship. If the four pillars show a strong body and prosperous wealth with a treasury, one will definitely have savings. If there is no treasury in one's destiny, encountering a treasury year during periods of financial luck will also result in savings. A treasury in the year pillar represents a large treasury, in the month pillar a medium-sized treasury, and in the day or hour pillar a temporary treasury. If the body is weak but there are many treasuries without companionship, one only saves without spending. If wealth enters the treasury and meets an official noble, one's financial virtue will be abundant. If one's destiny contains all four treasuries, they are a very wealthy person.

The fourth situation is when the wealth in one's destiny violates the three punishments, and during periods of financial luck, one may end up in prison due to wealth. If the three punishments meet the魁罡 (Quguang), one may become involved in organized crime. People with a weak body and excessive wealth are generally physically weak and often ill. When a weak body and prosperous wealth encounter a flow year, suffering caused by wealth violating the punishment ensues. When a weak body and prosperous wealth face surprises, they recklessly spend money even when they have it.

The fifth situation is when, in one's eight-character destiny chart, the seal and officials are prosperous, but the daily pillar is too weak and the wealth is not strong. Such people live their entire lives with external debts, unable to repay them. If the wealth in one's destiny falls into emptiness and extinction, the wealth throughout one's life will not be strong. Without encountering raiding wealth, external debts will be less; upon encountering raiding wealth, external debts will increase sharply.

In summary, to see when your financial luck is the strongest, it still needs to be determined based on the prosperity, decline, imprisonment, and death of the five elements: suitable comparison leads to obtaining wealth; suitable generation leads to increasing wealth. When the main element thrives, walking the path of food spirit can lead to wealth, but unexpected windfall should not be sought. During years of strong body and prosperous wealth, one can obtain unexpected wealth. However, here I would like to advise everyone that even if your financial luck year is approaching, careful planning is needed to make the financial luck smoother. Only through hard struggle can wealth be abundant.
