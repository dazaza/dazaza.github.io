Woman pulls neighbor's electricity meter to steal vegetables online

by mingdaolin on 2009-12-04 12:29:13

Zhao Jing (a pseudonym) and Tang Hong (a pseudonym) are neighbors on the same floor. They share a common hobby - being farmers on a certain website's farm, growing vegetables and stealing vegetables. Neither of them went out during the "National Day" long holiday, instead staying at home to be "urban farmers." Unexpectedly, a farce ensued.

They live in the Ma Lan Street Hong Ling Community in Dalian. The two often interacted with each other and were good friends. Zhao Jing had recently learned how to grow vegetables on a certain website's farm at her workplace. She found it interesting whether she grew them herself or stole from others, so she taught this to Tang Hong.

During the "National Day" long holiday, neither of them went on a long-distance trip. Instead, they stayed at home watching over their crops on the computer. Since they didn't have to work and had time to monitor their farms, both of them planted more valuable crops. When they chatted, they would say, "We must not let others steal them, otherwise we will be heartbroken."

On the evenings of October 4th and 5th, every time it was Zhao Jing's turn to harvest her crops, there would always be an inexplicable power outage at her house. The power would come back on within five minutes. During this time, many of Zhao Jing's crops would be stolen by her friends, which made her very angry. On the evening of October 6th, just as Zhao Jing's crops were about to ripen, she wasn't sitting in front of the computer but was instead peeking through the peephole on the door, suspecting that someone was sabotaging her. Sure enough, she saw this scene: Tang Hong walked to the electric meter box and pulled down Zhao Jing's circuit breaker.

At that moment, Zhao Jing became furious and shouted at Tang Hong, "Even if you want to steal my crops, you shouldn't pull down my circuit breaker!" The two began arguing, and neither spoke to each other.

On October 7th, after Zhang Ying, the mediation specialist of the Hong Ling Community, learned about this matter, she came to their homes. Zhang Ying said to them, "Just for this game, you've ruined your relationship as neighbors for so many years. Is it worth it? It's not like you're really winning houses or land."

Zhao Jing said to Tang Hong, "If you do this, what if my electrical appliances break down! If you want to steal my crops, just tell me directly! There's no need to make things so complicated!"

Tang Hong, however, said that she hadn't thought that much. It was just that Zhao Jing was experienced and could always steal her stuff, while she couldn't steal anything from Zhao Jing. In desperation, she came up with this foolish idea, and afterwards, she also felt that it was indeed wrong. "I definitely won't do it again, rest assured!" (Liaoning Daily provided the article.)
