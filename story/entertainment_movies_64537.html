Xiùqīng was very happy to see her son achieve something, but Jīnshí has always been down-to-earth and never liked Yonglai's speculative trading. Yonglai thought Jīnshí favored Rì'ān, which deepened the estrangement between father and son.

In the end, Yonglai was imprisoned, and Jīnshí also committed the crime of giving false testimony and obstructing judicial justice. Xiùqīng resented Jīnshí for not saving their son and angrily left home, demanding a divorce.

Jīnshí lived alone in seclusion, leading a lonely life. Finally, with Rì'ān's help, Jīnshí found Xiùqīng. Xiùqīng realized that spoiling her son was wrong, and they reconciled. In prison, Yonglai reflected and finally admitted his mistakes, apologizing to Jīnshí and Rì'ān with sincere repentance. Jīnshí and Xiùqīng felt comforted and promised to reunite as a family when Yonglai finishes his sentence.
