The prosecution accused her of extortion.

Subsequently, Xiao Li traveled to Beijing several times. She had trysts with Wang Xueming at hotels or his home. On one return journey, she suffered burns on both feet, which caused her to miss the college entrance exam (Gaokao). Because Wang Xueming promised to help her find a school to attend Peking University, Xiao Li unreservedly followed him for two years, but ultimately failed to realize her university dream. In April this year, after failing again in her request to attend university, Xiao Li threatened to harm Wang Xueming's family and demanded 300,000 yuan from him. Two days later, she was arrested by the police brought by her lover. Yesterday, it was learned that Xiao Li was suspected of extortion and had been approved for arrest by the prosecution, entering the review and prosecution stage.

During the investigation, the police discovered more than 20 incriminating text messages between the two, dated from last November to April 21 this year. From the initial "I have caused you a lot of pain," to "There is no turning back now, right?" and finally to "Yesterday you told me, today you would give me a satisfactory answer! What is your answer? Don't play with me like this, my family is already broken," the emotions became increasingly intense, reflecting the trajectory of their emotional breakdown.

Xiao Li: I have never been to Beijing. He said I could listen to his lectures at Peking University and meet capable people around him, so I agreed. After the first trip to Beijing, I fell in love with him. He said he was already divorced and wanted to marry me. I thought he was a Peking University teacher, so he should be cultivated. Whatever he said, I believed.

Xiao Li: Not very familiar, but I believed he wasn't deceiving me.

Reporter: Do you regret knowing this man?

Xiao Li: He treated me pretty well. He said he could help me academically, which was already good enough for me. Although he didn't help me, I still believe he is a good person.

Xiao Li: In our hometown, if you have a relationship with a man, you must marry him. Everyone in our hometown knows I have a boyfriend from Beijing. If I go back like this, I will have no face to show, so I got angry and wanted to ask him for money.

Xiao Li: Go back to Lijiang, I won't come to Beijing again, and I won't contact this man anymore.

During the return journey, Xiao Li was scalded on both feet by hot water on the train. Back in Lijiang, unable to continue reviewing her lessons, she had to stay at home to recover, thus missing the Gaokao that year. Upon learning about this, Wang Xueming sent her 5000 yuan to let her recuperate peacefully. In July, he received a call from Xiao Li, who hoped that he could use his identity as a Peking University professor to help her find a school. Xiao Li recalled that Wang Xueming did not immediately agree; instead, he consoled her to focus on studying at home and prepare for next year's Gaokao. He also promised that even if she missed the score next year, he would definitely find connections to get her into Peking University. At this point, her family had already known about Xiao Li's relationship with Wang Xueming but did not stop them from continuing their interactions.

Xiao Li: No regrets.

"If you don't agree, I'll make you experience the feeling of losing everything." This sentence from Xiao Li made Wang Xueming scared. He proposed to meet her Yi male classmate, which was rejected by Xiao Li. To calm Xiao Li down, Wang Xueming pretended to agree and arranged to eat McDonald's together in the evening. Xiao Li believed it. As soon as she entered McDonald's that night, she was arrested by the police brought by Wang Xueming. It turned out that Wang Xueming reported the incident to the Haidian police that afternoon, claiming that he was threatened by Xiao Li. If he didn't give her 300,000 yuan, he and his family would face personal danger.

In his report, Wang Xueming admitted to having an affair with Xiao Li. He also specifically mentioned that during their relationship, he had provided her with financial assistance multiple times. Xiao Li did not deny this. She stated that from the beginning of their relationship until her arrest, Wang Xueming had given her approximately 20,000 yuan. The reporter noticed that in the testimony, Wang Xueming did not mention Xiao Li's request to attend school. He told the police that Xiao Li repeatedly asked him for money, sometimes claiming she had uterine fibroids, other times saying she wanted to open an inn. Unable to endure it any longer, he decided to report it to the police.

More than 20 text messages revealed the fluctuations in their relationship.

In her confession, Xiao Li stated that the next day, Wang Xueming gave her 200 yuan, and she bought an old mobile phone with it, ecstatic. She came from a rural area in Lijiang, where both her parents were farmers, and she had a younger sister who was also studying. Due to economic difficulties, there was neither a telephone nor a mobile phone in the house. Subsequently, Xiao Li began using this mobile phone to frequently contact Wang Xueming. In her confession, she stated that Wang Xueming often expressed his affection through text messages, and she reciprocated.

A month later, Wang Xueming asked Xiao Li to come to Beijing and wired her 3,000 yuan. After receiving the money, Xiao Li secretly took the train to Beijing and met him at the south gate of Peking University. That night, Wang Xueming took Xiao Li to his home, and they had sexual relations. Since his wife and children were not at home, Wang Xueming let Xiao Li stay at his house for a week before sending her back to Lijiang to continue her studies. In May of that year, while preparing for the Gaokao, Xiao Li received another call from Wang Xueming, hoping she would come to Beijing again. After thinking about it for a moment, she bought a ticket and came to Beijing, staying at Wang Xueming's home and maintaining their illicit relationship.

In Lijiang Ancient City, Peking University professor Wang Xueming (pseudonym) and high school student Xiao Li (pseudonym) met by chance. That night, they checked into a hotel and became lovers, with an age difference of 26 years.

In May last year, as the Gaokao approached again. Xiao Li suddenly received a call from Wang Xueming, saying that she didn't need to take the exam because finding a way to attend Peking University required a large sum of money, which couldn't be done at the moment. Despite some disappointment, Xiao Li once again met Wang Xueming in Beijing upon his request. This time, Wang Xueming took her to Nandaihe and stayed by the sea for a month before returning to Beijing. During this period, they had sexual relations multiple times. According to her confession, when asked if she voluntarily had sex with Wang Xueming, Xiao Li frankly admitted that it was voluntary. She had repeatedly expressed admiration for Wang Xueming to her family and believed that he would certainly help her attend school, allowing her to leave the rural areas of Lijiang and integrate into urban life as an educated person. According to her own account, in frequent communications with Wang Xueming, she most often mentioned attending school. Initially, Wang Xueming patiently consoled her, but later simply said it couldn't be done.

Xu Xilong stated that ultimately, Xiao Li did not receive the 300,000 yuan. Therefore, criminal punishment should not be imposed on her. Currently, the case is under review and prosecution by the prosecution authority.
