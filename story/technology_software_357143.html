Dedicating Love and Light: The Great Ideal Staircase of the Affiliated Ideal Eye Hospital of Suzhou University

In our youth, we all had some wonderful, beautiful, yet somewhat naive and impractical ideals. It is a desirable, shining word that people praise, yearn for, and pursue because it brings hope and strength. The great writer Tolstoy once said: "An ideal is a guiding beacon; without an ideal, there is no firm direction; without direction, there is no life."

Dedicating light, soaring the wings of ideals

A great enterprise must have lofty ideals. A company with grand aspirations will surely have a commendable bright future! From ancient times to today, every successful person has their own story behind them. Chairman Chen Huijun of the Affiliated Ideal Eye Hospital of Suzhou University believes that success lies in ideals and beliefs. Since childhood, he aspired to be a useful person, to be a good person, do good deeds, and contribute love to society. This belief has always influenced his life and work later on. To soar his ideals, he never misses any opportunity to contribute to society.

There is an ancient Chinese saying: "The poor cultivate themselves alone, while the accomplished benefit the whole world." After years of relentless effort, not only has Chen Huijun achieved his own career development, but also made a leap in his personal value.

The establishment of the Affiliated Ideal Eye Hospital of Suzhou University originated from the experiences of Chairman Chen Huijun's friends. Many of his friends endured hardships traveling through numerous hospitals to treat glaucoma, myopia, cataracts... These difficult medical journeys made Chen Huijun acutely aware of the need for advanced equipment and highly technical professional ophthalmology hospitals. Thus, the idea of founding such a hospital was born. This idea quickly received support from Suzhou University, and thus, the first non-profit third-level ophthalmology specialty hospital in Suzhou - the Affiliated Ideal Eye Hospital of Suzhou University, was established by Hong Kong Asia Medical Investment Group and Suzhou University. The hospital is positioned as one of the top ophthalmology hospitals in East China, based in Suzhou and radiating nationwide. It is an international ophthalmology center integrating eye care, research, teaching, health care, and blindness prevention and treatment. It collaborates deeply with Suzhou University in medical technology, discipline construction, talent introduction, teaching and research, aiming to better bring light to eye disease patients across Suzhou and even the whole country.

Dedicating love, ideals and responsibilities after disasters

At 7:49 AM on April 14, 2010, a 7.1-magnitude earthquake struck Yushu in Qinghai. It was the homeland of King Gesar, preserving China's purest, most beautiful, and most precious landscapes and memories. It was the place where many people's dreams began, nurturing many people's ideals. When the strong quake hit, Yushu County suffered severely. The ground cracked significantly during the earthquake, and water infrastructure became badly damaged. Throughout Yushu County, ruins and collapsed buildings were everywhere, and thousands of lives were lost instantly, shattering the ideals of disaster-stricken people.

The world's attention focused on this event. Faced with the helplessness of the elderly, the innocence of the children, and the pain of the injured, what should we do? At this moment, we gradually understood: "A person truly lives not just for ideals, but also for responsibilities. Using one's ideals to bring hope to others, achieving others' ideals, being responsible for oneself, family, and more importantly, society."

New campuses will come! New homes will come!

Rescuing and caring for children must be prioritized in earthquake relief efforts!

Children cannot suffer under hardship, education cannot suffer under poverty.

To quickly restore the national flag in schools and make the sound of reading audible again, the nation is taking action, Qinghai Yushu is taking action, and the Affiliated Ideal Eye Hospital of Suzhou University is taking action...

On April 20, a large-scale fundraising event titled "Emotion Linked to Yushu · Boundless Love - Earthquake Relief Large-Scale Fundraising Activity Special Program" hosted by the Publicity Department of the Central Committee, National Radio and Television Administration, Ministry of Civil Affairs, and Red Cross Society of China, and organized by CCTV, was broadcast live on CCTV. Among the donations, the 1 million yuan donation from the Affiliated Ideal Eye Hospital of Suzhou University was prominently recorded in history, fully supporting the construction of the "Ideal Hope Primary School" project in the disaster area of Yushu, Qinghai.

Chairman Chen Huijun of the Affiliated Ideal Eye Hospital of Suzhou University stated: "After the earthquake in Yushu, Qinghai, the disaster situation touched the hearts of all employees of the soon-to-open Affiliated Ideal Eye Hospital of Suzhou University. The board decided to find ways to aid the disaster area. Since the Affiliated Ideal Eye Hospital of Suzhou University was jointly founded by medical institutions and educational institutions, educating people also bears some responsibility for the Affiliated Ideal Eye Hospital of Suzhou University. Therefore, all preparatory staff of the hospital conceived the idea of allocating 1 million yuan from the hospital's preparatory funds to construct a 'Ideal Hope Primary School' with a school building area of 1000 square meters for the disaster area in Yushu. Although we cannot go to the scene to help those suffering, we can extend our helping hands, bringing them hope and reviving their ideals. This act will inspire more individuals and enterprises to deeply integrate into the vitality, creativity, and cohesion rekindled in this ancient nation, brighter than ten thousand suns!"

What drives us to strive is the ideal, but what must be followed to achieve the ideal is responsibility. His words moved us, making us realize that although having ambitious ideals is important, more crucial is action, resilience in action, and unwavering belief. Also, whether the principle of action is bearing the responsibilities you should undertake; whether ideals and responsibilities are well combined.

This is an era that needs to highlight responsibility, needing a generation of corporate citizens willing to take on responsibilities. Taking on historical responsibilities and contributing to society is not just a stance, but a commitment. As the General Secretary hopes, "Truly strengthen the sense of social responsibility and historical mission, integrating personal growth into the tide of promoting national development and ethnic rejuvenation, striving tirelessly for the realization of lofty ideals."

The Yushu earthquake, in the form of a disaster, reshaped the outlook and values of corporate citizens, catalyzing boundless passion among the soon-to-open Affiliated Ideal Eye Hospital of Suzhou University, elevating its passion for societal contribution amidst the disaster. This passion includes respect for life, love for compatriots, and more so, responsibility to the nation, ethnicity, and society.

Chairman Chen Huijun of the Affiliated Ideal Eye Hospital of Suzhou University expressed: "Harmonious unity between contributing to society and self-development" is the purpose of the Affiliated Ideal Eye Hospital of Suzhou University. Contributing to society includes offering excellent technology, top-notch services, and sincere love. Raising 1 million yuan to build the "Ideal Hope Primary School" project is merely the starting point of the public welfare endeavors of the Affiliated Ideal Eye Hospital of Suzhou University. In the future development process, the Affiliated Ideal Eye Hospital of Suzhou University has the responsibility and obligation to bring light to all eye disease patients, continue to promote the spirit of dedication, track leading ophthalmology technologies at home and abroad, exchange the latest global ophthalmology achievements, and realize the vision of synchronizing Suzhou ophthalmology development with international standards. This enables local residents of Suzhou to enjoy the most cutting-edge ophthalmology medical technology at home and abroad, keeping various eye diseases and visual impairments away, advancing the development of universal blindness prevention and treatment. He also expressed: "Ophthalmology is a noble and beautiful cause that brings light to all humanity. The Affiliated Ideal Eye Hospital of Suzhou University has the confidence and determination to become a flagship in the domestic and even international ophthalmology field, creating an international hospital brand based on dedication."

His words moved us. We firmly believe that whether dedicating light, love, or to society, when the Affiliated Ideal Eye Hospital of Suzhou University is about to open, we will applaud enthusiastically. Together, let's add bricks and tiles to the great ideal staircase of the Affiliated Ideal Eye Hospital of Suzhou University tomorrow.

A harmonious Ideal Eye Hospital will make the eyes of all eye disease patients suddenly shine!

A developing Ideal Eye Hospital will undoubtedly win the trust and admiration of the world!
