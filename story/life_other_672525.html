Funeral of Love

In France, there was once such a love story. There was a boy named Xiaoxiong and a girl named Dandan. They were in the same school in Grade 6. Xiaoxiong was an honest and responsible little boy. Dandan was a gentle and kind girl, but unfortunately, she lost her eyesight due to an accident.

At school, many people didn't play with Dandan and looked down on her, yet Dandan did not feel sad or complain. Only Xiaoxiong played with her because he thought Dandan was a very cute girl who needed protection so that she could be happy.

One day, while Dandan was resting in the classroom, several male classmates came over and scolded her, then threw her books around. Xiaoxiong had just returned from the snack bar, saw Dandan being bullied, rushed over to stop them, but those male classmates did not stop; instead, they beat up Xiaoxiong. And Dandan didn't know about it. After those male classmates left, Xiaoxiong picked up the books and gave them back to Dandan.

Dandan asked Xiaoxiong: "Are you okay?"

Xiaoxiong shyly said: "I'm fine, haha..."

Dandan said: "That's good, thank you! Let's have lunch together."

Xiaoxiong shyly said: "Okay."

They brought their lunch boxes to the seaside to eat. After finishing their meal, they sat on the rocks enjoying the sea breeze.

Dandan asked Xiaoxiong: "What do you want to do when you grow up?"

Xiaoxiong said: "I don't know... maybe protect you!"

Dandan smiled and said: "Haha, really?"

Xiaoxiong said: "Yeah, really!"

Dandan said: "Alright, let's make a pinky promise. This is our promise!"

Xiaoxiong said: "Okay!"

Xiaoxiong asked Dandan: "So what do you want to do when you grow up?"

Dandan said: "I want to be a teacher, and I hope my eyes can get better soon, then I can see you who protects me!"

Xiaoxiong said: "Haha, yeah, work hard!"

Dandan said: "Yeah, you too! Let's go back to class!"

Xiaoxiong said: "Yeah, Dandan, actually..."

Dandan said: "What is it?"

Xiaoxiong said: "Nothing... let's go..."

After a few months, the teacher said in class: "Dear students, Dandan is going abroad for study and treatment. We must remember our classmate Dandan!"

The students in the class all clapped their hands. The few classmates who used to bully her came to Dandan and said, "Dandan, we're sorry for what happened before..." Dandan smiled and said: "It's alright!"

Xiaoxiong sadly looked at Dandan, but Dandan didn't notice his sadness. At this moment, Dandan went up, took off the necklace she wore, and gave it to Xiaoxiong. Xiaoxiong didn't say anything...

Dandan walked out of the school gate. Suddenly, Xiaoxiong rushed out and shouted loudly outside the window: "Dandan, actually that day by the sea, I wanted to tell you: 'I like you!'" But Dandan was too far away to hear it...

Sixteen years later, Xiaoxiong became a driver delivering things, and Dandan returned, becoming a teacher with her eyes cured. She met a boy abroad and was very happy with him.

When Xiaoxiong learned that Dandan had returned, he was very happy, but he didn't dare to visit her because Xiaoxiong knew she was now happily together with someone else. He didn't want to disrupt her life, so he could only silently watch Dandan from afar. Every year on her birthday, he would send Dandan flowers and gifts, but never signed his name.

One day after Dandan finished work, she encountered some thugs who were teasing her. Xiaoxiong saw this from afar and rushed over to fight the thugs. Dandan, hiding nearby, saw Xiaoxiong but didn't recognize him as Xiaoxiong didn't reveal his identity.

Xiaoxiong said: "Let me take you home." Dandan agreed.

On the way home, Dandan asked Xiaoxiong: "What's your name?" Xiaoxiong made up a name, Xiao Jun. "Haha, you were so cool just now! Thank you!" Dandan talked about her childhood: "When I was young, I couldn't see anything, had few friends. A boy named Xiong was my good friend, often protecting me, taking me to the seaside to enjoy the wind, swinging on the swings in the park. We even made a pinky promise that he would protect me, but he tricked me and disappeared when he returned."

Xiaoxiong chuckled stupidly, "Maybe, he secretly protected you." When they arrived at Dandan's house, suddenly a car rushed over. A man got out of the car and stabbed Xiaoxiong. Xiaoxiong fell to the ground, and a diary fell out of his pocket. Seeing this, Dandan panicked and called an ambulance...

After being sent to the hospital, Xiaoxiong was being rescued, while Dandan waited outside the operating room holding the diary that had fallen from Xiaoxiong. Curious, she opened it and couldn't help but cry. It turned out that every year, the person sending flowers and gifts was him. It turned out that he had been waiting for her, always protecting her, and he hadn't lied to her!

When Dandan was about to flip to the last page, the doctor came out and told Dandan: "We've done our best..."

Dandan saw the last page, which was the necklace she had given Xiaoxiong. Xiaoxiong had pasted it on the last page and wrote: "Our agreement on June 23, 1985. I will protect Dandan well because I love her." Unable to bear the shock, Dandan broke up with her boyfriend and cried for days and nights in her room holding the diary. Finally, Dandan committed suicide...

Dandan wrote a letter: "Mom and Dad, I'm sorry! Your daughter is unfilial! But I love him deeply. I hope that we can be buried together so that we can be together forever. I hope there can be a wedding at the funeral. This is my last request!"

Dandan's parents endured the pain of losing their daughter and finally fulfilled her last wish, burying Dandan and Xiaoxiong together and holding a wedding at the funeral!

Xiaoxiong waited for 20 years and ultimately never managed to say "I love you" to Dandan face-to-face!
