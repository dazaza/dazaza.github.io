In gynecological diseases, there are not a few related to emotions. Most of them are caused by unpleasant feelings such as sorrow and discomfort. Girls are often emotionally delicate and easily get upset when encountering unpleasant things, leading to emotional distress, which frequently makes them feel chest tightness and shortness of breath.

Women with chest tightness often experience spontaneous chest fullness and breathing difficulties, especially every morning after waking up. In fact, chest tightness is not a single disease symptom but rather the result of multiple causes. Chest tightness and shortness of breath are greatly associated with the secretion levels of sex hormones. Young women have abundant estrogen secretion, thickened subcutaneous fat, and fat tissue consumes more oxygen than muscle tissue, so young women may experience insufficient oxygen supply, leading to chest tightness and shortness of breath.

In gynecological diseases, there are many related to emotions, most of which are caused by depression and uncomfortable moods. Girls are often emotionally sensitive and tend to get upset easily when faced with unpleasant situations, leading to emotional distress and frequently feeling chest tightness and shortness of breath.

Of course, some cases of chest tightness are also associated with other reasons. For example, cervical discomfort can lead to chest tightness and shortness of breath. Clinically, it has been proven that cervical spondylosis reflexively induces coronary artery spasm and compression, causing myocardial ischemia and triggering arrhythmias. Besides chest pain, this can also cause chest tightness, palpitations, and shortness of breath.

During cold seasons, female chest tightness is quite common. How should we prevent it?

Firstly, ensure to enhance your own immunity to prevent colds and treat various respiratory system diseases in time because only strengthening the body's immunity can effectively combat viral invasions. Exercise more, pay attention to indoor ventilation and hygiene.

Secondly, women should focus on maintaining a balanced diet and adjust their eating habits, trying to eat light meals and avoid smoking as much as possible to avoid exposure to smoke, toxic gases, and other irritants that affect the trachea and bronchi.

Finally, when women experience temporary chest tightness and shortness of breath, they can try massaging the Neiguan acupoint. The Neiguan acupoint is an important point for heart health, capable of calming the mind, soothing the spirit, regulating qi, and relieving pain. It belongs to the Hand Jueyin Pericardium Meridian. With the palm facing up, when you clench your fist or raise your palm, you can see two tendons in the middle of your palm. The Neiguan acupoint is located between these two tendons, two cun above the wrist crease.
