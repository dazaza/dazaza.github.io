Recommendation of Eight New Functions of QQ Bookmarks That Are Easy to Overlook

by cacard on 2007-06-19 09:15:34

QQ Bookmarks was previously added to the QQ panel (Related article: QQ adds new feature: direct access to QQ Bookmarks on the panel). In fact, there are quite a few features of QQ Bookmarks that many people don't know about. Here, we introduce eight recommended tips.

1. QQ Bookmarks Firefox Add-on

What is Firefox? - Firefox is an open-source browser developed by Mozilla (download). One of its biggest features is its openness, with currently thousands of useful add-ons available for users to download. To make it convenient for Firefox users, apart from ensuring functional compatibility, QQ Bookmarks has also specially developed a lightweight QQ Bookmarks add-on. After installation, two buttons, "Bookmark to QQ" and "My QQ Bookmarks," will appear on your Firefox toolbar, allowing you to bookmark and manage your bookmarks with just one click. Related URL: http://shuqian.qq.com/xpi.php

2. Get timely reminders of cool sites from QQ Bookmarks

The "Cool Sites Compass" section on the right side of the QQ Bookmarks homepage will have dedicated personnel recommending the hottest and coolest websites recently. And by simply clicking "Use QQ Subscription," you can conveniently receive updates of cool sites on QQ in real-time!

3. Super simple QQ Bookmarks domain – sq.qq.com

Do you think the current domain shuqian.qq.com is too long? If you want to be lazy, you can just enter sq.qq.com, or if you prefer English, you can also use bookmark.qq.com.

4. Convenient login and bookmarking process

Many users who like QQ Bookmarks say they find the login and bookmarking process well-designed. If you're using a personal computer, the system can completely automatically record your login information for you, so bookmarking web pages only requires two clicks!

5. Latest bookmarks, popular bookmarks

Visiting the latest bookmarks and popular bookmarks on the QQ Bookmarks navigation bar is another great way to learn what other QQ Bookmarks users are currently viewing!

6. Large thumbnail display of recommended websites on the homepage, see more clearly!

To help you understand more about popular sites on QQ Bookmarks before clicking, a larger webpage thumbnail display has been added. On the homepage, just hover your mouse over the small image, and a large image will appear!

7. More flexible import function

Now when you choose to batch-import bookmarks from your web browser, you can flexibly edit and modify them during the second step of the import process!

8. New version of the bookmark and embed tool released, providing multiple styles to choose from

With the release of the new QQ Bookmarks icon, users who want to embed QQ Bookmarks' favorite icons into their personal blogs and webpages now have four different style options. Choose your preferred style here: http://shuqian.qq.com/help/tools.html#p4!
