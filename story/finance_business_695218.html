Since its establishment, National Gaochuang has been regarding innovation as the core competitiveness of the enterprise in casting processing.

by teqtniqj on 2012-03-07 14:55:51

Recently, the Ministry of Housing and Urban-Rural Development officially notified the Shandong Provincial Department of Housing and Urban-Rural Development, confirming Weifang Guojian Gaochuang Technology Co., Ltd. (www.wfgjgc.com) as a National Residential Industrialization Base. This is the fourth in Shandong Province, the first in Weifang City, and the only one in the heating measurement and energy-saving industry to receive this honor. Therefore, this base is also called the National Heating Measurement and Energy-Saving Industrialization Base, involving casting processing.

The National Residential Industrialization Base is an important industrial layout for cultivating and promoting residential development, construction, and related services at the national level, involving casting factories.

The National Residential Industrialization Base primarily requires a large industrial relevance and actively promotes the modernization of the residential industry. Heating measurement and energy-saving control products, as components of residential systems, play a significant role in new building projects and retrofitting existing residential buildings for heat measurement. The ultrasonic heat meter developed by Guojian Gaochuang has passed the national Kangju product certification, making it a backbone enterprise in this industry, greatly contributing to national Kangju engineering projects and the construction of green intelligent buildings.

Strong innovation capabilities are another key feature of the National Residential Industrialization Base. As a key supported thermal energy-saving enterprise by the Ministry of Housing and Urban-Rural Development, Guojian Gaochuang has made innovation its core competitiveness since its establishment. It founded the first and currently the only academician workstation in the heating measurement and energy-saving industry. Centered around the academicians and their teams, it established an enterprise R&D core, undertaking several research topics of ministerial-level science and technology planning projects.

High technological intensity and market development prospects are other important characteristics of the National Residential Industrialization Base. It possesses the ability and function to advance key common technologies in the industry, integrate applied technology, mass production, collaborative matching, market development and intensive supply, as well as technical diffusion and promotion application. The heating measurement renovation in northern heating areas was referred to by Vice Minister Qiu Baoxing of the Ministry of Housing and Urban-Rural Development as the most potential development field among the six major fields of building energy saving and is an important part of the country's energy conservation and emission reduction work during the '12th Five-Year Plan' period.

It is reported that with the continuous increase in the country's "thermal reform" efforts, Guojian Gaochuang focuses on systematic energy saving technology, integrating key technologies such as centralized heating temperature control and heat measurement, pipeline hydraulic balance adjustment, time-of-use zone control, weather compensation, system frequency conversion control, flue gas condensation recovery, centralized automatic control of heat sources and pipelines, and building envelope technology. In the renovation projects they undertake, they aim to achieve full implementation from the start, avoiding repeated construction later on. Related link: http://www.chinahouse.gov.cn/zzbp5/z1613.htm

After the base is built, it will gradually complete nationwide industrial base technological transformation and capacity expansion and ultimately form a nationwide industrial base layout, effectively driving further development of the heating measurement and energy-saving residential industry, enhancing the overall technical level of the residential industry, and leading the deep development of China's residential industrialization.
