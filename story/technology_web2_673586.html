Municipal government promotes the development of LED industry

by lcfledcom on 2012-02-23 17:18:10

Recently, a research group composed of Wu Zilü, member of the Standing Committee of the Municipal Committee and Executive Vice Mayor, Zou Jiajun, member of the Standing Committee of the Municipal Committee and Minister of Organization Department and responsible persons from relevant municipal departments came to Jianghai District for research. They required that the construction of LED exhibition and trade center (LED display screen for stage background) should be accelerated through the reverse forcing mechanism, and the development of LED industry should be promoted. District leaders like Liang Meixian, Liang Xuzan accompanied the research.

The research group listened to the report on the land requisition and demolition, investment promotion work of the district's LED exhibition and trade center as well as the construction situation of Lianhai North Road, and had a detailed discussion on the problems existing in the rapid advancement of the construction of LED exhibition and trade center and Lianhai North Road.

The development and construction of LED exhibition and trade center is the top priority of all current works in Jianghai District, it is a "top leader" project, it is the "No. 1 project" of the whole district. As an important supporting project of LED exhibition and trade center, the completion and opening to traffic of Lianhai North Road project can not only greatly improve the surrounding environment of the trading center, but also has great significance in unblocking the main artery of transportation for the development of Waihai (large LED screen for stage). At present, the construction work of the LED industrial base and the exhibition center in the district have both made new breakthroughs.

The research group affirmed the achievements made by the district in the construction of LED specialized market, and expressed that the Municipal Party Committee and the Municipal Government would continue to pay high attention to and support the construction of the project. It was required that the district should allocate more leaders and personnel according to the deployment and requirements of the municipal government, refine the working plan, implement various measures, strengthen the coordination between the city and the district, fully promote the land requisition and demolition work of the LED exhibition and trade center and Lianhai North Road, carry out the preliminary preparation work of Lianhai North Road construction synchronously, and ensure the construction of the trading center to be completed with high quality as planned.
