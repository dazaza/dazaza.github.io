The 13th Shanghai International Auto Show will be held on April 22, 2009.

One of the most worthwhile automotive events to attend globally in 2009, the 13th Shanghai International Automobile Industry Exhibition (shortened as: 2009 Shanghai International Auto Show), will present itself with a brand-new realm and face at the Shanghai New International Expo Center from April 22 to 28, 2009.

According to the latest statistics from the China Association of Automobile Manufacturers, in 2007, influenced by the strong momentum of rapid growth in the country's macroeconomic operation, urban and rural residents' income further increased, resulting in gratifying developments in China’s automobile production sector. In the automobile domain, affected by the price reduction of passenger cars stimulating consumer demand, the automobile industry overall continued the favorable development trend of the previous year. By the end of 2007, automobile production and sales reached 8.8824 million units and 8.7915 million units respectively, increasing by 22.02% and 21.84% year-on-year, both setting historical records. In the coming years, there is potential for this trend to continue, with 2008 expected to exceed 10 million units. China will become the world's third-largest automobile producing country and the second-largest automobile market.

Accompanying the development of China's automobile industry alongside the international automobile industry, after more than 20 years of accumulation, the Shanghai International Auto Show has grown into one of China's most authoritative and internationally influential automobile exhibitions, ranking among the A-grade international auto shows. The Shanghai International Automobile Industry Exhibition was founded in 1985, held every two years in Shanghai, making it China's earliest specialized international automobile exhibition. In June 2004, the Shanghai International Auto Show successfully passed the certification of the Union of International Fairs (UFI), becoming China's first UFI-recognized automobile exhibition. The 12th Shanghai International Automobile Industry Exhibition was successfully held at the Shanghai New International Expo Center from April 22 to 28, 2007. Over 1,300 exhibitors from 21 countries and regions participated, showcasing 868 whole vehicles, with 5 overseas models making their global debut. The exhibition area exceeded 140,000 square meters, attracting over 500,000 visitors from 108 countries and regions, with 103,000 visitors on the first public day. The exhibition welcomed 6,286 journalists from media outlets in 43 countries and regions. These figures not only set new records for the Shanghai Auto Show but also shone brightly in China's automobile industry. Moreover, the technical achievements displayed by manufacturers around the theme of "Perfect Harmony between People, Cars, and Nature," such as low energy consumption, solar power, pollution-free, zero emissions, etc., brought about significant and profound changes in automobile concepts. Meanwhile, the comprehensive rise of independent brands became an indispensable new force in this auto show. Independent brands matched foreign brands in terms of exhibition area and launched new cars and self-developed concept cars.

The 2009 Shanghai International Auto Show is co-organized by the China Association of Automobile Manufacturers, the Shanghai Branch of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade, and the Automotive Division of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade, and undertaken by the Shanghai International Exhibition Co., Ltd. of the Expo Group and the Munich International Trade Fairs/IMAG International Trade Fairs & Exhibitions Co., Ltd. The special supporting unit is the China Machinery Industry Federation, while the China Society of Automotive Engineers serves as the supporting unit.

The 2009 Shanghai International Auto Show will utilize all 11 indoor exhibition halls of the Shanghai New International Expo Center along with outdoor temporary exhibition halls, covering a total scale of 170,000 square meters. Regarding the exhibition schedule, to facilitate comprehensive and detailed interviews by journalists, the organizers have designated April 20 and 21 as media days, open exclusively to domestic and international media. From April 22 to 28 will be the exhibition time for whole vehicle manufacturers, while April 22 to 25 will be for parts manufacturers (subject to change).

The theme of the 2009 Shanghai International Auto Show is set as "Technology. Art. New Realm." In response to global climate change, energy and environment will be one of the greatest challenges humanity faces in the 21st century. The rapid development of the automobile industry and the widespread use of automobiles will inevitably have an impact on energy and the environment. However, the automobile industry, with nearly a century of technological essence, will continuously create new technologies—such as the research and promotion of automobile new energy and hybrid energy vehicles, the application of energy-saving and emission-reduction technologies, etc.—perfectly integrating automobiles with the latest technology. In addition to showcasing the latest cars and technologies, the 2009 Shanghai Auto Show will continue to innovate in booth arrangements, service facilities, and display methods, creating an artistic and harmonious atmosphere. This will allow a wide range of audiences and media to enjoy and marvel at this perfect art product upon entering the exhibition site, fully experiencing the comprehensive strength and deep cultural heritage of domestic and international automobile manufacturers. The 2009 Shanghai Auto Show will bring the latest models to China and the world while jointly leading and interpreting the future development of the automobile industry, explaining the theme of "Technology. Art. New Realm" and elevating the auto show to a completely new realm and height.

On the basis of inheriting the people-oriented service and management philosophy of the Shanghai Auto Show, the 2009 Shanghai International Auto Show will continue to innovate, striving to cultivate its own characteristics and temperament, making the Shanghai International Auto Show one of the ideal and most important platforms for display, release, and trade in China's and even the international automobile industry.
