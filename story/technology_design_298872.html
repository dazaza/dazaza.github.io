(1) An uncut cake: Large market blank, good market prospect. Mr. Chen Yuanbin, a franchisee from Jiangsu, entered the crystal jewelry industry 20 years ago and after efforts, he made his first pot of gold. With the continuous improvement of people's living standards and aesthetic taste, he originally thought that this business would add more luster, but the reality was a drastic decline. The reason is: ten years ago, there were only about three crystal jewelry stores in the city, and now there are nearly a hundred of various sizes. Ten years ago, a mid-range crystal jewelry cost between 200-300 yuan, and now some stores even sell for 30 yuan. Today's jewelry industry appears prosperous, but in fact, it is struggling to survive. Mr. Chen Yuanbin deeply realized: the jewelry industry has already reached its saturation point, and this cake has been divided up almost completely. It's time to find a new industry. But what kind of industry still has market blanks? Clothing, catering, car beauty, which one is not overcrowded with numerous practitioners?

One day, while flipping through magazines at a bookstall, he accidentally saw the Foyunlai mechanical Buddhist incense project and couldn't help but feel enlightened: with the further opening of religious beliefs and the continuous improvement of people's living standards, the demand for various kinds of incense products is getting larger and larger. Not only during the Spring Festival, Qingming Festival, July half, Buddha worship, god praying, temple fairs, funerals, and ancestor worship do people need to burn incense, but also in daily life, such as seeking wealth and warding off evil, wishing for promotion and blessings, achieving top rankings, wedding nights, moving into new homes, shop openings, air purification, disinfection, and sterilization, all require burning incense.

I want to write an article about me and her. Once said, I will no longer write half a word about love. Back then, how determined we were! My relationship with her wasn't love, wasn't loneliness, wasn't sentimentalism, but rather a sincerity...

It's been a long time since I've written anything emotional. How long has it been? Maybe it's been so long that I don't even remember the once skilled sentimentalist named July. All I remember is the July who became indifferent... the July who started to talk nonsense. Perhaps those who know me best might never have known how delicate I used to be...

In NetEase, all my friends know a girl named July; in Zhen Tu, all my friends know a girl named BaoBao. Some know her as Nongxian, others call her Lehe, and still others call her Xuefeng... How many names does she have? For a passerby on the internet, these don't matter. What matters is the story I'm about to tell you, a story that always brings me beauty when I recall it. I never write my own stories, but I like to cry over others'.

Before playing Zhen Tu, I had never touched any online game. I played Zhen Tu because of someone, because of my beloved BaoBao. All the clumsiness and helplessness in the game stemmed from one person... I wrote countless love stories about Zhen Tu, and privately, I would secretly boast: whose love is more beautiful than mine? A woman's drive for love is infinite, making the impossible possible. When everything settled down, I stayed in the game because of those people, because of those past experiences, because of those people we both once cherished...

In Zhen Tu, I was a rookie, a top-level rookie, unable to PK or complete tasks without dizziness. But the thing I enjoyed most was creating a bunch of small accounts, each with a beautiful name... That was my hobby, a pretty stupid one, which I admit myself...

When I met the little witch August, all my small accounts with beautiful names had already reached level 120... And every day, I would be in the same place, killing the same kind of monsters because those monsters dropped a stone called "chaos reduction," which was quite valuable at the time... Whenever I recharged my account, my eyes would be full of affection, even tenderly gazing, but I don't know why... that deep affection far lessened compared to the happiness brought by a single stone, as if I got an immense bargain, and I kept changing accounts to kill monsters for nearly half a year... I never felt bored, instead, I found it interesting. The first thing I did after logging in every day was to occupy a place. Thinking back to those times, they were actually the happiest. No matter how chaotic or noisy the country was, I was still hanging in the Jiaolong Cave, ignoring things outside...

The little witch appeared in that August two years ago, just like me, dragging along a whole team... which made me very angry. Clearly, she was here to snatch my territory. Her group of accounts were all higher levels than mine, so if my small accounts tried to attack, it would definitely be suicide... But if I used my main account to attack, I could definitely clear the field. At that moment, I indeed had the intention to kill...(Well, sometimes I am really shameless)

Witch August was a celestial fairy. After she hung her team, she began running back and forth, adding buffs to her small accounts, and incidentally added buffs to me too, but I didn't even say thank you, my mind was fully occupied by the thought that she came to snatch my territory...

After hanging out for two days, we were relatively peaceful towards each other. Because, no matter how shameless I was, I wouldn't hurt her. Gradually, we started chatting. This chat was serious. She was even more innocent than me. At that time, her bunch of small accounts had already reached above level 130, meaning that brushing monsters above level 115 wouldn't drop anything. I told her this, but she still didn't move, saying that experience was fine, and she wanted to keep me company...

Later, I learned that she was the same as me, a couple account. Just like me, the wife led a bunch of small accounts to brush monsters... Hehe!!, I never expected to meet a kindred spirit. The two of us chatted happily among a pile of monsters every day, and the game seemed increasingly interesting...

Our relationship became better and better. There was no possibility of romantic feelings between two women, but that sincerity was something rare and precious. I let her kick her husband aside, divorced him, and then married me. Looking back, I was really not a good person...

In Zhen Tu, I got married N times, several times I forgot. The first marriage was my main account, lazily married by a friend. The second marriage was to a person who kept looking for me for half a year, his persistence moved me... However, the one who went online wasn't me, I just went through the formality, and then the two men acted amorously together, my real-life husband and my game husband... The third time was my main account marrying my small account, an idea I considered quite genius... As for the rest, they were all marriages with Witch August, a total of six times...

The first time I married Witch August was lively, with many friends coming to congratulate. The sixth time we got married, when we told the family, they said, "BaoBao, you can go die." Aren't you just fooling around and shouting in the world for no reason... I was quite depressed, not knowing why we got married and divorced so many times, but definitely not for any principled reasons. It was just always interesting...

I am a rather unconventional person. In the game, Witch August is the only one I voluntarily gave my phone number to because she uses UT. The rest don't count, including the family members...
