"Happiness Blooms Quietly" - A Non-traditional Path of Love and Marriage

by jqrxass on 2009-08-31 12:51:27

"Silently Blooming Happiness" tells the story of a pair of quarrelsome lovers who have been at odds since their youthful days. Due to their incompatible temperaments, they often argue heatedly. Although during their subsequent interactions, both parties develop subtle feelings for each other, due to various reasons, they hastily embark on two different lives. Love remains unresolved.

Eight years later, he has become one of the main leaders in a state-owned enterprise. And she, with his covert help, has also become an excellent administrative officer. But sweet love still remains distant from them...

Finally, through a twist of fate, they come together, but their relationship is not normal - she becomes his mistress, not his girlfriend...

The path to happiness is bumpy, and the process of defending it even more unforgettable. Watch how this beautiful and rational woman preserves her happiness...

Silently Blooming Happiness Part One

If it hadn't been for that summer when Qiao Yizheng impulsively went to where she worked, she might still be there until now.

Shu Qing snaps back to reality amidst her aunt's cries. Though harboring resentment, seeing her heartbroken aunt and frustrated uncle, who have added many white hairs over these few years, she realizes they must also feel bad about her situation. Feeling somewhat guilty towards them, what else can she complain about? After all, her current life is still decent enough. Just like this, let life go on. But, asking her to find Qiao Yizheng...

Shu Qing softly says: "I didn't look for Qiao Yizheng."

Indeed, she did not seek out Qiao Yizheng; it was Qiao Yizheng who came looking for her.

Silently Blooming Happiness Part Two

As soon as Qiao Yizheng steps into the doorway, he smells the aroma of food wafting through the air, evoking a long-lost sense of home. He leans against the doorframe, watching Shu Qing busying herself in the kitchen. She wears a powder blue tight-fitting T-shirt and a bright yellow casual mid-length pants, hugging her youthful and graceful figure. With every bend of her waist, the hem of her T-shirt rises, revealing a section of her tender white waist. From behind, Qiao Yizheng wraps his arms around her slim waist, causing him to fantasize, pressing his face against Shu Qing's slender spine. The protruding vertebrae press against the flesh on his cheeks, yet Qiao Yizheng feels inexplicably at peace. He has longed for this day for too long, so long that he thought he would never have another chance, so long that he thought the two of them would never walk hand in hand.

It turns out that waiting silently will never bring the desired results. The secret to success mostly lies in having a tenacious and indomitable spirit, and daring to take risks and launch decisive attacks when necessary.

Silently Blooming Happiness Part Three

As for Qiao Yizheng, what puzzles Shu Qing is why he always seems fearful of losing her. At the slightest sign of trouble, he worries that she will leave him. This side of him is entirely different from his usual demeanor, lacking the strong, domineering arrogance and instead revealing only the sorrow and helplessness he never shows to others. Recalling every time she sees Qiao Yizheng's pained expression or his cautious demeanor, hears his bitter words, he resembles a child unable to get candy, hiding in his mother's embrace feeling wronged. In Shu Qing's heart, a place reserved for the softest part of her femininity transforms into a clear and transparent pool of green water, wanting to cleanse his grievances and gloom, wanting to protect him and tolerate him.
