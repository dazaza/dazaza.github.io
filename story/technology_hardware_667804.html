After scoring the first goal, Wu Ciniqi ran around the field wildly celebrating. In front of the broadcast cameras on the sidelines, he let out all the frustrations in his heart. Indeed, for him, this goal was not easy at all. After scoring twice, Wu Ciniqi happily embraced Totti to celebrate. The mutual understanding between these two key players of Roma moved people deeply. *Slow Lens* wrote, "For Ranieri, he used Wu Ciniqi to achieve the greatest result in a limited time. Before the end of the game, Wu Ciniqi came on as a substitute and then scored twice to repay his coach. What more do we want from him?" This Italian media gave the Black Hermit the highest score of 8 points in the entire match. Statistics from Opta showed that Wu Ciniqi had two shots and scored two goals within 10 minutes, with one pass becoming a key ball. Despite only coming on as a substitute, Wu Ciniqi proved with data that he was the protagonist of this game. For Wu Ciniqi, 2010 was a special year, especially the second half of the year. At the beginning of November, Wu Ciniqi welcomed his son, becoming a father. Meanwhile, he encountered a crisis in his career. After Borrello joined, Ranieri insisted on using captain Totti, and Wu Ciniqi lost his starting position. In the first half of the league, this ace forward of Roma in recent seasons only got 610 minutes of playing time, including 2 full games. Before the winter break, Wu Ciniqi encountered injury problems, and he was repeatedly left off the participant list. Rumors about the worsening relationship between Ranieri and Wu Ciniqi spread like wildfire. Afterwards, various transfer rumors continued endlessly, with clubs like Inter Milan and Wolfsburg becoming his rumored new teams. In recent years, Wu Ciniqi, who had been smooth sailing in Lecce and Roma, encountered the darkest moment of his career. Two months later, Wu Ciniqi scored again, and it was even a double. In the match against Catania, Wu Ciniqi came on as a substitute 10 minutes before the end of the game and scored two goals in just 10 minutes, helping Roma defeat Catania. Facing adversity, players' reactions are usually one of two: explosion or indulgence. As the captain of Montenegro and the "Montenegro Footballer of the Year 2010," Wu Ciniqi answered with goals, belonging to the latter. Ten minutes before the end of the game between Roma and Catania at home, Ranieri sent Wu Ciniqi to replace Taddei. Despite very limited playing time, Wu Ciniqi cherished every opportunity. Within 10 minutes, Wu Ciniqi had two attempts and scored two goals. In the 86th minute, Menez initiated an attack, Totti made a cross in the penalty area, and Wu Ciniqi surrounded the front point; despite being pulled and dragged by Catania's defenders, he still managed to score. In the last minute of stoppage time, Wu Ciniqi received Totti's cross and shot into the near corner. Additionally, he provided an assist to Totti, but unfortunately, Totti's shot went wide.
