(2) Wireless Mall

In 2006, Gome launched "Palm Gome," allowing people to access the latest product information via Gome's WAP website on their mobile phones. With the advent of the 3G era, the concept of mobile commerce has become increasingly ingrained, and many domestic companies have gradually realized the importance of corporate mobile informatization and mobile commerce for their own development. More and more companies are trying to establish their own WAP websites or sell their products in wireless malls, hoping to leverage this new phenomenon to boost their development. In this vast sea of information, how can your customers find you? How can WAP websites attract more visitors, and how can products displayed in wireless malls be viewed by more potential customers? To achieve these goals, professional promotional methods are needed, and mobile search engines are the most cost-effective and efficient way to promote mobile commerce, attracting more potential customers to WAP websites and products in wireless malls, thereby maximizing corporate benefits.

Studies show that mobile search engines are currently the most important and effective way to find mobile information resources and promote businesses, especially for finding wireless mall products, allowing users to quickly find suitable products and compare prices. Mobile search engines are the main way users acquire resources. When using wireless malls, users searching for product information tend to directly input product names into the mobile search input box, and a considerable proportion of users have this habit.

A global mobile e-commerce portal is the best shop for promoting products, allowing customers to conduct business transactions with merchants anytime and anywhere via their mobile phones. Merchants can release their product information, promotional information, etc., through the computer website (Web) platform, and the information needs to be published only once to appear on both the computer website (Web) and the mobile website (Wap).
