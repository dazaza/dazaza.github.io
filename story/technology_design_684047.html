Renovated Methods for Blog Promotion

1. Accurately Position Your Own Blog

Nowadays, many people have their own blogs, especially in various fields, where there are some relatively well-known bloggers. We need to establish relevant connections with them, and first, we need to build our own blog well. In choosing a place for the blog, we can often select some websites with high popularity to establish our own blogs, such as the blogs of major portals, Baidu Space, Hexun Blog, Tianya Blog, etc. Then position your own blog. With accurate positioning, it will be easier for relevant users to know us, and when the blog becomes popular, we can establish connections with some famous blogs. This lays a solid foundation for the next step of promoting our website.

2. Diligently Build Your Own Blog

If you plan to create a general website, then the content of your blog can be casual. However, if you want to elevate the quality of your blog, then you should be particular about the content when posting articles. When writing posts, pay attention to whether the article title aligns with the blog's positioning, whether the text layout is reasonable, whether the readability of the post is strong, and whether it brings an impact to the readers. If you can achieve that a reader not only visits your blog but also collects it, then you are not far from success.

My friend and I currently manage around 10 sites, and most of our articles come from various blogs; we have a lot of collected blog addresses on our computers. Many friends who run websites gather articles from blogs, so if your blog post gets reprinted by someone else’s site or blog, that would be a great thing.

3. Join Blog Circles, Blog Alliances, and Other Groups

We create blogs so that others can know us and understand our blogs, helping us to establish a wide circle of friends. Therefore, after setting up the blog for about two months, you can join some blog circles and add more bloggers related to your theme as friends. If you have time, exchange experiences with them more often. If you can connect with some well-known bloggers through your friends, that would be even better.

4. Do Well in Text Links and Use Friends' Blogs to Promote Your Website

All the previous steps are prerequisites for implementing this method. Now let's specifically talk about this method. My friend has nearly a hundred friends on his Sina blog, and his blog theme is about education. The related friends’ blogs are mostly related to education. Especially among these friends, there are some teachers who are quite reputable in the field of education. They may not have websites, but they do have very influential blogs. When promoting my themed class meeting website, he first established a super link titled "The Most Suitable for Elementary and Middle School Teachers to Use." The title was very attractive! Then he placed the other party's blog link in the most conspicuous position, followed by his own blog and website address. Afterward, he contacted those high-authority blog owners and uploaded the content to their blogs. Of course, here you need to know how to promote your products effectively. My friend told me that at the beginning of making friends, don't be afraid of losing out. Remember that loss is a blessing. He spent a few hundred dollars promoting his links with one of the very influential bloggers, and their relationship became even closer.
