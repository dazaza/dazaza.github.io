Wedding Website Planning

Planning Introduction:

Promoting wedding services and showcasing the brand of a wedding company through a website has certain market value. In theory: it's easier to promote on the internet, reaching a larger audience, and compared to real-world brand promotion, it can achieve a greater ripple effect. In reality: the demographic participating in weddings has gradually shifted from those born in the 70s to post-80s generations. A key characteristic of the post-80s generation is their strong internet awareness. The internet attracts a large number of post-80s users, and people typically search for wedding-related information online before hosting a wedding.

From the above perspective, we can see that the internet is a critical point for strategic development for businesses. It helps cover the target audience effectively, unlike real-world promotions which are more scattered and lack clear positioning. When considering the establishment of an enterprise website, we particularly focus on building the company's brand. Through reasonable website layout and specific corporate customization, we ultimately form a standard that fits the enterprise, making it more conducive for the company to establish its brand in the market.

A wedding website can connect the related wedding industry chain, forming an overall online wedding information city. When there is enough content, it can attract guests looking for wedding-related links, converting potential customers into actual customers, and further converting related customers into potential customers who eventually become clients. We can start from wedding-related themes such as: wedding photography, group weddings, special story-themed weddings, wedding travel, distinctive wedding banquets, wedding decorations, wedding car decorations, wedding hotels, etc. All these series of wedding-related items can be realized through the website, providing one-stop comprehensive services and consultations.

Specific Planning:

The final implementation of the entire plan is to build a comprehensive wedding-themed website, with a prominent theme, detailed classification, easy searchability, and multi-category links (one link can guide visitors to multiple wedding-related categories. In marketing terms, this can be seen as pursuing customers for related products, guiding them to purchase more services).

1. Prominent Website Theme:

Mainly includes: wedding hosting and recommendation themes, wedding planning themes, wedding travel themes, custom-made themes, wedding knowledge themes, member interactive communities.

Wedding Hosting and Recommendation Themes: Mainly presented in text form, showcasing the latest wedding fashion trends, wedding information from various regions, and comprehensive content texts.

Wedding Planning Themes: Focuses on a series of wedding planning services including makeup, wedding car decoration, hotel reservation, wedding photography, travel, etc., covering large-scale weddings, small weddings, banquets, shooting venues and costumes, equipment rental, etc.

Wedding Travel Themes: Targets honeymoon trips, "sunset red" memory tours for elderly weddings, hotel recommendations, etc.

Custom-Related Services: Displays relevant cases of the enterprise, highlighting its advantages. It can accept special customization services for celebrities and people of middle-upper social status, belonging to high-end services.

Wedding Knowledge Themes: Provides wedding-related information, such as selecting wedding attire, ordering wedding cars, wedding banquets, travel, seasons, offering knowledge in all aspects of weddings. This belongs to a textual section that can provide guests with a platform for information consultation and search, enhancing customer friendliness towards website information and increasing the chance of secondary browsing.

Member Interactive Community: Mainly establishes a popular communication platform like a forum, where users can chat and discuss wedding experiences. This place will also be where future website activities attract participants and is a good place for official interaction with guests.

2. Function Settings:

Function settings need to include the following: search function for searching website information and related news; online consultation function for visitors to consult when they encounter problems; message system for users to leave messages when administrators are offline; business order system for online ordering or reserving related services, allowing follow-ups via contact information left by guests to finally close deals; article comment function, enabling product evaluation by guests to understand the strengths and weaknesses of the enterprise in market operations, strengthening product optimization and resource integration, opening up new markets.

3. Page Structure:

Page design should conform to the general browsing taste of the public. The homepage should have more images and fewer texts, with dynamic image displays, attracting visitors' attention. The homepage should at least have one large image, making the website look grand while not lacking technical sophistication.

Section-wise: Ensure each prominent theme page has a unique structural design, giving a fresh feel without losing browsing fun. The sub-sections within each highlighted theme can adopt the same structural design.

Other Aspects: Include a site map and navigation bar for visitors to easily find the desired theme categories.

4. Graphic Design:

In graphic design, use festive colors as the website background color. Titles should stand out, navigation bars and borders should use colors suitable for the current theme or background.
