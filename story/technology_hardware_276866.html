Tianma River Manor Phase 2: Next to the metro station, inside the park, beside Tianma River

Tianma River Manor is located within the Tianma River International Park on Fengshen Avenue in Huadu District, Guangzhou City, west of the Xinhua toll station on the Guangzhou-Qingyuan Expressway, adjacent to the automobile city area which will become an important economic engine for Huadu District. It is approximately 800 meters from the "Automobile City" station of Guangzhou Metro Line 9. Tianma River Manor is a rare super-large high-end community in the urban center of Huadu District with extensive ecological green space, covering more than 700 acres with a total construction area exceeding 1 million square meters, making it a rare large community in the urban center of Huadu District. Phase 1 of Tianma River Manor consists entirely of Mediterranean-style villas and small high-rise apartment buildings, some of which have already been completed. The style is elegant and simple, with lush gardens, showcasing the characteristics of Mediterranean architecture. The "Blue Tianma River" group is the second phase group of Tianma River Manor, located to the south of the first phase group.

The "Blue Tianma River" group is expected to publicly release its first batch of units in December. The sales site is currently accepting discount registrations. Customers who register with their personal information will receive an additional 95% discount on the opening day offered by the developer. The expected public release price will be around 5,500-5,800 yuan/square meter, including 1,000 yuan/square meter for premium finishing. The "Blue Tianma River" group has a total of over 5,000 units, with the first batch releasing 1,000 apartments at once. It is understood that the monthly residential transaction volume in the entire Huadu District is about 1,000 units, making the second phase cargo volume of this project equivalent to five months of transaction volume in the entire Huadu District.

Tianma River Manor - Fengshen Commune (data, forum, second-hand)

Fengshen Commune, located on the north side of Fengshen Avenue in Huadu, is expected to go on sale at the end of November. The unit types range from 90-210 square meters, including two-bedroom to five-bedroom units, with bare concrete selling at 5,500 yuan/square meter. The project is already fully completed, and currently undergoing sincere registration, offering a 98% discount on house purchases and a 20,000 yuan housing purchase discount.

Fengshen Commune - Guangzhou Poly City Garden (data, forum, second-hand)

The Guangzhou Poly City Garden residential area located at the intersection of Fengshen Avenue and Huagang Avenue in the Huadu District automobile city has been completely sold out. Currently for sale are the "Mansion" townhouses of the Windsor Castle series, ranging in size from 230-445 square meters, with only more than ten sets available, starting at 10,000 yuan/square meter bare concrete. Purchasing a townhouse now comes with a 200,000 yuan discount per set. Poly City will launch a new apartment complex early next year, with hundreds of units expected to be released, mainly consisting of two- and three-bedroom units.
