China iOS Developer Salary Report

by anonymous on 2013-07-29 17:14:03

According to the data from Apple's third quarter financial report, the revenue in Greater China region this quarter was 4.65 billion US dollars, plummeting by 43% compared to the previous quarter and dropping by 14% compared to the same period last year. Despite the decline in sales performance, the revenue from Apple's China operations accounted for 14% of Apple's total revenue this quarter, which was just a few hundred million US dollars a few years ago. The progress of Apple's business in China is remarkable, and behind this progress lies a large number of 500,000 Chinese iOS developers, while there are only 300,000 iOS developers in the United States.

The number of Chinese iOS developers is not only large, but their income is also considerable. According to the "2013 iOS Developer Salary Survey Report" released by CocoaChina:

The monthly salary range of developers is mostly between 5,000-8,000 yuan, accounting for 25%; the proportion of those in the range of 3,000-5,000 yuan and 8,000-10,000 yuan is 17% each; the proportion of those in the range of 10,000-15,000 yuan is 19%; the proportion of developers with a monthly salary below 3,000 yuan is 10%; the proportion of people with a monthly salary above 25,000 yuan is 4%, and the proportion of developers with a monthly salary above 8,000 yuan is 48%.

Work experience:

For developers with less than one year of work experience, most of them have a monthly salary in the range of 3,000-5,000 yuan, and fewer have a salary above 8,000 yuan; For those with 1-3 years of work experience, most have a monthly salary of 5,000-8,000 yuan; those with a monthly salary above 10,000 yuan account for 26%; For those with 3-6 years of work experience, most have a monthly salary in the range of 10,000-15,000 yuan, accounting for 38%, followed by those in the range of 15,000-25,000 yuan, accounting for 21%, and only 16% of developers have a monthly salary below 8,000 yuan; For those with more than six years of work experience, most have a monthly salary in the range of 15,000-25,000 yuan, and 20% of them earn more than 25,000 yuan. It can be seen that as work experience increases, the salary also increases in a stepwise manner.

Development type:

Among developers working in enterprises on game development, 38% have a monthly salary above 10,000 yuan, and 30% are engaged in application development; Among developers working in enterprises on game development, 21% have a monthly salary below 5,000 yuan, and among developers working in enterprises on application development, 23% have a monthly salary below 5,000 yuan; Most enterprise game developers' monthly salaries are distributed in the range of 10,000-15,000 yuan, accounting for 22%, while the monthly salaries of developers working in enterprises on application development are mainly distributed in the range of 5,000-8,000 yuan, accounting for 28%.

From the data, it appears that the salary for game development is slightly higher than that for application development. However, game development has a higher threshold, so it seems reasonable that the salary is slightly higher.
