Sichuan Tangjiashan quake lake plans to build a 5A scenic spot

by syd17u123 on 2009-11-23 18:09:52

The Qiang Culture in Zhaohua Needs Urgent Protection and Has Been Included in the Investment Promotion

Beichuan is the only Qiang Autonomous County in China. Due to the destruction caused by the Wenchuan earthquake, the Qiang culture urgently needs protection and restoration. The Qiang cultural tourism project has also been included in Beichuan County's tourism and leisure resort industry investment promotion projects. According to relevant personnel from the Beichuan Tourism Bureau, a company from Beijing has expressed cooperation intentions for the Qiang cultural tourism project, and both parties are currently in negotiations.

Yesterday at the Western China International Fair, the Beichuan Investment Promotion Bureau and the Tourism Bureau brought 32 tourism development projects such as Tangjiashan quake lake and Ape King Cave scenic area to Chengdu for global investment promotion. It is reported that Beichuan plans to jointly develop the Beichuan National Earthquake Museum and Tangjiashan quake lake into a 5A-level scenic spot. Projects with scientific and educational nature such as the "Simulated Earthquake Experience Hall", water entertainment projects on Tangjiashan quake lake, and surrounding leisure projects will all be developed through investment promotion.

Creating Scenic Spots: Beichuan and Tangjiashan Build 5A-Level Scenic Spot

Relevant personnel from the Beichuan Tourism Bureau told reporters that the earthquake-formed natural heritage including more than ten large and small quake lakes like the old Beichuan county town and Tangjiashan, along with deformed geology and landforms due to the earthquake, can be developed into a large-scale tourism project integrating scientific research, education, tourism, and comprehensive development.

It is introduced that Beichuan plans to jointly develop the Beichuan National Earthquake Museum and Tangjiashan quake lake into a 5A-level scenic spot. Water-based and surrounding leisure and entertainment projects of Tangjiashan quake lake and hotels will be built through government-enterprise cooperation. After the earthquake, the old Beichuan county town was further damaged by mudslides, which are secondary disasters of earthquakes. Currently, the ruins, cracks, and mudslide sites caused by the earthquake have been well preserved for people to mourn the Wenchuan earthquake and for earthquake science education and scientific research. Due to the certain professionalism of this project, it will be built by government funding.

Investment Promotion: Building Simulated Earthquake Experience Hall

It is reported that near Beichuan Middle School, projects such as "Earthquake Museum", "Earthquake Disaster Rescue Training Center", "Simulated Earthquake Experience Hall", and "Love Park" will be built, among which the "Simulated Earthquake Experience Hall" with scientific and educational nature will become an investment promotion project. New hotels to be built within the new Beichuan county town and around Tangjiashan quake lake will be important investment promotion projects.

Relevant personnel from the Beichuan Tourism Bureau stated that Shanghai provides strong support for the tourism development of Beichuan. Tongji University has organized experts from 17 colleges to conduct surveys in Beichuan County and make plans for the protection and development of earthquake sites. Once the plan is released, it will clearly define the scale and required investment amount of the investment promotion projects.
