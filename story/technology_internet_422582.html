Thank you for letting me see the dawn of Hunan music.

by musicshow5 on 2011-04-29 10:26:48

Hunan Music Network, from its establishment till now, has been more than a month. At first glance, it seems to be in good shape. I smiled faintly, though I didn't say anything, but I didn't forget to thank the friends who worked hard for it. So today, I will write a blog post to thank everyone here.

Mr. Xie Jingbo, the general manager, I won't mention him here. We also talked about our friendship last time. This gratitude cannot be described by words.

Mr. Wang, the general manager of Zero Point Bar, was the first ground support for Hunan M Zone. I drank your fine wine and ate your late-night snacks. You sponsored the singing talent competition of Changsha Civil Affairs College by yourself, and you didn't forget to promote our website during the whole execution process. Hundreds of people participated in the activity. The semi-final made the bar crowded and bustling. A lot of promotional cards were handed out, which made me very grateful. Maybe because the preparatory work was not done well, only a few music enthusiasts entered the website, so I can only blame myself.

Yaoyao, the beauty from the Fifth Tone Coffee in Changsha, was the first store cooperation of the website, and thanks are necessary. The first special edition was opened for you, and you were the first moderator. I know you are very busy, but you still find time to manage the forum, release information, modify group announcements, modify the official announcement of Douban, Sina Blog, Weibo, etc. According to the promotion plan, we haven't started the first ground activity yet, but you have already taken the lead in publicity. Although there are not many members, I can understand. When activities are held at your place, the popularity and traffic will be excellent.

The next person to thank is Teacher Lai Xi, the leader of Hunan Guitar Alliance and a famous guitar training institution teacher. The second special edition was given to you. Your students are all talented, and playing with this forum is just wasting your money-making time on this nonsense, but you still came and brought your students to join our website. Every trade has its own experts, and according to your advice, the forum opened the first batch of sub-forums, making the guitar classification quite fine. All recommended sub-forum moderators are online, showing great care and support for our station.

... (Temporarily omit thousands of words, to be continued later)

I see everyone's support in my eyes and remember it in my heart. One day, when Hunan M Zone grows stronger, I will definitely express my gratitude for everyone's strong support during the difficult start-up period. Here, I sincerely ask other leading figures and music lovers with music resources to support us. Hunan music needs everyone's support!
