by cacard on 2007-05-16 12:05:32

Mister Wong Online Favorites Save web pages, each of us can create an online favorites sorted by tags, with which we can easily find our favorite collections anywhere in the world. At the same time, we are more likely to be inspired by sharing our bookmarks with others or creating groups. You will have a new Internet experience.

Mister Wong has all the features you can imagine. In general, people use Google and Baidu to search for topics they are interested in. However, when we enter a keyword in Google or Baidu, the search results are exactly the same every time, because all web pages are arranged in order according to certain rules. Therefore, in the traditional search engine, the highest click-through rate is often only the first 20 pages.

Now we have a new search mode, that is, web bookmarking. In the United States, where many programs have been successfully implemented, web bookmarking has become a very popular trend.

In web bookmarking, all links can be evaluated correctly; The web community determines which pages have higher value; The quality of web pages is no longer determined by individuals, but by groups. As a result, the search results are more humane, subverting the traditional search model.

So far, only a few web bookmarks exist, and most of them are English pages. Now Mister Wong has built a good platform for Chinese bookmarking websites, so that more people can easily use it, because it is characterized by high efficiency and high fun.

Functional features of Mister Wong

Private: Bookmarks can be set to public or private, and private bookmarks are only for personal browsing.

Groups: You can create a public or private group and choose a theme for it. Each member can bookmark relevant information in the group, share it with others, and rate the bookmarks. Anyone can be a member of a public group, but only by invitation can they join a private group. The group function is ideal for companies, organizations and students to gather information.

Friends: Anyone can be a friend, whether it's a friend or someone else, as long as you share the same concerns. Click to see your friends' bookmarks!

Bookmark: Add other users' links to your Mister Wong collection by clicking on them.

Mister Wong Toolbar: Add browser plugin you can easily use Mister-Wong. Click the Mister Wong icon to save the new page you are viewing to your favorites, display your personal favorites or go to the Mister Wong home page.

Blog button /Wordpress-Plugin: Insert Mister Wong button into your blog and let your visitors add your posts directly to their Mister Wong collection. If you're writing a blog using Wordpress, you can add it directly to your Mister Wong favorites by installing an add-on.

Mister Wong has comprehensive and up-to-date web bookmarking site features. The more people use it, the more powerful the search function becomes. Invite friends and colleagues, create your group, decide whether it's private or public ----- that's what sets Mister Wong apart. Plus, it's completely free and you can use it anywhere!

Install Mister Wong plug-in and experience a whole new experience: Wong the web!
