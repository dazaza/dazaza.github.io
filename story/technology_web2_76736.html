The Secrets of Japan's 11 Most Popular Blogs Revealed

by yinyin on 2008-07-07 20:42:14

Ichiro Kiyota, editor of the Japanese website Gizmodo, believes that "blogs are an evolution of Japan's diary culture. The blogs of social celebrities contain things they cannot tell mainstream media, and we can understand them better by reading their blogs." The diary culture in Japan can be traced back to the eighth century.

Japanese blogs cover a wide range of topics but share some common characteristics: beautiful appearances, geeky tendencies, and a love for blogging.

Here are Japan's 11 most popular blogs:

1. Blogger: Shoko Nakagawa

Age: 23

Blog site: blog.excite.co.jp/shokotan/

Reason for popularity: New queen of Japanese blog sites

Traffic: 100 million monthly page views (author's published figures, same below)

Profession: Actress, singer

Favorite topics: Nail polish, cakes, cats, reality shows. Recently sparked global controversy by putting a cat in her mouth.

2. Blogger: Kaori Manabe

Age: 27

Blog site: manabekawori.cocolog-nifty.com/

Reason for popularity: One of the first to use the internet to enhance personal influence and become one of the initial blog site queens.

Traffic: Data not available

Profession: Actress, writer, former swimsuit model

Favorite topics: Self-cooked food, being drunk

3. Blogger: Chiaki Kyan

Blog site: kyanchi.blog73.fc2.com/

Residence: Tokyo

Traffic: 25,000 daily page views

Profession: Bikini model

Favorite topics: Anime Mobile Suit Gundam, own cat, video websites Nico Nico Douga and YouTube

4. Blogger: Noriko Saito

Blog site: d.hatena.ne.jp/dropdb/

Residence: Tokyo

Age: 25

Profession: Web director at a media company
