Having escaped from the tiger's mouth, he fell into the wolf's den! As soon as Chen Mu set foot in Ami City, he became the "prisoner" of the Ning family, the overlord of Ami City, and quickly earned the honor of being an A-level card maker at the Ning family base. But Chen Mu, who had lost his freedom, was still secretly determined to leave this dangerous prison. However, could the Ning family, which had achieved a great victory in the war with the Zuo family thanks to the rain-shuttle cards invented by Chen Mu, let go of such a powerful and dangerous treasure so easily?

Three black figures darted out of the building one after another, and a game of speed and passion unfolded quietly in the perilous wild jungle. However, while the mantis was catching the cicada, the oriole was waiting behind. With a thunderous roar in the jungle, the Double Hook Beast, whose name struck fear into the hearts of birds and beasts, suddenly appeared before the three people. At this life-and-death moment, would they fight to the death or flee together?

On a cross night, in an ancient village, the disaster of Jiaoyun occurred, each event more magical than the last, and each more thrilling than the previous. In this contest between man-made and natural disasters, will Chen Mu escape his fate or survive against all odds?
