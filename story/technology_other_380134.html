Beware of the Pitfalls in Mealworm Farming

Mealworm farming is considered to be one of the most promising insect farming ventures and represents the best insect farming opportunity for industrialization. The Southern Rare Insect Breeding Research Center — the first authoritative and specialized mealworm industrialization research institute in China — has collaborated with South China Agricultural University, Sun Yat-sen University, and Guangdong Academy of Agricultural Sciences to end the grassroots research in this industry and strongly promote the industrial development of mealworm farming in China.

1. Mealworm Feeding Technology Pitfall: Anyone with feeding experience knows that feeding is a science, and feeding technology is a highly specialized field. To succeed in feeding, high-quality seed stock is the premise, while mature technology and management are key.

Mature mealworm feeding technology ensures successful feeding and good economic benefits. Although mealworms and yellow mealworms belong to the same genus, their living habits, growth environments, and nutritional needs differ significantly from those of yellow mealworms. Many feeding merchants claim that mealworms, also known as super yellow mealworms, are simply larger versions of yellow mealworms and can be raised the same way. This is actually a misleading and self-deceptive statement. Mealworms are very resilient and wild but react strongly to environmental stress, with extremely high rates of self-mutilation. The feeding technology for mealworms is more complex than for yellow mealworms. However, after receiving professional training and fully mastering the feeding technology, raising mealworms becomes easier than raising yellow mealworms, with higher productivity. Therefore, farmers should not easily believe certain people who exaggerate claims similar to those about raising yellow mealworms. Our center has received many farmers who failed miserably by following advice to raise mealworms like yellow mealworms. Additionally, whether the mealworm feeding technology is mature and advanced determines the market competitiveness of mealworm products and the size of return on investment. Strong technical support is the stepping stone to your success in mealworm farming. Choose reputable breeding companies, and wealth will come your way!

If you encounter any of the following situations from a breeder, be extremely cautious!

1. How much does a set of mealworm breeding stock cost? High prices with guaranteed buy-back.

2. Mealworms are a hybrid of yellow mealworms and black mealworms or the "N"th generation product of yellow mealworms.

3. Mealworms can automatically pupate like yellow mealworms.

4. The growth cycle of mealworms is the same as yellow mealworms, around 100 days.

Before entering the mealworm farming industry, visit authoritative mealworm websites to learn about mealworm basics and the development level of feeding technology; or gather cases of those who have been deceived, analyzing the reasons for deception, so you can make a clear judgment! Actually, as long as the reproduction method is correct and feeding is done properly, it doesn't matter where you buy the seeds from.

Mealworm Market Pitfall:

Mealworms are the best live bait insects and were previously used as live bait for various animals in foreign markets. When they entered the domestic market, they were mainly used for this purpose. Currently, the main uses in China include as live bait for valuable ornamental fish (such as golden dragon fish), money turtles, and ornamental birds, as well as being the best live feed for scorpions, Northeast forest frogs, and Southern stone frogs. Before resolving the large-scale deep processing market for protein source feed, food, health supplements, etc., it's easy to see how much demand there is for mealworms. Some merchants aggressively develop farmers, boasting about buying back products at high prices, which is suspicious. This rush-to-market approach, where everyone jumps in and then quickly disperses, taking what they can get before leaving, is typical seed speculation behavior, harming farmers and disrupting the healthy development of the industry. Previous hype about getting rich quick from raising yellow mealworms, ants, or ostriches has led many deceived farmers to lose everything!

Mealworm Seed Stock Pitfall:

To successfully farm mealworms, having high-quality mealworm seed stock is essential. Good seed stock can effectively reduce feed conversion ratios, greatly shorten feeding time, and have strong resilience to efficiently resist disease invasion, ensuring successful farming fundamentally. The cultivation of high-quality mealworm seed stock requires highly specialized technology, including the use of biogenetic techniques, physical and chemical methods, spending sufficient time to purify, crossbreed, and strengthen the stock. Besides the coordination of technology and time, advanced experimental instruments and equipment are naturally necessary tools for breeding. These are beyond the reach of ordinary feeding merchants. Therefore, when purchasing seed stock, always choose research institutions or farms with professional technical guarantees, and avoid dealing with the various "information departments" or "seedling companies" in society to prevent being deceived!
