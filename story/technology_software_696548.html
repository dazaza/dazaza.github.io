The daughter is a beauty in the making.

by muoehtprer on 2012-03-08 09:32:55

My mother is still in good health with all test indicators tending to be normal, and she has an annual health check-up. I deeply understand that my mother's life was saved by my father's love, the result of his utmost dedication, the brilliance of their thirty years of unwavering affection, and more importantly, the outcome of my father's consistent care and scientific diet as well as reasonable exercise. Now they are healthy, worry-free in their old age, joyful, and loving for a hundred years.

When I was a child, I did not understand the pain in my grandmother's heart. Mom, you have fewer books than her, but in terms of being a person and dealing with things, you are very sympathetic and reasonable, surpassing the general tolerance and greatness of a mother.

Last spring, my mother sowed seeds in the flowerpot again, sowing new hope. When encountering relatives or friends in trouble, my grandfather would take out some money from his originally meager retirement salary to help others. At this time, my grandmother would protect them outside.

Sometimes I think of my childhood dreams, they were so beautiful. In 1995, my father sent my second sister to school, passing through Qufu, and had dinner with colleagues in front of the Confucius Temple.

Especially for the health products my father saw on TV advertisements or newspapers, claiming to cure various cancers, I would buy them for my father without hesitation. Only when my father tried them and found them ineffective would I stop buying them.

Back then, the team lacked oxen power, so my father and several strong laborers carried the load on their shoulders.

In my memory, tomorrow is my daughter's birthday. As a child, he was not brave, even weak, once being beaten until he bled from the nose during a fight at school and could only cry without saying anything.

I proudly told her that our daughter is a beauty embryo, and will surely grow up to be a beautiful and charming woman. I hope my son will have a lifetime of peace, happiness, and become a pillar of the country with both virtue and talent.

I hung up the phone, loneliness, fear, sadness, and helplessness surged into my heart, but it only lasted for a few seconds, and I finally came to my senses and went to the hospital.

My heart aches so much, piercingly painful.

Little brother looked up and saw my face, not knowing what words were wrong, and became flustered...

I am going to work.

However, seeing you reviewing your homework at home every day gives some comfort to mom and dad. After all, our daughter is a obedient and sensible child.

Springtime makes people lazy. The flowers see the faint light through the curtains, stretch their waists, and don't want to get up.

There were several times when we didn't know, but grandmother secretly went to grandfather's grave alone and silently shed tears there.

On September 13th at 6:00 in the morning, my father called to tell me that Uncle had already passed away.

He wants to see you on Sunday.

Until today, the girl is still waiting bitterly, hoping for a miracle to appear, hoping to meet her best friend and confidant again.
