Become an expert in this field.

by shooter on 2007-06-07 22:18:28

An effective SEO strategy can bring long-term significant traffic to your website. Here are some key aspects:

**Improvement in Technical Means:** Learn about the characteristics of search engines to achieve better rankings.

**Marketing Methods:** What we used to talk about as building links, now serves more as a promotion and marketing model.

**Becoming an Expert in Your Field:** If you're not yet familiar with the industry your website is in, it’s best to spend some time and effort to understand it (or hire a professional).

**Information Architecture:** The website you create should allow target users to quickly and accurately find the information they need.

**Becoming an Expert in Your Field**

**Information Architecture:**

Wikipedia defines information architecture as: structuring information (knowledge or data) for a clear purpose. Simply put, design centered around the user. Here are the main points:

1. Fully understand how your users perceive the industry your site belongs to; keyword distribution plays a crucial role here. Tools like Keyword Discovery and Wordtracker are very useful. These tools can help you understand which keywords users might use when searching for content on your site. During this practice phase, you can create a map for your navigation based on the knowledge you've learned about the industry.

2. A clearly defined global navigation: Easy navigation is very important for users. This requires consistency in your website's positioning and structure. A breadcrumb-style navigation bar is very effective (for example: Search Engine Observation Forum > Search Engine Marketing Strategy > Search Engine Optimization), because it clearly shows the structure of a website, keeping users aware of which page they're on and which pages they’ve already visited.

3. Standard Firefox User Interface: There’s no need for innovation because users have formed fixed mindsets about where to find certain content. What you need to do is simplify.

**Improvement in Technical Means:**

Search engines are increasingly demanding that we design content for users rather than for search engines. However, search engines still have some basic requirements. If you can't meet these requirements, the future of your website will be bleak. Below are five suggestions you need to incorporate into your technical methods:

1. Insert a clear navigation plan that can be searched via text links;

2. Minimize the number of clicks to your key content; when analyzing a website, search engines usually judge which content is more important. If a piece of content requires four clicks from the homepage, how can you claim it’s important?

3. Add some content visible to search engines, meaning articles, descriptions, and links should all be presented in text form to search engine crawlers.

4. Conduct related content linking on your website, which is essential for consolidating page relevance.

5. Effective META description tags: These won’t help much with your ranking, but these descriptive texts allow search engines to judge the content of your website. Therefore, the description sentences should include words that users are most likely to click to enter your website.

**Marketing Methods:**

How to get others to link to your website remains an important part of marketing. We don’t recommend buying links; what we mean is that you don’t need to offer anything in return for them to link to you. If some people aren’t linking to you for money, then what are their reasons? Because they care about their users and believe your website has value to them, such as high-quality content. In the eyes of search engines, if the link to your website is of high quality, it will also give you a high rating.

Our industry calls this process "link building," but now we need to change this mindset. There’s nothing wrong with link building; links are still an important factor for websites to rank well in search engines. However, there are a few things to note:

1. Since we know the importance of search engine PR values, we should choose to link to content highly relevant to our website.

2. The efficiency of reciprocal linking has greatly decreased; this behavior is actually a waste of time unless you're exchanging links with highly relevant, high-quality websites. The best guideline is: Would you still link to the other party's website if they didn’t link back to you? If the answer is yes, then make the exchange.

3. For many, purchasing links may seem effective, but it carries significant risks, so it should be firmly abandoned.

In summary, you can obtain links through different channels. For a company, good reputation can establish relationships between users and partners. Simply put, build trust, and this will encourage others to link to your website. You need extensive professional knowledge, which is the prerequisite and foundation for your website to gain massive traffic. You must place some information users want to read on your website and establish connections. Therefore, only when users consider the content on your website to be of high quality can you truly manage a website in your industry.

Here are some suggestions to make your website look professional:

1. Write a large number of high-quality articles in your field (but don’t force them to immediately lead to product or service purchases).

2. Provide useful tools on your website that users appreciate.

3. Create a unique and valuable community on your website.

4. Become a news source in your industry.

In short, provide users with high-quality content, not link traps. Link traps aim for quick and easy connections to any resource, but here we recommend establishing trust with users. Trust isn’t just empty talk; it can steadily increase your performance and traffic.

If you focus on building business integrity, some techniques may distract you. Integrity is a commercial capital you need to maintain continuously. If you spend time on other things, your commercial foundation will weaken. If you consider website building as part of developing your business, don't let your attention wander.

But at the same time, you cannot ignore some technical demands of search engines. Don’t design your website solely for search engines, but rather design it for users while making it suitable for search engines.

Undoubtedly, using technical means provides many opportunities to increase search engine traffic. However, these technical means carry risks and can distract you. When search engines eliminate these techniques, the traffic you gained through technical means will disappear along with them. Worse still, you might be penalized by search engines for cheating.
