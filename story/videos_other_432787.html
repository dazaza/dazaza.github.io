According to Russian media reports on July 14, the tradition of the bride throwing flowers at the wedding has a long history in Italy. However, recently in Livorno, a western port city in Italy, a bizarre incident occurred during a wedding where a couple's flower-throwing act caused an air crash. Not only did it scare away all the guests and ruin the wedding, but also the crashed plane hit a nearby hotel, nearly causing a tragedy.

After the vows ceremony, the bride Isidoro Pensilri, who was sitting on the plane holding a bouquet of flowers, began throwing flowers from the sky. The moment the bouquet flew out of the plane, a terrifying scene happened: the powerful suction created by the engine instantly swallowed the bouquet. Subsequently, the engine caught fire and exploded, and the plane finally crashed into a hotel nearby where 50 people were staying. Fortunately, no one in the hotel was injured.

The single women who witnessed this scene scattered everywhere.

Fortunately, the bride Isidoro and the 61-year-old pilot Lucieno escaped from the cabin. Isidoro suffered multiple fractures and head injuries, but fortunately, her life was not in danger. Miraculously, the pilot was almost uninjured.

This incident completely ruined the wedding of the couple. After the accident, the frightened guests left the wedding scene one after another.

Tragically, the couple's wedding was thoroughly ruined. According to local media reports in Livorno, Italy, a happy couple welcomed their long-planned romantic wedding that day. In order to make the traditional wedding flower-throwing activity fresh and interesting, and to enhance the joyous atmosphere, the couple specially rented a light aircraft, which hovered above the single female guests who came to attend the wedding and were ready to snatch the lucky bouquet.

According to traditional beliefs, the single woman who catches the bouquet will be the next to get married.
