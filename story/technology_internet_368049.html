by shootero on 2010-10-15 10:20:59

At the Microsoft WP7 mobile phone listing conference launched at 9:30 am on October 11, New York time, Microsoft released a total of 10 mobile phones, which can be described as the most centralized single system conference in the history of mobile phones, Microsoft has been waiting for a day for a long time. But in the end of 2010, the smartphone market has been a little crowded, WP7 mobile phones are positioned high-end, in the market is bound to be iOS, Android fierce competition, the following author for everyone to take stock of a few WP7 had to face strong rivals.

Suitable for: high-end smartphone players and business people

After Microsoft's WP7 is listed, it is bound to be greatly impacted by the current hot Android, as Samsung's annual flagship, the powerful configuration of i9000 and the rich applications of Android will greatly show the charm of WP7 for the time being.

As the flagship new machine released by Samsung this year, the biggest feature of the officially released Samsung GalaxyS (GT-i9000) is the integration of the current various popular functions in the thin body of only 9.9 mm, so that the phone as a whole has reached the flagship level. For example, in the screen configuration, the machine is equipped with the same 4.0-inch WVGA resolution touch screen as the SONY Ericsson XperiaX10, and once again uses Samsung's own development of a new generation of SuperAMOLED screen, which features more advanced technology than the previous AMOLED screen. For example, the brightness has been enhanced by 20% compared with the past, and the thickness of the screen has been reduced, and what is more commendable is that it still maintains the same power consumption as in the past, coupled with the latest technology, making the SuperAMOLED screen have a very high display effect even in the case of direct sunlight outdoors.

In order to obtain more smooth running speed, Samsung Galaxy YS (GT-i9000) also has a built-in 1GHz processor, and based on the ARMCortex-A8 processor core, the processor level of this phone has reached the same level as SONY Ericsson XperiaX10. Although the built-in camera is a 5-megapixel camera, it also offers quite a wealth of shooting features, including autofocus, facial recognition and 720P high-definition video recording. Samsung GalaxyS (GT-i9000) also runs the latest version of the Android2.1 operating system, has a new version of the TouchWiz3.0 user interface, provides a multi-page standby home screen, and those who often need to visit social networking sites can change the layout of the home page. This allows faster access to Twitter, Facebook, and a host of other social networking services.

In terms of appearance, the HTCDesire adopts full touch design, the body circumference is: 119x60x11.9mm, the machine weight is 135g, and the body size and weight are relatively moderate. Equipped with a 3.7 inch AMOLED capacitive touch screen, the difference is that NexusOne uses a combination of touch buttons + trackball, while Desire uses a combination of physical keys + optical touch panel, the actual operation of the touch key is more sensitive and the physical key is more feel, which is better or less according to personal preferences.

It is equipped with a 5 megapixel camera, equipped with a flash, the ability to take pictures is good.
