Love Well

Some people and some things, once missed, will never come again. Woman, why do you make the person who deeply loves you sad? Woman, please don't forget that in this sentimental world, there is still a boy who loves that girl.

They loved each other very much. They planned to get married after graduation. However, regrettably, they didn't end up together. The boy promised to buy the girl the most beautiful and happiest white wedding dress in the world. They often went to a wedding dress shop where the bride wanted to choose a very beautiful wedding dress.

Because she wanted to become the most beautiful bride in his heart. In the blink of an eye, she saw one - a pure white wedding dress that was extremely white. She then said to him, "I want this one, I like it, it's been chosen by me."

The boy said, "Alright, let's buy it now." The girl smiled and said, "Silly, we're still in school. Don't you worry? Let's wait until we graduate to buy it. Haha..."

In 2003, she was diagnosed with leukemia. When I heard this news, I didn't know whether it was pain or numbness. Smiling at myself, I said, "Don't give up, don't cry, I will always be with you," but tears were streaming down my heart because I was afraid that if she saw me crying, she would feel heartbroken. Such heartwarming yet heart-wrenching words were everywhere.

She underwent a bone marrow transplant and had mild rejection reactions. On November 26, 2003, the rejection reaction suddenly worsened. At 3 o'clock in the morning on November 27, she left her beloved boyfriend and family.

"In her last moment of life, neither of us gave up! Although we regretfully couldn't be together, I will dress her in a wedding dress, present her with 99 roses, and together accomplish our greatest wish."

A white wedding dress, 99 fresh red roses, a special "wedding" quietly took place in the Baihe Hall of Huizhou Funeral Home.

After a year and a half of fighting against leukemia, the strong girl Chang Dongdong left with her boyfriend Ouyang Xiaojie's deep attachment. But yesterday was also the most beautiful day of her life - I dressed her in a wedding dress, fulfilling her long-held beautiful dream.

In front of the Baihe Hall of Huizhou Funeral Home, many people holding chrysanthemums gathered; they all came to bid farewell to Chang Dongdong. Subsequently, her boyfriend Ouyang Xiaojie walked into the Baihe Hall with her family. His face was haggard, and his lips were bloodless. In the center of the funeral hall, she lay quietly there, wearing a white wedding dress, surrounded by bright red rose petals.

"When she was critically ill, I asked her if she could hold on. She pulled my hand and nodded strongly with all her strength." During the ceremony, as I looked at my girlfriend's face and told about her strength, I suddenly burst out with a heart-rending cry while trying to suppress my emotions. Beside me, her parents and sisters had already sobbed uncontrollably, and everyone present shed tears.

During the final farewell to the remains, I kept caressing her face. In the last moment, I suddenly lowered my head and kissed my girlfriend deeply.

We can't be husband and wife in this world. We can be husband and wife in the next. I want to be with you forever. I cried for three days in front of her grave.

Wedding dresses are always related to love. Some say the length of the wedding dress represents how long I will love you. Many newlyweds even want to create the longest wedding dress in the world to show their deep affection for their loved ones.
