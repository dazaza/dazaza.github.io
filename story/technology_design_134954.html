Galaxy Education Center of Chengdu Galaxy Education

by yangguo9045 on 2009-03-28 11:54:24

Chengdu Galaxy Education includes training and educational institutions such as Chengdu Bocai Junior College, Chengdu Galaxy Education Center, Chengdu Galaxy Information Technology School, and Chengdu XinDa Vocational Technical School. Currently, it is the most authoritative international and national IT, management, and business certification training institution in Sichuan Province. It is a national information technology talent training base established by the state, a direct assessment designated unit for the qualification certification project of China's business professionals, a partner of the World Bank's China Project Center, a designated cooperative college for national informatization computer education certification in the Sichuan region, a council member unit of the Continuing Education Branch of the Chinese Electronics Education Society, and a council member unit of the Chengdu Private Vocational Skills Training Association. The center has also established good strategic partnerships with top global vendors: Microsoft Authorized Training Center, APPLE 2004 Annual Authorized Regional Management Center, Adobe Authorized Training and Examination Center, Sichuan Region Microsoft Windows Learning Center and Examination Center (MLC), Sichuan Region 2004-2007 Annual Authorized D-LINK Training Center and Examination Center, Sichuan Region Redhat Authorized Training Center and Examination Center, 2006-2007 Annual SUN Authorized Training Center and Examination Center, the only CISCO Network Academy in 2001 that can offer CCNP courses, Check Point First Authorized Training Center, an authorized examination center by Prometric, the world's largest educational examination certification company, an authorized examination center by American Vue, and an authorized test site for Cisco in the Sichuan region. The school has also collaborated with Chengdu University of Information Technology to establish the Bocai Teaching Department. The main courses offered by the school include various vocational training courses, management training courses, and academic education courses in collaboration with universities.

The school enjoys excellent customer relationships and has established good cooperative relationships with multiple large enterprises and institutions. It is the training partner of large enterprises and institutions such as Sichuan Provincial and Municipal Telecommunications and Southwest Airlines of Air China. It is also the training partner of Sichuan University, Chengdu University of Information Technology, Sichuan Normal University, Southwest Jiaotong University, Chengdu University of Technology, Xihua University, and Southwestern University of Finance and Economics. While introducing advanced course products that align with international standards, the school combines them with the domestic economic development’s demand for talent, developing a new vocational course system. Furthermore, it closely collaborates with local universities to launch the latest "academic diploma + vocational skills" dual certification education model, cultivating highly skilled technical talents urgently needed by employers.

The school is equipped with internationally standardized training classrooms, continuously updated experimental equipment, and has established Cisco CCIE Standard Laboratories, Remote Network Laboratories, 802.11 Wireless Network Laboratories, Network and Information Security Laboratories, Microsoft System Engineering Laboratories, Linux System Laboratories, SUN, IBM, DELL Server Laboratories, CG Art Studios, and Art Video Design Studios. The school boasts a team of renowned gold medal instructors in the industry and globally synchronized training materials, providing students with comprehensive learning conditions. It can design relevant management, business, and professional certification training courses according to different needs of enterprises and dispatch excellent instructors to provide on-site services. To date, the school has conducted numerous centralized or on-site trainings for well-known Chinese and foreign enterprises such as Cisco, IBM, Motorola, HP, Lenovo, Sifang, State Power Corporation, Sichuan Telecom, Sichuan Mobile, and Air China, as well as government agencies like the Ministry of Public Security, Ministry of Education, Construction Bank, General Staff Department, Sichuan Department of Transportation, Sichuan Life Insurance, and Sichuan Public Security Department, earning consistent praise from clients.

Adhering to the concept of "people-oriented," the school is committed to the cultivation and promotion of national information technology talents, engaging in international certification and domestic vocational certification education, and fully crafting new types of highly skilled technical talents. So far, more than 30,000 students have been trained at our center, and they are widely distributed across various fields nationwide. The vast majority of them have become technical experts in their units and companies.
