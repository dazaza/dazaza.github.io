Young lovers died in a car accident on their way to teach in the countryside, video Weibo spread online

by o6nihao8h on 2012-03-06 18:49:41

A Young Couple Meets Tragic End While Volunteering: Video Spreads on Weibo

Source: Liaoning TV's "Talking About the World"

The story came to an abrupt end at 7 PM on June 12, 2010, on the Yeduoshan Mountain in Nyingchi, Tibet (elevation 4568 meters), where they met with a car accident and unfortunately lost their lives.

Netizen Comments:

Youku netizen mkxiaobao: I was born in the same year as you. You were truly excellent. But honestly, I can't accept that such outstanding people are gone. I hope you two are happy and without sorrow or worries in heaven. If possible, I hope your dreams will continue.

"Chengdu, a place you don't want to leave once you've arrived." Li Zhuoling wrote this on her Renren profile via phone as she left Chengdu, which turned out to be her and her boyfriend's last day there. Mao Menguo and Li Zhuoling were volunteering at the Montop Primary School in Mingshan County, Ya'an City, Sichuan Province.

When people were sorting through Mao Menguo's belongings, they found some precious photos. At an elevation of over 4000 meters in Mingshan County, Ya'an City, Sichuan earthquake disaster area, there were many pictures of them with the children. The children there also really liked these young big brother and sister and prepared special gifts for Mao Menguo's upcoming birthday - everyone held cards with the words "Wish Mao Menguo Happy Birthday" to bless their beloved Coach Mao.

They went together to volunteer at Montop Primary School in Mingshan County, Ya'an City, Sichuan Province, bringing knowledge to the mountain children. However, after the volunteer teaching ended, they were unable to descend from the mountains. They both ascended to heaven in the plateau closest to the sky.

Netizen Foxiaohong: Next month I will also go to Sichuan to volunteer teach. I will take them as my role models. I wish this couple safe travels!

Li Zhuoling, like many college students, enjoyed sharing her life on Renren via her phone.

Upon seeing this activity announcement, Li Zhuoling immediately had a strong desire: "I want to go there and help those children."

On April 9, Li Zhuoling saw an activity announcement in the hometown activities section of Douban inviting volunteers to teach in Tibetan areas. This activity notice titled "Tibetan Orphans Need Chinese Teachers" read: In a lamasery at over 4000 meters elevation in Yajiang County, Garze Prefecture, Sichuan Province, there are more than 20 orphans without fathers or mothers this year. Although the lamas can provide food and clothing and Tibetan language education, they cannot provide Chinese language education to help them understand the outside world. If you are willing, please come to teach Chinese for several months, even one month would be helpful.

Mao Menguo, aged 21, was a football player for Hong Kong's Tai Po team. Not long ago, he had just been promoted to the main first team, starting his professional football career. Without this accident, upon returning, he would have become the team's main player, able to compete in the Hong Kong league.

Li Zhuoling wrote in her Renren diary: "University is about to graduate. In 21 years, I want to do the most meaningful thing in my life. I will probably set off for Sichuan around June, for about a month."

Although Mao Menguo and Li Zhuoling were not officially married before their death, with the consent of both sets of parents, it was decided to bury them together in Fenghuang Mountain in Changsha. The "life-and-death lovers" rest together in the earth...

The actions of the younger generation make us proud.

Around June 3, they reached their volunteer teaching destination, Montop Village, Mingshan County, Ya'an City, Sichuan Province. The night before they arrived, on June 2, Li Zhuoling wrote on Renren: "Tomorrow we enter the mountains to volunteer teach~ Oh la la!" expressing her joy.

Youku netizen Jian Dan Hao Hao: I can only silently wish you happiness. Although we are far apart, I think the thoughts and actions of the younger generation will keep your volunteer teaching journey going.

This is a video that has been forwarded over 15,000 times and commented on over 5,000 times on Sina Weibo (http://t.sina.com.cn). In this 5 minute 30 second video, it tells the story of a young volunteer couple departing from Changsha, Hunan to volunteer teach in a mountain village at 4000 meters elevation: the girl teaches Chinese, the boy teaches the children to play football, they play games together, take photos...

She wanted to do the most meaningful thing in her 21 years.

"The power of one person is very limited, I can't guarantee whether I can endure such a tough environment," Li Zhuoling wrote in her Renren diary before volunteering, saying her own power is weak. She hoped that people who read her article would feel a little moved and donate unused books or stationery, which she could bring to the children she would be teaching.

Many passionate youth bring ideals to realize their own value. I am proud of such peers. All misfortunes happen every day. Maybe worldly people would become numb, but I don't know why stories like this always move me. May the two deceased rest in peace in heaven.

Not officially married in life, buried together in death.

Mao Menguo and Li Zhuoling departed from Changsha to Sichuan on May 29 and only stayed in Chengdu for two days.

Reporter He Mingqiang

Li Zhuoling was very meticulous. While volunteering herself, she did not forget to bring some books and stationery for the local children. She called on people around her to donate some books in her diary and arranged the donation location well, placing them with friends in Sichuan, to be brought to the children when she entered the mountains.

On June 21, a new double tombstone was added at the beautiful Phoenix Mountain Cemetery in the southeastern part of Changsha City. The tomb houses a young couple who now rest together in death.

Li Zhuoling was also 21 years old this year, a 2010 graduate of Hubei Normal University's art department, majoring in graphic design, and loved photography. If not for this accident, she would have been like other Chinese university students, holding her graduation certificate and sharing the joy of graduation with her family.

After arriving, they began their volunteer teaching life. Li Zhuoling taught the children Chinese, while Mao Menguo taught them to play football and took photos... In this high-altitude plateau at 4000 meters, the lovers who had only been dating for about three months spent their joyful volunteer teaching life.

There is such a video online. In the video, the man is handsome and sunny, the woman beautiful and elegant. They are a couple from Hunan. His name is Mao Menguo, hers is Li Zhuoling.
