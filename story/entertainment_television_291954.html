Father-son Differences Often Cause Friction

Chu Fan and his son Chu Ci can be described as a pair of live nemeses. The two father and son are completely at odds, hence frequent frictions.

Ci works as a spokesperson for a tobacco company, and with his sharp tongue, he has gained the appreciation of his boss. However, Fan disapproves, believing that one should not promote smoking just for high salaries and benefits. Ci also resents Fan for clinging to his average noodle shop business, allowing his old partner Cheng Shiyi to slack off, and letting his friend Niu Zhengrong eat for free. To avoid arguments, Ci moved out early, resulting in a rather cold relationship between father and son.

Later, Fan develops Alzheimer's disease, forcing Ci to move back home to take care of his father, but their conflicts continue.
