QL Securities: Juyoung Co., Ltd. is still expected to have good growth potential in the future - Stock Forum

by dx0957095 on 2009-12-03 18:36:48

Investment Highlights: The company was founded based on soy milk maker, and its main business at present is the R&D, production and sales of soy milk makers and small kitchen appliances. Its main products include soy milk makers, induction cooker, food processor, juicer, electric pressure cooker, water boiler, purple sand pot, beans, and commercial soy milk maker.

Soy milk maker and induction cooker are the company’s main business products. According to the 2009 semi-annual report data, the revenue of soy milk maker and induction cooker accounted for 79.96% and 10.34% respectively of the company's main business. There are several reasons why soy milk maker still has a very broad development prospect in China. First, the penetration rate of soy milk maker is low; second, the growth of residents' income also provides a basis for soy milk maker to enter ordinary consumers' families; third, the increasing convenience of using soy milk maker makes people more willing to accept it; fourth, the consumption cycle of soy milk maker is short, product updates fast, generally the usage cycle is 2-5 years.

Currently, the main issue facing the company's soy milk maker development is the competition from Midea. However, various signs show that the company's soy milk maker business has not been significantly affected. First, the market share of the company's soy milk maker has not significantly decreased; second, the gross margin level of the company's soy milk maker products remains high, decreasing only by 1.7 percentage points in the first half of 2009 compared to the end of 2008. It can be seen that the company's gross margin has not significantly decreased due to competition; third, the company's new product launch speed is fast, and the sales situation is very good. Moreover, the company has newly launched a high-end product called "Ounando", with a high average selling price and good sales.

The poor sales of the company's induction cooker products is an important factor constraining the company's development. The reason for the decline in the sales of induction cooker products is related to the decline of the induction cooker industry itself. However, the lack of competitive advantage of the company's induction cooker products in the market becomes an important reason constraining the development of the company's induction cookers.

It is expected that the EPS of the company in 2009, 2010, and 2011 will be 1.34 yuan, 1.61 yuan, and 1.90 yuan respectively. Combining the above absolute valuation and relative valuation methods, we believe that the reasonable value range of the company is 31.36-36.18 yuan. Currently, the company's stock price is 28.88 yuan, so we upgrade the company's rating from "cautious recommendation" to "recommend".
