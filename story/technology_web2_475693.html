by ybcz0519 on 2011-08-12 21:42:45

Web2.0Share Weekly is a featured column of Tianya Haige, each week carefully selecting the latest outstanding startups in China to share with everyone. Startups can submit via Weibo: @web20share or email: watsonxu{at}web20share.com.

Multi-person Voice Chat and Friendship Platform - Pengwo Network: A web-based multi-person voice chat interactive platform. Through "Pengwo Network," users do not need to download or install any client software. With just a move of the mouse, they can easily enjoy a new entertainment experience of multi-person interactive voice chat.

Link: http://www.peng58.com/

Ranking Network is a service that helps users discover the most valuable information: "The most worthwhile movies to watch in the cinema," "Today's funniest Weibo jokes," "Good restaurants near Pinglu Road," "Personal reading records for this month"... Each item forms a list, using the power of the masses to help you filter out the most valuable information. Currently still in beta testing.

Link: http://paiming.com/

Taotaojing is an online service that provides Taobao stores with the ability to apply for mobile applications, creating iPhone, Android, and Symbian clients for Taobao merchants, offering try-on, order placement, and other functions.

Link: http://eshop.taotaojing.cn/

iShangman - China Original Comic Omnimedia Platform.

Link: http://www.ishangman.com/

Zuzuche - The Qunar of car rentals?: Provides real-time car rental price inquiries and car rental booking services for major cities across the country such as Beijing, Shenzhen, Shanghai, Guangzhou, etc., covering special offers and discounts from companies like Zhongguo Zuchewang, Yihaizuche, Rika Car Rental, Che Speedy, Che You Haina Car Rental, Dongfeng Nissan Yi Car Rental, etc., helping you find the lowest-priced quality airport pick-up services...

Link: http://www.zuzuche.com/

Xuanli: Instantly brighten your resume, an ultra-cool resume creation website that can turn your resume into an infographic-like effect, as shown below. Currently in beta testing, interested parties can request an invitation.

Link: http://www.colorfulcv.com/

Caimea Savings Browser: "Caimea" - a money-saving online shopping browser. Using Caimea to shop on large e-commerce sites like Taobao, Dangdang, JD Mall, etc., gives you up to 5%-45% cashback.

Link: http://www.caimaobuy.com/

PUPIMG.COM: A high-quality image community composed of many photography enthusiasts. The site aims to discover and share great photos, helping you find like-minded friends.

Link: http://www.pupimg.com/

Dreamore: Mass power driving personal dreams, publish your dreams, form a team, raise funds.

Link: http://dreamore.cn/
