According to preliminary estimates, the cliff is over ten meters high, and below it is under repair, full of rubble. The police's initial investigation revealed that the man surnamed Liu was from Fenggang County, Guizhou Province. Chongqing Evening News reporter Peng Yu, correspondent Liu Xingmin.

At around three in the morning on that day, at a barbecue shop near Hongcheng Hotel on Qinglong Road in Shuangjiang Subdistrict, Yunyang County, two young men came to eat barbecue. They noticed that the female owner was wearing a necklace around her neck, and they both had evil intentions. They grabbed the necklace from the owner and ran away.

Chongqing Evening News reports: While eating barbecue, they reached out and snatched the valuable necklace from the female owner's neck. However, in the darkness, he lost his way and fell off the cliff. In the early hours of the 14th, suspect Mr. Liu was found severely unconscious among the rubble.

Police officer Xu Shou'an from the Qingfeng Platform of Yunyang County introduced that the suspect's head was bleeding and he had fallen into a deep coma. The police immediately called the 120 ambulance and sent the youth lying among the rubble to the hospital for emergency treatment.

When it was fully light, the owner Lei Zuxiang followed the escape route of the youth, hoping to recover his wife's necklace. Unexpectedly, he discovered someone lying among the rubble outside the woods, who turned out to be one of the people who had snatched the necklace. Lei Zuxiang immediately reported it to the police.
