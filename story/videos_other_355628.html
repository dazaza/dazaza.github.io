Some of Adi's technologies

by adiddas53 on 2010-05-23 15:29:51

Explanation of FAB: F: Feature - characteristic, A: Advantage - benefit, B: Benefit - advantage.

1. Shoes:

A. Shoe Series Classification: Basketball shoes, tennis shoes, training shoes, Nike agency advertisement, cross-country shoes, running shoes, soccer shoes, aerobics shoes, etc.

B. Common shoe technology:

1. adiPRENE cushioning - Advantage: High buffer material absorbs harmful impact forces from the ground. Benefit: Absorbs vertical shock to the foot, preventing sports injuries.

2. adiPRENE+ propulsion - Advantage: High elasticity material provides more effective propulsion. Benefit: Helps lift the foot quickly and absorbs lateral impacts.

3. TORSION SYSTEM torsion - Advantage: Cooperates with the natural torsion of the forefoot and hindfoot. Benefit: Midsole support provides stability and safety during motion, preventing sprains.

4. adiWEAR - Advantage: Highly wear-resistant rubber outsole. Benefit: More durable wear, emphasizing shoe protection.

5. goeFIT foam padding - Advantage: Fits snugly around the heel, instep, and sides. Benefit: Enhances comfort while wearing, like putting a glove on your foot.

6. QS technology [quick strike] - Advantage: Lightweight TPU material. Benefit: Reduces shoe weight while increasing grip, commonly used in running shoes.

7. a③ Structure - Advantage: Provides perfect cushioning, guidance, and propulsion in three dimensions; improved TPU material is lighter and more wear-resistant. Benefit: Prevents sports injuries and enhances athletic performance, discounted Nike shoes.

8. Pro Moderator - Advantage: A 1.5 mm thick TPU film cast inside the sole, lighter and more wear-resistant than traditional midsole materials. Benefit: Prevents inward rolling of the foot during movement, supports the midsole, prevents EVA collapse, commonly used in running and basketball shoes.

9. TRAXION studs - Advantage: Lightweight, wear-resistant, non-slip material. Benefit: Provides the best grip, commonly used in trail running shoes, outdoor shoes, and soccer shoes.

10. FORMOTION Ground Control System divided into Adistar3D upgrade/supernova3D/and Response2D

- Adistar/3D upgrade:

1. Visible window enhances visual performance. [adistar control]

2. Large control area on the sole.

3. Two spring columns [two anti-shifters] can move up, down, forward, backward, left, right, abbreviated as 3D.

- Adistar/3D:

1. Invisible window. [adistar cushion]

2. Same control area as 3D upgrade.

3. Two spring columns [two anti-shifters] can move up, down, forward, backward, left, right, abbreviated as 3D.

- Supernova3D:

1. Invisible window, casual shoes. [Supernova cushion]

2. Control area covers part of the shoe.

3. One rubber ring [one anti-shifter] can move up, down, forward, backward, left, right, abbreviated as 3D.

- Response2D:

1. Invisible window. [Response control]

2. Smaller control area compared to Adistar/3D upgrade, Adistar/3D, supernova3D.

3. No spring columns or rubber rings.

Biggest benefit: Reduces knee stress.

11. no Seam - Once-formed upper eliminates original seams, making the shoe feel lighter, more aesthetically pleasing, and comfortable. Commonly used in running shoes.

12. Adidas Drainage System [ADS] - ADS is a patented drainage system. Complete embedded midsole with perforations achieves perfect drainage, cushioning, and comfort. This technology is only used in certain field shoes, suitable for very wet environments.

13. NON MARKING Water ripple non-marking rubber outsole - Advantage: Lightweight, wear-resistant, non-slip rubber outsole. Benefit: Provides stronger friction and stability, emphasizes floor protection, commonly used in basketball and training shoes.

14. adiTUFF - Advantage: High membrane material at the toe of tennis shoes [inner side of the forefoot]. Benefit: Prevents excessive wear on the upper, effectively extending the life of the shoe, commonly used in tennis shoes.

15. ortholite Antibacterial insoles and AGION lining - Advantage: Prevents bacterial growth, has some odor-removal function. Benefit: Protects foot health, commonly used in outdoor and running shoes.

16. Push Through Protection Plate [P.T.P.P] - Advantage: A 2 mm TPU plate placed between the midsole and outsole. Benefit: Increases stability and comfort, prevents injury from stepping on sharp objects during exercise, commonly used in outdoor shoes.

17. Thinsulate [new snowflake] - Advantage: Unique microfibers are 10 times finer than other fibers. Benefit: Blocks more air, reflects body radiation heat, achieving better insulation effects.
