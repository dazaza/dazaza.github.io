by xiaoxiao67 on 2012-03-02 17:08:57

I'm not afraid of being abandoned by the world, I'm just afraid of missing you.

In the evening, raindrops began to fall again in the sky. A wave of autumn rain brings a wave of cold, and the chill became more apparent. Autumn is almost over; winter is quietly approaching. Countless lives are also quietly fading away, like fallen yellow leaves, never to return! Unconsciously, I have already wrapped myself in a long shawl.

Reading your prose is like stepping into your life. There is no hypocrisy there, nor is there any need for pretense, because there, I see the most authentic you and your life experiences. My close friend said, "Do you know? Because of your writing, a group of people are following you in sorrow?" "I'm really sorry, I didn't know! Because I've never told you the address." "Occasionally, I can also see your words in your QQ space!" Once again, I say: I'm sorry! It's not my intention. I just like to occasionally put a few pieces of text with a faint melancholy on the space because I like this kind of feeling!

For some readers' inquiries, I can only remain speechless, because I don't know how to answer you, or what way to explain? I touch my chest, those truths, those stories, that heaviness, and that melancholy... they are all so real, but they don't necessarily have to happen to me personally to become my words!

In one's life, encountering someone who knows how to love you with their heart, or meeting someone who deserves your whole-hearted love, is a blessing! Love doesn't have miracles, nor does it have serendipitous encounters. In love, the person who stays with you till the end is the one who was meant for each other from the start. Looking at those around who already possess, they are full of complaints, let alone cherishing. Every time at such moments, I always feel a sense of relief, grateful that my waiting is better than those who already possess!

In the deep night, immersed in the network, using my limited time to write, whether it's my own story or someone else's, there seems to be no end... Mother says she will donate both of my laptops and my desktop computer, even saying she might throw them away. Because she doesn't like me staying at home all day, immersing myself in the world of writing. She says: Words give me nothing, they only waste my life! Actually, these simple truths, I understand them well too, but just can't quit!

Perhaps, it's because of loneliness! Someone said: If one day, I meet someone who loves me, perhaps these texts with a faint sadness will disappear! I don't know. But I know that now, I love words as if I've fallen in love with someone I deeply adore. When I'm tired, exhausted, I naturally hide in the world of words. Sometimes, I also want to distance myself from these words that make me sad, make me hurt, I also want to pursue a life that truly fulfills me; but I don't know where to look, or how to find it?

Often wandering in the depths of midnight, listening to the voice of my heart in the darkness, completely ignoring the complexities of the world, steadfastly guarding my proud heart. It's not that I'm too cold, nor am I unfeeling, it's just that when I'm lonely, I perceive the details of the human soul. Reality is always cruel, it mercilessly shatters our dreams, destroys the beautiful utopia we seek. It reminds us repeatedly, reality is so bare, so cold and ruthless. The only thing I can trust is the sincerity between people, yet, hypocrisy and deceit exist everywhere, infiltrating every gap, always piercing my dignity, defaming my character.

I am somewhat self-righteous, I can't stand this, look down on that. I detest deliberate performances; I hate those vile faces; I despise those uncultured, ignorant fools; I scorn those thoroughly dirty villains! I respect a person not because of his wealth, nor because of his status, his power, none of his belongings matter to me. Because my life is mine, unrelated to others. My success comes from my own struggle, through my own efforts. So I am at peace, I fear no one. As the saying goes: "If one's conduct is not upright, one's commands will not be followed; if one's conduct is upright, one's commands will be obeyed without being issued."

Opening the window, the rain outside continues, the whole world is crying! The autumn wind blows in my face, raindrops falling into my palm, carrying the panic of winter. My heart is full of helplessness, longing so hard to express! Do you know, actually I'm not afraid of these, even if the whole world abandons me, I don't care. What I care about is, I'm just afraid of missing you!
