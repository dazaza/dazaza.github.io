Firstly, this is a normal paparazzi event. A group of people go out for dinner, drinks, and entertainment. Most stars are far away from their girlfriends or wives while shooting films outside, so they inevitably feel lonely and restless. Under the influence of alcohol, combined with horseplay from friends, they may do some inappropriate things. Although it is indeed indecent, it is human nature. There is a little beast in everyone's heart. If you can't control it, then you become a person with a beast's heart.

Secondly, this could be a self-directed hype event by the production team. I think this is entirely possible. I remember that during the filming of a certain movie, they used this kind of self-directed paparazzi event to promote the film. Especially in this news report, Wang Jing's new film "Xiao Gong Zhen Wu Lin" is specifically mentioned. Regardless of Wang Jing's film-making ability, anyone who knows him knows that this director likes to do some deceptive things and rarely spends energy on important matters - carefully polishing a good movie. And for such a director, the actors and crew he finds, I don't think are of good quality. For these kinds of people, the only way to increase their fame is through negative news.
