Teacher in Zhenping County, Henan causes retinal detachment in primary school student through corporal punishment

by cdma73923 on 2011-05-12 12:21:07

The retina of the victim, Wang Tong, a 13-year-old student, is suspected to have detached due to being hit by a book from a female teacher.

Dahe News - On May 9th, reporters received another tip-off stating that in Chaye Primary School of Zhenping County in Nanyang City, a 13-year-old sixth-grade student lost sight in his right eye due to corporal punishment by his female head teacher for not memorizing an essay. Back on December 18th, 2008, in the fifth grade of Nieyang Sixth Primary School in Zhenping County, a 10-year-old primary school student was disfigured as his face was torn by his female head teacher for not completing homework. The victim's face required 52 stitches, and the school and teacher were disciplined and compensated the victim with 180,000 yuan. After receiving the tip-off again, reporters went to Zhenping County yesterday to investigate and understand this incident involving corporal punishment by the female head teacher.

A Student Punished by His Female Head Teacher for Not Memorizing an Essay

Yesterday afternoon, reporters met with the victim, Wang Tong (a pseudonym), at a two-story house on Zhongshan Street in Zhenping County. Wang Tong told reporters that he is 13 years old and a sixth-grade student at Chaye Primary School in Zhenping County. His parents run a shoe store on Zhongshan Street in the county town, and the family rents a small building behind the shoe store.

He recalled that on March 1st, after school, Ms. Guo Pengxiao, their Chinese language teacher and head teacher, assigned them a travelogue and required them to recite it the next day. During the second class on March 2nd, which was extracurricular activity time, students who could recite the text were reciting it to their group leaders. Due to playing around, Wang Tong failed to complete the recitation task. Another student named Xiao Yue (a pseudonym) also couldn't recite it. After the third class began, Ms. Guo called both of them to the podium and asked if they could recite it. Wang Tong said "yes," which made Ms. Guo very angry. She then questioned why he didn't recite if he could, and she picked up the essay textbook and slapped both of their faces a few times. While the teacher was hitting him with the book, he instinctively dodged backward, causing the book to hit his right eye. After being hit, they were penalized to stand for nearly ten minutes before returning to their seats.

After the incident, Wang Tong did not tell his parents because "teachers often beat us, and if my parents knew, I would be scolded at home." He said that his right eye started tearing up immediately after being hit, and it hurt for a while before gradually subsiding.

Half a Month Later, Retinal Detachment Found; Right Eye Vision Only 0.02

Wang Ling is Wang Tong's father. In the interview, he said that on the morning of March 16th, he received a call from the school's math teacher, saying that Wang Tong had not completed the previous day's math homework, hoping the parents would strengthen supervision. At noon when school let out, Wang Ling questioned his son why he didn't do the homework on time, and Wang Tong said that the homework was displayed on the electronic screen above the blackboard, and he couldn't see it clearly.

How is your eyesight? As parents, they dared not take it lightly, so he and his wife took their son to the Otorhinolaryngology Hospital in Zhenping County for examination that afternoon. The doctor who examined him said that the retina of his right eye had detached, and his vision was only 0.02, whereas his left eye's vision was 1.2 at the time.

The doctor informed Wang Ling that retinal detachment is generally associated with external force, and suggested asking the child if there had been any recent injuries to the eye. Under Wang Ling's persistent questioning, Wang Tong finally revealed that he had been physically punished by the teacher before.

Wang Ling said that on that afternoon, he went to the school to find Ms. Guo Pengxiao, who claimed "even if I hit him, I wouldn't hit him that hard." When he returned to the school the next day to inquire further, Ms. Guo outright denied ever hitting him.

On March 21st, Wang Ling took his son to the Provincial People's Hospital for surgery, costing nearly 20,000 yuan. Currently, with an 850-degree glasses prescription, Wang Tong's right eye vision can only be restored to 0.1.

Seeking Justice Leads to Unexpected Events; Family Members Violently Storm Police Station and Are Criminally Detained

In order to seek justice from the school for his injured son, Wang Ling and his wife, along with some relatives, blocked the school gate on March 20th, forcing the school to report the matter to the police. After receiving the report, Chengguan Town Police Station conducted an investigation and discovered that Wang Tong had indeed been physically punished.

Deputy Chief Liu Yongqiang of Chengguan Town Police Station introduced that the police had consulted the hospital, learning that the causes of retinal detachment are very complex, possibly caused by trauma or the patient's own condition. Therefore, they suggested that Wang Tong undergo a medical evaluation first to determine the cause of the retinal detachment. If it was determined to be caused by external force, a forensic evaluation would still be necessary to ascertain the nature of the case based on the injury.

On May 5th, Wang Ling and his wife took Wang Tong to the Third People's Hospital of Zhengzhou for a medical evaluation. It was then discovered that in the medical records Wang Tong brought, the prescription date was March 16th, consistent with the date of his visit to the Otorhinolaryngology Hospital in Zhenping County, but the ultrasound examination report mistakenly showed February 27th. Due to the mismatched dates, the evaluation was temporarily put on hold.

On the morning of May 6th, Wang Ling's family, who had been worried about their child's condition, lost control. They believed that the police station had altered the date on the ultrasound examination report, deliberately making the evaluation difficult, so they stormed the police station yard, breaking doors and verbally abusing and assaulting the officers until the special patrol police arrived and stopped Wang Ling's family's excessive behavior.

Afterward, Wang Ling's wife and younger brother were criminally detained by the police on suspicion of provoking trouble. With no resolution regarding his son's injury and two relatives now in detention, Wang Ling's mood plummeted to its lowest point.

School Meeting Instructs Students: Say Nothing

At around 5 p.m. yesterday, reporters went to Chaye Primary School in Zhenping County to gather information. A vice-principal surnamed Zhang stated that without approval from higher authorities, the school could not give interviews. After reporters left, this vice-principal called back, saying that regarding Wang Tong's injury, the child said the teacher hit him, but the teacher said she didn't. The school couldn't make a determination either. Currently, the police have intervened in the investigation, and once the results come out, whoever is responsible will bear the consequences. Vice-Principal Zhang added that after the parents caused a disturbance at the school, Ms. Guo Pengxiao has not come to work.
