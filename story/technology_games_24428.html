Shocking revelation of Tencent's new online game - QQ Flying Island

by yinyin on 2007-07-10 19:48:19

At the upcoming ChinaJoy 2007 gaming expo, Tencent is set to announce a new casual game title—QQ Flight Island! QQ Flight Island is a casual online game in the shooting genre developed in Korea and licensed by Tencent. It features cute Q-style graphics and is based on adventures and challenges taking place on the deserted island of "Nanaimo." The game adopts a flight-shooting combat style and integrates RPG elements such as a prop combination system, pet growth system, and housing system, creating an entirely new concept for casual online games!
