Henan Taikang traffic police were accused of arbitrary law enforcement and forced car scrapping

by anonymous on 2011-05-20 10:53:12

Wang Litan frankly said that the policemen should not have fined this car. After the incident, the county traffic police team compensated for Wang Jianli's economic loss of 50,000 yuan, and the scrappage center was also willing to purchase the car and compensate 32,000 yuan. However, due to Wang Jianli's high demands, it is still under coordination.

On May 17th, China News Service (reporter Qi Yong) reported that a Fukan sedan which had not reached its scrappage year and was still in normal use was identified as scrap by the traffic police of Taikang County, Henan Province, and then "revived". Today, Wang Litan, captain of the county traffic police team, told reporters from China News Service that the involved policeman made a mistake during law enforcement, and the County Public Security Bureau has severely reprimanded him. Meanwhile, the traffic police team has compensated for Wang Jianli's economic loss of 50,000 yuan. However, due to differences in compensation amounts between the scrappage center and the car owner, it is currently under coordination.

How can a car which has not reached its scrappage year and is still in normal use be scrapped so easily? Wang Jianli was very confused. In response, Wang Litan, captain of the Taikang County Traffic Police Team, told reporters from China News Service that the involved policeman made a mistake during law enforcement and did not send a letter to Beijing to verify whether the vehicle had reached its scrappage year. The County Public Security Bureau has given the involved policeman a serious warning and a major demerit.

After paying the fine, Wang Jianli was notified by the traffic police team that the vehicle had been confiscated, and he was issued a scrap car recovery certificate by Zhoukou Hui Xin Scrap Car Recycling Dismantling Co., Ltd. On November 23, 2010, the Taikang County Traffic Police Team showed Wang Jianli the cut chassis number beam and provided a scrap car dismantling information report form with complete vehicle information and pictures, indicating that the vehicle had been dismantled.

"Without clarifying the condition of the vehicle, the traffic police sent a car that should not have been scrapped to the scrappage center for scrapping. Is it enough to just receive a punishment for such a serious mistake?" "The mistake made by the traffic police is paid for by the traffic police team, but who is paying the bill for the traffic police team?" Some local residents were very dissatisfied with such arbitrary law enforcement behavior. (End)

According to reports, on November 3, 2010, Wang Guangming from Hepo Village, Longqu Town, Taikang County, drove a Fukan branded small sedan with the license plate number "Jing P200P7" back to his hometown from Beijing to visit relatives. When the car traveled to the second ring road of Taikang County, it was temporarily impounded by the traffic police of Taikang County on suspicion of theft. Subsequently, Wang Jianli, father of Wang Guangming and the registered owner of the car, carried the vehicle registration certificate, transaction invoice, etc., to find the involved policeman. After checking the vehicle documents, the involved policeman ruled out the suspicion of theft, but immediately changed the penalty to a fine of 1,000 yuan on the grounds that the car had no compulsory traffic insurance.
