**Position Summary:**

Comprehensively oversee the company's R&D and technical management, plan the technical development roadmap and new product development to achieve the company's innovation goals.

---

**Job Responsibilities:**

- Participate in formulating the company’s development strategy, annual business plans, and budget proposals.

- Lead research into the latest technological advancements in the industry and develop the technical strategic planning.

- Manage the company’s core technologies, organize and implement major technical decisions and solutions.

- Monitor and ensure the timely execution of the technical strategic plan.

- Lead the departments under your jurisdiction in developing and implementing annual work plans to achieve annual objectives.

- Oversee equipment selection, prototyping, improvement, and production line layout for new product projects.

- Make key decisions on the company’s technical development direction and plan its product portfolio.

- Guide and review overall technical solutions for projects, ensuring quality assessments are completed.

- Engage in technical exchanges with customers to understand their technical and business development requirements, and answer product-related technical questions.

- Track potential or specific projects and clients, manage site visits, technical discussions, proposal creation, and contract negotiations within the region.

- Develop training programs for technical personnel and organize technical training for other relevant staff within the company.

**Qualifications:**

**Educational Background:**

- Master's degree or above in a field related to the industry.

**Training Experience:**

- Training in strategic management, management capability development, project management, innovation management, and contract law.

**Experience:**

- More than 5 years of research and development and project management experience in the industry.

**Skills and Competencies:**

- Proficient in the latest technical methods within the industry.

- Ability to grasp industry technology trends and business development directions, with unique insights into key technologies.

- Strong creativity, expansion ability, abstract thinking, and project management skills.

- Familiarity with the structure, performance, principles, and usage methods of the company’s products, backed by solid theoretical foundations and technical work experience.

**Attitude:**

- Strong judgment, decision-making, planning, and execution capabilities.

- Excellent communication, coordination, organizational, and team-building skills.

- High level of work enthusiasm and good professional ethics.
