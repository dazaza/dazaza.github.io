They and they came to the seat beside her husband at the round table, and she handed over the house key to him with a pleasant face. He knew she only had one key and she stayed at home every day, so she didn't need another drawer key for her exclusive use. Sometimes he helped her lock the drawer and put the key in another unlocked drawer. Those times mainly happened when their daughter's classmates played at home for a long time, or when their whole family returned to their hometown for holidays and no one was at home. Afterwards, he would open the drawer again, put the key back in the unlocked drawer, restoring it to her preferred casual state.

She handed the house key to him, he was stunned for a second, then immediately said: "Sorry, sorry, my buddies are happy today. I was just saying I should go home." She turned back and called the dog: "Baby, say goodbye to daddy, we're leaving."

Her husband got nervous and said: "Let's go together, I was going to go home anyway." That group of middle-aged men all chimed in helpfully saying: "Old Lin said he should go home earlier, really." She didn't answer because she saw her two younger brothers playing cards here. It was originally very noisy here, and also because she hadn't lost her temper, their appearance didn't attract the attention of people at other tables. At this point, her tears fell like broken beads. She finally couldn't control herself and shouted: "Younger brother, if you two want to die, go home first and kill our parents, so they won't end up losing their sons in old age after working hard all their lives. If you want to die, do it quickly. Don't engage in this kind of slow suicide. Leave your wives with a bit of youth, don't make them old and unattractive, and make it hard for them to remarry."

She felt sorry for the natural gas and tap water, impulsively wanting to turn off the water valve and gas valve several times, but she didn't want to see his appearance. When she had no strength left to feel sorry, he turned off the faucet. However, he was still vomiting. She was exhausted by the sound, but she just lay there, not even thinking of taking care of him. She kept chanting spells: "Die, die..." When all the sounds disappeared, she suddenly stopped chanting, then her soul scattered, she slept without any signs of life...

He was dead. She thought. He fell in the bathtub next to the toilet, naked.

She always seized every opportunity for disinfection. After filling the water bottle for her daughter to take to school, she went back to bed as usual. When the wall clock in the study struck seven, her daughter said: "Mom, I'm going to school." She said: "Be careful on the way." The metal security door clanged, and her daughter went to school. She felt his carotid artery, there was no sign of pulse. The dog's front paws rested on the edge of the bathtub, its head turning left and right, looking at her curiously, then sniffing, wagging its tail, then letting out a hum and retreating to the door.
