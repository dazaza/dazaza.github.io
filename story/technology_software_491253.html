Look at Nalan's poetry

by haintaozny on 2011-09-18 20:53:16

I am fond of Nalan Rongruo's poems. I admire his name: Nalan, Na, embracing all rivers and accommodating everything; Lan, the orchid in the deep valley, fragrant and unconventional. Rongruo, with a demeanor and temperament that stands out from the crowd. For a long time, I unconsciously deified Nalan. I admired his extraordinary talent and lamented his untimely death.

I sing to the moon in hesitation, my shadow dances in disorder!

I wander alone, my heart tightly closed. I cry by myself, I fall in love with sadness. When I read Nalan's lines "Awake is boring, drunk is boring, dreams never reach Xieqiao", I feel an overwhelming sorrow. After Daiyu left this world, Bao Yu once dreamed of her, but she could not come into his dreams. I also wanted to dream of that youthful figure, but unfortunately, the dream was vague and distant. Young people don't know the taste of sorrow, I want to get drunk to forget all sorrows, but alas, the wine turns into tears of longing in my sorrowful intestines!

Finally, we each went our separate ways, not because our feelings faded or hit a dead end. But rather, we couldn't cross the distance standing before us. He said he couldn't give me happiness, so he chose to smile and bless me. For survival, he wandered everywhere. To find warmth, he got married and had children.

When I'm melancholy, I read Nalan's poems, listen to sad music, let my heart sink to the bottom of sadness, then when I surface, I feel refreshed. Although I have long distanced myself from sadness, it occasionally still blows towards me like the wind.

Who flips through the sorrowful tunes of the Music Bureau? The wind whistles, the rain falls, and the lamp wick burns down another night. I don't know what weighs on my heart, awake is dull, drunk is dull, and dreams never reach Xieqiao.

Another tune that haunts me is "Caosangzi."

Friends still remember saying meaningfully, if you met Nalan, you would be obsessed. Would I fall into an abyss of no return? I smiled faintly. No, Nalan is just a beautiful myth that has passed, Nalan cannot be replicated. If I love, I prefer real affection. I want to love ordinary mortals in this world.

Who thinks of the lonely coolness of the west wind? The yellow leaves rustle as they close the sparse windows. Thinking about past events under the setting sun. Don't be startled by the heavy spring sleep caused by drinking, the joy of gambling books is worth spilling tea. At the time, it seemed so ordinary.

Reading Nalan's verses, my tears flow in my heart, forming a river. And the pain in my heart, for a long time, had nowhere to be expressed. My inner world, I didn't want others to delve into. Dancing alone in sorrow, that's all there was. Not caring whether others understood, not minding their strange looks. My sad dance shadows are scattered all over the floor.

Once, I lay awake all night, writing about my longing and sadness. Though not every word was a tear, every sentence blood, it was still filled with deep emotion. And he, after receiving my words, could only laugh sadly.

The first impression that deeply affected me was a tune recalling the past, "Huanxisha."

That period of the past carried too much beauty and bitterness from youth.

Suddenly looking back, the talented lover who loved me was right beside me. He was passionate and gentle, yet not lacking in the fiery passion of a man, just the kind of excellent man I envisioned.

Reading these desolate lines, I also recall the events of the past. Once, vows of eternal love have long become pale and empty in the passage of time, without trace, not reminisced, not touched, only a vast expanse.

As the lamp wick burns down, night after night, where does the dream return?

No longer sad, I sweetly sleep on his arm. Years of pain, years of frost, instantly melt away.

In those days, the past me and him were like most lovers, thinking everything was so natural and ordinary. Only years later, leaning against the west wind, watching the yellow leaves fly, seeing the blood-red sunset, thoughts heavy, did we realize the beauty of the past was gone. That belonged to the green campus, a naive and pure beautiful love.

The book in my hands is also heavy, remembering the past. Thus, I fell in love with this poem, and also with Nalan's poetry.

Once, I waited for a call in the middle of the night. Then, the wind was strong, the rain urgent. We couldn't transcend space and time, he was at one end, and I was at the other. My voice began to choke, and he couldn't give me a warm hug.
