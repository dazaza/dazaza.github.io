[Reprint] Handle interpersonal relationships well, and let your work and life be full of fun

by koucai4472 on 2012-03-05 15:03:50

Interpersonal communication is a science. Good interpersonal relationships can make people feel like a fish in water, handling any task with ease and simplicity, often becoming the object of respect for others. On the other hand, poor interpersonal relationships can exhaust someone, making them look weary and full of sorrow, eventually leading to loss of confidence and even extreme behavior and thoughts. "Interpersonal" is fundamental. As social animals, humans cannot escape the interactions between people, which is a universally known truth. Overcoming one's own flaws and maintaining good interpersonal relationships is the most important task.

First, Communication: Communication is an essential part of interpersonal relationships and also a spiritual longing for people. Through communication, people understand each other and strengthen their relationships. Communication can be expressed through language and actions. It is an interaction between two parties, not a one-sided monologue. Communication should not always emphasize personal opinions but should pay more attention to the feelings of the other party, which is considered good communication.

Second, Privacy: Interpersonal relationships require attention to privacy protection, or distance. Distance creates beauty and security. Maintaining an appropriate distance can avoid unnecessary embarrassment and shows respect for both parties, elevating interpersonal relationships to a higher level.

Third, Purpose: Human energy is limited. To eliminate the dross and take the essence, it is necessary to conduct some screening and categorization. This helps you manage your interpersonal relationships and avoids unnecessary emotional investment that could lead to emotional setbacks. Therefore, having clear objectives is important. Although this may seem very utilitarian, investing more effort and time in more efficient areas is completely worthwhile. However, sincerity should always come first and should never be violated at any time.

Fourth, Presentation: Appropriately showcasing oneself and increasing exposure naturally leaves a good impression rather than a negative one. Displaying one's strengths will earn trust and admiration from others. People who are trustworthy usually have the most friends.

What is the most valuable thing in the 21st century? - "Interpersonal". Only a good interpersonal relationship network can help you achieve twice the result with half the effort. If you don't want to be eliminated by the times and want to stay on track with others, mastering interpersonal relationships is crucial.
