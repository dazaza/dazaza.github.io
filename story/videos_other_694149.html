Canvas bags combine the elements of environmental protection and fashion.

by katousheng on 2012-03-06 22:28:31

Canvas bags combine the elements of environmental protection and fashion. With their pure cotton texture and colorful patterns, they have already won the favor of a wide range of consumers. The full-cotton canvas bags feature unique pattern designs, large capacity, comprehensive functions, and convenient use. Especially during the manufacturing process, advanced techniques and strict quality control ensure that these non-woven eco-friendly bags receive consistent praise from a broad consumer base.

Canvas bags have been sold in Japan for many years and have recently started entering the Chinese market. We present to Chinese consumers the styles that have been best-sellers in Japan up until now. These original Japanese pure cotton canvas bags uniquely integrate fashionable trends from both Japan and Europe and America. The sales volume of canvas bags in Japan has been rapidly increasing. The Hebei Haozi design team works hard every day to design styles you'll love. These bags, which incorporate international fashion trends, have been featured in multiple Japanese fashion magazines and media reports. We hope that through our efforts, we can add more color and happiness to your daily life.

This canvas bag has a large capacity and can hold many items such as computers, documents, fashion magazines, makeup bags, etc. Its humanized design is both practical and stylish. Please note that due to manual production, there may be some size discrepancies, so please understand. Because of its large capacity, it's not only useful for leisure or shopping but also suitable for 2-3 day business trips or travel. It can be carried by hand, over the shoulder, placed in a bicycle basket, or hung on a motorcycle handle without damaging the canvas material. It's truly convenient and practical. Models showcase various bags with a sense of satisfaction.

The canvas bag is an eco-friendly cloth bag, known for being compact, durable, and non-polluting. Its biggest advantage is that it can be reused, thus significantly reducing environmental pollution. Canvas bags are currently very popular internationally. Canvas is derived from natural cotton, and most eco-friendly cotton bags are minimally dyed, making them environmentally friendly at the source. Additionally, because the fabric of cotton bags is of higher quality than non-woven fabric, companies and organizations that choose them generally prioritize environmental protection and have the financial strength to do so. Cotton bags are biodegradable and cause no environmental pollution. Their durability far exceeds that of non-woven fabric, and their fine texture allows for excellent printing results, surpassing those of non-woven fabric. The softness of the fabric makes it easy to fold and carry, and cleaning is simpler compared to non-woven fabric. This type of bag is most suitable as a shopping bag.

**Process Advantages:**

- Lowest printing cost

- Faster production speed

- Any pattern can be printed

- Smaller gradient colors or colored patterns are best suited for this process

**Applicable Scope:**

- Any pattern can be printed

- Minimum order quantity is relatively high, usually above 3000 pieces

**Lamination Printing Introduction:**

- Can print any pattern, even complex prints can be achieved.

- High printing plate costs, which depend on the area and number of colors used for the plate.

- Printing costs are proportional to the size of the printed area; lamination printing is suitable when the quantity is large and the printed area is extensive.
