Man presents banner of inaction to Public Security Bureau (Picture)

Reporters at Xianyang told us that "After that young man came, he unfolded the banner and stood in front of the gate of Xingping Public Security Bureau and let everyone see it. We can clearly see that there are several words on it saying 'malfeasance and inaction'." On February 3rd, a young man gave a banner of "inaction" to the staff of Xingping Public Security Bureau. Yesterday afternoon, we learned from Xingping Public Security Bureau that the bureau has started to investigate this matter. The police have established a special investigation team to thoroughly investigate the fight incident. Another merchant nearby told reporters that they later also saw this incident online and in the bar, but everyone does not understand why this young man did so. On the afternoon of February 5th, a post appeared on Huashang Network (Weibo) Xianyang Forum. The content is only a group of pictures: a bespectacled young man holding a banner with the words "malfeasance and inaction". In the picture, many people surrounded the man who gave the banner, full of doubts. The Public Security Bureau received the "inaction" banner. Yesterday afternoon, when I went to Xingping to learn about this matter, a merchant near the Municipal Public Security Bureau said that on February 3rd, the Xingping Public Security Bureau had cracked a criminal case, and someone set off firecrackers at the gate of the Public Security Bureau to express celebration and gratitude. "Just as the previous matter was over, a young man came, holding a banner standing at the gate of the Public Security Bureau. At that time, everyone thought it might be another person giving a banner to express gratitude. But when we approached, everyone stopped - the banner wrote 'malfeasance and inaction'. This young man is too bold. Later, a colleague from the Public Security Bureau took the banner." Relevant leaders of the Xingping Public Security Bureau yesterday expressed that regarding the incident where the party sent the "inaction" banner, the bureau attaches great importance to it, a special meeting was held in the morning to study this matter, and a decision was made: led by the discipline inspection secretary of the Xingping Public Security Bureau, a comprehensive investigation will be carried out regarding the party's complaint about the police "inaction", to understand whether the police really have "inaction" in this public security case; led by Deputy Director Zhang Zhicai, an investigation team composed of officers from the Legal Affairs Department, Public Security Brigade, and Xiwu Police Station was established to thoroughly investigate this fight incident; the handling situation of the case by the Public Security Bureau will be notified to the party Yuan. According to the introduction of the responsible person, the person who sent the banner is named Yuan, from Xingping City. The reason stems from a public security case last year. It is reported that at around 8:00 am on March 5, 2011, Yuan had a conflict with a bus driver on his way to work in Xianyang and was beaten and injured. Subsequently, the officers of the Xiwu Police Station of the Xingping Public Security Bureau came and handled the matter. Yuan was hospitalized for treatment at that time. Relevant persons of the Xingping Public Security Bureau introduced the situation and said that the police were not inactive in this matter, "After the police coordinated, the other party has compensated for Yuan's medical expenses. The reason he gave the banner is that the person who beat him has not yet been dealt with by the police. At that time, because there were many people involved in the fight, the scene was chaotic, and there was no other relevant evidence material on-site to determine the main responsible person, at the same time, Yuan's injury identification has not concluded, so it brought certain difficulties for the police to handle the relevant responsible persons and define the nature of the case." Yesterday, Yuan, who gave the banner, said that he did not want to say more about this matter, but he said that he understands and is satisfied with the police's current active handling attitude. Relevant responsible persons of the Xingping Public Security Bureau told reporters that due to the sudden occurrence of the incident, the staff subsequently took the young man who gave the banner to the petition office of the Public Security Bureau. After a brief inquiry about the relevant situation, the situation was promptly fed back to the Discipline Inspection Committee of the Xingping Public Security Bureau.
