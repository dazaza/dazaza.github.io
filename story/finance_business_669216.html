Louis Vuitton Monogram Canvas Sac Chasse - Louis Vuitton

by swatk386 on 2012-02-19 10:31:56

Louis Vuitton Monogram Canvas Sac Chasse, The LV Monogram Canvas Sac Chasse is part of the luggage collection within the Monogram Canvas-based family along with other LV items. This bag belongs to a family that shares similarities with the stylish 50 Eole LV Eole, the 55 Keepall Louis, the fairly large Cruiser Bag, and the elegant Heures LV Alize, among other laptop bags. Over the past decade or so, I have had the opportunity to use a wide variety of these Louis Vuitton items. However, out of all of them, I must admit that it's the LV Monogram that has captivated me the most. When you first lay eyes on the LV Monogram Canvas Sac Chasse, the first thing you notice is probably its size; this is quite a large bag. Indeed, this is what you would expect from a 'luggage' piece - which is exactly how the Louis Monogram Canvas Sac Chasse is classified. Objectively speaking, the Louis Monogram measures approximately 19 inches in length, around 16 inches in height, and about 9 inches in width, providing ample space for a good amount of 'luggage.' Color-wise, the LV Monogram Canvas Sac Chasse can be described as an elegant bag. Its primary color is dark brown, adorned with cream-colored crosses throughout; some are 'free-standing' while others are encased in spheres. The result is a bag that exudes sophistication, the kind you would expect to be a functional 'high-end' bag - even before seeing the 'Louis Vuitton' label. The material used to construct the Sac Chasse, as the name suggests, is Monogram Canvas; a substance renowned for its durability and elegance when properly painted. The fact that the Vuitton Monogram Canvas Sac Chasse is made from Monogram canvas is one of the features that make it suitable for use as a 'luggage' bag. Other features include a shoulder strap mechanism, making it one of the few carriers to come with a shoulder strap, and a handle that is rolled for comfort. With a removable ID tag (the type airlines use for luggage identification), the LV Monogram makes an excellent travel bag, especially for someone 'traveling light.' For items that you don't want to mix with the rest, the LV Canvas Sac Chasse comes with additional side compartments. The hardware on the Monogram Canvas Sac Chasse, including the buckles, is likely gold-colored - just like many LV products, the 'gold color' here is the kind that lasts long. For securing the bag, Vuitton provides customers with both a zipper and a set of buckles.

Louis Vuitton Monogram Canvas Sac Chasse - whether referred to as ルイ ヴィトン, ヴィトン ダミエ, or any other variation - remains a timeless classic in the world of luxury travel and fashion.
