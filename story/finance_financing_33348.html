Online accounting websites are receiving increasing attention and support from the media and white-collar workers!

With the strengthening of netizens' financial management awareness and the impact of skyrocketing prices, more and more "moonlight clan" members are joining the ranks of online accounting and financial management. This is either due to the market driving the emergence of online accounting websites or perhaps the appearance of these websites has spurred the development of the online accounting market.

Since China's earliest online accounting website — Caike Online (www.coko365.com) officially launched on February 10, 2006, and has been successfully operating until now, it marks that China's online accounting market has developed for over a year. After Caike Online was introduced, within half a year, online accounting websites in China sprang up like mushrooms after rain. Currently, there are nearly 50 online accounting websites of various sizes in China. Although the online accounting market is vast and there are many websites, they have not yet formed a scale. Therefore, the quality of online accounting websites varies greatly, making it difficult to distinguish between good and bad ones.

Caike Online, after more than a year of market accumulation, understands China's online accounting market most profoundly. Caike Online has undergone multiple renovations, with each renovation and new function development based on member feedback about the website. Caike Online actively collects members' opinions and suggestions on the website, including a customer message board section for convenient submission of suggestions. It is precisely due to the active participation of members that Caike Online was the first to develop distinctive functions for online accounting websites, such as an underlying architecture based on the internationally recognized "double-entry bookkeeping method," one-time borrowing and multiple repayments, powerful creditor-debtor management, rich report statistics functions with text and images, shopping plans, timed reminder functions, scheduled tasks, "automatic bookkeeping," "click-and-schedule," "input-and-check" functions, "data export" functions, unlimited levels of income and expenditure categories, one family with multiple accounts for separate bookkeeping, and a thoughtful "project management" function specifically designed for small and medium-sized enterprises.
