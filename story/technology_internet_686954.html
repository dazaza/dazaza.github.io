Feel the warmth! 16 (This is also warmth)

by obanma520 on 2012-03-02 12:07:39

February 12, 2012, Sunday, Sunny to Overcast

The temperature has just started to rise a bit, but now it's overcast again. The forecast says there will be a cold wave soon. No wonder Grass's leg was uncomfortable in the middle of the night yesterday, which woke her up and made her restless. She didn't fall back asleep until around four o'clock in the morning, so she woke up late this morning!

On the previous day, after washing clothes, Grass went online to take a break. Her son called, saying he and his colleagues were on a business trip and eating outside. They would come home for a short while later! Oh, since the fifth day of the first lunar month, her son rushed back to his mother-in-law's place with his wife and stayed until the fifteenth day. For the whole Spring Festival period, this young couple had no worries about food or drink, living quite comfortably (dialect)! Haha!

Her son came home without even changing shoes, taking a big step to sit down on the sofa. Grass could tell from his demeanor that he wouldn't stay long. After a brief chat, he said that due to some regulations at work, they were still short by one thousand yuan for a transaction, so he came back to ask if Grass had any money available. She quickly gave him what he needed, explaining that the car was waiting downstairs and they had to rush back in the afternoon. Thus, this visit was quick and hurried!

From her son's words, it seemed that his job responsibilities would be adjusted in the new year, meaning he would travel less to this area. This also implied fewer opportunities for him to return home, making it harder to see him. Therefore, Grass felt a sense of melancholy knowing her son would have fewer chances to enjoy her cooking.

These past few days, there hasn't been much important work, but many trivial matters. Yesterday being Saturday, because her grandson asked for dumplings on the Lantern Festival night, Grass bought good chives at the supermarket and made vegetable dumplings. She froze 30 or more dumplings on a tray, which she let them take away today! Due to the busy schedule before the New Year, she didn't have time to perm her bangs, so she went to Pure Passion Romantica Comic during her lunch break. Surprisingly, the price increased by five yuan, costing twenty yuan for just a small section of hair! Haha!

Today is Sunday, and her daughter and grandson are going to class, returning home for lunch. There's also a relative's child (the one who delivers pork) coming over for treatment after the New Year celebrations. So, Grass went to the market for the first time after the holiday, even though it was already ten o'clock. Despite the late start, the market was still bustling with people!

As she crossed the street, her phone rang. It was her teacher from school, who had a significant influence on Grass during her studies and often visited her workplace. Now that Grass has retired, they don't see each other as often. Maybe because Grass changed her number, they lost contact for over a year. Before the New Year, he called, saying he got Grass's number from a colleague. He mentioned seeing a book of essays where he found some of Grass's articles. Upon learning Grass lived nearby, he said his daughter also lived in the same neighborhood. Grass immediately invited him to call when visiting her daughter and stop by for a chat! Therefore, Grass sent New Year greetings to her teacher during the holidays. Today, the teacher called to ask: First, what does Grass mainly do after retirement? Second, whether she would like to join their elderly association. Grass replied that she hadn't done much substantial work, only handling trivial tasks, doing housework, and wandering around. Then Grass asked about the association's situation. The teacher said: Since you're not working anymore, participating in these organizations can be meaningful for your retired life. I am also a member of the association, and I believe your conditions meet the requirements. Not everyone can join; we have to review applications. Oh, yes, as the teacher said, it's an opportunity to learn from seniors, expand social contacts, and make retirement life more colorful. But how to handle the relevant procedures? "When there's an event, I'll notify you. You can participate and decide afterward!" The teacher knows Grass well, so he suggested this out of concern. Why wouldn't Grass be happy about such a good thing? Haha!

Last night, Grass took meat dishes out of the fridge. Today at the market, nothing caught her fancy, but seeing the newly arrived garlic sprouts, which were the thin kind she liked, she decided to buy them!

This vegetable must have come from the south, so it's rare and definitely not cheap - 7.50 yuan, reasonable! But the bundles weighed over a pound each, which seemed excessive. A little dish would be enough to try the fresh taste! A man picked up a smaller bundle, looked at it, and put it down. What happened? Grass saw that the outer layer of skin at the bottom was completely rubbed off. That man said, "Don't say the 'bundle' is too small!" Grass agreed, "Then let's split this bundle!" "Can we do that? Let's see if they agree," the man asked Grass. "If we both agree, why wouldn't they?" So, half for 3.50 yuan, placed in a bag, the other half for 4.50 yuan. "Which half do you want?" Grass politely asked him. "It doesn't matter, it's only a one-yuan difference!" "Oh, then I'll take this half!" Grass thought since there weren't many people eating lunch and her son-in-law wasn't coming back, one dish would be sufficient. This method of "split-buying" is really great! Haha!

According to her daughter, as soon as they entered the door, her grandson exclaimed, "Wow! Grandma has cleaned the house so neatly!" Haha, see? The grandson who used to talk late now speaks very fluently, haha!

The fatty meat was given by relatives-in-law. The garlic sprouts were tender, almost nothing wasted, making it very economical and delicious with rice!

The grandson said he was hungry upon seeing Grass, as he didn't like eating reheated rice during class in the morning. So, she fried dumplings. "I want to eat six!" The child ate all six, showing he was truly hungry. When eating rice, he barely touched it, not even looking at the meat he usually liked.

The platter contained three kinds of braised dishes left over from the New Year. The larger pieces were taken by her daughter-in-law back to her parents' home. After the New Year, just seeing meat was enough, but after steaming it today, it still smelled good, although not much was eaten. The green vegetable was very popular. What was it? It's wild greens called "huihui cai"! Haha, only Grass's family stored so many wild vegetables and green vegetables! Haha!

A soup made with meatballs fried by relatives-in-law, soft tofu, and Chinese cabbage! Simple and light, it was all consumed! Haha!
