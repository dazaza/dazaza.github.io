Events that will happen in 2009...

by leni on 2009-01-01 21:13:46

January

1st - The Provisional Regulations of the People's Republic of China on Business Tax came into effect.

1st - The Provisional Regulations of the People's Republic of China on Consumption Tax came into effect.

1st - The Provisional Regulations of the People's Republic of China on Value-Added Tax came into effect.

1st - The Law of the People's Republic of China on Promoting Circular Economy came into effect.

1st - The State Council decided to implement a refined oil tax reform, abolishing six charges previously levied outside the price of refined oil: highway maintenance fees, channel maintenance fees, highway transport management fees, highway passenger and freight surcharges, waterway transport management fees, and water transport passenger and freight surcharges. It will gradually and orderly cancel tolls for government-loan secondary highways; at the same time, the unit tax amount of gasoline consumption tax levied within the price will be increased by 0.8 yuan per liter, that is, from 0.2 yuan per liter to 1 yuan; the unit tax amount of diesel consumption tax per liter will be increased by 0.7 yuan, that is, from 0.1 yuan per liter to 0.8 yuan; the unit tax amounts of other refined oil consumption taxes will be correspondingly increased.

1st - The allowance standard for academicians of the Chinese Academy of Sciences and the Chinese Academy of Engineering was adjusted from 200 yuan per person per month to 1000 yuan per person per month.

1st - Hong Kong TVB launched its interactive news digital channel.

15th - Mobile and Unicom began implementing unified pricing for intra-network and inter-network services.

20th - Obama was inaugurated as the first African-American president in U.S. history.

February

18th - The opening of the 24th Winter Universiade.

20th - Electronic Arts released "The Sims 3".

March

5th - The preliminary Asian qualifying matches for the World Baseball Classic will start at Tokyo Dome in Japan, with the Japanese team playing against the Chinese team.

16th - Three people embarked on the Silk Road.

April

6th - Major League Baseball in the United States will begin its season on this day.

April 10-16 - China 2009 World Philatelic Exhibition (Luoyang, Henan)
