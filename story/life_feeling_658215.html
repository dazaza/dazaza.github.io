After the Summer, A Lifetime of Estrangement

Online Submission

The mottled light seeping through the curtains into the living room, a brightness amidst the gloom, vivid and blinding. In the quiet afternoon, a cup of coffee at the end of the computer, hiding away.

Many emotions linger in my chest, yet they are more than just wounds.

When online, netizens say I only respond to people I know, but in fact, I am just an idle person, tapping on the keyboard, finding a better way than talking to a mirror. I am deeply moved by the few good friends who always leave me messages and chat nonsense with me. Sincerely thank you for your comfort when I am disheartened.

December, year's end.

Looking at the daily visitor records of my space, the number of people is increasing, except for Douzi. Almost all are strangers. I don't know them, and they don't know me. Suddenly, a trace of sadness swept through my heart. In the past, I never paid attention to strangers, but now I irrevocably entrust myself to one stranger after another. Thus, many classmates and friends who I haven't contacted have been added to my blacklist. For those I know, I don't think the blacklist is a kind of hurt. Because we both no longer care about each other, we don't chat, we don't visit each other's spaces. Keeping QQ accounts, what does it even mean? But if I delete from my side, the other side still retains my space updates. It's better to be thoroughly forgotten, return to being strangers, without any attachment.

I have a heavy suspicion, I am not sure if all this is real. I don't know if everyone treats me sincerely. I am very afraid to answer such questions. Too many people leaving has repeatedly destroyed the little belief left in my heart. Actually, I also know there's no reason to question it.

Staying in one place for too long, I start to feel that it is the entire world. Relying on one person for too long, I don't want to leave. I'm very afraid of losing. The favored can always act without fear, but the abandoned are timid and startled.

Roses have thorns, yet for the sake of mutual warmth, they still bloom joyfully.

In these fleeting years, we hastily meet, then turn around and say goodbye, thus becoming an accident in each other's memories. You come and go, filling and emptying my heart, leaving me standing in place, quietly reminiscing. Yet, unknowingly, you are gradually forgetting me. Beyond redemption, I march forward against time, but can no longer find the initial happiness. Finally, I close my tearful eyes and smile in disappointment.

I know, actually everyone's life will slowly pass by like this. Now worrying about exams, and in the blink of an eye, we must resolutely rush towards a mature and cold life. Test papers folded into paper airplanes, softening and deforming in a downpour. That rain that lasted the whole night soaked everyone's youth into damp, soggy memories. But, tell me, what kind of mood should I use to recall you? What kind of way should I use to cherish you? Children's happiness is always very obvious, yet simple. If only we could be like them, how wonderful it would be! Actually, if there was no beginning, it would have been better. Once having seen the vast ocean, other waters are hard to impress, because you came, so I cannot erase my memories. Like paper, once creased, it's hard to flatten again.

Those warm scenes will never return. Never, ever again. Although they can never return, the original emotion has always remained. But, is it merely my exclusive memory?

Dogs won't lose weight because they don't miss, humans do because they are missing someone, like stray dogs wandering through the ages.

Burying love deep in the heart, perhaps one day it will sprout a dream again. And before that, it needs to carefully hide its shine, striving not to be discovered by others before your dull arrival.

And so, placing oneself in a well-guarded castle. No approach or being approached. Only personal experiences fill the originally empty life.

Walking alone in the wind, slowly getting used to, getting used to nights spent drinking coffee alone.

After the road is finished, loneliness remains in the end.

Heart broken, shattered for many years.

Saying goodbye to oneself. People around, have already walked far away.

Gradually falling in love with the faint solitude, walking on that endless path...

Time passes, circumstances change. Time moves on, probably leading to changes in our situations. We are all growing rapidly, unable to reclaim our former selves.

Goodbye. 2010. Year's end.
