To soothe his longing for Jinan and to foster a bond between his new wife and children, he persuaded his wife to get off the train and explore Jinan, known for its artistic heritage. JiPing, captivated by the local folk songs she heard for the first time, wandered further away with her brother, forgetting the time. As they were enjoying themselves, Zhang Zongchang's convoy passed by, causing chaos among the crowd. JiPing, holding onto her brother ZiJian, was swept away by the crowd. Upon realizing the commotion, Xia Xizun rushed to the streets but could only watch helplessly as his children disappeared into the crowd. JiPing tightly held onto ZiJian's hand, but after the chaos subsided, they found themselves lost.
