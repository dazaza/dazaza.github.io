Smuggling defense, Amendment (VII) of the Criminal Law of the People's Republic of China

by lilan2333 on 2009-11-27 11:44:08

I. The third paragraph of Article 151 of the Criminal Law shall be revised as follows: "For smuggling rare plants and their products or other goods and items prohibited from import or export by the State, the offender shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than five years or criminal detention, and/or fined; in serious cases, smugglers' defense, the offender shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than five years, and a fine."

II. The first paragraph of Article 180 of the Criminal Law shall be revised as follows: "For personnel who are informed of insider information on securities and futures transactions or those who illegally obtain such insider information, if they buy or sell securities before the issuance of securities, securities and futures transactions, or other information that has a significant impact on the price of securities and futures transactions is made public, or engage in futures transactions related to such insider information, or disclose such information, or explicitly or implicitly suggest others to engage in the aforementioned trading activities, in serious cases, they shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than five years or criminal detention, and/or fined one to five times the illegal gains; in particularly serious cases, they shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than five years but not more than ten years, and fined one to five times the illegal gains."

An additional paragraph shall be added as the fourth paragraph: "Employees of financial institutions such as stock exchanges, futures exchanges, securities companies, futures brokerage companies, fund management companies, commercial banks, insurance companies, and staff members of relevant regulatory authorities or industry associations who use non-public information obtained through job convenience other than insider information, violate regulations, engage in securities and futures trading activities related to such information, or explicitly or implicitly suggest others to engage in related trading activities, in serious cases, shall be punished according to the provisions of the first paragraph."

III. Article 201 of the Criminal Law shall be revised as follows: "Taxpayers who make false tax declarations or fail to declare taxes by means of deception or concealment, evade a large amount of taxes payable and account for more than 10% of the taxes payable, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than three years or criminal detention, and fined, criminal defense; if the amount evaded is huge and accounts for more than 30% of the taxes payable, they shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than three years but not more than seven years, and fined."

"If withholding agents adopt the means mentioned in the preceding paragraph and fail to pay or underpay the withheld or collected taxes in a large amount, they shall be punished according to the provisions of the preceding paragraph, death penalty defense lawyer."

"For repeated acts as mentioned in the preceding two paragraphs without handling, the cumulative amount shall be calculated."

"For those who commit the acts mentioned in the first paragraph, after receiving the legally issued notice of recovery from the tax authority, if they make up for the taxes payable, pay the overdue fines, and have been subject to administrative penalties, they shall not be held criminally responsible; however, those who have received criminal penalties for evading taxes within five years or have been given administrative penalties twice or more by the tax authority are excluded."

IV. A provision shall be added after Article 224 of the Criminal Law as Article 224-1: "For organizing and leading pyramid selling activities under the guise of selling goods or providing services, requiring participants to pay fees or purchase goods or services to obtain membership qualifications, forming hierarchical levels in a certain order, directly or indirectly using the number of recruited people as the basis for remuneration or return, luring or coercing participants to continue recruiting others, defrauding property, and disrupting economic and social order, the offender shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than five years or criminal detention, and fined; in serious cases, they shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than five years, and fined."

V. The third item of Article 225 of the Criminal Law shall be revised as follows: "Engaging in illegal operations of securities, futures, and insurance businesses without the approval of relevant national authorities, or engaging in illegal capital payment and settlement businesses;"

VI. Article 239 of the Criminal Law shall be revised as follows: "For kidnapping others with the purpose of extorting money, or kidnapping others as hostages, the offender shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than ten years or life imprisonment, and fined or have their property confiscated; in milder cases, they shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than five years but not more than ten years, and fined."

"For committing the crime mentioned in the preceding paragraph, resulting in the death of the kidnapped person or killing the kidnapped person, the offender shall be sentenced to death, and their property confiscated."

"For stealing infants with the purpose of extorting money, the offender shall be punished according to the provisions of the preceding two paragraphs."

VII. A provision shall be added after Article 253 of the Criminal Law as Article 253-1: "Staff members of state organs or financial, telecommunications, transportation, education, medical and other units who violate state regulations, sell or illegally provide citizens' personal information obtained during the performance of duties or provision of services by their units, in serious cases, they shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than three years or criminal detention, and/or fined."

"Stealing or obtaining the aforementioned information through other methods, in serious cases, the offender shall be punished according to the provisions of the preceding paragraph."

"For units committing the crimes mentioned in the preceding two paragraphs, the unit shall be fined, and the persons directly responsible and other directly responsible personnel shall be punished according to the provisions of each paragraph."

VIII. A provision shall be added after Article 262-1 of the Criminal Law as Article 262-2: "For organizing minors to engage in theft, fraud, robbery, extortion and other activities violating public security management, the offender shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than three years or criminal detention, and fined; in serious cases, they shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than three years but not more than seven years, and fined."

IX. Two paragraphs shall be added to Article 285 of the Criminal Law as the second and third paragraphs: "In violation of state regulations, intruding into computer information systems other than those specified in the preceding paragraph or adopting other technical means to obtain data stored, processed, or transmitted in such computer information systems, or implementing illegal control over such computer information systems, in serious cases, the offender shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than three years or criminal detention, and/or fined; in particularly serious cases, they shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than three years but not more than seven years, and fined."

"Providing specialized programs or tools for intrusion or illegal control of computer information systems, or knowingly providing programs or tools for others who implement criminal activities involving intrusion or illegal control of computer information systems, in serious cases, the offender shall be punished according to the provisions of the preceding paragraph."

X. A paragraph shall be added to Article 312 of the Criminal Law as the second paragraph: "For units committing the crime mentioned in the preceding paragraph, the unit shall be fined, and the persons directly responsible and other directly responsible personnel shall be punished according to the provisions of the preceding paragraph."

XI. The first paragraph of Article 337 of the Criminal Law shall be revised as follows: "In violation of state regulations concerning animal and plant quarantine, causing major outbreaks of animal and plant diseases, or posing a serious risk of causing major outbreaks of animal and plant diseases, the offender shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than three years or criminal detention, and/or fined."

XII. The second paragraph of Article 375 of the Criminal Law shall be revised as follows: "For illegally producing or trading military uniforms, in serious cases, the offender shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than three years, criminal detention, or public surveillance, and/or fined."

An additional paragraph shall be added as the third paragraph: "For forging, stealing, trading, or illegally providing or using special marks such as vehicle license plates of the armed forces, in serious cases, the offender shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than three years, criminal detention, or public surveillance, and/or fined; in particularly serious cases, they shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than three years but not more than seven years, and fined."

The original third paragraph shall become the fourth paragraph, revised as follows: "For units committing the crimes mentioned in the second and third paragraphs, the unit shall be fined, and the persons directly responsible and other directly responsible personnel shall be punished according to the provisions of each paragraph."

XIII. A provision shall be added after Article 388 of the Criminal Law as Article 388-1: "Close relatives or others who have close relations with state functionaries, through the official conduct of such state functionaries, or taking advantage of the power or status formed by such state functionaries, through the official conduct of other state functionaries, seek improper benefits for the entrustor, solicit or accept property from the entrustor, in a relatively large amount or under other relatively serious circumstances, the offender shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than three years or criminal detention, and fined; in a huge amount or under other serious circumstances, they shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than three years but not more than seven years, and fined; in an especially huge amount or under other especially serious circumstances, they shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than seven years, and fined or have their property confiscated."

"For former state functionaries or their close relatives or others who have close relations with them, who take advantage of the power or status formed by such former state functionaries to commit the act mentioned in the preceding paragraph, they shall be convicted and punished according to the provisions of the preceding paragraph."

XIV. The first paragraph of Article 395 of the Criminal Law shall be revised as follows: "If the property and expenditure of a state functionary obviously exceed his/her legal income by a huge margin, he/she may be ordered to explain the source; if he/she cannot explain the source, the difference shall be deemed as illegally obtained, and he/she shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than five years or criminal detention; if the difference is exceptionally huge, he/she shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than five years but not more than ten years. The difference part of the property shall be recovered."

XV. This Amendment shall enter into force upon promulgation.
