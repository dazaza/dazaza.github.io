How I Make 20,000 Yuan a Month

How I make 20,000 yuan a month. Some people may laugh at me for not having seen enough of the world. They say they make 30,000, 50,000 or even 100,000 yuan a month. You little guy, what are you bragging about here?

I want to write down my experience just to share my methods and experiences with everyone. My methods and experiences may not necessarily be suitable for you. They might even seem laughable. Also, they are not universal truths that apply everywhere.

However, in this forum, I see quite a few people sighing... saying that business is hard to do and so on. I just want to give everyone confidence.

My approach:

I run "2-in-1" games, one hour of synthesis like Kuang Long's, and two versions of "Heaven Chaos". I open two zones a day. Each series has its own zone.

The advertising sites for the synthesis game are: haosf.c om and xp13. For Heaven Chaos, only one site: ha osf.com

Each zone lasts three days until Sand Rush. The day of Sand Rush is also the day of zone merging.

If you pay attention, I am the one who opens new zones every day for Heaven Chaos. I am from Huai'an, Jiangsu Province. What series? Water Margin series, History and Culture series, World War II series, Thirty-Six Stratagems series. Bad Boy series. Famous Generals series, Ancient Historical Events series. Recently, I have been opening the Three Kingdoms series... You can check it out on Hao Si Fu.

You might ask, since I open so many zones, there must be a lot of servers, right?

Actually, I only have three servers.....

On the same day, if server A runs synthesis and server B runs Heaven Chaos, then server C doesn't open any zone. The next day, server A runs Heaven Chaos, server B runs synthesis, and server C doesn't open any zone. On the third day, server C either runs Heaven Chaos or synthesis, depending on which zone between server A and server B has fewer players, then another server is chosen to run the other zone..... It sounds complicated. In fact, it's a dual-server or triple-server setup on the same machine. One machine can run two to three zones. And we absolutely do not run two new zones on the same machine on the same day. Although it's a dual-server setup, monster spawning rates are not discounted, offline hanging still works. We will never sacrifice the game's characteristics for smoothness.

Dual-server setup on one machine is not a complex technology, many people know how to do it.... By alternating like this, the server won't lag, and costs are greatly reduced...

Cost reduction is also very important... Those who say as long as there are more players when opening a zone, they earn more money and don't care about the server cost....

Certainly, this statement is correct. But now, there are many factors affecting the opening of a zone, such as attacks, advertising effectiveness, equipment prices, etc., all these affect the benefits. The profit of a zone has greatly decreased. Therefore, I personally think reducing costs is necessary and feasible. Because the performance of the game will not decrease. There will be no lagging or unstable phenomena... Of course, this is just my method, I am just publishing my approach here for everyone's reference...
