"I like Uniqlo's flannel, I like Uniqlo's fleece jackets, I like Uniqlo's short polos, I like Uniqlo's casual business attire..." Since the international fashion brand Uniqlo entered the Chengdu market, its high-quality selected fabrics, minimalist and versatile styles, as well as vibrant colors, along with its energetic, comfortable, and simple style, have made Chengdu consumers unable to resist. It is reported that on May 14th, Uniqlo will grandly open in the 2nd-3rd floor of Fuli Tianhui Center. This will be the sixth store in Chengdu since opening its first shop in 2009, and the nearly 2000 square meter Fuli Tianhui new store will also be Uniqlo's first large store in Chengdu. At that time, Uniqlo will showcase multiple summer new products, bringing more complete product displays for Chengdu consumers. Creating clothes for all - making clothing for everyone.

Adhering to its consistent simple and refined brand style, Uniqlo will once again spark a "Mix & Match versatile fashion trend" in Chengdu, adding another delightful surprise of trendy clothing for Chengdu consumers!
