Eddie Luo and Han Leng authorize the theme song of "The Cool Wanderer" for the game "The侠客行" (The Wanderer)

by tg6911aac on 2012-03-09 12:56:39

Eddy Luo, Han Leng authorizes Xia Ke Xing's Cool Theme Song. As a representative pioneer of the post-80s generation, Han Leng continuously challenges authority and advocates for freedom of speech. Recently, he collaborated with his signed record company Universal Tianyun to authorize "Dream Chaser" from his personal music album "18+," to be used as the theme song for the online game "Xia Ke Xing," promoted by Dacheng Network.

Originally composed by Eddy Luo in memory of the wandering female writer Sanmao, "Dream Chaser" later became famous as the theme song of "The Snowy Mountain Fox." Perhaps due to the mutual appreciation among literati, Han Leng, a representative pioneer of the post-80s generation, covered this song. With its unrestrained spirit amidst wanderings and a melancholic jazz vibe, it offers a completely new experience for those who enjoy coolness.

Han Leng's version of "Dream Chaser" can be previewed at: http://music.sina.com.cn/yueku/m.php?id=8&FLAGADDLIST=&retcode=

The online game "Xia Ke Xing" promotes the concept of "cool martial arts," overturning traditional boring methods. The entire game emphasizes rich content, vivid activities, smooth combat, trendy gameplay, and a cool image, interpreting the joy and vengeance of Chinese martial arts with a modern mindset and model.

For such an online game, the selection of a theme song aims to combine classics with trends. Han Leng's "Dream Chaser" integrates Eddy Luo's classic work, Sanmao's wandering spirit, and Han Leng's cool attitude. Moreover, after being rearranged by Chang Shelai, the composer of the Olympic Games theme song, this tender song takes on a jazz style, catering more to the tastes of the younger generations.

Currently, this theme song will be included in the next version of the game, allowing cool masters to appreciate this "Xia Ke" theme song: Dream Chaser, before entering the game. An oasis appears, a spring emerges from the sand; a small courtyard of green stones, jade hidden in the city.
