Google launches online game "Bourne's Shadow"

by 127702 on 2007-07-14 09:14:19

July 13 news, Thursday this week, Universal Pictures and Google jointly released an online video game called "Google's Ultimate Search for Bourne". Players need to use a series of tools provided by Google, such as search, maps, images, and YouTube, to find clues in the game. At the same time, this online game also promotes the upcoming movie "Bourne Ultimatum".

According to Reuters, it is reported that the third part of the famous movie series "Bourne" will be launched on August 3. The game launched by Google this time will be released in seven countries. In the game, players play as a former CIA employee, uncovering the mysterious veil of spies through a series of clues. It is understood that before the release of the movie, any player can log in to www.google.com/bourne to enjoy the unique fun brought by Google. It is reported that there will be clips from the movie "Bourne Ultimatum 3" included in the game.

In fact, this is already the second online game provided by the search giant. Previously, players could also receive rich rewards while playing the game, such as the luxury car Touareg 2 provided by Volkswagen in Germany, which will appear in the movie "Bourne Ultimatum 3", and the winner of the game will also get the car Touareg 2. Meanwhile, MasterCard will provide the game winner with a $1000 discount. On the other hand, there are also Apple iPhones as prizes, as well as a direct bonus of $25000. The most exciting thing is that the winning players can also get the opportunity for free city tours, including New York, USA, Paris, France, London, UK, and Madrid, Spain, all of which are scenes in the movie.

It is reported that the game will be first released in seven countries, including the United States, Canada, Australia, New Zealand, Germany, the United Kingdom, and France.
