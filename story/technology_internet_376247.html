Integrating four major applications! Mobile QQ Desktop 1.0 Experience Report

by diglog on 2010-12-28 15:24:44

In the mobile Internet era, who will personalize your phone's desktop? The recently released Tencent Mobile Desktop 1.0 seems to provide a pretty good answer to this question. Through experiencing several details of the Mobile Desktop, one can see that Tencent's core strategy in the mobile Internet is simple yet practical —— through entry points such as the desktop and browser, it gradually integrates its own products starting from its advantageous businesses, step by step meeting users' multi-faceted needs. The author has discovered many clues through being one of the first to experience the recently released QQ Phone Desktop 1.0 beta1. QQ messages directly reach the desktop. Recently, Tencent's Mobile Desktop 1.0 beta1 version for the S60 V3 platform made an appearance, receiving much attention even during its testing phase. Besides its simple and practical style, this product also incorporates some commonly used Tencent mobile applications by mobile web users, reflecting its deep integration strategy. In terms of UI, at first glance, the QQ Phone Desktop 1.0 beta1 continues the consistent blue tone background of Tencent's products, providing a visually comfortable and bright experience.
