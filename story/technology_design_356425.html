Unveiling the Production Process of "If You Are The One": Post-80s Generation Supports the Backstage Team

Li Zheng, the chief editor of the program "If You Are The One," is mainly responsible for guest selection.

Producer Wang Gang attributes the success of "If You Are The One" to "the right time, place, and people." Among these, the "people" refer to the young backstage team of the program. From guest screening to on-site recording, to post-production, all are inseparable from the hard work of these young backstage staff. How exactly is this program made? How are the guests selected? Recently, a reporter visited the scene of "If You Are The One" to unveil the group of post-80s backstage team, and listen to them talk about the stories behind the program.

Revelation One: Guest Selection and Matching

Interviewee: Program Chief Editor Li Zheng, Function: Guest Selection

Selection Criteria: Those who are good at expressing themselves and dare to express themselves are given priority.

Ma诺, Ma伊咪, Zhu真芳, rich second-generation, penguin men... Several internet celebrities have emerged from "If You Are The One," and their popularity is inseparable from the keen eyes of the backstage directors responsible for guest selection. The production team of "If You Are The One" consists of eight directors, one chief editor, and one producer. Li Zheng serves as the chief editor of "If You Are The One," mainly responsible for guest selection. He joined from the tenth episode and has now "seen countless people." Li Zheng has a five-month-old baby, but due to his busy work schedule, he hasn't had time to accompany his wife and child for two consecutive months.

Currently, more than 160,000 people have registered to participate in "If You Are The One," and it will take a long time to process all these guests. Recently, registration points have been set up in Beijing, Shanghai, Guangzhou, and Nanjing, where guests are reviewed on-site. Each guest needs to fill out a registration form on-site, followed by a brief interview conducted by the program's director. The content mainly involves occupation income, interests, family background, emotional experiences, and partner selection criteria, etc. Generally speaking, during the interview, directors will mark those with special professions, identities, or experiences accordingly. After verifying the materials, the directors will contact the guests to shoot short videos, mainly about the guests' living conditions and friends' evaluations of them.

Li Zheng stated that when selecting guests, they hope to present "diversity" in terms of age, profession, marital status, and income. Moreover, whether the on-stage guests can "express" themselves is an important selection criterion. "The matchmaking on 'If You Are The One' is not ordinary online dating where posting photos and information is enough. Guests need to face the camera, so being brave and willing to express oneself is the most important."

Li Zheng also mentioned that Zhu真芳, the former No. 2 "mansion girl," was a typical example of someone who dared to express herself. She was criticized by netizens for saying things like "a monthly salary of 200,000 yuan is needed to touch my hand" and "my boyfriend must have a mansion." In fact, Zhu真芳 grew up in extreme poverty, and after starting work, most of the people she encountered were generous spenders, leading her to mistakenly believe that a monthly salary of 200,000 yuan was a very ordinary level. In the eyes of the production team, this was a classic case of being brave but not good at expressing oneself. Li Zheng said that after Zhu真芳, the production team would prioritize "being good at expressing oneself" when selecting guests. If other conditions are not particularly outstanding, but the guest is good at expressing themselves, they will be given priority consideration.

Guest Matching: Each of the Five Male Guests Has Unique Characteristics

The directors of "If You Are The One" are divided into A and B groups. One group is responsible for selecting guests and shooting materials, while the other group starts recording the program. To ensure the richness reflected in each episode, before recording the program, the guests will be matched according to the materials shot. Li Zheng said that among the five male guests in each episode, there must be one with a good image, one with a good profession, one with rich emotional stories, and one who dares to express certain values. This way, the program will have more watchable content within a limited time.

Program Format: Female Guests Can Stay Up to 20 Episodes

Due to the high ratings of "If You Are The One," many female guests with impure motives hope to stay on the stage longer and do not want to be chosen by male guests too early. Regarding this, Li Zheng said that since "If You Are The One" is not a variety show, it cannot use the format to determine the results. However, if a female guest stays on the stage for too long and causes doubts among netizens, the production team will communicate privately and ask the guest to set a final deadline. Currently, Ma伊咪 has stayed on the stage for 22 episodes, and the psychological bottom line of the production team is around 20 episodes.

Female Guest Profession Modelization: Position No. 11 Reserved for Professional Models

In the already aired episodes of "If You Are The One," No. 1 Xie佳, No. 11 Ma诺, Na笛, and No. 13 Ma伊咪 have gained quite high popularity online. With their successful pairing, the production team hopes to reserve their positions for female guests with similar characteristics. For example, position No. 1 will always be reserved for girls with a neutral style; position No. 11 will be reserved for professional models; the No. 5 female guest must have a high education level; the No. 24 female guest must be very tall; similarly, positions No. 20 and No. 21 will be reserved for a mother-daughter pair.

Revelation Two: On-Site Coordination

Interviewees: On-Site Director Chen辰, Female Guest Director Zhang铭铭, Male Guest Director Guo俊涛

On-Stage Functions: Overseeing the Entire Program On-Site

As mentioned earlier, the production team of "If You Are The One" consists of eight directors. Generally, there are three directors on-site during program recordings: On-Site Director Chen辰, Female Guest Director Zhang铭铭, and Male Guest Director Guo俊涛. On-Site Director Chen辰 also concurrently serves as the program's chief editor, a very young and experienced girl responsible for overseeing the entire on-site situation, handling emergencies, communicating with hosts and backstage staff, etc. Occasionally, when hosts or guests need touch-ups or equipment malfunctions occur, resulting in brief pauses, Chen辰 steps in to resolve various small issues on-site. Additionally, she reviews the VCR materials during male guest interviews and participates in post-production after the program ends.

Zhang铭铭, the female guest director, is a lively boy responsible for leading the female guests. Before going on stage, he reminds the female guests of some precautions. When the female guests' posture is not elegant or they whisper to each other, he gives them timely reminders. During the program recording, the female guest director closely monitors the female guests' performance on stage and can promptly handle any physical discomfort. According to Zhang铭铭, one female guest once fainted due to low blood sugar, but was quickly treated because it was discovered in time. When asked if he had any "heartthrob" among the many beautiful female guests, Zhang铭铭 shyly said, "I'm not handsome, so the female guests wouldn't like me. Maybe that's why the production team let me lead the female guests."

Guo俊涛, the male guest director, besides being responsible for the male guests' attire, also calms their emotions before going on and off stage. Before going on stage, many male guests get so nervous that they sweat profusely. Guo俊涛 then calms them down, mainly telling them "It's okay, just express yourself sincerely." Some male guests feel very disheartened after being "eliminated" from the stage, and the male guest director also provides psychological counseling. The male and female guests are strictly kept apart before the program recording to preserve the freshness between them.

Revelation Three: Program Music and On-Site Sound Effects

Interviewee: Sound Engineer Cai Kunyan

Role: An Indispensable Bonus Item in the Program

The great success of "If You Are The One" owes much to the music used on stage. The upbeat background music has become the first choice for audience members' mobile phone ringtones. Simply typing "If You Are The One music" into a search engine yields tens of millions of search results, and enthusiastic netizens have even compiled all the music from "If You Are The One."

At the "If You Are The One" site, the sound engineer is a young man named Cai Kunyan, who previously worked on the live sound effects for programs such as "Superman for All" and "Who Dares to Sing." All the background music on the stage is carefully selected by him. In an interview, Cai Kunyan admitted that before choosing the music, he analyzes the meaning conveyed by the lyrics and then repeatedly selects the most suitable one to be used as the fixed background music for the stage. As for some sudden situations on the stage, it relies on his improvisation skills. Cai Kunyan studied composition in college and has accumulated a large amount of musical material. The materials on his computer are categorized by emotion as "sad," "upbeat," "funny," etc., allowing him to quickly retrieve them when dealing with sudden situations on the stage.

When talking about his proudest moment of improvisation, Cai Kunyan said it was the background music he chose during the successful proposal of male guest Li Meng to female guest Li Yang, which came from JS's "I Love You More Than I Imagined." As Li Yang and Li Meng embraced tightly on stage, the melody of "I realized I love you more than I imagined, only accidentally missing you" resonated on the stage, mirroring the misunderstanding and separation in "If You Are The One," and realizing the importance of each other later. The background music not only moved all the audience, but also brought tears to the usually composed Meng Fei. Le Jia couldn't help but tease the sound engineer, "Your background music is too sentimental."

For Cai Kunyan, "If You Are The One" has become a stage where he can fully display his talents. More improvisations also include some quick and concise sound effects, such as "screaming" and "laughing" that match the atmosphere on the stage, which often greatly enliven the audience's mood. Host Meng Fei once talked about his tough days when he was young, and Cai Kunyan humorously paired it with a sorrowful piece "Reflection of the Moon on Erquan," causing bursts of laughter throughout the venue. Meng Fei himself couldn't help laughing, "Your music pairing is indeed tragic." Would the extensive use of background music on the stage cause copyright disputes? Relevant responsible persons said that the length of the music used on the stage is within the allowed range of copyright.

Revelation Four: Costumes and Makeup

Interviewee: Makeup Artist Xin Yuan

Disclosure: Female Guests Are Not Big Stars, Costume Props Are Free

The appearance of female guests on "If You Are The One" is always stylish and graceful. Many viewers are curious if they prepare their own costumes and makeup, and if they need to pay for it. Makeup artist Xin Yuan explained that most of the female guests' costumes are prepared by themselves, and occasionally when a female guest needs costumes provided by the program team, this rental is completely free. There are about ten people in the entire makeup team, with each makeup artist responsible for one female guest. It takes about an hour to apply makeup for each female guest, and many female guests are already very good at makeup themselves, so they come to the program already made up. Besides makeup, there is also a styling team with various styling tools that can quickly create various hairstyles. Sometimes on the program, all female guests are seen with buns or curly hair. The stylists explained that this is not deliberately arranged, but rather a "herd mentality" among the female guests. Sometimes when they see a hairstyle that looks good on a particular girl, they naturally request the stylist to try it on themselves as well.

When the female guests walk onto the stage accompanied by cheerful music, they appear as poised as models. In fact, they haven't undergone special training. Zhang Mingming, the director responsible for the female guests, said that they often tell the female guests to maintain good posture on stage with the phrase: "Being a woman is great," which means standing up straight. If they don't want to look unattractive on TV, they should pay attention to their posture and walking style. Despite the fatigue of standing for a long time on stage, beauty-conscious female guests still maintain an upright posture to leave the best impression on the audience.
