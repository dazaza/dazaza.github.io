Glest: A stunning yet completely free 3D real-time strategy game

by gnrsu on 2009-06-14 22:09:03

There are two races in the game can choose, one is the human race, one is the witch, and Warcraft in the same, there are a lot of arms, as well as ancient super weapons, the premise is to upgrade the main base to the top, there are a lot of buildings can be built, can physical attack can also be magic attack.

Originally, the English name of this game is called Glest, but this word does not exist in English, and can be regarded as a co-written "God Blest", so the Chinese translation "God bless" is more appropriate.
