The procedures for buying and selling stocks are as follows: When buying or selling stocks, you must specify whether to buy or sell, the name (or code) of the stock, quantity, price. And this commission is only valid on the day the commission is placed. The content of the commission includes the abbreviation (code) of the stock you want to buy or sell, the quantity, and the price of buying or selling the stock. The abbreviation of the stock is usually three to four Chinese characters, and the code of the stock is six digits. The code and abbreviation of the stock must be consistent when entrusting to buy or sell. At the same time, there are certain regulations on the quantity of buying and selling stocks: That is, the quantity of the entrusted purchase of stocks must be an integral multiple of 100, but the quantity of the entrusted sale of stocks does not have to be an integral multiple of 100.

There are four types of commissions: counter submission commission, telephone automatic commission, computer automatic commission, and remote terminal commission.
