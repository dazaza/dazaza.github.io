Hsu Lo (Vic Zhou)

An unsociable best-selling author of "Leaves" with sales exceeding 100,000 copies. Ding Yu Hao (Jay Chou)

A silent and reserved taxi driver. If Hsu Lo hadn't joined the taxi company, no one would have known that he was once a wealthy heir studying in the United States, nor would they know about his formerly bold and loyal personality in high school.

The unbearable past of squandering his family fortune and leaving the woman he loved has continuously haunted Ah Hao. This is why Ah Hao always builds a wall around himself, preventing others from getting close. Although he knows that Mr. Pan, a close friend of his deceased father, and his family have always encouraged him, and that Neng Xian's sister has feelings for him... Ah Hao still can't cheer up.

If it weren't for his former classmates Hsu Lo and Xiao Ma forcing him to recall those happy times; if it weren't for reuniting with his old lover, Ah Hao wouldn't have realized how many things are still worth cherishing...

Ma Yong Rui (Wang Chuan Yi)

Heir to the Xihua Hotel, with a free-spirited personality, doing whatever comes to mind. The only person he truly fears in his life is his brother - General Manager of the Xihua Hotel, Ma Yong Jie. He was a classmate in high school with Hsu Lo and Ah Hao, and the three were the best of friends at the time. However, after Ah Hao lost contact due to studying abroad, only Xiao Ma and Hsu Lo remained in touch.

During a media tasting event, Xiao Ma reunites with Lu Yi, the school beauty from high school whom he and his friends used to tease. The beautiful and intelligent Lu Yi is simply his ideal goddess, and the affection he had for her since high school resurfaces, prompting him to pursue her.

But the love-struck Xiao Ma, didn't notice the deeply hidden painful past that Lu Yi was concealing...

Lu Yi (Guan Ying)

The school beauty from Hsu Lo, Ah Hao, and Xiao Ma's high school era, currently working as an editor for a fashion magazine.

Despite being pursued by many admirers, Lu Yi understands that the wound caused by her once almost-marriage-ending romance has never truly healed...

If it weren't for Neng Xian, the public relations assistant of the Xihua Hotel, asking her to write a news article about their newly launched cuisine, Lu Yi might not have noticed how familiar the young boss looked, causing the sealed memories and secrets to flood back like Pandora's box...

Pan Neng Xian (Ruby Lin)

The second daughter of a traditional Chinese medicine clinic owner. Her lively and cheerful personality stems from a harmonious family environment and her parents' kind genetic traits. Although not a large hospital, the family's livelihood depends on the business of the clinic.

Having just graduated from university, Neng Xian successfully enters her dream hotel for an internship. If she passes the internship, she will become a regular employee. However, she breaks the hotel rules by saving a guest, Hsu Lo, who was about to commit suicide in the hotel; fortunately, on her last day of the internship, the young boss persuades the general manager to spare her.

For Neng Xian, the young boss is caring towards employees, handsome, and considerate, making him much more attractive than the shy and uncommunicative Hsu Lo. Yet, she didn't expect... How did she offend Cupid? She ends up forming an inseparable bond with Hsu Lo by the Ai River in Kaohsiung!!
