1. 【Original Slogan】Overbirth? Then get tied up! (An extremely leftist slogan from a place in Hunan)　【IT Slogan】Listed on the market, chaos ensues! (No need for examples, just look at those already delisted and about to be delisted.)2、【Original Slogan】One person overbirths, the whole village gets tied up! (A rural slogan from Chuxiong, Yunnan)　【IT Slogan】One company lists on the market, the whole industry goes chaotic!3、【Original Slogan】One person ties up, the whole family is honored! (An absolutely classic slogan from a national highway in Shandong)　【IT Slogan】One person in IT, the whole family is honored! (Though it seems to have declined somewhat in recent years. A few years ago, I heard a conversation between two neighborhood aunties: Auntie A: I hear your daughter is getting married, what does her partner do down south? Auntie B: Works in IT. Auntie A: That's great, really enviable!)4、【Original Slogan】A family planning slogan from a rural area in Shandong: "If it can be induced, induce; if it can be aborted, abort; it must not be born."　【IT Slogan】If it can be financed, go for financing; if it can list, go for listing; it must not collapse. (This was President Liu Xingliang's requirement at a group company meeting for all websites under his umbrella.)5、【Original Slogan】Promote one child, control two children, eliminate three children.　【IT Slogan】Invest in one, control two, eliminate three. (Suggested as a slogan for investment companies' walls.)6、【Original Slogan】In many places in the countryside of Jiangsu: "Rather blood flows into rivers than one overbirth allowed."【IT Slogan】Rather blood flows into rivers than miss one pornographic site. (Can serve as the Ministry of Public Security's guideline.)7、【Original Slogan】A birth plan slogan from a county in Hunan: "Whoever doesn't practice family planning will end up with a broken home and dead family members."【IT Slogan】Whoever doesn't follow the overtime policy will end up with a broken home and dead family members. (Don't want to work overtime? Not an option.)8、【Original Slogan】From a mountain village in Sichuan: "First birth allowed, second birth leads to sterilization, third or fourth births - scrape! Scrape!
