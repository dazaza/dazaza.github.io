The five major drifting attractions of Huangshan tourism will let you experience an exciting summer.

by jj0505 on 2012-02-28 14:28:13

The Huangshan Jiaxi River rafting is located at the foot of Mount Huangshan, 48 kilometers away from Huangshan City (Tunxi), 12 kilometers from the Huangshan Scenic Area (Tangkou Town), and 20 kilometers from the World Cultural Heritage sites of Xidi and Hongcun. It is also 20 kilometers away from the Jade Valley. The starting point is at the entrance of the Rucun Tunnel on the Tunhuang Highway, a golden tourist route, and the entrance of the Hongru Highway from Tangkou to Hongcun (from Hongcun to Rucun). Transportation is very convenient. The entire Jiaxi River rafting journey is about 5 kilometers long. As you drift downstream on a raft, you will be surrounded by beautiful scenery, with mountains encircling the area, cliffs standing tall, lush forests, vast bamboo seas, and endless tea plantations. The peaks and strange rocks stand majestically, while the blue sky and white clouds reflect in the water. In shallow areas, the water is crystal clear to the bottom, while in deeper areas, it is as green as jade. Fish and shrimp frolic in the water, and birds sing along the way, creating a refreshing experience. Drifting on the clear waves and being surrounded by picturesque scenery is sure to become the best part of your visit to the Huangshan scenic area. This is the perfect place for you to enjoy the beauty of Huangshan and play in the water.

The Yangsheng River Rafting is located at the southernmost end of Xiuning County, Anhui Province, within the Lingnan Provincial Nature Reserve. The scenic area combines rafting adventures with summer retreats and leisure activities. The two sides of the river are filled with beautiful landscapes, encircled by mountains. There are hundred-mu willow gardens, thousand-mu bamboo forests, and ten-thousand-mu tea plantations. Here, you can marvel at the wonders of nature such as broken willows, thousand-pig stones, twin dragons playing with pearls, roaring lions of the river east, She inkstones, scripture caves, and the Dizang Buddha's imprints. You can truly experience the poetic charm of "people walking in paintings, birds flying in screens."

The Yan Gong River Rafting Rural Tourism Project is located in Shandou Village, Shandou Township, Xiuning County, on the Huizhou-Wuyuan Golden Tourism Route, just 23 kilometers from the center of Huangshan City. Convenient transportation makes this a model new rural village of Huangshan City and one of the ancient villages in southern Anhui. The ecological environment here is exceptionally superior, making it an ideal place for agritourism. The Yan Gong River Rafting starts from Shandou Village in Shandou Township and ends at Jin Gou Shu Natural Village, covering nearly 3 kilometers. Along the way, the natural vegetation is well preserved, and the scenery is breathtaking, combining high-altitude surfing, water rafting, and bamboo garden sightseeing. With lush mountains, green waters, dense forests, and towering bamboos, the views are enchanting, offering another great place to experience excitement and enjoy coolness.

Construction of the Yan Gong River Rafting Rural Tourism Project began in 2008, with a planned total investment of 50 million yuan, including an initial investment of 5 million yuan. This project integrates high-altitude surfing, water rafting, the pastoral landscape of ancient Huizhou villages, and bamboo garden sightseeing into one comprehensive rural tourism project.

The Taoyuan Corridor Scenic Area is located in Yi County, Huangshan City, Anhui Province, a World Cultural Heritage site. It refers to the 10-kilometer stretch of the Zhang River Basin from Shishan to Yuting. Named after the famous poem "Peach Blossom Spring" written by the Eastern Jin Dynasty poet Tao Yuanming, which describes a beautiful and tranquil utopia beyond the mundane world, the rafting starting point is set at the foot of the picturesque Shishan Daifeng Mountain. The ending point is at the Shimen Mandarin Duck Bay upstream of Yuting Town. The entire rafting course is six kilometers long and takes approximately 1.5 hours to complete.

Starting from the foot of Daifeng Mountain, board a bamboo raft to drift along the crystal-clear river, splashing through streams and rapids... Up close, you'll see the shallow water teeming with fish and colorful stones. In the distance, verdant bamboo groves, clusters of mountain flowers, and peach orchards frame the view. Ancient Huizhou villages and homes are nestled among old trees, forming one magnificent scroll of Taoyuan landscape painting after another. Each Huizhou memorial archway and ancient ancestral hall stands like a century-old totem at the source of the Xin'an River, symbolizing the depth of history, the primitiveness of ecology, and the simplicity of local customs... This is the most authentic depiction of Tao Yuanming's "Peach Blossom Spring."

The Xiangxi Rafting Scenic Area is located at the eastern foot of the Huangshan Scenic Area, part of the Huangshan Scenic Area, with a total investment of 21 million yuan. The Xiangxi Rafting Scenic Area is situated in Shancha Village, Tangkou Town, Huangshan District, with an advantageous geographical location. It is only 4 kilometers from the South Gate of the Huangshan Scenic Area and 500 meters from the national 4A-level scenic spots of Jade Valley and Jiulong Waterfall, forming a certain tourism network with surrounding scenic spots and attractions. The completion of the Xiangxi Rafting Scenic Area has added a new and beautiful landscape to Huangshan District, providing tourists visiting Huangshan with a new destination. The Xiangxi Rafting Scenic Area focuses on the splendid water scenery of the river, integrating rafting, surfing, water play, mountain village sentiment, natural ecology, Huizhou folk customs, and leisure vacations. The natural scenery along both banks is picturesque, with overlapping green peaks, lush bamboo and trees, vibrant mountain flowers, colorful pebbles on the riverbank, clear and fragrant water, schools of fish swimming together, and deep pools alternating with shallow rapids, all creating a beautiful and interesting scene. Numerous colorful pools are scattered throughout the river. Riding a bamboo raft, paddling a kayak, rushing through rapids, and playing in the water pools in this splendid river is exhilarating.
