Promotion at the beginning of the year for the E-Class: a direct reduction of 1.1 million yuan, plus a gift of full insurance for two years.

by rabibxa370 on 2011-11-14 09:34:51

According to the sales staff of Penglong Starlight, there are sufficient stocks of 2011 E-Class Benz cars in the showroom and colors are available. In terms of price, not only can you enjoy an 80,000-110,000 yuan discount if you buy a car recently, but also get a free two-year full insurance for the new car. The price of E200L Elegant Type drops by 80,000 yuan, and the current selling price is only 385,000 yuan; the price of E300L Fashion Prestige Type drops by 110,000 yuan, and the current selling price is 600,000 yuan. Friends who are interested can call the dealer for more details. The length, width, and height of the new E-Class are respectively: 5012mm, 1855mm, 1464mm. The wheelbase is increased by 140mm compared with the old model, reaching 3014mm. The extended wheelbase makes the space inside the car very spacious, improving the comfort of rear seat passengers. In terms of configuration, the new E-Class adds high-tech equipment such as SBC electronic brakes, dynamic multiple support electric function seats, panoramic sunroof glass roof and solar car room cooling system, Keyless-go keyless entry system. At the same time, the new E-Class has also made significant improvements in safety, achieving the highest 5-star standard of NCAP in front and side collisions (W210 is four stars). EBD, BAS, ESP, ASR, Distronic automatic distance measurement system are standard or optional configurations. In terms of power, the new E300L is equipped with a 3.0L V6 naturally aspirated engine. Compared with the standard wheelbase E-Class sedan, the maximum power increases by 10kW, reaching 180kW (245 horsepower), and it is equipped with a seven-speed automatic transmission. The new E260L sedan is equipped with a 1.8 liter CGI direct injection turbocharged engine, with a power output of up to 150kW (204 horsepower), and it is equipped with a five-speed automatic transmission.
