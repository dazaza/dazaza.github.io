Beauty is everyone's pursuit, it's every woman's highlight. However, in life, many women feel frustrated and inferior due to minor flaws, becoming hesitant to wear spaghetti straps or ultra-shorts. Life hacks can reduce your worries and concerns! They can make your whole body shine!

Concealing small spots on the face:

Everyone has some kind of imperfection on their face, especially during urgent and important occasions when skin defects cannot be immediately resolved, requiring concealer to get through tough times. As long as the facial pimples and spots are well covered, your face can appear smooth and delicate again.

For freckles, a comprehensive consideration of their size and depth is necessary; for brown spots, a full understanding of their quantity and distribution is needed according to a makeup training school in Hefei [http://www.hfainisi.org].

The causes of freckles and brown spots are numerous, such as internal organ dysfunction, endocrine dysfunction, mental stress, etc. The source should be eliminated as much as possible, but there’s no need to be perpetually worried just because of a few freckles. There are four methods to cover freckles and brown spots, choose the one that suits you best.

Method one: Use fingertips to repeatedly apply foundation cream on the areas with freckles. If this method is ineffective, try adding some powder-type foundation cream to your setting powder.

Method two: Use foundation cream whose color lies between the freckle color and your skin tone from Hefei makeup training [http://www.hfainisi.org]. Using this method (without repeated application), the freckle color blends with the skin tone, making them less noticeable.

Method three: Cover with shiny foundation cream. With glowing skin, having a few freckles isn't so concerning. This is a method of using shine to overshadow freckles.

Method four: Use a bold individualistic makeup style to divert attention. For example, if there are freckles at the corners of the eyes, use bright lipstick; if the whole face has brown spots, ensure that the eye makeup and lipstick outline are clear and substantial.

Using foundation to conceal acne:

If the nose or cheeks are full of acne scars, looking like orange peel, use the following four methods to apply foundation cream, compare them, and find the right solution.

Method one: Apply powder-type foundation cream on the area full of acne scars, not liquid or cold cream type foundation creams. This makes them less visible without sealing them with paint.

Method two: Carefully refine with astringent foundation cream to tighten pores, then lightly apply liquid or slightly brighter foundation cream. Even if the concave areas accumulate foundation cream, they won’t appear darker. To maintain consistent overall color, apply slightly darker foundation cream.

If deep-colored foundation cream is used from the start, the concave areas will be filled and the accumulated color will look dark, making them more noticeable.

Method three: Abandon the idea of covering, thickly apply shiny foundation cream to shift people's focus. This method is suitable for those with mild symptoms.

Method four: Simply use strong coverage foundation cream for heavy makeup.

To switch from light makeup to darker makeup, simply sweeping on a darker shade works fine. But switching from dark makeup to lighter makeup doesn't necessarily require makeup remover. In fact, changing makeup easily removes foundation, which can dry out the skin. However, makeup artists have tricks up their sleeves. With the help of appropriate cosmetics or skincare products, makeup can be easily changed without harming the skin.

Method One: White eyeshadow cream replaces eyeshadow

After applying dark gray or black eyeshadow and wanting to switch to a fresh pink eye makeup, using makeup remover would erase the foundation, resulting in wasted time and effort. The easiest method is to dot white cream eyeshadow on the eyelid, then wipe it off with a triangular sponge. This way, foundation won’t be erased, and it can also serve as a primer, achieving two goals at once.

Method Two: Triangular sponge to lighten blusher

Similarly, trying to lighten intense blusher by wiping hard will only make the skin dry and flaky, and after removing the foundation, the face might become uneven ("panda face"). However, using a triangle-shaped sponge dampened with foundation liquid to gently wipe, the original complexion can be restored.

A triangular sponge is perfect for applying foundation liquid. Its thick, thin, and pointed parts can adapt to different facial areas. After using it in the morning to apply foundation, it can also be used to remove blush when changing makeup, ensuring convenience and effectiveness.

Step 1:

Place the sponge horizontally, then gently wipe from inside out until clean.

Step 2:

Then lightly press on a layer of loose powder.
