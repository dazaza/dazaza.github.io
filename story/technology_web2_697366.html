Knitmaster sells several clothing pattern books, stitch by stitch pattern books, connectors (like SC7), and if the MK70 fixed board is added, it can connect to KR5 and KR6 Knitradars.

The advertising literature states that the machine is lightweight, easy to use, and the actual pattern rows being knitted are visible on the knitting. It knits stockinette, fair isle, tuck, and slip stitches.

The MK70 was introduced to the UK by Knitmaster in the winter of 1980. It was called a compact knitting machine but quickly became known as a sweater knitting, hemming machine.
