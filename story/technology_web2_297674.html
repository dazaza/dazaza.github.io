"Rich Gate" plants love in morality and ethics, and it leans more towards the test of human nature. A group of men and women love deeply. Regardless of whether they have chosen the right person or entered the wrong door, they are all tangled up. They might as well be without regrets and choose a brilliant firework display. After the brilliance fades, it is unknown whether everything can return to its original point. The price of deep affection, the fireworks of love, was once secretly admired. When he was still Sa Fula, he embraced Rene passionately under the square, and the burning flame of love made him think it was unforgettable. Reality may have miraculous encounters, but there are no perfect endings. After countless trials, where is happiness? It turns out that everything has already lost its past beauty. He schemed at the bank of Wanji, with hidden turmoil. This man was very hypocritical in his career, in Hong Kong movies, and in love, he still had to confront "pseudo-siblings." He was originally unscrupulous as a gambling scammer, but the desire for human nature also drove him to become a womanizer. His care during their daily interactions and ambiguous probing were all aimed at disturbing her heart. Rene tried hard to defend her last line of defense, realizing that her feelings were not extinguished but still spreading. How terrible it is to possess love at all costs. If you cannot possess it, you would rather see it destroyed. He strives, is proactive, and upward-looking, yet his greed knows no bounds. He thought he had reached the other shore, but when he looked up, he realized this was hell. It was he who buried his own love with his own hands, leading to destruction. Perhaps this is the price of his unforgettable experience. Not all unforgettable experiences are love. Just like Gao Tuomin falling in love with Zhuo Yixin. Gao Tuomin calls her "Ding Jie," which is quite interesting, so everything starts from a bickering couple and then develops into lovers. There are obstacles, he used to be flirtatious and passionate, but loyalty is Gao Tuomin's strength. He will protect the woman he loves, he respects love, he is the one who saves Yixin, and ultimately, he should be the one who walks hand in hand with Yixin. Often, a long-lasting relationship is more worth cherishing than an unforgettable one. How to legitimize the term "third party"? This woman will definitely say: I am just having a romance that I won't regret, it has nothing to do with others. She is confident, unfortunately not carefree. Being obsessed with a few months' relationship for over ten years still cannot let go. This is a woman. A woman's obsession. Once thought that time could dilute everything, but still uses "ten years of life, two lives apart" to leave a mark on her love. Love spreads recklessly in secret admiration. When she meets Yuan again after 12 years, she firmly believes he is still the same person, still the ideal man she expects. Zhao Xuer sang "Talk a Romance That You Won't Regret," stopping at reminiscence, not intending to possess. Dong Lingzi is different; she moves from secret admiration to possession, even entering his home. For the man in her heart, she would self-harm her legs to gain entry into his house, she could even lose everything. Such love is too obsessive, too crazy. But how can snatched love be noble? Falling in love is a game played without regrets, while ethics and morality are a battle to defend marriage, thus creating a battlefield where two women carefully plan their strategies. He Gejue is noble and understands the bigger picture, but no matter how wise or tolerant she is, she ultimately loses her husband and a happy complete family. She thought that as long as she carefully managed her marriage, she could forever possess Zhuo Yi Yuan. Wrong, strong women also have times of failure. The mistake isn't hers, but her husband's. This man is very "great," telling the woman he has shared a bed with for over ten years that he loves both women. How can you have both fish and bear paws? Ming is greedy, but isn't Yuan also? His final defense was breached by Lingzi, from that moment on, he was deeply entangled. At his core, he isn't a good man. Regarding substitutes, they are not what he truly loves. For Zhuo Yi Ming and A Xin, it is a case of meeting the wrong person at the wrong time. Similarly fleeting sparks, Yi Xin gives him dreamy, endless infatuation, while A Xin can only be a substitute for love, still overshadowed by Yi Xin's shadow. There is no fairness in feelings, but there is a predetermined order. He loved Yi Xin first, deeply etched in his memory. Those funny, amusing expressions, for A Xin, are just a game, a dream. When the dream wakes up, it's time to part ways. Such fragile emotions leave only A Xin hurt in the end. If A Feng gave her the most calculating love, and Yi Ming gave her the most painful love, then Xiao Sha Baotou gave her the cutest angel secretly admiring and protecting her. Even if he can't be an orange, just being a tangerine makes him the best one. What the future holds, no one can predict. If given a glimmer of hope, they would be very happy. One is simple, the other is cute, together they are simple happiness. If they miss each other in the end, it would be the most heart-wrenching pain. Hopefully, we can preserve the purest feelings in our hearts.
