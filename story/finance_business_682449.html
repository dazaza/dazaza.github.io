February 27th National Main Highway Weather Forecast News Center Sina.com

by c6nop1lye on 2012-02-28 22:26:17

From 20:00 on the 27th to 20:00 on the 28th, the national weather forecast predicts light to moderate snow or sleet in western Xinjiang and along the Tianshan region, eastern Qinghai-Tibet Plateau, southeastern Northwest China, northern Heilongjiang, northern North China, southern Jianghan, southern Jianghuai, and northern Jiangnan areas. Parts of western Xinjiang will experience heavy to severe snow, according to a 10-day weather forecast. Light to moderate rain or showers are expected in eastern Southwest China, most parts of Jiangnan, and South China, with localized freezing rain in central and western Guizhou.

- National Highway 218: Yining-Yergen section in Xinjiang
