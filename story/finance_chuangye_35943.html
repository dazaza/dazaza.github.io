How many people's websites have ever been hacked?

On the evening of November 20th, the server of Caike Online (www.coko365.com) was attacked by hackers with unknown origins, causing some data to be affected and services to be interrupted for about an hour.

Fortunately, Caike Online has a good data backup mechanism. In a short period of time, the technical staff immediately repaired the data, except for the diary data from the last two weeks, all other data have been fully restored. So please feel free to use it.

Currently, Caike Online has strengthened and implemented preventive measures for server security. Some damaged diary data has not yet been fully repaired, and the technical staff is working on it as quickly as possible. Please understand.

With the rapid development of Caike Online, it has faced more and more hacker attacks. When Caike Online was still relatively unknown, there were no hacker attacks, but now they do occur. These bored hackers have caused some service disruptions to Caike, but they will absolutely not dampen the enthusiasm and entrepreneurial passion of the Caike team. Because Caike Online has become the most powerful in functionality, with the highest member activity, the largest traffic, and the highest website ranking within China's online accounting field.

In the process of every website growing larger, it must have encountered numerous hacker attacks. The losses hackers once brought to websites were certainly significant. But there's nothing that can be done, because when a website grows larger, it easily attracts unwanted attention.
