In memory of the lost love

by deaw53wsar on 2012-02-24 17:47:24

There was a man who loved me very much. But I, the beauty, dumped him ruthlessly. He was heartbroken and begged me for another chance. But I didn't give him any opportunity because I fell in love with a man I had only met twice. He treated me really well. For the first time, I felt so lucky. He took meticulous care of me, asked about my warmth and coldness. If I didn't answer his call, he would be worried and wonder if anything happened to me. When I went out with friends, he would ask me to go home early and call to urge me to come back sooner. Since I don't like the smell of cigarette, when his friends gave him cigarettes, he put the lit cigarette under the chair so that I couldn't smell it. When it was cold, he told me not to wash clothes with cold water and he washed clothes for me. He said since I had washed so many clothes for him, he should wash clothes for me at least once. Every time we ate, he ordered the dishes I liked, even though he didn't eat them himself. He said they were the dishes I liked. I thought our relationship could last forever. But I was still restless and fell in love with someone else. It was really unfair to him. So, feelings are fragile and always so vulnerable. Since I have fallen in love with someone else, I have to make a choice. Even though I know I will regret my choice, I still choose to give up a half-finished relationship. Not for anything else, just because I know we won't have a result. His heart has been broken by me. I can't expect anything more. I just hope he will be better. At the moment he turned around and left, I saw the tear in his eyes, which was so glaring under the sunshine, making people feel painful. Everything changed from the moment I said those words. We clearly agreed to walk together until the end, but only he kept insisting; it was supposed to be a dance of two people, but only he was dancing; I abandoned him, but he was still good to me, without complaint, saying that he would wait for me forever. In memory of the lost love, I can no longer possess it because my heart no longer belongs to him. Even if I regret it one day in the future, I still won't change my decision now because love always makes people lose their reason. I have no choice. Even if I am the only one left in the end, I think I will be fine. After all, why am I such a ruthless woman? More emotions are on Women Love Network.
