Pulling ears nourishes all organs.

The kidney is one of the main organs of the human body and the foundation of innate constitution. The normal function of the kidneys plays a crucial role in health and longevity.

According to Traditional Chinese Medicine's Five Elements theory, the kidney stores essence and connects to the ear. There are many acupoints for treating kidney diseases located in the ear. Therefore, frequently practicing ear exercises can strengthen the kidneys, support the waist, and promote longevity.

1. Earlobe pulling method: Place the index fingers behind the ear screen, then use the thumb and index finger to pull the ear screen and earlobe from inside outwards. Gradually increase the strength until there is no pain. Each session lasts 3-5 minutes. This method treats headaches, dizziness, neurasthenia, tinnitus, etc.

2. Earwheel rubbing method: Make empty fists with both hands, and use the thumb and index finger to rub up and down along the earwheel until it becomes warm and red. This method strengthens the brain, kidneys, improves hearing, and enhances vision. It can treat impotence, frequent urination, constipation, leg pain, cervical spondylosis, palpitations, chest tightness, headache, dizziness, etc.

3. Ear tip pulling method: Use the thumbs and index fingers of both hands to pinch the earlobe tip and pull upwards, knead, pinch, and rub 15-20 times until it feels warm and red. This method calms, relieves pain, clears the brain, improves vision, reduces fever, fights allergies, and nourishes the kidneys. It can treat hypertension, insomnia, pharyngitis, and skin diseases.

4. Earlobe rubbing method: Lightly pinch the earlobes of both ears with both hands, then rub them until they become warm and red. Then pull the earlobes downwards and let them bounce back. Do this 20 times, two to three times a day. This method promotes blood circulation in the ears and strengthens the kidneys and waist.

5. Double ear pulling method: Pull the right ear upwards dozens of times with the left hand over the top of the head, then pull the left ear with the right hand dozens of times. This exercise also promotes secretion from the submandibular gland and sublingual gland, alleviates throat pain, and treats chronic pharyngitis.

6. Double ear covering method: Cover both ears with the palms, rest the fingers on the back of the skull, and press the middle finger with the index finger to strike 24 times. You can hear a "rumbling" sound, called hitting the "heavenly drum." This stimulation activates the kidneys, strengthens the brain, improves vision, and enhances kidney function.

7. Full ear massage method: Rub the palms together until warm, then massage backward (the front side of the ear) and forward (the back side of the ear). Repeat this 5-6 times. This method unblocks meridians and benefits the kidneys and all internal organs.

8. Double ear sweeping method: Sweep the ears forward with both hands from behind. You will hear a "rustling" sound. Do this 20 times daily. Persisting long-term will strengthen the kidneys and improve health.

The above eight methods can be chosen according to individual needs, either singly or in combination. As long as you persist, you will achieve ideal results.

Simple and easy-to-learn kidney-strengthening and health-preserving methods

Traditional Chinese medicine believes that the kidney is the root of life, and if the kidney qi is abundant, one will be energetic, have strong muscles and bones, walk lightly, think quickly, and if the kidney qi is deficient, one will have weak yang qi, sore waist and knees, easily catch colds, and develop diseases. Kidney yang deficiency commonly shows limb cold intolerance, mental fatigue, sore waist and tinnitus, pale tongue, obesity, etc.
