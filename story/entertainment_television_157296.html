Introduction to the plot of "Operation Tian No. 1":

One and a half months before the National Day of 1950, on a night, the 201 Intelligence Bureau of our army stationed in Beiping received a confidential intelligence from the mole "China No. 1" who infiltrated into the Kuomintang intelligence agency. It was learned that a "Tian No. 1" strategic plan targeting the capital of the new China had quietly begun. Subsequently, both sides discovered at the same time that there were spies buried in their own ranks. The situation facing Yuan Fang, the senior leader of our intelligence department, was extremely severe - who is the enemy mole "Scorpion"? Who is the deeply hidden "Silver Dragon"? Who is the murderer of our intelligence agent 009? Has our mole, Zhong Hua, whose codename is "China No. 1", been exposed? What are the details of the "Tian No. 1" plan? Why are there so many unconventional and strange aspects about the enemy's sabotage this time?...

When Zhong Hua, who narrowly escaped death, was sent back to Beiping by Tao Ran, the head of the enemy's intelligence department, under the identity of the supervisor of the "Tian No. 1" plan, not only did the enemy situation not become clear, but it became even more confusing.

One covert enemy spy after another died before they were captured, eerie sounds came from the church at night, ghostly faces appeared repeatedly in the dark, innocent child Ye Xiaobao and old night watchman Qi Fu didn't escape the devilish black hand, some individual investigators inside the 201 bureau showed abnormal behavior... All these signs indicated that the enemy's plan, carefully planned and commanded by Tao Ran, was continuously deepening and developing without stopping for a moment. Yuan Fang was very anxious and thinking hard about how to respond to the enemy situation. He and Director Dong Chenggeng of the Intelligence Department analyzed the enemy situation and came to the same conclusion as Zhong Hua: Zhong Hua has been exposed, and behind the extremely abnormal order to continue sending him back to Beiping to supervise the implementation of the "Tian No. 1" plan, there must be a much bigger conspiracy hidden.
