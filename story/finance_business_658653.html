Leping Procuratorate: Being Prepared for Future Trouble and Maintaining Stability by Handling Petitions

by guanglian039 on 2012-02-04 11:27:39

For many years, the Commission for Discipline Inspection of Leiping City has been well prepared and adopted various ways to properly deal with contradictions and disputes, improve the emergency management mechanism, ensure the stability of letters and visits, and create a safe and stable social environment for the 60th anniversary of National Day. The municipal discipline inspection commission mobilized all levels, requiring all units to hold special research meetings in time, focus on the "three cases" prevention mechanism, and actively formulate rectification measures. All towns also held meetings for all government employees and village branch secretaries to deploy the work of letters and visits at all levels, further improve the ideological awareness of party members and cadres, and enhance the sense of responsibility for doing a good job in letters and visits. At the same time, it continues to promote the activity of household listening by deputy section-level leaders or above in the city, and organize all towns to carry out the special activities of "comprehensive investigation and mediation of contradictions and disputes, and building a people-oriented government". After a comprehensive investigation of contradictions and disputes, the municipal discipline inspection commission implemented each letter and visit case to a specific person one by one, formulated the case package responsibility book, and limited the mediation time at the same time. In addition, the municipal discipline inspection commission also implements the weekly report monthly supervision system. Every week, the responsible person of the key letter and visit case forms a written material on the cause, current situation and mediation measures of the letter and visit case for special reports; every month, the municipal discipline inspection commission organizes a patrol supervision to ensure that the problem is finally solved and the letter and visit is finally stopped. Since the beginning of this year, Zhongbu Town has received 12 cases through village visits, resolved 10 cases, persuaded and educated more than 40 petitioners. Through publicity, mobilization, investigation, rectification, the work of letters and visits in the city has shown a gratifying trend of "one rise, one fall, one more and one less", that is: the understanding of cadres and party members on the work of letters and visits has greatly improved; the letter and visit problems reflecting that cadres compete with the people for benefits and act recklessly have obviously decreased; more and more agency cadres go to villages and households to understand the real situation, do practical things, solve practical difficulties, and help practical reasons; the number of over-level visits, collective visits and纠缠visits of the masses has obviously decreased.
