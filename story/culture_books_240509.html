"The Color of Passion" Three Indivisible Segments of Love

by jqrxass on 2009-09-20 13:28:53

The young fashion designer Cheng Xi grew up in a single-parent family. She didn't want to immerse herself in the gloom of her family, so she came alone to the bustling city of Guangzhou, believing that "love is the best container for suffering." However, in this city full of desires and temptations, finding love and a sense of belonging always went against her wishes.

"Original Colors" revolves around the event of Cheng Xi's carefully crafted "Original Colors" fashion design being plagiarized, describing her transformation from a fashion designer to an online writer. During this time, three men with completely different personalities who each loved Cheng Xi in their own way gave her care and passion, but also brought her pain, confusion, bewilderment, and struggle. One was her first love; when they met again, both already had lovers, yet they couldn't forget their purest love. Another was her online lover, a spiritual support during lonely and helpless times, but transitioning from the virtual world to reality posed many obstacles. The last one was her cohabiting boyfriend, whose genuine concern and silent support made life peaceful, but over time became monotonous and dull.

Ideals versus reality, past versus future, rationality versus emotion - these three relationships intertwined like the three primary colors in a colorful life, portraying the rich forms of urban emotional life.

Original Colors Part 1

I grew up in a single-parent family. Since I could remember, my mother never laughed happily, and I even doubted if she was born unable to laugh. I lived with my grandmother and mother because my grandfather formed a new family outside. My grandmother's wrinkles were filled with painful memories.

When I was twelve years old, I found out that I had a father, but the person who raised me until I was six was my adoptive father. His glorious image in my mind collapsed, and for a while, I couldn't think straight, almost running away from home. My grandmother told me about my mother's past, and only then did I realize that someone who never smiled once bloomed like a flower many years ago. My mother also had a man who loved her, but when that man abandoned her and flew far away, she withered ever since. Afterwards, she had no choice but to marry my adoptive father, and as the price for burying that love, she conceived a small life, which was me. My grandmother said it was the fruit of pain, and my mother suffered from difficult labor when giving birth to me. At the time, she barely survived, and for this, she always resented fate for not letting her die peacefully.

Original Colors Part 2

He put down the blueprint and scanned the furnishings in the room. When he saw the men's leather shoes in the shoe cabinet behind the door, his face clearly darkened. "Shouldn't your boyfriend be back by now?" He asked tentatively.

"He's not my boyfriend," I was drawn to the shoe cabinet by his gaze. "There's a man who rents the same place as me. He likes nightlife, so I rarely see him." The room was very quiet, and we could hear each other's breathing. I wondered why I should explain so much to him. Apart from our old friendship, what else do we have to do with each other? I stood up and turned on the stereo. The loud music startled him, and I immediately turned down the volume. The room soon filled with the soft, melodious sounds of jazz blues, which always reminded me of the afternoon we spent watching movies.

Original Colors Part 3

"I want to see you - I want the feeling of holding you, girl, do you miss me?" His breathing was clear and audible, making me inexplicably excited.

Is this the feeling of love? This sound makes me feel happy, and I am instantly intoxicated by his voice.

"Yes, I want to see you too, really - very much." My voice was like under a spell, why do I want to see him so much? On such a night, facing the dim lights outside the window, I suddenly thought that if he were in this little room, tonight's lights would certainly be the most splendid and enchanting, and I would be that lamp, lighting up only for him.

"Girl, let me hold you on the phone, okay? I miss you so much." His panting hit my ears, making me feel a warm current flow through my heart again. I'm sure that's love.

Original Colors Part 4

His lips gently touched mine, and I struggled feebly before succumbing.

It didn't take long for my lips to go from stiff resistance to moistness. When that trembling gradually turned into a tacit acceptance, everything around us stopped moving. In this world, there were only the two of us, only our breaths intertwined. As he carried me towards the bed, I just hoped that all of this wouldn't end too quickly. I yearned for time to stop still for us, although that wasn't possible. If desire can reflect one's attitude towards life, for me, I only hope to give it to one person, it belongs only to the person I deeply love, though that isn't possible either.
