The night has already fallen.

by hairtaoyts on 2011-09-10 17:55:53

(Edited by: Absolute Love)

The night was deepening, and the night in Dongri obviously lacked one kind of music — the chirping of crickets. Thus, the night seemed even quieter. This is the first time I've written anything this year, on this sleepless night, and I really don't know what to write about. Over time, too many things have changed; there are fewer and fewer people around me that I can trust, and even fewer who care for me or love me.

In this late hour, the only thing that hasn't changed is still the music from yesterday, and I can only let it accompany me to sleep. The quieter the night, the more uneasy it makes people feel. I don't know who I've thought of again? Or maybe I didn't clearly say who it was. For a moment, so many faces appeared, some clear, some blurry, some pitiful, some loving. All of these always touch my heart in the dead of night. It's bitter, it's painful, and it always keeps me awake for a long time. These fleeting thoughts always bother me, and I can't help but wonder, if I never possessed something, why do I have such profound memories! Perhaps, this is just a feeling, a single feeling, and I can only believe that it is a feeling.

Stumbling along the way, fiery passion is always mercilessly put on hold. After walking for a long time, one gets tired, and when tired, everything seems to become clear. People are always sentimental while simultaneously acting coldly, which is undoubtedly self-deprecating. Life is like this, and the path of love is even more precarious! Last night, I had a dream, a distant and desolate dream, where flowers bloomed and withered. When the horses returned, their hooves trampled through the fields as petals fell with the wind, quiet as settled dust. In the year when the flowers fell, it was you who stirred my heartstrings, and in the subsequent years, they were alone in their drunkenness in dreams, as if the dreamers themselves were powerless to save themselves. Time flows easily, and the road ahead has always been cautious!

Feelings, because of feelings, this winter has been very long. I almost forgot the scent of flowers, forgot spring. Such nostalgia made me recall a line: "Seasonal birds miss their old forests, and pond fish think of their former springs." I don't know if this is attachment or habit, or perhaps just a way of life. Because life everywhere reveals love, and love is a distant road. This road is rugged and full of twists and turns, and unfeeling people always move forward uneasily on this road. The city nights are brilliantly lit by streetlights, yet everywhere there are crossroads. Everywhere there are signs, yet people are always confused. Bored and lost individuals always appear there, they are bewildered, aimless, thus losing the direction of life, without the focus of love. The urban night scenery is truly beautiful, but in my eyes, it suddenly seems so unreal.

At this moment, I can no longer feel the heartbeat, and what follows is chaotic thoughts. The evening city, though showing the setting sun, lacks the tranquility of a village. What prevails instead is congestion, busyness, and noise. Among all this, love and being loved are undeniably present. The struggles in the crowd, the sweat in the hustle, the helplessness in the clamor, all reveal the world's selfless and pure love. Yes, they crowd for love, they hustle for love, they clamor for love, just like a kind of love, once started, it never stops. I don't know when it began, but I like quietly gazing at construction sites, liking to watch them work. In summer, they are bare under the scorching sun, drenched in sweat. In winter, they brave the wind, and looking at their cracked hands, I begin to reflect! I once asked myself, what are those people striving for... Later, I understood, it's responsibility, it's love, it's fatherly love. Perhaps, life can be divided into several levels, or perhaps it can be ranked higher or lower. But when it comes to love, it is always equal on the scale, because of love, we learn to endure, because of love, we learn to persevere. And aren't our loved ones also treading this perilous path of love?

Because of life, we are destined to drift, and those who stand firm always hope for a harbor. Returning to the tranquil village, everything is peaceful, and this environment is undoubtedly the most beautiful under certain circumstances. Beautiful sunsets always give people different feelings. As the sun sets, broken-hearted people are far away. Ah! Such poetic evenings are bound to bury this poetic youth!

Walking along the country paths, going upwind, I realized that the roads in the village lack road signs, but ultimately, no one gets lost. Even strangers who come here have never heard of anyone getting lost because of unfamiliarity with a place, because there are always enthusiastic people willing to guide you. Isn't this also a kind of signpost! A signpost of love, forever in the heart. Here, the night sky is always bright, and tonight, it is full of stars. This beauty deserves praise and admiration! The night deepened, the starry river became even brighter, the village grew quiet, and did your heart also stir... ...

Written on the second night of the New Year
