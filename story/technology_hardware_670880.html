White Vinegar Beauty Method:

1. Each time you wash your face, put a small basin of water, add a small amount (about two tablespoons) of white vinegar, mix it evenly into the water, splash it on your face, or simply immerse your face in warm water. After that, pour out the water and start the normal face-washing procedure.

2. White vinegar can reduce wrinkles: After washing your face at night, mix 1 spoon of vinegar and 3 spoons of water, soak a cotton ball, gently rub on the areas of your face with wrinkles, then massage lightly with your fingertips, and rinse. This way can help eliminate small wrinkles on your face.

3. White vinegar makes the skin tender: First wash your face and hands, then immerse them in warm water with added vinegar to wash your face and hands. After 5 minutes, rinse with clear water. Doing this long-term can make the skin smooth and delicate. The amount of vinegar added to the water should be small, just enough so that the water does not change color.

4. White vinegar removes spots: Mix an appropriate amount of white atractylodes powder into white vinegar, seal it and soak for a week. Every day after washing your face, wipe the areas of your face with spots, over time it can gradually remove and fade the spots.

5. White vinegar removes fatigue: When taking a bath, add 1-2 tablespoons of vinegar to warm water. After bathing, it can not only remove the old stratum corneum of the skin but also eliminate fatigue, refresh the spirit, and make the face appear rosy.

6. White vinegar controls dandruff: Before going to bed every night, use a 1:1 mixture of vinegar and water to moisten the areas with dandruff, gently rub the roots of your hair, after 10 minutes, rinse with clear water. Doing this can control excessive production of dandruff and can ultimately cure it. Washing your hair with warm water containing vinegar also works.

7. White vinegar enhances hair beauty: After each wash with neutral shampoo, rinse your hair with warm water mixed with a small amount of vinegar, and rinse with clear water after 20 minutes. Gradually, your hair will become soft, shiny, black, and beautiful.

8. White vinegar softens the skin: Mix white vinegar and water in a 1:3 ratio, which can be separately stored in a mineral water bottle. Every morning and evening after washing your face, gently pat the vinegar water onto your face with your palms, you don't need to use too much, just a little bit. When you feel your whole face is moist, that's enough. Occasionally, you can also wash your face with this water.

Another method:

Every night after washing your face, apply a small amount of white vinegar evenly on your face (avoiding areas around the eyes), leave it for ten to fifteen minutes, until you feel a dry film on your face, then rinse with clear water. The first time you do this, you might feel a slight pain like you're disinfecting your face, don't worry because vinegar has a natural disinfecting effect. Endure it, after washing your face, you'll find the areas where you applied vinegar are slightly red, don't worry, sleep overnight and you'll be fine (so it can only be done at night). You need to persist; doing it once or twice won't show obvious results, but after a week, you'll find your skin becomes finer and you'll notice yourself getting whiter.
