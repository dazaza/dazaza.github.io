7 Small SEO Execution Optimization Methods

Heima SEO

1: BLOG Hosting Platform Selection.

From the moment Baidu Space was launched, we realized its power. After all, it's the son of Baidu, the Chinese search giant, and its ranking in Baidu is not to be underestimated. It’s worth mentioning that when I initially chose multiple websites to build the Heima blog group for marketing, it wasn’t aimed at creating a site cluster to participate in any competition. If there was participation, then it would be this one. Its friendliness is better, and I wanted to experiment following its approach. Now it's clear that Baidu Space has a significant advantage. Additionally, I found that industry blogs have very high weight.

2: Choice of Site Name.

This is also crucial. Why did I change the blog name from "Very Search Engine Optimization" to the current "SEO Beginner Books Official BLOG"? First, I used "veryseo" as the suffix of the space, highlighting the term "SEO," and directly named the blog "SEO Beginner Books." To prevent duplication with others, I added the phrase "Official BLOG" to increase credibility.

3: Blog Introduction.

This is equivalent to what we commonly refer to as the website description, and its writing method is also worth noting. During the competition, I noticed two sites whose rankings surged dramatically, even reaching the top at one point. Their introductions also highlighted keywords, but I felt it was a bit excessive, which could amount to keyword stuffing if overused.

4: Link Building.

Someone once said that besides internal optimization skills, SEO is also about competing for link resources. The importance of links is self-evident. How to build links? Some people might immediately think of mass distribution. Of course, mass distribution is much faster, saving time and effort. However, true SEO does not advocate mass distribution because the quality is generally low, and it can even backfire.

5: Article Update Techniques. First, search engines prefer original content. Time is an important factor in determining originality. I once wrote an article titled "How to Use Timeliness for Optimization." At appropriate times, highlight the time. For example, the college entrance exam date of June 7th, or the 10th anniversary of Hong Kong's return, etc. China is a country with a long cultural history, and every day is special. Remember, I often posted "Today in History" on my own blog marketing site. Furthermore, updates should be frequent; search engines don't like static sites. Moreover, article titles can include keywords, and the title is quite important. Additionally, while writing articles, consider adding keywords in the first paragraph.

6: Absorb others' experiences, communicate more, practice more, and you'll progress faster. When searching for SEO, the sites ranked at the front are generally good. You can take a look.

Why are experts so skilled? The key lies in the tools and resources they possess that you don't.

7: Innovation is essential. That is your advantage; the more advantages you have, the more opportunities you have to succeed. Simply copying others may yield short-term rankings.
