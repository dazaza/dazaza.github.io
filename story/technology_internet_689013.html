Two computer products from Patriot and Tsinghua Tongfang exceed radiation standards

by xue94fwsh on 2012-03-03 17:11:00

Shenzhen announces product quality inspection results

Xinkuaibao News - Reporter Xiaoxiao Dai, Yandan Feng, Intern Mingyue Li. Yesterday, the Shenzhen Market Supervision Administration announced the product supervision and inspection results for the 4th quarter of 2011 on its website. Among them, the Aigo (Weibo) tablet computer E700R and Tsinghua Tongfang K465 were found to exceed the standard for radiation disturbance, which could "interfere" with the normal operation of other electrical appliances during use. A Supor luxury smart rice cooker was found to have an unqualified abnormal working item, indicating insufficient safety protection for users.

Rare sales of two computer products

According to the "Announcement of the Quality Supervision and Inspection Results of Portable Computers in Shenzhen in 2011" reported by Xinkuaibao, a total of 36 batches of products from 14 enterprises in Shenzhen were inspected this time, with 34 batches passing the inspection. The Aigo E700R (4GB) tablet computer and Tsinghua Tongfang K465 portable notebook were found to have不合格in radiation disturbance items.

It is understood that when products with不合格radiation disturbance items work normally, the interference signals radiated into space will exceed the national standard, possibly affecting the normal use of surrounding equipment.

After visiting the Huaqiangbei computer market in Shenzhen, reporters found that the market price of the Aigo E700R (4GB) tablet computer was about 799 yuan, while the market price of the Tsinghua Tongfang K465 portable notebook was about 2999 yuan, but the sales of these two products were rare. A salesperson said that if a computer has quality problems, it should be returned to the factory for repair until it is qualified before being put back on the market.

Supor Rice Cooker Fails Inspection

In addition, in the fourth quarter of 2011, the Shenzhen Market Supervision Administration also conducted a supervisory inspection of automatic rice cookers sold in Shenzhen. This time, a total of 34 batches of products from 12 enterprises across the city were inspected, with 30 batches passing the inspection. Three batches of samples, including the Supor CFXB40FC11G-75 luxury intelligent rice cooker produced by Zhejiang Supor Home Appliances Manufacturing Co., Ltd., were found to have unqualified abnormal working items.

It is understood that the safety of appliances must be considered during normal use. When users operate improperly, lack electrical knowledge, or the components of the product fail, causing misoperation or abnormal operation, the structure of the appliance should still have certain protective functions to avoid fires, electric shocks, or mechanical damage accidents. Although this Supor rice cooker is "luxurious" and "intelligent", it lacks sufficient protection for users. It is understood that the market price of this product is about 300 yuan.

Regarding the quality problems found in this inspection, the Shenzhen Market Supervision Administration has taken active measures to carry out special inspections throughout the city, removing unqualified products with the above quality problems from the market and handling business units according to law. For products determined to be unqualified after monitoring, the monitored party should immediately stop selling them and take effective measures to inform consumers.

Consumer Reminder

Shenzhen Market Supervision Administration:

Do not blindly pursue "lightweight" when choosing computers

At the same time as announcing the product sampling results, the Shenzhen Market Supervision Administration reminded consumers that there is no need to blindly pursue performance indicators when purchasing portable computers, so as to avoid falling into traps. Tablet computers are currently a consumer hotspot, and many companies overemphasize the thin appearance, create "concepts", and exaggerate performance indicators in advertisements, which do not have much practical significance in actual use.

In addition, apart from the elegant appearance, consumers often easily ignore the "intrinsic quality" of computers. When selecting products, attention should also be paid to the safety and electromagnetic compatibility performance of the products, which are related to the life safety of consumers.
