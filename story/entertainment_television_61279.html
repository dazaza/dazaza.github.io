"Magic Mobile Phone" Plot Introduction:

In the year 2060 AD, mobile phones have already possessed advanced functions such as remote control, self-defense, and gene recombination. One day, a new function that can travel through multiple dimensions was invented, and scientists implanted this function into a mobile phone named "Sabina". During an experiment, the real-life version of Sabina traveled through time to the Tang Dynasty, where she helped Sun Wukong subdue the Yellow Eyebrow King. However, when Sabina's energy was depleted, the scientists urgently recalled her back to the year 2060. Unexpectedly, the Yellow Eyebrow King also clung onto Sabina's clothes and refused to let go. Additionally, the scientists mistakenly entered the year 2006 instead of 2060. Thus, Sabina and the Yellow Eyebrow King ended up in the year 2006 and got separated in the time tunnel. The lost mobile phone Sabina was picked up by the protagonist, Lu Xiaoqian. Xiaoqian is a person with a strong sense of justice but has a timid personality. He detests all kinds of injustices in society but lacks the courage to intervene. Upon encountering unfair situations, he dare not stand up for himself. The appearance of the magic mobile phone changed Xiaoqian's character, encouraging him to bravely fight against the main antagonist, You Suowei. Initially, You Suowei was a law-abiding youth in modern society, but it was also the magic mobile phone that altered his personality. To obtain the magic mobile phone, he resorted to any means necessary, committing many despicable deeds. In the end, under the efforts of Lu Xiaoqian, Sun Wukong, Zhu Bajie, and other good friends, both the magic mobile phone and the Yellow Eyebrow King were sent back to their original place. You Suowei once again became a law-abiding citizen, while Lu Xiaoqian no longer remained timid and cowardly as before.
