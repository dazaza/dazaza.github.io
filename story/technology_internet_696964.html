How could Mr. Kang's key open Ms. Yang's security door?

Mr. Kang lived on the 9th floor of the building and had been there for six years. Many of his neighbors in the building were old acquaintances. At that moment, he heard voices coming from the inner room and suddenly sobered up - his wife was on a business trip, so who could be in his house? Just then, the lights came on, and two people rushed out from the inner room. To his surprise, they turned out to be Ms. Yang, his downstairs neighbor, and her daughter.

Ms. Yang from the 6th floor said that she had been sleeping soundly when she was awakened by noises from the living room. Gathering her courage, she and her daughter got up to investigate and encountered Mr. Kang, which left them utterly confused. "Had I not known Mr. Kang as an old neighbor, I would have thought someone had broken into my house!" Ms. Yang remarked.
