The opening of the new store is entering the countdown - Yunnan Road Store in Nanjing Yanghe is looking forward to your visit!

by chjdcj on 2012-02-25 14:41:36

Nanjing Yanghe specialty stores currently has four, and the opening of the new store on Yunnan Road is also about to enter a countdown. The Yunnan Road store of Nanjing Yanghe is under the Nanjing Dazhongjiu Trading Co., Ltd. The company was authorized by Jiangsu Yanghe Distillery as the first-level agency group purchaser for the "Yanghe Blue Classic" brand series in Nanjing area. Established on January 5, 2009, the company mainly operates high-end white spirits such as Yanghe Blue Classic - Sea Blue, Sky Blue, Dream Blue series, Changyu, original bottle imported CASTEL, Lafite and other wines, as well as high-end cigarettes such as Zhonghua and Su Yan.

Nanjing, as the region with the highest sales volume of Yanghe liquor, has specialty stores at various locations. Longpan Middle Road, Central Road, Aoti, Baixia Road. For specific addresses, please click http://www.dcjoin.com [http://www.dcjoin.com] to view. Currently, the Yunnan Road store is the fifth branch store that is ready to go. It still mainly focuses on the Yanghe Blue Series as its main product. Customers who need can go there to purchase.

Currently, the Yunnan Road store of Nanjing Yanghe is also recruiting staff. Telemarketers and shop assistants are being recruited. Telemarketing is mainly done over the phone. Shop assistants work shifts, responsible for receiving customers in the store and finding new customers. Salary is base salary + commission + allowance, and once employed, the benefits are excellent. As long as the person agrees with the corporate culture and is willing to accept the challenge of working more and earning more, they can call Store Manager Shen Ying at 025-83208398.

Our advantages of Nanjing Yanghe specialty stores:

The company improves business process design through perfecting it, strictly controlling the product flow in every link. First, our goods are directly supplied by the Suqian Yanghe Distillery Co., Ltd., with a weekly supply of 1-2 times, each time around 400 boxes. Special personnel are responsible for warehouse entry and custody, with multiple surveillance cameras monitoring 24 hours a day. Outbound is through the financial department issuing an outbound order, warehouse picking and scanning the logistics code of each box of wine (the logistics code corresponds to the bottle code inside the box, which can uniquely identify the product source), summarizing the product name and the encoding of each box of wine, printing the warehouse delivery order, and stamping with the company's dedicated delivery seal. Each vehicle of the company is equipped with a GPS monitoring system to monitor the delivery route and prevent the goods from being swapped during transportation. When delivering to the customer, the customer verifies whether the product name and box code on the delivery note match the actual delivery. If there is no problem, sign for receipt, and the customer keeps the stamped copy for future reference.
