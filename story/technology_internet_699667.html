Second: Learn to combine theory with practice. As the saying goes, "Practice is the sole criterion for testing truth." How should I apply the information I have filtered out? For many webmasters, if after reading an SEO material they only stay at the level of reading without putting it into practice (http://www.seo-ee.com/Nanjing SEO), then everything they have seen and learned is just talk. Third: Cleverly use long-tail keywords instead of main keywords. A large portion of a website's ranking owes to keyword rankings. Therefore, keyword optimization has become an important part of search engine optimization. Of course, for a keyword to bring traffic to a site (http://www.seo-ee.com/Nanjing Baidu Optimization), selecting the right keyword is quite crucial. However, in today’s internet era where SEO is overused, it is almost impossible for new webmasters to compete with older sites for keyword rankings. Thus, long-tail keywords come into play. How should I select these long-tail keywords? In selecting long-tail keywords, I need to understand the user's psychology because long-tail keywords directly reflect user needs. For example, a commonly used phrase like "which SEO optimization company is good" indicates that users are focused on the price and effect of SEO optimization. Therefore, once they land on your site, the chances of cooperation increase. For beginners, when selecting long-tail keywords, they should find words related to their website content with a certain search volume and less competition, making it easier to see results and boost confidence.

Fourth: Update website content regularly. As the saying goes, "Laws are objectively present and do not change according to people's will." For a website, regularity is something webmasters should focus on. For instance, if a friend comes to your house every day but the time is uncertain, they may not find you often, and gradually stop visiting. On the contrary, if you are at home at a fixed time every day, friends will visit more frequently. Here, the so-called friend refers to Baidu Spider. Baidu Spider prefers websites with regular updates; otherwise, no matter how fresh your website content is, the spider may not detect it.

Fifth: Look ahead, insist on original content, and avoid pseudo-original content. Original content is particularly important for search engines, determining your website's inclusion, external links, rankings, etc. Some webmasters have said that pseudo-original articles can also bring good traffic and rankings to websites. It is undeniable that pseudo-original articles can bring some benefits to websites, but these benefits are temporary. From a long-term perspective, Baidu Spider is not an ordinary spider. After thoroughly analyzing your pseudo-original content, it will discard it while retaining original content. External links may experience significant fluctuations, easily leading to a decrease in website authority.
