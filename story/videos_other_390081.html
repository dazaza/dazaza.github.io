III. Market Research Summary Analysis

1. The 21st century is the digital information age, the Internet age. According to Taobao's publicly disclosed data, in 2007, Taobao's transaction volume was 43.3 billion yuan, ranking second in China's retail industry after the Bailian Group. Currently, the average daily transaction volume provided by their Alipay transaction amount is nearly 3 billion yuan. In contemporary society, with the deepening of China's reform and opening up, cities are getting bigger, work hours are getting longer, and traffic is getting busier. Shopping through e-commerce (online shopping) will only deepen further and be adopted by more people.

Advantages of the website: Buying a seasonal piece of clothing using traditional methods requires setting aside specific time, going to the business district to browse, pick, try on, and buy. If dissatisfied later, there's also the return process. From this aspect, buying this piece of clothing costs not only the price tag but also time and transportation costs, which are labor-intensive. In contrast, online shopping involves clicking a mouse, completing selection and comparison within half an hour, except for the inability to try on, everything is done, allowing one to wait at ease for the clothing to arrive. Moreover, the price is absolutely cheaper than in malls (because no property, personnel, advertising, etc., costs need to be averaged). It saves both time and money.

At the same time, we can frequently distribute questionnaires for terminal user surveys to understand what services terminal users truly need and how to promote our brand, always keeping track of market conditions.

2. Target Market Setting: Men's wear, women's wear, and children's wear focus on young consumer groups aged between 20 and 40. These consumers will be our largest customer base.

3. Product Consumer Groups, Consumption Methods, Consumption Habits, and Main Factors Affecting the Market Analysis: With the development of the Internet and e-commerce, most young consumers increasingly prefer online shopping because it saves shopping time and provides a large number of products for consumers to choose from, making it very convenient. Modern young people adapt well to online shopping, which is greatly advantageous for our online store, providing us with many business opportunities. However, the biggest concern with online shopping remains credibility issues. Therefore, our marketing model will adopt simultaneous operation of physical stores and online stores, significantly enhancing our online store's credibility.

4. Market Trend Forecast and Market Opportunities: After our market research, we found that the proportion of daily necessities purchases has significantly increased. This upward trend in the purchase of daily necessities will continue in the future and eventually become the mainstream of online shopping. Therefore, we have chosen this category as our main product.

IV. Competitive Analysis

1. Industry Monopoly: No industry monopoly exists.

2. Major Competitors' Situation: Company strength, product situation (types, prices, features, packaging, marketing, market share, etc.): After investigating several similar product online stores, we found that our competitors are relatively mature, but the operating models they use have some obvious mistakes. We conducted in-depth research on some consumer groups, and they still worry most about being deceived when shopping online (for consumers, the popularity of clothing items significantly affects their purchasing decisions). After these in-depth understandings, I believe it will be highly beneficial for the development of our online store. Currently, the variety of products in these online stores is acceptable, but for consumers, the credibility is still not high enough, and the product popularity is insufficient, which is advantageous for us.

3. Company Product Competitive Advantage: We adopt the simultaneous operation of online and physical stores to enhance our online store's credibility; furthermore, during the early stages of online store operations, we will vigorously promote advertisements to increase the popularity of our online store's products. On this point, we will collaborate with major clothing manufacturers, and every product purchased from our online store will have our online store's label or logo sewn onto the back collar of clothes or the back waistband of pants. This way, our online store's advertising costs will be significantly reduced while increasing our online store's popularity. And we provide online consultation to help customers understand our products at any time.

V. Main Strategic Development Goals and Stage Goals of the Online Store

1. Strategic Goal: To make online shopping simpler, more efficient, more trustworthy, and accepted by different groups of people. Let our customers enjoy a happy, simple life.

2. Stage Goals: Find suitable sources of goods for your online store to ensure quality and quantity, gradually penetrate the market, develop customers. To enhance the popularity of your own shop, during the early stages of opening the store, appropriate marketing promotions should be carried out, but limiting them to the internet alone is insufficient; multiple channels both online and offline should be used for promotion.

3. Schedule: First stage: Market research, analysis of questionnaires, understanding customer needs;

Second stage: Confirming the source of goods, developing customers;

Third stage: Opening of the physical store, opening and maintenance of the Taobao store;

Fourth stage: Promotion and publicity;

Fifth stage: Entering regular operations for both the online store and physical store;

Sixth stage: Expanding the online store;

Ultimate goal: Use the online store to create a globally connected chain "store", i.e., having warehouses in various locations across the country, capable of delivering orders accurately and promptly to specified locations.

VI. Marketing

1. Sales Policy Formulation: Physical store marketing + online marketing. A physically accessible store allows customers to feel a sense of security and stability when shopping online. Meanwhile, during the initial phase of online store marketing, due to the high cost of advertising, part of the funds can come from the profit of physical store sales. When the online store grows larger, it can, in turn, benefit the physical store, promoting each other, publicizing each other, combining the two naturally to bring faster economic returns.

Using multiple online stores to sell the same online store's products, i.e., in the early stages, we will use multiple Taobao accounts to uniformly sell our online store's products. This way, adding our brand to multiple Taobao stores will naturally increase the popularity and brand awareness of our online store.

2. Sales Channels, Methods, Marketing Links, and After-sales Service: Online sales + physical store sales, focused service

From storefront selection to storefront design, considering differences in region, season, consumption habits, and economic levels comprehensively for distributors. Assisting in reasonable inventory allocation, carefully planning promotional gifts, and meticulously arranging all aspects to provide comprehensive services and powerful market strategies, advertising promotion, image packaging, and breakthrough planning.

① The physical store will be located at the junction of the city center and suburban areas, ensuring a moderate expense while securing a steady flow of customers.

② During the early stages of the online store’s operation, delivery will be handled via courier services to save on logistics costs. Once the online store grows larger, and the volume of goods to be delivered increases significantly, we will opt for self-managed logistics to reduce expensive courier fees.

③ Warehouses across the country will stock different products based on local consumption levels and habits.

3. Main Business Relationship Status: Our online store is a retailer

Qualification Standards at All Levels: Payment methods: Online payment, cash on delivery, etc., available for consumer selection; Freight methods: Courier services and postal services initially, followed by logistics; Discount policies: Products are already sold at discounted prices to ensure affordability.

4. Sales Force Status and Sales Welfare Distribution Policies: Besides salaries and commissions based on sales volume, we will also allow all employees to own shares in our online store to fully motivate employee enthusiasm.

5. Promotion and Market Penetration (Methods and Arrangements, Budget)

① Main promotional methods: Offering small gifts, choosing to waive part of the shipping fee based on price, striving to maximize customer satisfaction.

② Payment methods: Multiple options including Alipay, online banking, cash on delivery, etc., enhancing user experience to promote sales.

③ Advertising/PR Strategies, Media Evaluation: Adding advertisements on large video websites (such as Youku, Tudou, Pipi Film, etc.), placing ads on the Taobao homepage, promoting through blogs, and posting promotional plans on Baidu Tieba.

Marketing Model Structure:

Self-developed online sales website + online store (Taobao, EachNet, Paipai) + Blogs (NetEase Blog, Sina Blog, Sohu Blog).

Core Marketing Operation Model:

User Membership System:

Although the membership system is old, it remains a core part of marketing online stores, serving as the link between the store and its members. The success of the membership system is a standard for evaluating the success of a marketing online store. From a Web 2.0 perspective, designing the membership system focuses on engaging users, enhancing interaction, incentivizing consumption, and retaining users.

① User Initiative: Providing a natural and free user experience where users can browse products freely and comment on and review products.

② User Interaction: Establishing a new section on the online store for users to exchange ideas, discuss, and share opinions on styles, colors, fabrics, etc.

③ User Incentives: Implementing a points system where all user actions on the online store can earn points. For example, commenting on products, sharing personal care tips, and purchasing products. Benefits of points: Dividing membership levels, enjoying discounts or special offers during holidays, free gifts, etc.

Product Structure Optimization:

① Friendly and Comprehensive Product Display: Presenting products in a friendly, comprehensive, and clear manner to leave a good first impression before transactions. Due to the extensive product system of online stores, the overall feeling might be cluttered. Now we categorize products in detail, specifying the number/types of products under each series.

② User-Oriented Product Display: Breaking down all products according to target user groups, guiding users to find suitable products quickly.

③ Intelligent Product Display: Adding search boxes for products and knowledge to facilitate user usage.

Word-of-Mouth Marketing:

Leveraging user promotion to advertise our online store and products, rewarding users who refer others with points and discounts. Word-of-mouth is the best marketing method. Users can promote both online and offline. Online methods include sending our website or product page addresses via QQ/MSN/email to friends and acquaintances. Offline methods involve introducing our online store and product names to family and friends via phone, text messages, or word of mouth.

Online Store Marketing Model:

Taobao (C2C/B2C)

Making full use of these online stores to gain direct buyers online, thereby bringing users to the main online store. (The key lies in practice and mastering online store trading skills.)

Blog Promotion Model:

Fully utilizing major portal blogs to promote our products and online store. Do not underestimate these blogs, as some blog sections have millions of online users daily.

Purpose and Advantages of the Online Store

Establishing a New Corporate Image: For a creative lifestyle products company, corporate branding is crucial. Especially in today's era of highly developed internet technology, most customers learn about corporate products, corporate image, and corporate strength through the internet. Therefore, the image of the corporate online store often determines customer confidence in the company's products. Establishing an international-standard online store can greatly enhance the overall image of the enterprise.

Optimizing Internal Corporate Management: Building the corporate online store will introduce a new management model within the company, with the online store serving as the platform for this model. It plays a significant role in reducing internal resource waste, cutting costs, strengthening connections and communication between employees and between the company and employees, ultimately achieving maximum optimization of the company's operations and functions.

Enhancing Sales Power: Sales power refers to the embodiment of the product's overall advantage in sales. Modern marketing theory suggests that sales are essentially communication, and the success of sales depends not only on whether the various advantages of the product can be fully communicated but also on how much effective information the target audience receives.

Due to the "one-to-one" nature of the internet, target audiences can autonomously choose useful information for themselves, which already indicates an interested premise. This makes the transmission of information no longer subjective but rather actively absorbed by the consumer. Simultaneously, advanced online store designs combine the advantages of newspapers in terms of large information volume with the integrated stimulation of sound, light, and electricity from television, firmly capturing the attention of the target audience. Therefore, the effectiveness of product information dissemination will be greatly enhanced, thereby also improving the product's sales power.

Increasing Added Value: Many people know that buying products involves not just visible physical items but also invisible after-sales services. This is the added value of the product, and the higher the added value, the more competitive the product becomes in the market and the more popular it is among consumers. Therefore, companies must strive to increase the added value of their products to win the market. At this stage, traditional after-sales service methods are far from meeting customer needs. Providing convenient, effective, and immediate 24-hour online services to consumers is a new way to reflect project added value. Customers worldwide can download the materials they need at any time through the online store, obtain answers to questions online, and submit their issues online.

Promotion Plan for Online Store Products

① Discount Promotion: Since online sales cannot give people a comprehensive and intuitive impression of the product, nor can they be tried or touched, along with the complexity of delivery costs and payment methods, these factors reduce the enthusiasm for online shopping and ordering. Large discounts can encourage consumers to try online shopping and make purchasing decisions.

② Gift Promotion: The advantages of gift promotions include enhancing your brand recognition; encouraging people to visit the online store frequently to get more discount information; summarizing and analyzing marketing effects and product reactions based on the enthusiasm of consumers seeking gifts. However, be sure not to choose inferior or substandard products as gifts, as doing so would have the opposite effect.

③ Limited-Time Supply: "Limited-time supply" is always tempting to consumers. Promotional slogans like "Special price for only 2 days!" can be used, but "limited-time supply" must be genuine; otherwise, you risk losing consumer trust.

④ Establishing More Friend Links with Other Online Stores: As the saying goes, "More friends, more paths." On the web, "more links, more windows." By exchanging store links, a small network can be formed, enhancing mutual influence. Especially when someone gets recommended on the homepage, you also benefit indirectly. When you become influential, others can also benefit from you. We will collaborate with sister online stores, such as giving away small gifts provided by sister stores when consumers buy from our online store, and vice versa, aiming to promote our small store's products.

⑤ Utilizing Other Promotion Methods: Our salespeople can promote in various forums, QQ groups, MSN, etc.

VII. Risk Analysis

1. Resource (Raw Materials / Suppliers) Risks

Our online store's goods will primarily be carefully selected by the owner, integrating fashion, individuality, and quality; secondly, they can add our online store's label (marking the online store's label or LOGO on the back collar of clothes or the back waistband of pants). However, our store will offer the best, finest, and most beautiful products, aiming to provide the most economical and convenient gift services, specifically offering fashionable, individual, and high-quality apparel. Selecting raw material suppliers and establishing good supply-demand relationships ensures access to first-hand trendy goods, guaranteeing low online sales prices, while ensuring all products carry our online store's label or LOGO.

2. Inventory Risks

① Single inventory not matching customer preferences.

② Quality issues with goods sourced from wholesale markets (like Guangzhou VIP E-commerce Technology Co., Ltd.) where returns or exchanges are not possible.

③ Work errors by inventory personnel.

④ Inaccurate procurement forecasts exceeding budgets.

⑤ Unexpected accidents during transport (traffic accidents causing casualties).

3. Market Uncertainty Risks

Market share is an important indicator of a company's successful development. Most of the funds for e-commerce companies are used for product procurement and inventory. The quantity of products must be determined based on market share and sales volume. Through market size, current status, trends, network consumer demands, pricing, marketing strategies, promotional activities; product procurement, inventory management, safety stock levels, etc., analyze the selection of target markets.

4. R&D Risks

Favorite styles and varieties may not appeal to customers, leading to incomplete varieties and specifications, resulting in product accumulation and putting corporate funds into an economic crisis.

5. Production Uncertainty Risks

Product quality is critical to the survival of a business, and quality is a key factor in enhancing corporate credibility. The chain reaction where one consumer influences at least 20 others around them shows the importance of quality to customers. Merchants bear responsibility for product quality issues.

6. Inventory Risk

① Accumulated inventory prevents capital circulation, lacking funds for purchasing and restocking.

② Accumulated inventory occupies storage space, preventing the purchase of new goods.

7. Cost Control Risks

Analyze the financial status of the enterprise mainly from capital investment, product cost analysis, period expenses, product sales profit, forecasted cash flow statement, forecasted profit statement, forecasted balance sheet, main business profit margin, total asset profit table, cost-expense profit margin, etc.

8. Cash Flow Risks

① Accumulated unsold inventory leads to uncollected payments.

② Unable to purchase new goods, leading to outdated products and loss of customer purchasing desire.

③ Inability to timely pay employee wages.

9. Competition Risks

In an era of fierce competition, it is necessary to clearly understand the various situations of competitors, comparing technical aspects, product prices, services, etc., to formulate competitive strategies, identifying strengths and weaknesses, adapting better to the times.

10. Transaction Security Risks

① Network data transmission risks of data theft or damage.

② Damage during transport or incomplete carton packaging.

11. Policy Risks

In the 21st century, the development of e-commerce has become a technical means and method of economic activity in the information age, entering the broadest application field of the Internet. Economic crises also bring risks, with unpredictable market changes, government decisions responding to market fluctuations, predicting government macro and micro policies.

12. Financial Risks

Since enterprises have liabilities, there are bound to be risks, requiring reduction of blindness and enhancement of scientific approaches at the start. When the enterprise's pre-tax and interest rate on borrowed capital exceeds the interest rate on borrowed capital, the profit obtained from using borrowed capital compensates for the interest and leaves surplus, thus increasing the profit on own capital; otherwise, the enterprise needs to repay principal and interest on borrowed capital, falling into financial risks, necessitating strict control over fixed costs relative to sales revenue. Due to the uncertainty of the difference between the enterprise's pre-tax and interest rate on borrowed capital, there needs to be a reasonable capital structure, maintaining an appropriate level of debt, fully utilizing borrowing as a means to achieve financial gains, enhancing profitability.

13. Management Risks (Including Personnel/Employee Turnover/Key Employee Dependence)

Investment is a business of managing talent. Initially, we build a management team with direct technology and experience, formulating practical decision-making, executing the most efficient tasks, inviting personnel with various professional techniques and management experience to join and assume important positions. Due to employee turnover, a reasonable employee hiring criterion is needed, creating a sustainable harmonious environment, controlling emergencies.

14. Bankruptcy Risks

Due to increasingly fierce market competition, lack of management experience, low management quality, single business operation modes, markets not fully accepting our products, product stagnation in online stores, the risk of bankruptcy is significant. Only by winning customer purchasing desires and making products unique can we secure a place in the market.

VIII. How to Deal with These Risks

1. Purchase from large nearby wholesale markets. Purchasing from wholesale markets requires strong bargaining power, striving to lower wholesale prices to the minimum, while building good relationships with wholesalers, clarifying the issue of returning and exchanging goods to avoid future disputes; finding products rarely seen offline or not yet circulating in the market, sourcing the cheapest, highest quality, stable supply channels, not just from one supplier, we can seek related unique products for sale and design new products for manufacturers to produce.

2. Purchase at an extremely low price and resell online, leveraging the advantages of online sales, using geographical or temporal price differences to earn sufficient profits. Therefore, you should frequently visit the market to closely monitor market changes.

3. After purchasing, notify both parties' contact details and conduct transactions according to agreed methods, choosing either face-to-face transactions or remittance and mailing methods, with real-time monitoring of items in transit.

4. Place sufficient emphasis on customer relations, establish customer contact cards, and widely publicize positive customer feedback.

5. Set clear goals for each marketing method so customers or potential customers know what to do next.

6. In the information age, use the internet to regularly send relevant product introductions to customers, enabling them to understand the latest discounts, promotions, and new products.

7. Innovation always comes with failure, but in continuous innovation exploration
