Recently, a post titled "Girl has no money to watch the Expo, street kissing seeks creative ideas" has become popular on major websites, drawing much attention from netizens. The girl in the post claimed that she really wanted to see the World Expo but didn't have enough money as she had only worked for two years. In order to raise funds, she wanted to participate in a creative competition. Netizens, however, severely criticized her actions as hype and disturbing the quiet life of the university campus. The girl's act of kissing in the university campus to seek creative ideas for the World Expo was accused by netizens of being a publicity stunt.

"The kiss girl" was accused of courting favor with the crowd.

The kiss to collect creative ideas met with mixed reactions.

At noon on the 13th, the "kiss girl" went to the university town to collect creative ideas from passers-by. She gave a business card with her lip print to those who came to "ask for a kiss." It happened to coincide with the graduation ceremony, and many students were taking pictures in front of the library. When the parents of the graduates saw the "kiss girl" holding a sign "kissing" to seek creative ideas, they all timidly watched from afar.

At the scene, some students did not approve of the "kiss girl's" behavior, thinking it was just a way to attract attention. In response, the "kiss girl" felt wronged and told reporters that she really wanted to visit the World Expo, www.meinv-go.com, but the savings from working for a few years were not enough to cover the travel expenses.
