Many people are surprised by the world under the camera of journalists, many photos are taken by them at the risk of life, many wonderful moments are so short seconds, it is not easy to capture these wonderful moments, I seem to remember there is a story about the world's best news photo selection, which tells the story of a news reporter who committed suicide after winning the first prize. The reason is that the news picture he took was a little African boy starving to death, with a vulture standing next to him, waiting for the child to die, and then eating his flesh. After winning the award, he deeply blamed his conscience for not being able to stand up and save the child, not to mention his human nature. What a heart-wrenching image he captured. Today, here are some of the best photo blogs and websites where you can view news images captured in real time by professional photojournalists.
