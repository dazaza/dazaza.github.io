The crisis prompted a tenfold increase in the number of bookkeepers online

by fenqu on 2009-02-03 18:30:10

This Spring Festival, people know how to budget more carefully. Yesterday, the reporter learned from several large accounting websites in China that during the Spring Festival, hundreds of thousands of people go online every day to record their expenses, treating dinner and sending lucky money has become the biggest expenditure project. It is reported that compared with six months ago, the number of members of many bookkeeping websites has quadrupled, and the activity has reached an all-time high.

Spring Festival spend half a year savings

"Caike Online" is one of the professional accounting websites with the largest number of members in China. Yesterday, the reporter saw on the website that for seven days during the Spring Festival, the number of people logging in to the website every day was more than 100,000. Xiao Qiang announced his Spring Festival consumption bill shows: the cumulative consumption of 13,550 yuan, of which 10,000 yuan is to honor the parents of both sides, the remaining 3550 yuan, in addition to the lucky money sent to the child, is to treat dinner spent. Financial guest online person in charge of introduction, the New Year seven days out of six months of savings are not a small number of people, and dinner, lucky money accounted for most of the cost. China account customer network, baby bookkeeping network and other domestic professional bookkeeping website rough statistics, during the Spring Festival online bookkeeping every day there are hundreds of thousands of people, and last year's Spring Festival, the most is only one to two thousand people, this year increased by about ten times.

Online bookkeeping membership has soared

According to Caike Online, the number of registered members of the website was more than 190,000 from July 1 to November 30, 2008, while the number of members was less than 57,000 from January 1 to June 31, 2008, which is equivalent to more than quadrupling in four months. It is reported that the website since January 2007, the total number of members of 274,200, in the second half of 2008, the number of growth accounted for 70 percent of the proportion. In addition to "financial guest online", the reporter learned that China account guest network, baby account network, etc., in the second half of last year, the number of members doubled, and the activity of new members reached 30%, the highest value in history.

The crisis has prompted more spending and less spending

The reporter learned that the bookkeeping staff, especially the company staff, large to buy a car to buy a house, small to buy food, pen and pen have records. Miss Liu, who has just become a member of the online bookkeeping, told reporters that the company is rumored to cut salaries this year, she then thought of making a family financial planning for herself in advance, thus judging what expenses can be saved, online bookkeeping can help her to summarize and analyze the family's income and consumption, which is conducive to open source and throttle. Accounting network professionals analysis, the number of online bookkeepers in the second half of last year surge, and the current global economic situation is not unrelated, in the economic outlook is not optimistic, people began to try to budget.
