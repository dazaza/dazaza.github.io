Tianyi F41M-MT Review: Usage Experience

by pp555 on 2008-04-06 20:25:02

I hesitated for a long time between the Tianyi F41M-MT and Acer 4710G before finally deciding to buy Lenovo because I thought the appearance of Tianyi was better than that of Acer. ZOL Lenovo Tianyi F41M-MT.

On November 3, I purchased it at Tangshan Nannbei Electronics City for 6040 yuan, including the invoice, bag, mouse, and Kingsoft Antivirus software. Actually, I was deceived by the merchant. When I bought it, he said there was no antivirus software in Lenovo's software package (and later I found out there was actually Norton). I said give me an antivirus software as a gift, and at the time he didn't respond clearly, but after installation, he said he couldn't give it for free and charged 100 yuan. ZOL Lenovo Tianyi F41M-MT.

Let me share my usage experience! The overall score is 5, and I'll give an impression score below. ZOL Lenovo Tianyi F41M-MT.

Appearance: 5. Stylish and individualistic, very beautiful, so I give it a full score. ZOL Lenovo Tianyi F41M-MT.

Heat dissipation: 4. In the first week after purchase, the computer was on for more than 12 hours every day without overheating, only slightly warm at the exhaust port. ZOL Lenovo Tianyi F41M-MT.

Sound: 5. Dolby sound effect, even when turned up to the maximum volume, there is no noise or distortion, so watching movies and listening to music is excellent. ZOL Lenovo Tianyi F41M-MT.

Hard drive noise: 2. There is a squeaky noise when reading the disk, and the sound is quite loud, which is very annoying.
