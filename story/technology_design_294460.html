Blue chips soar across the board, driving the market higher - Stock Market Basics

by fj0953210 on 2009-12-05 22:17:51

Affected by the international financial crisis, industry insiders generally expected that the profits of central enterprises this year would decline compared to last year. However, Huang Shuhé, Vice Chairman of the State-owned Assets Supervision and Administration Commission (SASAC) of the State Council, said at the 7th Transnational Corporations Enterprise Annual Meeting held on the 3rd day that the profits realized by central enterprises this year would be slightly higher than last year, achieving positive year-on-year growth. Analysts believe that there is no longer much suspense regarding positive year-on-year growth in profits for the whole year. On one hand, this is due to the low base of last year, and on the other hand, it also reflects the great success of a series of economic stimulus policies adopted by our country to deal with the financial crisis. However, once these unconventional policies are withdrawn, we currently do not know what the profitability status of our central enterprises will be. The withdrawal of central enterprises from some industries and encouraging the development of private capital may be more conducive to enhancing the vitality of our economy.

Former chairman of the China Securities Regulatory Commission, Zhou Daojiong, stated that the stock index futures were about to be launched. Since institutions need to obtain "discourse power" through weighted stocks in the stock index futures market, blue-chip stocks surged across the board today, especially in the banking sector. CITIC Bank hit the daily limit, as did China Pacific Insurance, while Sinopec rose 5.37% and PetroChina rose 3.33%. Xingma Automobile has been capped at the opening for five consecutive days, driving the continued strength of the automobile sector, leading to a surge in Jiangling Motors. Shanxi is promoting coal enterprise listings, making coal stocks relatively strong, with Xishan Coal Electricity rising 2.15%. Small and medium-sized boards, thematic investments, and the Growth Enterprise Market fell significantly, with Feida Environmental Protection down 6.65%, and Jifeng Agricultural Machinery hitting the daily limit. Additionally, sectors such as motorcycles, coal, and oil ranked among the top gainers, while shipbuilding, medical devices, and chemical fibers were relatively weaker.

Institutions believe that after the launch of stock index futures, institutional investors must hold sufficient large-cap blue-chip stocks to have discourse power over controlling the stock index. Our country's A-share stock index futures have selected the CSI 300 Index as the target. Therefore, large-cap blue-chip value stocks represented by the CSI 300 Index components will become the focus of investor attention in the future. Their stock prices must not only reflect their own investment value but also reflect their asset allocation value as super-large cap stocks, making them indispensable core configuration assets for institutional investors. Moreover, institutional investors conducting hedging also need to configure many large-cap blue-chip stocks and hold them strategically as basic positions.

The process of buying and selling stocks is as follows: When buying or selling stocks, you must specify whether you are buying or selling, along with the name (or code), quantity, and price of the stock. Furthermore, the commission is only valid on the day it is placed. The content of the commission includes the abbreviation (code) of the stock you wish to buy or sell, the quantity, and the price at which you want to buy or sell. The abbreviation of a stock usually consists of three to four Chinese characters, and the stock code is six digits. When placing a commission, the stock code and abbreviation must match. In addition, there are certain rules governing the quantity of stocks being traded: The quantity of stocks commissioned to buy must be a whole multiple of 100, but the quantity of stocks sold does not have to be a whole multiple of 100.

There are four ways to place a commission: counter submission, telephone automatic commission, computer automatic commission, and remote terminal commission.
