First, how to correctly understand methanol?

Methanol, also known as wood alcohol, methyl alcohol, methyl hydroxide, is the simplest saturated alcohol. Its chemical molecular formula is CH3OH. At normal temperature, methanol is a colorless liquid with a slight alcohol smell. Its freezing point is -93.9℃, boiling point 64.7℃, density 0.7914 g/cm3, can be dissolved in water and many organic solvents. Chuan Island environmental protection fuel. Methanol is toxic, excessive drinking can lead to poisoning, blindness.

Methanol has a wide range of uses and is a basic organic chemical raw material. It mainly has the following three uses: ① Used as industrial solvent; ② Used as basic raw material to produce formaldehyde, dimethyl ether, acetic acid, protein and other various organic products; ③ Since methanol belongs to monohydric alcohol, it burns very fully, and its combustion product is carbon dioxide and water (combustion equation: 2CH3OH+3O2=2CO2+4H2O), so the emission is less, no pollution, with greater environmental superiority. Moreover, due to the extremely low production cost of methanol, in recent years, it has been widely used in deep processing to produce new clean fuel for civilian or vehicle use.
