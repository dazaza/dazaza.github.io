From vehicle financing to vehicle safety and accessories financing, Lei Sa provides a one-stop financial processing plan for users' needs, "saving money and worry" transforms Foton Lei Sa's national smooth passage into a golden brand. Meanwhile, Lei Sa pump trucks use Foton Motor's global scope parts procurement system, adopting export components in key parts and assemblies, significantly improving the reliability and longevity of the whole vehicle, allowing social professional resources to achieve efficient integration. It is reported that this pump truck is also China's first self-own-brand pump truck to reach National IV emission standards, marking the strong offensive of China's self-developed pump truck towards the strategic high ground of social engineering machinery, making Foton Lei Sa become the most competitive self-own-brand force in China's engineering machinery field. In the production process, Lei Sa pump trucks especially introduced Toyota's TPS lean production model, adopting an integrated production line for chassis and complete vehicles, ensuring product quality reaches leading levels.

China’s engineering machinery gains another breakthrough: Foton Lei Sa National IV 52-meter pump truck attacks assets strategic high ground. On September 29, 2011, under much anticipation, the launch ceremony of Foton Lei Sa's National IV standard 52-meter pump truck was officially held. This followed Foton Motor's 2020 strategy formulation, which determined to position high-end equipment manufacturing as the focal point of Foton Motor's development, making it another major event for Foton Lei Sa's Pumping Industry Department. The raising of its low-carbon banner also indicated the position for China's high-end equipment manufacturing industry to participate in international competition and enter the global brand stage.

Integrated Research and Development: Attacking the engineering machinery "technology high ground". In today's rapidly developing engineering machinery industry, research and development have become the "source energy" for sustainable corporate development.

Through mastery and breakthroughs in core autonomous technologies, Lei Sa pump trucks have created the "three most" in high-end pump trucks: actively responding to the specific requirements of energy conservation and emissions reduction in the country's "Twelfth Five-Year Plan", being the first to reach National IV standards, it is known as the "most environmentally friendly pump truck"; integrating the gold supply chain, becoming the pump truck with the highest foreign-specific chassis; from the performance perspective, among equivalent 52-meter pump trucks, Lei Sa pump trucks have the largest and widest range of concrete placement, and the highest pumping volume. It is understood that both the Foton Lei Sa chassis and superstructure come from Foton's world-class digital factory, with a production capacity reaching 8,000 units (vehicles). Advanced computer control has improved the overall quality and efficiency of Lei Sa pump truck manufacturing, establishing a quality benchmark for Chinese pump truck manufacturing.

Relying on the new heights cultivated by integrated development, Foton Lei Sa's determined breakthrough becomes a significant mark for China's high-end equipment manufacturing enterprises to enter the high-end market and layout globally.

With the integrated manufacturing system originating from Foton Motor Group, Lei Sa represents the highest production level in China's engineering machinery field. To achieve efficient R&D, Foton Lei Sa relies on Foton Motor's integrated development advantages, introducing advanced overseas development technology, and using CAE engineering simulation comprehensive means to reduce the repetition of "design - manufacture - test - improve", better controlling and improving product quality and performance before product material investment and manufacturing.
