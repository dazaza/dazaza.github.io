Young couples are intimate in the restaurant living room after drinking.

by crqxv60507 on 2012-02-20 22:01:38

The two young people were 16-year-old Guo Da (a pseudonym) from Xiaojie Township and his 19-year-old girlfriend, Yang Hui (a pseudonym). Originally, the couple had drunk alcohol while eating at a restaurant, and under the influence of alcohol, both became somewhat agitated. The restaurant was converted from the owner's home, and seeing that the living room of the boss’s house was empty, they proceeded to engage in inappropriate behavior there.

"Good news doesn't travel far, but bad news spreads quickly." Xiaojie Township is not large, and within just over ten minutes, Wang Wei's house gathered onlookers among the villagers as well as the families of Guo Da and Yang Hui who came seeking an explanation.

On December 26th, under the mediation of the police officers from Xiaojie Police Station, the three parties reached an agreement where Guo Da would compensate Wang Wei's family with 600 yuan as "incense money."

In the eyes of the locals, outsiders engaging in sexual activities in their home is considered an act of destroying feng shui. After getting dressed, Wang Wei refused to let them leave, demanding an explanation. In panic, Guo Da disregarded Wang Wei's attempts to stop him, climbed upstairs and jumped out of the window to escape, resulting in a foot injury.

These past few days, Wang Wei, a villager running a restaurant in Xiaojie Township, has been feeling uneasy and angry due to an incident he found difficult to talk about.

Around 10 PM, after receiving the alarm, the police immediately rushed to the scene for investigation and carried out persuasion and education work. After more than two hours of mediation, the three parties agreed to meet at the police station on the 26th for further mediation.

When Wang Wei suddenly appeared, Guo Da and Yang Hui sobered up quite a bit, hurriedly dressing themselves.

The incident occurred on the evening of December 25th. Around 9 PM, after Wang Wei finished attending to the customers in his store, he vaguely heard strange noises coming from the adjacent living room. Curious, Wang Wei pushed the door open and was shocked by what he saw: on the sofa in the living room, a naked couple was being intimate... Upon closer inspection, Wang Wei realized that the couple was not from his own family but the young couple who had just been drinking in his establishment.

Villagers had differing opinions, talking all at once. Wang Wei insisted that Guo Da and Yang Hui must provide an explanation before leaving; Guo Da's family admitted their fault first, but since the children were young and naive, they were willing to take responsibility. However, due to Wang Wei's improper handling, which led to Guo Da's injury, Wang's family should bear the costs of Guo Da's medical examination and treatment; Yang Hui's family believed that both Guo Da and Wang Wei had damaged their reputation and demanded compensation for loss of honor...
