The project is located in the core area of Jimei New Town in Xiamen, south of Haixiang Avenue and north of the Xinglin Bay Business Operation Center, adjacent to the east to the 6.9 square kilometer "Xiamen West Lake", facing the Xiamen branch of the Chinese Academy of Sciences across the water; to the south is the central park and the new site of the Jimei District Government in the core area, and to the west is the Jimei New Town Business Center.

The project consists of five sub-plots, with a total construction area of 600,000 square meters, featuring complete community facilities such as leisure clubs, brand kindergartens, 60,000 square meters flagship commercial areas, and large parking garages. The first phase launched is Plot No. 3, with a total construction area of approximately 135,000 square meters, including 8 residential buildings of 11, 24, and 33 floors, as well as commercial and community service centers.

2. Product Characteristics

Frontline lake view / New Jia Geng Architecture / New Chinese-style Garden / Stone Dry Hanging

Chinese taste, world-class quality

A joint presentation by the Residential Group and Germany's WSP Design, a high-end work in the Lotus series matching the international planning and construction height of the core area, representing a new benchmark for urban human settlement evolution results.

It not only reflects the inheritance and development of traditional culture but also connects with the international community. On the basis of world-class product quality, it explores the advantages of surrounding lakes and cities, blending the prosperous and noble urban配套设施 with the quiet lakeside life, creating a modern high-grade residential area with traditional Chinese living atmosphere.

In terms of architecture, it adopts the new Jia Geng style, which blends and echoes with the new Chinese-style garden, perfectly combining tradition and modernity, East and West under the background of international urban planning.

A. New Jia Geng architectural art, luxurious exterior paved with noble stones

Reflecting the profound heritage of Jia Geng culture in Jimei for a hundred years, but also rich in modern aesthetic interest and tension. The exterior uses stone dry hanging, the new Jia Geng style is concise and elegant, classic and lasting, giving unique fashion beauty. In terms of architectural details, technical and building material means are used to adjust the contradiction between the specific climate of Xiamen at different times and the biological climate perception of the human body, maximizing the creation of a comfortable living experience for residents.

B. New Chinese-style garden, "Home in the Garden" aesthetic concept

The new Chinese-style garden blends and echoes with the new Jia Geng architecture, aiming to create a courtyard atmosphere of "Home in the Garden" for owners in traditional Chinese style. Here, traditional Chinese courtyard-style living is perfectly integrated with modern lifestyles.

Using the techniques of "borrowed scenery" and "opposite scenery", the lake outside the community, the municipal green belt around the community, the water features and vegetation in the atrium, even the flower beds inside the corridors of the building bodies are considered as a whole, forming mutually echoing landscapes, creating a living atmosphere where every corner is a garden.

In the atrium planning, a transparent and free group courtyard space sequence is formed, connecting the community entrance, the ground floor void layer of the residence, the corridor, the central green space, and the entrance of the residence through water systems and plants. This not only expands the green space but also makes the plan layout more lively and active, forming a visual space where the scenery changes with each step.

C. 89-116 square meters ultimate transparent house type

Adopting the rare plate-tower structure in Xiamen, creating an aerial external corridor, allowing the 89 square meter house type to achieve north-south ventilation. The setting of the aerial external corridor not only adds a north-facing ventilation and lighting surface to the south-facing house type, but also extends the natural atmosphere from the ground garden to the air through flower pools set on the corridor, making the feeling of "living in the garden" omnipresent for residents.

All functional spaces can be directly ventilated and illuminated. In terms of spatial layout, it realizes "public-private separation" and "residence-separation". Bedrooms are centrally arranged to achieve separation of dynamic and static areas, ensuring that the owner's private life is not disturbed.

The living room has a larger width and floor-to-ceiling windows, combined with the treatment of half-height glass balconies, making indoor and outdoor spaces more transparent. The bedroom windows adopt the form of convex windows with low window sills 0.2 meters off the ground and 2.2 meters high, thereby expanding the sense of space and transparency indoors.

In other details, full attention is paid to the comfort of living, such as all house types having a ceiling height of at least 3 meters, grand-scale mother-and-child doors with a width of 1.2 meters. A transitional space is set at the entrance of the door to place shoes and hats when entering and leaving, while ensuring the privacy of the family space.
