Yinzǐyán Cave in Lìpǔ

by zhou519621 on 2010-12-15 17:15:22

Knowing the beautiful Guilin landscape of China, starting from Li River and Silver Cave... Silver Cave Tourist Scenic Area is located in Malin Town, Lipu County, Guilin City. It covers an area of 1500 mu (Chinese acre), 20 kilometers away from Yangshuo County, 700 meters away from the National Highway 321. It was opened to the public at the beginning of 1999, invested by Guilin Tourism Co., Ltd., it is one of the first "AAA" level national scenic spots and awarded as the first "Civilized Tourist Scenic Spot Demonstration Site" in Guilin City, a brilliant pearl in the Greater Guilin tourism circle.

The Silver Cave Resort Scenic Area integrates nature and humanity: In front lies vast farmland, with elegant Xiaqing Mountain and Chaocui Mountain rising abruptly from the ground, thousand-mu peach orchards scattered among them. Fragrance of flowers and fruits travels for miles all year round. Boating and fishing on the Green Lake, rock climbing and fitness on the Red Cliff, leaving endless aftertaste, making people reluctant to leave; the legendary stories of heroes such as Yang Wenguang in Song Dynasty and anti-French hero Chen Jia add more cultural color.

Silver Cave Scenic Area is full of greenery. Mountains are green, water is green, land is green, trees are green, gathering various types of stalactites that have developed and grown in different geological ages. There are dozens of characteristic scenic spots, represented by Music Stone Screen, Guanghan Deep Palace, Snow Mountain Waterfall, "Three Agreements", and the "Three Treasures" including Buddha Discussing Scriptures, Single Pillar Supporting Sky, and Hunyuan Pearl Umbrella, vivid and lifelike.

The underground river inside the cave being developed meanders through the mountain, winding and twisting, mysterious and profound. The river water is clear, and the fish can be counted; along both sides of the river, the scenic spots are gorgeous and colorful, and boats can reach the external Green Lake. The Silver Cave scenery leads the trend with its grandeur, strangeness, seclusion, and beauty, and has been called the "World Karst Art Treasure House" by domestic and foreign cave, karst, and geological experts.

The Silver Cave Resort Scenic Area integrates tourism, food, accommodation, entertainment, and shopping. The Silver Cave Hotel is well-equipped with excellent service, safe and comfortable. We sincerely welcome guests from home and abroad to come and enjoy the scenic views, relax and take vacations, and spend wonderful time together.
