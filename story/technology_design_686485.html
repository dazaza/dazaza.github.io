Futures - The Hang Seng Index futures will still be under the pressure of 2700 in the later market, and may continue to slightly adjust.

by yanlaban on 2012-03-02 09:52:27

⊙ Northern Futures ○ Editor Liang Wei Yesterday, the stock index futures slightly rose after fluctuation in the morning session; After failing to break higher in the afternoon, they were under pressure from the high point and then retreated. Finally, they turned red and closed lower. As of closing, the main contract IF1203 was reported at 2639.0, down by 0.49%. In terms of news, China's manufacturing Purchasing Managers' Index (PMI) for February was 51.0%, a slight increase of 0.5 percentage points, continuing to rise for three months and above the critical point of 50% for expansion and contraction. The National Bureau of Statistics will release the Consumer Price Index (CPI) and the Producer Price Index (PPI) for February this year next Friday. With the continuous decline in food prices, especially vegetables and meat which have a higher weight and rose significantly during the Spring Festival, the market generally expects that the CPI in February will drop significantly below 4%, with a year-on-year increase of around 3.2%-3.5%, and will end the negative interest rate that has lasted for 24 months. In general, the spot market yesterday was still dragged down by the real estate sector. It is expected that the recent rally led by the real estate sector will weaken somewhat and may enter a stage of consolidation. Meanwhile, the trading volume in the spot market of Shanghai and Shenzhen shrank significantly compared to the previous day, while the open interest in the futures index hit a new high during the session, indicating a large influx of funds for arbitrage. In general, although the overall sustained strength of global stock markets and various domestic economic indicators gradually show a reduced risk of economic downturn, there was a significant increase in short positions in the futures index recently, and the upward momentum of the market in the short term is not strong. Operation-wise, it is expected that the market will continue to face pressure at 2700 points and may experience further minor corrections. A cautious observation is recommended.
