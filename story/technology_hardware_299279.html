[Company Introduction]

Xinxin Group (Jiangsu Xinxin Investment Guarantee Group Co., Ltd.) was established in September 2006, with a registered capital of 1.68 billion RMB. The group has subsidiaries including guarantee companies, asset management companies, asset appraisal companies, financial leasing companies, financial real estate companies, and cultural media companies. It is a diversified comprehensive investment group company. In 2009, it achieved nearly 3 billion RMB in investment and financing services. It is the only guarantee group company in Jiangsu Province that receives key support from four levels of institutions: the National Small and Medium-sized Enterprises Bureau, the Jiangsu Provincial Finance Department, the Nanjing Municipal Finance Department, and various district and county governments. Xinxin Group plans to achieve more than 10 billion RMB in investment and financing services within three years and become the first listed guarantee company in China. Due to business development needs, the company plans to add salaries totaling 10 million RMB in 2010 and sincerely invites the following talents:
