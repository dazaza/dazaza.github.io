The Guangdong-Hong Kong-Macao Special Cooperation Zone is about to be announced. The .HK domain names are facing another rush of registration.

by now on 2008-04-10 17:29:58

Not long ago, Hong Kong, with excellent performance in all aspects of the economic system, was once again brought to the public eye. According to the Outlook News Weekly, a special research group composed of multiple departments and institutions such as the Guangdong Provincial Department of Foreign Economic Relations and Trade, the Guangdong Provincial Development Research Center, and the Guangdong Provincial Academy of Social Sciences, is conducting a large-scale survey on the integration of Guangdong, Hong Kong, and Macao. The most eye-catching part of the survey outline is the hope to establish a special cooperation zone among Guangdong, Hong Kong, and Macao based on the comprehensive deepening of CEPA. Meanwhile, during this year's two sessions, Shenzhen also proposed "to become a city in China that can compete with the world," with a focus on promoting Shenzhen-Hong Kong cooperation as a breakthrough.

Analysts believe that under this situation, it is very likely that Guangdong, Hong Kong, and Macao will establish a special cooperation zone, thus forming China's most powerful super metropolitan area. As one of the central cities, Hong Kong will become a new battleground for many enterprises.

Experts pointed out that how to seize the opportunity in the new round of economic development has become an urgent task for many enterprises. As an important part of brand promotion, enterprise websites should naturally become the vanguard of entering Hong Kong. And ".HK" as the top-level domain name of the Hong Kong region, is undoubtedly a sharp weapon. It directly points the enterprise's development goal to the Hong Kong region, highlighting its unique characteristics, making the brand deeply rooted in people's hearts.

According to the sales manager of Time Interconnection (www.now.cn), the only gold award winner in 2007 from the mainland .HK domain registrar: ".HK has been warmly welcomed since last year when registration opened, and the number of registrations has already exceeded 150,000. After the concept of the Guangdong-Hong Kong-Macao Special Cooperation Zone was proposed, the attention to HK domains reached a new high, especially in places with more Hong Kong-funded companies such as Guangzhou, Shenzhen, Dongguan, and Shanghai, where HK domain registration has significantly increased." It is understood that Time Interconnection is the first domestic registrar of HK domains. With outstanding performance and significant contributions in 2007, it surpassed overseas Ipmirror and was rated as the only gold award winner of 2007 by HKDNR, becoming the top registrar of HK domains in China. The person in charge of Time Interconnection expressed that they will continue to leverage the company's strengths and fully promote the use of .hk domains.

Hong Kong has always been internationally recognized as one of the best investment regions, introducing foreign capital and technology with an open attitude. For enterprises, Hong Kong is a strategic stronghold. Through Hong Kong, enterprises can more conveniently attract investments and better develop overseas businesses. Some insiders pointed out that the hk domain is a clear identifier for Hong Kong-funded companies. With the imminent establishment of the Guangdong-Hong Kong-Macao Special Cooperation Zone, the hk domain will once again see a rush registration craze.
