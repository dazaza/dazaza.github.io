What is she really like in my heart?

by xiaoxiao67 on 2012-02-23 22:47:06

What is she like in my heart?

I have always believed that if I can't give happiness, I don't deserve to have love.

I remember after Amo left last year, I fell into painful memories and went crazy publishing articles on my blog. It was precisely because of this that I met her, a girl who made me feel infinitely guilty. She said she liked my articles, and reading them was like listening to an injured person pouring out their heart. Later, we became closer and closer in contact. Finally, we agreed to meet.

That day, she came to see me, and I took her to visit all the scenic spots in Quanzhou. That night, we opened a room in a hotel, but nothing happened. The next day, I took her to see the sea. She said it was her first time seeing the ocean, and she was so happy. Her voice, mixed with the sound of the waves, I secretly recorded on my phone and listened to it every night before going to sleep.

Later, she told me she liked me. Although she is three years younger than me, although we are separated by a city, although one of us is studying and the other is working, she doesn't care. She said no matter what, she likes me.

At that time, I was really foolish and naive. I kept telling her that I had been hurt by love before, so I would no longer believe in love. I told her not to fall for me because I couldn't give her happiness. I am a melancholy person, being with me would only make her suffer. I don't know how she felt hearing those words, but for more than a week after that, she didn't take the initiative to contact me. She didn't reply to my calls or text messages. Then, I took leave to go find her. That night, she cried for a long time in my arms. I didn't know what to do, I could only quietly listen to her cry and give her all the warmth I could offer.

Finally, she stopped crying and said: "I'm sorry, it's my fault for being too capricious and immature. It won't happen again in the future. Can you be my brother?" I agreed, after all, compared to love, family relationships are easier to accept. That night, we chatted until dawn. I told her about my bits and pieces with Amo, and she just quietly listened. But I saw tears flashing in the corner of her eyes. She kept comforting me, saying: "Brother, don't be sad. Brother will find happiness soon." I just lightly nodded my head.

Later, we continued to call each other brother and sister, and we were in contact every day. Occasionally, when she had holidays, she would come to see me, and sometimes I would secretly run to see her. Each time she left, I always teased her until she shed many tears.

One day, she told me that she had met a boy who loved her very much. He was two years older than her and worked not far from her. He came to pick her up every day after work and treated her well. She felt very happy. I just smiled and said, "It's good as long as you're happy. Your brother will forever bless you two." Later, she told me they were cohabiting. That night, I was very angry. I scolded her for the first time. I told her: "You're still so young now, and you've only known him for a few months. Do you understand him? What if he deceives you?" She cried and said he loved her very much, and she couldn't bear to refuse him. She said, "Brother, please don't be angry. I'll tell him tonight, and I'll move out tomorrow."

That night, I was sleepless all night. All I could think about was her figure. I began to realize that I cared about her, that I liked her. But what could I do? Now she has found happiness. How could I bear to destroy it for them? I am just an ordinary student. What can I give her? And what qualifications do I have to scold her? Later, she really moved out, and he didn't make it difficult for her. But after that, their relationship became distant. I don't know whether I should be happy or sad for her departure.

Perhaps from the beginning, I have been making mistakes all along. I shouldn't have rejected her, and I shouldn't have made them live separately. But everything has already passed and cannot be reversed. Now she is in another city, a city far away from me. We still call each other brother and sister. I don't want to break this layer of paper. I only hope that she can be happy. After all, I have nothing, I can't give her any promise, and I don't want her to wait for me painfully. Her happiness, I can't afford to delay.

Maybe I am too selfish. But Amo's sudden departure left me scarred. I can no longer bear it. I only wish that everyone around me can be happy, including her. I love her, but I cannot walk hand in hand with her. I will forever bless her.
