Aging room, constant temperature aging room, high-temperature aging room main body part: (Temperature aging room) The base of the temperature aging test room is welded with No. 6 channel steel in a mesh frame to support the weight of the room body, personnel, and test pieces under level conditions without causing unevenness or cracks on the bottom surface of the room. Load-bearing capacity: 200 kg per square centimeter; The room body is divided into six surfaces and two large doors, with door dimensions of 1500x1600mm. The outer material of the room body is high-quality color-coated steel plate, and the inner material is galvanized steel plate. The insulating medium is polyurethane foam, which is lightweight, impact-resistant, and has excellent thermal insulation properties. The door handle uses a container-type opening lock for convenient access by test personnel.

Air conditioning cabinet: This cabinet is the main component for temperature regulation and circulation in the test room. The heating system, temperature circulation fan, and balanced temperature inlet and outlet vents of this test room are all installed in the air conditioning cabinet.

Electrical control section: The electrical control cabinet of the temperature aging test room is made of A3 board coated with plastic, designed to match the overall appearance of the room body for better coordination. The temperature control instrument adopts a fully imported (Japan RKC) high-precision digital display PID + SSR microcomputer control instrument, providing high control accuracy and stability, enhancing the stability and lifespan of control elements and interfaces, effectively ensuring the temperature fluctuation range and the smooth operation of the test.

Control accuracy: ±0.1℃

Temperature sensor: Platinum resistor PT100Ω/MV

Touchscreen setting, digital, and direct display.

PID automatic calculation function, reducing inconvenience caused by manual settings.

If an error occurs during operation or setup of the temperature aging test room, it will provide an alarm signal.

Protection system: Over-temperature protection for the entire equipment, phase loss/reverse phase protection for the entire equipment, timing protection for the entire equipment, overload protection for the entire equipment. Other protections include leakage current, operational indicators, sound and light alarms, and automatic shutdown after alarms.

Heating system: The temperature aging test room uses a far-infrared nickel alloy high-speed heating electric heater. The high-temperature system is completely independent. The temperature control output power is calculated by a microcomputer to achieve high precision and efficient electricity usage benefits.
