The act of accepting bribes has been constituted.

by 2l7b86g2p on 2012-02-18 12:58:08

Zheng Shilu not only used his power for speculative gains, but also treated the job transfers of his subordinates as a "way to make money." Before the Spring Festival of 2006, in order to transfer to work at the Work Safety Supervision Bureau, Mr. Pang went to Zheng's house before the Spring Festival and gave him 10,000 yuan.

Weibo Recommendation | Today's Hot Topics on Weibo (Editor: SN014) Zheng Shilu's first act of bribery was due to an engineering project. At the end of 2002, then serving as the Secretary of Sanhe Town, Zheng Shilu handed over a digging project without going through the bidding process to Mr. Xu for construction and facilitated the settlement of the engineering payment. Afterwards, Mr. Xu gave Zheng 10,000 yuan.

After deliberation by the court, the court made a judgment on the spot, sentencing Zheng Shilu for taking bribes, sentencing him to 9 years and 6 months in prison, confiscating property worth 50,000 yuan, and ordering the return of illegally obtained proceeds amounting to 365,000 yuan.

In his final statement, Zheng Shilu expressed deep regret. Zheng mentioned three "I'm sorrys": Sorry for the many years of education and cultivation from the Party, sorry to the public and colleagues who supported his work, and sorry to his family for their concern and expectations. He stated that he would abide by the court's judgment and would not appeal. His defense lawyer believed that Zheng fully admitted to his criminal facts, had less subjective malice, and there were circumstances of voluntarily returning the embezzled funds and self-confession, suggesting the court to impose a lighter punishment.

In late 2010, a safety accident occurred at a company in Taizhou County, and the Work Safety Supervision Bureau imposed an administrative penalty of a 150,000 yuan fine and one month of closure for reorganization. In order to resume production as soon as possible, the person in charge of the enterprise went to Zheng's office and presented 100,000 yuan RMB, which Zheng accepted after some hesitation.

Currently 48 years old, Zheng Shilu served as the Director of the Taizhou County Work Safety Supervision Administration Bureau before the incident. He was criminally detained on May 21st this year on suspicion of taking bribes. Approved by the Taizhou People's Procuratorate, he was formally arrested on June 2nd.

According to China News Service on October 13th (reported by Li Feiyun, correspondent Zhu Liming), the former director of the Taizhou Work Safety Supervision Bureau, Zheng Shilu, exploited his authority to provide convenience to parties involved, thereby making illicit gains. On the morning of October 13th, the case of Zheng Shilu taking bribes was prosecuted by the Tiantai Procuratorate and tried in the first instance at the Taizhou Court.

According to the prosecution, from the end of 2002 to the end of 2010, during his tenure as the Secretary of Sankai Town and at the Work Safety Supervision Bureau, Zheng Shilu repeatedly accepted bribes from others, totaling 365,000 yuan RMB.

During the trial, the prosecutor argued that various pieces of evidence confirmed that Zheng, as a state official, illegally accepted property from others and provided benefits to others. The crime of accepting bribes was intentional, with clear objective actions. His actions seriously infringed upon the integrity of the duties of state officials, constituting the crime of accepting bribes. However, Zheng has confessed voluntarily, and it is recommended that the court sentence him between 9 to 11 years in prison.
