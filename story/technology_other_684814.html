To promote eight practical measures and push fire services onto a new level 18671079366

by xfjndf on 2012-03-01 10:48:37

The other day, the Party Committee of the Kunming Public Security Fire Brigade decided to take care of "8 practical things" for grassroots officers and soldiers within this year, so that they can truly benefit, further promoting the development of the whole city's fire brigade construction onto a new level.

These 8 practical things are: First, continue to promote the condolences system for injuries, illnesses, and deaths of officers and soldiers and their immediate family members; Second, comprehensively carry out joint construction cooperation with prestigious schools in the jurisdiction area, solving the problem of difficult school entry and daycare access for the children of officers and soldiers, continuing to promote the system of accompanying exams and rewards for middle (high) school entrance exams for the children of officers and soldiers. Third, invest 3 million yuan to purchase personal accident insurance for all firefighters and their families (husbands, wives, children) in the city; Fourth, invest 15 million yuan to purchase insurance for all fire trucks and administrative vehicles in the entire detachment. Fifth, invest 5 million yuan to subsidize grassroots police camp cultural construction; Sixth, organize labor employment skills training (at least one item) for the unemployed spouses of officers and soldiers, actively assisting in the employment of the spouses of officers and soldiers; Seventh, pay 5000 yuan social security starting capital for each family member who has not found employment of the officers determined to be transferred in 2012; Eighth, arrange for some officers with merit, outstanding contributions, and heavy task positions to go outside and enjoy reward-based inspections and recuperation.
