Cabinet Trends in 2011

by f34ttr4t5ye on 2011-06-04 16:09:12

After millenniums of development, human civilization has reached an extremely prosperous period. Our living standard has also gone up a new step. Especially the modern kitchen is more of a spiritual enjoyment. The trend of cabinet development in 2011 will be more professional and more humane.

Speaking of decoration, cabinets are very important. With the growing maturity of consumers' consumption mentality, ordinary shape design and simple functional application can no longer meet the high requirements of consumers for cabinets. In order to better understand the selection criteria of consumers when purchasing cabinets and the latest purchase needs, China Kitchen conducted an online survey.

According to the survey results, consumers attach increasing importance to the environmental performance of cabinets and the product reputation. Moreover, their interest in buying kitchenware in sets is also increasing. In the face of such consumer trends, how to improve brand recognition and how to gradually realize the concept of overall kitchen have become the inevitable topics for major merchants in the cabinet market in 2011.

Health and environmental protection are highly concerned

Environmental protection has always been one of the most concerned topics by consumers in the home market. Compared with other products, people have stricter environmental requirements for cabinets. According to the survey results, compared with the hidden dangers of cabinet decoration such as cutting corners and price traps, more consumers are still more worried about environmental issues. In addition, nearly 40% of consumers prefer to buy solid wood cabinets. In addition to their collectible value, the most important reason is that the environmental protection coefficient of pure natural wood is higher.

Zhang Qing said: Cabinets are products that come into direct contact with food every day. If they do not meet environmental standards, they will pose a threat to the health of family members. Therefore, when I choose cabinets, I not only look at whether the brand has an environmental inspection report issued by an authoritative department, but also consult in detail whether the basic panels, accessories, and later processing technology of the cabinet all meet environmental standards. In short, I must do my homework before making a choice. It is understood that with the growing maturity of consumers' consumption mentality, in addition to paying attention to environmental inspection reports when purchasing cabinets, whether the panels reach E0 level standard, and whether they can really achieve zero formaldehyde have all become topics of concern to consumers.

Brand reputation is the key to selection

Speaking of product reputation, it is nothing more than quality and service, only by doing these two aspects well can the brand achieve greater development. In addition, since cabinets belong to customized products, they have higher service requirements for merchants. Therefore, for the cabinet industry, detailed and comprehensive services are important conditions for capturing more market shares. This service does not only refer to pre-sales door-to-door measurement, mid-sales door-to-door installation, but also includes perfect after-sales services, such as timely problem solving, regular follow-up visits, etc. Dai Wei, manager of Debei Cabinet, mentioned quality service: We have always adhered to the brand route. No matter how we promote sales, our quality and service will never be discounted. We offer a 10-year warranty for all products, which is the highest in the same industry because we are very confident in our products. From this, we can see the degree of importance that merchants place on brand shaping and reputation accumulation.

Faced with the increasingly high requirements of consumers for environmental protection, the various cabinet merchants in the home market have become more and more concerned about the environmental performance of products. When Debei Overall Cabinets Chengdu area mentioned green decoration: Now people all advocate green, environmentally friendly, low-carbon life. Debei should say that it has done quite well in this aspect because the panels we use are relatively good domestically, and we will give all the panels four-sided sealing for you, so as to prevent the exposure of formaldehyde. In addition, we also consider the consumers in terms of hardware parts. We use damped hinges to effectively prevent noise pollution. Debei has been around for more than ten years, so we can still consider these details for consumers.

Improving the quality of life starts with the kitchen for food.
