Traditional enterprises have always been filled with various product information and brochures. With the accelerating update speed of new materials and products, the disadvantages of traditional paper brochures such as high cost, slow updates, single传播 direction, slow transmission speed, and difficult preservation and management are becoming increasingly obvious.

The emergence of electronic magazines as digital brochures and internal publications has become an inevitable trend. Not only can they be produced in large quantities at a low cost and be published and updated at any time, but they can also leverage the Internet to achieve multi-directional high-speed dissemination. As a leading digital publishing service provider in China, Readoo began to build a platform for enterprises to create electronic brochures and provide services even before the popularity of electronic magazines. Electronic brochures and internal publications can not only showcase enterprises and products using the original magazine, but also enhance the corporate image and increase the recognition of consumers and customers.

Readoo provides considerate services for customers in the production of electronic magazines. There is dedicated one-on-one consultation and production from pre-sales to after-sales. As long as the customer provides a well-typeset source file, it takes less than a day to obtain an electronic magazine with high-definition effects and unique reading functions. Taking into account the various needs of customers, both self-managed and fully managed versions are offered, allowing customers to release and promote their magazines promptly.
