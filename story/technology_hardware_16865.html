"The game console" made by senior students with components

by diglog on 2007-04-19 16:07:27

The author's online nickname is kisshss. He introduced: "I made a gaming console (Tetris) using the 51 MCU. During winter break, I had nothing to do at home, and since I'm in my senior year of university and need to find a job, I decided to make this gaming console to give myself an advantage. I used 89S52 + 74LS373 + 6264. The LCD was 128*64, which I bought used online for 10 yuan for two pieces, but one was broken. The program was written in assembly language, it's not very concise, being over 8K in size, so my C51 chip couldn't accommodate it anymore. P1 and P3 ports have extension ports, where I connected a simple self-made joystick. The PCB board was made using exposure boards purchased from Taobao."

Specific components are as follows:

- CPU: 89S52, 12MHz, 8-bit data bus, 16-bit address bus

- Motherboard: Sensitized board bought from Taobao, corroded with ferric chloride by myself.

- Memory: 256B internal memory of 89S51 plus external extended 6264 8K

- ROM: 8K internal memory of 89S51

- Display: 128*64 monochrome dot matrix LCD

- Keyboard: 16*16 scanning keyboard

- Expansion port: MAX232 serial port

- Operating system: Tetris
