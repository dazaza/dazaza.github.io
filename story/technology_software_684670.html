Man beats others claiming to be the nephew of the Public Security Bureau chief after traffic accident

by afg6b3po on 2012-03-01 09:40:36

At around 9 p.m. on November 26, a traffic accident occurred at the entrance of Handan Hotel on Zhonghua Street in Handan City. Zhang drove a BMW car and slightly grazed against Yang's motorcycle. After the accident, the police officers from Congxi Police Station and the Traffic Police Brigade No.1 Accident Team, as well as patrol officers, immediately arrived at the scene to handle the situation. Zhang and Yang voluntarily agreed to mediation (and later reached a compensation agreement). During the handling process by the police, Feng Taihui, the owner of the BMW, arrived at the scene with a strong smell of alcohol, then kicked Yang, and made outrageous remarks to the patrol officers who tried to stop him: "I am rich, I pay millions of yuan in taxes every year, you are all being supported by me." He also claimed, "My uncle is the city Public Security Bureau Director, if you dare to interfere, I will skin you alive and make you lose your job." He refused to cooperate with the police in their law enforcement.

Afterwards, during the investigation, Feng Taihui admitted that he is from Xingtai, operates a restaurant in Handan, and has no acquaintance or relationship with the city Public Security Bureau Director. On the night of the incident, he had been drinking with friends and falsely claimed "the Public Security Bureau Director is my uncle". Now, he deeply regrets his actions of verbally abusing and physically assaulting others.

Our reporter learned yesterday from the Provincial Public Security Department that Feng Taihui, the owner of a certain restaurant in Handan and the BMW driver, who falsely claimed to be the nephew of the Public Security Bureau Director while drunk on Zhonghua Street in Handan at around 9 p.m. on November 26, and physically assaulted and verbally abused others, has been detained by the Congtai Branch of the Handan Public Security Bureau.

The on-site police, patrol officers, and the public were extremely angry about his outrageous behavior. This incident has drawn widespread social attention and caused some misunderstandings.

Currently, the Congtai Branch of the Handan Public Security Bureau has legally detained Feng Taihui.
