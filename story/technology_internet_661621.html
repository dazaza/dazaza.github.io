Diamond Booth is a way to acquire traffic on Taobao through picture-based paid displays, commonly known as "small hard ads." The best use of Diamond Booth should focus on promoting brands and events. Creative picture materials and text are crucial factors influencing the traffic conversion rate. Merchants should choose creative pictures corresponding to the purpose of the Diamond Booth. If it’s for an event, it’s best to feature the store's best-selling products while simultaneously disseminating event information. In terms of text, it's preferable to focus on showcasing the numerical representation of the event's discount strength (data analysis shows that numbers with greater impact lead to higher click-through rates).
