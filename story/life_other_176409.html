"Anfang Network" Cartoon Mascot Solicitation Contest

by jiji_20009 on 2009-07-14 11:47:27

Competition Zone Address: http://pk.52design.com/hfhouse/

Event Organizer: Anhui Real Estate Trading Network, Hefei Real Estate Trading Network, Creative Online (formerly Wo Ai Design Network)

Participants: People from all walks of society, freelance designers, and design enthusiasts

Purpose of the Call for Entries: The Anhui Real Estate Trading Network at http://www.ahhouse.com is Anhui Province's first professional real estate website. Since its establishment in 2000, it has accompanied the growth of Anhui’s real estate industry. Anfang Network focuses on building a real estate information network platform for Anhui and serving the province's residents as its core pursuit. Its launch has injected new business concepts into the traditional real estate industry and built a bridge between developers and consumers. This competition coincides with the ninth anniversary of the founding of the Anhui Real Estate Trading Network. In order to better promote the company's brand image and effectively implement and promote the aforementioned corporate philosophy, the Anhui Real Estate Trading Network sincerely invites submissions from all sectors of society for a cartoon image spokesperson.

Design Requirements:

1. It should be coordinated and matched with the Anhui Real Estate Trading Network's LOGO;

2. Reflect the service spirit of enthusiasm, sincerity, and keeping pace with the times of Anfang Network;

3. The image design of the spokesperson should be vivid, dignified, generous, have an approachable and modern atmosphere;

4. Cartoon design should have simple and smooth lines, minimalist and bright color schemes, and a sense of fashion.

Matters Needing Attention:

1. When designing, fully consider the market positioning of the Anhui Real Estate Trading Network. Consider the needs of the cartoon image in corporate promotion, corporate image design, POP, and all related peripheral applications;

2. The style of the work is not limited, but it must be an original work. Submissions are limited to works that have not been publicly published, must be innovative, and must not be similar to other identifiers, logos, or images; strictly abide by national laws and regulations;

3. Authors need to retain high-precision .ai, .psd, .cdr format original drawings or hand-drawn originals to provide to the soliciting party after selection. The same author can submit multiple works without quantity or theme restrictions;

Call for Entries / Deadline:

March 1, 2009 - May 31, 2009

Submission Method:

Log in to the event special page and submit your design work online (no limit on the number of submissions)

http://pk.52design.com/hfhouse/

When uploading your work, please upload one "small image" and one "large image" respectively. To maintain the aesthetics of the submitted works, we have imposed size restrictions on the submitted works.

Small image size 125*100PX Large image size 600*600PX

Evaluation Method: (Voting + Review)

1. Online voting, which will run throughout the entire competition process.

2. After the collection of works is completed, a professional review panel composed of leaders of the Anhui Real Estate Trading Network and design experts will review and evaluate the collected works, selecting the winning and shortlisted entries, which will be announced in relevant media.

Prize Settings:

1. Winning Award: 1 winner, rewarded with 5000 yuan cash and a trophy honor certificate;

2. Creativity Award: 1 winner, rewarded with 2000 yuan cash and an honor certificate;

3. Shortlisted Award: 10 winners, rewarded with 300 yuan cash and an honor certificate;

4. Participation Award: 30 winners, rewarded with a fine gift each.

Copyright of the Works:

1. After the work is selected, the copyright, usage rights, publication rights, etc., belong to the organizing unit. The organizing unit has the right to display and disseminate the selected works in any form. The copyright of the unselected works belongs to the original author;

2. The works involve, quote pictures, sounds, texts, etc., must not infringe on any third party's intellectual property or other rights, must comply with the relevant laws and regulations of the People's Republic of China;

3. The group or individual submitting the work must own the ownership and usage rights of the work and all materials, must not plagiarize others' creative works. Once discovered to have violated the rules, the qualification will be immediately canceled. If legal disputes occur, the group or individual submitting the work shall bear the responsibility themselves;

4. At the time of submission, the submitter must ensure that they hold the copyright of the provided design plan. The competition organizer is not responsible for designs and intellectual property rights in dispute.
