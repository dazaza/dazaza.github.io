Classic beauty, hairdressing, and makeup

by king5c5t on 2012-02-28 13:27:41

The Classic Beauty, Hairdressing and Makeup Art School was founded by Ms. Liu Xiaomei, a master of Chinese beauty and makeup, and approved by the Human Resources Department and Social Security Department of Hubei Province. Over the years, with the strong support from all sectors of society and the joint efforts of teachers and students, the school has achieved remarkable results in all aspects of its work, realizing a qualitative leap in teaching. It has become a leader in the field of beauty, hairdressing, and makeup training in Hubei Province and has made positive contributions to promoting innovation, development, and progress in China's image design industry. Since its establishment over a decade ago, the Classic Beauty, Hairdressing, and Makeup School has adhered to the principle of "learning for application" and "quality first." On the basis of continuously improving and innovating its teaching model, it has deeply studied the essence of our national art aesthetics and integrated the fashionable elements of Western modern beauty, hairdressing, and makeup as the goal and direction of our teaching work. While focusing on enhancing students' professional qualities and strengthening their moral education, the school combines students' basic skills, fundamental theories, and creativity, providing them with a stage to showcase their talents. Over the years, the school has actively participated in various major events.

In 2007, our school organized students to participate in the opening ceremony of the 13th National Urban Games held in Wuhan, as well as the closing ceremony etiquette activities. We also took part in the supermodel hair styling and makeup work at the Shanghai Auto Show.

In 2008, we participated in the makeup and hair styling activities for programs such as the "Wuhan October Wedding Ceremony," Hunan TV's "Women Channel," Hubei Economic TV [Economic TV Storytelling], Hubei Three Channels [Car Tribe Style], CCTV [Fashion Trendsetters Show]. These efforts earned high praise from peers, and in 2006, our school was awarded the title of Employment Demonstration School. Behind these honors are the hardworking educators who have always prioritized strengthening and improving the faculty team as one of the most important tasks of the school. After many years of development, our school's teachers are predominantly highly educated, hold high academic titles, and are relatively young. There are 3 national-level judges for beauty, hairdressing, and makeup, 5 national first-class technicians for beauty, hairdressing, and makeup, and 10 second-class technicians for beauty, hairdressing, and makeup. They have long been engaged in teaching beauty, hairdressing, and makeup and have participated in numerous major etiquette hair styling and makeup activities hosted by national and provincial TV stations. They possess rich teaching, practical, and entrepreneurial experience, which ensures that our school maintains a leading position in the same industry.

Facing a new era, grasping the pulse of fashion, and creating new glories bring new challenges and opportunities to the Classic Beauty, Hairdressing, and Makeup School. As long as we persistently strive and follow the path of brand-specific educational development, the future of the Classic School will be even more wonderful.
