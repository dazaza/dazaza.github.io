March 16, 2007 | Discounted Air Tickets

Tags: Best Blog Group, Air Tickets, Sadness, Confusion

These days, every time I open a webpage, I see news about discounted air tickets, and each time it makes me very annoyed because it triggers my impulse to go home. Since starting university, I thought my feelings for home had become indifferent. I only spend half a month at home during summer and winter vacations, mostly going out. I don't know why, but when I'm far from home, the attachment to home seems to deepen. The things I want to do but can't, and the things I shouldn't do, are really a kind of torture for me. This thought is like a bug that constantly gnaws at me, making my mood unable to calm down, confused. I am an extremely freedom-loving person, even a little bit of restriction makes me feel suffocated, even dying. I always thought so. But now, my life seems to be in a state of complete restriction. I can't freely express my emotions and am constantly suppressing them. A year has passed, and I am still alive. Someone said I am a person who cannot stop, once I stop, I will feel oppressed. I don't feel that way. Maybe I am too eager for new things, or maybe I adapt easily to unfamiliar situations. In short, after a long time, I get annoyed and confused.
