2008 Beijing Auto Show - Longbo Extended Hummer

by foresttee on 2008-04-18 11:12:59

The Hummer off-road vehicle has a bold and rugged design, exuding masculinity throughout. If you're looking for more, you can opt for the extended version. Now, there's an option for you - Longbo International will be showcasing its Longbo Hummer at the 2008 Beijing International Auto Show, marking the global debut of this brand. The Longbo Hummer further emphasizes the owner's sense of strength, power, and control in its design. The extended version features external theater-style lighting, making it easily recognizable even at night. Additionally, Longbo has placed great emphasis on safety considerations, equipping the vehicle with a heavy-duty frame and full steel structure with side impact protection. Considering the Hummer's superior multifunctionality, Longbo has adopted rust-proof protection and added underbody rust treatment to meet customers' needs for various weather conditions. Longbo also utilizes the currently popular electroplating design, using extremely meticulous techniques to decorate multiple areas such as the hood switch, door handles, side air intakes, and rear emblem light frames. For the luxurious extended versions (80 cm and top-tier 138 cm), Longbo has incorporated even more electroplating designs, such as on the driving light frames, hood wind nets, and door edge trim strips. In the custom-designed Longbo Hummer being launched this time, Longbo has added many novel and unique highlights. To complement the electroplating design on the exterior, the interior designers at Longbo have used electroplated decorations on the gearshift and handles inside the car. In terms of leather selection, only the finest quality leather is used to meticulously craft the interior, with suede adorning the door panels and roof, and top-grade calf leather carefully selected for the seats.
