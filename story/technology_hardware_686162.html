Father, unhappy with daughter complaining online about doing too much housework, shoots computer

by xue86mwsh on 2012-03-01 22:41:59

The method of American father Tommy Jordan who shot his daughter's computer to teach her a lesson has aroused controversy. Jordan shot the computer According to the comprehensive report of Information Times, a father in North Carolina was dissatisfied that his daughter spoke rudely on the social website "Facebook" and complained about doing too much housework. He angrily took a gun and broke his daughter's laptop and then made it into a video. After the video was exposed online, the number of views exceeded 3.7 million in three days and also aroused no small amount of controversy. The man from North Carolina, Tommy Jordan, uploaded a video of himself taking a .45 caliber pistol and firing several shots at his 15-year-old daughter Hannah's laptop onto YouTube on the 8th day. He also gave the video a title called "Facebook Teach: To Problematic Teenagers." Jordan's behavior of teaching his daughter in such a violent way has aroused heated discussions among netizens, with both supportive and opposing opinions. As for the media wanting to interview his daughter Hannah, Jordan said he would not agree to let his daughter give an interview. Wang Chang The daughter complained online about doing housework without pay Hannah recently posted a short article to her parents on the "Facebook" website, writing that her parents shouldn't ask her to do any housework, "I am not your slave," having to help wash dishes, clean tables, sweep and mop the floor, make beds, wash clothes, take out the garbage when coming home after school every day, and doing these things without getting paid. She also asked her parents, "Do you know how crazy it is to have to do housework and homework?" She also wrote to her father: "One day, when you are too old to wipe your own butt and ask me for help, I won't come." The father was angry that his daughter didn't appreciate or respect her parents. Seeing his daughter's message, Jordan made a decision. He placed his daughter's laptop on the lawn, fired 9 shots to break it, and filmed the process. In the video, Jordan wearing a cowboy hat said that his daughter thought it was "cute" and "smart" to post rebellious words online to scold her parents. However, she forgot to respect her parents and also forgot that her laptop was upgraded the day before for $130, which was paid by her father. Jordan said in the video that when he was Hannah's age, he had already earned money to support himself, worked two part-time jobs while finishing university, and also served as a volunteer firefighter. Therefore, he told his daughter that compared to the hardships he endured when he was young, her current life was too happy. Jordan also said that each bullet he fired cost approximately $1, and he would demand reimbursement from his daughter for these costs along with the cost of upgrading the laptop.
