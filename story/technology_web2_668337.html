Analysis of the Women's Underwear E-commerce Market

by shenli477t on 2012-02-17 17:04:30

Since the introduction of the Internet to mainland China in 1995, people's attempts to use the Internet for sales and marketing have never ceased. The birth of EachNet and Taobao further catalyzed the online shopping market. According to authoritative data reports, from 2010 to 2011, China's online shopping market showed a strong development trend. In 2010, the transaction scale of China's online shopping market reached 461 billion yuan, accounting for 2.9% of the total retail sales of consumer goods in China. In 2011, the transaction scale of China's online shopping market reached 772 billion yuan, an increase of 67.5%. The continuously growing market, increasingly segmented industries, and increasingly clear prospects, especially the e-commerce transformation of the apparel industry which accounts for nearly one-quarter of the total market, have led to new horizons in online shopping.

However, behind the surging market demand, there are subtle pioneers who, like the trendsetters in the early stages of online shopping development, with their keen market sense and forward-looking vision, have pioneered the blue ocean of women's lingerie online shopping in the clothing e-commerce sector. Especially, the emergence of B2C professional shopping websites represented by Sa Feini Brand Lingerie Discount Network has opened up the precedent of细分 in clothing e-commerce platforms. According to Mr. Zhou of Sa Feini, the opportunities for the birth of the women's lingerie online shopping market and market development include the following aspects:

The unsaturated market catalyzes market capacity. The unsaturation of the supply market has made the corporate entities participating in fashionable lingerie online shopping increasingly diversified. There are both Internet brand clothing e-commerce companies and "Taobao brand" clothing companies, as well as Internet channel brand companies and traditional channel brand companies, and also traditional clothing companies. E-commerce companies born on the Internet conform to the "fast" characteristics of the Internet, flexibly applying online promotion methods and data-driven marketing methods to integrate upstream in the industrial chain; while traditional channel operators or brand operators rely on their experience in sourcing and operations in channels, gradually "touching electricity" to conduct online sales. The potential participants in the clothing online shopping market are diverse, but all aim to achieve online sales of clothing. Therefore, these participants will leverage each other's strengths and integrate mutually.
