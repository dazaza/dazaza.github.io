Two girls fell into an ice hole, and passers-by formed a chain to rescue them

by fg354nhhy on 2012-02-28 10:13:44

At around 4 PM yesterday, on the national highway 107 near Liu Lidian Village of Liulihe Town in Fangshan District, two of three girls playing on a frozen fishpond fell into an ice hole. A fast bus No. 835 from Tianqiao to Hancunhe in Fangshan happened to pass by, and the driver stopped immediately to rescue them. Some passengers on the bus and some drivers of long-distance buses passing by also got off their vehicles to help.

Twenty minutes later, the two girls were successively pulled out of the water. The policemen who arrived at the scene continued the rescue, using police cars to send the two children to hospital. Thanks to the timely rescue, the two girls are now out of danger.

The cries for help drew the attention of all the people on the bus. "The fast bus No. 835 hadn't reached the station of Liulihe Town Government yet, when the driver suddenly stepped on the brake and parked the vehicle by the roadside. He opened the door and rushed down without saying anything. I was wondering why he had stopped before reaching the station. Later, many passengers stood up to see what was going on. Not far away, it seemed that two children were struggling in the water!" Mr. Wu, a passenger who witnessed the entire rescue process on bus No. 835 said yesterday that after the driver rushed down, several quick-acting passengers also ran towards the fishpond.

This fishpond located by the side of National Highway 107 is about 100 meters long and its water surface is at least 50 meters wide. Mr. Wu, who doesn't have very good legs, also got off the vehicle and ran. In the center position of the fishpond's ice surface, two little girls were struggling in the cold water. On the shore, another little girl was crying loudly and had been completely scared.

Yesterday evening, the reporter contacted Han Yang, the driver of fast bus No. 835 who rushed first. He said that in the afternoon of that day, he and his colleague Zheng Qiulin drove from Tianqiao to Han Cunhe in Fangshan. Around 4 PM, when the vehicle was driving to the area near Liu Li Dian Village on National Highway 107, he saw an old lady shouting loudly on the slanting bank of the fishpond, "Who can swim? Come quickly to save people!"

Han Yang immediately stepped on the brake to stop the vehicle. Zheng Qiulin also heard the cry for help and shouted in the car, "I can." The two quickly rushed down the bus and ran to the shore. Subsequently, more than ten passengers on the bus also ran towards the fishpond.

Initially, the rescue process didn't go smoothly. Due to the high daytime temperature in recent days, part of the ice surface had started to melt. Han Yang, running ahead, stepped on the ice and hadn't walked a few steps when he heard a crackling sound, and his entire calf was submerged in icy water.

"Originally, I wanted to take a shortcut to save time rescuing, but instead, I fell in first, wasting time," Han Yang said. After he came out of the water, he didn't care about the water on his legs and continued running towards the direction where the old lady was calling for help, stepping on the ice again. Several bold passengers each held onto the waistband of another person, forming a human chain like monkeys fishing for the moon, helping Han Yang approach the ice hole.

At this time, more and more people who noticed the abnormality came to rescue. Mr. Wu said that a long-distance bus driver took down a sturdy hemp rope from his vehicle, tying it around the waists of several rescuers near the shore while others stood on the shore pulling the rope. Zheng Qiulin stood by the edge of the fishpond, organizing the rescue and calling emergency services and 110.

Han Yang said that the diameter of the ice hole was about two meters, and the two children had already been trapped in the water for several minutes. Initially, he tried to approach the two children by hand, but the distance was insufficient. At this moment, an old man living by the fishpond found an iron hook at home, which was passed to him via a human ladder.

To ensure Han Yang's safety, the rescuers attempted to tie a rope around Han Yang's body, but due to the insufficient length of the rope, a male passenger had to hold the rope with one hand and pull Han Yang's clothes with the other. At this point, Han Yang noticed that one of the girls in the ice hole had stopped struggling and was floating on the water surface. He shouted, "Don't grab my clothes, I can't reach the child, grab the waistband!"

Han Yang leaned forward with all his might, and the iron hook finally touched one of the girls. He used all his strength to pull the girl onto the ice. After the first child was rescued, she was still conscious, though her lips and small face were frozen purple, she shivered and said, "It's so cold... save my sister." Subsequently, other passengers took off their down jackets to wrap the little girl tightly and carried her to the shore.

Seeing that the other child in the water had stopped struggling, Han Yang and many rescuers continued without pause. "The child has floated up, I was afraid her head would be submerged, after hooking her, I flipped the iron hook to keep her head above water, then pulled her ashore," Han Yang said. Due to the prolonged drowning, the abdomen of the second child showed obvious swelling. With some knowledge of first aid, he immediately pressed his left hand on the child's chest and hit it with his right fist. A few minutes later, the child finally spat out several large mouthfuls of dirty water.

At this point, the police officers from Liulihe Police Station had already arrived at the scene. The officers made an immediate decision, immediately holding the two girls and rushing them to the Liulihe Health Center for emergency treatment. Afterwards, the rescuers tidied up their tools and left respectively. Han Yang and Zheng Qiulin, along with the passengers, returned to the vehicle. As soon as they got on the bus, Han Yang immediately apologized to the passengers: "Sorry everyone, I've delayed your time!" The passengers responded warmly with applause.

According to Fangshan police, the two drowned girls were nine and twelve years old respectively. Last night, the reporter contacted Mr. Li, the father of the second rescued child. His daughter is nine years old, and the other two children are his relatives. Mr. Li stated that after the children were sent to the Liulihe Health Center, the doctor said the situation was urgent and transferred them to Liangxiang Hospital. After rescue efforts, the child had escaped life-threatening danger. Currently, both are undergoing follow-up examinations at the Children's Hospital. "Thanks to our benefactors, we must thank them in person," Mr. Li said.

Reported by Liu Lin

Journalist's Notes

Last night, upon learning that both rescued girls were safe and sound, the journalist informed Han Yang of the news immediately. He happily repeated, "The children are fine, I'm relieved! Otherwise, my heart would always be hanging, not knowing how it would end."

Strangers, the old lady's cries for help, the relay rescue by bus drivers, passengers, and police officers... These thrilling scenes are unforgettable for the witnesses. When calling the hotline for our newspaper, passenger Mr. Wu expressed deep feelings, saying that if it weren't for the rescue efforts of so many kind-hearted people, the two children might not have survived. People say modern people are becoming increasingly indifferent, but he believes there are still more good people in society.

Han Yang said that at the time he stood by the ice hole, he didn't think about the outcome, his mind was solely focused on saving lives. And he believed that no matter who, in such a situation, would do the same. Afterwards, the reporter learned from the fast bus No. 835 team that Han Yang is 32 years old, has worked for five years, and recently suffered a left foot fracture, just finishing sick leave and returning to work.

Last night was Christmas Eve, in this cold northern night, the word "peace" became the most meaningful gift in the lives of the two drowning girls.
