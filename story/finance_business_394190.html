How exactly do you open an online store?

Online stores offer a wide variety of items such as cosmetics, clothing, digital products... Each project has great development potential and prospects. The key is how to do it, what suits you, which is the most important. Online stores also require skills, so let me teach you step by step how to open an online store.

One, What to sell online?

Just like traditional stores, the first step in opening an online store is to consider what to sell. The choice of products should be based on your interests, abilities, and conditions, as well as product attributes and consumer demands.

Since I love shoes and have been in this industry for many years, I naturally started doing shoe business.

Two, Preparations before opening a store

After choosing the product to sell, before opening an online store, you need to choose a website that provides a personal store platform and register as a user. To ensure transaction security, corresponding identity and payment method verification is also required.

I chose to open a store on Taobao because it's completely free. To open a store on Taobao, you need to meet three conditions: one, register as a member and pass verification; two, post more than 10 items (including 10); three, for convenient and secure transactions, it is recommended to activate online banking.

1. User registration

Log in to http://www.taobao.com and click "Free Registration" at the top of the page. In the opened page, input membership name, password, email, etc., and click the "Agree to the following service terms, submit registration information" button. Then, the registered email will receive a confirmation email. Open the link inside, confirm, and the user registration is completed.

Security tip: To ensure transaction security, avoid setting overly simple passwords. It is recommended to use a combination password of "English letters + numbers + symbols".

2. Identity verification

Taobao stipulates that only after passing real-name verification can you sell items and open a store. Therefore, after registering as a user, corresponding verifications (including personal real-name verification and Alipay verification) are also required. Specific operation steps are as follows:

Step 1: Log in to Taobao, click "My Taobao" at the top of the page. In the opened page, click "Please click here" next to the text "Want to sell items? First, perform Alipay verification."

Step 2: In the opened page, it will prompt that the Alipay account has not been activated. Click "Click here to complete Alipay account activation." In the pop-up page, input real name, ID type and number, Alipay password, etc., and click the "Save and immediately activate Alipay account" button.

Step 3: After successfully activating the Alipay account, go back to the original page, press F5 to refresh the page. Click the "Apply for Alipay personal real-name verification" button, read the Alipay verification service terms, then click the "I have read" button to continue.

Step 4: First, fill out personal information according to the prompts, click "Next"; then, select ID document verification. You can choose "Online upload" or "Mail" copies of ID documents, click "Next"; then, input bank card information, including bank, bank card number, province, city, etc., after completing the input, wait for Alipay to transfer funds within one day.

Friendly reminder: If you upload copies of ID documents online, the image file size should be controlled within 200KB; if it's an IC ID, you also need to provide the back picture.

Step 5: One day later, reopen "My Taobao," click the corresponding link in the verification area to open the "Alipay Verification" page. In the "Bank Account Verification" area, click "Confirm remittance amount," then input the amount of funds injected by Alipay into your bank account, and click the "Confirm" button.

3. Purchasing goods, taking pictures

A key factor in successfully opening an online store lies in the sourcing channel. For the same item, different sourcing channels result in different prices.

After passing identity verification, you need to organize the treasures you already have. To present the items for sale more intuitively to consumers, taking pictures is crucial, and it's best to use corresponding graphic/image processing tools for image format and size conversion, such as Photoshop, ACDSee, etc.

4. Posting items

To open a store on Taobao, apart from meeting the membership verification conditions, you also need to post more than 10 items. Thus, after organizing the product information and images, you need to start posting the first item.

Friendly reminder: If you haven't passed personal real-name verification and Alipay verification, you can post items, but they can only be posted to "Items in Warehouse," which buyers cannot see. Only after passing the verification can they be listed for sale.

Step 1: Log in to Taobao, click "I Want to Sell" at the top of the page. In the opened page, you can choose between "Fixed Price" or "Auction" posting methods. Here, click "Fixed Price."

Friendly reminder: "Fixed Price" has a set price, allowing buyers to purchase immediately; "Auction" starts without a reserve price, letting buyers bid to purchase.

Step 2: Select category, choose an appropriate category based on your product. For example, I selected the details of women's shoes. Click the "Selected, Continue" button to proceed to the next step.

Step 3: Fill in item information, this step is very important. First, in the "Item Information" section, create a good title, click the "Browse" button to upload the item picture, input item description information, quantity, start time, validity period, etc.; next, in the "Transaction Conditions" section, input the item's selling price, location, shipping fee, payment method, etc.; other information can remain default settings, such as default using Alipay payments. Finally, click the "Confirmed, Submit" button to post the item.

If the posting is successful, a success page will appear below. Click "Here" to view the posted item page, click "Continue Posting Items" to continue posting items.

Friendly reminder: Before the buyer bids, if you need to modify the posted item information, you can edit and modify it in "My Taobao - I Am Seller - Items On Sale." Wangdianle (www.wangdian6.com) teaches you how to open an online store.

After the item is posted, it's best to update and add regularly to prevent the store from being deleted by the system.

Three, Obtain a free store

Taobao offers free store openings for verified members. As long as you post more than 10 items, you can own a store and independent URL of your own. On this webpage, you can place all your items and arrange them according to your style.

In order to get my store up and running sooner, I initially posted 10 items all at once, spending almost half a day. After finishing, I couldn't wait to open "My Taobao - Free Store Opening." Next, in the opened page, I named my store "My Shoes, My Show"; in the "Store Category" section, I could choose the category of my items; in the "Store Introduction" section, I input the store's brief introduction content. Finally, I clicked the "Submit" button, and on the appeared page, it showed "Congratulations! Your store has been successfully created..." and provided the store's address.

Four, Store decoration is very important

After free store opening, the buyer can obtain their own space. Just like traditional stores, to operate normally and attract customers, the store needs corresponding "decorations," mainly including logo design, item classification, recommended items, store style, etc.

1. Basic settings

Log in to Taobao, open "My Taobao - I Am Seller - Manage My Store." In the left "Store Management," click "Basic Settings." In the opened page, you can modify the store name, store category, store introduction; main projects need to be manually input; in the "Logo" section, click the "Browse" button to choose the already designed store logo picture; in the "Announcement" section, input the store announcement content, such as "Welcome to our store!", click the "Preview" button to view the effect.

2. Item classification

Classifying items helps buyers find them easily. In the opened "Manage My Store" page, you can click "Item Classification" on the left; then, input the new classification name, such as "Four Treasures of the Study," and input the sort number (indicating the arrangement position), click the "Confirm" button to add. Click the "Item List" button corresponding to the classification, and you can add posted items through keyword search for classification management.

3. Recommended items

The "Recommended Items" function provided by Taobao allows you to showcase your best 6 items prominently in the store. Just open the "Manage My Store" page, click "Recommended Items" on the left; then, you can select recommended items in the opened page, click the "Recommend" button.

4. Store style

Different store styles suit different items, giving buyers different feelings. Generally, choose styles with light colors that look comfortable. I chose the "Green Wild Fairyland" style template, the preview appears on the right side, click the "Confirm" button to apply this style.

After decorating the store, a brand-new page appears in front of you.

Five, Promotion is the key to success

The online small shop is open, items are on shelves, characteristics are there, but weeks have passed without any sales, not even a buyer's comment, which is a common problem many new sellers encounter. At this point, you need to take the initiative.

I once encountered such troubles, so he decided to advertise his small shop through four ways: forum promotion, exchange links, window recommendations, and friend promotions.

1. Forum promotion

The main method of forum promotion is through posting advertisements and using signature files.

The former can be done on forums in various provinces or cities. If there is a section allowing advertisement posts, you can post ads, the content must be detailed, the product pictures must be exquisite, and keep regular updates and top postings.

The latter can change the signature file on the forum, changing it to the URL, store logo, slogan, and store name of your small shop. Post some exquisite posts to let interested friends visit your small shop through your signature file.

2. Exchange links

In the early stages of opening a store, to boost popularity, you can exchange links with popular stores, thus promoting your small shop without spending money. For example, Taobao provides up to 35 friendly links, and the method to add them is simple:

First, use Taobao's search function to search all stores, note down the names of popular store owners.

Next, log in to http://www.taobao.com/help/wangwang/wangwang.php to download the Taobao buyer and seller communication tool - "Taobao Wangwang," add these popular store owner names, and propose a request for exchanging links.

If they agree to exchange, finally, open "My Taobao - I Am Seller - Manage My Store," click "Friendly Links" on the left, then input the store owner name, and click the "Add" button.

3. Window recommendation

The "Window Recommendation" function provided by Taobao is a special feature for sellers. When buyers search or click "I Want to Buy" to search by category, window-recommended items will appear on the search results page. To set the "Window Recommendation" function, you can open "My Taobao - I Am Seller - Items On Sale," select the items to recommend in the window (items already recommended on the store homepage cannot be window-recommended again, i.e., marked with "Recommended"), and click the "Window Recommendation" button.

Six, After the item is sold

After the item is sold, in addition to receiving corresponding sales reminders, you need to actively contact the buyer, request the buyer to pay for the goods, ship the goods, and evaluate or complain after the transaction is completed.

1. Check sold items

If a buyer purchases an item, Taobao will notify the seller via "Taobao Wangwang" or email. The seller can also log in to Taobao, open "My Taobao - I Am Seller - Sold Items." In the "Contact Buyer" section, click "Leave Me a Message" to leave a message for the buyer via "Taobao Wangwang"; if the buyer does not use "Taobao Wangwang," you can also note down the buyer ID and send an internal message.

2. Contact transaction matters

After both parties contact each other, they agree on payment and shipping methods. To ensure the interests of both parties, it is recommended to choose Alipay payment.

3. Payment, Shipping

To prevent situations where the goods arrive but the payment is not made, sellers generally adopt the "Payment Received, Then Shipping" method.

First, request the buyer to pay, usually through Alipay. After the buyer pays the goods payment, the seller can open "My Taobao - I Am Seller - Sold Items" to check. If the transaction status shows "Buyer Paid, Waiting for Seller to Ship," it means Alipay has received the remittance, at this point, the seller can confidently ship the goods to the buyer.

Friendly reminder: For sellers using Alipay transactions, you can open "My Taobao - Alipay Zone - Transaction Management" to manage ongoing transactions, such as transaction queries, refund management, etc.

After the buyer receives the seller's goods, they confirm in the transaction status, and finally, Taobao will transfer the payment to the seller's Alipay account. Thus, the transaction is completed. Don't forget to stay in touch with the buyer, increasing the chance of them revisiting your store and buying items. 4. Evaluation, Complaint

After the transaction is completed, both the buyer and seller can open "My Taobao" for evaluations. Sellers can open "My Taobao - I Am Seller - Sold Items," click "Evaluate" on sold items, choose good, medium, or bad review based on actual circumstances, and can also input text content.

Friendly reminder: How to become a relatively high-credibility star store owner on Taobao?

To become a star store owner on Taobao, the credibility rating must be at least 4 points. The basis for the seller's credibility score is the evaluation after each successful Alipay transaction; if it's a "Good Review," add one point, "Neutral Review" adds no points, "Bad Review" deducts one point. Therefore, to become a star store owner, remember to provide honest service. If there are situations like online transactions but no purchase, receiving goods but not paying, sellers can open "My Taobao - Credit Management - Report" to complain or report, but need to collect evidence such as shipping receipts, buyer sign-off receipts, Wangwang screenshots, etc.

Although online stores have zero cost and low risk, examples of closing without making a single deal are also numerous. To survive online, the most important thing is "integrity." Only integrity can win the trust of buyers and gain good credit evaluations, allowing for development!
