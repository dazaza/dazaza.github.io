Synopsis:

The story of "Sword and Fairy 3" takes place fifty years before the events of the first "Sword and Fairy". At that time, Li Santai had just let out his first cry as a newborn; the Witch Queen was still an infant, and the Sword Saint had not yet entered Mount Shu to study swordsmanship...

Suddenly, the top of the Demon Sealing Tower collapses, breaking the seal and unleashing demons upon the human world. Tracing the cause and effect back a thousand years to the divine realm, General Fei Peng of the divine army and General Chong Lou of the demon army repeatedly clashed due to conflicts between the two realms, never reaching a decisive victory. Chong Lou proposed a duel in a newly discovered fairy realm to determine superiority. After much hesitation due to the heavenly laws, Fei Peng finally agreed under Chong Lou's persuasion. The evenly matched battle lasted for days until it caught the attention of the Heavenly Court. When celestial soldiers were sent to apprehend Fei Peng, he became distracted by sensing their approach, causing him to lose his sword, which was knocked down to the mortal realm by Chong Lou...

Time passed, and Fei Peng was punished by the divine realm, being demoted to a mortal. After several reincarnations, he became Jing Tian, a shop assistant at Xin'an Pawnshop in Yuzhou's Tang family. Meanwhile, Chong Lou, peeking into the mortal world from the demon realm, noticed Fei Peng's lowly and ordinary reincarnation and decided to help Jing Tian. He descended to the mortal world, disturbing Mount Shu in an attempt to help Fei Peng retrieve his lost sword. Inadvertently, he damaged the Demon Sealing Tower and accidentally obtained the Magic Sword forged by Long Yang, the crown prince of the Jiang Kingdom during Fei Peng's first life in the mortal world.

When Jing Tian acquires the Magic Sword, he meets Xuejian, the daughter of the Tang family, thus altering his life. Initially drawn into Xuejian's family internal strife and the conflict between the Tang family and Pili Hall, he later encounters Chang Qing, an expelled disciple of Mount Shu, and his fiancée Zi Xuan. Together, they embark on a long journey to assist Mount Shu in finding the Five Spirit Pearls and resealing the Demon Sealing Tower...
