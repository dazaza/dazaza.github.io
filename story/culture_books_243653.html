"Leaving You, My Unregretful Fulfillment" - Despair and Rebirth After Love Betrayal

by jqrxass on 2009-09-28 16:14:31

Jiang Weiguo, young and beautiful, had a perfect life, emotions, and marriage. But one day, on her QQ Zone, she suddenly became aware of her husband Zheng Xi's secret and witnessed his betrayal in her own warm home. Everything came too suddenly... In that instant, it felt like the sky had collapsed, and her world was turned upside down.

After a desperate divorce, she lived in decay for more than half a year. Time is an excellent remedy for healing wounds, and finally, Weiguo chose to leave everything behind and set out alone to fight for her breath and happiness...

She went alone to a bustling metropolis and was hired into the world-renowned KO Kitchen & Bath Group as the most ordinary lower-level OA (office assistant). Due to her hard work and honesty, Weiguo gradually gained recognition from her superiors and slowly built up her network in the office hall. Especially after experiencing several major events such as the new official's fire, sexual harassment in the office, dramatic changes at higher levels, a huge company embezzlement case, and the development of remote rural markets, she finally paved her way to success and was promoted to be the personal secretary of KO's CEO.

With career success but emotional confusion, on one side there was Xue Shao, deep in prison yet clean like white paper, and on the other side was Zhu Li, who once shared mutual trust and respect...

Her husband Zheng Xi, born in the 70s, had only one hobby: Taekwondo. Jiang Weiguo often joked that he was too outdated and couldn't keep up with the development of the times. However, despite her jokes, Jiang Weiguo actually loved this about him - traditional, conservative, and with a bit of patriarchal charm, making him look very manly. Not long after graduating from university, Jiang Weiguo eagerly married Zheng Xi, and now, after two years, honestly speaking, she was very satisfied, extremely satisfied.
