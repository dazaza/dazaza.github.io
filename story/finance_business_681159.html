WIFI327 WIFI OBD2 EOBD Scan Tool

The WIFI327 OBD2 scanner is the latest PC-based scan tool. It supports all OBD-II protocols and comes with a variety of compatible programs. The output protocol (connection to laptop) is via USB cable. It is user-friendly and highly effective for diagnosing vehicles. It uses WIFI327 software.

New function: WIFI + USB

Functions:

1. Reads diagnostic trouble codes, both generic and manufacturer-specific diagnostic software tools, and displays their meaning (over 3000 generic code definitions in the database).

2. Clears trouble codes and turns off the MIL ("Check Engine" light).

3. Displays current sensor data, including:

- Engine RPM

- Calculated Load Value

- Coolant Temperature

- Fuel System Status

- Vehicle Speed

- Short Term Fuel Trim

- Long Term Fuel Trim

- Intake Manifold Pressure

- Timing Advance

- Intake Air Temperature

- Air Flow Rate

- Absolute Throttle Position

- Oxygen sensor voltages/associated short term fuel trims

- Fuel System status

- Fuel Pressure

Specification:

1. OBD-II Protocols:

- ISO15765-4 (CAN)

- ISO14230-4 (KWP2000)

- ISO9141-2

- J1850 VPW

- J1850 PWM

2. Output protocol: RS232

- Baud rate: 9600 or 38400

- Indicator LEDs: OBD Tx/Rx, RS232 Tx/Rx, Power

- Operating voltage: 12V, with internal protection from short circuits/overvoltages

- Nominal idle current: 45 mA
