Sister, let me tell you some harsh truths that luxury cosmetic brands will never tell you.

by jgojid7 on 2012-02-22 16:00:08

Towards the end of summer, due to my business trip to Xishuangbanna during the summer, I was exposed to a lot of sunlight there. I found that my complexion looked terrible - dark yellow with obvious spots. These spots, regardless of whether my skin tone is light or dark, cause me endless annoyance. Every day, even after applying foundation, it feels like a layer is floating on my skin. I can't even bear to look in the mirror; I can't accept that my face has become so visibly marked by time. I feel depressed, even to the point of depression, but in reality, I am powerless. All I can do is stare at new cosmetics, hoping that these products will create a miracle. Sunscreen, primer, makeup base, foundation liquid, dry powder... Just think about it, how high the temperature is in Beijing during the summer! This suffering! I really can't take it, so I decided to go without makeup every day. But when facing others, I feel insecure. Or when I see someone with a young and full face, I deeply regret not having been born with good skin. Even if I wasn't naturally blessed, at least I could have painted something to appear as a beauty!

...Save me! I don't want to become so ugly!

Oh no, she hasn't enjoyed enough beauty yet, how could she become less beautiful?

In recent years, I've been using Estée Lauder skincare products. Gradually, I noticed that the originally clean cheeks (besides the forehead) started showing obvious spots. This was quite infuriating. One day, I suddenly had an idea: I decided not to trust those advertising pictures anymore, nor to fantasize about becoming the stunning beauty in the ads just by using their products... I put all my Estée Lauder skincare products, Chanel primers, etc., into the drawer (all bought with real money, throwing them away would be such a waste). I went to a small store downstairs and bought An'an cucumber cleanser, Pien Tze Huang pearl cream, Chunjuan huangqi cream, Qianqiancao rose water, Yongfang whitening cream, Dabao eye wrinkle cream... Ha ha, a total of 174 yuan, really stylish!

As of now, I've been using these for more than four months. No new scars have appeared, my skin tone has become much rosier. Without foundation, my complexion looks fine, and I feel good without makeup. The color of my eyebrows and eyelashes is natural and clean. Ha ha, you really shouldn't believe in those imported so-called high-end things. You can also try this for a few months.

Before this, I used Shiseido, Olay, L'Oreal, Ocean Aqua,玉兰油, Biotherm, Estée Lauder, Helena Rubinstein, Dior, Chanel, Laneige... Amway, Nu Skin... and many other so-called good products whose names I've forgotten... Nearly 15 years of using cosmetics, switching through countless products, spending so much money! It's like a patient seeking treatment, treating those saleswomen with red lips and perfect eye shadow as doctors, trying to fix this face. Thus, the worse the complexion gets, the more one believes it's because the cosmetics aren't high-end enough; the more acne appears, the more one tries to cover it up; the more wrinkles form, the more one believes in the advertisements' lies about leaving no trace of time!!

One day, I suddenly heard an old Chinese doctor on TV say: "This face of ours, must use cheap (products) but must eat good (nutritious) food. That way, your intestines will be comfortable, and your complexion will be beautiful and natural."

I thought this was completely true. My skin, the eye essence from Ou Shiman, whatever cosmetics I've used, in short, they are very thin. As long as I use foreign-made imported products, fat granules appear (to be honest, I think some newly produced domestic goods are also problematic, so I dare not use them), it should be the mineral oil component in these cosmetics. Fat granules are small white dots, and apart from pricking the surface with a needle to remove them, nothing else can make them disappear. Pricking needles on the face hurts, especially around the eyes; you can leave them alone too, over time the fat granules will oxidize due to sun exposure and turn into black spots on your face, or a blackhead settles down in your pores. If you choose between the two, no matter how painful it is, you would still prick them, but pricking might leave a small scar. Using exfoliating products makes the skin thinner, thus slowly forming faint red blood vessels on the cheekbones. Honestly speaking, even if you give me free high-end beauty salon cards, I wouldn't dare to go because that would only make my skin thinner.

Therefore, I put away those expensive, exquisite, crystal-clear little brown bottles, little blue bottles, and whatever bamboo water, green bamboo water, complexion repair creams, and began seriously using the domestic quality products I bought, absorbing pearl powder from Ou Shiman. At first, I felt that this water was too thin, and my face didn't feel anything after using it. It wouldn't sting my eyes like those big brand waters did. Before, I thought I was indeed sensitive because if there was a small corner being renovated in a mall covering several ten thousand square meters, I would immediately feel unable to open my eyes upon entering, so I didn't pay attention to this; applying Pien Tze Huang pearl cream felt thick, greasy, and not refreshing, but it would be fine after a while. Later, I discovered that applying Chunjuan huangqi cream left no oily residue but kept the skin moist. After two months, I really felt improvements. The fat granules were almost gone, and the spots had faded somewhat. Friends who hadn't seen me for a while also commented that my complexion had improved significantly. Ah ha, I'm still using them. A few days ago, a friend who used to be a beauty and is now the mother of a beauty came from out of town. After wandering around for a few days, her face hurt from Beijing's wind. After washing her face and applying toner, she immediately felt pain and had to wash it off again. I thought it was probably because the toner contained a high amount of alcohol, so she started using my 25-yuan-per-100ml rose water with her. She said it felt comfortable. When she left, she insisted on taking two bottles with her, saying she would save the money for high-end brands and try domestic products for a while.

During normal times, I also make my own masks, like honey green tea powder plus mineral water, honey rose powder plus mineral water, brown sugar honey together, royal jelly egg white, leftover milk after drinking washed on the face for a while before applying other things, fruit juice cucumber slices, etc.

Well, in fact, I've always been hesitant to tell those sisters that I'm using 25-yuan-a-bottle skincare products. They would definitely think it's too unreliable and wouldn't believe that these can resist the ravages of time on youth. But I'm truly scared of the previous products I've used. So I changed tactics, using domestic products for skincare and selectively using makeup. For example, Faceshop mascara is good, easy to wash off, and cheap; Chanel primer, Dior compact powder and blush, Clinique light lip gloss (using only this keeps the lips from peeling), and perfume, well, I like several kinds, anyway, foreign-made ones are better, like Dior, Chanel, Issey Miyake.

Uh~ dear friends, that's it. When you're at a loss, try my method. The face is an important reflection of the body's internal state. Women's blood and qi are most important, and getting enough sleep is crucial.

Of course, if your complexion isn't good, you should find a reliable traditional Chinese medicine doctor for a thorough examination, because that's the root cause of the problem.
