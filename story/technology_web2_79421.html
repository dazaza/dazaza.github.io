Say to you - Free video messaging service

by ofree on 2008-07-24 17:30:52

"Duoshuo" is a domestic free video messaging website that allows netizens to record their messages as videos online and send them to friends.

When recording a video message with this service, your computer needs to have a webcam and microphone installed. Before recording the video, please ensure these devices are correctly connected, drivers are installed, and they are not being used by other programs. "Duoshuo" supports video frames, video effects, and direct video uploads. The supported uploaded video formats include common video files such as AVI, MPEG, Quick Time, DivX, Xvid, H.264, 3GPP, WMV8, WMV9, and Real Media.

After successfully recording a video message, users can input the recipient's email, mobile phone number, or MSN, and set the sending time. The recipient will then receive the URL address of the video message and a verification code to view the message. Receiving SMS reminders on mobile phones is also free. Additionally, video messages recorded using "Duoshuo" can be up to 20 minutes long.
