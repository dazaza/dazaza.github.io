The old man lied that the young man was robbed after making a date with a female netizen

by zxyasdi20 on 2011-08-02 15:02:42

By reporter Ming Tu and correspondent Shicai Li

In the news: An old man from Jianou met a female internet friend online. After they met in person, he proposed that he wanted to have a relationship with the young female netizen, and she agreed. What the old man did not expect was that this was actually a trap set by her... Recently, this female netizen of the old man was approved for arrest by the Jianou City Procuratorate on suspicion of robbery.

Mr. Wu, a resident of Jianou City, is 60 years old and enjoys surfing the internet in his spare time. Not long ago, he met a 25-year-old woman named Ms. Yang online, on the blog "zzf000zxyr2". To win Ms. Yang's affection, Mr. Wu claimed he was only 26 years old.

At around 12 midnight on July 4th, Mr. Wu requested through QQ to meet Ms. Yang, and she agreed. At around 8 o'clock in the morning, when Ms. Yang arrived at a business hotel near the Jianou train station as per their agreement, she found that her so-called handsome online friend had turned out to be an old man in front of her eyes, but she didn't mind. However, Mr. Wu, who always thought about taking advantage, therefore assumed that Ms. Yang was a prostitute.

After having breakfast on the street, Mr. Wu took Ms. Yang to sit under the shade of the trees next to the Jianou Sports Center playground for a chat. During this time, Ms. Yang asked Mr. Wu to lend her his mobile phone so she could listen to some music. Subsequently, Mr. Wu made sexual advances towards Ms. Yang. Ms. Yang pretended to agree and asked to find a secluded place.

The two went to a nearby secluded toilet. While Mr. Wu was undressing, Ms. Yang grabbed his clothes lying on the ground and ran away. Despite the humiliation, Mr. Wu chased after her naked.

Just as Mr. Wu was about to grab back his clothes and phone, Ms. Yang pulled out a fruit knife and demanded 100 yuan from Mr. Wu to redeem his phone. Mr. Wu seized the opportunity to find a bamboo pole and knocked the dagger out of Ms. Yang's hand. Shocked, Ms. Yang dropped Mr. Wu's clothes and fled hastily, while calling a young man named Mr. Jiang to help her get revenge.

When Mr. Wu had just finished dressing and was about to leave, he was confronted by Mr. Jiang and his accomplices rushing over. Mr. Jiang used the excuse that Mr. Wu had molested and beaten his girlfriend, and gave Mr. Wu a severe beating. Another accomplice then searched Mr. Wu and took more than 400 yuan in cash from him. Afterwards, Mr. Jiang and others escaped on motorcycles.

After receiving Mr. Wu's report, the police successively arrested Ms. Yang and Mr. Jiang. Due to insufficient evidence, Mr. Jiang was later released on bail.
