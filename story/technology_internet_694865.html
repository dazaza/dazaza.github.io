26-Year-Old Woman Suspected to Have Died Outdoors Due to Drunkenness

by xue94fwsh on 2012-03-07 12:04:39

Although the long vacation of Spring Festival has passed, the Spring Festival in the hearts of the citizens is still continuing. There will be a lot of popular activities during the Lantern Festival period, and the most important thing is to go in and out safely during the festival. 　　The occurrence of these unsafe events such as fire, gas poisoning, drunken accidents, and theft should be especially guarded against in the joyful festival. Don't be careless and avoid tragedies. 　　No one knows what happened in the 6 days until Zhang's body was found. By that time, the 26-year-old Zhang had already died in the village grape garden, and his family was heartbroken. The police investigation ruled out the possibility of murder, and the forensic doctor determined that Zhang's death may be related to his long-term stay in a low-temperature environment after being drunk. 　　A 26-year-old woman disappeared on New Year's Eve. 　　Zhang, 26, is from Chang'an District, Xi'an, and works in a clothing store in Xi'an. On New Year's Eve, Zhang left home after dinner and never came back. 　　At first, Zhang's father thought his daughter was at home until 9:00 am on the morning of the first day of the new year, when his father called her to eat, and he realized that the child was not in the room. At that time, Zhang's brother thought his sister went to Xi'an, so the family didn't pay much attention. 　　On January 24, the second day of the new year, there was still no news of the daughter, and Mr. Zhang became worried and hurriedly called his daughter's mobile phone, but it was always turned off. After failing to find her anywhere, Mr. Zhang's family quickly reported the case to the Luntown Police Station of the Chang'an Branch of the Xi'an Public Security Bureau. 　　Found the daughter's body in the vineyard. 　　On the morning of January 28, someone passing by the vineyard outside the village discovered someone lying in the vineyard and immediately reported the incident. The police arrived and confirmed that the deceased was female and exactly the same as Zhang who had been missing for 6 days. 　　According to Zhang's family, after they learned of the news, they immediately rushed to the scene and saw Zhang lying on the ground with very disheveled clothes. Zhang's shoes were discarded in a nearby ditch, even the scarf was hung on a tree, and both the mobile phone and bag were gone. The whole family was overwhelmed with grief. 　　Yesterday afternoon, I learned from the Luntown Police Station of the Chang'an Branch of the Public Security Bureau that after receiving the alarm that day, they immediately went to the scene for investigation. After examination by the forensic doctor, the preliminary conclusion ruled out the possibility of murder. The forensic doctor determined that Zhang's death may be related to staying in a low-temperature environment for a long time after being drunk. 　　"These days, she and her boyfriend quarreled, which made her upset, and I guess she drank too much." According to Zhang's father, after his daughter disappeared, he found that the wine bottle at home was gone, and finally found the wine bottle at the south entrance of the village. He believed that his daughter had drunk the wine. 　　By Liu Lichun, this newspaper reporter 　　Drinking every day during the New Year, still dizzy when going to work 　　Reported by this newspaper (reporter Zhang Li) Seven days of happiness during the Spring Festival, but for Xiao Wang, who just entered the workplace, almost every day of this long holiday, he was pulled to drink. Although he was happy, he still felt dizzy yesterday when he went to work. 　　On the first day, he drank with his family, on the second day with friends, on the third and fourth days with relatives, on the fifth day with fellow townspeople, on the sixth day with classmates, and on the seventh day he was pulled to drink by colleagues. Listing the drinking account during the Spring Festival holiday, Xiao Wang helplessly said: "Drinking is harmful to health, but not drinking will hurt feelings." Xiao Wang just found a job last year. This Spring Festival, his family was also happy for him. Xiao Wang said that generally, when eating with the family, the family would not persuade him to drink, and he could drink as much as he wanted, but when eating and drinking with relatives, friends, and fellow townspeople, it was all about having fun. No drinking means no happiness, and they wouldn't let you leave unless you got drunk. Yesterday, although he went to work, he still felt dizzy and couldn't concentrate on his work. 　　Mr. Wang, who works in Xi'an, has a wife from Hunan. This year, he and his wife celebrated the Spring Festival in Hunan. Mr. Wang said that during the Spring Festival, he was also forced to drink by relatives who came to celebrate at his mother-in-law's house. Fortunately, his wife bought him some hangover medicine, so although he did not feel obvious discomfort, he always felt like he hadn't woken up yet. 　　Professor Shi Yongliang of the Department of Nutrition and Food Hygiene of the Fourth Military Medical University's Prevention System said that the effect of alcohol on the human body is related to the amount of drinking, and the size of its impact on the human body is also related to the physical condition of the drinker. Drinking can cause damage to the liver and other systems of the body. After drinking, attention should be paid to comprehensive nutritional supplementation and rest, and attention should be paid to protecting the liver. Generally, the damage caused by alcohol to the body can be alleviated through two ways: one is to protect the stomach lining, and the other is to help the liver detoxify. In fact, drinking a glass of milk or eating some food before drinking can play a role in protecting the stomach lining; drinking a glass of sugar water before drinking and supplementing some complex vitamins after drinking can also play a role in helping the liver detoxify. 　　However, the effect and effect of this on each person are different, and it only works for small amounts of drinking, but it doesn't work for excessive drinking. 　　Doctor's Reminder 　　Excessive drinking can lead to death in severe cases 　　Yesterday, I learned from multiple hospitals including the Provincial Hospital and the Xi'an Central Hospital that every winter, there are quite a number of people who go to the hospital for treatment due to excessive drinking. And during the holidays, family and friends gather together inevitably to drink, but many people often love to persuade others to drink. Drinking too much is extremely harmful to the body. Especially northerners like to drink to keep warm, but if the method is improper, it can have the opposite effect. 　　Doctors say that after drinking, blood vessels are in an expanded state. Drinking a small amount of alcohol at the beginning may have some warming effects, but drinking too much not only harms the body and organs, but also easily causes frostbite and other accidents due to drunkenness, and in severe cases, it can lead to alcohol poisoning or even death. 　　It is introduced that "drinking can improve blood circulation", "drinking can keep warm", "drinking can enhance literary creativity" are all wrong views. For example, after drinking, red face is actually related to blood vessel expansion; purple red face after drinking is related to blood stasis; pale face and cold limbs after drinking are related to blood vessel contraction and spasm. Drinking in a cold environment expands skin blood vessels, increasing body heat dissipation, which is not conducive to keeping warm. If skin blood vessels are congested or spastic, limb temperature drops, making frostbite more likely.
