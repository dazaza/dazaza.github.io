China Unicom WeChat Wo Card version upgrade launches a friendly experience for one million users

by anonymous on 2013-09-04 14:43:03

On September 3rd, it was reported that Unicom WeChat Woka received its first major upgrade since its official launch on July 30th. Guangdong Unicom stated that the new version 1.1 of WeChat Woka privileges includes an upgrade of group privileges from 60 people to 150 people, a game privilege that gives an extra 5 planes daily upon logging into "Classic Plane Battle", added free exclusive emoticons for expression privileges, and payment privileges that include merchant discounts as well as pre-paid phone fee gifts for WeChat directed traffic services.

The five privileges have been upgraded for the first time

As the world's first OTT cooperation product deeply customized by a carrier, WeChat Woka privileges underwent a comprehensive upgrade after going public on September 1st, officially entering version 1.1 based on version 1.0. The latest disclosed information shows that the number of WeChat Woka users has approached one million, and with the continuous increase in user needs, WeChat Woka will not only upgrade existing privileges but also continuously add more practical new privileges in the future.

WeChat Woka Privilege version 1.1 comprehensively upgrades the original five privileges. First, the upper limit of group members is upgraded from the original 60 people to 150 people; enjoy more exclusive emoticons for free, a new set of free exclusive emoticons will be added in September for users to use, and subsequently, more fun and creative exclusive emoticons will be regularly provided to users; payment privileges, realize the对接of the "Guangdong Unicom" official WeChat account with the Wo Le Huo lifestyle service platform, WeChat Woka users can enjoy special discounts on the Wo Le Huo lifestyle service platform, including merchant coupons, event discounts, etc.; game privileges, WeChat Woka users who log in daily to the "Classic Plane Battle" game will receive an extra 5 planes, and the two games "Everyday Love Elimination" and "Everyday Connect" will soon launch exclusive game privileges for WeChat Woka users. In addition, new and old WeChat Woka users can participate in data discount activities, where new users prepaying 120 yuan will receive a year of WeChat directed data package, and old users prepaying 60 yuan will receive a half-year WeChat directed data package.

In fact, WeChat Woka possessed the characteristics of openness, growth, and iteration of internet products right from its release. To get closer to the market and closer to users, taking user needs as the fundamental orientation, this product adopted a C2B customization model during its design phase, fully responding to market demands, and this privilege upgrade also brings better and more novel experiences to users. A relevant person in charge of Guangdong Unicom expressed that after the comprehensive upgrade, WeChat Woka privileges more deeply benefit a wide range of users, and in the future, WeChat Woka will gather advantages from multiple parties, continuously innovate, and further enhance the product quality and user experience of WeChat Woka.

Starting the friendly experience for one million users

It is understood that this upgrade of WeChat Woka is oriented towards the mass line, comprehensively expanding several privileges, more prominently emphasizing the principle of user demand orientation, making privilege services closer to the lives of the masses. To allow more users to feel the good experience of WeChat Woka, Guangdong Unicom officially launched two major activities: the "Plane Battle" good game of WeChat Woka and the "Friendly Experience for One Million Users". Focusing on users, efforts are made to meet actual user needs in various aspects such as game experience and product usage.

Starting from September 1st, Guangdong Unicom will gradually send out experience invitation messages to some old users. The first one million users who send application messages and successfully subscribe can freely experience all the privileges of version 1.1 of WeChat Woka privileges for one month. It is understood that the "Friendly Experience for One Million Users" activity starts from the user perspective, fully considering the experience needs of a wide range of users for new products, giving users the opportunity to experience the product and provide feedback. Combining user feedback opinions and suggestions, WeChat Woka can continuously improve product quality and service, further embodying the characteristic of internet customized products that "come from the users and go back to the users."

In addition, the nationwide craze sparked by "Classic Plane Battle" has expanded from online to offline. Co-hosted by Guangdong Unicom and Tencent, and co-organized by Samsung Electronics South China Sales Headquarters, the "Plane Battle" good game of WeChat Woka was officially launched on September 1st. Users can participate in the first round of "challenges" at Unicom business halls, some Samsung experience stores, and Unicom campus promotion points throughout Guangdong Province. Starting from September 10th, users can also follow the "Guangdong Unicom" official WeChat account, enter their contact information and participating region in the "Surprise Discounts - Plane Battle" section to register and participate in the online preliminary competition. The organizers have prepared multiple wonderful prizes, including a cash reward of 20,000 yuan, Samsung Note3 phones, Nintendo Vii, mobile hard drives, Monster headphones, thousand-yuan smartphones, and more.

Industry experts expressed that this upgrade of the five privileges of WeChat Woka fully verifies the previous statements from high-level executives of Tencent, Unicom, and others — that in the future, "WeChat Woka" will gradually develop and stack other privileges. WeChat Woka, with its five "privileges," not only demonstrates Guangdong Unicom's open attitude towards OTT cooperation but also brings better experiences and more practical services to a wide range of users.
