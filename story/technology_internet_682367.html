"Content is King, Backlinks are Queen" — these are the two most important aspects of SEO. If you do well in these two areas, your website's keywords will definitely rank high. First, you need to ensure that you read this article thoroughly because execution is a must-have condition for doing SEO well. If you can't even do that, how can you possibly succeed? Please take the time to read this article carefully, and from now on, you'll no longer worry about backlinks.

1. Acquiring Backlinks by Publishing Soft Articles for Enterprise SEO

A top-notch SEO expert is bound to be a soft article expert. This is the most effective method. A successful soft article can bring an uncountable number of backlinks. Nowadays, the internet has a popular phrase: "Share if you like." If the quality of your soft article is good enough, it can easily be reprinted thousands of times, which is equivalent to bringing you thousands of backlinks. Note: After writing the soft article, publish it on mainstream webmaster portals such as A5, Lowlife, and Chinaz. There will naturally be plenty of people willing to help you reprint it. Additionally, soft articles can also be purchased at reasonable prices, so you can try that too.

2. Acquiring Backlinks by Posting on Forums and Using Forum Signatures

This is the most commonly used method for acquiring backlinks. Here are some tips:

1. Don't directly add links in posts unless you're sure they won't be deleted as advertisements.

2. Make sure forum signatures can be used if available.

3. Check if the forum has sections specifically for ads or transactions where links are allowed.

4. If there's a section for applying for links, apply for one; it won't be deleted.

5. Lastly, register each forum you join in a table, noting down details like username, forum PR value, whether the signature is usable, etc. Collect a few usable forums every day, and after a few months, you won't have to worry about backlinks anymore.

3. Exchanging Friend Links (友情链接)

Friend links are usually homepage links with high authority. However, remember to check before exchanging: whether the other site has been penalized, whether the friend links use nofollow tags or JS calls, whether the content is illegal, and whether the site's snapshot is recent. Where can you find friend links? Search for friend link platforms on Baidu or search for friend links in QQ groups. Friend links should not be excessive; a new site should exchange 1-2 links per day, with a maximum of no more than 30. Stability is key.

4. Acquiring Backlinks from Query Websites

This is currently the hottest and simplest way to acquire backlinks. What kind of queries? ICP filing queries, website value queries, Whois queries, etc. Search for these websites on Baidu. There are many of them. By querying your own website on these sites, you get a webpage. Since these sites have high authority, when spiders crawl them, they count as our backlink resources.

5. Acquiring Backlinks from Question-and-Answer Platforms

Question-and-answer platforms include Baidu Zhidao, Yahoo Knowledge Hall, Qihoo, Sina Aiwen, SouSou WenWen, and Tianya WenDa. Two methods:

1. Find questions related to your website on the platform, answer them while naturally adding your website's link.

2. Register multiple accounts and ask and answer yourself, but remember IP addresses should not be the same. You can collaborate with other webmasters to ask and answer each other.

Additionally, Baidu Zhidao accounts are very valuable, so you can nurture them if you have spare time.

6. Acquiring Backlinks from Classified Information and Recruitment Websites

There are many classified information websites, such as 58 Tongcheng, Baixingwang, List, Ganji.com, etc. Search for classified information on Baidu, and check out those ranking on the first five pages. These websites have high authority, but some may not allow external links. Check beforehand to see if others have successfully posted links and how they did it. Recruitment websites are similar.

7. Acquiring Backlinks from B2B Websites (Essential for Business Websites)

For example, if my website is mechanical-related, then I can find some B2B websites related to machinery, set up a store, and post our products. While promoting our business, we also increase backlinks, achieving multiple benefits.

8. Blog Interlinking

Blog interlinking means linking your multiple blogs in a circular manner and then directing them singly to the target website you want to promote. The main purpose is to improve the website's authority, which will naturally have a better effect on its ranking. However, this is very labor-intensive, and nurturing blogs takes a long time, so short-term results may not be visible. If you want to do blog interlinking, strong execution is necessary, and team operations are best!

Method: Register 10 blogs (Sina, NetEase, Sohu, Hexun, etc.). The names and types of the blogs should not be consistent. Persist in publishing 1-2 original articles daily on the blogs for 2-3 months. Then, Blog A links to Blog B, Blog B links to Blog C, Blog C links to Blog D, and Blog D links back to Blog A. Finally, all link uniformly to your target website. Once the blog's authority increases, articles posted within the blog can use anchor text to link back to your target site.

9. Online Bookmarking

Online bookmarks, also known as web bookmarks, are link storage tools developed to address the inconvenience of system bookmarks. The reason for emphasizing external links from online bookmarks is that many people only know how to create external links through blogs and forums, lacking diversity. Such external links are extremely unnatural. If search engines determine that your external links are abnormal, they may likely lower your site's authority or reduce the effectiveness of these external links. Therefore, online bookmarks are one way to diversify your external links. Besides Baidu Collection and Soso Collection, there are many other web bookmarks you can try.

10. Purchasing Links

Purchasing links is the most common method used in black hat SEO. It can bring many external links in a short time. The guy who does Taobao affiliate marketing for weight loss drugs uses this method to build his business. When purchasing links, choose legitimate sources, especially those advertised on major webmaster portals.

Note: New sites should not purchase links. When purchasing, pay attention to the rhythm—don't buy 1 today and 10 tomorrow. Also, purchasing links is a long-term commitment; once started, it cannot be stopped.

11. Analyzing Competitors to Find Links

You can use Baidu to search [domain: competitor's website]. This allows you to see where your competitors' external links come from. Similarly, you can analyze some SEO experts to see where their external links come from. In this way, you can collect many good external link resources. Identify others' external links and learn their methods for obtaining them.

Finally, SEO is a job that requires strong execution. You can't do 100 backlinks one day, 10 the next, and none the day after. A qualified SEOer must plan their daily work and strictly follow the plan.
