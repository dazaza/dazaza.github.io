**Exhibition Overview:**

The U.S. International Solar Energy Exhibition (Solar Power International) is the largest and most influential professional solar conference and exhibition in the United States. The conference was founded in 1995 and first held as an exhibition in San Francisco, USA, in 2004. Since then, the exhibition has been held annually in different U.S. cities between September and October.

In 2012, the exhibition was held at the Dallas Convention Center, successfully attracting over 24,000 professional visitors from 125 countries and more than 1,200 exhibitors. More than 100 Chinese solar companies participated, with the number of Chinese exhibitors second only to those from the U.S. and Germany. Over 80% of the Chinese exhibitors highly recognized the scale and effectiveness of this exhibition and have expressed their intention to continue participating next year. Currently, the organizer has reserved positions for the Chinese delegation, allocating booths on a first-come, first-served basis for enterprises in the industry.
