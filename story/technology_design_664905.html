A gathering of industry professionals

by 9aincemiss on 2012-02-14 09:21:59

(Our website news, Beijing) On December 8th, the annual final of the Fourth Anlaiter Cup Underfloor Heating Installation Diary Online Competition was held at the Jing Feng Hotel in Beijing. Six annual top contestants previously selected competed in the year-end finals to decide one grand prize winner, two first prize winners, and three second prize winners. This final was outstanding in many ways. The six contestants were all elites from the front lines of domestic underfloor heating installation, with rich construction experience and excellent practical experience in engineering quality and project management. The final was divided into three parts: "True Self, Heroes Emerge, and Who is the Champion." Each of the six contestants showcased their own works and interacted with the experts and audience below the stage. After each contestant rallied votes for themselves, the expert judging panel, media review panel, and public review panel comprehensively voted based on the professionalism of the six contestants' works and their performances on stage to decide the award winners. Ultimately, Wang Anjia won the grand prize crown with a slight voting advantage, Weihai Jiazhong and Chongqing Xinqin Times won the first prize, Shanxi Kangsheng, Shanghai Anzhen, and Jiangsu Kening received the second prize.

The judging panel and question guests were ready. What made this competition different from previous years was that it invited a media review panel and public review panel to vote on-site. The main organizers specially invited representatives from more than ten media outlets, including China Construction Newspaper, Soufang Home Network, HVAC&R Online, Underfloor Heating Monthly, Heating & Cooling, China Underfloor Heating Network, Liaoning Underfloor Heating, Northwest Underfloor Heating, Heating Information, etc., to vote on-site, fully ensuring the openness, fairness, and impartiality of the competition.

At the PK competition site, colorful flags fluttered, and there were no empty seats; stars shone brilliantly as professionals from the underfloor heating industry gathered. Contestants for each award passionately competed in the PK, some showing off their singing abilities with great effort; others emotionally recited touching poems; some were well-versed in the intricacies of their contest entries, speaking eloquently and endlessly; still others cheered joyfully, interacting happily with the audience on-site. Amidst the shouts of the audience and the staff's rapid typing on keyboards, the large screen on-site continuously rolled. The organizing committee of the competition heavily drew lucky audience members, continually elevating the on-site atmosphere, increasing the audience's enthusiasm, and making the entire PK site boil over, pushing the 2011 Summit Forum to its climax.

It is understood that this competition was hosted by the Radiant Heating and Cooling Committee of the China Construction Metal Structure Association and undertaken by Warm Century Media·China Underfloor Heating Network. It is the only nationwide vocational skills competition in the underfloor heating and even cooling and quiet home industries, playing a significant role in promoting the standardized development of the underfloor heating industry and even the comfortable home industry. The competition is held annually, with each session spanning a year. Both the award ceremony of the current session and the launch of the next session are held during the annual China International Underfloor Heating Summit Forum.
