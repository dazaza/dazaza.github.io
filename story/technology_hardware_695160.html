Electric carriages: Citizens of Liaocheng in the Jiangbei Water City rush to buy Huayang electric carriages to pick up and drop off their children.

by ddjiaoche95 on 2012-03-07 14:36:04

Recently, careful citizens of Liaocheng would see Huayang electric vehicles shuttling back and forth outside many schools in Liaocheng, picking up and dropping off children.

These small and exquisite electric vehicles are safe to drive and easy to operate, becoming a beautiful scenery line in the water city north of the Yangtze River. Most drivers are middle-aged or elderly people or women. They use electric vehicles to pick up and drop off their children, protecting them from wind and rain, making people admire endlessly.

"In recent days, with the weather getting colder, there has been a constant stream of people coming to buy Huayang electric vehicles for picking up and dropping off their children," said Manager He, the sales manager of Huayang Electric Vehicles in Liaocheng on December 12th. Due to the low cost, simple procedures, safe and easy driving of electric vehicles, many parents choose electric cars to pick up and drop off their children. Currently, more than a thousand people in Liaocheng have purchased electric vehicles for this purpose.

Citizen Mr. Wang bought a Huayang electric car for his wife, saying that buying an electric car for her not only improves her quality of life but also makes it convenient to pick up and drop off their child, sparing both the adult and the child from discomfort.

On October 4th, Grandpa Li over 60 years old bought a Huayang electric car, saying it will be convenient to pick up and drop off his granddaughter for school, saving money as well. An electric car can save more than 10,000 yuan a year compared to a microcar in terms of fuel, maintenance, and insurance costs. Moreover, toll stations in Linqing hometown are free, which is really cost-effective.

Grandpa Liu Shicheng, 68 years old, now has nothing to do and often picks up and drops off his grandson at school. Public transportation is no longer usable, riding a tricycle is troublesome and unsafe, and it lacks dignity. Since purchasing the Huayang electric car, it has become convenient to pick up and drop off his child, and traveling is worry-free. He said that the cost of driving a Huayang electric car for 100 kilometers is only 8 yuan. Several of his friends, seeing him drive it well, have also purchased electric cars.

Liu Wei introduced that the main reasons for the popularity of Huayang electric cars in Liaocheng are threefold: one is the good reputation and quality of the Huayang New Energy brand, two is the good driving safety performance and comfort of Huayang electric cars, and three is the low usage cost, with a cost of only 8 yuan for every 100 kilometers. After the winter and with the rise in gasoline and diesel prices again, many urban and rural residents of Liaocheng who originally planned to buy cars have shifted their attention to Huayang electric cars. It is understood that the Huayang Panda Supreme Edition electric vehicle uses automatic transmission control, is simple and convenient to charge, and can be charged with civilian 220V electricity, available everywhere, safe and convenient. In addition to mature technology and reliable safety product advantages, Huayang electric vehicles also enjoy a whole vehicle warranty of 2 years/40,000 kilometers and convenient after-sales service.

The general agent Ms. Lü located in Xiangjiang, Liaocheng, said that on December 11th alone, they sold 4 electric cars. Huayang electric cars have been on the market in Liaocheng for more than half a year, accumulating over 300 sales, with up to 6 sold in a single day at its peak.
