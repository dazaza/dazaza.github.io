Application fields of motion controllers

by tfbgfnbv on 2009-11-26 14:42:40

Multi-axis interpolation and continuous interpolation functions: The multi-axis interpolation function provided by the general motion controller has gained wide application in the CNC machinery industry. In recent years, due to the rapid development of the carving machine market, especially the mold carving machine market, it has promoted the development of the continuous interpolation function of the motion controller. There are a large number of short small line segment processing in mold carving, requiring the fluctuation of processing speed between segments to be as small as possible, and the change of speed's inflection point should have a smooth transition. Therefore, the motion controller requires the functions of speed preview and continuous interpolation. Gotion High-tech Company has launched a specialized continuous interpolation type motion controller for small line segment processing technology, which has been well applied in mold carving, laser carving machines, plane cutting and other fields.

Electronic gear and electronic camshaft functions: Not only can they greatly simplify mechanical design, but also realize many functions that are difficult to achieve with mechanical gears and cams. Electronic gears can realize synchronous motion of multiple motion axes according to the set gear ratio, which makes the motion controller carving machine have good applications in fixed-length shearing and color registration printing without shaft transmission.
