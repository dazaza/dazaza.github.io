Zhang Yuxiang said that at about 9 p.m. that night, after he dropped off a passenger in front of Ouya Plaza and was about to leave, he suddenly felt his car's rear end being hit. Upon getting out to check, it turned out that the front of a sedan had scraped against the rear bumper of his car.

The next morning, Zhang Yuxiang reported the incident to the Wuchang Traffic Police Team. It was learned that there were surveillance cameras near the scene of the accident. The police officers stated that they would provide Zhang Yuxiang with an explanation after conducting an investigation.

The incident occurred on the road in front of Ouya Plaza on Xudong Street in Wuchang. At 11:20 p.m. that night, when the reporter arrived at the scene, Zhang Yuxiang was leaning back in the rear seat of his taxi, looking dejected.

The reporter saw that the rear bumper of the taxi driven by Zhang Yuxiang had a slight scratch mark. Around the car, nearly ten taxis were parked, and the taxi drivers were all expressing their indignation on his behalf. Bus drivers who ran red lights to deliver passengers to the hospital were rewarded.

"Since the matter seemed minor, I didn't say anything at first," Zhang Yuxiang said. However, after getting out of the car, the offending driver, full of alcohol, aggressively warned him: "You'd better go quickly; otherwise, if we make a fuss, it won't be good for either of us."

Zhang Yuxiang pointed out that the offending driver was driving under the influence. Unexpectedly, the other party shouted: "I did drink alcohol, so what?" and then proceeded to punch and kick him, knocking him to the ground. The two men and one woman accompanying the offending driver did nothing to stop the attack. After the beating, the offending driver threw down a hundred-yuan note and then drove away.

Chutian Metropolis Daily reported (by journalist Zhang Hao) that not only was his car damaged, but he was also beaten and left lying on the ground. On the evening of the 15th, Zhang Yuxiang, a 23-year-old taxi driver, was unable to calm down and stayed at the scene for more than two hours before going to the hospital for a physical examination.

At 11:43 p.m. that night, two police officers arrived at the scene. Zhang Yuxiang accompanied the police officers to the Xujia Pavilion Police Station to make a statement.
