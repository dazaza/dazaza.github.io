Zhang Shangwu started his job by receiving a "gift" and what he learned in a year is amazing_48325

by f86ziyk7 on 2012-02-20 14:28:43

Chen Guangbiao awards certificate to Zhang Shangwu - "Long guns and short cannons" echo "flanking welcome"

More than ten days ago, Zhang Shangwu, who was performing for a living at the Wangfujing subway station in Beijing, could never have imagined that in just over ten days, his life would undergo such a dramatic change.

On the afternoon of the 28th, under the company of Chen Guangbiao, Zhang Shangwu appeared at the Huangpu Company in Nanjing. Dozens of reporters from local and out-of-town media quickly aimed their "long guns and short cannons" at him; while the employees had already lined up on both sides, giving a flanking welcome to this new colleague. Zhang Shangwu thanked them calmly.

When visiting Chen Guangbiao's charity honor exhibition room, Chen Guangbiao couldn't wait to announce Zhang Shangwu's position in the company - Deputy Minister of Love Charity and Ambassador of Love Charity. In response, Zhang Shangwu said that although he felt some pressure, he was willing to take on this important task.

Subsequently, Zhang Shangwu got an early glimpse of his home in Nanjing - a seemingly newly renovated room on the top floor of the company. It wasn't very big, but it was very tidy.

"Ipad" and "Playboy" make their appearance

A normal labor contract and an additional contract were signed during the formal signing ceremony, where Chen Guangbiao fulfilled his previous words. However, when the lawyer slowly read out the terms of the additional contract, it still made the reporters present gasp in amazement. Broadcasting, hosting, singing, driving, engineering management, mechanical operation, cooking, and earthquake disaster prevention training... The breadth of what Zhang Shangwu would learn in the next year was indeed impressive.

In response, Zhang Shangwu said: "These are all survival skills, and the more the better. Although it might be a bit difficult for me, perseverance is the most important thing for an athlete. I have determination and perseverance, so I feel no pressure."

Chen Guangbiao's charitable actions continued his usual high-profile style. That salary card with a monthly income of more than ten thousand yuan was handed over on-site; the 50,000 yuan in cash for treating his grandfather who had been bedridden for a long time was handed over on-site; the 30,000 yuan in cash as a reward for his past achievements in bringing glory to the country was also handed over on-site; and there were another 20,000 yuan as a freely disposable charity fund given to Zhang Shangwu.

In addition, various items were already laid out on the table, with the Apple logo and the word "Playboy" standing out prominently. Chen Guangbiao handed them over one by one to Zhang Shangwu: "Give him a new mobile phone, the old one can be discarded; give him a branded camera, let him learn photography; give him an iPad, so he won't fall behind the times, let him learn more network knowledge; and three suits, as his attire, since he is the ambassador of love image, he needs to look good..."

The Yun-Gui-Chuan charity evening party held in September under the name of Chen Guangbiao has confirmed Zhang Shangwu's participation with a song. Chen Guangbiao said he should sing "Iron Window Tears" and "Friend", and mentioned that "Iron Window Tears" has important educational significance for young people, letting Zhang Shangwu sing this song is not deliberately linked to his past. As the charity ambassador, Zhang Shangwu is well aware of the weight of his responsibilities.

"After starting work, I have two goals. First, help children in poverty-stricken areas to grow up healthily; second, try my best to help retired athletes whose fate is similar to mine, so they can get out of difficulties..." Zhang Shangwu said.
