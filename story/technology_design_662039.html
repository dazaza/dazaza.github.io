Before 2007, Qiu Youfu, who lived in Liu Ying Village, Luhe Township, Suiyang District, Shangqiu City, had a happy family of four and was living a joyful life. However, one day in December 2007, his wife Guo Suiling suddenly suffered from a cerebral infarction and fell into a vegetative state. For five years, Qiu Youfu has been taking care of his wife without leaving her side. Now, Guo Suiling, who had been given a "death sentence" by the doctors, is showing signs of improvement.

□ Reporter Chen Tao of Eastern Today

□ Intern Yang Xiaogang / Text and Photos

【Incident】Wife suffers sudden cerebral infarction and becomes a vegetative state patient

Qiu Youfu's house consists of several bungalows, but both the yard and the house are very tidy.

"I clean up the house every day because I want Su Ling to wake up one day and see that everything is still the same as when she was not bedridden," said Qiu Youfu, 47, on September 26.

Qiu Youfu's wife, Guo Suiling, is 49 years old this year. She lies in bed and cannot understand any words. But what is comforting is that through her husband Qiu Youfu's care, she can now eat without a gastric tube. After using the toilet, she even has some awareness and asks her husband to help her clean up.

According to Qiu Youfu, one evening in December 2007, he returned home and found Guo Suiling lying in bed. He called out to her, but she had almost no consciousness at all.

Qiu Youfu immediately sent his wife to the First People's Hospital of Shangqiu City. After examination, it was found that Guo Suiling had suffered a sudden cerebral infarction, and most of her brain cells had already died. She had basically become a vegetative state patient, and there would be no recovery unless a miracle happened.

Qiu Youfu was unwilling to give up. He gathered over 30,000 yuan after much difficulty. After more than a month in the hospital, his wife's condition did not improve. "The doctor said it has come to this, don't waste money anymore. Go home. It will be good if she can live for another three or four years," Qiu Youfu said.

With no choice, Qiu Youfu took his wife home and began the task of taking care of her.

【Treatment】Over 200,000 calls, wife's condition improves

Qiu Youfu said that when they first returned home, Guo Suiling relied on a gastric tube for feeding, which had to be changed every four days. As Qiu Youfu was not a medical professional, he didn't know how to insert the gastric tube. Helpless, he went to the hospital and learned the technique of inserting the gastric tube from the nurse.

At that time, Qiu Youfu, who had never cooked before, also learned how to cook. Guo Suiling could only eat liquid food at that time. Eggs had to be stirred into noodle soup and beaten very finely so as not to choke her. Then, it had to be injected into the gastric tube with a syringe to enter the stomach.

After half a year, a miracle occurred. Guo Suiling's condition improved somewhat, the gastric tube was removed, and she could accept her husband's feeding. Later, she even regained some awareness and would make some humming sounds after using the toilet, reminding her husband to clean her up.

Qiu Youfu said that he calls out to his wife over 100 times a day, and now it has exceeded 200,000 times. At night, he sits by his wife's bedside and talks about things from the past, hoping to awaken her one day. "As long as there is a glimmer of hope, I will not give up. She lives another day, no matter what, our family is still a complete family, and our two children can still call her Mom," Qiu Youfu said emotionally.

【Impact】Qiu Youfu's persistence moves everyone

Neighbor Yang Huayu said that many people had advised Qiu Youfu to give up after Guo Suiling became like this, but he continued to take care of her without leaving her side. Everyone speaks highly of him.

"Friend Fu not only has to take care of his wife, but also has 8 acres of land at home and raises pigs and sheep. These years have been tough for him. The neighbors try to help as much as they can," neighbor Zhang Xueling said.

It is understood that after Guo Suiling became bedridden, the entire household depended on Qiu Youfu. Both of their children are attending university, and the annual tuition fee of more than ten thousand yuan is the biggest headache for Qiu Youfu.

After being moved by Qiu Youfu's story, Mr. Song, the manager of a car rental company in Shangqiu City, bought rice, oil, and other daily necessities to visit Qiu Youfu and handed over 2000 yuan to him. Mr. Song also expressed that he would continue to help this family.
