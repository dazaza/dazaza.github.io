To be a good man in the entertainment circle, the premise is quite harsh and also very gossipy. It has its own set of testing rules: to be handsome, wealthy, gentle, considerate, and responsible. The artists who can reach this high standard are probably one in a hundred. However, Huang Xiaoming accidentally achieved it with an extremely gossipy and sentimental method. He interprets how a deeply moving good man is made in a relaxed, humorous, and self-deprecating way!

Classification of male stars in the circle, Huang Xiaoming is evaluated as a Shandong family-oriented good man:

If the men in the circle are divided into the Jiangnan school, the Hong Kong-Taiwan school, and the Northeast school, Confucius and Mencius' countryman and land of courtesy, Huang Xiaoming will be a typical independent Shandong school. The characteristics of Shandong men are devout, cautious, humorous, and funny. Also, among all virtues, filial piety comes first. Huang Xiaoming is not only handsome and wealthy, but he is also a well-known family-oriented good man from Shandong in the circle. From the records in his online blog, we can read his strong sense of family and hometown feelings. He doesn't show off personal achievements or handsome photos, but rather pictures of his parents' lives and homesickness emotions. Between the lines, there is love for his parents. We even discovered a shocking secret from the photos - Huang Xiaoming's signature dimples were inherited from his beautiful mother. There are too many family scandals in the entertainment circle, and some stars go to court with their parents, giving people a very bad impression. But more touching are stars like Huang Xiaoming. After becoming famous, he first thought about how to repay his parents' kindness. As a son, he can be more thoughtful than daughters, like a male version of a small quilted jacket, which is amazing. Eating the tangyuan and dumplings made by his mother, enjoying the worldly affection, at this moment, Huang Xiaoming seems like a child, closer and more real than in TV dramas and movies, leading a warm era of good men and good children!

Revealing Huang Xiaoming's emotional world:

Although the rule that "bad men attract women" is especially popular, Huang Xiaoming still breaks the convention and stays true to himself. To judge whether a man is good or bad, look at his heart. In the world of emotions, Huang Xiaoming adheres to the style and mature mentality of a "good man." Just judging from the fact that his rumored girlfriends have nothing but praise for him gives us some insight. He is also not taboo about expressing his views on love. Being calm and gentle in love, the image of a good man is not determined by whether he grows old with his childhood sweetheart but by his respect for the past. In friendships, Huang Xiaoming reveals his cute and righteous side. The reason he wins the trust of his friends is because of his outgoing, enthusiastic, and hospitable nature, making it easy for him to get along with others. After Huang Xiaoming started his own studio, we realized that his ease and independence are closely related to his network of connections and his generosity towards others. He takes great care of his close buddies and sisters, always sending warm and sweet wishes on their birthdays. Zhao Wei was so moved she cried profusely. Family and friendship occupy a large part of his heart. Even the staff around him become his close friends, and he never forgets to take care of them, giving books and gifts to express friendship. Therefore, he becomes a trustworthy backbone in a team. His ability to make close friends is also extraordinary, quickly becoming close friends with a pilot he met by chance. Treating others with a grateful heart, naturally others treat him the same way, so his world is always like a spring day.

In front of fans, Huang Xiaoming is open-minded and meticulous. No matter how tired or busy he is, he always gives fans a sincere and energetic look, letting them feel that the idol of a good man, Huang Xiaoming, has never ignored them!

After acting for so many years, Huang Xiaoming has become more mature and confident. He is a brand spirit and even more so, a miracle in the entertainment industry.
