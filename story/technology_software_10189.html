TOP: The results of the "My Favorite Software" selection for the year 2006 are announced

by dutmcse on 2006-12-08 05:07:44

The results of the "My Favorite Software" selection initiated by Aptech in 2006 were announced. In terms of software, it was divided into ten categories for voting and selection. Among them, there are familiar IM software, antivirus software, and browser software, as well as new faces such as anti-spyware software and online video live streaming software. Overall, the rankings did not change much, with veteran software still dominating. However, there are also emerging new forces, such as QianQian Jingting in audio playback and 360 Security Guard in security software.
