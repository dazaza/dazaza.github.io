The park is actually not very large, but there were quite a number of families with children at the Changfeng Ocean World. The penguins there are adorable; it was my first time seeing so many penguins. On our way back, I bought a penguin souvenir at the entrance. It’s sound-activated, and whenever you clap your hands, it sings foreign songs. Now, it's still placed by my bedside. Every time I see it, I’m reminded of the penguins at the Ocean Park.

Fruitsmile [Netizen Review]: Another part of the Changfeng Ocean World is the aquarium, which is China's first large-scale thematic aquarium. The main building is located 13 meters beneath Yinchu Lake and houses more than 300 species and over 15,000 fish. The aquarium is divided into several areas including Jungle Adventure, Coral Reefs, Deep Sea Wrecks, Shark Tunnel, and Penguin House. Visitors in the shark tunnel can experience the thrill of a close encounter with the sea's dominant predators. The renovated jungle adventure area, with its realistic rainforest environment, is much loved by children. In the summer of 2010, rare double-headed sharks were introduced, pioneering the exhibition of double-headed sharks in domestic ocean parks. To date, the Changfeng Ocean World has received millions of visitors from home and abroad and is one of the national youth science popularization education bases. Meanwhile, the aquarium has successfully launched numerous services such as a diving club, membership club, underwater weddings, and overnight stays at the aquarium, achieving good brand recognition and commercial reputation.

This ocean world is one of the earlier established marine worlds. Many of the fish inside are quite interestingly shaped. Here, you can appreciate the beauty and mystery of nature. Occasionally, there are song and dance performances. It's really excellent. There are also quite a variety of species, and the jungle adventure is pretty good too.

I remember coming here for school-organized spring and autumn outings when I was younger. I really enjoyed watching the dolphin performances; they were very entertaining, and I had a great time then. Now, when I visit again, there are many children inside. They get excited and noisy when they see beautiful fish, after all, they are kids. Watching them reminds me of myself when I was young. The underwater world is also great; you can see many kinds of fish.

Dead Cat Special Operations Team [About Ocean World] LIANGSHA: After two renovations, the polar white whale performance hall at Changfeng Ocean World has become the largest in mainland China and the first marine animal performance hall in East China to have white whales. It can accommodate up to 1,500 people at the same time. Besides the star attraction, the little white whale, other marine mammals like California sea lions have been introduced, and soon visitors will be able to watch their wonderful performances and personally experience the surprise of harmonious coexistence between humans and animals. Animal performance stage plays such as "Little White Whale Finds Mom", "The Secret of the Treasure Chest", "Take Me to the Moon", and "Hello Shanghai" were successfully staged during the May Day and National Day Golden Week holidays, receiving enthusiastic applause from the audience.

Changfeng Ocean World is located within the picturesque Changfeng Park and belongs to the Australian Aoyang Group, integrating large-scale marine animal performances and aquarium fish exhibitions into a comprehensive marine theme park. The Aoyang International Group is the world's largest company in the aquarium industry, owning six aquariums globally, located in Australia's Sunshine Coast, Melbourne, South Korea's Busan, Shanghai, Thailand's Bangkok, and Dubai in the Middle East UAE.
