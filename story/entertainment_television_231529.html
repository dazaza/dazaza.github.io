Plot Summary of "Fortune and Good Health":

Sixty years ago, a mountain girl Huang Chunxiang (played by Tan Aizhen) accidentally saved Yan Wangcai (played by Ding Qiang), who had mistakenly fallen into a trap while collecting herbs and fainted. They fell in love at first sight, but unfortunately, they had to part ways under the beautiful Paulownia rain. Chunxiang kept the watch Wangcai left her for sixty years, a tragic story like a dream. Chunxiang has always hoped that one day Wangcai would come back to find her. This deep affection made her granddaughter Xie Fuan (played by Chen Qiaoen) deeply moved and yearned...

"If only I could have such a beautiful first love..."

Yan Yungen, the chairman of the world's largest traditional Chinese medicine group 'Bao Tang Pharmaceutical Group,' was in a coma due to a car accident and lay fragile in the ambulance rushing to the hospital. Soon after, his grandchildren, who were like wolves waiting for him to die so they could inherit his estate, arrived one by one. Seeing Yungen in critical condition, everyone except his grandson Yan Yang (played by Xiu Jiebai), who genuinely felt sad, was happy. Even when the doctor tried to save Yungen, they all strongly opposed it. "Let Dad (Grandpa) go peacefully!" These words reached Fuan's ears, who was secretly treating patients with athlete's foot to earn extra money, making her feel as if she were hearing something from outer space. With a strong sense of justice, Fuan decided not to let Yungen's grandchildren succeed in their plan. She sneaked into the emergency room and used Eucommia leaves to save Yungen. Smelling the familiar scent, Yungen woke up hazily, vaguely seeing Fuan's face, recalling a familiar face, and weakly called out: "Chunxiang?"

Yungen's eldest grandson Da Feng (played by Lan Zhenglong), upon seeing Yungen unexpectedly saved, immediately scolded Fuan for meddling. Both left bad impressions on each other, unaware that this was just the beginning of their fate.

Besides being very righteous, Fuan also loves her family very much, taking good care of Chunxiang, who is unwell, and her young brother Pi Dan. Since Fuan gave Yungen more time from death, upon waking up, Yungen was very disappointed with these inhumane grandchildren. However, Yungen knew this was his retribution for raising them to be like vultures. But this wasn't what Yungen regretted the most; what he regretted the most was lying to Chunxiang sixty years ago!

It turns out that the Wangcai mentioned by Chunxiang was him. He initially approached Chunxiang to deceive the secret formula for treating gastrointestinal discomfort and then changed his name to Yan Yungen after leaving the mountains. He used the formula to start Bao Tang and became wealthy. Ironically, Chunxiang held onto this first love until she met Fuan, which reminded Yungen of it...

Yungen decided to make up for Chunxiang and revealed that he would announce the successor on his birthday. This news caused great tension among the Yan family members, unsure of their chances. When Fu An heard that Da Feng was looking for Niu Zhang Zhi, she sternly lectured him. Just as Fu An went down the mountain, Yungen arrived at Fu An's home, looking at the ruins and listening to the old village chief recounting Chunxiang's years of hardship. Yungen decided to fully find the 'Fu Lan' and 'Pi Dan' mentioned by the village chief.

On his eightieth birthday, Yungen announced: "I will give the inheritance rights of Bao Tang worth 8 billion yuan... to the person who can marry the descendant of Xie Chunxiang—named Xie Fulan!"

Yungen accurately predicted that everyone would rush to search. However, Da Feng planned to find 'Fulan' first and kill her to end everything.

The real Fu An, while searching for Wangcai grandpa, accidentally saved Dongjie (played by Qiu Ze), who was attacked by someone. To compensate them, Dongjie ordered his men to secretly protect Fu An and her brother.

Fu An and Pi Dan arrived in an unfamiliar city with nowhere to go and could only temporarily stay overnight in the park. It rained at night, and Pi Dan developed a high fever the next day.
