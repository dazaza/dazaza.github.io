Put your hand inside the underwear

by concearcee on 2010-04-26 16:48:47

A beauty decided to spend a lot of money to lose weight. After spending more than 100,000 yuan, she felt very satisfied! On her way home, she bought a newspaper at a newsstand and asked the owner while making change: "Excuse me, can you guess how old I am?" The owner said 32.

She was so happy: "I'm actually 47!"

Then, she went to McDonald's and asked the same question to the cashier. The cashier guessed 29. She happily replied: "No, I'm 47!"

Feeling elated, she went to the Uni-President supermarket at the street corner to buy a pack of gum and couldn't help asking the cashier there again. The cashier said: "Hmm, I guess 30." She proudly said: "I'm 47, thank you!"

While waiting for the bus, she asked the elderly man standing next to her. The old man said: "I'm 78 years old, my eyesight is not good, I can't tell. But when I was young, there was a way to be sure. If I could put my hand inside your bra, I would absolutely know your age!"

After a long silence on the empty street, she finally couldn't resist her curiosity: "Alright, give it a try."

The old man inserted his hand into her shirt, then her bra, and began to slowly and carefully feel around.

A few minutes later, she said: "Alright, now guess how old I am?"

The old man gave one last pinch, withdrew his hand, and said: "Madam, you're 47 years old."

The beauty was greatly surprised and asked in astonishment: "That's amazing! How did you know that?"

"Can I guarantee you won't get mad?"

"I won't get mad!"

The old man's answer made the beauty faint:

The old man said: "Because I was behind you in line at McDonald's."
