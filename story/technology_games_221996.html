Tips for enhancing, synthesizing, and obtaining extreme weapons in "DD Tank"

by jaykong on 2009-08-12 22:24:59

Recently, I've been reading strategy articles on the official website and forums of "TanTan Tang", and found that most of them discuss weapon tactics and skill usage. There are very few articles about weapon enhancement and synthesis. In order to help my guild brothers save money and avoid taking detours, I would like to share some of my insights on enhancement, synthesis, and extreme weapon brushing, hoping it will be helpful to everyone.

Methods to increase the success rate of enhancement and synthesis:

Time: Timing is crucial. Enhancement must be done when the server is not lagging, which will increase the success rate. This is usually in the morning or at midnight.

Placement: It's recommended to place talismans first, then stones, and finally the weapon.

Time: Most people tend to press the enhance button immediately after placing all the items, but this is actually the main reason for enhancement failure. The correct method is to observe the success rate after placing the items. For example, if the success rate is 30%, use the formula to calculate the waiting time: 11-3 (30% is considered as 3, round off if it's not an integer) = 8. In general, if the server is running smoothly, you only need to wait 8 seconds after placing the items before pressing the button.

Stumbling: Many people have mentioned this, but I tried it myself and the effect wasn't ideal. The idea is to randomly enhance a weapon first, fail, then succeed with the second one, and then enhance the one you want to enhance and it will succeed!

Persistence: It's recommended to prepare three sets of stones and talismans when enhancing. If the first attempt fails, try again. Never stop halfway, which is a big taboo in enhancement.

Stopping: In general, using the above methods, either you fail once and succeed once, or you succeed twice directly. However, once you succeed consecutively, you will often fail later.

Therefore, it's suggested to stop for the day after consecutive successes and enhance again tomorrow.

Using all the above methods together makes it hard to fail, but I still want to remind everyone: Enhancement has risks! Please proceed with caution!

Methods to make money from refining:

Using three stones of the same level plus one stone of a lower level results in a success rate of about 70%. If using four stones, the success rate is around 90%. It's recommended to use this method when synthesizing low-level items.

Then comes the method of refining enhancement stones:

Everyone knows that using four synthesis stones of the same level but different attributes plus a condensation formula can produce enhancement stones of the same level with a 100% success rate.

For those who want to learn how to make money by selling level 4 enhancements:

Buy level 3 synthesis stones, each costing about 80 points, then use the same method to refine level 3 enhancements. Here's the key: you can add another level 3 synthesis stone, and when you check, you'll see a 25% chance of getting level 4 enhancement and a 75% chance of getting level 3 enhancement. In other words, no matter what, you will get enhancement stones, and the possibility of getting level 4 enhancement is 1/4. Considering the cost of 80x5=400, with good RP, there might be a valuable level 4 enhancement worth 1200. Even if you don't get it, you can continue, explode level 3, and then use the first method to train level 4.

It's also recommended to use the above methods in channels with fewer people at midnight or in the morning. If you're synthesizing in the morning, it's suggested to use all the synthesis methods and then wait 8 seconds after adding the formula. This will increase the success rate of getting level 4 enhancement. As for why, I won't disclose here.

Method for brushing extreme weapons:

First, ensure your item slot is full. Second, it doesn't matter how many people you play with in the arena. What matters is that you should not add items when opening boxes, but you can use planes. Also, while opening boxes, try not to attack others. This way, even if you don't get extreme weapons, you will get many good stones!

The most important thing about opening boxes is still RP. Finally, I wish everyone a pleasant game experience.
