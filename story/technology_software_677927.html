Sword Swallows the World. Always feel that I should write something as the New Year comes.

by sznstejx3q3 on 2012-02-27 10:43:18

However, I have no inspiration at all, Sword Swallowing the World... I don't know what to write about... After dinner, I always feel uncomfortable... Slowly walking in the yard, I smelled a few traces of firework, ah, it's already 2012. Suddenly, I feel a bit pathetic. Have I lost track of time? It's painful, this feeling of numbness. There's always a void in my heart, probably a kind of aftermath. I don't know when it will heal, but now I can speak steadily. At least I can control my tears, which is a form of progress.

Reflecting on the past year, I want to laugh. In the first half of the year, I was extraordinarily strong; in the second half, I was extraordinarily weak, so strange. In the first half of the year, I hardly cried. Even when I performed poorly in mathematics during the unified exam, I would still manage to smile and try my best. That's how the days passed, every day was full of happiness, chatter, and innocence. Then, with summer vacation as the dividing point, in the second half of the year... I cried nearly twenty times... And many more times I cried alone under the covers weren't counted. When I thought I didn't score 110 in the weekly math test, I cried... But when the paper was handed out... I scored 129... I cursed [what the heck, my tears were wasted!]. The feeling of weakness surrounded me completely, I became so fragile.

Sometimes I think, in 2012, there will be the college entrance examination. I feel powerless and painful. Finally, I reflect on myself: "What am I resisting?" Resisting my future, resisting my dreams. Yes, my dream of becoming a medical sage, another dream that I've buried in the summer, will bear fruit next year, but those fruits are not mine. Someone will gently pick them up. I just want to say to the person who picks it: "Cherish it." That dream seems to have become something I can never reach. So I might as well turn around and go against the current, after all, it doesn't belong to me.

In addition, I really want to mention someone. Sorry, we met at the wrong time. What I did back then was just to cut off any room for hesitation. If I hurt you, I'm sorry. In 2012, I look forward to your performance in the exam. My dad and I are similar types, both good at using reverse psychology, so... come on, but remember, I won't lose in the battlefield of the college entrance exam. I am the battle god of class five in junior three [I ranked in the top ten in the high school entrance exam].

The following people, listen carefully:

Kyouya Hibari, I like you the most. My cloud guardian, you as my spiritual pillar, you as my virtual guardian, you as the student council member, dominating yet gentle, protecting everyone.

Shabai, thank you for never abandoning me, never ever. During the summer vacation, I was thinking, if even you left me behind, then I would be truly a failure. Thank you for standing there guarding me all along. Your protection and Hibari's help me get through so many storms... We are best friends! Let Liangchuan be jealous! [There's some bias here, understand it yourself.]

Liangchuan, do you know, every time you call me Three Thousand or Xiao Three Thousand, I am particularly happy, but don't omit the word "thousand" [emphasis]. Do you remember the notes we passed? I showed them to Shabai. On the first day of 2012, thank you. Every time I was sad, looking up, I always saw you. Only in front of you can I cry without restraint. With you, I am so happy.

Tongtong, I remember your name "Tong", which means light. Yes, you are actually the light in my heart, shining into my dark inner world in junior three, waking me up, waking me up. Now when I see the small attackers, I just greet them, and the small defenders have forgotten me... The four-person group that used to walk down the street has almost disbanded. I like you the most.

In 2012, I don't know if there will be an end of the world. I don't know if I can hold on to my dream. I don't know, I don't know. All I know is, everything has passed, the sun will still rise.
