1. Goji Tea for Diabetes

[Formula] 10 grams of Ningxia goji berries.

[Preparation and Usage] Add 300 milliliters of water to the goji berries, boil for 1 to 2 minutes, cool down, drink the concentrated juice before breakfast. Then repeatedly add boiling water and drink it as tea, 4 to 5 cups per day (each cup 200 milliliters). Before sleeping, chew and swallow the remaining goji berries with the water.

[Effect] Used to treat diabetes.

2. Zucchini divided into 7 parts, each part added with 1 qian alum, Baihe Meiyi Hong capsules, boil one part daily, 3 zucchinis boiled for 21 days will cure, no salt added.

2. Pig Bladder for Diabetes

[Formula] 10 pig bladders (dried).

[Preparation and Usage] Cut open, burn until the smoke is gone, grind into fine powder, take at any time, each time 3 grams, use warm wine to mix.

[Effect] Used to treat upper consumption, unquenchable thirst in diabetes.

3. Winter Melon Seed and Ophiopogon Decoction for Diabetes

[Formula] 30 grams of winter melon seeds, 10 to 15 grams of Ophiopogon japonicus, 5 grams of Coptis.

[Preparation and Usage] Boil in water and take as a decoction.

[Effect] Used to treat diabetes patients with excessive thirst and frequent urination.

4. Land Snail Soak for Diabetes

[Formula] 14 land snails.

[Preparation and Usage] Soak overnight in water, then drink the water.

[Function] Used to treat diabetes patients with excessive thirst.

5. Earth Ginseng and Golden Raspberry Root Decoction for Diabetes

[Formula] 60 grams each of Earth Ginseng and Golden Raspberry root.

[Preparation and Usage] Boil in water and take as a decoction.

[Effect] Used to treat diabetes patients with excessive drinking and urination.

6. Sweet Potato Leaf and Winter Melon Soup for Diabetes

[Formula] 150 grams of sweet potato leaves, 200 grams of winter melon (with skin).

[Preparation and Usage] Add 500 milliliters of water to the sweet potato leaves and winter melon, cook until the winter melon is soft. Take in 1 to 2 servings.

[Effect] Suitable for diabetes.

7. Onion for Diabetes

[Formula] 100 grams of onion.

[Preparation and Usage] Wash the onions, blanch in boiling water, chop finely, add a little cooking oil for seasoning. Eat with rice daily.

[Effect] Used to treat diabetes, high blood pressure, and arteriosclerosis.

8. Persimmon Leaf for Diabetes

[Formula] An appropriate amount of fresh persimmon leaves.

[Preparation and Usage] Wash the persimmon leaves, soak in salt water, eat 5 to 6 pieces daily.

[Effect] Used to treat diabetes.

9. Mushroom for Diabetes

[Formula] An appropriate amount of mushrooms.

[Preparation and Usage] Cook or boil the mushrooms as soup and drink regularly.

[Effect] Used to treat diabetes. The mushroom culture solution has a blood sugar-lowering effect, and eating mushrooms regularly helps improve diabetes symptoms.

10. Glutinous Rice Straw for Diabetes

[Formula] 10 grams of glutinous rice straw.

[Preparation and Usage] Cut the glutinous rice straw into small pieces, put into a sand pot, soak with boiling water. Drink as tea daily.

[Effect] Suitable for diabetes patients with dry mouth and throat.
