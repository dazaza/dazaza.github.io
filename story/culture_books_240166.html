"Marrying the House" - How many heroes are baffled by a set of housing?

by jqrxass on 2009-09-19 15:20:47

Four co-renting friends all have their own ideals and decent jobs. They should be a group of spirited, carefree young people. However, they all encountered an insurmountable obstacle: housing.

When the newspaper reporter Liu Jimin moved into his new home, he did not feel happiness or satisfaction. Instead, he was deeply troubled by his conscience and morally blamed himself.

The government official Gu Wentao abandoned his beloved Wang Keli for housing and married Du Yuxin, the daughter of a certain leader. The unhappily married Gu Wentao rekindled his old flame with Wang Keli, with predictable consequences.

The technician at the network company, Liu Dongzi, worked overtime frantically for housing and collapsed forever at his computer desk on a snowy winter night.

The primary school music teacher Zhao Jian witnessed the misfortunes that befell three of his roommates in their quest to buy a house. He resolutely left the big city and went to a remote mountainous area to seek the beauty beyond materialism that had been forgotten by the metropolis.

"Married to Housing" Part One

"Liu Jimin, don't change the subject," Ye Qing said pointedly to Liu Jimin. "Not to be rude, but look around among our colleagues. What kind of parents do they have? There's no one like your parents. They didn't contribute a single penny when their son got married. At the very least, they should have given us the down payment for buying a house. Even if we earned money later and paid them back, isn't that reasonable?"

"This matter can't be rushed," Liu Jimin carefully considered before replying. "Even with the down payment, with our current salaries, paying two or three thousand yuan per month for the mortgage, how would we live?"

"What do you mean?" Ye Qing pushed Liu Jimin away from her chest, sat up and stared directly at him, saying, "So according to you, you never planned to buy a house and get married, right?"

"Married to Housing" Part Two

Du Yuxin's house was too large, so much so that it was ridiculous. Upon entering, there was a living room that made one feel irritable, and the height of the rooms inside the building was also quite out of proportion, high enough to form a duplex. Du Yuxin introduced that in their building, many families had formed duplexes, but they hadn't. That's because there were few people in their family, plenty of rooms, enough space to live, and no need to make a duplex.

The furnishings inside were also extravagant, with white Persian carpets on the ground and various imported high-end furniture such as Italian water buffalo leather sofas. Even the bathroom was extravagantly luxurious, equipped with infrared automatic faucets and hand dryers and other gadgets.

"Damn, are they planning to remodel it into a guesthouse or what?" Wen Tao muttered angrily while washing his hands in the bathroom.

"Married to Housing" Part Three

From talking about housing prices to the topic of women, Liu Jimin's heart became somewhat confused. These days, Ye Qing did not quarrel with him. After all, she just extorted 40,000 yuan from Factory Director Song, and her joy had not yet passed. In these days, she was doubly gentle to Liu Jimin. The reason for Liu Jimin's confusion was that he suddenly felt less confident. He did not know whether Ye Qing loved him more or loved the house more. For a house, she could torment him until he was in pain; for a house, she could let him take reckless risks without hesitation. Liu Jimin thought that, unknowingly, buying a house had become a prerequisite for Ye Qing to continue loving him. If he could never afford a house, sooner or later, Ye Qing would leave him. Thus, is Ye Qing marrying him or marrying a house? Liu Jimin couldn't figure it out, and his mind was in chaos.

"Married to Housing" Part Four

" Do you think I want to be this way?" Jiayi's face suddenly turned pale as she trembled and said, "Maybe I am too materialistic and vulgar. But life is like this. We are together, without money, without a house, what will we use to get married in the future, how will we live? Don't I have to consider these things? My cousin is an example. Two years ago, she insisted on marrying a man with poor economic ability despite her family's opposition. Back then, the two were madly in love, and no one could break them apart. But they broke up within two years of marriage. Why? It all comes down to housing and money. Life is like this, I don't want to replicate my cousin's life..."

"Damn, you're ruthless." Liu Dongzi rudely interrupted Jiayi's words, turned around and left her behind, quickly leaving the company.
