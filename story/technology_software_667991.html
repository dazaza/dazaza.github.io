"Golden Crown 4th Generation"

1. Function Introduction:

1. Card issuing rate controlled at five levels (95-99).

2. Genuine A four plums can unexpectedly obtain high multiples.

3. Adjustable highest and lowest betting scores.

2. Adjustment Diagram:

1. Press "Set" to enter the adjustment diagram, then input the password. (Initial password is 6 times "Deal";) Display: lNPUT PASSWORD * * * * * * Input your password (no numerical display, only the red star moves), pachinko type. After completion (all stars turn red).

If the password is wrong: The bottom will prompt PASSWORD ERROR, you can re-enter immediately.

If the password is correct: a. Press "Set" to reset the password. SET PASSWORD * * * * * * After completion (all stars turn red), press "Deal" to store the password and enter the main interface of the adjustment diagram. You can press "Keep 5" to exit if you regret.

b. Press "Deal" to enter the main interface of the adjustment diagram, pachinko gameplay.
