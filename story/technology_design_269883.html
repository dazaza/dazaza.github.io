Short-term Arbitrage Techniques for Small Capital

The favorite stocks of small and medium investors are usually short-term varieties that will reach the daily upper limit or soar significantly the next day. Generally speaking, short-term individual stocks that will reach the daily upper limit or soar significantly the next day have the following characteristics:

1. The stock market surges significantly the next day. The main basis for this judgment is: a major favorable news appears in the market before the opening of the second day; the market falls by more than 7% in a single day or the PSY indicator breaks through 18; the stock market shows an initial increase in price and volume on the previous day. These situations will lead to many stocks reaching their daily upper limits or soaring significantly. Among them, individual stocks with a circulating share capital of less than 50 million shares (smaller floating supply, generally lighter selling pressure), better daily K-line combinations (preferably conforming to some classic attack or about-to-rise patterns), 5-day, 10-day, and 30-day moving averages in a bullish alignment or quasi-bullish alignment, and strong technical indicators are very likely to reach their daily upper limits. Individual stocks that conform to favorable policies and meet the above technical characteristics tend to have larger increases and longer sustained rise periods.

2. A certain individual stock suddenly has a huge favorable news known by a relatively large number of market participants, but the stock price has not yet reacted to the rise. Such individual stocks often quickly reach their daily upper limits under strong buying demand in the market the next day. For such individual stocks, it is necessary to be well-informed and quick to react, especially for some sudden favorable news, such as pharmaceutical stocks targeted at atypical pneumonia.

3. Under the background of a strong stock market, if there are consecutive two days of price increase and volume expansion on Thursday and Friday, it is easy for the stock to reach its daily upper limit on the coming Monday.

The above three situations require coordination with information and the overall market background.

4. Individual stocks that meet the following conditions are also likely to surge significantly the next day:

(1) Circulating share capital is less than 30 million shares, and the stock price is low. Stocks priced at 3 yuan are much more likely to reach their daily upper limits than those priced at 15 yuan.

(2) The daily K-line combination is good, and the volume ratio has already started to expand.

(3) The 5-day, 10-day, and 30-day moving averages are in a bullish alignment or quasi-bullish alignment. Stock recommendations.

(4) Technical indicators show strength, especially when the daily MACD is about to produce red columns, and at least one or two red columns appear in the 5-minute, 15-minute, and 30-minute MACDs.

5. Individual stocks that meet the following situation are mostly likely to reach their daily upper limits the next day:

Individual stocks have favorable news or good topics + 4(1)(2)(3)(4) + the overall market does not fall (better if the overall market rises).

Generally speaking, selecting good stocks, especially short-term dark horses that will reach their daily upper limits or soar significantly the next day, is very difficult. However, paying attention to some tricks, widely and deeply collecting information on individual stocks in daily life, and conducting in-depth and detailed research on individual stocks with news can help. Individual stocks with real news and meeting 4(1)(2)(3)(4), after being selected in this way, often become short-term dark horses and tend to soar significantly the next day.
