How Giovanni Ice Crystal Paint Leads the Development of the Art Glass Industry (1)

How Giovanni Ice Crystal Painting Leads the Development of the Art Glass Industry - Market Observation - Industry News - Building Materials Network

The development of the art glass industry in recent years has been nothing short of remarkable, with significant advancements in both product types and styles. However, some situations have arisen where glass is slightly beautified and then marketed as art glass. As the saying goes: "Art glass is a basket, and everything gets thrown into it." Nevertheless, the emergence of Giovanni Ice Crystal Painting has somewhat changed this situation.

Art glass is often perceived as having elegant shapes but comes with a high price tag. The reason for the high cost is simple: complex craftsmanship. Therefore, anything remotely related is associated with art glass to fetch a better price. Indeed, in the past, an experienced master might take months to produce just one piece of art glass. The effort and dedication involved were evident. Nowadays, with technological advancements, many art glass effects can be designed on computers and produced by machines. However, investing in such a production line costs millions or even tens of millions, which also becomes a justification for high prices. Is it really worth that much? The sudden appearance of Giovanni Ice Crystal Painting has revealed many secrets.

Firstly, in terms of craftsmanship, machine-made art glass is becoming increasingly exquisite. However, there are still many clichés unavoidable, such as printing three-dimensional patterns on glass or applying bright, grand colors. This requires heating the glass to a high temperature until it melts, so consumers recognize its value. But can they create moonlight effects? Note, moonlight, not glow-in-the-dark. The difference lies in that glow-in-the-dark emits green light in the dark, which might be acceptable if seen for a while, but could be startling at first glance — you know why... Also, can they create three-dimensional effects without melting the glass? All these things can be easily achieved by Giovanni Ice Crystal Painting, fully demonstrating its high-tech content. If we were to compete solely on shape or color, wouldn't that be a bit outdated?

In terms of investment, the current price quote for Giovanni Ice Crystal Painting is less than 70,000 RMB. Those in the art glass industry know that even a domestically produced production line is very expensive, let alone imported ones. If you say no need for machines, just hire an experienced master craftsman. This statement is very correct. However, it would inevitably lead to another issue: how can humans compare with machines? In terms of efficiency, machines can operate non-stop for 24 hours; in terms of cost, machines require a one-time investment, whereas human expenses far exceed those of machines. Machines don’t leave, but people may not stay. Giovanni Ice Crystal Painting combines the advantages of both, lowering the requirements for worker qualifications through innovative technology. Even someone without experience can master all the techniques in five to six days. Once the techniques are mastered, the equipment becomes auxiliary, making operations very simple.

With these two major advantages, the market impact of Giovanni Ice Crystal Painting on the art glass market is undeniable. When Jinan Guangfa Technology Co., Ltd. first introduced this technology, the art glass industry did not realize the hidden value. Now, Giovanni Ice Crystal Painting has become a standard configuration for home decoration. A veteran in the home decoration industry once said, "There's only so much space in a home; if your stuff takes up the space, there won't be room for mine." So, although home decoration spaces look large, they are actually quite limited. By the time Giovanni Ice Crystal Painting gained ground, art glass dealers started panicking. Many art glass distributors found that ice crystal paintings were gradually encroaching on their territory, changing the market landscape. However, they soon realized: Isn't Giovanni Ice Crystal Painting the most beautiful art glass? No, it’s more than art glass; it's the art of glass. Although the wording is merely reversed, the implied praise is evident. The manufacturers of Giovanni Ice Crystal Painting have gained an additional sales channel, and traditional art glass operators have also gained a new way to make money, achieving a win-win situation.

Now, Giovanni Ice Crystal Painting continues to evolve, from effects to craftsmanship. Each transformation creates a wave of fashion in the market. What the art glass industry originally dismissed as a "trifle" has now become an industry trendsetter. This shows that regardless of what kind of product, it needs to meet market demand. Whether pioneers or latecomers, only by capturing the hearts of consumers can one truly dominate the market and become a leader. If one relies solely on "unspoken rules," so-called industry norms, or has a conservative mindset, they are destined to be eliminated.
