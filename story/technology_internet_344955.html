Related thematic articles: Untitled Document 　　Xiaowu gave Tang San a glare, and as soon as she thought of how she was badly hit by the stone last time, she became furious, "What are you waiting for? Who knows when you will be back. They have already bullied me at my door. That bastard Xiao Leader said to make me his pet rabbit. If I could still endure it, I wouldn't call myself Xiaowu." Tang San frowned and thought in his heart that such dirty thoughts were inappropriate for children this age, so he must teach them a lesson and let them know that working students are not easy to bully. With this thought, he wiped his finger on his waist and turned his eyes to the battlefield. By now, Wang Sheng had many bruises on his body, his roars continued, his soul power had become weaker and weaker. But his opponent had not been hit once, playing with him like a cat catching a mouse. Wang Sheng's attack method was too single. Taking advantage of his soul power not yet completely consumed, he launched the last wave of attacks. After running two steps, he rushed towards Liu Long again. This time, he had made up his mind. Even if he was beaten badly, he would leave some marks on his opponent, romance. Liu Long has been dealing with Wang Sheng for a long time, naturally he wouldn't fail to see his purpose. Seeing Wang Sheng rushing over, his expression became serious. He swung his long stick, hitting three consecutive sticks towards Wang Sheng's head and both shoulders. As long as he drove away Wang Sheng who was almost spent, he would no longer be able to launch another decent attack. But just at this moment, suddenly, Liu Long felt as if his hands holding the stick were bitten by mosquitoes, delaying his attack momentarily. His attack was originally calculated, but this delay immediately revealed a flaw. The first stick did hit Wang Sheng's head, but Wang Sheng still rushed forward, making his next two sticks miss. For an instrument soul master using long weapons, being close by a beast soul master was absolutely a devastating blow. Even though Liu Long's strength was higher than Wang Sheng's, the result was still the same. Bang, Wang Sheng slapped Liu Long's head with a tiger claw, sending him flying. Then he flipped his body, and whipped Liu Long's back with a tiger tail whip, smashing him heavily to the ground. Liu Long didn't have Wang Sheng's good resistance ability. His soul power was all on the long stick, and his own defense wasn't outstanding. These two attacks from Wang Sheng were full force. When Liu Long smashed heavily onto the ground, he immediately passed out. Wang Sheng excitedly roared to the sky, as if trying to release all the resentment in his heart these few years. Xiao Leader was originally watching leisurely as Liu Long played with Wang Sheng. The sudden reversal on the scene shocked him greatly. You must know, among his subordinates, Liu Long was also ranked among the top three in strength. If it weren't for him already having the first soul ring, there was no guarantee he could defeat Liu Long. Xiaowu mocked, "Defeated flowers and willows are indeed defeated flowers and willows, truly unable to withstand a blow. Xiao Leader, what do you say?" Xiao Leader waved his hand, letting other subordinates pull Liu Long down for emergency treatment. They were only students of the primary soul academy, with that much soul power, they couldn't kill anyone. "The first match we lost. Ling Feng, you go up." The second person Xiao Leader sent out was a tall senior student, small nose and small eyes, thin figure which didn't show that he was twelve years old. Instead, he seemed to be the same age as Xiaowu and Tang San. "Call Wang Sheng back. I'll go up." Tang San whispered in Xiaowu's ear. Xiaowu's eyes were also quite sharp. Naturally, he could see that the second person the opponent sent out was an agile type soul warrior, exactly suppressing Wang Sheng. Wang Sheng had also consumed too much soul power before, bound to lose. "Wang Sheng, come back. Let Little Three go up this round. You can rest." Wang Sheng was still not fully recovered from the excitement of defeating Liu Long before. Hearing Xiaowu's voice, he then realized that Tang San had returned at some point. Knowing his own situation, even though he defeated Liu Long, he indeed had no more fighting power left. How would he know, if it weren't for Tang San secretly launching three small stones, restricting Liu Long's attack, how could he have achieved the previous victory? "Are you a working student?" Ling Feng looked at Tang San, doubt flashing in his eyes. Tang San just nodded at him, "Please move." Although Ling Feng was short and thin, he was still taller than Tang San who was only in the first grade. This was a duel about who could become the student leader, so he didn't think any more. After taking another look at Tang San's belt, he leaped into the air, flipping nimbly, arms spread out, actually gliding two meters, pouncing towards Tang San's head. Beast soul, Tang San instantly saw what kind of soul his opponent had. Master's teaching these days wasn't wasted. This student named Ling Feng was an agile type battle soul master. His beast soul should be a flying beast. Judging from his not fierce appearance and attack method, it shouldn't be an eagle. So, it should be a swallow or crow type soul. Watching the opponent pounce towards him, Tang San stood still, as if the opponent didn't exist. Ling Feng's speed was very fast. In the sixth grade students, he was known as the fastest one, Xiao Leader's best friend, and his body was very nimble. Just as he was about to reach Tang San, suddenly, his body flipped again. Spreading his arms, he forcibly changed direction, coming behind Tang San, swinging both arms, chopping towards Tang San's neck. Until Ling Feng flipped over his head, Tang San didn't move. Facing an unknown opponent, unchanging to meet all changes was the best choice.
