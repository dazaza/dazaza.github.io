How to top up Q Coins with your phone?

1. The gifted Q Coin allowance that users receive will expire after 7 days, please transfer as soon as possible; 2. For the first time mobile users call "Wealth from Heaven", they will become new users and can only get 10 Q Coins per month; After 2 months, they will automatically upgrade to old users and be able to receive 20 Q Coins per month. If old users do not call "Wealth from Heaven" for two consecutive months, they will automatically become new users; Also note that the "monthly" here does not refer to a calendar month, but from the 11th of each month to the 10th of the next month constitutes one month; 3. New users can only transfer to QQ numbers with the same province as their mobile phone number, while old users have no such restrictions; 4. The upper limit of information fees generated by monthly calls to the 1259088xxxx number segment is 50 yuan (30 yuan for Liaoning Mobile and Jilin Mobile), if this upper limit is exceeded, normal use will not be possible.

3. Due to system reasons, there may be some delays in the gifted Q Coins for new users in some provinces. You need to wait several hours before checking and transferring: Provinces with a 4-hour delay: Liaoning, Jilin, Inner Mongolia, Xinjiang, Fujian, Chongqing, Jiangsu; Provinces with an 8-hour delay: Hainan, Gansu, Ningxia, Guangdong;

4. For other province new users and all old users, Q Coins will arrive within a few minutes (if the network is busy, please try again later, we guarantee that Q Coins will arrive within 24 hours);

5. Note, if you hear a voice indicating over-limit and prompting to hang up when calling, this part will not incur any charges.
