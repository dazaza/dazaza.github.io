Post-90s girl suspected of being addicted to stealing, judge conducts a psychological test in court

by xue94fwsh on 2012-02-29 14:04:32

What can be seen from a test paper with 399 multiple-choice questions? After preliminary investigation, Lu Zhengu informed Xiuxiu that she would take an exam. "The questions are very simple, all common sense in daily life. Considering your age is relatively small, we just do a test, hope you answer the questions truthfully." While Xiuxiu continued to be tried, Xia Lei and Chen Hongwei were "grading" the test paper. Half an hour later, the result came out. The reporter flipped through this test paper, a thick stack of it, all 399 questions are multiple-choice (part of the test questions and Xiuxiu's answers are shown in the figure below). More than half an hour later, Xiuxiu finished the test paper with 399 questions, returned to the courtroom, and continued to be tried. "Why did you steal money?" "I just wanted to steal, I feel like I can't control myself." "What did you do with the stolen money?" "Bought an Apple phone, also went to the Changzhou Dinosaur Park for fun." What is the use of psychological evaluation? "Xiuxiu often feels unwell physically, but does not know the specific uncomfortable parts, her mood tends to be more melancholic. This may be because she does not feel cared for by people around her, or in her view, when she needs help, friends or family are not there." Xia Lei said, besides, in the evaluation index of "deception", it can be clearly seen that she has a certain degree of concealment and disguise ability, "consciously avoided some key issues, which also shows from another angle that her mentality is mature enough." At 10 o'clock yesterday morning, in the criminal court of Jianye Court, presiding judge Lu Zhengu was talking with the suspect Xiuxiu (pseudonym). The defendant Xiuxiu on the stand was short in stature, spoke very softly. Jianye Court tries to use the evaluation results to help suspects reform. Share to: Welcome to comment, I want to comment. Xia Lei said, this test paper is a very famous psychological evaluation test paper internationally. From the current test results, there are too many hidden things in Xiuxiu's personality, she does not trust the outside world much, lacks care, and may have been hurt before. Not long ago, Jianye Court also handled a case where a blind person harmed others. During the trial, Lu Zhengu found that the blind person as the defendant had strong psychological defense, strong self-esteem, and very face-conscious, which was one of the direct causes of his crime. During the trial, the psychological counselor patiently guided him, half an hour later, the stubborn blind person voluntarily confessed his mistakes and said he would reform well. This is a new measure promoted by the Criminal Court of Jianye Court since the end of last year - introducing the psychological evaluation mechanism in criminal trials, the results will be used as a reference for judges' trials, and will be used as important data for future suspects' reform. Xiuxiu was brought to an office next to the courtroom. There were three staff members of Nanjing City's "Master Ning Hotline" (a free psychological counseling hotline established under the leadership of the Publicity Department of Nanjing Municipal Committee), two of whom were volunteers Xia Lei and Chen Hongwei. Both were national level II psychological counselors. After a brief conversation with Xiuxiu, they handed over the questions to her. "This kind of evaluation is different from psychiatric identification, the results will not directly affect the trial, nor will they become statutory sentencing circumstances," said presiding judge Lu Zhengu. At 5 o'clock in the afternoon of that day, the verdict of the case came out. Xiuxiu was sentenced to three years in prison for theft. This result was not affected by the evaluation results, "despite this, doing this evaluation still has great significance." □Fast reporter Tian Xueting Lu Zhengu said that the reasons why defendants in criminal cases stand on the defendant's seat are many, but it cannot be denied that when they harm others, they also passively become victims themselves, and their psychological changes cannot be ignored. Introducing the psychological evaluation mechanism, allowing psychological counselors to intervene in the trial process, giving necessary psychological counseling and help to suspects, "let them recognize their own mistakes from the heart, and be full of confidence in this society, see both the dark side and the good side, so that they can truly repent in their hearts, and directly make qualitative changes in serving sentences and reforming." Relevant personnel of the Jianye District Court said that the current psychological evaluation mechanism is still in the trial stage. The evaluation results will be included in the suspect's file materials. If the suspect eventually has to serve a sentence, these materials will be sent to the place of punishment. Judges will communicate with the educators, and the evaluation results will be used as important data for educational work, "targeted help them, only then can the possibility of recidivism be fundamentally reduced." A post-90s girl steals repeatedly Weibo recommendation | Today's Weibo hot topics Xiuxiu, born in 1991, is from Henan Province. She went out to work after graduating from junior high school. In January 2009, while surfing the Internet at a net bar in Henan, she stole 1000 yuan from someone else. In May 2009, Xiuxiu visited a friend's house, and during the night sleep, she stole a gold medal worth more than 3000 yuan from her friend's neck, and also stole 650 yuan in cash. In October 2011, Xiuxiu applied for a job at a hotel in Nanjing. After working for more than ten days, she took advantage of the financial manager's absence, broke the lock and stole 12000 yuan before fleeing. With this money, Xiuxiu first bought a mobile phone, then went to the Changzhou Dinosaur Park for fun, until she was caught.
