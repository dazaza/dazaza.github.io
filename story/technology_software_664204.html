Rose Fenjiu, also known as Rose Wine, is produced by Xinghuacun Fenjiu Co., Ltd. Both product quality and craftsmanship have seen significant improvements compared to the old version.

1) Product Quality: Base wine differs - Gem Rose uses 20-year aged Fenjiu as its base.

2) Blending Process: Secret recipes enable long-term storage of low-alcohol beverages. Aged Fenjiu serves as the foundation, with premium fresh rose petals soaked in vats for several months, followed by distillation and blending with oligofructose. After aging, filtering, and blending, the final product is a low-alcohol beverage that can be preserved for a long time.

The superior rose varieties are sourced from famous rose-producing areas like Pingyin, Shandong, and Miaofeng Mountain, Beijing. Every May, when the flower buds are about to bloom, they are collected for their aromatic richness, large size, thick petals, purple color, and lack of exposed stamens. These premium roses are steeped in aged Fenjiu for several months to create Rose Fenjiu, fully preserving the health benefits of roses. Drinking Method:

Rose Fenjiu transcends traditional baijiu. To enjoy its rich aroma and unique characteristics, use a dedicated rose wine glass. Its wide body and varying degrees of mouth contraction retain the fragrance optimally. When tasting, bury your nose in the glass to savor the concentrated aroma.
