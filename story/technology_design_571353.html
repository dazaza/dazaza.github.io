The method of making "Di San Xian": 1. Peel the eggplant and potato, cut them into irregular cubes; tear the green pepper into small pieces by hand. The method of making stir-fried pork slices: 1. First, cut the pork into slightly thick strips, add egg white, salt and water starch to stir evenly, marinate for a while 2. Heat the oil in the pot, fry the meat slices to golden yellow over medium heat, about one or two minutes, then drain the oil 3. Fry again for one minute 4. Leave a little oil in the pot, add chopped garlic, ginger and scallion to stir-fry until fragrant, cut the green pepper and red pepper into small pieces, put them in, stir-fry for a while 5. Add the meat slices and stir-fry 6. Add vinegar, cooking wine, sugar and a little starch juice used for marinating the meat slices, pour it into the pot, stir-fry evenly and it's done. The method of making braised pork ribs with green beans: 1. Cut scallions, ginger and garlic first, a little. Pour oil into the pan, stir-fry scallions, ginger and garlic until fragrant. 2. Put in a spoonful of sugar, a spoonful of soy sauce and stir-fry the pork ribs, until the bones on both sides of the ribs are exposed a bit, then add boiling hot water, a little more than covering the ribs, add green beans. 5. Add water and cornstarch, simmer over high heat until the sauce thickens. 3. Add a spoonful of cooking wine, a spoonful of soy sauce, a pinch of Sichuan peppercorn powder, and a pinch of pepper. Simmer over low heat for 10 minutes, then add some shredded scallions and continue stewing. 4. It can be eaten in about half an hour. The method of making dumpling soup: 1. After the pot is heated, add a little salad oil, heat it up, put the shredded pork into the pot first and stir-fry until the color changes, 2. Then add minced mushrooms and stir-fry until fragrant, add two small bowls of water and boil. 3. Mix half a bowl of flour with cold water into fine dough lumps and set aside. 4. After the soup boils, pour the dough lumps into the pot and boil, add eggs, then add chopped green vegetables and shrimp granules, and cook until the vegetables soften. 5. Before taking the pot off the heat, add sesame oil and a little salt for seasoning. The method of making chicken stewed with mushrooms: 1. Wash the chicken, chop it into small pieces; 2. Soak the mushrooms in warm water for 30 minutes, wash and set aside; 3. Heat the pot, add a little oil, add the chicken pieces and stir-fry until the chicken changes color, then add scallions, ginger, star anise, dried red chili peppers, salt, soy sauce, sugar, cooking wine, stir-fry until evenly colored, add an appropriate amount of water and stew for about ten minutes, then pour in the mushrooms, braise over medium heat for thirty to forty minutes until done. The method of making braised pork ribs: 1. Put an appropriate amount of vegetable oil in the pot, after heating, add ginger and scallions to stir-fry until fragrant, then put the spareribs in the pot and stir-fry, 2. When the meat turns white, add soy sauce, cooking wine, sugar, then add just enough boiling water to cover the spareribs, 3. After boiling over high heat, simmer over low heat for about 20 minutes. 4. Check if the spareribs are tender, add an appropriate amount of salt according to taste, then thicken the sauce over high heat. 5. Finally, a delicious braised pork rib dish is ready. 1. Pot-braised pork loin: 1 Cut the loin into 5mm thick slices and coat with water and cornstarch batter. 2 Mix sugar, rice vinegar, salt, monosodium glutamate, sesame oil, cooking wine as seasoning juice and set aside. 3 Heat the wok with oil to 60% hot, fry the loin slices one by one until golden brown and crispy outside but tender inside. Remove and set aside. 4 Heat a little oil in the wok, stir-fry the auxiliary ingredients until fragrant, then add the fried loin slices, quickly add the seasoning juice and stir-fry evenly. The method of making braised pig intestines: 1. Cut the delicious intestines into thick slices, mix with frying powder and a little water evenly, then put into the hot oil pot and fry as reserve. 2. Stir-fry the seasonings with 1 tablespoon of oil until fragrant, then add 3 tablespoons of soup stock. After the soup becomes thick, immediately add the pig intestines and stir-fry quickly. Before removing from the pot, add 1 teaspoon of aged vinegar, sprinkle with coriander. The method of making pork stewed with vermicelli: 1. Scrub the pork belly clean, cut into chunks. Soak the vermicelli until soft, cut the scallions into sections, slice the ginger. 2. Heat oil in the pot, stir-fry the pork until color changes, remove. 3. Stir-fry sugar to make caramel color, add broth, all seasonings and pork, bring to a boil over high heat, skim off the foam, then simmer until tender. The method of making pulled yam: 1. Peel the yam, cut into even small pieces, rinse with water. 2. Heat salad oil, dry the yam thoroughly, deep-fry slowly until golden brown, remove and set aside. 3. Heat the oil to 180 degrees Celsius, deep-fry the yam again until crispy. 4. Leave an appropriate amount of oil in the pot, then add sanding sugar and water, boil over fire, boil into a golden yellow transparent sugar paste, add yam and coat with sugar paste. 5. Coat the chopsticks with a little oil, serve the yam onto the plate. The method of making braised beef short ribs with potatoes: 1. Peel the potatoes, cut into small cubes, deep-fry in hot oil and remove; cut the beef short ribs into small cubes, blanch to remove blood water, rinse and set aside; peel the carrots, cut into small cubes. 2. Place the beef short ribs with star anise, tangerine peel into the pot, add water, bring to a boil, add wine and simmer for half an hour, then add carrots and potato cubes and cook together, add seasoning 1 and 3 cups of clear water. 3. Pick out the star anise and tangerine peel, thicken the soup with a little cornstarch, then serve. The method of homemade blood sausage: 1. Put the fresh pig's blood into a basin, break any solidified pieces; 2. Heat the white meat soup, add salt, Sichuan peppercorns, monosodium glutamate, black pepper powder, stir evenly and cool down; 3. Sieve and pour into the pig's blood basin, add chopped coriander and stir evenly; 4. Stuff into washed intestines, tie the mouth with string; 5. Boil the prepared intestines in a pot of clear water, after boiling, simmer for 15 minutes, take out, cool with cold water, untie the string. Then add the vermicelli and stew until flavorful. 2. Pour more oil into the pot, when it's seven-tenths hot, put the potato chunks in first, fry them until golden yellow and slightly translucent, then take them out and set aside. 3. Then put the eggplant into the oil pot, fry until golden yellow, add green pepper chunks and scoop out together. 4. Fry chopped green onions and minced garlic with a little hot oil, add broth, light soy sauce, sugar, salt, eggplant, potato and green pepper chunks, stir-fry briefly.
