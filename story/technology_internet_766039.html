Huawei Cooperates with CCTV to Build Big Data Storage System, with 60% Energy Saving

by anonymous on 2013-09-04 14:38:36

September 2nd news, Huawei announced that it has officially signed a cooperation agreement with CCTV to establish a strategic partnership in the field of big data storage and provide leading technology and application models for the media asset industry. It is reported that this big data storage system can save energy by 60%-80%.

CCTV is one of the most influential media institutions globally and has profound application experience and forward-looking strategies in the radio and television industry. They plan to develop and construct a leading, large-capacity, green energy-saving, and high-performance media asset storage system in the industry. Huawei is a global leader in ICT suppliers, focusing on the construction and business development of next-generation broadcasting networks, committed to serving the broadcasting media asset industry with innovative technology. This strategic cooperation is based on the common vision of both parties in the research of optical and electrical media asset technology, jointly developing an adaptive deep energy-saving high-density media asset storage system.

It is understood that the system will effectively provide large-capacity and high-performance storage, significantly enhancing the efficiency of media asset access and management. The system's energy saving reaches 60%-80%, making it the world's most green and energy-efficient media asset storage system.

Zheng Yelai, President of Huawei's IT Product Line, stated: "Huawei has a globally distributed R&D capability center, providing leading and competitive IT products and solutions. The strategic cooperation agreement signed today will comprehensively deepen the cooperation between Huawei and CCTV, jointly developing leading technology applications and products in the broadcasting media asset field, promoting the informatization development of the entire industry." (Kang Zhao)
