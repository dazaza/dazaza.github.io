In order to prepare for the Champions League final, Barcelona will silently face Real Madrid's challenge in the near future. After the end of this season, Barcelona will launch a comprehensive counterattack on the new battleship...

NetEase Sports reported on May 18:

Four rounds of national derby have almost broken the relationship between Spain's two major powers. Guardiola, who has always been good-natured, cursed Mourinho at a press conference. The top and bottom of Real Madrid criticized Barcelona for relying on UEFA protection to upgrade to the Champions League final, but they chose blindness to forget the benefits they received from the referee. Pepe, who trampled Messi, Abaloa, who failed to elbow and then trample Villa, and Marcelo, who trampled Pedro's knee and bled, were not all red-carded.

Daily Sport newspaper screenshot: Barcelona will comprehensively retaliate against Real Madrid after the May 28 Champions League match

" Mourinho is the x media champion," Guardiola once said. For the repeated challenges of Real Madrid, Barcelona has not responded much recently. After Mourinho bombarded Barcelona's victory with UEFA's dark support, the Barcelona official only sued him to UEFA. After the lawsuit was rejected by UEFA, Barcelona did not appeal. Real Madrid, which has countless hurtful moves, instead sued many Barcelona players for diving and asked UEFA to punish Busquets for alleged racial discrimination.

Real Madrid took the position of the victim and continued to challenge Barcelona, but Barcelona basically refused to respond. According to the Daily Sport revealed that the main purpose of Barcelona's move is to prepare for the Champions League final. Real Madrid no longer has a pursuit this season. The new battleship can transfer the attention of the media and fans by verbal warfare. Barcelona does not want to fall into the carefully designed trap of Real Madrid. The Red and Blue Army hopes that the players will focus all their attention on the Champions League final on the local time of May 28.

When will Barcelona be humble? According to the Daily Sport revealed, Barcelona will launch a comprehensive counterattack on Real Madrid after the Champions League final. After the end of this season, Barcelona will no longer cry in silence. Barcelona's current strategy is to break through from the drug scandal. Cope radio reporter Alcala, who first disclosed the news, revealed that his information came from inside Real Madrid. Barcelona has already sued Cope Radio Court. The Red and Blue Army wants Alcala to say who the mastermind is. If this thing was really made by Real Madrid, the new battleship will become the defendant in court.
