What to do with 100,000 yuan to start a business

by yuanshuai on 2010-04-05 14:40:19

100,000 yuan is a neither too much nor too little starting capital for budding entrepreneurs. For those entrepreneurs applying for the YBC 50,000 yuan interest-free loan, 100,000 yuan is enough to run their projects well; as for an entrepreneur who has been in the workplace for many years before starting a business, 100,000 yuan might be enough to make him feel nervous about choosing an unfamiliar project, even one related to health and weight loss common knowledge.

Here, we (China Entrepreneurship Portal) believe that an important principle in choosing a project is that the entrepreneur should have a "control degree" over their own project, which means trying to start a business in an industry or field you are familiar with; or if it's an unfamiliar field, having a partner who knows the industry well; otherwise, you must be prepared for failure and able to bear the risk of loss.

For what can be done with 100,000 yuan, here are some examples:

One, a small fast-food restaurant - catering is a permanent project. As long as you know how to operate a restaurant and find a good location, such a project is feasible. Moreover, you could even provide fast food delivery services to nearby office buildings.

Two, specialty stores for unique products - for example, many lazy person product shops, strange and unusual stores, and various creative gift shops that appeared in recent years. The key to these types of projects lies in the frequency of product updates and cost control.

Three, various clothing stores - good entrepreneurial projects in the clothing sector include women's clothing, children's clothing, maternity wear, plus-size clothing, and other specialized clothing options. If you're franchising, be very cautious, and if you're not familiar with this area, it's best to work in a clothing store for a while first or find someone experienced to partner with, learning how to maintain health and well-being.
