"Windu Water City" belongs to Beijing Hongfu Group, with a superior geographical location and convenient transportation environment. To the north, there is the only natural river flowing through Beijing - the Wen-Yu River. The Prince's Mansion of Windu Water City was built on the site of the old Pingxi Prince's Mansion during the Qing Yongzheng period. Designed by the Australian Design Group, it is an advanced and fully functional comprehensive holiday hotel. Major operating projects include Water Space, Hot Spring Health Club, Shuicheng International Grand Hotel, outdoor hot springs, quadrangle courtyards, commercial streets, and parking lots, etc. The total area is 184.5 mu (12.3 hectares), with a total construction area of 52,500 square meters. "Windu Water City" has abundant hot spring water. Currently, the five developed hot spring wells rank first in Beijing in terms of overall quality, with water temperatures reaching as high as 79℃.

HI Water Space

Covering an area of about 20,000 square meters, it is the largest comprehensive water recreation venue in Asia. It contains artificial waves, skateboarding, space basins, flying shuttles, beaches, competitive slides, standard indoor competition pools, children's water play pools, interactive castles, outdoor hot spring areas, dining areas, semi-underground water landscapes, coffee houses, merchandise departments, leisure areas, VIP rooms, bio-mimetic green plants, etc., capable of accommodating 5,000 people simultaneously to experience the pure joy of HI in the water. Additionally, the creatively designed underwater stage can host large events such as ethnic-themed song and dance evenings, water weddings, fashion shows, etc. To the north of the Water Space is a large parking lot that can accommodate 586 small passenger cars and 23 large buses. The Hot Spring Health Club, covering an area of approximately 6,870 square meters, introduces the concept of original ecological gardening, combining Asian hot spring bathing culture with the currently popular European water surfing fitness and aquatic sports organically within a specific area, presenting a quiet and cozy comfortable space integrating the five senses of sight, hearing, smell, taste, and touch.

Over fifty soaking pools are distributed across various environmental regions at different heights, integrating water massage, counter-current surfing, pool wall massage, neck impact baths, and massage walkways, allowing you to enjoy different life interests and wonders in specific environments. It includes health therapies (health hydrotherapy baths: wooden bucket baths, bubble baths, whirlpool baths, waist and foot soaking baths, chest and back massage baths, bubble massage baths, water curtain impact baths, temperature difference baths, etc.; special therapeutic baths: ginseng baths, angelica baths, aloe vera baths, lingzhi baths, mint baths, rose baths, red wine baths, milk baths, flower petal baths, Dead Sea mineral mud baths, salt mist baths, etc.), yoga wellness, beauty slimming, nutritious restaurants, saunas, technician services, etc.
