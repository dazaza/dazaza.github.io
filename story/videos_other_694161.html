There are also clues indicating that sperm is being sold outside Zimbabwe. The police have received leads and reports from all over the country showing that similar incidents often occur along the roadsides, speculating that it is related to religious rituals.

On November 28th, these three women were interrogated in Harare, the capital of Zimbabwe. Since Zimbabwean law does not recognize the crime of rape committed by women against men, these three women were ultimately charged with 17 counts of serious indecency.
