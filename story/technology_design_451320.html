Speed up the pace of economic growth.

by paef0621 on 2011-06-24 13:44:47

In recent years, while accelerating economic growth, Hazi Township has vigorously strengthened the construction of grassroots party organizations. It has effectively played the leading core role of the township Party committee, the fortress role of grassroots Party branch committees, and the vanguard model role of Party members, thereby promoting the healthy development of the three cultural constructions in the whole township. The township economy has grown rapidly, social and political stability has been maintained, and the people live in peace and contentment. A good situation has formed throughout the township where everyone jointly seeks stability and strives for development. The township Party committee has been rated multiple times by the county committee as an "excellent grassroots party organization." In 2004 and 2005, it was awarded the "Second Prize for Economic and Social Development Work" by the county committee and government. In 2005, the township's Gross Domestic Product (GDP) reached 75 million yuan, an increase of 37% year-on-year; per capita rural net income was 2829 yuan, an increase of 12% year-on-year.

Strengthening Party work and fully playing "three roles"

In recent years, the township Party committee, starting from reality, has seriously explored new paths for Party work under the new situation, continuously strengthening the construction of the Party committee leadership team, grassroots Party branches, and the Party member contingent, fully playing the "three roles," thereby promoting Party work to continuously reach new levels and standards.

Firstly, strengthening the construction of the leadership team itself, fully playing the leading core role. One is through intensive study, improving the policy theoretical level of the leadership team members. Leadership team members can stand at the height of the "Three Represents," persistently arming their minds with Marxism-Leninism, Mao Zedong Thought, Deng Xiaoping Theory, adhering to a regular study system. During studies, they have systems, plans, organizations, and measures, linking theory with practice. Two is establishing systems to standardize the behavior of leadership team members. They have established systems such as the "Leadership Team Members Grasping Key Economic Projects System," "Regulations on Strict Control of Public Mobile Phones and Official Telephones," "Vehicle Usage System," and "Reception Accompaniment Dining System," striving to achieve enhancing unity, strengthening team synergy; diligent learning, improving work ability; pragmatic innovation, increasing team vitality; integrity and fairness, enhancing team influence.

Secondly, strengthening grassroots Party branch construction, fully playing the fortress role. While earnestly grasping the construction of the Party committee leadership team itself, the township Party committee has also strengthened grassroots Party branch construction, establishing branch leadership teams adapted to the construction of two cultures. They pay attention to replenishing the ranks of young and middle-aged Party members who are strong in Party spirit, have good conduct, possess scientific and cultural knowledge, and have authority among Party members and cadres, leading the masses to prosperity and building a moderately prosperous society. At the same time, active measures are taken to train Party members and cadres. Each year, Party branch cadre training classes are held, both studying Party work knowledge and current policies, and popularizing cultural knowledge and market economy knowledge, comprehensively improving the overall quality of the branch leadership team. They also adopt forms such as external visits for learning and exchanges of work experiences, helping Party members and cadres liberate their thinking, broaden their horizons, and improve their work ability. Continuously strengthening and improving Party member education, and taking this as the central link in the construction of rural grassroots Party organizations. In terms of educational content, besides emphasizing the education of the Party's basic line and fundamental knowledge, they also strengthen the training of rural practical techniques. Over the past two years, various technical training classes have been held eight times, with a total of 240 Party members participating in the training. Through these measures, the ideological and political awareness of Party members has been improved, horizons broadened, talents increased, making them the backbone of rural production techniques and experts in developing the market economy. Meanwhile, the township Party committee continues to establish and improve systems for village-level organizational construction, successively perfecting systems such as the Party branch work system, the Party committee member responsibility system for Party branch construction, Party member education management, and Party organization life, putting Party construction on a track of institutionalization and standardization, effectively enhancing Party members' sense of organization and improving the combat effectiveness of Party organizations.

Thirdly, strengthening Party member team construction, fully playing the vanguard model role. Combining with the actual conditions of Hazi Township, the township Party committee widely carried out activities for farmers' Party members to set posts and define responsibilities, clarifying responsibilities and giving tasks to non-official Party members, and strengthened the education and management of Party members who have taken up positions, establishing a training system. This allows Party members who have taken up positions to master relevant policies and regulations, methods for mass work, improving their ideological awareness and work ability. Establishing a regular reporting system for Party members who have taken up positions. Grassroots Party organizations regularly hold reporting meetings for Party members who have taken up positions. Party members are required to report difficult and hot issues encountered in their work to the Party branch, allowing the Party branch to promptly understand public sentiment and timely resolve any problems that arise. Currently, there are a total of Party members who have taken up positions in the entire township, solving hot and difficult issues concerning the masses.

Strengthening structural adjustments, accelerating the pace of economic development

The township Party committee has always regarded economic development as the focal point and ultimate goal of strengthening Party work, organically combining the two. Adhering to the strategy of focusing on livestock husbandry, large-scale development of forestry and fruit industry, optimizing crop planting, developing facility agriculture, and promoting industrialized operation of agriculture, they liberated their thinking, clarified their understanding, and pooled collective wisdom to implement these strategies. They made great efforts in adjusting structures, thereby promoting the comprehensive development of the township economy.

Focusing on grain, cotton, livestock, and orchard base construction, ensuring grain area remains above acres, and through vigorously promoting high-quality seeds and various yield-increasing new technologies, continuously improving grain yield and quality, while maintaining cotton area around acres. The development of forestry and fruit industry follows the standards of "high starting point, high standards, high investment, high output, high efficiency" and the requirement of constructing non-toxic fruit product bases, strictly implementing quality management measures for forestry and fruit industry, renovating old orchards, completing a task of 3000 mu of new economic forests mainly consisting of walnuts. In terms of animal husbandry, through methods such as purchasing from outside, self-breeding, etc., vigorously developing dairy cow and beef cattle breeding. There are currently 3781 heads (units) of yellow cattle, and 23868 heads of livestock slaughtered. Simultaneously, doing well in disease monitoring and prevention work to ensure no major animal diseases are transmitted or occur.

Strengthening service functions, promoting the overall work of the township Party committee to a higher level

In practical work, all cadres in the township have strived to enhance market economy concepts, public consciousness, service concepts, and the concept of "grasping both hands," transforming passive management into proactive services, truly changing leadership and working methods. The township Party committee has always regarded providing good services as a catalyst for resolving contradictions between cadres and the masses, as an effective entry point for closely connecting Party-mass relations, and as the foundation for guiding cadres in their careers. They combine technical services, material services with smooth-flow services, fully utilizing the function of the integrated agricultural service center to serve the public, creating conditions for farmers to increase income and become wealthy. Increasing the intensity of technological services. Currently, the number of science and technology demonstration villages in the entire township has reached , and the number of science and technology demonstration households is . This has increased the technological content of agricultural products, promoted agricultural production and farmers' income, and at the same time reduced farmers' risks in entering the market.

Strengthening the Scientific Outlook on Development, Building a Harmonious Countryside

While doing Party work and economic service work, the township Party committee has consistently focused on maintaining stability and spiritual civilization construction, ensuring that the masses can live in peace and contentment.

Continuously increasing the crackdown on illegal activities, making every effort to maintain social stability. Strictly implementing the stability maintenance responsibility system, ensuring no major cases harmful to social stability occur; establishing and improving the mass prevention and control network, correctly handling internal contradictions among the people; doing well in "creating safety" work, using the actual results of "creating safety" to promote social stability; focusing on the work of mobile population and key populations; the rate of obtaining "two certificates and one card" has reached 100%, the rate of legal and moral education training has reached 100%; doing well in the assistance and education of former prisoners and labor camp returnees, keeping the reoffending rate of released prisoners below 2%, strengthening various educations, creating a good publicity atmosphere. Making everyone value and protect social stability in thought and action. Carrying out comprehensive management work well, significantly enhancing the masses' awareness of knowing the law, abiding by the law, and using the law.

To meet the material and cultural needs of the masses, the township Party committee raised funds from multiple sources to build cultural and sports activity centers for 12,000 people in three villages. And using occasions such as the month of citizen morality education and ethnic unity education month, they invested 22,000 yuan in organizing artistic performances and substantive creation activities, achieving an education coverage rate of over 98% among cadres and the masses, reaching the goals of the activities. An agricultural and pastoral folk sports meeting was organized during the May Day period, greatly boosting the work enthusiasm of all cadres and Party members in the township. Fully utilizing the advantages of the "Ten Up" movement entering the villages, strengthening the construction of platforms. Enriching and livening up the cultural lives of cadres. Achieving coordinated development of spiritual civilization construction with economic development and Party work, making significant achievements in the ideological, moral, legal, cultural, and environmental pollution construction of cadres and the masses.

It is precisely through the united struggle of the township Party committee leadership team that the township's economy and various social undertakings have continuously achieved new successes, the living standards of cadres and the masses have continuously improved, and the work of the township Party committee has received full affirmation from all the people in the township.
