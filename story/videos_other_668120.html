The dust has settled on the regular season battles, and the playoff matchups have been revealed. On February 22, Fenjiu Basketball Team will face the challenge of Shanghai Sharks. Regarding this, head coach Yang Xuezeng said: "I am full of confidence in myself and the team, and I will strive to go further in the playoffs!"

Not only did he lead his team into the playoffs, but he also achieved the excellent result of third place in the regular season, making Coach Yang Xuezeng of Shanxi Men's Basketball Team especially delighted. Yesterday happened to be his 56th birthday, and such impressive achievements were the best birthday gift he received. Looking forward to the first opponent in the playoffs, the sixth-placed Shanghai Team in the regular season, Coach Yang was very confident: "The third place in the regular season is a good position, which way, we have the home-court advantage, and facing the Shanghai Team in the playoffs is also relatively ideal. The defense of the Shanghai Team is good, but their outside line isn't tall. As long as we play fast and flexible in our offense, it's quite possible to defeat the Shanghai Team."

The playoffs use a 1-2-1-1 home-and-away format, with Shanxi Team playing away first but having one more home game than the opponent.

Shanxi Team, who owns a "devilish home court," achieved an impressive record of 14 wins and 2 losses at home during the regular season, with a win rate ranking third in the entire league. Although their away win rate is low (www.ra9981.com), their record of 6 wins and 10 losses still ranks third in the entire league. The home win rate of the Shanghai Team is the same as Shanxi Team, but their away win rate is lower than Shanxi Team. Some fans predicted that as long as we can maintain this momentum at home, even if we lose all the away games, we can still eliminate the Shanghai Team with the advantage of three home games. Regarding the win rates at home and away, Coach Yang didn't think too much about it; "Through hard work, the team has reached today, and for every opportunity, we must seize it. We only believe in ourselves and our strength."
