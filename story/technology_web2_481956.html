Shenzhen Xiaomeisha Ocean World has an initial investment of 3.6 billion yuan, covering an area of about 200,000 square meters. It is located on the eastern golden coastline of Shenzhen, in the Xiaomeisha seaside which is known as the "Oriental Hawaii". The Ocean World features "Ten Pavilions and Three Parks" (namely: Beluga Pavilion, Polar Bear Pavilion, Arctic Fox Pavilion, Penguin Pavilion, Aquarium, Fantasy Ocean Pavilion, Whale Shark Pavilion, Shark Pavilion, Seashell Pavilion, Science Education Pavilion, Model Ship Pavilion, Ocean Fun Park, Water Fun Park, Poseidon Garden), "Fourteen Special Shows" (namely: Extreme High-altitude Diving Show, Clown Humorous Diving Show from Foreign Countries, Adorable Seal Naive Show, Funny Sea Lion Humorous Show, Clever Dolphin Stunt Show, Passionate Ukrainian Underwater Ballet Show, Thrilling Human and Shark Dance Show, Amazing Bull Shark Hunting Show, Magical Ukrainian Mermaid Show, Romantic Underwater Dream Wedding Show, Underwater Ten Thousand Fishes Feeding Show, Leisure Deep-sea Diving Show, Wonderful Ukrainian Water Ballet Show, Frog Fairy Animation Show) as the main display. There are two unique selling points: one is the ocean park in southern China that displays the most polar animals, and the other is nearly a 2-hour, the largest scale "Sea-Land-Air" stunt variety show in the Asia-Pacific region.

Polar Pavilion: Currently investing nearly 100 million yuan to build the first large-scale "Polar Animal World" in southern China. The belugas, polar bears, arctic foxes, and penguins have already been ready to welcome guests. The three belugas, the princes of the icy sea, are multi-talented, able to sing, dance, spray water, and turn circles. The mighty king of the polar regions, the polar bear, lives in a refrigerated polar bear pavilion with a swimming pool. Six "Ice Mountain Snow Fox Female Arctic Foxes", backed by ice mountains and stepping on ice blocks, have snow-white fur and alluring eyes. Five penguins leisurely comb their feathers, showing the elegance of polar gentlemen to tourists.

Aquarium: There are 20 fish display tanks with different exhibition themes, raising more than 600 kinds of rare fish, totaling over ten thousand tails. The underwater theater raises various marine fish, and there are daily performances such as "Passionate Underwater Ballet", "Magical Mermaid", "Romantic Underwater Wedding", "Deep-sea Diving", etc. The underwater tunnel is magnificent, filled with numerous fish, making people feel as if they were walking under the sea. The spectacular "Ten Thousand Fishes Feeding Performance" takes place here every day. The "Seven-color Tank" and "Treasure Tank" raise some strange and interesting marine fish.

Ocean Theater: With nearly 3000 seats, it currently houses six dolphins, eight sea lions, and six seals. Every day, there are three rounds of nearly one-hour-long grand performances in the ocean theater. Brave Russian divers bring extreme high-altitude diving stunts, Ukrainian beauties perform passionate water ballets, international clowns present hilarious splashing shows, and the star marine animals, dolphins, sea lions, and seals give wonderful performances. Each performance receives nearly a hundred cheers and applause from the audience!

Shark Pavilion: This is currently the largest shark pavilion in the country, raising over thirty sharks including the amazing bull shark, fierce lemon shark, blacktip shark, whitetip shark, agile leopard shark, and the bulky nurse shark. In the shark pavilion, there are daily thrilling "Human-Shark Dance Performances."

Fantasy Ocean Pavilion: This is currently the largest indoor track-style exhibition hall in the country. Through thirteen themed ocean stories (such as The Fisherman and the Golden Fish, Robinson Crusoe, Nezha Creates Havoc in the Sea, etc.), it simulates and establishes a condensed timeline reflecting the history of human interaction with the ocean.

Whale Shark Pavilion: A specially built huge pavilion for a mysterious guest from the ocean - the whale shark. This shark is 8.34 meters long, weighs 3 tons, and is currently the largest whale shark discovered domestically. Here, there are also nearly a hundred rare fish specimens.

Seashell Pavilion: There are five exhibition rooms, displaying more than 600 kinds of rare marine shells, corals, ancient biological fossils, and precious dinosaur egg fossils.

Science Education Pavilion: Through film small theaters, models, and boards, it showcases the vastness of marine culture. Divided into themes such as "The Ocean - The Cradle of Life," "The Oldest Marine Creatures," "The Mysteries of the Ocean," "The Ocean and Humanity," etc.

Model Ship Pavilion: This is currently the largest, most diverse, most numerous, and thematically richest model ship pavilion in the country, and also the only large-scale model ship exhibition hall in Shenzhen. It is mainly divided into five themes: "Ancient Navigation Tools," "Sailing Age," "World Famous Ships," "Maritime Chronicles," and "Future Age." There are hundreds of exquisite pictures and ship models displayed, ranging from the most primitive dugout canoes and grass rafts to modern high-tech aircraft carriers and nuclear submarines.

Ocean Fun Park: Features thrilling rides such as "Hurricane Boat," "Surfing Boat," "Diving Chamber," "Dolphin Slide," and some children's entertainment projects, allowing you to fully experience the powerful force of the ocean and demonstrate your courage to battle the waves.

Xiaomeisha Ocean World is less than a hundred meters away from the Xiaomeisha beach, just across the road from Xiaomeisha Grand Hotel. With the opening of the second channel of Shenyan (Luoshan Interchange to Dameisha Expressway, free), and the convenient transportation network formed by Yanpai Expressway and Yanba Expressway, it is now more convenient to reach Dameisha and Xiaomeisha. The Dameisha and Xiaomeisha beaches + Shenzhen Ocean World will be jointly developed into a unique marine-themed refreshing holiday water world.
