Fiction: Female bus conductor stabs male passenger with a fruit knife

by sznstejx3q3 on 2012-02-28 10:03:18

● A female ticket collector of Route 8 bus, which belongs to Shilong Bus Company, stabbed a male passenger in the abdomen with a fruit knife.

● Surveillance video shows that after boarding the bus, the male passenger threw away his ticket casually, leading to an argument; the male passenger "quarreled" all the way.

● The female ticket collector claimed that the passenger called her a "little slut" and made an insulting gesture after getting off the bus, so she could not bear the humiliation and stabbed him once.

Yesterday morning, Liu Jun (a pseudonym), 46 years old, underwent his second surgery at Shilong Boai Hospital. On February 12, Liu Jun's left abdomen was stabbed by a female ticket collector on Route 8 bus, which belongs to Shilong Bus Company. For the past half month since that day, Liu Jun has been receiving treatment.

The conflict between Liu Jun and the female ticket collector started when Liu Jun casually threw away his ticket.

The reporter viewed the surveillance video inside the bus at the operation department of Shilong Bus Company. The surveillance showed that Liu Jun had been "arguing" for nearly 20 minutes from the time he boarded until he got off the bus. After getting off, he made a gesture with his finger that the female ticket collector considered as an insulting act.

Miss Xu, the involved female ticket collector, told the reporter that being cursed all the way, the action of Liu Jun after getting off ignited the situation, causing her to lose control. "I'm divorced, yet he still cursed me as a 'little slut,' cursing me all the way..."

Mr. Li, the head of the operation department of Shilong Bus Company, stated, "It is definitely wrong for the female ticket collector to stab someone with a knife, but they also need respect."

Currently, the biggest contradiction between both parties lies in the compensation issue.

By reporter Wang Wanli

**Injured Party: Bad attitude of the ticket collector made him angry**

At around 4 p.m. on February 12, Liu Jun boarded the No. 8 bus with the license plate number "Yue S73029" at Shilong Railway Station. His destination was Yuwu in Dongcheng. He held vegetables in his hand and leaned against the railing near the door playing with his phone... Lying in bed at Shilong Boai Hospital yesterday, Liu Jun vividly recalled everything that happened.

Liu Jun told the reporter that he just underwent his second surgery this morning. "Because the ultrasound found adhesions after the previous surgery, this surgery was to separate the adhesions." Liu Jun said that the stab from the female ticket collector luckily did not injure any internal organs. "At that time, after I got on the bus, I paid and got the ticket but didn't pay attention and threw it away casually." Liu Jun recalled, "After the female ticket collector noticed, she asked me to pick up the ticket, and that's how the conflict began."

He believed that the "insulting words used by the female ticket collector first" were the reason that made him angry, "She said if you have money, don't take the bus, drive your own car." Liu Jun said, "Why can't I take the bus? With such bad service attitude, what's wrong with me saying a few words?"

**Progress: Injured party demands 100,000 yuan in compensation, too large a gap to reach an agreement**

However, half a month has passed since the incident, and the matter remains unresolved. Mr. Li from the operation department of Shilong Bus Company said, "Mainly because the two sides cannot agree on the compensation issue." Liu Jun said that after the first surgery, he initially demanded 80,000 yuan in compensation, "Now that I've had a second surgery, 80,000 yuan is definitely not enough. At least 100,000 yuan in compensation is required."

Mr. Li pointed at the ticket collector, Miss Xu, and asked the reporter, "Do you think she is just a working woman, how much can she compensate? She can only pay 2,000 yuan." Mr. Li said, "This conflict is entirely a personal one. The company has already covered the medical expenses and cannot let the company bear this compensation."

Miss Xu spoke in a low tone, telling the reporter that she is 30 years old and due to emotional issues, she got divorced. She is most sensitive about others cursing or insulting her, "Yet he kept calling me a 'little slut'." She couldn't help feeling sad.

Mr. Li told the reporter that ticket collectors like Miss Xu work 20 days a month, from 6 a.m. to 10 p.m., working 16 hours a day, with a monthly salary of just over 2,000 yuan. They are very hardworking. "This incident is definitely wrong on the part of the ticket collector, but passengers should also empathize with them and respect them," Mr. Li said.

What exactly happened on Route 8?

**Surveillance: Male passenger throwing the ticket on the bus triggered an argument**

In the operation department office on the 5th floor of Shilong Bus Company, Mr. Li, the person in charge, retrieved the surveillance footage from the front and rear doors of the involved bus. He said that as the matter developed to this point, he hoped everyone could see the truth.

In the surveillance footage taken at the rear door, the reporter saw that at "17:59:26," Liu Jun boarded the No. 8 bus. Mr. Li said that because the surveillance system's time was not adjusted properly, the recorded time does not reflect the exact time of the event.

In the surveillance footage, the reporter saw that after Liu Jun received the ticket, he casually threw it away. The female ticket collector returned and said a few words, and Liu Jun continued to "argue." The surveillance could only capture people's appearances, not their voices. Liu Jun occasionally pointed his finger at the female ticket collector. Until he got off the bus, during the nearly 20 minutes he was on the bus, he kept "arguing." His actions drew the attention of other passengers who turned to look at him. During this time, Liu Jun and the female ticket collector only engaged in verbal arguments or pointing gestures, without any physical conflict.

**Conflict: Actions after getting off further angered the ticket collector**

"If he had gotten off the bus and I ignored him, then none of this would have happened." Facing the reporter, Miss Xu, the involved ticket collector, deeply regretted her actions.

After the incident, Miss Xu was detained for a day by the Dongcheng Sangyuan police station. The next day, Shilong Bus Company bailed her out. For the past half month, the bus company has not arranged for her to work, "fearing retaliation."

Staff played the surveillance footage from the front door of the involved bus. When Liu Jun got off the bus, Miss Xu was leaning against the front door railing looking at her phone angrily and glanced at him as he got off.

The surveillance showed that after Liu Jun got off and walked to the side of the road, he raised his left hand and made a gesture towards the departing bus, which appeared blurry in the recording. However, Miss Xu said, "After he got off, he continued to curse loudly and made that insulting gesture." This completely enraged her.

The surveillance showed that the bus stopped, and Miss Xu took a fruit knife with a blade about 5 cm long from her bag with her left hand and rushed off the bus. Upon noticing the bus had stopped, Liu Jun also rushed over. When the two met, Miss Xu stabbed him. In the surveillance footage, Liu Jun lifted his shirt, revealing blood on his left abdomen.

Upon seeing the blood on Liu Jun's abdomen, Miss Xu stood motionless at the bus door. Recalling this moment in the video, Miss Xu said that at the time, she never expected things to escalate this far, and only felt that "everything was over." Police officers arrived quickly, and the injured party was sent to the hospital. Based on the conclusion of "minor injury," Sangyuan police station detained Miss Xu.
