Wear the clothes you've worn.

by lwf225 on 2009-08-31 18:09:46

3:30 in the morning.

No doubt,

Another failed night.

When several roommates woke up at the same time,

I laughed cunningly.

When everything was quiet, I couldn't help but sigh.

Without a doubt,

I envy those who are born to sleep well,

Even though there are few people like me.

The sound of voices rises and falls, impacting me,

While I can only stay in the darkness,

I start to feel不甘.

The pills in my hand keep making trouble,

Until they soak my palm,

Wanting to go crazy but completely silent.

A friend dropped his phone,

And I scolded him harshly. He's not a little person,

Making low-level mistakes again and again.

Ignoring my own lost phone, bank card, and cash.

I can't remember what else I've lost, thinking until my head aches,

In the end, I had no choice but to give up.

When it's not safe enough, I habitually think of him.

He cares about my pain.

He sleeps well.

In the reality of being thousands of kilometers apart,

His good sleep cannot affect me. What does it matter?

I still coquettishly say, I can only fall asleep when you're here.

He replies with a calm smile.

But I am certain that he already understands. Moreover, he knows it all too well.

Dear, I just want to be with you.

More than any romantic scenery.

Finally unable to bear it, missing you too much.

I tightly hugged the clothes you left behind.

You said that was an important piece of clothing for you.

I naturally understand. When you decided to leave it with me,

I confirmed that I am someone very important to you.

I wrapped myself tightly in the clothes, as if feeling the warmth of your embrace.

Dear, you have once again accompanied me through a mundane hardship.

Dreaming while awake, your clear face tells me your return date,

I boast shamelessly, eventually cuddling for seven days and seven nights without stopping.

Every day, sending out resumes, spacing out, laughing, time passes quickly.

Not pleased by external gains, not saddened by personal losses. Only you.

As I move forward single-mindedly, you are the only certainty amidst my hesitations, and the rest of the path becomes natural.

Feeling your hard work, but you don't complain.

When you sleep peacefully, I no longer insistently demand you answer the phone as I used to,

Learning to smile and be considerate. Not for anything else, because I am your wife.

Dear, I love you. You'll be working hard for a few more months,

But from then on, I will walk the road with you.

In the midst of flying thoughts of longing, I hug myself again,

My cold body, long hair falling over my ears.

I'm not sick, it's just that longing makes me do as I please.

Wearing the clothes you've worn. Thinking of the words you've spoken.

Scattered phrases I can't remember. But two words linger in my heart,

Chen Wei.

Yet, they echo endlessly.
