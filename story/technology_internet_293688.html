Beijing Lianhuashan Ski Resort

by 7huaxue82 on 2009-12-05 08:45:13

Beijing Lianhuashan Ski Resort is only 30 kilometers from the Capital Airport. The majestic and simple wooden imitation sculpture gate of the ski resort is only 600 meters away from the Liangshan bus station on Shunping Highway (Shunyi-Pinggu), and the transportation is extremely smooth and convenient. Beijing Lianhuashan Ski Resort also has a super-large parking lot of 60,000 square meters and a ski equipment rental hall of 12,000 square meters. Accompanying facilities such as green landscape halls and uniquely shaped Russian villas can provide visitors with super-value accommodation guarantee and quality service.

1. This price includes the entrance ticket, insurance, ski equipment, cable car, and tow rope fees.

2. Please prepare a ski equipment deposit of 300 RMB, which will be returned by the ski resort cashier window if there is no damage to the equipment.

3. Please prepare lockers: 10 RMB each (it is suggested to share one locker between two people).

4. Please prepare skiing suits: 30 RMB per set, 20 RMB per piece (voluntary rental).

5. The ski resort offers self-service meals at 38 RMB per person.

Lianhuashan Driving Route:

Self-driving Route:

1. Take Jing-Shun Road, turn right at Kuliushu Roundabout, then go straight along Shunping Highway for about 30 kilometers (no tolls).

2. Take Airport Expressway, turn right north side of Terminal 1 building, go straight along Shunping Road for about 30 kilometers (one-way toll 10 RMB).

3. Take Jing-Tong Expressway to North Guan Roundabout, turn right at Yanjing Bridge, then go straight along Shunping Road for about 20 kilometers (one-way toll 10 RMB).

4. Take Jing-Cheng Expressway to connect with the Sixth Ring Road, exit at Shunyi Nanfaxin, then go straight along Shunping Road for about 25 kilometers (one-way toll 15 RMB).

Bus Route: Take Bus 918 from Dongzhimen Bus Station to Liangshan Station in Zhangzhen, Shunyi, then walk 600 meters to reach Lianhuashan Ski Resort.
