Another social music sharing community in China! Yeah, the domain name is pretty good. Other aspects need to be experienced.

Main functions: Music Hut: the ideal music world = discovering great music + inviting friends to listen here. Personalized homepage: tidy up your own place and let others know how unique you are. Music recommendation: facing you who loves music, what we are most willing to do is to keep sending you great music.

[Official "About Us"]

1. Our domain name:

What is "Wanwan Music"?

Wanwan Music - plaY Your music with me

Use a playful (play) mood to play (play) music, meet friends with similar tastes.

You can play cool, super magical, very high-spirited, free and easy; play sincerely, play tastefully, play sociably, or even play to gain fans.

Coolplay, Magicplay, Highplay, Freeplay... These words and ways are all prepared for you, it's up to you to choose how to experience them next.

2. Our slogan:

What does "Love new things, try Wanwan" mean?

Are you still listening to the TOP10 of dozens of charts? Where is your individuality?

Are you still looping songs from your hard drive? Where is your sense of freshness?

Are you still trusting recommendations from editors you don't know? Where is your soulmate in music?

You should know that what you are accustomed to having now is just the tip of the iceberg.

Starting from this moment, set off from here, take care of your ears, overturn your experience, refresh your listening history.

3. Our user experience and interface design:

Easy to use, fun, visual, childlike

This website is made for you, please don't miss it.

We are still exploring and improving, and we also invite you to make this website understand you better.

4. Our threshold:

Why must you register and log in?

This is not to keep you out, but to remember you more deeply, get to know you.

We can call your name at any time, familiarize ourselves with what time you usually listen to music, understand your music taste, know who your friends are.

We can also grasp your intentions, find more songs you may want to encounter for you, and pick out friends most like you from the vast sea of the Internet.

5. Our music library:

Why is the number of songs limited? Why choose legitimate versions?

We respect the industry, respect intellectual property rights, respect the source, creators, and the industrial chain, respect our partners.

We also deeply understand that only when the industry enters a virtuous cycle can we truly benefit, and we can truly solve one of the difficulties in China's digital music situation.

Therefore, we have chosen legitimate versions, and we only open the content we have signed agreements on.

This indeed limits us in quantity, but it does not hinder us from recommending music customized for your personality and preferences.

6. Our competitors and partners:

Who are the competitors? Who are the partners?

We respect our competitors as much as we respect our partners. We have a humble learning spirit, while holding onto the concept of winning persistently.

We hope to work shoulder to shoulder with teams, opponents, and partners who are equally full of ideals,

to watch the sunrise together on the mountain top, healthy competition can benefit both the industry and users.
