How to determine the quality of wallpaper installation [http://www.eilisha.com/], five steps to confirm the quality of wallpaper installation:

1. Before construction, the equipment or accessories protruding from the base surface should be removed, the nail caps should enter the base surface, and rust-proof paint should be applied. Nail holes should be filled with oil putty. The putty applied to the base should be solid and firm, not powdery, wrinkled, or cracked.

2. The base surface should be flat and dry, without gaps. For invisible convex or concave walls to the naked eye, touching by hand without large concavities or convexities is considered qualified.

3. Stick firmly, with consistent surface color. There should be no bubbles, hollow drums, sand particles, cracks, curled edges, wrinkles, spots, or adhesive marks. There should be no missed sticking, additional sticking, or layer shedding defects.

4. The patterns and patterns at the seams should match, the overlap should be light, and when viewed from 1.5 meters away from the wall, the seam should not be visible.

5. The wallpaper [http://www.eilisha.com/] should be tightly connected to the decorative line, baseboard, and wainscoting, without gaps, and the corners should be vertical.
