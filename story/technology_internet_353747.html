Original Poster Large Medium Small Posted on March 6, 2008 Only watch this author Journey: Magic Item Guide First enter the new world and complete the main line NPC task, then start a new Journey. The official website of Journey will give you 4 bound magic item fragments for free. You can use 4 magic item fragments to craft a magic item with NPC He Xiangu. Magic items are divided into four kinds: Golden Gang Bracelet, Kunlun Mirror, Hun Tian Ling, and Ding Hai Shen Needle. These are just formal differences. Choose one you like to craft a magic item. One. The quality of a normal magic item is determined by four coefficients: magic item attribute value, enlightenment value, magic item level, and magic item qualification. Let's first discuss the first two: attribute value and enlightenment value. Among them, the attribute value is divided into strength, agility, intelligence, spirit, and physique. These attributes bring you different bonuses compared to the old version. Strength only adds physical attack power, agility only adds physical defense power, intelligence only adds magical attack power, spirit only adds magical defense power, and physique only adds blood volume. Similarly, the enlightenment value is also divided into the above five types, which are the same type of bonus. Now observe the magic item you have crafted. It has all ten attributes, but you will find that there are some words behind one attribute value and enlightenment value: Advantage Attribute. This means that the magic item only has one main cultivation attribute. There are two advantage attributes here: attribute value and enlightenment value. The key problem appears. Since the magic item is randomly created after being crafted, it is possible that the advantage attribute is added behind any attribute value and enlightenment value. There are a total of 5*5=25 possibilities. After equipping your magic item, let's say the first situation where the advantage attribute is added behind the same attribute value and enlightenment value, namely dual attributes. Congratulations, you have succeeded in a small step. Because Journey is designed this way, the attribute value and enlightenment value complement each other. When both are strong, they form a strong alliance, and the bonus the magic item gives you reaches the optimal state. Why is it called a small success? Because if you are a wizard and you get a double-attribute strength magic item, it is also an inferior magic item. So a successful magic item is a double-attribute magic item corresponding to your profession. Of course, the choice of magic items is related to yourself. Personally, I think you should not use magic items that increase defense, because no matter how good the defense magic item is, such as a double-spirit magic item at level 160 adding more than 20,000 points of magical defense for you. Due to having a 100% damage reduction chain, this kind of bonus is like a chicken rib. In the second case, what to do when the two bonus attribute values and enlightenment values are different. Because the five attributes the magic item gives you early on are not much different, some people think it's acceptable, not bad. They upgrade the magic item level. This is a big taboo. The advantages of dual-attribute magic items become more evident as the magic item level increases. Don't think you can cultivate two magic items simultaneously. Without the strong stacking of attributes and enlightenment, the magic item can only play a tiny part. It will become more obvious later on. If so, someone would ask what to do. Method one, reforge. Need to buy 4 magic item fragments, each magic item fragment costs 5L gold coins. Salary cannot buy. The magic item forged is not bound, and the dual-attribute ones can be sold to other players (a business opportunity arises). Method two, change, sf. Every day riding the Taoyuan bicycle, the system will give you a Washing Jinsha. After using it, it's equivalent to reforging a magic item. The biggest disadvantage is that after using it, the magic item becomes level 1, the learned magic item skills are cleared, especially the innate skills are gone, which is very regrettable. If you have cultivated the magic item, you have wasted your efforts. Moreover, you can only change once a day. However, Journey also sells props: Purple Jinsha and Platinum Jinsha. Each costs 50L gold coins, used for washing attribute values and enlightenment values respectively. After washing, the attribute values and enlightenment values are randomly generated. There is a one-fifth chance to generate what you want. Does not change other aspects of the magic item. Suitable for players with lots of money. There is another situation where these two props need to be used, which will be explained later. What exactly are the specific numbers of the magic item attribute values and enlightenment values considered good? I've already said that a newly forged magic item has all 10 numerical values. Including two advantage attributes, one attribute value and one enlightenment value. Summarizing various types of magic items I've seen over the past few days, the total of the five attribute values (strength, agility, intelligence, spirit, physique) is 200 points, randomly divided by the system into five parts. One of them is significantly greater than the other four, this one is the so-called advantage attribute. The smallest value among the four disadvantaged items is 20 points (the value I have seen, if there is a smaller one, it won't differ much), the values of the four disadvantaged items generally don't differ much, and it's highly likely they're the same. If the sum of the four items is 80 points, then the specific value of the remaining advantage attribute is 120, which is hard to achieve. Personally, I think 110 or above is an excellent item, 105 or above is acceptable, 100 is the dividing line, and below that is considered substandard. The total of the five enlightenment values (strength, agility, sf, intelligence, spirit, physique) is 400 points, the system also randomly divides it into five parts. The smallest value of the four disadvantaged items is 38 points, then the maximum value of the remaining advantage item is 248 points. Anything above 235 is considered excellent, anything below 200 points is considered substandard. If both items are excellent, then you can open the chest containing the purple crystal key in the box. If one item is excellent and the other is not, and you pursue perfection and have plenty of money, then buy Purple Jinsha or Platinum Jinsha to wash it. Two. Magic Item Cultivation Remember, in order to waste as little effort and money as possible, please modify your magic item before cultivating it. There are four ways to cultivate a magic item. (One) You can team up (one male and one female) and go to the "Evening Glow Cliff" or "Cloud Sky Pavilion" in the new world to invite your teammates to cultivate magic items together. Complete tasks at the alchemy furnace within the map, each obtaining magic item experience. Initially, you gain 400 experience every 5 minutes, and later the magic item experience gained depends on the affection value between the two people. There is no daily limit to the number of times you can perform this task. It's recommended to find a fixed friend or marry. (Two) Accept tasks from Hua Yingxiong, kill 5000 monsters to gain 400 magic item experience. Not recommended. (Three) Exchange with personal charm value with NPCs. Significant effect, but few people can achieve this now. (Four) Buy props with gold to exchange for experience. Upgrading a magic item is similar to leveling up your character, the higher the level, the harder it is to upgrade. Moreover, Journey limits the maximum amount of experience you can gain daily. Initially, it's 5000, after level 15 of the magic item, it's 8000. Using 50L unbound silver coins can increase it by 10%. Using gold coins to buy and use the prop Ji Zi Bag can increase it by 80%. Besides giving you attribute bonuses, another highlight of magic items is their skills, including innate skills and cultivated skills. There isn't much to say about cultivated skills, each book costs 10L gold coins, as long as you reach the required level, you can learn whatever you want. Innate skills currently rely on luck, especially the useful ones. My current magic item is level 20, I've learned two skills, Spell Burning and Revival. After the magic item reaches level 30, each level-up will add attribute bonuses. These additions are random. If you want to add to the attributes you need, you'll need to use Chenbi Stone, which can be obtained from the Taoyuan collection, so I hope you can complete this task every day (Chenbi Stones can stack). Guarding the border missions can obtain either magic item skill books or magic item props, mostly books. It's also recommended to complete these missions. That's all for the initial magic item guide, more will be added later!
