Capsule apartment, it's not easy to say I love you

by 2taoting6 on 2010-04-13 09:51:29

Do you still remember a line of dialogue in the TV series "Snail House": What did people rush to grab in 2010? We grabbed houses, they grabbed land! A small piece of land could attract competition from many businesses, and one building after another was coveted by the general public. However, not long ago, a 78-year-old man named Huang Rixin built a "capsule apartment" and said that the design of the "capsule apartment" was not for profit, but to provide new university graduates with a transition period in terms of housing. In terms of design creativity, the "capsule apartment" is indeed very "fresh", and under the impetus of this "freshness", it has welcomed many visitors. However, from the debut of the "capsule apartment" to now, it has only welcomed one tenant! What is the reason for this? People with a spirit like Ah Q might say, perhaps the publicity is not enough. However, when the "capsule apartment" debuted, it caused a great stir. There were also news reports about the "capsule apartment" on relevant websites and even on the homepage as a top story, and related newspapers also made reports. Is such a level of publicity really not enough?

So what factors are causing the "capsule apartment" to be ignored? This still needs to compare the "capsule apartment" with "normal" houses. Our general living requirements are very simple, but we also need more than just a place to stay. Necessary conditions in a house such as water, bathrooms, and soundproofing are all lacking in the "capsule apartment". And when nature calls, does living in the "capsule apartment" mean that tenants have to fight for the bathroom? The first tenant of the "capsule apartment", Zhang Qi, said that living in the "capsule apartment" is very uncomfortable, the soundproofing is very poor, the temperature is low at night, and it's very drafty. Living in the capsule apartment is very inconvenient. And the "capsule apartment" is not suitable for just anyone to live in; it also has certain restrictions. Height cannot exceed 1 meter 8, and weight cannot exceed 180 pounds. Perhaps we can't even stretch our lazy waist in the "capsule apartment"!

The evaluations of the "capsule apartment" on the Internet are not very high, and most netizens have expressed unwillingness to live in such a house. Indeed, in an economic society where housing prices are rising, we have been seeking "snail living", but we won't eat anything that comes our way. Necessary conditions and facilities should still be present. Under such cold weather conditions, the "capsule apartment" does not have heating. Then, in summer, can the "capsule apartment" accommodate an air conditioner or a fan in such a confined space? Why do we work so hard to earn money? To enjoy life better, not to make ourselves suffer more. Buying a house with money buys not just a place to stay, but also a cozy place that can keep us warm in winter and cool in summer.

Perhaps, the "capsule apartment" is not yet mature, and improvements need to be made in some areas. But currently speaking, the market for the "capsule apartment" is not very large. Perhaps, housing prices will continue to rise, and we can't afford those expensive houses, but we can look forward to a huge market for "capsule apartments" in the near future. And this, requires a business opportunity.
