The legendary Pattaya:

During the Vietnam War, Pattaya was an important base for the U.S. military. For soldiers who were about to head to the battlefield with uncertain life or death, perhaps indulging in excess and pleasure was the most reasonable choice. The huge demand for sexual services stimulated the development of Pattaya's adult entertainment market, which continues to this day. Thus, in Thailand, a land of devout Buddhist faith in Theravada Buddhism, Pattaya became an exception.

Pattaya during the day is a coastal city with blue seas, silver sands, and sunshine, but it starts to boil over at night. The streets are filled with neon lights and glitz, the stages showcasing bizarre yet fascinating shadows of people, and the truth revealed through transformations all immersed in cigarettes, alcohol, and neon lights - leading to a life of drunken debauchery.

Recommended must-try activities for your trip to Pattaya, Thailand:

The indispensable ladyboy show

I remember reading a travelogue about Thailand in middle school where it mentioned the history of ladyboys, which seems to originate from Thailand's Buddhist belief that Buddha is genderless, neither male nor female. So boys dressed as girls as a form of offering to show respect for Buddha. The street-side open-air bars are another stage in Pattaya, where women wearing seductive and revealing clothing sit around the bar counter, and it’s not uncommon to see middle-aged Western men taking girls' hands and leaving in a car. It feels like stepping back into the Vietnam War era, with the air thick with desire, killing any semblance of calm and rationality, leaving only raw indulgence.

When Pattaya reaches midnight, the streets are still brilliantly lit, pulsating with energetic music, and the allure of hostesses swaying their hips. Tourists are everywhere, flowing continuously. Pattaya's second route open-air bar street can only be described with one word: High. Since the bars are outdoors, there are fewer restrictions, leading to more uninhibited behavior. If you're feeling high enough, you can dance up onto the bar, and no action is deemed excessive if you're sufficiently high. Whistles and screams mixed with alcohol fuel the passion of Pattaya.

Adult shows in Pattaya are found throughout the streets: Qi Gong performances, Mong Chacha shows, erotic dance shows, Golden Cat shows, mysterious shows, Paris GG shows; Pattaya also features the famous Oriental Princess ladyboy performance. Watching ladyboys is a must-do program for every tourist visiting Thailand. Professional ladyboys always maintain their charming smiles. It's said that the Oriental Princess gathers the prettiest ladyboys in Thailand, each exuding allure with their figures and appearances beyond reproach, even their skin smooth and delicate, and they possess remarkable talents with energetic dances. The Oriental Princess costs 800 baht, lasts an hour, and includes drinks, beer, and snacks. After getting high, you can find some slightly more reserved beachside bars along the seaside avenue to relax your feet. Besides the nightly revelry, exquisite dinners and shopping could also be excellent choices.
