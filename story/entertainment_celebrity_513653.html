Chen Yibing responded to Zhang Shangwu on Weibo, saying that face and self-respect are not given by others.

Related reports: Zhang Shangwu posted a blessing on Weibo for netizens, stating that he has devoted himself to charity and donated 6000 yuan to sponsor poor college students.

I am genuinely donating money to help those in need. What has he, Chen Yibing, donated? Why should he be so high and mighty just because he is an Olympic champion, while we are trampled underfoot? Due to the somewhat aggressive complaints of Zhang Shangwu, originally unrelated to the disputes between Zhang Shangwu and Chen Guangbiao, the "Prince of Hoisting Rings" Chen Yibing was also dragged into this war of words. In response, Chen Yibing did not choose silence; yesterday afternoon, Chen Yibing posted his advice to Zhang Shangwu on Weibo: Face and self-respect are not given by others but must be strived for through one's own efforts! It is not scary if others look down on you, but it is terrifying if you look down on yourself! If you want others to respect you, you must make more efforts! Society will never abandon hardworking people! Opportunities are reserved for those who are prepared! As a former teammate, I hope he can do better in the future!

Deep-seated resentment between the two

Zhang Shangwu re-entered the public eye due to a resignation scandal, which was originally only related to him and Chen Guangbiao, and had nothing to do with Chen Yibing (see page A38 of the Times on October 21). However, during an interview, this "champion performer" once again expressed dissatisfaction with Chen Yibing: Why should he, as an Olympic champion, be so high and mighty while we have to be trampled underfoot? When he, Chen Yibing, eats the cake, we don't even get to lick the crumbs?

The reason for the untimely criticism of Chen Yibing might be related to the latter's success at the recently concluded Tokyo World Championships, but more resentment stemmed from when the Zhang Shangwu incident was widely discussed: After Zhang Shangwu signed with Chen Guangbiao's company, Chen Yibing once targeted the former with 10 consecutive Weibo posts, accusing him of deceiving the public's sympathy with lies, slandering the gymnastics team, and being ungrateful.

Chen Yibing’s Weibo Counterattack

Although Zhang Shangwu did not formally respond to Chen Yibing's accusations at the time, the rift between the two was established then. Therefore, when Zhang Shangwu returned to his previous state of poverty, he could not help but bring up the old grievances again.

Chen Yibing did not avoid the issue either; instead, he chose to give Zhang Shangwu some advice via Weibo: I just want to tell him (Zhang Shangwu) a few sentences, hoping he can take them to heart: Face and self-respect are not given by others but must be earned through one's own efforts! It is not scary if others look down on you, but it is terrifying if you look down on yourself! If you want others to respect you, you must put in more effort! Society will never discard hardworking people! Opportunities are reserved for those who are prepared! As a former teammate, I hope he can do better in the future!
