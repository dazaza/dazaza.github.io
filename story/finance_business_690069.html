Classic lines about love in the sci-fi movie "I Do".

by hoouk686 on 2012-03-04 22:24:36

Watch the movie "I Will I DO", Learn about the Economics of Love

The Price of Dreams -

Does love have a price? Would its price change?

"My dream is still there, it's just become more expensive. If pork can rise in price, why should dreams be cheaper than pork?"

Overdraft Type of Love -

If the efforts put in by both parties in a relationship are unequal, how long can such a love last? What would a woman think about a man who lacks credibility?

"I don't need money, I just need you to restore your credit. There's nothing in this world that can be overdrafted infinitely, especially not love. You've maxed out my trust in you!"

Men and Money -

Many men believe that modern women only care about money. But do all women think this way?

"He has no money, but I can earn it! Why do I need so much money? What I want is a man!"

Men and Business -

For women who treat finding a rich husband as a career, the presence of another woman in the man's life is not an obstacle. How will they turn their disadvantage around?

"Finding a man is like doing business. If your customer was ruined by someone else, would you stop trying to connect with them? We compete on service, sincerity, patience, and strength!"

A Woman's Worth -

A woman's worth changes with age. Do you know the ultimate voice of remaining single women?

"When I was young, I always worried about being taken advantage of or deceived by men. Now at this age, I'm most afraid that I have nothing left to offer to men..."

High-Quality Stock Women -

It's often said, in this world, men must succeed, but women shouldn't be too strong. But does this really make sense?

"A man will never complain that a woman is too excellent, just as a woman will never complain that a piece of clothing is too beautiful. A woman gives up a piece of clothing for two reasons: one is the price, the other is that it doesn't fit. The same usually goes for why a man gives up on a woman."
