Luohe city has public employees who play games at work, and some of them even go to entertainment venues for a massage.

by 89km5n on 2012-02-04 15:36:59

[Most Units]

Are able to "do what they should do"

On the second day of the city-wide leaders' conference, Luohe Optimization Office acted quickly according to the requirements of the Municipal Party Committee and Municipal Government. By means of open and covert investigations, they inspected the work discipline enforcement of 45 municipal units directly under the jurisdiction of Luohe City as well as some district-level units, promoting a change in government work style.

Covert investigation found: Luohe Sha Li River Construction Management Organization had led its staff deep into the construction front line, advancing the development and construction of the Sha Li River; Luohe Agricultural Bureau organized technical personnel from plant protection, soil fertility, agricultural technology, and other fields to go to the fields to check on wheat growth; units such as the Letters and Calls Bureau, Human Resources and Social Security Bureau, Family Planning Commission, and Housing Administration have all entered working state; units like the Development and Reform Commission and Commerce Bureau were holding meetings to study and prepare for the city's key work conference.

[Individual Public Servants]

Not focusing on their duties during working hours

"Individual units have lax requirements and loose management over agency staff." The Optimization Office discovered in covert investigations that some staff members did not abide by work discipline, having a slack work style. During working hours, they traded stocks, watched movies, played games, and even bathed and received massages at leisure and entertainment venues.

On January 30th, inspections found: In rooms 616 and 617 of Luohe Statistics Bureau, each had one staff member trading stocks and checking lottery trend charts; at 1:45 PM, during an inspection of a bathing center, it was discovered that four people got out of a public vehicle from Luohe Agricultural Machinery Safety Supervision Office and entered the bathing center to bathe; in the courtyard of a foot massage center, three people got out of a public vehicle from Yuanhu District Finance Bureau to get foot massages; there was also a vehicle from the Economic Development Zone Management Committee parked at this foot massage center; at 2:35 PM, only five people were on duty at the Salt Bureau.

On January 31st, covert investigations found: At 11:20 AM, no one was present in six departments with sixteen rooms at the Quality Supervision Bureau; at 4:17 PM, only one person was on duty at the Water Conservation Office of the Water Bureau; at 4:24 PM, no one was on duty at the Bulk Cement Office of the Materials Group; in the Yuanhu District Administrative Service Hall, no one was on duty at the Development and Reform, Finance, Land and Resources, and State Tax windows; at the Grain Bureau, only ten people were on duty, with one person watching a movie and no one on duty at the subordinate secondary institution Supervision and Inspection Brigade; in the Chaoling District Administrative Service Center, thirteen people were absent, with two staff members watching movies and playing games; at the Disease Control Center, three staff members were playing computer games; at 3:10 PM, a vehicle from a unit stationed in Luohe was parked in the courtyard of a bathing center.

"Work discipline reflects the conduct of cadres and embodies their mental state. The aforementioned units and individuals will be strictly dealt with according to relevant regulations, as required by the Municipal Party Committee and Municipal Government," stated Luohe Optimization Office.
