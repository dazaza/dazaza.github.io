Plot for "Leaves Returning to Roots": Old Zhao is a farmer in his fifties who went south to Shenzhen to work. When his friend Old Wang dies on the construction site, Old Zhao decides to take Old Wang home for burial. First, Old Zhao disguises Old Wang as a drunkard to board a long-distance bus but unfortunately encounters robbers along the way...

Main Cast: Zhao Benshan plays Old Zhao, Song Dandan plays a scavenger woman, Guo Tao plays a beekeeper.

"The Great Mr. Guandong":

Plot: The story takes place in the old northern market of Shenyang during the Republic of China era. Like Beijing's Tianqiao, the old northern market was the economic and cultural hub of old Shenyang, bustling with activity. The drama is based on a series of stories involving various characters... (Total 45 episodes)

Cast: Zhang Xiaofei - Zhao Chun'an, Chen Meixin - Xiang Ling, Yu Yueshan - Ye Lisha, Liu Liu - Ha Beier Ye, Zhao Benshan - Xielengyan, Fan Wei - Han Tingyu

Director: Zhao Benshan

Guest appearances: Zhao Benshan plays Xielengyan, Fan Wei plays Han Tingyu, Xiao Shenyang plays Little Fengtian

"Countryside Love I" (Total 30 episodes)

Plot: Forestry University graduate Xie Yongqiang returns to his village while waiting for job placement. During this time, he falls in love with young woman Wang Xiaomeng. Xie Yongqiang's father, Xie Guangkun, has always hoped for his son to make something of himself...

"Countryside Love II" (Total 41 episodes)

Plot: Wang Xiaomeng’s tofu factory installs a new production line and invites an Er Ren Zhuan troupe for a performance. On stage, her future father-in-law Xie Guangkun goes up and starts babbling nonsense, which greatly displeases Wang Xiaomeng's father, Wang Laqi...

Main Cast: Wang Yabin as Wang Xiaomeng, Yu Yueshan as Xie Dajiao, Zhao Benshan as Wang Dina, Fan Wei as Wang Musen

"Countryside Love Story" also known as "Countryside Love III" or "Countryside Love Part Three" (Total 36 episodes)

Plot: Under the organization of acting Village Chief Liu Neng, a unique rural sports meeting causes many interesting incidents. Each household sends representatives, showcasing various amusing talents...

Cast: Wang Xiaoli, Yu Yueshan, Bi Chang, Xiao Shenyang, He Shufeng, Tang Jianjun

"Old Root Liu I" (Total 18 episodes)

Plot: After being replaced by his son as the respected old party secretary, he gains some urban experience and returns to his hometown to start a tourism catering business, fulfilling his lifelong dream of enriching his fellow villagers at the age of fifty...

"Old Root Liu II" (Total 22 episodes)

Plot: Old Root Liu develops Longquan Manor into a large enterprise group that integrates tourism, vacation, catering entertainment, and industrialized agriculture. However, as the enterprise grows larger, Liu Lao Gen doesn't know how to proceed...

Directed by Zhao Benshan

Main Cast: Zhao Benshan plays Liu Laogen, Gao Xiumin plays Ding Xiang, Fan Wei plays Yao Boxi

"Ma Dashuai I" (27 episodes)

"Ma Dashuai II" (30 episodes)

"Ma Dashuai III" (34 episodes)

Plot: Villager Ma Dashuai from Majiapu Village impulsively arranged a marriage between his daughter Ma Xiaocui and the village chief's son. On the wedding day, Ma Xiaocui disappears without a word...

Plot: After inheriting some wealth, Ma Dashuai becomes the principal of a school and focuses on improving it, facing numerous challenges...

Plot: It's been three years since Ma Dashuai moved to the city. Having experienced ups and downs in fate, with the school gone, he can no longer be the principal...

Directed by Zhao Benshan

Main Cast: Zhao Benshan plays Ma Dashuai, Fan Wei plays Fan Debiao, Wang Yajie plays Yu Fen
