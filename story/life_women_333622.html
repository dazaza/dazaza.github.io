Countermeasures for Diseases Commonly Seen in Female Office Workers

by hkypt on 2010-03-17 13:42:46

60% to 90% of human diseases are related to stress. The white-collar workers in Shenzhen who live under pressure for a long time, be careful that your body will protest in different forms. In fact, several physical discomfort symptoms caused by stress can also be prevented and relieved by ourselves in daily life. Below, the editor will tell you the simple way to temporarily deal with these diseases.

Symptom one: cold

If you feel exhausted and have a sore throat for several weeks in a row, it is very likely because stress has weakened the immune system function, making it easier for bacteria to invade the body and cause respiratory diseases.

Countermeasures:

Pay attention to taking anti-cold measures, such as drinking more water, insisting on washing face and hands with cool water, and try not to let your hands touch your mouth, nose, and eyes. Traditional cold-preventing Chinese medicine, such as garlic and fish mint, etc., can play an effective defensive role at the initial stage when the virus invades.

In addition, keeping a happy mood is also crucial. For those who are prone to depression, walking outdoors frequently will make you feel better, and if you do some exercise, it will be even better.

For symptoms such as fever, headache, nasal congestion, and runny nose, you can also choose some of the following over-the-counter antipyretic analgesics.

Aspirin: also known as acetylsalicylic acid, vinegar willow acid, it is the longest-used and most widely used antipyretic analgesic. It can reduce fever, headache, and general soreness caused by colds.

Calcium cabaprilin: also known as suketong, aspirin calcium urea. It is a complex of aspirin calcium and urea, its effect is the same as aspirin, but it is less irritating to the gastrointestinal tract than aspirin. It can be used for fever and headache caused by colds.

Acetaminophen: also known as paracetamol, bi li tong, Tylenol, etc. Its antipyretic effect is similar to aspirin, but it has little irritation to the gastrointestinal tract. People who cannot tolerate the side effects of aspirin or are allergic to aspirin can use this medicine.

Ibuprofen: also known as Fenbideng, etc. It can be used for fever and pain caused by colds.

A ben tablet: each tablet consists of 100 mg of aspirin and 10 mg of phenobarbital. Mainly used for children's antipyretic and prevention of irritability caused by fever.

Sanlitong tablet: each tablet consists of 0.25 g of acetaminophen, 0.15 g of isopropyl antipyrine, and 50 mg of caffeine. Its antipyretic effect is similar to aspirin, and its analgesic effect seems better than aspirin.

Symptom two: headache

Tension is the main root of migraines. Usually, one out of ten women suffers from varying degrees of migraines, but only half of those with migraines actively seek treatment. There are various opinions about the causes of migraines. Generally speaking, if you repeatedly feel pain around your eye sockets and nausea, it is very likely a sign of an impending migraine.

Countermeasures:

First, you should not rely on analgesics to avoid worsening headaches after reducing medication. The correct approach is: take medicine twice a week for several months to form a routine, then gradually reduce the frequency of taking medicine, such as once every four days or seven days; second, you can add some vitamin B2 for prevention; finally, pay attention to eating less cheese, wine, nuts, and meat foods.

Of course, before implementing this series of preventive measures, don't forget to give yourself a chance to change the air and stretch your limbs after sitting in front of the computer for a long time, even if it's just for five minutes.

Symptom three: insomnia

Insomnia worsens with increasing stress and burden. Some women are most likely to experience insomnia during their menstrual cycle. The usual situation is: sleepy during ovulation period, and unable to sleep after the ovulation period.

Countermeasures:

Eat a small amount of turkey, cheese, or tuna salad and drink a glass of milk one hour before bedtime, but do not eat sweets before bedtime.

If you still can't sleep, stop forcing yourself to count sheep, instead, get up and practice yoga. After suffering from insomnia all night, the remedy you can do the next day is to find a way to take a nap at noon.

Female insomnia can lead to endocrine disorders, dull complexion, grayish complexion without luster, facial pigmentation, wrinkles, and accelerated aging. Insomnia in adolescents can lead to poor development, low intelligence, memory loss, lack of concentration, neurasthenia, etc., seriously affecting learning.

Symptom four: oral ulcers

About 50 million people worldwide suffer from oral ulcers. High-pressure white-collar workers who are in a state of overload work for a long time are more likely to develop oral ulcers.
