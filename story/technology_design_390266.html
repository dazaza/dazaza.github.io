Taobao store opening process

Sometimes, doing a good job with a Taobao store does not require perfection in every link, perhaps excelling in just one or two links is enough!

So what are the steps involved in Taobao entrepreneurship, and what issues should be noted in each step?

Taobao entrepreneurship can roughly be divided into the following steps: 1. Website positioning 2. Sources 3. Online store setup 4. Promotion 5. Customer service and after-sales

1. Store positioning: That is, what you plan to sell, what price range the items are in, the main age and gender of the consumer group, and even their profession! The reason why store positioning is placed first is because it directly affects the subsequent four steps! Store positioning should be determined based on your own actual situation, generally choosing industries you are interested in or products you have sources for!

2. Sources: Finding good sources is at least one-third successful! And for most people, it's hard to find sources with significant advantages. Regarding how to find sources, please refer to the subsequent Taobao store opening source chapter!

3. Online store setup: Including store decoration, product description, and even product pricing! For a new store, it's best to try to perfect each link as much as possible!

4. Online store promotion: This should be considered the most important part. Common online store promotion methods include using Taobao's ranking rules, Taobao community promotion, Taobao affiliate promotion, independent website promotion, forum group buying, forum ad space, forum serial promotion, and Taobao Express and Google bidding promotion! Each promotion method will be specifically discussed in subsequent articles!

5. Customer service and after-sales! The main task of customer service and after-sales is to cultivate customer loyalty to your online store, that is, how to turn new customers into old customers! The techniques and matters to note will be covered in subsequent articles!

What should I sell in my Taobao store?

A good online store positioning equals half the success!

Website positioning, in simple terms, is what you plan to sell, what price range, whether the customer base is male or female, and which age group!

Good website positioning directly affects future profitability, so it is essential to carefully position your store in the early stages!

What to sell to make more profit is something most people pay attention to and worry about! Should you sell popular products or new and unique products?

I have summarized a few principles of website positioning, hoping to provide some help!

1. Products have repeat consumption characteristics

The most important thing in doing business is repeat customers, so the best products are those with repeat consumption characteristics!

Repeat consumption actually has two situations. A simple example: clothes and cosmetics both have repeat consumption characteristics, but customers won't buy the same style of clothes again, but they might buy other styles from you, while cosmetics might buy the same style or other brands or other products from the same brand! As long as the product has repeat consumption characteristics, it meets the first principle!

2. Familiar or interested fields

There are thousands of products, and each product surely has people who sell it well and others who can't sell it. I believe the products are all the same, the key is people's ability! As for what kind of product to choose to start your Taobao journey, I think it's best to choose something you are familiar with or interested in! Only when you are familiar with or interested in something will you have the motivation to continue!

3. Products have distinct features

What does it mean to have distinct features? Through some word descriptions, people can imagine what the product is!

For example, brand awareness, specialized functions, etc.! What is meant by specialized functions is actually the specificization of functions.

A simple example, if a cosmetic has whitening, spot removal, and acne removal functions, how should this cosmetic be packaged? Write down all the functions? No, it's best to highlight one function, which can give customers a subconscious awareness, if you want xxx, you must choose this product! Application example, Wang Lao Ji highlights the heat-clearing and detoxifying function!

4. Product profits are high enough

Unless you are selling in large quantities, otherwise choose products with high enough profit margins!

For most newly opened Taobao stores, sourcing is definitely a headache! Because, after all, only a small number of people have their own physical stores, then for others, how to solve the sourcing problem!

People without physical stores generally fall into the following three situations:

1. You are close to a large wholesale market, and when someone places an order, you go to the wholesale market to get the goods!

The advantage of this sourcing form is that it does not tie up funds and has a price advantage, but the disadvantage is that it is troublesome! Of course, many people are not close to large wholesale markets, and even if they are close to wholesale markets, those who do Taobao part-time may not have time to run every day!

2. Buy things in bulk from platforms like Alibaba and sell them on Taobao, the advantage is price advantage, the disadvantage is tying up funds! This sourcing form is generally suitable for items priced around 10 yuan, such as small accessories!

3. Drop shipping! This is also the main form, the advantage is no capital tied up, shipping is also simple, but if there is a problem, coordination is more troublesome! Cosmetics, clothing, shoes, etc., can all adopt this sourcing form! In addition to general products, you can also join a brand to do drop shipping, such as cosmetics brands, also adopting drop shipping, no capital tied up.

It's best to secure the exclusive agency of a brand, the fewer similar products, the better, such as smoking cessation products. There aren't many smoking cessation product sellers on Taobao, so if you can secure the exclusive agency of a new smoking cessation product, it will be relatively easier to promote!
