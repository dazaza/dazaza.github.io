**Title: "I'm in Kenting, the Weather is Clear" Episode 10**

**Introduction to "I'm in Kenting, the Weather is Clear":**

Growing up together in Kenting are two close friends, Hanwen and Liangliang. They become fans of the online pure love writer "Rain Never Stops" and frequently interact with her under the nickname "Weather is Clear" on her blog. Hanwen, who has never been in love, even falls for "Rain Never Stops," fantasizing that she is as pure and clear as the characters in her stories. In real life, "Rain Never Stops," whose real name is Xiaowei, is an emotionally intense girl whose love life keeps being hurt. After facing cruel treatment from her boyfriend, she abandons her soon-to-be-published novel and everything in Taipei, quietly retreating to Kenting. Anan, the illustrator for "Rain Never Stops'" novels, arrives in Kenting by bus due to a failed collaboration with the publisher and financial troubles with his girlfriend's studio, intending to collect a debt.

By chance, Anan meets Hanwen and Liangliang, and the three quickly become good friends. The frequent interactions between Anan and Liangliang make Azuo, who has feelings for Liangliang, extremely jealous. Hanwen, after meeting Anan, decides to open a surfing shop together with him, as they both share a deep connection with the sea.

Xiaowei, however, only socializes with the foreign friends who come to Kenting with her, maintaining a distant attitude that frustrates Hanwen. Yet, her coldness and melancholy attract Hanwen, making him question his feelings for "Rain Never Stops."

The foreign friends accompanying Xiaowei to Kenting have an ulterior motive. After a thorough tour guided by the warm-hearted and friendly Liangliang, they write a paper criticizing the contradictions and chaos in the management of Kenting National Park and suggest removing Kenting from the list of national parks. This action causes a stir and inspires Liangliang to help the national park from a broader perspective. She proposes managing the jet ski operators through a BOT bidding process, which triggers strong opposition from local operators. During a heated protest, Azuo is severely injured while protecting Liangliang.

Xiaowei returns to Taipei to resolve her contractual issues with the publisher. Her ex-boyfriend, facing a major career crisis, tries to use Xiaowei's fame to promote his latest project and approaches her under false pretenses. Watching this unfold from afar in Kenting, Hanwen wants to help Xiaowei solve her contractual problems but feels helpless due to his lack of legal knowledge.

After meeting Brother Chu, Anan gradually understands his deep affection for Kenting and his grand vision for the tourism industry. He hopes to collaborate with Brother Chu to start a business. A group of people join Brother Chu’s plan just as the business is about to take off, but Brother Chu suddenly disappears. Where did Brother Chu go? How will their dreams for Kenting continue...?
