Woman jumps from third floor in suicide attempt, caught by her boyfriend at the moment of landing

by gghot76hh1 on 2012-03-07 17:42:42

At around 8 p.m. the night before last, a woman in Xintiandi residential area of Jiangdong District attempted to commit suicide by jumping off a building due to emotional issues. As the Jiangdong firemen were preparing to lay down air cushions for rescue, the woman became emotionally agitated and jumped from the third floor. After being slightly cushioned by an air conditioner midway through her fall, she was caught by her boyfriend at the very moment she hit the ground, and both fell down.

Reported by correspondent Ming Lai, reporter Zhenfeng Jiang, intern Yiran Chen, with photographs by Dongliu Jia

The Woman Attempting Suicide by Jumping Off the Building

At 8:13 p.m. on May 17, when the firefighters arrived at the scene, a young long-haired woman was crouching outside the balcony of the third floor of the building, almost completely suspended in mid-air. A slight misstep would have caused her to fall from the balcony.

Meanwhile, two police officers were continuously shouting from the ground, trying to do ideological work with the woman, attempting to stabilize her emotions and persuade her to go back onto the balcony and not do anything foolish.

Beside them, a man in his twenties appeared extremely anxious. He kept apologizing to the woman, urging her not to act impulsively, but the woman refused to listen and became increasingly agitated, starting to cry. The number of onlookers grew, and everyone joined in to try and dissuade her from acting impulsively.

From the police, it was learned that the woman attempting suicide was surnamed Luo and came from Hunan province. The man standing beside the police trying to persuade her was Luo's boyfriend, surnamed Gao, from Deqing, Zhejiang. According to Mr. Gao, his girlfriend had become impulsive due to problems in their relationship and wanted to solve the issue by jumping off the building. After understanding the general situation, the police continued to persuade her from below, while the firefighters planned to enter the balcony from inside her room and, when she wasn't paying attention, grab her from behind and bring her down from the balcony.

Unexpectedly, the woman had already locked all the doors and windows from the inside, not allowing anyone to approach.

Critical Moment: Boyfriend Catches Her Barehanded

To be prepared for any eventuality, the firefighters were ready to lay down firefighting air cushions on the ground. Just as the firefighters were preparing to lay down the air cushions, Ms. Luo suddenly lost control of her emotions and directly jumped from the third-floor balcony, shocking everyone around her. Seeing his girlfriend falling, Mr. Gao immediately rushed forward. At the very moment Ms. Luo hit the ground, she was caught by her boyfriend Mr. Gao with both hands. Due to the immense inertia, Mr. Gao fell to the ground with his girlfriend the instant he caught her.

Ms. Luo's actions made many people break out in cold sweat. Mr. Yao, who lived across from them, told reporters that he hadn’t noticed any arguments between the two young people before. However, on the afternoon of the incident, he heard the two arguing downstairs, though he didn't know what they were arguing about. Mr. Yao said it was fortunate that the young man ran fast enough when the girl fell.

Both Were Uninjured

According to a firefighter who often participates in emergency rescues, Ms. Luo was lucky because during her fall, her body collided with an air conditioning unit outside the balcony on the second floor. The obstruction provided Mr. Gao with time to rush forward and catch his girlfriend, and also provided some cushioning for Mr. Gao to catch Ms. Luo. The firefighter explained that even the regular firefighting air cushions cannot guarantee complete safety for those falling. If someone falls from the third or fourth floor and lands directly on the air cushion, the safety factor is higher. However, if the fall is from a higher floor, the danger increases significantly.

After the incident, Ms. Luo was urgently taken to the Sixth People's Hospital of Ningbo for examination and treatment. It was learned that due to the impact, Ms. Luo showed symptoms of mild concussion, while Mr. Gao was unharmed.

Boyfriend Catches Falling Girlfriend Without Injury

Physics Teacher Explains Reasons

Generally speaking, catching someone falling from a building would also result in injury to oneself.

Consulting with physics teacher Mr. Zhou from a middle school in Yinzhou, he explained: "In terms of physics, under constant mass, the greater the speed, the greater the kinetic energy and the impact force. With some form of cushioning, the speed decreases, thus reducing the kinetic energy and impact force." Why was Mr. Gao able to catch his girlfriend and remain unscathed? Mr. Zhou explained: "It might be because there was some degree of cushioning during the fall, which reduced the impact force. Without this cushioning, whether Mr. Gao would have been injured is hard to say."

Mr. Zhou added that if the boyfriend hadn't caught her, or if the floor had been higher, regret would have been too late. Without the cushioning from the air conditioning unit, her action could have potentially harmed her boyfriend as well.
