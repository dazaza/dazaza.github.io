A girl has helped her disabled fellow villager to school for nine years.

by gf54h6bhhy on 2012-03-03 10:12:20

Xu Fengjiao (the first on the right) is discussing exam questions with Zou Mengjiao. This photo was taken by Ma Yuefeng, a reporter of our newspaper.

[Reading Tip]

Since she was 6 years old, from elementary school to junior high school and then senior high school, for nine years, she has been helping her disabled partner from the same village to go to school together every day, holding hands and never apart. She is Xu Fengjiao, a Grade One student at the Ninth Middle School of Laizhou City, Shandong Province.

On September 20th, at the dormitory of the Ninth Middle School of Laizhou City, Shandong Province. At 6:20 in the morning, ten minutes before the wake-up bell rang, Xu Fengjiao had already got up. At seven o'clock, Fengjiao helped Mengjiao climb step by step to the classroom on the third floor. At a little past eleven, Mengjiao was waiting at the canteen table while Fengjiao was queuing up in a long line...

This autumn, "Angel of Love" Xu Fengjiao and her partner Zou Mengjiao, whom she has been taking care of all along, both entered Laizhou Key High School - Laizhou No.9 Middle School with excellent results. On September 20th, I came to Laizhou No.9 Middle School and witnessed one day of the two girls.

Facing the future, Xu Fengjiao revealed her wish: "If possible, Zou Mengjiao and I will strive to enter the same university. I will still take care of her and be the crutch of her life." Their hand-holding companionship began 9 years ago when Xu Fengjiao was only 6 years old...

[Scene One]

It was snowing heavily and the road was extremely slippery. Xu Fengjiao supported Zou Mengjiao and carefully walked on the small road leading to the school. Suddenly, a strong wind mixed with snow blew towards them, causing both of them to fall into the snow. After struggling several times, but due to Fengjiao's lack of strength, they kept falling... As the time for school was approaching, Fengjiao cried and ran more than two miles to find the nearest house, asking for help to pull Mengjiao up who had fallen. The two frail figures hugged each other again.

Xu Fengjiao and Zou Mengjiao both live in Dongshige Village, Tuanwang Town, Laizhou City, Shandong Province. Zou Mengjiao was diagnosed with cerebral palsy at the age of 5, and despite multiple treatments, she still could not walk normally. "I want to go to school!" At the age of 7, Zou Mengjiao could no longer bear it and begged her parents to let her go to school. However, her father worked outside all year round, and her mother had to take care of the fields alone. No one could take care of Mengjiao going to school, which became the biggest worry for the whole family.

When 6-year-old Xu Fengjiao happily went to school with her schoolbag, Zou Mengjiao, who walked unsteadily, caught her attention. Xu Fengjiao actively reached out her hand: "Come on, hold my hand, it will be easier to walk!" And this hand has been held ever since.

Since then, every morning at around 6 o'clock, Xu Fengjiao would appear on time at Zou Mengjiao's doorstep. In fact, the two families were not close. To take care of Mengjiao, Xu Fengjiao had to start 20 minutes earlier every day, walking an extra mile to Mengjiao's home. But whether it was windy or rainy, heavy snow blocking the door, scorching sun or freezing cold, on the mountain path leading to the school, there would always be a girl supporting another girl with a leg disability, day after day, without interruption.

The "two beauties" not only stayed inseparable in life but also encouraged each other in their studies. Physical disabilities brought great inconvenience to Zou Mengjiao, and she had to put in much more effort than normal people to study. During the summer vacation of 2005, Zou Mengjiao underwent a leg correction surgery and took a half-year break from school. Due to missing too many classes, Mengjiao's parents planned to have her repeat a grade. However, Xu Fengjiao did not want her to delay her studies because they had already made an agreement: to study together and advance together. During the half-year break, Xu Fengjiao voluntarily took on the task of tutoring Mengjiao. She listened very carefully in class every day, marking all the key points taught by the teacher in her textbook. After school, she would go to Mengjiao's house first and teach everything she learned to her partner, then go home for dinner. As a result, Mengjiao not only caught up with all the lessons but also advanced to the fourth grade with excellent grades.

[Scene Two]

Mengjiao wanted to get up, so Fengjiao went over and extended her hand first. Once Mengjiao stood up, Fengjiao tightly held her hand, every step was so默契. The two held hands and went to the restroom together, washed up together, and thus a new day began.

Five years passed quickly, and Fengjiao and Mengjiao finished their primary school education. Over the five years, they had become inseparable good friends. Entering junior high school, Mengjiao's home was eight miles away from the school. As the opening date approached, Mengjiao and her mother gradually became anxious: Would Fengjiao still take care of her as before? Would they be in the same class in junior high school? In fact, Xu Fengjiao had already decided that she would not abandon Mengjiao. On the opening day, Xu Fengjiao held Zou Mengjiao's hand and came to Tuanwang Middle School together. Fengjiao actively approached the teacher and requested to be in the same class as Mengjiao. After understanding their situation, the school leaders assigned them to the same class, the same dormitory, and even the same desk. Thus, they started a new life.

Due to boarding and the more intense learning schedule in junior high school, Mengjiao faced greater difficulties in daily life, and Fengjiao's care for her became more meticulous. In everyone's eyes, Xu Fengjiao was Zou Mengjiao's "full-time nanny". When Mengjiao was unhappy, Fengjiao would chat with her about family matters and share heartfelt words. After class, the "two beauties" were intimate partners. In class, they were "competitors" striving to learn. Because of their diligence, their academic performance always ranked at the top of the class. The bits of love flowing between them, like an ink painting, slowly spread and dyed across the campus, gradually touching all teachers and students in the school.

In high school, Xu Fengjiao's "journey of love" continued. Fengjiao's family was not well-off, and the neighbors all said that she was a sensible child who knew how to care for others and empathize with others. However, taking care of a classmate who was not related for so many years, some people did not understand.

Fengjiao's mother felt sorry for her daughter. Once, she asked her, "Jiao Jiao, do you feel bitter taking care of Mengjiao? Do you regret it?" Fengjiao smiled and said, "Not at all. I think helping Mengjiao is something I should do because we are good friends. Besides, I am also a beneficiary. I have learned a lot from her, such as perseverance, optimism, and not bowing down to fate. Being with her makes me happy too."
