Reasons for floor waxing: 1. Protect stones and floors; 2. Enhance aesthetics; 3. Anti-slip; 4. Convenient cleaning. Floor analysis: 1. Hard floors such as granite, marble, terrazzo (two coats each of base wax and surface wax for better effect), mirror tiles, ceramic tiles, glazed tiles (waxing effect is not good, easy to fall off); 2. Soft floors such as rubber flooring series, PVC material flooring series; 3. Wooden floor series including solid wood floors and composite floors. Note: If soft floors or wooden floor series need waxing treatment, it's best to use high-gloss no-rub wax, and no polishing is required. Tools: Cleaning machine, water suction machine, high-speed polishing machine, drying machine, wax drag or tiger clamp, towel, wringer cart, work instruction sign and rags, etc. Agents: High-speed polishing wax, base wax, no-polish surface wax, floor cleaner (acidic cleaner, all-purpose water) or wax remover, neutral cleaner. Steps: 1. Clear ground debris and set up a work instruction sign; 2. Clean the floor thoroughly with neutral cleaner; 3. Apply two coats of base wax on clean and dry floor surfaces, then air dry; 4. After the base wax dries, apply two coats of surface wax on its surface, then air dry; 5. After the wax surface is completely dry, polish evenly and at a uniform speed using a high-speed polishing machine with a polishing pad; 6. After waxing and polishing, the floor will be bright, clean, and naturally reflective, reaching over 90%, with excellent sensory effects. Precautions: 1. Operations must strictly follow the procedure, that is, crisscross in a cross pattern, Qingdao cleaning service, the thinner the better, the more even the better; 2. Wax agents perform best between 5°C-40°C; 3. High-gloss no-rub wax, high-speed polishing wax, repair wax cannot be applied four times within 24 hours, base wax and surface wax must be used together, otherwise, good results cannot be achieved. Floor cleaning Standard and operation for floor cleaning: Disinfect and clean various types of soft and hard floors (marble, granite, tiles, cement, mosaics, wooden floors), remove all stains from the floor, and then carry out professional treatments such as vinegar grinding and polishing to quickly restore the dirty floor to cleanliness, Qingdao cleaning company. Tools and materials used: Floor washer, nylon brush disk, water suction machine, mop, bucket. Materials used: Weak alkali or neutral cleaner. Operation method: 1. Pour the diluted cleaning solution into the floor washer, start the machine and operate from back to front, walking in a straight line to avoid missing any spots; 2. While wiping, start the water suction machine to simultaneously wipe and suck away the dirty water. For areas where the floor washer cannot reach, use a mop soaked in cleaner and wrung out to wipe these areas; 3. After washing the floor once with cleaner and sucking up the dirty water, wash again with clear water in the same manner, and suck up the moisture to complete the process; 4. Precautions: It is forbidden to use acidic cleaners and powdered cleaners when cleaning the floor, as this may damage and corrode the stone surface. Avoid using rough objects to rub the floor to prevent permanent scratches on the stone surface; 5. Standards: Clean, shiny, no stains, no marks.
