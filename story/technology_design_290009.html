RS232 ET9919 Multiparameter Water Quality Rapid Flowing Laboratory

by yq91566g8 on 2009-12-03 10:53:49

Based on the characteristics of global water quality and monitoring standards, combined with our own technical advantages, using the building block configuration concept, we have ingeniously integrated the electrode method with microcomputer photometric measurement principles to launch the latest ET9919 multi-parameter mobile laboratory series. This series not only meets the requirements for water quality testing in various outdoor environments but is also suitable for rapid water quality detection during emergencies and routine water parameter measurements in laboratories, allowing users to meet their water testing needs with minimal investment.

The spectrophotometer built into this system features over 120 test method applications and electrode-based measurements, enabling it to measure more than 130 important water quality parameters. These are widely applied in water quality monitoring across industries such as industrial, municipal, environmental protection, and education sectors.

Key Features:

- Rapid measurement of various water quality parameters with user-friendly operations.

- Large capacity storage for ion concentration and turbidity data.

- RS232 and USB data interfaces allow for storing and managing data which can be transmitted to a computer for further analysis, printing, etc.

- Suitable for comprehensive water quality analysis in the field, at production sites, and in laboratories.

Specifications:

A. Photometric determination uses a wide range of continuous wavelengths, automatically selecting wavelength based on the measurement item;

B. Microprocessor patented technology, ORP57 type oxidation-reduction/temperature tester, user-friendly design with large screen display, flowchart graphic display, simple operation;

C. Conventional or fast measurement of pH, conductivity, dissolved oxygen, turbidity, COD, TOC, total chromium, total phosphorus, total nitrogen, ammonia nitrogen, fluoride, iron, copper, phosphate, sulfate, nitrate, and more than 130 other important parameters;

D. Large capacity data storage function, routinely storing 200 to 1000 data sets (limited to ion concentration and turbidity measurement values);

E. Use of pre-prepared reagents, GTOP-300B type intelligent lighted incubator (seed germination box), some reagent shelf life can reach 5-10 years;

F. Convenient connection to computers for real-time printing or data management (limited to ion concentration and turbidity measurement values);

G. Portable packaging, waterproof and shockproof design, economical consumables, suitable for field and laboratory analysis and testing.

ET9919 Models: Basic, General, Enhanced

Measurement Projects: Up to 125, 129, and 130 measurement parameter indicators respectively (detailed ion measurement specifications available in the documentation).

Acidity/ORP Range: pH -2.00 to 16.00 pH, slope 75 to 110%, ORP/mV ±699.9 mV, ±1999 mV.

Conductivity Range: 0.0 to 199.9 µS/cm, 0 to 1999 µS/cm, 0.00 to 19.99 mS/cm, 0.0 to 199.9 mS/cm.

Dissolved Oxygen Range: DO 0.00 to 45.00 mg/L (ppm), Saturation 0.0 to 300.0%.

Turbidity Range: Automatic three-range conversion from 0.00 to 1000 NTU.

Temperature Range: -20.0 to 120.0°C (-4.0 to 248.0°F).

Electrode Types: Acidity/ORP electrode, Conductivity electrode, Dissolved Oxygen electrode.

Data Management: Storage up to 1000 multi-parameter readings and 200 turbidity readings. Data interface includes USB and RS232.

Wavelength Range: 330 to 900 nm with self-calibration tungsten light source.

Calibration Mode: Automatic zero calibration, SMD microprocessor technology, factory calibration, and user-defined dual calibration mode.

Standard Configuration: Includes accessories package, dedicated carrying case, and bilingual manuals.
