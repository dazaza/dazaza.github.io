Ody芬 Brilliant Underwear Brand

by mttasod42 on 2009-12-07 01:26:04

Ou Difen understands that caring for women is not just about good product quality. Viewing lingerie as a long-term business, Ou Difen is also committed to the humanistic care of women. The Ou Difen International Group adheres to the corporate management philosophy of "sincerity, diligence, simplicity, and honesty", and with fashionable and innovative R&D concepts, has pioneered new highlights in the lingerie industry such as Chinese Red, outerwear-style lingerie, and water bag patents; taking the mission to inherit Chinese traditional culture and create lingerie fashion, it has founded the Ou Difen Lingerie Culture Festival over a decade ago, launched the large-scale multimedia stage play "In Search of Clothes", created lingerie trend reports, held the "OuDifen Cup" Chinese Lingerie Element Innovation Design Competition, established the Ou Difen Lingerie Design Talent Base, and organized the "995 Save Breasts" breast health public welfare activities. While caring for women, Ou Difen also spreads love to society, advocates environmental protection, donates to disaster areas, and promotes harmony!

Through years of operation, Ou Difen has made its mission to "create the most caring lingerie brand for women", forming five major advantages: "fashion taste, cultural temperament, charm appeal, innovative style, and public welfare spirit"! Ou Difen will continue to "move the world with lingerie culture"!

The five major advantages have achieved brilliance in the lingerie brand:

Fashion Taste - Cooperating with France's largest design company Peclers to create the Ruby brand, signing a ten-year cooperation agreement with China's fashion forefront - the China Fashion Designers Association, hosting the 2006 Huangpu River Brand Launch in Shanghai, and presenting Ou Difen's lingerie trend at the China International Fashion Week in 2007 and 2008.

Ou Difen insists on holding two European lingerie tours each year, participating in lingerie exhibitions in Lyon and Paris, deeply understanding international trends. Collaborations with Peclers and Carlin make their products embody international fashion and lead domestic lingerie trends. Introducing Italy's prestigious brand ID Sallya Li. Hosting the e-Generation Fashion Brand Launch at Beijing Kerry Center in 2002, and the Flower Dance Feast Brand Launch at Beijing Kerry Center in 2005, during the China International Fashion Week, hosting design competition press conferences. In 2006, Ou Difen became the first lingerie brand to hold a launch on the Huangpu River in the "Oriental Paris" - Shanghai. In 2007 and 2008, Ou Difen joined hands with the China International Fashion Week, showcasing the essence and charm of local Chinese lingerie brands to the world on this largest domestic fashion platform.
