The true cause of Gao Xiumin's death is exposed: Was it due to a change of heart? Or was it due to illness?

by gensen on 2008-12-17 14:24:02

This year, as the Spring Festival Gala is being prepared, He Qingkui has once again come out of retirement to take charge of the language-based programs for the gala. This is good news, proving that He Qingkui has moved past the sorrow of Gao Xiumin's passing and has returned to a normal creative state. However, Zhao Benshan still cannot find a partner, which makes people think of Gao Xiumin again. Gao Xiumin passed away suddenly at home on August 18, 2005 due to a sudden onset of heart disease in the middle of the night. But how was her heart disease triggered? —— This is actually the true cause of Gao Xiumin's death. Therefore, after Gao Xiumin's death, the media and internet have been continuously discussing the reasons behind her heart disease, and there are constantly new discoveries. It seems like this has become a way to remember Gao Xiumin.

Gao Xiumin has always been an actor that I admire. Perhaps northern audiences can more easily accept such actors because the country is vast and cultural differences are significant. Nonetheless, no matter what, when Gao Xiumin passed away, she was one of the few female comedic actors in the country. Especially her iron triangle collaboration with Zhao Benshan and Fan Wei once brought their careers to unprecedented heights. It was truly a time of great success. However, life is sometimes like this; sometimes none of us have the right to choose.

It would seem that it has been over three years since Gao Xiumin's passing, and people should gradually forget, after all, life goes on. However, because of the continuous news about her "former husband" He Qingkui, Gao Xiumin is often mentioned. Recently, through revelations from Gao Xiumin's close friends and family and He Qingkui's responses, Gao Xiumin's death has taken on more mysterious hues.

Revelation One: Gao Xiumin's family reveals that Gao Xiumin and He Qingkui did not have a marital relationship.

Possibly, the common people do not know that He Qingkui has never divorced his first wife and has three children and grandchildren. Even when he appeared publicly with Gao Xiumin as a "couple," they were not legally married.

According to Gao Xiumin's younger sister, "Couple" was something concocted by the media. They claimed to be a "couple" externally, and later became famous, so it was hard to back down. In reality, they were never legally married. The family has always resented He Qingkui for not divorcing his first wife and not marrying Gao Xiumin, so the relationship between the family and He Qingkui has never been good.

He Qingkui's response: I and Gao Xiumin had reached a consensus, and I am very remorseful towards Gao Xiumin for not having a proper name or status. It's quite unfair to Gao Xiumin. However, my first wife has given birth to three children for me, and my eldest son is in Guangzhou. My first wife also went to Guangzhou to take care of the grandchild, allowing us to develop our career here. I really couldn't bring myself to ask for a divorce. If I did, my first wife might not even have the will to live. I couldn't bring myself to do it. Gao Xiumin was also very kind-hearted and said she couldn't bear to do it either. So, it remained like this.

Commentary: I don't want to get involved in other people's family affairs, but I do want to question whether He Qingkui's situation constitutes bigamy? At least, it doesn't conform to China's ethical and moral values. Of course, Gao Xiumin was fully aware of this. If it was true love, we understand such women. However, if it was due to interests, then it is indeed hard to commend. At the very least, in Northeastern Chinese terms, it is deceiving the public. In the end, it is deceiving oneself. A woman's happiness absolutely cannot be shared with others. Moreover, how does He Qingkui's first wife and children feel? Indeed, it has hurt the feelings of all parties involved.

Revelation Two: Gao Xiumin's close friend reveals: Suspected that Gao Xiumin was argued to death by He Qingkui.

According to a close friend of Gao Xiumin, Gao Xiumin frequently argued with He Qingkui before her death, and during the last period of her life, the relationship between Gao Xiumin and He Qingkui was quite tense. She often argued with He Qingkui on the phone when he was not around. On the day she passed away, he was the first person to enter the scene. He saw that Gao Xiumin was holding a phone in her hand, so he suspected that Gao Xiumin died while arguing with He Qingkui.

He Qingkui's response: His (Gao Xiumin's friend) analysis is incorrect. How could they be arguing? Gao Xiumin actually died from suffocation caused by snoring. Snoring led to oxygen deprivation, causing deeper sleep. When she woke up, she vomited black-red blood, which got sucked back into her airway, blocking it and causing suffocation. Her face was black-purple, and her hands were grabbing her hair and clawing at the wall in front of her. During the final memorial service, I asked the makeup artist to cover it up with powder, but it couldn't be completely covered.

Commentary: First, we learned from He Qingkui's words that Gao Xiumin suffered greatly at the time of her death; secondly, both He Qingkui and Gao Xiumin's friends say that Gao Xiumin was holding a phone in her hand when she died, but He Qingkui claims that one of her hands was grabbing her own hair and the other was clawing at the wall in front of her, which seems contradictory. Thirdly, the hospital's final death certificate states that she died from a sudden onset of heart disease. So, which version is true? Of course, I believe the hospital's certificate more, but both versions of suffocation and argument-induced death come from people closest to Gao Xiumin, leaving much room for doubt.

Revelation Three: Gao Xiumin's close friend reveals: He Qingkui once asked Gao Xiumin to take care of his first wife.

A close friend of Gao Xiumin says that he/she once overheard a phone conversation between them. He Qingkui was negotiating with Gao Xiumin, saying that his eldest son had passed away, and he needed to take care of his grandson and write scripts while carrying him. Essentially, he wanted to bring his first wife along. Gao Xiumin wasn't angry about this. What made Gao Xiumin angry was He Qingkui asking her to take care of his first wife occasionally.

He Qingkui's response: We basically didn't argue, and almost not at all in the last year. The content of the phone call was: You (Gao Xiumin) shouldn't see my (He Qingkui's) world collapse, and I won't let your world collapse. But there is a condition: you need to be my backbone. As long as your backbone supports me, my world will never collapse. I want to create a miracle - writing plays while carrying my grandson. It's distressing. I write plays while carrying my grandson, sometimes recording with a voice recorder, creating under these adverse conditions.

Commentary: Ultimately, from He Qingkui's words, we still don't know if his first wife came or not. He just mentioned writing plays while carrying his grandson. From Gao Xiumin's close friend's mouth, there is no mention of He Qingkui's first wife coming. Thus, it is likely that He Qingkui wrote plays while carrying his grandson at his first wife's place. If this is the case, not returning to Gao Xiumin's side and staying with his first wife to look after the grandson, and arguing with Gao Xiumin, would be quite normal. From He Qingkui's "your world, my world" rebuttal, we still faintly hear He Qingkui negotiating with Gao Xiumin. Writers' speeches may all undergo artistic embellishment.

Up to this point, Gao Xiumin's cause of death has become a mystery. However, pretending to dive deep, let the past be past. She is gone, what's the point of talking about these things anymore? Gao Xiumin and He Qingkui never got married, after all, it's their own business. In this matter, family members are outsiders, let alone friends.

No one should reveal any more, no one should clarify any more. It's meaningless now. I heard that He Qingkui has now moved to Beijing to rent a house and live quietly, showing that the elder is also seeking peace. Previously, because of the "big forest planting" incident, he almost ended up in jail. With two of the closest people leaving one after another, anyone would have difficulties overcoming such hurdles. Most importantly, give Gao Xiumin a peaceful world. Before her death, she left laughter on the screen for us, but the stories behind the scenes were not pleasant. This is the sorrow of stars. At this moment, shouldn't relatives and friends recall more beautiful moments? Life is inherently a mix of sourness, sweetness, bitterness, and spiciness. Bringing it up is meaningless. As for why He Qingkui revealed the cause of Gao Xiumin's death, it might be for self-protection.
