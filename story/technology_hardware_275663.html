The South China Morning Post reported that the case of a Qingdao teacher hiring students to travel a thousand miles to kill a romantic rival, which had been widely followed by all sectors of society, first went to trial yesterday in Shenzhen Intermediate People's Court. However, five novice "young killers" mistakenly identified a female cleaner as their assassination target, driving a car to run her over, leaving her in a vegetative state. The victim's family demanded an astronomical compensation of 40 million yuan in court. Beijing Huatian Star Chain (Garden Bridge Store), Beijing, Haidian District, No. 33 Chegongzhuang West Road, ￥188 Reservation.

In court, Zhang Xuejing denied committing intentional homicide, while Shi Chunlong and the other five people admitted their guilt. Yesterday’s hearing lasted for three hours, after which the judge announced a recess and will announce the verdict at a later date. Hengyang Huatian Star Chain (Liberation West Road Store), Hengyang, Zhending District, No. 86 Liberation West Road, ￥148 Reservation. Xiangtan Huatian Star Chain (Jijianying Store), Xiangtan, Yuhu Lake District, No. 89 Shaoshan Middle Road, ￥148 Reservation.

The defendant Zhang Xuejing, aged 39, is a teacher at a school in Qingdao. The defendant Shi Chunlong, aged 20, is a student of Zhang Xuejing, while the other several killers are less than 19 years old. The prosecution charged that in mid-November last year, due to personal emotional entanglements, defendant Zhang Xuejing conspired with defendant Shi Chunlong to kill Li (female) who lived in Shenzhen, promising substantial monetary rewards afterward. On November 29th, Shi Chunlong and four peers drove thousands of miles to Shenzhen.
