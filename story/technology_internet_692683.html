by dyb44553 on 2012-03-06 11:10:40

How long after pregnancy is the best time for an abortion? Gynecological experts from Shanxi Huimin Hospital introduced that the timing of a painless abortion mainly depends on the size of the gestational sac after examination to determine whether a painless abortion can be performed. Choosing the most suitable painless abortion method at the optimal time will minimize the harm caused by the painless abortion. So, how long after pregnancy is the best time for an abortion?

Gynecological experts from Shanxi Huimin Hospital introduced that the best time for an abortion is between 40-50 days of pregnancy. At this stage, the fetus has not yet formed, the uterus is not too large, and the uterine muscle wall is still thick, making it easier to aspirate the embryo, thus shortening the surgery time with less bleeding, and allowing for quicker post-operation recovery. If the pregnancy duration is longer, the fetal bones have already formed, the uterus has increased in size, and the uterine wall has thinned, making the surgery more difficult, taking longer, resulting in more bleeding, prolonging the recovery period, and causing more suffering for the pregnant woman.

If the pregnancy exceeds 14 weeks, neither of the two aforementioned types of abortions can be performed, and instead, an induction must be done in the hospital, increasing the pain and risks for the pregnant woman. Although painless induction can reduce harm to the pregnant woman, compared to early-stage painless abortions, the damage from induction is significantly greater.
