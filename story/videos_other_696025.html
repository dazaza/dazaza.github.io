72 Fashion House: A 7-year-old boy fell from the 6th floor and was unharmed

by icwl6156 on 2012-03-07 19:20:34

Mr. Liu, aged 39, is Longlong's uncle by marriage and lives at Hengsheng South Garden on National South Road in Dafeng City. That noon, Longlong was picked up from kindergarten by his mother and brought to his uncle’s house, preparing to return to their hometown early in the afternoon. After lunch, Longlong's mother and aunt planned to go take a bath together, so they left Longlong in the care of Mr. Liu who was still at home at that time. By 2:30 PM, it was time for Mr. Liu to go to work. Seeing that his wife and sister-in-law hadn't returned yet, he turned on the computer to play cartoons for the child and repeatedly instructed Longlong, "Don't be naughty, watch TV obediently, your mom will be back soon." Then, he locked the door from the inside and went to work.

Afterwards, Mr. Liu learned that his neighbor on the first floor runs a store which is connected to the kitchen with aluminum skin covering. The store protrudes from the vertical face of the building. At that time, the neighbor was playing mahjong at home when they suddenly heard a "boom," startling everyone. They thought it was a flower pot from upstairs that had fallen down. Upon coming out, they saw a child lying on top of their kitchen roof. "The aluminum sheet was dented significantly." Looking further, they noticed a large crack on the plastic sunshade canopy of the fourth-floor neighbor's balcony. Mr. Liu analyzed the situation comprehensively and estimated that during Longlong's fall, he first hit the sunshade canopy on the fourth floor, then fell onto the roof of the store downstairs.

According to news reports, yesterday afternoon, seven-year-old Longlong (a pseudonym) climbed onto the window of the balcony using a stool in his home while there was no one around, trying to go outside. Unexpectedly, he fell from the sixth floor. Fortunately, due to the buffering effect of two obstacles in the air, Longlong landed unscathed.

Within half an hour of going to work, Mr. Liu received a call from his wife saying that Longlong had fallen from upstairs. When he rushed back home, many neighbors had already gathered. Surprisingly, Longlong was still playing happily as if nothing had happened. On the advice of the neighbors, the family took Longlong to Dafeng People's Hospital for a full-body checkup, and indeed, there were no issues found. When asked, Longlong also said he felt no pain and even impatiently remarked, "It's over, don't mention it again."

(Peter Yongfeng and Guo Xiaoquan)
