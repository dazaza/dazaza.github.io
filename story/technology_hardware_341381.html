Registration Process:

Registering a company in Hong Kong is simple. According to Hong Kong law, any individual (not necessarily a Hong Kong resident) or group can register and establish a Hong Kong limited company or purchase a shelf company upon meeting the conditions and procedures. After obtaining business registration, they can commence operations in Hong Kong. Unlike the domestic system, apart from some specialized fields such as finance, banking, healthcare, telecommunications, and tourism that require specific professional licenses, there are no restrictions on the scope of business for commercial registration in Hong Kong. Any business, including production, trade, wholesale, retail, services, colleges, exchange centers, research institutes, media groups, publishers, magazines, associations, federations, shipping agents, cosmetics, pharmaceuticals, auctions, groups, imports and exports, or food, can operate under one license.
