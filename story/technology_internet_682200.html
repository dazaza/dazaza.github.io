China Daily Network (by Zhang Huanrui, Ge Qiwei, and correspondent Xiong Xiaofei) - A 40-year-old female boss met a man ten years younger than her through QQ. After some time of QQ communication, they met on December 30th last year in the urban area. Unexpectedly, this meeting became the end of the female boss's life. The brutal net friend killed and dismembered the female boss and then dumped her body in the Xijiang River, while also withdrawing over RMB 15,000 from the female boss's bank card.

Police involvement pointed various evidence towards "Martian language"

Online flaunting wealth and mutual confession

On Christmas Eve night of December 25th, after having heated discussions online, they finally proposed a meeting request. "Martian language" invited Chen Ling to meet at his rented apartment in Zhaoqing city on December 30th. Chen Ling readily agreed. Soon it was December 30th, and Chen Ling arrived early in the morning from Sihui to Duanzhou urban area. Around 10 AM, the two people who had only been in contact through the internet finally met. The long-suppressed fictional emotions finally erupted in reality. When Chen Ling thought she had found the man of her life, an unexpected event occurred. The usually considerate net friend "Martian language" suddenly changed, revealing his true nature. He strangled Chen Ling with a cable from behind and tied her to a chair. At the same time, he robbed all the cash she carried. Faced with this sudden situation, Chen Ling was already terrified.

To avoid alarming the suspect prematurely, the police actively searched for Chen Ling's whereabouts while secretly investigating suspect Li Mou. When the officers reviewed the video surveillance around the suspect's rented neighborhood, they discovered that on December 30th, the suspect entered the community with a woman and the next day transported a large bag out of the community, heading towards Xiao Xiang direction. A good premonition immediately arose in the minds of the investigators. On January 19th, the police suddenly received a report from a fisherman who discovered a large bag by the Xijiang River containing a highly decomposed female corpse that had been dismembered. The police decisively took action. While dispatching forensic experts to identify the victim's identity, they arrested suspect Li Mou. The identification results soon came out, confirming that the dismembered body was indeed the female victim, Chen Ling. Facing the ironclad facts, suspect Li Mou confessed to the crime of killing the female victim for financial gain.

On February 1st, the Guangdong Zhaoqing Duanzhou police reported to the media that after more than 20 days of meticulous investigation, they successfully solved the "12.30" robbery murder dismemberment case, arresting the robbery murder suspect Chen Mou, known as "Martian language" on QQ.

In today's information age, the internet brings convenience to our work, life, and study, but it also provides a new platform for criminal activities. In recent years, many internet users have become enthusiastic about QQ online dating. The police here warn women to be cautious when making friends online, careful not to be scammed financially or sexually. If you encounter a situation similar to Chen Ling's, be sure to raise your vigilance. Do not be blinded by the so-called emotions at the moment. Respect yourself and do not easily go to the other person's residence or hotel. If you are in danger, report it to the police promptly.

Just as "Martian language" had plundered all of Chen Ling's belongings, unexpectedly, "Martian language" became even more audacious, demanding the passwords for the more than ten bank cards Chen Ling carried on her. Helpless, Chen Ling had no choice but to reveal all the passwords for her bank cards. However, what Chen Ling feared most happened. The ruthless suspect returned to the rental apartment after withdrawing over RMB 5,000 from the bank, strangling Chen Ling until she suffocated to death. To transport the body, suspect "Martian language" dismembered Chen Ling and threw her body into the Xijiang River.

Later, a person named "Martian language" began frequently chatting with Chen Ling via QQ. Due to boredom, Chen Ling was willing to use this method for entertainment. After some time of intense chatting, terms like "treasure" and "dear" appeared without reservation in their chat records. They affectionately called each other, creating an illusion of being a loving couple. During their chats, Chen Ling learned that the other party was ten years younger than her and also told him that she independently operated a bookstore. Although unmarried, she had accumulated a certain amount of savings. In just a few months of online interaction, apart from not meeting face-to-face, there were no secrets left between them (Chen Ling revealed all her privacy).

On January 5th, the case was transferred to the Zhaoqing Duanzhou police for handling. After understanding the basic case details, the leadership of the Duanzhou Public Security Bureau took it very seriously and immediately formed a special task force by drawing personnel from the Criminal Investigation Team and the Jiaoyuan Police Station. During the investigation, the task force discovered that on December 31st, a man wearing a motorcycle helmet and gloves withdrew over RMB 5,000 in cash from three of the victim Chen Ling's bank cards. Who was this man? Was Chen Ling kidnapped? The police continued their in-depth investigation. When reviewing Chen Ling's QQ chat records, "Martian language" finally came under the police's investigative radar. Through the chat records, it was discovered that Chen Ling went to Zhaoqing to meet this man named "Martian language." Thus, the police conducted an in-depth investigation around this QQ user named "Martian language." On January 15th, they finally identified that "Martian language" was actually surnamed Li and currently rented an apartment in a residential area in the urban district.

Passionate Meeting Ends in Tragedy

One day in late October 2011, Chen Ling (a pseudonym), a single female boss who had been running a book business in Sihui for many years, turned on her computer as usual in her store. Since business was slow in the morning, she directly logged onto QQ to chat with net friends. About an hour later, a flashing notification in the lower right corner of her computer caught Chen Ling's attention. She skillfully clicked on the notification, which turned out to be a request from someone with the QQ name "Martian language" asking to add her as a friend. Without much thought, Chen Ling added the person as a friend.

At the age of 40, the unmarried female boss met "Martian language" online.
