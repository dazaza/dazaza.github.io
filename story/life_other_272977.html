Whether it is true or false, give it a try! My wishes for all people in the world ---- good health!!!!

Currently, the H1N1 flu is rampant around the world. The US has declared a state of emergency, Canada is offering free vaccinations nationwide, and more than 50,000 people in China have been infected with the virus.

Last week, I had to cancel two luncheon meetings, and this week, several luncheon meetings are also uncertain whether they will take place because many people are sick (not necessarily with H1N1). This flu has indeed caused a lot of trouble and inconvenience in people's lives.

A Canadian friend sent me and some other friends the email below. I translated it and am sharing it with everyone, hoping that it will be helpful to you. I hope everyone stays healthy and safe without getting sick. Some of the information relates to our traditional Chinese medicine and Chinese diet.

When someone dies, a doctor visits various farms to see if he can help people fight the flu. Many farmers and their families were infected with the flu, and many died as a result. When the doctor visited one family, surprisingly, everyone in this household was very healthy.

The doctor asked what made this family different from others. The wife of this family said that she placed an unpeeled onion in every room of her house (probably just two rooms at that time).

The doctor found it hard to believe, so he asked if he could have an onion to examine under a microscope. She gave him one. When the doctor observed it, he really found flu bacteria on the onion. The onion absorbed the bacteria, which kept this family healthy.

Recently, I heard a similar story from my hairdresser in Arizona.

She said that a few years ago, many of her employees caught the flu, and so did many of her customers.

The following year, she placed a few fruit bowls with onions in her salon. Surprisingly, none of her employees got sick. It seems that onions really work...

The essence of the story is: buy some onions and place them in fruit bowls around your home.

If you work in an office, put a few onions in your office, under your desk, or on top of a cabinet. Try it out and see how it works. We did it last year, and no one caught the flu.

If this can help you and your loved ones avoid catching a cold, then that would be great. If you still catch a cold, perhaps the symptoms will be milder.

No matter what, what do you have to lose? Just a few onions!

Here’s the follow-up. I sent this to my friend in Orleans who often gives me health advice. She replied with an interesting experiment about onions:

I didn't know the story about the farmer, but I know I had pneumonia. Let me tell you, I was really sick. I read an article suggesting cutting both ends off an onion, sticking it on a fork, and placing the fork in a vase next to the patient at night. It said that the onion would turn black by morning due to absorbing the bacteria. The onion looked terrible, but I started feeling better. The article also mentioned that onions and garlic placed in houses saved many lives during the Black Death many years ago.
