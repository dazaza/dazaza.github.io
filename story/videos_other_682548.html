Looking back at Ding Junhui's performance over the past six years in his home country, we can see a strange phenomenon. In the first matches of these 12 tournaments, Ding Junhui achieved an 8-win 4-loss record. Among the 8 wins, except for the relatively easy victories in the 2007 and 2009 Shanghai tournaments, the remaining 6 games were very difficult, either 5-3 or 5-4. Currently, it seems unlikely that this situation will change in the short term. Ding Junhui can only try to adapt to this pressure and strive to convert it into motivation for better performance.
