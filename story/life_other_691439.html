In the car rental industry, service prices vary greatly across different seasons, regions, and periods. The sampling data is limited to the period from February 22 to the highest road rights in February. It should be specially noted that quotations for other times and different regions may not be comparable.

During the investigation, the reporter found that these three companies' vehicle models basically cover types such as compact cars, sedans, mid-to-high-end cars, SUVs, and MPVs.
