Teenage girl quarrels with her mother and jumps from the third floor, suffering fractures (Picture)

by xue94fwsh on 2012-03-05 14:08:46

On January 30th, at Hunan Province Children's Hospital, a 15-year-old girl named Xiaolong (a pseudonym) lay on the hospital bed receiving an IV drip. Her mother stood beside her, deeply worried. The previous evening, after a quarrel between mother and daughter, Xiaolong jumped from the third floor.

Reported by journalist Hua Jian

Written by reporter Shaolong Liu, correspondent Juxiu Liu, Changsha report

"Mom favors my brother, that's why I jumped."

"I don't favor anyone, I treat my son and daughter equally."

Yesterday morning, in the orthopedics ward of Hunan Province Children's Hospital, the 15-year-old girl Xiaolong (a pseudonym) from Anhua, Yiyang said the reason she couldn't let go was because she thought her mom favored her younger brother. Ms. Liao, her mother, sorrowfully said she treated both children equally.

The previous evening, after coming to Changsha for a reunion, Xiaolong had a few words with her mother and then jumped from the third floor, resulting in a comminuted fracture of both lower limbs.

After a quarrel, the daughter jumped from the third floor

During the Spring Festival, Ms. Liao brought her daughter Xiaolong and her 9-year-old son from their hometown to Changsha for the New Year celebration. On the seventh day of the first lunar month, the mother took the siblings to the supermarket. Seeing her favorite yogurt on the shelf, Xiaolong picked up two boxes while her younger brother demanded chicken legs.

Ms. Liao said she planned to buy two of each item so that each child could have one. She asked Xiaolong to share one box of yogurt with her younger brother, but Xiaolong refused.

After leaving the supermarket in anger, Xiaolong went to play with her cousin and didn't return until around 6 p.m. She was still upset when she returned and told her mother she wanted to go back to Yiyang. Her mother informed her that there was only one seat left on the bus going to Yiyang and it was too late to return that night. They would all go home together the next day.

Ms. Liao said that her daughter then angrily said, "If you won't let me go home, I'll jump off the building." In an instant, her daughter leapt from the third floor of the rented apartment, "It happened too fast... I thought she was just saying it out of anger, I didn't think she'd really jump."

"Mom, Mom..." came the painful cries of Xiaolong from downstairs. Ms. Liao rushed down, held her child, and immediately headed to Hunan Province Children's Hospital.

Orthopedic doctor Huang Shengxiang said that Xiaolong suffered from comminuted fractures of both lower limbs. The fractures may have affected the growth plate, which could leave some residual symptoms for her future ankle development, such as uneven leg lengths.

When the doctor asked Xiaolong why she jumped, Xiaolong replied, "Mom favors my brother."

Living with her grandmother since she was two years old

Ms. Liao said that Xiaolong had been living with her grandmother since she was two years old, spending very little time with her. Except during holidays, Xiaolong usually ate and lived with her grandmother. Normally, Xiaolong didn't talk much; if her parents didn't buy her what she liked, she would throw a tantrum.

In Ms. Liao's view, her daughter barely communicated with her, "Ever since she was little, she never cuddled up to me and acted cute." When asked about Xiaolong's academic performance, who is currently in the third year of junior high school, Ms. Liao said it was okay, but she wasn't sure about the specific scores. What does her daughter like to eat? She wasn't quite sure either.

Ms. Liao said that due to being separated for a long time, Xiaolong developed an independent and sensitive personality. Xiaolong also has a 9-year-old younger brother, who was also sent to be raised by their grandmother in the countryside. Ms. Liao said that in Xiaolong's mind, she always believed that her mother favored her younger brother, showing preference for boys over girls.

Regarding her daughter's thoughts, Ms. Liao felt very hurt, "How could I favor anyone? They're both my children!"

[Dialogue with Daughter]

"I love mom, but mom is a bit biased"

A 15-year-old girl, Xiaolong, argued with her mom and jumped three floors. Yesterday, after Xiaolong calmed down, the reporter had a conversation with this mother-daughter pair, trying to untie the knot in the child's heart.

Reporter: Why did you jump at the time? Weren't you scared?

Xiaolong: It hurts, now I can't move my legs. When I jumped, I only thought that mom was too biased, giving everything to my brother and not understanding or caring about me. (As Xiaolong said this, Ms. Liao, her mother, sobbed in the corridor outside the ward.)

Reporter: Why do you think mom is biased and doesn't care about you?

Xiaolong: Mom just treats my brother better, rarely talks to me, we spend too little time together, I want to be with mom.

Reporter: Is mom working hard outside? Do you love mom?

Xiaolong: I know mom works hard, our family owes money, so mom has to work. I do love mom, but mom is a bit biased, so I don't like it.

[Dialogue with Mom]

"I want to tell my daughter how much I love her"

Reporter: Your daughter thinks you are biased, what do you think?

Ms. Liao: My child and I see each other once a year, but I really treat both children equally. My son is more obedient, my daughter is more stubborn, but I truly haven't been biased. Our family owes money, I have no choice but to work outside, which results in less time spent with my daughter.

Reporter: Do you know about your daughter's daily life and studies?

Ms. Liao: I only know that my daughter's grades are okay, but I'm not clear on the specifics. We spend too little time together, indeed, I don't care enough for her.

Reporter: After something like this happens, what will you do in the future?

Ms. Liao: No matter what, I will take my daughter with me, tell her how much I love her, just as much as I love her younger brother.

(At this point, the mother and daughter hugged tightly, Xiaolong kissed her mother's cheek.)

Reported by Shaolong Liu, Correspondent Juxiu Liu

[Psychological Expert]

It's best to keep your child close, spend more time with her

Upon hearing about the incident involving Ms. Liao and her daughter Xiaolong, Professor Zhong Yan, director of the Child Health Department at Hunan Province Children's Hospital, said that Xiaolong rarely lives with her parents, fitting the status of a "left-behind child." Due to the long-term lack of parental affection, Xiaolong's inner world is closed off, making it easy to form characteristics such as self-abandonment, lack of confidence, and difficulty getting along with others and communicating well. Her psychological resilience is weak, even leading to feelings of abandonment, all of which severely impact the healthy psychological development of the child. Parents should spend more time with their children, bring them closer, and use love to melt the closed-off hearts of their children.
