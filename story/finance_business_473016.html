The suspect Li gathered several patient friends to establish fake companies to falsely hang insured participants. They repeatedly participated in the insurance in Zhuhai and Nanning and frequently saw doctors, suspected of defrauding the social security fund to pay high medical expenses. Moreover, the insured persons also resold surplus drugs obtained from frequent doctor visits to make profits.

Ultimately, the investigators locked on the main suspect of this case, "Xie", who was the head of an insured enterprise, Xiangzhou Trading Company in Zhuhai, and currently resides elsewhere. "Xie" surrendered to the Zhuhai Economic Investigation Team on March 9th.

At the end of 2010, during the medical treatment review work, the Zhuhai Social Security Fund Center found that eight insured persons including Xie were suspected of defrauding the social insurance fund: one uremic patient's labor relationship was hung under the name of an enterprise, four uremic patients were all employees of a certain trading company in Zhuhai, and all four employees of this company had undergone kidney transplant surgery, at the address of Guangzhou Ocean Park; the other three uremic patients were the only three employees of a certain trading company in Zhuhai.
