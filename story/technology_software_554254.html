Citizens buy one apple at the high price of 8800 yuan (picture)

by wenkuon18 on 2011-11-14 10:53:48

Staff introduced Zhaotong apples to the citizens of our city, reported by Zhou Ge. How much can an apple sell for? Yesterday morning, a Zhaotong Apple Chongqing promotion and exhibition was held in Chaotianmen Square. A red apple from Zhaotong was eventually auctioned off to a citizen surnamed Ma for a high price of 8,800 yuan. This is also currently the highest price for an apple sold in the southwestern region.

Yesterday at Chaotianmen Square, a gentle breeze accompanied by drizzle made the temperature pleasant, with many citizens coming to play. In the center of the square was an exhibition tower made of apples, and on one side of the square was the apple sales booth of the Zhaotong municipal government. The piles of apples attracted a large number of Chongqing citizens, and the staff responsible for promoting the apples were constantly peeling them. The peeled apples were quickly eaten up by the citizens.

Grandpa Lu from Jiangbei held an apple in his mouth and soon swallowed it satisfactorily. "It's so delicious. This apple is so crisp, has a good texture, and contains a lot of water. Young man, pack me two boxes." Grandpa Lu told the reporter that he had bad teeth but didn't like eating soft apples. Zhaotong apples are crispy and don't require too much effort to eat, and they taste good.

The reporter saw that citizens buying apples formed long queues, and within less than an hour, the mountain of apples behind the booth was reduced by half.

The most eye-catching part of yesterday's exhibition was the auction of the "King of Apples." The reporter noticed that the "King of Apples" was much larger than ordinary apples. The staff introduced that the "King of Apples" has a diameter of 15 centimeters and weighs 0.8 kilograms, carefully selected from the apple fields. From its appearance, the "King of Apples" does not look as red as other apples, but its pinkish skin makes it look more distinctive.

Mayor Wang Minzheng of Zhaotong City, Yunnan Province, told reporters that due to the particularly strong sunshine in Zhaotong, such a pinkish apple is the most authentic Zhaotong apple.

The auction started at 3,000 yuan, increasing to 3,500, 3,800...6,500, 6,800... After 24 rounds of bidding, a citizen surnamed Ma bought the "King of Apples" for 8,800 yuan.

How should an 8,800-yuan apple be handled? Mr. Ma told reporters that he does business in Chongqing and often travels to Zhaotong on business. Every time he brings back apples, they are snatched up by relatives and friends. He will take this "King of Apples" home to treasure.

Reported by Wang Yufeng

Mayor of Zhaotong personally promoted

To promote Zhaotong apples, Mayor Wang Minzheng of Zhaotong City, Yunnan Province, ate an unpeeled apple in front of the citizens. Seeing this action, a procurement officer buying gifts for the company immediately expressed his intention to buy 500 boxes.

Wang Minzheng was eating an apple while introducing to the citizens that only such unpolluted apples could be eaten with the skin on. In fact, eating apples with the skin on provides the highest nutrition, which is also the best-tasting apple in the world.

Why dare to call their apples the best-tasting in the world? Wang Minzheng told reporters that the taste of Zhaotong apples even surpasses that of Japanese Fuji apples, the reason being the amount of sunlight. Japanese apples usually need reflective mirrors to borrow sunlight, while Zhaotong apples often need film added on top to avoid excessive sunlight causing water loss, ensuring the taste of the apples. This results in the sweetness and sourness of Zhaotong apples being significantly stronger than other apples.

Hurry to Chaotianmen to purchase

To facilitate Chongqing citizens in purchasing Zhaotong apples, the Zhaotong municipal government specifically set up two apple sales points at Caoyuanba Fruit Market and Chaotianmen Square. Today, citizens can go to Chaotianmen for free tasting and purchase.

If you miss today's purchase deadline, you can still buy at the Caoyuanba fruit wholesale market. Currently, Zhaotong apples are being promoted in the Chongqing market at about 6 yuan per kilogram, 45 yuan per box when packaged, and are expected to rise to 10 yuan per kilogram in the future.

Creating the best-tasting apple

Yesterday, at the Zhaotong Apple Chongqing Promotion Exhibition, the Zhaotong municipal government embraced the "golden baby" - signing 40 agreements with a total investment reaching 291 million yuan. Among these, it includes building two apple cold storage facilities in Zhaotong.

Wang Minzheng introduced that the planting area of Zhaotong apples is 300,000 mu, with a production of around 450,000 tons and a value reaching 650 million yuan, including a large number of premium apple varieties such as Red Fuji, New Red Star, Jonagold, Shinseiki, 2001, Red King, and American No. 8.

After this successful investment attraction, Zhaotong will utilize its unique ecological, quality, early maturity, and geographical advantages to create the best-tasting apple in China.

Action

2

Three strategies to combat counterfeit products

Why has Zhaotong apples become such a large-scale industry? The reporter exclusively interviewed Zhaotong Mayor Wang Minzheng.

Reporter: Were Zhaotong apples previously sold in the Chongqing market?

Wang Minzheng: Our staff visited the Caoyuanba Fruit Market and Panxi Fruit Market in Chongqing some time ago. When we asked fruit vendors if they had sold Zhaotong apples, they all said they hadn't heard of them. However, Chongqing people love eating apples. If we lost this market, it would be quite regrettable. Therefore, we must bring Zhaotong apples to Chongqing so everyone can taste and fall in love with them.

Reporter: How will Zhaotong apples enter the Chongqing market?

Wang: First, we will invite businessmen to visit us in Zhaotong for inspection, sign sales contracts with enterprises in Chongqing, and deliver goods directly to their doorsteps; establish special apple sales points in Chongqing so Chongqing citizens can buy genuine Zhaotong apples.

Reporter: Are there any counterfeit Zhaotong apples?

Wang: Each Zhaotong apple has its own mark. To prevent counterfeits, we will also set up dedicated anti-counterfeit personnel in the Chongqing market. Upon discovering fake Zhaotong apples, we will resolutely report and destroy them.
