by mimi456 on 2012-02-05 23:01:44

Dreams make me lose dreams, dreams make me hurt.

His hands are soft, and the feeling of being held is warm, all the way to the bottom of my heart.

I forgot when was the last time we held hands. Hehe, in the past, he liked to hold my hand directly. Come to think of it, he hasn't naturally grabbed my hand like before for nearly half a year. I miss him.

Thinking about him under the quilt, waiting for his message, I sent him a text on Friday afternoon. It's been two days and he still hasn't replied to me. This weekend is almost over. I've been waiting for him. Every time my phone rings, I'm full of hope, but then------

Not long ago, I had made up my mind not to look for him or contact him anymore. I only lasted 20 days and then completely broke down. Why am I so indecisive? Is loving him enough?

I don't remember when he started treating me so perfunctorily. Actually, I shouldn't have told him what I truly feel inside. Maybe the once ambiguous distance between us was just right. But we broke the silence and entered each other's space, hurting each other. We can't sincerely face each other. Our situations prevent us from truly integrating into each other's lives. So these two straight lines, after intersecting, will separate again, getting further and further away. This is the destiny of bravely loving.

Did he care about me? There should have been a time when he did. I believe what he said: "It's just that work is too busy---". He should be a man who values emotions. I deeply love him.

I didn't have the courage to go with him before because I didn't have the capital. I had to maintain my pitiful self-esteem. I wanted to wait until I could support myself and then let go of my feelings and seriously love him. However, by the time I thought I had matured, he had already flown higher. I can't keep up with his speed, so I missed him. Just passerby A, passing by in a hurry, just a guest, but deeply influencing everything about me. My heart always has him.

I told myself: Don't look for him anymore, don't disturb his life, and take good care of your own happiness. When I can really stand on my own feet in this city, I'll let go of everything and chase after him. So the premise is that I am still young. Therefore, I can only work twice as hard. Perhaps a person can only accommodate one person in their heart in a lifetime. He moved in, but I can't be by his side.

Feng, I want to find you, take your hand, hold it tightly, and then listen to me gently telling you how much I love you.
