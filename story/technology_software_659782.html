Zhang Yuqing - Relaxing the Connection with Ancient Service Industry

Accelerating the development of the service industry, especially modern service industry, and increasing its proportion in the national economy, is the inevitable path to promote the adjustment of our city's economic structure and change the way of development. It is also a breakthrough to effectively alleviate the bottleneck constraint of energy shortage and improve the utilization rate of resources. We must seize the opportunity of "connecting with Greater Shanghai and integrating into the Yangtze River Delta", speed up the connection work of modern services, and promote the rapid development of modern services in our city.

In the process of developing the service industry, our city needs to focus on key areas of connection. First, production-oriented services, especially logistics industry. Compared with Shanghai, Jincheng has made great progress in life-oriented services since reform and opening-up, but the development of production-oriented services, especially logistics industry, lags behind. Based on practical considerations, we need to build logistics parks around the characteristics of Jincheng's industries and create public logistics information platforms; accelerate the construction of transportation infrastructure, construct an industrial products logistics system mainly consisting of coal, fertilizers, and industrial raw materials, build a centralized, efficient, and modern daily consumer goods logistics system, and establish agricultural product storage, cold fresh delivery, processing packaging, inspection and quarantine logistics systems. Second, tourism. Jincheng has abundant tourism resources and unique advantages. Building a mature tourism industry is an important part of our city's service industry development. We need to integrate natural scenery and cultural resources, create tourism brands, and enhance the quality and level of tourism products. Third, public services. Through the connection with Shanghai, we can promote the development of public welfare services mainly consisting of education, health, and sports, livelihood services mainly consisting of employment, social security, poverty alleviation, and assistance, and fundamental services mainly consisting of public facilities and public safety.

Zhao Jilun - Industrial Connection Requires Selecting Breakthrough Points

In the cooperation and development with Shanghai, we need to find the gaps between the two parties, see our own advantages and potential, select breakthrough points, find connection points, establish long-term cooperation mechanisms, and achieve win-win cooperation for both sides.

First, develop the auto parts industry. Shanghai is a relatively developed city in China's automobile industry, where General Motors and Volkswagen have laid out car production. In recent years, our city's automobile parts industry has developed rapidly. Especially during the "12th Five-Year Plan" period, Zhezhou County will comprehensively build a top-tier equipment manufacturing park in Shanxi Province in Bagon Industrial Park and construct the largest automobile parts production base in North China. The automotive parts produced by Fuchuan Iron & Steel will officially start production in the first half of next year. We need to use the automobile parts as a breakthrough point and accelerate project connection. Second, develop the optoelectronic industry. The optoelectronic and solar energy industries have great potential. New energy enterprises like Tangyi, Shuoguang Optoelectronics, and Ronggao Solar Energy have good cooperation prospects with Shanghai. Third, develop the sericulture industry. We need to leverage our advantages and strengthen project connections. Jincheng is the largest sericulture base in North China. Clothing and home textile products produced by enterprises such as Lanzhou Hemp and Jilier have occupied a certain market share domestically. Shanghai was once a relatively developed textile industry city in China, so there is much room for cooperation. Fourth, develop the tourism industry. Jincheng has rich tourism resources, and the development of tourism and modern service industries has huge development space. We need to select breakthrough points, take the initiative to connect, strengthen cooperation with Shanghai, and develop cooperation in broader fields and larger spaces.
