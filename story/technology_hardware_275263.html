Xiaoyue's Archives Blogs Albums Videos Shares I can see me in your eyes Homepage Sohu Blog > Xiaoyue > Blogs > My Diary 2006-12-03 | Meeting the Ticket Miss Tags: air tickets, miss, meeting, going home The case was finally calm at the end of the month after being busy. The storm has passed and good days should be left. Moreover, it is almost time for the Spring Festival, so I can go home! I found that preparing to go home is a super happy thing, extremely exciting. I started searching online for discounted air tickets. Air tickets to Kunming during the Spring Festival are usually quite tight because tourism is still very popular. By chance, I found a 50% discount ticket, so I quickly called to ask. The ticketing staff said there was no problem, then asked carefully for my name, ID number, etc. I felt a wave of happiness inside. At the end, she actually said: "I will reserve a seat for you, but the ticket cannot be issued yet due to it being too early. I will notify you when it's time..." I was stunned. Anyway, I originally planned to just wait for the notification. When strolling past Huameida, I still couldn't let go, so the two of us went in to check. The welcoming staff were very polite, saying that the person selling tickets wasn't there, but they would call her back immediately. We had to wait a bit. I thought we should leave, but since we had already called her back, it wouldn't be good to leave like this. Luckily, there were some nice magazines there, and the enthusiastic staff brought us hot water. The service at Huameida is pretty good. Well, let's wait... After about 15 minutes, the ticketing staff finally arrived, rushing in. She might have felt a little embarrassed for making us wait, so she immediately said: "Let me issue the tickets for you." Thinking I had made a mistake, I repeated: "I want tickets for the Spring Festival period. Can you issue them?" She said: "Yes, I'll handle it for you right away!" Wow, did she forget that she said on the phone that the tickets couldn't be issued? Anyway, as long as they could be issued, I didn't care too much. So I sat next to her and watched her issue the tickets. She was very friendly, and we chatted. She said her car was stolen last night, and the insurance company wouldn't compensate. I felt sorry for her. Suddenly, I felt that the distance between us wasn't so far. She was very helpful and even tried to apply for a 45% discount for us, although it wasn't approved, I still thanked her. In the end, she issued two 50% discount tickets, costing 1600 yuan in total. It was all over, and I suddenly felt that spending the money was very satisfying... Originally, I planned to spend Christmas at Victoria Harbor in Hong Kong, buying things like a camera, skincare products, etc. But now it seems I need to save money to go home for the New Year, alas~~~ Let's go later~~~~~ From now on, every day that passes makes me very happy because I'm getting closer to going home. | Comments (5) | Read Category (My Diary) | Published at 16:01 Tip: "Permanent Link" shows the fixed link to this article.
