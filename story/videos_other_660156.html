On September 29th, Shunde Court believed that Dai neglected national laws, with the intention of illegal possession, and during the execution of the contract, defrauded the victim's property, in an especially huge amount, constituting the crime of contract fraud. As such, he was sentenced to 13 years in prison, deprived of political rights for 2 years, and fined 13,000 yuan. The frozen deposits and sealed cars will be returned to the victim Tengyue Company.

Dai, dissatisfied, appealed to Foshan Intermediate People's Court.

In the trial, Dai retracted his confession, believing that he did not defraud Tengyue Company of project funds, and thus did not commit a crime.

The defense lawyer also questioned the audit report used as evidence for sentencing. He said that the audit clearly stated the lack of design drawings and on-site certification materials, etc., "the content of the conclusion has many uncertain factors." Moreover, Dai has always been actively fulfilling his contractual obligations, completing the installation, dismantling, and maintenance of the "hoist," merely involving the confirmation and settlement of actual project volumes, not falling under the relevant provisions of criminal law.

In the courtroom, Dai retracted his confession, believing that he did not defraud Tengyue Company of project funds, and thus did not commit a crime.

Shunde Procuratorate charged that since 1997, Dai had formed a long-term contractual cooperation relationship with Guangdong Tengyue Construction Engineering Co., Ltd. (hereinafter referred to as "Tengyue Company"), providing installation, dismantling, and maintenance services for material hoists (commonly known as "hoists") at Tengyue Company's construction sites. Between the end of 2007 and February 2010, Dai exaggerated his project volume and project funds through false reporting of project volumes, modifying settlement amounts, forging supervisor signatures, and repeated settlements, with the aim of defrauding Tengyue Company of project funds. After auditing, in nine projects developed by a well-known domestic real estate developer in places like Qingyuan, Zhaoqing, and Gaoming, Dai should have actually received project funds of 2,135,485.16 yuan, but Tengyue Company paid Dai 5,104,047.24 yuan through bank transfers. Thus, Dai defrauded Tengyue Company of a total of 3,760,915.58 yuan in project funds, with 792,353.50 yuan being unfulfilled attempts.

Dai's defense lawyer believed that this case was essentially a contractual dispute between Tengyue Company and Dai, a typical civil dispute. The investigative authorities using criminal means to intervene in civil disputes raised suspicions of abuse of public power. Whether Dai exaggerated the project volume and overcharged for project funds could be fully clarified by verifying relevant evidence materials, then resolved through negotiation or directly filed with the court according to the contract agreement.

After reviewing, Shunde Court believed that Dai's retraction of his confession lacked sufficient reason, and the appraisal procedures were legal. The opinions proposed by the defense counsel lacked sufficient reason and lacked contrary evidence, hence they were not adopted.

South China Morning Post reported (reporter/Jiameng) that foreman Dai had cooperated with a well-known domestic real estate developer for over ten years. Last year, he was accused of defrauding project funds worth over 3.7 million yuan through false reporting of project volumes and modifying settlement orders. Shunde Court ruled in the first instance that he committed the crime of contract fraud, sentencing him to 13 years in prison. In response, Dai and his defense lawyer insisted that the case was a civil dispute. Recently, Dai has appealed to Foshan Intermediate People's Court.
