ZIPPO's Play Styles

To bring out the maximum charm of ZIPPO, it needs to be accompanied by the posture during ignition as sold by crown shops on Taobao [http://www.taoiei.com]. Therefore, the technique of lighting also has levels ranging from ignorant, beginner, intermediate, advanced, and top-level. This is reflected in whether you light it like a child, with both hands, with one hand, or even with the top-level aerial somersault style.

Here, we introduce several styles of playing with a ZIPPO, including the common basic style and the top-level aerial somersault style.

(1) Basic Style (The Basic) - This is the simplest technique, suitable for beginners.

(2) Around the World (Around The World) - Place the bottom of the ZIPPO towards you, with the hinge side up, and use your knuckles around the right side of the lighter. The other edges are lightly wrapped with your middle finger and thumb. Next, push your middle finger while placing the other fingers on the lid, quickly flipping the ZIPPO so that the bottom faces up. Gently release your middle finger, your index finger and thumb. Once the ZIPPO opens, use your middle finger to spin the flint wheel. Tip: This method is more suitable for older ZIPPO lighters.

(3) The Dragon (The Dragon) - With the hinge facing down, firmly hold the ZIPPO with your middle finger, index finger, and ring finger. When opening the lid and turning the ZIPPO downward, ensure you have enough control over the lighter cover with your fingers. The enchanting ZIPPO will flip between your ring finger and middle finger, and use your thumb to turn the flint wheel to ignite.

(4) Snap (The Snap)

Place the bottom of the ZIPPO on your thumb, with your index and middle fingers on the lid, ensuring the hinge faces you, and firmly hold the ZIPPO. Next, quickly slide your index and middle fingers toward the hinge side and grab the side of the lighter, with your thumb on the other side. If done correctly, the lighter cover will open. (Be careful not to let the ZIPPO fly out of the room!) Once the cover is open, turn the flint wheel to ignite.
