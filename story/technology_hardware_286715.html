The addition of attributes for lovers, I have mentioned in many articles. You can see it by entering the lover section of our 07073 website. However, there is basically no information on how to add attributes for pets. Below, I will introduce the attribute addition for lover pets.

Currently, the main factors affecting pet attack power in the game are strength, intelligence, and agility. Luck increases critical hit chances. It's important to note that besides adding to attack power, agility also adds to attack speed, making it a very crucial attribute. Strength only adds to attack power and adds more compared to the other two attributes. Intelligence adds to the maximum magic points. The specific way to add attributes for pets mainly depends on the growth of the three attributes: strength, agility, and intelligence. Whichever has higher growth should be prioritized. Remember that thing called "Pet Source"? Use that to increase your pet's attributes. For luck, it depends on personal preference; if increased, it will raise the critical hit chance.

Also, just like characters, pets' attributes increase after reincarnation.

This is purely my personal experience. Everyone can try it out with a small account first! Don't feed the source given to beginners to your pet at the start. Wait until you have 46500 Sky Stones to buy "Little Cutie" and then feed it!

Level 1: Add all attribute points to strength (add all to strength before level 20, add all to endurance between levels 20-25). Have the beginner equipment (don't sell the weapon!), go talk to the mayor near the house, then claim the quest reward, and run to the Mushroom Forest to kill level 1 monsters until you reach level 3.

Level 3: Go back to the village and find the elder (the elder is below the mayor), talk to him, complete and submit the quest, then go kill blue mushroom monsters. Collect the blue fruits until you can submit the quest.

Level 4: After finishing the White Magic Pig quest, you can level up to 5. Go to the Flame Forest and finish all quests!... Then continue leveling up, finding people, and earn as much money as possible until you have around 300,000 gold, until you reach level 13!

Level 13: Sell the level 1 sword, spend money to buy all equipment (around level 13), don't buy duplicates, then purchase Ice Diamonds, combine them all to level 3, and embed them into the equipment. This way, the energy ball will become much larger!
