Eyebrow lift surgery can make your dream of having beautiful eyebrows come true.

by shww on 2012-03-06 11:37:14

Having beautiful eyebrows can make a person more beautiful, while unattractive eyebrows can greatly detract from one's overall appearance and make them appear less spirited. The so-called brow lift surgery involves removing the unappealing eyebrows and creating a new brow shape to make us look more attractive.

The ideal candidates for brow lift surgery are typically in the age range of 25 to 60 years old. Through this surgery, the signs of aging can be significantly reduced. Of course, it can also help people of all ages who have deepened forehead or frown lines due to excessive stress or muscle movement. For patients looking to improve their eyebrow shape, those with failed permanent makeup or scars left after eyebrow removal are best treated with a brow lift.

Brow lift surgery is especially suitable for individuals over a certain age experiencing upper eyelid skin laxity. Patients who think they need upper eyelid surgery will find that brow lift surgery provides better results. It not only solves the problem of eyebrows but also addresses the issue of loose skin around the eyes, achieving two goals at once.

For those whose eyebrow shapes have become unappealing due to permanent makeup, such as having insect-like brows, overly thick brows, overly blue coloration, or scarring left from laser, electrocautery, or chemical depigmentation treatments, these issues can all be resolved through surgery by completely or partially removing the problematic eyebrows to achieve a more aesthetically pleasing brow shape. If necessary, eyebrows can be re-done six months after the surgery.

Contraindications for brow lift surgery: 1) Individuals with keloid-prone skin; 2) Those with coagulation disorders; 3) People with scars on the upper eyelid skin; 4) Those with droopy upper eyelids.
