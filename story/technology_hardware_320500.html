German Boyfriend is an Architect

Designed the New CCTV Building

Ole Scheeren appears most frequently in the entertainment and fashion sections of Chinese media. Most readers don't know who this man is, but he has a label - Zhang Ziyi's new boyfriend.

Ole Scheeren was born in 1971 in Karlsruhe, southwestern Germany. He graduated from the University of Lausanne in Switzerland and the University of Karlsruhe in Germany. He furthered his studies at the Architectural Association in London and won the Royal Institute of British Architects Silver Medal. In 1995, he joined the Office for Metropolitan Architecture (OMA) founded by Rem Koolhaas in the Netherlands and became a partner in 2002. He is currently the director of OMA's offices in Rotterdam and Beijing, responsible for OMA's work throughout Asia. He was the chief designer and supervisor of the construction of Beijing Central Television (CCTV) and the Television Cultural Center (TVCC). He is also in charge of projects like the Beijing Book Building, the upcoming Prada flagship store in Shanghai, and the Los Angeles County Museum of Art (LACMA).

Love for Rock and Photography: Before entering the field of architectural design, Ole's favorite art forms were photography, film, and literature. In high school, Ole formed a rock band with friends, but it disbanded quickly due to insufficient skill. Later, Ole took vocal lessons for a few years. However, Ole Scheeren's favorite art form is photography. He once shot a short film called Game102 about Seoul using film, which was screened at the Rotterdam Film Festival in 2001. Additionally, he has made some very conceptual short films and tried writing movie scripts. He once told the media, “If I weren't an architect, I would definitely make movies or become a writer.”
