The story tells the amusing growth stories of Yu Ziyang, Yu Ziyan and a group of young people around them. In Yawen Middle School, the twins meet all kinds of people. Among these people, the twins constantly perceive the reality and hypocrisy of human nature, while expressing their own emotions of love and hate, praising truth, goodness and beauty, and satirizing and condemning falsehood, ugliness and evil. In Yawen Middle School, the Yu siblings have experienced many interesting stories with their classmates, full of wit and humor, making people can't help laughing.

Fate can destroy me, but it can never defeat me.

Satire is my knife, humor is my sword; I type on the keyboard at high speed, who says I have no ideals.

We are a generation of nonconformists; but one day we will support the world.

This is a story about unconventional growth.

A completely youthful and growing story.

Part One of Unconventional Growth

Because of the rampant pickpockets on Guangzhou buses at that time, Wenzhou had lost his wallet several times. Once he had an idea, so he put a big pack of toilet paper in an envelope and placed it in his pocket, intending to catch the thief red-handed. However, he caught nothing, and unexpectedly found that the envelope was gone when he got off the bus. The next day, he took the same bus again, and when he got off, he found a package in his pocket. When he took it out, he found that it was the thing he had lost yesterday, with a note attached: Boss, please don't joke like this in the future, it will affect my normal income, thank you. Over time, Wenzhou's various abilities in anti-pickpocketing and theft prevention improved to varying degrees, and his ultimate anti-pickpocketing trick while riding was to firmly hold the wallet in his pocket with his hand.

Part Two of Unconventional Growth

Ziyang listened to the bragging for half a day and felt extremely bored, as if being tortured mentally and physically, either playing the lute to a cow or polluting hearing. So he gazed out of the window and indulged in melancholy. Suddenly, he saw the writer's son come back with a small backpack. As soon as he stepped into the house, he kicked the backpack away, followed by a "pop" sound as it landed on the ground. Guo Baichuan was startled by the sound, and upon seeing it was his son's backpack, he scolded, "Hey brat, you're back! Go play by yourself without disturbing your old man blowing his own horn!" The little guy gave the old guy a dirty look, pouted, and muttered, "Hmph, wait until my mom comes back, and see if you still act so superior!" Then without taking off his shoes, he jumped onto the sofa, sat next to Ziyang, and angrily asked, "Hey, stinky kid, who are you? Why are you sitting on my sofa at my house?"

Part Three of Unconventional Growth

On the day before the holiday, Qiaoyun specially came to find Ziyang and asked why he was angry with her. Ziyang was blinded by anger at that moment, so he stubbornly ignored her, just standing there blinking. Later, Qiaoyun finally figured out the reason, how could such a trivial matter be difficult for a talented girl with an IQ of 184? So she pretended to know nothing and said gently to him, "You haven't forgotten our agreement, right? You haven't forgotten, right? But it has been more than three days, and you are still mad at me!" After saying that, she threw down her phone number and stormed off. People are often like this, no matter how many good things you do for others, they won't remember, but one bad thing you do can make them remember forever. Ziyang stood there thinking for a long time, assuming that he had accidentally done something earth-shattering with her, and later recalled that he once made such a promise with her: no matter what happens, he should not be angry with her for more than twenty-four hours.

Part Four of Unconventional Growth

After breakfast, Ziyang wandered aimlessly on the school road, wandering around, looking here and there. After walking for a while, he noticed that more and more students were doing morning exercises, especially those girls who were greedy eaters. Because they were afraid of only gaining fat and not growing taller, they got up early to run and lose fat to grow taller. Some overweight girls were even scarier, actually wearing super short skirts to show off their meaty legs, shaking hula hoops vigorously while wildly twisting their big butts. Every time Ziyang witnessed this scene, he couldn't help but laugh, even laughing himself half to death. But today he didn't laugh at all, because his mood was too heavy.
