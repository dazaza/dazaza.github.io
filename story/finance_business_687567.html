Welfare Lottery: Exchange Experience and Learn from Each Other's Strengths

by bct999888 on 2012-03-02 16:39:51

In order to further standardize the construction of lottery stations in the city and improve the management level of refinement, standardization and target orientation for these stations, the Zhengzhou Welfare Lottery Center, based on the unified deployment of welfare lottery work across the province and combined with the experience of star-rated station construction from other regions, has issued relevant implementation plans for star-rated station construction and produced conceptual effect diagrams for pilot renovations. After the successful completion of the pilot work, the center will follow government procurement procedures to conduct open bidding, ensuring that star-rated betting stations are uniformly neat, aesthetically practical, and can represent the image of Zhengzhou's welfare lottery. In 2011, a total of 534 star-rated stations were evaluated in Zhengzhou, including 89 five-star stations, 178 four-star stations, and 267 three-star stations. Currently, this work is still underway.

● "Point-to-point, face-to-face" drive

Management by regional responsibility

In daily work, the leadership of the center requires managers to frequently visit stations for training and guidance related to business and marketing, promptly passing on good suggestions and methods to the salespeople at the betting stations, serving as their "sales consultants." To promote the advanced experiences and practices of top-performing betting stations throughout the city, the center often organizes seminars and gatherings, bringing together well-operated, service-oriented, high-sales stations with poorly managed, low-sales stations for communication and exchange. Station owners are encouraged to "speak from personal experience," analyze reasons, identify gaps, improve measures, and enhance sales.

● Strengthening the construction of star-rated stations

After comparison, over 30 capable towns were selected to set up lottery stations. Both site density was ensured and gaps were filled, allowing the layout of the city’s betting stations to operate along a positive development track. To quickly adapt new lottery stations to market needs, Zhengzhou provided them with many preferential policies.

Under the guidance of the working principle "seeking benefits through management, promoting development through services," in 2010, the city sold a total of 756 million yuan worth of welfare lotteries, an increase of 223 million yuan compared to 2009, representing a growth rate of 41.8%. Among these, computer-generated tickets sold for 554 million yuan, instant scratch-off tickets sold for 129 million yuan, and China Welfare Online sold for 73 million yuan. The total lottery sales accounted for 51.91% of the city's total lottery market share, raising more than 2.6 billion yuan in public welfare funds, of which 60 million yuan were retained at the municipal level, providing important financial support for the rapid development of the city's welfare undertakings.

The Zhengzhou Welfare Lottery Center continuously improves the ideological quality and professional ethics level of employees through regular or irregular centralized learning sessions; quarterly meeting review systems require administrators to write investigation reports, analyzing and reviewing the reasons for changes in sales volumes at the various supervised lottery stations, urging employees to do market research work well; and participation in lottery marketing knowledge and skills training comprehensively enhances the business management level of employees.

● Conducting business training for managers

● Optimizing layout, expanding township stations

Last year, in response to the Henan Province Welfare Lottery Center's call to "reasonably adjust the layout of stations and actively expand township stations," the Zhengzhou Welfare Lottery Center adhered to the principle of "selecting good stations, using the right people, proper training, and prior publicity," with center leadership leading teams into county and township areas for inspections.

Emphasizing grassroots training

To better motivate staff members, the Zhengzhou Welfare Lottery Center implements an internal evaluation system to quantify work content. Sales targets for the current year are determined based on previous years' sales figures, with managers responsible for specific areas, and sales targets broken down layer by layer as part of the evaluation content. The performance of the managed stations directly affects the annual performance evaluation, making this evaluation an effective management method to encourage employees to work positively.

● Administrators divided by region

● Providing betting technique training for salespeople

"Years of work practice have made us realize that the steady development of the lottery industry can only be achieved through scientific management. Therefore, we adhere to the working principle of seeking benefits through management and promoting development through services," according to the staff of the Zhengzhou Welfare Lottery Center. The construction of lottery stations, personnel training, and center support are specific measures to improve basic construction through management and enhance efficiency.

In 2010, the Double Color Ball, 3D, and 22 Select 5 games in Zhengzhou developed steadily. However, for frontline salespeople, the mastery of marketing promotion and game techniques does not yet fully meet the needs of lottery players, necessitating further strengthening of related training. Since last year, Zhengzhou has organized training for salespeople. According to statistics, the city held a total of 12 training sessions last year alone.
