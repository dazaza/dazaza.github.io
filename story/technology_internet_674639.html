Nowadays, stairs are very common in homes. When choosing stairs, it is essential to select quality ones because the prevalence of villas, duplexes, and multi-level residences has not only given them a significant function but also changed people's traditional steps within their homes.

The primary function of stairs is to connect different levels and provide a surface for stepping, so their safety cannot be overlooked; this is the most crucial factor. Their location and design within a residence determine their usability. Often, good planning can bring lasting convenience, while poor planning can cause many inconveniences. After setting the shape of the stairs, it’s time to consider their style, material, and color. Currently, the types of stairs available on the market include steel-wood, steel-glass, wrought iron, and solid wood materials. Steel-wood suits modern and simple styles, with its sleek and straightforward feel being favored by the younger generation. Steel-glass stairs: The transparent glass material highlights individuality. Wrought iron stairs are often used in spacious areas like hotel lobbies. Solid wood stairs, with their warm and substantial characteristics, are naturally the preferred material. The stair steps should not be made too high. Stair handrails should ideally be designed with rounded edges without sharp corners.
