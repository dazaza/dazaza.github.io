Comprehensive list of holidays throughout the year

by dipper3379 on 2012-02-20 21:03:59

Here is a compilation of holidays throughout the year, which can be quite valuable for those involved in event planning, thematic planning, and marketing strategies. Websites or companies often conduct various marketing activities based on these holidays or events, including changing their logos (a concept first introduced by GOOGLE) and setting up content themes. This record can be referenced at any time.

In the first half of the year:

January:

- January 1: New Year's Day

- Last Sunday in January: International Leprosy Day

February:

- February 2: World Wetlands Day

- February 14: Valentine's Day

March:

- March 3: National Ear Care Day

- March 5: Youth Volunteer Service Day

- March 8: International Women’s Day

- March 9: Protect Mother River Day

- March 12: China Arbor Day

- March 14: White Day (Japan/Korea)

- March 14: International Police Day

- March 15: World Consumer Rights Day

- March 21: World Forest Day

- March 21: World Sleep Day

- March 22: World Water Day

- March 23: World Meteorological Day

- March 24: World Tuberculosis Day

- Monday of the last full week in March: National Safety Education Day for Primary and Middle School Students

- First Sunday after the Spring Equinox Full Moon: Easter (can fall between March 22 - April 25)

April:

- April 1: April Fools’ Day

- April 5: Qingming Festival (Tomb-Sweeping Day)

- April 7: World Health Day

- April 22: Earth Day

- April 26: World Intellectual Property Day

May:

- May 1: International Labor Day

- May 3: World Asthma Day

- May 4: Chinese Youth Day

- May 8: World Red Cross Day

- May 12: International Nurses Day

- May 15: International Family Day

- May 17: World Telecommunication Day

- May 20: National Student Nutrition Day

- May 23: International Milk Day

- May 31: World No Tobacco Day

- Second Sunday in May: Mother's Day

- Third Sunday in May: National Assistance Day for the Disabled

June:

- June 1: International Children's Day

- June 5: World Environment Day

- June 6: National Eye Care Day

- June 17: World Day to Combat Desertification and Drought

- June 23: International Olympic Day

- June 25: National Land Day

- June 26: International Day Against Drug Abuse and Illicit Trafficking

- Third Sunday in June: Father's Day

In the second half of the year:

July:

- July 1: International Architecture Day

- July 7: Chinese People's War of Resistance Against Japanese Aggression Memorial Day

- July 11: World Population Day

August:

- August 1: Army Day (Chinese People's Liberation Army Founding Day)

- August 12: International Youth Day

September:

- September 8: International Literacy Day

- September 10: Teachers' Day (China)

- September 16: International Day for the Preservation of the Ozone Layer

- September 20: National Teeth-loving Day

- September 21: International Day of Peace

- September 27: World Tourism Day

- Third Tuesday in September: International Peace Day

- Third Saturday in September: National Defense Education Day

- Fourth Sunday in September: International Deaf Day

October:

- October 1: National Day of the People's Republic of China

- October 1: International Music Day

- October 1: International Day of Older Persons

- October 4: World Animal Day

- October 5: World Teachers' Day

- October 8: National Hypertension Day

- October 9: World Post Day

- October 10: World Mental Health Day

- October 14: World Standards Day

- October 15: International White Cane Safety Day

- October 15: World Rural Women's Day

- October 16: World Food Day

- October 17: International Day for the Eradication of Poverty

- October 24: United Nations Day

- October 24: World Development Information Day

- October 28: China Men's Health Day

- October 31: Halloween

- First Monday in October: World Habitat Day

- Second Monday in October: Canadian Thanksgiving Day

- Second Wednesday in October: International Day for Disaster Reduction

- Second Thursday in October: World Sight Day

November:

- November 8: Chinese Journalists Day

- November 9: Fire Prevention Publicity Day

- November 14: World Diabetes Day

- November 17: International Students Day

- November 25: International Day for the Elimination of Violence against Women

- Last Thursday in November: American Thanksgiving Day

December:

- December 1: World AIDS Day

- December 3: International Day of Persons with Disabilities

- December 4: National Constitution Day

- December 9: World Football Day

- December 25: Christmas Day

- December 29: International Biodiversity Day

Lunar Calendar Holidays:

- Lunar New Year: Spring Festival

- Lantern Festival: 15th day of the 1st lunar month

- Dragon Boat Festival: 5th day of the 5th lunar month

- Qixi Festival (Chinese Valentine's Day): 7th day of the 7th lunar month

- Mid-Autumn Festival: 15th day of the 8th lunar month

- Double Ninth Festival: 9th day of the 9th lunar month

- Laba Rice Porridge Festival: 8th day of the 12th lunar month

- Traditional House Cleaning Day: 24th day of the 12th lunar month

Twenty-Four Solar Terms:

- Start of Spring: Around February 4

- Rain Water: February 18-20

- Awakening of Insects: March 5 (or 6)

- Spring Equinox: March 21 (or 22)

- Qingming Festival: Around April 5

- Grain Rain: Around April 20

- Start of Summer: May 5 or 6

- Grain Buds: May 20 or 21

- Grain in Ear: Around June 6

- Summer Solstice: Around June 22

- Minor Heat: Around July 7

- Major Heat: Around July 23

- Start of Autumn: August 7 or 8

- End of Heat: August 23 or 24

- White Dew: Around September 8

- Autumn Equinox: Around September 22

- Cold Dew: Around October 8

- Frost Descent: Around October 23

- Start of Winter: Around November 7

- Minor Snow: Around November 22

- Major Snow: Around December 7

- Winter Solstice: Around December 22

- Minor Cold: Around January 5

- Major Cold: Around January 20
