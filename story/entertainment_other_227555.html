What is Mobile Tencent Network?

Mobile Tencent Network is the mobile portal site of Tencent Company, a free wireless WAP site that can be visited at will when using mobile internet. It is real-time interconnected with the Internet and broadcasts major events live. Based on the resource advantages of Tencent Network, you can enjoy all kinds of information from the Internet and live broadcasts of major events anytime and anywhere with your mobile phone. Plus QQ, QQ online games, Qzone, Soso and other featured products, all of which are real-time interconnected with the Internet, allowing you to easily摆脱摆脱net wires and fully enjoy mobile life!

How to log in to Mobile Tencent Network?

1. Directly enter the URL on your mobile phone: 3g.qq.com

2. Get the login address by sending a text message: Send 3G to 10666226. Note: After receiving the SMS, click on the URL to enter. (Currently only supports China Mobile users, and please confirm that your phone allows receiving service information.)

How are charges applied for logging into Mobile Tencent Network?

All services provided by Mobile Tencent Network are free, but the GPRS network traffic fees incurred are charged by China Mobile.

How to light up the Mobile Tencent Network icon?

QQ users can log in to the 3GQQ of Mobile Tencent Network via their QQ number and password to light up the Mobile Tencent Network icon.

How to turn off the Mobile Tencent Network and Mobile QQ user icons?

The Mobile Tencent Network icon can now be manually turned off online if you no longer need it on your phone. Act quickly if you want to hide the icon!

There are two methods, let's look at the most convenient and quick one first:

First, go to http://3gqq.qq.com/iconset/icon_login.jsp.

After logging in, fill out a questionnaire to hide the icon, even if you log in again via your mobile device, this icon will not appear.

Note: Once hidden, it cannot be displayed again, so think carefully before proceeding!

You can also use the mobile internet method to turn off the Mobile Tencent Network icon:

1. Enter the address 3G.QQ.COM in the address bar of your mobile browser and open it;

2. On the main title of Mobile Tencent Network, click "QQ" and then log in with your QQ number;

3. See [3GQQ Chat], click "Enter Now>>";

4. Input your QQ number and password, after successful login, scroll down to the bottom, click "Mobile Tencent Network", it will automatically return to the main page;

5. At the bottom of the screen, click "Customer Service";

6. At the bottom of the screen, click "Customer Service Consultation";

7. Click "Icon Settings";

8. In the options, select "Hide" and click "Set Immediately";

9. Click "Confirm Hide" and you're done!

How to turn off the Mobile QQ User Icon?

Haha, the Mobile QQ User Icon can now be turned off. Act quickly if you want to hide the icon!

First, go to http://mobile.qq.com/iconset/icon_login.jsp.

After logging in, fill out a questionnaire to hide the icon, even if you log in again via your mobile device, this icon will not appear.

Note: After logging in with QQ on this page, it may redirect to an unknown page. If this happens, simply re-enter the address to see the questionnaire. Personally tested, it is very simple to cancel.
