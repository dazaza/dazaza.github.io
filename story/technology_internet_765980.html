Apple accessory ecosystem under hegemony: People are thinking of change and want to switch to Android.

by anonymous on 2013-09-04 13:52:12

After frequent accidents involving counterfeit chargers, Apple, which has always been aloof, suddenly developed a "conscience of the industry," but received little response.

In order to prevent users from encountering safety issues when using counterfeit accessories, Apple opened a special section on its official website in July this year titled "About Apple USB Power Adapters" and provided an overview on how users can identify official power adapters. It also recommended that users use the standard USB power adapter and USB cable.

Subsequently, Apple launched a USB adapter recycling program, allowing users to send non-Apple produced power adapters to Apple Store retail stores or authorized Apple service providers for processing. This seemingly populist activity did not receive much welcome. Consumers felt that even with an additional charge of 68 yuan, the replacement was still too expensive, and there were not many stores where replacements could be made; authorized Apple manufacturers expressed moderate anger. An unnamed authorized Apple charger manufacturer said: "This way, our authorization fees are wasted, and it greatly affects our brand."

In China, the popularity of Apple products has driven an Apple industrial chain from electronic components to peripheral accessories, from design and R&D to channel distribution. However, the recent decline in Apple smartphone shipments and the closed nature of its system may lead accessory manufacturers to vote with their feet and enter the relatively stable Android market, even though it means they will have to give up the high profits that Apple products bring.

Profit from Apple cases higher than Apple phones

Mobile phone accessories is a much larger market than imagined.

According to what the author learned at several electronics markets in Zhongguancun, with the decline of counterfeit mobile phones and the development of smartphones, traditional mobile phone distributors are struggling to survive under the impact of operator subsidies and online purchase discounts. Many have chosen to quit, while others have chosen to transform - selling mobile phone accessories to seek higher profits.

A dealer said that the sales volume ratio of mobile phones to accessories is about 1:1, and there have even been cases where people buy phone cases first before buying a phone. According to the internal classification standards of mobile phone stores, accessories can be divided into power type, audio type, application type, and protection type.

According to incomplete statistics, the scale of the smartphone accessories market last year exceeded 20 billion US dollars, accounting for more than half of the mobile phone accessories market. Market institutions predict that by 2017, the output value of smartphone accessories will grow to 38 billion US dollars, while the revenue of feature phone accessories market will fall to 12 billion US dollars.

In Zhongguancun, it is very obvious that there are far more dealers selling mobile phone accessories than mobile phone dealers. According to a dealer's introduction to the author: "Apple mobile phone accessories sell quite well, basically without worrying about sales volume."

However, the survival of entities in this large market varies greatly, and the total amount of "counterfeit" products far exceeds that of original authentic products. In addition to a wide sales network and convenient purchase, the most important factor is the low price. In Apple specialty stores, a regular charger costs 149 yuan, and a data cable costs 149 yuan, totaling 298 yuan, while in Zhongguancun, a set of chargers can be bought for just 15 to 20 yuan at any stall.

An insider revealed that if you buy over a thousand wires at once, each wire costs around 2 yuan. Nowadays, with the widespread internet, the price of mobile phones is basically transparent, and branded mobile phone accessories have become the only competitive advantage for individual merchants against specialty stores. A seller even said: "The profit from a branded Apple case is even higher than that of an Apple phone."

Compared to the mobile phone market or even IT product profits, the mobile phone accessories market is a gold mine.

Apple's配件ecosystem under hegemony

Compared to the diversity of Android phones, the relatively closed Apple has more say in the accessories market.

Apple's control over the industrial chain is reflected in adjusting the operating direction of manufacturers at the source. After multiple rounds of screening, the survival of the fittest market allows Apple to further fully control the downstream accessories industrial chain. In recent years, Apple has gradually increased the number of authorized accessory manufacturers. According to a description from an Apple accessory manufacturer, three years ago, this number was approximately only a dozen, but last year's Apple accessories conference had so many participants that some accessory manufacturers couldn't even find accommodation.

In this industrial chain, Apple benefits greatly. According to accessory manufacturers, the current authorization fee is 4 US dollars per accessory. And according to the CEO of a foreign company that acts as an agent for multiple IT manufacturers including IBM and HP: "Apple accessories still account for the largest proportion in all categories. When Apple releases new products, related accessory types can account for more than half of the total types, and at other times, they do not fall below 20%."

The change in the iPhone 5 interface continues to affect the survival status of Apple accessory manufacturers to this day: Apple strictly controls royalties by reducing the number of authorized manufacturers to over 60, while counterfeit manufacturers are still struggling to completely crack the interface chip.

In fact, embedding chips in accessories is not Apple's original idea. Tang Shouwen, an engineer at Hongyi Power Supply, revealed that most Motorola and HTC mobile phone batteries have chips called "code chips" embedded to prevent unauthorized production, but these manufacturers' chips were quickly cracked, rendering them useless.

Due to these factors, many small and medium-sized enterprises are no longer able to obtain Apple's certification authorization. Accessories like charging docks and speakers designed according to the old interface have experienced large-scale inventory accumulation, and many small enterprises have gone bankrupt due to poor cash flow.

Because of these factors, Apple accessory shipments have seen a significant decline. This has prompted more manufacturers to shift their focus to popular mobile phone accessory markets such as Samsung and Huawei. The long-established Apple accessory ecosystem is undergoing major changes.

Minds changing, turning to Android

The extremely small number of Apple devices makes the business of companies producing accessories for Apple very simple because this company only needs to design one or two protective cases for Apple's smartphones and tablets.

But with the decline in iPad and iPhone sales in China, accessory manufacturers have been forced to develop different size and appearance accessories for brands like Xiaomi and ZTE. A domestic Apple accessory supplier said: "Focusing solely on Apple is too risky."

According to research reports published by Canalys early this month, Apple's smart phone shipments in the Chinese market almost halved to 5% in the second quarter; in contrast, Samsung ranked first with 18% of shipments. Another research institution IDC showed that iPad shipments in China accounted for 28% of the tablet market's total shipments in the second quarter, down from 49% last year; Samsung rose from 6% last year to 11%.

Apple has responded. According to available information, Apple retail stores will launch discount promotions on some accessory products this month. Currently, two accessories have started promotions, and more promotional products will be continuously rotated. The currently discounted products include some Beats by Dre and speakers, but the 10% discount can only be considered average.

In the short term, accessory manufacturers will still go crazy for Apple. Compared to accessories of other electronic product brands, Apple accessories still offer the highest profit, and domestically produced mobile phones and other branded mobile phones cannot achieve such profit margins.
