"Driver" - The career advancement path of a disappointed driving school coach in the bureaucracy.

by jqrxass on 2009-08-23 13:08:11

The three main characters - Tian Jiang, Deputy Director Wang, Xu Lina, and others, through a series of events where they clash and interact with each other, reveal the essence of human nature - doesn't this remind us of many people and things around us, even making us think about ourselves?

Seeing the big picture from small details and achieving more with less is what experts do. The language and narrative style of "Small Car Driver" are simple and straightforward - the language is plain talk, discussing ordinary matters, but there's always more content hidden behind the words that makes one ponder.

The author excels at character portrayal. The numerous characters, including the three main ones, are vivid and lifelike, making it feel as if you can see them and hear their voices as you read.

Humor is another characteristic of "Small Car Driver" - unlike fake humor where only the person on stage laughs while the audience doesn't, or tickling to make them laugh: it's the kind of humor where the audience wants to laugh as soon as the speaker opens their mouth, and before the sentence is finished, the audience is already laughing uncontrollably.

"The Small Car Driver" Part 1 Driving School Coach

Driving school coach Tian Jiang had just parked in front of the water room when his coworker Wang Ping shouted: "Tian Rat! The head coach's office, Da Lei is calling!"

Tian Jiang placed the kettle on the car and turned toward the head coach's office, knowing that whenever this grandson calls, it's like the cawing of crows or the hooting of owls, nothing good will come out of it.

Upon entering the head coach's office, Grandson Li Da Lei was sitting with his leg on the table swinging, drinking a bit of liquor, and upon seeing Tian Jiang, said: "Tian Rat, last month the students failed the test once, deduct 100, late three times, deduct 150, total 250. Sign here."

"The Small Car Driver" Part 2 Small Car Driver (Upper)

Hearing that Tian Jiang was coming to the small car class made Xiao Zhuge, the small car driver, quite troubled. Originally, each person had their own small car, but Tian Jiang's arrival disrupted this balance. According to the rules, new drivers should go to the logistics department to drive, not directly get a small car right away. But this Tian Jiang's arrival was strange, it's unclear who might be backing him up. Of course, it wouldn't be anyone important - if it were an important figure, they wouldn't need to be arranged to drive! Still, one must consider the situation from all angles; even if it's just an average person causing trouble, isn't that enough to cause headaches?

It seems he must be given a small car to drive, but which one? And whose small car won't he be allowed to drive?

"The Small Car Driver" Part 3 Small Car Driver (Middle)

Tian Jiang began to suspect something internally, thinking, could it be that we've entered a restaurant? Upon receiving the order form and taking a look, he was truly amazed.

The order form was printed on high-quality copperplate paper. In the upper left corner of each page was a picture of a beauty, with nicknames below such as Hongyu, Lüzhu, Feiyan, Yuanyuan, all historically famous figures. Then followed detailed specifications such as height, weight, bust-waist-hip measurements, age, and years in the industry. Most astonishingly, there was a tagline in the lower right corner, expressing sentiments like "Life is short, do good deeds while you can," and "In the vast sea of humanity, meeting is fate." Tian Jiang's heart raced and eyes heated up, unable to put it down.

The receptionist couldn't wait any longer and reminded him: "Boss, have you decided yet?"

"The Small Car Driver" Part 4 Small Car Driver (Lower)

It was the Mid-Autumn Festival, and a piece of news spread in the political circles that wasn't too significant but also not trivial. The director of the Civil Affairs Bureau, Old Zhou, attempted suicide by taking sleeping pills in his office but failed and was saved. He was currently recuperating in the hospital.

Around the time of the Mid-Autumn Festival, it was generally a period of frequent social interactions within officialdom. Director Zhou harbored great aspirations, naturally leading to a series of major actions. During this process of paying respects, he heard an uncertain rumor saying that the current mayor might make some changes, and a candidate for the city government secretary had already been selected. Director Zhou halted the process halfway and returned to the office with Xiao Zhuge.
