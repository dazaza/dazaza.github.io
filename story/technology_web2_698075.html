Working as a hair stylist at a hairdressing salon in Haikou - wzcunzhang

by wzcunzhangk on 2012-03-08 23:27:47

After trial, it was found that Mr. Yan is from Haikou and works as a hairdresser in a beauty salon in Haikou with junior high school education. At around 1 o'clock in the morning on December 5, 2010, Mr. Yan and his friends were drinking at a barbecue garden in Wanlv Park. When going to the restroom, Mr. Yan had a friction with the victim Mr. Jiang (male, from Jiangyong County, Hunan Province). As a result, Mr. Yan and his friends, including Aye, beat up Mr. Jiang and his friends. Mr. Jiang ran to the east side of Wanlv Park where there was a smelly ditch. In order to save himself, Mr. Jiang jumped into the ditch in panic. Seeing this, Aye and others did not take any measures to help Mr. Jiang but left the scene instead. Eventually, Mr. Jiang drowned.

Recently, after being prosecuted by Longhua District People's Procuratorate, the court sentenced the defendant Mr. Yan for intentional injury, sentencing him to four years in prison and ordered him to compensate Mr. Jiang's father with 288,000 yuan.

On February 15, our reporter in Haikou learned from Ms. He Huirong, correspondent Mr. Yu Rongbiao, and Ms. Fu Caiyi that just because of some friction when going to the restroom, a post-90s hairdresser from Haikou harbored retaliatory thoughts, calling his friends to chase and beat the other party, forcing them to jump into a smelly ditch. However, the group of hairdressers did not attempt rescue, allowing him to drown. Ultimately, the hairdresser and his friends faced not only a four-year prison sentence but also a huge compensation of 288,000 yuan.
