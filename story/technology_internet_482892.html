Introduction to the Location of Erhai International Eco-city

Erhai International Eco-city is a health-care community that perfectly combines natural scenery with human habitation. It is surrounded by sea on three sides and located on a peninsula encircled by wetlands by the sea. The community integrates various health-care formats, services, and facilities that blend Chinese and Western cultures, such as sailing sports, medical care, nine sports parks, low-carbon six-star holiday hotels, a wealth center on the peninsula, high-end hydrotherapy spa, bilingual kindergartens, and five-star cinemas. This forms a unique health-care real estate development in Dali.

Surrounded by Sea on Three Sides, Beauty of Health Care

The beauty of Erhai enhances the beauty of Dali and bestows it with the charm of health care. Even if Dali's real estate projects do not promote health care, they always have the essential requirement of overlooking the sea, like Erhai International Eco-city. Located on the peninsula formed by the extension of Long Mountain, the dragon vein of Dali, into the sea, Erhai International Eco-city occupies the only piece of land so close to Erhai Lake. Here, it enjoys the widest scenic view of Erhai, approximately 127 kilometers long, and has the unique advantage of being able to overlook Erhai from three sides in Dali. Residents can directly face the beautiful water waves, kiss the humid air, and take in the layered mountains and streams of the Nineteen Peaks and Eighteen Creeks of Cangshan Mountain, as well as the ever-changing clouds in Dali's four seasons. This allows for an intimate connection with nature while enjoying the convenience of a modern city.

The health-care value of Erhai International Eco-city has been selected as one of China's top ten health-care real estate developments. To maintain the ecological balance of Erhai, the city has constructed a large-scale Erhai lakeside wetland park covering about 400 mu (approximately 267 acres), shouldering the responsibility of protecting and caring for the ecology of Erhai. The 270-square-kilometer Erhai presents rippling blue waves and forms a natural oxygen bar with nearly ten thousand negative oxygen ions per cubic centimeter. Every day, the green mountains covered with water, lush vegetation, birds flying and butterflies dancing, and the vast sea where fish leap freely, highlight Erhai International Eco-city as one of the most beautiful ecological resort developments in modern times. It is like a Peach Blossom Spring outside the noisy world, offering healthy and natural enjoyment of life and a happy time for nurturing the body and mind.
