Three major developing trends of China's furniture industry: refinement, scaling-up and branding

by anonymous on 2011-04-01 11:48:51

With the recovery of the global economy and the continuous improvement of people's living standards, more and more people are beginning to focus on furniture. Looking back over the past decade, China's furniture industry has experienced a period of rapid development.

During this period, the development of the furniture industry was mainly driven by expansion in volume. A relatively complete industrial system with a wide range of categories that aligns with international standards was initially established. Furniture products have reached a level where they can meet the needs of people’s lives domestically as well as the demands of the international market.

In the next 5 to 10 years, under the backdrop of the global furniture industry's transfer, China's furniture industry will enter its second phase of rapid development. Advances in modern technology are promoting the increasing scale of furniture production, and dramatic changes in cost and price will quickly break the existing competitive landscape. The introduction of automated production lines will inevitably increase fixed asset investment, reduce labor costs, and improve production efficiency; large-scale and standardized production will become possible. In the new stage of industrial development, the upgrading of competition in the furniture industry is inevitable.

Faced with fierce competition, what kind of sustainable development path should China's furniture enterprises take? This is a strategic issue of great concern to our industry. Mr. Huang, the person in charge of Meilele Furniture Network, which specializes in online furniture purchases, analyzed that in the next five to ten years, the Chinese furniture industry will inevitably experience large-scale and extensive industry consolidation. A large number of small and medium-sized enterprises will be eliminated, and a few outstanding enterprises will stand out in the industry through their brands, quality, services, and scale. In the future, China's furniture industry will develop towards three directions: refinement, scaling, and branding.

Refinement

The furniture industry is a traditional manufacturing sector with low technical requirements. To date, "carpenters" in rural areas still play a leading role in furniture manufacturing. Many furniture companies originated from such workshop-style furniture manufacturing and gradually expanded their scale by adding machinery and equipment. Due to this approach, some furniture companies have very coarse management. Coarse management leads to weak targeting, operability, and execution, unreasonable clarity in the organization structure and functional positioning of enterprises, many management levels, and very low management efficiency.

Competition among furniture companies is reflected in the competition of details, where subtleties demonstrate skill. Details influence quality, reflect taste, show differences, and determine success or failure. The precious value of details lies in creativity, achieving uniqueness. In modern society, people pursue higher standards. The furniture market emphasizes refined management, and details often reflect a company's professional level, highlight its internal qualities, and enhance the quality of its products.

Scaling

Looking at China's furniture industry, we find that regional agglomeration is becoming increasingly evident. At the same time, we also find that China's furniture industry is still an industry dominated by small and medium-sized enterprises. The industry concentration is very low, and currently, no single enterprise has a market share exceeding 1%. Large furniture enterprises are few and far between domestically. This is a double-edged sword. Furniture enterprises with industry advantages can take advantage of this good development opportunity and move forward. However, poorly managed, competitively weak small enterprises will face the fate of elimination.

In recent years, the construction of factories and industrial parks for some furniture enterprises has accelerated. With the advancement of production technology and the introduction of advanced production equipment, large-scale production in the furniture industry has become inevitable. As the production capacity of furniture enterprises continues to expand, it will inevitably lead to a significant drop in product prices. With the increase in labor costs and the shortage of skilled workers in the furniture industry, the profit margin for small and medium-sized enterprises will become smaller and smaller, and a large number of SMEs will gradually exit the market.

Branding

Consumers' furniture consumption concept is shifting from quantity consumption to quality consumption, and the era of brand consumption in furniture is coming. People's requirements for furniture are not just functional needs but also aesthetic and decorative needs, and more importantly, the need to display individuality. With the development of the domestic economy, people's lives have undergone fundamental changes, and the demand for furniture will inevitably develop towards health, environmental protection, and brands.

As consumers gain deeper understanding of furniture products, more consumers will establish brand awareness in furniture consumption, paying more attention to the aesthetics and artistry of furniture and valuing high-quality furniture consumption and service enjoyment. With the intensification of competition, many companies are increasingly focusing on packaging, transforming product competition into brand competition, and more and more companies are heavily advertising through public media. Companies with poor brand images and limited technical and financial strength will be at a growing disadvantage in the competition.

Meilele Furniture Network calls on China's furniture enterprises to strengthen basic enterprise management, emphasize enhancing the strategic competitive ability of enterprises, improve human resource development and management within enterprises, build excellent furniture brands, and actively respond to the intense competition that is about to come.
