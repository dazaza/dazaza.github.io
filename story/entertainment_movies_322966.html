Next stop, happiness - Fangchenggang Cinema

by 303729213 on 2010-01-07 14:01:27

Liang Mu-Cheng (played by Annie Anthony), 20~26 years old (six years later)

Bento Girl, Single Mother (six years later)

She understands and looks forward to true love, never becoming disheartened in the face of any difficulties, passionate and righteous. When she discovers that the goddess of happiness she has been longing for since childhood is in Guangxi's hands, she can't help but take a different view of this alien who constantly argues with her and goes against her... As happiness seems to be within reach, she leaves on the day of Guangxi's surgery after his mother's heartfelt pleas...

For six years, Mu-Cheng has lived strongly and optimistically in Huatian Village with her son, Xiao Le, who suffers from Type 1 diabetes but is the most precious gift in Mu-Cheng's life.

Only Taku, her childhood friend who has been by her side all along, understands that beneath Mu-Cheng's calm smile lies deep regret and sorrow. Unbeknownst to her, Guangxi re-enters her life, but with no memory of their past. Mu-Cheng can only suppress the stirrings in her heart as she offers her blessings for Guangxi's newfound life and happiness...

A familiar piano melody triggers a flood of lost memories, and Guangxi remembers Mu-Cheng and their love...

Faced with Guangxi's doubts and accusations, Xiao Le's excitement, and Taku's quiet companionship...

Where will happiness finally come to rest?...
