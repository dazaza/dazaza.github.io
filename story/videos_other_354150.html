2. Skill Explanation

Beginner: Skipped.

First Tier: Devil-subduing Staff for transition; Mingwang restores HP speed (these 3 points can be reconsidered); Yi Jin Classic accelerates skills, essential for chasing or escaping, and clears Dafan Bore cooldown; Great Compassion Mantra increases HP limit for Heavenly Drum Thunder Sound; Nirvana Mantra saves people.

Abandoned Skills: Reciting scriptures are basically not used; Bodhi Tree doesn't add much, www.lzgfz.com, 9 points too wasteful; Lion Roar is a front fan shape, requires higher operation, and needs Bodhi Tree for transition, wasteful points, abandoned; Ci Hang Pu Du adds less defense itself, abandoned.

Second Tier: Prajna Sutra for transition; Flying in the Air Technique for transition, and needed when chasing or escaping; All Fire combined with "Form is Emptiness", and currently looking at the situation, weakness resistance will also be an important part later on, maxed out for the Immortal Camp's Four Major Resistances - Immune to Stun; Great Vow and Great Compassion both cooperate with the Celestial Book to increase HP limit; All Wind Charm Resistance is better.

Abandoned Skills: Diamond Sutra and Bitter Sea are both early attack skills, later there are better ones, abandoned. Bright Mirror Platform + Defense too little, not worth it.

Third Tier: Subduing Demons Sutra for transition; Dizang Heart Sutra + Burst Damage and Transition; All Water combined with "Form is Emptiness", Tianyin itself has Clear Fixed State, so 1 point is enough; Avalokitesvara Sutra adds defense and cooperates with Celestial Book to clear Dafan Bore cooldown; Ultimate Bliss high single PK attack, Immortal Camp has Ultimate Bliss 2, adds damage based on percentage of Qi and Blood plus Ban Eating, must add; Buddha Light cooperates with Celestial Book to add HP limit; Diamond Body Protection adds defense; Four Earth Stun Resistance, must add.

Abandoned Skills: Heaven and Earth Same Longevity, similar to self-destruction skill, double-edged sword, Tianyin focuses on survival and control, abandoned.

Fourth Tier: Nianhua Miao Di for transition; Demon-breaking Secret Words clears Gui Dao's Heavenly Selection, better; Mahakaruna Mind Sutra itself has too long cooldown time, adding 1 point to clear Dafan Bore cooldown; Pure Land True Words cooperates with Celestial Book to add HP limit; Cause and Effect Cycle cooperates with Celestial Book enemy hurts me 100 self-damage 40, very useful in PK; Ci Hang Law Wish, Formation Effect increases attack, Immortal Camp's Ci Hang 2 group lowers enemy skill hit rate and dodge, must add.

Abandoned Skills: Six Paths Reincarnation fighting BOSS is still okay, easily interrupted in PK, abandoned; Buddha mass attack move, needs too many transition skills, later Infinite Mass Attack, abandoned; Jiemo Jingri short PK time long cooldown, requires high operation, can be abandoned, those who wish to add can add the 3 points from the first level Mingwang here.

Fifth Tier: Fully maxed out no explanation needed.

2. Celestial Book Explanation

Jiemie: Mingwang, Tianhua, Mingjing, Qiankun, Sekong, Jiasa are for transition, no more to say; Endless Bitter Sea - mainly reduces Wu Liang True Words cooldown time and adds attack, must max out (this charm is essential for Tianyin); Form is Emptiness - for stacking all resistances, must max out (this charm is essential for Tianyin, used for adding states); Cause and Effect Retribution - makes Cause and Effect Cycle reflect more damage; Pure World Buddha Heart - mainly to reduce Lei Yin Fa Yu cooldown time, Lei Yin is one of the few killing skills of Tianyin, essential.

Zhenmo: First column mainly for increasing defense with Jinggang Buyi, others for transition; Second column mainly for Buddha Sound Sacred Truth clearing Dafan Bore cooldown, others for transition; Third column mainly for Heaven Dao Cycle - Wu Liang's mana drain and increasing sleep ability, plus reflecting ratio for Cause and Effect Cycle, previous ones are for transition.

Chanxin: Pi Yu Zyan Yan - 3 points can add 2 hours of major defense, 2 points only 1 hour; Compassionate Heart - reduces healing skill cooldown, must add; Most of the others are for transition, Buddha Grace Extensive to increase HP limit, Light of Glory increases invincible time, adding 2 points to Dafan Bore gives 6 seconds of invincibility (this charm is essential for Tianyin, 8 seconds invincible); Heavenly Drum Thunder Sound - Hong Yuan, Great Compassion Mantra, Buddha Light Illuminates All, Pure Land True Words, Wuxiang Shengfo 5 moves total 52 points just for increasing HP function of Heavenly Drum, max effect increases 52*400=20800 HP, combined with Great Compassion, increases nearly 30000 HP, how effective you can feel it yourself.

III. Charm and Magic Treasure Choice

1. Charm: Form is Emptiness, Endless Bitter Sea, Light of Glory are all essential, if you have money change to Heavenly Drum Thunder Sound charm, survivability even stronger.

2. Magic Treasure: Completely agree with previous views, Fulu is the most suitable magic treasure for Tianyin, clears one beneficial state of the opponent + clears one detrimental state of oneself, still one sentence Tianyin survives longer, poses greater threat to enemies, control is the basic of Tianyin. And Fulu is not expensive, also a 135 magic treasure but can be used at 105, convenient to train.

IV. Summary

Tianyin is definitely focused on survival and control, but everyone wants to kill a few people in PK, so this point allocation is more inclined towards Immortal Tianyin, but after all, Tianyin needs better survival to bring greater effect, so many points are for Heavenly Drum Thunder Sound and all resistances. These states make Tianyin more survivable in PK, while cooperating with Yi Jin Classic's high speed, Demon-breaking Secret Words' status removal, and Avalokitesvara Sutra and Mahakaruna Mind Sutra's clearing Dafan Bore cooldown time, making Dafan Bore Invincible even smoother. Ultimate Bliss and Lei Yin Fa Yu are skills that Tianyin can rely on to instantly kill, so the point allocation and celestial book also enhance these two moves. Wu Liang True Words need not be said, Tianyin's magic treasure, therefore the enhancement of the celestial book to this skill is also necessary.
