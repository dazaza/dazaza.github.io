Woman injured in stabbing after attempting to break up with married partner

by dehua900 on 2011-05-08 17:07:59

Ms. Zhao, in her 40s, is from Dalian. She has a college education and also a very good job. In 2007, Ms. Zhao met Mr. Liu, who was about the same age as her. Although Mr. Liu was just a migrant worker with only a primary school education and not very good personal conditions, after some time of getting to know each other, Ms. Zhao developed feelings for Mr. Liu, and they eventually became a couple.

Almost a year after they started dating, Ms. Zhao suddenly discovered that Mr. Liu was already married. She then proposed breaking up with him, but Mr. Liu refused. After that, Mr. Liu visited Ms. Zhao's residence multiple times, wanting to reconcile with her, and even threatened her verbally. Ms. Zhao felt powerless against him.

On February 19th last year, Mr. Liu came to Ms. Zhao's place again. Ms. Zhao once more proposed breaking up with him and claimed she already had a boyfriend. Seeing Ms. Zhao's determination to break up, Mr. Liu became extremely angry. He suddenly thought of killing Ms. Zhao and then committing suicide. Taking advantage of Ms. Zhao resting on the bed in the bedroom, Mr. Liu quietly went to the kitchen, picked up a sharp knife used for cutting vegetables, and returned to the bedroom...

At this point, Ms. Zhao was sound asleep and completely unprepared. Suddenly, she felt an intense pain in her left arm. When she opened her eyes, she saw Mr. Liu holding the knife and stabbing her arm. Ms. Zhao was terrified. She shouted out her boyfriend's name, Liu, but he mercilessly strangled her neck. Ignoring Ms. Zhao's resistance, Mr. Liu stabbed her over a dozen times. Her abdomen was also cut open, exposing her intestines. Seeing this, Mr. Liu assumed Ms. Zhao would certainly die, so he stopped and tried to stab himself in the abdomen with the knife, but due to his nervousness, he failed several times and did not succeed in committing suicide.

When Mr. Liu attempted suicide, Ms. Zhao took the opportunity to hide in another room. She called her friend asking for help to call the police and also informed her relatives. Meanwhile, after failing to kill himself with the knife, Mr. Liu called his younger sister, saying that he had killed someone and wanted to jump off the building to commit suicide.

That afternoon at around 3 PM, the police and emergency medical personnel arrived at the scene. The police controlled Mr. Liu, while the emergency personnel carried Ms. Zhao on a stretcher into the ambulance. After being treated by doctors, Ms. Zhao eventually survived. Later, according to a forensic doctor's assessment, Ms. Zhao suffered more than ten stab wounds, classified as serious injuries, resulting in an eighth-level disability. Recently, the Intermediate People's Court of Dalian made a first-instance judgment on this case: Mr. Liu was found guilty of intentional homicide and sentenced to 15 years in prison. Reported by Zhang Xing.

A love affair that was fundamentally wrong ended tragically.

After discovering her boyfriend was a married man, Ms. Zhao proposed breaking up with him. Unable to accept it, her boyfriend attacked her with a knife while she was resting in bed, intending to take her life and then his own. This resulted in Ms. Zhao suffering severe injuries.
