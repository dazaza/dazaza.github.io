Cherish life.

by hunanshida on 2012-02-07 11:58:08

The incident of students kneeling down collectively to raise funds for a classmate has caused some controversy, but this practice and the spirit of these students are still worthy of respect. Therefore, if you are the parent or teacher of these students, you should be proud of what they have done. Moreover, you should guide them appropriately. Under the circumstances within their ability, they should use various ways to give as much love as possible.

Why is it said so? The teachers from the Self-study Examination Office of Hunan Normal University tell us an important reason: This is a form of expression of children's love, even though it may not be perfect. Nowadays, we often say that children are becoming more selfish, do not know how to love others, and do not know how to love others. In fact, this is mainly because we do not understand the children, nor do we guide them to learn how to understand love and give love. It is not true that children only need to seek love and do not need to give love.

In fact, the teachers from Hunan Normal University Self-study Examination Network remind us that giving love is happier than seeking love. That is why the children chose to kneel down collectively, with the aim of helping their sick classmates as much as possible. In this act of giving love, they did not feel ashamed. On the contrary, it was the power of love that supported them to kneel down on the ground in the city and in front of the crowd. This requires a great deal of courage!

Therefore, the teachers from Hunan Normal University Self-study Examination Recruitment Network remind parents that in daily life, we need to cultivate our children's love, allowing them to feel the happiness of enjoying love while also experiencing the value of giving love. Such things can often start with small everyday matters and do not necessarily require doing something grandiose.

For example, when you are tired, you might let your child share some household chores; when your neighbor needs help, you might encourage your child to knock on the door actively; when encountering hungry stray animals like cats or dogs, you might encourage your child to feed them a piece of bread or a sausage, or make them a warm little nest...

Over time, the awareness of children's love will gradually accumulate, and they will understand what kind of way can maximize the effect of giving love. Isn't it true that having a child who knows both how to enjoy love and how to give love can make you feel more happiness and joy in life?
