The first choice brand ranking in lighting fixtures: NVC, Philips, and Opple stage a "Romance of the Three Kingdoms"

by dengjuhk on 2010-04-09 23:06:25

On April 9, the China Real Estate Evaluation Center officially released the "2009 Top 500 Real Estate Developers in China Preferred Building Materials Brand List" at the Beijing Raffles Hotel. In the lighting fixtures category list, NVC received the most developers' favor and claimed the championship throne. The top preferred brands in the lighting fixtures category saw a three-way rivalry between NVC, Philips, and Opple. Philips ranked second, with Opple closely following behind. These three brands all had relatively high market shares, forming a tripartite balance of power. Together with Panasonic, SanXiong Jiguang, Yamming, TCL, Dibao, Osram, and Huatai, they constituted the top ten tier in the lighting fixtures category list.

Currently, China's lighting fixtures industry has nearly 3,000 enterprises, with production volume ranking first in the world. In the lighting fixtures category preferred brand list, NVC, Philips, and Opple are engaged in a three-way rivalry. However, there are only about a dozen large-scale enterprises, making the industry large but fragmented with low industrial concentration. Previously, indoor lighting decor suppliers dominated the high-end market with foreign brands such as Philips and Panasonic. In recent years, domestic self-owned brands like Opple and NVC have started to gain momentum, increasing their investment in independent research and development, competing with foreign brands in the high-end market.

The China Real Estate Evaluation Center found through research that currently, the vast majority of real estate developers prefer to choose lamp brands with significant brand influence. Among these, the adoption rates for the three major brands NVC, Philips, and Opple by developers were relatively high at 27.34%, 20.31%, and 17.97%, respectively. The combined usage rate of these three brands by developers accounted for a total of 65.62%. The first choice brands among the top ten national developers accounted for nearly 90% of the market total.

This indicates that the concentration of developers' choices in lighting fixtures is relatively high. The three giants NVC, Philips, and Opple Lighting occupy more than 60% of the market share, dominating domestic lighting market consumption and leading the lighting fixtures industry towards diversified development.

From the regional distribution of each brand’s developer preference rate, domestic major brands firmly occupied the top five positions in various regions. The three major brands NVC, Philips, and Opple stably ranked in the top three in all regions. While consolidating their regional markets, they are advancing into other regions by establishing branch companies or adopting regional agency models across major regions nationwide, striving to expand their brand's market share.

The research report pointed out that when selecting lighting products, developers mostly choose well-known brands with significant influence. Well-known brand lighting products offer a wide variety, large quantities, good quality, complete product lines, and ample selection options. Outstanding brand images can effectively enhance the quality and aesthetics of projects. Additionally, deeply ingrained brand impressions in consumers' minds also become an important factor for developers when choosing lighting brands.
