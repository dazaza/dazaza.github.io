How long can pregnant women in Taiyuan have an abortion? Experts from Shanxi People's Livelihood Hospital introduce that there are many ways of having an abortion nowadays, and the best time for an abortion has become somewhat uncertain. Below, we will listen to the gynecological experts of Shanxi People's Livelihood Hospital respectively introducing the optimal times for various methods of abortion:

1. Medication Abortion

Introduction: Medication abortion, also known as medical abortion, is a recent development over the past 20 years. The commonly used drugs are mifepristone and prostaglandin used in combination. For pregnant women in Taiyuan, these drugs cause the uterine decidua to degenerate and necrose, soften the cervix, and induce uterine contractions to expel the embryo.

Best Time for Abortion: Within 49 days of pregnancy. If it exceeds 49 days, or if the gestational sac is larger than 23mm, medication abortion is not suitable.

Required Time: 3-4 days

Day One: Routine examination and ultrasound check to rule out gynecological inflammation, ectopic pregnancy (an important contraindication for medication abortion), and other unsuitable conditions for medical abortion. Verify the gestational age. Pregnant women in Taiyuan should take mifepristone on an empty stomach (dosage according to doctor's instructions), then take another dose after 8-12 hours.

Day Two: Take mifepristone in the morning and evening.

Day Three: Take mifepristone on an empty stomach in the morning, and one hour later add prostaglandin at the original medical facility. About 70% of people will expel the gestational sac within 4-6 hours. During this time, stay at the hospital where doctors will observe bleeding, abdominal pain, and expelled material. After the pregnancy material is completely expelled and no abnormalities are observed, you may leave the hospital. One week later, have an ultrasound follow-up; if the medication abortion was incomplete, a curettage may be necessary.

2. Pain-free Abortion

Introduction: Pain-free abortion uses a new type of safe and effective intravenous general anesthetic. The patient is given the drug intravenously and falls asleep within about 30 seconds. The surgery is performed while the patient is unconscious and only takes 3-5 minutes.

Best Time for Pain-free Abortion: Can be chosen within 10 weeks of pregnancy, with the optimal surgical time being between 35-45 days of pregnancy.

Required Time: 3-4 hours

Preoperative examinations such as ultrasound need 1-2 hours. Anesthesia, surgery, and recovery take around 10 minutes total. Post-surgical observation lasts 2 hours, and if there are no abnormal situations, the patient can leave the hospital.
