Guangzhou Righteousness Foundation rewards teacher who killed bandit by car

by xue94fwsh on 2012-03-09 12:51:22

In recent days, Mr Zhang has become the focus of media attention. Intern Su Junjie and reporter Qiu Weirong took the photos. According to reports by Lu Jianluan and intern Li Tianyan: Mr Zhang who bravely chased down the robbers, had been uneasy for four days after the incident about how the police would classify his actions (the newspaper exclusively followed up on this story for three consecutive days). Yesterday, the Guangzhou Righteousness Foundation made a determination - Mr Zhang's actions were classified as righteous and brave, and the foundation presented him with a certificate in consolation. This allowed a good person to have a good year. "Finally I can sleep soundly," Mr Zhang said that this was his "New Year's gift" and he was prepared to go home immediately and tell his parents who came all the way from Northeast China to Guangzhou. "There is only one day until the Spring Festival, let's have a good year." Chairman Liu Jisheng of the Guangzhou Righteousness Foundation shook hands with Mr Zhang and presented him with the righteousness and bravery certificate. On the last working afternoon before the New Year, the righteousness and bravery foundation met with Mr Zhang who was caught in the center of public opinion, determining that his pursuit of the bandits was an act of righteousness and bravery, giving commendation to Mr Zhang. "Mr Zhang assisted the victim and pursued the criminal suspects." After repeated investigations, the police announced the latest investigation results yesterday. On the evening of the 17th, after the female victim was robbed by three bandits, Mr Zhang who happened to be passing by assisted her by driving after the criminals. During the chase, a traffic accident occurred where his car collided with the bandits' motorcycle resulting in one dead and two injured among the bandits. After the incident, the Guangzhou Public Security Bureau and Baiyun District Public Security Bureau submitted materials to the Guangzhou Righteousness and Bravery Foundation. "When the lives and property of others were threatened, Mr Zhang actively protected the female victim." Chairman Liu said. According to the regulations of the Righteousness and Bravery Foundation, Mr Zhang was determined to be acting righteously and bravely and was awarded a certificate of righteousness and bravery and a bonus of 3000 yuan. Mr Zhang could finally sleep well. Four days after flying his car to stop the thieves, driver Mr Zhang experienced an anxious and restless torment, but yesterday upon receiving the classification of righteous and brave, Mr Zhang finally sighed with relief. "These few days I was very worried, always waiting for the result." For this reason, Mr Zhang woke up in the middle of the night for several consecutive nights, and his wife also couldn't eat or sleep peacefully. "Now, at least I can sleep well." Mr Zhang said. "I haven't had time to tell my parents, as soon as I received notification from the foundation, I hurried over." Because of his own affairs, both sets of elderly relatives from both sides flew from Northeast China to Guangzhou and stayed squeezed in a small hotel every day worrying about him, which made Mr Zhang feel heartache when he thought about it. With the certificate in hand, Mr Zhang was prepared to go home immediately and tell them. These few days, colleagues, neighbors at the scene, and netizens supported Mr Zhang through various channels, believing that his righteous and brave actions should receive immunity and commendation. Mr Zhang in the midst of controversy felt greatly consoled: "Looking at the online comments, I feel there are still more good people, evil cannot suppress righteousness." "I hope the victimized lady can recover soon, and I hope my family stays safe." After the incident, Mr Zhang was always afraid that his family would be retaliated against by the accomplices of the bandits, but as soon as he mentioned if he encountered a similar situation next time, the 1.9-meter-tall man didn't hesitate to say: "I will still save people, if not, then the 'person' character composed of one stroke and one slash would not be formed." Mr Zhang said that this could be considered his New Year's gift. Although he was anxious inside, he always believed in the police. Through this matter, he hoped to awaken more people's sense of justice. He once forwarded the Xiao Yueyue event on Weibo and said: "I hope this society can have more good people." Netizens applauded and cheered for Mr Zhang. The official microblog of China Guangzhou Release: You are Guangzhou's pride! At 5:32 PM, the Guangzhou Public Security released a microblog post, announcing that driver Mr Zhang received commendation for righteousness and bravery, and within a short time, there were nearly a thousand forwards. After the news came out, netizens who were concerned about Mr Zhang clapped and cheered, praising the result as meeting everyone's expectations, promoting righteousness and suppressing evil.
