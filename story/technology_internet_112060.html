Rookie's Webpage Creation and Website Building Tutorial

In general, creating a website involves four steps: registering a domain name, applying for virtual hosting, making webpages, and promoting your website.

Registering a Domain Name:

First, you need to register a domain. It is preferable to register .com (international domain) or .cn (domestic domain). The domain should not be too long, should have some meaning, and be easy to remember. Nowadays, there aren't many good domains left. You can flexibly use combinations of numbers, English words, and pinyin, adding i, e, 51, ok, hao, 88, 163 etc., before or after the main part of the domain name to come up with many good domain names. After thinking of a domain name, you need to check if it is available at http://www.b86.net. If it cannot be registered, it means someone else has already taken it. If it can be registered, congratulations! Good domains are often in high demand, so act fast.

Applying for Virtual Hosting:

After getting your own domain name as your address, you will need space to build your house or establish your company, which is essentially your virtual host. Generally, virtual host providers offer users virtual hosting spaces ranging from 50M, 100M to 200M. For most corporate websites, choosing a virtual host around 150M-300M is sufficient. When buying a virtual host, consider several aspects: after-sales service, stability, and access speed. One important factor to note is the speed of the virtual host. If your clients or potential visitors are mainly from the north, then you can prioritize the China Netcom data center space. If they are mainly from the south, then first consider the telecom space. Website Promotion:

To enable customers to find your website, entrepreneurs must list their companies in search engines. If you have just opened your business, it may take several months for search engines to find your company. However, if you use paid systems like baidu.com and Google.com, customers can see your website immediately.
