Industry Analysis

Market analysis of Gululu (product marketability, current market status, potential market conditions)

Macromarket Overview:

Analysis of the current status and market prospects of China's toy market:

China's toy market has great development potential. Currently, China's per capita toy consumption level is relatively low, but with sustained economic growth and significant increases in urban and rural residents' income, China's toy market's astonishing potential will gradually manifest.

According to surveys conducted by the China Toy Association and the China Social Survey Institute, there are currently 286 million children and teenagers under the age of 14 in China. Their average annual toy consumption is only 20 RMB (2.4 USD) to 30 RMB (3.6 USD), far below the average annual toy consumption of 13 USD for Asian children and 34 USD for children worldwide. If China's toy consumption reaches the average level of Asia, the market size is expected to exceed 30 billion RMB. Additionally, a comprehensive survey report published by the Hong Kong Trade Development Council predicts that the mainland toy market will grow at an annual rate of 40%, with sales exceeding 100 billion RMB by 2010.

Furthermore, relevant estimates suggest that the adult toy market in China alone could reach approximately 50 billion RMB annually for young people. These individuals in their twenties and thirties, limited by economic conditions during their childhood, now harbor a "compensation" mindset and have a strong preference for adult toys. Moreover, with the accelerating pace of work and life, many people experience excessive psychological and physiological stress, and adult toys can alleviate or even help them forget external pressures, making this a huge market.

Gululu's Taobao Market Prospects:

The main retail channels for toys in mainland China include department stores, warehouse-style malls, specialty stores, individual retailers, and emerging children's product specialty stores. Among these, department stores and warehouse-style malls are the primary locations where consumers purchase toys. Manufacturers generally supply retailers through product agents or via toy wholesale markets. Data from the International Toy Association in 1998 indicates that in Europe, toy specialty stores, supermarkets, and department stores account for 23%, 19%, and 6% respectively of total toy sales. In China, most toy sales are completed by department stores, with a small portion beginning to emerge in the wholesale market over the past two years. The singularity of business formats makes it difficult for toy sales to adequately meet customer needs and create new demands. Changes in consumer lifestyle and perceptions, however, create space for new marketing concepts and retail formats. With the rapid development of e-commerce, consumer shopping channels are gradually shifting towards the internet. Surveys by the Hong Kong Trade Development Council show that China is expected to become the second-largest toy retail market after the United States and Europe. Therefore, the retail industry must possess keen market awareness, explore its own business creation capabilities, and adjust its offerings based on changes in consumer lifestyles, purchasing consciousness, and shopping methods. By using new methods to provide toy products, integrating advanced foreign business formats with the Chinese market, and changing traditional sales models, online retail breaks regional barriers and reduces consumer shopping costs while meeting consumer needs for convenience, selection, and cultural value. This aligns the toy market closely with holiday economics and entertainment economics; the integration and flexible use of new business formats creates a large toy sales market.

Psychological studies indicate that each person has corresponding psychological needs at different stages of life, and whether one's psychological needs during childhood are met will influence their lifelong mental development. Modern life is fast-paced, work pressure is high, and interpersonal relationships are increasingly tense. People seek to eliminate stress, combined with increased demand for leisure and entertainment, leading to a rapidly expanding consumer base for adult toys.

For the products sold in the Gululu Diary - creative dolls, alternative gadgets, trendy accessories, and unique home decor - all are distinctive within the toy category, possessing both entertainment and practical value. This meets the needs of our targeted customer age group.

While satisfying the needs of our store's customers, it also influences their lifelong mental development; alleviates issues such as the fast pace of modern life, high work pressure, and strained interpersonal relationships. Furthermore, it forms our unique sales style and sales culture, benefiting the development and expansion of our unique market.

Gululu's Pricing Strategy:

The prices of Gululu Diary products are set according to the needs and purchasing power of different consumer age groups. Ordinary products are generally priced within ranges acceptable to different consumer levels.

According to a survey by the China Social Survey Institute: 64% of consumers said they would consider buying toys suitable for themselves if conditions allowed. Among them, 33% of adults indicated they liked and were willing to buy toys. However, domestic consumer demand for toys mainly focuses on mid-range or lower-mid-range products, which account for over 80% of total toy sales. Although low-end toys have absolute price advantages, most of their products are of poor quality, very unsafe, and infringe on the patents of original products. However, the culture of Gululu Diary enables consumers to find high-quality, reasonably priced creative treasures, meeting the purchasing needs for high-quality low-end toys. Additionally, our mid-to-high-end products are rare and unique in the market, not only satisfying consumer purchases but also being cheaper than similar products, giving us a price advantage.

Through investigation and understanding of the current pricing of similar products by competitors, we understand the general price range. To set a pricing system for product marketing, prices are formulated according to low, medium, and high strategies. Low-priced products aim to attract popularity and credit, sometimes even at a loss, just for attracting traffic. Mid-range priced products target the mainstream consumer group, aiming for both transaction rates and profit. High-priced products target the high-end group, not requiring high transaction volumes, aiming to compensate for losses incurred from selling low-priced items, striving for balance.

Choosing product sources with relatively higher cost-performance, setting prices based on ensuring product quality and purchase price. Adopting competition-oriented pricing: During the initial operation period, adopting a strategy of thin profit and high turnover to quickly improve credit ratings or open up the market and increase sales volume. As the online store's reputation improves, appropriately increasing profit margins. However, product prices should be as cheap as possible while including reasonable profits, enhancing competitiveness in search-based price rankings. Including shipping fees, the price should be lower than the market price. For fashion items unavailable offline, prices can be slightly higher to avoid negatively affecting customer impressions. Pricing should be clear and transparent, clearly stating whether it includes shipping fees to avoid trouble and damage to reputation. Ambiguous pricing may cause potential customers to abandon purchases.

Payment Methods for Gululu:

Currently, online stores mainly offer several payment methods: online payment, postal remittance, bank transfer, and cash on delivery. To facilitate customer payments, multiple options should be provided rather than just accepting one method, as this might lose sales opportunities due to customer inconvenience. Generally, to reduce operational risks, cash on delivery is not accepted. Transactions are conducted through Alipay.

Product Packaging:

Packaging should be carefully done. Spend some thought and money on buying attractive packaging boxes when necessary, to hold the treasures purchased by our customers. Self-made cardboard boxes have their unique advantages: first, low cost, fully utilizing the recycling value of old cardboard boxes and boards, saving money for oneself and conserving resources for society. The packaging box for mailing should be as small as possible to reduce weight; instead of stuffing newspapers, use plastic foam as much as possible to reduce weight and save costs. Second, adaptability, custom-sized boxes can be made to fit the shape of the items, breaking the limitation of fixed-size postal boxes.

Gululu's Logistics:

1. Keep shipping receipts properly.

2. Specify telephone service hours in the store.

3. Reply to emails within 8 hours.

4. Avoid customer inquiries about orders by proactively sending tracking numbers to customers.

5. For cash on delivery, try contacting the customer beforehand.

6. After receiving a customer complaint call, give the customer a reassuring response.

Key Points:

Ship promptly. Use recommended logistics services for online orders (including YTO Express, STO E-logistics, Yunda Express, Fenghuo Logistics). If there are issues with the logistics company's service or goods, Taobao will file a claim with the logistics company. If the logistics company does not accept the claim, Taobao will compensate the seller first, prioritizing the seller's rights.

After-sales Service for Gululu:

Mainly concerns wear and tear, loss, or packaging issues during the mailing process. Neither the buyer nor the seller wants these incidents to occur, but when problems arise, as the seller, it is important to identify the cause of the accident immediately. If it is truly due to the seller's or the postal service's fault, a solution must be offered to the buyer promptly. This is a principle that sellers must adhere to. How can buyers not trust such sellers? This subtly enhances one's popularity. Sometimes, even if one loses profit temporarily, long-term profits accumulate precisely because of this.

Provide follow-up services after shipping. When sending express deliveries, inquire about the delivery situation and provide timely feedback to the buyer. This allows buyers to feel that we are serving them wholeheartedly. Not only can we monitor the shipping situation at any time, but it also bridges the gap between us and the buyers. Even if issues arise, the buyers will likely overlook them due to our service attitude.

Customer Management for Gululu:

1. Exchange Wangwang IDs with customers before or after each transaction and establish related after-sales service groups for future services and new product promotions, helping to develop old customers and bring in new ones.

3. Create data items to understand customer information (such as name, ethnicity, age, gender, purchase time, price range, product categories, city of residence), facilitating future classification searches.

5. Analyze customer purchase (order) times and previous interactions to determine their online activity periods for faster service.

6. Conduct targeted promotional campaigns for holidays, birthdays, and purchase anniversaries, sending cards to customers (preferably when they are online, as emails may be less appreciated).

7. Communicate with customers during specific times identified above, asking about product usage (giving them a VIP feeling) and recommending specific information for family members.

8. Learn to cater to customer interests by writing articles that interest them yet carry advertising nature, sending them to customers.

9. Recommend purchases based on weather and time.

10. Help solve customer problems (like financial advice, psychological counseling, etc., possibly as gift products).

11. Send "Gululu Diary" discount cards before the customer's birthday.

12. Consult customers on their expertise, offering discount cards as rewards, giving them a sense of self-fulfillment, prompting them to quickly use their "spoils."

13. Establish a "Gululu Diary" points system.

Risk Prediction for Gululu:

Small capital cost: Due to the early stage of store establishment and the store owner being a college student, funds are relatively tight.

Insufficient experience: Compared to most Taobao toy sellers, "Gululu Diary" is still in its infancy, with weak competitiveness and lacking experience compared to professional Taobao sellers of similar products.

Part-time sellers: As college students, the Sons and Daughters of the Rivers and Lakes have limited online time, resulting in customer loss.

Relevant Measures:

1) Try to keep the computer on, keep Wangwang online, and set up automatic replies including main business scope, recent promotional activities, contact information, phone number, mobile phone, QQ, email, MSN, etc.

2) Try to stay online during peak customer traffic times. According to statistics, the largest customer traffic on Taobao occurs from 14:00-17:00 and 19:00-22:00. Staying online during these golden hours ensures maximum potential buyers and highest transaction probabilities.

3) Change Wangwang status to reflect promotional activities and contact information.

4) For the issue of limited funds, establish long-term cooperative relationships with relevant physical stores.

5) Use low-price promotions to open up initial sales channels, intensify publicity, provide the best service, and create our unique "Gululu Diary" brand culture.

6) Humbly seek advice, pay attention to the strengths of other similar stores nearby, learn from experienced sellers' lessons, and carve out a path suitable for ourselves.
