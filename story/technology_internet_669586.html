Did Hua Yongqi go abroad? She was stunned for a moment, recalling the image of her being injured. She knew this event had a great impact on Hua Yongqi, and she never expected her to leave the country because of it.

"So, you have nothing to say?" Bai Xinyu's absent-minded appearance made Li Mu Yang more certain that she must have said something to Yongqi. Otherwise, she was just nowhere to be found before, now she went abroad directly without telling him where she was going.

"Master, I really don't know. Miss Three did come to me, but we didn't talk about anything!" His hand seemed to have already sunk into her flesh, pinching her throat. She grabbed his hand, but couldn't push it away. She couldn't help but suspect whether she would die at his hands in the next moment.

"Why would she leave if you didn't talk about anything?" Looking at her face turning red and her breathing becoming rapid, Li Mu Yang showed no sign of letting up. "Bai Xinyu, do you think you will have a chance because Yongqi left? Let me tell you, a woman as dirty and lowly as you doesn't even deserve to lick Yongqi's toes."

Bai Xinyu let go of her hand, tears streaming down her face. What did she do wrong? Why did everyone think that what happened was all her scheming! Yes, she wasn't well-born, but does that mean the body of someone not well-born is worth less? Is losing her chastity really so worthless?

Her tears fell, dripping onto the back of his hand that was pinching her neck. He felt disgusted and released her.

She coughed hard in distress, shedding even more tears. After catching her breath with difficulty, she said: "Master, I've never dared to think of climbing up to you. That night, I didn't know..."

"Shut up!" As soon as she mentioned the previous night, Li Mu Yang shouted loudly, "Don't you dare mention that night again! If you do, I'll kill you!" The thought of touching this woman that night made him want to skin her alive.

Bai Xinyu stopped talking in fear. She wanted to leave, but she didn't dare to move without his permission.

Damn it! Li Mu Yang looked at Bai Xinyu, feeling increasingly annoyed, reminding himself of the mistakes he had made. "I'm telling you, don't let me see you again. I swear, if I see you one more time, I will snap your neck." The girl in front of him was his shame.
