"Jia Junpeng, your mother calls you to go home for dinner" post goes viral online

by sisi1212 on 2009-07-17 10:11:26

Half a day to go red on the network, "Jia Junpeng", who is he?

A post with only one title has drawn more than 17,000 replies and been called a "network miracle" by netizens.

Yesterday afternoon, while browsing a certain bar, a reporter discovered that a post titled "Jia Junpeng, your mother calls you to come home for dinner" had been viewed by 390,617 netizens in just five or six hours, and already 17,028 netizens had replied.

Exactly, who is "Jia Junpeng"? Netizens seem to have no accurate explanation for this. Also, some netizens call the event of Jia Junpeng's sudden rise to fame in half a day a miracle of the Chinese-language network world.
