Mysterious Qing Palace Secret Recipe Reveals Itself, Sparks Buying Frenzy

At a recent launch event in Beijing for the modern new drug "Fengjing Capsule," developed based on the Qing Palace secret recipe "Fengjing Five-Flavor Powder," our reporter had the opportunity to interview Mr. Yan Chongnian, President of the Beijing Manchu Studies Society.

Founded in 1993, the Beijing Manchu Studies Society not only focuses on interpreting China's Qing history and Manchu culture from a historical perspective but has also been dedicated in recent years to the research and development of Manchu imperial medical secret recipes. The Fengjing Capsule, a renowned Qing dynasty imperial medicine jointly developed by the Beijing Manchu Studies Society and the Traditional Chinese Medicine (TCM) Association, represents the latest scientific achievement from these two organizations. Fengjing Capsule is a modern drug produced by integrating the secret recipe of the "Fengjing Five-Flavor Powder," which was an exclusive imperial medicine used during the Kangxi period of the Qing dynasty, with modern pharmaceutical technology. It has received production approval from the National Medical Products Administration and is known for its ability to boost immunity, improve sperm quality, replenish sperm, and enhance reproductive capacity.

During the launch event, a spokesperson for the Chinese TCM Association revealed that due to the inclusion of Rhodiola rosea, a mysterious component from Tibetan medicine, the drug activates the male spermatogenic system and improves human reproductive genes. Taking this medication can also significantly control the probability of conceiving a boy, effectively addressing the challenge of how to conceive a male child. The advantages of Fengjing Capsule over many modern fertility-enhancing drugs are as follows: first, it is based on an ancient formula with years of usage, making it healthy and natural; second, it takes effect quickly, is suitable for a wide range of people, and is reasonably priced; third, it has undergone scientific validation and has no side effects.

According to Qing palace archives, Emperor Kangxi fathered 55 children during his reign, including 30 princes and 25 princesses, breaking the limits of human reproductive capability. Moreover, Kangxi reigned for 61 years, the longest in Chinese history. This miracle occurred because Kangxi consistently consumed "Fengjing Five-Flavor Powder" prepared by the Chief Imperial Doctor Liu Shengfang during his reign.

The National Medical Products Administration also sent representatives to attend the launch event of Fengjing Capsule. According to staff from the administration, before approving the drug, clinical trials were conducted on more than 3,000 patients suffering from low sperm motility, seeking ways to improve sperm survival rates and sperm quality. Among those who took the drug for two courses or more, the effective rate reached 95%, and the majority of patients realized their dream of having a boy. The product has been praised by both consumers and experts as the "preferred pre-conception health supplement for men."

A spokesperson for the National Medical Products Administration further explained that since Fengjing Capsule is a pure TCM preparation, it overcomes the side effects of ordinary Western erectile dysfunction medications. Not only does it enhance human reproductive capabilities and fully activate the body’s spermatogenic system, but it also has anti-aging, anti-fatigue, and immune-boosting properties.

Following the new drug launch event, more than 20 top-tier hospitals across the country signed cooperation agreements with the manufacturer of Fengjing Capsule for sales collaboration. Several major international pharmaceutical giants also negotiated agency sales for overseas markets. At the sales venue of the launch event, a buying frenzy for Fengjing Capsule emerged, selling out over 3,000 boxes within 40 minutes. Many customers, having experienced good results, enthusiastically recommended it to friends, showcasing the immense market potential of Fengjing Capsule. We hope that Fengjing Capsule will benefit even more people in China.
