Wait in silence for the super主力 to show its power

by renyiqiou44 on 2008-05-21 15:48:22

Wenchuan is still in mourning, Sichuan is still in mourning, and the whole of China is still in grief. The author said yesterday that under this atmosphere, the A-share market should appear in a low-key manner as much as possible, otherwise it would be out of touch with the national sentiment. The management will not allow the market to have too much fluctuation, the main force institutions will not allow the market to have too much fluctuation, and we retail investors also do not want the market to have too much fluctuation. However, today's trend was somewhat unexpected. After the Shanghai Composite Index slightly turned red in the morning, it has been hovering in the blue area after 10:30. PetroChina, Industrial and Commercial Bank of China, Bank of China, China Shenhua, and Sinopec and other benchmark stocks still did nothing significant. Especially, the late-day selling spree of these benchmark stocks dragged the market down with increased volume. In the end, the market closed down by 161 points, with a severe overall decline in both cities. More than 100 stocks hit the daily limit down, showing heavy market selling pressure. Since most of the stocks hitting the limit down were those heavily speculated by hot money earlier, it can be characterized as profit-taking by the hot money.
