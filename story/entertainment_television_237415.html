Synopsis

The Birth of the Black Warrior

Ma Ti Shuang has always possessed a sharp business mind since childhood, understanding well that doing business is the path to wealth. Thus, he studies hard and completes his MBA at Stanford University in the U.S. through sheer effort. He then seeks opportunities abroad.

After graduation, Shuang sees potential in He Wentian's company and proactively approaches Wentian, bluntly stating that he does not want to be an employee but rather a partner. Even with just 0.01% of the shares, he is willing. Impressed by Shuang's uniqueness, Wentian allows him to join as a partner. Their relationship evolves into one of both mentorship and friendship, working seamlessly together in acquiring companies and reselling them, becoming renowned snipers in the American business world.

Later, Shuang becomes a well-known Black Warrior in the business world, ruthless and merciless, gradually gaining prominence. Similarly, differences in opinions between Shuang and Wentian increase. For the company's interest and development, Shuang decides to fully acquire Wentian's company. Unable to bear the blow, Wentian drives drunk, and Shuang learns that Wentian's car lost control and fell into the sea. After the incident, Wentian's body was never found, but Shuang firmly believes Wentian is dead, feeling immense guilt. Is the value of a single deal really more important than friendship or life?

Shuang reflects on everything in the past, deciding to no longer be the Black Warrior and withdraws from the business world. From the newspaper, Shuang learns that Wentian's widow and children are struggling financially in Hong Kong. Shuang feels responsible and decides to return to Hong Kong to take care of Wentian's family.

Miaomiao's Persistent Debt Collection

Back in Hong Kong, Shuang finds Wentian's wife, Sha Mu Lian, coincidentally staying at a hotel run by Tang Ji. He meets Tang Ji, who is troubled by the hotel's management issues. Tang Ji is a middle-aged playboy born into a commercial family, notorious outside, but filial to his father, surrounded by beauties.

Coincidentally, Shuang meets Lin Miaomiao at a luxury store, creating enmity between them. Miaomiao is a compulsive shopper, thus owing a large amount of credit card debt, forcing her to work as a debt collector. With a spirit of not charging unless successful, she becomes the ace of the debt collection company.

Mu Lian owes a significant debt to Miaomiao's finance company due to her grandmother Zhou Xiaoqiao being scammed. Miaomiao is responsible for collecting the debt, thus encountering Shuang again. Shuang takes over Mu Lian's debt, making Miaomiao initially mistake Shuang for Mu Lian's ex-husband who abandoned his family, leaving a bad impression.

Mu Lian is unaware that her husband's death was indirectly caused by Shuang and still believes her husband Wentian is alive. Mu Lian's grandmother even accuses Shuang of being the murderer of her son Wentian. Initially shocked, Shuang later discovers that the grandmother suffers from early-stage dementia, leading others to dismiss her words as nonsense.

Shuang Turns into a White Knight

Shuang is a regular customer at Qi's Noodle Shop. Seeing Mu Lian's difficulties, he introduces her to work at Qi's Noodle Shop. Mu Lian grew up in the U.S., straightforward and direct, but works diligently and enthusiastically at the noodle shop, injecting new energy, earning praise from Zhong Qi.

Qi's Noodle Shop faces threats from a newly opened cart noodle shop nearby. Shuang gifts the term CEO, and Miaomiao, to save the noodle shop, entrusts her brother Lin Musen to be the noodle shop's CEO. Musen takes over the noodle shop but performs poorly, causing chaos. Shuang advises Zhong Qi not to hand over the noodle shop to Musen, emphasizing the importance of not favoritism. Seeing Mu Lian's hardworking nature, Shuang recommends her for an important role to Qi and Miaomiao. Miaomiao, however, dismisses it, thinking Shuang is merely speaking casually.

Until Shuang helps the Bert Group acquire the old building where the noodle shop resides to convert it into a hotel and convinces Sen and Qiao to sell the shop. Miaomiao doesn't want her father's efforts to go to waste, determined to protect the noodle shop. She removes Musen and appoints Mu Lian as the CEO. As the noodle shop CEO, Mu Lian teams up with Miaomiao to negotiate with the Bert Group. With Shuang's covert assistance, Tang Ji regains autonomy for the first time, allowing Qi's Noodle Shop to overcome the crisis, achieving a win-win situation for both parties. From this point onward, Miaomiao deeply believes Shuang is a business genius and decides to learn business from him, hoping to alleviate poverty. Miaomiao persistently follows Shuang, who, though not verbally agreeing, secretly teaches her business strategies. Unexpectedly, on Miaomiao's business journey, she reunites with her ex-boyfriend Tong Yiben.

Miaomiao Gradually Develops Feelings for Shuang

To work, Mu Lian reluctantly sends Xiaoqiao to a nursing home. To comfort Xiaoqiao, Shuang also requests to stay there. Unbeknownst to them, the nursing home is funded by Tang Ji for his girlfriend, facing operational difficulties. During Tang Ji's tryst at the nursing home, he encounters Shuang and Miaomiao. Knowing Shuang's capabilities, Tang Ji hopes Shuang can solve the nursing home's problems. Miaomiao relentlessly pressures Shuang, eager to learn business from him. Reluctantly, Shuang decides to intervene.

After agreeing, Shuang appears indifferent. Seeing Shuang's laid-back attitude towards reforming the nursing home, Miaomiao decides to test him by suggesting the "Plant Filial Piety, Earn Bonuses" plan, which causes significant issues. Ultimately, Shuang steps in to resolve the nursing home's disturbance and surprisingly merges it with a neighboring kindergarten, creating a new generation of elderly-childcare center, solving all problems. Through this experience, the master-disciple relationship between Shuang and Miaomiao deepens significantly.

Miaomiao realizes she is gradually developing feelings for Shuang, but Shuang ignores her. Miaomiao assumes Shuang is a deeply emotional man with unresolved feelings for another woman. Later, she discovers this woman is Shuang's mother, Qingqing. Transferring his maternal affection onto Miaomiao, she tries her best to dissolve it. Seeing Shuang's continued refusal to accept her, she concludes that Shuang looks down on her. Determined to strengthen herself, she vows to make Shuang see her differently and thereby win the heart of the man she loves.
