"'Shop assistant' bloody pave the way for fame"

by 303729213 on 2010-06-24 18:00:56

Planned to kidnap a rich businessman to get money for shooting a film, but was exposed by lines

The TV series "The Storm Carved Building" is currently popular on Anhui TV. The actor who played the role of "Shop Boy", Cao Xiangdong, recently appeared in Taihe County. Unfortunately, he was not there for promoting his new drama, but was on trial for suspected involvement in a car-jacking and murder case. On June 21, 2010, it was reported by Taihe police that Cao Xiangdong was sentenced to death according to law.

Wandering teenager became an extra

In September 2006, in order to change the poor family situation, then 16-year-old Cao Xiangdong left school and went out to work. In May 2008, after traveling through many places, Cao Xiangdong arrived at the Shajia Bang water town film base and became an extra. Due to his outstanding performance, he later became a body double for some big productions. Facing the turning point in his destiny, Cao Xiangdong acted with great effort, improving his acting skills faster than other extras. While performing, he gradually became a "group leader" with a group of people around him. Gradually, people stopped calling him "A Dong," and many respectfully called him "Dong Ge."

Soon, fate favored this young man again. When the production team of "The Storm Carved Building" was shooting here, Cao Xiangdong became a special guest actor, obtaining the role of a "Shop Boy." The excellent performance of the "Shop Boy" made people in the industry look at Cao Xiangdong differently, which further ignited his star dream.

Crazy youth paved the way to fame with blood.

Out of the desire for fame, Cao Xiangdong quickly wrote the first two episodes of his TV series debut. Who should invest? Zhang Mingli, who was doing extra work together, suddenly came up with a bold idea: kidnap a rich man to get some money. Zhang Mingli's hometown is in Taihe County, where in recent years, some specialized pharmaceutical businessmen have become wealthy merchants, and Zhang Mingli thought it would be no problem to obtain a large sum of money.

At about 8 PM on June 7, 2009, it was raining heavily in Taihe County. Cao Xiangdong, Zhang Mingli, and four others appeared like ghosts at the traffic light intersection of Renmin Road and Construction Road in Taihe County. A taxi fell into their clutches; before the car had even stopped, Cao Xiangdong and those sitting behind cruelly killed the driver. They then drove to track their selected target, but due to the slippery road and Cao Xiangdong's nervousness after just killing someone, the taxi got stuck in the mud. Despite their best efforts, they couldn't push the car back onto the road. Helplessly, they looted the victim's wallet, cash, mobile phone, and documents before fleeing the scene.

After drinking, changed lines in the play and exposed themselves

On September 19, 2009, in a tavern in Shajia Bang Town, Jiangsu Province, two young men were completely drunk. When settling the bill, one of them quarreled with the waiter. This man was none other than "Shop Boy" Cao Xiangdong.

Perhaps moved by the scene, Cao Xiangdong suddenly remembered his role as "Shop Boy" in "The Storm Carved Building" while paying the bill. Drunk, Cao Xiangdong started showing off, indulging in being a "commander." However, the line from the commander in the drama, "Without me fighting the war against Japan, what kind of shop can you open, daring to ask me for money?" turned into, "I just robbed a car and killed someone in Taihe a couple of months ago, dare to ask me for money?" Eventually, Cao Xiangdong walked away without paying due to his drunkenness, and the waiter immediately reported it to the Changshu police.

This line spoken by Cao Xiangdong after drinking immediately aroused suspicion among the local police, who quickly informed the Taihe police. On September 22, 2009, with the cooperation of the Changshu Public Security Bureau, the Taihe Public Security Bureau arrested Cao Xiangdong. A month later, on October 22, 2009, Zhang Mingli was captured in Shandong.

Why did the ambitious young actor become a car-jacking murderer? In the Taihe County Detention Center, the reporter conducted a zero-distance interview with him.

Reporter: Which films and TV series have you participated in? Which one are you most satisfied with?

Cao Xiangdong: "Spring Goes, Spring Returns Again," "The Storm Carved Building"... I've shot about ten or so. I'm most satisfied with "The Storm Carved Building."

Reporter: What did you act as?

Cao Xiangdong: Soldier, bodyguard, etc., also played a Shop Boy.

Reporter: Do you know that "The Storm Carved Building" is currently popular on Anhui TV?

Cao Xiangdong: Really? I regret so much! (Bursts into loud crying)

Reporter: How do you feel now?

Cao Xiangdong: One must be pragmatic and not superficial. If I had taken steady steps, perhaps I could have succeeded...
