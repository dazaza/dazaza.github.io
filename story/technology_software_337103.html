Today, we will continue explaining practical experience and skills related to Taobao affiliates, hoping to give everyone a good start.

In the middle of 2008, the main source of advertising revenue was GG. Later, I learned about the existence of Taobao affiliates and thus created a dedicated Taobao affiliate website, which opened on February 1st, 2009. In the first few months after the website opened, the money earned through Taobao affiliates was 0, but there was an average daily income of about 2.5 yuan from Ali Mama advertisements. Below, I will summarize my own experiences and techniques for promotion and earning money, to be shared with all Taobao affiliates:

1. Personally, I think that for Taobao affiliate promotion, channel promotion is sufficient. These two methods not only serve as promotional tools but can also beautify the website. Placing them increases content and enhances the aesthetic appeal of the site. However, one drawback is that many people have already become familiar with these two promotion methods, so you can see identical things on many personal websites. But up until now, I haven't made any income from Taobao affiliate promotions.

2. Ad promotion is a fairly good method. Currently, netizens tend to rate GOOGLE's ad strategy highly. However, applying for GOOGLE requires a strict website review process. Personally, I think Ali Mama is also excellent, free to share practical tips and methods for being a Taobao affiliate. After all, both are under the same parent company, ensuring credibility, and you can see your daily income.

3. Another way to earn money is through alliances. However, after summarizing the experiences of many netizens, I found that the credibility of alliances is too low. Most of the time, it's just white promotion, or when you promote and the money comes in, they say your website doesn't meet the requirements. Therefore, I do not recommend this.

4. To make a website attractive, it needs good content. Additionally, I think borrowing a phrase from sales is crucial: "selling without selling." If you're going to place ads, it's best if they blend seamlessly with the website, making visitors not realize it's an ad. This way, you'll get clicks and generate income.

The above-mentioned techniques all require testing and exploration step by step. All successes are preceded by countless failures and sighs. Therefore, we should approach this special and potentially lucrative industry of online earnings with a good mindset.
