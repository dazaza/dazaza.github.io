The bottleneck in the development of the technical market

by keyizhang1 on 2012-03-01 14:52:19

With the deepening of reforms, China's technology market has rapidly developed from nothing to something, and from small to large. It includes permanent science and technology condition markets, technology property rights trading institutions, online technology trading markets (http://z.k8008.com/join/[http://z.k8008.com/join/]), etc. The scale of transactions continues to expand. A multi-level, multi-form technology trading system has been formed from cities to rural areas. The technology market has become an important channel for scientific and technological innovation and industrialization investment, an important way to convert technological resource advantages into actual productive forces, promoting the process of market-oriented research and development, commercialization of technological achievements, and industrialization of high-tech, greatly increasing the speed of achievement conversion and promoting the close integration of science and technology with the economy [http://www.k8008.com].

The technology market plays a foundational and important role in optimizing the allocation of scientific and technological resources. First, it promotes the rapid growth of patent technology transaction volumes. Second, introducing patent technology or trade secrets through the technology market and conducting extended R&D and re-innovation based on digestion and absorption to form new independent intellectual property rights is an important way for enterprises to enhance their independent innovation capabilities. However, the current technology market still has many problems and shortcomings.

1. The technology market is not well integrated with other types of markets, lacking coordination and supporting systems. The market legal system is not sound. There are issues with enterprise operating mechanisms; enterprises are both technology suppliers and technology demanders. Due to reasons such as enterprise systems [http://www.k8008.com], many enterprises have significant short-term behavior in the technology market, pursuing short-term benefits and failing to truly become market entities. Especially, most enterprises lack the motivation and pressure for technological innovation, resulting in insufficient technological investment and less than optimal allocation of technological resources, leading to single and repetitive technical commodities in the market. There are few technically advanced, high-quality commodities that meet market demands.

2. Some scientific and technological achievements or patent technologies only remain as laboratory results without undergoing intermediate trials or industrial-scale experiments, unable to produce qualified products. The non-intuitive nature of technological achievements means that simple textual descriptions are insufficient to attract investors' interest [http://www.k8008.com] and are even harder to serve as the basis for investment decisions. Worse still, there are some tricks in the technology market. For example, some technological achievements have obvious defects in principles, processes, or other aspects, which are concealed along with records of failures. Although the quantity is not large, the impact is extremely bad, making investors suspect fraud, slowing down the process of technology transactions, and seriously affecting the normal order of the technology market and the interests of the people.

3. People's concepts need further transformation. The policies and regulations (http://zc.k8008.com/[http://zc.k8008.com/]) and supervision systems of the technology market are not perfect. Currently, there is no specific law or regulation targeting the technology market in our country. The absence of policy and regulations at the national level leads to inconsistent supervision of the technology market in various places, unstandardized trading behaviors, inability to effectively protect the rights and interests of technical traders, increased risk costs of technical transactions, and affects the further standardization and development of the technology market. Most importantly, the circulation links of technical commodities are not smooth, and the intermediary forces are weak. The development of technical intermediary agencies mainly focused on intermediary consulting and services lags behind, lacking effective all-round services. Most scientific and technological achievements are still directly traded, and the intermediary role of the technology market has not been fully played. Technical commodities lack sales channels. Some technology markets lack compound talents, and team development is insufficient.

As the role of the technology market becomes increasingly recognized by the market, more technology property rights and venture capital will enter the technology market. The development of the technology market still needs improvement, and the level urgently needs to be raised. How to effectively promote the development of the technology market is a problem that needs urgent resolution.
