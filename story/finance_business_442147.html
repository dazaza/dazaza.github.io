Naval major loves disabled girl, six years of marriage full of酸sweet hardship

by kfjro779 on 2011-06-06 16:39:39

Key words: Li Shujie, escort mission, Gulf of Aden, Somalia, disability, love, military documentary

Navy Lieutenant Li Shujie and his finger-disabled wife Wang Hua have lived a unique life for six years. Not long ago, how did their moving love story come to light? CCTV-7's "Military Documentary" aired "The Love Choice of the Navy Lieutenant" on July 15 at 20:03, revealing why their marriage was opposed by the world.

On January 26, 2009, the first day of the lunar New Year, aboard Chinese naval vessels far in the Indian Ocean near the Gulf of Aden and Somali waters carrying out escort duties, navy officer Li Shujie was having a video call with his wife across the ocean.

Li Shujie: "Are you okay?"

Wang Hua: "I'm fine, everything is good at home!"

This was an ordinary holiday greeting video call, and we couldn't see anything special about it. But during this video call, his comrades were surprised to notice something unusual about Li Shujie's wife: throughout the entire video call, Wang Hua kept her hands under the table. What had happened to her hands? Even longtime comrades were curious, as if the couple had been hiding something from them.

In the year 2000, shortly after being discharged from the army, Wang Hua went on a trip with friends. On the way, disaster struck as a terrifying car accident took away seven of her fingers, altering Wang Hua's life forever.

This is a photo of Wang Hua when she was a telephone operator in the army. From this photo, we can clearly see that she once had very slender and beautiful hands. The sudden loss of the fingers she once took pride in made the strong-willed Wang Hua unable to accept it. Lying in bed, Wang Hua thought of ending her life many times.

Seeing her daughter's low spirits, Wang Hua's mother worried about what would happen to her daughter's future when she grew old and could no longer take care of her.

At this time, a soldier named Li Shujie suddenly proposed to Wang Hua's mother that he wanted to get to know Wang Hua.

It turned out that this soldier named Li Shujie was a teacher at a military academy, and Wang Hua had attended his tutoring class while she was in the army. After Wang Hua was discharged, they continued to stay in contact frequently.

During Wang Hua's hospital stay, Li Shujie often visited her. As Li Shujie carefully took care of Wang Hua, her dependence on him grew stronger.

When Li Shujie told Wang Hua's mother about wanting to date her, the elderly woman was skeptical. Although Wang Hua's mother was concerned, seeing her daughter so happy with Li Shujie, she temporarily agreed to let them date.

By the time the Spring Festival of 2002 arrived, Li Shujie returned home on leave and told his parents about dating a girlfriend. The elders never expected that their son would want to find a girl with disabled hands. If the villagers found out, they would talk behind their backs. Li Shujie's parents also felt ashamed of this matter.

In the eyes of the family, Li Shujie was one of the few college graduates in the village and a much-admired soldier. Even if he chose a spouse with average conditions, at least she should be physically intact.

Not only did the elders try to persuade their son to change his mind, but they also sought relatives to convince him. To avoid his family's advice, Li Shujie, who was originally on a 35-day vacation, returned to Beijing after only 20 days. After returning to Beijing, Li Shujie secretly continued to date Wang Hua without telling his family.

To stop Li Shujie from dating Wang Hua, his mother arranged introductions with several well-conditioned girls back in their hometown. Li Shujie, being very filial, didn't want to upset his parents and had to go home for a visit.

Li Shujie and Wang Hua's relationship not only faced opposition from his family but also received advice from friends around them to give up on this feeling. Just when everyone thought their persuasion had somewhat taken effect, Li Shujie made a surprising decision: he not only wanted to continue dating Wang Hua but also marry her. Li Shujie said that as a soldier, he must take responsibility for his lover, help Wang Hua escape her painful and helpless situation, and take better care of her.

Making the decision, Li Shujie anticipated the opposition from his relatives and friends, but he never expected that when he told Wang Hua about getting married, she refused. When Li Shujie approached Wang Hua's parents, their stance also surprised him. Wang Hua's parents weren't dissatisfied with Li Shujie; instead, they were worried that their daughter might burden him in the future.

To eliminate Wang Hua and her parents' concerns, Li Shujie communicated with them multiple times. His persistence finally moved them.

Meanwhile, Li Shujie's parents back in Hebei heard about his plan to marry Wang Hua. In anger, his father rushed to Beijing and sternly warned Li Shujie: if he didn't break off the relationship, they would sever their father-son relationship.

In the eyes of his parents, Li Shujie was obedient, filial, and very determined. To support Li Shujie and his brother's education, his father worked both in farming and outside jobs, leading a very difficult life.

Thinking about all this, Li Shujie hesitated. Would he really end the relationship with Wang Hua?

Despite the disapproval of Li Shujie's parents and relatives, he stubbornly defied their wishes.

In Li Shujie's heart, as a soldier, even when encountering difficulties, one must offer help. How much more so for someone he loved deeply.

In August 2003, amidst various objections, Li Shujie and Wang Hua got married. Unwilling to let too many people know about his wife's disability, they only invited a few relatives and friends for a meal together.

Taking his wife to school

Soon after their marriage, Li Shujie went to Xi'an to pursue his graduate studies, but soon his family heard that he had brought his wife to live with him in Xi'an!

Originally, considering that Wang Hua needed assistance for eating and dressing, in the second year of his graduate studies, Li Shujie rented a room near the school and brought his wife Wang Hua to Xi'an.

Bringing his wife Wang Hua to live with him caused misunderstanding among others and put great financial pressure on Li Shujie.

At school, to avoid gossip from classmates, Wang Hua always wore clothes with pockets to cover herself whenever they went out.

Apart from a classmate in Li Shujie's dormitory, none of the other mentors or classmates knew that while studying, Li Shujie also had to take care of his wife with disabled hands.

With Wang Hua by his side, Li Shujie studied harder and led a more fulfilling life. They studied together and traveled together.

Not long after bringing his wife to Xi'an, bad news came from his hometown in Hebei - his father tragically died in a car accident. The sudden blow left the couple deeply saddened.

Before his death, Li Shujie's father had consistently opposed his son's marriage. He had hoped to make amends through filial duty after the wedding, but his father's sudden passing became a lifelong regret for the couple.

Soon, the three-year campus life ended, and after graduating from his master's program, Li Shujie returned to Beijing with his wife to start work. This was supposed to be the beginning of their new life, but they hadn't expected various conflicts to arise.

Due to the nature of his work, Li Shujie often worked late into the night.

Without Li Shujie's company, after returning to Beijing, Wang Hua stayed at home every day unwilling to go out. Staying alone at home for a long time, Wang Hua began to feel restless and anxious. Seeing Wang Hua's unstable emotions, Li Shujie knew that this was a challenge brought by the new pace of life and that Wang Hua's psychological loneliness needed to be addressed. Whenever this happened, Li Shujie would always smile at his wife.

Li Shujie and Wang Hua have been living frugally, partly because Wang Hua has no economic source, and partly because they have always wanted to save money for something important.

In Li Shujie's home, we accidentally discovered a yellowed piece of paper on which it clearly wrote, "One day in the future, when global medical technology can restore my wife Wang Hua's hand functions to those of normal hands, I will bear all the costs, regardless of the country, and I will do everything possible to treat her."

Caring emotionally is just one way to solve problems. To enrich Wang Hua's monotonous life, Li Shujie encouraged her to try painting.

This was her first time picking up a paintbrush after the accident. At first, it was very strenuous, but Li Shujie was always there encouraging her.

Although she knew Li Shujie was comforting her, Wang Hua decided to paint a beautiful picture for him.

This is the painting Wang Hua gave to Li Shujie. From this painting, it can be seen that Wang Hua was relatively overweight at the time, but her eyes showed happiness. The head part of the painting reflected that due to her prolonged lack of interaction with the outside world, her thoughts and emotions had become more simple and mature.

However, Wang Hua did not meet Li Shujie's expectations and soon abandoned painting, starting to study recipes instead.

Originally, seeing her husband's increasingly fatigued body from long-term hard work, Wang Hua thought that proper diet could help regulate his health, thus motivating her to learn cooking. However, for Wang Hua with disabled hands, this was indeed very difficult. Seeing the efforts her wife made for him, Li Shujie felt very touched. After careful consideration, Li Shujie found a good solution. He bought cooking books and let her study recipes at home during the day, then cook together after he returned from work, so Wang Hua wouldn't get scalded again.

Six years of stormy love, without regrets

Five years passed quickly. The two experienced twists and turns, and their passionate love transformed into a contented companionship through the trials of life. In Li Shujie's home, we saw gloves of various styles everywhere. Why were there so many gloves stored at home? Among all these gloves, Wang Hua's favorite was a pair that was already worn out.

Wang Hua said, "This pair of gloves is the most meaningful. Look, they're torn and have holes. I've never thrown them away because these gloves were the first he bought for me. At that time, I thought if we didn't end up together, I could keep these gloves as a keepsake, and seeing the gloves would remind me of him."

Five years of marital life were filled with countless hardships and challenges, yet the two still walked hand in hand. Just as they were enjoying their sweet love, in December 2008, Li Shujie received orders to join the Chinese Navy fleet on a long journey to the Gulf of Aden and Somali waters to carry out escort missions.

Upon hearing the news, Wang Hua was both happy and reluctant. She felt fortunate that he had been selected, but at the same time, she was extremely reluctant to let him go for such a long period.

Considering Wang Hua's physical condition and not wanting his comrades to know, Wang Hua did not see Li Shujie off when he departed.

During Li Shujie's escort mission, Wang Hua and her parents watched TV reports related to the escort every day. Whenever there was coverage of the ship, she would be very excited.

In Li Shujie's home, we found a special calendar. Opening this calendar revealed that each important festival was marked with an expression. Originally, this was what Wang Hua had annotated while Li Shujie was at sea.

In fact, how could Li Shujie, who was on the mission, not miss his wife? Whenever he missed her, he could only look at her photos.

After a long wait, on April 28, 2009, the first batch of Chinese Navy escort fleets successfully completed their escort mission in the Gulf of Aden and returned triumphantly.

Recalling the scene of reuniting after four months apart, Wang Hua still vividly remembers it today.

It was precisely this escort mission that revealed Wang Hua's hand disability to Li Shujie's leaders and comrades. This hidden secret for many years deeply moved everyone. After learning about Li Shujie's deeds, the Navy leadership specially approved the allocation of a standard housing unit within the compound for him.

This is where Li Shujie's future home will be. It's only a seven to eight-minute walk from the office. Currently, the house is being renovated, and before long, the Li Shujie couple will be able to move in.

For six years, Li Shujie has quietly cared for his wife, working and living peacefully. His unwavering devotion to his wife has touched everyone around him. With his unique charisma and sense of duty as a soldier, he has created a legendary love story.
