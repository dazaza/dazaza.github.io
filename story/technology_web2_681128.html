Police car carrying suspects avoided pedestrians and hit the隔离带, 1 accompanying policeman dead

by xue94fwsh on 2012-02-28 11:22:40

The Accident was Caused by Dodging Pedestrians

An auxiliary police officer said in an interview with the regular police that they were driving back after completing a task. At that time, there were 2 regular police officers in the car. The three auxiliary police officers were all sitting on the back seat, and the car also carried a suspect. "We didn't notice what happened, but suddenly felt the car rushing to the left and had the feeling of emergency braking," said this auxiliary police officer. Their police car stopped after colliding with the innermost guardrail of the main road of the Fourth Ring Road. However, the driver was thrown out of the car, and he and the other two auxiliary police officers collided with other parts inside the car. The driver of this police car was a regular police officer. This auxiliary police officer said that the suspect on the car fled from the scene after the accident.

An informed person said that several pedestrians suddenly appeared in front of the police car crossing the main road of the Fourth Ring Road at the time of the accident. These pedestrians were scavengers. The car made an emergency avoidance, but eventually still hit one person. The car stopped after hitting the guardrail. A Yang surnamed regular police officer lost his life in the car.

At the incident site, about more than ten police cars, 2 fire trucks, and several ambulances were parked on the road. The traffic police were conducting investigations and taking photos. A "Transit" branded police car was parked in the middle of the road, with obvious collision marks on its right front side, and the door on the passenger side was open. There were two bodies on the ground, one wearing black clothing, the other wearing red clothing.

According to reports (by reporters Zhang Jian, Han Dapeng, Zhou Xin, and intern reporter Miao Fei), around 1 o'clock in the morning yesterday, a police car suddenly crashed into the separation zone on the main road near Yizhi West Bridge section of South Fourth Ring Road. As a result, a regular police officer on the car lost his life, and several auxiliary police officers were injured. The on-site emergency personnel said that another person also lost his life at the scene. The Beijing police reported that this police car belonged to the Ganjiakou Police Station of Haidian Public Security Bureau. The regular police made an emergency brake and actively avoided a fallen citizen on the road, causing the vehicle to collide with the center guardrail of the road.

In the morning yesterday, I visited Youanmen Hospital again. An auxiliary police officer surnamed Dong was still receiving treatment in the hospital. His right hand was fractured and already had a splint. But Mr. Dong politely declined the journalist's interview, only saying that he accidentally hurt his hand.

In this accident, the deceased police officer was named Yang Zaicheng, 40 years old, a public security police officer of the Ganjiakou Police Station of Haidian Branch. He joined the public security work in 1993 and worked successively in the Patrol Brigade and the Ganjiakou Police Station of the branch. He received commendations twice.

According to the on-site emergency personnel, two people lost their lives at the scene. One was the driver of the police car, dressed in a police uniform. The other was raggedly dressed, and there were some beverage bottles and other waste products in the bag on his body, looking like a scavenger. There were several other injured people at the scene, who were quickly sent to nearby hospitals.

Yang Zaicheng lived in Mentougou District. He was the second eldest among three brothers. His father passed away more than ten years ago. His mother has been chronically ill for a long time. His wife has no job, and his child has just started junior high school. His other two brothers have no fixed jobs. Yang Zaicheng was the main economic support of the family, and the family's daily necessities mostly depended on him.

In the eyes of relatives, Yang Zaicheng was the pillar of the family. He had to take care of his sick mother above and his junior high school child and farming younger brother below.

Auxiliary police claimed a suspect escaped.

Two people died in the police car accident.

At around 3:00 AM yesterday, in the emergency rescue room of Youanmen Hospital, three injured people were still receiving treatment. After inquiry, these three were auxiliary police officers on the police car. The doctors of the hospital said that among these three auxiliary police officers, one had a fractured right hand, the other two had superficial injuries, and none of them were in danger of losing their lives. Subsequently, a large number of police officers arrived at the hospital and questioned these people.

The location of the incident was located on the main road from east to west about 500 meters west of Yizhi West Bridge on the South Fourth Ring Road. At around 2:00 AM yesterday, about 200 meters east of the incident site, a police car was parked in the middle of the road. Several police officers stood on the main road of the Fourth Ring Road, reminding that there was an accident ahead and requiring passing vehicles to avoid it, but not to linger.

As of the time of writing last night, the police did not respond to the question of whether a suspect had escaped.

The house beam collapsed and a good man went away.

In the eyes of colleagues, Yang Zaicheng was a very enthusiastic person. He wasn't outstanding and very ordinary, but every thing he did made people feel warm. In daily patrol work, he often encountered lost or begging assistance cases. Although his own economic conditions were not good, he often took out his own money to help the parties involved; colleague Wei Yonghe had hypertension, so he silently bought a blood pressure monitor and gave it to him; in the summer of 2010, police officer Zhao Guisheng accidentally injured his leg in Beidaihe. Yang Zaicheng actively accompanied him back to Beijing for treatment from Beidaihe. To avoid jolts to Zhao Guisheng's injured leg during the journey, he sat on the ground and used his body to block his colleague's injured leg for three hours without complaint. The operation mode of the patrol team was four shifts with three rotations. If someone asked for leave or changed shifts, it would be very troublesome. However, everyone was willing to ask Yang Zaicheng to replace them because he never complained and always readily agreed.

After the accident, relevant leaders of the Municipal Public Security Bureau quickly rushed to the scene to organize rescue operations. The driving police officer died due to severe injuries, and another injured auxiliary police officer was sent to the hospital for treatment. The Municipal Public Security Bureau deeply mourned the loss of a respectable comrade.

The accident occurred when a police officer from the Ganjiakou Police Station of Haidian Branch was returning to the unit after completing a task at around 1 o'clock in the morning on December 2, 2011. When driving to 200 meters east of Majiɑlou Bridge on the inner main road of the South Fourth Ring Road in Fengtai District, the driving police officer suddenly found a fallen citizen on the road. The police officer made an emergency brake and actively avoided, causing the vehicle to collide with the center guardrail of the road.
