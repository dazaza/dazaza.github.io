Free antivirus software seems to be a trend, and it is believed that in the future, more and more security product manufacturers will join the free lineup. Although free products have some gaps compared to professional high-end products, it cannot be denied that there are many users in the world who need free products. Free has become inevitable, and the coexistence of free and paid services is also inevitable. It's more likely that in the future, companies, enterprises, and professional users will still use the paid professional high-end versions, while ordinary household consumers may have more choices, such as the following ten free antivirus software programs, some of which are already quite excellent.

1. Fortinet 4.1.0

Antivirus software Fortinet, known as "Feita" by Chinese users, comes from Sunnyvale, California, USA. Fortinet has a multi-layered network threat defense system, and in terms of monitoring levels and detection/removal capabilities, it does not lag behind other commonly used free antivirus software. In the face of the current wave of "free" antivirus software, "Feita" has also launched the FortiClient free version, which can be installed and enjoyed with all functions without any serial number: antivirus, firewall, web filtering, WAN optimization, etc. Now providing the latest version V4.1.2.138 of Fortinet in multiple languages (including simplified Chinese) for download ——

2. Avira AntiVir Personal - Free Antivirus

On March 30 this year, Avira officially landed in China, officially launching the simplified Chinese version of its antivirus software. Fans of Avira can now use the official simplified Chinese version instead of unofficial Chinese versions. Meanwhile, the update speed of the antivirus software's virus database will be improved. We know that Avira's free version has a good reputation. If the basic antivirus and detection functions meet your requirements, Avira is a good choice.

3. AVG Anti-Virus Free Edition

When discussing the topic of free antivirus software, it is impossible to leave out AVG Anti-Virus Free Edition. AVG has always been renowned for its strong ability to detect and remove trojans. A friend of mine, in order to enhance his computer's ability to detect and remove trojans, actually installed this AVG free antivirus software after installing an antivirus software. He said that currently, trojans are more rampant than viruses, and they are hard to prevent. Indeed, nowadays, the paralysis of computers, theft of account passwords... is not due to the rampant actions of viruses, but rather due to malicious software. Incidents like the "Nude Photo Scandal", "Qianqiu Scandal", "Campus Scandal"... and recent incidents like the "Beast Beast Scandal", "Technical School Scandal", etc., make people realize the necessity of prevention.

4. Comodo Internet Security

Comodo's firewall ranks among the top globally. But with the increasing number of antivirus software packages that combine antivirus software with firewalls, Comodo aims to maintain its market position and also hopes to gain a share in the antivirus software market. With the experience and technology accumulated in the firewall field, it is believed that Comodo Internet Security has considerable strength. Mainly because it includes Comodo's outstanding firewall technology, knowing that antivirus is the most important.

5. avast! Free Antivirus Software

avast! 5.0 features a new UI and logo, adding much color to it. Additionally, it has enhanced the capabilities of its antivirus software and anti-spyware engines, real-time protection, and Anti-Rootkit protection. The official claims that this software is the most popular free antivirus software globally: 100 million registered users and the number is constantly growing.

6. PC Tools Antivirus

PC Tools Antivirus is another powerful free antivirus software, providing scanning and removal of viruses, trojans, and worms, and offering IntelliGuard™ technology for real-time protection. Although it doesn't come with a firewall, its automatic protection function adds value. It has won numerous awards globally, receiving over 50 honors just in the US alone. These honors prove that PC Tools Antivirus indeed has very outstanding functions. Currently, PC Tools Antivirus supports the simplified Chinese version, making it even more convenient for us to use.

7. 360 Antivirus

A product from under the Tianchao umbrella, or perhaps inspired by the success of Rising, Kingsoft, and Jiangmin, wanting to get a piece of the pie. We know that 360 Safeguard has achieved quite good results in China. At least all the female classmates in our class have one, probably because the female classmates often step on landmines - trojans. For defending against and detecting/removing trojans and worms, 360 indeed has a set of skills, but whether 360 can also do well in antivirus software remains to be seen! Currently, the official data shows that 160 million netizens have chosen 360 Antivirus, indicating that it is indeed very popular! How effective it is? That depends on individual opinions!!

8. Microsoft Security Essentials

Microsoft's own product, before its release, many people doubted the actual significance of Microsoft Security Essentials. However, according to the current situation, Microsoft's strategy has been successful. First, although Microsoft Security Essentials has not reached a professional level, it has met its positioning - a simple and basic antivirus software; second, it is also a means to attract Microsoft users. Simple functions, forming a set with the Windows system's firewall. Naked friends might as well put on these shorts! @-@

9. Panda Cloud Antivirus

The last two free antivirus software programs seem somewhat unconventional compared to the previous ones, as they adopt the latest cloud computing technology. Last year, Panda announced its cloud-based free antivirus software, Panda Cloud Antivirus, which uses a relatively advanced cloud computing method. Panda calls it "collective intelligence," and through distributed computing and global community platforms, it will provide users with a completely different experience.

10. Immunet Protect

Following Panda Cloud Antivirus, another cloud-based antivirus software. The name is a bit long, but based on my experience, it is just a very basic antivirus software. However, because it is new and related to the popular cloud security, its development prospects may be bright. This software was jointly developed by Oliver Friedrichs (former Symantec director) and Alfred Huger (former McAfee and Symantec manager).

Immunet Protect only allows automatic virus scanning, you cannot scan files and folders individually. Nevertheless, due to its virus detection capability, Immunet Protect is still a very powerful antivirus software. Immunet Protect consumes fewer computer resources when running, so computers with lower configurations might consider it.
