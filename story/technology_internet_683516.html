75-Year-Old Woman Eats Steamed Bun with Hot Water and Sells Shoe Pads at Stall (Photo)

by xue94fwsh on 2012-02-29 14:46:24

Go with the flow, no intention to advertise Selling at stalls for earning money and seeking joy After the Weibo post, netizens affectionately called Grandma Zhang "Grandma Glasses", and many of them came up with advertising slogans. The day before yesterday, Weibo user "He Xiao Jian" posted: Grandma Zhang, 75 years old, has been nearly blind since she was 17 due to eye disease, with 1800-degree glasses, living alone for 30 years, selling shoe pads every day at the entrance of Shang Dynasty Ruins in Zhengzhou (the intersection of Cheng Mall Road and Chengdong Road). A bottle of cold boiled water and a plain steamed bun is her lunch. She hasn't left within a 100-meter radius of her home for more than 30 years because she dares not cross the road as she can't see clearly. Some people have given her counterfeit money. Please come up with an advertising slogan for me to put next to her as a sign. Thank you! She doesn't want to advertise, fearing that if her business gets better, others will not be able to sell their shoe pads. She said it's more comfortable spending money she earns herself, and she could also chat with acquaintances. The reporter went to chat with the elderly woman and found her cheerful and optimistic, without worries about food or clothing. Selling shoe pads is both for making money and for having fun, "I'm happy even if I only make 50 cents a day." Reporter's Notes Advertising Slogan @Shanxi Gao Yi Steel: Can't see the road clearly, but can see the warmth. @Henan Micro Mutual Assistance Initiative, breast enhancement massage technique video, friends passing by please buy a pair of shoe pads, let grandma's winter be warmer! Grandma Glasses: Selling shoe pads is also for having fun. Many people online want to help her, but Grandma says everything should go with the flow, selling shoe pads is also for chatting with acquaintances. Photo by Henan Business Daily reporter Wang Chunsheng. But when she learned that netizens were helping her collect advertising slogans online, Grandma Zhang waved her hand and said, "It's not good, it's not good. If I put up an advertisement, everyone will come to buy from me and not from others, there are two other shops nearby." Grandma Zhang's husband passed away in 1984, she has a son who is already married and has children, who recently moved out. Now she lives alone, saying she can go wherever she wants freely. On the cold streets of Zhengzhou in winter, the 1800-degree nearsighted grandma eats steamed buns with water while selling shoe pads at her stall, the netizens who saw her couldn't hold back, feeling sorry for her, so they took pictures and posted on Weibo to seek attention for Grandma. Next to Grandma Zhang's stall are steamed buns and a bottle of water, she said she makes do at noon, and goes home to eat noodles or stir-fried dishes in the evening. Netizens for the shoe pad-selling grandma This guest's words are well reflected in Grandma Zhang. In fact, she enjoys selling shoe pads more. Perhaps being alone at home is very boring, becoming a lonely elder, selling shoe pads gives her something to do and someone to talk to, and it becomes a lifestyle she likes. Grandma Zhang has been selling shoe pads at her stall for quite a few years. Initially, her son and daughter-in-law didn't agree, saying they weren't withholding her living expenses, and they worried she might hurt herself on the street, but she insisted. Grandma Zhang said she receives a thousand yuan monthly pension, setting up a stall is for money, but not entirely for money, "I'm happy even if I only make 50 cents a day." The best time she made over thirty yuan a day, but sometimes in summer, she didn't make any sales for two days, on average, she could earn over a hundred yuan a month. Yesterday morning, at the southwest corner of Cheng Mall Road and Chengdong Road in Zhengzhou, at the entrance of the Shang City Ruins, Grandma Zhang was chatting with acquaintances while guarding her shoe pad stall. Grandma Zhang's real name is Zhang Xiulan, 75 years old, with white hair, wearing 1800-degree myopic glasses, but they aren't very effective. She sees faces blurry even one meter away, so she usually stays between home and her stall. Grandma Zhang said that earning money by herself feels freer, and chatting with acquaintances outside every day makes her happy inside, "I'll laugh for a day, cry for a day, why not spend a day happily?" At 75, she has 1800-degree myopia, eating steamed buns with water while selling shoe pads at her stall. Netizens call her "Grandma Glasses" and post Weibo to seek advertising slogans. A successful person saw how hard she worked and wanted to help her, so he said he would buy all her shoe pads. She replied, "If I sell all my shoe pads to you, what will I sell later?"
