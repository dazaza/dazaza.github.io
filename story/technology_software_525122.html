Qiao Chunyang: Brand positioning and communication

(I) Establishing brand positioning

Firstly, according to the possible development paths of brand positioning points, list various positioning points and optimize and combine these positioning points. Discard unreasonable plans and keep feasible ones. Then, rigorously screen these feasible plans to establish the final brand positioning. The idea of brand positioning should be recorded in precise and concise words. For example, Miller Lite beer in the United States is described as follows: "A pure American standard strength beer, good tasting and quite refreshing, targeting males aged 18 to 24, standard beer drinkers, especially those who care about their external appearance."

(II) Communication of brand positioning

Brand positioning is a beginning rather than an end. After the brand positioning is established, it must be effectively and uniformly communicated. Communication does not create positioning but expresses it. Communication is the fundamental mechanism of brand positioning and the booster for the growth and development of a brand. Positioning provides conditions and requirements for communication.

Brand communication needs to fully express brand positioning, making target consumers familiar with, understand, and accept this positioning, creating resonance between the positioning and the hearts of consumers. Brand communication has multiple channels such as public relations, advertising, packaging, pricing, marketing channels, among which advertising is the most important. Advertising, through the combination of text and images and rich audio-visual methods, three-dimensionally reveals brand positioning, making it the most powerful means of communicating brand positioning. Through integrated communication, brand positioning continuously conveys relevant information to target consumers, thereby enhancing the "three degrees" (awareness, preference, loyalty) of the brand and elevating its image.

(III) Formation of brand positioning

There is only one truly effective positioning - consumer positioning. The goal of brand positioning is to occupy a unique and valuable position in the minds of consumers. Therefore, whether positioning is successful or not, consumers have the ultimate say. Consumer positioning depends on their recognition and acceptance of brand propositions, which depend on customer perception. The greater the customer perception, the higher the brand loyalty will be. The higher the loyalty, the more stable the brand positioning will become. Successful brands always firmly grasp customers, continuously strengthening their relationship with them and guiding them to recognize the brand, constantly creating customer-perceived value. The establishment of brand image and the enhancement of corporate competitiveness are all results of successful brand positioning.

All positioning ultimately moves towards the market, enduring market tests and receiving market validation.
