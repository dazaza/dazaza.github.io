Through a deep understanding and accurate interpretation of user needs, Feishimei, a video conferencing expert, has successfully signed a contract with Chongqing Heijin Industry in China, providing the best solution for remote communication in the coal industry.

### Need Positioning: Building a New Model of Efficient Meetings

Chongqing Heijin Industry is a large enterprise mainly engaged in coal mining and transportation. It was officially listed on the Australian Stock Exchange in 2011, making its mark on the international market. Currently, apart from its headquarters in Chongqing, Heijin Industry also has offices and mining centers in multiple counties and cities.

As the company's scale expands and its business increases, the need for remote communication becomes increasingly urgent: various policies and measures from the headquarters need to be quickly communicated to all offices; any emergencies occurring at the mining centers must be reported to the headquarters immediately; the transportation department needs to stay in touch with customers to understand real-time transportation conditions; fluctuations in foreign capital markets require the headquarters to keep shareholders informed about market trends... All these daily tasks involve remote communication.

After understanding Heijin Industry’s current situation, Feishimei accurately positioned the enterprise's needs — to provide a new model of efficient meetings so that all work within the company can run at high speed and in an orderly manner. Through Feishimei's video conferencing software, the headquarters and all offices can start meetings anytime and anywhere, with high-definition video quality that is no less effective than face-to-face meetings.

It is reported that the reason why Feishimei Network Meeting Room (www.facemeeting.cn) has such perfect video and audio effects is mainly due to its industry-first audio-video separation technology, which separates the encoding and transmission of images and sounds in meetings, prioritizing audio. Even if the network quality is particularly unstable and images cannot be transmitted normally, the sound remains smooth, ensuring the smooth progress of the meeting. In addition, Feishimei's powerful data-sharing function allows all parties involved in the meeting to share data, transfer files and videos, and demonstrate PowerPoint presentations, making the meeting more efficient and substantial.

### Deep Exploration: [Combining Software and Hardware] for the Best Remote Results

After identifying the basic needs of users, Feishimei further explored more remote communication needs. During the communication process, Feishimei learned that February this year marked the one-year anniversary of Heijin Industry's listing. The headquarters planned to organize a company-wide anniversary celebration for all employees to boost morale and enhance corporate cohesion. However, traditional celebrations are time-consuming, lack innovation, and fail to bring together employees from all branches, resulting in unsatisfactory outcomes. Is there a way to hold a [company-wide celebration] where all employees from different locations can participate while saving effort and money and creating something new? To meet this demand, Feishimei adopted a [software-hardware combination] method, helping Heijin Industry create a six-location joint celebration for all employees.

Firstly, Feishimei installed its video conferencing software at the headquarters and five branch offices, dividing the celebration venue into one main venue and five sub-venues. Employees from the headquarters and branches participated locally in the celebration, engaging in online speeches, summaries, mutual greetings, blessings, performances, and interactive games through Feishimei, creating a [high-tech interaction]. Secondly, to achieve better video communication results, Feishimei customized a hardware equipment solution for Heijin Industry. Based on the area and number of participants at each venue, it provided a complete set of hardware references, including cameras, audio systems, and other devices, and sent a technical team to Chongqing specifically to set up and debug the equipment, ensuring the best effect for the celebration.
