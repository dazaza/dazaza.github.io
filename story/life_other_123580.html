Shijiazhuang Oriental Science and Technology Secondary Professional School

by xie11 on 2009-02-09 16:42:21

The school's educational model integrates work and study, encourages students to work while studying, boasts first-class training facilities in the province, adopts a simple, fast, and accurate teaching model, implements fully enclosed quasi-military student management, ensures comprehensive employment placement, and offers the lowest tuition fees in the province.

Shijiazhuang Oriental Science and Technology Secondary Vocational School is a full-time comprehensive secondary vocational school approved by the Shijiazhuang Education Bureau and filed with the Hebei Provincial Department of Education. The school is located at No. 29, Shihuo South Road, Shijiazhuang City, Hebei Province, at the intersection of Xinhua Road and West Second Ring Road, with superior geographical conditions, convenient transportation, and beautiful surroundings.

Our school has always adhered to the development philosophy of "emancipating mind, being pragmatic and innovative, thinking hard and seeking change", constantly carrying out teaching and management reforms, firmly establishing the "market competition" awareness, taking the market as the orientation, taking the enterprise demand as the standard, exploring new ideas and methods for theoretical and practical teaching, accumulating rich teaching and management experience, and after students are employed, they have received widespread praise from employers.

1. With enterprise standards as guidance, we have equipped ourselves with first-class teaching facilities in the province.

Adequate and advanced teaching experimental equipment is the foundation and guarantee for doing a good job in vocational education. Our school has cumulatively invested more than 10 million yuan, building a CNC training workshop and a general machining training workshop equipped with more than 50 CNC lathes, CNC milling machines, ordinary lathes, ordinary milling machines, etc. The school has also built communication electronics laboratories, music rooms, electronic reading rooms, grinding rooms, dance studios, and new teaching buildings and other supporting facilities.

2. With employment positions as guidance, we have created an enterprise-oriented teaching model.

We established a teaching model mainly based on practice. In the teaching process, all students are managed as enterprise employees. Through competitive job placements, students take on roles such as team leaders, line supervisors, and employees in their "work". Evaluations and exams are used to select outstanding team leaders and skilled technicians among the students. This effectively promotes students' enthusiasm and initiative in learning.

Every day, there are quality monitoring indicators for the students' "work", which are statistically analyzed monthly. Based on the scores obtained each month, wages are calculated, consisting of: workload + performance evaluation + work discipline + appearance + safe operation + bonus, etc. This effectively motivates students' initiative in learning and fosters a sense of competition, allowing them to master knowledge and skills more quickly and better.

Through enterprise-oriented teaching reform, students can gradually experience and adapt to enterprise management models during their schooling period, adapting to the needs of employment positions and initially reaching the standards required by enterprises. The goal of enterprise-oriented teaching is to gradually surpass the enterprise employment standards.

3. With enterprise demand as guidance, we have created a fully enclosed quasi-military student management system and formulated a daily norm for students and a manual for practical operations in vocational quality training.

Comprehensively learning from the enterprise management model for employees, cultivating qualified talents that enterprises welcome. Enterprises not only need technically proficient skilled workers but also place greater emphasis on employees' overall qualities, such as team spirit, obedience, execution ability, and competitive awareness. The cultivation and improvement of overall qualities require the efforts and collaboration of all aspects of the school. Overall quality education runs through the entire learning process of students. To enhance the overall quality of our students, our school focuses on two distinctive management features:

Distinctive Management Feature One: Strict Quasi-Military Management

Our school adheres to the educational concept of "first becoming a person, then becoming talented," hiring retired military officers to specialize in quasi-military management of students. Starting from daily life systems, clothing, behavior, etiquette, and housekeeping, we let students develop good living habits, cultivate excellent organizational discipline styles, and make them quickly become disciplined talents with good military demeanor.

Distinctive Management Feature Two: Vocational Quality Education Management

During the students' learning process, we offer vocational quality courses divided into theoretical instruction and expansion practice. These two aspects complement and improve each other. Theoretical instruction mainly focuses on practical applications to transform students' thinking, while the expansion practice uses students' "personal experiences" to consolidate teaching results, making these vocational quality requirements the students' voluntary behaviors.

For vocational quality expansion training, our school signs agreements with enterprises, sending batches of our on-campus students to enterprise positions under the accompaniment of school leaders and teachers. After a period of quasi-employee life, students can understand society and enterprises before actual internship and employment, entering the state of formal enterprise employees in advance, laying a good foundation for adapting to employment positions.

4. With employment as guidance, we have properly completed employment placement work.

With the continuous improvement of our students' qualities and employment channels, we have widely established student internship and employment bases in the Pearl River Delta region, Beijing-Tianjin-Hebei-Shanxi area, and the Jiaozhou Peninsula.

The employment direction of our school's CNC major is mainly in the automotive parts manufacturing industry. All student internship units are world-renowned mechanical manufacturing enterprises ranked in the Fortune Global 500. The professional alignment rate for student internships and employment reaches 90%, and the student employment satisfaction rate exceeds 95%.

5. Thoughtful后勤 services, uniquely creating a "free meal system" for on-campus students in the province.

All on-campus students of our school implement the "free meal system" for three meals. On one hand, it reduces the burden on students' parents, and on the other hand, the school formulates scientific menus based on students' physical development needs, fully ensuring the nutritional needs for students' physical development and adaptation to intensive studies, effectively avoiding adverse consequences caused by students dining outside.

The school's student dormitory buildings, canteens, supermarkets, phone booths, bathrooms, and other service facilities are complete. The logistics management department always adheres to the principle of "service," striving to be proactive, considerate, intimate, and convenient.

6. Only miscellaneous fees are charged. The source of tuition and accommodation fees for three years.

Our school is a three-year secondary vocational school. With a strong sense of social responsibility originating from and giving back to society, relying on powerful educational strength and forward-thinking educational concepts, we aim to save costs for students' parents to the maximum extent. That is, students coming to our school only need to pay miscellaneous fees for three years, and the tuition and accommodation fees for three years are paid through work-study integration, i.e., vocational quality training. The school has also specifically established the "Hongzhi Class," offering full or partial exemptions of all miscellaneous fees for academically excellent and highly competent students from humble backgrounds. Our students enjoy the national scholarship of 1500 yuan per student per year.
