Indifference to fame and wealth

by bjseeprof on 2009-12-07 11:22:59

Speaking of indifference in dealing with the world, logo wall production, it is actually not indifferent. Why? Because love and hate are inevitable for everyone, forcing oneself to be indifferent is because there are things that cannot be obtained or have already been lost. Otherwise, in such a world, why bother to be indifferent? Recently, I watched the Korean hostage incident, thinking about the person who was shot dead, the first one among more than twenty people, why was it him? In society, I don't know what kind of person he was, whether he was beautiful or ugly, generous or stingy, fame-loving or wealth-loving, or indifferent. But I think it's unlikely that he was indifferent. If he was indifferent, he wouldn't have taken the group to Pakistan for fun. But as the first person to be killed, he was either selected or stepped forward voluntarily. I would rather believe that he stepped forward voluntarily. At that moment, the meaning of being a leader was far more than just a joke in daily life or taking care of logistics. It carried a heavy responsibility. At that moment, he took it on, not knowing if he went calmly, but he certainly had no regrets. During the 9/11 incident, it was said that a plane also crashed to the ground voluntarily by the passengers. Why? Just because they didn't want it to collide with their symbols. Mr. Lu Xun said, who is the backbone of China. I would say these people are the common backbone of "humanity". Why talk about the backbone? What does this have to do with indifference? It's because before these actions, they were not perfect, not gods. They were just ordinary people in this society, possibly those who were not successful in life. But they are still our heroes. Just like Lü Bu and Zhuge Liang, two completely different people, but both are heroic figures in history. In such a society, we always want to cultivate Lü Bu to have the wisdom of Zhuge Liang, and Zhuge Liang to have the martial arts of Lü Bu. So we cannot get what we desire. Indifference might just mean being in the midst of it all, seeing Lü Bu as a hero, seeing Zhuge Liang as a hero. Then we will know that we will become our own heroes. Then the people around us are all heroes. Indifference is not leaving the world, it's just recognizing its rules within the world and respecting one aspect of quality. Why be so demanding!
