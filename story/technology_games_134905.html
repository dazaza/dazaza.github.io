The Seal of the Gods: Ancient China's Battle with the Demon King

by youxiruanjian88 on 2009-03-28 08:45:43

【Basic Introduction】

The Chinese version of "Seal of the Gods" is a role-playing game produced by Paradise Bird Company. The story takes place in ancient China, describing the journey of Yanzi and his three companions as they search for the Hero's Horn while battling against the Hell Demon King Gonggu. The game features impressive visual effects during battle scenes.

【Usage Guide】

Stone Bowl Village

Yanzi lives in Stone Bowl Village in the southern Meng Kingdom. One day, his parents send him to fish in the east of the village. There, he encounters Axi and Yunque singing folk songs. While fishing, he notices that the village is on fire. Rushing back, he helps up Old Shantan who informs him that his parents have been captured by soldiers from White Fruit City, and his sister has already been killed by them. Determined to save his parents, Yanzi goes to White Fruit City to confront Leibang but gets severely beaten by the soldiers. Realizing he cannot win, Yanzi escapes and is saved by a woodcutter on the way back. Returning to Stone Bowl Village, Yanzi meets Old Shantan again, who tells him that Emperor Busi has hired a sorcerer named Gonggu. The emperor sold his soul for the sorcerer’s staff, allowing Gonggu to control the emperor’s body, leading to chaos in the kingdom. Shantan instructs Yanzi to follow Zhao Yong's example and cross the Ghost-Worrying Sea to Dragon Island to find the Horn of the Gods. Blowing it once allows one to ascend to heaven, twice to awaken the celestial deities, and thrice to return to the mortal world. This horn is a treasure used for communication between humans and gods. However, to reach Dragon Island, Yanzi must first gain magical power. Shantan sends Yanzi to Foggy Forest to find the Bodhi Nightlight Pearl and helps Yanzi build a boat. Entering the cave of Foggy Forest, Yanzi defeats the tree spirit inside and obtains the Martial God Gemstone. He then eliminates several monsters and meets Master Bodhi in the northeast direction, obtaining the Bodhi Nightlight Pearl. Returning to the village, Yanzi finds Old Shantan, who has already finished building the boat. Everyone sees Yanzi off at the beach as he sets sail towards Dragon Island.

Dragon Island

The sea is not peaceful either. Yanzi encounters many monsters (spider spirits, demon flowers) and kills a giant sea eel at Big Cliff Bay before reaching Dragon Island. At the dock, he defeats a fire dragon and acquires the Fire God Gemstone. Exhausted after the battle, Yanzi falls into the water but is luckily rescued by an island resident named Murong Xiaocui. Xiaocui takes him home to her parents, Murong Duan. Yanzi stays at Xiaocui's house to recuperate. One night, Xiaocui plays the flute and sings love songs, to which Yanzi responds with his own songs. Yanzi talks to Xiaocui about the situation in the Southern Meng Kingdom and learns that the Horn of the Gods is actually kept by Murong Duan. However, it cannot be lent out easily. To help Yanzi obtain the Horn of the Gods, Xiaocui takes him to Longevity Village in the north of the village to borrow an invisibility cloak. Returning home, they go to the backyard garden, where they use wine to intoxicate the four guards watching over the Horn of the Gods, successfully stealing it. Arriving at the beach, just as Yanzi is about to blow the horn, Murong Duan discovers their actions and leads people to intercept them at the beach. To prevent the horn from being taken back, Yanzi fights with Zhang Wanyong, Xiaocui's childhood friend. After defeating Zhang Wanyong, Yanzi blows the horn, ascending to the Heavenly Palace, but Dragon Island bursts into flames and sinks into the sea.

Heavenly Palace

Arriving at the Heavenly Palace, Yanzi first visits the Milky Way Palace to find the Goddess of the Milky Way. According to the goddess, the gods have not been seen recently, and there are many demonic creatures in the heavenly realm. Therefore, she agrees to lead Yanzi to meet the Celestial Emperor. Crossing the Milky Way, they fight various monsters rampant in the heavenly realm. In a tower, Yanzi can defeat the rock monster and obtain the Seven Gods Gemstone. Exiting the tower leads to a forest where defeating the tiger spirit grants the Water God Gemstone. In another room, they encounter merchants traveling between the human and heavenly realms, purchasing items. In a tower called the Warrior's Tomb located in the southeast, one door is sealed. Taking another path, they meet a sage who had met Zhao Yong in the past and helps the brave warriors who come to heaven 500 years later according to Zhao Yong's will. Yanzi and his group of four return to the previously sealed entrance, where the sage chants a spell to break the seal. Exiting the tower,

Continuing northward, they arrive at the Invincible Tower, eliminating countless monsters and passing through the tower to reach the gate of the Heavenly Palace. What was once bustling is now eerily quiet. Yanzi blows the Horn of the Gods, opening the gates of the Heavenly Palace. Approaching the palace, they meet the Celestial Emperor. The Celestial Emperor coldly dismisses their pleas, believing that humanity's mutual suspicions and sins are already numerous enough to warrant punishment from the demon realm. Regarding the appearance of monsters in the heavenly realm, the Celestial Emperor believes that heaven and hell are now one. Feeling this action is unjust, the Celestial Emperor angrily casts them down to the Eighteen Levels of Hell.

Hell

Cast down to hell by the Celestial Emperor, they are unwilling and seek a way back to the human world. Defeating many ghosts on the Bridge of Regret and encountering the Ferrying Ming King at the crossing of the Yin River, he demands ferry fees mid-journey upon seeing their precious items. Unable to bear it, they defeat the Ferrying Ming King, obtaining the Thunder God Gemstone. They approach King Yama, who shows no sympathy and sentences them to the oil pot. Enraged, they eliminate King Yama. Reaching the reincarnation cave, they kill countless demons. Meeting the Rebirth Ming King, a disagreement leads to his elimination, granting them the Celestial God Gemstone. Standing on the Bagua under the Rebirth Ming King, they are teleported to a room.

This room has eight doors, with a large Bagua in the middle. Different monsters hide behind the eight doors, with only one door leading out. After numerous battles, they discover the lower-left door is the exit. Yanzi decides to return to the human world and blows the Horn of the Gods, returning to Stone Bowl Village. That night, everyone has the same dream, dreaming of a deity descending on auspicious clouds to the human world. Originally, he is Tai Bai Jin Xing, saying the Celestial Emperor they saw in heaven was fake, being Gonggong's sworn brother, aiming to keep humanity perpetually suppressed. The true gods have been sealed by Gonggong, asking them to help lift the seal on the gods. Waking up, Yanzi and the others find many treasures left on the ground, deciding to lift the seal on the gods, starting with eliminating the imposter king Gonggu.

Southern Meng Kingdom

Leaving Stone Bowl Village, they head north to the Southern Meng Kingdom. On the road, they defeat many monsters. Along the way, they pass a bridge where one end is guarded by Gonggong's general, whom they defeat. Arriving at another small island, the forest is filled with demons. Exiting the forest leads to a grassland, with a village to the south for rest. Leaving the village and heading south, they reach the Southern Meng Kingdom. Battling into the palace, they see Gonggu above a room and defeat him. At this moment, Gonggong uses magic to appear before Yanzi and his companions, persuading them to surrender. Naturally refusing, a fierce battle ensues, eventually defeating Gonggong. Just as they rejoice, Gonggong moves again, revealing his original form. Yanzi summons the Martial God, Water God, Fire God, Earth God, and other gods to jointly attack Gonggong's original form. Golden light turns into golden swords, the sea rises in huge waves, divine light covers from above, and hellfire rises from below, all attacking Gonggong. To avoid the divine light, Gonggong collides with the Buzhou Mountain, causing earthquakes. Falling into the crack of Buzhou Mountain, Gonggong is thus sealed there, never to leave.

After Yanzi lifts the seal on the gods, the Celestial Emperor and the gods appear before Yanzi and his companions. Yanzi requests the Celestial Emperor restore happiness to the human world and revive the Dragon Island destroyed due to stealing the horn. The Goddess of the Milky Way also requests the restoration of peace in the Milky Way. The Celestial Emperor agrees to all, crowning Yanzi as the king of the Southern Meng Kingdom. However, Yanzi only wishes to return to Stone Bowl Village for a simple life and requests marriage with Xiaocui. The Celestial Emperor agrees to all. Afterwards, the two return to Stone Bowl Village and live happily ever after. The Milky Way Fairy is appointed to regulate the water system for favorable weather in the human world. The sage becomes the medicine god under Shennong. Murong Duan and the residents of Dragon Island are permanently listed among the immortals. Thus, the world enjoys eternal peace.
