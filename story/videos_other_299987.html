**Usage Method**:

1. Remove moisture and oil stains from the repair surface.

2. Shake the pen thoroughly to mix the pigment.

3. Unscrew the pen cap counterclockwise, dip an appropriate amount of pigment with a brush, and apply it to the repair surface.

4. After use, tighten the pen cap to prevent evaporation.

**Usage Precautions**:

- Do not open the pen cap when the product is placed horizontally or upside down to avoid liquid spillage.

- Store in a cool place out of reach of children.

- Avoid contact with skin or inhalation of volatile gases.

- This product should not be used for other purposes.
