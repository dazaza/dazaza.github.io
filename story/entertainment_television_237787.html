"Jewel in the Palace" is a family drama that tells the story of four siblings named after precious gems - Jade, Ruby, Coral, and Amber. Just like making a delicious bibimbap by mixing vegetables and meat, it was served to audiences on weekends. A MBC producer explained, "Jewel in the Palace tells the story of four children: Jade, Ruby, Coral, and Amber. When different gems are mixed together, will they really shine? When they collide, might they break? From the parents' perspective, all their children are equally dear. Thus, we use the metaphor of bibimbap." They further stated, "If family members coordinate well, they can become beautiful adornments; if not, they lose their luster. Jewel in the Palace presents diverse stories happening to each member of the family."
