" Newwebpick "

      Almost anyone who does design has seen Newwebpick, which brings together original content from the global design elite, and makes you think it is a foreign publication with a very good background in terms of international resources. However, it came from Hong Kong, China, and was founded by only two people. Over the years, NWP has adhered to its own ideals and responsibilities, and the works it chooses represent their own views and opinions.

      Every piece in Newwebpick will inspire people, whether in product design, packaging design or street art, all are creative and inspiring.

      In 2006, Newwebpick began charging for its fifth issue. Due to the high quality and high capacity of the previous four issues, NWP was still able to get the support of many readers after charging, and the number of customers who paid for subscriptions even exceeded the number of free downloads that year. A lot of people think that e-magazines can only be a free lunch and charging is not feasible, but Newwebpick tells us with practical actions that it is not because readers are too low in quality and do not want to pay, but because the content of many magazines is not good enough, and the really good content is still worth buying.
