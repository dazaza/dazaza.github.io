Equipment for National Defense Education Bases: The most advanced interactive simulation and large-scale weapon systems appear at the most impressive air defense national defense education base, surprising even the U.S. military! The first national-level defense culture project in China —— the China Air Defense Exposition Park in Zhaobao Mountain, Ningbo, Zhejiang Province is about to open. It is the first comprehensive venue in China to systematically exhibit and display knowledge of civil air defense and national defense education.

The Ningbo Air Defense Exposition Park has adopted for the first time the most advanced interactive simulation and large-scale weapon equipment developed by SuperView, a renowned expert in high-fidelity national defense education equipment manufacturing (website: www.super-view.com.cn, phone: 88553466, mobile: 13801105972).

After its opening, visitors will be able to directly participate in simulated interactive combat projects such as driving fighter planes, submarines, tanks, and sniping drug lords. This interactive high-fidelity combat system has evolved from the U.S. military's practical interactive training system.

1) Artillery experience with the theme of bombarding terrorists;

2) Submarine navy experience with the theme of underwater attacks;

3) J-20 fighter jet experience with the theme of "Eagle Strikes Long Sky";

4) Destroyer naval battle experience with the theme of Latin Bay peacekeeping;

5) Lone hero sniper experience with the theme of eliminating jungle terrorists;

6) Rocket launcher experience with the theme of hand-held rockets attacking armed helicopters;

7) Tank battle experience with the theme of offensive warfare.

The China Air Defense Exposition Park is located in the Zhaobao Mountain Scenic Area, with a total investment of 180 million yuan, covering an area of 40,000 square meters. It includes a prelude hall, a civil air defense tunnel exhibition hall, an outdoor weapon display area, and a military experience area. The park will feature a dedication by Defense Minister Liang Guanglie.

Inside the panoramic movie theater, visitors can watch films on a century of air defense history. A 1,700-meter-long civil air defense tunnel will form a light and shadow time-space tunnel where visitors can not only appreciate sculptures and murals but also experience multimedia technology-created war air raid environments, including London's air defense during WWII, Pearl Harbor's air defense, air defense during the Korean War, air defense in the former Yugoslavia during the Kosovo War, and air defense in northern Vietnam.

SuperView's most advanced interactive simulation and large-scale weapon equipment are located in the military experience area, which includes aviation, navigation, ground air defense, and land warfare zones. In the aviation zone, you can experience flying over your hometown, takeoff and landing, air-to-ground attack, air-to-sea attack, and air-to-air attack. In the navigation zone, you can experience the feeling of commanding a ship through the waves. In the ground air defense zone, there are experiences such as anti-aircraft machine gun shooting, 37mm artillery shooting, and shoulder-launched anti-aircraft missile shooting. In the land warfare zone, there are armored vehicle driving experiences and sharpshooter shooting experiences.

Of course, all these experiences feel just like real guns and ammunition, giving people a sense of being right there on the battlefield.

The exposition park will be a comprehensive tourism base integrating exhibition, academic research, cultural exchange, and tourism, and also a cultural project for patriotism and revolutionary heroism education. It can systematically spread air defense and civil air defense knowledge and skills to the public, making learning enjoyable, filling the gap in China for a systematic air defense education venue.
