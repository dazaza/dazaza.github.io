When purchasing incubators, livestock farms and professional breeders should consider the following aspects: 1. The hatching rate is the most important indicator to measure the quality of the equipment. It is also the main reason why many hatcheries are willing to spend a lot of money to upgrade to advanced incubation equipment. The measurement field inside the machine should be uniform without any temperature dead angles; otherwise, it will reduce the chick survival rate. In terms of temperature control precision, Han display intelligence is better than clear computers, and clear computers are better than integrated circuits. 2. Machine usage cost, such as electricity fees and maintenance costs. 3. Circuit design should be reasonable with perfect aging detection equipment. Moreover, after the entire machine is assembled, it should undergo an aging test for a period of time to ensure its performance before use. 4. Good after-sales service, which includes fast service speed and long-term service. You should choose manufacturers with larger scale, good development momentum, and the ability to provide long-term services as much as possible. 5. Long service life. Service life mainly depends on the materials used, thickness of materials, and quality of electrical components, so these should be carefully observed when purchasing. Additionally, the type of goods is also an important aspect to consider when choosing an incubator.
