Quanzhou Boshi Anorectal Hospital, the only professional anorectal hospital in Minnan, with expert consultations, advanced technology, and trusted by the people.

by xiaoyangzi1988 on 2012-03-09 10:16:40

Quanzhou Boshi Anus Hospital is a large anus hospital in the Minnan area that integrates prevention, treatment, rehabilitation, scientific research and academic exchange with both Chinese and Western medicine. The hospital is located at Yiquan Building on Yiquan Street in Quanzhou, with elegant and simple environment and convenient transportation.

Quanzhou Boshi Anus Hospital

The hospital relies on the brand and technical advantages of the Minnan Anus Disease Research Institute, embracing inclusiveness and combining Chinese and Western medicine to establish a systematic, scientific, and safe diagnostic and treatment system in the field of Chinese anal and intestinal diagnosis and treatment. The hospital has established long-term technical cooperation relationships with multiple well-known medical institutions, boasting a team of nationally renowned experts composed of leading figures and professors in the field of anorectal diseases from the Minnan and Southeast regions, with rich clinical experience and outstanding professional skills.

In terms of management, the hospital introduces international modern hospital management concepts, implementing standardized and regulated management. The hospital strictly adheres to various health management rules and regulations set by the national, provincial, municipal, and district levels. Each functional management department continuously strengthens quality and safety management, values the technical characteristics and rich experience of authoritative experts, effectively preventing the occurrence of medical disputes and accidents, allowing patients to "treat with peace of mind and recover comfortably."

Authoritative Experts Build a Nationally Renowned Anorectal Department

As a key distinctive specialty of Quanzhou, the Boshi Anorectal Diagnosis and Treatment Center possesses advanced diagnostic equipment and technology. Collaborating with the National Anorectal Society and the National Anorectal Treatment Center, the hospital invites multiple top experts, medical advisors, and renowned experts in the field of anorectal diseases for regular consultations. The hospital gathers the most authoritative individuals in the field of anorectal diseases from all over the country, relying on experienced anorectal disease experts from Beijing and Shanghai, establishing extensive academic exchanges with domestic and international hospitals and academic institutions, aiming to create a nationally renowned anorectal department.

Authoritative Therapies Highly Praised within the Industry and by Patients

Boshi Hospital adopts multiple cutting-edge technologies and scientific achievements from around the world, aligning the technical level of modern clinical diagnosis, treatment, and medical research with international standards. This not only significantly enhances the hospital's clinical diagnostic and treatment capabilities but also lays a solid foundation for the hospital's research work. By exploring the unique aspects of traditional Chinese medicine in the diagnosis and treatment of anorectal surgery and applying modern high-tech equipment and minimally invasive treatments, the duration of treatment is shortened, greatly reducing the pain and burden on patients, marking a significant breakthrough in the history of anorectal disease treatment.

Introduction of Internationally Advanced Diagnostic Equipment

Adhering to the concept of professionalism, minimally invasive procedures, and painless treatments, the Boshi Anorectal Diagnosis and Treatment Center meets the high-level needs of society for medical technology and services. It was the first to invest heavily in introducing advanced medical equipment systems from around the world, including the American Johnson PPH minimally invasive hemorrhoid treatment, American Cook Hemorrhoid Gun, An Therapy, Japanese Olympus electronic colonoscope, South Korean DR.camscoper computerized anorectal disease examination system, colonic hydrotherapy machine, DC-type ultrasonic nebulization fumigation instrument, American HCPT electrocoagulation and electrocutting, DNR plasma low-temperature treatment instrument, South Korean electronic anorectal endoscope, and more. These advancements ensure that the technical level of modern clinical diagnosis and treatment as well as medical research aligns with international standards. The application of modern high-tech equipment and minimally invasive treatments shortens the course of treatment, significantly reducing the suffering and burden on patients, marking a major breakthrough in the history of anorectal disease treatment.

Separate Diagnoses for Men and Women, Protecting Female Privacy

Surrounding the service philosophy of "people-oriented, patient-centered," Boshi Anus Hospital continuously strengthens its service awareness, standardizes service behavior, optimizes the service environment, and improves service quality. It was the first to implement the "separate diagnosis for men and women" system, setting up a female-specific diagnosis area, providing multiple services such as full female medical guidance, female expert consultation, and female expert surgeries. The private medical environment makes women feel particularly cared for. The hospital also implements multiple service systems such as "one doctor, one patient, one room," "first-visit responsibility system," "three-meter smile system," ensuring that patients feel secure, reassured, and comfortable during their visits. To facilitate patient visits, the hospital offers telephone reservations, online electronic registration, establishes a hospital website system, sets up a doctor-patient consultation interaction platform, and builds a bridge for communication between patients and the hospital.
