Case 3:

Three friends on a self-driving tour accidentally fell into a valley 150 meters deep, and were trapped for three nights and four days before being rescued. Reminder:

The nationwide 112 hotline can be dialed even when the phone has no signal reception, or when the power is extremely low. Any brand of phone can dial this number from any location. After dialing 112, you will enter a voice prompt as follows: This is the 112 mobile emergency rescue hotline. If you need to report a case, please press 0, we will connect you to the police station; if you need assistance, please press 9, we will connect you to the fire department. After the Chinese version, the English version will follow. At this point, pressing 0 or 9 will ensure someone answers. In the situation of these three individuals, or if lost while climbing mountains or encountering other difficulties, pressing 9 will provide timely assistance.
