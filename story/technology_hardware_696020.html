Yaying Official Flagship Store: 56-year-old Ice King buried in ice for 128 minutes, breaking the world record

by upnf5136 on 2012-03-07 19:19:02

Naked into the "South Pole" - The penguin exhibition area is set up to simulate the penguin's living environment - Antarctica. The temperature here is about -10℃, and the crystal box where Ice King is located will be further cooled down by ice blocks, with the temperature inside around -30℃. Staff members of the Polar Oceanarium introduced this.

Jin Songhao claims success was within expectations - Eating popsicles, drinking cold beer - Within expectations - Challenge successful.

Staff helped him remove the ice around him. After moving slightly, Jin Songhao walked to the backstage. His body showed no discomfort, just a bit redness. While drying off the ice chips on his body with a towel, he drank a cup of warm water. A few minutes later, he had already adapted to the room temperature of the "Northern Hemisphere." Walking out of the rest room, Jin Songhao smiled and said: "The environment of Tianjin Haichang Polar Ocean World is very suitable for challenging the limits of cold resistance. The enthusiasm of the tourists also gave me great support. So the success of this challenge can be said to be an expected event."

In an environment of -30℃, eating popsicles, drinking cold beer, drawing comics, feeding penguins... This year, Jin Songhao, who has just been honored with the title of "World's First Ice King," surpassed himself again at the Tianjin Haichang Polar Ocean World. He broke his own world record of 120 minutes by enduring being buried in ice naked for 128 minutes, successfully defending the title of "World's First Ice King."

Facing this stranger, the little penguins were very curious, constantly forming groups to come and watch. They either looked up or paced around, their cute actions making the surrounding tourists burst out laughing. Around 15:00, it was time to feed the penguins. Curious penguins abandoned watching and ran towards the keepers. Seeing this scene, the "Ice King" proposed to personally feed the penguins. A careful emperor penguin approached the "Ice King," hesitating several times before finally getting close. Then the "Ice King" handed the fish to the emperor penguin, who stretched out its long beak to snatch the fish and proudly turned away, as if boasting to the surrounding little penguins: "Did you see? The 'Ice King' personally fed me!"

Perhaps in order to further show his cold-resistant qualities, Jin Songhao asked the staff to bring three popsicles, which he ate one by one. Still not satisfied, he opened three bottles of chilled beer and drank them heartily. During the eating and drinking process, he didn't forget to showcase his talents, holding a pen in one hand and a board in the other, writing words such as "Tribute to the People of Tianjin" and "I Love Tianjin Haichang Polar Ocean World," and even drew a comic for the adorable penguins.

Newspaper reporter Wang Jing, intern Liu Jie

Writing and drawing with nimble fingers

When the timer broke the 120-minute mark, everyone present began cheering for the "Ice King," but the "Ice King" continued to challenge his own limits, pushing toward 128 minutes. Accompanied by a 10-second countdown, Jin Songhao overcame himself amidst the cheers of the tourists, creating a new record.

On August 6 at 13:30, the 56-year-old "World's First Ice King" Jin Songhao came to the Tianjin Haichang Polar Ocean World. Wearing only a pair of shorts, he walked into the penguin living area. This scene made the "little masters" of the pavilion curious, boldly looking at the "Ice King" following the keeper. After doing 25 minutes of warm-up exercises, Jin Songhao calmly sat in the prepared crystal box, as the staff poured about 0.8 cubic meters of ice into it, covering up to his shoulders.
