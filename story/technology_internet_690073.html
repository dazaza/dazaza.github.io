Itinerary Overview: Joint tour of three provinces (Anhui, Jiangxi, Zhejiang), great value and leisure - The World's First Strange Mountain - [Mount Huangshan], The First Fairy Mountain in the South - [Mount Sanqing], China's Most Beautiful Village - [Wuyuan], Hollywood of China - [Hengdian World Studios], Venice of China - [Ancient City of Shaoxing]

★ Day 1:

Meals: Self-catered

Accommodation: On the train

From Shenzhen to Jiangxi Shangrao. Enjoy the scenery on the train, entertainment activities organized in each carriage. Overnight on the train.

★ Day 2:

Meals: Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner

Accommodation: Tielong Mountain or Fengjing or Jiangwan

From Shangrao to Mount Sanqing (about 2 hours by car). After arrival, take the southern cable car up the mountain (optional, costs 70 yuan per person for the upward ride, about 3 hours if walking). Visit scenic areas such as Nanching Park, Donghai Coast, Xihai Coast, etc. Optional cable car down the mountain (costs 55 yuan per person for the downward ride, about 2.5 hours if walking). Check into a hotel near the foot of Mount Sanqing or Wuyuan (about 1.5 hours by car).

★ Day 3:

Meals: Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner

Accommodation: Huashan Hotel or Guomai Grand Hotel

Visit "Heavenly Jiangling", known as one of the "Four Great Rapeseed Flower Seas of China". Visit Moon Bay, also known as Little Moon Bay, and Hong Village, the hometown of longevity. Proceed to Huangshan city area. Dinner and check-in.

★ Day 4:

Meals: Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner

Accommodation: Huashan Hotel or Guomai Grand Hotel

One-day tour of Huangshan Scenic Area. Take an optional cable car up the mountain (about 10 minutes, about 2 hours if walking). Visit Yungu Cableway, Guangming Peak, Feilai Stone, Yunhai Pavilion, Beihai Scenic Area, etc. Optional cable car down the mountain (about 10 minutes, about 2 hours if walking). Proceed to Tunxi. Dinner and check-in.

★ Day 5:

Meals: Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner

Accommodation: Guomao Hotel or Guest House

Travel to Hengdian Film Studio (about 3.5 hours by bus). Participate in film shooting experience activities. Visit Qingyi Bamboo Health Living Museum. Optional visit to Mysterious Dream Valley.

★ Day 6:

Meals: Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner

Accommodation: Xin Zhou Gulf or Qinwang

Travel to Qin Wang Palace, visit Xishi’s Hometown in Zhuji. Participate in interesting sports events. Proceed to Shaoxing. Visit Dong Lake, enjoy Yue Opera performance at Shen Garden.

★ Day 7:

Meals: Breakfast, Lunch

Accommodation: On the train

Visit Lu Xun’s Hometown, stroll along Lu Xun Road. Proceed to Hangzhou, visit West Lake, Silk Performance. Return to Shenzhen by train.

★ Day 8:

Arrive at Shenzhen Train Station. Say goodbye to fellow travelers and end the trip.
