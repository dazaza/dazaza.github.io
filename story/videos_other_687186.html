Building the first brand of LeiXin no-fired brick machine with core competitiveness

In order to implement the "Twelfth Five-Year Plan for National Economic and Social Development", and enforce the basic national policies of saving resources and protecting the environment, relevant ministries have specially organized the compilation of the "Guiding Opinions on Wall Material Reform during the Twelfth Five-Year Plan". This document proposes the guiding ideology, basic principles, main objectives, key tasks, and policy measures for wall material reform during the Twelfth Five-Year Plan period.

The purpose of this initiative is to promote the rapid development of new types of wall materials. By 2011, the output of new types of wall materials across the country had accounted for 55% of the total output of wall materials, an increase of 11 percentage points compared to 2005, and a production and application pattern mainly based on new types of wall materials has basically taken shape. The cumulative area of newly constructed energy-saving buildings using new types of wall materials reached 4.8 billion square meters, more than three times the amount at the end of the Tenth Five-Year Plan period. The number of enterprises with an annual production capacity of over 60 million standard bricks of new types of wall materials reached more than 5,000, an increase of over 50% compared to the end of the Tenth Five-Year Plan period, changing the situation where traditional wall material enterprises were mainly small and scattered brick kilns. As the leader of LeiXin Machinery, the first brand in China's no-fired brick machine, brick machine, and brick making machine industry, Mr. Guo Lei always feels that the burden on his shoulders is very heavy. LeiXin Machinery, as one of the few domestic enterprises with independent intellectual property rights in the production of no-fired brick machines, brick machines, and hollow brick machines, has strong technical strength. The "LeiXin" series of brick machines produced by it fully comply with national product quality standards. Modernized production management and technical management systems, along with perfect after-sales service, ensure product quality and eliminate users' worries. Products are sold all over the country and are highly trusted by a wide range of users. Products are subject to "three guarantees", and professional technicians are equipped to install, debug, and train personnel on-site, fully serving customers well after-sales.

Mr. Guo also told reporters: "I do not quite agree with the approach of setting thresholds for the no-fired brick machine industry to squeeze out small individual households. Although the formulators of these thresholds have already become large and strong, they should also consider that ten years ago, they themselves may have been small individual households. Even if the industry really needs an entry threshold, I believe that this threshold must be the market, and it cannot be any kind of artificial coercive force. Through these years of development and reflection, LeiXin Machinery has deeply realized that to avoid being excluded by the market threshold, we must have a certain scale. For all no-fired brick machine, brick machine, and brick making machine enterprises, how to strengthen their core competitiveness is the key to improving profitability. It is not about fantasizing about setting thresholds. Only through full market competition will differentiation occur, and excellent no-fired brick machine, brick machine, aerated block enterprises will stand out." http://www.fsjljx.com
