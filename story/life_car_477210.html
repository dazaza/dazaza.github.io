From which three aspects can car maintenance start?

Nowadays, more and more families have their own private cars. In order to ensure that the car has good usability and reliability, people usually maintain the car to extend its service life and improve its safety, thus saving unnecessary car repair troubles. Therefore, the awareness of car maintenance must be strengthened. The so-called "seven-tenths maintenance and three-tenths repair" is the truth. If the car is well maintained, it can save more repair costs. Car maintenance mainly focuses on three aspects:

1. Body maintenance. Body maintenance is also commonly known as car beauty care. Its main purpose is to remove various oxidation and corrosion inside and outside the car body, then protect it, and try to highlight the beauty and brilliance of the car. It mainly includes: car paint maintenance, seat cushion carpet maintenance, bumper, car skirt maintenance, instrument panel maintenance, electroplating processing maintenance, leather plastic maintenance, tire, wheel hub warranty, windshield maintenance, chassis maintenance, engine exterior maintenance, etc.

2. Body renewal. This involves diagnosis and treatment of deep scratches, multi-material bumper repair, hard injury repair of wheel hubs (covers), material renewal of leather and chemical fibers, color renewal of engines, etc.

Car maintenance is divided into two major categories: regular maintenance and irregular maintenance:

Regular maintenance includes: daily maintenance, first-level maintenance, second-level maintenance;

Irregular maintenance includes: break-in period maintenance and seasonal maintenance. The main work of car maintenance does not go beyond cleaning, inspection, tightening, adjustment, and lubrication.

However, with the development of science and technology and the automobile industry, various advanced technologies centered on computers are widely applied in cars, making future cars gradually intelligent, and therefore the content of car maintenance is given new connotations.

3. Interior maintenance. Body maintenance aims to keep the car looking youthful, while interior maintenance aims to allow the car to travel hundreds of thousands of kilometers without major repairs, ensuring that the car remains in the best technical condition. It mainly includes: maintenance of the lubrication system, fuel system, cooling system, braking system, carburetor (injector) etc.

Many shops now offer car maintenance services, such as 4S stores and specialized beauty shops, all of which can perform repair and maintenance. Car maintenance is mainly aimed at maintaining and restoring the technical performance of the car, keeping it in a good condition. Therefore, for car owners, understanding the basic nursing knowledge of these three aspects of maintenance can help us better take care of our beloved cars.
