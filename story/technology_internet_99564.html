An Alternative Introduction to the Heroes of the Three Kingdoms~

by ufo on 2008-10-29 17:55:57

1. Emperor Xian of Han: A descendant of a wealthy family in decline. His ancestor, Liu Bang, had once enjoyed a period of glory. He had average academic performance and leadership skills but relied on his ancestors' reputation to secure the nominal position of class monitor. He was often manipulated by others, bullied by the class bully Dong Zhuo in his early days, and later exploited by the nouveau riche Cao Cao, leaving him extremely frustrated.

2. Cao Cao: Pale complexion, crafty, and skilled at winning people over. In his youth, he had secretly clashed with the class bully Dong Zhuo, who discovered him. Through his wit, Cao narrowly escaped unscathed and made a comeback. Afterwards, he frequently used the pretext of helping Class Monitor Emperor Xian to gang up against classmates Sun Quan and Liu Bei.

3. Liu Bei: Prone to crying, large ears. His family circumstances were average in his youth but gradually declined later, leading him to make a living selling mats. He had some familial connection with Emperor Xian, often referring to him as "Big Nephew." He was rich in scheming, frequently going out together with Guan Yu and Zhang Fei. Recently, he has been closely interacting with classmate Zhuge Liang.

4. Sun Quan: Pale purple complexion. His early performance was average, but he made significant progress under the guidance of his older brother Sun Ce. Although his sister once sat next to Liu Bei, they frequently had disputes, even escalating to irreconcilable conflicts.

5. Guan Yu: Red face, values righteousness, a close buddy of Liu Bei. Originating from wielding a knife, his academic performance was average. During battles, he was ruthless, having severely beaten classmates such as Yan Liang, Wen Chou, and Cai He.

6. Zhang Fei: Large eyes, dark face, irascible temperament, mature for his age, with a bristly beard. A close friend of Liu Bei, he rode a black horse and wielded a long spear. During the group fights involving Liu Bei and Cao Cao, he stepped forward courageously, shouting so loudly that he broke the Dangyang Bridge.

7. Zhuge Liang: Intelligent and studious. Before entering the scene, he lived without contention. Later, after being recruited by Liu Bei, he joined Liu's small group. After joining, he fully facilitated the relationship between Liu Bei and Sun Quan, enabling their alliance and gaining the upper hand in battles against Cao Cao. The only drawback is his penchant for fire attacks; since enrolling, he has set fires three times in different settings.

8. Zhou Yu: A close buddy of Sun Quan, handsome, and an excellent leader who sat next to the class beauty Xiao Qiao. Early in his life, he was very successful, but later frequently engaged in fierce competition with the intelligent and studious Zhuge Liang, often getting so angry that he fell ill.

9. Huang Gai: A close buddy of Zhou Yu, fond of wielding sticks. In his youth, he was mischievous and often got spanked by his parents, eventually developing an iron-clad bottom. During the alliance between Sun and Liu against Cao Cao, he used his bottom as the medium for the stratagem of feigned surrender, achieving remarkable success.

10. Jiang Gan: A close buddy of Cao Cao and a classmate of Zhou Yu from kindergarten. Average ability but eager to show off. During the battle between Cao Cao and the Sun-Liu coalition, he attended a kindergarten reunion hosted by Zhou Yu, where he couldn't help but flaunt himself. He stole a secret letter from Zhou Yu and handed it over to Cao Cao, causing classmates Cai Mao and Zhang Yun to be unjustly beaten by Cao Cao. After realizing this, Cao almost beat him.

11. Dian Wei: A close buddy of Cao Cao and one of the three strongest men in the class. When Cao Cao competed with Zhang Xiu for a girlfriend, Dian Wei bravely stepped forward and was severely beaten by Zhang Xiu and his gang of social loafers.

12. Diaochan: The only school belle in the class, one of the four greatest beauties in history, stunningly beautiful. After marrying, she was bullied by the class bully Dong Zhuo. Later, she was rescued by classmate Lü Bu, to whom she pledged herself. Unfortunately, fate was cruel, and soon after, Lü Bu was defeated by Cao Cao. She then left home and has been missing ever since.
