3. What is the most important thing for a successful store?

Store credibility, rather than the store's *********, because virtual point card product prices are all similar, with only a few cents difference, buyers don't care about that small amount. What they care about is service and credibility.

4. What should I sell to earn credibility when I first open my store?

5. Will anyone come to my store if I have no credibility when I first open it?

7. Why are the prices you give me similar to those on Paipai and Taobao? How can I make money then?

8. How much can I make in a month when I first open my store?

No one can answer this question for you. Making money isn't that easy. Please first ask yourself how much effort you've put in, then consider what kind of return you deserve! Without putting in any effort and expecting high income is impossible. In short, when you've put in a lot, you naturally understand how to make money. The internet is a money tree, whether you can shake it depends on yourself. As long as you put in the effort, there will always be rewards. If someone advertises guaranteeing you a certain income from opening an online store within a month, then you definitely shouldn't believe them; they're very good at deceiving you. The early stage of opening a store focuses on earning credibility, while the later stage considers making money. A smart person should understand this principle.

9. Can I make money selling cards?

Personal experience: Whether you want to make money or not is up to you. Big sellers rely on selling cards to make money, each card earning a few cents to a few jiao. Thin profit but large volume. Beginners are advised to sell at cost. Once you have high credibility, you can start making money. You can develop lower-level sellers, set the card purchase price for them, earn the difference, and charge agency fees.

10. Does the store need to be online 24 hours?

It doesn't need to be online 24 hours. As long as QQ is online, you can sell, it's not that complicated.

11. Does opening a store require a lot of capital turnover?

Not a lot is needed, because there's no need to stockpile card codes in advance. When a customer buys, you need to restock from the website, so a few dozen to a hundred yuan is enough for turnover.

Only automatic shipping products require purchasing card codes in advance and storing them on Paipai, requiring a large amount of capital.

11. How to set up automatic shipping?

Automatic shipping is Paipai's system. Paipai has requirements: store credibility of 30, store open for three months, apply for the integrity plan, and you're set.

(Automatic shipping requires purchasing card codes in advance and storing them in the Paipai system, meaning a lot of capital turnover is needed.)

If someone's advertisement tells you they'll teach you how to set up automatic shipping, don't believe them. They're very good at deceiving you. Once you hear automatic shipping, which allows sales 24/7, you'll be tempted. However, the automatic shipping they teach costs several jiao more than regular automatic shipping. For beginners, such expensive cards are almost unsellable, and they also require you to post hundreds of items one by one. Purely deceptive.
