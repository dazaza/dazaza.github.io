Feelings, February 23, 2012. The me you never understood,

by lp49x7 on 2012-02-27 12:17:25

When you clicked to enter this log, have you ever thought about whether you really understand me? The most popular shoes this year, even if only a little bit, the family around me, friends who accompanied me, or those so-called former lovers, I ask, do you understand me? First of all, let's talk to those friends I considered as friends: when reading here, some people may ask, how could they be considered as friends? Because somehow, I feel that after all these turns, I am still alone. I dare not say how much I have paid, but I have never wronged anyone or owed anyone anything. So why do I still feel my heart getting colder and colder? You may think I'm stupid. To put it nicely, I'm naive, right? You think that because I don't say anything, I don't understand anything. But is that true? In fact, I don't say anything because I care about your feelings. I really think that friends are not easy to make, so I cherish them very much. But I gradually realize that everything, real or fake, why can you know everything about me, but I know almost nothing about you? I don't like to ask questions because I think if you want me to know, you will tell me without asking. If you don't want me to know, asking is useless. But I was wrong. You didn't understand. You know me as someone with a single-minded thinking pattern. Even if I know that I will be hurt badly if I continue, I will still go on. But for what? Do you know? I don't want to regret the road I've walked. Just like now, I have no regrets. But for you, I don't know what to do. I have been away from Ming Shui for more than a year. It's not a long time, but it has made me see through too many things. I dare not imagine, it's too tiring. I started not contacting anyone, not letting anyone find me. But I didn't disconnect my QQ. I gave you a chance, and myself a chance. There are too many things pressing in my heart, making me unable to breathe. Waiting for too long makes my heart numb. I'm learning to let myself not care, but I still feel reluctant. At this moment, someone always tells me, just let it go, people leave and tea gets cold. It took me a long time to make myself accept it. While accepting this sentence, I forced myself to accept so-called reality. Do you know? I never think of anyone as extremely bad. In my world, everything is so beautiful and simple. My parents always tell me, in society, you won't get far by being like this. I never cared. I think if I treat you well, I will treat you well. If I don't like you, I won't pay attention to you. Straightforward. I always thought that if I show you my worst side and you can accept it, then you won't dislike the rest of me. Everything seems to be fine. But I didn't expect that the world is so ugly. Is it because I thought too little, or because you are too scheming? Just like when I pour out my heart completely, reality gives me a hard slap. It tells me, wake up! My world is actually small. I don't need many people around me for eating, drinking, and having fun. What I want is a lifetime friendship, even if there is only one friend who truly deserves the title. Do you understand? Although I am the youngest in front of anyone, I understand no less than you, and I think no less than you. Friends, unconsciously, everything is far away, changed. I don't know, after saying this, will anyone scold me? But I really hope someone will scold me. I'm mean, I want to know if anyone cares about me. Maybe no one does, because now in the world, except for my parents, no one makes me feel that I'm not alone. Friend, are you willing to be that person? Now, let's talk to my dear relatives: For the first time, I bring up the topic about you. In your eyes, I am sensible, obedient, caring for home, filial, capable. Perhaps besides having a bad temper and not liking studying, I have no blemish. These are correct because I haven't done anything wrong that makes you say bad things about me. I haven't made any mistakes, really none at all. But what you don't understand is the pressure. Since childhood, you watched me grow up, everywhere I went, I was liked because of being sensible. Moreover, I am the youngest. Dad and Mom only have me as their daughter, so I am pampered. A few years ago, I always thought I was the happiest, having everything. When I was a child, I just thought that as long as I don't do anything against my parents or make them lose face, it would be enough. Gradually, I grew up and vaguely realized that it wasn't the case. Truly understanding worldly affairs, I found everything so dark. I don't remember many things that shattered my fantasy about this world. I began to understand what happens when something goes wrong - there's only me left. Even if it's a small matter, I saw indifference. How ironic, yet I was sad. But dad told me this is normal. I didn't understand at that time, but I remembered everything. Later, I slowly realized that my parents live for me. Without me, their whole life would be meaningless. Dad also said never to disgrace our parents. I promised. From that day on, I kept telling myself that I cannot easily give up on anything, especially my life. It's not only mine. I decided to strive for dad's pride and not let anyone look down on me. I have been working hard. Therefore, I don't know why, but pressure came along with it. I didn't stop schooling because I disliked the school environment. I just wanted to rely on myself earlier. I like being at school because I don't have to think about so many things. But I'm not cut out for studying to become successful, so I don't want to waste time. In fact, I did pretty well. Ha, I'm happy that I can rely on myself. I achieved these, but what about the others? What I want to say is about my rare emotions. Since childhood, as an only child, I value the feelings between brothers and sisters. I never quarreled or got angry with my older siblings. It's because I am the youngest, and they let me. We were often not together playing. I am young, you didn't want to spoil me, I understand. But in recent years, as we are busy with our own careers or other things, we spend less and less time together. I'm afraid our feelings will fade, or because of too many things, there will be estrangement among us five. We have talked more than once about the grievances of the previous generation, which have nothing to do with us. We don't care, we don't ask, we deal with our own lives. Brothers and sisters, I hope more than you that it stays this way. After all, I am the youngest sister. You say, I understand. Brothers and sisters, do you know how tired your younger sister is? I don't know who to talk to about the things in my heart. You know your younger sister is sensible, but she has too many things in her heart, under too much pressure. I want you to see me bringing glory to the family. Among us five, I am the weakest link. Relatives, you don't know how much I have changed in a year. Some think our family is struggling and had to go abroad. Some say, living well at home, why go out? But who knows that I chose to come out myself. I want to do something to make our family better, to make my parents better. This time when I returned home, I saw many changes in people. Frankly speaking, I was sad. Dad came home drunk one night and cried hugging me. No one knew, only the two of us. Dad cried saying he doesn't have a son. My heart ached. But dad said he doesn't need one, because he thinks having me is enough. My heart aches not for other reasons. I blame myself for being 20 years old and not amounting to anything. I told dad, raise me as a son. But dad said, let them see that a daughter can do just as well. Dad said I can't go astray, otherwise, it would be the day he leaves me. I have been doing, striving hard. I must be stronger than anyone else. Yes, I am a girl, but that doesn't mean I can't do it. Dad said, we owe no one, no one owes us. So, I will keep my head high. I won't beg anyone. Debts of gratitude are unpayable, and I won't owe them. From the time I can remember until now, my parents have never hidden family matters from me. Whoever has been good to me, or whatever else, I remember every detail clearly. Relatives, don't think I am still a child. I give myself pressure to motivate myself. Then, should such a me be looked down upon just because I am a girl? Talking about former lovers: Actually, I want to ask, lover? Does it count? Maybe. This question is quite laughable because in love, I feel that I have always been in the role of a lover. I might not even know what it feels like to be loved or cared for by someone, of course excluding the love I didn't accept. Maybe someone cared about me, but I only had eyes for one person, or no one could enter my heart. I used to think this was the kind of love I wanted, knowing only to consider others' feelings while forgetting my true self. I accommodated everything, agreed with everything, as long as you're happy. But I seem to forget what you like about me. Each relationship ends with me losing myself. I thought it was because I wasn't good enough, but the answers were always that I was too good. Later, I realized it was because of my accommodation, my lack of self, making me seem boring. That's how I feel. But in each relationship, I treated it sincerely, yet they couldn't last. However, my persistence, my accommodation, my dedication, the belief that led me to the end, weren't wasted. Every relationship left me battered and bruised, and I only gave up when I couldn't love anymore. Only after the pain did I completely forget. Every relationship ended with me giving up in the end, and only when I gave up was it truly over. I tried my best, so I have no regrets. Not cherishing, I am happy that everyone who was with me regretted it, that's enough. I won't give anyone the chance to hurt me again. Now, I just want to find someone who can bring me happiness, someone who knows my worth and treats me well because of it. I appear strong, because no one can truly care for me, take care of me, allowing me to remove the mask that doesn't belong to me. I am not promiscuous, not someone who can fall in love with anyone. If you don't want to take responsibility, don't wander in and out of my world. If I love, it's hard to let go. If you're not brave enough to love such a person, why bother to provoke. Everyone has their own life. Now I am relying on myself, earning money. Someone asked, are you satisfied with your current life? I would say I am satisfied. Not as good as some, but much better than many. Looking at peers living luxuriously, are you envious? Yes, I am envious, but that's not hers. My luxury comes from my own earnings. Theirs either come from exploiting parents or selling themselves. I sympathize more than envy. Ironically, in the past year, after doing what I should do, I will be selfish once.
