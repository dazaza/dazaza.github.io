The Guangzhou Lijing Plaza VENNGER store underwent an image upgrade and rebranding (click on the picture to view the gallery). The new VENNGER Guangzhou Lijing Plaza store represents a new speed and starting point with an upgraded image that delivers a perfect visual feast!

On July 28, 2011, VENNGER, the British-style men’s wear brand, under the direction of the company’s upper management, saw collaboration among various functional departments as the Lijing Plaza store was reopened to the public after a three-month renovation and upgrade. In addition to expanding the area from the original 80 square meters to 120 square meters, the overall upgrade of the VENNGER specialty store image was more importantly applied. The design uses grids of varying intensity as the main design element, creatively addressing both branding and display needs.

New Image - Bold Experimentation, Leading Men's Fashion Trends

Approaching the 80-square-meter "VENNGER" Lijing store, a simple yet fashionable and fresh atmosphere immediately greets you. The clever use of transparent grilles paired with contrasting color schemes conveys a serene sense that transcends time, offering fashion enthusiasts and workplace elites a perfect shopping experience. The store combines an image showcase area with a product display area, making the entire space compact yet not crowded, creating a feeling of unexpected depth as you walk through it.

New Thinking - Creating Spatial Beauty for Consumers' Perfect Shopping Experience

The new "VENNGER" store achieves this by skillfully combining counters and display racks, providing consumers with ample space to explore and carefully select premium "VENNGER" products. Within this not-so-large space, the thoughtful layout makes the area appear grand yet delicate.

New Starting Point - Brand and Service Upgrade

From the store's decoration results to the product arrangement and equipment, the Lijing VENNGER store's renovation and upgrade have received high praise from loyal customers. As one of the most popular and influential men's fashion brands in the Lijing Plaza business district, VENNGER focuses on professional expertise. This renovation is just another starting point for the VENNGER brand.

The Shenzhen Wenger Apparel Co., Ltd., as the operating body of the "VENNGER" brand, will continue to build on its previous success based on product value. It aims to further explore the enhancement of the VENNGER brand value, offering consumers even more fashionable, considerate products and services, thus achieving even greater success.

For "VENNGER," this is a turning point, marking the beginning of enhancing brand strength...
