Man twice abducted cellmate's child after being released from prison

by xue96pwsh on 2012-03-08 14:26:01

Under the help of the policemen, Hu's daughter was finally back to her biological parents. One man didn't repent after he was released from jail and extended his poisonous hand to his former cellmate. He approached his old jail mate twice to win their trust and then took away their children for illegal trading. After being wanted on the Internet by the police in Zhengzhou and Luoyang for 8 years, this man was finally captured successfully by the policemen in Songshan Police Station. The policemen then rescued the two abducted children successfully within 2 months. By reporter Liu Tao and photojournalist Bai Tao of the Evening News. Conceiving evil thoughts, Zhang sold a former cellmate's son who was only 6 months old. Zhang Dahai, from Dancheng County of Zhoukou City, was sentenced to 15 years in prison for robbery in 1984 and was paroled at the end of 1995. Since then, he had been guarding bikes at the Zhengzhou Railway Station. In July 2003, Zhang met his former cellmate Chen Changhai loitering at the railway station. Zhang not only treated him to dinner but also invited him home in Xiaoli Zhuang of Erqi District. Unexpectedly, he "invited the wolf into his room". During this time, Zhang went out to do something and his wife was busy with housework, leaving her 6-month-old son with Chen Changhai. Chen said to her: "Sister-in-law, I will take the baby out for a walk." Zhang's wife agreed. Later, Chen sold the child to a Sun family in Sijia Zhuang for 3000 yuan. Hard to change bad habits, he extended his hands to another former cellmate's daughter. Worried that Zhang would report to the police, Chen started to escape everywhere and hid in a small inn near Luoyang Railway Station. When Zhang discovered that his son was taken away, he reported it to the police on July 8th in 2003. But after investigation, the criminal police team failed to capture the suspect, so they put Chen Changhai online as a wanted person. Having tasted the sweetness, during his work period, Chen met another former cellmate Hu Zongsheng from Jianxi District of Luoyang City. On October 12th, 2003, his 6-year-old daughter ran home and told him that Uncle Chen took her and her sister to have fast food and gave her a meal and then took her sister away. Mr Hu realized something wrong when he couldn't get through to Chen's phone several times and rushed to the local police station to report it. Based on clues, Luoyang police found that this was Chen's second "eating close grass", so the police in both places established a linkage mechanism to solve the case. Clearing net action capturing the rabbit that likes eating close grass. For 8 years, the police never gave up the chase, and families Zhang and Hu ran all over to find their flesh and blood. After the Ministry of Public Security launched the "Clear Net Action", Songshan Road Police Station formed a special investigation group for this case and went to Luyi County, Lumiao Township, Chen Changhai's hometown, for investigation. The policemen found that Chen Changhai has a brother named Chen Changbo, who is honest and has been farming at home and never went out. However, in 2008, he was sentenced to five months' detention by the court of Erqi District for suspected fraud. Various signs showed that someone was misusing Chen Changbo's identity. After tracking and investigation, the investigators learned that this "Chen Changbo" was actually Chen Changhai, temporarily residing in Shilipu Village of Guancheng District. On September 9th, Captain Chen Wei of the Case Investigation Team of Songshan Road Police Station and others captured Chen Changhai after stakeout and waiting. After interrogation, Chen Changhai confessed to the crime of abducting Zhang Dahai's son and admitted that when he was detained in 2008, he was afraid of being recognized as an escaped child trafficker, so he reported his brother's ID number and used his brother's name. On December 29th, Chen Changhai was approved for arrest by the People's Procuratorate of Erqi District. Thought-provoking Two children had complex emotions when recognizing their relatives. On September 15th, Chen Wei and others followed the clues and went to Sun's home in Sijia Zhuang and rescued Zhang Dahai's son. Now 9 years old, the boy refused to recognize his biological father Zhang Dahai. After persuasion from the police and Sun, the boy eventually returned to his biological parents. What Mr Hu's family didn't expect was how tortuous the process of finding his little daughter was. Chen Changhai sold her for 4000 yuan that year and only knew the buyer's nickname was "Laohei". After the police investigated "Laohei", they were told that the child had been taken away by a Kang surname friend and given to a distant relative surnamed Hou, who was from Baofeng County. Finally, the police officers took Kang to identify all the photos of men surnamed Hou in Baofeng County and rescued the renamed child on November 18th. When Mr Hu was preparing to take his daughter back to Luoyang, he faced strong resistance from the child. Faced with the law and family affection, the two couples cried over the same daughter, and even the police who had successfully solved many child trafficking cases couldn't help but shed tears. Eventually, both sides reached a friendly agreement: the couple Hou would send the child to Luoyang, and then the couple Hu would accompany the child to Baofeng County to recognize their relatives. They would let the child make her own decision when she grew up.
