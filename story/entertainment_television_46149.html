Introduction to the Plot of Still True to the Military Spirit by Episodes

Episode 6

Zhou Lingyun found Academician Gu Shixing of the Engineering Academy through Feng Xiaobing, who had already been transferred to work at the Municipal Commerce Bureau. Initially arriving in Beijing, Zhou Lingyun prepared one million yuan in cash to purchase the secret formula for Xinshuyin. He Mingcong brought Chen Zhijiang to Gu Shixing's house to demand the secret formula and unexpectedly met Zhou Lingyun. The two faced each other without yielding. Unexpectedly, Gu Shixing neither accepted Zhou Lingyun’s one million yuan nor received Chen Zhijiang, and he asked both of them to leave.

After returning to Nanjiang, Zhou Lingyun believed that the reason Gu Shixing did not want to give him the secret formula was mainly because he did not want to hand it over to a joint venture enterprise, fearing that the secret formula would fall into foreign hands. Zhou Lingyun quickly registered a sole proprietorship new factory and planned to go to Gu Shixing again under the name of a non-joint venture enterprise to obtain the secret formula. Compared with Zhou Lingyun, Chen Zhijiang didn't even have a penny or any factory equipment. Chen Zhijiang and He Ningsheng hoped He Mingcong could find out from his mentor Gu Shixing what the bottom price for the secret formula was, but unexpectedly, Gu Shixing quoted an astronomical price, leaving everyone greatly disappointed.

Although Zhou Lingyun returned empty-handed, Fuji believed that obtaining Gu Laoshi's secret formula was not entirely hopeless and gave Zhou Lingyun many promises. Zhang Chengli had been waiting for Zhou Lingyun to return from Beijing and introduced Zhou Lingyun to meet Hu Yue, an advertising planner. Under Hu Yue's planning, Zhou Lingyun launched a strong public opinion offensive, using media publicity to promote himself, hoping to make Gu Laoshi feel that Zhou Lingyun was the most suitable person to develop Xinshuyin. Both Chen Zhijiang and He Ningsheng knew that Gu Shixing's secret formula was a priceless national treasure, and trying to get the secret formula from Gu Laoshi without spending a penny was simply a pipe dream.

When Chen Zhijiang was worried about money, his childhood classmate Ajiao came forward voluntarily and offered to lend him money, but Chen Zhijiang refused. He Ningsheng did not understand and mistakenly thought there was some special relationship between Chen Zhijiang and Ajiao. In fact, Chen Zhijiang clearly understood that no one could afford the astronomical price set by Gu Shixing. Chen Zhijiang decided to go to Beijing again to visit Gu Shixing and make a final effort. Meanwhile, the publicity campaign planned by Zhang Chengli and Hu Yue also influenced Gu Shixing far away in Beijing. After seeing numerous reports about Zhou Lingyun's advanced deeds on newspapers, Gu Shixing developed a favorable impression of Zhou Lingyun.

Episode 7

He Mingcong further introduced Zhou Lingyun's character, and Gu Shixing wanted to see Zhou Lingyun again. Seeing the continuous reports about himself in the newspapers, Zhou Lingyun felt somewhat complacent, but Feng Xiaobing reminded him that this approach was somewhat excessive. Zhang Chengli and Hu Yue came to find Zhou Lingyun and told him that the next round of publicity was still coming. They said that once this publicity started, it would definitely move Gu Shixing, and the secret formula for Xinshuyin would absolutely belong to Zhou Lingyun. Zhou Lingyun was also confident that this time he would definitely succeed.

Chen Zhijiang visited Gu Shixing again and was rejected again. Even the return ticket was paid for by He Mingcong. However, Chen Zhijiang was not willing to return empty-handed and secretly refunded the ticket. Overnight, he prepared and drew up a plan for the new drug factory. Early the next morning, he rushed to the hutong where Gu Shixing lived. While having breakfast, he unexpectedly met Zhou Lingyun, who told him that this time he had brought three million yuan in cash. Zhou Lingyun believed he was sure to win, but Chen Zhijiang, like being splashed with cold water, still held onto the last hope and walked into Gu Shixing's home.

Leaders of the Traditional Chinese Medicine University heard that Gu Lao was going to donate the secret formula and came to congratulate him. Gu Shixing was surprised and asked how they knew he was going to donate the secret formula. The school leaders handed him the newspaper of the day, and Gu Shixing saw the publicity done by Hu Yue. Unexpectedly, the exaggerated publicity in the newspaper backfired on Zhou Lingyun, as Gu Lao was very dissatisfied with the inaccurate reporting. In front of the college leadership, Gu Shixing took out two pieces of paper with the words "gain" and "loss" written on them respectively. He asked Chen Zhijiang and Zhou Lingyun to each pick one, to see whose luck was better. Zhou Lingyun picked the word "loss" and was very disappointed, while Chen Zhijiang was confident. He knew that this was Gu Shixing helping him.

After Zhou Lingyun left disappointedly, Gu Shixing explained why he wanted to give the secret formula for Xinshuyin to Chen Zhijiang. Gu Shixing signed a contract with Chen Zhijiang for the use of the secret formula, stipulating that within six months, the development of the new drug must be initiated. If the trial production of the new drug was not carried out within the deadline, Gu Shixing had the right to reclaim the usage rights of the secret formula.

Episode 8

Zhou Lingyun eventually returned empty-handed, feeling very depressed. He Mingcong was also surprised that Gu Shixing could entrust the secret formula to Chen Zhijiang for research and development. Gu Shixing shared his inner thoughts with He Mingcong. Although Chen Zhijiang obtained the secret formula, truly developing it into a traditional Chinese medicine posed even greater difficulties—no money, no equipment, no factory or laboratory.

Even though Zhou Lingyun did not obtain the usage rights of the Xinshuyin secret formula, he did not give up. At an old comrades gathering, Zhou Lingyun proposed to Chen Zhijiang that he would fund the construction of a factory for Zhi Jiang Pharmaceutical Factory, and the rights for the development of the new drug would belong to Hongxing Factory. This proposal was immediately rejected by Chen Zhijiang because he wanted an independently researched new drug and could not transfer the core technology to others, leading to an unpleasant end to the meeting among old comrades.

He Ningsheng criticized Chen Zhijiang for showing no consideration for old comrades. Chen Zhijiang also felt that his actions were a bit excessive, but he did not know where he went wrong. He Ningsheng believed that since they had no money and disagreed to cooperate with others, dragging things out would benefit no one. Once the deadline arrived and Gu Lao reclaimed the Xinshuyin secret formula, all their efforts would be wasted. Chen Zhijiang realized the seriousness of the issue but could not wait any longer and actively sought ways to find a solution.

Hongxing Factory managed by Zhou Lingyun became increasingly unprofitable, and many employees resigned. Feng Xiaobing returned home dejectedly. Her daughter, Chen Yuanfan, asked her why she was angry with her father. Feng Xiaobing said that Chen Zhijiang did not come to rescue her. Chen Yuanfan felt that her mother always sided with Zhou Lingyun in the conflict between her father and Zhou Lingyun, indicating an emotional issue. Feng Xiaobing evasively said it was an emotional issue, but it was comradely affection. Various sizes of newspapers reported Chen Zhijiang's inability to develop Xinshuyin, making He Ningsheng very anxious. He believed that this must be something Zhou Lingyun did.

He Ningsheng also thought that Chen Zhijiang's actions were a bit excessive and should collaborate with Zhou Lingyun, but Chen Zhijiang had his own ideas. He believed that in commercial competition, one cannot only consider comradeship; market economy has its own rules which cannot be replaced by comradeship. To build a new factory, He Ningsheng went to the town government to request land, but unexpectedly encountered resistance.

Fuji Company decided to withdraw investment due to limited benefits in China after the withdrawal of Japanese funds, plunging Hongxing Factory managed by Zhou Lingyun into dire straits. Chen Zhijiang continued selling dumplings while preparing to build a new factory and test-produce new drugs. Many demobilized soldiers came to offer advice.

Episode 9

Chen Zhijiang needed money urgently to build a new factory. An old comrade suggested applying for a bank loan, and Chen Zhijiang thought it was worth a try. Before leaving, Fuji came to Chen Zhijiang's dumpling stall to bid farewell. Chen Zhijiang did not show him a good face, but unexpectedly, when Chen Yuanfan heard that Fuji was going to America, she immediately showed interest in Fuji. Chen Zhijiang looked everywhere for banks to raise funds for drug development, but was rejected by all the banks. He proposed using the secret formula as collateral, but the bank considered the secret formula worthless. Meanwhile, Gu Shixing in Beijing was also eager to learn about the progress of Chen Zhijiang's new drug development and asked He Mingcong to call and inquire.

After the withdrawal of Japanese funds, Fuji also submitted his resignation to the Japanese headquarters. When Fuji left Nanjiang, Chen Yuanfan appeared at the airport. She hoped that Fuji could help her achieve her wish to study in the United States after arriving in America, and Fuji readily agreed. Zhang Chengli told Zhou Lingyun that Chen Zhijiang was currently seeking loans everywhere but encountering obstacles at every turn. If the newspaper hype was intensified, it was highly possible that Chen Zhijiang, in a desperate situation, would transfer the secret formula.

Chen Zhijiang and He Ningsheng were at a loss and received a call from He Mingcong in Beijing asking about the progress of the research and development. They became even more anxious. Newspapers of various sizes reported that Chen Zhijiang was incapable of developing Xinshuyin, making He Ningsheng very worried. He believed that this must be something Zhou Lingyun did. He then found Zhou Lingyun, who expressed sympathy instead and proposed a joint research idea by the two families. Zhou Lingyun would provide the factory, equipment, and funding for Chen Zhijiang to build the factory, etc. He Ningsheng thought it was a good idea and believed he could persuade Chen Zhijiang to cooperate with Lingyun. Chen Zhijiang repeatedly studied the recent newspaper publicity and did not think it was a bad thing but rather a good thing. Only by riding on Zhou Lingyun's publicity momentum and turning the tables could they enhance the reputation of Xinshuyin, which would be beneficial for the next step in raising loans.

He Ningsheng proactively mentioned that Zhou Lingyun was willing to cooperate with the factory and equipment for joint research, but Chen Zhijiang believed this was Zhou Lingyun's tactic of attacking by defense and retreating to advance. Chen Zhijiang rejected the idea of joint research with Zhou Lingyun and decided to auction off the secret formula for Xinshuyin to increase its value. He Ningsheng strongly opposed it. Meanwhile, Gu Shixing in Beijing became uneasy upon hearing that his Xinshuyin was going to be auctioned and decided to go to Nanjiang but was dissuaded by He Mingcong.

Episode 10

Feng Xiaobing also believed that Chen Zhijiang's decision to auction off the Xinshuyin secret formula was inappropriate and did not believe that Chen Zhijiang would do such a stupid thing. When she called to ask Chen Zhijiang, he confirmed that it was indeed true. Zhou Lingyun believed that he could fully seize this opportunity of public auction to compete openly and legally obtain Xinshuyin. He Ningsheng persuaded Chen Zhijiang that instead of auctioning it off, it would be better to cooperate with Zhou Lingyun. If the auction price was lower than the amount Zhou Lingyun offered, it would be even less worthwhile.

Chen Zhijiang's mother heard that her son was going to auction off the secret formula for money and planned to sell the old house at home. Upon hearing that Chen Zhijiang was really going to auction off the Xinshuyin secret formula, He Mingcong hurriedly came from Beijing to Nanjiang to stop Chen Zhijiang from auctioning, but unexpectedly, Chen Zhijiang convinced He Mingcong. At this time, Zhang Chengli's Hongtu Pharmaceutical Factory went bankrupt. After selling the factory equipment, the bureau agreed that he could use four million yuan for a new project. Zhang Chengli encouraged Zhou Lingyun to bid for Xinshuyin and funded the participation in the auction competition.

The auction of the Xinshuyin secret formula officially began. Zhou Lingyun personally participated in the bidding and finally bid up to eight million yuan. Zhou Lingyun believed that the Xinshuyin secret formula was in hand, but unexpectedly, the highest bid of eight million yuan did not reach the reserve price of this auction, resulting in a failed auction. Originally, Chen Zhijiang set the reserve price at ten million yuan to ensure the protection of the secret formula. Due to the lack of a successful transaction, Chen Zhijiang decided to hold a second auction. To ensure the success of the auction, the auction house required the second auction to publicly disclose the reserve price. The first bid was unsuccessful, and Zhou Lingyun felt somewhat disappointed. Suddenly, he received a call from Fuji, who had returned to Nanjiang.

Fuji specifically came for the second auction. He told Zhou Lingyun that the Japanese side decided to invest twenty million yuan as support for Zhou Lingyun to participate in the second auction of the Xinshuyin secret formula. If the Xinshuyin secret formula could be obtained, the Japanese side would invest again to cooperate with him for the second time. With Fuji's support, Zhou Lingyun was full of confidence and decided to participate in the second competition. Feng Xiaobing, however, reminded Zhou Lingyun not to cooperate with Fuji, as businessmen like Fuji were profit-driven and unscrupulous, but Zhou Lingyun still hoped to compete with Chen Zhijiang and did not want to miss such an opportunity.
