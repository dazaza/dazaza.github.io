**Dior Homme Cologne Introduction**

For this men's fragrance, Dior specially chose iris as the main note to create an entirely new sense of masculinity. It conveys a classic male image through its strong iris scent. Iris belongs to the lily family and is a symbol of royalty in France.

Hedi Slimane created a perfume that directly reflects the classic image of the new generation of men by incorporating modern elements. The Dior Homme men's fragrance fully inherits the style of Dior Homme menswear, with the bottle's clean lines echoing the tailoring of the clothing. Most importantly, it blends traditional and modern scents to perfectly present a modern classic male image!

The initial notes are filled with iris, cocoa beans, and amber tones, creating a perfect blend of appropriate fragrances.

- **Top Notes:** Herbal notes (French lavender essential oil, French sage essential oil)

- **Middle Notes:** Spicy notes (Italian iris absolute, cocoa beans, amber)

- **Base Notes:** Woody notes (Tahitian vetiver essential oil, Indonesian patchouli essential oil, bark, cardamom)

**Dior Homme -** With iris as the central theme, symbolizing royal masculinity from France and Japan, Dior Homme also innovatively uses technological craftsmanship to incorporate steel tubes printed with the DIO R logo into the perfume bottle, making it an outstanding annual men's fragrance in terms of fashion spirit, perfume, and bottle design.

1. **Dior Homme Cologne**

This unique cologne contains the rare iris fragrance in men's perfumes, playing an important role in the original eau de toilette. In the cologne, it adds delicate powder and distinctive appeal.

Dior Homme sets standards for new perfume aesthetics, and this cologne represents a revival of tradition. It redefines classic rules while staying true to them: citrus themes gain a modern feel due to the dominant floral notes and high-quality raw materials (bergamot, citron, orange blossom).
