Resa Cars 'Made in China' Breaks the Value Barrier

by ftleisa on 2011-12-13 11:33:53

When the technical barriers of an industry hinder the liberation of social productivity, there will surely be a new force to break through it! In the field of pumping machinery, this force is Foton Lei Sa. A few days ago, Foton Motor, the world's largest commercial vehicle manufacturer, once again caught the attention of many with its new pumping machinery brand Lei Sa and the new product 52m pump truck, quickly stirring up a wave of excitement in the industry. The launch of Lei Sa's 52m pump truck has significant implications for the development of China's cement machinery industry. Not only does it create several firsts in the industry, but it also represents the first pump truck whose performance and quality align with international standards and which has been entirely self-developed. It successfully realizes the leapfrogging of domestically produced pump trucks and will lead the upgrading of the pumping machinery industry, becoming a milestone event in "Made in China" pump trucks.

Regarding the launch of Lei Sa's 52m pump truck, Zhang Xiaoyu, Vice President of the Chinese Mechanical Engineering Union and Chairman of the China Automotive Engineering Society, gave high praise: Lei Sa's 52m pump truck is the upgrade and innovation of "Made in China" cement equipment, which will further promote the development of national manufacturing.

"Made in China" breaks through value barriers

Covering multiple fields such as IT, home appliances, and automobiles, the development path of domestic brands has proven one fact: when the domestic industry does not master core technologies, high technical licensing fees, patent fees, import tariffs, and other additional costs form high value barriers, keeping the majority of the population outside the industry for a long time and making it difficult for them to enjoy the convenience, improved quality of life, and labor efficiency brought by technological progress.

This is also the case in the pumping machinery industry. For a long time, more than 90% of the products in China's pump truck market have used imported chassis. This current situation directly results in high prices and maintenance costs for pump trucks, prolonging users' cost recovery period and seriously hindering the widespread promotion and application within the pump truck industry.

The launch of Lei Sa's 52m pump truck will lead "Made in China" pump trucks to break through this current situation. Foton Lei Sa draws on the accumulated technology of Lei Wo pumping machinery and simultaneously leverages the integrated R&D advantages of Foton Motor headquarters. Through close cooperation between Beijing Development Headquarters and two major development centers in Germany and Japan, it overcomes multiple challenges in integrated pumping machinery technology, achieving a perfect match between domestic chassis and actor makeup.

To ensure the reliability and performance leadership of the 52-meter product, Foton Lei Sa selects key components from the global gold supply chain, including Cummins, Eaton, ZF, Bosch Rexroth, and HBC, ensuring the reliability of the engine, transmission, transfer case, and steering gear required for high-reliability pumping. Additionally, Lei Sa's 52-meter pump truck achieves maximum user benefits by reducing fault rates. After extensive research on the front lines, Foton Lei Sa found that the hydraulic system and electrical system are the parts most prone to failure in pump trucks. Lei Sa's 52-meter pump truck addresses customer concerns by using high-end configurations in critical areas, employing products from internationally leading brands such as Bosch Rexroth, Eaton, and others to ensure the stability and reliability of the hydraulic system; using Bosch Rexroth and Easyroad panels, German HBC remote sensing systems to ensure the reliability of the electrical system; and using Swedish-imported high-strength steel and Italian Veri or Rothead bearings for the arm system to ensure both the strength and safe stability of the arm operation.

Through multiple rounds of thorough testing and validation at different stages of development and production, as well as market customer validation processes, the technical compatibility and application reliability of the 52-meter product have been significantly improved, matching the quality of imported pump trucks. Meanwhile, Lei Sa pump trucks have the advantage of lower costs and rely on the integrated service network support of Foton Motor Group, resulting in low after-sales repair and maintenance costs, faster product profitability, and shorter payback cycles.

Foton Lei Sa uses technology to drive productivity improvements, breaking through the value barriers of imported pump trucks, bringing products with excellent performance, quality, and price to Chinese users, helping a wide range of users own their own pump trucks, and realizing the ideal value of cement machinery users.
