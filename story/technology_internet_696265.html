What to do if a woman's hair is dry, curly, and frizzy?

Hair care with olive oil:

Before you wash your hair, you can drip some olive oil on the comb, then comb your hair to evenly apply olive oil to your hair. Be careful not to get it on the scalp, otherwise the scalp will be greasy. Then wrap your hair with a hot towel for a while before washing your hair. Alternatively, you can pour some olive oil into the water you use to wash your hair. Remember not to pour too much, otherwise it will be greasy and if it's not rinsed clean, others may think you haven't washed your hair for days. You can also mix olive oil with your conditioner, hair mask or serum [http://www.toufa5.com]. After using shampoo to wash your hair for the first time, dry your hair slightly with a towel, then apply the mixture above onto your hair, then wrap your hair with a hot towel. You can wait for ten minutes or so, reheat the hot towel, then continue wrapping, and finally rinse it off. The benefit of hair care with olive oil is that your hair becomes smooth [http://www.toufa5.com], and it's not as rough. The effect is really good, just as good as beer. I love using olive oil for hair care very much, the effect is really great, and it is especially suitable for dry, fluffy and rough hair. There is another small method to make your hair straight: after washing your hair, don't dry it, comb your hair smoothly, let it dry naturally [http://www.toufa5.com], this will make your hair straighter. Also, you can carry a small comb with you, which is convenient for combing your hair more often and making it smoother and straighter.
