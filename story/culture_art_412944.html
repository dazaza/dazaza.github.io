Satisfactory Answer (Approval Rate: 66%): Hmm, you want to obtain one, right? Many people can arrange private school diplomas for you, but I must remind you of a few points that I hope you will consider: First, if the diploma is only verifiable on the school's website, it means the state does not recognize it. Second, it is not academic education but non-academic education. A junior college diploma certificate is called a factual certificate. If you urgently need a certificate, you can obtain a non-authentic one temporarily to meet immediate needs. After all, with your work experience, it should be easier to explain.

I personally suggest you consider taking self-study exams or correspondence courses, each having its own advantages and disadvantages.
