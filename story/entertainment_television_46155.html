Introduction to the plot of It Turns Out That I'm Not Handsome:

Ever since he could remember, "Xiaozhuang" always thought of himself as a handsome guy, and this belief wasn't entirely unfounded. From childhood to adulthood, whether it was relatives, elders, or the auntie at the snack stall, girls filling out questionnaires, ticket scalpers, or classmates borrowing his reports, everyone always sincerely called him "handsome." Therefore, Xiaozhuang truly believed that he was a handsome man... The person who shattered Xiaozhuang's dream of being handsome was none other than his two classmates and best friends: "Sisi," a quirky roommate full of strange ideas and humor, who is the vice president of the Pigeon Racing Club and claims that "Old Wang," the pigeon, is his only confidant; and "Akon," a self-proclaimed playboy with a smooth demeanor, who is also the founder and president of the Pigeon Research Club. Despite having some experience in love, Akon's advice on romance always seems to lead Xiaozhuang astray.

Xiaozhuang's connection with "Cindy" dates back even before they entered university. He once had an (unperfect) encounter with Cindy. To win her over, Xiaozhuang joined the Gourmet Club and mustered all his courage to confess his feelings to Cindy. However, Cindy simply and coldly replied with a "No." Devastated, Xiaozhuang asked why, and Cindy bluntly told him that he was "too ugly." This revelation left Xiaozhuang feeling like he had been shot twice.

The tangled relationship between Cindy and Xiaozhuang becomes more complicated with the addition of the beautiful "Qiaoping," forming a triangle. Meanwhile, Sisi, who is already dating the eccentric "Pinxuan," has no time to help Xiaozhuang anymore. Akon, on the other hand, faces his toughest romantic dilemma yet and struggles to assist Xiaozhuang while dealing with his clingy girlfriend "Manman." All three find themselves entangled in their respective romantic predicaments, weaving together a super romantic campus comedy filled with laughter through tears and exhaustion.

Age: 20

Major: Information Management, Sophomore

Xiaozhuang's quirky roommate #1, brimming with bizarre ideas and a talent for telling corny jokes. His specialty is draining people's energy within a minute. As the vice president of the Pigeon Club, Sisi dedicates himself to studying pigeon psychology but remains unrecognized by teachers and classmates alike. His only confidant is the pigeon "Old Wang," with whom he shares a deep bond beyond words.

Akon (played by Xunjie Zhang)

Age: 19

Major: Information Management, Sophomore

Clubs: Pigeon Research Club

Xiaozhuang's quirky roommate #2 and the founder/president of the Pigeon Research Club. His famous saying is: "A good teacher makes you issue cards until you're annoyed, but a bad teacher makes you receive cards until you're soft!" Despite his confidence, following his advice never seems to work for Xiaozhuang. Moreover, Akon conceals his exceptional basketball skills.
