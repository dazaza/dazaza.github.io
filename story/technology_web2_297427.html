Zhu Jun responds to Fudan professor's criticism of the letter to Shenhua Football Club: he will shape a champion with social influence

by kersfe3f23 on 2009-12-07 08:39:37

Zhu Jun responds to Fudan professor's criticism of Shenhua Football Club: aims to build a champion with social influence

According to Sina Sports, on December 5th, Zhu Jun, the investor of Shanghai Shenhua Football Club, while negotiating with Bora Milutinović for the position of head coach for the next season, personally replied to a letter from Professor Lin Rongri, a long-term Shenhua supporter and researcher at Fudan University's Institute of Higher Education. Regarding the sharp issues pointed out by Professor Lin, such as the stumbling blocks in the development of Shenhua Football Club and the lack of championship qualities, Zhu Jun accepted the opinions while also sharing his thoughts on the current state of the club.

Professor Lin's Letter Points Out Shenhua's Stumbling Blocks

Three days ago, Professor Lin Rongri, who has been a long-time supporter of Shenhua and is a researcher at Fudan University's Institute of Higher Education, published an open letter to Zhu Jun through Shanghai media. In this letter, Professor Lin pointed out what he saw as the existing stumbling blocks for Shenhua and the lack of championship qualities.

In the letter, Professor Lin wrote: "As a fellow overseas returnee, I both agree with and admire many of your actions, and I am very impressed by your boldness. However, I have always believed that people associated with Shenhua (including leaders and players) lack sufficient understanding and appreciation of traditional Chinese culture or Chinese cultural refinement, which is one of the main reasons why Shenhua struggles to truly succeed. Unlike in the West, it is extremely unwise for Chinese to intentionally provoke their opponents before a battle. However, Shenhua often does this, claiming things like 'Shenhua is the champion,' 'We must win by three goals,' 'No negotiation on winning the match,' or raising the championship slogan too early. The most inappropriate example is the slogan 'Shenhua is the champion.' Have Manchester United or Real Madrid ever said that? Last year, Cheng Yaodong of Xi'an Team (Shaanxi Zhongxin) made the same mistake when they were leading for the majority of the season. The result was predictable."

"Only fools trip over stones of their own making!" This Western proverb applies to Shenhua, who seem to create these stumbling blocks every year. Undoubtedly, Shenhua has the strength to be champions but lacks the qualities of a champion! Fans are adorable, but when they shout 'Shenhua is the champion,' does Shenhua really see itself as a champion? Since the league began, how many championships has Shenhua won? Ten? Eight? Overall, Shenhua might have invested the most money in the league, but how many championships have they actually won? Where is the benefit? Where is the problem? I won't say more. Please have your coaches and players learn Laozi's philosophy, such as 'Because he contends not, therefore no man in the world can contend with him' and 'The highest good is like water.' 'Be humble in your conduct, and diligent in your work.' If so, Shenhua might truly succeed. Love runs deep, pain cuts deep! If my words are too harsh, please forgive me. Wishing you greater success!"

Zhu Jun's Response Accepts Professor Lin's Views on Chinese Culture

On the afternoon of December 5th, Zhu Jun publicly responded to Professor Lin's views on his Weibo account. Zhu Jun frankly stated: "After reading the open letter from Mr. Lin Rongri, a law doctor from Fudan University, also known as Professor Lin Rongri from the Institute of Higher Education at Fudan University, I indeed had many reflections. I will respond to express my sincerity and hope for constructive exchange."

In his reply to Professor Lin, Zhu Jun wrote: "I feel very close upon receiving your letter, and I thank you for your concern for Shenhua Football Club and myself! I believe you and I are both deeply attracted by the beautiful sport of football. We love football, and we may also hate it a little because it brings us happiness and joy, but also some melancholy and troubles. But it doesn't matter, I always think that happiness outweighs melancholy, and joy outweighs trouble, don't you agree? After all, the essence of football is still healthy and positive."

"In the context of positivity and health, your letter gave me some valuable inspiration. I thank you for proposing the idea of incorporating more 'Chinese Culture' into managing Shenhua. In our further improvement of self-positioning, we can take your suggestions into consideration. You mentioned that 'Because he contends not, therefore no man in the world can contend with him' should be the demeanor that Shenhua needs to possess. Yes, I agree, but I apologize if the current Shenhua cannot yet reach this realm. 'Only those who do not contend with others will not be contended against by anyone in the world' — Personally, I believe that for a football club that still needs further improvement, further striving, and further advancement, such demeanor and breadth of vision require long-term cultivation to achieve. Of course, we will strive to cultivate ourselves well on the path towards maturity."

Regarding the claim of being champions, Zhu Jun insisted: "In my view, in this era, we must have enough courage to stand on the stage of football, which is essentially about fighting. Shenhua fans have already recognized Shenhua as champions. As Shenhua people, we must unconditionally take on the responsibility of striving for the championship. The fans are not wrong because that is their eternal hope and expectation, thus it is also Shenhua's eternal motivation and source of strength. Without the demands and encouragement of the fans, there would be no momentum or energy for the team. The cheers of the fans are an important part of the soul of Shenhua Football Club and its team. Any of their cheers are not wrong; it is their right, and Shenhua also has the obligation to realize those simple and straightforward cheers!"

In this response, Zhu Jun also expressed his hope to elevate his thoughts on championships: "Is the 'championship' that Shenhua strives for merely that golden trophy? No, the meaning of the 'championship' that Shenhua aspires to is much more complex and rich — we aspire to be the 'champion' of 'club standardization', the 'champion' of 'market industrialization', the 'champion' of 'social influence', and the 'champion' of 'cultural inheritance'... The word 'champion' is not simple or pure for Shenhua; it is an 'extraordinary' word. Therefore, we believe our path is arduous, and we have a long way to go!"

At the end of his response, Zhu Jun also expressed gratitude to Professor Lin and all those who care about and offer advice to Shenhua: "The charm of football lies in 'competition'. Shenhua is a collective that bravely fights on the football platform. To meet the expectations of our fans and fulfill the mission history has given us, we must bravely fight — Thank you for your kind warnings to me and Shenhua Football Club. We thank and appreciate every individual in society for their sincere contributions to Shenhua, regardless of wealth or status. We sincerely welcome more suggestions, cautions, blessings, and prayers from everyone." (Qu Xiaoyou)
