Our country is a water resource-deficient nation, especially severe in the northern regions, with magnesite material production processing application technology. People try to save water by collecting bathing water and vegetable washing water for reuse, which is both troublesome and labor-intensive. This technology can achieve the storage and secondary use of relatively clean reclaimed water such as bathing water and laundry water, which can be used for flushing toilets and mopping floors. It has a reasonable design, takes up less space, stores more water, is convenient to use, greatly improves the utilization rate of reclaimed water, benefits environmental protection, and saves water costs. The content involves family wastewater reuse, water recycling, wastewater utilization, water-saving devices, water recovery treatment, water recovery and reuse.
