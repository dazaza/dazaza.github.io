Refined Android Phone Motorola MB511

Motorola continues to embrace Android and has recently launched the Flipout MB511, which runs on Android 2.1 and features a swivel QWERTY keyboard. The specifications are not top-tier, with a 600 MHz processor, a 2.8-inch 320x240 screen, 512 MB RAM/ROM, a 3-megapixel camera, support for WIFI, Bluetooth 2.1+EDR, A-GPS, FM Radio, and MicroSD memory cards. The design of the Motorola Flipout MB511 clearly prioritizes compactness; its 2.8-inch square screen makes it about the same size as the Sony Ericsson Xperia X10. However, when the keyboard is flipped open, it is slightly larger than the Sony Ericsson Xperia X10. Although the QWERTY keyboard looks narrow, in fact, due to sufficient length, typing is more convenient than expected. Since the Flipout MB511 uses a 320x240 screen, some applications cannot be opened, which is worth noting. The Motorola Flipout MB511 comes in four colors, including black, white, green, and red, with a price of HKD$2,580.
