Introduction to the plot of "Hearts of Parents":

Cai Maosong, a county magistrate from a family of magistrates, is an upright and selfless person who loves his people and governs diligently. As a parent official, Maosong also has to take care of his family, but with the support of his virtuous wife Mingzhu, he manages to balance both roles. Whenever Maosong returns home and has free time, he will talk with his three children and accompany them in their growth with love. However, as children grow up, they gradually develop their own opinions, and Maosong must figure out how to be a competent father in his children's careers and love lives...

Huang Zhaodi, a tragic woman, had no choice but to enter the world of prostitution due to her family's difficulties when she was young. She thought that meeting Maosong was true love in her life, but unfortunately, Maosong broke up with her for his political career. After Maosong left, Zhaodi discovered she was pregnant. To avoid being labeled as a family destroyer, Zhaodi decided to raise her daughter Lele alone. The obedient and understanding Lele harbors no resentment towards her mother for being an illegitimate child, instead becoming her mother's spiritual pillar. But Zhaodi feels immense guilt towards Lele for not being able to give her a complete family...
