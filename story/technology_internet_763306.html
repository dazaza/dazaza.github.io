Guangdong Telecom to Launch Weibo + WeChat定向 Data Package in August: 6 Yuan for 2GB of Data

by anonymous on 2013-07-29 18:23:58

On July 29, according to an insider from Guangdong Telecom, Guangdong Telecom will launch a WeChat + Sina Weibo exclusive data package in August. For a monthly fee of 6 yuan, users can get 2GB of directed traffic for Tencent's WeChat and Sina Weibo.

Previously, Guangdong Unicom had announced that it would collaborate with Tencent to introduce a WeChat exclusive custom package and "WeChat Wo Card." Both parties are set to officially disclose the details of their cooperation on July 30.

In fact, Guangdong Telecom had previously launched a 5-yuan 1GB WeChat exclusive data card and a 5-yuan 1GB Weibo exclusive data card. Guangdong Unicom also introduced a Wo Pai package in 2011, which included WeChat directed traffic for a monthly fee of 36 yuan. However, due to various sensitive issues, neither Guangdong Unicom nor Guangdong Telecom promoted or advertised these packages on a large scale at that time.

However, after the "WeChat charging" controversy in early 2013, China Unicom and China Telecom began discussions with Tencent about the possibilities and plans for collaboration. China Unicom Chairman Chang Xiaobing even described the relationship between operators and OTT services as a "fish-water relationship."

Earlier this year, the unlimited WeChat data package launched by Hong Kong telecom operator PCCW in collaboration with Tencent, priced at 8 Hong Kong dollars per month, was regarded by the industry as a classic case of cooperation between telecom operators and OTT service providers.

It is understood that telecom operators can launch exclusive data cards due to the introduction of CCG (Content Charging Gateway). This means that the method of calculating data traffic by telecom operators has shifted from the past extensive type to a targeted precise type, separating the traffic of different products.

For operators, CCG can provide more refined value-added services, more accurate billing points, and integration, thereby enhancing their operational capabilities and levels. By leveraging the services of internet companies, they can convert data into value-added services. For users, they can receive more transparent and satisfactory services and choose different exclusive data packages based on their actual usage, spending less while gaining more data. This also increases customer loyalty for operators.

An insider from a telecom operator stated that the CCG Content Charging Gateway is an important network node for telecom operators to achieve more refined operations in the construction of 3G and even future 4G mobile internet.

Due to the shift in how operators calculate data traffic, more targeted exclusive data cards have emerged. According to the aforementioned Guangdong Telecom insider, Guangdong Telecom has already launched a 9-yuan 3G App Store download data card. It was revealed that Guangdong Telecom will also launch next month a 9-yuan 3G QQ Music exclusive data card, a 3-yuan 500M Ai Music data card, a YiTV video data card, and other exclusive data cards targeting different applications.

It is introduced that the aforementioned several types of targeted exclusive data cards, including the "WeChat + Weibo package," will be launched in the form of a Fly Young New Privilege Card + targeted exclusive data package. Both new and old users can apply. The Fly Young New Privilege Card costs 19 yuan/month and includes 80 minutes of provincial call duration (including local or domestic long-distance calls, regardless of direct dialing or 17909 IP), 80 domestic text messages, 20M mobile internet, and 8 hours of WIFI internet access duration.
