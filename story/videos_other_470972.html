Korean drama "The Princess' Man" Episode 2

A lie encounter. To facilitate his future ascension to the throne, Daegun Seoyang arranged for his daughter Seo-ling to be married to Seung-u, the son of right prime minister Kim Jong-rye. To confirm Seung-u who had just been appointed as the teacher of Crown Princess Gyeong-hye, Seo-ling took the place of Crown Princess Gyeong-hye in listening to lectures...

Daegun Seoyang discovered that the princess mentioned in Seong-yu's letter was Seo-ling. Seo-ling begged her father to forgive Seong-yu and threatened him that if he did not release Seong-yu, she would not be able to live either. Helpless, Daegun Seoyang released Seong-yu. As soon as Seong-yu came out of prison, she asked Shin-myun to find Seo-ling. Shin-myun did not intend to tell Seong-yu who Seo-ling really was because he also fell in love with Seo-ling. The princess married Jeong-jong but found out that he was the man who intruded into the princess's palanquin that day. Jeong-jong also realized that the princess was actually the beautiful woman in the palanquin that day. Finally, Seo-ling met Seong-yu unintentionally on the street...

Korean drama "The Princess' Man" Episode 5

To preserve power, King Munjong issued an edict selecting Seung-u as the candidate for the prince consort... To save Seo-ling who almost fell off a cliff while riding a horse, Seung-u forcibly pulled her to a brothel... Crown Princess Gyeong-hye forcibly recognized Seung-u as her husband to protect her younger brother...

Korean drama "The Princess' Man" Episode 3

Kim Seong-yu saved Seo-ling from a horse and they galloped together on one horse. To prevent Kim Seong-yu from becoming the prince consort, Seo-ling's father, Daegun Seoyang, arranged for someone to shoot arrows at Seong-yu. Fortunately, Seong-yu and Seo-ling were saved by Shim-myun, who managed public security. And Shim-myun developed feelings for Seo-ling during their interactions. The princess understood the political nature of the alliance with the Kim family and told Seo-ling that Seong-yu would become the prince consort. Seo-ling and Seong-yu had made an appointment to meet on the full moon day, so they strolled around intimately. Seong-yu still thought she was the princess, feeling delighted; Seo-ling, however, was disheartened because she already knew the truth. Seong-yu bought gifts and wrote a letter recalling their wonderful experiences. The princess got angry and threw the letter on the ground, but it was picked up by someone with ulterior motives. To win over Daegun Seoyang's trust in the king, Daegun Seoyang promised to arrange for Seo-ling to marry Shim-myun. Shim-myun learned Seo-ling's true identity but agreed to keep it a secret for her. Kim Seong-yu finally met the real princess.

After knowing the truth, Seong-yu could hardly accept it, and all this was seen by Seo-ling hiding in the inner room. During his routine patrol, Shim-myun encountered Seo-ling secretly shedding tears. Seong-yu got drunk in the brothel, and the courtesans talked about Seong-yu and the princess, which was overheard by someone following them. Although the designated prince consort was Seong-yu, according to the rules, Seong-yu and another candidate, Jeong-jong, entered the palace together. In the side hall, the astrologer who had been threatened indeed said that Seong-yu and the princess had incompatible zodiac signs. Seong-yu was arrested on the charge of taking the princess out of the palace for debauchery, with the courtesan as the witness and the letter as evidence. Coincidentally, Seo-ling saw this after being dismissed from school and begged the princess to save him. Seong-yu feared that telling the truth would implicate Seo-ling, but fortunately, the princess arrived in time to save his life, though he lost his qualifications as the prince consort. Seo-ling went to the prison to see Seong-yu, just as she was about to reveal her true identity, her father Daegun Seoyang came...
