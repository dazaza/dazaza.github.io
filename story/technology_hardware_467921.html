The abandoned son's right to be raised will not be followed up.

by doudouqaj on 2011-07-29 19:09:14

Nicholas Tse and Zhang Baiji are back together. Of course, this is a prediction. The outside world must be curious about what happened in the process that quietly "revived" the precarious marital relationship between Nicholas Tse and Baiji Zhang. Previously, Wei Ai exclusively analyzed five reasons why Tse and Baiji wouldn't divorce (click to read). Now, it seems that Nicholas Tse's agent's response indicates that Tse and Baiji indeed won't divorce. Comprehensive analysis shows that besides all the reasons outsiders can find for hoping they won't divorce, the most important reason that quickly halted the marriage crisis was: Nicholas Tse has love, and responsibility.

Before Nicholas Tse decided to marry Baiji Zhang, he certainly knew a lot about her private life. But being in the entertainment industry, who doesn't have some issues? At the time, Nicholas Tse deeply loved Baiji Zhang, and no gossip could shake his determination. Even after the sex photo scandal shocked the entertainment industry, Nicholas Tse still forgave Baiji Zhang because it happened before their marriage. Some people say that this incident is actually more serious than the sex photo scandal because it happened during the stable period of Zhang and Tse's marriage, and it was the most foolish thing done by the mother of their two sons. What softened Nicholas Tse's heart was that after the incident, Baiji Zhang first thought she hadn't done anything wrong, but only fell seriously ill after Nicholas Tse seemed to have given up completely, pleading with him to forgive her one more time.

It is believed that Nicholas Tse also went through painful struggles and reflections internally, realizing that he still loved Baiji Zhang, just as Baiji Zhang still loved Nicholas Tse. When the aerial photos were released and the public overwhelmingly criticized Baiji Zhang, Nicholas Tse must have thought that she was indeed a responsible and virtuous wife and mother, having given birth to two sons for the Xie family and even rushing to have a third child. If Baiji Zhang didn't fully love Nicholas Tse, how could she have worked so hard? It is believed that this was the biggest consideration for Nicholas Tse when making his decision: a wife who has borne your children, no matter how big her mistake, is still the mother of your children and a significant contributor to your family!

Since there will be no divorce, the negative impacts must be quickly mitigated. Even mutual goodwill between the parties involved, as mentioned by Nicholas Tse's friend who said they had just talked on the phone, reinforces this "rumor-busting" message to the public that nothing really happened.

In Chinese culture, people tend to encourage reconciliation rather than separation. So, since Nicholas Tse as a husband has forgiven Baiji Zhang for her mistakes, shouldn't we as outsiders offer them more blessings? After such a storm, the way Nicholas Tse and Baiji Zhang handled their marital crisis might be worth emulating for many. Most importantly, in the face of unpredictable and ever-changing marriages, responsibility and righteousness come above everything else.
