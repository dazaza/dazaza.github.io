One-day tour of the essence of Lijiang River scenery

by zengxr840824 on 2008-05-30 09:09:40

Itinerary: Pick up guests from Guilin hotel in the morning and take a bus to Yangshuo to visit the five major land attractions: Big Banyan Tree, Moon Hill, Odd Stone Palace, Julong Cave, Jian Mountain Temple. After lunch, go to Xingping Ancient Town to take a boat trip and enjoy the essence of Lijiang River scenery. Experience the breathtaking beauty of "Guilin's landscape is the best under heaven, and Yangshuo's landscape is in Xingping". Then return to Guilin hotel by special car and end the pleasant journey.

Quotation:

2. Lunch (boxed meal)

3. Excellent tour guide service fee

4. Air-conditioned tourist bus
