All bloggers want their posts to be appreciated, commented on, and replied to. They pursue higher click-through rates or aim to become popular blogs. One of the values and pleasures of blogging lies in its powerful communication and interaction functions, which is one of the important reasons that attract us and represents the vitality and vigor of blogs. If the blog cancels the comment function, there probably wouldn't be many people willing to start a blog.

1: Likes leaving comments everywhere

Anywhere you can type, such as blogs, forums, guestbooks, QQ/MSN, microblogs, crowdsourcing platforms... As long as someone is talking, you jump in and follow up; if no one's talking, you start yourself. It doesn't matter if you don't understand what others are saying, just keep talking. Say whatever you want, however much you want, for as long as you want.

Characteristic: Wherever I can type, there will be my name.

Representative figure: Lu Song Song

2: Door-to-door solicitation

Leave comments on strangers' blogs without reading their articles. Go straight in and try to lure them to your own blog by any means necessary. Then copy and paste this comment and invitation to more unfamiliar blogs. Many people use this method.

Characteristic: Rush into others' homes to bring guests back to your own, regardless of whose territory it is, just focus on luring people.

Representative figure: Tang Blog Magazine Station Classic sentence: Your blog is great. First visit after National Day, hope for a return visit.

3: Snatching the sofa and squatting on the stool

You should know, those who sit on the sofa get quite high exposure. Especially on high-traffic blogs. As soon as someone posts, there will be someone rushing to post "sofa" first. That's not all, the second person often posts: stool. As for the content of the post, it can be completely ignored.

Characteristic: The content of the article is not important, the key is the speed of reply.

Representative figure: Zhi Yan

Classic sentence: Sit with you on the sofa.

4: Piercing comments

In order to attract attention, make a piercing comment. Honestly, Lu Song Song thinks this way of leaving comments is pretty good. And I often apply it to my own website. This kind of comment is likely to make a bunch of people remember you.

Characteristic: Words that don't shock are not worth dying for.

Representative figure: Weizhi Blog, Bao Jie

Classic sentences:

Xiangzi says: Your blog posts are all wonderful, collected!

Weizhi Blog: Where did you collect it?

Bao Jie: Collected in the wardrobe?

5: Swearing

This is especially prominent in Chinese Webmaster Station and A5. At the slightest inconvenience, they insult all your female relatives and all collateral relatives from eighteen generations of ancestors downwards. If things don't go well, prepare for the other party to have car accidents, be struck by lightning, dismembered into eight pieces, and left without a burial place...

Characteristic: Don't cross me, otherwise bear the consequences yourself.

Blogging has become an important part of one's learning and life. Others' evaluations, whether positive or negative, have certain value and can give some inspiration, also serving as motivation to write better blogs.
