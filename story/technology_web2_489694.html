◆ I love you, but I don't dare to say it; I'm afraid that if I do, I'll die immediately. I'm not afraid of dying, I'm afraid that after I die, no one will love you like me. I can't help but love you, because I'm afraid no one will love you like me; I don't dare to love you, because I'm afraid you won't love me the way I love you.

◆ Don't love the wrong person because of loneliness, and don't be lonely for life because of loving the wrong person. Try to trust in order to gain happiness. Fate is a book, flipping through it carelessly will cause you to miss it, reading it too seriously will make you cry. Fate is a mysterious arrangement, unpredictable, and cannot be deliberately sought. A beautifully covered book easily attracts people's attention, but there are few worth savoring and collecting; those who write books with sweat often have no time or money to decorate; many books are monotonous from form to content, but plain narration is indeed one kind of life.

◆ The distance between roads does not represent the distance between hearts, fewer phone calls does not mean less concern, infrequent meetings does not mean no longing. Even in busy years, cherish this care. Cowherd and Weaver Girl is a beautiful myth. Newton is the recognized scientist, he would tell you about gravity and distance.

◆ In the world of love, no one owes anyone else, only those who do not know how to cherish each other. Not only should we cherish each other, but also our own feelings. For those who do not cherish you, there is no need for them to say sorry, you should take the initiative to say sorry (subtext: goodbye!).

◆ This lifetime's greatest love will leave no trace in the next life. Loved ones, hold her hand tightly, in the next life, she won't be by your side anymore. In the next life, will you still remember who you loved?

◆ If there is such a person, perfect in your heart without any flaws, you hope and fear to get close to him, this is not love, but admiration. For those you admire, you can only be their slave, impossible to be their lover.

◆ Grievances that can be expressed don't count as grievances, lovers that can be taken away don't count as lovers. Unspoken grievances can only oppress oneself, not letting go of someone who doesn't love you means you won't get someone who does.

◆ After separation, you cannot become friends again because you have hurt each other. After separation, you cannot become enemies either because you deeply loved each other. So we become the most familiar strangers.

◆ Many things are beautiful because they are distant, and can be longed for and pursued. Many things are distant because they are beautiful, and can only evoke a sigh of helplessness.

◆ It's good to cry, it's a sign of beginning to heal. Do not cry for too long, a healed wound getting wet again may lead to re-infection.

◆ Some things, when we are young we cannot understand, when we understand we are no longer young. Some things in the world can be compensated, some things can never be compensated. Grow up while maturing, mature while aging. Adapt to nature, smile at life. Do not grow up too early, nor age prematurely.

◆ Some people you look at for a lifetime but ignore for a lifetime, some people you only glance at once yet influence your entire life, some people enthusiastically bring you joy but are cold by you, some people give you fleeting joy but chain your thoughts, some people have unrequited love for N years but are rejected for N years, some people's unintentional expression becomes eternal yearning. This is life.

◆ No matter what, a person excusing themselves for corruption is never worth understanding. The more no one loves you, the more you should love yourself.

◆ Why must forgetting someone last a lifetime? Because you haven't really tried to forget, but keep mourning, waiting, and dreaming! What remains unforgettable, cannot be forgotten, let it occupy a place in your heart, not all of it.

◆ Sorrow is real, tears are false, nothing needs to be clung to; a hundred years ago, you didn't know me, I didn't know you; shadows are real, people are false, there was originally no cause and effect; a hundred years later, there will be neither you nor me.

◆ Loving someone doesn't necessarily mean being together, liking Provence doesn't necessarily mean wandering barefoot among lavender fields.

◆ Two people who have been together for a long time are like left and right hands, even if they are no longer in love, they choose to stay together because giving up so many years takes great courage. Perhaps life will present someone you love, but they are just passers-by, you will still hold on to your left or right hand and walk on, happiness has nothing to do with love.

◆ When you give up, you must bear more, including having no regrets about what you've given up.

◆ Love turns strangers into lovers, then lovers back into strangers, it's a game.

◆ I've never liked others seeing my tears, I'd rather let others feel I'm happily carefree than let myself appear pitiful and wronged.

◆ Some things we ourselves cannot control, we can only control ourselves.

◆ Vows are words written on water, instantly disappearing; love is a house built on water, swaying endlessly; apologies are winter snow blankets, always coming too late. So like me and you are doves in the hands of angels, but cannot fly together forever.

◆ Women remember men who make them laugh, men remember women who make them cry, but women always stay with men who make them cry, men stay with women who make them laugh. But whether crying or laughing, both reveal true emotions. For most people, laughter is better than tears.

◆ Men are like onions, to see the heart of an onion requires peeling layer by layer, but during the process, you will constantly shed tears, peel to the end only to find that onions have no heart. Women are like roses, until the last petal unfolds, you cannot see her heart.

◆ Others fear parting, I fear reunion, wait until tears turn into diamonds, wait until romance turns into reason, suddenly dream of past vows, thinking that seeing makes love immortal, because that year we were reckless children. Now finally realize, no matter how much you like someone, if they aren't yours, no matter how much you are infatuated, you must let go.

◆ I don't know which of my current actions are right and which are wrong, and when I finally grow old and die, I will know these. So now all I can do is try my best to do every single thing well, then wait for death.

◆ Remember what should be remembered, forget what should be forgotten, change what can be changed, accept what cannot be changed.

◆ Give me a pair of hands, let you depend on, give me a pair of eyes, send you away, just like butterflies cannot cross mulberry fields, no one can blame harshly, give me a moment, let me adore you, give me a lifetime, send you away.

◆ Only two things can wash everything away, tears and time. Use time to push away feelings, the longer the time, the lighter the conflict, just like tea becoming diluted over time.

◆ Human tears represent kindness, ghost tears represent rebirth, angel tears represent failed love.

◆ Two stars can never meet, so we are meteors, we meet, we collide. But, as meteors, we are destined to disappear.
