The driver fled after he hit and killed a mother and her child while driving under the influence of alcohol

by zzf000zxye0 on 2011-08-05 14:37:09

Xinhua News Agency, Hangzhou, August 25 (reported by Pang Rui) - On August 25, the Traffic Police Detachment of Tiantai County in Taizhou City, Zhejiang Province held a public meeting to determine the responsibility for the "8・16" traffic accident. Dai Tianya, a 24-year-old BMW owner who drove under the influence and killed a mother and her son on August 16, was determined to have fled the scene after attempting to evade an alcohol test following the incident.

This traffic accident occurred during a nationwide concentrated campaign to strictly investigate drunk driving offenses, thus attracting significant social attention. The focus was on whether the perpetrator was identified as driving under the influence or being intoxicated, and whether it could be classified as a hit-and-run traffic offense. On the morning of August 25, representatives from the Tiantai County Traffic Police Detachment, both parties involved in the case, and individuals from various sectors of society attended the responsibility determination meeting.

Previously, according to the identification results from the Taizhou Public Security Bureau, blood tests showed that Dai Tianya's blood alcohol content was 0.72 mg/ml, which constituted drunk driving. Since the accident happened at around 22:25 on August 16, and Dai Tianya was taken to the hospital for a blood sample after 2:10 AM on August 17, with a gap of four hours in between, the victim's family expressed dissatisfaction with the alcohol test results despite being satisfied with the police response time.

Regarding this issue, the Tiantai County Traffic Police Detachment explained during the meeting that after the incident, Dai Tianya abandoned his car and left the scene while turning off his mobile phone, preventing the investigating officers from contacting him in a timely manner. After efforts by the police, Dai Tianya turned himself in at the Traffic Police Detachment at 2:10 AM on August 17, following persuasion from his family members, and was immediately taken to the People's Hospital for a blood sample.

According to the data provided by the Tiantai County Traffic Police Detachment, based on Article 85 of Chapter 11 (Supplementary Provisions) of the Regulations on Procedures for Handling Road Traffic Accidents, "hit-and-run in a traffic accident" refers to the act of a party involved in a road traffic accident fleeing the scene either by driving away or abandoning their vehicle in order to avoid legal responsibility. In this case, Dai Tianya abandoned the vehicle and fled the scene to evade alcohol testing after driving under the influence, which clearly meets the criteria specified in the above provisions, hence he was determined to have committed a hit-and-run offense.

At the same meeting, the Tiantai County Traffic Police Detachment also presented a medical diagnosis certificate issued by the Tiantai County People's Hospital on August 23. This certificate indicated that after blood tests were conducted on the victim Xi Liping, it was confirmed that she was not pregnant.

Tiantai County has strengthened its efforts to investigate traffic violations, recently catching 89 cases of drunk driving, including 13 cases of driving while intoxicated, resulting in administrative detention for 13 individuals.
