III. Market Survey Summary Analysis

1. The 21st century is the digital information age, the age of the Internet. According to the data published by Taobao, in 2007, Taobao achieved a transaction volume of 43.3 billion yuan, ranking second in China's retail industry after the Bailian Group. Currently, the average daily transaction volume provided by their Alipay exceeds close to 3 billion yuan. In contemporary society, with the deepening of China's reform and opening up, cities are getting bigger, work is getting later, and traffic is getting busier. Online shopping will only deepen and be adopted by more people through electronic commerce.

The advantage of online stores: To buy a seasonal piece of clothing using traditional methods requires setting aside specific time to go to commercial areas for browsing, selecting, trying on, and purchasing. If dissatisfied afterward, there is also the return process. In this aspect, besides the price tag, buying this piece of clothing also involves time cost, transportation cost, etc., which can be mentally and physically exhausting. In contrast, online shopping allows you to complete selection and comparison within half an hour by clicking a mouse, without being able to try on, but everything else is done, allowing you to wait for the clothes to be delivered. Moreover, the price is definitely cheaper than in malls (because there is no need to pay for mall property, personnel, advertising, etc.). This saves both time and money.

At the same time, we can frequently distribute questionnaires for terminal user surveys to understand what services terminal users truly need and how to establish our brand, always keeping track of market conditions.

2. Target Market Setting: Men's wear, women's wear, and children's wear focus on young consumer groups aged between 20 to 40. These consumers will be our store's largest customer base.

3. Analysis of Product Consumer Groups, Consumption Methods, Consumption Habits, and Main Factors Affecting the Market: With the development of the Internet and e-commerce, most young consumers increasingly prefer online shopping because it saves shopping time and provides a large number of products for customers to choose from, making it very convenient. Modern young people are very adaptable to online shopping, which is a significant advantage for our online store, providing us with numerous business opportunities. However, the biggest concern about online shopping remains credibility issues. Therefore, our marketing model will adopt simultaneous operation of physical stores and online stores, greatly enhancing our online store's credibility on the web.

4. Market Trend Forecast and Market Opportunities: Through our market research, we have found that the proportion of daily necessities purchases has significantly increased. This upward trend in daily necessities purchases will continue and eventually become the mainstream of online shopping. Therefore, we have chosen this type of product as our main product.

IV. Competitive Analysis

1. Industry Monopoly: No industry monopoly

2. Main Competitor Situation: Company strength, product situation (type, price, features, packaging, marketing, market share, etc.): After investigating several similar product online stores, we discovered that our competitors are relatively mature. However, the operating models they use contain some obvious errors. We conducted in-depth research on some consumer groups, who remain concerned about being deceived when shopping online (for consumers, the reputation of clothing products is a significant factor affecting their purchase decisions). Through these in-depth understandings, I believe it will be highly beneficial for the development of our online store. Currently, these online stores offer a decent variety of products, but their credibility is not high enough for consumers, and the product's reputation is insufficient, which is advantageous for us.

3. Company Product Competitive Advantage: We adopt simultaneous operation of physical stores and online stores to enhance our online store's credibility on the web; furthermore, during the initial phase of online store operations, we will heavily promote advertisements to increase the awareness of our online store's products. In this regard, we will collaborate with various clothing manufacturers. Every product purchased from our online store will have our online store's label or logo sewn onto the back collar of shirts or the back waistband of pants. This way, our online store's advertising costs will be reduced significantly, while simultaneously increasing our online store's popularity. We also provide real-time online consultations to help customers understand our products.

V. Main Development Strategic Goals and Stage Goals of the Online Store

1. Strategic Goal: Make online shopping simpler, more efficient, more credible, and accepted by different demographics. Let our customers enjoy happy and simple lives.

2. Stage Goals: Find suitable sources of goods for the online store to ensure quality and quantity, gradually penetrating the market and developing customers. To boost the popularity of our shop in the early stages, appropriate marketing promotions should be carried out, but relying solely on the internet is insufficient; multiple channels, both online and offline, should be used for promotion.

3. Progress Schedule:

First Stage: Market research, analyzing questionnaires, understanding customer needs;

Second Stage: Confirming supply chain, developing customers;

Third Stage: Opening of the physical store; opening and maintaining a Taobao store;

Fourth Stage: Promotional activities;

Fifth Stage: Regular operation of the online store and physical store;

Sixth Stage: Scaling up the online store;

Ultimate Goal: Use the online store to create a globally connected "store" network, meaning setting up warehouses nationwide to deliver orders accurately and promptly to designated locations.

VI. Marketing

1. Sales Policy Formulation: Physical store marketing + online marketing. Having a physical store that can be found in real life gives customers a sense of security and stability when shopping online. At the beginning of online store marketing, since advertising costs are relatively high, part of the funds can come from the profits of our physical store sales. When the online store grows larger, it can also reciprocally benefit our physical store, promoting each other mutually, leading to faster economic gains.

Using multiple online stores to sell the same products, i.e., initially adopting multiple Taobao accounts to uniformly sell our online store's products. By integrating our brand into multiple Taobao stores, we naturally increase the popularity and brand recognition of our online store.

2. Sales Channels, Methods, Marketing Links, and After-sales Service: Online sales + physical store sales, focusing on service.

From storefront selection to store design, considering differences in region, season, consumption habits, and economic levels comprehensively for dealers. Assisting in reasonable inventory allocation, meticulously planning all aspects such as promotional gifts for opening, providing comprehensive services including market strategies, advertising, image packaging, and public relations planning.

① The physical store will be located at the intersection of downtown and suburban areas, ensuring a reasonable cost while guaranteeing customer flow.

② During the early stages of online store operations, delivery will be handled via courier services to save on logistics costs. As our online store expands, the volume of shipments will increase significantly, at which point we will handle logistics ourselves to reduce expensive courier fees.

③ Warehouses across the country will stock different products based on local consumption levels and habits.

3. Primary Business Relationship Status: Our online store belongs to retailers.

Qualification Recognition Standards at All Levels: Payment Method: Online payment, cash on delivery, etc., available for consumers to choose from; Freight Method: Initially couriers, post offices, etc., later logistics; Discount Policy: Products are already discounted to ensure low prices.

4. Sales Team Situation and Sales Welfare Distribution Policies: Besides salaries, commissions based on sales volume, we will allow all employees to own shares in our online store to fully motivate employee enthusiasm.

5. Promotion and Market Penetration (Methods and Arrangements, Budget):

① Main Promotion Methods: Give away small gifts, choose to waive part of the shipping fee based on price, make every effort to satisfy customers.

② Payment Methods: Multiple options like Alipay, online banking, cash on delivery, etc., improving user experience to promote sales.

③ Advertising/PR Strategy, Media Evaluation: Add advertisements on large video websites (such as Youku, Tudou, Pipi Video, etc.), add advertisements on Taobao’s homepage, advertise on blogs, and promote plans on Baidu Tieba posts.

Purpose and Advantages of the Online Store:

Establish a New Corporate Image: For a lifestyle creative living supplies company, the corporate brand image is crucial. Especially in today's highly developed Internet technology era, most customers learn about corporate products, corporate image, and corporate strength through the web. Therefore, the image of the corporate online store often determines customer confidence in the company's products. Establishing an international-standard online store can significantly enhance the overall corporate image.

Optimize Internal Corporate Management: Building a corporate online store will introduce a completely new management model within the company, with the online store serving as the platform for this model. It plays a huge role in reducing internal resource waste, cutting costs, strengthening connections and communication among employees, and between the company and its employees, ultimately achieving maximum optimization of corporate operations and functioning.

Enhance Sales Power: Sales power refers to the comprehensive advantage of products reflected in sales. Modern marketing theory suggests that sales are also forms of communication, and the success of sales depends not only on whether the various advantages of the product can be fully communicated but also on the amount of effective information the target audience receives.

Due to the "one-to-one" nature of the Internet, target audiences can autonomously select useful information, which already indicates an interested premise for consumers absorbing information rather than having it subjectively imposed upon them. Additionally, product information presented through advanced online store designs combines the advantages of newspapers (large information volume) with the comprehensive stimulation of TV (sound, light, electricity), firmly attracting target audiences. Therefore, the effectiveness of product information dissemination will be greatly enhanced, which also increases the product's sales power.

Increase Added Value: Many people know that purchasing a product involves not just tangible items but also intangible after-sales services. This is the added value of the product, and the higher the added value, the more competitive the product is in the market and the more popular it is with consumers. Therefore, companies must strive to increase the added value of their products. In the current stage, traditional after-sales service methods cannot fully meet customer needs. Providing convenient, effective, and immediate 24-hour online services is a new way to demonstrate project added value. Customers worldwide can download required materials from the online store at any time, obtain answers to questions online, and submit their issues online.

Online Store Goods Promotion Plan

① Discount Promotion: Since online sales cannot give people a full, intuitive impression of products and do not allow for trial use or touch due to distribution costs and complex payment methods, resulting in decreased enthusiasm for online shopping and ordering, significant discounts can encourage consumers to attempt online shopping and make purchase decisions.

② Gift Promotion: The advantages of gift promotions include enhancing your visibility; encouraging people to visit the online store frequently to obtain more discount information; and summarizing and analyzing marketing effects and product responses based on consumer enthusiasm for gifts. However, avoid choosing inferior or substandard gifts, as doing so would counteract the intended effect.

③ Limited-Time Supply: "Limited-Time Supply" is always tempting to consumers. Phrases like "Only 2 days left at this special price!" But "Limited-Time Supply" must be genuine; otherwise, it will ultimately lose consumer trust.

④ Establish More External Links with Other Online Stores: As the saying goes, "More friends, more paths." On the web, this translates to "More links, more windows." By exchanging store links, a small network can be formed, enhancing mutual influence. Especially when someone gets featured on the homepage, you benefit indirectly. And when you're doing well, others can benefit from your success too. We will collaborate with peer online stores, for example, if customers buy from our online store, we'll give them small gifts provided by peer stores, and vice versa, aiming to promote our store's products.

⑤ Utilize Other Promotion Methods: Our salespeople can promote in various forums, QQ groups, MSN chats.

VII. Risk Analysis

1. Resource (Raw Materials/Suppliers) Risks

Our online store primarily selects fashionable, personalized, and high-quality products carefully curated by the owner. Secondly, products can carry our online store labels (clothes with our store's label or LOGO on the back collar, pants with the same on the back waistband). However, our store commits to offering the best, finest, and most beautiful products, adhering to the principle of providing the most economical and convenient gift services, specifically offering fashionable, personalized, and high-quality clothing for users. Selecting raw material suppliers and establishing good supply-demand relationships ensures access to first-hand trendy goods, guaranteeing low online sales prices while ensuring all products bear our online store's LOGO label.

2. Inventory Risks

① Single source of goods that may not appeal to customers.

② Quality issues with goods sourced from wholesale markets that cannot be returned or exchanged.

③ Mistakes by procurement staff.

④ Inaccurate procurement forecasts exceeding budget.

⑤ Accidents during transport (traffic accidents causing casualties).

3. Market Uncertainty Risks

Market share is a critical measure of a company's success. Most of the capital for e-commerce enterprises is used for product procurement and inventory. The quantity of products must be determined based on market share and sales volume. Analyze the choice of target markets through market size, status, trends, online consumer demand, pricing, marketing methods, promotional activities, product procurement, inventory management, and safety stock levels.

4. R&D Risks

Styles and varieties liked by oneself may not necessarily appeal to customers. Incomplete product lines and specifications, overly single products may lead to overstocking, placing the enterprise's capital in an economic crisis.

5. Production Uncertainty Risks

Product quality is key to the survival of a company. The quality of products is a crucial factor in enhancing the company's reputation. The impact of one consumer can affect at least 20 other consumers, highlighting the importance of quality to customers. If there are quality issues with the product, the merchant must assume responsibility.

6. Overstock Risks

① Overstocked goods prevent capital circulation, lacking funds for restocking and replenishing.

② Overstocked goods occupy inventory space, preventing the purchase of new goods.

7. Cost Control Risks

Analyze the financial status of the company mainly through capital investment, product cost analysis, period expenses, product sales profit, Guangzhou VIP Information Technology Co., Ltd., predicted cash flow statement, predicted income statement, predicted balance sheet, main business profit margin, total assets profit table, cost-expense profit margin, etc.

8. Capital Chain Risks

① Overstocked goods cannot be sold, resulting in no receipt of payment.

② Unable to purchase new goods, preventing product updates and losing customer purchasing desire.

③ Employee wages cannot be paid on schedule.

9. Competition Risks

In an era of fierce competition, it is necessary to clearly understand the various situations of competitors, comparing with them in terms of technology, product price, service, etc., to formulate competitive strategies, identifying strengths and weaknesses, adapting better to the development of the times.

10. Transaction Security Risks

① Network information data transmission may result in data theft or damage.

② Damage during transportation or incomplete box packaging.

11. Policy Risks

In the 21st century, the development of e-commerce has become a technical means and method of economic activity in the information age, entering the broadest application field of the Internet. Economic crises may also pose risks to us, as markets are unpredictable, and the government may make decisions to respond to market fluctuations, predicting macro and micro policies.

12. Financial Risks

Since enterprises have liabilities, there will inevitably be risks. At the beginning, it is necessary to reduce blindness and improve scientificity. When the pre-tax and interest rate on borrowed capital is higher than the interest rate on borrowed funds, using borrowed capital generates profit that compensates for interest payments and leaves surplus, thus increasing the profit of owned capital. Otherwise, the enterprise needs to use borrowed capital to repay principal and interest, falling into financial risks, necessitating strict control over fixed costs relative to sales revenue. Due to the uncertainty of the difference between the pre-tax and interest rate on borrowed capital and the interest rate on borrowed funds, there needs to be a reasonable capital structure, maintaining an appropriate level of debt, fully utilizing debt financing to gain financial returns, and enhancing profitability.

13. Management Risks (Including Personnel/Employee Turnover/Key Employee Dependence)

Investment is a business of managing talent. At the start, we build a management team with direct technology and experience, formulating feasible decisions for the company, executing the most efficient tasks, and inviting professionals with various technical and management experiences to join and hold important positions. Due to employee turnover, there needs to be a reasonable employee hiring criterion, creating a sustainable development harmonious environment, and controlling unexpected events.

14. Bankruptcy Risks

Due to increasingly fierce market competition, lack of management experience, low management quality, and monotonous business models, the market may not fully accept our products, leading to product stagnation in the online store, posing a significant risk of bankruptcy. Only by stimulating customer purchasing desires and making products unique can we secure a place in the market.

VIII. How to Deal with These Risks

1. Purchase goods from surrounding large wholesale markets. When purchasing goods from wholesale markets, strong bargaining skills are needed to lower wholesale prices as much as possible. Good relationships should be established with wholesalers, and agreements regarding returns and exchanges should be clarified to avoid future disputes. Also, look for products not commonly seen offline or not circulating in the market to find the cheapest, highest quality, and stable supply channels. Instead of finding just one supplier, seek related unique products for sale and design some new products for manufacturers to produce.

2. Buy goods at an extremely low price and resell them online, utilizing the advantages of online sales to gain sufficient profits through regional or temporal price differences. Therefore, you should frequently visit the market and closely monitor market changes.

3. After shopping, notify both parties' contact information and conduct transactions according to agreed methods, choosing either face-to-face transactions or transactions through remittances and mail, with real-time monitoring of items in transit.

4. Place sufficient emphasis on customer relationships, establishing customer contact cards and widely promoting positive customer feedback.

5. Each marketing method has clear goals, informing customers or potential customers what to do next.

6. In the information age, use the internet to regularly send relevant product introductions to customers, allowing them to understand the latest discounts, promotions, and new products.

7. Innovation always comes with failure, but in continuous innovation exploration, risks can be minimized, giving us good psychological resilience.

8. Find the unique selling points of your online store.

9. Regularly send newsletters to customers. Using the email addresses and contact information left by customers, regularly send them information about discounted products, member-exclusive gifts, and thoughtful birthday gifts for members.

IX. Required Skills

1. Select products, determine market positioning for prices, and lock in target consumer groups.

2. Experiment, experiment, and experiment again (because you will learn many things in this process, accumulating bit by bit).

3. Learn value innovation. Simply selling products will not make money; the money earned will only cover daily expenses, also known as pocket money.

4. Be familiar with the rules of online trading platforms.

5. Grasp sales techniques and maintain customer relationships, welcoming customers with integrity as the foundation. Answer comments promptly and honestly, resolving buyer doubts and increasing their trust. Online stores have low costs, no need for complicated procedures, generally offering more affordable prices. Some stores also use methods like discounts, giving gifts, and holding lotteries to provide benefits. Doing business online requires valuing credit; only with good credibility can more stable customers be won. Some stores also use methods like introducing star-rated customers, sharing life experiences, and publishing random thoughts, turning their small stores into communities and transforming customers into friends.
