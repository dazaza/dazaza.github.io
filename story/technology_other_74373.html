**Legendary Designated Teleportation Player Script**

**Function:**

- Teleport yourself

---

[@Main]

Do you want to assassinate your enemy?

Is your friend being chased?

Do you want to team up with your friend to fight a BOSS?

Does your friend run out of potions while fighting the BOSS?

For just 10 Yuanbao, I can teleport you directly to their side!

Note:

---

[@Teleport Yourself]

#IF

CHECKGAMEGOLD > 19

#ACT

GAMEGOLD - 10

#SAY

; After confirming the input, it will jump to [@InPutString1]

#ELSEACT

MessageBox You don't have 10 Yuanbao to pay for this service...

CLOSE

break

---

[@InPutString1]

#ACT

REGOTO S0

; REGOTO teleports yourself to a specific player who must be online

; The characters entered by the player are saved by default in the string variable S0
