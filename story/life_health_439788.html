The difference between beauty crowns and porcelain teeth

by 454kq on 2011-05-31 10:56:58

The Difference Between Beauty Crown and Porcelain Teeth, Beauty Crown technology is the latest dental aesthetic project. It can quickly and effectively help you solve a series of problems such as tooth necrosis and tooth discoloration. Many people confuse the beauty crown restoration technique with porcelain teeth restoration. In fact, these two techniques are completely different, although they can both effectively solve dental problems.

Below, let's learn about some specific differences:

First, in terms of function, porcelain teeth emphasize the treatment and restoration of teeth to achieve a re-treatment effect, while beauty crown focuses more on the whitening and beautification of teeth while ensuring the basic function of teeth.

Secondly, from a technical perspective, porcelain teeth belong to a traditional dental treatment method, while beauty crowns use modern scientific and technological achievements in the field of dentistry, including 3D digital positioning technology, Vita color matching technology, and painless technology.

In addition, in terms of processing and production technology, porcelain teeth use traditional dental treatment techniques, which are relatively outdated and conventional in operation, while the beauty crown technology uses precise oral restoration techniques, which are more precise in operation.

Finally, in terms of the final color and effect of the teeth, porcelain teeth mostly use traditional color matching technology, so the color of the teeth is prone to distortion and inconsistent tones. The beauty crown uses advanced foreign color matching technology, which makes the color of the teeth more natural and realistic after beautification, and has no impact on the oral cavity after restoration.
