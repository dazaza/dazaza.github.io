Zhengzhou Huashan Hospital "Affordable Medical Care, Benefit for People's Livelihood"_ News Center_ Shangdu Network

by noprefect on 2011-03-27 10:10:15

"Difficult and expensive medical treatment" has always been a highly concerned and strongly reflected social issue by all sectors of society and the people. As a national people's hospital, Zhengzhou Huashan Hospital strictly adheres to the guiding ideology of "patient-centered, striving to reduce medical expenses and continuously improving medical service quality". It focuses on medical quality management, formulates and implements management regulations and monitoring measures such as "accurate diagnosis, standardized treatment, transparent charging, high-quality and efficient", so that the people can afford and get good medical treatment.

Authoritative and trustworthy hospital, professional and reliable

Zhengzhou Huashan Hospital is a comprehensive hospital integrating medical treatment, scientific research, teaching, and health care. It is one of the first hospitals in China to be rated as a National People's Hospital. The "Minimally Invasive Diagnosis and Treatment Center for Obstetrics and Gynecology" carefully created by the hospital has been commended many times by governments at all levels. Zhengzhou Huashan Hospital has been rated as a "Provincial, Municipal, Urban and Rural Medical Insurance Designated Hospital", "Maternity Insurance Designated Hospital", "Zhengzhou Honest Hospital", etc. The hospital always adheres to development as the theme, focusing on the construction of medical ethics and style, continuously enhancing its core competitiveness and overall strength, and achieving an excellent hospital that is "trusted by the people".

Transparent prices, trusted by the people

In response to the long-term difficulty of medical treatment for vulnerable groups, the Ministry of Health proposed at the National Health Work Conference that each city should choose some hospitals as pilot projects for the transformation of operating mechanisms, establishing affordable hospitals or affordable wards, while controlling the special needs services of hospitals.

As a comprehensive specialized hospital, Zhengzhou Huashan Hospital strictly implements the national medical service charge standards, firmly implements the patient charge one-day list system, cost inquiry system, main charge item public disclosure system and other open, transparent, and reasonable charging systems. In order to implement the spirit of the notice issued by the National Development and Reform Commission and the Ministry of Health on reforming the management of medical service prices (Ji Jia [2000] No. 962), legally priced, fees disclosed principles, our hospital specifically announces the costs of abortion and cervical erosion:

[Affordable Healthcare, Benefiting the People] "Sleep-like painless abortion" 450 yuan

In order to further enhance the credibility of the hospital and make every charge more open and transparent, Zhengzhou Huashan Hospital has set up a price display screen in the hospital to publish detailed information such as drug names, specifications, manufacturers, and retail prices, increasing public supervision while promoting the improvement of the hospital's internal price management system. Zhengzhou Huashan Hospital has won the recognition of all sectors in Henan Province and has successively been awarded the honorary titles of "Zhengzhou Honest Hospital" and "Hospital Trusted by the People".

Scientific examination, reasonable treatment, affordable creation of the first people's hospital

According to the needs of patients' conditions, Zhengzhou Huashan Hospital strictly stipulates the indications for various clinical examinations, fully respects the right to know and choice of patients, and doctors must inform patients and their families of the necessity and precautions of the examination, as well as the pros and cons before the examination. On the basis of full understanding by patients and their families, patients are allowed to choose autonomously. The hospital resolutely implements not using special examinations if general instruments can be used for clear diagnosis, and not using imported expensive examinations if domestic ones can achieve the purpose. If it is indeed necessary to use them, the patient or family must sign the informed consent for special examination. Now, Zhengzhou Huashan Hospital advocates spending less money to prevent major diseases and conducts the "Couple Disease and Male Difficult Disease Collection Activity (Second Stage)", which has received good feedback and enthusiastic responses. Even more than a week after the deadline, the hospital continues to receive letters and calls from respondents.

Experts at Zhengzhou Huashan Hospital kindly remind:

Winter is coming, and 40% of women are prone to gynecological diseases.

According to data released by the World Health Organization (WHO), 40% of Chinese women suffer from varying degrees of reproductive tract infections and other gynecological diseases, and the incidence rate among married women is as high as 70%. Zhengzhou Huashan Hospital once conducted a gynecological disease census on 460 people, and the results showed that nearly half of the women had gynecological inflammation, including 169 cases of cervical erosion, 57 cases of uterine fibroids, and 3 cases of early-stage cervical cancer patients, with the youngest being only 30 years old.

Therefore, gynecological experts at Zhengzhou Huashan Hospital suggest that women over 35 years old should have an annual check-up. However, this does not mean that young women can be careless, because the onset age of many diseases is gradually becoming younger.
