China Telecom's new version of iPhone pre-sale details: five channels open on the 13th

by anonymous on 2013-09-12 12:15:04

On September 12th, regarding the new version of iPhone which is highly anticipated by users currently, China Telecom announced that from 15:00 on September 13th Beijing Time, all channels will open up for pre-orders of the telecom version of the iPhone 5s and 5c. Users can reserve the new version of the iPhone by paying a deposit of 200 yuan for prepaid phone credit, and will be prioritized to receive the new version of the iPhone upon its official launch. They will also receive a gift of 300 yuan in phone credit or other presents.

Pre-orders fully open at 15:00 on the 13th

In the early hours of September 11th Beijing Time, Apple held a new product launch event at its headquarters in California, USA, officially releasing the long-awaited products - the iPhone 5s and iPhone 5c to the global market. At 10:00 AM on September 11th, Apple held a product launch event in mainland China, where China will become one of the first countries to have the new version of the iPhone available.

As one of the first operators in mainland China to introduce the iPhone, China Telecom showcased the telecom version of the new iPhone at the launch event, introducing the full range of colors and models of the iPhone 5s and iPhone 5c. Among them, the appearance of the telecom version of the iPhone 5s is basically the same as the iPhone 5, but there will be certain upgrades in configuration and some new functions embedded within it. The telecom version of the iPhone 5c can be seen as an economical and practical version of the iPhone 5, with five color options available, and priced lower than the iPhone 5s.

Regarding the question of when the new version will be launched, it is reported that the telecom version of the iPhone 5s and iPhone 5c are expected to be officially launched on September 20th, synchronized globally.

In the Beijing area, a deposit of 200 yuan will grant 300 yuan in phone credit

It is understood that the preferential policies for pre-orders have been announced, such as during the pre-order period for the telecom version of the iPhone 5s and 5c, users in the Beijing area will be offered various preferential measures including gifts for pre-orders, premium number reservations, contract price reductions, and free 4G offers.

Among these, the reservation for the next generation of iPhones is divided into star-level reservations, full-price reservations, and free reservations. Star-level reservation means that users pay a deposit of 200 yuan in prepaid phone credit when making the reservation. Upon officially signing the contract, they will have two exclusive preferential options to choose from: a gift of 300 yuan in phone credit; or two tickets to the Beijing leg of the Voice of China concert.

Full-price reservation refers to the contract price, and is limited to electronic channel processing. The preferential policy enjoyed is the same as the star-level reservation.

Users can also opt for a free reservation, leaving their reservation information so that Beijing Telecom will notify them promptly when sales begin. When signing the contract, they can also enjoy direct price reduction benefits and 4G data gifting.

Five channels open for reservations

China Telecom stated that the reservation channels for the telecom version of the iPhone 5s/5c are very extensive. Users can make reservations through nearly 6000 authorized stores nationwide, as well as the customer service hotline 10000 in all 31 provinces across the country. They can also log onto China Telecom's online business hall www.189.cn, Tianyi Mobile Phone Network www.hicdma.com, China Telecom's Taobao Tmall flagship store, JD.com, Amazon, etc., for online reservations. China Telecom specifically pointed out that in this new version of the iPhone reservation, Weibo and Yixin exclusive reservation and pre-sales will be attempted for the first time, bringing a brand-new experience.

Specifically in the Beijing area, apart from the aforementioned reservation channels, reservations for the telecom version of the iPhone 5s and 5c can also be made at three major malls - Suning, Gome, and Dazhong, or four large chains - Dixintong, Leyu Communications, Huaxin Tong, and Xingqiu Tong. This way, users who pre-order the new telecom version of the iPhone will avoid having to queue up and pay extra.

Meanwhile, other places across the country will also conduct pre-orders for the telecom version of the iPhone 5s and 5c through various social channels.

The iPhone event will be held on September 10th.
