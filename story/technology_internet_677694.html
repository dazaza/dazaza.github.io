Nine freshmen, both male and female, were expelled for hanging out in the girls' restroom.

by uelu6c6b on 2012-02-27 09:10:50

Xinhua News Agency, February 25 (by Yan Cui) - Not long ago, nine freshmen students from Jinan No. 2 Middle School received a school notification stating that they would be expelled for "severe disciplinary violations" due to male and female students being found together in the girls' restroom. The students were dissatisfied with the decision, and their parents repeatedly negotiated with the school authorities. On February 25, the parents of the students contacted the reporter, saying that the school agreed to first have the students write a confession letter, and then decide on further actions after reviewing it.

On the evening of January 10 during self-study time, these nine students were caught by the school security guards and teachers inside the girls' restroom located north of the school playground, which was originally only used by athletes. The restroom was filled with the smell of cigarette smoke.

"The children smoking is wrong, but they should be educated. Isn't expulsion too severe?" said Ms. Lu, a parent. According to the school's requirements, all these students have been sent home, "and every day at home means missing a day of class."

"Smoking is a minor issue; the key is the location. Male and female students together in the girls' restroom is inappropriate and constitutes serious disciplinary violations," said Zou Aijun, Vice Principal of Jinan No. 2 Middle School. He added that the school, as a sacred place for teaching and nurturing, had witnessed such major disciplinary violations by these nine students within half a year of their enrollment, which was rare.

Zou Aijun showed the reporter a document titled "Investigation and Handling Decision on the Serious Disciplinary Violation Incident During Evening Self-Study on January 10." The handling decision described the situation on that day: Around 8:45 PM during the third self-study period, the on-duty security guard heard male voices coming from the girls' restroom while patrolling the campus, assuming them to be outsiders. However, the security guard did not dare to enter the girls' restroom without permission, so he waited outside for about ten minutes. During this period, the security guard heard some female voices saying, "We are beauties, we will strip naked." Sensing the severity of the situation, the security guard called the duty leader. Subsequently, the duty leader, along with a female teacher, went to the girls' restroom and caught six boys and three girls inside the toilet stalls. At the time, there was a strong smell of cigarette smoke in the restroom.

According to the handling decision, "This is a serious case of student misconduct, and it is the most severe violation to date, reflecting that these students have no sense of rules or discipline, act recklessly, and completely disregard strict school regulations. To uphold school discipline, mitigate the adverse effects caused by this incident, and ensure the normal conduct of educational work, after deliberation by the principal's office meeting, it has been decided to expel the above-mentioned nine students."

Upon receiving the expulsion notice, the students became depressed, either shutting themselves in their rooms without speaking much, or one girl even attempted suicide by taking sleeping pills, fortunately saved in time by the hospital.

"The children can't think clearly, what if something happens?" Not just Ms. Lu, all the parents were extremely anxious, "The children are so young, what will happen if they don't go to school?"

For several days, the parents have been trying to contact the school and conducted multiple negotiations, hoping the school would give their children another chance.
