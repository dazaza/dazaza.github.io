The discharged female soldier Zhang Xifeng is the village director and head of the Women's Federation of Zhangling Village. She is currently attending a "Double Study Double Compare" on-site meeting in the town. At this time, Ping Taoming, the son of the former village chief Ping Wu who serves as the captain of an engineering team in the city, has returned to the village to recruit workers for his newly contracted project. As a result, the usually peaceful Zhangling Village is in turmoil. Young and able-bodied men such as Jing Cai, Wang Xuebu, Siku, and Wang Xiaoqing have all signed up to work with Ping Taoming in the city. When Zhang Xifeng, who had just returned from the higher-level Women's Federation with an ecological pig farming project she had fought hard for, excitedly came back to the village, the men ready to work in the city were already packed and ready to go. Thus, it became a true "women's village." These left-behind women and left-behind children demonstrated self-entrepreneurship in the new era, showcasing the style of discharged soldiers in building a harmonious society.

Zhangling Village, nestled by mountains and water, has a relatively backward economy. The village director, Zhang Xifeng, brought back materials for developing a pig farming base from the town's Women's Federation. She handed them over to the accountant Xiuyu at the village entrance before rushing home. At this time, the two little white pigs her family had just bought had wandered into Li Erbai's yard and were hidden by him, who loves to take advantage of small benefits. However, their neighbor Chen Lianhua knew about it.

Li Erbai refused to return the pigs to Xifeng and decided to dye them black with ink. Guangda went to buy ink, and when Xiangchun asked what he was doing, he lied and said he was practicing calligraphy. In the evening, Chen Lianhua heard the pigs squealing in the neighboring yard and told her husband Siku about Li Erbai hiding the pigs.

Ping Taoming expanded his engineering team and recruited workers in the village, with many villagers signing up enthusiastically. Despite being newly married, Xiuyu reluctantly agreed to let her husband work in the city after much persuasion by Wang Xiaode. Siku received his wife’s support to work in the city, while Yuan Ling's husband Xuebu refused to go, leading Yuan Ling to angrily sign herself up instead. Due to lack of skills, the single man Erwang from the village was rejected by Ping Taoming, and Guangda was stopped by Erbai when he tried to sign up. Upon learning that Taoming was recruiting workers in the village, Xifeng tried to negotiate with him to temporarily halt recruitment in order to retain the limited male labor force to build the pig farming base, but Taoming would not budge.

Yang Minghua then asked her daughter Yang Mei to persuade Taoming. That night, Taoming found Siku and asked him to inform others about the suspension of recruitment in the village. The next day, the men planning to work in the city came to find Xifeng for answers. After failing to persuade them otherwise, Xifeng had no choice but to agree.
