A New E-commerce Model is About to Appear

It is reported that the 9th China (Zhongshan) International Electronic Information Products and Technology Exhibition (referred to as Electric Fair) will be held at Zhongshan Torch International Convention and Exhibition Center on December 18, 2009. The digital trade industry will participate in the exhibition for the first time with a third-generation new e-commerce model. At that time, Shanghai CR Express will spark a "Digital Trade Whirlwind" of a new e-commerce model. During the conference, an intelligent e-commerce information transaction website portal was born, developed by Shenzhen Jianhua Porter Network Technology Co., Ltd. in collaboration with Peking University, featuring the PT37 "Transaction Portal Platform + Intelligent Shopping Guide Robot." Relevant experts stated that:

The PT37 website has overturned traditional e-commerce application concepts and will play an important role in future e-commerce fields. Shanghai CR Express provides full one-stop services for small and medium-sized enterprises urgently needing to upgrade their e-commerce informatization applications.

"Online consumption is gradually becoming a mainstream consumption method. As a new model of third-generation e-commerce, digital trade is dedicated to building direct trade channels between merchants and consumers," said Ou Yangjinquan, Deputy Director of the Development Zone Management Committee, in an interview with reporters. Entering the e-commerce field strengthens overseas promotion, expanding exhibition booths to 700, with 31 special booths, expecting about 20,000 domestic and foreign professional buyers... Whether in terms of booth numbers or buyer quality, this year's scale of the Shanghai CR Express has reached a completely new level, ensuring convenience for both merchants and consumers.

One of the biggest highlights of this year's electric fair is the overseas promotional cooperation reached with professional media such as Alibaba and慧聪(Hc360). This has also become a hot topic among many industry insiders. According to relevant responsible persons from the Electric Fair Organizing Committee, in fact, since the first electric fair, the organizers have intended to make it an international exhibition. This overseas promotional cooperation with professional media officially signals the electric fair's alignment with international markets.

Under the large background of social economic informatization development, using e-commerce's low cost and high efficiency for online trade has quickly become the top choice for SMEs. Numerous SMEs are utilizing e-commerce platforms to expand business networks and channels for online trade.

Based on the concept of internationalized exhibition hosting, this year’s electric fair has made its first clear functional division in the exhibition hall setup, aiming to enhance the overall exhibition level. It is introduced that this electric fair will be divided into three exhibition halls: the Latest Technology and Application Hall, the One-stop Procurement Service Hall, and the Digital Life Special Hall. Moreover, this year's electric fair has specially invited the Guangzhou offices of more than 20 countries, organizing enterprises from their own countries to visit and purchase, thereby improving the quality of international buyers.
