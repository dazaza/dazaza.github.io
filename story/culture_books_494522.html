"Xinhai Revolution" presents the full historical truth of the Xinhai Revolution

by man8737 on 2011-09-24 15:53:02

"The Xinhai Revolution" is the complete novel version of the movie authorized exclusively by the film crew for the 100th anniversary of the Xinhai Revolution.

This book truly displays the epic scenes of the Xinhai Revolution, vividly portrays historical figures such as Sun Yat-sen, Huang Xing, Xu Zonghan, Qiu Jin, Lin Juemin, Li Yuanhong, and Yuan Shikai, and brilliantly recreates major historical events such as the Yellow Flower Mound Uprising, the Wuchang Uprising, the Yangxia Defense Battle, and the establishment of the Republic.

Compared to the homonymous movie, this book focuses on telling the intricate background of the situation before and after the Xinhai Revolution, and introduces the life stories of nearly a hundred historical figures. With its irresistible readability, respect for the authenticity of objective history, and abundant knowledge, it has become the first choice for readers to learn about the history of the Xinhai Revolution.
