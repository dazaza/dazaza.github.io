Magic damage reduction is the same as above. Physical absorption is 1% + 500 power. Spell absorption is the same as above. For War Gods and Sword Immortals: 1 point of strength adds 240 power; for Celestial Masters and Netherworld: Strength does not add to power. Agility is the same as above, 1 point of agility adds 48 power. Intelligence does not add to power, 1 point of intelligence adds 240 power. 1 point of constitution adds 108 power (same for all classes). 1 point of spirit adds 60 power for Celestial Masters and Netherworld, while 1 point of spirit adds 132 power for War Gods and Sword Immortals.

In fact, all the information mentioned above can be obtained in the game. It is written here just to make players understand more clearly and accurately. If you want to know how much power your equipment needs to reach Mythic status, you can press the golden thumb icon next to the equipment slot. Below are some materials found online.

Equipment tiers are represented by 'd'...

Increase in life limit by 1 point: Wu Saint and Sword Immortal gain 5 points of power; necklaces and Celestial Master/Netherworld gain 6 points of power value.

Increase in spell limit by 1 point: Adds 1 point of power value to the equipment.

Physical/Spell Defense by 1 point: Necklaces add 40 power for Sword Immortals, 36 power for Wu Saints, 30 power for Celestial Masters and Netherworld.

Minimum/Maximum Physical/Spell Defense by 1 point: Necklaces add 20 points of power for Sword Immortals, 18 points of power for Wu Saints, and 15 points of power for Celestial Masters and Netherworld.

Physical/Spell Attack by 1%: 1D600, 2D700, 3D1000, 4D1200, 5D1400, 6D1900, 7D2400.

Physical/Spell Critical by 1%: 1D200, 2D300, 3D1000, 4D1100, 5D1300, 6D1600, 7D1600.

Hit Penetration by 1%: Celestial Masters, Netherworld, Sword Immortals, and rings add 100 power, Wu Saints add 200 power.

Dodge/Block by 1%: Wu Saints add 1D2D100 points of power, 3D and above add 200 points of power.

Necklaces and Celestial Masters/Netherworld add 1D2D2003D and above 300 points of power.

Sword Immortals add 1-4D200 points of power, 5D and above add 300 points of power.

All attributes by 1 point: Rings, necklaces, Wu Saints, and Sword Immortals add 648 points of power, Celestial Masters and Netherworld add 528 points of power.

Physical Rebound Damage by 1 point: Adds 300 points of power to the equipment.

Anti-Critical by 1%: 1D400, 2D500, 3D600, 4D700, 5D700, 6D900, 7D700.

Note: Physical attack/critical only increases the power of Wu Saint and Sword Immortal equipment; spell attack/critical only increases the power of Celestial Master and Netherworld equipment.

Defense increase by 1 point refers to the maximum and minimum base defense of the equipment each increasing by 1 point. The rest follows this pattern.

The additional attribute power of rings and necklaces is not affected by class.
