Successful websites all succeed by changing people's internet lifestyle habits!

Today, while chatting with my business partner, he said that perhaps our free online accounting book — Coko365 ( www.coko365.com) might become as big and influential as blogs. Currently, Coko365 ( www.coko365.com) is the first in China to introduce the concept of "Caike" ("财客"), which refers to those who enjoy keeping accounts or use online accounting services. Just like blogs, three years ago, not many people used or understood blogs, but now they have swept across the global internet, undeniably a network miracle. Why has blogging achieved such great success? This is due to the efforts of blog sites in promoting and operating them, turning non-bloggers into bloggers, forming the habit of writing blogs among netizens. I remember in junior high school, teachers would ask us to write diaries every day, and we would just record trivial daily affairs to get it over with. Even after entering the workforce, we never formed the habit of writing diaries. But when blogs became popular in China, I started writing blogs every day, casually recording things, almost every day without fail, which has become a part of my daily life! Therefore, my goal with my business partner is to change the accounting habits of Chinese netizens, cultivating their habit of online accounting and financial management. Let online accounting become a part of internet lifestyle! Only by changing netizens' internet lifestyle habits can one succeed, just like blogs.

Therefore, after analyzing various website models, I have come to a general conclusion in the internet industry: successful websites all succeed by changing people's internet lifestyle habits! If you don't believe me, let's continue to analyze some currently successful websites in China.

1. B2B: There are many successful B2B websites, with representative ones being Alibaba, Global Sources, and MaiMai.com. Why do these websites succeed? It's because they changed the habits of their target members, specifically the habit of doing business. They made target members subconsciously recognize that doing business online is feasible and highly effective, so B2B websites succeeded. Regardless of the B2B model and functional columns, on the internet, those who start early and have enough money to persist will succeed.

2. Tourism: Successful tourism websites in China include Ctrip and eLong. Why do they succeed? Simply put, they changed people's habit of booking tickets. In the past, people would buy tickets in person or order by phone, but now? You can book through websites, simple, convenient, and cost-effective.

3. Recruitment: Traditional corporate recruitment methods involve going to job fairs. However, everyone knows that although there are many people at job fairs, due to time constraints, you can only meet a few candidates. In summer, the weather is hot, making it even more uncomfortable, and weekends require going out, which is quite troublesome. But since online recruitment came about, it changed people's recruitment methods and habits. The benefits of recruitment websites naturally far outweigh those of job fairs, otherwise, they wouldn't have developed to today's scale!

4. Search: Traditionally, if people wanted to find information, they would go to libraries, consult friends, or publish in newspapers, etc. But since search engines appeared, they changed the way people find information. When netizens want to find information online, they naturally think of search engines, thus search engines succeeded because they changed people's habits.

5. Games: Traditionally, people spent their idle time playing cards or mahjong, but these activities required certain conditions, such as time, place, and people. But since online games came about, as long as you have a computer, you can play, whether it's against netizens from afar or against computers.

6. Blogs: Previously, if people wrote diaries, they would simply record daily events in a notebook, merely text. So many people didn't have this habit. Since blog websites appeared, people discovered that writing blogs online is not only interesting but also better, combining text, sound, and images. Blogs changed the way people write diaries.

7. Caike: Chinese people manage finances and keep accounts either by using notebooks or not at all. Coko365: www.coko365.com aims to change the accounting and financial management habits of its target members, making them gradually like and naturally use the free online accounting book developed by Coko365 for accounting and financial planning. Only by forming a trend and changing the habits of target members can Coko365 naturally succeed! Perhaps the next hotspot in the internet world will be "Caike". Currently, on the internet, there are terms like Weike, Pinke, Huanke, etc., and we believe that "Caike" will certainly become one of the most dazzling among them.

8. Online Shopping: Traditionally, people generally shopped at supermarkets or small stores, but since the advent of the internet, people can select products online, buying them from home, saving time going to the bank or supermarket, and instantly seeing products from all over China, comparing prices, etc. Thus, online shopping changed people's shopping habits.

Successful websites all succeed by changing people's internet lifestyle habits!
