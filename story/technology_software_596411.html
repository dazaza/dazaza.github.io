Lingshan Yiyuan Lixing Hot Spring Tourism

by lingxi54 on 2011-11-30 17:01:26

The relatively well-known hot springs in the East China region are Tangshan and Wuyi. There are nearly forty hot spring bathing places of various sizes in the town of Tangshan, with prices ranging from one yuan to nearly a hundred yuan, offering a wide range of options. During the peak season for hot springs, there are often queues due to the large number of people. The most famous is the Tangshan Yishang Open-air Hot Spring Resort, which features 50 open-air hot spring pools and 30 indoor specialty hot spring rooms. It takes about 3.5 hours to drive from Shanghai to Tangshan. The Wuyi Hot Spring Resort contains multiple hot spring hotels and resorts, the most famous being the "Tangfeng Open-air Hot Spring", which offers nearly thirty types of open-air hot spring experiences. Next is the Clear Water Bay Hot Spring Resort, designed and built to three-star tourist foreign-related hotel standards. The main feature of Wuyi Clear Water Bay Hot Spring is its large-scale water entertainment projects, suitable for families with children or young couples. It takes around four hours to drive from Shanghai to Wuyi.

According to staff at the Ling Mountain Yuan Yi Lixing Hot Spring Club, many members frequently travel to Japan for vacations to experience hot springs. After coming here, they feel it's no less enjoyable than soaking in Japanese hot springs. They believe that the Zen culture of Ling Mountain creates an atmosphere of mental and physical return. On weekends, you can enjoy the feeling of soaking in a Japanese hot spring without leaving the country through the Ling Mountain Yuan Yi Lixing Hot Spring Tour.

Compared to the crowded scenes at Nanjing Tangshan Hot Spring and Wuyi Tangfeng Hot Spring, Ling Mountain Yuan Yi Lixing Hot Spring is a six-star international hot spring club. Its dense forest hot spring area and business lake-view hot spring area cater to the high-end crowd's preference for the environment of hot spring experiences. The quality of the hot spring experience not only depends on the quality of the water but also on the overall environmental atmosphere. For high-end customers, they prefer hot spring venues with a certain level of privacy and the integration of people with the environment during the experience. Located by Taihu Lake and at the foot of Ling Mountain, Ling Mountain Yuan Yi Lixing Hot Spring has a unique Southeast Asian style theme architecture, decorated with original Chinese traditional culture, blending Ling Mountain Zen culture, and is popular among high-end customers in the Yangtze River Delta. This time, the reporter drove two hours from Shanghai, taking the Xicheng Expressway to the Mashan Toll Station in Wuxi, then driving along the Lu Ma Highway towards the Ling Mountain Scenic Area direction. There are route signposts for the Wuxi Ling Mountain Yuan Yi Lixing Hot Spring Resort Hotel along the way, greatly facilitating tourists traveling by car.

Ling Mountain Yuan Yi Lixing Hot Spring Resort Hotel is a strong collaboration between the Yuan Yi Group and the Lis International Group. Lis International promotes itself with the slogan "cherishing elites, respecting celebrities," aiming to provide high-end wealth living for businessmen, elites, and celebrities in mainstream society. The esteemed service concept of Ling Mountain Yuan Yi Lixing Hot Spring reveals itself in the details, such as the staff driving guests from Hilton Garden Inn to Lixing Hot Spring, slowing down when passing speed bumps to minimize vibration, making the guests feel extremely cared for and detailed attention.

Passing service, kneeling service, and bowing etiquette are the ways of treating guests that the staff of Ling Mountain Yuan Yi Lixing Hot Spring have always adhered to. Regardless of where you are in the hotel, you can receive the most timely and enthusiastic service from the staff. A Mr. Qian, who does business in Shanghai, told reporters that he accompanied his business partner from Nanjing to experience the Ling Mountain Yuan Yi Lixing Hot Spring. Even clients from Nanjing who regularly visit Tangshan Hot Spring praised Ling Mountain Yuan Yi Lixing Hot Spring highly. "When soaking in the hot spring here, whether you're a member or not, you feel like a distinguished guest, which is an important reason why I choose to bring friends here, for Ling Mountain Yuan Yi Lixing Hot Spring Tourism." Mr. Qian said, "Additionally, the environment here is more secluded, not too crowded or noisy, fitting the preferences and status of my clients."
