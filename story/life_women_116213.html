If Demi Moore were born in our country, I think the first thing she would need to do to become a movie star would be to undergo square chin plastic surgery.

Therefore, if the jawbone below the ear root is in a right-angled square shape, it can give a male a tough impression, but if it’s a woman, it will give an impression of being stubborn. Therefore, most women hope their face shape becomes slim and long.

* Square Chin Correction Surgery (Mandibular Angle Reduction Surgery)

First, an incision is made inside the mouth, then the excess mandible bone is removed. The line that needs to be removed is determined using X-ray photos before the operation. The stitches inside the mouth will use materials that can be absorbed by the human body. In addition, compression bandages need to be used for about two days to prevent swelling, and the patient can be discharged the day after the operation.

Three days after the operation, the swelling begins to subside, and after about two weeks, the swelling disappears basically, allowing the resumption of normal social life.

On the first day after the operation, only water can be consumed, but starting from the second day, rice soup, porridge, and other liquid foods can be eaten, and this process needs to continue for about a week.

To prevent infection, antibiotics need to be taken continuously for about a week after the operation. Since the incision is inside the mouth, the mouth needs to be rinsed and disinfected after each meal to prevent infection.

B. Long Chin

The normal occlusion state of teeth is that the upper teeth are located in front of the lower teeth and slightly cover the lower teeth. However, for people with long chins, the lower teeth are located in front of the upper teeth, so one can check their occlusion state by naturally closing their lips to determine if they have a long chin.

Although there are no major obstacles in bodily function, a long chin can give an impression of being too stubborn. Additionally, there may be issues such as unclear pronunciation or chewing difficulties.

Through long chin correction surgery, the troubles brought by a long chin can be resolved.

* Long Chin Surgery (Correcting Mandibular Prognathism)

If the mandible is too prominent, the patient's tooth occlusion status must be judged to determine whether the entire mandible needs to be corrected or just its front end. If the entire mandible needs surgery, orthodontic treatment must be performed beforehand.
