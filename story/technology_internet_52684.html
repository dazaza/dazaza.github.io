A website traffic statistics and analysis system with very high domestic evaluations

by flyfisher on 2008-03-11 14:13:55

Currently, for website traffic statistics in China, there are quite a few free options available, such as 51la, itsun, 51yes, cnzz, Baidu Statistics, Yahoo Statistics, and Google Analytics. However, the common feedback is that these tools have limited functionality, unstable statistical systems, inaccurate results, inability to analyze historical data, too many unknown IP addresses, and inability to track dwell time, among other issues. Since they are free, we can't expect too much. Among the paid domestic options, there are 99click, wodoo, and JYC. The 99click tool provides only overall data with very few functional items, no detailed data, and you can only view data from half an hour ago (it uses a method of synchronizing the database every half hour, with separate collection and customer query databases). The wodoo statistics website is currently not functioning properly; even though it’s a paid service, its features are similar to those of free tools.

By chance, I came across the "2005 China Online Advertising Industry Competition and Development Trend Research Report" [http://www.reportking.cn/Shop/Class19/200612/12169.html], which mentions the JYC statistics tool from www.adjyc.com. After testing it, the functions are indeed impressive. It can not only truly reflect real-time online visitor conditions but also provide a clear understanding of where visitors come from and where they go. Moreover, there are no unknown IP addresses (apparently, they monitor unknown IPs daily and update them as needed to valid IP address information). In short, there are many functional items, and I haven't had time to fully understand all the intricacies yet. It will take some time to digest everything.

You can register for free at http://stat.adjyc.com:89, though it seems like it's not the latest version.

Their homepage is http://www.adjyc.com. If you're interested, you can check out the latest information there.
