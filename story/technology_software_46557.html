QQ Member Account Defense - Free Use of Kingsoft Antivirus

by yinyin on 2008-02-02 09:02:37

Dear QQ members, your distinguished status highlights your uniqueness, but it also brings the threat of viruses and malware. In order to protect you from the threat of theft and allow you to enjoy the prestigious membership services with peace of mind, as the New Year approaches, Tencent has joined hands with Kingsoft to present a security gift for QQ members: Kingsoft Antivirus QQ Member Privilege Edition.

During the event period, QQ members can obtain different durations of privilege usage based on their VIP level.

Event Rules:

1. This event is open to all QQ member users.

2. During the event period, newly registered member users must use the service for 10 days before they can apply for the free Kingsoft Antivirus service.

3. During the event period, except for newly registered member users, all member users can immediately obtain the Kingsoft Antivirus free privilege pass.

4. All QQ member users can participate in the event by logging in and activating their member numbers. Each member can only claim the Kingsoft Antivirus free privilege pass once based on their corresponding level.

5. Usage Method: Download the Kingsoft Antivirus QQ Member Privilege Edition, install it, and then log in with the activated member number to use it.

6. Members of different levels can enjoy different durations of free service:

- Members of levels 1-4: 3 months

- Members of levels 5-6: 6 months

7. The validity period of the Kingsoft Antivirus privilege pass is until January 31, 2009 (including January 31). Please enter it before this date; it will expire afterward.

8. This event marks the beginning of the QQ member series of security privileges, with more exciting activities to be launched soon!

9. For any questions about the event, please contact Tencent customer service at 0755-83765566. The interpretation rights of this event belong to Kingsoft Antivirus and Tencent Company.

10. For inquiries regarding the Kingsoft Antivirus QQ Member Privilege Edition product and pass usage, please contact Kingsoft customer service at 010-82331816.
