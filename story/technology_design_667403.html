What to eat when menstrual flow is light

by wnqvck38 on 2012-02-16 18:54:45

If a woman's menstruation ends in two or three days each time, and during the menstrual period, less than a pack of sanitary napkins is used, and there is very little blood on each napkin, it indicates that the menstrual flow is too light. So, what should women with light menstrual flow eat?

1. For those with deficiency of essence and blood: 30g of Astragalus, 15g of Polygonatum, 12g of Cornus officinalis, 12g of Morinda officinalis, and 9g of Angelica sinensis. Grind these herbs into powder and make honey pills. Take 2-3 times a day, 3g each time.

2. If you have blood stasis, follow this method: 30g of Rhizoma zedoariae, 15g of Atractylodes lancea, and 30g of red dates. Boil them together, divide the decoction into two days, take it twice a day, 50ml each time.

3. For those with insufficient essence and blood and blood stasis: 60g of Motherwort, 30g of red dates, and 10 eggs. Cook them together and drink the soup, and eat the red dates and eggs.

Causes of light menstrual flow:

1. Women who excessively lose weight or diet may experience light menstrual flow.

2. Excessive mental tension and anxiety can lead to irregular menstruation.

3. Irregular daily life, lack of warmth during the menstrual period, or eating cold food during the menstrual period can cause excessive contraction of blood vessels in the pelvis, resulting in light menstrual flow, or even amenorrhea.
