Solidify in the air

by qinqnkei on 2011-07-13 11:41:02

12 Father's Day blessing messages, remember to send one to express your feelings.

1. A father's love is as towering as a green mountain; as pure as snow; as warm as the sun; and as vast as the sea! Dad, Happy Father's Day! Forever your loving daughter!

2. I grew up in your love, now I am grown up and need to repay you with more love, my most beloved - Dad.

3. I ask for a gentle breeze to pray to heaven to bless your father with health and happiness! His hard work has enabled who you are today, and also allows me to feel the beauty of this world because of you.

4. I am doing well far away. In my inner thoughts, my appearance may change, but my love and longing for you will always remain the same! Wish Dad happiness every day!

5. Your selfless contribution has given me a brilliant life lesson. Although the years have burned your youth, it only makes it more enduring and clear. Care and encouragement will accompany me through the stormy journey of life. Dad, happy holidays!

6. The youthful years, the unfinished journey, was you who led me bravely through life; your regretless care, your uncomplaining true love, how much can I give back to you? Wish father eternal happiness!

7. It was you who raised me, giving me strong bones and a robust physique; it was you who educated me, giving me a pure soul and beautiful dreams... I sincerely thank you - Father!

8. Look up and it's spring, look down and it's autumn, may all happiness follow you. Perhaps you receive countless blessings, but a child's blessing is unique. Happy Father's Day!

9. No matter how many towering mountains cannot outline your greatness; no matter how many extraordinary years cannot depict the weathering on your face, Dad, thank you for everything you've done for me.

10. A song that started singing from childhood and never gets old: straw shoes are boats, Dad is the sail, accompanying me on my journey.

11. Father, I love you! Ten thousand thoughts solidify in the air, the wind carries them to you, bringing my blessings, my thoughts, and my ten thousand blessings. Thinking of you is my happiness, I respect you, my father!

12. Your blood flows in my veins, your mark is deeply imprinted on my character, your wisdom continues in my thoughts, all of which I will never forget. Dad, happy holidays!
