The Criminal

Around 23:00 on August 10, the public security authorities arrested Li RenGui at the QiPu Hotel on Station Front Road in Hefei City. Subsequently, they found items used by Li RenGui during his crime, including a camera and a laptop computer. Faced with the evidence, Li RenGui had no choice but to confess to the rape. However, as the case investigation deepened, the police discovered that Li RenGui's atrocities went far beyond this.

Yang Lianwen, Reporter

Two Applicants Raped

On August 10, 2010, Li RenGui saw a post on a Hefei forum job section where a female college graduate from a certain university named Xiao Hua was looking for work. He added her on QQ and told her his company was hiring, asking her to come for an interview. Afterward, he raped Xiao Hua and took nude photos and videos of her. After the incident, Li RenGui even asked Xiao Hua to burn the evidence. When Xiao Hua left, Li RenGui stuffed 200 yuan into her bag. Later, Xiao Hua reported the incident to the public security authorities accompanied by a close friend.

After working for a while, due to low wages, Li RenGui resigned and ventured into the clothing business. Thereafter, with his sharp mind, he expanded his business significantly, becoming a distributor for some famous brand down jackets. However, just as his career was going smoothly, his marriage came to an end due to emotional incompatibility with his wife. After the divorce, he moved from his hometown to Hefei and rented a house in Zhejiang Business Trade City on Station Front Road in the XinZhan District of Hefei (the site of the rape case) as his business premises. Subsequently, Li RenGui began hiring employees to expand his clothing business scope. Meanwhile, in his early thirties, while seeking excitement, his soul gradually became severely twisted.

Abandoning Teaching for Business as a Boss

The court ruled that defendant Li RenGui violated women's wills, using violence and other means to forcibly rape Xiao Hua and Xiao Hong respectively, constituting the crime of rape. Li RenGui raped two women, committing three rapes, which could be punished more severely at the court's discretion. During the rape process, Li RenGui forcibly took nude photos of the two victims and filmed the act of rape, which is uncommon in rape cases. Therefore, the court legally imposed a heavier punishment, sentencing Li RenGui to eight years in prison and confiscating one camera, one laptop computer, and one USB drive.

Case Replay

Upon investigation, it was found that Li RenGui's confession during the investigation phase matched Xiao Hong's account of being raped in terms of time, location, and method of rape. As for Xiao Hong's explanation for not reporting the incident at the time, it aligns with the complex mindset of a girl who has been victimized. Moreover, on her first day of work, after meeting boss Li RenGui only three times, Li RenGui immediately had sexual relations with her twice, taking nude photos and videos. Li RenGui claimed in court that it was voluntary on Xiao Hong's part, which does not match the objective facts and goes against common sense. Afterward, Xiao Hong rejected Li RenGui's offer of two days' salary and did not go to the company to work, indicating her unwillingness subjectively. The above evidence proves that Li RenGui violated women's wills, forcing Xiao Hong to have sexual relations twice, sufficient to constitute the crime of rape.

Highly Anticipated Social Case

In the hiring process, Li RenGui, a clothing merchant in Hefei, raped two female applicants and forcibly took nude photos and filmed the rape process. Yesterday afternoon, the Yaohai District People's Court of Hefei publicly announced the verdict against defendant Li RenGui, sentencing him to eight years in prison. After the verdict, the presiding judge exclusively revealed to our newspaper how Li RenGui, a successful businessman, degenerated into a rapist.

It is understood that in court, Li RenGui admitted no objection to raping Xiao Hua but argued that raping Xiao Hong was consensual. The defense attorney further raised doubts about the evidence and facts accusing Li RenGui of raping Xiao Hong, suggesting they should not be recognized.

Still Arguing in Court

Li RenGui grew up in a rural area of Jinhu County in northern Jiangsu Province. His parents were honest farmers, and the family situation was relatively poor. To change his circumstances, Li RenGui studied diligently from a young age and later entered a normal college for his undergraduate studies. After graduation, he became a teacher at a middle school in his hometown.

On December 2, due to the case involving the privacy of the parties involved, the Yaohai District People's Court of Hefei conducted a non-public trial.

At the end of July and early August this year, Li RenGui saw a job-seeking post by a young woman named Xiao Hong on a Hefei website. He called Xiao Hong and invited her for an interview, subsequently hiring her. On August 7, Xiao Hong's first day at the company, she was raped twice by Li RenGui, who also forcibly took nude photos and videos of her. Then Li RenGui took Xiao Hong to the bathroom to burn the evidence. Afterwards, Xiao Hong did not report the incident to the police in a timely manner due to fear of lacking evidence. It wasn't until she saw related media reports later that she mustered the courage to report the case to the public security authorities.
