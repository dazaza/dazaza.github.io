How did we end up together?? I don't remember.

by xjnp2615 on 2011-05-27 14:44:09

Now, this blog is a tribute to the "pig" before today~ I have never thought of writing something about the pig because she has always been around me. There was nothing dramatic between us. We didn't form a gang to rob and plunder, nor did we have any estrangement, counter-espionage or stealing each other's boyfriend. In short, it was peaceful and tranquil. How close were we? The teachers and classmates would confuse who is who between us... By the way, our characters are different. Pig is an outgoing type while I am an introvert; Our appearances are different. Pig has a small face with big eyes, while I have a big face with small eyes, and Pig wears glasses; Our heights are different. I am 3 centimeters taller than Pig, hahaha~~~; Our figures are different. Pig is plump while I am bony.

Our favorite ice creams are the red bean flavored ones from Ice Factory and the torches with crispy coating. After class and before evening self-study, we would sneak to the small grocery store at the school gate, buy a pile of snacks that cost 50 cents or 1 yuan, the most should be plum candies, followed by spicy ones, then all sorts of things that look appetizing. If it's delicious, we buy two bags, one for each of us. If not, we buy one bag to share. Then we must go to the ice cream shop in front of the cinema to buy ice cream. That was really a great place~~ There was a huge cold storage there, it seemed like an ice cream wholesale point, with all kinds of flavors. We tried this and that, and at the peak times, we could eat 4 or 5 sticks each, I don't know how we managed to eat them back then... But the most bought ones were still the red bean flavored ones from Ice Factory~~ If the snacks were not enough, we would even run to the small store near the primary school, search for food among the children around 1 meter tall, and see their envious and admiring eyes staring at the snacks in our bags, which made us feel so good~~ Now, the couple who runs the small store near the second school still remember us, hehe~ Then, there are many places to buy things but few places to eat. It's not suitable in the classroom, and outside it's windy and sunny, and wherever we go, there are people coming and going looking at us. Finally, we found a quiet place, which was the corner of the staircase on the second floor of the cinema. It was warm in winter and cool in summer, and no one passed by. At that time, we didn't think of carving some words there, um, because we are both well-behaved children... We both abide by laws and regulations, we don't throw garbage on the ground, we hold it in our hands until we get home, and we firmly throw it into the trash can.

Speaking of the torches, there is something a little embarrassing. After class, we basically played basketball with boys. Pig has a talent for ball games, she has a good sense of touch, plays basketball and volleyball very well. I just have a height advantage and was infected by Pig, so I fell in love with basketball. Playing basketball is the second favorite sport after shopping. Back to the main topic~ When the boys play a full court game or don't let us play, or when we are tired, we usually want to eat an ice cream to cool down. But the small grocery store in the canteen of Mengzhong does not accept cash, so we robbed Blackie classmate, or it can also be regarded as Blackie's humanitarian aid to us. Then we ran to the canteen to buy three torches, and gave one to Blackie just for show. Gradually, the kind-hearted Blackie's aid behavior became a daily consumption item... I don't remember how long it lasted, at least half a year... Now thinking about it, I think we were quite shameless back then... The boarding students' meal expenses were eaten by us. But Blackie never said anything... Bow to Blackie~

My zodiac sign and Pig's zodiac sign match rate is 90%, isn't that one of the factors?? Pig has the best character among my friends, she is virtuous and understanding~~~ My character is as strange and capricious as Pig is gentle and sensible. My mother, after saying, if you buy sheep, you should buy SW... How profitable, then looked at me angrily and said, if SW was my child, how obedient and diligent, look at you~~!!

I and Pig only had one quarrel, and it seems to be my fault... And it seems that I threw a tantrum... I forgot what it was specifically. Me and Pig had a cold war~~~ for several days... Then Pig sneaked up to me quietly, nudged me a bit, and we were good again.

Pig is gentle and considerate. One afternoon, during the last chemistry class, Teacher Haishan was mumbling above, I had a headache and felt nauseous, I didn't know what happened, so I slipped out through the back door along the last row of desks... When I got home, I felt so nauseous, as a rising Scorpio, I threw up everything, even water, seeing anything made me feel nauseous, and it seemed that I had a fever tendency. Finally, I covered myself with a quilt, brewed some orange juice, and thought, why am I so pitiful, feeling so bad and no one cares, people outside the window on the playground are jumping around happily, and I am here alone getting sick. As soon as class was over, Pig came to see me, and bought two ice creams, I remembered they were orange flavored frozen ones, I forgot the specific name. I felt better after eating the ice cream, then I went out with Pig.

Also, one summer, Pig accidentally scalded her calf with a pot, leaving a 5 cm scar, she couldn't see this or that, and stayed at home for several days. When I went to see her, she said these days were boring, so she picked a few pretty colored yarns to knit something, a glove with five fingers. Then I saw Pig rummaging in her room and taking out something like a glove with five fingers. The length ratio of the five fingers, even if the order of human five fingers was changed, needed to be extended a bit to fit properly, the shape of the five fingers was twisted like patients with arthritis, and there was no wrist... Finally, in order not to hurt Pig who was seriously injured, I used the method of comparative argumentation and magnification of advantages, successfully comforting Pig who was seriously ill. The original words were like this, No.1: Scorpio - injury index: 100, oh~~~ yours is much better than mine, I can only knit those without fingers, you are so good. The color, online naming, matches really well eh~~~

Me and Pig skipped classes together, chatted online together, played large games together, peeked at the Mongolian kids through the back window of my house after interval exercise together, ate delicious knife-cut noodles at the small noodle shop next to the vocational education center together, and wandered all over Xilinhot together... In the three years of high school life, having Pig stopped me from becoming a weird and secluded girl, but I also brought Pig a bit of anger... Well, just ramble to here. When I started writing, tears were rolling in my eyes, I don't know why, Pig told me not to lose face... Now I have too much emotional fluctuation in a short period of time, I need to control it... Dear Pig~~~ don't come over, go do your big thing peacefully~~~~
