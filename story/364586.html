Gansu Convention and Exhibition

Correspondent Ma Jun - Source of manuscript: The provincial leaders inaugurated the Gansu Exhibition Center Puma Runway in a ribbon-cutting ceremony. News report: On June 24th, Yanchangbao Centertan on the northern bank of the Yellow River was adorned with blooming flowers and fluttering flags as the opening ceremony of the Gansu Exhibition Center was held here with great solemnity. This marked the achievement of phase results for the Gansu Exhibition Center building complex project and also signified the official unveiling of the main venue for the 16th Lanzhou Investment Trade Fair (Lan洽会).

Leaders at the provincial and municipal levels, including Lu Hao, Chen Xuehen, Liu Weiping, Feng Jian Shen, Jiang Xin Zhi, Lu Wu Cheng, Li Pei Wen, Shi Jing, and Yuan Zhan Ting, attended the opening ceremony.

At 9:18 AM, amidst the sound of salutes, the Provincial Party Secretary and Chairman of the Standing Committee of the Provincial People's Congress, Lu Hao, announced the official opening of the Gansu Exhibition Center. Standing Committee Member of the Provincial Party Committee and Standing Deputy Governor Feng Jian Shen, along with the Second Secretary and Mayor of Lanzhou, Yuan Zhan Ting, respectively delivered speeches representing the Provincial Party Committee, Provincial Government, Municipal Party Committee, and Municipal Government.

Subsequently, the attending leaders and guests jointly visited the newly inaugurated Gansu Exhibition Center. Representatives from relevant provincial and municipal departments and over 60 construction units involved in the Gansu Exhibition Center building complex project participated in the opening ceremony.

On the same day, Lu Hao unveiled the theme sculpture of the exhibition square.

Supplementary Information:

The theme sculpture of the exhibition square was created by renowned sculptor He E from our province, with "Yellow River, Loess" as the creative inspiration. The main body of the sculpture is 8 meters high and 6 meters wide. The work is heavy, grand, majestic, and beautiful, blending masculine strength with feminine grace and fluidity. It highlights the surging momentum of the great river and the majesty of the plateau loess, showcasing the profound cultural heritage of the Longyuan land and reflecting the vitality of the ancient and modern Silk Roads. Correspondent Ma Jun.
