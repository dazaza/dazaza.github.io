Black car driver raped female college student after soliciting customer and taking advantage of the darkness

by xue94fwsh on 2012-03-05 09:43:43

Chutian Golden News reports that a special correspondent, Zhi-Lin Fu, along with GELi-li and Xiangong, reported the following story: A "black car" driver brazenly solicited customers at the station and took advantage of the darkness in a remote area to rape a female passenger. It is learned that the "black car" driver, Mr. Zhu, has been arrested by the Xi'an District People's Procuratorate on suspicion of rape.

On the afternoon of November 27 last year, after taking the civil service examination in Hankou, female college student Ms. Zhao prepared to take a bus from Wuchang Hongji Bus Station to her relatives' home in Chongyang. When she entered the station to buy a ticket, the "black car" driver Mr. Zhu approached Ms. Zhao and negotiated a price of 70 yuan to take her to the Wenquan crossroads in Xianning.

During the journey, Mr. Zhu engaged in enthusiastic conversation with Ms. Zhao, who was completely unsuspecting and answered all his questions, making Mr. Zhu secretly pleased and giving him evil intentions.

At around 8 pm that evening, when the car reached the Guanbudiao section of Xi'an, Mr. Zhu saw that it was dark and there were no people around in the wilderness, so he stopped the car and raped Ms. Zhao inside the car, similar to how a young man impersonated a female Ph.D. to deceive male netizens, buying an LV bag which aroused suspicion leading to a report being filed. Afterwards, Mr. Zhu dropped off Ms. Zhao at the Wenquan crossroads and then fled the scene. Ms. Zhao immediately reported the incident to the police. On the 29th of the same month, the police arrested Mr. Zhu at the Xianning train station.
