6. Honeymoon, mainly traveling to Hong Kong, Macao, Singapore, Malaysia, Thailand, Yunnan, Hainan, average cost per person at 8,000 yuan, estimated at 8,000 x 2 = 160,000 yuan.

7. From dating to deciding to get married, including going out to eat, buying gifts, entertainment, travel, giving festival gifts to the girlfriend's parents, etc., averaging 1,000 yuan per month, over 2 years, estimated at 1,000 x 12 x 2 = 240,000 yuan.

In summary, total costs amount to 4.8 + 0.5 + 0.3 + 1 + 0.16 + 0.24 = 7 million yuan.

With a family property of 2 million yuan and an annual income of 500,000 yuan for the man, (7 - 2) / 0.5 = 10 years. The final conclusion is: the man spends all his family's fortune + works without eating or drinking for 10 years = the cost of marrying a Nanjing wife with upper-middle conditions.
