Thirty-Six Stratagems of Love

Life is like a song, and love is like its melody. A beautiful love is a special gift bestowed upon us by fate. Everyone yearns for a sincere, pure, simple, and transparent love—free from impurities and temptations.

Love is like a melody; sometimes it's soft and enchanting, other times joyful and blissful, yet at other moments it brings the foreboding of storms or the mingling of joy and sorrow. What is love? It's the yet-to-be-composed chapter between you, me, and him. Whether this chapter is beautiful or sorrowful will be written by ourselves. Each of us is an expert in composing the lyrics and tunes of the love melody, creating different rhythms...

**First Tune: Deceptive Cover-Up**

In love, deception is the most intolerable act. Men and women caught in the whirlpool of love cannot bear each other's lies and deliberate concealments. When there are attempts to cover up with lies, a rift begins to form. Don't think your lies won't be uncovered; after one lie, you'll need more to cover the previous ones. How many lies can withstand the test of time? This deceptive tune is not recommended for anyone to compose.

**Second Tune: Diverting Resources**

The original meaning of "Diverting Resources" is to attack Wei to save Zhao. In the context of love, don't try to fill a gap in your relationship while neglecting to protect what you already have. This allows opportunists to take advantage. When you're constantly diverting resources, you may find yourself unable to extricate yourself, making this tune unsuitable.

**Third Tune: Borrowed Knife Murder (Underhanded Tactics)**

This is one of the sharpest tunes among the thirty-six. It applies to the third party, commonly known as the "other woman." The interloper often uses underhanded tactics such as pretending to be intimate with the partner in front of the other person, leading to arguments and even breakups. Lovers should beware of this tune.

**Fourth Tune: Rest and Regain Strength**

This elegant tune is favored by those who defend their love well. Instead of losing control and creating a scene when faced with interference, they conserve their strength and prepare for a long-term strategy. This high-class and graceful tune is recommended for lovers.

**Fifth Tune: Plunder Amidst Chaos**

When love is fragile, it can encounter tragic melodies where outsiders take advantage of the situation. Here, what's lost isn't material wealth but the emotional foundation of love. Without steadfastness, love can be easily taken over by others. This sad tune is not suggested.

**Sixth Tune: Feint East, Attack West**

Even the sweetest love can face fierce storms. To defend, you must confuse the opponent before attacking. This tune suggests using unexpected moves that catch the other off guard.

**Seventh Tune: Making Trouble Out of Nothing**

Reason is essential in everything. In love, irrational suspicions without logic can destabilize the relationship. Such baseless accusations can make the relationship shaky. Use with caution!

**Eighth Tune: Secret Seduction**

External temptation can disrupt even the sweetest love. Someone might intervene, splitting a couple apart. Engaging in secret affairs is strongly discouraged.

**Ninth Tune: Watching From Afar**

People who enjoy this symphony prefer a detached approach to love. Although love doesn't require vows, if one partner faces difficulties and the other only watches from afar without offering help, the relationship is bound to end quickly.

**Tenth Tune: Hidden Knives Behind Smiles**

This unpredictable tune keeps people on edge. When facing rivals, if they use deceitful tactics like hidden knives behind smiles, it becomes hard to defend against. But when encountered, one must face it head-on.

**Eleventh Tune: Suffering Together**

True love requires mutual support. If difficulties arise, both should stand together and create a beautiful, inspiring melody.

**Twelfth Tune: Taking Advantage Easily**

This lazy tune involves stealing someone else's cherished love, which is highly discouraged. It also harms friendships deeply.

**Thirteenth Tune: Alert to Every Move**

Without thorough planning, things can backfire. Acting hastily due to small disturbances can scare away your loved one.

**Fourteenth Tune: Rebirth After Loss**

After a breakup, some people rebuild themselves completely, emerging anew. This isn't a sign of insincerity but rather a necessary step toward moving forward.

**Fifteenth Tune: Misdirection**

A complex and unpredictable tune, often used by third parties to split couples apart.

**Sixteenth Tune: Playing Hard to Get**

This advanced tactic involves maintaining a certain distance to spark curiosity. It's a powerful tool in winning over rivals.

**Seventeenth Tune: Insight Through Exchange**

By sharing your thoughts, you can gain deeper insights into your partner's strategies, improving your ability to protect your love.

**Eighteenth Tune: Catching the Leader First**

Facing challenges head-on is effective. Just as striking a snake at its vital point, targeting the leader can neutralize threats.

**Nineteenth Tune: Addressing the Root Cause**

To solve problems thoroughly, one must address the root cause openly and honestly.

**Twentieth Tune: Taking Advantage of Confusion**

This reckless tune involves exploiting confusion in relationships, leading to cracks and conflicts.

**Twenty-First Tune: Transformation**

Like a butterfly emerging from a cocoon, true love evolves beautifully over time.

**Twenty-Second Tune: Keeping Issues Private**

Issues within a relationship should be handled privately to avoid public embarrassment.

**Twenty-Third Tune: Strategic Alliances**

Building alliances can help eliminate close rivals effectively.

**Twenty-Fourth Tune: Deception**

Using deception can confuse others, but ultimately it leads to self-destruction.

**Twenty-Fifth Tune: Substitution**

Confusion arises when distinguishing between truth and falsehood becomes difficult.

**Twenty-Sixth Tune: Indirect Criticism**

Criticism through innuendo can harm the relationship instead of resolving issues.

**Twenty-Seventh Tune: Feigned Innocence**

A gentle tune suggesting mutual tolerance and understanding in love.

**Twenty-Eighth Tune: Strategic Retreat**

Retreating strategically can help gather information and plan effectively.

**Twenty-Ninth Tune: Step-by-Step Progress**

Carefully building trust and affection step by step ensures stability.

**Thirtieth Tune: Turning Guest Into Host**

A subtle yet dangerous tune where a third party tries to take over.

**Thirty-First Tune: Beauty Strategy**

Maintaining beauty and charm can enhance the longevity of love.

**Thirty-Second Tune: Empty City Strategy**

Using calm and confidence to deter rivals can be highly effective.

**Thirty-Third Tune: Counterintelligence**

Creating doubt can protect your relationship from external threats.

**Thirty-Fourth Tune: Suffering to Gain Sympathy**

Demonstrating suffering can evoke sympathy, but only if the other still cares.

**Thirty-Fifth Tune: Chain Strategies**

Using multiple sweet and romantic gestures to keep the love strong and vibrant.

**Thirty-Sixth Tune: Letting Go**

If love has truly ended, it's better to let go and seek new happiness. Walk forward to find your real happiness.

Let go and search for your true happiness!
