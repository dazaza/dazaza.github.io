The Collagen Wife's Anti-Aging Maintenance Rules

by gyfhtt8asd on 2011-12-13 16:02:52

After getting married and taking care of her husband and children, a happy wife doesn't seem overwhelmed. She appears in public with glowing skin that seems to radiate light! How do happy wives take care of themselves daily? And how can they maintain a balance between their husbands, mothers-in-law, and children? What are the small, happy moments in the life of a contented wife? Let us share with you the pink-glowing happiness of several blissful wives!

### Always Remember to Express Love

In the past, I couldn’t cook at all; my only specialty was instant noodles. But now, because of pregnancy, I started making my own dishes. My husband likes simple, grilled steak without spices, so sometimes I’ll cook it for him. I believe the most important thing in marriage is to always stay in an environment of "love." Many people say that after being married for a long time, feelings fade, but the feeling of love doesn't fade—it evolves into a family relationship. It still requires mutual effort and respect for each other, as well as constantly expressing love to last.

#### Chloe's Happiness Wisdom:

- **Be Sweet to Your Mother-in-Law**

The most important thing is not to offend your mother-in-law verbally. Always say nice things and agree with her. Just be sweet with your words.

- **Don’t Let Arguments Last Overnight**

There will definitely be one person who comes to ask if you want something to eat or wants to buy a DVD. This is a sign of "Hey, I don’t want to argue anymore." If not, there will be some affection before going to bed.

- **Honesty is Key When Choosing a Husband**

You can tell if someone is genuine by observing how he interacts with his friends and how he talks about them. Is he consistent inside and out? Does he exaggerate himself excessively?

- **Love Should Be on Your Lips**

Couples should pay attention to each other’s image. Just because you're married doesn't mean you can be slovenly at home all day. We intentionally dress up formally for a date once a week, and we also express our love every day so that the other person knows.

### Love Each Other's Family as Your Own

I think the definition of happiness and joy is simply doing what you need to do every day well. This includes taking care of your appearance, looking beautiful every day, and then facing others. People who interact with you will also feel happy and content. My husband loves my family very much and takes good care of my parents. I am also very grateful to my mother-in-law and appreciate the care from my husband's family. I think this is how couples should treat each other—valuing and caring for each other's families. This kind of love is very important.
