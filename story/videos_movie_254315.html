Introduction to the plot of "The Rumored Seven Princesses": This TV series is essentially a family story set in a kingdom of daughters. While the overall atmosphere unfolds humorously, the scriptwriter uses real-life stories to allow the audience to experience laughter and tears alongside the characters.
