Professor Qi Lanfen from the Faculty of Education gave us a report on early childhood science education, which was very enlightening. Modern international competition centers on the competition of science and technology. Modern education, especially modern science education, is the means to cultivate modern producers. The entire science education system includes education from kindergarten to university-level science stages. It is essential to carry out scientific enlightenment education from an early age to lay a solid foundation for nurturing workers with scientific literacy and specialized personnel in science and technology. During the teachers' training session, the kindergarten teachers realized that small research projects stem from teaching practices, and that conducting research within teaching practice is grassroots research that everyone can participate in. It requires teachers to be adept at identifying issues from minute educational phenomena, capture research content, conduct joint research as a group within the teaching and research team, and then share and exchange findings. The fruits of the research can be immediately applied to teaching and education. After the training, our school's Educational Research Office received 10 applications for small-scale research projects, all of which originated from teaching practices. To promote the routine implementation of comprehensive practical activities, after returning from external learning, Li Hongli collaborated with experts and teachers who had also attended the external training in the city to jointly develop a comprehensive practical activity lesson plan titled "How Policemen Solve Cases." This lesson aims to cultivate students' inquiry abilities and归纳reasoning skills.

On the afternoon of November 3rd, our school specially invited Mr. Liu Hengzhong to deliver a special lecture titled "The Path to Teacher Professional Happiness." Teachers from Liuhé Primary School and Dajiao Primary School joined us in listening to the report, and Deputy Director Chen Yanhua of the Dongbao District Education Bureau attended and delivered an important speech.

Education is not merely about learning knowledge from textbooks. In a broad sense, any activity that enhances people's knowledge and skills and influences their moral character is considered education. Senior high schools send a large number of college entrance exam candidates, but most universities are oversaturated with admissions, and many college students complain that "graduation equals unemployment." Currently, the professional settings and the number of students recruited by many universities in our country are not very scientific. There is a misconception that continuously expanding enrollment numbers and improving hardware facilities equates to being a good university, without adequately considering future employment rates for students. Therefore, I believe that higher education in our country has already begun to show signs of overabundance, leading to many adverse issues such as unemployment and psychological problems. Popularizing education should focus on effectiveness rather than speed! Conversely, primary education, as the foundation for nurturing students, deserves more attention. Good psychological qualities should be cultivated from a young age, and various excellent talents should be discovered and nurtured early on. Furthermore, many parents now encourage their children to develop in multiple areas, which reflects a lack of confidence in higher education. Many parents believe that attending university does not necessarily make it easier to find a good job compared to those who did not attend university; instead, attending a vocational middle school and learning a skill might make it easier to secure a good job. Given these reasons, I suggest extending the twelve years of compulsory education to include kindergartens, elementary schools, and junior high schools. This way, the quality of our citizens can be improved and exercised from a young age, preparing them more fully for future higher education!
