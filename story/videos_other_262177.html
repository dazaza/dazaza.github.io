A few days ago, the price of 24-karat gold jewelry reached as high as 312 yuan per gram. Compared to the same period last year, the current gold price is much higher, so overall sales are not as good as in previous years. Additionally, the sales of 24-karat gold figurines are not as strong as before, as consumers still prefer products with moderate investment value. Among these, gold bar sales outperform figurines because the price of gold bars is currently cheaper by dozens of yuan per gram in processing fees. This was stated by a staff member from the Kunbai Grand gold and jewelry counter.

Gold Jewelry Sells Better Than Platinum Jewelry      During interviews conducted by Beijing Men Left Women Right 3-9 Yuan Mini Supermarket Franchise, some jewelry brand merchants reported an abnormal phenomenon this year: gold jewelry sales significantly exceeded those of platinum jewelry. This year, platinum jewelry in their counters has not been as popular as gold jewelry, whereas in previous years, platinum jewelry dominated the market. Currently, gold jewelry accounts for two-thirds of their sales, while platinum jewelry and others account for only one-third. The cause, according to the staff, is mainly related to the large fluctuations in platinum prices and the need for investors to hedge risks. Last year, 990 platinum maintained a price of 698 yuan per gram, but now it has dropped to over 400 yuan per gram. During price fluctuations, platinum's rise and fall often occur in increments of tens of yuan per gram, making its value preservation ability seemingly inferior to that of gold. Therefore, consumers may be more cautious when selecting platinum jewelry.

Currently, multiple brands' 24-karat gold jewelry prices have broken through 300 yuan, according to information gathered from the Kunming jewelry market, which has been influenced by the continuous rise in international gold prices. The price has reached 309 yuan per gram, and the price of gold bars has increased to 289 yuan per gram. According to industry insiders, the price of 24-karat gold jewelry was between 260 yuan and 270 yuan per gram in July and August. Since September, the price of gold jewelry has almost been adjusted every two to three days, continuously rising by 1 to 3 yuan intervals, reaching the range of 280 yuan to 294 yuan per gram. Currently, the price of 24-karat gold jewelry in Kunming has risen above 300 yuan, which is quite rare in the past two years.
