Forever seated ticket

by ygndyg5d4 on 2012-02-23 20:56:44

Forever seated tickets - life is really interesting: if you only accept the best, you will often get the best. There was a person who traveled frequently and often could not buy reserved tickets. But whether it was a long or short journey, no matter how crowded the train was, he always found a seat. His method was actually very simple, just patiently searching through each carriage one by one. Although this method does not seem particularly clever, it is very effective. Each time, he was prepared to walk from the first carriage to the last, but every time he didn't have to go all the way to the end before finding an empty seat. He said this was because there were not many passengers like him who would persistently look for seats. Often, in the carriage where he sat, there were still several seats available, while in other carriages' aisles and junctions, people were packed. He said that most passengers are easily deceived by the seemingly crowded appearance of one or two carriages and do not carefully consider the numerous opportunities for seating hidden in the flow of passengers getting on and off at dozens of stops. Even if they think about it, they lack the patience to search. A small standing space easily satisfies most people; some even think it's not worth the trouble to move around with their luggage for one or two seats. They also worry that if they can't find a seat, they might not even have a good place to stand. Just like some people in life who are content with the status quo, fear failure, and lack ambition, these passengers who don't actively look for seats usually stand in their initial spot from boarding until they get off.

Reminder: Confidence, persistence, foresight, and diligence in practice will give you a forever seated ticket for the journey of life.
