Price Fluctuation: Since the end of the year, the availability of Bora has been very tight. Currently, the discount on Bora has slightly loosened, but the supply remains very limited. Consumers can also consider pre-ordering.

Model Review: The exterior of the 2011 Bora is almost identical to its older model. The only change in the front is the removal of the chrome trim around the logo, making the front face appear more three-dimensional and grand. It is worth noting that despite little change in the overall interior layout, whether it's the Golf 6-like steering wheel, silver brushed decoration strip, or multiple chrome decorations, they all give a fresh feeling. Additionally, based on improvements in the interior and configuration, there have been comprehensive upgrades in power performance, handling, driving pleasure, and riding comfort.
