Watch the lady at the gas station door while putting on gloves.

by baiyasin2s on 2012-02-27 20:39:22

She was slender, a little pale, perhaps due to nervousness, and kept looking around restlessly. Her hair had loose curls that were casually tied up at the back of her head. The curls swayed with her head movements, giving an impression of laziness and dizziness. Her face didn't leave much of an impression, but she seemed sensitive. Like everyone else, she was holding the day's entertainment tabloid - which was given out for free at the gas station.

I walked over and waved at her: "Are you Miss Gao Xia? You're right on time!" She gave me a forced smile and nodded slightly. I quickly added: "I'm Consultant No. 2481, you can call me Teacher Tong like online, or my name: Tong Li! Don't misunderstand, I have no other intentions. I just wanted to meet you. Usually, if my patient's condition improves significantly under my help and they agree, I would like to meet them as friends. But this violates the rules, so thank you for keeping it confidential!"

"It's nothing, I haven't let anyone know about the counseling thing anyway. But I don't feel like my condition has improved at all, I keep thinking about it repeatedly," Gao Xia looked at me in confusion.

"Oh, your next treatment plan is ready, you no longer need my help. You could say you've graduated from my care. Next time you log in, you'll be informed directly, without needing to find me. So we can consider this a farewell!" As I said this, I handed her the shopping bag I was holding, "It's cold, put it on quickly!"

She took it, opened it, and looked at me even more confusedly: "You're giving me a hat and scarf because you want to give me one? I was wondering why you didn't use a phone, this old-fashioned way of meeting has long been outdated!" Still, she took out the hat and scarf from the shopping bag and put them on. They were carefully selected by me, with matching patterns and simple styles, very easy for the general public to accept.

I smiled at her, "We who deal with psychology inevitably have some abnormal places in our minds. I have a strong aversion to electronic products like phones, yet I work for a website, how ironic is that! Let's not stand here, come on, there's a park over there. Let's take a walk and chat inside!"

Gao Xia was my most recent patient on the psychological counseling website, a relatively typical case of mental disorder, mainly suicidal tendencies. Recently, her responses to projective tests became slower and more precise. My mentor's opinion was that her depression had become severe enough to require medication, exceeding the scope of general psychological counseling, and she should be directly transferred to the psychiatric department. I completely agreed with my mentor's opinion, except for one small detail: I believed that even if she was transferred to the psychiatric department, her suicide would be unavoidable!

In the bleak winter park, with sparse tree shadows and few people around, we aimlessly wandered. I hoped to go deeper into the park. We talked about the entertainment tabloid in her hand, which reported on a series of murders committed by a deranged killer in the city. Gao Xia didn't believe it because the major newspapers had already debunked the rumors saying it didn't happen. I thought the reports from the major newspapers were even less reliable! We sat down on a bench deep in the woods and focused on discussing this issue.

"Look," Gao Xia said, "the victims are all young women, living alone, with stable jobs and economies, introverted personalities. This group of people, like me, are actually lower-risk victims. We live regular lives, going back and forth between home and work, rarely going anywhere else, paying the most attention to self-protection. It's hard to kill one of us, let alone five in three years!"

I patiently guided her: "Look, I work in psychology. I think the newspaper's description is quite consistent with the modus operandi of a sexually deviant serial killer!" "There were no signs of sexual assault!" Gao Xia pointed at the newspaper arguing, "but there were obvious signs of sexual preference disorders!" Gao Xia's eyes widened as she listened. I continued analyzing: "All the victims were women of the same age group, indicating the killer's sexual orientation is towards women; all the cases happened in winter, the killer likely experienced a strong stimulus in winter that triggered the impulse to commit the crime; all the victims were wearing the same hats and scarves when they were killed, indicating this strong stimulus was related to this set of scarves; the killer's sexual preference is this set of scarves, not a sexual attraction to the victims."

Gao Xia stared at me blankly for a while before asking, "Then analyze what kind of person the killer might be?"

"Male, if female, then a lesbian. Age between 25 and 35, introverted personality, living alone, probably doesn't have a high level of education, but has some knowledge of anatomy, possibly from a single-parent family or parents who don't get along. Since he needs to track the victims' routines for a long time, his job is probably unstable or irregular."

"How do you know he has some knowledge of anatomy?"

"Because the killer used sharp blades to puncture the left carotid artery of the victims, with skilled techniques and definite effects."

"The paper didn't say anything about puncturing the artery!"

"It didn't say, but the police asked our mentor about it. These are internal messages, these are our mentor's estimates of the killer."

"Ah, so you knew!"

"Do you want to see the victim's set of scarves?"

Gao Xia turned her face to look at me, her eyes shining with excitement. To make this lady, who was trying every possible way to commit suicide, interested in something else made me happy too.

"Bend down!"

Gao Xia looked at the scarf she was currently wearing, suddenly raised her head, and the terror in her eyes appeared simultaneously with the knife blade inserted into her left carotid artery, and disappeared together. Then, quiet and bloody heat.

I am not a sexual deviant, I just want to liberate those women who always ask me for help in committing suicide. I am completely opposite to the description of the killer, I just disguised myself psychologically.
