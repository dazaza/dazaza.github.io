The constellation woman who is most worried about not being able to get married_小白菜_Sina Blog

by qaswr769 on 2011-05-27 20:29:10

They are beautiful, virtuous, and independent, and someone is pursuing them. So do you really think they don't worry about being left unmarried? They also worry about becoming a "leftover woman" as time goes by, regardless of their excellent appearances. This feeling of worrying about being left unmarried is like hiding under the eaves on a rainy day but still worrying about getting wet. Sometimes it makes people around them feel helpless... Then, after all, which zodiac sign's worries make you most confused?

First Place: Cancer

Representative Practice: Internet user Blue Phantom - The worrying Cancer woman~

Cancer is a zodiac sign that always holds an optimistic attitude towards life, and this can also be extended to the fear of no one marrying Cancer women. They easily lack self-confidence and are afraid of becoming a "hard case". Moreover, getting married and having children is the highest pursuit for homemaking type women, and Cancer women's thinking tends in this direction. Therefore, they look forward to getting married and are afraid of not being married. In their youth, Cancer women may be a bit picky, but when it's time to settle down and start a family, even if Cancer women are talented, have outstanding jobs, live independently, and have their own territory, they still feel that they must get married to be proper. Thus, worries come. In the tragic imagination of Cancer women, there are these scenarios: What if one day I fall and get injured at home and no one sends me to the hospital because I live alone? What if everyone around me has a place to belong, and I can't find anyone to chat and confide in? When beloved parents show concern, how can I make them feel at ease... These speculations make Cancer women's psychology fragile, and their fear becomes deeper. But people around them all want to say that you are actually good, and you should be more positive!

Second Place: Gemini

Representative Theory: Internet user Impatient - Unexplained panic~ Feeling that no one can be relied on but still wanting to depend on someone

Geminis, who are usually confident, may worry about marriage issues because they want someone to accompany them long-term. Even the liveliest gathering has its end, and everyone needs a place to return to; even the strangest and most interesting single life has a boring day. How can sociable Geminis endure their lives heading towards loneliness? Geminis have a very practical and simple understanding of marriage: someone to talk to when bored, someone to lean on when feeling down, someone to unconditionally share troubles with, etc. All of this cannot be obtained in a single life, and friends will have their own lives. Their lives need someone to participate in them at a deeper level, so despite thinking that no one can be completely relied upon, Geminis still want to find someone to depend on. Of course, Gemini women won't easily panic. The psychology of "worrying about being left unmarried" mostly appears during the helpless period when Gemini women are older and have no one to accompany them. At that time, they become urgent and melancholic.

Third Place: Leo

Representative Theory: Internet user Fragrance at the Bottom of the Cup - Regarding face-saving issues, the older the age of marriage, the easier it is to generate feelings of insecurity.

Leo women rank up here not because they are afraid of comparing themselves with others in this aspect. After all, the saying "a girl should marry when she grows up" is there, and most people don't think long-term singlehood is good. Leo women don't think being single is bad. Independent personalities make them feel that being a single woman is quite good, without family constraints, doing what they want freely, buying what they want freely, and feeling very pleased. However, when their close girlfriends all have families, Leo women, upon reflecting on their own single lives, feel potential problems. Some special moments when there's no one around always seem a little sad. Others have care and protection that they don't have. Especially during holidays, people around them are paired up sweetly, while they might remain single due to being too high-profile. But when compared with others or when they compare themselves, they often feel inferior. The older they get, the more they feel their dignity is hard to maintain, and they can't help but suspect that they have no market value.
