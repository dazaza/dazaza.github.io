Theoretical Expansion of the Project "Blind Array"

by chandadada on 2012-02-18 20:23:02

The trainees will experience a period of confusion under such serious communication barrier, but they can usually adjust in time. The team member who holds the rope will untie it and inform everyone about the relevant information. At this moment, someone from Beijing Outward Bound Company will organize everyone (not necessarily the captain, sometimes the person holding the rope will become the organizing center) to brainstorm. Some people may participate passively because the group's plan doesn't match with their own, some people are eager to express their ideas without following ONE VOICE (one voice), which interferes with everyone's thinking process. It is very difficult for people to reach a unified solution through effective communication under extreme conditions, and it is also hard for organizers to make decisions. Often, one plan is executed halfway when another plan emerges, leading to chaos. Sometimes, problems occur during the inspection phase when the square has already been pulled well, but ultimately, issues can usually be solved through the PLAN, DO, CHECK, ACTION (plan, operation, check, implementation) process.

Project Monitoring:

1. Observe whether the trainees are actively participating;

2. Observe when the trainees start communicating orderly;

3. Observe small teams within the communication among trainees;

4. Who stands out first to organize the team to work;

5. Can the students proposing ideas persuade others to follow his opinion;

6. Whether they can listen to other people's thoughts;

7. Throughout the process, who remains silent and works;

8. During the process, are there any individuals outside the team, why does this state occur;
