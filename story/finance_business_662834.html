Shenzhen Valentine's Day Events 2012: An Overview

Tianhong Mall: From February 10th to 14th, winter clothing clearance at 2 to 4 times discount, selected spring outfits new arrivals at 80% off. Customers who spend a certain amount will receive a love certificate as a gift.

Yitian Holiday Plaza: From February 10th to 14th, customers who accumulate spending up to a specified amount on the same day will have the chance to win rings, chocolates, and other sweet gifts.

KK MALL: On February 14th, female customers visiting KK MALL will be gifted a romantic rose. Those who spend a certain amount will also receive movie tickets.

Taiyang Department Store: From February 10th to 14th, "In the Name of Love" Valentine’s Day mega sale. On Valentine's Day, there will be a romantic live violin and saxophone performance. There will also be a special opportunity for patrons to request love confession tunes.

Haiya Department Store: From February 10th to 14th, launch of "Sweet Gifts Witnessing Romantic Moments". Discounts on all spring outfits new arrivals and autumn-winter wear clearance starting from 30% off.

Jockey Club Shenzhen: On Valentine's Day, take a photo with the "Romantic Lover Street" themed backdrop and receive an instant camera photo as a keepsake.

Mao Ye Department Store: On February 14th at 5 PM, participate in a kissing competition for couples at the entrance of Huaqiangbei Mao Ye for a warm prize. Local Shenzhen shopping and discount information.

Rui Ou Department Store: From February 9th to 14th, customers who spend a certain amount can join the "REEL Moment Surprise Gachapon Event" with chances to win a 3-night-4-day Southeast Asia trip for two, a couple’s dinner, movie package tickets, and exquisite gifts.
