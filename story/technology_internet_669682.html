Occasionally, I encounter pure eyes and warm bright smiles, and my heart will feel joy. Occasionally, I remember the boy who appears cold but has a warm smile, and my heart will chuckle. Occasionally, I see slim women, and my heart will envy a little, appreciate them while studying whether the golden ratio is like this. Occasionally, someone tells me directly that I am lazy, and my heart will be hurt a little, then silently give myself a chance to repent. Occasionally, I hear praise from female companions, and my heart will be proud a little but still have some unease. Occasionally, I receive care from people around me, and my heart will be moved a little and also feel at a loss. Occasionally, I receive phone calls or messages from old friends, and my heart feels full of happiness and also senses the deepest attachment in the bottom of my heart. Occasionally, I lose and feel melancholy, which is the signal that my heart needs peace and rest... Occasionally, I encounter what my heart desires, and in the bitter laugh, silently tell the joys and sorrows. My heart will tell me, this is what I want. If it is within my ability, one day, I will realize it for her. Even if it is after a long time, as long as the promise to myself is fulfilled in one's lifetime, it is not too late... Suddenly, I recall a question I asked a friend a long time ago: Why do I live? I think, I live for my heart, not for anything else, just because my heart is still beating... This kind of life is not living alone... Even if the world gives me a bustling garden, my heart builds a quiet little house behind the garden. It may not be beautiful and peaceful enough, but it is the last pure land where I exist... I, Xuefang, exist like this... My hometown is snowing again, heavily! I heard my parents describe it on the phone, so I named it "Listen to Snow"! Dad said it was the biggest snowfall in nearly ten years. The reason for the biggest is that the snow kept falling after the snowfall, so there was a spectacular scene of the whole hometown frozen into a white expanse! This can't help but let me remember the snowy scenes in my childhood. Back then, we would turn the long stools at home upside down, tie them together with ropes, push them to the top of the slope on the road, and sit on them with more than ten people sliding down. That scale was quite spectacular in the memory of our childhood! At that time, it seemed that most families had fire guns. Every time it snowed, I followed my older brother and others carrying fire guns to hunt in the mountains. But, my group of older brothers were terrible hunters with poor gun skills. In my memory, we only hit a wild rabbit and a nearly frozen wild chicken! And that rabbit only got a slight injury on its leg!... These days in Wenzhou are also very cold, but seeing snow is probably as likely as winning the lottery! After being here for so many years, I've only seen snow once, and it was only symbolically floating a little! Compared to that, it might be better not to snow at all! These years wandering outside, I haven't seen snow in my hometown for many years. I miss standing in the yard as a child looking up at the snowflakes falling from the sky, playing and running in the snow, making snowballs, building snowmen, and having snowball fights. The memories of childhood are as pure as snow! Now, if I were to be in the snow again, I might not run in the snow anymore, but I will definitely stroll in the snow, facing the slowly caressing snowflakes, letting the white and divine snowflakes fall on my body and face.
