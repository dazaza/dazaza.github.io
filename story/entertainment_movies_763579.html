Synopsis:

The story takes place in an ordinary small town in Florida where people live their lives day after day in monotony. Matt Lee Whitlock (Denzel Washington), the town's detective, is currently in a cold war with his wife Alex (Eva Mendes), who is also a police detective. One day, Ann Murray (Sanaa Lathan) and her husband Chris (Dean Cain), a former football star paralyzed from a stroke due to an accident, move back to the town. She happens to be Matt's first love from high school.

On the same day that Alex files for divorce, Matt learns that Ann is in the late stages of cancer and can only extend her life by undergoing an expensive specialized treatment in Europe. A year ago, Chris had purchased a $1 million life insurance policy. Perhaps Ann could use it to quickly secure some cash.

Whether out of pity or nostalgia, Matt agrees to Ann's request. She changes the beneficiary of the insurance policy to his name, while he uses his position to take a large sum of black money confiscated from drug trafficking out of the police department. However, on a night not long after, Ann and Chris' apartment is destroyed in a fire. In the rubble, the police find the bodies of a man and a woman, but the money mysteriously disappears...

Matt's wife Alex is responsible for investigating the case, and Matt soon realizes that before long, all the evidence will point to him as the suspect. Now, to avoid being sent to prison, Matt must find the killer before his own wife does. At the same time, he has to figure out a way to regain his wife's trust, hoping she still feels kindly towards him.

Unexpected events keep happening, and it seems Ann did not die in the fire. Everything might be part of a carefully planned conspiracy for revenge. Can Matt manage to preserve his reputation and job? In this race against time, who will ultimately be the winner?

When all the evidence points to you as the murderer, the only thing left to do is race against time to uncover the truth...
