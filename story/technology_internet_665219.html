**Dr. Yang Lijuan Profile:**

Dr. Yang Lijuan, female, Chief Physician, Associate Professor, born in 1963, graduated from the Fourth Military Medical University with a Ph.D. in Medicine. She completed her postdoctoral studies at the University of Pittsburgh, USA, and is listed on the Chinese Famous Doctors website. She has been engaged in clinical and research work in endocrinology and metabolism for 15 years, including the diagnosis and treatment of diabetes, thyroid, adrenal, and pituitary diseases. Her focus is on the prevention, diagnosis, and treatment of diabetes and its complications. She has received funding from the National Natural Science Foundation of China.
