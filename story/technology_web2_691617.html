How to Get Rid of Elephant Legs After Childbirth

by ciaifuke on 2012-03-05 16:49:05

Many women develop "elephant legs" after childbirth, while others become very slim. So, how can you get rid of those pesky elephant legs after giving birth? Thick calves can make skirts or boots look unappealing. How can we achieve more balanced calf lines?

Let's take a look at how to slim down different types of thick calves:

1. Slimming plan for fatty legs:

Judgment criteria: If you pinch your leg and can easily grab a handful of fat, it looks chubby and disproportionate to your actual weight.

Slimming tips: Besides exercise, there is nothing more effective than avoiding high sugar and high oil foods. Eat more low-calorie seafood and vegetables, eat fewer snacks, and do more aerobic exercises such as running, swimming, and cycling to eliminate excess fat from the legs.

Slimming method one: Postpartum leg calisthenics

Start doing postpartum leg calisthenics on the fifth day after delivery until the end of the month. Exercise the leg muscles and improve the return flow of venous blood in the lower extremities. Sit on the ground with both legs stretched out together, back straight, arms extended behind you, fingers spread to support the ground. When inhaling, try to curl your toes up, and when exhaling, try to straighten your toes; then lie on your back, both legs extended slightly apart, arms placed beside the body. When inhaling, bend and stretch the left leg towards the chest with the toes straight; when exhaling, straighten the left leg at a right angle to the upper body with the toes curled up. Alternate between the two legs.

Calisthenics are suitable for mothers who have had normal deliveries. Since new mothers are generally weak, they should exercise according to their own conditions and not rush things. Each set of exercises should last two or three minutes, twice a day in the morning and evening. Especially pay attention to coordinating breathing with movements during exercise. After the full moon, various muscle group exercises can be performed to restore the strength and elasticity of thigh muscles. Appropriate exercises include slow running, leg stretching exercises, swimming, etc.

Slimming method two: Gua Sha leg slimming method

Every night after bathing, apply baby oil or olive oil with lubricating effects on the calves. Use a Gua Sha board (or the back of a wooden comb, a smooth spoon, etc.) to scrape the calf muscles hard from the ankle all the way up to the knee bend, 100 strokes per leg.

Gua Sha should be done after bathing. It's best to drink a little warm water after Gua Sha. If you can also do some air bicycle or scissor leg exercises (lying in bed, lifting both legs, stretching them out, opening them as wide as possible to each side, pausing for a few seconds, then closing them like scissors), if you're not trying to lose weight due to overall obesity, there's no special dietary concern, just eat less greasy and high-sugar foods. If you can also reduce dinner intake, you can slim down faster.

Effect: Generally, after persisting for two weeks to a month, you can clearly see the circumference of the legs decrease by one size.

Plan three: Leg liposuction

To solve the problem of thick calves caused by fat accumulation, you can also consider leg liposuction, although this process may be somewhat painful.

The usual procedure involves making a 0.5 cm incision inside the ankle or at the back of the knee, injecting swelling anesthesia, using ultrasound outside the body to break down and dissolve fat cells, and sucking them out with negative pressure. The surgery time is controlled based on the amount of fat in the calves, usually taking about 1.5 hours for both calves.

Surgery removes fat cells from the body, compared to methods like exercise, dieting, and medication, which can significantly reduce the number of fat cells, so there will be no rebound after surgery.

Cost is approximately 3000-4000 RMB.

2. Slimming plan for varicose veins and "edema" legs:

Judgment criteria: If pressing on the calf muscles results in poor and slow skin rebound, it falls under this type.

Slimming tips: Avoid fast food, vacuum-packed food, pastries, and salty foods. Do not drink carbonated beverages like cola or Sprite, and do not stay in one position for more than an hour. Doing some simple muscle-strengthening exercises can help alleviate swelling.

Plan one: 10-minute quick slimming method

Thick calves are often due to edema. To make your legs look more perfect, massage them quickly 10 minutes before a date. After massaging, allow the body to expel accumulated waste products, then use slimming cream, lift your legs high, rest for a moment, and you can reduce the size by 2-3 cm.

1. Alternate hand massages. Place both palms around the calf area and alternately massage upwards. Do this 10 times.

2. Twist and knead with both hands. Firmly cover the calf muscles with both palms, twist and knead until the entire calf becomes warm.

Plan two: High leg raise method

If you sit for too long, place your legs higher than your heart. Before sleeping, you can rest your legs against the wall, or elevate your legs 20-30 cm while sleeping. Lie on your side, supporting your upper body with your hand. Bend one leg and keep the other straight. Then repeatedly lift the bent leg, 10 times, then turn over and repeat with the other leg.

Plan three: Slapping and massaging method

Slap and massage along the lymph nodes of the leg to make the lymph nodes and blood circulation smoother. Moderate massage can eliminate the heavy feeling and edema in the legs.

Start slapping rhythmically from the ankle towards the back of the knee with both hands. After finishing the right leg, do the same with the left leg.

When you decide to choose this leg slimming method, you should massage at least twice a week, and each massage session should last an hour to achieve the slimming effect. You can do it while watching TV at night.
