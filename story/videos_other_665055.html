Quenching technology is widely used in modern mechanical manufacturing industries. Important parts in machinery, especially steel parts used in automobiles, airplanes, and rockets, are almost all treated with quenching. To meet various technical requirements, different quenching processes have been developed. For example, according to the part being treated, there is overall quenching, local quenching, and surface quenching; according to whether the phase transformation during heating is complete, there is complete quenching and incomplete quenching (for hypoeutectoid steel, this method is also called subcritical quenching); according to the content of the phase transformation during cooling, there is分级quenching, isothermal quenching, and under-speed quenching, etc.
