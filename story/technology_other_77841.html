I. Company and Product Introduction

Guizhou Maotai Winery Group Beer Co., Ltd is located in Huichuan District, Zunyi City, a famous historical and cultural city, and is a wholly-owned subsidiary of Guizhou Maotai Winery Group in China. The company owns world-class modern beer production lines produced by German companies Krones and Steinnik, as well as Italian Sacib Company, realizing computerized management throughout the entire production process from raw material input, saccharification, fermentation, filtration, canning to packaging. Meanwhile, it boasts strong technical capabilities with national-level brewing experts and national beer tasting committee members. In January 2003, the company became the first in the industry to pass the integrated certification of ISO9000, ISO14000, and OHSAS18000 in one go; and in December 2005, it passed the HACCP and QS system certification, achieving systematization, proceduralization, and standardization of internal management.

The company's products include Maotai Beer, Maotai Beer King, Maotai 1098 Beer, Maotai Bifidus Factor Beer, and Plateau Beer. The company has successively won titles such as "National Outstanding Enterprise in the Liquor Industry" and "Trustworthy Unit for Consumers" at provincial, municipal, and district levels, as well as "Contract Honoring and Credit Keeping Unit."

The "Maotai" brand series of beers are made from premium Australian malt, premium imported hop extract, high-quality glutinous rice, and karst mountain spring water with unique recipes and brewing processes, carefully crafted. The beer is clear and transparent with a golden color, pure and refreshing taste, harmonious and soft mouthfeel, prominent hop aroma, white and delicate foam, long-lasting cling to the glass, and distinctive flavor. It has successively won titles such as "China's High-Quality New Beer," "China's Famous Innovative Beer Product," and "Guizhou Province Brand Product." In December 2005, at the Diaoyutai State Guesthouse in Beijing, the Maotai low-alcohol high-concentration (bifidus factor) beer independently developed and innovated by the company passed the expert group appraisal of the China Food Industry Association. The appraisal indicated that this product filled the gap of no low-alcohol high-concentration (bifidus factor) products in China's beer industry. The "Plateau" brand beer is a long-standing favorite among consumers, having been awarded honors such as "National Food Industry Brand Product" and "Guizhou Province Brand Product." The "Plateau" trademark has been rated as "Famous Trademark of Guizhou Province" multiple times.

Maotai Beer, the Maotai of beers —— During the development and reform process, Maotai Beer Company will continue to learn and apply advanced domestic and international management concepts, advanced production processes and technologies, adhere to independent innovation, develop and produce high-quality products, and offer a high-quality lifestyle to a wide range of consumers.
