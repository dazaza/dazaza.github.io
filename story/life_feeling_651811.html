How are you this season?

by lili1 on 2012-01-03 00:36:16

This season, how are you?

Time flies by, that year, that moment, things remain but people have changed!

When the flowers on the other shore bloom, is it destined that we won't meet again?

What is it that stops us from meeting? Is it really because we're all too busy, or does leaving mean that separation and not seeing each other again is inevitable?

When you leave a place, does it mean that the friends who stay here are destined to be forgotten? Are they really no longer in our hearts? Can the memories of what we've shared together really be forgotten completely? Can we really stop thinking about them? Are there really no moments worth remembering?

It's deep autumn now, this season always makes people feel melancholic and lost...

Yesterday, I saw my friend off. I know that this farewell means forever...

Actually, being alone is pretty good!

When you're alone, you're really free, unrestrained, and can do as you please. It's just that after being alone for so long, you don't even know what you truly want anymore. It's not that no one is pursuing me, and it's not that I don't want to love. I'm just not used to living a life with two people, or maybe it's because I haven't met the person who can make my heart beat faster. ---- Perhaps, my stubbornness has destined me to be single for a very long time...

People who have loved me, how are you this season?

Friends who were together, are you still lonely this season?

The person I love, this season, will you remember me?

In this season, who will still remember who? Is it true that no one remembers the playmates we once had?

Real life really makes us feel so helpless. The people I cherished, and those who cherished me, are we all doing well?

Meeting is hard, parting is also hard, the east wind cannot help the hundred flowers bloom!

As long as you're in my heart, it's good!

This season, friend, how are you?
