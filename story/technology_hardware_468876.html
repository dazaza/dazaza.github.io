Female bus driver was insulted by a male passenger for refusing to open the door because the station had not been reached

by zzf0zxyh00 on 2011-08-01 16:31:27

Our reporter Feng Jinsong, correspondents Tao Xunyi and Li Jing - A section of surveillance video from the No. 4 electric bus route yesterday had viewers shaking their heads in disapproval: In the seven-minute recording, a middle-aged man could be seen pointing at and continuously hurling insults at a female driver for five minutes. The reason? He insisted that the driver open the door mid-route despite the bus not yet reaching the station.

The footage showed that on the morning of the 13th at 7:12 AM, Fu Youhua, a female driver with the electric bus company, was driving the No. 4 electric bus from Hankou to Wuchang. When she reached the First Uprising Square, traffic came to a halt due to congestion. At this moment, a man around 40 years old wearing a pink T-shirt approached the driver's cabin and demanded that Fu Youhua open the door.

Fu Youhua explained: "According to regulations, we are still far from the stop, so I cannot open the door." The man retorted: "I will be late for work, open the door quickly!" Fu Youhua advised him to wait a little longer, assuring him that the door would be opened once they reached the station.

At this point, the man became uncontrollably angry: "If it gets blocked all day, you won't open the door?" Seeing that Fu Youhua continued to ignore him, he began hurling various obscenities at her. Nearby, another man wearing glasses joined in and chimed in with the insults.

Hearing the offensive abuse, two female passengers seated at the front tried to intervene, but unexpectedly, the "pink-shirted man" started arguing with the female passengers and shouted at Fu Youhua: "If you weren't a woman, I'd beat you up." By this time, tears welled up in Fu Youhua's eyes.

At 7:19 AM, the No. 4 electric bus finally moved forward. The "pink-shirted man" had been cursing non-stop for five minutes. As he got off the bus, he threatened: "Whenever I see you in the future, I'll curse you again. You're really worthless."

After the "pink-shirted man" left, Fu Youhua could no longer hold back her emotions and burst into tears. The passengers on the bus then condemned the man's lack of morality while consoling Fu Youhua. Through her tears, Fu Youhua restarted the bus.

Yesterday, after watching the video, the management team of the No. 4 Electric Bus decided to reward Fu Youhua 500 yuan for her principled stand against the abusive behavior.
