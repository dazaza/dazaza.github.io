Select the best feeds for diglog's new discoveries

by cacard on 2006-07-22 15:23:42

polymerization feed has been an idea for a long time and has never been introduced. If you aggregate A large number of feeds into A discovery , you will become a hodgepodge of links. In addition, will users feel that they are finished subscribing to these feeds directly and have to go through diglog?

Actually, that's not the case, and we've started to try to do that by selecting a small number of very good ones that relate to diglog topics. The feed is automatically updated in the new discovery section and filtered to the front page by the user through the digg mechanism. On the one hand, it solved the lack of information sources in the early stage of diglog development, and at the same time promoted and publicized excellent Chinese blogs in China.

  You can recommend good feeds to diglog, you can submit your own feeds to us, and if you have a wide range of blog posts, you can create a separate column and create a corresponding feed for us.   After passing the review, the diglog Feed Star will be obtained and published.

please try to provide by the font & have spent managed feed to ensure stability.   Please recommend or submit here. We can't wait to show this off until the great feed arrives.

update

Special attention, our Feed requirements are very strict, should conform to the RSS version specification (pay attention to the date format, time zone adjustment), description (description node part) can not be attached to the end of the text outside the link, Tag should be preferably in the category node section. Even if a very good Feed is in RSS format and the content does not meet the requirements, we will not choose it.
