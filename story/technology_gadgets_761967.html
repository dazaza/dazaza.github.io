2199 yuan Meitu phone: "I have no words to describe most front cameras."

by geekzhang on 2013-05-16 21:36:56

Under the slightly ambiguous purple lighting at the scene of MeituKiss, Cai Wensheng and Wu Xinhong took the stage. They narrated on one hand about the recent developments of Meitu, a desktop and mobile application that suddenly became popular and entered people's field of vision. On the other hand, they launched MeituKiss, a smartphone specifically developed for self-portrait needs.Cai Wensheng summarized three development stages of Meitu. From 2008 to 2013 over five years, the team’s products moved from PC to mobile end, and now stepping into hardware. He believed that the future trend will definitely be the combination of software + hardware + cloud. He thought that the future development of mobile phones would be vertical, and pictures have become the main means of communication on the Internet. Cai Wensheng said this is why Meitu chose to develop a mobile phone.Cai Wensheng also revealed the development status of Meitu products:The CEO of Meitu, Wu Xinhong, is an entrepreneur with legendary experience. He dropped out of high school and started his career in domain name trading around the same time as Cai Wensheng. Later, after meeting Cai Wensheng, he joined the latter's team and was responsible for the product development department. Before developing Meitu, he developed more than 30 products, including video and information websites.After Wu Xinhong took the stage, he said he found that the proportion of users who like selfies among Meitu users is getting higher and higher, rising from 5% in 2008 to 68%. Of course, later Meitu also became a tool biased towards processing selfies. Wu Xinhong and Cai Wensheng share the same view: pictures have become the main means of communication.Since many users like selfies, but Wu Xinhong felt that the current tools haven't met user needs well enough. Wu Xinhong said, for most front cameras, I can't help but criticize them. In terms of hardware, most mobile phones' front cameras have low pixels, no large aperture or super wide angle, resulting in noisy images, lost details, and poor color reproduction.If the front camera cannot handle selfies, can the rear camera do it? He also held a negative attitude. Because of the inconvenience of operation, using the rear camera often leads to focusing errors, human face not being placed within the viewfinder, and inappropriate expressions. He summarized that taking selfies with the rear camera requires god-like skills!Because there are not yet good enough products on the market, we introduce a xxx. In fact, we have heard such reasons many times.So what are the outstanding features of MeituKiss from a product perspective? Wu Xinhong believed that its 4.5-inch screen size makes it convenient for single-handed grip, making it easy for people to take selfies; it has a selfie button on the side, which is relatively convenient to use; moreover, the most important feature is that it has an 8-megapixel front camera.Although from the early promotion, Meitu did not want MeituKiss to become a product competing on hardware, Wu Xinhong still inevitably compared it with the front cameras of iPhone4S and iPhone5 at the scene - the former has 300,000 pixels, and the latter has 1.2 million pixels, far lower than MeituKiss.Wu Xinhong emphasized that the camera of MeituKiss comes from Sony, with excellent performance, such as F/2.2 large aperture, auto focus, 5-layer optical lens, 28mm super wide angle, and a CMOS of 1/3.2 inches in size. Then, he compared this lens with the front camera of iPhone5. At least, the super wide-angle lens can accommodate more people simultaneously, suitable for group shots.The front camera of MeituKiss is also equipped with an ISP (image signal processor) from Fujitsu. Wu Xinhong also compared pictures processed by ISP and those not processed by ISP. In fact, Samsung and Meizu phones also have ISP chips, but not for the front camera.After introducing the ISP, Wu Xinhong raised a question: Will high-definition cameras + ISP chips capture all the flaws on the face? Subsequently, Wu Xinhong began to introduce the automatic beauty technology of MeituKiss, which can combine facial recognition technology to identify parts of a person's eyes, nose, and mouth, and then carry out targeted beautification. And from the PPT at the end of the press conference, the facial recognition technology of MeituKiss may come from Face++ of Tsinghua University.In addition, the front camera of MeituKiss can automatically measure light. If the person is in a darker environment, the software will automatically adjust to at least take the picture. MeituKiss also has real-time beauty preview function. Before taking a selfie, you can find the best effect suitable for yourself, and it can even turn a fat face into a slim face, etc.At the scene, Wu Xinhong compared six groups of self-portraits taken under different environments and with different devices to highlight the competitiveness of MeituKiss.However, how many consumers can a mobile phone that emphasizes selfies attract? It seems that Wu Xinhong himself does not intend to spread the volume from the beginning. He said that MeituKiss will launch a limited purchase of 18,888 units. For a niche market-targeted mobile phone product, the model of MeituKiss indeed has no precedent for success. No wonder Wu Xinhong is so cautious.
