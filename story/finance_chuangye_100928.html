The little-known blood and tears history of how Song Zude made his fortune

by huangxin27 on 2008-11-04 14:00:33

"After graduating from the Mechanical Engineering Department of Shanghai Jiaotong University (u88.cn small enrichment projects), I was assigned to Nanjing Railway Bureau. Our unit was very corrupt and also very large, with many incompetent people as leaders. I couldn't stand it, so I often wrote complaint letters to city leaders and the city discipline inspection committee secretary. One day, our leader called me to his office, and the complaint letter I wrote was on his desk. I knew I was in trouble, never expecting that the city leader would forward my complaint letter to him. In just half a year, I was demoted three times."

"On the first day in Haikou, I couldn't even find a place to sleep. I slept on a stone bench in the park at night and was awakened by a police flashlight. Thinking quickly, I took out a newspaper with my own work and said, 'Hainan Daily has already hired me as a grassroots entrepreneur website, it's just that the housing problem hasn't been resolved yet.'"

"The first time I picked food, I hid behind a utility pole and watched a woman feeding her child cookies. When she left, the woman casually threw the bag with one or two cookies left on the ground. Song Zude rushed up and grabbed the bag. At that moment, the child in the woman's arms turned around and said, 'Shame, shame, shame...'. His face turned red, and he quickly hid back behind the utility pole, stuffing the cookies into his mouth while crying."

"The 'love-seeking incident towards Zhang Yu' reported by entertainment media further solidified his image as a loudmouth."

"In fact, no matter how outrageous Song Zude's words are, many entertainment reporters still 'don't hate him at all.' The entertainment circle is so boring, stars only speak clichés, smiles are fixed patterns, but this Mr. Song, he has material, is funny, gives out red envelopes, and is generous."

"He looks very cute - look at his buck teeth," said Zhang Jie of Grassroots Entrepreneurship Network.

"In Song Zude's self-imagination, he is like Don Quixote fighting windmills, as well as Lu Xun and Li Ao who dare to criticize the secular world."

"In 1968, Song Zude was born in a 'mud hut' in Tuanjie Town, Jingjiang City, Jiangsu Province. In the fourth grade of elementary school, he set a grand wish: to become a poet when he grew up."

"Years later, he was quite satisfied with his title: Poet-Entrepreneur Song Zude. Reports mentioned that his poems 'were carved on bamboo in the campus of Sun Yat-sen University by students.'"

"Song Zude graduated from the Mechanical Engineering Department of Shanghai Jiaotong University in 1992 and was assigned to Nanjing Railway Bureau. He is a 'truth-telling' person. 'Our unit was very corrupt and also very large, with many incompetent people as leaders. I couldn't stand it, so I often wrote complaint letters to city leaders and the city discipline inspection committee secretary. One day, our leader called me to his office, and the complaint letter I wrote was on his desk. I knew I was in trouble, never expecting that the city leader would forward my complaint letter to him here, which means they have close relations. Later, I was demoted several levels, first to the institute, then to the workstation, and was demoted three times in just half a year.'"

"It's said that on the day he resigned, he showed great individuality, 'I, Song Zude, will definitely have a bright future, we'll see!'"

"At the age of 39, Song Zude's first half of life was full of storms and hardships, like a personal struggle legend. In 1993, the trend of going to Hainan was flourishing, he sold his 'broken bicycle' for 60 yuan and boarded the southbound train."

"On the first day in Haikou, I couldn't even find a place to sleep. I slept on a stone bench in the park at night and was awakened by a police flashlight. Thinking quickly, I took out a newspaper with my own work and said, 'Hainan Daily has already hired me as a grassroots entrepreneur website, it's just that the housing problem hasn't been resolved yet.'"

"At that time, you couldn't resign easily, I ran away myself without the unit's approval. The unit found my home, and my father even wanted to go to Hainan to bring me back. I lied to them, saying I had found a good job in Hainan."
