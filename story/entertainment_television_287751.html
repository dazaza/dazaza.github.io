Plot introduction:

It is said that men should marry when they grow up, and women should marry when they grow up. Men, in addition to doing business, their greatest wish is how to marry a good daughter-in-law. If they have a good daughter-in-law, life will be peaceful...

Maodou Dou, an emergency nurse, and Yu Wei, a photographer at a portrait studio, were on a blind date, but met Maodou's first love, Li Ruqiu. The two blind dates ended unhappily.

In the evening, Maodou's younger brother - avant-garde magician Mao Feng brought his girlfriend Qin Susu home for dinner and announced that the two would soon get married. Maodou's couple had no way with their son, and the wedding was held as scheduled. Unexpectedly, the day after the new marriage, the groom announced that he was afraid of marriage and disappeared on his own, and proposed divorce. Su Su helplessly proposed to leave her 7-year-old daughter at Maodou's house for custody as a condition for divorce. A day of marriage ended like this.

Maodou began to go on blind dates one after another. Incredible is that every object introduced by different people is Yu Wei. After accepting their fate, Maodou and Yu Wei can't help but seriously examine each other. Yu Wei frankly stated the purpose of consecutive blind dates. It turns out that he has been pretending to be his buddy who drowned and died, and sent his photos to his grandmother for many years. And now the grandmother, because of her old age, hopes to see his "grandson" find a wife as soon as possible, so Yu Wei wants to satisfy the elderly's wish. Moved by Maodou's kindness, the two gradually developed feelings. Soon it was time to meet both sets of parents. Yu Wei told Maodou in advance about the complicated family situation that he had "two fathers, two mothers, and a sister who lost her father on the second day of her marriage." He said that his father and mother divorced and remarried respectively, and the two mothers were irreconcilable. After much hesitation, Maodou finally summoned up the courage to ask Yu Wei to arrange a meeting between both sets of parents. The scene was predictable, and Maodou was caught in the middle, at a loss what to do.

Finally, Yu Wei and Maodou got married. After the wedding, they returned home, and Yu Wei's sister who lost her father informed them that she would live with Yu Wei and his wife. While complaining, the two had to force a smile and welcome.

Post-marriage life was relatively calm, although Yu Wei's sister Yu Hao, who had an eccentric personality, constantly nitpicked and caused trouble, it all passed smoothly because of Maodou's tolerance and carefree attitude. But the existence of two mothers-in-law really made Maodou feel difficult. Even any link such as the number of times Maodou visited separately and the number of gifts bought could be used as evidence of favoritism.

On one side, the war broke out again and again. On the other side, Maodou's younger brother Mao Feng was preparing to marry a rich woman Long Jin when he was abandoned by Long Jin. He was very depressed because before there was only the situation where he didn't want women and not women didn't want him. In despair, he met the rural girl Pan Meili, calling her an "original ecology" girl. The two quickly got married in everyone's amazement. Soon, Pan Meili's various "original ecology" simple performances before marriage gradually became unacceptable to Mao Feng. His flirtatious nature made him tangled with Long Jin again. However, Pan Meili consistently showed him primitive love.

Yu Wei's stepfather was suddenly found to have cancer, and Yang Yifan, his stepfather's son, rushed back from the island. He was a soldier guarding the island. Faced with the proud Yu Hao, Yang Yifan actually felt a little moved. Because of Yang Yifan's important and busy work, he couldn't stay at home for long. Yu Hao and Maodou and others voluntarily took on the task of taking care of Yang's father. When Yang Yifan returned to the island, he didn't have time to get off the train when the train started moving, and followed the train to the next station. Yang Yifan was not sure that Yu Hao could take the train back alone, so he sent her off again. During the journey, the two had more exchanges, and when they parted, there was a little something special in both of their eyes.

On the other hand, Mao Feng, who was entangled with Long Jin again, believed Long Jin's plan to make money, and encouraged friends and relatives around him to invest in Long Jin's so-called project. Pan Meili, who deeply loved and trusted him, even encouraged her parents and brother-in-law to participate in the investment. When everyone was dreaming of making money, Long Jin disappeared. Only then did Mao Feng realize that he had been fooled by Long Jin again, and he was extremely disheartened. At this time, Pan Meili did not abandon him, telling him that she was willing to start over with him. Mao Feng was deeply moved.

Although Yang's father was in good spirits under everyone's care, the devil was ruthless. Yang's father was critically ill, and he hoped to see his son again. Yang Yifan hurried back, but still failed to see his father again. He only saw his father's will, and the content of the will shocked everyone. Yang's father hoped to be buried with Yang Yifan's mother - Yang Shu's ex-wife after his death. Everyone didn't dare to tell Yu Wei's mother about this matter.

Yang Yifan will soon return to the army. Yu Hao went to see him off again. The train started moving, and Yu Hao still didn't get off the train, but this time she decided to stay herself. She mustered up the courage to confess her feelings to Yang Yifan, saying that she was willing to be the wife of a soldier.

Yu Wei's mother soon learned of Yang's father's last wish. At first, she couldn't accept it, and her mood was extremely bad. As a result, many unintentional actions of Maodou and the children became the targets of her accusations. But under the comfort and tolerance of the children, she finally let go of her heart and fulfilled Yang's father's wish.

Yang Yifan did not want to drag Yu Hao down because he was stationed on the island all year round. But at this time, Yu Hao was exceptionally determined. The two met at the train station because Yang Yifan went to military school. When the train started moving, Yu Hao jumped on the train again. She was determined to marry this soldier.

At Yang Yifan and Yu Hao's wedding, Maodou's two mothers-in-law also reconciled under the encouragement of a group of young people.

The three daughters-in-law are full of confidence facing future life...
