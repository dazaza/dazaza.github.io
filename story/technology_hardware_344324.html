A man's level is determined by what he cares about.

by qingchuyulan on 2010-04-12 22:11:04

This is another article that seems easy to do but is actually difficult, but at least remember the title.

1. Possess Confidence and Elegance

After men reach their twenties, they need to start learning how to carefully manage themselves; this is reflected in one's thoughts and cultivation. Confidence is the most important quality for a man. A confident man is like a seagull fighting in a storm, saying: "Let the storm rage more fiercely!" because he has no fear. A confident man can always influence others, whether they are friends or strangers. To make others have confidence in you, you must first be full of confidence in yourself. A confident man can overcome all difficulties. A man with authority is like a sea, accepting everything from droplets to rivers. Having elegance makes a man gain more favor. Not being limited by what is in front of you allows you to see the whole world, and acquiring others' knowledge can bring you much benefit. As the saying goes, "A prime minister’s belly can hold a boat," and a man with a heart like the sea might not know how many boats his belly could hold! Elegance makes a man look free and unrestrained.

2. Develop the Habit of Reading and Writing

After men reach their twenties, they begin entering society. In the process of interacting with others, speech and cultivation are the most capable of convincing people. A knowledgeable man is certainly someone who often reads, and a wise man is certainly someone who often writes. No matter how busy you are, you should find time to read books and write articles, because doing so can change a man's thoughts and actions. The first thing a man needs to do to change his way of thinking is to read a good book. Reading a book is like making a good friend who can help you walk your own path. The life of reading is the most colorful, and the time of writing is the most inspiring. Men who love reading and writing can definitely cultivate a good mindset. Because the sea of knowledge and wisdom is boundless, but men who love reading and writing can achieve persistent pursuit. Pursuit is a man's thought and action, and an unyielding pursuit constantly encourages men to fight. This fight enables a man to experience the baptism of storms and grow into a towering tree. Reading makes a man calm, and writing makes a man mature.

3. Try to Create Truth, Goodness, and Beauty in Life

After men reach their twenties, they need to learn how to face life. What is "truth"? Nowadays, men increasingly don't understand it, because modern men are very restless and they don't know what it means to "truly" live. "Truth" means doing what you can do without deceiving yourself or others. "Truth" also means being honest in your actions and dealings. Honest men are the most lovable. "Goodness" naturally refers to kindness. Treating others kindly is treating your own life kindly. Kindness is actually all around us—don't embarrass others, don't mock them, and don't insult them. Sometimes a little bit of kindness can bear good fruit and make your life happier. Philosophers say, "There is no lack of beauty in life, only a lack of eyes that see it." Indeed, life is exactly like that. Don't dwell on your misfortunes, because doing so will only make your life worse. You feel "unlucky" because you cannot face life optimistically. Life is always filled with hope. As long as you often look up and see the sunshine, you can feel warmth. In warmth, optimistically pursue a beautiful life, and you can naturally discover beauty.

4. Make Friends with People Who Have Ideas

After men reach their twenties, they need to start purposefully choosing friends. Social connections are very important, and your circle of friends will have a significant impact on your life. Men should make fewer friends but seek out sincere ones and avoid harmful ones. Sincere friends are those who are helpful to you, and these people are usually quite excellent themselves. Making sincere friends is very beneficial to one's life and career. But finding true sincere friends is not easy because such friends require you to show great sincerity and genuine authenticity.

5. Must Change Bad Habits

After men reach their twenties, they need to change their bad habits. Bad habits are formed, and good habits are also formed. Cultivating good habits means changing bad ones. If a man reaches his twenties and still has various bad habits, that is a very serious problem. These bad habits can hinder personal development, make life less glorious, and make it harder to achieve greater success in one's career. If you still love sleeping late, then the first morning sunlight will never shine on your smile. If you still procrastinate, then you will never sit on the champion's seat. If you still don't take the initiative to study and work, then your future can only be a gray void. I believe no man wants a gray life. All men hope to have a colorful life. Therefore, to make your life colorful, after reaching your twenties, you must change your bad habits.

6. Learn to Endure and Be Tolerant

After men reach their twenties, they need to learn to endure and be tolerant. In society, there are often things you cannot avoid but must accept, which requires your endurance. Enduring others is also being tolerant towards them. A man who can tolerate others appears very generous. Successful men are often those who can endure burdens. Men who can endure loneliness are not lonely. A man's endurance is for better release.

7. Maintain a Good Mindset and Take Care of Your Body

After men reach their twenties, they need to learn to adjust their mindset and take care of their body. The body is the capital of revolution, and the mindset is the banknote detector. To acquire more wealth, a man needs a strong physique. To sustain wealth over the long term, a man needs a positive and optimistic mindset. Pay attention to diet, exercise regularly, and ensure adequate sleep to guarantee a healthy body. Don't worry excessively, work hard, and let your mindset determine everything.

8. Always Keep Your Smile and Let Politeness Become Your Business Card

After men reach their twenties, they need to always keep their smile. Smiling shows that you are a kind person, and everyone likes to interact with kind people. Don't put your troubles on your face, as it will only make others stay away from you. Others judge what kind of person you are by whether you smile when greeting them, and this judgment plays an important role in your impression in people's hearts. People often say, "Details determine success or failure," and these details often reflect whether you are polite. Polite people know how to care for others, and others will give you opportunities to succeed because of your politeness and concern. A man's business card is his smile and politeness, and it is the passport to achieving success in his career.

9. Seek True Love, and You Won't Regret It for Life

After men reach their twenties, they gradually desire love. This is a very normal thing, and you should not suppress this emotion from within. A man is at his wisest and cutest when he falls in love with a woman. When you fall in love with a woman but don't know if she loves you back, you must choose the right time to express your feelings. Women most long for a man who truly loves her.

10. Devote Everything to Your Career

After men reach their twenties, they must strive hard for their careers. The most important thing for a man is his career. A man without a career cannot be considered a true man. A man's career doesn't have to be grandiose, but it must have achievements that make him feel proud. A man with a strong sense of career is also a man with a strong sense of responsibility. A man with a weak sense of responsibility is naturally not satisfied with mediocrity. A man with a strong sense of responsibility is never afraid of difficulties. A man becomes great through his successful career and gains respect and trust from others.

As a man, you must devote everything to your career. To succeed, you must pay a price, and paying a price means giving up some things. If you are unwilling to pay any cost for your career, then you will lose even more, and in the end, you will gain nothing.

11. Love and Career Can Coexist

After men reach their twenties, they should strive for both love and career. People often say that if a man chooses love, he loses his career, and if he chooses his career, he loses love. I, however, do not agree with this. On the contrary, I believe that the more a man cherishes love, the more successful his career will be. Love and career are two different things, and loving greatly does not hinder one from having a successful career. A man nourished by love is surely more mature, charming, and determined to achieve something.

12. Strive to Do Better Than Women

After men reach their twenties, in every aspect, they must strive to do better than women. Even in areas where men cannot surpass women, they should try to do as well as women. This is not to say that men are naturally superior to women, but rather that men should naturally aim to do better than women. Because as long as women work hard, they can easily surpass men. So, men should naturally strive to do better than women. In today's society, women's status is getting higher, and women first have the right to choose their partners. So, if you, as a man, cannot do better than women, why would women choose you?

Women often say, "Women can hold up half the sky," and now women excel in some areas traditionally dominated by men. So, men must not only excel in their own domains but also perform equally well in areas traditionally dominated by women.

13. Help Your Wife Achieve Her Dreams

After men reach their twenties, if you are already married, please make sure to help your wife achieve her dreams. People often say, "Men conquer the world to conquer women, and women conquer men to conquer the world." So, a woman who has conquered you, you must help her conquer the world. A woman's dreams will not disappear just because she lives with a man; instead, they become stronger. This strong desire and dream may not be visible to you, but you must understand it in your heart.

Your wife is the core of your life, and whether your wife lives happily, you must care about. Don't think that once a woman marries, she is there to serve you. Every man's wife cares about her husband, and every man must also learn to care about his wife. And the best way to care about your wife is to care about her dreams and figure out how to help her achieve them.

14. Learn to Be Brave and Shoulder Your Responsibilities

After men reach their twenties, they must learn to be brave enough to shoulder their responsibilities. Filial piety to parents, caring for your spouse, and succeeding in your career are all responsibilities of a man. A man's responsibilities revolve around family and career. These responsibilities are indeed heavy, but as a man, you must take them on. There is no reason other than the fact that you are a man.

Men have the right to cry, but not the right to cry aloud. Men have the right to shed tears, but not the right to cry in front of their children or spouse. Men have the right to give up, but not the right to abandon their responsibilities towards family and career. As a man, you have no choice but to choose to take on the responsibility and use your shoulders to carry it.

15. Never Give Up on Yourself, Believe That You Can Succeed

After men reach their twenties, they must never give up on themselves. The reason others give up on you is that you have already given up on yourself. Believe that as long as you do not give up on yourself, no one will give up on you, or rather, no one can give up on you. Believing in yourself is the source of strength for a man to overcome everything. A man who does not believe in himself has no reason to succeed because one foot has already stepped into the grave of failure.

An advertising slogan says, "Believe in yourself, strength is in your heart." Another slogan says, "I can." Yes, I believe that if every man can believe in himself, you will surely succeed.
