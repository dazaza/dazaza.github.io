The Only Story That Belongs to You

The night is deep and quiet, but my heart has already stirred ripples. Memories always accompany the midnight with heartbreak and pain, quietly landing. I still love you... until the day you no longer love me.

- Preface

I take you back in time to visit our past and see what kind of scene it was; now we've been together for almost half a year. Recent events have made our distance further, causing conflicts! So I want to take you back to look at those happy times and record this long diary! I hope you can slowly and carefully savor some people, some things, some principles, and some things you don't know!

Do you remember when we first met? You asked me to talk, but I didn't agree. It's because I think love is between two people who love each other, but at that time, I didn't love you, nor could I be sure if you really loved me. Also, we hadn't truly understood each other yet. So I refused and just let you be my sister, developing slowly. I know that you hesitated for a long time before agreeing! Maybe you've forgotten, but I still remember vividly. After that, life took a turn, beginning to lean towards our happiness.

Do you remember when you told me very sadly that you would spend Christmas at No. 3 Middle School and asked me to treat you to food, but we hadn't even met, so how could I dare to promise?

Do you remember during the cold winter days, when our hands and feet were icy cold, we warmed each other with words and cared for each other! Even though our bodies were cold, our hearts were warm.

Do you remember when we once discussed boring topics: "hoeing the corn under the noon sun" and "shooting wolves and ghosts"? But I knew all those things, I just wanted to humor you!

Do you remember when you got mad and asked me if my seatmate was male or female? Thinking about your angry face made me want to laugh. However, it was the first time I heard you say you liked me. I was really happy, but I still couldn't be sure because you hadn't seen me.

Do you remember on weekends, when we missed each other, you thought about kissing me and wanted my first kiss? At that time, I was speechless.

Do you remember when I called you "baby" and asked you to listen to me, it felt quite warm!

Do you remember when you said you like eating cookies, jelly, chocolate, and plums? When you're not happy, eating something makes you feel better. I remember all these! I really admire you, if I could live as carefree as you do, it would be great! But I always fail to do so.

You said I'm the best guy you've ever met, but I don't think so. There are many people better than me, you just haven't met them! I don't think I'm as good as you said in any way! It's just that I can see some things clearly!

I remember asking you once if I approached you, would you agree? Your response was "what about him?" That was sincere and also an experiment. I hoped you could understand what real love is, not just playing house as kids! That day, I realized my mistake. I felt guilty inside when you said "I really like you." I really wanted to be good to you forever, protect you, and keep you from being hurt! In the afternoon of that day, your attitude towards me changed a lot, I was very disappointed. It was the first time I felt so uncomfortable - all because of you.

Later, we met twice. Do you remember after you were deceived emotionally, you said you wouldn't date anymore and decided to pursue me sincerely. Then the winter break came, we stopped chatting, gradually drifting apart, but I still remembered the feelings I had for you, which were between love and friendship.

In the spring of 2011, around March or April, we started interacting again. That day, I saw you online, we chatted for a while, and I said I would find you. I knew you didn't believe me, but I still went. I found you, and I saw you spitting continuously, as if you were sick. At that moment, I really wanted to pat your back and comfort you, but I saw someone else beside you, so I thought we had no relationship, it wasn't my place to care! But I couldn't bear seeing you like that, so I just went online myself. After finishing, I sent you home, we swayed along the way, and you rode my electric scooter. You said no, and also mentioned that my personality is similar to Wu Tao's. I didn't mind! Later, we continued like before, but some things had slowly started to change.

My feelings for you grew deeper and deeper, until the day you gave me that box, I was deeply moved - a title, a ring, a gift. It made me try to fall in love with you, recording all the kindness you showed me. On April 6th, I proposed to start dating, but let you think about it overnight. This one night wasn't meant to excite you, but to consider whether you have the confidence to face all kinds of difficulties with me in the future, and whether you can stay committed to this love together with me!

April 7th was the day I ended my 17 years of bachelorhood, the first day of my first love! It was also a turning point in my life. It was also the first day we were together.

After that, our days together were ordinary but not dull. Remember when you didn't believe that I loved you, I showed it through actions by saying "I love you"; remember when you said you like eating chocolate, so I brought you chocolate; remember the first time I admitted my mistake, fearing you might leave; remember when I stole a kiss while we were playing together; remember when we kissed and could hear each other's heartbeats; remember every time I waited for you after school, I could wait endlessly without wanting to leave, hoping you could see me, not letting you feel disappointed, even if it took a long time, I would wait for you;

Do you remember the first time you made me angry, I cried so badly, had no mood to do anything else, my heart really hurt, and I went to sleep very late. But the next day, I forced myself to smile despite the heavy pressure in my heart, because I didn't want my emotions to affect you, because I trusted you at that time, believing you could handle those things well. I believed you loved me...

So I didn't tell you! I accepted and endured on my own. Later, we wandered aimlessly every day! Until your high school entrance exam, we contacted less!

When summer vacation arrived, I was already in senior three. During the first day of supplementary classes, you said you were afraid of leaving me. During that period, we made a vow:

You will only have one never-ending romance with me in your lifetime, and you will only love me in your lifetime. I will never leave you.

You also asked me to promise you one thing:

Let my heart be full of you from now on, and there will be no other girls.

——— I still remember all of these.

Afterwards, due to my senior three supplementary classes, we often complained to each other about missing each other. Then I had a holiday, and you started supplementary classes at Longmen. During your classes, I really missed you, and you didn't contact me either. I really thought you disappeared and left me. Then we made a plan! During the summer, we spent every day together, each getting used to the other's presence. Forever and ever, never separate ~~~~

Then you entered high school life and started high school military training. I missed you so much during that time, and you really changed a lot. Later, when you started classes, you didn't contact me for a whole week, I missed you so much. Finally, Pan Junyu said you were doing sports training, so I had the chance to find you. But your attitude towards me was terrible, and I was very disappointed in you. You said you were very busy, with many "wives" accompanying you. Maybe you no longer needed me, maybe you were tired of me! Then I also gradually tried living without you. But I still couldn't get used to having you in my life. During the Mid-Autumn Festival, I lent my phone to you, hoping you could chat with me. But you rarely replied to me and started ignoring me. I felt so bad, you could be busy and not reply to my messages, not care about me anymore, you didn't have to tell me where you were going, log off yourself without caring about me, making me wait for you for a long time, and got scolded by my mom many times. But for you, I was willing to wait, because you are my wife, right? But sometimes waiting only brought back a cold sentence from you; really, my heart was so cold. Do you know? I suffered through those few days. That day, you texted me saying your mom found out about our relationship and asked us to break up. You said you were reluctant. But I was thinking: if you found someone who could replace me, would you give up on me? But we stayed awake all night! Later, you said you missed me and cried terribly! At that moment, I really hated myself, why didn't I go in and check when I passed No. 1 Middle School? Why wasn't I by your side to wipe away your tears when you cried? But later, your attitude towards me changed again, becoming distant and cold, then close and warm again. I really didn't know what you were thinking. You stopped replying to my messages! On the night of September 20th, I had a dream that was very realistic.

I dreamed that I saw you and Fang Chengyu walking on Beizheng Street, then I ran to find you, you asked me to leave first and find me after you finished eating. At that moment, I asked you, what happened, why is your attitude towards me so cold, are you tired of me? You said maybe you really are tired of me! Then I woke up from the dream. But I cried! On the evening of the 21st, I logged into your account to check, and I saw your flirtatious conversation with others, calling you "wife". At that moment, my heart broke, I silently cried alone in the darkness, so I sent a message to you that night, just wanting to ask clearly. You said we should be more friends and less lovers, how am I supposed to understand your words? I really wanted to think positively, but I didn't want to deceive myself. I didn't mean to call you cheap, why did you have to act this way? Was the Cheng Peng of the past really gone?

Neither of you wanted to hurt him, but you were willing to hurt me the most in an invisible way. You still lack maturity and wisdom in dealing with relationships between men and women. When you say certain things to others, you think it's fun, but people who like you will remember what you said, at least they take it seriously. I really don't want you to have too ambiguous relationships with others, that's all! I really hope you understand; I hope you can calmly think about it. If you don't love others, don't drag their hearts. Let go! Is there still a place for me in your heart? Do you still love me?

But I still love you~ Endless giving is just to win back your unchanging heart for me!

Our future is in your hands, in your words! I hope you can seize it well.

I love you, until the day you no longer love me.

Thank you for accompanying me for such a long time, when love fades, we should part ways! You will slowly let go of everything about me, until you find someone who can completely replace me, blessings to you, sincerely hoping that the ones who once loved can be happy! Better than me, I say the last sentence, we can't go back to the past, nor can we become siblings again! I hide myself, licking my wounds, slowly healing.
