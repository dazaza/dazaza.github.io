Leping Procuratorate: Striding Towards a Mountain-Water Garden Ecological City

by guanglian039 on 2012-02-04 16:48:14

At the beginning of the new year, Shu Daofu, one of "China's Top Ten Outstanding Jiangxi Businessmen" who returned to his hometown to attend the CPPCC meeting of Leping City, enthusiastically visited the city construction projects with a group. Wherever they went, they saw newly developed neighborhoods with towering high-rise buildings and lush green trees, newly completed parks with beautiful flowers and picturesque scenery, and newly constructed wide and straight roads that were well-connected in all directions. The scenes before his eyes made him exclaim: "I haven't been back for more than a year, and Leping has become so beautiful!"

The year 2008, which just passed, was the fastest developing year in the history of Leping City's urban development with the most remarkable results. The city seized the opportunity to create a provincial-level garden city and health city. Following the overall urban development strategy of "administrative northward, commerce eastward, industry southward," it improved urban infrastructure focusing on urban road network, parks, water supply and drainage, garbage disposal, as well as greening and lighting, comprehensively promoted the construction of the northern part of the city and Donghu New Area, and moderately renovated the old urban area. The city arranged more than 20 key engineering projects including new area development, old city renovation, market construction, affordable housing projects, urban roads, and landmark buildings. To broaden the financing channels for urban construction, the city gradually pushed all urban infrastructures that could be put into market operation towards the market, activated various factors of capital, revitalized existing land, and actively promoted vehicle economy and real estate, maximizing the utilization and development of urban resources. Through investment attraction, government investment, social fundraising and other ways, last year's urban construction cumulatively completed an investment of more than 8 billion yuan, an increase of 68% over the previous year.

Clear urban construction ideas and sufficient financial input have quickly brought about significant changes in the appearance of Leping City. The urban road network framework has been fully unfolded, greatly improving the traffic conditions within the city. Tianhu Road, Dongfeng Road, Min Dian Road, Construction Road, Hubin Road and other roads are fully connected, forming a "five vertical six horizontal", well-connected urban road pattern; residential areas such as Phoenix Century Huacheng, Dadi Sunshine, Jin Hui Lan Ting, Ju Long Plaza, etc., which are planned and constructed to high standards, compete in beauty and harmony, making them livable. In the whole year, the real estate development investment completed was 5.4 billion yuan, with a construction area of 550,000 square meters; urban public facilities are becoming increasingly complete. Facilities such as technical investigation, criminal investigation, vehicle inspection, road management, disease control center, cultural museum, etc., have been successively built. The administrative group centered on the people-serving service center and facilities such as long-distance bus transport company, bus company, Donghu affordable housing, cultural center, People's Hospital, etc., are being urgently constructed. While accelerating the construction of new areas, the renovation of the old urban area pays more attention to improving functions and facilitating the masses. The city invested more than 3 million yuan to renovate 15 old streets and alleys with a long history into culturally distinctive fine alleys, renovated 20 public toilets in the urban area, newly built 3 garbage transfer stations, and added 1,000 streetlights. More than 10 new bus stop shelters have been built, some buses have been updated, and the installation of streetlights on urban arterial roads and most alleys has been completed, with the streetlight illumination rate reaching 98%.

In the process of urban planning and construction, the city particularly emphasized creating and protecting the ecological environment. In 2008, more than 50 million yuan was invested, utilizing the natural conditions of being surrounded by mountains and water, to build urban leisure ecological gardens such as Tianhu Park, Donghu Park, Nanhe Park, and plant 4,500 ornamental trees; more than 30 million yuan was invested to plant large trees like camphor and Du Ying along both sides of newly built roads such as Dalian Road, Dongfeng Road, and Jingying Expressway branch lines, making them green scenic lines. Since last year, the built-up area of the city has added 10,000 square meters of green space, increasing the greening rate by 23%. Meanwhile, an ecological park integrating mountains, forests, and grass slopes has completed its design and is about to start construction, which can be completed within the year. Moreover, to strengthen urban environmental protection, the newly built environmental monitoring building and urban garbage disposal site have basically been completed, and the urban sewage treatment plant is accelerating its progress. According to the goal of "building gardens in cities and cities in forests," Leping is vigorously advancing towards a mountain-water garden ecological city.
