Usage and Features     This pump is suitable for conveying various oils with a viscosity below 300C.s.t, such as crude oil, diesel, lubricating oil, animal and plant oils, etc. With copper gears, it can convey low flash point liquids like gasoline and benzene. The medium temperature should not exceed 70℃, and the use temperature of high-temperature resistant oil pumps does not exceed 300℃. The pump itself is equipped with a safety valve, which serves as safety protection in case of overload. There are three types of shaft seals: skeleton oil seal, mechanical seal, and packing seal. Please specify when ordering.
