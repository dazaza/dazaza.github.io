[Anchorpersons who have left CCTV]

by jay on 2006-11-20 17:45:41

When Chen Luyu was at CCTV, she hosted "The Art Garden". However, after working there for a while and as she began to understand and get familiar with television, she found that her current job could not satisfy her. Subsequently, Luyu went abroad for further study, and after returning home, she joined Phoenix TV.

In May 1991, Xu Gehui won the first prize in CCTV's Youth Amateur Host Competition, thus entering the television field. From 1991-1995, she hosted various fixed columns of different types as well as large-scale special programs and galas at CCTV. In March 1996, she joined Hong Kong's Phoenix Chinese Satellite Television and hosted the popular entertainment information program "Gathering at Phoenix".

Seven years ago, the pretty-looking Zhao Lin was the only officially assigned host at CCTV. Her fresh and natural hosting style in the "Life" program was highly praised, and a year later, she was rated as the "Most Popular Economic Program Host by Viewers". At that time, she also politely declined to act in Zhao Baogang's drama "Like Mist, Like Rain, And Like Wind", which later made many people famous, and focused on "life". Zhao Lin said that when she left CCTV, a big platform envied by many people, she initially had pressure, especially after her first leading role in the series "Farewell, Vancouver", where she faced a lot of criticism. But now, she has felt that the road is getting wider and wider, and the stage of life is colorful and varied. She deliberately chooses some roles with great contrast, not only playing innocent and ignorant girls, lively and cute young ladies, but also female SWAT officers, bad women, etc. She wants to avoid the impression of Xiaoxue's weakness in "Farewell, Vancouver".
