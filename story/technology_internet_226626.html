2) Why can't I log in to my free email?

To be able to log in to your free email, please pay attention to the error prompts that appear after logging in.

Generally, there are three situations.

Error situation one:

After logging in, the page prompts "No such member ID"

The reason is that your member ID is incorrect.

Many users confuse their email addresses with their member IDs. SINA's free mail is just one of the services provided to members, and to use this service, you must use your member ID. Please remember your member ID clearly to avoid unnecessary trouble.

In addition, English codes should be in lowercase, we only support lowercase letter codes.

Error situation two:

Prompt "Incorrect member ID or password"

First, please verify your password and pay attention to the case of the password. If the password is indeed wrong, please retrieve the password.

Error situation three:

No prompt, this page repeats:

If the member ID and password are correct when logging in to the mailbox but you still cannot log in and the homepage of the mail keeps showing, preventing you from logging in to the mailbox. This is because the browser you are using has disabled cookies. Sina Mail requires cookie support. As long as cookies are enabled, everything should work fine. The setting method varies depending on your browser version as follows:

First, click "Internet Options" under the "Tools" menu of the browser.

If your browser version is 5.0 or 5.5:

1. After entering Internet Options, first click the Security tab.

2. Then click "Custom Level" at the bottom of the page.

3. After clicking "Custom Level", find cookies in the "Security Settings" that pop up.

Set this item.

4. Then select the "Enable" option below.

5. Then adjust the security level to medium, and click the OK button at the bottom.

Now, you can try logging into your mailbox again, and everything should be normal!

For IE6.0 browsers, after entering Internet Options, please first click "Privacy",

After clicking Privacy, two different screens may appear.

IE6 first situation:

If the screen below appears after clicking Privacy, first click "Advanced".

Click "Advanced":

Select the left option on this page, then click OK and Apply at the bottom of the page.

This indicates that the settings were successful, and you can log in again.

IE6 second situation:

If the screen below appears after clicking "Privacy",

You can directly click the "Default" button at the bottom of the page, then click OK,

Then you should be able to log into your mailbox.
