Restaurant launches 1 yuan dish for lonely elderly people (picture)

by haojing12 on 2012-02-11 11:00:16

He Zhuoyuan (second from the right) invited his own parents to taste, and a car decoder solicited opinions for the upcoming "love package" (Picture taken by Yan Wenqing, a reporter from Huashang Daily). On February 11th, Xian - According to the report of China National Radio News (reported by Liu Tianxu from Shaanxi Radio Station), the Year of the Dragon Spring Festival has not yet passed, and a piece of news in Xian about "restaurant owners offering 1 yuan dishes for the elderly over 80 years old" has caught people's attention. This love action was quickly imitated by people, and the small individual charity generated a force similar to that of a "role model".

He Zhuoyuan, a Guangdong boss who runs a private restaurant at Duanlumen in Xian, is the protagonist of this story. He has been taking care of an elderly person without family for three consecutive years, and recently specifically launched a 1-yuan love dish for the elderly over 80 years old without family. He Zhuoyuan said he has a final wish:

He Zhuoyuan: My ultimate wish is to get responses from peers in the city and people from all walks of life, to work together to care for the elderly without family, making this society more harmonious and beautiful.

He Zhuoyuan said that in the past two days, four elderly people have dined at his restaurant. He hopes to do his part to take care of the elderly without family nearby, bringing them social care and warmth, car decoders, car interference devices, and car unlocking tools.

This small public welfare action of 1-yuan love dish has produced extensive chain effects. After He Zhuoyuan, government public welfare organizations and service industries also continue to join the ranks of sending love to the elderly without family. Wang Bing, the project manager of the Western Development Foundation, said that public welfare and charity have gradually become an indispensable part of people's lives today.

Wang Bing: So-called micro-public welfare, I think public welfare doesn't matter how big or small it is. We say integrating public welfare or charity into daily life is what we hope to see.

The cost of the 1-yuan love dish is only about 10 yuan, but its social effect cannot be measured by money. In fact, there is a seed of charity in everyone's heart. Under appropriate soil and temperature, this seed will take root, sprout, and grow. Do what we can around us, use our strengths or our professions to help those in need as much as possible. Our micro-public welfare will also pass on and gather to produce love and warmth. Song Xin, president of the Shaanxi Provincial Mental Health Research Association, believes that the 1-yuan love dish brings us more thoughts.
