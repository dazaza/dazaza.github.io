Top 10 most expensive scenic spots in China

by geekzhang on 2010-08-20 10:19:01

Compared with foreign countries, tickets for scenic spots in China are generally expensive. For example, Sichuan Jiuzhaigou peak season tickets 220 yuan, Tibet Potala Palace peak season 200 yuan; Some foreign attractions such as Japan's Mount Fuji free, South Korea's Mount Halla free, Russia's Kremlin 150 rubles (about 45 yuan), France's Louvre 8.5 euros (about 85 yuan). Compared with foreign developed countries that tourism relies on chain economic benefits created by sightseeing spots, the tourism economy of many places in our country relies mainly on ticket income, so high ticket is becoming a common phenomenon. Xiaobian has taken stock of the prices of well-known domestic scenic spots, and I recommend to you the top ten scenic spots with the highest tickets, and their tickets are basically more than 200 yuan/person.

The west of Wuyi Mountain is a key area for global biodiversity conservation, with the most complete, most typical and largest subtropical primary forest ecosystem in the same latitude zone of the world. The perfect combination of mountains and water, the organic integration of humanity and nature, the eastern region is renowned for its beautiful scenery such as beautiful waters, strange peaks, secluded Wuyi Mountain and Jade Mountain Valley, and dangerous valleys, as well as its long history and culture and numerous cultural relics. 9. Qufu three holes 185 yuan

Qufu "three holes" scenic spot ticket price will be adjusted to 185 yuan per person, Confucius temple, Confucius house, Confucius Lin ticket prices were raised to 110 yuan, 75 yuan, 50 yuan.

8. Huanglong Scenic Area 200 yuan

Huanglong Scenic Area is located in Songpan County, Aba Tibetan and Qiang Autonomous Prefecture, Sichuan Province. The main scenic area, Huanglong Valley, is located under Xuebao Ding, the main peak of Minshan Mountain. It is named after Huanglong Temple, a famous Buddhist temple. It is a long about 7 kilometers, wide about 300 meters of calcified gorge, here the mountain is like a dragon, also known as "Tibetan dragon Mountain". Huanglong is famous for the "four wonders" of painted pools, snow-capped mountains, canyons and forests, and is the only well-protected plateau wetland in China. The area is also home to many endangered animals, including the giant panda and the Sichuan warty snub-nosed monkey.

Regular ticket: 200 yuan/person

7. Potala Palace 200 yuan

High season (May 1 to October 31 each year) tickets 200 yuan, low season (November 1 to April 30 each year) tickets 100 yuan. The Golden Top ticket is 10 yuan, the jewelry exhibition is 10 yuan, and the Pagoda Hall is 10 yuan. You can visit the snow city scenic spot at the foot of the Potala Palace for free. The peak season should be 1 day in advance to the west gate with ID card to get the booking ticket, the second day according to the booking ticket time (20 minutes in advance) by the main gate (south gate) to buy tickets to visit, limited to 2300 tickets per day.

6. Jiuzhaigou, 220 yuan

Located in Jiuzhaigou County, Aba Tibetan and Qiang Autonomous Prefecture, Sichuan Province, Jiuzhaigou Valley is a branch of the Baihe River upstream of the Baigou River, which is named after the nine Tibetan villages (so it is also called Heyao Jiuzhai). With an elevation of more than 2 kilometers, Jiuzhaigou is covered with primeval forests and 108 lakes, which is known as the "fairy tale world". Jiuzhaigou Valley is a national key scenic spot and has been included in the World Heritage list.

Regular price tickets:

Off-season price 80 yuan/person Peak season price 220 yuan/person

Sightseeing Tickets:

Off-season price 80 yuan/person Peak season price 90 yuan/person

4. Huangshan 230 yuan

Huangshan scenic area ticket prices have been increased many times, during the May Day in 2009, and from 200 yuan to 230 yuan.

3 Zhangjiajie 245 yuan

Zhangjiajie is a prefectural city under the jurisdiction of Hunan Province, located in the middle and upper reaches of the Lishui River in the northwest of Hunan Province, and is one of the most important tourist cities in China. In September 1982, Zhangjiajie became the first national forest park in China. In August 1988, Wulingyuan was included in the second batch of 40 key scenic spots. In 1992, the Wulingyuan Natural Scenic Area, which consists of Zhangjiajie National Forest Park, Suoxi Valley Scenic Area and Tianzi Mountain Scenic Area, was inscribed on the World Natural Heritage List by UNESCO.

Entrance ticket to Wulingyuan Core Scenic spot is 245 yuan/person, insurance is 3 yuan.

2 Hengdian World Studios 255 yuan and 420 yuan

Hengdian World Studios is located in Hengdian Town, Dongyang City, Central Zhejiang Province, China. Since 1996, Hengdian Group has invested a total of 3 billion yuan in the construction of Hengdian World Studios. Now it has built 13 buildings spanning thousands of years, including Guangzhou Street, Hong Kong Street, Ming and Qing Dynasty Palace, King of Qin Palace, Qingming River Map, Dream Valley, Pingyan Cave Mansion, Dazhi Zen Temple and Ming and Qing Dynasty Residence Expo City. Bringing together the characteristics of the North and South of the film and television shooting base and two super large modern studio. It has become the largest film and television shooting base in Asia, and is called "China Hollywood" by the American Hollywood magazine.

Ticket: 255 yuan/person Dream Valley: 160 yuan/person

Joint ticket plus Dream Valley: normal price 360 yuan/person winter price 305 yuan/person

Ticket: normal price 420/ person winter price 360 yuan/person

1 Tibet Yarlung Zangbo River Grand Canyon 270 yuan Package 680 yuan

Yarlung Zangbo Grand Canyon Tourist area is located in the Nyingchi area of the middle and lower reaches of the Yarlung Zangbo River, starting from Bayi Town, through the junction of the Niyang River and Yarlung Zangbo River to the bend of the Grand Canyon, with a total length of more than 100 kilometers. With a total length of 504.6 kilometers and an extreme depth of 6009 meters, Yarlung Zangbo Grand Canyon is the largest canyon in the world. The Yarlung Zangbo Grand Canyon is rich in tourist resources, with famous peaks such as Nanga Bawa and Garabai, the hiking Medoh route, which has been rated as the first of the "Top ten hiking routes in China", and the religious tour of Gara with a long tradition. The Brahmaputra Canyon hike is one of the world's most arduous scientific treks.

Regular ticket 270 yuan/person, package ticket 680 yuan/person (including cruise)
