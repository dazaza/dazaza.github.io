Vice President Commits Suicide by Poison After Suffering from Illness, Leaves Behind a Note Saying to Leave the Money for Children's Education

by zzf000zxye on 2011-06-13 15:32:56

Our staff reporter (Xiao Gang Yang, Intern Zhou Yang) - Vice principal of a primary school in Zhouzhi County, surnamed Wang, who was suffering from various diseases including uremia, heart disease, and nephritis, could not bear the psychological pressure and committed suicide by taking poison at home on the evening of the 21st. Before he left, he left a will: "Keep the funeral simple, leave the money for the children to go to school."

At around 12 o'clock yesterday noon, I came to the deceased's home in Zhuyu Town, Zhouzhi County. When mentioning this matter, the villagers all nodded their heads: "It's very pitiful. He has a pair of underage children. How can his wife take care of everything alone?"

In the middle of the village, a tent was set up at a villager's home for the memorial service. Relatives and friends all came to help. A couplet on the tent reads: "How could you have the heart to leave your children behind and depart? zzfhhxy369_Baidu Space." Walking into the villager's home, every relative looked sad. The deceased was 49 years old and committed suicide by taking poison at home on the evening of the 21st.

A middle-aged woman claimed to be the sister of Mr. Wang's wife. She said that the deceased was the vice principal of a primary school in a nearby village. He had suffered from nephritis earlier and also had heart problems. On the evening of the 21st, Mr. Wang left a will: "Leave the money for the children to go to school."

The sister of Mr. Wang's wife said that in the will, Mr. Wang told his wife: "My departure is a relief for me, and also a relief for the family. Do not waste the money at home. Keep my funeral simple, leave the money, give it to the children for schooling, and make sure they study well..." "He might have thought that the cost of treatment for his illness would require a lot of money. Even if tens of thousands of yuan were spent and he couldn't be cured, it would be better to leave the money for the children's education. This is all due to excessive psychological burden..."

It is understood that Mr. Wang has a son and a daughter. The son is in the third year of high school this year, and the daughter is in the first year of high school. His wife works as a farmer at home. Apart from a few acres of farmland, there is no other income for the family.

Yesterday afternoon, a staff member of the Zhouzhi County Education Bureau said: "We are actively assisting his family in handling his posthumous affairs."
