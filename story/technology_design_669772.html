Hertz Car Rental: No worries for first-time car renters

by juyaqufa004 on 2012-02-20 10:56:59

As a global leading car rental company, Hertz Rent A Car provides car rental services in 146 countries around the world, with more than 8,500 rental outlets and over 40 million bookings per year. Among these, North America, Europe, Australasia, and major countries in Asia are the largest and most mature car rental markets, while China is increasingly becoming one of the fastest-growing emerging car rental markets, especially for airport transfers. Many first-time car renters lack experience and don't know how to choose the right vehicle for their needs. To help these beginners, Hertz has provided some key points to consider when renting a car.

For new drivers, selecting a car based on its intended use can greatly enhance travel comfort. For short-distance suburban trips, it's best to choose small-displacement cars like the Citroën C-Elysée, Peugeot 207, or Chevrolet Sail. These models not only have lower daily rental rates but are also fuel-efficient, easy to maneuver, and economical on gas. If you're going on a self-drive tour, you should consider the storage space; the Hyundai Elantra could be an excellent choice. If you'll be driving on rough roads, a vehicle with off-road capabilities would be preferable. For business activities, luxury cars such as the Buick LaCrosse or Mercedes-Benz C-Class would likely be the top choices.

A relevant official from Hertz introduced that Hertz offers a wide variety of car models that can meet various customer needs for different purposes.

After selecting the appropriate model, the next step for new drivers is signing a lease contract with the rental company. It’s important to understand the overtime and over-mileage fee standards beforehand. Carefully read the renewal terms and the fee standards for exceeding the agreed time to avoid disputes if you cannot return the car on time due to unforeseen circumstances. If you find any unreasonable clauses in the lease contract, promptly negotiate with the rental company for modifications to protect your rights.

When picking up the car, it's crucial for beginners to carefully fill out the inspection form. This process allows both parties to jointly confirm the condition of the vehicle. First, check the exterior of the car for scratches, ensure that all lights are intact, and verify that the locks are functioning properly. Confirm that all necessary documents, such as the vehicle registration certificate and road maintenance fee vouchers, are present. Then, open the hood to inspect the coolant, engine oil, and battery status. Take the car for a test drive to assess its basic condition and familiarize yourself with any special features or functions of the specific model to avoid operational confusion. A relevant official from Hertz stated that most Hertz vehicles are less than a year old and undergo regular inspections and maintenance, so customers can rent with confidence.

Due to the recent increase in car rental demand, Hertz also advises beginners to check online for their preferred car models and prices before booking through multiple channels such as online reservation, telephone reservation, or in-store reservation to secure their desired vehicle.

Hertz Rent A Car continues to provide high-quality service to ensure customers enjoy a comfortable and convenient car rental experience.
