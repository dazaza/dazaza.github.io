Bond Yuan Outward Bound Training Institute is a subsidiary of Guangzhou Bond Yuan Management Consulting Co., Ltd. It specializes in the training of outward bound trainers (ETCT初级, 中级, and 高级 classes are offered on a rolling basis throughout the year) as well as courses to enhance the management capabilities of middle and senior-level corporate managers. The institute is dedicated to becoming the most professional cradle of experiential trainers in China, offering popular open courses on the Chinese Management Training Network from August to October!

The founder of the institute is one of the earliest outward bound training mentors in mainland China and has long provided high-quality services and solutions for enterprises in various fields regarding development strategy, leadership ability, team building, customer service, business management, human resources, and corporate culture.

Over the past twelve years, driven by their passion for the experiential training industry, Bond Yuan has established the Bond Yuan International Outward Bound Training Institute. They have developed a series of innovative, unique, and comprehensive courses that integrate thoughtfulness, education, challenge, and practicality for enhancing the management capabilities of middle and senior-level managers, employee motivation training, and outward bound trainer training. These efforts have cultivated a large number of outstanding outward bound trainers for the industry, playing an immeasurable role in its rapid development.

Since 2009, we have incorporated Confucian thought into experiential training, combining Confucius' ritual and music teaching model with outdoor training, achieving extraordinary training results and enterprise recognition. This method, which focuses on quickly "producing" qualified sales personnel, is a first in China's experiential training field. We believe this approach will flourish in our institute's training, benefiting more enterprises and individuals while promoting the understanding and inheritance of thousands of years of Chinese culture!

Through relentless efforts over the past twelve years, Bond Yuan Outward Bound has built a renowned brand in the experiential training world and earned excellent reputation and brand recognition among clients for the Bond Yuan International Outward Bound Training Institute.

Service Philosophy:

Customer interests come first; customer-centric, with customer satisfaction as the starting point. Help customers solve management problems and learn how to resolve them.

Bond Yuan Mission:

To convey international management concepts and methods, provide effective and substantive solutions, promote implementation and development, assist local enterprises in improving management, and cultivate sustainable core competitive capabilities.
