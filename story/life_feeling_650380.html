New Year's greeting

by liwuyou on 2011-12-31 17:23:56

At this moment, my sky is not dotted with stars but is splendid with fireworks. I hope I can send you and your family the joy I am feeling now, and firmly believe that the feelings between you and us are as beautiful as a field of fireflies.

Family affection, friendship, all kinds of emotions accompany each other; songs, laughter, voices of blessings!

The distance does not mean separation, lack of contact does not mean forgetting, and inability to meet does not mean indifference. All because we live in busy intertwined times, but I still remember you. Happy New Year!

Harmonious relationships among relatives and neighbors, old and new friends being happy, simple and joyful lives, steady and peaceful dealings with others, every day filled with good moods, every year filled with happiness.

In the sound of firecrackers, another year is gone, spring wind brings warmth into Tusu wine, thousands of doors and windows open to the bright sun, always replacing the old peachwood charm with a new one. On this Spring Festival, I wish you a prosperous Year of the Ox!

Wishing you good fortune, prosperity, longevity, everything goes smoothly in your affairs, life gets better step by step, lottery tickets win every time, good luck every day, gold found in the cupboard, and a prosperous New Year!

Everything goes smoothly, life steps higher, winning at cards every time, delicious meals every meal, getting younger, finding gold at home, money growing on the walls, and wishing the whole family a happy New Year!

Life is a landscape, happiness is a state of mind, spring sees peaches, summer sees willows, autumn admires chrysanthemums, winter appreciates plum blossoms. May happiness and peace be with you!

Happy New Year: May you have everything go well, all things Fanta, daily Wahaha, monthly Pepsi, yearly Coca-Cola, a mood like Sprite, forever clear-headed, happy and smooth sailing!

As the New Year approaches, here's a big package of prosperity for you: one, may you have a money tree; two, may you have benefactors; three, may you have good work; four, may you have no worries; five, may your money fill your chest; six, may you always be healthy!

Wishing you swift success in the New Year, riding the gentle spring breeze toward a brilliant future without stopping or slowing down!

May the spring breeze embrace you, your family care for you, love nourish you, the god of wealth remember you, your friends be loyal to you, and I bless you here; may the lucky star always shine upon you!

New and old clothes both keep you warm, housing conditions should not be compared, wide spaces sleep well, narrow ones sleep peacefully too. A harmonious family life, bitter or sweet, is worthwhile. Face troubles wisely, remain optimistic day and night!

The romance of wind bells lies in evoking people's aspirations for a better life, the depth of camel bells stirs up people's dreams of a bright future; text message rings let you know someone still cares about you, even though you're not seen, you're still missed!

Some emotions cannot be expressed in words, some thoughts linger in the heart, some friendships are indescribable, some friends last forever! If a breeze or drizzle passes by you, it’s my greeting reaching you. Be happy every day!

People become familiar through character, respect through virtue, interact through emotion, follow through righteousness, embrace through propriety, trust through honesty, and stay together through love! Wishing you a pleasant life!

The night sky is beautiful because of the stars, dawn colorful because of the sunrise, life wonderful because of friends, and friendship blissful when heartfelt. May those who love you cherish you more, and those you love understand you better. May all the best things in the world belong to you!

Often, the most touching moments come from being remembered by a friend, and often, the happiest moments arise from remembering a friend. Even without an appointment, there's a tacit understanding. Wishing you happiness today!

Mom: I am very healthy because of your care; I am deeply moved because of your sacrifices; I am sad because of your white hair. It's getting cold, so please add more clothes!

A house becomes a home with its occupants, a person becomes richer with their friends. The more windows a house has, the brighter it is. I wish to be your largest and brightest window facing the sun, sending you floral scents in spring and fresh breezes in summer. May happiness be yours every day, and health always by your side!

It's hard to express some emotions, some thoughts linger silently, some friendships are indescribable, and some friends last forever! If a breeze or drizzle brushes past you, it’s my greeting reaching you. Stay happy every day!

To those who take care of me, I am grateful; to those who think of me, I am touched; to those who love me, I am honored. As the weather turns colder, please take care of yourself!

Family: My bond with you runs deep like the sea, brotherhood is unbreakable; teachers' guidance is unforgettable, and friends' companionship lasts long. May all kind-hearted people enjoy family happiness, sibling harmony, harmonious relations with elders, and compatible friendships. Happy New Year!

As the end of the year follows the beginning...the beginning leads to the conclusion of the year. Let us lay down our weary bodies and listen to the silent footsteps of time. Let the joys and tears of the past come to an end here. Happy New Year!

Hope the flowers of spring never fade, the waters of summer flow endlessly, the maples of autumn red with your heart, and the snows of winter pure as you. More than anything, may you, busy as you are, always be happy and content. Happy New Year!

Parents' love is as deep as the sea, brotherly unity as strong as gold; teachers' teachings unforgettable, friends' interactions long-lasting. May all kind-hearted people enjoy family happiness, sibling harmony, harmonious relations with elders, and compatible friendships. Happy New Year!
