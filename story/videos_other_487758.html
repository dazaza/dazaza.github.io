The past shrouded by the passage of time

by wendrwlove on 2011-09-10 18:01:41

(: The End)

The past veiled by the years goes by like a white colt rushing through a gap, casting a touch of sadness. When the splendor turns into sorrow and then into loneliness, don't be disheartened, because we have lingered.

Silent · Xiaohao

In the dilapidated years as fragile as paper, we will never see the color behind it. Every stroke and every trace conceal intense sorrow. Longing is like the mournful cry of a lone goose flying south, hoarse yet lonely. A poison called time has erased you and me who were wandering in dreams, but left us with a memory called sorrow.

Who gently played a tune on the lute, dispersing the separation at the end? Hearing the strings break, breaking the tenderness like water. Watching the leaves dyed crimson against the sky, dancing and scattering. Recalling the breakup, only sighing that we are warming old memories while the old friends have parted. Turning back and yearning longingly, yearning for the kindness hall, the water falls revealing the green lotus under the moon. Remembering the past tour of Xiling, alas, revisiting the old place, people are nowhere to be seen, the water flows in vain...

Gazing out of the window at the misty rain, a faint beauty awaiting its demise, yet leaving traces as brilliant as meteors. In the worldly splendor, all the glitz and glamour ultimately turn into emptiness. Looking back at the bitterness of the fleeting years, wanting to cry but no tears come, only wishing that after the splendor fades, the other shore still waits for you. The past polished by time flies away, lightly brewed into a cup of new tea, from astringency to bitterness...rotten to the core...

The vague and hazy memories, mixed with the potion of loneliness, are packed up and sent to me. Chanting a song of parting, seeking a loyal heart. The leaves fall at this moment, people part at this time. The drizzle carries the wind whispering softly, another desolate season of autumn. Wanting to turn around and forget, only seeing the autumn remains, people are thin and gaunt. Flower vines curl up against the wall, the sunset kisses the grass. Leaving behind a lifetime of fragrance, but no one cares. In this life's decline, what can be seen is only the eerie moonlight and the solitude of music. The rest is nothing but illusion, a brief bloom of the night-blooming cereus, leaving no trace...

Walking shallowly in the sadness before sunset, just waiting for the darkness to come. When the fading sun retreats, the wind turns into tears. When the gray birds fly over the roof, when the fireworks fall and shatter, what we see in our eyes is the beauty of time turning into ashes. Magnificent and charming. When silence and night sing the nightmare, passing over the prosperity, composing all our ways home, looking back at this shore, even if we find the scenery lingering, there is no power to hold it.

Because we have already died in the crevices of the years... decayed.

A cup of wine intoxicates an old friend, a look brings tears. An empty stomach sends messages, not knowing who to ask. The light smoke passes loneliness, the flowing water shows no affection, longing hides in the heart. If we meet again someday, I'll send you a tune, not asking about the dispersal after the tune ends, drinking all the wine in the cup. In the flow of years, dreams return several times, stopping by the winding stream, watching the fireworks scatter and the moon reunites. Please remember, the silent nights of the past, three people playing in the dark alley.

Dispersing the separation, the departure surpasses melancholy beauty. After being scattered to the edges of the sky, longing becomes hard to rely on. The desolate moon carries sorrow, alas, worry reflected on the river walls. Light rain sings a duet, hearing replies but seeing no one. Dreaming of the remaining memories of you and me, only seeing autumn leaves drift away, wanting to catch them, reaching out only to grasp emptiness. When the dream is startled awake, only then realizing that feelings have become weak to the extreme.

Those memories frozen in the depths of the heart have beginnings, but no endings. Yearning is the most beautiful punishment. One can see happiness, but after happiness comes the swirling sorrow. Hair blown by the wind can be combed anew, but how do we console the nostalgia submerged in memories? That year, the cherry blossom lingering in the mortal world has long since withered. But beneath its withered and rotten leaves, a new bud is blooming and dancing gracefully...

When the tragedy of the broken qin at the sound of running water in high mountains repeats, perhaps the distant breathtaking scenery will no longer sob. When the pain awakens the memory once more, please remember not to be sad.

Lonely paths and proud trees, how many new leaves have drifted. High mountains and flocks of geese, leaving behind a trail of sorrow. Playing a tune on the flute accompanies new faces, yearning turns into illness, torrential tears drench dreams unseen.

Only sighing that sorrowful love is hard to forget, worries linger in the heart.

Unable to cry, because tears only prove our weakness....
