Warriors' Battle: "Ten Years, One Sword" Jianghu Kill

Due to Coat Brother's recent rise to fame, he also gained recognition in the classic martial arts web game "Ten Years, One Sword." Equipped with top-tier gear, his performance in Jianghu Kill ranks among the best. Jianghu Kill is the competitive system of "Ten Years, One Sword," essentially a place to determine one's ranking in the Jianghu. Not everyone can participate in Jianghu Kill; only those who have reached level 28 and whose corresponding Lucky Star ranking is within the top 325 can join.

In the martial world, those who stand at the peak of the Forbidden City invariably possess extraordinary skills and make sacrifices unknown to others. As the saying goes, "A general's success comes at the cost of countless lives." Those who dominate at the peak of the Forbidden City are no different, having continuously defeated and slain enemies to reach this point. Only those who disregard any power and surpass everyone can be called the best under heaven! In "Ten Years, One Sword," to enter Jianghu Kill, become the champion, and gain fame across the land, one must first improve their Lucky Star ranking.

What then is Lucky Star? How does one enter the top 325 in Lucky Star rankings? Lucky Star is an original system in "Ten Years, One Sword" where players can divine and select various stars, gaining large amounts of experience, silver coins, or talent books for generals, and even Gold, a currency usually obtainable only through recharging! The Lucky Star ranking represents your position among all players in the server.

Lucky Star ranking determines your opponent. In "Ten Years, One Sword," you can only challenge players ranked within the top 15 of the initiator. When you defeat your opponent, you take their place in the rankings. However, "Challenging stars carries risks, proceed with caution," if your equipment, generals, or martial arts are not superior to others, you will easily be defeated.

Improving oneself is simple—continuously practice martial arts, complete daily quests, or level up offline in the Heroes Gathering. You can recruit powerful generals at the inn, allowing you to slay elite monsters and obtain high-quality equipment, eventually entering the mysterious Seven Stars Ancient Tomb to acquire legendary sets and exceptional generals. In this way, you will surely be invincible in the martial world, and in the Jianghu Kill competition, you will rise to fame like Coat Brother, becoming renowned across the land.
