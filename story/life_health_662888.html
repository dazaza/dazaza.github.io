Post-abortion care is very important. What are the post-abortion care measures?

1. A painless abortion surgery only takes three minutes, is safe and quick. After the surgery, there is no need for hospitalization; you just need to rest for two hours in the hospital. If there are no unexpected situations, you can go home.

2. On the first day after a painless abortion, there may be a low fever, mild and intermittent abdominal pain within 2-3 days after the surgery, which will gradually ease. Within one week after the surgery, there may be slight vaginal bleeding, menstruation may not return for about a month, and menstruation may be heavier for 2-3 months after the surgery. These are all normal phenomena after an abortion. Women should not worry if they experience these symptoms, as they will slowly alleviate and disappear with time and wound healing.

3. After an abortion, pay attention to good rest, strengthen nutrition, and enhance physical resistance. It is best to rest in bed for 2-3 days after a painless abortion. Avoid heavy physical labor and cold water work within half a month. Pay attention to keeping warm, avoid catching a chill, eat foods like chicken, lean pork, eggs, milk, and soy products to supplement protein and enhance physical resistance. Eat more fresh vegetables and fruits to supplement vitamins, stay hydrated, and eat more animal livers to supplement iron.

4. Some foods are not conducive to the recovery of women after an abortion, such as raw and cold foods, especially for women with poor spleen and stomach function. Oily foods like fatty meat and animal oils are not easy to digest. Cold-natured foods like crabs, clams, and snails are not beneficial for women's recovery. After a painless abortion, women's diet should primarily focus on warming tonics. Stimulating foods like chili peppers and vinegar can exacerbate bleeding. Smoking and drinking alcohol must be strictly prohibited, as they cause the most harm to women's bodies.

5. Pay attention to keeping the external genitalia clean. Strictly prohibit sexual intercourse within one month. After an abortion, the uterine orifice has not completely closed, and the endometrium needs time to heal. During this period, special attention should be paid to maintaining cleanliness of the external genitalia. Wash 1-2 times daily with warm water, frequently change sanitary pads, underwear, and wash them regularly. Do not take baths to prevent dirty water from entering the vagina and causing infection. If vaginal bleeding exceeds menstrual flow, lasts too long, or is accompanied by lower abdominal pain, fever, foul-smelling discharge, etc., seek medical attention promptly.

6. One month after a painless abortion, menstruation will resume. At this time, women should preferably go to the hospital for a follow-up examination, such as a vaginal ultrasound, gynecological examination, and routine secretion tests, to confirm the final recovery status and whether complete healing has occurred. Simultaneously, women should note that they must take proper contraceptive measures. Multiple abortions in a short period can cause significant harm to women, so they must take contraception seriously.
