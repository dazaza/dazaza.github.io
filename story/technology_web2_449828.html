Another situation is that large gambling companies often merge with local small gambling companies to enter a certain country to expand their market. There is another situation: after entering a certain market, gambling companies often launch specialized websites targeting the betting habits of gamblers in that country. There is another situation that falls under "illegal relationships" (or no relationship at all), which can be quite frustrating for many gamblers. Their tactics usually involve having a domain name similar to a legitimate gambling company or a homophonic Chinese name to deceive gamblers. Here, I warn all gamblers to be cautious when choosing gambling companies to avoid unnecessary losses.

Under normal circumstances, websites belonging to the same company will offer the same odds because they use the same betting platform, like the several companies under bwin. For us gamblers, we need to pay more attention to companies using the same platform because it significantly increases the betting volume, making the changes in odds more accurately reflect the market betting situation.
