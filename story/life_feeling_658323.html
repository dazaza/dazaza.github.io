After all the twists and turns, after going in circles, I've returned to where I started! - Preface

That year, she was 16 years old, and he was 18! In the end, they couldn't resist fate's arrangement, and they met! Destiny is truly a wondrous thing! Who would have thought that two people who originally passed by each other like floating萍water could spark such fireworks!

How should we tell our story? That year, when she was 16, she was plump like a cute "little pig," and you were so handsome! At 18, you shone wherever you went! The light from your body stung my eyes! In the vast sea of humanity, we just happened to meet!

Why did someone as outstanding as you choose me? Why were you so good to me? Why did you spoil me so much? Back then, I was so ordinary among the crowd, so insignificant that whether or not I existed didn't matter. Yet, you burst into my world with sunshine! You had a very nice name: "Xuan."

Back then, I was like a hedgehog! Because I was betrayed by the person I trusted most, I covered myself in wounds and hurt others endlessly! But you continued to spoil me as always. Faced with you, I didn't promise anything. I merely allowed you to stay by my side. I gave you nothing, and I even got angry at you. But you said nothing, continuing to treat me well and love me as before. But me? Perhaps I was too greedy! I kept testing your limits, but you said nothing! No matter how painful it was, you let me be myself and indulged my caprices! I don't know when I began to get used to your presence, but when I realized it, you were still by my side!

In the same season, flowers bloom and fall. I remember back then, I was like a desperate little beast! Wrapping myself in a cold world, constantly hurting myself and others, my coldness made everyone afraid to approach me. But you stayed with me, giving me warmth and security! I don't know when those extreme emotions slowly faded! Honestly, for one and a half years now, you're still by my side!

Do you remember? Back then, I cried in despair because of something. It was you who told me: "Tears won't solve problems." It was you who made me understand: "No matter what happens, don't give up until the very end." It was you who taught me to "be strong" when I was feeling disheartened. It was you who, when I grew tired of studying, made me think clearly about "what I want and what I'm pursuing." You were absolutely right: "If you don't want to go to school, that's fine, you support me! But is the proud Ya'er in my heart willing? Is the arrogant Xiao Ya only capable of this? Is the proud and free-spirited girl ready to run away after encountering a little setback?"

"No! Xiao Ya isn't that fragile. Give me some time, and I'll forget and return to my strong self!" Your nonchalant tone angered the pride deep within me: "I'll show you that I won't disappoint you!" You smiled faintly: "Alright, don't forget, you said it. I'll wait for your news."

I threw away my phone and distanced myself from the internet! Regarding those people, those harms, I began to forget them. I reined in my restless, playful heart and started studying seriously! Whether it was sketching, dancing, or playing instruments, I didn't miss any of it. The studies I had abandoned were gradually caught up on, and I even became more excellent! I can no longer recall those things. Later, I even forgot if these people ever existed! In the more than a year you accompanied me, I learned a lot, but every time I returned, those ruffian-like tendencies came back to me! I started becoming extreme, but you never criticized me. Instead, you always found ways to make me happy, smoothing out my extremes, allowing me to return to sunny days! From then on, you wouldn't allow me to go back to that place, but I kept making you sad over and over again!

In September 2011, I left stubbornly! You said nothing, and looking at the diaries you wrote for me, I started to feel pain. Seeing you harm yourself for me, I felt so guilty! Originally, I had already gotten used to having you around, and at that moment, I started to feel sorry for you!

"As long as I'm happy, that's all that matters."

"I'm sorry." "Silly, don't think too much. When you're done playing, come back."

It turns out it wasn't that you said nothing; it was your silence that let me be stubborn. It turns out you spoiled me so much! Rather than complain about me, you preferred to endure the pain yourself! I want to say: "I won't be stubborn anymore. After the college entrance exam is over, let's leave together! With dreams, let's take another step forward!"

"Be a proud woman, look down on this world with your perspective." I promise you, I will definitely make myself better!

I admit I am a selfish child; I admit I only love myself! I never care about others' thoughts, nor consider their feelings! In the end, in September 2011, I hurt you again. I started getting entangled with another boy. Initially, I didn't expect to get stuck. A month later, he left, leaving me alone in sorrow! It was you who entered my life at that time, teaching me to view the world objectively. When I was helpless and crying hysterically, you came, staying by my side, slowly calming my extreme emotions. I was like a hedgehog, and when you hugged me and blood dripped on you, you said I had hurt you, but you didn't realize that the blood was mine! I understood, all the pride I took for granted was the light you projected into my heart, reflecting back to me. I want to say: "Thank you for being with me all along!"

I gave myself six years to change myself! At the same time, I gave us a chance! Six years, with you by my side, it's enough!

I always thought eternity existed, but now I realize how ignorant I was. Someone, take your forever and go! You don't even deserve to be scolded by me. I've said what I need to say for you, you don't need to say anything, and I don't want to hear it either. Those who stay by my side will never change, nor will they hurt me! Auntie said: "If you get upset or hold grudges because of certain things, it only shows that you lack cultivation!" So, I've forgotten! It's very warm in Auntie's arms. "In the future, be more rational, don't be stubborn anymore." Walking with Auntie on bustling streets, I suddenly understood that life's journey needs to be walked like this!

It turns out that the people who have been with me till the end are not those so-called friends, nor those so-called brothers or sisters. They are you, Guaibao, Huihui, Xuan, and Auntie. Thank you for accompanying me through a bleak youth!

You don't allow me to have any contact with that place again, and you don't allow me to go back! Now I agree with all of you! From now on, everything there has nothing to do with me. Yes! Those are just expired things, there's no need to keep missing them. They are right; that was just a small interlude in my life! I should be very clear about what I need and what I pursue. There's no need to give up on what I originally pursued because of unrelated matters!

With all of you by my side, I believe I can walk this path strongly. The journey ahead will surely be brilliant!
