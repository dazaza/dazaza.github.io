It is understood that among the 11 kind-hearted citizens, there are enterprise employees and ordinary workers; there are young men and middle-aged elderly people. "This is a trivial matter, not worth promoting" "The main achievement is who who, I just participated with limited strength"... When the reporter wanted to understand the situation from them, these were the most heard voices. The masses worked together to pull Uncle Chen out of the water. Suzhou media provided the photo. At 8:30 PM on January 14th, near the east side of Taiping Bridge on Ganjiang Road in Suzhou, a 65-year-old uncle accidentally fell into the cold Ganjiang River. In a critical moment, the police officer nearby and passers-by, including tricycle drivers, decoration workers, and enterprise employees, all reached out to help. After more than 10 minutes of joint rescue, the old man was finally pulled ashore. Coincidentally, this rescue scene was recorded by a local Suzhou media journalist passing by. After a search, all 11 participants in the rescue were found. Yesterday morning, the Public Security Bureau of Suzhou City and the Justice Foundation of Gusu District jointly held a commendation meeting for "Love Deeds, Supporting Life" and commended these brave people. At this time, Sun Hongqi, a 51-year-old decorator, also joined the rescue team when he saw the situation. He took out the construction wire from his car and threw it into the river, allowing Chen Linsen to tie one end of the wire around his wrist. Everyone formed a "crowd" human ladder, the people behind firmly held the ones in front, layer by layer exerting force, and finally managed to save Chen Linsen. Yesterday, Chen Linsen, 65 years old, told the reporter about the incident. At 8:30 PM, after having dinner at a relative's house, he returned home. Since he had drunk a bit of alcohol and it was raining slightly at night, when he arrived at the east side of Taiping Bridge on Ganjiang Road, he mistakenly took the river for the road and fell into the river. The cold river water immediately woke him up, and he quickly shouted for help. In a critical moment, dozens of citizens came, all reaching out to help. "When I was pulled ashore, I was both cold and scared, my consciousness was blurred, and I couldn't clearly see who saved me." Uncle Chen frankly said that after being saved, he thought every day about finding those kind-hearted people and thanking them face to face. A tricycle driver named Liu Guilin and citizen Liu Dong also arrived upon hearing the news. Liu Guilin found a stick nearby and quickly extended it into the river. Chen Linsen tightly grabbed one end of the stick, using its leverage to wait for everyone's rescue. Reporter He Jie. At the commendation site, a police officer in uniform particularly caught attention. It turns out that he is Officer Zhong Jian from the Pingjiang Brigade of the Suzhou Traffic Police Corps. Zhong Jian told the reporter that at the time, due to a two-car rear-end accident nearby, he received the information and rushed to the scene to handle it. To prevent the accident vehicles from affecting traffic, he first guided the two vehicles to the side of Taiping Bridge for handling. At this point, he saw someone floating about 30 meters away on the eastern side of the river surface, so he ran over with the two car owners.
