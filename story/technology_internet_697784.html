Pacific Direct Purchasing Network constitutes pyramid selling and is investigated, deposit is divided layer by layer

by bingo123 on 2012-03-08 17:39:15

On February 15, the publicly released judgment document "(2012) Que Xing Chu Zi No. 2" from the Que Shan County People's Court in Henan Province showed that the business practices of Jiangxi Colorful Life Investment Development Co., Ltd. constituted pyramid selling with the subjective intent to gain improper benefits. The lawsuit was dismissed. Additionally, the Changchun Administration for Industry and Commerce also issued a decision based on this information to confiscate illegal earnings amounting to RMB 33.128 million and impose a fine of RMB 2 million. In response, Ling Yun, head of the China Anti-Pyramid Selling Volunteer Alliance, told Tianfu Morning Post that the Pacific Direct Purchase website under its umbrella was essentially engaging in dangerous practices.

### Exposure: Questionable Model - Security Deposits Divided Layer by Layer

Recently, our newspaper has continuously received calls from readers wanting to understand the relevant rules of Jiangxi Colorful Life Investment Development Co., Ltd. (hereinafter referred to as "Colorful Life") and its subsidiary Pacific Direct Purchase Official Website (hereinafter referred to as "Pacific"). Ling Yun stated that the Pacific Direct Purchase Official Website officially launched on December 18, 2008. By lowering the membership entry threshold, it expanded its membership base, which not only injected new funds but also, in such an environment, created popularity through expanding memberships. He said that when registering on Pacific, there is a mandatory field, which is the inviter. If A invites B, B invites C, and C invites D, then B, C, and D are all alliance members of A, and the points they generate are all alliance points of A. They continuously incite people to pay security deposits to reserve PVs (points), directly reaching a certain level. For example, if you pay RMB 7,000 to reserve 1,000 PV points and become a qualified and trustworthy channel merchant, then each level within this alliance will receive commissions as follows: a qualified and trustworthy channel merchant gets a 20% PV point rebate, which is RMB 1,400; a fifth-level trustworthy channel merchant gets a 6% differential, which is RMB 420; a fourth-level trustworthy channel merchant gets a 6% differential, which is RMB 420; special fourth, second, and super zone trustworthy channel merchants get a 3% differential, which is RMB 210; a second-level trustworthy channel merchant gets a 6% differential, which is RMB 420; first-level and premier trustworthy channel merchants get a 4% differential, which is RMB 280; a large zone trustworthy channel merchant gets a 7% differential, which is RMB 490; and a special global trustworthy channel merchant gets a 6% differential, which is RMB 420.

### Penalty: Investigation Results - Confiscation of Illegal Income Amounting to RMB 60 Million

The website promoted its "saving + earning", "employment + entrepreneurship" "new business model," claiming that as long as one joined, they could enter a fast track to wealth where the more they consumed, the more they saved and earned. Ling Yun introduced via Weibo that most of the leadership of the Pacific Direct Purchase Official Website came from the original Lixin Century, including former Lixin Century diamond dealer Chen and Blue Gem President Liu. The East China, North China, and South China operation centers were successively established, headed by former high-level dealers of Lixin Century, Tong and Liu.

It is understood that the "consumption enrichment" model of Colorful Life and the Pacific Direct Purchase website attracted over 10 million members to join. Just in Zhumadian and Changchun alone, law enforcement departments have discovered that Colorful Life collected security deposits from several channel merchants amounting to more than RMB 66 million. Faced with crackdowns by law enforcement agencies, Colorful Life and the Pacific insisted that no one could stop the Pacific from moving forward.

In August 2011, the Zhumadian Administration for Industry and Commerce ordered Colorful Life to immediately correct its illegal behavior and made a penalty decision to confiscate illegal gains of RMB 33.71 million and impose a fine of RMB 2 million. Dissatisfied, Colorful Life sued the bureau. On February 15, the publicized judgment document "(2012) Que Xing Chu Zi No. 2" from the Que Shan County People's Court showed that the business practices of Colorful Life constituted pyramid selling with the subjective intent to gain improper benefits, and the lawsuit was dismissed.
