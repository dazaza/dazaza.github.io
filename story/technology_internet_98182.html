Company Profile

Wenzhou Lejia Decoration Engineering Co., Ltd. is a professional decoration enterprise engaged in interior design and construction. Relying on the strong design force of Shanghai Chongyan Interior Design, and with high-quality construction as our backbone, we professionally undertake home office, commercial, entertainment space, and residential interior design and construction.

The company adheres to the corporate culture of "returning to nature, people-oriented, striving for perfection, and inspiration." In design, we emphasize the combination of color, lighting, materials, and humanization. We uphold the design philosophy of "not being repetitive, paying attention to spatial details, and making the limited display infinite." We adhere to the principle that "design is the soul, quality is life," and take "honesty and credibility" as our business philosophy. With a strict, pragmatic, meticulous attitude, we pursue top-notch quality.

Using our skilled craftsmen, we create for you a perfect working and living environment that is natural, comfortable, cultural, and meaningful. Lejia believes that with our efforts, we can create a warm and happy home for you.

The company implements a one-year warranty, lifelong maintenance system, and regularly conducts warranty follow-up visits to provide quality tracking services for your project, truly achieving the assurance that choosing us means choosing peace of mind.

Development and innovation are endless. We focus on the decoration industry.

Design Concept:

Scientific, reasonable, and fashionable designs guide construction. Implementing a designer responsibility system will better express the design intention in construction. From the preliminary design plan to on-site guidance during the project and post-decorative configuration, all are led by designers, truly realizing the reasonableness of spatial layout planning and aesthetic decoration perfectly presented in on-site construction. A scientifically managed and excellent engineering team and processing factory ensure the full execution of the designer's concept. We present a more complete representation of the overall design in soft furnishings. Our group of outstanding designs has won a good reputation in the industry.

Project Management:

Quality is the most concerning issue for all renovation owners and also a common problem encountered during construction. Yanlong combines design with construction to solve the shortcomings of conventional management, which is the dual quality assurance of cooperation between designers and on-site project managers. We have a team of qualified decoration construction workers with years of experience. Each construction team undergoes strict job training and strictly follows the 3.15 standard for each construction phase acceptance.

From production to acceptance, we always adhere to "design guiding construction." Design personnel guide the size and aesthetic rationality of production detail processes to prevent disconnection caused by construction personnel misunderstanding the drawings. Designers and quality inspectors immediately rectify issues found on-site, eliminating potential quality risks at the bud stage. On-site construction project managers adopt double inspection standards for key parts such as concealed works, water and electricity works, waterproof works, etc., meaning self-inspection by the company's quality inspection department followed by secondary verification with the owner and supervisory department, striving to ensure each part meets the "Decoration Project Acceptance Standard."

Yanlong provides unified training for construction personnel in various trades such as demolition, water and electricity, masonry, carpentry, and painting. Training content includes technical training for each trade and comprehensive training focusing on learning how to coordinate between different trades and managers, making the construction team more sophisticated and coordinated, ensuring a satisfactory and reliable project for customers.

Price Management:

Each of our budget quotations is strictly based on national standard quotas, emphasizing detailed, reasonable, and transparent pricing. Regardless of the project size, charges are consistent without overcharging or random fees, truly achieving reasonable prices. Offering diverse options such as full-package, semi-package, and clear-package methods, we maximize customer choice. Our service is determined according to the owner's requirements. Owners can freely choose construction methods—full-package, semi-package, or pure design—and we can provide after-sales services such as on-site consultation and guidance by designers regardless of the chosen method.
