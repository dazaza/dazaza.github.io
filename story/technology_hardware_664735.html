Saving money, going to the bank for a loan, searching everywhere for suitable real estate projects, participating in lotteries, signing contracts, waiting for delivery of the house... buying a house is a series of long and complicated steps. After obtaining the house, one cannot relax, because decoration is also a headache-inducing big project: sourcing various decoration materials, finding a decoration company, supervising the decoration process, all of which require great patience and effort.
