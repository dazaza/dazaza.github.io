Jeju Island is South Korea's most famous health retreat and also a beautiful volcanic island. Since the island was formed by volcanic eruptions, Jeju Island is centered around Hallasan Mountain, surrounded on all sides by strange rocks and cliffs, with the outer edge bordered by the sea. Coupled with a warm climate throughout the year, Jeju Island has become a scenic tourist area with beautiful landscapes. The scenery of Jeju Island is the most beloved by Koreans; the city-like mountains make one completely forget the noise and irritation of urban life. Standing on the grassy plains connected to these mountain cities, looking up at the peaks and down at the sea, everything is so tranquil that it feels like a journey to paradise. Hallasan National Park has become an academically valuable national park due to its vertical plant ecosystem composed of over 1800 types of plants and more than 4000 species of animals. The Tian Deyuan Waterfall, divided into three sections, is surrounded by a temperate forest made up of precious plants such as pineleaf orchids, filled with birdsong and flowers, creating an ethereal mist that leaves visitors entranced.
