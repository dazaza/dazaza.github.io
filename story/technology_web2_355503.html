**Differences between Adjustable and Non-adjustable Disc Samplers (Knives):**

- The adjustable disc sampler (knife) is equipped with a regulating nut and a fixed adjusting knob, while the non-adjustable type does not have these features. Instead, it only has a locking switch (as shown in the comparison image).

**Advantages of the Adjustable Type:**

- Allows adjustment of the blade depth based on the thickness of the sample, extending the life of the blade and rubber pad.

- Facilitates smoother cutting during rotation with less effort.

**Disadvantages of the Non-Adjustable Type:**

- Without an adjustable screw, the blade extends at least 6mm below the base when the locking switch is opened.

- When cutting thin samples, most of the cutting occurs on the rubber pad, which easily bends or breaks the blade and shortens the life of the pad.

- Requires more force and effort during rotation due to higher resistance.

**(Cutter for Circular Samples)** The disc sampler (grammage meter) can quickly, accurately, and safely cut out circular samples of 100 square centimeters (1% of 1 square meter). The weight of the sample can then be measured using a precision balance (accuracy of 0.01 grams). Multiply the result by 100 to obtain the grammage per square meter of the fabric (or paper).

This instrument is suitable for cutting round samples of various textile fabrics with a thickness of less than 5mm. Sampling area: 100cm².
