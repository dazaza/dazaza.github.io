Opening a Paipai store is as follows, the steps are as follows:

Step 1: Log in to the Paipai homepage and click on the "Free Store Opening" in the upper left corner of the webpage, or follow the guidance in the image to enter the seller certification entry.

Step 2: After entering, you will see the "Certification and Process Introduction" page. Read the certification introduction and "Seller Guidelines and User Agreement", check the agreement with this user agreement, and click the "Mainland User Certification" button.

Step 3: At this point, you enter the "Pay Deposit" page. Please confirm that your Tenpay account has at least 20 yuan balance (you can recharge 20 yuan to your Tenpay account), input your payment password as the certification deposit. We will freeze 20 yuan as a deposit in your Tenpay account.

Step 4: Then enter the "Fill Personal Information" page, carefully fill in necessary personal information, including name, phone number, ID number, etc., and upload digital photos or scanned images of your ID card. Confirm that the information is correct and click the "Submit Personal Information" button.

Step 5: After submission, we will review within three working days. After passing the review, you can promptly post items for sale on Paipai. If the "Personal Real Name Certification" application review is passed, you can become a Paipai seller; if your certification does not pass the review, the 20 yuan deposit you paid will be automatically unfrozen, please log into Tenpay to check at that time. After successful certification, you still need to pay a corresponding integrity deposit of 1000-3000 yuan!

The process for opening a Taobao store is as follows:

Step 1: The goal is not just online but in your mind. Many people want to open stores, some have indeed opened their stores, but the products in their stores are all over the place, barely managing to gather 10 items. However, they have no clear idea about who their target audience is or the future direction of their shop. Therefore, the first step is to conduct market research, understand our own business goals and the market, and have a clear understanding and reasonable plan for the future direction of our store.

Step 2: Apply for an account. A prerequisite for this step is to activate online banking (recommended China Construction Bank online banking, Industrial and Commercial Bank of China online banking, Agricultural Bank of China online banking, China Merchants Bank online banking, etc.). After ensuring that you have activated online banking from one bank, we need to complete the following steps: First, apply for a Taobao account. Applying for a Taobao account is as simple and quick as applying for a QQ number. We just need to fill in the relevant information according to the prompts (Note: try to fill in real information). Second, apply to activate Alipay. Alipay is like cash for daily transactions regarding Taobao. Alipay is an insurance tool provided by Taobao for online transaction payments. Almost all C2C transactions on Taobao are realized through Alipay. Applying for Alipay is similar to applying for a Taobao account. After activating Alipay, we need to obtain Taobao's real-name verification. Once the real-name verification is completed, everything is ready.

Step 3: Procurement. Just like opening a physical store, procurement is a necessary part of opening a store. There are many channels and various ways to procure goods. You can get supplies from nearby markets, familiar areas, relatives, and friends, or find online store agents. Online store agents can save many intermediate steps. Among the main sources of supply: 1. Wholesale markets. This is a relatively common channel for procurement. For example, clothing can choose large clothing wholesale markets, where large clothing wholesale markets have significant price advantages. If you have good eloquence or luck and can build a long-term cooperative relationship with the boss of the wholesale market, you may receive many benefits, such as returns and exchanges. 2. Manufacturer supplies. Especially during this particular period, manufacturers have a lot of inventory. For companies mainly focused on exports, affected by the international financial tsunami, warehouses are filled with a large amount of inventory products, and product quality naturally does not need to worry. 3. Online store agents. As an online agent, we don't need inventory; we just need to reach agreements with merchants in certain aspects, and we can directly use their supplies to open our own store. Merchants providing online agents generally have sufficient supplies, a wide variety of products, complete data, and good service attitudes, but they may require a certain franchise or agency fee. 4. Large wholesalers. For larger wholesalers, we can search in "Ali Purchase". However, there is a large amount of information here, so we need to carefully analyze and compare, find merchants that meet our requirements, and ensure security. Note that the wholesale volume here is generally large. If you have a few acquaintances who can purchase together, this is a good choice.

Step 4: Upload products. Taobao provides sellers with a free product upload tool - Taobao Assistant. This tool makes uploading products more humanized, convenient, and quick. We can first edit the relevant product information offline (name, origin, location, nature, appearance, quantity, transaction method, transaction deadline, etc.), then perform batch uploads, and even batch edit product information.

Step 5: Marketing Promotion. Once the store is up and running, how to increase your popularity becomes a critical issue. There are many ways to promote marketing, such as browsing forums, replying to posts, attaching your store address, promoting among classmates and friends, attaching your store address in QQ groups and Wangwang group chats, all of which are excellent methods. If you're still a student, you can also promote your store through posters.

Step 6: Mid-Sale Service. The quality of mid-sale service can directly affect the success or failure of this transaction. When customers are deciding whether to buy a product, they have many uncertainties. They hope to further communicate with the seller to eliminate these uncertainties. During communication, we should be as sincere as possible, answer their concerns, and appropriately introduce the advantages of the product to gain their approval.

Step 7: Transaction. Once both parties agree on the terms of the transaction, the deal begins.

Step 8: After-Sales Service. Don't think the transaction is complete once it's finished. On the contrary, after completing the transaction, we need to appropriately remind the buyer when shipping, or inquire about matters related to this transaction. If there is a problem with the product, it needs to be resolved promptly and decisively, and we must never keep finding excuses for ourselves.

Step 9: Evaluation. After a successful transaction between both parties, they can evaluate each other. Credit is a very important factor in online transactions. To jointly build a credit environment, if the transaction is satisfactory, it's best to give the other party a positive review and gain their positive review through good service. If the transaction fails, give a negative review or complain to the website to reduce losses and warn others. If the other party complains, handle it as soon as possible to avoid leaving a stain on your credit.

Finally, wish you booming business. Please adopt if satisfied.

When you have these accounts and pass the real-name certification, newcomers face a choice - What should my store sell? As a newbie seller, there are generally the following situations:

1. If you have a relatively advantageous source of goods or time to organize them, you can sell your own goods. 2. If you don't have a source of goods and don't have time to organize them, you can act as an agent for some products online. That is, others have products, and you upload the relevant introductions and pictures of their products to your own store, helping others sell things. If they give you something for 10 yuan, you sell it for 12 or 15 yuan, etc. You set the price yourself and earn the difference. This is also suitable for newbie sellers, but there is a fatal weakness: the quality of online goods varies widely, and you can't see the goods yourself. You don't know much about the quality or color, so when buyers ask questions, you can't explain them clearly. Buyers receiving the goods may be dissatisfied, leading to bad reviews or returns. Similarly, newbie agent stores also have low credibility, and when someone sees a product, they won't buy directly but search instead, choosing a store with higher credibility.
