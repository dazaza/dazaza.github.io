Weibo recommendation | Today's popular topics on Weibo Southern Daily News (reporter / He Jianwen correspondent / Zhang Zhiwei) Yesterday morning, a suspected mentally ill man climbed onto the top of Gaobu Bridge completely naked and kept walking back and forth on the top of the bridge playing, even claiming that he was "Sun Wukong". Fortunately, firefighters cleverly quoted martial arts anecdotes to persuade him down. Around 10:15 am yesterday, the firefighter ascended the ladder after climbing up and began a dialogue with the man. Afterwards, according to the firefighter who carried out the persuasion task, the man claimed that he was Sun Wukong. Seeing this, the firefighter decided to follow the content of what the man was saying and lied that he was the master who could take him as an apprentice. The author found that the man's behavior was very strange, tearing wires and plastic pipes on the bridge at one time, and walking back and forth on the top at another time. After on-site observation, the fire department decided that the firefighter would ascend the ladder fire truck to persuade the man and lay large air cushions on the bridge surface. When the author arrived at the scene, the Gaobu Public Security, Fire Department, Hospital and other departments were already organizing rescue operations. It was rush hour for work, so the Gaobu traffic police temporarily closed the Gaobu Bridge. Therefore, the Gaobu Avenue and Wan-Gao Road sections were once congested. Around 10:30 am, the firefighter successfully brought the man back to the ground. It is reported that the man was then sent to the hospital by medical personnel.
