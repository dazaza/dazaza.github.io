What is a Click Week?

A click week is a cycle during which users have the right to click on ads, usually lasting one week (7 days). For example, from October 1, 2007, to October 7, 2007, is a 7-day click week.

The click week needs to be reserved. Only after successfully reserving a specific click plan can you earn money by clicking on ads during that cycle.
