Beijing Super 8 (Dongsi) Tianmu Lake Bamboo Sea in the South Mountain

by y32ewf217x on 2010-04-02 11:40:55

In celebration of Women's Day, this year's Tianmu Lake South Mountain Bamboo Sea Hiking Festival grandly launched the "Beauty's Journey". Online media from Shanghai, Suzhou, Hangzhou, Nanjing and other places jointly recruited "professional beauties" for a self-driving tour of the South Mountain Bamboo Sea, igniting enthusiasm among many netizens to sign up. After careful selection, the outstanding "professional beauties" became important guests at the hiking festival activities. During the festival, they could not only visit the Tianmu Lake South Mountain Bamboo Sea scenic area for free but also receive a complimentary ticket to the Yu Shui Hot Spring. These youthful and attractive professional beauties, after climbing to the top of South Mountain to admire the vast bamboo sea, would then soak in the Yu Shui Hot Spring, enjoying the comfort of an aromatic SPA to eliminate fatigue and rejuvenate their health.

By the banks of Tianmu Lake, with friends exploring the early spring, the fairy tale little train carries you along the mountain path upwards, transforming you into princes and princesses, experiencing romance. The gentle cable car takes you through the air to experience the magnificence of the bamboo sea, like rippling green waves moving with the wind, the masterful craftsmanship of nature leaves one awestruck. Retracing the ancient official road, pondering the sentiments of predecessors, observing bamboo carvings, appreciating the extraordinary skills of craftsmanship. At the Wu Yue "younger brother" peak, holding a cup of Nan Shan寿mei tea, viewing the beautiful scenery of three provinces with friends, such a scene is truly divine.

The fifth Tianmu Lake South Mountain Bamboo Sea Hiking Festival launches the Beauty's Journey. Tianmu Lake always evokes such involuntary affection, the serene lake, willows swaying gently, whispering the tenderness of spring. Such a picturesque place is never short of lovers leaning on each other, softly narrating their sweetness in the landscape painting of Tianmu Lake's mountains and waters.

South Mountain Bamboo Sea, climbing high to look far and admire the emerald bamboo. Spring is the perfect time for hiking, and the Tianmu Lake South Mountain Bamboo Sea Hiking Challenge has become a classic event of this spring. Winding mountain paths follow the stream, stretching from before the Nan Shan longevity elder towards the depths of the bamboo sea. The Wu Yue "younger brother" peak at 508 meters above sea level is the endpoint of the hiking competition. A journey of over a thousand meters is ideal for friends, classmates, and colleagues to participate in a hiking race together. What matters is not who reaches the summit first, but the scenery along the way and the good mood while enjoying it.

At the lakeside and on the mountain, admire the talents of scholars from the past in the Zhuangyuan Pavilion, inspiring future generations to strive upward. There are also thrilling high-altitude rope descents that bring excitement and joy of spring. In the countryside, the ancient mill and Jiangnan water wheels sway quietly; under the spring sun, stepping on the ancient water wheel irrigates the fields and vegetable gardens, as if returning to a distant past. On the Jiangnan streets, various handicrafts dazzle the eyes, and delicious snacks open the appetite in spring. There’s also a fun carnival where riding a water bicycle brings unique pleasure. Moreover, attractions like Butterfly Valley, Happy World, and high-altitude flying descents will make your early spring trip distinctive and warm.

The Tianmu Lake South Mountain Bamboo Sea Hiking Festival runs from March 6th to March 28th. Different from previous festivals, this year's event not only continues the popular South Mountain Bamboo Sea spring hiking competition beloved by tourists but also incorporates healthy concepts like hot spring wellness. The Tianmu Lake South Mountain Bamboo Sea scenic area collaborates with the Yu Shui Hot Spring to create a healthy journey, allowing people who have been cooped up in cities all winter to exercise in the early spring South Mountain Bamboo Sea and bathe in the Yu Shui Hot Spring for wellness, enjoying the most comfortable and fashionable spring health journey.

With fragrant flowers and lush vegetation, it's the most beautiful time of smoky rain in southern Jiangnan. Tianmu Lake resembles a newly awakened Jiangnan woman, dressing up beautifully, charming and captivating. Whether strolling by the lake, where the beauty of the scenery complements the charm of the people, or wandering deep in the bamboo forest, experiencing the beauty of fish, birds, flowers, and fragrant spring, Tianmu Lake, with its superior natural resources, excellent landscapes, and sincere service, themed around "the most beautiful place in Jiangnan," has been awarded the title of "Yangtze River Delta Expo Theme Experience Tour Demonstration Site," warmly embracing the Expo with a renewed posture to welcome visitors. This spring, let's meet at the edge of Tianmu Lake and privately whisper about the beauty of Jiangnan.

As the ancients say, mountains need not be high, with immortals they become magical. The bamboo of South Mountain always makes one believe in the existence of immortals. Floating on a bamboo raft, facing the still lake with bamboo forests on both sides, the rustling sound of bamboo leaves fills the ears, white egrets fly lightly, mandarin ducks play in the water. In such scenery, there's no need for much talk, just quiet meditation is enough. Amidst the winding corridors, every step reveals a new view, every step brims with emotion. Searching and seeking, suddenly, a white-bearded immortal old man appears singing in the mountains...
