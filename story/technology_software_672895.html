Making money online requires time, so I suggest that working friends do not resign and cherish your current job. Building a website and making profits from it is not an easy task. My first site took about two months from the completion of the website construction to when the keywords ranked on the first page of Baidu and started generating profits. Of course, the profit was not much, just enough to cover the cost of building the site. Since this site had suspicions of cheating, along with rarely updating articles and changes in Baidu's algorithm, the Baidu keyword ranking dropped from the second position on the first page to the second page. Therefore, this all depends on many factors. You need to conduct market research, build a website, write content, promote, and these are not things that can be completed in a short time. I am a typical representative of joining work. I have a formal job during the day and use my spare time at night to make money online. When the money I earn online can cover my living expenses, I will resign.

A few years ago, Dondon's Creative Online Earning once became popular for a time, regardless of past and present attitudes towards him which vary greatly among people. But in fact, he did lead a group of people to make money, and he himself was once a glorious miracle of internet earning. It was also from that time that more and more people began to join the ranks of online earning. With increasing attention to online earning, more and more people have developed into daydreaming in front of their computers without considering the actual execution of online earning. Although there are more and more creative ideas, those who truly execute them are very few. Although ideas are increasing, each idea ends in failure. We don't even consider whether these ideas are feasible; just seeing how people change their online earning methods every day is enough to make one feel that the possibility of success is so low. Having innovative ideas for online earning is important, but if these ideas are not truly executed, no matter how successful the ideas are, they cannot generate profits in the end, or if they do, they are very few.

Online earning, abbreviated as net earning, I believe the term "net earning" is familiar to everyone, but how many people actually make money using the internet? It's not that online earning projects don't make money, but you haven't done a good job executing them. The key to starting online earning is execution; without fierce execution, there is no possibility of making big money. Don't say that you can make money without execution by doing two things at once. Ask yourself how many times you have such opportunities. Only through fierce execution can you make money in the long run. Online earning is not as simple as we imagine, something that can be accomplished with just a computer and an internet connection.

There are many forms of online earning. In the early stages, I played around with Taobao affiliates, simply helping Taobao sellers sell items and then receiving commissions from the system. There are N ways to promote just one Taobao affiliate: blogs, forum posts, diaries, SEO websites, microblogs. No matter what, as long as buyers enter the Taobao seller's store through your link and a transaction occurs, you will receive a commission. I don't do it anymore mainly because some things on there are too fake, and there are too many scams online. Not long ago, I watched "Focus Interview," which said that a Taobao Crown Store was selling counterfeit goods. The condition for making money is that you must be fair to consumers.

Here, I would like to give a warning to new online earners. There are quite a few scams online now, the reason being that the threshold for making money online is relatively low, or perhaps too many daydreams have been made. In short, many people think making money online is a very simple thing. So some unscrupulous people take advantage of the consumer psychology of these groups to lure you, making you involuntarily send money into their pockets. Therefore, be cautious about those requiring payment. Although paying is supposed to motivate you to better execute, you should also think carefully. Making money online consumes a lot of energy, and there are significant risks involved, and it may not achieve the results you want. Although there are free online earnings, can you really do them? Even if free ones can make money, can you earn enough for a pack of good cigarettes in a month?
