Plot Summary

As a regular white-collar worker who works from nine to five, apart from having a pretty face that everyone likes, twenty-something-year-old Zhang Xiao is no different from other busy urban people around her. Zhang Xiao has had several love affairs that ended without result, not deeply touching but still affecting her a bit. She does a job that she neither likes nor dislikes, but for more salary and a higher position, she can happily immerse herself in the cruel workplace competition. She methodically plans her future life - house, travel, romance.

In Zhang Xiao's first half of life, whether what she did or what she thought, almost everything was the same as ordinary people, striving for a better life, never tiring. But deep inside Zhang Xiao, there was always a pull, something she couldn't quite articulate. It felt like there was another kind of life waiting for her somewhere in the universe...

That day, Zhang Xiao's fate was completely rewritten by a car accident. She watched helplessly as the out-of-control truck crashed her soul out of her body. And instead of being taken to the hospital or heaven, she was thrown into the Qing Dynasty's imperial court. Like countless novels written about time-travel, Zhang Xiao's soul crossed through time tunnels and was reborn inside the body of a sixteen-year-old girl named Maltai Ruoxi in the Qing Dynasty...

It was the forty-third year of Emperor Kangxi. The world was peaceful, but a historical storm known as the "Nine Sons' Struggle for the Throne" was brewing beneath the surface. The battle for power and the burning passion of love intertwined on this grand historical stage, inevitably crossing the paths of Zhang Xiao, or rather, Maltai Ruoxi, with those of the various princes...

She knew she shouldn't be involved in the struggle for the throne among the nine princes, but her heart wouldn't let her stay away, because there were those she loved, and those who loved her...

Whom will Ruoxi ultimately choose - the elegant Eighth Prince, the cold-hearted yet warm Fourth Prince, or someone else entirely unexpected? After the dust of history settles, who will remain by her side to watch the sunset and enjoy the clouds rolling by?
