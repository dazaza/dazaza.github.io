Keywords: My Dearest Person, The Grand Finale of My Dearest Person, Grand Finale, Qiu Yu, 68 Episodes, Plot of Episode 68, Full Series

The small theater had no air conditioning, and the smell of sweat and the clamor made people feel suffocated. Despite the troupe leader Ah Jun's efforts to liven up the atmosphere, despite the horn player Li Minghui's hard work in blowing the horn, and despite Qiu Yu and Qiu Mei, the mainstays of the dance troupe in their prime, dancing gracefully on stage and singing a song called "My Dearest Person," the restless audience wanted more. They weren't looking for grace; they were looking for a "Cool Show." This was also the strong medicine that Troupe Leader Ah Jun secretly prescribed after seeing the terrible box office, which indeed boosted today's super-popular box office, but the people wanted their money's worth. Although Ah Jun tried his best to delay, the audience was already impatiently throwing melon seeds and fruit peels at the stage.

Ah Jun helplessly pulled over Qiu Yu and told her he hoped she could make the sacrifice, or the troupe would face disbandment. Qiu Yu was in a dilemma. For the sake of the troupe, she should make the sacrifice, but this sacrifice required her to undress. Minghui was even more distressed. Since joining the troupe, he had been deeply attracted by Qiu Yu. Now, the person he admired was facing such an embarrassing choice, and there was nothing he could do to help. This anxiety and self-reproach made him angry like a trapped beast...
