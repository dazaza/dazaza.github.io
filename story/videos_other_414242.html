In the wuxia world of Poly.com's "Proud Sword", players can feel more than just a hint of the江湖气息. In this wuxia world, there are martial arts experts with extraordinary skills everywhere. If you also want to become one of them, understanding the four major sects in Poly.com's "Proud Sword" is an essential step. "Proud Sword" is a game based on Jin Yong's wuxia novel "The Legend of the Condor Heroes". The four major sects in the game are: Shaolin Sect, the paramount force in Chinese martial arts, Quanzhen Sect founded by Wang Chongyang and famous at the time, Peach Blossom Island with its talented people and auspicious land, and Gumu Sect known as the "graveyard of the living". Each sect has its own unique martial arts characteristics. Today, let us take a look at the four major sects of "Proud Sword".

The Seven Disciples of Quanzhen Renowned Under Heaven

Wearing dark green Taoist robes, a Taiji Bagua emblem slowly unfolds before their chest, hair tied up, a sharp sword carried on their back, as the gentle breeze blows, they exude an air of ethereal transcendence. The history of Quanzhen Sect dates back a long way. The Quanzhen Sect in Jin Yong's works is also derived from the real-life Daoist sect Quanzhen Sect during the Southern Song Dynasty. In the novels, we learn about Wang Chongyang's extraordinary martial arts and the essence of the Seven Disciples of Quanzhen's sword formation. "Proud Sword" will also focus on developing these aspects. In "Proud Sword", there is a splendid array system and an exciting PK setting. The combination of these special elements will allow Quanzhen Sect to perform differently from before. Although Wang Chongyang has passed away, Quanzhen Sect continues to thrive.

Gumu Sect's Divine Powers Overwhelm the Heroes

Xiaolongnü's appearance embodies the cold and proud character of the Gumu Sect. Without going into how many people envied and hated Yang Guo, it is said that the Gumu Sect only accepts female disciples and has few of them. Compared to large sects like Shaolin and Quanzhen, it may seem weaker in strength. Compared to prestigious families like Peach Blossom Island, it may seem less influential. However, the Gumu Sect has always been focused on quality over quantity. Its ability to remain steadfast throughout the martial arts world of the Southern Song Dynasty proves its formidable strength. The Jade Maiden Heart Sutra, Jade Bee Golden Needle, and Golden Bell Lock of the Gumu Sect are all top-notch martial arts of the time. Like plum blossoms defying the winter wind, the Gumu Sect rarely involves itself in worldly affairs, does not form deep relationships with others, and remains mysterious and feared. What draws the most attention from the world regarding the Gumu Sect is the succession of beauties within it. At some point, people outside the novels and TV shows have expressed their admiration for Xiaolongnü with the phrase, "If I could have Xiaolongnü in this life, what else would I need?"

Shaolin Disciples Practicing Good Deeds in the World

I have a left blue dragon and a right white tiger, an ox around my waist, if a Buddha blocks my way, I'll kill the Buddha, if a god blocks my way, I'll kill the god... Of course, "Proud Sword" doesn't go so far as to be humorous. In "Proud Sword", there is a Shaolin Temple. In the game, players will assume the role of lay disciples of Shaolin, entering the world to practice chivalry. Shaolin has always been renowned as the grandmaster of martial arts. The saying "All martial arts under heaven originate from Shaolin" refers precisely to this. Although Shaolin's Zen philosophy is profound and compassionate, it is often said, "If I don't enter hell, who will?" Punishing evil and promoting good, eradicating villains and demons is also one of Shaolin's main principles. Shaolin combines Buddhist studies with martial arts, not only capable of fighting but also enduring. In times of chaos, would it merely preach or collect alms?

The Sound of the Waves Blows through Peach Blossom Island

Do you remember Huang Yaoshi, tall and slim, his figure elusive like a ghost? With ease, he could blow the sound of the waves and place his opponents in mortal danger. His disciples were all highly skilled in martial arts. Moreover, Huang Yaoshi's daughter, Huang Rong, was mischievous yet devoted in love, adding a romantic touch to Peach Blossom Island. In "Proud Sword", Peach Blossom Island continues this style. Its beautifully dreamlike skills are a characteristic of Peach Blossom Island. Sometimes, it dances gracefully like a charming maiden, and other times, it looks down upon the world like a noble celestial being. Roaming through the late Southern Song Dynasty in "Proud Sword", Peach Blossom Island attracts a large number of female players with its bright and distinctive features.

Poly.com's "Proud Sword" has received excellent praise from both the industry and players as the hottest online game in 2011. The various interesting systems continuously introduced in the game allow players to fully experience the legendary journey of traveling through the Southern Song Dynasty while also enabling players to understand the joy of the wuxia world. Poly.com's "Proud Sword" faces all wuxia players with an attitude of continuous exploration and striving for excellence.
