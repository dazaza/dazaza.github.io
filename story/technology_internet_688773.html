Care suggestions to transform "dry hair" into beautiful hair, if followed seriously, can yield significant results:

Use an appropriate amount of hair care products, preferably only applying them to the surface of the hair without making the hair and scalp greasy. Excessive hair care products can clog pores and burden the hair.

Generally, wash the shampoo with slightly warm water and rinse the conditioner with cooler water because lower temperatures can help the outer layer scales of the hair contract moderately, assisting in locking in nutrients and moisture.

Avoid rubbing your hair with a towel forcefully, as this can make the hair more prone to splitting. Typically, wrap your hair with a soft, dry towel and gently press the scalp to let the towel absorb the water from the hair and scalp, giving the hair ample time to air-dry naturally.

If using a hairdryer, it's best to use a low-temperature, low-speed dryer while keeping the nozzle 20~30 centimeters away from the hair.
