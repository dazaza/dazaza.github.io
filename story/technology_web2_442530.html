"Cage Match" Surprisingly Won the Big Award, Best Film Award

Among the five nominated films, "Ip Man 2" indeed achieved a greater commercial success than the first one, but there was no bigger breakthrough in artistic standards. And since the first one had already won the Best Film Award, it was one of the films that were not favored from the beginning. The extremely polarized reputation of "Sword Rain" also determined its inability to win more awards. The two most powerful contenders for this award were the commercial blockbuster "Detective Dee and the Mystery of the Phantom Flame" directed by Tsui Hark and "Eye in the Sky" by Dante Lam. In the end, the highly impressive "Cage Match" surprisingly won the Best Film Award. This film was regarded as representing the spirit of Hong Kong cinema, and at its thirtieth anniversary, the biggest award was given to this movie.

With the decreasing production of pure Hong Kong films, the advantage of co-production films has become increasingly evident in the past few sessions of the Hong Kong Film Awards. While the Film Awards are becoming more open, how to not lose the original intention and role of encouraging and guiding the development of local films is also a question worth considering. Thus, the signal transmission and attitude expression symbol of "Cage Match" being able to be nominated and winning several important awards including the Best Film Award became even more significant.

On the evening of April 17, the 30th Hong Kong Film Awards ceremony was held at the Hong Kong Cultural Center. The opening ceremony looked back on the best film clips and the award-winning scenes of actors and actresses from the previous 29 editions of the Film Awards through the singing of actresses like QiQi, presenting the thirty-year journey of the Film Awards in the warmest way to the audience. Even in the subsequent tense award-giving session, historical footage was replayed multiple times, creating an atmosphere of nostalgia throughout the entire award ceremony. In the end, the film "Cage Match" surprisingly won the Best Film Award and also swept two major acting awards, Best Supporting Actor and Best Supporting Actress.

Tsui Hark Won the Best Director Award with "Detective Dee and the Mystery of the Phantom Flame"

The five directors nominated for the Best Film Award were also simultaneously competing for the Best Director Award. Tsui Hark won the Best Director Award with the popular film "Detective Dee and the Mystery of the Phantom Flame." The film is filled with Tsui Hark's signature free-wheeling narrative style and spatial imagination. Tsui Hark expressed his happiness to receive this award among so many nominees and thanked Nansun Shi for her support all along. This is my first award, and it has been a long time since the last one, but I feel that this award should be shared with them. I hope to share this attitude and spirit with you.

Tsui Hark
