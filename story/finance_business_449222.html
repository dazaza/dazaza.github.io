Laozi statue gilded with 33 kilograms of gold continues: developers exaggerate publicity to attract attention

by jassweew on 2011-06-20 22:08:24

Henan Lingbao city invested tens of millions yuan to build a giant statue of Laozi, and used purple copper forging and gold plating, which triggered wide controversy on the Internet. In this regard, the developer admitted in an interview with the reporter that the actual investment of Laozi statue was not so much as the outside publicity claimed. The reason for exaggerating the promotion was to attract attention through the stir, thereby forming a selling point for the scenic spot.

It is understood that the Laozi Holy Statue is part of the current enhancement project of Hangu Pass scenic area, which has been basically completed, and held an unveiling ceremony in March 2011. Recently, it was reported online that the completed Laozi statue was as high as 28.09 meters, weighing 60 tons, made of purple copper forging and gold plating, with a total investment of 25.88 million yuan, including as much as 33 kg of gold for the gold plating, which immediately aroused widespread doubts among netizens.

In this regard, Chen Rongliang, general manager of Hangu Pass Historical and Cultural Tourism Zone Management Co., Ltd., the implementer of the expansion project, told reporters that the total investment of Laozi Holy Statue was 18.2 million yuan, and the cost mainly included several parts such as design, materials, foundation and pedestal, installation construction and surface gold foil anti-corrosion treatment process fees.

This year is the 2582nd anniversary of Laozi's birth, but it seems not good to directly say the investment of 25.82 million yuan, so it became 25.88 million yuan. Chen Rongliang said that if the actual situation was said to be more than 10 million yuan, it would be difficult to attract people's attention.

As for the gold plating, Chen Rongliang said that since the holy image was made of purple copper, the surface gold foil anti-corrosion treatment process was adopted, and the expenditure for this item was 2.28 million yuan, which was only the cost of the process. In fact, there was no expenditure on gold.

The market price of gold is 360,000 yuan per kilogram. If 33 kilograms of gold are plated, the cost of this item alone would reach 11.98 million yuan. Chen Rongliang said that the claim of plating 33 kilograms of gold was to echo Laozi's saying that one becomes two, and two become three, thereby creating a stir.

Chen Rongliang admitted that since the scenic spot expansion project was independently operated and profit-and-loss bearing by the company, coupled with the funding problems encountered during the current construction, the operation was relatively difficult. Therefore, there was intentional exaggeration in the external publicity, hoping to attract attention in this way and create a selling point for the scenic spot.

Cheng Yunze, deputy minister of the Publicity Department of Lingbao City, told reporters that as a scenic spot within Lingbao City, Hangu Pass was both a historical military stronghold and the place where Laozi wrote "Tao Te Ching" in his day. For many years, the local area has always hoped to expand and strengthen this scenic spot around famous people, famous passes, and famous works.

Although this is an enterprise behavior, from the current perspective, this malicious hype behavior also brings negative impact to the local government, and the lesson is worth learning, Cheng Yunze said.

An Yimin, a researcher at the Institute of Philosophy of the Henan Academy of Social Sciences, believed that in essence, this kind of cultural stage-building and economic singing practice was a vulgarization of traditional culture. In fact, the role of culture should not be just stage-building, nor should it be limited to stage-building. To truly inherit and promote traditional culture, including Taoist spirit, we still have a lot to do.
