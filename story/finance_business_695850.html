The cost of studying abroad is an issue that many students and parents are concerned about. Many international students have no experience of independent living before they leave China, so they don't know much about daily expenses. Therefore, for students who are about to choose to study abroad, the amount of tuition and living expenses is also very important. Jinuohua International will give you a detailed introduction about the tuition and living expenses for studying in Canada. The tuition fee for studying in Quebec is 70,000 yuan, and the living expense is 70,000 yuan (RMB, per year). Students can apply for "High School Waiver Award" scholarships for master's degree programs in Quebec, with a ratio of 90% and an amount as high as 100,000 yuan. Therefore, the total cost for a two-year master's program is less than 150,000 yuan.
