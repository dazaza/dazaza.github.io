That single tear, so salty

Submitted online

A single tear flowed down from the corner of her left eye, slowly running down her cheek and into the corner of her mouth.

I tasted that tear, so salty, so spicy, so painful... In an instant, the sky blurred, the rainy night she went to find him blurred, and the figure lying in the heavy rain blurred...

She never woke up, having met with a car accident on her way to find her husband! He had turned off his phone to avoid her nagging while playing cards at a colleague's house all night. When he was sent home by his colleague after losing everything the next day, he saw her message when he turned on his phone: "Dear, today is our wedding anniversary, I'm coming to find you..."

He lost her, and the whole world.

It turns out, tears can be so painful, hurting as if the world were destroyed, as if floods were bursting forth!

When a person leaves, they can be so resolute!

Loving him, she gave her entire life.

In this lifetime, how is he supposed to repay her?

This is just a story, but I believe it's real, as if it happened right beside me.

The weather has been gloomy for several days, my heart following the weather without any light, until I read it this morning, and finally, it started raining...
