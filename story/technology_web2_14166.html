Digg-like sites claim the crown of the most investment-worthy site of Sci-Tech China in 2007.

by vbird on 2007-03-05 15:58:34

[Repost] After a voting process lasting more than three months, the Internet Lab's "Top 100 Chinese Websites with the Most Investment Value in 2007" voting event has come to an end. The web2.0 website Longhu Ranking (www.longhu123.com) from Shenzhen has claimed the top honor of the Internet Lab's "Top 100 Chinese Websites with the Most Investment Value in 2007". Other web2.0 websites that made it into the top ten include 58同城, Dianping, 51.com, and Fuma Corporate TV. The video website Six Rooms (www.6rooms.com) took the crown in the video category. Other websites in the top ten include marbar, verycd, Douban, and Juwang.

This event was guided by the Ministry of Information Industry, organized by the China Internet Association, and hosted by the Internet Lab. Co-organizers included Science and Technology China, Blogchina, Tsinghua Science Park, Beijing Youth Daily, and other organizations. The results of the selection were jointly recommended, evaluated, and voted on by experts and netizens. A total of more than 600 websites from all over the country were recommended for participation in the selection. By noon on March 1, 2007, when the voting ended, the statistics showed that a total of 2.56 million valid votes were received. These winning websites demonstrate innovation, unique advantages, high growth potential, and sustainable development capabilities in terms of technology, model, and brand, and are expected to become future stars of China's internet!

Since its official launch in November 2006, the event has received high attention and enthusiastic support from netizens across the country. Every day, a large number of media outlets, organizations, and individuals from all over the country have recommended the top 100 websites with the most investment value to the organizing committee via phone calls and emails. The event planner, Fang Xingdong, said: "The selection of the most investment-value websites has evolved from an ordinary selection event into a highly influential rookie selection event in the internet industry. The nationwide netizen voting is one of the factors that ensures the fairness and impartiality of the selection results."

The recommendation system of the "Top 100 Chinese Websites with the Most Investment Value in 2007" voting event is also an important guarantee for the success of the activity. The advisory committee for this event consists of 15 experts, scholars, and well-known figures from the IT and internet industries, including Ni Guangnan and Jiang Qiping. During the recommendation process, each advisor carefully filled out their evaluations of the candidate websites as well as their opinions and suggestions on the voting selection event itself, which were promptly fed back to the organizing committee.

Taking into account multiple factors such as the number of netizen votes, advisor opinions, and website teams, the organizing committee selected the "Top 100 Chinese Websites with the Most Investment Value in 2007". The award ceremony revealing the voting results was held at the Beijing International Convention Center on March 21, 2007. The award ceremonies for "Top 100 Influential Figures" and "Top 100 Websites That Influence China's Internet" will also be held simultaneously.
