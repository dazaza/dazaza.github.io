Wenchuan Story 'Paradise on Earth' Premieres in Xi'an, Writer Gets Emotional and Cries Before the Birthday - Chinese Film

by letaoedh on 2011-06-03 00:39:26

Xi'an people support their own, and Article's mother sends love-filled dumplings

Article's family came with a group of over 30 relatives and friends. Article's father, mother, and grandmother were all present at the scene. I felt that my son performed exceptionally well, portraying a foolish yet touching feeling. After watching the movie "Ocean Paradise," Article's mother showed no hesitation in expressing her love for her son and even went on stage to give him steaming hot dumplings. The word for dumplings is a homophone for "pride," and she expressed immense pride in her son!

At the premiere, the thoughtful organizers also prepared a birthday cake in advance for Article, who was about to have his birthday. When the host asked the media present to turn off their cameras and camcorders, saying that we had arranged a segment that we didn't want to be disturbed, the entire venue immediately darkened. Then, three autistic children, accompanied by their parents and teachers, approached Brother Article and said "Happy Birthday" in their innocent voices. From Article's surprised expression, it was clear that the organizers had successfully kept this touching birthday surprise from him. Article's tears streamed uncontrollably, and the audience was moved by this moment as well, wiping away their own tears. The atmosphere on site was deeply touching.

On the day of the premiere, the halls screening "Ocean Paradise" in Xi'an's five major cinemas were all fully seated. Many viewers and Article's fans who heard the news even waited outside the cinema, making tickets hard to come by. The cinema manager stated that this was the first time such a situation had occurred since "Avatar." Article is one of our own, so of course, we will fully support him. The audience expressed that not only because Article is from Xi'an, they also placed great importance on the content and theme of this movie, looking forward to being moved by it.

Autistic children celebrate Brother Article's birthday, moving him to tears

On June 18th, directed by Xue Xiaolu and starring Jet Li, Article, and Shu Qi, the emotional blockbuster of 2010, "Ocean Paradise," was warmly released nationwide. Recently, Director Xue Xiaolu and lead actor Article came to Article's hometown of Xi'an and together attended the premiere event held at the five major cinemas in Xi'an.
