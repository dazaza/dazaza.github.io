China's hacker industry uncovered: making 6 million overnight

by diglog on 2011-09-23 08:12:32

"My friend once made 6 million yuan in a single night by hacking into a database, and the next day he bought a sports car to show off." Li Qi, an advocate of the Chown Group (COG), told a reporter about the experience of hackers getting rich quickly. Li Qi, whose online name is Liwrml, is one of the founders of China's earliest hacker organization, the "Green Corps." Yesterday, the COG-2011 Information Security Forum, organized by the non-profit research institute Chown Group, which focuses on network information security, was held in Shanghai, with nearly 400 "hackers and information security professionals" participating, including leaders of China's top-level hacker organizations: Goodwell, founder of the Green Corps, Wanto, representative of the Hawk faction, Lion and Knownsec representative Li Qi. What is referred to as 'database scraping' is when hackers intrude into a website server to steal data within the database. According to Li Qi's statement, this is one of the top-tier hacker technologies and also the cutting-edge of the hacker industry.
