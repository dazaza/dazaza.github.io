8:47 AM

by lieencq09 on 2011-09-13 18:50:37

Chongqing Evening News reporter Yong-song Fan, intern Zhang-hao Liu, correspondents Ping Xiong and Xing-qiang Zhu, photography report

When he saw his grandson about to fall into a 15-meter deep well at any moment, Li Yongfu quickly knelt down and grabbed his grandson's left hand, trying to pull him out of the wellhead. Unfortunately, the wellhead was too small, and the child was stuck too far away. The old man could only hold onto three fingers of his grandson, unable to drag him out of the wellhead for business investigation. To prevent the child from falling, the elderly man could only tightly hold on, and the situation remained in a stalemate.

"Help! Come and save my grandson!" At around 8 o'clock, Grandma Liu from Group 4, Zhonghua Village, You Ting Town shouted. Her husband Li Yongfu hurriedly put down his hoe and ran home in shock: their 1-year-old grandson Jiajia accidentally fell into the deep well beside the water tank while playing in the kitchen. His thin body was stuck not far from the wellhead, crying loudly in fear.

Firefighters immediately split into two groups and took turns working, using picks and electric drills to dig holes to save people. To prevent soil from loosening and falling into the deep well, the firefighters brought iron rings to fix the walls of the deep well and used safety ropes to tie Jiajia's left hand to prevent him from falling into the deeper part.

At 12:40 PM, after four hours of hard work, little Jiajia was successfully rescued from the well. Due to the long duration of rescue operations and the dense air inside the well, the doctors on site successively administered 4 bags of oxygen to Jiajia, ensuring that Jiajia had no life-threatening dangers despite being trapped for a long time.

At 8:47 AM, the Dazu County Fire Brigade rushed to the scene. After exploration, only Jiajia's head and hands were visible. The diameter of the wellhead was only 23 centimeters, and adults basically couldn't drill into the water well to rescue Jiajia. The only method was to dig a hole next to the wellhead to save the child, but it was necessary to prevent the collapse of the well wall which could endanger the safety of both the child and the grandfather.

The scene of the fire rescue operation

Chongqing Evening News reported that yesterday morning, in Group 4, Zhonghua Village, You Ting Town, Dazu County, a 1-year-old child accidentally slipped into the family's deep well. Due to the narrow wellhead, the child was stuck near the wellhead with his body suspended in mid-air. The grandfather returned and tightly held onto his grandson's little hand for four hours without letting go. Finally, the firefighters came and successfully saved the child.
