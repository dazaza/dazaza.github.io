Rose Wine for Liver Regulation and Depression Relief

There are many methods for making rose wine circulating online, which is regarded as a holy grail for women's beauty. Zeng Huifang, the chief pharmacist of the First Affiliated Hospital of Guangzhou University of Chinese Medicine, introduced that roses have the functions of regulating qi, relieving depression, harmonizing liver and stomach, and activating blood circulation to relieve pain. Many female friends often worry about spots and melasma on their faces. These conditions are often related to liver stagnation and qi stagnation, accompanied by chest and rib pain, irregular menstruation, or with chest tightness, qi stagnation, sighing, depression, etc. In addition, many women encounter breast hyperplasia and other breast diseases, which are related to liver qi stagnation. These conditions are suitable for drinking rose wine.

The method for making rose wine is very simple. Take 350 grams of fresh roses and 1500 milliliters of yellow wine, soak the roses in the wine. Since the rose petals are very thin, their components easily seep out, so it can be drunk after soaking for a few days. Its efficacy is relatively mild.

Donkey Hide Gelatin Wine for Blood Replenishment and Moisturization

Donkey hide gelatin has good effects of replenishing blood, stopping bleeding, nourishing yin, and moisturizing dryness. It is also one of the best supplements for women to strengthen their bodies and beautify their skin. Donkey hide gelatin is especially suitable for women with irregular menstruation. Blood deficiency can be replenished with donkey hide gelatin, excessive blood can be regulated, and insufficient blood can be promoted. Besides, there is a theory that donkey hide gelatin is rich in protein degradation components, which replenishes blood and moisturizes the skin, benefiting skin health. After taking it, the complexion becomes rosy, the skin becomes fine and lustrous with good elasticity, and it also has certain effects on removing spots, so it is also used for skin care and beauty.

Every day, you can use 25 milliliters of yellow wine, put 3-9 grams of donkey hide gelatin into the wine, heat it until melted, and then drink it. It can be divided into two doses in the morning and evening.

Four Substances Wine for Blood Replenishment and Menstrual Regulation

Many women are familiar with "Four Substances Decoction", which consists of angelica, rehmannia, ligusticum, and peony as the "four main ingredients". It is a classic prescription in traditional Chinese medicine for replenishing blood and regulating menstruation. In addition to boiling it, it can also be used to make wine. Many women are prone to dysmenorrhea due to blood deficiency, have a sallow complexion lacking redness, and are prone to cold hands and feet in winter. All these symptoms can be alleviated by soaking these four herbs in wine. Persistent drinking can activate blood circulation, remove blood clots, reduce menstrual pain, improve anemia, and make hands and feet no longer cold. When qi and blood flow smoothly, the complexion naturally becomes rosy, the skin smoother, and one looks several years younger!

The method for making Four Substances Wine is to use 1000 milliliters of yellow wine or white wine, each of angelica, rehmannia, ligusticum, and peony at 50 grams, and soak them for a month before drinking.

Four Gentlemen Wine for Qi Replenishment and Spleen Strengthening

"Four Gentlemen Decoction", with party ginseng, fried white atractylodes, poria, and honey-fried licorice as the main ingredients, is also a classic prescription in pharmacopoeia. Its main function is to replenish qi and strengthen the spleen. Urban women, due to high work pressure and frequent staying up late, often suffer from qi deficiency. They feel tired in the afternoon at work, lack sufficient energy when speaking, have poor appetite, eat little, and find it difficult to form stools. "Four Gentlemen" can be boiled and drunk, or used to make wine. For women with pale complexion and weak spleen and stomach, it can significantly improve the condition.

The method for making Four Gentlemen Wine is to use 1000 milliliters of yellow wine or white wine, each of party ginseng 60 grams, fried white atractylodes 60 grams, poria 60 grams, and honey-fried licorice 30 grams, and soak them for a month before drinking.

TIPS

What kind of alcohol can be used to make medicinal wine?

For preparing medicinal wine, generally well-brewed baijiu (i.e., qujiu, white dry, sorghum wine, distilled spirits), huangjiu, or rice wine favored by southerners is used as the base wine. Among them, sorghum-brewed baijiu and glutinous rice-brewed huangjiu are the best. The higher the alcohol content, the better the extraction effect. Usually, 40-60 degree high-alcohol dissolves and extracts the effective components of the herbs more effectively in a shorter time, and the efficacy of the medicinal wine is also better. However, for women who do not drink well, using low-alcohol wine with 20-40 degrees is also acceptable.

How much should be consumed daily? When to drink?

Medicinal wine is not about quantity, but persistence. The general method of drinking medicinal wine is to consume 10-30 milliliters each time, 2-3 times a day (such as within one hour before meals, within 30 minutes after meals, or within 30 minutes before sleeping).

People with small alcohol tolerance can add appropriate rock sugar (or honey), or mix with warm water in a 1:10 ratio, stir evenly before drinking. People allergic to alcohol cannot drink medicinal wine.

Can medicinal wine be consumed during menstruation?

Women should avoid drinking medicinal wine during their menstrual period, especially those with blood-activating and qi-replenishing effects, as they may cause excessive menstruation. Additionally, stop taking it if you have a cold, fever, sore throat, or bronchitis. Also, people with hypertension, heart disease, liver disease, and severe gastric disease should use it cautiously.
