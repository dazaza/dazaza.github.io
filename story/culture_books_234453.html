The adventure story of human-ghost romance: Twilight

by jqrxass on 2009-09-07 15:33:57

Bella exiled herself to Forks, a remote and perpetually rainy small town. She could never have imagined that this decision would lead her to meet him at the crossroads of fate. The moment their eyes met, they both understood that what awaited them was not only the allure of happiness but also the abyss of danger. Swinging between love and peril, the two spent each fresh and stimulating day together, like long-awaited sunlight falling on the romantic Twilight City.

Since he possesses a charming appearance, superhuman strength, and the ability to see through people's hearts, who exactly is he? And she is just an ordinary girl with poor athletic coordination and pale skin that looks sickly. They were both seventeen when they met. Time stood still for him, but it raced forward for her. His youth will last forever, while hers will eventually decay. Do their worlds truly intersect? Since Adam and Eve ate the forbidden fruit, faced with the vortex of intertwined love and hatred, humanity has always been at a loss.

Happiness advances slowly, while storms lie in wait. When a fragile girl tries to integrate into a world of extraordinary beings, the dangers she faces are far more challenging than kissing a benevolent devil. At twilight after the beautiful daylight, this tumultuous adventure story is just beginning.

Twilight - Part One

The sight of Edward in the sunlight was astonishing. Although I had been staring at him all afternoon, I still couldn't get used to it. Even though his skin was slightly reddened from yesterday's hunt, it was still very fair, almost radiant, as if embedded with countless tiny diamonds. He lay motionless on the grass, his shirt open, exposing his healthy, clean chest and white arms. His shiny pale purple eyelids were closed, although he wasn't asleep, lying there like a statue cast from marble as smooth and crystal-clear as glass.

Twilight - Part Two

When Carlisle spoke, three things seemed to happen simultaneously: my hair moved in the breeze, Edward froze, and the second man among them, James, suddenly shook his head back and forth, staring at me with flared nostrils.

James suddenly took a step forward and crouched down, everyone immediately stunned. Edward bared his teeth, crouched into a defensive posture, and emitted a fierce growl from his throat. It was not the playful sound I heard this morning; it was the scariest sound I had ever heard, making me feel cold from head to toe.

Twilight - Part Three

Edward suddenly appeared by my side, firmly grabbing me and pressing me tightly against him. He pulled my face close to his, lifting me up so my feet left the ground, seemingly unaware that his family was watching. His icy and hard lips briefly touched mine before letting go. He set me down but still held my face in his hands, his captivating gaze emanating a fiery light that pierced through my eyes.
