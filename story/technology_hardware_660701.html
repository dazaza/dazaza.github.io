Agricultural Products Brand Marketing Strategy

by sggotl38 on 2012-02-08 09:43:31

Agricultural product marketing is related to the value realization of agricultural products, and it is an important part in narrowing the income gap between urban residents, improving people's living standards, and realizing the industrialized operation of agriculture. By analyzing the feasibility and necessity of implementing brand marketing for agricultural products in our country, strategies for developing the brand marketing of China's agricultural products are proposed accordingly.

Keywords: Agricultural Products, Brand Marketing, Feasibility, Necessity, Strategy

In more than 30 years since the reform and opening up, with the transformation of China's agricultural management system, the widespread application of agricultural science and technology, and the acceleration of adjustment of the agricultural industrial structure, agricultural productivity has greatly improved, the scale of the agricultural product market has continued to expand, and the supply of agricultural products has shifted from previous overall shortages and demand exceeding supply to the current situation of oversupply, basically evolving into a buyer's market. At the same time, after joining the WTO, China's agricultural product market has become increasingly open, with large amounts of foreign agricultural products flooding into China, making the commoditization and marketization degree of domestic agricultural products continue to rise, and market competition becoming increasingly fierce, bringing new challenges to the marketing of China’s agricultural products continuously. Agricultural product marketing not only concerns the issue of value realization of agricultural products but also plays a significant role in increasing farmers' income levels, improving the quality of life for urban and rural residents, narrowing the income gap between urban and rural areas, and better achieving social equity.

1. The Practical Significance of Implementing Agricultural Product Branding

In the era of economic shortage, agricultural products were typical products without branding characteristics. With the continuous development of the economy, the increasing abundance of material living resources, and the continuous improvement of people's living standards, the pursuit of green brand consumption of agricultural products has become a trend of the times. Currently, a considerable portion of China's agricultural product production enterprises are overly dispersed and small in scale, have backward production technology, weak competitive capabilities, and find it difficult to form their own advantages, and the quality of their products is even harder to ensure to a certain extent. After entering the WTO, compared with the prices of foreign agricultural products, China's agricultural product prices are still relatively low, and the quality lacks certain standards, making it difficult to form its own brands. Therefore, implementing a branding strategy for agricultural products is of great significance in stabilizing agricultural product prices and enhancing the competitiveness of agricultural products themselves.

2. Economic Analysis of Agricultural Product Brands

Currently, the market is full of well-known industrial brands, while due to their own characteristics, agricultural products face certain difficulties in forming brands. Compared with general industrial product brands, the formation of agricultural product brands is influenced by the following factors:

1. The Characteristics of Agricultural Production and Agricultural Products Themselves on Supply and Demand in the Agricultural Product Market

Agricultural production is a process of mutual interweaving of natural reproduction and economic reproduction, significantly affected by natural conditions, mainly reflected in:

(1) Strong regional nature. Generally speaking, industrial products, due to their own production characteristics - usually produced indoors - have less regional nature. However, agricultural products are influenced by local soil, climate, and other natural conditions, resulting in unique characteristics in color, flavor, aroma, taste, and appearance. Therefore, we should develop local agricultural products according to local conditions, vigorously develop local famous, excellent, and special products, thereby creating local branded agricultural products.

(2) Strong seasonality. Generally speaking, the production of industrial products is not much affected by the production season. However, agricultural products are different. Due to the influence of natural climatic conditions on production in different seasons, suitable agricultural products are produced in different seasons. Although technological development has made some improvements in the seasonal aspects of agricultural product production, this situation remains in the minority, and most agricultural products' production is still affected and constrained by seasons. Thus, due to the cyclical nature of agricultural product production, it is difficult to ensure the continuity of supply in the market, which will restrict the formation of agricultural product brands.

From the above analysis, it can be seen that there may be certain difficulties in the formation of agricultural product brands, but this precisely provides possibilities for the formation of branded agricultural products. In the current era of relatively abundant agricultural product production, agricultural products can also become famous brands. For example, the world-famous agricultural product Nestle Coffee had a brand value of $10.37 billion in 1997, ranking 14th among the world's most valuable brands, occupying a certain position in the world brands.

3. Agricultural Product Brand Marketing Strategies

Generally speaking, the more mature industrial product brand strategies mainly include brand naming, quality assurance, multi-branding, brand advertising, brand positioning, brand differentiation, etc. As for China's agricultural products, attention should be paid to the following aspects:

(1) First, determine the market positioning of the brand

China has a vast territory spanning several climate zones, with significant differences in climate and temperature. Agricultural product brands should be developed appropriately based on local conditions, producing distinctive agricultural products according to target markets. Due to the individual characteristics of agricultural products—greater individual differences—the selection of target markets for agricultural products differs from industrial products. Generally, agricultural product prices are relatively lower, and the profit per unit product is naturally not high, so it can only achieve the purpose of expanding profits by increasing absolute sales volume. Therefore, the target market should choose the middle-income class in cities. Only in this way can a certain total profit be achieved, market share increased, and one's advantages highlighted in competition, thereby forming one's own brand status.

(2) Pay Attention to the Quality of Agricultural Product Brands

After determining the target market, organize production while ensuring quality meets the corresponding standards after entering the WTO. Currently, although China has green food standards, as one of the WTO member countries, we must strictly follow the SPS agreement standards required by the World Trade Organization in organizing production, adopting all standard-compliant production materials and raw materials, and continuously improving our environmental conditions to meet the corresponding standards. We should also note: first, focus on the agricultural industry, strengthen the cultivation of superior varieties of crops, livestock, and aquatic products, optimize variety structures, and form specialized varieties with unique characteristics. Second, strengthen research and development work on crop tillage reforms, precise sowing, formula fertilization, water-saving irrigation, and livestock, poultry, and fish feeding supporting technologies, ensuring the production of nutritious, safe, high-quality agricultural products.

(3) Actively Develop Marketing Channels for Branded Agricultural Products

For China's agricultural products, strong marketing measures can be taken during the sales process, fully leveraging the marketing network advantages of circulation enterprises—especially large chain operating enterprises' supermarkets. Large well-known chain operating enterprises and supermarkets are one of the best channels for agricultural product sales. From an economic perspective, chain enterprises and supermarkets can form economies of scale, reduce the intermediate distribution links of agricultural products, give agricultural products a certain price advantage in competition, further allow economic information to be widely disseminated, and enable agricultural products to reduce advertising costs to a certain extent, forming price competitive advantages.

4. Multi-Brand Strategy

When enterprises choose what kind of agricultural products as their brands, they should consider the market potential of the selected agricultural products and adopt a certain multi-brand strategy, but not too many, otherwise, it will disperse the enterprise's funds and reduce its competitiveness in the market. They can choose a few past combination-named local products that are now not famous brands but can form a certain scale production advantage as leading brands to drive the entire enterprise's development.

5. Government Support Policies

Since agricultural products belong to typical traditional industries, developed countries generally adopt policies to maintain agricultural product prices and provide subsidies to agriculture. As a developing country, more support policies should be adopted for agriculture, supporting the construction of agricultural enterprise organizations led by brand production, guiding enterprises through the market, forming farmer production professional groups, and conducting scaled production and operation. First, establish professional agricultural production associations where households voluntarily participate and must fulfill the responsibilities of the agricultural association while enjoying services provided by the agricultural association. Second, establish shareholding origin branded agricultural product wholesale markets, allowing farmers to directly enter the market and adjust production according to market demand. The government should provide necessary policy support for agricultural trade organizations. Third, through the establishment of agricultural enterprise organizations, form an operational model of companies + farmers, where producers and consumers jointly bear the risks of production and operation according to the principles of shareholding, strengthen the fine processing of agricultural products, reinforce product quality, improve product packaging, and increase market share. Fourth, the government should establish corresponding agricultural product testing centers in certain regions, enabling farmers to promptly understand the relevant standards of products and organize production in time.

III. Feasibility of Agricultural Product Brand Marketing

1. China's rich agricultural product resources are the realistic basis for implementing agricultural product brand marketing strategies. China has vast national land area, rich agricultural product resources, numerous varieties, and large output. Most places have characteristic agricultural products relying on their own geographical advantages, and many places have formed local characteristic agricultural industries, such as Hami melons from Xinjiang, apples from Yantai, Longjing tea from West Lake, Pu'er tea from Yunnan, etc., all of which are renowned agricultural products both domestically and internationally. If these agricultural product resources are packaged and integrated, and promotional efforts are intensified, shaping representative and well-known agricultural product brands is not a difficult task to accomplish.

2. Farmers have the practical need to implement agricultural product brand strategies. After more than 30 years of rapid development since the reform and opening up, China's agricultural productivity has greatly improved, production has rapidly developed, accompanied by increases in farmers' income and improvements in living standards, but compared with urban residents, there is still a significant gap. How to realize farmers' income growth and prosperity has always been an important issue of concern to the Party and government. Since 2004, the central No. 1 document has focused on "agriculture, rural areas, and farmers" issues for seven consecutive years, showing the state's emphasis on agricultural production increases and farmers' income increases. In the long run, implementing agricultural product brand strategies is the correct path to increase farmers' income levels, improve their quality of life, and achieve social fairness.

3. Government support is a strong guarantee for implementing agricultural product brand marketing strategies. In recent years, the Ministry of Agriculture has successively issued documents such as "Several Opinions of the Ministry of Agriculture on Creating Famous Agricultural Products" (1999) and "Opinions of the Ministry of Agriculture on Further Promoting Agricultural Brand Work" (2006), guiding the creation and promotion of agricultural product brands. Governments at all levels and relevant competent departments have also strongly promoted the implementation of agricultural product brand marketing, providing reasonable guidance, and giving strong support in terms of policies, laws, and funds, thus effectively ensuring the progress of agricultural product brand marketing work.

IV. Necessity of Agricultural Product Brand Marketing

1. Farmers' income growth and prosperity require the implementation of agricultural product brand marketing. Implementing brand marketing strategies helps increase farmers' income levels, narrow the income gap between urban and rural residents, improve farmers' quality of life, and better achieve social fairness. The implementation of brand marketing strategies brings about an increase in the added value of agricultural products, and the sales prices of agricultural products will be higher than ordinary agricultural products. Since most agricultural products are necessities of life, their price elasticity is very small, and consumption volumes often remain stable, without drastic fluctuations. Moreover, with the enhancement of people's brand awareness, the demand for branded agricultural products is also increasing, and the simultaneous increase in quantity and price will inevitably lead to an increase in farmers' income and improvement in their quality of life, positively contributing to the development of the socialist new countryside construction cause and the building of a harmonious socialist society.

2. Expanding domestic demand and driving economic growth requires the implementation of agricultural product brand marketing. Implementing brand marketing strategies helps tap the consumption potential of agricultural products, improve the consumption level of agricultural products, and stimulate the consumer demand for agricultural products. With the improvement of people's living standards, people's consumption concepts and consumption methods are constantly changing, paying more attention to consumption quality, and consumer demands are moving towards high-end and branded developments. Consumers' demands for agricultural products have shifted from simple quantity pursuits to quality demands, paying more attention to the nutritional value and green environmental protection aspects of agricultural products, thereby satisfying their psychological needs. Due to the homogeneity characteristics of agricultural products, implementing brand marketing strategies can better distinguish them from general agricultural products in the market, meeting consumers' brand demands, and achieving an increase in product market share. It can be foreseen that with the continuous improvement of people's living standards, the demand for branded agricultural products with good quality assurance will grow larger, and more branded agricultural products will appear on the tables of ordinary people, implying business opportunities that are self-evident.

3. Enhancing the market competitiveness of agricultural products and better responding to the impact of imported agricultural products require the implementation of agricultural product brand marketing strategies. Implementing brand marketing strategies helps enhance the market competitiveness of agricultural products and better respond to the market impact of imported agricultural products, thereby achieving the goals of going global and enhancing the international image of Chinese agriculture. Branded agricultural products generally go through strict cultivation, screening, processing, and packaging processes, and the final finished products undergo multiple inspections, ensuring good product quality. Moreover, due to market promotion of the brand, the added value of the product is enhanced, distinguishing it from ordinary agricultural products, naturally improving the market competitiveness of the product and enhancing its ability to resist market risks, thereby better responding to the market impact of imported agricultural products and participating better in international agricultural product competitions, significantly enhancing the international image of Chinese agriculture.

4. Optimizing and upgrading the agricultural industrial structure requires the implementation of agricultural product brand marketing. Through the implementation of brand marketing strategies, pre-production, mid-production, and post-production departments in agriculture will accelerate separation from the industry, forming integrated operations of production, supply, and sales, planting, breeding, and processing, and agriculture, industry, and trade, forming a complete industrial system. The division of labor and cooperation between various departments will become more detailed, thus promoting the advancement of agricultural productivity levels.

V. Strategies for Developing Agricultural Product Brand Marketing

1. Oriented by market demand, based on household operations, led by leading enterprises, promote the branding process. Oriented by market demand indicates the market positioning issue after implementing agricultural product marketing. Implementing brand marketing strategies can distinguish specific consumer groups from many consumers, making related publicity and promotion work proceed better and smoother, enabling products to be distinguished from ordinary agricultural products, helping to expand market share and occupy advantageous market opportunities. At the same time, brand promotion work cannot be carried out by individual households and requires cooperation with capable leading enterprises, enabling the work to proceed smoothly, forming a win-win situation.

2. Enhance brand awareness, strictly ensure quality, shape brand images. Due to the reasons of the quality of China's agricultural producers themselves, the overall brand awareness of products is not strong. Brand awareness affects the maintenance and promotion of products in the market. Therefore, local governments should increase education for farmers, improve their quality, and make them realize the importance of brands. Once farmers have brand awareness, they will pay attention to the quality of agricultural products, focusing on all aspects of cultivation, screening, processing, and sales of agricultural products, doing well in brand and trademark registration work, ensuring the product's image. Over time, the product's brand image will naturally improve, forming its own intangible value.

3. Accelerate the construction of marketing channels for branded agricultural products. In recent years, with the establishment of various agricultural product information websites, agricultural market information has been disseminated through major websites, TV, journals, newspapers, radio, and other media, and farmers have more channels to obtain relevant information, all of which can become intermediary channels for agricultural product brand marketing. Meanwhile, the emergence of modern large-scale chain-operated supermarkets makes agricultural product brand marketing have the best channel. Cooperating with major agricultural product professional websites and chain supermarkets helps gain economies of scale, occupy advantageous market positions, and deserves attention.

4. The government should increase its support for agricultural product brand marketing. The government should increase its support for agricultural product brand promotion enterprises, giving preferential treatment in brand and trademark registration, taxation, reducing related fees for enterprises, and providing some policy and market guidance to help enterprises grow stronger. Guide the establishment of specialized agricultural product production cooperatives and agricultural cooperative enterprises, encourage farmers to invest assets, technology, land, etc., conduct cooperation, carry out professional production technology training and marketing training, and improve their market competitiveness. After enterprises grow stronger, they can not only increase local fiscal revenue and bring economic vitality but also lead more farmers onto the path of wealth accumulation. Therefore, government departments should spare no effort to do a good job in supporting work.
