Innovate life, share dreams

by shouiz56 on 2010-04-11 22:30:14

On May 5, 2008, the cooperation launch ceremony of CITIC Bank - Tencent QQ UnionPay Standard Credit Card was held in Shenzhen. CITIC Bank joined hands with Tencent Technology Company and China UnionPay to make a new push into the online credit card domain. The picture shows the host of the press conference, short track speed skating world champion Yang Yang. Tencent Finance exclusively live-streamed this press conference. Below is the speech by晨晔 Xu Chenye, Tencent's Chief Information Officer at the press conference.

Dear respected guests, friends from the media:

Good afternoon!

I am very glad to be here with all of you today to witness the launch of the QQ credit card, jointly introduced by Tencent Company, CITIC Bank, and China UnionPay. On behalf of Tencent Company, I would like to extend a warm welcome to all of you for your presence!

The Internet industry and the financial industry have always been considered important representatives of modern service industries. This is not only because both industries possess high added value, are knowledge-intensive, and have strong industrial driving capabilities, but more importantly, both industries have powerful innovation capabilities. Innovation in the financial industry is reflected in the significant enhancement of banks' competitiveness and service levels, while innovation in the Internet industry is reflected in the transformation of traditional lifestyles. Today, we three companies have come together, hoping that our innovations can form a synergy and provide the most suitable financial services for China's Internet users.

Our cooperation will first be reflected at the level of brand innovation. QQ is one of the most influential brands under Tencent. It covers more than 90% of Chinese Internet users and has great influence among mainstream users. The QQ credit card launched through our three-party cooperation will focus on sports, reflecting mainstream users' pursuit of sports, health, and fashion. This will be our positive sharing of the same dream concept of 2008.

Secondly, our cooperation will also be reflected in the field of business innovation. We will conduct in-depth exploration in the online payment domain, combining modern financial services. Through Tencent's payment tool Tenpay and e-commerce platform Paipai.com, we will focus on developing emerging e-commerce businesses, fully utilizing the diversified payment methods of credit cards to create a full-line payment experience and personal financial management services for users from online to offline.

Finally, our cooperation will also be reflected in the innovation of concepts. As our theme today suggests, "Creating and Sharing for Win-Win", we hope that through cross-industry cooperation between strong players, we can jointly provide the best service experience for users.

Wishing Tencent Company's cooperation with CITIC Bank and China UnionPay a complete success!

Thank you all!
