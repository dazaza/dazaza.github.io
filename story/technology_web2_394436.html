Female college students make enough 300,000 yuan in 5 years through their self-employment project of opening online shops

by anonymous on 2011-03-28 10:27:03

A female college student's independent entrepreneurship project: Starting from her second year in university in 2005, she began to open an online store and independently started a business. She operated Shenyang specialty foods, Korean and Japanese leather bags, lingerie, and other clothing items. In five years, the initial capital of 500 yuan for this college student's independent entrepreneurship project has rapidly grown to over 300,000 yuan.

Among more than 2 million online stores, most people are still striving to gain a few more diamonds (a rating system on Taobao). However, Suddenly's Shop has grown from a single red heart to the current double crown, which is indeed a miracle. A 26-year-old girl, without hiring anyone to artificially increase her ratings or advertising on Taobao, has almost entirely relied on her own efforts to build up a small shop bit by bit. It has now become a well-known specialty store on Taobao. How did she succeed? What experiences can she share with readers? Yesterday, our reporter visited Suddenly's small shop at VIP.com Information Technology Co., Ltd., and interviewed her face-to-face.

College Student Independent Entrepreneurship Project: Starting in the Second Year

Suddenly's small shop is located in a 60-square-meter apartment on the first floor of a residential complex near the Shenyang Real Estate Building. There are many plastic mannequins in the room, wearing down jackets that are newly arrived stock.

In her second year of university, Suddenly registered this online store called "Suddenly's Specialty Search Store" online.

"At that time, I just randomly came up with the name; it didn't have any special meaning. Initially, the store only resold Shenyang specialties like Bu Lao Lin candy and other local delicacies. I bought samples myself, took pictures, uploaded them to the online store, and when out-of-town netizens placed orders, I would buy the goods from Carrefour, at a cost price of 7.8 yuan per bag, and sell them for 8.8 yuan per bag, then mail them to the customers. In the first month, I actually made more than 100 yuan, and I was extremely excited. Later, I added more varieties to the store, such as export trade bags and clothing, and the number of customers also increased. After half a year, I could make over 1,000 yuan a month. At that time, I provided free delivery within Shenyang city, taking the bus to deliver the goods personally. Although it was busy, my heart was full of joy," Suddenly said.

College Student Independent Entrepreneurship Project: Opening an Online Store with Pocket Money

During her studies, she only invested 20% of her energy and could earn more than 1,000 yuan a month. If she devoted herself fully after graduation, she would surely earn even more. As graduation approached, while many classmates were busy looking for jobs, Suddenly decided to focus on running her online store after graduation. Unexpectedly, this idea was rejected by her parents first.

"My parents believed that opening an online store wouldn't be sustainable, especially for a girl, it would be even harder. Finding a stable job and working regularly would be better. But I am someone who is very stubborn; once I make a decision, I won't easily change it," Suddenly said. With no financial support from her parents, she saved money from her pocket money. She reinvested the profits from her online store, and by the time she graduated from university in 2006, the level of her online store had already reached three diamonds, with monthly sales exceeding ten thousand yuan, and monthly income reaching 2,000 yuan.

College Student Independent Entrepreneurship Project: A "Housebound Girl" Earning Over Ten Thousand Yuan Monthly

Suddenly said that opening an online store has low costs, and currently, there is no need to pay taxes temporarily. There is no need to rent a shop on bustling commercial streets; finding a house on an ordinary residential area's first floor would suffice. One can buy a computer to go online and open a store, and it can also serve as a warehouse. She handles all aspects herself: purchasing goods, taking photos, editing and uploading posts, chatting with customers, and packaging and delivering goods. She works over 10 hours a day, and currently sends out about 50 packages a day.

She operates specialty items, sometimes personally going to Qingdao and Hangzhou to purchase goods, ensuring quality and authenticity. Last November, her monthly sales even exceeded 60,000 yuan.

There is always a return for effort. Currently, Suddenly's business is thriving, and her monthly salary exceeds ten thousand yuan. The initial investment of 500 yuan has snowballed into over 300,000 yuan. She has just become the Shenyang agent for a clothing company in Shandong, purchased a batch of winter clothes, and is preparing to work hard during the autumn and winter seasons.
