Steps/Methods:

1. Enrollment: Submit all required materials - original ID card (we need the original ID card because the vehicle management office needs to verify it), leave contact information. Non-local residents need to obtain a temporary residence permit (the household registration is based on the valid ID card location, except for Beijing, all others are non-local. A temporary residence permit in Beijing for more than one year is required). Note: Some students have transferred their ID cards but haven't updated the address yet, so they need to apply for a temporary ID card at the police station. Submit the archives of other types of driver's licenses (so if you have other types of driver's licenses, remember to inform the school during enrollment, otherwise submitting the archive cannot handle the acceptance business). After taking photos and recording fingerprints, the school will print out the application form and physical examination form for you to sign personally, then go to the designated place for a physical examination. After passing, submit the invoice, pay all fees, and receive practical learning materials. You can read books at home and take online practice exams. During this period, the school needs to organize student files and submit them to the vehicle management office for acceptance, which takes about a week.

2. Subject One Traffic Regulations Exam: About a week later, the school will notify you to take the theoretical exam of subject one. Arrive at the designated location on time according to the notification (our driving school roll call is in the opposite comprehensive teaching area of the office building) to wait for roll call and distribution of ID cards (if unable to attend due to certain matters, please inform the school when notified). Then wait for the exam, during which staff will inform you of exam precautions, listen carefully. During the exam, check whether the information on the screen matches your own, there will be camera surveillance and random photo-taking during the exam, make sure not to move your face away from the camera, if the photo does not meet the requirements, the score is invalid. After the exam, report the score to the school so they can arrange driving practice or retake.

3. Subject Two Pile Test Practice and Exam: Subject two is divided into pile test and nine-item site training. According to your own time, book lessons with the school. In the first two days since it's the foundation stage, it's best to participate continuously for more complete instruction by the coach. Thereafter, inform the coach of your available times. The pile test exam time is ten days after passing subject one, and requires advance booking. Before the exam, the coach will ask if you want to participate in this session. Generally, after practicing for five days, you can take the exam. If you can schedule both practice and exam, you can sign up for the reservation. If unable, please inform the school. The pile test is an electronic pile test, before the exam, the coach will inform you of precautions and conduct simulated electronic pile practice, which is very important (electronic pile practice is mostly done in the evening, participation is highly recommended. Except for our school where simulation practice is possible, many other schools do not offer any opportunities for practice). Familiarizing yourself with the site significantly improves the pass rate because changing venues may cause discomfort. Simulation practice helps identify differences between the exam site and the practice site, often a small detail can help you pass smoothly. When taking the exam, arrive early at the waiting area, enter the exam venue in order, verify your ID and take photos before getting in the car (you must carry your ID card for each subsequent exam, don't forget).

After passing the exam, wait until all batch mates finish the exam, sign the exam result sheet, and then leave.

4. Subject Two Site Nine-Item Practice and Exam: The nine items include side parking, continuous curves, undulating roads, right-angle turns, speed-limited width gates (these five items are easier to control), hill start, continuous obstacles, single bridge, and hundred-meter gear shifting. Side parking (mandatory item), continuous curves, undulating roads, and right-angle turns can be passed smoothly by controlling the speed slowly following the methods. When passing through limited-width gates, attention should be paid to not over-turning the steering wheel. Driving skills and test techniques will be explained in detail by the coach. Hill start (mandatory item) testing techniques will also be explained in detail by the coach. Continuous obstacles require repeated practice and passage by feel. To improve the pass rate, don't rely on alignment for passing. For the single bridge, the first bridge can be passed using alignment methods, while the second bridge requires feeling the position of the right wheel. Even if you can't feel it, the coach will have mechanical methods to help you pass (single bridges are very useful for actual driving, it's recommended to practice feeling the right wheel position as much as possible). Hundred-meter gear shifting can be easily achieved by long-term repetitive monotonous practice following the coach's instructions.

5. Subject Three Road Test: Through the practice of the nine items, students basically master the driving of cars. Road tests focus on safety awareness based on driving skills, such as deceleration, observation, and horn warning when passing various intersections, stations, pedestrian crossings... During practice, listen carefully to the coach's explanation of precautions and memorize the test requirements. Many experienced old drivers fail the test while inexperienced beginners pass because they are too confident and act unprofessionally, whereas beginners dare to act cautiously without making mistakes, thus meeting the test requirements and passing smoothly.

Precautions: Practice more and think more during driving practice, most people can pass in one attempt.
