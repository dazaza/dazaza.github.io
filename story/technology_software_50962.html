The world's largest company for fiber optic sensing

by micronoptics on 2008-03-05 10:08:04

Micron Optics (Micro Optics, abbreviated as MOI), is a leading American company in the field of tunable optical technology. The company provides comprehensive services and device/instrument solutions for the sensing, bio, and telecom markets.

For 16 years since its establishment, Micron Optics has been committed to providing high-precision, high-resolution, high-transmission performance, and dynamic range tunable optical technology and products. Innovative products based on this patented tunable optical technology include: tunable filters, scanning lasers, tunable optical modules, and optical test instruments, which are respectively used in fiber optic sensing, biological, and optical communication systems.

Fiber optic sensing products can be applied in energy, aviation, civil engineering structures, transportation, and security industries. Hundreds of fiber optic sensing instruments from Micron Optics have been widely used across five continents and three oceans. These instruments all possess high speed, high precision, and multiple grating measurement points.

MOI has opened up new application fields such as medicine and biotechnology, and MOI has become a new leader in the commercialization of scanning laser technology in many key industries. Instruments now cover various wavelength ranges and scanning speeds.

Telecom tunable devices, OEM modules, and test instruments produced by MOI offer higher signal processing, channel multiplexing, and spectral analysis measurement solution capabilities.

Currently, fiber optic sensing products include: fiber optic filters, distributed fiber sensor measurement systems, distributed fiber temperature sensor systems, distributed fiber strain sensor systems, fiber Bragg grating sensors, fiber Bragg grating demodulators, high-speed grating demodulators, fiber optic sensing analyzers, high-speed fiber vibration mode analyzers, fiber strain sensors, fiber temperature sensors, fiber displacement sensors, fiber stress analyzers, fiber displacement meters, thermometers, strain gauges, femtosecond lasers, OCT, scanning laser light sources, etc.

Company Mission:

MOI's mission is to develop advanced tunable optical technology and provide our customers with high-quality products and services.
