by qiyouchax12 on 2011-05-23 11:02:01

The taxi violation exposure platform introduced today by the media and the city's transport management bureau has seen 40 taxis "listed". According to investigations by the city窶冱 transport management department, in front of Fangchang Long Distance Station, on May 19th, a total of five taxis were fined 100 yuan each for "not maintaining vehicle cleanliness as required." On June 7th and 9th, two untidy taxis were also penalized at Qingdao Train Station.
