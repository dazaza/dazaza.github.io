Yesterday afternoon, a staff member surnamed Feng from the Publicity Department of Wuxi Public Security Bureau confirmed this matter to the reporter, but indicated that the traffic police in Wuxi also borrowed the idea from other peers and it was not an original creation. A man spent 3 million yuan buying parts to assemble an airplane (with pictures). Yesterday, an online post titled "Super NB High-speed Police Car" went viral on various forums. The post was first released by netizen "bandeng569" at 19:27 on the evening of the 13th on Tianya Forum, then was forwarded by various forums and Weibo accounts. Southern Daily reporter Ding Jianting posted three pictures in the online post, with text descriptions for each picture, making many netizens laugh after seeing them. The introduction for the first picture read "From afar, it looks like a high-speed police car, possibly measuring speed, so one would hit the brakes," the second picture's description read "Then one would be shocked, there are actually such things," and the third picture's description read "There is even solar power and police car lights on top."
