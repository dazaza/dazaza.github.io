Police immediately requested that the driver pull over for inspection. To their shock, they found out that this large bus had been involved in an accident and sent to a repair shop. The driver did not have enough money to pay for repairs. After a quick check, the driver realized that although the vehicle's appearance was deformed, it could still operate, so he decided to drive it back to Zhengzhou, ignoring both his own life and others'. Finally, the police severely criticized the driver for his reckless behavior and promptly persuaded him to return off-road, requiring the driver to repair the vehicle nearby.

The front and rear windshield of the vehicle were completely shattered, the entire body tilted 15 degrees to the right, with cracks all over and exposed wires. In order to withstand the cold wind on the highway, the driver was fully equipped, pulling up his down jacket hood, wrapping himself tightly, preparing to pass through the checkpoint like this.
