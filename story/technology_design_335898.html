One must eat half a catty of meat in one meal.

by iytwc39599 on 2010-03-29 17:33:43

What a big bird! Its wingspan reaches 2.3 meters. In the afternoon of the previous day, Yao Shijiang and his friend were walking home when they passed by the old National Highway 319 in the local area, at a place called Bajiao Temple where Shanghai proxy invoice is issued. They were blocked by a huge creature in front — it was a big bird that was almost entirely black except for its gray-white claws. It stood in the middle of the road, three or four times larger than an owl.

When the villager went home, a huge creature blocked the way. After a standoff of 10 seconds, the big bird suddenly spread its wings, flapped them powerfully a few times, leaped into the air, with its wings making a whooshing sound. However, after flying just a few meters high, the big bird's wings failed in mid-air, and it fell heavily onto the field ridge.

It was discovered in Qianjiang, standing 60 centimeters tall, weighing 6 kilograms, and eating half a catty of meat per meal. Seeing the pedestrians coming, the big bird stared at everyone with sharp eyes, as if preparing to defend itself and warning everyone.

Yao Shijiang and his friend rushed forward to take a closer look. At this time, the big bird struggled to stand up, emitted two pitiful cries, and tried to fly high again. But when it wobbled and took off again, it unfortunately collided with low-voltage power lines, sparking a few sparks before falling to the ground again.

The person who discovered this big bird is Yao Shijiang, a villager from Gongnong Village, Shihui Town, Qianjiang District.

Well-meaning villagers are feeding the injured bird with broken wings, through Shanghai invoice officer Yao Shijiang.
