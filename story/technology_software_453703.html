Two people caused the death of five in a robbery at night at their original workplace

by zzf0zxyh20 on 2011-06-29 16:35:08

Yesterday, a reporter learned from the Intermediate People's Court of Bozhou City that the first-instance verdict was announced for the "4・09" Extraordinary Murder and Robbery Case in Mengcheng County. The court found Yu Laiyou guilty of robbery, sentencing him to death with the deprivation of political rights for life and confiscation of all personal property. Wang Ruihua was found guilty of harboring criminals and sentenced to eight years in prison. He Fangxue was also found guilty of harboring criminals and sentenced to six years in prison. Additionally, they were ordered to compensate the plaintiffs in the attached civil lawsuit for various economic losses.

The court investigation revealed that on April 5, 2010, He Mingming proposed robbing a rolling shutter factory in Mengcheng County where he had previously worked, and Yu Laiyou agreed. The next day, the two traveled to Mengcheng County. On the evening of April 8, they sneaked into the rolling shutter factory but returned to their inn due to the large number of people inside making it inconvenient to act. At around 8 PM on April 9, the two carried tools for the crime and went to the rolling shutter factory again, entering the factory through the toilet. Yu Laiyou picked up a metal pipe. Around 11 PM, the two stealthily reached the second floor of the factory and kicked open the doors of the living room and the east bedroom. Victim Dai heard the noise and came out from the north bedroom. Yu Laiyou struck his head violently with the metal pipe, then chased him into the north bedroom and forced Dai, Zhang, and Dai's son to stand against the wall. During this time, the three victims managed to restrain Yu Laiyou. Dai's son immediately called the police.

He Mingming stabbed victims Zhang and He after injuring them, then broke into the north bedroom with a knife, allowing Yu Laiyou to break free from control. After stabbing Dai, Zhang, and Dai's son, the two fled with 1800 yuan in cash. On the way, He Mingming informed He Fangxue over the phone that he had killed someone and called Wang Ruihua to tell her something had happened. After receiving clothes delivered by He Fangxue, He Mingming and Yu Laiyou escaped to Changchun on the evening of April 11. On April 12, the two met with Wang Ruihua in Changchun. Despite knowing about their crimes, Wang Ruihua still instructed them to flee and provided them with cash. On the night of April 14, He Mingming committed suicide by poisoning himself in Nong'an County, Changchun. On the morning of April 15, when Wang Ruihua met with Yu Laiyou in Changchun, she provided him with cash again and instructed him to flee. On April 27, Yu Laiyou was arrested.

According to forensic examination, victim Dai died from hemorrhagic shock combined with brain injury; Dai's son died from severe brain injury; victims Zhang, Zhang (another person), and He died from hemorrhagic shock; He Mingming died from "methomyl" poisoning. The court believed that the criminal facts of Yu Laiyou, Wang Ruihua, and He Fangxue were clear, and the charges against them were established. It was learned that due to dissatisfaction with the judgment, the defendants have already appealed, and the case has now entered the second-instance stage.

Yang Guoming, this report by journalist Yuan Xinghong.
