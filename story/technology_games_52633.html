The perfect launch of urban life

by aisong602 on 2008-03-11 11:01:42

The first virtual interactive community combining virtual and real life, "Metropolitan Life," carefully crafted by the metropolitan circle, was finally officially launched on February 25th. This virtual community game upends the traditional model, pioneering a blueprint of real life with a virtual space as its framework, allowing players to shuttle between real and virtual time-space, giving users an unprecedented entertainment experience. "Metropolitan Life" is China's first AVATA virtual community that combines graphic network with real cities, developed using Flash AS3.0, without downloading any client software you can enter into a colorful virtual world. "Metropolitan Life" is a wonderful combination of virtual life and real cities; you can grab housing property rights like in the real world, purchase furniture to decorate your house, change outfits at will, or make friends with the opposite sex, even getting married and cohabiting. Users who want to play the game "Metropolitan Life" only need to lightly click on the "Metropolitan Life" button on the homepage of the Metropolitan Circle (www.o.cn) to easily enter the game interface of "Metropolitan Life"; you can also log in after entering various city interfaces, there are also login icons for "Metropolitan Life" at the upper right corner of each city interface (such as: http://gz.o.cn), after clicking the login icon, the website will pop up a login window, users can choose user login or enter the game as a visitor. After users enter the game, they first enter their own room ohome, here, you can buy properties, furniture, clothing, decorate rooms, sing karaoke, video chat, raise pets, play games to earn money, work to earn money, chat online, check visitor messages, upload photos, etc., to interact with netizens, make money and consume activities, completely equivalent to a real home. Currently, this game "Metropolitan Life" has been launched in four major cities: Guangzhou 3D map Metropolitan Life, Zhongshan 3D map Metropolitan Life, Macao 3D map Metropolitan Life, Quanzhou 3D map Metropolitan Life, other cities will be launched successively, please stay tuned.
