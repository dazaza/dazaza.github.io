Summary of Recent Major News on Season Four of "Prison Break" [Repost] by Zuo Ba on 2008-8-20

Boring TV series continue to be filmed, and boring people continue to look forward. That's it.

The male star of "Prison Break," Wentworth Miller, was photographed on set on his 36th birthday on June 2nd, enjoying a happy birthday with Sara by his side. Additionally, there has been a lot of buzz recently that our handsome Mi has become the spokesperson for a well-known domestic clothing brand. The authenticity of this information is very high, and we may soon see related advertisements and promotions. Furthermore, the premiere of Season Four of "Prison Break" has been postponed by one week from the original plan, with the first episode airing on September 1st for two hours.

Season Four Shooting Site

1. Season Four of "Prison Break" will premiere on August 25th this year, with two episodes airing back-to-back on the same night. It will then air weekly on Mondays, but the time will change from the original 8 PM to 9 PM, following the second season of "Terminator: The Sarah Connor Chronicles."

2. Lead actor Wentworth Miller confirmed that Michael will remove the tattoos from his body.

3. Michael Rapaport (pictured above) joins as a regular cast member in Season Four, playing Don, an FBI trainer who helps Michael and Lincoln.

4. In Season Four, the main characters are no longer in prison, but it's only a matter of time before Michael is arrested again. Mahone joined the company just to obtain internal intelligence. T-Bag will attempt to escape. Whistler and Susan B will work closely together.

5. At the end of Season Three, Michael and Lincoln went their separate ways, but they won't be apart for long. The news that Sara is still alive brings them back together. When Michael learns that Sara is still alive, his thoughts become confused. The brothers realize that the only way to freedom is to find Sara, bring down the company, and expose its secrets.

With the help of government personnel (FBI trainer Don), they assemble a team of avengers, including some familiar faces: Mahone, Sucre, and Bellick.

The brothers must also win over T-Bag. They need T-Bag's help to unite everyone from Fox River towards the goal of bringing down the company, and T-Bag unknowingly becomes the key to helping the brothers overcome difficult challenges. They will soon discover that breaking in is much harder than breaking out.

6. The first episode of Season Four is titled "Scylla" (originally referring to the sea monster in Greek mythology that devours sailors). Sara will appear and explain her previous experiences. There will be scenes featuring both Michael and Sara. Most of the main characters will appear in the first episode with corresponding explanations, and a few will leave.

7. The bald general will appear in the first episode. New character Roland Glenn will debut in the second episode and stay for at least six episodes, aged 25-35, a computer expert, poor social skills, and a "big mouth." His job is to help Michael and others overthrow the company. Actors are currently being auditioned for this role.

8. Season Four is likely to be the final season of "Prison Break."

"The Dad" from "Family War" Joins Season Four of "Prison Break" as a Government Agent

Michael Rapaport, who played the lead role of "dad" in FOX's comedy "Family War," has secured a role as a government agent in "Prison Break."

The 38-year-old Michael will play a new regular character in Season Four of "Prison Break," offering an interesting proposal to the two brothers in the show.

Michael also appeared as a supporting actor in NBC's comedy "My Name Is Earl." He also guest-starred in a few episodes of the classic comedy "Friends" as Phoebe's adorable boyfriend.

As a young man, he was a die-hard fan of the famous actor Eddie Murphy (who also voiced the donkey in "Shrek"), imitating him by wearing white suits and gold ties. Later, he really got the chance to collaborate with Eddie Murphy in the films "Metro" and "Dr. Dolittle 2." Standing at 1.92 meters tall, he once had aspirations of becoming a basketball player.

Season Four of "Prison Break" will have 22 episodes and is expected to return in September 2008, with Sara, previously thought to be dead, making a comeback.

To be honest, this is a piece of news that I find a bit surprising. I'm not sure if Michael Rapaport is going to play the "good guy" or the "smiling villain" (each camp in Season Four adds one character). His participation actually increases my interest in watching. If he plays the good guy, "Prison Break" will be very interesting; if he's the smiling villain, we'll have to see how good his acting is.

In "Family War," his daughter was quite cute, and it was a bit of a pity that the show was canceled last year.

Wentworth Miller Wins Award in Germany for "Prison Break," Stunning Popularity!

Last Saturday in German local time, the "2008 Bravo SuperShow" was held at the Arena Nurnberg in Nuremberg. Male actor Wentworth Miller, who became globally famous through the hit American drama "Prison Break," was selected as the "Most Popular Star" by Germany's most popular teen magazine "Bravo." As a result, he won the "Shooting Star" award, igniting a热烈 atmosphere at the event.

There were too many screams! Miller answered appropriately while walking the red carpet, remaining charmingly handsome, and also mentioned someone returning in Season Four. It seems like it was Miller's first time winning an award in such a grand setting, so he was a bit excited. In the end, the towel given by the host to wipe Miller's sweat was tightly grabbed by three girls, and it's unclear whether they figured out a fair distribution plan! Miller is probably the most popular TV star in the world right now. Season Four of "Prison Break" returns on September 12, 2008, and will have 22 episodes.

Filming for Season Four of "Prison Break" begins next month, adding one character to each side.

The production crew of the popular American drama "Prison Break" will reconvene next month to shoot Season Four. It was previously reported that Sara Tancredi, played by Sarah Wayne Callies, would return in Season Four.

In the previous season, Lincoln found Sara's head in the box given to him by "The Company." Regarding this, Matt Olmstead, the executive producer of "Prison Break," told the media that they will explain this issue through a series of flashbacks about Sara. Recently, there have been reports that two new characters will join Season Four of "Prison Break."

American media recently reported that Season Four of "Prison Break" will add one character to each side, who will become key players in the showdown between Michael and "The Company." On Michael's side is FBI agent Don, who will try to dismantle "The Company" from within using strategy and tactics. On "The Company's" side is the dangerous killer Penrab, ready to eliminate certain individuals at any time.

However, like previous characters in "Prison Break," these new characters aren't necessarily set in their roles. For example, Mahone and Kellerman were once villains but later did some good for Michael and Lincoln.

On the DVD front, the Season Three DVD of "Prison Break" will be released first in the UK on May 19th, priced at 39.99 pounds, which is estimated to be twice the price in the US. This season's DVD includes bonus scenes chosen by the director, as well as behind-the-scenes footage from the first episode of Season Three. The producers have not yet announced the release date for North America.
