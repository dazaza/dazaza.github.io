The Mobile Advertising Journey of Chenzhou Real Estate

by robooseo514 on 2012-03-02 17:16:17

The Mobile Advertising Journey of Chenzhou Real Estate: In 2011, as the information of mobile internet swept across China, many merchants had already smelled the strong business opportunities. Not only did various small and medium-sized enterprises in the southeastern coastal areas take action, but also merchants in Chenzhou, Hunan, had already felt the charm of mobile internet. At the end of 2011, Mr. Huang from Chenzhou City registered the keyword "Chenzhou second-hand housing" on Roboo (www.roboo.com), opening the era of mobile advertising for Chenzhou real estate.

"Chenzhou second-hand housing" (http://czesf.roobo.com/) became the first second-hand housing mobile website in Chenzhou, integrating three networks and serving the Chenzhou area, facing city-wide recruitment, and shops that entered first could enjoy free entry for three months. Mr. Huang, who registered Roboo's mobile top ad "Chenzhou second-hand housing," told us that before he registered "Chenzhou second-hand housing," he had already noticed that mobile phones had become a socially popular product, with almost everyone owning one. At that time, the mobile commerce he knew was merely making phone calls to do business; he didn't know that websites similar to those on the internet could be published on mobile phones. In 2011, Mr. Huang accidentally browsed some clothing websites on his mobile phone and found registration and ordering information. The one-touch dialing function could be used for business consultation at any time. Mr. Huang associated this with his surroundings, where it could be said that everyone owned a mobile phone, and during their free time, they were all using their phones to surf the web, read news, and chat. With so many users and such convenient information, Mr. Huang thought this was a rare opportunity.

In the second half of 2011, a dealer of Roboo Technology in Hunan contacted Mr. Huang and explained the development trend of mobile internet and the superior services of Roboo’s top ads. This time, Mr. Huang did not hesitate. He was confident and signed a contract for mobile top ad services with Roboo Technology. In this contract, it was written that a dual-mode website with both a 3G mobile site and a PC site would be gifted for free. In this way, Mr. Huang saved himself the trouble of finding someone to build a website. Moreover, the backend of the 3G website provided by Roboo was easy to operate and manage. Roboo Technology provided the backend account and password, allowing merchants to add freely. Mr. Huang said he didn't understand websites, but now, after the cooperation dealer of Roboo explained it to him, he could skillfully operate the website.

Now, Mr. Huang's dual-mode second-hand housing website has gone online. Whether it is the PC internet or the 3G mobile network, you can find "Chenzhou second-hand housing." On the website, you can clearly see industry news about second-hand housing and release information about second-hand housing. If you have an intention, you can contact Mr. Huang through the one-touch dialing function within the website to inquire about "Chenzhou second-hand housing."

Mr. Huang's collaboration with Roboo Technology has now stepped into the field of mobile internet. Hand in hand with Roboo, through Roboo's platform, the information about Chenzhou second-hand housing is displayed in front of hundreds of millions of mobile phone users, meeting the supply and demand in the industry.

Roboo Technology is an internet company specializing in mobile search engine development. It was the first in the industry to launch mobile top ad services, providing a mobile platform for various small and medium-sized enterprises to showcase product information and service information. Roboo's mobile search engine is a new generation of internet model developed based on the mobile platform. It breaks through many restrictions of traditional internet, attracting hundreds of millions of customers with its anytime browsing method, drastically increasing the number of people paying attention to website information, and paving a broader road for enterprise information publication and website promotion. Under the dual refinement of technological development and market development, Roboo Technology develops steadily, gradually occupying the forefront position in the mobile search industry.

This time, "Chenzhou second-hand housing" chose to cooperate with Roboo Technology because it saw its immeasurable commercial prospects. Through this cooperation with Roboo Technology, the information about "Chenzhou second-hand housing" has been known by many merchants in the industry and a large number of mobile phone users, bringing a certain amount of commercial traffic. Mr. Huang believes that this cooperation with Roboo Technology, stepping into the mobile commerce industry, will bring his "Chenzhou second-hand housing" to a new height and generate more profits.
