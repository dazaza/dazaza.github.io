Online accounting gains popularity, guiding new consumption habits

The green pepper costs 1.8 yuan, the green vegetable 2 yuan, soy milk 1 yuan, and hidden consumption items amount to 90... This is the account of a netizen named "Gua Peng's You Lai Wo Wang..." on an online account book. As a star account book, this netizen has well combined the dullness and interest of accounting, and made a proper adjustment between lifestyle regulation and daily habit correction. Perhaps, "梳理我的生活" (organizing my life) becoming the slogan of this online account book indeed makes sense.

Online account books integrate fashion and practical elements

As a convenient and flexible online accounting platform, Caike Online Accounting Website has always been close to the basic accounting needs of netizens during its development process. It takes consumption and income as the basic elements of the system and adds interactive content such as comments. For the "thumb generation," it also increases the mobile phone accounting function. All these are provided for free, meaning that users only need to pay the necessary internet fees, and Caike Online Accounting Website does not charge any other fees.

According to relevant personnel from Caike Online Accounting Website, the website is developing some new functions, such as consumption shows, which allow netizens to showcase their experiences in the consumption process through pictures and comments.

It is reported that this part of the function is currently being tested intensively. Many merchants have taken action, especially multiple catering and clothing merchants who have called to inquire and expressed their desire to open online shops on Caike Online Accounting Website. In response, Caike Online Accounting Website will consider these demands in subsequent functional improvements and solicit opinions from some netizens.

Considering that the core function of an account book is accounting, and some netizens like to keep accounts but do not want some consumption items to be known by others. After learning about this demand from netizens, to protect their privacy, Caike Online Accounting Website specially set up hidden items. Because of this matter, a development convention was formed within the network, that is, as long as netizens actively propose requirements, and after discussion, they are feasible, all accounting needs can be met. Moreover, this is just a small episode among the many thoughtful services of the account book.

Guiding new consumption habits, online account books have achieved remarkable results

For white-collar workers with a single source of income and complicated consumption expenditures, they bear a shiny profession and endure great living pressure. In the city, they are often the "moonlight clan." Many people want to change their consumption methods, but the inconvenience of traditional account books and the trouble of professional online financial account books make them lack good strategies. Therefore, they urgently hope to find a relatively simple online accounting platform to help them organize their lives and occasionally exchange consumption insights with netizens. Regarding this situation, Caike Online Accounting Website considered the core value behind these demands during the product development process. It gave full consideration to page settings, menu allocation, and humanized design of accounting, and gradually incorporated the refinement of details into daily work.

Since the accounting group is relatively complex but concentrated on women, Caike Online Accounting Website's overall style tends to be more friendly, making family financial management people feel solid and warm. For these matters, Ms. Li, who once "had no way to manage her household," deeply understands. Back then, because she spent money without counting, at the end of each month, she would rack her brains to find every bit of expenditure, only to discover that she didn't know where her money went. For this reason, her husband said she spent money recklessly, and she felt wronged. After several times, the two started arguing. Fortunately, someone introduced an online account book to her. She thought about it, treating it as trying to save a dead horse, and the result was that after a month of keeping accounts, she not only formed a habit but also found the reason for excessive spending: originally, there were too many unnecessary purchases, which were simply wasteful. Later, the two reconciled as a result, and she happily said, "Caike Online Accounting Website not only changed my habit of reckless spending but also restored our marital happiness."

Seventy percent of the accounting population are women, mostly housewives and urban white-collar workers

Regarding women keeping accounts, although it is the result of the teaching of thrift, nowadays, the purpose of urban women keeping accounts is more out of a sense of responsibility for the family and control over their own lifestyle. Many career women pursuing economic independence and housewives seeking family happiness are full of passion for life and can actively seek solutions. The online account book thus came into being, making everything seem natural and highlighting the irresistible nature of the changing times and fashion trends.

Miss Liu used to be a spoiled girl at home, always having things go smoothly. After graduating from university, she successfully entered a foreign company. However, since starting work, despite a generous salary, she has always been part of the "moonlight clan," occasionally needing financial support from her family. Over time, as she grew older, it was time to find a place to belong. But after several failed relationships, her boyfriends left her due to her extravagant spending. Reflecting on her mistakes, she began to keep accounts, but the account book would often go missing, and eventually, she reverted to her old ways. Finally, under the introduction of a close friend, she started using the online account book. After a few months, she actually saved some money. Happy, she treated her close friend to a big meal, but the meal cost less than 30 yuan. If it had been in the past, it would have cost more than 200 yuan. After this incident, her close friend not only did not blame her but praised her for finally knowing how to spend money wisely...

Additionally, it is understood that the number of registered users on Caike Online Accounting Website steadily grows each month, with the registration volume already exceeding 20,000. The main user group is still predominantly female, and among women, housewives and urban white-collar workers hold an absolute advantage. Recently, the number of college students registering has been increasing, and it is believed that in the near future, Caike Online Accounting Website will flourish in university campuses.

After all, through the method of online accounting, netizens can avoid some unnecessary expenses, develop rational consumption habits, and exchange money-saving secrets with other netizens. With limited income, one can live a noble life. With such joyous events, why wouldn't everyone participate?
