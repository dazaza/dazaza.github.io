Introduction to the full series of A Single Father in Love:

Kang Bong-Ho is a single father with a 7-year-old son. By day, he works as a pest control specialist, and by night, he moonlights as an amateur boxer. Naturally cheerful, he always wears a smile despite any difficulties. He can sacrifice everything for his son. In the world of Kang Bong-Ho and his son San, a girl named Jeon Ha-Ree enters their lives. Moreover, Ha-Ree's father Jeon Gi-Seok and San's mother Yoon So-I are in a romantic relationship... Although it showcases paternal love, the main theme of this drama remains a love story. Here we witness the laughter and tears that embellish the love story between the single father Bong-Ho and Ha-Ree. Facing tragic fates, they console each other and soothe each other's pain.

Compared to many previously depicted love stories filled with betrayal, revenge, and hatred, here in A Single Father in Love, we see more beautiful emotions such as forgiveness, comfort, passion, and innocence.

Main Characters Introduction of A Single Father in Love:

Kang Bong-Ho (Male, 30 years old) - Played by Oh Ji-Ho

A representative Korean single father who becomes stronger with challenges. His professions are pest control specialist and amateur boxing fighter. Despite becoming an orphan at a young age, he is naturally optimistic, like a pine tree—straightforward and strong. After being abandoned by his first love, he has been raising his son San alone.

He would do anything for San. Though living happily with San, deep inside, he carries the scars left by San's mother. After his first love, he began to distrust women. When Ha-Ree launches a fierce romantic pursuit on him, his frozen heart begins to thaw.

At this moment, he reunites with San's mother, his first love Yoon So-I, and learns that So-I will become Ha-Ree's stepmother. He decides to give up his romance with Ha-Ree. The news that San has a brain tumor draws Ha-Ree, Bong-Ho, So-I, and Gi-Seok into the whirlpool of fate. With the determination to save San at all costs, Bong-Ho spares no effort to earn money, but medical expenses seem bottomless. Deceived countless times, his body broken down, yet not even God can stop his paternal love.

Yoon So-I (30 years old) - Played by Kang Seung-Yeun

Selfish, rational, elegant, and confident pianist. She lost her parents in an accident and was subsequently raised by relatives. More than anyone else, she yearns for success and recognition from others, but the reality of having no money or background prevents her from showcasing her talent.

When she felt most desperate, she met Gi-Seok. She then abandoned her two deepest loves—Bong-Ho and her son San—to study abroad with Gi-Seok. Though not a cold-hearted woman, she does not want to disrupt her current peaceful and happy life, so she distances herself from Bong-Ho and her son San.

She genuinely likes and respects her sponsor Gi-Seok. Though dreaming of marrying him, due to San's illness, all truths are revealed, leading her to decide to break off her engagement with Gi-Seok.

Watching her sick son, she is saddened by the reality that she cannot do anything for him.

Jeon Ha-Ree (24 years old) - Played by Heo Yi-Jae

Born between a surgeon father and a pianist mother, she grew up in a happy family, developing a cheerful and cute personality. After her mother passed away, she worked hard to fill the void left by her mother and became an understanding daughter.

Unexpectedly, six years ago, her father went abroad with her piano teacher, turning her sense of betrayal into rebellion. Having almost zero experience or feelings in love, she falls for the single father Bong-Ho. Are former rivals destined to remain rivals forever?

She and Yoon So-I, who once became rivals because of her father, again find themselves competing for Bong-Ho's affection. This time, she absolutely cannot lose! There is no word for failure in her dictionary! She stays by the side of Bong-Ho, who has been constantly fatigued, letting him feel pure love.

Kang San (7 years old) - Played by Ahn Do-Gyu

Bong-Ho's son. Curious about everything, he enjoys digging into details. Like his father, he is handsome and good at fighting. Strong-willed, he dislikes losing but often ends up crying. Hearing from his dad that his mom looks like Kim Tae-Hee, he likes looking at Kim Tae-Hee's photos to imagine his mother's face. Despite his father's opposition, he secretly learns piano from Ha-Ree.

Diagnosed with pediatric brain tumors, he still fights the disease cheerfully. Worried about making his father sad, he acts even more mischievous in front of Bong-Ho.

Jeon Gi-Seok (Around 50 years old) - Played by Park Chan-Hwan

Ha-Ree's father, So-I's husband, neurosurgeon. After meeting So-I, who resembled his deceased wife, he fell in love and voluntarily became her sponsor, leaving his daughter alone in America. For six years, he has been feeling guilty for neglecting his daughter Ha-Ree.

He is a gentle and tolerant man. He believes that the happiness of the family is the most important thing in the world. As long as Ha-Ree is happy, he can forgive and face anything. Therefore, when he learned about his daughter's feelings for the single father Bong-Ho, he happily prepared to accept their relationship. Unexpectedly, Bong-Ho turned out to be So-I's ex-husband, which made him hesitate. Even after learning that San is So-I's son, he still performs the surgery for San, understands So-I's desire to live with her son, and accepts San as his own son.
