I. The required materials are as follows:

1. Copies of the business opening certificate or passport for foreign companies or individuals, requiring original documents certified by the Chinese embassy in their respective countries (2 copies).

2. Original bank credit certificates for foreign companies or individuals (2 copies).

3. Copy of ID card or passport of the legal representative of the foreign company.

4. Resume and 2-inch bareheaded photos (2) of the proposed legal representative of the foreign-invested production company (if a board of directors is to be established, a list of board members and appointment letters, copies of passports or ID cards, and 2 photos are required).

5. More than five alternative names for the proposed foreign-invested production company.

6. Business scope of the proposed foreign-invested production company.

7. Original house lease contract, copy of house ownership certificate, factory floor plan, etc.

8. Introduction of the foreign company, application letter from the foreign company for investment in Shanghai.

9. Two entrustment letters to our company.

All Chinese translation pieces of the above documents.
