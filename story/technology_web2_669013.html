Children's suitcases are both flashy and trendy, and can also practice independence_34

by qazwsx852 on 2012-02-18 19:16:25

Children's suitcases are both flashy and trendy, and can also practice independence. "Wherever we go, whether at the airport or in the amusement park or shopping street, my 4-year-old son pulls his Trunki suitcase himself, attracting a lot of attention and smiles from passers-by, and some even ask where to buy it, which is quite impressive!" Trendy mom Fancy has just returned from a trip to Europe with her family, and surprisingly, the most eye-catching item was the children's suitcase she bought for her son before departure. With the summer vacation more than halfway over, family tours are still the main force for Shenzhen residents traveling outside, and this trend has driven the sales of the relatively "niche" children's suitcases.

Among children's suitcase brands, the trendiest one is probably the British brand Trunki that Fancy mentioned, which has quite a high recognition rate among fashionable moms in Shenzhen. Xiao Tao is the owner of a Taobao online store in Shenzhen, and in the past month, her store has sold 40 Trunki suitcases. Actually, this is not much; some online stores have sold more than 150 in a month! "Their colors are too beautiful, like blue, purple, peach pink, apple green, etc., and their shapes are very cartoonish, some look like tigers, some like beetles, some like fire trucks. Kids and moms often fall in love with them at first sight. The most special thing is that babies can sit on them directly when they are tired, which makes the design much more considerate compared to other brand suitcases!" Xiao Tao said.

The price of Trunki suitcases varies between 300-500 yuan depending on color and pattern. In addition to online shopping, trendy moms in Shenzhen often go to O LE supermarket, maternal and infant physical stores, or Hong Kong to purchase. The price at O LE supermarket is about 100 yuan more expensive than online, but sales staff said, "Since the summer vacation, this series has suddenly become very popular, and it's often the children themselves who choose the style, then the parents pay the bill."

In addition to Trunki, THOMAS$FRIENDS, HELLOKITTY, Disney, Astro Boy, and other brands of children's suitcases each have their own group of fans. They are widely distributed, with more counters in茂Ye, Tianhong, and MixC malls, making them convenient to purchase. Another trendy mom, XiXi, found after comparing: "Trunki is more suitable for younger children, as it doesn't look like a regular suitcase, without a pull rod. Children either pull it with a strap or push it like a small stool. If it's not loaded with many things, they can also carry it on their backs. Some of my friends bought it when their children were one or two years old, and the kids really loved it. Other brands are more suitable for older children because the suitcases are mini versions of adult suitcases, able to hold quite a bit, and taller children can pull them easily."

Is it necessary to prepare a separate suitcase for children when traveling? Fancy explained the reasons why children's suitcases are so popular: although children will definitely feel tired sometimes and don't want to pull the suitcase themselves, thus adding to the burden of adults, overall, it benefits the child's independence. "He will choose the toys, books, and some items he wants to take by himself, pack them into the suitcase himself, and mostly pull the suitcase by himself. This process helps him become more independent, less reliant on adults, and gives him a sense of equality with adults."
