Can this save my barbaric girlfriend?

My girlfriend is someone who doesn't like to compromise easily and takes everything very seriously, especially our anniversaries and her birthday, which she remembers well and has high expectations for. However, I am the type of person who is very careless and often forgets these dates. She then assumes that I don't care about her or love her. To be honest, I can't stand this kind of temperament, it's not that I don't love her because I forgot these days, but rather because I was too busy and didn't intentionally forget them.

Her birthday is coming again, my god! What should I give her? Every time I get annoyed by such things, it gives me a headache. Last year, I gave her the most cliché roses, she didn't get angry, but she wasn't too happy either. She just said, flowers will wither, you can only look at them for a few days. Next time, could you give me something with more commemorative value? Since she said that, I really hope to give her a surprise~~

As the saying goes, "Three cobblers with their wits combined equal Zhuge Liang, the mastermind", so I sought help from Taizhou Shopping Network. There were many enthusiastic people inside, some suggested giving small animals, unfortunately my girlfriend hates animals; some suggested books, equivalent to giving knowledge, but my girlfriend doesn't like reading... Later, someone told me that his girlfriend had sensitive skin and lacked energy at work. He accidentally found a product on Taizhou Saint Pai - Samsung Plasma Air Sterilizer. It turns out that our living air contains a lot of harmful substances, these harmful substances lower our immunity and cause asthma and coughing. With this Samsung Plasma Air Sterilizer, it kills viruses, bacteria, smoke smell, neutralizes harmful active oxygen, and provides fresh air. When he gave this to his girlfriend, she was very satisfied, and their relationship deepened. After hearing his story, I was a bit moved, this thing is good for health, and the price is acceptable.

Later, I went and bought it, it looked pretty good. But I was still a little scared, what if she's still not satisfied?
