The Dragon Boat Festival long weekend has begun, and today is the first day. Just the first day... I can only say it's been a real letdown.

What bad luck! Finally, I get some time off and wanted to catch up on sleep, but I was woken up by dripping rainwater! It turns out this house leaks. Woe is me!

Maybe because the rain was so heavy that water seeped in...

(Since I live on the top floor, it's one of those panel houses. Sigh, life abroad is tough. Don't feel sorry for me!!)

I got so angry that I decisively called the landlord and decided to evacuate from here quickly; I couldn't take it anymore. I had previously asked the landlord to install an air conditioner, but he kept putting it off...

While moving, my beloved phone took a heavy fall to the ground. What heartache!! Fortunately, the screen didn't break.

After all the hustle until now, I can finally rest a bit. I imagine you've already finished dinner by now,

while I haven't even had lunch yet. Breakfast? Forget about it. I forgot hunger; I'm used to it.

The new place is better, but just now... JUST NOW!!!!

My finger was mercilessly cut by a sharp piece of metal. There was so much blood!

I can't help but remark: My luck today has truly been great, not just ordinarily good.

The weather forecast says there will be rain every day during the Dragon Boat Festival in Hangzhou, so it looks like I'm destined to stay indoors for three days.

Alright, I accept it. I'll sleep through day and night, catching up on all the sleep I've missed in these past two months of work!

I wonder how the website is doing today. I haven't managed it at all. Everyone should be resting today, so those people probably won't spam advertisements as much, those junk messages are really like "wildfires that never end, spring breezes that bring them back to life" - they're endless. There's nothing I can do except understand; after all, they're living too.

That's all for now. My hand still hurts a little, and my stomach is growling. Time to eat! I want zongzi (rice dumplings)!

Wishing all friends a happy Dragon Boat Festival, and hoping you all enjoy a pleasant holiday!(zsezt2011bo)
