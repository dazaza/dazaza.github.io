Newton's problems, see how many you can get right.

### Translation:

**[1]** Suppose there is a pond with an infinite amount of water. There are two empty water jugs with capacities of 5 liters and 6 liters, respectively. The question is: how can you use only these two jugs to obtain exactly 3 liters of water from the pond?

---

**[2]** Zhou Wen's mother is a lab technician at the Yulin Cement Factory. One day, Zhou Wen came to the lab to do her homework. After finishing, she wanted to go out and play. "Wait," her mother said, "I want to test you with a question." She continued, "Look at these six glass cups used for experiments. The first three are full of water, and the last three are empty. Can you rearrange them so that the full cups and empty cups alternate by moving only one cup?" Zhou Wen, who loves thinking, is well-known in her school as "Little Smart." She figured it out after just a little thought. Can you figure out how "Little Smart" did it?

---

**[3]** Three young men fell in love with the same girl. To decide who would marry her, they decided to have a duel using pistols. Xiao Li has an accuracy rate of 30%, Xiao Huang is better, with an accuracy rate of 50%, and the best marksman is Xiao Lin, who never misses, with an accuracy rate of 100%. Because of this obvious disparity, they decided on the following order: Xiao Li shoots first, Xiao Huang second, and Xiao Lin last. Then the cycle repeats until only one person remains. Of the three, who has the highest chance of surviving? What strategies should they adopt?
