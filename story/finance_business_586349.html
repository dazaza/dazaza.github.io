Update on the rescue of a woman in a wedding dress who attempted to jump from a building: her rescuers will be nominated for bravery awards

by hj1718004 on 2011-11-25 19:07:11

Message Replay: At 15:00 on the 17th, a 22-year-old woman dressed in a wedding dress sat on the windowsill of the 7th floor of a residential building at the intersection of Guiyang Street and Wusong Road in Changchun City, emotionally impulsive. At around 16:21, the woman suddenly jumped down. In a critical moment, Guo Zhongfan, director of the Xinfaxiang Subdistrict Office of Chaoyang District in Changchun, caught her neck in mid-air and saved her after 54 seconds.

Video: A woman in a wedding dress jumped from a building but was saved when caught by the neck in mid-air. Source: Oriental Satellite TV "Oriental Noon News"

From when Li climbed onto the windowsill to the end of the 54-second rescue, what happened during this hour touched every onlooker. Some took out their own inflatable arches, others helped with traffic direction. After successfully saving Li, more people expressed admiration for Guo Zhongfan. The crowd applauded him for a full minute, and a veteran even went to his office to salute him as a sign of respect, saying, "You must have been in the army before, I thank you, you are a real man!"

The girl left Changchun smiling, saying she deeply regretted it. The hero who saved her received praise from Secretary Gao Guangbin.

While expressing admiration for the heroic rescuer Guo Zhongfan, many people were also concerned about Li's current situation. It was understood that Li had already left Changchun with her mother. When she left, she had realized many things and said she would not do anything foolish again. On the 19th, Gao Guangbin, member of the Standing Committee of the Provincial Party Committee and Secretary of the Changchun Municipal Party Committee, made comments on Guo Zhongfan's act of saving the suicidal girl on the 7th floor on the 17th, calling on grassroots cadres to learn from him.

Gao Guangbin called for learning from the heroic rescuer. Gao Guangbin, member of the Standing Committee of the Provincial Party Committee and Secretary of the Changchun Municipal Party Committee, made comments on the 19th after learning about Guo Zhongfan's act of courageously saving the suicidal girl despite the danger of possibly being pulled down himself on the 17th.

Gao Guangbin’s comments on Guo Zhongfan's achievements: Comrade Guo Zhongfan, a grassroots street cadre, is worthy of praise and learning for his willingness to risk his life and bravely save lives. Grassroots cadres directly face the masses, and their words and actions represent the image of the party and government. We should learn from Comrade Guo Zhongfan, fulfill our responsibilities, be close to and care for the people, and win the trust and respect of the masses through our practical actions!

Had she stored her boyfriend's father's phone number as "Dad"?

"This is the girl's first love, she was particularly devoted to her feelings," an unnamed insider said. Li started dating Wang at the age of 18 and was completely dedicated to him. Later, when they wanted to get married, they first faced opposition from Li's family, then later, Wang proposed breaking up.

On the day of the incident, Li threw her mobile phone down and it broke into pieces. To find Li's family, the police officers at the Beijing Street Police Station put Li's SIM card into another phone and made calls according to the contact list. The police first dialed a number stored as "Dad", but after connecting, the person on the other end hesitated, asked about the situation, then said he did not know Li, and hung up. It was understood that this person was not Li's father. Some speculated that the number stored as "Dad" might be Wang's father's.

"The bride in white" has left Changchun, feeling regretful.

After the story of the 22-year-old girl jumping from a building in her wedding dress was reported, domestic media outlets contacted our newspaper wanting to understand Li's condition and hoped to counsel her well. On the 19th, the reporter followed up on Li's situation again. An insider said that Li and her family left the hospital accompanied by police officers from the Beijing Street Police Station in Kuancheng District, Changchun Public Security Bureau, then went to the police station, and finally left the police station smiling after being counseled by Ma Sai, a police academy student interning at the police station. The insider also said that Li expressed her intention to live a good life in the future, "She expressed deep regret for doing this. Now, people are very concerned about her, hearing such words should make everyone feel relieved."

That night, Li and her family left Changchun and returned to her mother's home. However, no one knows the location of Li's mother's home. On the afternoon of the 19th, the reporter called Li's mother's phone, but it remained off. It was understood that Li's parents had divorced years ago, and her father currently works in Shijiazhuang. After learning about Li's intention to jump from the building, he tried calling her but could not get through. Mr. Li had not seen his daughter for half a year and was unaware of her relationship with her boyfriend.

The unit plans to nominate the heroic rescuer for an award for bravery.

Guo Zhongfan's actions moved many people, and some netizens suggested nominating him for an award for bravery. On the 19th, it was learned that the Xinfaxiang Subdistrict Office of Kuancheng District in Changchun City is collecting materials and preparing to submit an application to the Political Department of the Kuancheng Branch of the Changchun Public Security Bureau to nominate Guo Zhongfan for an award for bravery.

According to the provisions of the Regulations on Rewards and Protection of Persons Who Act Bravely issued by the Provincial People's Congress in October 2004, citizens who go beyond their statutory duties or contractual obligations, in order to protect public interests and the safety of others' persons and property, disregard personal danger, fight against illegal criminal acts that are happening or participate in disaster relief and rescue operations, can be nominated by themselves, their units, or the rescued parties. Whether Guo Zhongfan's actions can be recognized as acts of bravery will need to be determined after discussion by the review committee.

All those who participated in the rescue are heroes.

"Actually, the person supporting the girl's feet was also in great danger. He looked short, standing on the windowsill like he was about to fall." This is what Ms. Liu, an enthusiastic reader of our newspaper, said. So who is the man supporting Li's feet?

Zhang Shubin is a security officer at the Beijing Street Police Station, 53 years old, and has been doing this job for 6 years. At 16:21 on the 17th, it was he who first noticed a high heel shoe on the window ledge and immediately jumped onto the window to support Li's feet. Although Zhang Shubin is not tall and looks slightly thin, he is especially brave and reacts very quickly. "When I saw the high heel shoe there, I knew the girl hadn't fallen yet, so I quickly jumped up," Zhang Shubin said.

Looking back at the entire process of Li's rescue that day, there were countless unsung heroes. Pan Chengjie, who helped Zhang Shubin support the girl, is from the Xinfaxiang Subdistrict Office. Zhu YanSheng, who smashed the glass, is from the Demolition Office of the Xinfaxiang Subdistrict Office. He first used his elbow to smash the glass, making a clanging sound, and later someone handed over a stick to break the glass. Ma Sai, an intern at the police station, started counseling the girl when she was safe until she turned her sadness into joy and finally stopped talking satisfactorily.
