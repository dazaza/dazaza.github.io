KANS Hansoo Endorsed by Sexy Goddess Choi Ji-yoo of Korean Drama Queen

by hanshu2009 on 2009-12-02 21:02:57

Recently, the famous skin care brand KANS Hanshu signed a contract with the queen of Korean drama, Choi Ji-woo, to promote its classic series "KANS Ultra Series" launched in 2009. This is another skin care brand endorsement by Choi Ji-woo after Dior.

It is understood that the shooting location and content of this advertisement were basically kept confidential throughout. Choi Ji-woo and her working assistants came to China as early as October and shot the entire advertisement in Guangzhou.

A relevant person in charge of the KANS Hanshu brand expressed: "Choosing Choi Ji-woo to endorse Hanshu is not only because she is a super star in Korea. But more importantly, her gentle and delicate temperament has been influencing fans worldwide. Her intellectual and elegant temperament, along with her fine and white skin, perfectly matches the诉求of the KANS Ultra Series 'Beyond Time and Space, Eternal Beauty'. Therefore, she became the best choice for the KANS Hanshu brand!"

Since her debut in "First Love" in 1996, and then through works such as "Winter Sonata" and "Heaven's Stairs", Choi Ji-woo has grown into one of the most well-known Korean actresses in Asia.

During the shooting, Ji-woo frankly stated: "I really like the positive and confident self-attitude conveyed by Hanshu's 'Miracles Happen Because of You'. She said she would share the beauty miracles brought by the KANS Ultra Series with her friends and fans."

In the already popular advertisement, Choi Ji-woo elegantly strolls in a bright red scene, with a fresh and ethereal temperament and crystal-clear white skin, leaving a deep impression on all consumers. Especially in the KANS Ultra Series advertisement, the confident voice-over "Whiter than you, smoother than you" conveys the stunning beauty brought by Hanshu's grand offering - the Ultra Series in 2009.
