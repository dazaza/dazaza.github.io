At the turn of the century, Foyunlai began his artistic experiments using incense as raw material. Over the years, his footprints have covered incense machine manufacturers across the country, even including small hand workshops deep in remote mountains in the south, which he has without exception visited and investigated.

The creative inspiration for Foyunlai's "Incense Art" comes from the high-grade century-old sandalwood given to him by a friend years ago. In the swirling blue smoke, "perfumed" came the world-shaking incense art. Foyunlai - Practitioner of the Cultural Creative Industry, Pioneer of Contemporary Art

So he called on his peers to "self-rescue", and in the early 1990s, he personally founded the Sunbrand Management and Design Company, which later became quite famous in the management and design fields. Foyunlai's timely transformation was not unrelated to his earlier "change" themed series of works, "Everything is changing, only change is eternal." Over several decades, while changing himself, he actively participated in societal changes...and now magically produced his "Incense Art."
