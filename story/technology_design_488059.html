Never mind.

by haiktaoraf on 2011-09-11 17:29:11

Never mind, don't think about it anymore. It's already the middle of the night. Chen Xiaobing stuck her head out from under the covers and took deep breaths of fresh air. In a few days, school would start again. She might as well relax for now; who knows, maybe in the future she wouldn't even have time to think about him...

Her thoughts returned to the busy days of junior high school. The homeroom teacher was writing lesson plans with an expression that seemed like a different person altogether. His silver hair seemed to have grown a bit more, and his wrinkles were deeper, as if carved by a few more strokes of a knife. Lin Sa still stared at Xiao Rou, the most important girl in his life. Her gentle hair cascaded down, covering her ears and making her appear even more charming. Her large eyes looked downward, clearly lost in thought.

Lin Sa's focused profile wasn't exactly ugly, but Chen Xiaobing inexplicably felt a twinge of pain...

The agreement they had made regarding their feelings only lasted two and a half days before both pairs announced their breakups. The news spread through the QQ group as usual. Thinking about the dispirited Lin Sa and Wang Hong, Chen Xiaobing felt a strange sensation in her heart.

Hmph, Wang Hong was a seasoned player in matters of love. Of course, he wouldn’t be affected. Chen Xiaobing wasn’t worried about him. Besides, he wasn’t even her close friend, so she didn’t feel obligated to care about him. As for Lin Sa, he must be grieving while questioning what he did wrong. This game had started but was doomed from the very beginning—an irreversible cruelty. Lin Sa was destined to be the biggest loser. Hmph, she sneered. What should she say? Offer comfort? Stand idly by? Or not even have the right to comment? She had already warned Lin Sa about all the consequences and outcomes, yet he insisted on continuing, walking towards a frightening darkness. She couldn’t stop him—what gave her the right to do so?

She knew that if given another chance, Lin Sa would still choose this fleeting happiness. Even though he knew it was short-lived joy followed by long-lasting pain, he still yearned for that instant warmth and tenderness, willing to give up everything...

She admired Lin Sa so much—he could love so bravely, shielding Xiao Rou from the wind and rain, sacrificing everything to become her loyal umbrella of protection. But what about her? Her love was so fragile that she didn’t even have the chance to find happiness. All she could do was wait silently, unable to take any action because she was a girl! Oh no, she also glorified Song Yuhao. Under the brilliance of Song Yuhao, she was just a shadow. She didn’t deserve Song Yuhao, this prince who was almost perfect in every way.

Physics class.

The teacher was explaining the concepts of convex lenses when Chen Xiaobing started feeling drowsy again.

Lin Sa was quietly playing with his phone under the desk, yet he could still answer the teacher’s questions effortlessly. Chen Xiaobing strained to keep her eyes open, staring at the teacher and the blackboard, but the teacher’s questions made her head ache. Finally, when another question came, Chen Xiaobing awkwardly lowered her head. Jiang Xun and Lin Sa perked up, whispering reminders to Chen Xiaobing, but she stood there blankly, unable to hear a word. Dark clouds covered the sky, the weather turning gloomy with thunder rumbling continuously. White lightning flashed across the sky like pure angels, and she was lost in thought, gazing at the scenery outside the window as if her soul had left her body.

Cough cough cough! The teacher finally couldn’t hold back and coughed loudly, pulling Chen Xiaobing’s wandering thoughts back slightly.

Jiang Xun and Lin Sa wore expressions of "you're beyond help," lazily lying on the desks, sticking their tongues out at the teacher helplessly. The teacher looked at his two beloved students, sighed, and let her sit down.
