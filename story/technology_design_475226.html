Man commits 11 rapes of minors while adults sleep

by zxyasdg00 on 2011-08-12 13:31:50

Huang Xianyue Sentenced to Death in the First Trial

News Recap: At around 1 o'clock on May 13, 1998, a man sneaked into a resident's home in a town of Dongfeng County, Liaoyuan City. The adults in the family were sound asleep when he carried a 10-year-old girl outside and raped her. In the following three years, over ten similar cases of raping young girls occurred in Dongfeng County.

In 2002, this series of cases was listed as the top public case by the Provincial Public Security Department. On October 25, 2008, the suspect Huang Xianyue was captured by the police while fleeing on a motorcycle at the 20-kilometer mark of the 102 National Highway in Dehui City. The trial took place non-publicly on June 12, 2009, at the Intermediate People's Court of Liaoyuan City.

Our reporter Li Meng reports from Liaoyuan: Yesterday at 9:30 AM, the Intermediate People's Court of Liaoyuan City publicly announced the verdict for defendant Huang Xianyue in the Dongfeng County series of rapes of young girls. Huang Xianyue was sentenced to death in the first trial for rape and theft; Jin Guihua (Huang Xianyue's wife) was sentenced to three years in prison for harboring a criminal.

Claiming it was "molestation," the court did not accept his defense.

During the trial, Huang Xianyue defended himself by stating that he only molested multiple little girls and should not be charged with rape.

The court believed that Huang Xianyue intruded into the victims' homes at night, taking advantage of sleeping adults, forcibly carrying the victims to secluded locations such as mountains, fields, greenhouses, or even toilets. Subjectively, he intended to commit rape, and objectively, he used violent threats such as holding weapons, covering mouths, choking necks, and slapping, committing a series of rape acts.

Although some crimes were not actually completed, since the victims were young girls, contact between their reproductive organs is considered completed. Therefore, his defense does not comply with legal regulations and was not accepted by the court.

Among the victims of the rape crimes, the youngest was only five years old. Most of the victims were forced to change their names or move away from their original residences; the only adult victim also divorced because of this. His criminal actions lasted for ten years, causing great panic and adverse effects on local security and people's lives.

Sentenced to Death, Fined 30,000 RMB

The court believed that from May 1998 to August 2008, defendant Huang Xianyue committed 16 offenses successively in Dongfeng County and surrounding towns, as well as in Andar City, Heilongjiang Province. Among these, there were 11 cases of raping young girls, with seven being completed and four uncompleted; five cases of raping women, with three being completed and two uncompleted. Except for one adult woman, the other four victims were all under 18 years old.

Huang Xianyue, while being wanted for rape crimes, committed two thefts during his escape, with stolen items valued at more than 31,000 RMB. His actions constituted both rape and theft crimes. Given his despicable motives, cruel methods, raping multiple women and young girls, long-lasting criminal activities, significant social harm and influence, extremely bad circumstances, and especially severe consequences, he should be punished heavily according to law; his theft crime involved a large amount, but since he voluntarily confessed to this crime, it can be regarded as self-reporting, allowing for a lighter punishment.

Huang Xianyue was sentenced to death for rape and theft, deprived of political rights for life, and fined 30,000 RMB.

Defendant Jin Guihua knowingly harbored Huang Xianyue despite knowing about his major criminal behavior, helping him escape by obtaining a fake ID. She was found guilty of harboring a criminal, with serious circumstances, and sentenced to three years in prison.

Claiming Mental Issues, Both Appeal

Both Huang Xianyue and Jin Guihua have appealed.

Huang Xianyue claimed that before committing the crimes, he had long-term arguments with his wife, which stimulated mental issues, and he was not fully conscious when committing the crimes.

Jin Guihua claimed that she didn't believe her husband would do such things. After hearing from relatives that her husband would definitely be sentenced to death, she helped him escape to give him a few more days of life due to her weak legal awareness. Additionally, she suffers from cancer and hopes for leniency.

Yesterday morning, several relatives of the defendants attended the announcement of the verdict. Among them, the 23-year-old son Huang Mou of the two defendants cried continuously. When the verdict was announced and the defendants were taken out of the detention room, Huang Mou tried to forcefully enter the detention room to see his parents but was stopped by the judicial police. Huang Mou was emotionally agitated and refused interviews from reporters.
