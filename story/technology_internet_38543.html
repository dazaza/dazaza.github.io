The **1st Shanghai SEO Gathering**, hosted by the Shanghai SEO Lab, will officially open in Shanghai on December 22, 2008!

**Detailed Content of the SEO Gathering:**

We've been wanting to organize this gathering for a long time, but there was never a strong enough draw. Now we have one—because Kyw has met two former Google employees who are closely related to SEO.

**Guest Speaker One: TONY**

- **Background:** Former Chief SEM at Google (with an official certificate from Google; there is only one such certificate in China). He specializes in optimizing Google AdWords (GGAW), Baidu bidding, and more. In the past, he managed and optimized accounts like Taobao's GGAW account.

- **Speech Topic:** GGAW Optimization - How to Get Your Ads on the Left Side of Google (tentative title)

- **Speech Duration:** 20 minutes

- **Speech Topic:** To be determined

**[Details of the Shanghai SEO Gathering]**

- **Gathering Time:** December 22, 2007, from 1:00 PM to 5:00 PM

- **Participants:** Professionals in SEO and SEM fields

- **Gathering Size:** Limited to fewer than 30 people

- **Location:** Room 2006, No. 393 Dongdaming Road, Shanghai (the large classroom of Yiwen SEO Training Center)

- **Environment/Atmosphere:** Tea party style—everyone gathers together to drink tea, chat, eat snacks, listen to speeches, interact with guest speakers, and engage in free discussions.

- **Event Sponsor:** Kang Yiwen (Kyw) from Shanghai Yiwen Network Technology Co., Ltd.
