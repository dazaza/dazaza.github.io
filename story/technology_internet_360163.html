Xinyi Glass's construction of waste heat power stations shows significant benefits

by ditan360 on 2010-07-07 16:30:14

Low-carbon Network Special - The development of waste heat power generation in China's glass industry is still at an initial stage, and the technology is gradually maturing. Xinyi Glass took the lead in responding to national policies and has invested heavily in building a waste heat power station. By the end of 2006, it was fully operational for cooling and heating, and electricity supply has been fully provided recently. The economic benefits, environmental benefits, and social benefits of the waste heat power station are very significant, bringing nearly 2 million yuan in daily revenue to Xinyi Glass.

Xinyi Glass uses pure low-temperature waste heat power generation technology to recover the thermal energy from exhaust waste heat. Targeting the characteristics of waste heat resources from glass melting kilns and their working features, they have developed waste heat power generation technology suitable for Xinyi Glass' melting kiln characteristics. Through the heat exchange between the heat boiler and flue gas, saturated steam is generated, which is then processed through a saturated steam power system, achieving a new type of integrated cold supply, heat supply, and electricity use supply chain within the company.

The establishment of Xinyi Glass' waste heat power station does not require fuel consumption, produces no pollution during power generation, and is a clean and environmentally friendly green power generation technology that complies with national clean energy-saving industrial policies, with broad development space and prospects. He Wensan, the project leader of the Xinyi Glass waste heat power station, introduced that the establishment of the waste heat power station will solve all electricity needs (including production electricity), cold and hot air, and domestic hot water problems for Xinyi Glass. Currently, there are 13 sets of central air conditioning hosts installed on the company’s coating and laminated production lines, and a total of 500 central air conditioning sub-units in office areas, canteens, and dormitory buildings, all using steam refrigeration. Steam is used for cooking, disinfection in the canteen, and hot water supply in the dormitories. Xinyi Glass' waste heat power station generates 1,080 tons of steam daily and 3,500 watts per hour. Calculated at the current market price of 185 yuan per ton of steam, the waste heat power station brings nearly 2 million yuan in daily revenue to Xinyi Glass. If calculated in terms of standard coal, it is equivalent to saving over 150 tons of standard coal daily. Moreover, the waste heat produced by the glass melting kiln can be continuously recycled, and the benefits generated by such a circular economy cannot be measured by specific numbers. The establishment of the waste heat power station is a new emerging industry for Xinyi Glass, not only conserving energy usage but also avoiding pollution caused by smoke emissions from the glass melting kiln to the surrounding environment.

Under the increasingly stringent economic conditions of resource and environmental constraints, Xinyi Glass’ construction of a waste heat power station is an important measure to respond to the country's advocated energy and environmental protection policies, taking a path of low energy consumption and low pollution. Additionally, under the current background of rising energy prices, the establishment of the waste heat power station plays a crucial role in reducing costs, enhancing corporate competitiveness, and achieving sustainable development for Xinyi Glass.

About Xinyi Group

Xinyi Group (Glass) Co., Ltd. was founded in 1988 and currently employs more than 10,000 people. It is one of the main manufacturers in the global glass industry chain. Xinyi Glass is committed to providing products, services, and solutions that meet customer needs, creating long-term value and potential growth for customers and partners.

Xinyi Glass products and solutions cover areas such as float glass, ultra-white photovoltaic glass, automotive glass, and architectural glass. With flexible supply chain capabilities, reasonable production costs, one-stop supply methods, and professional logistics channels, we deliver our products to customers around the world.

Xinyi Glass products and solutions have been applied in over 100 countries and regions worldwide, included in the global supplier systems of whole vehicle manufacturers like Ford, General Motors, and Volkswagen, and formed long-term strategic partnerships with domestic brands such as Chery, Yutong, and Foton.

Xinyi Glass has consistently ranked first in China's automotive glass exports for four consecutive years, holding more than 18% of the global automotive glass replacement market and over 10% of China's LOW-E energy-saving architectural glass market. Its automotive windshield glass production capacity ranks first in China. Currently, Xinyi Glass has provided high-quality architectural glass products for landmark buildings in domestic and international cities, including the Shanghai Expo China Pavilion, the main venue of the World University Games, the Information and Communication Center of the 2008 Beijing Olympics - Digital Beijing Building, Guangzhou Victor Tower, Shenzhen Excellence Times Square, and Singapore Biopolis.
