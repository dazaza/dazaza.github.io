Synopsis:

Xu Xun, a female novelist portrayed by Angelica Lee, becomes an overnight sensation with her first published romance novel, becoming a popular writer. Her depiction of romantic love in the novel moves every reader. During a book fair, she announces her plan to write a new novel exploring the supernatural world, titled "Gwai Wik" (Ghost Domain). Given the impact of her first romance novel, all readers believe that Xu Xun can certainly bring everyone into a mysterious and eerie space through her writing skills and talent, captivating readers once again with an intriguing "haunted realm." After the book fair, Xu Xun begins to conceptualize and write "Gwai Wik," but after several attempts, the plot ideas she comes up with are not satisfactory, leaving Xu Xun in a creative rut. At this time, her former lover reappears in her life, further complicating her thoughts and leaving her without any creative state. In anger, she deletes the initial draft of "Gwai Wik" from her computer, and strange events begin to occur. Xu Xun starts experiencing unusual hallucinations and witnesses inexplicable phenomena! It seems as if everything supernatural she had fictionalized in her novel is now manifesting in reality! Mysterious objects appear in her room, broken strands of hair are scattered on the floor, making Xu Xun feel that she is not alone in the room! Repeated appearances of shadowy figures increase her fear, and these terrifying phenomena seem to be linked to the deleted draft on her computer. The eerie events she described in her novel start unfolding right before her eyes in real life.

One night, while riding the elevator alone, Xu Xun encounters a ghostly old woman who leads her suddenly into another bizarre dimension... an unimaginable ghostly domain!

In this strange space, Xu Xun meets her daughter from the real world whom she had aborted. Together, they venture into a graveyard filled with wandering spirits. Hordes of restless ghosts wander there, both terrifying and eerie. These spirits, having received no offerings for a long time, chase after any living person who approaches, seeking sacrifices. Fortunately, Xu Xun, guided by the elder ghost Liu Shaoming, has prepared a large amount of flowers and paper offerings. She scatters them among the ghosts in a critical moment, allowing her to escape...
