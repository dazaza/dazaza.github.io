The 5 Essential Secrets for Newcomers to Online Stores!

Store Owner's Space: http://space.taobao.com/4f563dc96ab25c0e7fbad65f502895a9.htm

Many friends who have just started their online stores will go through a long and painful process, during which business is not good. Therefore, many friends try every possible way to promote their stores. After promotion, there might be some effects, but the results are not particularly significant. Why are the results not satisfactory? The main reason is that the right promotional methods have not been found. Promotion requires skills to achieve twice the result with half the effort.

Based on my own experience in online promotion and operation, I would like to share with everyone the secrets of promoting an online store.

1. Train your mindset first, persistently promote: Many friends, when promoting, post promotional information on some forums or QQ, only to provoke the anger of many netizens or even malicious defamation. At this point, you must not get discouraged or give up, because this is the darkest time before success. So you should take advantage of the situation and promote even more crazily. One of the treasures of promotion is persistent promotion. Only by persistent promotion can you touch the hearts of customers. You cannot be hot one moment and cold the next. Even if you are wanted by all the netizens in the world and cursed by them, you cannot give up. Therefore, at the beginning of promotion, it is not about promoting the product or finding methods and techniques, but about training your mindset and psychological quality.

2. Always take the initiative to approach customers: Many friends think that after continuously posting promotional information, customers will come to you. This is not necessarily true. Why? Because even if customers see your information, why should they believe you? Why should they buy from you? Is it because of the low price? Without establishing trust with the customer, the customer generally will not choose a new seller for transactions. Besides, after you post promotional information, it is not certain that your target customers will see your information. Therefore, to make your business better, the most important thing is to take the initiative. In the process of fighting with customers, you must not play defense unless the customer really likes your product. No matter what, you must choose to take the initiative. How do you take the initiative? Find ways to dig out customers, find customers. For example, search on Baidu or GOOGLE for "want to buy *** product" or "is *** product good", there must be many potential customers who want to buy products and ask some netizens, especially digital products. For example, as a cosmetics seller, I often look for potential buyers on Taobao's "Beauty Clothing Home" or on some professional beauty and cosmetics exchange websites.

3. Attack the heart: There is a sentence in "The Art of War" by Sun Tzu: "Attack the city below, attack the heart above." People who run online stores should not think that doing business online is virtual, and customers cannot see themselves, so their words and actions are unimportant. In fact, the network may be virtual, but business is real. Therefore, doing business must be serious, honest, and sincere. Use your service, product, and attitude to infect your customers. Attack the heart above. Often many customers complain about the bad attitudes of large shops, so they are unwilling to buy from them and instead choose small shops. As an online store owner, how do you attack the heart? Enthusiastic service, serious service, meticulous service, chat with customers, talk about what customers like, etc. One of my customers often chats with me at around 1 a.m. Actually, I am very tired, but she seems energetic. I endure it because losing a customer means losing a group of customers.

4. You should learn to be a general: There is a saying in military strategy: "A soldier who does not want to be a general is not a good soldier." Indeed, it is easy to get a thousand soldiers, but hard to find a good general. Therefore, friends who run online stores must also have this mindset, and must learn to be a general rather than a soldier. Many online store owners work hard to promote themselves, act as customer service, upload items, and are very busy. Therefore, I suggest that all online store owners learn to be generals. Why do I say that? Soon after my store opened, a little sister from Shanghai University of Finance and Economics, who is from the north, came to buy my items and haggled with me. I did not lower the price because my product profit margin was very low. After chatting with her, she realized that she was wrong to ask me to lower the price. After our conversation, she happily became my friend and voluntarily became one of the promoters of my online store, enthusiastically recommending my products and store to her classmates and friends. By capturing the heart of a customer, I actually recruited an excellent promoter for my store. If I can gather enough such customers, then I can sit back as a general.

5. Strategy is correct, tactics change endlessly: Many friends who run online stores just want to make more money, but they haven't seriously considered how to make money. Relying on selling items to make money? What items to sell to make money? How much can be earned? When deciding to sell a certain item, have you thought about what special features and advantages your store should have? I see that many stores on Taobao have no special features, and they are competing on price, resulting in decreasing profits and eventually closing down. To avoid closing down and continue forever, you must have a clear strategic direction. Once the direction is correct, you can discuss tactics, because tactics are endless. What are tactics? For example, a friend of mine specializes in selling jeans, so his strategic direction is "to become the most professional jeans specialty store on Taobao." Such stores have characteristics, professionalism gives more advantages, and are more advantageous and viable than those who sell all kinds of products. The positioning is jeans, nothing else is done.

The above are just some insights I gained while operating my own online store. I hope we can all exchange ideas!
