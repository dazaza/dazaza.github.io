China Wedding Expo solves the problem of selecting wedding dresses for new couples in Guangzhou.

by zcs1272 on 2012-02-16 12:00:33

Unmarried men and women from Guangzhou wedding photography studios share the little things they observe in their lives. Before the wedding, I discussed with my wife whether we should rent or buy a wedding dress, and finally decided to buy it. After a month of shopping on the streets, we finally settled on a style and bought the desired wedding dress, returning home satisfied. These three experiences of choosing a wedding dress, as seen on HeXi.net, left a deep impression on me and evoked many feelings. I strongly wanted to document them and share them with everyone.

On the street full of wedding dresses, couples walked hand in hand, moving through various wedding dress stores, comparing, and picking. You don't realize it until you're getting married, but there are so many people getting married that almost every Guangzhou wedding photography studio has several couples, and larger stores are packed with people. Trying on wedding dresses is hard work; you have to keep trying them on, compare prices, styles, materials, accessories, whether the wedding dress matches the veil and gloves, how the dress looks under different lights, and more factors. During countless times when my wife tried on wedding dresses at the Guangzhou wedding photography studio while I waited, I noticed a very common phenomenon: all the soon-to-be grooms accompanying their fiancées were speechless and helpless when asked for opinions on one wedding dress after another. These workplace elites, rare types, successful in their careers, and socially adept men all experienced a mental blank and severe oxygen deficiency when faced with the question of whether the wedding dress style was beautiful. I noticed this phenomenon, observed each case, and ultimately confirmed that there were hardly any men behind the wedding dresses who were enthusiastically selecting them.

I eventually approved several of my wife's choices, pinpointed a wedding dress with absolute accuracy, gave several reasons, and she finally decided to take it, successfully completing the task of choosing a wedding dress. Before the wedding, I asked the staff at the Guangzhou wedding photography studio, "Is the wedding dress really that important to women? Why do you have to wear a wedding dress to get married?" My colleague looked at me as if I were an alien and said, "Ridiculous! Why would you get married without wearing a wedding dress?" I first laughed, then fainted, until someone called me to play cards after work, and I slowly regained consciousness. Since the wedding dress is a woman's favorite, as a man, instead of being dragged around by your partner, it's better to offer advice early and settle things quickly. After following my wife around and figuring out the tricks, I presented her with several reasons, successfully settling on the wedding dress style. Here, I share this with those who have already gone through it and those who will.

Dedicated to the male compatriots who have endured great torment at Guangzhou wedding photography studios! Brave groomsmen will be remembered forever in history!
