IBM will provide Lashou.com with global ERP implementation services to build an integrated solution targeting group-buying websites.

by beetgk2958 on 2012-02-23 14:08:42

Senior Internet expert, CEO of Shanjuyuan, Liu Xingliang said that informatization can closely link enterprises, merchants, and consumers in the enterprise operation process. It can also manage the supply chain system to achieve orderly group-buying operations. In the eyes of insiders, the "marriage" between Lashou.com and IBM heralds the beginning of group-buying informatization. Wang Rulin refers to Lashou.com as a "valuable reference" for other group-buying websites. Group-buying informatization is not only an internal need for the development of group-buying enterprises but also a necessity for the industry to undergo a transformation after experiencing vicious competition. "The strategic cooperation between Lashou.com and IBM will have a significant impact on the informatization of the group-buying industry," according to Su Meng, associate professor of marketing at Guanghua School of Management, Peking University. Informatization has great significance for group-buying and even the entire e-commerce industry. "Through informatization management, group-buying enterprises can better understand users' gender and preferences, thereby making precise and real-time analysis and predictions in the shopping process, guiding the signing activities with merchants, managing inventory and the supply chain system." Recently, Lashou.com announced its cooperation with IBM, which will provide global ERP implementation services to build an integrated solution for group-buying websites. It is reported that this is the first time IBM has cooperated with a group-buying company to specially design for the combination of online and offline operations of Chinese group-buying companies. IBM's smart strategy will help Lashou.com real-time grasp internal and external changes of the enterprise team, achieving maximization of corporate economic benefits. Internet companies in the O2O era have complex and ever-changing informatization needs, posing unprecedented challenges even for IBM, the world's top management expert. Wu Bo, founder and CEO of Lashou.com, believes that the cooperation between Lashou.com and IBM will effectively enhance the maintenance service of Lashou.com's core business system, strengthen enterprise informatization construction, improve management efficiency, and maximize consumer rights protection. Group-buying has entered a new era of intensive cultivation. Currently, the group-buying industry is gradually entering a stage of healthy competition. When each enterprise is pursuing innovation and openness, informatization has become the new standard for the group-buying industry. Wang Rulin, deputy director of the E-commerce Professional Committee of the China Information Economics Society and a well-known practical e-commerce expert, believes that Lashou.com's adoption of the ERP system marks a major awakening of group-buying enterprise managers, which will greatly improve the management efficiency of group-buying websites. Upgrading management, informatization will become the new standard for the group-buying industry. Every change in the industry has a leader, and this time is no exception.
