"70% Mortgage Rate Discount" to End? Recently, some media reported that several banks would increase the minimum mortgage discount. So, what's the situation in Chongqing?

1. Slight Differences in Loan Policies Among the Four Major State-Owned Banks

Bank of China (Chongqing Branch)

Conditions for applying for a 70% rate discount at Bank of China include: First, it must be the first home purchase, previously enjoying an 85% discount, and loans issued before October last year. Second, no early repayment within three years after application; if there is early repayment, a penalty fee for the early repaid portion will be charged. Third, one of the following conditions must be met: 1. The main borrower’s housing fund details for one year, with a monthly amount of more than 750 yuan; 2. The main borrower’s salary details, with a monthly income of more than 8,000 yuan; 3. A large fixed deposit in the loan handling bank for more than three months, with amounts of 50,000 or 100,000 yuan depending on different conditions.

Industrial and Commercial Bank of China (Chongqing Branch)

ICBC's detailed rules for adjusting existing mortgage rates stipulate that personal housing loans issued before October 27, 2008, with an execution benchmark rate of 85%, can enjoy a 70% preferential rate starting from February 10, 2009, provided that the borrower has no continuous default records of two periods (inclusive) or more in the past two years and has no bad records for other loans in ICBC.

Construction Bank (Chongqing Branch)

The Construction Bank raised the threshold for first-home mortgages. Only those with a down payment of more than 40% can enjoy a 70% rate discount. If the down payment is 20%, the rate can only be discounted by 85%. This is a unified requirement by the head office for all branches nationwide. In addition to raising the down payment requirements, the size of the house also directly affects the available discount, meaning the larger the area of the first home purchase, the smaller the discount enjoyed, and it may even be the benchmark rate.

Agricultural Bank of China (Chongqing Branch)

For mortgage customers above 300,000 yuan without any bad credit record, they will automatically enjoy a 70% rate discount; for high-quality customers below 300,000 yuan, they need to apply independently, and if they do not meet the high-quality customer standards, it will be difficult to enjoy a 70% rate discount.

2. New Highlights of Seven-Fold Mortgage Discounts in Medium and Small Banks

Minsheng Bank (Chongqing Branch)

For customers executing the People's Bank of China's published benchmark interest rate with a 15% downward adjustment, the bank will adjust their loan interest rate after January 1, 2009. The new rate will execute the People's Bank of China's published benchmark interest rate with a 30% downward adjustment. However, for customers who owe principal and interest on the bank's loans or have other default situations, they will not enjoy the interest rate discount.

Everbright Bank (Chongqing Branch)

For customers without overdue repayments, the bank system will automatically adjust to a 70% benchmark rate. If the first home loan has been fully repaid, and the second home is below the city's per capita housing standard, it can also enjoy a 70% rate discount.

CITIC Bank (Chongqing Branch)

Customers with a loan amount over 300,000 yuan, without two overdue payments of more than 10 days within a year, can enjoy a 70% rate discount.

Chongqing Rural Commercial Bank

To enjoy the preferential rate for existing mortgages, two conditions must be met: First, the existing personal mortgage has already executed a 15% downward adjustment on the benchmark rate; Second, since January 1, 2008, there have been no continuous defaults of two periods (inclusive) or more and no other bad loan balances in the rural commercial bank. No application is required, and starting from February 1, 2009, they will automatically enjoy a 30% downward adjustment (i.e., 70% preferential rate). For customers enjoying the 70% preferential rate, if they continuously default on two periods (inclusive) or more during the remaining repayment period, the 70% preferential rate will be canceled, and interest and penalty interest will be charged according to the original contract terms. It is understood that the policy launched this time by the rural commercial bank has the shortest bad credit evaluation period among all banks, only 12 months, starting from January 1, 2008, which is expected to expand the number of beneficiaries.

China Merchants Bank (Chongqing Branch)

To apply for a 70% discount on existing mortgage rates, several conditions must be met. First, the local real estate management department must issue a query result of the total housing area based on the property registration information, which should be lower than the local per capita living area (using the statistical department's previous year's statistical results as a reference standard); Second, the purchased house must be ordinary self-occupied housing, so buying villas or houses with excessively large areas may not pass the approval; Third, comprehensive creditworthiness must be evaluated, including not only the ability to repay credit cards and house loans but also possibly checking the payment status of water, electricity, and property management fees; Fourth, the historical contribution level to China Merchants Bank.

Industrial Bank (Chongqing Branch)

Industrial Bank stated that when borrowers apply for adjustments in the downward execution ratio of mortgage rates, they must first meet the definition of residents' first purchase of ordinary self-occupied housing. Secondly, maintain good credit records in the People's Bank of China's personal credit report without overdue records; or have overdue records but can provide reasonable supporting materials proving that the overdue was not due to subjective intent, also allowing them to enjoy the policy of downward adjustment of existing mortgage rates. For borrowers meeting the above conditions simultaneously, each branch can determine whether the loan interest rate will be reduced and the level of reduction based on the principle that the lowest mortgage execution rate shall not be less than 0.7 times (inclusive) the benchmark interest rate of the same term and tier of the People's Bank of China. Each branch mainly depends on the regional economic conditions, the implementation policies of peer banks regarding existing mortgage rates, and the specific circumstances of the borrowers.
