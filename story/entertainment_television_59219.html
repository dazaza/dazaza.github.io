The long-running drama "Unforgettable Intentions" Season 2 will start airing two episodes daily at 17:00 on CCTV Channel 8 starting tomorrow (February 15th). According to Wang Guohui, Director of the CCTV Film and Television Department, "Unforgettable Intentions" started broadcasting on September 2nd last year with 168 episodes, achieving a peak viewership rate of 2.23%. Therefore, CCTV has introduced another 160 episodes, scheduled to air during the Spring Festival.

Wang Guohui stated that previously, CCTV had never introduced such a long TV series like "Unforgettable Intentions". Initially, they weren't very confident about its viewership, especially not expecting a show starting at 5 PM to achieve a peak viewership rate of 2.23%, even higher than some prime-time dramas. Hence, after the first season ended, another 160 episodes were introduced, making "Unforgettable Intentions" broadcast a total of 328 episodes. The airing time for the remaining episodes will depend on the viewership ratings, and there is a high possibility of continuing broadcasts. If the entire series is introduced, the 526-episode "Unforgettable Intentions" will surpass the Korean drama "Mermaid Lady" to become the longest series on CCTV.
