Tourist vehicles in Jiabei Water City: residents of Liaocheng rush to buy Huayang electric cars to pick up and drop off children

by ddjiaoche95 on 2012-03-07 14:33:56

Recently, careful residents in Liaocheng would see Hua Yang electric vehicles shuttling back and forth outside many schools in Liaocheng when they pick up or drop off kids.

These exquisite electric vehicles are safe to drive and easy to operate, becoming a beautiful scenery line in the water city north of the river. The drivers are mostly middle-aged or elderly people or women who use electric vehicles to pick up or drop off their children, which can protect them from wind and rain, making people admire endlessly.

"These days, with the weather getting colder, there is a continuous stream of people coming to our place to buy Hua Yang electric vehicles for picking up and dropping off their children." On December 12th, the sales manager of Hua Yang Electric Vehicle in Liaocheng, Mr. He, told me that because electric vehicles save costs and procedures, many parents choose electric cars to pick up and drop off their children. Currently, in the entire Liaocheng, more than a thousand people have purchased electric vehicles for this purpose.

Mr. Wang bought a Hua Yang electric car for his wife. He said that buying an electric car for his wife could improve her quality of life, mainly for the convenience of picking up and dropping off their child, sparing both the adult and the child from trouble.

On October 4th, Grandpa Li, over 60 years old, bought a Hua Yang electric car. He said it would be convenient to pick up and drop off his granddaughter for school, and it saves money. An electric car can save more than 10,000 yuan a year on fuel, maintenance, and insurance compared to a microcar. Moreover, when returning to Linqing's hometown, the toll station is free, which is really cost-effective.

Grandpa Liu Shicheng, 68 years old, now has nothing to do and often picks up and drops off his grandson for school. Since public transportation cannot be used, riding a tricycle is troublesome, unsafe, and lacks dignity. After purchasing a Hua Yang electric car, he finds it convenient to pick up and drop off his grandson, and traveling becomes worry-free. He said that driving 100 kilometers with a Hua Yang electric car only costs 8 yuan. Several of his friends, seeing him driving well, also bought electric cars.

Liu Wei introduced that the main reasons for the popularity of Hua Yang electric cars in Liaocheng are threefold: first, the Hua Yang new energy brand is good with solid quality; second, the Hua Yang electric car is easy to drive, safe, performs well, and offers high comfort; third, the operating cost is low, with a cost of only 8 yuan per 100 kilometers. With the winter arrival and the rise in gasoline and diesel prices, many urban and rural residents in Liaocheng who originally planned to buy cars have shifted their attention to Hua Yang electric cars. It is understood that the Hua Yang Panda Supreme Edition electric vehicle uses automatic gear control, is simple and convenient, and can be charged easily with civilian 220V power, accessible and safe. Besides the advantages of mature technology and safety reliability, Hua Yang electric vehicles also enjoy a two-year/40,000-kilometer warranty and convenient after-sales service.

Ms. Lv, the general agent located in Xiangjiang, Liaocheng, said that on December 11th, four electric cars were sold in one day. Hua Yang electric cars have been on the market in Liaocheng for more than half a year, accumulating over 300 sales, with as many as six sold in one day at its peak.
