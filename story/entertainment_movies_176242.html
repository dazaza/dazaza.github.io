by qvodfans on 2009-07-14 07:44:29

Mike was a prominent figure in school. As a basketball player, he originally received a scholarship that would have directly sent him to college. However, in order to be with his girlfriend, Scarlett, he gave up this opportunity.

25 years later, Mike, now a middle-aged man, has achieved nothing. He broke up with his wife, Scarlett, and rarely shares common language with his children. Moreover, his work at the pharmaceutical company is utterly boring. The dispirited Mike returned to his high school, reminiscing about his former glory. There, he met a janitor, and during their conversation, Mike confessed his desire to return to the age of 17. If everything started over, he wouldn't give up the scholarship and the chance to further his studies, which would make his life very different.

On his way back from school, Mike accidentally discovered that the mysterious janitor was preparing to jump into the river. Surprised, Mike quickly jumped out of the car to save him, but unexpectedly, the janitor suddenly disappeared. Instead, Mike, who ran too fast, fell into the river and miraculously transformed into his 17-year-old self.

Afterward, with the help of his friends, Mike returned to high school. He had many difficulties to solve, such as being classmates with his unknowing children. It was through a series of encounters at school that Mike realized that his original choice was actually the most perfect life; he just didn't know how to cherish it. Now, having repented, his most urgent task is to restore his original appearance and win back his wife and children...
