Definition of winning numbers:

The numbers bet in the current period nationwide, the combination method with the least is the winning number, you should understand this right? For computer lottery with huge prizes, the numbers sold each period (the numbers that lottery players buy) will be counted after the sales stop. Whether one first prize or twenty first prizes are drawn, these numbers are the combination of numbers with the least bets nationwide in the current period, and are not limited to a single number.

Prize issuing conditions:

Based on the accumulation of the prize pool and the combination of numbers sold nationwide in the current period. The two are closely combined. If the "mandatory indicator" of the prize pool accumulation does not meet the standard, no first prize will be issued. When the prize pool accumulates to a certain extent, the center will release water, in order to "wake up" the lottery players, there will be a sudden explosion of more than ten first prizes, even more than twenty. Then continue with the "chain" where 1-5 first prizes are issued, and then recover funds in the next period with 0-2 first prizes, which is called entering the so-called "normal track". Then it repeats over and over again in a cycle.

Remember: The center is not just to "take care" of the grand prize winners, but also to consider all award levels for the current period as a whole! The total sales and the total amount of all awards distributed are macro-controlled, and the former must reach the mandatory indicator.

Grand Prize Base:

Why do we say there are so many grand prizes when we see more than ten first prizes? This is the problem of the grand prize base. Suppose since the establishment of the award, the base is set at 30-50 people per period, if a certain period draws 10-20, you would think there are not many people this period! Conversely, I don't need to say more, right? In fact, the base is set, the more or less in a certain period completely depends on the overall situation of the current period.

About prediction:

Some people say, I think a certain number should come out this period, and it did, how to explain this? I think everyone has ignored a condition, which is, we now analyze lottery data quite dependent on the trend chart, this trend is a man-made inevitable result. 85-90% of the national lottery players look at the trend, maybe the combination of numbers with the least bets nationwide in the current period includes the number you like, this can only be said to be an accidental result in the inevitability given by people. I post threads every period, sometimes I can guess a few, it's not that we can predict, it's just coincidence.
