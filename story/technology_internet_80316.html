5 Online Alarm Clocks

by mylovehisa on 2008-07-29 14:42:50

From a real alarm clock to the one on mobile phones, after having computers, it always seems like there is something missing from this all-powerful computer. For example, if you are boiling water and an engaging movie or TV drama makes you forget the time, it is only when steam fills the room that you realize - oh, the water is about to boil dry. An online alarm clock can fill this gap, allowing you to set an alarm without leaving your computer, reminding you of what you need to do. Just like Song Zhen, who continues playing games at mealtime; this alarm clock would be useful for her. Below, I will introduce a few online alarm clocks:

1. Kuku Klok: The panel on the left is used to adjust the time, while the one on the right is for selecting the alarm sound type. There are four sound options available: rooster crowing, classic old alarm clock, electronic alarm clock, and heavy metal guitar. After setting, just click "Set Alarm."

2. Onlineclock: The interface is black, simple, and straightforward, just like a real alarm clock. You can adjust the font size and background color.

3. Nakedalarmclock: The top row displays the current time. The triangles above and below are used to adjust the time. The black and white fan-shaped areas represent 15 minutes, 30 minutes, and 45 minutes respectively. What's different about this alarm clock compared to the ones mentioned earlier is that it allows you to set seconds. AM stands for morning, PM for afternoon. Select the alarm tone using 1, 2, 3, 4, then after setting the alarm with "Set Alarm," it's done.

4. Music-alarm-clock: What's different about this one is that you can choose multiple alarm tones, and it also offers a radio option. When the time comes, the radio will play directly. So this alarm clock can also be used as a timed radio. It's in three steps: set the time, choose the tone, then "Alarm ON."
