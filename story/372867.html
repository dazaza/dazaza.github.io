Guiyang Countryside Pure Play Private Tour, 4 Days 3 Nights

by zhou519621 on 2010-12-01 11:46:15

Departure Time: Every day

Relevant Attractions: Seven Stars Park, Diecai Mountain, Elephant Trunk Hill, Jingjiang Palace, Yulong River, Xingping Ancient Town, Xingping Fishing Village, Silver Cave, Gu Dong Waterfall

Itinerary Arrangement:

Day 1:

Arrive in Guilin and check into the hotel. In the evening, you can take a boat or walk to visit the Guilin circular water system -- [Two Rivers and Four Lakes], enjoying the unique night scenery of Guilin's mountains and waters, and feeling one of China's top ten livable cities - Guilin city. Stay in Guilin.

Day 2:

Visit Guilin's city emblem -- [Elephant Trunk Hill]: This has always been a tourist attraction, with its charming scenery, magical image, and moving legends that have captivated countless literati; then visit [Jingjiang Palace] Duxiu Peak Scenic Area: The poem "Guilin's landscape is the best under heaven" is engraved on Duxiu Peak. The Jingjiang Palace will let you feel the feng shui treasure land atmosphere favored by ancient Guilin princes and nobles, while also being able to climb to the top of [Duxiu Peak] for a panoramic view of Guilin! After lunch, we approach the confluence of rivers and mountains - [Diecai Mountain]. We continue to tour the large ecological garden praised as "the celestial Big Dipper, the earthly Seven Stars" - [Seven Stars Park] (Flower Bridge, Camel Peak, Huaxia Light Square, Clinton President Speech Place). Stay in Guilin.

Day 3:

After breakfast, take a bus to Xingping, and take a boat or bamboo raft to tour the famous [Xingping Li River] visited by Dr. Sun Yat-sen and President Clinton. Have lunch in Xingping, and visit the typical Ming and Qing period northern Guangxi residential area - [Xingping Ancient Town]. Then take a bus to Yangshuo. Upon arriving in Yangshuo, you can fully enjoy the leisure time here. You can find a café in West Street and sit quietly reading books or newspapers, or watch passersby, watching time flow through your hands. In the evening, you can choose to watch the world-famous performance - [Impression Liu Sanjie] at the largest natural theater in the world, experiencing the rich ethnic art style. In the evening, return to the bars on West Street for revelry, where the charm of the ancient town blends perfectly with modern lifestyles, making it hard not to get lost in the experience. Stay in Yangshuo.

Day 4:

Sleep in naturally this morning before planning today’s itinerary. You can head to the nearby [Silver Cave] scenic area to see the magnificent karst landforms; then go to [Yulong River] to experience the fun of bamboo raft drifting. For those who live long-term in urban areas, just approaching the Yulong River will evoke an irresistible sense of closeness, making you want to jump right into the river and become one with it. At noon, you can taste the locally renowned beer fish at Moon Mountain. Depending on the tourists' return schedule, when the time is appropriate, take a bus back to Guilin and conclude the pleasant journey.
