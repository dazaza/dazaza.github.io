Nanning Youai Hospital: Don't Forget the Six Laws of Autumn Weight Loss

by abizpb6901 on 2011-10-03 13:13:12

Losing weight is a lifelong pursuit for women. If you miss the best time to lose weight in summer, then losing weight in autumn is also a good option as long as you use the right methods. Here are six rules for losing weight in autumn.

Note 1: Drink an appropriate amount of water or soup for weight loss.

Drinking water is an essential need in people's daily lives. In summer, you can eat watermelon and tomatoes to quench your thirst. Watermelon juice and winter melon soup can also promote urination. Overly restricting water intake can cause sweat gland disorders in overweight individuals, making it difficult to regulate body temperature. Especially, concentrated urine makes it hard to excrete metabolic residues, which may lead to intense thirst, headaches, and fatigue. Drinking an appropriate amount of water can replenish fluids and regulate fat metabolism.

Frequently drinking soup ensures health. Drinking soup is beneficial for health. Studies have found that soup is a good appetite suppressant. Therefore, some obese individuals use drinking soup as a method for weight loss. Drinking more soup during regular meals can reduce the intake of staple foods, thereby achieving the goal of weight loss.

Note 2: The new revolution of high-protein diets and vinegar for weight loss

Principle: High-carbohydrate foods can cause obesity, uncontrollable hunger, and appetite. Therefore, limiting carbohydrate intake and consuming large amounts of high-protein foods can reduce weight and maintain a slim figure.

The cycle of this weight-loss diet is two weeks. In the 14-day weight-loss plan, you can only eat an appropriate amount of vegetables but can consume enough milk, eggs, meat, and fish. This puts the body into a state where it relies on fat rather than carbohydrates for energy.

Note 3: Dietary fiber for weight loss

Fiber can hinder food absorption. Fiber absorbs water in the stomach and expands, forming a larger volume, giving a feeling of fullness and helping to reduce food intake, playing a certain role in controlling weight. Eating foods rich in fiber allows for proper digestion and absorption within a certain period of time, followed by the excretion of waste. People who consume more dietary fiber chew more times, thus slowing down their eating speed. As a result, the small intestine can slowly absorb nutrients, making it difficult for blood sugar levels to rise.

Since dietary fiber promotes intestinal peristalsis, if consumed in large quantities, constipation naturally decreases, and the incidence of colon cancer also drops. Therefore, it is beneficial for obese individuals to consume more fiber.

Note 4: Control staple food intake and limit sweets

If the original food intake was large, the staple food can be reduced gradually, decreasing by 50 grams for each of the three meals a day. Gradually control the staple food intake to around 250-300 grams. Grains like wheat, rice, and some coarse grains can be selected, but the quantity must be strictly limited, developing the habit of eating until you're about 70-80% full. For starchy and extremely sweet foods such as sweet potatoes, potatoes, lotus root powder, jam, honey, candy, preserved fruits, malted milk essence, and sweet snacks made from fruit juice, try to eat less or avoid them altogether.

For foods high in fat, such as peanuts, walnuts, sesame seeds, various animal oils, cream, and fried foods, they should also be consumed in moderation. Side dishes can include lean meat, fish, eggs, soybean products, and vegetables and fruits with low sugar content.

Note 5: No eating five hours before bedtime

One major taboo of weight loss is eating before going to bed. When sleeping, the body does not need movement, and all the food eaten will be absorbed by the body and stored as fat. If you cannot resist hunger, you can only eat a small amount of boiled greens or fruit.

Note 6: Stand for half an hour after meals

In fact, the biggest reason women gain weight is neglect. Due to busy work and study schedules, there is no time to reasonably adjust their lifestyle and arrange their diet and rest. Actually, you can stand at least half an hour after eating a full meal. This not only avoids the trouble of fat accumulating around the abdomen but also saves the trouble of making up afterward.
