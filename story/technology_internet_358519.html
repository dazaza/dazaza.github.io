Website Program Development

News Information Release System

    The News Information Release System provides clients with instant publishing needs, conveniently and quickly updating website content. In addition to basic text editing functions, it also includes features like image information, static page links, related topic information, information classification, keyword search, maintenance and update management. It offers a complete set of workflow management methods for published information and can flexibly meet customer demands. The system is easy to use, requiring only an ordinary computer clerk to perform publishing and maintenance work. It provides date retrieval functionality for effective management of historical data.

Product Display Release System

   The Product Display Release System manages updates to news, new product releases, and industry dynamics on web pages, systematically and standardly releasing them onto websites. Front-end users can browse and query through the pages, while back-end management can handle product prices, descriptions, sample images, and more types of information. This system can further be upgraded and expanded into an advanced online store system.

   The front end can categorize products into several major categories, allowing browsers to search and query products by category, name, price, etc., with results displayed in a list. The back-end sets up an administrator maintenance interface, enabling editing of each product's information, setting product images, querying, modifying, adding, or deleting products based on their category, model, content, etc. Product categories are dynamically managed, with the back-end maintenance interface adding categories and product types, which instantly appear on the front end.

Website Forum System

   The forum system is the place on the website with abundant information and popularity. A good BBS can attract a considerable number of visitors and also shoulders the responsibility of external publicity, message release, and collecting customer feedback for the website. It is an indispensable part of both websites and internal networks within organizations.

Online Mall System

   Providing rented or free network space for merchants to conduct online sales, showcasing their products for online sales.

   If a company originally mainly sold products or services via mail order, or if there are many potential customers outside the local area to explore, then establishing an online store is very suitable. This helps increase sales volume. Online stores typically provide visitors with an online shopping list, allowing customers to place orders and make purchases online using e-commerce software and credit cards.

Consultation Feedback System

  The Consultation Feedback System establishes a communication channel between the website and users, promptly reflecting user needs regarding the company's products and services. To some extent, it addresses user questions or provides solutions. The system uses electronic forms so users can fill out feedback information according to predefined tables. After submission, the information is stored in a database, where administrators can log into the back-end management system to filter and reply. Alternatively, authorized personnel can respond on the front-end feedback list page. Based on actual needs, replies can automatically notify users via email.

Online Voting System

  Users can utilize the voting system on the website to conduct surveys and statistics, collecting public attitudes towards products or events. The site can define survey content at any time and instantly statistically analyze the results. For projects requiring feedback, the system allows administrators to customize this function.

Online Recruitment System

  Online recruitment is an application that enables real-time recruitment and management through a website. Enterprises can publish job information as needed through the back-end management interface, managing, analyzing, searching, and statistically analyzing the published and feedback job and application information.

Supply and Demand Information System

  The Supply and Demand Information System is specifically developed for websites establishing B2B information platforms, providing enterprises with a platform to publish supply and demand information and facilitate trade information exchange. It aggregates enterprise supply and demand information, fully leveraging the advantages of the network to ensure reasonable allocation and sharing of resources.

Membership Management System

  Visitors fill out registration forms online, becoming full members after system approval. Pages add login verification functions, allowing front-end members to maintain their personal registration information, modify and delete personal information. If passwords are forgotten, they can be queried online. The back-end sets up a membership management interface where administrators can classify and query member information (by date, name), and delete entries.

  Website content is personalized for members, displaying different content based on membership levels. Members can choose personalized page designs suited to themselves. The back-end management interface can classify members based on certain rules (e.g., gender, age group, region, cumulative shopping) and set membership levels, supporting automatic upgrades based on rules.
