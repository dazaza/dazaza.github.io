Giving up being a beauty to become a male servant! If you go out for sale, wouldn't you earn more!" "Hey, what are you talking about?" Yun Xiaoxiao got a little angry: "One last chance, do you want me to replace you? If not, I'll leave, I'll find other work, and you will be a servant for generations! Goodbye, offspring of a servant!" Yun Xiaoxiao stood up, took a bite of bread, and chewed hard. It was really tasteless. Alas! There used to be so much delicious food, why didn't I eat more? What a loss! "Wait!" The young man grabbed his yellow hair and compromised: "Let's discuss how to operate without getting caught, most importantly... my mom can't know!" "Where is your mom?" Yun Xiaoxiao felt happy inside. It seems there is a way. Four thousand yuan, Haitai customer outdoor equipment, haha, Yun Xiaoxiao isn't useless after all! She looked at her hands, those fingers were as white as jade onions. Hopefully, no one will suspect. "In the countryside, far away from here..." "Far away, what are you afraid of! I'll replace you, use your name to work as a male servant, they will tell your mom that you have reported, and I'll deal with the rest as appropriate, so you can live freely!" Yun Xiaoxiao analyzed enthusiastically. The young man thought for a moment: "The idea is good, but if we get caught, it will be because of the fact that you're a woman!" "Don't worry, I have a way!" Yun Xiaoxiao chuckled. "That's great, my name is Yu Shangtai, this is the address of the villa I'm going to, and my phone number. Call me if anything goes wrong! I'll come to save you!" The young man handed over a piece of paper with his name and phone number on it: "Remember, you must be in place before tomorrow afternoon, I entrust you with this!" "No problem! I want to go today, I have nowhere to stay!" Yun Xiaoxiao took the piece of paper and gently unfolded it. When she saw the address written on it, she was instantly stunned. Wasn't this address the current Yun family villa? "Wait, wasn't this the person who was hiring servants before? The Yun family has gone bankrupt! No need to go!" "No, it's the new owner, surnamed Ou, they just moved in recently!" "Surnamed Ou?" Yun Xiaoxiao sat down dejectedly. So there was a new owner, no wonder those people took away everything from her house. It seems the Yun family is really finished, even the villa belongs to others now. How sorrowful. I don't know what it feels like to revisit old places, but unfortunately, my identity has changed. From a young lady to a male servant, from being served to serving others, alas! Yu Shangtai raised his fist high: "I am finally... free!" Watching Yu Shangtai's flying yellow hair, Yun Xiaoxiao really envied him. Didn't she used to be like that too? Every time she skipped class, she would also raise her fist high and cheer. Now she is not Yun Xiaoxiao anymore, let Miss Yun become history. That spoiled young lady who often hung out in dance halls, game rooms, and high-end stores no longer exists. The person standing here is called Yu Shangtai. "I am Yu Shangtai~ ~ ~" Yun Xiaoxiao stepped onto the bench, stretched out her arms, and shouted loudly. Yu Shangtai stared blankly at Yun Xiaoxiao. Is she really that happy? To be a servant and use someone else's name? "Being a servant, you look like you're about to become an empress!" "Empress?" Yun Xiaoxiao was startled for a moment. Didn't she used to be an empress? Unfortunately, that destiny is no longer hers. She continued shouting: "Farewell, the empress of the past!" After Yun Xiaoxiao had shouted enough, she jumped off the bench and extended her hand in friendship: "That's it! Yu Shangtai, I hope we both succeed!" "Oh!" Yu Shangtai also extended his hand. The two shook hands in friendly manner, sincerely blessing each other, then parted ways in the park. There's work now, and she doesn't have to be Xiao Xiao anymore. The airs of a young lady can be put aside, no need to worry about those embarrassing things. Yun Xiaoxiao suddenly felt very satisfied. Different status leads to different requirements for life. Yun Xiaoxiao, this name, no longer exists. It can only represent Xiao Xiao's past. Now, completely abandoning it, she no longer feels sorry. Come on, poor little male servant! The first thing Yun Xiaoxiao needs to do is cut off her long, shiny black hair. From now on, she will no longer be Yun Xiaoxiao, thoroughly burying that name. She gathered up her courage and walked into the barber shop. Decide, stop being a woman, become a man! The barber hurried out. "Miss, what hairstyle would you like?" "Cut short!" Yun Xiaoxiao gripped her waterfall-like long hair and gritted her teeth, saying those two words. "How short? To the ears?" "Cut it into that hairstyle!" Yun Xiaoxiao's finger pointed to a poster on the wall. The barber followed her finger and looked, hesitating for a moment, feeling a bit troubled. "Miss, that poster's hairstyle is for men..." "Just cut it, stop blabbering! If it looks like a woman, I won't pay you!" Yun Xiaoxiao rudely sat down. The barber frowned. Why did he encounter such a customer today? Insisting on cutting her hair like a man's, and looking like a woman is unacceptable. But the customer is God, cut whatever she wants! As long as she pays, it's fine. "Rest assured, our barbershop's principle is customer satisfaction! Guaranteed to make you look like a handsome young man after the cut!" It didn't take long, the haircut was done. Yun Xiaoxiao looked at herself in the mirror and was momentarily stunned. A super handsome young man appeared, if she changes clothes... probably could pass as a man, the only drawback is the feminine features? Must figure out a way to deal with it.
