TCL and iQIYI Join Hands to Promote Internet TV: Cloud Computing and Big Data Support the TV+

by anonymous on 2013-09-04 16:01:19

Last night, TCL and iQIYI jointly launched an Internet TV product called "TCL iQIYI TV - TV+", a company that claims to know TV best, partnering with a company that considers itself the most knowledgeable about the Internet. Behind TV+, supporting the name of the Internet TV, are cloud computing and big data.

Internet and TV crossover collaboration

TCL Multimedia CEO Hao Yi said this cooperation is a combination of "rich and handsome + rich and handsome". With 32 years of accumulation and 150 million TVs designed, researched, manufactured, and serviced, combined with iQIYI's monthly coverage of 400 million PC, PAD, and mobile users' Internet video service capabilities.

Gong Yu, founder and CEO of iQIYI, stated that currently, video users' viewing habits are shifting from PCs and mobile ends to TV screens. The traditional TV industry is also facing an entertainment transformation in the living room, and TV will gradually become the center of home entertainment. Under this trend, iQIYI and TCL concentrate their respective advantages to lead market changes, aiming to provide users with excellent experiences that follow trends. TV+ is the first product of the cooperation between iQIYI and TCL, and also the pioneering work of the "iQIYI inside" strategy. iQIYI is committed to working with outstanding partners to provide excellent Internet video services to Chinese users and netizens.

In terms of product hardware configuration, TV+ is equipped with professional TV chips, A++ screens, and has pioneered an independent four-drive speaker system and five-in-one remote control.

In addition, TV+ aggregates the high-definition video resources of iQIYI and PPS, providing more clear, smooth, and abundant free content for users without additional fees, bringing ultimate operational experience and one-stop Internet services. Gong Yu, founder and CEO of iQIYI, said that users do not need to pay extra fees to permanently watch over 200,000 episodes of high-definition video content on TV+, covering movies, popular dramas, variety shows, anime, and more, with real-time updates.

Worth noting is that the globally first-of-its-kind "piano key-style" five-in-one controller of TV+ has many highlights. The entire remote control only has 17 keys but integrates the control of TV, set-top box, voice, motion sensing, and air mouse into one body. The ability to synchronize operations with the set-top box is the biggest highlight, completely solving the current situation of alternating use of two remote controls for TV and set-top box.

Behind TV+: Cloud Computing, Big Data

Hao Yi emphasized that TV+ is a deeply customized product, with both parties putting in a lot of effort. It adheres to the concept of continuous upgrades for Internet TVs, constantly releasing new upgrade packages, and must uphold an open attitude, including the construction of ecosystems, etc., which are all very important.

"Currently, there are many so-called Internet TVs on the market, such as smart TVs, cloud TVs, Internet TVs, network TVs, many of which claim to be Internet TVs just by plugging in a network cable. TCL has gone through that era, but with the further deepening of broadband China, Internet TVs will definitely have large-scale breakthroughs." said Hao Yi.

Gong Yu pointed out that the already-born Internet TVs have not reached the original design goals of Internet TVs. In terms of user experience, content, interface, smoothness, clarity, and cloud processing capabilities, TV+ has far surpassed previous products, supported by cloud computing and big data.

TCL iQIYI TV is uniquely bound to the Central Galaxy Internet TV Integration Operation Platform, with content operation and services handled by the Galaxy Internet TV Company.

Priced at 4567 Yuan

It is understood that early this morning, TCL iQIYI TV - TV+ was officially launched on JD.com. TCL and iQIYI announced the launch of two versions of TV+: the Noble Edition and the Classic Edition. The Noble Edition of TV+ was launched first, priced at 4567 Yuan, with the first batch of JD.com flash sales offering a gift package worth 1357 Yuan.

Hao Yi introduced that the first 2000 flash purchasers can receive a JD.com return coupon worth 359 Yuan; the first 3000 flash purchasers can receive a dedicated camera worth 399 Yuan and a super game controller worth 599 Yuan by posting reviews or comments on JD.com. All netizens who purchase TV+ before midnight on September 20 will have the qualification to participate in the zero-yuan auction of a Mercedes-Benz smart car.

At the same time, iQIYI will launch the "iQIYI VIP Member Million Bonus Distribution Plan," where iQIYI members purchasing TV+ will have the opportunity to enjoy an additional 500 Yuan rebate on top of the JD.com event.

Besides the Noble Edition, the Classic Edition of TV+ will be released in November this year, priced at 2999 Yuan. Compared to the Noble Edition, the Classic Edition of TV+ will have certain differences in hardware configuration, content services, and bundled accessories. The hardware configuration will relatively follow the market mainstream, content services will relatively reduce exclusive services, and bundled accessories will mainly consist of standard components to better meet the needs of cost-effective consumers.
