Grand Celebration for the First Anniversary of the Opening of Maple Flooring Suzhou Factory in North America – Corporate Updates - Industry News - Building Materials Network

On September 28, a grand celebration was held to mark the first anniversary of the opening of Maple Flooring's Suzhou factory in North America. Present at the event were leaders from relevant associations, local government officials, as well as many peers from other flooring companies who came to congratulate them. This event signified that Maple Flooring was announcing to the market and industry the completion and operation of China's largest production-scale factory for multi-layer solid wood flooring, while also comprehensively showcasing the achievements and new image of the "Maple Flooring" brand.

**Date:** September 28, 2006

**Location:** Jiafeng Wood Industry (Suzhou) Co., Ltd.

**Event Theme:**

As a product with relatively mature technology and craftsmanship but still in its market development phase, multi-layer solid wood flooring is about to enter a new period of vigorous growth. Taking advantage of the opportunity provided by the first-anniversary celebration, "Maple Flooring" announced the completion and operation of China's largest production-scale factory for multi-layer solid wood flooring, fully displaying the achievements and new image of the "Maple Flooring" brand. Additionally, it collaborated with national industry authorities to invite mainstream brands in the multi-layer solid wood flooring sector for a development cooperation seminar, discussing industry trends with partners within the sector, advocating for a fair and orderly business environment, and jointly embracing new opportunities for the development of multi-layer solid wood flooring. Simultaneously, key topics affecting the industry, such as the collection of consumption taxes and export tax rebates, were discussed, hoping that relevant state departments would provide policy support for multi-layer solid wood flooring to jointly promote the prosperity and development of the market and the industry.

**Main Invited Participants:**

- President Zhang Senlin, China National Forestry Industry Association

- Director Lü Bin, Testing Center for Artificial Boards, China National Forestry Industry Association

- Standing Secretary-General Zhang Wenling, Floor Special Committee, China National Forestry Industry Association

- President Zeng Zhiwen and Secretary-General Li Honggang, Shanghai Building Materials Industry Association Floor Committee

- Professors Gao Jianming and Zhao Guangjie, Beijing Forestry University

- Professor Wu Zhihui, Vice Dean of Nanjing Forestry University College of Engineering, Doctoral Supervisor

- Professor Zhang Yang, Nanjing Forestry University

- Expert Zhang Zhiyu, Shanghai Building Research Institute

- About 15 leading figures from Suzhou City and District governments, including the Deputy Secretary of the Municipal Party Committee

- Brand Endorser Mr. Da Shan (Mark Rowswell) for Maple Flooring

- Senior management representatives from over a dozen major floor enterprises in the multi-layer solid wood flooring industry, including Nature, Anxin, LifeStyle, and Homner.

The following is an introduction to the Maple Flooring production plant:

Jiafeng Wood Industry (Suzhou) Co., Ltd., located in the Wu Zhong Development Zone of Suzhou, is a wholly-owned subsidiary of the Canadian Jiahan Forest Products Group, specializing in the manufacture of high-quality multi-layer solid wood flooring.

Jiafeng Wood Industry (Suzhou) Co., Ltd.'s business philosophy is the sustainable use of natural resources. Its investment in equipment and technology for multi-layer solid wood flooring is considered top-tier in the industry. The total investment in the factory is approximately RMB 3 billion, covering an area of 326 mu. Phase I of the project was completed at the end of 2005, with an annual production capacity reaching 5 million square meters. Phase II is expected to be completed by the end of 2006, at which point the annual production capacity will reach 10 million square meters/year, making it one of the largest specialized production bases for multi-layer solid wood flooring in China.
