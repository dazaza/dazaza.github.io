"Boxin Gold" ranks in Hexun China Financial Economy Chart

by bxgold147t on 2012-03-01 13:30:32

Congratulations to Bocoon Gold and Silver Industry Co., Ltd. for being awarded the "Most Promising Gold Company of 2011" at HeXun's 9th China Financial Whos Who Awards, which is a full affirmation of Bocoon Gold's efforts and achievements over the past year. The China Financial Whos Who Awards are a large online selection event initiated by HeXun and jointly organized by the China Securities Market Research and Design Center (SEEC) and other institutions, targeting the financial industry and successfully held for eight consecutive years. The awards combine offline evaluations with online voting, using comprehensive professional data, user and investor votes, and in-depth analysis from industry institutions and experts to determine the final results. The awards cover various sectors including automobiles, gold, insurance, securities, listed companies, futures, banks, and funds, making it one of the most authoritative and influential annual selections in the financial industry, closely followed and widely reported by mainstream domestic media. On January 11, 2012, the award ceremony for HeXun’s 9th China Financial Whos Who Awards was grandly held at the JW Marriott Hotel in Beijing, jointly organized by HeXun and SEEC. Hosted by well-known presenters from Beijing Television and CCTV, the ceremony reviewed the achievements of the financial world in 2011 and looked forward to the direction of industrial development in the coming year, with attendance by heavyweights from the political, business, and academic circles, making this award ceremony comparable to the Oscars of China's financial world. The organizers set up more than 100 awards, among which BMW, Ping An Insurance, Huatai Securities, Qingdao Haier, GF Futures, Industrial Bank, China Merchants Fund, and other hundreds of well-known domestic enterprises were honored. As a new force in the gold industry, Bocoon Gold received the title of "Most Promising Gold Company of 2011," sharing the honor with so many excellent enterprises, making this award even more meaningful and serving as a great encouragement, representing that our persistence and efforts have stood the test. Bocoon Gold believes that only companies adhering to integrity can win longer-lasting and richer rewards. In the future, Bocoon will continue to uphold the development concept of "The Dignity of Gold, The Choice of Confidence," striving tirelessly for the continuous and healthy survival and development of the enterprise. Thank you to all netizens and all sectors of society for giving us this honor, please continue to provide us with motivation, encouragement, and assistance, allowing us to jointly create a beautiful future! The award for the 9th China Financial Whos Who Awards - Most Promising Gold Company of 2011, won by Bocoon Gold and Silver Industry Co., Ltd., was received by the manager of Bocoon's market department.
