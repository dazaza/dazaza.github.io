Love Should Be Cherished

The continuous rainy days made this small town experience rain every day for a month. Fortunately, they were all showers. A happy couple was separated for 15 days due to some reason, without seeing each other or making phone calls. This was the first time since their love began that they had been apart for such a long time without any contact. On the 5th day, they met again. The boy came from another place on the earliest train to this small town just to see his girlfriend and spend a good day together. Before he arrived, the girl did not know he was coming. The boy gave the girl a big surprise by visiting their meaningful internet cafe in the morning where the girl happened to be online.

The boy came and gave the girl a big surprise. Then they met face-to-face. The boy tightly hugged the girl, so tight that they could hardly breathe. Separation is painful, but reunion is so happy. They told each other how much they missed each other.

In the afternoon, they strolled on the street, rested and chatted in a small pavilion. The weather was sultry, and the sun was strong. As they were playing around, the weather suddenly changed, and it started raining. The rain got heavier and heavier. The girl didn't take it seriously because she knew it was just a shower and would stop soon. But today's rain was different. It got heavier and heavier, and there was thunder. The thunder wasn't loud at first, but then hail started falling, as big as glass balls. The girl became anxious. It was already 5:30, and she had to go home by 6 o'clock. At this moment, the thunder and lightning were one after another, getting louder and louder. In the small pavilion, the boy held the girl, and they prayed for the rain to become lighter. The thunder scared the girl so much that she tightly clutched the boy.

Another flash of lightning came, followed immediately by thunder. They looked up and saw that the pavilion was built with iron. They began to worry because iron conducts electricity, and outside, there was still hail. The boy was very worried and also afraid. Without saying anything, the girl was already frightened and at a loss. The boy remained calm after a while, smiling and comforting the girl. The thunder roared one after another. The girl wore the boy's coat, and the boy covered her ears so that the thunder wouldn't sound so harsh to her. While listening to the harsh sounds himself, the girl shouted almost crying, telling him to take care of himself, she was fine, and she wasn't scared.

Time kept passing, and it was almost six o'clock. Both of them were uneasy under the iron pavilion. The boy screamed, "Heaven, what do you mean? We finally meet, why do you treat us like this? Are you worried that I will abandon her?"

"Heaven, I will always love her, love her. Stop roaring!" After shouting, the girl kissed the boy's lips, a deep kiss. The deafening thunder sounded especially loud this time, lightning shot near the pavilion, and the sky turned black. The environment became increasingly harsh. It was six o'clock, and the girl must go home. Her family couldn't know that she was still dating the boy. The hail stopped, the rain became smaller, but the frequency of lightning and thunder remained the same. This was a dangerous place. Hurriedly saying goodbye, the girl ran out of the pavilion and towards the way home, and the boy also left.

On the boy's way back, he suddenly looked down and found that the necklace on his neck was gone. The necklace was given by the girl, and the boy cherished it greatly. It was a gift from the girl when they celebrated one month of their relationship. He thought maybe it fell off while running. Regardless of the rain and thunder, he searched along the road for the necklace. He didn't find it along the way, so he returned to the small pavilion and found it next to an iron pillar inside. His expression was no longer tense. He took out his mobile phone and informed the girl that he had found the necklace. They chatted affectionately for a while. Just as they said goodbye, another thunderclap sounded, unlike the others. It was extremely loud. A flash of lightning blinded the boy momentarily, and his head buzzed as he collapsed on the ground...

Under the dangerous iron pavilion, the boy was struck dead. There was no preparation; he just bid farewell to the girl and departed forever. He went looking for the necklace the girl gave him, without bidding farewell to his parents or saying goodbye to his loved ones. He was the only child in his family and the emotional support for his parents. It seemed like the sky had fallen!

Afterword: When the girl heard about the boy's departure, she cried endlessly and fainted several times.

The girl went to the morgue. He lay there cleanly and peacefully, with a mischievous smile still on his lips. The girl gently stroked the boy's head, and tears streamed down her cheeks. She didn't want to cry out loud because the boy disliked her crying.

A few days later, the girl visited their beloved haven and the boy's room before he passed away. She saw a wish bottle on the table filled with many cards saying, "Marry me, you'll never regret it for a lifetime. You'll feel warmth for a lifetime. I will make you happy for a lifetime. I will always be by your side. I will wake you up every day, cook your favorite breakfast, take you to work, always think of you, call and message you anytime so loneliness doesn't accompany you for a single moment. In the evening, I will pick you up from work, cook dinner for you, and let you sleep in my arms before I quietly fall asleep."

Household matters are up to you, but I'll handle the chores since I'm in better health. If I have social engagements, I'll be home before 11 PM. If I'm on a business trip, I'll prepare food for these few days, including your favorite snacks. Actually, love is simply the happiness of two people. Our happiness has just begun. I hope...

Suddenly, memories of the past flooded her heart. She blamed herself, regretted, and complained...

Thinking about who betrayed that once beautiful thing

Past happiness became sharp, past laughter turned gray

On the day the boy was cremated, the girl didn't go

Every day, the pain of missing him tormented her until she decided to find him far away

Dear, I've come. Life without you is so hard. You were so foolish for a necklace, but I understand it's because you love me.

These days since you left, I keep reminiscing about our time together, your body temperature, your scent, your mischievous smile, and the meals you cooked. You're a liar; you said you'd protect me for life. Without you, sleeping alone is so cold. Without you cooking, I'm hungry. Without you by my side, I'm lonely. Walk slowly, I've come. Although you didn't fulfill your promise, I still truly love you. Bad guy, I've come. Walk slowly, wait for me ahead, I've come...

Friends in love, cherish it well. Perhaps this world doesn't have perfect love, but this world has the person who loves you the most and the person you love the most. When the person who loves you the most and the person you love the most is the same person, it tells you that you are fortunate to walk together. It's fate. Walking together is happiness.
