The answer is blowing in the wind.

by 894874unrk on 2011-04-13 19:56:21

A few weeks ago, the 70-year-old Bob Dylan held a concert in China, which made the so-called Chinese intellectual and artistic circles lose their usual composure and show their ugly side. It was really embarrassing.

Regarding Bob Dylan, I once read part of his autobiography, then later listened to some of his popular songs. In my impression, he is an awesome person, but he still surpassed my imagination. So I went back to find his songs and listen to them repeatedly. It is said that Dylan is good at poetic lyrics, so I carefully studied his lyrics. However, my English is too poor to fully understand its essence, which makes me regretful! Fortunately, there is the Internet, so I found some Chinese translations of his lyrics online and used translation tools to study them. Now I have a general understanding. The more you get close to him, the more you feel how awesome he is (of course not only for his songs). What I say is superfluous, now I will post some relatively better Chinese translated lyrics I found online as follows:
