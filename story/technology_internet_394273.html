Taobao Section

For many friends who want to start an online store but don't have a background in e-commerce or computer technology, even with limited education, these shouldn't stop us from opening an online store and blocking our path to success. Below is a step-by-step tutorial for opening a Taobao store, which you can definitely learn step by step.

What is the charm of online stores?

Nowadays, starting an online store is not just about entrepreneurship; many people use it as a way to expand their skills and as a form of leisure. What exactly is the appeal of online stores? Summarizing the main points, there are the following aspects:

1. Zero cost, low risk

Compared to traditional brick-and-mortar stores, online stores don't require renting a storefront, paying taxes or utility bills. They only charge minimal fees for listing items and transactions, and some are even free. Moreover, you can purchase goods as needed without worrying about overstocking.

2. Flexible business model

Not restricted by traditional business hours or locations, operators can run the store full-time or part-time without having to spend a lot of time physically managing the store.

3. Wide consumer base:

Because online stores are on the internet, they face all possible consumers or netizens, potentially reaching the entire nation or even globally.

How exactly do you open an online store?

Online stores offer a wide variety of items such as cosmetics, clothing, digital products... Each category has great potential for development and a promising future. The key lies in how to proceed and what suits you best. Here's a step-by-step guide on how to start an online store.

One: What to sell online?

Just like traditional stores, the first step in opening an online store is deciding what to sell. The choice of products should be based on your interests, abilities, conditions, product attributes, consumer needs, etc.

Since I enjoy watching Korean dramas, and due to the recent popularity of Korean-style women's clothing among Chinese women, I naturally started selling Korean-style women's clothing.

Two: Preparations before opening a store

After selecting the products to sell, before opening an online store, you need to choose a website that provides a personal store platform and register as a user. To ensure transaction security, you also need to undergo corresponding identity and payment method verification.

I chose to open a store on Taobao because it's completely free. To open a store on Taobao, you need to meet three conditions: one, register as a member and pass certification; two, post 10 or more items; three, for convenient and secure transactions, it's recommended to activate online banking.

1. User Registration

Log in to Taobao and click the "Free Registration" link at the top of the page. On the opened page, input membership name, password, email, etc., and click the "Agree to the following service terms, submit registration information" button. Then, the registered email will receive a confirmation email. Open the link inside, confirm, and the user registration is complete.

Security Tip: To ensure transaction security, avoid setting overly simple passwords. It's recommended to use a combination of "English letters + numbers + symbols".

2. Identity Verification

Taobao stipulates that only after passing real-name verification can you sell items and open a store. Therefore, after registering as a user, you also need to undergo corresponding verifications (including two processes: real-name verification and Alipay verification). Specific operation steps are as follows:

Step One: Log in to Taobao, click "My Taobao" at the top of the page. On the opened page, click "Please click here" next to the text "Want to sell items? First, undergo Alipay verification."

Step Two: On the opened page, it will prompt that the Alipay account hasn't been activated yet. Click "Click here to activate the Alipay account." In the popped-up page, input real name, ID type and number, Alipay password, etc., and click the "Save and immediately activate the Alipay account" button.

Step Three: After successfully activating the Alipay account, go back to the original page, press F5 to refresh the page. Click the "Apply for Alipay Personal Real Name Verification" button, read the Alipay verification service terms, then click the "I have read..." button to continue.

Step Four: First, fill out personal information according to the prompts, click "Next"; then, select ID verification. You can choose "Online Upload" or "Mail" copies of your ID, click "Next"; then, input bank card information including the issuing bank, bank card number, province, city, etc., and wait for Alipay's deposit within one day.

Friendly Reminder: If uploading a copy of your ID online, the image file size should be controlled within 200KB; if it's an IC card ID, you also need to provide a picture of the back.

Step Five: One day later, re-open "My Taobao," click the corresponding link in the verification area to open the "Alipay Verification" page. In the "Bank Account Verification" area, click "Confirm Deposit Amount," then input the amount of funds Alipay injected into your bank account, and click the "Confirm" button.

3. Inventory and Photography

A key factor in successfully opening an online store lies in the sourcing channels. For the same item, different sourcing channels result in different prices.

After passing identity verification, you'll be busy organizing your existing items. To display the items for sale more intuitively to consumers, taking pictures is crucial. It's best to use corresponding graphic/image processing tools for image format and size conversion, such as Photoshop, ACDSee, etc.

4. Posting Items

To open a store, besides meeting the certification member conditions, you also need to post more than 10 items. Thus, after organizing the product information and images, you can start posting your first item.

Friendly Reminder: Without passing personal real-name verification and Alipay verification, you can post items, but they will only be posted to "Items in Warehouse," invisible to buyers. Only after passing the verification can they be listed for sale.

Step One: Log in to Taobao, click "I Want to Sell" at the top of the page. In the opened page, you can choose between "Fixed Price" or "Auction" posting methods. Here, click "Fixed Price."

Friendly Reminder: "Fixed Price" has a set price, allowing buyers to purchase immediately; "Auction" starts without a reserve price, allowing buyers to bid for purchase.

Step Two: Select a category, choose a suitable category based on your item. For example, I selected details for women's shoes. Click the "Selected, Continue" button to proceed to the next step.

Step Three: Fill in item information, this step is very important. First, in the "Item Information" area, create a good title, click the "Browse" button to upload item images, input item description information, quantity, start time, validity period, etc.; next, in the "Transaction Conditions" area, input the item's selling price, location, shipping fee, payment method, etc.; other information can remain default settings, such as using Alipay by default. Finally, click the "Confirm No Errors, Submit" button to post the item.

If posting is successful, a success page will appear below. Click "Here" to view the posted item page, click "Continue Posting Items" to continue posting items.

Friendly Reminder: Before the buyer bids, if you want to modify the posted item information, you can edit and modify it under "My Taobao - I am Seller - Items on Sale."

After completing the item posting, it's best to update and add regularly to avoid the store being deleted by the system.

Three: Obtain a Free Store

Taobao offers certified members the opportunity to open a free store. As long as you post more than 10 items, you can own your own store and independent URL. On this webpage, you can place all your items and decorate it according to your style.

To get my store up and running quickly, I initially posted 10 items, spending almost half a day. After finishing, I eagerly opened "My Taobao - Free Store Opening." Then, on the opened page, I named the store, calling it "My Shoes, My Show"; in the "Store Category" section, I chose the category for my items; in the "Store Introduction" section, I input the store's summary content. Finally, clicking the "Submit" button, on the appeared page, it showed "Congratulations! Your store has been successfully created..." along with providing the store's address.

Four: Store Decoration is Important

After opening a free store, buyers can obtain their own space. Just like traditional stores, to operate normally and attract customers, the store needs appropriate "decoration," mainly including store logo design, item classification, featured items, store style, etc.

1. Basic Settings

Log in to Taobao, open "My Taobao - I am Seller - Manage My Store." In the left "Store Management," click "Basic Settings." On the opened page, you can modify the store name, store category, store introduction; main operating projects need to be manually entered; in the "Logo" area, click the "Browse" button to select the already designed store logo image; in the "Announcement" area, input the store announcement content, such as "Welcome to our store!" Click the "Preview" button to see the effect.

2. Item Classification

Classifying items makes it easier for buyers to search. In the opened "Manage My Store" page, you can click "Item Classification" on the left; then, input the new classification name, such as "Four Treasures of the Study," and input the sorting number (indicating the order), click the "Confirm" button to add. Click the "Item List" button corresponding to the classification, and you can add the posted items through keyword searches for classification management.

3. Featured Items

The "Featured Items" function provided by Taobao allows you to recommend your best six items, displaying them prominently in the store. Simply open the "Manage My Store" page, click "Featured Items" on the left; then, you can select the recommended items on the opened page and click the "Recommend" button.

4. Store Style

Different store styles suit different items and give buyers different feelings. Generally, choosing a style with elegant colors that looks comfortable is sufficient. I chose the "Green Wild Fairyland" style template, which displays a preview on the right side. Clicking the "Confirm" button applies this style.

After decorating the store, a refreshed page appears.

Five: Promotion is Key to Success

The online store is open, items are listed, and features are present, but after several weeks, there are still no transactions, not even any buyer messages. This is a common problem encountered by many novice sellers. At this point, you need to take the initiative.

I once faced such troubles, so I decided to advertise my store through four methods: forum promotion, link exchange, showcase recommendations, and friend promotion.

1. Forum Promotion

The main method of forum promotion is through posting advertisements and using signature lines.

The former can be done on forums across provinces or major cities. If there's a section allowing ad posts, you can post ads with detailed content, high-quality item images, and regular updates and top placements.

The latter involves changing your signature line on forums to include your store's URL, logo, slogan, and store name. Post some attractive threads to let interested friends visit your store through your signature.

2. Link Exchange

In the early stages of opening a store, to increase popularity, you can exchange links with popular stores, thereby promoting your store for free. For example, Taobao provides up to 35 friendly links, and adding them is simple:

First, use Taobao's search function to find all stores and note the names of popular store owners.

Next, log in and download Taobao's buyer-seller communication tool - "Wangwang," add these popular store owners' names, and request link exchanges.

If they agree, finally, open "My Taobao - I am Seller - Manage My Store," click "Friendly Links" on the left, then input the owner's name and click the "Add" button.

3. Showcase Recommendations

The "Showcase Recommendation" feature provided by Taobao is a special function for sellers. When buyers search or click "I Want to Buy" to search by category, showcase-recommended items will appear on the search results page. To set the "Showcase Recommendation" feature, open "My Taobao - I am Seller - Items on Sale," select items to recommend to the showcase (items already recommended to the store homepage cannot be showcased again, marked with "Recommended"), and click the "Showcase Recommendation" button.

Six: After Selling Items

After selling an item, besides receiving corresponding sales reminders, you need to actively contact the buyer, request payment, ship the item, and handle evaluations or complaints after the transaction is complete.

1. Check Sold Items

If a buyer purchases an item, Taobao will notify the seller via "Wangwang" or email. Sellers can also log in to Taobao, open "My Taobao - I am Seller - Sold Items." In the "Contact Buyer" area, click "Leave me a message" to leave a message for the buyer through "Wangwang"; if the buyer doesn't use "Wangwang," you can also note the buyer's ID and send a site message.

2. Contact Transaction Details

After both parties communicate, they agree on payment and shipping methods. To ensure the interests of both parties, it's recommended to use Alipay for payment.

3. Payment and Shipping

To prevent situations where the item arrives but isn't paid for, sellers generally adopt a "payment received before shipping" method.

First, request the buyer to pay, usually through Alipay. After payment, the seller can open "My Taobao - I am Seller - Sold Items" to query. If the transaction status shows "Buyer has paid, waiting for seller to ship," it indicates that Alipay has received the payment, and the seller can confidently ship the item to the buyer.

Friendly Reminder: For sellers using Alipay transactions, you can open "My Taobao - Alipay Zone - Transaction Management" to manage ongoing transactions, such as transaction queries, refund management, etc.

After the buyer receives the seller's goods, they confirm in the transaction status, and Taobao will transfer the funds to the seller's Alipay account. This completes the transaction. Don't forget to stay in touch with the buyer to increase the chances of them revisiting your store and purchasing items again.

4. Evaluation and Complaints

After completing the transaction, both buyers and sellers can open "My Taobao" for evaluation. Sellers can open "My Taobao - I am Seller - Sold Items," click "Evaluate" on sold items, and choose positive, neutral, or negative reviews based on actual circumstances, with the option to input text content.

Friendly Reminder: How to become a Taobao seller with relatively high credit rating?

To become a star-rated seller on Taobao, the credit rating must be at least 4 points. The seller's credit score is based on the buyer's evaluation after each successful Alipay transaction. Positive reviews add one point, neutral reviews add no points, and negative reviews deduct one point. Therefore, to become a star-rated seller, remember to provide honest service. If there are cases of online transactions without purchase or receipt without payment, sellers can open "My Taobao - Credit Management - Report" to file complaints and reports, requiring evidence such as shipping receipts, buyer sign-off receipts, Wangwang screenshots, etc.

Postscript: Although it is said that online stores have zero cost and low risk, examples of failed transactions and closing down are also common. To keep your small online store alive, the most important thing is "honesty." Only honesty can win the hearts of buyers and gain good credit ratings, leading to a steady stream of wealth!

Blog Section

It is commonly believed that popularity is crucial for selling things on blogs. The traffic of online stores centers around products, whereas blog traffic forms based on the blogger's fame and written content. Generally speaking, the latter generates much more traffic. For instance, Xu Jinglei's blog has tens of millions of views, which is hard to imagine for an online store.

Many bloggers have built a fixed readership through writing, despite varying numbers, painstakingly establishing interpersonal networks through one article after another. This group often shares consistent ideas, concepts, emotions, and styles. Loving the blogger's products can also lead to fans liking the blogger's preferred items, and upgrades or replacements of the blogger's products can draw attention from fans.

The textual power of blogs effectively helps products create impact. Product descriptions in stores, no matter how good the content, are seen as product explanations. However, in blogs, product descriptions are considered literary works. Bloggers fully display their personalities and expertise in describing products, often going beyond direct descriptions and writing stories, novels, essays, or poetry to resonate with appreciators. Blogs also become new means for bloggers and followers to establish emotional connections through product sales.

Additionally, the interactivity of blogs is more prominent than online stores. A product can spark discussions and become a hot topic on blogs, whether praised or criticized, always leading to hot sales. I once saw a blog selling tools for making Western pastries, with replies to many consultations about pastry-making, introductions to Western dining etiquette, news on group-buying imported cream, and notifications of club activities. The more guidance the blogger provides, the more potential customers hope to find this seller. In such blogs, what you see is not a store atmosphere but rather a club where friends integrate their passion, interest, wisdom, and needs.

The tag function of blogs is better than general online stores, not only highlighting keywords in products but also effectively helping products appear in network search systems through many keywords in articles. Blog links also expand the influence of products, allowing more people to see the products through links shared by fellow bloggers.
