[Basic Introduction]

Google Kingsoft Dictionary Collaborative Edition is jointly developed by Kingsoft and Google for the online translation market. It is a free translation software for personal users. The software includes some local dictionaries, with only 23M size, making it lightweight and easy to use. This version inherits the classic functions of Kingsoft Dictionary such as word fetching, word lookup, and sentence lookup, and adds new features like full-text translation, web page translation, and an online dictionary that covers new words and popular terms. It supports queries in Chinese, Japanese, and English, and includes authentic pronunciation for 300,000 words, along with pronunciations for 50,000 long and difficult words.

[Software Features]

Classic Dictionary, Accurate! Inheriting the content quality of Kingsoft Dictionary for ten years!

It includes classic dictionaries such as "Modern Comprehensive English-Chinese Dictionary" and "Chinese-English Dictionary" (New), covering over a million entries from Kingsoft Dictionary.

Online Dictionary, Vast! Covers all new and popular words!

The "Aiciba Encyclopedia Dictionary" co-built by a million Aiciba friends and the vast "Google Online Dictionary", encompassing all new and popular words, keeping content up-to-date.

Scenario Sentences, Practical! Covers all aspects of life scenario conversations!

800,000 scenario sentences, where you can input a sentence or keyword to find all related sentence patterns and usage examples, helping you learn more through analogy.

Full-text Translation, Intelligent! Leading online engine!

Using a leading online engine, combined with powerful translation technology based on rich corpora, making full-text translation results intelligent and accurate.

Web Page Direct Translation, Quick! Enjoy online surfing!

Supports web page translation, where you can directly input a URL and choose a language, showing translation results quickly and in real-time. Supports 4 languages in 7 directions.

Screen Word Fetching, Easy to Use! Point and translate explanations at your convenience!

Leading screen word-fetching technology, with added "translate within translate" function, allowing selection of any word or phrase, supporting the new Windows Vista operating system, and supporting word-fetching in PDF document format.

Authentic Voice Pronunciation, Pure! Helps you correct pronunciation!

300,000 pure authentic voice pronunciations, including 50,000 long words, difficult words, and phrases in English, helping you correct English word pronunciation.

Download and Launch, Lightweight! Fast and resource-efficient!

Fast download, fast launch, effectively saving system resources. Convenient and quick to use, occupying little space.
