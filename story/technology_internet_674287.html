Dongguan 38-Year-Old Billionaire Falls to His Death at Home

by king2z9o on 2012-02-24 11:37:06

At around 10 p.m. on the evening of February 22nd, a man fell to his death from the seventh floor of an apartment building in Zhangmutou, Dongguan. According to informed sources, the man, surnamed Yang and born in 1974, was the chairman of a group company based in Tangxia. It is conservatively estimated that his personal wealth exceeded one billion yuan, with business interests spanning commercial investment, cultural entertainment, hotel catering, electronic technology, real estate, and more.

The deceased owned multiple subsidiaries. Eyewitnesses reported that at approximately 10 p.m. on February 22nd, a loud sound was heard in a residential area in Zhangmutou. A man had fallen from a higher floor, and at the scene was a young man who appeared to be a family member of the deceased. Informed individuals revealed that the deceased was Mr. Yang, chairman of Sheng × Group Company located in Tangxia, Dongguan. Established in 2006, the group owned several highly successful subsidiaries, operating broadly across various sectors including commercial investment, cultural entertainment, hotel catering, electronic technology, and real estate. The deceased was known as a prominent figure in Tangxia. A familiar enterprise owner stated that it was conservatively estimated that Mr. Yang's assets were worth over one billion yuan.

According to those in the know, at around 10 p.m. on February 22nd, after drinking with friends, Mr. Yang returned to his home in a high-end residential area in Zhangmutou Town. At the time, a trusted female subordinate was also inside, chatting with him. During their conversation, Mr. Yang suddenly walked to the balcony, placing both hands on the railing in an attempt to sit on it. Unbeknownst to him, he applied too much force, causing him to lose balance and fall headfirst from the seventh-floor balcony, resulting in his immediate death.

Upon the incident, Mr. Yang's female subordinate immediately alerted the police. Informed sources revealed that the woman claimed that after drinking that night, the two had a minor argument over some trivial matters. Around 10 p.m., they returned to Mr. Yang’s residence and continued chatting in the living room. Mr. Yang often had the habit of sitting on the balcony railing to smoke. She had previously warned him against this behavior multiple times. Perhaps due to some distress that night, Mr. Yang again approached the balcony, placed both hands on the railing intending to sit down for a smoke. However, this time he exerted too much force, causing him to topple over the railing and fall to his death.

Yesterday, the Zhangmutou police confirmed that after receiving the report, they conducted an on-site investigation and promptly reviewed the surveillance footage of the residential complex. Preliminary investigations determined that Mr. Yang's death was due to an accidental fall, ruling out the possibility of suicide.

The surveillance footage corroborated the account provided by Mr. Yang's female subordinate. It was confirmed that Mr. Yang had consumed alcohol that evening, but whether or not he was intoxicated could not be verified.
