A high-quality website includes two aspects: First, the user experience aspect, which addresses user needs; second, the SEO optimization aspect. http://www.njyixan.com/Nanjing SEO, which helps users find the website's services and products through search engines, includes the following four elements. Nanjing SEO summarizes them as follows:

1. Website domain and hosting space

The stability of the hosting space is crucial for both user experience and website optimization, forming the hardware foundation of a website. When choosing a domain name, shorter is better, making it easier to remember. Avoid using Chinese domain names or .inf domains. If a short domain is unavailable or insufficient, consider customizing a domain with keyword pinyin. The principle is to make it memorable, convenient, recognizable by search engines, and phonetically matched. It’s recommended to choose reputable domain providers like Xinnet or Wanwang, which offer stable domains and fast DNS resolution.

2. Website structure and color scheme

In terms of SEO optimization during website construction, the website structure should be logical, concise, and clear, highlighting key points with a well-defined central theme. Navigation should be designed reasonably, and an F-shaped structure is the most suitable. Important information should be placed in the upper-left corner of the website, which is where Baidu spiders frequently visit and where users are accustomed to starting their browsing. For more details, see the structure of my Rugao Talent Network.

3. Website language and database

Regarding SEO optimization, it's important to note that website languages are not ranked as first or second tier. However, the choice of website language and database should match the hosting space. The language used in website construction is critical. For example, .NET has high server requirements. If your server is underperforming, it's best not to choose .NET, as slow website loading speeds can lead to errors, reducing user experience and search engine friendliness.

4. Website optimization

The amount of information on the homepage should be as large as possible, with higher relevance between the information and keywords being preferable. However, be cautious not to excessively copy or stack keywords for the sake of relevance, as this could backfire and cause trouble.
