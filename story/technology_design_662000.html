Chongqing Electric Power Company ensures trouble-free power supply during the Spring Festival

by dwjcblog on 2012-02-09 16:01:46

It is understood that, apart from the necessary emergency repair work, Chongqing Power Grid will not arrange for any planned overhaul work during the Spring Festival and it will keep the mode of full wiring and full protection operation. Chongqing Company also requires that all power supply units and power generation enterprises should develop corresponding power guarantee work contingency plans in accordance with the city dispatching power guarantee work solutions and combining with the actual conditions of their own units, implement the measures to protect the power grid, protect the equipment and ensure supply, and ensure the safe and stable operation of the power grid and reliable power supply during the Spring Festival. Recently, the rural electricity load has increased due to the return of migrant workers. Safe, continuous and reliable power supply will be the focus of customer attention recently. In order to ensure that the people of the whole city spend a harmonious traditional festival, Chongqing Company requests that all units strengthen the inspection and maintenance of power supply equipment, and complete the voltage quality management targets and tasks; at the same time strengthen service management and execution, ensure the quality of customer service during the Spring Festival. The 95598 hotline will provide uninterrupted services for 24 hours to ensure timely transmission of received information, grassroots units respond quickly to deal with faults, business outlets remain fully open for business services, ensuring that a large number of electric power customers spend the Spring Festival happily. Currently, Chongqing has entered the coldest period of winter. There is much wire icing in many areas of Southeast Chongqing and Northeast Chongqing. Chongqing Company requires relevant units to do well in running and patrolling work, and at the same time do well in accident contingency plans and emergency repairs. It is required to do well in power guarantee and prevention work for high-altitude holiday scenic spots such as Fairy Mountain and Four-face Mountain. During the Spring Festival, in order to prevent load climbing brought by extreme weather, the "State Grid Red Rock Power Supply Repair Service Team" consisting of more than 5000 people in 36 teams are ready for emergency rescue preparations at any time. Emergency materials and vehicles have been prepared to ensure the smooth development of emergency rescue work.
