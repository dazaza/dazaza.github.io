Naming solicitation for green public welfare environmental protection activities

by fashioninchina on 2008-05-10 12:52:20

The hosting of the Green Olympics has made China the focus of world attention. Domestically, calls for environmental protection are growing louder: we want to prove that our ancient civilization is lush with greenery and that the concept of environmental protection has taken root in people's hearts! However, despite the loud slogans, environmental protection campaigns have fallen far short of their goals. Take the previously held "Green Environment, Bag by Bag" campaign as an example—despite the mobilization of all teachers and students from a primary school, the activity ultimately ended in failure.

Sura Industry is currently preparing for an environmental protection publicity campaign and is now soliciting names for Sura’s Environmental Protection Alliance online store and its supporters. Below is the preliminary planning document for the event:

### 1. Activity Background:

What is the reason for the vast gap between the public and environmental actions? Even though many companies in the country have joined the ranks of environmental protection and invested substantial human and material resources into promoting environmental awareness, there hasn’t been much visible effect. How can more people be encouraged to join environmental protection efforts? After continuous research and investigation, Sura Industry discovered that the reason lies in these companies not properly targeting their audience. Many companies promote environmental protection by distributing eco-friendly bags to the public; however, the distribution sites are often supermarkets or places where crowds gather, attracting mostly women or older groups who are only interested in getting practical benefits. On the other hand, those who truly understand the concept of environmental protection and are willing to spend time spreading this idea are the younger generation who embrace new things. If environmental campaigns could target this younger demographic, guiding these energetic and influential groups to join environmental protection efforts, it would effectively realize environmental protection.

### 2. Activity Purpose:

To take the younger generation as the main force, awaken the public's awareness of green public welfare and environmental protection, guide more people to participate in environmental protection efforts, and ensure that those who engage in environmental protection receive tangible discounts.

### 3. Target Audience:

After research and analysis, Sura decided to target internet users as the audience group. Since most internet users today are young people or fashionable individuals who embrace new things, they are more likely to accept new concepts and are willing to support public welfare environmental protection activities.

### 4. Activity Content:

Sura will form alliances with various online stores across the network, reaching agreements with these stores. Any online store that forms an alliance with Sura Industry will offer certain discounts to buyers who support environmental protection. Buyers (i.e., internet users) simply need to commit to environmental protection to receive discount coupons provided by Sura Industry. These coupons can be used when shopping to enjoy discounted prices.

### 5. Activity Methods:

For Internet Users (Buyers):

1. Cast a Vote for the Environment. Internet users can go to Sura Industry's event webpage and click "Cast a Vote for the Environment" to become an environmental supporter.

2. Enjoy Discounts. By accessing Sura's Environmental Protection Alliance online stores through Sura Industry's website, buyers can enjoy discounted prices when shopping.

3. Environmental Action. After receiving discounts, internet users should self-supervise and practice environmental protection in their daily lives, forming good environmental habits.

For Environmental Protection Alliance Online Stores (Sellers):

1. Commit to Offering Discounts for Environmental Protection. The "Environmental God of Wealth" brings a steady stream of environmentally conscious customers to your store.

2. Establish Environmental Discounts. Based on their situation, each Environmental Protection Alliance online store can set up discount methods. This not only practices environmental protection but also offers discounts to customers. The alliance focuses on expressing support for environmental protection. Even small discounts contribute to environmental action.

3. Build Environmental Credibility. As the concept of environmental protection becomes increasingly ingrained, more people hope to purchase products from merchants dedicated to public welfare causes.

Sura Industry hopes to practice corporate social responsibility more effectively. We want more people to feel that environmental protection actions can also bring real discounts. We do not force anyone to join our environmental team; we want the public to voluntarily fulfill environmental responsibilities and develop good habits.

We now hope to gather wisdom from all sides to name Sura Industry's Environmental Protection Alliance online store—sellers and environmental supporters—buyers.

Requirements:

1. Novel, unique, youthful, energetic, passionate, healthy

2. Able to convey the relationship between sellers and buyers

3. Easy to remember

We hope everyone can support public welfare and environmental protection actions together! We look forward to suggestions from all sides, thank you!
