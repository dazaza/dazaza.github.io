Father sells花椒 in hospital for daughter's medical expenses, gets 500 yuan from selling 10 jin

by zzf000zxye0 on 2011-06-17 10:28:11

Have you ever spent 100 yuan to buy one jin of peppercorn? Moreover, there are people who spent 100 yuan and didn't even take an ounce of peppercorn... Last night at 9 PM, the neurology department hallway of West China Hospital was bustling with activity. Mr. Lao brought 150 jin of donated peppercorn from his hometown, planning to sell it to raise money for his daughter's medical treatment for spinal inflammation. Many patients offered high prices for his peppercorn, and some even gave him money without taking any peppercorn. In just an hour, over 10 jin of peppercorn "sold" for a total of 525 yuan.

At 10 PM last night, a reporter went to the neurology department of West China Hospital, where a big bag of peppercorn was placed in the corner of the hallway, with Mr. Lao standing beside it holding a scale. "This is the money given by Uncle Gao, he said he doesn't want any peppercorn." A nurse came in and handed over 50 yuan to Mr. Lao. Mr. Lao wanted to give her some peppercorn as a token of appreciation, but the nurse firmly refused.

Mr. Lao said that his family is from Mao County, and after his daughter fell ill, they quickly spent the 5000+ yuan they had brought along. Relatives and friends were also unable to support them financially, so they managed to gather 150 jin of peppercorn for him. Starting at 9 PM last night, he began selling in the hallway, intending to sell each jin for 30 yuan. Soon, a sister came to buy, she only took one jin but gave 50 yuan. Later, someone spent 100 yuan for one jin, and others directly gave 50 or 100 yuan without taking even an ounce of peppercorn before leaving. Mr. Lin, one of the donors, said: "Everyone has difficult times, help when we can."

Mr. Lao said that everyone buying his peppercorn at high prices made him feel that "there are still more good people in this world", but selling peppercorn in the hospital would disturb other patients' rest, so he plans to go to nearby neighborhoods today to sell.
