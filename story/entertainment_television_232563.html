Alley 54 of Guangming Street was a common cul-de-sac in Taipei during the 1980s. The few families living there were particularly close-knit, such as the Li family who ran an ice shop at the alley entrance, the Fang family whose father was a local police officer, the Xiang family who operated a clinic, and the Chen family hiding their family secrets. Among these four families were pure mainlanders, pure Taiwanese, half-and-half, and even some with Hakka ancestry. Despite their varied backgrounds, they had one thing in common: each family had a daughter born in 1954 who attracted more attention than their sons.

Fang Zhongqiang (played by Na Weixun) and Xiang Guangzheng (played by Zhang Zhaozhi) were close friends, but Guangzheng's fiancée Yuexia (played by Li Peizhen) ruined Zhongqiang and Baoqin's (played by Wang...) wedding night. This caused a rift between Baoqin and Yuexia until three years later when Guangzheng moved his clinic across from Zhongqiang's house. On the same day, Baoqin and Yuexia gave birth to daughters named Yian (played by Bai Xinhu) and Qianqian (played by Linda), respectively, which finally mended the two women’s relationship. Although relieved, the men knew that women's hearts are fickle, and the two still often quarreled over trivial matters.

Baoqin and Yuexia were delighted to have daughters, whereas Chen's mother, Gui Mei (played by Xiuzhen), felt disheartened after giving birth to Yuting (played by Li Jiayu). Only having a son could improve her status as a concubine, which was a secret no one dared speak of, causing the eldest son Pinzhang (played by Li Yunqing) to rebel.

Yian, Qianqian, and Yuting were all born in the same year, marking the start of Taiwan's economic self-sufficiency. However, the three girls were destined to begin life from different starting points.

The Fang family's daughter, Yian, inherited the tall and robust physique of Shandong people. She did not become the lady her mother expected due to strict family teachings. Instead, she liked to take charge, was full of righteousness, but lacked judgment. The Xiang family's daughter, Qianqian, was the most beautiful, loved by all. As an only child, she was spoiled and owned Alley 54's first Barbie doll, making the world revolve around her. The Chen family's daughter, Yuting, had the most ordinary looks but was the best student in Alley 54. However, being the child of a concubine in a male-dominated family, she silently bore her mother's resentment towards her.

At the age of ten, Yian stood up for Qianqian to retrieve her beloved Barbie doll and teamed up with Pinzhang to crack the head of the bully Chengcai (played by Hao Shaowen), thus becoming famous in Alley 54. Consequently, Yian, Qianqian, and Yuting swore to support each other and never separate. When everyone thought Yian was the child leader of the alley, the mysterious Li family moved into Alley 54. Peifen (played by Dai Junzhu), seemingly possessing mysterious powers, was smart, optimistic, and refused to bow to adversity. After helping everyone recover their first earned money, she gradually became admired by all. Thus, the four girls of Alley 54 embraced their shining days amidst the tides of the times, and their story begins here...
