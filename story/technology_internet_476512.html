With the development of modern medicine, especially after China's entry into the WTO, traditional Chinese medicine (TCM) faces unprecedented opportunities. International pharmaceutical institutions no longer require TCM products to be single products with known structures; they can be mixtures with fixed components, stable efficacy, and reliable safety, providing favorable conditions for TCM to go global. Centipedes, as traditional animal-based Chinese herbs, have a history of thousands of years.

In terms of nourishing health care, centipede-based health supplements with health benefits are increasingly attracting people's attention and affection. Shenlong Wine is based on the traditional integrated healthy therapy of food therapy, treatment through diet, and dietary supplementation. It is a fitness beverage wine produced with modern technology. Studies on its toxicology, nutrition, and efficacy show that Shenlong Wine is highly nutritious, containing more than 15 types of amino acids and trace elements required by the human body, with evident analgesic and detoxifying effects. Moreover, centipede health supplements have various functions such as tonifying the kidneys, enhancing male potency, and boosting the immune function of the body. Therefore, vigorously developing centipede farming and extensively developing centipede series products to gradually move towards industrial development and utilization has become a contemporary production need.

For a long time, the state has been calling for large-scale capturing of centipedes. In some main centipede-producing areas, seasonal capturing and seasonal purchasing have gradually evolved into cross-seasonal capturing and year-round purchasing. The acquisition price of each centipede in major Chinese herbal medicine markets has risen from a few cents to over 1.5 yuan, with even higher export prices and growing demand, leading to annual price increases. However, due to deforestation, pesticide and fertilizer pollution, and other ecological damage, wild resources are becoming increasingly scarce, resulting in a severe shortage of centipede resources and rising prices. Therefore, an opportunity that comes once in a hundred years has arrived unexpectedly. In the coming years, the centipede farming industry will undoubtedly become a shining highlight among special farming industries, with significant economic and social benefits.

As a long-term development project, when farmers sell their centipedes, they can choose to retain high-quality female centipedes for reproduction, thereby increasing efficiency annually. Advantages of farming:

1. Easy to farm: Century Xinxin golden-headed centipedes are gentle and can be farmed at home or in factory-like three-dimensional farms. Factory-like three-dimensional farming is our company's original creation and is the most efficient and fastest method of centipede farming.

2. Strong reproductive capacity: Laying 60-80 eggs per clutch, under constant temperature farming, they lay eggs twice a year.

3. Low food consumption, wide range of feed: Large-scale farming can involve artificially breeding feed, while natural farming only requires wheat bran, vegetable leaves, fruits, insects, animal viscera, bone powder, etc., almost without cost.

4. Strong disease resistance: Strong adaptability, vigorous vitality, not prone to illness, absolutely no risk of diseases like avian influenza (chickens), foot-and-mouth disease (pigs), or mad cow disease (cows).

5. Saves labor and time: Generally fed between 6-8 pm, it can be farmed during leisure time.

6. Low investment: Apart from introducing species, almost no additional investment is needed. Regular households can use empty rooms, balconies, courtyards, or areas around the house for farming using vats, boxes, or pools. For large-scale farming, enclosures or three-dimensional farming methods can be used, along with self-cultivated green feed and self-raised insects.

Hubei Agricultural University Support: As a key university under the national "211 Project" construction, Huazhong Agricultural University collaborates with Wuhan Nongke Humin Bioengineering Co., Ltd. to jointly research and develop scientific farming techniques for centipedes, specifically the Humin No. 2 Golden-headed Centipede.

Advanced Technology Advantage: Through many years of observation, multiple biological experts and professors from Wuhan Nongke Humin Bioengineering Co., Ltd. and Huazhong Agricultural University have utilized selection purification technology. Wuhan Nongke Humin Bioengineering Co., Ltd. has used bio-genetic improvement engineering to cultivate a new generation breed - Humin No. 2 Golden-headed Centipede.
