Night, the heart is hurting in memory!

Online submission

Night, so quiet, so cold.

The house, empty, I am alone.

I don't know when I started to get used to this feeling, one person, one song, one day, just like that, one person enjoying loneliness happily.

Laughing alone, crying alone, in my own world, no need to care about others' thoughts, how refreshing.

Loneliness is wearing a smiling mask and secretly counting behind one's back.

Happiness is suppressing sadness and pretending to smile.

This is my understanding.

Sometimes, really lonely, really alone.

Who can understand the surging of my heart?

I want to get used to it, but there is a protest deep in my heart.

I don't expect my life to be as dramatic as an idol drama, but I always look forward to my life being as meaningful as an idol drama.

Expectation... expectation... but what does expectation ultimately bring?

Now I only have an empty skeleton, that soul has already fallen behind, just a light touch and I will fall apart, and thus end...

In empty days, I can only entertain myself.

I conceive many story plots in my mind, making myself the protagonist of the story, rising and falling tides, and I also get some fun from it.

Unaccompanied nights are very desolate, only the diary understands my mood.

I don't know when I gradually started to like recording events, writing on the computer, in notebooks, a lot, every mood, only they understand the most.

Folding and folding, each star represents my mood, whether happy or sad, the bottle can no longer see the bottom.

The happiness of loneliness is finding happiness in loneliness and counting loneliness in happiness.

Night... so cold...

"When night falls, what should I do? Who is by my side in loneliness... let memories grow wings and fly, empty your heart, and loneliness will be gone..."

Yes. Isn't it just loneliness, isn't it just one person, the simplest explanation is just one person. What's so difficult about it...

...After being lonely for a long time... you will also feel disgusted...

Go out for a walk alone... walking alone on the road, where to put your hands? Feeling uncomfortable all over...

Sleeping, temporary amnesia. Forget reality, experience a different life in dreams. If it could be frozen in that dream, how nice it would be.

I don't know when I slowly began to feel that I have become a person who often lingers...

I don't know when I slowly began to feel that I have become a person who often looks up at the sky...

Think of some people. They are like fireworks that bloomed in my world, forced to ascend to the high sky and painfully burst, then disappear. It seems that the meaning of our encounter lies only in the brilliant moment of intersection.

What's left is nothing more than some bleak things, some unimportant things.

Thus, I gently remember him and her. My heart feels warm.

Everyone lives in the depths of their own abyss. Yes. One day we will all grow old and die. Happiness may ultimately be an ultimate symbol, not bringing liberation.

But there will be some things, some people, who make us silently melancholy when we are alone, yet without any regret.

There are some things, some people, reminding us that we once illuminated each other's eyes dramatically, and still remain in thought.

Here and now. This is the divinity of life.

You never know how she will come and what final outcome she will bring.

Its nobility cannot be detected at all. Like those wounds and memories hidden in our hearts.
