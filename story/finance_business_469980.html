"Riddles That Will Make You Spill Blood"

1. There is a grassland ahead. (Guess a plant) Answer: Plum blossom (no flower)

2. There is another grassland ahead. (Guess a plant) Answer: Wild plum blossom (no flower either)

3. A herd of sheep came. (Guess a fruit) Answer: Strawberry (grass gone)

4. A pack of wolves came. (Guess a fruit) Answer: Waxberry (sheep gone)

5. Potato wants to duel with steamed bun, but there is a river in front that he cannot cross. (Guess a plant) Answer: Snow pea (river blocking the bean)

6. The duel begins, potato stabs the steamed bun with a fatal knife. (Guess a food) Answer: Red bean paste bun (bean kills bun)

7. Rat says to wild horse: "I went on a date with a cat yesterday!" (Guess a food) Answer: Sweet potato chips (rat deceives)

8. The wild horse doesn't believe it and lifts the rat up. (Guess a vegetable) Answer: Potato (horse lifting rat)

9. Pig says: "Come on!" (Guess a food) Answer: Chocolate (pig encourages)

10. A sheep is eating grass, a wolf passes by the sheep but does not eat the sheep. (Guess a seafood) Answer: Shrimp (blind)

11. Another wolf passes by the sheep but still does not eat the sheep. (Guess a seafood) Answer: Prawn (still blind)

12. A third wolf passes by, the sheep shouts loudly, but the wolf still does not eat the sheep. (Still guess a seafood) Answer: Lobster (deaf and blind)
