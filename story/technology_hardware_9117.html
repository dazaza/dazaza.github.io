Online diary to expose sexual abuse, Zhao Zhongxiang unaware of the matter

Morning Post reporter Zhu Weijie - The much-talked-about affair between famous host Zhao Zhongxiang and Rao Ying has been quiet for a long time, but it seems that the matter is not over yet. Yesterday, a website called Paparazzi Network published "Rao Ying's Diary", which detailed the process from their meeting to the incident involving Rao Ying and Zhao Zhongxiang. The content was explosive and would be serialized, even claiming to expose "details of sexual abuse". For this, the reporter conducted a telephone interview with both parties involved, Rao Ying and Zhao Zhongxiang.

The diary claims it will publish details of sexual abuse.

The affair between Rao Ying and Zhao Zhongxiang was a major event in the entertainment industry in 2004. When Rao Ying revealed that she had a seven-year emotional entanglement with Zhao Zhongxiang, and that she had been hospitalized twice due to his behavior, it caused her great mental anguish.

The two even took the matter to court.

However, more than two years after the matter had subsided, yesterday on a website called "Paparazzi Network", an unexpected "Rao Ying's Diary" suddenly appeared. The diary detailed the relationship between the two, with explosive text content, and the title was even processed as "Rao Ying's Diary Exposes Zhao Zhongxiang's Sexual Abuse Details". After reading the text, the reporter found that it was written in the first person, and the details were consistent with the events exposed at the time. It described many emotional details and published numerous details that were not disclosed to the media at the time. It is claimed that the article will continue to be serialized and will "expose details of sexual abuse."

The website claims exclusive exposure, but regarding the source, the website manager said it was the work of netizens participating in an exposé competition held by the site.
