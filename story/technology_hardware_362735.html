Apple VS Nokia: Tactics Beat Strategy_Xinhua News Agency Finance Channel

by cacard on 2010-08-20 22:49:05

Nokia's market position is steadily declining. According to the National Business Weekly, in July this year, Nokia announced its Q2 operational financial report, showing a net profit of 227 million euros, which represents a 40% drop compared to the 380 million euro net profit of the same period in 2009. In terms of market share, according to statistics from IT data research company Gartner, the market share of Nokia's Symbian system smartphones has dropped from a dominant 73% in 2006 to 34% currently, decreasing on average by 10% annually.

The root cause of Nokia's decline is Apple. Even though Apple's iPhones account for less than 15% of the global smartphone market share, the wave of mobile innovation triggered by the iPhone has severely impacted Nokia.
