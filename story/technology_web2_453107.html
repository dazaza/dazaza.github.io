Objectivity and Novelty in News Reporting - Master's Thesis Writing Service

[Keywords] Objectivity of broadcasting, freshness

Propaganda was called in the past in academia: the specific form of broadcast news to meet the essence of higher-level issues. Any subjective point of view in news reporting can mislead the audience. Therefore, objective news broadcasting is the starting point and destination that radio news strives for. Some announcers do not know or cannot grasp the principle of objectivity in received draft broadcasts, and cannot sit down to think about the article to express a fact, what background, simply put, is to weigh the weight of the manuscript, be well-prepared, and then broadcast it. However, they just pick up the manuscript and straighten it out, without dealing with obscure words, and end up broadcasting, wasting a lot of emotion but far from the content of the manuscript.

Firstly, news scripts determine their own feelings about the freshness of the news being broadcast. News is the reporting of facts that have recently occurred or were previously unknown, and are the earliest reported facts. The first news is its true vitality, the second news is its new vitality, new news brings new ideas, new angles, new presentation techniques, and language forms a new sense of freshness in the news script, which naturally leads to a new choice in the broadcasting of news. Secondly, the broadcasting of news also determines the goal of freshness in the broadcast of news. Teacher Qi Yue once said: In a sense, words are just the shell of sound in broadcasting, and this feeling is its soul. Only when sound, meaning, and emotion are integrated, only when the broadcast has vitality. Freshness is the novelty of the news program, so it can attract the rationality of the audience. When the audience hears something fresh, they are willing to listen and accept, thereby achieving public information, education, and stirring effects. Currently, some broadcasting companies have changed the extreme and one-sided pursuit of friendly broadcasting too fast in the past. These methods have caused the news programs to lose their freshness, making listeners bored. So, how do we make our news broadcasts fresh? To understand the freshness within the news script. The way is to establish a complete broadcast, which requires broadcasters to find the freshness in the news script, whether it is a new fact, a new policy, or a new development change in an event. For the same news content, finding new places, a new era, new methods, or if no new news can be found, the news broadcast will become like clear water rivers or rootless trees. In terms of voice, language, and technique, announcers should use their natural vocal range freely when broadcasting new news, the volume should not be too loud, real voice should be used, low volume should not be listed, pronunciation must be accurate, pure sound, articulation should be clear and natural, the sound should be generous and fluent, faster speed but not chaotic, to express the freshness of the news program. Meanwhile, the language of the news should be concise, more lean meat writing, turning into ordinary language text for a more natural sound, so that the audience hears that the announcer must be clear and strive to achieve easy acceptance. Only when the broadcast sound follows strong rules, is clean and neat, and properly handled emphasis, can freshness be fully embodied. However, attention should be paid to the fact that focusing on the freshness of the broadcast does not mean abandoning the principle of objectivity in news broadcasts. The freshness in news broadcasts reflects the psychological response of the audience, which is subjective. An experienced broadcaster should correctly grasp the degree from the manuscript, rather than making articles about freshness and novelty, exaggerating, or overacting, thus endangering the principle of objectivity in broadcast news. The novelty involved in news reporting no longer exists within such limits. This kind of freshness, new news comes from reality, that is, establishing a broadcast based on the overall grasp of the overall image of news facts. Based on the above experience, I believe that the principles of objectivity and freshness are the most basic principles of broadcasting news in radio news. Both sides complement each other. The principle of objectivity is the foundation, while freshness is better expressing a fact, i.e., the objectivity of the news.
