4488 yuan! It's the iPhone 5c, not a cheap version of iPhone.

by anonymous on 2013-09-11 14:36:24

Well, a rumor of many years finally came true. No matter you call it the low-cost iPhone or plastic iPhone, we are referring to the same mobile phone - iPhone 5c.

If you have followed our previous reports, you will find that we have explained the "low-cost iPhone" many times. This mobile phone won't be very cheap, but rather a mid-range one. So before the name iPhone 5c came out, I have always added quotation marks to the word "cheap".

If there were not so many exposures and leaks before, this product released together with iPhone 5s would definitely surprise us. What? Apple actually releases two new mobile phones in a single launch, but too much information and too long a leak cycle give us time to calm down and think about the meaning of this product's existence.

Market

According to Canalys' data previously, Apple's market share in China has fallen to the seventh place in the second quarter, even behind Xiaomi. And IDC's data in the first quarter also shows that Apple has ranked fifth in China. In the world, facing Android's rapid expansion, Apple can only stay put due to its relatively slow product iteration.

Therefore, facing the pressure of the market, Apple needs to become faster. Facing the increasingly fast and large Android phones on the market, the 3.5-inch iPhone 4 and 4S which are not cheap do not have much competitiveness. As a replacement for them, iPhone 5c has a larger screen and better experience, theoretically speaking, it will also be more competitive.

Although some compromises have been made in appearance and craftsmanship, Apple's brand is still a golden signboard. Considering these factors, plus a very competitive contract price and Apple's good ecosystem, the market for iPhone 5c is still broad, and its competitiveness is not weak, provided that it is a contract phone. But assumptions and market reactions are often inconsistent. We internally also have different opinions on the sales prospect of iPhone 5c. Whether iPhone 5c can help Apple make a big counterattack in the market remains to be seen, at least now, the sales situation of the domestic version in China will not be optimistic.

The starting price of the 2-year contract for the 16GB version is $99, which is $100 lower than last year's starting price of $199 for the 16GB version of iPhone 5 and this year's starting price of $199 for iPhone 5c, but the unlocked version's price is still not cheap: $549 for the 16GB version and $649 for the 32GB version.

But for the starting price of 4488 yuan in the Chinese market, I can only say that this price is very awkward in the domestic market, not much different from the price of 5288 yuan for iPhone 5c, but a huge gap compared to a series of sub-flagship Android models.

Cost

Personally, iPhone 5 is the most exquisite mobile phone I have ever seen, but behind this exquisite craftsmanship is the increasing difficulty of processing. I believe many people have heard rumors about the low initial yield rate of iPhone 5, and the craftsmanship of the identical-looking iPhone 4 and 4S is not simple either. However, the plastic shell of iPhone 5c makes the processing technology of this mobile phone much simpler. The prices of chips, storage and other internal components will decrease due to Moore's Law, but metals and glasses are not affected by this. Compared to expensive metals and fragile glasses, plastic is obviously an easier material to handle.

In addition, iPhone 5c replaced the position of iPhone 5. Little did we know that all configurations of 5c are consistent with the previous ones. Under the condition that the internal cost remains unchanged, the shell material is changed to plastic with greatly reduced processing difficulty.

Simplified product lines, simpler technology, and more common components bring cost reduction to Apple.

New

I believe Apple must hate the three words "low-cost version" very much. I believe the loss of control over these three words will affect the sales of this mobile phone to some extent. If the name iPhone 5c was exposed earlier to curb the spread of these three words, the impact on the Apple brand might be smaller. If the contract version can still be called a "low-cost version", then the domestic release price has nothing to do with the "low-cost version".

When browsing in an Apple store last weekend, suddenly a few girls came in and said to the iPod Touch: "Is this the iPhone 5c? It looks so thin, the colors are quite nice!"

Although it is an individual case, we can speculate from this: the image of iPhone 5c has already reached the public. Even people who do not pay much attention to technological trends like those girls already know about iPhone 5c. With the announcement of pricing and configuration, the three words "low-cost version" may be forgotten by people. As long as this model has an outstanding and conspicuous appearance, people's acceptance of it will be better than expected.

Apple did not explain much about the configuration of iPhone 5c during the launch, but according to the introduction on the official website of Apple, iPhone 5c is basically on par with iPhone 5. As a new product, positioned in the mid-range to compete with a series of sub-flagship Android phones, it will attract many undecided people, except in the Chinese market. The advantages of iPhone 5c over iPhone 4 and 4s are not only in the screen size, but also in the word "new".

New products, new colors, and better support for the new iOS 7 still make iPhone 5c quite attractive compared to 3.5-inch iPhones.

However, from another perspective, iPhone 5C can also be seen as Apple playing an iPhone 5 shell-changing game with us.

However, compared to the introduction of iPhone 5s's progress and length, the iPhone 5c at this launch seems somewhat perfunctory. Most of the time was spent on appearance, color, and material. This move by Apple perhaps indicates that the main market for iPhone 5c is young people who pursue novelty but have limited budget. And this group of people may also be Apple's new users.

And the starting price of 4488 yuan in the Chinese market proves that the c in iPhone 5c does not stand for Cheap, nor China.

The iPhone event will be held on September 10th.
