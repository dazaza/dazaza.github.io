With numerous social engagements and parties, to maintain a decent makeup look, one must prepare by nurturing hydrated skin. White Bone Spirits often experience several major dehydrating scenarios, making their skin more prone to dryness and flakiness than usual. Therefore, it is crucial to intensify special moisturizing efforts.

08:00 After makeup, apply [water] gel

[White Bone Spirits] move through various settings all day long, applying light makeup daily and heavy makeup for evening banquets. Everyone has experienced how the powder on the nose can become blotchy and how terrible dry lines appear at the corners of the eyes. Many people wish they could hydrate their skin after makeup, but is this possible?

Selection: Generally, these products are semi-transparent gels, slightly more nourishing than regular gels. They must work quickly when applied to the skin. If they are difficult to absorb or overly greasy, they are not suitable for use after makeup.

Usage: It’s best not to apply it all over the face to avoid smudging the makeup. Apply only on key areas such as the nose, corners of the mouth, corners of the eyes, and nasolabial folds. When applying, do not massage in circles; gently press it on and lightly pat a few times. Be sure to avoid shifting the makeup, and pat in place.

Tip: Post-makeup rescue is not the best method. To ensure makeup adherence, it's better to apply such products all over the face before foundation, let it fully absorb, and then apply makeup for a very hydrated look.

12:00 On the plane, apply [water] mask

12:00 On the plane, apply [water] mask

[Business travelers] cannot tolerate the dryness on planes. It's not just dry, but the entire face feels "collapsed" and very tired. This is because the dehydration level on planes is extremely high. Celebrities often apply a mask during flights to hydrate their skin and pass the time.

Selection: Choose cream masks that don't need to be washed off, with high hydration and moisturizing properties, preferably ones that can also be used around the eyes. If you're not afraid to scare others, sheet masks or paper masks are also fine.

Usage: First, use a slightly damp tissue to wipe away dust from your face. Then squeeze the mask onto your palm, spread it out, and evenly apply it on your face in a thin layer. You can then close your eyes and rest for a while, allowing your skin to rest too. Before disembarking, if you feel a bit greasy, you can gently wipe away any residue.

Tip: It is essential to drink plenty of water on the plane, continuously drinking. You can also drink some juice to supplement vitamins. However, avoid tea or coffee, as they are diuretics and will worsen dehydration.

17:00 At work, spray [water] mist

Why do office ladies often have sensitive skin? This is related to the air conditioning being on all year round in offices. The first issue is dryness, and secondly, the temperature differences when entering and exiting can easily make the skin fragile. As the weather gets colder and the hot air conditioning turns on, the face may become red and warm, so it's important to hydrate promptly to prevent dryness and sensitivity.

Selection: Choose thermal spring sprays, which won't irritate sensitive skin. They contain low concentrations of minerals and trace elements that can soothe and repair the skin. Alternatively, choose magnetized sprays containing certain magnetic water components that can alleviate skin fatigue.

Usage: Tilt your head up 45 degrees, hold the spray bottle in one hand, and spray it about 20 centimeters away from your face to ensure the mist reaches your face evenly. Wait a moment, then cover your face with a tissue and gently blot away any excess moisture. Remember to blot it away, otherwise, the remaining water will evaporate and take away internal skin moisture. You can hydrate like this approximately every two hours while closing your eyes for a brief rest.

Tip: Some thermal spring sprays can be used without avoiding the eyes, meaning you can spray them even with your eyes open, and they can also hydrate dry eyes from prolonged computer use.

00:00 During an all-nighter, seep [water] essence

00:00 During an all-nighter, seep [water] essence

People who frequently stay up all night know that looking in the mirror the next day reveals a face that looks like a "ghost." In reality, sleep and a good mood are truly the keys to beauty. But if you must pull an all-nighter, is there anything you can do to keep your skin in relatively good condition since you still have to see people the next day?

Selection: The reason for choosing a hydrating essence as a must-have for overnight work is that essences have small particles that can penetrate deep into the skin, providing better hydration that lasts longer. Choosing an essence containing collagen is also beneficial, as collagen is not only an excellent anti-aging product but also a perfect hydrator.

Usage: If you know you'll be working late, make sure to remove your makeup completely. Wearing makeup overnight is a heavy burden on the skin and can easily clog pores. The hydration applied the previous day would have been exhausted, so it's necessary to redo the entire skincare routine. Cleanse, hydrate, apply essence (since it's nighttime, the skin should be in self-repair mode, but without sleep, the repair mechanism cannot activate, so using essence becomes even more important), apply moisturizer, and sunscreen (if using a computer).

Tip: After staying up all night, the skin's keratin actually accelerates metabolism and death, so you can appropriately increase the frequency of exfoliation. Regularly use detoxifying masks, as staying up increases free radical damage. Use detoxifying masks to neutralize free radicals and reduce their harm to the skin, which essentially lowers the aging rate.
