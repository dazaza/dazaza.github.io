Auto insurance renewal: paying more attention to service than price

by anonymous on 2012-03-09 15:32:39

At the end and beginning of a year, it is the peak season for purchasing or renewing car insurance. The other day, Mr. Li bought a new Buick LaCrosse 2.4L Comfort Edition sedan at a purchase price of 244,000 yuan. Apart from consulting quotations from various insurance companies, what else should car owners pay attention to when buying car insurance for their new cars? Under the current insurance regulatory environment, there is almost no difference in premium prices for new cars among different insurance companies. Factors that affect car insurance premiums mainly include: the purchase price of the new car, the higher the value of the car, the higher the premium; rate discount preferences, if multiple types of insurance are purchased simultaneously, and there were no claims or traffic violations in the previous year, the vehicle premium for the next year can enjoy corresponding discounts; model risk coefficients, some models have higher coefficients for vehicle damage insurance or theft insurance, and the premium for these models will be correspondingly higher when purchasing vehicle damage insurance or theft insurance. In comparison, the services provided by insurance companies are more important and should be the focus for car owners to consider when insuring. Currently, many domestic property insurance companies led by PICC Property have made significant optimizations in terms of insurance payment deadlines. Their "notification of compensation within one hour for accidents below 10,000 yuan not involving personal injury" car insurance claims service commitment is much shorter than the 10-day legal deadline stipulated in the Insurance Law for paying insurance compensation. In addition, non-road accident rescue services are also an aspect that car owners need to consider. Mr. Li stated that this was already his second car. In fact, due to having purchased insurance beforehand, the vehicle damage caused by traffic accidents almost doesn't need to be worried about. Instead, encountering problems such as breakdowns, flat tires, or running out of fuel during daily driving is the most headache-inducing. It is understood that the Shanghai branch of PICC Property provides unlimited non-road accident rescue services throughout the year for customers who purchase car insurance through telesales or online sales channels. Within the validity period of the policy, if a vehicle encounters an emergency while driving within the jurisdiction of Shanghai, the customer can call the 24-hour customer service hotline at any time to request services such as towing, tire replacement, water delivery, and battery charging. A relevant person in charge of the Shanghai branch of PICC Property reminded car owner friends: After purchasing car insurance, carefully read the car insurance clauses, focusing on insurance liabilities, exclusions and special agreements, rights and obligations of the insured, calculation of deductibles or deductible rates, claim application procedures, provisions regarding cancellation and depreciation, whether the insurance company has explained exclusions, and whether they provide additional insurance coverage for exclusions. New service measures from the Shanghai branch of PICC Property: Starting from October 1st, customers who purchase car insurance through telesales or online sales channels will enjoy free door-to-door collection of claim documents for single or double car accidents not involving personal injury.
