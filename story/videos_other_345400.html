LEVI’S--the "pioneer" of denim, Bao'er Jeans Company; LEVI’S was named after Levi Strauss (Levi & Strauss), the inventor of the world's first pair of jeans. The California gold rush could be considered the catalyst for the birth of this brand. Ever since then, people have enthusiastically expressed their love for this tough brand of jeans. To this day, as denim has become an eternal trend, Levi’s has also become a symbol of a world-famous jeans brand. Prices: Low-end (made in Hong Kong or Suzhou, mostly TYPE 1 or 523 or REDLOOP) RMB 599-899; Mid-range (mostly made in the USA, some in Japan) RMB 1000+; High-end (LEVI’S VINTAGE CLOTHING) RMB 1500+.

Lee--a carrier of tradition and fashion. Lee is one of the three great classics of American denim culture. In 1975, the women's jeans brand Lee was born, and Bao'er Jeans joined to launch the women's jeans series called "Fit For Girls," which gave people more space for thought with its simple and clear logo. Founder H.D. Lee initially started as a workwear manufacturer, mainly supplying everyday work clothes to farmers and workers in the central and western United States during this century. Some of the classics from that time are still popular today, such as the overalls "BIB’N’BRACE." In 1997, the Lee company showcased a Guinness record-breaking 23-meter-long Lee10IZ giant jeans in Beijing and Shanghai, giving people a feast for the eyes. Price: RMB 500-700 (series like 720, 733, and 735 are all priced at 590 yuan, while most of the 408 series are around 600 yuan).

CK--sexy in its bones. Calvin Klein (Calvin & Klein), known as the "fashion prince" of New York's Seventh Avenue. "Minimalist style" is Calvin Klein's "registered trademark" in design. Everyone must surely remember the globally famous advertisement slogan from those years: young Shields shakes her flowing hair, lightly placing her hand on her hip, her magnetic voice saying, "There's nothing between Calvin and me" (I wear Calvin close to my skin). Price: RMB 700-1200.

Wrangler--wildness from the West. In 1940, the Blue Bell Overall Company was founded, introducing a workwear-style jeans brand named "Wrangler." The biggest difference between Wrangler and Lee jeans lies in Wrangler replacing the double arch decorative stitching on the back pocket with a "W"-shaped visible stitching. From the early 1940s to the late 1950s, Blue Bell invested heavily in Hollywood Western movie production, greatly increasing Wrangler's fame. In the 1960s, Blue Bell launched a series of denim products such as saddles, boots, and cowboy hats. With the passage of time, Wrangler has developed into a rugged yet confident, classic yet truly embodying the essence of the American West and fashion style. Its product designs are mainly inspired by the "spirit of the American West," designed according to the needs of real American cowboys, practical without being exaggerated, fully displaying the bold yet reserved feeling of American cowboys. Price: RMB 500-900 (men's styles start with 3, 5, 6, or W; women's styles start with 7 or 9).

DKNY--the embodiment of New York's freedom. DKNY was launched in 1988, short for Donna Karan New York, and is the younger secondary line created by Konna Karan for her daughter Gabby. Influenced by the city they live in, DKNY naturally incorporates the independent and free spirit of New York into its design. The large and bold DKNY not only appears on fabric labels but is often used as clothing patterns, representing a symbolic spirit. With the development of DENY JEANS, its energetic, vibrant, charming, and dynamic uniqueness has been deeply loved by the youth. Price: RMB 1200+.

Dior--luxury's fatal attraction. Most top-tier brands' jeans use denim produced by traditional Japanese looms, and Dior is no exception. Using such high-grade fabric allows for arbitrary "ruining" of the jeans without adverse effects. Overall, almost every pair of these jeans is handmade, including whitening, burning, destruction, crushing effects, etc. Therefore, each pair is unique, none exactly alike, and naturally, the price is relatively expensive. Price: RMB 2500-8000+.

GUESS--bold romantic love. GUESS was founded in 1981, and its sexy, bold brand style is closely related to the background of its founders, the Marciano brothers. The Marciano brothers grew up in the passionate cultural southern region of France, thus deeply planting the French romantic way of life in their hearts. Since GUESS originally started with denim clothing, its design tone has always retained a western flavor. Later, influenced by fashion elements, GUESS gradually introduced some form-fitting, sexy, minimalist designs. Price: RMB 1500~3000.
