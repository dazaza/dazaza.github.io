Another main culprit, Ding LongQuan, and other suspects are currently being actively pursued. Thirty specially selected personnel from the Dongguan Public Security Bureau's "Crack Down on Four Black Markets and Eliminate Four Evils" special operation task force and the Zhongtang Public Security Division formed a special investigation team, increasing the pursuit efforts and conducting round-the-clock operations to chase down relevant suspects.

Some raw materials came from Zengcheng, mainly flowing into factory canteens. On October 17th, relevant departments in Zhongtang Town, Dongguan, received reports and jointly struck down a black den located in the processing area of the Jiangnan Agricultural Wholesale Market producing preserved meat and sausages. The raw materials used to make preserved meat were all diseased dead pork. After confirmation by relevant departments, the den was operating without a license. To prevent maggots in the diseased dead pork, the black den owner mixed "industrial salt + sodium nitrite + pesticide Dichlorvos" for stirring, marinating the diseased dead pork, which could prevent maggots, flies, and long-term preservation.

According to reports, this black den had existed for nearly two years, specifically purchasing diseased dead pork from places like Boluo in Huizhou, Zengcheng in Guangzhou, and Shenzhen, processing them into preserved meats through salting and smoking. Every day, they produced 2000-2500 kilograms of preserved meat and sausages, which flowed into factory canteens and restaurants through the Jiangnan Agricultural Wholesale Market and Xinli Agricultural Wholesale Market in Zhongtang, Dongguan.

Currently, the special investigation team is conducting further interrogations of the eight captured suspects and accelerating the investigation of the case, tracing the sources of raw materials and product destinations. Next steps will involve transferring the case to judicial authorities to hold those involved criminally responsible.

Event Review:

Recently, relevant law enforcement departments in Zhongtang, Dongguan, received reports about a black den in the Jiangnan Agricultural Wholesale Market processing area using diseased dead pork to produce preserved meat and sausages, producing thousands of pounds daily. During the operation, authorities seized 500 kilograms of sausages, 500 kilograms of preserved meat, 2 tons of live pork without quarantine certificates, and a batch of industrial salt and Dichlorvos, extracting some samples for testing. Following the incident, it drew high attention from leaders in Guangdong Province and Dongguan City. On the 21st of this month, a joint inspection team from the Guangdong Provincial Public Security Department and the Guangdong Provincial Quality and Technical Supervision Bureau organized a special inspection meeting with members of the Dongguan Food Safety Committee in Zhongtang Town, conveying instructions from leaders Wang Yang and Liang Weifa. Both Dongguan City and Zhongtang Town established special working leading groups and special investigation teams for the case, thoroughly investigating the matter, and launching grid-style inspections throughout the town.
