Dong Yi's serialized work on the super special issue of Shonen Sunday, the heroine of the romantic comedy manga "Super Girl 4946" with a height of 49 meters and 46 centimeters, the first volume was officially released on the 17th. Akihabara stores put up advertising slogans such as "As long as there is love, height difference doesn't matter" and "The first love story of someone who is 4946 tall."

The story has an entirely unpopular protagonist, Tobu Makoto, a third-year junior high school student who dreams of dating girls. However, because of his short stature, he leads a miserable life where he cannot even get a girl to turn her head and look at him. But then, his fated goddess arrives, but she is as tall as 49 meters and 46 centimeters. The greatest height difference in the history of romantic comedies is about to create a love miracle!

The editor commented on this work, saying it created the largest "difference" couple in human history. Makoto was attacked by an alien, and at that moment, the "human guardian angel" - Mana of the Palace appeared in front of him. After Makoto saves Mana from danger again, she confesses her love to him.

The charm of this work lies in the romantic knot with the giant girl. Although she is large in size, her heart is more feminine than anyone else's. Makoto falls into a state of alternating joy and worry... The heroine fights tragically against monsters, and the enthusiasm of the protagonist to protect the heroine are important reasons that move readers. Content Summary: "Ultra Alloy Club 2" is currently serialized in "Man You, Manga 100", a super parody comic drawn by the well-known artist LING. This comic based on youth campus life humorously explains young people's passion for anime in school life.

A regular girl named Ruan Ying, with average grades, appearance, and intelligence, becomes the president of the school's comic club "Pure Color Shop" under the persistent persuasion of her good friend Xiaoxi. This club was once glorious but is now facing the crisis of being disbanded. Although the members all love comics in their own unique way, they seem to lack "team spirit"... The first task after Ruan Ying takes office is to prevent the club from being disbanded!
