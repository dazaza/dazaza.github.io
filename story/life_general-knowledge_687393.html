The Dark Night Cat: The Dark Night Affection

Night, so dark! I move forward in the darkness; I am a sprite of the night! Darkness gives me strength and allows me to reveal my truest self! ——Preface

Lazily perched on the wall, I close my eyes in comfort. I am a cat! Yes, I am guarding this courtyard to prevent it from being destroyed. This is an ancient and mysterious little yard filled with stories of strength. And the protagonist is me, the brave warrior of the night —— the Dark Night Cat!

Many years ago, a man left his hometown. His face was sharp, with distinct features, and his temples were reddish-brown. He had thick eyebrows and large eyes, yet his complexion was as pale as jade. He wore plain clothing adorned with light gauze, and at his waist hung a black dragon sword sheathed with an emerald pendant carved with the word "Affection." This person was the eldest son of the first great family in the江湖 (jianghu) world: the "Dark Night" family, known as Dark Night Affection, nicknamed the "Dark Night Reaper!" The Dark Night family appeared in this world a hundred years ago, a rising family whose master was named Dark Night Soul! Most people in the family were righteous, especially this Dark Night Affection, who was kind-hearted and proficient in both literature and martial arts. This family never bullied the common people, so the common folk harbored no hatred towards them. There's a legend that no one knows where the base of the Dark Night family is located! They are like sprites, coming and going without a trace!

Dark Night Affection left home this time for a mission! Three days ago, news came from Qingyun Town: all two hundred sixteen households and over two thousand four hundred people were killed, with no survivors! Tragically, not even the children were spared! (Qingyun Town is under the jurisdiction of the Dark Night family.) Family scouts reported seeing a group of people staying at the "Peaceful Sky" inn in Wangji Town, twenty miles away from Qingyun Town. And this group belonged to the Dugu family, the third largest family after the Dark Night family and the Zhou family! The Dugu family has a three-hundred-year history, mainly relying on underworld activities for survival! People in the Dugu family are all treacherous, but it must be admitted, their intelligence is quite high! This time, Dark Night Affection's main task is to investigate the reason why the Dugu family wiped out Qingyun Town!

Wangji Town. "Hey, sir, please come in!" "Let's drink!" "Waiter, bring some wine!"... Dark Night Affection just arrived at the "Peaceful Sky Inn" in Wangji Town! This inn was visited by Dark Night Affection three years ago with his father! Nothing has changed, it's still bustling with activity! The owner of this inn once received favors from Dark Night Affection's father, Dark Night Soul, so Dark Night Affection also knows the innkeeper! "Oh! Isn't this Master Dark Night? How do you have time to visit our humble place! Come, come, come, upstairs to the private room!" The innkeeper approached Dark Night Affection! "Mr. Wang, too kind! It's really improper for me to disturb you here!" "Not at all! The visit of Master Dark Night truly honors our humble establishment! Please come inside!" "Please!" In the private room. "Mr. Wang, regarding the incident in Qingyun Town three days ago," Dark Night Affection paused and said, "I assume you've already heard about it?" Mr. Wang's expression suddenly changed, just for an instant, then returned to normal! But that moment was already observed by Dark Night Affection! Dark Night Affection had a hint of an answer in his heart! "This, this event I've heard about! I don't know who could be so cruel! It must be the Ten Great Evils in the jianghu! Yes, it must be them!" Mr. Wang straightened his expression! "Ten Great Evils? I fear they wouldn't have such a grand scheme!"
