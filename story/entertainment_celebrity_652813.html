On December 11, 2011, an extremely embarrassing "dress-trampling" incident occurred on the red carpet at the Beijing Fashion Man event. Sun Feifei, dressed in a white strapless long dress, made a sexy appearance, only to have her dress accidentally stepped on by host Fang Ling, causing her flesh-colored breast tape to be exposed. The scene was extremely awkward, and her assistant quickly went on stage to drape an outer garment over her before they left the event directly. (Click on the image to enter Sun Feifei's Weibo.)

Afterwards, Fang Ling stated that she would willingly let Sun Feifei step on her again without wearing any breast tape. She also mentioned that if Sun Feifei wanted to stir up publicity, she wasn't really her type. In response, Sun Feifei's agent, Xu Zhenyan, questioned whether Fang Ling had premeditated the incident, pushing the "dress-trampling" incident back into the spotlight. Fang Ling later expressed that because she wasn't famous, no one believed what she said, and she didn't want to bring up the matter anymore, hoping it would soon be forgotten.

Sun Feifei herself, who hadn't made any public statements since the incident, responded for the first time on Weibo yesterday: "I don't remember which wise person once said, 'One's heart grows larger from being wronged.' This life, my bust may not grow bigger, but my heart can expand infinitely. Fang Ling, I also had inappropriate actions in this matter, and I hope you can forgive me. Can we be friends?"
