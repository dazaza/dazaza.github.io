Southern Metropolis Daily Report by Zhang Shuzhou from Hunan

On November 28, a netizen named "Mountain Beyond" posted on Rednet, alleging that "the Xintian County TCM Hospital in Yongzhou City, Hunan Province refused to treat and abandoned teacher Xie Tongjun, who was severely injured in a car accident, in a garbage pile." According to investigations by the Southern Metropolis Daily, Xie Tongjun was found unconscious on the side of the road a few days prior and was subsequently sent to the Xintian County TCM Hospital via 120 emergency services. However, a few days later, the family found him lying unconscious on the roadside outside the hospital.

The post claimed that on November 15, Xie Tongjun, a teacher at Dapingtang Middle School in Xintian County, Yongzhou City, was involved in a motorcycle accident while returning to the county from school. Three days later, he was discovered unconscious by villagers. On the afternoon of November 18, he was admitted to the Xintian County TCM Hospital. However, when the family found Xie Tongjun on November 22, he was lying beside a garbage pile outside the hospital gates. The family then attempted to readmit him, but the hospital turned a deaf ear, implying that Xie Tongjun had been abandoned by the hospital.

Family members claimed that after the incident, leaders from the Xintian County government instructed the deputy director of the Health Bureau and the deputy director of the Judicial Bureau to form an investigation team, holding a coordination meeting on the evening of November 27. One decision made during the meeting was that all medical expenses for Xie Tongjun would be covered by the Xintian County TCM Hospital.

Xie Tongjun, aged 33, is a physics teacher at Dapingtang Middle School in Xintian County. His father-in-law, Mr. Zheng, told the Southern Metropolis Daily that because Xie Tongjun had not shown up for work since November 15, his school colleagues came to the house on November 21 to inquire about his whereabouts. Mr. Zheng immediately organized relatives to search along the roads. Eventually, a villager surnamed Deng informed them that on November 18, he had found someone unconscious on the side of the road and had subsequently notified the 120 emergency services, after which the injured person was taken to the Xintian County TCM Hospital. On the morning of November 22, Mr. Zheng and several relatives went to the TCM hospital to search for Xie Tongjun but could not find him. Around 10 o'clock, however, they found Xie Tongjun unconscious on a small road outside the hospital. "A motorcyclist outside the hospital gate said that around 7 o'clock in the morning, he saw three people from the hospital carry him out and leave him there."

When the Southern Metropolis Daily reporter inquired about the investigation results from Mr. Li, the deputy director of the Judicial Bureau of Xintian County and a member of the investigation team, he was told that he had not directly participated in the investigation and was unaware of the details. Another member of the investigation team, Deputy Director Lei Yuanwu of the Health Bureau, stated that he was attending a conference locally but had already instructed that treating the patient should be prioritized, requiring the TCM hospital to pay the medical fees. Lei Yuanwu also mentioned that it was necessary to determine whether Xie Tongjun had been involved in a car accident or a robbery, and the county leadership had directed that "investigations should be conducted where necessary, and matters handled appropriately." Regarding the question of whether "Xie Tongjun was abandoned by the TCM hospital on the roadside," Deputy Director Lei stated, "It is still under investigation."
