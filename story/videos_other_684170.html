Objects/ Materials, Steps/ Methods, Shoufeng Driving School Subject Two Exam Precautions: First, candidates must bring their ID cards and gather on time (those who are late will have their exam qualifications revoked), then proceed to the exam area with a hanging tag in line led by the driving school staff, with sealed garbage trucks. (In order not to affect the next subject's exam time for candidates, please return the hanging tag and admission ticket to the driving school staff for registration after the exam). Candidates in the waiting area are not allowed to smoke, make noise, be uncivilized, or use mobile phones (which must remain off). The waiting area should be kept quiet, and loud talking is prohibited.

When recording fingerprints for the桩exam, the examiner will randomly assign a number on-site. After obtaining the number, candidates should go to the corresponding warehouse for the exam and avoid entering the wrong warehouse. When entering the exam area, follow the designated warehouse number and wait under that number’s sign.

The桩exam lasts 10 minutes. After completing the桩exam, candidates must drive the car back to the starting point, cut the infrared beam, and wait for the computer to announce the pass before opening the door and getting out of the car. Proceed to the next waiting area (2+1) with the admission ticket. (Note: If the computer does not announce a pass, it means the infrared beam was not cut, so continue driving forward and do not waste time).

For the second half of the exam, when recording fingerprints, the examiner will randomly assign a lane and vehicle number on-site. After receiving this information, candidates should go to the corresponding lane and vehicle for the exam and avoid boarding the wrong vehicle. After boarding, verify your own information; if there is an error, report it immediately to the on-duty examiner and do not continue the exam.

On-site exam items include: hill start, parallel parking, right-angle turn, curve driving, and speed-limited passing through wide gates, all of which have signal lights. Candidates must follow the signal light instructions when passing through these items. Red lights indicate you cannot enter the item’s exam, while green lights allow entry.

Parallel parking has two warehouses per lane, allowing you to choose one for the exam. If the chosen warehouse is already in use, bypass from the left side.

If assigned three lanes, all exam items must be completed within those lanes. After finishing, drive the car back to the starting point of the third lane ramp before exiting the vehicle.

Considerations for enrolling in driving lessons: Whether taking the exam or enrolling, original ID cards are required. Students should not feel nervous during exams.
