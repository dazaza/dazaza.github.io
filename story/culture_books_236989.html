"Royal Youth" - A song of youth romance between a mixed-blood prince and an ordinary girl.

by jqrxass on 2009-09-12 15:09:58

He is a mixed-blood child with green eyes. At the age of six, he drifted to a poor small town and became Joanne's older brother, but was called a little monster by the ignorant townsfolk.

Once, she thought that the winding life line in his palm was her whole life, her chaotic and long afterlife. When she grew up, she realized it was an unforgivable self-deception.

Is there a place for her to settle down in his life plan? After a turbulent growth, can they still grasp each other's remaining breaths? Can that restless youth shake off the fate of breaking up...

Part One: The Royal Boy

Suddenly, my small figure was overshadowed by another shadow. I looked up and saw a white-clothed boy. He was all in white, like an angel from a fairy tale, radiating dazzling light around him. That elegant and noble temperament seemed to be innate, making people feel happy and relaxed as if basking in the spring breeze.

Most importantly, he was different from any child in Willow Town. His eyes were green, misty and mysterious green, clear but bottomless, making people fall into them like moths drawn to fire. In that era full of bullying, it was like the stars behind the dark clouds, brightening my entire night sky.

Part Two: The Royal Boy

My dear brother, as long as you are fine, everything is fine.

From childhood to adulthood, every smile and every sorrow on your face has been my emotional compass. If there are signs of gloom in your eyes, then the sun in my world cannot rise from the east.

"Brother, what is your dream?" I gazed at the twinkling stars in the sky and asked softly.

"To be a jewelry designer, the royal jeweler of European royalty." At that moment, I saw exceptionally dazzling light in Shenqu's eyes, along with burning ambition like wildfire spreading across the land.

Part Three: The Royal Boy

The bright window in my heart slowly closed... One day, it will eventually shut completely, leaving only thick dust telling secrets that can never be revealed.

Suddenly, I felt so sad. I told Luokuo that I would go back to the classroom to get something and ran downstairs alone.

I hope Shenqu can be happy, but I also hope he doesn't leave too soon. If only there weren't people like Yiguang, then he wouldn't so easily forget about me and ignore me. How can you, Qiao'an, be so petty? Shenqu is doing so well among the princesses, shouldn't you be happy for him?

Part Four: The Royal Boy

"Afterward, wherever you want to go, just tell your brother, and I'll take you there." After drying my hair, Shenqu suddenly said to me.

"Eh?!" Should I be happy or sad that Shenqu said that?

My dear brother, he doesn't know that after Yiguang, I no longer dare to make any requests of him. I'm afraid of disturbing their happiness with my excessiveness, and I'm also afraid of seeing Yiguang's attitude while being by my brother's side.

If I say that I want to find back the lost sky from my childhood, would my brother take me there too?
