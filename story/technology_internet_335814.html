**80’s-inspired Coach Poppy Collection**

5 Mar 2010

Author: Sarah

In: Coach Handbags

In picture: poppy metallic leather glam tote

The warmth of gold on rosy shades makes this metallic leather bag confidently high-fashion and feminine.

- Metallic leather with foil overlay

- Inside zip, cellphone, multifunction pockets

- Zip-top closure, fabric lining

- 20” handles with 8 1/2” drop

- 16 3/4 (L) x 12 1/4 (H) x 4 (W)

The Coach Poppy Collection has been an 80's-inspired collection aimed at younger women with its bright colors and lower prices, helping to increase store traffic from the previous quarter. The average price of a Poppy bag is $260, about 20 percent less than the usual Coach purse. It is said that the line’s introduction is part of a plan by Coach to reduce overall prices by 10 to 15 percent and appeal to more cost-conscious shoppers.
