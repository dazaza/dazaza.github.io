Guiyang self-help travel

by zengxr840824 on 2008-06-01 11:31:25

Self-service tourism is a kind of tourism that has emerged in recent years. The travel agency provides services such as hotel and flight reservations, travel insurance, tour guides, and transportation. After the tourists arrive at their destination, they can use the vehicles provided by the travel agency to freely travel to any scenic spot, as they wish, freely and easily. It gets rid of the previous mode of traveling according to the pre-arranged itinerary of the travel agency. After this model was launched, it was loved by tourists, promoted the development of tourism, and was a symbol of the development of tourism.

One night for two days:

Route One:

Day One: Board the ship in Guilin to visit the Li River, then arrive in Yangshuo to visit the Banyan Tree, Peach Blossom Spring, and Clustered Dragon Cave, then return to Guilin. In the evening, you can choose a water tour of the Two Rivers and Four Lakes, stay overnight in Guilin.

Day Two: A day within Guilin city, Reed Flute Cave, Elephant Trunk Hill, Seven Star Park, Folded Brocade Mountain, Lijiang Folklore Garden, etc., adjust flexibly according to the time of your return trip. Note: This is a very traditional Guilin tourist route, with mountains, water, caves, parks, and folklore. It suits business guests with little time, as well as family tours. Relatively speaking, the price is relatively high.

Route Two:

Day One: Board the ship in Guilin to tour the Li River, then arrive in Yangshuo for free activities. In the afternoon, cycle to the Banyan Tree and Moon Hill. In the evening, relax in bars on West Street, stay overnight in Yangshuo (or choose a small boat to tour Xingping - Yangdi in the morning or afternoon, the rest remains the same).

Day Two: Drift down the Yulong River (if there's still time, you can explore Buddha Water Cave), plus Water Cave back to Yangshuo around 6 pm.

Note: This route is quite popular, doesn't cost much, and doesn't require much physical strength. It suits most tourists.

Route Three:

Day One: Hike along the Li River (Yangdi - Xingping, you can choose to hike completely), relax in bars on West Street, stay overnight in Yangshuo.

Day Two: Cycle along the Yulong River, Banyan Tree, Moon Hill, Buddha Water Cave, return to Yangshuo via country roads around 4 pm.

Note: This route suits people with abundant energy, costs very low. It suits students, backpackers, and weekend tourists.

One night for three days:

Three-day series are not specifically described here, there are too many combinations. You can add the following items based on the above two-day options:

A. One-day tour of Peace Longji (you can return the same day whether starting from Guilin or Yangshuo, return to Guilin around 5 pm)

B. Half-day tour of Crown Cave (it's recommended to start from Guilin, if starting from Yangshuo, transfer is needed)

C. One-day tour of the downstream Li River welfare, Liugong Village, Three Color Pool (return to Yangshuo around 4 pm)

D. Half-day tour of Silver Rock in Lipu (it's recommended to start from Yangshuo, convenient transportation)

E. One-day tour of Yangshuo Grape Stone City (start from Yangshuo, return to Yangshuo around 4 pm)

F. One-day tour of Le Man Di Leisure World in Guilin (it's recommended to start from Guilin, return to Guilin around 4 pm)

G. One-day tour of Yao Mountain and Jingjiang King Mausoleum in Guilin (start from Guilin, return to the city center around 3 pm)

One night for four days:

Route One:

Day One: Arrive directly in Guilin, take a bus and transfer to Dazhai (depart Guilin before 2 pm), arrive in Dazhai in the afternoon, watch the sunset at Observation Point No.1 or No.2, stay overnight in Tian Tou Zhai.

Day Two: Watch the sunrise over Longji in Dazhai at Observation Point No.2 or No.1 in the morning, go to Observation Point No.3. After eating, stroll around the village, descend the mountain around 10 am. Have lunch in Longsheng County, arrive in Guilin around 3 pm, arrive in Yangshuo before 5 pm, rest in Yangshuo.

Day Three: Hike along the Li River (Yangdi - Xingping, you can choose to hike completely).

Day Four: Cycle along the Yulong River, Banyan Tree, Moon Hill, Buddha Water Cave, return to Yangshuo via country roads around 4 pm.

Note: This route fits the backpacking style, costs cheap, all visited scenic spots can be considered classic, requires high physical strength, everyone is welcome to try. On the third day, you can choose to tour the essence section of Xingping - Yangdi by small boat plus Xingping Fishing Village, which can be more relaxing. If you return to Guilin early on the fourth day and your departure time is late, you can see the Elephant Trunk Hill.

Route Two:

Day One: One-day tour of Peace Longji, return to Guilin, in the evening, you can choose a water tour of the Two Rivers and Four Lakes.

Day Two: Board the ship in Guilin to tour the Li River, then arrive in Yangshuo for free activities. In the afternoon, cycle to the Banyan Tree and Moon Hill, relax in bars on West Street in the evening, stay overnight in Yangshuo.

Day Three: Xingping Fishing Village, Old Fort Mountain (or Peach Blossom Spring plus Yulong River drift), stay overnight in Yangshuo.

Day Four: Return to Guilin, tour Crown Cave or several attractions within the city.

Note: Very popular route, suitable for all groups.

Route Three:

Day One: Hike along the Li River (Yangdi - Xingping, you can choose to hike completely).

Day Two: Xingping Fishing Village, in the afternoon, cycle to the Banyan Tree and Moon Hill in Yangshuo, stay overnight in Yangshuo.

Day Three: Peach Blossom Spring plus Yulong River drift, stay overnight in Yangshuo.

Day Four: Return to Guilin, tour Crown Cave or several attractions within the city.
