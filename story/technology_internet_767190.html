Surface 2/Pro 2 Pre-orders Officially Open: Starting at 3288/6588 Yuan

by anonymous on 2013-10-10 18:30:33

At 12 o'clock today, the new Surface 2 and Surface Pro 2 (the Chinese edition/professional edition) will officially be available for pre-order domestically (address).

The Surface 2 price is: 32GB version at 3288 RMB, 64GB version at 4088 RMB (both with 2GB of memory); The Surface 2 Professional Edition/Chinese Edition's prices are as follows: 64GB (4GB memory) version at 6588 RMB, 128GB (4GB memory) version at 7388 RMB, 256GB (8GB memory) version at 9688 RMB, 512GB (8GB memory) version at 13388 RMB. Users who purchase any of these devices will receive two years of free 200GB SkyDrive storage space.

Compared to the previous generation of products, the Surface 2 and Surface Pro 2 have seen a very significant improvement in hardware. The Surface 2 has been upgraded to a 1.7GHz Tegra 4 processor, with the resolution increased to 1920x1080; The Surface Pro 2 features a fourth-generation Intel Core i5-4200U processor (base frequency 1.6 GHz, Turbo boost up to 2.6 GHz), integrated Intel HD Graphics 4400 graphics card, with options for 4GB or 8GB memory, and comes equipped with larger capacity SSDs.

According to previous reports, the sales of the Surface 2 and Surface Pro 2 abroad have been very optimistic, with repeated news of stockouts. Microsoft has confidently stated that the second-generation Surface will sell out during pre-orders. It remains to be seen whether this trend will continue domestically.
