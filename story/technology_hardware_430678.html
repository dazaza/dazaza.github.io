What trick is the 139 Moving Salesman talking about?

by xjnp2615 on 2011-05-13 16:40:00

By Wang Yi Jian

Every move made by the mobile industry can attract attention within the sector. In recent years, after achieving great success in its mobile phone business, Mobile has extended its reach to the internet. For Mobile, entering the internet business was just a matter of time because PCs and mobile phones are becoming increasingly integrated, with the boundaries between them becoming more blurred, thus creating an opportunity for Mobile to enter the internet.

Just like the recent revamp of the 139 Community, it is considered an important step for Mobile's entry into the internet. In the most popular Twitter field of 2009, the 139 Community also launched a Twitter service suitable for mobile devices called "Talker." However, we need to note that Mobile's Talker is quite different from other Twitter services because Mobile has incorporated the element of mobile phones. This advantage clearly distinguishes Talker from ordinary Twitter and differs from regular SNS sites. Perhaps the emergence of Talker represents a revolutionary change.

If you haven't tried it, you will never understand its mysteries. The future use of mobile phones will no longer be limited to sending text messages and making calls. With the arrival of 3G, the mobile phone will become an important terminal for the internet. On one hand, accessing the internet via mobile becomes easier and more convenient; on the other hand, the usage frequency and ownership of mobile phones exceed that of computers. Precisely because of this, Mobile has aggressively expanded in this area.

If you haven't tried it, you will never understand Mobile's unique advantages. This advantage is evident in the growth of its user base. Since 139.com went online in Guangdong, in just two short months, Guangdong Mobile had over 4 million users, and nationwide over 6.5 million users. So, from a detailed functional perspective, what features does Mobile offer in Talker?

Firstly, it bridges the gap between mobile phones and the internet because its ID is determined through a phone number. Phone numbers can be linked to many services and have high user stickiness, allowing internet services such as Talker to fully penetrate the user base. Determining users through phone numbers also makes it less chaotic than ordinary services, ensuring that you are not completely unaware of the other party. This ensures the authenticity and reliability of user interactions, undoubtedly representing another feasible and good exploration of real-name systems. People always complain why he (she) doesn't change for me.

In terms of functionality, you can not only talk to friends but also send and receive pictures via MMS. In short, both mobile phones and computers can be used. You can achieve the social networking function of SNS through it, as well as the chat function of Twitter, even including sending jokes, blessings, sweet nothings, etc., all covered within. Of course, the involvement of mobile phones greatly enhances the timeliness and serviceability of its services. You can keep your phone on 24 hours a day, but it's rare to stay in front of a computer for 24 hours.

To maximize the integration of mobile phones and the internet, the 139 Talker allows users to add all friends from their mobile phonebook into the community's friend circle through a mobile client, truly achieving interoperability of SNS on both mobile and PC platforms. This promotion method has an advantage over ordinary email promotions because the importance of phone numbers far exceeds other contact methods like email.

If you haven't tried it, you won't understand what trick Mobile Talker is playing. Of course, Mobile's Talker still has many shortcomings and needs continuous development and progress, such as actively achieving interconnectivity with other internet companies.
