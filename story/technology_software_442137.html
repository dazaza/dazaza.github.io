"Together with Meteor Shower" is a school idol drama produced by Hunan Satellite TV in China. The show is considered to be adapted from the Japanese manga "Hana Yori Dango" by Youko Tsukimiya, though the production crew denies this and Hunan TV has stated that the series is an original work. However, both the plot and characters are thought to resemble "Hana Yori Dango", especially the Taiwan version of "Meteor Garden". In fact, Hunan TV's blog on Tencent compares some of the main characters to those in the Taiwan version of "Meteor Garden", referring to this as the "Mainland Version of Meteor Garden", indirectly admitting it's another version of Meteor Garden. The cast includes winners from "Happy Boy" and a group of new actors.

[Edit Section] Plot Introduction

Chu Yujuan, born from humble origins, is an excellent senior high school girl whose biggest dream is to attend the prestigious aristocratic university, Ailis Dun College. With money borrowed from her uncle Xuecun, Yujuan is sent to Ailis Dun. Yunhai, Du Mu Lei, Shangguan Ruiqian, and Ye Shuo are the four young princes of Ailis Dun, each sunny and handsome, becoming idols worshipped by all the girls in the school. Unsatisfied with the lives arranged by their parents, unwilling to attend Ailis Dun, they conspire various pranks in hopes of being expelled by the principal, thus forming the troublesome H4 group. Inevitably, Yujuan meets the H4 group, becomes a target of their pranks after standing up for someone else, but through challenges and competitions, they come to admire each other and become friends. The H4 group is moved by Yujuan's strength and independence, thus changing their ways, starting to study hard, and eventually becoming the top four students in the school.
