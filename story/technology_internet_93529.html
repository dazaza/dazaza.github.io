Packaging machinery refers to machinery that can complete all or part of the packaging process for products and goods. The packaging process includes major procedures such as filling, wrapping, and sealing, as well as related preceding and succeeding procedures like cleaning, stacking, and dismantling. Additionally, packaging may also include processes such as measurement or stamping on the packaging. Using mechanical packaging can improve productivity, reduce labor intensity, meet the needs of large-scale production, and satisfy requirements for cleanliness and hygiene. The development of packaging machinery has provided the necessary guarantee for the modern processing and mass production of food and pharmaceuticals.
