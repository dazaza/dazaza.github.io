When I found you

"Hey, not bad, even though you're dressed like this with high heels and leather shoes wholesale, it still can't hide the charming beauty of your features," Zhuo Benqi said. "Enough talk, quickly take me to the supermarket or shopping center here, the competition time is almost here," Yunhai said awkwardly while wearing that outfit. After Zhuo Benqi came out in his changed clothes, he handed Yunhai a broken bowl. "Why are you giving me a broken bowl?" Yunhai asked curiously. "To be a beggar, you need such a bowl," Zhuo Benqi said. "Oh, I almost forgot, beggars do have broken bowls," Yunhai said. In the shopping center, as Zhuo Benqi had said, the streets were exceptionally clean, and even the street cleaners seemed noble and dignified. The people coming and going were all dressed meticulously, stylish men and women. Yunhai, in his beggar's clothing, could feel the disdainful looks from those gentlemen and ladies. Yunhai saw Zhuo Benqi start walking around with his broken bowl, hunched over, bowing, looking righteous among the crowd. Zhuo Benqi, isn’t he a wealthy businessman? How could he put aside his self-respect and beg? Yunhai doubted in his heart. Yunhai imitated Zhuo Benqi, hunching his body, bowing, holding the broken bowl, walking around in the crowd. Suddenly, a well-dressed middle-aged woman wearing high heels approached Yunhai. This middle-aged woman touched Yunhai's chin and smiled, saying, "Oh, daring to beg here? You've got quite some guts, you and that other beggar over there are the only two beggars here." Yunhai proudly said, "So what if there are only two of us? Is that not allowed?" The woman touched Yunhai's face and said, "Why so proud? Your face is nice, but how about this: come home with me, I'll take care of you, be my lover, I promise my husband won't know." Yunhai was furious, grabbing the woman's clothes and shouting, "What did you say? Say it again! I'll ruin your reputation! You wicked woman!" The woman raised her eyebrows and kicked Yunhai's foot with her high heels, "How dare you talk to me like that! A beggar dares to speak to me like that!" The woman then walked away without looking back. Just as Yunhai wanted to chase after her, Zhuo Benqi stopped him, "Don't be foolish, do you want more trouble? If she tells her husband, you're dead for sure." Yunhai rubbed his kicked foot and complained, "It hurts so much, this female shoe wholesale thing really hurts. You're amazing, Zhuo Benqi, why must we compare on this? I, I, I am speechless!" Zhuo Benqi patted his shoulder and said, "I knew it, you can't last long. Give up, I guarantee you won't win against me." Zhuo Benqi confidently smiled at Yunhai. Next, Yunhai, fearing rejection, found a corner of the street and quietly waited for people to give him money voluntarily, but after a long time, only a few kind-hearted people threw him some coins. But Zhuo Benqi was a different situation. Yunhai saw Zhuo Benqi begging with a servile attitude, his extremely flattering demeanor made Yunhai feel sick. However, the reality couldn't be denied, Zhuo Benqi's broken bowl clearly had more money than Yunhai's. 7.4 In the evening, the streets were basically empty now, only a few cleaners were working. Zhuo Benqi walked to Yunhai and confidently said, "Meng Rong Yunhai, you lost." Yunhai sat silently in the corner, admitting that he indeed lost. "Looks like you can't let go of your pride!" Zhuo Benqi said confidently. "Just state your conditions, don't beat around the bush, but you can't take Yuqin away." "How can you be sure I can't take Yuqin away?" Zhuo Benqi said. "Yuqin loves me, she doesn't love you, so she won't go with you," Yunhai said confidently. "But what if I can make Yuqin willingly go with me? What would you do then?" Zhuo Benqi retorted. Yunhai thought inside, impossible? No way. In Zhuo Benqi's villa, the black cloth over Yuqin's eyes was untied by Yunhai. Yuqin opened her eyes and immediately asked, "How was the competition? Did you lose or win, Yunhai?" Yunhai remained silent. At this point, Zhuo Benqi said, "He couldn't let go of his pride, he lost." Yuqin patted Yunhai and gently said, "Don't be sad, Yunhai, don't worry, I absolutely will not go with him. Even if he wins, in my heart, you are still my best Yunhai!" Yuqin said confidently. Yunhai was instantly moved. Zhuo Benqi angrily said, "Why do you always protect him? Even if he lost, you still protect him?" Yunhai hearing Zhuo Benqi's words, chuckled stupidly. Yuqin said, "Of course I protect him. Besides, I don't even know you, what nonsense are you talking about? Although I appreciate your care during this time, I really don't remember who you are." Yuqin said apologetically. At this moment, Zhuo Benqi burst into tears like a child, scaring Yunhai and Yuqin. Zhuo Benqi said slowly, "How could you forget me? Why did you forget me? I miss you so much, do you know how sad I am because you forgot me? You don't recognize me anymore! You truly don't recognize me! You have completely forgotten me!" Zhuo Benqi's voice echoed throughout the villa. 7.5 Zhuo Benqi narrated his story word by word. "When I was young, my family was very poor. My parents went out to work, and I lived with my grandmother. When I was 10 years old, my grandmother passed away, and since then, I never heard from my parents again. Initially, my neighbors helped me wholeheartedly, giving me food and clothes, but as time went by, they gradually stopped helping me, and I had no choice but to beg. Those days of begging were truly painful; I endured people's insults and contempt. Sometimes, if I was lucky, I could get a little money, but if I wasn't lucky, I would not only get nothing but also get kicked by those rich madams with their high heels. One day, my savior finally came. There was a young man with his daughter shopping, this man was very kind. He and his daughter took me to a restaurant where I filled my stomach, then brought me back to their house to take a bath, gave me new clothes, and later this man even found an orphanage for me. Every weekend, he and his daughter would visit me at the orphanage, and I played happily with his daughter, I really liked her. But suddenly one weekend, they didn't come anymore, and I could feel something was wrong. Later, a wealthy family with the surname Zhuo adopted me. I studied hard, just to repay that man when I grew up and marry his daughter. So I searched for 4 years in the sea of people before I found you. Later, I learned that your father suddenly passed away at that time." As Yuqin listened to his description, an image of a little boy appeared in her mind, and she was surprised to say, "You're that little boy named Yang Ge?" At this moment, Zhuo Benqi burst into tears, "You finally remembered me, you finally remembered me!" Zhuo Benqi continued, "Yes, Yang Ge is my real name, it was only after I went to my adoptive parents' house that they changed my name. Yuqin, when I found you, I knew your father had already passed away, and I cried the entire night. If it weren't for your father's wholehearted help, I wouldn't be here today." Yunhai listened to this strange story and spoke after a long pause, "Things happened so long ago, yet you still remember Yuqin?" Zhuo Benqi said, "Back then, Yuqin was quite chubby, at first, I didn't like her too much, but later I found she was a very lovely girl. Playing with her, I started to like her." Zhuo Benqi sweetly said, "Yuqin, now you're so thin, I barely recognize you. Unfortunately, you married Yunhai, if you weren't married now, I would definitely compete fairly with Yunhai!" Yunhai smiled and said, "If that's the case, why did you kidnap Yuqin?" Zhuo Benqi said, "I wanted to compete with you alone, at least let me get to know you, see if Yuqin married the right person." Yunhai laughed after hearing this, and at this point, Yuqin also laughed. Yuqin laughed and said, "We did play happily together in the orphanage back then, I liked you too back then." Hearing this, Yunhai became jealous, pinching Yuqin's nose unhappily, "Did you play happily with Zhuo Benqi or with me?" Yuqin intentionally said, "Of course, I had more fun with Zhuo Benqi, why did you keep bullying me in Eliston?" Both Zhuo Benqi and Yunhai laughed after hearing this. After Yuqin finished speaking, she felt a sharp pain in her abdomen and began sweating cold sweat. Seeing Yuqin sweating profusely, Yunhai felt something was wrong and asked, "Yuqin, what's wrong with you?" At this point, Yuqin was in too much pain to speak. Realizing immediately, Yunhai anxiously picked up Yuqin and shouted to Zhuo Benqi, "Drive the car, quickly send Yuqin to the hospital, calling an ambulance won't make it in time!" 7.6 Yuqin was sent to the hospital. Shen Hanfeng and Yun Duo arrived at this time, "What happened to Yuqin?" Shen Hanfeng asked anxiously. "She said her stomach hurt badly, I immediately picked her up and rushed her to the hospital," Yunhai said, sweating profusely. "Is she about to give birth?" Yun Duo asked. "Isn't it still two weeks until the due date?" Yunhai asked in confusion. "Xiaohai, did Yuqin receive any shock? That's why her stomach hurts?" Shen Hanfeng asked. "No, how could she receive a shock? Zhuo Benqi treated her well, and his subordinates took good care of her," Yunhai said, glancing at Zhuo Benqi sitting nearby. Zhuo Benqi stood up and said to Yunhai, "I'll check now to see if it's because of inadequate care from my subordinates." After finishing speaking, Zhuo Benqi ran out, yelling as he went, "Yunhai, call me if there's any news about Yuqin, don't forget!" Yunhai sighed watching his back. At this point, the doctor came out of the emergency room. Shen Hanfeng rushed over and asked anxiously, "Doctor, how is my daughter-in-law?" The doctor removed his mask and said, "The problem is serious!" Shen Hanfeng anxiously asked, "What's the problem? Is it difficult labor?" The doctor remained silent and said, "Actually, there's nothing seriously wrong with this young lady's body." Shen Hanfeng patted his chest, sighing in relief, and continued asking, "Then how about the baby?" The doctor said, "The baby is healthy. We just performed an ultrasound on this young lady, and the baby is fine." Yunhai asked, "Has the baby been born?" The doctor shook his head, "No, the mother just experienced mild contractions. These pre-labor contractions are normal." The doctor continued, "However, for unknown reasons, the baby's position is incorrect." Upon hearing "incorrect position," Shen Hanfeng was terrified and fainted. 7.7 Yuqin lay quietly on the hospital bed. At this time, Shen Hanfeng and Yun Duo went to the office to ask the doctor about Yuqin's condition, while Yunhai stayed by Yuqin's bedside. Yuqin slowly opened her eyes and tried to sit up, Yunhai supported her. "How is the baby?" Yuqin asked Yunhai as soon as she woke up. Yunhai hesitated and said, "Yuqin, I told you, don't be sad." Yuqin took a deep breath, "Tell me." Yunhai sorrowfully said, "The doctor said your baby's position is incorrect, so to safely deliver, you need a cesarean section." Upon hearing this, Yuqin was stunned, murmuring as if to herself, "This means whether I can successfully give birth depends on God's will? Is a cesarean section dangerous?" Yunhai tightly held Yuqin's hand and nodded. Tears streamed down Yuqin's face as she touched her belly and said, "Baby, mom is sorry, mom can't guarantee if I can successfully give birth to you, please forgive me, okay?" Hearing Yuqin's words, Yunhai's heart was instantly torn apart. He hugged Yuqin tightly and said, "Yuqin, it's fine, our baby will surely be born safely. Our baby is as strong as you, as determined as you, he will certainly be born safely!" Yunhai sobbed softly. At this moment, Shen Hanfeng walked in with red eyes. She smiled at Yuqin and said, "Yuqin, you're my daughter-in-law, and also my grandson's mom, please be strong, alright? For Yunhai, for the baby, and for me, be brave!" Shen Hanfeng continued, "For the next two weeks, you'll stay in the hospital, it's safer for you. I'll immediately call and buy the best medical equipment for the hospital, so you can relax." 7.8 Yuqin slowly walked along the small path in the hospital garden, her large belly making her steps slow. She had been gloomy for several days, pushing Yunhai away, wanting to walk alone. She couldn't figure out why, despite her efforts to take care of herself and the baby in her belly, things turned out this way. At this moment, Mother Chu approached her. "Yuqin, can I talk to you?" Mother Chu approached and said to Yuqin. "Mom, I just want to be alone right now," Yuqin refused. "I want to talk to you not about the child, but about your dad," Mother Chu said. "Dad?" Yuqin was surprised, "Mom, you haven't mentioned him to me since Dad passed away, why are you bringing him up today?" "Yuqin, I didn't bring him up before because I thought it wasn't the right time, but now I feel I can talk to you about your dad," Mother Chu said confidently. "Yuqin, your dad was originally a child from a wealthy family, spoiled from a young age, but your dad never acted arrogantly or bullied others. I met him in university; your dad was very smart, always ranking first in exams, and many girls liked him, including me. Back then, he and I were just ordinary friends, but gradually, we appreciated each other and fell in love. When it came time to discuss marriage, their family looked down on mine because we were poor. Back then, it was important to have equal social status. Your dad's parents didn't approve of me, but your dad insisted on marrying me, so he broke ties with his family, abandoned the fortune they gave him, handed all his assets to his younger brother, and then married me. Our life after marriage was very happy, although your dad's salary wasn't very high at the time, it was enough for our family of three to live comfortably, those were the happiest times for me. But good times didn't last long, your dad passed away, and I couldn't bear the burden of the family alone, so I had to ask your uncle for help." Mother Chu started crying as she spoke. Wiping away her tears, she continued, "Yuqin, actually, you are very much like your dad, strong, resilient, intelligent, always striving ahead, always getting top marks in exams. Sometimes when I see you, Yuqin, it's like seeing your dad, I'm proud to have a daughter like you." Yuqin silently listened to her mother's recounting, silently feeling her father's presence. Mother Chu took out a letter from her handbag and handed it to Yuqin, saying, "Yuqin, this is a letter your dad left me before he passed away, instructing me to give it to you when you have children. I originally planned to give it to you when my grandchild was born, but now I can't wait to give it to you." Yuqin opened the envelope. It was a very elegant piece of stationery, shaped like nettles. Beautiful steel pen handwriting flowed on the stationery. Yuqin: When you read this letter, you have become a mother, congratulations, you have finally grown up. Please forgive me for not being able to accompany you through your growth, I always hoped to see you grow up, but now it seems that I cannot. My condition has worsened. Yuqin, Dad wants to tell you, life has many things that we cannot predict, I hope that no matter what difficulties you encounter in the future, you can overcome them, Dad prays for you from heaven. Dad hopes you can be strong and brave, just like your name, just like nettles, growing strongly in any environment, shining your own light, I believe you can do it, because you are my daughter. Child, don't be sad about Dad's departure, because Dad will always be by your side, the blood flowing in your veins carries the same resilience as mine. Yuqin, keep going, Dad silently blesses you from heaven. Treat everyone around you well. Your Dad Yuqin read this letter, clear tears continuously rolling down her cheeks.
