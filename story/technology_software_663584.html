This period, industry insiders teach small tricks for using floor heating and remind readers that if they have installed floor heating at home, they must conduct environmental protection acceptance.

After the installation of floor heating is completed, the step of environmental protection acceptance cannot be omitted. Generally speaking, after the floor used in the floor heating system is heated, the release amount of formaldehyde will exceed the normal temperature state, and environmental protection acceptance can ensure that the release amount of free formaldehyde reaches the standard regulations.

When purchasing floor heating panels, consumers should first choose professional brands and products with good performance, buy from professional and capable regular companies, and not seek cheap deals from small manufacturers and mobile vendors. The floor heating industry has always had the saying of "three parts product, seven parts installation". Floor heating is a systematic project. From design planning, product selection, construction installation to after-sales service, it requires professional personnel to design and operate. If something goes wrong in any link, it will not only be difficult to repair but also affect the normal use of the entire heating system and reduce the service life of the entire system.

Secondly, try to use marble, tiles, or floors in combination. If choosing wooden floors, composite floors with better stability, lower moisture content, and smaller harmful substance release are preferred. They are less likely to deform when heated and are more environmentally friendly; the dimensions of the floor should be thin rather than thick, narrow rather than wide, which facilitates anti-deformation and heat conduction requirements; the cushion layer under the floor should not be too thick.

When using floor heating, remember three don'ts. First, avoid placing large areas of decorative items (such as carpets) on the floor as much as possible, so as not to reduce the effective heat dissipation area and weaken the thermal effect due to the flow of hot air; second, do not rush to turn on the floor heating system, it is best to increase the temperature by 1°C per hour. Keep the ground clean and dry before heating to prevent the floor from cracking and twisting due to rapid heating; third, do not hit, knock, or stab the ground with any sharp objects. The floor heating pipe laid under the ground is only about 3-4 centimeters away from the floor surface and can easily be damaged.

In addition, since the floor heating system belongs to low-temperature radiation heating, users should note that the water supply temperature should not exceed 60℃, and the working pressure of the heating system should not be too high, otherwise, it will not only cause discomfort in heating but also severely reduce the service life of the entire system.
