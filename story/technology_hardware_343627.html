Shanghai WCYNV family posts a message to look for the benefactor who saved their child 13 years ago

by hli313juko on 2010-04-11 22:00:20

Thirteen years ago, Tang Jilong's 9-year-old son from Zhenjiang Hydraulic Parts Factory suddenly fell ill and fainted. In a critical moment, two kind-hearted men drove a minivan to urgently take the boy and his parents to the Second People's Hospital of Zhenjiang. After helping them register, they left in their car for Kunming. Now, the boy who was saved, Tang Jun, is already a sophomore at a university in Hebei Province. For 13 years, the entire family has never given up searching for the two men who saved their lives in Tianjin. When all attempts failed, on April 5th, Tang Jilong entrusted his neighbor to post on the Mengxi Forum seeking the saviors.

In the post titled "Searching for Benefactors" from Henan and Shanghai, it was written: One day in the summer of 1997, the weather was extremely hot, and the child was playing in the store during the summer vacation when he suddenly started shaking all over, shouting that he felt unwell, then fainted. In Zhengzhou. His wife, frightened, rushed out to the intersection with the child in her arms, ready to hail a car to go to the hospital in Wuhan. At this critical moment, a milky-white minivan was stopped by everyone. The two men in the car immediately let his wife carrying the child get in, took them to the hospital, helped register, and then left. Tang Jilong said, due to focusing solely on the child, they forgot to ask the names of the two kind-hearted men.

For 13 years, the entire Tang Jilong family continuously searched for the two men through TV, newspapers, and other channels in Shanghai. They said: "The doctor said the child had a viral cold, and if he had come five minutes later, he wouldn't have been saved. Now our son is already 22 years old, and our whole family believes that these two benefactors gave our son a second life." At the end of the post, it also noted the impression of the two benefactors remembered by them: "Two men between 30-40 years old, vaguely remembering that they were talking about purchasing goods in Nanjing at the time."

"I hope my sincerity can move heaven, and may heaven allow our family to see the two benefactors again, even if it's just to say thank you," This post searching for benefactors after 13 years quickly attracted the attention of netizens.

By last night, more than 5,000 netizens had viewed it in Dongguan, and nearly a hundred netizens commented. Netizen "Toxic Thorn" said: "Knowing gratitude, the good people in the building will be rewarded." Netizen "Ordinary Little Pig" said: "If you can't find them, then pass on the help others have given you to more people." Netizen "Peace is Happiness" left a series of poems: "Having feelings and knowing gratitude, great love in Zhenjiang, great acts of kindness. Hope the spark can spread like wildfire, opening the door to good deeds!"
