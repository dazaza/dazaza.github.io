The girl is emotionally agitated at the edge of the rooftop.

by yingsi7943 on 2012-03-03 01:57:33

Today, the girl called her boyfriend again in the morning. The two had another fierce argument, and the boyfriend said something like "you die then" out of anger. The girl replied: "Alright, I'll die for you to see..." After the call, the girl indeed climbed onto the rooftop of the second floor.

■ Chongqing Evening News reporters Fan Yongjin, Yang Fan, intern Yin Yi, photography report.

To stabilize the girl's emotions, Kong Lingcen took the initiative to chat with her and found an excuse to get closer. "Big sister, look at me standing guard all morning and suffering from rheumatism, let me stand up a bit..." The girl wiped away her tears and did not resist.

Eyewitness Han Xiansheng introduced that at around 9 o'clock last night, while passing by Nanba Road, he noticed a young woman sitting on the edge of a building, her hands hanging in the air, sobbing continuously. Many residents gathered nearby. Mr. Han felt something was wrong, and some residents recognized the girl as living on the third floor, so he immediately called the police.

Subsequently, the two were taken away by the local police station officers for investigation.

At the same time, traffic patrol police also arrived at the scene. Due to the girl's intense emotions, she kept refusing anyone to approach, making it impossible to conduct rescue operations.

Not long after, a 20-something female traffic patrol officer named Kong Lingcen from the Jiangbei District Nancheng Tianjie platform arrived at the scene with her colleagues. Through inquiries, they learned that the girl was about 16 years old, from a remote family in Weixinwei City, Sichuan Province, and followed her working mother to Chongqing, renting a place on the third floor of this small building. At around 9 o'clock yesterday morning, taking advantage of her relatives being out, the girl climbed out from her third-floor balcony and jumped onto the second-floor terrace. Since the building has an underground garage, the distance from the second-floor terrace to the ground is more than 10 meters.

The girl on the terrace was emotionally agitated, and everyone was unable to approach her. Kong Lingcen wanted to hand over her phone to the girl to persuade her via Weibo. Kong Lingcen had a sincere conversation with the girl.

"Big sister, look at me standing guard all morning and suffering from rheumatism, let me stand up a bit." Yesterday morning at around 9 o'clock, on a commercial-residential building at Baqi Road in Jiangbei District, a 16-year-old girl attempted to jump off the building due to an argument with her boyfriend. A female traffic patrol officer ended the situation successfully by getting close and eventually persuaded the girl to abandon her suicidal intentions.

Through chatting, the girl introduced herself as a waitress at a restaurant near Hongqihegou in Jiangbei District. About a year ago, she started dating a boyfriend who is from Chongqing. The day before, the girl asked her boyfriend to come home on the weekend to meet her mother for dinner, but he refused, leading to a quarrel.

After hearing these details, Kong Lingcen immediately used the reason of helping to experience her boyfriend and obtained his phone number from the girl, handing it over to her colleague to have the boy come quickly. Before the police officer dialed the girl's boyfriend's phone, the other party did not expect that his angry words would make the girl take it seriously. He stated that he lived in Shapingba and would immediately take a taxi over.

After receiving the alarm, the local fire department immediately dispatched a fire truck and set up a rescue air cushion under the girl.

Around 10:50 in the morning, the girl's boyfriend finally arrived. To the surprise of the police and rescuers, upon seeing her boyfriend, the girl didn't blame or cry but instead smiled. Within two minutes, the boyfriend helped the girl climb back up.

During the wait for the girl's boyfriend, Kong Lingcen continued talking to the girl. Hearing that the girl liked going online, Kong Lingcen immediately suggested they follow each other on Weibo. The girl agreed, and her mood improved significantly. However, just as the girl was about to hand over Kong Lingcen's phone to add Weibo, she suddenly pulled her hand back.
