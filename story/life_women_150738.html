Several valuable advices from a woman to a divorced man

by longersing on 2009-05-18 23:41:12

Several Gu Baiyou warnings from women to divorced men stem from their lives filled with grievances. When they endure too much, these fears naturally arise, leading them to give warnings to men. Just like in the Gu Baiyou competition we participate in, many personal attacks come from grievances.

If your love relationship gives you more rights while placing more obligations on the other party, you should try to change that. Love follows economic principles; only a win-win situation can last. Now, let's listen to the Gu Baiyou warnings from divorced men!

Gu Baiyou Warning One:

Don't frequently test a man, and definitely don't use breaking up as a threat. If you often give him such hints, his subconscious will prepare for a breakup.

Gu Baiyou Warning Two:

Don't expand your rights endlessly just because a man loves you. Don't interfere with his ideals, beliefs, and pursuits. He must have some qualities you don't understand.

Gu Baiyou Warning Three:

Don't be habitually late. Don't assume that because he loves you, he should have unlimited patience. A person's patience has limits, and once it runs out, so does the love.

Gu Baiyou Warning Four:

Don't believe the phrase "If you love me, you should know what I'm thinking." This is pure nonsense. No one can fully know what the other is thinking. It's foolish to conclude that a man doesn't love you simply because he didn't promptly understand your thoughts.

Gu Baiyou Warning Five:

Don't constantly ask men to accompany you shopping. Few men truly enjoy shopping, and forcing them will eventually lead to resistance.

Gu Baiyou Warning Six:

What a man does for a girl during the heat of romance shouldn't be expected to continue indefinitely in everyday life. Smart girls usually cut expectations by half.

Gu Baiyou Warning Seven:

Don't try to change a man. Don't think he will become the ideal excellent male under your training. Adapting to him is wiser than trying to change him.

Gu Baiyou Warning Eight:

Don't be overly confident in your charm. Few men can permanently tolerate inconsistency. Few men can be summoned or dismissed at will, unless this man has ulterior motives for loving you.

Gu Baiyou Warning Nine:

Don't guide your love by looking for evidence in a man's words and actions that he doesn't love you. Men cannot always focus on women, nor can they notice every hint or dissatisfaction women express. When you look for dust with a magnifying glass, you will surely find it.

How much have you learned? Do you want to be a perfect Gu Baiyou man?
