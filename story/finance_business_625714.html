Guangzhou Lanbio o

by jgpe3fyum2 on 2011-12-15 16:39:46

As the Spring Festival is approaching, the festive atmosphere is getting thicker, people are getting busier, and the weather is getting colder! I am also getting more and more restless! The reason is that my family's Spring Festival Gala program is basically ready, but my Tibetan dance "Good Fortune Song" is still half-baked. What should I do? In order to make myself stand out in the family Spring Festival Gala and show my unique artistic charm and extraordinary artistic talent, I specially hired a dance tutor to refine each of my dance moves, aiming for a big success! To stand out like a crane among chickens and become famous overnight!

The tutor came, and during the learning process, I was full of anger inside. She was patient, repeating over and over again without tiring, but this dance teacher not only didn't encourage me, but also looked down on me, mocked me, and hurt me! She is the most annoying choreographer I've ever encountered in my dance career. Her way of mocking me is different from others; it sounds like praise but is actually sarcasm, seemingly harmless but deeply hurtful. "What kind of ethnic dance are you doing? It looks like Korean dance and Miao dance at the same time. You're even better than Yang Liping! One dance, multiple ethnicities!" She also said, "Your rhythm is either half a beat too fast or half a beat too slow. You're not suitable for group dances, but you're perfect for leading dances." Isn't that infuriating? It's unbearable! If I could dance well, would I still be in Jilin? I only hired you because I can't dance well. Why are you pretending? My creative dance posture is something ordinary people can't even achieve!

Once, I pleaded with her, "Choreographer, your Indian dance..." She glared at me, "With your dancing style, it will take ten years of practice just to get on stage!" Ha! A bucket of cold water poured over my head, who would still have the impulse to continue dancing? Unless my brain got hit by a door hinge, it will take another ten years before I can face an audience? How old would I be then? Is that even a reasonable statement? It's truly amusing and makes me laugh! Oh well, if I'm going to suffer insults, let it be. Who made me so clumsy? Swallowing my pride, I continued to dance! Dance as hard as I want, however I want, as long as my body doesn't fall apart, I'll keep dancing! Anyway, a dead pig isn't afraid of boiling water. Whatever ethnicity the dance belongs to, it's fine. Self-indulgence and self-appreciation! As long as I feel good about myself, it's okay! Let others talk while I dance my own dance! If it looks like anything, it's fine as long as it looks human!

This private tutor I hired is really difficult to please. She acts superior, just because she has a good figure and dances a bit better! What's so great about that? Not only does she look down on me, but she's also picky about food and drinks. I prepared meals for her, but she refused and insisted on ordering herself. And instead of ordering something nutritious, she chose tasteless things like pickled vegetables. Do you think my house is a restaurant? Acting like she's at home? Asking for whatever she wants? In such cold weather, where am I supposed to buy pickled vegetables? When I offered her apples, she said they were too hard to bite, so I squeezed juice, but she complained it was too sour. Then when I gave her radishes and onions, she complained they were too spicy. Pfft! Are big shots really above eating normal food? Well, I'll just deal with it and serve whatever! Reasonable or not, eat or don't eat!

After four days of teaching, I've learned pretty well. I'm exhausted, breathless, and insulted until I'm black and blue. Watching her dance so harmoniously and beautifully makes me angry, so I dismissed her! Fire her! Go do whatever you want! I might hire you again for the Dragon Year Spring Festival, hehe! By then, we'll see how I handle you! Dealing with you, I'm still very confident and have a clear plan! Just wait and see... you annoying tutor who makes me hold my breath and hurts my dignity.
