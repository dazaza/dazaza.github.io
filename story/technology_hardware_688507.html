Episode Summary:

Episode 1: Chinese magic with a history of thousands of years formed three major schools by the end of the Qing Dynasty: the Northern “Shen Family Colorful Magic,” “Mo Family Hand Magic,” and the Southern “Ghost Path Magic.” These three magic schools not only became famous in the world of magic, but also each master possessed a secret manual. When combined, these manuals form the coveted Chinese magic manual "The Divine Magic" that Japanese magicians covet.

Master Mo Er invites Shen Wan Kui, master of Shen Family Colorful Magic, to discuss safeguarding "The Divine Magic" from foreign bandits. Unexpectedly, Shen Wan Kui detects poison in the wine and mistakenly believes Mo Er wants to harm him to dominate "The Divine Magic," leading to his furious retaliation where he kills Mo Er on the spot! Coincidentally, Mo Er's six-year-old son Laibao, hiding inside a prop box, witnesses everything. In the chaos, Shen Wan Kui mistakenly cuts off Laibao's little finger...

Three years later, young Laibao and other street beggars survive on the streets when they accidentally intrude upon Shen Wan Kui’s street magic booth. Recognizing Shen Wan Kui as his father’s killer, Laibao slaps him instead of showing gratitude for being saved...

Eight years later, Laibao meets Situ Kun and Situ Jin, descendants of Ghost Path Magic who are seeking refuge with Shen Wan Kui. He helps Situ Jin escape Japanese pursuers, and feelings develop between them. Coinciding with Shen family’s recruitment, Laibao, who has missed the registration deadline, gets help from Situ Jin to successfully enroll. Laibao happily encounters Situ Jin again at a night market and meets Shen Wan Kui's daughter Xinping. Laibao contemplates his revenge plan...

Episode 2: Shen family holds a grand ceremony for new apprentices. Situ Jin gives Laibao a pinky ring to cover his severed finger, deeply moving him. The competition begins, and Laibao performs exceptionally well, but his severed finger shocks Shen Wan Kui, causing him great unease...

Wu滕 Zhang sends people to sneak into Shen's residence to steal "The Divine Magic," but they are fooled by Shen Wan Kui’s pre-planned magic tricks, stealing only a fake copy. Wu滕 Zhang is enraged.

When the results are announced, Shen Wan Kui lets Jin's nephew Qi San replace Laibao’s position, angering Laibao. Seeing Laibao's sorrow, Shen Xinping feels pity and cries. Laibao tightly embraces her.

Episode 3: Laibao uses a leg-cutting magic trick to scare away Zhang Ha Ma, successfully rescuing Qi San, who becomes very grateful. Laibao hints that Qi San owes him a favor...

Failing to steal "The Divine Magic," Wu滕 Zhang rents out the Tianxian Theatre where Shen Wan Kui performs, infuriating him. Shen Wan Kui performs for free at the Qingfeng Teahouse, confronting Wu滕 Zhang. Suddenly Lan Boss appears, performing to support Shen Wan Kui. Both artists admire each other's patriotism. Wu滕 Zhang bribes Jin to close down the Qingfeng Teahouse and forces Shen Wan Kui and Lan Boss to watch his performance. Laibao replaces Mao Tou, acting as a "human turning into a dog" in Wu滕 Zhang’s magic show. During the act, Laibao cleverly insults the Japanese audience, pleasing the national heart, earning Shen Wan Kui's respect. Unfortunately, Laibao is severely beaten by Wu滕 Zhang’s men and carried home by Shen Wan Kui to recuperate.

Episode 4: To save Laibao, Shen Wan Kui spends heavily on Jin’s ginseng but is extorted by Jin, demanding half of Shen’s performance fee. In desperation, Shen Wan Kui agrees, unaware of Jin and Wu滕 Zhang’s conspiracy. On the way home, Wu滕 Zhang disguises himself as an old woman to switch the ginseng and demands "The Divine Magic" in exchange.

Situ Jin worries about Laibao, crying beside his bed, which Shen Xinping overhears. She learns of Situ Jin's affection for Laibao. Shen Wan Kui uses Situ family heirlooms to pass off as "The Divine Magic" to exchange back the ginseng. Wu滕 Zhang cannot open the box and is furious.

Laibao survives, kneeling to ask Shen Wan Kui to take him as a disciple, but is politely refused. Angry, Laibao leaves. After narrowly escaping death, Mao Tou gifts Laibao a jade pendant lost by his long-separated sister, deepening their brotherhood. After Cao arrives in Tianjin to take office, he specifically requests to see Lan Boss's performance. Jin flatters Cao by evicting Shen Family Troupe from Tianxian Theatre to let Lan perform, greatly pleasing Lan. Laibao retrieves the lion head thrown away by Lan and returns it to Shen Wan Kui.

Episode 5: Finding the lion head again makes Shen Wan Kui extremely happy. Laibao kneels again, begging Shen Wan Kui to teach him magic. Shen Wan Kui agrees to let Laibao work around Shen’s house. Shen Family Magic secrets are not passed outside, so Laibao becomes more devoted to Shen Xinping.

Without a stage to perform, Shen Family Troupe loses spirit. Qi San recalls his past experience of being tricked by Lan Boss and conspires with Laibao to hire Zhang Ha Ma to destroy Lan’s voice. Cao and Jin come to reprimand Shen Wan Kui, who is deeply saddened by Lan’s plight and volunteers to replace Lan to perform in Beijing. Shen Wan Kui entrusts the Tianxian Theatre performances to Situ siblings, whose performance wins applause.

Episode 6: Laibao and Situ Jin grow closer, secretly pledging lifelong devotion. Situ Kun disapproves strongly, forbidding Situ Jin from seeing Laibao. To help Laibao gain fame quickly, Situ Jin secretly teaches him Ghost Path Magic, improving his skills rapidly. The popularity of Situ’s Ghost Path Magic pleases Shen Wan Kui. With Shen Wan Kui’s mother’s birthday approaching, everyone in Shen family prepares. Laibao takes this opportunity to learn writing from Shen Xinping. Laibao buys two fans, giving one each to Situ Jin and Shen Xinping, increasing their affections for him. On Shen grandmother’s birthday, Laibao leads a group of street children to celebrate, delighting the old lady. Shen Xinping accidentally drops the fan given by Laibao, seen by Baoqi and Situ Jin, leaving both feeling uneasy... Situ Jin confronts Laibao, while Shen Xinping defends Laibao, earning Situ Jin’s forgiveness.

Episode 7: Shen Wan Kui reluctantly agrees to perform in Baoding for financial reasons. Qi San meets Zhang Ha Ma on the street; during their drinking session, Zhang reveals his past abuse of Qiaolian. Drunk, Qi San tries to molest Qiaolian, who drives him out. Laibao consoles Qiaolian, discovering she has a jade pendant matching Mao Tou’s, reuniting the siblings. Qiaolian is grateful to Laibao. Sobering up, Qi San regrets his actions, kneeling to apologize to Qiaolian, who promises not to tell anyone. Qi San tells Laibao about Qiaolian’s ordeal, asking him to keep it secret. Shen Family Troupe’s carriage breaks down on the road, forcing them to return home early.

Laibao overhears that Xu Boss sent Shen Wan Kui to perform in Baoding to clear the stage for Situ siblings to perform Ghost Path Magic, which Situ firmly rejects. To sow discord between Situ Kun and Shen Wan Kui, Laibao deliberately tells Qi San this news, who immediately informs Shen Wan Kui. Xu Boss writes “Situ Ghost Path Magic Supreme in Tianjin” on the poster, inciting resentment among Shen Family Troupe apprentices towards Situ siblings. A quarrel ensues outside the theatre, ending unhappily.

Episode 8: Qi San stirs trouble with Shen Wan Kui, claiming Situ family’s Ghost Path Magic might aim to replace Shen Family Troupe’s status in Tianjin, making Shen Wan Kui ponder deeply... Situ Kun proposes marriage, wanting Baoqi to marry Situ Jin. Shen Wan Kui happily agrees to arrange the matchmaker, though Baoqi secretly likes Shen Xinping but cannot defy his teacher’s orders. Situ Jin resolutely opposes marrying Baoqi, leading to sibling disputes. Shen Family begins preparing for the wedding, while Situ Jin anxiously seeks ways to cancel it...

Situ Jin plans to elope with Laibao to England. Although Laibao likes her, he hesitates, unwilling to abandon his revenge plan. Shen Xinping discovers Situ Jin missing and reports it to Shen Wan Kui. Everyone searches for Situ Jin. Laibao sees Situ Jin waiting alone at the dock far away, heartbroken, ultimately not appearing. Situ Jin, disappointed, boards the ship to England alone.

Episode 9: Shen Wan Kui finds Situ Jin’s suitcase in Laibao’s room, repeatedly questioning Laibao about Situ Jin’s whereabouts, who claims ignorance. Angered, Shen Wan Kui expels Laibao. Situ Kun finds Situ Jin’s letter, learning she went to England to seek her uncle, regretting too late. Wu滕 Zhang poisons Shen Family to prevent them from rehearsing for their Beijing performance, buying large quantities of dysentery medicine and burning them, coincidentally seen by Laibao...

Cao and Jin suddenly visit Shen’s home to check the program prepared for Wang’s birthday banquet, finding Shen Family all sick. Cao becomes furious. Shen Family searches everywhere for dysentery medicine, discovering all have been bought out. Learning this is another scheme by Wu滕 Zhang, fortunately, an old doctor offers a folk remedy, sending the only healthy person, Boru, to buy it. Boru meets Laibao on the road and shares the situation, separating afterward. Boru accidentally falls into a pit, pleading for Laibao’s help, who hesitates and does not rescue him, all witnessed by Zhang Ha Ma, planning to blackmail Laibao. Laibao lies to get through. Laibao delivers the medicine to Shen’s home in the snow, earning Shen Wan Kui’s renewed respect.

Episode 10: Everyone in Shen’s home recovers after taking Laibao’s medicine. To silence Zhang Ha Ma, Laibao mentions his past abuse of Qiaolian to Mao Tou, who angrily kills Zhang Ha Ma and is arrested. Shen Wan Kui is forced to sign a military order guaranteeing the success of the performance to save Mao Tou from Jin. However, Shen Wan Kui knows the program ‘Six Children Paying Respect’ cannot proceed without Boru, potentially ruining the family. At this moment, Situ Kun suggests using Laibao to solve the urgent need. Shen Wan Kui suspects Laibao is Mo Er’s son but is deeply moved by his determination, deciding to take him as a disciple, replacing Boru’s position and starting intensive rehearsals with Shen Family Troupe for ‘Six Children Paying Respect.’ Boru remains trapped in the pit, calling out in vain. Finally, on Wang’s birthday banquet day, Boru struggles immensely to climb out of the pit, encountering several Japanese soldiers attempting to rape a civilian girl. He sacrifices his life to save her, killed by the Japanese. Meanwhile, the banquet performance is a great success, Shen Wan Kui delights, planning to take Laibao as a disciple. Baoqi and Situ Kun advise him to think carefully until Boru is found, overheard by Laibao. Laibao attempts to rescue Boru but discovers his corpse, deeply saddened. Boru’s body is eventually found, shocking everyone in Shen’s home. Shen Wan Kui discovers the murderer is Japanese.
