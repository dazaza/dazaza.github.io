Jinan equips with mini police cars, traffic police patrol in Mercedes-Benz Smart cars

by anonymous on 2012-02-12 16:20:43

Indoor Design: Jinan Equips Mini Police Cars, Traffic Police Patrol in Mercedes Smart, Southern Metropolitan Daily [Weibo] February 11, 2012, 10:17 AM (0 Comments) Font Size: T|T

Yesterday afternoon, the International Online website released a set of photos titled "Jinan Female Traffic Police Drive Mercedes Smart Police Cars for Road Patrols." According to the introduction, the Smart car in the photo "made its debut at the Jinan Shizhong Traffic Police Brigade's Jingqi-Wei’er Women's Detachment." The photos show that this "mini police car" is painted with the English word POLICE and the police emblem, equipped with police lights on the front, but it bears a civilian vehicle blue license plate "Lu A1006Y."

At the same time, multiple netizens also noticed this car on Weibo. Netizen "Anti-Corruption 007" said, "I passed by the Jingqi-Wei’er intersection after work and found a Smart police car parked beside the female traffic police detachment's post." Netizen "gg Smile Only Out of Politeness" also photographed a Smart police car with the license plate "Lu A1006Y," and remarked, "The Jinan traffic police are truly mighty and awe-inspiring; their police cars are directly upgraded to Mercedes Smart."

The reporter noted that on February 5, the Jinan Lixia District Traffic Police Brigade confirmed in response to netizen "Soaring Great Peng"'s doubts that each district traffic police brigade in the city was equipped with one Smart car, "for urban area patrols and rapid accident handling." It is understood that the Smart is a car series developed by the German automobile manufacturer Mercedes-Benz, with domestic automobile websites quoting prices ranging from approximately 115,000 to 225,000 yuan.

From the online feedback, these Smarts immediately sparked some netizens' doubts upon their debut. Netizen "Miao Zhu'er Her Dad" asked, "What exactly is the rationale for using the Smart as a police car? Is it because of its surround sound audio effect? Or does it make it easier to park?" Netizen "Who Snatched Yangjiao Jiao" stated, "Putting aside the Mercedes brand, just the maintenance and repair costs alone don't justify taxpayers' money, which doesn't fit the national conditions."

Southern Metropolitan Daily reporters found that previously, there were already netizens posting on the "Auto Home" website, claiming that their colleague's Smart blew a tire and damaged the wheel hub. When they took it back to the Mercedes-Benz 4S shop for repairs, they were told that "one wheel hub costs 6,000 yuan, one tire costs 2,000 yuan, and including the labor costs for repairs, the total cost is nearly 8,500 yuan." After conducting an on-site investigation of the maintenance and parts prices of Smarts in Shanghai and Beijing, they suggested "to have some mental preparation before purchasing."

Note: The original Chinese text mentions various types of flooring and home design elements, which seem unrelated to the main content about police cars. These elements have been excluded from the translation for clarity.
