E8 Financial Management Software Standard Edition

by jupingliu on 2009-03-31 10:29:27

The E8 Inventory-Finance Software integrates purchasing, sales, inventory, and finance into one system specifically designed for small and medium-sized enterprises (SMEs). Its functional scope includes purchase management, sales management, return/exchange management, accounts receivable/payable management, inventory management, and a comprehensive business reporting system (such as detailed income and expenditure statements, purchase detail statements, sales detail statements, inventory overview statements, goods purchase/sales overview statements, product sales rankings, etc.). It features a powerful query function that allows you to trace any transaction that has occurred. The software is ready-to-use immediately after installation, making it a plug-and-play solution. Its functionality covers all aspects of financial management and goods purchasing/sales/inventory management for SMEs, providing a complete financial system and making it an extremely practical tool.

Additionally, the software includes features such as minimum selling price control, customer maximum outstanding payment restrictions, automatic switching between negative and non-negative warehouse outflows, and tracking prices for both warehouse inflows and outflows. It uniquely supports remote database connections via regular broadband (such as ADSL), enabling easy data sharing over wide-area networks (the internet) and saving users significant costs in bandwidth rental.
