Synopsis:

**First Part:**

Jason Bourne (Matt Damon), is rescued from the sea in Italy with no memory of who he is, except for a Swiss bank account number on his hip, which is his only clue to prove his identity.

Jason finds a large amount of cash, six passports, and a gun at a Swiss bank. He also discovers his exceptional skills in combat, firearms, and languages, leading him to investigate his true identity. In the process, he involves an unfamiliar woman, Marie (Franka Potente). Together, they travel from Switzerland to Paris, pursued by a mysterious group of assassins, facing numerous dangers.

Can Jason escape these threats and uncover his real identity? Will he have the courage to face the truth?

**Second Part:**

After the bloody events of the first part, Bourne (Matt Damon) and his girlfriend Marie (Franka Potente) retreat to a secluded seaside town in India, hoping to forget their past lives. One day, a killer from the mysterious organization arrives, shattering the tranquility of Bourne and Marie’s life. During their escape, Marie is killed.

Determined to uncover the truth, Bourne resumes his fight. He learns that the CIA, where he once worked, has started searching for him again. He finds himself implicated in an assassination plot, accused of disrupting a CIA intelligence deal and killing agents. With no other choice, Bourne must find the person impersonating him to clear his name and avenge his girlfriend.

**Third Part:**

Having lost the woman he loved, Bourne (Matt Damon) faces even more challenging days. Despite his efforts to recall, he can only be awakened by fragmented flashbacks in his dreams, still unable to remember his true identity.

But Bourne is destined not to live an ordinary life. Before he can calm down and "rediscover" his true self, CIA assassins are sent after him again. After several intense confrontations, Jason Bourne is finally pushed to the brink, deciding to strike back—actively pursuing the killers through Moscow, Madrid, Paris, London, and Tangier.

As one problem after another is resolved, Jason returns to New York, where he will find all the answers and reclaim his identity.
