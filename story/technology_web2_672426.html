This might be one of the toys with the fewest movable joints in the Takara Tomy Chogokin Die-Cast series. In the official photos released on the Takara Tomy Chogokin Die-Cast tokusatsu series website, Pixar's animated Cars character Lightning McQueen is shown with a configuration of seven movable joints. Additionally, both front wheels are equipped with Takara Tomy die-cast joints, allowing McQueen to perform actions such as high-speed drifts or jumping off the road, which are quite interesting. The eye and mouth parts can be swapped out, and the stickers on McQueen's body accurately replicate his appearance from the animation. It is scheduled for release on January 15, 2012, with a body length of approximately 8 cm and a price of around 2,400 yen.
