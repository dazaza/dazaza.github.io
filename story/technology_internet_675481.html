Industrial design, 3D design, mold design, Quanzhou Gao Li high-salary class. What industry has the widest range of uses: molds. As long as there is production and life involving products, molds are indispensable, from small daily necessities like mobile phones to cars and airplanes; all are results of mold production...

What profession becomes more valuable with time, more valuable with age, and at the same time earns societal respect and dignity? Design.

What industry has the widest application, convenient employment, and a good income: industrial design, mold design, CNC technology, shoe mold 2D, 3D design...

In the Quanzhou area, which vocational education institution produces the best quality graduates? Graduates from the Geometric Design Technology Center already make up over 65% of the design and technical personnel in the Quanzhou region... The Quanzhou Geometric Design Center was jointly established by the Geometric Design Office and the Dacheng Mold Company.

Unlike regular vocational schools or training classes set up by ordinary factories, this center is a product research and development platform for enterprises in the Quanzhou area, covering various light industries such as electronics, electrical appliances, toys, daily necessities, and communications. It provides industrial design technical services to enterprises, including product development, mold design, 3D laser scanning, CNC processing, and advertising design. After many years of cooperation with enterprises, its design capabilities have been recognized. Since 2005, it has accepted enterprise commissions for pre-job training and advanced training for design technicians. After the new Labor Law was introduced in 2008, our center became a technical center for industrial design in the Quanzhou area. Every year, the design personnel we send out receive positive reviews. Currently, in the Quanzhou area, personnel in industrial product design [http://www.gotook.net/2010/deign.html], mold design [http://www.gotook.net/2010/modl.html], packaging and advertising design [http://www.gotook.net/2010/ping.html], and CNC technology [http://www.gotook.net/2010/cnc.html] account for more than 40% of the industry's workforce. We collaborate with nearly 5000 enterprises, ensuring that students who complete their courses according to the schedule receive corresponding graduation certificates, can participate in relevant professional certifications, obtain corresponding certificates, and get recommended jobs.

Since China is a world production powerhouse and is likely to become a major player in product design and R&D, as well as a major exporter of technology, we offer specialized programs in product design for product research and development, and mold design and manufacturing [http://www.gotook.net/2010/modl.html] and CNC processing technology [http://www.gotook.net/2010/cnc.html] needed for mass production. In nearly ten years of enterprise talent collaboration, our students have received praise from employers both in terms of technical skills and overall quality. To meet the needs of enterprise development, we have added programs in product packaging design [http://www.gotook.net/2010/ping.html] and online marketing [http://www.gotook.net/2010/shop.html].
