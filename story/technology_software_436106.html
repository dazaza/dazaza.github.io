On December 26th, her condition suddenly worsened, and all her internal organs showed signs of failure. The doctor announced: Li Zhong might not be saved, she might have only two hours to two days left. In the hospital corridor, more than ten people were crying uncontrollably. Zhong Tingjun, taking antihypertensive drugs, insisted on her decision: "I will not give up treatment, I believe in Li Zhong!" In the hospital, everyone stayed awake for a day and night, and Li Zhong temporarily got through the danger. When Li Zhong was six years old, her father had a brain tumor and underwent four major surgeries in ten years. Her mother went through great pain and pressure. But when she was fifteen, her father still passed away, leaving her and her mother to rely on each other. From that day on, they only had each other in their eyes. Zhong Tingjun devoted all her energy to her daughter, spoiling her as much as possible, giving her money every year to travel. Zhong Tingjun is a middle school English teacher, beautiful and elegant. Although she is middle-aged, she still has many admirers around her. But in order not to trouble her daughter, she never remarried. Li Zhong was very obedient and always thought about her mother. Every time she went somewhere, she prepared gifts for her mother and encouraged her mother to remarry. But Zhong Tingjun was very determined. Helpless Li Zhong made an agreement with her mother: "I will take care of you for life."

The girl with severe pneumonia fights death every day

In 2007, Li Zhong traveled to Yunnan and met Zhao Xiang by chance, becoming a couple. Long-distance love always troubled them. Last year, in order to accompany his girlfriend, Zhao Xiang quit his good job in Kunming and worked at a small advertising company in Chengdu. Later, Li Zhong followed Zhao Xiang to live in Kunming for several months. Since both of them couldn't bear to leave their parents, they never decided where to settle down. Finally, they decided that whoever found a stable job locally, the other would unconditionally move with their family to get married.

Li Zhong's illness before

For more than 50 days while her daughter was seriously ill, her mother kept vigil outside the intensive care unit 24 hours a day

Li Zhong usually loved climbing mountains and taekwondo. She didn't expect that a minor cold almost cost her her life. On November 26th last year, Li Zhong caught a cold and after three days of intravenous fluids at the hospital, she saw no improvement and kept coughing. On December 1st, her mother, Zhong Tingjun, took her to Huaxi Hospital for examination. Suddenly, her breathing became difficult, and she was immediately sent for emergency treatment. The CT scan results showed deep lung infection, with only one-fifth of her lungs functioning normally. Multiple indicators were below normal levels, severely affecting multiple functions of her body, and her organs were at risk of failure. The doctor told Zhong Tingjun: "Li Zhong's chances are very slim, you need to take care of your own health." This sentence plunged Zhong Tingjun into despair, her daughter was her life's support. She knelt on the ground and cried uncontrollably: "Doctor, please save her, if she's gone, I won't live either."

"Only this position allows me to let her feel the spiritual connection between mother and daughter." On January 22nd, the doctor announced that Li Zhong had passed the critical period, and Zhong Tingjun finally breathed a sigh of relief. She returned home for the first time, lay in bed, and cried for over an hour. Once her spirit relaxed, she immediately fell ill.

In Zhao Xiang's eyes, Li Zhong was "baby," even though she had shaved her head, he still found her adorable. He said to Li Zhong: "In a few days, I'll also shave my head and grow hair with you." Every day during visiting hours, he rushed in quickly. Although Li Zhong couldn't speak, watching her blink her eyes and stick out her tongue, every small action was recorded in his blog.

"Our precious baby's hair is starting to grow back, her cute appearance makes me reluctant to leave." This was written in the blog on the 18th. Since Li Zhong was hospitalized, Zhao Xiang had taken a long leave, waiting in the hospital every day, applying moisturizing lotion to Li Zhong daily, making her happy, and recording everything in his blog. Everything he did moved Li Zhong's family. Although Li Zhong had escaped the danger, this serious illness would definitely have a great impact on her body. Before the health assessment came out, the family thought Zhao Xiang should have the choice. Zhong Tingjun said to him: "You're a good kid, we remember everything you've done for her. We don't want to put pressure on you, but you have the freedom to choose. If your family disapproves, don't force yourself. Whether or not you choose Li Zhong, you're our family." Deeply in love with his girlfriend, Zhao Xiang was unwilling to give up easily, "This is between me and Li Zhong, we'll decide together once she's discharged."

In the ward, Li Zhong continued to persevere. She held her mother's hand and mouthed, "Thank you."

Li Zhong endured unimaginable pain that ordinary people could hardly endure. If it weren't for her family's support, she would have almost given up. Once, after getting through a crisis, she signaled her boyfriend Zhao Xiang to call her uncle. With foam coming out of her mouth, she painfully told her uncle, "Please take care of my mom for me." Her uncle was heartbroken, "Child, do you not want to keep going?" She shook her head, "It hurts too much." She wanted to give up, but she couldn't let go of her mom.

Li Zhong had been in the ICU for nearly two months, with lost lung function and organ failure. The doctors issued multiple critical notices, and once even informed the family that she wouldn't survive for more than two hours. In despair and anxiety, Li Zhong's family never gave up, waiting outside the ward for over fifty days and nights. The corridor outside the ICU became their temporary home, sleeping on benches and the floor every night, silently cheering for Li Zhong. Finally, Li Zhong escaped life-threatening danger. Behind her, her boyfriend persisted in writing her hospital diary on his blog every day.

Since Li Zhong was hospitalized, Zhong Tingjun chose the closest spot to the ICU ward in the corridor, sleeping on a bench every night.

Yesterday, at the intensive care unit of Sichuan University's West China Hospital, 24-year-old girl Li Zhong, with a shaved head and big eyes, still communicated with her mother through a respirator due to lung function loss. For nearly two months, everyone communicated through her lip movements. When her lip movements were unclear, her mother would bring out a notebook for her to write. "I want to drink chicken soup," looking at her crooked handwriting, her mother smiled. "Little glutton, I'll ask the doctor if you can eat it." Her mother held her hand tenderly, rubbing it, tears welling up in her eyes.

Since that day, Li Zhong had been staying in the ICU, with her mother, boyfriend, friends, and other relatives keeping vigil outside the ward.
