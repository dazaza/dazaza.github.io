Security electric baton: Huang Jianxiang VS Zhou Liang, who is the master and who is the apprentice?

by anonymous on 2012-02-14 20:42:33

At around 0:00 am Beijing time on November 5, the long-awaited Jianxiang Huang finally returned to the seat of commentator. Although this time he just appeared in the role of a "guest host" for the Setanta Sports Football Channel, it's not difficult to see from the fierce promotion by the media before the game and the importance of the Juventus VS Inter Milan match that "Little Huang" is stepping back onto his old path. Since the well-known "commentary scandal", Little Huang has become an entertainment figure who has been busy writing books, making guest appearances, recording singles, and acting as a judge - but no longer doing commentary work. After taking up the "Daily Sports Meeting" program on Phoenix TV, Little Huang seemed to be transitioning towards becoming a sports talk show host. However, due to the limited coverage of the TV station, fewer people have been able to see this program, and more fans are still hoping that he will return to the ranks of commentators.

Now, this Italian Derby has given Little Huang such an opportunity. By his side, his apprentice Liang Zhou respectfully accompanied him as the commentator for Setanta Sports. The match was delayed by 15 minutes due to traffic congestion involving the Inter Milan team bus, during which time Zhou kept praising Little Huang, calling himself just a "newcomer to the French team" while comparing Little Huang to Zidane. Although Little Huang modestly denied it, his words revealed the pride of being a master.

Unfortunately, after being away from the battlefield for so long, Little Huang did not bring more authoritative commentary. He excused himself by saying he couldn't watch Serie A matches, and multiple times raised some basic questions about players. As a result, Zhou had to explain in detail, like teaching a primary school student, various aspects about some Juventus players, such as who Morinari is and what Nocerino's characteristics are. As professional sports news workers, one must do their homework before commentating on a match. This last-minute cramming approach really makes it seem like Little Huang has been too much into entertainment. Looking at other guests on Setanta Sports, such as Yuankui Li and Xirong Chen, they have a comprehensive understanding of every player on the field. Even Yang Xu from CCTV-5 clearly did sufficient background research before the match. If Little Huang is simply too big-headed and forgets what a host should do, then it is indeed laughable.

Subsequently, Little Huang's commentary skills were evident; his analysis of the situation on both sides and grasp of the direction of the game process were very accurate, showing his experienced side. Perhaps this is a good-sounding commentary, but Little Huang's casual attitude towards the match is regrettable. If he truly becomes a member of Setanta Sports eventually, it is hoped that he can put down his airs and become a truly professional commentator. In front of him, it's not just his apprentice Liang Zhou, but also Dong Su, who is proficient in the Premier League - whose preparation is quite excellent.
