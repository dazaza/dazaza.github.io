"Hardware spraying" joins hands with Rubo platform to achieve the future.

by robooseo514 on 2012-03-02 17:10:52

In the past year, whether it is the high-profile entry into the mobile Internet industry or the explosive development of smart phones, all are proving the charm of mobile Internet and predicting the future market pattern of the Internet. Under the influence of various factors such as market, prospect, opportunity and challenge, enterprises in and out of the industry, even individuals, have focused their attention on the mobile Internet. They unanimously agree that the main focus of the Internet market will be on the mobile Internet.

The Roboo company located in Suzhou Industrial Park can be said to be prescient. As early as 2006, under the leadership of its CEO Dr. Han Song, it gathered a large number of high-tech workers to establish Roboo company and dedicated to the development of mobile search engines. In 2009, when the national 3G license was issued, Roboo took the lead in launching mobile top-advertising services and made great strides into market operations, serving a wide range of mobile phone users with a series of application businesses such as mobile top-advertising, mobile simulators, and mobile searches. Roboo's mobile top-advertising business has been sold by dealers cooperating with Roboo since its establishment, with each dealer responsible for their own region, providing mobile top-advertising sales and services.

In June 2012, a Roboo partner in Guangdong made contact with Mr. Li during a top-advertising sale, and both parties reached an agreement in early July, signing a five-year Roboo mobile top-advertising contract, registering the keyword "Hardware Spraying". Afterwards, Mr. Li again registered the Roboo top-advertising keyword "Hainan Tourism Network". Mr. Li is engaged in hardware spraying business, with the mobile top-advertising keyword mainly being the 3G website "Hardware Spraying", which serves as the company's external publicity portal and business platform. The 3G website "Hainan Tourism Network" is an investment method for Mr. Li in the mobile Internet. Facing the booming development of the mobile phone market, Mr. Li believes that the mobile Internet will eventually exceed the scale of computer-based Internet and obtain the greatest commercial value through continuous marketization. Mr. Li sees potential in the mobile Internet, believing it not only has the aspect of developing the company's business but also offers effective investment opportunities, which must be seized and utilized.

The 3G website "Hardware Spraying" mainly includes eight sections: member management, process introduction, news updates, industry products, industry information, advertising space rental, supply and demand information, and contact us. The 3G website comprehensively and clearly introduces the industry dynamics of hardware spraying and the specific business of the company, along with contact information so customers in need can directly contact the owner. Under the impetus of the mobile Internet, "Hardware Spraying" breaks away from traditional sales models, accompanied by technological progress, pioneering new promotion and marketing strategies combining physical enterprises with Internet websites. Mr. Li said that he had been operating this hardware spraying company for several years, and during these years, his clients were mostly familiar customers around him, limiting his sales volume. In 2011, Mr. Li noticed that mobile Internet access was becoming increasingly common, with everyone having a mobile phone and being able to surf the web anytime and anywhere. When Mr. Li used his mobile phone to surf the web and saw some advertisements for clothing on websites, he conceived the idea of publishing information about his hardware spraying business on mobile websites. In June 2011, when a Roboo partner contacted Mr. Li, he felt that an opportunity had come. After the staff of the Roboo partner explained all the situations to Mr. Li, he decided to register the Roboo top-advertising keyword "Hardware Spraying". Since its official operation began in July 2011, the "Hardware Spraying" 3G website has been registered for half a year. During this time, under the joint maintenance and continuous optimization promotion by Roboo staff and Mr. Li, the "Hardware Spraying" 3G website has gained a relatively high user browsing volume and ranks at the top of the Roboo mobile top-advertising hot word recommendation column. Such achievements not only make more people know and understand Mr. Li's hardware spraying business, but also bring him a certain amount of customers and profits.

As the mobile Internet sweeps across the Internet world, it also influences the development pattern of the commercial market. More and more enterprises and individuals prefer to place advertisements on mobile websites, for reasons such as low cost of mobile website promotion, audience exceeding hundreds of millions, and the potential to become the future Internet. Roboo comes at the right time, pushing its scientific and technological achievements of many years to the forefront of marketization, leading numerous enterprises to enter the mobile Internet market, seizing opportunities, and advancing against difficulties under new challenges. And the "Hardware Spraying" 3G website registered by Mr. Li will gain the greatest improvement under the influence of the mobile Internet market, promoting the leapfrogging of the company's own business.
