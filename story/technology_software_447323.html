On the 18th, the pre-release version of "Myth" will be launched to warm up for the official release, introducing a brand new ten-thousand-player dual-line server. Core message from 52pk.

by xinsdyn56 on 2011-06-16 23:27:27

The large-scale 3D online game "Myth" will welcome its open beta version in the scorching month of July. Before this, to allow a wide range of players to experience the content related to the open beta in advance, "Myth" will open a ten-thousand-player dual-line server on June 18th (Saturday) at 2:00 PM. At that time, the vast number of players can log into this server in advance to experience some of the content of the "Myth" open beta and feel the revolutionary task system. With an abundance of story-based tasks and perfect visual experiences, more fresh gameplay awaits in the open beta pre-heating version!

Server opening situation:

June 18th (Saturday) 14:00 New server opens:

Reappearance of the Ghostly Beauty, personally experience the love between man and ghost.

In the new version, the task system will undergo significant changes, and players will experience unprecedented task modes. The novice tasks are tightly connected with complete stories, allowing players to continuously experience one climax after another brought by the new mode! Players can help Chenxiang split the mountain to save his mother, meet Lady Xiaojian alongside Ning Caishen, join Yan Chixia in defeating the Black Mountain Old Demon, or witness the touching love story of Niulang and Zhinv. More timeless legends await for players to discover and personally experience.

Truly stunning visual graphics

Chenxiang's divine axe used to split the mountain and save his mother must be obtained through a mini-game.

An abundance of exciting activities will offer limited-time props such as multi-person mounts, a large amount of yuanbao, and gems to a wide range of players. Players can log into the event page from June 13th to June 17th, click to participate, and easily join the pre-registration gift-giving event. Both new and old players can participate and obtain limited-time props to equip themselves. Such luxurious gifts, hurry up and join now!

Event One: Free Six-Person Mount

Players can register from June 13th to June 17th to experience the preheating version of the open beta. After the preheating version of the open beta is launched, all successfully registered players will have the opportunity to obtain the limited six-person mount, Long! Players can log into the preheating version experience server from June 18th to July 8th to claim their prizes.

Event Two: 500 Yuanbao for Everyone

Successfully registered players can log into the experience server after the preheating version of the open beta is launched. Once they reach level 30, they can receive 500 yuanbao tickets. This upgrade and send yuanbao activity allows players to easily experience green online games! Players can log into the preheating version experience server from June 18th to July 8th to claim their prizes.

Event Three: Upgrade and Send Gems

Successfully registered players can log into the experience server after the preheating version of the open beta is launched. Once they reach levels 40 and 50, they can receive level 3 gem exchange coupons gifted by the game. Players can use these exchange coupons to obtain level 3 gems and easily equip their gear. Players can log into the preheating version experience server from June 18th to July 8th to claim their prizes.

Event Four: True Exchange, Win Gems Happily

Event Content:

From June 18th to June 26th, 2011, each character who makes their first top-up of 120 points at the Luoyang Silver Bank NPC God of Wealth and exchanges it for 240 golden tickets will receive two level 3 gem exchange cards. These level 3 gem exchange cards can be used to exchange for various bound level 3 gems at the Luoyang Money Shop Rabbit.

Event Time:

From June 18th to June 28th, 2011, 23:59

Event Reward:

Two level 3 gem exchange cards (bound)

Precautions:

(1) The characters participating in the event must make their first exchange of golden tickets at the Luoyang God of Wealth and the quantity must be 240 golden tickets.

(2) Each character can only participate once in this event.

(3) Ensure there are at least two empty slots in your item bag before exchanging. If the conditions are met but the item bag lacks space, the exchange will fail, and you will not lose the eligibility for this event.

Event Five: Apex Showdown, Gifts Await!

Event Content:

1. When the event ends, the top 10 players in terms of level, wealth, and mental methods rankings will each receive 2000 yuanbao tickets!

2. The top player in the level, wealth, and mental methods rankings will additionally receive a special mount.

Event Time:

From June 18th to June 25th, 2011, 23:59

Event Rewards:

Yuanbao ticket grand prize, special mount

Precautions:

1. The rewards for level ranking, wealth ranking, and mental method ranking do not conflict with each other.

2. The award evaluation will be based on the official system ranking data at the end of the event.

3. The rewards will be distributed within ten working days after the event ends. For details, please pay attention to the award announcement.

Event Six: I'm the Best at Life Skills

Event Content:

At the end of the event, the top 10 players in terms of proficiency in the five major life skills—herbal gathering, mining, fishing, farming, and cooking—will each receive a reward of 2000 yuanbao tickets!

2. The player ranked first in the proficiency of the five major life skills—herbal gathering, mining, fishing, farming, and cooking—will additionally receive a reward of 2000 yuanbao tickets!

Event Time:

From June 18th to June 25th, 2011, 23:59

Event Rewards:

Yuanbao ticket grand prize

Precautions:

1. The rankings for each life skill will be based on the final query results announced officially.

2. The event rankings primarily focus on the five major life skills—herbal gathering, mining, fishing, farming, and cooking. Other life skills do not participate in this event's rankings.

After the event ends, the list of winning players and the method of reward distribution will be announced in the official award announcement.
