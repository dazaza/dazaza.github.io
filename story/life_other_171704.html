Company Profile:

CONILE products are researched, designed and manufactured by Guangdong Zhongshan Oubao CONILE Furniture Co., Ltd. Oubao CONILE is jointly invested by the Italian famous brand Oubao Furniture (abbreviation: OBOO) and Guangdong Zhongshan CONILE Company.

The China headquarters is located in Zhongshan City, Guangdong Province. The main CONILE products include: overall kitchen cabinets, wardrobes, sliding doors, etc. It has participated in the Cologne Furniture Fair in Germany several times. The company's designers have absorbed the most advanced design concepts in Europe and combined them with traditional Chinese cultural elements to design products that fully meet the Chinese people's preference for fashion, practicality, multifunctionality, etc.

CONILE has successively established production bases in Zhongshan, Beijing, Shanghai, Xian, etc. On the basis of optimal product quality, it maximizes the saving of product costs.

Custom-made integrated furniture makes the fullest use of house space. CONILE’s high-quality home products made through standardized industrial production lines can ensure a perfect overall effect and excellent quality while maximizing the use of house space.

Healthy and Environmentally Friendly

CONILE's integrated wardrobes and cabinets are all made from 100% natural small-diameter original wood pulp, natural resin glue, linseed oil, etc., to produce truly healthy E1-level environmentally friendly home products.

CONILE Integrated Home uses very little paint process, fundamentally eliminating defects such as surface cracking, aging, wear resistance, and environmental unfriendliness.

Excellent Quality

Unique T-shaped back dragon bone design ensures that the cabinet body does not deform under load.

Modular design, wardrobe versatility changes as you wish. CONILE wardrobes adopt modular design, which can freely adjust shelves and clothes hangers under the premise of ensuring product quality optimization. They can move up and down, disassemble and assemble at will. Drawers, trouser racks, tie grids, full-length mirrors and other functional components can be placed in any module according to human needs. Truly personalized smart home.

Mildew-proof and breathable, let clothes be stored safely. CONILE designers have specially added a 5mm ventilation seam between the inner layers of the cabinet, allowing the air inside the wardrobe to micro-circulate, ensuring the dryness and breathability of the wardrobe to the greatest extent, keeping clothes odor-free and mold-free.

Quality hardware, confidence booster. All CONILE home products use high-quality stainless steel or aluminum alloy connectors that never rust, greatly increasing the service life of the product under the premise that hardware parts do not rust.

Fifteen-year product quality guarantee. CONILE integrated wardrobes and cabinets provide a fifteen-year quality warranty, so you can use them with peace of mind.
