Hong Kong and Taiwan dramas >> Turns out I'm Not Handsome

by qvodfans on 2009-07-19 08:31:44

Since he could remember, Xiaozhuang had always thought of himself as a handsome guy. This belief was not entirely baseless. From childhood to adulthood, whether it be relatives, the auntie at the food stall, the pretty girls filling out questionnaires, the middle-aged women selling scalped tickets, or classmates borrowing notes, everyone always greeted him with admiration in their eyes. Therefore, Xiaozhuang firmly believed that he was indeed a handsome guy...

It was his two classmates and best friends who woke Xiaozhuang from this "handsome man" dream. "Sisi", a quirky roommate full of strange ideas and humor, was the vice president of the Pigeon Racing Club. Sisi claimed that besides studying pigeon racing psychology, the pigeon "Old Wang" was his only kindred spirit! "Akon", a self-proclaimed playboy and smooth talker, was the founder and president of the Pigeon Racing Research Society. Having experienced a few romances, Akon loved to act as Xiaozhuang's love counselor. However, what drove Xiaozhuang crazy was that following Akon's advice never seemed to work!

In fact, Xiaozhuang's connection with "Cindy" went way back, even before they entered college. Once, he had an (unfortunate) encounter with Cindy. Surprisingly, for Cindy's sake, Xiaozhuang even joined the Foodie Club. Mustering all his courage, Xiaozhuang confessed his feelings to Cindy, only to see her coolly reply with a simple "No." Feeling as if he had been shot in the chest, Xiaozhuang struggled to rise from the metaphorical blood pool and asked why. Cindy looked at Xiaozhuang and said, "Because you're too ugly." In that instant, Xiaozhuang felt as if he had been shot again...

The complicated relationship between Cindy and Xiaozhuang became even more tangled when the beautiful Qiaoping entered the picture, forming a love triangle. With SiSi already dating the quirky girl "Pinxuan", he no longer had time to deal with Xiaozhuang’s affairs. Meanwhile, Akon faced the toughest love triangle of his life and couldn’t help much anyway. To make matters worse, Akon also had to deal with the clingy "Manman." Thus, these three brothers-in-woes wove a super romantic campus comedy filled with laughter through tears, and exhaustion mixed with joy.
