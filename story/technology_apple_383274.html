Must-read before purchasing: Hands-on with the iPad2 and usage experience

by gadgetoflife on 2011-03-03 22:48:48

According to Engadget, after the launch they had a chance to try out the iPad2 and brought us a hands-on video. They said that the first feeling of using iPad2 is that it's very fast, saying "it's like having TURBO installed". From the video, we can see that when flipping pages or scrolling through web pages on iPad2, there is basically no lag and the response speed is extremely fast. They also mentioned that even with the same software, it runs much faster on the second generation, such as Safari which is very noticeable. However, currently everyone still doesn't know how much memory the iPad2 has. In addition, although the screen resolution hasn't been upgraded, they think the game graphics look pretty good. Since iPad2 is thinner and lighter, it feels more comfortable to hold compared to the first generation, giving you more of a magazine reading experience. It's hard to imagine that Apple could make it this thin.
