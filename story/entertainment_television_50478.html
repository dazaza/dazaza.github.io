So he truly believed he was handsome... Until two classmates and best friends woke him up from this handsome dream: "Si Si", a roommate full of odd ideas, brimming with humor as the vice president of the pigeon racing society, whose only soulmate is the pigeon "Old Wang". With Cindy and Zhuang's complicated relationship, the addition of the beautiful "Qiaoping" forms a triangular equation. This leaves Si Si, already dating the quirky girl "Pinxuan", no time to care about Zhuang, and A Kang facing the hardest love triangle of his life. All three simultaneously fall into a romantic dilemma, weaving a super romantic campus love comedy filled with laughter and tears, exhaustion and joy.
