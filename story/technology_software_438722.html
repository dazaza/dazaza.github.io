Dialogue Design: Who did I offend? Why am I the first to be banned?! Influence Index:

All advertisements she endorsed were pulled down, and she was given instructions: no promotion, no showing off! Although SARFT did not provide any reason, Tang Wei's "death" seems somewhat unfair and "pitiful." Although "Lust, Caution" caused significant impact, it shouldn't have come to such extremes.
