My heart laughs because of you.

by pex9wew8n on 2011-08-08 15:41:43

Those who mention the vow of the end of time, exactly is how lusty.

Those who speak of eternal vows, just how resolute are they?

--------------------------------------------

Tired of life. Everyone says it's exhausting to be a person, and I feel this way too.

You have to wear a wig, put on a mask, wear glasses, and force a smile. Put on your underwear, then your clothes, and finally your coat.

Put on your underpants, then your pants, and fasten your belt. Put on your socks, then your shoes, and tie your shoelaces.

Every day is like this, until you go to heaven.

I like having lots of friends. I really enjoy having many friends. Friends, friends, friends, friends, friends, friends, friends.

But now, none of them are really friends. I just want to talk to you all.

I wish you were still strangers.

Hello, stranger.

I'm glad I don't know you.

If I knew your name, I wouldn't be able to speak my mind.

Let's go back to the day we introduced ourselves. Hello, hello, hello, hello, hello, hello.

Let's pretend we're still six years old.

If we were only six, I would treasure what I should treasure so much more.

I inexplicably feel lonely sometimes, even though I have brothers and sisters, family, and friends around me.

But I still feel like I'm sometimes just an extra, an outsider.

Let's be good friends. I'm afraid I'll cry when we part, cry and cry and cry.

That would be such a pity.

When you cherished me, I didn't cherish you;

When I wanted to cherish you, I couldn't;

When you were about to leave me, I began to cherish you.

Is it because you moved too fast, or I couldn't keep up with your pace?

We missed Noah's Ark, we missed the Titanic, and I keep missing things.

In the morning, we set out with smiles.

At noon, we circle in the same place, maintaining our dignity firmly.

In the evening, we see the same scenery and sunset.

At night, we console ourselves, believing that our efforts for the day weren't wasted.

The same eyes see differently. The same ears hear differently.

The same mouth speaks differently. The same heart thinks differently.

Is it because of this that the same life has different sorrows?

Summer is about to pass. There has been so much rain.

The rain keeps falling, and I keep watching.

The rain keeps falling, and I keep listening.

When the weather clears, I must.

Go for a walk in the mountains, play by the sea,

Look at flowers in the garden, pick fruits in the orchard.

Visit friends, stroll through the market,

Air out the quilts, and air myself out too. I must.

I will not regret always treasuring what I have.

On the day I was closest to the moon, the moon told me a secret: he said he was actually the sun.

On the day I was closest to the moon, I told him a secret: I said I was actually afraid of heights.
