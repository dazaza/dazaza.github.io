Business projects suitable for women to start and make money

by huangxin27 on 2008-11-10 09:46:03

Here are a few projects suitable for women entrepreneurs to make money, for your reference.

Open an outdoor beer bar

In the sweltering summer, people are willing to drink a few refreshing and tasty beers to cool off. Therefore, you can choose a street square, a corner of the park or a space in the residential community, set up a few sun umbrellas, place tables, chairs, and beer, and the outdoor beer bar is born. You can provide freshly brewed beer, ice beer, and other high-quality new types of beer, allowing the beer bar to attract customers with its novelty and characteristics.

Ice beer was popular in foreign countries in the 1990s. It involves uniformly delivering young beer to a scraper-type heat exchanger, cooling the beer to the most suitable temperature, causing it to form ice crystals, and then sending the ice crystal-containing beer to a recrystallization tank, where after stirring, the ice crystals and beer are fully mixed.

This makes the beer cool and pure, with a soft and refreshing taste. Especially, its calorie content is 20% lower than that of ordinary beer, but it effectively retains the nutrients in the beer, making it suitable for men, women, and children alike. Therefore, if you want to open an outdoor beer bar, it's best to purchase a set of equipment for making ice beer. According to the current market situation, the price of this equipment is around 10,000 yuan, plus other costs, requiring only an investment of 20,000 yuan, and the outdoor beer bar will belong to you.

Open a teahouse

For modern people, a teahouse is truly a sunny place to cultivate one's mind, relax oneself, and anchor one's mood. Opening a uniquely distinctive teahouse doesn't necessarily have to be in the bustling areas of the city, nor does it need a large room, but the store decoration must be unique, the interior settings must be fresh and elegant, the service staff should possess necessary tea ceremony knowledge and artistic level, and the store name should be as poetic and elegant as possible.

The store can have background music, a few bookshelves with various leisure newspapers and magazines, and various chess games and calligraphy entertainment projects can also be set up, allowing tea guests to savor life and enjoy living in a pot of tea and a book, or a pot of tea and a game of chess. Of course, opening a teahouse requires you to possess a certain level of cultural cultivation and aesthetic standards. Only in this way can your teahouse highlight the essence of tea ceremony, thus being extraordinary and attracting customers.

Open a specialty snack shop

If you are confident in your performance in the kitchen, I suggest you consider opening a specialty snack shop in a busy downtown area. Hire only one waiter, add rent and cooking utensils, control the required working capital and other costs, and the investment can be kept below ten thousand yuan.

Your snack shop mainly operates home-style dishes. You take on two roles, both as the boss and the chef, operating on the principle of thin profit but high volume sales. The service targets mainly include workers, students, and migrant populations, with breakfast and fast food as the main business methods, and local specialty dishes as the main business varieties, focusing on refined preparation of coarse grains. In this way, your daily average revenue will exceed 150 yuan, and after deducting all expenses, the daily net income will be between 40-50 yuan.

After reading the above store opening guide, you might say that these stores require relatively large investments, while your funds are too little, making them unsuitable for you. Don't worry, if you can raise ten thousand yuan, one of the following small stores will definitely suit you.

Open a children's bookstore

Opening a bookstore specifically serving children, choose a location near a primary school, with a business area of 20 square meters sufficient. The cost mainly includes rent and working capital. The target audience of the children's bookstore should primarily be preschool children and elementary school students, with over a thousand varieties of books available. Currently, the general discount rate for children's books in the market is 40% or 30%, plus mail-order fees, the pure income from books is roughly around 30%. So, when you sell 100 yuan worth of books, you will gain a profit of 30 yuan. Of course, while selling books, you can also concurrently sell stationery and other learning supplies for elementary school students, which will bring profits to your bookstore.

Open a consignment store

Currently, some companies, in order to expand their market share and reduce costs, have introduced the agency system in their sales methods. If you meet the conditions, you may cooperate with these companies and open a consignment store. A consignment store has low investment and minimal risk. After determining the business premises, the company usually only charges you a certain deposit, with no other significant investment. The goods operated by the consignment store are delivered by the manufacturer, prices are uniformly set by the manufacturer, and after-sales service costs are also borne by the manufacturer. You just need to focus on sales and receive a fixed percentage from the company. By showcasing your talents and seriously studying the market, you will achieve unexpected results.

Open a car beauty shop

With the development of China's automobile industry, more and more cars are entering households, and the prospects of the car beauty market are very broad. For this reason, you can open a car beauty shop near a traffic artery, mastering necessary car beauty technology, and equipping related tools and facilities. The car beauty shop can mainly focus on car washing, while also offering paint repair and interior decoration services. As income increases, it is best to continuously expand the scale of operations and increase service categories. At that time, your car beauty shop will surely become increasingly prosperous.

Open a housekeeping service society

If you are familiar with the community population, you can combine their needs and open a housekeeping service society within the community, which will certainly be very popular. Opening a housekeeping service society requires low investment, minimal risk, quick returns, and can also arrange reemployment for laid-off workers, with good development prospects. Your business items can be single or comprehensive, such as opening an elderly care center, kindergarten, elementary school pick-up service department, information service center, marriage introduction agency, vegetable delivery service society, laundry service store, etc., all of which should be considered.
