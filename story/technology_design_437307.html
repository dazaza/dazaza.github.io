Here is not the prosperity of a big city.

by fkflr067 on 2011-05-26 10:51:54

There are no particularly desirable scenic spots here, yet this place has cultivated an "educational myth" - Hebei Hengshui High School. The school has long been renowned and has received countless honors. For example, it has been awarded the "Top Ten Advanced Schools in China for Nurturing Students," and the "National Advanced Group in Educational Research." In 2010, the school's college entrance examination results were astonishingly good: 78 students were admitted to Tsinghua University and Peking University, with about an 85% pass rate for key universities. Seven out of the top ten students in Hebei Province in both liberal arts and sciences came from this school. Its college entrance examination achievements have ranked first in Hebei Province for 11 consecutive years. Faced with these breathtaking numbers, it is hard to imagine that this educational miracle occurred in Hengshui, a small city. More commendably, the school's moral education work ranks at the forefront nationwide. Riding on the wave of national moral education reform, the "Third National Middle School Head Teacher Professional Development Seminar and Hebei Hengshui High School Moral Education Field Observation Conference" was held here. At the conference, Wang Wenzhan, former director of the Department of Basic Education of the Ministry of Education and national vice supervisor, comprehensively interpreted the "National Medium and Long-Term Education Reform and Development Plan Outline." He pointed out that the "Outline" has grand goals, clear policies, focuses on improving quality, promotes fairness, facilitates the scientific development of education, and marks a new stage in the development of our country's educational reforms.

Part Two: Management Philosophy

(1) Advanced Educational Philosophy

Hengshui High School’s educational philosophy is very advanced, evident from their motto, educational pursuits, core concepts, teaching philosophies, and more, as seen in their implementation. Below are the school motto and a series of educational philosophies:

Motto: Strive for Excellence

Educational Pursuit: Stand firm in reality, think about the future, embrace China, look towards the world

Core Concept: A school should be a "spiritual special zone"

Teaching Philosophy: Quality education can better improve "college admission rates"

Management Philosophy: Management is communication, service, and leadership

Survival Philosophy: Let the campus become a paradise of burning passion

Nurturing Philosophy: Cultivate harmonious individuals through unforgettable education

From this, we can see that the people of Hengshui have forward-looking vision, clear pursuit goals, and lofty ambitions to lead the way. Advanced educational philosophies have become the foundation of their success.

However, the people of Hengshui are not satisfied with this. They annually send teachers to observe, learn, and further train locally, and even dispatch large numbers of teachers abroad for inspection and study. This undoubtedly broadens the teachers' horizons and keeps their educational philosophies at the forefront of the world. Such internationalized educational insight is quite rare and valuable.

(2) Teaching Philosophy

Emphasis on Research:

1. Focus on Exam Guidelines Research: What the exam guidelines do not require —— don't review

What the exam guidelines lower requirements for —— don't expand

For what the exam guidelines require —— put great effort into reviewing

Ensure direction is accurate, time is not wasted, and truly improve review efficiency. For instance, examples in the exam guidelines are very important; the examination of backbone knowledge is "concentrated"; the examination of basic knowledge is "fresh and flexible", meaning solidify basic knowledge and know how to apply it flexibly. Ability assessment is "comprehensive", including reading ability, the ability to extract effective information, information processing ability, the ability to correctly write chemical equations, relative molecular mass calculation ability, substance amount calculation, etc.

2. Study Test Writers: Research based on the characteristics of questions set by test writers, simulate comprehensive tests from all angles, such as one physics test being difficult while biology and chemistry are easy; another one having difficult chemistry but easy physics and biology. Let students adapt to various question styles, stay calm under pressure, and remain unchanged in the face of changes, achieving victory in the college entrance examination.

3. Research the Value of Three-Round Reviews

The first round review can be seen as transporting food and building fortifications, the second round review is training soldiers, and the third round review is practical exercises. All the work and the content of senior three classes are the essence of collective lesson preparation. In the senior three lesson preparation group, the ancestral precepts are written as follows:

Don't be lazybones, everyone should make real contributions

Don't rely on charity, everyone should have dignity

No complaints, everyone strives for excellence

No regrets, everyone practices hard work

Hengshui teachers cultivate themselves with the following philosophy:

Cultivate your own learning so that it flows like a fountain

Cultivate your behavior so that it is standardized and professional

Cultivate your language so that it is captivating

Cultivate your expression so that it is lively

Cultivate your temperament so that it transcends the ordinary

Cultivate your eyes so that they are expressive and rich

Cultivate your voice so that it is enchanting

From these details, it can be seen that teachers have strict requirements for themselves, and their demands on students can be described as almost harsh. For example, every self-study period is treated as an exam, each exam is treated as a college entrance exam, no reference materials allowed, no discussions allowed, and no late submission of homework allowed. At the same time, they often encourage students, such as writing comments on assignments: "Only strive brings beauty, waiting brings no brilliance," "How big your heart is, how big your stage will be," "Not afraid of hardship, endure for a moment; if you fear hardship, suffer for life," giving different students spiritual motivation.

From the entire trip to Hengzhong, the impression is of harmony among teachers and between teachers and students. There are slogans like: "All natural science subjects form one family, one voice shouting to the end, one belief thinking to the end, one fist working to the end." The head teacher adopts the management philosophy of "soldiers leading soldiers, soldiers managing soldiers, soldiers teaching soldiers, soldiers learning from soldiers." Everyone is striving, everyone is fighting, and there is still much room for improvement. As a lesson preparation group leader, collective lesson preparation needs more attention, knowledge needs to be more systematic, and understanding of the college entrance exam situation needs to be more accurate. In future work, we should inspire and spur ourselves with the spirit of Hengshui High School, making our own contributions to the progress and development of Beipiao High School.

Part Three: Impressions of Hengshui

1. This is a Humanistic Campus. Being in Hengshui High School, one strongly feels the carefully crafted educational atmosphere and cultural environment, experiencing humanistic care everywhere. Special emphasis is placed on tapping into the humanistic educational resources of the school, making alumni who have made outstanding contributions to Hengshui High School role models for teachers and students, encouraging current Hengzhong people to keep advancing. For example, the corridors of the teaching buildings are decorated with a doctorate corridor, featuring portraits and photos of past students who have become doctors; major paths in the school have beautiful bulletin boards displaying pictures, achievements, and messages of excellent students. In other areas, famous quotes and inspiring phrases written by students are everywhere, deeply engraved in one's mind. I believe that anyone in such an environment would be influenced and inspired by this upward atmosphere. Living in such an environment makes one self-disciplined, proud, invigorated, and motivated. This is probably the charm of the "spiritual special zone" created by Hengshui High School.

2. This is an Enthusiastic Campus. Teachers and students have good spirits, always full of enthusiasm for work and study. The office area is filled with laughter, teaching research activities are eloquently presented, classrooms are lively, and footsteps are brisk. Everyone has clear goals and a strong sense of competition, with the determination not to fall behind. The most distinctive feature is Hengzhong's break-time exercises, where students quickly gather after the bell rings. Students from one grade level, divided by class, march with robust steps, comparable to well-trained troops, stepping in unison like one person, with powerful and stirring steps. Passionate slogans like "Diligent and united, hardworking and striving, revitalizing our nation," "Self-respect, self-love, self-reliance, self-strengthening, standing alone, creating glory together," neat steps, loud slogans, youthful passion, high morale, and crazy drive, precisely because of such spiritual capital, there is the indomitable, never-say-die Hengzhong spirit, and the dare-to-fight, dare-to-climb Hengzhong people.

3. This is an Enjoyable Campus. Every student and staff member of Hengzhong treats the school with the affection of home, working wholeheartedly and protecting and caring for the school's growth and strength loyally. Emphasizing the expression of individuality and the particularity of humanity, allowing each Hengzhong person to play their talents down-to-earth, giving them a stage to showcase their abilities, constantly encouraging their morale. They regard work and study as a responsibility and duty. Working and studying happily.

4. This is an Elegant Campus.

Strict discipline, strict management. All 3000+ students at Hengshui High School live on campus, and the school is fully enclosed, which not only facilitates centralized management and unified guidance but also helps students develop good behavioral habits, such as self-management skills, self-discipline awareness, and a sense of discipline. The school has very strict management over students, such as students not being allowed to leave the school premises during normal times, and even parents delivering goods must hand them over at the gate. Students are not allowed to be late or absent, and must not delay returning to school, otherwise, they will be named and criticized at school meetings.

Lofty spiritual pursuits. Advocating the "Four Let Principles": Let teachers be far from vulgarity, let teachers be far from greed, let teachers be far from low-level interests, let teachers always have pursuits. "Four Educations": Social ethics education, ideological inspiration education, self-reliance education, hard work education. "Four Spirits": Dedication spirit, enterprising spirit, pragmatic spirit, innovation spirit. "Four Goals": Harmonious interpersonal relationships, warm humanistic environments, a diligent and upward spirit, and passionate work and study conditions.

Detailed moral education work. They guide students well through constraints, incentives, feedback, and evaluations. Specifically, through one body (the Student Union), two wings (Class Committees responsible for behavior habit formation and Youth League Branches responsible for moral education). By comparing culture, integrity, hygiene, morning exercises, studies, requiring students to be polite, run with passion, eat with standards, maintain hygiene, gain from internet use, have study goals, have no violations during rest periods, read selectively, act with integrity. In life, reducing idleness, idle talk, and idle thoughts. During self-study, achieving quietness, mental peace, and competitiveness. Morning exercises require tight formations, fast step frequencies, and loud slogans. Through these educational activities, students' willpower is tempered, helping them learn to seek knowledge and learn to be human. Letting students understand that "nothing can be entirely satisfactory, but strive to be without regret."

Rigorous teaching work. Truly implementing the "Five Must-Lets": In classroom teaching, if students can observe, let them observe; if they can think, let them think; if they can express, let them express; if they can operate themselves, let them operate themselves; if they can summarize themselves, let them deduce conclusions themselves. Teachers cannot monopolize or replace. It has the following distinct features: ① Emphasizing textbooks, focusing on basics, fundamental concepts and principles come easily. Teachers stress the accuracy and standardization of concept and principle language expressions. ② Timely in-class practice, reinforcing problem-solving approaches and methods, promoting flexible application of learned knowledge. ③ Focusing on cultivating students' study habits. Particularly emphasizing the cultivation of self-learning abilities. Returning time to students, with many self-study sessions. Beneficial for timely reinforcement, organizing knowledge, and allowing students to independently catch up on weaker subjects. ④ Systematically handling wrong questions. Each student has a pair of scissors and a glue stick, cutting out wrong questions and recording them in a wrong question notebook.

Part Four: Insights from Hengshui

(Fine-Grained)- Strict control and detailed management, cultivating self-discipline and self-reflection in learning and living habits

In Hengzhong, you won’t see students chasing and playing around during breaks, walking aimlessly, eating while walking, littering, wearing odd clothes, or using modern communication tools like mobile phones and MP3s. Everything is orderly, and the entire campus is quiet, with teachers working diligently and students studying intently.

After class, Hengzhong students maintain order, with only a few students leaving the classroom to handle personal matters. Upon entering the classroom, I was surprised to find that all the students stood at their desks, holding different English books and reading aloud. Isn't the Hengzhong students' gold-like appreciation of time worthy of learning by both teachers and students?

(Stimulating)- Stimulating and encouraging, tapping into students’ perseverance in learning

At Hengshui High School, you can see large promotional posters everywhere, at the school gates, cafeteria entrances, walls of the teaching buildings, and even on toilet doors. Photos and stories of students from Tsinghua and Peking Universities are spread throughout the campus, even on the columns of the teaching buildings wrapped with motivational oaths from outstanding students. These images, stories, and words cannot fail to inspire Hengzhong students to strive positively.

(Practical)- Practical teaching and learning, effectively improving teaching and learning efficiency, truly achieving the "five must-lets": In classroom teaching, if students can observe, let them observe; if they can think, let them think; if they can express, let them express; if they can operate themselves, let them operate themselves; if they can summarize themselves, let them deduce conclusions themselves. Teachers cannot monopolize or replace. It has the following distinct features: ① Emphasizing textbooks, focusing on basics. ② Timely in-class practice, reinforcing problem-solving approaches and methods, promoting flexible application of learned knowledge. ③ Focusing on cultivating students' study habits. ④ Systematically handling wrong questions. Students cut out wrong questions and record them in a wrong question notebook.

Human potential is infinite, and only reasonable development can make each student a rich treasure. Hengshui High School teachers fully commit to teaching and research every day:

1. Teachers genuinely improve classroom teaching efficiency. Hengshui High School does not assign homework that lasts overnight; they advocate and practice this. Hengshui's classroom is simple but effective, and teachers' explanations are not blind but highly targeted. Their "buffet" is a full page every day. It includes derivations of basic knowledge, typical problems, and extracurricular reading materials. The buffet is something students can freely handle, but most students can complete it well.

2. Teachers prepare lessons meticulously. The teaching processes, including study guides and exercises, are prepared two weeks or even three weeks in advance. Their creation of study guides goes through five steps: First, two teachers design the lesson plan, which includes key points and difficulties. Second, during collective lesson preparation, they discuss and adjust, removing unnecessary parts and adding important ones. Third, they revise according to the discussion results and reprint it. Fourth, two teachers proofread it. Fifth, an experienced teacher conducts the final review to form the final draft.

3. Collective lesson preparation is genuinely effective. During the preparation process, teachers speak freely and without reservation. The main speaker introduces the key uses of a word and explains their thoughts, concluding with: "Do you have any supplements or opinions?" Then, the teachers express their views. Thus, collective lesson preparation is completed in a relaxed, lively, yet serious atmosphere. Their collective lesson preparation truly achieves the goal of sharing resources and is beneficial for young teachers' development.

Part Five: Enlightenment

In fact, educational work is roughly the same. What they thought of, we also thought of; what they did, we also did. But we always feel there is a significant gap. The reasons are: one, lacking rational discernment, enterprising beliefs, innovative spirit, pioneering power, and wise summarization. Two, lacking implementation and execution capabilities.

1. Increase the training intensity for middle-level cadres. Whether work is implemented and executed well depends on the middle level. Therefore, focus on promoting, appointing, training, and evaluating middle-level cadres, establishing an efficient operational mechanism.

2. Pay attention to detail management. There are no small things in schools. Once recognized, matters must be pursued thoroughly for practical effects, not just starting strong and ending weak. We are not short of plans and systems, nor ideas and methods, but actions, persistent actions, and persistence.

3. Strengthen school moral education. School moral education not only emphasizes form but also content, using people and events around us to motivate students, promoting positive energy, and boosting morale.

4. Organize meaningful extracurricular activities.

5. Strengthen classroom teaching, strengthen routine, organize and use study guides well.

6. Pay attention to details, such as timekeeping books, wrong question collections, and morning exercises.
