1. Ma Jiajue won "Second Prize in the National Olympiad in Physics Competition."

2. Ma Jiajue's dormitory mates once urinated on his quilt.

3. During winter when the temperature was relatively low, Ma Jiajue's dormitory mates once gave him a couple of yuan, asking him to wash their clothes for them. Since Ma had no money, he washed them.

4. In prison, Ma Jiajue wore the best clothes he had ever worn in his life—prison uniform.

"This is the best clothing I've ever worn," Ma Jiajue said today, and these words made all the policemen guarding him cry.

5. Due to not having shoes to wear, Ma Jiajue skipped classes for a few days while waiting for his student loan to be disbursed.

6. Ma Jiajue's family relied on ironing clothes for others to make a living. His mother lost 100 yuan (the money earned from ironing 200 pieces of clothing), and Ma Jiajue deliberately dropped the 100 yuan in the hallway so that his mother could pick it up!

7. Ma Jiajue borrowed money all the way from home to school to pay the 5000 yuan tuition fee.

8. Ma Jiajue refused to surrender himself and also rejected four lawyers offering free defense for his innocence because he only sought death.

9. To attend university, Ma Jiajue was already in debt for 10,000 yuan, working part-time jobs to subsidize his living expenses.

Ma Jiajue wrote a letter before his execution:

The spring sky of Kunming was raining,

With a hint of chilly wind,

I looked at the rusty iron window,

And thought of my pitiful parents,

Who worked day and night in the fields to support their children's education,

And even lit candles to iron clothes for others, earning fifty cents per piece.

That time, my mother lost one hundred yuan,

She painfully said it was the money earned from ironing two hundred pieces of clothing,

Seeing my mother's sorrowful face,

I threw the one hundred yuan I earned from hard labor onto the ground,

And told my mother: Mom, here's your one hundred yuan!

My mother showed a faint smile,

In fact, she knew it was me who threw it,

I don't fear enduring hardships alone,

I can't bear seeing my parents endure hardships,

During my years in university, I never asked my family for a single cent,

I always hoped my parents wouldn't worry about me,

They are old,

Worked hard all their lives,

How could I bear adding to their burden?

But the tuition was high,

I had to work hard labor myself,

Missing studies was inevitable,

I silently endured the hard labor alone,

I secretly ate only two steamed buns a day,

Winter, I feared the cold more,

Because I am from the south,

But to save a few yuan for hot showers,

I persisted in taking cold showers throughout the winter,

Shivering from the cold,

I smiled at my classmates and said,

Young people need physical exercise,

That day, I didn't have shoes to wear,

I was embarrassed to go to class,

Until the school gave some relief funds,

I bought a cheap pair of slippers and entered the classroom,

My family has always been very poor,

I grew up in poverty,

I tasted the hardship of my family since I was young,

Even as a child, I felt sorry for my parents' toil,

Wanted to alleviate my parents' burden with my small hands,

I said: Parents, you've worked hard, I've prepared dinner, please eat quickly!

I have always studied hard,

The neighbors in the village and my middle school teachers

All know I am a diligent, polite, and honest student,

I won the second prize in the national physics Olympiad in middle school,

I was discriminated against in high school and became depressed,

But during the months approaching the college entrance examination,

I endured various pressures and studied hard,

Thus, as a poor student, I achieved excellent results,

My college entrance exam score exceeded the key line of Guangxi Province by more than 50 points that year,

Completely capable of attending prestigious universities like Wuhan University or Harbin Institute of Technology,

However, considering the distance from home and higher costs,

I chose Yunnan University, which was geographically closer and had lower living expenses,

I was filled with hope,

An agrarian child,

Possessing the simplicity and honesty of the countryside,

Came to Yunnan University,

When I saw Chairman Mao's handwriting of the four big characters,

"Yunnan University,"

A ripple of sunlight stirred in my heart,

I resolved to continue studying hard,

To find a good job and repay my parents,

To change our impoverished fate,

And use my knowledge to work hard for society and the country,

To seriously become a respected person,

To be a contributor to society.

After entering university, I found that most people did not like studying,

Every evening they talked about girls,

Which girl was sexy and beautiful,

And which girl provided more pleasure in bed,

Wealthy students boldly pursued girlfriends,

Rented houses near the school and cohabited openly,

Everyone loved playing computer games,

And mocked me as a bumpkin,

Not knowing how to do this or that,

So, to get along well with my classmates,

I also learned to play computer games and became better at it than them,

I later became more interested in playing computers,

I used the money I earned from part-time jobs and borrowed some money,

To buy an old computer,

I was generous,

My computer was available for my classmates to play anytime,

I hoped to get along harmoniously with my classmates,

Time flew,

A few years of university passed,

I basically didn't return home during summer and winter vacations,

Instead, I worked hard in Kunming to earn money,

I consoled my parents,

Saying, Mom and Dad, I'm doing well at Yunnan University,

Teachers often invited me to their homes as guests,

Actually, every time I said such things, I lied in my heart,

Sometimes, I only had enough money for one meal that would last two days,

Often, I just ate two steamed buns a day,

I was never afraid of hardship or resentment towards anyone,

I never thought of stealing or robbing due to lack of money,

I was strong,

I was proud of myself,

I didn't let down my parents,

I didn't let down myself,

I didn't let down my classmates,

I didn't let down society,

But there were always some classmates who intentionally or unintentionally discriminated against me,

Sometimes saying things that hurt me deeply,

They thought my dressing style was strange,

They thought my behavior was strange,

So I started working quietly,

Not wanting to be seen as different,

Fortunately, I thought I had a few good fellow townspeople and classmates,

So I ignored those classmates' discrimination and contempt for my personality,

Many male students in university were boldly pursuing the girls they liked,

Many male students were in love,

Under this atmosphere and with encouragement from a few classmates,

I also boldly wrote a love letter,

And handed it to a girl I had secretly admired for a long time,

Due to my unattractive appearance,

Plus having no money,

And appearing rustic and introverted,

The girl mercilessly tore my heartfelt and sincere letter into pieces in front of many people,

I only felt inner pain,

I didn't resent anyone,

I just felt I wasn't good enough to deserve her,

I told my parents the same thing,

I was self-aware, I wouldn't fall in love,

Besides, university students should focus on their studies,

Time passed quickly,

University graduation was approaching,

Only one semester left,

During the last winter vacation,

I still didn't return home,

Still working hard in Kunming,

A few days before school started,

Some classmates came back early,

Perhaps all for job hunting reasons, they returned to school early, and I was happy,

Because I was so lonely during the entire winter vacation,

I'm not afraid of hardship,

But people are afraid of loneliness,

When I saw my classmates, I was very enthusiastic,

They invited me to play cards to pass the time,

I gladly accepted,

Actually, we often played cards before,

No need to hide,

I really have a high IQ,

So I often win when playing cards,

Several classmates suspected I was cheating,

I insisted I wasn't,

Thought treated me equally,

And exposed many of my past wounds,

Including the incident where the girl tore up my love letter,

Whatever suffering, whatever poverty, whatever tough life,

I could endure,

Other people's discrimination and contempt,

I could endure,

But these few classmates who seemed slightly better,

Turned out to cruelly and ruthlessly trample and humiliate my personal dignity,

It turned out everyone had been discriminating against me all along,

Mocking me cruelly,

My heart was in pain,

My tears fell quietly,

I am a strong person,

I have never been defeated by a hard and poor life,

But when my dignity was trampled beyond recognition,

When my past wounds were brought up again for ridicule,

My heart was bleeding,

And the ones who trampled on me were actually classmates and fellow townspeople I thought had decent relationships with me!

I couldn't stand under this atmosphere anymore,

It was they who were cruel to me,

It was they who didn't leave me any way out,

They didn't give me any room to retreat,

After thoroughly humiliating me, they were still so arrogant and happy,

Because their living conditions were still relatively good, they still had the means to chase after girls,

My broken heart had nowhere to turn!

Their images of thoroughly humiliating me kept flashing in my mind,

I had no way out, I decided to destroy everything together,

I decided to teach a lesson to those who discriminate against poor people and look down on poor people,

I decided to teach a lesson to those who ruthlessly trample and cruelly humiliate the dignity of poor people,

Their mercilessness when mocking me,

Their detestable faces when humiliating me,

Good people will be rewarded with goodness!
