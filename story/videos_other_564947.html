Grade 1 high school boy miraculously survived after falling from the fourth floor of the dormitory

by wenkuon59 on 2011-11-17 14:16:13

On August 5, a Grade 1 student from Wuwei No.3 Middle School fell mysteriously from the fourth floor of the student dormitory after an argument with the building manager during the summer school-organized tutoring session and miraculously survived. Currently, the local police have already intervened in this fall case.

The injured cried and told his story: "I fainted after being beaten by the building manager."

On August 11, reporters came to the Orthopedics Department of Wuwei People's Hospital and saw the student who fell from the building, Tang Peiyun (a pseudonym), groaning painfully on the bed. The 17-year-old Tang Peiyun is a student of Class 7, Grade 1 at Wuwei No.3 Middle School. He cried and told the reporter: "In the afternoon of August 5, I proposed not to stay in the school dormitory and got my parents' approval. Around 4 p.m., I went to the school dormitory building and told the building manager that I would not stay in the school dormitory anymore. He asked me to pay 20 or 30 yuan, another staff member said if not money then give cigarettes. I asked why, they said it was for accommodation fees, I said shouldn't the accommodation fee be paid when the school starts, then they cursed me, I replied them, and they started beating me. They beat me three times on the first floor, then I lost consciousness. When I woke up, I found myself in the hospital."

Family Doubts: Some students said the jump was related to the beating.

According to Tang Peiyun's father, Tang Haosheng, "My child started tutoring from the afternoon of July 29, the school said the tutoring fee would be collected together when enrolling at the start of the semester. On the afternoon of August 5, everything was normal when my child left home. At around 7 p.m., I received a notification and rushed to the school, only then did I know that my child fell from the fourth floor at around 4 p.m. that day. At that time, my child had already been taken to the 120 emergency vehicle, I saw my child covered in blood, unconscious." Tang Haosheng said: "Some students at the scene told me that there was a tussle between my child and the building manager before the incident. Later, we received a call from a student, saying that Tang Peiyun's jump was related to the beating. On August 6, when Tang Peiyun regained consciousness, I asked him how he fell from the building, he cried in委屈and said why would he commit suicide by jumping from the building, and only said that he lost consciousness after being beaten." Tang Haosheng said: "Our child told us that someone from the school was extorting money and cigarettes from the students, so he felt unsafe and proposed not to stay in the school dormitory. We agreed that he wouldn't stay in the school dormitory, but unexpectedly, disaster struck. After the incident, we saw bloodstains on the stairs from the first to the fourth floor of the school dormitory building, and blood was also flowing from our child's nose. We are suspicious about the claim that our child committed suicide by jumping from the building."

Journalist Interview: Both teachers and students at the school avoided the questions cautiously.

From Wuwei People's Hospital, the reporter learned that after emergency treatment, Tang Peiyun has currently escaped life-threatening danger, but due to the fall, his body suffered from compression fractures of the spine, fractures in all four limbs, pelvic fractures, and the possibility of lifelong disability is very high. Then, what exactly happened to Tang Peiyun at the school? Does the safety hazard within the school mentioned by his family exist? How did Tang Peiyun end up falling from the fourth floor? What happened after the argument between the two parties? With these series of questions, on the afternoon of August 11, the reporter went to Wuwei No.3 Middle School for an interview. The reporter saw that many classrooms were still having classes. In response to the reporter's questions, both teachers and students of the school maintained caution and avoided answering. Subsequently, the reporter went to the administrative office of the school and proposed interviewing the principal. A staff member told the reporter: "Principal Zhao went to a meeting at the Education Bureau and cannot be contacted."

Cause of the Fall: The police are currently investigating.

In the morning of August 11, the reporter went to the Education Bureau of Liangzhou District for an interview. Relevant personnel from the bureau stated that after the incident, the Education Bureau attached great importance to it and required the school to focus on the treatment of the student. This person responsible said that this fall incident was triggered because the school staff required the student to handle normal procedures for leaving the dormitory building, but the student did not cooperate. Tang Peiyun was emotionally agitated at the school, and some students witnessed Tang Peiyun jumping himself. As for the specific cause of the case, the police are currently investigating.

In the morning of August 11, the reporter learned from Wunan Police Station of Liangzhou District Public Security Bureau that after the incident, the police had already intervened in the investigation. Preliminary findings confirmed that before the incident, Tang Peiyun had an argument with the building manager and was beaten by the school staff. Whether Tang Peiyun jumped from the building to commit suicide or if there were other reasons, the police are further investigating.
