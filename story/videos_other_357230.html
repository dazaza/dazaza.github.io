Shanghai World Expo Stampede Rumored to Leave One Teenager Dead

by kqpziifd on 2010-05-31 23:41:29

A piece of news I saw just now: On May 30, a trampling accident happened in the Shanghai World Expo because of crazy fans of Hallyu scrambling for tickets. An accident happened that a girl was forced to fall from the second floor and died. At the same time, hundreds of people were injured. After the trampling accident of the Shanghai World Expo happened, authorities urgently dispatched more than one thousand armed police to seal off the exhibition area for processing, but the relevant news was not released by the Shanghai World Expo authorities last night. So no one knows whether the trampling accident is true or false. On May 30, actors were recruited. The Korean National Exhibition Hall of the Shanghai World Expo was scheduled to hold a Korean music festival at night. The Korean stars who attended included currently very popular Super Junior, Kim Seung-hyun, etc. Yesterday the hall distributed 5,000 free tickets in the Expo Culture Center. Tickets could be exchanged for free with an Expo ticket. This triggered frenzy among the star-chasers in Shanghai, which led to the trampling accident. According to the report of Apple Daily, the Korean side originally planned to distribute free tickets three times: in the morning, at noon and in the evening. By 9:30 am yesterday, there were nearly ten thousand people queuing in front of the Culture Center. When the door opened for distributing tickets, it was extremely crowded on site. After distributing part of the tickets, the authority stopped the distribution, which aroused discontent among the masses. Staff explained to the masses that all tickets were the responsibility of the organizer and the decision of not distributing tickets was made by the Korean organizer. But the queuers did not believe this, scolding the staff and even abusing each other. During this period, some people tore at the policemen maintaining order on site and scratched their hands and faces. They also shouted that they would complain to the Organizing Committee of the World Expo. I would like to remind everyone that visiting the World Expo is certainly important, but life is more precious. Without life, how can we visit? If you love stars, you should pay more attention to your own safety, otherwise we will lose a star-chaser. According to the current information, because of the fatal accident, not only did the Expo Culture Center close yesterday afternoon for aftermath, but also the Korean concert scheduled for last night was delayed. For our safety and for the successful hosting of the Shanghai World Expo, all compatriots please show the virtues of our Chinese nation and show the high quality of Chinese people.
