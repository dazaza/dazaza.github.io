This week, the developer restrains its urgency and prepares to launch wedding apartments on Valentine's Day _1395 Yuzhou Sunshine Flower City

by xmnqhqzi on 2012-02-12 12:56:55

Roommi News, February 10: After the traditional Lantern Festival, Valentine's Day is coming next Tuesday. Originally, it was thought that developers would take this opportunity to launch new housing units; however, based on current information, developers are still holding back—zero launches and zero additional offerings for this Valentine’s Day. In terms of events, they are not holding back, promoting special discount units or themed activities under the guise of "honeymoon homes."

It is evident that the lackluster performance of the Xiamen real estate market during the seven-day Spring Festival has made many developers who originally planned to launch or add new units after the holiday more hesitant. However, recent reactions from homebuyers have given developers a bit more confidence. Zheng Wei, Vice President of United Real Estate, stated, “In the past half-month since the Spring Festival, whether it be the number of homebuyers visiting the sales offices or inquiries via phone calls, there has been an increase, basically returning to the figures of last September, significantly more than in November and December of last year.”

On the other hand, starting from November last year until now, the sluggishness of the real estate market has far exceeded three months. For many real estate companies, both cash flow and capital chains are somewhat tight. Therefore, regardless of any uncertainties developers may have, ensuring the rapid recovery of funds and the timely entry of new housing units into the market remain necessary strategies.
