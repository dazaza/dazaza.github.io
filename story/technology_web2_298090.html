We always define home as a warm harbor, we always expect our home to be warm and peaceful. Of course, this should be attributed to the mutual tolerance, understanding, and consideration among family members, but a harmonious environment is also indispensable. The family's restaurant is the best place for family members to rest and talk after a day's work outside. Creating a warm and quiet restaurant will make the home atmosphere much better.

Owner: Mr. Ying and his wife

Profession: White-collar worker

House Type: Two bedrooms, two living rooms

Decoration Space: Restaurant

Decoration Cost: 8042 yuan

Mr. Ying and his wife are a young couple who have been married for only one year. Like many young people nowadays, both of them are white-collar workers in their companies. Since they haven't worked for many years, when they bought this house, the down payment was paid by both sets of parents, and the decoration costs were borne by the young couple themselves. Then they started to live a life of paying off the mortgage every month. Although such a life has some pressure, the couple's relationship is very good. When introducing to the editor, the two still look like they are in love, making the editor envious.

When decorating their house, Mr. Ying and his wife did not hire any special designer. All matters were decided by the two of them through consultation. Therefore, there is no specific style, but many different elements were absorbed.

Domestic Sanli recessed lights, high cost-performance ratio. Let's start with the hard finishes. First, a metal-textured frosted glass door replaced the traditional wooden door, making the entire restaurant space appear transparent and open. The glass door is not expensive, custom-made at a nearby hardware store for only 200 yuan.

The black ceiling is the craftsmanship of the workers, with black walnut bark veneer attached, and the effect is very good. With a metal border, it only costs 550 yuan. The recessed lights installed on the ceiling were bought together with lighting fixtures at the lighting market, branded Sanli. They cost 25 yuan each, with excellent quality and have never broken after more than a year of use. The pendant light purchased is from Yongzhe brand, originally priced at 586 yuan but negotiated down to 500 yuan.

The floor used Bao Deli teak solid wood flooring, which is relatively expensive, costing 87 yuan per piece. Mrs. Ying managed to negotiate it down to 85 yuan per piece. There are reasons and values for the high price. This type of flooring is hard, highly durable, and most importantly, it looks good with fine texture and delicate wood grain.

Cute little pig placemats, let's take a look at the soft furnishings in Mr. Ying's house after discussing the hard finishes.

The dining table was bought at Jin Hai Ma Furniture, currently the most popular glass steel tube structure, equally transparent and crystal clear. The chairs are paired with fabric-covered steel tube structure chairs, with sponge and fabric material for the seat and backrest, providing great comfort when sitting. The dining table and six dining chairs cost a total of 1800 yuan.

A cabinet with four drawers is simple and elegant, very practical in the hall, and can also be decorated with vases. It was bought along with the furniture for only 199 yuan, offering excellent value for money.

The curtains selected are plain coffee color, thick material, somewhat similar to velvet and suede, with very good light-blocking effect. In terms of style, it does not have fancy European patterns but instead uses the simplest design, creating a tranquil warmth.

Finally, the beautiful wedding photos hanging on the wall of the restaurant are free gifts from the wedding photo session. Aren't they pretty?

Haha, finally, here is the decoration list for Mr. Ying's restaurant:

Ceiling: 550 yuan

Recessed Lights + Pendant Light: 575 yuan

Dulux Haolang Full Effect Wall Paint: 418 yuan

Bao Deli Teak Solid Wood Flooring: 3400 yuan

Dining Table + Dining Chairs: 1800 yuan

Hall Cabinet: 199 yuan

Curtains: 900 yuan

Total: 8042 yuan
