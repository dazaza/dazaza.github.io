The Concubine's Curse

by di6688ji on 2009-12-06 01:37:55

Introduction to Consort Disaster: Without hearing about being struck by lightning and then transported to another era, I am that unfortunate person! Heaven must have thought I was too comfortable in the past, so it sent me here to suffer. The handsome man is very handsome, but he's prickly! Suspecting me for no reason, imprisoning me, torturing me, well, it's just pain, pass out and there's no feeling! I'll endure it! Demoting me to a lowly slave, forcibly taking me, yet still despising me, calling me a harlot, shaving my hair, never mind, short hair is also quite fashionable. Carving his words on an intimate part of my body, doesn't matter, consider it a tattoo! I want to leave, but he uses the child as an excuse to hold me back, confining me. He has a wife, I don't want to calculate, having me, yet finding a new love, even making her enter on the same day as me, but she is the secondary consort while I am the concubine! Finally one day, when I was framed, caught committing adultery with his guard, when the child in my belly was cruelly aborted by him, my heart slowly turned cold-blooded, I should not and will no longer trust anyone, from now on, I will no longer be weak, I must become strong! On the day I left him, I resolutely told him, "I hope we never meet again in this life, if we do, we will be enemies!" —————————————— At the banquet for the coronation of the crown prince, Simu Ying sat on the nine-dragon auspicious gold-inlaid sandalwood chair, his innate majesty and noble elegance blending perfectly, extraordinarily handsome, and I sat by his side, in the most noble position among the consorts. "Zheng'er, come quickly and meet Consort Lan," Simu Ying waved to Simu Zheng. "Your humble son..." When he clearly saw my face, shock filled his eyes...he stood there like a stone statue, frozen in place, the person he had been frantically searching for night and day finally appeared before him, but in such a situation...the emperor's woman...never would have dreamed... ——————————————— However, I am not satisfied with this. The revenge has just begun, I can no longer stop... Mad revenge, in the end I lost myself... I used my power, cultivated loyal followers, interfered in politics, eliminated rivals. A sudden political upheaval, self-inflicted massacre! But unexpectedly, a mantis catching a cicada didn't see the oriole behind... And at this moment, I was shocked to discover that what I thought was my greatest advantage wasn't what I thought it was... National disgrace, family vengeance, internal strife and external troubles, fratricidal warfare, I was the culprit... I regretted it endlessly, but those who loved me, and those I loved, were all bruised because of me... Heaven, I already know I'm wrong! Is there any way you could give me another chance, to start over?
