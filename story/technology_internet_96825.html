3G Era: Hardware Equipment Becomes the Focus of Information Investment

by now on 2008-10-16 17:16:53

With the advent of the 3G era and the rapid development of broadband technology, mobile internet will become a new battleground for various websites to compete over. From mobile portal services to mobile search, websites will invest more resources into innovation and development of related businesses. This will also drive an increased demand for enterprise-level hardware that emphasizes high stability and low energy consumption. Virtualization technology will become an important means for websites to control the scale of data centers, improve the utilization rate of unit hardware, and thereby enhance corporate core competitiveness.

In 2007, the total amount spent on informatization by Chinese websites in terms of enterprise-level hardware, software, and services reached $390 million. Among these, hardware equipment (mainly including servers, storage, and network devices) remained the focus of informatization investment, reaching $280 million, accounting for 71.7% of total expenditure. IDC predicts that from 2008 to 2012, website spending on IT will continue to grow at an average rate of over 12%, reaching $734.3 million in 2012, with a five-year compound annual growth rate of 13.5%.

As of June 30, 2008, the number of websites in China was 1.919 million, with a large number of enterprises starting informatization construction. However, looking at recent developments, many enterprises' online marketing results have not been ideal. Enterprise informatization is a global inevitability; whether it's website administrators or enterprises, once they reach a certain scale, they will set up their own servers. Considering factors such as business needs and security, placing servers inside the company can bring many inconveniences, and pulling a sufficient bandwidth line also invisibly increases the operating costs of enterprises. Therefore, the business of server colocation has emerged accordingly.

So, how to choose a server? A responsible person from Era Interconnect stated that a server could be considered the core of the entire network. How to choose a server that matches the business scale is something both decision-makers and technical personnel need to consider. When selecting a network server, attention should be paid to its performance and after-sales service.

Before setting up a server, preparations should be made thoroughly, whether it's selecting the server, the data center, or the lines—all these aspects need careful consideration. Besides choosing an IDC provider with a reputation guarantee, attention should also be given to technical expertise and service quality. Manager Yang from Guangzhou told the author that Era Interconnect (www.todayidc.com) is an excellent IDC service provider. Professional engineers with years of network maintenance experience have provided many excellent solutions for our company’s website construction. With a 20G fiber optic connection directly linked to the Guangdong backbone node of ChinaNet, it ensures customers can access websites quickly and stably. It allows businesses to easily conduct e-commerce anytime and anywhere, making it the best choice for companies building websites.

Surveys show that currently, the number of small and medium-sized enterprises and non-public enterprises in China has exceeded 42 million. The enormous market potential of network products is undeniable, and users' demands for network products are rapidly increasing, which also stimulates healthy competition among IDC suppliers. How to ensure customers buy with confidence and use with comfort is a severe test facing domestic IDC service providers.
