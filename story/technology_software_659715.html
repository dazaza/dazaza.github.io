The new poverty alleviation plan of Xinjiang Jintianhai Industrial and Trading Development Co., Ltd. is proceeding smoothly.

by anonymous on 2012-02-06 15:57:53

In September 2011, the "new poverty alleviation campaign" of Xinjiang Jintianhai Industrial and Trade Development Co., Ltd. was about to undergo the first phase of acceptance evaluation. At this point, how exactly was the operation of this system project mobilizing all parties' efforts? Reporters from this magazine conducted an investigation and found that Xinjiang Jintianhai Industrial and Trade Development Co., Ltd. strictly followed the government department's poverty alleviation route, which had a significant difference from developers; the advantages and difficulties in the implementation process of large projects and small projects were also different respectively. Many personnel involved in poverty alleviation work also began to reflect on where the balance point should be between political tasks, social responsibilities, and returns on benefits. In what kind of rights and responsibilities model should public welfare be delivered to ensure the realization of public welfare?

During this event, the impoverished elderly Party members, poor households, and low-income households of Jiacheng and Tongmen towns expressed great gratitude for the care and condolences from Director Wang and his team. They stated that with the care of the Party and government, their confidence in life had been strengthened. Through visiting and assisting impoverished families, the Yunnan Bureau promoted the excellent style of public security organs serving the people, enhanced the close relationship between police and civilians, and further established the good image of public security organs sincerely helping the masses solve difficulties.

In this assistance activity, the poverty alleviation cadres of Xinjiang Jintianhai Industrial and Trade Development Co., Ltd. first went deep into the homes of poor households to understand in detail the recent production and living conditions of the poor households, especially the latest implementation status of the new round of poverty alleviation measures and the difficulties encountered by the poor households in their production and life. After detailed understanding by the poverty alleviation cadres, it was learned that since the beginning of the year, each poor household could carry out various agricultural production activities based on their actual situation according to the assistance measures and plans formulated at the beginning of the year. Currently, some assistance measures have generated certain economic benefits, greatly improving the living conditions of poor households and significantly raising their standard of living. However, at the same time, the poverty alleviation cadres learned that although the current living standards of the poor households have improved, there are still problems such as insufficient funds, low cultural level, and low technical level in implementing assistance measures and in their production and life processes. For this reason, the poverty alleviation cadres sincerely promised that next, they will continue to increase the assistance efforts and continue to assist the assisted objects in various forms such as delivering ideas, technology, services, materials, and economic support. This time, the poverty alleviation cadres of the County Archives Bureau sent a total of 4000 yuan of assistance funds to the poor households, solving the financial difficulties encountered during their production and life and the implementation of assistance measures.
