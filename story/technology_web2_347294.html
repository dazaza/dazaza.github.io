Are well-known enterprises playing monkey with job seekers? andyxy18 Interview position: finance Interview company: a well-known enterprise Party concerned's own statement: On January 31, 2010, I received a text message notifying me to attend the "Chengdu Well-known Enterprise Finance Special Recruitment Fair". I went to the interview location early in the morning. The interviewer of the first round was a professional in finance, who asked me in detail about my work experience and professional knowledge; then I entered the second round of interviews, which was actually 16 interviewees divided into two groups for competition. After the second round of interviews, I smoothly became one of the four winners; the third round of interviews was conducted in the afternoon, and the content of the interview was to do psychological test questions. The last round of interviews was a talk with the HR manager, who asked me if I was willing to work in Shenzhen. My answer was no, because I originally intended to apply for the position in Chengdu. Then he asked me about my salary requirements. Since my current position is at the supervisor level, with a monthly salary of 4500 yuan, I required a monthly salary of 5000-6000 yuan for the new job. The HR manager did not respond to my answer, just letting me give all the original certificates to another HR person for inspection. Finally, he told me to wait for notification within two weeks. Until the New Year, I did not receive the OFFER from the company. What made me angriest was that after spending 8 hours on this interview, I didn't even get a rejection reply. Later, I sent an email myself to inquire about the interview results. I thought this matter was over, but after the holiday when I started working again, I received another text message from this well-known enterprise. The message roughly said that my resume had passed the preliminary screening, inviting me to the same location as before for the interview, different only in that this time the interview time was 3:30 PM. I went there full of doubts, and after the interview, I talked to the interviewer about my previous interview experience, while raising my confusion about the interview process and recruitment plan. The interviewer's answer was that the company has its own selection principles. As a job seeker, I still don't understand why the company acted this way. Is it treating applicants like monkeys, or does the company not have any positions in Chengdu but is recruiting for the Shenzhen headquarters? Or can it not offer the rumored high treatment? In short, the company's recruitment behavior and methods give me the feeling of no sincerity at all. I advise job seekers interested in this company to be cautious.无忧点评 (Wuyou Comment): Don't blindly believe so-called "well-known enterprises", and don't subjectively impose the so-called "should" framework on the enterprise. Finding a company is like wearing shoes, whether they fit or not can only be known after trying them on.
