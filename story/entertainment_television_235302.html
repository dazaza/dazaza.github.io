Introduction to the plot of Season 3 of "Ugly Girl":

As an inspirational drama, the heroine Lin Wudi in the series has braces, big glasses, and a braided ponytail, dressing in an old-fashioned way. However, she is hardworking, diligent, and kind-hearted, eventually winning the appreciation and trust of her boss, as well as capturing the hearts of many viewers. The image of Ugly Girl reflects the majority of people in real life, satisfying a self-examination and self-confirmation effect. However, just as "aesthetic fatigue" can occur, "reviewing ugliness" can also lead to fatigue. To avoid this situation, Season 3 of "Ugly Girl" will make significant adjustments in terms of plot and character styling. It is understood that Wudi will experience a romantic relationship in the new season, encountering her true love, which will bring about a significant change in her appearance. It is not ruled out that Wudi will remove her signature braces and big glasses in Season 3.

Currently, the selection activity for the new male lead of Season 3 of "Ugly Girl," co-hosted by Hunan Satellite TV, Hunan Entertainment Channel, Beijing Xiangchao, and Golden Eagle Network — titled "Invincible No. 1" — has started accepting entries. Any male over 18 years old can sign up to participate. The selection process for "Invincible No. 1" will break away from traditional talent shows. From the preliminary rounds onward, there will be no traditional celebrity judges; instead, they will be replaced by a fan base composed of "Ugly Girl" enthusiasts — the Invincible Fan Club. This includes the Invincible Teenager Group, the Invincible Single Women Group, and the Invincible Mother Group, led by actors from the "Ugly Girl" series. They represent three stages of how women perceive men throughout their lives and also set the standard for female TV audiences selecting the new male lead.

The preliminary selections will air on Hunan Entertainment Channel starting February 27th at 7:30 PM. The grand final will be broadcast simultaneously on Hunan Satellite TV, Hunan Entertainment Channel, and Golden Eagle Network on March 29th. At that time, the selected "Invincible No. 1" will compete with Ferdinand to become the new male lead in Season 3 of "Ugly Girl" and launch a special pursuit campaign against Wudi in an unprecedented way.
