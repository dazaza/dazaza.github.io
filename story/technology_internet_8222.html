Recently, I have been planning this Blogger team!

Http//fwblog.unxnu.com

The name of this team Blog is [Write... (FW-Bteam)]. The letters after the brackets mean: F=Free, which means freedom and randomness; W=Writing, which means writing; Bteam should be understood separately, B=Blog or Blogger, and team means group or team. I believe that after such an explanation, everyone will understand much more...

Write... (FW-Bteam) is a civilian Blogger team organization! It is different from ordinary Blogger organizations, which just collect the Blogs of its members for display. The concept of Write... (FW-Bteam) is: sharing, writing, freedom, and communication. In Write... (FW-Bteam), everyone has absolute freedom to write. Anyone (including visitors) can freely publish their own content. However, the writer must first understand our sharing spirit, so that everyone can freely write and communicate on the basis of sharing, and showcase your personal charm in the process of sharing.

Sharing: Voluntarily share your content with everyone for "appreciation, comments, and reprints" and make any legal changes on the basis of "re-attribution - non-commercial use - shared in the same way". If you do not want others to modify your content, please make sure to note it prominently in the main text when publishing the content.

Writing: On the basis of agreeing and abiding by the sharing principle, you can write the content you are willing to share with everyone. However, the prerequisite is: the content you share must have already been published on a known Blog that can be accessed normally, and when writing, fill in the effective address that can be accessed on the Internet.

Freedom: You can freely publish your content without worrying about external factors such as quantity, length, and time. However, freedom does not equal indulgence. Please pay attention to your words and deeds according to the laws of the People's Republic of China to ensure that you can normally and freely publish your content!

Communication: You can connect with everyone through our platform...
