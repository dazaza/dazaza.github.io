Louis Koo turns into "Smart Dog" and has new feelings for Big S

by baobao520 on 2009-07-29 14:41:48

Thirteen years later, Wong Jing once again released "Royal Tracers Lingling Dog", choosing Louis Koo to take the lead as the successor to Stephen Chow. Wong Jing also appeared very confident: "In 'Royal Tracers Lingling Dog', Louis Koo's comedic ability is not inferior to Stephen Chow. Stephen Chow can create his own classic style, and during the filming process, I can feel that Louis Koo is making efforts in this direction. I believe after watching this movie, the audience will have a new understanding of Louis Koo's comedic abilities, and I also believe that in the future, his comedies will become increasingly successful!"

"More handsome, younger girlfriend, more creative inventions"

In "Royal Tracers Lingling Dog", the secret agent code-named Lingling Dog played by Louis Koo is still someone who neglects martial arts and focuses on science, persisting in his belief in technology and diligently researching inventions, a scientific eccentric. The similar character design inevitably reminds people of the classic character Zero Zero Fa created by Stephen Chow 13 years ago in "Royal Tracer Zero Zero Fa". When asked about the differences between the two characters, Louis Koo joked, "I am handsomer than Stephen Chow, my girlfriend (Xiǎo S) is younger than his girlfriend (Carina Lau), and my inventions are more creative than his. My girlfriend has great kung fu skills and often protects me. The Lingling Dog I play is a role without martial arts but full of wisdom, creating many strange and peculiar inventions, some of which may even set trends, especially the invention of the transformer, which is extremely hilarious."

"Watch 'Royal Trainers Lingling Dog' and laugh heartily"

Director Derek Yee once lamented the lack of male actors in Hong Kong, while Director Wong Jing believed it was time for a generational change in Hong Kong's male comedy actors. In his view, Louis Koo is the best comedian actor in Hong Kong after Stephen Chow. After his excellent performances in the comedies "River East Lion Roar" and "The Best Bra", Louis Koo himself has developed a particular fondness for comedy, "I really like performing in comedies. But actually, acting in comedies is very difficult. On set, you need to communicate with the director and other actors, designing different dialogues and actions to perform well, which requires a lot of effort and careful planning. However, the happiest reward for me is when the audience laughs heartily. For example, when I go out to eat or shop, I haven't even said a word, and there are people looking at me and laughing, which makes me very happy because I know they like my performance in comedies. So I hope everyone comes to watch 'Royal Tracers Lingling Dog' and enjoys a good laugh."

"A new feeling towards DaS"

"Royal Tracers Lingling Dog" is the second collaboration between Louis Koo and DaS (Xu Xi Yuan) after "Keep Talking and Nobody Explodes". Louis Koo said, "Although DaS and I collaborated in 'Keep Talking and Nobody Explodes', we only had one real scene together, so it felt like we finished shooting without seeing each other much." This regret was fortunately resolved while shooting "Royal Tracers Lingling Dog", yet the first scene Louis Koo shot upon joining the production was DaS forcefully kissing him. Louis Koo said, "Our relationship in this movie is a complete reversal from 'Keep Talking and Nobody Explodes'. Last time, I protected her, this time, she protects me. Through our collaboration in 'Royal Tracers Lingling Dog', DaS has given me a completely new impression. I was surprised to discover that DaS can act in comedies so well. She is very dedicated, and her performances are wildly funny, quite different from her usual image of a noble lady."

The movie "Royal Tracers Lingling Dog" starring Louis Koo and Xu Xi Yuan (DaS) will be released in Hong Kong during the summer season on August 6th. In the film, DaS plays the fiancée of Louis Koo, trying to win his affection by seducing him, a task so nerve-wracking that she needed guidance from Louis Koo and Wong Jing. According to reports from Hong Kong's "Wen Hui Bao", there is a scene where Louis Koo is halfway through bathing when DaS suddenly appears, startling him, but DaS herself becomes nervous upon seeing Louis Koo. "Facing Louis Koo with his bare upper body dripping water, I always feel a bit embarrassed," said DaS. Therefore, Louis Koo often jokes around with DaS, splashing water on her to make her act more boldly, even laughing uncontrollably due to the butterfly bow towel on Louis Koo's head, causing multiple takes. Although they have previously collaborated in action films, this time shooting a comedy, DaS found Louis Koo's timing in comedic moments very precise, praising him as an invaluable multi-talented actor, and expressing her desire to learn more from Louis Koo.
