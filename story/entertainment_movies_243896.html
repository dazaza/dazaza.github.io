【Movie Introduction】: A fanatical priest founded the Kolun Church. He predicted that the Day of Judgment was near and secretly sent his assistant to Tierra del Fuego, the southernmost tip of America, to dig up the vampire queen buried underground for four hundred years, in order to kill "impure" sinners on behalf of the "Lord"... Female vampires who crave fresh and powerful hearts and blood don't have to go through the trouble of first getting their victim's "heart" like male vampires do, then getting their "body".

The cunning vampire queen Lily (played by Angie Everhart) hides in the basement of a monastery to practice her powers while running a brothel. Patrons must undergo an "entrance" ceremony led by a priest and pass the "coffin flying car" test. Those who slide into the basement are allowed to become guests of the topless ladies' club! For these patrons obsessed with "ghosts", the monastery has only an entrance, no exit. The townsfolk all think they are indulging in pleasure and forget to return. Young men in the town keep disappearing one after another, shrouding the monastery in a mysterious aura. A down-on-his-luck private detective, Ralph (played by Dennis Miller), volunteers to find the missing younger brother Caleb (played by Corey Feldman) for the anxious Catherine (played by Erika Eleniak). Unfortunately, Catherine is deceived by the charlatan priest Karren (played by Chris Sarandon) and blindly follows his so-called miracles, completely ignoring Detective Ralph's investigation report on the forbidden brothel of female vampires, ultimately leading to a chaotic battle between the female vampires, the persistent private detective, and the sorcerer's magic.
