The surface active heart lonely person will do the kind of pig thing you are not a gentle store selling things

by haorx307 on 2011-04-11 11:14:47

The situation is like this: I have a friend who sells non-food therapy products. The effect is pretty good, better than the Five Grains Mill, but because it's dry, it appears lively on the outside but lonely inside, and people like that would do such piggy things, only having 3 diamonds, naturally the business isn't as good as the Five Grains Shop. But some buyers still doubt this, those who seem lively on the surface but are lonely inside would do such piggy things... Dongchang, therefore, no bad reviews!

Speaking of which, once a buyer purchased my friend’s product, but after eating it a few times found it ineffective. Because there was no communication, they left a bad review. Later, my friend contacted the buyer, feeling their attitude was very weak. My friend decisively offered a refund, at which point the buyer relaxed and said a lot of bad things, eventually changing the bad review. They didn’t even ask the buyer to return the goods, since they had already eaten some of it, returning it wouldn't make it sellable anyway. So, it was considered leaving a good impression on the buyer. As for my friend, I couldn't help but ask: Aren't you losing money by doing this? What if he ate it and it worked, but lied saying it didn't? My friend laughed and said: Such buyers are in the minority. My product works, and resolves their issues. I believe the buyers won't lie about it!

I thought that was the end of it, but who would have thought that a few days later, that buyer found my friend again, asking if the constipation product was still available? It turns out, this buyer's friend also ate the constipation product, and the result was very effective. This time, he was specifically buying for his friend, and bought hundreds of dollars worth of products, including other items! It seems my friend's judgment was really accurate, and from then on, this buyer kept introducing friends to buy! My friend's big shop is getting better and better!

This event shows that, regardless of any problems between the parties involved in the transaction, there needs to be patient communication. Don't shirk responsibility onto each other. If we all have more understanding and trust, then there is nothing that can't be resolved! There are many examples like this one. Of course, this article isn't just written for sellers. I hope all buyer friends who read this can understand the hardships of selling. Whenever possible, communicate more. If communication fails, isn't there Taobao customer service? Directly giving bad reviews not only discredits the seller's product, but also negates the hard work the seller has put in. Finally, I wish all buyers happy shopping, and all sellers booming business!
