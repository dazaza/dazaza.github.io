The Network Marketing Management Institute, specializes in cultivating practical talents in network marketing.

The Network Marketing Management Institute (http://www.cnseoo.net) is co-organized by Hebei Net Communication Education and China Network Marketing Management Network, specializing in network marketing planning and training.

China Network Marketing Management Network is a professional educational institution that provides society with skills training such as network marketing and mobile phone repair. It is also a professional network marketing consulting service company and a leading domestic "overall solution provider for network marketing". The service scope includes overall solutions for enterprise network marketing, industry network marketing, career training for network marketing talents, and enterprise network brand services. Based on years of teaching experience and combined with the employment needs of today's IT enterprises, it is committed to cultivating technical and capable network marketing talents for society. China Network Marketing Management Network is currently the largest, most visited, richest in resources, and most diverse in learning methods for network marketing learning portal websites. Its comprehensive simulation learning environment, distinctive homework system, and new evaluation methods have won the welcome of teachers, students, and professionals, and received positive feedback from every certified network marketing professional.

The knowledge system of network marketing training is based on practice, aiming to cultivate urgently needed talents in society who possess hands-on ability, network marketing project management ability, and practical experience in network marketing trade. The Network Marketing Management Institute ensures the normal development of the project system and timely provides high-quality services to each training institution and student through its rigorous training, certification, and management guarantee system; it ensures the professional level of certified faculty through its strict registered trainer system; certified students receive long-term continuous services through certification, and being responsible for each student is our unwavering commitment.

Currently, the Network Marketing Management Institute officially offers nationwide intensive classes, advanced classes, and corporate classes for "Network Marketing." The institute also provides nearly 20 specialized modules of distance education based on personalized needs, such as search engine optimization, keyword marketing, blog marketing, website diagnosis, website construction, and online promotion professional marketing training courses. The institute also opens personal blogs and college communities to provide all students and working professionals who love e-commerce and network marketing with a platform for sharing and exchange.
