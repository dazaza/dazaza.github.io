Cardinal Road高级男装品牌零售公司

by wuhuishen1j on 2012-02-14 14:41:05

Cardinluo (002656.SZ) is a domestic professional high-end menswear brand retail company. Since its establishment, it has mainly been engaged in the research and design of its own brand Cardinluo (CANUDILO) high-end menswear, organizing outsourced production, brand promotion, and terminal sales; while also conducting agency sales business for international first-tier brands. The company has always adhered to the design concept of "refinement, simplicity, and quality", paying attention to exchanges with internationally renowned designer teams, insisting on cooperating with internationally renowned fabric suppliers and high-quality contract manufacturers, relying on leading product design capabilities and outstanding product quality, continuously enhancing the reputation and renown of the Cardinluo (CANUDILO) brand. In recent years, the sales of Cardinluo products have grown rapidly. In 2009, 2010, and 2011, the company's operating revenue was 2.489105 billion yuan, 3.370676 billion yuan, and 4.614687 billion yuan respectively. In 2010 and 2011, the growth compared to the previous year was 35.42% and 36.91%, achieving a faster-than-industry growth record. The funds raised this time will mainly be used for marketing network construction projects and information system technology renovation projects. After the implementation of the project, the number of flagship stores and standard stores can reach more than 450, covering all provincial capitals, municipalities directly under the central government, as well as economically developed cities. This can enhance the company's brand image, broaden the marketing network, and strengthen the company's profitability. The main reasons for the growth in the company's operating benefits are: the enhancement of capital strength and the integration of assets have improved the company's overall strength and ability to open new stores, resulting in a significant increase in the number of stores in 2008 compared to the previous year; the company has increased the market promotion efforts of the Cardinluo (CANUDILO) brand, enhancing brand awareness and increasing the average sales amount per store; with the expansion of the company's scale, economies of scale become more prominent; with the enhancement of brand recognition, the company's product pricing ability has strengthened, leading to an upward trend in product gross margin. Cardinluo is committed to becoming an international high-end fashion menswear brand retailer. In recent years, with the rapid improvement of the company's store expansion and management capabilities, supply chain management capabilities, as well as the continuous accumulation of high-quality suppliers and channel resources, the business scale has developed rapidly. As of December 31, 2011, the company had opened 318 stores in high-end department stores, high-end shopping malls, major airports, and five-star hotels in large and medium-sized cities across the country and in Hong Kong and Macao regions, including 175 directly-operated stores (including 5 international brand agency stores) and 143 franchise stores. Currently, Cardinluo has become one of the few high-end Taobao menswear brand retailers in China that fully covers the core retail business districts of first-tier cities. The company has established stable long-term cooperative relationships with landmark high-end chain department stores such as Renhe Spring Department Store, Wangfujing Department Store (600859), Xin World Department Store (600628), Shanghai Bailian, Guangzhou Friendship (000987), Hunan Friendship Apollo, Zhuozhan Group, Yintai Department Store, Shenzhen MixC, Nanjing Golden Eagle, Paris Spring, etc., laying a good foundation for the continuous expansion of the company's market share. While expanding its marketing network, Cardinluo pays attention to enhancing brand control and leveraging the radiation effect of directly-operated stores, adopting a channel model of "mainly developing directly-operated stores in first- and second-tier cities, and mainly developing franchise stores in other cities" to ensure the sustainability and relative stability of the company's marketing network advantages. Cardinluo has established a mature store expansion and management standardization system, ensuring the efficient and rapid expansion of the company's stores. The company has accumulated rich and practical experience in directly-operated store expansion, forming a complete set of store expansion standards and processes, including market analysis, store location selection, cooperation models, environmental design, prop design, settlement systems, and other aspects.
