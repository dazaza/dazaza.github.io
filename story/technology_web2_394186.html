How to Increase Traffic to Your Online Store

by anonymous on 2011-03-28 09:21:05

1. Diligence

Diligence refers to our passion for opening a store. Since the online shop initially lacks regular customers and has no fame, we should first focus on improving the internal aspects of the shop, promoting it more, and not giving up on any customer, even those who just come to inquire. You never know, they might buy your products one day.

2. Brainstorming

Brainstorming refers to continuously identifying issues during the operation of an online store. Our shop should make good use of search engines since most internet users look for products through them. For example, if someone wants to buy a [mobile phone], they may search for [mobile phone] on a search engine. You can check the ranking of your product name. If the ranking is not good, ask yourself why your product's keyword ranking is so low. Here, it is suggested that the online store system should ideally generate HTML pages.

Every time you upload a product, you can use Baidu keyword queries to analyze your product’s keywords. It is important to emphasize that instead of focusing on single names like [digital camera], which would be hard to rank in the top 3 pages in the short term, consider using something like [Sony digital camera latest price]. This way, this product introduction page could potentially bring some sales. The more products you have, the better the effect will be because the more products there are, the more search engines will index, bringing more traffic and increasing sales.

3. Persistence

Many people give up on their online stores after a few months or half a year mainly because they don't earn much money in the early stages and cannot persist. Initially, most people have idealistic views about online stores, given the numerous fantasies associated with them. Once reality deviates from these ideals, it becomes easy to abandon the idea.

In fact, within half a year, you might already have some customers. If the after-sales service is good and the product quality passes inspection, repeat customers will still return. Moreover, customers might refer others, leading to better sales. Successfully running an online store at www.mideso.com is not something that can be achieved in a day or two, with a few words. Observing physical stores reveals that most rely on repeat customers or referrals. They impress customers with their service, so we should approach our online stores with a calm mindset, which often yields unexpected results.

4. Inform Family and Friends

If you’ve opened an online store, do your family know? Do your classmates and friends know? It’s essential to inform them as soon as possible. If you tell 100 people, each of them will tell more, spreading the word rapidly. There will be countless people knowing about your store in a short time. Leave messages in alumni records and share the joy of opening an online store with your family and classmates. Don’t worry about not having friends; even a hitman had elementary school classmates.

5. Properly Exchange Friend Links

After running the store for a while, you can privately contact others to exchange friendship links. By exchanging store links, you can form a small network, enhancing each other’s influence. Try to choose stores that are not in the same category to avoid competition and promote mutual growth. If the other party’s store name is too long, affecting the overall aesthetics of your store, you can appropriately modify the displayed name. This method can bring a certain amount of traffic to your online store.

6. Reasonably Plan Product Release Times

Releasing products is a science. It can be exaggeratedly said that releasing products outside peak times is equivalent to not releasing them at all. Peak times are generally: 10:00-11:00, 14:00-17:30, 19:30-22:00.

Some say the number of products should vary by person, but I disagree. Isn't having more products better? Quantity should be high but without repetition. The advantage of having more products lies in showcasing your strength. Buyers find products by entering keywords, and Taobao's natural sorting is based on remaining time. Generally, buyers only browse the first three pages. If many of your products appear on these three pages, it’s unlikely they won’t visit your store.

For example, if you release many products during peak hours today, then a week later, during the peak hours of the same day, many of your products will appear on the first three pages. Of course, set the time to seven days and don’t be lazy. If you do this every day for seven days, then during the peak hours each day, your products will appear on the first three pages (you need to release the products over these seven days).

Pricing also needs attention (it shouldn't disrupt market order, as Taobao is very adept at handling such situations). Because what buyers use most is 'price from low to high sorting,' if your product lacks price advantages, sorting will render your efforts useless.

Product descriptions must not be overlooked.

Product descriptions and store decoration go hand in hand. I'll discuss both here. Product descriptions create the first impression for customers. The name should be practical rather than merely pleasant-sounding. Thirty characters should be well utilized; don't waste them.

Product descriptions should include as many real images as possible, detailing functions and features while clearly stating precautions, reducing future troubles.

When writing product descriptions, pay attention to font size and color, making it clear for customers (if I can't see it clearly, I'll mark it with an X).

Store decoration should, of course, be as aesthetically pleasing as possible to attract people. More importantly, let buyers feel your professionalism and reliability.

7. Joint Promotions

Simple friendship links placed on the homepage of the store have limited effects and generally do not result in actual sales. If store owners engage in joint promotions, they can mutually benefit, not only increasing store traffic but also boosting cash flow. When several sellers collaborate on joint promotions, different effects can be achieved. Try to find complementary stores. For instance, a photography book specialty store can cooperate with a digital camera specialty store. Hang recommendations for each other's products on the product pages. If possible, add attractive text introductions. For example, sdf1206 held an event: Good news! Our store has launched a new activity. Customers purchasing digital cameras from our store can add 15 yuan to select a professional photography book from the "Premium Store" (in the friendship link on our homepage). (Price limit is within 30 yuan), which we will purchase at the original price, and the bookstore owner will directly mail it to you! Note: If the price exceeds 30 yuan, the excess amount will be paid by the buyer to our store, within 30 yuan only 15 yuan is required, the rest is paid by our store.

8. Take Initiative, Seek Buyers

Business isn't about waiting; often, we can seek buyers. Some buyers prefer to use purchase requests, so you can frequently check the request marketplace to see if they are looking for items you sell. Visit related or unrelated forums to understand people's preferences through their articles. Finding the right people means achieving half the success. Fully understand what you operate and study who would need them. Avoid blindly posting advertisements (which is a violation in many forums).

9. Participate in Activities, Bring Traffic

Specialized forum communities are our stages for activities. Read posts carefully and thoughtfully respond. Reading posts increases knowledge, and the benefits of responding don't need elaboration. (Just a reminder: posts should not be aimless or take too long to get to the point. Be detailed and easy to understand, ensuring readers understand what you're saying. You can repost highly popular threads from other forums.) Forums have daily activities, so stay updated on the latest dynamics. www.mideso.com essay contests, charity sales, you can sponsor prizes or donate. This is similar to manufacturers sponsoring sports events, a win-win situation. Be an active member, letting more people know about you. (Remember to read Taobao rules.)

Join Business Alliances, Move Forward as a Team

Business alliances consist of sellers from a particular city or a specific category of sellers. You need to first apply to Taobao, and once approved, you can appear as a collective entity. Joining business alliances has many benefits, allowing you to meet many sellers. The alliance leader regularly organizes gatherings or training exchanges, which is the fastest way for newcomers to grow. The Taobao homepage also has a dedicated page for business alliances, providing more exposure opportunities.

10. Print Business Cards

Print your own business cards and include a few when mailing products to buyers. If they are appealing, they might be collected and even recommended to others. If you have a physical store, consider placing business cards there for others to take freely. Even when they leave your store, they might continue browsing online. Conversely, the same applies.

11. Use Forums and Blogs More Often

Forums hide many potential buyers, so don't overlook their importance. Remember to set your avatar and signature properly and make them visually appealing. Combine them with good posts, whether they are original or replies (mutual benefits). Others will notice you. www.mideso.com share your business experiences, the ups and downs of operating on Taobao, the joys of reading and listening to music, etc. Regularly update your signature to notify others of the latest policies in your store.

Nowadays, almost every website has forums, and blogs can be applied for by anyone, making it free advertising!

12. Taobao Wangwang, Also a Promotion Platform

Many people have good businesses because Wangwang is always online, providing convenient communication. When you're away from your computer, change your status to away or others, and remember to set up an automatic reply. The advantage is that if the other party receives the automatic reply, they'll know you're not available and won't continue sending messages. If you don't set it up, buyers might think you're unresponsive and go to other sellers. Remember to utilize statuses and automatic replies.

13. Utilize Search Engines

Perhaps you've never thought about finding your store through search engines? Some opportunities need to be seized.

Frequent Baidu users might notice a section called Tieba, which is essentially a forum. Unlike typical forums, Baidu's Tieba is mostly self-organized. That is, every word can become a Tieba.

When you input content like 8210, "Heaven Without Thieves," or "hand-drawn" in Baidu, corresponding Tiebas will appear on the right side. If none exist temporarily, you'll be prompted to create one. If it already exists, the latest three posts will be shown.

14. Email Signature

Email advertising is currently popular, but never use mass email distribution, as it's easily blacklisted or reported. This isn't an exaggeration.

You can add a signature to your emails and change it periodically. Recipients (friends) might inadvertently be attracted. I once added signatures to text messages I sent to friends, almost frustrating them (later I stopped doing it).

15. Unique Strategies

During university, there was a small barbecue shop nearby whose owner was skilled in management and promotion. Each city has radio song-request programs, and their boss frequently called to request songs, always adding at the end: "Welcome to Small Hat Barbecue, our address is xxx, phone number is xxx." A call costs only a few cents, but everyone listening to the program hears this advertisement. Compared to those who spend money on radio ads, this saves money while being effective. Consider whether there are resources around you that can be utilized?

16. Fun Advertising

A friend from another school told me: there were two pancake vendors near their school. One always shouted out promotions, while the other adopted a different strategy. He got a blackboard that read: "Pancake, silence is golden"; before and after the New Year, it changed to: "Pancake, come home often"; for a while, he disappeared, and when he returned, the blackboard read: "Pancake, back in the game..."

When your products don't differ significantly from others, you can advertise uniquely. If one day I see him and his blackboard on the street, I will definitely buy a pancake just for the nostalgia his story brings me over the years.

17. Personal Space

The new version of personal space has many functions, so make full use of it. Its role is second only to its community, serving as your platform to showcase yourself. Many customers come specifically for your personal space.
