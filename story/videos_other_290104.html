Informed person said that a worship place of Leslie Cheung is in Chengdu, and since his death 6 years ago, people keep coming to pay homage there

by djbgufgire on 2009-12-03 11:26:55

Insiders said that the spirit tablet of Leslie Cheung is in Chengdu, and people have been paying their respects for six years since his death. (Photo on file) On March 31st, 2009, fans mourned as usual for the sixth anniversary of Leslie Cheung's death. "Leslie Cheung's spirit tablet is inside the Wenshu Monastery, and it was established in the year he passed away!" Upon hearing this news, I was as shocked as you are! Recently, an insider informed a reporter that for six years, the brother has been by our side all along. The spirit tablet is in Chengdu; could his ashes also be here? Yesterday, the reporter went to Wenshu Monastery to investigate... There are actually two spirit tablets. According to the tipster, Leslie Cheung's spirit tablet is within the Jile Hall crypt at Wenshu Monastery. The Jile Hall crypt is located behind the monastery, and after descending more than four meters underground via the steps, one finds oneself in a brightly lit crypt where there is a solemn Buddha statue at the main position, while both sides are filled with countless spirit tablets... There is no inscription; how can it be proven that this is Leslie Cheung's spirit tablet? "This indeed is Leslie Cheung's spirit tablet." A staff member from the Jile Hall crypt then confirmed this claim to the reporter, but he told the reporter that Leslie Cheung's ashes are not inside. He stated that the reason there are two spirit tablets is because within the spirit tablet at row 18, number 5, there is a memorial tablet with Leslie Cheung's image, while inside the spirit tablet at row 19, number 5, there are albums and singles released by Leslie Cheung during his lifetime, as well as small gifts sent by fans to accompany him..."There are no ashes of Leslie Cheung; essentially, this is equivalent to Mr. Zhang's symbolic tomb." For six years, people have been paying respects continuously... Why is Leslie Cheung's spirit tablet enshrined in the Jile Hall crypt at Wenshu Monastery in Chengdu? Was it Leslie Cheung's wish during his lifetime, or an arrangement made by his relatives? "Neither." The staff member explained that the establishment of these two spirit tablets has no relation to Leslie Cheung or his family members. It was set up through donations by Leslie Cheung's fans in Sichuan. In 2003, when Leslie Cheung passed away, fans in Sichuan spontaneously raised more than 10,000 yuan and established these two spirit tablets here to facilitate nearby worship by fans. Since the establishment of these two spirit tablets, word has spread among fans, and frequently fans from all over Sichuan come to pay their respects, sometimes even fans from other provinces show up. Every April 1st, many fans gather to pay respects, and on this day, fans send a large number of flowers, so much so that sometimes there are too many to fit in the venue... Later, the reporter conducted online research and found that many people in the fan circle in Sichuan know about the spirit tablet of Leslie Cheung enshrined in Wenshu Monastery, and quite a few fans have already paid their respects there. There are at least a dozen other places with Leslie Cheung's spirit tablets. Subsequently, the reporter discovered through online searches that apart from Chengdu, fans in many places have enshrined similar spirit tablets locally. Nanjing Linggu Temple, Hangzhou Shangtianzhu Faxi Temple, Zhengzhou Yunmeng Mountain Resort (Zhengzhou Cemetery), Shanghai Yuanming Lecture Hall, Wuhan Baotong Temple, Changsha Lushan Temple, Jilin Beishan Yuhuang Pavilion, Beijing Guanghua Temple, Harbin Ji Le Buddhist Temple, Wuxi Nanchan Temple, Hong Kong Po Lin Monastery... Fans from various places not only voluntarily donated money to establish Leslie Cheung's spirit tablets, but also shared information to inform fans who need to pay their respects about the exact location of the spirit tablets in each place.
