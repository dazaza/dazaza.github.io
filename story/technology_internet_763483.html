Redmi smartphone officially released: 799 yuan / quad-core / 4.7 inches

by anonymous on 2013-08-02 10:22:29

At 2 o'clock this afternoon, Xiaomi jointly released with QQ Zone the much-anticipated Redmi smartphone. The detailed specifications of the Redmi phone have already been announced at the press conference, and are basically consistent with previous rumors.

According to Xiaomi, the Redmi phone is positioned for the general public, focusing on user experience and high cost-performance, with top-tier suppliers and components. The choice of TD dual SIM dual standby was made because 68% of Xiaomi users are using China Mobile cards. In addition, Xiaomi also stated that the thousand-yuan phone segment is the main battleground for smartphones, and they hope that the Redmi phone can change the public's impression, striving to be the best experience in the thousand-yuan phone category.

According to official information from Xiaomi, the Redmi phone will be equipped with a 4.7-inch IPS Retina display (1280x720 resolution, 312PPI, Corning second-generation Gorilla Glass), powered by a quad-core 1.5G MediaTek MT6589T Turbo processor (GPU is PowerVR SGX 544), with 1GB RAM + 4GB ROM, supporting up to 32GB TF card expansion.

The Redmi phone also features an 8-megapixel rear camera (Samsung/OV sensor, back-illuminated, F2.2 large aperture, supports 1080p recording) and a 1.3-megapixel front-facing camera (back-illuminated, supports 720p recording). The battery capacity is 2000mAh, supporting TD-SCDMA+GSM dual SIM dual standby, running MIUI V5 system.

In addition, the Redmi phone is expected to offer three color options for users to choose from: metal gray, ivory white, and China red. The price is 799 yuan, with the first booking starting on QQ Zone this afternoon, officially launching on August 12th, with an initial batch of approximately 100,000 units.
