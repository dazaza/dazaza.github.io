**multiupload.com helps you upload files to multiple free online storage services**

Many people use multiple free cloud storage services simultaneously. On the one hand, it may be because the files they want to upload are important, and they don't want to lose them due to issues with a single storage service. On the other hand, it could be because the file is large, so they split it and store parts in different cloud storage services.

**multiupload.com** is an excellent online tool that can help you upload files to multiple free cloud storage services. These include Rapidshare, Megaupload, Mediafire, Shared, and more. Of course, these are all foreign free online storage services.

1. First, open the website: http://www.multiupload.com/. You'll notice there are no annoying ads, and the page is clean and simple. You don't need to register to use multiupload.com, but the site does offer a Multiupload Filemanager tool for registered users, which is quite convenient.

**swfcabin – Free Online SWF File Upload and Sharing Space**

Currently, uploading and sharing videos online is very popular, and there are many websites providing such services. However, if you have SWF files that you need to upload and share, you can try the free cloud storage space **swfcabin** introduced by Free Resources Tribe.

**swfcabin** is a specialized website for uploading and sharing Swf files. You can upload your own Swf files to this site, and the site will provide a viewing link. Send this link to your friends, and they can watch the Swf file online by clicking it. Of course, you can also search for and enjoy excellent Swf files created by others.

**How to Use swfcabin:**

1. First, open the website: http://www.swfcabin.com/. At the bottom of the page, click "Select File" to choose a file from your local computer to upload. You can set the Swf title and decide whether to allow voting or make it searchable, as shown in the image below.
