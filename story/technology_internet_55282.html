IDC Industry Continues to Rectify in 2008, Internet Basic Services Become More Rational

by now on 2008-03-20 10:15:57

In 2007, the IDC industry underwent a large-scale cleanup and rectification. A number of IDC service providers had their data centers urgently shut down due to issues such as websites hosted on virtual hosts and servers not being registered with the ICP (Internet Content Provider), or containing illegal information. A batch of websites faced atypical shutdowns, and chaotic market operations were also deterred during this wave of rectification. After the rectification, the order of the IDC industry began to be restructured. Despite the continued industry rectification by 13 ministries in 2008, the IDC industry still developed rapidly with an unstoppable momentum.

The IDC is a massive industry. Industry insiders analyzed that IDC has attractive market prospects. By December 31, 2007, the number of Internet users in mainland China reached 210 million, ranking second in the world just 5 million people behind the United States. It was expected to surpass the United States in 2008 and become the country with the largest number of Internet users globally. The huge Internet user market awaits development, and many people are considering how to get a share of it. Internet basic services have been brought to the core of Internet applications, and the market situation is very promising. This has attracted wave after wave of network agents into the field of network basic services.

Despite the joint efforts of the Ministry of Public Security, the Central Propaganda Department, the Ministry of Information Industry, and 10 other ministries in 2008 to continue the rectification of the IDC industry and further carry out activities to crack down on and rectify the Internet according to law across the country, the network basic service market remains strong. A person in charge of the channel department of NowCN (www.now.cn) analyzed that "in a regulated Internet environment, competition in the IDC industry will become more intense and orderly. Short-sighted business practices aimed at illegally earning short-term benefits will significantly decrease. Numerous network agents will also act cautiously when choosing major service providers, no longer prioritizing low prices but paying more attention to factors affecting the continuous operation and development of their upstream agents, such as credibility and service."

For mainstream service providers of Internet basic services, these more rational network agents have become an indispensable force for their channel development. The competition over network agent channels among major service providers is fierce. Experts analyzed that having outstanding agency development and management strategies can help stand out in fierce competition. The rapid growth of NowCN's channel also verifies this market rule. NowCN adopts an attitude of "cultivating," "supporting," and "helping" towards agents, aiming to create more opportunities and benefits for them. Recently, NowCN launched a promotion for its agents: pay 10,000 yuan and immediately receive a 2,000-yuan corporate email account as a first experience. Additionally, the .com domain price for agents has been reduced to the lowest in the industry at 49 yuan, establishing a prominent channel advantage within the sector.

With the rapid development of China's Internet, the rectification of the IDC industry is inevitable. In an increasingly purified environment, whether it’s domestic mainstream service providers or agents at all levels, they should seize the opportunity to develop rationally and sustainably, jointly creating a better future for Internet services.
