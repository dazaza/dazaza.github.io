Mission Hills Haikou Resort launches preferential package to enjoy the fun of golfing on volcanic rock

by golf8279 on 2010-06-01 18:28:35

Sohu Sports News: Experience the fun of swinging on volcanic rocks in advance. The Mission Hills Haikou Resort offers a variety of discount packages.

You may have experienced the feeling of swinging in the wilderness, desert sand, or conquered a pure links course, but if you enjoy challenging different courses, you will definitely want to experience the fun of swinging on volcanic rocks that have been dormant for ten thousand years.

The rugged and primitive black volcanic stones, lush ancient lychee forests, endless horizon lines, distant vaguely visible volcanic craters, low and simple stone walls, and underground springs with surging undercurrents make up a completely unique course style. Nature's masterful craftsmanship has created a completely different golf course style. The Mission Hills • Haikou International Golf Resort is such a special place where you can experience unprecedented golfing fun.

From March 18-20, the "Golf and Tourism Theme Forum" of the Boao Forum for Asia was successfully held at the Mission Hills Haikou Resort. Elite representatives from the global golf and tourism industries, as well as guests from all walks of life gathered together to offer advice on the development of world golf and the relationship between golf and tourism. The Mission Hills Haikou Resort received unanimous praise from the attending guests for its uniquely characteristic courses, complete facilities, and professional organizational reception capabilities.

Currently completed facilities at the Mission Hills Haikou Resort include an 18-story luxury resort hotel and a 9-story annex, a three-story clubhouse, global cuisine, and hot spring water therapy, along with three championship-level courses. From now until September 30, 2010, the Mission Hills Haikou Resort specially offers a variety of experience packages to provide global golf tourism enthusiasts with the opportunity to experience it first-hand.
