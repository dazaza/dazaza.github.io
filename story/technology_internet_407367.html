Host - Wu Changzhao: Teacher Yue, how should a newly opened company choose a domain name? Is it good to choose a pure English name?

Guest - Yue Hao: As a new company, when choosing a domain name, you must consider the product being developed. First, the domain name should be short and easy to remember, preferably limited to within 7-8 characters. "Loschina" meets this condition and is relatively easy to remember.

Secondly, the domain name should have some significance and can be associated with the product or enterprise. "Los" is a phonetic approximation of "Luo," while "china" here is somewhat勉强 (forced), but could be considered if doing foreign trade. If only doing domestic trade, it's recommended to find a more meaningful domain name, such as "lostie." "Tie" is the abbreviation for the English word "tie" (as in a necktie). With one glance at the domain name, people will know what the business is about, and it will also be easier to remember.
