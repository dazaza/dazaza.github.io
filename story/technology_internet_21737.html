by yinyin on 2007-06-06 13:42:27

Hello! QQ bookmarks (http://shuqian.qq.com/) has launched a lot of new features, are waiting for you to experience.

1. Directly access QQ bookmarks on the QQ panel:

Now you can in the QQ bookmark home page, click on the right "put my bookmark on QQ" button, you can use their own bookmark collection on QQ, very convenient.

2. List of Heroes:

Want to know what everyone's been hiding these days? Want to know who's been searching the most lately? Look at the "collection hero list" on the right side of the QQ bookmark home page, maybe we have some common interests.

3. Cool Sites of the day:

Recommend the latest, coolest and most interesting websites for you every day.

Thank you very much for your support for QQ bookmark at the same time, if you are in use, if you have any questions and Suggestions, welcome to the QQ bookmarks feedback forum: http://shuqian.bbs.qq.com/
