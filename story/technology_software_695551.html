Website Optimization by Yi Xiang: How should webmasters deal with the problem of too many ads on their website?

by jackntav9612 on 2012-03-07 16:00:54

Among the user harms, the greatest one should be pop-up ads. Here I will introduce two types of pop-up ads. The first type is that when you open a page, a new page will pop up. This kind of pop-up ad is the most harmful to user experience, and some friends who are not very good at surfing the Internet even think that their computers have been infected with viruses again? This kind of pop-up ad that hurts users is naturally disliked by search engines. Search engines have explicitly proposed to reduce the weight of this kind of ad because it completely violates the user's wishes under the circumstances that the user is unaware and unwilling. It is also not an ad that the user can control. Search engines absolutely cannot tolerate it.

The other type is exit-intent ads. The form of this ad is that when you enter a website and close a webpage, a new page will pop up. The nature of this ad is as bad as the pop-up ad introduced earlier. It is not liked by search engines, or even hated. I once had such an experience. That was a traffic site, and I also made pop-up ads. When I went to exchange friendly links with others, the other party actually refused to exchange friendly links with me because my site had pop-up ads. This gave me a small shock because the other party thought that sites generally making pop-up ads were some junk sites, so they did not exchange with me. Therefore, websites making pop-up ads can basically be labeled with the same tag as junk sites. Because of this, I immediately removed the pop-up ads that I just put up not long ago. Because pop-up ads cause the greatest harm to search engines, I introduced them a little more here. I suggest all webmasters not to make pop-up ads.

There is another type called floating ads. This kind of ad is also harmful to search engines because it affects the loading speed of webpages. Google has now clearly proposed that the loading speed of a website will affect the keyword ranking of a website. Another aspect is that floating ads will affect users' browsing, which is very harmful to users. If you really want to make this kind of ad, then you'd better rent a better server. Speed up the opening speed of the website, reduce the impact of this floating ad to a minimum. On the other hand, try to reduce the size of this floating ad as much as possible, so that users can browse your website pages more conveniently.

Currently, there are more and more animated ads. Baidu and Google have now launched this kind of ad. This kind of ad is also very unfavorable to websites and has a greater impact than image ads. The reason is that it will seriously affect the quality of webpages. In addition, if you play this ad, you need to load it. Baidu plays automatically, while Google is more humanized. You need to click to play.

There are also many picture ads. Picture ads are similar in nature to the floating ads introduced earlier, but they are better than floating ads because they do not follow users like floating ads and do not affect users' webpage browsing.

Text-based ads should be relatively better among the previous few types of ads, but I can't make too many because it will affect the page quality. Let me give a simple example. I once saw a riddle-guessing website where the main content of a webpage was only a few words, and the most was no more than twenty or thirty words, but the number of words in its text ads was far beyond the main content of the webpage. Later, I checked its website's inclusion, which was pitifully low. Judging from the situation, a large part of the reason should be that the number of words in its text ads exceeded the main content. Here, I don't recommend a website to make too many text ads. The correct practice should be not to exceed 30% of the main content.
