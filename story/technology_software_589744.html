Classic toast: Wishing friends

by chunjie55 on 2011-11-27 09:46:52

● In this season of blessings, I sincerely make three wishes for you: a beautiful wish for you - happiness in succession; a wonderful wish for you - all things go well; a beautiful wish for you - sweet smiles. ● Don't let time dilute the wine of friendship, don't let distance pull apart the hand of missing, I bless you, forever, forever... ● In every sleepless night I think of you, friend! Remember the days we spent together, the singing when the wind rises, and the heartache when the tears fall! ● Adversity has a scientific value, and a good scholar will not give up such an opportunity to learn. This famous saying once gave me courage and strength, and now I give it to you. ● There is a kind of falling called standing up, a kind of loss called harvest, a kind of failure called success - be strong, my friend, tomorrow will belong to you! ● I quietly approach you, for fear of disturbing your peace, the New Year's bell tolls from afar, do you hear it? That's my blessing for you. ● You give me the purity of moonlight; I give you the passion of the sun. Friend, I hope our friendship lasts as long as the sun and moon shine in the sky, unchanged forever. ● Deep friendship and blessings, continuous thoughts and greetings, on this beautiful day, I bring these wishes with this card to you far away. ● My heart has three wishes: one is that friendship warms the heart, the second is that happiness stays forever, the third is that I often meet you. ● Dream what you want to dream, go where you want to go, do what you want to do, because life only comes once, opportunities won't come again! ● Success in life is not about getting a good hand, but about how to play a bad hand well. ● I quietly get close to you, for fear of disturbing your peace, the sound of the New Year's bells coming from afar, can you hear it? That's my blessing for you. ● You give me the purity of moonlight; I give you the passion of the sun. Friend, I hope our friendship lasts like the sun and moon shining in the sky, constant and unchanging. ● Deep affection and blessings, continuous thoughts and greetings, on this beautiful day, I bring these wishes with this card to you who are far away. ● Blue, in the sky, is so clear and transparent, it represents hope; blue, in the sea, is so deep and steady, it represents perseverance. May your life always be filled with blue. ● I offer sincere blessings to my most missed friend, haven't seen you for a long time, how have you been recently? Wish every moment of your time is filled with safety and happiness. ● Having gone through so many storms, frost has inadvertently crept into your temples, that touch of heart-wrenching white, I want to tell you, take care of yourself, everything is fine, the rest, doesn't matter!
