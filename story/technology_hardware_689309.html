Semtech introduced phase shift LED backlighting for Ultrabooks.

by ledlamp8 on 2012-03-03 23:43:21

The SC5010 can use a miniature inductor that is 1mm tall and 2.2μH, enabling an efficient and ultra-thin display with a thickness of just 4mm. On September 1, 2011, in Camarillo, California, Semtech Corporation, a leading provider of analog and mixed-signal semiconductors, announced the first device in its innovative phase-shift-boost LED driver platform. The new SC5010 is an 8-channel white LED driver that uses an innovative PWM transpose light control method, allowing the use of an ultra-thin inductor. This enables LED drivers to be directly mounted on super-thin LCD panels (Ultrabooks) and ultra-mobile PCs (UMPCs). The new SC5010 extends battery life in Tablet PCs, automotive, and other medium-sized display applications by enabling ultra-thin LCD displays.

When using inductors of the same size as those in existing phase-shift-based LED drivers, the SC5010 provides higher efficiency. Unlike other devices, the SC5010 maintains high efficiency even when using ultra-small inductors. This allows LED drivers to be directly embedded within thin panels, including those as thin as 4mm. This is due to its advanced control loop structure, which permits the use of small inductors, such as a 2.2μH inductor (height 1mm).

SC5010's phase shift technology minimizes input and output capacitors while providing the same phase shift efficiency as traditional non-switching regulators. This technology has very low noise, low EMI (electromagnetic interference), and does not require noise filtering components. According to Athar Zaidi, Director of Marketing for Semtech's Power Management Division, "Until now, this has been a barrier for super-thin and other very small-sized PCs. The SC5010 offers an advanced phase-shift LED driver solution, significantly reducing the trade-off between inductor size and display efficiency."

The SC5010 is an 8-channel boost LED driver capable of powering up to 112 LEDs per channel with currents up to 30mA. The device’s integrated control loop architecture supports programmable switching frequencies up to 2.2MHz, which is key to achieving very small and thin inductors. Additionally, the design eliminates the need for external compensation and noise filtering components, reducing the size and cost of the BOM (Bill of Materials).

Semtech's innovative circuit architecture prevents line transients (common in automotive applications) from causing display flickering or shutdowns. The SC5010 features ultra-fast transient response and can handle line transients up to 10V/μs.

The SC5010 is designed for next-generation display markets and includes a rich feature set, such as I2C flexibility and three PWM dimming control modes. At 200Hz, it offers a 5000:1 PWM wide input adjustment range with a dimming frequency range from 100Hz to 30kHz. The SC5010 also provides user-selectable 9-bit or 10-bit dimming resolution, along with a 5-bit analog dimming register, enhancing dimming resolution through both registers and PWM.

The phase-shift PWM dimming method operates by turning LED strings on and off at the same frequency and duty cycle but calculates the phase difference between different LED strings automatically. This reduces the requirement for input and output capacitors, decreases radiated switching current, improves EMI performance, and enhances dimmer linearity.

The SC5010 ensures accurate backlight color temperature and full-screen brightness with channel-to-channel current matching of ±1%, ±1.5% accuracy. It includes comprehensive protection features such as open LED protection, programmable LED short-circuit protection, adjustable overvoltage protection (OVP), over-temperature protection, and undervoltage lockout (UVLO) shutdown.

Key Features of SC5010:

- Wide VIN Range: 4.5V to 27V

- High VOUT Range: up to 50V

- Can use a thin 2.2μH inductor (height 1mm)

- 8-channel LED driver with currents up to 30mA per channel, driving up to 112 white LEDs

- Phase-shift PWM dimming

- Handles line transients up to 10V/μs with fast transient response

- Highest efficiency of 92%

- Programmable switching frequency from 200kHz to 2.2MHz

- Thin 28 MLPQ package with cooling plate: 4 x 4 x 0.6mm

Applications:

The SC5010 is most suitable for various LED-backlit LCD display applications, including:

- Automotive: audio/video modules, infotainment systems, rear seat displays, dashboards

- Consumer Electronics: LCD display backlights for super-thin, ultra-mobile PCs, notebooks, Tablet PCs
