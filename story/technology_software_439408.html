Cabinet Anatomy: Bones - Hardware Pieces

by f34ttr4t5ye on 2011-05-30 16:17:13

The cabinet is the heart of the kitchen, and the skeleton of the cabinet is the hardware, which plays a complete bridging role in modern cabinets.

The commonly referred hardware accessories mainly include seven types: hinges, slides, dampers, pressure supports, hanging codes, adjustment feet, and toe kick boards. Selecting suitable hardware accessories is very important for improving the overall quality of the cabinet and extending its service life. Currently, the top manufacturers producing hardware on the market include blum, hettich, and glass, all of whose hardware accessory products are trustworthy.

1. Hinges: This is the most important hardware accessory in the cabinet. According to statistics, the cabinet door will be opened and closed more than 100,000 times in 10 years! Therefore, the amount of hinges is crucial for the normal opening and closing of the cabinet door. There are two indicators to measure the quality of hinges, one is structural strength, the standard is whether it can open and close 100,000 times under a load of 75 pounds. The other is surface corrosion resistance, the indicator is whether it can withstand corrosion for 48 hours after salt water spray. In addition, hinges are divided into ordinary type and quick-install type, mainly different in installation methods. European and American countries have developed quick-install hinges to improve installation efficiency and reduce labor costs. As the world's first brand of hardware - Blum from Austria is currently the best quality hinge.

2. Slides: Drawer slides are second only to hinges in importance in the cabinet. The main types of slides used in cabinets are ordinary bottom support type, metal side panel type, and luxury fully enclosed type. The appearance, performance, and price of these three types differ significantly. The structure of the ordinary bottom support type slide is simple and the price is low, and the drawer side panels need to be made of wooden boards. The structure of the metal side panel type slide is similar to the former, but due to the built-in steel plates on both sides of the drawer in its own structure, it appears more refined, and is more solid and durable compared to the former, with a moderate price. The new generation of luxury fully enclosed drawer slides has patented hidden wheels, producing extremely low noise when sliding, large drawer carrying capacity, and the smoother the heavier. The inner corner is arc-shaped, without any hygiene dead angles, making cleaning convenient. The full pull-out design allows items stored in the drawer to be clearly visible, making direct access and retrieval easy. Similarly, Blum from Austria is currently the best quality hinge.

3. Dampers: The characteristic of this innovative product lies in that, no matter how much force is used to close the drawer or cabinet door, it can play a sound-damping cushioning role at the end of closure. The humanized design of this product fulfills people's desire for a quiet kitchen. Its incomparable perfect effect represents the trend of the development of future cabinet hardware. Without doubt, Blum damper is the best damper.

4. Gas struts: Cabinet gas struts originate from the support system of modern car trunk doors. The supporting force of a single gas strut is 80 Newtons, one is enough for small cabinet doors, and two are needed for larger ones. German SUSPA, STABILUS, etc., are well-known manufacturers of automotive gas struts, and their product quality is reliable.

5. Hanging codes: The suspension method of the upper cabinet involves safety of use. The correct and reasonable method of fixing the upper cabinet should use ABS metal combination hanging codes to fix the upper cabinet on the wall, which is safe, beautiful, and can conveniently adjust the spacing between the cabinets and countertops.

6. Adjustable feet: There are two types of adjustable feet, PVC and ABS. PVC has a low cost and is often made from recycled plastic, with poor load-bearing performance. The adjustable feet made from ABS engineering plastic are sturdy and durable, with good load-bearing performance, and can more effectively support the weight of the entire cabinet.

7. Toe kick: Toe kick is divided into wooden toe kick, PVC toe kick, and aluminum toe kick. Some manufacturers use leftover scraps from making the cabinet body to make wooden toe kick to reduce costs, but because the distance between the toe kick and the ground is very close, wooden materials easily absorb water and become damp, and after some time of use, the wooden toe kick will swell and rot. Both PVC toe kick and aluminum toe kick are recognized by experts, not only waterproof and moisture-proof, do not mold or rust, but also beautiful and durable. Especially aluminum toe kick is almost indestructible for life.

Choosing a set of cabinets starts with selecting good hardware accessories, which is the pivot point ensuring the full longevity of the cabinets.
