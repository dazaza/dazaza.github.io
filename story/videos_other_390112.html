Online Store Promotion (2) Method One: Techniques for Listing Items

1. It's recommended not to list all your items at once, but rather in three separate batches! Why is this? Let me explain. Everyone knows that on Taobao, the closer an item is to its end time, the higher it ranks! If you list all your items at the same time, then only one day this week will your items be at the top. By listing them in three separate batches over different days, you get three chances in a week. Guangzhou VIP Information Technology Co., Ltd.! This is why the listing period is 7 days instead of 14 days. The shorter the shelf time, the better. When searching for items, the default sort order is by time. Check if it works! Haha, this isn't enough yet; we need to add something more.

2. The timing of item listings should also be within the same time frame! Do you know when online transactions peak? It's from 9 AM to 12 PM, 2 PM to 5 PM, and 8 PM to 10 PM! So I suggest you list your items at 12 PM, 5 PM, or 10 PM. Haha! This way, during peak hours, your items will rank higher, increasing their views and thus sales!

Method Two: Usage of Window Recommendations

1. There are tricks to recommendations too! You divided your items into three batches, remember to recommend the second batch after the first, the third batch after the second, and the first batch after the third! (To clarify, wait until the first batch automatically delists before recommending the second batch). This is how my items are handled! Hehe! This can increase the view count of each item! For example, when the first batch is about to end, it ranks higher, and so do the recommended items. Exaggerating a bit, all your items could rank high for 7 days!

2. Dividing items into three batches has another advantage! Taobao gives each seller a few recommendation slots, usually 5 window positions for beginners, but with many items, who gets the slots? Here's the key: window recommendations only work for items ending within 2 days, so naturally, you'd give the slots to the items about to end. For sellers below 4 hearts, 5 to 20 slots should be sufficient! Haha... Recommendation slots are hard to come by, but when my two items got them, their views increased significantly! However, most buyers still prefer cheaper items, so price advantage is crucial.

3. Items you think are both cheap and unique must be placed in the store's recommendation slots. This is the benefit of posting and replying!

I often see some sellers' personal signatures and click into their stores, even if I don't buy, it at least increases their views. Because items with high time, price, and popularity are always the keywords people search for.

The front-to-back order of items on Taobao is:

1. Items with window recommendations rank higher than those without (in cases where both have window recommendations, they are ranked by remaining time).

2. Without window recommendations, items are ranked by remaining time, with shorter times ranking higher.

Everyone says window recommendation slots are important (duh, you don't need to say that). Sellers can participate in the "Consumer Protection Program" to get 5 permanent recommendation slots.

3. Item Name:

There are many great posts on Taobao about item name composition. Here are my thoughts:

First, let's look at Taobao's guidelines for item names:

Product Release Management Rules

Rule Eight: Misuse of Keywords Product Management

Definition: Sellers use brand names or unrelated terms in product titles to make them stand out or to increase searchability, disrupting Taobao's normal operational order. Taobao determines such products as misusing keywords.

Details:

1. Sellers use terms in product titles not intended to describe the actual product.

2. Sellers intentionally use Taobao's hotly promoted keywords in titles, with no direct relation to the content of the product.

3. Sellers use specific brand names not used by the manufacturer or producer of the product.

4. Sellers compare their products with others using phrases like "Comparable to LV."

5. Sellers use superlatives like "Best," "Highest," "Greatest" in titles.

6. No descriptions of free gifts or prizes are allowed in titles, considered misuse of keywords. Promotional content can be added to item descriptions instead.

7. Sports category products cannot use multiple attribute keywords in the same item.

8. Without exclusive dealership rights, claims like "Taobao Exclusive" or "Authorized Dealer" are forbidden.

9. In the virtual goods trading area, product titles under QQ zones cannot include terms from other categories.

These are Taobao's rules regarding keyword management. Please review and self-reflect.

Composition of Item Names: The item name is the most direct method for buyers to search for products, so it should include the following elements: brand name, origin name (or style name), attribute name, keywords (cold and hot words), model number (color, etc.), as well as: credibility, activity, features.
