He Qi Jian (Wong Zi Ho), known as the "First Villain of Guangzhou", runs amok with the backing of the police commissioner, oppressing people with his power. He spares no means to seize control of the old lady's house, leading to a fierce rivalry with the owner Ling Yu Cui (Ye Tong). Although Cui has buck teeth, she meets the righteous Wong Fei Hung (Lam Ka Keung), and their relationship develops to the point of discussing marriage. Jian continues to stir up trouble under the guise of defeating the ten tigers of Guangdong, using it as a publicity stunt for his martial arts school. Hung seeks justice by challenging Jian, but Jian uses his father's spirit tablet as a threat, forcing Hung to concede defeat. Cui misunderstands and refuses the marriage proposal, allowing Jian to take advantage of the situation. She is convinced by Jian and gives up the rights to the old lady's house. Jian then reveals his true nature, publicly humiliating Cui. Later, when Jian falls out of favor and becomes a public enemy, he begs Hung to take him as a disciple.
