Latest Quests and Walkthrough of Lu Xiaofeng Legend - Warcraft Secrets

by qhmywllf on 2009-04-12 15:59:13

I've been playing LXF Legend for over a month. At first, I liked this map because of its background music - Chinese Paladin. I think many people play this map also because of this music. Just because we all have a legendary martial arts dream in our hearts.

A friend introduced me to version 0.7 at the beginning. I didn't play for two days, and then version 1.0 was officially released. At that time, I didn't know about private rooms. Then I went on the forum and finally found the group. After entering, I realized that the difficulty of version 1.0 was really high. At that time, we were considered one of the first groups to pass level 3. As for level 4, it seemed out of reach for us. By the end of June, QQ released the first video of three people flying through level 4. Then Ximen ChuiShui, Absolute Field, and I imitated QQ's strategy and passed once. After that, there was no progress until recently. I feel that my skills have truly improved, and I want to share some game experience with everyone.

For new players, here are a few issues:

1) Jiujianxian's barrel

First, enter the main interface of Warcraft and set up: "Options" - "Graphics" - "Unit Shadows" - On. After setting the unit shadows on, you can see the shadow under the barrel. In this way, you can directly click on the shadow below and keep pressing ESC to jump and pick up the barrel. The barrel moves in a straight line, so as long as you stand on that line.

2) Herb task

In the early stages, the herbs obtained from the herb task can be sold for 67500 gold. This money is very important for the early development. With this money, you can quickly produce offspring or kill some deer for protective gear. For intermediate-level players, a martial hero will get a level five glove, and a level five robe to directly make stones. Some people say after using an invincibility potion, the effect disappears immediately and they are killed by birds. Actually, you can eat one first, then drop the invincibility on the ground, let the bird pick it up, and then continue eating. This way, the invincibility won't disappear, and you can smoothly obtain the herbs.

3) Sandworm task

If the robber positions refreshed on the map block various roads, a martial hero can buy a level one armor first. Wearing the armor allows safe killing of sandworms. As for spiritual heroes, you can use the W skill to remotely attack the robbers. Since the robbers' vision is limited, they will automatically move a little bit. If you think you can safely navigate past, then go ahead.

4) Attribute points

Among the nine characters in the game, they are mainly divided into martial and spiritual categories. One is single martial damage output, and the other is single spiritual damage output. Martial power mainly includes: Dabeiheshang, Baiyun Chengzhu, Ximen ChuiXue, Sun Xiuqing, Sikong Zhaixing; Spiritual heroes mainly include: Lu Xiaofeng, Huamanlou, Xuebing, Liu Yuhate. Martial heroes should add all attribute points to martial power, while spiritual heroes should add all attribute points to spiritual power. However, Ximen ChuiXue and Lu Xiaofeng can be half spiritual and half martial. If you want to create something new, adding more spiritual points to Ximen is good, and adding all martial points to Xiaofeng is also fine, but only if you're aiming for difficulty level 3 or lower. As for Sikong Zhaixing, since the stealing skill requires high spiritual power to steal more money, the general situation is to add 8 points to martial and 2 points to spiritual.

About Advanced Process

As players become more skilled, you may find that experts complete tasks much faster than yourself, and their equipment reaches godly levels much earlier. I usually exchange ideas with Pooh more often. Here, I will briefly introduce his strategy process for the flying immortal difficulty.

1) Before the first boss

First, do basic tasks: frogs, sandworms. At this point, you have 35,000 gold (the following does not count the money given by each wave of the system).

Buy invincibility to collect herbs. After completing the herbs, add the remaining 20,000, totaling 87,500, buy five-level gloves, five-level armor.

Complete the remaining basic tasks: wolves, venison, bandits. You have 12,500 left, then add the money given by each wave of the system.

Spend 20,000 to buy a Corrosion Ball or Poison Ball (for ranged or spiritual heroes), buy some health potions and magic potions to do the dual stone task.

The stone task is usually completed between the 3rd and 4th waves, then go to the forest to kill the Wolf King and Deer King. Use the Black Gold Hammer obtained from killing the Wolf King to immediately borrow a bird from the monk to synthesize a Break Armor Knife. Having the Break Armor Knife makes brushing money and doing tasks much easier. Before the first boss appears, continue mixing in the forest, brush until you get 6 Deer Kings (each worth 22,500 gold). Of course, if you encounter a Wolf King while brushing, kill it too, try to get another hammer as material for Fang Tian. During this period, prioritize getting the Heart Guard Armor first, then the Mother-Son Set. Under normal circumstances, the current equipment is: 2 pieces of Golden Silk (1 piece from Deer King, 1 piece from Ice Stone), Heart Guard Armor, Mother-Son Set, Breaker Knife, Black Gold Hammer, Arhat Armor, Purple Gold Helmet. If your luck isn't good or there are too many people, you might not be able to kill 6 Deer Kings, nor get the Golden Silk and Purple Gold Helmet, so I won't talk about that.
