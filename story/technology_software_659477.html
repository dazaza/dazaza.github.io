Lingling, only 6 years old, was beaten by her biological mother until her skin broke and flesh showed. On June 2nd and 3rd, our newspaper followed up on this case, which caused a strong reaction in society. CCTV Channel 7's "Life 567" program contacted our reporter to inquire about Lingling's latest condition and agreed to come to Changshu for an interview during the Dragon Boat Festival.

Ms. Huang, a citizen who came with her son to visit Lingling, told the reporter that after she learned about Lingling's plight, she mentioned it to her husband and son. Her 10-year-old son started crying as he listened and insisted that she bring him to the hospital to see Lingling. Classmate Shi Xinyu from Grade 4(2) of Xin Zhuang Central Primary School gave Lingling a painting, with the words "Wish you a speedy recovery."

It is understood that the Changshu police have already intervened in the investigation of this matter. However, since Wang Changxiu is pregnant, the police educated her and then let her go home. Vice President He Miaoyun of the Women's Federation of Changshu said: "Next, we will continue to pay attention to this matter. If we find that Lingling's mother continues to abuse her, we will contact relevant departments and firmly protect the rights of this little girl."

The biological mother broke her 6-year-old daughter's seven ribs - Continued Report (3). Lingling happily ate a zongzi (rice dumpling).

Yesterday morning, Mr. Xu and his wife specially drove from Yancheng to visit Lingling at the Traditional Chinese Medicine Hospital of Changshu. Mr. Xu told the reporter that after seeing our previous reports, his wife cried, and they didn't sleep all night. They got up early the next day and drove to Changshu to see Lingling. The couple also decided to adopt Lingling. "I told my dad that we want to adopt this child," Mr. Xu said. According to Lingling's current economic situation, her future life would be very difficult, and her treatment cannot be guaranteed. If Lingling's parents agree, they will go to the civil affairs department to handle the adoption procedures, take Lingling back home to raise her, and also take her to the best hospital in Shanghai for treatment so that she can recover and heal the trauma in her heart. "After adopting the child, if her father misses her, he can always come to see her."

Regarding whether Lingling might suffer the same nightmare again after being discharged from the hospital, Li Fanghui said: "I guarantee that such incidents will not happen again." During the interview, Lingling kept affectionately calling out to her father. For Li Fanghui, Lingling is no longer unfamiliar, but when mentioning her mother, Wang Changxiu, Lingling shows a frightened expression. Li Fanghui also told the reporter that on June 2nd, after Lingling was transferred from ICU to a general ward, he sent his wife, Wang Changxiu, back to Suining, Xuzhou, mainly to terminate the pregnancy in her womb.

Yesterday afternoon, when the reporter arrived at Ward 10 of the Traditional Chinese Medicine Hospital of Changshu, Lingling was drinking fruit juice. "This is a doll, a beautiful doll." Seeing the reporter, Lingling happily picked up the doll on the bed and said. At this time, Lingling's sister and younger brother were there too, playing happily. Li Fanghui was accompanying Lingling by her bedside.

"My own biological daughter, I am reluctant to give her away. At the time, I neither agreed nor refused. After the child is discharged from the hospital, I will think about it again." Said Lingling's father, Li Fanghui. Li Fanghui's concern is that Lingling has lived with her grandmother for more than four years and has become estranged from her parents. He fears that if he does not live together with Lingling for a long time, Lingling will stop calling him "Dad."
