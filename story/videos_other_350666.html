Related thematic articles: Untitled Document After coming online in the early morning, Lin Li still logged into his mage account as usual. The Bright Moon City on Sunday was always particularly lively, and the number of players was more than doubled compared to normal days. Even the expensive private warehouses were packed with people like Yu Qiuyu at this time. "Could you please take out..." Lin Li finally squeezed to Soran's front, preparing to ask the beautiful administrator to help him take out the mailed items. However, before he could finish his sentence, someone pushed him from behind. "Have you seen the words outside? This is a private warehouse. What are you doing here with your small account?" The speaker was a level forty warrior, followed by four or five players, looking like the leader of a small group. After the warrior spoke, the several players behind him also expressed their dissatisfaction one after another: "Is there something wrong with this small account? Why come to squeeze in the private warehouse for no reason?" "Who knows, probably thought we're all rich people here, wanting some money to buy medicine and upgrade." "If you want money, why not go to the auction house? There are all rich people there. If you encounter someone in a good mood, they might throw you dozens of gold coins, then you won't have to worry about the cost of upgrading drugs." Lin Li just glanced at these flies and then patiently squeezed to Soran's front again: "Could you please take out all the items I mailed yesterday." "Sure, please wait a moment." Then the crowd of onlookers saw various kinds of ore ingots being taken out from the private warehouse, including common mithril and silver, as well as rare adamantite and mythril. The special luster of magical metals flashed brilliantly inside the private warehouse, dazzling all the players' eyes. The noise that had been going on suddenly stopped, and the only sound heard in the entire private warehouse was the sound of swallowing saliva... Before the crowd could shift their gaze from the large amount of ore ingots, the beautiful administrator took out another pile of things from the warehouse. It was hundreds of neatly stacked black dragon skins. The fine dragon scales reflected a captivating light under the luster of magical metals. These were genuine black dragon skins, a perfect combination of magical and physical defense. Equipment made from them would be at least legendary level each piece. These hundreds of black dragon skins almost meant hundreds of pieces of equipment at least legendary level! And it wasn't over yet. Beneath the hundreds of black dragon skins were dozens of already smelted divine metals. As soon as the dozens of divine metals appeared, the entire private warehouse became excited instantly. "Look quickly, that thing seems to be the legendary divine metal!" "What do you mean 'seems'? That is divine metal, my goodness! A whole twenty-something pieces, did this little mage just rob a dragon?" This guy guessed half right. Lin Li indeed just swept through a dragon's lair, but while sweeping through the dragon's lair, he also conveniently killed the Destroyer Dragon Azadas. There were no shortage of players specializing in forging among those present, and they were very clear about the value of divine metal. Equipment forged with divine metal may not have prominent attributes at first, but as the usage time gets longer and the user's level gradually increases, the terrifying growth characteristics of divine metal will slowly reveal themselves. Many experts in forging on the official forum once predicted that if a player starts using equipment forged with divine metal from level one, then by the time he reaches level seventy, this equipment will be at least epic level even if it's not a divine artifact! And this beast directly took out two dozen pieces, does he even let others live? Everyone watched Lin Li put twenty pieces of divine metal into his backpack, and the look in their eyes was simply like seeing their own wives go into a hotel with other men... When Soran took out a scroll from the warehouse again, the looks the crowd gave her were already somewhat dazed. A beast who could casually take out two epic-level equipment, along with hundreds of dragon skins and dozens of divine metals, what else can't he do? But when the scroll was handed to Lin Li, the crowd immediately collapsed. "Wish spell! That's a wish spell scroll!" As soon as the scroll changed hands, an informed player called out. "How is this possible! Are you mistaken?" "It's real, that's really a wish spell scroll!" The appearance of this scroll caused the previously quiet private warehouse to become excited again. This was no longer something that could be described as extravagantly wealthy; a wish spell scroll meant that the owner could make a wish to the system main god. As long as it was within the game rules, any wish could be realized. This wish could be infinite wealth, a powerful piece of equipment, having the system main god help kill a myth-level creature, or even a vast land to establish your own kingdom. At this point, the looks everyone gave Lin Li already contained some other meanings. However, Lin Li didn't pay any attention to them at all. He extended two fingers, lightly pinched the wish spell scroll and put it into his backpack, thus strutting out of the private warehouse. Lin Li walked along the canal towards the trade area, but there were dozens of tails following him, each with ulterior motives. "They really followed..." Lin Li looked back and couldn't help but laugh. Killing and looting without checking the situation, you better follow closely. After I'm done with my main business, I'll definitely switch to my hunter account to have a good talk with you guys. In fact, Lin Li's hunter account was currently parked inside the Bright Moon City auction house. That was a super hunter holding the mythical weapon Star Wrath. The mage was merely a warehouse minor account. It was just that Lin Li's luck seemed too good. Originally, the mage minor account was intended to be used as a warehouse, but it miraculously got double full stats for intelligence and spirit, causing Lin Li to always feel troubled later on. What should such an excellent minor account be used for? Finally, thinking that such an excellent minor account shouldn't be wasted, Lin Li decided to let the minor account practice various life professions. This guy was also ruthless. If he doesn't practice, he doesn't practice; if he does, he practices everything. As long as it was a learnable life skill, he didn't miss a single one. Now, a level one minor account has already practiced the five major skills of forging, alchemy, potion making, enchanting, and inscription to advanced levels, and even a series of miscellaneous studies such as drug knowledge, mineral knowledge, cooking skills, and scroll making have all been upgraded to above advanced levels. Not hurrying, Lin Li stopped in front of the blacksmith shop in the trading area. Dozens of pieces of mithril were poured into the furnace, and Lin Li stood beside it waiting for two minutes, not caring whether the mithril melted or not, he took out a piece and started hammering it on the anvil. Behind him, dozens of players only saw sparks flying everywhere, and valuable mithril worth hundreds of gold coins instantly turned into a piece of scrap iron. For senior blacksmiths, turning mithril into scrap iron is quite normal, but whether it turns into scrap iron or not makes no difference to Lin Li. He isn't short of those hundreds of gold coins or those few pieces of mithril. In fact, in many places like the Flashing Plains Thousand Needle Stone Forest, he owns his own private mine veins, which just no one knows about... Turning a few pieces of mithril into scrap iron is nothing, as long as the skill points increase. Among the blood-spitting expressions of the blacksmiths, Lin Li continued to work busily, block after block, spending a good half hour, completely scrapping a hundred pieces of mithril, and naturally, his forging skill soared rapidly. After mithril came silver, after silver came mythril, after mythril came adamantite... When millions of worth of ores were hammered into scrap iron, the pleasant system notification sound finally came: "Congratulations on advancing to Master Blacksmith!" Rubbing his sore arms, Lin Li looked back at the stunned onlookers and then tidied up his tools, turning to walk towards the potion shop direction. Another million gold coins worth of herbs were thrown away, and the potion-making skill smoothly reached the master level. Then came alchemy... followed by enchanting... and finally inscriptions... By the time Lin Li upgraded all five skills to the master level, it was almost noon. A group of trailing players dumbfoundedly watched him waste materials. Initially, they felt sorry for him, but eventually, they became completely numb. The onlookers even began speculating whether this guy was a rich man's son, running to the game to vent because of something that happened in reality. Successfully upgrading the five skills to the master level, Lin Li finally paused to catch his breath. Just when everyone thought he was going to stop, Lin Li took out the wish spell scroll from his backpack. "What is this guy planning to do?" The power of the wish spell scroll was truly terrifying. Lin Li had just taken it out when a few players behind him were on high alert, ready to defend. After all, this guy didn't seem normal, and he was holding such a monstrous wish spell scroll. What if he got upset and wished for the system main god to start a massacre or something? Standing here wouldn't be self-destructive? "You guys really think highly of yourselves..." Lin Li glanced at those players on high alert, thinking how foolish it would be for him to use a wish spell scroll against you. Don't even know your own weight, are you worth this price? As the wish spell scroll was gently unfolded, a rainbow suddenly appeared in the sky above Bright Moon City. The colorful lights softly sprinkled down, making the entire Bright Moon City appear as if enveloped in a dream, solemn and sacred chanting sounds accompanied by a dignified voice echoed, as if far away in the heavens, yet close by at the same time. "What is your wish?" Lin Li looked at the rainbow in the sky, hesitated slightly, and then deeply inhaled: "My wish is to enhance the level of all my life skills!" The moment the wish was made, the rainbow in the sky transformed into countless beams of light. Seeing the sparkling lights slowly fall from the sky, like a seven-colored silk ribbon wrapping around Lin Li, Lin Li could clearly feel that with every breath, countless beams of light merged into his body. When the last bit of light was completely absorbed, endless knowledge surged into his mind, including forging, alchemy, potion making, enchanting, inscriptions, and even drug knowledge, mineral knowledge, and other miscellaneous studies. These knowledge were as if innate, merging with the original memory as soon as they emerged, allowing Lin Li to easily apply them effortlessly. Lin Li checked his skill status and found that the four main skills had indeed reached the grandmaster level, while various miscellaneous studies were mostly above the master level. Thinking about the Infinite World open for over a year, let alone finding a grandmaster in five fields, even finding a single-field master would be hard. Life skills cannot be compared to personal level. Personal level can be accumulated with enough time, constantly killing monsters, constantly completing tasks, accumulating enough experience to level up. But life skills, once reaching the advanced level, further improvement becomes extremely difficult. Only someone like Lin Li, with great wealth, would think of using advanced materials to boost skills. Think about today, improving five skills cost nearly five million gold coins. Besides Lin Li, who just raided a dragon lair, who else could afford it? And the master level is like a watershed. Once the skill reaches the master level, it is no longer a matter of money. Perhaps ten thousand successful forgeries won't improve your skill even a bit, but one failed forgery might lead to a rapid advancement. No one dares to say that they will definitely reach the grandmaster level one day, even if they are just one step away from grandmaster. Of course, correspondingly, compared to masters, grandmasters are like heaven and earth apart. Master-level skills can only make legendary items at most, and success rate out of a thousand attempts is uncertain, while grandmaster-level skills can not only make legendary items at will, but also easily make epic items. Although there is still a certain failure rate, it is incomparable to the master level. As for mythic items, that is beyond what ordinary people can imagine. People who can make divine artifacts, regardless of their profession, forever have only one title - Divine Artisan! The wish spell scroll is a good thing. Using it to gain tens of millions in wealth, or killing a myth-level creature, or even establishing your own kingdom, is undoubtedly a good choice, but Lin Li always believed that using it to become a grandmaster in all professions is the wisest choice. Lin Li glanced at the rainbow dissipating in the sky and slowly put the remaining materials for skill training into his backpack. After witnessing the miracle, the dozens of onlookers had long abandoned the idea of killing and looting. They dumbly watched Lin Li pack up the materials, none daring to move. How could someone who could casually take out countless top-notch materials, knocking hammers and shaking medicine bottles, throwing away millions of gold coins, be someone they could provoke? Without mentioning anything else, taking out a few hundred thousand gold coins would be enough to hire people to delete their accounts. Killing and looting? Suicide is more like it... After putting all the materials into his backpack, Lin Li was about to log off and change accounts, intending to use the hunter to have a good talk with the onlookers, when a sharp alarm suddenly sounded. "The system encountered an unknown error, please log off immediately!" "The system encountered an unknown error, please log off immediately!" "The system encountered an unknown error, please log off immediately!" The alarm rang three times, and just as Lin Li was about to log off, he suddenly found that his character was no longer under his control. Before he could figure out what was happening, the feeling of losing control had spread to his real body. It felt as if his soul was separating from his body. He could see himself, yet could not feel his own existence. Everything around him seemed soft and mushy, and no matter how Lin Li struggled, he could not escape that desperate sense of powerlessness. Lin Li gradually felt that a thread of his consciousness was floating upwards. He could even see another version of himself lying limply on the chair. Shocked, Lin Li suddenly raised his head, only to find pitch darkness above him. It was true darkness, without a single impurity mixed in. Even a ray of light would be completely absorbed by that boundless darkness... "Who is so despicable, hiding a black hole in my home!"
