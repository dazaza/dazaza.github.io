Guoxin Securities: Growth of the wastewater business of Bluestar Cleaning is expected to accelerate＿Stock Forum

by fj0953210 on 2009-12-05 21:58:44

Matters: On December 1, 2009, the Major Restructuring Committee of China Securities Regulatory Commission reviewed and conditionally approved the significant asset swap and issuance of shares to purchase assets and related party transactions of Lanxing Cleaning. The company will issue another announcement after receiving the relevant approval documents from the CSRC.

Comments: The restructuring is basically completed. From the release of the restructuring draft in June to the approval on December 1, the entire restructuring process has proceeded smoothly. Currently, the following steps are still needed: (1) Exemption of tender offer obligations. Since Xingrong Company holds 52.3% of the company's equity, which exceeds 30%, it still needs the CSRC's approval for the exemption of its tender offer obligations; (2) Asset delivery. We maintain the judgment that "the asset delivery will be optimistically completed within the year," based on (1) the determination of both parties to restructure and support from all sides, as the time from the release of the restructuring draft to the submission of materials was less than a month; (2) According to experience, under normal circumstances, the exemption of tender offer obligations will be approved within one month after the review, and the asset delivery matters will be confirmed. At that time, the entire restructuring process will be completed.

Taking a key step forward, the growth of sewage treatment business is expected to accelerate. The company is transforming into a leading sewage treatment company in Southwest China, owning eight sewage plants of Chengdu Drainage Company (with a daily production capacity of about 1.3 million cubic meters), responsible for sewage treatment in the central urban area of Chengdu, with clear assets. With the basic completion of restructuring, we believe that the company will shift its focus to increasing the growth of the sewage business, which will greatly promote the resolution of the pipeline bottleneck and the intensification of external expansion. The probability of achieving an "annual growth of 10%-15% in sewage treatment volume" increases, and the performance growth rate in the next two years is expected to exceed expectations, reaching around 20% growth.

Maintain "Recommend" rating. Basically, the end of the year can be taken as a node, with almost all of the 2010 income coming from newly injected assets. Without considering subsequent asset injections and sludge projects, the diluted EPS for 2010 and 2011 are 0.50 and 0.60 yuan respectively, with the current stock price at 12.3 yuan, the valuation is at the bottom of the entire industry (the PE level for 2010 is close to 40), with a high safety margin. Meanwhile, Xingrong Company develops both internally and externally: (1) Located in Chengdu, the urbanization speed in recent years has accelerated, undoubtedly promoting water usage and sewage treatment volumes; (2) Government support can be seen throughout the entire restructuring process; (3) Operational strength and leading position in the Chengdu waterworks market, regardless of sewage or tap water, there is a large space for expansion. Maintain the company's "Recommend" rating.

The procedures for buying and selling stocks are as follows: When buying or selling stocks, you must specify whether you are buying or selling, the name (or code) of the stock, the quantity, and the price. This order is only valid for the day it is placed. The content of the order includes the abbreviation (code) of the stock you want to buy or sell, the quantity, and the price at which you want to buy or sell the stock. The abbreviation of the stock is usually three to four Chinese characters, and the code is six digits. The code and abbreviation of the stock must match when placing an order. At the same time, there are certain regulations regarding the quantity of stocks being bought or sold: the quantity for purchasing stocks must be a whole multiple of 100, but the quantity for selling stocks does not have to be a whole multiple of 100.

There are four methods of placing orders: counter submission, telephone automatic order, computer automatic order, and remote terminal order.
