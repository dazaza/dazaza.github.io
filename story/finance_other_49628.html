Security Surveillance - Analyzing the Storage Secrets of Video Monitoring

by silenfir on 2008-02-27 21:12:53

The surveillance industry is rapidly evolving. From a technical perspective, digital video surveillance is becoming the dominant trend in the development of the surveillance industry; from an operational model perspective, the centralized construction of surveillance systems is gradually replacing the previous user self-construction model. Operators are becoming a key link in the surveillance industry chain. They leverage their own bandwidth and technical advantages to build carrier-class digital video surveillance systems, providing users with solutions from network to equipment, and from construction to maintenance.

In this way, centralized data processing not only significantly improves data security efficiency and protection, but also allows end-users to avoid spending huge amounts of money on system construction and maintenance, thereby reducing overall investment costs. Nowadays, this model is becoming an important direction for the development of the video surveillance industry.
