The spicy and seductive photos of Xu Qingqing have been circulating on the Internet.

Spicy photos, stunning figures, exaggerated expressions - she's known as the "second Furong Jiejie". Recently, a Wenzhou girl named Xu Qingqing has quickly become famous online due to her "explosive" photos. On September 21st, a reporter from Wenzhou Network contacted Xu Qingqing by phone, who then shared the process of posting her photos online and her personal thoughts.

How did Xu Qingqing become famous?

"Half a year ago, I registered for a Renren account (formerly called Xiaonei), and posted some of my photos on it, which attracted a large number of fans." On the other end of the phone, Xu Qingqing sounded very cheerful. In the photos, Xu Qingqing is heavily made up, dressed revealingly, with exaggerated expressions and striking poses.

About a few days later, Xu Qingqing gained more and more friends on Renren. "At its peak, ten people were viewing my photos every minute," said Xu Qingqing. Later, she also posted her photos on QQ Space, but due to reaching the friend limit, she had to move her "home" again.

On August 31st, Xu Qingqing published her first article titled "Gracefully Flying" on Sina Blog, and also moved over a hundred photos to the same platform. "On the first day, there were 70,000 page views, and the popularity was unstoppable," said an excited Xu Qingqing. She mentioned that it was her charm that attracted the fans.

"Disgusting enough to make me want to die." "Xu Qingqing, the rising star!" "She is unique, irreplaceable, and no one can substitute her!" "This is purely hype." For a time, Xu Qingqing's photos quickly sparked discussions among netizens, and she herself emerged into the public eye amidst the controversy.

What kind of person is Xu Qingqing?

According to reports, Xu Qingqing is 23 years old this year, born in Dongtou, Wenzhou. She once attended Dongtou No.1 Middle School, then went on to Taizhou Vocational and Technical College, and last year was admitted to Zhejiang University of Science and Technology's Biochemical College for a specialized-to-undergraduate program.

"The style in the photos is just me; I am the unique me," said Xu Qingqing. In real life, she loves beauty, art, and excels in talent performances. It was learned that during her studies in Taizhou in 2006, she won the first prize in the school's "Jasmine Clear Tea Love Angel Final Competition" and also won the first prize in the college's "Stepping on Grass Reflections" essay competition.

"My family conditions are average, and I haven't received any specialized training, but I have a great love for talent performances," said Xu Qingqing. She mentioned that her academic performance at school is also decent, and she studies quite diligently.

"Don't ask about my school or family, otherwise I will refuse the interview." When asked about her family and school, Xu Qingqing firmly rejected the inquiries, indicating that she does not want to involve her school or family. "Don't bring them into it," said Xu Qingqing, adding that she doesn't want outsiders to disturb her parents. "My family knows about my photo postings, and they haven't opposed it."

Is this a case of online hype?

"There is too much hype on the Internet these days, and using a college student for such hype is truly inappropriate." Many netizens believe that "Xu Qingqing, the second Furong" is merely a publicity stunt orchestrated by many commercial companies.

"At the time, I unconsciously uploaded the photos without anyone helping me plan it," said Xu Qingqing. She mentioned that the photos she posted were taken and uploaded immediately, and she showed strong resentment towards the allegations of hype and planning. She stated that she attracts fans through her charm.

When asked why she took those photos, she replied, "I want to show everyone my most beautiful side." The reporter asked, "Were your photos taken in the dormitory?" Xu Qingqing responded, "There were only a few taken in the dormitory." The reporter asked, "Who helped you take the photos?" Xu Qingqing answered, "I cannot disclose that."

"I will not post any more photos within a year. Next year, I will present a more sexy and stunning side to everyone. What you see now is just the tip of the iceberg," said Xu Qingqing. She added that during this year, she will continue to "hone her skills."
