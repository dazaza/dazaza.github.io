Marriage is the grave of romance.

by haivtaokqp on 2011-09-18 20:27:40

Tears are women's most primitive weapon, and also the most powerful one that men can hardly resist.

People always say that marriage is the graveyard of love, because before marriage, they have already been on the way to the graveyard. Even if they don't get married, they will break up in front of the tomb. Why rush into the grave without breaking up first?

Do you love him? If you love, tell him, why hide the pain of missing deep in your heart. Afraid of mismatched looks, status, or identity? Don't be afraid, loving someone is beautiful.

It's already wrong to get married hastily, and never divorce hastily. Try it first, it's not too late to divorce if it really doesn't work out.

In fact, after the loved one dies, the living person continues to love through memories, so don't think that the death of a loved one is love. If you truly love, live for your loved one, until very old.

When it comes to both parties' personalities, I've seen many handsome men paired with ugly women, and ugly women paired with handsome men.

If you want to be loved by others, you must first make yourself worthy of love, not just for a day or a week, but forever.

It's fine to fall in love with someone who has many different living habits, but when getting married, be cautious and consider whether you can endure each other's differences for a long time.

No matter how big or old, no matter how much family and friends urge you, don't take marriage lightly. Marriage is not like playing cards; reshuffling comes at a great cost.

Whether a man can give you a sense of security has nothing to do with his height, but everything to do with his mentality. I've seen many tall men who are wimpy, and short men who are spirited. A man should be proud and confident, which makes him manly. Of course, the prerequisite is to have talent.

Marriage is the graveyard of love, but if you don't get married, love will have nowhere to die.

People often talk about masculinity and femininity. Do you know what masculinity smells like and what femininity tastes like? Masculinity is being open and brave, while femininity is being gentle and considerate.

Some people say that in love, you should find someone you like, and in marriage, you should find someone who likes you. Both are one-sided. What's the point of a lover who doesn't like you? How can a wife live with someone she doesn't like for a lifetime?

What is charm? Charm is not beauty; a beautiful woman isn't necessarily attractive to me, but an elegant and dignified woman is what I prefer. So, you don't need to worry about not being pretty enough.

"Plainness is true," yes, but that should be the calm after passion, then passion again, then calm again. Passion and calmness should alternate in waves. What's the point of a life that's only mundane without passion? As long as you truly love him, you'll have passion until death.

Don't get married out of a sense of responsibility. After all, marrying someone you don't love is the most irresponsible act. Even though it may hurt the other person at the moment, it's better than making them sad for years or even a lifetime.

Most first loves don't understand love, so there are more failures than successes. When getting married, it's best to find someone who hasn't been married before because everyone prefers original packaging. But when it comes to dating, it's better to find someone who has dated before because those who have experienced love know what love is and how to love.

If you want to be loved by others, you must first make yourself worthy of love, not just for a day or a week, but forever.

Truly loving someone means trying to make them happy. When they're happy, you'll be happy too, and then both sides will have passion.

It's true that men turn bad when they have money, many men do this. However, a man who turns bad as soon as he has money won't be much better even if he doesn't have money.

Don't date out of loneliness. Time is a devil, over the years, if you're a sentimental person, even if you don't love the other person, you might develop feelings eventually. What will you do then?

Why do we rarely see legendary and touching romances in real life? Because such emotions are extremely precious, and rare things aren't easy to come by. Are gold and diamonds easy to find?

Women should learn to dress up themselves, don't use simplicity as an excuse, don't use housework as a reason. If you don't understand fashion, you're not a complete woman.

Young men, especially students, prefer feminine allure rather than women who make them wait until they get annoyed.
