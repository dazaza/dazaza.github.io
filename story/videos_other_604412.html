If you really have been in love, what if your parents disagree? I'm so moved. I'm going to cry!

by kaixinren6 on 2011-12-05 14:21:52

Boy: Honey, it's me. What are you doing?

Girl: Oh...nothing...

Boy: Why have you been ignoring me? Did I do something wrong?

Girl: I haven't ignored you...

Boy: Then why didn't you answer my calls or reply to my messages the other day? Did you find someone new?

Girl: I answered now, didn't I? I haven't found anyone. Stop guessing.

Boy: Alright, I hope you're not lying to me.

Girl: Hmm...I'm not lying.

Boy: Darling, I miss you so much. Do you miss me?

Girl: Hmm...

Boy: Hmm what? Do you miss me?

Girl: Yes...

The boy laughed; You've never told me you love me. Can you say it now?

Girl: Hehe...no! It's embarrassing.

Boy: Not at all. Please, I want to hear it.

Girl: ...

Boy: If you won't say it, shall I?

Girl: Go ahead!

Boy: Wife, I love you. I really love you a lot. My wife, I truly love you.

The girl laughed; I want to hear more.

Boy: Enough already? I'm on the phone in public; many people are watching me.

Girl: I don't care. I want to hear it. Tell me.

Boy: You won't say it first?

Girl: I can't bring myself to say it.

Boy: I really want to hear you say "I love you."

Girl: ...

Boy: Wife, I love you.

Girl: The same words go back to you.

Boy: No, that's not right. You're not my wife yet.

Girl: Then correct that word...

The boy laughed;

Girl: That's it. I'll hang up now! Bye...

Boy: No, I haven't talked enough!

Girl: Are you serious? It's almost been an hour.

Boy: Not yet, only ten minutes left. Can we talk for five more minutes?

Girl: Alright.

Boy: Can I kiss you?

Girl: No way!

Boy: Please...

Girl: No.

Boy: Then can I kiss you instead?

Girl: Uh...Hmm...

The boy kissed the girl over the phone.

Girl: Five minutes are up!

Boy: Not yet!

Girl: Time's up.

Boy disappointingly said; Alright, you hang up then!

The girl hung up the phone. She wasn't sure whether she should tell him they should break up. The girl still loved the boy, but his parents opposed their relationship. A few days ago, the boy's mother even visited the girl, asking her to leave the boy. She said the girl would ruin the boy's life. The boy's parents also warned the girl that if she didn't break up with him, they would make sure her family wouldn't have peace. Afraid of the rich, the girl hesitated but didn't want to break up with the boy.

The next day, he called her again.

Girl: Hello, why so early?

Boy: I missed you, I wanted to hear your voice.

Girl: Have you eaten breakfast?

Boy: No, since you don't cook for me.

Girl: I can't cook well anyway.

Boy: Doesn't matter, as long as there's food.

Girl: Maybe it's not even edible.

Boy: It's fine, as long as there's something to eat.

The girl smiled;

The boy also smiled;

Girl: I have to work now, see you later, bye...

Boy: Why are you hanging up so quickly?

Girl: I have things to do!

Boy: Then tell me you love me first.

Girl: I...you...

Boy: You missed saying "love".

Girl: I really need to work now!

Boy: Alright!

Five minutes passed, the boy said: Why haven't you hung up yet?

Girl: I want you to hang up first.

Boy: No problem, you hang up!

Girl: You hang up first.

Boy: Alright then.

The sound of beeps came from the phone. The girl thoughtfully realized how unpleasant it was to be hung up on. She had made up her mind; she didn't want to harm him. She didn't want her family to suffer!

On the morning of the third day, she dialed his number. Excitedly, he picked up and said: Honey, you finally called me! Were you missing me?

Girl: I...

Boy: What's wrong?

Girl: Nothing.

Boy: Oh! By the way, I dreamed about you last night.

Girl: What did you dream about me?

Boy: I dreamed that you came to Shenzhen, you were by my side. Leaning on my shoulder, we watched stars, counted them, told stories, and eventually caught fireflies. You kept laughing foolishly, ha ha... When I'm not busy with work, I'll come find you. Let this dream become reality...

The girl interrupted the boy: Li Jianming.

(Supplement: the boy's name is Li Jianming, and the girl's name I won't mention because it's inconvenient.)

Boy: What's wrong? Are you upset?

Girl: Let's break up.

The boy paused, after a while he asked: Why?

Girl: Because I love you. So I don't want to harm you.

Boy: Harm me? How could you harm me? Did my dad say something to you? I'll deal with him. I'll tell him we truly love each other.

The girl sobbed: No, I don't want our father-son relationship to be strained. We must break up. Please forget me.

The boy cried, the girl cried too. They both fell silent! Silence reigned except for their sobs. Half an hour later, the boy broke the silence: Alright, I respect your decision!

Girl: Thank you, goodbye...

Boy: Goodbye, my love.

The girl hung up the phone and cried harder. That night, it rained heavily. The girl sent a text message to the boy which read: Beloved, do you see the rain outside the window? We're breaking up, even heaven feels sadder than us. Heaven cries harder than we do. Ha... You must live a happier life than me. If possible, I will definitely marry you next life. I love you. He didn't reply. He didn't call her either. On the evening of the fourth day, the boy called. The girl answered but didn't speak.

Boy: Sweetheart, please allow me to call you this one last time. This will be my final call to you. I want you to sing a song. Could you do that for me?

Girl: Sure, what song do you want me to sing?

Boy: "Dear, I'm Sorry"

After the girl finished singing the song, she asked: Are you still there?

Boy: Yes, sweetheart, I'm very sleepy, I want to sleep now.

Before the girl could respond, the boy had already hung up the phone! Soon after, she received a text message from the boy which read: Sweetheart, I'm sorry. Making you sing "Dear, I'm Sorry" was my way of telling you how I feel. By the time you receive this message, I will have gone to a place without worries, only happiness. Sorry, I couldn't live without you. So I'm leaving. Since we cannot be together in this life, I will definitely marry you in the next life. That's what you said, so don't go back on your word. Be happy and live well, alright? That's my final wish. I will be very happy here. Don't worry about me. The woman I love most in this life...

After reading the message, the girl fainted due to overwhelming sadness.

It turned out that after calling the girl, the boy took sleeping pills and committed suicide. This is real-life love! If you're moved, please leave your mark! Finally, may all lovers in the world end up together...
