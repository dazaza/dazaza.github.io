Car beautification and decoration: Don't trade safety for personality

With the increase in private cars, car beautification and decoration has become a pursuit for car owners. Industry insiders believe that enhancing the appearance of one's car should be done with both advantages and disadvantages in mind, knowing what to do and what not to do; otherwise, it may backfire and lead to poor results.

Liu Jun, a mechanic at a car repair company in Changchun, told reporters that many people come here for repairs because there are too many hanging decorations inside their cars, obstructing the driver's view and leading to accidents.

He reminds car owners that when decorating their cars, they should not focus solely on aesthetics while ignoring practicality and safety, which can sometimes affect driving visibility. The "Road Traffic Safety Law of the People's Republic of China" clearly stipulates that car hanging decorations should not interfere with the driver's line of sight.

Regarding some individuals who privately install sunroofs on their cars, industry experts believe that adding a sunroof to a car without one will destroy its overall structure, posing safety hazards to drivers and passengers. Some cars, after installing sunroofs, experience leaks due to poor sealing, causing severe corrosion of the roof, all of which can affect driving safety.

According to Master Liu, there are certain things to keep in mind when modifying or decorating a car. For example, when installing anti-theft facilities, do not alter the electrical circuitry; when adding a "big bumper," do not remove the original bumper; do not trade safety for individuality.

Like house renovation, car decoration is also embracing a minimalist style. Many mid-to-high-end sedans in China come fully equipped from the factory, while more economical models only need an anti-theft system, seat covers, floor mats, footrests, and window films installed to be basically ready. As for cushions, air fresheners, and small hanging decorations, a little embellishment is enough; otherwise, it might appear messy, lacking taste and potentially creating driving safety hazards.

Regardless, the primary consideration in car decoration should still be health, safety, convenience, and practicality.

Experts in car beautification, decoration, and modification offer the following suggestions:

In general, wax removal, installation of anti-theft systems, interior decoration, and audio-visual systems are necessary. Additionally, the seats are the most noticeable part of a car, and choosing leather or fabric covers or various seat designs reflects the owner's taste. However, whether you choose leather or fabric, just remember two standards: comfort and aesthetics. Of course, selecting floor mats is also essential; the best choice for car floor mats is those with no odor, easy cleaning, and good fire resistance. The trunk is generally best lined with rubber flooring from a practical perspective. After completing these basic decorations, the rest is about how to make your beloved car reflect your personal taste and individuality. Various types of car perfumes, differently shaped small hanging decorations, convenient and practical storage boxes, tissue holders, and even custom car paint jobs, are all choices that showcase your individuality.

1. Express individuality rationally through modifications

The booming car market has driven the development of the car modification industry. Especially young car owners who like to express individuality have made their beloved cars unique.

However, how and what to modify? Relevant regulations in our country have been established; otherwise, the car won't pass inspection and could even create safety hazards. The new "Road Traffic Safety Law" explicitly states that no unit or individual shall assemble motor vehicles or arbitrarily alter the already registered structure, construction, or characteristics of motor vehicles. Vehicle structures include body color, length, width, height, engine, and relevant technical parameters.

Therefore, the legality of vehicle modifications depends on whether the vehicle matches the photo on the registration certificate and the technical parameters at the time of manufacture. If it doesn't match, it won't pass the annual inspection.

It is understood that currently, there are generally two situations for car modifications in our country: one refers to specialized manufacturers that produce modified cars using engines, chassis, or assemblies approved by the state, redesigning and modifying them into cars different from the original model; the second refers to licensed cars that undergo technical modifications for specific purposes based on the original assembly. Modified cars are collectively referred to as modified vehicles. When licensed cars undergo modifications, they must register with the vehicle management office and submit a modification technical report for review and approval before proceeding with modifications. After completion, they must also go to the vehicle management office to handle modification change procedures.

Industry insiders believe that choosing modification projects suitable for actual use needs and the condition of the car body is crucial, avoiding blind modifications and staying rational to avoid falling into car modification pitfalls.

2. Secrets behind beauty products

It is learned that some unscrupulous car beauty shops use domestically produced anti-explosion films to impersonate imported goods from Europe and America; even worse, substandard sun protection films are passed off as high-quality ones. Substandard products cost hundreds of yuan per roll, while genuine ones cost over a thousand yuan, with a significant difference between the fake and real ones. Substandard products fade within a year or two and fail to provide sun protection. Besides anti-explosion films, there are tricks involved in car leather seat covers. Uninformed consumers don't know that car leather covers are divided into A, B, and C grades. Some car decoration shops pass off C-grade leather as A-grade leather, which fades and changes smell after a year or two.

There are also car beauty shops that deceive newly purchased car owners by installing counterfeit audio equipment. Although it is cheaper than the genuine product by nearly half, there are no manuals or quality assurance cards, making it impossible to warranty.

3. Pseudo-experts pretending

According to industry insiders, low professional standards among employees and lack of standardized technical operation standards are one of the weaknesses of today's car beauty service market. Many beauty maintenance workers only master some basic automotive electromechanical principles, and for the use of maintenance and beauty products, they mostly operate according to the manual, rarely studying or not studying their working principles at all. Moreover, new technologies in the automotive industry are being applied more widely, such as computer systems and electronic technology upgrades, which non-professional maintenance workers cannot troubleshoot.

Nowadays, many roadside shops can only handle minor tasks but still pretend to be "technical experts" without equipment, technology, or professional personnel, making arbitrary modifications to customers' cars. Clearly, their service motto can only be "cheat as many as possible."
