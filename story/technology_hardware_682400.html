2008 Year-end Summary of the Seed Management Station: The year 2008 was comprehensively marked by the implementation of the spirit of the 17th Party Congress, it was an Olympic year for Beijing, the 30th anniversary of reform and opening up, and a great year to carry out the eleventh five-year plan. In 2008, under the correct leadership of the Planting Service Center, guided by Deng Xiaoping Theory and the important thought of the Three Represents, we seriously studied and implemented the spirit of the Party Congress, deeply implemented the scientific outlook on development, closely surrounded safe Olympics with good service, and well completed the work plan set earlier. Below is a brief summary report.

**One, Strengthening Party Construction and Ideological Political Work, Promoting the Work**

In one year, the Party branch of the Seed Management Station conducted in-depth study and implementation of the Party Congress conference spirit, comprehensively implemented the speech spirit of General Secretary Hu Jintao at the Central Committee's seventh plenary session, carried out the municipal and district party Lianzheng construction working conference spirit, closely followed the district Party committee and district government’s implementation program work requirements, firmly grasped the implementation of the responsibility system for party conduct and Lianzheng construction, and advanced the punishment and prevention system construction according to administer thoroughly, integrated processing, both punishment and prevention, paying attention to prevention. This obtained certain results.
