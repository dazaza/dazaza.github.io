The price of wild croakers exceeds thousand yuan, and hotels are treading on thin ice in holding Dragon Boat Festival banquets.

by qozrjzo1 on 2012-02-25 19:52:38

Our hotel doesn't supply Donghai wild large yellow croakers this year. The price is over 1000 yuan per jin, and not many people order it, so this year we have launched an imported wild yellow croaker, which is more affordable at 128 yuan per serving." Yesterday, the manager of a four-star hotel in the urban area revealed to the reporter. The Dragon Boat Festival will be on the 28th day. As a characteristic dish for the Dragon Boat Festival, the price of yellow croakers will naturally soar. Many hotels have introduced "five yellow" dishes this year, but they don't seem very interested in Donghai wild large yellow croakers because fewer people consume them, and they dare not take such risks. Yesterday, I inquired about the situation of the Dragon Boat Banquet from several high-end hotels in the urban area, and the results were unexpectedly quiet. Some hotels disclosed that there would be wedding banquets on the Dragon Boat Festival day; other hotels said that it would be like usual days without special promotions, only adding some "five yellow" dishes; while some hotels indicated that Shaoxing residents are accustomed to eating at home during the Dragon Boat Festival, so they pay more attention to the upcoming college entrance examination catering business opportunities. A food and beverage department manager of a four-star hotel in the urban area told the reporter that their hotel used to launch Donghai wild large yellow croakers every year, with a price of over 1000 yuan per 500 grams, but the actual number of customers was very small, plus the short shelf life of yellow croakers, putting pressure on the hotel's cost. This time, the imported wild yellow croakers come from Southeast Asia, available at Lapis Casa Hotel in Shanghai, with a price of 128 yuan per serving, but the price is rising every day now, and the hotel is also losing money. Yellow croakers have always been best-sellers before the Dragon Boat Festival. The price of high-end wild Donghai large yellow croakers is over 1000 yuan per 500 grams this year, with a quantity similar to last year, and very few. Traders said that you need to book half a month or even earlier to get the goods. In the Dàyún and Dōngshèng markets in the urban area, I found that there didn't seem to be much seafood at the seafood stalls. A seafood trader told me that starting from June 1st, the annual "fishing ban period" begins, and the amount of seafood will definitely decrease then. Most of the large yellow croakers sold at the stalls are farmed ones, with a price of around 20 yuan per 500 grams. Meanwhile, the price of wild small yellow croakers has also risen significantly. Besides yellow croakers, the price of loaches has slightly increased as well, currently selling at 22 yuan per 500 grams, and larger loaches are even more expensive. Additionally, the prices of cucumbers, salted duck eggs, and other Dragon Boat Festival foods remain relatively stable. A trader revealed that Shaoxing residents do not like to purchase festival foods in advance, so the prices of yellow croakers and loaches will continue to rise these days, making it quite unprofitable to eat them during the Dragon Boat Festival. Once the Dragon Boat Festival is over, the prices of these products will fall.
