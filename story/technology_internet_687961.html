A Review of the Top Ten Online Marketing Strategies

by izhaoqian669 on 2012-03-02 22:04:08

With the continuous heating up of Internet advertising, enterprises need more precise customer communication media when facing diverse advertising placement platforms such as search engines, portal sites, social media, blogs, etc. Enterprises are facing more and more choices when selecting online marketing channels. Here, we will briefly analyze the advantages and disadvantages of various online marketing methods.

Search Engines: Finding Vitality in Tradition

In 2010, the usage rate of search engines surpassed online music for the first time, becoming the top application for internet users. The usage rate of search engines reached 81.9%, with a user base of 375 million. In today's rapidly expanding Internet information environment, the status of traditional portal sites has somewhat declined, while search engines, as the driving force behind the development of the Internet, increasingly exhibit the characteristics of a "new gateway."

However, with the increase in data volume and the popularization of bidding rankings, the rate of invalid clicks has been rising year by year. According to data from a professional survey agency, in a sampling survey of 1000 cases, the inefficiency rate was over 30%.

Bidding-style Online Marketing: Gradual Surge Trend

In 2011, the first bidding-style online marketing website, ZhaoQian.com (www.iZhaoQian.com), was born. According to official statistics, the current number of online members has reached the ten-thousand level, with hundreds of companies promoting online. It is introduced that netizens can register on ZhaoQian.com before purchasing products or services, which is considered as issuing a tender. After purchasing goods or services from anywhere, uploading invoices allows them to receive promotion fees from all bidding companies—even if they did not purchase products from these companies. Even without buying products from the bidding companies, one can still receive money from these bidding companies, allowing consumers to truly experience the feeling of being a super VIP and realizing the ideal of consumer sovereignty.

The goal of ZhaoQian.com is to become a one-stop labor service exchange and flexible work organization platform, enabling ordinary consumers to gain value and benefit from every action they take online, achieving returns and happy experiences, satisfying the demand for everyone to easily participate in online world work and obtain real income at any time.

On this platform, with consumer sovereignty at the center, starting from the consumer's needs, focusing on consumer welfare. Starting point: One-stop investigation of consumer demands, promotion of consumer demands, service of consumer demands, management of consumer demands, satisfaction of consumer demands; endpoint: Decomposition of the elements of each link in product production (from product design plan collection, market research, development subcontracting, manufacturing outsourcing, advertising promotion, logistics distribution to customer service), attempting creative standardization, modularization, quantification, and automation of network transactions, so that consumers can have the opportunity to participate in production customization and marketing processes as flexible workers, gaining considerable income returns or consumer welfare returns. ZhaoQian.com has currently opened personal open bidding functions, where personal members earn paid survey fees and promotion fees by participating in product bidding promotions and surveys. A market department staff member joked to reporters, "Now there are many white-collar workers on ZhaoQian.com who can earn thousands of yuan every month, it can be said that they make money while shopping!"

For enterprises, bidding-style survey promotion is arranged according to the specific product or service needs bid by consumers, providing targeted and interactive precise advertising placements and product surveys. On one hand, it precisely delivers information about products and brands that meet consumer needs to those who are bidding; on the other hand, it accurately obtains feedback information on consumer product needs surveys, providing action guidelines for product improvement. Interacting with consumers through bidding-style promotion surveys benefits enterprises twofold. The bidding-style survey promotion method of ZhaoQian.com achieves the ultimate effect of advertisements.

Currently, the bidding-style online marketing model has become an emerging precise marketing channel, gaining rapid popularity and attention.

Weibo: Pain and Joy Together

In 2010, the scale of Weibo users in China reached 63.11 million, accounting for 13.8% of internet users. Overall, domestic enterprises are just beginning to use Weibo marketing and are still in the trial stage. Although the number of companies setting up official Weibo accounts is increasing daily, very few companies can successfully conduct Weibo marketing. Most Weibo marketing efforts are simple and homogeneous, making it difficult to engage user participation.

Water can carry a boat but also capsize it. Based on Weibo public sentiment monitoring and crisis public relations in 2011 also need to be on the agenda. Weibo is an extremely interactive and fast-spreading tool, with information spreading even faster than the media, and it has a strong social media attribute. Weibo has "opened" the way for corporate crises, making them occur more frequently, more suddenly, more harmful, and faster spreading, becoming one of the important factors triggering corporate crises.

Video Websites: Integration Pending Battle

After breaking through successfully in 2008 and continuing to develop in 2009, video marketing exploded in 2010. With the deepening integration of the three networks, online videos are penetrating into traditional TV terminals and mobile terminals, gradually showing great power. Video marketing has become an increasingly important part of integrated marketing communications, attracting more and more industry insiders.

Mobile Internet: Emerging Market

Modern society is showing "fragmentation," with people's time and attention scattered. Mobile terminals appear in the form of "lightweight" and "patch-type" media, filling the fragmented life gaps. Then based on the "intimate type," even the underwear-type media of the mobile Internet, capturing all the fragmented time of modern society, deeply satisfying the media consumption needs of modern society.

As the internet speed becomes faster and mobile terminals become more powerful, the opportunities and temptations revealed by the mobile internet are making the internet industry unable to stop. At the 2010 China Internet Conference, Vice Minister of the Ministry of Industry and Information Technology Xi Guohua stated that with the development of 3G and the enhancement of mobile communication and WEB2.0 technology, the mobile internet will surely become a bigger emerging market.

Word-of-Mouth Marketing: Order after Chaos

In September 2009, the monthly coverage of communities exceeded 2 billion people, and communities also became one of the important channels for internet users to seek information before shopping, undoubtedly promoting the quantity and stickiness of community users well. Word-of-mouth marketing in communities thus developed rapidly.

Under such a favorable background, word-of-mouth marketing developed rapidly in 2010 but also encountered growing pains. Word-of-mouth marketing has gone through many storms, not because there was a problem with word-of-mouth marketing itself, but because there were issues with the understanding and operation of word-of-mouth marketing. After great chaos, there must be great order. In 2011, word-of-mouth marketing will start a professional, non-toxic green journey.

Industry Websites

While the number of online users is growing rapidly, traditional information websites (portals and vertical) are facing the situation of users being continuously分流ed. Business, social networking, and entertainment are currently hot topics in the internet field, and traditional websites are facing unprecedented challenges: firstly, the分流of users; secondly, the business model of "content-advertising" is becoming increasingly single; most importantly, people no longer satisfy themselves with simple product information but need comprehensive services from product to application, delving into every link. What direction will vertical websites go in the future? The answer is closer to users and deeper internet services. Internet applications are quickly penetrating into every aspect of people's lives, and vertical websites can play a more important role in this process. Morgan Stanley's report pointed out that after studying IT, digital, aviation tourism, automobiles, and other highly informatized products, vertical network media played an absolutely advantageous media role.

Continuous Growth of E-commerce

From flash sales to group buying in 2010, the e-commerce sector was particularly lively. Among the overall urban mass groups, it was 1.3% in 2008 and 11.8% in 2010. Among China's newly rich population, 50.3% are engaged in online shopping, especially after the economic crisis, many affluent consumers found that top brands could also be purchased at relatively cheaper prices online, so online shopping began to become popular. As the main force of online shopping—those born in the 70s, 80s, and 90s—gradually change their shopping patterns and become more reliant on online shopping, online shopping will continue to grow in 2011.
