Our city carries out the activities of Guanxi Greening and winter-spring water conservancy construction.

by cits123573 on 2012-02-15 11:37:57

On the morning of February 14th, the Municipal Party Committee and the Municipal Government carried out a tree planting and winter-spring water conservancy construction activity throughout the city called "Green Full BavGui". More than 50,000 people participated in this event including government officials, members of the Communist Youth League, ordinary citizens, and soldiers. They planted more than 110,000 trees, cleared about 50 kilometers of channels, and removed approximately 5000 cubic meters of silt.

Leaders of the city including Liu Jun, Li Zhigang, Su Zenglin, Pan Yongjian, Shi Donglong, Chen Lihua, Qin Chuncheng, Jiang Bingkui, Wang Zhuguang, Zhang Xiaowu, Ye Zhaoquan, Guo Weqi, Lu Shengfa, Shi Chunlian, Xiao Lihua, Zhou Wei, Wang Jianyi, Yuan Xuxiang, Jiang Tingchun, Commander Xu Qingduan and Political Commissar Hu Anling of Guilin Militia District, President Liao Shaokun of the Intermediate People's Court of the city, and Procurator Meng Yaohong of the People's Procuratorate of the city respectively participated in the activities at different labor points.

It is understood that the purpose of carrying out voluntary tree planting activities is to implement the instructions of the Autonomous Region Party Committee and the government on the "Green Full BavGui" forestation greening activities, scientifically protect the Lijiang River, accelerate the construction of the Garden Expo Park, create new green spots in the city, and spark a new climax of nationwide voluntary tree planting. On that day, our city established 12 tree planting sites covering Lingui County, Lingchuan County, Xing'an County, Yangshuo County, Pingle County, and the five urban districts. These covered the channel greening, urban greening, and village greening of the entire city, divided into two main battlefields: one was the ecological construction project along the Lijiang River, and the other was the beautification project of "one road, three parks, and three schools" (Wanfu Road; the Garden Expo Park of the second Guangxi Garden Horticultural Exposition, Yuzi Paradise, Yanshan Park; Yanshan Campus of Guangxi Normal University, Guilin University of Technology, Guilin Tourism College).

The Second Guangxi Gardening Horticultural Exposition will be held in Guilin in September this year, and the construction of the Garden Expo Park has gradually started. Carrying out tree planting activities in the Garden Expo Park is very timely. After arriving at the Garden Expo Park, leaders of the four municipal bodies including Liu Jun and Li Zhigang joined the cadres and masses to enthusiastically participate in voluntary tree planting. Placing seedlings, swinging shovels, cultivating soil, watering... Cadres, masses, and soldiers worked enthusiastically in pairs or groups. In no time, rows of saplings stood upright in the wind, adding the first patch of green to the Garden Expo Park. Meanwhile, other tree planting sites also presented a lively scene of labor. Along the banks of the Lijiang River, beside the Guiyang Highway, on both sides of the Gui-Xing Expressway, in the Huixian Wetland of Lingui and Jinzi Ridge of Pingle County, everyone worked with high enthusiasm, using shovels to dig pits and cultivate soil to beautify their homes and make the urban and rural areas more charming. In Huixian Wetland, a local resident told reporters that Huixian Wetland is an important functional area of the Guilin World Tourism City. Voluntary tree planting not only enhances everyone's awareness of loving and protecting greenery and the ecological environment but is also a concrete action to support the construction of the World Tourism City.

According to incomplete statistics, a total of 52,000 people in the city participated in tree planting activities that day, planting more than 110,000 trees including osmanthus, weeping willows, and metasequoias.

Vice Mayor Zhou Wei introduced that since 2008, our city has deeply carried out voluntary tree planting activities focusing on stone mountain greening and Lijiang River bank greening. In response to the situation where most stone mountains and riverbanks have barren soil layers, poor planting conditions, fragile natural ecology, and difficult greening, measures such as "combining mountain closure for forest regeneration with artificial forestation" and "combining mass forestation with professional greening" were adopted. Artificially carrying soil, fertilizer, and water up the mountain for tree planting and greening improved the green coverage rate of stone mountains. At the same time, native plants resistant to water were selected to carry out large-scale greening plantings along rivers such as the Lijiang River and Taohua River passing through the city, playing an important role in embellishing the landscape and improving the urban ecology. In more than three years, our city has successively planted over 200,000 plants of various kinds, achieving good ecological benefits.

Water conservancy is the lifeline of agriculture. Vigorously developing water conservancy and improving agricultural infrastructure can provide guarantees for rural development, increased agricultural efficiency, and increased farmers' income. After participating in the voluntary tree planting activities, leaders of the city including Liu Jun and Li Zhigang went to Etou Village, Tangxiang Township, Xiangshan District to participate in water conservancy construction activities. The Xiganqu Guilin branch channel located in Etou Village was severely silted up, with a thick layer of debris accumulated in the channel. A shovel would stir up a foul smell. Without hesitation, leaders including Liu Jun and Li Zhigang picked up shovels and went down into the channel. Everyone had clear divisions of labor, some scooping sludge in the channel, some transporting sludge on the shore, and some cutting weeds along the edge of the channel. After intensive labor, about one kilometer of the channel was cleaned up. At other water conservancy construction labor sites, after joint efforts by cadres and masses, six channels were dredged and cleared, creating favorable conditions for spring farming production. Among them, the Qixing District organized more than a hundred people to clear a 1.5-kilometer-long channel in Pantang Village, Zhujiang Village Committee.

Vice Mayor Wang Jianyi introduced that this year our city invested 2 billion yuan to carry out winter-spring water conservancy construction projects. Due to early arrangements and planning last winter, the construction work achieved significant results and has already completed more than 85% of the construction tasks.

This tree planting activity will play an important role in embellishing the landscape and improving the urban ecology. Guilin China International Travel Service (CITS) calls for cherishing precious tourism resources, and Guilin will become more beautiful.
