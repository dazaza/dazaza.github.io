I just feel sorry for my own incompetence. Yesterday, Dong Jie, who has always had a smooth career and love life, suddenly made such a sigh on Weibo.

It turned out that Dong Jie's two-year-old son Dingding was unable to enroll in a certain bilingual kindergarten because he is Chinese, which made her, as a mother, very upset. Subsequently, Pan Yueming also lamented on Weibo that some people running kindergartens should also attend kindergarten! Pitiful!

This incident immediately sparked heated discussions among netizens, with some netizens indicating that this phenomenon exists in many domestic bilingual (international) kindergartens. (Click on the picture to enter Dong Jie's Weibo)

Dong Jie's Weibo: Child rejected from entering kindergarten due to being Chinese

Dingding, born in 2009, has reached the age of going to kindergarten, and Dong Jie and Pan Yueming have been actively looking for a kindergarten for their child. What was originally a simple matter has troubled the couple.

Yesterday, Dong Jie revealed on Weibo that she was looking for a kindergarten for her son, but before even entering, she was questioned by a security guard at a certain kindergarten. The first question was whether the parents were foreign nationals. After receiving a negative response, the security guard then asked if the child was a foreign national. Receiving another negative response, the security guard hesitantly said: We don't accept children from the mainland.

The security guard's words left Dong Jie speechless, and she helplessly expressed on Weibo that such incidents are common, I just feel sorry for my own incompetence.

Pan Yueming's Weibo: Domestic original equipment is not liked by anyone, it's pitiful

Not long after, husband Pan Yueming also forwarded this Weibo post and self-deprecatingly said, looked at several bilingual kindergartens, seemingly all only accepting foreign children, giving the impression that domestically produced ones are not liked by anyone, maybe this way it appears more high-end. At the same time, Pan Yueming also frankly stated, suggesting opening abroad would be more convenient, right? Some people running kindergartens should also attend kindergarten! Alas, pitiful!

After these two Weibo posts were released, they immediately attracted a large number of netizens to come and watch, sparking heated discussions about children entering school. Many netizens expressed understanding for the feelings of this young couple, as parents always hope to give their children the best. Actually, there's no need to be so hard on oneself, it's not like there are only a few kindergartens in the world. Meanwhile, some enthusiastic netizens extended invitations, come to our kindergarten.

However, some netizens angrily expressed, since these kindergartens are opened in China, why refuse Chinese children? It's simply incomprehensible and absurd.

Relevant Response: Find one that meets the conditions next

Tianfu Morning Post reporter contacted Mr. Zhao, Dong Jie's publicity director yesterday. Regarding Dong Jie and Pan Yueming's complaints, Mr. Zhao frankly stated, we won't respond to this, nor can we respond.

Mr. Zhao explained that it is actually quite a common social phenomenon for children not to find suitable kindergartens. Dong Jie and Pan Yueming are like ordinary couples, although they carry the halo of celebrities, they still encounter these difficulties in actual daily life. At the same time, Mr. Zhao revealed that Dong Jie is now very helpless, if the conditions are not met, it cannot be forced. Next, they will look for a kindergarten that meets the conditions.

A Doubt: Why do celebrities love bilingual kindergartens?

Ma Yili: So the child doesn't receive too much attention

Due to Dingding's kindergarten issue, many netizens also expressed confusion, why do celebrities' first choice always be bilingual kindergartens? In fact, in many cases, celebrities choose international kindergartens for their children not to make them appear different, but rather to help them better integrate into the environment.

Ma Yili once revealed, when we were looking for a kindergarten for Aima (Ma Yili's child), we thought about whether we should choose those so-called international kindergartens, and later decided that we should go. But it's not because of vanity. During early education classes, when the teacher knew that Aima was the daughter of me and Wen Zhang, they would treat her exceptionally well for no reason, she didn't do anything special, yet the teacher would keep praising her. Hearing these things scared me, I hope the child grows up like an ordinary girl. If she goes to an international kindergarten, the teachers there and other parents might not even know who Ma Yili or Wen Zhang is, allowing Aima to grow up ordinarily.
