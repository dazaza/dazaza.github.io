Ouyang Zhenhua plays Jiang Dong-geun

Encounter

Jiang Dong-geun came to Hong Kong from the mainland as a child and lived with his father and Hong Kong wife Song Chu-qiao. Geun was very dissatisfied with his father abandoning his mother and remarrying someone else in Hong Kong. Determined to study hard, he became self-reliant and eventually entered medical school to become a doctor, leaving his father and bringing his mother Xi to Hong Kong to live together. Mother and son relied on each other.

After graduating from medical school, Geun was hired by Tang Jing-tian's pharmaceutical company as a research doctor and successfully developed a highly effective anti-cancer drug. However, as a businessman, Tian cut costs and rushed the drug to market, contradicting Geun's principles. Tian forced Geun to reduce the number of clinical trial participants, excluding some patients whose conditions were improving, leading to their deaths. Unable to face this, Geun became disillusioned with the pharmaceutical company and resigned to become a clinic doctor.

Geun devoted himself to research and running the clinic, becoming somewhat reclusive and poor at socializing. For years, he only had one close friend and capable partner, Si-mei Wen Jia-ling. Geun regarded Ling as a brother and was unaware of her feelings for him. Despite being confined to the clinic, Geun met the love of his life, Fang Xiao-fang. Fang suffered from chemical sensitivity, breaking out in rashes whenever she had an episode, causing her great distress. During the treatment of Fang, Geun gradually became attracted to her cute and straightforward personality. Unfortunately, medical ethics dictate that doctors cannot have romantic relationships with patients within three years, so Geun kept his feelings hidden. Soon after, Fang disappeared. Geun could only hope to find Fang after three years to confess his feelings. Meanwhile, Geun worked hard on relevant drugs, hoping to cure Fang's sensitivity syndrome completely.

Three years later, Geun found Fang's whereabouts, but at this time, he encountered a traffic accident! On the brink of death, Geun met the Devil Tai Fung (played by Lin Feng), but because of unfinished wishes, Geun pleaded for mercy and eventually survived. Upon waking up, the first thing Geun did was to confess his love to Fang, only to be rejected, greatly disappointing Geun. After recovering, Geun returned to the clinic, expecting everything to return to normal, but unexpectedly met a patient without a heartbeat - Tai Fung, realizing that he was the Devil he met on the brink of death!

Background

At the moment when the inexperienced Geun reunited with Fang, he encountered a fatal car accident, colliding with Tai Fung (played by Lin Feng), both sustaining serious injuries. Geun's heart stopped momentarily, and while on his deathbed in the hospital, he met "the Devil" in a dream. Geun pleaded with the Devil, claiming he was a good person with an uncompleted drug that would help many patients. Once completed, he could also confess to an important woman, asking not to take his life prematurely.

After coming back to life, Geun immediately confessed his love to Fang, only to be rejected, leaving Geun disappointed. At this time, Fung visited Geun as a patient. Geun discovered that Fung had no heartbeat, shocked to learn that Fung was dead and had transformed into a Devil come to claim Geun's life!
