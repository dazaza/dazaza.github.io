According to reports by Southern Metropolis Daily, reporter Lan Xinguang, yesterday morning, on the street in front of the office building of the Xunjing Team of Hongmei Public Security Bureau in Dongguan City, about 10 patrol officers of the team were seen openly barbecuing a wild cat by the roadside, one of them claimed he was making hot pot. This action caused many passing citizens to condemn it. The director of the Hongmei Public Security Bureau said that according to preliminary understanding, these people should not be the police officers of their Xunjing Team, whether they are security personnel of an enterprise or security personnel of the Xunjing Team is still under investigation. He indicated that if they are their security personnel, once verified, they will be dismissed.

Yesterday afternoon, the reporter called the Hongmei Public Security Bureau to understand the situation. Director Luo Youtong of the bureau said that according to his preliminary understanding, these people should not be the police officers of their Xunjing Team. "Police officers would not do such things," he said. If they wear "police uniforms", they should be security personnel, but whether they are security personnel of an enterprise or security personnel of the Xunjing Team, he is still investigating. "This kind of behavior affects the image of the police." He indicated that if they are their security personnel, once verified, they will be dismissed.

Yesterday morning, several men were seen openly burning a wild cat by the roadside. Photographed by Southern Metropolis Daily reporter Lan Xinguang.

At just past noon yesterday, when the reporter passed by Kangxia Road in Hongmei Town, he saw a plume of thick smoke rising in the distance. Citizens and passers-by thought something had happened and gathered around. The reporter curiously asked a motorcycle taxi driver who was picking up customers nearby, he said: "Those people are preparing food."

"What are they eating?" The reporter walked over and observed carefully. On both sides of this road are the Hongmei Police Station and the Hongmei Patrol Police Team office building, with several patrol cars and riot police vehicles parked around. In between the two office buildings by the roadside, two men wearing "patrol" uniform clothes piled a few bricks on the ground, placed two iron rods on top, then placed the freshly slaughtered wild cat on it, using straw to burn off the cat's fur. Around these two men, there were seven or eight men dressed in the same uniform standing nearby watching.

Man: Roast off the cat's fur to make hot pot

One of the men introduced to the reporter that this wild cat was just caught by them, they would first burn off its fur, then make it into hot pot. After seeing the reporter taking photos, some men avoided him. By around 12:20, the two men had finished burning off the wild cat's fur, then took it into the opposite Hongmei Patrol Police Team office building.
