A man held a rose and successfully proposed to a female attendant on the EMU train.

by lhltckfrtg on 2012-02-27 10:35:40

At 12:28 yesterday, Luo Lin boarded the D5183 EMU train crewed by He Wenjing in Nanchong, but his final destination was not only Chengdu. Luo Lin held 11 pink roses anxiously sitting in carriage No. 4 waiting for He Wenjing's arrival. The long five minutes passed, but she did not appear.

In fact, He Wenjing had not fallen in love with Luo Lin at first sight, but eventually Luo Lin won her heart through his persistence and thoughtfulness.

On the platform, there was a waiting love.

April 17, 2011, Chengdu to Beijing West D318 train

Riding the Chengdu-Beijing sleeping car, the train attendant became a dream lover

Holding the ticket to propose marriage, he successfully won the beauty's hand

Why choose to propose on the EMU? In fact, they met and fell in love because of the EMU.

Tianfu Morning Post reporter Zhang Shu (photos provided by Chengdu Railway Bureau Chengdu Passenger Segment)

To find out the situation, He Wenjing called Luo Lin three times repeatedly, and Luo Lin opened his sleepy eyes. "At that moment, I felt this girl was the one who often appeared in my dreams." Ten minutes later, he decided to look for He Wenjing, but she was cold and distant. In the last three hours of the journey, Luo Lin would take any opportunity to talk to He Wenjing and got her phone number.

Luo Lin did not give up. Afterwards, whenever He Wenjing was on duty on the EMU sleeping car, Luo Lin would come to the station with flowers to express his love. To let He Wenjing eat the freshest cherries, Luo Lin specially flew to send them to her. Gradually, He Wenjing accepted this persistent and thoughtful young man.

The two young people who met because of the EMU completed their proposal on the EMU.

A romantic proposal on the EMU

Origin

The successful proposer Luo Lin said that in order to be with his beloved day and night, he would resign from his job in Beijing and return to Chengdu to develop.

With the help of conductor Huang Jia, He Wenjing was called to carriage No. 4. At this time, Luo Lin knelt down holding roses and loudly said to her who they had been in love for nearly a year: "Last April 17th, this ticket made us meet, and then fall in love. I am willing to protect you and love you for a lifetime. Are you willing to marry me?" Luo Lin took out a ticket that witnessed the beginning of their love, the 2011 April 17th Chengdu to Beijing West D318 train ticket, and formally proposed to He Wenjing.

When Luo Lin waited for his girlfriend's answer, the surrounding passengers shouted loudly, "Marry him! Marry him!" Luo Lin saw that his beloved accepted the flowers and immediately took out the prepared ring and put it on He Wenjing's finger.

Luo Lin held 11 roses and a ticket that witnessed the beginning of their love, knelt down and loudly said to He Wenjing: "Are you willing to marry me?"

This EMU trip ended when Luo Lin asked He Wenjing when they could meet again. He Wenjing said, "Take more EMU sleeping cars." The second meeting was because He Wenjing's train arrived at Beijing West Station at 7 o'clock in the morning. In order not to miss it, Luo Lin had been waiting near Beijing West Station the day before. The next morning, he showed up on the platform on time with roses and two handwritten love letters and expressed his feelings to He Wenjing. Unexpectedly, He Wenjing politely refused him in a text message.

"Because of love, it does not easily hurt, so everything is happy; because of love, it grows simply, still can go crazy for you anytime." A song "Because of Love" by Faye Wong and Eason Chan makes people feel the faint love deeply. In reality, because of love, a "EMU romance" was performed. Yesterday, on the D5183 train from Nanchong to Chengdu, the two young people who met because of the EMU completed their proposal on the EMU, and their sweet EMU love bore fruit.

The male protagonist of this "EMU romance" is named Luo Lin, a white-collar worker in Beijing. The female protagonist is named He Wenjing, an EMU attendant - a "train sister", with a sweet and lovely appearance and warm smile. They are both from Dazhou.

Last August, the EMU sleeping car service stopped, but their relationship did not fade because of it.

Last April, the EMU sleeping car between Chengdu and Beijing was still in operation. Luo Lin, who was briefly staying in Chengdu, was preparing to return to Beijing, "I originally planned to buy a plane ticket back, but while buying tickets for my parents to return home to Dazhou, I also bought a ticket for the EMU sleeping car to Beijing."

Among the train attendants on duty on this EMU sleeping car was He Wenjing, responsible for carriages No. 10 to 12. Luo Lin, who was supposed to stay in compartment No. 3 of carriage No. 11, stayed in carriage No. 10 by mistake. He Wenjing thought Luo Lin was a passenger in the soft seat carriage who sneaked into the sleeping car without permission, "At that time, I asked him if this was his berth? He answered 'yes'."

Luo Lin, who slept in the wrong berth, was woken up by He Wenjing. When he opened his eyes, he was moved: This girl, isn't she the person who often appears in my dreams?
