by xaikxh36 on 2011-09-15 09:49:14

In 2010, under the guidance and care of the district education bureau, I was able to seriously study the party and national guidelines and policies, abide by laws and regulations, love my profession, rely on the broad masses of faculty and staff, work together, unite in struggle, strive for self-improvement, add pressure to myself, constantly move upward and forward, comprehensively promote quality education. In 2005, the school's high school entrance examination results made a significant improvement, and all kinds of work have achieved certain results. Now, I will summarize the main work achievements of this year as follows:

Organizing faculty members to conduct political and professional learning to improve teacher quality

Whether it is the teacher meeting or the administrative meeting, a certain amount of time is arranged to study relevant laws and regulations of education and educational research articles. At the same time, irregularly organize party members, league members, and teachers to conduct practical learning and check learning notes.

Under the relatively tight financial situation, we prioritize and strongly support teachers' participation in new curriculum knowledge training for various subjects, encourage teachers to continue participating in various forms of learning to broaden their knowledge horizon, comprehensively improve teachers' political level and professional quality, and strengthen teachers' sense of responsibility in nurturing students.

Strengthening moral education management and purifying the environment for nurturing students

Lead the school's moral education group, with the school Youth League committee specifically responsible for class and student inspection and management work, so that moral education work can be implemented effectively. The school's school style and academic atmosphere have entered a virtuous cycle track.

Continuously strengthening the management of head teachers, regularly convene work routine meetings, study the teaching and learning situations of each class, exchange management experiences of each class, discuss the ideological dynamics of students, summarize the achievements and existing shortcomings of each class for the week. At the same time, require each class to frequently provide students with habit education based on the "Daily Behavior Standards". Through relentless efforts, students' good daily behavior habits and excellent ideological qualities are cultivated and developed. Fully utilize various public opinion and publicity platforms to strengthen patriotism and collectivism education for students. Every Monday morning after raising the national flag, there is an arrangement for student representatives and school leaders to speak under the national flag. Fully utilize the school radio station, bulletin boards, blackboards, etc., as publicity media to guide students to study hard, introduce learning methods and experiences, report various good deeds and literary works, and provide free song requests for teachers and students, making students spend every day in a relaxed and happy environment.

Pay attention to parents as powerful assistants in the school's teaching management work, let parents who care about education and love the education cause participate in the school's educational management work, establish a parent committee, regularly invite them to the school to guide and inspect the work. Their participation plays a supervisory role in the management of education and teaching, receiving consistent praise from society.

Strictly grasp routine teaching management and strive to improve the quality of education and teaching

Teaching work is the core work of the school, and teaching quality is the lifeline of the school's work. We take the quality of education and teaching as an important standard for measuring teachers' work, and as the main basis for various evaluations and commendations, forming an excellent situation where quality strengthens the school and teaching quality determines heroes.

Grasping teaching quality starts from routine teaching. We insist on weekly random inspections of lesson plans, monthly collective inspections of lesson plans, advocate collective lesson preparation, insist on preparing lessons one week in advance, and require "five fixed, five preparations, four unifications." That is, fix the time for teaching research activities, fix (prepare) the content of teaching research activities, fix the key speaker, inspect on time, and publish results regularly. Teachers of the same grade and subject in collective lesson preparation prepare teaching content, students' reality, teaching methods, learning methods, and exercises. In teaching, unify goals, unify priorities, unify progress, and unify testing.

We boldly innovate and explore in classroom teaching, incorporating teaching methods that focus on cultivating innovation and thinking abilities throughout various aspects of the teaching process. Place the focus of "preparation" on understanding students and analyzing textbooks; place the focus of "teaching" on guiding students' learning methods; place the focus of "revision" on layered requirements and classified improvements; place the focus of "guidance" on smoothing out students' psychology and thoughts; place the focus of "examination" on cultivating students' self-study ability and innovation ability, teach according to individual aptitude, often encourage students, let each student be full of confidence, and address each student's actual problems appropriately, allowing each student to happily learn knowledge and grow healthily.

We always require teachers to fully correct and revise students' homework, inspect and announce once a month. Criticize teachers who are not serious and responsible at the entire teacher meeting. For mid-term and final exams, strictly follow the requirements of the high school entrance exam. The number of students in the exam room must not exceed 40, implement cross-proctoring, cross-grading of test papers, maintain strict exam discipline, and promote the formation of an excellent academic atmosphere.

Catch up on teaching and research to promote teaching reform, cultivate innovative awareness, and comprehensively improve teaching quality

Each teaching and research group in the school persists in conducting teaching and research day activities every week. Each teaching and research group has an administrative leader stationed there, supervising and participating in the implementation and enforcement of teaching and research reforms. This year, the school assigned a vice-principal to mainly focus on teaching research work, and each teaching and research group undertakes research topics of different levels. In teaching practice, we require emphasis on teaching method reform and learning method guidance. Every semester, we ask teachers to write teaching reflections and teaching research and reform papers. Especially for teachers under 40 years old, having teaching research and reform achievements is a necessary condition for annual evaluation and year-end assessment. Therefore, the school has initially achieved a transformation of teachers from "teaching book type" to "research type," with noticeable teaching research and reform results.

Promote quality education, develop colorful extracurricular activities, and fully mobilize students' learning enthusiasm

In the past year, despite difficulties in having a complete set of teachers, we have offered all minor subjects, basically ensuring class hours for each subject. On this basis, the school has carried out a series of activities such as the "Art Activity Group," "Music Interest Group," "Sports Interest Group," and the "Literature Club," which have cultivated a large number of students with special talents. Each class also conducts various social investigation activities and social practice activities based on its own actual situation. The school also held exhibitions of students' reading notes, broadcast gymnastics, athletics, fun sports meets, ball games competitions, and other activities, enriching students' campus life, guiding students' excess energy towards meaningful things, and cultivating students' excellent interests.

Increase revenue and reduce expenditure, ensure the needs of the front line teaching, prevent and treat groups, create a civilized and clean campus

In the past year, our school has persisted in organizing后勤 personnel for policy, theory, law, and business learning, reinforcing the awareness of serving the frontline among后勤 personnel. The school strengthened the "double signature" of finance, ensuring normal income and expenditure of school funds, and no disciplinary violations occurred in financial work. At the same time, our school strictly enforced one-time fee standards, standardized the management of school property and materials, and ensured the normal operation of all school work.

The school adopted various measures such as adjusting rent to retain original residents, absorb new residents, and encourage surplus teachers to engage in part-time work and frugal learning. The school shops were publicly bid, alleviating the contradiction of school fund shortages to a certain extent, creating conditions for improving teaching conditions and employee benefits.
