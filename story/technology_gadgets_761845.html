Nokia London Launch Event, Lumia Ad先行

by geekzhang on 2013-05-13 10:14:24

Just last week, Nokia launched a new platform, Asha, targeted at emerging markets, and this week, on May 14th, Nokia will hold another launch event in London. The slogan on the invitation reads: "See what's next," followed by "The Nokia Lumia story continues." It seems that this event will focus more on the Lumia series.Over the weekend, Nokia has already released the Verizon version of the Lumia 928, available for $99 with a two-year contract. Compared to the Lumia 920, it features some hardware optimizations but lacks significant highlights. This makes the London event even more enticing as the big boss hasn't appeared yet, and the true flagship device is still hidden behind the final curtain. Remember last year's Lumia 920 launch? Nokia wouldn't treat its flagship device carelessly.According to The Verge, Nokia has started airing alluring advertisements on TV about the next Lumia model.Video source: YoukuThe video is brief, showing only the back of the new device, featuring a large lens and flash, making it a real "back-killer." The tagline is "More than your eyes can see," suggesting that this phone will once again prioritize camera functionality.Could this be the flagship device? Perhaps not. The Verge believes it resembles the previously leaked device codenamed "Catwalk," an aluminum-bodied Lumia 925, which reportedly has hardware specs similar to the Lumia 920.What's confusing is that Nokia is also preparing a 41-megapixel EOS Windows Phone, which is said to have a design similar to the Catwalk.Recalling last year's Lumia 920 launch, where the mid-range Lumia 820 was introduced alongside it, we can speculate that there will be more than one Lumia device unveiled at the London event. According to internal information obtained by ifanr, there will be more than one new model, and a series of new phones will continue to be released after the event.Main image source: The Verge
