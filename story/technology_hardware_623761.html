Since its launch, FOTON REESE has been highly favored.

by ftleisa on 2011-12-14 17:01:16

China's first National IV standard self-own-brand福田Rayson 52 meters pump truck was launched in Beijing a few days ago and drew high attention from people in the industry. Experts believe that this marks another step towards high technology, high environmental protection, and high quality world standards for Chinese pump trucks. In recent years, relying on breakthroughs in core self-owned technologies,福田Rayson has quickly established its professional and reliable market image. The newly launched National IV 52-meter pump truck has also created the "three most" of Chinese pump trucks: the most environmentally friendly pump truck, the highest pumping height with domestically produced special chassis, and the most advanced performance pump truck.

Integrated development, integrated manufacturing, and integrated service are the three major strengths of福田Rayson. In the Rayson pump truck "integrated manufacturing" plant, it can be seen that the production process especially introduces Toyota's TPS lean production model, adopting an integrated production process for the chassis and upper structure, ensuring product quality reaches leading levels. Moreover, Rayson pump trucks draw on international resources, using the global parts procurement system of Foton Motor. They use imported parts where appropriate in key components and assemblies, not only significantly increasing the lifespan and reliability of the whole vehicle but also enhancing the market competitiveness of core components, successfully achieving efficient integration of global specialized resources.

Foton Rayson's emphasis on "integrated service" makes many people feel that Rayson adheres to international standards and concepts, establishing brand value beliefs and behaviors. The company owns a nationwide network of 1,100 integrated service stations for chassis and upper structures, enabling one-stop solutions for vehicle issues. From vehicle financing to vehicle guarantees and parts financing, Rayson provides users with one-stop financial solutions. These loans have low down payments, long loan terms, low interest rates, simple procedures, flexible repayment options, and minimal burdens. This customer-centric integrated service allows consumers to practically experience the feeling of "saving money and less worry."

Wu Yuejun, Vice President of Foton Motor Group, said that starting from the previous year, Foton has unveiled the prelude to its 2020 strategy. In the next decade, Foton will follow the concept of "world standards, mid-range products," continuing to develop existing businesses while focusing on expanding five pillar industries: new energy vehicles, automotive Internet of Things, financial services, new energy, and high-end equipment manufacturing, transforming into a comprehensive transportation tool company.

Experts express that in international market competition, the "cost advantage" that Chinese companies have long relied on is gradually diminishing. Meanwhile, the "technical barriers" and "environmental barriers" set up by Western developed countries are becoming stumbling blocks for Chinese companies to expand their global markets and create world brands. The launch of福田Rayson's new pump truck with higher environmental standards and higher technical levels has shown people the determined efforts of Chinese companies to reshape the global industrial landscape.
