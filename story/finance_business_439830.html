Father of the sick child continued to hold hostages for applying relief, four charges waived by the hospital

by zxytyzzfe on 2011-05-31 11:39:40

Enthusiastic people visit little Sun Kai.

续: The critically ill child leaves a last will, the father applies for relief and hijacks Red Cross employees:

■ Nearly 190,000 yuan donated by all sectors

On the afternoon of April 27, Mr. Li from Cangzhou came to the hospital with his wife to visit Little Sun Kai. "Our couple has been paying attention to the reports about Little Sun Kai recently. Knowing that he was admitted to the Central Hospital, we specially rushed over to take a look," said Mr. Li. He also mentioned that he has a friend in Baoding who is also very concerned about Little Sun Kai, and this time they were specially entrusted to encourage the child to face difficulties bravely. "This 1200 yuan is a token of my and my wife's as well as our friend's heart, wishing Little Sun Kai a speedy recovery," said Mr. Li.

After learning about the reunion of Sun Kai's family, Mr. Zhang, who had previously donated money to Sun Kai and personally visited from Langfang, called the reporter, indicating that after returning to the Zuo Ge Zhuang Primary School in Langfang, his son Zhang Qi told his teachers and classmates about Brother Sun Kai's plight, hoping they could help him too. "The teachers were deeply moved after hearing it and are willing to bring the students to visit Sun Kai. I will guide them to Cangzhou!"

By 5 PM on April 27, Sun Kai's love account had received more than 150,000 yuan in donations. Adding the more than 37,000 yuan personally delivered by all sectors of society and loving individuals, Little Sun Kai has received nearly 190,000 yuan in donations.

■ Cangzhou Women's Federation visits Little Sun Kai

On the afternoon of April 26, three members of the Cangzhou Women's Federation went to the Hematology Department Ward No. 1 at the Cangzhou Central Hospital to visit Little Sun Kai lying in bed. The staff brought the blessings of the leaders of the Cangzhou Women's Federation, delivered 4000 yuan in love funds, and had face-to-face conversations with Sun Kai's parents, carefully asking about Little Sun Kai's condition.

It was reported that after seeing the report in our newspaper about Little Sun Kai suffering from severe aplastic anemia and his family's predicament, the chairwoman of the Cangzhou Women's Federation took it very seriously and immediately ordered the vice-chairwoman and the heads of the Children's Department and Rights Department to visit together. They encouraged Little Sun Kai and his family to enhance their confidence and courage to overcome the disease, telling them that with the attention of all sectors of society and the help, care, and support of many loving individuals, they believe that Sun Kai will recover under the expectations and blessings of all sectors of society.

■ The hospital will waive four fees

On the morning of April 27, President Wen Xiuling of the Cangzhou Central Hospital brought nutritional supplements and flowers to visit Little Sun Kai's ward. After understanding the situation of Little Sun Kai's family through our newspaper, Wen Xiuling promised to waive four fees for Little Sun Kai.

At around 10 AM on April 27, Wen Xiuling came to Little Sun Kai's bedside, detailedly inquired about his condition and treatment, and instructed Deputy Director Wang Juan of Hematology Department No. 1, "We must invite the best experts for consultation and spare no effort to treat the child's illness."

Wen Xiuling said that after reading the report about Little Sun Kai in the Yan Zhao Daily, the hospital actively transferred Little Sun Kai from Langfang for treatment and promised to waive the operation treatment fee, examination fee, bed fee, and expert consultation fee for the child. It hopes that the hospital's actions can lead all sectors of society to contribute their love and save Little Sun Kai's family.

Wen Xiuling encouraged Little Sun Kai to be strong, have the confidence to overcome the disease, know gratitude, work hard to study after recovering, and return to society when grown up.

■ A farmer woman from Xian County asks her children to donate

At noon on April 27, Bi Junlan from Guo Shangzhuang Village, Shangzhuang Town, Xian County, called the reporter to inquire about Little Sun Kai's latest situation.

Bi Junlan said that a few days ago she saw Little Sun Kai's family's plight in the newspaper and felt very sympathetic. Because her home is in the countryside and she hasn't seen the newspaper recently, she didn't know how the child was doing, so she was particularly anxious. "I am 50 years old this year, with poor economic conditions, but I still hope to donate some money to this family and contribute my love," said Bi Junlan. To help Little Sun Kai, she also called her daughter working far away in Tianjin and her son serving in the army, letting them also contribute their efforts. "I told my son to smoke less and asked my daughter to buy fewer clothes, saving money to donate to Little Sun Kai," said Bi Junlan. Hearing that all sectors of society are helping Little Sun Kai, she was deeply moved, "Sun Kai has been saved, and I am relieved. I also hope that other children with serious illnesses can be so lucky and receive the attention of the whole society."
