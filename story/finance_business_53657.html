Top Ten Websites for Finding Dropshipping and Wholesale Sources to Open an Online Store

by longyi on 2008-03-14 18:47:36

Because I wanted to open a store, I searched online for sources of goods. During this period, I also encountered many websites with viruses. Eventually, I succeeded. I thought others might be looking too, so I listed 10 excellent websites that I had visited. This way, you don't have to go through the trouble. In fact, for friends who want to open an online store, online consignment is a good choice because firstly, you don't need capital to stock up on goods, and secondly, you don't have to worry about unsold inventory. Moreover, many suppliers who provide online consignment for online stores do not charge franchise fees. Zero-cost online store opening has become the choice of many store owners. Many store owners already have their own sources of goods and want to expand their inventory. They can sell their own goods and also consign other people's products, achieving two goals at once. Here, online consignment as a trend, the key is to find capable and trustworthy suppliers. Good luck to you.

1. Alibaba www.alibaba.com

Ma Yun's Alibaba is naturally the first choice website for finding sources of goods. There are many kinds of products on Alibaba. You can search for the products you want to consign or wholesale on the homepage, or you can look for the products you want to consign in the "online entrepreneurship" category under the "entrepreneurship franchise" channel.

2.慧聪网www.hc360.com

Although Hc360 is not as famous as Alibaba, it is not without reason that it is called China's second largest industry website. Searching for "online store agency" or "network agency" in the search box on the homepage will yield thousands of online consignment supply information. If you want to wholesale, you can directly search for the product name.

3. Taobao www.taobao.com

Many store owners on Taobao sell things while also recruiting online store consignment agents. You can directly search for consignment on Taobao and choose high-credit store owners' consignment goods.

4. Paipai www.paipai.com

Paipai naturally also has many store owners recruiting consignment agents. You can directly search for the product + consignment, or directly search for "consignment" in the store name.

5. Baidu Tieba (Consignment Bar, Wholesale Bar): tieba.baidu.com

Baidu Tieba has very high popularity, and the popularity of the Consignment Bar and Wholesale Bar will naturally not be low. There are many suppliers in the Consignment Bar recruiting online store consignment agents. As for the authenticity, please identify it yourself. Friends who want to wholesale products can also find sources of goods in the Wholesale Bar.

6. Online Consignment Home www.53daixiao.com

This is a new generation of online consignment websites. It is currently the most professional online consignment and wholesale source exchange community in China. It is suitable for netizens who want to open an online store without capital to consign goods at zero cost online. You can find online consignment products here for consignment, and you can also exchange experience with other online store consignment agents here.

7. Wholesale Search (Jewelry Network) www.pifasou.com

Wholesale Search is one of the most influential websites in the jewelry industry. There are many suppliers providing jewelry consignment and wholesale. Friends who want to consign and wholesale jewelry should not miss it.

8. Alipay Community (Recommended Merchant Section) club.alipay.com

Many suppliers are recruiting consignment agents there. You can take a look.

9. I Love Bags www.5i88.net

This is a website that provides various bag retail, consignment, and wholesale. There are more than ten kinds of bags. Friends who want to consign bags can come here and take a look.

10. Maoce www.maoce.cn

Maoce's slogan is "Did you use the restroom today?" It provides consignment for dozens of products. Friends who want to consign can directly contact them to negotiate.
