Man dies in car crash on the day his son is born

by baimao55537682 on 2012-03-05 16:32:50

At around 9 o'clock in the evening on August 4, 2011, Li drove a tractor at an intersection of Xianyin North Road in Qixia District of Nanjing city and ran down an army officer. After the accident, Li fled. At about 5 p.m. on August 27, 2011, the injured army officer died in hospital after failing to be effectively rescued. The traffic accident determined that Li should bear the main responsibility for the accident. His behavior violated the criminal law. On December 2, 2011, the People's Court of Qixia District heard this case in court. The court sentenced the defendant Li for committing the crime of causing a traffic accident and sentenced him to three years and five months in prison. Within ten days after the judgment takes effect, the defendant Li shall compensate RMB 548,828.6 yuan. (The characters in the article are pseudonyms). Zhang Yi Wei Xiaoxin. The main difficulty in the case was that the family economy of the driver Li in this case was also not good. After divorce, he independently raised three minor children. The tractor he drove did not participate in any insurance. He clearly indicated that he could not compensate for civil losses. The aid lawyer contacted the defendant and his family many times to communicate with them, informing them that actively compensating and obtaining the victim's understanding would help with the sentencing of the defendant Li. After much work, Li realized his own mistakes, indicating that he would certainly compensate for the corresponding losses and prayed for the understanding of the victim's family. Xiao Wang is the only son in the family. His parents are old and weak, suffering from various diseases and losing their ability to work. When the mainstay fell, the whole family was on the verge of collapse. The family came to the Legal Aid Center of Qixia District to apply for legal aid because the family was very difficult. The aid center soon signed a legal aid agreement with them and assigned lawyers to provide them with legal aid. After accepting this case, the handling lawyer asked the assisted person in detail about the course of events and relevant situations. The aid lawyer decided to help the assisted person initiate a civil compensation lawsuit attached to the criminal case. The procedure of the criminal attachment can quickly compensate. The deceased Xiao Wang was a deputy battalion-level officer of a certain unit in Nanjing. On August 4th, after working overtime in the unit, he wanted to go home to visit his pregnant wife, so he asked for leave to go home. Unfortunately, a traffic accident occurred and he was injured. The hit-and-run driver Li caused Xiao Wang to pass away at 5 p.m. on August 27th. At 10:15 a.m. on the same day, his son was born into the world. The little baby didn't get to see his father in time and thus they were separated by life and death. His wife couldn't bear the shock and fainted multiple times in the hospital bed.
