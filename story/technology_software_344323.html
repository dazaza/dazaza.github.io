Samsung S8000C: Full Touchscreen "Jet" Work Speed to China

by ymaxi68k on 2010-04-12 22:09:02

Following the global launches in London, Dubai, and Singapore, Samsung's groundbreaking full-touchscreen phone, the "Jet" S8000C (forum), has arrived in China. The Samsung S8000C is known as "Jet" overseas and retains the same name in Chinese. As a strategic full-touchscreen smartphone launched globally by Samsung, the S8000C offers users swift and smooth touch experiences, stunning visual enjoyment, and powerful extended functions. Below are images of the Samsung S8000C.

Just like a jet rapidly crossing the sky, a new era of mobile touch control has been born!

**No Jet, No Speed**

The full-touchscreen era is an era of speed. The Samsung S8000C is equipped with an 800MHz high-speed processor, a configuration that not only sets a precedent in the industry but also lays a solid foundation for the smooth operation of various programs and a fluid touch experience akin to writing on clouds.

To ensure users have more freedom during touch applications, this time the Samsung S8000C features the newly upgraded TouchWiz 2.0 user interface and innovatively applies a 3D touch interface. Users need only press a single button to enter this mode. Displayed before the user is a true rotatable 3D cube, allowing them to freely rotate the cube to find and quickly access pre-set application shortcuts. This completely revolutionary interface design concept marks the beginning of the 3D full-touchscreen era.

The Samsung S8000C also has multi-tasking capabilities, enabling users to switch effortlessly between running applications. The new interface significantly improves web browsing speed and touch command response compared to previous versions.

Additionally, the S8000C features a unique one-finger zoom function, allowing single-handed control for enlarging or shrinking images and documents, making mobile internet surfing fast and convenient.

**No Jet, No Glamour**

The full-touchscreen era is a multimedia era. To provide users with perfect visual experiences, the Samsung S8000C is equipped with a 3.1-inch WVGA resolution, 16 million color AMOLED touch display, with a resolution as high as 480x800, which is four times clearer than ordinary WQVGA screens! Due to the use of innovative AMOLED material, the Samsung S8000C screen performs excellently in color reproduction, brightness, and contrast. A 180-degree viewing angle allows users to watch from any angle.

In terms of audio-visual performance, the Samsung S8000C delivers equally impressive results. It is equipped with a 5-megapixel digital camera and dual LED flash, supporting professional functions such as smile mode, panoramic shooting, and photo geotagging similar to digital cameras. The Samsung S8000C also supports D1 level video playback and D1 @30fps video recording. For Divx and Xvid format videos, they can be played upon download without conversion. In music, the Samsung S8000C uses excellent DNSe & SRS sound effect technology. All these high-performance features give users an incredibly dazzling sensory experience.

In terms of design, the S8000C impresses with its ultra-glamorous overall shape and meticulous detail handling. The entire body is ultra-thin and rounded, with a thickness of just 11.9mm, enhanced by ergonomic curves, making the S8000C look cool and feel great in hand. The shell of the S8000C is made of matte material, paired with a vibrant AMOLED screen, further enhancing its delicate and refined appearance. Red-black patterned textures subtly appear on the back of the body, elegant and noble, and do not easily leave fingerprints. Additionally, at the center below the body, there is a unique crystal 3D button, echoing the 3D touch interface inside and out, and conveniently opening the function menu with a single touch.

**No Jet, No Ability**

The full-touchscreen era is an all-in-one era. Besides daily communication functions, mobile phones have become indispensable tools for business work and entertainment. The Samsung S8000C also features Samsung's self-developed motion recognition engine "motion gate", allowing users to complete common operations such as accessing multimedia files and quick dialing through simple motions (shaking, double-tapping, etc.).

To enhance the practicality of the full-touchscreen, the Samsung S8000C also includes an "intelligent unlock" function, which allows users to set corresponding characters for phone unlocking and menu operations, facilitating personalized usage according to their own preferences. Moreover, the Samsung S8000C also installs an A-GPS application, providing users with real-time navigation services and convenient point-of-interest query functions, ensuring ease of use even in "driving" mode.

With high-speed processing capability, high-quality audio-visual presentation, and efficient comprehensive application functions, the Samsung S8000C not only brings users an "unparalleled touch" but also redefines full-touchscreen phones.
