"Sunny Weather in Kenting" Plot Introduction

Good friends who grew up together in Kenting, Hanwen and Liangliang, became infatuated with the online pure love writer "Rain Never Stops," and frequently interacted with her under the pseudonym "Sunny Weather" on her blog. Having never been in love, Hanwen even fell for "Rain Never Stops," fantasizing that "Rain Never Stops" was as pure and clear as the stories she wrote. In real life, "Rain Never Stops," whose real name is Xiaowei, is an emotionally intense girl whose love life has been repeatedly hurtful. After facing cruel treatment from her boyfriend, she abandoned her soon-to-be-published novel and everything in Taipei, quietly escaping to Kenting. Anan, the illustrator for "Rain Never Stops'" novels, due to unsuccessful collaborations with the publishing house and poor management of the studio he ran with his girlfriend, also took a bus to Kenting to collect debts.

By chance, Anan met Hanwen and Liangliang, and the three became good friends. The frequent interactions between Anan and Liangliang made Azuo, who secretly loved Liangliang, extremely jealous. After Hanwen met Anan, the two big boys who loved surfing and had feelings for the sea decided to open a surf shop together.

Xiaowei only mixed with the foreign friend who came to Kenting with her, and her distant attitude made Hanwen very frustrated. However, her coldness and sadness attracted Hanwen, making him start to question his feelings for "Rain Never Stops."

The foreign friend who came to Kenting with Xiaowei actually had a purpose. After being warmly guided by Liangliang, this friend wrote a paper exposing contradictions and disorder in the management of Kenting National Park, suggesting removing Kenting from the list of national parks. This behavior caused a stir and also inspired Liangliang to help the national park from a broader perspective. She proposed managing jet ski operators through BOT bidding, which caused strong opposition from local operators. In the fierce protests, in order to protect Liangliang, Azuo was severely injured in an attack.

Xiaowei returned to Taipei to resolve contract issues with the publishing house. Her ex-boyfriend, who was facing a major crisis in his career, wanted to use Xiaowei's fame to promote his latest project, so he approached Xiaowei under false pretenses.

Hanwen, separated from Xiaowei, wanted to help her solve her contract problems but felt like a fool because of his lack of legal knowledge, unable to assist Xiaowei...

After meeting Brother Chu, Anan gradually understood Brother Chu's deep feelings for Kenting and his grand vision for the tourism industry, hoping to cooperate with Brother Chu to start a business. A group of people joined Brother Chu's plan, just as their business was about to take off when Brother Chu suddenly disappeared. Where did Brother Chu go? How will they continue their dream for Kenting...
