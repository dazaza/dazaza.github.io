What is a newbie? It's someone who knows absolutely nothing about online stores. To be honest, what takes us just a few minutes to decorate a template now takes a newbie a whole day. This isn't an exaggeration - it's the reality of where we started versus where we are now. Because you know nothing, in this day and age, experience and skills are very important. There are software programs that can be easily downloaded from websites, yet some people sell them on Taobao for 20 or even 50 yuan each. Who buys them? Are they useful? Yes, they are used, but mostly by newbies. This isn't to look down on newbies; it's because you don't understand and end up wasting money. Can't you use that 50 yuan for something else? Even Bill Gates would pick up a penny he dropped. Money should not be wasted.

Currently, Taobao's anti-cheating system is very powerful. In 2011, Taobao will heavily crack down on stores that engage in credit brushing. There are even dedicated personnel monitoring groups that offer credit brushing services. Everyone knows that if you search for "credit brushing" on Baidu, many platforms offering such services appear. Does Taobao not know about these? They just haven't been affected enough yet. By the end of this year and into next year, Taobao will start paying more attention to this issue because it is starting to affect them. In short: What goes around comes around! When the time comes, they'll investigate you and take away all your profits. You shouldn't spend money on credit brushing either. The price to brush one credit is around 200 yuan now. Even if you brush a lot, you risk being investigated later, which not only wastes time and energy, but also delays opportunities. Time is the most precious resource! If you want to succeed on Taobao, you need to do it honestly. Otherwise, you will fail miserably. This is our conclusion, as we have experienced being penalized by Taobao for credit brushing. My other blog post explains how Taobao detects credit brushing. So, never do that!

For new store owners selling physical goods initially, you must possess the following points to do well:

1. Have a sourcing advantage, meaning you are a manufacturer or produce your own goods, or have good relationships for sourcing quality products at competitive prices. Without these, it's hard to compete with big sellers on Taobao, especially as a new seller.

2. Have sufficient capital. Don't try to sell physical goods without enough funds to even add consumer protection. Adding consumer protection is essential, otherwise no one will dare to buy from you, and your items won't show up in search results either, as top-ranked sellers usually have consumer protection.

3. Be available online all day. This isn't for those looking to make quick money. Physical goods require building credibility over a long period. You might not get any sales for two months, or if you're frequently offline, potential customers will quickly leave your shop.

4. Have promotional experience. Waiting for customers to come to your store doesn't work anymore. Promotion is key, followed by product quality, whether online or offline.

5. Know how to design your store, including templates, style, item descriptions, categories, etc. These are crucial. A store's success depends on three factors: design, price, and traffic.

As for sourcing, that comes later. Here, we're talking about store performance, not customer retention rates. Customer retention isn't solely dependent on product quality.

If you meet the above criteria, go ahead and start selling physical goods. I believe you will succeed because you have advantages others don't.

If you don't meet these criteria but still want to open an online store, consider the following:

Distribute virtual software. Why?

Because you lack the aforementioned advantages, so you need to take shortcuts. Just like someone who can afford a car and buys a Mercedes, while another person buys a Santana. Both are cars, but one generates profit (Santana) while the other offers enjoyment (Mercedes). People should enjoy life, but only if they have the advantage. You need to develop creativity within the same category.

Back to software, what does doing software bring to newbies without these advantages?

1. You gain the years of experience of your upstream supplier. This is something money can't buy, nor can you casually ask someone for it. Many software distributors have such methods, and some even more. Of course, this is a commitment and obligation after selling the software to downstream agents.

2. You can use the software's recharge function for mobile phone credits, games, point cards, game accounts, equipment, and many other virtual businesses. While earning store credibility, you can also make money.

3. Promotional methods. Many things aren't due to a lack of ability, but rather a lack of understanding. Once you know, you can too. Online stores are developing rapidly, and there's much potential for earning money. Mastering promotional methods, selling software isn't impossible. Consider how to use software to earn tens of thousands of yuan per month (I'll explain why software can generate such income below).

4. Learn store decoration methods, including sidebars, categories, templates, background music, images, PS image processing software, Taobao Assistant uploads, etc.

5. Not a large investment. Suitable for those wanting to invest in opening an online store and use it to make money.

6. No need to be online all day. Software can operate automatically and offline.

7. Quick capital turnover. After successful recharges, buyers typically confirm receipt immediately, unlike physical goods which take days.

8. Saves time from constantly listing and delisting physical goods and sending express deliveries, allowing full utilization of time for promotion.

The above is just part of it. I hope everyone will point out anything I've missed. The purpose of raising these points is to tell newbies that opening a store is simple, but succeeding requires methods. If you close your store less than a month after opening, I advise you not to waste your energy. Also, many friends ask me what kind of free physical goods are good for newbies to distribute. I think there are none. Without sources or advantages, even if you find physical goods to distribute, they won't give you an edge, and you won't be able to sell them. Considering distribution is something to think about when your store reaches 2-3 diamonds in credibility. Currently, all brands requiring free agent recruitment for online stores demand at least 2 or 3 diamonds, and explicitly reject credit brushing, even if you reach crown level, it won't work. For example, Taoshoe's sources are relatively reliable, all genuine products, large companies, but their requirements are high. Also, Mibag, Tebu official, Li Ning official, Hunan Yuni Fang skincare, and many other brand authorized suppliers require at least 2-3 diamonds and consumer protection with at least 8 hours of online time.

Actually, doing legitimate channel distribution without your own sources can be profitable, meeting the needs of store owners without advantages. As for newly opened stores without consumer protection, finding those physical goods sources won't last long and is a waste of time. Taobao's future development direction is network branding. Many large brands have already entered. Taobao plans to move the consumption of 1 billion Chinese people entirely online. It's clear what their moves mean. Therefore, Taobao is currently cracking down heavily on fake and imitation goods stores, closing stores, clearing out non-compliant ones, like Guangzhou VIPshop Information Technology Co., Ltd.

However, one thing Taobao always emphasizes is providing employment opportunities for young people. As Taobao becomes fully branded, entrepreneurs can join as brand agents. Initially, you need to lay the foundation for your store, otherwise, when the time comes, if your store lacks credibility or experience, you'll miss out on opportunities. What happens five years from now is determined by what you do now.

Finally, let's talk about why virtual software can earn over ten thousand yuan per month, just sharing experiences.

Software has two ways to make money. First is recharging. Doing recharging well can earn several thousand yuan per month. Some people specialize in this on Taobao Mall. Taobao Mall's annual fee is 6800 yuan, and the deposit is 10000 yuan. Clearly, this can be profitable. Many of our agents also specialize in recharging. There are methods involved, which are more popular among part-time sellers.

Second is selling software. Many full-time sellers choose this strategy. That's why so many agents answer questions on Baidu Zhidao. We did this in the beginning too. I remember bringing in nearly 18 customers from Baidu Zhidao in the first month of being an agent. At the time, it felt great. This is just one method of promotion. We have many, which we teach all our agents.

Selling software comes with commissions. For example, if you sell a set of software to me, you get a primary commission of 185 yuan. If I sell another set, I get 185 yuan, and you get a secondary commission of 55 yuan. This reflects teamwork in profitability. Like many of our agents who don't have time to help new customers with store decoration, we do it for them. Because you earn commissions from sales, and we benefit too, helping you is natural. China's rules must be like this, otherwise, no one would help for free. Everyone wants to make money, and asking them to tell you things is a very realistic issue.

Therefore, our software almost daily earns secondary commissions. One day, we earned secondary commissions from about 10 agents, totaling 550 yuan. This thanks our agents, and of course, they made money too.

Following this commission model, your income will definitely exceed ten thousand yuan per month. What you need to do is serve your agents well, increase sales promotion courses to guide your subordinates on how to promote effectively. This is very important. Chen Anzhi once said, "When you want to make money, you can't. (This sounds contradictory, but he added) When you become the best or second-best in this industry, money will keep coming to you!" Here, it means consolidating your experience, knowledge, and abilities.

Selling goods online, you may know the Matthew Effect, which means the better-selling ones sell even better, and the worse-selling ones sell even worse. This concept was proposed by a foreign scholar and applies both online and in real-life sales. For example, between two restaurants, one is packed while the other is empty. Customers always choose the crowded one. Since we don't have this effect initially, we need to create it, and there are methods to do so.
