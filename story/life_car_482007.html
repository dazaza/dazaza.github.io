Car Beauty Knowledge: Car Maintenance Solutions

Car beauty knowledge and car maintenance are essential, just like humans need care, cars require even more attention. To achieve both the beautification and maintenance of a car, one must understand relevant maintenance knowledge as well as the frequently seen car beauty products and car maintenance products on the market today.

Understanding these allows for comparison and the ability to discern between good and bad, genuine or fake products. Some car accessories may seem very cheap, such as car wax, which costs around 5 yuan per box. Just like when we mention snow cream, everyone knows that twenty years ago young girls used it, costing 2 yuan per bag, but now people use 200-yuan bottles instead. These seemingly cheap products do not contribute to the maintenance or beautification of the car; in fact, they can be harmful to the vehicle's body. Below, we will address several common issues encountered by car owners during car maintenance or washing.

A. What to do if ice forms while washing the car?

Answer: Use warm water to wash your car as freezing weather often leads to instant icing. The biggest taboo when washing your car is allowing water droplets to freeze on the vehicle, which can directly result in paint layer cracking. Therefore, it’s best to use warm water instead of cold water when washing your car. If possible, wash indoors. After the vehicle enters the workstation, let it sit for 5 to 10 minutes until the engine cools down before washing. Sudden temperature changes are detrimental to the paint surface.

B. What is the difference between polymers and silicones?

Answer: Car waxes contain different types of polymers. Polymers usually enhance the durability of the product and/or seal the car paint, protecting it from environmental pollution and corrosion. Silicon components are used to enhance the brightness of the paint.

C. How to wash your car during snowy winter days?

Answer: If it snows continuously for several days, Mr. Li keeps postponing cleaning his car. By the time he finally wipes it off, he finds that the car paint has lost its former luster. Wiping the car should be timely. Even if the weather forecast predicts snowfall in the next few days, don't delay wiping your car. As soon as the car is covered with snow, immediately rinse it off with clean water. Snow contains corrosive components, and whether it's the paint surface, undercarriage, tires, or rims, long-term coverage by snow will corrode the car paint, which is why the car loses its shine.

D. Are the dashboard and doors made of real leather?

Answer: No (very expensive cars might have real leather). They are leather goods, so they only need surface polishing and do not require penetrating nourishing liquids.

E. What is the difference between leather and synthetic leather?

Answer: Synthetic leather is artificial and lacks pores, only having a shiny surface. Real leather is similar to our skin; its internal structure is very much like a dense bundle of silk threads, connected by protein chains. Once these protein chains shrink and dry out, the real leather begins to crack.
