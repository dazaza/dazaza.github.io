Helping infants and toddlers improve indigestion:

Carrot Water (for formula milk or mixed with rice porridge)

Ingredients: 1 carrot or an appropriate amount, 2 jujubes.

Instructions:

1. Peel the carrot, wash and slice it; wash the jujubes.

2. Boil an appropriate amount of water, add the jujubes and carrots. After boiling, simmer for another hour until the flavor is extracted, then strain out the residue.

3. Pour the carrot water into a thermos to keep warm, use it to prepare formula milk or rice porridge. Carrots already have a slight sweetness, so adding sugar is optional.

1. Carrots are rich in beta-carotene, which helps widen the middle energizer, regulate qi, strengthen the stomach, aid digestion, and prevent diseases caused by vitamin A deficiency.

2. Infants or toddlers with indigestion or heatiness (internal heat) can be fed milk or rice porridge made with carrot water, which has certain therapeutic effects.
