One person walks, one person sleeps, one person thinks, one person becomes intoxicated.

by gege on 2011-12-23 10:53:03

Walking alone, sleeping alone, thinking alone, indulging alone.

Being busy alone, being tired alone, being annoyed alone, and experiencing alone.

From hope to despair, from anticipation to disappointment. From dreams to wild imaginations, from wishful thinking to the decision of not thinking anymore.

I miss you so much, I miss the you from the past,

The you with a flawless smile,

The you who was willing to give everything for me,

The you who gazed at me with loving eyes.

Perhaps, love is just a kind of fantasy we create in our minds.

Perhaps, love is just something we cling to when we're too lonely.

Perhaps, love is just a dependency that we don't know how to interpret.

Perhaps, love is just wanting to give oneself fully.

Perhaps, love is just telling ourselves that we have loved.

Perhaps, love is just about trying to become the person we imagine ourselves to be.

In the back-and-forth testing of glances between people, in the torment of waiting and giving up on love or lack thereof.

After hearts collide and start protecting themselves, in the days of letting go or holding on, and dealing with loneliness,

It turns out that unhappiness can't be faked, it turns out that longing can't be hidden, it turns out that sorrow must be borne alone.

It turns out that the past can only be forgiven, silence doesn't equal forgetting, and disappearing doesn't mean one has truly left.

All I really need isn't much.

A word of comfort when sad, a word of praise when proud,

A shoulder to lean on when tired, a warm embrace when heartbroken.

Missing is a recurring drama in human life,

We always experience missed opportunities again and again.

The right person but the wrong time.

The right time but the wrong place.

The right place, but the person beside us isn't the one we want.

And then we blame endlessly.

If only it had happened a little earlier,

If only we had walked one more round,

If only we had stayed a little longer.

A terrifying cycle,

Yet an inescapable fate.

If silence is a form of harm, please choose to leave.

Now, I choose to leave.

Because after enduring deep pain,

I must grow.

Don't waste time anymore,

In the end, no one will truly change.

Remember, forget.

Care, or let go.

Persistence may just be an inability to let go of memories.

No one can truly keep another by their side forever.

Perhaps in the end,

We can only say —— in a certain year, a certain day, a certain place

Because love was too profound,

So I chose to let you go

I begin preparing to face everything alone.

So what?

In fact, I've been alone all along.

Moments I can never touch again.

I decide to close the space in my heart that I've kept for you.

I decide to stop delaying both you and myself.

I decide to stop loving you.

The worn ring that's always been on my ring finger will disappear from my world along with you; I don't want either of you anymore.

Because I'm starting to love myself.

Without question,

Time is the deadliest poison.

It creates everything,

And destroys everything.

So, let me forget, bit by bit.
