Shanghai's First Mayor's Quality Award Sets an Excellent Benchmark

Han Zheng Attended the Award Ceremony and Delivered a Speech

Report by our staff (reported by Huo Yifu) - On October 30th, at the award ceremony of the first Shanghai Mayor's Quality Award, Shanghai Mayor Han Zheng said that the pursuit of excellence in quality awareness is an important part of Shanghai's city spirit. Quality is the lifeblood of Shanghai, it relates to the immediate interests of the people, the integrity of the city, and quality is the competitiveness of this city.

Han Zheng hopes that the Shanghai Mayor's Quality Award will further awaken the quality consciousness of the whole society, embodying the concept of quality, quality awareness, and quality management methods in all aspects and links of Shanghai's economic and social development. He hopes that more organizations and individuals will take the awardee units and individuals as examples, vigorously promote technological innovation, strive to improve management quality, make new contributions to Shanghai's accelerated advancement of four pioneers, expedite the construction of four centers, and spare no effort to do a good job in all preparatory work for the World Expo.

The organization that won the first Mayor's Quality Award is Baoshan Iron & Steel Co., Ltd. Baogang Branch. The company actively practices the "Criteria for Excellence Performance Evaluation", adheres to the strategy of winning by quality, standard leadership, and high-quality products, with the goal of building the most competitive steel enterprise. The individual winner is Fan Bingxun, chairman of Shanghai Mitsubishi Elevator Co., Ltd. In order to change the backward situation of China's elevator industry, he has cultivated a continuous pursuit of excellence in quality awareness among all employees, and has been courageous in innovation for 20 years.

When talking about the significance of the Mayor's Quality Award, Huang Xiaolu, director of the Shanghai Quality Supervision Bureau, said that according to advanced, scientific, systematic evaluation standards and the scientific, fair, impartial, and open evaluation principles, the introduction of the Shanghai Mayor's Quality Award will help form a multi-level atmosphere of quality management exploration, enhance the quality awareness of the whole society, and together with the Shanghai Quality Gold Award, build a step-by-step quality promotion system, playing a guiding role in quality work at both upper and lower levels, and improving management levels.
