Spring breeze and timely rain: Funing power supply service promotes local economic development

At 13:54:31 on November 2, at the eighth plenary session of the twelfth session of the Funing County Committee in Jiangsu Province, Wang Jingsheng, the party secretary of Jiangsu County, specially gave high praise again to the full efforts of the county's power supply company in serving the local economy. For the party secretary in office to publicly praise the same unit at two important meetings at the beginning and end of the year in the same year is unprecedented in the history of the county. It can be said that the Funing County Power Supply Company is like the spring breeze and timely rain, sincerely serving the people and society, and writing a new chapter of electric power.

Adhering to role reversal, detailed services show sincerity

As an assistant to the government in implementing economic development strategies, how can power supply enterprises effectively fulfill their commitment to serve the local economic development and serve a large number of electricity customers? The employees of this company have the same answer: "role reversal". They consider issues from the customer's perspective, think what the customers think, and continuously extend various distinctive services while providing routine services.

Role reversal is reflected in their efforts to create a "pro-business, beneficial to business" customer service platform. In 2006, they changed the situation where the window department sang a "solo" to the entire company singing together. The VIP electricity manager responsibility system they launched allows merchants to enjoy designated special customer managers, personalized value-added services, and free door-to-door tracking services, ensuring "one visit, one person, and everything done at once." In 2007, they prioritized serving the construction of industrial development zones, clearly holding communication exchanges with enterprises in the economic development zone on the 20th of each month, exchanging gains and losses and needs between both parties, promoting cooperative development between enterprises and users, and truly delivering "quality service, detailed service, value-added service" to the doorstep of entrepreneurs. In 2008, as the scale of the economic development zone gradually expanded, to facilitate the handling of electricity business for enterprises, they specifically added major customer representatives in the park, responsible for transmitting and expediting the electricity business process for customers, reducing business processing steps and time. Since then, enterprises in the Funing Economic Development Zone can complete all electricity procedures without leaving their doors, fully enjoying personalized, differentiated, and ultra-value services. Now, they are also promoting and applying this service model to the chemical park.

Role reversal has brought real benefits to investors in the area and earned them widespread praise. During a home visit service to the Taiwanese-funded Quanqi Feng Garment Company, the company's marketing personnel learned that the enterprise had installed a 500 kVA transformer. When they found that the actual electricity consumption was lower than the rated capacity of the transformer installed by the enterprise, standing in the customer's shoes, they actively helped them change the relevant billing method, saving the enterprise nearly 58,800 yuan in electricity costs in one year. Affected by the global financial and economic environment, production enterprises have experienced unprecedented severe challenges. This company took the initiative to help enterprises overcome difficulties and continue production by saving energy, reducing consumption, and lowering costs. The Xingle Copper Factory located in the private science and technology park of Yilin Town in the county, due to the impact of price reductions in copper materials domestically and internationally, was operating at a loss and struggling to continue. After multiple on-site inspections by Funing County Power Supply Company, based on the actual production status of the enterprise, they suggested stopping the operation of one of the two transformers, saving the enterprise 14,000 yuan in basic electricity fees per month. Considering the operating cost of the enterprise, they suggested using the nighttime electricity price for high-load production processes, which could save the enterprise 100,000 yuan per month.

In the process of serving the key investment promotion project in Yancheng City, "Aoyang Chemical Fiber", facing 90% rainy weather, in order to catch up with the schedule, all construction personnel braved heavy rain, wading through knee-deep mud, pushing and carrying construction materials to the site, setting poles, stringing wires, and installing transformers in the rain, thus ensuring the timely implementation of the power supply plan. When seeing the report titled "Full of Aoyang," then Party Secretary Qi Biao personally wrote in his comments, "Learn from the power supply people's support for major projects!" Current Party Secretary Wang Jingsheng specifically praised at the fifth plenary session of the twelfth session of the county committee in early 2008: "The power supply company has made positive contributions in serving the local economic development and social construction."

Using the barrel theory flexibly, improving service and management

The "barrel theory" states that the amount of water a barrel can hold does not depend on the longest plank in the barrel wall but precisely depends on the shortest plank in the barrel wall. Comprehensive service for the development of the local economy and achieving overall improvement in work levels is the winning strategy of the power supply company, earning widespread praise.

Their "power supply meticulous service" focuses on the big picture while starting small, establishing the service concept that "one answer represents the company's image, and one moment reflects the company's appearance," starting from every small detail. Carefully refining every service process and every service link becomes a distinctive feature of the enterprise's development. Multiple media outlets, including the State Grid News and China Electric News, have made special reports on this. Due to the comprehensive promotion of "power supply meticulous service," many "firsts" in service measures have emerged in Funing Power Supply across the city: the first to send electricity bills to customers, the first "community power supply service demonstration point," the first standardized construction promotion meeting in residential areas, etc., allowing all the people of the county to fully enjoy high-quality and efficient power supply services. Wei Xiuzhen, the first elderly resident of Jianyin Residential Area to receive her electricity bill, still remarks with emotion: "We common people can use electricity clearly and transparently. The power supply company is wholeheartedly for the people and enterprises, truly being our close friends!"

With the full-scale launch of rural power grid transformation projects this year, General Manager Cheng Zhiming of the company, analyzing the situation, proposed the establishment of "on-site technical navigation" work requirements. That is, in response to the overall lack of construction capabilities among rural power employees, line maintenance personnel with relatively higher skills in this aspect participate in guiding technically demanding pole erection/removal and conductor installation/removal work. This strengthens the contact and communication between them and the power supply stations in the line regions and enhances the enthusiasm of the power supply stations to provide related assistance in future line maintenance work. This cross-departmental collaboration undoubtedly greatly accelerates the entire construction process.

Without rules, there can be no standardization. The company has always been committed to promoting the institutionalization, standardization, and normalization of power supply services. In 2006, the company established the first dedicated quality service inspection team in the city, forming a "three-level guarantee network" together with departments and teams, thereby realizing the thorough implementation and closed-loop management of various rules and regulations. In the management of key service projects, members of the leadership team go deep into the field, teams, and staff to work and monitor together, widely soliciting opinions and suggestions from customers, thus promoting the transformation of cadres' and employees' service styles. At the same time, they also clearly define quantitative evaluations for responsible persons at all levels. Once external service errors are discovered, they are strictly evaluated according to relevant standards, without going through the motions or showing favoritism to any rule-breaking behavior or individuals.

In addition, on the basis of hiring conduct supervisors, they invite all sectors of society to regularly hold conduct work seminars. Through interactive activities between power suppliers and consumers, they evaluate conduct, investigate violations, correct shortcomings, and create a strong atmosphere of internal motivation and external cooperation in building a good conduct culture. At the conduct supervisor seminar in March this year, the supervisors unanimously gave such an evaluation: the power supply company takes the initiative to shoulder social responsibilities, strengthens conduct construction with a rigorous work style, and plays a good exemplary leading role in serving the local socio-economic development.

A hundred boats vie for the lead; those at the forefront strive ahead. Looking to the future, the people of Funing Power Supply will take on more glorious responsibilities with even more remarkable achievements, march towards greater brilliance with even more resounding strides, and write even more splendid chapters with even more passionate fervor.
