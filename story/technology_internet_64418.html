Everyone is writing their own blog.

by free98 on 2008-04-21 16:31:11

Everyone is writing their own Blog. After finishing the Blog, if many people come to browse it, that would be a rather happy thing. So, we need to study the optimization and promotion of Blogs. The following are my personal opinions for everyone's reference:

1) High-quality log content is the key: The content of the Blog should be interesting, each article should focus on a theme, and distribute certain keywords in the article. Frequent updates are necessary. Search engine spiders will often come to fetch new content and display the update date in the search results.

2) Optimize the Blog structure: It's best to set the content of the articles on the left and the column navigation on the right. In terms of code representation, the former appears before the latter. This will help search engines search for textual content on the web page.

3) Mutual linking between articles: When mentioning related content written in the past, you can naturally link to other articles.

4) Use of theme-related article plugins: This allows users to get more information about related articles. Secondly, it can enhance the search engine's indexing of the webpage and strengthen the association between articles.

5) Exporting external links: Log in to search engines and RSS news aggregators, add logs to major bookmarking sites, increase friendly links, etc.
