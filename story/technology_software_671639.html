Dr. Zhang Zhonglan Biography: More Doctors

Zhang Zhonglan, female, Deputy Chief Physician, Associate Professor, graduated from Third Military Medical University.

Studied at the Seventh Military Medical University in Shanghai (now Third Military Medical University) from 1971-1974, studied at the Advanced Studies Institute of 301 Hospital from September 1981 to December 1982, and attended the Senior Doctor Training Program at 301 Hospital from March 1990 to December 1992. Currently serves as the head of the Haidian District Family Planning Expert Group. Member of the Beijing Branch of the Family Planning Society of the Chinese Medical Association. In 1992, she specialized in gynecological endocrinology and family planning, undertook sub-topics of the national "Ninth Five-Year" and "Tenth Five-Year" old-age disease osteoporosis research projects, and served as a member of the Chinese Osteoporosis Society. She has published more than ten related papers, including "The Effect of Calcium Supplements on Postmenopausal Bone Loss", "Study on Continuous Combined Treatment with Estradiol Skin Patch and Progesterone for Postmenopausal Symptoms and Prevention of Lumbar Vertebrae Bone Loss", "Study on Etidronate Disodium for Preventing Postmenopausal Bone Loss", "Effect of Raloxifene Hydrochloride on Bone Density, Bone Metabolism Biochemical Indicators, and Lipids in Postmenopausal Osteoporotic Women". Since 1992, she has specialized in osteoporosis, menstrual disorders, menopause, and infertility.
