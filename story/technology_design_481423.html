by ttykbss9 on 2011-08-25 12:19:42

Xinhua News Agency, Yongzhou, Hunan, June 1 - According to reports from Xinhua News Agency journalists covering in Yongzhou, and confirmed by the Higher People's Court of Hunan Province and the Publicity Department of Yongzhou Municipal Committee, a shooting incident occurred during the trial process at Lingling District Court of Yongzhou City, Hunan Province on the morning of June 1st, resulting in four deaths, including one person who committed suicide on the spot.

According to another report from China News Service, according to information provided by local informed sources, a serious shooting incident occurred during the trial process at Lingling District Court of Yongzhou City, Hunan Province on the morning of June 1st, when one party opened fire with a gun, leading to the immediate death of three judges and injuring three others. The suspect committed suicide on the spot. Further details are currently under further investigation.

Justice Online reported that relevant leaders of Yongzhou City Procuratorate, Hunan Province have rushed to the scene.

Facts and reasons:

I am the plaintiff Zhao Yunhai, and the defendants are Dao County People's Government and Dao County Land and Resources Bureau.

In July 2001, the plaintiff signed a land use right transfer agreement with Dao County Fugang Fish Farm, agreeing that Dao County Fugang Fish Farm would transfer its ownership of 250 square meters of land for the plaintiff's use. Fugang Fish Farm filled in the plaintiff's name on the planning map drawn by the Planning Bureau of Dao County and stamped it with the official seal, submitting it to the Planning Bureau. On January 12, 2007, the Dao County Planning and Construction Bureau legally issued (0701120018) No. construction land planning permit to the plaintiff. The plaintiff submitted relevant procedures to the Dao County Land and Resources Bureau to apply for a land use certificate, but the Dao County Land and Resources Bureau neither informed the reasons nor fulfilled the relevant obligations according to law. In November 2010, the plaintiff learned that the Dao County Land and Resources Bureau had confirmed the land enjoyed by the plaintiff for use by Xu Shanlin with the Dao Ji Jian (2001) No. 0814164 collective land construction land use certificate. The plaintiff believes that the act of issuing the above-mentioned land use certificate by the Dao County Land and Resources Bureau violated legal procedures, was factually incorrect, and lacked sufficient evidence, violating the law and causing damage to the plaintiff's legitimate rights and interests, and should be revoked according to law.

The first-instance and second-instance courts held that according to Article 63 of the "Land Management Law of the People's Republic of China": The right to use farmers' collectively-owned land shall not be transferred, transferred or leased for non-agricultural construction. Fugang Fish Farm is an agricultural collective economic organization, subordinate to the Fugang Township People's Government of Dao County, and the nature of its land is collectively owned. The agreement signed between Dao County Fugang Fish Farm and the plaintiff without authorization is invalid, therefore not protected by law. The issuance of certificates by the defendant has no legal interest relationship with the plaintiff, thus dismissing the lawsuit.
