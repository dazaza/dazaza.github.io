Cloud Talk: The Relationship between Entrepreneurship and Reading

"Many times, entrepreneurs don't start a business because they can't figure things out, but in reality, after you do figure it out, you will be even less likely to start a business. It doesn't matter if you haven't read many books, what's scary is not reading from the book of society." "Scholarly entrepreneurs often face the same problem, they always move from macro to micro perspectives, according to the economic trends of this country or even international economics, some entrepreneurial directions can certainly be predicted, these things are particularly academic, often I get very excited when I hear them but I don't know how to act on them. In fact, sometimes a good trend doesn't necessarily mean good for you, and a bad trend doesn't necessarily mean bad for you."

Jack Ma: Service is the Most Expensive Product in the World

All start-up companies face the issue of survival before development. Some companies hope to quickly become large and strong at the beginning, but for a company to survive, its first thought should be to do well, not to become big. We are an e-commerce company, a service-oriented industry, and service is the most expensive product in the world. The best service is no service, and the best service is one that doesn't require any service. Establishing a good system is the most important thing. From China's perspective, service is the most expensive product, and service is also a future trend. However, the ultimate goal of a service-oriented enterprise must be to make your customers not need service, only then can the company succeed. Integrity is not a kind of sales, nor is it a profound and hollow concept, it is the solid action of keeping promises and paying attention to every detail. Integrity cannot be used for sales, and it cannot be used as a concept.

Jack Ma: Find the Most Suitable People for Entrepreneurship

During the start-up period, do not look for star teams, do not gather successful people together, especially those who are already rich and successful at the age of 35 or 40. It is difficult for those who have already succeeded to start another venture together. During the early stages of entrepreneurship, search for those who have not yet succeeded, those who yearn for success, and cohesive teams. When the business reaches a certain level, then invite some talents. For entrepreneurship, find the most suitable people, not necessarily the most successful ones. Female entrepreneurship is a challenge, the biggest challenge and breakthrough for female entrepreneurship lies in employing people, and the greatest breakthrough in employing people lies in trusting them.

Jack Ma: The Most Unreliable Thing in the World is Relationships

"I have no connections, no money, I started from scratch. I believe that relationships are especially unreliable, doing business cannot rely on relationships, doing business cannot rely on small smartness, doing business is most importantly understanding what customers need, creating value substantially, and persisting. The most unreliable thing in the world is relationships."

Entrepreneurs must first have a dream.

Jack Ma said: I think entrepreneurs must first have a dream, this is very important, if you don't have a dream, you just do for the sake of doing, and you let others tell you what to do, you won't do well. You must be strong, secondly, you must have perseverance, without perseverance, you won't do well. From my own experience, every time I start a business, there is a beautiful conception process, but often when you get there, it may not be beautiful.

Jack Ma said: So you have to tell yourself, every day on your journey, you encounter a lot of things. In 1995, I founded the Yellow Pages, and then started Alibaba. I felt that I had already been unlucky, this didn't work, that didn't work, anyway, doing something unlucky for ten years doesn't matter, perseverance is very important.
