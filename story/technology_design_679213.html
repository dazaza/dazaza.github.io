Bulgari Spring/Summer 2011 New Men's Collection

by baiyasin2s on 2012-02-27 20:37:32

Accessories are primarily characterized by their practicality, unique style, and exceptional quality. Carefully selected fashion accessories create the image of a modern urban man who dresses meticulously and is impeccable in any situation.

The leather accessories collection naturally exudes his understated elegance, whether he's strolling through city streets, working busily in the office, hosting business dinners, or taking a relaxing vacation away from the hustle and bustle. He can find the perfect match for every occasion.

The Urban series captures the dynamism and speed of modern urban life and incorporates it into functional and precise designs. This series offers everything from elegant briefcases to spacious shopping bags, casual crossbody bags, and stylish travel bags. All styles are made from soft black cowhide leather and are adorned with metal pieces featuring the etched BVLGARI logo, showcasing unparalleled excellence and fine Italian craftsmanship.

The Week-end series uses black coated canvas with black cowhide leather trim. The various pull-along suitcases and travel bags have a calm and restrained style, interpreting travel as a life experience in a modern way, while also providing an opportunity to express individuality and charm through distinctive fashion accessories.

Bulgari men’s accessories for Spring/Summer 2011 also include a variety of silk products, adding an indispensable sensual touch for gentlemen who personally manage their accessories.

The BVLGARI BVLGARI series of men's silk scarves are made from noble silk jacquard fabric in light blue and purple, with a special process that gives the yarns an iridescent effect, making the repeated logo patterns appear with a fascinating translucent quality.

The Mediolanum series of ties perfectly embodies exquisite handcraftsmanship. This series is crafted using classic seven-fold techniques and 100% jacquard silk material, showcasing Bulgari's pursuit of excellence. These beautifully crafted ties come in various color combinations such as blue with light gray stripes, cream base with blue stripes, and more.

A deep blue "Naturalia" patterned saglione silk tie

Rich colors are the unique elements of the Naturalia series. The distinctive fish-shaped tie bar comes from Bulgari's eponymous jewelry collection, forming a striking color contrast with the light purple, blue, or other colored backgrounds of the refined seven-fold twill ties.

A light violet "Trapani" patterned fine jacquard silk tie

The Trapani series' seven-fold jacquard silk ties pay tribute to the ancient and eternal art of top-level tailoring, recreating the unique gauze openwork weaving technique of 19th-century looms. This technique involves two warp threads: one remains constant while the other spirals left and right each time it passes over the weft, creating a distinctive honeycomb effect. The final fabric produced is exquisite and durable, fully displayed in the Trapani series of ties, available in light blue, light purple, and other colors.

This season's men's belt series interprets a relaxed youthful style. The belts are woven from elastic rubber yarns, with bright metal buckles featuring the double-ring BVLGARI BVLGARI logo etched on them.

These luxurious belts are carefully woven by master tanners at Bulgari's studio in Florence, ensuring lasting quality. The oval buckle style is available in blue and beige, while the round buckle style is paired with black and gray.

This season's new arrivals also include an elegant and charming optical frame and sunglasses series, whose design blends nostalgic retro flair with modern appeal, reminiscent of 70's aviator glasses.

The Tubogas series eyewear features the classic elastic metal ring shape originally created by Bulgari, used as a unique decorative detail on acetate frames. Colors include classic black and the new horn gray. The extremely lightweight material and the laser-etched BVLGARI logo on the metal parts create an innovative and modern image.

The Parentesi series optical frames use the Parentesi classic pattern as an elegant decorative element embedded in red-black acetate frames. The Parentesi pattern is also integrated into the double-hinge design of luxury metal sunglasses, paired with adjustable leather nose pads, adding a bold personality to these sunglasses. Colors include bronze shiny gray and others.

The new men's accessories for Spring/Summer 2011 will be available starting from autumn 2010 at Bulgari boutiques and authorized dealers.
