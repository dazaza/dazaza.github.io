Everyone who comes here wants to learn about online promotion. So how do we conduct online promotion? The common methods of network promotion that many people may not know, here I will list eight commonly used network promotion methods for your reference.

1. Baidu Promotion: Why do I put Baidu in the first place? In today's network era, search engines are already well-known network media, and they are an indispensable tool for customers to accurately find relevant information. Its charm lies in how to let others see our promotion and click to communicate with us in time. The text here needs careful planning. What kind of language can make patients accept and become curious, and communicate with them?

2. Google Promotion: Most people think that it has no big effect, everyone in China uses Baidu, who still uses Google? Whether or not to use it is one thing, how to use it well is another. Google's search does not adopt Baidu's bidding ranking system, but adopts side column bidding promotion. From the perspective of online marketing, this form of promotion has little effect, only patients with real intention will click and consult.

3. News Marketing and Broad Marketing: News marketing, as the name suggests, refers to making a guidance on a well-known news event. For example, a certain star got sick, leading to what consequences, through this event to let everyone understand where? This will not make netizens hate, and will bring considerable traffic. This promotion method can consider forum marketing and Baidu Zhidao, Yahoo Knowledge Hall, etc. Use a well-known news event to reach the promotion strategy. It is better not to appear the name of the hospital when using this type of promotion, after all, forums and other places where information is released are the places with the most public opinion, and it is easy to be attacked by peers or netizens. Therefore, this promotion method should be determined according to the hospital. Then there is broad marketing, which is a word I came up with myself. Broad means wide coverage, how to make it cover a large area? Take e-commerce for example, what is e-commerce? Simply put, it's selling things online, B2B, B2C, etc., these are good promotion platforms. They sell products, we provide services, why can't we use these resources to do SEO and external links for the website? Isn't this a good opportunity to advertise yourself? There are also places like 114 Yellow Pages, etc., where we can register medical websites to promote the hospital's image and introduction. No charge, and search engines quickly index these things, as long as our introduction is not too obvious, most platforms can pass. Such promotion does not cost money, does not occupy manpower, and does not waste time, it is a very good online marketing strategy.

4. Local Online Marketing: In the vast network world, we are all insignificant, but in the community of a city, we can build our own reputation. Local online media is a marketing method that can be utilized.

5. Student Marketing: This method can be referenced. Of course, I am not talking about market marketing, but online marketing. For this network, it is naturally the place students like to go, internet cafes! Everyone has experienced school days, especially in the era of popular network. When in college, students like to call friends to go to the internet cafe to play games, watch movies, check data, etc. For those who are pregnant early or have embarrassing secrets, the network is undoubtedly a place for them to find their answers. Why don't we seize this market and help them find the answers they want? A disease consultation window can be set on the desktop. But attention should be paid to the degree, if it's too much, students will feel nauseous. Of course, this also depends on communication with the internet cafe owner. I believe all bosses like money. Giving them some promotion fees every month, they will thank you. More exchanges and cooperation, going to the internet cafe to distribute magazines and cards, etc., these ideas may be old-fashioned, but I have been marketed once, and I also went to the hospital with the card to see a doctor, so this part of the internet cafe marketing needs to penetrate. College students see more, even if they joke around, they will say a sentence or two of your hospital's advertising slogan, and this effect is achieved. In the current situation where university campuses are clustered, this is a great platform. In today's open society, the boundaries between boys and girls have greatly relaxed, which undoubtedly brings good business to internet cafes and hotels near schools. These places can also be used to place advertisements. For example, if a girl gets pregnant and is embarrassed to tell her family, she can only go to the internet cafe to seek help. She opens the IE homepage to search, and immediately sees your hospital's website. At this point, the patient comes. She simply looks at it and clicks into the online doctor chat. Internet cafe marketing is often such a simple matter. The key is to do it sincerely and consider it from the right perspective.

6. Network SEO: SEO refers to website optimization and promotion optimization. How can we make our website bring more traffic? How can we make our website more practical and competitive? The concept of keywords appears. From the perspective of website inclusion, a good copy is still very useful. A good original article not only brings pleasant words, but also a better way for patients to find us on the Internet. Do more soft articles with keywords. As for Baidu, it likes to include summary-type articles more. Website content is king, more content, search engine spiders will visit your site frequently. How to attract it to crawl frequently? Simply put, look at it from five aspects.

1. The title of the website is the most important. A good title is the positioning of a website, and a good factor to seize the ranking position in search engines. Whoever can stand firm in natural search, the title is very important.

2. Keywords of the website, this is an indispensable thing. A good keyword can increase the weight of your website. The higher your PR value, the more search engines like to come here. It thinks the things here are useful, not garbage or copies. Of course, setting keywords still needs a certain limit, not too many, about 5 is fine.

3. Description of the website, this thing appears in the description part of the search engine, this block is a very important thing. A good description, an attractive description, will make netizens click in. This description should pay more attention to keyword optimization. How to optimize it? Pay attention to the coordination of keywords and words.

4. The overall structure of the website, which program is used to complete the website, whether it is more conducive to the opening speed and framework of the website. Personally, I think DIV+CSS is a very practical program from the optimization point of view. Whether it's opening speed or search optimization, try to avoid calling JS, which will affect the overall optimization of the website. Try to use simple HTML static code to achieve it. After all, search engines frequently crawl static websites. A pretty website may be liked by many, but it is not the best optimization effect. Consider it from multiple perspectives.

Pay attention to every detail, avoid invalid links. Increase friendly links and exchange friendly links with hospitals in other places, the premise is that its PR value must be above 4, which will be more effective than exchanging links with some junk websites. More external links of the website are a favorable way to bring traffic to the website. There is a lot of knowledge about SEO, actually doing network work often learning, researching, and practicing, you will soon master these skills, as long as you do it sincerely. Traffic will come to us soon!
