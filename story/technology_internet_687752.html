Disfigured girl Zhou Yan to be hospitalized for rehabilitation treatment, already suffering from severe depression

by erng6q4b on 2012-03-02 17:56:17

More than 3.5 million netizens focus on Weibo donation, CEO of a Beijing-based website donates 100,000 yuan in cash and kind to Hefei

The recent disfigurement case of a young girl in Hefei has drawn great attention from the public. Yesterday morning, a company based in Beijing contacted our newspaper, rushed to Zhou Yan's home and donated 100,000 yuan in cash and kind on the spot, and brought letters written by netizens for Zhou Yan. In addition, considering Zhou Yan's condition, the Zhou family decided to send her to the hospital for rehabilitation treatment soon.

A Beijing-based enterprise donates more than 100,000 yuan in cash and kind to Hefei

Yesterday morning at around 10 o'clock, a group of five people from a certain website in Beijing specially rushed to Hefei. Under the leadership of the reporter, they went to the Zhou family and delivered donations and medicines to Zhou Yan, with a total value of more than 100,000 yuan.

As soon as they entered the door, Zhou Yan's mother came up to welcome them, hugging to express her gratitude, "You have come a long way to see Zhou Yan, I am really grateful, I don't know how to repay you good-hearted people."

"We are very concerned about Zhou Yan's unfortunate encounter. The other day, after we saw the media launch a fundraising campaign, we wanted to come to Hefei and give Zhou Yan some practical help," the website CEO Zhu Yali told the reporter. Their website also specially opened a special topic called "A Letter to Zhou Yan", and received a lot of care and blessings from netizens in recent days. "This time I also brought these letters, hoping to read them to Zhou Yan, which can encourage her, make her cry less, and move forward with a smile." At the donation site, a staff member of the company squatted next to Zhou Yan and quietly read a pile of letters, conveying care and encouragement to Zhou Yan.

"Now, if you have any difficulties or needs, please tell us." Hearing Zhu Yali's words, Zhou Yan's mother couldn't help but shed tears, and they hugged again.

"The outside world should not sprinkle salt on the child's wound anymore, please give Zhou Yan more practical help." Zhu Yali also told the reporter that they specially formed a legal team, ready to provide free legal advice and help for Zhou Yan's lawsuit in the long term.

Hope our newspaper helps recommend a rehabilitation hospital

Yesterday, Zhou Yan's aid lawyer Li Zhixian revealed that since the second half of 2010, Zhou Yan has suffered from severe depression. "We obtained the medical records and Zhou Yan's medication when she took a leave of absence from school, confirming this point. This is the main reason for Zhou Yan's leave of absence, on the other hand, it is also to avoid Tao Rukun's harassment." Lawyer Li also hopes that everyone does not disturb Zhou Yan too much, so as not to cause secondary harm to Zhou Yan.

In the past two days, every time the reporter came to Zhou Yan's home, he found that either Zhou Yan was resting or her emotions were very unstable, completely different from the state when she could tell her story before. "The child's condition has not been optimistic these few days. The doctor once suggested actively treating scars for a year, strengthening functional exercises, and avoiding infection at all costs. Since the house is relatively small and there are many people coming and going, we are worried whether she has been infected externally. Therefore, the family is considering sending the child to a professional burn hospital for rehabilitation." Zhou Yan's aunt introduced.

It is understood that currently, multiple hospitals in Beijing, Shanghai, Jiangsu, Anhui and other places have expressed their willingness to provide Zhou Yan with free treatment.

"As for which hospital to choose, the family is temporarily considering, it would be best to decide in the next few days." Zhou Yan's aunt said to the reporter, everything will be done from the perspective of benefiting Zhou Yan's rehabilitation and later treatment, "We also hope you can help us check, and recommend the most suitable hospital for Zhou Yan's rehabilitation."

More than 3.5 million netizens focus on Weibo donation

In recent days, the Weibo donation activity [Restore Zhou Yan's Youth] jointly initiated by our newspaper and a certain portal website has received strong support from netizens. As of yesterday when the reporter filed the article, more than 3.5 million netizens have paid attention to it. Numerous netizens and warm-hearted people from all over the country have proactively contacted our newspaper, hoping to donate money and goods to Zhou Yan, which has deeply moved Zhou Yan's family.
