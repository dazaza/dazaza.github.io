Thanks for your cruelty, which enables me to harvest love again.

by jiating87674 on 2012-02-04 14:11:59

I looked at Yan Lin and couldn't say another word. Yan Lin was even more resolute than me; I had been persuading him to go back, but he turned a deaf ear to my advice, so I gave up. I still helped my parents manage the fruit shop every day, while Yan Lin eventually went to the city. I didn't know what he was busy with, but every weekend, he would come to the shop on time to help with business, and occasionally followed my father to the market to purchase goods. If I said I wasn't impulsive, that would be a lie, but since my heart belonged to Su Chen, I refused to accept Yan Lin.

Once, my aunt came to visit my parents in the city and happened to meet Yan Lin helping out with the business. She mistakenly thought Yan Lin was my boyfriend and secretly told my mother that Yan Lin was a good boy. Later, my aunt started chatting with Yan Lin, and I felt helpless and wanted to clarify things, but I also feared hurting Yan Lin.

"Where are you from, young man?"

"Auntie, I'm from Shaanxi," Yan Lin replied.

My aunt, who always spoke her mind, said right in front of Yan Lin: "Shaanxi? That's too far away. You see, we only have Xiaoyu as our daughter, and her parents wouldn't want her to marry someone from there." I glared at my aunt and stomped my feet. Unexpectedly, Yan Lin said, "Uncle and Auntie, you don't need to worry. We have two brothers in my family, and my parents never interfere with my personal matters. If Xiaoyu is willing, we can buy a house here too. But to be honest, Auntie, I haven't managed to win Xiaoyu's affection yet."

I was even angrier and pulled Yan Lin outside, saying, "Yan Lin, stop messing around. I tell you, it's impossible between us."

Yan Lin's smile faded, and he asked, "Do you really not feel anything for me?"

I hardened my heart and said, "Yes, from start to finish, I've only loved Su Chen. Whether he abandons me or doesn't want me, I accept it." Yan Lin's cold expression made me panic, and I didn't know what to do. He said, "Alright, I won't bother you anymore," and then turned and left. Watching his retreating back, I felt extremely sad.

I sat alone on a bench in the park, quietly reflecting on all the events that had happened recently. I felt an unprecedented sense of guilt towards Yan Lin. On second thought, if I impulsively accepted his love, it would also be disrespectful to him. Thus, I became more determined about my choice.

After Yan Lin returned home and called my parents, he left. I returned to my room feeling gloomy. My mother knocked on the door and sat next to me on the bed, earnestly saying, "Xiaoyu, Mom doesn't understand the emotions of you young people, but we see how good Yan Lin has been to you. This boy may not be eloquent, but he's filial to us. Most importantly, he came all this way for you. How many people could do that? Many times, Mom didn't ask you about it, but it doesn't mean I don't understand. The feelings between you and Su Chen might just be like flowers in the mirror and moon in the water. Let bygones be bygones!"

I said, "Mom, I know Yan Lin is good, but matters of the heart can't be forced!"

"Child, trust Mom's judgment. Yan Lin is worth your love, and feelings can be cultivated. Just like your dad and me, we hardly saw each other twice when we were young, but we ended up getting married and raising you. We've supported each other through life."

I buried my face in my mother's arms, silent. My mother continued, "You still have a long road ahead. Do you plan to mourn this relationship forever and reject everyone else?"

I said, "Isn't it better if I stay with you and Dad forever?"

"Foolish child, of course it's good, but when we're old, who will take care of you?" Listening to my mother's words, I felt a bit of sorrow and began to waver in rejecting Yan Lin. Maybe what Mom said was right—feelings can indeed be cultivated.

Because of my parents, my attitude towards Yan Lin started to soften. Seeing my change, Yan Lin no longer held a grudge and continued as usual, coming to help at home every weekend, becoming like a member of the family. Over time, my parents grew to like Yan Lin even more. I didn't know whether I should be happy or sad.

It seemed Yan Lin understood my thoughts. One day, he privately found me and said, "Xiaoyu, if you really don't feel anything for me, I won't force you. I know matters of the heart can't be forced; otherwise, both parties would end up suffering. I know you're still waiting for Su Chen. If he comes back for you, I'll step aside without hesitation. As long as you're happy, whatever happens to me doesn't matter."

Yan Lin's words made me confront my own cruelty. For the sake of a complicated wait, I was harming someone who loved me so much. I didn't know how to make up for Yan Lin and could only say, "Give me some time, I will definitely forget Su Chen. Until then, I can't accept you; it wouldn't be fair to you."

Yan Lin's face lit up with a smile, and he said, "If only you give me a chance to get closer to your heart, I'd be willing to wait however long it takes. As long as there's a chance, there's hope. What I fear most is you not giving me any chance at all." I looked at him bitterly, unable to calm down for a long time.

Yan Lin truly kept his promise. He never forced me and continued to give selflessly without expecting anything in return. The fortress in my heart gradually began to loosen its grip. But during quiet nights, I still thought of Su Chen. No matter how long I sealed those memories, they remained vivid as if they happened yesterday. I tried hard to forget, but I failed every time!

Time passed quickly, and a year went by. With his talents, Yan Lin used all his savings and borrowed money from the bank to establish a small company in the city. Though small, it was the result of all his hard work. He said, "The city is a second-tier key city, and it will surely develop better in the future. I want to take a chance. If I fail, I'll start over."

I believed in Yan Lin's abilities and fully supported his decision. When the company opened, I went to help, and Yan Lin was beaming with joy. There were fewer than ten employees in the company, but everyone was full of enthusiasm and fighting spirit. I believed that under Yan Lin's leadership, this small company would one day grow strong.

Zi Ye and Zhou Yang finally achieved their happy ending and planned to get married. I sincerely rejoiced for her, so when she asked me to be her bridesmaid, I readily agreed.

Yan Lin said to me, "Last year, I left Hangzhou on May Day. This year, I didn't expect to go back again. But what I least expected was going to attend Zhou Yang's wedding."

I said, "Indeed! Life changes, and no one can foresee the future." Yan Lin held my hand and said, "Let's not think about the future. Let's live in the present." I thought he was right; if we can't handle the present, what's the point of talking about the future? In the end, Yan Lin and I returned to Hangzhou together. In over a year, Hangzhou had undergone many changes. The originally unfinished buildings were now completed, mostly high-rise buildings that pierced into the sky like sharp cones. I felt dizzy and couldn't help asking Yan Lin, "Is Hangzhou very prosperous?"

Yan Lin looked back at me and said faintly, "It's very prosperous, but it's easy to lose direction in such a bustling city." Pondering Yan Lin's words, my eyes gazed out the window. Like flowing water that doesn't rot, everything in this world changes. More than a year ago, the person beside me was Su Chen, and now it's Yan Lin.

Zhou Yang and Zi Ye met us at the station, and we were all delighted to see each other. Zhou Yang said to Yan Lin, "Brother, I've finally left the ranks of single men. When will I be able to drink your wedding wine?"

Yan Lin laughed and said, "Just keep waiting, my friend." They chatted happily.

Zi Ye held my arm and asked, "Xiaoyu, what's going on between you and Yan Lin? I heard he gave up a high-paying job here to find you, and I almost cried. Don't miss out on such a good man."

I smiled and said, "Just wait until you become a bride. I'll figure it out." In the evening, we gathered together. Zhou Yang was indeed very good to Zi Ye, and seeing Zi Ye find her happiness made me very happy. Zi Ye was luckier than me. Her parents-in-law opposed her relationship with Zhou Yang at first, but they respected his decision and gave their blessings. This reminded me of Su Chen. If his parents hadn't opposed us, would we have had a good outcome?

On May Day, Zi Ye and Zhou Yang's wedding took place as scheduled. Many former colleagues attended, and since Yan Lin was once the manager of the direct sales department, everyone exchanged pleasantries upon meeting. After greeting him, I went backstage to accompany Zi Ye. As soon as I entered, Zi Ye pulled me and said, "Xiaoyu, I'm so nervous!"

I consoled her, "Relax a little. Every woman looks forward to walking into marriage with a deeply loving partner. Your happiness is right before your eyes, and I'm even a bit envious!"

"Do you know? It wasn't easy for Zhou Yang and me to be together. His parents initially had a lot of objections to me, and now they've finally accepted me. I wonder if I'll be able to integrate into their family in the future?"

I said, "Silly, that will be your own home. As long as Zhou Yang loves you firmly, you two will definitely be happy in the future."

Zi Ye held my hand seriously and said, "Xiaoyu, thank you! Having a friend like you in this lifetime makes me incredibly happy!"

"I'm the same."

"Xiaoyu, can I ask you a question?"

"What is it? Ask away."

Zi Ye stammered, and I urged her, "Speak up, or I won't answer."

"Don't... I want to ask if you still love Su Chen?" For a moment, I completely lost myself. For over a year, I had constantly tried to control my longing for Su Chen, but he was like a tumor in my brain—impossible to remove, impossible to eliminate. After a long pause, I said, "I've never stopped loving him."

Zi Ye showed surprise and said, "What about Yan Lin? Aren't you trying to date him?"

"No, Yan Lin promised me that unless I fall in love with him, he won't pressure me."

"What? Is there someone in this world dumber than our Zhou Yang? According to what you're saying, the possibility of you falling in love with Yan Lin is almost zero."

I sighed deeply, sadly saying, "What can you do about matters of the heart?"

"Xiaoyu, there's something I've never told you, but now I want to tell you." Zi Ye shifted her tone and continued, "Do you remember when we had a particularly bad period?"

I questioned suspiciously, "What's wrong?"

Zi Ye said, "Actually, it was because of me."

I was even more confused and urged her to finish her sentence, impatiently. Zi Ye hesitated for a moment and then revealed her past infatuation with Su Chen.

I was greatly surprised, and the idea that Zi Ye had liked Su Chen lingered in my mind. Now recalling how Zi Ye's attitude toward me changed back then, it made sense. I said, "Why didn't you say anything before? We're best friends!"

Zi Ye said, "Precisely because we're best friends, I knew it was wrong to envy you, but I couldn't control myself from liking Su Chen. He's so excellent. From the first time I saw him, I fell in love with him. I worked hard, hoping that one day I could get promoted, bringing me closer to him."

Listening to everything Zi Ye said, I couldn't accept it. I didn't know what to say, or perhaps nothing needed to be said. Zi Ye thought I was angry and immediately explained, "Xiaoyu, I don't mean anything by it. Now I have Zhou Yang, and my feelings for Su Chen have long been washed away by time. Because of this, I wanted to tell you not to dwell on the past. Give yourself a chance, and maybe you'll gain more."

The rest of Zi Ye's words were somewhat confusing to me, but I understood her feelings. Soon, I composed myself and said, "Don't say anything more, and don't worry about me! Today is the most important day of your life."

I accompanied Zi Ye through the happiest day of her life. When Zhou Yang faced everyone present and said to her with deep emotion, "Wife, from now on, I will cherish you properly. I won't let you be sad because of me. Whatever happens in the future, I will stand by you and love you for this lifetime," Zi Ye cried uncontrollably, and even I was deeply moved, nearly shedding tears from the emotional impact.

Half a month after Zi Ye's marriage, I stayed in Hangzhou. Yan Lin originally planned to stay with me, but since his company was just starting, he couldn't linger too long. Reluctantly, he had to leave first. Before leaving, he worriedly said, "Xiaoyu, why don't you go back with me?"

I said, "It's rare for me to meet Zi Ye, and I want to spend more time with her."

Yan Lin said, "Zi Ye and Zhou Yang are newlyweds; they won't have time for you."

His words frightened me. Yan Lin was so smart that he must have known this was my excuse. I thought that if he continued, I might give in and go back with him. Unexpectedly, he said, "If you want to stay longer, I won't force you to go back. Just take care of yourself."

Yan Lin's tolerance made me feel terrible about myself. But there were too many memories here related to me and Su Chen. I wanted to reminisce one last time, and afterward, I would completely forget. I convinced myself with this reason, and in the end, I cruelly let Yan Lin leave alone!

After Yan Lin left, I went to the West Lake. Coming back here after over a year, I felt sad despite myself, but thinking about the past brought a bit of joy. Su Chen was like a passerby in my life—though he stayed, he couldn't remain forever. I wondered how he was doing now and if he missed me. But no matter what, I lacked the courage to call him. After such a long time, perhaps he had already changed his number. I laughed at myself for being foolish, still revisiting old places just to feel sorrow!

I took out the set of keys from my bag, originally planning to return them when I left Hangzhou. After much deliberation, I ultimately couldn't bear to sever my connection with him. For many days and nights, I stared blankly at these keys, wondering why Su Chen hadn't fulfilled his promise, yet I still missed him so much. Since I was already in Hangzhou, I suddenly felt an urge to go to Xiamen and find him, to ask him for an answer. Regardless of the outcome, even if it meant letting go, I could finally move on!

At that moment, I thought of Xin Yao. After over a year of no contact, I couldn't determine if she had changed her number. Still, I dialed her phone number just in case, and surprisingly, Xin Yao's number hadn't changed. I was both shocked and delighted.

"Hello! May I ask who this is?" Xin Yao politely asked.

"Xin Yao, it's me—Xiaoyu."

"Xiaoyu, how can it be you? Why haven't you contacted me for over a year? Do you know how hard my brother searched for you?"

I couldn't believe my ears when Xin Yao mentioned Su Chen searching for me.

"What's wrong between you and my brother? Why did you disappear?"

Xin Yao's words threw me into turmoil. Unable to control myself, I asked, "Did my brother really look for me?"

"Do you know how badly you hurt my brother when you left? And now you're still asking such questions? I really doubt if I misjudged you."

As Xin Yao grew angrier, I hurriedly asked, "Can you tell me how your brother is doing now? Is he alright?"

"Xiaoyu, first, tell me what happened in the past year. Why did you leave Hangzhou?"

Anxious, I said, "Xin Yao, if you trust me, please tell your brother that I haven't changed. I'm still in Hangzhou, and I've always upheld my promise to him."

Xin Yao agreed to my request, and I was grateful, my heart filling with hope. Perhaps it wouldn't be long before Su Chen's call would come. Then I thought, Xin Yao said I had hurt Su Chen, but he was the one who never returned. What exactly happened in between? No matter how much I pondered, I couldn't figure it out.

Zi Ye was slightly surprised and said after a while, "Why are you asking this?"

I said, "Today I called Xin Yao, and she said I hurt her brother. I'm completely confused. Su Chen told me to wait for him when he left, speaking so earnestly. Could there be a misunderstanding between us?"

Zi Ye immediately said, "Why are you so naive? Xin Yao is Su Chen's sister, of course, she would side with her brother. Don't let her manipulate you."

I firmly said, "Impossible. Xin Yao wouldn't lie to me. Zi Ye, did Su Chen ever ask about me?"
