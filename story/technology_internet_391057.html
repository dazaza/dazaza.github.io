by zuoliyou on 2011-03-26 11:32:29

Skills for recommending products to customers of different personality types

Zuo Li You Silver Jewelry has summarized the following skills for recommending products to customers of different types:

1. For impatient customers, speed is key. You need to be quick in your actions but not rushed in your speech. For example, you should quickly retrieve items or materials. Additionally, if a customer says they will come today, make sure you are prepared in advance. Generally, impatient customers are hurried in all aspects, such as asking about prices as soon as they arrive. In these situations, you need to handle them flexibly.

2. When dealing with shrewd customers, be patient and detailed in your introduction, focusing on providing more details.

3. Fashionable white-collar customers value novelty and beauty, so emphasize design, prestige, and class.

4. Practical customers require explanations that focus on convenience, utility, and details.

5. For customers who need advice, share your opinions and views, but avoid taking over their decision-making process.

6. For decisive customers, pay close attention to them and encourage them to express their opinions. Guide them to voice their thoughts and then follow their lead to help them make decisions.

7. For indecisive customers, sometimes you need to offer some advice to help them make a decision.

8. For showy customers, listen more, agree, and acknowledge their preferences, and act accordingly.
