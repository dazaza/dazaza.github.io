The material for the rectification of Party member cadre style, based on the analysis provided by the county Party committee and county government regarding work styles and cadre rectification, has been carefully studied. Through this study, I have gained a deep understanding of the speech on the Congress spirit regarding the rectification of cadre style, combined with actual work to conduct self-examinations. I have conducted serious analyses and deeply felt that there are still many problems and shortcomings in my learning life and work. According to the requirements for cadre style rectification, I propose the following reorganization goals and plans:

One, Existing Problems and Shortcomings:

1. Theoretical knowledge is insufficient and not profound.

2. The study of Marxism-Leninism, Mao Zedong Thought, Deng Xiaoping Theory, and important ideological systems is inadequate, lacking a grasp of their scientific system and essence.

3. In terms of study content, cadres focus more seriously on policies, laws, and regulations related to work, ignoring market economy theory and modern science and technology knowledge.

4. The mode of learning lacks sufficient integration of theory with practice to guide practical applications; truly studying while working and working while studying needs improvement.

5. Work style is not solid enough. Although some results have been achieved, the consciousness of serving economic development is not impressive, privilege thoughts exist, and work style is not deep or solid.

6. Lack of innovation consciousness. Sometimes using past experiences to consider issues, wanting to base work on reality but lacking bold exploratory means.

7. Standards are not refined enough.

8. Handling problems sometimes involves fear of difficulty, indecisiveness, and concern over personal gains and losses. Ignoring work enthusiasm, initiative, and creativity reduces working standards.

Two, Rectification Goals:

1. Enhance party spirit consciousness and ideal faith.

2. Study Marxism-Leninism, Mao Zedong Thought, Deng Xiaoping Theory, especially earnestly studying and practicing the important thought of the Three Represents, and thoroughly implement study from beginning to end.

3. Enhance the sense of purpose, putting service to the masses as the highest standard.

4. Abide by the party constitution, strictly observe party discipline, follow rules and regulations, and consciously safeguard the image of cadres.

5. Strengthen study and continuously improve operational capacity and efficiency.

6. Keep pace with the times, renew ideas, work boldly, and constantly achieve new results.

Three, Rectification Measures:

1. Enhance learning and improve one's own quality. Study Marxism-Leninism, Mao Zedong Thought, Deng Xiaoping Theory, the important thought of the Three Represents, grasp their essence, deeply understand the theory of communism and the inherent theoretical quality of advancing with time. Combine study with work practice, continuously improve the ability to apply Marxist-Leninist theory, and enhance political, ideological, and cultural qualities.

2. Remember the party's purpose well and set strict demands on oneself. Always abide by the party constitution, conscientiously fulfill the obligations of Party members, actively participate in party organization activities, unite comrades, and consciously safeguard the image of the party, maintaining high agreement in thought, words, and deeds with Party organizations. Enhance party spirit conception and remain steadfast in the construction of socialism with Chinese characteristics. Ideal belief is the soul of a person and the spiritual pillar of each member and the conduct of the country, ambition/will not. Practice has proved that each party member's struggle consciousness and sense of responsibility are decided by ideal beliefs not to move or retreat.

3. Change work style cogently, strive to do one's own work. Frequently conduct thorough grassroots investigations and studies, guidance, especially focusing on how to solve and handle work experience in new situations and new problems at the grassroots level, go among the masses for thorough investigation, understanding, listening, studying, and exploring new situation * * * work in new situations, new problems, the * * * work to adapt to the new situation, the new tasks.

4. Enhance innovation consciousness, strengthen leading work. Advance with the times, blaze new trails, renew ideas further, work boldly, be diligent in thinking, ask questions, think of ways, explore multiple methods. Change style, steady work, strive to practice occupational moral models, become the vanguard of the party. Maintaining the advanced nature of Communists is important to keep the thought of keeping pace with the times. In future jobs and life, I will continue to enhance the sense of responsibility, mission, promote the truth-seeking spirit and style, close to the central task and work.

5. Strengthen moral quality, establish a civilized image. Leading advocates and practices Communist civilization, dedication, sacrifice, having intense enterprise and sense of responsibility, down-to-earth work style, hard work, practice occupational moral models.

6. Maintain close contact with the masses, establish people-loving hearts. Take people's satisfaction as the first standard to measure our work, maintain close contact with the broad masses of the people, let our actions always be under the supervision of the people and society in every aspect.
