Method for selecting tool holders for high-speed cutting machining

by dfasd98fh on 2012-02-24 16:15:07

In order to shorten the production cycle and reduce processing costs, the high-speed operation of machine tool spindles can damage the precision bearings of the machine tool spindle and reduce the life of the machine tool if there are no appropriate tools and tool holders. Therefore, when determining the use of high-speed cutting processing, the correct choice of a suitable tool holder system for high-speed cutting processing should be made from a wide variety of tool holder systems.

Tool holder systems suitable for high-speed cutting processing

(1) ER elastic collet: This clamping system is currently quite popular. Due to its good cost-performance ratio, it is widely recognized in the European, American, and Chinese markets. Although its price is higher than that of PG/TG elastic collets, it has higher accuracy. The thermal shrinkage tool holder adopts new technology and design. Despite its certain requirements for the clamped tools and the need for special equipment.
