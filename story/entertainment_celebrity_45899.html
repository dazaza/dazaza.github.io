by 304885069 on 2008-01-30 12:30:16

Recently, a passionate photo of Charlene Choi with her rumored boyfriend Edison Chen has been circulating online, causing a sensation in the entertainment industry. On the 28th, the artist management department of Charlene Choi's company, Emperor Entertainment Group, filed a report with the police and issued a serious statement condemning the illegal elements for using cut-and-paste techniques to create and spread indecent photos of Charlene Choi. They also entrusted a lawyer to follow up on the case, and once the identities of the distributors and uploaders are confirmed, they will be held legally responsible. The other party involved in the incident, Edison Chen, also issued a statement through his lawyer strongly condemning this behavior and reserving the right to legal recourse.

According to reports, after the incident occurred, people in the circle condemned the falsifiers and rallied their support behind Charlene Choi. Edison Chen's current girlfriend, Vincy, also publicly supported her boyfriend, stating that regardless of whether the photos are real or fake, she does not care about past matters.

Additionally, many netizens have left comments in discussion areas debating the authenticity of the bed photos. Many Photoshop experts have analyzed the photos with their professional knowledge. Some pointed out that the noise referred to as "rainbow spots" across the entire photo is very even, and even when the light is adjusted, no flaws can be seen. If it were a composite photo, the person who made the photo would need extremely high skills.
