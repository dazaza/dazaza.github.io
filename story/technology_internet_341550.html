The purpose of the journal is to implement the secondary school English teaching syllabus issued by the National Education Commission and cultivate English talents for reform and opening-up.

Its main feature is grade-specific publication, divided into editions for Grade 10, Grade 11, and Grade 12. The content arrangement is synchronized with teaching, integrating knowledge and interest, combining lecture and practice, focusing on key points analysis. It serves as both a teaching content guide and an in-depth study method guidance and quality education.

It has a strong editorial team, consisting of thousands of specially invited editors and writers who are special-grade and senior English teachers from the front line of teaching. The editorial department of the high school version is led by experienced teachers and reviewed by famous scholars domestically.

The journal has a wide range of columns, compiled according to the requirements of the new curriculum standards. It closely follows the curriculum standards, provides synchronous tutoring, has strong targeting, is highly practical, and accurately positioned. The test question design is reasonable, comprehensive, cultivating students' ability to learn independently, forming good learning habits and strategies.
