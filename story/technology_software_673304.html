This is a story I saw online. Whether it's true or made up by someone, it's worth a read! The content is very touching... Take a little time to experience the most sincere love in this world! Rui is a handsome boy who gets along well with everyone at school... Every student and teacher likes him. But he has an incredibly ugly mother, not only unattractive but also one-eyed. Rui's friends have never seen his mom, and naturally, no one would ask. Life seems to be going pretty well... One day, it was raining heavily outside... As the classmates were all going home with their umbrellas, Rui could only stand under the eaves because he didn't bring an umbrella. Then he saw his ugly mom coming towards him with a black umbrella, saying "Child, come quickly, be careful not to get wet." Instantly, Rui was dumbfounded. His friends had never seen his mom... What would they think? Everyone started laughing when they saw the scene...! Someone even said to Rui "So this is your mom, so ugly, aren't you scared being with her every day? Hahaha..." Speechless, he knew it was useless to say anything now... When he got home, "I never want to see you again! Why are you so ugly? And missing one eye? You're not my mom, I won't have such an ugly mom!" Rui's mother didn't say anything, just walked into the room with tears in her eyes. Time flies... Many years have passed... Rui has grown up, still as handsome and charming, with many girls chasing after him. After that incident, his mom never mentioned it again. A month later, Rui's mom passed away from illness... She left so peacefully, leaving nothing behind except for a letter. Rui opened the letter written by his mother before her death and read it: Dear child, how much I love you, for you I can disregard everything. I know you always thought I was ugly, one-eyed, and unworthy to be your mom. But now I'm going to tell you something, 20 years ago when I gave birth to you in the hospital, I was so happy, it felt like God had given me the most precious gift. Just as I was about to see you, the doctor came over, looking pale and said "Sorry, your child was born missing one eye..." Although it was hard to accept at first, like falling from the sky into a valley, I firmly told the doctor "Give my eye to him!" I have never regretted it... Even though you said those cruel words to me that time. I don't regret, nor am I sad. Because I love you. Love you, Mom No matter how sorrowful and regretful Rui is at this moment, his mom cannot come back to life...
