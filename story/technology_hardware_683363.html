Post-90s girl suspected of being addicted to stealing, judge conducts a psychological test in court

by xue94fwsh on 2012-02-29 14:03:46

What can be seen from a test paper with 399 multiple-choice questions? After a preliminary investigation, Lu Zhenguo informed Xiuxiu that she would take an exam. "The questions are very simple, all common sense of daily life. Considering your young age, we will just do a test, hope you answer the questions honestly." While Xiuxiu continued to accept the trial, Xia Lei and Chen Hongwei were "correcting" the test papers. Half an hour later, the results came out. The reporter flipped through this test paper, which was a thick stack of 399 questions all in multiple choice (part of the test questions and Xiuxiu's answers are shown in the figure below). More than half an hour later, after finishing the test paper with 399 questions, Xiuxiu returned to the courtroom to continue accepting the trial. "Why did you steal money?" "I just wanted to steal it, I felt like I couldn't control myself." "What did you do with the stolen money?" "I bought an Apple phone and went to play at the Changzhou Dinosaur Park." What is the use of psychological evaluation? "Xiuxiu often feels unwell physically, but does not know the specific uncomfortable parts, her mood tends more towards melancholy, this may be because she does not feel the care of people around her, or in her view, when she needs help, friends or family members are not by her side." Xia Lei said, in addition, in the evaluation index related to "deceitfulness", it can be clearly seen that she has a certain degree of concealment and deceit ability, "consciously avoiding some key issues also shows from another angle that her intelligence is mature enough." At 10 o'clock yesterday morning, in the criminal court of Jianye Court, presiding judge Lu Zhenguo was talking with the suspect Xiuxiu (alias). The defendant Xiuxiu on the defendant's seat was short in stature and spoke in a low voice. Jianye Court attempted to use the evaluation results to help suspects reform. Share to: Welcome to comment, I want to comment. Xia Lei said that this test paper is a very famous psychological evaluation test internationally. From the current test results, there are too many hidden things in Xiuxiu's character, not very trusting of the outside world, lacking care, possibly having been hurt before. Not long ago, Jianye Court also reviewed a case where a blind person injured others. During the trial, Lu Zhenguo found that the blind person as the defendant had a strong psychological defense mechanism, very self-respecting, and very concerned about face, which was one of the direct causes of his crime. During the trial, the psychological counselor patiently guided him, and after half an hour, the stubborn blind person voluntarily confessed his mistakes and said he would reform well. This is a new measure promoted by the Criminal Court of Jianye Court since the end of last year - introducing a psychological evaluation mechanism in criminal trials, and the results will be used as a reference for judges during trials and as important material for future suspects' reform. Xiuxiu was brought to an office next to the courtroom. There were three staff members from Nanjing's "Master Ning Hotline" (a free psychological counseling hotline established under the leadership of the Publicity Department of the Nanjing Municipal Committee), including two volunteers, Xia Lei and Chen Hongwei, both national level-two psychological counselors. After a brief conversation with Xiuxiu, they handed her the questions. "This kind of evaluation is different from psychiatric identification, the result will not directly affect the trial, nor will it become a statutory sentencing circumstance," said presiding judge Lu Zhenguo. At 5 p.m. that day, the verdict of the case came out, Xiuxiu was sentenced to three years in prison for theft, and this result was not affected by the evaluation result, "Even so, doing this evaluation still has great significance." □Fast News Reporter Tian Xueting Lu Zhenguo said that there are many reasons why defendants in criminal cases stand on the defendant's seat, but it cannot be denied that when they harm others, they also passively become victims themselves, and their psychological changes cannot be ignored. Introducing a psychological evaluation mechanism, allowing psychological counselors to intervene in the trial process, giving necessary psychological guidance and help to suspects, "let them recognize their mistakes from the heart, and have confidence in this society, see the dark side, but also see the good side, this way, they can truly repent inside, and it will directly lead to a qualitative change in serving sentences for reform." Relevant personnel of the Jianye District Court said that the current psychological evaluation mechanism is still in the trial stage, the evaluation results will be included in the suspect's file materials, if the suspect ultimately has to serve a sentence, these materials will be sent to the place of punishment, the judge will communicate with the educator, and the evaluation results will be used as important materials for educational work, "targeted help can reduce the possibility of recidivism fundamentally."
