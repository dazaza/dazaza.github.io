Things that you only do with your wife in a lifetime, saw 49 of them and almost cried... [Picture]

by tuoqing1188 on 2011-08-03 13:30:31

[B]1. Buy you a big house, reserve a nursery for our baby.

2. Let you lie on my lap quietly reading, then stroke your hair.

3. When you say you're too tired to cook, I'll carry you to bed, cover you with blankets, and then personally make my best instant noodles for you!

4. Coax you to eat when you don't want to eat, and if that doesn't work, feed you.

5. Take you to the Maldives and feel the stillness of time.

6. Then take you to Africa so you can feel how white you are.

7. Secretly switch my toothbrush with yours.

8. Leave a special memory in your heart on every special day.

9. Help you scrub your back while showering, and rub your feet after bathing.

10. Massage your shoulders when you're tired.

11. Kiss you without any reason.

12. Be the first reader of what you write, and encourage you no matter how it is.

13. Go mountain climbing together every autumn.

14. Often hold or carry you, even in public.

15. After waking up every day, look at your sleeping face first, and give you a gentle kiss.

16. Often make you both cry and laugh.

17. Accompany you shopping and cook together.

18. When walking on the street, never let you be more than one meter away from me, and never let you walk on the side closer to the road.

19. Hold your hand when crossing the street.

20. During your special period, bring you the red sugar poached egg I made, and see you wipe your tears on my chest.

21. Give way to you in life; even if you're wrong, it's my fault, and I will definitely apologize after an argument. But I will stick to principles regarding right and wrong.

22. Listen to you call me "fool" or whatever name you want to call me.

23. Wake you up deliberately at night to hear your lazy voice.

24. Be by your side and help you when you're helpless.

25. Comb your hair without hurting you.

26. Not disturb you when you're concentrating on reading.

27. Compete with you for food, leaving the most nutritious and delicious parts for you.

28. Listen to your phone calls from the seaside, hearing your cries as you step on the waves.

29. Buy you a bunch of flowers while buying groceries for you.

30. Stay by your side when you're sick, tuck you in, touch your forehead, and embrace you.

31. Always have your photo or family portrait in my wallet.

32. Raise children together with you.

33. After work, if I see the lights at home are off, I will definitely open the door myself and not wake you up.

34. Deliberately kick your blanket off in the middle of the night so you'll crawl into my bed.

35. Follow your advice: no smoking, less drinking, more fruit, and regular meals.

37. Say to you during thunderstorms, "Come, let me hold you and sleep," because I know you're afraid of thunder.

38. Drink milk together every day.

39. After we argue, I will sleep in the living room so you can think it over, and then wait for you to come and cover me with a blanket.

40. Grow a little beard and secretly prick your face when I kiss you.

41. When you're 70, accompany you to watch the sunset.

42. Watch horror movies together and comfort you saying, "Don't be afraid, I'm here!"

43. When you lean on the balcony railing, hold you from behind.

44. Don't allow you to say words like "thank you" or "I'm sorry," as that would seem too formal.

45. Appear in front of you when it rains.

46. When we're short of money, I will embarrassingly say, "Honey, give me some pocket money!"

47. Sit aside enjoying while you and the child play in the park.

48. Listen carefully to every word you say.

49. If anything unexpected happens to me, I hope you can find someone better than me who loves you.

50. Every day, thank heaven for letting us meet, know each other, and fall in love...
