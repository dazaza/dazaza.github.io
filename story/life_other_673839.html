Such love, dare you to accept it?

by xiaoxiao67 on 2012-02-23 22:49:15

Can you accept such love?

In France, there was once a romance. There was a boy named Little Bear and a girl named Dandan. They were in the same school, in Grade 6. Little Bear was an honest and responsible little boy. Dandan was a gentle and kind girl, but unfortunately, she lost her sight in an accident.

In the school, many people did not play with Dandan and looked down on her, but Dandan did not feel sad or complain. Only Little Bear played with her because he thought Dandan was a cute girl who needed protection so that she would not be sad.

One day, while Dandan was resting in the classroom, some male classmates came over to scold her and threw her books around. Little Bear had just returned from the snack bar. Seeing Dandan being bullied, he rushed forward to stop them, but those male classmates did not stop and instead hit Little Bear. Dandan didn't know what happened. After the male classmates left, Little Bear picked up the books and gave them back to Dandan.

Dandan asked Little Bear: "Are you okay?"

Little Bear shyly said: "I'm fine, haha..."

Dandan said: "That's good, thank you! Let's go have lunch together."

Little Bear shyly replied: "Alright."

They brought their lunch boxes to the seaside for a meal. After eating, they sat on the rocks enjoying the sea breeze.

Dandan asked Little Bear: "What do you want to be when you grow up?"

Little Bear said: "I don't know, maybe protect you!"

Dandan smiled and said: "Haha, really?"

Little Bear said: "Yeah, really!"

Dandan said: "Alright then, let's make a pinky promise! This is our agreement!"

Little Bear said: "Okay!"

Little Bear asked Dandan: "Then you, what do you want to be when you grow up?"

Dandan said: "I want to be a teacher, and I hope my eyes will get better soon, so I can see you who protects me!"

Little Bear said: "Haha, okay, work hard!"

Dandan said: "Okay, you too! Let's go back to class!"

Little Bear said: "Alright, Dandan, actually..."

Dandan said: "What's wrong?"

Little Bear said: "Never mind... let's go..."

A few months later... The teacher said in class: "Classmates, Dandan is going abroad to study and treat her eyes. We should remember Dandan!"

The students in the classroom all clapped their hands. Those who used to bully her came to Dandan and said: "Dandan, we're sorry..." Dandan smiled and said: "It's okay!"

Little Bear looked at Dandan sadly. Dandan didn't know that Little Bear was looking at her sadly. At this time, Dandan went up, took off the necklace she was wearing, and gave it to Little Bear. Little Bear didn't say anything...

Dandan walked out of the school gate. Little Bear suddenly rushed out and shouted loudly outside the window: "Dandan, actually that day at the seaside, I wanted to tell you: 'I like you!'" But Dandan was too far away to hear...

16 years passed. Little Bear became a driver delivering things, and Dandan came back, becoming a teacher and having her eyes cured. She met a boy abroad and they were very happy together.

When Little Bear knew Dandan came back, he was very happy, but he was too shy to find Dandan because Little Bear knew she was now happily with another person. He didn't want to destroy her happiness and could only silently watch Dandan from afar. Every year on her birthday, he sent flowers and gifts to Dandan, but never wrote his name.

One day after Dandan finished work, she encountered several hoodlums teasing her. Little Bear saw this from afar and rushed forward to beat those hoodlums. Dandan, hiding nearby, saw Little Bear but didn't know who he was. Little Bear also didn't tell her who he was.

Little Bear said: "Let me send you home." Dandan agreed.

On the way home, Dandan asked Little Bear what his name was. Little Bear casually said a name, Xiao Jun. Haha, you were really good just now, thank you! Dandan talked about her childhood: "When I was a child, I couldn't see, and I had few friends. There was a boy named Xiong. He was a good friend of mine. He often protected me and accompanied me to the seaside for the sea breeze, and we swung in the park. We even made a pinky promise to protect me. He tricked me, I didn't see him when he came back."

Little Bear foolishly laughed and said: "Maybe he secretly protected you." After sending Dandan home, suddenly a car rushed over. A man got out of the car and stabbed Little Bear with a knife. Little Bear fell to the ground, and a diary fell out of his pocket. Dandan panicked and called an ambulance...

After being sent to the hospital, Little Bear was under emergency treatment. Dandan was outside the operating room holding the diary that Little Bear had dropped. Out of curiosity, she took a look and couldn't help crying. It turned out that the person who sent flowers and gifts every year was you. It turned out that you have been waiting for me, protecting me all along, and you didn't deceive me!

When Dandan was about to turn to the last page, the doctor came out and told Dandan: "We've done our best..."

Dandan saw the last page, which was the necklace that she had given to Little Bear. Little Bear stuck it on the last page. It was written: "Our agreement on June 23, 1985, I will take good care of Dandan. Because I love her." Dandan couldn't bear the shock, broke up with her boyfriend, and cried for several days and nights in the room. Then, Dandan committed suicide...

Dandan wrote a letter: "Mom and Dad, I'm sorry! I am unfilial! But I love him very much. I hope that the two of us can be buried together so that I can be with him forever. I hope a wedding can be held at the funeral. This is my last request!"

Dandan's parents endured the pain of losing their daughter and finally fulfilled Dandan's last wish by burying Dandan and Little Bear together and holding a wedding at the funeral!

Little Bear waited for 20 years and still did not say "I love you" to Dandan in person!
