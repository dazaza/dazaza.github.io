Vol.02 It turns out he is "NO.2 Xuan" The desperate scream of the "no" girl pierces through the clouds and echoes endlessly above the entire "Three Xuan" expressway bridge. Only now do the "rice bugs" wake up from their stupor, frantically taking pictures of this intimate scene. The boy steps back, glances at me indifferently, as if what just happened was the most normal and natural thing: "I hate apple-flavored lip gloss. Remember to switch to lychee flavor next time." I widen my eyes... My goodness, what is this guy talking about?! This was my first kiss with Belulu! How dare he steal my first kiss without my consent and then criticize the taste of my lip gloss so arrogantly! "Xianze, have you decided to date her? Xianze, you are really leaving me behind, aren't you?!" The girl reaches out, grabbing at something in the air, trying hard to grab the boy, but after a while, she grabs nothing. "I like you, I like you, I like you... I like you!" The paper airplane sways and drifts down in the cold wind, lonely like a child who can't find its home. The boy straightens his body, lazily glancing at the girl: "Don't say you like me, or I'll throw up." This... this line! A flash of white light instantly flashes through my mind. No wonder I felt his name was so familiar. It turns out he is Li Xianze, the "NO.2 Xuan" of the "Three Xuan Wangcheng"! How could I be so stupid, last night when I was browsing the "Three Xuan Wangcheng" website, I saw this name quite frequently. I... *^_^* I was actually kissed by the "NO.2 Xuan" of "Three Xuan Wangcheng", how happy... The lips that were kissed still retain the boy's unique faint fragrance, as if branded with an indelible mark. Wait a minute! You idiot Belulu! Now is not the time to be happy! Don't forget the purpose of your transfer to "Three Xuan Wangcheng" Academy. You bear such a heavy responsibility, how can you afford to be infatuated here?! And don't forget, you like Yin Luo, the Yin Luo you've had a crush on for ten whole years! Damn it, what should I do! I'm going crazy! After saying that sentence, the boy went straight into the blue-black "Bugatti Veyron" sports car, and before those rice bugs could react, he had already started the engine. At the same time, the girl screamed "Li Xianze, I will forever and ever like you" and then jumped off the helicopter, falling towards the river like a bullet. I and the "rice bugs" froze. The helicopter pilot screamed in shock. But the black-blue "Bugatti Veyron" did not stop, resolutely and ruthlessly drove out of the "Three Xuan" expressway bridge, leaving a group of ants on the bridge in a panic. In an instant, shouts, cries for help, and car horns sounded together. Indeed, this year I am cursed by Tai Sui. As soon as I transferred to "Three Xuan Wangcheng", I encountered such an unlucky event. I gritted my teeth, turned my car sideways and rushed out of the "Three Xuan" expressway bridge, not wanting to deal with the chaos behind me. There's no other way. With my terrible luck, if I don't run away, I'd be an idiot! Three minutes later, I drove my pink princess car shaped like a rabbit to the gate of "Three Xuan Wangcheng". Tall walls with thick black glass, magnificent pillars with colored glass, fountains and swan statues made of white glass, plus the blue school sign shining with a cold light above my head... The entire school is like a crystal palace carved out of silver and jade. And beside the school gate, there is a "Three Xuan Fengxing Edition" made of transparent glass, which automatically scrolls to broadcast all kinds of latest and coolest information rankings. The "Three Xuan Wangcheng" academy has three "Xuans": "NO.1 Xuan" is Zuo Ge from the sports department - cruel and almost brutal, he is revered as a god by the girls of the "Three Xuan Wangcheng" academy. He holds 101 girlfriend candidate emblems in his hands, of which 100 have already been distributed. In his world, there is no pity for beauty, no pure and perfect love. Girlfriends only mean pets, vases, servants... His catchphrase: When you fall, do you want to sit, lie down, or kneel? "NO.2 Xuan" is Li Xianze from the art department - a fairy-like boy who is stunningly handsome and makes the earth shake. He can kiss any girl he doesn't like, but he cannot accept the sincere feelings of a girl who likes him; he can talk and laugh freely with the ugliest girl in the world, but he cannot give the love that the most beautiful girl in the world desires. His catchphrase: Don't tell me you like me, or I'll throw up. "NO.3 Xuan" is Yin Luo from the academic department - a genius teenager with an IQ of two hundred, he is the myth in the hearts of the girls of the "Three Xuan Wangcheng" academy and the emperor in the hearts of the boys. Champion of the past few international Olympiads, physics competitions, and chemistry competitions. Every innovation of his must participate in international competitions. It's said he's even busier than Bill Gates, and in a semester, the total number of classes he attends is less than a week. His catchphrase: Give me a problem, if it can stump me for how long, I will date you for that long. Alas... And the person I want to pursue, who appeared in the photo, is the "NO.1 Xuan" of "Three Xuan Wangcheng" - the notorious bad-tempered young master of the city, Zuo Ge! I've heard about his various misdeeds since the time in "William Castle", and yesterday when I was browsing the website and forum of "Three Xuan Wangcheng", I also saw many of his terrible deeds... Why am I so unfortunate, Belulu, to bump into him right away!～～～＞O＜～～～ This school is divided into three departments: the art department, the sports department, and the academic department. The east side is the school gate, the west side is the sports department, the south side is the academic department, and the north side is the art department! I am proficient in piano, violin, harp, guzheng, guitar... everything except sports. To conveniently pursue Zuo Ge from the sports department, I had to hide all my artistic talents and asked my father to smooth things over to get me transferred to this department. Sniff... I don't know any sports at all. If someone finds out about my artistic talent, I will definitely be forced to transfer to the art department! Don't think about it! Don't think about it! I shake my head wildly, maybe things aren't as bad as I imagined! Let's hurry up and go to class, if I keep dragging, it will be almost time to go home.
