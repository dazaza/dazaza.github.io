Are there any differences between correspondence study and self-study exams? Private Education Service Network

by sudaa4t5 on 2012-02-28 12:58:40

After taking the adult college entrance examination, you need to attend classes. After taking the self-study examination, there is no need to attend classes, and the graduation certificate is also from the self-study examination. Here are ten advantages of the self-study examination:

1. "Wide entry, strict exit," without an entrance examination. High school graduates or those with equivalent qualifications can enroll directly, and there is no limit on the number of places by the national plan. This opens a convenient door for the largest number of people who want to receive higher education.

2. Implementation of "separation of teaching and examination." The national and provincial/municipal education departments control the teaching and examination standards, ensuring the quality of teaching.

3. Most of the main examination institutions are key universities, such as Peking University, Tsinghua University, Renmin University, etc. The graduation certificates are jointly issued by these institutions and the National Self-examination Committee, and are recognized by the state. The authority and credibility of the graduation certificate are high.

4. China's self-study examination graduates are recognized in some major countries such as the United States, the United Kingdom, Canada, and Australia, and are connected with the education systems of many countries around the world.

5. The self-study examination has low fees, and full-time study costs only half of what high vocational colleges charge; the registration fee and textbook fee for self-study are only dozens of yuan per subject.

6. Students can freely choose their majors. They can choose majors based on their interests, strengths, and potential abilities, which is beneficial for future development.

7. The self-study examination system is the most flexible. You can study full-time or part-time. There are few restrictions on the graduation time limit, and completing the required credits within 8 years makes you a qualified graduate.

8. Free choice between associate degree and bachelor's degree. Continuing from an associate degree to a bachelor's degree does not require another examination, greatly facilitating further education for graduates at the associate degree level.

9. The self-study examination has no provincial or regional restrictions and covers the widest area.

10. This form of self-study education promotes independent learning among students, cultivating good habits of self-study. This is a very important ability and condition for achieving talent, and its long-term impact surpasses the learning of knowledge itself.
