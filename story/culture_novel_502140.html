Five figures rushed straight towards the gun-shaped spiritual treasure floating in mid-air, their eyes burning with fervor.

Lin Dong's gaze swept over the other four people, then paused on a middle-aged man standing at the far left. The imposing fluctuations of Yuan Power emanating from his body let Lin Dong know that his strength was at the Great Completion stage of the Genesis Sphere.

The other three were still only at the Lesser Completion stage. Though they weren't weak, they didn't pose much of a threat to Lin Dong.

"So many experts!"

This was just one of the competition zones with the fewest participants, yet it already contained one Great Completion and three Lesser Completions. This intense competition made even Lin Dong inwardly marvel.

But despite his admiration, Lin Dong showed no mercy in his actions. Although experts at the Genesis Sphere could hover in mid-air, they were far from being able to condense their Yuan Power into various shapes like Lin Langtian and the others. Thus, their flexibility while airborne was far inferior to Lin Dong, who had mental energy support.

A flash of cold light appeared beneath his feet, accelerating his figure. In an instant, he approached the gun-shaped spiritual treasure emitting a faint luster.

"Boy, you're looking for death!"

The four individuals behind, seeing Lin Dong take the lead, became furious. With a wave of their hands, four ferocious streams of Yuan Power erupted, viciously smashing towards Lin Dong's back.

"Hmph!"

Sensing the fierce gusts coming from behind, Lin Dong let out a cold snort. With a flicker of thought, a robust mental defense formed behind him, repelling the four streams of Yuan Power.

"Shoo!"

As he resisted the four people's attack, Lin Dong flicked his finger, causing four long swords to dart out from his spatial ring. They then surged forward with sharp sword qi, shooting towards the four people behind. A chaotic series of slashes left the four temporarily flustered, after all, being in mid-air without any leverage made them seem somewhat clumsy.

Amidst the chaos of the four, Lin Dong also neared the gun-shaped spiritual treasure, then reached out to grab it.
