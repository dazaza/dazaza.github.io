Conditions for citizens applying for overseas travel on official business: According to the regulations stipulated in the "Implementation Rules of the Law of the People's Republic of China on Entry and Exit Administration", the term "official business" refers to: settling down, visiting relatives and friends, studying (including self-funded study abroad and public-funded study abroad), traveling (including individual overseas travel and group overseas travel), working abroad, inheriting property, and other non-official activities. Specifically:

1. If one spouse has settled abroad or been invited by a foreign resident friend to settle, or is going abroad to inherit property, or has obtained a settlement permit issued by the destination country, they can apply for overseas settlement;

2. If invited by relatives or friends abroad who have permanent residency rights or valid long-term residence documents in the destination country to visit relatives and friends, they can apply for overseas visits to relatives and friends;

3. If dispatched by the state or employer to study abroad or go abroad for public-funded study, they can apply for overseas study;

4. If participating in an overseas tour organized by a travel agency approved by the state with "entry tourism" operating rights, or going abroad individually for tourism, they can apply for overseas tourism;

5. If signing a contract to work abroad with foreign employers or authorized overseas employment service institutions, or being dispatched by domestic employment companies for overseas employment, they can apply for overseas labor and employment;

6. Various enterprise personnel who need to be sent abroad due to business needs can apply for overseas business;

7. If needed for other non-official activities.

Conditions:

1. Self-funded study abroad is not restricted by academic qualifications or age. Anyone who meets the conditions can apply for self-funded study abroad to attend high school (arts and sciences), graduate school, or vocational training.

2. Graduates and self-funded students applying for self-funded overseas study can directly apply to the public security authorities at their place of household registration for self-funded overseas study procedures with relevant certificates and materials.

3. Self-funded students and graduates below the junior college level (with junior college academic qualifications or above) can apply for self-funded overseas study after serving a certain number of years domestically or repaying higher education compensation fees. (Service period: five years for undergraduate graduates, three years for graduate school graduates, and two years for junior college and adult university graduates; Repayment of cultivation fees: 1,500 yuan per year for junior college students, 2,500 yuan per year for undergraduate students, 4,000 yuan per year for master's degree students, and 6,000 yuan per year for doctoral students.)

4. Doctoral graduates going abroad for post-doctoral research are exempt from paying higher education cultivation fees.

5. In addition to the above conditions, self-funded overseas study should also have: First, sufficient foreign exchange sponsorship or overseas scholarships obtained through legal and legitimate procedures, or holding economic guarantee letters required by sponsors (which must be recognized by the destination country), or holding scholarship or financial assistance certificates required by overseas colleges, scientific research institutions, or foundations. The obtained foreign exchange sponsorship or overseas scholarships should be sufficient to maintain one's study and life abroad. Second, one should have a corresponding level of foreign language proficiency.

Procedures and Relevant Regulations:

After receiving the admission certificate and foreign exchange sponsorship certificate, if the applicant is a current student or employed person, they should first submit an application to their unit or school, and the unit or school should sign an opinion. Then, the applicant should hold relevant materials and the unit-signed proof to apply for an overseas passport at the local public security department. The public security department will issue approval according to relevant regulations and issue a passport. The holder of the passport should then go to the Chinese embassy or consulate of the destination country to handle the entry visa.

Undergraduate, junior college students, and graduate students in universities can retain their student status for one year when going abroad. Self-funded overseas study personnel studying graduate studies abroad can choose to become self-funded public dispatch overseas study personnel if they wish, and the Chinese embassy or consulate abroad will issue a "State Dispatched Overseas Study Personnel Certificate," without changing the passport and visa.

Self-funded overseas study is handled by the local public security department. Current students or employed persons applying for self-funded overseas study should have their schools or units sign opinions.
