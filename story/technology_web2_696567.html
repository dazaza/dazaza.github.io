Vigorously promote the culture of celebrities - speed up the construction of a culturally renowned county

by xyxandy8 on 2012-03-08 09:39:39

Promoting the culture of famous people and accelerating the construction of a cultural county, yesterday Pingyang County held an academic symposium on "Huang Gongwang and Pingyang" as well as the groundbreaking ceremony for the Huang Gongwang Cultural Plaza. Attendees included Zhao Derun, a member of the Central Institute of History and Culture and editor-in-chief of Chinese Calligraphy and Painting Magazine; Xue Yongnian, a member of the Central Institute of History and Culture and director of the Theory Committee of the Chinese Artists Association; Wen Zhou's vice mayor Qiu Yangjun; and local leaders Wang Zhongyi, Huang Min, Sun Ruiqing, Hu Fangyong, among others.

The academic symposium on "Huang Gongwang and Pingyang" was an important part of the China (Wenzhou) Calligraphy and Painting Exhibition —— "Huang Gongwang’s Paintings Return to His Hometown" and the 2011 National Famous Artists Chinese Painting Exhibition. The painter Huang Gongwang, born in 1269, was the leading figure among the "Four Masters of the Yuan Dynasty." In all historical documents and inscriptions signed by Huang Gongwang that indicate his place of origin, he is identified as "Huang Gongwang from Pingyang," thus confirming him as a native of Pingyang. Huang Gongwang's highest artistic achievement is represented by the painting "Dwelling in the Fuchun Mountains." This long scroll uses clear and moist brushwork and a simple yet distant artistic conception to depict the beautiful scenery along the banks of the Fuchun River in early autumn. Its layout is both sparse and dense, with infinite variations, vividly portraying the vast and continuous landscape of southern Jiangnan, achieving the realm of "mountains and rivers are rich and lush." Later generations, including the artist Zou Zhilin, praised this work as the "Lanting Xu" of painting.

Qiu Yangjun delivered remarks at the academic symposium on "Huang Gongwang and Pingyang." He said that in recent years, while fully advancing economic development, the Wenzhou Municipal Party Committee and Government have attached great importance to the construction of a major cultural city, particularly focusing on the research, promotion, and inheritance of historical and cultural figures as an essential component of this effort. As an outstanding representative of Wenzhou's historical figures, strengthening research into Huang Gongwang's cultural history holds significant meaning for exploring the origins of Wenzhou's culture, showcasing its cultural heritage, and promoting the great development and prosperity of Wenzhou's culture. He sincerely invited renowned masters to share their insights and offer valuable opinions regarding the excavation, research, and publicity campaign surrounding Huang Gongwang's culture and traditional historical culture.

Party Secretary Wang Zhongyi said in his speech that culture is the soul of a city and also an important support for the economic and social development of a region. Accelerating the construction of a culturally renowned county and enhancing cultural soft power is currently a very important and urgent task for Pingyang County. During the "Twelfth Five-Year Plan" period, we will take the creation of a historic and cultural city as our focus, deeply implementing the "Four Two" project, vigorously promoting the 1700-year-old brilliant history of Pingyang, continuously improving cultural connotation and taste, and driving Pingyang to transition from a culturally renowned county to a strong cultural county. As the hometown of Huang Gongwang, it is our duty without shirking responsibility to publicize, excavate, and promote the cultural heritage of Huang Gongwang. This academic symposium will surely fill the gap in academic research on "Huang Gongwang and Pingyang" and will surely have a positive impact on the future cultural development of Pingyang.

Zhao Derun introduced the basic situation of Huang Gongwang's paintings and a series of art discussions conducted around the world this year concerning the "Dwelling in the Fuchun Mountains," highly praising Wenzhou and Pingyang's proactive attitude towards cultural inheritance.

At the conference, experts present discussed the theme of "Huang Gongwang and Pingyang." It is reported that the results of this discussion will be compiled into a book. (Reported by Huang Jianping of the Pingyang News Network)
