April is just the wedding season.

by hjdsk11879 on 2011-06-11 18:15:01

Countless couples are busy preparing for their weddings. And merchants related to the wedding industry, such as wedding photography studios, wedding planning companies, specialized suppliers of cigarettes and alcohol for weddings, clothing stores, jewelry stores, and home appliance and furniture malls, are all vying to seize business opportunities and make profits from weddings. So, how much does a wedding cost nowadays? Is it worth spending that much money?

According to investigations, the cost of an average wedding these days generally falls around 70,000 to 80,000 yuan. But where exactly does this money go? To answer this question, reporters conducted interviews.

**Wedding Banquet**

Average table price: 1000 yuan

"Hosting a wedding banquet used to be profitable, but now it's a loss," complained Mr. Li, who is getting married on May Day. "The prices for banquet tables are quite high these days. Even mid-range hotels have table prices over 1000 yuan, some even reaching over 2000 yuan." Mr. Li eventually booked a mid-range hotel with a table price of 998 yuan, which is considered a medium price.

It is understood that with the rise in various raw material and labor costs, the standard of wedding banquets in Shijiazhuang has indeed been increasing in recent years. Yesterday, reporters interviewed several larger-scale restaurants capable of hosting wedding banquets. Among them, high-end hotels like World Trade Plaza Hotel, China Grand Hotel, International Building Hotel, and Century Grand Hotel have minimum table prices above 1180 yuan; while large restaurants like Baoding Guild Hall, Guangming Fish Port, and Xiangjunfu offer wedding banquet prices starting at 888 yuan, 780 yuan, or even 688 yuan (with a requirement of at least 40 tables). According to restaurant staff, most guests tend to choose mid-range pricing rather than the lowest available.

In addition to the table price, the necessary expenses for cigarettes, alcohol, candy, and tea must also be calculated separately. "Each table gets two packs of cigarettes, and the quality can't be too low, so they should cost at least 10 yuan per pack; then there's the alcohol, whose quality varies greatly, making the price difference significant. For ordinary people's weddings, if the alcohol needs to be decent, it should cost at least 40-50 yuan per bottle, putting two bottles on each table would cost around 100 yuan; plus soft drinks like Coca-Cola, Sprite, orange juice, and snacks like peanuts, sunflower seeds, and candies, none of which can be missed," said Mr. Li, sighing about the cost of weddings. "A friend of mine got married last month, with tables priced at 1280 yuan, and hosted 60 tables. Including cigarettes, alcohol, candy, and tea, the wedding banquet alone cost nearly 100,000 yuan!"

Calculation: One banquet table at 1000 yuan + cigarettes at 20 yuan + alcohol at 100 yuan + beverages and snacks at 20 yuan = 1140 yuan per table. If hosting 40 tables, the total cost would be 1140 * 40 = 45,600 yuan.

**Attire**

From bride and groom's wear to "family wedding attire"

"Now is the peak season for weddings, and our 'family portraits' are selling particularly well," said a relevant person from Beiguo Mall. Here, the "family portrait" isn't a photograph but refers to "family wedding attire," including the groom's suit, bride's dress, outfits for both sets of parents, and the flower girl's dress. Generally, this includes seven or eight sets of clothing, which can be purchased individually or as a set. If bought as a set, several thousand yuan would cover everything.

A mall staff member mentioned that according to their statistics, the price range for grooms purchasing a wedding suit is from 1000 yuan to 20,000 yuan, with the most popular being between 2000 and 5000 yuan, which is considered the mass-market price; brides' cheongsams and dresses are slightly cheaper, with those priced between 800 and 3000 yuan being the most popular. "When selecting wedding attire, newlyweds usually consider practicality, hoping to still wear the clothes after the wedding. Therefore, the design of the bride's dress incorporates more fashion elements, making the price slightly higher than previous years," said the salesperson. The outfits for the parents-in-law are a new trend in recent years, and both sets of parents will definitely take care to dress up. Buying a basic outfit for the father-in-law or mother-in-law might cost around 500-600 yuan. A typical flower girl's dress costs around 200-300 yuan.

Additionally, the wedding day requires a bridal gown, which most brides do not purchase due to only wearing it for one day. Instead, they rent or borrow one. Renting a stunning gown from a bridal shop typically costs around 800 yuan/day, while a regular gown would be sufficient at 300-400 yuan/day.

Calculation: Groom's suit at 3000 yuan + Bride's dress at 1000 yuan + Father-in-law and mother-in-law's outfits at 600 yuan each + Parents' outfits at 600 yuan each + Flower girl's dress at 300 yuan + Rental of bridal gown at 400 yuan = 5900 yuan

**Jewelry**

Complete jewelry set generally costs around 10,000 yuan

At every jewelry store and counter in malls, there is a wave of newlyweds purchasing wedding rings. "Wedding rings are a must, but having only wedding rings is not enough," laughed Mr. Wang, who is preparing for his wedding. "Nowadays, getting married usually involves buying at least three pieces of jewelry: ring + necklace + earrings, preferably matching diamond jewelry. If not going for something extravagant, 10,000 yuan would cover it."

From the jewelry counters in malls, it was learned that although various brands offer discount promotions for newlyweds, the overall price of wedding rings this year is still higher than previous years, whether it's diamond rings or gold rings. "Both gold and diamond prices have increased, and the processing fees for some beautifully designed jewelry have also gone up," said a salesperson at a jewelry counter in a mall. Currently, the price of gold jewelry has risen to 388 yuan/gram, and buying a gold bracelet requires about 20 grams, costing nearly 8,000-9,000 yuan for just one piece.

Calculation: Diamond ring + Necklace + Earrings = Approximately 10,000 yuan

**Ceremony**

Decorations, emcee, and car rentals all require expenses

The ceremony on the wedding day is undoubtedly the "main event," as much of the work done by the bride and groom beforehand is aimed at making a stunning appearance at the wedding. Based on the reporter's investigation, the vast majority of couples getting married today choose wedding planning companies because they handle the atmosphere and wedding etiquette more professionally. However, the quotes from wedding planning companies have also increased significantly in recent years. Yesterday, when consulting a wedding planning company, the price for an emcee + videographer + on-site decoration ranged from 4000 to 6600 yuan, with the emcee's fee alone varying from 800 to 2800 yuan.

"Renting wedding cars is also costly," said Mr. Li, who is preparing for his son's wedding. He inquired at several wedding planning companies and found that if the wedding convoy consists entirely of Audis, the cost per car is 450 yuan, totaling 3600 yuan for eight cars. "This is already on the cheaper side. I originally wanted BMWs, but their rental price is 900 yuan per car, twice as expensive. In the end, we decided to use Audis."

If you prefer convenience, entrusting the wedding venue decoration, emcee, wedding cars, and videography to a wedding planning company would cost over ten thousand yuan.

Calculation: Emcee + Videographer + Venue Decoration + Wedding Car Fleet = Approximately 8000 yuan

**Wedding Photography**

Four to five thousand yuan is the common price

For newlyweds, taking wedding photos is a "must-have activity." It's unimaginable for older generations that a set of wedding photos now costs three to five thousand yuan.

Yesterday, reporters consulted multiple wedding photography studios, finding that the price for a set of wedding photos generally ranges from 2000 to 10,000 yuan, with 3000 to 5000 yuan being the common price.

Among the various styles of wedding photos, most newlyweds prefer to try both modern and classical styles. A manager of a wedding photography studio said that they indeed launched some personalized wedding photo packages, such as lively, funny, or cute types, mainly to cater to the individualized styles of the post-90s generation. However, the most popular choice among newlyweds remains traditional wedding photos, where they wish to be draped in white veils and holding flowers to leave everlasting memories. Classical wedding photo packages are also quite popular.

Calculation: Outdoor photography + Indoor photography + Different styles = Approximately 4000 yuan

**Voices**

"Don't spend all your parents' retirement money on your wedding"

A survey showed that among 70 newlywed couples, 87% spent their parents' money on their weddings, 51.43% relied mainly on their parents' support, and only three couples fully self-funded their weddings, accounting for 4.28%.

During interviews, reporters noticed a phenomenon: most "post-80s" newlyweds do not overly concern themselves with the grandeur of their weddings, whereas their parents are the ones who place the most importance on it. Most parents believe that marriage is a big deal, and since families these days often have only one child, they want to make sure their children are satisfied.

In response, the head of the Wedding Celebration Committee of Shijiazhuang Commercial Federation analyzed that out of love for their children, parents will always try their best to make their children's weddings grand and festive. As a result, what should have been their children's joyous occasion ends up depleting the parents' retirement savings, and often leaves them with a large debt. This actually hinders the cultivation of their children's sense of responsibility. Therefore, weddings should be within one's means, and blind competition should be avoided.

**Case**

"We spent 110,000 yuan on our wedding"

Mr. Lin, a citizen, had just finished hosting his wedding. Yesterday, he detailed the wedding expenses to reporters:

Clothing: Bought a suit for himself at 3980 yuan, and shoes weren't cheap either, costing 880 yuan after discounts. His wife was more frugal, only buying a cheongsam for 280 yuan and shoes for 500 yuan. Both sets of parents bought clothes for the wedding day, but since they paid for it themselves, the exact amount is unclear and thus not included.

Jewelry: Spent 5800 yuan on a diamond ring and 2900 yuan on a platinum necklace, neither being too expensive.

Wedding Scene: Hired a wedding planning company for the on-site decoration, costing 3800 yuan; the emcee was introduced by a friend, and he was paid 1000 yuan. Regarding the wedding car fleet, instead of hiring a wedding planning company, relatives and friends helped, arranging eight Audis, saving money but requiring more effort in coordination and repaying favors.

Banquet: Chose a hotel of acceptable quality, with each table costing 1288 yuan, totaling 60 tables, spending 77,280 yuan. All the cigarettes, alcohol, candy, and tea were purchased personally, at a medium price level, not too expensive but still presentable, approximately costing 10,000 yuan.

Wedding Photos: Took outdoor photos at the zoo, with satisfactory results, costing over 4000 yuan.

Calculation: Clothing + Jewelry + Emcee + Videographer + On-site Decoration + Banquet + Wedding Photos = 110,420 yuan (Reported by Xiao Liu)
