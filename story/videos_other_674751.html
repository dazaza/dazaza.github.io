Credit brushing Introduction Taobao credit brushing www.yingkeduo.com, that is to say, in the Taobao shopping website, both buyers and sellers aim to raise their credit or make "satisfactory" and positive reviews without actual transactions. Process The detailed process of credit brushing is basically as follows: first, the buyer reaches a purchase agreement with you through QQ chat》the buyer places an order for the item you request》the buyer pays the goods money》you ship the goods on Taobao or Paipai》the buyer receives the goods and gives a positive review》you reply to the review, and the transaction ends. The Taobao credit brushing platform proceeds synchronously with the above steps. Methods Currently, there are several popular methods of credit brushing online: Proxy credit brushing for Taobao Proxy credit brushing for Taobao refers to entrusting the store to the proxy credit brushing party, then no management is required, the proxy party will brush to the required level, and payment is made after the brushing is completed. The cost is guaranteed by Alipay transactions. The characteristics of this method are time-saving, labor-saving, and secure. It does not require the store owner to operate themselves, avoiding risks caused by insufficient experience. Free group credit brushing A group of people in a group mutually brush credits. The drawback is low efficiency because there are not many people, the authenticity is not very strong, and the repetition rate of IP addresses is also high, making it easy to be blocked. There are many scammers, and fraud claims often occur! It is recommended to choose some super groups with 500 members. Ideally, choose a group that charges but offers compensation, if it's a three-person mutual brushing model, there will be almost no demotion. Risk level three to four stars! Platform credit brushing is an upgraded version of Taobao credit brushing. It is free, has a large number of people, high efficiency, safety, and relatively strong authenticity. Currently, there are many credit brushing platforms in the market. Some large platforms usually recruit professional brushers to take tasks. These people use small accounts to take hundreds of tasks a day. If the small account gets blocked, your Taobao account may also be affected. Therefore, it is necessary to find a safe platform. Risk level one star. Credit brushing preparation involves multiple small accounts mutual brushing What is meant by small accounts are unverified Taobao accounts. Every account registered on Taobao can only open a store after verification. An account verified is considered your main account. To ensure the mutual safety and benefits of everyone, each member should follow the rule: main account sells, small accounts buy. When members take tasks, please use the small account to place orders and pay. The most direct way for buyers to identify credit brushing 1. Look at the "credit evaluation" followed by "virtual commodity transaction". If this item is around 30%, then 90% of the credit is likely obtained through credit brushing. If the seller sells recharge cards or game coins, it can be ruled out as being brushed. For physical goods sellers, if this item is around 30%, the credit is definitely brushed. 2. For Taobao credit brushing, look at the transaction evaluations. Generally, the credit brushed behind will have amounts of 0.01 yuan, with item names like QQ numbers or QQ coins. These can be confidently judged as brushed credit. 3. Some careful buyers can see that products placed at the same time have evaluation records within a few minutes afterward, which is definitely brushed credit. Among the 80% of deceived buyers, they were cheated when buying from stores that had brushed their credit. Sometimes the product received is significantly different from what was ordered, or sometimes it's just an empty box. Buyers see the diamond or crown credit of the seller and lower their guard, resulting in being deceived. Here, I remind all Taobao friends, sellers and buyers, that credit is built on mutual trust, not through credit brushing. At the same time, buyers should not choose shopping stores solely based on credit levels. Some newly opened stores may not have much credit, but their product quality and prices are more advantageous than those crown stores. Let's all work together to completely eliminate credit brushing. Status Analysis If netizens buy goods on Taobao and give a positive review for the product, they contribute one credit to the seller. Most users consider how many credits a seller has as an important indicator when shopping on Taobao. For sellers, not brushing credit means getting hardly any orders in a month. This is a paradox of which came first, the chicken or the egg. Since Taobao has become so massive, newly registered store owners will find that all products already have sellers. Their newly registered stores have no competitive advantage. When users search for similar products, sellers with higher credit values rank higher, while new registered stores, having just started, have not accumulated enough credit yet. Their products often rank on pages one or two hundred later, and no buyers have the patience to flip through a hundred pages. Credit brushing (a colloquial term for credit brushing) is the only way to solve this paradox. First, create false transactions to brush the online store to three diamonds, four diamonds, or even crown-level stores, and then truly start doing business. At this point, since the store ranks higher, it is easier to attract customers. The phenomenon of credit brushing is deeply entrenched.
