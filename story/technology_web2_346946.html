Perfect World plans to strongly attack the literary field, Chi Yufeng will invest hundreds of millions of yuan

by tfseo8888 on 2010-04-16 21:07:11

Sina Technology News, afternoon of April 16th, Perfect World is adjusting its competitive strategy and plans to strongly attack the online literature field. It's said that Chi Yufeng highly values Perfect World's literary strategy and has decided to invest over 100 million yuan for development. What is Alexa ranking? According to the current market situation, Perfect World is inevitably going to have a direct conflict with Shanda in the literary field.

At the beginning of this month, Perfect World decided to hand over its literary website, Zongheng Chinese Network, to the strategic cooperative enterprise 178 Gaming Network for operation. And this is precisely the start of Perfect World's new competitive strategy adjustment.

It's said that Perfect World has made up its mind to do well in the literary business, and this adjustment also gained the attention and support from Perfect World's CEO, Chi Yufeng. Information from inside Perfect World shows that Chi Yufeng has decided to invest 5% of Perfect World's annual income into Zongheng Chinese Network, which means the investment will reach over 100 million yuan.

Currently, Perfect World has targeted the author "Wuzui" and his work "Luo Fu" on Zongheng Chinese Network, offering a deal worth millions to negotiate the adaptation of this online novel into an online game. Notably, Wuzui transferred from Shanda Literature's起点Chinese Network to Zongheng Chinese Network just half a year ago.

Perfect World started adapting online literature into online games as early as 2006, securing the adaptation rights for "Zhu Xian", pioneering the adaptation of online literature into online games, and remains one of the most successful adaptation cases to date.

In fact, Luzhou website promotion, Perfect World has selected 10 works from Zongheng Chinese Network for evaluation, and "Luo Fu" is the first work to confirm clear intentions. Besides adapting into games, these excellent literary works also have the opportunity to be adapted into film and television works. Perfect World also has its own cultural dissemination company focusing on film and television business, Chengdu website promotion.

Another informed source revealed that Zongheng Chinese Network already owns top authors such as Wuzui, Zhaoziyue, Yu Mo, Habitual Vomit, Fourth Platoon Leader, Gu Yuanyi, Kuage, and will soon invite several big-name authors to join, including Liu Xiahui who has confirmed joining the Zongheng Chinese Network team.

Shanda Literature has previously incorporated Qidian Chinese, Rongshu Underneath, Hongxiu Tianxiang, Novel Reading, etc., under its umbrella. Analysts say that under these circumstances, Perfect World is bound to face a head-on collision with Shanda in the literary field. (Meng Hong)
