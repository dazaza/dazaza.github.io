Weibo marriage certificate goes viral online, netizens parody KFC and McDonald's (pic)

by yanr201txue on 2012-03-09 12:11:28

Online screenshots By reporter Zhu Yangjian These days, "Weibo addicts" are all busy getting their certificates. What kind of certificate? A marriage certificate! To be precise, it's called a "Weibo Marriage Certificate." How many people have gotten one? By 9:00 AM yesterday, in just three days, more than 30,000 netizens had already obtained their certificates. Why get a marriage certificate? The netizens quoted a line from the movie "If You Are the One 2": "Life is short, I'm willing to go along with the mistake!" How to get the certificate? On Sina Weibo's special page, netizens only need to upload a photo of "you and him/her", fill in your nickname and declaration of love, and after confirming and publishing, a "Weibo Marriage Certificate" will be generated, complete with a big red stamp. Successful certificate applicants will automatically generate a Weibo post: "The China Wedding Expo approved our marriage!" Simple, fun, fresh, fashionable... Thus everyone became bold: "Not even close," but they quickly made up for it; those who secretly admired each other confessed their feelings; loving couples got their certificates again to relive the sweetness! The declarations of love were very touching. "In the morning, at night, in dreams, thinking about your beautiful and charming face, this certificate proves that we will last forever. If you don't agree, how dare I sign." "Love, there is nothing worth or not worth it, as long as you love me and I love you, that's enough." There were also some funny ones. "If you dare to cheat, you'll bear the consequences!" "Remember, yours is mine, and mine is still mine!" Of course, any online activity is bound to be parodied. So, on Weibo, some netizens "married" Jay Chou and Li Yuchun, others married Xiao Yueyue and Zhu Bajie, and some even acted as matchmakers, helping Brother Sharp and Sister Feng "flash marry." Not to be left out, Grandpa KFC and Uncle McDonald got their marriage certificates these past two days, with the love declaration: "Since I met you, life has been so delicious, I really like it." Even "Little Dragon" who is currently in the spotlight got married and received his certificate, unfortunately not with "He Jie" but with "Hao Shaowen." Netizens even wrote their love declaration: "When we were young, we were childhood sweethearts, now we are together, He Jie is just a fleeting cloud." Pitifully, even "Ji Xiaolan" and "He Shen" couldn't escape, their love declaration was "You are my He Fatso, I am your Lan Baby." ... Whether sincere or jesting, the "Weibo Marriage Certificate" is very popular. It was the weekend yesterday, and the number of people getting certificates surged! I just checked and then went back to look again, wow, it has already exceeded 40,000. After a quick calculation, nearly 10,000 people got their certificates in over an hour, "sweat"! In order not to be "out" (outdated), I hurriedly got one too! By the way, have you gotten yours?
