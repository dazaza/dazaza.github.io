Plot Summary

The story tells of A Jiu and A Ming from the previous life who could not be together due to the hatred between their two families. A Ming became a monk but still met his end being killed. In the final feud, A Jiu and A Ming both die under a thousand-year-old ginkgo tree. Before dying, A Jiu makes a pact with A Ming to meet again in this life, promising that she will come back to this ginkgo tree to find him, no matter what they have become, they will reunite. Then, without any ties of hatred, they will love each other wholeheartedly...

After A Jiu dies, she quickly reincarnates... But A Ming, fearing that A Jiu may come to this ginkgo tree to look for him at any moment, dares not leave, missing the opportunity to reincarnate, and can only linger as a ghost between the cycles of rebirth, waiting for this-life A Jiu to reappear... However, he can only stay on the cycle path for fifty years... Fifty years pass, but A Jiu still hasn't come... A Ming's time is up...

Just as A Ming is about to leave, A Jiu finally appears... She finally comes to this ginkgo tree... But this-life A Jiu is no longer the same as her past-life self; in this life, she is a young woman named Xiao Yu, who has completely forgotten her past-life memories. Moreover, she has a happy family and a loving husband whom she deeply loves... What will A Ming say to her before he leaves? Should he reveal himself to her? Can this-life Xiao Yu recall her past-life promise?... Can the unfinished love from the past life continue in this life?
