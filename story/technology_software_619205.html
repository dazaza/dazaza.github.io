Dietary therapy for the disappearance of breast pain in women

1. Breast Inflammation Pain

Traditional Chinese Medicine (TCM) believes that this condition is mostly caused by liver and stomach heat stagnation and milk accumulation.

Dietary Therapy Principle: Clear liver and stomach, detoxify, unblock meridians, dissipate lumps.

Commonly Used Dietary Therapy Prescriptions:

1. Silk Gourd Stewed Tofu: 150g silk gourd, 100g tofu, cook together with water for 20 minutes, add ginger, green onion, and sesame oil before serving. Once daily.

2. Honey Juice Figs: 100g figs, 30g hawthorn, boil for 20 minutes with an appropriate amount of water, take the juice and add honey before consumption. Twice daily.

3. Platycodon Red Bean Porridge: 10g platycodon, 10g soap thorn, boil for 20 minutes with an appropriate amount of water, take the juice and mix it into red bean porridge before consumption. Twice daily. Suitable for the purulent stage of mastitis.

4. Pollen Angelica Porridge: 10g astragalus, 10g pollen, 5g angelica, boil for 20 minutes with an appropriate amount of water, take the juice and add brown sugar before consumption. Twice daily. Suitable for the ulcerative stage of mastitis.

2. Breast Hyperplasia Pain

TCM believes that this condition is mostly caused by liver qi stagnation, phlegm coagulation, and blood stasis.

Dietary Therapy Principle: Soothe liver and regulate qi, clear heat and dissipate lumps, adjust Chong and Ren meridians.

Commonly Used Dietary Therapy Prescriptions:

1. Corn Silk Gourd Mesh Soup: 100g corn, 50g silk gourd mesh, 10g tangerine kernel, 1 egg. Boil the first three ingredients for 1 hour, add egg flower, water starch, and rock sugar before serving. Twice weekly.

2. Kelp Lettuce Stew: 100g kelp, 100g lettuce, a little ginger and green onion minced. Cook the kelp in water for 30 minutes, add lettuce, seasonings, and sesame oil before serving. Once daily.

3. Cold Mixed Celery Kelp: 100g kelp, 100g celery, a little ginger and green onion minced. Blanch the kelp and celery, place in a dish, add seasonings and sesame oil before serving. Once daily.

4. Prunella Angelica Porridge: 10g prunella, 10g angelica, 10g cyperus, boil for 20 minutes with an appropriate amount of water, take the juice and mix it into white porridge with brown sugar before consumption. Twice weekly.

3. Breast Cancer Pain

TCM believes that the pathogenesis of this condition involves imbalance of Chong and Ren meridians, liver and kidney deficiency, liver depression, phlegm stasis, and toxic evil accumulation.

Dietary Therapy Principle: Should be combined with comprehensive treatment without affecting regular treatment.

Commonly Used Dietary Therapy Prescriptions:

1. Roast an appropriate amount of crab shells dry, grind into powder for standby use. Take 2g each time, three times daily, drink with yellow wine.

2. Stew and eat 30-90g of winterstem. Twice weekly.

3. 10g pangolin scales, 15g salvia, boil for 20 minutes with an appropriate amount of water, mix into white porridge before consumption. Once daily.

4. 10g beehive, 10g Zhejiang fritillary bulb, 10g mountain corydalis, 10g zedoary rhizome, 30g snake tongue grass, 30g coix seed, boil for 20 minutes with an appropriate amount of water, take the juice and add an appropriate amount of rock sugar before consumption. Twice weekly.

Long-term Health Network Reminder: There are several types of breast pain. Choose suitable dietary therapy methods according to different types of pain. The TCM prescriptions introduced above have good effects in relieving pain and are widely welcomed.
