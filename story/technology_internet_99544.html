100 Life Hacks to Last a Lifetime

by luomazhe on 2008-10-29 15:06:51

【Pain Relief】 1, Clever use of toothpaste: If there is a small area of skin damage or burns, scalds, applying a little toothpaste can immediately stop bleeding and relieve pain, and also prevent infection, with quite good results.

【Glass】 2, Clever way to remove grease from screens: Place laundry detergent and cigarette butts in water until they dissolve, then use it to clean glass windows and screens, both with good effects.

【Eating Shrimp】 3, Secret tip for stir-frying shrimp: Place the shrimp in a bowl, add a little salt and edible alkali powder, rub with your hands for a while, then soak in clear water, and wash again with clear water. This makes the stir-fried shrimp transparent like crystal, tender and delicious.

【Dumplings】 4, Secret tip for kneading dumpling dough: Add six egg whites to one pound of flour to increase the protein content in the dough. When the dumplings are cooked, the protein will quickly coagulate and shrink, making the dumplings quick to drain water after being served, and less likely to stick together.

【Tea Leaves】 5, Soak leftover tea leaves in water for several days, then water the plants at the roots to promote plant growth; dry the leftover tea leaves, burn them in the toilet or ditch, which can eliminate bad odors and repel mosquitoes and flies.

【Cooking Rice】 6, Re-cooking underdone rice method: If the rice is undercooked, use chopsticks to poke some holes through to the bottom of the pot, sprinkle a little cooking wine, and re-steam. If only the surface is undercooked, just turn the top layer to the middle and steam again.

【Dumplings】 7, When cooking vegetables if blanching is necessary, it's best to make full use of the blanched water. For example, when making dumpling stuffing, an appropriate amount of the blanched water can be added to the meat filling, thus preserving nutrients and making the dumpling filling flavorful and juicy.
