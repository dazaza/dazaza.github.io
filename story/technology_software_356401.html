The proportion of Guangdong women buying houses is increasing.

by cscj010181 on 2010-05-27 17:16:28

In today's world where there are more and more powerful women, it is apparently out of date to think that men alone can decide on buying a house. Reporters have noticed the increasing proportion of home-buying women in Zhaoqing, Guangdong, and analyzed their purchasing and living characteristics.

The "moonlight clan" (those who spend all their income each month): romantic lovers

Calling single ladies before marriage the "moonlight clan" is because they tend to be romantic when in love. Local surveys show that such "moonlight clan" ladies already account for 18% of all female home buyers locally. The majority of these moonlight clan members were born after 1980 and generally work as senior white-collar workers in enterprises or public institutions for over a year.

Among the moonlight clan, reasons for buying houses include experiencing the troubles of moving and thinking that they are young and not ready for marriage but want to own their own space. Meanwhile, daily life expenses plus rental fees take up most of the monthly salary of moonlight clan ladies. Therefore, they mainly rely on parents' help for down payments, treating their previous rental fees as mortgage payments.

Baby Clan: Welcoming baby's birth

Surveys show that married ladies with children, known as the baby clan, account for approximately 42% of all female home buyers locally.

These home-buying women have limited working years due to family commitments. After forming a family, they start to learn how to manage finances carefully. For the happiness of the next generation, they have certain requirements for housing. The baby clan believes that the reason for buying a house is to welcome the birth of the baby and expand their living space.

Investment type: Pursuing stable returns

The term investment type refers to middle-aged women. According to local statistics, they account for about 16% of all female home buyers. The investment type has a certain economic foundation and thus regards real estate trading as their main investment direction.

The investment type believes that doing other businesses is too tiring or that stock trading carries high risks. Real estate investment offers high and stable returns. In terms of home purchase methods, they either pay in full or take out small loans and repay them early if there is cash flow.

The goal of the investment type is to reduce investment risks and pursue medium- to long-term, stable investment returns.

Elderly care type: Seeking a peaceful retirement

According to local statistics, elderly care types account for 24% of female home buyers. Having struggled in bustling cities for most of their lives, they hope to choose a place with good environment, fresh air, convenient transportation to enjoy a peaceful retirement at the age of retirement.

The home purchase reasons for the elderly care type are simple: enjoying a peaceful retirement and desiring a living place conducive to physical and mental health. Their home purchase method is also full payment. Moreover, they prefer places with excellent air quality, fewer residents, relatively convenient transportation, and very smooth roads to hospitals.
