On Tuesday, July 7th at 10 a.m. local time in Los Angeles (1 a.m. Beijing Time on Wednesday, July 8th), the funeral and public memorial service for pop king Michael Jackson will be held in Los Angeles. The location of Jackson's funeral is the "Forest Lawn" Cemetery in Los Angeles, while the memorial service will take place at Staples Center. At that time, Tencent.com will start reporting related developments from 9 p.m., and provide full live coverage of the funeral and memorial service, including on-site video live streaming, pictures, text, and other comprehensive information sources. Netizens are welcome to participate online and interact at that time.

According to foreign media reports, residents of Los Angeles have also prepared for large crowds on that day. They said that since 9/11, Jackson's funeral is the first time that news other than sports has been broadcast on large screens in the area. The police have warned the public to consider public safety, and those without tickets are better off watching TV broadcasts at home. And according to traffic control by the police, it will be difficult for people without tickets to get close to the "Forest Lawn" Cemetery or Staples Center, let alone enter the site.

Jackson's public funeral will be covered live by several major American news organizations, and several major American television networks will also broadcast live. Commentators pointed out that the live broadcast of Jackson's public funeral will become the grandest celebrity funeral in history, and it will become a milestone in the history of American television. This can be seen from the increasing number of people around the world who are watching the funeral online, and its popularity may even surpass the broadcast of U.S. President Obama's inauguration ceremony.
