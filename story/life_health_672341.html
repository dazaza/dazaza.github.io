by betty2012 on 2012-02-22 17:20:18

How to use a pregnancy test strip most accurately? Pregnancy test strips are a product designed for women to conveniently check if they are pregnant. They have the advantages of simple operation and quick result display, but they are not 100% safe and reliable. Experts from a gynecological hospital in Xi'an pointed out that the correct testing rate of pregnancy test strips varies greatly, ranging from 50% to 98%. The usage method of pregnancy test strips is very simple. Below, we will take a detailed look.

How to use pregnancy test strips most accurately?

1. Pay attention to the production date on the packaging of the pregnancy test strip

Before using the pregnancy test strip, you should first check its production date. Do not use expired test cards, as chemical agents that have been unused for a long time will become ineffective. Pregnancy test strips that have lost their potency are useless. Therefore, women must confirm the production date before using them.

2. Check the operating instructions

Before going to the bathroom to operate, women should carefully read the instructions for the test card, then follow the instructions carefully. When operating, pay attention to the length of urine that should immerse the test strip. Sometimes, immersing too much urine may make it difficult to judge the results.

3. Use morning urine for testing

Morning and evening pregnancy tests may have some impact on the results. Morning urine generally has the highest HCG level, so many instructions suggest testing when you wake up in the morning, but this is not absolute.

4. Determine the testing time

HCG usually appears in urine a few days after the fertilized egg implants, and it needs to reach a certain amount to be detected. Therefore, women with regular menstrual cycles need to wait until their period is delayed to possibly detect HCG in their urine. Women with longer menstrual cycles or ovulation abnormalities may only be able to detect it 40-44 days after their last period.

5. Drink less water

Drinking too much water can dilute the urine, which may lead to false negative results. Therefore, it's best not to drink water before testing.
