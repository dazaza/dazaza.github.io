1. Women who have had an abortion should undergo genetic testing, and both partners should simultaneously undergo chromosomal testing.

2. Female friends should avoid pregnancy within six months after an abortion, waiting until after six months before becoming pregnant again. This reduces the occurrence of abortion.

3. Those with cervical incompetence can undergo cervical cerclage surgery.
