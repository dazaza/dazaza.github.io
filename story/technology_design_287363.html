Introduction: When beauty pageants no longer reject "artificial beauties"

Ten years ago, plastic surgery was a taboo in beauty contests. It was common to hear news about popular contestants having to withdraw due to plastic surgery. Today, plastic surgery has become a fashionable lifestyle. More and more candidates admit that plastic surgery makes it easier for them to achieve the ideal "three dimensions" compared to contestants ten years ago. Some top domestic beauty pageants have also opened their doors to "artificial beauties". For many ordinary-looking people, beauty and dreams are no longer out of reach. More often than not, attitude is everything.

The "New Four Beauties" make their debut

"Beauty" cannot be resisted. Artificial "Yang Guifei" and "Wang Zhaojun" win consecutive beauty pageant awards.

"The charming smile of Yang Guifei could outshine all the other beauties." Historically, Yang Guifei was known for her extraordinary beauty and talent. Similarly, the artificial "Yang Guifei" today is equally talented and stunning. On August 18th, the finals of one of China's top model contests, the "2009 New Silk Road Model Contest Henan Province Final," concluded. Li Na, the new Yuhuan among the "New Four Beauties," won the gold award in the contest's print category with her extraordinary beauty and remarkable talent.

Not long ago, Guo Zhiyan, the new Diaochan, won the bronze medal at the 2009 International Fashion Supermodel Contest Central China Finals. Within a short period, the "New Four Beauties" have gained prominence in several major beauty contests, achieving continuous success. These four exceptionally beautiful girls have quickly become focal points of attention and hot topics of discussion. Numerous media outlets have covered their stories, and experts and scholars have provided commentary. Recently, Henan TV's popular program "DV Observation Xiao Han Talk Show" conducted a special interview with He Jie, the president of Zhengzhou Oriental Plastic Surgery Hospital, who played a pivotal role in creating the "New Four Beauties." The stories of the "New Four Beauties" have thus entered countless households, becoming inspiring legends of dream-chasing.

"Yang Yuhuan" faced near disfigurement due to failed double eyelid surgery

Li Na, the new Yuhuan, was originally a fitness coach. She was lively and outgoing, with a dream of one day walking on the T-stage as a "super model." However, she was very self-aware at the time: "I used to have inner double eyelids, round eyes, and droopy inner corners. My face was round with no dimensionality, my chin was so short it looked like I didn't have one. Although my face wasn't big, every photo made me look like I had a pancake face." Her ordinary appearance made her feel like an ugly duckling who couldn't stand on stage. The world of models seemed colorful yet distant to her.

Two years ago, Li Na had a tiny cyst on her left eyelid. The doctor suggested she undergo double eyelid surgery, which could both treat the condition and enlarge her eyes. Li Na happily agreed, but the results were disappointing: "One eye was larger than the other, one double eyelid was obvious while the other wasn't, one eye line was longer and the other shorter. The small cyst was removed, but the stitching at the corner of the eye looked unnatural and quite scary. I even thought about dying."

Despite the heavy blow from the failed double eyelid surgery, Li Na did not fall into despair. Instead, she began actively seeking ways to improve her beauty, paying unprecedented attention to plastic surgery information. Over time, her preference leaned towards Zhengzhou Oriental Plastic Surgery Hospital. She planned to go there to repair her eyes. Around this time, the grand "New Four Beauties" recruitment event began. With her multi-talented skills in singing and dancing, Li Na felt this was an excellent opportunity and enthusiastically registered. As expected, whether during the preliminary rounds or the finals, Li Na shone brightly and stood out as the most dazzling star. She ranked first in the preliminary rounds and again in the finals, indisputably becoming the first talented woman among the "New Four Beauties" and easily winning the championship in the Yang Yuhuan group.

Over the following two months, Li Na underwent eight surgeries at Zhengzhou Oriental Plastic Surgery Hospital, including opening the inner and outer corners of her eyes, repairing the failed double eyelid, reshaping her nose with 3D technology, enhancing her jawline, removing cheek fat pads, and injecting Botox to slim her masseter muscles. Ultimately, she transformed into a stunningly beautiful woman. Encouraged by friends, Li Na participated in the 2009 New Silk Road Model Contest and successfully won the gold award. Reflecting on her award-winning experience, Li Na deeply said: "When I saw how I had changed, I suddenly realized that dreams are no longer far away. In fact, if you're willing to strive for your dreams, they will be within reach."

The "New Wang Zhaojun" once sold goods on the street to make ends meet

Guo Zhiyan, the new Wang Zhaojun, has bright eyes and a radiant smile. After returning from the 2009 International Fashion Supermodel Contest, she exuded confidence and optimism, a stark contrast to her former timidity and melancholy. Who would have guessed that three months ago, Guo Zhiyan was just a young girl selling goods on the street, with no education or work experience, unsure where her future lay?

Due to financial difficulties, Guo Zhiyan only studied drama performance up to junior high school. Despite being intelligent and diligent, with solid basic skills, she could only play minor roles in film crews, never getting the chance to lead. Partly because she was too young, partly because of her looks: "My mouth didn't look good, paired with my chin, making me look old. Friends said my face looked like a moon from the side. My nose was short and low, and my facial features were disproportionate. Makeup made me look worse than without makeup. From childhood, I could only play elderly roles!"

With no prospects in the film industry, Guo Zhiyan left her beloved stage to seek work. But lacking work experience, she faced rejection everywhere, deepening her feelings of inadequacy. She felt worthless and even questioned her existence.

When all hope seemed lost, fate offered a glimmer of light. Enrolling with her sister in the "New Four Beauties" competition unexpectedly won her the championship in the Wang Zhaojun group, giving her a glimpse of dawn. Perhaps this was a turning point? She silently prayed.

At Zhengzhou Oriental Plastic Surgery Hospital, Guo Zhiyan underwent seven surgeries, including 3D nose reshaping, enlarging the outer corners of her eyes, autologous fat injection to plump her temples, filling nasolabial folds, cheeks, Botox injections to slim her masseter muscles, and Fotona laser treatment for acne and whitening. The surgeries were successful, and Guo Zhiyan was amazed to find herself more beautiful. Her nose became straight, tall, and delicate. Most importantly, her mouth and chin now looked perfect. She felt as if magic had been cast upon her, making her instantly radiant, and her life, like her appearance, transitioned from darkness to brightness.

"I participated in the International Supermodel Contest and won a bronze medal; it feels like a dream!" Guo Zhiyan said. Now, several modeling agencies want to sign her, and commercial offers are abundant. However, her wish is to earn enough money to return home and start a school, allowing more children like her to gain knowledge.

"The Four Beauties were not only beautiful but also talented, which allowed them to influence history. In today's society, beauty may give you relatively more opportunities than others, but ultimate success still depends on one's knowledge and ability."

Postscript: An American scholar once said, "Beauty is something we strive for, not something we are born with." Li Jingyi (new Xishi), Guo Zhiyan (new Zhaojun), Cao Yingzi (new Diaochan), and Li Na (new Yuhuan) were originally ordinary girls. Because they dared to change and challenge themselves, they won the favor of destiny, not only acquiring stunning beauty but also embarking on a more splendid new life.

"The continuous awards won by the 'New Four Beauties' in various beauty pageants represent society's recognition and affirmation of artificial beauties." President He Jie of Zhengzhou Oriental Plastic Surgery Hospital believes that with the improvement of living standards, plastic surgery has become an increasingly common phenomenon in society. Even in countries renowned for producing natural beauties, such as Venezuela and Brazil, the top beauties selected each year are "artificial beauties." The development of plastic surgery technology provides more and more ordinary girls with the right and convenience to realize their dreams. Before their transformations, the "New Four Beauties" were also very ordinary girls. In the future, there will no longer be a distinction between "artificial" and "natural," only between beautiful and not beautiful.
