Reunion Yi Nanan Plot Introduction:

The 4th part of the long-running drama "Yi Nanan" will start airing two episodes daily from April 6, 2009, at 5 PM on CCTV-8. CCTV's "Reunion Yi Nanan" continues the plot from Part 3, with a total of 168 episodes, continuing from the original version of Minshi’s 280 episodes.

The first 168 episodes broadcast on CCTV mainly describe the story of the protagonist Wang Shengtian building his business from scratch and striving hard, intertwined with his emotional entanglements with three women. Director Wang Wei stated that the second season's 160 episodes focus more on the second generation of the main characters. As time passes, Wang Shengtian and his younger brother Shengzhi shift their businesses towards electronics and trade with Japanese people, making them commercial rivals once again. Moreover, Tianzhu, the son of Shengtian and Lizhu, has grown up but ends up helping outsiders scheme against his own father. Lin Dazhong's wife Xuelian died after falling off a cliff in the first season, surprising many viewers. Wang Wei explained that this was because Han Yu, who played Xuelian, had to film another drama, so they designed this plot twist. However, due to the audience's fondness for Han Yu, they demanded her "resurrection." Therefore, after she finished filming the other drama, a woman named Shanni, who looks exactly like Xuelian, appeared in the drama. Wang Shengtian still harbors feelings for Xuelian, and Shanni's appearance causes ripples in Shengtian's heart. In summary, the second season not only features conspiracies and calculations in the business world but also includes brotherly affection, father-son relationships, and mother-in-law-daughter-in-law relationships, making it more exciting than the first season.

CCTV Film and Television Department Director Wang Guohui expressed that before this, CCTV had never introduced such a long television series as "Yi Nanan," and initially had little confidence in its ratings. They especially did not expect that a drama starting at 5 PM could achieve a peak rating of 2.23%, higher than some dramas aired during prime time. The audience's demand for urgent introduction was very high, and the Chief Editor's Office and the Film and Television Department received numerous calls every day. After fully considering the audience's opinions, they quickly introduced another 160 episodes after the first season ended. Thus, "Yi Nanan" has already aired 328 episodes. Depending on the ratings of the remaining episodes, there is a significant possibility that they will continue to air. The audience can see the complete "Yi Nanan," while CCTV also sets a new record, with the 526-episode "Yi Nanan" surpassing the Korean drama "Mermaid Lady" to become the longest series. Furthermore, "Yi Nanan" enriches the variety of dramas broadcast by CCTV during the Spring Festival. On February 9, "Rural Love 2," starring Zhao Benshan and Fan Wei, aired on "One Yellow," which is a rural comedy featuring China's most popular comedians, making it a dish very suitable for the festive atmosphere. On "Eight Yellow," "Flower of Women," starring Qin Hailu and Liu Tao, aired, which is a period drama. Meanwhile, "Yi Nanan," with its dramatic twists and touching character fates, pairs well with "Rural Love 2," giving viewers different choices.
