The heavy-duty GF wheel lock (vehicle immobilizer) is a specialized product designed by our company for heavy-duty trailers, dump trucks, large container trucks, and large buses. Product features: 1. The structure adopts the optimal mechanical principle, designed with three-claw fixation. While the nail cover square disc tightly adheres to the wheel, the locking device can firmly clamp the wheel. 2. Material-wise, it uses three robust rectangular pipes measuring 400mm*800mm*3mm. The nail cover square plate is made from double-layered 5mm high-quality steel plates formed by mold pressing. The lock weighs up to 33KG, making it sturdy and durable. 3. In construction, it employs an exclusive three-claw fixation and a freely extendable tire locking device, capable of locking tires with diameters of 900 models or larger on heavy vehicles. 4. A dual insurance mechanism design ensures that the special handle and imported rectangular lock are used together, none of which can be missing, allowing it to withstand any tool's smashing and prying.
