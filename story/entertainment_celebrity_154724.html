Who on earth is Dong Weijia, the affair partner of Lam Man Lung?

by addintime on 2009-06-01 00:26:13

TVB model couple, Lam Man Lung and Gigi Leung, are experiencing a tumultuous marriage due to the interference of mainland newcomer, Dong Weijia. A leaked phone recording suspected to be between Lam Man Lung and Gigi Leung's agent has brought the truth of the matter to light. Gigi Leung's agent confirmed Lam Man Lung's infidelity and stated that a statement regarding the matter will be released soon.

Dong Weijia and Lam Man Lung fell in love while filming together. Dong Weijia, a graduate of the Shanghai Theatre Academy, has previously worked as a female host on TV programs such as "Entertainment Online." In recent years, she has also acted in films and TV dramas like "Wrong Love II," "来电奇缘" ("来电奇缘"), and "朝九晚无" ("9 to 5"). In particular, in the popular drama on Dragon TV, "狐步谍影" ("Foxtrot Spy Shadow"), she portrayed a Japanese anti-war officer who sacrificed herself to stop a war. In 2007, it was rumored that she and Lam Man Lung developed feelings for each other while filming in Shanghai.

In her blog post on October 5th of last year, Dong Weijia extensively discussed her views on love: whether good or bad, the most important thing is knowing the truth. Subsequently, in her blog post on October 20th, she revealed her mood, stating that due to a friend's recommendation, she watched the movie "Sex and the City." She said she felt a strong connection after watching it, and the storyline of the lead character going through a breakup felt like it happened to her own life. "When heartbroken, every moment memories of her Mr. Wrong (the wrong man) and his past keep flashing in her mind." This suggests she had gone through some emotional trauma.
