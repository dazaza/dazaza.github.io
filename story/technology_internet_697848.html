Experts from Taiyuan Skin Disease Hospital pointed out that keratosis pilaris mostly occurs on the upper arms, outer thighs, hairline areas, and buttocks of human body. Some patients may also be affected on the abdomen. Due to the obvious location, severe cases can increase the psychological burden of patients. Experts from Taiyuan Skin Disease Hospital pointed out that keratosis pilaris is recurrent and difficult to eradicate. Keratosis pilaris is manifested as hard papules ranging from pinpoints to millet-sized, not fused together, with light brown keratin plugs on the top containing coiled hair. After removing the keratin plug, a funnel-shaped small depression can be seen, but soon new keratin plugs will grow in this depression. The inflammatory degree of the papules varies, there may be no redness or obvious redness, the latter easily leads to post-inflammatory pigmentation. Patients with keratosis pilaris will find that the affected areas have a special rough feeling, which worsens in winter and alleviates in summer. Experts from Taiyuan Skin Disease Hospital pointed out that keratosis pilaris is a chronic follicular keratotic skin disease, commonly seen in children and adolescents. If symptoms are found, treatment should be started as soon as possible to avoid irreversible damage to the skin.
