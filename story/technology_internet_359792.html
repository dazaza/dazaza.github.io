Eliminate the "white terror" starting with the "heart"

by king0r8k on 2010-07-03 08:47:40

    Vitiligo is caused by the reduction or disappearance of tyrosinase activity in melanocytes within the skin and hair follicles, leading to a progressive decrease or disappearance in the generation of melanin granules, resulting in localized or generalized depigmentary lesions. The incidence rate is about 0.5%-2%, occurring worldwide. The pathogenic mechanism of this disease is complex with many influencing factors, including mental and chemical factors, autoimmune factors, melanocyte self-destruction factors, endocrine factors, free radical damage factors, epidermal oxidative stress factors, relative deficiency factors of tyrosine and copper ions, and genetic and infection factors. In recent years, experts have found that vitiligo is closely related to mental factors. A authoritative institution in our country once conducted a survey on the inducing factors of 904 patients with vitiligo, among which mental factors accounted for 29.65%. These patients, at the onset of the disease or when the disease has stabilized, improved, or even been cured, due to the influence of stressful mental factors such as mental trauma, overwork, and tension, cause the white spots to expand, increase, or recur.

Regarding the topic that www.jkykkfw.com netizens are concerned about ——the relationship between mental factors and vitiligo, the author made an appointment via telephone 010-63620988 to interview experts from Beijing Jinkē Vitiligo Hospital, obtaining their insightful and unique responses.

  Experts from Beijing Jinkē Vitiligo Hospital pointed out that many patients with vitiligo become disheartened after learning they have the condition, feeling nervous and always worried, fearing others will look down on them, and fearing their illness cannot be cured. Feelings of inferiority, anxiety, and tension... various negative emotions are likely experienced by each patient with vitiligo. Such psychological factors are not conducive to recovery, as these factors will affect the immune defense function through endocrine effects, causing thymus involution and obstacles in the maturation of immune lymphocytes, thus making it easier for vitiligo to develop and spread, complicating treatment. When the body becomes overly stressed, it secretes excessive adrenaline in response, consuming tyrosine, which is an essential raw material for melanin synthesis, thereby affecting melanin synthesis and making it unfavorable for the treatment of vitiligo.

At the same time, when a person is excessively mentally stressed, the body secretes excessive adrenaline in response, consuming leucine, which is also an essential raw material for melanin synthesis, thereby affecting melanin synthesis and making it unfavorable for the treatment of vitiligo. Moreover, adrenaline also hinders melanin metabolism.

Finally, the expert emphasized that eliminating white terror should start from the heart.

Firstly, one's spirit should be optimistic, and the mood should be cheerful, without pessimism and impatience. One should try to overcome all unnecessary concerns, remove impatience and fear and other negative factors, actively cooperate with the doctor's treatment, and through early, timely, and effective treatment, the vast majority of patients can be cured.

Secondly, alleviate excessive mental stress. Moderate mental stress is needed in life, work, and study. However, stress that affects normal life, work, and study is harmful to health. Anyone in life may inevitably encounter unfortunate events, but everyone can also learn methods to cope with stress and avoid excessive mental stress.

 

Finally, maintain a reasonable diet and control what you eat. Diet is also a concern for many patients. It is advisable to consume more foods rich in tyrosine and substances like copper, zinc, and iron, such as lean meat, eggs, various animal offal, milk, loofah, eggplant, carrots, and other fresh vegetables, as well as bean products.
