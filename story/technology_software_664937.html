Four things that must be done for a successful personal website operation

We all hope to do our best and no one is willing to spend time without getting satisfactory returns. However, rewards are proportional to efforts. Without extraordinary efforts, there won't be astonishing achievements. There are many factors that determine the success of an event, but the key elements are just a few. As long as we grasp these factors, success will no longer be far away.

First: Accurate positioning, highlighting characteristics. When criminals do bad things, they always have certain motives. Without motives, they wouldn't take action. For example, when we usually do things, we have a clear goal in mind. If we follow others' footsteps, we will eventually end up in a dead end. Everyone has different strengths, so the development space is completely different. Moreover, different people have significant differences in personality. We cannot listen to what others say and then do it. Such practices will only lose our own independence and cause changes in destiny. When we do a personal website, we cannot see something profitable and then do it. Every industry has a cycle, this cycle is like a curve with ups and downs, prosperity and decline convert, it's impossible to remain at the same level continuously. When an industry is not favored, you can choose to do it because not many people are doing it. This is the most appropriate time to act, with weak competition. If many people are doing it, there is simply no money to be made. Since many people are doing it, the entire market is divided into small pieces. Before making a personal website, conduct a survey online. Look at which industries have fewer participants and still have development potential, or can make big money. There is no need to follow others' thoughts; having your own ideas is the most important. Without ideas, nothing great can be accomplished. Many seemingly unachievable things may become possible if you try. People who don't even dare to think about them absolutely have no chance of success. Following trends will only make you pay a heavy price, as many people have said. Only by developing based on our own foundation can we make our personal websites better.

Second: Stable space, advanced domain name. We live in a stable environment and enjoy a good life. In chaotic times, life is truly unbearable. No one wants to live in a messy environment, as it greatly affects their future. Similarly, a website's space must be stable and good. Without a stable space, it will have a great negative impact on the development of the website. First, if the space is unstable, the website's opening speed will slow down significantly, and there will also be a risk of being shut down. If you really want to develop long-term, you must spend more money on this aspect. There is no free lunch in the world, and even if there is, it's a tempting trap. We cannot save a little money by finding free space. A stable space must cost money. Good things are worth spending money on, and then realizing their maximum value. Without a stable space, there won't be stable traffic. We cannot lose sight of the bigger picture and be overly concerned with minor issues. Whatever we do, we must first invest before receiving returns; there is no such thing as a free lunch. Nowadays, society should not believe in falling pies from the sky. A stable space is not enough; we also need a memorable domain name. A memorable domain name can put your website ahead of competitors unconsciously. No one has succeeded by giving up halfway. Many people leave regrets because they couldn't persist until the end. Giving up because of a little setback or temptation, God only favors those who can sincerely and genuinely pay for success and those who persist until the end. Doing promotional work is a very long process. No one can skip this part. Going through this part is necessary, and this experience can increase potential for development.
