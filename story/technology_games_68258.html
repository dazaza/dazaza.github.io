Open the "Gate of Heroes" and explore the storm of online games.

by 56963tg on 2008-05-12 20:14:30

In today's world of increasingly more MMORPGs, most players are probably starting to get tired of spending all day in the game killing monsters and leveling up. Otherwise, they have to resort to using game cheats. If your computer specs are slightly less than ideal or your internet speed is limited, you will be at a huge disadvantage when PKing, turning heroes into cowards. How can we find an entertainment method that is both relaxing and interesting? Recently, I learned about a game from the staff of "China Game Center." This game doesn't require installing any game programs; you just need to register an account on http://www.chinagames.net to start playing. You might ask, how do you PK then? Ha ha, this is the essence of this webgame: the game's battle system. In the game "Gate of Heroes," you need to establish your kingdom and build your own army, letting heroes with various attributes lead your army to attack other enemies. This battle mode isn't the traditional point-and-click + shortcut key for PKing. It's more like a chess-like combat style, testing the player's understanding of troop combinations and counter-troop strategies. By utilizing different troop and hero ability combinations, you can create countless configurations tailored to various situations, opponents' tactics, and troop types. Thus, this kind of PK lets you experience being a military strategist. Once poorly combined, it's easy to lose the kingdom you've built, making it exciting, challenging, thrilling, and psychologically stimulating.

This is the latest closed beta test WEB game from China Game Center called "Gate of Heroes." Users can log in through the Zongyou "Gate of Heroes" special zone homepage to participate in the game's closed beta test. During the closed beta test period, all players who log into the game and reach level 5 will receive a beginner help card when the internal test begins, allowing them to use the same account to log in and play. The beginner card includes one silver coin card and 100,000 copper coins (one silver coin card can be exchanged for 60 silver coins in the game!).

I believe many players are still unfamiliar with WEB games. Do these games, which don't require downloading and only need internet access, require long hours in front of the computer? No! As long as you set your plan, your great kingdom will grow according to your ideas. Below, I'll briefly explain how to play this WEBGAME.

Entering this game is very simple; just log in to the website address to immediately start your gaming journey.

After entering the game, choose an empire that suits your preference (Holy/Wizard/Dark) and character. Unlike other games, in this game, you don't have to slowly level up like a common soldier to gain power and territory. From the moment you enter the game, you have the potential to become a king (haha, so refreshing, where are my concubines??), then begin managing your country by building your nation, controlling taxes, recruiting and training soldiers to expand your influence. With strength, you rely on attacking other "countries" to grow your power.

After entering the game, the upper-left corner will display the player's basic character information.

You can always check your level and wealth status.

Alright, after introducing all the function buttons, we can take a stroll around.

I chose the human race (Holy Empire), so let me explain some initial points from the human perspective. After entering the game, you will automatically enter the village elder's house, where the elder gives you a task: retrieve the lost scepter for the princess (why does the king visit the village elder's home? And why does he have to handle the affairs of a young princess? Funny enough, the game automatically assigns you a daughter upon entry, which is quite awkward). Just press the confirmation button on the right side to accept the task. Then, after stepping outside, you can see the mini-map (as shown below).

Click on the target location's big tree, and our king's hero will lead the peasant army to eliminate the enemy. The battle process is very simple and requires almost no operation because the system will automatically conduct the battle based on both sides' troops. After winning easily, the player can gain experience points and items.

After completing the first novice-level task and handing it over to the mysterious daughter who appeared out of nowhere, you officially grasp the general gameplay of the game. Remember, at this point, you can collect taxes once; don’t let those dead peasants off the hook. Collect all the money they owe, haha. Then develop technology and buildings, and you can go out to complete tasks and fight monsters.

One thing to note is that recruiting neutral troops in the wild requires conditions, but the arena on the map can make your leveling journey easier.

Now, I've basically introduced the basics of this game, and I believe players have a preliminary understanding. Below are some small tips to keep in mind:

1. Focus on developing domestic policies, don't forget to collect taxes. You can collect them after a certain amount of time has passed. Only with sufficient resources can you develop and grow.

2. Recruit some soldiers and replace the farmers. Let the farmers go do what they're supposed to do.

3. Recruiting multiple heroes is the best way to quickly grow stronger.

4. Take a walk around the city occasionally; occupying external resource points will allow you to develop faster.

5. If you want to be lazy, you can send your hero to the arena to level up, which is very convenient.

For more details, please follow the latest activity announcements from China Game Center yourself~
